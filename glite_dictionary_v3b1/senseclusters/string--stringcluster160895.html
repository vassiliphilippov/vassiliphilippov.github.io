string

noun
tough fiber in vegetables or meat
1.7
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ / (AmE), / strɪŋ / (BrE)
a tough, fibrous strand found in vegetables (especially beans) or meat, often making the food difficult to chew

Examples

Bean string
Before cooking the beans, she removed the string from each pod.
He found a string in his stew and had to spit it out.
Older celery often has a tough string running down its stalks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fiber - a threadlike structure forming part of meat or vegetable tissue
