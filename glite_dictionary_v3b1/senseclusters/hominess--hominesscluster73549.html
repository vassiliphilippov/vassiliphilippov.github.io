hominess

noun
plainness of appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊminəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊminəs / (BrE)
the quality of being plain or unattractive in appearance

Examples

Her hominess
Despite her hominess, she was admired for her kindness and intelligence.
The artist embraced the hominess of his subjects, finding beauty in their ordinary features.
Some critics commented on the hominess of the building, calling it unremarkable but welcoming.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beauty - the quality of being pleasing to the senses or attractive
  • synonym: plainness - the quality of being simple or not beautiful
  • synonym: ugliness - the quality of being very unattractive
