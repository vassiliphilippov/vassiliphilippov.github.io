Jerusalem cross

noun
cross with arms ending in small crossbars
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈruːsələm krɑs / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm krɒs / (BrE)
a cross with four equal arms, each ending in a small crossbar, often used as a Christian symbol

Examples

A Jerusalem cross pendant
He wore a pendant shaped like a Jerusalem cross around his neck.
The Jerusalem cross is often used in heraldry and Christian iconography.
The church was decorated with a large Jerusalem cross above the altar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross potent - a cross with crossbars at the end of each arm
