unburn

verb
to reverse burning
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɜrn / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɜːn / (BrE)

Examples

To unburn a document
Once a document is destroyed by fire, you cannot unburn it.
Scientists are searching for ways to unburn materials damaged by heat.
It is impossible to unburn a piece of wood once it is ash.
