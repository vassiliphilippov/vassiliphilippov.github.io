knuckleball

noun
baseball pitch with little spin
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌkəlˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈnʌk.əl.bɔːl / (BrE)
A baseball pitch thrown with little spin or speed, causing erratic movement.

Examples

A knuckleball pitch
Catching a knuckleball is challenging due to its unpredictable movement.
He became famous in the league for his impressive knuckleball.
The pitcher threw a knuckleball that danced across the plate, confusing the batter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curveball - a baseball pitch with a curved trajectory
  • antonym: fastball - a baseball pitch thrown at high speed
