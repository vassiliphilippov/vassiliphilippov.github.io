tobacco shop

noun
shop selling tobacco products
Pronunciation: / təˈbækoʊ ʃɑp / (AmE), / təˈbækəʊ ʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop where tobacco products such as cigars, cigarettes, pipes, and pipe tobacco are sold.

Examples

A tobacco shop sells cigars
During his walk, he stopped by the tobacco shop to pick up some cigarettes.
He bought a new pipe from the tobacco shop on Main Street.
The tobacco shop offers a wide selection of imported cigars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tobacconist - person who sells tobacco and related products
