Glassite

noun
Scottish Christian sect member
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsaɪt / (BrE)
a member of a Scottish Christian sect founded in the 18th century by John Glas

Examples

The Glassite sect
The Glassite community was known for its strict interpretation of Christian doctrine.
As a Glassite, he followed the teachings of John Glas closely.
Several churches in Scotland were established by Glassite congregations in the 18th century.
