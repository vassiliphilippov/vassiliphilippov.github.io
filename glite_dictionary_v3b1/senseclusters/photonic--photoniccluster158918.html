photonic

adjective
relating to photons
7.4
Pronunciation: / foʊˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / fəʊˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to photons or the use of light, especially in technology or scientific applications

Examples

Photonic circuits
Researchers are developing photonic circuits that can process data faster than traditional electronics.
The new photonic sensor detects changes in light with remarkable accuracy.
Advances in photonic technology are transforming telecommunications and medical imaging.

Related Terms
