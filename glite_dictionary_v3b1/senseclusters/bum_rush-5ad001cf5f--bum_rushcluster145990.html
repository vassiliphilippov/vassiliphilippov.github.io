bum rush

verb
to rush at or invade suddenly and forcefully
Pronunciation: / bʌm rʌʃ / (AmE), / bʌm rʌʃ / (BrE)
to attack, enter, or approach a place or person suddenly and forcefully, often as a crowd

Examples

To bum rush a door
Security stopped a group from bum rushing the entrance at the concert.
The crowd tried to bum rush the stage when the band started playing.
The protesters attempted to bum rush the city hall doors.
