Montessori

proper noun
educational method
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːntəˈsɔːri / (AmE), / ˌmɒntəˈsɔːri / (BrE)
a method of education for young children that emphasizes hands-on, independent learning and self-directed activity

Examples

The Montessori method
The preschool follows the Montessori approach, allowing children to choose their own activities.
Many parents appreciate the independence encouraged by Montessori education.
She trained as a Montessori teacher to help children learn through exploration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progressive education - an educational movement emphasizing student-centered learning
  • antonym: traditional education - a conventional teacher-centered approach to education
