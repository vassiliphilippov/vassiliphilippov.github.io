unicity

noun
uniqueness; singularity
Pronunciation: / juːˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / juːˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unique or one of a kind

Examples

Unicity of style
The unicity of her artistic style set her apart from other painters.
Mathematicians value the unicity of solutions in certain types of equations.
The unicity of the ancient artifact made it a prized museum piece.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commonness - the state of being common or ordinary
  • synonym: singularity - the state of being singular or unique
  • synonym: uniqueness - the quality of being the only one of its kind
