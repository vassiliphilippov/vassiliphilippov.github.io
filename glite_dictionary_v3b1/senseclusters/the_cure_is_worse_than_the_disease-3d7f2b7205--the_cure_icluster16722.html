the cure is worse than the disease

x
when a solution makes things worse
Pronunciation: / ðə ˈkjʊɹ ɪz wɝs ðæn ðə dɪˈziz / (AmE), / ðə ˈkjʊə ɪz wɜːs ðən ðə dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
used to say that the attempt to solve a problem has made the situation worse than the original problem itself
