Constance

proper noun
a lake on the borders of Germany, Austria, and Switzerland
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.stəns / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.stəns / (BrE)
a large lake in south-central Europe, situated on the borders of Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, and forming part of the Rhine River; also known as Lake Constance.

Examples

Lake Constance
Many tourists visit Constance each year to enjoy its picturesque setting and clear waters.
Sailing is a popular activity on Constance, especially during the warm summer months.
The Rhine River enters Constance from the southeast and exits at the northwest end of the lake.
