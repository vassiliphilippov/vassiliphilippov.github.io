nonacademic

noun
staff not teaching
5.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.æk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.æk.ə.ˈdem.ɪk/ (BrE)
a person who works at a school, college, or university in a role that does not involve teaching or academic research

Examples

A nonacademic staff member
The university employs many nonacademics to handle administrative and support tasks.
As a nonacademic, she manages the student housing office rather than teaching classes.
Many nonacademics play a vital role in keeping the campus running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for managing the operations of an organization
