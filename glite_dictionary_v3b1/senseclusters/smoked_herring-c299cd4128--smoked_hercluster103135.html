smoked herring

noun
dried and smoked herring
Pronunciation: / smoʊkt ˈhɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / sməʊkt ˈhɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a herring that has been dried and smoked, typically giving it a reddish color

Examples

A piece of smoked herring
For breakfast, she enjoyed a piece of smoked herring with toast and eggs.
The fisherman sold several freshly prepared smoked herring at the market.
Traditional recipes often include smoked herring to add rich flavor to the dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kipper - a herring that has been split, salted, and smoked
