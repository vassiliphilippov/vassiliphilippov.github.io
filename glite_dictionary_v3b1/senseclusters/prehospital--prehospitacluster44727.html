prehospital

adjective
before hospital arrival
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈhɑːspɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈhɒspɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to medical care or procedures provided before a patient arrives at a hospital

Examples

Prehospital care team
Paramedics provided prehospital care to stabilize the patient before reaching the emergency room.
Effective prehospital treatment can greatly improve survival rates in trauma cases.
The new protocol focuses on improving prehospital response times for heart attack victims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emergency - a serious, unexpected situation requiring immediate action
  • antonym: inpatient - relating to care given after hospital admission
