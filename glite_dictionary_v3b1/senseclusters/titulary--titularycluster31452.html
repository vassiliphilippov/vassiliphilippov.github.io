titulary

adjective
relating to legal or formal title
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtʃəˌleri / (AmE), / ˈtɪtjʊləri / (BrE)
relating to or designating a legal title to something, especially a formal or official title.

Examples

A titulary position
He held only a titulary position, without any real power.
In medieval society, titulary claims often led to conflict between nobles.
The dispute centered on titulary rights to the estate.
