typewriting

noun
writing done using a typewriter
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of writing using a typewriter

Examples

The art of typewriting
Before computers, most office documents were produced by typewriting.
He completed the manuscript through painstaking typewriting over several weeks.
Her excellent typewriting made her an invaluable secretary in the 1950s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: typing - the act of writing by means of a keyboard; now usually refers to computer keyboards
