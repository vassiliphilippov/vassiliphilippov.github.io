townhome

noun
attached row house
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊnˌhoʊm / (AmE), / ˈtaʊnˌhəʊm /; / ˈtaʊnhəʊm / (BrE)
a house that shares one or more walls with neighboring houses, usually in a row, but is individually owned and has its own entrance

Examples

Each townhome in the complex has its own small backyard and garage.
The couple preferred a townhome over an apartment because of the extra space and privacy.
They decided to buy a townhome because it offered both privacy and access to shared amenities.
The new townhome development includes a swimming pool and landscaped gardens for all residents.
Each townhome has its own garage and a small backyard.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apartment - a set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a building
  • antonym: detached house - a house that stands alone and does not share walls with another
  • synonym: townhouse - a house in a row of similar houses joined by shared walls
