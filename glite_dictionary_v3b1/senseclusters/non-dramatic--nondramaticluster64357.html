non-dramatic

adjective
not theatrical
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn drəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn drəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not related to drama or theatrical performance; not intended as a play or stage production

Examples

Non-dramatic performance
The documentary took a non-dramatic approach, focusing on facts rather than emotional storytelling.
Her writing style is intentionally non-dramatic, avoiding suspense or theatrical elements.
Unlike his earlier works, this novel is completely non-dramatic and reads more like a memoir.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dramatic - relating to drama or performance
  • antonym: theatrical - relating to drama or the theater
