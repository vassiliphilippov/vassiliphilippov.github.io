nickelodeon

noun
coin-operated music-playing machine
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪkəˈloʊdiən / (AmE), / ˌnɪkəˈləʊdiən / (BrE)
a coin-operated cabinet with an automatic record player, typically found in public places, where records are played upon inserting a coin

Examples

Old nickelodeon machine
He found a vintage nickelodeon at the antique store and restored it to working condition.
People would gather around the nickelodeon and pick their favorite records to play for a nickel.
The old bar down the street still has a nickelodeon in the corner, playing classic tunes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jukebox - a machine that plays music when a coin is inserted; popular in the mid-20th century
