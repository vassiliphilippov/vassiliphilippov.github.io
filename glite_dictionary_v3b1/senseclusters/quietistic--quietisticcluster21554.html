quietistic

adjective
calmly passive
9.9
Pronunciation: / kwaɪˈətɪstɪk / (AmE), / kwaɪˈətɪstɪk / (BrE)
characterized by a calm, passive, or non-interventionist attitude, especially in spiritual or philosophical matters

Examples

Quietistic attitude
His quietistic attitude made him avoid arguments and seek inner peace.
The philosopher's quietistic beliefs emphasized acceptance over action.
She adopted a quietistic approach, choosing not to intervene in the dispute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - taking action rather than being passive
  • synonym: passive - accepting or allowing what happens without active response
