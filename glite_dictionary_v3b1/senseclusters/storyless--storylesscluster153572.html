storyless

adjective
without a narrative
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔːriləs / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriləs / (BrE)
lacking a story or narrative element

Examples

Storyless movie
The director's latest film was beautifully shot but ultimately storyless and hard to follow.
Some modern art installations are intentionally storyless, focusing on visual impact rather than narrative.
Critics argued that the novel was too storyless to keep readers engaged.
