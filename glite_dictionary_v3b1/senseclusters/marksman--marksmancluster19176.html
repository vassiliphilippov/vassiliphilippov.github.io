marksman

noun
video-game ranged damage dealer
5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːrks.mən/ (AmE), /ˈmɑːks.mən/ (BrE)
(in video games) a playable character or player role that specializes in long-range, precise attacks and high sustained physical damage, usually relying on basic attacks and positioning.

Examples

Marksman character
The team relied on its marksman to pick off enemies from a safe distance.
In the tournament, skilled marksmen focused on farming resources before major team fights.
When camping the objective, the marksman stayed hidden and provided steady damage from afar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sharpshooter - a person skilled in accurate shooting; similar in meaning though not specific to games
  • synonym: sniper - a shooter who engages targets at long range; related concept in both real life and games
