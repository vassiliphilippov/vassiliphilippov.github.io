picturesqueness

noun
quality of being visually attractive or vivid
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛsknəs / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃəˈrɛsknɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being visually attractive or charming, especially in a way that looks like a picture
