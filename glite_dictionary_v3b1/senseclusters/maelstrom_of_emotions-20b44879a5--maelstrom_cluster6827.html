maelstrom of emotions

noun
state of emotional chaos or turmoil
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪlstrəm əv ɪˈmoʊʃənz / (AmE), / ˈmeɪlstrəm ɒv ɪˈməʊʃənz / (BrE)
A chaotic or turbulent state of emotional upheaval, often involving intense and conflicting feelings.
