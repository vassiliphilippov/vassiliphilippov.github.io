sweet-natured

adjective
kind and gentle in temperament
Pronunciation: / ˈswitˌneɪtʃərd / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌneɪtʃəd / (BrE)
Having a kind, gentle, and pleasant disposition.

Examples

Sweet-natured person
Despite her sweet-natured manner, she stood firm when needed.
Everyone loved his sweet-natured humor and cheerful greetings.
The sweet-natured child comforted her friend when he was sad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good-natured - having a pleasant and cooperative disposition
  • antonym: ill-natured - having an unpleasant or unkind disposition
