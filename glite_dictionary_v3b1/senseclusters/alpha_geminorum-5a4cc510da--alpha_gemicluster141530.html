Alpha Geminorum

proper noun
bright multiple star in Gemini (Castor)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.fə dʒem.ɪˈnɔːrəm/ /; / /ˈæl.fə dʒem.ɪˈnɑːrəm/ (AmE), /ˈæl.fə dʒem.ɪˈnɔː.rəm/ (BrE)
the second brightest star in the constellation Gemini, a multiple star system with six components, also known as Castor

Examples

Alpha Geminorum star system
Astronomers have studied Alpha Geminorum to better understand multiple star systems.
