cycloramic

noun
circular panorama
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkləˈræmɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkləˈræmɪk / (BrE)
a circular panoramic image or display, such as a painting or photograph that surrounds the viewer

Examples

A cycloramic painting
The museum's new cycloramic gave visitors a 360-degree view of the historic battlefield.
Artists worked for months to complete the massive cycloramic that encircled the exhibition hall.
Standing in the center of the cycloramic, she felt completely immersed in the painted landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cyclorama - a large circular painting or display surrounding the viewer
  • synonym: panorama - a wide, unbroken view or representation of a scene
