jelled

adjective
set into a jelly-like form
Pronunciation: / dʒɛld / (AmE), / dʒɛld / (BrE)
(of a substance) congealed or set into a jelly-like form; solidified by cooling.

Examples

Jelled into jelly
After several hours, the mixture jelled into a firm dessert.
She checked if the jam had fully jelled before pouring it into jars.
The sauce had jelled in the refrigerator overnight, making it easy to slice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congealed - having become semi-solid, especially by cooling
