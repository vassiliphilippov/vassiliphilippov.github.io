takeaway

noun
main point learned
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈteɪkəweɪ / (BrE)
an important point, lesson, or piece of information to remember from a discussion, event, or experience

Examples

Main takeaway
The biggest takeaway from the conference was the need for better teamwork.
After reading the article, my main takeaway was to always double-check the facts.
Each student shared a personal takeaway from the group project at the end of the semester.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conclusion - a judgment or decision reached after consideration
  • antonym: detail - a small part of something; not the main point
  • synonym: lesson - something learned from an experience or event
