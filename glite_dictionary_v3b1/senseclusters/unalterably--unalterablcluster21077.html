unalterably

adverb
in an unchangeable manner
9.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɔltɚəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːltərəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that cannot be changed or altered.

Examples

To be unalterably fixed
He remained unalterably committed to his principles, despite all opposition.
Her decision was unalterably opposed to the proposal.
The rules were set unalterably by the founding committee.
