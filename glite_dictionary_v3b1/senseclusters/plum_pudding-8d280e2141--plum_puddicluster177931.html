plum pudding

noun
rich steamed or boiled fruit dessert
3.7
Pronunciation: / plʌm ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / plʌm ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a rich dessert made by steaming or boiling a mixture of dried fruit, suet, flour, spices, and sometimes alcohol, traditionally served at Christmas in Britain

Examples

Traditional plum pudding
Every Christmas, my grandmother makes a traditional plum pudding for the family.
Many consider plum pudding the highlight of a classic British holiday meal.
The chef flamed the plum pudding with brandy before serving it.
