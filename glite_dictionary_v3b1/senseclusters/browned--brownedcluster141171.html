browned

adjective
deeply suntanned (skin)
4.1 2.8
Pronunciation: / braʊnd / (AmE), / braʊnd / (BrE)
(of skin) deeply suntanned; darkened in color from exposure to the sun.

Examples

She admired the browned arms she had gotten from hours at the beach.
The mountaineer’s browned skin testified to his days in the high altitudes.
