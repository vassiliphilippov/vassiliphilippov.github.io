commish

verb
to hire for art
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪʃ / (AmE), / kəˈmɪʃ / (BrE)
to officially hire or request someone, especially an artist or writer, to create something (informal)

Examples

To commish an artist
They often commish local artists to paint murals for the annual festival.
If you want a custom portrait, you can commish her through her website.
Many fans commish their favorite illustrators to create unique artwork.
