unbounded two-dimensional surface (math)

Pronunciation: / pleɪn / (AmE), / pleɪn / (BrE)
(mathematics) An unbounded flat two-dimensional surface extending infinitely in all directions.

Examples

A flat plane
Any point on a plane can be described using two coordinates.

The intersection of two planes in three-dimensional space is a line.
