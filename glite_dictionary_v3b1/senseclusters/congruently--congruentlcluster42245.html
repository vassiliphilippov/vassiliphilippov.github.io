congruently

adverb
in equal shape
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːəntli / (AmE), / kənˈɡruːəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows exact equality in size and shape, especially in geometry

Examples

Congruently matching shapes
The two shapes fit together congruently after being rotated on the plane.
In mathematics, figures that overlap congruently have the same size and shape.
The engineer checked if the parts aligned congruently before assembling the machine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differently - in a way that is not the same
  • synonym: identically - in exactly the same way or form
