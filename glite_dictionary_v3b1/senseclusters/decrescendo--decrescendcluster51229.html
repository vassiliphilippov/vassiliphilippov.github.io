decrescendo

verb
grow gradually quieter (music, verb)

Pronunciation: /diː.krə.ˈʃen.doʊ/ /; / /də.krə.ˈʃen.doʊ/ (AmE), /diː.krə.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ /; / /də.krə.ˈʃen.dəʊ/ (BrE)

To decrescendo slowly

As the storm passed, the thunder decrescendoed into gentle rumbles.
The choir decrescendoed beautifully at the end of the hymn.
