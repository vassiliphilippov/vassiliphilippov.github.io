ab

noun
abdominal muscle (informal)
Pronunciation: / æb / (AmE), / æb / (BrE)
an abdominal muscle, especially as developed or prominent in physically fit individuals; usually used in the plural

Examples

Strong abs muscles
He's been working out every day to get defined abs.
Her abs are so strong she can hold a plank for minutes.
You need to engage your ab muscles when doing sit-ups.
