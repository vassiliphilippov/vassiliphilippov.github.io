Ebionitism

noun
early Christian sect
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛbiəˌnaɪtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈiːbɪəˌnaɪtɪzəm / (BrE)
the beliefs and teachings of the early Christian group called the Ebionites, who saw Jesus as a human and followed Jewish law
