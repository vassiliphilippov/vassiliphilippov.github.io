flusher

noun
pipe-cleaning machine
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌʃ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈflʌʃ.ə/ (BrE)
a machine or tool that uses water, air, or another fluid under pressure to clean or clear pipes or systems

Examples

Pipe flusher
The maintenance team brought a flusher to remove debris from the storm drains.
After the heavy rain, a flusher was used to clear the clogged pipes quickly.
City workers operated the flusher to ensure the sewer system stayed clean and functional.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blockage - an obstruction that blocks a pipe or passage
  • synonym: jetter - a machine that uses high-pressure water jets to clean pipes
