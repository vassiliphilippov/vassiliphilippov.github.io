earthily

adverb
in a soil-like way
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθɪli / (AmE), / ˈɜːθɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that suggests the smell, taste, or qualities of earth or soil

Examples

Tastes earthily rich
The soup tasted earthily of root vegetables and fresh herbs.
After the rain, the garden smelled earthily and refreshing.
The mushrooms added an earthily rich flavor to the dish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificially - in a way that is not natural; made by humans
  • synonym: naturally - in a way that relates to nature or the natural world
