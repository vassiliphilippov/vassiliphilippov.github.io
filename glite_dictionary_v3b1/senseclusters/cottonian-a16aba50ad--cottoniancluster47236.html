Cottonian

adjective
of Sir Robert Cotton
Pronunciation: / kəˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / kɒˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to Sir Robert Cotton or to his renowned collection of manuscripts

Examples

Cottonian manuscripts
The Cottonian library contains some of the most valuable medieval manuscripts in England.
Scholars often refer to the Cottonian collection when studying early British history.
A rare Cottonian document was recently displayed at the national museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manuscript - a handwritten or typed document, especially one of historical value
