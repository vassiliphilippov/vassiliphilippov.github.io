yadda

interjection
and so on
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjædə / (AmE), / ˈjædə / (BrE)
an interjection used to indicate omitted, obvious, or repetitive material, similar to 'and so on' or 'etc.'

Examples

Yadda yadda yadda
He started listing all the rules, but after a while just said yadda to skip the rest.
The teacher explained the process—first you gather materials, then you mix them, yadda.
She told me about her busy day: meetings, emails, yadda.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blah - an interjection for boring or repetitive speech
  • synonym: etcetera - used to indicate additional, similar items not mentioned
