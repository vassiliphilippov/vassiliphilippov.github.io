mandibular

adjective
of the lower jaw or jawbone
8.2
Pronunciation: / mænˈdɪbjələr / (AmE), / mænˈdɪbjʊlə / (BrE)
Relating to the lower jaw or jawbone, especially in vertebrates.

Examples

Mandibular fracture
A mandibular fracture requires immediate medical attention to prevent complications.
Some snakes have a highly flexible mandibular structure for swallowing large prey.
The dentist examined the patient’s mandibular arch for signs of decay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maxillary - relating to the upper jaw
