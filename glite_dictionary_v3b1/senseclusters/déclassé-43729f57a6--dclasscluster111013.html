déclassé

noun
person of lower status
Pronunciation: / deɪˈklɑːseɪ / (AmE), / deɪˈklɑːseɪ / (BrE)
a person who has lost social status or is considered socially inferior or unfashionable

Examples

A social declasse
After the scandal, he became a déclassé in his former social circle.
The novel tells the story of a déclassé struggling to regain respect in society.
She felt like a déclassé at the exclusive party, ignored by the other guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or excluded from society
  • synonym: pariah - a person who is despised or avoided by others
