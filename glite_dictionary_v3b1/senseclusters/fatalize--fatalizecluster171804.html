fatalize

verb
to see as inevitable
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to regard or represent something as being determined by fate and therefore inevitable

Examples

To fatalize events
Some philosophers fatalize all events as inevitable and beyond human control.
It is unhelpful to fatalize every setback instead of looking for solutions.
Writers sometimes fatalize their characters' destinies to create a sense of inevitability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determine - to decide or control the outcome of something
  • synonym: predestine - to determine in advance by fate or destiny
  • antonym: randomize - to make something happen by chance rather than by fate
