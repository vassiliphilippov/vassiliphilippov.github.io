fleshling

noun
very young animal
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛʃlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛʃlɪŋ / (BrE)
a newly born or very young animal or creature, especially one that is small and weak.

Examples

A newborn fleshling
The farmer found a weak fleshling beside its mother in the barn at dawn.
Predators often target a fleshling because it cannot defend itself yet.
The mother animal stayed close to her fleshling to keep it safe and warm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - a fully grown animal or person
  • synonym: newborn - a recently born animal or human
