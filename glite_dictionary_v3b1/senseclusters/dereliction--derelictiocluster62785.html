Synonyms

  • neglect of duty
    5.1 0.05/m
    neglect of duty is failing to carry out job responsibilities in a professional role, while dereliction suggests a serious, blameworthy failure.
  • nonfeasance
    9.9 0.04/m
    nonfeasance is a legal term for not acting when required, while dereliction covers any failure to meet duties.
  • breach of duty
    6.5 0.09/m
    breach of duty is breaking a legal or moral obligation, while dereliction focuses on failing to do what you should.
  • negligence
    6.5 4.32/m
    negligence is careless action or lack of care that often causes harm, while dereliction is a failure to perform duties.
