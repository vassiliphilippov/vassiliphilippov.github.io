trifurcation

noun
division into three branches
Pronunciation: / ˌtraɪfərˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtraɪfəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of dividing or splitting into three branches or parts

Examples

A trifurcation of roads
A sudden trifurcation in the path left us unsure which trail to follow.
The river underwent a trifurcation just before it reached the delta.
Vascular surgeons carefully studied the trifurcation of the main artery.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bifurcation - the act of dividing into two branches or parts
