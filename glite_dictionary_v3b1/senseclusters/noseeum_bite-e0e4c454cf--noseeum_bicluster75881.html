noseeum bite

noun
bite from a tiny biting insect
Pronunciation: / noʊˈsiː.əm baɪt / (AmE), / nəʊˈsiː.əm baɪt / (BrE)
A bite caused by a very small biting insect, commonly known as a no-see-um, which often results in skin irritation or itching.

Examples

Noseeum bites on skin
After walking through the marsh, Sarah discovered several noseeum bites on her ankles.
He applied lotion to soothe the noseeum bites he got while camping.
The itching from a noseeum bite can be worse than a mosquito's.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ant bite - a bite from an ant
  • synonym: mosquito bite - a bite from a mosquito
