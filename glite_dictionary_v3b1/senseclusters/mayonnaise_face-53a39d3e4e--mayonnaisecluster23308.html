mayonnaise face

noun
person with a pale or unhealthy-looking face
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.oʊˌneɪz feɪs / (AmE), / ˈmæɪ.ə.neɪz feɪs / (BrE)
A slang or humorous term describing someone with a pale or unhealthy complexion, likened to the color of mayonnaise.

Examples

Mayonnaise face joke
He was tired of his friends calling him a mayonnaise face every time he skipped the beach.
In the harsh fluorescent light, everyone looked like a mayonnaise face.
People joked that after the long winter indoors, she turned into a mayonnaise face.
