drag

verb
to inhale (air or smoke), forcefully
1.0
Pronunciation: / dræɡ / (AmE), / dræɡ / (BrE)
to inhale or draw in (air, smoke, etc.), especially forcefully or as with a cigarette

Examples

She paused to drag deeply on her cigarette before answering the question.
