missionize

verb
to do missionary work
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃənaɪz / (BrE)
to carry out missionary work or attempt to convert people to a religion

Examples

To missionize a village
The organization sent volunteers to missionize in several rural communities.
She felt called to missionize after learning about the needs in distant countries.
Many groups have traveled abroad to missionize and offer humanitarian aid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convert - to persuade someone to change their religious beliefs
  • synonym: evangelize - to preach or spread a religious faith
