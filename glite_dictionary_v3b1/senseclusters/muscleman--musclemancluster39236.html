muscleman

noun
man hired as a thug or bodyguard
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌman / (BrE)
A man whose strength is used to intimidate or protect others, often employed as a thug or bodyguard.

Examples

Mob muscleman
Rumor has it the mob boss always travels with a muscleman beside him.
Seeing the muscleman at the door, the reporters kept their distance.
The club owner hired a muscleman to keep troublesome patrons in check.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodyguard - a person employed to protect someone
  • synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
