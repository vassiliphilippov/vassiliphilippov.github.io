Tucanae

noun
Latin genitive form
9.9
Pronunciation: / tuːˈkɑːniː / (AmE), / tjuːˈkɑːniː / (BrE)
the Latin genitive form used in scientific names to mean 'of or from Tucana', indicating origin or association with Tucana

Examples

Species Tucanae
The species name Tucanae was chosen to indicate the bird's origin in the Tucana region.
Researchers described a new butterfly as Papilio Tucanae after finding it in South America.
In taxonomy, Tucanae is used to show that a species comes from Tucana.
