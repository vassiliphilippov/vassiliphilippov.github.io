funky

adjective
stylish in an unconventional way
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋki / (BrE)
stylish and modern in an unconventional or striking way, often with a sense of originality or retro appeal

Examples

Funky clothes
He always wears funky glasses that make him stand out in a crowd.
Her apartment is decorated with funky lamps and colorful artwork.
The new café has a funky interior design that attracts lots of young people.
