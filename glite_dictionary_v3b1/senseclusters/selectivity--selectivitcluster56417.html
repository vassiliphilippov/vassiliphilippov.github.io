selectivity

noun
the quality of being selective
1.0 5.4
Pronunciation: /sɪ.lek.ˈtɪv.ə.tiː/ /; / /sə.lek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ (AmE), /sə.lek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ /; / /ɪ.lek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ (BrE)

Examples
