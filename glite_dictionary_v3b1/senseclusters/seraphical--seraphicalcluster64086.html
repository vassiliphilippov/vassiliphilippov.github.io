seraphical

adjective
relating to a seraph; angelic
Pronunciation: / səˈræfɪkəl / (AmE), / səˈræfɪkəl / (BrE)
Relating to a seraph or to angelic purity and beauty; angelic, especially in the highest, most exalted sense.

Examples

Seraphical smile
He regarded her with a seraphical smile, full of kindness and warmth.
Her seraphical singing brought comfort to everyone in the chapel.
The painting glowed with a seraphical light that seemed almost otherworldly.
