crabeater

noun
crab catcher
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbˌiːtər / (AmE), / ˈkræbˌiːtə / (BrE)
a person who catches or collects crabs, especially for food or sale

Examples

Professional crabeater
The experienced crabeater returned to shore with a full basket every morning.
As a young crabeater, she learned the best spots to set her traps from her grandfather.
Many coastal communities rely on the work of the local crabeater for fresh seafood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisherman - a person who catches fish for a living or as a hobby
  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals for food or sport
