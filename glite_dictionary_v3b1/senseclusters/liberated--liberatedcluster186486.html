liberated

adjective
released from chemical combination
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪbəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a gas or substance) released from chemical combination by a chemical reaction.

Examples

Liberated gas
Carbon dioxide is liberated when an acid reacts with baking soda.
The reaction caused oxygen gas to be liberated from the compound.
When heated, ammonia is liberated from ammonium salts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: released - set free or allowed to escape
