The noun, noun phrase, or pronoun in a sentence that typically indicates what the sentence is about and performs the action or is described.

Examples

The grammatical subject
Even in the passive voice, the grammatical subject may be acted upon rather than performing the action.
In the sentence 'Birds sing at dawn,' 'Birds' is the grammatical subject.
To find the grammatical subject, look for the noun or pronoun that the verb agrees with.
