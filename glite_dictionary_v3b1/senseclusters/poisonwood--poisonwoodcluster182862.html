poisonwood

noun
toxic tropical tree
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzənˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzənˌwʊd / (BrE)
a tropical tree with toxic sap that can cause skin irritation

Examples

Poisonwood tree
The poisonwood tree is native to Florida and the Caribbean islands.
Hikers are warned to avoid touching poisonwood because its sap can cause severe rashes.
Researchers studied the effects of poisonwood exposure on local wildlife.
Hikers were warned to avoid touching the poisonwood along the trail.
The sap of the poisonwood can cause a painful rash if it gets on your skin.
Local guides pointed out the poisonwood so tourists would not accidentally brush against it.

  • synonym: poison ivy - a North American plant causing skin irritation
  • synonym: sumac - a plant of the cashew family, some species of which are toxic
