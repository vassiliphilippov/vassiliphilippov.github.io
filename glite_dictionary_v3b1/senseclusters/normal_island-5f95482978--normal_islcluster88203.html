Normal Island

noun
metaphorical place of ordinary or mundane life
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrməl ˈaɪ.lənd / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.məl ˈaɪ.lənd / (BrE)
a fictional or metaphorical place representing normalcy, ordinary behavior, or mundane living, often used humorously or ironically

Examples

Normal Island life
After years of chaos, she finally settled on Normal Island and embraced a simple routine.
He often jokes that his vacation was a ticket to Normal Island, far from any excitement.
Some people mock those who live on Normal Island, but others find comfort in the ordinary.
