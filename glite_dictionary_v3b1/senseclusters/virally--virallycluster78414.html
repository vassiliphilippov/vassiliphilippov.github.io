virally

adverb
by viral infection
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪrəli / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəli / (BrE)
in a way that is caused by, involves, or is related to a virus or viral infection

Examples

Infect virally
The disease spread virally among the population, leading to a rapid increase in cases.
Doctors confirmed that the symptoms were transmitted virally rather than through bacteria.
Some illnesses can be passed virally even before symptoms appear in the infected person.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bacterially - in a way caused by or involving bacteria
