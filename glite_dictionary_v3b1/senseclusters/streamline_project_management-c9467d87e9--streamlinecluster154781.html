streamline project management

verb
make project management more efficient
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstrimˌlaɪn ˈprɑːdʒɛkt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈstriːmlaɪn ˈprɒdʒɛkt ˈmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of managing projects, often by simplifying processes or using better tools

Examples
