filmic

adjective
thin film-like layer
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmɪk / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmɪk / (BrE)
forming or consisting of a thin film or layer, especially describing a coating or surface in scientific or technical contexts

Examples

Filmic layer
A filmic layer formed on the glass after the chemical reaction.
The scientist observed a filmic residue covering the surface of the solution.
After drying, the paint left a filmic coating that protected the metal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coated - covered with a layer of something
  • antonym: thick - having a large distance between opposite surfaces
