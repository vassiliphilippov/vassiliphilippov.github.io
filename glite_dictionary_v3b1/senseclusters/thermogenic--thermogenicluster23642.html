thermogenic

adjective
produced by heat
7.5
Pronunciation: /θɜːr.mə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /θɜːr.moʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (AmE), /θɜː.mə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /θɜː.məʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (BrE)
formed or produced by heat, especially referring to substances created through thermal processes such as decomposition or transformation.

Examples

Thermogenic gases
The thermogenic gases found in the rock samples suggest a history of intense underground heat.
Scientists study thermogenic processes to understand how oil and gas are formed deep within the Earth.
Some dietary supplements claim to have thermogenic effects that increase the body's heat production.
