Helmut Schmidt

proper noun
German chancellor (1974–1982)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlmuːt ˈʃmɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlmʊt ˈʃmɪt / (BrE)
(1918–2015) German statesman who served as chancellor of West Germany from 1974 to 1982.
