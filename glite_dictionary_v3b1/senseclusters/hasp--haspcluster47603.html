hasp

noun
hinged metal fastener for doors or lids
9.2
Pronunciation: / hæsp / (AmE), / hæsp / (BrE)
A fastening device consisting of a hinged metal plate that fits over a staple and is secured with a pin or padlock, used to secure doors or lids.

Examples

Metal hasp lock
Before installing the hasp, make sure both parts are properly aligned.
He replaced the rusty hasp on the old chest to keep its contents safe.
The shed door was secured with a sturdy hasp and padlock.
