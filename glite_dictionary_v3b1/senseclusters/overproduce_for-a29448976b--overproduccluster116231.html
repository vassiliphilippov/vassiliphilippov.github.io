overproduce for

verb
produce more than needed for a specific purpose
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərprəˈdus fɔr / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprəˈdjuːs fə / (BrE)
to produce more of something than is needed for a particular occasion, demand, or purpose

Examples

The company did not want to overproduce for the summer sale and risk excess inventory.
