grumble about

verb
complain about something in a discontented way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌm.bəl əˌbaʊt / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌm.bəl əˌbaʊt / (BrE)
to complain or express dissatisfaction about something, often in a low or muttering voice

Examples

To grumble about work
He tends to grumble about his workload whenever deadlines approach.
She often grumbles about the food in the cafeteria but never brings her own lunch.
There's no use in grumbling about the situation; we need to find a solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance about something
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for something
