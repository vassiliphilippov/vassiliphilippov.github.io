fisher

noun
person who catches fish for a living
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is to catch fish, especially for a living

Examples

A skilled fisher
As a skilled fisher, he knows the best spots along the river.
Local fishers depend on the lake for their livelihoods.
The government introduced new regulations to protect fishers and marine life.
