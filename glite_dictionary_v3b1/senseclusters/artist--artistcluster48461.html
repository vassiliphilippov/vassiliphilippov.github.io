artist

noun
a creator of imaginative or sensitive works of art
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates works of art, especially one whose work demonstrates imagination and sensitivity

Examples

A artist creates art
As a renowned artist, he was invited to exhibit his sculptures in Paris.
Every artist has a unique vision that shapes their work.
The artist spent years perfecting her technique in watercolor painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artisan - a skilled craftsperson or manual worker
  • synonym: painter - a person who paints artworks
  • synonym: sculptor - a person who creates sculptures
