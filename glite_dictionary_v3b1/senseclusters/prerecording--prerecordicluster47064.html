prerecording

noun
advance recording
4.0
Pronunciation: /priː.rɪ.ˈkɔːr.dɪŋ/ /; / /priː.rɪ.ˈkɑːr.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /priː.rɪ.ˈkɔː.dɪŋ/ /; / /prɪ.rɪ.ˈkɔː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
an audio or video recording made in advance, or the act of making such a recording before it is broadcast or used

Examples

A prerecording of concert
The radio station played a prerecording of the concert during the evening show.
Because of scheduling conflicts, the interview was a prerecording rather than a live segment.
They used a prerecording of the speech for the awards ceremony broadcast.
The prerecording of the interview allowed producers to edit out mistakes before airing.
Because of technical issues, the concert was a prerecording rather than a live performance.
She preferred a prerecording so she could review her speech before it was shown.

Related Terms

  • antonym: live broadcast - a program transmitted in real time as it happens
  • synonym: recording - an audio or video file that has been captured and stored
