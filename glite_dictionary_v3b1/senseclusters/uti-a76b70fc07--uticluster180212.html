UTI

noun
urinary tract infection
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː tiː ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌjuː tiː ˈaɪ / (BrE)
an infection in any part of the urinary tract, such as the bladder or urethra

Examples

A UTI infection
The doctor prescribed antibiotics to treat her UTI quickly.
Drinking plenty of water can help prevent a UTI from developing.
He missed work for two days because of a painful UTI.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cystitis - inflammation of the bladder, often due to infection
  • synonym: urinary tract infection - an infection affecting the urinary system
