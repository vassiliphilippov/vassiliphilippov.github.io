Synonyms

  • present
    1.0 40/m
    present describes something existing or happening right now in a place, while current is a more general term for now.
  • contemporary
    4.2 10/m
    contemporary refers to things from the same time or modern life, while current simply means happening now.
  • present-day
    3.2 2/m
    present-day describes features or situations of today's era, while current focuses on what is happening at this exact time.
  • modern-day
    3.0 4/m
    modern-day is used to talk about things typical of our modern era, while current means happening at this moment.
  • up-to-the-minute
    4.4 0.5/m
    up-to-the-minute gives a sense of the very latest news or updates, while current means simply happening now.
