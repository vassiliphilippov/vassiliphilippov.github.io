Sharpie

noun
permanent marker pen (brand)
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
a brand of permanent marker pen with indelible ink that can write on most surfaces

Examples

A black Sharpie

The teacher used a black Sharpie to write on the poster board.
