unlisted

adjective
not publicly visible online; accessible by link
4.1
Pronunciation: / ənˈlɪstɪd / (AmE), / ənˈlɪstɪd / (BrE)
(on digital platforms) not made publicly visible or included in search results or public listings; accessible only to people with a direct link or with specific permission.

Examples
