loganberry

noun
hybrid red bramble fruit
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ɡənˌbɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ɡənˌbɛr.i / (BrE)
a large, tangy red or reddish-purple berry that is a hybrid between a raspberry and a blackberry
