Mordecai

proper noun
male biblical name
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrdəˌkaɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːdəkaɪ / (BrE)
a male given name, especially known as the cousin and guardian of Esther in the biblical Book of Esther

Examples

Biblical Mordecai
In the Book of Esther, Mordecai played a key role in saving the Jewish people.
Many parents choose the name Mordecai for its strong biblical association.
The story of Mordecai is often read during the Jewish festival of Purim.
