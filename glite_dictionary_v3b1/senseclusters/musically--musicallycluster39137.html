musically

adverb
in a musical manner
4.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmjuː.zɪ.kliː/ /; / /ˈmjuː.zɪ.kə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈmjuː.zɪ.kliː/ /; / /ˈmjuː.zɪ.kə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
