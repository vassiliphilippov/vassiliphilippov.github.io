loudness

noun
intensity of sound
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊdnəs / (AmE), / ˈlaʊdnəs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of being loud; the magnitude or intensity of sound, especially as perceived by the human ear

Examples

The loudness level
Scientists measure the loudness of sounds in units called decibels.
She turned down the loudness on her headphones to protect her ears.
The loudness of the thunder startled everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quietness - the quality of making little or no noise
  • synonym: volume - the degree of loudness or the intensity of a sound
