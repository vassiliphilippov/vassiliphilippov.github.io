loudness

noun
intensity of sound
1.1 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈlaʊd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈlaʊd.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or degree of being loud; the magnitude or intensity of sound, especially as perceived by the human ear

Examples
