relationship

noun
connection or dealings between people or groups
1.0 1.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ /; / /riː.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
the way in which people, groups, or countries are connected or interact with each other

Examples

Work relationship
Her relationship with her colleagues is built on trust and respect.
Negotiations improved the relationship between the rival political parties.
The two countries have established a strong relationship based on trade and cultural exchange.
After the argument, there was tension in their relationship for several weeks.
She maintained a close relationship with her grandparents throughout her life.
Their professional relationship developed into a lasting friendship over the years.

  • synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else
  • synonym: relation - a connection between people or things
  • antonym: separation - the state of being apart or disconnected
