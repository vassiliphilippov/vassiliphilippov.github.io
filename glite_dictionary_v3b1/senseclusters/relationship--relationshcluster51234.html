Synonyms

  • relation
    2.8 80/m
    relation names a direct link between people, groups, or things, while relationship describes their longer or ongoing interactions.
  • human relationship
    2.7 1/m
    human relationship refers to the emotional or social bond between people, while relationship can mean any connection among people, groups, or countries.
  • interrelationship
    5.9 0.7/m
    interrelationship shows mutual influence between things or people, while relationship is a general connection that may not be mutual.
  • connection
    1.9 70/m
    connection is a link or tie between things or people, while relationship emphasizes ongoing interaction and social or political ties.
  • tie
    2.4 30/m
    tie suggests a bond often emotional or practical, while relationship covers the full pattern of interactions between people or groups.
  • association
    3.0 17/m
    association can mean an organization or a loose link between people, while relationship usually means a direct, ongoing connection.
