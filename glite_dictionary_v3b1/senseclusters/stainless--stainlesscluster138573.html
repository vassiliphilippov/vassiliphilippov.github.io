stainless

adjective
free from moral blemish; untainted
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnləs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnləs / (BrE)
(of reputation or character) free from moral blemish or dishonor; pure and without fault.

Examples

Stainless reputation
Despite years in politics, her stainless reputation remained untouched by scandal.
The hero was admired for his stainless character and unwavering honesty.
They trusted him completely, believing his motives to be stainless.
