Acheron

proper noun
river of the underworld in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈækəˌrɒn /; / ˈækərˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈækərɒn / (BrE)
in Greek mythology, a river in the underworld (Hades) over which the souls of the dead were ferried by Charon

Examples

The poet described the Acheron as a boundary between the world of the living and the dead.
