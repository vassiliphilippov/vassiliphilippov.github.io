Acheron

proper noun
river of the underworld in Greek mythology
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈæk.ə.rən/ (AmE), /ˈæk.ə.rɒn/ /; / /ˈæk.ə.rən/ (BrE)
in Greek mythology, a river in the underworld (Hades) over which the souls of the dead were ferried by Charon
