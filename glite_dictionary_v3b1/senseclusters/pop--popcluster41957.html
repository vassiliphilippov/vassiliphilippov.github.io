pop

verb
bulge outward suddenly
1.0 6.31/m
Pronunciation: / pÉ‘p / (AmE), / pÉ’p / (BrE)
to burst, bulge, or cause something to burst or make a sudden, explosive sound

Examples

To pop a balloon
Bubbles can pop as they bulge under pressure.

The children laughed when they popped all the party balloons.

The kids were excited when the balloon popped loudly at the party.
