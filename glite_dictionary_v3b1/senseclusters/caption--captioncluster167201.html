caption

noun
subtitled translation on screen
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpʃən / (AmE), / ˈkæpʃən / (BrE)
Words shown on a film or television screen that translate or transcribe spoken dialogue, typically appearing at the bottom of the screen.

Examples

Movie captions
He turned on the captions to follow along with the rapid dialogue.
Some streaming services allow you to customize the appearance of captions for easier reading.
The foreign film included English captions so everyone could understand the story.

Related Terms
