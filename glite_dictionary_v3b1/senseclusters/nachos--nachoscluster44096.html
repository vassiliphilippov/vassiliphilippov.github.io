nachos

noun
plain tortilla chips
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːtʃoʊz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃəʊz / (BrE)
thin, crispy tortilla chips served plain as a snack, without any toppings such as cheese or salsa

Examples

Plain nachos snack
We shared a bowl of plain nachos while waiting for our meals at the restaurant.
He grabbed some nachos from the snack aisle for the road trip.
At the party, there were several bags of nachos for everyone to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: potato chips - thin slices of potato that are fried and eaten as a snack
  • synonym: tortilla chips - thin, fried or baked chips made from corn tortillas
