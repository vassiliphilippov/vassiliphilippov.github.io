once or twice

adverb
occasionally; a few times
1.0
Pronunciation: / wʌns ər twaɪs / (AmE), / wʌns ɔː twaɪs / (BrE)
on one or two occasions; a few times but not very often

Examples

Once or twice before

You might get lucky once or twice, but you can't rely on it.
