second

adjective
lower or subordinate musical part or instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
(in music) denoting a part, voice, or instrument that is lower in pitch or subordinate to the first in a section or ensemble.

Examples

Second violin part
After years of leading, he chose the role of second trumpet for the new concert.
She was assigned to play the second clarinet in the orchestra this season.
The second violins provided a rich harmony beneath the melody.
