Medicare payment

noun
reimbursement under Medicare for healthcare costs
3.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmed.ɪ.kər ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈmed.ɪ.keə ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (BrE)
a payment made by the government or insurance provider to reimburse an eligible person, usually an elderly or disabled individual, for healthcare expenses under the Medicare program

Examples

Accurate documentation is essential for a Medicare payment to be approved.
