clubwoman

noun
female club member
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈklʌbˌwʊmən / (BrE)

Examples

As a dedicated clubwoman, she attended every meeting and helped plan community events.
Many clubwomen in the city work together to support local charities and schools.
