trekker

noun
a person hiking long distances, often in mountains
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈtrɛkə / (BrE)
a person who undertakes a long and difficult journey, especially on foot and often through mountainous or remote regions

Examples

After weeks on the trail, the trekker was relieved to reach the village.
Many trekkers visit Nepal each year to explore the Himalayas.
The experienced trekker navigated narrow mountain passes with confidence.
