comburant

adjective
substance supporting combustion
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəmˈbʊr.ənt / (AmE), / kəmˈbjʊər.ənt / (BrE)
(of a substance) supporting combustion; able to act as an oxidizer and enable burning.

Examples

A comburant substance
A fire needs both a fuel and a comburant to sustain itself.
Oxygen is the most common comburant used in industrial processes.
Scientists tested different comburant gases to study their effects on the speed of combustion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxidizer - a substance that provides oxygen to support combustion
