genus Meles

noun
type genus of the badgers (Melinae)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmiːliːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmiːliːz / (BrE)
The type genus of the subfamily Melinae, comprising the Eurasian badgers.

Examples

The genus Meles badgers
Scientists have reclassified several species within the genus Meles based on recent genetic studies.
The genus Meles is the most widely recognized group of badgers in zoological taxonomy.
The Eurasian badger belongs to the genus Meles, which is widespread throughout Europe and Asia.
