Pinguicula

noun
genus of carnivorous bog plants (butterworts)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪŋɡwɪˈkjuːlə / (AmE), / ˌpɪŋɡwɪˈkjuːlə / (BrE)
A genus of almost stemless carnivorous bog plants, commonly known as butterworts, found in Europe, America, and as far south as Antarctica.

Examples

Pinguicula plant species
Botanists discovered a new Pinguicula species during their expedition to South America.
Many species of Pinguicula can trap and digest insects on their sticky leaves.
The Pinguicula thrives in damp, nutrient-poor soils where few other plants survive.

Related Terms
