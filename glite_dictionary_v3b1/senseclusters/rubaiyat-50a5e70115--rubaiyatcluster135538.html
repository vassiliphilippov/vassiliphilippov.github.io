Rubaiyat

noun
Persian poetry collection
Pronunciation: / ruːˈbaɪ.ət /; / ruːˈbaɪjɑt / (AmE), / ruːˈbaɪ.ət /; / ruːˈbaɪjɑːt / (BrE)
a poem or collection of poems made up of quatrains (four-line verses), especially in the Persian tradition and often referring to the famous work by Omar Khayyam
