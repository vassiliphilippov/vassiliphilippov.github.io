Rubaiyat

noun
Persian poetry collection
9.9
Pronunciation: /ruː.baɪ.ˈjɑːt/ /; / /ruː.bi.ˈjɑːt/ (AmE), /ruː.baɪ.ˈjɑːt/ /; / /ruː.bi.ˈjɑːt/ (BrE)
