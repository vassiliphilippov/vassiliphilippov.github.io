self-seeking

adjective
concerned with personal advantage
3.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /self.ˈsiː.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /self.ˈsiː.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
concerned chiefly with your own personal gain or advantage, often disregarding others

Examples

Self-seeking attitude
He avoided self-seeking behavior by always considering the needs of others.
Her self-seeking attitude alienated her friends and family over time.
The public grew tired of the self-seeking politicians who seemed uninterested in real change.

Related Terms
