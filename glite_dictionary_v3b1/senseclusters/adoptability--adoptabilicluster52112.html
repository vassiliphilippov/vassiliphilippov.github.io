adoptability

noun
suitability for adoption
4.6
Pronunciation: / əˌdɑptəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌdɒptəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or able to be adopted, especially by a person or family

Examples

Adoptability of a pet
The adoptability of a pet often depends on its health and temperament.
Shelters work to improve the adoptability of animals by providing training and medical care.
Factors like age and behavior can affect the adoptability of children in foster care.
