puppy fat

noun
temporary fat in children, lost during adolescence
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpi fæt / (AmE), / ˈpʌpi fæt / (BrE)
a layer of fat on a baby or child, typically regarded as healthy and temporary, that usually disappears with adolescence

Examples

Puppy fat on children
Don't worry about the puppy fat; he'll outgrow it by his teens.
His cheeks were round with puppy fat when he was eight years old.
Many children have a little puppy fat that vanishes as they grow older.
