pedicle

noun
connecting stalk
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.dɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈpe.dɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
a small stalk or stem that connects one part of the body to another, especially in anatomy or biology

Examples

Surgical pedicle
The surgeon carefully examined the pedicle connecting the tumor to the tissue.
In some plants, the flower is attached to the stem by a thin pedicle.
The spinal nerve passes through a bony pedicle in the vertebra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stalk - a thin supporting structure connecting parts
  • synonym: stem - the main structural part connecting or supporting
