AUC

noun
Colombian paramilitary organization
Pronunciation: / eɪ juː siː / (AmE), / eɪ juː siː / (BrE)
a Colombian paramilitary organization formed in 1997 as an alliance of local and regional groups, financed by narcotics earnings and involved in protecting members' economic interests

Examples

AUC paramilitary group
Many communities were displaced by actions taken by the AUC and rival groups.
Reports linked the AUC to both drug trafficking and political violence across rural regions.
The AUC played a significant role in Colombia's civil conflict during the late 1990s and early 2000s.
