cosmopolitan

adjective
widely distributed around the world (esp. of organisms)
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈpɑːlətən / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən / (BrE)
(of a plant, animal, or other organism) found in many different parts of the world; not restricted to a particular geographic region.

Examples

Cosmopolitan species range
Many bacteria are cosmopolitan, existing in diverse environmental conditions worldwide.
The dandelion is a cosmopolitan species, thriving on every continent except Antarctica.
This weed's cosmopolitan distribution makes it difficult to control in any one location.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endemic - restricted or native to a particular region
