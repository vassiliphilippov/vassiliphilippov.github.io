jugal point

noun
craniometric point on the zygomatic bone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuɡəl pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɡəl pɔɪnt / (BrE)
the point on the skull where the frontal and temporal processes of the zygomatic bone meet, used as a craniometric landmark

Examples

The jugal point on skull
In anthropological studies, the jugal point is measured to assess craniofacial variation.
Precise location of the jugal point is essential in reconstructing three-dimensional skull models.
The surgeon identified the jugal point before proceeding with the facial reconstruction procedure.
