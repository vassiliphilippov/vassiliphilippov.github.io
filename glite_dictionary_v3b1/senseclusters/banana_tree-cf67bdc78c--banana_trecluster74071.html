banana tree

noun
tropical plant bearing bananas
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə ˌtri / (AmE), / bəˈnænə ˌtriː / (BrE)
a tropical or subtropical plant of the genus Musa, resembling a tree and bearing large leaves and clusters of elongated edible fruit (bananas)

Examples

A banana tree plant
A single banana tree can yield dozens of bananas in a season.
Farmers in tropical regions often cultivate the banana tree for its nutritious fruit.
The banana tree in their backyard produces fruit every summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banana - the elongated, edible fruit produced by banana trees
