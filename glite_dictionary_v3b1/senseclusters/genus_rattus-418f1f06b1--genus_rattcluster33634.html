genus Rattus

noun
group of rat species (rodents)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈrætəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈratəs / (BrE)
a group of rodents including common house rats, characterized by upper incisors with a beveled edge

Examples

The genus Rattus includes species that have adapted to urban environments worldwide.
