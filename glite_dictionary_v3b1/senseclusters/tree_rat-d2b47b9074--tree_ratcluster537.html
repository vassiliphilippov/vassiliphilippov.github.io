tree rat

noun
slang term for a squirrel
Pronunciation: / tri ræt / (AmE), / triː ræt / (BrE)
(slang) A squirrel, especially when regarded as a pest or nuisance.

Examples

After years in the city, she learned to spot tree rats nearly everywhere.
Some people find tree rats cute, while others see them as pests.
The gardener complained about the tree rats stealing all his birdseed.
