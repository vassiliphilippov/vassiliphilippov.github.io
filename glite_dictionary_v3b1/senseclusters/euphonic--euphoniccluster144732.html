euphonic

adjective
pleasing in sound
Pronunciation: / juˈfɑnɪk / (AmE), / juːˈfɒnɪk / (BrE)
pleasing or agreeable in sound; characterized by euphony.

Examples

A euphonic sound
She was drawn to names that sounded especially euphonic and graceful.
The euphonic quality of the music soothed the audience.
The poet chose euphonic words to create a sense of harmony in the stanza.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cacophonic - having a harsh, discordant sound
  • synonym: euphony - the quality of being pleasing to the ear
