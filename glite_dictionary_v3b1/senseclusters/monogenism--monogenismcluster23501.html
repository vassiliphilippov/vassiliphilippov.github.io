monogenism

noun
single origin theory
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑdʒəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈdʒɛnɪzəm / (BrE)
the theory that a group (such as a species, language family, or all humans) originated from a single ancestor or source
