pneumograph

noun
breathing recorder
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːməˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈnjuːməˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
a device that records the movement of the chest or lungs during breathing

Examples

A pneumograph device
The technician attached the pneumograph to the patient's chest to monitor breathing during the test.
Researchers used a pneumograph to study how stress affects respiratory patterns.
A pneumograph can help detect irregularities in a person's breathing while they sleep.
