stockman

noun
male livestock farmer or herder
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːkmən / (AmE), / ˈstɒkmən / (BrE)
A man whose job is to breed or raise livestock, such as cattle or sheep.

Examples

A stockman on farm
After decades as a stockman in the Outback, he finally retired to the city.
As a skilled stockman, he knew every animal on the ranch by name.
The stockman checked on the cattle every morning before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, especially in the US
  • synonym: herdsman - a person who tends livestock on the range
  • synonym: shepherd - a person who tends and rears sheep
