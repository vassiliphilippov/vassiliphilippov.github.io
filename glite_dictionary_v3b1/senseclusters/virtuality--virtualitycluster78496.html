virtuality

noun
state of being virtual
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝːtʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌvɜːtʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)

Examples

In gaming, virtuality allows players to experience worlds that do not exist in reality.
Some people find it difficult to separate virtuality from real life after spending hours online.
