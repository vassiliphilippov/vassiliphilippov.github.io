virtuality

noun
state of being virtual
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌvɝːtʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / ˌvɜːtʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being virtual, especially in digital or computer-generated environments

Examples

Virtuality in games
The virtuality of online meetings has changed the way many companies operate.
In gaming, virtuality allows players to experience worlds that do not exist in reality.
Some people find it difficult to separate virtuality from real life after spending hours online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: virtual reality - a computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional environment
