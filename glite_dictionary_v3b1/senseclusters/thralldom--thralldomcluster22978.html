thralldom

noun
state of being under another's control
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɔl.dəm / (AmE), / ˈθrɔːl.dəm / (BrE)
The state of being under the power or control of another person; bondage or enslavement.

Examples

Under thralldom control
After years of thralldom, the people finally won their independence.
She escaped the thralldom of a controlling relationship and started a new life.
The king's thralldom over his subjects was finally broken by rebellion.
