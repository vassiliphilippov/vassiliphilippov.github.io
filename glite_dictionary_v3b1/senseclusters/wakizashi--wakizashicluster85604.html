wakizashi

noun
short Japanese sword
Pronunciation: / ˌwɑːkɪˈzɑːʃi / (AmE), / ˌwækɪˈzæʃi / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese short sword, usually carried by samurai along with a katana, used for combat, utility, rituals, and as a symbol of status
