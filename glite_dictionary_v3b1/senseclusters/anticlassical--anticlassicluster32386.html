anticlassical

adjective
opposed to classical
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈklæsɪkəl / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting classical theories, styles, or principles, especially in art, literature, science, or mathematics
