Ethril

noun
brand name for antibiotic erythromycin
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθrɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛθrɪl / (BrE)
A trademarked brand of the antibiotic erythromycin, used to treat infections caused by various bacteria.

Examples

Ethril antibiotic
After discovering his allergy to penicillin, he was switched to Ethril for his pneumonia.
Some strains of bacteria have developed resistance to Ethril over time.
The doctor prescribed Ethril to treat her respiratory infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erythromycin - a broad-spectrum antibiotic produced by Streptomyces erythreus
