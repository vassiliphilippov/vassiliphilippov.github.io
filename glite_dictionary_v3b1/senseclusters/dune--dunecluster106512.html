dune

noun
ridge of sand formed by wind
2.7 8.47/m
Pronunciation: / duÀên / (AmE), / djuÀên / (BrE)
A hill or ridge of sand formed by the wind, typically found in deserts, near lakes, or along coastlines.

Examples

Sand dune hill
Tall grasses help to stabilize the dunes along the coast.
The desert was dotted with dune after dune, stretching to the horizon.
We walked along the beach and climbed a dune to get a better view of the sea.

Synonyms

  • sand dune
    3.2 1.32/m
    sand dune is a wind-formed ridge of loose sand on coasts or deserts, while dune is the general, single-word term used in maps and speech.
  • sandhill
    3.8 0.30/m
    sandhill is a broader sandy area or low hill often used in place names, while dune means a wind-made ridge of loose sand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hill - a naturally raised area of land, not as high as a mountain
