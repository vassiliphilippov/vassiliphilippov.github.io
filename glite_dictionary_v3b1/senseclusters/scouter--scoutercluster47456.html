scouter

noun
person who scouts
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊtər / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtə / (BrE)
a person who searches or explores an area to gather information, especially for military or scouting purposes

Examples

Military scouter
The scouter moved quietly through the forest, looking for signs of the missing hikers.
As a skilled scouter, she was often chosen to lead the way on expeditions.
The army relied on the scouter to report any enemy movement near the border.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explorer - a person who investigates unknown regions
  • synonym: scout - a person sent out to obtain information
  • synonym: spy - a person who secretly collects information about an enemy
