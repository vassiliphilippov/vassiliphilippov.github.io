publicizer

noun
someone who brings things to public attention
5.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌb.lɪ.saɪ.zər/ /; / /ˈpʌb.lə.saɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈpʌb.lɪ.saɪ.zə/ /; / /ˈpʌb.lə.saɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person or organization whose job is to make something widely known, especially to promote a person, event, or product, or to bring it to public attention
