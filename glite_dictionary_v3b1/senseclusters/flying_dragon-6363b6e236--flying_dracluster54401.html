flying dragon

noun
small gliding Asian lizard
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈdræɡən / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈdræɡən / (BrE)
any of several small tropical Asian lizards (genus Draco) capable of gliding by spreading winglike membranes on each side of the body.

Examples

The flying dragon lizard
Many tourists are fascinated by the colorful appearance of the flying dragon.
Scientists observed a flying dragon gliding gracefully through the rainforest canopy.
The flying dragon uses its winglike membranes to glide from tree to tree.
