styrene

noun
colorless liquid used to make polystyrene
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪˌrin / (AmE), / ˈstʌɪriːn / (BrE)
a colorless, oily liquid hydrocarbon used as a monomer in the production of polystyrene plastics and resins
