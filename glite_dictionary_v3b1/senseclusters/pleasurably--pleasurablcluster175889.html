pleasurably

adverb
in a pleasurable manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛʒ.ər.ə.bli / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒ.ər.əbli / (BrE)

Examples

To pleasurably enjoy

The audience was pleasurably surprised by the band's encore performance.
