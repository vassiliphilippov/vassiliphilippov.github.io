pearlescent

adjective
having a pearl-like, iridescent sheen
7.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / p…úrÀàl…õs…ônt / (AmE), / p…úÀêÀàl…õs…ônt / (BrE)
having a lustrous, iridescent appearance like that of a pearl, showing changing rainbow-like colors when viewed from different angles

Examples

Pearlescent finish
She wore a dress with a subtle pearlescent finish that caught the eye.
The artist used a pearlescent glaze to add depth and shine to the ceramic vase.
The car's pearlescent paint shimmered beautifully under the sunlight.

  • opalescent
    8.2 0.34/m

  • pearlized
    6.0 0.00/m

  • pearl-like
    4.0 0.01/m

  • pearly
