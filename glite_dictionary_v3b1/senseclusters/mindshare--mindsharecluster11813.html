mindshare

verb
to share ideas
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪndˌʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈmaɪndˌʃeə / (BrE)
to share ideas, opinions, or information within a group to reach common understanding or align thinking

Examples

Teams mindshare ideas
Before the launch, the team will mindshare ideas to ensure a consistent message.
We need to mindshare our thoughts on the project to make sure everyone is aligned.
The manager encouraged the group to mindshare during the weekly meeting.
