homo-

x
human-related prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊməʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'man' or 'human', used in scientific names and words referring to humans

Examples

Homo- sapiens
The prefix homo- is used in 'Homo sapiens' to indicate the human species.
Words like 'hominid' and 'homozygous' both contain the combining form homo-.
In scientific classification, homo- helps identify terms related to humans.
