please

verb
to wish or choose (to do something)
1.0
Pronunciation: / pliːz / (AmE), / pliːz / (BrE)
to choose or wish (to do something); to have the will or desire (to do something)

Examples

He pleases to leave
He may leave early if he pleases.
She did exactly as she pleased, regardless of anyone else's opinion.
You may sit wherever you please.
