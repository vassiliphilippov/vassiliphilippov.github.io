strengthen

verb
make body or muscle firmer and healthier
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛŋθən / (AmE), / ˈstrɛŋθən /; / ˈstrɛŋθ(ə)n / (BrE)
to make or become stronger or more effective, either physically (such as muscles) or in general (such as relationships or organizations)

Examples

To strengthen muscles
Physical therapy helped strengthen her injured knee after surgery.
Regular exercise will strengthen your leg muscles and improve flexibility.
Swimming is a good way to strengthen your back and core.
Diplomats hope to strengthen relations between the two countries through trade agreements.
Regular exercise can strengthen your heart and muscles.
The government took steps to strengthen the economy after the crisis.
After months of physical therapy, his muscles finally strengthened.
Daily exercise can strengthen your immune system and overall health.
The team's resolve strengthened as they faced new challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortify - make stronger, especially to protect against attack
  • synonym: tone - make muscles firmer and healthier through exercise
  • antonym: weaken - make something less strong or effective
