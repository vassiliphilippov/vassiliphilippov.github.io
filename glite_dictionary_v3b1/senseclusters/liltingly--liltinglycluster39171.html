liltingly

adverb
in a rhythmic way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪltɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈlɪltɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that is pleasantly rhythmic, with a rising and falling quality like a song

Examples

To speak liltingly
The storyteller spoke liltingly, captivating the children with her melodic voice.
Birds sang liltingly outside the window as the sun rose.
He read the poem liltingly, giving each line a musical quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monotonously - in a dull, unchanging, or flat manner
  • synonym: musically - in a way that is melodious or like music
