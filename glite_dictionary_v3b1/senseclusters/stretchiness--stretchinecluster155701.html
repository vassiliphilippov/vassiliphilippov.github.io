stretchiness

noun
ability to be stretched
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɛtʃ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈstrɛtʃ.i.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being able to be stretched without breaking.

Examples

Good stretchiness of fabric
Good stretchiness is important for materials used in athletic wear.
She tested the stretchiness of the balloon before blowing it up.
The stretchiness of the fabric makes these leggings comfortable for exercise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elasticity - the ability to resume original shape after being stretched
