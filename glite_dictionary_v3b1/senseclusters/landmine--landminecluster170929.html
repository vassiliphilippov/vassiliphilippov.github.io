landmine

verb
to plant explosives
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlænd.maɪn/ (AmE), /ˈlænd.maɪn/ (BrE)
to place or bury explosive devices in an area, making it dangerous to cross.

Examples

To landmine a road
The rebels planned to landmine the bridge to prevent enemy vehicles from crossing.
Soldiers were warned not to landmine civilian areas due to the risk to local people.
They decided to landmine the path as a defensive measure before retreating.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demine - to remove landmines from an area
  • synonym: mine - to place explosive devices underground
