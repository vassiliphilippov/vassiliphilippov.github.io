good-time girl

noun
fun-loving, sociable woman
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊd ˌtaɪm ɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈɡʊd ˌtaɪm ɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who is sociable, lively, and seeks pleasure or amusement, often at parties or social events

Examples

A good-time girl at parties
Although she was called a good-time girl, she was also known for her loyalty to friends.
She had a reputation as a good-time girl who brought laughter to every gathering.
The story follows a good-time girl in search of something more meaningful in life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party girl - a woman who enjoys going to parties and socializing frequently
