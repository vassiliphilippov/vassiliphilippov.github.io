Pleurobrachiidae

noun
family of marine animals (sea gooseberries)
Pronunciation: / plurəˌbrækiɪˌdiː / (AmE), / pljʊərəˌbrækiɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine animals within the phylum Ctenophora, commonly known as sea gooseberries, distinguished by their transparent, round bodies and rows of ciliary combs.

Members of the Pleurobrachiidae are often called sea gooseberries because of their distinctive shape.
