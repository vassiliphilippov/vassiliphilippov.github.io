renunciant

adjective
characterized by renunciation; self-denying
Pronunciation: / rɪˈnʌnsiənt / (AmE), / rɪˈnʌnsiənt / (BrE)
characterized by or relating to renunciation or the act of voluntarily giving up something, especially as a moral or religious discipline.

Examples

A renunciant attitude
Her renunciant attitude inspired others to live more simply.
She made renunciant choices throughout her life, always prioritizing duty over comfort.
The monk led a wholly renunciant existence, renouncing material possessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - practicing severe self-discipline and abstention from indulgence
  • antonym: self-indulgent - characterized by excessive or unrestrained gratification of one's own desires
