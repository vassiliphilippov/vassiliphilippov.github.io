tread

verb
to brace a bow with the foot
6.1
Pronunciation: / trɛd / (AmE), / trɛd / (BrE)
(of an archer) to brace a bow by pressing the foot against its center.

Examples

To tread a bow
Historically, archers would tread their bows to ensure proper tension.
She demonstrated how to tread the center of the bow with her foot.
The archer skillfully tread the bow before stringing it.
