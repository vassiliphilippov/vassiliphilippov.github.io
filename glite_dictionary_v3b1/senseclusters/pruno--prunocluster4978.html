pruno

noun
improvised prison-made alcoholic drink
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəʊ / (BrE)
an alcoholic drink made by fermenting fruit, sugar, and other ingredients, typically improvised by prison inmates

Examples

Prison pruno drink
Despite its foul taste, pruno remains popular among prisoners seeking a way to drink alcohol.
She read an article describing how pruno is secretly made in prisons across the country.
The inmates brewed a batch of pruno using apples and bread from the cafeteria.
