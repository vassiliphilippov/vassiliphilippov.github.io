corniced

adjective
with snow overhang
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪst / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪst / (BrE)
having an overhanging mass or projection of windblown snow at the edge of a ridge or cliff

Examples

Corniced ridge
The mountaineers carefully avoided the corniced section of the ridge to prevent accidents.
A corniced cliff can be dangerous because the snow overhang may collapse unexpectedly.
They paused to assess the stability of the corniced summit before continuing their ascent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overhanging - extending or hanging over something
  • antonym: sheer - very steep or almost vertical
