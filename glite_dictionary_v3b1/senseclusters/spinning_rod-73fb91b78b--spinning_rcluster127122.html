spinning rod

noun
fishing rod for spinning lures
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪŋ rɑd / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪŋ rɒd / (BrE)
a fishing rod specifically designed for casting spinning lures, typically equipped with a spinning reel

Examples

A spinning rod for fishing

He caught three trout using his new spinning rod and a small silver lure.
She chose a lightweight spinning rod for fishing in the river.
