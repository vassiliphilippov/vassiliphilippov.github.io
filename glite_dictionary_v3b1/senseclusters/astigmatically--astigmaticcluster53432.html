astigmatically

adverb
in an astigmatic way
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˌstɪɡˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / əˌstɪɡˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is affected by astigmatism, especially in optics or vision

Examples
