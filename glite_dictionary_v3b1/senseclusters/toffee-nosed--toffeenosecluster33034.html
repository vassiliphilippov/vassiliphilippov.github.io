toffee-nosed

adjective
snobbish or pretentiously superior (British)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔfi ˌnoʊzd / (AmE), / ˈtɒfi nəʊzd / (BrE)
Pretending to be more important or superior than others; snobbish or conceited, especially in a way that is affected or pretentious.

Examples

A toffee-nosed attitude
He tried not to sound toffee-nosed when he described his expensive boarding school background.
Many locals viewed the new manager as somewhat toffee-nosed and out of touch with the community.
She dismissed the toffee-nosed guests who looked down on everyone else at the party.
