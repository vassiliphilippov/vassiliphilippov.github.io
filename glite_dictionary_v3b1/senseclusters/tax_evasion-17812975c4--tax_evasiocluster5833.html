tax evasion

noun
illegal avoidance of paying taxes
Pronunciation: / tæks ɪˈveɪʒən / (AmE), / tæks ɪˈveɪʒən / (BrE)
The illegal act of deliberately not paying taxes by concealing income or providing false information to tax authorities.

Examples

Illegal tax evasion
Authorities are cracking down on tax evasion schemes across the country.
He was sentenced to prison for committing tax evasion over several years.
The company was charged with tax evasion after an investigation revealed unreported income.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tax avoidance - the act of minimizing tax liability by legal means
