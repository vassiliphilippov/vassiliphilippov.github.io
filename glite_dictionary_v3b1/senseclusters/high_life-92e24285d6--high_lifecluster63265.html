high life

noun
lifestyle of extravagant spending and luxury
Pronunciation: / haɪ laɪf / (AmE), / haɪ lʌɪf / (BrE)
a way of life marked by extravagant spending, luxury, and indulgence in pleasures

Examples

Living the high life
After winning the lottery, she started living the high life in a fancy penthouse.
He left his small town to pursue the high life in the big city.
Many celebrities are drawn to the high life and all its temptations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luxury - a state of great comfort and extravagant living
  • antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
