cheeselike

adjective
resembling cheese in texture or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃizˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the texture, appearance, or qualities of cheese.

Examples

Cheeselike texture
Some fungi develop a cheeselike coating on their surfaces as they mature.
The researcher described the ancient residue as having a distinctly cheeselike smell and appearance.
The sauce had a cheeselike consistency that made the pasta very creamy.
