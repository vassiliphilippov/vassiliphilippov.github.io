nonreaction

noun
lack of response
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnriˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnriˈækʃən / (BrE)
a lack of response or reaction to an event, stimulus, or situation

Examples

Complete nonreaction
His complete nonreaction to the criticism made it difficult to know what he was thinking.
The teacher was surprised by the students' nonreaction to the announcement of the test.
Despite the shocking news, her nonreaction left everyone in the room confused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indifference - a lack of interest or concern
  • antonym: reaction - a response to an event or stimulus
