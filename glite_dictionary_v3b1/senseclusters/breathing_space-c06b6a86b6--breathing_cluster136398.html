breathing space

noun
an area or period allowing more freedom or relief
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːðɪŋ ˌspeɪs /; / ˈbriðɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ ˌspeɪs / (BrE)
a short period or situation that gives relief or a break from pressure, difficulty, or stress

Examples

A quiet breathing space
After the meeting, she went for a walk to get some breathing space.
The new regulations will give small businesses some much-needed breathing space.
They cleared out the clutter to create a little breathing space in the office.
After the busy holiday season, she needed some breathing space to recover.
The negotiations gave both sides a breathing space before making further decisions.
With the deadline extended, we finally have a bit of breathing space to complete the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathing room - sufficient room for easy breathing or movement
  • synonym: respite - a short period of rest or relief from something difficult
