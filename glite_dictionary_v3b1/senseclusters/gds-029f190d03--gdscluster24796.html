GDS

sym
travel booking system
7.4
Pronunciation: / dʒiː diː ɛs / (AmE), / dʒiː diː ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for a computerized network system used by travel agencies to access and manage travel information and bookings

Examples

Travel GDS system
Many travel agencies rely on a GDS to find the best flight and hotel options for their clients.
The airline updated its GDS to include new international routes and services.
Training staff to use the GDS efficiently is essential for smooth travel bookings.
