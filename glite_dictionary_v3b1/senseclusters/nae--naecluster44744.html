nae

interjection
Scots exclamation for no
9.9
Pronunciation: / neɪ / (AmE), / neɪ / (BrE)
an exclamation meaning 'no', used as a brief reply or refusal, especially in Scottish English

Examples

He said nae
When asked if he would join the game, he replied with a firm nae.
She shook her head and said nae when offered more tea.
The child answered nae to every question his mother asked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aye - an exclamation meaning 'yes', especially in Scottish English
  • synonym: no - used to give a negative response
