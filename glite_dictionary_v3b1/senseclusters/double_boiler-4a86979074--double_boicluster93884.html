double boiler

noun
two nested saucepans for gentle heating
8.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌb.əl ˈbɔɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈdʌb.əl ˈbɔɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a pair of saucepans, one fitting inside the other, used for gentle heating or melting foods by placing one pan over simmering water in the lower pan

Examples

A double boiler set

The recipe recommends preparing the custard in a double boiler.
