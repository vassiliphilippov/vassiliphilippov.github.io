spindle-legged

adjective
having long, slender legs
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪndl̩ˌlɛɡd / (AmE), / ˈspɪndlˌlɛɡd / (BrE)
having long, slender legs, typically in a way that is thin and somewhat delicate in appearance.

Examples

A spindle-legged chair
A spindle-legged chair stood in the corner, its delicate legs barely touching the floor.
She watched a spindle-legged colt take its first cautious steps in the field.
The spindle-legged heron waded carefully through the shallow water.
