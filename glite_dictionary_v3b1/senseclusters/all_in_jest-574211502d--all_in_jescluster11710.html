all in jest

x
meant as a joke; not serious
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɔl ɪn dʒɛst / (AmE), / ɔːl ɪn dʒɛst / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is said or done as a joke and is not meant to be taken seriously

Examples

All in jest comment
His comment about quitting was all in jest, so there's no need to worry.
She apologized, explaining that her remarks were all in jest and not meant to offend.
When he teased his friend about the haircut, it was all in jest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: serious - not joking or intended as a joke
