pipelining

noun
installing pipelines
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪplaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪplaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of installing or using pipelines to transport water, oil, gas, or other fluids

Examples

Oil pipelining
The company invested millions in pipelining to connect remote oil fields to the main refinery.
Advances in technology have made pipelining safer and more efficient than ever before.
Environmental regulations must be followed during pipelining to protect local ecosystems.
The company invested heavily in pipelining to move natural gas across the country.
Advances in pipelining have made it safer to transport oil over long distances.
Strict regulations govern the pipelining of hazardous materials to protect the environment.

  • synonym: pipeline construction - the building of pipelines for transporting fluids
