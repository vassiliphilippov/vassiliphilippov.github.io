collect

adjective, adverb
requiring payment by recipient on delivery
3.1 7.4
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkt / (BrE)
(of a call, letter, or parcel) with payment to be made by the recipient, not the sender

Examples

Collect call
If you send the package collect, the recipient will cover the shipping cost.
She made a collect call to her parents when she lost her wallet.
The parcel was sent collect, so I had to pay when it arrived.
He made the long-distance call collect because he had no change for the phone.
If you can't afford postage, you can always send the parcel collect.
She refused to accept any packages sent collect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: COD - cash on delivery
  • synonym: reverse charge - a system where the receiving party pays the cost of a phone call
