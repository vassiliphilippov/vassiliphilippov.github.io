respray

verb
to repaint surface
3.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈspreɪ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈspreɪ/ (AmE), /riː.ˈspreɪ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈspreɪ/ (BrE)
to spray something again with a liquid, especially to apply a new layer of paint or treatment

Examples

To respray a car
After the accident, they decided to respray the car to hide the damage.
