transfer

noun
ticket for changing vehicles on a journey
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtræns.fɚ / (AmE), / ˈtræns.fə / (BrE)
a ticket issued to a passenger that allows them to continue their journey on a different vehicle without paying a new fare

Examples

A bus transfer ticket
I lost my transfer and had to pay for a second subway trip.
The transfer is valid for 90 minutes after your first ride begins.
You must ask the driver for a transfer if you plan to switch to another bus.

  • synonym: ticket - a printed pass allowing entry or travel
