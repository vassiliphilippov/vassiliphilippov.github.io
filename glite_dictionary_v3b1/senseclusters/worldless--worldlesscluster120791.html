worldless

adjective
inexperienced; naive
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldləs / (BrE)
lacking practical experience or understanding of ordinary life; inexperienced or naive.

Examples

Worldless youth
Her worldless attitude made her vulnerable to people taking advantage of her.
The worldless intern was surprised by the complexity of office politics.
He seemed worldless when faced with the realities of living alone for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexperienced - not having much experience or knowledge
  • synonym: naive - lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment
  • antonym: worldly - experienced and knowledgeable about life
