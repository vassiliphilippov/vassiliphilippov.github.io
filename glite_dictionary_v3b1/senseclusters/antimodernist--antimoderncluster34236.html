antimodernist

noun
opponent of modern ideas
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɑdərnɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɒdənɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes or rejects modern ideas, values, or styles

Examples

An antimodernist critic
The art historian was called an antimodernist because he preferred classical paintings over abstract works.
Many antimodernists argue that new technologies harm traditional values.
Her antimodernist views made her unpopular among progressive thinkers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modernist - a person who supports modern ideas or styles
  • synonym: traditionalist - a person who supports traditional ideas or practices
