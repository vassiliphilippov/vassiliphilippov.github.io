calcium sulfate

noun
a white inorganic salt, CaSO₄
Pronunciation: / ˈkælsiəm ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪəm ˈsʌlfeɪt / (BrE)
a white, odorless, inorganic salt (CaSO4) used in construction, food, and medicine; occurs naturally as gypsum and anhydrite

Examples

Calcium sulfate powder

Food manufacturers sometimes add calcium sulphate as a firming agent.

The laboratory ordered more calcium sulphate for chemical experiments.

  • synonym: plaster of Paris - a form of calcium sulphate used for casts and molds
  • synonym: plaster of paris - a form of calcium sulfate hemihydrate used for casts and sculptures
