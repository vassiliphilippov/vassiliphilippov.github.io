spinothalamic

noun
nerve tract to thalamus
9.9
Pronunciation: /spɪn.oʊ.θə.ˈlæ.mɪk/ /; / /spɪn.ə.θə.ˈlæ.mɪk/ (AmE), /spɪn.əʊ.θə.ˈlæ.mɪk/ /; / /spɪn.ə.θə.ˈlæ.mɪk/ (BrE)
