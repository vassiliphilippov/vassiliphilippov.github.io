convocation

noun
formal gathering or assembly
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnvəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnvəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of calling people together for a formal meeting or assembly

Examples

The convocation ceremony
A special convocation was called to discuss the urgent issue facing the community.
The bishop addressed the annual convocation of the clergy on important church matters.
The university held a convocation to welcome new students and faculty.
