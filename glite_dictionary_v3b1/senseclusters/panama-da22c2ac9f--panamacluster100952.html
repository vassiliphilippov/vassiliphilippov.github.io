Panama

noun
a straw hat with a flat crown
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəmɑ / (AmE), / ˈpænəmə / (BrE)
a light, stiff hat made of fine straw, with a flat crown and brim, traditionally made in Ecuador and typically worn in warm weather

Examples

A Panama hat
A classic Panama is both fashionable and practical in hot weather.
He shaded his eyes with a stylish Panama during the summer cruise.
Her grandfather always wore a crisp white Panama to the garden party.
