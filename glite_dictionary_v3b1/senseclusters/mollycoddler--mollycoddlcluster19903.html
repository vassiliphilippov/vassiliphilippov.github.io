mollycoddler

noun
someone who overindulges others
8.3 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑliˌkɑdlɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɒliˌkɒdlə / (BrE)
a person who pampers or spoils someone by giving them too much care or indulgence

Examples
