mollycoddler

noun
someone who overindulges others
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑliˌkɑdlɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɒliˌkɒdlə / (BrE)
a person who pampers or spoils someone by giving them too much care or indulgence

Examples

A mollycoddler parent
As a mollycoddler, he never let his children struggle or fail on their own.
Rather than acting like a mollycoddler, she encouraged independence in her students.
Some people accused the teacher of being a mollycoddler who gave out too many compliments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mollycoddle - to treat someone with excessive indulgence or care
