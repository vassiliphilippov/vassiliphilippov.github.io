refusenik

noun
person denied emigration
9.8
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfjuːz.nɪk/ /; / /rə.ˈfjuːz.nɪk/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈfjuːz.nɪk/ /; / /rə.ˈfjuːz.nɪk/ (BrE)
