refusenik

noun
person denied emigration
9.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfjuznɪk /; / rɪˈfjuzˌnɪk / (AmE), / rɪˈfjuːznɪk /; / rɪˈfjuːz.nɪk / (BrE)
a person, especially in the former Soviet Union, who was denied permission to emigrate, often for political or religious reasons
