Synonyms

  • mentally ill
    3.4 4.63/m
    mentally ill describes someone with a diagnosable mental disorder, while crazy is an informal, vague word for madness.
  • insane
    4.0 15/m
    insane means showing or suffering severe mental illness or irrational thought, while crazy is more casual and broad.
  • mad
    4.8 8.23/m
    mad often means seriously mentally ill or disturbed, but crazy is more informal and can sound playful.
  • deranged
    7.6 2.90/m
    deranged shows a person has lost normal thought and control, while crazy can mean less severe madness.
  • demented
    7.9 2.38/m
    demented describes long-term brain decline causing confused, wild behavior, while crazy is a general word for madness.
  • unhinged
    7.8 1.13/m
    unhinged feels informal and emotionally unstable, while crazy is broader and can also be insulting or playful.
  • unbalanced
    5.7 0.68/m
    unbalanced suggests mental instability or uneven feelings, while crazy is a looser, more slangy way to say madness.
