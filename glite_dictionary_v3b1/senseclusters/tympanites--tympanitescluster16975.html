tympanites

noun
abdominal distension from gas (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪm.pəˈnaɪ.tiz / (AmE), / tɪm.pəˈniː.tiːz / (BrE)
A swelling or distension of the abdomen due to the accumulation of gas in the intestines or peritoneal cavity.

Examples

Abdominal tympanites
After the surgery, he experienced tympanites, which resolved with appropriate treatment.
Prolonged tympanites can be a symptom of an underlying gastrointestinal disorder.
The doctor diagnosed the patient's severe abdominal pain as tympanites, caused by trapped intestinal gas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloating - a general term for abdominal swelling often from gas or fluid
