mew

verb
to utter a high-pitched cry
7.9 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / mjuÀê / (AmE), / mjuÀê / (BrE)
to make a soft, high-pitched crying sound like a cat, kitten, or young bird

Examples

To mew softly
Late at night, the stray cats mew beneath my window, looking for food.
She heard a kitten mewing softly from inside the box.
The gulls mew overhead as they circle the fishing boats.
I heard a stray cat mewing outside my window all night.
She found a tiny kitten mewing in the garden.
The kitten mewed softly as it waited for its mother.
The tiny kitten kept mewing softly until its mother returned.
Birds were mewing in the nest early in the morning.
She heard a stray cat mewing outside her window on a rainy night.

Synonyms

    1.0 0.35/m

  • mewl
    9.9 0.10/m
