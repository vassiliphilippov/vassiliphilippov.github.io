holla at

verb
contact or get in touch (informal)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːlə æt / (AmE), / ˈhɒlə æt / (BrE)
(informal) to contact or get in touch with someone, especially by phone, text, or social media.

Examples

Holla at me online
If you need anything, holla at me on WhatsApp before the meeting tomorrow.
She told him to holla at the design team for the latest file updates.
I'll holla at you after I check my schedule and confirm the flight time.
