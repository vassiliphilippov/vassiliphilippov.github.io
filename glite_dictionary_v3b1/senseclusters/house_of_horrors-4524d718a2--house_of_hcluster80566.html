house of horrors

noun
place of terrifying or gruesome events
Pronunciation: / haʊs əv ˈhɔɹɚz / (AmE), / haʊs əv ˈhɒɹəz / (BrE)
a place or building marked by terrifying or gruesome experiences, often associated with fear, suffering, or horror
