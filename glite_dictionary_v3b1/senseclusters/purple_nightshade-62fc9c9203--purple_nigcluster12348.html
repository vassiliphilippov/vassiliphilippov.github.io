purple nightshade

noun
a plant with silvery foliage and purple or white flowers
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈnaɪtˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈnaɪtʃeɪd / (BrE)
A weedy plant with silvery leaves and violet, blue, or white flowers, producing a roundish berry used to curdle milk, native from the central United States to South America.

Examples

The purple nightshade berry
Farmers sometimes use the berries of purple nightshade to curdle milk for cheese.
I admired the silvery leaves and delicate flowers of the purple nightshade in the botanical garden.
The fields were dotted with purple nightshade among the grasses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightshade - a plant of the Solanum genus, often poisonous
