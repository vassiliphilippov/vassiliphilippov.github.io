madwoman

noun
insane or mentally ill woman
Pronunciation: / ˈmædˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈmadˌwʊmən /; / ˈmædˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is mentally ill or behaves in a wild, uncontrolled, or insane way

Examples

A madwoman behaves wildly
Despite being labeled a madwoman, she proved her innocence in court.
Rumors spread that the mysterious figure in the woods was a madwoman escaping from an asylum.
The novel tells the tragic story of a madwoman locked away in an attic.
She burst into the room like a madwoman, shouting and waving her arms.
After hearing the news, he said she drove away like a madwoman in the storm.
The children watched as their mother danced around the kitchen like a madwoman.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is insane or acts in a wildly foolish way
  • synonym: madman - a man who is mentally ill or insane
  • antonym: sane woman - a woman who is mentally healthy
