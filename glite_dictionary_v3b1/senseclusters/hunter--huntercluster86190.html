hunter

noun
person who searches for something
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌnɚ / (AmE), / ˈhʌntə / (BrE)
a person who actively searches for or seeks out something, especially as a pursuit or hobby

Examples

A job hunter
As a job hunter, she spent hours each day searching the listings online.
He became a successful hunter of rare antiques at local auctions.
The show follows a group of tornado hunters chasing storms across the Midwest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seeker - a person who is attempting to find or obtain something
