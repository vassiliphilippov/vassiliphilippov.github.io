hunter

noun
person who searches for something
1.8 6.30/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆhÊŒnÉš / (AmE), / ËˆhÊŒntÉ™ / (BrE)
a person who actively searches for or seeks out something, especially as a pursuit or hobby

Examples

A job hunter
As a job hunter, she spent hours each day searching the listings online.
He became a successful hunter of rare antiques at local auctions.
The show follows a group of tornado hunters chasing storms across the Midwest.

Synonyms

  • searcher
    2.3 1.18/m
    a searcher looks carefully through places or records to find something, while a hunter actively searches as a pursuit or hobby.
  • seeker
    4.0 7.30/m
    a seeker seeks ideas, truth, or goals with thought and study, while a hunter searches more by action outdoors or as a hobby.
  • pursuer
    4.2 1.73/m
    a pursuer chases someone or something directly, often in law or sport, while a hunter seeks things as an active pursuit or hobby.
