amortization

noun
gradual reduction of asset or debt value
4.4 5.8
Pronunciation: /æm.ər.tɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.mɔːr.tə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.mɔː.tə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.mɔː.tɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of gradually reducing a debt or the value of an asset by spreading payments or costs over a period of time
