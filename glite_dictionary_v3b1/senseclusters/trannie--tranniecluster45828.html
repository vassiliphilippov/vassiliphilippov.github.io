trannie

noun
vehicle transmission
Pronunciation: / ˈtræni / (AmE), / ˈtræni / (BrE)
an informal term for a vehicle transmission or gearbox, the mechanism that transfers engine power to the wheels

Examples

Car trannie
The mechanic said the trannie needed to be replaced after years of heavy use.
He learned how to fix the trannie himself to save on repair costs.
A faulty trannie can cause serious problems while driving on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gearbox - a set of gears in a vehicle that controls speed and torque
  • synonym: transmission - the system in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels
