common moonseed

noun
woody North American vine with dark fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈmuˌnsid / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈmuːnsiːd / (BrE)
A woody vine native to eastern North America, with large oval leaves, small white flowers, and purple to blue-black fruits.
