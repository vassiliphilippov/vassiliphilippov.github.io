ripple-grass

noun
a type of plantain with ribbed leaves
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪpəl ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈrɪpəl ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
an Old World plantain (Plantago lanceolata) with long narrow ribbed leaves, widely found in temperate regions

Examples

Long ripple-grass leaves
Children sometimes weave chains using the long stems of ripple-grass.
The narrow leaves of ripple-grass make it easy to distinguish from other wild plants.
You can find ripple-grass growing along country roadsides across Europe and North America.

  • synonym: ribwort - another name for the same plant species (Plantago lanceolata)
