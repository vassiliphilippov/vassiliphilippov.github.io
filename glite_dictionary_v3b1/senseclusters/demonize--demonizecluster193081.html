demonize

verb
portray as evil or demonic
6.7 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.mə.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.mə.naɪz/ (BrE)
to portray or treat someone or something as evil, wicked, or threatening, often unfairly

Examples

The media often demonise minority groups, leading to increased prejudice.
