sweat room

noun
room for steam bathing (Finnish style)
7.7
Pronunciation: / swɛt ruːm / (AmE), / swɛt ruːm / (BrE)
A room, particularly in Finnish tradition, where steam is produced by pouring water over heated rocks for bathing and sweating.

Examples

A sweat room bath
After a long hike, we relaxed in the sweat room to soothe our muscles.
Many cultures have their version of a sweat room for health and relaxation.
The resort featured a traditional sweat room next to the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sauna - a Finnish steam bath; room using hot steam for bathing and relaxation
