verb
to restore structure

to restore or rebuild the structure or arrangement of something by bringing its parts together again, often used figuratively

Examples

To reweave a friendship
After years of separation, they tried to reweave their friendship and trust.
The author managed to reweave the story to give it a more hopeful ending.
Community leaders are working hard to reweave the social fabric after the crisis.

    3.9 3/m
