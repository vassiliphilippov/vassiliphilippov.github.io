reweave

verb
to restore structure
6.2
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈwiːv/ /; / /rɪ.ˈwiːv/ /; / /rə.ˈwiːv/ (AmE), /riː.ˈwiːv/ /; / /rɪ.ˈwiːv/ /; / /rə.ˈwiːv/ (BrE)

Examples

Community leaders are working hard to reweave the social fabric after the crisis.
