spray paint

verb
apply paint with an aerosol can
Pronunciation: / spreɪ peɪnt / (AmE), / spreɪ peɪnt / (BrE)
to apply paint to a surface using an aerosol spray can

Examples

To spray paint a wall
He watched his neighbor spray paint the mailbox a bright red.
They decided to spray paint the old chairs to give them a fresh look.
Vandals spray painted graffiti on the wall last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brush paint - to apply paint with a brush, not a spray
  • synonym: paint - to cover a surface with paint
