misvalue

verb
to assign wrong value
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈvæljuː / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈvæljuː / (BrE)
to assign an incorrect or inappropriate value or worth to something

Examples

To misvalue a painting
The art dealer misvalued the painting, causing it to sell for much less than it was worth.
If you misvalue your skills, you might accept a lower salary than you deserve.
The assets were misvalued on the balance sheet, leading to confusion among investors.
