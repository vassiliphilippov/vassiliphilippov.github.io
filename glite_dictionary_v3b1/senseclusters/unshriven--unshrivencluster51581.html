unshriven

adjective
without absolution
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃrɪvən / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃrɪvən / (BrE)
not having received absolution or forgiveness for sins, especially before death

Examples

Unshriven before death
The knight feared dying unshriven after the fierce battle.
She prayed for the souls of those who passed away unshriven.
Legends tell of ghosts who wander the earth unshriven and restless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shriven - having received absolution for sins
  • synonym: unforgiven - not forgiven for wrongdoing
