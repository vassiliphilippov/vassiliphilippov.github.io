Opisthobranchia

noun
group of soft-bodied marine gastropods
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˌpɪsθoʊˈbræŋkiə / (AmE), / əʊˌpɪsθəˈbræŋkɪə /; / əʊˌpɪsθəˈbraŋkɪə / (BrE)
a group of soft-bodied marine gastropod mollusks (such as sea slugs, sea butterflies, and sea hares) with gills behind the heart and no operculum
