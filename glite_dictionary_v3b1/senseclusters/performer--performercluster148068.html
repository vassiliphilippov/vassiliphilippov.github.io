performer

noun
person who entertains an audience
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɔrmər / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmə / (BrE)
a person who entertains an audience by acting, singing, dancing, or playing music; an entertainer

Examples

A performer on stage
Each performer took a bow at the end of the concert.
He is a talented performer known for his powerful stage presence.
The performer captivated the audience with her graceful ballet routine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audience - people watching or listening to a performance
  • synonym: entertainer - a person whose job is to entertain others
