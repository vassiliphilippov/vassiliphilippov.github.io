performer

noun
person who entertains an audience
2.5 18/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàf…îrm…ôr / (AmE), / p…ôÀàf…îÀêm…ô / (BrE)
a person who entertains an audience by acting, singing, dancing, or playing music; an entertainer

Examples

A performer on stage
Each performer took a bow at the end of the concert.
He is a talented performer known for his powerful stage presence.
The performer captivated the audience with her graceful ballet routine.

Synonyms

  • entertainer
    2.1 5.71/m
    an entertainer works to amuse an audience in many settings, while a performer is any person who presents music, dance, or drama.
  • performing artist
    3.4 0.28/m
    a performing artist presents music, dance, or drama on stage and often has formal training, while a performer is any stage entertainer.
  • artiste
    7.4 0.26/m
    an artiste is a showy or French-influenced word for a singer or dancer in show business, while a performer is neutral for any entertainer.
  • renderer
    6.6 0.00/m
    a renderer often refers to someone who interprets or presents a work in specialist contexts, while a performer is a general entertainer.
