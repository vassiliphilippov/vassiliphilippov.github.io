single

adjective
uniform or universal; not varying
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəl /; / ˈsɪŋɡl / (BrE)
focused on one thing; undivided or uniform

Examples

Single system
The country adopted a single system of taxation across all regions.
There is a single health policy that covers all employees.
We need a single set of guidelines for every department.
His affection for his childhood home remained single throughout his life.
She pursued her goal with single devotion, never allowing distractions to interfere.
The teacher gave single attention to each student during the session.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exclusive - restricted to a single object or person; not shared
  • synonym: undivided - not separated or shared; whole or entire (especially in reference to attention or feeling)
  • synonym: unified - made into or formed as a unit
  • antonym: varied - differing in some way; not uniform
