wale

verb
to cause welts
Pronunciation: / weɪl / (AmE), / weɪl / (BrE)
to mark the skin with raised ridges or welts, typically as a result of being struck or rubbed repeatedly

Examples

To wale the skin
The teacher warned that the stick could wale the students' hands if used too harshly.
After the long hike, the tight straps began to wale his shoulders.
Repeated blows from the belt would wale the skin, leaving visible marks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bruise - to injure the skin causing discoloration
  • antonym: heal - to become healthy again after injury
