piso

noun
Philippine currency unit
5.4 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀêso ä / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀês…ô ä / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in the Philippines, also used to refer to the one-peso coin

Examples

A piso coin
She handed the cashier a piso to pay for her bus fare.
The souvenir cost only ten piso, making it an affordable gift.
He found a shiny piso coin on the sidewalk while walking home.

Synonyms

  • Philippine peso
    2.9 0.01/m
    Philippine peso is the country's official currency used in banks and prices, while piso is the local name and also the one-peso coin.
  • peso
    1.6 1.19/m
    peso is a generic currency name used in several countries, while piso is the Filipino term and can mean the one-peso coin.

Related Terms
