randite

noun
rare uranium mineral
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrænˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˈrænˌdaɪt / (BrE)
a rare mineral consisting of hydrated calcium uranium vanadate, typically found as yellow crystals

Examples

Yellow randite crystals
The geologist identified the yellow crystals as randite during the mineral survey.
Samples of randite were collected from the remote mining site for further analysis.
Because of its uranium content, randite is handled with special safety precautions in laboratories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uraninite - a major ore of uranium, usually black in color
