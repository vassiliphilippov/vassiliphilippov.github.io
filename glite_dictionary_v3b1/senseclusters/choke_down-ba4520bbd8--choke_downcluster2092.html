choke down

verb
suppress an emotion or reaction
3.2 6.2
Pronunciation: /tʃoʊk daʊn/ (AmE), /tʃəʊk daʊn/ (BrE)
to suppress or restrain an emotion or reaction, preventing it from being expressed

Examples

To choke down tears
Despite the painful memory, he managed to choke down his tears and continue speaking.
He tried to choke down his disappointment and smiled politely.
She choked down her anger when she heard the unfair remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle up - to keep an emotion or feeling inside rather than expressing it
  • synonym: suppress - to prevent the expression of an emotion or feeling
