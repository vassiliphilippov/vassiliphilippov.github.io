wrencher

noun
tool for tightening
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈrɛntʃə / (BrE)
a tool or machine designed to turn, tighten, or loosen nuts, bolts, or pipe fittings by applying torque

Examples

Heavy wrencher
The mechanic used a wrencher to remove the stubborn bolt from the engine.
She purchased a new electric wrencher for her plumbing business.
A heavy-duty wrencher is essential for working with large pipe fittings.
