appropriately

adverb
in a suitable or proper manner
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊpriətli / (AmE), / əˈprəʊpriətli / (BrE)
in a way that is suitable or proper for a particular situation or purpose

Examples

To act appropriately
Please handle the equipment appropriately to avoid damage.
She responded appropriately to the unexpected news.
The guests were appropriately dressed for the formal dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inappropriately - in a way that is not suitable or proper
  • synonym: properly - in an appropriate or correct manner
