Polynesian

noun
Austronesian language group of Polynesia
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑː.ləˈniː.ʒən / (AmE), / ˌpɒl.ɪˈniː.ʒən / (BrE)
the group of Austronesian languages spoken throughout the islands of Polynesia and parts of the Pacific, including Hawaiian, Maori, and Samoan

Examples

Polynesian languages
Linguists study the similarities between different Polynesian languages to trace their origins.
Many Polynesian words have been borrowed into English, especially in the Pacific region.
The Polynesian languages include Maori, Hawaiian, and Tongan.
