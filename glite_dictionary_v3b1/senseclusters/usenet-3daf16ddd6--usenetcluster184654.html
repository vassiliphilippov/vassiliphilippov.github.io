Usenet

proper noun
early online network
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːzˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈjuːzˌnɛt / (BrE)
a global distributed system for sharing messages and files online, used especially before the rise of modern internet forums

Examples

Usenet newsgroups
Many early internet communities formed around Usenet newsgroups in the 1980s and 1990s.
Before social media, people shared ideas and files on Usenet.
Some archives of Usenet discussions are still accessible online today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forum - an online platform for discussion
  • synonym: newsgroup - an online discussion group within Usenet
