puckery

adjective
wrinkled or astringent
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌkəri / (AmE), / ˈpʌkəri / (BrE)
having a wrinkled, tightened, or drawn appearance or causing a sensation of tightness, especially in the mouth

Examples

A puckery mouth
The unripe persimmon left a puckery feeling in my mouth.
Her lips looked puckery after tasting the sour candy.
The fabric had a puckery texture after being washed in hot water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astringent - causing a dry, puckered feeling in the mouth
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, unwrinkled surface or sensation
