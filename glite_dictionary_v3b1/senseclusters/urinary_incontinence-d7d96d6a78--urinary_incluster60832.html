urinary incontinence

noun
inability to control urination
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrəˌnɛri ɪnˈkɑntɪnəns / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪnəri ɪnˈkɒntɪnəns / (BrE)
the inability to control the flow of urine, resulting in involuntary urination

Examples

Urinary incontinence problem
After her surgery, she temporarily suffered from urinary incontinence.
Medications and physical therapy can help manage urinary incontinence in many patients.
Some older adults experience urinary incontinence as a result of weakened pelvic muscles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continence - ability to control the excretory functions
  • synonym: incontinence - inability to control bodily functions, especially urination or defecation
