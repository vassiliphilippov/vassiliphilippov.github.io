waah

interjection
exclamation of surprise
Pronunciation: / wɑː / (AmE), / wɑː / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express surprise, amazement, or admiration

Examples

Waah at fireworks
When she saw the fireworks for the first time, she exclaimed, "waah!"
"Waah, that magician's trick was unbelievable!" said the amazed child.
He opened the gift and shouted, "waah! This is exactly what I wanted!"
