endocytic

adjective
relating to endocytosis
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving endocytosis, the process by which cells take in material from outside by engulfing it

Examples

Endocytic pathway
The endocytic pathway is essential for nutrient uptake in cells.
Researchers studied the endocytic mechanisms involved in virus entry into human cells.
Certain drugs can interfere with endocytic processes, affecting how cells absorb substances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endocytosis - the process by which a cell takes in material by engulfing it
  • antonym: exocytic - relating to exocytosis, the process of releasing material from a cell
