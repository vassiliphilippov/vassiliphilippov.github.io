singlehood

noun
state of being single
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəlˌhʊd / (BrE)
the state of being single, especially not being married or in a romantic relationship

Examples

Embrace singlehood
After her divorce, she embraced singlehood and focused on her career.
Some people choose singlehood to enjoy more independence and freedom.
He found that singlehood allowed him to travel and explore new hobbies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
