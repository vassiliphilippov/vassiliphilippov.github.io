Ancient Greek

noun
Greek language of ancient times
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈɡrik / (AmE), / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈɡriːk / (BrE)
the form of the Greek language used before and during classical antiquity, prior to the Roman Empire
