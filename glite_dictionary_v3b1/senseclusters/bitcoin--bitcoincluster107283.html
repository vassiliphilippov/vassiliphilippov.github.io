bitcoin

noun
digital currency system
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtˌkɔɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɪtˌkɔɪn / (BrE)
a digital currency that operates without a central authority and is used for online transactions and investment

Related Terms

  • antonym: cash - physical money in the form of coins or banknotes
  • synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital currency that uses cryptography for security
  • antonym: fiat money - government-issued currency not backed by a physical commodity
