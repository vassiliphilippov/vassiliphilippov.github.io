level crossing

noun
where railway and road cross at same level
8.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlev.əl ˈkrɔː.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈlev.əl ˈkrɑː.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlev.əl ˈkrɒs.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈlev.əl ˈkrɔː.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
A place where a railway and a road cross at the same level, usually with barriers or gates that close when a train passes.

Examples

A level crossing gate
