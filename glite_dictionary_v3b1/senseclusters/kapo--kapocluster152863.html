kapo

noun
collaborator with oppressors
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpəʊ / (BrE)
a derogatory term for a person from an oppressed group who collaborates with oppressors by enforcing their rules or punishing their own people

Examples

A kapo collaborator
During the occupation, he was called a kapo for helping the authorities punish his neighbors.
Some survivors refused to speak to anyone who had served as a kapo in the camps.
The term kapo is still used today to accuse someone of betraying their own group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaborator - a person who cooperates with an enemy or oppressor
  • antonym: resister - a person who opposes or fights against authority or oppression
  • synonym: traitor - a person who betrays their own group or country
