unbillable

adjective
not chargeable
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪləbəl / (BrE)
not able to be charged to a client or customer for payment

Examples

Unbillable hours
The hours spent on administrative tasks were marked as unbillable by the accounting department.
Lawyers often have to track both billable and unbillable time for their records.
Training sessions are usually considered unbillable and cannot be invoiced to clients.

Related Terms

  • antonym: billable - able to be charged to a client or customer
