protea

noun
African shrub with cone-like flower heads
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtiə / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtɪə / (BrE)
a genus of tropical African shrubs with rigid leaves and dense, cone-shaped, colorful flower heads

Examples

A protea flower
Many species of protea are endemic to South Africa's fynbos region.
The protea is prized for its striking, long-lasting blooms in floral arrangements.
The gardener planted a row of proteas to add vibrant color to her yard.
