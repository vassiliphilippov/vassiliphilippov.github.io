Serbia

proper noun
historical region in Yugoslavia, home of Serbs
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜr.bi.ə / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.bi.ə / (BrE)
A historical region in central and northern Yugoslavia, inhabited by Serbs since the 6th and 7th centuries; now primarily part of the modern state of Serbia.
