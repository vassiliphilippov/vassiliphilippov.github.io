Examples

Lanterns flame brightly
During the festival, lanterns flame brightly along the riverbank.

Synonyms

  • blaze
    3.4 5.71/m

  • burn
    2.3 8.39/m
    burn means producing heat or damage by fire over time, while a flame is the visible, bright part of that fire.
