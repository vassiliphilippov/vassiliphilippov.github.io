nonsubmergible

adjective
not able to be submerged
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈmɝː.dʒə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈmɜː.dʒɪ.bəl / (BrE)
Not able to be submerged or sunk under water.

Examples

Nonsubmergible materials
Only nonsubmergible materials were used to construct the floating platform.
The rescue boat needed to be nonsubmergible to ensure the safety of those on board.
This type of plastic drum is nonsubmergible and will always float to the surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: submersible - capable of being submerged
