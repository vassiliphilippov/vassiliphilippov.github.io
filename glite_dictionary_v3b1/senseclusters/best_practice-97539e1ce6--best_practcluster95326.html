best practice

noun
recommended superior method or technique
Pronunciation: / bɛst ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / bɛst ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
a method or technique that has been generally accepted as superior to alternatives because it produces the best results

Examples

Industry best practice
Following industry best practice can help ensure compliance with regulations.
Teachers often share their best practices at educational conferences.
The company implemented several best practices to improve workplace safety.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: standard practice - a usual or commonly accepted method
  • antonym: worst practice - a method or technique considered particularly ineffective or harmful
