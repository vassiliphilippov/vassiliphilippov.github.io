lend oneself

verb
to be suitable or applicable
Pronunciation: / lɛnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / lɛnd wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
(of a thing or person) to be suitable or applicable for a particular purpose or use.

Examples

Lends itself to use
Her writing style lends itself to a wide range of genres, from fiction to journalism.
The park's design lends itself beautifully to outdoor concerts and public gatherings.
This material lends itself to easy cleaning, making it ideal for busy kitchens.

  • synonym: suit - to be appropriate for a particular purpose
