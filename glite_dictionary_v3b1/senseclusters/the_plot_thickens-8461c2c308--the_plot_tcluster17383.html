Pronunciation: / ðə plɑt ˈθɪkənz / (AmE), / ðə plɒt ˈθɪkənz / (BrE)
used to indicate that a situation is becoming more complicated or interesting, often due to new developments

Examples

The plot thickens suddenly
Every time more information is revealed, the plot thickens for everyone involved.
Just when they thought the mystery was solved, the plot thickens with the arrival of another suspect.
With this unexpected discovery, the plot thickens and the investigation takes a new turn.
