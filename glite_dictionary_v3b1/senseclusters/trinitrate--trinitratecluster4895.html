trinitrate

noun
compound with three nitrates
Pronunciation: / traɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (AmE), / traɪˈnaɪtreɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound that contains three nitrate groups in its structure

Examples

A trinitrate medicine
Nitroglycerin is a well-known trinitrate used to treat heart conditions.
The chemist explained how a trinitrate differs from a dinitrate in its chemical structure.
Some explosives are based on the properties of trinitrate compounds.
