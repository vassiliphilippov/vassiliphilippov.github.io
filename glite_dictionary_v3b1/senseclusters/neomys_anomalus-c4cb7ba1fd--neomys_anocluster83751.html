Neomys anomalus

noun
a species of water shrew
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊˈmɪs əˈnɒmələs / (AmE), / ˌniː.əˈmɪs əˈnɒmələs / (BrE)
A species of water shrew found in parts of Europe, known for its semi-aquatic habits and pointed snout.

Examples

Neomys anomalus water shrew
Researchers tracked the distribution of Neomys anomalus across central Europe.
The Pyrenean Neomys anomalus is adapted to life near streams and rivers.
Unlike its relatives, Neomys anomalus relies heavily on aquatic insects for food.
