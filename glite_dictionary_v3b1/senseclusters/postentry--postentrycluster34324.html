postentry

noun
later record entry
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌɛntri / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌɛntri / (BrE)
an entry made after the original or initial entry, especially in a record, log, or online discussion

Examples

A postentry update
After reviewing the data, she made a postentry in the project log to clarify the results.
The accountant created a postentry to correct the previous mistake in the financial records.
In the online forum, a postentry was added to address new questions from users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addendum - an additional item added at the end of a document
  • degree lower: entry - a written record in a book, log, or database
