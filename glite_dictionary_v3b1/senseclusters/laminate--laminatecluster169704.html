laminate

verb
bond layers to form a material
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmɪˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈlæmɪneɪt / (BrE)
to create a material or object by bonding together several thin layers of material

Examples

To laminate wood panels
Some manufacturers laminate wood veneer onto particle board to create a durable surface.
To improve durability, they decided to laminate several sheets of plastic together.
We laminated the paper to protect it from moisture and wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layer - to arrange or form in layers
