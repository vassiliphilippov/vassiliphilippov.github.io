remedial

adjective
curative; intended to restore health
1.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmidiəl / (AmE), / rɪˈmiːdiəl / (BrE)
intended to cure or restore health; providing a remedy for a disease or medical condition

Examples

Remedial treatment plan
After months of remedial treatment, his health gradually improved.
Certain plants have long been valued for their remedial properties in traditional medicine.
The doctor recommended remedial exercises to help strengthen her injured knee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curative - able to cure disease or restore health
  • antonym: harmful - causing injury or damage; the opposite of curative or beneficial
  • synonym: therapeutic - having healing or curative properties
