going away

noun
the act of departing
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
the act of leaving or departing from a place or person, especially for an extended period or permanently

Examples

A going away party
Her colleagues gave her a gift at her going away celebration.
The going away was harder than he expected, as he left behind old friends.
We're throwing a going away party for Susan before she moves to Australia.
