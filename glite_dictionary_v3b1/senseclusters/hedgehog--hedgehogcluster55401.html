hedgehog

noun
small spiny nocturnal mammal
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdʒˌhɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdʒˌhɒɡ / (BrE)
a small nocturnal mammal with a coat of stiff, sharp spines and a pointed snout, known for rolling into a ball for defense
