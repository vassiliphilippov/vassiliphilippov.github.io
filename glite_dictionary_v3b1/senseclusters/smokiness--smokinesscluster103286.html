smokiness

noun
quality of being smoky
Pronunciation: / ˈsmoʊkinəs / (AmE), / ˈsməʊkinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being like smoke, especially in flavor, smell, or appearance

Examples

The smokiness of meat
The smokiness of the grilled meat added to its appeal.
She enjoyed the subtle smokiness in the flavor of the tea.
The room's smokiness made it hard to see across to the window.
