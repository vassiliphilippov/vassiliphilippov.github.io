light minute

noun
distance light travels in one minute
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪt ˌmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪt ˌmɪnɪt / (BrE)
The distance that light travels in a vacuum in one minute, approximately 18 million kilometers.

Examples

One light minute
A signal from Mars can take several light minutes to reach Earth.
Astronomers often measure vast distances using units such as the light minute.
The sun is about eight light minutes away from our planet.
