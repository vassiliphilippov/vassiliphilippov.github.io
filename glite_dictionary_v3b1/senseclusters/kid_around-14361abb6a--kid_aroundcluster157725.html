kid around

verb
joke or play in a light-hearted way
1.0
Pronunciation: / kɪd əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / kɪd əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to behave in a playful or joking way, not taking things seriously

Examples

To kid around playfully
He always likes to kid around with his colleagues to break the ice.

My brother and I used to kid around for hours when we were children.
Please stop kidding around and focus on your homework.
They were just kidding around, so don't take their comments too seriously.
