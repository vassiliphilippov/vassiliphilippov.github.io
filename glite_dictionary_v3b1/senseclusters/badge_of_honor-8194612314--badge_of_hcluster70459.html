badge of honor

noun
symbol of pride or distinction
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbædʒ əv ˈɑnər / (AmE), / ˈbædʒ əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
A symbol, mark, or act regarded as a source of pride, achievement, or distinction.

Examples

She wears her first rejection letter as a badge of honor in the publishing world.
Years of hard work and dedication are now his badge of honor.
