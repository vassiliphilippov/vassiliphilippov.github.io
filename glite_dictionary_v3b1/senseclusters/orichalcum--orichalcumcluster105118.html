orichalcum

noun
legendary valuable metal
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɪˈkælkəm / (AmE), / ˌɒrɪˈkælkəm / (BrE)
a legendary metal from ancient texts, often described as highly valuable and second only to gold

Examples

Legendary orichalcum metal
Legends say the lost city of Atlantis was built with walls of orichalcum.
Historians debate whether orichalcum was a real metal or simply a myth.
Ancient coins made of orichalcum have been discovered in archaeological sites.
