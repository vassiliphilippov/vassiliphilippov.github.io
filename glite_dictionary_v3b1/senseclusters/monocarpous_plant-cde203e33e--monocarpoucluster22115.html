monocarpous plant

noun
plant that fruits once and dies
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑnəˈkɑrpəs plænt / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈkɑːpəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
A plant that bears fruit only once before dying.
