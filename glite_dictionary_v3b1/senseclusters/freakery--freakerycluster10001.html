freakery

noun
display of oddities
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkəri / (AmE), / ˈfriːkəri / (BrE)
a public display or exhibition of people or things considered strange, unusual, or abnormal, often for entertainment

Examples

Freakery on display
The museum's new exhibit was criticized for its focus on freakery rather than education.
Some people find freakery in old circuses disturbing and exploitative.
The documentary explored the history of freakery in popular entertainment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normality - the state of being usual or expected
  • synonym: spectacle - a visually striking performance or display
