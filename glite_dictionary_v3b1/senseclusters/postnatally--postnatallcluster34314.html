postnatally

adverb
after birth
4.6
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈneɪtəli / (AmE), / pəʊstˈneɪtəli / (BrE)
after birth; relating to or occurring in the period following birth.

Examples

Monitored postnatally care
The doctor monitored the baby's health postnatally to ensure proper development.
Vaccinations are often given postnatally to protect newborns from certain diseases.
The mother received support postnatally to help her recover after childbirth.
