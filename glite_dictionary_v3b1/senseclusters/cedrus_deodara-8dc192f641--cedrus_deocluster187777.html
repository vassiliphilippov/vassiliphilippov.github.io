Cedrus deodara

noun
Himalayan cedar tree species
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛdrəs diːˈoʊdərə / (AmE), / ˈsiːdrəs diːˈɒdərə / (BrE)
A tall Himalayan cedar tree with spreading branches and drooping tips, valued for its ornamental beauty and timber.

Examples

Cedrus deodara tree
Carpenters highly value Cedrus deodara wood for its durability and pleasant aroma.
Many temple courtyards in the Himalayas are adorned with majestic Cedrus deodara trees.
The Cedrus deodara is prized for its graceful, drooping branches and towering height in gardens and parks.
