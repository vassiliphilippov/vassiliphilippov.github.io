swamp pine

noun
pine tree found in wetlands
5.0 4.6
Pronunciation: /swɑːmp paɪn/ /; / /swɔːmp paɪn/ (AmE), /swɒmp paɪn/ /; / /swɔːmp paɪn/ (BrE)
