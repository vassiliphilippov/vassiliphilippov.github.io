Rigelian

noun
fictional Rigel native
9.9
Pronunciation: / raɪˈdʒiːliən / (AmE), / raɪˈdʒiːliən / (BrE)

Examples

Legends say that a Rigelian spaceship once landed in the desert at night.
In the movie, the Rigelian characters have blue skin and advanced technology.
