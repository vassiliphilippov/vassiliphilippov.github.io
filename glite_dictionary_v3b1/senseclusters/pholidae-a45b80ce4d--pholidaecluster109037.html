Pholidae

noun
family of elongated marine fishes (gunnels)
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊ.lɪˌdi /; / ˈfoʊlɪˌdaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfɒl.ɪˌdiː /; / ˈfɒlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of elongated marine fishes (gunnels) in the suborder Blennioidea, found in cold and temperate northern waters
