spry

adjective
moving quickly and lightly
Pronunciation: / spraɪ / (AmE), / spraɪ / (BrE)
(especially of an older person or animal) moving quickly and lightly; agile and energetic.

Examples

A spry old person
Despite his age, the gardener remained remarkably spry and tended to his plants every day.
She was surprisingly spry on the dance floor, spinning and leaping with ease.
The spry puppy dashed through the yard, chasing its tail in circles.
