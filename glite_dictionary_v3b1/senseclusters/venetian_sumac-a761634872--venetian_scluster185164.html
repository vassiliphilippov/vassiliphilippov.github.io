Venetian sumac

noun
Old World shrub with feathery yellowish flower plumes
9.9
Pronunciation: / vəˈniʃən ˈsuːmæk / (AmE), / vɪˈniːʃən ˈsjuːmæk / (BrE)
an Old World shrub (Cotinus coggygria) having large, yellowish, feathery flower plumes that resemble puffs of smoke

Examples

The Venetian sumac shrub
In early summer, the feathery blossoms of the Venetian sumac added texture to the landscape.
She planted a Venetian sumac in her yard for its ornamental plumes and autumn foliage.
The garden's centerpiece was a Venetian sumac, its flowers billowing like clouds of smoke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smoke tree - a shrub or small tree with feathery flower clusters, also called Cotinus coggygria
