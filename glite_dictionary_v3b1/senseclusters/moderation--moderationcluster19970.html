Examples

Synonyms

  • tempering
    5.6 0.02/m
    tempering is a gradual adjustment that tones down extremes, while moderation is the general act of avoiding or reducing excess.
  • mitigation
    5.6 3.77/m
    mitigation focuses on reducing harm or serious impact, while moderation covers lowering intensity or avoiding extremes in general use.
  • abatement
    6.8 1.80/m
    abatement refers to a reduction, often of a problem or nuisance, while moderation is a broader move to lessen intensity.
  • lessening
    3.8 0.63/m
    lessening means a decrease in size or amount, often passive, while moderation implies active control to avoid extremes.
