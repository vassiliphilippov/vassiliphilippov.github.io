neck brace

noun
supportive medical device for the neck
8.8
Pronunciation: / nɛk breɪs / (AmE), / nɛk breɪs / (BrE)
A medical device worn around the neck to provide support and restrict movement, typically used to aid recovery from injury.

Examples

A neck brace device
After his car accident, he had to wear a neck brace for six weeks.
She felt uncomfortable but safe while wearing the neck brace during recovery.
The doctor recommended a soft neck brace to ease her pain.
