assault gun

noun
armored vehicle with a large mounted gun
8.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsɔːlt gʌn/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsɔːlt gʌn/ /; / /ə.ˈsɒlt gʌn/ (BrE)
