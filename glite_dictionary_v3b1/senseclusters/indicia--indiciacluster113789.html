indicia

noun
publication details section
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪʃiə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪsɪə / (BrE)
the printed information in a periodical, such as a magazine or comic, that gives details about the publisher, issue, and legal notices

Examples

Magazine indicia
The indicia at the bottom of the page listed the magazine's publisher and copyright information.
Collectors often check the indicia to confirm the issue number and publication date of a comic book.
Legal notices and publisher details are usually found in the indicia of a periodical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masthead - the section of a newspaper or magazine giving publication details
