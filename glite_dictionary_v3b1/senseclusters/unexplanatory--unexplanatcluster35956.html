unexplanatory

adjective
not explained
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈsplænətɔri /; / ˌʌnɪkˈsplænəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈsplænətəri / (BrE)
not explained or not providing an explanation; unclear or lacking an explanation

Examples

An unexplanatory reason

Her disappearance was completely unexplanatory, leaving her friends and family confused.
The report was criticized for being unexplanatory and lacking important details.
The instructions were so unexplanatory that I could not assemble the furniture.
Her response was brief and unexplanatory, leaving everyone confused.
He gave an unexplanatory statement that did not address the main issue.
