midge

noun
tiny non-biting flying insect
Pronunciation: / mɪdʒ / (AmE), / mɪdʒ / (BrE)
a very small flying insect, often found near water (some types may bite)

Examples

A midge swarm
A swarm of midges hovered above the surface of the pond at dusk.
Although midges resemble mosquitoes, they usually do not bite humans.
Walking by the lake, we were surrounded by clouds of tiny midges.
We wore hats and nets to keep the midges away during our hike by the lake.
In the summer evenings, midges can be a real nuisance near the river.
The campsite was swarming with midges after the rain stopped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gnat - a small flying insect that often bites
  • synonym: mosquito - a small flying insect that bites and sucks blood
