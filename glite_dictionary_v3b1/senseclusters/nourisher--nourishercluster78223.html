nourisher

noun
provider of nourishment
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝːɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈnʌrɪʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that provides nourishment or sustenance

Examples

A nourisher of children
The mother is the main nourisher of her child.
Rain acts as a natural nourisher for the plants in the garden.
Milk is often considered a vital nourisher for young mammals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provider - a person or thing that supplies something needed
  • antonym: starver - someone or something that deprives of food
  • synonym: sustainer - someone or something that supports or maintains life
