assignment

noun
task or duty given to someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪnmənt / (AmE), / əˈsaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a task or duty that someone is given to do, especially by a teacher, employer, or authority

Examples

School assignment
After completing his assignment, he reported back to his commanding officer.
Her latest assignment required her to travel overseas for six months.
The soldier accepted the assignment despite knowing it would be dangerous.
Each student must present their assignment in class tomorrow.
She worked late into the night to finish her assignment.
The teacher gave us an assignment to complete by next week.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: project - an individual or collaborative enterprise that's carefully planned
  • synonym: task - a piece of work to be done
