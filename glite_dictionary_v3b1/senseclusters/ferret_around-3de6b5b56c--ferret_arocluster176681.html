ferret around

verb
to search or hunt for something
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛr.ɪt əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈfɛr.ɪt əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to search or rummage for something in a thorough or inquisitive way, especially among many things or in a confined space

Examples

To ferret around a bag
He had to ferret around in his bag to find his wallet.
I spent an hour ferreting around the garage, looking for the missing toolkit.
She loves to ferret around in old bookstores for rare finds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rummage - to search through something, especially by moving things around
