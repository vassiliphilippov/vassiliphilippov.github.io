lamp

noun
electric lighting device
1.6
Pronunciation: / læmp / (AmE), / læmp / (BrE)
a device that produces light, such as by electricity, oil, or gas, used to illuminate an area

Examples

The lamp in the living room casts a warm, cozy glow.
She turned on the lamp next to her bed to read her book.
The antique lamp in the hallway gives off a warm glow.
We bought a new desk lamp for the study.
