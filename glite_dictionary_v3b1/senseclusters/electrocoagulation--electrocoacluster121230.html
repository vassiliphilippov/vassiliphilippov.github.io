electrocoagulation

noun
electric tissue clumping
8.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.lek.trə.koʊ.ə.ˈɡjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.lek.trə.koʊ.ə.ˈɡuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪ.lek.trə.kəʊ.ə.ˈɡjuː.leɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪ.lek.trə.kəʊ.ə.ˈɡuː.leɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a process that uses electric current to make particles or tissues clump together, often to stop bleeding or for water treatment
