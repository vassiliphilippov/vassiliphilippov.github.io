solar panel

noun
device that converts sunlight to electricity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˌpænəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˌpænəl / (BrE)
a device made up of connected solar cells that converts sunlight into electricity, typically used as a source of renewable energy

Examples

Solar panels on roofs
Many homeowners install solar panels on their roofs to save on energy costs.
The farm reduced its carbon footprint by switching to solar panels for power.
The new building is equipped with solar panels to generate electricity from the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photovoltaic cell - a device that converts sunlight into electric energy
  • degree higher: solar battery - a battery charged by solar energy
