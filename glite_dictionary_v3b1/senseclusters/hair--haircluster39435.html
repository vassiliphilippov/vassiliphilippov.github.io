hair

noun
cloth made from horsehair or camelhair
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɛr / (AmE), / heə / (BrE)
A cloth woven from horsehair or camelhair, used for upholstery or for stiffening garments.

Examples

Hair cloth
A layer of hair provides extra resilience in quality furniture cushions.
The antique chair was upholstered with sturdy hair to ensure durability and shape.
Traditional tailors sometimes use hair as interlining to stiffen jackets.
