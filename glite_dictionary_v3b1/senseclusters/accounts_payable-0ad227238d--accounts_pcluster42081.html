accounts payable

noun
amounts a business owes to suppliers
5.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkaʊnts ˈpeɪ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkaʊnts ˈpeɪ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
money owed by a company to suppliers for goods or services bought on credit, recorded as a liability on the balance sheet
