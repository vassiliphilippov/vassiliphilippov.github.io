stops

noun
a gambling card game using separate suit layouts
9.9 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘ps / (AmE), / stÉ’ps / (BrE)
A gambling card game in which chips are placed on the ace, king, queen, and jack of separate suits laid out from a separate deck; players take turns playing successively higher cards of a suit until the sequence ends, and a player matching a card in the layout wins all the chips on that card.
