neurilemoma

noun
benign tumor of a nerve sheath
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊrɪlɪˈmoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərɪlɪˈməʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor arising from the sheath (fibrous covering) of a peripheral nerve.

Examples

A neurilemoma tumor
A biopsy confirmed that the mass was a benign neurilemoma and not a malignant tumor.
Patients with a neurilemoma often present with a slow-growing, painless lump.
The surgeon removed a neurilemoma from the patient's forearm without damaging the nerve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schwannoma - benign tumor arising from Schwann cells of the nerve sheath
