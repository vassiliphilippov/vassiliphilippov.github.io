Aegilops triuncalis

noun
a species of barbed goatgrass
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.dʒɪ.lɑːps traɪ.ʌn.ˈkæl.ɪs/ (AmE), /ˈiː.dʒɪ.lɒps traɪ.ʌn.ˈkæl.ɪs/ (BrE)
A species of European grass, known as barbed goatgrass, that has become a weed in North America; its sharp-pointed seeds can injure livestock when eaten.
