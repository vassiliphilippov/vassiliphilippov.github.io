preseminal

adjective
before semen production
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɛmɪnəl / (AmE), / priːˈsɛmɪnəl / (BrE)
occurring or existing before the production or release of semen

Examples

Preseminal fluid sample
Scientists collected preseminal fluid samples to analyze for infectious agents.
The study focused on the properties of preseminal secretions in the male reproductive system.
Certain viruses can be detected in preseminal fluid before ejaculation occurs.
