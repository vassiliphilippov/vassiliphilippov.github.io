headrail

noun
top bar for blinds
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌreɪl / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌreɪl / (BrE)
a horizontal bar at the top of a window blind or curtain from which it is hung and operated

Examples

The headrail of a blind
The installer attached the headrail securely before hanging the new blinds.
If the headrail is not level, the blinds may not function properly.
She cleaned the dust off the headrail while doing her spring cleaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curtain rod - a bar from which curtains are hung
  • degree lower: valance - a decorative drapery hung across the top of a window
