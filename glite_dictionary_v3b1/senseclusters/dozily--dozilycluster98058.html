dozily

adverb
sleepily or drowsily
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊzɪli / (AmE), / ˈdəʊzɪli / (BrE)
in a sleepy, drowsy, or inattentive way; lacking energy or alertness

Examples

Blink dozily awake
She blinked dozily at the bright morning sunlight streaming through the window.
The cat stretched dozily on the warm windowsill, barely opening its eyes.
He listened dozily to the teacher, struggling to stay awake after a late night.
He replied dozily to the teacher's question, barely aware of what was being asked.
The cat stretched dozily in the warm sunlight, not ready to wake up yet.
After staying up all night, she moved dozily through her morning routine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertly - in a way that shows you are fully awake and attentive
  • synonym: drowsily - in a sleepy or lethargic manner
  • synonym: sleepily - in a way that shows you are tired or ready to sleep
