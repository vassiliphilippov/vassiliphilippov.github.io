embryological

adjective
of embryo development
Pronunciation: / ˌɛmbrioʊˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɛmbrɪəʊˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the development and formation of embryos or the study of embryos

Examples

Embryological development
The scientist presented her findings on embryological development at the international conference.
Understanding embryological processes is essential for advances in genetics and medicine.
The textbook includes detailed diagrams of embryological stages in various animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embryonic - relating to an embryo or the earliest stage of development
  • antonym: fetal - relating to a fetus, the later stage of development after the embryo
