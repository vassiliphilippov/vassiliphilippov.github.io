radicular

adjective
of a root
Pronunciation: / rəˈdɪkjələr / (AmE), / rəˈdɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or affecting a root, especially of a nerve or tooth

Examples

Radicular nerve pain
The dentist diagnosed a radicular cyst at the base of the patient's tooth.
She suffered from radicular pain radiating down her leg after the injury.
An MRI revealed radicular symptoms caused by a compressed spinal nerve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: root - the part of a plant or tooth that attaches to the ground or body
