rhombencephalon

noun
hindbrain region of the brain
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑːmbɛnˈsɛfəlɒn / (AmE), / ˌrɒmbɛnˈsɛfəlɒn / (BrE)
the hindmost part of the brain that includes the cerebellum and brainstem

Examples

The rhombencephalon part
Damage to the rhombencephalon can affect balance and vital autonomic functions.
In vertebrates, the rhombencephalon develops early in embryonic life.
The rhombencephalon contains structures vital for motor coordination and basic life support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forebrain - the anterior part of the brain
  • synonym: hindbrain - the posterior part of the brain; another term for rhombencephalon
