kick plate

noun
protective bottom panel
Pronunciation: / kɪk pleɪt / (AmE), / kɪk pleɪt / (BrE)
a removable panel at the bottom of an appliance or door that protects it or provides access to internal parts

Examples

Appliance kick plate
The technician removed the kick plate to check the wiring inside the dishwasher.
A stainless steel kick plate protects the bottom of the door from scuff marks.
To clean the filter, you first need to take off the kick plate at the base of the appliance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel - a flat piece forming part of a surface
  • synonym: toe kick - a recessed area at the bottom of cabinets for foot space
