Atakapa

noun
language of the Atakapa people
Pronunciation: / əˈtækəpə / (AmE), / əˈtækəpə / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Atakapa people, formerly found along the Gulf Coast of Louisiana and Texas

Examples

Atakapa language
Few speakers of Atakapa remained by the twentieth century.
Linguists have worked to document the endangered Atakapa language before it disappeared.
The museum showcased recordings and artifacts relating to Atakapa history and language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Tunica - a Native American language geographically and culturally related to Atakapa
