Synonyms

  • attribute
    3.8 30/m
    attribute is used to say that something comes from a particular person or source in analysis, while assign links it to that source.
  • ascribe
    6.8 5/m
    ascribe is a formal verb often used in writing to name a source, while assign is more general and also means to give credit.
  • credit
    3.5 12/m
    credit means publicly acknowledge someone as the source or maker, while assign can mean to attribute something to them.
  • impute
    8.7 1/m
    impute usually blames or charges someone with responsibility, often unfairly, while assign simply says that something comes from a person or source.
