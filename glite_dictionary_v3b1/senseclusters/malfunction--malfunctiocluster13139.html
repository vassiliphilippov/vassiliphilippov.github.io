malfunction

noun
failure to function properly
Pronunciation: / ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
A failure to operate or function normally or properly.

Examples

A malfunction in equipment
A computer malfunction caused the website to go offline for hours.
Pilots are trained to respond quickly if a technical malfunction occurs during flight.
The engine shut down due to a malfunction in the cooling system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breakdown - a mechanical or technical failure
  • synonym: failure - lack of success or inability to function
  • antonym: function - operate or work in the correct way
