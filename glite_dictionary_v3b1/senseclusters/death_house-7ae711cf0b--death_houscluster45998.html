death house

noun
prison section for those awaiting execution
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ haʊs / (BrE)
the section of a prison in which prisoners sentenced to death are held while awaiting execution

Examples

The death house cell
After years in the death house, the inmate was finally granted a retrial.
Journalists were given rare access to the death house to document the conditions inmates face.
The film portrays the harrowing last days of prisoners inside the death house.

  • synonym: death row - the area of a prison housing inmates awaiting execution
