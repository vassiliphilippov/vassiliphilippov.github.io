bulletproofing

verb
to strengthen security
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
to make something stronger or more secure so that it cannot be easily attacked, criticized, or fail

Examples

To bulletproof a system
The engineers are bulletproofing the new bridge to withstand extreme weather conditions.
She spent weeks bulletproofing her business plan before presenting it to investors.
By bulletproofing their security system, the company reduced the risk of cyberattacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortify - to make something stronger or more secure
  • antonym: weaken - to make something less strong or secure
