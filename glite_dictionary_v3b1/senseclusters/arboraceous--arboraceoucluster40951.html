arboraceous

adjective
rich in or covered with trees
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbəˈreɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌɑːbəˈreɪʃəs / (BrE)
abounding in or covered with trees; wooded

Examples

A arboraceous forest
Botanists were attracted to the arboraceous nature of the region.
She preferred to walk in arboraceous surroundings rather than open fields.
The park has an arboraceous landscape with ancient oaks and tall pines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arboreous - relating to or resembling trees; abounding in trees
  • antonym: treeless - lacking trees; without trees
  • synonym: wooded - covered with trees, especially densely so
