lady's man

noun
man popular with women
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiz mæn / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz mæn / (BrE)
a man who is popular with women or who seeks the company of women, often gaining their affection or attention

Examples

A lady's man
Despite being a lady's man, he was looking for a serious relationship.
Everyone knew Jake was a lady's man who always had a date for the weekend.
His reputation as a lady's man preceded him wherever he went.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ladies' man - alternative form: man popular or attractive with women
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who habitually has many short relationships with women
