gingerroot

noun
the rhizome of the ginger plant used as a spice
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪndʒɚˌruːt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒəˌruːt / (BrE)
The pungent, knobby rhizome of the ginger plant, used as a spice or seasoning in cooking, especially in Asian dishes.

Examples

Fresh gingerroot
Fresh gingerroot is often used in stir-fried dishes for its distinctive aroma.
She grated some gingerroot into the soup to add more flavor.
To make the tea, slice a piece of gingerroot and steep it in hot water.
