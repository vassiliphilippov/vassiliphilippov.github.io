Finlandization

noun
neutrality policy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪnləndəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɪnləndʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or policy by which a smaller country or organization limits or adapts its actions to avoid provoking a more powerful neighbor
