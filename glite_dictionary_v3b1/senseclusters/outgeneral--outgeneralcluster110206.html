outgeneral

verb
defeat by superior strategy
4.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /aʊt.ˈdʒen.ə.rəl/ (AmE), /aʊt.ˈdʒen.ə.rəl/ (BrE)

The young commander managed to outgeneral his more experienced rival in the decisive battle.
They tried to predict his moves, but he always seemed to outgeneral them.
