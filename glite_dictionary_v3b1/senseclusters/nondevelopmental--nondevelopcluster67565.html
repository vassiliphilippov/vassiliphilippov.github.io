nondevelopmental

adjective
not involving growth
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˌvɛləpˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
not related to or resulting from growth, progress, or development

Examples

Nondevelopmental issue discussed
The committee discussed several nondevelopmental concerns that were unrelated to student progress.
This medication is used for nondevelopmental disorders that do not affect growth.
The report highlights nondevelopmental factors influencing the outcome of the project.
