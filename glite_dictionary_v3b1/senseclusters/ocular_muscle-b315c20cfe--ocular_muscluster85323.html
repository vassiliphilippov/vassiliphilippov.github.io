ocular muscle

noun
muscle that moves the eyeball
2.9 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.kjə.lər ˈmʌs.əl/ /; / /ˈɑː.kjʊ.lər ˈmʌs.əl/ (AmE), /ˈɒ.kjə.lə ˈmʌs.əl/ /; / /ˈɒ.kjʊ.lə ˈmʌs.əl/ (BrE)
any of the small muscles surrounding the eye that control the movement and rotation of the eyeball

Examples

The ocular muscle movement
Injury to an ocular muscle can limit a person's ability to move their eye in certain directions.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging any ocular muscle during the procedure.
Weakness in an ocular muscle may result in double vision or strabismus.
