pray tell

interjection
expression requesting information or clarification
Pronunciation: / preɪ tɛl / (AmE), / preɪ tɛl / (BrE)
used, often humorously or ironically, to request information or clarification, especially in response to a surprising or doubtful statement

Examples

Pray tell me
Pray tell, how do you plan to solve this problem?
Pray tell, what time do you expect to arrive?
If you didn't eat the cake, pray tell, who did?
