garb

noun
distinctive style of clothing
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrb / (AmE), / ɡɑːb / (BrE)
clothing of a particular type or style, especially for a specific occasion or role.

Examples

Traditional garb
Monks are easily recognized by their brown garb and simple sandals.
She arrived at the costume party in medieval garb, complete with a velvet cloak.
The chef wore his traditional garb while preparing the meal.
