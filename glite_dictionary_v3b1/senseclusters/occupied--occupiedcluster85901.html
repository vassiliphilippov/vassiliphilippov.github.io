occupied

adjective
being held, filled, or in use
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkjupaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒkjʊpaɪd / (BrE)
(of a space, seat, or position) being held, filled, or in use by someone.

Examples

Occupied seat
Every seat was occupied when the performance started.
She found her favorite table occupied by another group.
The restroom is currently occupied, so you'll have to wait a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vacant - not filled, occupied, or in use
