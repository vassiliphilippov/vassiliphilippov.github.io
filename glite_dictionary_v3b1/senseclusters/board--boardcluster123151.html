board

noun
food or meals, especially with lodging
Pronunciation: / bɔrd / (AmE), / bɔːd / (BrE)
food provided in return for payment, especially as part of accommodation; meals in general.

Examples

Board and lodging
He worked on the farm in exchange for board and lodging.
Many students choose a plan that includes both room and board.
The hotel charges extra for board in addition to the room rate.
