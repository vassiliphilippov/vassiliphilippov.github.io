monarchy

noun
system ruled by a monarch
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnərki / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəki / (BrE)
a system of government in which a country is ruled by a monarch, such as a king or queen, who usually inherits their position

Examples

A monarchy system
Britain still maintains a constitutional monarchy with a ceremonial royal family.
Many European nations were once governed by a monarchy with absolute power.
The country transitioned from a monarchy to a democracy after the revolution.
