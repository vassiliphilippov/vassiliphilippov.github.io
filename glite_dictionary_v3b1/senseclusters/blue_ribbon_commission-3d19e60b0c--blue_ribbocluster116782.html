blue ribbon commission

noun
independent expert investigatory group
8.4
Pronunciation: / blu ˈɹɪb.ən kəˈmɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / blu ˈɹɪb.ən kəˈmɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an independent group of distinguished experts and leaders appointed to investigate or make recommendations on a significant public issue

Examples

A blue ribbon commission formed
A blue ribbon commission was established to reform the education system.
The findings of the blue ribbon commission were published in a comprehensive report last year.
The government formed a blue ribbon commission to examine the causes of the financial crisis.
