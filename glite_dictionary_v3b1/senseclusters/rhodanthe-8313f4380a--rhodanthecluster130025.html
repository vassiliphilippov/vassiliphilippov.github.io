Rhodanthe

noun
genus of xerophytic herbs and shrubs
Pronunciation: / roʊˈdænθi / (AmE), / rəʊˈdænθi / (BrE)
A genus of xerophytic herbs and shrubs native to South Africa and Australia, sometimes included in the genus Helipterum.

Examples

Rhodanthe plants
Botanists have debated whether to classify certain species under Rhodanthe or Helipterum.
Many species of Rhodanthe are prized for their attractive and colorful flowers.
The arid landscapes of Australia are home to several Rhodanthe species.
