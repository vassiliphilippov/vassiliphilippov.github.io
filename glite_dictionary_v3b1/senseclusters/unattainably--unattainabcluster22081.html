unattainably

adverb
in an impossible-to-achieve manner
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈteɪ.nəb.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈteɪ.nəb.li / (BrE)
In a way that is impossible to achieve or reach.

Examples

Unattainably high goals
After the new policy, promotions seemed unattainably rare for junior staff.
She set her standards so unattainably high that nobody could satisfy her expectations.
The mountain peak rose unattainably above the clouds, beyond any climber's reach.
