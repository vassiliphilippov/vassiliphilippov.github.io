schizophrenically

adverb
inconsistently; erratically
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈfriːnɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is extremely inconsistent or marked by rapidly changing, contradictory attitudes or behaviors

Examples

Schizophrenically changing plans
The government responded schizophrenically to the crisis, changing its policies every few weeks.
Her opinions on the project shifted schizophrenically from strong support to harsh criticism.
The team's performance was schizophrenically up and down throughout the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consistently - in a regular and unchanging way
  • synonym: erratically - in an unpredictable or inconsistent way
