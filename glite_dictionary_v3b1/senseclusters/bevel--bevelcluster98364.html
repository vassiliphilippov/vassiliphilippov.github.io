bevel

noun
tool for marking or measuring angles
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛvəl / (AmE), / ˈbɛvəl / (BrE)
A hand tool with two hinged arms used for marking or measuring angles of any size.

Examples

A bevel tool
A bevel can be set to any angle, making it a versatile tool for craftsmen.
She adjusted the bevel before marking the line on the wood.
The carpenter used a bevel to measure the precise angle for the frame.
