muster in

verb
enroll into the armed forces
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌstə ɪn / (BrE)
to formally enroll or enlist someone into the armed forces.

Examples

To muster in soldiers
He was officially mustered in as a private last week.
Many young men were mustered in at the beginning of the war.
The sergeant mustered in the new recruits at sunrise.
