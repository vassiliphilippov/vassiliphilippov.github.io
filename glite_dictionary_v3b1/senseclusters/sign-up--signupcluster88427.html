sign-up

adjective
relating to registration
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪn ʌp / (AmE), / ˈsaɪn ʌp / (BrE)
relating to or used for the process of registering or enrolling for something, especially before a noun.

Examples

Sign-up form
The company is offering a sign-up bonus to attract new employees.
Please complete the sign-up form before attending the event.
They launched a sign-up campaign to increase membership in the club.

Related Terms

  • synonym: registration - the act of enrolling or signing up for something
