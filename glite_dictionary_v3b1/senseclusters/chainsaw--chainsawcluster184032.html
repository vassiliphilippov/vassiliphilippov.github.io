chainsaw

noun
portable power saw with a rotating chain
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪn.sɔ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪn.sɔː / (BrE)
a portable power tool with a set of teeth attached to a rotating chain, used for cutting wood or other materials

Examples

A chainsaw tool
Before operating a chainsaw, it is important to wear protective gear.
He used a chainsaw to cut through the thick branch blocking the road.
The sound of a chainsaw echoed through the forest as the workers cleared trees.
