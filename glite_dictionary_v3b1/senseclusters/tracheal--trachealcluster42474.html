tracheal

adjective
of or relating to the trachea
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪkiəl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪkɪəl / (BrE)
relating to, resembling, or functioning like the trachea (windpipe).

Examples

Tracheal system
Certain insects have a unique tracheal system for respiration.
Swelling in the tracheal region can make breathing difficult.
The doctor examined the patient's tracheal tube for any signs of blockage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trachea - the windpipe; a tube that connects the larynx to the bronchi
