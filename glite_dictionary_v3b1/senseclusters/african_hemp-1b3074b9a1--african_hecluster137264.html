African hemp

noun
South African shrub with hairy leaves and white flowers
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.rɪ.kən hɛmp / (AmE), / ˈæf.rɪ.kən hemp / (BrE)
A large shrub native to South Africa with many hairy branches, large hairy leaves, and clusters of conspicuous white flowers.

Examples

African hemp shrub
In some regions, African hemp is used as a natural hedge due to its dense growth.
The African hemp is often grown in gardens for its ornamental white flowers.
You can recognize African hemp by its large, conspicuously hairy leaves and branches.
