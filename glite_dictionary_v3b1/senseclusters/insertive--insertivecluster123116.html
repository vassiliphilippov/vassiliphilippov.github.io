insertive

adjective
performing penetration
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɜrtɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːtɪv / (BrE)
describing a sexual partner who performs penetration or insertion during sexual activity

Examples

Insertive partner
In some studies, the risk of infection is higher for insertive partners than for receptive partners.
The survey asked participants whether they preferred an insertive or receptive role during sexual activity.
Medical guidelines often distinguish between insertive and receptive behaviors when discussing transmission risks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: receptive - describing a sexual partner who receives penetration
