hooflike

adjective
resembling a hoof in texture or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈhʊfˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a hoof, especially in having a hard, horny texture

Examples

Hooflike hard texture
After years of walking barefoot, he developed thick, hooflike calluses on his soles.
The fossil showed evidence of hooflike structures, hinting at its evolutionary history.
The lizard's feet had a hooflike appearance, adapted for walking on rocky terrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoof - the hard foot of an ungulate animal
