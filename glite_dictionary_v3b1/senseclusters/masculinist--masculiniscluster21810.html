masculinist

adjective
favoring male roles
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskjəlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmæskjʊlɪnɪst / (BrE)
emphasizing or supporting traditional male qualities, roles, or perspectives, often to the exclusion of others

Examples

Masculinist attitudes
The article criticized the company's masculinist culture for discouraging female participation in leadership roles.
Some traditional sports are often seen as promoting a masculinist view of strength and competition.
Her research explores how masculinist attitudes influence workplace dynamics and gender equality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - supporting equal rights for women
  • synonym: patriarchal - relating to a system controlled by men
