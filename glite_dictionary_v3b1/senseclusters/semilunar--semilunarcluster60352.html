semilunar

adjective
crescent-shaped; half-moon shaped
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈluːnɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈluːnə / (BrE)
shaped like a half-moon or crescent; resembling the new moon in shape.

Examples

A semilunar shape
She admired the semilunar windows that adorned the old cathedral.
The artist drew a semilunar curve to represent the horizon at dusk.
The valve gets its name from its semilunar shape, resembling a small crescent moon.
