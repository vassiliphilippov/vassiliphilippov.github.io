damndest

adjective
extremely remarkable
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmdɪst / (AmE), / ˈdæmdɪst / (BrE)
extremely strange, surprising, or remarkable in an unusual way.

Examples

The damndest thing

He came up with the damndest excuse for being late to work.
It was the damndest coincidence that they met again after so many years.
