clopidogrel bisulfate

noun
blood-thinning medication
Pronunciation: / kləˌpɪdəˌɡrɛl baɪˈsʌl.feɪt / (AmE), / kləˌpɪdəˌɡrɛl baɪˈsʌl.feɪt / (BrE)
A medication used to prevent blood clots by inhibiting platelets, commonly prescribed to reduce the risk of heart attack and stroke.
