harsh

adjective
unpleasantly severe or demanding
2.9 8.24/m
Pronunciation: / h…ër É / (AmE), / h…ëÀê É / (BrE)
Unpleasantly severe or demanding, especially of environments, conditions, or treatment.

Examples
