lumberjack

noun
a short, warm outer jacket
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbəˌdʒak / (BrE)
A short, warm outer jacket, typically with a checkered pattern and associated with outdoor work, especially logging.

Examples

A warm lumberjack jacket
He put on his lumberjack before heading out into the chilly morning.
She bought a vintage lumberjack at the thrift store for hiking trips.
The classic red and black lumberjack is coming back into fashion this winter.
