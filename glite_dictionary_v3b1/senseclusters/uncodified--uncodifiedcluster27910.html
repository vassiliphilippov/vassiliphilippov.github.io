uncodified

adjective
not formally coded
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkoʊdɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkəʊdɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not written down or organized into a formal code or system

Examples

An uncodified system
The country's laws remain largely uncodified, making them difficult to interpret.
Because the procedures were uncodified, new employees struggled to follow them correctly.
Much of the traditional knowledge is still uncodified and passed down orally.
The country relies on an uncodified constitution based on tradition.
Many social customs remain uncodified but are still widely respected.
Unlike some nations, their laws are largely uncodified and based on precedent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: codified - arranged or organized into a formal code or system
  • synonym: unstructured - not organized in a specific or formal way
  • synonym: unwritten - not written down; existing only in practice or tradition
