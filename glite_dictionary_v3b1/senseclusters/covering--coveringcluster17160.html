covering

noun
act of protecting by covering
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌv.ɚ.ɪŋ /; / ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ /; / ˈkʌvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of putting something over or around something else to protect, conceal, or coat it

Examples

The covering of plants
A thin covering of snow protected the early spring flowers from the frost.
He used a tarp as a temporary covering for the broken window.
The wound needs a sterile covering to prevent infection.
A thick covering of snow hid the grass from sight.
She used a blanket as a covering for the sleeping child.
The camouflage netting served as a covering to hide the equipment.
A thick covering of paint was applied to the metal fence to prevent rust.
The final covering of varnish protected the wooden table from water damage.
The instructions specify a covering of adhesive before placing the tiles.

  • synonym: cover - something that lies over or encloses another
  • antonym: exposing - the act of making something visible or open
  • synonym: protection - the act of protecting or being protected
