glassblowing

noun
shaping glass with air
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌbloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌbləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the art or process of shaping glass by blowing air into molten glass

Examples

Glassblowing art
The artist demonstrated glassblowing techniques to a group of fascinated students.
She became interested in glassblowing after visiting a studio on vacation.
Traditional glassblowing requires skill, patience, and careful control of temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glassmaking - the process of making objects from glass
  • synonym: sculpture - the art of making three-dimensional forms, especially by carving or shaping materials
