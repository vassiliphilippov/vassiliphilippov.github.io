circulator

noun
event mingler
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who moves among people at an event to socialize, introduce guests, or distribute information or materials

Examples

Event circulator
The circulator made sure everyone at the party felt welcome and included in conversations.
As a skilled circulator, she introduced new guests to each other at the networking event.
A circulator distributed flyers and answered questions during the busy conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attendee - a person who is present at an event
  • synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
