mutually exclusive

adjective
not able to be true or exist together
6.4 2.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôli …™kÀàskluÀês…™v / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôli …™kÀàskluÀês…™v / (BrE)
(of two or more things) not able to exist, occur, or be true at the same time.

Examples

Mutually exclusive options
The two proposals are mutually exclusive; you cannot implement both at the same time.
Their statements are mutually exclusive, so both cannot be correct.
Winning the gold and silver medals are mutually exclusive for a single athlete in an event.
