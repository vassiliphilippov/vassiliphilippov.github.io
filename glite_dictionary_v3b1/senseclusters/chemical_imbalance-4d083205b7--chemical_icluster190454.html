chemical imbalance

noun
abnormal balance of chemicals in the body
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl ɪmˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl ɪmˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
a disruption or abnormality in the normal balance of chemicals in the body, especially in relation to mental health or brain function

Examples

Research on chemical imbalance has led to new treatments for mental illnesses.
