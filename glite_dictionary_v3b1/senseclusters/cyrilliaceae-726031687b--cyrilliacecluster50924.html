Cyrilliaceae

noun
a family of shrubs and trees (Cyrilla, Cliftonia)
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˌrɪliˈeɪsi.iː / (AmE), / sɪˌrɪlɪˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of shrubs and trees with leathery leaves and small white flowers in racemes, including the genera Cyrilla and Cliftonia
