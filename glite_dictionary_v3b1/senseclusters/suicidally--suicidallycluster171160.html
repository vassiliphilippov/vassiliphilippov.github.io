suicidally

adverb
in a suicidal manner
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːɪˈsaɪdəli / (AmE), / ˌsuːɪˈsaɪdəli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely dangerous to oneself, either literally wanting to end one's own life or acting with reckless disregard for one's own safety

Examples

Drive suicidally fast
He drove suicidally fast, ignoring all the warnings from his friends.
After the tragic news, she spoke suicidally about her future.
The character in the novel acted suicidally when faced with overwhelming grief.
He drove suicidally fast through the narrow mountain roads, ignoring all warnings.
The climber acted suicidally by attempting the ascent without any safety equipment.
She spent money suicidally, not caring about the consequences for her future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefully - in a way that avoids danger or harm
  • synonym: despairingly - in a way that shows a loss of hope
  • synonym: recklessly - in a way that shows a lack of care about danger or consequences
