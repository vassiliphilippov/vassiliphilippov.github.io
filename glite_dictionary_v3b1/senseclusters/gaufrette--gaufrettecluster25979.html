gaufrette

noun
waffle-cut potato chip
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈfrɛt / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈfrɛt / (BrE)
a thin, crisp slice of potato cut in a waffle or lattice pattern and fried until crunchy
