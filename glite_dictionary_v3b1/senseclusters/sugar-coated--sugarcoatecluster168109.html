sugar-coated

adjective
covered with sugar or syrup
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊɡɚ ˌkoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʊɡə ˌkəʊtɪd / (BrE)
Covered with a layer of sugar or syrup, typically for flavor or preservation.

Examples

Sugar-coated strawberries
I bought some sugar-coated strawberries at the market yesterday.
The chef prepared delicious sugar-coated almonds as a dessert treat.
Traditional Turkish delight is often sugar-coated to prevent sticking.
