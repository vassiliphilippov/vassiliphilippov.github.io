snowshoer

noun
person using snowshoes
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌʃuːər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌʃuːə / (BrE)
a person who travels over snow using snowshoes

Examples

An experienced snowshoer
The experienced snowshoer led the group through the quiet, snowy forest.
Each snowshoer carried a backpack with water and snacks for the long trek.
As a beginner snowshoer, she quickly learned how to walk on deep snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiker - a person who walks long distances in the countryside
  • synonym: skier - a person who travels over snow on skis
