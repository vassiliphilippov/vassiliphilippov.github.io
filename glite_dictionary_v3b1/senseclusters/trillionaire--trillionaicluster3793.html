trillionaire

noun
person with trillion wealth
Pronunciation: / trɪl.jəˌnɛr / (AmE), / trɪl.jəˌneə / (BrE)
a person whose wealth is at least one trillion units of currency, such as dollars or euros

Examples

A trillionaire person
The company founder became a trillionaire after the value of his shares skyrocketed.
Many people cannot imagine what life would be like as a trillionaire.
The world's first trillionaire is expected to emerge in the coming decades.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: billionaire - a person whose wealth is at least one billion units of currency
  • degree lower: multimillionaire - a person whose wealth is several million units of currency
