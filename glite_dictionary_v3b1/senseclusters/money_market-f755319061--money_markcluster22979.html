money market

noun
DeFi lending/borrowing platform
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈmɑːr.kət/ (AmE), /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌn.ɪ ˈmɑː.kət/ (BrE)
(in cryptocurrency and decentralized finance) a smart-contract platform or protocol that allows users to lend, borrow, or supply digital assets and earn interest, typically via on-chain lending pools.

Examples

Crypto money market
Investors moved funds into the money market on the DeFi platform to earn interest.
The protocol's money market experienced heavy withdrawals after the flash loan exploit last night.
She integrated a stablecoin into her app's money market so users could supply assets and earn yield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lending pool - a collective pool of supplied crypto assets that borrowers draw from and suppliers earn interest on
  • synonym: lending protocol - a blockchain protocol that enables lending and borrowing of digital assets through smart contracts
