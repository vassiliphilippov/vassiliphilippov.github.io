corn

verb
to make into grains
Pronunciation: / kɔrn / (AmE), / kɔːn / (BrE)
to form a substance into small hard grains, crystals, or granules, especially sugar or gunpowder

Examples

To corn sugar
The factory specializes in corning gunpowder to ensure it burns evenly and safely.
After corning the sugar, the crystals were perfect for decorating the pastries.
Traditional methods of corning salt are still used in some rural areas.
