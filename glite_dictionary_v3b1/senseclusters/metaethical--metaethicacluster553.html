metaethical

adjective
about ethics theory
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈɛθɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈɛθɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of the nature, meaning, or foundations of ethical terms and moral judgments

Examples

Metaethical questions
The philosopher discussed several metaethical questions about the objectivity of moral values.
Her research focuses on metaethical debates concerning the meaning of 'good' and 'bad'.
Students often find metaethical issues more abstract than practical ethical dilemmas.
