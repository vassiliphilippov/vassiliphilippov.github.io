Tiberius

proper noun
second Roman emperor (42 BC–AD 37)
Pronunciation: / taɪˈbɪəriəs / (AmE), / tɪˈbɪəriəs /; / taɪˈbɪəriəs / (BrE)
the second Roman emperor (42 BC–AD 37), successor and stepson of Augustus, known for his military leadership and later autocratic rule

Examples
