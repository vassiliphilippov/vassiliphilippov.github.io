Tiberius

proper noun
second Roman emperor (42 BC–AD 37)
9.4
Pronunciation: /tɪ.ˈbɪr.ɪ.əs/ /; / /taɪ.ˈbɪr.ɪ.əs/ (AmE), /tɪ.ˈbɪə.ri.əs/ /; / /taɪ.ˈbɪə.ri.əs/ (BrE)
