freestanding

adjective
independent; not affiliated
4.5 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / \u02ccfri\u02d0\u02c8st\u00e6nd\u026a\u014b / (AmE), / \u02ccfri\u02d0\u02c8st\u00e6nd\u026a\u014b / (BrE)
(of an institution, facility, or business) independent and not affiliated with, attached to, or controlled by a larger organization or network.

Examples

A freestanding clinic
The city opened a freestanding urgent care clinic to reduce hospital admissions.
They launched a freestanding research institute unaffiliated with any university or corporate sponsor.
As a freestanding bookstore, the shop survives by community events and careful curation.
