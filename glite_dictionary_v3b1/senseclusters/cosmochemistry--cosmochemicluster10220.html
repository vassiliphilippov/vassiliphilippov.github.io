cosmochemistry

noun
chemistry of the universe
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
the scientific study of the chemical composition and processes of matter in the universe

Examples

Cosmochemistry study
Researchers in cosmochemistry analyze meteorites to learn about the early solar system.
Advances in cosmochemistry have revealed the origins of elements found in distant stars.
A career in cosmochemistry often involves studying samples collected from space missions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astrochemistry - the study of chemical processes in space
  • synonym: geochemistry - the study of the chemical composition of the Earth
