vetiver

noun
fragrant tropical grass
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛtɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛtɪvə / (BrE)
a tall tropical grass whose fragrant roots are used in perfumes and for preventing soil erosion

Examples

Vetiver grass roots
The roots of vetiver are often used to make essential oils for perfumes.
Farmers plant vetiver to help prevent soil erosion on steep hillsides.
Many luxury soaps contain the distinctive scent of vetiver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemongrass - a tropical grass with a lemon scent used in cooking and perfumes
