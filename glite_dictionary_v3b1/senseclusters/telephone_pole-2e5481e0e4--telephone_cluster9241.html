telephone pole

noun
pole supporting telephone wires
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn poʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn pəʊl / (BrE)
a tall, usually wooden or concrete pole used to support telephone wires above the ground

Examples

A telephone pole outside
A flock of birds rested on the telephone pole outside my house.
He accidentally crashed his car into a telephone pole at the corner.
The workers replaced the damaged telephone pole after the storm knocked it down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: utility pole - a pole that supports overhead utility wires, including those for electricity and communications
