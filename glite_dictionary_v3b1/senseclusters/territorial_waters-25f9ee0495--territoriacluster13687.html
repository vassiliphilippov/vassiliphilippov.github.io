territorial waters

noun
sea under a country's jurisdiction
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈtɔriəl ˈwɔtɚz / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl ˈwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the area of sea around a country's coast recognized as under that country's jurisdiction, usually extending up to 12 nautical miles from shore

Examples

Territorial waters of a country
By law, foreign vessels cannot fish freely in our territorial waters.
Disputes sometimes arise over where territorial waters begin and end.
Fishing boats were detained for operating within another country's territorial waters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: international waters - areas of the sea not under any country's jurisdiction
  • synonym: maritime boundary - the dividing line between the territorial waters of neighboring states
