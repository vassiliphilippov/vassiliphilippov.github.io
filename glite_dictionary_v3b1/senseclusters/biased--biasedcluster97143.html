biased

adjective
unfairly favoring one side
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪəst / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəst / (BrE)
unfairly preferring or favoring one person, group, or side over others; not neutral or impartial

Examples

Biased opinion
It's difficult to get a fair opinion from someone who is already biased.
She admitted that her judgment was biased by her personal feelings.
The news report was clearly biased toward one political party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partial - favoring one side over another; biased
  • antonym: unbiased - not favoring any side; impartial
