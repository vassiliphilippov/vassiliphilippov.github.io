unfaced

adjective
without a finished surface
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪst / (BrE)
not having a finished, covered, or decorated surface; lacking a facing or outer layer

Examples

Unfaced wall
The unfaced wall showed the bare bricks underneath.
They installed insulation on the unfaced side of the panel.
The builder recommended covering the unfaced surface to protect it from moisture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or decorated; exposed
  • antonym: faced - having a finished or covered surface
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or lacking a final surface
