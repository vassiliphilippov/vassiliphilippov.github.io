squadron

noun
naval group of ships
7.4 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈskwɑː.drən/ (AmE), /ˈskwɒ.drən/ (BrE)
a naval unit consisting of a group of ships detached from the fleet for a particular mission or task

Examples

Naval squadron
A squadron of destroyers was detached to support the amphibious assault.
After completing its mission, the squadron rejoined the main fleet at the harbor.
The admiral ordered the squadron to patrol the eastern coastline for enemy activity.
