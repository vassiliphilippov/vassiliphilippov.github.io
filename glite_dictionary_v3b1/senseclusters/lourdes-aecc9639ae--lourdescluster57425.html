Lourdes

proper noun
Catholic pilgrimage site
7.5
Pronunciation: / lʊrdz / (AmE), / lʊədz / (BrE)
a town in southwestern France famous as a Catholic pilgrimage site, especially known for Marian apparitions and its reputed healing waters
