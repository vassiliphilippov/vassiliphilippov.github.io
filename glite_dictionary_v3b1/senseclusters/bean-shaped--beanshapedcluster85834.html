bean-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape of a bean
Pronunciation: / ˈbin ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈbiːn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape that resembles a bean; curved and elongated with rounded ends.

Examples

Bean-shaped leaves
He admired the bean-shaped leaves of the plant in his garden.
The architect designed a bean-shaped pool for the backyard.
The bacteria have a distinctive bean-shaped appearance under a microscope.
