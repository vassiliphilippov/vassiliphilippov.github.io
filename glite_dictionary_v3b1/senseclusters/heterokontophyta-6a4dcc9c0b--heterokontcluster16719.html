Heterokontophyta

noun
a group of algae with unequal flagella
9.9
Pronunciation: /het.ə.roʊ.kɑːn.tə.ˈfaɪ.tə/ (AmE), /het.ə.rəʊ.kɒn.tə.ˈfaɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a group of algae with chlorophyll a (and often c) and flagella of unequal lengths; sometimes used instead of 'Chrysophyta' in classification

Examples

Heterokontophyta algae group
Heterokontophyta are distinguished by their chloroplast pigments and the presence of unequal flagella.
