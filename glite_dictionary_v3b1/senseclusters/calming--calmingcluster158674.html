calming

noun
act of soothing or making calm
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making something or someone less agitated, angry, or restless; soothing or appeasing.

Examples

The calming of anger
Crowd control often involves the calming of anger before it escalates.
Meditation is a useful technique for the calming of anxiety and stress.
The teacher focused on the calming of the students after the fire drill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitation - the state of being anxious, nervous, or disturbed
  • synonym: soothing - the act of making someone or something calm or less agitated
