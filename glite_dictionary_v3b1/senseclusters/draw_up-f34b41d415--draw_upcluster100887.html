draw up

verb
stop a vehicle after moving
Pronunciation: / drɔː ʌp /; / drɔ ʌp / (AmE), / drɔː ʌp / (BrE)
to bring a vehicle to a stop or for a vehicle to come to a stop, especially at a specific place

Examples

The taxi drew up
As soon as the taxi drew up, she hurried outside to meet it.
Our bus drew up at the station right on time.
The limo drew up outside the theater, waiting for the guests to arrive.
He drew up his car in front of the hotel and quickly got out.
The taxi drew up outside the train station, waiting for passengers.
We watched as the limousine drew up beside the red carpet.
