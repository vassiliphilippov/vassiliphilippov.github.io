French door

noun
a door with glass panels along its length
8.1
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ dɔr / (AmE), / frɛntʃ dɔː / (BrE)
a door, usually one of a pair, with glass panes extending for most of its length

Examples

A French door with glass
She opened the French door and stepped onto the balcony.
The garden could be accessed through French doors at the back of the house.
They installed elegant French doors to let more natural light into the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patio door - a door, often made of glass, leading to a patio or garden
