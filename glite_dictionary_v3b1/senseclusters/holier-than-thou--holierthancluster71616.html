Synonyms

  • self-righteous
    7.8 2.62/m
    self-righteous is a common adjective for people who feel morally superior, while holier-than-thou is a snide idiom about that attitude.
  • sanctimonious
    8.5 1.04/m
    sanctimonious shows a false, showy piety to impress others, while holier-than-thou describes a smug sense of moral superiority.
  • pharisaical
    9.9 0.04/m
    pharisaical sounds formal and old-fashioned, often implying religious hypocrisy, while holier-than-thou is a modern, mocking way to show moral pride.
  • pietistic
    9.9 0.00/m
    pietistic focuses on exaggerated religious devotion and strict piety, while holier-than-thou refers to general moral superiority and self-righteous attitude.
