silklike

adjective
resembling silk in smoothness and shine
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a smooth, soft, and lustrous quality that resembles silk

Examples

Silklike smooth texture
His voice had a silklike smoothness that captivated the audience.
She wore a silklike dress that shimmered in the sunlight.
The cat's silklike fur was a pleasure to touch.
