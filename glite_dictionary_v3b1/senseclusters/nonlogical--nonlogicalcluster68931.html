nonlogical

adjective
not logical
7.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not based on or relating to logic; lacking logical reasoning or structure

Examples

Nonlogical reasoning
The committee rejected the proposal because it was based on nonlogical assumptions.
Her explanation seemed nonlogical and failed to convince anyone in the room.
It is important to avoid nonlogical thinking when making important decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illogical - not logical or reasonable
  • antonym: logical - using clear and sound reasoning
