jager

noun
light infantry soldier
Pronunciation: / ˈjeɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈjɑːɡə / (BrE)
a member of a light infantry unit, especially in German-speaking armies, known for speed and marksmanship

Examples

A jager soldier
The jager units were known for their marksmanship and agility on the battlefield.
A skilled jager could move quickly through forests and engage the enemy from a distance.
During the parade, several veterans wore the traditional green uniforms of the jager regiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infantryman - a soldier who fights on foot
  • synonym: rifleman - a soldier armed with a rifle
