treble

verb
sing the highest part in choral music
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛbəl / (AmE), / ˈtrɛbəl / (BrE)
(of a singer) to sing the treble (highest) part in choral music.

Examples

When rehearsing, she enjoys trebling alongside the other sopranos.
