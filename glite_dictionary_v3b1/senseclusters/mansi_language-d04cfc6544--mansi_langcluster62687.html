Mansi language

noun
Ugric language of western Siberia
Pronunciation: / ˈmænsi ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmansi ˌlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an Ugric language closely related to Hungarian, spoken by the Mansi (Vogul) people of western Siberia

Examples

Mansi language speakers
He documented local folktales in the Mansi language during his fieldwork in Siberia.
Linguists have studied the Mansi language to trace its connections with Hungarian.
The Mansi language is considered endangered, with only a small number of fluent speakers remaining.
