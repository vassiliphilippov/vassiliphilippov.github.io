deco

noun
1920s–30s design style with geometric forms
3.8 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈde.koʊ/ (AmE), /ˈde.kəʊ/ (BrE)
a style of design popular in the 1920s and 1930s, characterized by stylized forms, bold geometric shapes, and luxurious materials, often associated with architecture and decorative arts

Examples

Classic deco style
His apartment features bold geometric patterns reminiscent of classic deco design.
Many collectors seek out vintage deco jewelry and furniture.
The lobby of the old theatre was decorated in a striking deco style.
