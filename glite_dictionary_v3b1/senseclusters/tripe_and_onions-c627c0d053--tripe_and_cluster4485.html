tripe and onions

noun
dish of stewed tripe and onions
Pronunciation: / traɪp ənd ˈʌnjənz / (AmE), / traɪp ən ˈʌnjənz / (BrE)
a traditional dish made from stewed tripe and onions, often regarded as old-fashioned or unappetizing

Examples

Traditional tripe and onions
Many people today find tripe and onions too strong in flavor for their taste.
She prepared a hearty meal of tripe and onions for her visiting relatives.
The pub near my house still serves traditional tripe and onions every Thursday.
