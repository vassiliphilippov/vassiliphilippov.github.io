pseudonymously

adverb
using a false name
Pronunciation: / suːˈdɑːnɪməsli / (AmE), / sjuːˈdɒnɪməsli / (BrE)
in a way that uses a false or assumed name instead of one's real name

Examples

To write pseudonymously
The novel was released pseudonymously to keep the author's identity secret.
She often wrote articles pseudonymously to avoid public attention.
Many artists have published their work pseudonymously throughout history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymously - without revealing one's name or identity
