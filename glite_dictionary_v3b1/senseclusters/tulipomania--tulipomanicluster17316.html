tulipomania

noun
speculative financial bubble
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtuːlɪpoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌtjuːlɪpəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a period of extreme enthusiasm or financial bubble, especially the 17th-century Dutch craze for tulip bulbs, marked by rapidly rising prices and public excitement
