unsterile

adjective
not sterile
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɛrəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɛraɪl / (BrE)
not free from germs, bacteria, or other living microorganisms; not sterile

Examples

Unsterile medical tools
The doctor warned that using unsterile equipment could cause serious infections.
Food left uncovered in the open air may become unsterile and unsafe to eat.
He realized the wound was treated with an unsterile bandage, increasing the risk of complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contaminated - containing harmful substances or germs
  • antonym: sterile - completely free from germs or microorganisms
