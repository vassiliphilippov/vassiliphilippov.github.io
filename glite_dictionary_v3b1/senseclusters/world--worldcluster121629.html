world

noun
everything that exists; the universe
Pronunciation: / wɝːld / (AmE), / wɜːld / (BrE)
the whole of everything that exists; all of space, time, matter, and energy considered as a single entity

Examples

The world exists
Many people wonder whether life exists elsewhere in the world.
Some scientists devote their careers to understanding how the world began.
The mysteries of the world have fascinated humans for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: universe - all of space and everything in it, including stars, planets, and galaxies
