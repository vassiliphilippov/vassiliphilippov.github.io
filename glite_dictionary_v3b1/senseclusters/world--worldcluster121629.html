everything that exists; the universe

Examples

Synonyms

  • universe
    3.2 61/m
    universe means all space and everything in it, while world often refers to Earth or the everyday reality people live in.
  • macrocosm
    8.9 0.25/m
    macrocosm refers to the whole of existence in a formal, literary way, while world is a common word for total reality.
  • cosmos
    5.8 6.57/m

  • existence
    2.8 62/m
