monochromous

adjective
having only one color
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːkroʊməs / (AmE), / məˈnɒkrəməʊs / (BrE)
Having or appearing to have only one color.

Examples

A monochromous color
He preferred a monochromous scheme for his living room to keep it calm and simple.
Some insects appear monochromous, but their wings may reflect light in hidden colors.
The artist painted the entire landscape in a monochromous blue, creating a surreal effect.
