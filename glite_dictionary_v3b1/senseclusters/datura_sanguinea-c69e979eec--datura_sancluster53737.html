Datura sanguinea

noun
South American shrub with large orange-red flowers
Pronunciation: / dəˈtʊrə sæŋˈɡwɪniə / (AmE), / dəˈtjʊərə sæŋˈɡwɪniə / (BrE)
A South American shrub with large, orange-red trumpet-shaped flowers, often cultivated for its striking appearance; also known as the red angel's trumpet.

Examples

Datura sanguinea plant
Botanists admire Datura sanguinea for both its beauty and its toxic properties.
She planted Datura sanguinea along the walkway for its showy blooms.
The brilliant flowers of Datura sanguinea attract hummingbirds to the garden.
