flunk out

verb
be dismissed from school for poor grades
6.2
Pronunciation: / flʌŋk aʊt / (AmE), / flʌŋk aʊt / (BrE)
to be dismissed from a school or course because of failing grades or unsatisfactory academic performance

Examples

To flunk out of college
If you don't improve your grades soon, you might flunk out of college.
Many students feel pressure not to flunk out during their first year at university.
She was devastated when she learned she would flunk out because of her low scores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fail - not succeed academically or in a test
  • antonym: pass - to succeed in a test or course
