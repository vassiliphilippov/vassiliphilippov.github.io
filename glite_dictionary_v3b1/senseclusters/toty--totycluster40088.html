toty

adjective
unsteady or shaky
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊti / (AmE), / ˈtəʊti / (BrE)
unsteady, shaky, or likely to fall over, especially due to instability

Examples

A toty ladder
The old wooden ladder was so toty that no one dared to climb it.
After years of use, the table became toty and rocked with every touch.
He avoided the toty bridge, fearing it might collapse under his weight.
