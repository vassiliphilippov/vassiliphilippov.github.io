impositional

adjective
printing page arrangement
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the arrangement of pages, type, or images for printing so they appear in the correct order

Examples

Impositional layout
The designer checked the impositional settings before sending the file to the printer.
Proper impositional planning ensures that booklets are assembled in the right sequence.
Mistakes in the impositional process can lead to pages being printed out of order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imposition - the process of arranging pages for printing
