ex nihilo

adjective
from nothing
Pronunciation: / ɛks ˈniːhɪloʊ / (AmE), / ɛks ˈniːhɪləʊ / (BrE)
created or made from nothing, without using any preexisting materials or sources

Examples

Ex nihilo creation
The artist claimed her concept was created ex nihilo, with no outside influences.
Some philosophers debate whether the universe could have arisen ex nihilo.
The company developed a new technology ex nihilo, rather than building on existing designs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: derivative - copied or developed from something else
  • synonym: original - existing from the beginning; not derived from something else
