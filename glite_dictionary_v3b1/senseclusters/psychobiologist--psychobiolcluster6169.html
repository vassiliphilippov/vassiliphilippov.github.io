psychobiologist

noun
biological behavior scientist
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies how biological processes influence behavior and mental functions

Examples

A psychobiologist studies behavior
The psychobiologist conducted experiments to understand how hormones affect stress responses.
As a psychobiologist, she investigates the links between brain chemistry and emotions.
Many psychobiologists work in research labs at universities or hospitals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neuroscientist - a scientist who studies the nervous system
  • synonym: psychologist - a scientist who studies the mind and behavior
