peacefully

adverb
in a calm, non-violent way
1.3 6.92/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpisf…ôlli / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀêsf…ôli / (BrE)
In a calm and quiet way, without violence, disturbance, or conflict.

Examples

To peacefully protest
Protesters marched peacefully through the city, carrying signs and singing songs.
She peacefully drifted off to sleep as the rain tapped against the window.
The two countries resolved their disagreement peacefully without any armed conflict.
