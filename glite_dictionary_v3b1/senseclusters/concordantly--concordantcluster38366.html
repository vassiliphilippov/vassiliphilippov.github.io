concordantly

adverb
in parallel alignment
9.7
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɔrdəntli / (AmE), / kənˈkɔːdəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that is parallel to or continuous with surrounding rock layers or structures, especially in geology.

Examples

Concordantly layered rocks
The magma moved concordantly between the sedimentary layers, creating a sill.
Geologists observed that the new rock formation developed concordantly with the existing strata.
The mineral veins run concordantly to the surrounding rock, indicating a gradual formation process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discordantly - in a way that is not in agreement or alignment
  • synonym: parallelly - in a way that is parallel
