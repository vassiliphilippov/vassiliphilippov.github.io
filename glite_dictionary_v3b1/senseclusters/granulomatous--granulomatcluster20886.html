granulomatous

adjective
characterized by granulomas (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræn.jəˈloʊ.mə.təs / (AmE), / ˌɡran.jʊˈləʊ.mə.təs / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by granulomas (small areas of inflammation in tissue, often found in certain diseases)

Examples

Granulomatous disease
Sarcoidosis is a granulomatous disease affecting multiple organs.
The biopsy revealed a granulomatous reaction in the lung tissue.
The patient showed signs of a granulomatous inflammation after infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granuloma - a small area of inflammation in tissue
