inaptness

noun
lack of appropriateness or suitability
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæptnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈaptnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being unsuitable or inappropriate for a particular purpose or situation

Examples

The inaptness of choice
Critics pointed out the inaptness of the plot twist in the final chapter.
She apologized for the inaptness of her response during the interview.
The inaptness of his comments made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone present.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aptitude - natural ability or suitability
  • synonym: inappropriateness - the quality of being unsuitable or improper
