monkey jacket

noun
waist-length formal officers' jacket
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
A waist-length jacket, often tapering to a point at the back, traditionally worn by officers at formal dinners in the mess.

Examples

A monkey jacket uniform
He admired the crisp cut and polished buttons of the captain's monkey jacket.
The naval officer put on his monkey jacket before heading to the evening's formal dinner.
Vintage clothing shops sometimes carry a well-preserved monkey jacket from the early twentieth century.
