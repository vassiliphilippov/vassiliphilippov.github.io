unsorry

adjective
without regret
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɑːri / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɒri / (BrE)
not feeling or showing regret or remorse for something done or said

Examples

She stays unsorry
Despite the criticism, she remained unsorry for her decision.
He was completely unsorry about missing the meeting and offered no explanation.
They seemed unsorry even after realizing the mistake they had made.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remorseful - feeling or showing regret for wrongdoing
  • synonym: unapologetic - not showing regret or remorse for actions or words
