hieroglyphically

adverb
in the manner of hieroglyphs
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪrəˈɡlɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or resembling hieroglyphs; by means of or using hieroglyphs.

Examples

Written hieroglyphically
Instructions were hieroglyphically encoded to conceal their true meaning.
Some symbols on the wall were hieroglyphically arranged to form a complex story.
The message was hieroglyphically inscribed on an ancient tablet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hieroglyphic - written in or relating to hieroglyphs
