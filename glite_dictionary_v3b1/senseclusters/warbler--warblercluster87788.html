warbler

noun
small songbird
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrb.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.blə / (BrE)
A small, active songbird known for its musical or varied singing.

Examples

A yellow warbler
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare species of warbler in the marsh.
Many warblers migrate thousands of miles each year to find suitable breeding grounds.
The warbler perched on a branch, filling the early morning with its cheerful song.

  • synonym: songbird - a bird known for singing musical songs
