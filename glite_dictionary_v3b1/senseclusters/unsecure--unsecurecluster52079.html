unsecure

verb
to unfasten or loosen
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to release something from being fastened, fixed, or held in place; to unfasten or loosen.

Examples

To unsecure the latches
Before opening the hatch, you must unsecure all the safety latches.
The workers carefully unsecured the beams so the structure could be dismantled.
Please unsecure your seatbelt after the vehicle has come to a complete stop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loosen - to make something less tight or fixed
  • antonym: secure - to fasten or fix something firmly in place
  • synonym: unfasten - to open or undo something that is fastened
