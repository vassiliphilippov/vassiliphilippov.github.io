photostable

adjective
resistant to light
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈsteɪbəl / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈsteɪbəl / (BrE)
not easily changed, broken down, or degraded when exposed to light, especially sunlight

Examples

Photostable medication
This medication is photostable, so it does not lose effectiveness when stored in sunlight.
Scientists developed a photostable dye for use in outdoor paints and fabrics.
The sunscreen contains a photostable ingredient that remains effective in sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lightfast - not fading or changing when exposed to light
  • antonym: photosensitive - easily affected or changed by light
