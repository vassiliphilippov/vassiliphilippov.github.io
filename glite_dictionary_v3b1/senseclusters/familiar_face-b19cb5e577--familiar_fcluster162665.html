familiar face

noun
recognizable or known person
Pronunciation: / fəˈmɪljɚ feɪs / (AmE), / fəˈmɪliə feɪs / (BrE)
a person whose appearance is known and easily recognized, typically because of previous encounters or acquaintance

Examples

A familiar face appears
After moving to the new city, seeing a familiar face brought her comfort.
At the conference, he searched for a familiar face among the attendees.
It was reassuring to spot a familiar face in the crowded hall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stranger - a person whom one does not know or recognize
