hang together

verb
to be connected or form a coherent whole
2.6
Pronunciation: / hæŋ təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / hæŋ təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to be logically or meaningfully connected as a single unit; to form a coherent or unified whole

Examples

The story hangs together
Her explanation did not hang together with the facts we know.
The novel's various storylines hang together remarkably well.
Without evidence, his argument simply doesn't hang together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohere - to stick together; be united
  • antonym: fall apart - to fail to remain connected or coherent
