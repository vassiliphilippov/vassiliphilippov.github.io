Indri

noun
large Madagascan short-tailed lemur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndri / (AmE), / ˈɪndri / (BrE)
a large, short-tailed lemur of Madagascar with thick, silky fur in black, white, and fawn colors

Examples
