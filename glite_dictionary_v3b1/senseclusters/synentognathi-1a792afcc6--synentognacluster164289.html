Synentognathi

noun
order of spineless-finned fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˌnɛntəgˈneɪθaɪ / (AmE), / sɪˌnɛntɒgˈneɪθɪ / (BrE)
an order of fishes characterized by spineless fins, including species such as needlefishes, sauries, flying fishes, and halfbeaks
