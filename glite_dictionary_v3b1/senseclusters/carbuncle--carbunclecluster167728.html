carbuncle

noun
large skin infection with multiple pus openings
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêrÀåb å≈ãk…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêb å≈ãk…ôl / (BrE)
A severe, painful infection of the skin composed of a cluster of boils, with several openings for the discharge of pus.

Examples

Painful carbuncle infection
A carbuncle often needs to be drained by a medical professional to prevent further infection.
She suffered from a painful carbuncle on her shoulder that took several weeks to heal.
The doctor diagnosed the swollen, red lump on his neck as a carbuncle requiring immediate treatment.
