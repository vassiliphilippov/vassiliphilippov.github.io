beggary

noun
the state of being a beggar
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛɡəri / (AmE), / ˈbɛɡəri / (BrE)
the state of being extremely poor and having to beg for money or food

Examples

Extreme beggary
Centuries ago, war and famine drove many into beggary across the region.
His devotion to charity began after he witnessed the beggary in the city streets.
The collapse of the business left the family on the brink of beggary.
He fell into beggary after losing his job and home.
Street beggary is still a common sight in many large cities.
The city's efforts to curb beggary have sparked debate among local residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: begging - the act of asking for money or food as charity
  • degree higher: charity - voluntary giving to those in need
  • synonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • antonym: wealth - an abundance of valuable possessions or money
