causelessly

adverb
without reason
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔzləsli / (AmE), / ˈkɔːzləsli / (BrE)
in a way that has no cause or reason

Examples

Dismissed causelessly
He was dismissed causelessly from his job, with no explanation given.
The dog began barking causelessly in the middle of the night, startling everyone.
She felt anxious causelessly, unable to find any reason for her worry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbitrarily - in a way based on random choice or personal whim
  • antonym: justifiably - in a way that can be shown to be right or reasonable
