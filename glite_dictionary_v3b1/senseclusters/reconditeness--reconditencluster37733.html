reconditeness

noun
quality of being obscure or hard to understand
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑndɪtnɪs /; / rɪˈkɑn.dɪt.nəs / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒndɪtnɪs /; / rɪˈkɒn.dɪt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being difficult to understand, obscure, or very complex
