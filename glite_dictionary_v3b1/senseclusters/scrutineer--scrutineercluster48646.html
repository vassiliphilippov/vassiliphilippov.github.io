scrutineer

noun
person who examines votes at an election
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌskruːtəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌskruːtəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who observes and examines votes during an election to ensure fairness and legality

Examples

Election scrutineer
A scrutineer checked each ballot carefully for any signs of tampering.
Serving as a scrutineer at the election gave him insight into the voting process.
The candidates both appointed a scrutineer to oversee the counting of votes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: observer - a person who watches an event or activity for accuracy
