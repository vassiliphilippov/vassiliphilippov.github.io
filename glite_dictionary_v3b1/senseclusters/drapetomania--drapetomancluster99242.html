drapetomania

noun
urge to escape
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdreɪpɪtoʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌdreɪpɪtəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for a strong urge to escape or leave a place or situation

Examples

Travel drapetomania
After months of lockdown, she joked she had drapetomania and needed to go travelling.
His drapetomania made it hard for him to stay in one job for long.
Whenever he felt trapped by routine, a sense of drapetomania would push him to seek adventure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restlessness - the inability to stay still or content in one place
  • synonym: wanderlust - a strong desire to travel or explore
