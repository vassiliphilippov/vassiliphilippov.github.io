creaser

noun
tool for making folds
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsər / (AmE), / ˈkriːsə / (BrE)
a tool or machine used to make a line or fold in material such as paper, leather, or cardboard

Examples

A creaser tool

A leather creaser helps make precise lines for stitching.
She bought a new creaser for her bookbinding projects.
