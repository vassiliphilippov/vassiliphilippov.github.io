remembrancer

noun
one who reminds
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈmem.brən.sər/ /; / /rə.ˈmem.brən.sər/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈmem.brən.sə/ /; / /rə.ˈmem.brən.sə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that reminds someone of something, or a historical official who reminded others of their duties

Examples

A remembrancer of the past
The old church bell served as a remembrancer of the town's history.
He kept a photograph as a remembrancer of his childhood home.
In medieval England, a remembrancer was responsible for reminding officials of their duties.
