hypothyroid

noun
person with hypothyroidism
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈθaɪrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəʊˈθaɪrɔɪd / (BrE)
a person who has an underactive thyroid gland, resulting in hypothyroidism

Examples

A hypothyroid patient

Many hypothyroid patients benefit from daily medication to manage their symptoms.
