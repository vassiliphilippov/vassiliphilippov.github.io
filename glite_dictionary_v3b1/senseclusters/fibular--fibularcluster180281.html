fibular

adjective
of the fibula
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪbjələr / (AmE), / ˈfɪbjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the fibula, the outer and thinner bone of the lower leg

Examples

Fibular fracture
The athlete suffered a fibular fracture during the soccer match.
Doctors checked the fibular nerve for any signs of damage after the accident.
A fibular graft was used in the reconstructive surgery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tibial - relating to the tibia, the inner and larger bone of the lower leg
