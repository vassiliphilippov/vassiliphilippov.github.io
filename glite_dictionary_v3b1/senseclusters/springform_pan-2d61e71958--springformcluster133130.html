springform pan

noun
removable baking pan
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋˌfɔrm pæn / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋˌfɔːm pæn / (BrE)
a round baking pan with removable sides and a latch, used to easily release cakes without damaging them

Examples

A springform pan latch
She carefully released the cheesecake from the springform pan to keep the edges smooth.
A springform pan is ideal for baking delicate desserts like tortes and cheesecakes.
He bought a new springform pan to try making a layered mousse cake at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cake pan - a pan used for baking cakes
  • antonym: loaf pan - a rectangular pan used for baking bread or loaf cakes
