education reform

noun
changes to improve an education system
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒəˈkeɪʃən rɪˈfɔrm / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈkeɪʃn rɪˈfɔːm / (BrE)
the process of making changes to an education system to improve its effectiveness

Examples

Education reform process
Many politicians have promised education reform, but little has changed in the classroom.
Significant education reform is needed to address the challenges faced by disadvantaged communities.
Teachers and parents gathered to discuss the impact of education reform on their local schools.
