dressman

noun
male wardrobe consultant
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsmæn / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsmæn / (BrE)
a man who advises clients on clothing choices and styles, acting as a personal stylist or wardrobe consultant

Examples

Personal dressman advice
The dressman selected a suit that perfectly matched the client's personality and event.
She hired a dressman to help her update her wardrobe for the new season.
A skilled dressman can transform your appearance with the right clothing choices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stylist - a person who gives advice on fashion and clothing
