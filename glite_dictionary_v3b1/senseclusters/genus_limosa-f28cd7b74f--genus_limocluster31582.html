genus Limosa

noun
a genus of wading birds (godwits)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs laɪˈmoʊsə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs lɪˈməʊsə / (BrE)
A genus of large, long-billed wading birds known as godwits, found in wetlands and coastal regions.
