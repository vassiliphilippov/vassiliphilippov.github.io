fruitlessly

adverb
without producing results; unsuccessfully
5.9 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfruːt.ləs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈfruːt.ləs.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that does not produce any useful result; without success or achievement.

Examples

Argue fruitlessly
He argued fruitlessly with the manager about the unfair policy.
The team searched fruitlessly for any sign of the missing document.
We waited fruitlessly at the gate for hours, but the train never arrived.
