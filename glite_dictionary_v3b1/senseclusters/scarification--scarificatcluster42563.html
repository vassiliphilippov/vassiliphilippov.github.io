scarification

noun
intentional skin scarring
Pronunciation: / ˌskærəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌskærɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the deliberate creation of scars on the skin for body modification, decoration, or cultural identification
