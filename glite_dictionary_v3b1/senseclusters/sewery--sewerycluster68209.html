sewery

adjective
like a sewer
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːəri / (AmE), / ˈsjuːəri / (BrE)
resembling or having the unpleasant qualities of a sewer, especially in smell or appearance

Examples

A sewery smell
After days of rain, the alley took on a sewery odor that was hard to ignore.
The old pipes gave the kitchen a slightly sewery atmosphere in the mornings.
He wrinkled his nose at the sewery water pooling in the street.

  • synonym: malodorous - smelling very unpleasant
