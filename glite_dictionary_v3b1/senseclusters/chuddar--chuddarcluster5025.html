chuddar

noun
a cloth head covering for women
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌdər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌdə / (BrE)
a large scarf or cloth worn as a head covering, veil, or shawl by Muslim and Hindu women

Examples

A chuddar scarf
Many women wear a chuddar when visiting the temple.
She draped her chuddar over her head before leaving the house.
The colorful chuddar complemented her traditional dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chador - a similar type of large cloth worn as outer garment or veil by women
