Synonyms

  • brachycephalic
    9.9 0.01/m
    brachycephalic is a medical term for a short, broad head, while short-headed is a plain description used more in everyday speech.
  • brachycranial
    9.9 0.00/m
    brachycranial is a technical term for a short, wide skull, while short-headed is a simpler way to describe that head shape.
  • roundheaded
    8.1 0.00/m
    roundheaded describes a broad, rounded head, while short-headed stresses a head that is short and wide rather than just rounded.
  • broad-headed
    8.2 0.00/m
    broad-headed means having a wide head, while short-headed adds that the head is also relatively short, not only wide.
