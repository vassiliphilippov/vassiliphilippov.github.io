Examples

Synonyms

  • Virginia creeper
    5.4 0.04/m

  • Parthenocissus quinquefolia
    9.9 0.00/m
    Parthenocissus quinquefolia is the scientific name for a vigorous vine with palmately compound leaves, while woodbine is the common folk name.

    5.0 0.00/m
