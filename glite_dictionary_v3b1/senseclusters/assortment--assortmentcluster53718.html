assortment

noun
collection of various types
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔrtmənt / (AmE), / əˈsɔːtmənt / (BrE)
a collection of different types of people or things grouped together

Examples

An assortment of snacks
On the shelves was an impressive assortment of rare and antique books.
The bakery offers a wide assortment of pastries every morning.
The gift basket included an assortment of gourmet cheeses, crackers, and chocolates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: selection - a group of things chosen from a larger number
  • synonym: variety - a number of different types of things, especially in the same category
