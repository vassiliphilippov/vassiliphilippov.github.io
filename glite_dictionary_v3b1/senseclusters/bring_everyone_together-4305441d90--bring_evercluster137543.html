bring everyone together

verb
to unite people into a group
1.0
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ ˈɛvriˌwʌn təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / brɪŋ ˈɛvriwʌn təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to unite a group of people, making them feel part of a single community or cause

Examples

To bring everyone together peacefully
Her speech helped bring everyone together after a difficult year.
The festival was created to bring everyone together and celebrate different cultures.
We hope this project will bring everyone together as a community.
