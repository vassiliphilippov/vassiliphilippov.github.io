scaredy-cat

adjective
easily frightened
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛrdi kæt / (AmE), / ˈskeədi kæt / (BrE)
informal; easily frightened or showing fear in situations that are not very dangerous

Examples

Scaredy-cat behavior
My little brother is such a scaredy-cat when it comes to watching scary movies.
Don't be a scaredy-cat; the roller coaster is perfectly safe.
She called herself a scaredy-cat because she was afraid to try bungee jumping.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not easily frightened
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
