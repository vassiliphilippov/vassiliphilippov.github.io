astride

adverb
with a leg on each side
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈstraɪd / (AmE), / əˈstraɪd / (BrE)
with one leg on each side of something (sitting or standing)

Examples

Sitting astride a log
He posed astride a fallen log for the photo.
She rode the horse astride, her posture confident and secure.
The boy sat astride the fence, watching the field.
He stood astride the stream, looking down into the water.
She sat astride the horse, ready to ride.
The boy walked astride the fallen tree, carefully balancing himself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straddle - sit or stand with a leg on each side of something
