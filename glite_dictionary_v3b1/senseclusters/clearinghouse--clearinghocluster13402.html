clearinghouse

noun
information or payment center
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪŋˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪŋˌhaʊs / (BrE)
an organization or place that collects, processes, and distributes information, payments, or transactions between parties

Examples

Bank clearinghouse
The clearinghouse ensures that all payments between the banks are settled accurately and on time.
A national clearinghouse collects and shares data about missing persons with law enforcement agencies.
The organization acts as a clearinghouse for information on environmental regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exchange - a place where things such as information or money are traded or processed
  • synonym: repository - a place where information or items are stored and managed
