lose one's head

verb
become confused or panic
1.7
Pronunciation: / luːz wʌnz hɛd / (AmE), / luːz wʌnz hɛd / (BrE)
to become very upset, confused, or unable to think clearly, especially in a difficult or stressful situation

Examples

Try not to lose your head during the emergency; stay calm and focused.
