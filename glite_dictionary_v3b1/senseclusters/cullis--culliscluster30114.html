cullis

noun
roof gutter; drainage channel
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɪs / (AmE), / ˈkʌlɪs / (BrE)
a groove or channel, especially a gutter in a roof for carrying off rainwater

Examples

Stone cullis groove
The old manor boasted carved stone cullises to channel water away from the walls.
The rainwater flowed along the cullis and off the roof.
Years of debris had blocked the cullis, causing leaks in the attic.
