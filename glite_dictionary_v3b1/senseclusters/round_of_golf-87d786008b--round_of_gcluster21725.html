round of golf

noun
an 18-hole session of golf
Pronunciation: / raʊnd əv ɡɑlf / (AmE), / raʊnd əv ɡɒlf / (BrE)
an occasion of playing an entire game of golf, typically consisting of 18 holes

Examples

A round of golf game
A typical round of golf takes about four hours to complete.
He usually books a round of golf with his colleagues every Friday afternoon.
We played a round of golf at the country club last weekend.
