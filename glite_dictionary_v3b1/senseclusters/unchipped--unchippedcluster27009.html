unchipped

adjective
without chips or cracks
3.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪpt / (BrE)
not having any chips, cracks, or broken pieces; intact and undamaged at the edges or surface

Examples

Unchipped plate
The antique plate was surprisingly unchipped after so many years of use.
Please check that all the glasses are unchipped before setting the table.
He was happy to find the statue unchipped despite the long journey.
