premigration

adjective
before migration
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
happening or existing before migration, especially referring to activities or conditions prior to moving from one place to another

Examples

Premigration health check
The government requires a premigration health screening for all applicants.
They attended a premigration orientation session to prepare for life in a new country.
A premigration assessment helps identify potential challenges before families relocate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postmigration - happening or existing after migration
