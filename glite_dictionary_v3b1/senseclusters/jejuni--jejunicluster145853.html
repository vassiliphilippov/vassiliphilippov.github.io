jejuni

adjective
dull or insipid
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈdʒuːnaɪ / (AmE), / dʒɪˈdʒuːni / (BrE)
lacking in interest, substance, or nutrition; dull or insipid

Examples

Jejuni lecture
The professor's lecture was so jejuni that many students struggled to stay awake.
Critics described the film as jejuni, offering little substance or excitement.
His writing style is often criticized for being jejuni and lacking depth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - not interesting or exciting
  • synonym: insipid - lacking flavor, interest, or excitement
  • antonym: interesting - holding attention or arousing curiosity
