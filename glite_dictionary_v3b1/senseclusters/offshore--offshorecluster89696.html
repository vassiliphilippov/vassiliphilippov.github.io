offshore

verb
to relocate abroad
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈʃɔr / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈʃɔː / (BrE)
to move business operations or jobs to another country, usually to reduce costs

Examples

To offshore jobs
The company decided to offshore its customer service department to save money.
Many manufacturing jobs have been offshored to countries with lower labor costs.
Some critics argue that businesses should not offshore work at the expense of local employment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outsource - to obtain goods or services from an outside supplier
  • synonym: relocate - to move to a new place
