carnitas

noun
Mexican pork dish
4.9
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈnitəs / (AmE), / kɑːˈniːtəs / (BrE)
a Mexican dish of slow-cooked, shredded or chopped pork, often served as a taco or burrito filling

Examples

Mexican carnitas tacos
We ordered carnitas tacos from the food truck for lunch.
The chef prepared homemade carnitas using a traditional Mexican recipe.
I love adding fresh salsa to my carnitas burrito for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulled pork - slow-cooked, shredded pork, often used in sandwiches
