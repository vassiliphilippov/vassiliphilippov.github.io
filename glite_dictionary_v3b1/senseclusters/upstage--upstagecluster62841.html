upstage

verb
attract attention away from someone
2.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /əp.ˈsteɪdʒ/ /; / /ʌp.ˈsteɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ʌp.ˈsteɪdʒ/ /; / /əp.ˈsteɪdʒ/ (BrE)
to attract attention away from someone else, especially by outshining them

Examples

To upstage a friend
He was careful not to upstage his friend at the awards ceremony.
