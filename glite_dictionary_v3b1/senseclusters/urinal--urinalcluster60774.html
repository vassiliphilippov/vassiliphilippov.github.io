urinal

noun
wall-mounted toilet for urinating (men)
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊrɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈjʊərɪn(ə)l / (BrE)
A fixture, typically attached to a wall in a public restroom, used by men for urinating while standing.

Examples

A urinal in restroom
He stopped at the urinal before leaving the restroom.
Please do not throw paper towels into the urinal.
The restroom was clean, and each urinal was well-maintained.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toilet - a fixture for defecation and urination, typically with a seat
