center punch

noun
tool for making indentations in metal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntər pʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɛntə pʌntʃ / (BrE)
a tool with a conical point used to make an indentation in metal to mark the location for drilling or to prevent slipping of a drill bit

Examples

A center punch tool
He used a center punch to mark the spot before drilling the hole into the steel plate.
Make a small indentation with a center punch so the drill bit doesn't wander.
The center punch left a clear mark, ensuring accurate placement for the screws.
