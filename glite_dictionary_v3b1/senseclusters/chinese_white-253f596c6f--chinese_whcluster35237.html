Chinese white

noun
zinc oxide white pigment
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈniz waɪt / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈniːz waɪt / (BrE)
a white pigment made of zinc oxide, used especially in paints and drawing

Examples

Chinese white pigment
Chinese white is often preferred in drawings for its opaque coverage.
Manufacturers gradually replaced lead-based pigments with safer options like Chinese white.
The artist mixed Chinese white with watercolors to lighten the shades.
