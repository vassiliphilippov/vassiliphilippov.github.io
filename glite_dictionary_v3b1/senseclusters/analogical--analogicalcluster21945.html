analogical

adjective
relating to analogy
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌænəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on an analogy; expressing or resembling an analogy.

Examples

Analogical reasoning

Linguists often study analogical patterns in language change.
The scientist used an analogical approach to explain the complex concept.
