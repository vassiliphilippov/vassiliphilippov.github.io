blank out

verb
remove or obscure text in a document
1.1
Pronunciation: / blæŋk aʊt / (AmE), / blæŋk aʊt / (BrE)
to remove or obscure words or information in a document, often for political or sensitive reasons

Examples

To blank out words
Officials decided to blank out sensitive information from the files.
Some names in the document have been blanked out for security purposes.
The classified sections of the report were blanked out before publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: censor - examine and suppress unacceptable parts of publications
  • synonym: redact - edit or prepare official documents, often by removing sensitive information
