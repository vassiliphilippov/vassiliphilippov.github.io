trey

noun
three-point shot
Pronunciation: / treɪ / (AmE), / treɪ / (BrE)
a basketball shot that scores three points

Examples

A long trey
The crowd erupted when she hit a clutch trey in the final seconds.
He practiced his treys every day to improve his long-range shooting.
With the game tied, Marcus attempted a risky trey from the corner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: layup - a close-range basketball shot, usually off the backboard
  • synonym: three-pointer - a basketball shot made from beyond the three-point line
