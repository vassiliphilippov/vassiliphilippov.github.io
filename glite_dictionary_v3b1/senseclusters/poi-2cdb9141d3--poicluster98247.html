POI

noun
point of interest (map marker)
6.1
Pronunciation: / piː.oʊ.aɪ / (AmE), / piː.əʊ.aɪ / (BrE)
(abbr., in mapping and navigation) a specific location, site, or landmark marked on a map or in an app as being of interest to users.

Examples

POIs on map
The travel app highlights nearby POIs like museums, parks, and popular restaurants for tourists.
She added her favorite café as a POI on the map before the road trip.
The GPS searches for nearby POIs to show on-screen directions and local information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landmark - a prominent or well-known feature or building used for navigation or interest
  • synonym: waypoint - a marked intermediate point on a route or map used for navigation
