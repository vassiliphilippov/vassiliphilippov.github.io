overlordship

noun
position or authority of an overlord
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àào äv…ôrÀål…îrd É…™p / (AmE), / Àà…ô äv…ôl…îÀêd É…™p / (BrE)
the position or authority of an overlord; supreme rule or dominion over others, especially in a feudal or hierarchical system.

Examples

The overlordship of kings
During the Middle Ages, overlordship often involved collecting tribute from subordinate rulers.
The dispute arose when a rival lord challenged his claim to the overlordship of the land.
The king asserted his overlordship over the smaller kingdoms in the region.
