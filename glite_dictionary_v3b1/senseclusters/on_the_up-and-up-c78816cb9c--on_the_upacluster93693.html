on the up-and-up

adjective
honest and legitimate
8.3
Pronunciation: / ɑn ði ˈʌp ənd ˈʌp / (AmE), / ɒn ði ˈʌp ənd ˈʌp / (BrE)
Honest and legitimate; conducted in a truthful or lawful way.

Examples
