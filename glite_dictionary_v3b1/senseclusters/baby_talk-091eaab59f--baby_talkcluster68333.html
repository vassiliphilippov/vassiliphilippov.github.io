baby talk

noun
speech of a very young child
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi tɔk / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi tɔːk / (BrE)
the speech or utterances of a very young child learning to talk, often marked by simplified sounds and grammar

Examples

A child's baby talk
He recorded his son's baby talk to remember those early learning moments.
Her daughter's baby talk was full of amusing made-up words and sounds.
Parents often find baby talk both adorable and fascinating as children learn to speak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - incoherent or meaningless speech, especially that of a young child
