arcade video game

noun
type of action video game with simple controls and fast pace
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈkeɪd ˈvɪdioʊ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪd ˈvɪdiəʊ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A video game featuring short levels, simple controls, and rapidly increasing difficulty, often emphasizing quick reflexes and high scores.

Examples

Classic arcade video game
He spent hours mastering retro arcade video games at the local gaming convention.
Many classic arcade video games rely on fast-paced action and quick reflexes to keep players engaged.
The new release was designed in the style of an arcade video game, featuring simple controls and challenging levels.
