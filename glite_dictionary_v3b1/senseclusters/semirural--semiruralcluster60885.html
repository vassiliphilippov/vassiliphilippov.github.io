semirural

adjective
partly rural, partly urban
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈrʊrəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈrʊərəl / (BrE)
having characteristics that are partly rural and partly urban, often located between city and countryside

Examples

A semirural area
They bought a house in a semirural community just outside the city limits.
The school is located in a semirural area, surrounded by fields and small shops.
Living in a semirural neighborhood offers both quiet countryside and easy access to urban amenities.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: rural - relating to the countryside
  • synonym: suburban - relating to an area on the edge of a city
  • degree higher: urban - relating to a city or town
