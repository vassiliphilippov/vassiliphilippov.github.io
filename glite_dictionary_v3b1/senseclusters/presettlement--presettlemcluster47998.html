presettlement

noun
before settlement
8.0
Pronunciation: / priːˈsɛtəlmənt / (AmE), / priːˈsɛtəlmənt / (BrE)
the period or condition before a formal agreement or settlement is reached

Examples

Presettlement phase
The lawyers discussed the presettlement phase before entering negotiations.
All presettlement documents must be reviewed carefully to avoid future disputes.
During the presettlement period, both parties gathered evidence to support their claims.
