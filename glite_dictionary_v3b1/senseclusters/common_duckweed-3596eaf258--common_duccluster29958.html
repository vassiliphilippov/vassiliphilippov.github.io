common duckweed

noun
a widespread small aquatic plant
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëm…ôn Ààd åkÀåwid / (AmE), / Ààk…ím…ôn Ààd åkÀåwiÀêd / (BrE)
a small free-floating aquatic plant (Lemna minor) found in temperate regions outside eastern Asia and Australia, often covering the surface of still or slow-moving water

Examples

Small common duckweed plant

The rapid growth of common duckweed can sometimes block sunlight for underwater plants.
