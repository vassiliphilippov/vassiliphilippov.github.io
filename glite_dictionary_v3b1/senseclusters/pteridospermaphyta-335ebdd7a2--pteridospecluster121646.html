Pteridospermaphyta

noun
group of extinct seed ferns (fossil plants)
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrɪdoʊˌspɜːrməˈfaɪtə /; / ˌtɛrɪˌdoʊˌspɜrməˈfaɪtə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪdəʊˌspɜːməˈfʌɪtə /; / ˌtɛrɪdəʊˌspɜːməˈfaɪtə / (BrE)
a group of extinct seed-producing plants, also called seed ferns, known from fossils and classified as gymnosperms (coextensive with the order Cycadofilicales)
