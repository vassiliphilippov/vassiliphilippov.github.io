Pteridospermaphyta

noun
group of extinct seed ferns (fossil plants)
9.9
Pronunciation: /te.rɪ.doʊ.spɜːr.mə.ˈfaɪ.tə/ (AmE), /te.rɪ.dəʊ.spɜː.mə.ˈfaɪ.tə/ (BrE)
