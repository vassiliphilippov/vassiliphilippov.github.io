iconoclast

noun
person who destroys religious images
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑnəˌklæst / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnəˌklæst / (BrE)
A person who destroys images, especially those used in religious worship.

Examples

Religious iconoclast
During the Byzantine Empire, many iconoclasts destroyed religious artworks in churches.
Some historians believe the rise of iconoclasts led to lasting changes in church decor.
The actions of the iconoclast were considered controversial by many worshippers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: iconophile - a person who supports the veneration of icons
  • synonym: image-breaker - a person who destroys religious images
