papergirl

noun
female newspaper deliverer
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpərˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpəˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl or young woman who delivers newspapers or other printed materials to people's homes or in public places

Examples

A papergirl delivers newspapers
The papergirl woke up early every morning to deliver newspapers before school.
Everyone on the block knew the friendly papergirl who always smiled as she worked.
After a year as a papergirl, she saved enough money to buy a new bicycle.
The papergirl handed out free newspapers at the train station every morning.
During the campaign, a papergirl distributed flyers to people passing by the park.
At the festival, a papergirl gave out event programs to visitors near the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a person who hands out or delivers items to others
  • antonym: newsagent - a person or shop selling newspapers and magazines
  • synonym: paperboy - a boy or young man who delivers newspapers
