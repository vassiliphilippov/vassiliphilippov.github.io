problematic

adjective
uncertain or open to question
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑː.bləˈmæt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌprɒb.ləˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
difficult to deal with, causing problems, or not certain

Examples

A problematic situation
His recovery from the illness is problematic at this point, according to doctors.
The timeline for the project remains problematic and may need revisions.
Whether the new law will have the intended effect is highly problematic.
Finding a reliable solution to the issue proved problematic for the entire team.
It became problematic when nobody could agree on the best course of action.
This experiment yielded some problematic results that scientists struggled to explain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: challenging - demanding effort or skill
  • synonym: difficult - not easy; requiring much effort
  • synonym: questionable - doubtful or open to question
  • antonym: settled - resolved or firmly decided
  • antonym: straightforward - easy to deal with or understand
  • synonym: uncertain - not able to be relied on; not known or definite
