ameloblastic

adjective
from ameloblasts
Pronunciation: / əˌmiːloʊˈblæstɪk / (AmE), / əˌmiːləˈblæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from ameloblasts, the cells responsible for forming tooth enamel

Examples

Ameloblastic tumor
The dentist explained that the ameloblastic tumor originated from cells that form tooth enamel.
Researchers studied the ameloblastic tissue to better understand enamel development.
A rare ameloblastic lesion was discovered during the patient's oral examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enamel - the hard, outer surface layer of teeth
