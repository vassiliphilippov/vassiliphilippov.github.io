crypto

adjective
relating to cryptography
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪptoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪptəʊ / (BrE)
relating to cryptography or the use of cryptographic techniques to secure information or communications

Examples

Crypto security protocols
The company uses crypto protocols to keep customer data safe from hackers.
Many modern apps rely on crypto methods to secure user communications.
Developers must understand crypto standards when building secure payment systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unencrypted - not protected by cryptographic methods
