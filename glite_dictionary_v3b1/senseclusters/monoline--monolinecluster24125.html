monoline

adjective
uniform line thickness
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
having lines of uniform thickness, or relating to a single line of business or activity

Examples

Monoline style
The designer used a monoline approach to give the logo a modern and clean appearance.
Many calligraphy pens are not monoline, as they create strokes of varying thickness.
A monoline insurer specializes in only one type of insurance product.
