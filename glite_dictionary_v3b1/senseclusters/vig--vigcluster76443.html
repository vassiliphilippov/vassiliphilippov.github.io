vig

noun
bookmaker's commission
Pronunciation: / vɪɡ / (AmE), / vɪɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for the commission or fee charged by a bookmaker or lender, especially on bets or loans

Examples

Vig on a bet
The bookmaker kept ten percent as his vig on every losing bet that night.
If you borrow money from a loan shark, the vig can be extremely high.
He asked what the vig was before placing his bet at the casino.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commission - a fee paid to an agent or intermediary for services
  • synonym: vigorish - the fee or commission taken by a bookmaker or loan shark
