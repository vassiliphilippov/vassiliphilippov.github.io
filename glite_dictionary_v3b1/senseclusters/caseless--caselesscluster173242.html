caseless

adjective
without cartridge casing
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsləs / (BrE)
designed to use ammunition that does not have a separate metal or plastic casing

Examples

Caseless ammunition
The new rifle uses caseless ammunition to reduce weight and improve efficiency.
Engineers are developing caseless bullets to make weapons lighter for soldiers.
A caseless design eliminates the need to eject spent cartridges after firing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cased - having a separate casing or cartridge
