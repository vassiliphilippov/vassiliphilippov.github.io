dumbstruck

adjective
unable to speak due to shock or surprise
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈdʌmˌstrʌk / (BrE)
So shocked or astonished that one is temporarily unable to speak.

Examples

Look dumbstruck
After hearing the unexpected announcement, everyone in the room looked dumbstruck.
She was dumbstruck when she opened the door and saw her childhood friend standing there.
The audience sat dumbstruck as the magician made the statue disappear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dumbfounded - greatly astonished or amazed
  • synonym: speechless - unable to speak, often due to strong emotion
  • antonym: unfazed - not perturbed or affected by surprise or shock
