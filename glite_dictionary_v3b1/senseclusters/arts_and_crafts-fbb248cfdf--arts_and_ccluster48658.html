arts and crafts

noun
manual decorative handicraft activities or their movement
Pronunciation: / ɑrts ənd kræfts / (AmE), / ɑːts ənd krɑːfts / (BrE)
activities involving the making of decorative objects by hand, or the decorative design movement emphasizing handmade craftsmanship.

Examples

Arts and crafts activities
Children learn arts and crafts at summer camp every year.
The arts and crafts fair featured handmade pottery, jewelry, and textiles.
The movement sought to revive traditional arts and crafts in response to industrialization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handicraft - skill or activity of making things by hand
