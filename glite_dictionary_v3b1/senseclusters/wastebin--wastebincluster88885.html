wastebin

noun
container for rubbish
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstˌbɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪstˌbɪn / (BrE)
a container for holding rubbish or waste until it is collected

Examples

Office wastebin
Please remember to empty your wastebin before leaving the office.
She threw the empty bottle into the wastebin by the door.
The kitchen wastebin was full and needed to be replaced with a new bag.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dustbin - a British English synonym for wastebin
  • synonym: trash can - a container for holding waste, chiefly American English
