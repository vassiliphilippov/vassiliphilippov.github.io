ouf

noun
wild or eccentric person
9.9
Pronunciation: / uːf / (AmE), / uːf / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who behaves in a crazy, wild, or eccentric way

Examples

He is an ouf
Everyone at the party thought he was an ouf after he started dancing on the table.
My brother can be such an ouf when he gets excited about something.
She acted like a real ouf during the festival, wearing a costume and singing loudly.
