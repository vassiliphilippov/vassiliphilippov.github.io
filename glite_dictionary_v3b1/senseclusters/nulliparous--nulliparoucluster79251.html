nulliparous

adjective
never given birth
9.9
Pronunciation: / nʌˈlɪpərəs / (AmE), / nʌˈlɪpərəs / (BrE)
describing a woman or female animal who has never given birth to offspring

Examples

nulliparous woman
The clinical trial included only nulliparous women to reduce potential complications.
A nulliparous patient may have different health risks compared to someone who has given birth.
Veterinarians noted that the nulliparous mare showed no signs of pregnancy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parous - having given birth to offspring
  • degree higher: primiparous - having given birth once
