Balaamite influence

noun
deceptive or negative influence (biblical allusion)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪləm aɪt ˈɪnflʊəns / (AmE), / ˈbaləm aɪt ˈɪnflʊəns / (BrE)
A negative or deceptive influence, often one that leads people astray, likened to Balaam from the Bible who misled others for personal gain.

Examples

Balaamite influence misleads people
She warned the committee about a Balaamite influence that could undermine their purpose.
Some critics argue that media bias can act as a modern Balaamite influence on public opinion.
The company's sudden policy change was attributed to a Balaamite influence within upper management.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beneficial influence - an influence that is helpful or positive
  • synonym: deceptive influence - an influence that misleads or deceives
