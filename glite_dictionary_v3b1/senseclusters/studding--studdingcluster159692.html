studding

noun
wall framework
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of fitting with studs or the arrangement of studs in a structure, especially in building walls

Examples

Wall studding layout
The builder checked the studding to ensure the wall would be strong enough to support the shelves.
Before hanging the heavy mirror, she located the studding behind the plaster.
Properly spaced studding is essential for a stable and safe house frame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framing - the structure that gives shape and support to a building
  • antonym: paneling - wooden boards or panels used to cover walls
