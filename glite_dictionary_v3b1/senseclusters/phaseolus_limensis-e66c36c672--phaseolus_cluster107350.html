Phaseolus limensis

noun
species of bean plant (Lima bean)
9.1
Pronunciation: / fəˈsiːoʊləs laɪˈmiːnsɪs / (AmE), / fəˈsiːələs lɪˈmiːnsɪs / (BrE)
A species of bean plant, also known as the Lima bean, that produces large flat edible seeds and may grow as a bush or tall vine.

Examples

Phaseolus limensis plant
Phaseolus limensis is widely cultivated for its nutritious seeds in several parts of the world.
Botanists studied the growth habits of Phaseolus limensis in both bush and climbing forms.
The seeds of Phaseolus limensis are often referred to as Lima beans in English-speaking countries.
