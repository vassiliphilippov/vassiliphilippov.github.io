Examples

Synonyms

  • hunched
    4.1 0.2/m
    hunched describes someone with a strongly curved upper back and folded shoulders, while stooped describes a more general bent posture.
  • round-shouldered
    4.8 0.07/m
    round-shouldered refers to shoulders and upper back pushed forward, while stooped describes an overall bent stance often from age or tiredness.
  • round-backed
    5.9 0.01/m
    round-backed means having a curved back like a small hump, while stooped means a forward-bent posture of back and shoulders.
