glissade

noun
controlled mountain slide
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɡlɪˈsɑd / (AmE), / ɡlɪˈsɑːd / (BrE)
a controlled slide down a steep slope, especially on snow or ice in mountaineering

Examples

Snow glissade
After reaching the summit, the group enjoyed a fast glissade down the snowy slope.
The mountaineer demonstrated how to perform a safe glissade with an ice axe.
A glissade can be an efficient way to descend steep, snow-covered terrain.
