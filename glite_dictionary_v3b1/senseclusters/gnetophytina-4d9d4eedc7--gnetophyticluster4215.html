Gnetophytina

noun
a group of gymnospermous plants related to conifers
Pronunciation: / ˌgniː.t̬oʊ.faɪˈtaɪ.nə /; / ˌnɛt̬oʊfaɪˈtiːnə / (AmE), / ˌniː.təʊ.faɪˈtiː.nə /; / ˌniːtəʊfaɪˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a group of gymnospermous plants considered by some scientists as a link between conifers and flowering plants (angiosperms)
