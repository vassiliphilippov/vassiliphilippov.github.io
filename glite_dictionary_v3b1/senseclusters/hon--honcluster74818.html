hon

noun
affectionate address
8.2
Pronunciation: / hʌn / (AmE), / hʌn / (BrE)
an affectionate term used to address someone you care about, such as a partner or close friend

Examples

Hey, hon
Could you pass me the salt, hon?
Thanks for making dinner, hon, it was delicious.
I'll see you after work, hon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dear - a term of endearment for someone you care about
  • synonym: honey - an affectionate term for someone you love
