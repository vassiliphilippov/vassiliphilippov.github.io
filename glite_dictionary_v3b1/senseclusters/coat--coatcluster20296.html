coat

noun
thin layer covering something
Pronunciation: / koʊt / (AmE), / kəʊt / (BrE)
a thin layer of a substance spread over a surface

Examples

A coat of paint
Apply a thin coat of varnish for a smooth finish.
She spread a coat of frosting over the cake.
The table needs another coat of paint to look new again.
