mental exhaustion

noun
extreme tiredness affecting the mind
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.t̬l ɪɡˌzɑs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.tl ɪɡˌzɔːs.tʃən / (BrE)
A state of extreme fatigue affecting one's mental acuity, often resulting from prolonged cognitive activity or emotional stress.

Examples

Severe mental exhaustion
After studying for hours without a break, she felt a deep sense of mental exhaustion.
The team canceled the meeting because mental exhaustion was affecting everyone's performance.
Too much screen time can lead to mental exhaustion and decreased productivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burnout - physical or mental collapse caused by overwork or stress
  • synonym: mental fatigue - temporary inability to concentrate due to prolonged mental activity
  • antonym: physical exhaustion - state of extreme bodily fatigue
