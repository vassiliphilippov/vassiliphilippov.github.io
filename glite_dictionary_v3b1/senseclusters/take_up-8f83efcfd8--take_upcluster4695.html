take up

verb
absorb or soak up
3.9
Pronunciation: / teɪk ʌp / (AmE), / teɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to absorb or soak up something, either physically (like a liquid or gas) or metaphorically (like information)

Examples

The cloth quickly took up the spilled wine.
