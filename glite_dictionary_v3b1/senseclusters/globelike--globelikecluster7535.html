globelike

adjective
spherical in shape
Pronunciation: / ˈɡloʊbˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡləʊbˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having the shape or appearance of a globe or sphere

Examples

Globelike shape
The museum displayed a globelike model of the Earth in the main hall.
She admired the globelike lanterns hanging from the ceiling at the festival.
Raindrops formed globelike beads on the surface of the car after the storm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - having a level surface without curves
  • synonym: round - having a circular or curved shape
  • synonym: spherical - shaped like a sphere
