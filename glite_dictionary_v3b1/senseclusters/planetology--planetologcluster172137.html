planetology

noun
study of planets
Pronunciation: / ˌplænəˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌplænɪˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of planets, including their structure, composition, and processes

Examples

Planetology study
She decided to specialize in planetology after becoming fascinated by Mars.
Advances in planetology have revealed new details about the surfaces of distant exoplanets.
The university offers a course in planetology for students interested in planetary systems.
