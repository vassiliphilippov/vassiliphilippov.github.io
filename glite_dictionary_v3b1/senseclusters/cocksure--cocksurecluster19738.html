cocksure

adjective
excessively confident or arrogant
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑkˌʃʊr / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.ʃʊə / (BrE)
very confident in a way that is arrogant or presumptuous; excessively sure of oneself or one's opinions.

Examples

A cocksure attitude
As a young man, he was as cocksure as he was inexperienced.
He spoke in a cocksure manner, dismissing everyone else's opinions as irrelevant.
Her cocksure attitude quickly alienated her new colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modest - having a humble opinion of oneself; not arrogant
  • synonym: overconfident - excessively confident
