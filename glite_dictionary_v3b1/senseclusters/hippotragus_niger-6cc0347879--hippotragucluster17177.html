Hippotragus niger

noun
large black African antelope with curved horns
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪpəˈtreɪɡəs ˈnaɪɡər / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈtrɑːɡəs ˈnaɪɡə / (BrE)
a large black antelope of East and Southern Africa, known for its sharp backward-curving horns
