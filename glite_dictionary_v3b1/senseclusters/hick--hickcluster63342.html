hick

noun
unsophisticated rural person (often derogatory)
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɪk / (AmE), / hɪk / (BrE)
an unsophisticated or socially backward person from the countryside, often considered unintelligent or uncultured (often used disparagingly)

Examples

A hick from town
He refused to let their hick jokes hurt his confidence in his rural upbringing.
She always felt out of place at university, worried others would see her as just a hick from a small town.
The city folks looked down on him, calling him a hick because of his accent and manners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bumpkin - an awkward and unsophisticated country person
  • antonym: city slicker - a sophisticated person from a city
  • synonym: yokel - an uneducated and unsophisticated person from the countryside
