underprepared

adjective
not ready enough
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərprɪˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəprɪˈpeəd / (BrE)
not sufficiently ready or equipped for an important task or event

Examples

Feeling underprepared for interview
She felt underprepared for the interview and worried she might not answer the questions well.
The team was clearly underprepared for the competition and struggled to keep up with the others.
Because he was underprepared, he found the test much harder than he expected.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prepared - ready or equipped for something
  • synonym: unprepared - not ready or equipped for something
