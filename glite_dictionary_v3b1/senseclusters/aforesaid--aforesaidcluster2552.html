aforesaid

adjective
previously mentioned; spoken of before
6.4
Pronunciation: / əˈfɔrˌsɛd / (AmE), / əˈfɔːsɛd / (BrE)
mentioned or referred to previously; the one just spoken of, especially in legal or formal writing.

Examples

The aforesaid property
All rights regarding the aforesaid property shall revert to the original owner.
The aforesaid parties have agreed to the terms outlined in the agreement.
The contract clearly states that the aforesaid conditions must be met before payment is released.
