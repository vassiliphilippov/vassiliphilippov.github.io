pane of glass

noun
sheet of glass for windows or doors
Pronunciation: / peɪn əv ɡlæs / (AmE), / peɪn əv ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
A flat sheet of glass, usually shaped to fit into a window or door frame.

Examples

A pane of glass
He replaced the cracked pane of glass with a new one.
Sunlight streamed through the clean pane of glass, brightening the entire room.
The stone shattered the pane of glass in the front window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: windowpane - a single sheet of glass in a window
