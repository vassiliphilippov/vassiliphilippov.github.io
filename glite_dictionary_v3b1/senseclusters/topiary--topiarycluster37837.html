topiary

noun
decorative shaped plant
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊpiˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈtəʊpiəri / (BrE)
a bush or tree that has been trimmed and shaped into a decorative form, often found in gardens

Examples

A topiary animal
The grand estate was famous for its elaborate topiary shaped like animals and geometric patterns.
She spent hours each week maintaining the topiary in her front yard.
Visitors admired the garden's topiary, which included a dragon and a peacock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hedge - a row of bushes or small trees planted close together
  • synonym: sculpture - a three-dimensional work of art, often carved or shaped
