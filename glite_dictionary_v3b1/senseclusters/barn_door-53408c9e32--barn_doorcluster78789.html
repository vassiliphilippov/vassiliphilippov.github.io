barn door

noun
large door of a barn
9.2
Pronunciation: / bɑrn dɔr / (AmE), / bɑːn dɔː / (BrE)
a large door at the entrance of a barn, used for moving animals, equipment, or produce in and out

Examples

The barn door opens
Fresh hay was piled high near the open barn door in the sunlight.
He struggled to close the barn door as the wind picked up.
The old barn door creaked loudly on its hinges every morning.
Don't forget to close the barn door after feeding the animals.
The paint on the old barn door was peeling and faded.
They installed a new barn door to replace the broken one.
