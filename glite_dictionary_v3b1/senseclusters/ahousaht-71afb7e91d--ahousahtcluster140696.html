Ahousaht

noun
Indigenous community member
Pronunciation: / əˈhaʊsɑːt / (AmE), / əˈhaʊsɑːt / (BrE)
a person who is a member of the Ahousaht people, an Indigenous community on the west coast of Vancouver Island, Canada

Examples

An Ahousaht elder
An Ahousaht elder spoke to the students about their community's fishing traditions.
The Ahousaht have a rich history of living along the coast of Vancouver Island.
Many Ahousaht participate in cultural ceremonies to preserve their heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Nuu-chah-nulth - an Indigenous people of the Pacific Northwest Coast of Canada
