semiannual

adjective
occurring twice a year
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛm.iˈæn.ju.əl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈanjʊəl / (BrE)
occurring or payable twice each year; happening every six months

Examples

Semiannual payments
Our mortgage payments are semiannual, due every six months.
The semiannual sale attracts hundreds of shoppers each time.
The company issues a semiannual report in both June and December.
