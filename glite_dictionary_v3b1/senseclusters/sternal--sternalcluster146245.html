sternal

adjective
relating to the sternum
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜrnəl / (AmE), / ˈstɜːnəl / (BrE)
of or relating to the sternum (breastbone)

Examples

sternal incision
A sternal incision is often required during open-heart surgery.
He complained of pain in the sternal region when breathing deeply.
The doctor examined the patient for sternal tenderness after the accident.

Related Terms
