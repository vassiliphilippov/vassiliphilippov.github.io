Synonyms

  • put forward
    3.2 4.21/m
    put forward is a common phrase for presenting an idea in discussion, while advance is a more formal verb for proposing it.
  • propose
    3.0 84/m
    propose is a formal verb used to put forward plans or offers, while advance emphasizes offering something for acceptance.
  • suggest
    1.6 151/m
    suggest is a softer, less formal verb for offering an idea for others to consider, while advance implies a stronger proposal.
