advance

verb
put forward for consideration
4.1
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
to put forward or propose something for consideration or acceptance

Examples

To advance an idea
During the debate, she advanced several compelling arguments.
He advanced his proposal at the board meeting for consideration.
The scientist advanced a new theory to explain the phenomenon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propose - to put forward (a plan or suggestion) for consideration
  • synonym: suggest - to mention or recommend something for possible consideration
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove or take back an argument or proposal
