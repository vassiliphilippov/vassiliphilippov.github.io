last-ditch attempt

noun
final, desperate effort
4.4
Pronunciation: / læst dɪtʃ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / lɑːst dɪtʃ əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
a final and desperate effort to achieve something when all else has failed

Examples

A last-ditch attempt
He made a last-ditch attempt to save the business from bankruptcy.
Her last-ditch attempt to convince the jury was passionate but ultimately unsuccessful.
In a last-ditch attempt, the team worked through the night to finish the project.
