intern

verb
work as an intern
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrn / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːn / (BrE)
to work as an intern, especially to gain practical experience in a professional field

Examples

To intern at a hospital
After medical school, he interned at a hospital in New York.
Many students intern at major companies during the summer to gain work experience.
She is interning at a design firm while completing her degree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apprentice - to work as an apprentice
  • degree lower: volunteer - to work without pay, often to gain experience
