blazingly

adverb
very intensely
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a very intense, extremely bright, or obvious way

Examples

The sun shines blazingly
The sun shone blazingly over the desert, making it hard to see.
She finished the race blazingly fast, breaking the previous record.
The fireworks exploded blazingly in the night sky, lighting up the whole area.
The answer was blazingly clear to everyone in the room.
Her talent was blazingly apparent from the very first performance.
It became blazingly obvious that the plan would not work.
