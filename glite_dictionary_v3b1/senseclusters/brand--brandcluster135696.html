brand

noun
a burned or burning piece of wood
Pronunciation: / brænd / (AmE), / brænd / (BrE)
a piece of wood that has been burned or is burning, especially one taken from a fire

Examples

A burning brand
Ashes fell as the old brand crumbled beneath his feet.
He picked up a brand from the hearth to light his way through the night.
The brand glowed with embers at the edge of the dying fire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firebrand - a burning stick taken from a fire
