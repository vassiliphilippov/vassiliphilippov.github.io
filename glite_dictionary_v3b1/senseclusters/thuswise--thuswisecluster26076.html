thuswise

adverb
in this manner
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈðʌsˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈðʌsˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in this way; thus.

Examples

Arranged thuswise on shelf
He arranged the books thuswise on the shelf, with the largest at the bottom.
The instructions said to fold the paper thuswise to create the proper shape.
She decorated the table thuswise, placing flowers in the center and candles at each end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thus - in this way; as a result
