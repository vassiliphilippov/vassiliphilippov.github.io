newscast

noun
news broadcast on TV or radio

A broadcast of news or commentary on current events, typically on television or radio.

Examples

Evening newscast
Our local station's newscast begins at six o'clock every night.
She watches the morning newscast while having breakfast.
The evening newscast covered the latest developments in the election.

Synonyms

  • news program
    1.9 1.32/m
    a news program is a broadcast that reports current events with interviews and commentary, while a newscast names that scheduled news broadcast.
  • news show
    1.0 1.09/m
    a news show is a TV or radio program that reports events and may include lighter segments, while a newscast is a straight news broadcast.
  • news
    1.0 121/m
    news can mean the information about recent events or a program that reports them, while a newscast specifically means a broadcast of that news.
