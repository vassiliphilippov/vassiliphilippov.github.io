Cebuella pygmaea

noun
smallest monkey; Amazon rainforest species
Pronunciation: / sɛˈbjuː.ələ pɪɡˈmiːə / (AmE), / sɛˈbjuː.ələ pɪɡˈmiːə / (BrE)
A species of very small monkey, known as the pygmy marmoset, native to the tropical forests of the Amazon basin.
