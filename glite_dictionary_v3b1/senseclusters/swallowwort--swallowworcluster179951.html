swallowwort

noun
perennial herb with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊˌwɔːt / (BrE)
A perennial herbaceous plant with branched woody stems and bright yellow flowers, especially of the genus Vincetoxicum or related genera.

Examples

Yellow-flowered swallowwort
Gardeners often mistake swallowwort for other wildflowers due to its yellow blossoms.
Some species of swallowwort can spread aggressively and become invasive.
The fields were dotted with swallowwort blooming brightly in early summer.
