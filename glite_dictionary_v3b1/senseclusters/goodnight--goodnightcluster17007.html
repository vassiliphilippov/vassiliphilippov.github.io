goodnight

interjection
nighttime farewell
Pronunciation: / ɡʊdˈnaɪt / (AmE), / ɡʊdˈnaɪt / (BrE)
a polite expression used to wish someone a pleasant night before they go to sleep or leave for the night

Examples

Say goodnight to friends
She waved to her friends and softly said goodnight before heading upstairs.
After the movie ended, everyone exchanged hugs and wished each other goodnight.
He texted his parents to say goodnight before going to bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - a polite expression used when parting
  • antonym: hello - a greeting used when meeting someone
