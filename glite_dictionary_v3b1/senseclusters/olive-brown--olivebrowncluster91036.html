olive-brown

adjective
brown with a greenish tinge
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv ˌbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ˌbraʊn / (BrE)
A brown color with a greenish tinge, resembling the color of green olives.

Examples

Olive-brown color
She chose an olive-brown jacket that complemented her natural complexion.
The artist mixed several paints to achieve the perfect shade of olive-brown for the landscape scene.
The bird's feathers were a distinctive olive-brown color, helping it blend into the forest floor.
