pyrethroid

adjective
containing synthetic insecticide
Pronunciation: / paɪˈriːθrɔɪd / (AmE), / paɪˈriːθrɔɪd / (BrE)
containing, treated with, or relating to pyrethroids, which are synthetic insecticides similar to natural pyrethrins

Examples

Pyrethroid treated crops
The researchers tested the effectiveness of pyrethroid sprays against agricultural pests.
Many mosquito nets are pyrethroid-treated to provide extra protection from bites.
Farmers prefer pyrethroid solutions because they act quickly on insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecticidal - having the quality of killing insects
  • antonym: non-toxic - not harmful or poisonous
