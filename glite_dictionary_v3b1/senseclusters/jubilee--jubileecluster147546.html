jubilee

noun
special anniversary celebration
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːbəˌliː / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːbɪliː / (BrE)
A special anniversary, especially a celebration marking a notable milestone, such as 25, 50, or 60 years.

Examples

A jubilee celebration
Many people attended the church’s silver jubilee celebration.
Queen Elizabeth II celebrated her Platinum Jubilee in grand style.
The city held a jubilee to mark its 100th anniversary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anniversary - the date on which an event took place in a previous year
