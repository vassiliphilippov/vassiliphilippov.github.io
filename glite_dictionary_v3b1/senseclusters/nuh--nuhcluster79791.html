nuh

interjection
informal no
Pronunciation: / nʌ / (AmE), / nʌ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to say no or show disagreement

Examples

Nuh, not today
"Nuh, I don't want to go out tonight," she replied.
When asked if he liked broccoli, the child simply said, "nuh."
I suggested we watch a movie, but he just shook his head and said, "nuh."

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - used to refuse or disagree
  • antonym: yeah - used to express agreement or affirmation
