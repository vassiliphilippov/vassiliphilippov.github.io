microvasculature

noun
smallest blood vessels
8.5
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lə.tʃər/ /; / /mɪ.krə.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lə.tʃər/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lə.tʃə/ /; / /mɪ.krə.ˈvæs.kjʊ.lə.tʃə/ (BrE)

Examples

Human microvasculature

Healthy microvasculature is essential for proper blood flow to all parts of the body.
