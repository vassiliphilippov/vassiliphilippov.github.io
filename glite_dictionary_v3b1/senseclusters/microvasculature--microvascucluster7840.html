microvasculature

noun
smallest blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈvæskjəlˌtʃʊr / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈvæskjʊlətʃə / (BrE)
the network of the smallest blood vessels, including capillaries, arterioles, and venules, in the body

Examples

Human microvasculature
Damage to the microvasculature can affect how tissues receive oxygen.
Researchers are studying how diabetes impacts the microvasculature in the eyes and kidneys.
Healthy microvasculature is essential for proper blood flow to all parts of the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capillary - a very small blood vessel connecting arterioles and venules
  • degree higher: vasculature - the arrangement of blood vessels in an organ or body
