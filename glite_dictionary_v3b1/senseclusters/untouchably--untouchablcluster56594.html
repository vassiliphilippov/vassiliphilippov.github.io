untouchably

adverb
in a shunning way
9.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈtʌtʃ.ə.bliː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtʌtʃ.ə.bliː/ (BrE)
in a manner that treats someone as socially unapproachable or unworthy of contact, often by shunning or ostracizing them

Examples

Treated untouchably by others
After the scandal, her colleagues treated her untouchably and avoided all contact.
He was regarded untouchably by the community after the rumors spread.
The group acted untouchably toward anyone who disagreed with their views.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inclusively - in a way that includes or welcomes others
  • synonym: ostracizingly - in a way that excludes or shuns someone from a group
