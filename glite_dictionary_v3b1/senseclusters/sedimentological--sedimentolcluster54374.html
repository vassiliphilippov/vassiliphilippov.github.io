sedimentological

adjective
about sediment study
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛdəməntəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛdɪmɛntəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of sediments, their formation, and how they are deposited

Examples

The sedimentological evidence
The sedimentological evidence suggested that the area was once covered by a shallow sea.
Researchers conducted a sedimentological survey to understand the river delta's development.
A sedimentological approach can reveal important details about past environmental conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geological - relating to the science of the Earth's physical structure
  • synonym: sedimentary - relating to or formed from sediment
