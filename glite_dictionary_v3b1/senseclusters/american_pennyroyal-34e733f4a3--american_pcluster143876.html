American pennyroyal

noun
North American herb; insect repellent
9.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kən ˈpen.ɪ.rɔɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈme.rɪ.kən ˈpen.ɪ.rɔɪ.əl/ (BrE)
A hairy, branching herb native to North America, with purple-blue flowers, used as an insect repellent and sometimes in folk medicine.

Examples

American pennyroyal plant
American pennyroyal grows wild in many meadows across the Midwest.
The essential oil of American pennyroyal is often used to repel mosquitoes and other insects.
Traditional remedies sometimes utilized American pennyroyal for its supposed medicinal properties.
