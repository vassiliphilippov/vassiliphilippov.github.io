Stradivarius

noun
violin made by Stradivari
Pronunciation: / ˌstrædəˈvɛriəs /; / ˌstrædəˈværiəs /; / ˌstrædɪˈvɛriəs / (AmE), / ˌstrædɪˈveəriəs /; / ˌstradɪˈveəriəs /; / ˌstrædɪˈvɛːriəs / (BrE)
a violin or other stringed instrument made by Antonio Stradivari or his family, famous for their exceptional quality and value
