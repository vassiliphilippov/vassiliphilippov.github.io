textile machine

noun
machine for making textiles
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstaɪl məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstaɪl məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used for producing textiles such as yarn, cloth, or fabric.

Examples

A textile machine produces fabric
Early industrialization was driven by the invention of the textile machine.
Technicians are required to maintain each textile machine regularly.
The factory invested in a new textile machine to increase production of cotton fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinning machine - machine for spinning fibers into yarn
