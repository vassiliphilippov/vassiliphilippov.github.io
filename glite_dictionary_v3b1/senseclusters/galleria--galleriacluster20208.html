galleria

noun
large shopping mall
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæləˈriːə / (AmE), / ˌɡæləˈriːə / (BrE)
a large shopping mall or a covered area with shops and restaurants, often featuring glass roofs or walkways

Examples

A galleria mall
The new galleria downtown attracts thousands of shoppers every weekend.
We enjoyed lunch at a café inside the galleria after browsing the boutiques.
A glass ceiling lets natural light flood the entire galleria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arcade - a covered passageway with shops on both sides
  • synonym: mall - a large building or series of connected buildings containing many shops
