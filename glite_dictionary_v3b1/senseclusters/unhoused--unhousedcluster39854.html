unhoused

adjective
without a home
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaʊzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaʊzd / (BrE)
without a permanent home; lacking stable housing

Examples

Unhoused people
The city is working to provide services for unhoused residents.
Many unhoused people struggle to find safe places to sleep at night.
Volunteers distributed food and blankets to the unhoused community during the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeless - without a home or permanent place to live
  • antonym: housed - having a home or place to live
