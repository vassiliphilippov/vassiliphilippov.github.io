puxy

noun
spongy decayed wood
a soft, spongy mass of decayed wood, often found in fallen trees or logs

Examples

A puxy mass
The old log was covered in puxy after years of lying on the forest floor.
They used pieces of puxy to start the campfire because it caught fire easily.
Mushrooms grew from the puxy left behind by the decaying tree.

  • synonym: rot - the process or result of decaying organic matter
