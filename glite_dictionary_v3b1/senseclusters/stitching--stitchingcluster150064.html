stitching

noun
combining photos into a panorama
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪtʃ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈstɪtʃ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in digital imaging and photography) the process of combining multiple overlapping photographs or image tiles into a single, seamless image (for example, a panorama) using software or algorithms.

Examples

Photo stitching software
The photographer relied on automated stitching to turn several overlapping frames into a single wide panorama.
After shooting the landscape, she checked the camera app's stitching for ghosting or visible seams between photos.
The mapping team improved the satellite image stitching to produce a seamless high-resolution map of the coastline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: image stitching - the process of joining multiple images to create a single composite or panorama, typically using software
  • synonym: panorama stitching - a technique of stitching intended specifically to produce wide panoramic images from overlapping photos
