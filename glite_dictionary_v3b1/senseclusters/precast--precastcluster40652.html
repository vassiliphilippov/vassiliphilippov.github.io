precast

adjective
cast in final shape before installation
8.6 4.9
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈkæst/ /; / /ˈpriː.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.kɑːst/ /; / /priː.ˈkɑːst/ (BrE)
(of building materials, especially concrete) cast in its final shape before being transported to the site for installation.

Examples

The new bridge uses precast concrete beams for faster assembly on site.
