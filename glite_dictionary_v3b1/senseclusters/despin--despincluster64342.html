despin

noun
spin-reducing device
9.9
Pronunciation: / diːˈspɪn / (AmE), / diːˈspɪn / (BrE)
a device or mechanism used to reduce or stop the spinning motion of an object, especially in spacecraft or rockets

Examples

Despin device
The satellite was equipped with a despin to ensure its instruments pointed correctly.
Engineers tested the despin mechanism before launching the new rocket stage.
A malfunction in the despin caused the probe to spin uncontrollably after separation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stabilizer - a device that makes something steady or stable
