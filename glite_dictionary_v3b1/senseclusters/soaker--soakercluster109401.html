soaker

noun
absorbent object
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊkər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊkə / (BrE)
an object or material that absorbs or holds a large amount of liquid

Examples

A soaker pad
After the rainstorm, the old rug acted as a soaker and kept the floor dry.
This new mop is a great soaker and makes cleaning up spills much easier.
He used a thick towel as a soaker to dry off the dog after its bath.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absorbent - able to soak up liquid easily
  • synonym: sponge - a soft material that absorbs liquid easily
