cayenne

noun
hot red pepper spice
9.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /keɪ.ˈen/ /; / /kaɪ.ˈen/ (AmE), /keɪ.ˈen/ /; / /kaɪ.ˈen/ (BrE)
A hot-tasting red powder made from ground dried pods and seeds of certain varieties of capsicum peppers, used as a spice.
