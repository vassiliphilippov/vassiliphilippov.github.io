indexically

adverb
in an indicative way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛksɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves an index or indication, especially by pointing out or referring to something directly

Examples

Indexically pointing to people
In linguistics, certain words function indexically to point to people or places in context.
Gestures can communicate meaning indexically by directly indicating objects or directions.
The sign on the door works indexically by showing where the exit is located.

Related Terms

  • synonym: referentially - in a way that refers to something
  • antonym: symbolically - in a way that uses symbols to represent ideas or qualities
