coin

noun
a piece of metal money
1.4
Pronunciation: / kɔɪn / (AmE), / kɔɪn / (BrE)
a flat, usually round piece of metal that is used as money

Examples

A gold coin
He dropped a coin into the vending machine and waited for his drink.
Some rare coins can be worth a lot more than their face value.
The cashier handed me my change in small coins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: banknote - a piece of paper money
  • synonym: token - a piece resembling a coin, used in place of money
