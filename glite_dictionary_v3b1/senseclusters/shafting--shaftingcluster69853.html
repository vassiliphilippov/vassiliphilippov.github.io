shafting

noun
unfair treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːftɪŋ / (BrE)
unfair or dishonest treatment of someone, especially in a situation where they are cheated or treated badly

Examples

A shafting by company
After all his hard work, he felt the company gave him a real shafting when they denied his promotion.
Many customers complained about the shafting they received from the airline after their flights were canceled.
She warned her friend to watch out for any shafting in the contract negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betrayal - the act of being disloyal or unfaithful
  • synonym: cheating - acting dishonestly or unfairly to gain an advantage
  • antonym: fairness - the quality of treating people equally or justly
