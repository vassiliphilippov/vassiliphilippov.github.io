stoppered

adjective
sealed with a stopper
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑpərd / (AmE), / ˈstɒpəd / (BrE)
(of a container) fitted with a stopper in its opening to seal it.

Examples

A stoppered bottle
He placed the stoppered bottle on the shelf to keep the contents fresh.
Old apothecary jars were usually stoppered to prevent contamination.
The laboratory requires all chemicals to be stored in stoppered containers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unstopped - (of a container) not closed with a stopper
