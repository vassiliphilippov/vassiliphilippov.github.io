militarize

verb
to make military in character or use
4.8 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪz /; / mɪˈlɪtəˌraɪz / (AmE), / mɪˈlɪtəraɪz /; / ˈmɪlɪtəraɪz / (BrE)
