visitorial

adjective
of official inspection
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪzɪˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / ˌvɪzɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to the power or right of official inspection, supervision, or oversight, especially by an authority

Examples

Visitorial authority
The board exercised its visitorial powers to review the college's financial practices.
A visitorial authority may inspect the institution to ensure compliance with regulations.
The law grants certain officials visitorial rights over private organizations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspectorial - relating to inspection or an inspector
  • synonym: supervisory - having the function of overseeing or managing
