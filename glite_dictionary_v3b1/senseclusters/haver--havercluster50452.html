haver

noun
foolish talker
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪvər / (AmE), / ˈheɪvə / (BrE)
a person who talks foolishly or says things that are silly or meaningless

Examples

A silly haver
At meetings he was dismissed as a haver who never contributed anything useful.
Nobody listened to the haver as he rambled on about nonsense.
She was tired of being called a haver just for sharing her ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babbler - a person who talks rapidly and continuously in a foolish way
  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially about trivial things
  • antonym: thinker - a person who thinks deeply and seriously
