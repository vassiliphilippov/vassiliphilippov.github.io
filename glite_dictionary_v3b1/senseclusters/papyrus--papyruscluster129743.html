papyrus

noun
ancient document written on papyrus
Pronunciation: / pəˈpaɪrəs / (AmE), / pəˈpʌɪərəs / (BrE)
an ancient document or manuscript written on sheets made from the papyrus plant

Examples

An old papyrus
Scholars studied a papyrus to learn about life in classical Alexandria.
The papyrus was carefully unrolled to reveal a lost Greek poem.
The museum displays an ancient papyrus containing hieroglyphs from Egypt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manuscript - a handwritten or typed document, especially before printing was common
