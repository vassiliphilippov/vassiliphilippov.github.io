Amerasian

noun
child of American and Asian parents
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈreɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌæməˈreɪʒən / (BrE)
a person who has one American parent and one Asian parent, especially in the context of children born during or after wars involving the United States in Asia

Examples

An Amerasian person
After the war, many Amerasians faced challenges in their home countries.
The documentary tells the stories of several Amerasian children searching for their families.
She identifies as an Amerasian and shares her experiences growing up in two cultures.
