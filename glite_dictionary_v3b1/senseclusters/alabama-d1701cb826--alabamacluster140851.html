Alabama

proper noun
a river in Alabama, U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.əˈbæm.ə / (AmE), / ˌæl.əˈbæm.ə / (BrE)
a river in the state of Alabama, United States, formed by the joining of the Coosa and Tallapoosa Rivers and flowing southwest to join the Mobile River.
