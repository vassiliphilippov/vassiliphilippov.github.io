option

noun
a choice or alternative
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑpʃən /; / ˈɑːpʃən / (AmE), / ˈɒpʃən / (BrE)
a choice or alternative; the act or power of choosing between possibilities

Examples

A menu option
After reading the menu, she realized that the vegetarian option sounded best.
The only option left was to start over from scratch.
We were given the option of working from home or coming into the office.
She weighed each option carefully before making a decision.
Taking the train is a convenient option for commuters.
You always have the option to decline the invitation if you're not interested.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternative - another possibility or choice
  • synonym: choice - the act of choosing or the option selected
  • antonym: necessity - something that is unavoidable or required, opposite of optional
  • synonym: selection - the action or fact of carefully choosing someone or something as being the best or most suitable
