Examples

A menu option
After reading the menu, she realized that the vegetarian option sounded best.
The only option left was to start over from scratch.
We were given the option of working from home or coming into the office.
She weighed each option carefully before making a decision.
Taking the train is a convenient option for commuters.
You always have the option to decline the invitation if you're not interested.

Synonyms

  • choice
    1.0 240/m
    choice is the everyday word for something you pick, while option is a general term for an available possibility.
  • alternative
    3.2 51/m
    alternative is one specific possibility often presented as a substitute, while option is any available choice among possibilities.
  • selection
    1.8 17/m
    selection is the process or careful choice made from many items, while option is any choice or possible course to take.
  • pick
    1.0 18/m
    pick is a casual word for the thing you choose, while option is a neutral term for any available choice.
