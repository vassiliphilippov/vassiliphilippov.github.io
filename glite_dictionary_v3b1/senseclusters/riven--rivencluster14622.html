riven

verb
to split apart
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.vən/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.vən/ (BrE)
to split or tear something apart violently, or to cause deep division, especially in a figurative sense.

Examples

To riven a group
The community was riven by years of conflict and mistrust.
A powerful earthquake had riven the landscape, leaving deep cracks in the ground.
The team was riven by disagreements over their strategy for the final match.

Related Terms
