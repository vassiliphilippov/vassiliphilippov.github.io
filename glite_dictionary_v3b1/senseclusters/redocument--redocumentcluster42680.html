redocument

verb
to record again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈdɑːkjəmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdɒkjʊmənt / (BrE)
to record or document something again, especially for archival or study purposes

Examples

It is important to redocument the site before any major changes are made.
