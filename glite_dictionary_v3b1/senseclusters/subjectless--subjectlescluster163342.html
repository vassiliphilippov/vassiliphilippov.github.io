subjectless

adjective
without a subject
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbdʒɪktləs / (AmE), / ˈsʌbdʒɪktləs / (BrE)
lacking a grammatical subject in a sentence or clause

Examples

Subjectless sentence
In English, commands like 'Go!' are often subjectless sentences.
Some languages allow subjectless clauses more frequently than others.
The teacher explained that the imperative form is usually subjectless.
