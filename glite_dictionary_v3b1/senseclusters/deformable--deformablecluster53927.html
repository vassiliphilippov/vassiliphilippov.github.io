deformable

adjective
can change shape
5.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfɔːr.mə.bəl/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfɔː.mə.bəl/ (BrE)
able to be changed in shape, especially without breaking

Examples

Deformable material
The engineer designed a deformable barrier to absorb the impact of collisions.
Some plastics are highly deformable and can be shaped into many forms.
The robot's deformable arm allows it to squeeze through tight spaces.
The packaging uses a deformable material to protect fragile items during shipping.
Scientists tested how deformable the new plastic was under extreme pressure.
Unlike glass, rubber is highly deformable and can stretch without breaking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • synonym: malleable - able to be shaped or bent easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed in shape
