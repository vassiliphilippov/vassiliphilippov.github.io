Examples

Synonyms

  • malleable
    6.0 0.24/m
    malleable is easily shaped often by hammering or pressure, while deformable simply means able to change shape without breaking.
  • pliable
    4.2 0.73/m
    pliable is easily bent or adapted to different shapes and feels flexible, while deformable is a general ability to change shape.
  • bendable
    3.0 0.10/m
    bendable can be bent or flexed without breaking, while deformable covers changing shape in many ways, not only bending.
  • moldable
    4.1 0.16/m
    moldable can be shaped when soft or pressed and often needs work, while deformable means it can change shape without breaking.
