crayon license

noun
mock certificate for preschoolers' crayon use
4.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkreɪ.ən ˈlaɪ.səns/ /; / /kræn ˈlaɪ.səns/ /; / /ˈkreɪ.ɑːn ˈlaɪ.səns/ (AmE), /ˈkreɪ.ən ˈlaɪ.səns/ /; / /ˈkreɪ.ɒn ˈlaɪ.səns/ (BrE)
a humorous or mock certificate given to children, especially preschoolers, to signify their 'official' ability to use crayons or as a playful mark of graduation from an early childhood class.
