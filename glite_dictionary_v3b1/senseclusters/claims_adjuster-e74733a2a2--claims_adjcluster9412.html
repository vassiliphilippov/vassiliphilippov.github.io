claims adjuster

noun
insurance claims investigator
Pronunciation: / kleɪmz əˈdʒʌstɚ / (AmE), / kleɪmz əˈdʒʌstə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to investigate insurance claims and recommend an appropriate settlement

Examples

Claims adjuster inspects damage
After the accident, a claims adjuster came to inspect the damage to the car.
Many claims adjusters work directly for insurance companies but some are independent.
The claims adjuster reviewed all the documents before making a recommendation.
