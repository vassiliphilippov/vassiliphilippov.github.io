unpowdered

adjective
not in powder form
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpaʊdərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpaʊdəd / (BrE)
not in powder form; consisting of whole or larger pieces rather than being ground into a fine powder

Examples

Unpowdered spices
The chef prefers to use unpowdered spices for a fresher flavor in his dishes.
You should buy unpowdered sugar if you want to make the syrup from scratch.
Some teas are made with unpowdered leaves to preserve their natural taste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: powdered - in the form of a fine powder
  • synonym: whole - not broken or ground; complete
