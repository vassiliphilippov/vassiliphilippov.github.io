oophorectomy

noun
surgical removal of an ovary
Pronunciation: / oʊˌɑːfəˈrɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌuː.ə.fəˈrɛk.tə.mi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove one or both ovaries

Examples

Bilateral oophorectomy
A bilateral oophorectomy is performed when both ovaries are removed.
After her oophorectomy, she discussed hormone replacement therapy with her doctor.
The patient underwent a oophorectomy to treat ovarian cancer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hysterectomy - surgical removal of the uterus
