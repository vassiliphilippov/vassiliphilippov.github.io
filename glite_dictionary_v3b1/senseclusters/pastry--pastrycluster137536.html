pastry

noun
baked food made of dough
4.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.striː/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.striː/ (BrE)
a food made from a mixture of flour, fat, and water that is rolled flat and baked, often with a filling or topping, or any baked goods made from this mixture

Examples

A sweet pastry
He learned how to make puff pastry at culinary school.
She enjoyed a fresh pastry with her morning coffee.
The bakery displays a delicious selection of sweet and savory pastries.
