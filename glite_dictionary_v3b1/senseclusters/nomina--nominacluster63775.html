nomina

noun
plural of nomen
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːmɪnə / (AmE), / ˈnɒmɪnə / (BrE)
the plural form of 'nomen', meaning names, especially used in legal or scholarly contexts

Examples

Nomina of senators
The ancient manuscript listed the nomina of all the Roman senators.
In the appendix, you will find the nomina of the contributors to the research.
Legal documents often include the nomina of all parties involved in the agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: names - words by which people or things are known
  • synonym: nomen - a name, especially in legal or scholarly use
