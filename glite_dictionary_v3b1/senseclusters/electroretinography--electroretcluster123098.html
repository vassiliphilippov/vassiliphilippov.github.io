electroretinography

noun
retinal electrical test
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌrɛtəˈnɑːɡrəfi /; / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌrɛtəˈnɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌrɛtɪˈnɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a medical test that measures the electrical activity or response of the retina to light
