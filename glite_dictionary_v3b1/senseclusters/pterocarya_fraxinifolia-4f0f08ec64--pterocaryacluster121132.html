Pterocarya fraxinifolia

noun
Caucasian wingnut tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈkɛəriə ˌfræksɪnɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈkeəriə ˌfræksɪnɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
a medium-sized, much-branched tree native to the Caucasus, distinguished by its winged fruit, also known as the Caucasian wingnut

Examples
