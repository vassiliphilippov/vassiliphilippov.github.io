unchildlike

adjective
not childlike
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪk / (BrE)
not having the qualities, innocence, or behavior typical of a child

Examples

An unchildlike attitude
His unchildlike sense of responsibility impressed everyone at the meeting.
The girl's unchildlike comments surprised her teachers during the discussion.
Despite his age, his unchildlike behavior made him seem much older.

Related Terms

  • antonym: childlike - having the innocence or qualities typical of a child
  • synonym: mature - showing qualities of an adult rather than a child
