opposite number

noun
person with corresponding role elsewhere
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑpəzɪt ˈnʌmbər /; / ˈɑːpəzɪt ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈɒpəzɪt ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
