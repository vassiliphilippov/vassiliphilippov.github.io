politician

noun
scheming or manipulative person
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːləˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
A person who uses cunning, scheming, or underhanded methods to gain advantage or power in an organization.

Examples

A clever politician
Don't trust him—he's a real politician who knows how to manipulate every situation.
He was considered a politician in the company, always working behind the scenes to advance his own agenda.
Some say you have to be a bit of a politician to survive the corporate ladder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manipulator - someone who controls or influences others in a clever, often unscrupulous way
  • synonym: schemer - a person involved in making secret or underhanded plans
