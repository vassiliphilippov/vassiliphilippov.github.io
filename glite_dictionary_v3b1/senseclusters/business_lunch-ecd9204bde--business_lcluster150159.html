business lunch

noun
lunch meeting for business discussions
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs lʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs lʌntʃ / (BrE)
a lunch at which business matters are discussed, typically held at a restaurant and often paid for as a business expense

Examples

A business lunch meeting
During the business lunch, they reviewed the company's quarterly financial report.
She often has a business lunch with clients to negotiate new deals.
The two executives arranged a business lunch to discuss the terms of the contract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: working lunch - a lunch during which work or business is conducted
