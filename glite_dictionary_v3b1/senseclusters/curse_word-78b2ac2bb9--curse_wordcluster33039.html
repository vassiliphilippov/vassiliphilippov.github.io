curse word

noun
profane or obscene expression
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrs wɝd / (AmE), / ˈkɜːs wɜːd / (BrE)
a word or phrase that is considered profane, obscene, or offensive, typically used to express strong emotions such as anger or surprise

Examples

A curse word shouted
He shouted a curse word when he stubbed his toe on the table.
Please don't use any curse words in front of the children.
She muttered a curse word under her breath when the computer crashed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expletive - an oath or swear word; something said in anger or surprise
  • synonym: swear word - an offensive word used to express anger or surprise
