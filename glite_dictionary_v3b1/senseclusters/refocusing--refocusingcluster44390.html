refocusing

noun
the act of focusing again
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈfoʊkəsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈfəʊkəsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of focusing again or adjusting focus after it has been lost or changed

Examples

The refocusing process
After a short break, the team began the refocusing process to return to their original goals.
Continuous refocusing is often needed when working on complex projects.
The rapid refocusing of the camera allowed the photographer to capture the fleeting moment.
