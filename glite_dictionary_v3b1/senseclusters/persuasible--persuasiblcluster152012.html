persuasible

adjective
easily persuaded
9.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈsweɪzəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈsweɪzəbl̩ / (BrE)
Easily persuaded or susceptible to persuasion.

Examples

Easily persuasible person
Children are often more persuasible than adults when it comes to advertising.
Her persuasible nature made her a valuable mediator in disputes.
The committee found him persuasible on most points, especially after new evidence was presented.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intransigent - unwilling to change one's views or be persuaded
  • synonym: persuadable - capable of being persuaded
