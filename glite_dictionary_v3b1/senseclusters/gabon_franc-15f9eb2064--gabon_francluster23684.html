Gabon franc

noun
currency of Gabon
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈbɔn fræŋk / (AmE), / ɡəˈbɒn fræŋk / (BrE)
the official currency and basic monetary unit of Gabon, subdivided into 100 centimes

Examples

The Gabon franc currency
All transactions in Gabon are conducted in the official currency, the Gabon franc.
The price of imported goods has risen sharply with the decline of the Gabon franc.
Tourists should exchange their dollars for Gabon francs at a local bank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CFA franc - currency used by several Central African countries, including Gabon
