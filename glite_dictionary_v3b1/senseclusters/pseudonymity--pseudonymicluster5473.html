pseudonymity

noun
use of a false name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdəˈnɪməti / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəˈnɪmɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of using a false or fictitious name to conceal one's identity

Examples

Pseudonymity in writing
Writers sometimes choose pseudonymity to separate their personal and professional lives.
The platform encourages pseudonymity to help users feel more comfortable sharing their opinions.
In some online communities, pseudonymity is valued as a way to protect privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymity - the state of being anonymous or unknown by name
  • antonym: identifiability - the quality of being able to be identified
