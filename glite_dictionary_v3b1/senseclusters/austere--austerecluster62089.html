austere

adjective
practicing strict self-denial or simplicity
Pronunciation: / ɔˈstɪr / (AmE), / ɔːˈstɪə / (BrE)
showing strict self-discipline, avoiding luxury and comfort; practicing great self-denial.

Examples

Austere lifestyle
Despite his wealth, her father maintained an austere approach to spending.
He lived an austere life, giving up all unnecessary comforts and pleasures.
The monastery was known for its austere rules and daily routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - leading a simple life with self-discipline and no luxury
  • synonym: spartan - marked by simplicity and lack of luxury
