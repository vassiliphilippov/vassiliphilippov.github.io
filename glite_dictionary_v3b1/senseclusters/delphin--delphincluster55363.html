delphin

noun
plant compound; archaic dolphin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛlfɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɛlfɪn / (BrE)
a chemical compound found in certain plants, especially in the petals of larkspur, or an archaic term for dolphin
