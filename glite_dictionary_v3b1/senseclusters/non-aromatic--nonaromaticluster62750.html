non-aromatic

adjective
not aromatic (esp. in chemistry)
5.4 5.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.rə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /nən.ə.rə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /nɒn.æ.rə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /nən.æ.rə.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
(of a compound) not containing a stabilized ring of atoms as in aromatic compounds; lacking aromatic properties.

Examples

Non-aromatic compound
Some organic molecules are non-aromatic and therefore do not participate in aromatic substitution reactions.
The chemist explained the difference between aromatic and non-aromatic hydrocarbons.
Unlike benzene, methane is a non-aromatic compound.

Related Terms
