clogger

noun
clog dancer or blocker
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈklɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who performs clog dancing or someone who blocks or clogs something

Examples

Talented clogger dances
The talented clogger wowed the audience with her fast footwork.
A clogger in the pipe caused the water to back up into the sink.
He trained for years to become a professional clogger in traditional folk festivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocker - a person or thing that blocks something
  • synonym: dancer - a person who dances
