spanner in the plan

noun
unexpected problem disrupting plans
Pronunciation: / ˈspænər ɪn ðə plæn / (AmE), / ˈspænə ɪn ðə plæn / (BrE)
an unexpected problem or obstacle that disrupts or hinders progress or plans

Examples

A spanner in the plan
Budget cuts have become a major spanner in the plan for development.
His illness put a spanner in the plan for the team's project launch.
The sudden weather change threw a real spanner in the plan for our outdoor event.
