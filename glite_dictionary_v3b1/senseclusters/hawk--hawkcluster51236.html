hawk

noun
mason's handheld mortar board
Pronunciation: / hɑk / (AmE), / hɔːk / (BrE)
a square board with a handle underneath, used by masons to hold mortar or plaster while working

Examples

A plaster hawk
Every apprentice learns how to carry a hawk without spilling its contents.
She balanced a loaded hawk in one hand while plastering the wall with the other.
The mason skillfully scraped mortar off the hawk and spread it onto the bricks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trowel - a small hand tool with a flat, pointed blade, used by masons for applying and spreading mortar or plaster
