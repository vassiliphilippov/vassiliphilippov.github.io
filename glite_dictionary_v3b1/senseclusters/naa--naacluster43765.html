naa

interjection
informal 'no'
Pronunciation: / nɑː / (AmE), / nɑː / (BrE)
an informal way of saying 'no' or showing disagreement, especially in casual conversation

Examples

Naa thanks
"Naa, I don't think that's a good idea," she replied.
When asked if he wanted more food, he simply said, "naa."
You might hear someone say naa instead of 'no' among friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - a word used to express refusal or disagreement
  • antonym: yeah - an informal way of saying 'yes'
