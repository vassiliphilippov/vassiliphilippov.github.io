tonsillitis

noun
inflammation of the tonsils
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːnsəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌtɒnsɪˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the tonsils, especially the palatine tonsils at the back of the throat, often causing sore throat and discomfort

Examples

Tonsillitis infection
Children are especially prone to developing tonsillitis during the winter months.
He missed several days of school due to a severe case of tonsillitis.
The doctor diagnosed her with tonsillitis after examining her throat.
