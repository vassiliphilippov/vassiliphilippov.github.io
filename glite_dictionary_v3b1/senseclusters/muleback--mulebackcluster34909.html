muleback

adjective
carried by mule
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈmjuːlˌbæk / (BrE)
carried on the back of a mule or transported by mule.

Examples

Muleback delivery
The supplies were transported over the mountain on muleback due to the rough terrain.
They relied on muleback delivery to bring food to the remote village.
The expedition's equipment was carried muleback through the dense forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horseback - carried on the back of a horse
  • synonym: pack animal - an animal used to carry loads
