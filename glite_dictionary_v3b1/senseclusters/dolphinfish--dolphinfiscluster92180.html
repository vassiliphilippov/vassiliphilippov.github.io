dolphinfish

noun
large food and game fish of warm seas
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊlfɪnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdɒlfɪnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large, slender fish found in warm seas, valued as food and for sport fishing, especially around Hawaii; also known as mahi-mahi.
