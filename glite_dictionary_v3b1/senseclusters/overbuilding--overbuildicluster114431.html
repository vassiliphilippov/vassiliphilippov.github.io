overbuilding

noun
adding to buildings
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the construction of extra floors or structures on top of an existing building

Examples

Overbuilding extra floors
The city approved the overbuilding of several historic structures to create more office space.
Concerns about safety arose after the overbuilding of apartments on top of the old warehouse.
The architect presented plans for an overbuilding that would add three new floors to the hotel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demolition - the act of tearing down a building
  • synonym: extension - a new part added to a building
