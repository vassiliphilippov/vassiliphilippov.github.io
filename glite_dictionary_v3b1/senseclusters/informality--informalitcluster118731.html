informality

noun
state of being relaxed and unconstrained
3.2 4.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.fər.ˈmæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.fə.ˈmæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of being relaxed, casual, and not concerned with strict rules or formality

Examples
