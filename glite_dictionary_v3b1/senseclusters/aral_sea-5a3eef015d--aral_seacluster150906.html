Aral Sea

proper noun
large, shrinking lake in Central Asia
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.əl siː/ /; / /ˈær.əl siː/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.rəl siː/ /; / /ˈær.əl siː/ (BrE)
