booster cable

noun
cable for starting vehicle batteries
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstɚ ˌkeɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈbuːstə ˌkeɪbəl / (BrE)
a heavy-duty insulated cable used to connect the batteries of two vehicles to start a car with a dead or weak battery

Examples

A set of booster cables
He kept a set of booster cables in the trunk in case of emergencies.
The mechanic attached the booster cable to both car batteries to jump-start the engine.
Without a booster cable, we couldn't get the stalled car to start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumper cable - a cable for connecting vehicle batteries to enable jump starting
