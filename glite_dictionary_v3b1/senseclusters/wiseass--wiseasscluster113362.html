wiseass

noun
sarcastic person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.zæs/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.zæs/ (BrE)
a person who makes sarcastic, clever, or annoying remarks, often to show off or irritate others

Examples

A wiseass at work
Nobody likes a wiseass who interrupts every conversation with a sarcastic comment.
She called him a wiseass after he made another clever remark during class.
If you keep acting like a wiseass, people might stop taking you seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smart aleck - a person who behaves as if they know everything and makes irritating remarks
