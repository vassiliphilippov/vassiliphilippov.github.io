attack aircraft carrier

noun
warship designed to launch aircraft for attack
Pronunciation: / əˈtæk ˈɛrˌkræft ˈkæriɚ / (AmE), / əˈtak ˈeəkrɑːft ˈkarɪə / (BrE)
a large warship equipped with a long, flat deck designed for launching and landing military aircraft, primarily for offensive operations

Examples

An attack aircraft carrier
During the operation, the attack aircraft carrier launched multiple fighter jets for support.
Modern attack aircraft carriers are equipped with advanced radar and missile defense systems.
The navy commissioned a new attack aircraft carrier to strengthen its fleet.
