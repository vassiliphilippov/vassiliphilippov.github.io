enactive

adjective
relating to enactment
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈnæktɪv / (BrE)
relating to the process of enacting or putting something into effect, especially as law or formal practice

Examples

Enactive law process
The committee discussed the enactive measures needed to implement the new policy.
Legal scholars analyzed the enactive process that led to the adoption of the regulation.
The enactive stage of the law involved detailed planning and public consultation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: executive - relating to the execution or carrying out of laws
  • synonym: legislative - relating to the making or enacting of laws
