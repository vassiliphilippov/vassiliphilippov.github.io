aridly

adverb
in a dull way
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈærɪdli / (AmE), / ˈærɪdli / (BrE)

Examples

The professor explained the theory aridly, making it hard for students to stay engaged.
She described the events aridly, without any emotion or excitement.
The report was written aridly, lacking any vivid details or interesting insights.
