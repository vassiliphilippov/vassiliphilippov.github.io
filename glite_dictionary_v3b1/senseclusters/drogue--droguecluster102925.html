drogue

noun
funnel-shaped aerial target towed by aircraft
8.7
Pronunciation: / droʊɡ / (AmE), / drəʊɡ / (BrE)
a funnel-shaped device that is towed behind an aircraft and used as a target for gunnery practice or missile testing

Examples

Target drogue
A drogue is attached to a cable and towed behind a plane for gunnery practice.
During training, the fighter pilot aimed at the drogue to demonstrate his accuracy.
New pilots must successfully hit the drogue before moving on to live-fire exercises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: target - an object aimed at in shooting practice or attacks
  • synonym: tow target - an object towed behind an aircraft for use in target practice
