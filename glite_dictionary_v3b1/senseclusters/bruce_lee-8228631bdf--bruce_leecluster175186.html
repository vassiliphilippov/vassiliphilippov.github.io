Bruce Lee

proper noun
Hong Kong–American martial artist and film actor
Pronunciation: / bruːs liː / (AmE), / bruːs liː / (BrE)
(1940–1973) A Hong Kong–American actor, director, and martial artist known for popularizing martial arts in film.
