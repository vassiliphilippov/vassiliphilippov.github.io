Shih Tzu

noun
small, long-haired Chinese dog breed
Pronunciation: / ʃiˈtsu / (AmE), / ʃiː ˈtsuː / (BrE)
a small, long-haired breed of dog originating in China, with a short muzzle and a distinctive flowing coat

Examples

A Shih Tzu dog
Many people choose the Shih Tzu for its friendly personality and small size.
My grandmother owns a Shih Tzu that loves to sit on her lap all day.
The Shih Tzu requires regular grooming to keep its coat looking beautiful.
