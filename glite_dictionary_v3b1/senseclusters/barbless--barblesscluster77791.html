barbless

adjective
without a barb
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbləs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbləs / (BrE)
having no barb, especially referring to a hook or similar object without a sharp backward-facing point

Examples

Barbless hook
The fisherman chose a barbless hook to make releasing the fish easier.
Many conservation areas require anglers to use barbless equipment to protect wildlife.
Using a barbless hook can reduce injury to fish during catch-and-release.
