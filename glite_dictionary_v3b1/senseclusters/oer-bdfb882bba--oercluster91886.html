OER

noun
military officer assessment
9.8
Pronunciation: / oʊ iː ɑːr / (AmE), / əʊ iː ɑː / (BrE)
a formal report evaluating a military officer's performance, used for promotions and assignments

Examples

Officer OER report
The captain submitted his OER to headquarters at the end of the year.
A strong OER can help an officer advance more quickly in rank.
Before the promotion board met, each candidate's OER was carefully reviewed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fitness report - a report on an officer's suitability and performance, especially in the military
  • synonym: performance review - an evaluation of an employee's work performance
