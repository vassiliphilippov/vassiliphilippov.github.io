Synonyms

  • control
    2.4 90/m
    control means holding power to direct or manage people or things, while govern means to officially guide or shape how they act.
  • rule
    1.0 30/m
    rule is to govern or have authority over a country or group in a formal way, while govern describes guiding behavior or events more generally.
  • dictate
    5.0 13/m
    dictate means to order or command with strong authority, while govern can mean to guide, influence, or manage more broadly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follow - to act according to rules or advice (as an antonym in some contexts, when a person is not governed by something)
