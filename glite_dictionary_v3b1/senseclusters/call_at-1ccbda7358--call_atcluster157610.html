call at

verb
stop at a place (transport context)
3.8
Pronunciation: / kɔl æt / (AmE), / kɔːl æt / (BrE)
(of a ship, train, etc.) to stop at a port, station, or other designated place during a journey.

Examples

To call at a port
After sailing all night, the vessel finally called at Singapore this morning.
Our train was delayed because it had to call at an unscheduled station.
The cruise ship will call at several Mediterranean ports during its voyage.
