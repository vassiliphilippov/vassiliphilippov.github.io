nominate

verb
formally appoint or assign
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑː.mə.neɪt /; / ˈnɑːməˌneɪt /; / ˈnɑː.məˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɒ.mɪ.neɪt /; / ˈnɒmɪneɪt /; / ˈnɒm.ɪ.neɪt / (BrE)
to formally suggest or appoint someone for a position, role, or honor

  • antonym: elect - to choose someone by voting for them
