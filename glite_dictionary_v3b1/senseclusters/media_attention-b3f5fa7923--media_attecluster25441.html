media attention

noun
notice or interest from the media
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiə əˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə əˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
notice or interest from the press, television, and other media, especially in relation to a person, event, or issue.

Examples

Media attention from press
His unique invention quickly attracted international media attention.
The charity hopes to raise awareness through positive media attention.
The scandal received extensive media attention across the country.
