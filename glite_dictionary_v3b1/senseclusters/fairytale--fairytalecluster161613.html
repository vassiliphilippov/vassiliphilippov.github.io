fairytale

noun
a story involving magical beings, esp. for children
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈfeə.teɪl / (BrE)
A children's story about magical and imaginary beings and lands, especially one involving fairies or enchantment.

Examples

A classic fairytale
Every night, she would read a fairytale to her daughter before bed.
Many fairytales teach children important lessons about good and evil.
The old castle looked like something straight out of a fairytale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folktale - a traditional story passed down orally among people
  • synonym: legend - a traditional story sometimes believed to be historical but unauthenticated
