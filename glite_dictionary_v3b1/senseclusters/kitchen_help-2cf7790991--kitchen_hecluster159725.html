kitchen help

noun
person hired to assist in a kitchen
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən hɛlp / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃən hɛlp / (BrE)
a person or group of people employed to assist with routine tasks in a kitchen, especially in restaurants or institutions
