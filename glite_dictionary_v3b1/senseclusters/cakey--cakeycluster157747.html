cakey

adjective
dry or crumbly texture
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkeɪ.kiː/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.kɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkeɪ.kiː/ /; / /ˈkeɪ.kɪ/ (BrE)
having a texture or appearance like cake, often used for food or makeup that is dry, crumbly, thick, or spongy

Examples

A cakey foundation
After several hours, her foundation became cakey and started to flake off.
The brownies turned out too cakey instead of being fudgy as she wanted.
If you apply too much powder, your skin might look cakey and unnatural.
The brownies turned out a bit too cakey for my taste, rather than fudgy.
Some makeup can look cakey if you apply too much powder on your face.
He prefers pancakes that are light and airy, not cakey and dense.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crumbly - easily breaking into small pieces
  • synonym: dense - having a thick or heavy texture
  • antonym: fluffy - light and soft in texture
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface or texture
