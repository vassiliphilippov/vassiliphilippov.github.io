dash down

verb
write down hastily
Pronunciation: / dæʃ daʊn / (AmE), / dæʃ daʊn / (BrE)
to write down something quickly and without much care

Examples

To dash down notes
During the meeting, Lisa dashed down a few important points to share later.
He always dashes down ideas for his stories in a little notebook.
She paused to dash down the phone number before she forgot it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jot down - to write something quickly
  • synonym: note down - to write something down in order to remember it
