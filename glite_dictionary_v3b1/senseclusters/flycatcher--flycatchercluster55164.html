flycatcher

noun
insect-eating bird that catches prey in flight
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌkætʃər /; / ˈflaɪˌkætʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌkætʃə / (BrE)
a small bird that catches insects in the air, found in many parts of the world
