demilitarization

noun
removal of military presence
Pronunciation: / diˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌmɪlɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing or reducing military forces, weapons, or influence from an area or organization, often as part of a peace agreement
