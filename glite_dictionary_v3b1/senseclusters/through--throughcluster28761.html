through

noun
passage or opening
4.6 0.74/m
Pronunciation: / Œ∏ru / (AmE), / Œ∏ruÀê / (BrE)
a passage or opening that allows movement from one side to another, especially in a barrier or fence

Examples

A narrow through
The old stone wall had a narrow through where children could slip by.
Farmers built a small through in the fence for sheep to move between fields.
We found a hidden through that led directly to the garden.
