antisocialist

adjective
opposed to socialism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
opposed to socialism or socialist ideas and policies

Examples

Antisocialist laws
The government passed several antisocialist laws to limit the influence of left-wing groups.
His antisocialist views were clear in every speech he gave during the debate.
Many voters supported the antisocialist candidate in the recent election.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-communist - opposed to communism or communist ideas
  • antonym: prosocialist - supporting socialism or socialist ideas
