child welfare service

noun
agency for children's social welfare
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪld ˈwɛlfɛr ˈsɜrvɪs / (AmE), / tʃaɪld ˈwɛlfeə ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
an organization or agency responsible for protecting the well-being of children and providing social services and vocational training to them

Examples

The child welfare service
Many families rely on the child welfare service for guidance and support.
The child welfare service intervened after reports of neglect.
The local government increased funding for the child welfare service this year.
