debatable

adjective
open to question or dispute
3.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /də.ˈbeɪ.tə.bəl/ /; / /dɪ.ˈbeɪ.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈbeɪ.tə.bəl/ /; / /də.ˈbeɪ.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
open to question, argument, or discussion; not certain or settled

Examples

A debatable point
Some historians consider the evidence for the event to be debatable at best.
The claim that the earth is flat is highly debatable given current scientific evidence.
Whether the new policy will be effective remains debatable among experts.

The benefits of the policy remain debatable among experts.
Whether the new law will be effective is highly debatable.
It is debatable whether his theory actually applies to this situation.
The outcome of the vote remains debatable among political analysts.
Whether this policy will succeed is highly debatable at this stage.
