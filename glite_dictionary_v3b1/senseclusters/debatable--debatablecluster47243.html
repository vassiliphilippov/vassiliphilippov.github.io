debatable

adjective
open to question or dispute
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbeɪtəbl̩ /; / dɪˈbeɪdəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪtəbl̩ /; / dɪˈbeɪtəb(ə)l / (BrE)
open to question, argument, or discussion; not certain or settled
