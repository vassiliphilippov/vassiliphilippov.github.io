Synonyms

  • arguable
    6.0 1.02/m
    arguable is reasonable to support with facts or reasons, while debatable means open to question or discussion.
  • disputable
    6.6 0.25/m
    disputable invites strong argument or doubt, while debatable is a general term for something not settled.
  • contestable
    6.8 0.14/m
    contestable can be argued against with evidence, while debatable is more neutral and simply open to discussion.
  • controvertible
    8.3 0.01/m
    controvertible sounds formal and fits legal or academic debate, while debatable is more common in everyday talk.
  • moot
    7.4 0.84/m
    moot often means a point that no longer matters or is for discussion, while debatable stays general and unsettled.
  • problematical
    7.7 0.18/m
    problematical sounds formal and suggests a real problem to solve, while debatable only means open to argument or question.
  • dubitable
    9.9 0.00/m
    dubitable feels old or rare and focuses on doubt about truth, while debatable is common and means open to discussion.
