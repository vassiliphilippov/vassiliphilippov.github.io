wire cloth

noun
fabric made of woven metal wire
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚ klɔθ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪə klɒθ / (BrE)
A fabric woven from metallic wires, used for sieves, screens, and industrial applications.

Examples

Wire cloth fabric
Wire cloth is often used in construction to reinforce plaster or concrete.
He ordered stainless steel wire cloth for the new industrial screens.
The filter was covered in fine wire cloth to separate out small particles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mesh - a material of interlaced strands of fiber, wire, or other flexible materials
