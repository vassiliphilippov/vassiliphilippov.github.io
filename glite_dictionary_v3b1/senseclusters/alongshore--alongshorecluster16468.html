alongshore

adverb
parallel to shoreline
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔŋʃɔr / (AmE), / əˈlɒŋʃɔː / (BrE)
in a direction parallel to the shoreline or coast

Examples

Walk alongshore
The fishermen walked alongshore searching for a good place to cast their nets.
After the storm, debris was scattered alongshore for miles.
We decided to travel alongshore to explore the small villages by the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastwise - along or near the coast
  • antonym: inland - away from the coast toward the interior
