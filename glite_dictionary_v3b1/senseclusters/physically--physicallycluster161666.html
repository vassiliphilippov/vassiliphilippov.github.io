physically

adverb
in bodily manner
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪkli / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the body or material things, rather than the mind or emotions

Examples

Physically strong
After the accident, he was physically unable to walk for several months.
The job is physically demanding and requires a lot of strength.
She felt physically exhausted after climbing the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodily - in a way that involves the body
  • antonym: mentally - in a way that relates to the mind or intellect
