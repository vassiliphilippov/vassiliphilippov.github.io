mark

verb
celebrate or observe an occasion
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to celebrate or observe an important event or occasion with a ceremony or activity

Examples

To mark the day
Every year, people mark Independence Day with fireworks and festivities.
He sent flowers to mark the occasion of their retirement.
The school held an assembly to mark its 100th anniversary.
