Eleusinian

adjective
of Eleusis or its rites
Pronunciation: / ˌɛljuˈsɪniən / (AmE), / ˌɛljuːˈsɪniən / (BrE)
relating to the ancient Greek city of Eleusis or its famous religious mysteries

Examples

Eleusinian Mysteries
The Eleusinian Mysteries attracted visitors from all over ancient Greece.
Scholars still debate the exact rituals of the Eleusinian ceremonies.
Artifacts from the Eleusinian sanctuary reveal much about ancient religious practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystical - relating to mysteries or spiritual rituals
  • antonym: profane - not relating to religious or sacred things
