horned toad

noun
spiny lizard of western North America
Pronunciation: / hɔrn(d) toʊd / (AmE), / hɔːnd təʊd / (BrE)
An insect-eating lizard native to western North America, with hornlike spines on its head and spiny scales on its body.

Examples

The horned toad lizard
Children were fascinated by the horned toad basking on the warm rock.
The horned toad uses its spiny appearance as a defense against predators.
While hiking, I spotted a horned toad quickly scurrying across the desert sand.

Related Terms
