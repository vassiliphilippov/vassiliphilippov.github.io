extruder

noun
machine for shaping material
7.9
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈtruː.dər/ /; / /ek.ˈtruː.dər/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈtruː.də/ /; / /ek.ˈtruː.də/ (BrE)
a machine that shapes material by forcing it through a mold or die

Examples

Plastic extruder machine
The factory uses an extruder to produce plastic tubing.

Modern 3D printers often include an extruder to feed and melt the printing material.
