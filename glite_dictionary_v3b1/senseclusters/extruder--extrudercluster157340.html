extruder

noun
machine for shaping material
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstruːdər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstruːdə / (BrE)
a machine that shapes material by forcing it through a mold or die

Examples

Plastic extruder machine
The factory uses an extruder to produce plastic tubing.
An extruder can shape metal, plastic, or food products by pushing them through a die.
Modern 3D printers often include an extruder to feed and melt the printing material.
The factory uses an extruder to make plastic pipes for construction.
Modern food production often relies on an extruder to create snacks and cereals.
Engineers adjusted the extruder settings to improve the quality of the finished product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: molder - a machine that forms material into a specific shape
  • synonym: press - a machine that shapes or compresses material
