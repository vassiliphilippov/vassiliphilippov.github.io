parolee

noun
person released from prison on parole
Pronunciation: / pəˈroʊli / (AmE), / pəˈrəʊli / (BrE)
a person who has been released from prison on parole subject to continued supervision

Examples

A parolee under supervision
After serving ten years in prison, the parolee was closely monitored by his parole officer.
The community offered support to help the parolee reintegrate into society.
Violating any conditions could send the parolee back to jail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probationer - person released on probation, under supervision but not previously imprisoned
