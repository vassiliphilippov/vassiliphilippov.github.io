t

noun
metric ton; unit of weight (1,000 kg)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ti / (AmE), / ti / (BrE)
A unit of weight equivalent to 1,000 kilograms; also known as a metric ton or tonne.

Examples

1,000 kg t
A single wind turbine can reduce carbon emissions by more than 1,500 t per year.
Steel production in the country reached 3 million t last year.
The shipment weighed over 50 t, requiring several trucks for transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ton - imperial unit of mass equal to 2,240 pounds (long ton), or 2,000 pounds (short ton)
  • synonym: tonne - metric unit of mass equal to 1,000 kilograms
