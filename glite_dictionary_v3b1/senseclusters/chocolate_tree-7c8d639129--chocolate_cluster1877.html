chocolate tree

noun
tree producing cacao beans
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːklət triː / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət triː / (BrE)
A tropical American tree that produces cacao beans, the seeds from which chocolate is made.

Examples

The chocolate tree beans
Farmers harvest beans from the chocolate tree to produce cocoa and chocolate products.
She visited a plantation where hundreds of chocolate trees grew in neat rows.
The chocolate tree thrives in humid tropical climates, especially in South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cacao tree - the tree Theobroma cacao, source of cocoa and chocolate
  • synonym: cocoa tree - another term for the cacao tree; tree from which cocoa is obtained
