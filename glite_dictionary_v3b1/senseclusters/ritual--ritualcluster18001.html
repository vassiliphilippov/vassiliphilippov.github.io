Synonyms

  • routine
    1.0 16/m
    a routine is a practical series of daily actions done for efficiency, while a ritual is often symbolic or formal and follows tradition.
  • pattern
    2.3 50/m
    a pattern is a repeated way of doing something you can observe, while a ritual is a set action often with meaning or ceremony.
  • habit
    1.0 30/m
    a habit is an automatic action you do without thinking, while a ritual is a conscious or formal set of actions with purpose.
  • custom
    1.3 7/m
    a custom is a social practice shared by a group over time, while a ritual is a formal set of acts often tied to religion or ceremony.
  • tradition
    2.0 50/m
    a tradition is a long-held belief or practice passed through generations, while a ritual is a specific set of repeated actions within that tradition.
