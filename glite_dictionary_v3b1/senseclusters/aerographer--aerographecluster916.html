aerographer

noun
weather observer
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈɡræfər / (AmE), / ˌɛərəˈɡrɑːfə / (BrE)
a person who observes, records, or reports information about the atmosphere, especially weather conditions

Examples

The aerographer reports weather
The aerographer carefully measured wind speed and humidity before sending his report.
During the storm, the aerographer provided crucial updates to the ship's captain.
Each morning, the aerographer recorded temperature and cloud cover for the weather station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorologist - a scientist who studies and forecasts the weather
  • synonym: weather forecaster - a person who predicts weather conditions
