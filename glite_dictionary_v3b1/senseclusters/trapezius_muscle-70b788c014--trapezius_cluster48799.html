trapezius muscle

noun
a major muscle of the upper back and shoulders
8.7
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiziəs ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / trəˈpiːzɪəs ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
either of two large, flat, triangular muscles that extend over the back of the neck and shoulders and help move the shoulders and arms

Examples

The trapezius muscle pain
Pain in the trapezius muscle is common among people who sit at a computer all day.
Physical therapists often recommend stretches to relieve tension in the trapezius muscle.
The trapezius muscle helps you shrug your shoulders and rotate your neck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deltoid muscle - a thick, triangular muscle covering the shoulder joint
