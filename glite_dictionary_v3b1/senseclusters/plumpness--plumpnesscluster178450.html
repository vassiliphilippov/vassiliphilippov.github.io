plumpness

noun
the state of being full or rounded
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmp.nəs / (AmE), / ˈplʌmp.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasantly full or rounded in form

Examples

The plumpness of fruit
He admired the kitten's plumpness and healthy, rounded shape.
Her face had a youthful plumpness that made her look younger than her age.
The plumpness of the berries made them especially appealing at the market.
