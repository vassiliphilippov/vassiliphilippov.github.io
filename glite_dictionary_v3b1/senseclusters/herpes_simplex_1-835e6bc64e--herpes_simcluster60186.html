herpes simplex 1

noun
herpes virus causing oral herpes
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrˌpiz ˈsɪmˌplɛks wʌn / (AmE), / ˈhɜːpiːz ˈsɪmpleks wʌn / (BrE)
a herpes virus (HSV-1) that commonly causes oral herpes, typically resulting in cold sores around the mouth

Examples

Herpes simplex 1 virus
Although herpes simplex 1 usually causes oral symptoms, it can also infect other parts of the body.
Doctors can test for herpes simplex 1 with a simple oral swab.
Most cases of cold sores are caused by herpes simplex 1.
