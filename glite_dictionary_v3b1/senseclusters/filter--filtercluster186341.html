filter

noun
device that removes impurities
3.2 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪl.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɪl.tə / (BrE)
A device or material that removes impurities or unwanted elements from a liquid, gas, light, or sound that passes through it.

Examples

A water filter
She poured the water through a filter to remove any dirt or debris.
The air filter needs to be changed every three months to ensure the system runs efficiently.
The photographer attached a red filter to his camera lens for special effects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strainer - a device for separating solid matter from a liquid
