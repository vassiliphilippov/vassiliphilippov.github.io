wallaba

noun
tropical hardwood tree
Pronunciation: / wəˈlɑːbə / (AmE), / wəˈlɑːbə / (BrE)
a tropical South American tree known for its hard, durable wood, often used in construction

Examples

A wallaba tree
The builders chose wallaba for the deck because of its resistance to weather.
In Guyana, wallaba is commonly used for making shingles and posts.
The dense wood of the wallaba tree makes it ideal for heavy construction projects.
