gas burner

noun
device producing a flame by burning gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈbɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈbɜːnə / (BrE)
a device that produces a steady flame by burning combustible gas, used for heating or cooking

Examples

A gas burner flame
A laboratory gas burner provides a reliable flame for experiments.
Make sure the gas burner is turned off after you finish cooking.
The chef adjusted the gas burner to simmer the sauce at a low heat.

Related Terms
