eructation

noun
expelling gas from the stomach by mouth
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrʌkˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌrʌkˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of noisily expelling gas from the stomach through the mouth; a burp or belch

Examples

A loud eructation
After drinking soda quickly, John experienced a loud eructation that startled the people around him.
She apologized to the table for her accidental eructation during dinner.
The doctor explained that frequent eructation can be a symptom of digestive disorders.
