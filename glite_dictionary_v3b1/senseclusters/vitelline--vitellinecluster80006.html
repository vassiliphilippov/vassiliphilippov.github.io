vitelline

adjective
of egg yolk
Pronunciation: / vɪˈtɛlɪn / (AmE), / vɪˈtɛlɪn / (BrE)
relating to or resembling egg yolk, especially in biological contexts

Examples

Vitelline membrane
The vitelline membrane protects the developing embryo inside the egg.
Scientists studied the vitelline layer to understand how nutrients pass to the embryo.
The color of the vitelline substance can indicate the health of the egg.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yolk - the yellow part of an egg
