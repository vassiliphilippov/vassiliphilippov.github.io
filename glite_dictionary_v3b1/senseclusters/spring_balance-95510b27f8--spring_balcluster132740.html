spring balance

noun
device for measuring weight using a spring
8.0
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈbæl.əns / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈbæl.əns / (BrE)
a device for measuring weight by the tension applied to a helical spring, indicated on a scale

Examples

A spring balance scale
A spring balance is often used in classrooms to demonstrate the concept of force.
Always check that your spring balance is calibrated before weighing objects.
The chemistry teacher used a spring balance to measure the weight of the samples.
