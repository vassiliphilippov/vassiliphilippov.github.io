reproduce

verb
to recreate or copy something such as a sound or image
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.prəˈdus / (AmE), / ˌriː.prəˈdjuːs / (BrE)
to create again or make a copy of a sound, image, idea, mood, or atmosphere

Examples

To reproduce a sound
It is difficult to reproduce the warm atmosphere of her old studio in a new space.
The artist managed to reproduce the intricate patterns of the tapestry in his drawing.
This state-of-the-art speaker can reproduce the subtle notes of a live concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copy - to make a thing that is similar or identical to another
  • synonym: replicate - to make an exact copy of; to duplicate
