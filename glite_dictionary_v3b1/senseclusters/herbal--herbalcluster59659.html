herbal

noun
book on medicinal plants
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrbəl / (AmE), / ˈhɜːbəl / (BrE)
a book that describes plants and their uses, especially for medicine

Examples

An old herbal book
The ancient herbal was filled with detailed drawings of rare plants.
She consulted a herbal to learn about natural remedies for headaches.
Many medieval doctors relied on a herbal to treat their patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botany - the scientific study of plants
  • synonym: pharmacopoeia - an official book listing medicines and their uses
