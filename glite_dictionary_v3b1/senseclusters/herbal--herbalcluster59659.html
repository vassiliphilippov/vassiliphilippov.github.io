herbal

noun
book on medicinal plants
4.7 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…úrb…ôl / (AmE), / Ààh…úÀêb…ôl / (BrE)

Examples

An old herbal book

Many medieval doctors relied on a herbal to treat their patients.
