tillering

noun
bow limb adjustment
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪlərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪlərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of adjusting or shaping a bow's limbs so they bend evenly and the bow performs correctly

Examples

Bow tillering process
Proper tillering is essential to ensure the bow bends smoothly and shoots accurately.
He spent hours on tillering to make sure both limbs of the bow were balanced.
Mistakes during tillering can weaken the bow and affect its performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bow making - the craft of constructing bows for archery
  • synonym: tuning - the process of adjusting an instrument for optimal performance
