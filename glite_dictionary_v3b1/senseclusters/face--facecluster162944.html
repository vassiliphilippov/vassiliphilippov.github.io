face

noun
dignity or reputation as seen by others
Pronunciation: / feɪs / (AmE), / feɪs / (BrE)
A person's dignity or prestige as viewed by others; the respect or status someone has in the eyes of other people.

Examples

Lose face
After the scandal, the politician struggled to regain his face in the community.
In many cultures, saving face is extremely important during business negotiations.
She handled the criticism gracefully, determined not to lose face in front of her colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honor - high respect or esteem; a sense of ethical conduct
  • degree lower: lose face - to be humiliated or lose respect in front of others
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions held about someone
