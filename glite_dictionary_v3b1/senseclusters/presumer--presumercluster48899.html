presumer

noun
one who assumes
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzuːmər / (AmE), / prɪˈzjuːmə / (BrE)
a person who assumes or believes something to be true without having proof

Examples

A presumer of facts
As a presumer, she often jumped to conclusions without checking the facts.
The article warned against being a presumer when reading news online.
He was labeled a presumer because he believed every rumor he heard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assumer - a person who takes something for granted
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions accepted opinions
