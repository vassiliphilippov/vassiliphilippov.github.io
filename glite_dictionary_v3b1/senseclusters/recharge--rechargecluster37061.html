recharge

verb
to restore energy
3.6 1.15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàt É…ërd í / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàt É…ëÀêd í / (BrE)
to restore energy, power, or strength, especially after being depleted or used up

Examples

To recharge your energy
After a busy week, he took the weekend to recharge and relax at home.
You should recharge your phone before leaving for the trip.
A short nap helped her recharge and finish her work with renewed focus.
