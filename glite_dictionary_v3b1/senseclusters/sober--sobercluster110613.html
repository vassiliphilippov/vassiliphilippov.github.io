sober

adjective
not under the influence of alcohol or drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
Not affected by alcohol or drugs; not under the influence of a chemical substance.

Examples

Stay sober at parties
After years of struggling with addiction, she has been sober for three years.
He decided to stay sober at the party so he could drive home safely.
It's important for pilots to be completely sober before flying an aircraft.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drunk - affected by alcohol; intoxicated
  • antonym: intoxicated - under the influence of alcohol or drugs
