mono

adjective
single-channel audio
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀêno ä / (AmE), / Ààm…ín…ô ä / (BrE)
(of sound) transmitted, recorded, or reproduced using a single audio channel, as opposed to stereo.

Examples

Mono sound system
Some audiophiles prefer mono recordings for their clarity and focus.
The album was originally released in mono before the stereo version came out.
This old radio only plays in mono, not stereo.

Synonyms

  • monophonic
    7.0 0.02/m
    monophonic is the technical adjective for single-channel sound in recording and playback, while mono means one-channel audio.
  • single-channel
    4.8 0.27/m
    single-channel refers to using only one audio channel for recording or transmission, while mono is the usual term for that sound.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stereo - (of sound) using two or more channels to create an impression of directionality and depth
