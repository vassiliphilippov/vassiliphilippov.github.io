mono

adjective
single-channel audio
5.8 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊ / (BrE)
(of sound) transmitted, recorded, or reproduced using a single audio channel, as opposed to stereo.

Examples

Mono sound system
Some audiophiles prefer mono recordings for their clarity and focus.
The album was originally released in mono before the stereo version came out.
This old radio only plays in mono, not stereo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stereo - (of sound) using two or more channels to create an impression of directionality and depth
