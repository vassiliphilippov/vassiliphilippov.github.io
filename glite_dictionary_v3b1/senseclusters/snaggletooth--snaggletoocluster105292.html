snaggletooth

noun
crooked tooth
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæɡəlˌtuθ / (AmE), / ˈsnæɡl̩ˌtuːθ / (BrE)
a tooth that is crooked, uneven, or sticks out from the others in the mouth

Examples

A crooked snaggletooth
The little boy's snaggletooth made his smile unique and charming.
She decided to get braces to fix her snaggletooth before graduation.
Despite his snaggletooth, he was always confident when speaking in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crooked tooth - a tooth that is not straight or aligned
  • antonym: straight tooth - a tooth that is properly aligned with the others
