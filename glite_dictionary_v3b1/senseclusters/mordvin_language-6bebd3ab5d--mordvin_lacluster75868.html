Mordvin language

noun
Finnic language of Mordvinians
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrdvɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːdvɪn ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Finno-Ugric language or group of languages spoken by the Mordvinian people in Russia

Examples

Mordvin language spoken
Some ancient texts are preserved in the Mordvin language.
The Mordvin language has two primary dialects: Erzya and Moksha.
There are efforts to revive the Mordvin language among younger Mordvinians.
