convalescence

noun
period of recovering health after illness
7.6 0.31/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀênv…ôÀàl…õs…ôns / (AmE), / Àåk…ínv…ôÀàles…ôns / (BrE)
the gradual recovery of health and strength after illness or injury, especially through rest

Examples

A convalescence period
After surgery, his convalescence lasted several weeks before he could return to work.
She spent much of her convalescence reading and taking short walks.
The peaceful gardens were designed to aid patients in their convalescence.

Synonyms

  • recuperation
    5.6 0.75/m
    recuperation is the process of slowly regaining physical strength through rest and care, while convalescence emphasizes gradual recovery after illness.
  • recovery
    3.0 16/m
    recovery means returning to a normal state of health or mind after illness or difficulty, while convalescence focuses on slow, rest-based return of strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recovery - the process of becoming well again after illness
  • antonym: relapse - a return of illness after a period of improvement
