upside-down

adjective
with top and bottom reversed
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌp.saɪdˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ˌʌp.saɪdˈdaʊn / (BrE)
with the top and bottom positions reversed; turned so that the upper side is where the lower normally is.

Examples

Upside-down position
He accidentally held the map upside-down and got lost.
Please check if the label has been put on upside-down.
The picture frame was hanging upside-down on the wall.
