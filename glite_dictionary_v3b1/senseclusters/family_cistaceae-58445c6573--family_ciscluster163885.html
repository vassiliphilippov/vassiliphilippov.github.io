family Cistaceae

noun
a family of Mediterranean shrubs or woody herbs
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli sɪˈsteɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli sɪˈsteɪsiː / (BrE)
A family of shrubs or woody herbs found in temperate regions, especially the Mediterranean, known for their simple leaves and showy flowers.

Examples

family Cistaceae shrubs

Many members of the family Cistaceae are cultivated for their attractive flowers.
The family Cistaceae includes well-known species such as the rockrose.
