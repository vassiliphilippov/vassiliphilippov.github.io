blossomy

adjective
rosy or pinkish
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːsəmi / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəmi / (BrE)
having a color, appearance, or quality like blossoms, especially rosy or pinkish in tone

Examples

Blossomy colors
The artist painted the sky in blossomy shades of pink and orange at sunset.
Her cheeks looked blossomy after a brisk walk in the cold air.
He admired the blossomy glow of the spring garden in the morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: florid - having a red or flushed complexion
  • antonym: pale - having little color or lacking brightness
  • synonym: rosy - having a pink or reddish color like a rose
