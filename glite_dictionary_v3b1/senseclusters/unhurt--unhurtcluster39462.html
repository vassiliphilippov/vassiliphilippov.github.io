Synonyms

  • unharmed
    2.1 1.36/m
    unharmed describes a person who has no injury after a fall, while unhurt is a shorter, everyday way to say that.
  • uninjured
    1.8 0.47/m
    uninjured focuses on having no wounds after an accident, while unhurt is a broader everyday adjective for not being harmed.
  • unscathed
    6.3 1.87/m
    unscathed suggests coming through danger with no harm at all, while unhurt is a plain word for not being injured.
  • without a scratch
    2.7 0.28/m
    without a scratch paints a vivid image of no visible marks after an accident, while unhurt simply means not harmed.
  • safe and sound
    1.5 1.19/m
    safe and sound often describes being free from danger and worry, while unhurt focuses only on physical lack of injury.
