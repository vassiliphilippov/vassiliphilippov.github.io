coin blank

noun
flat metal disk for coin-making
Pronunciation: / kɔɪn blæŋk / (AmE), / kɔɪn blæŋk / (BrE)
a flat, undecorated metal disk that is prepared to be stamped or struck to create a coin

Examples

A coin blank disk
Collectors sometimes seek out rare coin blanks as numismatic curiosities.
Each coin blank must meet strict standards of weight and composition before use.
The factory produces thousands of coin blanks each day before they are struck as coins.
