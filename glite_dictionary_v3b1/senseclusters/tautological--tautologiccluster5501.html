tautological

adjective
needlessly repetitive; redundant in wording
8.3 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / t…ît…ôÀàl…ëd í…™k…ôl / (AmE), / t…îÀêt…ôÀàl…íd í…™k…ôl / (BrE)
expressing the same idea more than once in different words, so that the statement is needlessly repetitive

Examples

Tautological statement
His explanation became tautological, repeating the same point with different words.
The phrase 'free gift' is tautological because all gifts are by definition free.
Writers are advised to avoid tautological expressions in academic essays.
