wash-and-wear

adjective
requiring little or no ironing after washing
5.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /wɑːʃ.ən.ˈwer/ /; / /wɑːʃ.ænd.ˈwer/ (AmE), /wɒʃ.ən.ˈweə/ /; / /wɒʃ.ænd.ˈweə/ (BrE)
