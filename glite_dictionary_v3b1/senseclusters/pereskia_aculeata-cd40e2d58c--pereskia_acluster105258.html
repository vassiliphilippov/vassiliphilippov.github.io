Pereskia aculeata

noun
a spiny, climbing plant with white flowers and small fruits
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛskiə əkjuˈleɪtə / (AmE), / pəˈrɛskiə əkjuːˈleɪtə / (BrE)
A species of West Indian woody climbing plant with spiny stems and clusters of fragrant white flowers, followed by small yellow to orange fruits.
