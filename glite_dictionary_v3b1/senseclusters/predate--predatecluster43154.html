predate

verb
hunt or prey on (animals)
3.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈdeɪt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈdeɪt/ /; / /prə.ˈdeɪt/ (AmE), /priː.ˈdeɪt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈdeɪt/ (BrE)
(of an animal) to hunt, kill, and eat another animal; to prey on.

Examples

Lions predate zebras
Larger fish often predate on smaller species in the reef.
The birds predate insects that hide beneath the leaves.
Wolves typically predate deer during the harsh winter months.
