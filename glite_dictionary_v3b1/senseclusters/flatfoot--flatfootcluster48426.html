Synonyms

  • sunken arch
    6.1 0.00/m
    sunken arch is a flattened foot arch that touches the ground, while flatfoot is the common name for this condition.
  • pes planus
    7.3 0.00/m
    pes planus is the medical Latin term used by doctors, while flatfoot is the everyday name people use in speech.
  • fallen arch
    7.4 0.01/m
    fallen arch names the arch that collapses when standing, while flatfoot refers to the whole foot appearing flat and spread.
  • splayfoot
    9.3 0.00/m
    splayfoot describes a flattened foot that is also abnormally wide, while flatfoot focuses on the arch being low and sole touching ground.
