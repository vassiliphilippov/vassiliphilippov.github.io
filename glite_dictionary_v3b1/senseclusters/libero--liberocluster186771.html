libero

noun
football sweeper
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈliːbəˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈliːbərəʊ / (BrE)
a defensive player in football (soccer) who plays behind the main defenders to intercept attacks and start new plays

Examples

Soccer libero
The team's libero was crucial in stopping the opponent's strikers during the match.
As a skilled libero, she often anticipated passes and started quick counterattacks.
Many coaches value a libero who can read the game and organize the defense.
