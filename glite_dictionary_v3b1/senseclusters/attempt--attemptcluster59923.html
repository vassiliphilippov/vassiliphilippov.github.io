attempt

verb
try to begin something (esp. difficult)
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
to try to do or achieve something, especially something difficult or new

Examples

To attempt a task
I wouldn't attempt such a complex recipe without some help.
She decided to attempt a solo climb of the mountain despite the risks.
The team will attempt to break the world record this year.
He attempted to lift the heavy box, but it was too much for him.
She decided to attempt the challenging hike despite the bad weather forecast.
The company will attempt to launch its new product next month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fail - to not achieve the desired result
  • antonym: succeed - to achieve the desired result
  • synonym: try - to make an effort to do something
  • synonym: undertake - to begin to do or take responsibility for something
