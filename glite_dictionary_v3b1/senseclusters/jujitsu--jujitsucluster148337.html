jujitsu

noun
Japanese martial art of self-defense
5.8 7.7
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈdʒɪtsu / (AmE), / dʒuːˈdʒɪtsuː / (BrE)
A Japanese martial art of self-defense using holds, throws, and the attacker's own force, traditionally practiced without weapons.

Examples

Jujitsu martial art
He has studied jujitsu for over ten years and now teaches beginner classes.
Many self-defense programs incorporate basic jujitsu techniques.
She used her knowledge of jujitsu to defend herself during the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: judo - A modern Japanese martial art derived from traditional jujitsu
  • antonym: karate - A Japanese martial art with a focus on striking
