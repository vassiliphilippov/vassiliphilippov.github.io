cigar-shaped

adjective
tapering at both ends; cigar-like in shape
Pronunciation: / sɪˈɡɑr ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / sɪˈɡɑː ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
tapering at both ends; having an elongated shape like that of a cigar.

Examples

A cigar-shaped stone
He brought back a cigar-shaped stone he found on the mountain trail.
Many submarines have a cigar-shaped design to minimize resistance underwater.
The scientist described the mysterious object as cigar-shaped in the telescope.
