Synonyms

  • spindle-shaped
    6.3 0.04/m
    spindle-shaped describes objects that are narrow at both ends and wider in the middle, while cigar-shaped means long and tapering like a cigar.
  • fusiform
    9.9 0.06/m
    fusiform is a technical word for shapes that taper at both ends and swell in the middle, while cigar-shaped is a more common, everyday description.
