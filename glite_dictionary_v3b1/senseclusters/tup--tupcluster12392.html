tup

verb
to mate (of sheep)
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʌp / (AmE), / tʌp / (BrE)
to mate, especially referring to a ram mating with a ewe

Examples

To tup a ewe
The farmer watched as the ram tupped several ewes in the field.
During breeding season, the shepherd made sure each ewe was tupped by a healthy ram.
It is important to record which rams have tupped which ewes for accurate breeding records.
