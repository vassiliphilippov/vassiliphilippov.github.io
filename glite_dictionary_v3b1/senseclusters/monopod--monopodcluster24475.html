monopod

noun
single-legged robot
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑnəˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəˌpɒd / (BrE)
a single-legged robotic platform or vehicle used for movement, balance research, or navigating uneven terrain

Examples

Robotic monopod platform
The engineers tested the monopod on rocky ground to evaluate its stability.
A monopod can hop over obstacles that would stop wheeled robots.
Researchers observed how the monopod maintained balance while carrying a load.
