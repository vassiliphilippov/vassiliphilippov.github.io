recycler

noun
person who reuses materials
3.0
Pronunciation: / riˈsaɪklər / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪklə / (BrE)
a person, company, or machine that collects and processes used materials so they can be used again instead of thrown away

Examples

A recycler collects bottles
The city hired a new recycler to manage its plastic and paper waste.
Many recyclers sort materials by type before sending them to processing plants.
A dedicated recycler can help reduce the amount of trash sent to landfills.
The recycler separates paper, plastic, and metal before sending them to the factory.
Many cities encourage every household to use a recycler for their waste materials.
A modern recycler can process tons of glass bottles every day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: environmentalist - a person who works to protect the environment
  • synonym: recycling center - a place where materials are collected and processed for reuse
  • synonym: waste collector - a person who collects garbage or recyclable materials
