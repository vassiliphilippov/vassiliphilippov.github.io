stake

verb
kill by piercing with a sharp pole
3.8
Pronunciation: / steɪk / (AmE), / steɪk / (BrE)
to kill someone by piercing them with a sharp pole or spear, especially as a form of execution

Examples

To stake a vampire
In some legends, a vampire can only be killed by staking it through the heart.
The ancient executioners staked the prisoners at dawn.
The tyrant ordered his enemies to be staked as a warning to others.
