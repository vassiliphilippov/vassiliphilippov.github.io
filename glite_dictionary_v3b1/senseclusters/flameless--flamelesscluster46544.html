flameless

adjective
without a flame
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmləs / (BrE)

Examples

The flameless heater is ideal for use in tents and small spaces.

A flameless cooking device can be used safely in offices and dorm rooms.
