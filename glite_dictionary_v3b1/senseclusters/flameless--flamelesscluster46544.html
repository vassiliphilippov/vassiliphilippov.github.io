flameless

adjective
without a flame
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmləs / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmləs / (BrE)
not producing or using a visible flame, especially for safety or convenience

Examples

A flameless heater
The flameless heater is ideal for use in tents and small spaces.
Parents often choose flameless candles to avoid fire hazards at home.
A flameless cooking device can be used safely in offices and dorm rooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireless - not involving or producing fire
  • antonym: flaming - burning with a visible flame
