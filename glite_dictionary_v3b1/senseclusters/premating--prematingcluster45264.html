premating

noun
period before mating
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the period or behaviors occurring immediately before mating, often involving courtship or preparation for reproduction

Examples

A premating period
Researchers observed the premating behaviors of the birds to study their selection process.
Environmental factors can influence the length of the premating period in many animal species.
The premating rituals of some insects involve complex dances and displays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtship - the behaviors animals use to attract a mate
  • antonym: postmating - the period or events following mating
