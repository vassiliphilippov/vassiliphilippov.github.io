gold

noun
a deep yellow color
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld / (AmE), / ɡəʊld / (BrE)
a deep yellow color resembling that of the metal gold

Examples

A gold color
As autumn arrived, the leaves turned a brilliant gold.
Her dress shimmered in a soft shade of gold under the stage lights.
The artist chose gold for the background to add a feeling of warmth.
