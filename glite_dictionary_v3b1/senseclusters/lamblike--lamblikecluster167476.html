lamblike

adjective
meek and gentle; like a lamb
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlæmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
meek and gentle; resembling a lamb in behavior or temperament

Examples

A lamblike manner
He accepted the criticism in a lamblike manner, never protesting or raising his voice.
Her lamblike demeanor made her instantly likable to everyone she met.
The students listened with lamblike obedience to the principal’s instructions.

Related Terms
