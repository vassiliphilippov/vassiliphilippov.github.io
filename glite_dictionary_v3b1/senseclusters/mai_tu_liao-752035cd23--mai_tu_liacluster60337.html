Mai tu liao

interjection
Cantonese exclamation of surprise or frustration
Pronunciation: / maɪ tu liːˈaʊ / (AmE), / maɪ tuː lɪˈaʊ / (BrE)
An exclamatory phrase, borrowed from Cantonese, used to express surprise, disbelief, or frustration.

Examples

Mai tu liao expression
Mai tu liao! I can't believe we missed the last train.
She walked into her flooded kitchen and muttered, 'Mai tu liao.'
When his laptop crashed during the exam, he exclaimed, 'Mai tu liao!'
