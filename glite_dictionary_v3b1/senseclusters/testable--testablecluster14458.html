testable

adjective
able to be tested
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be checked, examined, or proven by experiment or evidence

Examples

A testable theory
A good scientific theory must be testable through observation or experiment.
The teacher asked if the students' questions were testable in a classroom setting.
His claim was not testable, so it could not be accepted as scientific fact.

Related Terms

  • antonym: untestable - not able to be checked or proven by experiment
  • synonym: verifiable - able to be proven true or false
