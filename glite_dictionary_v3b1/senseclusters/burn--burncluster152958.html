burn

noun
painful feeling of heat, as from injury
Pronunciation: / bɝːn / (AmE), / bɜːn / (BrE)
a painful feeling of heat experienced in the skin or body, as if being on fire, often caused by injury, irritation, or certain chemicals

Examples

A sharp burn
After spilling the hot coffee, she felt a sharp burn on her hand.
He described the sunburn as an intense burn that lasted for several days.
The ointment soothed the burn he felt from the chemical exposure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sting - a sharp, burning pain
