green

noun
urban open grassy area
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
an area of grassy land in a town or city, kept open for public use or recreation

Examples

Village green
Children played happily on the green in the center of the village.
Locals gather on the green for community events during the summer.
We spread a blanket on the green and enjoyed a picnic lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: park - a large public green area in a town, used for recreation
  • synonym: square - an open, often grassy area in a town, typically surrounded by buildings
