heart quicken

verb
when the heart beats faster
5.7
Pronunciation: / hɑrt ˈkwɪkən / (AmE), / hɑːt ˈkwɪkən / (BrE)
(of one's heart) to begin to beat faster, especially because of excitement, fear, or anticipation.

Examples

My heart quickened
His heart quickened with anticipation as the results were announced.
The sight of his childhood home made his heart quicken with nostalgia.
Whenever she heard footsteps behind her, she felt her heart quicken.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heart race - when one's heart beats very fast, often from excitement or anxiety
