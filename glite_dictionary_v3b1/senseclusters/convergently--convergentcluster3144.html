convergently

adverb
in a similar direction
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrdʒəntli / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a manner that moves or develops toward the same point or result as something else

Examples

Develop convergently toward a goal
The two technologies developed convergently to solve the same problem in different industries.
Several artists arrived convergently at similar styles despite never meeting each other.
The rivers flow convergently toward the central lake, joining together at its edge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divergently - in a way that moves apart or in different directions
  • synonym: similarly - in a similar way
