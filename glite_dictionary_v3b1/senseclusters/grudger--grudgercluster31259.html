grudger

noun
person who holds grudges
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌdʒər / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌdʒə / (BrE)
a person who holds a grudge and is unwilling to forgive others for past wrongs

Examples

A grudger holds anger
Everyone knew him as a grudger who never forgot a slight.
It is difficult to work with a grudger because they bring up old issues.
She tried not to be a grudger, but some betrayals were hard to forgive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiver - a person who is willing to forgive others
  • synonym: resentful - feeling or expressing bitterness or indignation
