commissariat

noun
supply department
8.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɪs.ə.rɪ.ət/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɪs.ə.rɪ.ət/ (BrE)
a department or organization responsible for supplying food, equipment, and other provisions, especially in the military or police

Examples

Army commissariat
The commissariat was responsible for distributing food to the soldiers during the campaign.
After the disaster, the local commissariat organized emergency supplies for the affected families.
He worked in the police commissariat, ensuring officers had the equipment they needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supply department - a division that provides necessary materials and provisions
