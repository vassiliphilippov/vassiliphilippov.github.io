non-radical

adjective
without roots
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈrædɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒn ˈrædɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving a radical sign or root; expressed without root expressions in mathematics.

Examples

Non-radical expression
The teacher asked students to rewrite the equation in a non-radical form.
A non-radical solution is often easier to work with in algebraic problems.
After simplifying, the expression became completely non-radical and easier to evaluate.
