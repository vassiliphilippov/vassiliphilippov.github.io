testy

adjective
easily annoyed or irritable
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛsti / (AmE), / ˈtɛsti / (BrE)
Easily annoyed, impatient, or irritable; quick to become angry or agitated.

Examples

A testy reply
As the meeting dragged on, their testy exchanges became more frequent.
He grew testy after waiting an hour for his appointment to begin.
She gave a testy reply when asked about the delays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily angered or agitated
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or made angry
