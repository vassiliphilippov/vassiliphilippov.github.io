reducer

noun
device that decreases
5.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈduːsər / (AmE), / rɪˈjuːsə / (BrE)
a device or substance that decreases the level, amount, or intensity of something

Examples

Noise reducer device
The factory installed a noise reducer to make the environment quieter.
A water pressure reducer was added to prevent damage to the pipes.
The doctor recommended a stress reducer to help manage anxiety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amplifier - a device that increases the level or strength of something
  • synonym: dampener - a device that reduces vibration or noise
