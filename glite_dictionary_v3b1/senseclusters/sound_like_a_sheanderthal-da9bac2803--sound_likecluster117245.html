sound like a sheanderthal

verb
seem to have primitive views, esp. on gender
9.9
Pronunciation: / saʊnd laɪk ə ʃiːˈæn.dərˌθæl / (AmE), / saʊnd laɪk ə ʃiːˈæn.dəˌθal / (BrE)
to appear or be regarded as having outdated, primitive, or backward views, especially regarding gender equality.
