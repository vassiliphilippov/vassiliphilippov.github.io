giant clam

noun
very large tropical clam
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt klæm / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt klæm / (BrE)
a very large tropical clam inhabiting coral reefs, especially in the South Pacific, sometimes weighing up to 500 pounds

Examples

A giant clam shell
A single giant clam can live for over 100 years in its natural habitat.
Divers can often spot a giant clam wedged into coral reefs.
The giant clam is famous for its massive shell and striking colors.
