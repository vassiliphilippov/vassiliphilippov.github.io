Kuwaiti dinar

noun
basic monetary unit of Kuwait
5.2
Pronunciation: / kuˈweɪti dɪˈnɑr / (AmE), / kuːˈweɪti dɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Kuwait, equal to 1,000 fils
