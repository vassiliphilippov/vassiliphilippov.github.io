angiolipoma

noun
benign fatty tumor
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊlaɪˈpoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌændʒiəʊlɪˈpəʊmə / (BrE)
a benign tumor consisting of fat and blood vessels, usually found under the skin

Examples

An angiolipoma tumor
After noticing a small lump on his arm, he learned it was an angiolipoma after medical tests.
The surgeon removed the angiolipoma quickly, and the patient recovered without complications.
Most angiolipomas are harmless and do not require treatment unless they cause discomfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemangioma - a benign tumor made of blood vessels
  • synonym: lipoma - a benign tumor made of fat tissue
