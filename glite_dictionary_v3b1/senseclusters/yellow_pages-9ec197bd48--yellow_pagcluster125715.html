yellow pages

noun
telephone directory for businesses and services
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ ˌpeɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ ˌpeɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
a telephone directory or a section of one, traditionally printed on yellow paper, listing businesses and services by category, often with advertisements

Examples

The yellow pages directory
He flipped through the yellow pages looking for a nearby car repair shop.
The company decided to stop advertising in the yellow pages and focus on online marketing instead.
You can find a local plumber in the yellow pages under 'Plumbing Services'.

Related Terms

  • antonym: white pages - telephone directory listing individuals alphabetically by name
