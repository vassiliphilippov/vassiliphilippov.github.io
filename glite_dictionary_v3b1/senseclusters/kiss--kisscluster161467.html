kiss

verb
touch gently or lightly (literary)
Pronunciation: / kɪs / (AmE), / kɪs / (BrE)
(literary) To touch lightly or gently in a way that evokes tenderness, as with a soft breeze or gentle rain.

Examples

To kiss the skin
A gentle breeze kissed her cheek as she walked along the shore.
The morning sun kissed the mountain peaks, bathing them in golden light.
The soft mist kissed the fields at dawn, leaving everything glistening.
