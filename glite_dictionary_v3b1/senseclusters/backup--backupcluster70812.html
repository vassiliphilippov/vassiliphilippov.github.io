backup

noun
a substitute or supporting person
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.ʌp/ /; / /ˈbæk.əp/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.ʌp/ /; / /ˈbæk.əp/ (BrE)
