paneling

noun
decorative panels on walls or doors
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpænəlɪŋ / (BrE)
A set of flat or decorated panels that form part of the surface of a wall or door, often used as decoration or to cover the surface.

Examples

Wood paneling on walls
Oak paneling was installed on the lower half of the dining room walls for an elegant touch.
The dark wood paneling in the library gave it a warm, traditional feel.
They decided to remove the old paneling and paint the walls a light color instead.
The old library has ornate panelling along all four walls.
The room's wooden panelling gave it a warm, classic feel.
Victorian houses often feature decorative panelling on their doors and hallways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel - a flat or decorated piece forming part of a surface
