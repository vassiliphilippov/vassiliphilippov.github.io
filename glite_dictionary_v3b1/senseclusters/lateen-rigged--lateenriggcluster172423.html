lateen-rigged

adjective
rigged with a triangular sail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ləˈtin rɪɡd / (AmE), / ləˈtiːn rɪɡd / (BrE)
(of a sailing vessel) fitted with a lateen sail, which is a triangular sail mounted on a long yard set at an angle to the mast.

Examples

Lateen-rigged sailing vessel
Many traditional Mediterranean fishing vessels are lateen-rigged for better maneuverability.
The boat was lateen-rigged, allowing it to sail efficiently against the wind.
We admired the elegant lines of the lateen-rigged dhow as it crossed the bay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: square-rigged - of a sailing vessel rigged with square sails
