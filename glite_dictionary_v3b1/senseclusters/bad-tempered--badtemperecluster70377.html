bad-tempered

adjective
easily annoyed or angered
1.6
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈtɛmpərd / (AmE), / bæd ˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
often becoming easily annoyed or angry; having a tendency to show irritation or anger

Examples

A bad-tempered person
Children often avoid their bad-tempered neighbor, who rarely smiles.
She became bad-tempered after a long and stressful day at work.
The bad-tempered manager shouted at his staff for making a minor mistake.
