pronglike

adjective
resembling a prong
Pronunciation: / ˈprɔŋˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈprɒŋˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a shape or appearance similar to a prong or pointed projection

Examples

Pronglike shape
The insect's pronglike legs help it grasp onto leaves.

The sculpture featured several pronglike extensions jutting out from its base.
