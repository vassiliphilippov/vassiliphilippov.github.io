submergence

noun
the act of being submerged in liquid
8.4 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / s…ôbÀàm…ùÀêd í…ôns / (AmE), / s…ôbÀàm…úÀêd í…ôns / (BrE)
the act or process of sinking or being completely covered with water or another liquid

Examples

Complete submergence in water
The plant cannot survive prolonged submergence in saltwater.
The sudden submergence of the coastline was caused by the powerful tsunami.
Years of flooding led to the gradual submergence of the ancient city.

Synonyms

  • submersion
    6.2 0.08/m
    submersion is putting something completely under a liquid, while submergence can mean sinking or being covered by water.
  • immersion
    4.4 0.70/m
    immersion is the act of being completely covered in a liquid, while submergence also includes sinking and passive covering.
  • sinking
    2.8 1.28/m
    sinking is the process of going down below the surface of water, while submergence also means being fully covered by liquid.
