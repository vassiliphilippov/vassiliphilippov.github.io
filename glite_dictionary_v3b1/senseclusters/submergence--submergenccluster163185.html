submergence

noun
the act of being submerged in liquid
6.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈmɜːr.dʒəns/ (AmE), /səb.ˈmɜː.dʒəns/ (BrE)
the act or process of sinking or being completely covered with water or another liquid

Examples

Complete submergence in water
The plant cannot survive prolonged submergence in saltwater.
The sudden submergence of the coastline was caused by the powerful tsunami.
Years of flooding led to the gradual submergence of the ancient city.
