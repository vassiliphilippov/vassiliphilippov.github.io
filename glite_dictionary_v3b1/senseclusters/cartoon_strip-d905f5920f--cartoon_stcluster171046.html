Synonyms

  • comic strip
    1.8 1.35/m
    comic strip is the standard term used in newspapers and comic books, while cartoon strip is an alternative name for the same short drawn story.
  • strip
    3.4 4.22/m
    strip is a shorter, more general word for a sequence of panels in newspapers, while cartoon strip names that comic feature.
  • funnies
    5.8 0.35/m
    funnies refers to the informal comics section with many strips and jokes, while cartoon strip names a single drawn story.
