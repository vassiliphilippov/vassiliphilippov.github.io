hospital bed

noun
adjustable bed used in hospitals
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑspɪtl bɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl bɛd / (BrE)
a bed designed for use in hospitals with adjustable sections to raise or lower the head, middle, or foot as needed for patient care

Examples

Modern hospital beds
Modern hospital beds can be adjusted electronically with a remote control.
Nurses regularly check to make sure each hospital bed is clean and functioning properly.
The patient was moved to a hospital bed designed for maximum comfort and support.
