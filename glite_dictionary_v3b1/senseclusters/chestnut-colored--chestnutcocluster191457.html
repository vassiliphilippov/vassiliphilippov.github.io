chestnut-colored

adjective
of a rich chestnut brown color
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛs.nət ˌkʌ.lɚd /; / ˈtʃɛsˌnʌt ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nət ˌkʌl.əd /; / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt ˌkʌl.əd / (BrE)
