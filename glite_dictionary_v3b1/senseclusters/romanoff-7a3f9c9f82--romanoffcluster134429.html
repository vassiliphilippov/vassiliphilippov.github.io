Romanoff

proper noun
the Russian royal family (1613–1917)
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.məˌnɔf /; / ˈroʊ.məˌnɑf / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.məˌnɒf / (BrE)
the Russian imperial family that ruled from 1613 to 1917, or a member of that family
