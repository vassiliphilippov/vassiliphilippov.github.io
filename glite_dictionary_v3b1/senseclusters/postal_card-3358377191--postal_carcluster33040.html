postal card

noun
official card for mailing without envelope
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstl kɑrd / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstl kɑːd / (BrE)
a card issued by a postal authority for sending messages without an envelope

Examples

A postal card message
He wrote his address on the postal card before sending it to his friend.
The postal card was stamped and sent directly to her office.
You don't need an envelope when you use a postal card to send a message.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postcard - a card for sending messages by mail, typically with a picture on one side
