equipollent

adjective
equal in magnitude and direction
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.kwɪ.ˈpɑː.lənt/ /; / /iː.kwɪ.ˈpɑː.lənt/ /; / /e.kwɪ.ˈpɑː.lənt/ (AmE), /ɪ.kwɪ.ˈpɒ.lənt/ /; / /iː.kwɪ.ˈpɒ.lənt/ /; / /e.kwɪ.ˈpɒ.lənt/ (BrE)
having the same magnitude and direction, so that one can be moved to coincide exactly with the other, especially in geometry or vector mathematics
