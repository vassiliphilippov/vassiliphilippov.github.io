flask

noun
bottle with a narrow neck
Pronunciation: / flæsk / (AmE), / flɑːsk / (BrE)
A bottle with a narrow neck, typically used for holding liquids, especially in laboratories or for carrying alcoholic drinks.

Examples

A flask of liquid
He poured the chemicals into a flask and placed it on the table.
She carried a small flask of whiskey in her pocket.
The scientist labeled each flask for the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottle - a container with a narrow neck for storing liquids
