business line

noun
type of product or service offered
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznɪs laɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs laɪn / (BrE)
a set of products or services of a similar type that a company offers

Examples

A business line of products
Technology is now the most profitable business line for the corporation.
The company is planning to expand its business line to include eco-friendly products.
They discontinued one business line to focus on their core services.
