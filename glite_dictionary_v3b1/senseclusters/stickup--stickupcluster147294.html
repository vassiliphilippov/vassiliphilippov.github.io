Synonyms

  • hold-up
    5.4 0.34/m

  • armed robbery
    3.2 1.83/m

  • heist
    6.9 2.38/m
    heist is a carefully planned theft of goods or money, often large-scale, while stickup means a quick armed robbery in person.
