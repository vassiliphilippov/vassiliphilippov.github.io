mile

noun
a very large distance or amount (informal)
Pronunciation: / maɪl / (AmE), / maɪl / (BrE)
(informal) A very large distance or amount by which someone or something falls short or exceeds a target, expectation, or comparison.

Examples

Missed by a mile
She was off on her estimate by at least a mile.
The pizza shop is better than the others by a mile.
You missed the target by a mile, but at least you tried.

Related Terms

  • synonym: league - a long distance (figurative sense)
