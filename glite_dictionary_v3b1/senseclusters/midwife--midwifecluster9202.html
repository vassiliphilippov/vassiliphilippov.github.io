midwife

noun
person trained to assist childbirth
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.waɪf / (BrE)
a person, typically a woman, trained to assist women in childbirth

Examples

A midwife helps birth
Our local clinic has an experienced midwife to support expecting mothers.
She trained for several years to become a certified midwife.
The midwife calmly coached the mother through contractions during the home birth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gynecologist - a doctor specializing in women's reproductive health
  • synonym: obstetrician - a physician specializing in childbirth and pregnancy
