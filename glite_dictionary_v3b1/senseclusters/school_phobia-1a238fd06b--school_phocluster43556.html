school phobia

noun
fear of attending school (children)
Pronunciation: / skuːl foʊˈbiːə / (AmE), / skuːl fəʊˈbɪə / (BrE)
an intense and persistent fear of attending school, typically affecting children

Examples

School phobia in children
After therapy, her school phobia gradually lessened, allowing her to return to her studies.
Many parents struggle to distinguish between mere reluctance and school phobia in their children.
The child's refusal to go to class was eventually diagnosed as school phobia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agoraphobia - fear of open or public places
  • synonym: separation anxiety - anxiety in children due to separation from home or family
