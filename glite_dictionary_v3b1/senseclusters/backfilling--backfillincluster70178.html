backfilling

noun
refilling excavated area
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbækˌfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of refilling an excavated area, such as a hole or trench, with soil or other material

Examples

Backfilling a trench
After the foundation was poured, the crew started backfilling around the new building.
Proper backfilling is essential to prevent the ground from sinking later.
The construction team completed the backfilling of the trench before paving the road.
