scape

noun
view or scene
6.2
Pronunciation: /skeɪp/ (AmE), /skeɪp/ (BrE)
a view or scene, especially as used in compound words like 'landscape' or 'cityscape'

Examples

Mountain scape
The mountain scape was filled with snow-capped peaks and deep valleys.
She admired the city scape from the rooftop restaurant as the sun set.
Artists often paint a beautiful scape to capture the feeling of a place.
The artist painted a beautiful scape of rolling hills and distant mountains.
Many photographers travel to capture the perfect natural scape at sunrise.
The word 'cityscape' uses the suffix scape to describe a view of a city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cityscape - a view or picture of a city
  • synonym: landscape - a view or picture of the natural scenery of an area
  • synonym: panorama - an unbroken view of a wide area
