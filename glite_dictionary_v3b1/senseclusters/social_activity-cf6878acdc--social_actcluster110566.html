social activity

noun
online engagement on social media
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsoʊ.ʃəl æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈsoʊ.ʃəl æk.ˈtɪv.ə.ti/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.ʃəl æk.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ˈsəʊ.ʃəl æk.ˈtɪv.ə.ti/ (BrE)
the level or pattern of a person's engagement on social media platforms, especially actions such as posting, commenting, liking, or reacting to content.

Examples

Social activity on Facebook
Her social activity on the platform included posting, commenting, and reacting to friends' updates.
The company's analytics team tracked customers' social activity to measure campaign reach and engagement.
He reduced his social activity during exams, posting less and avoiding online discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: social media activity - actions taken by users on social platforms, including posting, commenting, and reacting
