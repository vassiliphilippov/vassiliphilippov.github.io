ethambutol

noun
tuberculosis medication
Pronunciation: / ɪˌθæmˈbjuːtɔl / (AmE), / ɪˌθæmˈbjuːtɒl / (BrE)
a drug used to treat tuberculosis by preventing the growth of bacteria in the body

Examples

Ethambutol drug treatment
The doctor prescribed ethambutol as part of the tuberculosis treatment.
Patients taking ethambutol should have their vision checked regularly.
A combination of drugs, including ethambutol, is often used to treat tuberculosis effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isoniazid - a drug used to treat and prevent tuberculosis
  • synonym: rifampicin - an antibiotic used to treat tuberculosis and other bacterial infections
