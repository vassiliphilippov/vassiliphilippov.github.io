hovercar

noun
vehicle that floats
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌvərkɑːr / (AmE), / ˈhʌvəkɑː / (BrE)
a vehicle that floats or travels above the ground, typically using advanced technology such as air cushions or magnetic levitation

Examples

Futuristic hovercar ride
In the future, people might commute to work in a hovercar instead of a regular automobile.
The science fiction novel described a city filled with hovercars gliding silently above the streets.
He dreamed of owning a hovercar that could soar over traffic jams with ease.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: car - a road vehicle with four wheels, powered by an engine
  • synonym: hovercraft - a vehicle that travels over land or water on a cushion of air
