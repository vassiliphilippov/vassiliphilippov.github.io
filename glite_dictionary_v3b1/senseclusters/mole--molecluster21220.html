mole

noun
small dark spot on the skin
Pronunciation: / moʊl / (AmE), / məʊl / (BrE)
a small, dark, permanent spot or raised area on the skin, usually present from birth

Examples

A skin mole
Dermatologists recommend monitoring any changes in the size or color of a mole.
He has a small mole just above his left eyebrow.
She noticed a new mole on her arm and scheduled a check-up with her doctor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freckle - a small, light brown spot on the skin, often caused by exposure to the sun
