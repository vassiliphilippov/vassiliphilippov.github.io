gypsum board

noun
building material for interior walls
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪpsəm bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪpsəm bɔːd / (BrE)
A building material consisting of a core of gypsum plaster pressed between sheets of paper or fiberboard, used for making interior walls and ceilings.

Examples

Install gypsum board
After installing the gypsum board, they began taping and sanding the seams.
Most modern homes rely on gypsum board instead of traditional plaster for walls and ceilings.
The contractor recommended using gypsum board for the new interior walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drywall - a panel made of calcium sulfate dihydrate, similar to gypsum board; commonly used synonym
  • synonym: plasterboard - another term for gypsum board, mainly used in British English
