gantlet

noun
series of challenges
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡænt.lɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡænt.lɪt / (BrE)
a series of difficulties, challenges, or tests that someone must go through to achieve something

Examples

Face the gantlet
After moving to a new city, he faced the gantlet of finding a job and a place to live.
The contestants must run the gantlet of difficult challenges to win the competition.
She survived the gantlet of tough questions during the interview process.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easy ride - a situation without difficulties or challenges
  • synonym: ordeal - a difficult or painful experience
  • synonym: trial - a test of someone's patience or endurance
