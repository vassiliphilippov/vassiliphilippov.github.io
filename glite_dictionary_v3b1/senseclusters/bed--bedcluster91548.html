bed

noun
plot of ground for plants
Pronunciation: / bɛd / (AmE), / bɛd / (BrE)
a plot of ground, typically within a garden, where flowers or other plants are grown

Examples

A flower bed
A colorful bed of petunias brightened up the center of the park.
She arranged the tulips neatly in a bed by the front walkway.
The children helped to weed and water the vegetable bed every weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - a strip of ground along the edge of a garden for planting flowers
  • antonym: lawn - an area of short, mown grass in a garden or yard
