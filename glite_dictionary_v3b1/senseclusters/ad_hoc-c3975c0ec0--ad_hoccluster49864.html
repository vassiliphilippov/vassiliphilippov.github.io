ad hoc

adjective
for a particular purpose; improvised
6.3
Pronunciation: / æd ˈhɑk /; / æd hɑk / (AmE), / ad ˈhɒk /; / ad hɒk / (BrE)
formed or done for a particular purpose as needed; not planned in advance (improvised)

Examples

Ad hoc committee
Her team developed an ad hoc solution when the system failed.
The board created an ad hoc committee to investigate the sudden issue.
We held an ad hoc meeting to resolve the problem quickly.
She made an ad hoc decision without consulting anyone else.
The committee formed an ad hoc group to address the budget shortfall.
We need an ad hoc solution for this unexpected problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvised - done without preparation, created on the spot
