phenazine

noun
bacterial pigment compound
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnəziːn / (AmE), / ˈfiːnəziːn / (BrE)
a nitrogen-containing aromatic chemical compound, often produced by bacteria, used as a pigment or in dyes and antibiotics
