lognormal

adjective
statistical distribution
9.1
Pronunciation: / lɔgˈnɔrməl / (AmE), / lɒgˈnɔːməl / (BrE)
describing a distribution in which the logarithm of the variable is normally distributed

Examples

Lognormal distribution
The researchers found that the data fit a lognormal distribution rather than a normal one.
In economics, income levels are often modeled using a lognormal curve.
The lognormal property of the data was confirmed through statistical analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - describing a distribution that is symmetric and bell-shaped
