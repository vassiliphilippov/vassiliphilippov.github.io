hard-bitten

adjective
tough and unemotional from experience
8.9
Pronunciation: / hɑrdˈbɪtən / (AmE), / hɑːdˈbɪtən / (BrE)
tough and unemotional, especially as a result of difficult or harsh experiences

Examples

Years in the army had made him truly hard-bitten and fearless.
