eco-friendly practice

noun
environmentally safe method or action
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌikoʊˈfrɛndli ˈpræktɪs / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˈfrɛndli ˈpræktɪs / (BrE)
an action or method that does not harm the environment and promotes sustainability

Examples

An eco-friendly practice method
Composting kitchen waste is one simple eco-friendly practice anyone can start at home.
Many businesses are adopting eco-friendly practices to minimize their environmental impact.
Using solar panels is an eco-friendly practice that reduces reliance on fossil fuels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polluting practice - method that harms the environment
  • synonym: sustainable practice - method that can be maintained without depleting resources
