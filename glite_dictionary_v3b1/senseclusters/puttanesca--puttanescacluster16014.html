puttanesca

adjective
with tomato-olive sauce
Pronunciation: / ˌputəˈnɛskə / (AmE), / ˌpʊtəˈnɛskə / (BrE)
prepared with or containing a sauce made from tomatoes, olives, capers, and garlic, typical of Italian cuisine

Examples

Puttanesca pasta dish

She prefers puttanesca sauce on her spaghetti for its strong, tangy flavor.
