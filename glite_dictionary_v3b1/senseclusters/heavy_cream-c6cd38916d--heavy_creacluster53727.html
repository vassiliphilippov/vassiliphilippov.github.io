heavy cream

noun
cream with high butterfat content
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi ˈkrim / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi ˈkriːm / (BrE)
A type of cream that contains more than 36% butterfat, commonly used in cooking and baking.

Examples

A cup of heavy cream
Pour one cup of heavy cream into the bowl and whisk until soft peaks form.
The recipe calls for half a cup of heavy cream to add a smooth texture.
You can substitute heavy cream for milk to make the sauce richer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double cream - very rich cream used in the UK (48% butterfat)
