Synonyms

  • sparkle
    1.0 1/m
    sparkle shows many small bright points, like sunlight on water, while scintillate is a more literary verb for shining.
  • coruscate
    9.9 0.01/m
    coruscate throws bright, often sharp flashes of light in a showy way, while scintillate simply means to sparkle or shine.
  • twinkle
    1.5 1/m
    twinkle gives a gentle, flickering shine like stars or eyes, while scintillate can mean brighter, sharper sparkling.
  • flash
    1.0 4/m
    flash gives a single sudden bright light or short burst, while scintillate suggests repeated small sparkling or shining.
  • flare
    4.2 0.8/m
    flare makes a large, sudden and often uncontrolled bright blaze, while scintillate suggests many small twinkling flashes.
  • blink
    2.0 2/m
    blink means to switch on and off quickly like a lamp or signal, while scintillate means to sparkle or shine.
