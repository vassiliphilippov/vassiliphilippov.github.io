scintillate

verb
emit flashes of light; sparkle brightly
5.9 8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪn.təˌleɪt /; / ˈsɪn.tɪ.leɪt /; / ˈsɪntɪˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪn.tɪ.leɪt /; / ˈsɪntɪleɪt / (BrE)
