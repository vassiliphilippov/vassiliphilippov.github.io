orphan

noun
a young animal without its mother
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr.fən / (AmE), / ˈɔː.fən / (BrE)
A young animal whose mother has died or is otherwise absent.

Examples

An orphan animal
After a storm destroyed the nest, a wildlife volunteer found an orphan owl chick.
Many animal shelters have special programs for caring for each orphan until it can survive alone.
The rescue center took in the tiny orphan and hand-fed it until it grew stronger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orphan - a child whose parents have died
