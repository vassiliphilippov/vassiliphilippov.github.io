Andaman redwood

noun
wood of Pterocarpus indicus
Pronunciation: / ˈændəmæn ˈrɛdˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈandəmæn ˈrɛdwʊd / (BrE)
the mottled, curly-grained wood from the tree Pterocarpus indicus, valued for furniture and decorative work

Examples

Andaman redwood wood
Artisans imported Andaman redwood for their most prized creations.
Many high-end instruments use Andaman redwood because of its attractive grain.
The furniture was crafted from Andaman redwood, giving it a rich, reddish hue.
