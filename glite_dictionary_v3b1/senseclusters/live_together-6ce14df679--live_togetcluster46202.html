live together

verb
(of a couple) cohabit without marriage
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɪv təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / lɪv təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
(of two people) to share a home and have a sexual relationship without being married.

Examples

To live together as partners
Are you and your partner planning to live together anytime soon?
In many countries, it is common for couples to live together before marriage.
They decided to live together after dating for two years.
