croquet

noun
outdoor game with mallets and hoops
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / krəʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
A lawn game in which players use mallets to hit wooden balls through a series of hoops, aiming to be the first to complete the course and strike a central peg.

Examples

A game of croquet
She taught her children the rules of croquet during their summer holiday.
The garden was large enough for a lively croquet match with friends.
We enjoyed a relaxing afternoon playing croquet on the lawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polo - a sport played on horseback with long-handled mallets
