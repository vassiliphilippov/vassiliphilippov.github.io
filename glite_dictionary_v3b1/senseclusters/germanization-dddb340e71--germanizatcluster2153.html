Germanization

noun
making more German
8.2
Pronunciation: /dʒɜːr.mə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dʒɜː.mə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of making something or someone more German in language, culture, or character

Examples

Germanization of culture
The Germanization of local customs changed the region's traditions over time.
During the occupation, authorities encouraged the Germanization of schools and public institutions.
Many place names underwent Germanization as part of the new policy.
