ever

adverb
emphasizer meaning 'very' (esp. 'ever so')
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛvə / (BrE)
(especially in the phrase 'ever so') Used to emphasize the degree of an adjective or adverb; very.

Examples

Ever so happy
He is ever so grateful for your help during his studies.
She looked ever so pleased when she received the award.
The cake was ever so delicious that everyone asked for seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: very - to a high degree; used to emphasize an adjective or adverb
