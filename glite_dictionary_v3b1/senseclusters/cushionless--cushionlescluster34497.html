cushionless

adjective
without a buffer
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈkʊʃənləs / (BrE)
having no protective buffer or reserve, making one vulnerable to shocks or unexpected losses.

Examples

Cushionless finances
After losing his job, he found himself cushionless and worried about paying the bills.
The company entered the recession cushionless, with no savings to fall back on.
Without insurance, they were completely cushionless when disaster struck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protected - kept safe from harm or danger
  • synonym: unprotected - not protected or shielded from harm
  • synonym: vulnerable - easily hurt or affected by something
