woofy

adjective
excessive bass sound
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwuː.fi/ /; / /ˈwʊf.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwuː.fi/ /; / /ˈwʊf.ɪ/ (BrE)
informal: (of a sound or audio) having too much low-frequency or bass, making it sound muffled or boomy

Examples

Woofy sound
The old speakers made the music sound woofy and hard to enjoy.
After adjusting the equalizer, the audio was less woofy and much clearer.
His voice became woofy when he spoke too close to the microphone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to hear and not distorted
  • synonym: muffled - not clear or sharp in sound; muted
