kain

noun
traditional cloth garment
9.9
Pronunciation: / kaɪn / (AmE), / kaɪn / (BrE)
a traditional Southeast Asian garment, typically a length of cloth wrapped and worn as a skirt or dress

Examples

Traditional kain dress
She wore a colorful kain to the cultural festival.
The dancers performed in beautifully patterned kain during the ceremony.
He bought a handwoven kain as a souvenir from his trip to Indonesia.
