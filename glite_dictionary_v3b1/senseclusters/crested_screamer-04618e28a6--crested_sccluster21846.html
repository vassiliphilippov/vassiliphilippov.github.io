crested screamer

noun
large South American wetland bird with a feathery crest
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛstɪd skriːmər / (AmE), / ˈkrɛstɪd ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
A large South American bird (Chauna torquata) characterized by a feathery crest on the back of its head, found in wetlands and distinguished from the horned screamer.

Examples

A crested screamer bird
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a flock of crested screamers during their trip to the wetlands.
The crested screamer can often be found near marshlands in Argentina and Uruguay.
Unlike the horned screamer, the crested screamer has a distinctive feathery crest on its head.
