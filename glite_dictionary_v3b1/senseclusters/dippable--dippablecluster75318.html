dippable

adjective
suitable for dipping
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be dipped, especially into a liquid or sauce without breaking or falling apart

Examples

Dippable crackers
These crackers are especially dippable and hold up well in thick cheese spreads.
He prefers vegetables that are dippable for his hummus snack.
The breadsticks were so dippable that everyone enjoyed them with the soup.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • synonym: sturdy - strong enough to withstand pressure or use
