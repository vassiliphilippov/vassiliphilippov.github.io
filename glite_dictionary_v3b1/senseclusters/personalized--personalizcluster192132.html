personalized

adjective
concerning someone's individual or private matters
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlaɪzd / (BrE)
referring to or concerning a particular person's individual personality or private affairs, often in a way that is intrusive or offensive

Examples

Personalized comment

Her comments became too personalized and hurt his feelings.
The debate was civil until one side started making personalized attacks.
