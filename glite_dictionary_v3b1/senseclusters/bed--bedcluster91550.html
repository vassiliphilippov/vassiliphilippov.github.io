bed

noun
printing press surface for type form
Pronunciation: / bɛd / (AmE), / bɛd / (BrE)
the flat surface of a printing press on which the type form is laid during printing

Examples

Printing press bed
After inking the plates, they lowered the paper onto the bed to produce the first print.
Some old letterpress machines have a bed made of solid metal for durability.
The compositor placed the type form carefully on the bed of the printing press.
