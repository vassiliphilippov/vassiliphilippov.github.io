homestand

noun
series of home games
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌstænd / (BrE)
a series of games played by a sports team at their home venue without traveling to play away games

Examples

A homestand series
The baseball team started their homestand with three consecutive wins against tough opponents.
Fans filled the stadium for every game during the week-long homestand.
After a long road trip, the players were happy to begin a new homestand in their own city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: road trip - a series of games played away from a team's home venue
