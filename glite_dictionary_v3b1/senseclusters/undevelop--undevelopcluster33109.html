undevelop

verb
to reverse development
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləp / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvɛləp / (BrE)
to reverse or halt the development process, especially in photographic film or prints.

Examples

To undevelop a photo
The photographer tried to undevelop the print to lighten the image.
In some experimental techniques, artists intentionally undevelop film to create unique effects.
She read about how to undevelop negatives in a specialized photography magazine.
