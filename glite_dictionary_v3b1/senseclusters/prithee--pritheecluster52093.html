prithee

interjection
archaic polite request
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪði / (AmE), / ˈprɪði / (BrE)
an old-fashioned way to say 'please' or 'I ask you', used to make a polite request

Examples

Prithee, tell me
Prithee, tell me the truth about what you saw in the garden.
He exclaimed, 'prithee, listen to my warning before it is too late!'
In old plays, characters often say 'prithee' when making a request.

Related Terms

  • synonym: please - used to make a polite request
