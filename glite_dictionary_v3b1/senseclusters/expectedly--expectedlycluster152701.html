expectedly

adverb
in an anticipating way
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛktɪdli / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛktɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that shows someone is waiting for or anticipating something to happen.

Examples

Waits expectedly
The children waited expectedly by the door for their father to return home.
She glanced expectedly at her boss, hoping for some good news about the project.
He smiled expectedly as the waiter approached with the dessert menu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopefully - in a way that shows hope or expectation
  • antonym: unexpectedly - in a way that is not anticipated or foreseen
