pit

noun
Hell; place of eternal punishment
4.4
Pronunciation: / pɪt / (AmE), / pɪt / (BrE)
(in Christianity) Hell; the place where Satan and evil forces dwell, and where sinners endure eternal punishment.

Examples

The eternal pit
He preached about salvation from the eternal pit.
Legends tell of demons that escaped the pit to wreak havoc on Earth.
The wicked were believed to fall into the pit for their sins.
