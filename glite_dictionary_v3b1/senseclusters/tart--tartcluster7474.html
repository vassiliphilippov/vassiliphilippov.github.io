tart

noun
offensive slang for prostitute
Pronunciation: / tɑrt / (AmE), / tɑːt / (BrE)
(slang, offensive) A woman who engages in sexual intercourse for money; a prostitute.

Examples

Call her a tart
Calling someone a tart is considered highly disrespectful and offensive.
In the old British novel, the character was described as a street tart frequenting the night.
Some people use the word tart to insult women they perceive as promiscuous.

Related Terms
