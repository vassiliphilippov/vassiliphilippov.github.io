Nidulariales

noun
order of fungi including Nidulariaceae
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɪdʒʊˌlɛriˈeɪliːz / (AmE), / ˌnɪdjʊˌlɑːrɪˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
An order of basidiomycetous fungi that includes the families Nidulariaceae and Sphaerobolaceae.

Examples

The Nidulariales order
Both the Nidulariaceae and Sphaerobolaceae families are classified under the Nidulariales.
Researchers are studying the dispersal mechanisms of fungi in the Nidulariales order.
The Nidulariales are known for their unique bird's nest-like fruiting bodies.
