exhibitionism

noun
exposure of genitals in public; sexual disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.sɪˈbɪʃ.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˌɛk.sɪˈbɪʃ.ɪ.nɪ.zəm / (BrE)
the act of deliberately exposing one's genitals in public to attract attention or for sexual gratification

Examples

Sexual exhibitionism
Laws against exhibitionism are designed to protect public decency.
Psychiatrists classify exhibitionism as a type of paraphilic disorder.
The man was arrested for public exhibitionism after exposing himself in the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modesty - behavior, manner, or appearance intended to avoid indecency
  • synonym: voyeurism - sexual gratification from observing others undressed or engaged in sexual activity
