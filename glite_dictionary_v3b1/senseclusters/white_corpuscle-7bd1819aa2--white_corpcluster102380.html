white corpuscle

noun
a blood cell involved in immune defense
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈkɔrpəsəl / (AmE), / waɪt ˈkɔːpʊsl̩ / (BrE)
a type of blood cell that helps defend the body by engulfing and digesting bacteria and fungi
