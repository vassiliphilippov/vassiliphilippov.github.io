gas gun

noun
gun firing gas-filled shells
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ɡʌn / (AmE), / ɡæs ɡʌn / (BrE)
A gun designed to fire shells that release gas, often used for riot control or non-lethal purposes.

Examples

A gas gun launcher
A gas gun can launch canisters filled with tear gas or other chemical agents.
Scientists tested a new type of gas gun for non-lethal animal control.
The police used a gas gun to disperse the crowd during the protest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firearm - a portable gun
  • synonym: tear gas - irritant gas used in riot control
