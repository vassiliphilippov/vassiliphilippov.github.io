plump

verb
make fatter or rounder
6.1 3.2
Pronunciation: / plʌmp / (AmE), / plʌmp / (BrE)
to make someone or something fuller, rounder, or fatter in shape (by feeding, shaking, adding, or fluffing)

Examples

To plump a pillow
A good diet will soon plump her up after her illness.
The chef tried to plump the raisins by soaking them in warm water.
Winter feeding helped to plump the cattle before market.
She likes to plump the pillows before guests arrive.
He plumped the sofa cushions to make them look more inviting.
