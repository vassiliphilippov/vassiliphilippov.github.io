dipper

noun
a small North American diving duck
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpər / (AmE), / ˈdɪpə / (BrE)
a small North American diving duck, especially the male, which has bushy head plumage

Examples

A male dipper duck
Several dippers nested near the riverbank this spring.
The male dipper is easy to recognize by its bushy head plumage.
We spotted a dipper swimming quickly underwater in the lake.
