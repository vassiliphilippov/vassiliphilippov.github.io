dao

noun
universal principle
9.8
Pronunciation: / daʊ / (AmE), / daʊ / (BrE)
the fundamental principle or way that underlies the universe and guides all things in Chinese philosophy

Examples

The dao of life
Many people seek to understand the dao in their daily lives.
The concept of the dao is central to Taoist philosophy and practice.
He studied ancient texts to learn more about the dao and its teachings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tao - another spelling of 'dao', the universal way in Chinese philosophy
  • synonym: zen - a school of Buddhism emphasizing meditation and intuition
