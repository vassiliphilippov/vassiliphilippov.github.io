diddler

noun
time-waster
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈdɪdlə / (BrE)
a person who wastes time or behaves in a silly, aimless, or unproductive way

Examples

Lazy diddler
Instead of finishing his assignments, he acted like a diddler and played games all afternoon.
The manager grew frustrated with the diddler who never seemed to get any real work done.
No one wanted to partner with a diddler on the group project because of his lack of focus.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardworker - a person who works with energy and commitment
  • synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or spends time doing nothing
  • synonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort
