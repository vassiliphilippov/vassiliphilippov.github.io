lightsomely

adverb
in a light and graceful manner
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtsəmli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtsəmli / (BrE)
In a manner that is quick, light, and graceful in movement.

Examples

To dance lightsomely
Despite her heavy skirt, she stepped lightsomely onto the boat.
She danced lightsomely across the polished floor, barely making a sound.
The cat moved lightsomely along the top of the fence, tail held high.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsily - in an awkward or ungraceful way
  • synonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant way
