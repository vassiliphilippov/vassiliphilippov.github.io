cohune oil

noun
fat from cohune palm nuts
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhuːn ɔɪl / (AmE), / kəʊˈhuːn ɔɪl / (BrE)
A semisolid fat extracted from the nuts of the cohune palm, used in cooking and in making soap.

Examples

Cohune oil for cooking
Artisans sometimes incorporate cohune oil into their handmade soaps for added moisturizing qualities.
The production of cohune oil plays a significant role in some Central American economies.
Traditional communities often use cohune oil for frying and other cooking purposes.
