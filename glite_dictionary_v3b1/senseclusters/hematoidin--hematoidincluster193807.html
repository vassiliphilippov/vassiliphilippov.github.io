hematoidin

noun
orange-yellow pigment from hemoglobin in bile
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːməˈtɔɪdɪn /; / hiːˌmætɔɪˈdɪn / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɔɪdɪn /; / hiːˌmatɔɪˈdɪn / (BrE)
an orange-yellow pigment derived from hemoglobin, found in bile and sometimes deposited in tissues, especially under pathological conditions such as jaundice
