nonunion

noun
failure of bone healing
7.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈjuːniən / (AmE), / nɒnˈjuːniən / (BrE)
a failure of parts, especially bones, to join or heal together after an injury or fracture

Examples

Bone nonunion
The X-ray revealed a nonunion where the broken bone had not healed properly.
After several months, the patient was diagnosed with a nonunion of the femur.
Surgery may be required to treat a nonunion and help the bone heal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malunion - healing of a fractured bone in a misaligned position
  • antonym: union - the successful joining or healing of bones after a fracture
