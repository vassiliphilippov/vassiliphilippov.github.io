heterosex

noun
heterosexual norm
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛtərəˌsɛks / (AmE), / ˈhɛtərəˌsɛks / (BrE)
the social system or cultural norm that regards heterosexual relationships as the standard or preferred form of sexuality

Examples

Heterosex as a norm
Many activists challenge the dominance of heterosex in mainstream media and education.
The film explores how heterosex shapes people's expectations about relationships.
Some laws are criticized for reinforcing heterosex as the only acceptable form of partnership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heteronormativity - the belief that heterosexuality is the default or normal sexual orientation
  • antonym: homonormativity - the belief that homosexuality is the norm or preferred orientation
