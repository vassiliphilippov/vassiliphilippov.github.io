schemer

noun
a person who devises secret plans
7.0 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskiÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàskiÀêm…ô / (BrE)
A person who makes secret or elaborate plans, often to achieve their own aims, sometimes in a cunning or dishonest way.

Examples

A schemer with plans
No one trusted him because he was known as a schemer with hidden motives.
She didn't want to be seen as a schemer, so she kept her ambitious plans to herself.
The novel's villain is a clever schemer who manipulates everyone around him.

Synonyms

  • intriguer
    8.9 0.05/m
    an intriguer makes secret plans to manipulate others for personal gain, while a schemer often plans secretly and acts dishonestly.
  • plotter
    6.4 0.79/m
    a plotter plans harmful or illegal acts in secret, while a schemer may plan for personal gain but not always for crime.
  • conniver
    8.4 0.11/m
    a conniver secretly helps or agrees to dishonest plans with others, while a schemer usually devises plans, sometimes working alone.
  • contriver
    8.3 0.03/m
    a contriver invents clever or resourceful plans, often without bad intent, while a schemer suggests secret, often dishonest intentions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspirator - a person who takes part in a secret plan to do something unlawful or harmful
  • antonym: innocent - a person not involved in secret or deceptive plans
  • synonym: plotter - someone who secretly plans something, especially something illegal or harmful
