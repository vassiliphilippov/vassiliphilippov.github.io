schemer

noun
a person who devises secret plans
4.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈskiː.mər/ /; / /ˈskɪ.mər/ (AmE), /ˈskiː.mə/ /; / /ˈskɪ.mə/ (BrE)
A person who makes secret or elaborate plans, often to achieve their own aims, sometimes in a cunning or dishonest way.

Examples

A schemer with plans
No one trusted him because he was known as a schemer with hidden motives.
She didn't want to be seen as a schemer, so she kept her ambitious plans to herself.
The novel's villain is a clever schemer who manipulates everyone around him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspirator - a person who takes part in a secret plan to do something unlawful or harmful
  • antonym: innocent - a person not involved in secret or deceptive plans
  • synonym: plotter - someone who secretly plans something, especially something illegal or harmful
