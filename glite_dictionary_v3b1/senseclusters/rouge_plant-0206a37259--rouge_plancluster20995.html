rouge plant

noun
tropical bushy plant with scarlet berries
Pronunciation: / ruːʒ plænt / (AmE), / ruːʒ plɑːnt / (BrE)
A bushy tropical houseplant with white to pale pink flowers, followed by clusters of bright scarlet berries; native to the Americas.

Examples

The rouge plant berries
After flowering, the rouge plant produces long clusters of scarlet berries that remain through winter.
Many gardeners prize the rouge plant for its delicate pink blossoms and decorative appeal.
The rouge plant adds a splash of color to my living room with its bright red berries.
