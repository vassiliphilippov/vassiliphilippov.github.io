total whackjob

noun
completely crazy person (informal)
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊɾl ˈwækˌdʒɑb / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈwækˌdʒɒb / (BrE)
A person who is regarded as completely crazy or insane, especially in a way that seems extreme or unreasonable.

Examples

A total whackjob person
Everyone at the meeting thought he was a total whackjob for his bizarre ideas.
My sister says the new neighbor is a total whackjob because he talks to his plants.
Nobody takes her advice seriously, since most people think she's a total whackjob.
