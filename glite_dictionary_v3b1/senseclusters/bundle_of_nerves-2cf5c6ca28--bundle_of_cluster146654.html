bundle of nerves

noun
very anxious or tense person
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌn.dəl əv nɝːvz / (AmE), / ˈbʌn.dəl əv nɜːvz / (BrE)
a person who is extremely anxious or tense; a state of extreme nervousness.

Examples

A bundle of nerves before interview
Before her interview, she was a bundle of nerves and could hardly sit still.
He turned into a bundle of nerves when he realized he had to give a speech.
I'm always a bundle of nerves before flying, no matter how many times I've traveled.

Related Terms
