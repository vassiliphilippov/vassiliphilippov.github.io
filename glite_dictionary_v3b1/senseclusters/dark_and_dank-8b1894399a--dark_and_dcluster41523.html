dark and dank

adjective
gloomy and unpleasantly damp
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɑrk ənd dæŋk / (AmE), / dɑːk ənd daŋk / (BrE)
(of a place) gloomy and unpleasantly damp or moist

Examples

The dark and dank room
Mold grew quickly in the dark and dank corners of the abandoned building.
She hesitated to enter the dark and dank tunnel beneath the old mansion.
The cellar felt dark and dank after years of neglect.
