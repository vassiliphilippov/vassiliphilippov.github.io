bladeless

adjective
without leaf blade
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈbleɪdləs / (BrE)
having no leaf blade or lacking the broad, flat part typical of most leaves

Examples

Bladeless leaves
Some desert plants have bladeless leaves that help them conserve water.
The cactus is known for its bladeless spines instead of typical leaves.
Researchers studied the adaptation of bladeless foliage in arid environments.
