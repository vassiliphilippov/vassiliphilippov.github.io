hypoadrenocorticism

noun
adrenal cortex insufficiency disorder

A medical condition caused by decreased activity of the cortex of the adrenal gland, characterized by anemia, extreme weakness, and darkened skin.

Examples

Hypoadrenocorticism symptoms
Chronic fatigue and weight loss may be symptoms of hypoadrenocorticism in patients.
Prompt treatment can help manage hypoadrenocorticism and prevent life-threatening complications.
The veterinarian diagnosed the dog with hypoadrenocorticism after a series of blood tests revealed low cortisol levels.
