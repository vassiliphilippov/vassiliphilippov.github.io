fibronectin

noun
blood-clotting protein
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbrəˈnɛktɪn /; / ˌfaɪbroʊˈnɛktɪn / (AmE), / ˌfɪbrəˈnɛktɪn /; / ˌfɪbrəʊˈnɛktɪn / (BrE)
a protein in the body that helps cells stick together and supports tissue repair and healing
