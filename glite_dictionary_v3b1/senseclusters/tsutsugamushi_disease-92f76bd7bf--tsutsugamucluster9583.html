tsutsugamushi disease

noun
scrub typhus; mite-borne infectious disease in Asia
9.6 9.7
Pronunciation: /tsuː.tsuː.ɡæ.ˈmuː.ʃiː dɪz.ˈiːz/ /; / /tsuː.tsuː.ɡæ.ˈmuː.ʃiː dɪ.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /tsuː.tsuː.ɡæ.ˈmuː.ʃiː dɪ.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
a disease, also known as scrub typhus, caused by the bacterium Orientia tsutsugamushi, transmitted by the bites of larval mites and widespread in Asia, characterized by fever, rash, and sometimes severe complications
