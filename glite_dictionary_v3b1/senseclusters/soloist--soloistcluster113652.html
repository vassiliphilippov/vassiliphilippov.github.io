soloist

noun
a musician or singer who performs a solo
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.loʊ.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ləʊ.ɪst / (BrE)
A musician or singer who performs a solo, especially one featured as the main performer within an ensemble or concert.

Examples

The soloist performs
It takes confidence and skill to perform as the lead soloist in a major production.
She is renowned as a jazz soloist with a unique interpretation of classic pieces.
The orchestra featured a brilliant soloist in last night’s concert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ensemble - a group of musicians, singers, or dancers who perform together
  • synonym: performer - a person who entertains an audience
