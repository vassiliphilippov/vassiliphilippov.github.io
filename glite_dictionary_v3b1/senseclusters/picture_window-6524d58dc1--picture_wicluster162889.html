picture window

noun
large fixed window for a view
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃər ˌwɪndoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌwɪndəʊ / (BrE)
a large fixed window with a single pane of glass designed to offer an unobstructed view, especially of the outside scenery

Examples

A large picture window
A large picture window makes the space feel bright and airy.
She sat by the picture window, enjoying the sunset every evening.
The living room features a picture window that overlooks the entire valley.
