geologist

noun
a scientist studying the earth
1.2
Pronunciation: / dʒiˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / dʒiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A scientist who studies the earth's physical structure, substances, history, and processes.

Examples

A geologist studies rocks
A team of geologists mapped the mineral deposits in the area.
As a geologist, she travels frequently to volcanic regions.
The geologist examined rock samples from the excavation site.

  • synonym: geology - the scientific study of the earth
