expansiveness

noun
friendliness and openness in conversation
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ɪkˈspansɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, open, and willing to talk or share ideas, especially in conversation

Examples

Social expansiveness
He is admired for his warmth and expansiveness in any social setting.
Her expansiveness made everyone feel welcome at the party.
The interviewer's expansiveness encouraged the guest to open up about personal topics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly and pleasant
  • antonym: reserve - the quality of being reluctant to speak freely; restraint
