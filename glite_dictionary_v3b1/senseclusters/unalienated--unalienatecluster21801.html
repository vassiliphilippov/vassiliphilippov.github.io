unalienated

adjective
not isolated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈeɪliəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeɪliəneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not feeling isolated, estranged, or separated; having a sense of belonging or connection.

Examples

Unalienated workers
In her new job, she finally felt unalienated and part of the team.
A supportive family can help children remain unalienated during difficult times.
The club's welcoming atmosphere made every member feel unalienated and valued.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alienated - feeling isolated or estranged
  • synonym: connected - feeling joined or linked with others
