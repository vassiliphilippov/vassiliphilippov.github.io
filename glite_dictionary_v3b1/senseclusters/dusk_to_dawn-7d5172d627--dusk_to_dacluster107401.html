dusk to dawn

preposition
from evening until morning; all night
Pronunciation: / dʌsk tə dɔn / (AmE), / dʌsk tə dɔːn / (BrE)
lasting or happening from evening until morning; throughout the night.

Examples

dusk to dawn lights
Security lights operate dusk to dawn for maximum safety.
The party lasted dusk to dawn, with guests dancing all night.
They offered food and shelter dusk to dawn for the marathon runners.
