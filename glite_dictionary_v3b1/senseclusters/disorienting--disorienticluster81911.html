disorienting

adjective
causing confusion or loss of direction
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɔriˌɛntɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪsˈɔːri.əntɪŋ / (BrE)
Making someone confused or unable to think clearly or find their way.

Examples

A disorienting maze
She found the maze disorienting and struggled to keep track of her path.
The constant noise and flashing lights were extremely disorienting for new visitors.
Waking up in a strange hotel room can feel quite disorienting at first.
