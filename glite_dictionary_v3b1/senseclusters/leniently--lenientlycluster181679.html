leniently

adverb
in a tolerant or permissive way
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈliːniəntli / (AmE), / ˈliːniəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is tolerant, permissive, or not strict

Examples

Rules enforced leniently
Some believe the rules are enforced too leniently in this department.
Teachers who grade too leniently may encourage students to put in less effort.
The judge decided to sentence him leniently due to his remorse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strictly - in a severe or harsh manner, without leniency
