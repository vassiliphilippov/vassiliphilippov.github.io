A mild form of diabetes mellitus that usually develops in adults and can often be managed without insulin injections, typically controlled by diet and oral hypoglycemic agents.

Examples

Type 2 non-insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus
Many adults are diagnosed with non-insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus as they age and gain weight.
She controls her non-insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus by following a strict diet and taking oral medication.
The risk of developing non-insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus increases with family history and lifestyle factors.
