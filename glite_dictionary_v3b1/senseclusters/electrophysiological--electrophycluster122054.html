electrophysiological

adjective
electrical activity in biology
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌfɪziəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the electrical properties or activity of living tissues or cells

Examples

Electrophysiological study
The doctor performed an electrophysiological study to diagnose the patient's heart rhythm problem.
Researchers use electrophysiological techniques to measure brain activity in response to stimuli.
Abnormal electrophysiological signals can indicate nerve damage or disease.
