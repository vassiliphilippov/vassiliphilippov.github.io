insultingly

adverb
in a disrespectful or insulting way
5.4 4.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsʌl.tɪŋ.li/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsʌl.tɪŋ.li/ (BrE)
in a way that is insulting, disrespectful, or meant to offend

Examples

Speak insultingly to someone
He spoke insultingly to the waiter, causing embarrassment.
She rolled her eyes insultingly when his name was mentioned.
The comments were insultingly dismissive of her hard work.
He was insultingly dismissive of her suggestions during the meeting.
She spoke insultingly to the staff, which embarrassed everyone in the room.
The referee was accused of behaving insultingly toward the players.
