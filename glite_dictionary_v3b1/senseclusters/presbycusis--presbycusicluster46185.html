presbycusis

noun
age-related hearing loss
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌprɛz.bɪˈkjuː.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌprɛz.bɪˈkjuː.sɪs / (BrE)
gradual loss of hearing that happens as people get older

Examples

Age-related presbycusis
Many elderly people experience presbycusis as they age.
Doctors often check for presbycusis during routine hearing tests for older adults.
Hearing aids can help manage the effects of presbycusis in seniors.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: deafness - complete or severe loss of hearing
  • synonym: hearing loss - partial or total inability to hear
