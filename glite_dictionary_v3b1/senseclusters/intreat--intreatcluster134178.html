intreat

verb
to beg earnestly
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtriːt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtriːt / (BrE)
to ask someone earnestly or anxiously for something; archaic form of 'entreat'

Examples

To intreat for help
He did intreat her to stay a little longer.
The villagers would intreat the king for mercy during hard times.
She would often intreat her friends for help with her studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beg - to ask for something urgently or repeatedly
  • antonym: demand - to ask for something forcefully or insistently
  • synonym: entreat - to ask someone earnestly or anxiously for something
