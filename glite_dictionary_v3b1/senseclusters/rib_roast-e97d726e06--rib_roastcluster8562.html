rib roast

noun
meat cut including several ribs, roasted
Pronunciation: / rɪb roʊst / (AmE), / rɪb rəʊst / (BrE)
a cut of meat from beef or venison that includes several ribs and the meat attached to them, often roasted as a whole piece

Examples

A rib roast cut
The chef expertly carved the rib roast at the table.
This recipe calls for a four-bone rib roast seasoned with garlic and herbs.
We ordered a rib roast for the holiday dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prime rib - a rib roast of beef, often roasted and served rare
