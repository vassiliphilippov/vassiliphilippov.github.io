in its own right

adverb
independently; by one's own achievement
Pronunciation: / ɪn ɪts oʊn raɪt / (AmE), / ɪn ɪts əʊn raɪt / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone or something has a particular quality or status independently, not because of a connection with someone or something else.

Examples

In her own right
Despite being the daughter of a famous actor, she is successful in her own right.
He is an accomplished scientist in his own right, not just because of his family background.
The painting is beautiful in its own right, regardless of who created it.
