fluorescently

adverb
in a glowing way
Pronunciation: / flʊˈrɛsəntli / (AmE), / flɔːˈrɛsəntli /; / flʊəˈrɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that glows or appears very bright, like fluorescent light or colors

Examples

Fluorescently glowing clothes
The safety vests glowed fluorescently when the headlights shone on them at night.
Under the special lamp, the artwork appeared fluorescently colored and almost surreal.
The scientist observed the cells shining fluorescently after adding the dye.
The signs on the street glowed fluorescently in the darkness, attracting everyone's attention.
She decorated her room fluorescently, using neon paints and glowing stickers.
The costumes were designed fluorescently to stand out under the stage lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brightly - in a way that gives off a lot of light
  • synonym: brilliantly - in a very bright or striking way
  • antonym: dimly - in a way that gives off little light
  • antonym: dully - in a way that is not bright or vivid
