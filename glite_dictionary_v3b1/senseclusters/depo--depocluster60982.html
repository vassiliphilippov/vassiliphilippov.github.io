depo

noun
informal for deposition
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɛpəʊ / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a deposition, meaning a witness's sworn out-of-court testimony recorded for legal proceedings

Examples

Legal depo
The lawyer asked if I could attend the depo scheduled for next Tuesday morning.
She reviewed the transcript from the depo before preparing her case for trial.
After the depo, the attorneys discussed the witness's statements in private.

Related Terms
