party

noun
social gathering or celebration
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːr.t̬i /; / ˈpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.ti /; / ˈpɑːti / (BrE)
an event or social gathering where people meet to celebrate, socialize, or have fun, often with food, drinks, and entertainment

Examples

Birthday party
He was invited to a Halloween party at his friend's house.
The office party featured music, games, and a lot of delicious food.
We threw a big party for her graduation last weekend.
After the ceremony, everyone went to the garden for a small party.
She met many new friends at the party last night.
We are having a party at our house this weekend to celebrate her birthday.
