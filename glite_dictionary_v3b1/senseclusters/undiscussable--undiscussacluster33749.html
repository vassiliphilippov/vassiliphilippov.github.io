undiscussable

noun
taboo topic
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈskʌsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈskʌsəbəl / (BrE)
a subject, issue, or topic that people avoid talking about because it is taboo, painful, or forbidden

Examples

A family undiscussable
The family’s financial troubles became an undiscussable that no one dared to mention at gatherings.
In the workplace, the manager’s favoritism was an undiscussable that employees whispered about but never addressed openly.
Cultural differences can make certain topics an undiscussable in some communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: open secret - something widely known but not openly discussed
  • synonym: secret - something kept hidden or not talked about
  • synonym: taboo - a subject that is avoided or prohibited in society
