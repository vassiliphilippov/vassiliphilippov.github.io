castoff

noun
rejected person
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstˌɒf / (BrE)
a person who has been rejected, abandoned, or excluded by others

Examples

A social castoff
After the scandal, he became a castoff among his former colleagues.
The group welcomed the castoff and helped him rebuild his confidence.
She felt like a castoff after being excluded from the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insider - a person who is accepted as part of a group
  • synonym: outcast - a person who is rejected or excluded from society
  • synonym: pariah - a person who is despised or avoided by others
