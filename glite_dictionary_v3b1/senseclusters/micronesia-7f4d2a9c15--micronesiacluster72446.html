Micronesia

proper noun
region of small islands in Oceania
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈniː.ʒə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəˈniː.zi.ə / (BrE)
A region comprising numerous small islands in the northwestern part of Oceania, including the Caroline, Marshall, Mariana, and Gilbert Islands.

Examples

Micronesia consists of thousands of small islands spread across the Pacific Ocean.
