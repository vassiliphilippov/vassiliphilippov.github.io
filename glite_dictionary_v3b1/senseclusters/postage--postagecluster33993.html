postage

noun
adhesive token for paid postal fees
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstɪdʒ / (BrE)
A small adhesive token, such as a stamp, affixed to mail to show that postal charges have been paid.

Examples

Postage stamp
She collects old postage from different countries as a hobby.
The postage for international letters is higher than for domestic ones.
You need to put sufficient postage on the envelope before mailing it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stamp - a small piece of paper indicating payment for post
