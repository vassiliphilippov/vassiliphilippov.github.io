pole bean

noun
climbing variety of bean plant
8.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /poʊl biːn/ (AmE), /pəʊl biːn/ (BrE)
a type of climbing bean plant that grows by twining around supports such as poles, walls, or trellises

Examples

Pole beans climb poles
The pole beans grew quickly and climbed up the trellis by midsummer.
This year I'm planting pole beans along the garden fence to save space.
Unlike bush beans, pole beans require sturdy supports for optimal growth.
