ten-spot

noun
playing card with ten pips
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌspɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌspɒt / (BrE)
A playing card that has ten pips or spots on its face, being one of the four in a standard deck with this value.

Examples

The ten-spot card
After shuffling, the ten-spot landed on top of the deck.
He drew the ten-spot of hearts on his last turn.
The winning hand included a ten-spot and two face cards.
