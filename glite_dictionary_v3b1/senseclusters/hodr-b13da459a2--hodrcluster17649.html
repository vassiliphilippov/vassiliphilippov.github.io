Hodr

proper noun
blind Norse god who killed Balder
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊdər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊdər / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, a blind god who was deceived by Loki into killing his brother Balder.

Examples

Hodr in Norse myths
In Norse legends, Hodr was manipulated by Loki to kill Balder with a mistletoe dart.
Many stories describe Hodr as both blind and tragic in Norse mythology.
The tale of Hodr and his brother Balder remains one of the most famous in Norse myth.
