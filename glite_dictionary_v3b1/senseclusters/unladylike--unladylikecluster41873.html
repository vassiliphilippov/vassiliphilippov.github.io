unladylike

adjective
not proper or refined for a lady
5.2 6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈleɪdiˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪdiˌlaɪk / (BrE)
(of a woman or her behavior) not considered appropriate to, or characteristic of, a refined or proper woman.

Examples

Unladylike behavior
Despite being told her interest in football was unladylike, she joined the team anyway.
Her friends criticized her for using such unladylike language at the dinner table.
She laughed in a loud, unladylike manner that startled everyone in the room.
