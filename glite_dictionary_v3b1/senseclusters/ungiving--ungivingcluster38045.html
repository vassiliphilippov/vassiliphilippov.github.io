ungiving

adjective
barren or unproductive
8.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing crops, vegetation, or useful results; barren or unproductive.

Examples

Ungiving land
The desert was an ungiving landscape, offering little hope for survival.
After months of effort, the soil remained ungiving and nothing would grow.
They struggled to find food in the ungiving wilderness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barren - unable to produce plants or useful results
  • antonym: fertile - able to produce abundant crops or results
