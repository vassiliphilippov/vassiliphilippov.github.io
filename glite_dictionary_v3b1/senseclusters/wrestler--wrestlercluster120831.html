wrestler

noun
person who wrestles
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛs.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɛs.lə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in wrestling, attempting to throw or pin an opponent to the ground

Examples

A wrestler in match
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a professional wrestler.
The wrestler attempted a daring move to pin his opponent to the mat.
The crowd cheered when their favorite wrestler entered the arena.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boxer - a person who takes part in boxing
  • synonym: fighter - a person who fights, especially as a sport or profession
  • synonym: wrestling - the sport or activity of grappling with and trying to throw or pin an opponent
