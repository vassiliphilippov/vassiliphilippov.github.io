bookless

adjective
without books
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkləs / (AmE), / ˈbʊkləs / (BrE)
without books or lacking access to books

Examples

A bookless classroom
The small classroom was completely bookless before the donation arrived.
Growing up in a bookless home made it difficult for her to develop a love of reading.
He was surprised to find the hotel lobby almost bookless except for a few magazines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: book-filled - containing many books
  • antonym: library - a place with a collection of books
