snatch

noun
a weightlifting lift overhead in one motion
8.6
Pronunciation: / snætʃ / (AmE), / snætʃ / (BrE)
a weightlifting movement in which a barbell is lifted overhead from the floor in one continuous, rapid motion

Examples

A snatch lift
Coaches often focus on improving technique in the snatch to prevent injuries.
His personal record for the snatch is 100 kilograms.
She performed a flawless snatch during the weightlifting competition.
