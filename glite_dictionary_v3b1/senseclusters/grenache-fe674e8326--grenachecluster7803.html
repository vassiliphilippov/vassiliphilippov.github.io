Grenache

noun
wine grape variety
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈnɑʃ /; / ɡrəˈnɑːʃ / (AmE), / ɡrəˈnaʃ /; / ɡrəˈnæʃ / (BrE)
a type of red grape used to make wine, or the wine made from these grapes
