nontricyclic drug

noun
antidepressant not tricyclic or MAO-inhibiting
8.4
Pronunciation: /nən.traɪ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk drʌg/ /; / /nɑːn.traɪ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk drʌg/ (AmE), /nɒn.traɪ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk drʌg/ /; / /nən.traɪ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk drʌg/ (BrE)
an antidepressant drug that is neither a tricyclic drug nor acts by inhibiting monoamine oxidase (MAO)

Examples

A nontricyclic drug
Many patients respond well to nontricyclic drugs when traditional antidepressants are not tolerated.
Some nontricyclic drugs are preferred in cases where tricyclic drugs could interact with other medications.
The doctor prescribed a nontricyclic drug to treat her depression due to fewer side effects.
