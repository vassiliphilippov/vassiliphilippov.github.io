photoplay

noun
early film script
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌpleɪ / (BrE)
a written script or scenario for a motion picture, especially used in early cinema

Examples

Photoplay script
In the silent era, every photoplay was carefully crafted to tell a story without spoken words.
The director praised the originality of the photoplay before starting production.
She dreamed of writing a photoplay that would become a classic film.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screenplay - the script of a film, including dialogue and instructions
  • synonym: script - the written text of a play, film, or broadcast
