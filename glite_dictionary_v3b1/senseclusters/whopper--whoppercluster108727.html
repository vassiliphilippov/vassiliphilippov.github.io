whopper

noun
a blatant or extravagant lie
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈwɒpə / (BrE)
A gross or extravagant lie; a blatant untruth.

Examples

Told a whopper lie
Everyone knew her story was a whopper and laughed out loud.
He told a whopper about why he was late to work.
It's hard to believe he thought we'd fall for such a whopper.
