local produce

noun
food grown locally
2.2 0.26/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo äk…ôl Ààpro ädus / (AmE), / Ààl…ô äk…ôl Ààpr…ídjuÀês / (BrE)
food that is grown or produced in the local area, especially used to refer to fresh fruit, vegetables, or other agricultural products.

Examples

Fresh local produce
Buying local produce helps support small farmers and reduces environmental impact.
Many restaurants in the area take pride in serving local produce to their customers.
The supermarket features a special section dedicated to fresh local produce.

Synonyms

  • produce
    1.1 13/m
    produce refers to general fresh fruits and vegetables sold at markets, while local produce means items grown nearby and sold locally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: local food - food produced within a short distance of where it is consumed
