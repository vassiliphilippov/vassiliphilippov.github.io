lay into

verb
attack or hit violently
7.2
Pronunciation: / leɪ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / leɪ ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to attack or hit someone or something forcefully and violently

Examples

To lay into a car
A group of kids laid into the old car with sticks until it was completely smashed.
She suddenly laid into him, hitting and kicking in a fit of anger.
The coach laid into the team for their poor performance after the game.
