azure

adjective
deep bright blue color
Pronunciation: / ˈæʒər / (AmE), / ˈæʒə / (BrE)
of a deep, bright, somewhat purplish blue color, like that of a clear sky

Examples

Azure sky
She wore an azure dress that matched her eyes perfectly.
The azure waters of the lagoon sparkled in the midday sun.
The sky was a clear, azure blue as the sun rose above the horizon.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blue - the color of the clear sky or the deep sea
  • degree higher: sapphire - brilliant blue color, resembling that of a sapphire
