him

pronoun
informal subject pronoun
2.8
Pronunciation: / hɪm / (AmE), / hɪm / (BrE)
used informally as a subject pronoun instead of 'he', especially in nonstandard or colloquial English

Examples

Him helps out
Him and his brother always help out at the community center on weekends.
Whenever there was trouble, him would be the first to step in and solve it.
In our village, him and I would fix the bicycles and chat in the evenings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: he - the standard subject pronoun for a male person
