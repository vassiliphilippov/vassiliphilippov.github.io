galette

noun
flat round pastry
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ɡaˈlɛt / (BrE)
a flat, round pastry, often with a sweet or savory filling, typically baked without a pan

Examples

Apple galette dessert
She served a warm apple galette with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
For dinner, they made a savory galette filled with cheese and vegetables.
The bakery is famous for its rustic berry galette during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pie - a baked dish with a pastry crust and filling
  • synonym: tart - a baked dish with a pastry base and filling
