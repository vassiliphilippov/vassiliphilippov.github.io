cereal grass

noun
grain-producing grass (e.g. wheat, rice)
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəl ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any type of grass grown for its edible starchy grains, such as wheat, rice, rye, oats, maize, buckwheat, or millet

Examples

A cereal grass plant
Rice, another important cereal grass, is a staple food for much of the world's population.
The farmer rotated different cereal grasses to maintain soil fertility and reduce pests.
Wheat is a widely cultivated cereal grass used as the basis for bread and pasta.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grain - the small, hard seeds of cereal grasses used as food
  • synonym: wheat - a widely cultivated cereal grass, source of flour
