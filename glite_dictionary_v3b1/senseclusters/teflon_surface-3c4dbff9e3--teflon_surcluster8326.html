teflon surface

noun
surface with a non-stick Teflon coating
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛflɑn ˈsɝːfɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɛflɒn ˈsɜːfɪs / (BrE)
A surface coated with Teflon, providing a non-stick finish for easy cleaning and reduced sticking of foods or materials.

Examples

A teflon surface
Be careful not to scratch the teflon surface when using metal utensils.
Most modern frying pans have a teflon surface to prevent food from sticking.
The teflon surface makes these baking trays much easier to clean than traditional ones.

  • synonym: non-stick - a surface preventing food from sticking
