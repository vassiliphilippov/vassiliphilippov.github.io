leap out

verb
be highly noticeable or obvious
3.9 0.30/m
Pronunciation: / lip a ät /; / liÀêp a ät / (AmE), / liÀêp a ät / (BrE)
to be very noticeable or to stand out clearly, especially in a way that attracts attention
