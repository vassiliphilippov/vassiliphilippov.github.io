trainy

adjective
with a trailing part
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪni / (AmE), / ˈtreɪni / (BrE)
having a long trailing part, such as a train on a gown, robe, or costume

Examples

A trainy gown
The designer created a trainy dress that swept elegantly across the floor.
Her trainy costume made a dramatic entrance at the masquerade ball.
He admired the trainy robe as it glided behind the actress on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fitted - closely following the shape of the body without extra fabric
  • synonym: flowing - hanging or draping loosely and gracefully
