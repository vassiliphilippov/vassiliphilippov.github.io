inefficaciously

adverb
in an ineffective manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.ə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
in an ineffective or unsuccessful manner; failing to produce the desired result

Examples

To act inefficaciously
Despite her efforts, she tackled the issue inefficaciously and made little progress.
He protested inefficaciously, and his objections were ignored by the committee.
The medication acted inefficaciously, providing no relief to the patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineffectively - in a way that does not produce the intended effect
