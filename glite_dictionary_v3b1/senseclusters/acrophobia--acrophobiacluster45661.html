acrophobia

noun
fear of heights
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæk.rəˈfoʊ.bi.ə / (AmE), / ˌæk.rəˈfəʊ.bi.ə / (BrE)
An extreme or irrational fear of heights.

Examples

Acrophobia fear heights
Despite his acrophobia, he decided to try indoor rock climbing with his friends.
Her acrophobia made it impossible for her to cross the tall suspension bridge.
People with acrophobia may feel dizzy or panic when looking down from high places.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fearlessness - lack of fear, especially in dangerous situations
  • synonym: vertigo - a sensation of dizziness, often related to heights
