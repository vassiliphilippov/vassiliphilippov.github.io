Turkic

adjective
relating to Turkic languages or peoples
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrkɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɜːkɪk / (BrE)
relating to the peoples who speak Turkic languages, or to the Turkic languages themselves

Examples

Turkic peoples
Several Turkic peoples have distinct histories and traditions.
The Turkic languages are spoken across a large area from Eastern Europe to Siberia.
The museum features artifacts from various Turkic cultures.
