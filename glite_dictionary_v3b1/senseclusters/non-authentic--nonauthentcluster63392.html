non-authentic

adjective
not genuine
Pronunciation: / nɑn ɔːˈθɛntɪk / (AmE), / nɒn ɔːˈθɛntɪk / (BrE)
not genuine or real; lacking authenticity

Examples

Non-authentic signature
The expert determined that the signature on the document was non-authentic.
Many tourists unknowingly buy non-authentic souvenirs at the market.
The museum removed the non-authentic artifact from its display after new evidence emerged.
