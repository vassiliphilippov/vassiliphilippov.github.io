epizoan

adjective
relating to organisms living on animals
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈzəʊən / (BrE)
Relating to epizoa; living on the surface of another animal.

Examples

Epizoan barnacles
Some barnacles are considered epizoan organisms because they attach to whales.
The presence of epizoan algae can indicate changes in water quality.
The researcher studied several epizoan communities on marine turtles.

Related Terms
