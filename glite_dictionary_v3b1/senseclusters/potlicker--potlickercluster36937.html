potlicker

noun
one who licks pots
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌlɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌlɪkə / (BrE)
a person or animal who licks a cooking pot clean, often in a humorous or affectionate way

Examples

The family potlicker
After dinner, the family dog acted as the perfect potlicker and left the kitchen spotless.
Grandpa joked that he was a true potlicker because he never let good stew go to waste.
The cat became a potlicker whenever there was leftover gravy in the pan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scavenger - an animal or person who searches for and eats leftover food
