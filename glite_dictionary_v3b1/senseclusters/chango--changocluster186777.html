chango

noun
playful boy
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɑːŋ.goʊ/ /; / /ˈtʃæŋ.goʊ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɑːŋ.əʊ/ /; / /ˈtʃæŋ.əʊ/ (BrE)
an informal term, especially in parts of Latin America, for a boy or young man, often playful or mischievous

Examples

A young chango

Everyone laughed as the chango played tricks on his older sister.
In the village, the neighbors often called him a chango because of his endless energy.
