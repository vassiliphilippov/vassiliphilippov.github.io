cybernate

verb
control by computer
1.0 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.bər.neɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.bə.neɪt/ (BrE)
to control or operate a function, process, or system by means of a computer

Examples

To cybernate a system
As technology advances, more workplaces will cybernate their basic operations.
Many modern factories are partially cybernated to improve efficiency and reduce labor costs.
The airline plans to cybernate its booking system, making reservations fully automated.
