park

verb
stop and leave a vehicle in a place
2.9
Pronunciation: / pɑrk / (AmE), / pɑːk / (BrE)
to bring a vehicle or object to a stop and leave it in a particular place temporarily

Examples

To park a car
He quickly parked and hurried into the store before it closed.
Please park in the designated area so other vehicles can pass easily.
She had to park her car several blocks away from the restaurant.
I told him to park the kids with their grandparents for the afternoon.
She parked her suitcase in the hallway while waiting for her room.
You can't park your car here overnight without a permit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drive - to control a vehicle and make it move
  • antonym: remove - to take something away from where it was placed
  • synonym: station - to position or place something or someone for a purpose
  • synonym: stop - to cease movement; come to a halt
