Cuniculus

noun
genus of large South American rodents (pacas)
Pronunciation: / kuːˈnɪkjʊləs / (AmE), / kjuːˈnɪkjʊləs / (BrE)
A genus of large, nocturnal rodents known as pacas, native to Central and South America.

Examples

The Cuniculus rodent
Bones belonging to Cuniculus were discovered in the archaeological site.
Researchers have studied the behavior of Cuniculus in their natural habitats.
The genus Cuniculus includes two recognized species of pacas found in rainforests.
