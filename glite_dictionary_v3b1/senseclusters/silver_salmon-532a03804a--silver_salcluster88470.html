silver salmon

noun
a type of salmon flesh used as food
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ ˈsæmən / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈsæmən / (BrE)
the pinkish, fatty flesh of a small salmon species (the coho salmon) found in the Pacific and Great Lakes, used as food

Examples

Silver salmon fillet
Anglers are often excited to catch silver salmon during the fall spawning season.
The chef prepared a delicious meal featuring silver salmon with lemon and herbs.
You can find fresh silver salmon in local fish markets along the Pacific coast.
