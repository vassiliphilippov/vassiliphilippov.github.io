Atayalic

noun
Austronesian languages of northern Taiwan
Pronunciation: / əˌtaɪəˈlɪk / (AmE), / əˌtaɪəˈlɪk / (BrE)
a group of Austronesian languages spoken by the Atayal people of northern Taiwan (formerly Formosa)

Examples

An Atayalic language
Only a few thousand people are fluent in an Atayalic language today.
Scholars have conducted extensive research on the Atayalic dialects to preserve their linguistic heritage.
The Atayalic languages are primarily spoken in the mountainous regions of northern Taiwan.
