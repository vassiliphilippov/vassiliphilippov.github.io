hyperinflationary

adjective
extreme inflation
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərɪnˈfleɪʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of extremely rapid and excessive inflation in an economy

Examples

A hyperinflationary economy
The government introduced new policies to control the hyperinflationary spiral affecting the economy.
People lost confidence in the currency during the hyperinflationary period.
A hyperinflationary environment can make everyday goods unaffordable for most citizens.
