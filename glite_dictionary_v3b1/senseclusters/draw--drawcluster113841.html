draw

noun
the act of hauling or pulling
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
an act of pulling or hauling something, especially by force

Examples

A heavy draw
A heavy draw up the muddy bank left the wagon wheels stuck.
The final draw of the sled took all their strength.
The old engine struggled with each draw as it hauled the carts uphill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pull - the act of applying force to move something toward oneself
  • antonym: push - the act of exerting force to move something away
