bacteriologist

noun
a scientist specializing in bacteria
5.2
Pronunciation: / bækˌtɪriəˈlɑdʒɪst / (AmE), / bækˌtɪəriəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies bacteria, especially their structure, function, and importance

Examples

A bacteriologist studies bacteria
As a bacteriologist, she is consulted for cases involving foodborne illnesses.
The bacteriologist spent years researching antibiotic resistance in hospitals.
The work of a bacteriologist is essential for developing new vaccines.
