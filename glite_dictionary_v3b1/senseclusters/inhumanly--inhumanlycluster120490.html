inhumanly

adverb
beyond human limits
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhjuːmənli / (AmE), / ɪnˈhjuːmənli / (BrE)
to an extreme or unnatural degree, beyond what is considered normal for humans, especially in speed, strength, or intensity

Examples

Inhumanly strong effort
The climber endured inhumanly harsh conditions at the mountain's summit.
She worked inhumanly long hours to finish the project before the deadline.
The athlete pushed himself inhumanly hard to break the world record.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humanly - to a degree possible for humans
  • synonym: superhumanly - to a degree greater than what is humanly possible
