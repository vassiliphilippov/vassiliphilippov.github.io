plastic art

noun
art of molding or shaping materials
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk ɑrt / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk ɑːt / (BrE)
the art forms involving the moulding, modeling, or shaping of materials, particularly sculpture and carving

Examples

Plastic art sculpture
Courses in plastic art teach students how to shape materials into expressive forms.
She dedicated her life to mastering the techniques of plastic art such as sculpture and pottery.
The gallery features exhibitions focusing on plastic art rather than painting or photography.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sculpture - the art of making three-dimensional forms by carving or modeling
