thrive on

verb
flourish due to a particular thing or condition
Pronunciation: / θraɪv ɑn / (AmE), / θraɪv ɒn / (BrE)
to flourish, succeed, or be energized as a result of a specific thing, activity, or situation, especially something challenging or stimulating

Examples

To thrive on challenge
Children often thrive on encouragement and support from their teachers.
Many entrepreneurs thrive on the excitement of launching new ventures.
Some plants thrive on neglect rather than regular attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flourish - to grow or develop successfully
  • synonym: prosper - to be successful, especially in financial terms
