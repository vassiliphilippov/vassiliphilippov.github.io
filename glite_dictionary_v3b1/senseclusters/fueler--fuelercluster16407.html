fueler

noun
source of support
5.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàfjuÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / Ààfj ä…ôl…ô / (BrE)
a person or thing that provides energy, support, or encouragement for an activity, feeling, or process, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Her encouragement was the main fueler of his determination to succeed.
Negative comments can act as a fueler for further conflict in online discussions.
