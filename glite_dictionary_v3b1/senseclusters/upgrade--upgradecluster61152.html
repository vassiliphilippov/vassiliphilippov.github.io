upgrade

verb
promote to a higher position
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌpɡreɪd / (BrE)
to move someone to a higher position or rank, especially as a promotion in their job or status

Examples

To upgrade an employee
After years of dedicated work, Susan was finally upgraded to a management position.
He hopes his performance review will lead them to upgrade him to a senior role.
The company decided to upgrade several employees within the department this quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demote - to move someone to a lower position or rank
  • synonym: promote - to raise someone to a more important or responsible position
