vault of heaven

noun
poetic term for the sky
Pronunciation: / vɔlt əv ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / vɔːlt əv ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
a poetic or literary term for the sky, regarded as a vast dome under which the sun, moon, and stars appear to move

Examples

The vault of heaven
Ancient observers gazed in wonder at the vault of heaven above them.
Brilliant stars filled the deep blue vault of heaven on that clear summer night.
Poets through the centuries have described the vault of heaven in their verses.
