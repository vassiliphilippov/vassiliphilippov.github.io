hypercube

noun
multidimensional cube
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpərˌkjuːb / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpəkjuːb / (BrE)
a geometric figure that is the four-dimensional or higher-dimensional analogue of a cube

Examples

A four-dimensional hypercube
A hypercube is difficult to imagine because it extends beyond three dimensions.
Mathematicians use the concept of a hypercube to study higher-dimensional spaces.
The animation showed a rotating hypercube projected into three dimensions.
