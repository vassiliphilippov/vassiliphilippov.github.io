spiral ratchet screwdriver

noun
screwdriver with ratchet and spiral drive
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪ.rəl ˈrætʃ.ɪt ˈskruˌdraɪ.vər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪ.rəl ˈrætʃ.ɪt ˈskruːˌdraɪ.və / (BrE)
a screwdriver equipped with both a ratchet mechanism and a spiral in the handle, allowing the blade to rotate when downward pressure is applied

Examples

A spiral ratchet screwdriver
A spiral ratchet screwdriver makes repetitive screwing tasks much easier and faster.
He pressed down on the spiral ratchet screwdriver to drive the screw into the wood.
The mechanic used a spiral ratchet screwdriver to quickly remove the panel.
