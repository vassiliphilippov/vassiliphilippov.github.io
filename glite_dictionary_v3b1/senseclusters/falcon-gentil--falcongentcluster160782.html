falcon-gentil

noun
female falcon, esp. peregrine
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfælkənˈʒɛntil / (AmE), / ˌfɔːlkənˈʒɛntil / (BrE)
A female falcon, especially a female peregrine falcon.

Examples

The falcon-gentil female
Medieval texts often refer to the falcon-gentil as the preferred bird for noblewomen.
She trained a young falcon-gentil to participate in the competition.
The falcon-gentil is highly prized among falconers for its hunting abilities.
