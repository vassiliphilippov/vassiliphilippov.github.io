coupon queen

noun
woman skilled at using coupons for discounts
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːpɑːn kwiːn / (AmE), / ˈkuːpɒn kwiːn / (BrE)
a woman who is extremely skilled and enthusiastic at finding and using coupons to obtain discounts when shopping

Examples

My sister is a real coupon queen and saves our family a lot of money.
