trapper

noun
performer or producer of trap music
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtræpər / (AmE), / ˈtræpə / (BrE)
a musician or producer who performs or creates trap music, a subgenre of hip-hop noted for heavy 808 bass, rapid hi-hats, and dark lyrical themes.

Fans praised the new album because the trapper blended melodic hooks with gritty street lyrics.
