snap

noun
American football: putting the ball in play
Pronunciation: / snæp / (AmE), / snæp / (BrE)
(in American football) the act of putting the ball in play by passing it backward (between the legs) to a back, typically the quarterback.

Examples

A football snap
A bad snap cost the team valuable yards during the play.
After the snap, the quarterback quickly passed the ball to the running back.
The center delivered a perfect snap to the quarterback.
