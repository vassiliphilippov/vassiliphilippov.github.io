neurobiology

noun
study of the nervous system in biology
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of biology concerned with the structure, function, and diseases of the nervous system

Examples

The study of neurobiology
Advances in neurobiology have led to new treatments for neurological disorders.
She decided to specialize in neurobiology after taking an introductory neuroscience course.
The latest research in neurobiology has shed light on how memories are formed and stored.
