mohawk

noun
hairstyle with a central strip of hair
6.7 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààmo ä.h…ëk / (AmE), / Ààm…ô ä.h…ëÀêk / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the sides of the head are shaved, leaving a strip of noticeably longer hair in the center.

Examples

A mohawk hairstyle
After shaving the sides, she sculpted the perfect mohawk for the party.
He dyed his mohawk bright green to stand out at the concert.
The punk rocker's distinctive mohawk instantly caught everyone's attention.
