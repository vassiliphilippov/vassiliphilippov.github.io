silviculture

noun
science of forest management
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɪˌkʌltʃər / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvɪˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the science and practice of controlling the establishment, growth, composition, and quality of forests.

Examples

Silviculture science
He studied silviculture to learn how to manage forest ecosystems effectively.
Many countries invest in silviculture to increase both timber production and biodiversity.
Sustainable silviculture practices help preserve forests for future generations.
