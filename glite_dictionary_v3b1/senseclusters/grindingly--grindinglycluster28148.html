grindingly

adverb
harsh or grating manner
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves grinding, crushing, or producing a harsh, grating sound or effect

Examples

Grindingly loud noise
The old train moved grindingly along the rusty tracks, making everyone inside uneasy.
He spoke grindingly, his voice rough from hours of shouting.
The wheels turned grindingly as the cart struggled up the steep hill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harshly - in a rough or unpleasant manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a gentle or even manner
