trollish

adjective
like a troll
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtrɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling a troll, especially in being ugly, coarse, or brutish in appearance or manner

Examples

Trollish face
The villain's trollish features made him stand out in the crowd.
She gave him a trollish grin that revealed her rough sense of humor.
His trollish behavior at the party made everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grotesque - strangely or comically ugly or distorted
  • antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance or manner
