unblest

adjective
not blessed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɛst / (BrE)
not blessed; lacking good fortune, happiness, or divine favor

Examples

An unblest village
The village remained unblest by the prosperity that neighboring towns enjoyed.
He felt unblest with the talents his siblings seemed to possess naturally.
In the story, the hero was unblest by fortune until the very end.
The villagers believed the old house was unblest and avoided it after dark.
He felt unblest as he faced hardship after hardship with no relief in sight.
Legends spoke of an unblest forest where travelers often lost their way.

  • synonym: cursed - affected by a curse; under a spell of misfortune
  • synonym: unfortunate - not having good luck or favorable circumstances
