boatman

noun
man who drives or rows a boat
1.5 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.mən/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.mən/ (BrE)
a man whose job is to row, steer, or drive a boat, especially as a ferryman or guide

Examples

A boatman rows boats
As a child, he dreamed of becoming a boatman like his father.
The boatman skillfully guided the canoe down the winding river.
We hired a boatman to take us across the lake early in the morning.
