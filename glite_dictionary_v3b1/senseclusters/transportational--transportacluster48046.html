transportational

adjective
relating to transport
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænspɔrˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the act or process of moving people or goods from one place to another

Examples

The government invested in new transportational infrastructure to support economic growth.
