uraeus

noun
Egyptian cobra emblem
Pronunciation: / jʊˈriːəs / (AmE), / jʊˈriːəs / (BrE)
a stylized upright cobra symbol used in ancient Egyptian art and worn on the headdresses of pharaohs and deities as a sign of authority and protection
