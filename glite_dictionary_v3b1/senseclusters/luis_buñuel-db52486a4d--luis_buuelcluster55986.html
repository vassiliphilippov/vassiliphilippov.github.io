Luis Buñuel

proper noun
Spanish surrealist film director
Pronunciation: / luˈis buˈnwɛl / (AmE), / luˈiːs bʊˈnjuːɛl / (BrE)
(1900–1983) Spanish film director known for his influential surrealist and avant-garde works.

Examples

Luis Buñuel film director
Luis Buñuel is best known for directing the surrealist classic "Un Chien Andalou."
Film students often study the work of Luis Buñuel for its groundbreaking use of symbolism.
The influence of Luis Buñuel can be seen in the works of modern filmmakers.
