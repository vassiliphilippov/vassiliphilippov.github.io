spastically

adverb
awkwardly, offensively
Pronunciation: / spæˈstɪkli / (AmE), / spæˈstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or relates to spastic movements, often used offensively to describe awkward or uncontrolled actions.

Examples

Move spastically with arms
He waved his arms spastically when he lost his balance on the stage.
The character in the film moved spastically, which some viewers found offensive.
Using the word spastically to describe someone's actions can be hurtful and inappropriate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncoordinated manner
  • antonym: gracefully - in a smooth and elegant manner
