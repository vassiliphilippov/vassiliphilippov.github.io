prick

noun
small carved or scratched depression
Pronunciation: / prɪk / (AmE), / prɪk / (BrE)
a small indentation or depression scratched or carved into a surface, often as a mark or guide

Examples

A prick mark
Each prick on the chart indicated a measurement taken during the experiment.
She identified the stonework by the tiny prick left by the mason.
The craftsman made a prick on the wood to mark the starting point.
