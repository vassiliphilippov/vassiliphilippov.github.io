newspaperish

adjective
like newspaper writing in style
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌpeɪpərɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of newspaper writing, especially in style or tone

Examples

A newspaperish style
Her writing became noticeably more newspaperish after she started working at the local press.
Some editors prefer a less newspaperish tone for feature stories.
The critique argued that the essay's newspaperish style made it less engaging for academic readers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalistic - relating to newspaper or magazine style
