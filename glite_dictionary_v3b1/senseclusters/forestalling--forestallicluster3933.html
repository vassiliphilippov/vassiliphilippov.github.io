forestalling

noun
the act of preventing by anticipation
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈstɑlɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːˈstɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of preventing something by anticipating it and taking effective action

Examples

Effective forestalling problems
Effective forestalling of problems can save a company time and money.
Her forestalling of criticism prevented any objections from being raised.
Through careful forestalling of objections, the proposal was passed smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prevention - the action of stopping something from happening
  • antonym: reaction - acting after something has happened, as opposed to anticipating it
