unembarrassing

adjective
not causing embarrassment
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈbærəsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbærəsɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing shame, discomfort, or embarrassment

Examples

An unembarrassing answer
He gave an unembarrassing answer to the difficult question during the interview.
The teacher created an unembarrassing environment where students felt safe to ask questions.
Their honest discussion about mistakes was refreshingly unembarrassing for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comfortable - making you feel relaxed and at ease
  • antonym: embarrassing - causing shame or discomfort
