grouse

verb
to complain; to grumble
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊs / (AmE), / ɡraʊs / (BrE)
to complain, often about something unimportant

Examples

To grouse about work
He tends to grouse about the weather whenever it rains.
Stop grousing and help us finish the work.
They groused about the new policies all afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complain - to express dissatisfaction or annoyance
  • synonym: grumble - to complain in a quiet, bad-tempered way
