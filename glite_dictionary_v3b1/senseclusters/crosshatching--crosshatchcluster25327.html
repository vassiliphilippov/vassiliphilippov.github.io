crosshatching

noun
drawing shading technique
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌhætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌhætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a drawing technique in which intersecting sets of parallel lines are used to create shading or texture

Examples

Crosshatching drawing technique
The illustrator used crosshatching to add depth and shadow to the landscape drawing.
Learning crosshatching can help artists create more realistic textures in their sketches.
She practiced crosshatching techniques to improve the shading in her comic book art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatching - a drawing technique using closely spaced parallel lines for shading
  • synonym: stippling - a drawing technique using dots to create shading or texture
