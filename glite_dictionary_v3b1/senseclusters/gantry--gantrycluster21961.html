gantry

noun
framework for mounting or supporting equipment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæntri / (AmE), / ˈɡæntri / (BrE)
A framework of steel bars supported on side structures, used to bridge over or around something, such as for mounting railway signals or supporting a traveling crane.

Examples

A large gantry frame
A large gantry spanned the entire construction site, carrying a traveling crane.
Technicians inspected the gantry for any signs of structural weakness before starting the lifting operation.
The new railway station was fitted with a gantry to hold multiple signal lights over the tracks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridge - a structure built to span physical obstacles
  • synonym: crane - a machine for lifting heavy objects
