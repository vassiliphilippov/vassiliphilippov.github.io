centration

noun
lens alignment
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.treɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈsen.trə.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈsen.treɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈsen.trə.ʃən/ (BrE)
the precise alignment of the optical centers of lenses with the wearer's pupils or visual axes, especially when making spectacles

Examples

Glasses centration
Proper centration of the lenses is essential for comfortable vision with new glasses.
The optician double-checked the centration before finalizing the order for the spectacles.
Incorrect centration can cause headaches and visual discomfort for the wearer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alignment - the arrangement in a straight line or correct relative positions
  • antonym: decentration - the misalignment of optical centers with the wearer's pupils
