bloodless revolution

noun
nonviolent change of power
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌd.ləs ˌrɛv.əˈluː.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs ˌrɛv.əˈluː.ʃən / (BrE)
a change in government or power that occurs without the use of violence

Examples

A bloodless revolution succeeds
Historians praised the bloodless revolution as a model for peaceful political change.
The bloodless revolution in the country astonished international observers with its peaceful transition of power.
Their campaign aimed to achieve a bloodless revolution through civic engagement rather than street protests.
