Archaeornis

noun
genus of extinct primitive birds (Jurassic)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrkiˈɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˌɑːkiˈɔːnɪs / (BrE)
a genus of extinct primitive birds known from Jurassic fossils, considered an early link between dinosaurs and modern birds.
