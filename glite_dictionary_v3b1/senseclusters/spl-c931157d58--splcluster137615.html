SPL

proper noun
Scottish football league
Pronunciation: / ɛs piː ɛl / (AmE), / ɛs piː ɛl / (BrE)
the Scottish Premier League, the top division of professional football in Scotland from 1998 to 2013

Examples

Scottish SPL football

The SPL attracted many talented players from around the world during its existence.
After 2013, the SPL was replaced by the Scottish Premiership.
