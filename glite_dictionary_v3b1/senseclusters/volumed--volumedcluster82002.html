volumed

adjective
having a specified number of volumes
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑljumd / (AmE), / ˈvɒljuːmd / (BrE)
(often used in combination) consisting of or having a specified number or type of volumes.

Examples

A six-volumed set

The library has a rare, three-volumed edition of the novel.
Their research is detailed in a five-volumed report.
