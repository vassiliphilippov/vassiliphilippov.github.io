colonial architecture

noun
architecture from colonial times
2.3 3.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈloʊ.niː.əl ˈɑːr.kə.tek.tʃər/ /; / /kə.ˈloʊ.njəl ˈɑːr.kə.tek.tʃər/ (AmE), /kə.ˈləʊ.niː.əl ˈɑː.kɪ.tek.tʃə/ /; / /kə.ˈləʊ.njəl ˈɑː.kɪ.tek.tʃə/ (BrE)
