trade

noun
business or custom received by a shop
1.0
Pronunciation: / treɪd / (AmE), / treɪd / (BrE)
the amount of business or custom a shop, company, or commercial establishment receives from its customers

Examples

Busy trade at shops
Bad weather kept much of the usual trade away from the market.
Holiday sales brought extra trade to local stores.
The café relies on lunchtime trade from nearby offices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: business - the commercial activity of providing goods and services
  • synonym: patronage - the support given by customers to a business
