scorcher

noun
exceptionally impressive event or performance
Pronunciation: / c8sk54rt835a / (AmE), / c8sk54d0t8359 / (BrE)
an event, performance, or thing that is exceptionally impressive, exciting, or excellent.

Examples

A scorcher show
The Friday night concert was a scorcher, drawing huge crowds and rave reviews.
Her opening set was a scorcher that left the audience cheering for several minutes.
Despite poor weather, the final match turned into a scorcher remembered for skill and drama.
