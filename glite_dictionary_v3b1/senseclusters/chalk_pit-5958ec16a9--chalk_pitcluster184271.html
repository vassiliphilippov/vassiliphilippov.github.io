chalk pit

noun
a quarry for extracting chalk
Pronunciation: / tʃɑk pɪt / (AmE), / tʃɔːk pɪt / (BrE)
a large hole or open excavation in the ground from which chalk is extracted

Examples

A chalk pit site
A new housing development was planned on the site of a former chalk pit.
The children explored the old chalk pit behind their village.
Wildflowers have started to cover the abandoned chalk pit near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quarry - a place where stone or other materials are extracted from the earth
