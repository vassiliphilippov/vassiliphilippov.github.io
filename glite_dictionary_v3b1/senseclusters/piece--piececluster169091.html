piece

noun
portion of a natural object
Pronunciation: / pis / (AmE), / piːs / (BrE)
a part, portion, or share of something that has been separated or divided from a larger whole (such as food, objects, or profits)

Examples

A piece of cake
A piece of marble broke off the ancient statue during the excavation.
He picked up a piece of driftwood from the beach.
They each took a piece of fruit from the basket.
Each piece of the puzzle fits perfectly into place.
I found a piece of glass on the floor after the vase broke.
The sculpture was missing a crucial piece when it was discovered.
He ate the last piece of bread with some jam.
She cut a piece of cheesecake and handed it to me.
Would you like another piece of pizza?

Related Terms

  • synonym: part - a portion or division of a whole

  • synonym: share - a part or portion allotted to, or belonging to, a person
  • synonym: slice - a thin, broad piece cut from something larger, especially food
  • antonym: whole - an entity regarded as a complete unit
