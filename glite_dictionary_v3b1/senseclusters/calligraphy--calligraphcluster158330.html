calligraphy

noun
the art of beautiful handwriting
4.1
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪɡrəfi / (AmE), / kəˈlɪɡrəfi / (BrE)
the art of writing beautifully by hand, or beautiful, decorative handwriting itself

Examples

Beautiful calligraphy
He practiced calligraphy every evening, hoping to perfect his elegant script.
Invitations written in calligraphy added a touch of elegance to the wedding.
She admired the ancient calligraphy displayed in the museum's manuscript collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: penmanship - the art or skill of writing by hand
  • antonym: scribbling - careless or hurried writing
