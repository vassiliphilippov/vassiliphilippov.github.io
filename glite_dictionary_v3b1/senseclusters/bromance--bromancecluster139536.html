bromance

noun
intense but non-sexual male friendship
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊˌmæns / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊˌmæns / (BrE)
a close but non-sexual and affectionate friendship between two men

Examples

Some people mistake their bromance for something romantic, but it's just a deep friendship.
The movie explores the bromance between two lifelong friends facing new challenges.
Their bromance was the talk of the office, as they always did everything together.
