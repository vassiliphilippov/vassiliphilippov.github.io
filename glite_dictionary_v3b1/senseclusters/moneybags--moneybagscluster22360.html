moneybags

noun
bag for money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌbæɡz / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌbæɡz / (BrE)
a bag or pouch used for carrying or storing money, especially coins or cash

Examples

A moneybags full of coins
The thief stuffed the stolen coins into a moneybags before escaping.
She kept her savings in a small moneybags hidden in her closet.
Old western movies often show robbers carrying moneybags after a bank heist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purse - a small bag for carrying money, especially by women
  • synonym: wallet - a flat case for carrying money and cards
