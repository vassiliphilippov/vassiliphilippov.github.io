oil painting

noun
picture made with oil paints
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ɔɪl ˈpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a picture created by applying oil-based paints to a surface, typically canvas or panel

Examples

A beautiful oil painting
