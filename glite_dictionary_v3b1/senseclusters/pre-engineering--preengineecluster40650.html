pre-engineering

noun
engineering preparation course
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪr.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
a course or program designed to prepare students for further study in engineering

Examples

Pre-engineering program
Many high schools now offer pre-engineering programs to introduce students to technical concepts.
Completing a pre-engineering course helped him decide which field of engineering to pursue.
She chose pre-engineering as her elective to prepare for university studies.
