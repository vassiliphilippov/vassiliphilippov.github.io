tool

noun
implement for work or a profession
Pronunciation: / tuːl / (AmE), / tuːl / (BrE)
an instrument or implement, especially one held in the hand, used for carrying out a particular function in a trade or profession

Examples

A tool box

Electricians carry a variety of tools for different types of work.
She used a special tool to carve the intricate design.
