objective

noun
goal; aim to be achieved
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
a goal or purpose that a person or organization aims to achieve

Examples

Project objective
Her objective was to finish the project before the deadline.
Setting a clear objective helps employees stay focused on their work.
The team's main objective is to reduce costs by 20% this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goal - the result or achievement toward which effort is directed
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
