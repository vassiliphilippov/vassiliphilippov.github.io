statesmanlike

adjective
having qualities of a statesman
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtsmənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtsmənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Possessing the qualities or conduct considered appropriate for a statesman, such as wisdom, fairness, and diplomacy.

Examples

A statesmanlike solution
A statesmanlike solution requires patience, vision, and careful diplomacy.
His statesmanlike response to the crisis earned him respect across party lines.
The committee admired her statesmanlike approach to negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diplomatic - having or showing skill in dealing with people or sensitive matters
  • synonym: politic - showing good judgment, especially in political or practical matters
  • antonym: unwise - lacking good judgment; not prudent
