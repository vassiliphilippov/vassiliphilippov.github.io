daydreaming

noun
absentminded dreaming while awake
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌdriːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌdriːmɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of letting your mind wander to pleasant or imaginative thoughts while awake and not concentrating on what is happening around you.

Examples

Daydreaming during work
During meetings, I often find myself daydreaming about my next vacation.
His constant daydreaming sometimes gets in the way of finishing his work.
She was caught daydreaming in class instead of paying attention to the lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reverie - a state of being pleasantly lost in one's thoughts; a daydream
