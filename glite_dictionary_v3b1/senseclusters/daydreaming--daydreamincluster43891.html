daydreaming

noun
absentminded dreaming while awake
3.2 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààde…™ÀådriÀêm…™≈ã / (AmE), / Ààde…™ÀådriÀêm…™≈ã / (BrE)
The activity of letting your mind wander to pleasant or imaginative thoughts while awake and not concentrating on what is happening around you.

Examples

Daydreaming during work
During meetings, I often find myself daydreaming about my next vacation.
His constant daydreaming sometimes gets in the way of finishing his work.
She was caught daydreaming in class instead of paying attention to the lesson.

Synonyms

  • dreaming
    2.8 0.57/m
    dreaming can mean having visions while asleep or fancy thoughts while awake, while daydreaming is waking pleasant mind-wandering.
  • daydream
    2.6 2.58/m
    daydream is a short set of pleasant images or thoughts, while daydreaming is the ongoing activity of letting your mind wander.
  • reverie
    7.5 1.94/m
    reverie is a quiet, often poetic state of being lost in thought, while daydreaming is casual, awake mind-wandering.
  • woolgathering
    9.0 0.00/m
    woolgathering is being absent-minded and careless because of dreamy thoughts, while daydreaming is simply pleasant, waking imagination.
