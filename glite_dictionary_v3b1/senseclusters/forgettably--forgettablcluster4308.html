forgettably

adverb
not memorably
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtəbli / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is not memorable or is easily forgotten

Examples

Spoken forgettably
The speech was delivered so forgettably that few people remembered its main points.
Despite the hype, the concert ended forgettably and failed to excite the audience.
He performed forgettably in the competition and did not stand out among the other contestants.

Related Terms
