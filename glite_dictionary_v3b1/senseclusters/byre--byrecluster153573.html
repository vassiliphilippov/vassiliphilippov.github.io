byre

noun
barn or shed for cows
Pronunciation: / baɪɚ / (AmE), / baɪə / (BrE)
a building or shed where cows are kept; a cowshed

Examples

A byre for cows
A pungent smell drifted from the byre every morning as the cows were milked.
During the winter months, the farmer kept his herd safe in the byre.
The old stone byre was filled with the gentle sound of cows lowing at dusk.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: barn - a large farm building for storing grain or housing animals
  • synonym: cowshed - a shed or shelter for cows
