heinously

adverb
in a terribly evil or wicked manner
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪ.nəs.li / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.nəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is shockingly wicked, evil, or reprehensible.

Examples

Behave heinously
She condemned the dictator for behaving heinously toward his people.
Such crimes are heinously brutal and receive the harshest punishments.
The criminal acted heinously, shocking the entire community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocently - in a manner free from evil or wrong
  • synonym: wickedly - in a wicked or evil manner
