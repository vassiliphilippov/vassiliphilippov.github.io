salt

verb
add salt to season or preserve
1.0 2.19/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ”lt / (AmE), / sÉ’lt / (BrE)
to add salt to food or another substance, especially to season or preserve it

Examples

To salt the food
Before grilling the steak, make sure to salt it generously on both sides.
She forgot to salt the soup, so it tasted bland.
The fishermen would salt the fish to preserve them during long journeys.
He spent the afternoon salting hams in preparation for the coming season.
Long before refrigeration, people would salt fish so it could last through the winter.
They used to salt pork to keep it edible during long voyages.
