stock

noun
lumber used as material
6.7
Pronunciation: / stɑk / (AmE), / stɒk / (BrE)
a supply of goods, merchandise, animals, or materials kept for future use or sale

Examples

Stock of goods
The carpenter selected a piece of stock to craft the table legs.
They milled the round stock to the precise diameter needed.
We ordered extra lengths of oak stock for the staircase project.
The manager checked the stock to make sure there were enough supplies for the week.
They ran out of printer paper because their stock was not replenished in time.
We need to reduce old stock before ordering new products.
Disease among the stock can cause serious financial losses for a ranch.
Good pasture is essential for keeping healthy stock on the farm.
The farmer counted all the stock in his barn before winter set in.
