pullback

noun
decorative device for holding curtains aside
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpʊl.bæk / (BrE)
a decorative cord, loop, or band used to draw curtains or draperies to the side and hold them open

Examples

Curtain pullback
She replaced the old pullbacks with modern metallic ones to give the room a fresh look.

Without pullbacks, the thick curtains would obscure much of the daylight.
