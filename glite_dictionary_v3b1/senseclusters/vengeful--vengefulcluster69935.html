vengeful

adjective
seeking revenge
5.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈvendʒ.fəl/ (AmE), /ˈvendʒ.fəl/ (BrE)
Having or showing a strong desire for revenge; seeking to harm someone in return for a perceived injury or wrong.

Examples

A vengeful person
He became vengeful after losing everything to his rivals.
She gave him a vengeful glare after hearing his cruel remark.
The movie tells the story of a vengeful spirit haunting the old mansion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiving - willing to forgive; not seeking revenge or holding a grudge
  • synonym: vindictive - having or showing a strong or unreasoning desire for revenge
