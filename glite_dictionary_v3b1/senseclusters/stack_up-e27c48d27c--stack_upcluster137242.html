stack up

verb
arrange in a pile or stack
Pronunciation: / stæk ʌp / (AmE), / stæk ʌp / (BrE)
to arrange things in a neat pile or stack, one on top of another

Examples

To stack up boxes

Every morning, the bakery stacks up fresh loaves on the shelves.
