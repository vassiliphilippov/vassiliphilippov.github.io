boeuf fondu bourguignon

noun
beef cubes cooked in oil, dipped in sauces
7.1
Pronunciation: / bəʊ f fɒnˈduː bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (AmE), / bəʊ f fɒnˈduː bʊəˌɡiːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
