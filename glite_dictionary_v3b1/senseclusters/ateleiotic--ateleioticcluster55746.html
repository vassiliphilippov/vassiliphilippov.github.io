ateleiotic

adjective
relating to ateleiosis (dwarfism with normal proportions)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌætəˌliːˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌætiːliˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of ateleiosis, a form of dwarfism with normal proportions of the body.

Examples

Ateleiotic dwarfism
A study of ateleiotic individuals revealed no abnormalities in intelligence.
Children showing ateleiotic growth may reach only a short adult height but with balanced body features.
The patient was diagnosed as ateleiotic due to her proportional dwarfism.
