show off

verb
display or behave to attract attention
1.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /ʃoʊ ɔːf/ /; / /ʃoʊ ɑːf/ (AmE), /ʃəʊ ɒf/ /; / /ʃəʊ ɔːf/ (BrE)
