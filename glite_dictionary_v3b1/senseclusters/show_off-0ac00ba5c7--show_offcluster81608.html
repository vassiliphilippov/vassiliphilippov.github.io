show off

verb
display or behave to attract attention
1.1
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ ˈɔf /; / ʃoʊ ɒf /; / ʃoʊ ɔf / (AmE), / ʃəʊ ˈɒf /; / ʃəʊ ɒf / (BrE)
to behave or display something in a way that is meant to attract attention or admiration, often in a proud or boastful way
