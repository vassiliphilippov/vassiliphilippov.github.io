trapezial

adjective
of the trapezium bone
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiːziəl / (AmE), / trəˈpiːziəl / (BrE)
relating to the trapezium bone in the wrist, located at the base of the thumb.

Examples

Trapezial bone pain
The doctor noted swelling in the trapezial region after the injury.
A trapezial fracture can make it difficult to move your thumb.
Physical therapy helped restore strength to her trapezial area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpal - relating to the bones of the wrist
