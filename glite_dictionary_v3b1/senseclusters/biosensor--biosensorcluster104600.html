biosensor

noun
biological measuring device
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌsɛnsər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌsɛnsə / (BrE)
a device that uses biological materials to detect and measure substances or changes, often for medical or scientific purposes

Examples

Biosensors measure glucose
Biosensors are used to monitor glucose levels in diabetic patients.
Researchers developed new biosensors to detect harmful bacteria in water supplies.
A biosensor can provide rapid results for medical diagnostics in remote areas.
The hospital installed a new biosensor to monitor patients' blood sugar levels automatically.
Researchers developed a portable biosensor that can detect toxins in drinking water.
A biosensor can provide real-time data about environmental pollution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyzer - a device that examines substances to determine their composition
  • synonym: detector - a device that discovers or measures something
