ordinate

verb
to arrange systematically
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdɪneɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪneɪt / (BrE)
to arrange or organize things in a particular order or pattern

Examples

To ordinate items by type
The librarian ordinated the books by subject to make them easier to find.
For the experiment, the samples were carefully ordinated according to their chemical properties.
She ordinates her tasks each morning to stay organized throughout the day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorder - to make something untidy or not organized
  • synonym: organize - to arrange or structure things systematically
