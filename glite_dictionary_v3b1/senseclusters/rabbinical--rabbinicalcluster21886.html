rabbinical

adjective
relating to rabbis or their teachings
Pronunciation: / rəˈbɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / rəˈbɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to rabbis or their teachings and writings, especially in Judaism.

Examples

Rabbinical school
He attended a prestigious rabbinical school in Jerusalem.
His rabbinical training prepared him for a career in religious leadership.
Many rabbinical texts are written in Hebrew and Aramaic.
