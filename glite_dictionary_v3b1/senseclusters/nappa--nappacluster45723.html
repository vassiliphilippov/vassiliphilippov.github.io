nappa

noun
Chinese cabbage variety
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpə / (AmE), / ˈnæpə / (BrE)
a type of Chinese cabbage with pale, tightly packed leaves, commonly used in East Asian cuisine

Examples

Chopped nappa cabbage
She added chopped nappa to the soup for a mild, crunchy texture.
Many Korean dishes use nappa as a main ingredient, especially in kimchi.
At the market, he bought fresh nappa to prepare a traditional stir-fry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bok choy - a type of Chinese cabbage with dark green leaves and white stalks
  • degree lower: lettuce - a leafy green vegetable often used in salads
