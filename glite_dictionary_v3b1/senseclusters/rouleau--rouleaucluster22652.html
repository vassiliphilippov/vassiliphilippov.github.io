rouleau

noun
roll of coins wrapped in paper
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruːˈloʊ / (AmE), / ruːˈləʊ / (BrE)
A roll of coins, especially one wrapped in paper for ease of handling and counting.

Examples

A rouleau of coins
Bank tellers often prepare each denomination in a separate rouleau for quick distribution.
She collected old coin rouleaux as a hobby.
The cashier handed me a rouleau of quarters for the vending machine.
