drinkability

noun
quality of being drinkable
Pronunciation: / ˌdrɪŋkəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌdrɪŋkəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being safe, easy, or pleasant to drink

Examples

The drinkability of water
The drinkability of the tap water was questioned after the recent contamination scare.
Craft brewers often focus on the drinkability of their beers to appeal to a wider audience.
She praised the wine for its smoothness and high drinkability at the tasting event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palatability - the quality of being pleasant to taste
  • synonym: potability - the quality of being safe to drink
  • antonym: undrinkable - not safe or pleasant to drink
