motivationally

adverb
in an encouraging way
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌməʊtɪˈveɪʃənəli / (BrE)

Examples

The speech was delivered motivationally to encourage everyone to reach their goals.
