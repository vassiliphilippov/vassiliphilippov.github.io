annuity in advance

noun
annuity paid at start of each period
2.0 8.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnuː.ə.t̬i ɪn ədˈvæns / (AmE), / əˈnjuː.ɪ.ti ɪn ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
An annuity in which each payment is made at the beginning of each period, rather than at the end.

Examples

An annuity in advance payment
An annuity in advance ensures the recipient receives payment at the start of every term.
If you pay your rent at the beginning of each month, it is considered an annuity in advance.
Mortgage repayments structured as an annuity in advance can have different present value calculations than those at period end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annuity due - an annuity with payments at the beginning of each period
  • antonym: ordinary annuity - an annuity with payments at the end of each period
