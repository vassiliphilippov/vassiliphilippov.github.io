carry oneself with dignity

verb
behave with composure and self-respect
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæɹi ˈwʌnsɛlf wɪð ˈdɪɡnɪti / (AmE), / ˈkæɹi ˈwʌnsɛlf wɪð ˈdɪɡnɪti / (BrE)
to behave in a manner that shows composure, self-respect, and pride, especially in difficult circumstances

Examples

To carry oneself with dignity
Despite the harsh criticism, she carried herself with dignity throughout the ordeal.
Even in defeat, he managed to carry himself with dignity and grace.
They advised him to carry himself with dignity during the difficult negotiations.
