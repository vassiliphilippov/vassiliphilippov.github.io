not guilty

adjective
found not to have committed a charged crime
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑt ˈɡɪlti / (AmE), / nɒt ˈɡɪlti / (BrE)
Legally judged to have not committed the crime with which one was charged.

Examples

Found not guilty
After months of testimony, the judge pronounced her not guilty on all counts.
The defendant was found not guilty by the jury and was released from custody.
The lawyer argued persuasively for a not guilty verdict.
