buff-brown

adjective
brown tinged with buff
9.4
Pronunciation: / bʌfˈbraʊn / (AmE), / bʌfˈbraʊn / (BrE)
Of a brown color with a slight yellowish or light tan tinge; brown tinged with buff.

Examples

A buff-brown coat
Her coat was a warm buff-brown shade, perfect for the autumn season.
The bird's buff-brown feathers provided excellent camouflage among the dry grass.
The puppy had a distinctive buff-brown patch above its eyes.
