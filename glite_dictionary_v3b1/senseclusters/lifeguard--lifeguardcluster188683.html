lifeguard

verb
to supervise swimmers
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌɡɑrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌɡɑːd / (BrE)
to work as a person responsible for watching over swimmers and ensuring their safety in or near water

Examples

To lifeguard at a pool
During the summer, she plans to lifeguard at the community pool every weekend.
He learned important safety skills while lifeguarding at the beach last year.
Many students lifeguard at local pools to earn extra money during their holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rescue - to save someone from danger
  • synonym: supervise - to watch over and be responsible for others' safety or actions
