lifeguard

verb
to supervise swimmers
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪf.gɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪf.gɑːd/ (BrE)
to work as a person responsible for watching over swimmers and ensuring their safety in or near water

Examples

He learned important safety skills while lifeguarding at the beach last year.
Many students lifeguard at local pools to earn extra money during their holidays.
