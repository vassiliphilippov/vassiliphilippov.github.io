intonaco

noun
smooth plaster layer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.toʊˈnɑː.koʊ /; / ɪnˈtoʊnəˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˌɪn.təˈnɑː.kəʊ /; / ɪnˈtɒnəkəʊ / (BrE)
a final smooth layer of plaster applied to walls or ceilings, often used as the surface for painting (such as frescoes)
