Piedmontese

noun
person from Piedmont
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdmɒnˈtiːz / (AmE), / ˌpiːdmɒnˈtiːz / (BrE)
a person who was born in or whose family comes from Piedmont, a region in northwest Italy

Examples
