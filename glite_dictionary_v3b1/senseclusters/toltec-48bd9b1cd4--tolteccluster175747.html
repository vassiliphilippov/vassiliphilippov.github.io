Toltec

noun
follower of Toltec spirituality
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊl.tek/ /; / /ˈtɑːl.tek/ (AmE), /ˈtɒl.tek/ /; / /ˈtəʊl.tek/ (BrE)
(in modern spiritual or New Age contexts) a person who follows or practices Toltec-inspired teachings or spirituality, often drawing on contemporary interpretations of ancient Mesoamerican ideas.

Examples

Modern Toltec teachings
She identifies as a Toltec, studying modern interpretations of ancient Mesoamerican spiritual teachings.
Many readers of 'The Four Agreements' consider themselves Toltec and apply its practical teachings daily.
At the retreat, several self-described Toltec practitioners explored adapted rituals and reflective exercises.
