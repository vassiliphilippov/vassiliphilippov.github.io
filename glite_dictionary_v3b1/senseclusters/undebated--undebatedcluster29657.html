undebated

adjective
not discussed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈbeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈbeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not discussed, argued about, or questioned; accepted without debate

Examples

An undebated issue
The new policy was left undebated at the conference due to lack of time.
Several important issues remained undebated by the committee this year.
His suggestion went undebated, so no changes were made to the plan.
The results of the experiment remained undebated among the scientific community.
His authority in the field was undebated for many years.
The policy was accepted as undebated fact by everyone in the organization.

  • antonym: debated - discussed or argued about formally
  • synonym: uncontested - not disputed or challenged
  • synonym: undisputed - not challenged or questioned
