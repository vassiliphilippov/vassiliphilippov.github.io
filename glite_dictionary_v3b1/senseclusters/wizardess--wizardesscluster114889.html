wizardess

noun
female wizard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪzərdɛs / (AmE), / ˈwɪzədɛs / (BrE)
a woman who practices magic or is skilled in wizardry

Examples

A wizardess casts spells
The wizardess cast a powerful spell to protect the village.
Legends tell of a wise wizardess who lived deep in the forest.
The young girl dreamed of becoming a famous wizardess one day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sorceress - a woman who uses magic powers
  • synonym: witch - a woman thought to have magical powers
  • synonym: wizard - a person, usually a man, who practices magic
