spiker

noun
editor who cancels stories
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪkər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪkə / (BrE)
an editor or journalist who refuses to publish or cancels a story, especially by removing it from publication consideration

Examples

Newspaper spiker
The chief spiker at the newspaper decided not to run the controversial article.
After the story was removed, the reporter blamed the spiker for suppressing important news.
Many journalists fear their work will be stopped by a spiker before reaching the public.
