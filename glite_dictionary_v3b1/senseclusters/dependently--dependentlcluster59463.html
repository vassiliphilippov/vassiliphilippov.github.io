dependently

adverb
in a reliant manner
3.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɛndəntli / (AmE), / dɪˈpɛndəntli / (BrE)
in a way that relies on or is controlled by someone or something else

Examples

Live dependently on parents
She lived dependently on her parents until she found a job.
The puppy behaved dependently, always following its owner around the house.
Some students work dependently with tutors to improve their grades.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independently - in a way that does not rely on others
