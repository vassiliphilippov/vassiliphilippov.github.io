habit

noun
mode of growth (botany, mineralogy)
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
the characteristic form or mode of growth of a plant or crystal, especially as determined by its structure or environment

Examples

Crystal habit
Many minerals are classified by their crystal habit, such as prismatic or tabular.
The cactus displays a columnar habit that distinguishes it from other desert plants.
This shrub has a spreading habit, making it ideal for ground cover.
