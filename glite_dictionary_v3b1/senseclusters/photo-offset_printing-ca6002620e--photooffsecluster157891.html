photo-offset printing

noun
printing process using photomechanical plates
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊ ˈɒfˌsɛt ˈprɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊ ˈɒfˌsɛt ˈprɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a printing technique in which images are transferred from photomechanical plates to a rubber blanket, then onto the printing surface.

Examples

Photo-offset printing technique
Advancements in photo-offset printing have improved the quality and speed of modern publications.
Many books published in the mid-20th century relied on photo-offset printing techniques.
The magazine was produced using photo-offset printing for sharper images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offset printing - a general method of printing where inked images are transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket and then to the surface
