closing time

noun
time a business closes to the public
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
the time of day when a business or public place stops serving customers and closes for the day

Examples

Closing time at shops
He rushed to the library, hoping to arrive before closing time.
Just before closing time, the staff began cleaning up the cafe.
Please be aware that closing time is at 10 PM sharp.
Many people rush to finish their shopping right before closing time.
The store manager announced that it was closing time and asked everyone to leave.
We planned to meet just before closing time so we could have one last drink.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opening time - the time when a business or establishment opens to the public
