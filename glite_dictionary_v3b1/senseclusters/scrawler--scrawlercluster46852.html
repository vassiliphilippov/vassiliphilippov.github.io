scrawler

noun
person with messy handwriting
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɔːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈskrɔːlə / (BrE)
a person whose handwriting is untidy and difficult to read

Examples

A scrawler person
As a lifelong scrawler, she often struggled to read her own handwriting.
His notes were such a mess that everyone called him a notorious scrawler.
The teacher could barely make out what the scrawler had written on the exam paper.
