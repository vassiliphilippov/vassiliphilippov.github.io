culminant

adjective
at the highest point
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlmɪnənt / (AmE), / ˈkʌlmɪnənt / (BrE)
reaching the highest point or most significant stage of something

Examples

Culminant moment
The final scene was the culminant moment of the entire play.
Her career reached its culminant phase with the release of her bestselling novel.
The festival's culminant event drew visitors from all over the country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low - at the lowest or least significant point
  • synonym: peak - the highest or most important point
