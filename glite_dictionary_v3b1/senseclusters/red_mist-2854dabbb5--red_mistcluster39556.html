red mist

noun
state of rage clouding judgment
7.2
Pronunciation: / rɛd mɪst / (AmE), / rɛd mɪst / (BrE)
a state of intense anger or rage that impairs one's judgment or self-control

Examples

A red mist of fury
As soon as she heard the news, a red mist of fury clouded her thoughts.
He apologized later, saying he was overtaken by the red mist and didn't mean what he said.
When the referee made a bad call, the red mist descended and he yelled at everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anger - a strong feeling of displeasure or hostility
  • antonym: calm - the state of being peaceful and free from anger
  • synonym: rage - violent and uncontrollable anger
