BPD

noun
fetal head measurement
Pronunciation: / biː piː diː / (AmE), / biː piː diː / (BrE)
the biparietal diameter, a measurement across the fetal head used in ultrasound to estimate fetal size and growth

Examples

Fetal BPD measurement
The doctor checked the BPD during the ultrasound to monitor the baby's development.
A normal BPD is an important indicator of healthy fetal growth.
The technician recorded the BPD along with other measurements for the obstetrician.

Related Terms

  • synonym: femur length - a measurement of the length of the fetal thigh bone used in ultrasound
  • synonym: head circumference - the distance around the largest part of a baby's head
