hindermost

adjective
farthest to the rear
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪndɚˌmoʊst / (AmE), / ˈhɪndəməʊst / (BrE)
farthest at the back; located most to the rear.

Examples

The hindermost animals
During the migration, the hindermost animals were the most vulnerable to predators.
In the hindermost row of the theater, the view was partially obstructed.
The hindermost car in the convoy kept its headlights on for visibility.

  • synonym: rearmost - furthest at the back
