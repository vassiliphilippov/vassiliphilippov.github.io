prickly personality

noun
sensitive or difficult character
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli pərˈsæ.nəl.i / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli pəˈsɒn.əl.i / (BrE)
a character that is easily irritated, sensitive, or difficult to get along with

Examples

A prickly personality
Despite his prickly personality, he is very loyal to his close friends.
Her prickly personality often led to misunderstandings with colleagues.
Working with someone with a prickly personality can be a real challenge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easygoing - a relaxed and tolerant character
  • synonym: sensitive - a person easily affected emotionally
