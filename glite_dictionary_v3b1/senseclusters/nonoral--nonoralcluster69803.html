nonoral

adjective
not by mouth
7.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɔrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈɔːrəl / (BrE)
not involving, relating to, or administered through the mouth or spoken communication

Examples

Nonoral medication
The doctor recommended a nonoral medication for patients who have difficulty swallowing pills.
Some vaccines are given in nonoral forms, such as injections or nasal sprays.
Communication with deaf individuals often relies on nonoral methods like sign language.
