bespoke

adjective
custom-made clothing
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspoʊk / (AmE), / bɪˈspəʊk / (BrE)
(of clothing) made to the customer's specifications; custom-made.

Examples

Bespoke suits
He prefers to wear bespoke suits that are tailored to his exact measurements.
The designer is known for creating bespoke wedding dresses for high-profile clients.
Their shop offers both ready-to-wear and bespoke clothing options.

Related Terms

  • antonym: off-the-rack - (of clothing) ready-made and not tailored to the individual
