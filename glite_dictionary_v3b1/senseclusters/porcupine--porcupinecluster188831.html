porcupine

noun
large rodent with sharp quills
8.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔːr.kjə.paɪn/ /; / /ˈpɔːr.kə.paɪn/ (AmE), /ˈpɔː.kjə.paɪn/ /; / /ˈpɔː.kə.paɪn/ (BrE)
a large rodent with a coat of sharp spines or quills mixed with its fur, used for defense against predators

Examples

A porcupine animal
