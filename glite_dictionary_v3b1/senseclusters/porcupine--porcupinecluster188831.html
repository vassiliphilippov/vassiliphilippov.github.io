porcupine

noun
large rodent with sharp quills
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːr.kjuˌpaɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.kjʊ.paɪn / (BrE)
a large rodent with a coat of sharp spines or quills mixed with its fur, used for defense against predators

Examples

A porcupine animal
Careful not to touch a porcupine; its quills can be very painful if they get stuck in your skin.
The porcupine is mostly nocturnal and feeds on leaves, herbs, and twigs.
We saw a porcupine in the forest, its quills raised as it sensed our presence.
