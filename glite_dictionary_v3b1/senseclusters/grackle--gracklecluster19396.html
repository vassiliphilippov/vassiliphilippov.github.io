grackle

noun
a North American blackbird
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrækəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrækəl / (BrE)
a long-tailed American blackbird with iridescent black plumage, belonging to the family Icteridae

Examples

American grackle bird
A noisy grackle perched on the fence, its feathers gleaming in the sunlight.
In the park, flocks of grackles can often be seen scavenging for food.
The distinctive call of the grackle filled the air at dawn.
