camphor ball

noun
sphere used to repel moths from clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈkæm.fɚ bɔl / (AmE), / ˈkæm.fə bɔːl / (BrE)
a small white ball containing camphor or naphthalene, used to protect stored clothing from moths

Examples

A camphor ball in closet
Be careful not to let children handle a camphor ball, as it can be toxic if ingested.
My grandmother always put a camphor ball in each drawer to keep the moths away.
The scent of a camphor ball reminds me of old wardrobes and suitcases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mothball - a small white ball containing a chemical to protect clothes from moths
