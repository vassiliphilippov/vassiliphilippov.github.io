impeccable

adjective
perfect; faultless
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛkəbəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛkəb(ə)l / (BrE)
in accordance with the highest standards; faultless or perfect

Examples

Impeccable quality
He always delivers work of impeccable quality, no matter the deadline.
Her impeccable manners impressed everyone at the dinner party.
The chef is known for his impeccable attention to detail in every dish he prepares.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faultless - without any fault; perfect
  • antonym: imperfect - not perfect; having faults or errors
