sabra

noun
native-born Israeli
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːbrə / (AmE), / ˈsɑːbrə / (BrE)
A native-born Israeli, especially one born to immigrant parents; often used to emphasize a perceived toughness and resilience.

Examples

A sabra Israeli
Although her parents moved from Poland, she was a true sabra, born and raised in Tel Aviv.
Many sabras are known for their straightforward manner and strong connection to Israeli culture.
The author, a proud sabra, described childhood during the early years of Israel.
