interpretation

noun
explanation or understanding of meaning
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɝːprəˈteɪʃən /; / ɪnˌtɚprəˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜːprɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
an explanation or understanding of the meaning or significance of something

Her interpretation of the poem gave it a whole new meaning for the class.
