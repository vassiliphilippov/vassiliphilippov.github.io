smell up

verb
fill with a bad or strong smell
3.5
Pronunciation: / smɛl ʌp / (AmE), / smɛl ʌp / (BrE)
to cause something to have a strong or unpleasant smell; to fill a place with a bad smell

Examples

To smell up a room
If you burn popcorn in the microwave, it'll smell up the entire house.
Someone left their lunch in the fridge for a week and it really smelled up the whole office.
Wet dog hair can smell up a car pretty quickly.
