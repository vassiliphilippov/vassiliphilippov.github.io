bugbane

noun
North American medicinal plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A North American plant with large leaves and racemes of yellowish-green flowers, known for yielding a toxic alkaloid used medicinally.
