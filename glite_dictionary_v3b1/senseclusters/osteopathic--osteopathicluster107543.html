osteopathic

adjective
relating to osteopathy
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
relating to a system of medical practice that emphasizes the body's ability to heal itself, often using manual therapy

Examples

Osteopathic treatment
She received osteopathic treatment for her back pain after trying other therapies.
Many patients choose osteopathic medicine for its holistic approach to health.
The clinic offers both traditional and osteopathic care for a variety of conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allopathic - relating to conventional medical treatment using drugs or surgery
  • synonym: osteopathy - a medical practice focusing on the body's self-healing abilities
