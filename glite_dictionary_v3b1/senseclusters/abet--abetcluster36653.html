abet

verb
assist or encourage wrongdoing
6.1
Pronunciation: / əˈbɛt / (AmE), / əˈbɛt / (BrE)
to actively help or encourage someone to do something wrong or illegal

Examples

To abet a crime
He was accused of trying to abet the criminals in their escape.
Her silence could be seen as an attempt to abet the wrongdoing.
It is illegal to abet someone in committing fraud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aid - to help or support someone or something
  • antonym: hinder - to make it difficult for someone to do something or for something to happen
