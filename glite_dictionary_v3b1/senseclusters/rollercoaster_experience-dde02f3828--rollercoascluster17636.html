rollercoaster experience

noun
an unpredictable, emotional situation
3.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.lər.koʊ.stər ɪk.ˈspɪr.iː.əns/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.lə.kəʊ.stə ɪk.ˈspɪə.rɪəns/ (BrE)
a situation or period of time that is filled with many sudden or extreme changes, especially in emotions or circumstances

Examples

A rollercoaster experience of emotions
Her first year at university was a real rollercoaster experience, full of challenges and triumphs.
Starting my own business has been a rollercoaster experience—some days are exhilarating, others completely nerve-wracking.
The merger turned out to be a rollercoaster experience for everyone involved, with many unexpected ups and downs.
