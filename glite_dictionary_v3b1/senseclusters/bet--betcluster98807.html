bet

noun
an act of gambling; a wager
1.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /bet/ (AmE), /bet/ (BrE)
an act or amount of risking money or something valuable on the outcome of an event; a wager

Examples

Place a bet
He lost his bet and had to pay for everyone's dinner.
On a dare and a bet, he jumped into the freezing lake.
She placed a bet on the outcome of the football match.
He lost his bet after the team unexpectedly lost the game.
She placed a bet on her favorite horse to win the race.
The total bet on the roulette table exceeded two thousand dollars.
