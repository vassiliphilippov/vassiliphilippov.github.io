Sparidae

noun
family of marine fishes (porgies, scups)
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛr.ɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈspɑː.rɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine fishes, commonly known as porgies or scups, characterized by laterally compressed bodies and strong teeth.
