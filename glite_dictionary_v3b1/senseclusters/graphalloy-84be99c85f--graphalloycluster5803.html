Graphalloy

proper noun
self-lubricating bearing material
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfəˌlɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡræfəˌlɔɪ / (BrE)
a trademarked brand of self-lubricating bearing material made from a combination of graphite and metal

Examples

Graphalloy bearing material

Because Graphalloy does not require oil, it is ideal for environments where lubrication is difficult.
Many pumps in the chemical industry are equipped with Graphalloy components for increased reliability.
