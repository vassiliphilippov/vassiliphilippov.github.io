conga

noun
Latin American drum
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə / (BrE)
a tall, narrow drum from Latin America, played with the hands, often used in Afro-Cuban music

Examples

A conga drum
The percussionist played a solo on the conga during the concert.
She learned to play rhythms on the conga in her music class.
The band added a lively beat with the sound of the conga.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bongo - a pair of small, open-bottomed drums played with the hands
  • synonym: djembe - a West African drum played with bare hands
