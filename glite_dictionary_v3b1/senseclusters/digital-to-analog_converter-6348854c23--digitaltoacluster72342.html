digital-to-analog converter

noun
device converting digital to analogue signals
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl tu ˈænəlˌɑːɡ kənˈvɝːt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl tə ˈænəlɒɡ kənˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a device that converts digital signals into analogue signals, often used in audio and video equipment

Examples

A digital-to-analog converter device
