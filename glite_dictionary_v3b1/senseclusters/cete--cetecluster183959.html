cete

noun
small group (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːt / (AmE), / ˈsiːt / (BrE)
an assembly or small group of people gathered together (archaic)

Examples

A cete of villagers
In the old village, a cete would gather to make important decisions.
The historian described a cete of elders who led the community.
A cete assembled in the town square to celebrate the harvest festival.
