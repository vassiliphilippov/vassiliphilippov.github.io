chalcidfly

noun
tiny parasitic wasp-like insect
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.sɪdˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkalsɪdflaɪ / (BrE)
A very small wasp-like insect whose larvae are parasites on the eggs and larvae of other insects, often used as a natural control of pests.
