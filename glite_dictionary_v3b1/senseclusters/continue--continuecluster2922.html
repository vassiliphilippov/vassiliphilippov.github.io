continue

verb
extend over an area or period
1.1 15/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàt…™n.ju / (AmE), / k…ônÀàt…™n.juÀê / (BrE)
to extend or last over a specific distance, area, or period of time

Examples

The road continues for miles
The coastline continues uninterrupted until it reaches the river delta.
The dry season continues from November to April every year in this region.
The old city walls continue for several kilometers beyond the main gate.
