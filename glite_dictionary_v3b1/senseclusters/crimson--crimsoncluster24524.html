Examples

Synonyms

  • flushed
    3.3 0.00/m
    flushed is a warm, often pink or red face after exercise or strong feeling, while crimson is a deep, vivid red.
  • red-faced
    3.4 1.24/m
    red-faced describes someone with a clearly red face from anger or shame, while crimson suggests a richer, darker red on the face.
  • reddened
    3.6 0.04/m
    reddened means the skin has become red often from irritation, exertion, or emotion, while crimson names a strong deep red on the face.
  • blushing
    1.4 0.00/m
    blushing is a light, involuntary pink from embarrassment or shyness, while crimson is a stronger deep red from exertion or strong emotion.
