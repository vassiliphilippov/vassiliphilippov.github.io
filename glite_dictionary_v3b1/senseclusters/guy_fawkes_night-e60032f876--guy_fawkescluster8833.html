Guy Fawkes Night

noun
British annual event on November 5th
Pronunciation: / ˌɡaɪ ˈfɑks ˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪ ˈfɔːks ˌnaɪt / (BrE)
A British annual event on November 5th commemorating the failure of the Gunpowder Plot, marked by fireworks and the burning of Guy Fawkes effigies.

Examples

Guy Fawkes Night fireworks
Children often make effigies to burn on Guy Fawkes Night.
Many people light fireworks and bonfires to celebrate Guy Fawkes Night.
We watched a spectacular display in the park on Guy Fawkes Night last year.
