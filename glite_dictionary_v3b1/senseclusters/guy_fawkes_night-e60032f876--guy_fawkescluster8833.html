Synonyms

  • Guy Fawkes Day

    Guy Fawkes Day is a traditional British name for the November 5 festival, while Guy Fawkes Night is the common public term.
  • Bonfire Night
    6.7 0.01/m
    Bonfire Night focuses on community bonfires and fireworks, while Guy Fawkes Night highlights burning an effigy of Guy Fawkes.
  • November 5

    November 5 refers to the calendar date marked by bonfires and fireworks, while Guy Fawkes Night is the named festival celebrating the plot's failure.
