nonutilitarian

adjective
not utilitarian
8.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.juː.tɪl.ə.ˈter.iː.ən/ /; / /nɑːn.juː.tə.ˈter.iː.ən/ (AmE), /nɒn.juː.tɪl.ɪ.ˈteə.ri.ən/ /; / /nɒn.juː.tɪl.ə.ˈteə.ri.ən/ (BrE)
not intended for practical use or utility; valued for other reasons such as beauty or meaning

Examples

Nonutilitarian art
