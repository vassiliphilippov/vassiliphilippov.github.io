locale

noun
place where an event occurs
3.9
Pronunciation: / loʊˈkæl / (AmE), / ləʊˈkɑːl / (BrE)
a place or setting where an event or activity occurs, especially the scene of a particular event or story

Examples

The locale of event
Many mystery novels use a dark, gloomy manor as the main locale.
The remote mountain village served as the locale for their annual festival.
We need to choose a suitable locale for the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: setting - the place or surroundings where something is positioned or where an event takes place
