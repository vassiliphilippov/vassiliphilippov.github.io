harquebus

noun
obsolete long-barreled firearm
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrk(w)ɪˌbəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːkwɪbəs / (BrE)
an early firearm with a long barrel, used in the 15th to 17th centuries; now obsolete

Examples

A harquebus firearm

The soldiers lined up with their harquebus ready for the command to fire.
