minority leader

noun
leader of the minority party in a legislature
Pronunciation: / məˈnɔrəti ˈliːdər / (AmE), / maɪˈnɒrəti ˈliːdə / (BrE)
the leader of the political party with the second largest number of seats in a legislative body

Examples

The minority leader speaks
After the election, she was appointed as the minority leader of the House of Representatives.
The minority leader criticized the new bill during yesterday's parliamentary session.
The minority leader met with the majority leader to discuss a possible compromise.
