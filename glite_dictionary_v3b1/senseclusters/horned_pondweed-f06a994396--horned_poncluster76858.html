horned pondweed

noun
an aquatic plant found in ponds and slow waters
7.0 8.0
Pronunciation: /hɔːrnd ˈpɑːnd.wiːd/ /; / /hɑːrnd ˈpɑːnd.wiːd/ (AmE), /hɔːnd ˈpɒnd.wiːd/ (BrE)
an aquatic plant of the genus Zannichellia, commonly found in still or slow-moving fresh or brackish water and used to oxygenate ponds and aquaria

Examples

Horned pondweed plant
Horned pondweed provides excellent habitat for aquatic insects and larvae in small lakes.
Many aquarium owners add horned pondweed to help oxygenate the water naturally.
The shallow edges of the river were filled with horned pondweed and other submerged plants.
