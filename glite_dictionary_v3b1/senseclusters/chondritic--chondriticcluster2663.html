chondritic

adjective
having a structure like chondrites
6.6
Pronunciation: / kɑnˈdrɪtɪk / (AmE), / kɒnˈdrɪtɪk / (BrE)
having a granular structure like that of chondrites, especially used of certain meteorites

Examples

Chondritic meteorites
Most primitive meteorites are classified as chondritic due to their characteristic granular composition.
Researchers noted that the chondritic material provided clues about the early solar system.
The scientist examined the chondritic texture under a microscope to study the origins of the meteorite.
