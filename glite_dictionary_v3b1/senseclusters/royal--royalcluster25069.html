royal

adjective
of the rank of a monarch
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl /; / ˈrɔɪəl / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl / (BrE)
relating to, characteristic of, or suitable for a king, queen, or other monarch; belonging to royalty
