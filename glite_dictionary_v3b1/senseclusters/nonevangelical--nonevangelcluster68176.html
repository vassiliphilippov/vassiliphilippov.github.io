nonevangelical

noun
non-evangelical person
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪˌvænʤəˈlɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪˌvænʤɪˈlɪk / (BrE)
a person who does not identify with or belong to evangelical Christianity

Examples

A nonevangelical believer
As a nonevangelical, she felt comfortable exploring different Christian traditions.
The conference welcomed both evangelical and nonevangelical participants from around the world.
He described himself as a nonevangelical but still attended church regularly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evangelical - a person who belongs to or supports evangelical Christianity
