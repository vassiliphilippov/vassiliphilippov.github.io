uncompetitively

adverb
without competitiveness
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛtətɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that lacks competition or is not able to compete effectively with others

Examples

Priced uncompetitively
The company priced its products uncompetitively and lost market share.
If you bid uncompetitively, you are unlikely to win the contract.
The team played uncompetitively and was quickly eliminated from the tournament.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitively - in a way that is able to compete successfully
