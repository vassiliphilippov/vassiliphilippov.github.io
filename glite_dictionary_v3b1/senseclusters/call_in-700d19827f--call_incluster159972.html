call in

verb
make a brief visit
4.1
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɪn / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪn / (BrE)
to pay a short visit to someone, often without prior arrangement

Examples

To call in on a friend
I might call in on my way home from work if I have time.
She loves it when friends call in unexpectedly for a chat and a cup of tea.
The doctor decided to call in and check on his elderly patient yesterday.
