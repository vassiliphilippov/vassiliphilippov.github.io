Synonyms

  • disband
    4.3 3/m
    disband ends a group's existence and is used for organizations, while dissolve sounds more formal and is common for agreements.
  • break up
    3.4 5/m
    break up means making a group or meeting separate and can be informal, while dissolve is a formal ending of organizations or agreements.
