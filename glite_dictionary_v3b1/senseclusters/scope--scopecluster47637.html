scope

noun
extent or range of something's influence
4.8
Pronunciation: / skoʊp / (AmE), / skəʊp / (BrE)
the extent or range covered by an activity, subject, or authority; the area within which something has effect or influence

Examples

Scope of the project
The scope of her research includes both ancient and modern history.
The committee debated whether the project fell outside their scope.
These rules are only valid within the scope of this agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambit - the scope, extent, or bounds of something
  • synonym: extent - the range to which something spreads or extends
  • antonym: limit - a point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass
  • synonym: range - the extent covered between limits; the set of limits of action or influence
