autonomously

adverb
independently
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɔˈtɑːnəməsli / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɒnəməsli / (BrE)
in a way that is independent and not controlled by others

Examples

To autonomously operate devices
The drone can fly autonomously for several hours without human intervention.
After years of training, she managed to work autonomously on complex projects.
Some vehicles are now able to drive autonomously in certain traffic conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependently - in a way that relies on others
  • synonym: independently - without help or control from others
