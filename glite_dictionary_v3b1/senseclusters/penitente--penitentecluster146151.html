penitente

noun
spike of snow or ice
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnteɪ / (AmE), / ˌpɛnɪˈtɛnteɪ / (BrE)
a tall, thin spike of hardened snow or ice formed at high altitudes, especially in the Andes

Examples

Andes penitente formation
The mountaineers had to navigate carefully through the field of penitentes on the high-altitude glacier.
Sunlight and wind shape the unique penitentes that cover the slopes in the Andes.
Photographers are fascinated by the dramatic appearance of penitentes rising from the snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serac - a block or column of glacial ice
