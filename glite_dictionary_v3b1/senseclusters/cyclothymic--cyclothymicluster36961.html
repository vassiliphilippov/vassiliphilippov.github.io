cyclothymic

adjective
relating to cyclothymia (mood disorder)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪ.kləˈθaɪ.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌɪ.kləˈθʌɪ.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or exhibiting cyclothymia, a mood disorder marked by alternating periods of mild depression and hypomania.

Examples

cyclothymic mood swings
Her cyclothymic temperament made her moods unpredictable but rarely severe.
Patients with a cyclothymic disorder may not recognize the alternating mood swings as unusual.
The psychiatrist identified his symptoms as consistent with a cyclothymic personality.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: bipolar - relating to or having bipolar disorder, featuring extreme mood swings
  • synonym: cyclothymia - a mood disorder characterized by alternating periods of mild depression and hypomania
  • degree lower: depressive - experiencing or relating to depression
