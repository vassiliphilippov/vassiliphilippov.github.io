musk

noun
aromatic deer secretion used in perfume
3.4 6.9
Pronunciation: / mʌsk / (AmE), / mʌsk / (BrE)
a strong-smelling substance from certain animals (especially the musk deer) or plants, used in perfumes and sometimes medicine

Examples

Deer musk
Harvesting musk from deer is controversial due to animal welfare concerns.
The rare and expensive musk is often used in luxury perfumes.
The scent of musk can linger on fabrics for days.
In the past, hunters would extract musk from male deer for use in medicine and fragrance.
Some perfumes still attempt to recreate the warm scent of natural musk.
The musk from the deer was highly prized by perfumers in ancient times.
The new cologne features a subtle musk that lingers for hours.
Some flowers produce a natural musk to attract pollinators.
He recognized the distinctive musk in the old leather-bound books.

  • synonym: fragrance - a pleasant, sweet smell
  • antonym: odor - a smell, especially an unpleasant one
  • synonym: perfume - a fragrant liquid typically made from essential oils and aroma compounds, used to give a pleasant scent to the body
