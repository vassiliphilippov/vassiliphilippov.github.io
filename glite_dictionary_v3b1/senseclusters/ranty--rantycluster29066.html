ranty

adjective
loud and complaining
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrænti / (AmE), / ˈrænti / (BrE)
speaking or writing in a loud, angry, or complaining manner

Examples

Ranty emails
Her ranty emails made it difficult for her colleagues to respond professionally.
The comedian's ranty style divided the audience between laughter and discomfort.
After a long day, he became quite ranty about the smallest inconveniences.

Related Terms
