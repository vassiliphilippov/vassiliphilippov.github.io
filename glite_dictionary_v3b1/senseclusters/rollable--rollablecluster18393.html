rollable

adjective
able to be rolled
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊləbəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləbəl / (BrE)
able to be rolled or moved by turning over and over, especially on wheels or a surface

Examples

Rollable office chair
The new office chairs are rollable, making it easy to move around the workspace.
She bought a rollable suitcase to make traveling through airports more convenient.
The whiteboard is rollable so it can be used in different classrooms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immobile - not able to be moved
  • synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
