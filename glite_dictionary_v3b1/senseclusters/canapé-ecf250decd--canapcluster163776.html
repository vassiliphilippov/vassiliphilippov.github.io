canapé

noun
small decorative sofa
Pronunciation: / ˈkænəˌpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkænəpeɪ / (BrE)
a small, upholstered sofa or couch, often decorative and used for seating in a living room or parlor

Examples

Velvet canapé
The elegant canapé in the corner was upholstered in deep blue velvet.
Guests gathered around the antique canapé during the afternoon tea party.
She bought a vintage canapé to add a touch of style to her living room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: armchair - a comfortable chair with side supports for a person's arms
  • synonym: couch - a comfortable piece of furniture for sitting or lying on
  • synonym: sofa - a long, upholstered seat with a back and arms
