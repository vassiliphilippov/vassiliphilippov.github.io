thyrohyoid

adjective
of thyroid and hyoid
Pronunciation: / ˌθaɪroʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌθaɪrəʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to the thyroid cartilage and the hyoid bone in the neck

Examples

Thyrohyoid membrane
The thyrohyoid membrane connects the thyroid cartilage to the hyoid bone.
Damage to the thyrohyoid muscle can affect swallowing and speech.
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully identified the thyrohyoid structures in the neck.
