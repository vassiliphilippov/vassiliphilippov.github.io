telemarketer

noun
telephone sales caller
3.6
Pronunciation: /te.lə.ˈmɑːr.kɪ.tər/ (AmE), /te.lə.ˈmɑː.kɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person or automated system that tries to sell products or services by making phone calls to people

Examples

A telemarketer calls
A telemarketer called me last night to offer a new credit card.
Many people hang up immediately when they realize a telemarketer is on the line.
The company uses an automated telemarketer to reach thousands of potential customers each day.
A telemarketer called me yesterday to offer a special deal on internet service.
Many people hang up when a telemarketer tries to sell them something over the phone.
The company hired a new telemarketer to increase their sales by reaching out to more customers.
A telemarketer called me during dinner to offer a new credit card.
Many telemarketers work from call centers and follow prepared scripts.
She found a job as a telemarketer selling magazine subscriptions over the phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold caller - a person who makes unsolicited calls to sell products or services
  • antonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services
  • synonym: salesperson - someone whose job is to sell products or services
