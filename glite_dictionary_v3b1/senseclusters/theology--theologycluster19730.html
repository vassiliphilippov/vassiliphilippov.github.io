theology

noun
system of religious beliefs
1.0
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / θiˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A particular system or school of religious beliefs and teachings.

Examples

Catholic theology

Contemporary Jewish theology addresses modern ethical questions.
