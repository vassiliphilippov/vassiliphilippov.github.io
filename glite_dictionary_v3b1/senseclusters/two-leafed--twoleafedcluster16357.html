two-leafed

adjective
having two leaves
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtuˌliːft / (AmE), / ˈtuːˌliːft / (BrE)
Having two leaves; bearing exactly two leaves.

Examples

Two-leafed seedling
Many seedlings are identified in the early stages by their two-leafed appearance.
The two-leafed plant sprouted quickly in the spring sunlight.
The botanist examined the rare two-leafed specimen found in the forest.
