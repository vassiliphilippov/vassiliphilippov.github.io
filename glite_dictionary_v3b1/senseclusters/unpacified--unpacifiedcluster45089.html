unpacified

adjective
not calmed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæsɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpæsɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not calmed, subdued, or brought to a peaceful state

Examples

An unpacified region
The region remained unpacified despite several peace efforts.
After hours of negotiation, the crowd was still unpacified and refused to leave.
The child's cries were unpacified even after being comforted by her parents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pacified - calmed or brought to peace
  • synonym: unsettled - not calm or stable
