blind stitching

noun
barely visible sewing technique
3.9
Pronunciation: / blaɪnd ˈstɪtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / blaɪnd ˈstɪtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of sewing in which the stitches are not easily seen on the finished surface

Examples

Blind stitching technique
He learned the technique of blind stitching in his advanced sewing class.
The tailor used blind stitching to hem the trousers, so no threads were visible.
You can achieve a more professional finish by using blind stitching on curtains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invisible stitching - a sewing technique where stitches are hidden from view
