ductal

adjective
of a duct or tube
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌktl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdʌktl̩ / (BrE)
relating to a duct or tube in the body or another structure

Examples

Ductal tissue
The doctor explained that the tumor was located in the ductal tissue of the pancreas.
Researchers are studying ductal changes that occur in certain liver diseases.
A ductal carcinoma develops in the milk ducts of the breast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tubular - having the form or characteristics of a tube
