sugar corn

noun
a sweet variety of corn for eating
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsʊɡɚ kɔrn / (AmE), / ˈsʊɡə kɔːn / (BrE)
a variety of corn developed to produce sweet, edible young ears, typically eaten as a vegetable

Examples

Grilled sugar corn
Grilled sugar corn is a popular summer snack at fairs and festivals.
The farmer harvested the sugar corn early in the morning for the local market.
You can steam or boil sugar corn to bring out its natural sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweet corn - a type of corn with high sugar content eaten as a vegetable
