mercy killing

noun
the painless killing to relieve suffering
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜr.si ˌkɪ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜː.si ˌkɪ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of killing someone painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness

Examples

Mercy killing act
Debates about whether mercy killing should be legalized are ongoing around the world.
Some view mercy killing as an act of compassion, while others consider it morally unacceptable.
The doctor was accused of performing a mercy killing on his terminally ill patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: euthanasia - the painless killing of a patient to relieve suffering
