rearticulation

noun
saying again clearly
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of pronouncing or expressing a sound, word, or phrase again, often to make it clearer or correct a mistake

Examples

Clear rearticulation
After the student mispronounced the word, the teacher offered a slow rearticulation to help him learn.
The rearticulation of the instructions made the process much easier for everyone to follow.
Sometimes a simple rearticulation can resolve confusion during a conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarification - the act of making something clearer or easier to understand
  • synonym: repetition - the act of doing or saying something again
