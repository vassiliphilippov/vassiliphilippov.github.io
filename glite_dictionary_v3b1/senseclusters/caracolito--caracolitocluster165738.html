caracolito

noun
Costa Rican tree used for cabinetry
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrəkoʊˈlitoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkærəkəʊˈliːtəʊ / (BrE)
a large Costa Rican tree with light-colored wood, used for cabinetry and similar in structure to the African lepidobotrys

Examples

The caracolito tree
Botanists note the similarities between the caracolito and the African lepidobotrys in their fruit and leaves.
Efforts to conserve the caracolito have increased due to its declining numbers in Costa Rican forests.
The wood from caracolito is highly valued for making fine furniture.
