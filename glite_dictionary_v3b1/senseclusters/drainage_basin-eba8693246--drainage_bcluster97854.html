drainage basin

noun
area draining into a river or water body
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪnɪdʒ ˈbeɪsən / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnɪdʒ ˈbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
the entire area of land from which rainfall and other water drains into a particular river, stream, or body of water

Examples

The drainage basin area
Flood management strategies must consider the entire drainage basin.
Many cities rely on the local drainage basin for their freshwater supply.
The Amazon drainage basin covers a vast area of South America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catchment area - the area from which rainfall flows into a river, lake, or reservoir
  • synonym: watershed - the land area that channels rainfall to rivers and streams
