unmasker

noun
tool to reveal data
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæskər / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːskə / (BrE)
a tool or computer program that reveals original information by reversing data masking or anonymization

Examples

Data unmasker
The cybersecurity team used an unmasker to identify the original users behind the encrypted records.
An advanced unmasker can sometimes defeat even strong data protection methods.
After running the unmasker, the researchers could see the true contents of the hidden files.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deanonymizer - a tool that removes anonymity from data
  • antonym: masker - a tool that hides or anonymizes data
