camelid

noun
camel family mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməlɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæməlɪd / (BrE)
a mammal belonging to the biological family that includes camels, llamas, alpacas, vicuñas, and guanacos

Examples

A camelid animal
The camelid family includes animals like camels, llamas, and alpacas.
Scientists studied the unique blood cells of a camelid to understand its adaptation to dry climates.
Farmers in the Andes often raise camelids such as alpacas for their wool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camel - a large, humped mammal adapted to desert life
  • synonym: llama - a domesticated South American mammal related to the camel
