crunk

noun
energetic hip-hop style
Pronunciation: / krʌŋk / (AmE), / krʌŋk / (BrE)
a style of hip-hop music from the southern United States featuring heavy beats, loud bass, shouted vocals, and energetic party lyrics

Examples

Crunk music
The club erupted with energy when the DJ played a classic crunk anthem.
Many artists from Atlanta helped popularize crunk in the early 2000s.
Fans love the loud bass and shouted vocals that define crunk music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hip-hop - a genre of popular music developed in the United States featuring rhythmic speech and beats
  • synonym: trap - a subgenre of hip-hop music with aggressive lyrics and heavy bass
