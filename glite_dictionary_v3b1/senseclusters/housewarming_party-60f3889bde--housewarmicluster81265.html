housewarming party

noun
party for moving into a new home
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌwɔr.mɪŋ ˈpɑr.ti / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌwɔː.mɪŋ ˈpɑː.ti / (BrE)
a party held to celebrate moving into a new home, often attended by friends and family

Examples

A housewarming party celebration
She received many thoughtful gifts at her housewarming party.
They hosted a casual housewarming party after settling into their new apartment.
We invited all our neighbors to our housewarming party last weekend.
