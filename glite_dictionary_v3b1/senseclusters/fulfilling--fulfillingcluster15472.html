fulfilling

adjective
satisfying and meaningful
Pronunciation: / fʊlˈfɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / fʊlˈfɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
making you feel happy and satisfied because you are doing something meaningful or worthwhile

Examples

A fulfilling job
She finds her volunteer work very fulfilling because she helps others every day.
Many people search for a fulfilling career that gives them a sense of purpose.
Traveling to new countries can be a fulfilling experience for those who love adventure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disappointing - not as good as expected; causing unhappiness
  • synonym: rewarding - giving satisfaction or pleasure as a result of achievement
  • synonym: satisfying - giving a feeling of pleasure or contentment
