zoom in

verb
join a Zoom meeting
Pronunciation: / zum ɪn / (AmE), / zuːm ɪn / (BrE)
to join or attend a meeting using the Zoom video‑conferencing platform (often used with a time or place: 'zoom in at 10').

Examples

To zoom in to a meeting
I'll zoom in halfway through the meeting because I have a dentist appointment this morning.
She zoomed in from home to give her presentation to the international team.
They suggested zooming in as an option for colleagues who could not travel to the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attend - to be present at a meeting, event, or gathering
  • synonym: join - to become a participant in a meeting, group, or event
