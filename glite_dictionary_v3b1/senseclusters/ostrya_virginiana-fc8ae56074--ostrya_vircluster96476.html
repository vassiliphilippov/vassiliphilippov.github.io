Ostrya virginiana

noun
North American hop hornbeam tree species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstriə vɜrˌdʒɪniˈeɪnə / (AmE), / ˈɒstriə vɜːdʒɪnɪˈɑːnə / (BrE)
A medium-sized deciduous tree native to eastern North America, also known as the American hophornbeam, with hard wood and hop-like fruit clusters.
