strength

noun
quality of being strong
1.6
Pronunciation: / strɛŋkθ / (AmE), / strɛŋθ / (BrE)
the quality or state of being physically or mentally strong; having great power or ability

Examples

Physical strength
After the surgery, he gradually regained his strength and was able to walk again.
Mental strength is just as important as physical power in sports.
She admired his strength in facing difficult challenges.
He lifted the heavy box with surprising strength.
The strength of the wind knocked over several trees during the storm.
The bridge was designed to withstand enormous strength from water currents.
