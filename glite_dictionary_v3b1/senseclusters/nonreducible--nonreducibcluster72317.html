nonreducible

adjective
cannot be simplified
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrɪˈduːsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈdjuːsəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be explained, described, or accounted for in terms of something more basic or different, especially in philosophy or theory

Examples

Nonreducible concept
Some scientists believe that consciousness is nonreducible to physical processes alone.
The theory proposes that certain social phenomena are nonreducible to individual actions.
He argued that moral values are nonreducible and cannot be fully explained by science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreducible - impossible to make simpler or smaller
  • antonym: reducible - able to be made simpler or explained in terms of something else
