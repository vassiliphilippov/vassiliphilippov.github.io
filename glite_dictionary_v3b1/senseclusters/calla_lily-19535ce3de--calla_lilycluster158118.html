calla lily

noun
ornamental plant with white spathe and yellow spadix
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkælə ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ˈkælə ˌlɪli / (BrE)
A South African plant with a large, showy white spathe and yellow spadix, widely grown as an ornamental.

Examples

A calla lily flower
