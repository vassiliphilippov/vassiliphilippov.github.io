kickstart

noun
initial boost
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪk.stɑːrt/ (AmE), /ˈkɪk.stɑːt/ (BrE)

Examples

A kickstart for growth
The new grant acted as a kickstart for the community art project.
A strong marketing campaign gave the product the kickstart it needed to succeed.
Sometimes a small investment can provide the kickstart a business needs to grow.
