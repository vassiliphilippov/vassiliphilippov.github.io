bent

noun
natural talent or inclination
7.4
Pronunciation: / bɛnt / (AmE), / bɛnt / (BrE)
a natural talent, inclination, or tendency for a particular activity or subject

Examples

Bent for music
Even as a child, he showed a bent for music and the arts.
She has a bent for solving difficult mathematical problems.
The company is looking for employees with a bent for innovation and creativity.
His artistic bent was apparent even in childhood, when he loved to draw and paint.
She has a scientific bent and spends her free time reading about physics.
With a natural bent for leadership, she quickly rose to a management position.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aversion - a strong dislike or disinclination
  • synonym: inclination - a tendency to do something or feel a certain way
  • synonym: knack - a special skill or talent for doing something easily
  • synonym: predilection - a preference or special liking for something
