dribbler

noun
player advancing ball by dribbling
5.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪb.lər/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪb.lə/ (BrE)
a player who moves the ball by dribbling (repeatedly bouncing or controlling it), especially in basketball or soccer; also, rarely, a person who lets saliva or liquid drip from their mouth

Examples

A dribbler in basketball
As a young soccer player, he developed into a skillful dribbler.
The coach praised her for being the fastest dribbler on the team.
The point guard was an excellent dribbler who could move past any defender.
Among the basketball team, Jordan is the best dribbler with the ball.
Her baby is quite a dribbler and always needs to wear a bib.
The young soccer player was an excellent dribbler and could get past any defender.
