evoker

noun
one who brings forth
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvoʊkər / (AmE), / ɪˈvəʊkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings something into mind or existence, especially feelings, memories, or images

Examples

A strong evoker of memories
The painting was a powerful evoker of childhood memories.
Music can be a strong evoker of emotions and past experiences.
As an evoker of nostalgia, the old photograph brought tears to her eyes.

  • synonym: summoner - a person who calls forth something or someone
