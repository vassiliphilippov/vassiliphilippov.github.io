thalmencephalon

noun
part of the forebrain in the brain
9.9
Pronunciation: /θæl.mɛn.ˈsef.ə.lən/ (AmE), /θæl.mɛn.ˈsef.ə.lɒn/ (BrE)
the posterior part of the forebrain that connects the cerebral hemispheres with the midbrain (mesencephalon)

Examples

During embryonic development, the thalmencephalon differentiates into several important brain structures.
The thalmencephalon plays a crucial role in relaying sensory information to the cerebral cortex.
