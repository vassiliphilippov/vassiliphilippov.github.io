Harkat-ul-Mujahidin

proper noun
militant group active in Pakistan and Kashmir
Pronunciation: / hɑːrˌkɑːt ʊl muːˈdʒɑːhɪdɪn / (AmE), / hɑːˌkæt ʊl mjuːˈdʒɑːhɪdiːn / (BrE)
an Islamic militant group founded in Pakistan, involved in insurgency operations in Afghanistan during the 1980s and currently designated as a terrorist organization active mainly in Kashmir
