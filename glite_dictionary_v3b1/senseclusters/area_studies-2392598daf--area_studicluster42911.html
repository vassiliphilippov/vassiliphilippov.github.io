area studies

noun
interdisciplinary field on specific regions
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriə ˌstʌdiz / (AmE), / ˈeəriə ˌstʌdiz / (BrE)
An interdisciplinary academic field focusing on the study of a particular geographical, national, or cultural region.

Examples

Area studies program
