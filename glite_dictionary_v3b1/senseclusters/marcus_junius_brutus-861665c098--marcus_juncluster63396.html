Marcus Junius Brutus

proper noun
Roman assassin of Julius Caesar
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrkəs ˈdʒuːniəs ˈbruːtəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkəs ˈdʒuːnɪəs ˈbruːtəs / (BrE)
(c. 85–42 BC) A Roman statesman and one of Julius Caesar's assassins, noted for his role in the conspiracy with Cassius to kill Caesar.
