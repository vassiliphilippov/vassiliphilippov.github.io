music box

noun
mechanical instrument that plays music
3.1 0.87/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêz…™k b…ëÀêks / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêz…™k b…íks / (BrE)
A small mechanical instrument that plays a tune when a key is wound, using pins on a revolving cylinder to pluck the tuned teeth of a metal comb.

Examples

An antique music box
He collects antique music boxes from different countries.
My grandmother gave me a beautiful music box that plays a gentle melody when opened.
The children listened in awe as the music box played its nostalgic tune.
