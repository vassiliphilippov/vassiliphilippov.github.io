Rohingya

noun
Indo-Aryan language
9.6
Pronunciation: / roʊˈhɪŋjə / (AmE), / rəʊˈhɪŋjə / (BrE)
the Indo-Aryan language spoken by the Rohingya people, mainly in Myanmar and Bangladesh

Examples

Rohingya language
Many children in the refugee camp speak Rohingya as their first language.
She is fluent in both Rohingya and Burmese, which helps her communicate with the community.
The poem was recited in Rohingya during the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bengali - an Indo-Aryan language spoken in Bangladesh and eastern India
