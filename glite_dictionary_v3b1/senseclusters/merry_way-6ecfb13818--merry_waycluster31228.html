merry way

noun
proceeding cheerfully or contentedly
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri weɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛri weɪ / (BrE)
to proceed or continue in a cheerful or contented manner, usually after completing something or moving on from an interaction

Examples

Go on merry way
After dropping off the package, she went on her merry way to the next stop.
He finished his meal and continued on his merry way without a care.
They chatted for a few minutes, then each went their separate and merry way.
