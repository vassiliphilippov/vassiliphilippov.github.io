umbilicate

adjective
navel-shaped; with a central depression
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɪlɪkeɪt / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɪlɪkeɪt / (BrE)
Having a central depression resembling a navel; navel-shaped.

Examples

Umbilicate ancient coins
A few ancient coins were umbilicate, bearing a small hollow at the center.
Some fungi are described as umbilicate due to the shape of their cap.
The shell is umbilicate, showing a distinct central depression.
