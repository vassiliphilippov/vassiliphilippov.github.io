Sukur

noun
a Chadic language of Nigeria
Pronunciation: / suˈkʊr / (AmE), / suːˈkʊə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, especially in the Mandara mountains of Nigeria, notably in Adamawa State, Michika LGA

Examples

Sukur language
Efforts are being made to preserve Sukur as fewer young people learn the language.
Researchers documented traditional stories passed down in Sukur.
The Sukur language is spoken by a small community in northeastern Nigeria.
