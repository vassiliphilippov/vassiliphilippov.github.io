tasseling

noun
corn flowering stage
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtæsəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæsəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the stage in the growth of corn when tassels appear at the top of the plant, marking the start of pollination

Examples

Corn tasseling stage
Farmers pay close attention to the tasseling of their corn to ensure a healthy crop.
The field was a sea of green stalks, each one tasseling under the summer sun.
Proper irrigation during tasseling can significantly improve corn yields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollination - the transfer of pollen to enable fertilization in plants
  • degree higher: silking - the stage when corn plants produce silks for pollination
