kilderkin

noun
old British unit (18 Imperial gallons)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪldərkɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪldəkɪn / (BrE)
an old British unit of capacity equal to 18 Imperial gallons, now obsolete

Examples

A kilderkin of beer
A kilderkin was larger than a firkin but smaller than a barrel.
Historical records showed taxes levied per kilderkin of beer produced.
The brewery once supplied ale in kilderkins to local taverns.
