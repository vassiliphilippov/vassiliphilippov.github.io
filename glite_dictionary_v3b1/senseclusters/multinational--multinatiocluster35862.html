multinational

adjective
involving several countries or nationalities
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənl / (BrE)
involving, operating in, or relating to several countries or nationalities, especially of companies or organizations

Examples

Multinational company
His experience working with multinational teams has given him a global perspective.
Many multinational companies have offices on several continents.
The organization took a multinational approach to solving the crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic - relating to or originating within one country
  • synonym: international - existing, occurring, or carried on between two or more nations
