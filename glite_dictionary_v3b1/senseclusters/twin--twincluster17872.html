twin

noun
duplicate or exact copy
Pronunciation: / twɪn / (AmE), / twɪn / (BrE)
a thing that is an exact copy or very similar duplicate of another, including a digital replica

Examples

Twin copy
Each astronaut was given a twin of the access key for safety purposes.
She keeps a twin of her favorite novel in case one gets damaged.
The painting is so well forged that it looks like the twin of the original.
The company built a twin of the jet engine to simulate performance under extreme conditions.
Engineers feed live sensor data into the twin to predict when maintenance will be required.
City planners used a digital twin of the neighbourhood to test traffic and emergency response scenarios.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital twin - a detailed digital representation of a physical asset, system, or process used for simulation and analysis
  • synonym: duplicate - An exact copy of something
  • synonym: simulation model - a computational model used to simulate the behaviour of a system under different conditions
