jump to conclusions

verb
make a hasty judgment
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /dʒʌmp tə kən.ˈkluː.ʒənz/ /; / /dʒʌmp tuː kən.ˈkluː.ʒənz/ (AmE), /dʒʌmp tə kən.ˈkluː.ʒənz/ /; / /dʒʌmp tuː kən.ˈkluː.ʒənz/ (BrE)

Examples

To jump to conclusions quickly
Before you jump to conclusions, let's review all the evidence thoroughly.
She regretted jumping to conclusions after learning the real story.
They often jump to conclusions and later find out they were completely wrong.
