finisher

noun
baseball relief pitcher who closes games
2.8 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃər / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃə / (BrE)
(in baseball) a relief pitcher who is brought in to protect a lead in the last inning or two of the game.
