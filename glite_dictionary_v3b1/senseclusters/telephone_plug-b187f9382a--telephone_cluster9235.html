telephone plug

noun
device for connecting telephones
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn plʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈtel.ɪ.fəʊn plʌɡ / (BrE)
a device that connects a telephone to a telephone line or network by fitting into a socket

Examples

A telephone plug connector
Make sure the telephone plug is firmly inserted to restore your landline connection.
She replaced the old telephone plug with a new one to fix the static noise.
Without a telephone plug, the phone cannot connect to the network.
