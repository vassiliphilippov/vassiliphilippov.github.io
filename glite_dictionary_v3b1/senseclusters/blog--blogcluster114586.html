blog

verb
write or edit an online journal
Pronunciation: / blɑg / (AmE), / blɒg / (BrE)
to write, read, or edit entries in a shared online journal or website, typically reflecting personal opinions, experiences, or information

Examples

To blog about life
He started blogging in college and quickly gained a large following.
Many people blog as a way to express their opinions and connect with others.
She plans to blog about her trip to Japan every day.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: microblog - to post short updates or content to a microblogging platform (e.g. Twitter)
  • synonym: weblog - a website on which someone writes regularly about topics that interest them
