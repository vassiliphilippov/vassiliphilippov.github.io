gargoyle

verb
to decorate with grotesques
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrˌɡɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːɡɔɪl / (BrE)
to form into or decorate with grotesque figures resembling a gargoyle.

Examples

To gargoyle a building
The cathedral was gargoyled with fantastic stone creatures along its roofline.
They gargoyled the fountain to give it a medieval appearance.
Many historic buildings have been gargoyled to create a dramatic effect.
