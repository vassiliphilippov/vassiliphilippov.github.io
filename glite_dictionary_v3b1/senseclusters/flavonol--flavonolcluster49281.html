flavonol

noun
plant antioxidant compound
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvəˌnɑl /; / ˈfleɪvəˌnɒl / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvənɒl / (BrE)
a type of plant compound with antioxidant properties, often found in fruits and vegetables and sometimes responsible for yellow or pale colors in plants

Related Terms

  • antonym: anthocyanin - a plant pigment responsible for red, purple, and blue colors
  • synonym: antioxidant - a substance that prevents or slows damage from oxidation
  • synonym: flavonoid - a group of plant compounds including flavonols
