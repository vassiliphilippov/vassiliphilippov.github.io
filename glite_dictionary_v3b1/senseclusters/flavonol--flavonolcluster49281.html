flavonol

noun
plant antioxidant compound
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈflæv.ə.nɑːl/ /; / /ˈfleɪ.və.nɑːl/ (AmE), /ˈflæv.ə.nɒl/ /; / /ˈfleɪ.və.nɒl/ (BrE)
