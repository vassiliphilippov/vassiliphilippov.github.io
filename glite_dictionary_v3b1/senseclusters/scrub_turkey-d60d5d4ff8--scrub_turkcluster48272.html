scrub turkey

noun
wild or less desirable turkey
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskrʌb ˌtɝːki / (AmE), / ˈskrʌb ˌtɜːki / (BrE)
a wild turkey or a turkey considered less desirable, often used to refer to a tough or gamey bird caught in the wild

Examples

A wild scrub turkey
Hunters often encounter at least one scrub turkey during the fall season.
The meat from a scrub turkey can be a bit tough compared to farm-raised birds.
We roasted a scrub turkey for Thanksgiving dinner.
