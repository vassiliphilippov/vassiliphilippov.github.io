ecofeminist

adjective
ecology and feminism
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌiːkoʊˈfɛmənɪst / (AmE), / ˌiːkəʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
relating to the combination of ecological and feminist ideas or principles, especially in social or political contexts

Examples

Ecofeminist ideas
The ecofeminist movement highlights the connection between gender equality and environmental protection.
Her ecofeminist perspective influenced the way she approached community gardening projects.
Many ecofeminist writers argue that caring for the earth is linked to social justice for women.
