wallower

noun
one who wallows
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːloʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɒləʊə / (BrE)

Examples

He became a wallower in his own sadness after the breakup.
Among the herd, one buffalo stood out as a constant wallower in the riverbank mud.
