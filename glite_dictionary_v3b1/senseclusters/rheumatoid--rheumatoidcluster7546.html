rheumatoid

adjective
relating to rheumatoid arthritis
Pronunciation: / ˈruːməˌtɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈruːmətɔɪd / (BrE)
Relating to, or affected by, rheumatoid arthritis or a form of rheumatism.

Examples

Rheumatoid arthritis
His rheumatoid symptoms worsened during the cold winter months.
Patients with rheumatoid conditions often experience joint pain and swelling.
The doctor diagnosed her with rheumatoid arthritis after reviewing her symptoms and test results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthritic - Relating to or affected by arthritis
  • synonym: rheumatic - Relating to or suffering from rheumatism
