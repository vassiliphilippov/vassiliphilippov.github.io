pavor nocturnus

noun
sleep disorder with intense fear during sleep
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːvɔr nɑkˈtɜrnəs / (AmE), / ˌpɑːvɔː nɒkˈtɜːnəs / (BrE)
a sleep disorder characterized by episodes of intense fear, screaming, and panic occurring during non-REM sleep, far more severe than a typical nightmare

Examples

pavor nocturnus episodes
A diagnosis of pavor nocturnus is made when repeated night terrors disrupt normal sleep.
Children experiencing pavor nocturnus often wake up screaming and confused.
Parents were startled by the frequency and severity of their son's pavor nocturnus.
