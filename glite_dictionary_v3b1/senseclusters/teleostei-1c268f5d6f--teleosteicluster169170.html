Teleostei

noun
major group of bony fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛliˈɑstiˌaɪ /; / ˌtɛliˈɑːstiː / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪˈɒstiːˌaɪ /; / ˌtɛliˈɒstiː / (BrE)
