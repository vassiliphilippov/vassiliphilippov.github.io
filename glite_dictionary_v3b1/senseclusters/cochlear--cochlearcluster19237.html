cochlear

adjective
of the cochlea in the inner ear
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊk.li.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒk.li.ə / (BrE)
relating to the cochlea, the spiral-shaped cavity in the inner ear involved in hearing

Examples

Advanced cochlear implants
Advanced cochlear implants have restored hearing to many patients with severe hearing loss.
Scientists study cochlear mechanics to improve treatments for deafness.
The surgeon explained how the cochlear nerve transmits sound signals to the brain.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cochlear implant - an electronic device that compensates for damaged inner ear
