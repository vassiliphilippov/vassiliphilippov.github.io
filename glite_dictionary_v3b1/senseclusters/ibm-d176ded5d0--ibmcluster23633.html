IBM

proper noun
stock ticker symbol
Pronunciation: / aɪ biː ɛm / (AmE), / aɪ biː ɛm / (BrE)
the stock-market ticker symbol for International Business Machines, used to refer to the company's publicly traded shares

Examples

IBM stock
Many investors track the performance of IBM on the New York Stock Exchange.
After the merger announcement, IBM experienced a significant increase in trading volume.
She decided to buy shares of IBM for her long-term investment portfolio.
