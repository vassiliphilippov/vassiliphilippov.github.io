nite

interjection
informal farewell
Pronunciation: / naɪt / (AmE), / naɪt / (BrE)
an informal way to say 'good night' as a farewell, especially in writing or casual speech

Examples

Nite as a goodbye
She texted her friend 'Sweet dreams, nite!' before going to bed.
After the party, everyone said nite and headed home.
He waved and called out 'nite!' as he left the group chat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good night - a polite expression used when parting at night
