Synonyms

  • musical rhythm
    3.2 0.02/m
    musical rhythm sets the pattern of beats in a song, while rhythm names any regular, repeated pattern of sound, movement, or sight.
  • rhythmic pattern
    5.6 0.25/m
    rhythmic pattern describes the arrangement of stressed and unstressed syllables in verse, while rhythm is any repeated pattern of sounds or movement.
  • beat
    1.0 20/m
    beat is the steady pulse you tap with your foot in music, while rhythm is the wider pattern made from many beats.
  • pulsebeat
    4.8 0.01/m
    pulsebeat is the single steady accent or pulse felt in music or poetry, while rhythm is the full repeated pattern.
