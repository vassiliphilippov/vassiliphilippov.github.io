rhythm

noun
the basic rhythmic unit in music
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪð.əm /; / ˈrɪðəm / (AmE), / ˈrɪð.əm /; / ˈrɪðəm / (BrE)
a regular, repeated pattern of sounds, movements, events, or visual elements, especially in music, dance, or design

Examples
