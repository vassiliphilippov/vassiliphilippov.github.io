drama

noun
quality of excitement or intense emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑː.mə /; / ˈdrɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈdrɑː.mə /; / ˈdrɑːmə / (BrE)
excitement, emotional intensity, or a situation that is full of strong feelings or tension
