paradigmatic

adjective
relating to a grammatical paradigm or pattern
Pronunciation: / ˌpær.ə.dɪgˈmæt̬.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpær.ə.dɪgˈmæt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or serving as a grammatical paradigm or model pattern, especially in linguistics.

Examples

Paradigmatic pattern
A paradigmatic change in a language may involve altering its system of grammatical endings.
Students studied the paradigmatic forms of Latin nouns in their grammar class.
The word 'run' follows a paradigmatic pattern of verb inflection in English.
