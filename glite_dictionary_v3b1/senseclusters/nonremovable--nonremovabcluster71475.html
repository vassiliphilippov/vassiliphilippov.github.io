nonremovable

adjective
cannot be removed
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈmuː.və.bəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈmuː.və.bəl / (BrE)
not able to be removed or taken away from its place or position

Examples

A nonremovable battery
The phone's nonremovable battery makes it difficult to replace when it stops working.
Some security tags are designed to be nonremovable to prevent theft.
The sculpture was installed with nonremovable bolts for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to be moved
  • synonym: permanent - lasting or intended to last indefinitely
  • antonym: removable - able to be taken away or detached
