Claytonia

noun
genus of North American succulent herbs
Pronunciation: / kleɪˈtoʊniə / (AmE), / kleɪˈtəʊniə / (BrE)
a genus of mostly North American succulent herbs with white or pink flowers, commonly known as spring beauties
