leptocephalus

noun
larval stage of eels and some fishes
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛptəˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌlɛptəˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
the slender, transparent larval stage of eels and some related fishes

Examples

A leptocephalus larva
A leptocephalus can be difficult to spot due to its transparent body.
Scientists collected samples of leptocephali to study their migration patterns.
The leptocephalus of the eel drifts in ocean currents for many months before becoming an adult.
