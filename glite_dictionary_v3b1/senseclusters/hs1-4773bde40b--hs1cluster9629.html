HS1

noun
herpes virus causing oral herpes
4.8
Pronunciation: / eɪtʃ ɛs wʌn / (AmE), / eɪtʃ ɛs wʌn / (BrE)
a type of herpes virus (herpes simplex virus type 1) that is a common cause of oral herpes, such as cold sores

Examples

HS1 virus
HS1 is responsible for most cases of cold sores around the mouth.
A doctor explained the differences between HS1 and other herpes viruses.
Testing can confirm whether a patient carries HS1 or another type of herpes virus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: HSV-1 - herpes simplex virus type 1, causes oral herpes
  • degree lower: HSV-2 - herpes simplex virus type 2, often causes genital herpes
