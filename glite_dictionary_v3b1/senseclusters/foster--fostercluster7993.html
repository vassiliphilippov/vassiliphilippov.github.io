foster

verb
to encourage development or growth
3.7 22/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆfÉ‘stÉ™r / (AmE), / ËˆfÉ’stÉ™ / (BrE)
to encourage or promote the development or growth of something

Examples

To foster growth
Open communication helps foster trust within a team.
Parents should foster a love of reading in their children.
Strong leadership can foster innovation in the workplace.
Regular reading helps to foster a lifelong love of learning.
Teachers can foster creativity in their students by encouraging open-ended projects.
Their organization works to foster cooperation among local businesses.

  • nurture

  • cultivate
    4.1 9.81/m

  • develop
