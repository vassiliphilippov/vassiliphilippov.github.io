misadministration

noun
bad management
8.3
Pronunciation: /mɪs.əd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /mɪs.əd.mɪn.ɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of managing or administering something in a poor, inefficient, or improper way

Examples

Misadministration of funds
The hospital faced criticism for the misadministration of patient records.
Years of misadministration led to the decline of the once-successful organization.
The investigation revealed widespread misadministration in the distribution of public funds.
