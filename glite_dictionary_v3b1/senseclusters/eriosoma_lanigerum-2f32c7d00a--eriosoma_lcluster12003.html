Eriosoma lanigerum

noun
species of aphid pest of apple trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrioʊˈsoʊmə læˈnɪdʒərəm / (AmE), / ˌɪəriˈəʊsəmə læˈnɪdʒərəm / (BrE)
A species of aphid that feeds on the bark and roots of apple and other trees, often causing damage to orchards.

Examples

Eriosoma lanigerum aphid
Biological control methods have been developed to manage Eriosoma lanigerum populations.
Many orchardists struggle with infestations of Eriosoma lanigerum on their apple trees.
The galls on the twigs were caused by a colony of Eriosoma lanigerum.
