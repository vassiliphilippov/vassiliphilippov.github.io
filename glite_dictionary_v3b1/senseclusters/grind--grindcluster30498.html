grind

verb
make a grating sound by rubbing
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
to make a grating or grinding sound by rubbing objects together, especially in a repetitive or harsh way

Examples

To grind metal parts
As the boulders shifted, they ground against each other, producing an ominous rumble.
She would sometimes grind her teeth in her sleep, disturbing her partner.
The door hinges began to grind as he pushed it open, echoing through the hallway.
