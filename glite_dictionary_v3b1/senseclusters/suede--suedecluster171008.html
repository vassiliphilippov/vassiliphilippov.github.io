suede

verb
to make velvety
Pronunciation: / sweɪd / (AmE), / sweɪd / (BrE)
to treat fabric or leather to give it a soft, velvety surface similar to suede.

Examples

To suede fabric
The manufacturer sueded the cotton fabric to give it a luxurious feel.
After being sueded, the jacket had a much softer texture than before.
They sueded the leather to create a finish that looked and felt like real suede.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buff - to polish or smooth a surface by rubbing
  • antonym: roughen - to make a surface less smooth
