clearance

noun
vertical space under an object
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr.əns / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.rəns / (BrE)
the amount of vertical space available under something, allowing objects or people to pass beneath it safely

Examples

Clearance under a bridge
Always check the clearance before driving under a low bridge.
The parking garage has limited clearance for tall vehicles.
The tunnel's clearance is only 3 meters, so large trucks cannot pass through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headroom - the vertical space above someone's head, especially in a vehicle or under an object
