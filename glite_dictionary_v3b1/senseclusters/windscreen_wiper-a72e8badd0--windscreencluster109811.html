windscreen wiper

noun
device for clearing a vehicle's windscreen
2.3 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…™ndÀåskriÀên Ààwa…™p…ôr / (AmE), / Ààw…™ndskriÀên Ààwa…™p…ô / (BrE)
a mechanical device with a rubber blade used to clear rain, snow, or debris from a vehicle's windscreen (windshield)

Examples

Car windscreen wiper
She replaced the old windscreen wiper on her car for better visibility.
The windscreen wiper struggled to keep up with the heavy rain during the storm.
Without a working windscreen wiper, driving in snowy conditions can be dangerous.

Synonyms

  • windshield wiper
    2.3 0.70/m
    windshield wiper is the common US name for the same car blade, while windscreen wiper is used mainly in British English.
  • wiper
    2.1 1.72/m
    wiper is a short, general name for the device on a vehicle window, while windscreen wiper is the British full term for the front blade.
