windscreen wiper

noun
device for clearing a vehicle's windscreen
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌskriːn ˈwaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndskriːn ˈwaɪpə / (BrE)
a mechanical device with a rubber blade used to clear rain, snow, or debris from a vehicle's windscreen (windshield)

Examples

Car windscreen wiper
She replaced the old windscreen wiper on her car for better visibility.
The windscreen wiper struggled to keep up with the heavy rain during the storm.
Without a working windscreen wiper, driving in snowy conditions can be dangerous.
