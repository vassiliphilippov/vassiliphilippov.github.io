bilobed

adjective
having two lobes
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌloʊbd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪ.ləʊbd / (BrE)
having or divided into two distinct lobes.

Examples

Bilobed tongue
Certain species of animals possess a bilobed tongue, assisting them in catching prey.
The leaf of the plant is bilobed, with a deep notch in the middle.
The liver was described as bilobed in the medical report, indicating two distinct sections.
