celadon

noun
pale green pottery
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlədɑn / (AmE), / ˈsɛlədɒn / (BrE)
a type of pottery or glaze, originally from East Asia, known for its pale green or bluish-green color

Examples

Celadon pottery
The museum displayed a rare celadon bowl from the Song dynasty.
Collectors prize celadon ceramics for their delicate greenish glaze and smooth finish.
She bought a beautiful celadon vase to decorate her living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: porcelain - a white, fine, translucent ceramic
  • synonym: stoneware - a type of pottery fired at high temperatures
