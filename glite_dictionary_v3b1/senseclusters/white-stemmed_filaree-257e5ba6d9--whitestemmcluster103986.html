white-stemmed filaree

noun
a European herb with white stems, naturalized in America
Pronunciation: / waɪt stɛmd fəˈlɛri / (AmE), / waɪt stɛmd fɪˈlɑːri / (BrE)
A low annual European herb, naturalized in America, similar to alfilaria, with white stems.
