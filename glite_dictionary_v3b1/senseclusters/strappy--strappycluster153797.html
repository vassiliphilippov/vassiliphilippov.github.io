strappy

adjective
with thin straps
Pronunciation: / ˈstræpi / (AmE), / ˈstræpi / (BrE)
having one or more thin straps as part of its design, especially in clothing or footwear

Examples

A pair of strappy sandals
She wore a pair of strappy sandals to the party.
The dress was elegant and strappy, perfect for a summer evening.
He bought a strappy top for his vacation at the beach.
