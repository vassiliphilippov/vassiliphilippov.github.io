egg-producing

adjective
able to produce eggs
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡ pɚˌduːsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡ prəˌdjuːsɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal or organism) capable of producing eggs and bearing offspring.

Examples

Egg-producing animals
Chickens are among the most egg-producing animals on farms worldwide.
Scientists studied the egg-producing abilities of the endangered species.
Some fish have a distinct season when they become highly egg-producing.

Related Terms
