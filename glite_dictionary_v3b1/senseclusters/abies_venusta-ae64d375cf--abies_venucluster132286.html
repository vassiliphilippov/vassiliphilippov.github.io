Abies venusta

noun
a fir tree species of southwestern California
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.bi.iːz vəˈnʌstə / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.bɪ.iːz vɪˈnʌstə / (BrE)
A pyramidal fir tree native to southwestern California, characterized by spiny pointed leaves and cone scales with long spines.
