blurp

verb
to make silly noise
9.5
Pronunciation: / blɜːrp / (AmE), / blɜːp / (BrE)
to make a sudden, often silly or accidental, noise or utterance

Examples

To blurp a noise
He couldn't help but blurp out a laugh during the meeting.
Sometimes she would blurp a strange sound when she was nervous.
The baby blurped unexpectedly, making everyone in the room smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mumble - to speak quietly and not clearly
