religious leader

noun
person who leads a religious group
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈliːdər / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who leads or holds a position of authority in a religious group, organization, or community

Examples

A religious leader speaks
After years of study, she became the youngest religious leader in the organization.
Many people view the religious leader as a guide for both spiritual and moral decisions.
The community gathered to listen to their religious leader speak on the importance of compassion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clergy - formal group of people authorized to perform religious duties
  • antonym: layperson - a non-ordained member of a religious community
