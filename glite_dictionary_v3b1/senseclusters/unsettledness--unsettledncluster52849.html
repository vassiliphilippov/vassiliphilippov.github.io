unsettledness

noun
instability or changeability
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (BrE)
the state of being changeable, unstable, or likely to shift, especially in reference to weather conditions.

Examples

Unsettledness of the weather
The unsettledness of the weather made it difficult to plan outdoor activities for the weekend.
There was a sense of unsettledness in the air as dark clouds gathered over the city.
Farmers worry that continued unsettledness could harm their crops this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instability - the state of being unstable or likely to change
  • antonym: stability - the state of being steady and unchanging
  • synonym: volatility - the quality of being likely to change suddenly
