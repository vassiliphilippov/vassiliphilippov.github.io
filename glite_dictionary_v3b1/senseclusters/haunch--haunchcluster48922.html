haunch

verb
to crouch low
Pronunciation: / hɑntʃ / (AmE), / hɔːntʃ / (BrE)
to crouch or sit with knees bent and body close to the ground, often ready to move.

Examples

To haunch down
The rabbit haunched in the grass, watching for danger before moving.
She haunched down beside the child to tie his shoelaces.
Before the race, the runner haunched at the starting line, ready to sprint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squat - to crouch down close to the ground with knees bent
  • antonym: stand - to be in an upright position on the feet
