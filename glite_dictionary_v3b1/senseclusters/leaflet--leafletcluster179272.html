leaflet

noun
flap of a heart valve
8.4 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàliÀêfl…ôt / (AmE), / ÀàliÀêfl…ôt / (BrE)
a thin segment or flap of a heart valve that helps control the flow of blood through the heart

Examples

Heart valve leaflet
A damaged leaflet can interfere with the proper functioning of the mitral valve.
Calcification may cause a heart valve leaflet to become stiff and less flexible.
Surgeons repaired the torn leaflet to restore normal blood flow in the patient.

Synonyms

  • cusp
    8.1 0.01/m
    a cusp is a thin triangular flap that directs blood in one way, while a leaflet is a valve flap controlling flow.
  • valvule
    9.9 0.00/m
    a valvule is a small biological valve that controls blood or fluid, while a leaflet is the thin flap inside a heart valve.
