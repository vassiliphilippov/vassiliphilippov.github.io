Secretary of the Treasury

noun
head of the Treasury Department (US)
5.3
Pronunciation: / sɛk.rəˌtɛr.i əv ðə ˈtrɛʒ.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈsɛkrətəri əv ðə ˈtrɛʒəri / (BrE)
the official who is the head of the Treasury Department, especially in the United States government

Examples

First Secretary of the Treasury
Alexander Hamilton was the first Secretary of the Treasury of the United States.
The Secretary of the Treasury announced new economic policies during the press conference.
The President nominated a new Secretary of the Treasury following the resignation.
