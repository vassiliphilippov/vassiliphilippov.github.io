sentient

noun
conscious being
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnʃənt / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃɪənt / (BrE)
a being that is able to think and feel, having consciousness or awareness

Examples

A sentient robot
Science fiction often explores the rights of sentients such as advanced robots or aliens.
The debate continues about whether animals should be considered sentients with legal protections.
Some philosophers argue that only sentients can truly experience happiness or suffering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consciousness - the state of being aware and able to think
  • synonym: creature - a living being, especially an animal
  • antonym: object - a thing that does not have thoughts or feelings
