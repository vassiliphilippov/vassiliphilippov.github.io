unobtainable

adjective
impossible to obtain
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈteɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈteɪnəbl / (BrE)
not able to be obtained or acquired; impossible to get.

Examples

An unobtainable prize
For many collectors, the rare coin remains forever unobtainable.
In some remote regions, clean drinking water is nearly unobtainable.
Tickets for the concert became unobtainable within minutes of the box office opening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: available - able to be obtained or used
  • synonym: inaccessible - not reachable or obtainable
