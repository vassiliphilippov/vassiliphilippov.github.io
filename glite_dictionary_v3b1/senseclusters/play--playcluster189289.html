play

noun
recreational activity for amusement
1.0 17/m
Pronunciation: / pleÉª / (AmE), / pleÉª / (BrE)
activity done for fun, amusement, or recreation, especially by children or for enjoyment

Examples

Children's play
Much of early childhood learning happens through play rather than formal instruction.
She watched the puppies at play and smiled at their energy.
The children forgot their chores while they were lost in play in the garden.
Many psychologists study how play helps children develop social and cognitive skills.
The living room was filled with toys scattered from an afternoon of play.
The teacher encouraged creative play during recess so the children could use their imagination.
