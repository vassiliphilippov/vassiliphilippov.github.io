intestinal juice

noun
digestive fluid secreted in the intestines
4.1 4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl dʒus / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɛstɪnəl dʒuːs / (BrE)
a fluid secreted by glands lining the walls of the intestines, containing enzymes that aid in digestion

Examples

Intestinal juice enzymes
Intestinal juice contains enzymes that help break down food in the small intestine.
Doctors study the composition of intestinal juice to better understand certain diseases.
When the body produces less intestinal juice, digestive problems may occur.
