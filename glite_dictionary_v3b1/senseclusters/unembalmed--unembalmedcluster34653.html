unembalmed

adjective
unsanitized; unvarnished
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈbɑːmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbɑːmd / (BrE)
not presented in a way that hides unpleasant facts; unsanitized or unvarnished, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Unembalmed truth
The journalist offered an unembalmed account of the city's struggles with poverty.
Her memoir is refreshingly unembalmed, revealing both the joys and hardships of her childhood.
Critics praised the film for its unembalmed depiction of life in the remote village.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sugarcoated - made to seem more pleasant or acceptable than it really is
  • synonym: unsanitized - not made more acceptable or palatable
  • synonym: unvarnished - not disguised or made to seem more pleasant
