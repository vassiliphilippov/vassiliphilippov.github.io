gathering place

noun
place where people meet or assemble
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæðərɪŋ pleɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡæðərɪŋ pleɪs / (BrE)
a place where people regularly meet or assemble, often socially

Examples

A gathering place for friends
That café is a favorite gathering place for students after class.
The community center has become a popular gathering place for local residents.
The park serves as a gathering place for weekend events and family picnics.
