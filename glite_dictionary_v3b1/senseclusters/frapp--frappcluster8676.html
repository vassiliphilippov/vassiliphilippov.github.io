frapp

noun
blended iced coffee
Pronunciation: / fræp / (AmE), / fræp / (BrE)
a slang term for a blended iced coffee drink, often sweetened and topped with whipped cream

Examples

A frapp drink
On her way to work, she picked up a cold frapp from the coffee shop.
He loves to treat himself to a chocolate frapp during the summer.
The café introduced a new seasonal frapp with pumpkin spice flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iced coffee - coffee served cold, often with ice
