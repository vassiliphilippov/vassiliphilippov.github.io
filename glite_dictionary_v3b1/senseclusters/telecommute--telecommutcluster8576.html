telecommute

verb
to work remotely
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪkəˌmjut / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪkəˌmjuːt / (BrE)
to work from home or another remote location by using the internet or other electronic communication instead of traveling to an office

Examples

To telecommute from home
Many employees now telecommute several days a week.
She prefers to telecommute because it saves her time and money.
The company allows staff to telecommute during bad weather or emergencies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commute - to travel regularly to and from work
  • synonym: work from home - to do one's job from home instead of the office
