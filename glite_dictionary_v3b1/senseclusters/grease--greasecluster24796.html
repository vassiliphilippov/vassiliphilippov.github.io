grease

verb
apply grease to lubricate
Pronunciation: / ɡris / (AmE), / ɡriːs / (BrE)
to apply grease to something, especially to lubricate moving parts so they work smoothly

Examples

To grease the hinges
Remember to grease the hinges, or the door will squeak.
The mechanic greased all the parts before reassembling the engine.
You should grease the bike chain regularly to keep it running smoothly.
