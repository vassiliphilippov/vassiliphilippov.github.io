music layer

noun
one sound track in music composition
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌleɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːzɪk ˌleɪə / (BrE)
a distinct level or track of sound in a musical composition, often combined with others to create texture or complexity

Examples

A music layer track
By muting one music layer, the entire mood of the song changed.
Each instrument was recorded as its own music layer in the studio session.
The composer added a soft piano music layer to enrich the overall sound.
