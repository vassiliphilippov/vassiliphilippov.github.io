kookaburra

noun
Australian bird with a cackling call
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːkəˌbʌrə / (AmE), / ˈkʊkəbʌrə / (BrE)
An Australian kingfisher known for its loud, distinctive cackling call.

Examples
