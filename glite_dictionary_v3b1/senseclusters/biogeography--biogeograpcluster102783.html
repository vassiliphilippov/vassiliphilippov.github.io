biogeography

noun
study of the distribution of organisms
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.dʒiːˈɑː.ɡrə.fi / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.dʒiːˈɒ.ɡrə.fi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the geographical distribution of animals and plants

Examples

Study of biogeography
His research in biogeography has uncovered new patterns in plant migration across continents.
Modern biogeography combines data from genetics, fossils, and geography to map species distributions.
The field of biogeography helps explain why some animal species are unique to certain islands.

Related Terms
