subservience

noun
usefulness or submissiveness in serving a goal
3.7 5.8
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɝː.vi.əns / (AmE), / səbˈsɜː.vi.əns / (BrE)
The state or quality of being useful or subordinate to achieving an end or carrying out a plan; submissive or subordinate behavior.

Examples

Subservience to a goal
All his actions were in subservience to the general plan.
She resented the expectation of complete subservience to her manager's wishes.
The committee valued creativity over blind subservience to old procedures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subordination - the act of placing in a lower rank or position; subjection
