fructify

verb
make productive or fruitful
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌk.təˌfaɪ /; / ˈfrʌktəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌk.tɪ.faɪ /; / ˈfrʌktɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)
to make or become productive or fruitful; to cause to yield results

Examples

The seeds eventually fructify after long months of care.
Thoughtful investments can help ideas fructify into successful ventures.
With proper irrigation, the barren land began to fructify.
