polo pony

noun
horse bred and trained for polo
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.loʊ ˈpoʊ.ni / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ləʊ ˈpəʊ.ni / (BrE)
A small, agile horse specially bred and trained for playing polo.

Examples

A polo pony horse
Each polo pony is carefully trained to respond quickly to its rider's commands.
He has ridden the same polo pony for the last five seasons.
The club imported new polo ponies from Argentina for the tournament.
