phonically

adverb
by using phonics
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑnɪkli / (AmE), / ˈfɒnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to phonics, especially by using the relationship between sounds and letters in reading or teaching.

Examples

Phonically teaching reading
Children are taught to read phonically by connecting letters to their sounds.
The new curriculum encourages teachers to introduce words phonically rather than by sight.
She explained the spelling rule phonically to help students decode unfamiliar words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phonetically - in a way that relates to the sounds of speech
