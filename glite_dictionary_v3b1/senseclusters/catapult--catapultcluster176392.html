catapult

noun
warship device launching aircraft
7.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkæ.tə.pʌlt/ (AmE), /ˈkæ.tə.pʌlt/ (BrE)
a device for launching objects with force, such as an ancient weapon, a toy, or a machine on a ship for launching aircraft
