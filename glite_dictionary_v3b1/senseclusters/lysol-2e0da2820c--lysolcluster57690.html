Lysol

noun
brand of disinfectant solution
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsɔl / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsɒl / (BrE)
a brand of clear, oily brown disinfectant solution made from cresols and soap, used as an antiseptic and disinfectant

Examples

Lysol disinfectant spray
Please use Lysol to wipe down the tables before the next meeting.
She cleaned the kitchen counters thoroughly with Lysol to kill germs.
The smell of Lysol lingered in the hospital corridors after disinfection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfectant - a chemical liquid that destroys bacteria
