selenographer

noun
lunar surface scientist
Pronunciation: / səˈliːnəˌɡræfər / (AmE), / sɪˈliːnəɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person who studies or maps the surface and features of the Moon

Examples

A selenographer studies the Moon
The famous selenographer contributed to our understanding of the Moon's craters.
As a dedicated selenographer, she spent years mapping the lunar surface in detail.
Early selenographers relied on telescopes to create the first accurate maps of the Moon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronomer - a scientist who studies celestial bodies and space
  • synonym: cartographer - a person who draws or produces maps
