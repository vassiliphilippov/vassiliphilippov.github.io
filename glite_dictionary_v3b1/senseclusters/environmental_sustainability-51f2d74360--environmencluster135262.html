environmental sustainability

noun
maintenance of ecological balance
4.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.vaɪ.rən.ˈmen.təl sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ən.vaɪ.rən.ˈmen.təl sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.vaɪ.rən.ˈmen.təl sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/ /; / /ən.vaɪ.rən.ˈmen.təl sə.steɪ.nə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.ti/ (BrE)
the practice of using resources in a way that maintains ecological balance and avoids depletion or long-term harm to the environment

Examples

Environmental sustainability practices

Many companies are investing in environmental sustainability to reduce their ecological footprint.
