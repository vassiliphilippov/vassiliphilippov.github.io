golden-yellow

adjective
yellow tinged with gold
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
Of a yellow color that is tinged with gold; rich, warm yellow resembling gold.

Examples

A golden-yellow dress
She wore a golden-yellow dress that caught everyone's attention at the party.
The autumn leaves turned a beautiful golden-yellow before falling to the ground.
The field was covered in golden-yellow sunflowers swaying in the breeze.
