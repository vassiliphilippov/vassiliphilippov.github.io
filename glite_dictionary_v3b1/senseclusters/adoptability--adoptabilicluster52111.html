adoptability

noun
ease of acceptance
Pronunciation: / əˌdɑptəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌdɒptəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy or likely to be accepted and put into use by people or organizations

Examples

Adoptability of new ideas
The adoptability of the new technology depends on how user-friendly it is.
Before launching the policy, the team assessed its adoptability among different departments.
High adoptability is crucial for the success of any innovative product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acceptability - the quality of being accepted as satisfactory or suitable
  • antonym: resistance - the refusal to accept or comply with something
  • synonym: usability - the quality of being easy to use
