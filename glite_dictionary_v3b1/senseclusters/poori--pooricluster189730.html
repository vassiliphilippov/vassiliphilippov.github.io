poori

noun
Indian fried bread
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊri / (AmE), / ˈpʊəri / (BrE)
a small, round, deep-fried Indian bread made from unleavened wheat flour

Examples

A plate of poori
For breakfast, we enjoyed spicy potatoes with freshly made poori.
The restaurant serves poori with a variety of vegetarian curries.
She learned how to make poori from her grandmother during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chapati - an unleavened flatbread from South Asia, cooked on a griddle
  • antonym: naan - a leavened, oven-baked flatbread from South Asia
