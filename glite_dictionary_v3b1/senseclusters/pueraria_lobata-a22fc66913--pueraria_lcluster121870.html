Pueraria lobata

noun
invasive Asian climbing vine; kudzu
9.9
Pronunciation: / puˈɛəriə ləˈbeɪtə / (AmE), / pjʊəˈrɑːrɪə ləˈbɑːtə / (BrE)
a fast-growing perennial vine native to eastern Asia, known for its invasive growth and tuberous roots; also called kudzu
