lead

noun
rope or chain for restraining animals
Pronunciation: / lid / (AmE), / liːd / (BrE)
A restraint consisting of a rope or light chain used for guiding or holding an animal, especially a dog.

Examples

Dog lead
Always keep your dog on a lead in public parks.
She bought a new red lead for her puppy.
The owner clipped the lead to the dog's collar before leaving the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leash - a restraint for holding or guiding an animal
