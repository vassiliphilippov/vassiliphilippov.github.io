strong ally

noun
powerful supporter
2.7
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ ˈælaɪ / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈælaɪ / (BrE)
A person, group, or nation that provides powerful and reliable support for a cause or another party.

Examples

A strong ally
Having a strong ally can make a significant difference in challenging situations.
She became a strong ally for the environmental cause.
The company proved to be a strong ally during the negotiations.
