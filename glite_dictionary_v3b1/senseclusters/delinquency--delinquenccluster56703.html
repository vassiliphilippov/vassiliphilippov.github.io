delinquency

noun
minor crime or antisocial act by a youth
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪŋkwənsi / (BrE)
A minor crime or antisocial act, especially one committed by a young person in violation of the law.

Examples

Youth delinquency
Concern over rising delinquency led to the introduction of after-school programs for teenagers.
His early delinquency was attributed to a lack of positive role models at home.
The city is taking steps to reduce youth delinquency in the neighborhoods most affected by crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crime - an act that is against the law
  • antonym: obedience - compliance with laws or rules
  • synonym: offense - a breach of a law or rule; an illegal act
