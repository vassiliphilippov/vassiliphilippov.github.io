A minor crime or antisocial act, especially one committed by a young person in violation of the law.

Examples

Youth delinquency
Concern over rising delinquency led to the introduction of after-school programs for teenagers.
His early delinquency was attributed to a lack of positive role models at home.
The city is taking steps to reduce youth delinquency in the neighborhoods most affected by crime.

Synonyms

  • juvenile delinquency
    4.9 0.46/m
    juvenile delinquency means illegal or antisocial acts by minors, while delinquency is a broader term for minor crimes or acts.
  • misdemeanor
    6.0 4.69/m
    misdemeanor is a formal legal label for a less serious crime, while delinquency often means a minor offense or youth wrongdoing.
  • infraction
    7.2 2.45/m
    infraction is a very minor rule or law breaking, often not criminal, while delinquency implies minor crime or antisocial acts.
