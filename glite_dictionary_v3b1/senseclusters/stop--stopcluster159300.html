stop

noun
type of consonant sound
4.1
Pronunciation: / stɑp / (AmE), / stɒp / (BrE)
a consonant sound made by completely blocking the airflow in the vocal tract and then suddenly releasing it

Examples

A stop sound
In English, the stop sounds include 'p', 't', and 'k'.
Some languages have voiced and voiceless stops that contrast with each other.
The linguist analyzed the way he pronounced each stop in the syllable.
