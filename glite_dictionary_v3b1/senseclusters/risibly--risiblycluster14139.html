risibly

adverb
in a laughable way
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪzəbli / (AmE), / ˈrɪzəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is laughable, amusing, or ridiculous

Examples

React risibly
The idea was so impractical that everyone reacted risibly to the suggestion.
He risibly claimed he could finish the marathon without any training.
The movie's plot twists were risibly over-the-top, making the audience laugh out loud.
He responded risibly to the unexpected question, making everyone in the room laugh.
The idea was so impractical that it was almost risibly absurd.
She dismissed the rumor risibly, unable to take it seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absurdly - in a way that is wildly unreasonable or illogical
  • synonym: laughably - in a way that is so silly or unreasonable as to be amusing
  • synonym: ludicrously - in a way that is absurd or ridiculous
  • antonym: seriously - in a way that is earnest or not intended to cause laughter
