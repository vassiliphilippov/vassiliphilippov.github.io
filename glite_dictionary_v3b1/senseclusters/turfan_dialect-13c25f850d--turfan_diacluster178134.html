Turfan dialect

noun
Tocharian dialect of Qarašähär and Turpan
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrˌfæn ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.fæn ˈdaɪ.ə.lɛkt / (BrE)
