samara

noun
winged dry fruit, e.g. of maple

Pronunciation: /ˈsæ.mə.rə/ /; / /sə.ˈme.rə/ (AmE), /sə.ˈmeə.rə/ /; / /ˈsæ.mə.rə/ (BrE)

Examples

The samara of the maple tree spins as it falls, carrying the seed farther from the parent tree.
