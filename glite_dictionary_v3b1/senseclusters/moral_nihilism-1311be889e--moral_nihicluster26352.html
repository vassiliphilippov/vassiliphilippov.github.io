moral nihilism

noun
denial of objective moral values
9.7 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmɔːr.əl ˈnaɪ.ə.lɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈmɑːr.əl ˈnaɪ.ə.lɪz.əm/ (AmE), /ˈmɒr.əl ˈnaɪ.ə.lɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈmɒr.əl ˈnaɪ.hɪ.lɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the philosophical belief that there are no objective or inherent moral values or truths

  • degree lower: moral relativism - the belief that moral judgments are true or false only relative to some standpoint
