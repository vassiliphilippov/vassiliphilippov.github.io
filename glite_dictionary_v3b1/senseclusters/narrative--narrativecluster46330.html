narrative

adjective
relating to storytelling
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnærətɪv / (AmE), / ˈnærətɪv / (BrE)
consisting of or characterized by the telling of a story; relating to narration or storytelling

Examples

Narrative style
He prefers narrative art forms that tell a coherent story.
Much of the film's appeal lies in its narrative structure.
The author developed a highly narrative style in her latest book.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descriptive - giving details about how something or someone looks, sounds, etc., rather than telling a story
