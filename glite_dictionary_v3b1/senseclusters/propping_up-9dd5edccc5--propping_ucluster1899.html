propping up

noun
the act of supporting something with props
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑpɪŋ ˈʌp / (AmE), / ˈprɒpɪŋ ˈʌp / (BrE)
the act of supporting or holding something in position, especially with physical supports or props

Examples

Temporary propping up
Temporary propping up was necessary until permanent repairs could be made to the bridge.
The propping up of the old wall prevented it from collapsing during the renovation.
Their propping up of the sculpture ensured it stayed upright throughout the exhibition.
