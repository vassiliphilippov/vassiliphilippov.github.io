enterprise

noun
readiness to take bold risks; initiative
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tɚˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə.praɪz / (BrE)
the willingness or readiness to take bold risks or embark on new ventures; initiative and resourcefulness

Examples

Show enterprise at work
Her enterprise led to the development of several innovative products.
It takes a lot of enterprise to start a new business in a competitive market.
Young people are encouraged to show enterprise and creativity in problem-solving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiative - the ability to assess and initiate things independently
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
