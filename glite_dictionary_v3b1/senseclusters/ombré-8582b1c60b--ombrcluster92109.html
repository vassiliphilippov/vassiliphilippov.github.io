ombré

noun
gradient color effect
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmˌbreɪ / (AmE), / ˈɒmˌbreɪ / (BrE)
a style in which colors or tones blend gradually from light to dark, often used in clothing, hair, or design

Examples

An ombré effect
The artist painted an ombré background that faded from yellow at the top to deep orange at the bottom.
She chose an ombré hairstyle that transitioned smoothly from dark brown roots to blonde tips.
The living room curtains featured an elegant ombré effect, blending light gray into navy blue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gradient - a gradual change in color or shade
  • antonym: solid - having a single, uniform color or shade
