argan

noun
Moroccan oil tree
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡən / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡæn / (BrE)
a tree native to Morocco whose seeds are used to produce a valuable oil

Examples

An argan tree
The argan grows mainly in southwestern Morocco and is well adapted to dry climates.
Farmers harvest the fruit of the argan to produce oil for cooking and cosmetics.
Many beauty products highlight the use of argan as a key natural ingredient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argan oil - oil extracted from the seeds of the argan tree
