Heideggerian

noun
follower of Heidegger
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdəˈɡɛriən / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdɪˈɡɛəriən / (BrE)
a person who follows, advocates, or studies the philosophical ideas of Martin Heidegger

Examples

A Heideggerian thinker
As a dedicated Heideggerian, he often referenced 'Being and Time' in his lectures.
The conference attracted many Heideggerians interested in existential philosophy.
She became a prominent Heideggerian after years of studying German philosophy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialist - a person who follows or studies existentialist philosophy
  • synonym: phenomenologist - a person who studies or follows phenomenology
