bail

noun
water-removal container
7.5
Pronunciation: / beɪl / (AmE), / beɪl / (BrE)
a container, such as a bucket or scoop, used for removing water from a boat or area

Examples

A bail bucket
He used a bail to remove water from the bottom of the boat.
The old wooden bail was kept under the seat in case of leaks.
After the storm, she filled the bail with rainwater to empty the canoe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bucket - an open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids
  • synonym: scoop - a utensil used to move or remove substances, especially liquids
