greater kudu

noun
large African antelope species
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪtər ˈkuːduː / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪtə ˈkuːduː / (BrE)
a large species of African antelope with long spiral horns and distinctive white stripes on its body

Examples

A greater kudu antelope
During our safari, we spotted a herd of greater kudu grazing near the acacia trees.
The greater kudu is known for its impressive spiral horns and striking appearance.
Unlike the lesser kudu, the greater kudu inhabits a wider range of African woodlands.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lesser kudu - a smaller species of African antelope closely related to the greater kudu
