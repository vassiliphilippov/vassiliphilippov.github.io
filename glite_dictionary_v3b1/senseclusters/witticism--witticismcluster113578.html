witticism

noun
clever or witty remark
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪt.ɪˌsɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈwɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm / (BrE)
a clever or witty remark that is intended to amuse or provoke laughter

Examples

A clever witticism
He entertained the guests with a clever witticism that had everyone laughing.
Her sharp witticism during the meeting lightened the tense atmosphere.
The comedian's trademark is his quick witticism about daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bon mot - a clever or witty saying; a witticism (French origin)
  • synonym: joke - a humorous anecdote or remark
  • synonym: quip - a witty or sarcastic remark
