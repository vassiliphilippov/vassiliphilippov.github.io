repay

verb
pay back or return something
4.7
Pronunciation: / riˈpeɪ / (AmE), / rɪˈpeɪ / (BrE)
to pay back money that you have borrowed or received

Examples

To repay a loan
He struggled for months to repay his student loans after graduation.
I hope one day I can repay your kindness with my own help.
She promised to repay the money she borrowed from her friend next week.
I'll be sure to repay the money I borrowed from you next week.
She finally repaid her student loans after ten years.
You can't expect him to repay your kindness with rudeness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: owe - to be under obligation to pay or repay
  • synonym: refund - to pay back money, especially after returning purchased goods
