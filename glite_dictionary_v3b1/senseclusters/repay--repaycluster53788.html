Synonyms

  • pay back
    1.6 14/m
    pay back means returning borrowed money in everyday speech, while repay is a more formal verb for the same action.
  • give back
    1.0 30/m
    give back is used for returning objects or things you borrow, while repay specifically means paying money back to someone.
  • return
    1.0 50/m
    return means bringing something back to its owner, place, or condition, while repay means giving money back that you owe.
