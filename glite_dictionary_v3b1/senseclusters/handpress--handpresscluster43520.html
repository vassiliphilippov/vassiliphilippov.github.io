handpress

noun
manual pressing tool
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈhændˌprɛs / (BrE)
a small manually operated press used to compress, shape, or assemble materials in workshops or craftwork

Examples

Jeweler's handpress
The jeweler used a handpress to carefully flatten the silver sheet for the pendant.
In the workshop, a handpress is essential for assembling small metal parts by hand.
She bought an old handpress at the market to use in her printmaking projects.
