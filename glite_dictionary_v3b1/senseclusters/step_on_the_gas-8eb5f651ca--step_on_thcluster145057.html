step on the gas

verb
press the accelerator; go faster
Pronunciation: / stɛp ɑn ðə ɡæs / (AmE), / stɛp ɒn ðə ɡas / (BrE)
to press the accelerator of a vehicle to make it go faster; to increase speed or effort quickly

Examples

To step on the gas pedal
He stepped on the gas as soon as he saw the highway was clear.
If you want to meet the deadline, you'll have to step on the gas and work faster.
When the light turned green, she stepped on the gas and sped through the intersection.

  • synonym: accelerate - to move or cause to move faster
