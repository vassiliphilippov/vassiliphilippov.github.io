inpour

noun
an inflow or influx
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌpɔr / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌpɔː / (BrE)
an act or instance of flowing in; an inflow.

Examples

An inpour of questions
After the announcement, there was an inpour of questions from the audience.
Heavy rains caused an inpour of water into the basement.
The sudden inpour of donations overwhelmed the small charity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influx - an arrival or flowing in of a large amount of something
  • antonym: outflow - the act or process of flowing out
