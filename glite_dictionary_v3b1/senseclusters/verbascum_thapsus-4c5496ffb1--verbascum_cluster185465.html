Verbascum thapsus

noun
a tall, woolly plant (great mullein) with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / vɚˈbæskəm ˈθæpsəs / (AmE), / vəˈbæskəm ˈθæpsəs / (BrE)
A tall, woolly plant with densely packed yellow flowers, commonly known as great mullein, used historically as torches by ancient Greeks and Romans.

Examples
