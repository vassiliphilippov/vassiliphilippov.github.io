cat's eye

noun
reflective road marker
Pronunciation: / ˈkæts aɪ / (AmE), / ˈkæts aɪ / (BrE)
a small reflective device set into roads to mark lanes or a glass marble with a colored core resembling a cat's eye

Examples

Road cat's eye
The car's headlights glinted off the cat's eye embedded in the road at night.
He found a shiny cat's eye marble while playing in the schoolyard.
Drivers rely on the cat's eye markers to stay in their lanes during foggy weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflector - a device that reflects light, often used for safety
