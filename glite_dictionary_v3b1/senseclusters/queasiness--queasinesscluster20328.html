queasiness

noun
feeling of nausea or unease
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiz.i.nəs /; / ˈkwiziˌnɛs / (AmE), / ˈkwiːz.i.nəs /; / ˈkwiːzɪnəs / (BrE)
a mild feeling of nausea or discomfort in the stomach, sometimes with a general sense of unease

Examples

Feeling queasiness in stomach
As the boat rocked, a wave of queasiness swept over her.
He felt a sudden queasiness before going on stage to give his speech.
The horror movie left me with a lingering sense of queasiness.
A sudden wave of queasiness made him sit down and rest for a moment.
She complained of queasiness after eating the seafood.
The roller coaster ride left me with a lingering queasiness in my stomach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discomfort - a feeling of slight pain or unease
  • synonym: nausea - a feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit
  • synonym: sickness - the feeling or condition of being ill, especially with stomach upset
