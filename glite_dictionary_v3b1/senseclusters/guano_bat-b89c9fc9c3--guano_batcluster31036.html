guano bat

noun
southern U.S. freetail bat species
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgwɑː.noʊ bæt/ (AmE), /ˈgwɑː.nəʊ bæt/ (BrE)
A species of freetail bat (Tadarida brasiliensis) commonly found in the southern United States, known for its short velvety fur and southward migration in winter.

Examples

The guano bat is frequently seen at dusk, hunting insects over open fields.
