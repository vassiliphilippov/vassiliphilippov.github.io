Synonyms

  • widespread destruction
    4.8 0.07/m
    widespread destruction is extensive damage across a large area, while mass destruction implies very large-scale damage affecting many places or things.
  • devastation
    4.8 4/m
    devastation is great, often complete ruin of a place, while mass destruction refers to wide, powerful damage across many areas or things.
  • widespread damage
    3.8 0.1/m
    widespread damage is harm that affects many people or places across a region, while mass destruction means broad, total destruction on a large scale.
