Minamata Bay

proper noun
bay in Kyushu, Japan, site of mercury poisoning incident
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌminəˈmɑːtə beɪ / (AmE), / ˌmiːnəˈmɑːtə beɪ / (BrE)
a bay on the west coast of Kyushu, Japan, known for severe mercury poisoning among residents due to industrial pollution in the 1950s

Examples

Minamata Bay disaster
