solo blast

noun
baseball: home run with bases empty
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊloʊ blæst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊləʊ blɑːst / (BrE)
A home run hit with no runners on base, scoring a single run.

Examples

A solo blast home run
His first major league home run was a solo blast that gave his team the lead.
The batter hit a solo blast over the left-field wall in the third inning.
The game started with a solo blast, putting the visitors up by one run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: home run - A hit allowing the batter to circle all bases and score
