needlessness

noun
state of being unnecessary
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈniːdləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being unnecessary or not required

Examples

The needlessness of meeting
The needlessness of the meeting became clear when no new information was shared.
He was frustrated by the needlessness of the complicated procedures.
They questioned the needlessness of buying extra supplies for the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: necessity - the state of being needed or required
  • synonym: redundancy - the state of being not needed or superfluous
