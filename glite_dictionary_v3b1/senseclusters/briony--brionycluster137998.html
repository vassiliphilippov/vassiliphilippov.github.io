briony

noun
a climbing plant of the Bryonia genus
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪəni / (AmE), / ˈbraɪəni / (BrE)
A climbing plant of the genus Bryonia, having large leaves, small flowers, and an acrid juice with emetic and purgative properties.

Examples

Briony climbing plant
Some herbal remedies use briony, but it must be handled with caution due to its toxic properties.

We found clusters of briony leaves and berries growing along the hedgerow.
