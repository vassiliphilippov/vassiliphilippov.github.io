beetroot

noun
root vegetable with red flesh
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtruːt /; / ˈbiːtˌrut / (AmE), / ˈbiːtruːt / (BrE)
a round red root vegetable with dark purple flesh, eaten as food, used for its juice, or as a natural dye
