autumn

noun
season between summer and winter
1.0 12/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.t…ôm / (AmE), / Àà…îÀê.t…ôm / (BrE)
The season of the year between summer and winter, when leaves fall from the trees and the weather becomes cooler.

Examples

The autumn season
Many people enjoy hiking in autumn because of the beautiful scenery.
The leaves in our garden turn golden in autumn.
We always visit my grandparents during autumn when the air is crisp and cool.

Synonyms

  • fall
    1.0 59/m
    fall is the common North American name for the season of cooling weather and falling leaves, while autumn is used elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fall - (North American) the season between summer and winter
  • antonym: spring - the season between winter and summer
  • antonym: summer - the warmest season of the year, preceding autumn
  • antonym: winter - the coldest season of the year, following autumn
