Ponca

noun
Native American language of the Ponca people
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑŋ.kə / (AmE), / ˈpɒŋ.kə / (BrE)
the Dhegiha dialect of the Siouan language family spoken by the Ponca people, a Native American group originally from the Midwest United States
