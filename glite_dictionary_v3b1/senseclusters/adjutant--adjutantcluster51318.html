adjutant

noun
military assistant officer
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈædʒədənt / (AmE), / ˈadʒʊtənt / (BrE)
an officer in the armed forces who acts as an administrative assistant to a more senior officer

Examples

Army adjutant
As adjutant, his responsibilities included organizing the daily schedule for the colonel.
She was promoted to adjutant after several years of exemplary service in the army.
The adjutant delivered the message to the commanding officer without delay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aide-de-camp - an officer acting as a confidential assistant to a senior officer
