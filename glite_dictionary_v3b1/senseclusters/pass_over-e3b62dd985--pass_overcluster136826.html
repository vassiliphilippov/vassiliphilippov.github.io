pass over

verb
travel across; go over
Pronunciation: / pæs ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / pɑːs ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to move, travel, or go across or above something (such as an area, surface, or obstacle)

Examples

Clouds pass over the hills
A thick mist passed over the valley early in the morning.
The bird passed over the field as it searched for prey.
The hikers passed over the rocky terrain before reaching the lake.
As the plane passed over the city, the passengers admired the view below.
Several flocks of birds passed over the valley at dawn.
The helicopter will pass over the stadium during the parade.
Clouds often pass over the city without bringing any rain.
The migratory birds pass over our village every spring on their way north.
The plane passed over the mountains before beginning its descent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross - to go from one side to the other of something
  • synonym: fly over - to travel above something in the air
