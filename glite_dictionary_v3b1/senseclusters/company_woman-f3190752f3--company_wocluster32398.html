company woman

noun
a woman loyal first to her employer
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.pə.ni ˈwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pə.ni ˈwʊm.ən / (BrE)
a female employee whose primary loyalty is to the company, often over her colleagues

Examples

A company woman works late
As a true company woman, she never hesitated to work late when the business needed her.
Everyone in the office knows that Janet is a company woman who will always support management’s decisions.
Her reputation as a company woman helped her climb the corporate ladder quickly.
