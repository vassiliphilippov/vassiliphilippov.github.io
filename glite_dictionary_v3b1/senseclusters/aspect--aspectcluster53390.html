aspect

noun
facial expression showing emotion
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɛkt / (AmE), / ˈaspɛkt / (BrE)
the appearance or expression of someone's face, especially as it shows emotion

Examples

A cheerful aspect
He entered the room with a cheerful aspect that immediately lifted our spirits.
Her solemn aspect made everyone in the room fall silent.
The child's frightened aspect revealed how nervous she was.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demeanor - outward behavior or bearing
  • synonym: expression - the look on someone's face that shows what they are thinking or feeling
