traitorous

adjective
showing betrayal or treachery
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪ.tɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈtreɪ.tər.əs / (BrE)
having the character or nature of a traitor; showing betrayal or treachery towards someone or something.

Examples

Traitorous behavior
Her traitorous behavior shocked everyone who trusted her.
Rumors spread about a traitorous plot within the organization.
The general was executed for his traitorous actions against his country.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loyal - showing firm and constant support or allegiance
  • synonym: treacherous - guilty of or involving betrayal or deception
