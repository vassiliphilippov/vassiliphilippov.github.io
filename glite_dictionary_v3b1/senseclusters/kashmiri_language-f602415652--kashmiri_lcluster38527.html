Kashmiri language

noun
language spoken in Kashmir
Pronunciation: / kæʃˈmiːri ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kaʃˈmɪəri ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the official language spoken in Kashmir, belonging to the Dardic group of the Indo-Aryan language family.

Examples

Kashmiri language speakers
Many people in India and Pakistan speak Kashmiri language as their mother tongue.
She is learning the Kashmiri language to connect with her cultural roots.
The Kashmiri language has its own unique script and literary tradition.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Hindi - widely spoken language in northern India
  • synonym: Urdu - language also spoken in Kashmir region
