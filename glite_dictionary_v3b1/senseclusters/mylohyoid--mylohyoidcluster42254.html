mylohyoid

adjective
jaw-to-hyoid muscle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪloʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌmaɪləʊˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to the muscle that connects the lower jaw (mandible) to the hyoid bone in the neck

Examples

Mylohyoid muscle injury
The mylohyoid muscle plays an important role in swallowing and speaking.
During surgery, the doctor carefully avoided damaging the mylohyoid nerve.
Pain in the mylohyoid area can make it difficult to open your mouth fully.
