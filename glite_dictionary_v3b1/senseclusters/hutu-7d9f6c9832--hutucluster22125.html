Hutu

noun
member of a Bantu ethnic group in Rwanda and Burundi
Pronunciation: / ˈhuːtuː / (AmE), / ˈhuːtuː / (BrE)
A member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group living primarily in Rwanda and Burundi.

Examples

A Hutu village
He studied the traditions and customs of the Hutu for his anthropology research.
Many Hutu people live in rural areas where agriculture is the main occupation.
The conflict between Hutu and Tutsi populations has shaped the history of Rwanda.
