oop

interjection
informal exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / uːp / (AmE), / uːp / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to acknowledge a small mistake, accident, or surprise

Examples

Oop, my mistake
She bumped into the table and said, "oop, sorry about that!"
He dropped his pen during the meeting and quietly muttered, "oop."
As the door closed behind her, she realized, "oop, I left my phone inside!"
