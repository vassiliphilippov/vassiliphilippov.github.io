microscopically

adverb
in a way visible only with a microscope
1.8 5.7
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈskɑː.pɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈskɒp.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that can only be seen with a microscope, or by means of a microscope; on a very small scale

Examples

Microscopically examine cells
The blood sample was examined microscopically for the presence of bacteria.
The crystals were microscopically small, requiring special equipment to see them.
When viewed microscopically, the tissue showed signs of infection.
