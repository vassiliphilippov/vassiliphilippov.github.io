hire

noun
the act of hiring something or someone
Pronunciation: / haɪɚ / (AmE), / haɪə / (BrE)
the act of paying to use something or employ someone for a period of time

Examples

Short-term hire
She found temporary work through an agency that specializes in short-term hire.
The company charges a daily fee for equipment hire.
We arranged a hire car for our trip to Scotland.
