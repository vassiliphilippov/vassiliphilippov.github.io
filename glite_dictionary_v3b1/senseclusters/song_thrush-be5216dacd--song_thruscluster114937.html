song thrush

noun
Eurasian songbird with a musical call
Pronunciation: / sɔŋ θrʌʃ / (AmE), / sɒŋ θrʌʃ / (BrE)
a common Eurasian thrush (Turdus philomelos) known for its melodious and repetitive song

Examples

A song thrush singing
A song thrush was perched on the branch, singing early in the morning.
Many gardeners welcome the song thrush because it eats harmful snails and insects.
The song thrush is easily recognized by the repeated phrases in its beautiful song.
