sick

adjective
affected by illness; unwell
Pronunciation: / sɪk / (AmE), / sɪk / (BrE)
affected by illness or impaired in normal physical or mental function; unwell.

Examples

Sick in bed
He called in sick and spent the day resting in bed.
Several students stayed home because they were sick with the flu.
She felt sick after eating food that had gone bad.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - in good health; not sick or injured
  • synonym: ill - unwell; affected by disease or illness
