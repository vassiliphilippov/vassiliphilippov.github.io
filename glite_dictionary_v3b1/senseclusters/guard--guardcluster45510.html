Synonyms

  • security guard
    1.3 8/m
    a security guard works to protect buildings and control access, while a guard is any person who protects people or property.
  • sentry
    6.0 2/m
    a sentry is a soldier posted to stop people or danger, while a guard can be any person assigned to protect.
  • sentinel
    7.4 3/m
    a sentinel keeps watch for expected threats in formal or military settings, while a guard covers many protection roles.
  • watchman
    4.1 1/m
    a watchman works mainly at night to watch buildings quietly, while a guard may work any time protecting people or property.
