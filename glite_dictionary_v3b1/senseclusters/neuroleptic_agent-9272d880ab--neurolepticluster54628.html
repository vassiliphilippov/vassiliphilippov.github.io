neuroleptic agent

noun
drug for treating psychosis with calming effect
8.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /njʊ.r.oʊ.ˈlep.tɪk ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (AmE), /njʊə.rə.ˈlep.tɪk ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (BrE)
a drug that reduces nervous tension and is used to treat psychotic disorders by producing a calming effect

Examples
