St. Nicholas

proper noun
4th-century bishop; inspiration for Santa Claus
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈnɪkələs / (BrE)
A 4th-century bishop in Asia Minor who is the historical figure behind the legend of Santa Claus.

Examples

St. Nicholas gifts
Children left out their shoes for St. Nicholas to fill with gifts.
Many European countries celebrate the feast day of St. Nicholas on December 6th.
The legend of St. Nicholas inspired the modern image of Santa Claus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Santa Claus - the legendary figure who delivers gifts at Christmas, based on St. Nicholas
