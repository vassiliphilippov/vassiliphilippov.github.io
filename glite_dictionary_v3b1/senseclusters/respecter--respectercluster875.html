respecter

noun
person who respects; often in 'no respecter of'
2.9 7.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɛktər / (AmE), / rɪˈspɛktə / (BrE)
A person who shows respect for someone or something; often used in the phrase 'no respecter of', meaning treating everyone or everything the same without favoritism or distinction.

Examples

No respecter of tradition
He is no respecter of tradition and does things his own way.
Justice should be no respecter of persons, treating everyone equally before the law.
The storm was no respecter of property, damaging homes both old and new.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respect - a feeling of admiration for someone or something
