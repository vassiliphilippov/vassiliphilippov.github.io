haha

interjection
laughter exclamation
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːˌhɑː / (AmE), / ˈhɑːˌhɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation used in writing or speech to represent laughter or amusement

Examples

Texting haha

When he slipped on the banana peel, everyone burst out with a loud haha.
He replied with a quick haha to show he found the joke amusing.
