surface

noun
outward or superficial appearance
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.fɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.fɪs / (BrE)
the outward or superficial appearance of something, as distinct from its true character or substance

Examples

Surface appearance
Although he seemed calm on the surface, he was actually very nervous inside.
Don't be deceived by the glossy surface of the advertisement; the product isn't as good as it seems.
Her cheerful surface hid deeper feelings of insecurity and sadness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appearance - the visible or outward aspect of someone or something
  • antonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality of something
  • synonym: façade - an outward appearance maintained to conceal a less pleasant reality
