luncheon voucher

noun
employer-issued meal coupon
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌn(t)ʃən ˈvoʊt͡ʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.tʃən ˈvɒtʃ.ər / (BrE)
a coupon provided by an employer, redeemable at restaurants or cafés, entitling the holder to a meal or its monetary value

Examples

luncheon vouchers from employer
Many companies used to give their employees luncheon vouchers as a workplace benefit.
She paid for her sandwich and drink using a luncheon voucher at the café.
The government once encouraged employers to distribute luncheon vouchers to promote worker welfare.
