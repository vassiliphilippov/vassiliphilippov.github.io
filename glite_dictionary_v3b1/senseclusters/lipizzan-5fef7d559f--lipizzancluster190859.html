Lipizzan

noun
Austrian breed of dressage horse
9.0
Pronunciation: /lɪ.ˈpɪt.sən/ /; / /lɪ.ˈpɪz.ən/ (AmE), /lɪ.ˈpɪt.sən/ /; / /lɪ.ˈpɪz.ən/ (BrE)
a breed of compact, sturdy saddle horse originally bred in Austria and known for its intelligence, docility, and excellence in dressage, especially at the Spanish Riding School in Vienna
