Eretmochelys imbricata

noun
species of tropical sea turtle
Pronunciation: / əˌrɛtməˈkiːlɪs ˌɪmbrɪˈkeɪtə / (AmE), / əˌrɛtməʊˈkiːlɪs ˌɪmbrɪˈkeɪtə / (BrE)
A tropical sea turtle with a hawk-like beak, known for its beautiful shell used to make tortoiseshell and listed as critically endangered.
