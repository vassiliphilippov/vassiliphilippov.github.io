unthread

verb
to separate posts
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɛd / (BrE)
to remove or separate one or more messages or posts from an online discussion thread, usually by a moderator

Examples

To unthread a message
The moderator decided to unthread the heated argument from the main discussion.
After several off-topic replies, the admin chose to unthread those posts into a new topic.
If a conversation drifts too far, a moderator may unthread it for clarity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: merge - to combine two or more things into one
  • synonym: split - to divide something into parts
