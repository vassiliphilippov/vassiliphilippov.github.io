an ounce of prevention is better than a pound of cure

x
proverb: prevention is easier than cure
1.0
Pronunciation: / æn aʊns əv prɪˈvɛnʃən ɪz ˈbɛtər ðæn ə paʊnd əv kjʊr / (AmE), / ən aʊns əv prɪˈvɛnʃən ɪz ˈbɛtə ðən ə paʊnd əv kjʊə / (BrE)
used to say that it is better and easier to stop a problem from happening than to fix it after it has happened

Examples

An ounce of prevention is better than a pound of cure
Installing antivirus software may seem unnecessary, but an ounce of prevention is better than a pound of cure.
My grandmother always said that an ounce of prevention is better than a pound of cure when it came to staying healthy.
When it comes to maintaining your car, an ounce of prevention is better than a pound of cure.
