post-haste

adverb
as quickly as possible
Pronunciation: / poʊst ˈheɪst / (AmE), / pəʊst ˈheɪst / (BrE)
as quickly as possible; with all possible speed or urgency

Examples

To act post-haste
She called a taxi post-haste after realizing she was late for the meeting.
The manager asked for the report to be delivered post-haste.
When the storm warning was issued, the campers packed up post-haste and left the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immediately - at once; without delay
  • synonym: quickly - at a fast speed; rapidly
