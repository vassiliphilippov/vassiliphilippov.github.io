interorbital

adjective
between orbits
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɔːrbɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɔːbɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between the orbits of celestial bodies or spacecraft, especially in the context of orbital mechanics

Examples

Interorbital transfer
The spacecraft performed an interorbital maneuver to reach its final destination.
Accurate calculations are essential for any interorbital transfer between satellites.
Scientists discussed the challenges of interorbital travel during the conference on space exploration.
