unadaptive

adjective
unable to adjust
7.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ə.ˈdæp.tɪv/ /; / /ən.ə.ˈdæp.tɪv/ (AmE), /ʌn.ə.ˈdæp.tɪv/ /; / /ən.ə.ˈdæp.tɪv/ (BrE)
not able or not tending to adjust or respond to new conditions or environments

Examples

An unadaptive system
Some traditional business models are unadaptive in the face of rapid technological change.
