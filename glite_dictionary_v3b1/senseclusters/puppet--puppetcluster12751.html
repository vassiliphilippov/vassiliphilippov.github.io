puppet

noun
person controlled by others
6.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌ.pɪt/ /; / /ˈpʌ.pət/ (AmE), /ˈpʌ.pɪt/ /; / /ˈpʌ.pət/ (BrE)

Examples

Political puppet

Many believed the new leader was merely a puppet for the powerful corporation.
She refused to become a puppet and insisted on making her own decisions.
