risotto

noun
Italian creamy rice dish
3.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / rɪˈzɒtəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian dish of rice cooked slowly in broth to a creamy consistency, often with cheese and other ingredients

Examples

Creamy risotto dish
She ordered a delicious mushroom risotto at the Italian restaurant.
The chef taught us how to make creamy risotto from scratch.
They served pumpkin risotto as the main course for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paella - a Spanish rice dish cooked with saffron and various ingredients
  • synonym: pilaf - a dish in which rice is cooked in seasoned broth
