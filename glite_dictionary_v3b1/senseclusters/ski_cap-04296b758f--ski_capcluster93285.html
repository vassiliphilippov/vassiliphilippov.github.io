ski cap

noun
close-fitting woolen winter cap
Pronunciation: / ski kæp / (AmE), / ski kap / (BrE)
A close-fitting woolen cap, often with a tapering tail and a tassel, worn to keep the head warm; commonly used in cold weather or outdoor winter sports.

Examples

A ski cap with tassel
Children bundled up in scarves and ski caps before heading out to play in the snow.
He wore a red ski cap to protect his ears from the biting wind.
She pulled her ski cap down over her forehead as the snow began to fall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balaclava - a close-fitting, knitted covering for the whole head and neck, except for parts of the face
  • synonym: beanie - a small, close-fitting hat
