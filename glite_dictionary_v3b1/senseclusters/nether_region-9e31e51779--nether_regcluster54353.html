nether region

noun
the abode of Satan; hell
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛðər ˈriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈneðə ˈriːdʒən / (BrE)
(in Christianity) The place believed to be the abode of Satan and evil spirits; hell.

Examples

Burn in the nether region
In his sermons, he warned that sinners would end up in the nether region.
Many medieval texts describe the nether region as a place of torment and fire.
The painting depicted lost souls trapped in the nether region for eternity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heaven - the place of God and the blessed after death
  • synonym: hell - the place where the wicked are believed to be punished after death
