labor

noun
workers organized for collective bargaining
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bɚ /; / ˈleɪbɚ / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə /; / ˈleɪbə / (BrE)
the workers in society, especially as a group or class, often organized in unions or considered as a political or economic force

Examples

Local businesses depend on seasonal labor for harvesting crops.

He has been a member of the Labour movement since his youth.
The new policy was criticized by Labour for weakening workers' rights.

  • synonym: union - an organized association of workers formed to protect and further their rights
  • synonym: workforce - all the people engaged in or available for work
