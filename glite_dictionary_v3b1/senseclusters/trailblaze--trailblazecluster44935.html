trailblaze

verb
to pioneer
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlˌbleɪz / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlˌbleɪz / (BrE)
to be the first to do something new or innovative, creating a path for others to follow

Examples

To trailblaze new ideas
She worked hard to trailblaze new methods in environmental science.
Many young artists hope to trailblaze in their creative fields.
The company aims to trailblaze by launching the first fully electric truck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follow - to come after or use the path made by someone else
  • synonym: innovate - to introduce new ideas or methods
  • synonym: pioneer - to be among the first to develop or use something new
