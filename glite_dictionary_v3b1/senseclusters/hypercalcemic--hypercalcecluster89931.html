hypercalcemic

noun
person with high blood calcium
9.3
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.kæl.ˈsiː.mɪk/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.kæl.ˈsiː.mɪk/ (BrE)
a person or animal who has an abnormally high level of calcium in the blood

Examples

Hypercalcemic individual
The doctor treated the hypercalcemic with intravenous fluids to lower their calcium levels.
A hypercalcemic may experience symptoms like confusion, muscle weakness, or dehydration.
Veterinarians sometimes see a hypercalcemic dog after it eats certain types of plants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypocalcemic - a person or animal with abnormally low calcium in the blood
