propyl group

noun
organic group from propane, C3H7–
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.pəl ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.pɪl ɡruːp / (BrE)
a monovalent organic group with the formula C3H7–, derived from propane by removing one hydrogen atom
