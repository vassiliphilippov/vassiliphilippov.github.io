lunatic

noun
insane or wildly foolish person
9.8 3.95/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀên…ôt…™k / (AmE), / ÀàluÀên…ôt…™k / (BrE)
(dated, offensive) A person who is insane or considered to be mentally ill; an extremely foolish or eccentric person.

Examples

A lunatic in an asylum
It's offensive to call someone a lunatic when referring to mental illness today.
People once believed that a lunatic was someone influenced by the phases of the moon.
The old castle was rumored to have been built by a lunatic centuries ago.
