honeybunch

noun
term of endearment
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌbʌntʃ / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌbʌntʃ / (BrE)
an affectionate term used to address someone you love or care about deeply

Examples

You are my honeybunch and I love you more every day.
