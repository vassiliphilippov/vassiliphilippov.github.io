street drug

noun
illegal drug used recreationally
7.4 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / strit dr åg / (AmE), / striÀêt dr åg / (BrE)
An illegal drug taken for recreational, non-medical purposes, often with mind-altering effects and potential for addiction or harm.

Examples

A street drug problem
Many teens are unaware of the dangers associated with using a street drug.
The police seized several types of street drug during the raid.
Treatment centers work to help people addicted to at least one street drug.

Synonyms

  • drug of abuse
    5.8 0.08/m
    drug of abuse can mean any substance taken nonmedically, while street drug refers to illegal recreational drugs sold on the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illicit drug - a drug forbidden by law; illegal drug
  • synonym: recreational drug - a drug (legal or illegal) used for pleasure rather than for medical reasons
