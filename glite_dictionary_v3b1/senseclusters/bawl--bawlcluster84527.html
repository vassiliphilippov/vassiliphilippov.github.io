Synonyms

  • blubber
    6.1 0.62/m
    blubber is noisy, messy crying where someone may speak while bawl is louder and more uncontrolled crying or shouting.
  • wail
    3.2 4.22/m
    wail is a long loud cry that shows grief or pain, while bawl is more general loud crying or shouting.
  • yell
    1.0 56/m
    yell is to shout loudly from anger or excitement, while bawl often means uncontrolled crying or loud shouting.
  • howl
    2.6 3.84/m
    howl is a long, animal-like or sorrowful cry, while bawl is loud, uncontrolled crying or shouting by a person.
