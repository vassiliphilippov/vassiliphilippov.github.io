sandspur

noun
prickly grass with spiny burs
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌspɝ / (AmE), / ˈsændˌspɜː / (BrE)
a grass native to the eastern United States and tropical America, known for its spikelets surrounded by prickly burs that can attach to skin or clothing

Examples

The sandspurs plant
After hiking through the field, I found several sandspurs stuck to my socks.
Be careful where you walk barefoot, as there are many sandspurs in this area.
The children learned to identify sandspur plants to avoid their painful burs.
