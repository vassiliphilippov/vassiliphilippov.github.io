masculinist

noun
male superiority advocate
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskjəlɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈmæskjʊlɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that men are inherently superior to women and should have social or political dominance

Examples

A masculinist thinker
The professor was criticized as a masculinist for his views on gender roles in society.
Some political groups have been accused of promoting masculinist ideologies in their campaigns.
Her article challenged the arguments made by several prominent masculinist writers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feminist - a person who supports equal rights for women
  • synonym: sexist - a person who discriminates based on sex, especially against women
