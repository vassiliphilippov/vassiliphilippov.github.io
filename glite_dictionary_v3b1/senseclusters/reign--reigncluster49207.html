reign

verb
to rule as monarch or sovereign
2.7
Pronunciation: / reɪn / (AmE), / reɪn / (BrE)
to rule as a monarch or sovereign, especially for a specified period

Examples

To reign as queen
For centuries, emperors reigned over vast empires stretching across continents.
Many believe that a just and fair king should reign with wisdom and compassion.
Queen Elizabeth II reigned over the United Kingdom for more than seventy years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abdicate - to give up the throne or sovereign power
  • synonym: rule - to exercise control or authority over a country or people
