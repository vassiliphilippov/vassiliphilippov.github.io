vandal

noun
person who intentionally damages property
Pronunciation: / ˈvændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvændl̩ / (BrE)
a person who deliberately damages or destroys public or private property

Examples

A vandal breaks windows
Police arrested a vandal who had smashed several car windows downtown.
The city installed surveillance cameras to catch any vandal targeting public parks.
The museum was shocked to find that a vandal had painted graffiti on its historic walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - a person who has committed a crime
  • antonym: protector - someone who keeps property safe from harm
