Examples

To declass a document

Synonyms

  • declassify
    6.1 1.34/m
    declassify is the formal act of removing security labels to make files public, while declass is used specifically for government secrecy.
  • unclassify
    7.6 0.01/m
    unclassify means removing an item from any category without implying secrecy, while declass always refers to removing security classification.
