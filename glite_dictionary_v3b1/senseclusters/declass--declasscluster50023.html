declass

verb
to remove classification
Pronunciation: / diːˈklæs / (AmE), / diːˈklɑːs / (BrE)
to remove the official security or secrecy classification from information or documents, making them unclassified

Examples

To declass a document
The agency decided to declass several old documents to increase transparency.
After much debate, the government agreed to declass the files related to the investigation.
Journalists requested that the authorities declass the report for public review.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classify - to assign a security status to information or documents
  • synonym: declassify - to officially declare information or documents no longer secret
