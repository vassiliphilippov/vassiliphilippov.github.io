semeion

noun
miraculous sign
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmaɪ.ɒn / (AmE), / sɪˈmiː.ɒn / (BrE)
a miraculous sign or wonder regarded as evidence of divine power, especially in biblical or religious contexts

Examples

A divine semeion
In the ancient text, the sudden appearance of light was described as a semeion from the heavens.
The priest spoke of the healing as a semeion that strengthened the faith of the community.
Many believers gathered to witness what they hoped would be a semeion during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miracle - an extraordinary event believed to be caused by divine power
  • synonym: omen - a sign thought to predict the future
