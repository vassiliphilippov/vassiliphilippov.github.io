give

verb
present as a gift
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪv / (AmE), / ɡɪv / (BrE)
to hand, offer, provide, or transfer something (such as an object, information, permission, or attention) to someone else

Examples

To give a gift
I decided to give my brother a book for Christmas.
She plans to give him her old bicycle as a birthday present.
We should give the teachers something to show our appreciation.
She gave the students extra homework as punishment for their behavior.
The judge gave him five years in prison for his crime.
The teacher gave a detention to everyone who was late.
Don’t give the dog any chocolate—it can be harmful to him.
Please give the baby some mashed vegetables if he's hungry.
The volunteers came to give soup and bread to the homeless.
