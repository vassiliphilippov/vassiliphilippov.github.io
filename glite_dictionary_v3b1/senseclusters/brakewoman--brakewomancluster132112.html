brakewoman

noun
female railroad employee managing train brakes
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a female railroad employee responsible for operating or maintaining a train's brakes

Examples

The brakewoman on train
As the first female brakewoman in the company, she inspired many others to join the railroad industry.
During emergencies, the brakewoman worked quickly to activate the safety mechanisms.
The brakewoman ensured the train stopped smoothly at every station.
