saddle

verb
put a saddle on an animal
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæd.əl / (AmE), / ˈsæd.əl / (BrE)
to put a saddle on (a horse or other animal) in preparation for riding

Examples

To saddle a horse
He quickly saddled his horse before the sun rose.
Make sure to saddle the new mare before the guests arrive.
They saddled the horses and set out on their journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unsaddle - to remove the saddle from a horse or animal
