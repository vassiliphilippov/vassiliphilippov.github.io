sorcerer

noun
a person who practices magic or sorcery
7.9 4.29/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…îrs…ôr…ôr / (AmE), / Ààs…îÀês…ôr…ô / (BrE)
a person who practices magic or sorcery, especially by using supernatural powers

Examples

A sorcerer uses magic
In ancient legends, a sorcerer often possessed secret knowledge of potions and charms.
The sorcerer cast a powerful spell over the village, causing fear among the townsfolk.
The story follows a young apprentice learning under a wise old sorcerer.

  • mage

  • conjurer
    9.0 0.07/m

  • magician
