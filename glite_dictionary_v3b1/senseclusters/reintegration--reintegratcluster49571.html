reintegration

noun
return to society
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌɪntəˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming part of a group, society, or whole again after being separated from it

Examples

Reintegration into society
The government launched new programs to support the reintegration of refugees into local communities.
Successful reintegration after a long absence can be challenging for many people.
The charity focuses on the reintegration of former soldiers into civilian life.
After years abroad, his reintegration into the local community was challenging but rewarding.
The government launched programs to support the reintegration of former prisoners into society.
Successful reintegration of the departments improved communication and productivity across the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusion - the state of being left out or not allowed to join a group
  • synonym: integration - the process of combining parts into a whole
  • synonym: rehabilitation - the process of helping someone return to a normal life after imprisonment or illness
  • synonym: reunion - the act of coming together again after separation
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
