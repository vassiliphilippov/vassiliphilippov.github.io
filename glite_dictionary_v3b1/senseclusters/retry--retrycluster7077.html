retry

verb
re-attempt a failed operation (computing)
2.7
Pronunciation: / riˈtraɪ /; / ˌriːˈtraɪ / (AmE), / riˈtraɪ /; / ˌriːˈtraɪ / (BrE)
to try to do something again, especially after failing or being interrupted

Examples

To retry the task
