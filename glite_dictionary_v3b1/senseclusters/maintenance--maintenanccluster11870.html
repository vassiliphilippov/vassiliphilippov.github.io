Synonyms

  • support payment
    3.5 0.5/m
    a support payment is a regular court-ordered sum for someone's living needs, while maintenance is the general legal term for that support.
  • alimony
    6.3 2/m
    alimony is money a court orders one spouse to pay after divorce, while maintenance can mean any family support payment.
  • child support
    2.7 5/m
    child support is money for the children's living costs paid to the custodial parent, while maintenance covers support for any family member.
