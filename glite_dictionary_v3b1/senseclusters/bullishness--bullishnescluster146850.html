bullishness

noun
muscular strength
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪʃnəs / (BrE)
a quality of having strong physical strength or a heavy, muscular build like a bull

Examples

Physical bullishness
His bullishness made him stand out among the other athletes on the team.
The wrestler's bullishness was obvious from his broad shoulders and powerful arms.
She admired the bullishness of the workers who carried heavy loads all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brawn - physical strength and muscular power
  • antonym: frailness - the state of being physically weak or delicate
