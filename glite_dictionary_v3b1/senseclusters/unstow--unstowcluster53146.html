unstow

verb
to remove from storage
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstoʊ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstəʊ / (BrE)
to remove something from where it has been stowed or stored

Examples

To unstow luggage
After landing, passengers unstowed their luggage from the overhead bins.
The crew quickly unstowed the emergency equipment during the drill.
He unstowed his tools from the back of the truck before starting work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stow - to put something away in a storage place
  • synonym: unpack - to remove items from a container or storage
