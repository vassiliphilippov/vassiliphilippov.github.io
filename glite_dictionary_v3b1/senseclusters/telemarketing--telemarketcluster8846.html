telemarketing

noun
selling by telephone; telephone marketing
4.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /tə.lə.ˈmɑːr.kɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /tel.ə.ˈmɑːr.kɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /tə.lə.ˈmɑː.kɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /tel.ə.ˈmɑː.kɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity of promoting and selling products or services by contacting potential customers by telephone

Examples

Telemarketing calls
I received several telemarketing calls offering me insurance products yesterday.
Many companies use telemarketing to reach new customers and boost their sales.
The government introduced rules to protect consumers from unwanted telemarketing calls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold calling - unsolicited calls to potential customers to sell products or services
  • synonym: direct marketing - marketing directly to consumers rather than through retailers
  • antonym: door-to-door - method of sales involving visits to people’s homes
