quagmire

noun
difficult situation
Pronunciation: / ˈkwæɡˌmaɪr / (AmE), / ˈkwæɡˌmaɪə / (BrE)
a difficult, complicated, or messy situation that is hard to escape or resolve

Examples

A legal quagmire
After the new regulations, the company found itself in a legal quagmire that lasted for months.
Negotiators struggled to find a way out of the political quagmire created by the disagreement.
She realized too late that her careless words had landed her in a social quagmire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dilemma - a situation requiring a difficult choice between options
  • synonym: predicament - a difficult or unpleasant situation
  • antonym: solution - an answer to a problem or difficult situation
