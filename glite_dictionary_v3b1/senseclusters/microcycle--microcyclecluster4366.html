microcycle

noun
short repeating cycle
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
a short, repeating period or cycle, especially used in training programs or biological processes

Examples

A microcycle plan
The coach designed a microcycle to help athletes recover between competitions.
In biology, a microcycle can refer to a brief phase in a microorganism's life.
Each microcycle in the training plan lasts one week and focuses on different skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cycle - a series of events that are regularly repeated in the same order
  • antonym: macrocycle - a long-term cycle, especially in training or planning
