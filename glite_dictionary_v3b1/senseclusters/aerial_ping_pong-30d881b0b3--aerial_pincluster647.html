aerial ping pong

noun
an airborne version of ping pong
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriəl pɪŋ pɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈeəriəl pɪŋ pɒŋ / (BrE)
a game similar to ping pong, played with flying discs, balloons, or other objects in the air, often in an informal or recreational setting

Examples

Aerial ping pong game

You can play aerial ping pong indoors with balloons to avoid breaking anything.
