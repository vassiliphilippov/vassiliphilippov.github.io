renationalize

verb
to restore citizenship
8.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈnæ.ʃən.ə.laɪz/ /; / /rɪ.ˈnæ.ʃən.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /riː.ˈnæ.ʃən.ə.laɪz/ /; / /rɪ.ˈnæ.ʃən.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to restore a person's nationality or citizenship that was previously lost or revoked

Examples

To renationalize a citizen
The government decided to renationalize individuals who had lost their citizenship decades ago.
After the law changed, many people applied to renationalize and regain their former nationality.
She was relieved when the authorities agreed to renationalize her after years of being stateless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: denaturalize - to revoke someone's citizenship
  • synonym: naturalize - to grant citizenship to someone not previously a citizen
