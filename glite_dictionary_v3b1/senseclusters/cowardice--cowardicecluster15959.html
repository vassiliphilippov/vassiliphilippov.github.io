cowardice

noun
lack of courage
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊ.ər.dɪs / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.ə.dɪs / (BrE)
lack of courage to face danger, difficulty, or pain; fearfulness in the face of adversity

Examples

Act of cowardice
He was accused of cowardice when he refused to defend his friend.
Her cowardice prevented her from speaking out against the injustice she witnessed.
The general would not tolerate cowardice in his army.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • antonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one
  • synonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
