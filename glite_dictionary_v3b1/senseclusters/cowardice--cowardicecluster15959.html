Synonyms

  • pusillanimity
    9.9 0.05/m
    pusillanimity is a formal, bookish word for weak courage and lack of resolve, while cowardice is the everyday term.
  • cravenness
    9.9 0.05/m
    cravenness is a mean, harsh word for shameful fear and lack of bravery, while cowardice is a general name for fear.
  • dastardliness
    9.9 0.00/m
    dastardliness feels old-fashioned and moralizing, pointing to despicable cowardly acts, while cowardice simply names fear or lack of courage.
