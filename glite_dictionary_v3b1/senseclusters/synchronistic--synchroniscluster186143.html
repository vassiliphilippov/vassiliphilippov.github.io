synchronistic

adjective
meaningful coincidences
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋkrəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to meaningful coincidences or connections between events that appear related but have no clear causal link.

Examples

Synchronistic coincidences
Many people find synchronistic events fascinating and look for hidden meanings in them.
The author described a series of synchronistic encounters that changed her life.
He believed the synchronistic timing of their meeting was more than just chance.
