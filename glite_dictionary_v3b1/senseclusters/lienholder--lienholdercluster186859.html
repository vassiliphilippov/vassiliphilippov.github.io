lienholder

noun
holder of legal claim
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈliːn.hoʊl.dər/ /; / /ˈliː.ən.hoʊl.dər/ (AmE), /ˈliːn.həʊl.də/ /; / /ˈliː.ən.həʊl.də/ (BrE)
a person or organization that has a legal right to keep possession of property until a debt is paid

Examples

The lienholder on car
The bank is the lienholder on her car until she finishes paying the loan.
If you default on your mortgage, the lienholder can repossess your house.
Before selling the property, he had to get permission from the lienholder.

Related Terms
