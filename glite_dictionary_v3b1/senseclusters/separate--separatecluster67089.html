separate

verb
to differentiate or distinguish
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛp.əˌreɪt /; / ˈsɛpəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɛp.əˌreɪt /; / ˈsɛpəreɪt / (BrE)
to recognize, show, or arrange the difference between things; to sort or distinguish

Examples

To separate the types
Experts often separate the various species by looking at small differences in their leaves.
It can be difficult to separate the facts from opinions in news articles.
Young children sometimes struggle to separate imagination from reality.
Researchers separated the data by age groups for the analysis.
The librarian will separate the books into fiction and non-fiction sections.
When you separate items by color, it makes sorting laundry much easier.

  • synonym: differentiate - to show or recognize difference between things
  • synonym: distinguish - to recognize or point out a difference
