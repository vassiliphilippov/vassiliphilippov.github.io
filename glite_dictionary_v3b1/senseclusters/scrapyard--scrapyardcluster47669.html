scrapyard

noun
place for scrap metal
2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskræp.jɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈskræp.jɑːd/ (BrE)
a place where old or unwanted vehicles, machinery, or metal are collected, dismantled, and recycled or sold for parts

Examples

A scrapyard for cars
The old truck was finally taken to the scrapyard after years of use.
He found several useful parts at the local scrapyard for his restoration project.
Many abandoned vehicles end up rusting away in a scrapyard outside the city.
The old car was towed to a scrapyard where it was taken apart for parts.
He found a rare motorcycle part at the local scrapyard after searching for weeks.
Many vehicles end up in a scrapyard when they are no longer roadworthy.
The old car was towed to the scrapyard after it stopped working.
Many valuable metals can be recovered at a scrapyard and sold for profit.
He found a rare engine part while searching through the scrapyard.

  • synonym: junkyard - a place where discarded or scrap items are collected
  • synonym: recycling center - a facility where materials are processed for reuse
