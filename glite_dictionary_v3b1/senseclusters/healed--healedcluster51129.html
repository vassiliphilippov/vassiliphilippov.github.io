healed

adjective
no longer ill or injured
1.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / hiÀêld / (AmE), / hiÀêld / (BrE)
No longer suffering from illness or injury; restored to health or wholeness.

Examples

Fully healed wound
After months of treatment, her broken leg was finally healed.
He felt healed both physically and emotionally after his long recovery.
The doctor said the wound looked completely healed and no longer needed a bandage.

Synonyms

  • cured
    2.7 0.00/m
    cured is a medical term meaning the disease is removed, while healed is broader, often used for wounds or emotional recovery.
  • recovered
    3.1 0.00/m
    recovered often means regaining strength or normal health over time, while healed means fully restored to health or wholeness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cured - freed from illness or disease
