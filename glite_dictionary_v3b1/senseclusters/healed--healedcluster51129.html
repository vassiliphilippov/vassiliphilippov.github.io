healed

adjective
no longer ill or injured
Pronunciation: / hiːld / (AmE), / hiːld / (BrE)
No longer suffering from illness or injury; restored to health or wholeness.

Examples

Fully healed wound
After months of treatment, her broken leg was finally healed.
He felt healed both physically and emotionally after his long recovery.
The doctor said the wound looked completely healed and no longer needed a bandage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cured - freed from illness or disease
  • antonym: injured - harmed or wounded; not fully healthy
  • synonym: recovered - returned to a normal state of health
