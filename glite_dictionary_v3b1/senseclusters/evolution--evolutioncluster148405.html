Synonyms

  • organic evolution
    4.7 0.03/m
    organic evolution is gradual biological change in living species over time, while evolution is the general scientific name for that process.
  • phylogenesis
    9.9 0.01/m
    phylogenesis traces the evolutionary development and diversification of a species group, while evolution covers all natural change across generations.
  • evolutionary change
    4.4 0.56/m
    evolutionary change refers to small step-by-step transformations, while evolution specifically names the biological process of species change over generations.
  • phylogeny
    9.9 0.64/m
