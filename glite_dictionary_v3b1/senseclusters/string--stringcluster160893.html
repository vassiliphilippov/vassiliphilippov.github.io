string

noun
cord for tightening or closing an opening
8.2
Pronunciation: / strɪŋ / (AmE), / strɪŋ / (BrE)
a thin piece of cord or fiber used for tying, fastening, or tightening things

Examples

A string for tying
He tightened the hood of his jacket by pulling the string.
She lost the string from her sweatpants and had to use a shoelace instead.
The bag closes securely with a simple string at the top.
A ball of string was left on the kitchen counter.
He replaced the broken string on his kite.
Tie the package together with a piece of string.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cord - a long, slender, flexible material, typically for tying or fastening
  • degree higher: rope - a thick, strong string of fibers twisted together
  • degree lower: thread - a thin strand of fiber, usually for sewing
