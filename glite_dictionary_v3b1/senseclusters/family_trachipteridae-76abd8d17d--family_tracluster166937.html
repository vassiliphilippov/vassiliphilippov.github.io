family Trachipteridae

noun
a family of ribbonfishes (Trachipteridae)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌtrækɪptəˈraɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌtrakɪptəˈraɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of elongated, ribbon-like marine fishes known as ribbonfishes, characterized by their laterally compressed bodies and long dorsal fins.
