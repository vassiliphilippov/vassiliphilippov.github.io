cryptically

adverb
in a mysterious or ambiguous way
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪptɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkrɪptɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is mysterious, ambiguous, or difficult to understand.

Examples

To speak cryptically
His message was written so cryptically that only those close to him understood it.
She answered the journalist's question cryptically, leaving everyone curious about her true intentions.
When asked where he was going, he smiled cryptically and walked away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptic - having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure
  • antonym: straightforwardly - in a clear and direct manner
