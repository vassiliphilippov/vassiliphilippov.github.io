hydrometeorological

adjective
water and weather
8.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.drə.miː.tɪ.ə.rə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /haɪ.drə.miː.tɪ.ə.rə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to the study or effects of water (including rain, snow, or ice) and weather conditions

Examples

Hydrometeorological forecast
The hydrometeorological forecast predicted heavy rainfall and possible river flooding in the region.
Scientists use hydrometeorological data to assess the risk of droughts and floods.
A hydrometeorological station measures both rainfall and atmospheric conditions for research purposes.
The hydrometeorological report warned of possible flooding due to heavy rainfall.
Scientists use hydrometeorological data to predict severe weather events like hurricanes.
Accurate hydrometeorological measurements are essential for managing water resources in the region.

  • synonym: meteorological - relating to the science of weather and atmosphere
