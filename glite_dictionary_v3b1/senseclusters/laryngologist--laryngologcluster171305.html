laryngologist

noun
throat specialist
Pronunciation: / ˌlærɪnˈɡɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌlærɪnˈɡɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the larynx and throat

Examples

The laryngologist doctor
The laryngologist recommended surgery to treat the patient's vocal cord problem.
After losing his voice, he scheduled an appointment with a laryngologist for a thorough examination.
A laryngologist can help diagnose chronic throat pain and hoarseness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: general practitioner - a doctor who treats common medical conditions
  • synonym: otolaryngologist - a doctor specializing in ear, nose, and throat disorders
