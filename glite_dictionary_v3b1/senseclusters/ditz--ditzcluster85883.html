ditz

noun
silly or forgetful person
Pronunciation: / dɪts / (AmE), / dɪts / (BrE)
a person, especially a woman, who is silly, scatterbrained, or forgetful

Examples

A silly ditz
Everyone laughed when the ditz forgot her own birthday again.
He called himself a ditz after losing his keys for the third time this week.
Despite being a bit of a ditz, she always brings joy to the group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airhead - a person who is not very intelligent or is easily confused
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is disorganized or forgetful
