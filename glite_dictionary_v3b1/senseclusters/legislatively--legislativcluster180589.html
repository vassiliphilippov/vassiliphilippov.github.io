legislatively

adverb
by means of legislation
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛdʒɪˈsleɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪtɪvli / (BrE)
by means of legislation; in a manner relating to or determined by legislation

Examples

Changed legislatively
The minimum wage was increased legislatively after much debate in parliament.
The policy was changed legislatively rather than through executive action.
These rights can only be restricted legislatively and not by decree.
