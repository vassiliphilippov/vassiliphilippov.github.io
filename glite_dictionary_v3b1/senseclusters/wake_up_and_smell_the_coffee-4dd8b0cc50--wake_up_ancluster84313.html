wake up and smell the coffee

verb
become aware of reality
4.5
Pronunciation: / weɪk ʌp ænd smɛl ðə ˈkɑːfi / (AmE), / weɪk ʌp ənd smɛl ðə ˈkɒfi / (BrE)
to become aware of an unpleasant reality or to face the facts of a situation

Examples

To wake up and smell the coffee
If we don't wake up and smell the coffee, we'll keep repeating the same mistakes.
It's time for you to wake up and smell the coffee before things get worse.
She told him to wake up and smell the coffee about his reckless spending habits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bury your head in the sand - to ignore or refuse to acknowledge a problem
  • synonym: face the music - to accept the unpleasant consequences of one's actions
