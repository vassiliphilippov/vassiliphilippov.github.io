deal-breaker

noun
decisive negative factor
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdiːlˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a factor or issue that causes someone to reject an agreement, relationship, or offer

Examples

A deal-breaker in dating
For some people, smoking is a complete deal-breaker when choosing a partner.
The lack of remote work options was a deal-breaker for many job applicants.
If the apartment does not allow pets, that will be a deal-breaker for us.
