top

verb
finish or complete by adding something extra
3.2 5.39/m
Pronunciation: / tÉ‘p / (AmE), / tÉ’p / (BrE)
to finish or complete something by adding a final detail or flourish; to be the final or most impressive part

Examples

The gala dinner will top off a week of festivities.
