top

verb
finish or complete by adding something extra
1.2
Pronunciation: / tɑp / (AmE), / tɒp / (BrE)
to finish or complete something by adding a final detail or flourish; to be the final or most impressive part

Examples

You can top your presentation with a memorable closing statement.
He gave an inspiring closing address that topped the conference.
Her dazzling performance topped the evening's celebrations.
The gala dinner will top off a week of festivities.
