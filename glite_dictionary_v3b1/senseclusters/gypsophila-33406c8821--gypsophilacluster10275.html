Gypsophila

noun
a genus of small-flowered herbs (baby's-breath)
Pronunciation: / dʒɪpˈsɑfɪlə /; / dʒɪpˈsɑːfɪlə / (AmE), / dʒɪpˈsɒfɪlə / (BrE)
a genus of Mediterranean herbaceous plants in the carnation family, known for their small white or pink flowers; commonly called baby's-breath
