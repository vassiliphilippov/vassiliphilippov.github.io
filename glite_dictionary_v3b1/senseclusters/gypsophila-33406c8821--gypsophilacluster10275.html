Gypsophila

noun
a genus of small-flowered herbs (baby's-breath)
8.9 9.3
Pronunciation: /dʒɪp.sə.ˈfɪl.ə/ (AmE), /dʒɪp.sə.ˈfɪl.ə/ (BrE)
