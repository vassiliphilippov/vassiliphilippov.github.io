Triatoma

proper noun
kissing bug genus
Pronunciation: / traɪˈætəmə / (AmE), / traɪˈætəmə / (BrE)
a genus of blood-sucking insects, commonly called kissing bugs, some of which can transmit Chagas disease

Examples

Triatoma species found
Scientists discovered a new species of Triatoma in the Amazon rainforest.
The bite of a Triatoma can sometimes transmit serious diseases to humans.
Health officials warned residents about the presence of Triatoma in the area.
