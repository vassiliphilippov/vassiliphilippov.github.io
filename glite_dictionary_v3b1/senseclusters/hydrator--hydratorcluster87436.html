hydrator

noun
person giving water
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdreɪtə / (BrE)
a person who provides water or drinks to others to keep them hydrated

Examples

Marathon hydrator
During the marathon, a hydrator handed out water to the runners at every checkpoint.
The team assigned a hydrator to make sure everyone stayed refreshed during the hike.
At the sports event, the hydrator was busy filling bottles for the athletes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: server - a person who serves food or drinks to others
  • synonym: water bearer - a person who carries or supplies water to others
