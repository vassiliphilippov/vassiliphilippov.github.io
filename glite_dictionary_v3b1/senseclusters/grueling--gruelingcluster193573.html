grueling

adjective
extremely demanding and exhausting
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡruːəlɪŋ /; / ˈɡruː.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Extremely demanding and exhausting; requiring great effort to the point of exhaustion, especially physical effort.

Examples

A grueling hike
After a grueling hike through the mountains, they finally reached the campsite.
The team finished a grueling shift late last night but felt proud of their effort.
Training for the marathon was a grueling experience that tested her endurance every day.
After a gruelling day of hiking up the mountain, they finally reached the summit.
She went through a gruelling training program to prepare for the marathon.
The tennis match was so gruelling that both players could barely stand by the end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arduous - involving or requiring strenuous effort; difficult and tiring
  • antonym: easy - not difficult or demanding
  • synonym: punishing - physically or mentally demanding and tiring
