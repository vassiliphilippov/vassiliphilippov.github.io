nosebag

noun
bag for feeding an animal, worn over the muzzle
3.4 7.7
Pronunciation: /noʊz bæg/ (AmE), /nəʊz bæg/ (BrE)
A bag, usually made of canvas, placed over an animal's muzzle to allow it to eat while preventing spillage or competition from other animals.

Examples

A nosebag for animals
Each animal was given a separate nosebag to avoid fighting over food.
She filled the nosebag with oats and secured it to the pony's head.
The horse munched quietly from its nosebag as it rested by the stable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feedbag - a bag used for feeding horses or cattle, usually attached to the animal's head
