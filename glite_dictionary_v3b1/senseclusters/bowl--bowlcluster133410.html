bowl

noun
amount held by a bowl
Pronunciation: / boʊl / (AmE), / bəʊl / (BrE)
the amount that a bowl can hold; a quantity contained in a bowl

Examples

A bowl of soup
He poured himself another bowl of soup.
She ate a bowl of cereal before leaving for work.
The recipe calls for one bowl of fresh strawberries.
