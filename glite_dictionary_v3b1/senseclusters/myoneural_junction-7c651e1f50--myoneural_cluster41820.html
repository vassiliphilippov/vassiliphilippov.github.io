myoneural junction

noun
junction where nerve meets muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.oʊˈnʊr.əl ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.əʊˈnjʊə.rəl ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən / (BrE)
The point of contact where a nerve fiber meets the muscle it controls, allowing nerve impulses to trigger muscle contraction.

Examples

The myoneural junction point
Certain toxins block transmission at the myoneural junction, causing paralysis.
Damage to the myoneural junction can lead to muscle weakness and fatigue.
Researchers studied diseases that affect the myoneural junction in order to develop new treatments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neuromuscular junction - the synapse or junction between a nerve cell and a muscle cell
