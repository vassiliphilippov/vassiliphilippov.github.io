aurify

verb
make or turn golden
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪˌfaɪ / (BrE)

Examples

To aurify a statue
Artists often attempt to aurify everyday objects in their work, giving them a luminous, precious quality.
The poet described how autumn leaves aurify the landscape each year.
The setting sun seemed to aurify the clouds with a brilliant golden light.
