demoness

noun
cruel or vicious woman
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːməˌnɛs / (AmE), / ˈdiːmənɪs / (BrE)
a woman who is considered extremely cruel, vicious, or malicious, often used as an insult or in a figurative sense

Examples

Cruel demoness
The villain in the novel was described as a heartless demoness who showed no mercy to her enemies.
Some tabloids unfairly labeled the actress a demoness after the heated argument went public.
He warned his friends about his ex-boss, calling her a real demoness in the workplace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - a kind and good person
  • synonym: fiend - a wicked or cruel person
