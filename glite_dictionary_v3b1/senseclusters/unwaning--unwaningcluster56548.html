unwaning

adjective
not fading
8.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
not decreasing, fading, or becoming weaker; remaining strong or constant over time

Examples

Her unwaning optimism
Her unwaning optimism inspired everyone around her, even during difficult times.
The team's unwaning dedication led them to achieve remarkable results year after year.
Despite many setbacks, his unwaning passion for music never diminished.

  • synonym: persistent - continuing firmly or obstinately over a period of time
