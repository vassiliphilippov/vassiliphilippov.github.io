tidy sum

noun
a considerable amount of money
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdi sʌm / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdi sʌm / (BrE)
a fairly large amount of money, especially one that is considered sizable or pleasant to receive

Examples

A tidy sum of money
After years of saving, he finally accumulated a tidy sum in his bank account.
She inherited a tidy sum from her grandmother, enough to buy a new house.
Winning the lawsuit awarded them a tidy sum in damages.
