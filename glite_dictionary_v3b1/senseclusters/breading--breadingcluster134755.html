breading

noun
coating for food
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
a dry mixture (like breadcrumbs or flour) or the layer it forms on food when used to coat it before cooking

Related Terms

  • antonym: batter - a liquid mixture of flour and other ingredients used to coat food before frying
  • synonym: crumbs - small pieces of bread, often used as a coating for food
  • synonym: crust - the hard outer layer formed on food during baking or frying
