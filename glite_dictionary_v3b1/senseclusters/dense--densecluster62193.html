dense

adjective
thick, hard-to-pass-through growth
4.9 2.4
Pronunciation: / dɛns / (AmE), / dɛns / (BrE)
(of vegetation or growth) difficult to pass through because the plants or objects are closely packed together.

Examples

Dense forest
The forest was so dense that hardly any sunlight reached the ground.
Thick mist rose above the dense undergrowth, making the trail nearly invisible.
We had to cut a path through the dense jungle to reach the village.
