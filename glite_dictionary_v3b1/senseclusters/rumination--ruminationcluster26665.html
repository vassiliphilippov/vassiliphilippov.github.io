rumination

noun
lengthy, thoughtful consideration
Pronunciation: / ˌruːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌruːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
Careful, prolonged, and thoughtful consideration of something.

Examples

Deep rumination
During a quiet afternoon, he found himself in deep rumination about his future.
Her rumination on the problem led to a creative solution.
Long periods of rumination can sometimes make it harder to reach a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contemplation - deep reflective thought
  • synonym: reflection - serious thought or consideration
