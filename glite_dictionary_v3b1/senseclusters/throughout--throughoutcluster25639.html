throughout

adverb
from beginning to end; during the whole period
Pronunciation: / θruˈaʊt / (AmE), / θruːˈaʊt / (BrE)
in or during the whole period of a particular event, activity, or process; from beginning to end

Examples

Calm throughout the day
She remained calm throughout the stressful interview process.
The orchestra played beautifully throughout the entire performance.
There was tension in the room throughout the meeting.
