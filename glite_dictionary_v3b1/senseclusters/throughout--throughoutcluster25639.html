throughout

from beginning to end; during the whole period

Synonyms

  • all along
    1.5 13/m
    all along says something happens continuously from the start, while throughout means during the whole period from beginning to end.
  • all the while
    2.9 3/m
    all the while describes something happening during the entire time, while throughout means it happens in every part of that period.
  • over
    1.0 170/m
    over can mean during a whole time or across an area, while throughout stresses every part of a period.
  • end-to-end
    4.6 0.1/m
    end-to-end describes covering every stage from start to finish, while throughout simply means during the whole period.
  • around
    1.0 17/m
    around often means near or about a time or place, while throughout means during every part of a period.
