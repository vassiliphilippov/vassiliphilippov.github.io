seatpost

noun
bicycle saddle tube
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsiːt.poʊst/ (AmE), /ˈsiːt.pəʊst/ (BrE)
the tube on a bicycle that connects the saddle to the frame and allows adjustment of the seat height

Examples

Bicycle seatpost tube
He adjusted the seatpost to make the bicycle more comfortable for his height.
The mechanic recommended replacing the rusty seatpost before the next long ride.
A carbon fiber seatpost can help reduce the overall weight of your bike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frame - the main structure of a bicycle to which components are attached
  • synonym: saddle - the seat on a bicycle where the rider sits
