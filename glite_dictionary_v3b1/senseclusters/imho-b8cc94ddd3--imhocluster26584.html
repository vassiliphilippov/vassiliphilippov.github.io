IMHO

noun
online opinion abbreviation
Pronunciation: / aɪ ɛm eɪtʃ oʊ / (AmE), / aɪ ɛm eɪtʃ əʊ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'in my humble opinion', used to introduce a personal opinion in online messages or forums

Examples

A quick IMHO
He ended his comment with IMHO to show he was just sharing his thoughts.
Many people use IMHO in online discussions to soften their opinions.
She wrote IMHO before giving her advice on the forum.
