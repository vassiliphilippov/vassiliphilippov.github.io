Il Duce

noun
epithet for an authoritarian leader
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / il \u02c8du\u02d0t\u0283e\u026a / (AmE), / il \u02c8du\u02d0t\u0283e\u026a / (BrE)
(informal, figurative) an epithet used to describe a political leader who behaves like a dictator; used to compare someone to Benito Mussolini.

Examples

A new Il Duce
Several columnists warned that the prime minister was becoming the new Il Duce with strict media controls.
Protesters chanted that he was an Il Duce, accusing him of silencing opposition and removing checks on power.
The historian argued that comparing modern leaders to Il Duce risks oversimplifying complex political situations.
