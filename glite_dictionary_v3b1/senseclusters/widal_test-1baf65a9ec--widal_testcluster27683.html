Widal test

noun
test to detect typhoid and salmonella infections
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwiː.dəl test/ /; / /ˈwiː.dɑːl test/ (AmE), /ˈwiː.dəl test/ /; / /ˈwiː.dɑːl test/ (BrE)

Examples
