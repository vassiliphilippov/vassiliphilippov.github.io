Jehoshaphat

proper noun
biblical place of judgment
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhɑːʃəˌfæt / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɒʃəˌfæt / (BrE)
a place-name in the Hebrew Bible, often symbolizing a location of divine judgment, especially the 'Valley of Jehoshaphat'

Examples

Valley of Jehoshaphat
Many prophecies mention the Valley of Jehoshaphat as the site of final judgment.
Pilgrims visited the area believed to be Jehoshaphat to reflect on its biblical significance.
The story describes nations gathering in Jehoshaphat for a day of reckoning.
