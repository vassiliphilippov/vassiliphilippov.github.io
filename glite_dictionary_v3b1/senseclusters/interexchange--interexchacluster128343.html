interexchange

adjective
between exchanges
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌɪntərɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
relating to communication, connections, or transactions that occur between separate exchanges or carriers, especially in telecommunications.

Examples

Interexchange carrier network
The company offers interexchange services that connect calls between different telephone networks.
New regulations require transparency in interexchange fees charged by telecom carriers.
Competition in the interexchange market has led to lower long-distance call rates.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraexchange - relating to communication or transactions within a single exchange
