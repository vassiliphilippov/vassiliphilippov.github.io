snippet

noun
a small piece or extract
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪp.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsnɪp.ɪt / (BrE)
a small piece or brief extract of something, especially one that has been cut out or separated from a larger whole

Examples

A snippet of information
He found a snippet of information that helped solve the mystery.
She shared a snippet of her latest poem during the meeting.
The article included a snippet from the interview with the artist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fragment - a small part broken off or detached
