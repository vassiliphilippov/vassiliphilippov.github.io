Sauropoda

noun
suborder of large long-necked dinosaurs
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɑː.ˈrɑːp.ə.də/ /; / /sɔː.ˈrɑːp.ə.də/ (AmE), /sɔː.ˈrɒp.ə.də/ (BrE)
a group of very large, plant-eating dinosaurs with long necks, long tails, and four thick legs, such as Brachiosaurus and Diplodocus
