neologist

noun
creator of new words or expressions
Pronunciation: / niˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / niˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A person who creates new words or expressions, especially in language or lexicography.

Examples

A neologist creates words
As a renowned neologist, she contributed dozens of new technical terms to the scientific lexicon.
Journalists sometimes act as informal neologists, shaping language by popularizing new expressions.
The neologist was often criticized for introducing words that never caught on in everyday speech.

  • synonym: neologism - a newly coined word or expression
