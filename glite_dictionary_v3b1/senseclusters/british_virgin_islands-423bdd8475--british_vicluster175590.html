British Virgin Islands

proper noun
offshore financial jurisdiction
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɪt.ɪʃ ˈvɜːr.dʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz/ (AmE), /ˈbrɪt.ɪʃ ˈvɜː.dʒɪn ˈaɪ.ləndz/ (BrE)
the legal and regulatory jurisdiction of the British Virgin Islands as an offshore financial centre, often used to refer to its company-registration, trust, and other financial services.

Examples

British Virgin Islands company registration
Many multinational firms register their holding companies in the British Virgin Islands to reduce tax liabilities.
The lawyer advised setting up a trust in the British Virgin Islands to protect family assets overseas.
Journalists reported that the leaked records revealed thousands of shell companies registered in the British Virgin Islands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offshore financial centre - a jurisdiction offering financial services such as incorporation and trusts, often with favourable regulation or tax rules
  • synonym: tax haven - a country or territory that provides low taxes and financial secrecy, often used for company registration and asset protection
