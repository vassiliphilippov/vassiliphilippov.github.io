giggle

noun
a light, silly, or nervous laugh
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡl / (BrE)
a light, silly, or nervous laugh

Examples

A quiet giggle
A sudden giggle escaped from the group of children.
He struggled to suppress a nervous giggle before giving his speech.
She tried to hide her giggle during the serious meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chuckle - to laugh quietly or to oneself
  • degree higher: laugh - to show amusement with vocal sounds; general term for laughter
