sun porch

noun
room enclosed with glass for sunlight
1.0
Pronunciation: / sʌn pɔrtʃ / (AmE), / sʌn pɔːtʃ / (BrE)
a room enclosed largely with glass, designed to let in sunlight and provide exposure to the sun

Examples

A sun porch room
The sun porch offers beautiful views of the garden and plenty of warmth in winter.
They converted their old patio into a spacious sun porch for year-round relaxation.
We had breakfast on the sun porch while soaking up the morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunroom - a glass-enclosed room, typically attached to a house, designed to let in sunlight
