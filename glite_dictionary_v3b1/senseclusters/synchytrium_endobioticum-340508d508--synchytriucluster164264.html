Synchytrium endobioticum

noun
fungus causing potato wart disease
Pronunciation: / sɪŋˈkaɪtrɪəm ˌɛndoʊbaɪˈɑːtɪkəm / (AmE), / sɪŋˈkɪtrɪəm ˌɛndəʊbaɪˈɒtɪkəm / (BrE)
a species of fungus that causes potato wart disease, affecting potato tubers and reducing crop yield
