donut

noun
ring-shaped chart (data visualization)
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.nət/ /; / /ˈdoʊ.nʌt/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.nət/ /; / /ˈdəʊ.nʌt/ (BrE)
A ring-shaped chart (also called a doughnut chart) with a hollow center used to show proportions of a whole, similar to a pie chart.

Examples

Donut chart
The report uses a donut to compare sales percentages across product lines.
She preferred a donut chart because its center space lets her show totals clearly.
During the presentation he used a small donut to display each region's share of web traffic.
The dashboard uses a doughnut chart to show market share by region.
She preferred a doughnut visualization to a pie chart when presenting sales percentages to the team.
For the report, they included three small doughnuts to compare device usage across age groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donut chart - alternative name for a doughnut-shaped chart used to display proportions.
  • synonym: doughnut chart - alternate name for a donut chart; a ring-shaped chart with a central hole
  • synonym: pie chart - a circular chart divided into sectors to show relative proportions
