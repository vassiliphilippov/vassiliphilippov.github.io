snowbound

adjective
unable to leave due to heavy snow
4.3 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsnoʊ.baʊnd/ /; / /ˈsnəʊ.baʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈsəʊ.baʊnd/ (BrE)
unable to leave a place because of heavy snowfall; confined or shut in by snow

Examples

Snowbound by heavy snow
Several motorists became snowbound when the highway was suddenly blocked by drifting snow.
The small village was snowbound for three days after the blizzard.
We spent the weekend snowbound in a remote cabin in the mountains.
