horny frog

noun
lizard with hornlike spines, western North America
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrni frɔg / (AmE), / ˈhɔːni frɒg / (BrE)
a small insect-eating lizard with hornlike spines on its head and spiny scales on its body, native to western North America

Examples

The horny frog lizard
Naturalists studied the horny frog to learn more about its unique method of hunting insects.
Several species of horny frog can squirt blood from their eyes as a defense.
The horny frog is well-camouflaged in the deserts of the American Southwest.
