Aleurites

proper noun
genus of tropical trees (candlenut genus)
Pronunciation: / əˈlʊr.ɪˌtiːz / (AmE), / aljʊˈraɪtiːz / (BrE)
A genus of tropical trees in the spurge family, including the candlenut tree, known for their large oily seeds.
