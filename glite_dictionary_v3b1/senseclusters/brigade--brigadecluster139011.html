brigade

noun
organized group
4.0
Pronunciation: / brɪˈɡeɪd / (AmE), / brɪˈɡeɪd / (BrE)
a group of people organized for a specific purpose or activity, especially in emergency services or the military

Examples

Fire brigade
The fire brigade responded quickly to the emergency.
A volunteer brigade helped clean up the park after the festival.
The army formed a new brigade to handle the mission overseas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individual - a single person, not part of a group
  • synonym: squad - a small group of people organized for a particular purpose
  • synonym: team - a group of people working together towards a common goal
