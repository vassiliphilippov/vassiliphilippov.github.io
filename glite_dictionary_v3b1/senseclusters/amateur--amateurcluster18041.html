amateur

noun
unpaid athlete
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæ.mə.tʃ.ɜːr/ /; / /ˈæ.mə.t.ɜːr/ /; / /ˈæ.mə.t.ər/ (AmE), /ˈæ.mə.tə/ (BrE)
a person who does an activity, especially a sport or hobby, for pleasure and not as a job or profession

Examples
