giftless

adjective
without gifts
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪftləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɪftləs / (BrE)
not having received any gifts or presents; without gifts

Examples

Giftless birthday
After the party, he was the only giftless guest and felt a little left out.
The children were giftless this year because their parents could not afford presents.
She remained giftless despite her hopes for a surprise on her birthday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gifted - having received gifts or presents
  • synonym: unrewarded - not given a reward or gift
