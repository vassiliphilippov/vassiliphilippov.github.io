illumination

noun
spiritual enlightenment or awareness
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˌluːməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌljuːmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a state or condition of spiritual enlightenment or awareness, often regarded as being granted by a divine source or higher power

Examples

Spiritual illumination
After years of meditation, she finally experienced illumination.
Many mystics describe illumination as a moment of profound inner clarity.
The poet wrote about his search for illumination through contemplation and prayer.

  • synonym: enlightenment - a state of having gained spiritual knowledge or insight
