Xenosauridae

noun
family of Mexican lizards (Xenosaurus)
Pronunciation: / ziːnəʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / zɛn.oʊˌsɔːrəˈdiː / (AmE), / ziːnəʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / zɛn.əʊˌsɔːrɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a family of lizards native to Mexico, containing only one genus (Xenosaurus), known for their scaly skin and distinct appearance
