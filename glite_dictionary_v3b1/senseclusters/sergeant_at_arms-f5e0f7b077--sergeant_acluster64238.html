sergeant at arms

noun
official who maintains order; executive officer
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.dʒənt æt ˈɑːrmz / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.dʒənt ət ˈɑːmz / (BrE)
an officer appointed by a legislative body, court, or other organization to keep order and enforce rules or commands

Examples
