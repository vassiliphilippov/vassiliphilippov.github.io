Synonyms

  • determinative
    8.2 0.63/m
    determinative is a formal term used to show what decides an outcome, while decisive is a general word for a turning point.
  • pivotal
    4.9 12/m
    pivotal describes something central that causes a major change, while decisive means having a strong effect on the result.
  • crucial
    3.6 41/m
    crucial is used for something extremely important for success, while decisive focuses on directly deciding the final outcome.
  • critical
    4.0 93/m
    critical often describes a dangerous or urgent point in a situation, while decisive refers to what decides the final result.
  • conclusive
    4.8 3.71/m
    conclusive suits evidence that ends doubt and proves a point, while decisive marks a turning factor that decides outcomes.

  • synonym: crucial - decisively important or critical
  • antonym: indecisive - not having or showing the ability to make decisions quickly and effectively; not producing a definite result
