underground

adjective, adverb
secret; hidden from authorities
2.5 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.graʊnd/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.graʊnd/ (BrE)
secret or hidden; done in a way to avoid being discovered, especially by authorities

Examples
