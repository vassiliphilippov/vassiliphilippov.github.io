witch alder

noun
deciduous shrub with showy flowers, genus Fothergilla
6.9
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ ˈældɚ / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ˈældə / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub of the genus Fothergilla, native to the southeastern United States, known for its brushlike white spring flowers and bright red or orange autumn leaves.
