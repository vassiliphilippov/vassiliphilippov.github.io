nondependent

adjective
not reliant
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛndənt / (BrE)
not depending on or controlled by something else; independent or not contingent on other factors

Examples

Nondependent organization
The organization remained nondependent on government support throughout the year.
Her decision was completely nondependent on her friends' opinions.
A nondependent system can function even if one part fails.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dependent - relying on or controlled by something else
  • synonym: independent - not influenced or controlled by others
