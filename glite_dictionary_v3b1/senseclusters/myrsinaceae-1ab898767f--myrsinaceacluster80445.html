Myrsinaceae

noun
family of tropical trees and shrubs
Pronunciation: / mɪrˌsɪˈneɪsiː /; / mɪrˌsaɪnəˈsiː.iː / (AmE), / mɪˌsɪnˈeɪsiː /; / mɪˌsɪnəˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
a family of mainly Old World tropical trees and shrubs, some of which are found in Florida (botany)
