chupacabra

noun
source of trouble
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃuːpəˈkɑːbrə / (AmE), / ˌtʃuːpəˈkɑːbrə / (BrE)
a person or thing that causes sudden or mysterious damage, loss, or trouble, often in a surprising way

Examples

A real chupacabra problem
The new computer virus was a real chupacabra for businesses trying to protect their data.
For months, the mysterious leak acted as a chupacabra in the old apartment building.
The sudden frost became a chupacabra for the region's early crops this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessing - something that brings good or helps
  • synonym: menace - a person or thing that is likely to cause harm
  • synonym: scourge - a source of great pain and suffering
