channeling

noun
directing along a path
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of directing something along a particular path or through a channel

Examples

Channeling water flow
Proper channeling of water prevents flooding in the fields.
The channeling of resources to the right departments improved efficiency.
Effective channeling of energy can help athletes perform at their best.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diverting - the act of changing the direction of something
  • synonym: guiding - the act of showing or directing the way
