substance

noun
essential or most important part
1.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌb.stəns/ (AmE), /ˈsʌb.stəns/ (BrE)
the most important or essential meaning or main idea of something spoken, written, or argued

Examples

The substance of the speech
Let's focus on the substance of the proposal instead of minor details.
She summarized the substance of the meeting for those who arrived late.
The substance of his speech was that more funding is needed for education.

The substance of her argument was lost in the lengthy introduction.

It's important to focus on the substance of her argument rather than her tone.
