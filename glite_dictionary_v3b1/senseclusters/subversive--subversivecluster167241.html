subversive

noun
person seeking to overthrow authority
8.1 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / s…ôbÀàv…úrs…™v / (AmE), / s…ôbÀàv…úÀês…™v / (BrE)
a person who seeks to undermine or overthrow an established system, especially a government or political authority

Examples

A subversive leader
He was accused of being a subversive simply because he criticized the authorities.
Police arrested several suspected subversives believed to be plotting against the regime.
The government cracked down on the subversive who was organizing protests in the capital.
