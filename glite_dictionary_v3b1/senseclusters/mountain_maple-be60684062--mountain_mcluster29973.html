mountain maple

noun
a North American shrubby maple
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn ˈmeɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn ˈmeɪpəl / (BrE)
a small shrubby maple tree of eastern North America, noted for its scarlet autumn foliage

Examples

The mountain maple tree
Hikers often spot mountain maple growing along shady woodland trails.
The mountain maple prefers cool, moist environments at higher elevations.
The leaves of the mountain maple turn a brilliant red in the autumn.
