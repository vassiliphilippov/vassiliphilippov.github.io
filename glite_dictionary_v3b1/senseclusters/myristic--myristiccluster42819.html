myristic

adjective
from myristic acid
9.0
Pronunciation: / maɪˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / mɪˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from myristic acid, a type of fatty acid found in some fats and oils

Examples

Myristic acid
The laboratory identified a myristic compound in the coconut oil sample.
Some soaps are made using myristic acid for its cleansing properties.
Researchers studied the effects of myristic fats on human health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palmitic - relating to or derived from palmitic acid, another type of fatty acid
  • synonym: stearic - relating to or derived from stearic acid, a saturated fatty acid
