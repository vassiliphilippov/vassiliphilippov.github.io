dining car

noun
railway car for serving meals
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪnɪŋ kɑr / (AmE), / ˈdaɪnɪŋ kɑː / (BrE)
a railway passenger car where meals are served and eaten during a journey

Examples

The dining car serves meals
After a long afternoon, she strolled to the dining car for dinner and coffee.
The train's dining car offered a full breakfast menu with table service.
We met some interesting travelers while sharing a table in the dining car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restaurant car - another term for a dining car
  • antonym: sleeping car - railway car with beds for passengers to sleep
