myriameter

noun

Examples

A myriameter unit
In the early days of the metric system, the myriameter was sometimes used to measure large distances.
The railway stretched nearly one myriameter across the continent, a remarkable feat in its time.
Today, kilometers and meters have replaced the myriameter in most scientific and everyday use.
Although rarely used today, the myriametre was once part of the metric system.
The distance between the two cities was measured as five myriametres.
The scientist recorded the length as 2 myriametres, equivalent to 20 kilometers.
