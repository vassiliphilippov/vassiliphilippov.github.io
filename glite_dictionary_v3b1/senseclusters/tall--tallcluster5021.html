tall

adjective
impressively difficult; very challenging
5.1 6.0
Pronunciation: /tɔːl/ (AmE), /tɔːl/ (BrE)
(of a task, demand, or order) impressively difficult or challenging to achieve or fulfill.

Examples

It would be a tall task to convince them to change their minds now.
Winning the championship after losing so many games is certainly a tall challenge.
