overglaze

noun
ceramic decorative coating
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌɡleɪz / (BrE)
a decorative or protective layer applied over the glaze on ceramics and fired again to fix the design
