overglaze

noun
ceramic decorative coating
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.ɡleɪz/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.ɡleɪz/ (BrE)
a decorative or protective layer applied over the glaze on ceramics and fired again to fix the design

Examples

A colorful overglaze
The artist applied a delicate overglaze to enhance the colors of the ceramic plate.
Gold overglaze was used to create intricate patterns on the antique vase.
After the first firing, a blue overglaze was added and the piece was fired again.
The artist applied a colorful overglaze to enhance the vase's appearance.
After the first firing, a thin overglaze was added for extra shine.
Museum staff carefully restored the antique plate's overglaze to its original brilliance.

  • synonym: glaze - a glassy coating applied to ceramics for decoration or protection
  • antonym: underglaze - a decorative layer applied to ceramics before glazing
