podsolic soil

noun
soil in temperate/cold, moist climates with a leached layer
6.7 7.8
Pronunciation: /pɑːd.ˈzɑːl.ɪk sɔɪl/ /; / /pə.ˈzɑːl.ɪk sɔɪl/ (AmE), /pɒd.ˈzɒl.ɪk sɔɪl/ /; / /pə.ˈzɒl.ɪk sɔɪl/ (BrE)
