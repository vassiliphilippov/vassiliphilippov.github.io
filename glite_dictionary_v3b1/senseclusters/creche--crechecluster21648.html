creche

noun
nativity scene display
Pronunciation: / kreɪʃ / (AmE), / kreɪʃ / (BrE)
A model or tableau representing the scene of Christ's birth in the stable at Bethlehem, typically displayed at Christmas.

Examples

Christmas creche
A handmade wooden creche stood on the mantel throughout December.
Every Christmas, the church sets up a beautiful creche near the altar.
The children enjoyed placing the figures into the creche as part of the holiday tradition.
