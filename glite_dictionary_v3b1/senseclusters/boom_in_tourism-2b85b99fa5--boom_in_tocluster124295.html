boom in tourism

noun
rapid increase in tourist numbers
Pronunciation: / buːm ɪn tʊˈrɪzəm / (AmE), / buːm ɪn ˈtʊərɪzəm / (BrE)
a period in which there is a rapid and significant increase in the number of tourists visiting a place

Examples

A boom in tourism growth
Many local businesses benefited from the boom in tourism last year.
The city experienced a boom in tourism after the new airport was built.
The government launched several campaigns to encourage a boom in tourism during the summer months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline in tourism - period in which the number of tourists decreases sharply; opposite of boom in tourism
  • synonym: tourism boom - significant growth in tourist activity; synonym for boom in tourism
