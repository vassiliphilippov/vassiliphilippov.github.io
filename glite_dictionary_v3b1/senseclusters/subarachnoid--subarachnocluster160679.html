subarachnoid

adjective
beneath arachnoid membrane
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈræk.nɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌsʌbəˈræk.nɔɪd / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the arachnoid membrane of the brain or spinal cord

Examples

Subarachnoid hemorrhage
A subarachnoid hemorrhage can be a life-threatening medical emergency.
Doctors detected blood in the subarachnoid space during the scan.
The patient was treated for a subarachnoid bleed after the accident.
