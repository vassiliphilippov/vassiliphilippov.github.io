killer whale

noun
large predatory black-and-white whale
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪlər weɪl / (AmE), / ˈkɪlə weɪl / (BrE)
A large, predatory black-and-white toothed whale (Orcinus orca) with a tall dorsal fin, found in cold and temperate seas.

Examples

A killer whale pod
A pod of killer whales was spotted off the coast yesterday.
The killer whale is a top predator in the ocean ecosystem.
The killer whale is known for its striking black and white coloring.
