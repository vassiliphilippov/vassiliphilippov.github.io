mentalist

noun
mind reader performer
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who claims to have or performs with special mental or psychic powers, such as mind reading or telepathy

Examples

Famous mentalist show
The mentalist stunned everyone by revealing the number a volunteer was thinking of.
At the party, a famous mentalist entertained guests with his mind-reading tricks.
Many people wonder how a mentalist can appear to know their private thoughts.
The detective hired a mentalist to help solve the mysterious case.
In the movie, the main character is a mentalist who can read people's thoughts.
Many people are fascinated by stories featuring a mentalist with supernatural abilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illusionist - a performer who creates illusions, often as part of a magic act
  • synonym: magician - a person who performs magic tricks for entertainment
  • synonym: psychic - a person who claims to have supernatural mental abilities
