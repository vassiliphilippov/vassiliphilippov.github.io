nationless

adjective
without national ties
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪʃənləs / (AmE), / ˈneɪʃənləs / (BrE)
not belonging to, limited by, or defined by any single nation; international or transnational in scope

Examples

Nationless company
The organization operates as a nationless entity, serving people across the globe.
A nationless approach to business can help companies reach a wider audience.
Some cryptocurrencies are designed to be nationless, not controlled by any government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international - involving or relating to two or more countries
  • antonym: national - relating to or characteristic of a nation
