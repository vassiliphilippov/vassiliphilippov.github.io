sayyid

noun
Muslim family name
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪjɪd / (AmE), / ˈsaɪjɪd /; / ˈseɪjɪd / (BrE)
a family name or title used by some Muslim families, especially for male descendants of the Prophet Muhammad
