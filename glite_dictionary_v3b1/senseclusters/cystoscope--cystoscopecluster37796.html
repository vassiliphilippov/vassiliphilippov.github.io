cystoscope

noun
bladder examination tool
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəskəʊp / (BrE)
a medical instrument used to look inside the bladder through the urethra

Examples

A cystoscope device
The urologist inserted a cystoscope to inspect the patient's bladder for any abnormalities.
A cystoscope allows doctors to diagnose and treat problems inside the urinary bladder.
After the procedure with the cystoscope, the patient was able to go home the same day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endoscope - an instrument for examining the interior of a bodily organ
