tergiversate

verb
be deliberately ambiguous to mislead
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜr.dʒɪ.vɚ.seɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.dʒɪ.və.seɪt / (BrE)

She was known to tergiversate whenever she was asked uncomfortable questions.
The politician continued to tergiversate when pressed for a clear answer.
