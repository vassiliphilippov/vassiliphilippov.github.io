Jewish Bolshevism

noun
discredited theory linking Jews to Bolshevism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːɪʃ boʊlˈʃɛvɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːɪʃ bɒlˈʃɛvɪzəm / (BrE)
The controversial theory or claim, originating in early 20th-century Europe, that there was a close connection between Jewish people and Bolshevik communism; widely discredited and critiqued for its inaccuracy and use in antisemitic propaganda.

Examples

Jewish Bolshevism conspiracy theory
He wrote an article debunking the conspiracy theory known as Jewish Bolshevism.
Many historians have shown that Jewish Bolshevism is a baseless and harmful stereotype.
The myth of Jewish Bolshevism was propagated by some groups in the early twentieth century.
