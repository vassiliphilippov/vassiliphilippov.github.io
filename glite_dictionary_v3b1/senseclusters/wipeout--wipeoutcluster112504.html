wipeout

noun
a fall or crash in sports
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪpˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpˌaʊt / (BrE)
a sudden, often dramatic fall or crash during a sporting activity, such as from a bicycle, skis, or surfboard

Examples

Surfing wipeout
Despite an early wipeout, he got back on his bike and finished the race.
He took a massive wipeout while surfing the big waves this morning.
The video replay showed her dramatic wipeout just before the finish line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crash - a violent fall or collision, especially in sports or vehicles
  • synonym: spill - a fall, especially from a vehicle or in sports
