hypercritic

noun
excessive fault-finder
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who criticizes excessively or finds fault with everything

Examples

A hypercritic complains
The film director was tired of dealing with the same hypercritic at every premiere.
As a hypercritic, she rarely found a book that met her high standards.
No matter how well the team performed, the hypercritic always pointed out their flaws.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirer - a person who regards someone or something with respect or approval
  • synonym: critic - a person who judges or evaluates something
  • synonym: faultfinder - someone who is always looking for mistakes or flaws
