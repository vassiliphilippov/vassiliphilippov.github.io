soft sell

noun
subtle or gentle sales approach
4.7
Pronunciation: / sɔft sɛl / (AmE), / sɒft sɛl / (BrE)
A method of selling that is gentle, subtle, or suggestive, rather than forceful or aggressive.

Examples

A soft sell approach
She preferred the soft sell over aggressive marketing tactics.
The advertisement used a soft sell to appeal to potential buyers.
The company's soft sell approach made customers feel at ease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hard sell - an aggressive, direct sales approach
