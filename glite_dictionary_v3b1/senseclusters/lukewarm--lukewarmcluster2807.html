lukewarm

adjective
unenthusiastic or indifferent
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔkˌwɔrm / (AmE), / ˈluːkˌwɔːm / (BrE)
showing little enthusiasm, interest, or conviction; unenthusiastic or indifferent

Examples

Lukewarm response
He gave a lukewarm endorsement of the candidate, failing to show real support.
Her performance received only lukewarm applause from the audience.
The proposal was met with a lukewarm response from the committee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiastic - feeling or showing intense interest or excitement
  • synonym: halfhearted - showing little enthusiasm or effort
