Nation of Islam

noun
Black nationalist religious movement
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪ.ʃən əv ˈɪz.ləm/ /; / /ˈneɪ.ʃən əv ɪsˈlɑːm/ (AmE), /ˈneɪ.ʃən əv ˈɪz.lɑːm/ /; / /ˈneɪ.ʃən əv ɪsˈlɑːm/ (BrE)
A religious and political organization in the United States that combines elements of traditional Islam with Black nationalist ideas, advocating for the empowerment and independence of Black Americans.
