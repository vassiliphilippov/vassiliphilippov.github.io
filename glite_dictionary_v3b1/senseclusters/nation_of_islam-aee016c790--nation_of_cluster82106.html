Nation of Islam

noun
Black nationalist religious movement
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪʃən əv ˈɪslɑːm / (AmE), / ˈneɪʃən əv ˈɪslɑːm / (BrE)
A religious and political organization in the United States that combines elements of traditional Islam with Black nationalist ideas, advocating for the empowerment and independence of Black Americans.

Examples

The Nation of Islam movement
He joined the Nation of Islam to support Black empowerment and spiritual development.
The Nation of Islam played a significant role in the civil rights movement during the 1960s.
The beliefs of the Nation of Islam differ in several respects from mainstream Islamic teachings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Black nationalism - Advocacy for social, political, and economic independence for Black people
  • degree lower: Islam - World religion founded by Muhammad
