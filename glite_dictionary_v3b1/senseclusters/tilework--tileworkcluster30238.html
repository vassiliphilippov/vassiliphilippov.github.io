tilework

noun
decorative tile designs
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪl.wɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪl.wɜːk/ (BrE)
the art, process, or craft of creating decorative patterns or surfaces using tiles

Examples

Tilework craft
The ancient palace was famous for its intricate tilework covering the walls and floors.
She admired the colorful tilework in the entryway of the historic building.
Modern architects often incorporate tilework to add texture and color to their designs.

Good tilework can greatly increase the value and appearance of a home.
