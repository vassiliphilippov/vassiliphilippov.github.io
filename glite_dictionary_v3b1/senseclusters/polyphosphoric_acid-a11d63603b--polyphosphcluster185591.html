polyphosphoric acid

noun
a group of oxyacids of phosphorus
6.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.fɑːs.ˈfɑːr.ɪk ˈæs.əd/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.fɒs.ˈfɒr.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ (BrE)
any of a series of oxyacids of phosphorus that contain more than one phosphoric acid unit linked together, often used in chemical synthesis and as a dehydrating agent
