belated

adjective
happening or arriving late
Pronunciation: / bɪˈleɪtɪd / (AmE), / bɪˈleɪtɪd / (BrE)
Happening or arriving later than expected or usual.

Examples

A belated birthday gift
He received a belated birthday gift from his friend.
She sent a belated apology after missing the meeting.
The couple offered their belated congratulations after hearing the news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: late - occurring or coming after the usual or expected time
  • antonym: timely - occurring at a suitable or opportune time
