manufacturable

adjective
able to be produced
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn.jəˈfæk.tʃɚ.ə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌmæn.jʊˈfæk.tʃər.ə.bəl / (BrE)

Examples

The engineers ensured the new product was easily manufacturable using existing equipment.
A design that is not manufacturable can lead to delays and increased costs.
We need to check if this prototype is actually manufacturable before moving to mass production.
