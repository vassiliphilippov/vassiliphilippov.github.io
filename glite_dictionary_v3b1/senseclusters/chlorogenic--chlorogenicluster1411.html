chlorogenic

adjective
from chlorogenic acid
Pronunciation: / ˌklɔːrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌklɔːrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from chlorogenic acid, a compound found in certain plants such as coffee beans

Examples

Chlorogenic acid compound
Coffee beans are a rich source of chlorogenic compounds.
Scientists studied the chlorogenic content in different types of fruit.
The chlorogenic properties of the extract may contribute to its antioxidant effects.

  • synonym: phenolic - relating to or derived from phenol or its derivatives
