Marchantia

noun
genus of liverworts (Marchantiaceae)
Pronunciation: / mɑːrˈkænʃiə / (AmE), / mɑːˈkæntɪə /; / mɑːˈkæntiə / (BrE)
a genus of liverworts in the family Marchantiaceae, known for reproducing asexually by gemmae and having stalked antheridiophores
