cowbell

noun
bell worn by cow for locating it
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌbɛl / (BrE)
A bell hung around the neck of a cow so that it can be easily located, especially in open countryside.

Examples

A cowbell on neck
He attached a new cowbell to the old dairy cow's neck.
The sound of a cowbell is often associated with rural life.
We could hear the cowbell ringing from across the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bell - a hollow device that makes a ringing sound when struck
