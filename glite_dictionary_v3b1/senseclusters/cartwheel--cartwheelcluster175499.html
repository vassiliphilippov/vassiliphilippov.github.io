cartwheel

noun
sideways acrobatic revolution
3.7 0.67/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ërtÀåwiÀêl / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêtÀåwiÀêl / (BrE)
an acrobatic movement in which a person turns sideways with their hands and feet touching the ground alternately, resembling the motion of a wheel

Examples

To do a cartwheel
Excited by the news, the children did cartwheels on the lawn.
Learning to do a cartwheel takes balance and confidence.
She performed a perfect cartwheel across the gym mat during practice.
