Mami

proper noun
Mesopotamian goddess of birth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːmi / (BrE)
A Mesopotamian goddess, worshipped as a mother goddess and associated with childbirth, also known as Ninkhursag.

Archaeologists discovered a temple where Mami was worshipped alongside other deities.
