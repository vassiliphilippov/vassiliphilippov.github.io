dimerization

noun
formation of a dimer
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪmərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdaɪmərəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which two molecules combine to form a dimer

Examples

Protein dimerization
The dimerization of certain proteins is essential for their biological activity.
Researchers studied how temperature affects the dimerization of the chemical compound.
Abnormal dimerization can lead to malfunctioning enzymes in the body.
