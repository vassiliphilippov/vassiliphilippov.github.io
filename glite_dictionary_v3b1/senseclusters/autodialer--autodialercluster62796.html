autodialer

noun
automatic calling device
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌdaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌdaɪlə / (BrE)
a device or software that automatically calls many telephone numbers, often for telemarketing or sending notifications
