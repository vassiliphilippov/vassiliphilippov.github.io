photolithographic

adjective
using light for patterning
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the process of using light to transfer patterns onto surfaces, especially in electronics manufacturing

Examples

Photolithographic process
Modern microchips are produced using photolithographic techniques to create tiny circuits.
The photolithographic process allows for precise patterning on silicon wafers.
Advances in photolithographic technology have enabled smaller and faster electronic devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lithographic - relating to lithography, a printing process using a flat surface
  • synonym: photographic - relating to the process of making photographs
