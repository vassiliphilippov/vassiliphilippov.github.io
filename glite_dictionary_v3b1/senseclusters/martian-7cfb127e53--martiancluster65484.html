Martian

adjective
relating to Mars or its inhabitants
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːʃən / (BrE)
of or relating to the planet Mars or its (especially fictional) inhabitants.

Examples

Martian soil
Scientists are studying possible Martian microbes found in meteorites.
She imagined what life would be like in a Martian colony.
The rover was designed to withstand the harsh Martian climate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Venusian - of or relating to the planet Venus or its inhabitants
  • antonym: terrestrial - of or relating to the Earth or its inhabitants
