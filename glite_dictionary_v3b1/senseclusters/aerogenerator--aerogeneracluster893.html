aerogenerator

noun
wind-powered electricity generator
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛroʊdʒəˈnɛreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌeərəʊdʒəˈnəːreɪtə / (BrE)
A machine that generates electricity by harnessing energy from the wind, commonly known as a wind turbine.

Examples

A aerogenerator machine
After installing the aerogenerator, the village had a steady supply of renewable electricity.
Engineers are researching ways to make aerogenerators more efficient in low-wind conditions.
The new farm will use state-of-the-art aerogenerators to harness wind energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wind turbine - a device that converts kinetic energy from the wind into electrical power
