spokesman

noun
male official representative
5.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈspoʊks.mən/ (AmE), /ˈspəʊks.mən/ (BrE)
a man who officially speaks for a group, organization, or another person

Examples

Government spokesman
A government spokesman announced new regulations at the press conference.
He served as the main spokesman for the environmental group.
The company's spokesman declined to comment on the merger rumors.
The company appointed a new spokesman to address the media after the incident.
As the official spokesman, he delivered the statement on behalf of the organization.
The spokesman clarified the government's position during the press conference.
