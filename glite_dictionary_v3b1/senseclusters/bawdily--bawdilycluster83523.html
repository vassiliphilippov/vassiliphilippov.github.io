bawdily

adverb
in a lewd or indecent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪli / (BrE)
In a manner that is humorously indecent or lewd.

Examples

To laugh bawdily
She laughed bawdily at his risqué story, showing no signs of embarrassment.
The actor delivered his lines bawdily, adding a mischievous edge to the scene.
The comedian joked bawdily about topics that made the audience both laugh and blush.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lewdly - in a lewd manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a modest or reserved manner
