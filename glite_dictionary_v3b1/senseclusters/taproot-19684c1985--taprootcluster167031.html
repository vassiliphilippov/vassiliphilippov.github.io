proper noun
Bitcoin protocol upgrade (2021)

A 2021 Bitcoin protocol upgrade (a backward-compatible soft fork) that improves privacy and scripting capabilities by enabling Schnorr signatures and more flexible smart-contract constructions.

Examples

Taproot upgrade
Bitcoin's 2021 upgrade, Taproot, improved transaction privacy and enabled more flexible smart contracts.
Miners signalled support for Taproot before the soft fork activated on the Bitcoin network.
Developers praised Taproot for enabling Schnorr signatures that reduce transaction size and improve privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Schnorr signature - a digital-signature scheme that Taproot enabled for Bitcoin to improve efficiency and aggregation
  • synonym: soft fork - a backward-compatible change to a blockchain protocol; the category of upgrade Taproot used
