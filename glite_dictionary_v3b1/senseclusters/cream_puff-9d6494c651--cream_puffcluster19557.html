cream puff

noun
pastry filled with cream or custard
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːm ˌpʌf / (AmE), / ˈkriːm ˌpʌf / (BrE)
A light, hollow pastry filled with whipped cream or custard, often topped with powdered sugar or chocolate.

Examples

A cream puff dessert
His grandmother taught him how to make homemade cream puffs filled with vanilla custard.
She ordered a cream puff and a cup of coffee at the bakery.
The cream puffs were the highlight of the dessert table at the party.
