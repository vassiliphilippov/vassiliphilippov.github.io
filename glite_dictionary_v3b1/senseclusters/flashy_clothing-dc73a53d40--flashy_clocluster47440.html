flashy clothing

noun
showy or ostentatious clothing
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃi ˈkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflæʃi ˈkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
clothing that is designed to attract attention by being very bright, expensive-looking, or showy

Examples

Flashy clothing style
Celebrities often wear flashy clothing to stand out on the red carpet.
His flashy clothing drew everyone's attention at the party.
She prefers comfort over flashy clothing when choosing her outfits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain clothing - clothing that is simple and not designed to attract attention
  • synonym: showy clothing - clothing that is designed to be very noticeable
