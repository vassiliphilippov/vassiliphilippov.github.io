day

noun
the light period between sunrise and sunset
1.0 97/m
Pronunciation: / deÉª / (AmE), / deÉª / (BrE)
The period of time during which it is light outside, between sunrise and sunset.

Examples

Sunny day
Owls sleep in the day and hunt at night.
She prefers to work during the day when the sun is shining.
The park is usually crowded in the day but quiet after sunset.

Synonyms

  • daylight
    1.0 15/m
    daylight is the natural sunlit brightness you see outside, while day is the whole period when it is light.

Related Terms
