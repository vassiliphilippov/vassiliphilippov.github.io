Dahomeyan

adjective
of Dahomey
Pronunciation: / dəˈhoʊmiən / (AmE), / dəˈhəʊmiən / (BrE)
relating to Dahomey, a former kingdom and French colony in West Africa, or its people, culture, or history

Examples

Dahomeyan culture
The exhibition featured traditional Dahomeyan textiles and royal regalia.
Historians study Dahomeyan society to understand West African kingdoms of the past.
Many Dahomeyan customs influenced the culture of present-day Benin.

Related Terms
