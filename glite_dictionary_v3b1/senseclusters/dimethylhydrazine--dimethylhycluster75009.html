dimethylhydrazine

noun
rocket fuel chemical
Pronunciation: / daɪˌmɛθəlhaɪˈdreɪziːn / (AmE), / daɪˌmiːθəlhaɪˈdræziːn /; / daɪˌmɛθəlhaɪˈdreɪziːn / (BrE)
a colorless, toxic, and flammable chemical compound used as rocket fuel, in chemical manufacturing, and in laboratory research
