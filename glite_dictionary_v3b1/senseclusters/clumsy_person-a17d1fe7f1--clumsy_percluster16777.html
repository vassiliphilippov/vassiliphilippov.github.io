clumsy person

noun
person who is physically awkward
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmzi ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ˈklʌmzi ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who moves awkwardly or lacks physical coordination

Examples

A clumsy person
As a clumsy person, she often tripped over her own feet.
Being a clumsy person, he spilled coffee on his shirt almost every morning.
He was such a clumsy person that he knocked over three glasses at dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: graceful person - a person with smooth, coordinated movements
  • synonym: klutz - a person who is physically awkward or prone to accidents
