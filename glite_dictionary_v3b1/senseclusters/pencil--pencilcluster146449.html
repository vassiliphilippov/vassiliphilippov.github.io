pencil

noun
writing instrument with a graphite core
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.səl / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.səl / (BrE)
a thin, usually cylindrical writing instrument made of a rod of graphite or a similar substance encased in wood or another material, used for writing or drawing

Examples

A pencil for writing
He forgot to bring a pencil to the exam and had to borrow one from a friend.
She sketched the portrait with a sharp pencil on plain white paper.
The artist preferred using a pencil for his initial drawings before adding color.
