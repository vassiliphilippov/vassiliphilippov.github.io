native holly

noun
Australian evergreen shrub with winged pods
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪt̬ɪv ˈhɑli / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪv ˈhɒli / (BrE)
a low, spreading evergreen shrub of southern Australia with triangular or heart-shaped leaves, orange-yellow flowers, and flat winged seed pods

Examples

The native holly shrub
Botanists studied the habitat of the native holly to understand its adaptation to local conditions.
The native holly is popular in Australian gardens for its attractive foliage and unusual seeds.
The garden features several native holly shrubs that bloom with orange-yellow flowers in spring.
