screenful

noun
amount filling a screen
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːnfʊl / (AmE), / ˈskriːnfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of text, data, or images that fills one computer or device screen at a time

Examples

One screenful of text
He read one screenful of the article before taking a break.
The report was so long that it took several screenfuls to read it all.
Please review each screenful of data carefully for any mistakes.
