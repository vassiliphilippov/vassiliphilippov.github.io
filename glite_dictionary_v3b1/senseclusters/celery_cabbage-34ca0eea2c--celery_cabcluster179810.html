celery cabbage

noun
elongated leafy vegetable, also called Napa cabbage
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛləri ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɛləri ˈkabɪdʒ /; / ˈsɛləri ˈkæbɪdʒ / (BrE)
an East Asian vegetable with an elongated head and crisp, celery-like stalks, used in cooking

Examples
