A family of chiefly herbaceous flowering plants, commonly known as the geranium family, including the genus Geranium.

Examples

The Geraniaceae family
Botanists often study the Geraniaceae because of their diverse leaf shapes and flower colors.
Members of Geraniaceae are found in temperate and tropical regions around the world.
The Geraniaceae family includes well-known garden plants like pelargoniums and cranesbills.
