rambutan

noun
Malayan fruit with soft spines
5.5 7.5
Pronunciation: / ræmˈbuːtɑn / (AmE), / ræmˈbuːtæn / (BrE)
A bright red oval tropical fruit from Southeast Asia, covered with soft spines and having pleasantly acidic flesh.

Examples
