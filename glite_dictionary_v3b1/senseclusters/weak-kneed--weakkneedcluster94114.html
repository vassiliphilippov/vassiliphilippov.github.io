weak-kneed

adjective
physically or emotionally unsteady
7.0 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / wik nid / (AmE), / wiÀêk niÀêd / (BrE)
feeling physically weak or unsteady, especially because of strong emotion or nervousness

Examples

Feel weak-kneed
After running the marathon, he felt weak-kneed and had to sit down.
She became weak-kneed at the sight of her idol.
The unexpected compliment made him weak-kneed.
