schnorrer

noun
person who habitually seeks handouts
9.9 9.5
Pronunciation: / ʃnɔɹɚ / (AmE), / ʃnɒɹə / (BrE)
a person who habitually seeks to obtain things, especially money or food, from others without offering anything in return; a scrounger

Tired of being treated like a schnorrer, she finally offered to pay for lunch.
