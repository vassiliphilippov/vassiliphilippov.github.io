distributively

adverb
in a distributive manner; separately to parts
5.1 7.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪb.ju.tɪv.li /; / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪb.jʊ.tɪv.li /; / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that applies to each member or item separately, rather than as a whole

Examples

Apply distributively to elements
In mathematics, the operation applies distributively to each element of the set.
The force acts distributively along the entire length of the beam.
The rewards were shared distributively among all team members.
Consider the rights of the parties distributively, not as a collective entity.
Each item should be treated distributively rather than combined.
The law applies distributively to each member of the group.

  • antonym: collectively - in a collective manner; as a group
