deferentially

adverb
in a respectful, deferential manner
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛfəˈrɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ˌdɛfəˈrɛnʃəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows respect or submission to someone or something

Examples

To bow deferentially
He bowed deferentially to the elderly professor before entering the room.
She listened deferentially as her mentor shared advice from years of experience.
The staff spoke deferentially to the hospital director during the meeting.
He stepped aside deferentially to let the guests pass first.
She nodded deferentially when her manager entered the room, eager to show her respect.
The assistant always spoke deferentially to the board members during meetings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insolently - in a manner that shows a lack of respect
  • synonym: respectfully - in a way that shows respect
