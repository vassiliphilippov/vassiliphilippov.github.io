exhume

verb
dig up a buried body
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzum / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzjuːm / (BrE)
to dig up (a body) from the ground, especially for examination or reburial

Examples

To exhume a body
Authorities had to exhume several graves during the investigation.
The family requested that the remains not be exhumed without their consent.
The medical examiner decided to exhume the body for further testing.

Related Terms
