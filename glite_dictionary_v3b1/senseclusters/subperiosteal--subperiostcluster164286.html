subperiosteal

adjective
beneath periosteum
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbpɛriˈɑstiəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbpɛriˈɒstɪəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the periosteum, the membrane covering a bone

Examples

Subperiosteal abscess
A subperiosteal abscess can develop after a bone infection spreads beneath the periosteum.
The dentist recommended a subperiosteal implant for patients with insufficient jawbone height.
During surgery, the doctor carefully inserted the device into the subperiosteal space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subcutaneous - located or applied under the skin
