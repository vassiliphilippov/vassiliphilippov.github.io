gratuitous

adjective
done without good reason or cause

done without a good reason or justification; unnecessary or uncalled for

Examples

A gratuitous insult
His comment was a gratuitous insult that served no purpose.
The manager's criticism seemed entirely gratuitous and unfair.
There was a scene of gratuitous violence in the film that many found disturbing.
She was angered by his gratuitous comment about her appearance.
The movie included a gratuitous plot twist that added nothing to the story.
The violence in the film was criticized for being gratuitous and excessive.

Synonyms

  • uncalled for
    5.1 0.72/m
    uncalled for is unnecessary or inappropriate in a situation, while gratuitous describes something done without good reason or justification.
  • unwarranted
    7.0 2.62/m
    unwarranted is not justified or deserved and often used in formal contexts, while gratuitous implies unnecessary or uncalled for.
  • needless
    3.0 8.28/m
    needless means not necessary or required in a plain way, while gratuitous suggests something done without a good reason or justification.

  • synonym: unnecessary - not needed; not essential
  • synonym: unwarranted - not justified or authorized
