flanging

noun
forming a flange
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈflæn.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfleɪn.dʒɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈflæn.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfleɪn.dʒɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of forming or attaching a flange, a projecting rim or edge, to a workpiece, especially in metalworking or pipe construction

Examples

Flanging metal edges
The factory uses automated machines for flanging metal sheets into precise shapes.
Proper flanging ensures that the pipes fit securely and prevent leaks.
After flanging, the edges of the ductwork were much stronger and easier to connect.
