beaded lizard

noun
a venomous lizard of western Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdɪd ˌlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪd ˌlɪzəd / (BrE)
a large venomous lizard of western Mexico, distinguished by its black and yellow beadlike scales
