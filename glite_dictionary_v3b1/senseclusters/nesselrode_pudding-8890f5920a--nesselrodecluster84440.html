Nesselrode pudding

noun
rich frozen dessert with chestnuts and fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛsəlˌroʊd ˈpʊdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɛsəlˌrəʊd ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a rich frozen dessert made with chestnuts, maraschino cherries, candied fruits, and flavored with liqueur or rum
