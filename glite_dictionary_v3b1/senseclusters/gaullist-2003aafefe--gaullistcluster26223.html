Gaullist

adjective
of de Gaulle's policies
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːlɪst / (BrE)
relating to Charles de Gaulle, his political ideas, or the movement supporting his policies and leadership style in France

Examples

Gaullist policy
The new president adopted a Gaullist stance on foreign policy, emphasizing national sovereignty.
Many older politicians still hold Gaullist views about the importance of a strong executive branch.
The party's Gaullist traditions influence its approach to economic and social reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gaullism - the political ideology and policies associated with Charles de Gaulle
