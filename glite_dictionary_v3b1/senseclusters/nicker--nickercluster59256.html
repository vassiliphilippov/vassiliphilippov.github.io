nicker

noun
a horse's soft, throaty sound
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪkɚ /; / ˈnɪkər / (AmE), / ˈnɪkə / (BrE)
the soft, low sound made by a horse, often as a greeting or in anticipation of food

Examples

A horse's nicker
As I entered the barn, I was greeted by the gentle nicker of several horses.
Early in the morning, the soft nicker of the mare filled the stable.
The horse gave a friendly nicker when it saw its owner approach.
As she approached, the horse gave a low, welcoming nicker.
The gentle nicker of the stallion greeted her every morning.
The stable fell quiet except for the soft nicker of a foal missing its mother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neigh - a loud, high-pitched sound made by a horse
