nondepartmental

noun
independent public body
8.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.dɪ.pɑːrt.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /nɑːn.də.pɑːrt.ˈmen.təl/ (AmE), /nɒn.dɪ.pɑːt.ˈmen.təl/ /; / /nɒn.də.pɑːt.ˈmen.təl/ (BrE)
a public organization that operates independently and is not part of a government department or under direct ministerial control

Examples

A nondepartmental body
The new nondepartmental was established to regulate environmental standards independently.
Funding for the nondepartmental comes directly from Parliament, not from any ministry.
Many experts believe a nondepartmental can act more impartially than a government department.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quango - a semi-public administrative body outside the civil service
