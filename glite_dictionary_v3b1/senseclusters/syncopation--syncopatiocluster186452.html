syncopation

noun
accenting of off-beats in music
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋkəˈpeɪʃən /; / ˌsɪŋ.kəˈpeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋkəˈpeɪʃən /; / ˌsɪŋ.kəˈpeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a musical rhythm or effect where the accent is placed on normally weak beats, making the rhythm lively or unexpected
