association

noun
formal organization or society
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an organized group of people with a common purpose, interest, or activity, often with a formal structure

Examples

A sports association
After college, she became a member of a professional association for engineers.
He was elected president of the local business association.
The American Heart Association holds an annual conference to discuss new research.
