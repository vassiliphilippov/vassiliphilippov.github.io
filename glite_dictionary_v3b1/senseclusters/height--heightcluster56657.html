height

noun
distance above sea level or ground
Pronunciation: / haɪt / (AmE), / haɪt / (BrE)
the elevation or distance of something above sea level or the earth's surface

Examples

Height above sea
At this height, the temperature drops significantly during the night.
Climbers often experience shortness of breath at higher heights.
The pilot announced that the plane would reach a height of 30,000 feet soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altitude - the height of an object or point in relation to sea level or ground level
  • antonym: depth - the distance from the top downwards to the bottom of something
