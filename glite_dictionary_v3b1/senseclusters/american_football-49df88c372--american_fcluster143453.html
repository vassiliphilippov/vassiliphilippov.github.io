American football

noun
team sport played mainly in the US
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
A team sport played by two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field, where the aim is to move the ball into the opposing team's end zone by running or passing plays.

Examples

American football game
He explained the rules of American football to his friends from overseas.
Many high schools in the United States have a American football team that plays in local leagues.
Watching American football on Sunday afternoons is a popular tradition for many families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: football - a general term for various team sports involving a ball kicked or carried to score points
