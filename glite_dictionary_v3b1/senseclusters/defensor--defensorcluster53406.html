defensor

noun
defensive player
7.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnsɔr / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsɔː / (BrE)
a player whose main role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring, especially in sports like football or rugby

Examples

Football defensor
The defensor made a crucial tackle to stop the opposing striker from scoring.
During the match, the team's defensor was praised for his strong performance.
Every successful team needs a reliable defensor to protect their goal.
