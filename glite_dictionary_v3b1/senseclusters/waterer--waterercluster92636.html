waterer

noun
person or device that supplies water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtərər /; / ˈwɔtərər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərə / (BrE)
a person or device that supplies or distributes water, especially for plants, crops, or drinking

Examples

A waterer for plants
During the marathon, a waterer handed drinks to the runners along the route.
Each waterer was responsible for refilling the livestock's troughs on the farm.
The waterer made sure every plant in the greenhouse received enough moisture each morning.
She worked as a waterer on the estate during the dry season.
The greenhouse waterer checks every pot each morning.
Without a reliable waterer, the seedlings struggled to survive.
