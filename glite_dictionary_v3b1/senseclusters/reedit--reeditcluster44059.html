reedit

noun
new edited version
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.ed.ɪt/ /; / /ˈrɪ.ed.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈriː.ed.ɪt/ /; / /ˈrɪ.ed.ɪt/ (BrE)
a new version of a recording, film, or text that has been edited and reworked from previously released material

Examples

A reedit of a film
The director released a reedit of the film with additional scenes and improved sound.
Fans were excited to hear the DJ's latest reedit of a classic dance track.
The publisher announced a reedit of the novel to include updated content and corrections.

  • synonym: remix - a new version of a song created by altering or adding to the original recording
