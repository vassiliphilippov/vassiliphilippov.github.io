Basiliscus

noun
genus of lizards called basilisks
Pronunciation: / bəˈsɪlɪskəs / (AmE), / ˌbæsɪˈlɪskəs /; / bəˈsɪlɪskəs / (BrE)
a genus of large, tropical lizards (basilisks) from Central and South America, known for their ability to run across water surfaces
