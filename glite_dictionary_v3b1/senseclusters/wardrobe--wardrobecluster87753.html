wardrobe

noun
tall clothes storage furniture
1.0 1.99/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…îÀêrÀådro äb / (AmE), / Ààw…îÀêdr…ô äb / (BrE)
A large, tall piece of furniture with doors, used for storing clothes by hanging or shelving them.

Examples

A wooden wardrobe
Emma kept her favorite dresses neatly in the wardrobe beside her bed.
He opened the wardrobe to choose a suit for the wedding.
We bought a new wardrobe for the guest room last weekend.

Synonyms

  • press
    8.4 1.06/m
    a press is a tall, plain furniture piece with doors and rails for hanging clothes, while a wardrobe is a large, common clothes storage unit.
  • armoire
    8.1 1.00/m
    an armoire is often ornate and freestanding for storing clothes, while a wardrobe is a more general, plain clothes cupboard.
  • closet
    1.0 32/m
    a closet is a small room or built-in space for hanging or storing clothes, while a wardrobe is a freestanding, larger piece of furniture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: closet - a small room or enclosed recess for storing clothes or other items
  • synonym: cupboard - a cabinet with shelves for storing dishes, food, or clothes
