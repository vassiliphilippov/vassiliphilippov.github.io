head

verb
to go or direct in a particular direction
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
to move or go in a particular direction, or to be at the front or top of something

Examples

After the meeting, we will head north towards the mountains.
If you head down this street, you'll reach the river.

She headed straight for the exit as soon as the meeting ended.
We're planning to head north before sunset to avoid traffic.
A photo of the winner headed the article in the newspaper.
Her name always heads the honor roll list each semester.
The new policy document was headed by a summary of key points.
