bargeman

noun
a man who operates a barge
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrdʒ.mən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒ.mən / (BrE)
a man who operates or works on a barge, especially transporting goods on rivers or canals

Examples

The bargeman loads crates
As a child, he watched the bargeman load crates onto the barge every morning.
The bargeman skillfully steered the vessel through the narrow canal.
The bargemen often shared stories while waiting at the locks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargee - a person who works on or operates a barge
  • synonym: boatman - a man who works on or operates a boat
