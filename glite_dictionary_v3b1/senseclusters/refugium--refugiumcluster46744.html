refugium

noun
aquarium compartment
9.9
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfjuː.dʒiː.əm/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfjuː.dʒəm/ (AmE), /rə.ˈfjuː.dʒiː.əm/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfjuː.dʒəm/ (BrE)
a separate compartment or small tank connected to an aquarium that provides a safe habitat for certain organisms and supports biological filtration

Examples

Aquarium refugium
She installed a refugium to help control algae and provide shelter for small invertebrates.
A well-maintained refugium can improve water quality in a saltwater aquarium.
Many aquarists use a refugium to grow beneficial plants and support delicate species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sump - a separate tank used to hold water and equipment for an aquarium
