hing

noun
pungent spice
Pronunciation: / hiŋ / (AmE), / hiŋ / (BrE)
a pungent spice made from the dried resin of a plant, commonly used in South Asian cooking

Examples

A pinch of hing
She added a pinch of hing to the curry for extra flavor.
Many vegetarian dishes use hing to enhance the taste and aroma.
The strong smell of hing disappears after it is cooked in hot oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asafoetida - a strong-smelling spice made from plant resin, used in Indian cuisine
  • synonym: garlic - a pungent bulb used as a spice or seasoning
