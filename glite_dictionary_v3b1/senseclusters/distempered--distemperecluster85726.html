distempered

adjective
painted with distemper
9.1
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɛmpərd / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
painted, coated, or treated with distemper, a traditional water-based paint used on walls or furniture

Examples

Distempered walls
The old cottage had distempered walls that gave it a soft, rustic appearance.
After renovation, the furniture was carefully distempered to match the original style.
They admired the distempered ceiling, which had a smooth, matte finish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unpainted - not covered with paint
  • synonym: whitewashed - painted with a white, water-based paint
