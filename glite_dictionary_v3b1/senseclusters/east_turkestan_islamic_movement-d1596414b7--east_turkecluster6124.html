East Turkestan Islamic Movement

noun
Uighur militant group in Xinjiang, China
Pronunciation: / ist tɜːrkəˌstɑːn aɪˈslɑːmɪk mʊvˈmənt / (AmE), / iːst tɜːkɪˌstæn ɪˈslɑːmɪk muːvˈmənt / (BrE)
a militant group of Uighur Muslims seeking independence for Xinjiang from China; considered a terrorist organization by China and several other countries

Examples

East Turkestan Islamic Movement group
International organizations have debated whether to label the East Turkestan Islamic Movement as a terrorist group.
