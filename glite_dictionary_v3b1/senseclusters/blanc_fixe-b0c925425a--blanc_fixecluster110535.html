blanc fixe

noun
white insoluble pigment (barium sulfate)
9.9
Pronunciation: / blɑŋk ˈfiks / (AmE), / blɒ̃ fiːks / (BrE)
A white, insoluble, radiopaque powder (barium sulfate) used as a pigment, especially in paints and paper.

Examples

Blanc fixe pigment
