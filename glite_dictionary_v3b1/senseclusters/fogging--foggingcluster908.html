fogging

noun
surface condensation
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of a surface becoming covered with mist or condensation, making it difficult to see through

Examples

Window fogging
The fogging on the car windows made it hard to drive safely in the morning.
After a hot shower, the fogging on the bathroom mirror took several minutes to disappear.
Goggles with special coatings can help prevent fogging during swimming or skiing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearing - the process of making a surface free from mist or condensation
  • synonym: condensation - the process by which water vapor becomes liquid on a surface
