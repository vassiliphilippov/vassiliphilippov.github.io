muley

noun
hybrid animal (mule)
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːli / (AmE), / ˈmjuːli / (BrE)
a hybrid animal that is the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse, also known as a mule; dialectal use

Examples

A muley plowing
The farmer relied on his muley to help plow the fields every spring.
They trained a sturdy muley to carry supplies up the mountain during the expedition.
A muley is often valued for its strength and endurance in rural communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hinny - the hybrid offspring of a male horse and a female donkey
  • synonym: mule - the hybrid offspring of a male donkey and a female horse
