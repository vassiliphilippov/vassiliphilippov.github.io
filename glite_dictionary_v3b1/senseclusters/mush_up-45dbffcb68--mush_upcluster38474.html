mush up

verb
to blend until soft or smooth
4.7
Pronunciation: / mʌʃ ʌp / (AmE), / mʌʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to crush, mix, or blend a substance until it becomes soft, smooth, or pulpy.

Examples

To mush up potatoes
If you mush up the potatoes too much, they'll turn gummy.
She likes to mush up bananas before adding them to the batter.
The baby enjoys it when I mush up her vegetables so they're easier to eat.

Related Terms
