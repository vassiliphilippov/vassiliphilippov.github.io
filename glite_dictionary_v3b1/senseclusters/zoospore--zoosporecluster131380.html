zoospore

noun
motile asexual spore in some algae and fungi
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊˌspɔr / (AmE), / ˈzəʊˌspɔː / (BrE)
An asexual spore in certain algae and fungi that is capable of movement using flagella.

Examples
