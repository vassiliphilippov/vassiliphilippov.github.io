shaped

adjective
formed or molded to a shape
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
formed or molded to fit a particular shape, often by altering or working a pliable material.

Examples

Custom-shaped insoles
He bought a pair of custom-shaped insoles for greater comfort.
The shaped cushions fit perfectly into the antique chairs.
The artist created a shaped vase to match the room's décor.
