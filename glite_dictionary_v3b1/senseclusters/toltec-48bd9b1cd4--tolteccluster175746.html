Toltec

proper noun
ancient Mexican civilization
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊltɛk / (AmE), / ˈtɒltɛk / (BrE)
a member of an ancient civilization that lived in central Mexico from around the 10th to 12th centuries CE

Examples

Toltec civilization
The Toltec people built impressive pyramids and temples in Tula.
Historians believe the Toltec civilization greatly influenced the Aztecs.
Artifacts from the Toltec era have been discovered throughout central Mexico.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aztec - a member of a later Mesoamerican civilization in central Mexico
  • synonym: Maya - a member of an ancient Mesoamerican civilization in southern Mexico and Central America
