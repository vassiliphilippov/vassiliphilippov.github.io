concourse

noun
large public gathering area
7.9 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.kɔːrs/ /; / /ˈkɑːŋ.kɔːrs/ (AmE), /ˈkɒŋ.kɔːs/ /; / /ˈkɒn.kɔːs/ (BrE)
