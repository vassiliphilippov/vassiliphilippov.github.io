nonexclusive

adjective
not limited to one
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪkˈskluːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪkˈskluːsɪv / (BrE)
not limited to only one group, person, or thing; open to more than one possibility or participant

Examples

A nonexclusive contract
The contract is nonexclusive, so you can work with other companies at the same time.
They have a nonexclusive agreement, allowing both parties to collaborate with others.
Membership in the group is nonexclusive and open to anyone interested.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusive - limited to only one group, person, or thing
  • synonym: inclusive - open to all; not excluding any group or person
