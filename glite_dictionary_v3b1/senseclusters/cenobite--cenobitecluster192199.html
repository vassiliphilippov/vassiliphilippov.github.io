cenobite

noun
member of communal religious order
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnəˌbaɪt /; / ˈsiːnəˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsiːnəbaɪt / (BrE)
a member of a religious order who lives in community rather than in solitude

Examples

The strict schedule of the coenobite left little time for personal pursuits.
Unlike an anchorite, a coenobite prefers the company of fellow monks and communal living.
