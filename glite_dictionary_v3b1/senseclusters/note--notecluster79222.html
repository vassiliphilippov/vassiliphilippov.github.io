note

noun
tone indicating emotion or attitude in voice
Pronunciation: / noʊt / (AmE), / nəʊt / (BrE)
a particular quality, feeling, or tone expressed in someone's voice, writing, or behavior

Examples

A note of sadness
His voice carried a note of excitement when he announced the good news.
She answered with a note of irritation, making her feelings clear.
There was a note of caution in her speech that surprised everyone.
His apology had a sincere note that made it believable.
The speech ended on an optimistic note, leaving the audience hopeful.
There was a note of uncertainty in her voice as she asked the question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuance - a subtle difference in meaning, expression, or sound
  • synonym: tone - the general character or attitude of a place, piece of writing, situation, etc.
