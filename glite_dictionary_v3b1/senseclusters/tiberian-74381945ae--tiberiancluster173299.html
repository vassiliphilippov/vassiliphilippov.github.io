Tiberian

adjective
of Tiberius's era
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈbɪəriən / (AmE), / taɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the Roman emperor Tiberius or to the period of his rule (AD 14–37)

Examples

Tiberian emperor
The Tiberian government introduced several reforms that changed the structure of Roman administration.
Artifacts from the Tiberian period reveal much about daily life in ancient Rome.
Many historians study Tiberian policies to understand the early Roman Empire.
