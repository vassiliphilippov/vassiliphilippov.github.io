negative pole

noun
terminal of a battery with negative potential
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈneɡ.ə.tɪv poʊl/ (AmE), /ˈneɡ.ə.tɪv pəʊl/ (BrE)
the terminal of a battery or electrical device that is connected to the negative plate and has a deficiency of electric potential relative to the positive pole

Examples

Negative pole of a battery
Be sure to connect the red wire to the positive pole and the black wire to the negative pole.
Reverse polarity can occur if you accidentally swap the positive and negative pole connections.
The corrosion was more severe near the negative pole of the old battery.
