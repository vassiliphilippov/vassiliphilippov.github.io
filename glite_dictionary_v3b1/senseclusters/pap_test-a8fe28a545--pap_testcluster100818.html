Pap test

noun
medical screening for cervical cancer
3.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /pæp test/ (AmE), /pæp test/ (BrE)
a medical test in which cells are taken from the cervix and examined to detect cancer or precancerous conditions

Examples

Pap test for cancer
A Pap test can detect abnormal cells in the cervix before they turn into cancer.
My doctor recommended a Pap test as part of my routine health checkup.
Women are encouraged to have a Pap test every three years starting at age 21.
