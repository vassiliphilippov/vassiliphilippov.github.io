refry

noun
second frying
Pronunciation: / ˈriˌfraɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːˌfraɪ / (BrE)
an act or process of frying food again after it has already been cooked once

Examples

A refry of potatoes
The refry of yesterday's potatoes made them extra crispy for breakfast.
After the initial cooking, a quick refry gave the dish a delicious golden color.
She prepared a refry of the leftover rice to serve with dinner.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fry - to cook food in hot fat or oil
  • synonym: reheat - to heat food again after it has cooled
