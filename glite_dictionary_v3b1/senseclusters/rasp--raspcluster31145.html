rasp

verb
scrape or file with a rasp
3.5
Pronunciation: / ræsp / (AmE), / rɑːsp / (BrE)
to scrape or shape a surface with a coarse file or rasp tool

Examples

To rasp wood
After shaping the clay, she carefully rasped its surface to create a rough texture.
He used a metal tool to rasp away the paint on the doorframe.
The carpenter began to rasp the edges of the wooden plank to smooth them.
