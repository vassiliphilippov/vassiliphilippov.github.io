philhellenist

noun
admirer of Greece or Greek culture
Pronunciation: / fɪlˈhɛlənɪst / (AmE), / fɪlˈhɛlɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who admires Greece and Greek culture, especially classical Greece

Examples

A philhellenist admirer
As a lifelong philhellenist, she decorated her home with statues inspired by ancient Athens.
Lord Byron was a noted philhellenist who supported Greek independence in the 19th century.
The philhellenist society meets monthly to discuss the influence of Greek art and philosophy.
