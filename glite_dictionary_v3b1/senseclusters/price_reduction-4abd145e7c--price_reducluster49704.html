price reduction

noun
a decrease in selling price
Pronunciation: / praɪs rɪˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / praɪs rɪˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)

Examples

A price reduction

Customers are always looking for a price reduction before making a big purchase.
