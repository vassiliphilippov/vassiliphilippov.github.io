Louis XV

proper noun
King of France, 1715–1774
Pronunciation: / luːˈi ˌfɪfˈtiːnθ / (AmE), / luːˈi ˌfɪfˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
(1710–1774) King of France from 1715 to 1774, grandson of Louis XIV, who led France into the War of the Austrian Succession and the Seven Years' War.

Examples

Louis XV of France

The extravagant court of Louis XV contributed to France's growing financial problems.
