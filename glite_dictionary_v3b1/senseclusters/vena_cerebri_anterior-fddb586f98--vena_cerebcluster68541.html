vena cerebri anterior

noun
a vein in the brain accompanying the anterior cerebral artery
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə sə.ˈriː.bri æn.ˈtɪr.i.ər/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə sə.ˈriː.bri æn.ˈtɪə.ri.ə/ (BrE)
a vein of the brain that accompanies the anterior cerebral artery and drains into the basal vein.

Examples

The vena cerebri anterior vein
During the surgery, the vena cerebri anterior was carefully preserved to prevent any complications.
Radiologists examined the patient's vena cerebri anterior for signs of blockage.
The vena cerebri anterior drains blood from the frontal lobes of the brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basal vein - a large vein into which the vena cerebri anterior drains
