Bahia grass

noun
tropical perennial pasture grass
7.8
Pronunciation: / bəˈhiː.ə ˌɡræs / (AmE), / bəˈhiː.ə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A perennial tropical grass of American origin, commonly used as pasture grass in arid and warm regions, especially in the southeastern United States.

Examples

Bahia grass pasture
Bahia grass is often planted along highways for erosion control in warm climates.
Many lawns in the southern United States use Bahia grass for its durability.
The farmer chose Bahia grass for his pasture due to its drought resistance.
