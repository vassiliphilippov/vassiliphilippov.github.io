artificial

adjective
made by humans, not natural
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑːr.t̬əˈfɪʃ.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl / (BrE)
made or produced by humans rather than occurring naturally, often as a copy of something natural
