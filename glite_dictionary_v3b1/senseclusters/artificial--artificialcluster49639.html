artificial

adjective
made by humans, not natural
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑːr.t̬əˈfɪʃ.əl / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃəl /; / ˌɑː.tɪˈfɪʃ.əl / (BrE)
made or produced by humans rather than occurring naturally, often as a copy of something natural

Examples

Artificial lake
An artificial lake was created to supply water to the city.
He prefers artificial light to natural sunlight for painting.
The garden was filled with artificial flowers that looked almost real.
He prefers natural light to artificial lighting in his studio.
Some candies contain artificial coloring to create brighter hues.
The museum displayed artificial flowers that looked almost real.

Related Terms

  • antonym: natural - existing in or caused by nature; not made by humans
  • synonym: synthetic - made by chemical synthesis, especially to imitate a natural product
