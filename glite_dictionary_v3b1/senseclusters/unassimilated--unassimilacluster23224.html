unassimilated

adjective
not adapted linguistically
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈsɪməˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈsɪmɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not adapted to the phonology, morphology, or grammatical rules of a language, retaining a foreign form or character

Examples

Unassimilated words
Some words in English remain unassimilated and keep their original spelling and pronunciation.
The linguist studied several unassimilated terms borrowed from French and German.
Many unassimilated expressions are still used in specialized scientific fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreign - from another country or language; not native
