overmuchness

noun
excessiveness; too much
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈmʌtʃnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈmʌtʃnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being excessive or more than is appropriate

Examples

The overmuchness of comments

Some people criticize the overmuchness of regulations in the industry.
The overmuchness of praise made her uncomfortable during the ceremony.
