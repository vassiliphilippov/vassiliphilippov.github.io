term

noun
condition of an agreement or contract
Pronunciation: / tɝm / (AmE), / tɜːm / (BrE)
(usually plural) a condition or stipulation specified as part of an agreement, contract, or negotiation.

Examples

Terms of a contract
The terms of the agreement must be read carefully before signing.
The terms outlined in the policy are binding for all parties involved.
They negotiated the terms of the contract over several meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condition - a stipulation or requirement in an agreement
  • synonym: provision - a specific condition in a formal document
