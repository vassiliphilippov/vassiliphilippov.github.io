number

noun
symbol representing a numerical value
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a numeral, symbol, or concept that shows how many of something there are

Examples

A number 5

The teacher asked the students to count and name every number up to ten.
Zero is a number that represents the absence of any quantity.
