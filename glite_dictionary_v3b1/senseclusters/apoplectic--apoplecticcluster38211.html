apoplectic

adjective
extremely angry
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈplɛktɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəˈplɛktɪk / (BrE)
extremely angry or furious, often to the point of being unable to speak or act calmly

Examples

Apoplectic with rage
He became apoplectic when he discovered the mistake in the report.
The coach was apoplectic after the referee made a controversial call.
She was so apoplectic that she could barely get her words out.

  • synonym: enraged - filled with intense anger
  • synonym: furious - very angry
