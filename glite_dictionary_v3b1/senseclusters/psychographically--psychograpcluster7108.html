psychographically

adverb
by psychological traits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

The report analyzes voters psychographically rather than just by age or location.
