Synonyms

  • migratory
    5.0 0.33/m
    migratory describes groups that move seasonally for work or food, while migrant names a person who moves to find work.
  • nomadic
    4.9 2.39/m
    nomadic describes people or groups who move instead of settling permanently, while migrant focuses on someone who moves to seek work.
  • peripatetic
    9.5 0.00/m
    peripatetic names a person who travels for work or duties, while migrant refers to someone moving for seasonal work or better living.
  • roving
    4.1 0.16/m
