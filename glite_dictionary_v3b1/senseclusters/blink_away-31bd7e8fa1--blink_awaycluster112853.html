blink away

verb
hold back tears by blinking
Pronunciation: / blɪŋk əˈweɪ / (AmE), / blɪŋk əˈweɪ / (BrE)
To try to prevent tears from overflowing or to hold back emotion by blinking.

Examples

To blink away tears
He paused, blinking away the sudden emotion in his eyes.
I quickly blinked away the tears before anyone could notice.
She tried to blink away her tears as she spoke.
