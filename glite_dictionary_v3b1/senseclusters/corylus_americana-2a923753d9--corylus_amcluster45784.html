Corylus americana

noun
American hazelnut shrub species
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːrɪləs ˌæməˈrɪkənə / (AmE), / ˌkɒrɪləs ˌæməˈrɪkənə / (BrE)
A species of nut-bearing shrub native to eastern North America, known as the American hazelnut.
