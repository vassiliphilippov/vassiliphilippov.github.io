croute

noun
scab or crust
Pronunciation: / kruːt / (AmE), / kruːt / (BrE)
a dry scab or crust that forms over a wound or sore as it heals

Examples

A wound croute
After the cut healed, a hard croute remained where the skin had been damaged.
The doctor explained that the croute would fall off naturally as the wound healed.
He tried not to scratch the croute on his knee to avoid leaving a scar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crust - a hard outer layer that forms on a surface, especially over a wound
  • synonym: scab - a hard, dry covering that forms over a wound as it heals
