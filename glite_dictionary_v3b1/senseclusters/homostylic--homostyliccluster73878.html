homostylic

adjective
having styles of the same length (botany)

Pronunciation: /hoʊ.mə.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ /; / /hoʊ.moʊ.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ (AmE), /həʊ.mə.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ /; / /həʊ.moʊ.ˈstaɪ.lɪk/ (BrE)
(of a flower) having all the styles of equal length.

Examples

Homostylic flower styles
Primrose populations can be either homostylic or heterostylic, depending on the length of their floral styles.
Some species exhibit homostylic flowers as an adaptation to self-pollination.
The homostylic form is rare in plants that typically depend on pollinators.
