capsule

noun
spacecraft designed for human transport
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp.suːl / (AmE), / ˈkæp.sjuːl / (BrE)
a spacecraft designed to carry people and sustain human life in outer space

Examples

A space capsule
After docking with the space station, the capsule provided living quarters for the astronauts.
The Apollo mission used a capsule to bring astronauts safely back to Earth.
The crew re-entered the atmosphere in a specially designed capsule.
