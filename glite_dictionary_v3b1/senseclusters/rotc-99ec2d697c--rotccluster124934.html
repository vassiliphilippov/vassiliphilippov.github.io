ROTC

noun
college military training program
Pronunciation: / ɑr oʊ ti si / (AmE), / ɑːr əʊ tiː siː / (BrE)
a college program that trains students to become commissioned officers in the armed forces

Examples

ROTC college program
During his time in ROTC, he learned leadership skills and military discipline.
Many students choose ROTC because it offers scholarships in exchange for military service after graduation.
She joined the ROTC program at her university to help pay for college and serve in the army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Officer Candidate School - military school training civilians to become officers
