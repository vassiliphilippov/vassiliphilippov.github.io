world-wide

adjective
extending over the whole world
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝldˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Extending or applying to the whole world; affecting or involving all parts of the world.

Examples

World-wide cooperation
Environmental issues such as climate change require world-wide cooperation and solutions.
Her music is world-wide famous, topping charts in dozens of countries.
The internet has become a world-wide phenomenon, connecting people from every country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: global - relating to or covering the whole world
  • antonym: local - relating to a particular area only, not the whole world
  • synonym: universal - existing everywhere or involving everyone
