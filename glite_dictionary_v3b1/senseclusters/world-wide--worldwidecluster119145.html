Synonyms

  • worldwide
    2.1 9/m
    worldwide is the common modern form used in writing and speech, while world-wide is the hyphenated or older variant.
  • global
    2.7 50/m
    global describes things involving the whole world, often about systems or markets, while world-wide stresses spread across all countries.
  • universal
    4.2 20/m
    universal means true or applying to everyone or every case, while world-wide refers mainly to geographic reach across the world.
