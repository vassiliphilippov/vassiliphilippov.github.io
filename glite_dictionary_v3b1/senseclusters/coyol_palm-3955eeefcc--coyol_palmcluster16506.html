coyol palm

noun
tropical palm with edible nuts and fiber
Pronunciation: / koʊˈjɔl pɑm / (AmE), / kɔɪˈɒl pɑːm / (BrE)
A tropical American palm tree (Attalea cohune) that produces edible nuts and a valuable fiber.
