eastward

adverb
toward the east
Pronunciation: / ˈiːstwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈiːstwədz / (BrE)
In the direction of the east.

Examples

Birds fly eastwards
The birds flew eastwards across the open fields at dawn.
The storm is expected to move eastwards overnight, bringing heavy rain to the region.
We decided to travel eastwards in search of warmer weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eastward - toward the east; in the direction of the east
  • antonym: westwards - in the direction of the west
