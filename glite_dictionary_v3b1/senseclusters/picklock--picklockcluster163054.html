picklock

verb
to open lock illegally
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈpɪkˌlɒk / (BrE)
to open a lock without a key by manipulating its mechanism with a pick or similar tool

Examples

To picklock a door
The burglar tried to picklock the door but was caught by the police.
She learned how to picklock old safes as part of her training.
It is illegal to picklock someone else's property without permission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pick - to open a lock without a key using tools
  • antonym: unlock - to open a lock using the correct key or method
