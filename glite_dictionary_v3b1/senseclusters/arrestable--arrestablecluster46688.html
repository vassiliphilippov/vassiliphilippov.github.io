arrestable

adjective
able to be stopped
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstəbəl / (AmE), / əˈrɛstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be stopped, halted, or brought under control, especially in a physical, medical, or mechanical context.

Examples

Arrestable disease
The spread of the disease was still arrestable with immediate medical intervention.
Engineers worked quickly to ensure the malfunction was arrestable before causing further damage.
Doctors considered the condition arrestable if treated in its early stages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controllable - able to be managed or kept under control
  • antonym: unstoppable - impossible to stop or prevent
