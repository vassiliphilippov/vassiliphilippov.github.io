cabriolet

noun
two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkæbriəˈleɪ / (AmE), / ˌkæbrɪəˈleɪ / (BrE)
a small, light, two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage with two seats and a folding hood

Examples

A cabriolet carriage
During the 19th century, people often used a cabriolet for short trips around the city.
The museum displays a restored cabriolet among its collection of historical vehicles.
The wealthy merchant arrived at the party in a stylish cabriolet with a bright red hood.
