installation

noun
act of setting up
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnstəˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of putting something in place so that it is ready to use

Examples

Installation of equipment
The installation of the new air conditioner took only a few hours.
After the installation of the security system, the building felt much safer.
Proper installation of the equipment is essential for it to work correctly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: removal - the act of taking something away or out
  • synonym: setup - the process of preparing something for use
