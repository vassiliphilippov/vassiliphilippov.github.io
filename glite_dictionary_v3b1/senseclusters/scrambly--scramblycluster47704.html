scrambly

adjective
soft, loose texture
Pronunciation: / ˈskræmbli / (AmE), / ˈskræmbli / (BrE)
having a soft, loose, and slightly curd-like texture, especially like that of scrambled eggs

Examples

Scrambly eggs
The chef served a plate of scrambly eggs with fresh herbs on top.
She likes her tofu cooked until it is soft and scrambly for breakfast.
The cottage cheese had a scrambly consistency that paired well with fruit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firm - solid and not soft or loose
  • synonym: fluffy - light and soft in texture
