themed

adjective
designed with a motif
3.9
Pronunciation: / θimd / (AmE), / θiːmd / (BrE)
organized or designed to reflect a particular subject, idea, or motif

Examples

A themed party
They attended a themed dinner where everyone dressed as characters from the 1980s.
The hotel offers several themed rooms, including one inspired by the rainforest.
For her birthday, she wanted a unicorn themed cake with colorful decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorated - adorned or embellished for a particular effect
  • antonym: plain - without decoration or a specific motif
