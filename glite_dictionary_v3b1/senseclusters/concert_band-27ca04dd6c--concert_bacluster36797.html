concert band

noun
large wind and percussion musical ensemble
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.sərt bænd/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.sət bænd/ (BrE)
a large ensemble of musicians who play brass, woodwind, and percussion instruments, typically performing concert music rather than marching music

Examples

A concert band performance
A concert band typically includes flutes, trumpets, trombones, and a variety of percussion instruments.
She joined the concert band in high school and learned to play the clarinet.
The university's concert band will be performing in the auditorium tonight.
