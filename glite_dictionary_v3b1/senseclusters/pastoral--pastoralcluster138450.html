pastoral

noun
letter from a pastor to a congregation
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstɚəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstərəl / (BrE)
a letter or message sent by a pastor to a congregation, often giving guidance or instructions

Examples

A pastoral letter
A pastoral was read aloud at the Sunday service to convey important changes in church policy.
The bishop issued a pastoral before Easter to encourage reflection among the parishioners.
The congregation received a thoughtful pastoral on the topic of forgiveness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epistle - a formal letter, especially one sent on a religious subject
