surmount

verb
reach the highest point of
Pronunciation: / sɚˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / səˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to reach the highest point of (something), especially after climbing or overcoming it

Examples

To surmount a mountain
After hours of hiking, we finally surmounted the ridge and took in the view.
It takes strength and determination to surmount such a formidable peak.
The climbers managed to surmount the steep north face before dawn.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descend - go down from a higher to a lower place
  • synonym: scale - climb up to the top of
