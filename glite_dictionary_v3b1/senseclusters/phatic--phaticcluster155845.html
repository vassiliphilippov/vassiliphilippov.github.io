phatic

adjective
social interactional
Pronunciation: / ˈfætɪk / (AmE), / ˈfætɪk / (BrE)
used to describe speech or expressions intended for social interaction rather than to convey information

Examples

Phatic greeting
When you greet someone with 'Good morning,' it is a phatic expression.
Small talk at the start of meetings often serves a phatic function.
The phrase 'Nice to meet you' is commonly used as a phatic remark in English.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversational - relating to informal talk or conversation
  • antonym: informational - intended to provide information
