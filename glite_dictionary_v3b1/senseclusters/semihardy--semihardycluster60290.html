semihardy

adjective
tolerant of mild cold
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈhɑːrdi / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈhɑːdi / (BrE)
able to survive moderate cold but not severe frost, especially of plants

Examples

Semihardy plant
This semihardy plant can be grown outdoors in regions with mild winters.
Gardeners often choose semihardy flowers for areas that experience only light frosts.
Because it is semihardy, this vegetable needs protection during very cold nights.
