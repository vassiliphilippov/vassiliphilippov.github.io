Indian senna

noun
medicinal shrub (Senna alexandrina)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈsɛnə / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈsɛnə / (BrE)
an erect shrub (Senna alexandrina) with yellow flowers, whose dried leaves are used medicinally as a laxative

Examples

Indian senna leaves
Indian senna is traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat constipation.
Some teas include Indian senna extract for its digestive benefits.
The leaves of Indian senna contain compounds that act as natural laxatives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cathartic - a substance that accelerates defecation
  • synonym: senna - a group of leguminous plants used medicinally as laxatives
