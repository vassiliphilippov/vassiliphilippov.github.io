take the cake

verb
be the most extreme (often negative) example
9.9
Pronunciation: / teɪk ðə keɪk / (AmE), / teɪk ðə keɪk / (BrE)
to be the most extreme, surprising, or remarkable example (usually in a negative or unusual way)
