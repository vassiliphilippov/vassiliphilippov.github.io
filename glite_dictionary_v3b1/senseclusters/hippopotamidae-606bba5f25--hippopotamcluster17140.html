Hippopotamidae

noun
the family of animals including hippopotamuses
Pronunciation: / hɪpəˌpɑːtəˈmædəˌdi / (AmE), / hɪpəˌpɒtəˈmædɪdiː / (BrE)
the family of large, mostly herbivorous, semi-aquatic mammals that includes the hippopotamus

Examples

The Hippopotamidae family
Exhibits on the Hippopotamidae in the museum detailed their evolution and ecological role.
Scientists classified pygmy hippos as part of the Hippopotamidae family.
The Hippopotamidae are native to sub-Saharan Africa and are known for their amphibious lifestyles.
