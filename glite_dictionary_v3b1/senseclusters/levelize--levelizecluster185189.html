levelize

verb
to make equal
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something equal or steady across different points or periods of time

Examples

To levelize prices
The new policy aims to levelize electricity rates for all regions.
They worked to levelize the workload among team members throughout the year.
Efforts to levelize prices helped reduce sudden spikes in costs for consumers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance - to make different parts equal or stable
  • synonym: equalize - to make things equal in amount or value
  • antonym: uneven - not equal or balanced
