protoanthropology

noun
study of humans before writing
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtoʊˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəʊˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of human beings and societies before the invention of writing
