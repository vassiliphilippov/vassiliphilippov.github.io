polychrome

noun
a multi-colored work of art
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːliˌkroʊm / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪˌkrəʊm / (BrE)
A work of art composed of or decorated in many colors.

Examples

A polychrome artwork
The artist created a stunning polychrome using glass and ceramic tiles.
The museum displayed a rare polychrome from the medieval period.
We admired the intricate details and vibrant hues of the ancient polychrome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monochrome - a work of art using only one color or shades of one color
