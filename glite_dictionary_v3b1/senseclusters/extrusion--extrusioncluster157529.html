extrusion

noun
projecting or bulging part
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstruːʒən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstruːʒən / (BrE)
something that sticks out from a surface or projects outward from its surroundings; a protuberant part or feature.

Examples

A bony extrusion
A bony extrusion above his eye gave his face a unique profile.
On the cave wall, a strange extrusion caught the light of his flashlight.
The geologist pointed out the hardened lava extrusion on the mountainside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulge - a rounded swelling or projection
  • antonym: depression - a sunken or lowered area compared with the surroundings
  • synonym: protuberance - something that juts out from the surrounding surface
