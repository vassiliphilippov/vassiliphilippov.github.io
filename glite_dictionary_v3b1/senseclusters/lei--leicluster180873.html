lei

noun
flower garland, especially Hawaiian
9.1 4.9
Pronunciation: /leɪ/ (AmE), /leɪ/ (BrE)
a garland or wreath of flowers or foliage, typically worn around the neck as a symbol of affection or welcome, especially in Hawaii

Examples

A lei of flowers
Each guest received a fragrant lei upon entering the ceremony.
He wore a colorful lei around his neck at the luau.
She was greeted with a beautiful lei as she arrived in Hawaii.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garland - a wreath or chain of flowers, leaves, or other material, worn for decoration or presented as a mark of honor
  • synonym: wreath - an arrangement of flowers, leaves, or stems fastened in a ring and used for decoration
