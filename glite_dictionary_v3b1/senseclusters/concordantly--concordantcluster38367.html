concordantly

adverb
in matching manner
8.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàk…îrd…ôntli / (AmE), / k…ônÀàk…îÀêd…ôntli / (BrE)
in a way that two or more related individuals or cases show the same condition, trait, or result

Examples

Concordantly affected siblings
The siblings were concordantly affected by the inherited disease.
Both test results came back concordantly positive for the same genetic marker.
Researchers observed that the twins responded concordantly to the new treatment.
