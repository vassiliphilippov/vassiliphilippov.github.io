pisiform

noun
small pea-shaped wrist bone
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪsəˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈpɪsɪfɔːm / (BrE)
a small, pea-shaped bone in the wrist that articulates only with the triquetral bone

Examples

The pisiform bone
Doctors examined the pisiform to assess damage associated with carpal tunnel syndrome.
The pisiform is located on the ulnar side of the wrist, near the palm.
X-rays revealed a fracture in the pisiform after she fell on her outstretched hand.
