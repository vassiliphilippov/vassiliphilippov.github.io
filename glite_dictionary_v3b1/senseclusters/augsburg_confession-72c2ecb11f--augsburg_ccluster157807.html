Augsburg Confession

noun
foundational Lutheran doctrinal document (1530)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːgz.bɜːrg kən.ˈfeʃ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈɔːgz.bɜːg kən.ˈfeʃ.ən/ (BrE)
