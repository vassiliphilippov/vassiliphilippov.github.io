personal belief

noun
an individual's conviction or value
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl bɪˈliːf / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
a conviction or value held by an individual, often influencing thoughts or actions

Examples

A personal belief
Laws should not force someone to act against their personal belief.
My personal belief is that honesty is always the best policy.
She refused the job offer because it conflicted with her personal belief about animal rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opinion - a belief or judgment that is not necessarily based on fact or knowledge
