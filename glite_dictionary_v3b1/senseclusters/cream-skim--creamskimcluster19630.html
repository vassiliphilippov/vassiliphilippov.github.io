cream-skim

verb
select the best or easiest options
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɹim skɪm / (AmE), / kɹiːm skɪm / (BrE)
to select only the best, most profitable, or easiest options from a group, often leaving less desirable choices behind.

Examples

To cream-skim customers
Insurance companies sometimes cream-skim by offering the best deals only to low-risk clients.
Some universities have been accused of trying to cream-skim the highest-achieving students for their programs.
The company was criticized for cream-skimming projects that guaranteed the largest profits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherry-pick - to selectively choose the best items from what is available
