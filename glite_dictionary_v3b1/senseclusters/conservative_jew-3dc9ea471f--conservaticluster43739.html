Conservative Jew

noun
member of Conservative Judaism
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝːvətɪv dʒuː / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːvətɪv dʒuː / (BrE)
A member of a branch of Judaism who maintains some traditional Jewish laws while adapting others to fit modern circumstances.
