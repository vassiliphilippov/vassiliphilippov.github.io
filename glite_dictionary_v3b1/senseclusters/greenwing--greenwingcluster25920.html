greenwing

noun
a species of small duck with a green wing patch
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnwɪŋ / (BrE)
A small duck, also known as the common teal, found in Eurasia and North America, distinguished by a green patch on its wings.

Examples

A greenwing duck
Birdwatchers gathered early to observe a flock of greenwing in the marsh.
The shimmering patch on a greenwing's wing makes it easy to spot.
Thousands of greenwing migrate south every autumn along the wetlands.
