greenwing

noun
a species of small duck with a green wing patch
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgriːn.wɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgriːn.wɪŋ/ (BrE)
A small duck, also known as the common teal, found in Eurasia and North America, distinguished by a green patch on its wings.
