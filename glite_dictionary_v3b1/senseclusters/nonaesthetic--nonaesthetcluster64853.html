nonaesthetic

adjective
not about beauty
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
not related to beauty, art, or the appreciation of appearance

Examples

Nonaesthetic choice
The engineer chose a nonaesthetic solution that prioritized safety over appearance.
Some industrial products are intentionally nonaesthetic to reduce costs and focus on function.
His office was practical and nonaesthetic, with no decorations or artwork.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aesthetic - concerned with beauty or artistic appearance
