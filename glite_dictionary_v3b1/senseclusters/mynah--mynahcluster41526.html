mynah

noun
tropical Asian starling bird
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
A tropical Asian bird of the starling family, known for its ability to mimic human speech.

Examples

A mynah bird
A mynah bird can often be taught to imitate human words.
