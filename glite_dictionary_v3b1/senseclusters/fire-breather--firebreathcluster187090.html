fire-breather

noun
performer who breathes fire
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚˌbriːðɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbriːðə / (BrE)
a performer who entertains by expelling a fuel from the mouth and igniting it to create a flame

Examples

Circus fire-breather
At the festival, a fire-breather performed alongside jugglers and acrobats.
The fire-breather amazed the crowd with a gigantic plume of flames.
The children were fascinated by the daring fire-breather at the circus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire-eater - a performer who places flaming objects into the mouth
