unrounded

adjective
vowel without lip rounding
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈraʊndɪd / (BrE)
describes a vowel sound produced without rounding the lips

Examples

Unrounded vowel sound
In English, the vowel in 'see' is an unrounded vowel.
Linguists classify the vowel in 'cat' as unrounded because the lips are relaxed.
The teacher explained that 'i' in 'machine' is pronounced as an unrounded vowel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rounded - describes a vowel sound produced with rounded lips
