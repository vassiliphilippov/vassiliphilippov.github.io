boaster

noun
a very boastful person
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊstər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊstə / (BrE)
a person who talks with excessive pride about their achievements or possessions

Examples

A boaster talks proudly
He was known as a boaster who exaggerated every story about his past successes.
Nobody liked the boaster in the group because he turned every conversation into a competition.
Rather than being humble, she developed a reputation as a boaster at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast or show off
