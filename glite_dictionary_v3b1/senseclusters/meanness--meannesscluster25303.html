meanness

noun
extreme stinginess
2.9 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmiː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmiː.nəs/ (BrE)
Extreme unwillingness to spend money or share resources; stinginess.

Examples

Financial meanness
Her meanness prevented her from even giving a small tip to the waiter.
His meanness showed when he charged his friends for every little thing.
The old landlord was notorious for his meanness and refusal to repair anything.
