jerky

adjective
abrupt or uneven in movement or change
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːki /; / ˈdʒɝki / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːki /; / ˈdʒəːki / (BrE)
moving in a sudden, uneven, or irregular way; not smooth or steady

Examples

Jerky movements
His handwriting was so jerky that it was hard to read.
The car made a jerky stop at the traffic light.
The film's editing was criticized for its jerky scene transitions.
His jerky handwriting made the note hard to read.
The bus came to a jerky stop, making everyone sway in their seats.
The film was difficult to watch because of its jerky animation.
