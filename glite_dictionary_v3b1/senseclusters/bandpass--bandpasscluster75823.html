bandpass

adjective
frequency range filtering
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbændˌpæs / (AmE), / ˈbændˌpɑːs / (BrE)
allowing only a specific range of frequencies to pass through while blocking others

Examples

Bandpass filter
The engineer installed a bandpass filter to isolate the desired radio signal.
A bandpass system is essential for many audio and communication devices.
They adjusted the bandpass settings to improve the clarity of the recording.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filter - a device or process that removes unwanted parts from a signal
  • antonym: high-pass - allowing only frequencies above a certain cutoff to pass
  • antonym: low-pass - allowing only frequencies below a certain cutoff to pass
