hoodlum

noun
violent or criminal young person
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊd.ləm / (AmE), / ˈhʊd.ləm / (BrE)
a young person who engages in criminal or violent behavior, typically as part of a gang

Examples

A hoodlum gang
A group of hoodlums were seen fighting near the subway station last night.
Even as a teenager, he was known around town as a hoodlum and troublemaker.
The police arrested a hoodlum who had been vandalizing cars in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delinquent - a young person who regularly commits minor crimes
  • antonym: law-abiding citizen - someone who obeys the law
  • synonym: thug - a violent person, especially a criminal
