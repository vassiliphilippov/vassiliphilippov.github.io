adrenaline junkie

noun
person addicted to excitement
5.2
Pronunciation: / əˈdrɛnəlɪn ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / əˈdrɛnəlɪn ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to excitement and seeks out thrilling activities or situations

Examples

An adrenaline junkie seeks thrills
After bungee jumping, the adrenaline junkie started planning his next adventure.
My brother is a true adrenaline junkie who loves skydiving and mountain biking.
The movie is perfect for any adrenaline junkie craving action and suspense.
