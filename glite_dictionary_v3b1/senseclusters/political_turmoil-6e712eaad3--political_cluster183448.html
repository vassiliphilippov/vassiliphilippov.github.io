political turmoil

noun
instability or disorder in political affairs
1.4 4.2
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl ˈtɜːr.mɔɪl/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪ.tə.kəl ˈtɜːr.mɔɪl/ (AmE), /pə.ˈlɪ.tɪ.kəl ˈtɜː.mɔɪl/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪ.tə.kəl ˈtɜː.mɔɪl/ (BrE)
