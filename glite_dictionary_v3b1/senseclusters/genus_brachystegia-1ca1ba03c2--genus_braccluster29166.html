genus Brachystegia

noun
African genus of timber trees
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːnəs ˌbrækɪˈstiːdʒiə / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːnəs ˌbrækɪˈstiːdʒɪə / (BrE)
