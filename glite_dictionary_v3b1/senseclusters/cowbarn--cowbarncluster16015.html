cowbarn

noun
a barn for cows
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌbɑrn / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌbɑːn / (BrE)
A barn where cows are kept, typically for shelter or milking.

Examples

The cowbarn shelter
During winter, the cows slept inside the cowbarn to stay warm.
The old cowbarn was renovated and turned into a studio.
You can smell the hay as soon as you walk into the cowbarn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: byre - a cowshed; a barn or shed for cows
  • synonym: stable - a building for housing animals such as horses or cattle
