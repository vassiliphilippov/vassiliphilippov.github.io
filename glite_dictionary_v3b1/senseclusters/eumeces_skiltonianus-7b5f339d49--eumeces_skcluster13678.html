Eumeces skiltonianus

noun
species of skink (western North America)
Pronunciation: / juˈmiːsiːz skɪlˈtoʊniˌænəs / (AmE), / juːˈmiːsiːz skɪlˈtəʊniˌænəs / (BrE)
