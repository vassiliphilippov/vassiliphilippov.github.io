her ladyship

noun
formal title for a woman of rank
4.5
Pronunciation: / hɜr ˈleɪdiˌʃɪp / (AmE), / hə ˈleɪdiʃɪp / (BrE)
A formal and polite title used to address or refer to a woman with the rank of lady, especially in the British peerage.

Examples

Her Ladyship title
It was considered a great honor to receive an invitation from Her Ladyship.
Please inform Her Ladyship that dinner is served.
We await the arrival of Her Ladyship in the drawing room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: His Lordship - A formal title of respect for a nobleman or lord
