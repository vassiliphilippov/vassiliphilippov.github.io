her ladyship

noun
formal title for a woman of rank
Pronunciation: / hɜr ˈleɪdiˌʃɪp / (AmE), / hə ˈleɪdiʃɪp / (BrE)
A formal and polite title used to address or refer to a woman with the rank of lady, especially in the British peerage.

Examples

Her Ladyship title
