unsettlingly

adverb
causing unease
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛt̬lɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes discomfort, worry, or unease

Examples

Unsettlingly quiet
The movie was unsettlingly quiet, making the audience feel tense throughout.
He smiled unsettlingly, leaving everyone in the room feeling nervous.
The news was unsettlingly vague, which only increased our anxiety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried or more at ease
  • synonym: disturbingly - in a way that causes worry or distress
