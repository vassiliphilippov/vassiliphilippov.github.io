recrown

verb
to crown again
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkraʊn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkraʊn / (BrE)
to crown someone again, especially by placing a crown on their head for a second time

Examples

The ceremony to recrown the queen was attended by thousands of citizens.
Historians debated whether it was necessary to recrown the monarch after the civil war.
