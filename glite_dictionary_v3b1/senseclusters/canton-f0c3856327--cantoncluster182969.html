Canton

proper noun
old name for Guangzhou, China
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.tɑn / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tɒn / (BrE)
the former English name for Guangzhou, a major city in southern China and the capital of Guangdong province

Examples

Canton city

Many Westerners still refer to Guangzhou as Canton in historical contexts.
Tourists visit Canton to experience its rich cultural and culinary heritage.
