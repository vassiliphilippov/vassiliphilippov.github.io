top

verb
reach the highest point of something
6.1 1.4
Pronunciation: /tɑːp/ /; / /tɔːp/ (AmE), /tɒp/ (BrE)
to reach, be at, or form the highest point or part of something; to provide with a top

Examples

To top the cake

The climbers topped the ridge just as the sun began to set.
We cheered as we finally topped the last hill of the marathon.

A bright star tops the Christmas tree during the holidays.
A delicate sculpture topped the ornate building, drawing everyone's attention upwards.
The sundae was topped with a cherry and some whipped cream.
