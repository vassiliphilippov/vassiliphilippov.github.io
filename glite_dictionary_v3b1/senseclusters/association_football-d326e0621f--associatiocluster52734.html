association football

noun
soccer; team sport played with a round ball
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
The game commonly known as 'football' or 'soccer', in which two teams of eleven players compete to score goals by kicking or heading a ball into the opposing team's net.

Examples

Association football match
Although it's called soccer in the United States, most countries refer to the sport as association football.
Many young people around the world play association football in local clubs and leagues.
The FIFA World Cup is the most prestigious tournament in association football.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rugby football - A different code of football played with an oval ball and different rules
  • synonym: soccer - Another term for association football, especially in American English
