chew over

verb
think about carefully
Pronunciation: / tʃuː ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / tʃuː ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to think about or discuss something carefully and for a long time

Examples

To chew over ideas
I need some time to chew over your proposal before making a decision.
She was chewing over the idea of changing careers.
They chewed over the problem for hours but couldn't find a solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mull over - to think about deeply or consider carefully
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully
