papyrology

noun
study of papyrus texts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpiˈrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpæpɪˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)

Examples

The university offers a course in papyrology for students interested in ancient languages and documents.
