pay cut

noun
reduction in salary
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ ˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ ˌkʌt / (BrE)
a reduction in salary or wages for an employee

Examples

A pay cut decision
After the pay cut, many employees felt less motivated at work.
She decided to accept a pay cut in order to keep her job during the recession.
The company announced a pay cut for all senior staff to reduce costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salary reduction - the process of lowering a person's salary
