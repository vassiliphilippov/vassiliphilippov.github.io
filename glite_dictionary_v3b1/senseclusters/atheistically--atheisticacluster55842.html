atheistically

adverb
in an atheist manner
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌeɪθiˈɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or expresses atheism or disbelief in the existence of God or gods

Examples

Argue atheistically
She argued atheistically during the debate about the existence of a higher power.
The book discusses the origins of life atheistically, without reference to any deity.
He often speaks atheistically about topics that others approach from a religious perspective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: devoutly - in a way that shows deep religious feeling or commitment
  • synonym: irreligiously - in a way that shows lack of religious belief or respect
