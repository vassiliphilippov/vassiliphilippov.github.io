hydraulic engineering

noun
branch of civil engineering for moving water
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk en.dʒə.ˈnɪr.ɪŋ/ /; / /haɪ.ˈdrɔː.lɪk en.dʒɪ.ˈnɪr.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪk en.dʒɪ.ˈnɪə.rɪŋ/ /; / /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪk en.dʒə.ˈnɪə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
