snare

noun
a trap for small animals or birds
Pronunciation: / snɛr / (AmE), / snɛə / (BrE)
a trap, often with a noose, used for catching birds or small animals

Examples

A rabbit snare
A bird became entangled in the snare near the field.
She learned how to build a simple snare from tree branches and string.
The hunter set a snare in the woods to catch rabbits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trap - a device or enclosure designed to catch and retain animals
