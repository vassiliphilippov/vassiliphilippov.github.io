outfling

noun
act of throwing out
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌflɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌflɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of flinging or spreading something outward; the action or event of throwing out

Examples

Sudden outfling of stones
The sudden outfling of stones surprised the hikers as they passed the wall.
A dramatic outfling of confetti marked the end of the celebration.
The volcano's outfling of ash covered the nearby fields in gray dust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispersal - the act of spreading things over a wide area
  • antonym: gathering - the act of collecting things together
