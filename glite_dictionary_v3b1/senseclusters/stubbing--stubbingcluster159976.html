stubbing

noun
hitting body part
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of accidentally hitting or striking a part of the body, especially a toe, against an object

Examples

Stubbing your toe
Stubbing your toe on the door frame can ruin your morning.
He winced after stubbing his foot against the rock while hiking.
Children often cry after stubbing their toes on furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoiding - the act of keeping away from hitting something
  • synonym: bumping - the act of hitting something with a part of the body
