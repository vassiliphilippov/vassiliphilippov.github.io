applewood

noun
wood from an apple tree
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəlˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈapəlˌwʊd / (BrE)
the hard, fine-grained wood from any of several apple trees, often used for smoking food or woodworking

Examples

A piece of applewood
He carved the figurine from a piece of applewood he found in the orchard.
The pork was smoked over applewood for a sweet, delicate flavor.
They chose applewood because of its durability and attractive grain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apple tree - tree of the genus Malus that produces apples
