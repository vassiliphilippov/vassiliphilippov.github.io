Examples

An exothermic reaction releases heat
Combustion of gasoline is an exothermic reaction that powers car engines.
Mixing water and quicklime produces an exothermic reaction that releases a lot of heat.
When an exothermic reaction occurs, the temperature of the surroundings usually rises.
