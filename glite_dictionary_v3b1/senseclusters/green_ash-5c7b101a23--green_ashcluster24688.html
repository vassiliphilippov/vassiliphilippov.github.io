green ash

noun
North American species of ash tree
8.7
Pronunciation: / ɡrin æʃ / (AmE), / ɡriːn æʃ / (BrE)
a North American ash tree (Fraxinus pennsylvanica), distinguished by its glossy branchlets and pale lower leaf surfaces

Examples

A green ash tree
In the park, several green ash trees shaded the pathway.
The green ash is often used for landscaping because of its resilience to urban conditions.
We identified the tree in the backyard as a green ash by its light-colored leaves beneath.
