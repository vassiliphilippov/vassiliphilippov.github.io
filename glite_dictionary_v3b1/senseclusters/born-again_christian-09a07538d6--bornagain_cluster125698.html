born-again Christian

noun
Christian with renewed spiritual faith
Pronunciation: / ˌbɔrn əˈɡɛn ˈkrɪstʃən / (AmE), / ˌbɔːn əˈɡɛn ˈkrɪstʃən / (BrE)
A Christian who has experienced a dramatic personal conversion or renewal of religious faith, often emphasizing a spiritual rebirth in Jesus Christ.

Examples

Born-again Christian believer
After her conversion, she began identifying as a born-again Christian and attending church regularly.
He credits his success to his faith as a born-again Christian.
Many born-again Christians share stories of personal transformation and renewed purpose.
