baby tooth

noun
temporary tooth in children
1.0 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.biː tuːθ/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.biː tuːθ/ /; / /ˈbeɪ.biː tʊθ/ (BrE)
A temporary tooth in young children that is later replaced by a permanent tooth.

Examples

A baby tooth
A baby tooth usually falls out without causing much pain.
My daughter lost her first baby tooth at the age of six.
The dentist explained how a baby tooth is gradually pushed out by the permanent tooth.
