lecherous

adjective
having excessive sexual desire
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtʃ.ɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˈlɛtʃ.ər.əs / (BrE)
having or showing excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustful or lascivious

Examples

Lecherous smile
He wore a lecherous smile that made everyone uncomfortable.
She avoided the lecherous glances from the men at the party.
The manager was fired after several lecherous comments to his colleagues were reported.
