rowel

verb
to spur with a wheel
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.əl/ (BrE)
to prick or urge an animal, especially a horse, with a small spiked wheel on a spur

Examples

To rowel a horse
The cowboy roweled his horse gently to encourage it to move faster.
She roweled the animal, making sure not to hurt it with the spurs.
The rider quickly roweled the horse as they approached the final jump.
