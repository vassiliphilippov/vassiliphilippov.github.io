Crucifixion of Jesus

noun
the death of Jesus Christ by crucifixion
Pronunciation: / ˌkrusəˈfɪkʃən əv ˈdʒiːzəs / (AmE), / ˌkruːsɪˈfɪkʃən əv ˈdʒiːzəs / (BrE)
the execution of Jesus Christ by being nailed to a cross, regarded as a central event in Christianity
