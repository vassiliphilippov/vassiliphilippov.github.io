hogpen

noun
pig enclosure
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑɡˌpɛn / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌpɛn / (BrE)
an enclosure or pen where pigs are kept

Examples

A hogpen for pigs
The children watched the pigs play in the hogpen behind the farmhouse.
Every spring, the old hogpen was repaired to keep the animals safe.
A strong fence surrounded the hogpen to prevent the pigs from escaping.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barn - a large farm building for storing animals or crops
  • synonym: pigsty - an enclosure for pigs
