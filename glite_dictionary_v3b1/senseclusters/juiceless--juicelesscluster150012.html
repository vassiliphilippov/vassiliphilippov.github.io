juiceless

adjective
lacking juice; dry
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːsləs / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːslɪs / (BrE)
(of food, especially fruit) lacking juice; dry or without moisture.

Examples

Juiceless fruit
After several hours in the oven, the meat became tough and juiceless.
The orange looked fresh but turned out to be juiceless and disappointing.
The summer drought left most of the berries shriveled and juiceless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry - lacking moisture; not wet or juicy
