sunblock

noun
cream for protecting skin from the sun
1.0 1.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆsÊŒnËŒblÉ‘k / (AmE), / ËˆsÊŒnËŒblÉ’k / (BrE)
A cream or lotion applied to the skin to protect it from the sun's ultraviolet rays, preventing sunburn.

Examples

Apply sunblock cream
Don't forget to put on sunblock before you go to the beach.
Most dermatologists recommend using sunblock with a high SPF for outdoor activities.
She applied extra sunblock to her shoulders to avoid getting burned.
