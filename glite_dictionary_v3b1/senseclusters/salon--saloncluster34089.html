salon

noun
elegant sitting room for receiving guests
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑn / (AmE), / səˈlɒn / (BrE)
an elegant sitting room in a large house, used for receiving guests

Examples

Elegant salon
Her Parisian salon was renowned for lively discussions among artists and writers.
The guests gathered in the salon for afternoon tea before dinner was served.
The mansion's elegant salon overlooked the gardens through tall french windows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drawing room - a room in a large house where guests are received and entertained
