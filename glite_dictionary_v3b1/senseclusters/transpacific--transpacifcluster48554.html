transpacific

noun
Pacific Ocean crossing
6.7
Pronunciation: /træn.spə.ˈsɪf.ɪk/ (AmE), /træn.spə.ˈsɪf.ɪk/ (BrE)
a voyage, flight, or journey that crosses the Pacific Ocean from one side to the other

Examples

A transpacific journey
The airline launched a new transpacific route connecting Los Angeles and Tokyo.
A transpacific journey can take many hours depending on the departure and arrival cities.
Cargo ships often make transpacific crossings to transport goods between Asia and North America.
