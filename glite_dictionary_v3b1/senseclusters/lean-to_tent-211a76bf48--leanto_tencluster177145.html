lean-to tent

noun
tent attached to a building
9.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈliːn.tuː tent/ /; / /ˈliːn.tə tent/ /; / /ˈliːn.tuː tɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈliːn.tuː tent/ /; / /ˈliːn.tə tent/ /; / /ˈliːn.tuː tɪnt/ (BrE)
A tent with a single sloping roof that is attached to the side of a building or another structure for support.

Examples

A lean-to tent shelter
A lean-to tent can provide quick and effective protection from wind when anchored to a solid wall.
The hikers constructed a makeshift lean-to tent beside the old barn.
We set up the lean-to tent against the cabin for extra shelter.
