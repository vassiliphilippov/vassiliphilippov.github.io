People against Gangsterism and Drugs

proper noun
vigilante group in South Africa opposing drugs and gangs
Pronunciation: / ˈpipəl əˈɡɛnst ˈɡæŋstərɪzəm ənd drʌɡz / (AmE), / ˈpiːpl əˈɡenst ˈɡæŋstəˌrɪzəm ənd drʌɡz / (BrE)
A South African organization formed in 1996 to oppose drug lords and gangsterism, later accused of vigilante activities and linked to Islamic extremist groups.
