heterosexuality

noun
attraction to the opposite sex
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬·əˌsɛk·ʃuˈæl·ə·ti / (AmE), / ˌhɛt.əˌsɛk.ʃuˈæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
