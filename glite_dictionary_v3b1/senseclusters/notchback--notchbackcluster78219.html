notchback

noun
car body style
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑtʃˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈnɒtʃˌbæk / (BrE)
a car body style in which there is a clear, distinct step between the roof and the trunk

Examples

A notchback model
The manufacturer released a new notchback model with improved fuel efficiency.
She preferred the classic look of a notchback over a hatchback.
Many family cars in the 1980s featured a notchback design for a more traditional appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fastback - a car body style with a continuous slope from the roof to the rear
  • antonym: hatchback - a car with a rear door that swings upward to provide access to the cargo area
  • synonym: sedan - a car with a separate trunk and passenger compartment
