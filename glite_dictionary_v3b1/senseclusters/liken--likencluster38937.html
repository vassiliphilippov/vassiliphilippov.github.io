liken

verb
compare as similar to something else
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkən / (BrE)
to describe or regard someone or something as being similar to another person or thing

Examples

To liken to something
Historians often liken the economic crisis to the Great Depression.
Many critics liken the new artist's style to that of Monet.
She likened her childhood to a rollercoaster ride, full of ups and downs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compare - to examine two or more things to see similarities or differences
