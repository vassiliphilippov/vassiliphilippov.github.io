radiotoxic

adjective
harmful from radiation
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdioʊˈtɑksɪk / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəʊˈtɒksɪk / (BrE)
harmful or poisonous to living organisms due to the effects of radiation

Examples

Radiotoxic waste
Plutonium is considered extremely radiotoxic and must be handled with great care.
Scientists study the radiotoxic effects of certain isotopes on human cells.
Even small amounts of radiotoxic substances can pose serious health risks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmless - not causing any harm or damage
  • synonym: radioactive - emitting harmful radiation
  • synonym: toxic - poisonous or harmful to living things
