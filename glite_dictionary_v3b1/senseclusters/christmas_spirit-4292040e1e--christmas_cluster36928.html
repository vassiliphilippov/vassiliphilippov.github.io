Christmas spirit

noun
feeling of festivity at Christmas
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs ˈspɪrət / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
a feeling of happiness, generosity, and festivity associated with the Christmas season

Examples

The Christmas spirit of giving
Even as an adult, I try to keep the Christmas spirit alive in my heart every December.
His cheerful decorations brought the Christmas spirit to the whole neighborhood.
Volunteering at the shelter filled her with a genuine Christmas spirit.
