dialogic

adjective
interactive exchange
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving a back-and-forth exchange, especially between a user and a computer system or program

Examples

Dialogic interface
The software features a dialogic design that helps users solve problems step by step.
A dialogic approach in education encourages students to participate in discussions.
Modern chatbots rely on dialogic interactions to provide personalized assistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interactive - allowing communication or direct involvement between people or systems
  • antonym: monologic - relating to a single speaker or one-way communication
