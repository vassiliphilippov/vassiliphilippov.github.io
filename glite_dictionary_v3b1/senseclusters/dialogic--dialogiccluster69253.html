dialogic

adjective
interactive exchange
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving a back-and-forth exchange, especially between a user and a computer system or program
