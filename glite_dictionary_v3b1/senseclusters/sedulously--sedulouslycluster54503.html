sedulously

adverb
in a diligent and thorough manner
7.2 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛdʒələslɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛdjʊləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows careful and persistent effort or attention; diligently and thoroughly.

Examples

To sedulously work
He sedulously avoided distractions in order to finish his thesis on time.
She sedulously researched every detail of the project before presenting her findings.
The gardeners sedulously tended to the roses, ensuring they bloomed perfectly each year.
