Arctic ground squirrel

noun
large northern North American ground squirrel
3.1 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːrk.tɪk graʊnd ˈskwɜːr.əl/ /; / /ˈɑːr.tɪk graʊnd ˈskwɜːr.əl/ (AmE), /ˈɑːk.tɪk graʊnd ˈskwɪ.rəl/ (BrE)
