imageable

adjective
can be imaged
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be represented, captured, or visualized as an image, especially by medical or scientific equipment

Examples

Imageable tumor on MRI
The tumor was clearly imageable using the latest MRI technology.
Some ancient manuscripts are barely imageable due to their faded ink and fragile pages.
Doctors rely on imageable structures to diagnose internal injuries with ultrasound scans.

  • synonym: visible - able to be seen
