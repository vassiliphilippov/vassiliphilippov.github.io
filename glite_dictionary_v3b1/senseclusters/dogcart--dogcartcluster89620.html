dogcart

noun
small cart drawn by a dog
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌkɑːt / (BrE)
a small cart, typically with two wheels, that is pulled by a dog, historically used for carrying goods or people

Examples

An old dogcart
He restored the antique dogcart as a hobby, fascinated by its unique design.
In Victorian England, a dogcart was often used to deliver milk to customers' homes.
The old photograph showed a dogcart making its way down a country lane.
