nonmetropolitan

adjective
outside major cities
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnmɛtrəˈpɑlɪtən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmɛtrəˈpɒlɪtən / (BrE)
located outside a large city or metropolitan area; relating to rural or less urbanized regions

Examples

Nonmetropolitan rural areas
The government is investing in nonmetropolitan areas to boost economic development.
Many nonmetropolitan communities rely on agriculture as their main industry.
Healthcare access can be more limited in nonmetropolitan regions compared to urban centers.
Many nonmetropolitan areas have different local government structures than major cities.
The report compared economic growth in nonmetropolitan counties to that in metropolitan regions.
She moved from a nonmetropolitan district to London for her new job.

Related Terms

  • antonym: metropolitan - relating to a large city or its surrounding area
  • synonym: rural - relating to the countryside rather than the city
  • antonym: urban - relating to a city or town
