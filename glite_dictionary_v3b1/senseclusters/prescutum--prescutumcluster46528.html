prescutum

noun
insect thorax plate
9.9
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈskjuː.təm/ /; / /prɪ.ˈskjuː.təm/ (AmE), /prə.ˈskjuː.təm/ /; / /prɪ.ˈskjuː.təm/ (BrE)
the front section of the upper plate on the thorax of an insect

Examples

The prescutum plate
The prescutum is often the largest part of the thoracic plate in many insects.
Scientists examined the prescutum to identify differences between beetle species.
Damage to the prescutum can affect an insect's ability to move its wings.
