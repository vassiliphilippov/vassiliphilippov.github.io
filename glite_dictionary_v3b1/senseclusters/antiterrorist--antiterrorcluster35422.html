antiterrorist

noun
terrorism prevention agent
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪst / (BrE)
a person who works to prevent or combat terrorism, especially as part of a specialized police or military unit

Examples

An antiterrorist officer
The airport hired an experienced antiterrorist to oversee security operations during the busy season.
Members of the antiterrorist squad trained regularly to respond to potential threats.
An antiterrorist must stay alert and prepared for any suspicious activity at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterterrorist - a person or action aimed at preventing terrorism
  • antonym: terrorist - a person who uses violence and intimidation for political aims
