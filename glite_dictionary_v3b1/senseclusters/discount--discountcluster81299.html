discount

noun
reduction from usual price
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an amount or percentage by which the usual price of something is reduced

Examples

All store items are available at a 20% discount this week.
He bought the shoes at a discount during the holiday sales.
