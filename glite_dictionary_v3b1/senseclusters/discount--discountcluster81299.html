Synonyms

  • price reduction
    2.0 0.46/m
    a price reduction lowers the selling price in general, while a discount usually names a specific amount or percent taken off.
  • price cut
    1.4 0.24/m
    a price cut is often a public or sudden lowering of the usual price, while a discount is the amount taken off at sale.
  • markdown
    3.8 0.22/m
    a markdown is a retailer's temporary price drop to boost sales, while a discount is the amount or percent reduced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: surcharge - an additional charge or payment
