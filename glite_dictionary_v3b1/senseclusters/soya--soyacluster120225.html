soya

noun
the soya bean plant
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.jə / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪ.ə / (BrE)
A bushy annual plant native to Asia, widely grown for its nutritious seeds, used especially for food, oil production, and animal feed; also known as the soya bean.

Examples

Soya plant
Soya crops improve soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.
Many vegetarian foods are made with soya as a main ingredient.
The farmer planted rows of soya to harvest in the autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soybean - seed or plant of the soya bean; another common name for soya
