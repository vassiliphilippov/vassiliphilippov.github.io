mantle

noun
symbol of authority or responsibility
8.2 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.təl/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.təl/ (BrE)
a symbol of authority or responsibility; something that represents the position, role, or duties of a person, especially when passed from one person to another

Examples

Mantle of leadership
After the CEO retired, the mantle of leadership was passed to her successor.
He struggled to bear the mantle of responsibility that came with his new job.
Many hoped she would assume the mantle of party leader after the election.
