figure 8

noun
shape like the number eight
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjɚ eɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə eɪt / (BrE)
a shape or curve consisting of two loops that cross in the middle, resembling the number eight

Examples

A figure 8 shape
She traced a figure 8 in the sand with a stick.
The race track was shaped like a figure 8 to increase difficulty.
The skating routine included a perfect figure 8 on the ice.
