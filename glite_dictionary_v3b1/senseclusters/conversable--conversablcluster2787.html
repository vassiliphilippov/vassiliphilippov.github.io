conversable

adjective
sociable; talkative
6.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrsəbəl / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːsəbəl / (BrE)
willing or able to engage in conversation; sociable and easy to talk to

Examples

A conversable person
She is a conversable person who enjoys meeting new people at social events.
Despite being shy as a child, he became quite conversable in adulthood.
The conversable atmosphere at the café made everyone feel comfortable sharing their thoughts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reserved - slow to reveal emotions or opinions; not talkative
  • synonym: sociable - enjoying the company of others; friendly
  • synonym: talkative - inclined to talk a lot
