Synonyms

  • stormy
    3.1 1/m
    stormy describes weather or angry, changeable feelings in people or relationships, while tempestuous implies more violent, stormy emotion.
  • tumultuous
    7.5 3/m
    tumultuous shows loud confusion, disorder, or excited activity, while tempestuous adds violent, stormy emotion and strong personal upheaval.
  • turbulent
    6.6 3/m
    turbulent describes unrest or disorder in situations or times, while tempestuous focuses on stormy, emotional disturbance in people or relationships.
  • raging
    3.9 0.04/m
    raging means very intense or violent and often shows strong single feelings like anger, while tempestuous suggests broader stormy turmoil.
