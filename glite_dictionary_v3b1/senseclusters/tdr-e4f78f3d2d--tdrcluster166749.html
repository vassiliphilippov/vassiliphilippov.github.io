TDR

sym
spinal disc surgery
7.4
Pronunciation: / ti di ɑr / (AmE), / tiː diː ɑː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'total disc replacement', a surgical procedure to replace a damaged spinal disc with an artificial one

Examples

TDR surgery
After years of back pain, she decided to have a TDR to improve her mobility.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of TDR before scheduling the operation.
Many patients recover quickly after undergoing TDR for degenerative disc disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disc replacement - surgical procedure to replace a spinal disc
  • antonym: spinal fusion - surgical procedure to join two or more vertebrae
