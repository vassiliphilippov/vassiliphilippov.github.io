silver-white

adjective
white resembling silver in color
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪlvɚˈwaɪt / (AmE), / ˌsɪlvəˈwaɪt / (BrE)
having a white color that resembles the shine or tone of silver.

Examples

Silver-white color

The silver-white tips of the mountains shone in the morning light.
The moon cast a silver-white glow over the quiet lake.
