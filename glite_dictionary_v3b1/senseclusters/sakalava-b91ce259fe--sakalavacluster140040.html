Sakalava

noun
Malagasy dialect
Pronunciation: / sɑːkəˈlɑːvə / (AmE), / sækəˈlɑːvə / (BrE)
the variety or dialect of the Malagasy language spoken by the Sakalava people of Madagascar

Examples

Sakalava language
She learned to speak Sakalava while living in western Madagascar.
The documentary featured interviews in Sakalava to highlight local traditions.
Linguists study Sakalava to understand the diversity of Malagasy dialects.
