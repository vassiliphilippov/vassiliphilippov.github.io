mint

noun
large amount of money
Pronunciation: / mɪnt / (AmE), / mɪnt / (BrE)
a large amount of money, especially when acquired quickly or easily

Examples

Earn a mint
He hopes to earn a mint by investing in real estate.
It must have cost a mint to renovate that old mansion.
She made a mint from selling her startup company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortune - a very large amount of money or assets
  • antonym: pittance - a small amount of money
