grievable

adjective
causing sorrow
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡriːvəbəl / (BrE)
causing grief or sorrow; deserving to be mourned or regretted

Examples

Grievable loss
The loss of such a historic building is truly grievable for the entire community.
It was a grievable mistake that affected many people in the organization.
The end of their long friendship was a grievable event in both their lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: joyful - causing happiness or delight
  • synonym: mournful - causing or expressing sadness or grief
  • synonym: tragic - causing great sadness or suffering
