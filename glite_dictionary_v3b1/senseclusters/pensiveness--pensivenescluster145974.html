pensiveness

noun
deep serious thoughtfulness
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsɪvnɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsɪvnɪs /; / ˈpensɪvnəs / (BrE)
a state or quality of being deep in serious or reflective thought

Examples

His pensiveness was evident as he stared out the window, lost in thought.
