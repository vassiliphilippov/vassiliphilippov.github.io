somberly

adverb
in a serious or solemn manner
5.5 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ëÀêmb…öli / (AmE), / Ààs…ímb…ôli / (BrE)
In a serious, sad, solemn, or grave manner; without cheerfulness or brightness.

Examples

To speak somberly
He spoke somberly about the challenges facing the community.
She somberly delivered the news to the team.
The crowd listened somberly as the names of the victims were read aloud.
He walked sombrely through the silent, empty streets.
She spoke sombrely about the challenges facing the company.
The ceremony was conducted sombrely, with everyone dressed in black.
