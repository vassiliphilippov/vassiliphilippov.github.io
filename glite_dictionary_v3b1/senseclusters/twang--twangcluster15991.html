twang

noun
sharp vibrating sound, as from a plucked string
Pronunciation: / twæŋ / (AmE), / twæŋ / (BrE)
a sharp, vibrating sound, especially one produced by plucking a string

Examples

A guitar twang
Every arrow was released with a satisfying twang from the bow.
She heard a metallic twang as he snapped the wire with his fingers.
The twang of the guitar string filled the silent room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ring - a resonant or ringing sound
  • antonym: thud - a dull sound, as of something heavy hitting a surface
