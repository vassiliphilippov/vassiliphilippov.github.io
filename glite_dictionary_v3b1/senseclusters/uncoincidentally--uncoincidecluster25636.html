uncoincidentally

adverb
not by chance
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkoʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is not due to coincidence; as a result of a deliberate cause or connection

Examples

Used uncoincidentally in speech
Uncoincidentally, the announcement came just after the competitor released their new product.
The two friends met at the café uncoincidentally, having planned it earlier that day.
The changes in policy and the rise in profits were, uncoincidentally, closely linked.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coincidentally - in a way that happens by chance
  • synonym: deliberately - in a way that is intentional or planned
