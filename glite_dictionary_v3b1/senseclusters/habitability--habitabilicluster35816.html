habitability

noun
suitability for living in or on
Pronunciation: / ˌhæb.ə.təˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌhæb.ɪ.təˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or fit to live in or on

Examples

The habitability of a region
Factors such as climate and available resources affect the habitability of a region.
Scientists are studying the habitability of planets outside our solar system.
The habitability of the apartment was questioned after the water damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhabitability - the condition of being capable of being inhabited
  • antonym: uninhabitability - the condition of not being fit to live in or on
