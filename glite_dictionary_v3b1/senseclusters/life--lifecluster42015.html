life

noun
period between birth and now (or death)
1.0
Pronunciation: / laɪf / (AmE), / laɪf / (BrE)
the state or period of being alive, including all experiences and activities from birth to death

Examples

A happy life
All my life, I've dreamed of traveling around the world.
He spent most of his life working in the same company.
I've known her all her life and she's always been cheerful.
Doctors worked tirelessly to save the patient's life after the accident.
No one could explain how life first began on Earth.
Water is essential for all forms of life on the planet.
After the accident, her outlook on life completely changed.
He wanted to make the most of his life before it was too late.
The book is an inspiring story about overcoming obstacles in life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the end of life; the state of no longer living
  • synonym: existence - the state of being, especially with reference to living
  • synonym: lifetime - the duration of a person's life, from birth to death
