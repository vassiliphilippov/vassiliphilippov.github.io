downgrade

noun
a downward slope
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌɡreɪd / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnɡreɪd / (BrE)
a downward slope, especially on a road or railway

Examples

Steep downgrade
Cyclists must be careful when descending a steep downgrade on slick roads.
The car gained speed as it rolled down the downgrade.
The train picked up momentum as it traveled along the downgrade section of the track.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decline - a downward slope or movement
  • antonym: upgrade - an upward slope, especially on a road or railway
