slobbery

adjective
wet with saliva
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑbəri / (AmE), / ˈslɒbəri / (BrE)
very wet and covered with saliva, especially as a result of an animal's mouth

Examples

Slobbery dog toy
The puppy left a slobbery mess on the floor after playing with its toy.
She wiped her hand after receiving a slobbery greeting from the friendly dog.
His shirt was slobbery from holding the drooling baby all afternoon.
