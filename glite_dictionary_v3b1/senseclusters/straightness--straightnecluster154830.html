straightness

noun
trueness or directness of course or aim
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being direct or true in course toward a goal

Examples

Straightness of her answer
He admired the straightness of her answer, never dodging the question.
Pilots must constantly adjust to maintain the straightness of their flight path.
The straightness of her drive toward success impressed everyone at the company.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deviation - a turning aside from a course or standard
  • synonym: directness - the quality of being straightforward or direct in action or course
