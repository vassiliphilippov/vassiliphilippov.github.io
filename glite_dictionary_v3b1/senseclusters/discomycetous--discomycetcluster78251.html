discomycetous

adjective
relating to the Discomycetes fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskoʊmaɪˈsiːtəs / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəʊmaɪˈsiːtəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of fungi belonging to the subclass Discomycetes, which produce disc- or cup-shaped fruiting bodies.

Examples

Discomycetous fungi
Many discomycetous fungi are important decomposers in forest ecosystems.
The scientist observed several discomycetous species under the microscope.
This discomycetous organism produces distinctive cup-shaped fruiting bodies.
