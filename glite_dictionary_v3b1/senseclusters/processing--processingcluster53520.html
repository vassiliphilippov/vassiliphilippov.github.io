processing

noun
systematic preparation or treatment
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈprɒsɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
the action of preparing or treating something by carrying out a systematic series of actions, especially according to a prescribed procedure

Examples

Food processing
Advancements in food processing have made many products safer and longer-lasting.
After processing the evidence, the laboratory sent a detailed report to the police.
The processing of visa applications can take several weeks.
