namelessly

adverb
without a name
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪmləsli / (AmE), / ˈneɪmləsli / (BrE)
in a way that is without a name or not identified by name

Examples

Donated namelessly
The donation was made namelessly, so no one knew who gave the money.
She worked namelessly behind the scenes, never seeking recognition for her efforts.
Many artists have contributed namelessly to the mural, leaving their work unsigned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymously - in a way that does not reveal one's name or identity
