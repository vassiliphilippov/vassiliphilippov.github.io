oleum

noun
sulfur trioxide solution
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊliəm / (AmE), / ˈəʊliəm / (BrE)
a solution of sulfur trioxide in sulfuric acid, used in chemical manufacturing processes

Examples

Industrial oleum
The chemical plant uses oleum in the production of detergents and dyes.
Workers must wear protective gear when handling oleum because it is highly corrosive.
Special containers are required to safely store oleum at the factory.
