War of the Spanish Succession

proper noun
major European war (1701–1714) over the Spanish throne
8.9
Pronunciation: /wɔːr əv ðə ˈspæn.ɪʃ sək.ˈseʃ.ən/ (AmE), /wɔː əv ðə ˈspæn.ɪʃ sək.ˈseʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a major European conflict (1701–1714) fought over who should inherit the Spanish throne after the death of Charles II, involving most of the great powers of Europe.

Examples

The War of the Spanish Succession
Historians continue to debate the causes and consequences of the War of the Spanish Succession.
Many treaties were signed at the end of the War of the Spanish Succession.
The War of the Spanish Succession reshaped the balance of power in Europe.
