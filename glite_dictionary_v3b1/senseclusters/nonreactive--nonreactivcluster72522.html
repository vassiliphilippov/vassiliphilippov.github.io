nonreactive

adjective
not responsive
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnriˈæktɪv / (BrE)
not showing any emotional, physical, or chemical response to a stimulus or provocation

Examples

Nonreactive patient
Despite the loud noise, the patient remained nonreactive during the examination.
The teacher was surprised by how nonreactive the class was to the exciting announcement.
This material is nonreactive and will not corrode when exposed to chemicals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reactive - showing a response to a stimulus or provocation
  • synonym: unresponsive - not reacting or responding to something
