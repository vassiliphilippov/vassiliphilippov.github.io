malapportioned

verb
to divide unfairly
Pronunciation: / ˌmæl.əˈpɔːr.ʃənd / (AmE), / ˌmæl.əˈpɔː.ʃənd / (BrE)
allocated or divided, especially political representation or seats, in an unfair or unequal way.

Examples

To malapportion seats
The voting districts were malapportioned, giving some regions much more influence than others.
Critics argued that the resources had been malapportioned among the different departments.
After the census, it was clear the seats had been malapportioned for political gain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apportioned - divided or allocated fairly
  • synonym: gerrymandered - manipulated electoral boundaries for unfair advantage
