zoysia

noun
a resilient creeping grass, genus Zoysia
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɔɪ.zi.ə / (AmE), / ˈzɔɪ.zi.ə / (BrE)
a creeping grass of the genus Zoysia, often used for lawns and turf due to its dense growth and tolerance to heat and drought

Examples

Zoysia grass lawn
Experts recommend zoysia for areas with poor soil, thanks to its ability to thrive in tough conditions.
Our front lawn was recently replanted with zoysia to better withstand the summer heat.
The golf course uses zoysia for its fairways because it's durable and easy to maintain.
