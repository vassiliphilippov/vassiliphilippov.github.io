gluteus muscle

noun
large muscle of the buttock
Pronunciation: / ɡluˈtiəs ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ɡluːˈtiːəs ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
Any one of three large muscles in the buttock that move the thigh.

Examples

The gluteus muscle pain
An injury to the gluteus muscle can make walking or running quite painful.
Squats are a great exercise for strengthening the gluteus muscle and improving lower body power.
The physical therapist focused on stretching my gluteus muscle to relieve tension in my lower back.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: gluteus maximus - largest gluteal muscle, forming the bulk of the buttocks
  • synonym: gluteus medius - middle of the three gluteal muscles
  • degree lower: gluteus minimus - smallest of the three gluteal muscles
