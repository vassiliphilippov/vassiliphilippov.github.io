copyleft

verb
to license for free use
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.pɪ.left/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.pɪ.left/ (BrE)
to apply a copyleft license to a work, allowing others to freely use, modify, and distribute it under the same terms

Examples

To copyleft software
The developer chose to copyleft her new software so the community could improve it.
Many artists now copyleft their work to encourage collaboration and sharing.
He decided to copyleft his book, making it available for anyone to adapt or distribute.

Related Terms

  • antonym: copyright - to legally protect a work so others cannot use it without permission
  • synonym: open-source - to make software or content freely available for anyone to use or modify
