hardware

noun
metal tools and implements
2.7 7.97/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêrdÀåw…õr / (AmE), / Ààh…ëÀêdÀåw…õ…ô / (BrE)
tools, implements, or equipment, especially those made of metal, used to carry out practical tasks.

Examples

Hardware store tools
He keeps all the household hardware in the toolbox in the garage.
Old-fashioned hardware like cast iron pans is often more durable than modern alternatives.
The shop sells gardening hardware such as shovels, rakes, and hoes.

Synonyms

  • equipment
    1.9 84/m
    equipment includes all necessary tools and machines for a task, while hardware refers especially to metal tools used in practical work.
  • implement
    4.1 2.40/m
    implement is a single tool made for a specific job, while hardware usually names many metal tools or fittings used in work.
  • gear
    2.1 31/m
    gear is the equipment for an activity, often informal or varied, while hardware is mainly metal tools for practical tasks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: software - programs and operating information used by a computer
