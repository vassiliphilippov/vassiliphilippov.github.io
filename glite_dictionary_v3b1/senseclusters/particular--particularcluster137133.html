particular

adjective
fussy or exacting about details
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
very careful and exact about details; fussy or hard to please

Examples

Particular about food
My grandfather was particular about the freshness of his morning coffee.
She is very particular about the way her clothes are folded.
Some customers can be extremely particular when it comes to service.
