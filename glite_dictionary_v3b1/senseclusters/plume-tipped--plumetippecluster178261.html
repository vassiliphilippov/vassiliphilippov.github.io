plume-tipped

adjective
having a plume at the tip
Pronunciation: / plum tɪpt / (AmE), / pljuːm tɪpt / (BrE)
having a tip or end adorned with or resembling a feather or plume, especially in plants or animals.

Examples

Plume-tipped feathers
Several species of birds have plume-tipped tails that fan out during courtship displays.
The plume-tipped flower stood out among the grasses with its feathery ends.
We admired the plume-tipped reeds swaying gently by the lake shore.
