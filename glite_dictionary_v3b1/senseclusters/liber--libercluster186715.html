liber

noun
book (Latin term)
Pronunciation: / ˈliːbər / (AmE), / ˈliːbə / (BrE)
a book, especially used in Latin or scholarly contexts to refer to a written work

Examples

Ancient liber
The scholar referenced an ancient liber in his research on Roman history.
In classical studies, the term liber often appears in citations of Latin texts.
The rare manuscripts included a beautifully illustrated liber from the Middle Ages.

Related Terms
