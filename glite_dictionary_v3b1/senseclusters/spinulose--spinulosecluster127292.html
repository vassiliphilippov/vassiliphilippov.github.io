spinulose

adjective
with small spines
9.9
Pronunciation: / spɪˈnjuːloʊs / (AmE), / spɪˈnjuːləʊs / (BrE)
having small spines or spine-like projections, especially on the surface of a plant or animal

Examples

Spinulose stems
The cactus has spinulose stems that protect it from animals.
Under the microscope, the insect's legs appeared spinulose with many tiny projections.
Some ferns have spinulose leaves that feel rough to the touch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; not rough or spiny
  • synonym: spiny - covered with or having many spines
