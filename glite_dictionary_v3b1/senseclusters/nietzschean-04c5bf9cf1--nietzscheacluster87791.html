Nietzschean

noun
follower of Nietzsche
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈniːtʃiən / (AmE), / ˈniːtʃiən / (BrE)
a person who follows or supports the philosophy or ideas of Friedrich Nietzsche

Examples

A Nietzschean thinker
The young professor was a committed Nietzschean, often defending individual excellence over egalitarian policies.
As a self-described Nietzschean, she often challenged traditional moral values in her essays.
The debate featured a Nietzschean arguing for the importance of personal strength and willpower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: existentialist - a person who believes in existentialism, a philosophy emphasizing individual freedom and choice
