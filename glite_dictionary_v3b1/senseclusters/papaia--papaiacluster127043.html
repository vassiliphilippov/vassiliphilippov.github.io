papaia

noun
tropical fruit tree; variant of papaya
Pronunciation: / pəˈpaɪ.ə / (AmE), / pəˈpaɪ.ə / (BrE)
A tropical American shrub or small tree with large deeply divided leaves and oblong yellow fruit, commonly known as papaya.

Examples

A papaia fruit
In many markets, papaia is sold alongside mangos and pineapples.
She planted a papaia tree in her garden to enjoy its tropical fruit each year.
The ripe papaia is sweet and juicy, perfect for a summer breakfast.
