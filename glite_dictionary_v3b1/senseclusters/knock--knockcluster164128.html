knock

verb
hit or collide with force
Pronunciation: / nɑk / (AmE), / nɒk / (BrE)
to hit or strike something or someone with a sudden or forceful blow, often by accident

Examples

To knock your head
Be careful not to knock your head on the low ceiling.
I accidentally knocked my elbow against the door frame on my way out.
The bike knocked into a pole and fell over.
He accidentally knocked the lamp off the table.
She knocked on the door before entering the room.
The boy knocked the ball over the fence with one swing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bump - to hit or collide with something, often accidentally
  • synonym: strike - to deliver a blow or impact to something
  • degree lower: tap - to touch lightly, especially so as to make a noise
