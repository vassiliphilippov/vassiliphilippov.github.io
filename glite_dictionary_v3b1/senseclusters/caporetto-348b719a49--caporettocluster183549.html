Caporetto

noun
1917 WWI battle; Italian defeat
Pronunciation: / ˌkæpəˈrɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌkapəˈrɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a major battle of World War I in 1917, in which Italian forces suffered a significant defeat by combined Austrian and German armies near the town of Caporetto (now Kobarid, Slovenia)

Examples

Caporetto battle
After Caporetto, Allied forces provided more support to stabilize the Italian front.
Historians still debate the causes of the defeat at Caporetto in 1917.
The disaster at Caporetto forced the Italian army into a long retreat during World War I.
