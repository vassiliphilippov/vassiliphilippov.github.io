unstoppably

adverb
without being stopped
4.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑːpəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be stopped or prevented

Examples

Flow unstoppably
The river flowed unstoppably after days of heavy rain, flooding the nearby fields.
She laughed unstoppably at the comedian's jokes, unable to catch her breath.
Rumors spread unstoppably through the small town, reaching everyone within hours.
The wildfire spread unstoppably through the dry forest, threatening nearby homes.
She worked unstoppably toward her goal, never letting setbacks slow her down.
The crowd cheered unstoppably as the team scored the winning goal in the final seconds.
