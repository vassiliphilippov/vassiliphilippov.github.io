enclavement

noun
state of isolation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈkleɪvmənt /; / ɪnˈkleɪvmənt / (AmE), / ɛnˈkleɪvmənt /; / ɪnˈkleɪvmənt / (BrE)
the state or process of being enclosed, isolated, or set apart within a surrounding area or group
