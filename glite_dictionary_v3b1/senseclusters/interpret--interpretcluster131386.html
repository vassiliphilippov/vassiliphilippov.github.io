interpret

verb
to perform or render artistically
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɝːprət / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːprət / (BrE)
to perform a piece of music, a role, or a work of art in a way that shows your own understanding or feelings about it

Examples

To interpret a song
Many critics praised how the orchestra interpreted the complex symphony.
She interpreted the song with great emotion during the competition.
The actor interpreted the character in a way that surprised the audience.

  • synonym: render - to perform or present something, especially artistically
