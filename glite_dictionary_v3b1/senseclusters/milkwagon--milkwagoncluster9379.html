milkwagon

noun
wagon for delivering milk
9.2 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌwæɡən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌwaɡən / (BrE)
A wagon used for delivering milk, especially in the past when milk was delivered directly to households.

Examples

The milkwagon arrives
Children used to wait eagerly for the milkwagon to arrive with fresh bottles.
His grandfather once drove a horse-drawn milkwagon through the city.
The old milkwagon rattled down the street every morning at dawn.
