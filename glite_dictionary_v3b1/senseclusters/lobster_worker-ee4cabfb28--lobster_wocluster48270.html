lobster worker

noun
person who catches lobsters
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbstər ˈwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈlɒbstə ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to catch lobsters, usually for commercial purposes

Examples

A lobster worker at sea
As a lobster worker, she spends long hours at sea during the summer season.
Many coastal towns rely on lobster workers for their main source of income.
The life of a lobster worker can be both challenging and rewarding.
