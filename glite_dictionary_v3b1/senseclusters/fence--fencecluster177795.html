fence

noun
one who deals in stolen goods
Pronunciation: / fɛns / (AmE), / fɛns / (BrE)
a person who buys and sells stolen goods, acting as an intermediary between thieves and buyers

Examples

A fence for jewelry
He acted as a fence for the stolen electronics, quickly selling them to unsuspecting buyers.
It's hard for thieves to make money without a reliable fence to move the goods.
The police arrested the fence who had bought jewelry from the burglar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: receiver - a person who receives stolen goods
