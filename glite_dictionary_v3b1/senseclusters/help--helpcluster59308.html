help

noun
person employed to assist with tasks
2.5
Pronunciation: / hɛlp / (AmE), / hɛlp / (BrE)

Examples

The help cleaned
He asked the help to arrange the chairs before the guests arrived.
She has help come in three times a week to clean the house.
The restaurant hired extra help to manage the dinner rush.
