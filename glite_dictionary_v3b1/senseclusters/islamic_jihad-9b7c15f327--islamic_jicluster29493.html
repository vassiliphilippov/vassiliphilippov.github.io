Islamic Jihad

proper noun
Shiite militant group allied with Iran
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈslɑːmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd / (AmE), / ɪˈslæmɪk dʒɪˈhɑːd / (BrE)
