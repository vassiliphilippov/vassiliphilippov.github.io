a spit in the ocean

noun
insignificant contribution or amount
7.4
Pronunciation: / ə spɪt ɪn ði ˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / ə spɪt ɪn ði ˈəʊʃən / (BrE)
a very small or insignificant amount compared to what is needed or compared to the whole situation

Examples

A spit in the ocean
Giving up one plastic bag is a spit in the ocean when it comes to reducing pollution.
His efforts to solve the issue were just a spit in the ocean against the scale of the crisis.
The money we raised was a spit in the ocean compared to what the project needed.

Related Terms
