laptop

noun
portable personal computer
5.8 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlæp.tɑp / (AmE), / ˈlæp.tɒp / (BrE)
A portable computer that is small and light enough to be used on one's lap.

Examples

She carries her laptop to the coffee shop every morning to get some work done.
The batterylife of this laptop lasts up to twelve hours on a single charge.
