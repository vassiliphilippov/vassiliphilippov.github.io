claw hatchet

noun
hatchet with nail-pulling claw
Pronunciation: / klɔː ˈhætʃɪt / (AmE), / klɔː ˈhætʃɪt / (BrE)
A small axe with a split, curved back used for pulling nails as well as cutting.

Examples

A claw hatchet tool
A claw hatchet is ideal for small demolition tasks and pulling nails from wood.
He packed his claw hatchet along with other essential tools for the hiking trip.
The carpenter reached for his claw hatchet to remove the stubborn nail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: claw hammer - a hammer with a curved, forked end for pulling nails
