participative

adjective
involving group input
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈtɪsəˌteɪtɪv / (AmE), / pɑːˈtɪsɪpətɪv / (BrE)
involving or encouraging the active involvement of many people in a process, activity, or decision-making

Examples

Participative management style
The manager used a participative approach to make sure everyone felt included in the decision.
A participative classroom encourages students to share their ideas and opinions.
Many organizations are moving toward a more participative style of leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authoritarian - favoring strict obedience to authority with little input from others
  • synonym: collaborative - involving two or more people working together
