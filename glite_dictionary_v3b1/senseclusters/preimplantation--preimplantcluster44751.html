preimplantation

noun
embryo stage before implantation
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɪmˌplænˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːɪmˌplænˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the stage or period of an embryo before it implants in the uterus

Examples

Preimplantation stage
Researchers study the preimplantation period to improve the success rates of in vitro fertilization.
Genetic testing can be performed during the preimplantation stage to detect certain inherited conditions.
The preimplantation development of embryos is critical for understanding early human growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: implantation - the process by which an embryo attaches to the uterus
