bibliophilic

adjective
having a love of books; relating to bibliophiles
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪbliəˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a bibliophile; having a love of books.

Examples

Bibliophilic book lover
He decorated his house in a distinctly bibliophilic style, with shelves in every room.
Her bibliophilic tendencies led her to collect first editions and rare manuscripts.
The bibliophilic atmosphere of the old library drew in book lovers from all over town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bibliophile - a person who loves or collects books
