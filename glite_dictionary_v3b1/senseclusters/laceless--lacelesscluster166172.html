laceless

adjective
without laces
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈleɪsləs / (BrE)
without laces, especially referring to shoes or other items that are usually fastened with laces

Examples

Laceless shoes
He prefers laceless shoes because they are easy to put on.
The new sneakers are completely laceless, making them perfect for quick changes.
Many children find laceless footwear more convenient than traditional shoes with laces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laced - having laces or fastened with laces
  • synonym: slip-on - a shoe that does not use laces or fasteners
