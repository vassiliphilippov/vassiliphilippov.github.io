stress

verb
experience mental or emotional tension
1.8
Pronunciation: / strɛs / (AmE), / strɛs / (BrE)
To experience mental or emotional tension or worry, especially as a result of demanding circumstances.

Examples

To stress about exams
If you stress too much, it can affect your health.
Many people stress over financial issues during the holidays.
She began to stress about the upcoming exam and couldn't sleep.

  • synonym: worry - to feel anxious or concerned about something
