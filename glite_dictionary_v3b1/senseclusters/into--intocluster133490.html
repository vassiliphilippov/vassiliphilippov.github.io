into

preposition
movement inside
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ˈɪntu / (BrE)
used to indicate movement or direction toward the inside or interior of something

Examples

Pour into a glass
She poured the juice into the glass carefully to avoid spilling.
The children ran into the house when it started to rain.
He put his keys into his pocket before leaving.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in - used to indicate location within something
  • antonym: out of - used to indicate movement from the inside to the outside
