Age of Reptiles

noun
Mesozoic era when reptiles dominated the Earth
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒ əv ˈrɛptaɪlz / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒ əv ˈrɛptaɪlz / (BrE)
the geological era from about 230 million to 63 million years ago, dominated by reptiles such as dinosaurs; also known as the Mesozoic Era

Examples

The Age of Reptiles era
Many dinosaur fossils have been found from the Age of Reptiles.
Paleontologists study the Age of Reptiles to understand Earth's prehistoric life.
The extinction event at the end of the Age of Reptiles led to the rise of mammals.
