glue

verb
join or attach with glue
1.0 4.95/m
Pronunciation: / …°luÀê / (AmE), / …°luÀê / (BrE)
to join or attach something using glue; to stick things together as with glue

Examples

To glue paper
He carefully glued the fragments of the vase back together.
Please glue the label to the package before shipping it.
The children spent the afternoon gluing colored paper onto their art projects.

Synonyms

  • stick
    1.0 29/m
    stick is a common word for fixing things by adhesion, while glue is the actual adhesive used to join surfaces.
  • bond
    3.2 1.93/m
    bond is a strong joining often by force or adhesive, while glue is a common sticky substance used to join things.
  • paste
    2.9 5.40/m
    paste is a soft, wet substance used to join paper or cover surfaces, while glue is a wider term for adhesives.
  • attach
    2.5 39/m
    attach means to fasten or connect things in many ways, while glue means to join them using an adhesive.
  • affix
    5.0 2.32/m
    affix is a formal word for fastening or fixing something to a surface, while glue specifically means using an adhesive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detach - to separate or unfasten something that is attached
  • synonym: stick - to attach things so that they stay together, especially using an adhesive
