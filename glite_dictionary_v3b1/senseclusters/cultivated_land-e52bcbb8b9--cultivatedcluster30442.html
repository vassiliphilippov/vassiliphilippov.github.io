cultivated land

noun
land used for growing crops
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌl.tɪˌveɪ.tɪd lænd / (AmE), / ˈkʌl.tɪ.veɪ.tɪd lænd / (BrE)
Land that has been prepared and used for growing crops by plowing, sowing, and raising plants.

Examples

Cultivated land for crops
After years of neglect, the old fields were restored to productive cultivated land.
Much of the cultivated land in this region is used to grow wheat and corn.
The farmer expanded the area of cultivated land to increase his crop yield.
