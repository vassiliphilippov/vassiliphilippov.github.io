Sarcochilus falcatus

noun
Australian orchid species
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑrkoʊˈkaɪləs fælˈkeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌsɑːkəˈkaɪləs falˈkeɪtəs / (BrE)
A small Australian orchid with loosely arranged racemes of fragrant white flowers marked with purple and orange on the lip.
