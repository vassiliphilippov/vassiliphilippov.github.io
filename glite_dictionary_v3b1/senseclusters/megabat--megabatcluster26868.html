A large fruit-eating bat found in warm and tropical regions of the Old World, belonging to the family Pteropodidae.

Examples

A megabat species
Many species of megabat play an important role in pollinating tropical plants.
The flying fox is among the largest megabat species in the world.
Unlike microbats, a megabat navigates mainly by sight and smell rather than echolocation.
