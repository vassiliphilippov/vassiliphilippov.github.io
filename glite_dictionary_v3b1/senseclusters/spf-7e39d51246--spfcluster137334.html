SPF

noun
email authentication framework
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɛs pi ˈɛf / (AmE), / ɛs pi ˈɛf / (BrE)
An email authentication framework that lets domain owners specify which mail servers are authorized to send mail for that domain, using DNS records to help prevent address spoofing.

Examples

Email SPF record
The IT team added an SPF record to authorize their mail servers.
Before sending the campaign, she checked the company's SPF to avoid deliverability problems.
Many receiving servers reject messages that fail an SPF check as possible spam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Sender Policy Framework - the full name of the SPF email authentication protocol
