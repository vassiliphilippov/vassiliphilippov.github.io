blacklegged

adjective
affected by blackleg
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈblæ.kleɡd/ (AmE), /ˈblæ.kleɡd/ (BrE)
affected by blackleg, an infectious disease that causes darkening or decay of legs or stems in animals or plants

Examples

Blacklegged cattle
The farmer isolated the blacklegged cattle to stop the infection from spreading.
Several blacklegged plants in the field showed signs of stem decay.
Veterinarians warn that blacklegged animals should be treated quickly to prevent losses.
