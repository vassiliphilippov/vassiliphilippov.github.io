rank

noun
row or line of people
8.1
Pronunciation: / ræŋk / (AmE), / ræŋk / (BrE)
a row or line of people, especially soldiers or police, standing side by side

Examples

Rank of soldiers
At the ceremony, the children formed a neat rank along the edge of the playground.
The soldiers stood in a rank that stretched the length of the parade ground.
There were several ranks of police officers blocking the entrance.
