nonperiodic

adjective
not recurring at regular intervals
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌpɪriˈɑdɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪk / (BrE)
Not occurring or recurring at regular intervals.

Examples

Nonperiodic events
Some natural phenomena, such as earthquakes, are nonperiodic and difficult to predict.
The arrangement of atoms in a quasicrystal is nonperiodic, unlike in a normal crystal.
The scientist observed a nonperiodic pattern in the data that did not repeat regularly.
