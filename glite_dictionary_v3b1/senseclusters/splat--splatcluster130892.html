splat

noun
sound or mark of wet impact
Pronunciation: / splæt / (AmE), / splæt / (BrE)
a sound or mark made when something wet or soft hits a surface with force

Examples

A splat of paint
A big splat of paint landed on her shirt.
He slipped in the mud and landed with a messy splat.
The egg hit the sidewalk with a loud splat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - a sound or mark caused by something striking liquid or a liquid striking something
