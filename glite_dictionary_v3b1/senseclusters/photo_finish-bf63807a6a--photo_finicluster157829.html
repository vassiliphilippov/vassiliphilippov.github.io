photo finish

noun
very close race finish decided by photo
5.3 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ ˈfɪn.ɪʃ/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.təʊ ˈfɪn.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ ˈfɪn.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
a very close finish in a race or contest, especially one where the winner is determined by examining a photograph taken at the finish line

Examples

A photo finish race
It was such a close contest that only a photo finish could determine the winner.
The athletes pushed themselves so hard that the championship came down to a photo finish.
The race ended in a photo finish with both runners crossing the line almost simultaneously.
