cube

noun
multi-dimensional data structure (OLAP)
7.0
Pronunciation: /kjuːb/ (AmE), /kjuːb/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially data warehousing and OLAP) a multi-dimensional data structure that organizes measures (for example, sales or counts) across multiple dimensions (such as time, product, and region) to allow fast aggregation and analysis.

Examples

A data cube
The analysts drilled down the cube to compare regional sales across time.
We built a cube with dimensions for product, region, and month to speed reports.
Using the OLAP cube, she quickly found which city had the biggest revenue increase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: OLAP cube - an implementation of a cube used by OLAP systems for fast multi-dimensional queries and analysis
  • synonym: data cube - a multi-dimensional array of values commonly used in data warehousing and OLAP
