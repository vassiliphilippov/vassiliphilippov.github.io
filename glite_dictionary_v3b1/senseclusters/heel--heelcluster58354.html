heel

verb
to tilt or lean to one side
7.0
Pronunciation: / hil /; / hiːl / (AmE), / hiːl / (BrE)
(of a boat or object) to tilt or lean to one side, especially because of wind or movement

Examples

Boat heels over

As the yacht heeled sharply, everyone grabbed onto the railings for support.
If the boat heels too much, it can become unstable and capsize.
