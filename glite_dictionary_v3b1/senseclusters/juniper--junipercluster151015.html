juniper

noun
desert shrub with small white flowers, biblical reference
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːnəpɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːnɪpə / (BrE)
A desert shrub of Syria and Arabia with small white flowers, mentioned in the Old Testament, sometimes classified in the genus Genista.

Examples

Archaeologists debated whether the juniper mentioned in Scripture is actually a species of Genista.
