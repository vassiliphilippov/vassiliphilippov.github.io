Yuman

proper noun
Native American group
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːmən / (AmE), / ˈjuːmən / (BrE)
a member of a group of Native American peoples from the southwestern United States and northern Mexico

Examples

Many Yuman communities continue to celebrate their cultural heritage in Arizona and California.
Researchers have documented the languages spoken by various Yuman groups in the region.
