sempiternity

noun
endless duration; perpetuity
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛm.pɪˈtɝː.nə.ti / (AmE), / ˌsɛm.pɪˈtɜː.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being everlasting or perpetual; endless duration

Examples

The sempiternity of love
Poets often speak of love as having sempiternity beyond the bounds of time.
Some philosophers have debated whether true sempiternity can ever be achieved by mortal beings.
The sempiternity of the universe has been a topic of speculation for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternity - infinite or unending time
  • antonym: temporality - state of existing within or having some relationship with time
