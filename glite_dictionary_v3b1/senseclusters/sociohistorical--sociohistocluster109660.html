sociohistorical

adjective
social and historical

Examples

Sociohistorical changes
The documentary examines the sociohistorical changes that shaped the city over the last century.
Understanding the sociohistorical context helps explain why these traditions developed.
Her research focuses on the sociohistorical influences affecting language use in different communities.
