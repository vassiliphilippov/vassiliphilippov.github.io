agrarian

noun
supporter of farming
Pronunciation: / əˈɡrɛriən / (AmE), / əˈɡreəriən / (BrE)
a person who supports or is involved in farming or rural life

Examples

An agrarian leader
The agrarian spoke passionately about the importance of sustainable farming practices.
Many agrarians gathered to protest changes in land ownership laws.
As an agrarian, she valued rural traditions and agricultural life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • antonym: urbanite - a person who lives in a city
