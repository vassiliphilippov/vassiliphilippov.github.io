random

noun
unknown person
Pronunciation: / ˈrændəm / (AmE), / ˈrændəm / (BrE)
a person who is unknown or not part of a particular group, especially in informal or social situations

Examples

A random at the party
A couple of randoms showed up at the party uninvited.
We were just hanging out when some randoms joined our table.
The event was open to everyone, so there were a lot of randoms I had never met before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: friend - a person whom one knows and has a bond with
  • synonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
  • synonym: stranger - a person whom one does not know
