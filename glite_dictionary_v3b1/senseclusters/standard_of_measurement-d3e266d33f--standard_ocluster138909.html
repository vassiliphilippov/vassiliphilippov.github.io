standard of measurement

noun
recognized reference for measurement
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɚd əv ˈmɛʒɚmənt / (AmE), / ˈstandəd əv ˈmɛʒəmənt / (BrE)
an officially recognized or generally accepted unit or reference used as a basis to measure or compare quantities or qualities

Examples

The standard of measurement for length
The standard of measurement for length in most countries is the meter.
We must agree on a common standard of measurement before comparing results.
Without a clear standard of measurement, scientific data can be misleading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benchmark - a standard or point of reference against which things may be compared
  • synonym: yardstick - a standard used for comparison
