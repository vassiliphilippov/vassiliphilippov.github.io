spine

noun
sharp projection on a plant
9.9 3.4
Pronunciation: /spaɪn/ (AmE), /spaɪn/ (BrE)
a sharp, stiff projection on a plant, animal, or object, such as a thorn, quill, or needle-like structure

Examples
