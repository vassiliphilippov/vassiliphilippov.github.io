Hellespont

proper noun
strait between Aegean and Sea of Marmara
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlɪˌspɒnt / (AmE), / ˈhɛlɪspɒnt / (BrE)
the narrow strait between the Aegean Sea and the Sea of Marmara, separating European Turkey from Asian Turkey
