shoulder cord

noun
braided cord worn on uniform shoulder
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər kɔrd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə kɔːd / (BrE)
a decorative braided cord worn on one shoulder as part of military or ceremonial uniforms

Examples

A shoulder cord on uniform
Cadets are required to attach the shoulder cord to their uniforms during parades.
The blue shoulder cord indicated his position in the regiment.
The officer wore a bright gold shoulder cord as part of his ceremonial attire.
