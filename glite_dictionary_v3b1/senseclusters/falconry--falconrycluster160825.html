falconry

noun
the sport of training falcons to hunt
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfæl.kən.ri / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl.kən.ri / (BrE)
the art and practice of training falcons or other birds of prey to hunt wild animals and return to their handler

Examples

The art of falconry
Many medieval nobles considered falconry a symbol of status and refinement.
She developed a passion for falconry after attending a demonstration in the countryside.
The ancient sport of falconry requires patience, skill, and a deep understanding of birds of prey.
