white goods

noun
large household appliances (refrigerator, washing machine)
Pronunciation: / waɪt ɡʊdz / (AmE), / waɪt ɡʊdz / (BrE)
large electrical household appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines, or stoves, typically finished in white enamel.

The store is offering discounts on all white goods this weekend, including fridges and washing machines.
