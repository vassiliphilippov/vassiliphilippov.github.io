patchouly

noun
heavy perfume from the patchouli plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈtʃuːli / (AmE), / pəˈtʃuːli / (BrE)
A heavy, musky perfume made from the oil of the patchouli plant, often used in fragrances and incense.

Examples

Patchouly perfume scent
Many perfumes in the 1970s featured patchouly as a base note.
She wore a scarf scented with patchouly, filling the room with its rich aroma.
The shop was filled with incense and the distinctive fragrance of patchouly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patchouli - the plant (Pogostemon cablin) from which patchouly oil is derived
