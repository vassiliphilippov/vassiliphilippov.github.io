procellariiform seabird

noun
long-winged seabird with tubular nostrils

Pronunciation: /prə.sel.ə.ˈrɪ.fɑːrm ˈsiː.bɜːrd/ /; / /prə.sel.ə.ˈrɪ.fɔːrm ˈsiː.bɜːrd/ (AmE), /prə.sel.ə.ˈrɪ.fɔːm ˈsiː.bɜːd/ (BrE)
