sodium salicylate

noun
analgesic and antipyretic salt
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊdiəm səˈlɪsɪˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌsəʊdiəm səˈlɪsɪˌleɪt / (BrE)
A white crystalline salt used as an analgesic and antipyretic, often in the treatment of pain and fever.
