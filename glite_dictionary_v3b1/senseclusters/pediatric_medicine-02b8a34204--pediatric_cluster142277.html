pediatric medicine

noun
medical care of children
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdiˈætrɪk ˈmɛdəsən / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪˈatrɪk ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
The branch of medicine concerned with the medical care and treatment of infants, children, and adolescents.
