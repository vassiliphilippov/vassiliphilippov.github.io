nonquantitative

adjective
not numerical
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌkwɑntɪˈteɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌkwɒntɪˈteɪtɪv / (BrE)
not involving or based on numerical measurement or quantity

Examples

Nonquantitative research method
The researchers chose a nonquantitative method to explore participants' personal experiences.
Her analysis was entirely nonquantitative, focusing on themes rather than numbers.
A nonquantitative approach can provide deeper insight into complex social issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: qualitative - relating to the quality or nature of something rather than its quantity
  • antonym: quantitative - involving the measurement or amount of something in numbers
