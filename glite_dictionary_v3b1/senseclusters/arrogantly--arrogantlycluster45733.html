arrogantly

adverb
in an arrogant manner
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrəɡəntli / (AmE), / ˈærəɡəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities; with arrogance.

Examples

To arrogantly act proud
He arrogantly assumed that he was the only one qualified for the job.
She spoke arrogantly to her colleagues, ignoring their opinions completely.
The manager arrogantly dismissed all suggestions, insisting his way was best.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming manner
  • synonym: proudly - in a way that shows pride
