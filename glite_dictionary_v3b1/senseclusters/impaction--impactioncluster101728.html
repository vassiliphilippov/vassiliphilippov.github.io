impaction

noun
sharp collision or forceful contact
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpækʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˈpækʃən / (BrE)
A sharp collision or forceful contact produced by striking or dashing against something.

Examples

Hammer impaction
Repeated impaction of the hammer weakened the metal plate.
Sudden impaction can damage even the most durable materials.
The impaction of the meteorite caused a large crater in the desert.
