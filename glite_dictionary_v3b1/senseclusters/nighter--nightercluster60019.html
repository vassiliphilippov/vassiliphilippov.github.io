nighter

noun
all-night event
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtə / (BrE)
an event, party, or performance that lasts all night or continues until morning

Examples

A club nighter
The club is hosting a special nighter with live music and dancing until dawn.
After the nighter, everyone was exhausted but happy from a night of fun.
They planned a nighter to celebrate the end of exams with friends.
