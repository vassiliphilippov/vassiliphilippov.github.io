John Constable

proper noun
English landscape painter (1776–1837)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑn ˈkʌn.stə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒn ˈkʌn.stə.bl̩ / (BrE)
(1776–1837) an English painter known for his landscape paintings of the English countryside.
