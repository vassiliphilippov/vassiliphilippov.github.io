mancala

noun
traditional board game
8.7
Pronunciation: / mænˈkɑːlə / (AmE), / mænˈkɑːlə / (BrE)
a traditional board game in which players move stones or seeds between pits on a board, aiming to capture more pieces than the opponent

Examples

To play mancala game
Children in many countries enjoy playing mancala with colorful stones and wooden boards.
He taught his friends the rules of mancala during their game night.
The museum displayed a beautifully carved mancala board from West Africa.

  • synonym: checkers - a board game where players move pieces diagonally to capture the opponent's pieces
