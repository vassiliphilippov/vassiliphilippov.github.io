Festuca

noun
genus of perennial grasses (fescues)
Pronunciation: / fɛˈstuːkə /; / fɛsˈtuːkə / (AmE), / fɛsˈtjuːkə / (BrE)
a genus of tufted perennial grasses, commonly called fescues, found in temperate regions and often used for lawns and pastures
