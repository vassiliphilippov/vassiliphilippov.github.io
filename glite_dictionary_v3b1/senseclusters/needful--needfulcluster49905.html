needful

adjective
necessary; required
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdfəl / (AmE), / ˈniːdfəl / (BrE)
necessary; required for a particular purpose or to achieve a result, often used in formal or old-fashioned contexts.

Examples

The needful action
Please do whatever is needful to resolve the issue quickly.
The package included all the needful supplies for the expedition.
The workers were given the needful tools to complete their job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessary - needed to be done, achieved, or present; essential
  • antonym: unnecessary - not needed or required
