staringly

adverb
obviously, conspicuously
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstɛə.rɪŋ.li / (BrE)
in a way that is very obvious, conspicuous, or difficult to ignore

Examples

Staringly obvious mistake
The error was staringly clear to everyone in the room.
Her dress was staringly bright, making her stand out at the party.
The solution to the puzzle was staringly simple once you saw it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspicuously - in a way that is easily seen or noticed
  • antonym: subtly - in a way that is not obvious or easily noticed
