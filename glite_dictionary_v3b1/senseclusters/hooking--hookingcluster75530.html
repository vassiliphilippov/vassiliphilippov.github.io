hooking

noun
golf shot curving left (right-handed player)
Pronunciation: / ˈhʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
in golf, a shot that curves sharply to the left for a right-handed player

Examples

A hooking shot

He's been struggling with hooking in his drives lately, so he booked a lesson with a pro.
Proper grip and stance can help reduce hooking in your golf shots.
