snow flurry

noun
light, brief snowfall with wind
5.1
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ˈflɝːi / (AmE), / snəʊ ˈflʌri / (BrE)
A brief, light fall of snow, often accompanied by gusts of wind.

Examples

A snow flurry falls
A sudden snow flurry dusted the cars and streets in white as we left the café.
Hikers paused under a tree to shelter from a brief snow flurry on the trail.
The weather forecast called for a snow flurry in the afternoon, but no significant accumulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snow shower - a brief period of moderate snowfall
  • degree higher: snowstorm - a heavy fall of snow with strong winds
