huppah

noun

a canopy under which a Jewish couple stands during their wedding ceremony

Examples

Wedding huppah
The couple exchanged vows beneath the huppah in front of their families.
A beautifully decorated huppah stood at the center of the wedding hall.
Traditionally, the huppah symbolizes the new home the couple will build together.
