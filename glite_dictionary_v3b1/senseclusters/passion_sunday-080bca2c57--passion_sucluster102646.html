Passion Sunday

noun
fifth Sunday in Lent, before Easter
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃən ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈpæʃ.ən ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
the fifth Sunday in Lent, observed as the beginning of Passiontide, the period commemorating the sufferings of Christ, and falling two Sundays before Easter

Examples

Passion Sunday in Lent
Passion Sunday marks the beginning of Passiontide in the Christian calendar.
Many churches hold special services on Passion Sunday to reflect on the sufferings of Christ.
The sermon on Passion Sunday often focuses on the events leading up to Jesus' crucifixion.
