Brassica oleracea acephala

noun
type of cabbage with loose, curly leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌbræsɪkə oʊˈlɛriːəsiə æˈsɛfələ / (AmE), / ˌbrasɪkə əʊˌlɛːriːˈeɪsiə æˈsɛfələ / (BrE)
A hardy vegetable of the cabbage family with coarse, curly leaves that do not form a dense head; commonly known as kale or collard greens.
