burley

noun
tobacco variety
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈbɜːli / (BrE)
a light, air-cured variety of tobacco mainly used in making cigarettes

Examples

Burley tobacco leaves
The farmer grew burley to supply the local cigarette factory.
Many cigarettes are made using a blend of burley and other tobaccos.
After harvesting, the burley leaves are air-cured in special barns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Virginia tobacco - a type of flue-cured tobacco used in cigarettes
  • antonym: cigar tobacco - tobacco varieties mainly used for making cigars
