Olympia

proper noun
ancient Greek site of the original Olympic Games
6.5
Pronunciation: / oʊˈlɪmpiə / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpiə / (BrE)
an ancient plain in northwestern Peloponnese, Greece, famous as the chief sanctuary of Zeus and the site of the original Olympic Games.

Examples

Ancient Olympia Greece
Archaeologists uncovered fascinating artifacts at Olympia dating back to classical Greece.
Many tourists visit Olympia to see the ruins of the ancient stadium and temples.
The original Olympic Games were held at Olympia in honor of Zeus.
