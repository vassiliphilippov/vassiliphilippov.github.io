foresightfulness

noun
the state of planning prudently for the future
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈsaɪtf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈsaɪtf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having or showing careful planning for the future; prudence in anticipating future needs or problems

Examples

Her foresightfulness in planning
Her foresightfulness allowed the company to avoid many potential crises in the market.
The government's policies reflected a rare degree of foresightfulness about environmental challenges.
We admired the foresightfulness of the architect in planning for future expansions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foresight - the ability to predict or plan for the future
