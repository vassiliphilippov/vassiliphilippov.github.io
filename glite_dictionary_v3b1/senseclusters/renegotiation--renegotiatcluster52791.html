renegotiation

noun
changing agreement terms
Pronunciation: / ˌriːnɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːnɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of discussing and changing the terms of an agreement or contract

Examples

Contract renegotiation
After several months, the union called for a renegotiation of the workers' contract.
The renegotiation of the trade deal resulted in better terms for both countries.
Due to changing circumstances, a renegotiation of the lease was necessary.
