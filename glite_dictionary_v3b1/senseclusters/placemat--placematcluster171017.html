placemat

noun
table mat for plates
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌmæt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsˌmæt / (BrE)
a small mat placed under a plate to protect the table and add decoration during a meal

Examples

A placemat on table
She chose a floral placemat to brighten up the dining table.
Each child made their own placemat in art class for the school lunchroom.
The restaurant set a clean placemat at every seat before opening.
