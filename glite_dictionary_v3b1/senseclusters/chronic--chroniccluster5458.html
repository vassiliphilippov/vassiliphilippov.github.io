chronic

adjective
consistent or habitual (often negative)
5.4 2/m

(of a person or behavior) having a particular bad habit or characteristic for a long time and difficult to change.

Examples

Chronic liar
He's a chronic liar and no one trusts what he says anymore.
She's a chronic worrier, always expecting the worst.
The company has a chronic habit of missing deadlines.
