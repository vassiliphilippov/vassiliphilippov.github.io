resound across

verb
to echo or spread widely
5.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaʊnd əˈkrɔs / (AmE), / rɪˈzaʊnd əˈkrɒs / (BrE)
to echo or be heard widely in a place or throughout a group

Examples

Cheers resound across city
Cheers from the stadium resounded across the city streets long into the night.
Her words resounded across generations, inspiring many to take action.
The news of their victory resounded across the organization, lifting everyone's spirits.
