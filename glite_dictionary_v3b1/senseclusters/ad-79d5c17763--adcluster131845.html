AD

noun
label for paid promotion
2.2
Pronunciation: /æd/ (AmE), /æd/ (BrE)
A paid announcement or sponsored content; a label indicating material is a commercial promotion, especially online or in media.

Examples

Instagram AD

When watching tutorials, I usually skip any in-stream AD that interrupts the explanation.
The website annoyed readers with pop-ups and one large full-page AD covering the article.
