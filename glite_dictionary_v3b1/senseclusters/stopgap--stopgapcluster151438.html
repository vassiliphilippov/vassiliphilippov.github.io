stopgap

noun
temporary solution or substitute
4.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːp.gæp/ (AmE), /ˈstɒp.gæp/ (BrE)
a temporary solution or substitute used to address an urgent need or emergency until a permanent answer is found

Examples

A stopgap solution
Hiring temporary staff is just a stopgap measure while we search for permanent employees.
The tent served as a stopgap until they could build a proper shelter.
They put up a curtain as a stopgap to block the broken window.
