solvable

adjective
capable of being solved
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlvəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɒlvəbəl / (BrE)
(of a problem or puzzle) capable of being solved or resolved.

Examples

A solvable problem
Many complex equations are actually solvable with the right approach.
The crossword puzzle was surprisingly solvable for a beginner.
The detective assured us that the mystery was difficult but solvable.
