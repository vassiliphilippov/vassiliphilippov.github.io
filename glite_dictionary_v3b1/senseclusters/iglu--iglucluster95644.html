iglu

noun
dome-shaped snow hut of Arctic peoples
9.1 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɪ.gluː/ /; / /ˈiː.gluː/ (AmE), /ˈɪ.gluː/ /; / /ˈiː.gluː/ (BrE)
A dome-shaped hut traditionally built from blocks of sod or snow by some Arctic Indigenous peoples, especially the Inuit.

Examples

A traditional iglu
Inuit families traditionally constructed an iglu for warmth and protection during the winter.
The explorer spent a night in an iglu to shelter from the storm.
Tourists were fascinated by the structure of the iglu built entirely from snow blocks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: igloo - a dome-shaped hut of snow used by Inuit peoples
