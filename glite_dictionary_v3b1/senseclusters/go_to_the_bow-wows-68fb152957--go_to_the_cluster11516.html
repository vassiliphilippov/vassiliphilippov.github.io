go to the bow-wows

verb
be ruined or fail utterly (idiom)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ tə ðə ˈbaʊ waʊz / (AmE), / ɡəʊ tə ðə ˈbaʊ waʊz / (BrE)
