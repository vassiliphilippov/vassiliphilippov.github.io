curse

noun
profane or obscene word or expression
Pronunciation: / kɝs / (AmE), / kɜːs / (BrE)
a profane or obscene word or expression, especially as an expression of anger or surprise

Examples

A curse word
He muttered a curse under his breath when he saw the damage.
In a fit of anger, she let out a loud curse.
The movie was full of violent scenes and the occasional curse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blessing - a prayer or expression asking for divine favor, opposite of curse
  • synonym: expletive - an oath or swear word, especially one used as an interjection
  • synonym: oath - a profane or offensive word used to express strong emotion
