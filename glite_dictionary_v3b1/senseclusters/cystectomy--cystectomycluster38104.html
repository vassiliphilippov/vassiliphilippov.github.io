cystectomy

noun
removal of a cyst
Pronunciation: / sɪsˈtɛktəmi / (AmE), / sɪsˈtɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove a cyst from an organ or tissue

Examples

Cystectomy for ovarian cyst
The doctor recommended a cystectomy to treat the large ovarian cyst.
After the cystectomy, the patient recovered quickly and returned to normal activities.
A cystectomy may be necessary if the cyst is causing pain or other symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biopsy - the removal of tissue for diagnostic examination
  • synonym: excision - the act of cutting something out, especially surgically
