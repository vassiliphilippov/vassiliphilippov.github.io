nostril

noun
one of the two openings in the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːstrəl / (AmE), / ˈnɒstrɪl / (BrE)
either of the two external openings of the nose through which air enters and leaves the nasal cavity

Examples

Left nostril cut
A small cut appeared just below her left nostril.
He flared his nostrils in anger when he heard the news.
The doctor checked each nostril for any signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nares - the nostrils considered together as a pair
