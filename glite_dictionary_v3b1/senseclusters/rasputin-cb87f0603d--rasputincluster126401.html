Rasputin

proper noun
Russian mystic and royal advisor (1872–1916)
Pronunciation: / ræˈspjutɪn / (AmE), / ræˈspjuːtɪn / (BrE)
(1872–1916) A Russian mystic and religious figure who became an influential advisor to the family of Tsar Nicholas II and was assassinated by Russian nobles.

Examples

The mysterious circumstances surrounding Rasputin's death have inspired many books and films.
