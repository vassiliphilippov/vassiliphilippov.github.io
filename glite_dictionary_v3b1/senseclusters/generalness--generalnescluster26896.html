generalness

noun
lack of specificity
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnərəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being general rather than specific or detailed

Examples

The generalness of report
The generalness of the report made it difficult to understand the exact issues involved.
Her instructions were criticized for their generalness and lack of practical detail.
Because of the generalness of the guidelines, employees were unsure how to proceed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generality - the quality of being general or not specific
  • antonym: specificity - the quality of being specific or detailed
