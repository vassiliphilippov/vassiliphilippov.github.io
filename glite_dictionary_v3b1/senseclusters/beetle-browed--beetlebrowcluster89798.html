beetle-browed

adjective
having thick, overhanging brows; sullen-looking
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtəlˌbraʊd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtlˌbraʊd / (BrE)
Having thick, prominent eyebrows that give a sullen or unfriendly appearance.

Examples

Beetle-browed face
He had a beetle-browed expression that discouraged strangers from approaching him.
Her beetle-browed features gave her a stern and intimidating look.
The guard's beetle-browed glare made everyone uneasy as they entered the hall.
