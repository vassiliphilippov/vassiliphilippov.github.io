projective

adjective
relating to projection
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
relating to projecting, representing, or forming images, especially in mathematics or psychology

Examples

Projective test
The psychologist used a projective test to explore the patient's unconscious thoughts.
In geometry, projective properties remain unchanged under projection transformations.
Her artwork had a projective quality, as if it extended beyond the canvas.
