ruffly

adjective
slightly disordered
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌfli / (AmE), / ˈrʌfli / (BrE)
having a rumpled, creased, or slightly disordered appearance, as if ruffled.

Examples

Ruffly collar
After the long trip his collar was ruffly, and he smoothed it before the meeting.
She wore a ruffly dress that looked as if it had just come out of a suitcase.
The bedspread looked ruffly after the children jumped on it all morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rumpled - creased or wrinkled in appearance
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface without wrinkles
