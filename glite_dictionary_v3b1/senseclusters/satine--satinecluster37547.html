satine

noun
tropical hardwood or fabric
Pronunciation: / sæˈtiːn / (AmE), / sæˈtiːn / (BrE)
a tropical hardwood tree of South America or its reddish wood, sometimes also referring to a glossy fabric

Examples

Reddish satine wood
The craftsman carved the table from rare satine to highlight its beautiful reddish color.
Collectors value furniture made of satine for its durability and unique appearance.
Some designers use satine fabric to create elegant evening gowns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a reddish-brown tropical hardwood
  • synonym: satin - a smooth, glossy fabric
