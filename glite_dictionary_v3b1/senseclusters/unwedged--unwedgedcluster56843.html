unwedged

adjective
not held by wedge
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛdʒd / (BrE)
no longer held in place by a wedge; released from being wedged or stuck

Examples

The unwedged window
After several attempts, the stuck window was finally unwedged and could be opened.
The mechanic unwedged the part so the machine could run smoothly again.
Once the rock was unwedged from the tire, the car moved forward easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: wedged - held tightly in place by a wedge
