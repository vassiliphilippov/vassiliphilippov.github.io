Fagopyrum esculentum

noun
species of buckwheat plant
Pronunciation: / fəˌɡoʊˈpaɪrəm ˌɛskjəˈlɛntəm / (AmE), / fəˌɡəʊˈpɪərəm ˌɛskjʊˈlɛntəm / (BrE)
An annual Asian plant with clusters of small pinkish-white flowers and edible triangular seeds that are used whole or ground into flour; commonly known as buckwheat.
