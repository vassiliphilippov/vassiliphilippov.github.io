amarelle

noun
type of sour cherry tree or fruit
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈrɛl /; / əˈmærəl / (AmE), / ˌaməˈrɛl /; / əˈmarɛl / (BrE)
a type of sour cherry tree that produces pale red fruit with colorless juice, or the fruit itself

Examples

Unlike the morello, the juice from an amarelle is nearly colorless, giving desserts a delicate blush.
