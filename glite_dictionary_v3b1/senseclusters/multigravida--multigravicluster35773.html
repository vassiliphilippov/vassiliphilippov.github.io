multigravida

adjective
pregnant more than once
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈɡrævɪdə / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈɡrævɪdə / (BrE)
having been pregnant more than once; used to describe a woman who has had multiple pregnancies

Examples

Multigravida patient
The doctor noted that the patient was a multigravida during her prenatal assessment.
As a multigravida, she was familiar with the process of pregnancy and childbirth.
The study compared health outcomes between primigravida and multigravida women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: primigravida - a woman who is pregnant for the first time
