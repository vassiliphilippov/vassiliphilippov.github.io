locus

noun
the place where something happens
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəs / (BrE)
the place where something happens, especially an event or meeting

Examples

Main locus of activity
Archaeologists identified the ancient temple as the primary locus of religious activity in the city.
Her home soon became the locus of lively discussions among local artists.
The committee chose the library as the central locus for the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: location - a particular place or position
  • synonym: site - the area where something is situated or occurs
