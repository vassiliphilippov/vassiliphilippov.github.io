idiot light

noun
warning light for car problems
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɪ.diː.ət laɪt/ /; / /ˈɪ.dɪ.ət laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈɪ.dɪ.ət laɪt/ /; / /ˈɪ.diː.ət laɪt/ (BrE)
a warning light on a vehicle's instrument panel that illuminates to indicate a mechanical problem, such as low oil pressure or overheating, often instead of a gauge

Examples

Car idiot light
My car's idiot light came on, so I pulled over to check the engine.
Some older vehicles don't have gauges, only idiot lights for major problems.
When the oil pressure idiot light glows red, you should stop the car immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warning light - an indicator light to alert the driver to a potential problem
