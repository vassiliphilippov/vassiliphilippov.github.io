neutralizer

noun
agent that disables
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person, tactic, or agent that stops, disables, or makes an opponent, threat, or harmful influence ineffective

Examples

Virus neutralizer
The security team deployed a neutralizer to prevent the virus from spreading through the network.
In the basketball game, the coach relied on his best neutralizer to guard the opposing star player.
A chemical neutralizer was added to the solution to counteract the acid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countermeasure - an action or device designed to prevent or reduce a threat
  • synonym: defender - a person who protects against attack or danger
  • antonym: threat - a person or thing likely to cause damage or danger
