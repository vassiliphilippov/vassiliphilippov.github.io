go to pot

verb
to deteriorate or be ruined
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɡoʊ tə ˈpɑt /; / ɡoʊ tə pɑt / (AmE), / ˌɡəʊ tə ˈpɒt /; / ɡəʊ tə pɒt / (BrE)
to become ruined, neglected, or to decline in quality or condition

Examples

To go to pot quickly

If you stop taking care of your garden, it will quickly go to pot.
Many small businesses went to pot during the economic crisis.
