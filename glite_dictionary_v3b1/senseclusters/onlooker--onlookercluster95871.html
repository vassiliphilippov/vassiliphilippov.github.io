onlooker

noun
spectator; observer at an event
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌlʊkər / (AmE), / ˈɒnˌlʊkə / (BrE)
A person who watches an event or activity without taking part.

Examples

Curious onlookers watch
As the magician performed, dozens of curious onlookers gathered around.
Several onlookers applauded as the firefighters rescued the cat.
The accident attracted a crowd of onlookers who watched from a safe distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event but does not participate
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity or event
