affaire d'honneur

noun
a duel to settle a point of honor
Pronunciation: / æˌfɛər dɑːˈnɜːr / (AmE), / aˌfɛər dɒˈnɜː / (BrE)
a duel, typically prearranged and fought with deadly weapons to settle a dispute regarding honor

Examples

An affaire d'honneur duel
He challenged his rival to an affaire d'honneur to defend his family's name.
Historical novels often describe an affaire d'honneur as a way to resolve insults.
The argument between the two gentlemen ended in an affaire d'honneur at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duel - a formal fight between two people to settle a dispute or point of honor
