cavalier

adjective
showing haughty disregard; unconcerned
Pronunciation: / ˌkævəˈlɪr / (AmE), / ˌkævəˈlɪə / (BrE)
showing a lack of proper concern; displaying a haughty disregard for others or for important matters

Examples

Cavalier attitude
He made a cavalier remark about people's concerns, dismissing them entirely.
Her cavalier attitude towards safety regulations alarmed her colleagues.
The manager's cavalier approach to employee feedback frustrated the staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disdainful - showing contempt or lack of respect
  • synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
  • antonym: respectful - showing respect or deference
