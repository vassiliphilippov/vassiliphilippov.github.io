Napoleonic Wars

proper noun
wars between Napoleonic France and European powers, 1799–1815
6.5
Pronunciation: / nəˌpoʊliˈɑnɪk wɔrz / (AmE), / nəˌpəʊliˈɒnɪk wɔːz / (BrE)
a series of wars (1799–1815) fought between France, led by Napoleon Bonaparte, and various European coalitions including Britain, Prussia, Russia, and Austria.

Examples

The Napoleonic Wars
Many historians view the Napoleonic Wars as a turning point in European history.
Numerous works of art and literature were inspired by the events of the Napoleonic Wars.
The Napoleonic Wars dramatically altered the political landscape of the continent.
