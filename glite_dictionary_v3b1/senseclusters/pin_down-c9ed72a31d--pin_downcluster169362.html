pin down

verb
to define or specify something clearly
2.5
Pronunciation: / pɪn daʊn / (AmE), / pɪn daʊn / (BrE)
to clearly define, identify, or specify something; to make something precise or certain

Examples

Pin down the details

The cause of the problem was difficult to pin down.
After hours of research, he finally managed to pin down the source of the error.
It's difficult to pin down exactly what she meant by that statement.
We need to pin down the cause of the problem before proceeding with repairs.
