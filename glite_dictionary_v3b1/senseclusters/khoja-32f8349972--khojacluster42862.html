Khoja

proper noun
Muslim honorific title
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdʒə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdʒə / (BrE)
a historical title or honorific meaning 'master' or 'teacher', used in Central and South Asian Muslim and Sufi contexts

Examples

Khoja title
The villagers gathered to hear the wise words of the Khoja before making their decision.
In many regions, the Khoja was respected as both a teacher and a spiritual guide.
They invited the Khoja to lead the ceremony and offer his blessings.
