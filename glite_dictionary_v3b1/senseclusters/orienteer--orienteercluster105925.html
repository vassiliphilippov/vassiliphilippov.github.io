orienteer

noun
orienteering participant
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriənˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌɒriənˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in the sport of orienteering, navigating through terrain using a map and compass

Examples

A skilled orienteer
Each orienteer must find all the checkpoints using a map and compass.
The experienced orienteer quickly planned her route through the dense forest.
During the competition, every orienteer faced unique challenges in the rugged terrain.
