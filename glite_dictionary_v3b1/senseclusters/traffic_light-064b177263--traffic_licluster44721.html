traffic light

noun
device controlling traffic with colored lights
4.2 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtræ.fɪk laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈtræ.fɪk laɪt/ (BrE)
a device that uses colored lights to control vehicle and pedestrian movement, typically at road intersections

Examples

Red traffic light
Because the traffic light was out of order, a police officer directed cars manually.
She waited for the traffic light to change before crossing the street.
The traffic light turned red just as we approached the intersection.
