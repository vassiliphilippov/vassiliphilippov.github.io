home key

noun
main key in a piece of music
4.7
Pronunciation: / hoʊm kiː / (AmE), / həʊm kiː / (BrE)
the principal key in which a piece of music is written or to which it tends to resolve

Examples

The home key in music
After a contrasting section in G minor, the music returns triumphantly to the home key.
Musicians often feel a sense of resolution when a melody returns to its home key.
The symphony begins in C major, which remains the home key throughout most of the first movement.
