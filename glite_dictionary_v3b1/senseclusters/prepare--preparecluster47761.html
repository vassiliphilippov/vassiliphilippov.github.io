prepare

verb
to cook food
Pronunciation: / prɪˈpɛr / (AmE), / prɪˈpeə / (BrE)
to make food ready for eating by applying heat; to cook

Examples

To prepare dinner
Could you prepare lunch for us today?
She likes to prepare fresh vegetables for dinner every evening.
We need to prepare the fish before serving it to guests.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bake - to cook food using dry heat, usually in an oven
  • synonym: cook - to prepare food by heating it
  • degree higher: grill - to cook food over direct heat
