fluffy

adjective
soft and light, like down
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌfi / (AmE), / ˈflʌfi / (BrE)
soft and light, like down; having a light, airy, or feathery texture or appearance

Examples

A fluffy towel
She wrapped herself in a fluffy towel after showering.
The kitten's fluffy fur felt warm and comforting in my hands.
We enjoyed fluffy pancakes for breakfast, piled high on the plate.
