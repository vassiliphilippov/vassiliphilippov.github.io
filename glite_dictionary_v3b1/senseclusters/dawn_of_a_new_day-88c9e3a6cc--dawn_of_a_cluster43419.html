dawn of a new day

noun
beginning of a new period or opportunity
Pronunciation: / dɔn əv ə nuː deɪ / (AmE), / dɔːn əv ə njuː deɪ / (BrE)
the beginning of a new period or opportunity, often marking a fresh start

Examples

The dawn of a new day
After losing his job, he saw the move to a new city as the dawn of a new day.
The peaceful agreement marked the dawn of a new day for the two neighboring countries.
With each January first, people often feel the dawn of a new day in their lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: end of an era - the conclusion of a significant period
  • synonym: new beginning - a start of something new
