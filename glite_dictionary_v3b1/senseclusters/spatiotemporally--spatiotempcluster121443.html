spatiotemporally

adverb
in space and time
Pronunciation: / ˌspeɪtioʊˈtɛmpɚəli / (AmE), / ˌspeɪtɪəʊˈtɛmpərəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to both space and time together

Examples

To spatiotemporally relate events
The migration patterns of birds are spatiotemporally influenced by climate changes.
Researchers analyzed how the disease spread spatiotemporally across the region.
The two phenomena are spatiotemporally connected, occurring together in both space and time.
