multiengine airplane

noun
airplane with more than one engine
3.5 3.8

An airplane with two or more engines, designed for greater power and performance than single-engine aircraft.

Examples

A multiengine airplane design
A multiengine airplane offers better performance and safety in the event of an engine failure than a single-engine plane.
Commercial airlines typically operate multiengine airplanes due to their power and reliability.
