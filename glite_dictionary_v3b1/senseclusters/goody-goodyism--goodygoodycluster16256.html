goody-goodyism

noun
excessive or insincere virtue
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɡʊdi ˈɡʊdiˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɡʊdi ˈɡʊdiɪzəm / (BrE)
Excessive display of virtue or eagerness to please or obey rules, often regarded as insincere or annoying.

Examples

Goody-goodyism attitude
He was criticized for his goody-goodyism during the team meeting.
Her goody-goodyism often irritated her more rebellious classmates.
There's a fine line between genuine kindness and obvious goody-goodyism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goody-goody - a person who is ostentatiously virtuous or well-behaved
