capataz

noun
work supervisor
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːpəˈtɑːz / (AmE), / ˌkapəˈtaz / (BrE)
a person in charge of workers, especially on a farm or ranch

Examples

The capataz gives orders
The capataz gave instructions to the field workers early in the morning.
After the harvest, the capataz checked that all the workers had finished their tasks.
The capataz was respected for his fairness and leadership on the ranch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreman - a person in charge of a group of workers
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees others at work
  • antonym: worker - a person who does physical or manual labor
