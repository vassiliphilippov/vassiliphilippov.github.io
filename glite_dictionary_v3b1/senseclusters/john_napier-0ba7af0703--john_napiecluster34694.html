John Napier

proper noun
Scottish mathematician; inventor of logarithms
6.2
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ˈneɪpiɚ / (AmE), / dʒɒn ˈneɪpiə / (BrE)
(1550–1617) Scottish mathematician who invented logarithms and introduced the use of the decimal point in writing numbers.

Examples

John Napier mathematician
John Napier's contributions made complex calculations much more manageable.
