CPM

noun
advertising cost metric
6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.piː.em/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.piː.em/ (BrE)
a marketing term meaning the cost paid for one thousand advertising impressions

Examples

CPM for ads
The CPM for online ads has decreased as competition increases.
Advertisers compare CPM rates to choose the most cost-effective platform.
A low CPM can help companies reach a larger audience within their budget.
