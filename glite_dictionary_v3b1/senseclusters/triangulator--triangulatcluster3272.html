triangulator

noun
triangulation device
8.9
Pronunciation: / traɪˈæŋɡjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / traɪˈæŋɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a device or tool used to determine distances or locations by means of triangulation

Examples

Survey triangulator
The engineer set up the triangulator to measure the distance between the two towers.
A modern triangulator can quickly calculate precise locations using advanced sensors.
Survey teams often rely on a triangulator for accurate mapping in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rangefinder - a device for measuring distance from the observer to a target
  • synonym: theodolite - an instrument for measuring horizontal and vertical angles
