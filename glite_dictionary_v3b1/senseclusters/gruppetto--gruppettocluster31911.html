gruppetto

noun
musical ornament
Pronunciation: / gruˈpɛtoʊ / (AmE), / ɡruˈpɛtəʊ / (BrE)
a musical ornament consisting of a group of quick notes played around a main note, also known as a turn

Examples

A gruppetto ornament
The violinist added a delicate gruppetto to embellish the melody.
Learning to play the gruppetto smoothly can be challenging for beginners.
The composer marked a gruppetto above the note to indicate the ornament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trill - a rapid alternation between two adjacent notes
  • synonym: turn - a musical ornament of notes played around a main note
