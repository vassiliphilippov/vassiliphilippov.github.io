machan

noun
tree platform for hunting
Pronunciation: / məˈtʃɑːn / (AmE), / məˈtʃɑːn / (BrE)
a platform built in a tree or on poles, used for hunting or observing animals, especially in South Asia

Examples

A tree machan
The hunters spent the night in a machan waiting for the tiger to appear.
From the machan, the researchers could observe the animals without disturbing them.
Villagers built a sturdy machan to keep watch over their crops at night.
