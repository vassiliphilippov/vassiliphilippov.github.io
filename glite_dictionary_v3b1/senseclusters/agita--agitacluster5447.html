agita

noun
anxiety or agitation
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːdʒɪtə / (AmE), / ˈædʒɪtə / (BrE)
a feeling of anxiety, agitation, or nervousness, especially in a stressful situation

Examples

Work agita
The constant noise from the construction site gave him a lot of agita during the day.
She felt a wave of agita before her big presentation at work.
All this last-minute planning is causing unnecessary agita for everyone involved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry or nervousness
  • antonym: calm - a state of being relaxed and free from worry
