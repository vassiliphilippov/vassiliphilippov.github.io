stocked

adjective
supplied or furnished with plenty
Pronunciation: / stɑkt / (AmE), / stɒkt / (BrE)
(of a place or container) supplied or furnished with more than enough of something, especially goods or resources.

Examples

They keep their first aid kit stocked at all times in case of emergencies.
