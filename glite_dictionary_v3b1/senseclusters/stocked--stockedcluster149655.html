stocked

adjective
supplied or furnished with plenty
2.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘kt / (AmE), / stÉ’kt / (BrE)
(of a place or container) supplied or furnished with more than enough of something, especially goods or resources.

Examples

Well stocked shelves
The library is stocked with a wide variety of books and magazines.
The pantry was fully stocked with fresh ingredients for the holidays.
They keep their first aid kit stocked at all times in case of emergencies.

Synonyms

  • replete
    6.6 2.28/m
    replete is a formal word meaning well supplied with plenty, while stocked describes a place supplied with goods for sale or use.
  • filled
    1.0 0.00/m
    filled is used for something full to capacity or overflowing, while stocked means a place is supplied with goods or resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empty - lacking contents or supply
