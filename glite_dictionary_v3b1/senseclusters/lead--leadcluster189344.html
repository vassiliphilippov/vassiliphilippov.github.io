lead

verb
to provide access or a way to a place
3.9
Pronunciation: / lid / (AmE), / liːd / (BrE)
(of a road, path, or object) to provide access or a way to a place or result in reaching a place.

Examples

Path leads to lake
A short staircase leads down to the wine cellar.
The narrow path leads to a beautiful meadow beyond the trees.
This hallway leads directly into the conference room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: end - (of a road, path, etc.) to come to a stop at a place
  • synonym: go - (of a path, road, etc.) to extend to or reach a place
