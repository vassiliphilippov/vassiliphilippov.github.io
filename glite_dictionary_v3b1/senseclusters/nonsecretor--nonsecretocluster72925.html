nonsecretor

noun
person lacking antigens in fluids
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪˈkriːtər / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪˈkriːtə / (BrE)
a person whose bodily fluids, such as saliva, do not contain certain blood group antigens

Examples

A nonsecretor person
A nonsecretor may have different susceptibility to some infections.
Doctors determined that he was a nonsecretor after analyzing his saliva.
Being a nonsecretor can affect how your body responds to certain diseases.
