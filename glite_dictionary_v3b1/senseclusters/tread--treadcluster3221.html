tread

noun
a step in walking or running
5.5
Pronunciation: / trɛd / (AmE), / trɛd / (BrE)
a single step or the sound made by a step in walking or running, often used in literary or formal contexts

Examples

A heavy tread
Only the tread of her boots disturbed the silence of the early morning.
She recognized his light tread on the stairs before she saw him.
The soldier's heavy tread echoed down the corridor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: step - a movement made by lifting the foot and setting it down
