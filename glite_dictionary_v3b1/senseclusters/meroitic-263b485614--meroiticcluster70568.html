Meroitic

noun
ancient Sudanese script
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛroʊˈɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɛrəʊˈɪtɪk / (BrE)
the ancient script used to write the Meroitic language of the Kingdom of Meroë in present-day Sudan

Examples

Meroitic script tablet
The museum displayed a tablet inscribed with Meroitic characters from the ancient kingdom.
Researchers are still working to fully decipher the Meroitic script found on temple walls.
Few people today can read Meroitic, making translation of old texts challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cuneiform - an ancient writing system used in Mesopotamia
  • synonym: hieroglyph - a character of the ancient Egyptian writing system
