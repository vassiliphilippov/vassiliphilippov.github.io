buttery

adjective
excessively suave or ingratiating (of manner or speech)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈbʌt.əri / (BrE)
(of speech or manner) unpleasantly suave, ingratiating, or excessively flattering.

Examples

Buttery voice
He smiled with a buttery manner that seemed anything but genuine.
Her buttery compliments made everyone suspicious of her real motives.
The politician gave a buttery speech full of unnecessary praise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oily - (figurative) overly smooth or polite in a way that is not sincere
  • antonym: sincere - genuine, not feigned or insincere
  • synonym: smarmy - insincerely flattering or ingratiating
  • synonym: unctuous - excessively flattering, insincerely earnest
