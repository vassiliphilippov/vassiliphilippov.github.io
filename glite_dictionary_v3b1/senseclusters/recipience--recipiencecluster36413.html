recipience

noun
openness to receiving
9.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɪpiəns / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪpiəns / (BrE)
the capacity or tendency to receive or be open to something; receptiveness.

Examples

Recipience to feedback
Her natural recipience to new ideas made the team's brainstorming sessions very productive.
The recipience of the audience to the speaker's message was clear from their enthusiastic applause.
A culture of recipience can help organizations adapt quickly to change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: openness - the quality of being open to new ideas or experiences
  • synonym: receptiveness - willingness or ability to accept new ideas or experiences
  • antonym: resistance - refusal to accept or be open to something
