Convolvulus

noun
genus of climbing plants (bindweed)
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɑːlvjələs / (AmE), / kənˈvɒlvjʊləs / (BrE)
A genus of mostly climbing or scrambling herbs and shrubs, commonly known as bindweed.

Examples

Convolvulus plant
Many gardeners struggle to control Convolvulus due to its rapid growth and ability to spread underground.
Several species in the Convolvulus genus are known to be invasive weeds in gardens.
The flowers of Convolvulus are typically trumpet-shaped and can be white, pink, or blue.
