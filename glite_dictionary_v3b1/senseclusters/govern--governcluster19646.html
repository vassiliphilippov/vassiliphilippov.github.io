govern

verb
control by rules or regulations
6.3 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərn / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvən / (BrE)
to control or influence something by establishing rules, principles, or regulations

Examples

Rules govern behavior
Strict standards govern the production of organic food in this country.
The school board governs all policies regarding student behavior and dress code.
These procedures are governed by federal law to ensure fairness.
