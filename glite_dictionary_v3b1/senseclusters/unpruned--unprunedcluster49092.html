unpruned

adjective
not trimmed or edited
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpruːnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpruːnd / (BrE)
not reduced, shortened, or edited by removing unnecessary parts; left in its original, full form.

Examples

Unpruned report
The report was left unpruned, containing every detail from the initial research.
She submitted an unpruned version of her essay, which was much longer than required.
The list of candidates remained unpruned, making the selection process more difficult.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pruned - reduced or trimmed by removing unnecessary parts
  • synonym: unedited - not revised or altered from the original
