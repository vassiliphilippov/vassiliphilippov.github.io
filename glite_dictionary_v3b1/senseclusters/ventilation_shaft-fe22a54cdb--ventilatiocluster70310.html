ventilation shaft

noun
air passage for ventilation in buildings
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən ʃæft / (AmE), / ˌvɛn.tɪˈleɪ.ʃən ʃɑːft / (BrE)
a vertical or horizontal passage in a building or structure designed to allow fresh air to circulate and to remove stale air for ventilation purposes

Examples

A ventilation shaft opening
Smoke filled the ventilation shaft, making it dangerous for the firefighters to enter the building.
The new skyscraper was designed with a central ventilation shaft to improve air quality on every floor.
Workers discovered a bird's nest in the ventilation shaft during routine maintenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airshaft - a vertical or sloping passage for air ventilation, often in buildings or mines
