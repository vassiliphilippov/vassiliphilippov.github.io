genus Alca

noun
genus of birds including the razorbill
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈælkə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈælkə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Alcidae, comprising solely the razorbill (Alca torda).
