selenolatry

noun
the worship of the moon
Pronunciation: / sɛlɪˈnɑlətri / (AmE), / sɛlɪˈnɒlətri / (BrE)
the worship of the moon as a deity or the practice of moon worship

Examples

Selenolatry of moon
Some cultures practiced selenolatry alongside sun worship to honor both celestial bodies.
Studies of selenolatry reveal the importance of the moon in various early civilizations.
The ancient tribe's selenolatry played a central role in their rituals and calendar.
