directorate

noun
organizational division
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛktərət / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛktərət / (BrE)
a department or division within an organization that is responsible for managing a specific area or function

Examples

IT directorate
The new directorate was established to oversee digital transformation within the company.
Each directorate is responsible for reporting its progress to the board of directors.
She was promoted to lead the research directorate after years of dedicated service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: department - a distinct part of an organization with a specific responsibility
  • synonym: division - a major section of an organization with its own management
