som

noun
currency of Kyrgyzstan
Pronunciation: / sɔm / (AmE), / sɒm / (BrE)
the official unit of currency in Kyrgyzstan, equal to 100 tyiyns.

Examples

Kyrgyz som note
The price of a taxi ride was 300 soms across town.
The restaurant only accepted payment in som, not foreign currency.
You can exchange dollars for som at the airport upon arrival in Bishkek.
