patty

noun
small pie or pasty
Pronunciation: / ˈpæti / (AmE), / ˈpæti / (BrE)
A small pie or pasty, often filled with meat, vegetables, or other ingredients and baked or fried.

Examples

A chicken patty pie

Jamaican patties are popular snacks filled with spicy beef or vegetables.
