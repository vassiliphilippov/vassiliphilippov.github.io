piezoelectric

adjective
relating to electricity from mechanical stress
9.3 8.6
Pronunciation: /paɪ.zoʊ.ə.ˈlek.trɪk/ /; / /paɪ.zɪ.oʊ.ˈlek.trɪk/ (AmE), /paɪ.zɪ.əʊ.ˈlek.trɪk/ /; / /paɪ.ə.zəʊ.ˈlek.trɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to or involving the generation of an electric charge in certain materials when they are subjected to mechanical stress.

Examples

Piezoelectric materials
Piezoelectric materials are commonly used in ultrasound machines and microphones.
Researchers developed a piezoelectric device that generates electricity from footsteps.
The sensor uses a piezoelectric crystal to detect pressure changes.
