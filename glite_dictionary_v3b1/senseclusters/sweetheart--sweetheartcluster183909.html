sweetheart

noun
privileged or favorable treatment (often unethical)
Pronunciation: / ˈswitˌhɑrt / (AmE), / ˈswiːthɑːt / (BrE)
privileged or especially favorable treatment, often granted to a person or corporation because of special influence or relationships, sometimes considered unethical or improper

Examples

Sweetheart deal
Many politicians have been accused of arranging sweetheart deals for their supporters.
The company received a sweetheart contract from the government, sparking public criticism.
There was outrage over the sweetheart agreement that allowed the corporation to avoid millions in taxes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: favoritism - the practice of giving unfair preferential treatment to one person or group
  • antonym: meritocracy - a system in which advancement is based on individual ability or achievement
  • synonym: nepotism - the practice among those with power of favoring relatives or friends
