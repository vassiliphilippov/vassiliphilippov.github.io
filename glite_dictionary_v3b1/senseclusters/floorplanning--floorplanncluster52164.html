floorplanning

noun
store layout planning
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈflɔːr.plæn.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈflɑːr.plæn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈflɔː.plæn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of arranging products, fixtures, and aisles in a store to optimize customer movement and sales

Examples

Store floorplanning
Good floorplanning can help customers find what they need quickly and encourage them to buy more.
The manager reviewed the floorplanning to ensure the new products would be easy to locate.
After changing the floorplanning, the store noticed an increase in customer satisfaction and sales.

  • synonym: store layout - the arrangement of aisles, shelves, and displays in a retail space
