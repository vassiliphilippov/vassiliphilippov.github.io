booster shot

noun
an extra vaccine dose for renewed effectiveness
2.9 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbuÀêst…ôr  É…ët / (AmE), / ÀàbuÀêst…ô  É…ít / (BrE)

Examples
