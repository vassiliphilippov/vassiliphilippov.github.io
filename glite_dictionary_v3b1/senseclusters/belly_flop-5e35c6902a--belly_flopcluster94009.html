belly flop

noun
spectacular, embarrassing failure
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli flɑp / (AmE), / ˈbɛli flɒp / (BrE)
An embarrassing or spectacular failure of a plan, event, or performance; a complete flop.

Examples

A business belly flop
The product launch proved a belly flop for the startup.
Critics called the new movie a cinematic belly flop that lost millions at the box office.
After months of hype, the campaign turned into a belly flop and damaged the brand's reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flop - a failure or lack of success
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a desired aim or result; opposite of a failure
