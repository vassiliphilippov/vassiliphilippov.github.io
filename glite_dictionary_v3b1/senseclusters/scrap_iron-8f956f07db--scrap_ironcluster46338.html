scrap iron

noun
waste iron for recycling
Pronunciation: / skræp ˈaɪɚn / (AmE), / skræp ˈaɪən / (BrE)
Iron that has been discarded or rejected as waste, especially to be melted down and reused.

Examples

Scrap iron pieces
During the war, families donated scrap iron for the nation's steel production.
He sold the old machinery as scrap iron to a local dealer.
The factory collected tons of scrap iron to melt for new products.
