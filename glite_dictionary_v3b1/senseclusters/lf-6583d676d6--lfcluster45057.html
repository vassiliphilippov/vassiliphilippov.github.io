LF

noun
online 'looking for' (seeking players/items)
9.70.04/m
US/ 5bl 5bf /
UK/ 5bl 5bf /
A short chat message that says someone is looking for a player or group

Examples

LF group members
In game chat, players typed 'LF healer for the raid starting in an hour.'
After logging on, he posted 'LF tank and healer' to quickly form a dungeon group.
The guild chat often uses LF followed by the role to request players for events.
