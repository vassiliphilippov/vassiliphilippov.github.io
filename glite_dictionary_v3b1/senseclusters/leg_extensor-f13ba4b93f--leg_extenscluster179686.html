leg extensor

noun
exercise to strengthen leg extensor muscles
Pronunciation: / lɛɡ ɪkˈstɛnsər / (AmE), / lɛɡ ɪkˈstɛnsə / (BrE)
an exercise intended to strengthen the extensor muscles of the leg, usually performed with a specialized machine by straightening the knee against resistance

Examples

Leg extensor exercise
He did three sets of ten leg extensor exercises to strengthen his quadriceps after knee surgery.
Physical therapists often recommend the leg extensor for patients recovering from knee injuries.
The leg extensor machine helps isolate and develop the front thigh muscles.
