parasitica

proper noun
historical wasp suborder
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkə / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkə / (BrE)
a former scientific suborder of parasitic wasps within the insect order Hymenoptera, now considered outdated in taxonomy

Examples

Parasitica suborder
The classification of parasitica is no longer used in modern entomology.
Many species once grouped under parasitica have been reassigned based on genetic research.
Textbooks from the early 20th century often mention parasitica as a major wasp group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wasp - a stinging insect related to bees and ants
