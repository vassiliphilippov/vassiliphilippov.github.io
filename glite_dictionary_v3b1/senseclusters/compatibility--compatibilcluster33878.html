compatibility

noun
ability to coexist harmoniously
1.8
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpætəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / kəmˌpatəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the ability of two or more things to exist or work together without conflict or problems

Examples

Compatibility with devices
Before installing the new software, check its compatibility with your computer's operating system.
Their friendship was built on a foundation of mutual respect and compatibility.
There was a surprising level of compatibility between their working styles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incompatibility - the state of being unable to exist or work together without conflict
