lush

noun
habitual drunkard
Pronunciation: / lʌʃ / (AmE), / lʌʃ / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks excessive amounts of alcohol; a habitual drunkard

Examples

A lush at the bar
At family gatherings, Uncle Joe was careful to hide his habit so no one would call him a lush.
Despite being a brilliant artist, his reputation as a lush often overshadowed his work.
He’s known as the lush of the neighborhood, always stumbling out of bars at closing time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkard - a person who is frequently drunk
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
