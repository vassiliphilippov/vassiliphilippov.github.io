curbstoner

noun
unlicensed car seller
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːrbˌstoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈkɜːbstəʊnə / (BrE)
a person who sells cars without a dealer license, often illegally or unethically

Examples

Used car curbstoner
The city warned buyers to avoid purchasing vehicles from a curbstoner.
He was caught acting as a curbstoner after selling several cars without a license.
Many unsuspecting people have lost money to a curbstoner offering deals on used cars.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dealer - a person or business licensed to sell cars
  • synonym: scammer - a person who cheats others, especially for money
