drawdown

noun
pension withdrawal
6.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààdr…îÀåda än / (AmE), / Ààdr…îÀêda än / (BrE)
the act of withdrawing money from a pension or retirement account to provide income, especially during retirement

Examples

Pension drawdown
After retiring, he set up a regular drawdown from his pension fund to cover his expenses.
The financial advisor explained how a flexible drawdown could help manage retirement income.
Many retirees choose a partial drawdown rather than taking all their savings at once.
