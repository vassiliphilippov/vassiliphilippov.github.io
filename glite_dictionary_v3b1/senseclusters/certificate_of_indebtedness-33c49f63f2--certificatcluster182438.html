certificate of indebtedness

noun
written promise to repay a debt
Pronunciation: / sɚˌtɪfɪkət əv ɪnˌdɛtɪdnəs / (AmE), / səˌtɪfɪkət əv ɪnˌdɛtɪdnəs / (BrE)
a written document acknowledging a debt and containing a promise to repay it

Examples

A certificate of indebtedness
Many municipalities use a certificate of indebtedness to document their obligations to bondholders.
She received a certificate of indebtedness as proof of the loan agreement.
The company issued a certificate of indebtedness to raise capital from private investors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a certificate issued by a government or company promising to repay borrowed money with interest
