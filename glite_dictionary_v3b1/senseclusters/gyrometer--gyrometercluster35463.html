gyrometer

noun
instrument for rotation
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪrəˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪərəˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure or detect rotational movement or angular velocity

Examples

A gyrometer device
The gyrometer provided accurate data on the drone's angular velocity during flight tests.
Engineers installed a gyrometer in the spacecraft to monitor its rotation in orbit.
Before launching the new robot, the team checked the gyrometer for calibration errors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accelerometer - an instrument for measuring acceleration
  • synonym: gyroscope - a device for measuring or maintaining orientation based on rotation
