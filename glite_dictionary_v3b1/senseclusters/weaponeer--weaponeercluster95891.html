weaponeer

noun
weapons expert
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛpəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌwiːpəˈnɪə / (BrE)
a person who is highly skilled in the use or handling of weapons, especially in combat or military contexts

Examples

Expert weaponeer in combat
The army relied on the weaponeer to train new recruits in advanced firearms techniques.
As a skilled weaponeer, she was responsible for maintaining and operating complex weapon systems.
The film's main character is a legendary weaponeer with expertise in every type of weapon.
