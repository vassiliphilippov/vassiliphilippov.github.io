corniness

noun
old-fashioned sentimentality
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being sentimental, cheesy, old-fashioned, or lacking sophistication or originality, often in a way that is funny, charming, or embarrassing

Examples

The corniness of jokes
Despite the corniness of the movie, many people found it heartwarming and enjoyable.
He laughed at the corniness of the old jokes his grandfather used to tell.
Some people appreciate the corniness of traditional holiday decorations and music.
The corniness of the speech made the audience both laugh and cringe.
Despite its corniness, the commercial was surprisingly effective at getting people's attention.
He tried to avoid the corniness often found in romantic comedies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheesiness - the quality of being cheap, tacky, or overly sentimental
  • synonym: sentimentality - excessive or exaggerated emotion or nostalgia
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being genuine or honest
  • antonym: sophistication - the quality of being refined or cultured
