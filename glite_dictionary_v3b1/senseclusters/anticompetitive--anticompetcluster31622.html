anticompetitive

adjective
unfairly limiting competition
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntikoʊmˈpɛtətɪv / (AmE), / ˌæntikəmˈpɛtətɪv / (BrE)
describing actions or practices that unfairly limit or reduce competition, especially in business or markets

Examples

Anticompetitive practices
The new law aims to prevent anticompetitive practices in the technology sector.
Regulators fined the company for engaging in anticompetitive agreements with its rivals.
Mergers that result in anticompetitive effects may be blocked by authorities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitive - encouraging or involving competition
  • synonym: monopolistic - relating to or having the characteristics of a monopoly
