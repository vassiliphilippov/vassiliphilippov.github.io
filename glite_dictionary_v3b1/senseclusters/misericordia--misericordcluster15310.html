misericordia

noun
charitable organization
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪzərɪˈkɔːrdiə / (AmE), / ˌmɪzərɪˈkɔːdiə / (BrE)
a charitable organization, hospital, or religious group, especially Catholic, that provides care and relief for the poor and sick

Examples

Misericordia hospital
The local misericordia organized a fundraiser to support families in need.
Volunteers from the misericordia visited the elderly and brought them meals every week.
After the earthquake, the misericordia provided shelter and medical care to the injured.
