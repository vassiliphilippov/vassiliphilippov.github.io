disqualifier

noun
person who disqualifies
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkwɑːlɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / dɪsˈkwɒlɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a person or official who disqualifies someone from a contest, office, or activity

Examples

The disqualifier at the race
The disqualifier announced that the athlete had broken the rules and could not continue.
As the official disqualifier, she had to make a difficult decision during the finals.
A strict disqualifier ensures that all participants follow the competition's guidelines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: judge - a person who decides the results in a competition
  • antonym: qualifier - a person or event that allows someone to participate
  • synonym: referee - an official who enforces the rules in a contest
