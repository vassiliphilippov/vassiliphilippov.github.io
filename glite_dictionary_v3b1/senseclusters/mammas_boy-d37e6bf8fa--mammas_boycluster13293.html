mamma's boy

noun
a boy overly attached to his mother
3.1 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.məz bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈmæm.əz bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.məz bɔɪ/ /; / /ˈmæm.əz bɔɪ/ (BrE)
