thunderbolt

noun
a lightning flash with thunder
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌndɚˌboʊlt / (AmE), / ˈθʌndəˌbəʊlt / (BrE)
a flash of lightning followed by a crash of thunder, typically regarded as a single event

Examples

A thunderbolt strikes
A sudden thunderbolt struck the tree, splintering it instantly.
The ancient myth described Zeus hurling a thunderbolt from the sky.
We heard a thunderbolt in the distance as the storm approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lightning - the flash of light in the sky during a thunderstorm
