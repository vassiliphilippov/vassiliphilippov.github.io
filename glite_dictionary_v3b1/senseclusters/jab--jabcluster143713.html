jab

noun
quick injection
Pronunciation: / dʒæb / (AmE), / dʒæb / (BrE)
a quick, sharp push or injection, especially with a needle for medical purposes

Examples

A jab with a needle
The nurse gave him a jab in the arm before the procedure.
Many people lined up to get their flu jab at the clinic.
She was nervous about her first jab, but it was over quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injection - the act of putting a drug or vaccine into the body with a needle
  • synonym: shot - an injection of medicine or vaccine
