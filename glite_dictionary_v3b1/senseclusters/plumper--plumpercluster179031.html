plumper

noun
lip-enhancing cosmetic
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmpər / (AmE), / ˈplʌmpə / (BrE)
a cosmetic product or device used to make lips appear fuller and more voluminous

Examples

Lip plumper
She used a plumper before applying her favorite lipstick for a fuller look.
Many people choose a plumper to enhance their lips without injections.
The new plumper claims to give instant volume and shine to your lips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lip gloss - a cosmetic applied to the lips for shine and subtle color
  • synonym: lipstick - a cosmetic for coloring the lips
