rottenly

adverb
in a terrible or bad way
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑtnli / (AmE), / ˈrɒtnli / (BrE)
In a very bad, unpleasant, or morally corrupt manner.

Examples

Behave rottenly
He treated his coworkers rottenly, never showing them any respect.
She felt rottenly disappointed after her team's loss.
The bread was rottenly stale, making it impossible to eat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: terribly - in a very bad or unpleasant way
  • antonym: well - in a good way; opposite of 'badly'
