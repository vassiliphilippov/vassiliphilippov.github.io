rote

noun
memorization by repetition
4.9
Pronunciation: / roʊt / (AmE), / rəʊt / (BrE)
memorization or learning achieved by repeating information over and over without necessarily understanding it

Examples

Learning by rote
Relying solely on rote can limit critical thinking and understanding.
She learned the multiplication tables by rote as a child.
The students recited the poem from rote without truly grasping its meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - repetitive practice for skill or memorization
  • antonym: understanding - comprehension of meaning, as opposed to rote
