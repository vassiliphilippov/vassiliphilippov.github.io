pound

noun
act of repeated heavy blows
Pronunciation: / paʊnd / (AmE), / paʊnd / (BrE)
the act of striking or hitting repeatedly with heavy or forceful blows

Examples

The pound of drums
He winced at the pound of footsteps racing down the hallway.
The pound of drums could be felt throughout the stadium.
The constant pound of rain kept everyone awake all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hammer - a heavy blow or a series of heavy blows
  • degree lower: tap - a light or gentle stroke
