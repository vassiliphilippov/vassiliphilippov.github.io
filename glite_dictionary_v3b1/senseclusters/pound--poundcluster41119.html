pound

noun
act of repeated heavy blows
4.7 0.99/m
Pronunciation: / paÊŠnd / (AmE), / paÊŠnd / (BrE)
the act of striking or hitting repeatedly with heavy or forceful blows

Examples

Synonyms

  • hammering
    2.8 0.87/m
    hammering is repeated heavy blows often like a tool striking wood or metal, while pound means striking again and again more generally.
  • beating
    4.2 9.90/m
    beating is striking someone repeatedly often as punishment or violence, while pound means forceful repeated blows in any context.
  • bashing
    3.2 2.89/m
    bashing is hitting very hard, often to damage something or someone, while pound means repeated heavy blows in general.
  • buffeting
    7.0 0.17/m
    buffeting is being struck again and again by wind or waves, while pound means actively hitting with heavy force.
