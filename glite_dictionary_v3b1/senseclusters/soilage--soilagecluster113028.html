soilage

noun
fresh animal feed
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔɪlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
fresh green fodder crops that are cut and fed directly to livestock without drying

Examples

Fresh soilage for cows
The cows were fed soilage freshly cut from the field every morning.
Farmers often use soilage in the summer when grass is abundant and easy to harvest.
Feeding livestock with soilage helps maintain their health during the growing season.
