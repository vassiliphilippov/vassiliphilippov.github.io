commissioned

adjective
having official authority or approval
Pronunciation: / kəˈmɪʃ.ənd / (AmE), / kəˈmɪʃ.ənd / (BrE)
officially appointed or given authority to perform a particular role, duty, or function

Examples

Commissioned agent
As a commissioned broker, she was legally allowed to complete major transactions.
He worked as a commissioned officer in the navy for over twenty years.
Only commissioned representatives can approve these types of contracts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appointed - officially assigned to a role or position
  • synonym: authorized - having official permission or approval
  • antonym: uncommissioned - not given official approval or authority
