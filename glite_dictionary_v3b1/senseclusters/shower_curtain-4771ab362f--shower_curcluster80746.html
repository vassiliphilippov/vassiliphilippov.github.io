shower curtain

noun
waterproof curtain for showers or bathtubs
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚ ˌkɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə ˌkɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
a curtain, usually made of waterproof material, that is hung to keep water from splashing out of a shower or bathtub area

Examples

The shower curtain rod
Please make sure the shower curtain is inside the tub to prevent water from spilling onto the floor.
She replaced her old shower curtain with a brightly colored one.
The hotel provided a clean shower curtain in every bathroom.
