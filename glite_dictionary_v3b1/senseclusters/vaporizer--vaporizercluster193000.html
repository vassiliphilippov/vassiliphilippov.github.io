vaporizer

noun
electronic device for vaping nicotine or cannabis
Pronunciation: / c8ve6ap59ccra6az59r / (AmE), / c8ve6ap59ra6az59 / (BrE)
a consumer electronic device that heats a liquid or plant material to produce an inhalable aerosol or vapor, typically used for nicotine or cannabis.

Examples

Electronic vaporizer
He bought a small vaporizer to switch from cigarettes to vaping on weekends.
The shop sold refill cartridges for the popular vaporizer that uses nicotine e-liquid.
During the festival many people used a pocket vaporizer to enjoy legal cannabis vapor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: e-cigarette - a battery-powered device that delivers nicotine in a vapor form; often synonymous with consumer vaporizers
  • synonym: vape - to inhale and exhale the vapor produced by an electronic vaporizer; also the noun for the device or the act
