levitation

noun
rising or floating in the air, often seemingly supernatural
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛvɪˈteɪʃən /; / ˌlevɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlɛvɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
