Eriophyllum lanatum

noun
A woolly-leaved golden-flowered plant of western North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛriˈɑːfɪləm ləˈneɪtəm / (AmE), / ˌɛriˈɒfɪləm ləˈneɪtəm / (BrE)
A greyish, woolly-leaved perennial plant with branched stems topped by stalks bearing golden-yellow flower heads, native to dry areas of western North America; also known as Oregon sunshine or woolly sunflower.
