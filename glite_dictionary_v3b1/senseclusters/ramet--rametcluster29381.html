ramet

noun
independent plant part
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.mət/ /; / /ˈræ.met/ (AmE), /ˈræ.met/ /; / /ˈræ.mət/ (BrE)
an individual plant (or part of a plant) that is part of a larger clonal colony and can grow independently, genetically identical to others in the group
