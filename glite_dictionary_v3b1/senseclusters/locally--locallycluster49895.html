locally

adverb
on the user's device (not remote)
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.kə.li/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.kə.li/ (BrE)
(computing) on or within the user's own device, machine, or local environment rather than on a remote server or in the cloud.

Examples

Runs locally on device
The app runs locally on your phone, without sending data to the cloud.
For better privacy, the program stores and processes data locally instead of on remote servers.
During testing they executed the script locally to avoid network delays affecting results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on-device - performed on the user's physical device rather than on external servers
  • antonym: remotely - performed from or on a different computer or server, often over the internet; opposite of locally in computing
