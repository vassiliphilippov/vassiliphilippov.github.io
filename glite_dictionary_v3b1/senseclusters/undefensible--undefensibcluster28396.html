undefensible

adjective
cannot be justified
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈfɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈfɛnsəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to defend or justify, especially in an argument or situation

Examples

An undefensible action
The lawyer admitted that the client's actions were undefensible in court.
It is undefensible to treat people unfairly based on their background.
His argument was so weak that it became completely undefensible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indefensible - impossible to defend or justify
  • antonym: justifiable - able to be shown to be right or reasonable
