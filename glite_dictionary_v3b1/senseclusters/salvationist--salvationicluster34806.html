salvationist

noun
believer in salvation
Pronunciation: / sælˈveɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / sælˈveɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or promotes the Christian doctrine of salvation, especially emphasizing personal conversion or redemption

Examples

A devoted salvationist
The salvationist spoke passionately about the importance of personal faith at the community meeting.
Many salvationists dedicate their lives to spreading messages of hope and redemption.
As a committed salvationist, she organized events to encourage others to seek spiritual renewal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evangelist - a person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions religious beliefs
