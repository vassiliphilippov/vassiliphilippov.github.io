cheese plane

noun
kitchen tool for slicing cheese thinly
9.9 2.4
Pronunciation: /tʃiːz pleɪn/ (AmE), /tʃiːz pleɪn/ (BrE)
a kitchen tool with a flat blade for producing thin, even slices of semi-hard or hard cheese

Examples
