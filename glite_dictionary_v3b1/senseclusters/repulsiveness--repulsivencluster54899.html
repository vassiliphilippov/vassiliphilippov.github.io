repulsiveness

noun
quality of being extremely unpleasant
1.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌlsɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌlsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely unpleasant or disgusting to the senses or emotions

Examples

The repulsiveness of injuries
He tried to hide the repulsiveness of his injuries with a bandage.
She was disturbed by the repulsiveness of the crime scene described in the novel.
The repulsiveness of the rotten food made everyone leave the room immediately.
