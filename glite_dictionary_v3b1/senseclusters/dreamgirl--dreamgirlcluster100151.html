dreamgirl

noun
girl in a dream
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈdriːmˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a girl who appears in someone's dreams while they are sleeping

Examples

Dreamgirl in dreams
He described the mysterious dreamgirl he met while sleeping last night.
After waking up, she tried to remember the face of the dreamgirl from her dream.
The story is about a boy searching for the dreamgirl who visits him in his dreams.
