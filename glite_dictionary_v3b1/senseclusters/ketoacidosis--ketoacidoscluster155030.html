ketoacidosis

A serious condition in which the body produces high levels of ketone bodies, causing a dangerous buildup of acids in the blood, often seen in diabetes mellitus.

Examples

Diabetic ketoacidosis
Early symptoms of ketoacidosis include nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain.
The patient was admitted to the hospital in a state of ketoacidosis after experiencing rapid breathing and confusion.
Untreated type 1 diabetes can result in ketoacidosis, which is life-threatening if not addressed promptly.
