winkingly

adverb
in a winking manner
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈwɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that involves or resembles a wink, suggesting playfulness, secrecy, or secret understanding

Examples

Look winkingly at
She glanced winkingly at her friend after making the clever remark.
The magician winkingly revealed the secret behind his trick to the audience.
He answered the question winkingly, hinting that there was more to the story.
She winkingly agreed to the plan, letting everyone know she was in on the secret.
The teacher winkingly ignored the harmless prank, showing her quiet approval.
He winkingly passed the note, making it clear he understood the joke.
