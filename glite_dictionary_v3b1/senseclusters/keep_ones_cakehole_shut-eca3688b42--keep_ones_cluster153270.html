keep one's cakehole shut

verb
remain silent; not talk
6.7
Pronunciation: / kip wʌnz ˈkeɪkˌhoʊl ʃʌt / (AmE), / kiːp wʌnz ˈkeɪkˌhəʊl ʃʌt / (BrE)
(informal) to stop talking; to remain silent or not reveal something

Examples

To keep one's cakehole shut
If you can't keep your cakehole shut, you'll ruin the whole plan.
She had to keep her cakehole shut or risk offending her boss.
They begged him to keep his cakehole shut about the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keep quiet - to refrain from speaking or revealing information
  • antonym: spill the beans - to reveal a secret
