strike

noun
a gentle blow
Pronunciation: / straɪk / (AmE), / straɪk / (BrE)
a gentle or light blow, especially with the hand or a tool

Examples

A gentle strike
He gave the horse a gentle strike on the flank to encourage it forward.
She woke the sleeping child with a light strike on the shoulder.
The artist applied each strike of the brush with care and precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow - an act of striking or hitting, especially with the hand or a weapon
  • degree higher: hit - an act of striking someone or something with force
