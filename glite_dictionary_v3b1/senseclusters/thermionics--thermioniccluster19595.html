thermionics

noun
study of thermionic phenomena in electronics
Pronunciation: / θɜrˌmaɪˈɑnɪks / (AmE), / ˌθɜːm.aɪˈɒn.ɪks / (BrE)
the branch of electronics that deals with the emission of electrons from heated substances, especially with thermionic vacuum tubes
