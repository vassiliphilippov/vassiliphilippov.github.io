monofilm

noun
single-layer plastic film
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəʊˌfɪlm /; / ˈmɑːnoʊˌfɪlm / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊˌfɪlm / (BrE)
a single-layer, often transparent, flexible plastic film used in packaging, printing, lamination, or making items like windsurfing sails

Examples

A monofilm layer
The company uses monofilm to package its snacks because it is lightweight and cost-effective.
Switching to a recyclable monofilm helped the business reduce its environmental impact.
Engineers tested the durability of the monofilm before approving it for lamination.
The sailmaker recommended using monofilm for its light weight and transparency.
After several seasons, the monofilm on his windsurfing sail began to show signs of wear.
Many athletes prefer monofilm sails because they allow a clear view through the material.

  • synonym: polyester film - a strong, transparent plastic film made from polyester
  • synonym: polyfilm - a plastic film made from polymers, often used in packaging
