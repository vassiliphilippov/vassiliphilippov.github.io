Shaiva

noun
devotee of Shiva
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪvə / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪvə / (BrE)
a follower of the Hindu tradition who is devoted to the god Shiva

Examples

A Shaiva devotee
As a Shaiva, he observes special rituals during the festival of Maha Shivaratri.
The temple was built by a community of Shaivas centuries ago.
Many Shaiva traditions emphasize meditation and devotion to Shiva.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Vaishnava - a follower of the Hindu tradition devoted to the god Vishnu
