mitred

adjective
edges joined at angle
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.tərd/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.təd/ (BrE)
having edges or ends joined together at an angle, especially to form a corner in carpentry or construction

Examples

Mitred corners
The cabinet doors were carefully mitred to ensure a seamless fit at the corners.
He admired the mitred joints on the window frames, which showed great craftsmanship.
Each piece of trim was mitred so the edges would meet perfectly at the corners.
