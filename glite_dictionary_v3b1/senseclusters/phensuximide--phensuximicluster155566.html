phensuximide

noun
anticonvulsant drug for absence seizures
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɛnˈsʌksɪˌmaɪd / (AmE), / fɛnˈsʌksɪˌmiːd / (BrE)
A drug used as an anticonvulsant to treat absence (petit mal) seizures; also known by the trade name Milontin.

Examples

Phensuximide drug
After starting phensuximide, his seizure frequency decreased significantly.
Some patients experience mild side effects when taking phensuximide.
The doctor prescribed phensuximide to control her absence seizures.
