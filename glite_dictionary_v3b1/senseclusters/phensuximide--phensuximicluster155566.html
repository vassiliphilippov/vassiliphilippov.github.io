phensuximide

noun
anticonvulsant drug for absence seizures
9.9
Pronunciation: /fen.ˈsʌk.sɪ.maɪd/ (AmE), /fen.ˈsʌk.sɪ.maɪd/ (BrE)
A drug used as an anticonvulsant to treat absence (petit mal) seizures; also known by the trade name Milontin.

Examples

The doctor prescribed phensuximide to control her absence seizures.
