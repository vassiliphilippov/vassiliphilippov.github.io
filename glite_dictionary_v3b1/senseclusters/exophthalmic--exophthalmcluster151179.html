exophthalmic

adjective
protruding eyeballs
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkˌsɑfˈθælmɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛksɒfˈθælmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by abnormal protrusion of the eyeballs

Examples

Exophthalmic eye condition
The doctor noted the patient's exophthalmic appearance during the examination.
Graves' disease often results in exophthalmic symptoms that require medical attention.
Her exophthalmic eyes were a clear sign of an underlying thyroid disorder.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - not showing abnormal protrusion of the eyeballs
  • synonym: proptotic - having forward displacement of the eye
