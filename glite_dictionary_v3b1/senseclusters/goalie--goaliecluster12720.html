goalie

noun
player who guards the goal in team sports
5.0 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊ.liː/ (BrE)
the player in sports like soccer, hockey, or lacrosse whose job is to guard the goal and stop the other team from scoring
