besmear

verb
cover or smear with a substance
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈsmɪr / (AmE), / bɪˈsmɪə / (BrE)
to cover or smear (a surface) with a sticky or greasy substance

Examples

To besmear with oil
His hands were besmeared with oil from repairing the car.
The child besmeared the page with finger paint.
The walls were besmeared with soot after the fire.
