howdy

noun
informal greeting; hello
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊdi / (AmE), / ˈhaʊdi / (BrE)
An informal greeting, used especially in the southern United States, meaning 'hello'.

Examples

Howdy greeting
Howdy! Nice to see you again after such a long time.
He tipped his hat and said, howdy as he walked into the store.
The cowboy greeted everyone at the party with a cheerful howdy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hello - A standard greeting used when meeting someone.
  • synonym: hi - A friendly informal greeting.
