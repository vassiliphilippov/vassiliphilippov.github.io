palate cleanser

noun
food/drink that refreshes taste between courses
6.0 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpæl.ət ˈklen.zər/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪt ˈklen.zər/ /; / /ˈpæl.ət ˈkliːn.zər/ (AmE), /ˈpæl.ət ˈklen.zə/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪt ˈklen.zə/ /; / /ˈpæl.ət ˈkliːn.zə/ (BrE)
A food or drink served to refresh the taste buds and remove lingering flavors, often between courses during a meal.

Examples

A palate cleanser between courses
A traditional palate cleanser helps diners enjoy each course with a fresh sense of taste.
At the elegant dinner, a small lemon sorbet was served as a palate cleanser between courses.
He offered a slice of cucumber as a palate cleanser after the spicy appetizer.
