arc light

noun
lamp producing light by electric arc
5.4 6.5
Pronunciation: / ɑrk laɪt / (AmE), / ɑːk laɪt / (BrE)
a lamp that produces light by an electric arc between two electrodes, typically used in early lighting and film projectors.

Examples

An arc light lamp
In early cinema, filmmakers often relied on the intense brightness of arc lights.

The theater was illuminated by an old arc light hanging from the ceiling.
