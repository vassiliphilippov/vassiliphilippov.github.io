immensely

adverb
to a very great extent
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɛnsli / (AmE), / ɪˈmɛn(t)sli / (BrE)
to an extremely great extent; very much

Examples

To be immensely grateful
She was immensely grateful for their help during the crisis.
The view from the mountain was immensely beautiful and unforgettable.
This discovery will immensely improve the effectiveness of the new treatment.
