derby

noun
local sports match
3.9 2.31/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd…úrbi / (AmE), / Ààd…ëÀêbi / (BrE)
a major horse race, sports match (especially between rivals), or other important race or contest

Examples

A football derby
The annual derby between the two cities is the highlight of the football season.
Fans lined the streets to watch the famous horse derby on Saturday afternoon.
Winning the local derby meant a lot to both the players and their supporters.
The city hosts a famous derby every summer that draws large crowds.
She trained her horse for months to compete in the derby.
Fans eagerly awaited the start of the annual car derby.
