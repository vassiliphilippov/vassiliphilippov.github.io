wacker

noun
slang hired killer
Pronunciation: / ˈwækər / (AmE), / ˈwækə / (BrE)
a person who kills or attacks others, especially as a hired killer or enforcer (slang)

Examples

A mob wacker
The mob hired a wacker to eliminate the witness before he could testify.
Rumors spread that a notorious wacker was behind the recent attacks in the city.
He feared crossing paths with a wacker after refusing to pay his debts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassin - a person who murders someone for political or monetary reasons
  • synonym: hitman - a person hired to kill someone
  • antonym: victim - a person harmed or killed as a result of a crime
