blameworthy

adjective
deserving blame or censure
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪmˌwɝði / (AmE), / ˈbleɪmwɜːði / (BrE)
Deserving blame or disapproval because of wrong or harmful actions.

Examples

blameworthy actions
Her blameworthy actions led to severe consequences for the entire team.
Some people found the manager's decision blameworthy, while others thought it was justified.
The judge considered him blameworthy for the accident because he ignored the safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: culpable - deserving blame or censure
  • antonym: innocent - not guilty of a crime or offense
