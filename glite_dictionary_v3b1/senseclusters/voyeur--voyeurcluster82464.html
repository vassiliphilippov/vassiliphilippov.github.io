voyeur

noun
a person who observes sexual acts or nudity for pleasure
9.1
Pronunciation: /vɔɪ.ˈjɜːr/ /; / /vɔɪ.ˈjuːr/ (AmE), /vɔɪ.ˈjɜː/ /; / /vɔɪ.ˈjʊə/ (BrE)
a person who gains sexual pleasure from watching others when they are naked or engaged in sexual activity, especially secretly

Examples

Many films have explored the psychology of the voyeur and the thrill of watching from a distance.
