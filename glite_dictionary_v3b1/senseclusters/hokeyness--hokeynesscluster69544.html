hokeyness

noun
silly or fake quality
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊkinəs / (AmE), / ˈhəʊkinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being silly, overly sentimental, or fake in a way that is not taken seriously

Examples

The hokeyness of a show
The hokeyness of the commercial made everyone laugh, even though it was meant to be serious.
Despite the hokeyness of the movie, it became a cult classic among fans.
He couldn't get past the hokeyness of the speech and found it hard to take seriously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corniness - the quality of being overly sentimental or cheesy
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being genuine and honest
