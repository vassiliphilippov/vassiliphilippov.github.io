(of light) so bright as to make it difficult to see; shining intensely.

Examples

A blinding light

Synonyms

    dazzling shines with such intense light that it makes you look away, while blinding describes light that makes seeing difficult.
  • glaring
    3.8 0.07/m
    glaring is a harsh, bright light that hurts the eyes, while blinding means light so strong it prevents clear sight.
  • brilliant
    3.0 4.15/m

  • blazing
    3.5 0.04/m
