blinding

adjective
shining very brightly
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of light) so bright as to make it difficult to see; shining intensely.

Examples

A blinding light
A blinding sunrise greeted us as we reached the mountain peak.
She shielded her eyes from the blinding light of the camera flash.
The blinding snow made it almost impossible to see the road ahead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzling - so bright as to blind temporarily; extremely impressive
  • synonym: glaring - giving out or reflecting a strong or dazzling light
