anthropomorphite

noun
person attributing human traits
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.θrə.pə.ˈmɑːr.faɪt/ /; / /æn.θrə.pə.ˈmɔːr.faɪt/ (AmE), /æn.θrə.pə.ˈmɔː.faɪt/ (BrE)
a person who attributes human characteristics, feelings, or intentions to animals, objects, or other non-human entities

Examples

An anthropomorphite and animals
As an anthropomorphite, she believed her cat understood every word she said.
The writer, a known anthropomorphite, often gave emotions to the trees in his stories.
Some psychologists study why a child becomes an anthropomorphite and talks to their toys.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animist - a person who believes that objects and natural phenomena have a spirit or consciousness
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts things as they are and does not attribute human traits to non-human things
