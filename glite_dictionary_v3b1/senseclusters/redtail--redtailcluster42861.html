redtail

noun
European songbird with reddish tail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌteɪl / (BrE)
A European songbird with a reddish breast and tail, related to Old World robins.

Examples

A singing redtail
A redtail was spotted singing from the top of the old oak tree.
Many birdwatchers hope to catch a glimpse of the elusive redtail during spring migration.
The redtail perched briefly on the garden wall before fluttering away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redstart - A European songbird with a red tail, closely related to the redtail
