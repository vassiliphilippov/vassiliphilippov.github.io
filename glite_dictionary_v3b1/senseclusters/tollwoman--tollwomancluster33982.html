tollwoman

noun
female toll collector
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊlˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to collect tolls at a tollbooth or tollgate

Examples

A tollwoman at booth
A friendly tollwoman waved us through the crowded toll plaza.
After years as a tollwoman, she knew most of the regular commuters by name.
The tollwoman smiled as she handed me my change at the booth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toll collector - a person who collects tolls
  • synonym: tollman - a man whose job is to collect tolls
