start a new life

verb
begin living differently after change
Pronunciation: / stɑɹt ə nu laɪf / (AmE), / stɑːt ə njuː laɪf / (BrE)

Examples

Many people move to big cities to start a new life and pursue their dreams.
She wanted to leave her past behind and start a new life with her children.
