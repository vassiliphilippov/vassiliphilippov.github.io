gladiola

noun
a flowering plant of the genus Gladiolus
Pronunciation: / ɡlæd.iˈoʊ.lə / (AmE), / ɡlæd.iˈəʊ.lə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Gladiolus, having sword-shaped leaves and spikes of brightly colored funnel-shaped flowers, widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.
