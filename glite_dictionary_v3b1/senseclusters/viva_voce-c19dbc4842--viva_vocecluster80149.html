viva voce

adverb
orally; by word of mouth
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌviːvə ˈvoʊtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˌviːvə ˈvəʊtʃi / (BrE)
orally; by word of mouth, especially when spoken aloud rather than written down, often used in formal or academic contexts.

Examples

Viva voce instructions
All instructions were given viva voce to ensure clarity and immediate feedback.
She delivered her thesis defense viva voce, impressing the examiners with her eloquence.
The committee conducted the examination viva voce instead of requiring a written test.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in writing - in written form, not spoken
  • synonym: orally - by spoken word rather than in writing
