red wine

noun
wine made from dark grapes; red in color
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɛd waɪn / (AmE), / rɛd waɪn / (BrE)
Wine with a red color, made from dark-colored grape varieties where the skins remain during fermentation.

Examples

A glass of red wine
A glass of red wine pairs well with steak or other rich dishes.
She prefers red wine over white when dining with friends.
The sommelier recommended a bold red wine from France for the main course.
