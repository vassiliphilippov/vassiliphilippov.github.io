Synonyms

  • crossing
    7.2 0.5/m
    crossing is the genetic act of breeding two varieties or species to make hybrids, while hybridization is the general process of producing such hybrids.
  • crossbreeding
    4.4 0.1/m
    crossbreeding mixes breeds or species on farms or gardens to create desired hybrids, while hybridization refers to the broader scientific process of making hybrids.
  • intercrossing
    7.0 0.01/m
    intercrossing means breeding between different groups to introduce hybrid traits, while hybridization names the full process of producing those hybrids.
  • interbreeding
    6.2 0.3/m
    interbreeding is mating between populations or species that can produce hybrids, while hybridization focuses specifically on creating hybrid offspring.
  • cross
    5.9 8/m
    cross is the resulting hybrid animal or plant or the act that makes it, while hybridization names the overall scientific breeding process.
