stilt

noun
supporting post for buildings
7.4
Pronunciation: / stɪlt / (AmE), / stɪlt / (BrE)
a long, upright post or column, especially one used to support a building above the ground or water

Examples

House on stilts
Many coastal homes in the area stand on stilts above the marshy ground.
The house was built on stilts to protect it from flooding during the rainy season.
Workers drove several stilts into the sand to support the new pier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pile - a long, slender column driven into the ground to support structures
