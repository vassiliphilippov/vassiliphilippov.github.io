precorrection

noun
correction before publication
8.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåpriÀêk…ôÀàr…õk É…ôn / (AmE), / ÀåpriÀêk…ôÀàr…õk É…ôn / (BrE)
an editorial change or correction made to a document or draft before it is published or distributed

Examples

Editorial precorrection of drafts
The editor performed a thorough precorrection of the manuscript before sending it to the publisher.
A careful precorrection can prevent embarrassing mistakes from appearing in the final document.
She always does a precorrection to catch formatting errors before submitting her reports.
