Krampus

noun
harsh punisher
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkræmpəs / (AmE), / ˈkrampəs / (BrE)
a person who harshly punishes or scolds others, especially children, often used metaphorically for someone strict or severe

Examples

A real Krampus at school
After the students broke the rules, the principal acted like a Krampus and handed out extra homework.
Nobody wanted to cross Mr. Lee, who had a reputation as the office Krampus for his strict policies.
When her little brother misbehaved, she jokingly called herself the family Krampus for scolding him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disciplinarian - a person who enforces strict discipline
  • antonym: lenient - not strict; tolerant or permissive
  • synonym: martinet - a strict or demanding person, especially in authority
