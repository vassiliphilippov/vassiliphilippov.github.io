nonprescribed

adjective
not authorized by doctor
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnprɪˈskraɪbd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnprɪˈskraɪbd / (BrE)

Only approved treatments are allowed; any nonprescribed therapies must be reported.
