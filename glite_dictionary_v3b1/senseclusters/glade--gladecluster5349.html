glade

noun
open area in a forest
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪd / (AmE), / ɡleɪd / (BrE)
an open area within a forest where there are few or no trees

Examples

A glade in forest
A family of deer grazed peacefully in the glade surrounded by tall pines.
Sunlight streamed into the glade, warming the wildflowers that covered the ground.
We set up our tent in a small glade near the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clearing - an area in a forest where trees have been removed
