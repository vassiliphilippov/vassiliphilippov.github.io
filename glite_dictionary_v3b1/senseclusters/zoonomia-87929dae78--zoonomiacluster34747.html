Zoonomia

proper noun
mammalian genome project
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈnoʊmiə / (AmE), / zəʊˈnəʊmiə / (BrE)
the name of an international scientific project and consortium that compares mammalian genomes to study evolution, conservation, and genomic function
