sundries

noun
miscellaneous small items
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌndɹiz / (AmE), / ˈsʌndɹiːz / (BrE)
various small, miscellaneous items or articles, often of little value or too numerous to name individually

Examples

Box of sundries
After cleaning his desk, he stored all the sundries in a small box.
She bought a few sundries such as soap and toothpaste at the corner shop.
The drawer was full of sundries like paper clips, buttons, and old batteries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscellany - a mixture or collection of various things
