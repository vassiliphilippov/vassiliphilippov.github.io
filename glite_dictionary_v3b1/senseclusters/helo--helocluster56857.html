helo

verb
to move by helicopter
9.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhiÀêlo ä / (AmE), / ÀàhiÀêl…ô ä / (BrE)
to transport or insert people or supplies by helicopter, especially in military operations

Examples

To helo supplies
The rescue team will helo supplies to the remote village after the flood.
They plan to helo the engineers directly onto the oil platform.
During the operation, soldiers were heloed into the combat zone at dawn.
