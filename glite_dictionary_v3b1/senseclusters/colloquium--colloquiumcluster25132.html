colloquium

noun
academic seminar or meeting
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊkwiəm / (AmE), / kəˈlɒkwɪəm / (BrE)
an academic meeting or seminar, often for discussion or presenting research

Examples

Academic colloquium meeting
Each colloquium explores a different topic and is led by a guest lecturer.
She presented her findings at a scientific colloquium attended by leading experts in the field.
The department hosts a weekly colloquium featuring prominent researchers from around the world.
At yesterday’s colloquium, a visiting scholar discussed the ethics of artificial intelligence.
She attended a colloquium on climate change hosted by the university.
The department holds a weekly colloquium where faculty and students can present their research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a large formal meeting for discussion
  • synonym: seminar - an academic meeting or class for discussion
