call it a night

verb
stop activities for the evening
3.0
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɪt ə naɪt / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪt ə naɪt / (BrE)
to stop what you are doing for the evening, especially in order to go to bed or leave for home

Examples

To call it a night early
After four hours of studying, they agreed to call it a night and get some rest.
It's late, so let's call it a night and finish up tomorrow.
The group decided to call it a night after the last song played.
