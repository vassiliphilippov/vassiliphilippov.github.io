terazosin

noun
drug for high blood pressure and prostate
7.5
Pronunciation: / təˈræzəsɪn / (AmE), / təˈræzəsɪn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat high blood pressure and benign prostatic hyperplasia by relaxing blood vessels and muscles in the prostate
