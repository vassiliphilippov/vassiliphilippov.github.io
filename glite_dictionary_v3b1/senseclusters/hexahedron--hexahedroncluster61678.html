hexahedron

noun
solid with six flat faces
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛksəˈhiːdrən / (AmE), / ˌhɛksəˈhiːdrən / (BrE)
A solid geometric figure with six flat faces.

Examples

A hexahedron shape
A dice is a common example of a hexahedron, since it has six sides.
In geometry class, we learned that a hexahedron has exactly six faces.
Unlike a sphere, a hexahedron has flat surfaces and straight edges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cube - a regular hexahedron with six square faces
