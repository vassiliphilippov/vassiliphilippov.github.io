bidirectionally

adverb
in both directions
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪdəˈrɛkʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌbaɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəli / (BrE)
in both directions or ways at the same time

Examples

To bidirectionally flow information
Information can flow bidirectionally between the teacher and the students in an interactive classroom.
The device is designed to transmit signals bidirectionally for more efficient communication.
Modern trains can operate bidirectionally without needing to turn around at the end of the line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unidirectionally - in only one direction
