community

noun
group of interacting organisms in a habitat (ecology)
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
(Ecology) A group of interdependent organisms living together in the same area and interacting with each other.

Examples

Forest community
A forest community includes various species of trees, plants, animals, and microorganisms.
Ecologists study how energy flows through a community of organisms.
The decline of one species can disrupt the balance of the entire community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecosystem - a biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment
