permanency

noun
lasting quality or durability
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.mə.nən.si / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.mə.nən.si / (BrE)
The quality or state of lasting for an indefinite or unlimited duration; permanence.

Examples

The permanency of foundations
Architects discussed the permanency of the building's foundations before beginning construction.
Job security and permanency are major factors when considering a new position.
Many people value the permanency of a lifelong friendship above all else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permanence - the state or quality of lasting or remaining unchanged
  • antonym: temporariness - the state of lasting only for a limited period
