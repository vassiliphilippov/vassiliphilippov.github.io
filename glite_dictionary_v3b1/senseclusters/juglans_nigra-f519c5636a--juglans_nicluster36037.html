Juglans nigra

noun
North American black walnut tree
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡ.lænz ˈnaɪ.ɡrə / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡ.lænz ˈnɪɡ.rə / (BrE)
A North American walnut tree with hard, dark wood and edible nuts, commonly known as the black walnut.
