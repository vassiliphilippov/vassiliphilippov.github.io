lotta

noun
informal large amount
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːtə / (AmE), / ˈlɒtə / (BrE)
an informal way of saying a large amount or number of something

Examples

A lotta money
We have a lotta work to do before the deadline.
There's a lotta excitement about the new movie coming out this weekend.
She brought a lotta snacks for the road trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: few - a small number of things or people

  • synonym: plenty - a large or sufficient amount or quantity
