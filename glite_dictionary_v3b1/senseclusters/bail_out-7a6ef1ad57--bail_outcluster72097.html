bail out

verb
remove water from a boat
4.9
Pronunciation: / beɪl aʊt / (AmE), / beɪl aʊt / (BrE)
to remove water from a boat by repeatedly scooping it out and throwing it over the side

Examples

To bail out a boat
After the storm, they had to bail out the boat to keep it from sinking.
She grabbed a bucket to bail out the water accumulating inside the dinghy.
We took turns bailing out as quickly as possible during the leak.
