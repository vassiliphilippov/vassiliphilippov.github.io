thought bubble

noun
cartoon element showing a character's thoughts
7.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /θɔːt ˈbʌb.əl/ /; / /θɑːt ˈbʌb.əl/ (AmE), /θɔːt ˈbʌb.əl/ (BrE)
a shaped visual device in cartoons and comics that represents a character's unspoken thoughts, typically shown as a cloud-like bubble containing text or images

Examples

A thought bubble in cartoons
Children enjoy adding a thought bubble to their drawings to express their characters' feelings.
In many graphic novels, a thought bubble is used to reveal a character's inner monologue.
The comic artist drew a thought bubble above the character's head to show what he was thinking.
