(psychiatry) A psychological disorder affecting thought or emotion; a term considered more neutral than mental illness.

Examples

A mental disturbance case
A variety of therapies are available to treat different types of mental disturbance.
He struggled with a mental disturbance that affected his daily life for years.
The patient showed signs of a mental disturbance following the traumatic event.

Synonyms

  • psychological disorder
    4.6 0.34/m
    psychological disorder is a clinical, neutral term used by doctors and researchers, while mental disturbance sounds milder and less clinical.
  • mental disorder
    3.3 2.29/m
    mental disorder is a standard clinical label for significant thought or mood problems, while mental disturbance feels more neutral and general.
  • mental illness
    3.1 8.98/m
    mental illness is often used to name serious conditions needing treatment, while mental disturbance is a more neutral, less medical phrase.
