cultural Marxist

noun

A person who advocates or is associated with cultural Marxism, a theory in sociology that critiques capitalist culture and ideology; often used pejoratively, especially in discussions of perceived influence in societal institutions.

Examples

cultural Marxists in society
He accused them of being cultural Marxists who want to change traditional values.
Some commentators warn about the influence of cultural Marxists in universities.
The article argued that cultural Marxists are reshaping societal norms through media and education.
