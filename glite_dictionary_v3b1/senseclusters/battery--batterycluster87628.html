battery

noun
group of stampers for crushing ore
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ˈbatəri / (BrE)
a set of heavy stampers in a single mortar, used for crushing ores in mining processes

Examples

Mining battery
A five-stamp battery was installed to improve the mine's productivity.
The gold was extracted after the ore passed through the battery for crushing.
The noise from the operating battery could be heard across the valley.
