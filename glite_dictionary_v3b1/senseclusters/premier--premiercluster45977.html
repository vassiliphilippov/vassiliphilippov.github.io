premier

verb
to be shown or performed for the first time
1.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈmɪr / (AmE), / ˈprɛm.i.eə /; / prɪˈmɪə / (BrE)
to present or perform something, such as a play or film, for the first time

Examples

To premier a film
The director chose Paris as the city to premier his latest movie.
The musical premiered to sold-out audiences and received rave reviews.
The new ballet will premier at the Royal Theatre next month.
A new musical is premiering on Broadway this fall.
The film will premier at the international festival next month.
The symphony was premiered in Vienna to great acclaim.
