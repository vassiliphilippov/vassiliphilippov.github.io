sidhe

noun
Irish fairies
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃiː / (AmE), / ʃiː / (BrE)
supernatural beings or fairies in Irish folklore, often believed to inhabit hills or mounds

Examples

The sidhe people
Ancient stories tell of the sidhe living beneath grassy hills in Ireland.
Some people leave offerings for the sidhe to bring good luck and avoid mischief.
The legend describes the sidhe as beautiful and mysterious beings who rarely appear to humans.

  • synonym: fairy - a small, magical being in folklore
