fenestrated

adjective
having small openings
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛnəˌstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɛnɪstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
having one or more small openings or perforations, especially in medical, biological, or anatomical contexts

Examples
