Synonyms

  • contemplative
    6.0 2.07/m
    contemplative is calm and focused on deep thought, while reflective is a general word for thinking seriously about things.
  • musing
    6.1 0.00/m
    musing is quietly lost in thought or daydreams, while reflective describes more deliberate, serious consideration of ideas or events.
  • ruminative
    7.9 0.08/m
    ruminative repeats thoughts and can feel heavy or stuck, while reflective means thinking deeply in a calmer, clearer way.
  • thoughtful
    2.6 6.57/m
    thoughtful often means kind and considerate as well as reflective, while reflective focuses on thinking deeply about ideas or events.
  • pondering
    5.5 0.00/m
    pondering is the act of turning an idea over in your mind now, while reflective describes a general habit of deep thought.
  • meditative
    5.6 1.53/m
    meditative is quiet, calm thinking like in meditation, while reflective is broader and can include practical or active thought.
  • pensive
    7.0 1.24/m
    pensive is thoughtful with a sad or serious mood, while reflective is a neutral word for calm, careful thinking or curiosity.
  • introspective
    7.4 1.66/m
    introspective looks inward at one's own feelings and thoughts, while reflective can mean thinking about many things beyond the self.
  • self-examining
    6.8 0.00/m
    self-examining inspects personal feelings or actions in detail, while reflective can be used for any careful thought, not only about oneself.
