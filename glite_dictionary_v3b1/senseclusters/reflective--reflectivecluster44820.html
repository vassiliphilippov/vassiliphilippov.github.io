reflective

adjective
deeply thoughtful; contemplative
5.6 4.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛktɪv / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛktɪv / (BrE)
thinking deeply or seriously about things; thoughtful or contemplative

Examples

Reflective mood
After the meeting, she became reflective, considering how her actions had affected the team.
During long walks, Tom often grew reflective and pondered the direction of his life.
His reflective nature made him an excellent writer of personal essays.
He is a reflective person who often spends time considering complex issues.
She became particularly reflective after reading the philosopher's work.
The film has a reflective tone, encouraging viewers to think about their own lives.
