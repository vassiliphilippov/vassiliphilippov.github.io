lucidness

noun
clarity; ease of understanding
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being clear and easy to understand.

Examples

Clear lucidness
Achieving lucidness in your speech can help others follow your arguments more easily.
Her explanation had a remarkable lucidness that made complex ideas seem simple.
The lucidness of his writing has earned him many admirers.
