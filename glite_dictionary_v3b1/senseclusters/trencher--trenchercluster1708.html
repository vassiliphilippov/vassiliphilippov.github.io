trencher

noun
person who digs trenches
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈtrɛntʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to dig trenches, especially for construction or military purposes

Examples

A trencher worker
Each trencher was given sturdy tools to ensure the trench was dug quickly and safely.
Experienced trenchers are often hired for large pipeline projects.
The trencher worked tirelessly through the night to complete the fortification.
