troop

noun
a group unit of Girl or Boy Scouts
2.7 2.64/m
Pronunciation: / truÀêp / (AmE), / truÀêp / (BrE)
a unit of Girl Scouts or Boy Scouts, forming an organizational group within the scouting movement

Examples

A scout troop
Each troop meets weekly to plan activities and earn badges together.
My brother joined a new Boy Scout troop after moving to the city.
The local Girl Scout troop organized a community cleanup event last weekend.
