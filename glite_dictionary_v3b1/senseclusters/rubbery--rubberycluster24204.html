rubbery

adjective
flexible and tough like rubber
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ər.i / (BrE)
having a texture that is flexible and tough like rubber

Examples

Rubbery fingers
After hours in the water, my fingers felt rubbery and wrinkled.
The steak was overcooked and had a rubbery texture that made it hard to chew.
The toy's rubbery material made it safe for children to squeeze and bend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic - able to resume its normal shape after stretching or compression
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed easily; stiff
