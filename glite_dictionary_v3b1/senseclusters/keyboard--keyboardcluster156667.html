keyboard

verb
to type on keyboard
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkiːbɔːd / (BrE)
to enter data or text into a computer or device using a keyboard

Examples

To keyboard data
She keyboarded all the survey responses into the database before the deadline.
After the meeting, he quickly keyboarded his notes so he would not forget any details.
The students keyboard their essays directly into the online system for submission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: input - to enter data into a computer
  • synonym: type - to write using a keyboard or typewriter
