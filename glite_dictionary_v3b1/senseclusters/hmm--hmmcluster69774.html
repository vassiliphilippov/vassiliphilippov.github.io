hmm

interjection
expressing pleasure
7.8 0.88/m
Pronunciation: / hm / (AmE), / hm / (BrE)
used to express pleasure, enjoyment, or approval, especially when tasting or experiencing something pleasing

Examples

Hmm after tasting
She tasted the cake and said, "hmm, this is delicious!"
After smelling the fresh coffee, he smiled and murmured, "hmm, that smells wonderful."
The child took a bite of the fruit and exclaimed, "hmm, I like this!"

Synonyms

  • mmm
    8.9 4.33/m
    mmm is a low, satisfied sound people make when tasting food, while hmm is a quieter, more ambiguous murmur of approval.
  • yum
    7.5 1.10/m
    yum is an open, clear exclamation used to call food delicious, while hmm is a quiet, often softer murmur of approval.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ugh - used to express dislike or disgust
