repentance

noun
sincere regret for wrongdoing
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛntəns / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛntəns / (BrE)
the feeling or expression of sincere regret or remorse for wrongdoing or past conduct

Examples

Deep repentance
Her deep repentance moved everyone in the room to forgive her.
The priest spoke about repentance during his sermon on forgiveness.
True repentance involves more than just saying sorry; it requires a change in behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrition - the state of feeling sorry for bad behavior
  • antonym: impenitence - lack of regret or remorse for past conduct
  • synonym: remorse - deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed
