Joseph Goebbels

proper noun
Nazi Germany's propaganda minister, 1897–1945
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊzəf ˈɡɜbəlz / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊzɪf ˈɡɜːbəlz / (BrE)
(1897–1945), German politician who served as the Minister of Propaganda in Nazi Germany and was instrumental in promoting the regime's anti-Semitic policies and wartime propaganda.

Examples

Joseph Goebbels Nazi propaganda
Joseph Goebbels was a key architect of the Nazi propaganda machine during World War II.
Many historians study the speeches of Joseph Goebbels to understand the spread of Nazi ideology.
The documentary examined the impact of Joseph Goebbels's propaganda on German society.
