ditcher

noun
person who abandons
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈdɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person who abandons or deserts someone, especially suddenly or without warning

Examples

A ditcher friend
After waiting for hours, she realized her friend was a ditcher and had left her alone.
No one likes being called a ditcher after skipping out on group plans.
He earned a reputation as a ditcher because he often left events early without telling anyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandoner - someone who leaves a person or thing behind
  • antonym: companion - a person who stays with and supports someone
  • synonym: deserter - a person who leaves someone or something behind, especially in a disloyal way
