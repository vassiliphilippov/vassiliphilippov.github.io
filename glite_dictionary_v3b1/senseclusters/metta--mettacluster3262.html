metta

noun
Buddhist loving-kindness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtə / (AmE), / ˈmɛtə / (BrE)
a Buddhist concept of loving-kindness, goodwill, and benevolence toward oneself and others

Examples

Metta meditation
Practicing metta meditation can help develop a more compassionate attitude toward others.
The monk spoke about the importance of metta in daily life and relationships.
She tries to approach every situation with metta, even when facing difficulties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassion - sympathetic concern for the suffering of others
  • antonym: hatred - intense dislike or ill will
