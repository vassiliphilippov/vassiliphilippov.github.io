anemic

adjective
lacking energy or vigor
2.2
Pronunciation: / əˈnimɪk /; / əˈniː.mɪk /; / əˈniːmɪk / (AmE), / əˈniːmɪk /; / əˈniː.mɪk / (BrE)
lacking in strength, energy, color, or spirit; weak or feeble (can also refer to the medical condition anemia)

Examples

The patient's skin appeared pale and anemic during the examination.

His argument for the proposal was anaemic and failed to persuade the board.
The play received anaemic reviews from the critics, who found it slow and unconvincing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anemic - suffering from or relating to anemia (American spelling)
  • synonym: feeble - lacking physical strength, especially as a result of age or illness
  • synonym: pallid - pale, typically because of poor health
  • antonym: robust - strong and healthy; vigorous
  • antonym: vigorous - strong, healthy, and full of energy
