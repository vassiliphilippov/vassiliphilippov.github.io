chlorinator

noun
chlorine dispenser
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklɔːrəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈklɔːrɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a device that adds chlorine to water to disinfect or purify it, especially in swimming pools or water treatment systems
