splendid

adjective
impressive or magnificent
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɛn.dɪd /; / ˈsplɛndɪd / (AmE), / ˈsplɛn.dɪd /; / ˈsplɛndɪd / (BrE)
very impressive, beautiful, or excellent; magnificent

Examples

A splendid view

She wore a splendid gown that caught everyone's attention at the gala.
The palace was decorated in splendid colors for the festival.
We had a splendid time hiking in the mountains last weekend.
