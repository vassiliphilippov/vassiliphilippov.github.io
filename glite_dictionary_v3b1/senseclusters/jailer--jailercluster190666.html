jailer

noun
person who guards prisoners
9.9 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒeɪ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒeɪ.lə/ (BrE)
a person in charge of guarding prisoners in a jail or prison

Examples

The jailer guards prisoners
After years as a jailer, he knew every corner of the prison.

In old novels, the gaoler is often portrayed as a harsh and unfeeling character.
She pleaded with the gaoler to let her see her brother one last time.
The gaoler checked each cell carefully before his shift ended.
