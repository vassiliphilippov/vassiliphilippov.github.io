medial

adjective
relating to the middle or midline
7.4 1.53/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàmiÀêdi…ôl / (BrE)
situated in or relating to the middle or midline, especially in anatomy

Examples

The medial line

In linguistics, a medial sound occurs in the middle of a word.
