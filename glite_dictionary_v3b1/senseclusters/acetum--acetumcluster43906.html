acetum

noun
dilute solution of acetic acid
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈsiːtəm / (AmE), / əˈsiːtəm / (BrE)
A dilute solution of acetic acid, sometimes used as a solvent, especially in pharmacology or chemistry.

Examples

Acetum solution
Doctors sometimes prescribed medicines dissolved in acetum for easier absorption.
Historically, certain tinctures used acetum as a solvent instead of alcohol.
The laboratory technician prepared an acetum solution for testing.
