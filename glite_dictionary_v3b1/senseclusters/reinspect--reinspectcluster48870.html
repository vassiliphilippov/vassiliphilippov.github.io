reinspect

verb
to examine again
3.4
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈspekt/ /; / /rɪ.ɪn.ˈspekt/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈspekt/ /; / /rɪ.ɪn.ˈspekt/ (BrE)
