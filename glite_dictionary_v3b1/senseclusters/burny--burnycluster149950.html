burny

adjective
causing burning sensation
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrni / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni / (BrE)
causing a burning or stinging sensation, especially in the mouth or on the skin

Examples

Burny feeling on skin
The ointment had a slightly burny feeling when I applied it to my skin.
After eating the hot peppers, he complained of a burny sensation in his mouth.
Some people find that strong mouthwash leaves a burny taste after rinsing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cooling - causing a sensation of coolness
  • synonym: spicy - having a strong, hot flavor that produces a burning feeling
  • synonym: tingly - causing a slight stinging or prickling sensation
