American olive

noun
tree of southern U.S. with purple fruit
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɑlɪv / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɒlɪv / (BrE)
a small tree native to the southern United States, bearing clusters of dull white flowers and dark purple fruits

Examples

The American olive tree
The American olive attracts many birds with its dark purple fruits.
The wood from the American olive is sometimes used for small crafts.
We planted an American olive in our backyard for its decorative flowers.
