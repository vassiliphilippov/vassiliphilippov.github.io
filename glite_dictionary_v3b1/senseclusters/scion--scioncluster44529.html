scion

noun
descendant or heir, esp. in a notable family
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈsaɪ.ən / (BrE)
a young member of a wealthy, influential, or notable family, especially one regarded as a descendant or heir

Examples

A scion of family
He is the only scion of a powerful business dynasty.
Many expected the scion of the famous family to enter politics.
The company was inherited by a scion of its founder.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ancestor - a person from whom one is descended
  • synonym: descendant - a person descended from a particular ancestor
