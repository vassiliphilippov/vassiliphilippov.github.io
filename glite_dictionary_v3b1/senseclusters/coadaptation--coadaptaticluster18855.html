coadaptation

noun
mutual adaptation
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˌædæpˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or state in which two or more organisms, genes, or parts of a system adapt or evolve together in ways that benefit each other
