ultralight

adjective
extremely lightweight
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
extremely light in weight, especially compared to similar items

Examples

Ultralight backpack
The ultralight backpack made hiking up the mountain much easier.
She bought an ultralight jacket for her summer travels.
Many campers prefer ultralight gear to reduce the weight they carry.
