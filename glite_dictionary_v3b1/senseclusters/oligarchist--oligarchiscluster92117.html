oligarchist

noun
member of oligarchy
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːlɪˈɡɑːrkɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪst / (BrE)
a person who is a member of or supports rule by a small, powerful group (an oligarchy)

Examples

The oligarchist supports power
The wealthy oligarchist influenced government decisions behind the scenes.
Many citizens protested against the power held by each oligarchist in the council.
An oligarchist often benefits from policies that favor the elite minority.
The oligarchist believed that only a select few should make important decisions for the country.
Critics accused the senator of being an oligarchist because of his support for elite rule.
During the debate, she was labeled an oligarchist for opposing broader participation in government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aristocrat - a person belonging to or supporting the aristocracy
  • antonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy or majority rule
  • synonym: oligarch - a member of a small ruling group in an oligarchy
