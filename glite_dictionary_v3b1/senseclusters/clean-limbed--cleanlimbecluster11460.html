clean-limbed

adjective
having well-proportioned limbs
Pronunciation: / ˈklin lɪmd / (AmE), / ˈkliːn lɪmd / (BrE)
Having well-shaped and proportionate limbs, especially suggesting strength and grace.

Examples

clean-limbed horses
Farmers preferred horses that were clean-limbed and sturdy for work in the fields.
In the portrait, the young man appeared vigorous and clean-limbed, the picture of health.
The athlete was described as clean-limbed and graceful as he crossed the finish line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkward - Ungainly or lacking grace in movement or proportion
  • synonym: well-proportioned - Having pleasing proportions, especially of the body or limbs
