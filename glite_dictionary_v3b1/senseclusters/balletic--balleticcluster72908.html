balletic

adjective
resembling ballet in style or movement
Pronunciation: / bæˈlɛdɪk / (AmE), / bæˈlɛtɪk / (BrE)
characteristic of or resembling ballet, especially in terms of grace, elegance, or movement.

Examples

Balletic movements
Her balletic movements captivated everyone in the audience.
His gestures had a balletic quality, smooth and controlled.
The football player's balletic leap amazed the spectators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graceful - showing grace or elegance in movement or form
