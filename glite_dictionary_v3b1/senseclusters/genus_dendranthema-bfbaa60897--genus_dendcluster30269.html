genus Dendranthema

noun
a genus of flowering plants, including florist's chrysanthemums
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.drænˌθi.mə / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.drænˌθiː.mə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae, often included in the genus Chrysanthemum, comprising species that are important as florist's chrysanthemums.
