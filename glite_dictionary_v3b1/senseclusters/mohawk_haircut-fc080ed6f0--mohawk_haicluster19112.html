mohawk haircut

noun
hairstyle with a central strip of hair
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊˌhɑk ˈhɛrˌkʌt / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌhɑːk ˈheəˌkʌt / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which both sides of the scalp are shaved, leaving a strip of noticeably longer hair in the center.

Examples

A mohawk haircut style
He decided to get a mohawk haircut before the music festival.
Her parents were surprised when she came home with a bright green mohawk haircut.
The football player was known for his distinctive mohawk haircut.

Related Terms
