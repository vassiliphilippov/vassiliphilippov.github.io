irruption

noun
sudden violent entry or invasion
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
a sudden and violent entry or invasion into a place or situation

Examples

An irruption of thieves
The city suffered an irruption of illegal activities during the festival.
The protest led to the irruption of people into the parliament building.
There was a sudden irruption of laughter after his unexpected joke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eruption - a sudden outbreak or release, especially of something forceful like lava, emotion, etc.
