axisymmetric

adjective
symmetrical around axis
Pronunciation: / ˌæksɪsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌæksɪsɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
having symmetry around a central axis, so that the object looks the same when rotated about that axis

Examples

Axisymmetric shape
A spinning top is axisymmetric because it looks the same all around its axis.
The engineer designed an axisymmetric nozzle to ensure even flow in all directions.
Many planets are nearly axisymmetric due to their rotation and gravitational forces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asymmetric - not having symmetry
  • synonym: radially symmetric - having symmetry around a central point or axis
