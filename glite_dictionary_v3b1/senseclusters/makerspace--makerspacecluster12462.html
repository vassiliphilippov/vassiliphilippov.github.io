makerspace

noun
collaborative workshop
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkərˌspeɪs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkəˌspeɪs / (BrE)
a shared space with tools and materials where people can create, build, invent, and learn together, often collaboratively

Examples

A makerspace workshop
The school opened a new makerspace where students can experiment with robotics and electronics.
Anyone interested in woodworking or 3D printing can join the community makerspace downtown.
She spent her afternoon at the makerspace designing a prototype for her science project.
The community center built a makerspace where people can use 3D printers and woodworking tools.
Students collaborated on their science projects in the school's new makerspace.
Many libraries now offer a makerspace for creative activities like coding and crafting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laboratory - a place equipped for scientific experiments or research
  • synonym: workshop - a room or building where things are made or repaired using tools
