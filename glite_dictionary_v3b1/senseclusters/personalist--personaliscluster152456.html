personalist

noun
individuality advocate
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or advocates the importance of individual personality and personal experience

Examples

A personalist thinker
As a personalist, she emphasized the unique value of every individual in her philosophy.
The personalist perspective influenced the way the organization treated its members.
He was known as a personalist who believed that personal stories shape society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collectivist - a person who values the group over the individual
  • synonym: individualist - a person who values independence and self-reliance
