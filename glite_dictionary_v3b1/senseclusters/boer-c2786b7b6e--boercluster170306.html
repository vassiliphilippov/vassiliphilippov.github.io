Boer

noun
descendant of Dutch settlers in South Africa
8.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / b är / (AmE), / b…îÀê / (BrE)
A white native of South Africa, especially of the Cape Province, descended from Dutch settlers and speaking Afrikaans.

Examples

Boer farmers
During the 19th century, many Boer farmers migrated inland to escape British rule.
Her great-grandfather was a Boer who established a ranch near Johannesburg.
The Boer War was fought between the British Empire and the descendants of Dutch settlers in South Africa.

Synonyms

  • Afrikaner
    7.2 0.71/m
    Afrikaner refers to a whole South African ethnic group with Afrikaans as a first language, while Boer often means rural farmers of that group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Afrikaner - a descendant of predominantly Dutch settlers in South Africa, Afrikaans-speaking
