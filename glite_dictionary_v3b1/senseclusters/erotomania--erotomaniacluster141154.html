erotomania

noun
delusional love belief
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌroʊtəˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ɪˌrɒtəˈmeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a psychological disorder in which a person has a delusional belief that someone, often of higher status, is in love with them

Examples

Erotomania about a celebrity
The patient was diagnosed with erotomania after insisting a famous actor was secretly in love with her.
In some cases, erotomania can lead to persistent attempts to contact the imagined admirer.
The film explores the consequences of erotomania on both the sufferer and the person they believe loves them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delusion - a false belief held despite evidence to the contrary
  • antonym: paranoia - an irrational suspicion or mistrust of others
