paraph

noun
decorative flourish on a signature
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpær.əf / (AmE), / ˈpær.æf / (BrE)
a flourish or decorative stroke added after or under a signature, originally to help prevent forgery

Examples

A paraph flourish
His paraph was so distinctive it made his signature nearly impossible to forge.
Some artists include a unique paraph with their names to authenticate their works.
The notary added an elegant paraph beneath her signature on the official document.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signature - a person's name written in one's own hand; a signed name
