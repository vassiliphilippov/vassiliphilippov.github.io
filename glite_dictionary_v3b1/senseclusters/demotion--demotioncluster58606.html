demotion

noun
the act of lowering in rank or position
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
the act of reducing someone to a lower rank or less important position

Examples

Job demotion
After the investigation, the manager faced demotion for violating company policies.
His demotion came as a surprise to many of his colleagues.
The threat of demotion motivated employees to improve their performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: promotion - the act of raising someone to a higher rank or position
