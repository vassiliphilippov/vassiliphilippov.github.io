pangenesis

noun
universal creation
Pronunciation: / pænˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / panˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the origin or creation of all things in the universe; universal generation, especially in philosophical or archaic contexts

Examples

The pangenesis of life
Ancient philosophers debated the concept of pangenesis as the source of all life.
The myth describes a moment of pangenesis when the universe first came into being.
In his writings, he explored pangenesis as a way to explain the diversity of nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creation - the act of bringing something into existence
  • synonym: origin - the point or place where something begins
