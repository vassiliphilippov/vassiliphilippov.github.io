Synonyms

  • trembling
    2.6 0.01/m
    trembling is an unsteady, often uncontrollable shaking from fear, cold, or weakness, while shivering is a slight shaking from cold or excitement.
  • shivery
    4.2 0.01/m

  • quivery
    5.0 0.02/m
