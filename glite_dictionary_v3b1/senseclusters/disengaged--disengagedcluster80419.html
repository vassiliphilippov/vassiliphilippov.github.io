disengaged

adjective
not involved
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒd / (BrE)
not involved, interested, or connected with something or someone

Examples

Disengaged students
During the meeting, he appeared disengaged and rarely contributed to the discussion.
Many students become disengaged when the lesson does not interest them.
Her disengaged attitude made it difficult for her to connect with her coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detached - not emotionally involved or connected
  • antonym: engaged - actively interested or involved
  • antonym: involved - actively participating or connected
