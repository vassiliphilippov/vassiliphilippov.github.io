Examples

Strobe light flashes
Safety drills used a strobe light to alert workers of an emergency evacuation.
The club used a strobe light to enhance the atmosphere on the dance floor.
When the strobe light flashes, the spinning fan appears to stop moving.

Synonyms

  • strobe
    6.3 1.38/m
    strobe makes rapid, regular flashes used often in clubs, shows, or labs, while strobe light is the common name for that device.
  • stroboscope
    9.8 0.01/m
    stroboscope is a precise scientific instrument that times flashes to study motion, while strobe light is the general, non-technical flashing lamp.
