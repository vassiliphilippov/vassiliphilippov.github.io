subproject

noun
part of larger project
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a smaller project that is part of a larger overall project

Examples

A subproject plan
Each subproject must be completed before the main project can finish.
The team divided the work into several subprojects to make it more manageable.
She was responsible for leading a subproject focused on software development.
