motivator

noun
something or someone that motivates
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.tɪˌveɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tɪ.veɪ.tə / (BrE)
A person or thing that provides a reason or incentive for someone to act or achieve something.

Examples

A strong motivator
A good leader is often a strong motivator for their team.
Her success served as a motivator for others to pursue their dreams.
Money can be a powerful motivator for employees to work harder.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demotivator - something that reduces motivation
  • synonym: incentive - something that encourages someone to do something
