fibrillar

adjective
fiber-like structure
Pronunciation: / faɪˈbrɪlər / (AmE), / fɪˈbrɪlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling small fibers, especially in biological tissue

Examples

Fibrillar tissue pattern
The scientist observed a fibrillar pattern in the tissue sample under the microscope.
Certain diseases can cause fibrillar changes in muscle fibers.
The fibrillar appearance of the material suggested it was composed of many tiny threads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrous - containing, consisting of, or resembling fibers
