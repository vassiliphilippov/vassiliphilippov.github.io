burse

noun
old word for purse
Pronunciation: / bɜrs / (AmE), / bɜːs / (BrE)
an old word for a purse or a flat case used for holding money or documents

Examples

A leather burse
He placed the coins carefully into his burse before leaving the shop.
The merchant kept important documents in a leather burse at his side.
In olden times, a burse was a common accessory for travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: purse - a small bag for carrying money
  • synonym: wallet - a flat case for carrying money and cards
