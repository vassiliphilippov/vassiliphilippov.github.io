too soppy

adjective
overly sentimental or emotional
2.7 7.5
Pronunciation: / tu ˈsɑpi / (AmE), / tu ˈsɒpi / (BrE)
Excessively sentimental or emotional, often to the point of being cloying or overly sweet.

Examples

A too soppy story
I found the ending too soppy and predictable to enjoy.
Some people love romantic comedies, but others find them too soppy and cliché.
The novel was too soppy for readers who prefer realistic stories.
