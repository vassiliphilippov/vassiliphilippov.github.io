GDS

noun
gross domestic savings
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒiː diː ɛs / (AmE), / dʒiː diː ɛs / (BrE)
the total amount of national savings in a country during a specific period, usually measured annually

Examples

National GDS
The government reported that GDS increased by 5 percent compared to the previous year.
Economists analyze GDS to understand a country's ability to invest in future growth.
A decline in GDS can signal potential economic challenges for a nation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: GDP - gross domestic product, the total value of goods and services produced in a country
  • synonym: national savings - the total savings of a nation from households, businesses, and government
