frozen

adjective
preserved by freezing; not thawed
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzən / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən / (BrE)
preserved or stored at a very low temperature, especially food

Examples

Frozen vegetables
Make sure the frozen berries are thawed before adding them to the batter.
She put the frozen chicken in the microwave to defrost it quickly.
The vegetables were still frozen when I took them out of the freezer.
After the harvest, the berries are quickly frozen to lock in nutrients.
She always keeps a variety of frozen meals in her freezer for busy nights.
The vegetables were frozen to keep them fresh until ready for use.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: chilled - food preserved or served at a low temperature above freezing
  • antonym: fresh - food not preserved by freezing or any process
  • antonym: thawed - having been warmed to no longer be frozen
