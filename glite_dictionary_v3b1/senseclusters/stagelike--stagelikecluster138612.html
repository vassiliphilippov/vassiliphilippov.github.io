stagelike

adjective
in distinct phases
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪdʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsteɪdʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)
consisting of or occurring in clearly defined stages or phases, especially in structure or development

Examples

Stagelike development
The child's development was stagelike, with each phase building on the previous one.
The training program uses a stagelike approach, introducing new skills at each level.
Some theories suggest that learning is not continuous but rather stagelike in nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gradual - happening slowly over time, in steps
  • synonym: stepwise - proceeding in a series of steps or stages
