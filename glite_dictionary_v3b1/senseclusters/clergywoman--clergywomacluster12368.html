clergywoman

noun
female member of the clergy
Pronunciation: / ˈklɜːrdʒiˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈklɜːdʒiwʊmən / (BrE)
A female member of the clergy who acts as a spiritual leader in the Christian Church.

Examples

A clergywoman leads service
As a clergywoman, she supports various charitable organizations in her parish.
Many communities have welcomed their first clergywoman in recent years.
The clergywoman delivered a thought-provoking sermon on Sunday morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clergyman - a male member of the clergy
  • synonym: priest - an ordained minister of the church
