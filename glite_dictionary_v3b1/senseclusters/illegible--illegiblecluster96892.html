illegible

adjective
impossible or difficult to read (writing/print)
5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛdʒəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛdʒɪbəl / (BrE)
(of writing or print) impossible or very difficult to read because of poor handwriting or printing.

Examples

Illegible handwriting
Her notes were illegible after getting wet in the rain.
The doctor's handwriting was so illegible that the pharmacist had to call for clarification.
The text on the old sign had faded and become completely illegible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: legible - clear enough to be read
