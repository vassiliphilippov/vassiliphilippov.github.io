Vireo olivaceous

noun
a North American songbird; red-eyed vireo
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪrioʊ ˌoʊlɪˈveɪsiəs / (AmE), / ˌvɪərɪəʊ ˌɒlɪˈveɪsiəs / (BrE)
A North American songbird with red irises, an olive-grey back, and white underparts, commonly known as the red-eyed vireo.

Many birdwatchers hope to spot a Vireo olivaceous during spring migration.
