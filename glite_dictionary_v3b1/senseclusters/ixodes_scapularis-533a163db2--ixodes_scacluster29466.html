Ixodes scapularis

noun
species of tick, Lyme disease vector
9.8
Pronunciation: / aɪˈksoʊdiːz skæpˈjʊlərɪs / (AmE), / ɪkˈsəʊdiːz skæpˈjʊlərɪs / (BrE)
