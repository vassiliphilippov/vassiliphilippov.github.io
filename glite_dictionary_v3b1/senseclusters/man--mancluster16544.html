man

noun
a member of the armed forces
Pronunciation: / mæn / (AmE), / mæn / (BrE)
A person, especially a male, who serves in the armed forces; a member of a military force.

Examples

Army man
During the war, thousands of young men enlisted to defend their country.
Every man in the battalion was ready for inspection.
He served as a man in the navy for over ten years.
