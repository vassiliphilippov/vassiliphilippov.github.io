unflaggingly

adverb
persistently, tirelessly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈflæɡɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that does not weaken, tire, or lose enthusiasm; persistently and with constant effort

Examples

Work unflaggingly hard
The volunteers worked unflaggingly to help the community after the storm.
She pursued her goals unflaggingly, never letting setbacks slow her down.
Despite many challenges, he remained unflaggingly optimistic about the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lazily - in a way that shows little effort or energy
  • synonym: persistently - in a way that continues firmly or obstinately
  • synonym: tirelessly - in a way that shows no sign of becoming tired
