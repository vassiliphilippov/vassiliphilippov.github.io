Azadirachta indica

noun
the neem tree, a medicinal tree species
Pronunciation: / ˌæzə.dɪˈræktə ˈɪndɪkə / (AmE), / ˌazədɪˈraktə ˈɪndɪkə / (BrE)
a large semi-evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent, known as the neem tree; valued for its medicinal bark, aromatic seed oil, and gum.

The seeds of Azadirachta indica are processed to obtain neem oil, which is used in cosmetics and traditional medicine.
