body-surf

verb
ride a wave without a board
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi ˌsɝf / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi ˌsɜːf / (BrE)
to ride on the crest of a wave without using a surfboard, propelled only by one's own body.

Examples

To body-surf a wave
Do you know how to body-surf safely at the beach?
He loves to body-surf when the waves are high in the summer.
Many children enjoy body-surfing near the shore.
