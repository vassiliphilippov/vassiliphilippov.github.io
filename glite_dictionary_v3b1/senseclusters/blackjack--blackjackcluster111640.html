blackjack

noun
pirate flag with skull and crossbones
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌdʒæk / (AmE), / ˈblakˌdʒak / (BrE)
a flag, typically with a white skull and crossbones on a black background, used to indicate a pirate ship

Examples

Pirate blackjack flag
A notorious pirate ship raised its blackjack as it approached the merchant vessel.
Historians believe the blackjack was designed to intimidate and warn other ships.
The sight of the blackjack struck fear into the hearts of sailors on the high seas.
