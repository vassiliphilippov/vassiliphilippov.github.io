giant

noun
large and powerful company or organization
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt / (BrE)
an organization or company that is unusually large and powerful, especially in a particular area of business

Examples

A business giant
Several media giants are competing for the rights to the championship.
She dreamed of working for a giant in the automotive industry.
The tech giant continues to expand its influence in global markets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: behemoth - a very large and powerful organization or entity
