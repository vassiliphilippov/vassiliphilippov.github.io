Izanami

proper noun
Japanese goddess; creator with Izanagi
9.9
Pronunciation: / iːˌzɑːˈnɑːmi / (AmE), / iːˌzɑːˈnɑːmi / (BrE)
In Japanese mythology, the goddess who, with her brother and consort Izanagi, created the islands and gods of Japan.

Examples

Izanami goddess

The tragic death of Izanami led to her descent into the underworld.
