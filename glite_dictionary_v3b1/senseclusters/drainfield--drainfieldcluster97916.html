drainfield

noun
septic system soil area
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪnˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnˌfiːld / (BrE)
an area of soil where wastewater from a septic tank is filtered and absorbed into the ground

Examples

A drainfield area
The plumber inspected the drainfield to ensure it was working properly.
A clogged drainfield can cause wastewater to back up into the house.
Regular maintenance of your drainfield helps prevent costly repairs to your septic system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leach field - an area where septic tank liquid is distributed and filtered
  • synonym: septic tank - an underground tank for sewage treatment
