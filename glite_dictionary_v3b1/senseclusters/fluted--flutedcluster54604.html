fluted

adjective
with grooves or ridges
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈfluːtɪd / (BrE)
having long, rounded grooves or ridges along the surface, especially as a decorative feature

Examples

Fluted vase
The vase had a beautiful fluted design that caught the light from every angle.
Many classical buildings feature fluted columns as part of their architecture.
She chose a fluted glass for her dessert to make it look more elegant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grooved - having narrow cuts or channels on the surface
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface without grooves or ridges
