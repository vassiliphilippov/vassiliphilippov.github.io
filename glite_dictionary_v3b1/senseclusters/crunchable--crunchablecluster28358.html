crunchable

adjective
suitable for analysis
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌntʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrʌntʃəbəl / (BrE)
suitable for being processed or analyzed by computers, especially in terms of numerical data

Examples

Crunchable data
The survey results were finally in a crunchable format for the data analysts.
Only crunchable information can be used in the automated reporting system.
They spent hours cleaning the data to make it crunchable by the software.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyzable - able to be examined or studied in detail
  • synonym: processable - able to be processed or handled by a computer
  • antonym: unmanageable - difficult or impossible to handle or process
