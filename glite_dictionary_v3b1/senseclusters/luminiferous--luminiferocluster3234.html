luminiferous

adjective
producing light
Pronunciation: / ˌluːmɪˈnɪfərəs / (AmE), / ˌluːmɪˈnɪfərəs / (BrE)
producing or transmitting light, especially used in scientific or technical contexts

Examples

Luminiferous ether
The scientist discussed the properties of the luminiferous ether in his lecture.
Early theories suggested that light traveled through a luminiferous medium in space.
The term luminiferous is now mostly found in historical scientific texts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dark - having little or no light
  • synonym: luminous - emitting or reflecting light
