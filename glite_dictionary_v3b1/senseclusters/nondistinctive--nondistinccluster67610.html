nondistinctive

adjective
lacking unique features
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈstɪŋktɪv / (BrE)
not having qualities that make something different or easy to recognize; lacking distinctive features

Examples

Nondistinctive logo design
The nondistinctive logo failed to attract much attention from customers.
Her handwriting is so nondistinctive that it is hard to tell it apart from others.
Many modern office buildings have a nondistinctive appearance and look very similar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distinctive - having a quality that makes something different and easy to recognize
  • synonym: ordinary - not special or different in any way
  • antonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
