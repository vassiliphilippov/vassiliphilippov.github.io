stoolpigeon

noun
police informer or decoy
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːlˌpɪdʒən / (AmE), / ˈstʃuːlˌpɪdʒən / (BrE)
a person who acts as an informer or decoy for the police

Examples

A stoolpigeon informer
He refused to become a stoolpigeon, fearing what the others might do if they found out.
Many classic crime novels feature a stoolpigeon working undercover to help the authorities.
The criminal gang suspected they had a stoolpigeon among their members when the police showed up suddenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informer - a person who provides information about criminal activity to the police
  • antonym: police officer - a member of a police force
  • synonym: snitch - a person who tells the authorities about someone else's wrongdoing
