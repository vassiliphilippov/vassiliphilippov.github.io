practicer

noun
professional worker
Pronunciation: / ˈpræktɪsər / (AmE), / ˈpræktɪsə / (BrE)
a person who carries out a profession or occupation, especially in fields such as medicine or law

Examples

A medical practicer
The rural clinic relied on a single practicer who handled most patients and minor emergencies.
As a dedicated practicer of law, she served her community for over twenty years.
Every practicer must keep up with the latest developments in their field to provide the best service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practitioner - a person actively engaged in a profession, especially medicine or law
