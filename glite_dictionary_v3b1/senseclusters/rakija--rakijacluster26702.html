rakija

noun
Balkan fruit brandy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkiˌjɑː / (AmE), / ˈrækiˌjɑː / (BrE)
a strong distilled alcoholic drink from the Balkans, usually made from fermented fruit such as plums or grapes

Examples

A glass of rakija
At the village festival, everyone was offered a glass of homemade rakija.
He brought a bottle of rakija back from his trip to Serbia.
Drinking rakija is a traditional way to welcome guests in many Balkan countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brandy - a strong alcoholic spirit distilled from wine or fermented fruit juice
  • antonym: vodka - a clear distilled alcoholic beverage from Eastern Europe
