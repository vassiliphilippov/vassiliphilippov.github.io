thermonuclear warhead

noun
warhead delivering a thermonuclear bomb
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌθɝr.moʊˌnu.kli.ɚ ˈwɔrˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˌθɜː.məʊˌnjuː.kli.ə ˈwɔːˌhɛd / (BrE)
