polynucleotide

noun
polymer of nucleotides in nucleic acids
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊliˈnuːkliəˌtaɪd / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈnjuːkliətaɪd / (BrE)
a linear polymer consisting of many nucleotide units, forming part of a nucleic acid molecule

Examples

A polynucleotide chain

Each polynucleotide strand is made up of a specific order of nucleotide bases.
The scientist analyzed the sequence of the polynucleotide to understand genetic mutations.
