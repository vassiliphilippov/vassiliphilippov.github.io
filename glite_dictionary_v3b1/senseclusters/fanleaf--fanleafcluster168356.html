fanleaf

noun
fan-shaped leaf or disease
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈfænˌliːf / (BrE)
a plant leaf shaped like a fan, or a disease that causes leaves to develop this shape, especially in grapevines

Examples

Fanleaf disease
The grapevine showed signs of fanleaf after the virus spread.
Farmers worry that fanleaf could reduce the quality of their grape harvest.
The botanist identified the unusual fanleaf pattern on several plants in the vineyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grapevine fanleaf virus - a virus that causes fan-shaped leaves in grapevines
  • synonym: leaf curl - a plant disease causing leaves to curl or deform
