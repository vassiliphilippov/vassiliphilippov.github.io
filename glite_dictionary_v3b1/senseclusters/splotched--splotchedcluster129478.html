splotched

adjective
marked with irregular spots or blots
Pronunciation: / splɑtʃt / (AmE), / splɒtʃt / (BrE)
marked with irregularly shaped spots or blots, usually in a random pattern

Examples

Splotched jeans
Her jeans were splotched with mud after the hike through the wet forest.
Sunlight filtered through the window, falling on the splotched fur of the calico cat.
The old map was splotched with ink stains and coffee rings.
