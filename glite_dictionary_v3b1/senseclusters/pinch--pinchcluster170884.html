pinch

noun
two-finger zoom gesture
3.7
Pronunciation: /pɪntʃ/ (AmE), /pɪntʃ/ (BrE)
a two-finger gesture on a touchscreen in which fingers are moved together or apart to zoom content in or out.

Examples

A pinch gesture
A quick pinch on the photo will zoom in so you can read the small text.
During the demo he showed a two-finger pinch that zoomed smoothly into the map.
If the app ignores the pinch, try cleaning the screen or restarting your device.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zoom - an action that changes the magnification of an image or view on a screen
