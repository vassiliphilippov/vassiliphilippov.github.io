denture adhesive

noun
substance for securing dentures
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛn.tʃɚ ædˈhi.sɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɛn.tʃə ədˈhiː.sɪv / (BrE)
A product applied to dentures to help keep them securely in place in the mouth.

Examples

Denture adhesive cream
He picked up a tube of denture adhesive at the pharmacy before heading home.
My grandmother uses denture adhesive so her dentures stay firmly in place all day.
Without denture adhesive, some people find their dentures move when they eat or speak.
