touch

verb
handle or interfere with
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ / (AmE), / tʌtʃ / (BrE)
to handle, move, or interfere with something, especially without permission

Examples

To touch my things
He warned his little brother not to touch his model airplane.
If you touch that vase, it might break.
Please don't touch anything on my desk while I'm away.
He was so upset that he wouldn't touch his dinner.
I haven't touched a cigarette in ten years.
She rarely touches alcohol these days.

  • synonym: eat - to put food into the mouth, chew, and swallow it
  • antonym: leave alone - to not touch or interfere with something
  • synonym: tamper - to interfere with something in an unauthorized way
