slopeside

noun
ski slope accommodation
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊpˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsləʊpˌsaɪd / (BrE)
a building or accommodation located directly beside a ski slope, offering immediate access to skiing

Examples

A slopeside condo
We booked a slopeside condo so we could ski right from our door each morning.
The hotel’s slopeside location made it easy for guests to enjoy the mountain all day.
Families love staying in slopeside lodges for the convenience and beautiful views.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offsite - located away from the main site or area
  • synonym: ski-in ski-out - accommodation with direct access to ski slopes
