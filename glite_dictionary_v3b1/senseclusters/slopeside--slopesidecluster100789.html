slopeside

noun
ski slope accommodation
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsloʊp.saɪd/ (AmE), /ˈsləʊp.saɪd/ (BrE)
a building or accommodation located directly beside a ski slope, offering immediate access to skiing

Examples

Families love staying in slopeside lodges for the convenience and beautiful views.
