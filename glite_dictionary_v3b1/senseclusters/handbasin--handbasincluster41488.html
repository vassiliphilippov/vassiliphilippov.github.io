handbasin

noun
basin for washing hands
5.2 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbeɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
A basin fixed to a wall or set into a surface, used for washing the hands.
