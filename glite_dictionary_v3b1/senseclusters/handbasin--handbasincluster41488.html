handbasin

noun
basin for washing hands
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌbeɪsɪn / (AmE), / ˈhændˌbeɪsɪn / (BrE)
A basin fixed to a wall or set into a surface, used for washing the hands.

Examples

A handbasin in bathroom
He dropped the soap in the handbasin while washing up.
Please wash your hands in the handbasin before dinner.
The bathroom was small but had a modern handbasin and mirror.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sink - a fixed basin with a water supply and drain
  • antonym: toilet - a device for disposing of human waste; opposite in bathroom context
  • synonym: washbasin - a basin for washing, usually in a bathroom
