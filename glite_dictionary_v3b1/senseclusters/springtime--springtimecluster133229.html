springtime

noun
the season of spring
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋˌtaɪm / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋtaɪm / (BrE)
The season of spring; the period of the year when plants begin to grow and the weather becomes warmer after winter.

Examples

The springtime season
After months of snow, the arrival of springtime brought fresh green leaves and blooming flowers.
Every year, the city holds a flower festival in springtime to celebrate the changing season.
Many animals have their young during springtime when food is plentiful and the weather is mild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spring - the season between winter and summer in which vegetation begins to appear
  • antonym: summer - the warmest season of the year, following spring
  • antonym: winter - the coldest season of the year, preceding spring
