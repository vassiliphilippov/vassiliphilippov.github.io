nonpolarizing

adjective
not causing division
7.0
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpoʊləˌraɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈpəʊləˌraɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing division into opposing groups or not creating electrical polarity

Examples

Nonpolarizing statement
The committee aimed to draft a nonpolarizing statement that everyone could agree on.
Her nonpolarizing approach helped bring together people with very different opinions.
Engineers developed a nonpolarizing material for use in sensitive electronic devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral - not supporting any side in a conflict or disagreement
  • antonym: polarizing - causing people to divide into opposing groups
