alpenglow

noun
rosy mountain light
Pronunciation: / ˈælpənˌɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈalpənˌɡləʊ / (BrE)
a reddish or pinkish light seen on mountains just after sunset or just before sunrise, when the sun is below the horizon

Examples

Pink alpenglow on mountains
The hikers paused to admire the pink alpenglow lighting up the mountain peaks.
Photographers often wait for alpenglow to capture the most beautiful images of the Alps.
As dawn approached, a gentle alpenglow appeared on the snowy ridges.
The snow-capped peaks glowed with a beautiful alpenglow as the sun disappeared behind the horizon.
Photographers waited patiently to capture the fleeting alpenglow on the mountain ridge.
Early risers were rewarded with a stunning alpenglow before the day began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afterglow - the light remaining in the sky after the sun has set
  • synonym: twilight - the soft light in the sky when the sun is below the horizon
