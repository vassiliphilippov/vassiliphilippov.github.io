ribboning

noun
cutting into strips
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪbənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪbənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cutting or slicing something into long, thin strips that resemble ribbons

Examples

Ribboning carrots
The chef demonstrated ribboning the carrots to create delicate strips for the garnish.
Proper ribboning of the zucchini gives the salad a beautiful texture and appearance.
She used a special tool for ribboning the fabric before adding it to her craft project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dicing - the act of cutting into small cubes
  • synonym: shredding - the act of cutting into thin pieces or strips
