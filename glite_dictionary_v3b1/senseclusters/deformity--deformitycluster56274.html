deformity

noun
misshapen or abnormal bodily appearance
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɔrməti / (AmE), / dɪˈfɔːməti /; / dɪˈfɔːmɪti / (BrE)
a condition in which a part of the body is misshapen or abnormal, often considered undesirable or caused by birth, injury, or disease

Examples

Birth deformities
Many birth deformities can be corrected with surgery today.
She became self-conscious about her facial deformity as she grew older.
The accident left him with a noticeable deformity on his hand.
Advances in medicine have made it possible to correct certain facial deformities.
The deformity of his hand did not prevent him from becoming a skilled pianist.
The child was born with a deformity of the spine that required surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disfigurement - the action or result of spoiling the appearance of something or someone
  • synonym: malformation - an abnormal formation or structure, especially in the body
  • antonym: normality - the state of being normal, especially in shape or structure
