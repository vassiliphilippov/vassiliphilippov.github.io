scallion

noun
young onion with long green leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈskæliən / (AmE), / ˈskælɪən / (BrE)
a long, thin young onion with a small white base and green leaves, used in cooking for its mild flavor

Examples

Fresh scallions sliced
A stir-fry often tastes better with a handful of sliced scallions.
She sprinkled chopped scallion over the soup for a fresh flavor.
The salad recipe calls for one finely diced scallion.
Chopped scallion adds a fresh, mild onion flavor to salads and soups.
He sprinkled finely sliced scallions over the noodles for extra color and taste.
You can substitute scallion for chives in this recipe if needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: green onion - young onion with a long green stalk, used in salads and garnish
  • synonym: spring onion - another term for scallion, especially in British English
