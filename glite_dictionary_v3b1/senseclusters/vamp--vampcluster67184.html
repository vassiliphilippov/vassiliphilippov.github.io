vamp

noun
repeated improvised musical accompaniment
6.4
Pronunciation: / væmp / (AmE), / væmp / (BrE)
a short, simple musical phrase or accompaniment that is repeated and often improvised, typically to fill time or provide a backing for a soloist or singer

Examples

A jazz vamp
During the jazz session, the band improvised a bluesy vamp to keep the energy up.
He asked the guitarist to vamp until the rest of the band was ready.
The pianist played a vamp while the singer found her place in the melody.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riff - a short repeated phrase in popular music and jazz, typically used as an accompaniment or introduction
