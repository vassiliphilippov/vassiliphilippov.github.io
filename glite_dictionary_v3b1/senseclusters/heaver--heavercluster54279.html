heaver

noun
tool used as a lever or for twisting rope
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːvər / (AmE), / ˈhiːvə / (BrE)
a bar or tool used as a lever, especially for twisting rope or heavy lifting

Examples

Metal heaver tool
A wooden heaver can help twist wire when building fences.
The sailor used a heaver to tighten the rope around the mast.
With a sturdy heaver, the workers managed to move the heavy crate into place.
