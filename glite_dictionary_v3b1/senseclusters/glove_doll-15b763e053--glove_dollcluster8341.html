glove doll

noun
puppet worn on the hand

A puppet with a soft cloth body and a hollow head, designed to be worn over the hand like a glove and operated by moving the fingers inside.

Examples

A glove doll puppet
He slipped his hand into the glove doll and brought the character to life.
She made a colorful glove doll for the puppet show at school.
The children laughed as the glove doll waved its arms and told a story.

Synonyms

  • hand puppet
    2.5 0.15/m
    hand puppet covers any puppet worn on the hand and used by one person, while glove doll names the soft cloth, glove-style version.
