periglacial

adjective
cold-climate landforms
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛriˈɡleɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌpɛrɪˈɡleɪsɪəl /; / ˌpɛriˈɡleɪsiəl / (BrE)
relating to cold regions near glaciers, especially where freezing and thawing shape the land
