white rust

noun
plant disease with white powdery fungus
5.1
Pronunciation: / waɪt rʌst / (AmE), / waɪt rʌst / (BrE)
a plant disease caused by certain fungi, characterized by a white powdery mass that appears on affected plant parts

Examples

White rust disease
Applying fungicide early can help prevent white rust from damaging your garden.
Farmers noticed white rust spreading quickly across the leaves of their crop.
The spinach plants were lost to white rust before the harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downy mildew - another fungal disease affecting plants
