friar's-cowl

noun
Mediterranean plant with cowl-shaped spathe
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfraɪərz ˌkaʊl / (AmE), / ˈfraɪəz ˌkaʊl / (BrE)
A tuberous perennial plant with a cowl-shaped maroon or violet-black spathe, native to the Mediterranean, Canary Islands, and Azores.
