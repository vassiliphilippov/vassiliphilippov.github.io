bijou

noun
small, delicately crafted item
8.4
Pronunciation: / biˈʒu / (AmE), / ˈbiːʒuː / (BrE)
A small, delicately crafted item, especially a piece of jewelry or ornament.

Examples

A bijou jewel
Each bijou was handmade by local artisans and detailed with tiny gemstones.
She wore a bijou around her neck that sparkled in the sunlight.
The shop displayed a collection of bijoux in a glass case.
