bamboula

noun
Caribbean drum or dance
9.9
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuːlə / (AmE), / bæmˈbuːlə / (BrE)
a type of drum or lively dance of African origin, especially found in the Caribbean

Examples

A bamboula drum
The musicians played the bamboula during the festival, filling the air with rhythmic beats.
Dancers moved energetically to the sound of the bamboula at the Caribbean celebration.
He learned to play the bamboula as part of his cultural heritage.
