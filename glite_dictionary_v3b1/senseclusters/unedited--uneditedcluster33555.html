unedited

adjective
not modified by editing
2.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɛdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɛdɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a text, recording, or similar item) not altered, corrected, or modified by editing.

Examples

Unedited photos

The publisher accidentally released an unedited version of the manuscript.
Viewers were shocked by the unedited footage shown on live television.
