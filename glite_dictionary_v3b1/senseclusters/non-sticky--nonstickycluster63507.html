non-sticky

adjective
not adhesive
2.8
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈstɪki / (AmE), / nɒnˈstɪki / (BrE)
not tending to adhere to surfaces; not sticky

Examples

A non-sticky surface
The non-sticky coating on the pan made it easy to clean after cooking.
She prefers non-sticky notes because they do not leave residue on her desk.
The glue was advertised as non-sticky once dry, making it safe for children.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sticky - tending to adhere to surfaces; having a glue-like quality
