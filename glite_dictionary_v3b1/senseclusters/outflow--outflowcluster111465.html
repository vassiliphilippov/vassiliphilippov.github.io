outflow

noun
natural movement of water flowing outward

Synonyms

  • outflux
    7.5 0.00/m
    outflux is a general term for something moving outward, while outflow describes the movement from a place, system, or container.
  • efflux
    9.9 0.22/m
    efflux is a technical word for substances moving out, while outflow is the common term for movement out of a place or system.
  • effluence
    9.3 0.04/m
    effluence is often used for liquids flowing out from a source, while outflow covers any movement of something outward.
