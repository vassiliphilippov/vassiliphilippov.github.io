skip the gym

verb
miss a gym workout
Pronunciation: / skɪp ðə dʒɪm / (AmE), / skɪp ðə dʒɪm / (BrE)
to intentionally miss or decide not to attend a planned workout session at a gym

Examples

To skip the gym
He felt guilty after he skipped the gym for the third time that week.
I decided to skip the gym last night and watch a movie instead.
Sometimes it's okay to skip the gym if you're feeling unwell.
