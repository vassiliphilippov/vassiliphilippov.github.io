centripetal

adjective
tending to unify or bring together
6.5
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtrɪpɪtəl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtrɪpɪtəl / (BrE)
tending to unify or bring things together toward a central point or common goal

Examples

Centripetal force in society
Cultural symbols can act as a centripetal force within a community.
The leader's speech had a strong centripetal effect on the divided group.
The new policy aims to be centripetal by uniting various regional teams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centrifugal - moving away from a center or tending to separate
