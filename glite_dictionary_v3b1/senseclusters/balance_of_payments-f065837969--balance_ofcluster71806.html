balance of payments

noun
record of a country's foreign transactions
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbæl.əns əv ˈpeɪ.mənts/ (AmE), /ˈbæl.əns əv ˈpeɪ.mənts/ (BrE)
the record of all monetary transactions made between one country and the rest of the world over a specific period, usually a year

Examples

The balance of payments record
A persistent deficit in the balance of payments can affect a nation's currency value.
Economists analyze the balance of payments to assess a nation's economic interactions with the world.
The country improved its balance of payments through increased exports and reduced imports.

  • synonym: trade balance - the difference between a country's exports and imports of goods
