flopper

noun
player who fakes falls
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈflɒpə / (BrE)
a player in sports who pretends to fall or be fouled in order to gain an advantage

Examples

Soccer flopper
The coach was frustrated with the flopper who kept trying to trick the referee.
Fans booed the flopper after he exaggerated the contact and fell to the ground.
In basketball, a known flopper may not always get the referee's sympathy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheater - a person who acts dishonestly to gain an advantage
  • synonym: diver - a player who pretends to be fouled, especially in soccer
  • antonym: sportsman - a person who plays fair and follows the rules in sports
