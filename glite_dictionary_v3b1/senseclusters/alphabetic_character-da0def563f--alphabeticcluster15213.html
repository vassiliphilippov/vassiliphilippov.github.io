alphabetic character

noun
letter of the alphabet
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.fəˈbɛ.tɪk ˈkɛr.ək.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌæl.fəˈbɛt.ɪk ˈkæ.rək.tə / (BrE)
A letter or symbol used as one of the basic written elements in an alphabet to represent sounds of speech.
