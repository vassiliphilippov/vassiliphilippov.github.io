Synonyms

  • take part
    1.4 13/m
    take part is what people say when they join an activity with others, while participate sounds slightly more formal.
  • join in
    1.1 9/m
    join in is used for jumping into an activity already happening, while participate is more neutral and fits many settings.
  • engage
    3.4 80/m
    engage is more formal and often means taking an active role in something, while participate is common everyday language.
  • partake
    5.5 2/m
    partake often means sharing or joining in something like food or pleasure, while participate focuses on joining activities with others.
