wiffle

verb
to make swishing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪfəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪfəl / (BrE)
to make a soft, airy swishing sound as something moves quickly through the air

Examples

To wiffle softly
The shuttlecock would wiffle as it flew across the court.
You could hear the toy sword wiffle through the air during their play fight.
As the paper plane soared, it began to wiffle softly before landing.

  • synonym: whiff - to move with a slight sound or smell through the air
