beatbox

noun
vocal percussionist
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtbɑks / (AmE), / ˈbiːtbɒks / (BrE)
a person who creates drum beats, rhythms, and musical sounds using their mouth, lips, and voice

Examples

A skilled beatbox
The talented beatbox amazed the crowd with realistic drum sounds and rhythms.
At the talent show, a young beatbox performed alongside a rapper.
Many hip-hop groups include a beatbox to add unique rhythms to their music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beatboxer - a person skilled in making drum sounds with their mouth
  • synonym: vocalist - a person who sings or uses their voice musically
