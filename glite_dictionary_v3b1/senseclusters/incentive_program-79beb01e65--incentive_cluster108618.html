incentive program

noun
formal motivation scheme, esp. for employees
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛntɪv ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛntɪv ˈprəʊɡram / (BrE)
a formal scheme designed to motivate individuals, especially employees, to achieve specific goals or perform certain actions

Examples

The incentive program bonus
Her participation in the incentive program resulted in a significant year-end bonus.
Many organizations use an incentive program to reward employees for meeting targets.
The company introduced an incentive program to boost sales performance among its staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonus plan - a program offering extra pay for achieving targets
  • synonym: reward scheme - a plan that offers rewards for desired behaviors or achievements
