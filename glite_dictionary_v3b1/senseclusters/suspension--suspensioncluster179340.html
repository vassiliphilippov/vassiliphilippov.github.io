suspension

noun
vehicle system of springs and shock absorbers
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛnʃən / (AmE), / səˈspɛnʃən / (BrE)
the system of springs, shock absorbers, and other devices that connects a vehicle's wheels to its chassis, allowing for a smoother ride and better handling

Examples

Car suspension system
Mechanics spent hours working on the truck's suspension system to ensure a smooth ride.
The car's suspension was damaged after driving over a deep pothole.
Upgrading the suspension can improve both comfort and handling on rough roads.
