compose

verb
to form or make up (something)
1.8
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpoʊz / (AmE), / kəmˈpəʊz / (BrE)
to form or constitute; to make up the parts or elements of (something)

Examples

Values compose a team
Honesty and compassion compose the foundation of his character.
Proteins and water compose the majority of a cell's structure.
Several smaller streams compose the main river that flows through the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comprise - to consist of; to be made up of
  • synonym: constitute - to be a part of a whole; to make up
