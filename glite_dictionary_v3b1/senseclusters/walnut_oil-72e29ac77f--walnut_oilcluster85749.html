walnut oil

noun
oil made from walnuts
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔlˌnʌt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlˌnʌt ɔɪl / (BrE)
Oil extracted from walnuts, often used in cooking and salad dressings.

Examples

Walnut oil for cooking
Chefs often use walnut oil as a finishing touch on roasted vegetables.
For a delicate flavor, drizzle a little walnut oil over your salad.
The recipe calls for both olive oil and walnut oil to enhance the taste.
