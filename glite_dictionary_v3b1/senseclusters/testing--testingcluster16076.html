testing

noun
the act of administering a test
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving someone a test, especially to assess their knowledge, ability, or progress

Examples

Standardized testing is often criticized for its impact on education quality.
The school has implemented regular testing to monitor students' progress throughout the year.
