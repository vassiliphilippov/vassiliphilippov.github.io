pouter

noun
someone who habitually looks sullen or pouts
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊtər / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtə / (BrE)
a person who often has a sullen or gloomy expression, especially due to habitually pouting

Examples

A gloomy pouter
Ever since losing the game, he's been a real pouter around the house.
She didn't want to be labeled a pouter, so she tried to hide her disappointment.
The teacher urged the pouter in the back row to join the class activity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smiler - a person who smiles habitually; the opposite character type
  • synonym: sulker - a person who sulks; someone given to moody silence
