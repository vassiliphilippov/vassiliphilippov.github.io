locavore

noun
local food eater
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkəˌvɔr / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəˌvɔː / (BrE)
a person who prefers to eat food that is grown or produced locally

Examples

A locavore eater
As a proud locavore, he buys vegetables only from nearby farms.
The restaurant attracts locavores by using ingredients sourced within a 50-mile radius.
Many locavores believe eating local food is better for the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foodie - a person with a strong interest in food
  • antonym: omnivore - an animal or person that eats both plants and animals
