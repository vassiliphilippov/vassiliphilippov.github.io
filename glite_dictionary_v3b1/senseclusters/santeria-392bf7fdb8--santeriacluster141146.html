Santeria

noun
Afro-Caribbean religion
9.7
Pronunciation: /sæn.ˈte.riː.ə/ /; / /sæn.ˈtɪə.riː.ə/ (AmE), /sæn.ˈteə.riː.ə/ /; / /sæn.ˈtɪə.riː.ə/ (BrE)
a religion that blends West African Yoruba traditions with Roman Catholicism and indigenous Caribbean beliefs, especially practiced in Cuba and the Caribbean
