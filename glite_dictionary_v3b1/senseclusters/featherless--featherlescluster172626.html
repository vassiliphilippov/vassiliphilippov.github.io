featherless

adjective
having no feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛðərləs / (AmE), / ˈfɛðələs / (BrE)
(of a bird or other creature) lacking feathers.

Examples

Featherless bird
Due to a rare genetic condition, the bird remained featherless throughout its life.
The freshly hatched chick was completely featherless and looked fragile.
The museum displayed a featherless specimen to show the bird's anatomy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: feathered - having feathers
