medkit

noun
video game health item
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdˌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdˌkɪt / (BrE)
an item in a video game that restores a character's health or heals injuries

Examples

Video game medkit
The player quickly grabbed a medkit to recover after taking damage from the enemy.
You should always keep a medkit in your inventory for emergencies during the mission.
After the boss fight, she used her last medkit to heal before moving on.
