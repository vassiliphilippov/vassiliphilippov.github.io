lap of honour

noun
victory lap around a track or stadium
4.2
Pronunciation: / læp əv ˈɑːnɚ / (AmE), / læp əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
A circuit of a track or stadium made by a victorious competitor or team to celebrate a win, usually while acknowledging the spectators.

Examples

A lap of honour
After winning gold, she waved to the crowd during her lap of honour.
The football team jogged around the pitch in a lap of honour to thank their fans.
The runner took a lap of honour after breaking the national record.
