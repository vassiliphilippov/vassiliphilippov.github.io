Trinil man

noun
early human fossil from Java
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtrɪ.nɪl mæn/ (AmE), /ˈtrɪ.nɪl mæn/ (BrE)
the fossil remains of an early human (Homo erectus) discovered in Java, Indonesia, originally named Pithecanthropus erectus

Examples

The discovery of Trinil man in 1891 marked a milestone in paleoanthropology.
