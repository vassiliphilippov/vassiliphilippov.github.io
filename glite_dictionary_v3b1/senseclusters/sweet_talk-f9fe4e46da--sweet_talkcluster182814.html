sweet talk

noun
flattering speech to gain favor
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /swiːt tɔːk/ (AmE), /swiːt tɔːk/ (BrE)
flattering or insincere speech used to persuade or influence someone

Examples

Sweet talk to persuade
He used a little sweet talk to convince them to donate to his cause.
No amount of sweet talk will get you out of doing your homework.
She tried to win the manager over with some sweet talk about his leadership skills.
Don't let his sweet talk fool you into trusting him.
He used a lot of sweet talk to persuade her to accept the offer.
She tried to win over the committee with her sweet talk, but they remained unconvinced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blandishment - a flattering or pleasing statement or action used to persuade
  • synonym: coaxing - gentle persuasion or urging
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym: flattery - excessive or insincere praise
