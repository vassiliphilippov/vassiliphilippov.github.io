upkeep

noun
maintenance; care to keep in good condition
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌkiːp / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkiːp / (BrE)
the process or activity of maintaining something in good working order, especially through regular care and repair

Examples

Property upkeep
He complained about the high cost of property upkeep every year.
Regular upkeep is crucial to ensure your car runs smoothly.
The upkeep of an old house can be both time-consuming and expensive.
