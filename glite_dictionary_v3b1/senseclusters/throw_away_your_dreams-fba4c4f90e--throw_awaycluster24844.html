throw away your dreams

verb
abandon one's aspirations
1.0
Pronunciation: / θroʊ əˈweɪ jʊr driːmz / (AmE), / θrəʊ əˈweɪ jɔː driːmz / (BrE)
to abandon or dismiss one's aspirations or hopes for the future

Examples

To throw away your dreams completely
Despite all the setbacks, she refused to throw away her dreams of becoming an artist.
He told his son never to throw away his dreams, even when others doubted him.
When life gets tough, it's easy to throw away your dreams, but persistence can lead to success.
