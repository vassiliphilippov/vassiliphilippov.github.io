conversionary

noun
religious persuader
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜːrʒəˌnɛri / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːʃənəri / (BrE)

Examples

A conversionary preacher

Many viewed the conversionary with suspicion, unsure of his intentions.
As a dedicated conversionary, she spent years working to spread her faith abroad.
