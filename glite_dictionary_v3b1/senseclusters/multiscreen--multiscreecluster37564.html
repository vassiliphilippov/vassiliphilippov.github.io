multiscreen

noun
combined screen display
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌskriːn / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌskriːn / (BrE)
a display system made up of several screens joined together to create one large visual surface

Examples

Multiscreen display wall
The conference room featured a multiscreen setup for presentations and video calls.
Engineers monitored the data on a massive multiscreen display in the operations center.
The art installation used a multiscreen to show synchronized videos from different angles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single screen - a display system with only one screen
  • synonym: video wall - a large display made by combining multiple screens
