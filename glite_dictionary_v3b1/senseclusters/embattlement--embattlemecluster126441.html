embattlement

noun
building defenses
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbætəlmənt / (AmE), / ɪmˈbætəlmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of building or strengthening defenses, especially in preparation for attack

Examples

Embattlement of defenses
The embattlement of the castle walls took several months to complete.
During the war, the city's rapid embattlement helped protect its citizens from invasion.
The general ordered the immediate embattlement of all strategic positions along the border.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attack - an aggressive action against a place or person
  • synonym: defense - the action of protecting from attack
  • synonym: fortification - the act of building military defenses
