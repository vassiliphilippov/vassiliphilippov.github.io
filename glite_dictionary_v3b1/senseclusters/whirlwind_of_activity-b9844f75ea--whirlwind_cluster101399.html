whirlwind of activity

noun
very busy, hectic period
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝlˌwɪnd əv ækˈtɪv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlˌwɪnd əv əkˈtɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
a period during which many things happen quickly and chaotically

Examples

A whirlwind of activity
After the announcement, the office became a whirlwind of activity as everyone prepared for the event.
Moving to a new city was a whirlwind of activity and excitement for the whole family.
The week before the wedding was truly a whirlwind of activity for the couple.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flurry of activity - a sudden and brief period of busy activity
  • synonym: hive of activity - a place where many people are busy doing many things
