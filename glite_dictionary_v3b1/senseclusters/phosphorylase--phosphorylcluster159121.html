phosphorylase

noun
enzyme adding phosphate
Pronunciation: / fɑsˈfɔːrəˌleɪs /; / fɑsˈfɔrəˌleɪs / (AmE), / fɒsˈfɒrɪleɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that adds a phosphate group to another molecule, especially to help break down complex substances in cells
