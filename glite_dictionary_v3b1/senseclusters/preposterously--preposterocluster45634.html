preposterously

adverb
in an extremely absurd or laughable way
Pronunciation: / priˈpɑstɚəsli / (AmE), / prɪˈpɒstərəsli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely silly, unreasonable, or absurd to the point of arousing laughter.

Examples

To be preposterously optimistic
He was preposterously optimistic about finishing the project in one day.
The house was preposterously large for just two people.
The price of the luxury handbag was preposterously high.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absurdly - in a ridiculously unreasonable manner
  • synonym: ridiculously - in a way that is extremely silly or unreasonable
  • antonym: sensibly - in a way that shows good sense or judgment
