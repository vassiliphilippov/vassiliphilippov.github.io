Synonyms

  • legal action
    3.8 2.60/m
    legal action is a formal process started in court to resolve a dispute, while litigation is the whole process of bringing and handling such cases.
  • legal proceeding
    4.8 0.69/m
    legal proceeding refers to the formal steps taken in court to decide a matter, while litigation is the broader process of suing and defending.
  • judicial proceeding
    6.3 0.33/m
    judicial proceeding is a court process led by a judge to decide rights, while litigation covers the whole path of taking and arguing a case.
  • proceeding
    7.9 5.68/m
    proceeding describes a series of court steps to resolve an issue, while litigation refers to the full legal process of taking and defending a case.
  • lawsuit
    4.8 30/m
    lawsuit is the formal claim brought by one party to court for a remedy, while litigation means the ongoing process of that legal fight.
  • suit
    5.6 21/m
    suit is a legal case one party starts in court to get a remedy, while litigation is the process of carrying out such suits.
  • action
    6.8 31/m
    action is a legal step where someone sues to protect a right in court, while litigation is the whole dispute process.
  • action at law
    7.9 0.03/m
    action at law is a formal old-fashioned term for a court suit brought to protect rights, while litigation is the general process of suing.
  • case
    3.1 213/m
