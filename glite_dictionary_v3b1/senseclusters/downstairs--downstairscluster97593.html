downstairs

adjective
on or of a lower floor
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈstɛrz / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈstɛəz / (BrE)
Situated on or relating to a lower floor of a building, especially the floor immediately below the one being considered.

Examples

Downstairs bedroom
The downstairs bathroom was recently renovated.
They have a downstairs office that opens onto the garden.
We put the guests in the downstairs bedroom so they wouldn't have to use the stairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upstairs - located on or relating to a higher floor of a building
