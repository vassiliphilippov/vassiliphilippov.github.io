gutbucket

adjective
raw, emotional music
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌtˌbʌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtˌbʌkɪt / (BrE)
describing music that is raw, unrefined, and full of strong emotion

Examples

Gutbucket blues sound

Fans love the gutbucket sound because it feels honest and full of energy.
Her gutbucket singing style set her apart from more polished performers.
