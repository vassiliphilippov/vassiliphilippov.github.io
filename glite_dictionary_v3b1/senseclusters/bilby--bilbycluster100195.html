bilby

noun
Australian marsupial with long ears
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪl.bi / (AmE), / ˈbɪl.bi / (BrE)
a small nocturnal marsupial with long ears and a pointed snout, native to Australia, also known as the rabbit-eared bandicoot.

Examples

The bilby animal
Conservation efforts are helping the endangered bilby return to the wild.
Easter in Australia is sometimes symbolized by the bilby instead of the rabbit.
The bilby is active at night, foraging for seeds and insects in the desert.
