vaunter

noun
boastful, talkative person
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɔːn.tə / (BrE)
a person who boasts excessively and talks about their achievements in a proud way

Examples

A vaunter boasts
Her reputation as a vaunter made others reluctant to share their own stories.
Instead of being humble, he acted as a vaunter at every gathering.
Though he achieved much, nobody liked the vaunter at the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boaster - someone who boasts; speaks with excessive pride
  • synonym: braggart - a person who brags a lot; boaster
  • antonym: modest person - a person who does not boast about achievements
