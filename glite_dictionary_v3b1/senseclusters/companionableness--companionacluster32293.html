companionableness

noun
quality of being a good companion
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənəblnəs / (AmE), / kəmˈpanjənəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being friendly and pleasant to be with; suitability for being a companion.

Examples

Companionableness of a person
Her companionableness made her popular among both colleagues and neighbors.
In the interview, he emphasized the companionableness of his team members.
The dog's companionableness was evident in the way it greeted every visitor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: companionability - quality of being companionable
  • synonym: friendliness - quality of being friendly or kind
  • antonym: unsociability - quality of being unsociable or not companionable
