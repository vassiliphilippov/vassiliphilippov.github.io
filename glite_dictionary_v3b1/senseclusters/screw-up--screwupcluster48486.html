screw-up

noun
careless mistake
6.1 0.98/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàskruÀê  åp / (AmE), / ÀàskruÀê  åp / (BrE)

Examples

A big screw-up

He apologized for his screw-up and promised to be more careful next time.
After the screw-up, the team worked together to fix the problem quickly.
