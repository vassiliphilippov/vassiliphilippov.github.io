screw-up

noun
careless mistake
Pronunciation: / ˈskruː ʌp / (AmE), / ˈskruː ʌp / (BrE)
a careless mistake or blunder, especially one that causes problems or embarrassment

Examples

A big screw-up
The project was delayed because of a major screw-up in the planning stage.
He apologized for his screw-up and promised to be more careful next time.
After the screw-up, the team worked together to fix the problem quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a serious or embarrassing mistake
  • synonym: error - a mistake or incorrect action
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a desired result
