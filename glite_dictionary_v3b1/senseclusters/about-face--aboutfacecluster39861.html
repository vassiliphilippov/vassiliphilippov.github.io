about-face

verb
to turn 180 degrees suddenly
Pronunciation: / əˈbaʊt ˌfeɪs / (AmE), / əˈbaʊt ˌfeɪs / (BrE)
to turn so as to face the opposite direction, especially by turning 180 degrees suddenly

Examples

To about-face quickly
At the command, the soldiers about-faced and marched back across the field.
During the drill, the officer shouted 'halt and about-face!'
He about-faced quickly when he realized he was walking in the wrong direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turn around - to change direction to face the opposite way
