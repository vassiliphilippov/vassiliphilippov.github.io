mixer

noun
kitchen utensil for mixing foods
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksər / (AmE), / ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil or appliance for mixing foods, especially by beating, stirring, or blending ingredients together

Examples

Kitchen mixer

She used an electric mixer to blend the cake batter smoothly.
The recipe called for beating the eggs with a mixer until they were fluffy.
