mammary

adjective
relating to the milk-producing gland
Pronunciation: / ˈmæm.ə.ri / (AmE), / ˈmam.əri / (BrE)
relating to the milk-producing glands of female mammals

Examples

Mammary glands
She noticed swelling in her mammary tissue and consulted her doctor.
Some cancers may develop in the mammary glands.
The mammary glands play a vital role in nourishing newborn mammals.
