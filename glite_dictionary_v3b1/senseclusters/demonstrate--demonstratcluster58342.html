Examples

Synonyms

  • substantiate

    substantiate means giving specific evidence to support a claim, while demonstrate focuses on showing or proving it with examples.
  • prove
    3.1 156/m
    prove demands strong evidence or logic to accept truth, while demonstrate often uses examples or experiments to show it.
  • establish
    4.1 29/m
    establish is to prove something so others accept it, while demonstrate often shows truth through tests or examples.
  • confirm
    1.8 62/m
    confirm checks or agrees that a fact is correct, while demonstrate shows or proves that fact using evidence or experiments.
  • evince
    8.2 1.39/m

  • attest
