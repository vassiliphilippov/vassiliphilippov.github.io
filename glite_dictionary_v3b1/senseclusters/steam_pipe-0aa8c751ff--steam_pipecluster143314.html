steam pipe

noun
pipe for conveying steam
8.6
Pronunciation: / stiːm paɪp / (AmE), / stiːm paɪp / (BrE)
a pipe designed to convey steam, typically under pressure, from one location to another

Examples

The old factory still uses a steam pipe system to heat its buildings.
