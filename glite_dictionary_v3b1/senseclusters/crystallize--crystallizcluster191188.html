crystallize

verb
make clear or definite
3.3 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪs.tə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪs.tə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to make ideas, plans, or feelings clear and definite; to become clear or take a definite form

Examples

Her doubts crystallized when she read the final report and understood the situation clearly.
The company's vision began to crystallize after the new CEO explained his strategy.
This meeting helped to crystallize our goals for the year ahead.
His ideas slowly began to crystallize after talking with colleagues.
The coach's speech crystallized the team's goals for the season.
The moment helped crystallize her decision to change careers.

It took a while for my thoughts to crystallize into a proposal.
The committee helped crystallize a clear plan for the event.
