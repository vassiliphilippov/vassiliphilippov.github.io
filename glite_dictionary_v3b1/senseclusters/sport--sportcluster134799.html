sport

verb
display or wear ostentatiously
Pronunciation: / spɔrt / (AmE), / spɔːt / (BrE)
to wear or display something in a way that is intended to attract notice or admiration

Examples

To sport a new hat
He arrived at the party sporting a bright red tie.
She often sports expensive sunglasses when she goes out.
The young man proudly sporting a tattoo drew everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: show off - to display something proudly, often to attract attention
