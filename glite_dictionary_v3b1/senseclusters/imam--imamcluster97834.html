imam

noun
Muslim religious leader; prayer leader in mosque
5.0 5.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɑm / (AmE), / ɪˈmɑːm / (BrE)
a Muslim religious leader, especially the person who leads prayers in a mosque; for Shiites, an imam is also a spiritual guide and authority on Islamic law and theology

Examples

The imam leads prayers
Many people attended the mosque to hear the imam speak about community service.
Shiite Muslims regard the imam as both a religious and spiritual leader.
The imam gave a sermon on compassion during Friday prayers.
