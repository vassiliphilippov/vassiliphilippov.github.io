piezo

noun
piezoelectric device
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀêzo ä / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀê…õts…ô ä / (BrE)
a device that uses the piezoelectric effect to convert mechanical pressure or vibration into an electrical signal

Examples

Piezo vibration sensor
The engineer installed a piezo to monitor vibrations in the machine.
A faulty piezo can cause inaccurate readings in sensitive equipment.
Many electronic drum kits use a piezo to detect when the pad is struck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensor - a device that detects or measures a physical property
  • synonym: transducer - a device that converts one form of energy into another
