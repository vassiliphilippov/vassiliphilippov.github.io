neighboring

adjective
adjacent or next to (something or somewhere)
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪbɚɪŋ /; / ˈneɪbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈneɪbərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of places, areas, or things) situated next to or very near to another; having a common boundary or edge.

Examples

Many neighboring countries
Many neighboring countries share similar cultural traditions.
She visited the neighboring village every weekend to see her friends.
The new store is popular among residents of the neighboring towns.
Several neighbouring countries signed a peace agreement last year.
The council is considering merging with the neighbouring town for improved services.
We walked through the park to explore the neighbouring neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjacent - next to or adjoining something else
  • antonym: distant - far away in space
  • antonym: remote - far away in distance or relation
