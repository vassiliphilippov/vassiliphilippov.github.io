Synonyms

  • adjacent
    3.8 14/m
    adjacent is commonly used to say something lies directly next to another place, while neighboring stresses being near or sharing a boundary.
  • contiguous
    7.0 2.23/m
    contiguous often refers to areas that touch directly or follow in time, while neighboring focuses on places that are simply near each other.
  • conterminous
    9.5 0.05/m
    conterminous is a formal word for things that share the same border, while neighboring is the common, everyday term for being next to.
