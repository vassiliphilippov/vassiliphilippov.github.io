tinily

adverb
in a small way
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪnɪli / (AmE), / ˈtɪnɪli / (BrE)
in a very small, delicate, or faint manner

Examples

The bell rang tinily
The bell rang tinily in the distance, barely audible over the wind.
She spoke tinily, her voice almost lost in the large, empty hall.
Raindrops fell tinily on the metal roof, creating a soft, delicate sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minutely - in a very small or precise way
  • degree higher: slightly - to a small degree or extent
