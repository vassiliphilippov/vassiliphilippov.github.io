unaffiliated

adjective
not connected to a group or organization
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈfɪliːeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not officially connected or associated with any group, organization, or party.

Examples

An unaffiliated voter
Many voters remain unaffiliated with any political party.
She is unaffiliated with any religious institution.
The charity helps unaffiliated artists gain recognition for their work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affiliated - officially attached or connected to an organization
  • synonym: independent - not belonging to or controlled by a group or organization
