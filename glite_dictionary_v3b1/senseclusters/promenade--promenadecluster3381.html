promenade

noun
public walkway for leisure
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑməˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌprɒm.əˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a public place or path, especially along a waterfront, where people may walk for pleasure

Examples

Seaside promenade
On summer evenings, families strolled along the promenade by the sea.
The city built a new promenade to encourage people to walk along the waterfront.
Vendors lined the promenade, selling snacks and souvenirs to tourists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boardwalk - a wooden walkway along a beach or waterfront
