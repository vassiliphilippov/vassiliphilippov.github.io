biovular

adjective
derived from two different eggs (twins)
9.9
Pronunciation: / baɪˈɑvjələr / (AmE), / baɪˈɒvjʊlə / (BrE)
(of twins) derived from two separate fertilized ova.

Examples

Biovular twins
Because the sisters were biovular, they did not look identical.
Fraternal twins are biovular, meaning they come from two different eggs.
The doctor explained that the likelihood of biovular twins increases with maternal age.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monovular - (of twins) derived from a single fertilized ovum
