Dagestani

noun
member of an ethnic group in southwestern Russia/Azerbaijan
Pronunciation: / ˌdɑːɡəˈstɑːni / (AmE), / ˌdæɡəˈstɑːni / (BrE)
A member of an ethnic group native to the region around the Caspian Sea in southwestern Russia and Azerbaijan.

Examples

Dagestani people
Many Dagestani communities maintain their own unique languages and customs.
She interviewed a Dagestani about his experience living in the Caucasus.
The exhibition highlighted the traditional crafts of the Dagestani people.
