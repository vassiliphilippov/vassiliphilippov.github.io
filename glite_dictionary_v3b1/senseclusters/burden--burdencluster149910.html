burden

noun
heavy load or weight to be carried
3.4 36/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ùÀêd…ôn / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêd…ôn /; / Ààb…úÀêdn / (BrE)
a heavy load, or something that causes worry, difficulty, or hardship (physical or emotional)

Examples

A heavy burden
She carried the emotional burden of her past wherever she went.
The bridge is designed to support a great burden of freight traffic.
The donkey struggled with the burden of heavy sacks on its back.
Caring for his sick mother was a heavy burden for him.
She felt the burden of expectation as she accepted the award.
The financial burden of the new tax fell on low-income families.
