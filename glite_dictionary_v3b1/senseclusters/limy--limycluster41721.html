limy

adjective
like lime fruit
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪmi / (AmE), / ˈlaɪmi / (BrE)
having the taste, smell, or character of lime fruit; citrus-like

Examples

Limy taste

The air was filled with a limy aroma after slicing the fruit.
