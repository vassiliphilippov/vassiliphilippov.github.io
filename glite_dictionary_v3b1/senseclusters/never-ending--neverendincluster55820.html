never-ending

adjective
without end; continuous
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛvɚˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɛvəˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing without stopping or seeming to have no end

Examples

A never-ending stream
She faced a never-ending stream of emails each day at the office.
The argument turned into a never-ending debate that lasted until midnight.
The series is famous for its never-ending plot twists and surprises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endless - having or seeming to have no end or limit
  • antonym: finite - having limits or bounds; not endless
  • synonym: interminable - endless or seemingly endless; often used to express annoyance
