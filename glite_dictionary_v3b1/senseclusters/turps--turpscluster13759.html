turps

noun
volatile solvent distilled from turpentine
6.2
Pronunciation: / tɝps / (AmE), / tɜːps / (BrE)
A volatile liquid distilled from turpentine oleoresin, used as a paint thinner, solvent, and sometimes in medicine.

Examples

Turps paint thinner
Always use turps in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling fumes.
He cleaned his brushes with turps after finishing the painting.
The smell of turps filled the small art studio.
