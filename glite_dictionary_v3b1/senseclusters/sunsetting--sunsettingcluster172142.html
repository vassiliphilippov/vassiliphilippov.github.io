sunsetting

adjective
like sunset light
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
having the colors, light, or qualities characteristic of the time when the sun sets.

Examples

Sunsetting sky colors
The artist captured the sunsetting glow on the mountains in her painting.
We enjoyed a picnic under the sunsetting sky, watching the colors change.
The sunsetting light made the city skyline look especially beautiful that evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dusk - the time just after the sun sets, when the sky is partly dark
  • synonym: sunset - the time in the evening when the sun disappears below the horizon
