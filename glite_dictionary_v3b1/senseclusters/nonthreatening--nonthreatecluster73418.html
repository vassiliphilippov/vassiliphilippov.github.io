nonthreatening

adjective
not causing fear
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈθrɛtənɪŋ / (BrE)
not likely to cause fear, harm, or intimidation

Examples

A nonthreatening manner
The teacher's nonthreatening manner helped new students feel comfortable in class.
His nonthreatening smile put everyone at ease during the meeting.
The animal appeared completely nonthreatening as it approached the children in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: intimidating - making someone feel frightened or nervous
