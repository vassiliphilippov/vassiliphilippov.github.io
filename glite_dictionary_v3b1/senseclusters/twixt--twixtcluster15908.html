twixt

preposition
archaic for between
Pronunciation: / twɪkst / (AmE), / twɪkst / (BrE)
an old-fashioned or poetic word meaning between

Examples

Land twixt mountains
The old poem spoke of a land twixt mountains and sea.
He found himself twixt laughter and tears as he read the letter.
Legends tell of a hidden path twixt the ancient trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: between - in the space separating two things or people
