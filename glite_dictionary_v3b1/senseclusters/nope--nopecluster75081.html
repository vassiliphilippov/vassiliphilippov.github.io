nope

noun
informal refusal
7.1
Pronunciation: / noʊp / (AmE), / nəʊp / (BrE)
an informal way of saying 'no', used as a response or answer

Examples

A quick nope
When asked if he wanted dessert, he simply replied with a cheerful nope.
The teacher asked if anyone had finished the assignment, but all she heard was a quiet nope from the back.
I checked my schedule to see if I was free, but it was a definite nope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no - a negative answer or refusal
  • antonym: yes - an affirmative answer or agreement
