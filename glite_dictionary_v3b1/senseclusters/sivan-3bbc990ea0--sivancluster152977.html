Sivan

noun
month in the Jewish calendar (May/June)
Pronunciation: / siˈvɑn / (AmE), / siˈvɑːn / (BrE)
the ninth month of the civil year and the third month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, usually falling in May and June

Examples

The month Sivan
Jews around the world observe important traditions throughout the month of Sivan.
She was born during Sivan, which usually falls in late spring.
The festival of Shavuot is celebrated in Sivan according to the Jewish calendar.
