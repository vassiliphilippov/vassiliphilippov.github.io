protagonism

noun
active advocacy or promotion of a cause
Pronunciation: / proʊˈtæɡəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / prəˈtæɡənɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
active support, advocacy, or promotion of a particular idea, cause, or policy, especially through argument or action

Examples

Protagonism in politics
Her protagonism in the environmental movement has inspired many to take action.
His protagonism for human rights was evident in every speech he gave.
Without strong protagonism from community leaders, the project would have failed to gain support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocacy - public support for or recommendation of a particular cause or policy
  • antonym: opposition - active resistance or dissent, in contrast to supporting a cause
