crosier

noun
ecclesiastical staff with a curved top
8.1 9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊʒər / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊzɪə / (BrE)
a ceremonial staff with a curved top carried by bishops and other high-ranking clergy as a symbol of their pastoral office

Examples

A crosier staff

The bishop raised his crosier as he blessed the congregation.
The ornate crosier was crafted from silver and decorated with jewels.
