Bikram

proper noun
yoga in heated room
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪkrəm / (AmE), / ˈbɪkrəm / (BrE)
a style of yoga practiced in a heated room, consisting of a set sequence of postures, named after its founder Bikram Choudhury

Examples

Students traveled from around the world to train with Bikram in California.
