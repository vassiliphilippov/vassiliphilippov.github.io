dancing

noun
rhythmic movement to music
Pronunciation: / ˈdæn.sɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɑːn.sɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of moving your body rhythmically, usually in time to music, especially as a form of entertainment or social interaction.

Examples

Competitive dancing
Competitive dancing requires a lot of practice and dedication.
She has always loved dancing and attends classes every week.
The music was so lively that everyone joined in the dancing.
