prerevolutionary

adjective
before a revolution
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˌrɛvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌpriːˌrɛvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to the period or conditions existing before a revolution takes place

Examples

Prerevolutionary period
The museum displays artifacts from prerevolutionary France, showing life before the upheaval.
Many traditions from the prerevolutionary era were lost after the political changes.
Historians study prerevolutionary societies to understand the causes of major revolutions.
