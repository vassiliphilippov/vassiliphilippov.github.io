tantalizer

noun
one who teases by withholding something desired
6.5 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈtæn.tə.laɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person who teases or torments by offering something desirable but keeping it just out of reach

Examples

A playful tantalizer
She found him to be a tantalizer, stringing her along with promises he never intended to keep.
The aroma of baking bread acted as a cruel tantalizer for the hungry children outside the bakery.
The mysterious auctioneer was a true tantalizer, always hinting at treasures but never revealing anything too soon.
History remembers the myth of Tantalus, the greatest tantaliser of all.
She accused him of being a tantaliser after he showed her the surprise but wouldn't reveal it.
The cruel tantaliser waved the treat just out of the dog's reach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: teaser - a person who makes fun of or provokes others in a playful way
  • synonym: tormentor - a person who causes someone to suffer
