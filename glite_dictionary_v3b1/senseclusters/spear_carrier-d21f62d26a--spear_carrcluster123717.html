spear carrier

noun
minor actor with a nonspeaking role
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪr ˌkæriər /; / spɪr ˈkæriər / (AmE), / ˈspɪə ˌkæriə /; / spɪə ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a person who has a very minor or insignificant role, especially in a play, movie, or group
