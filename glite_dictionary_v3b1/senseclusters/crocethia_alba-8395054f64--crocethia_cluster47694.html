Crocethia alba

noun
species of small sandpiper (sanderling)
9.9
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈsiːθiə ˈælbə / (AmE), / krəʊˈsiːθɪə ˈalbə / (BrE)
A small sandpiper species that breeds in the Arctic and migrates south along sandy coasts worldwide, also known as the sanderling.

Examples

The Crocethia alba bird
Crocethia alba is commonly seen darting along the shoreline, probing the wet sand for food.
Birdwatchers often spot Crocethia alba in large flocks during their migration along coastal areas.
The breeding grounds of Crocethia alba are found in the high Arctic tundra.
