novelet

noun
short trivial story
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑvəlɛt / (AmE), / ˈnɒvəlɛt / (BrE)
a short work of fiction, usually longer than a short story but shorter than a novel, often light or sentimental in style

Examples

A novelet story
The magazine published a novelet that was easy to read on a short train ride.

She enjoyed reading a novelet before bed to relax after a long day.
The magazine featured a gripping novelet by a promising new author.
She enjoyed reading a novelet on the train because it was the perfect length for her commute.
His first published work was a novelet that received critical acclaim.
