software optimization

noun
improving software performance or efficiency
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔft.wɛr ˌɑp.tə.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˈsɔftˌwɛɹ ˌɑptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈsɒft.wɛː ˌɒp.tɪ.maɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˈsɒftweə ˌɒptɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
