digital plethysmograph

noun
device for measuring blood flow in a finger or toe
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬l plɛθˈɪzməˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl plɛθˈɪzməˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A medical instrument attached to a finger or toe to measure blood flow in the skin.

Examples

A digital plethysmograph device
A digital plethysmograph can help diagnose peripheral artery disease quickly and painlessly.
Researchers relied on a digital plethysmograph during the vascular study.
The doctor used a digital plethysmograph to assess blood circulation in her finger.
