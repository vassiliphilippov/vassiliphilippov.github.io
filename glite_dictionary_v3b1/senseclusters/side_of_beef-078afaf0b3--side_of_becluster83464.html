side of beef

noun
one half of a dressed beef carcass
4.3
Pronunciation: / saɪd əv bif / (AmE), / saɪd əv biːf / (BrE)
one half of a beef carcass that has been dressed and prepared for butchering or sale

Examples

A side of beef
A side of beef typically weighs several hundred pounds after dressing.
The butcher showed me the side of beef hanging in the cold room.
They bought a side of beef to store meat for the entire winter.
