berserker

noun
Norse warrior fighting in a frenzy
Pronunciation: / bərˈzɜrkər / (AmE), / bəˈzɜːkə / (BrE)
in Norse legend, a warrior who fought with wild, uncontrollable ferocity, often said to enter a trance-like rage before battle

Examples

A berserker warrior
In the ancient sagas, the berserker was feared for his uncontrollable rage on the battlefield.
Legends say that a berserker could defeat multiple enemies while in his frenzied state.
The reenactment included a fierce berserker charging into battle with a shout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warrior - a person who fights in battles or wars
