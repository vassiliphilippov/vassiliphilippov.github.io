support stocking

noun
elastic stocking for vein support
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt ˈstɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt ˈstɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
An elasticized stocking worn on the leg to reduce pressure on veins, often used to treat or prevent varicose veins and related conditions.

Examples

A support stocking
After her surgery, the doctor recommended wearing a support stocking to improve blood circulation in her leg.
He struggled to put on his support stocking but found it really helped with his aching legs.
Many people with varicose veins use a support stocking daily to reduce swelling and discomfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compression stocking - a stocking designed to apply pressure to the legs
