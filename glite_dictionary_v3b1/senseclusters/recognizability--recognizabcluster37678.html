recognizability

noun
ability to be identified
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkəɡˌnaɪzəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌrɛkəɡnaɪzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be identified or accepted, especially by an algorithm or automaton in mathematics or computer science
