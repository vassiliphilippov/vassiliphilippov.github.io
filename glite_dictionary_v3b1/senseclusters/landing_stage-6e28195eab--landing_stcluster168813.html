landing stage

noun
platform for loading/unloading boats
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlændɪŋ steɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈlændɪŋ steɪdʒ / (BrE)
A platform built beside water for loading or unloading passengers and cargo from boats or ships.

Examples

The landing stage platform
The ferry arrived at the landing stage just as the sun was setting.
Tourists gathered on the landing stage, waiting to board the riverboat.
We placed our luggage on the landing stage before climbing aboard the ship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dock - an artificial platform or area for loading and unloading ships
  • synonym: jetty - a structure projecting into water, used as a landing place or to protect a harbor
