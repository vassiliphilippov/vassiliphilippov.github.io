trail bike

noun
off-road motorcycle
Pronunciation: / treɪl baɪk / (AmE), / treɪl baɪk / (BrE)
a lightweight motorcycle designed for use on rough or unpaved terrain, typically fitted with rugged tires and suspension for off-road riding

Examples

A trail bike ride
After buying a new trail bike, she joined a local off-road riding club.
He prefers to ride his trail bike through the forest trails on weekends.
The trail bike is built to handle rocky and muddy paths with ease.
