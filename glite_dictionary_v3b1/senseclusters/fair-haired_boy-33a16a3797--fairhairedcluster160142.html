fair-haired boy

noun
a favored or preferred person
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfer her̩d ˈbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˌfeə hɛəd ˈbɔɪ / (BrE)
a person who is specially favored or preferred over others, often receiving preferential treatment

Examples

The fair-haired boy of the team
Although many were qualified, Jack quickly became the fair-haired boy of the department.
She resented the fact that her brother was always the fair-haired boy in their parents' eyes.
The coach clearly saw Ethan as his fair-haired boy, giving him more opportunities than the others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: golden boy - a person who is greatly admired and favored
