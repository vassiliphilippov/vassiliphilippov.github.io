tenderize

verb
make food, especially meat, softer
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dəˌraɪz /; / ˈtɛndəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.də.raɪz /; / ˈtɛndəraɪz / (BrE)
to make meat or food softer and easier to eat by marinating, pounding, or using a tenderizer

Examples

To tenderize meat
A meat mallet can be used to tenderize tough cuts of beef.
If you tenderize the steak before cooking, it will be much easier to chew.
Some chefs tenderize chicken by soaking it in buttermilk overnight.
Many chefs tenderise tough cuts of beef by pounding them with a mallet.
She always tenderises the steak before cooking it for dinner.
You can tenderise chicken by marinating it in lemon juice overnight.

  • synonym: tenderize - make meat tender (American spelling of 'tenderise')
