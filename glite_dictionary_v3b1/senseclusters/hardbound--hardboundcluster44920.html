hardbound

adjective
having a hard cover (book)
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
(of a book) having a hard cover or binding.

Examples

A hardbound book
A hardbound book usually lasts much longer than one with a soft cover.
She prefers to collect hardbound editions of classic novels for her library.
The dictionary comes in both hardbound and paperback versions to suit different preferences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardback - a book with a rigid protective cover
  • antonym: paperback - a book with a flexible paper cover
