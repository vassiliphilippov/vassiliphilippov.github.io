demonetize

verb
remove legal status of currency
Pronunciation: / diˈmɑːnəˌtaɪz /; / diˈmɑːnɪˌtaɪz /; / diˈmʌnəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / diˈmɒnɪtaɪz /; / ˌdiːˈmɒnɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to officially remove the status of legal currency from money, so it can no longer be used as payment
