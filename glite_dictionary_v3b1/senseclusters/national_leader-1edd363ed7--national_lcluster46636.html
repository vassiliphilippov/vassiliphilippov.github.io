national leader

noun
chief leader of a nation
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəl ˈliːdər / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl ˈliːdə / (BrE)
a person who is respected as the chief political figure of a nation or as a leader in national or international affairs

Examples

A national leader speaks
As a national leader, she worked tirelessly to improve her country's economy.
Many people regard Nelson Mandela as a national leader who stood for justice and equality.
The summit was attended by several national leaders from around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: head of government - the chief executive of a government, e.g., a prime minister
  • synonym: head of state - the official leader of a country, especially a president or monarch
