insuperable

adjective
impossible to overcome
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsuːpərəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈsjuːpərəbəl /; / ɪnˈsuːpərəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to overcome or defeat (describing a difficulty, obstacle, or challenge)

Examples

An insuperable problem
For many, the challenge of climbing the mountain seemed insuperable.
Her determination helped her overcome what others saw as insuperable odds.
The explorers faced insuperable obstacles during their journey across the desert.
He faced what seemed like insuperable odds but refused to give up.
The challenges presented by the terrain were nearly insuperable for early explorers.
The language barrier proved to be an insuperable obstacle during negotiations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurmountable - impossible to overcome (synonym)
  • synonym: invincible - impossible to defeat (synonym in heroic context)
  • antonym: surmountable - able to be overcome (antonym)
