ballbuster

noun
painful testicle injury
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌbʌstə / (BrE)
a blow or injury that causes severe pain to the testicles, often used informally or as slang.

Examples

A painful ballbuster
He doubled over in pain after taking a direct ballbuster during the soccer match.
The comedian joked about how a surprise ballbuster can ruin anyone's day.
After the accidental ballbuster, he needed a few minutes to recover before continuing the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groin injury - an injury to the area between the abdomen and thigh
