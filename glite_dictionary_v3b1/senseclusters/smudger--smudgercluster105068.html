smudger

noun
one who makes smears
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌdʒər / (AmE), / ˈsmʌdʒə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes smears, marks, or stains, either intentionally or accidentally

Examples

Careless smudger
The artist used a smudger to blend the charcoal lines on her drawing.
After eating chocolate, he became an accidental smudger on the white sofa.
A good makeup smudger helps create a soft, smoky eye effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleaner - a person or thing that removes dirt or marks
  • synonym: smearer - a person who smears or spreads something
