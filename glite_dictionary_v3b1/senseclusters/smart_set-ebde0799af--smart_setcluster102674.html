smart set

noun
fashionable social elite
Pronunciation: / smɑrt sɛt / (AmE), / smɑːt sɛt / (BrE)
A group of people who are considered to be fashionable, stylish, and part of the social elite.

Examples

The smart set at a party
Many aspiring artists hoped to be noticed by the smart set in Paris during the 1920s.
She was eager to join the smart set and become a part of fashionable society.
The gala was attended by members of the city's smart set and several celebrities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: high society - the social class made up of people who are rich and fashionable
  • synonym: jet set - wealthy and fashionable people who travel widely for pleasure
