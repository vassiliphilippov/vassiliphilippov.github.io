thirsty

adjective
needing moisture (of land, plants, etc.)
3.9 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈθɜːr.stiː/ /; / /ˈθɜːr.stɪ/ (AmE), /ˈθɜː.stiː/ /; / /ˈθɜː.stɪ/ (BrE)
(of land, plants, etc.) needing moisture; very dry or parched.

Examples

Thirsty land cracks

The thirsty soil cracked in the summer heat from lack of rain.
