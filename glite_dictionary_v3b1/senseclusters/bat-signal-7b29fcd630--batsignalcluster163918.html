Bat-Signal

noun
distress signal to summon Batman
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (AmE), / ˈbæt ˌsɪɡ.nəl / (BrE)
a large spotlight with an image of a bat, used in the Batman comics and films as a distress signal to summon Batman

Examples

The Bat-Signal shines
During emergencies, the city's authorities rely on the Bat-Signal to request Batman’s help.
The iconic Bat-Signal has become a symbol of hope for Gotham’s citizens.
When crime rates soared, the commissioner ordered the Bat-Signal to be projected into the night sky.
