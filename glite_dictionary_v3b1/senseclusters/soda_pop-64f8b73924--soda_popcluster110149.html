soda pop

noun
sweet carbonated soft drink
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə pɑp / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə pɒp / (BrE)
a sweet, flavored carbonated drink; a soft drink

Examples

A glass of soda pop
Children at the party drank soda pop and ate pizza together.
He opened a cold soda pop to enjoy with his lunch.
In some regions, people use 'soda pop' instead of just 'soda' when ordering a drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soda - a sweet carbonated beverage
  • synonym: soft drink - a non-alcoholic, sweetened, carbonated beverage
