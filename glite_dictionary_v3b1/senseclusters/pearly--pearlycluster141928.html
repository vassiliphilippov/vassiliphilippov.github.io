pearly

noun
informal term for a tooth
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːli / (BrE)
(informal) A human tooth, especially when referring to its whiteness or attractiveness.

Examples

Show off your pearlies
After the dental cleaning, her pearlies were sparkling.
He flashed his pearlies in a wide grin.
He lost one of his pearlies during the hockey game.
