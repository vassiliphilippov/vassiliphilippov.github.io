louvered

adjective
fitted with angled slats or louvers
9.6 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.vərd/ (AmE), /ˈluː.vəd/ (BrE)
fitted with louvers, or slats, usually to allow air or light to pass through while keeping out rain or direct sun

Examples

We replaced the old closet door with a louvered one to improve airflow.
