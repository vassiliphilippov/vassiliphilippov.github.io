bard

verb
put ornamental covering on a horse
8.3
Pronunciation: / bɑrd / (AmE), / bɑːd / (BrE)
to cover (a horse) with ornamental cloth or armor, especially for ceremonial or military purposes

Examples

To bard a horse
Before the tournament, the squires spent hours barding the horses with elaborate caparisons.
For the parade, each horse was carefully barded in vibrant, embroidered cloth.
The knights prepared for battle and barded their steeds with decorative armor.
