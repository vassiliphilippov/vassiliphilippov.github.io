fagoting

noun
openwork embroidery tying threads with fagot stitches
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡ.ə.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfæɡ.ət.ɪŋ / (BrE)
an embroidery technique in which groups of parallel threads are tied together with fagot stitches to create an openwork pattern

Fagoting embroidery technique

The antique dress featured intricate fagoting along the seams for a decorative touch.
