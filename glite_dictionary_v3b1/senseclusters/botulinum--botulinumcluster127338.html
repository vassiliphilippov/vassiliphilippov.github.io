botulinum

noun
bacterium that produces botulin toxin
7.2 8.4
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈtjuː.lɪ.nəm/ /; / /boʊ.ˈtuː.lɪ.nəm/ (AmE), /bə.ˈtjuː.lɪ.nəm/ /; / /bəʊ.ˈtjuː.lɪ.nəm/ (BrE)
An anaerobic bacterium of the genus Clostridium that produces botulin, the toxin responsible for botulism.
