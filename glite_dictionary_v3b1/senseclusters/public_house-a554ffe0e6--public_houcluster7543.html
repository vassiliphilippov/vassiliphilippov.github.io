public house

noun
a bar or tavern, especially in Britain
4.8 1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk haʊs / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk haʊs / (BrE)
A building where alcoholic drinks are sold and consumed, often providing light meals and sometimes accommodation; commonly referred to as a 'pub' in Britain.
