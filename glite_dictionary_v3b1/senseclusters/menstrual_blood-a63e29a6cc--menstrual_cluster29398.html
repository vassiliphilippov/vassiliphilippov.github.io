menstrual blood

noun
blood discharged during menstruation
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnstruəl blʌd / (AmE), / ˈmɛnstrʊəl blʌd / (BrE)
the blood and tissue discharged from the uterus through the vagina during menstruation

Examples

Menstrual blood flow
Doctors may ask about the color or amount of menstrual blood during a check-up.
Proper hygiene is important when managing menstrual blood during periods.
Some women experience heavier menstrual blood flow than others.

Related Terms
