vena trachealis

noun
small veins of the trachea
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈviː.nə trəˈkiː.ə.lɪs / (AmE), / ˈviː.nə trəˈkiː.ə.lɪs / (BrE)
Any of several small veins that drain blood from the trachea.

Examples

Vena trachealis veins
Inflammation of the vena trachealis may contribute to complications in certain respiratory diseases.
The vena trachealis are responsible for draining blood from the tissues surrounding the trachea.
The vena trachealis can be observed during a dissection of the neck region.
