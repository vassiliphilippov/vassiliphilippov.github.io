panga

noun
fishing boat or fish
Pronunciation: / ˈpæŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈpæŋɡə / (BrE)
a small, flat-bottomed fishing boat commonly used in Central America and Africa, or a type of freshwater fish farmed for food

Examples

A panga boat
The fishermen rowed their panga out to deeper waters early in the morning.
Many restaurants serve grilled panga as a healthy and affordable fish option.
After a long day at sea, they returned with their catch in the old panga.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catfish - a type of freshwater fish with whisker-like barbels
  • synonym: dinghy - a small open boat often used for fishing or recreation
