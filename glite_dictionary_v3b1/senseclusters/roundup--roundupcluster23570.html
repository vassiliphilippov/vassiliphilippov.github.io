roundup

noun
a summary review of events or items
3.7 1.21/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆraÊŠnËŒdÊŒp / (AmE), / ËˆraÊŠndÊŒp / (BrE)
a summary or review of events or information, especially presented in a concise form

Examples

News roundup
The magazine features a roundup of the best restaurants in the city.
The report ended with a roundup of this month's key economic statistics.
Today's paper included a roundup of the week's most important news stories.

Synonyms

  • recap
    3.2 2.31/m

  • sum-up

  • summing up
