shepherd

noun
person who herds sheep
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛpərd / (AmE), / ˈʃɛpəd / (BrE)
a person whose job is to take care of and guide a flock of sheep, especially on open land

Examples

A shepherd with sheep
As a shepherd, he spent most of his days outdoors in the hills.
The shepherd led the flock across the field at dawn.
The loyal dog helped the shepherd keep the sheep together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herdsman - a person who looks after a herd of livestock
  • synonym: sheepdog - a dog trained to help with gathering and moving sheep
