agglomerate

verb
to cluster or collect into a mass
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈɡlɑː.məˌreɪt / (AmE), / əˈɡlɒm.əˌreɪt / (BrE)
to collect or be gathered together into a cluster or mass; to form into one group or mass

Examples

To agglomerate into groups
As the snow fell, it began to agglomerate around the branches of the trees.
Small particles can agglomerate to form larger clusters in certain chemical reactions.
The dust bunnies under the bed tend to agglomerate over time if not cleaned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregate - to gather into a whole; to collect
