physical geography

noun
study of earth's physical features
2.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl dʒiː.ˈgɑː.rə.fiː/ /; / /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl dʒɪ.ˈgɑː.rə.fiː/ (AmE), /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl dʒɪ.ˈgɒ.rə.fiː/ /; / /ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl dʒiː.ˈgɒ.rə.fiː/ (BrE)

Examples
