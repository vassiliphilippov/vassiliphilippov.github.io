photogrammetric

adjective
relating to measurement from photos
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəɡræˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəɡræˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the science or methods of making measurements from photographs, especially for mapping or surveying

Examples

Photogrammetric analysis
The researchers used photogrammetric analysis to determine the height of the ancient ruins.
A photogrammetric survey can quickly produce accurate maps of large areas.
Modern drones often carry cameras for photogrammetric mapping of landscapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartographic - relating to the making of maps
  • synonym: photographic - relating to photographs or photography
