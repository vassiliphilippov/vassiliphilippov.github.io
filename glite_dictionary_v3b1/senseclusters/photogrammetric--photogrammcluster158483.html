photogrammetric

adjective
relating to measurement from photos
9.4
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.ɡræ.ˈmet.rɪk/ /; / /foʊ.tə.ɡrə.ˈmet.rɪk/ (AmE), /əʊ.tə.ɡræ.ˈmet.rɪk/ /; / /əʊ.tə.ɡrə.ˈmet.rɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
