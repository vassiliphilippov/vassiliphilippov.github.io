trapezoid bone

noun
a wrist bone between trapezium and capitate
7.6
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiː.zɔɪd ˌboʊn / (AmE), / trəˈpiː.zɔɪd ˌbəʊn / (BrE)
the small wrist bone situated between the trapezium and capitate bones in the human hand
