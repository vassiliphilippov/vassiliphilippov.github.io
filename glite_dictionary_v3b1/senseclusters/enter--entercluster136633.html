enter

verb
be included as a part or factor
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛn.tə / (BrE)
to be included as a part or factor in something; to play a role in a situation or process

Examples

Enter into discussion
Financial considerations often enter into major business decisions.
Her personal beliefs did not enter into her scientific judgment.
Weather conditions are likely to enter into the team's strategy for the race.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to leave out or not include
  • synonym: figure - be part of something, as in 'figure in' a discussion
