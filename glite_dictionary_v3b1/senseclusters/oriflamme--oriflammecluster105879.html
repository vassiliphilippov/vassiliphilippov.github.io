oriflamme

noun
inspiring symbol or rallying point
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪˌflæm / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪˌflam / (BrE)
an inspiring symbol or ideal that serves as a rallying point, especially in a struggle; originally, a battle standard

Examples

The oriflamme of freedom
Liberty, once accepted, is the oriflamme under which generations fight oppression.
The revolutionary leader became the oriflamme for those seeking change.
Their community's flag served as an oriflamme in the battle for rights.
