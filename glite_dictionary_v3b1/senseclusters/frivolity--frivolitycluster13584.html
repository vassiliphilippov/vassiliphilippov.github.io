frivolity

noun
lack of seriousness; lightheartedness
Pronunciation: / frɪˈvɑːlɪti /; / frɪˈvɑlɪti /; / frɪˈvɑl.ɪ.ti / (AmE), / frɪˈvɒlɪti /; / frɪˈvɒl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
silly or lighthearted behavior, or things that are not serious or important
