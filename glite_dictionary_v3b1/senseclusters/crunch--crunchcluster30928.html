crunch

verb
reduce to small pieces by crushing
1.4
Pronunciation: / krʌntʃ / (AmE), / krʌntʃ / (BrE)
to reduce something to small pieces or particles by crushing or grinding, typically with force

Examples

To crunch biscuits
She used a rolling pin to crunch the biscuits into crumbs for the cake base.
The chef crunched the nuts before adding them to the sauce.
You can crunch the ice cubes in a blender to make a smooth drink.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grind - to crush something into small pieces or powder by pressing
  • synonym: pulverize - to crush into powder or dust
