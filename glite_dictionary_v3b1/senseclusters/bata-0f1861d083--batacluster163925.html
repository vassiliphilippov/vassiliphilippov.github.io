Bata

noun
a Chadic language of Nigeria
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑː.tə / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.tə / (BrE)
a Chadic language spoken mainly south of Lake Chad, especially in Adamawa State, Nigeria.

Examples

Bata language
Linguists have studied the grammar of the Bata language for decades.
Several villages in Adamawa State still speak Bata as their primary language.
