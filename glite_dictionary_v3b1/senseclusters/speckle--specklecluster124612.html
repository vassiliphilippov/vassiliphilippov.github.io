speckle

noun
small contrasting spot or mark
6.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈspek.əl/ (AmE), /ˈspek.əl/ (BrE)
a small spot or mark of contrasting color, especially one of many on a surface

Examples

A speckle pattern
Dark speckles scattered across the surface of the rock added to its beauty.
Her dress was white with delicate red speckles along the hem.
The robin's eggs were pale blue with tiny speckles on them.
