deck chair

noun
folding outdoor chair with canvas seat
2.0
Pronunciation: / dɛk tʃɛr / (AmE), / dɛk tʃɛː / (BrE)
a folding outdoor chair with a wooden frame and a strip of fabric for sitting, typically used on decks or patios

Examples

A deck chair outside
He carried a deck chair to the garden for an afternoon nap.
She stretched out on the deck chair and enjoyed the sunshine.
The cruise ship's top deck was filled with passengers relaxing in deck chairs.
