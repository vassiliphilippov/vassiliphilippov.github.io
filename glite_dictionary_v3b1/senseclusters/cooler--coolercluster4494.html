cooler

noun
container or device for keeping things cool
2.0 1.22/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀêl…ö / (AmE), / ÀàkuÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a container or device, often electrically powered, used to keep liquids or food cool, especially a small refrigerator or an insulated portable box.

Examples

A picnic cooler
Keep the milk in the cooler so it doesn't spoil.
The office cooler is stocked with cold water and juices.
We filled the cooler with ice and sodas for the beach trip.

Synonyms

  • refrigerator
    1.0 19/m
    a refrigerator is an electric appliance for storing food at home, while a cooler is often a smaller fridge or a portable insulated box.
  • ice chest
    1.7 0.49/m
    an ice chest is a portable insulated box that uses ice to chill drinks, while a cooler can be electric or a simple travel box.
