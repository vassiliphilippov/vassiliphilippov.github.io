wheatland

noun
area for wheat farming
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːtlænd / (AmE), / ˈwiːtlænd / (BrE)
a large area of land used for growing wheat

Examples

A wheatland field
The golden wheatland shimmered under the afternoon sun, ready for harvest.
Farmers in the wheatland work long hours during the growing season.
A small village was surrounded by miles of wheatland stretching to the horizon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmland - land used for farming crops or raising animals
  • antonym: pasture - land covered with grass for grazing animals
