orienteering

noun
map-based sport
Pronunciation: / ˌɔriənˈtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɒriənˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport in which participants use a map and compass to navigate through unfamiliar terrain as quickly as possible

Examples

Orienteering race
We joined a local club to try orienteering in the national park.
During the orienteering competition, each team had to find all the checkpoints in order.
Many schools offer orienteering as an outdoor activity to teach navigation skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiking - the activity of walking in natural environments
  • synonym: navigation - the process of planning and following a route
