thingie

noun
unnamed object
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈθɪŋi / (BrE)
an informal word for an object whose name you do not know or cannot remember

Examples

A thingie on table
Could you pass me that thingie next to the lamp?
I need the thingie you use to open bottles, but I can't remember its name.
She pointed at the thingie on the shelf and asked what it was for.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thingamajig - an informal word for an object whose name is unknown or forgotten
  • synonym: whatchamacallit - an informal term for something whose name you do not know
