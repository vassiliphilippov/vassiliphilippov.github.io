inspector

noun
person who examines or investigates
3.5 2.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɛktər / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɛktə / (BrE)
A person whose job is to examine things carefully in order to check quality, safety, or compliance with rules.

Examples

Building inspector
A building inspector found several code violations during the routine check.
Detective Lee works as an inspector in the city’s police department.
The inspector visited the factory to ensure all safety protocols were being followed.

  • synonym: examiner - a person who inspects or investigates closely
