unlove

verb
to stop loving
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlʌv / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌv / (BrE)

Examples

To unlove a person

She tried to unlove her favorite song, but the memories remained.
Over time, he managed to unlove the city he once called home.
