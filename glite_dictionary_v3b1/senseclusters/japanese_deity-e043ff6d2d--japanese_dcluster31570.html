Japanese deity

noun
supernatural being in Japanese religion
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz ˈdiːəti / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈdiːəti / (BrE)
a supernatural being worshipped in the religious traditions of Japan, such as Shinto or Buddhism

Examples

Japanese deity Amaterasu
Artists often depict the Japanese deity Amaterasu with a radiant sun motif.
Many shrines in Japan are dedicated to a particular Japanese deity from Shinto mythology.
The legend describes how a Japanese deity created the islands of Japan.
