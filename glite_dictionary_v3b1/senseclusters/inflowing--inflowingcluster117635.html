inflowing

adjective
flowing inward
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially a liquid or gas) moving or flowing inward.

Examples

Inflowing water
Scientists studied the inflowing water to understand its impact on the lake's ecosystem.
The inflowing tide quickly filled the empty harbor.
Warm, inflowing air can lead to sudden changes in local weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outflowing - flowing outward
