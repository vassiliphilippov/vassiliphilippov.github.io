misprint

noun
an error in printed text
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.prɪnt / (BrE)
A mistake in printed text caused by mechanical or typographical error.

Examples

A misprint in a book
Despite several edits, a misprint still appeared in the final edition.
I noticed a misprint in the first paragraph of the article.
The publisher apologized for the misprint on the book's cover.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correction - a change that rectifies an error in printed material
  • synonym: typo - a minor typing error in printed or digital text
