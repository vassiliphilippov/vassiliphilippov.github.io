preceptorial

noun
university tutorial
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsɛpˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːsɛpˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
a small class or meeting for instruction, usually led by a preceptor, especially at a university

Examples

A preceptorial class
Each week students attend a preceptorial to discuss the readings with their tutor.
The preceptorial format encourages active participation and close interaction with the instructor.
She found the preceptorial helpful for understanding complex topics in her philosophy course.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lecture - an educational talk to a large group of students
  • synonym: seminar - a class at a college or university where a small group discusses a topic
