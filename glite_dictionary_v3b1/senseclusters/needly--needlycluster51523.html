needly

adverb
necessarily, inevitably
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdli / (AmE), / ˈniːdli / (BrE)
in a way that is necessary or by necessity; inevitably (archaic or literary)

Examples

Act needly
If the bridge is unsafe, we will needly find another route before nightfall.
He needly accepted the king's command, for he had no other choice.
The crops needly failed after months without rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inevitably - in a way that cannot be prevented
  • synonym: necessarily - in a way that cannot be avoided
