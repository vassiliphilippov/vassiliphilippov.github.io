lathi

noun
Indian police baton or stick
9.9 9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːti / (AmE), / ˈlɑːti / (BrE)
A heavy stick, often made of bamboo and sometimes reinforced with iron, used especially by police in India as a weapon or for crowd control.

Examples

A lathi charge
A lathi charge was ordered to control the unruly mob.
He suffered injuries after being struck by a lathi during the demonstration.
The police dispersed the crowd with their lathis during the protest.
