attic

noun
space or room below roof for storage
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈætɪk / (AmE), / ˈætɪk / (BrE)
A space or room just below the roof of a house, often used for storage.

Examples

A dusty attic
During storms, the attic often makes strange creaking sounds.
She converted the dusty attic into a quiet office space.
We found a box of old photographs in the attic while cleaning the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: basement - a floor of a building that is partly or entirely below ground level
  • synonym: loft - an upper room or space just under the roof, often used for storage
