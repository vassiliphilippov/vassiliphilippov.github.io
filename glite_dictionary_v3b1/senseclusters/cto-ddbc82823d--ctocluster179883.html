CTO

noun
executive for transformation
Pronunciation: / si ti oʊ / (AmE), / siː tiː əʊ / (BrE)
an executive responsible for planning and leading major organizational change or transformation initiatives

Examples

Change CTO leader
The company hired a CTO to oversee its digital transformation strategy.
As CTO, she introduced new technologies to improve efficiency across all departments.
The board credited the CTO with successfully leading the merger and integration process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chief Executive Officer - the highest-ranking executive in a company
  • synonym: Chief Operating Officer - an executive responsible for daily operations
