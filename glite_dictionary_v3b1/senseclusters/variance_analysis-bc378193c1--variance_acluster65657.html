variance analysis

noun
analysis of financial performance deviations
1.0 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈver.ɪ.əns ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /ˈvær.ɪ.əns ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈveə.rɪ.əns ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
the process of examining differences between actual financial performance and budgeted or standard figures, to understand the causes of those differences

Examples

Financial variance analysis
Accurate variance analysis helps management identify problem areas and reduce costs.
Each month, managers perform variance analysis on departmental budgets to keep spending in check.
The finance team conducted a variance analysis to determine why actual expenses were higher than planned.
