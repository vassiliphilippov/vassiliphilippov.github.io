antisocial

adjective
hostile or disruptive of social norms
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈsoʊ.ʃəl / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈsəʊ.ʃəl / (BrE)
hostile to or disruptive of accepted standards of social behavior, especially in a way that violates the rights of others

Examples

Antisocial behavior
Persistent antisocial behavior can lead to serious legal consequences.
The new law aims to tackle antisocial activities in urban areas.
Vandalizing public property is considered an antisocial act by most communities.
