infernal

adjective
characteristic of intense, hellish fire
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɜrnəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɜːnəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a very intense or uncontrolled fire; resembling hellfire in heat or appearance.

Examples

Infernal flames
Flames rose to the ceiling, creating an infernal scene of chaos and destruction.
The forest was consumed by an infernal blaze that lasted for days.
We struggled to work in the infernal heat of the engine room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blazing - burning fiercely or brightly
  • synonym: fiery - resembling fire; intensely hot or burning
