role

noun
part played by an actor
1.0
Pronunciation: / roʊl / (AmE), / rəʊl / (BrE)
the part played by an actor portraying a character in a play, film, or other performance

Examples

Lead role in a play
He prepared for weeks to get into character for the demanding role of Hamlet.
Playing a comic role was a new challenge for the experienced actor.
She landed the leading role in the school play.
