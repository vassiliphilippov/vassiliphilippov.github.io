frizzle

verb
to curl hair tightly
3.2 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzəl / (BrE)
to make hair or material curly, frizzy, or twisted, often using heat, chemicals, or by damage

Examples

To frizzle hair
If you frizzle the ribbons with scissors, they will curl nicely for gift wrapping.
She loves to frizzle her hair before a night out.
The humid weather caused my bangs to frizzle uncontrollably.
After a long day at the beach, her hair frizzled from the salty air and sun.
If you use too much heat on your clothes, the fabric might frizzle and lose its smoothness.
He accidentally frizzled his hair by leaving the straightener on for too long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crimp - to make wavy or curl by pressing or pinching
  • synonym: curl - to form into a spiral or ringlet shape
  • antonym: smooth - to make something even and free from curls or frizz
  • antonym: straighten - to make hair or another material straight, removing any curl
