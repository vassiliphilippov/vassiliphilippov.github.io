impulse

noun
electrical signal in a nerve
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.pʌls / (AmE), / ˈɪm.pʌls / (BrE)
an electrical signal that travels along a nerve fiber, transmitting information within the nervous system

Examples

A nerve impulse
A nerve impulse travels quickly from your hand to your brain when you touch something hot.
Doctors study the speed of impulses along nerves to diagnose certain diseases.
The transmission of a single impulse can activate several muscles at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: action potential - a rapid rise and fall in electrical potential across a nerve cell membrane
  • synonym: nerve signal - a signal transmitted along a nerve fiber
