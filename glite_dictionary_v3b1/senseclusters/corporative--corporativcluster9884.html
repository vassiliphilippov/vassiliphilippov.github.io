corporative

adjective
relating to corporatism
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔːrpərətɪv / (AmE), / ˈkɔːpərətɪv / (BrE)
relating to corporatism, where organized groups such as businesses or labor unions are officially involved in state decision-making.

Examples

Corporative system of groups
The new constitution established a corporative system to manage relations between workers and employers.
Some historians argue that corporative structures limited political freedoms in the country.
The party promoted corporative policies to integrate industry and government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporatist - relating to the theory or practice of corporatism
  • antonym: individualistic - favoring individual freedom over group control
