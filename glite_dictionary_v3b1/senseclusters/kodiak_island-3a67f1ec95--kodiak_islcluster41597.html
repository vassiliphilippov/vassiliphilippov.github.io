Kodiak Island

proper noun
island in the Gulf of Alaska, southern Alaska
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdiˌæk ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdiˌæk ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
an island in the Gulf of Alaska off southern Alaska, known for its historical Russian settlement in 1784
