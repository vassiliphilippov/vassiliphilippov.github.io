objectivism

noun
poetry movement, 1930s
9.8
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪˌvɪzəm / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪˌvɪzəm / (BrE)
a modernist poetry movement of the 1930s that emphasized clarity, sincerity, and treating the poem as an object
