indefinably

adverb
unclearly or vaguely
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfaɪnəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be clearly described or defined

Examples

To be indefinably drawn
He was indefinably drawn to the painting, though he could not explain why.
The atmosphere in the room was indefinably tense as everyone waited for the announcement.
Her smile was indefinably sad, leaving him unsure of what she was feeling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
  • synonym: vaguely - in a way that is not clear or definite
