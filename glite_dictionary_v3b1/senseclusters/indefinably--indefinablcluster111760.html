Examples

To be indefinably drawn
He was indefinably drawn to the painting, though he could not explain why.
The atmosphere in the room was indefinably tense as everyone waited for the announcement.
Her smile was indefinably sad, leaving him unsure of what she was feeling.

Synonyms

  • inexpressibly
    7.7 0.14/m
    inexpressibly describes a feeling that cannot be told in words, while indefinably focuses on being hard to define clearly.
  • ineffably
    9.8 0.06/m

  • unutterably
    8.1 0.11/m

  • unspeakably
    6.8 0.44/m
    unspeakably describes an extreme quality often too bad or shocking to mention, while indefinably means unclear and hard to define.
