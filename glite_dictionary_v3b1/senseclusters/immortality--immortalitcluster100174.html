immortality

noun
eternal or unending life
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmɔːrˈtæləti /; / ɪˌmɔrˈtæləti / (AmE), / ɪˌmɔːˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the state of living forever or never dying (including biological, spiritual, or mythological life after death)

Examples

Biological immortality
Her groundbreaking discovery could be the key to achieving biological immortality.
Many religions promise immortality to those who follow their teachings.
The ancient myth speaks of gods blessed with immortality and unlimited powers.
Many myths and legends have explored the concept of immortality.
The search for immortality has fascinated philosophers and scientists for centuries.
The vampire's immortality allowed him to witness centuries of change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternity - endless or infinite time
  • antonym: mortality - the state of being subject to death
