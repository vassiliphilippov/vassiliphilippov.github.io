crossed eye

noun
eye turning inward; type of strabismus
5.8 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / krÉ”st aÉª / (AmE), / krÉ’st aÉª / (BrE)
A condition in which one or both eyes turn inward toward the nose; a form of strabismus.

Examples

A crossed eye condition
Her crossed eye became noticeable in photographs taken from the side.
Many people with a crossed eye can benefit from vision therapy.
The doctor diagnosed the child with a crossed eye and recommended corrective glasses.

Synonyms

    9.9 0.00/m

  • convergent strabismus
    8.3 0.00/m
    convergent strabismus names inward eye turning in clinical language, while crossed eye is the everyday, simple phrase people use.
