noncompletion

noun
failure to finish
6.3 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àån…ënk…ômÀàpliÀê É…ôn / (AmE), / Àån…ínk…ômÀàpliÀê É…ôn / (BrE)
the state or fact of not finishing something, especially a course, task, or requirement

Examples

The noncompletion of tasks
The noncompletion of the training program meant she could not receive her certificate.
Students with repeated noncompletion of assignments may need additional support.
His noncompletion of the contract led to a penalty from the company.

Synonyms

  • incompletion
    5.6 0.03/m
    incompletion is a general state of being unfinished, while noncompletion refers often to failing to finish a specific task or required course.
  • incompleteness
    5.9 0.73/m
    incompleteness describes lacking parts or quality, while noncompletion names not finishing an action, task, or required course.

Related Terms

  • antonym: completion - the act of finishing something
  • synonym: incompletion - the state of being unfinished or incomplete
