urological

adjective
urinary system medical
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊrəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the medical study or treatment of the urinary system and its disorders

Examples

Urological clinic
The patient was referred to a urological clinic for further tests.
Advances in urological surgery have improved outcomes for many bladder conditions.
He specializes in urological diseases affecting both men and women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nephrological - relating to the medical study of the kidneys
  • synonym: urinary - relating to urine or the organs that produce and carry it
