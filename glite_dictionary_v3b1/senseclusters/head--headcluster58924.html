head

noun
person in charge of a school
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
the person who is in charge of a group, organization, or activity; a leader or principal

Examples

Head of the company
If you have any concerns, please contact the head of the school.
The head addressed all the students during assembly.
The new head introduced several reforms to improve education standards.
As head of the committee, he is responsible for all major decisions.
She was appointed head of the marketing department last year.
The school head announced a new policy for students and staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - a person with a high rank or in charge of an organization
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads a group, organization, or country
  • synonym: principal - the person with the highest authority in a school
  • antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority or control of another within an organization
