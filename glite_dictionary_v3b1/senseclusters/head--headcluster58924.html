Synonyms

  • leader
    1.0 252/m
    leader inspires and guides people and often wins support, while head is the formal person who is in charge.
  • chief
    1.9 90/m
    chief holds the highest authority in a group and makes final decisions, while head is the person in charge.
  • principal
    3.6 38/m
    principal is the main or most important person in many settings, while head usually means the official person who runs the group.
  • chairperson
    3.5 2.81/m
    chairperson leads meetings and represents a board, while head often refers to the overall leader of an organization or activity.
  • boss
    1.0 68/m
    boss gives orders to workers and manages day-to-day tasks, while head names the person who holds top authority in a group.
