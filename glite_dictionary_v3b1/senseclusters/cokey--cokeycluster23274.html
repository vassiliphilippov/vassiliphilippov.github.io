cokey

adjective
slang; like on cocaine
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊki / (AmE), / ˈkəʊki / (BrE)
slang describing someone who is under the influence of cocaine or behaving in a way that resembles its effects

Examples

Cokey behavior
After disappearing for a while, he came back looking a bit cokey and talking nonstop.
Her cokey behavior at the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
He was acting so cokey that people wondered if he had taken something.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sober - not affected by drugs or alcohol
  • synonym: wired - very energetic or nervous, often due to stimulants
