the last thing one needs

x
something particularly undesirable or unwelcome
3.9
Pronunciation: / ðə læst θɪŋ wʌn nidz / (AmE), / ðə lɑːst θɪŋ wʌn niːdz / (BrE)
used to refer to something that is very undesirable or unwelcome in a particular situation

Examples

The last thing one needs now
A power outage during the big game is definitely the last thing one needs.
Another delay is the last thing one needs when trying to catch a connecting flight.
With all the chaos at work, a car breakdown is the last thing one needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: the icing on the cake - something that makes a good situation even better or, ironically, a bad situation worse
  • synonym: the last straw - the final problem in a series of problems that makes a situation unbearable
