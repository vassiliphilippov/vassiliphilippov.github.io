server

noun
person serving food in a restaurant
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːvɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to serve food and drinks to customers at their tables in a restaurant or similar establishment

Examples

Restaurant server
As a server, she often works double shifts at the busy bistro.
The server brought our drinks quickly and with a smile.
We thanked our server for the excellent service and left a generous tip.
