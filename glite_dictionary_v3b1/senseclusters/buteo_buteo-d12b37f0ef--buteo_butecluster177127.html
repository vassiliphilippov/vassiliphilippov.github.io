Buteo buteo

noun
the common buzzard, a European bird of prey
Pronunciation: / ˌbjuːti.oʊ ˈbjuːti.oʊ / (AmE), / ˌbjuːtɪəʊ ˈbjuːtɪəʊ / (BrE)
The common buzzard, a medium-sized bird of prey native to Europe and parts of Asia, known for its broad wings and rounded tail.
