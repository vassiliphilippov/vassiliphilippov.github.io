curved shape

noun
a form with a continuously bending outline
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜrvd ˌʃeɪp / (AmE), / ˈkɜːvd ˌʃeɪp / (BrE)
a form or outline that bends continuously without sharp angles, traced by a point that changes direction as it moves

Examples

A curved shape
A rainbow forms a beautiful curved shape across the sky after a storm.
The architect designed the building with a distinctive curved shape to make it stand out.
The artist used a curved shape to suggest movement and fluidity in the sculpture.

  • synonym: curve - a smooth, continuously bending line
