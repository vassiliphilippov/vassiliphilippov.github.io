romancer

noun
pursuer of romance
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmænsər / (AmE), / rəʊˈmænsə / (BrE)
a person who pursues romantic relationships or charms others, or tells romantic or imaginative stories

Examples

A charming romancer
The romancer swept her off her feet with grand gestures and poetic words.
As a skilled romancer, he was always the center of attention at parties.
Many novels feature a daring romancer who seeks adventure and love.
He was known as a romancer who charmed everyone he met.
The famous romancer captivated his audience with tales of adventure and love.
She called him a romancer because of his poetic words and dreamy ideas about relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flirt - a person who behaves as if attracted to others playfully
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts things as they are and deals with them practically
  • synonym: storyteller - a person who tells stories
