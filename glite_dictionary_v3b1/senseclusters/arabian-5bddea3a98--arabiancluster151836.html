Arabian

adjective
relating to Arabian horses
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Arabian breed of horse, known for its endurance, high tail carriage, and distinctive head shape.

Examples

Arabian horse breed
Many horse shows feature competitions just for Arabian horses.
She rides an Arabian stallion at the equestrian club.
The Arabian breed is famous for its stamina and elegance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arabian horse - a breed of horse renowned for endurance and refined features
