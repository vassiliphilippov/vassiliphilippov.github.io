revoicing

noun
restating others' words
5.7
Pronunciation: / riˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈvɔɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of restating or paraphrasing someone’s words, especially to clarify or share them with a group

Examples

Teacher revoicing
The teacher used revoicing to help the class understand the student's answer.
In group discussions, revoicing can ensure everyone’s ideas are heard and clarified.
Effective revoicing by the facilitator encouraged more students to participate in the conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraphrasing - expressing the meaning of something using different words
  • synonym: summarizing - giving a brief statement of the main points
