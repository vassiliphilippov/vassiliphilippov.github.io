sanguinely

adverb
confidently, optimistically
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋɡwɪnli / (AmE), / ˈsaŋɡwɪnli / (BrE)
in a hopeful, confident, or optimistic manner

Examples

To speak sanguinely
Despite the setbacks, she continued to speak sanguinely about their chances of success.
He sanguinely predicted that the project would finish ahead of schedule.
The coach addressed the team sanguinely, encouraging them to believe in victory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidently - in a way that shows certainty or self-assurance
  • synonym: optimistically - in a way that shows hope or confidence about the future
  • antonym: pessimistically - in a way that expects the worst or sees the negative side
