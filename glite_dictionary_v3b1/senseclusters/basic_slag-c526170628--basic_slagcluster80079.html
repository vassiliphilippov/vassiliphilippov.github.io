basic slag

noun
fertilizer byproduct from steelmaking
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsɪk slæɡ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsɪk slaɡ / (BrE)
a byproduct of steelmaking containing low silica and high calcium phosphate, used as a fertilizer

Examples

Basic slag fertilizer
Farmers sometimes spread basic slag on acidic soil to correct its pH balance.
The steel mill produced large quantities of basic slag as a byproduct.
The use of basic slag has improved crop yields in many agricultural areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slag - stony waste matter separated from metals during smelting
