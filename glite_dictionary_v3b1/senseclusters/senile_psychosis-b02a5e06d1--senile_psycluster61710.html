senile psychosis

noun
dementia in the aged due to brain degeneration
8.9 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsiː.naɪl saɪˈkoʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˈsiː.naɪl saɪˈkəʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
A type of dementia occurring in old age, caused by degeneration of the brain that is not linked to cerebrovascular disease.
