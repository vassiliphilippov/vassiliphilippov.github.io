wander the streets

verb
roam aimlessly in urban areas
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑndɚ ðə strits / (AmE), / ˈwɒndə ðə striːts / (BrE)
to walk around urban areas without any particular purpose or destination, often over an extended period

Examples

To wander the streets alone
After losing his job, he would often wander the streets late at night, unsure of what to do next.
The old man was spotted wandering the streets in a daze, looking for his childhood home.
Tourists are warned not to wander the streets alone after dark in unfamiliar neighborhoods.
