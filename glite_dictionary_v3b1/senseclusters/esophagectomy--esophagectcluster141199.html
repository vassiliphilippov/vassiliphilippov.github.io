esophagectomy

noun
removal of esophagus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˌsɑfəˈdʒɛktəmi / (AmE), / ɪˌsɒfəˈdʒɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of the esophagus

Examples

An esophagectomy operation
After being diagnosed with cancer, he had an esophagectomy to remove the affected tissue.
Recovery from an esophagectomy can take several weeks and requires careful monitoring.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of an esophagectomy before the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gastrectomy - surgical removal of all or part of the stomach
