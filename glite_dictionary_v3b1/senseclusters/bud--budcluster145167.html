bud

noun
plant growth swelling; develops leaf or flower
1.0 10/m
Pronunciation: / bÊŒd / (AmE), / bÊŒd / (BrE)
a small swelling on a plant that will grow into a leaf, flower, or shoot

Examples

A flower bud
In early spring, tiny buds appeared on the bare branches of the tree.
She carefully examined the bud to see if it would bloom soon.
The rose bud opened slowly in the morning sunlight.
In spring, green buds appear on the trees before the leaves unfurl.
She pinned a small bud behind her ear for a fresh, floral scent.
The rose bud opened fully after a few warm days.

Synonyms

  • bouton
    9.9 0.00/m
    bouton is a botanical term for a small plant swelling seen before a flower opens, while bud is the common everyday name.
  • knop
    9.9 0.00/m
    knop is a small rounded swelling often used to mean a knob in older texts, while bud is the usual plant term.
