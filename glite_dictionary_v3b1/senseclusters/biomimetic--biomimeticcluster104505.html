biomimetic

noun
nature-inspired device
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊmɪˈmɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪəʊmɪˈmɛtɪk / (BrE)
a material, device, or system designed to imitate the structure or function of a biological organism or process

Examples

A biomimetic material
The engineers developed a biomimetic that mimics the flexibility of human skin.
Researchers are testing a new biomimetic to improve water filtration systems.
A biomimetic inspired by shark skin helps reduce drag on boats.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificial - made by humans rather than occurring naturally
  • synonym: bionic - having artificial body parts that imitate natural ones
