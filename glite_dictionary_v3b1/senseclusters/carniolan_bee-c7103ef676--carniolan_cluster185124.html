Carniolan bee

noun
subspecies of gentle European honeybee
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrniˈoʊlən biː / (AmE), / ˌkɑːnɪˈəʊlɪən biː / (BrE)
a greyish, highly productive subspecies of European honeybee known for its gentle temperament

Examples

The Carniolan bee colony
Beekeepers prize the Carniolan bee for both its gentle nature and its ability to build up colonies rapidly.
Many apiaries in Europe now prefer to raise Carniolan bees over other honeybee subspecies.
The Carniolan bee is well suited to cold climates and shows a strong resistance to several bee diseases.
