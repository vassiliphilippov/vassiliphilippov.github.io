hydro

x
water prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'water', used in compound words to indicate a relation to water

Examples

Hydro prefix word
The prefix hydro is found in words like hydroelectric and hydrotherapy.
Scientists often use hydro to form terms related to water in chemistry and biology.
In the word hydrology, hydro refers to the study of water.
