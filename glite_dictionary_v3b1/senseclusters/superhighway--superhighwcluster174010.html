superhighway

noun
broad highway for high-speed traffic
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌhaɪ.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌhaɪ.weɪ / (BrE)
A very wide and well-constructed road designed for high-speed traffic, often with multiple lanes and limited access points.

Examples

Busy superhighway traffic
Driving on the superhighway at night requires extra caution due to higher speeds.
Plans are underway to expand the superhighway to include more lanes for increased capacity.
The new superhighway connects the two cities and has greatly reduced travel time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeway - a highway without tolls or intersections, designed for fast traffic
