reputational

adjective
concerning reputation
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛpjəˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛpjʊˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the reputation or public perception of someone or something

Examples

Reputational risks
The company faced significant reputational risks after the data breach.
A reputational crisis can affect a business for many years.
They hired a consultant to manage their reputational image online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disreputable - having a bad reputation
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions that are generally held about someone or something
