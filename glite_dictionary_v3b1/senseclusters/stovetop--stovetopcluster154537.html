stovetop

noun
surface for cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊvˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈstəʊvˌtɒp / (BrE)
the flat upper surface of a stove where pots and pans are placed for cooking or heating food

Examples

A pot on the stovetop
She placed the pot of soup on the stovetop to let it simmer for an hour.
After cleaning the stovetop, he noticed it looked almost brand new.
Always turn off the stovetop before leaving the kitchen for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooktop - a flat cooking surface with burners or heating elements
  • antonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for baking or roasting food
