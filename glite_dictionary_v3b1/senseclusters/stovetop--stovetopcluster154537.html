stovetop

noun
surface for cooking
2.4 0.83/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆstoÊŠvËŒtÉ‘p / (AmE), / ËˆstÉ™ÊŠvËŒtÉ’p / (BrE)
the flat upper surface of a stove where pots and pans are placed for cooking or heating food

Examples

A pot on the stovetop
She placed the pot of soup on the stovetop to let it simmer for an hour.
After cleaning the stovetop, he noticed it looked almost brand new.
Always turn off the stovetop before leaving the kitchen for safety.

Synonyms

  • hob
    5.1 0.03/m
    hob is the British word for the flat cooking surface on a cooker, while stovetop is the common term for a stove's top.
  • cooktop
    3.6 0.55/m
    cooktop is a flat, built-in surface with burners set into a counter, while stovetop is the top surface of a freestanding stove.

Related Terms
