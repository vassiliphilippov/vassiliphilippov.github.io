answerless

adjective
without a reply
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈænsərləs / (AmE), / ˈɑːnsələs / (BrE)
not giving or showing any reply; silent or unresponsive

Examples

Answerless stare
He stared at the letter with an answerless expression, unsure how to respond.
The room remained answerless after she asked her difficult question.
Her message was met with an answerless silence that made her feel ignored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: silent - not speaking or making noise
  • synonym: unresponsive - not reacting or replying
