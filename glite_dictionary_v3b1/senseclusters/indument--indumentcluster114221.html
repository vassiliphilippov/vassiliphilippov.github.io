indument

noun
fine hairy or scaly covering (in plants or insects)
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndjəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪndjʊmɛnt / (BrE)
a covering of fine hairs or sometimes scales, especially on a leaf or insect

Examples

Fine indument on leaf
Some insects possess a protective indument made up of fine hairs or tiny scales.
The indument on the stem helps to reduce water loss in arid environments.
The leaf surface was covered in a dense indument that gave it a silvery appearance.
