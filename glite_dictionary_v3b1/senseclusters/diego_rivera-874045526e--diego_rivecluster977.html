Diego Rivera

proper noun
Mexican muralist and painter (1886–1957)
Pronunciation: / ˈdjeɪgoʊ rɪˈvɛrə / (AmE), / ˈdiːəɡəʊ rɪˈvɛərə / (BrE)
(1886–1957) a Mexican painter renowned for his large mural works and his influence on modern art, especially noted for his socialist themes.

Examples

Diego Rivera murals
Frida Kahlo and Diego Rivera were both prominent figures in 20th-century art.
Many tourists visit Detroit to see the murals painted by Diego Rivera.
The influence of Diego Rivera on Mexican art remains significant today.
