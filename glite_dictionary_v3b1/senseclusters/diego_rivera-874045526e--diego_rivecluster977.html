Diego Rivera

proper noun
Mexican muralist and painter (1886–1957)
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdjeɪgoʊ rɪˈvɛrə / (AmE), / ˈdiːəɡəʊ rɪˈvɛərə / (BrE)
(1886–1957) a Mexican painter renowned for his large mural works and his influence on modern art, especially noted for his socialist themes.

Examples

Diego Rivera murals
