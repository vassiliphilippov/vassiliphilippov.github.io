castrate

verb
remove the testicles of a male
7.8 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstreɪt / (AmE), / ˈkæstreɪt / (BrE)
to remove the reproductive organs (testicles or ovaries) of a person or animal, making reproduction impossible

Examples

He learned how to castrate sheep safely during his agricultural training.

Veterinarians routinely castrate male cats and dogs to control the pet population.

Many shelters require that adopted animals be castrated or spayed before going to a new home.
The veterinarian will castrate the female rabbit tomorrow as part of the procedure.
