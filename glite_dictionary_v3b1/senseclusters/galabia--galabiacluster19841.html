galabia

noun
traditional loose robe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɑːbiə / (AmE), / ɡəˈlɑːbiə / (BrE)
a long, loose-fitting traditional robe worn by men or women in parts of North Africa and the Middle East

Examples

A white galabia
He wore a white galabia while visiting the market.
Many villagers dress in a galabia to stay cool in the desert heat.
She admired the colorful embroidery on the galabia displayed in the shop window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kaftan - a long, loose garment with wide sleeves, worn in some Middle Eastern countries
  • synonym: tunic - a loose garment, typically sleeveless and reaching to the knees
