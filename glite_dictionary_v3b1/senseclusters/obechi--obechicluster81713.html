obechi

noun
West African tree; source of pale soft wood

A large West African tree (Triplochiton scleroxylon) with palmately lobed leaves and cymose clusters of small white flowers, valued for its soft pale wood.

Examples

Soft obechi wood
Carpenters value obechi for its ease of working and uniform texture.
Extensive logging has threatened some obechi forests in West Africa.
The soft, pale wood of obechi is often used for making plywood and furniture.
