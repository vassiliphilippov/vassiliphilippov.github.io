Synonyms

  • permission
    1.0 40/m
    permission is formal or informal allowance to act, while authorization is an official grant of power or formal permission by authorities.
  • approval
    1.6 30/m
    approval is an official agreement that something is acceptable, while authorization is formal permission or power to carry out an action.
  • sanction
    5.7 2/m
    sanction is an official permission often used by governments or authorities, while authorization is the formal grant of permission or power.
  • consent
    3.2 20/m
    consent is an explicit personal or group agreement to allow something, while authorization is an official grant of permission or power.
  • authority
    3.1 18/m
    authority is the person or body that has legal power to decide, while authorization is the formal permission or power they give.
