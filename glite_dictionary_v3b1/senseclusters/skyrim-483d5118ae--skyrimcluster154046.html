Skyrim

proper noun
fantasy game region
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪrɪm / (AmE), / ˈskaɪrɪm / (BrE)
a fictional northern region in a popular fantasy video game series, known for its snowy landscapes and ancient ruins

Examples

The land of Skyrim
Many players enjoy exploring the vast world of Skyrim in the video game.
The snowy mountains of Skyrim are filled with ancient secrets and dangerous creatures.
He spent hours completing quests across Skyrim and discovering new locations.
