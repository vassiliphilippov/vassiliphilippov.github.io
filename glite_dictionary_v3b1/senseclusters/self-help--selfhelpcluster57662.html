self-help

noun
improving oneself without outside help
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈhɛlp / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈhɛlp / (BrE)
The act of improving or helping oneself without relying on others; the practice of taking independent action to solve one's own problems, often through books, courses, or techniques.

Examples

Self-help books
Many people turn to self-help books when they want to improve their lives.
She credits her success to self-help techniques she learned online.
The self-help industry has grown rapidly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-improvement - the process of making oneself better, especially intellectually or morally
