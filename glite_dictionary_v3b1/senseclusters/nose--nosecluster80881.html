nose

noun
instinctive ability to detect or recognize
3.4
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
an instinctive ability to detect or recognize something, especially in a particular subject or area

Examples

A nose for news
His nose for trouble often got him involved in dangerous situations.
Journalists with a nose for a good story can make a real difference.
She has a remarkable nose for finding the best restaurants in the city.
