telepathize

verb
communicate by telepathy
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɛˈlɛpəˌθaɪz /; / təˈlɛpəˌθaɪz / (AmE), / tɛˈlɛpəˌθaɪz /; / tɛˈlɛpəθaɪz / (BrE)
to communicate directly from one mind to another, either by telepathy or using technology

Related Terms

  • synonym: brain–computer interface - a device or system that enables direct communication between a brain and an external device, used here as the technology enabling telepathic transmission
  • synonym: telepathy - the ability to communicate by transmitting thoughts directly without using sensory channels
