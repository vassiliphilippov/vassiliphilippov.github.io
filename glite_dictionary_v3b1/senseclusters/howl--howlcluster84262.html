howl

verb
laugh loudly and uncontrollably
1.8
Pronunciation: / haʊl / (AmE), / haʊl / (BrE)
to laugh loudly and uncontrollably

Examples

To howl with laughter
The comedian's jokes made the entire audience howl with laughter.
We all howled at her hilarious impersonation of the teacher.
When he heard the news, he began to howl so hard he could barely breathe.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: chuckle - to laugh quietly or to oneself
  • synonym: roar - to laugh very loudly
