riverway

noun
path beside river
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌweɪ / (BrE)
a path or landscaped area alongside a river, designed for walking or recreation

Examples

Scenic riverway walk
Joggers enjoy the scenic riverway every morning before work.
The city built a new riverway to encourage outdoor activities along the waterfront.
Families often picnic on the grassy areas beside the riverway during summer weekends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promenade - a public place for walking, often along water
  • antonym: road - a wide way leading from one place to another, especially for vehicles
  • synonym: walkway - a path for walking, especially in a park or garden
