freesia

noun
a fragrant flowering plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːʒə / (AmE), / ˈfriːzɪə / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Freesia, native to southern Africa, known for its fragrant, one-sided clusters of tubular yellow, white, or pink flowers.

Examples

A freesia flower
Many gardeners enjoy growing freesias for their beautiful, delicate flowers.
She placed a bouquet of freesia on the table, filling the room with a sweet scent.
The pink freesia bloom became the centerpiece of the wedding decorations.
