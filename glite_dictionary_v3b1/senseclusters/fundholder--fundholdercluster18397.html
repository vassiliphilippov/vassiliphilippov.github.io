fundholder

noun
money manager
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person or organization that manages or controls a sum of money for a specific purpose

Examples

Fundholder manages money
The fundholder must ensure that all expenditures are properly documented and justified.
As a fundholder, the organization is accountable for the distribution of grant money.
Each project team reports their expenses to the central fundholder every quarter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beneficiary - a person who receives money or benefits from a fund
  • synonym: trustee - a person or organization that holds and manages assets for others
