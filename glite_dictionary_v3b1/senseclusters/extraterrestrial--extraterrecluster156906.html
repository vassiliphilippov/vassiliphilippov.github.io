extraterrestrial

noun
a being from beyond Earth
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.strə.təˈrɛs.tri.əl / (AmE), / ˌɛk.strə.təˈrɛs.tri.əl / (BrE)
a being or life form originating from outside the Earth or its atmosphere, especially in science fiction or discussions of space

Examples

An extraterrestrial being
Many scientists believe it is possible for extraterrestrial life to exist elsewhere in the universe.
Some people claim to have seen extraterrestrial spacecraft in the night sky.
The movie features a friendly extraterrestrial who visits Earth.
