duteous

adjective
obedient out of duty or respect
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈduːtiəs / (AmE), / ˈdjuːtɪəs / (BrE)
Willingly obedient, respectful, and showing a sense of duty or obligation.

Examples

A duteous child
His duteous attitude earned him the admiration of his teachers.
She remained duteous to her parents throughout their old age.
The duteous servant followed every instruction without complaint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dutiful - obedient and showing a sense of duty
