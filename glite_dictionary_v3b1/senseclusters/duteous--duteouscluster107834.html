duteous

adjective
obedient out of duty or respect
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀêti…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀêt…™…ôs / (BrE)
Willingly obedient, respectful, and showing a sense of duty or obligation.

Examples

A duteous child

Synonyms

  • obedient
    1.0 3.14/m
