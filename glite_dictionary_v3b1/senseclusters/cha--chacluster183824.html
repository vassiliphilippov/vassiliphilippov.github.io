cha

noun
sharp quick sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɑ / (AmE), / tʃɑː / (BrE)
a short, sharp sound, often used in writing to imitate a quick or sudden noise.

Examples

Cha sound on floor
The dancer's shoes made a crisp cha on the wooden floor with every step.
He wrote cha in the comic to show the sudden sound of the door slamming.
A loud cha echoed through the hall as the drumstick hit the cymbal.
