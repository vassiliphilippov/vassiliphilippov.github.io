A fruit formed from the merging of several separate ovaries of a single flower, resulting in a cluster of small fruits, as in blackberries and raspberries.

Examples

An aggregate fruit cluster
A blackberry is an example of an aggregate fruit composed of many small drupelets.
The raspberry, an aggregate fruit, contains multiple fused segments, each with its own seed.
Unlike a simple fruit, an aggregate fruit develops from several ovaries in one flower.
