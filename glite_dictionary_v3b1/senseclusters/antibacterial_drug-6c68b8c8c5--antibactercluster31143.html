antibacterial drug

noun
a drug that kills or stops bacteria
Pronunciation: / ˌænti bəˈktɪriəl drʌg / (AmE), / ˌænti bækˈtɪəriəl drʌg / (BrE)
a drug that destroys bacteria or inhibits their growth

Examples

An antibacterial drug treatment

The doctor prescribed an antibacterial drug to treat her infection.
