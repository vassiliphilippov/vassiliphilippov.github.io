countrywide

adjective
across an entire country or nation
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntriˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌntrɪˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Happening or existing throughout an entire country or nation.

Examples

A countrywide event
The election results were broadcast on television countrywide.
The government launched a countrywide vaccination program to protect all citizens.
There was a countrywide shortage of fuel after the crisis.

  • synonym: nationwide - happening or existing throughout a nation
