chink

verb
make a high-pitched ringing sound
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋk / (AmE), / tʃɪŋk / (BrE)
to make or emit a thin, high-pitched ringing sound, like that of small metal objects striking each other

Examples

To chink glasses
A distant bell chinked faintly in the evening air.
As she walked, her bracelets chinked with each step.
The coins chinked together as he dropped them into the jar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clink - to make a short, sharp ringing sound, as of glasses striking together
  • synonym: tinkle - to make a series of light, clear ringing sounds
