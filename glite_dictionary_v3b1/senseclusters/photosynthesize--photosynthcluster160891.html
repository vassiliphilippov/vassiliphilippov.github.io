photosynthesize

verb
to make food from sunlight
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈsɪnθɪsaɪz / (BrE)
to produce food and energy from sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide, as plants do

Examples
