heroize

verb
to make into a hero
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪroʊˌaɪz / (AmE), / ˈhɪərəʊˌaɪz / (BrE)
to treat or represent someone as a hero, often by emphasizing their admirable qualities or achievements

Examples

To heroize a person
The documentary tends to heroize the scientist, focusing on her groundbreaking discoveries.
Some historians argue that we should not heroize political leaders without considering their flaws.
The novel heroizes its protagonist, portraying him as a symbol of courage and hope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glorify - to praise or honor greatly
  • antonym: vilify - to speak or write about someone in a very negative way
