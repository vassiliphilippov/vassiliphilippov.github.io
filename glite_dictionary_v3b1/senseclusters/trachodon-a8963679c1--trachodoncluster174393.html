Trachodon

noun
obsolete dinosaur genus
Pronunciation: / ˈtræk.əˌdɑn / (AmE), / ˈtrak.ə.dɒn / (BrE)
a genus of extinct duck-billed dinosaurs (hadrosaurids) from the Late Cretaceous, now considered obsolete in scientific classification

Examples

Trachodon dinosaur fossils
Fossils once assigned to Trachodon have since been reclassified into other genera.
In early paleontology, Trachodon was thought to be a typical duck-billed dinosaur.
The name Trachodon is now considered outdated by most paleontologists.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Edmontosaurus - a genus of duck-billed dinosaurs formerly assigned to Trachodon
  • synonym: hadrosaur - a family of duck-billed herbivorous dinosaurs
