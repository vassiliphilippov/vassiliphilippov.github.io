brittle

verb
to become fragile
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɪtəl / (BrE)
to make or become hard and likely to break easily

Examples

To brittle leaves
The cold wind brittled the leaves, making them crumble at a touch.
Over time, exposure to sunlight brittled the plastic toys in the garden.
If you heat glass too quickly, it can brittle and shatter unexpectedly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harden - to make or become hard or harder
  • antonym: soften - to make or become soft or softer
