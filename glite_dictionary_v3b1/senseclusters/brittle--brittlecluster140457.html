brittle

verb
to become fragile
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɪt.əl/ (AmE), /ˈbrɪt.əl/ (BrE)

Examples

To brittle leaves
The cold wind brittled the leaves, making them crumble at a touch.
Over time, exposure to sunlight brittled the plastic toys in the garden.
If you heat glass too quickly, it can brittle and shatter unexpectedly.
