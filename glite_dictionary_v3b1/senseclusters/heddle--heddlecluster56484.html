heddle

verb
to thread loom heddles
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.əl/ (AmE), /ˈhed.əl/ (BrE)
to pass or thread warp threads through the heddles of a loom or to fit a loom with heddles

Examples

To heddle threads
Before starting the weaving process, she carefully heddled each warp thread.
The instructor showed the students how to heddle the loom for their first project.
It took him an hour to heddle all the threads for the new pattern.
