drip culture

noun
fashion subculture valuing flashy style
8.5
Pronunciation: / drɪp ˈkʌltʃər / (AmE), / drɪp ˈkʌltʃə / (BrE)
An online or street-fashion subculture that values conspicuous, trend-driven displays of clothing, accessories, and visible wealth.

Examples

Street drip culture
Social media trends celebrated bold outfits and luxury brands, epitomizing drip culture among younger shoppers.
Retailers use influencer photos to target audiences drawn to drip culture and expensive streetwear drops.
Critics argue that drip culture encourages conspicuous consumption and blurs the line between style and status.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drip - slang for stylish, fashionable clothing or overall flashy personal style
  • synonym: streetwear - casual clothing style influenced by urban culture and skate or hip-hop fashion
