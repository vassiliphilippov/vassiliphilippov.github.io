expansive

adjective
friendly, open, and communicative
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspænsɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɑːnsɪv / (BrE)
friendly and open, willing to talk and share feelings or opinions freely

Examples

Expansive personality
After a few glasses of wine, she became quite expansive and shared stories from her past.
His expansive mood made everyone at the dinner table feel welcome and comfortable.
The boss grew expansive when discussing his vision for the company's future.
