right hemisphere

noun
the right half of the brain
5.9 3.7
Pronunciation: /raɪt ˈhem.ɪ.sfɪr/ /; / /raɪt ˈhem.ə.sfɪr/ (AmE), /raɪt ˈhem.ɪ.sfɪə/ /; / /raɪt ˈhem.ə.sfɪə/ (BrE)
the half of the brain located to the right of the corpus callosum, which controls the left side of the body and is associated with certain cognitive functions

Examples
