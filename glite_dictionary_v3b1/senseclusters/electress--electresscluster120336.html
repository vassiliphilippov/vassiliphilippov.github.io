electress

noun
wife of an elector
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrəs / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪs / (BrE)
the wife of a prince who had the right to elect the Holy Roman Emperor

Examples

The royal electress

As electress of Saxony, she was known for her charitable work.
