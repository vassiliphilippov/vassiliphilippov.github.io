air attack

noun
attack using military aircraft
Pronunciation: / ɛr əˈtæk / (AmE), / eə əˈtæk / (BrE)
an attack on a target using military aircraft; a strike carried out by planes against forces or installations on the ground or sea

Examples

An air attack strike
Civilians took shelter as the air attack began at dawn.
The city was heavily damaged during the air attack last spring.
The commander ordered an air attack on enemy positions along the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air raid - an attack in which bombs are dropped from aircraft on a ground target
  • antonym: ground attack - a military attack carried out by troops on the ground
