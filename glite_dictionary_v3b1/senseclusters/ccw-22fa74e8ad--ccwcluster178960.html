CCW

proper noun
arms control treaty
9.4
Pronunciation: / si si ˈdʌbəl.juː / (AmE), / siː siː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
an international treaty, the Convention on Certain Conventional Weapons, that restricts or bans specific types of conventional weapons

Examples

The CCW treaty
The United Nations held a meeting to discuss updates to the CCW regarding new weapon technologies.
Many countries have signed the CCW to limit the use of certain dangerous weapons in conflict.
Debates about autonomous weapons systems are ongoing within the framework of the CCW.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Geneva Conventions - international treaties on the treatment of war victims
  • synonym: treaty - a formal agreement between countries
