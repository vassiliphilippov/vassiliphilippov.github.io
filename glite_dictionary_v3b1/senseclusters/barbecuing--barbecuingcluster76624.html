barbecuing

noun
outdoor cooking over an open fire
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbɪkjuɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪkjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of cooking food, especially meat, on a grill or revolving spit outdoors over an open fire

Examples

Outdoor barbecuing meat
Barbecuing requires patience and attention to control the temperature of the fire.
The tradition of barbecuing outdoors is popular in many cultures around the world.
They spent the afternoon barbecuing a whole pig for the neighborhood feast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grilling - cooking food on a grill over direct heat
