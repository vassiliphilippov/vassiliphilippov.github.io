barbecuing

noun
outdoor cooking over an open fire
4.1 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbɑːr.bə.kjuː.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbɑː.bə.kjuː.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
