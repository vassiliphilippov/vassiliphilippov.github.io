program music

noun
music evoking images or events
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm ˌmjuːzɪk /; / ˈproʊɡræm ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡram ˌmjuːzɪk /; / ˈprəʊɡræm ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
