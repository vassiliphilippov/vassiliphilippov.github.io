hairbreadth

noun
very small distance
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌbrɛdθ / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbrɛdθ / (BrE)
a very small distance or narrow margin, often used to describe a close call or near miss

Examples

A hairbreadth escape
The cyclist avoided the accident by a hairbreadth.
She won the race by a hairbreadth, just inches ahead of her rival.
The vase fell off the table but missed breaking by a hairbreadth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraction - a very small part or amount
  • antonym: mile - a large distance or margin
