buck

noun
gymnastics vaulting apparatus
Pronunciation: / bʌk / (AmE), / bʌk / (BrE)
a gymnastic apparatus resembling a horse without pommels and with one end elongated, used lengthwise for vaulting exercises

Examples

A gym buck
Before using the buck, make sure it is stable and properly positioned.
Older gymnasiums often have a wooden buck instead of a modern vaulting table.
The gymnast completed a perfect jump over the buck during her routine.
