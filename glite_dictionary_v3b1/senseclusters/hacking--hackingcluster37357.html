hacking

noun
unauthorized computer access
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhækɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Hacking a computer

Laws against hacking are becoming stricter to protect sensitive information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cybercrime - criminal activities involving computers or networks
