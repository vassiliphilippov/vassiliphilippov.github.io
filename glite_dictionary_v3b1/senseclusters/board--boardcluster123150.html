board

noun
supervisory committee or managing group
3.2 79/m
Pronunciation: / b…îrd / (AmE), / b…îÀêd / (BrE)
a group of people officially chosen to manage or oversee the activities of an organization, company, or institution

Examples

Board of directors
She was recently appointed to the board of directors.
The board meets once a month to discuss major policy decisions.
The company's board decided to approve the new merger plan.

Synonyms

  • board of directors
    3.1 4.38/m
    a board of directors runs a company's top management and makes official decisions, while a board is any chosen group that oversees an organization.
  • governing board
    3.5 0.78/m
    a governing board officially directs an institution's affairs and policies, while a board is a general group chosen to oversee an organization.
  • governing body
    3.4 1.56/m

  • board of trustees
    3.2 0.86/m
    a board of trustees often sets policies for schools or charities and holds legal responsibility, while a board is any chosen oversight group.
