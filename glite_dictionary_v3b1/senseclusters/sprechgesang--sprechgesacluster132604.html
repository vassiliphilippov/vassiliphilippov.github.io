sprechgesang

noun
musical vocalization between singing and speaking
Pronunciation: / ˈʃprɛkxgəˌzɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃprɛkxgəˌzaŋ / (BrE)
a style of dramatic vocalization in music that lies between singing and speaking, often used in opera or modern music
