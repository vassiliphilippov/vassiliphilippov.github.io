ill-treatment

noun
cruel or inhumane treatment
Pronunciation: / ɪlˈtritmənt / (AmE), / ɪlˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
cruel or inhumane treatment of a person or animal, especially over a period of time.

Examples

Reports of ill-treatment
Reports of ill-treatment in the institution led to a public outcry.
She spoke out against the ill-treatment of animals in entertainment.
The investigation exposed years of ill-treatment suffered by workers at the factory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - cruel or violent treatment of someone
  • antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
  • synonym: mistreatment - the action of treating someone badly or unfairly
