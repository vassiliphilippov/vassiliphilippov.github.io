Romany

noun
member of an itinerant ethnic group originally from India
8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.mə.niː/ /; / /ˈroʊ.mə.niː/ (AmE), /ˈrɒ.mə.niː/ /; / /ˈrəʊ.mə.niː/ (BrE)
a member of a traditionally itinerant people originating from northern India, now living worldwide, especially in Europe, North Africa, and North America, noted for their distinct language and culture

Examples
