get your shit together

verb
organize oneself or one's things
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt jɔr ʃɪt təˈɡɛðɚ / (AmE), / ɡɛt jɔː ʃɪt təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) To organize one's personal things or affairs; to become more organized or focused, especially after a period of disorder.

Examples

To get your shit together quickly
After missing several deadlines, he finally decided to get his shit together.
I spent the weekend trying to get my shit together after moving into the new apartment.
You really need to get your shit together before the new manager starts next week.
