velvet

verb
to make soft and smooth
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛlvət / (AmE), / ˈvɛlvɪt / (BrE)
to give something a soft, smooth texture like velvet, especially by a specific cooking or fabric treatment.

Examples

To velvet chicken
The chef velveted the chicken to keep it moist and tender in the stir-fry.
To achieve a delicate texture, the shrimp were velveted before cooking.
Some recipes call for meat to be velveted to enhance its smoothness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roughen - to make something less smooth or soft
  • synonym: tenderize - to make food softer and easier to eat
