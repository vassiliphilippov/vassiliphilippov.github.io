falls

noun
downward-bending outer petals (iris)
Pronunciation: / fɔlz / (AmE), / fɔːlz / (BrE)
the outer petals or sepals of a flower, especially of an iris, that bend downward

Examples

Iris falls
Bees often land on the colorful falls to access the nectar.
Each iris flower displays three upright standards and three drooping falls.
The characteristic purple falls of the iris add elegance to the garden.
