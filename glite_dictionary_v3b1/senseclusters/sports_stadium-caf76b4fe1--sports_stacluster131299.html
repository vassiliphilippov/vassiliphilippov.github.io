sports stadium

noun
large structure for open-air sports events
Pronunciation: / spɔrts ˈsteɪdiəm / (AmE), / spɔːts ˈsteɪdiəm / (BrE)
A large building or structure designed for open-air sports events or entertainments, typically with seating for spectators.

After the match, crowds flooded out of the sports stadium and into the nearby streets.
Many concerts are held in the sports stadium because it can accommodate a large audience.
The city opened a new sports stadium last year, attracting thousands of fans to every event.
