embrace

verb
adopt or accept an idea or belief
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈbreɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈbreɪs / (BrE)
to accept or adopt an idea, belief, system, or practice with enthusiasm or conviction

Examples

To embrace new ideas
After much thought, she decided to embrace a vegan lifestyle for health and ethical reasons.
He wholeheartedly embraced the philosophy of mindfulness and made it a part of his daily routine.
Many companies now embrace digital transformation to stay competitive in the modern market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adopt - to take up or start to use an idea, method, or course of action
  • antonym: reject - to refuse to accept, believe in, or agree with something
