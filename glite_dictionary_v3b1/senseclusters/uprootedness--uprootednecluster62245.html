uprootedness

noun
feeling of displacement
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈruːtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ʌpˈruːtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the condition of having been removed from one's home or familiar environment, often leading to feelings of displacement

Examples

Emotional uprootedness
After years of moving from city to city, she struggled with a sense of uprootedness.
The war caused widespread uprootedness among families forced to flee their homes.
He tried to overcome his uprootedness by building new friendships in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: displacement - the state of being forced to leave one's home or usual place
  • antonym: rootedness - a strong connection to one's home or environment
