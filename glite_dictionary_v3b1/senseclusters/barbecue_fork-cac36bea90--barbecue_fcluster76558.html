barbecue fork

noun
long fork for turning barbecue food
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː ˌfɔrk / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː ˌfɔːk / (BrE)
a long, two-pronged fork used for lifting and turning food while it is being cooked on a barbecue or grill

Examples

A barbecue fork tool
He used a barbecue fork to lift the steak off the grill.
Make sure to handle the barbecue fork carefully, as the prongs are very sharp.
The set includes a spatula, tongs, and a barbecue fork for outdoor cooking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fork - an implement with two or more prongs used for eating or serving food
