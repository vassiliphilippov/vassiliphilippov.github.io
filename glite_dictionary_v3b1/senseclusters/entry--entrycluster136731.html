entry

noun
doorway or passage for entering
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntri / (AmE), / ˈɛntri / (BrE)
a place or opening through which one can enter or leave; an entrance or passageway

Examples

Entry door

Fire regulations require that every building has a clearly marked entry and exit.
