Ruta graveolens

noun
European perennial herb (rue)
Pronunciation: / ˈruːtə ɡræviˈoʊlənz / (AmE), / ˈruːtə ˌɡrævɪˈəʊlɛnz / (BrE)
A strong-scented perennial European herb with grey-green, bitter-tasting leaves, sometimes used in traditional medicine and cooking.
