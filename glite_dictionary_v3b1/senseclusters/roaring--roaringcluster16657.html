Synonyms

  • roar
    1.0 7/m
    roar is a loud, deep, continuous sound often from animals or engines, while roaring describes that same strong, long noise.
  • bellow
    4.6 0.9/m
    bellow is a very loud, rough shout used by animals or people, while roaring is more often a powerful natural or machine noise.
  • rumble
    3.1 1/m
    rumble is a low, continuous sound you feel as a distant roll, while roaring is higher, louder, and more forceful.
  • boom
    2.1 9/m
    boom is a sudden, deep explosion-like sound that hits the ear, while roaring is long, steady, and more animal or wind-like.
