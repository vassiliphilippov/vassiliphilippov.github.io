roaring

noun
deep, loud, prolonged noise
2.1 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a deep, loud, and prolonged noise, often like an animal's roar or a powerful force

Examples

A roaring noise

A sudden roaring from the jungle startled the campers during the night.
The arena was filled with the roaring of the crowd as the team entered.
They heard a terrible roaring outside, as if a wild creature was approaching.
