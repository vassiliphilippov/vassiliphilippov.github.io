nondiscriminating

adjective
treating equally
4.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.dɪ.ˈskrɪ.mɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /nɑːn.də.ˈskrɪ.mə.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /nɒn.dɪ.ˈskrɪ.mɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /nɒn.də.ˈskrɪ.mə.neɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
not making distinctions or treating all people or things equally without bias or preference

Examples

Nondiscriminating policy
The hiring process was completely nondiscriminating, giving everyone an equal opportunity.
A nondiscriminating policy ensures that all students are treated fairly regardless of background.
The club has a nondiscriminating membership policy open to anyone interested.
