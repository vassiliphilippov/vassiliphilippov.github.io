coryza

noun
inflammation of the nasal mucous membrane
Pronunciation: / kəˈraɪzə / (AmE), / kəˈraɪzə / (BrE)
an inflammation of the mucous membrane in the nose, usually causing a runny nose or nasal congestion

Examples

Nasal coryza symptoms
Early symptoms of flu include fever, headache, and coryza.
Some allergy sufferers experience coryza with frequent sneezing and nasal discharge.
The child was absent from school due to a severe case of coryza.
