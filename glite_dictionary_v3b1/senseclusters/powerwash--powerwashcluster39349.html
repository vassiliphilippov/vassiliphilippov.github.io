powerwash

verb
factory-reset a device
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / c8pa8a59rccw5183 / (AmE), / c8pa8a59ccw5283 / (BrE)
(in computing, especially Chrome OS) to erase all user data and settings on a device and restore it to factory/default state; perform a factory reset.

Examples

To powerwash a Chromebook
After the malware infection, she had to powerwash the Chromebook to remove the corrupted user profile.
He powerwashed the laptop yesterday, returning it to factory settings before selling it to a friend.
If your Chromebook keeps freezing, consider powerwashing it after backing up important files to cloud storage.

Synonyms

  • clear
    4.9 1.02/m
    clear removes data or settings to make a device empty or reset, while powerwash erases users' data and restores factory defaults.
  • wipe
    3.6 12/m
    wipe erases or sanitizes storage so data cannot be recovered, while powerwash resets a device and removes all user data and settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: factory reset - to return a device to its original system state by erasing user data and settings
  • synonym: wipe - to erase data from a storage device
