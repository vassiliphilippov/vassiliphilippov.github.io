Mazzini

proper noun
Italian nationalist and activist (1805–1872)
Pronunciation: / mɑːˈtsiːni / (AmE), / matˈsiːni / (BrE)
(1805–1872) An Italian nationalist and political activist whose writings and actions promoted the unification and independence of Italy.

Examples

Giuseppe Mazzini leader
Giuseppe Mazzini played a crucial role in promoting the idea of Italian unification.
Many regard Mazzini as one of the key figures in the history of modern Europe.
The influence of Mazzini's writings continued long after his death.
