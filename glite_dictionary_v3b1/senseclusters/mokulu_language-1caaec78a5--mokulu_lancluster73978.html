Mokulu language

noun
a Chadic language of central Chad
Pronunciation: / moʊˈkuːluː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / məʊˈkuːluː ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Chadic language spoken in central Chad, especially in the Guera Region and surrounding villages.

Examples

Mokulu language in Chad
Linguists are working to document the grammar and vocabulary of the Mokulu language.
Many children in the villages around Guera learn the Mokulu language as their first language.
The Mokulu language is one of several Chadic languages spoken in the Guera Region of Chad.
