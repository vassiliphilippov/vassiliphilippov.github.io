schizophrenia

noun
a severe mental disorder
7.5
Pronunciation: / skɪt͡səˈfriːniə / (AmE), / skɪt.səˈfriː.ni.ə / (BrE)
a serious mental disorder characterized by distortions of reality, disturbances in thought and language, and withdrawal from social interaction
