endolymphatic

adjective
inner ear fluid
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊlɪmˈfætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊlɪmˈfætɪk / (BrE)
relating to the fluid or structures within the inner ear called endolymph

Examples

Endolymphatic duct
The endolymphatic duct helps regulate the pressure of fluids in the inner ear.
Doctors examined the endolymphatic sac to diagnose the patient's balance disorder.
Damage to endolymphatic structures can result in hearing and balance problems.
