electrocardiographically

adverb
by heart monitoring
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˌkɑrdiəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌkɑːdiəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the recording of the heart's electrical activity using an electrocardiograph

Examples

To be electrocardiographically monitored
The patient's heart was electrocardiographically monitored throughout the surgery.
Doctors assessed the rhythm electrocardiographically to detect any abnormalities.
The study measured cardiac function electrocardiographically before and after the treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrocardiographic - relating to the recording of the heart's electrical activity
