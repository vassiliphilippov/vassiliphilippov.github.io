Directoire

noun
French historical period
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˌrɛkˈtwɑr / (AmE), / diˌrɛkˈtwɑː / (BrE)
the period of French history from 1795 to 1799 when the Directory governed France after the Revolution

Examples

Directoire period history
The Directoire was marked by political instability and frequent changes in leadership.
Fashion during the Directoire period reflected the neoclassical tastes of the time.
After the fall of the Directoire, Napoleon Bonaparte rose to power in France.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Napoleonic era - the period in France following the Directory, when Napoleon ruled
  • synonym: Revolutionary France - the period in France during and after the French Revolution
