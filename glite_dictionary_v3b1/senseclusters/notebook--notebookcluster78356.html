notebook

noun
portable personal computer (laptop)
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌbʊk / (BrE)
a small, lightweight, and portable personal computer, typically with a thin profile and battery power, designed for mobile use

Examples

A notebook computer
My old notebook finally stopped working after five years of daily use.
She carried her notebook to the café to finish some work.
The company issued new notebooks to all employees for remote work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laptop - a portable personal computer that is small enough to use on one's lap
