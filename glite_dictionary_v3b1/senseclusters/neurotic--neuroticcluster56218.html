neurotic

noun
person suffering from neurosis
Pronunciation: / nʊˈrɑːdɪk / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒtɪk / (BrE)
a person who suffers from neurosis, exhibiting excessive anxiety or emotional instability

Examples

He is a neurotic
As a neurotic, he often worried excessively about minor problems.
Some neurotics respond well to therapy and medication.
The hospital had a separate ward for neurotics and those with more severe conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: psychotic - a person suffering from psychosis
