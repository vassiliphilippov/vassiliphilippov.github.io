diffusely

adverb
in a spread-out or scattered manner
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfjʊsli / (AmE), / dɪˈfjuːsli / (BrE)
In a way that is spread out over a wide area or among many people or things; not concentrated or localized.

Examples

Colors blend diffusely
In the painting, colors are blended diffusely to create a soft, hazy effect.
Sunlight filtered diffusely through the thick clouds.
The disease affected the organ diffusely rather than being limited to one area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: locally - in a limited or confined area
