overabundantly

adverb
excessively plentifully
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndəntli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəˈbʌndəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is more than enough or excessively plentiful

Examples

Overabundantly filled garden
The garden was overabundantly filled with blooming flowers of every color.
He decorated the room overabundantly for the party, leaving no empty space.
Resources were overabundantly provided, far more than the team actually needed.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • synonym: excessively - to a degree that is more than necessary
