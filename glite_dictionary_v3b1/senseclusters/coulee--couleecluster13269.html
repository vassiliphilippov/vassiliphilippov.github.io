coulee

noun
deep dry ravine
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkuː.liː/ /; / /kuː.ˈleɪ/ (AmE), /ˈkuː.liː/ /; / /kuː.ˈleɪ/ (BrE)
a deep ravine or valley, often dry, formed by water or lava, especially in dry regions
