word

noun
a unit of language; a lexical item
1.6
Pronunciation: / wɝd / (AmE), / wɜːd / (BrE)
A single distinct meaningful element of speech or writing, used with others (or sometimes alone) to form a sentence and typically shown with spaces on either side when written or printed.

Examples

A word in a sentence
Each word in that sentence has a specific meaning.
He couldn't think of the right word to express his feelings.
She wrote the unfamiliar word down in her notebook to look up later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: term - a word or phrase used to describe a thing or to express a concept
