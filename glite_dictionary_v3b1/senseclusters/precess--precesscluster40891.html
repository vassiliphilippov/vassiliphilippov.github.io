precess

verb
rotate or move slowly around an axis
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / pr…™Ààs…õs / (AmE), / pr…™ÀàsiÀês / (BrE)
to move or rotate slowly around an axis, usually in a conical or gyrating fashion, especially as applied to the axis of a spinning object

Examples

To precess slowly
Over long periods, the Earth's rotation axis will precess due to gravitational forces from the Moon and Sun.
The axis of a spinning top can precess under the influence of gravity.
When a gyroscope is disturbed, its axis begins to precess instead of simply tipping over.

  • synonym: rotate - to turn in a circle around an axis or center
