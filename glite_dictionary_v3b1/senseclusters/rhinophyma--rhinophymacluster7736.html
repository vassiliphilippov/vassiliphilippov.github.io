rhinophyma

noun
enlargement of the nose due to skin thickening
9.9 9.7
Pronunciation: /raɪ.nə.ˈfaɪ.mə/ /; / /raɪ.noʊ.ˈfaɪ.mə/ (AmE), /raɪ.nə.ˈfaɪ.mə/ /; / /raɪ.nəʊ.ˈfaɪ.mə/ (BrE)
