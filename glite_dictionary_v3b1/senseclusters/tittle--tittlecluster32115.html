tittle

noun
dot on 'i' or 'j'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈtɪtəl / (BrE)
a small dot or mark above the letters 'i' and 'j' in handwriting or print

Examples

The tittle on 'i'
Without the tittle, the letter 'i' could be confused with the letter 'l'.
She carefully dotted each tittle when writing her name in cursive.
The font designer adjusted the size of the tittle to improve readability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diacritic - a mark added to a letter to alter its pronunciation or to distinguish it
