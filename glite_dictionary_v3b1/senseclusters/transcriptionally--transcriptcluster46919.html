transcriptionally

adverb
in relation to transcription
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the process of making a written or typed transcript from spoken language or recorded speech
