transcriptionally

adverb
in relation to transcription
8.4
Pronunciation: / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈskrɪpʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the process of making a written or typed transcript from spoken language or recorded speech

Examples

Transcriptionally analyzed speech
The audio files were transcriptionally analyzed to ensure accuracy in the research data.
She worked transcriptionally to convert the interviews into readable documents for the team.
Errors can occur if the conversation is not processed transcriptionally with attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: verbally - in spoken words rather than written
