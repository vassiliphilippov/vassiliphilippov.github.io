fornicatress

noun
a woman guilty of adultery or fornication
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrnɪˈkeɪtrɪs / (AmE), / ˌfɔːnɪˈkeɪtrɪs / (BrE)
a woman who commits fornication or adultery, especially as condemned by religious or moral standards
