dead of winter

noun
coldest part of winter
Pronunciation: / dɛd əv ˈwɪn.tɚ / (AmE), / dɛd əv ˈwɪn.tə / (BrE)
the coldest and most severe period of winter

Examples

The dead of winter
Even in the dead of winter, the streets were busy with people hurrying to work.
Hot soup is especially comforting during the dead of winter.
They moved to Florida to escape the dead of winter up north.
