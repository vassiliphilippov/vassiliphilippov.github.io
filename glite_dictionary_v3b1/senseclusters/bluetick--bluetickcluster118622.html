bluetick

noun
social-media verification badge
5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbluː.tɪk/ (AmE), /ˈbluː.tɪk/ (BrE)
A small blue verification mark shown on a social-media profile to indicate that the platform has confirmed the account's identity or authenticity.

Examples

A bluetick badge
After months of emailing, she finally got the bluetick on her professional Instagram account.
Many creators feel more trusted when their social media profiles display a bluetick next to the name.
The platform removed the bluetick from accounts that violated its new verification rules.
