garden nasturtium

noun
annual climbing plant with orange flowers
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn nəˈstɜrʃəm / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn nəˈstɜːʃəm / (BrE)
a strong-growing annual climbing plant with large orange to yellowish-orange flowers and seeds sometimes pickled and used like capers, cultivated as an ornamental

Examples

Bright garden nasturtium flowers
Children enjoyed watching the bright orange blooms of the garden nasturtium climb the garden fence.
Some chefs use the seeds of the garden nasturtium as a substitute for capers in recipes.
The garden nasturtium adds a burst of color to any summer flower bed.
