heat lamp

noun
electric lamp that provides heat
4.6
Pronunciation: / hit læmp / (AmE), / hiːt læmp / (BrE)
an electric lamp that produces heat, especially by emitting infrared radiation, used to warm objects or spaces

Examples

A heat lamp for chicks
Chicks were kept warm under a red heat lamp in the barn.
My bathroom has a heat lamp to make showers more comfortable in winter.
The restaurant used a heat lamp to keep the food warm until it was served.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infrared lamp - a lamp that emits infrared radiation, primarily for heating
