cloverleaf

noun
plant or road junction
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊvərˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈkləʊvəliːf / (BrE)
a plant with leaves divided into three or four rounded parts, or a road junction with a shape resembling such leaves

Examples

A green cloverleaf
The new highway includes a massive cloverleaf to help manage traffic flow.
She found a four-leaf cloverleaf in the field and considered it lucky.
Engineers designed the cloverleaf interchange to reduce congestion at the busy intersection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interchange - a road junction that typically uses ramps to allow traffic to change roads without crossing
  • synonym: shamrock - a plant with three-lobed leaves, especially associated with Ireland
