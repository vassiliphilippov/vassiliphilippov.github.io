watery-eyed

adjective
having eyes full of tears
5.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔːtəri aɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɒtəri aɪd / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having eyes full of moisture, especially tears; appearing tearful or as if about to cry.

Examples

Watery-eyed look
His watery-eyed expression made it clear he was moved by the touching speech.
She sat in the cold wind, watery-eyed and shivering.
The child looked up at her mother with watery-eyed innocence after scraping her knee.
