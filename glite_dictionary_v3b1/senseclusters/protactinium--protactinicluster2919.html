protactinium

noun
radioactive metallic element (Pa)
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtækˈtɪniəm / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtækˈtɪniəm / (BrE)
a short-lived radioactive metallic element (symbol: Pa) that occurs in uranium ores and decays into actinium and then lead

Examples

Protactinium element
Protactinium is highly radioactive and is found in some uranium ores in minute quantities.
Because protactinium is toxic and radioactive, it requires special handling procedures in the lab.
Researchers isolated a small amount of protactinium for their nuclear chemistry experiments.
