breath of fresh air

noun
welcome change or relief
3.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /brɛθ əv frɛʃ er/ /; / /brɛθ əv frɛʃ ɛr/ (AmE), /brɛθ əv frɛʃ eə/ /; / /brɛθ əv frɛʃ ɛə/ (BrE)
a person, thing, or idea that brings a welcome and refreshing change from what is usual or unpleasant

Examples
