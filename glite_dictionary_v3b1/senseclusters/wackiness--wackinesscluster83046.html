wackiness

noun
amusing oddness
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwækɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈwækɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being amusingly odd, silly, or unpredictable

Examples

The wackiness of comedy
The wackiness of the comedy show made it a favorite among teenagers.
Her wackiness always brings a smile to everyone in the office.
The movie's wackiness sets it apart from more traditional romantic comedies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or not funny
