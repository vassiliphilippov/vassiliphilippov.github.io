protozoic

adjective
relating to protozoa
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.təˈzoʊ.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ.təʊˈzəʊ.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of protozoa, a diverse group of single-celled microscopic organisms.

Examples

Protozoic organisms
Many protozoic organisms can be observed under a simple microscope.
Some diseases are caused by protozoic parasites living in water sources.
The scientist studied the protozoic layers found in the ancient rock samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protozoan - relating to or resembling a protozoon; a protozoic organism
