spillikins

noun
game involving removing sticks carefully
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪlɪkɪnz / (AmE), / ˈspɪlɪkɪnz / (BrE)
A game in which players use skill to remove small sticks, each called a spillikin, from a loose pile without disturbing the others.

Examples

A spillikins game
He found an old set of spillikins in the attic and taught the game to his friends.
The careful hand required in spillikins makes it a challenging pastime for children and adults alike.
We used to play spillikins with my grandmother on rainy afternoons.
