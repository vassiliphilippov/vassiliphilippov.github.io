custodial account

noun
third-party-held crypto account

(in cryptocurrency and digital assets) an account or service where a third party stores and manages a user's digital assets or private keys on the user's behalf, so the user does not have sole control of the assets.

Examples

Crypto custodial account
Many beginners store crypto on an exchange as a custodial account for convenience.
You should understand fees and security risks before moving funds into a custodial account.
Unlike a noncustodial wallet, a custodial account means a third party holds your private keys.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custodial wallet - a wallet or service that holds users' private keys and manages crypto on their behalf
  • antonym: noncustodial wallet - a wallet where the user alone controls the private keys and has sole control of their funds
