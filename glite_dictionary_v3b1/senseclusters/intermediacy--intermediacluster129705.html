intermediacy

noun
state of mediation
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈmiː.dɪ.ə.siː/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈmiː.dɪ.ə.siː/ (BrE)
the state or role of being an intermediary or acting as a mediator between people, groups, or things

Examples

Intermediacy of the agency
The intermediacy of the agency was crucial in reaching a fair agreement between the two companies.
Without the intermediacy of a neutral party, the negotiations might have failed.
Her role of intermediacy helped bridge the gap between the conflicting groups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: directness - the quality of being direct, without intermediaries
  • synonym: intermediation - the act of serving as a go-between in transactions or negotiations
  • synonym: mediation - the process of intervening to resolve a conflict or facilitate agreement
