upsettingly

adverb
distressingly
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛtɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes distress, worry, or emotional disturbance

Examples

Ends upsettingly
The movie ended upsettingly, leaving many viewers in tears.
She spoke upsettingly about the recent events in her hometown.
The results were upsettingly different from what everyone had expected.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that provides comfort or reassurance
  • synonym: distressingly - in a way that causes distress or anxiety
