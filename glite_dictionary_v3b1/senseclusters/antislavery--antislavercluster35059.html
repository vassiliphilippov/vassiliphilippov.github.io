antislavery

noun
opposition to slavery
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsleɪvəri / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsleɪvəri / (BrE)
the belief in or movement for ending the practice of slavery

Examples

Antislavery movement
The antislavery movement gained strength in the early nineteenth century.
Many activists dedicated their lives to the cause of antislavery.
Public antislavery sentiment influenced important political decisions of the era.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abolitionism - the movement to end slavery
  • antonym: proslavery - support for the continuation of slavery
