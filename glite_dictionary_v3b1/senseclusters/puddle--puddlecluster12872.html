puddle

verb
work or manipulate a wet mixture

Pronunciation: /ˈpʌd.əl/ (AmE), /ˈpʌd.əl/ (BrE)
to work or manipulate a wet mixture, such as concrete or mud, especially to make it more compact or uniform

Examples

Builders puddle mud
