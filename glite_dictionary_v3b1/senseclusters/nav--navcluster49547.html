nav

noun
navigation system
7.5
Pronunciation: /næv/ (AmE), /næv/ (BrE)
an informal term for a navigation system or the process of navigating, especially in vehicles or vessels

Examples

Car nav system
The car's nav guided us through the city without any problems.
He checked the nav before starting the boat to make sure the route was clear.
When the nav stopped working, we had to use a paper map instead.
