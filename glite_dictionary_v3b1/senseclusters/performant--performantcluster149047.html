performant

adjective
related to performance
7.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɔːrmənt / (AmE), / pəˈfɔːmənt / (BrE)
relating to performance as an artistic or theatrical activity; suitable for or concerned with staged performance.

Examples

Performant stage role
The director requested a more subtle, performant interpretation for the play's climactic scene.
Her performant skills impressed the entire cast during rehearsals.
The festival featured several performant pieces that explored new forms of stage expression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dramatic - relating to drama or the performance of plays
  • synonym: theatrical - relating to the theater or stage performance
