contralto

noun
lowest female adult singing voice
9.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræltoʊ / (AmE), / kənˈtræl.təʊ / (BrE)
the lowest adult female singing voice, or a woman singer who has this voice

Examples

A famous contralto singer
Many famous operas have challenging parts written specifically for the contralto voice.
She trained as a soprano but later discovered her true range was contralto.
The lead role was sung by a renowned contralto with a rich, deep tone.
Many famous arias are written specifically for the contralto voice.
She is recognized as a leading contralto in the world of opera.
The choir's contralto added a rich depth to the harmony.
