Livonian

noun
member of the Livonian-speaking people of Latvia
Pronunciation: / lɪˈvoʊniən / (AmE), / lɪˈvəʊniən / (BrE)
a member of the Livonian-speaking people indigenous to northern Latvia

Examples

Livonian people
Historians study the ancient settlements of the Livonian people along the Latvian coast.
Only a small number of Livonians remain in modern Latvia today.
The Livonian community has worked to preserve its unique language and traditions.
