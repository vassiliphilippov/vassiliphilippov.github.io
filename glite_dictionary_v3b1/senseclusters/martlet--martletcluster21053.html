martlet

noun
heraldic bird symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrtlɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtlɪt / (BrE)
a stylized bird, usually depicted without feet, used as a symbol in heraldry

Examples

Heraldic martlet
The ancient shield bore a single golden martlet as its main decoration.
In heraldry, the martlet is often shown without feet to symbolize continuous effort.
The university's emblem features a red martlet above the motto.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swallow - a small migratory bird with long pointed wings
