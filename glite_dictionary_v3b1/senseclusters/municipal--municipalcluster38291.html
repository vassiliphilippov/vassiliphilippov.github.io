municipal

adjective
of or relating to a municipality or local government
Pronunciation: / mjʊˈnɪsəpəl /; / mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl / (AmE), / mjuːˈnɪs.ɪ.pəl /; / mjuːˈnɪsɪpəl / (BrE)
relating to a city, town, or its local government

Examples

Municipal government
He invested his money in municipal bonds issued by the city government.
The municipal elections will decide who manages the local services and infrastructure.
The town council approved a new municipal swimming pool for the community.
Elections for municipal offices are often held separately from national elections.
Residents pay taxes to support municipal services such as water supply and waste collection.
The municipal government is responsible for maintaining local roads and public parks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civic - relating to a city or town, especially its administration; municipal
