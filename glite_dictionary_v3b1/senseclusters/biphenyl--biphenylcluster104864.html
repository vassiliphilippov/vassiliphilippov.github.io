biphenyl

noun
two-ring compound
8.6
Pronunciation: / baɪˈfiːnɪl /; / baɪˈfiːnəl / (AmE), / baɪˈfiːnɪl / (BrE)
a chemical compound made of two benzene rings connected by a single bond

Examples
