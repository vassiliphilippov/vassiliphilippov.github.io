ugali

noun
East African maize porridge
Pronunciation: / uˈɡɑːli / (AmE), / uˈɡɑːli / (BrE)
a type of stiff porridge made from maize flour, commonly eaten in East Africa

Examples

A bowl of ugali
For lunch, we enjoyed ugali served with fried fish and greens.
Many families in Kenya eat ugali as a staple food every day.
She learned how to cook ugali from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fufu - a West African dish made from boiled and pounded starchy vegetables
  • synonym: polenta - a dish of boiled cornmeal, common in Italian cuisine
