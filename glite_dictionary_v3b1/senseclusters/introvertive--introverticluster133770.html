introvertive

adjective
inward-looking, focused on inner feelings
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈvɝ.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈvɜː.tɪv / (BrE)
Characterized by a focus on one's own inner thoughts or feelings rather than external things; inward-looking.

Examples

Introvertive personality
Her introvertive nature made her prefer quiet nights at home over social gatherings.
The artist's introvertive style is reflected in his deeply personal paintings.
Therapists sometimes encourage introvertive reflection to help clients understand their emotions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extrovertive - characterized by attention directed outward; concerned with external things
  • synonym: introverted - having a personality that is shy and reserved, focused on internal thoughts
