aboral

adjective
away from the mouth (biology)
Pronunciation: / əˈbɔr.əl / (AmE), / əˈbɔː.rəl / (BrE)
(biology) relating to or situated on the side or end that is opposite to or away from the mouth of an organism.

Examples

The aboral surface
In sea urchins, the anus is located on the aboral surface.
Researchers examined the aboral region to compare its structure with the oral surface.
The aboral side of the jellyfish contains key sensory organs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oral - relating to the mouth
