futurism

noun
Italian art movement inspired by the machine age
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈfjʊːtʃərɪzəm / (BrE)
an early 20th-century artistic and social movement originating in Italy, emphasizing speed, technology, and modernity inspired by the machine age

Examples

Italian futurism art
He wrote his thesis on the impact of futurism in modern architecture.
Many artists embraced futurism as a way to celebrate the technological advances of their time.
The influence of futurism can be seen in the bold lines and dynamic compositions of the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cubism - an early 20th-century art movement emphasizing abstract structure
  • synonym: modernism - a style or movement in the arts that aims to break with classical and traditional forms
