overanxiety

noun
excessive or undue anxiety
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvəræŋˈzaɪəti / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərəŋˈzaɪəti / (BrE)
Excessive or undue anxiety; a level of anxiety greater than what is considered normal or reasonable.

Examples

Excessive overanxiety
Her overanxiety about her children often leads her to worry unnecessarily.
The doctor's calm manner helped to ease the patient's overanxiety.
Trying to control everything can sometimes result in overanxiety and stress.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: anxiety - a feeling of worry or unease
  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being free from agitation or strong emotion
