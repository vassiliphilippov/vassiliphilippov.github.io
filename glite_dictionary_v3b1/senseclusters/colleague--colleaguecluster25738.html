Examples

A colleague at work

Synonyms

  • co-worker
    1.8 0.01/m
    co-worker is a neutral term for someone you work with, while colleague often sounds slightly more formal and professional.
  • workmate
    2.2 0.11/m
    workmate is an informal British word for someone at the same workplace, while colleague sounds more general and professional.
  • fellow worker
    2.0 0.44/m
    fellow worker is a formal or old-fashioned phrase for someone in your organization, while colleague is the common modern word.
  • officemate
    3.5 0.11/m
    officemate refers to someone who shares your office or room, while colleague can mean any person who works with you in the same company.

  • antonym: subordinate - a person of lower rank or status at work
  • antonym: superior - a person of higher rank or status at work
