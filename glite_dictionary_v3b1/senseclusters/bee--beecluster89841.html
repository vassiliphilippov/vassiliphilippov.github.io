bee

noun
communal work or contest gathering
8.2
Pronunciation: / bi / (AmE), / biː / (BrE)
a social event where people gather to work together on a communal task or take part in a competition, such as a spelling bee or quilting bee

Examples

A spelling bee
Every December, our local club hosts a fundraising bee for charity.
Grandma remembers attending a quilting bee when she was a girl.
The community held a bee to sew blankets for the shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quilting bee - social gathering for making quilts collectively
  • synonym: spelling bee - contest where participants are asked to spell words
