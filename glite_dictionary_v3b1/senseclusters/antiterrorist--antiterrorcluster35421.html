antiterrorist

adjective
against terrorism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪst / (BrE)
designed to prevent, oppose, or fight against terrorism

Examples

Antiterrorist unit
The country established a special antiterrorist unit to respond to threats quickly.
New antiterrorist laws were passed to strengthen national security.
The airport increased its antiterrorist measures after the recent incident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterterrorism - activities or measures intended to prevent terrorism
  • antonym: terrorist - relating to or supporting terrorism
