streamingly

adverb
in a flowing manner
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstriːmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that flows continuously like a stream, often referring to something moving steadily or in large amounts

Examples

Rain falls streamingly
Rainwater flowed streamingly down the window during the heavy storm.
Her hair fell streamingly over her shoulders as she brushed it.
Sweat poured streamingly from his forehead after the intense workout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuously - without stopping or interruption
  • synonym: steadily - in a regular and even way
