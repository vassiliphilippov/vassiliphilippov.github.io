pontifically

adverb
in a formal, ceremonial way
9.8
Pronunciation: /pɑːn.ˈtɪf.ɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /pɑːn.ˈtɪf.ə.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /pɒn.ˈtɪf.ɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /pɒn.ˈtɪf.ə.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
