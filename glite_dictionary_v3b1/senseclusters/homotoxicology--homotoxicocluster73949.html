homotoxicology

noun
analysis of disease via toxins in alternative medicine
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmoʊˌtɑksɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməʊˌtɒksɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
A system of alternative medicine that analyzes disease as the result of the body's inability to eliminate toxins, typically using homeopathic remedies.

Examples

Homotoxicology alternative medicine
She attended a seminar to learn more about homotoxicology and its use in alternative therapy.
Some practitioners use homotoxicology to interpret chronic illnesses as accumulations of toxins in the body.
The theory behind homotoxicology has been widely criticized by mainstream medical professionals.
