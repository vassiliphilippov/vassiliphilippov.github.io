spinsterly

adjective
like a spinster
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnstərli / (AmE), / ˈspɪnstəli / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics associated with a spinster, especially in an old-fashioned or prim way

Examples

A spinsterly woman
Her spinsterly habits included knitting by the fire every evening.
The room was decorated in a spinsterly style, with lace curtains and porcelain figurines.
He teased his sister about her spinsterly attention to neatness and order.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times; not old-fashioned
  • synonym: old-maidish - having the qualities of an old maid; prim or fussy
