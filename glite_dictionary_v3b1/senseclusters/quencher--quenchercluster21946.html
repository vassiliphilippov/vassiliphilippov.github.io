quencher

noun
fire-extinguishing device
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛntʃər / (AmE), / ˈkwɛntʃə / (BrE)
a device or substance used to extinguish or put out a fire or flame

Examples

Fire quencher
The laboratory kept a quencher nearby in case of chemical fires.
After the campfire got too large, they used a quencher to put it out safely.
Every kitchen should have a quencher for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extinguisher - a device used to put out fires
  • antonym: igniter - a device used to start a fire
