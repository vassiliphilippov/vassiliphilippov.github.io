petrolatum gauze

noun
gauze dressing with petrolatum for wounds
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛtrəˈleɪtəm ɡɔz / (AmE), / ˌpiːtrəˈleɪtəm ɡɔːz / (BrE)
a type of gauze dressing that is impregnated with petrolatum, used to protect wounds and promote healing by keeping the area moist

Examples

Petrolatum gauze dressing
Doctors recommend petrolatum gauze dressings for skin graft sites to reduce adherence.
The nurse applied petrolatum gauze to the burn before wrapping it with a bandage.
Using petrolatum gauze helps maintain a moist environment for wound healing.
