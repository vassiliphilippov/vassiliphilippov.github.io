dahoon

noun
holly tree berry
Pronunciation: / dəˈhuːn / (AmE), / dəˈhuːn / (BrE)
the small red berry or fruit produced by the dahoon holly tree, native to the southeastern United States

Examples

Dahoon berries
The bright red dahoon attracts many birds during the autumn months.
Children collected dahoon from beneath the holly trees for a school project.
Wildlife in the region depends on the dahoon as a food source in winter.
