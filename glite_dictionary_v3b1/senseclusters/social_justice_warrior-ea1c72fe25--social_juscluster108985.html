social justice warrior

noun
derogatory term for a progressive activist
2.8 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˈdʒʌstɪs ˈwɔriər / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃ(ə)l ˈdʒʌstɪs ˈwɒriə / (BrE)
A derogatory term for a person who expresses or promotes progressive social causes, especially regarding issues such as feminism and civil rights, often seen as doing so in a self-righteous or performative way.

Examples

Social justice warrior protests
She was called a social justice warrior because she often protested against discrimination.
Some people use the label social justice warrior to disparage those who speak out for equality online.
The comment section was filled with arguments between trolls and social justice warriors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activist - a person who campaigns for social change
  • degree lower: slacktivist - a person who gives only token support to social causes
