pop

noun
a sweet, carbonated soft drink
1.0 4.65/m
Pronunciation: / pÉ‘p / (AmE), / pÉ’p / (BrE)
a sweet, carbonated soft drink, often flavored and sold in bottles or cans

Examples

A cold pop
In some parts of the US, people call all carbonated drinks pop.
She picked up a cold pop from the fridge on a hot day.
Would you like a pop with your burger?
