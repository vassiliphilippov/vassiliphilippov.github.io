unculturable

adjective
not able to be civilized
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkʌl.tʃər.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkʌl.tʃə.rə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

It is unfair and incorrect to label any group as unculturable based on cultural differences.
