unculturable

adjective
not able to be civilized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkʌltʃərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkʌltʃərəbəl / (BrE)
incapable of being civilized, educated, or influenced by cultural refinement

Examples

Unculturable people
The explorers described the remote tribe as unculturable, but this view was later proven false.
Some educators believed the students were unculturable, underestimating their potential for learning.
It is unfair and incorrect to label any group as unculturable based on cultural differences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cultivable - able to be educated or improved by culture
  • synonym: incorrigible - impossible to correct or reform
