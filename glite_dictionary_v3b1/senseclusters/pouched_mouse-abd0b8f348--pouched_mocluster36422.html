pouched mouse

noun
small marsupial resembling a mouse
Pronunciation: / paʊtʃt maʊs / (AmE), / paʊtʃt maʊs / (BrE)
Any of several small, sharp-nosed insectivorous marsupials that superficially resemble mice or rats.

Examples

A pouched mouse species
Scientists discovered a new species of pouched mouse in the Australian outback.
The pouched mouse is known for its pointed snout and insect diet.
Unlike true mice, the pouched mouse carries its young in a pouch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marsupial - mammal with a pouch for carrying young
