LGBTQIA

noun
the non-normative gender and sexual identity community
5.8
Pronunciation: /el dʒiː biː tiː kjuː aɪ eɪ/ (AmE), /el dʒiː biː tiː kjuː aɪ eɪ/ (BrE)
