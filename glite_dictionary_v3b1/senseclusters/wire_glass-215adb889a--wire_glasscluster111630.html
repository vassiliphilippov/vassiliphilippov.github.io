wire glass

noun
glass with wire mesh for safety
4.6
Pronunciation: / waɪɚ ɡlæs / (AmE), / waɪə ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a type of glass containing a layer of wire mesh, used to reduce the risk of shattering and increase safety

Examples

Wire glass safety
Fire doors are frequently fitted with wire glass to comply with safety regulations.
The hospital installed wire glass in all the hallway doors for added protection.
You can often see wire glass in schools and factories to prevent injuries from broken windows.
