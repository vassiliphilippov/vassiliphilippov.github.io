ape

noun
tailless or short-tailed primate
Pronunciation: / eɪp / (AmE), / eɪp / (BrE)
a primate of a group that includes the gibbons, great apes, and humans, distinguished by having no tail or a very short tail

Examples

A wild ape
Chimpanzees are a type of ape closely related to humans.
Some species of ape use simple tools to obtain food.
The zoo recently welcomed a newborn ape into its primate exhibit.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monkey - a primate with a long tail, typically smaller and more agile than apes
