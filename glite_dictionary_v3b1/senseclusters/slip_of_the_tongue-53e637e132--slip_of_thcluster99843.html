slip of the tongue

noun
minor accidental speaking mistake
Pronunciation: / slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (AmE), / slɪp əv ðə tʌŋ / (BrE)
an accidental and minor mistake made while speaking, such as saying the wrong word or phrase

Examples

A slip of the tongue
During the speech, she made a slip of the tongue that made everyone laugh.
He apologized for the slip of the tongue, saying he didn't mean to offend anyone.
It was just a slip of the tongue when he called her by the wrong name.
He apologized for his slip of the tongue during the meeting.
Her slip of the tongue made everyone in the room laugh.
Sometimes a slip of the tongue can lead to embarrassing moments.
