secondhand car

noun
a used car; not new
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkəndˌhænd ˈkɑr / (AmE), / ˌsekəndˌhænd ˈkɑː / (BrE)
A car that has been previously owned by one or more people; not a new car.

Examples

A secondhand car
After months of saving, she finally bought a secondhand car to commute to work.
He preferred to buy a secondhand car rather than spending more on a brand new model.
The secondhand car looked almost new and came with a full service history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: new car - a car that has not been owned before; just made
  • synonym: used car - a car that has been owned by someone else before
