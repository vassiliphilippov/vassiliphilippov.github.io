network

noun
a mesh of threads, wires, or ropes
3.0 1.47/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…õtÀåw…ùk / (AmE), / Ààn…õtÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
an open structure made of threads, wires, or ropes woven together at regular intervals; a mesh or net.

Examples

A network of threads
A delicate network of silk covered the window.
Fishermen repaired the torn network they used for catching fish.
The fence was reinforced with a network of steel wires for extra security.
