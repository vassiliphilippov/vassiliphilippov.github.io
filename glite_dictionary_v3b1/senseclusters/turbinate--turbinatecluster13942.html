turbinate

noun
scroll-shaped nasal bone
9.9 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆtÉœrbÉªËŒneÉªt / (AmE), / ËˆtÉœËbÉªneÉªt / (BrE)
any of the spongy, scroll-shaped bones located inside the nasal passages of humans and other vertebrates, which help to filter and humidify the air we breathe

Examples

Swollen turbinate
Allergies can cause swelling of the turbinate bones, leading to nasal congestion.
In mammals, the turbinate serves to warm and humidify the air before it reaches the lungs.
The surgeon carefully examined the patient's turbinate during the procedure.

Synonyms

  • turbinal
    9.9 0.00/m
    turbinal refers to the scroll-shaped spongy nasal bones also called nasal conchae, while turbinate is the common modern name.
