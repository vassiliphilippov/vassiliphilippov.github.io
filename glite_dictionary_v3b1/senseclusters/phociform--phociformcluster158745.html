phociform

adjective
relating to true seals
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊsɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈfəʊsɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of true seals or the taxonomic group Phociformes.

Examples

Phociform mammals
The phociform mammals are well adapted to life in cold marine environments.
Scientists studied the phociform features that distinguish true seals from other pinnipeds.
Several phociform species were observed resting on the ice during the expedition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: otariid - relating to or belonging to the family of eared seals
  • synonym: phocid - relating to or belonging to the family of true seals
