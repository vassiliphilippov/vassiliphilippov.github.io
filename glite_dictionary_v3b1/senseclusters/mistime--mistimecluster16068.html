mistime

verb
to time incorrectly
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈtaɪm / (AmE), / mɪsˈtaɪm / (BrE)
to do something at the wrong time or with incorrect timing

Examples

To mistime a jump
He mistimed his jump and landed awkwardly on the mat.
If you mistime your swing, you won't hit the ball properly.
She mistimed her entry and missed the opening scene.
