paralyzing

verb
to make unable to move
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpær.ə.laɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpær.ə.laɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
making someone or something unable to move, act, or function by disabling or stopping them completely.

Examples

Snowstorm paralyzing traffic
The sudden snowstorm is paralyzing traffic throughout the entire city.
Fear of failure can be paralyzing when you are about to try something new.
The strike is paralyzing essential services, causing major disruptions for residents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activate - to make something start working or functioning
  • synonym: disable - to make something unable to work or function
  • synonym: immobilize - to prevent something or someone from moving
