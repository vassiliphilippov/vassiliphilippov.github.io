potentiometric

adjective
measuring electric potential
8.3
Pronunciation: / pəˌtɛnʃiəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / pəˌtɛnʃɪəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of electric potential, especially in chemical or electrochemical analysis

Examples

Potentiometric measurement method
The laboratory uses potentiometric methods to determine the concentration of ions in a solution.
A potentiometric titration can provide accurate results for acid-base analysis.
Engineers installed a potentiometric sensor to monitor changes in voltage during the experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voltammetric - relating to the measurement of current as a function of voltage
