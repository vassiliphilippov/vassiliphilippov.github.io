blazing

adjective
shining very brightly
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbleɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
shining very brightly or intensely

Examples

Blazing sun
He stared directly into the blazing headlights of the oncoming car.
Snow covered the field, reflecting a blazing white light that made me squint.
The blazing sun beat down on the desert without mercy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dazzling - shining intensely and often blindingly
  • antonym: dim - not shining brightly or clearly
  • synonym: glaring - shining with a very bright, often harsh light
