Synonyms

  • brilliant
    3.0 4.15/m
    brilliant shines with very strong, clear light, while blazing suggests an intense, often fiery or very bright shine.
  • dazzling
    3.1 0.39/m
    dazzling gives a sudden bright flash that can hurt the eyes, while blazing means a steady, very intense shine.
  • blinding
    4.1 0.14/m
    blinding is so bright it makes seeing hard, while blazing describes strong vivid light without causing harm.
  • ablaze
    5.5 1.16/m
    ablaze often means burning with fire or full of color, while blazing focuses on very strong bright shining.
  • radiant
    4.1 4.39/m
    radiant glows warmly and beautifully, while blazing suggests a more fierce, very intense and sometimes fiery light.
  • alight
    6.1 0.46/m
    alight is lit up or on fire in a small way, while blazing means shining extremely bright or burning strongly.
  • aglow
    5.5 0.56/m
    aglow shines with a soft, gentle light like candles, while blazing is much stronger and often feels fierce or fiery.
  • bright
    1.0 45/m
    bright simply means full of light or color, while blazing implies a much stronger, intense, and often fiery brightness.
