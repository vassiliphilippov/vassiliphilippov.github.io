corporatize

verb
to apply corporatist structure
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.pə.taɪz/ /; / /ˈkɔːr.pə.taɪz/ (AmE), /ˈkɔː.pə.taɪz/ (BrE)
to organize or restructure a society, economy, or institution according to corporatist principles, formally integrating interest groups into governance

Examples

To corporatize public services
The new policy aims to corporatize public services by involving major industry groups in decision-making.
Some critics argue that efforts to corporatize education reduce the influence of individual teachers and students.
The government decided to corporatize the health sector to better coordinate between hospitals and insurance providers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: institutionalize - to establish something as a formal system or practice
  • antonym: privatize - to transfer from public to private ownership or control
