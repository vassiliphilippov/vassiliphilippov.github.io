swing

noun
a back-and-forth movement
4.0
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ / (AmE), / swɪŋ / (BrE)
a movement back and forth or from side to side, often in a repeated and regular way

Examples

The swing of branches
Children watched the birds with the gentle swing of the tree branches.
The boat was caught in the swing of the current, rocking back and forth.
The steady swing of the pendulum kept perfect time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sway - to move slowly from side to side
