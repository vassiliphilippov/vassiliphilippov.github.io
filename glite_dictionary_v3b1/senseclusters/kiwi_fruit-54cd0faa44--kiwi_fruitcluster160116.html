kiwi fruit

noun
small brown fruit with green flesh
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːwi ˌfrut / (AmE), / ˈkiːwi ˌfruːt / (BrE)
An oval, fuzzy-skinned brown fruit with bright green flesh and a slightly tart taste, eaten raw or in desserts.

Examples

A kiwi fruit snack
Kiwi fruit is rich in vitamin C, making kiwi fruit a healthy snack choice.
She sliced the kiwi fruit and added it to the fruit salad.
The skin of a kiwi fruit is edible but often removed before eating.
