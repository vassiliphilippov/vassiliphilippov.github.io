singleness

noun
unmarried state
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡlnəs / (BrE)
the state of not being married or in a romantic relationship

Examples

Her singleness
She values her singleness and the independence it brings.
Many people enjoy the freedom that singleness offers before settling down.
His singleness allowed him to travel and focus on his career.

Related Terms

  • antonym: marriage - the state of being married
  • synonym: singlehood - the state of being single
