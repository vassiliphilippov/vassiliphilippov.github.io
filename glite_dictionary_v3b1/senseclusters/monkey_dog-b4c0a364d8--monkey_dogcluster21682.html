monkey dog

noun
small European terrier-like dog breed
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki dɔg / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki dɒg / (BrE)
A European breed of small dog resembling a terrier, notable for its dark wiry hair and tufted muzzle, also known as the Affenpinscher.

Examples

The monkey dog breed
At the dog show, the monkey dog attracted a lot of attention from the judges.
She adopted a monkey dog because she liked its expressive face.
The monkey dog is known for its playful nature and distinctive appearance.
