watch

verb
be vigilant or careful
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ / (AmE), / wɒtʃ / (BrE)
To be alert, vigilant, or careful, especially in order to avoid danger or trouble.

Examples

To watch for danger
She told her children to watch when playing near the river.
Tourists must watch for scams in the busy market.
You should watch when crossing the street at night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention; fail to notice or consider
  • synonym: look out - to be vigilant or careful, especially to avoid danger
