Clara Schumann

proper noun
German pianist and composer (1819–1896)
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːrə ʃuˈmɑːn / (AmE), / ˈklɑːrə ʃuːˈmæn / (BrE)
(1819–1896) A German pianist and composer, renowned for her performances and piano compositions, as well as her interpretations of her husband Robert Schumann's music.

Examples

Clara Schumann pianist
Clara Schumann was celebrated for her virtuosic piano performances throughout Europe in the nineteenth century.
Many consider Clara Schumann a pioneering woman in the world of classical music.
The festival featured a recital dedicated to the works of Clara Schumann.
