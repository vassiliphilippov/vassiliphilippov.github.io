coldness

noun
the sensation of low temperature
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkoʊld.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkəʊld.nəs/ (BrE)
the state or quality of being cold; the sensation or presence of low temperature

Examples

Winter coldness
His hands were numb from the coldness outside.
She could feel the coldness in the air as winter approached.
The coldness of the room made it hard to sleep without a blanket.
A sudden wave of coldness filled the room when the window was opened.
The coldness of the water shocked him as he jumped into the lake.
The coldness outside makes it necessary to wear a heavy coat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chill - a moderate but perceptible degree of coldness
  • synonym: chilliness - the state or sensation of being slightly cold
  • degree lower: coolness - moderate coldness, less intense than coldness
  • antonym: heat - a state of being hot; high temperature
  • antonym: warmth - the quality or sensation of being warm
