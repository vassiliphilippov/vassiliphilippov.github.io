rye vodka

noun
vodka distilled from rye
Pronunciation: / raɪ ˈvɑːdkə / (AmE), / raɪ ˈvɒdkə / (BrE)
Vodka that is distilled mainly from rye grains, giving it a characteristic flavor profile.
