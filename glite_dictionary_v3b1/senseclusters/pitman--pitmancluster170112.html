pitman

noun
a coal miner (man)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtmən / (AmE), / ˈpɪtmən / (BrE)
a man who works in a coal mine, especially underground

Examples

A pitman in coal
After years as a pitman, his hands were tough and calloused from the hard work.
Her grandfather was a pitman in one of the largest mines in the region.
Stories of life as a pitman have been passed down in that community for generations.
