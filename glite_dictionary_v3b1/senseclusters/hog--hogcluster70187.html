hog

noun
greedy or selfish person
4.5
Pronunciation: / hɑɡ / (AmE), / hɒɡ / (BrE)
A person who is extremely greedy, especially one who takes more than their fair share.

Examples

A greedy hog
Don't be such a hog—leave some cake for the rest of us.
He acted like a total hog at the buffet, piling his plate high.
She called him a hog for refusing to share the remote.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glutton - a person who overindulges in food or drink
  • antonym: miser - a person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible
