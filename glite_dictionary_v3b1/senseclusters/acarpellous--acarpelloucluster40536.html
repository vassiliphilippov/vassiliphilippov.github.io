acarpellous

adjective
having no carpels (botany)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈkɑrpələs / (AmE), / əˈkɑːpɪləs / (BrE)
(of a plant or flower) having no carpels.

Examples

Acarpellous flower
In botany, an acarpellous flower cannot produce seeds because it does not have carpels.
Researchers studied the acarpellous plants to understand the evolutionary loss of certain flower structures.
The rare species was identified as acarpellous, meaning it lacked female reproductive organs.
