submarining

noun
sudden return after silence
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbməˈriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌsʌbməˈriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of suddenly reappearing and resuming contact after a period of ignoring someone, especially in online communication or dating

Examples

Online submarining
After months of no messages, his sudden submarining left her confused and frustrated.
Many people find submarining in online dating to be disrespectful and hurtful.
She was surprised by his submarining after he had ignored her for so long.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ghosting - ending all communication suddenly and without explanation
  • synonym: orbiting - staying present on someone's social media without direct contact
