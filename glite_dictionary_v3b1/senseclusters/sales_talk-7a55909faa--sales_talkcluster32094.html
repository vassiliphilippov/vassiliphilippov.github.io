sales talk

noun
persuasive talk to sell something
3.0 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / se…™lz t…îk / (AmE), / se…™lz t…îÀêk / (BrE)
speech or conversation intended to persuade someone to buy something, often by emphasizing the benefits of the product or service

Examples

Sales talk to customers
Despite all the sales talk, I wasn't persuaded to sign the contract.
She was put off by the aggressive sales talk and decided to shop elsewhere.
The car dealer's sales talk was so convincing that I almost bought a new model on the spot.

Synonyms

  • sales pitch
    3.2 1.19/m
    sales pitch is a short, planned presentation that highlights product benefits, while sales talk is any persuasive conversation used by sellers.
  • pitch
    4.2 5.94/m
    pitch can mean a vendor's selling spot or a focused presentation, while sales talk specifically means speech aiming to persuade buyers.
  • spiel
    7.9 1.06/m
    spiel is a smooth, often lengthy promotional talk with a slightly glib tone, while sales talk is a general persuasive sales conversation.
