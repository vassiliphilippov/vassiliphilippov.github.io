nonassociation

noun
lack of relationship
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence of a statistical or measurable relationship between two variables or factors

Examples

Nonassociation of results
The study found nonassociation between the two medications and any significant side effects.
Researchers were surprised by the nonassociation of diet and blood pressure in the results.
A clear nonassociation was observed between the variables after adjusting for age and gender.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correlation - a mutual relationship or connection between two or more things
  • synonym: independence - the state of being unrelated or not influenced by something else
