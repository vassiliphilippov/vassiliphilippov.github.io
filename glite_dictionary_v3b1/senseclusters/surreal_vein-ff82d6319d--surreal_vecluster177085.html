surreal vein

noun
style marked by dreamlike or surreal imagery
Pronunciation: / səˈriːəl veɪn / (AmE), / səˈrɪəl veɪn / (BrE)
a style or manner that is characterized by dreamlike or fantastic imagery, often resembling surrealism

Examples

A surreal vein style
Her sculptures display a surreal vein that recalls the work of Dalí.
The book is written throughout in a surreal vein, blending reality with fantasy.
The filmmaker's use of color and imagery shows his penchant for working in a surreal vein.
