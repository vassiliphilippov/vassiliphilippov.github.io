mud fence

noun
fence made of mud
Pronunciation: / mʌd fɛns / (AmE), / mʌd fɛns / (BrE)
A fence constructed primarily from mud, used to enclose or protect an area.

Examples

A mud fence barrier
Farmers in the region often build a mud fence to keep livestock from wandering off.
Her grandfather showed her how to repair the cracked mud fence after the rainstorm.
The old barn was surrounded by a mud fence that had stood for decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fence - A structure serving as an enclosure or barrier
