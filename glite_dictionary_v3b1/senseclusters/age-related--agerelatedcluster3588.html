age-related

adjective
associated with aging
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪdʒ rɪˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈeɪdʒ rɪˌleɪtɪd / (BrE)
changing or affected by a person's age; associated with or caused by aging

Examples

Age-related changes
Her vision problems were the result of age-related degeneration.
Many diseases, such as Alzheimer's, are considered age-related conditions that primarily affect older adults.
The risk of osteoporosis increases due to age-related changes in bone density.
