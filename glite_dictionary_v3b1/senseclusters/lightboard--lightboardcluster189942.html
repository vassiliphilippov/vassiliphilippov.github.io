lightboard

noun
transparent illuminated board
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtbɔːd / (BrE)
a transparent board lit from the edges, used for writing while facing an audience or camera, especially in teaching or presentations

Examples

Teaching lightboard
The teacher used a lightboard to make her online lessons more interactive and engaging.
Engineers demonstrated their design process on a lightboard during the virtual conference.
With the lightboard, presenters can write and face the camera at the same time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blackboard - a dark surface for writing on with chalk
  • synonym: whiteboard - a smooth white surface for writing or drawing on with markers
