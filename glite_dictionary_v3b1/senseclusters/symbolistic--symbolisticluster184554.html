symbolistic

adjective
using symbols
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪmbəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsɪmbəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by the use of symbols, especially in art or literature

Examples

Symbolistic meaning in stories
The novel's symbolistic approach invites readers to interpret deeper meanings behind the characters' actions.
Her paintings have a highly symbolistic style, filled with recurring images and motifs.
Critics praised the film for its symbolistic use of color and light to convey emotion.
