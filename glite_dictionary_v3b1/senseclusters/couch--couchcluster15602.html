couch

noun
upholstered seat for several people
2.5
Pronunciation: / kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
A long, comfortable upholstered seat with a back and arms, designed to seat more than one person.

Examples

A couch in the living room
She fell asleep on the couch while watching television.
The cat curled up at one end of the couch.
They bought a new leather couch for their living room.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: armchair - a comfortable chair with sides that support your arms
  • synonym: sofa - a similar upholstered seat usually with arms and a back
