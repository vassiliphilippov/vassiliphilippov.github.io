Erythronium montanum

noun
perennial herb with white flowers from NW US
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɪθroʊniəm mɒnˈtænəm / (AmE), / ɪˌrɪθrəʊniəm mɒnˈtɑːnəm / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the northwestern United States, noted for its large white flowers with orange markings, often found near the snow line.
