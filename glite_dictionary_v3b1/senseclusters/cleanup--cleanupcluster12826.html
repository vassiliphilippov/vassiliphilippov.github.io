cleanup

noun
fourth batter in baseball lineup
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklinˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈkliːnʌp / (BrE)
(Baseball) The fourth position in the batting order, typically assigned to the team's most powerful hitter.

Examples

Batting cleanup
Batting cleanup, he hit a home run that cleared the bases.
He was proud to be chosen for the cleanup position after a strong start to the season.
The manager decided to move her into the cleanup spot for tonight's game.
