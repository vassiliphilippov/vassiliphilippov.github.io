Nemertea

noun
phylum of unsegmented marine worms
9.9
Pronunciation: / nəˈmɜrtiə /; / nəˈmɜːrtiə / (AmE), / nɛˈmɜːtiə /; / nɪˈmɜːtɪə / (BrE)
a group (phylum) of mostly marine, elongated worms with a unique, extendable proboscis, also called proboscis worms
