rosin bag

noun
bag of rosin for pitchers' grip
7.8 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈrɑː.zən bæg/ (AmE), /ˈrɒ.zən bæg/ (BrE)
A small bag filled with powdered rosin, used by baseball pitchers to improve their grip on the ball.

Examples
