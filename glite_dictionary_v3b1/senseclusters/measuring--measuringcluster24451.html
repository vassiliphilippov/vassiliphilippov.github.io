measuring

noun
the process of assigning numbers or values
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of assigning numbers or values to things according to a specific rule or standard

Examples

Accurate measuring
Accurate measuring is essential in scientific experiments.
His mental measuring of the distance was remarkably accurate.
The technician devoted several hours to the careful measuring of the samples.
