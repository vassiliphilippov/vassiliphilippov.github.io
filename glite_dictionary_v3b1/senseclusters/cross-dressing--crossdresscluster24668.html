cross-dressing

noun
wearing clothes of the opposite sex
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔs ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒs ˌdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
The practice of wearing clothes typically associated with the opposite sex.

Examples

Cross-dressing for fun
He took part in a charity event that encouraged cross-dressing for fun.
Some famous performers have made cross-dressing a central part of their stage act.
The play explored themes of cross-dressing and identity.
