tasting

noun
event for sampling food or drink
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪstɪŋ / (BrE)
an event or occasion where people sample and compare different foods or drinks

Examples

A tasting event
She offered a tasting of her homemade desserts at the festival.
The chef invited guests to a cheese tasting before the dinner.
We attended a wine tasting at the local vineyard last weekend.
He discovered his favorite tea at a tasting organized by the club.
The restaurant offers a wine tasting every Friday evening.
We attended a cheese tasting at the local market last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sampling - the act of trying a small amount of something for evaluation
  • antonym: smelling - the act of sensing odors with the nose
