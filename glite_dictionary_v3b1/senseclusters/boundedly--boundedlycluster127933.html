boundedly

adverb
in a limited way
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊndɪdli / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is limited or restricted within certain bounds

Examples

To grow boundedly
The population of the species grows boundedly due to limited resources in the environment.
In mathematics, a sequence may increase boundedly without ever exceeding a certain value.
The company's profits rose boundedly over the last decade, never surpassing a set threshold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: limitedly - in a limited or restricted manner
  • antonym: unboundedly - in a way that is not limited or restricted
