Lycopodiaceae

noun
a family of clubmosses (plants)
Pronunciation: / laɪkəˌpoʊdiˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / laɪkəˌpɒdiˈeɪsiː /; / laɪkəˌpəʊdiˈeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of spore-producing vascular plants, commonly called clubmosses, belonging to the order Lycopodiales
