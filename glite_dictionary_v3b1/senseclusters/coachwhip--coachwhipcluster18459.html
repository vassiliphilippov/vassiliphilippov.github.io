coachwhip

noun
a slender south US/Mexico whipsnake
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtʃˌwɪp / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtʃˌwɪp / (BrE)
A long, slender whipsnake native to the southern United States and Mexico, with a tail resembling a braided whip.

Examples

A coachwhip snake
The coachwhip can move quickly across the desert to escape predators.
We spotted a coachwhip basking in the sun near the rocky outcrop.
With its distinctive, braided-looking tail, the coachwhip is easily recognized by herpetologists.
