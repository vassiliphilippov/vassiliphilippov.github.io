klong

noun
Thai canal
Pronunciation: / klɔŋ / (AmE), / klɒŋ / (BrE)
a canal, especially in Thailand, used for transportation or drainage

Examples

Thai klong canal
The boat slowly moved down the klong, passing houses built on stilts along the water.
Many locals use the klong to travel quickly through the city during rush hour.
During the rainy season, the klong helps prevent flooding in the surrounding neighborhoods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canal - an artificial waterway for boats or irrigation
  • antonym: river - a large natural stream of water flowing in a channel
