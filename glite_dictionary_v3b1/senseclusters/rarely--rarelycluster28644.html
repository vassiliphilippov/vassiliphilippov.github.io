rarely

adverb
not often; infrequently
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛrli / (AmE), / ˈreəli / (BrE)
Not often; on very few occasions.

Examples

To rarely happen
Rarely do we see such honesty in politics today.
She rarely eats dessert after dinner.
The old book was rarely opened, except on special occasions.
