garam

adjective
spicy or warming
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːrəm / (AmE), / ˈɡʌrəm / (BrE)
describing food or drink that is spicy and produces a warming or burning sensation in the mouth

Examples

Garam curry
The chef prepared a garam curry that warmed everyone on the cold evening.
She prefers her tea mild, but her brother likes it garam with plenty of ginger.
A bowl of garam soup is perfect for chilly winter nights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - lacking strong flavor or spice
  • synonym: spicy - having a strong, hot flavor from spices
