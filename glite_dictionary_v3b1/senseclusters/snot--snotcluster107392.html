snot

noun
nasal mucus
2.6
Pronunciation: / snɑt / (AmE), / snɒt / (BrE)
Nasal mucus, especially when it is visible or produced in excessive amounts.

Examples

Wipe snot away
A cold often causes children to have a lot of snot.
He wiped the snot from his nose with a tissue.
She sneezed loudly, splattering snot on her sleeve.
