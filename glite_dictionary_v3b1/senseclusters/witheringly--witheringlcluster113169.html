witheringly

adverb
in a scornful or devastating manner
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪð.ər.ɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪð.ə.rɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a way that is intended to scorn, devastate, or overwhelm; with intense force or contempt.

Examples

To reply witheringly
During the debate, he replied witheringly to his opponent's weak arguments.
She looked at him witheringly, causing him to shrink back in embarrassment.
The critic witheringly dismissed the new play as uninspired and dull.
