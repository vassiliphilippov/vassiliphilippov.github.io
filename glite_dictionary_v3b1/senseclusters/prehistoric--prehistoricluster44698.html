prehistoric

adjective
before written history
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.hɪˈstɔːr.ɪk /; / ˌpriːhɪˈstɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriː.hɪˈstɒr.ɪk /; / ˌpriːhɪˈstɒrɪk / (BrE)
