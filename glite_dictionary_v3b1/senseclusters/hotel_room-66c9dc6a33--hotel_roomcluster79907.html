hotel room

noun
room in a hotel for guests
1.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈtɛl ruːm / (AmE), / həʊˈtɛl ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a hotel that is available for guests to sleep in, typically including a bed and a bathroom

Examples

A hotel room with bed
After a long drive, they were relieved to finally check into their hotel room.
She reserved a hotel room with a view of the ocean for their anniversary.
The hotel room was clean and spacious, with fresh towels and complimentary toiletries.
