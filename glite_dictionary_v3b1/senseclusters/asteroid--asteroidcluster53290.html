asteroid

noun
small rocky body orbiting the sun
2.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈæs.tə.rɔɪd/ (AmE), /ˈæs.tə.rɔɪd/ (BrE)
a small rocky or metallic celestial body that orbits the sun, especially between Mars and Jupiter

Examples

A asteroid belt
Scientists discovered a new asteroid orbiting between Mars and Jupiter.
Some asteroids contain valuable metals that could one day be mined in space.
The Earth is occasionally threatened by large asteroids passing nearby.

  • synonym: meteorite - a fragment of an asteroid or comet that survives passage through the atmosphere and lands on Earth
