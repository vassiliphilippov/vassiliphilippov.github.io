overexcitability

noun
excessive sensitivity
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪkˌsaɪtəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪkˌsaɪtəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
a state of being unusually or excessively sensitive or responsive to stimuli, experiences, or emotions

Examples

  • synonym: hypersensitivity - an increased or excessive sensitivity to stimuli
  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of strong reaction to stimuli
