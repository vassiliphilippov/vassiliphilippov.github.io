pique

noun
a sudden feeling of anger
9.9
Pronunciation: / piːk / (AmE), / piːk / (BrE)
a feeling or sudden outburst of irritation or anger, often because of wounded pride

Examples

A fit of pique
He left the meeting in sudden pique, unable to hide his anger.
Her pique was obvious, but she quickly regained her composure.
She slammed the door in a fit of pique after the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state free from anger or agitation
  • synonym: resentment - bitterness or anger at being treated unfairly
  • synonym: temper - a person's state of anger or mood
