smothery

adjective
overly controlling
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌðəri / (AmE), / ˈsmʌðəri / (BrE)
showing excessive or overprotective affection or attention that makes someone feel restricted or suffocated emotionally

Examples

Smothery mother
Her smothery behavior made her son feel like he had no independence.
Some people find constant check-ins from friends a bit too smothery for their taste.
He realized his smothery attention was pushing his partner away instead of bringing them closer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overbearing - excessively controlling or domineering
  • antonym: supportive - providing encouragement or help without being controlling
