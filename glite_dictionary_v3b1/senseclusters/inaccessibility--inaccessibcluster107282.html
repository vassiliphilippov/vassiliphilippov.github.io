inaccessibility

noun
the state of being inaccessible
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəkˌsɛsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪnəkˌsɛsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being difficult or impossible to reach, approach, or obtain

Examples

The inaccessibility of places
Many complained about the inaccessibility of the website for people with disabilities.
The inaccessibility of the mountain peak attracts only the most experienced climbers.
The inaccessibility of the remote village made delivering medical supplies extremely challenging.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accessibility - the quality of being easy to reach or obtain
  • synonym: inaccessible - not able to be reached or approached
