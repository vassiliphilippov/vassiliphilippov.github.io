palingenetic

adjective
relating to palingenesis or rebirth
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpælɪn.dʒəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpalɪndʒɪˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of palingenesis, especially in reference to the theory of regeneration or rebirth, particularly in biology or philosophy.

Examples

Certain religious beliefs emphasize the palingenetic nature of the soul.
The palingenetic cycle of the mythical phoenix fascinated ancient philosophers.
The scientist proposed a palingenetic interpretation of evolutionary development.
