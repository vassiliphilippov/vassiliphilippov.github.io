chickee

noun
open-sided shelter
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪ.kiː/ (BrE)
a small, open-sided shelter with a thatched roof, often raised on posts, used for shade or rest in outdoor areas

Examples

Picnic chickee
We ate lunch under a chickee by the river to escape the midday sun.
The park built a new chickee for visitors to use during picnics and gatherings.
Fishermen often rest in a chickee while waiting for a good catch.
