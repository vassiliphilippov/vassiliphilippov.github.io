froe

verb
to split wood
Pronunciation: / froʊ / (AmE), / frəʊ / (BrE)
to split wood along the grain using a froe, a wedge-shaped tool.

Examples

To froe a log
The carpenter expertly froes the logs to make wooden shingles for the roof.
After cutting the tree, they froed the trunk into planks for building.
Learning to froe wood properly requires patience and the right technique.
