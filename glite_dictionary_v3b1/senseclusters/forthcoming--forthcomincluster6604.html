forthcoming

adjective
ready or available when needed
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrθˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
available when needed or promised; ready to be provided or given when required

Examples

Forthcoming help
Although funds had been promised, government support was not forthcoming when the crisis hit.
Despite multiple emails, no apology was forthcoming from the company.
The report was requested last month, but it is still not forthcoming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: available - able to be used or obtained; at someone's disposal
  • antonym: unavailable - not able to be used or obtained
