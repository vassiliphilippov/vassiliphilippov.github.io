Welsh language

noun
the Celtic language of Wales
Pronunciation: / wɛlʃ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / wɛlʃ ˈlaŋgwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Celtic language spoken in Wales, forming part of the Brythonic branch of Celtic languages

Examples

Welsh language spoken in Wales
Many people in Wales still speak Welsh language at home and in schools.
She is taking a course to learn the Welsh language so she can communicate with her grandparents.
The preservation of the Welsh language is an important cultural goal in Wales.
