Cadillac

proper noun
French explorer
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkædɪˌlæk / (AmE), / ˈkædɪlæk / (BrE)
Antoine de la Mothe, sieur de Cadillac, a French explorer and colonial administrator who founded Detroit in 1701.

Examples

Cadillac founded Detroit
In 1701, Cadillac established a trading post that later became the city of Detroit.
Cadillac played a significant role in the early history of French North America.
Many historians study the life and achievements of Cadillac in colonial America.
