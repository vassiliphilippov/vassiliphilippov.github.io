cool

noun
pleasantly low temperature
Pronunciation: / kul / (AmE), / kuːl / (BrE)
the quality or state of being at a pleasantly low or refreshing temperature

Examples

Morning cool
Early risers appreciate the cool before the sun gets too strong.
The cool of the shaded verandah offered relief from the humidity.
We sat outside enjoying the cool of the evening after a hot day.

  • antonym: warmth - the quality or state of being warm
