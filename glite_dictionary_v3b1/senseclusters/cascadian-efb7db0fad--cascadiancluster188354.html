Cascadian

noun
supporter of Cascadia
9.9
Pronunciation: / kæsˈkeɪdiən / (AmE), / kæsˈkeɪdiən / (BrE)
a person who supports the Cascadia bioregional movement or advocates for regional autonomy or independence in the Pacific Northwest

Examples

A Cascadian activist
As a proud Cascadian, she attends rallies promoting environmental protection in the Pacific Northwest.
Many Cascadians believe in creating a sustainable and independent region.
The flag of Cascadia is often displayed by dedicated Cascadian activists at community events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a larger group or country
  • antonym: unionist - a person who supports political or regional unity
