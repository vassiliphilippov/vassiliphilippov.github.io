puddling

noun
iron or soil treatment
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpʌdlɪŋ / (BrE)
a process of stirring molten iron or wetting soil to improve its properties, especially in metallurgy or agriculture

Examples

Puddling of iron
The puddling of iron was a key step in early steel manufacturing.
Farmers use puddling to prepare rice fields by wetting and working the soil.
Advances in puddling technology improved the quality of metal produced in the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refining - the process of purifying a substance, especially metals
  • synonym: smelting - extracting metal from its ore by heating and melting
