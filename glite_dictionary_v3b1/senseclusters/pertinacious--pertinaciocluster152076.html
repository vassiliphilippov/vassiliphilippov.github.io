pertinacious

adjective
stubbornly persistent or unyielding
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝː.tɪˈneɪ.ʃəs / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.tɪˈneɪ.ʃəs / (BrE)
holding firmly to an opinion, purpose, or course of action; stubbornly persistent.

Examples

A pertinacious person
Despite criticism from her peers, she remained pertinacious in her pursuit of the truth.
Only someone truly pertinacious could have endured so many setbacks and still succeeded.
The lawyer argued his case with pertinacious determination, refusing to back down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstinate - stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion
  • synonym: tenacious - tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent
