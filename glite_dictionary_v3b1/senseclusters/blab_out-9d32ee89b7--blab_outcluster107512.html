blab out

verb
divulge secrets carelessly
Pronunciation: / blæb aʊt / (AmE), / blab aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

To blab out secrets
Don’t blab out the surprise party plans to anyone.
He tends to blab out confidential information when he’s nervous.
She accidentally blabbed out the secret during lunch.
