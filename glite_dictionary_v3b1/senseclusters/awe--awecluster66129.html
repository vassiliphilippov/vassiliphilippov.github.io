awe

verb
to inspire awe in someone
4.1 2.56/m
Pronunciation: / …îÀê / (AmE), / …îÀê / (BrE)
to fill someone with a feeling of wonder, respect, or fear; to inspire awe in someone

Examples

To awe the crowd
Even as an experienced scientist, she was awed by the complexity of the universe.
The breathtaking view awed everyone who reached the mountain summit.
The magician's talent never failed to awe his audience.

  • astound
    4.2 2.68/m
