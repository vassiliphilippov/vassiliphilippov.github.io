predatorily

adverb
aggressively or exploitatively
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛdətɔrəli / (AmE), / ˈprɛdətərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is aggressive or exploits others, like a predator

Examples

To act predatorily in business
The company expanded predatorily by buying out its smaller competitors.
He looked predatorily at the opportunity to take over the project.
Some animals hunt predatorily to survive in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggressively - in a forceful or attacking way
  • synonym: exploitatively - in a way that takes unfair advantage of others
  • antonym: passively - in a way that does not take action or initiative
