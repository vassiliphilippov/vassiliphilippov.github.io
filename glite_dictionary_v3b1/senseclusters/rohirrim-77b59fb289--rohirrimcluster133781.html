Rohirrim

proper noun
people of Rohan
Pronunciation: / roʊˈhɪrɪm / (AmE), / rəʊˈhɪrɪm / (BrE)
the people of Rohan in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth, famous for their horse-riding and bravery
