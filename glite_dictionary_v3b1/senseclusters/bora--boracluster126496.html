bora

noun
Aboriginal ceremonial ground
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːrə / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrə / (BrE)
an Australian Aboriginal ceremonial ground used for initiation ceremonies and important traditional rites

Examples

Sacred bora ground
The elders gathered at the bora to prepare for the annual initiation ceremony.
Visitors are not allowed to enter the sacred bora without permission from the community.
Stories about the bora have been passed down through generations in Aboriginal culture.
