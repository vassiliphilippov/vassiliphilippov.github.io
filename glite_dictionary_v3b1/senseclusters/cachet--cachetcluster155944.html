cachet

noun
royal French warrant for arrest or death
7.9 9.2
Pronunciation: /kæ.ˈʃeɪ/ (AmE), /kæ.ˈʃeɪ/ (BrE)
a warrant formerly issued by a French king, authorizing imprisonment or death, typically in the form of a signed letter under royal seal
