miracle

noun
extraordinary, supernatural event
3.5 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪr.ə.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈmɪr.ə.kəl/ (BrE)
an extraordinary and welcome event that cannot be explained by natural or scientific laws and is often attributed to divine agency; an amazing or wonderful occurrence

Examples

A miracle recovery
Some people considered his recovery a medical miracle.
They prayed for a miracle when the storm hit their village.
Winning the lottery twice is often described as a miracle.
It will take a miracle for the team to win the championship this year.
Some people consider the birth of a healthy baby a miracle.
Winning the lottery was nothing short of a miracle for the struggling family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phenomenon - an observable event, especially one that is impressive or extraordinary
  • synonym: wonder - something that causes amazement or admiration
