scenery

noun
natural features of a landscape
1.0 5.66/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀên…ôri / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀên…ôri / (BrE)
the natural features of a landscape considered as a whole, especially when they are beautiful

Examples

Beautiful scenery
He stopped to take a photo of the rural scenery stretching before him.
The mountain scenery was breathtaking during our drive.
Visitors come here to enjoy the beautiful coastal scenery.

Synonyms

  • landscape
    3.0 25/m
    landscape is an area of land often shown in paintings and photos, while scenery refers to the whole natural features seen.
  • view
    1.0 68/m
    view is what you can see from a place, often changing and personal, while scenery means the natural landscape as a whole.
  • scene
    1.9 19/m
    scene is a single moment or picture that may include people or buildings, while scenery means the natural landscape seen as a whole.
