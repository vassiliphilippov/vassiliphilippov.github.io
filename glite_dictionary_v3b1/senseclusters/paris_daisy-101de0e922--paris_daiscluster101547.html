Paris daisy

noun
ornamental plant with pale yellow daisylike flowers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛərɪs ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈparɪs ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a perennial subshrub native to the Canary Islands, with typically pale yellow, daisylike flowers, often grown as an ornamental and sometimes included in the genus Chrysanthemum

Examples

The Paris daisy flower
In spring, the Paris daisy blooms attract a variety of pollinators to the garden.
