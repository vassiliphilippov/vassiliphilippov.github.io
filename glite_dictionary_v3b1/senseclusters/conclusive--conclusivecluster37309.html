Synonyms

  • decisive
    3.2 0.5/m
    decisive is clear and settles a choice or result quickly, while conclusive focuses on ending doubt about evidence.
  • definitive
    5.6 4/m
    definitive gives a final, authoritative decision or answer, while conclusive is evidence that ends doubt in a case.
  • unequivocal
    6.7 1/m
    unequivocal is completely clear with no ambiguity, while conclusive is evidence or argument that proves a case.
  • undeniable
    4.2 3/m
    undeniable seems impossible to deny or argue against, while conclusive means the evidence actually proves the matter.
  • proven
