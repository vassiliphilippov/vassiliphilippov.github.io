slash

noun
punctuation mark: /
Pronunciation: / slæʃ / (AmE), / slæʃ / (BrE)
the punctuation mark (/) used to separate alternatives, related items, or parts of information

Examples

A slash mark
Always use a slash to indicate alternatives such as 'and/or' in writing.
Dates are often written with a slash, like 12/31/2022.
The web address should contain only one forward slash after 'http:'.

Related Terms

  • antonym: backslash - the punctuation mark (\) used chiefly in computing
  • synonym: solidus - another term for the slash punctuation mark
