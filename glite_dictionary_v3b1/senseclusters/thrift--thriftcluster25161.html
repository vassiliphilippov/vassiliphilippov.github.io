thrift

noun
secondhand store
Pronunciation: / θrɪft / (AmE), / θrɪft / (BrE)
a store that sells used goods, such as clothing and furniture, at low prices

Examples

Thrift store shopping
She found a vintage jacket at the local thrift.
Many students shop at a thrift to save money on clothes and furniture.
The thrift near my house always has interesting books and unique decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charity shop - a shop selling donated items to raise money for charity
  • synonym: consignment shop - a store selling secondhand goods on behalf of the original owner
