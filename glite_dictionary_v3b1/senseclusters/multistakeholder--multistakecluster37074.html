multistakeholder

adjective
involving many parties
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsteɪkˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsteɪkhəʊldə / (BrE)
involving or relating to several different parties or groups with an interest in a process or decision

Examples

Multistakeholder approach
The multistakeholder committee included representatives from government, business, and civil society.
A multistakeholder approach can help resolve complex issues by considering different perspectives.
Effective multistakeholder engagement is essential for successful international negotiations.

Related Terms
