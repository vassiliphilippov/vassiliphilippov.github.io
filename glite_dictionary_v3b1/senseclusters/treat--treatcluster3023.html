treat

verb
hold negotiations to reach an agreement
5.1
Pronunciation: / trit / (AmE), / triːt / (BrE)
(archaic) To engage in formal negotiations or discussions in order to reach an agreement.

Examples

To treat with rivals
In the seventeenth century, nations would often treat directly with hostile powers.
Several envoys were sent to treat for peace after years of conflict.
The ambassadors were instructed to treat with the rebels before any action was taken.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negotiate - to discuss something formally in order to reach an agreement
