legibly

adverb
in a readable manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒəbli / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear enough to read.

Examples

To write legibly
He addressed the envelope so legibly that the mail carrier had no trouble delivering it.
Please print your name legibly on the application form.
The teacher reminded the students to write legibly so she could read their answers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illegibly - in a manner that is difficult or impossible to read
