Chilean firebush

noun
South American shrub/tree with scarlet flowers
8.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈliən ˈfaɪɚˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / tʃɪˈliːən ˈfaɪəˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
