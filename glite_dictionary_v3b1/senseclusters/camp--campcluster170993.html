Synonyms

  • campground

    a campground is an area set aside for tents or RVs during holidays, while a camp can also mean military or refugee sites.
  • encampment
    6.0 2.35/m
    an encampment is a temporary, often organized shelter area used by soldiers or refugees, while a camp covers many short-term stays.
