output-to-input ratio

noun
ratio of output power to input power
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtpʊt tuˈɪnˌpʊt ˈreɪʃioʊ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtpʊt tuːˈɪnpʊt ˈreɪʃɪəʊ / (BrE)
the ratio of the output power of a transducer to its input power

Examples

High output-to-input ratio
A high output-to-input ratio is desirable when designing energy-efficient transducers.
Engineers measured the output-to-input ratio to assess the efficiency of the solar panel system.
The output-to-input ratio of the new amplifier is significantly higher than that of the old model.

  • synonym: efficiency - the percent of input energy converted to useful output
