heroical

adjective
having qualities of a hero
Pronunciation: / hɪˈroʊɪkəl / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊɪkəl / (BrE)
Having or showing the qualities or courage expected of heroes; heroic.

Examples

Heroical efforts
Her heroical efforts during the crisis inspired everyone.
The general was celebrated for his heroical deeds in battle.
The poet described the knight’s journey in a series of heroical verses.
