Examples

A book review article

Synonyms

  • review
    3.0 39/m
    review gives a general evaluation of many works in newspapers or online, while a book review focuses only on one book.
  • critical review
    4.1 0.12/m
    critical review gives a detailed judgment in formal writing and academic contexts, while a book review appears in media for readers.
  • review article
    3.9 0.95/m
    review article summarizes and evaluates existing works in academic journals, while a book review speaks directly to general readers about one book.
  • literary review
    4.2 0.03/m
    literary review discusses novels and poems in depth for literary audiences, while a book review aims to guide general readers about a single book.
  • critique
    5.6 15/m
    critique is a careful, often academic analysis of a work's strengths and faults, while a book review is a public evaluation for readers.
  • criticism
    3.7 8.40/m
    criticism is the broader practice or field of judging art or literature, while a book review is a single published evaluation of a book.
