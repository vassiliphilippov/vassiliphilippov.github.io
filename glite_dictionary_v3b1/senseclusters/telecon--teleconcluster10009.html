telecon

noun
teleconferencing system
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
a system or service that enables teleconferencing, allowing people to communicate remotely by audio or video.

Examples

Company telecon system
The company upgraded its telecon to support more participants during international meetings.
Problems with the telecon delayed the start of the virtual conference by half an hour.
She scheduled a telecon with the overseas team to discuss the project updates.

  • synonym: videoconference - a meeting held by video link between people in different locations
