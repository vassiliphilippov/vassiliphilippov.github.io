ecumenically

adverb
promoting religious unity
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkjuːˈmɛnɪkli / (AmE), / ˌiːkjʊˈmɛnɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that promotes cooperation or understanding between different religions or faiths

Examples

Ecumenically join religions
The conference was organized ecumenically to encourage dialogue among various religious communities.
Leaders from different faiths worked ecumenically to address social issues in their city.
The charity operates ecumenically, welcoming volunteers from all religious backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interfaith - involving or relating to different religions or faiths
  • antonym: sectarian - relating to or promoting the interests of a particular religious group, often excluding others
