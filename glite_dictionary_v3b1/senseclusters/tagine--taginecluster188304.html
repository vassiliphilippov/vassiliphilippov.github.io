tagine

noun
North African stew or pot
6.1
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈʒiːn/ /; / /ˈtæ.dʒɪn/ (AmE), /tə.ˈʒiːn/ /; / /ˈtæ.dʒɪn/ (BrE)
a North African stew slow-cooked with meat, vegetables, and spices, or the earthenware pot used to cook it

Examples

A tagine stew

A traditional tagine is cooked slowly in a cone-shaped clay pot.
We tried a vegetarian tagine filled with chickpeas and root vegetables at the Moroccan restaurant.
