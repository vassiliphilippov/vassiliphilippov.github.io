tagine

noun
North African stew or pot
6.1
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈʒin / (AmE), / tæˈʒiːn / (BrE)
a North African stew slow-cooked with meat, vegetables, and spices, or the earthenware pot used to cook it

Examples

A tagine stew
The chef prepared a fragrant lamb tagine with apricots and almonds for dinner.
A traditional tagine is cooked slowly in a cone-shaped clay pot.
We tried a vegetarian tagine filled with chickpeas and root vegetables at the Moroccan restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casserole - a dish cooked slowly in an oven, often in a covered dish
  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
