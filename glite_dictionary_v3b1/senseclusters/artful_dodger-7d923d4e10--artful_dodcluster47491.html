artful dodger

noun
clever, evasive person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrt.fəl ˈdɑ.dʒər / (AmE), / ˈɑːt.fəl ˈdɒdʒ.ə / (BrE)
a clever person who skillfully avoids responsibilities or blame, especially by using cunning or deceit

Examples

The artful dodger of chores
Among his friends, Tom was known as the artful dodger of chores and responsibilities.
She called him an artful dodger for slipping away when work was assigned.
The artful dodger always managed to escape the manager’s attention during cleanup duty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evader - a person who avoids doing something, especially by cleverness
  • antonym: scapegoat - a person blamed for the faults of others
