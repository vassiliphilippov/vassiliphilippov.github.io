reborn

adjective
restored to new life or vigor
Pronunciation: / riˈbɔrn / (AmE), / riˈbɔːn / (BrE)
restored to a state of renewed existence, strength, or importance; revived or given new life

Examples

Reborn city
After years of neglect, the historic theater has been reborn as a vibrant cultural center.
Her passion for painting was reborn after she enrolled in an art class.
The city felt reborn after the successful completion of urban renewal projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restored - brought back to a former or original state
  • synonym: revived - given new strength or energy; brought back to life or consciousness
  • antonym: stagnant - showing no activity or development; dull and inactive
