form

verb
impress or establish in the mind
3.9 8.07/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrm / (AmE), / f…îÀêm / (BrE)
to establish or impress (an idea, belief, habit, etc.) firmly in the mind

Examples

To form a habit
She tried to form a positive self-image in her daughter's mind.
Teachers strive to form good study habits in their students from an early age.
Travel can form lasting memories and perspectives in a young mind.
