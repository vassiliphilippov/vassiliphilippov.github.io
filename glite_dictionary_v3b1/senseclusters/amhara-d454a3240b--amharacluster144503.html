Amhara

noun
member of an Ethiopian ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈhɑrə / (AmE), / amˈhɑːrə / (BrE)
a member of a Semitic-speaking ethnic group native to northern Ethiopia

Examples

Amhara people
Many Amhara speak the Amharic language and practice Ethiopian Orthodox Christianity.
She learned about the traditions of the Amhara during her visit to northern Ethiopia.
The Amhara have played a significant role in Ethiopia's history and culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Oromo - member of a major ethnic group in Ethiopia
