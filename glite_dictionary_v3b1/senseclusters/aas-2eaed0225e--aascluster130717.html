AAS

noun
associate degree in applied science
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌeɪ ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌeɪ ˈɛs / (BrE)
an associate degree awarded for studies in applied science, typically after a two-year program at a college or technical school

Examples

An AAS degree
Many students pursue an AAS to quickly enter the workforce.

With an AAS, you can qualify for various technical positions in healthcare and industry.
