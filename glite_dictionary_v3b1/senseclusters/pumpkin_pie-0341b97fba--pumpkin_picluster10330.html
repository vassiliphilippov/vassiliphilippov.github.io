pumpkin pie

noun
sweet pie with pumpkin filling
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌmpkɪn paɪ / (AmE), / ˈpʌmpkɪn paɪ / (BrE)
A sweet pie with a spiced filling made from mashed pumpkin, milk, eggs, and sugar, baked in a pastry crust.

Examples

A pumpkin pie slice
Her homemade pumpkin pie won first place at the county fair.
We always serve pumpkin pie for dessert at Thanksgiving dinner.
Would you like a slice of pumpkin pie with whipped cream?
