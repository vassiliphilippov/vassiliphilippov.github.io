brume

noun
state of vagueness
9.9
Pronunciation: / bruːm / (AmE), / bruːm / (BrE)
a figurative or literary state of vagueness, confusion, or gloom that obscures perception or understanding

Examples

Brume of confusion
After the shocking news, a brume of confusion settled over the entire office.
The novel's ending left readers in a brume of uncertainty and speculation.
His memories of childhood were shrouded in a brume that made details hard to recall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: haze - a state of mental confusion or lack of clarity
