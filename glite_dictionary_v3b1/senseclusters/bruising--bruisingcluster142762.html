bruising

noun
skin discoloration from injury
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbruːzɪŋ / (BrE)
discolored areas on the skin caused by injury or impact that breaks blood vessels under the surface

Examples

Severe bruising
The athlete had extensive bruising on his legs after the match.
Doctors examined the bruising to determine how serious the injury was.
She noticed some bruising on her arm after bumping into the table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contusion - an area of injured tissue or skin where blood vessels have been broken
  • synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a part of the body, often due to injury
