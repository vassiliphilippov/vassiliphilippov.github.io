ultrawideband

adjective
radio technology type
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈwaɪdbænd / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈwaɪdbænd / (BrE)
relating to or using radio technology that transmits signals over a very wide range of frequencies.

Examples

Ultrawideband technology
The new smartphone supports ultrawideband communication for fast and precise location tracking.
Researchers are developing ultrawideband sensors to improve wireless data transfer in hospitals.
Many modern devices use ultrawideband technology for secure and accurate short-range communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadband - using a wide range of frequencies for data transmission
  • antonym: narrowband - using a small range of frequencies for data transmission
