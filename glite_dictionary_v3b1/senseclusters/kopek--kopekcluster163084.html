kopek

noun
Russian currency unit (1/100 ruble)
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌpɛk / (AmE), / ˈkəʊpɛk / (BrE)
A monetary unit of Russia, equal to one hundredth of a ruble.

Examples
