invasive

noun
harmful spreading species
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈveɪsɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈveɪsɪv / (BrE)
a species that spreads rapidly and can cause harm to native environments or ecosystems

Examples

Invasive plant species
Many invasives threaten the balance of local ecosystems.
The government is working to control invasives that damage native plant life.
Gardeners are encouraged to remove invasives to protect local biodiversity.
