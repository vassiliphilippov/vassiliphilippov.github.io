mix-up

noun
mistake due to confusion
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmɪksˌʌp / (BrE)
a mistake resulting from confusing one thing with another, often leading to misunderstanding or disorder

Examples

A mix-up with tickets
A ticket mix-up forced several passengers to miss their flight.
The company apologized after a mix-up delayed all the deliveries in the area.
There was a mix-up at the pharmacy, so I received the wrong medication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blunder - a careless or serious mistake
  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and free from confusion
  • synonym: confusion - a state of being unclear or mistaken about something
