hypothesis

noun
idea based on limited evidence
1.0 6.1
Pronunciation: / haɪˈpɑθəsɪs /; / haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs / (AmE), / haɪˈpɒθəsɪs /; / haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs / (BrE)
an idea or explanation that is suggested as a possible answer to a question, but has not yet been proven
