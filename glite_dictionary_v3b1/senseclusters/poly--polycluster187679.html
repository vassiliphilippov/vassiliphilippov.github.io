poly

particle
prefix meaning many
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑli / (AmE), / ˈpɒli / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'many' or 'multiple', used to form compound words

Examples

Poly- prefix
The prefix poly is used in words like 'polygon' to mean 'many sides'.
In 'polyglot', poly indicates someone who knows many languages.
Scientists use poly in terms like 'polyatomic' to describe molecules with many atoms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mono- - a prefix meaning 'one' or 'single'
  • synonym: multi- - a prefix meaning 'many' or 'more than one'
