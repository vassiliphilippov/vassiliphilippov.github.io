hammock

noun
suspended canvas or rope bed
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmək / (AmE), / ˈhæmək / (BrE)
a bed made of canvas or rope mesh, suspended at both ends, typically between two trees or posts, and used for resting or sleeping

Examples

A hammock between trees
She read her book while gently swinging in the hammock on the porch.
The campsite included a tent and a cozy hammock for afternoon naps.
We strung a hammock between two palm trees and relaxed in the shade.
