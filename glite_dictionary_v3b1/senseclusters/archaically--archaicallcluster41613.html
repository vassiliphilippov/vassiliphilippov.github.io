archaically

adverb
in an old-fashioned way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈkeɪɪkli / (AmE), / ɑːˈkeɪɪkli / (BrE)
in an old-fashioned or outdated manner, typical of a much earlier time

Examples

Written archaically
The document was written archaically, making it difficult for modern readers to understand.
He spoke archaically, using expressions that had fallen out of everyday use.
The ceremony was conducted archaically, following traditions from centuries ago.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modernly - in a way that is typical of the present or recent times
  • synonym: old-fashionedly - in a way that is typical of the past
