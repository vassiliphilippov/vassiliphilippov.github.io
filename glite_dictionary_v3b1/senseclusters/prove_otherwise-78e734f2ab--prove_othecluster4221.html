prove otherwise

verb
show the opposite or a different result
1.0
Pronunciation: / pruv ˈʌðɚˌwaɪz / (AmE), / pruːv ˈʌðəwaɪz / (BrE)
to show that the opposite or a different result from what was claimed or expected is true

Examples

To prove otherwise
He believed the rumors until the facts proved otherwise.
Many expected the plan to fail, but her leadership proved otherwise.
The evidence may prove otherwise if examined closely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disprove - to show something is not true
