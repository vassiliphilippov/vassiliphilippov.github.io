charity

noun
organization providing help or support
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃær.ə.t̬i /; / ˈtʃæɹəti / (AmE), / ˈtʃær.ɪ.ti /; / ˈtʃærəti / (BrE)
an organization that helps people in need or supports a public cause

Examples

A charity organization
Several charities have come together to address homelessness in the city.
She left a portion of her estate to a charity supporting children’s education.
The local charity organizes food drives every winter to help families in need.
She donates a portion of her salary to a local charity every month.
The charity organized a fundraising event to help homeless families.
The hospital is run by a well-known charity that supports cancer research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foundation - an institution supported by an endowment, often for charitable purposes
  • synonym: nonprofit - an organization not established for profit
