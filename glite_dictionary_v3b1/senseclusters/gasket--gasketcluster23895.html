gasket

noun
seal for pipes or machine parts
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡaskɪt / (BrE)
a flat ring or piece of material placed to seal the junction between two surfaces in a machine or pipe to prevent leakage

Examples

A gasket seal
Always replace the old gasket when installing a new water pump to ensure a tight seal.
Rubber gaskets are commonly used in plumbing to prevent water from escaping.
The engineer inspected the gasket to find the source of the oil leak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seal - a device or substance that is used to join two things together tightly to prevent anything passing between them
