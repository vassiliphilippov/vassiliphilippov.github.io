cutting

noun
newspaper or magazine excerpt
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtɪŋ /; / ˈkʌt̬ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
a piece that has been cut off from something larger, such as a newspaper article, a plant part for growing, or a sample

Examples

Gardeners often use a cutting from a healthy plant to create new plants.
