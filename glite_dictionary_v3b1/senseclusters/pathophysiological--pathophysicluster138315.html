pathophysiological

adjective
disease-related processes
9.2
Pronunciation: /pæ.θoʊ.fɪ.ziː.ə.ˈlɑː.dʒɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /pæ.θəʊ.fɪ.ziː.ə.ˈlɒ.dʒɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to the abnormal processes or changes in the body that happen because of disease or injury

Examples

Pathophysiological changes
The doctor explained the pathophysiological mechanisms behind the patient's symptoms.
Understanding pathophysiological changes is crucial for developing new treatments.
Researchers are investigating the pathophysiological effects of the virus on lung tissue.
The doctor explained the pathophysiological changes that occur in diabetes.
Understanding the pathophysiological basis of the illness can help improve treatment.
Researchers are investigating the pathophysiological mechanisms behind chronic pain.

  • antonym: physiological - relating to the normal functions of living organisms
