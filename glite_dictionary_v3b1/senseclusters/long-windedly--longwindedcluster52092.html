long-windedly

adverb

Examples

To speak long-windedly
He tends to explain technical issues long-windedly, making it hard for listeners to stay focused.
She long-windedly recounted her vacation, describing every meal and activity in great detail.
The senator spoke long-windedly about every detail of the proposal, causing many to lose interest.
