staff

noun
lines for writing musical notes
9.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /stæf/ (AmE), /stɑːf/ /; / /stæf/ (BrE)
(music) A set of five horizontal lines and four spaces used in musical notation to represent different musical pitches.

Examples

Music staff
As a beginner, she practiced drawing a staff before writing any notes.
Each line and space on the staff indicates a different musical pitch.
The notes were written on the staff using both treble and bass clefs.
