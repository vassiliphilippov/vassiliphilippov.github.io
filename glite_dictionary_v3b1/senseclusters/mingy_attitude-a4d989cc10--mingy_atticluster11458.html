mingy attitude

noun
a stingy or miserly mindset
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪndʒi ˈæt.ɪ.tud / (AmE), / ˈmɪndʒi ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd / (BrE)
A stingy or miserly way of thinking or behaving, especially in relation to giving or sharing.

Examples

A mingy attitude to giving
Despite their wealth, the couple’s mingy attitude was evident in their reluctance to donate.
Her mingy attitude made the office holiday gift exchange awkward for everyone.
It was his mingy attitude that led people to stop inviting him to group dinners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous attitude - a mindset characterized by willingness to give or share
  • synonym: stingy - unwilling to give or spend; ungenerous
