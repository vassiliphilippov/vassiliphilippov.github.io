Army of the Righteous

noun
Islamic militant terrorist group in Kashmir
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrmi əv ðə ˈraɪtʃəs / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi əv ðə ˈraɪtʃəs / (BrE)
A militant Islamic terrorist group, primarily active in Kashmir, that fights against India with the stated aim of restoring Islamic rule over the region.

Examples

Army of the Righteous group

Many countries have designated the Army of the Righteous as a terrorist organization.
