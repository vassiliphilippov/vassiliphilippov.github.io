term

verb
to formally name or designate
Pronunciation: / tɜrm / (AmE), / tɜːm / (BrE)
to give a specific name or designation to something, especially in a formal context

Examples

To term a period
Historians have termed this period the Age of Enlightenment.
The agreement was termed a milestone in diplomatic relations.
This phenomenon is often termed 'the butterfly effect' in scientific literature.
