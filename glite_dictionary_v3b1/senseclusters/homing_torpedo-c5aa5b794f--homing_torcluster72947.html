homing torpedo

noun
self-guiding torpedo weapon
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmɪŋ tɔrˈpiːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmɪŋ tɔːˈpiːdəʊ / (BrE)
a torpedo equipped with a guidance system that allows it to actively seek out and follow its target, often by tracking sound or other signals emitted by enemy vessels.

Examples

A homing torpedo missile
A single homing torpedo was enough to force the vessel to change its course.
Advancements in technology have made the homing torpedo more effective at targeting ships in motion.
The navy deployed a homing torpedo to track the enemy submarine with remarkable precision.
