hydremia

noun
excess fluid in blood (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdriː.mi.ə/ /; / /haɪ.driː.ˈmi.ə/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdriː.mi.ə/ /; / /haɪ.driː.ˈmi.ə/ (BrE)
