blowpipe

noun
tube for shooting darts by blowing
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌpaɪp / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌpaɪp / (BrE)
A tube used to shoot darts or other projectiles by blowing through it.

Examples

Hunt with a blowpipe
Ancient tribes often used a blowpipe for hunting small animals.
He crafted a blowpipe from bamboo and practiced shooting darts in the backyard.
The hunter skillfully aimed the blowpipe at the target.
