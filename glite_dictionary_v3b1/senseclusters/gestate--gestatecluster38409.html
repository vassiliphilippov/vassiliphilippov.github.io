gestate

verb
be pregnant with; carry in the womb
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛˌsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛsteɪt / (BrE)
(of a female animal or woman) be pregnant with (a child or offspring); carry in the womb during the period from conception to birth.

Examples

To gestate a baby
Many animals gestate their young for a fixed period before giving birth.
She gestated twins, which made the pregnancy especially challenging.
The elephant gestates its calf for nearly two years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carry - to be pregnant with and transport in the womb
