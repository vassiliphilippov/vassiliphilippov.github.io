outsider art

noun
art by self-taught or untrained artists
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtsaɪdər ɑrt / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈsaɪdə ɑːt / (BrE)
art created by self-taught or untrained artists, often outside the established art scene, and typically unconcerned with mainstream artistic conventions

Examples

Outsider art style
Collectors are increasingly interested in outsider art for its raw originality and authenticity.
He discovered his passion for outsider art after stumbling upon a homemade sculpture in a rural village.
The museum features a new wing dedicated to outsider art from around the world.
