revivifier

noun
one who restores life
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɪvəˌfaɪər / (AmE), / rɪˈvɪvɪfaɪə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings something back to life, activity, or success

Examples

The revivifier of success
The new coach became the revivifier of the once-struggling football team.
Fresh investment acted as a revivifier for the declining neighborhood.
She was hailed as the revivifier of traditional art in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reviver - someone who brings something back to life or consciousness
