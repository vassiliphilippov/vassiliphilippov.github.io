hoom

noun
low humming sound
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / huÀêm / (AmE), / huÀêm / (BrE)
a low, continuous humming or droning sound, especially as heard in the background

Examples

A distant hoom
A faint hoom from the distant machinery filled the empty warehouse.
The hoom of the air conditioner was the only sound in the quiet office.
At night, a gentle hoom from the city traffic lulled him to sleep.
