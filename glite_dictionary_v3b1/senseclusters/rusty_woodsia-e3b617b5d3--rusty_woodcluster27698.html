rusty woodsia

noun
a rock-inhabiting temperate fern with rusty-brown stalks
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti ˈwʊdziə / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti ˈwʊdziə / (BrE)
A common fern of northern temperate regions, typically found on rocks, with rusty-brown stalks and lance-shaped, divided fronds.
