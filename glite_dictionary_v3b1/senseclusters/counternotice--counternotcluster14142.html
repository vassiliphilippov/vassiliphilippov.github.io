counternotice

verb
to dispute a notice
8.8
Pronunciation: /kaʊn.tər.ˈnoʊ.tɪs/ /; / /kaʊn.tər.ˈnoʊ.təs/ (AmE), /kaʊn.tə.ˈnəʊ.tɪs/ /; / /kaʊn.tə.ˈnəʊ.təs/ (BrE)
to submit a formal response to a legal or administrative notice, often to dispute a claim or request restoration of content.

Examples

To counternotice a claim
After receiving a copyright takedown, she decided to counternotice the platform to restore her video.
The company chose to counternotice the claim, arguing that their use was fair and lawful.
If you believe the notice is incorrect, you can counternotice to challenge the removal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comply - to act in accordance with a request or order
