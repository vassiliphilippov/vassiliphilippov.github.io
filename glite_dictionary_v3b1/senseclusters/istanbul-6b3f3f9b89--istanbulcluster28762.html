Synonyms

  • Constantinople
    8.3 2.21/m
    Constantinople is the historic medieval name for the city used for centuries, while Istanbul is the modern name of the same city.
  • Byzantium
    8.7 0.06/m
    Byzantium is an ancient Greek settlement name used for the old city, while Istanbul is the large modern metropolis that grew from it.
  • Stambul
    9.3 0.00/m
    Stambul is an older alternative name seen in some histories, while Istanbul is the current official name used in Turkey today.
  • Stamboul
    9.6 0.03/m
    Stamboul refers to the old central part of the city in older writings, while Istanbul is the modern whole city name used today.
