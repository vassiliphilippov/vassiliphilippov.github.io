sense of peace

noun
feeling of calm and tranquility
Pronunciation: / sɛns əv pis / (AmE), / sɛns əv piːs / (BrE)
a feeling of deep calmness and tranquility, often experienced in peaceful surroundings or states of mind

Examples

A sense of peace
After moving to the countryside, she found a sense of peace she had never known before.
Meditating every morning gives me a sense of peace that lasts throughout the day.
Walking along the empty beach at sunrise, he was filled with a sense of peace.

  • synonym: peace - a state of tranquility or quiet
