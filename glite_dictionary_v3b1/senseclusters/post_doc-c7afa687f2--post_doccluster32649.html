post doc

noun
grant for postdoctoral research
2.3
Pronunciation: / poʊst ˈdɑk / (AmE), / pəʊst ˈdɒk / (BrE)
a grant awarded to support postdoctoral research or study, typically after completion of a doctoral degree

Examples

A post doc grant
After completing her PhD, she applied for a post doc to fund her research in genetics.
Many scientists spend several years on a post doc before securing a permanent academic position.
Securing a post doc allowed him to continue his studies at an internationally renowned university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fellowship - a grant or stipend awarded to support academic study or research
