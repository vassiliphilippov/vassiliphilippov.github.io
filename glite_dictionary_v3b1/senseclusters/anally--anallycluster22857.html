anally

adverb
excessively orderly
Pronunciation: / ˈænəli / (AmE), / ˈænəli / (BrE)
in an excessively orderly, fussy, or obsessive manner, especially about small details (often disapproving or informal).

Examples

Anally organized desk
She organized her desk anally, making sure every pen was perfectly aligned.
He checked the report anally, correcting even the tiniest formatting errors.
Some people find it annoying when tasks are done anally instead of more flexibly.
