floral arrangement

noun
decorative display of flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrəl əˌreɪndʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈflɒrəl əˌreɪndʒmənt / (BrE)
a decorative display or composition made of cut flowers, often arranged artistically in a container

Examples

A floral arrangement display
A fresh floral arrangement can brighten up any room in the house.
She took a class to learn how to make a stunning floral arrangement for the wedding.
The centerpiece was a beautiful floral arrangement in shades of pink and white.
