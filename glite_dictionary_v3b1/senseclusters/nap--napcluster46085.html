nap

noun
card game similar to whist
Pronunciation: / næp / (AmE), / næp / (BrE)
A card game similar to whist, usually played for stakes, in which players bid on how many tricks they will take.

Examples

A game of nap
He's an expert at nap and often wins small stakes from the group.
Many people consider nap to be more exciting than traditional whist.
