Synonyms

  • wear out
    2.3 1/m
    wear out means becoming unusable after long or repeated use, while wear is the general process of becoming damaged over time.
  • wear away
    3.8 0.1/m
    wear away means material is slowly removed by rubbing or weather, while wear is the general damage or loss from use over time.
  • wear down
    4.1 1/m
    wear down means make something or someone weaker by steady pressure or use, while wear means objects become damaged through use.
