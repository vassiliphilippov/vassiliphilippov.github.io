miswire

noun
incorrect wiring
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌwaɪər / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌwaɪə / (BrE)
an instance of incorrect wiring or a wiring error in an electrical system

Examples

A miswire in equipment
A miswire in the control panel caused the machine to malfunction unexpectedly.
The inspector found a miswire that could have led to a serious electrical fire.
After fixing the miswire, the lights in the room worked perfectly again.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correct wiring - proper and safe arrangement of electrical wires
  • synonym: short circuit - an electrical circuit that allows current to travel along an unintended path
  • synonym: wiring error - a mistake made in connecting electrical wires
