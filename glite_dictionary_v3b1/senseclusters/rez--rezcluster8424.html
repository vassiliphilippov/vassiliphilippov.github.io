rez

noun
Native American reservation
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɛz / (AmE), / rɛz / (BrE)
an informal term for a Native American reservation, an area of land managed by a Native American tribe.

Examples

Life on the rez
She spent her childhood summers visiting family on the rez in New Mexico.
Many traditions are still practiced on the rez despite changes in modern life.
He returned to the rez to help with community projects after college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reservation - an area of land set aside for Native Americans
