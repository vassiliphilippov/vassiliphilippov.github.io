antisexist

noun
opponent of sexism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsɛksɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsɛksɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes sexism and supports equal rights and opportunities for all genders

Examples

An antisexist speaker
As an outspoken antisexist, he regularly speaks at events promoting gender equality.
The organization hired a well-known antisexist to lead their diversity training sessions.
Many young people today identify as antisexist and challenge discriminatory attitudes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminist - a person who supports equal rights for women
  • antonym: sexist - a person who discriminates based on gender
