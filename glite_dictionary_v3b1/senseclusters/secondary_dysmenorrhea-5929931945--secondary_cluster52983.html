secondary dysmenorrhea

noun
menstrual pain due to a specific medical disorder
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛk.ən.ˌdɛɹ.i dɪsˌmɛn.əˈɹi.ə / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.ənd.ˌdɛɹ.i dɪsˌmiː.nəˈriː.ə / (BrE)
menstrual pain caused by an underlying medical disorder, such as endometriosis

Examples

Secondary dysmenorrhea pain
A thorough examination is necessary to diagnose the cause of secondary dysmenorrhea.
Unlike primary dysmenorrhea, secondary dysmenorrhea usually develops in older women.
Women with secondary dysmenorrhea often require treatment for the underlying condition, such as endometriosis.
