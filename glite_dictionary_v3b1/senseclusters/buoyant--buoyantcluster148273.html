buoyant

adjective
able to float or rise in liquid or air
5.3 0.90/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…î…™…ônt, ÀàbuÀêj…ônt / (AmE), / Ààb…î…™…ônt, Ààb äj…ônt / (BrE)

Examples

Buoyant object in water
Cork is a naturally buoyant material commonly used in life vests.
Helium makes balloons buoyant enough to rise quickly into the sky.
The wooden raft was so buoyant that it easily supported their weight on the lake.
