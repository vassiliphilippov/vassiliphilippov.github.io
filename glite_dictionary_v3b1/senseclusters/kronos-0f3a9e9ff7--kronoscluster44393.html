Kronos

proper noun
mythic time entity
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊnoʊs /; / ˈkroʊnɒs / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊnɒs / (BrE)
a figure from Greek mythology, the leader of the Titans and father of Zeus, often associated with time; also used in modern fiction as a powerful being with control over time

Examples

The ancient Greeks believed that Kronos ruled during a golden age of peace.
