interventricular

adjective
between ventricles
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərvɛnˈtrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntəvɛnˈtrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring between the ventricles of the heart or brain

Examples

The interventricular septum
The interventricular septum separates the left and right ventricles of the heart.
Doctors examined the interventricular groove for signs of heart disease.
An interventricular hemorrhage can occur between the brain's ventricles in newborns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ventricular - relating to a ventricle of the heart or brain
