multicellularity

noun
state of many cells
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˌsɛljuˈlærɪti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˌsɛljʊˈlærɪti / (BrE)
the state or condition of being made up of more than one cell, especially in living organisms
