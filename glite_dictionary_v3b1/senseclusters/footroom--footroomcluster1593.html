footroom

noun
space for feet
2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfʊt.ruːm/ (AmE), /ˈfʊt.ruːm/ (BrE)
the amount of space available for your feet, especially when sitting in a car or under a table

Examples

Extra footroom in car
The new car model provides extra footroom for passengers in the back seat.
I like this desk because it has plenty of footroom underneath.
Tall people often complain about the lack of footroom on airplanes.
