spray

noun
device for dispersing liquid as fine mist
Pronunciation: / spreɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ / (BrE)
a device that disperses a liquid, such as water or perfume, as a fine mist or droplets

Examples

A perfume spray
I use a spray to water the delicate flowers in my garden.
She bought a new perfume in a convenient spray bottle.
The artist applied paint with a fine spray for a smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atomizer - a device for converting liquid into a fine spray
  • synonym: dispenser - container or apparatus for releasing a substance
