bani

noun
Sikh sacred hymns
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːni / (AmE), / ˈbɑːni / (BrE)
sacred hymns or religious verses in Sikhism, considered as scripture and recited in worship

Examples

Morning bani recitation
The morning prayers at the gurdwara began with the recitation of bani by the congregation.
Many Sikhs memorize important bani to recite during daily worship.
Listening to bani brings a sense of peace and devotion to followers.
