slide

noun
smooth movement along a surface
1.0 0.42/m

Examples

A muddy slide
After the rain, the path was so muddy that every step felt like a slide down the hill.

Synonyms

  • coast
    3.5 0.88/m

  • glide
    3.4 0.75/m
    glide is a smooth continuous motion often through air or with little contact, while slide keeps full contact with the surface.
