river dolphin

noun
freshwater dolphin of rivers
6.3 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪv.ər ˈdɑːl.fɪn/ (AmE), /ˈrɪv.ə ˈdɒl.fɪn/ (BrE)
A long-snouted dolphin that lives primarily in freshwater rivers of South America and southern Asia.

Examples

A river dolphin swimming

The Amazon river dolphin is famous for its pink color and playful nature.
