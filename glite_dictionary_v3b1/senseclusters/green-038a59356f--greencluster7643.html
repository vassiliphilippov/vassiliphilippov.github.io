Green

noun
member of the Green Party
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
a member or supporter of the Green Party, especially one active in environmental and ecological issues

Examples

A Green activist
As a Green, she campaigns for climate change initiatives in her community.
Many Greens advocate for stricter environmental regulations and sustainable development.
The Green spoke passionately about renewable energy policies at the town hall meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - a member or supporter of a Conservative Party
  • synonym: environmentalist - a person concerned with or advocating protection of the environment
