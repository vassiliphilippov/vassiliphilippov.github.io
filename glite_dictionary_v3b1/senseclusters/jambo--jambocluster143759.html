jambo

x
Swahili greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒæmbəʊ / (BrE)
a Swahili greeting meaning hello, used especially by English speakers in East Africa

Examples

Say jambo
The guide welcomed the group with a friendly jambo as they arrived at the hotel.
Children on the street waved and shouted jambo to the tourists passing by.
When visiting Kenya, it is common to greet people with jambo.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodbye - a common phrase used when parting from someone
  • synonym: hello - a common greeting used when meeting someone
