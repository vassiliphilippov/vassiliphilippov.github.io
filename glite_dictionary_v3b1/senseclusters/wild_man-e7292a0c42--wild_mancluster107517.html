wild man

noun
uncivilized or unsocialized man
1.8
Pronunciation: / waɪld mæn / (AmE), / waɪld man / (BrE)
A man who is not socialized and behaves in an uncivilized or untamed way.

Examples

A wild man lives alone
After years alone on the island, he was found living like a true wild man.
His unkempt appearance and strange habits led some to call him a wild man.
Rumors spread throughout the village about a mysterious wild man living in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbarian - a person perceived as uncivilized or primitive
  • degree lower: hermit - a person living in solitude, often for religious reasons
  • synonym: savage - a person regarded as primitive or uncivilized
