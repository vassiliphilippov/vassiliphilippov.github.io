torc

noun
ancient metal neck ring
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɔrk / (AmE), / tɔːk / (BrE)
a large, rigid metal ring or collar, often open-ended, worn around the neck (sometimes arm or wrist) as jewelry or a symbol of status in ancient times
