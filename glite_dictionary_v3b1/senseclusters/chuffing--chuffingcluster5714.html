chuffing

noun
puffing sound
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
a short, sharp puffing sound, especially one made by a steam engine or similar machinery

Examples

Chuffing of a train
The chuffing of the old steam train echoed through the valley as it climbed the hill.
We could hear the steady chuffing of the engine as the boat moved down the river.
Late at night, the chuffing from the factory kept some of the neighbors awake.
