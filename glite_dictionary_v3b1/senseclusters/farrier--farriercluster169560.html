farrier

noun
person who shoes horses
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfæriər / (AmE), / ˈfæriə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make and fit horseshoes

Examples

A farrier fits horseshoes
A skilled farrier is essential for maintaining the health of horses' hooves.
After training as a farrier, she opened her own business serving local farms.
The farrier visited the stable to replace several worn horseshoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmith - person who forges and shapes iron, often making horseshoes
