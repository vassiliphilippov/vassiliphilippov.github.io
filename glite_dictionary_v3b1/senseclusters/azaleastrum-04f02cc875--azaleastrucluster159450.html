Azaleastrum

noun
former genus of shrubs; now in Rhododendron
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˌzeɪliˈæstrəm / (AmE), / əˌzeɪliˈæstrəm / (BrE)
a former genus of evergreen or deciduous shrubs now classified under the genus Rhododendron

Examples
