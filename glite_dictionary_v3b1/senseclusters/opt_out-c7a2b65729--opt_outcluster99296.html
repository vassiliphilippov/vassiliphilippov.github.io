opt out

verb
choose not to take part
6.3 3.0
Pronunciation: /ɑːpt aʊt/ (AmE), /ɒpt aʊt/ (BrE)
to choose not to participate in something; to decide not to be involved in an activity, arrangement, or system

Examples

To opt out of plans
Employees can opt out of the company's health insurance plan if they have other coverage.
He decided to opt out of the survey because he valued his privacy.
Some parents chose to opt out of the school trip for personal reasons.
