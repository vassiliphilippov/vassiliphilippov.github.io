dab

noun
a small amount of something moist or liquid
2.7
Pronunciation: / dæb / (AmE), / dæb / (BrE)
a small amount of something moist or liquid, usually applied lightly

Examples

A dab of butter
He added a dab of butter to the pan before cooking the eggs.
She put a dab of cream on her finger and gently applied it to the cut.
There was just a dab of sauce on the edge of his plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smear - a small amount of a substance that has been spread on a surface
  • synonym: splash - a small quantity of liquid thrown or falling onto a surface
