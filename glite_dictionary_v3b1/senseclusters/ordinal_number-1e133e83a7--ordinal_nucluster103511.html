ordinal number

noun
number indicating position in a sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɪnəl ˈnʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪnəl ˈnʌmbə / (BrE)
a number that indicates position or order in a sequence, such as first, second, or third
