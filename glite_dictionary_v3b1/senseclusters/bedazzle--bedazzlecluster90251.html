bedazzle

verb
temporarily blind or confuse by intense light
6.3
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdæzəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdæzəl / (BrE)
to temporarily blind or confuse someone, especially by a strong or dazzling light

Examples

To bedazzle with headlights
Driving into the setting sun can bedazzle even careful drivers.
The flash from the camera briefly bedazzled her, leaving her unable to see.
The stage lights were so bright they bedazzled the audience for a moment.
