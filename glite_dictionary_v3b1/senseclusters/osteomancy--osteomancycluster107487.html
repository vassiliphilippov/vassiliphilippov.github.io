osteomancy

noun
divination with bones
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstiəˌmænsi / (AmE), / ˈɒstiəˌmænsi / (BrE)
the practice of divination or fortune-telling by interpreting the shapes or patterns of bones

Examples

Osteomancy by bones
In some ancient societies, priests performed osteomancy to guide important decisions.
The ritual of osteomancy involved carefully examining the cracks in animal bones.
Historians have found evidence of osteomancy in archaeological sites across Asia.
