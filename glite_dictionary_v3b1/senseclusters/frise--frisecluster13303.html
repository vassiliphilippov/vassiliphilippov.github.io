frise

noun
coarse woollen cloth
Pronunciation: / friːz / (AmE), / friːz / (BrE)
a coarse woollen cloth with a raised nap, used historically for warm clothing and furnishings

Examples

A frise blanket
The old trunk contained a blanket made of thick frise to keep travelers warm.
In the past, many winter coats were fashioned from durable frise fabric.
The museum displayed a sample of frise among other historical textiles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frieze - a heavy, coarse woollen cloth with a nap, used for coats
  • synonym: tweed - a rough woollen fabric, often used for jackets and suits
