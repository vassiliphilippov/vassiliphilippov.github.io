inquiringly

adverb
in a curious or questioning manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwaɪɚɪŋli /; / ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwaɪərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner showing curiosity, interest, or a desire to know more.

Examples

Look inquiringly
He raised his eyebrows inquiringly, waiting for her response.
She glanced inquiringly at her boss, hoping for permission to speak.
The child looked inquiringly at the strange machine, eager to know what it did.
He raised his hand enquiringly, unsure if he was allowed to speak.
She glanced enquiringly at her professor, hoping for a hint about the assignment.
The child looked enquiringly at his mother when he heard the unfamiliar word.
