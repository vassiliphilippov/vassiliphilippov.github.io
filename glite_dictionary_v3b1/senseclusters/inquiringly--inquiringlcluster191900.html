Synonyms

  • inquisitively
    4.5 0.1/m
    inquisitively shows eager, probing interest like someone leaning in to learn more, while inquiringly is a polite questioning tone.
  • curiously
    2.6 3/m
    curiously expresses general interest or surprise when noticing something, while inquiringly signals a direct desire to ask or know more.
  • questioningly
    3.3 0.5/m
    questioningly is used when someone shows doubt or asks with a raised eyebrow or look, while inquiringly is simply curious and polite.
