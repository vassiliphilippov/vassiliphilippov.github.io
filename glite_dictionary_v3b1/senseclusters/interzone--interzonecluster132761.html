interzone

noun
area between zones
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.tər.zoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.tə.zəʊn/ (BrE)
a region or area located between two distinct zones, often serving as a transitional space

Examples

Interzone between regions
The rare plant thrives only in the interzone where the desert meets the forest.
Scientists studied the unique animal species living in the interzone between the river and the grassland.
Urban development is expanding into the interzone separating the city from the surrounding countryside.
