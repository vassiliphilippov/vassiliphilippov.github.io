nontelescoping

adjective
not telescopic; not able to extend or collapse
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌtɛləˈskoʊpɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˌtɛlɪˈskəʊpɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or part) not able to be extended or collapsed like a telescope; not telescopic.

Examples

A nontelescoping mast
His design featured a nontelescoping mast for increased stability.
The new safety regulations require nontelescoping barriers in certain construction zones.
Unlike the previous model, the nontelescoping handle cannot be adjusted for length.
