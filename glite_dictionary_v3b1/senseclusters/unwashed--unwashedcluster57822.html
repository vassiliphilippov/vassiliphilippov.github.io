unwashed

adjective
not cleaned; not washed
2.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɑʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒʃt / (BrE)
not cleaned with or as if with soap and water; not having been washed

Examples

Unwashed hands
His hands were still unwashed after gardening all day.
The kitchen sink was overflowing with unwashed dishes.
There was a pile of unwashed laundry in the corner of his room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: washed - cleaned with water or soap and water
