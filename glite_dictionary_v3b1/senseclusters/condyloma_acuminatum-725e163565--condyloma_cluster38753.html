condyloma acuminatum

noun
genital or anal wart (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dɪˈloʊ.mə ˌæk.jə.mɪˈneɪ.təm / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dɪˈləʊ.mə ˌæk.jʊ.mɪˈneɪ.təm / (BrE)
A small, benign wart that appears on or around the genitals and anus, typically caused by human papillomavirus (HPV) infection.

Examples

A condyloma acuminatum wart
Individuals with condyloma acuminatum are advised to discuss HPV vaccination with their healthcare providers.
The doctor identified a condyloma acuminatum during the patient's routine examination.
Treatment for condyloma acuminatum may involve topical medications or minor surgical procedures.
