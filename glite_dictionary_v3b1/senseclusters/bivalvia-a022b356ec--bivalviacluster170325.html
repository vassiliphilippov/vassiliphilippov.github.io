Bivalvia

noun
class of mollusks with two-part shells
Pronunciation: / baɪˈvæl.vi.ə / (AmE), / baɪˈval.vɪ.ə /; / baɪˈval.vi.ə / (BrE)
a class of aquatic mollusks (such as oysters, clams, scallops, and mussels) with shells made of two hinged parts

Oysters and mussels belong to the class Bivalvia.

Related Terms
