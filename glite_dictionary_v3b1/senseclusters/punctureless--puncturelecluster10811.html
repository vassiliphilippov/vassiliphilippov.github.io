punctureless

adjective
without punctures; impervious to being punctured
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋktʃər.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋktʃə.ləs / (BrE)
not having any punctures or incapable of being punctured.

Examples

A punctureless tire
Engineers are searching for materials that are completely punctureless for use in medical devices.
She preferred punctureless packaging to reduce the risk of leaks during shipping.
The company developed a punctureless bicycle tire for safer urban commuting.
