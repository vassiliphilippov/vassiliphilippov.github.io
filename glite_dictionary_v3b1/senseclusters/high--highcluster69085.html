high

noun
highest forward gear in a vehicle
6.7 0.94/m
Pronunciation: / haÉª / (AmE), / haÉª / (BrE)
the forward gear in a vehicle's transmission that produces the greatest speed for a given engine speed; the highest gear

Examples
