high

noun
highest forward gear in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
the forward gear in a vehicle's transmission that produces the greatest speed for a given engine speed; the highest gear

Examples

Drive in high gear
Driving up the mountain in high can strain the engine.
He shifted into high to increase his speed.
Make sure the car is in high before you get on the highway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: low - the lowest forward gear in a vehicle's transmission
