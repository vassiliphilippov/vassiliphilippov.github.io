tearlessly

adverb
without crying
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪrləsli / (AmE), / ˈtɪələsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not involve crying or shedding tears

Examples

Tearlessly listen to news
He listened tearlessly to the sad story, showing no outward emotion.
Despite the heartbreaking news, she managed to respond tearlessly and comfort her friends.
The child faced the disappointment tearlessly, surprising everyone with his composure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful or composed manner
  • antonym: tearfully - in a way that involves crying or shedding tears
