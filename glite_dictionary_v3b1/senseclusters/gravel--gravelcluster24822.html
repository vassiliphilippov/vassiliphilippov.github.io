gravel

verb
cover with gravel
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrævəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrævəl / (BrE)
to cover (a surface) with a layer of gravel

Examples

To gravel a path
The city gravels the roads every spring to improve traction.
They spent all day graveling the new parking lot before painting the lines.
This summer, we gravelled the path around the garden to keep weeds down.
