Boeing B-52 Stratofortress

proper noun
U.S. long-range strategic bomber aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊɪŋ ˈbi ˈfɪfti tu ˌstrætəˈfɔrtrəs / (AmE), / ˈbəʊɪŋ ˈbiː ˈfɪfti tuː ˌstrætəˈfɔːtrəs / (BrE)
A long-range, subsonic, jet-powered strategic bomber used by the United States Air Force, first introduced in the 1950s.
