galoot

noun
clumsy or disreputable man
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlut / (AmE), / ɡəˈluːt / (BrE)
a man who is awkward, clumsy, or of questionable character

Examples

That galoot man
Don't let that galoot mess up the decorations again.
Everyone laughed as the big galoot tripped over his own feet.
My brother can be such a galoot when he's tired.
