half-heartedly

adverb
in an unenthusiastic or indifferent way
7.4 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌhæfˈhɑːrtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌhɑːfˈhɑːtɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows little enthusiasm, energy, or commitment.

Examples

To half-heartedly agree
He nodded half-heartedly, showing no real interest in the discussion.
She agreed to help, but only half-heartedly completed the work.
They cheered for their team half-heartedly, disappointed by the score.
