germ theory

noun
theory that diseases are caused by microorganisms
4.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /dʒɜːrm ˈθiː.ə.riː/ /; / /dʒɜːrm ˈθɪə.riː/ (AmE), /dʒɜːm ˈθɪə.riː/ /; / /dʒɜːm ˈθiː.ə.riː/ (BrE)
the scientific theory that infectious diseases are caused by microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, or fungi

Examples
