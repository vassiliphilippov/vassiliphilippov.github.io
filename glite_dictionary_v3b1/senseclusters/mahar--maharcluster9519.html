mahar

noun
Islamic marriage payment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːhɑr / (AmE), / ˈmɑːhɑː / (BrE)
a payment or gift given by a groom to his bride as part of an Islamic marriage contract

Examples

Wedding mahar payment
The amount of mahar was discussed and agreed upon by both families before the wedding.
She received her mahar as part of the marriage ceremony, as required by tradition.
In some cultures, the mahar can be money, jewelry, or another valuable gift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bride price - money or property given to the bride's family by the groom
  • synonym: dowry - property or money brought by a bride to her husband on marriage
