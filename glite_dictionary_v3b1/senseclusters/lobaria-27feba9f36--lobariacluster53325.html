Lobaria

proper noun
lichen genus
Pronunciation: / loʊˈbɛəriə / (AmE), / ləʊˈbɛəriə / (BrE)
a genus of leafy lichens commonly found in forests, often used as indicators of environmental health

Examples

Lobaria lichen genus
Lobaria grows on the trunks of old trees in temperate rainforests.
