tribeswoman

noun
a woman belonging to a tribe
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪbzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who is a member of a tribe, especially an indigenous or traditional community

Examples

A tribeswoman in village
A young tribeswoman welcomed visitors and introduced them to her community's traditions.
As the only tribeswoman on the council, she spoke passionately about preserving their culture.
The tribeswoman skillfully crafted baskets using local materials found in the forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tribesman - a man who is a member of a tribe
