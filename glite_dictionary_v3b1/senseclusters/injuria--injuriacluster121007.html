injuria

noun
insult or offense
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒʊəriə / (BrE)
an insult or offensive act that harms a person's reputation or dignity

Examples

A personal injuria
The lawyer argued that the statement was a clear injuria against his client.
He could not forgive the injuria he suffered during the heated debate.
In ancient law, a public injuria could lead to serious consequences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • synonym: insult - a remark or action that is rude or offensive
  • synonym: offense - an act that causes someone to feel hurt or upset
