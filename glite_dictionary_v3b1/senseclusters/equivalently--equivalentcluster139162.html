equivalently

adverb
in an equal way
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪvələntli / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪvələntli / (BrE)
in a way that has the same meaning, value, or effect as something else

Examples

Used equivalently in math
A kilometer is equivalently 1,000 meters.
The two statements can be expressed equivalently using different words.
You can pay with cash or, equivalently, by credit card.

Related Terms

  • antonym: differently - in a different way
  • synonym: similarly - in a similar way
