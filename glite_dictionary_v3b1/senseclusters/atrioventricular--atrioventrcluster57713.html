atrioventricular

adjective
relating to atria and ventricles of the heart
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ.tri.oʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ.tri.əʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting both the atria and ventricles of the heart.

Examples

Atrioventricular valve
An atrioventricular valve prevents blood from flowing backward between the heart chambers.
Researchers are studying atrioventricular conduction in patients with irregular heartbeats.
The doctor explained that an atrioventricular block can disrupt the electrical signals in the heart.
