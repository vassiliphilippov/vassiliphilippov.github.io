battue

noun
indiscriminate mass slaughter
Pronunciation: / bæˈtu / (AmE), / baˈtjuː / (BrE)
an act of indiscriminate slaughter or mass killing, especially a brutal or systematic one

Examples

A brutal battue
International leaders condemned the battue as a crime against humanity.
The documentary examined the causes and consequences of the infamous battue in the capital.
The rebels' surprise attack resulted in a battue that shocked the entire region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloodbath - an event or situation in which many people are killed in a violent way
  • synonym: massacre - the killing of a large number of people, especially those who are not involved in fighting
  • synonym: slaughter - the killing of many people cruelly and unfairly, especially in a war
