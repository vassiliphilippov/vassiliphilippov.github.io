revealed religion

noun
religion based on divine revelation
6.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvild rɪˈlɪdʒən / (AmE), / rɪˈviːld rɪˈlɪdʒən / (BrE)
A religion based mainly on divine revelations communicated by God to humanity, rather than on human reason alone.

Examples

Revealed religion by God
Christianity and Islam are often classified as revealed religion because they are based on scriptures believed to come from God.
Many people find comfort in following a revealed religion that offers divine guidance.
The distinction between revealed religion and natural religion is important in theology.

Related Terms

  • antonym: natural religion - a religion founded on reason and ordinary experience of nature, not on revealed truths
