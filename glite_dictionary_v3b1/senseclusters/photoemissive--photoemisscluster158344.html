photoemissive

adjective
emitting electrons when exposed to light
9.6 8.1
Pronunciation: /foʊ.toʊ.ɪ.ˈmɪ.sɪv/ /; / /foʊ.tə.ɪ.ˈmɪ.sɪv/ (AmE), /fəʊ.təʊ.ɪ.ˈmɪ.sɪv/ (BrE)
Relating to or involving the emission of electrons from a material when it is exposed to light.

Examples

Photoemissive surface
A photoemissive surface releases electrons when exposed to ultraviolet light.
Researchers designed a new photoemissive coating to improve solar cell performance.
The photoemissive properties of the metal make it suitable for use in light sensors.
