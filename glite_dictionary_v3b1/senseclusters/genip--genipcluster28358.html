genip

noun
small Caribbean fruit with sweet pulp
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnɪp / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnɪp / (BrE)
A small, round Caribbean fruit with green leathery skin and sweet, juicy, translucent pulp, commonly eaten fresh like grapes.

Examples

A ripe genip fruit
After peeling the skin, she popped the juicy genip into her mouth.
The sweet flesh of the genip makes it a popular summertime treat in the Caribbean.
We bought a bag of fresh genip from a street vendor in Jamaica.
