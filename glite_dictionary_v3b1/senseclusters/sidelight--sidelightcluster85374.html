sidelight

noun
window beside door
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdlaɪt / (BrE)
a narrow window or pane of glass set at the side of a door or another window, often to let in extra light

Examples

Door sidelight
The new front door has a sidelight that brightens the entryway.
She admired the stained glass sidelight next to the old wooden door.
Cleaning the sidelight made the hallway much sunnier in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transom - a window above a door or another window
  • synonym: window - an opening in a wall fitted with glass to let in light or air
