bask on the beach

verb
relax in the sun on a beach
1.1
Pronunciation: / bæsk ɑn ðə bitʃ / (AmE), / bɑːsk ɒn ðə biːtʃ / (BrE)

Examples

To bask on the beach

We spent the afternoon basking on the beach with cold drinks and good books.
