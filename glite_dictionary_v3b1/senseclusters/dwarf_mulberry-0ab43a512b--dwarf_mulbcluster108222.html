dwarf mulberry

noun
creeping plant with yellow or orange berries
2.9
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf ˈmʌlˌbɛri / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈmʌlbəri / (BrE)
a creeping plant of north temperate regions with yellow or orange raspberry-like berries, also known as creeping raspberry

Examples

The dwarf mulberry plant
Gardeners often plant dwarf mulberry as a ground cover in shaded areas.
The dwarf mulberry thrives in cool climates and produces sweet, orange berries.
The fruit of the dwarf mulberry attracts birds and wildlife to the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creeping raspberry - a low-growing raspberry plant with yellow or orange fruit
