burble

verb
flow with a bubbling sound
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝː.bəl / (AmE), / ˈbɜː.bəl / (BrE)
(of a liquid) to flow with a low, continuous, bubbling sound; to make such a noise while moving.

Examples

A brook burbled softly
A brook burbled cheerfully near the edge of the meadow.
She listened to the fountain burbling softly in the courtyard.
The little stream began to burble as it flowed over the smooth stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to make a continuous, low, murmuring sound, like water flowing gently
  • synonym: gurgle - to make a hollow bubbling sound like that made by water running out of a bottle
