bolter

noun
fast eater
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊltər / (AmE), / ˈbəʊltə / (BrE)
a person who eats their food very quickly, often finishing before others

Examples

Fast-eating bolter
At family dinners he was a bolter, finishing his plate before anyone else had started.
She became known as a bolter because she always finished her lunch in just a few minutes.
If you eat like a bolter, you might not enjoy your food as much.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gobbler - a person who eats food quickly and greedily
  • antonym: slow eater - a person who eats food slowly
