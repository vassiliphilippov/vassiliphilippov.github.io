set

verb
(of plant) begin to produce fruit
9.0
Pronunciation: / sɛt / (AmE), / sɛt / (BrE)
(of a plant) to begin producing fruit or seeds after flowering.

Examples

Plants set fruit
If the weather stays warm, most of the tomato plants will set fruit soon.
Orchard owners hope that the blossoms will set well after pollination.
The lemon tree in our garden has finally set this year.
