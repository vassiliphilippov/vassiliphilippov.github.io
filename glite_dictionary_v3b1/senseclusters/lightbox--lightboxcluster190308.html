lightbox

noun
illuminated display box
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.bɑːks/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.bɒks/ (BrE)
a box with a translucent surface and internal lighting, used for displaying, viewing, tracing, or photographing images or objects

Examples

Artist's lightbox
The artist used a lightbox to showcase her latest photographs in the gallery window.
A brightly lit lightbox above the entrance made the restaurant easy to find at night.
They designed a custom lightbox to display the company's new logo at trade shows.
The artist used a lightbox to trace the details of her new illustration.
Photographers often review negatives on a lightbox to check image quality.
He bought a portable lightbox for his graphic design projects.
The photographer placed the jewelry in a lightbox to capture clear, shadow-free images.
Using a lightbox helps online sellers display their products with professional-looking photos.
She built a homemade lightbox from cardboard and white paper for her art project.

  • synonym: softbox - a device that diffuses light for photography
  • degree higher: studio - a room where photographs are taken
  • synonym: tracing table - a flat surface with light underneath for tracing images
