tokenize

verb
to split text
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊkəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtəʊkənaɪz / (BrE)
to break text into individual words, symbols, or tokens for processing, especially in computing or linguistics

Examples

To tokenize a sentence
Many natural language processing tools can tokenize large amounts of text quickly and accurately.
Before analyzing the data, the program will tokenize each sentence into words and punctuation marks.
You need to tokenize the input so that each word can be processed separately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combine - to join or merge things together
  • synonym: segment - to divide something into separate parts
