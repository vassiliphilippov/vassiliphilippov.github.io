marcato

noun
musical emphasis mark
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈkɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / mɑːˈkɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
a musical notation mark indicating that a note or passage should be played with strong emphasis

Examples

Marcato symbol
The composer used a marcato to make the melody stand out in the orchestra.
When you see a marcato above a note, play it with extra force.
The violinist emphasized the passage marked with marcato during the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accent - a musical symbol indicating emphasis on a note
  • antonym: staccato - a musical style where notes are played sharply separated from each other
