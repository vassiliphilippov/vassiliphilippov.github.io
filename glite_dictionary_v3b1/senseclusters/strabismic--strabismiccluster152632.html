strabismic

adjective
having misaligned eyes
Pronunciation: / strəˈbɪzmɪk / (AmE), / strəˈbɪzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by strabismus, a condition in which the eyes are not properly aligned

Examples

Strabismic eye
The specialist recommended surgery for the child's strabismic eye.
A strabismic patient may have difficulty focusing on objects.
Early treatment can help correct a strabismic condition in children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-eyed - having eyes that turn inwards or are misaligned
  • synonym: strabismus - a condition in which the eyes do not properly align with each other
