elastic

noun
stretchable fabric containing elastic
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪk / (BrE)
A fabric made of threads containing elastic material, used especially for making items that need to stretch.

Examples

A piece of elastic
He sewed a piece of elastic into the waistband of his trousers.
The mask stays on your face with a strip of elastic behind your head.
You can buy elastic at any fabric or craft store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic band - a loop of elastic fabric or rubber
