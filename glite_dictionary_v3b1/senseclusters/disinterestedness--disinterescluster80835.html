disinterestedness

noun
impartiality; absence of bias
4.8
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɪn.t̬ɚˌrɛs.tɪd.nəs / (AmE), / dɪsˈɪn.trɪ.stɪd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being impartial and free from bias or selfish motives

Examples

Academic disinterestedness
Academic research requires disinterestedness to maintain credibility and trust.
Her disinterestedness in the dispute allowed her to mediate effectively.
The judge's disinterestedness was evident in her fair ruling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bias - prejudice in favor of or against
  • synonym: impartiality - the quality of not being biased or partial
