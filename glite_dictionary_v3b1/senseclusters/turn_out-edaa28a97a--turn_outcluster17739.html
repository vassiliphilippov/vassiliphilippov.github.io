turn out

verb
rotate outward; move outwardly
4.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /tɜːrn aʊt/ (AmE), /tɜːn aʊt/ (BrE)
to rotate or move something so that it points outward or away from the center or body

Examples

Dancers turn out feet

He tried to turn out his feet, but found the motion difficult.
Many birds are able to turn out their toes to maintain balance on narrow branches.
