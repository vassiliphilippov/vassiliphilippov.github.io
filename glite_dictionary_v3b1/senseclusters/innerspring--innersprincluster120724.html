innerspring

noun
spring-core mattress
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnərˌsprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɪnəˌsprɪŋ / (BrE)
a mattress that has a core made of metal springs to provide support and comfort

Examples

An innerspring mattress
After years of use, the old innerspring started to sag in the middle.
Many people prefer an innerspring for its firm support and durability.
The hotel upgraded all its rooms with new innerspring mattresses for better guest comfort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foam mattress - a mattress made from layers of foam without springs
  • synonym: spring mattress - a mattress containing metal springs for support
