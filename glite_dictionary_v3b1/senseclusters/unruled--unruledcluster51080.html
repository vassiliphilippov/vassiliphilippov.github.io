unruled

adjective
without printed lines
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈruːld / (AmE), / ʌnˈruːld / (BrE)
not having printed or drawn lines, especially referring to paper or sheets.

Examples

Unruled notebook pages
She bought a notebook filled with unruled pages for her sketches.
Many artists prefer unruled paper because it gives them more creative freedom.
The teacher asked the students to use unruled sheets for their drawings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blank - having no writing, marks, or lines
  • antonym: ruled - having printed or drawn lines, especially on paper
