loyal ally

noun
trusted and consistent supporter
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔɪəl ˈælaɪ / (AmE), / ˈlɔɪəl ˈælaɪ / (BrE)
A person or group who supports another consistently and can be trusted to remain faithful.

Examples

A loyal ally
During negotiations, she emerged as a loyal ally when support was needed most.
Every leader values a loyal ally who stands by them in difficult situations.
His actions demonstrated that he was a loyal ally, always putting the team's interests first.
