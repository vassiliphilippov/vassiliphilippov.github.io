travel

noun
distance covered in movement
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl / (BrE)
The movement or distance covered by something when it moves from one place to another, especially as part of a mechanism or system.

Examples

Travel of the slider

The piston has a maximum travel of 10 centimeters within the cylinder.
