comfort

noun
physical or emotional ease or satisfaction
6.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.fərt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.fət/ (BrE)
a state of physical ease and relaxation, free from pain or discomfort, or something that provides this feeling

Examples

Physical comfort
A warm blanket can provide comfort on a chilly night.

Proper shoes are essential for your walking comfort on long hikes.
She found great comfort in her warm, cozy bed after a long day.
