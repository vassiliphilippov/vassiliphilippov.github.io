afternoon delight

noun
pleasant treat or activity in the afternoon
Pronunciation: / ˌæftɚˈnun dɪˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ˌɑːftəˈnuːn dɪˈlaɪt / (BrE)
a pleasant treat, meal, or leisure activity enjoyed in the afternoon, often as a special indulgence

Examples

A sweet afternoon delight
A long walk by the river was their favorite afternoon delight.
She considered a nap in the hammock the perfect afternoon delight.
We met at the café for an afternoon delight of scones and tea.
