non-Western

adjective
outside Western world
4.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈwɛstərn / (AmE), / nɒnˈwɛstən / (BrE)
relating to cultures, countries, or traditions outside the Western world, especially those of Europe and North America

Examples

Non-Western cultures
The museum features a large collection of non-Western artifacts from Asia and Africa.
Many non-Western philosophies offer different perspectives on life and society.
She is interested in studying non-Western music traditions for her research project.
