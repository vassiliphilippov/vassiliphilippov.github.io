unpredictive

adjective
lacking predictive value
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prɪˈdɪk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prɪˈdɪk.tɪv / (BrE)
Not having predictive value; unable to indicate or predict future outcomes.

Examples

An unpredictive algorithm
An unpredictive algorithm fails to provide useful forecasts for decision-makers.
The economic model turned out to be largely unpredictive during volatile market conditions.
These test results are considered unpredictive of actual patient outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictive - having the quality of predicting future events or outcomes
