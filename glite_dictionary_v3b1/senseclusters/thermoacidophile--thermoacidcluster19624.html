thermoacidophile

noun
microorganism thriving in acid and heat
4.5 9.9
Pronunciation: /θɜːr.moʊ.ə.ˈsɪd.oʊ.faɪl/ (AmE), /θɜː.məʊ.ə.ˈsɪd.ə.faɪl/ (BrE)
