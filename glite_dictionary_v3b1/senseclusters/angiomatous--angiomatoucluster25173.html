angiomatous

adjective
relating to an angioma
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiˈɑmətəs / (AmE), / ˌandʒɪˈəʊmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an angioma; characterized by the presence of blood vessel tumors.

Examples

Angiomatous growth
A rare angiomatous growth was detected during the scan.
Histological examination revealed angiomatous changes in the tissue.
The doctor noted several angiomatous lesions in the patient's liver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angioma - a benign tumor consisting of blood or lymph vessels
