pensioner

noun
person receiving a pension
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnʃənər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnʃənə / (BrE)
a person who receives a pension, especially because of retirement from work

Examples

A retired pensioner
After working for forty years at the factory, he became a pensioner and enjoyed his retirement.
Many pensioners rely on government benefits to cover their living expenses.
The town organized a weekly tea for elderly pensioners in the community hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retiree - a person who has retired from active working life
