chillum

noun
conical smoking pipe
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪləm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪləm / (BrE)
a straight, conical pipe traditionally used for smoking, especially in South Asia

Examples

A chillum pipe
The traveler admired the craftsmanship of the chillum he found in the market.
Sadhus in India are often seen smoking from a chillum during religious ceremonies.
He learned how to use a chillum while visiting friends in South Asia.

  • synonym: pipe - a tube used to smoke tobacco or other substances
