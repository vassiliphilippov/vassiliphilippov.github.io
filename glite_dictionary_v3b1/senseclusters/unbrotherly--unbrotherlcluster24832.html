unbrotherly

adjective, adverb
lacking brotherly kindness
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbrʌðərli / (AmE), / ʌnˈbrʌðəli / (BrE)
not showing kindness, friendliness, or affection as a brother would; in a way that lacks the kindness or friendliness expected between brothers or close friends

Examples

Unbrotherly attitude
His unbrotherly attitude made it difficult for his siblings to trust him.
It was unbrotherly of him to ignore his sister when she needed help.
The argument ended with some unbrotherly words exchanged between the two brothers.
He spoke unbrotherly to his friend, showing no warmth or support.
After the disagreement, she acted unbrotherly and ignored her sister for days.
It was unbrotherly of him to refuse help when his brother needed it most.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brotherly - showing affection or kindness like a brother
  • synonym: coldly - in an unfriendly or unemotional way
  • synonym: unkind - not friendly or considerate
