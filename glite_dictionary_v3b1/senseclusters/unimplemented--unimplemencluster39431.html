unimplemented

adjective
not put into effect
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɪmpləˌmɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪmplɪˌmɛntɪd / (BrE)
not yet put into effect, carried out, or made operational, especially in software or plans

Examples

An unimplemented feature
The new security protocol remains unimplemented due to technical challenges.
Several features listed in the documentation are still unimplemented in the software.
Because the policy is unimplemented, employees continue to follow the old procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: implemented - put into effect or made operational
  • synonym: pending - awaiting action or completion
