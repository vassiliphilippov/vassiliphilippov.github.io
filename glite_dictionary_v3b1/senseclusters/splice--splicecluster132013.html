splice

verb
join genes or DNA together
Pronunciation: / splaɪs / (AmE), / splaɪs / (BrE)
to join together segments of DNA or genes to form new combinations, especially in genetic engineering

Examples

To splice DNA
Researchers are splicing DNA fragments to study genetic diseases.
Scientists can splice different genes to create genetically modified crops.
The lab successfully spliced a gene from one bacterium into another species.
