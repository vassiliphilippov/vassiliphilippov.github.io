unfundable

adjective
unable to get funding
7.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfʌndəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌndəbəl / (BrE)
not able to receive or attract financial support or funding

Examples

Unfundable project
The committee rejected the proposal as unfundable because it lacked a clear business plan.
Many innovative ideas remain unfundable due to limited resources and high competition.
If a project is deemed unfundable, it may never move beyond the planning stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fundable - able to receive or attract financial support
  • synonym: unviable - not capable of working or succeeding
