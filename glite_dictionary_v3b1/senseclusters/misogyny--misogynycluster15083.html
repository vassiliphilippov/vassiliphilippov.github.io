misogyny

noun
hatred or prejudice against women
Pronunciation: / mɪˈsɑdʒəni / (AmE), / mɪˈsɒdʒəni / (BrE)
dislike of, contempt for, or ingrained prejudice against women.

Examples

Deep misogyny
His comments were criticized as an example of deep-seated misogyny.
Many activists are working to combat misogyny in society.
The documentary explores the effects of misogyny in modern media.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misandry - dislike of or prejudice against men
