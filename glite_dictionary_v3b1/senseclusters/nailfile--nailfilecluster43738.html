nailfile

noun
small tool for shaping nails
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪl.faɪl / (AmE), / ˈneɪl.faɪl / (BrE)
a small, flat tool with a rough surface used for shaping and smoothing the edges of fingernails or toenails

Examples

A nailfile tool
After trimming his nails, he used a nailfile to smooth the rough edges.
She always keeps a nailfile in her purse for quick touch-ups.
The salon offered him a new nailfile for each visit.
