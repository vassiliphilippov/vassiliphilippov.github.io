dangler

noun
unclear modifier
Pronunciation: / ˈdæŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈdæŋɡlə / (BrE)
an informal term for a word or phrase, especially a modifier, that is not clearly attached to what it is supposed to modify in a sentence

Examples

A grammar dangler
The teacher pointed out a dangler in my essay that made the meaning unclear.
Writers should revise sentences to remove any dangler that could confuse readers.
After editing, there was no dangler left in the final draft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangling modifier - a word or phrase that does not clearly modify any word in a sentence
  • degree lower: modifier - a word or phrase that describes or limits another word
