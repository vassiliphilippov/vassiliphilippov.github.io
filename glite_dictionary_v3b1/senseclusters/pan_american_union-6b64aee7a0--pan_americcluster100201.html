Pan American Union

noun
former agency of American States organization
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈjuː.njən / (AmE), / ˌpan əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈjuː.njən / (BrE)
