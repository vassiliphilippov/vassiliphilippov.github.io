Karakoram Range

proper noun
mountain range in northern Kashmir
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkær.əˈkɔː.rəm reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌkær.əˈkɔː.rəm reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a mountain range in northern Kashmir, extending from the Hindu Kush, which contains K2, the world's second highest peak
