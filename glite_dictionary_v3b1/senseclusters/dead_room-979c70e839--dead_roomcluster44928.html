dead room

noun
room for keeping dead bodies
8.2
Pronunciation: / dɛd ruːm / (AmE), / dɛd ruːm / (BrE)
a room or building where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation; a mortuary

Examples

The dead room
After the accident, the bodies were taken to the dead room for identification.
Local regulations require a dead room in all major hospitals for storing corpses temporarily.
The hospital's dead room was cold and silent, awaiting the arrival of the undertaker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mortuary - a room or building for the storage of dead bodies
