Synonyms

    9.1 0.01/m

  • unpassable
    6.9 0.03/m
    unpassable describes a road or path not able to be crossed or traveled through, while impassable is the standard term.
  • uncrossable
    5.6 0.06/m
    uncrossable means you cannot cross or pass over something, while impassable is used for roads or areas you cannot travel through.
  • impenetrable
    7.3 0.9/m

  • inaccessible
