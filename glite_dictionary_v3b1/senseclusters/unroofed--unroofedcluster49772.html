unroofed

adjective
without a roof
6.3 0.03/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàruÀêft / (AmE), /  ånÀàruÀêft / (BrE)
(of a building or structure) lacking a roof; without a roof covering.

Examples

An unroofed building
During the storm, the unroofed barn offered little protection from the rain.
The ancient ruins stood unroofed and open to the sky.
They sheltered equipment in an unroofed shed at the edge of the field.

Synonyms

    4.1 0.32/m
