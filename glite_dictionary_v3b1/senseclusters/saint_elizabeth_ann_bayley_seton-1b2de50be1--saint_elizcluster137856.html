Saint Elizabeth Ann Bayley Seton

proper noun
first U.S.-born canonized saint
9.9
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ɪˈlɪzəˌbɛθ æn ˈbeɪli ˈsiːtən / (AmE), / seɪnt ɪˈlɪzəbəθ æn ˈbeɪli ˈsiːtən / (BrE)
(1774–1821) The first person born in the United States to be canonized as a saint; she was a religious leader and founder of the first Catholic girls' school in the U.S.
