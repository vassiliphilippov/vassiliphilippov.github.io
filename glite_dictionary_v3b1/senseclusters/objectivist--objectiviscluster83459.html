objectivist

adjective
relating to objectivism
8.8
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
relating to the belief that reality and truth exist independently of personal feelings or interpretations, or to the philosophy of Objectivism.

Examples

Objectivist viewpoint

The debate contrasted objectivist and subjectivist views on the nature of truth.
He wrote an objectivist analysis of the novel, avoiding emotional interpretations.
