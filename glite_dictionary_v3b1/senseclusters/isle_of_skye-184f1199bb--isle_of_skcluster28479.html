Isle of Skye

proper noun
Scottish island with rugged scenery
Pronunciation: / aɪl əv skaɪ / (AmE), / aɪl əv skaɪ / (BrE)
an island off the northwest coast of Scotland, famous for its rugged landscapes and dramatic mountain scenery
