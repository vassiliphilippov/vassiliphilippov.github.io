chevre

noun
goat's milk cheese
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛvɹə / (AmE), / ˈʃɛv.rə / (BrE)
a type of cheese made from goat's milk, often with a soft texture and tangy flavor, originating from France

Examples

A wheel of chevre
At the market, I bought a wheel of local chevre for the picnic.
She ordered a salad topped with creamy chevre and roasted beets.
The tangy flavor of chevre pairs well with fresh herbs and bread.
