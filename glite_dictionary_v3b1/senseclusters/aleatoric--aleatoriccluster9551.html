aleatoric

adjective
random or chance-based
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪliəˈtɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪliəˈtɒrɪk / (BrE)
involving elements of chance or randomness, especially in art, music, or literature

Examples

An aleatoric music
The artist's aleatoric approach allowed the final painting to be shaped by chance.
Some modern composers use aleatoric methods to introduce unpredictability into their music.
The novel's plot followed an aleatoric structure, with events determined by the roll of dice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deterministic - completely determined by previously existing causes
  • synonym: random - made, done, or happening without method or conscious decision
