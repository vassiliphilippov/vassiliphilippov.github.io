aleatoric

adjective
random or chance-based
9.9
Pronunciation: /eɪ.liː.ə.ˈtɔːr.ɪk/ /; / /ə.liː.ə.ˈtɔːr.ɪk/ (AmE), /eɪ.liː.ə.ˈtɔː.ɪk/ /; / /ə.liː.ə.ˈtɔː.ɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
