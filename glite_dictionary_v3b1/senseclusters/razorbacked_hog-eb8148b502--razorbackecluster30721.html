razorbacked hog

noun
wild hog with a ridged back, US South
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzərˌbækt hɑɡ / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəbækt hɒɡ / (BrE)
a wild or semi-wild hog with a thin body, long legs, and a prominent ridged back, found mainly in the southeastern United States

Examples

Wild razorbacked hogs
In parts of the rural South, razorbacked hogs still roam the forests in small herds.
Locals warned us to be cautious of aggressive razorbacked hogs in the area.
The farmers tried to keep the razorbacked hog from rooting in their vegetable gardens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: razorback - a wild hog with a sharp ridged back, especially in the southern U.S.
