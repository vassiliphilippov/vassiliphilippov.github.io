pleasure seeker

noun
person who seeks sensual pleasures
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛʒər ˌsiːkər / (AmE), / ˈplɛʒə ˌsiːkə / (BrE)
a person who is motivated by the pursuit of sensual or physical pleasures

Examples

A pleasure seeker lifestyle
As a lifelong pleasure seeker, she never missed an opportunity to travel to exotic destinations.
He did not want to be seen merely as a pleasure seeker but as someone with deeper ambitions.
The city attracts pleasure seekers from all over the world during the summer festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascetic - a person who abstains from sensual pleasures for spiritual reasons
  • synonym: hedonist - a person who believes that pleasure is the most important thing in life
