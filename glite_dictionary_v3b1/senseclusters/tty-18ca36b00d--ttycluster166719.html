TTY

noun
device for typed calls
8.5 0.25/m
Pronunciation: / ti ti wa…™ / (AmE), / tiÀê tiÀê wa…™ / (BrE)
a telecommunications device that enables deaf or hard-of-hearing people to communicate by typing text over telephone lines

Examples

Hotel TTY device
The hotel provided a dedicated TTY at reception to assist guests with hearing impairments.
If you need to contact customer service, you can use the TTY number listed on their website.
Many public institutions offer TTY access to ensure communication is available for everyone.

Synonyms

  • teletypewriter
    8.8 0.02/m
    teletypewriter prints typed messages onto paper and serves telegraph or phone systems, while TTY lets deaf users type live text over telephone lines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: TDD - telecommunications device for the deaf, another term for TTY
  • antonym: telephone - a device for transmitting voice over distances
