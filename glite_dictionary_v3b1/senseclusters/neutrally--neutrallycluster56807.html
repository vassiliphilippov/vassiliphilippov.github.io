neutrally

adverb
without charge or acidity
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəli / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəli / (BrE)
in a way that has no electrical charge or is chemically neither acidic nor basic

Examples

React neutrally
The solution reacted neutrally when tested with litmus paper in the laboratory.
Because the atom is neutrally charged, it does not respond to the electric field.
The water in the experiment remained neutrally balanced, showing neither acidic nor basic properties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acidically - in a way that is acidic
  • antonym: basically - in a way that is basic or alkaline
  • synonym: electrically - in a way relating to electricity
