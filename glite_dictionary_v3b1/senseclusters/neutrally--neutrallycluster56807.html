neutrally

adverb
without charge or acidity
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈnuː.trə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈnjuː.trə.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that has no electrical charge or is chemically neither acidic nor basic

Examples

React neutrally

Because the atom is neutrally charged, it does not respond to the electric field.
The water in the experiment remained neutrally balanced, showing neither acidic nor basic properties.
