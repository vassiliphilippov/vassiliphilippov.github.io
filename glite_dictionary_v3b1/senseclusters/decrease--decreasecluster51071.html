decrease

verb
make smaller or less
3.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkris / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːs / (BrE)
to become or make something smaller or less in size, amount, or degree

Examples

To decrease the risk
She decided to decrease her sugar intake for better health.
The company plans to decrease its workforce by ten percent this year.
We need to decrease our energy consumption to help the environment.
As the evening progressed, the noise from the street gradually decreased.
Doctors recommend decreasing your salt intake to lower blood pressure.
Sales continued to decrease throughout the year despite marketing efforts.
