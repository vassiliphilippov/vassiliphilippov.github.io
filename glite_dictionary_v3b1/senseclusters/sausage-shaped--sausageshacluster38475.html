sausage-shaped

adjective
long, cylindrical, rounded like a sausage
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈsɒsɪdʒ ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a sausage; long, cylindrical, and rounded at the ends.

Examples

A sausage-shaped object
He was served a sausage-shaped portion of mashed potatoes on his plate.
The old water tank was oddly sausage-shaped rather than round.
The worm had a sausage-shaped body that made it easy to identify.
