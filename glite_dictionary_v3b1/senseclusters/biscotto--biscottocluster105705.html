biscotto

noun
Italian twice-baked cookie
Pronunciation: / bɪˈskɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / bɪˈskɒtəʊ / (BrE)
a dry, crunchy Italian cookie, typically containing nuts, that is baked twice and often served with coffee or tea

Examples

A biscotto cookie
She dipped her biscotto into the hot coffee and savored the flavor.
At the Italian bakery, I bought an almond biscotto to enjoy with tea.
The chef explained how to make a traditional biscotto by baking it twice for extra crunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biscotti - the plural form of biscotto, Italian twice-baked cookies
  • antonym: cake - a sweet baked dessert, usually soft and spongy
  • synonym: cookie - a sweet baked treat, usually round and flat
