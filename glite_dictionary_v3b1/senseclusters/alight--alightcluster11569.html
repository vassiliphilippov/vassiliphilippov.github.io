alight

verb
to get off a vehicle
4.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈlaɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈlaɪt/ (BrE)
to get off or come down from a vehicle, animal, or elevated place

Examples

Passengers alight from bus
Please alight at the next stop for the museum.
Passengers should alight from the train carefully when it arrives at the platform.
She alighted from the bus and walked towards the city center.
Please alight from the bus at the next stop.
The children alighted quickly when the train reached the station.
Passengers should alight carefully to avoid slipping on the platform.
