tonneau

noun
large wine barrel
Pronunciation: / təˈnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɒnəʊ / (BrE)
a large cask or barrel, especially one used historically for storing wine or other liquids

Examples

Wine tonneau
The winery stored aging wine in a large oak tonneau behind the tasting room.
A decorative tonneau stood in the corner of the old cellar, evoking memories of past harvests.
They purchased a vintage tonneau to use as a unique table in their dining room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrel - a large, round container for storing liquids
  • synonym: cask - a container for holding liquids, especially alcoholic drinks
