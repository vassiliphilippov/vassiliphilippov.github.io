vertical fin

noun
airplane tail stabilizer
7.2 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːtɪkəl fɪn / (AmE), / ˈvɜːtɪkəl fɪn / (BrE)
A vertical stabilizing surface that is part of the tail structure of an airplane, helping to maintain directional stability.

Examples

The vertical fin of airplane
Damaged vertical fins can reduce an aircraft's ability to stay on course.
Engineers redesigned the vertical fin to improve aerodynamic efficiency.
The vertical fin helps keep the airplane stable in flight.
