vertical fin

noun
airplane tail stabilizer
4.3 0.02/m

A vertical stabilizing surface that is part of the tail structure of an airplane, helping to maintain directional stability.

Examples

The vertical fin of airplane
Damaged vertical fins can reduce an aircraft's ability to stay on course.
Engineers redesigned the vertical fin to improve aerodynamic efficiency.
The vertical fin helps keep the airplane stable in flight.

Synonyms

  • vertical stabilizer
    5.9 0.01/m
    vertical stabilizer is the standard technical name in manuals, while vertical fin is the informal, shorter label.
  • tail fin
    4.2 0.06/m
    tail fin is a common, simple name for the vertical surface at an aircraft's tail, while vertical fin is the formal technical term.
  • vertical tail
    6.0 0.02/m
    vertical tail is the wider upright assembly that can include fin and rudder, while vertical fin names the single stabilizing surface.
