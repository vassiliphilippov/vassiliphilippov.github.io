ghast

adjective
pale from fear
Pronunciation: / ɡæst / (AmE), / ɡɑːst / (BrE)
very pale or deathly in appearance, as from shock, fear, or illness; appearing frightened or ghostlike (archaic or dialectal)

Examples

Ghast face
He looked ghast after hearing the terrible news late at night.
The child appeared ghast when she saw the shadow move across her window.
After the accident, her face was so ghast that everyone grew concerned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ashen - extremely pale, especially from shock or illness
  • synonym: pale - having little color in the face, often from fear or illness
  • antonym: rosy - having a healthy pink color
