silent

adjective
not speaking when expected
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪlənt / (AmE), / ˈsaɪlənt / (BrE)
not saying anything or refusing to speak when speech or communication is expected

Examples

Silent reply
He was silent when his opinion was needed most.
She remained silent during the entire investigation, refusing to answer questions.
The room fell silent as everyone waited for the announcement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mute - refraining from speech or temporarily speechless
  • antonym: vocal - expressing opinions or feelings freely or loudly
