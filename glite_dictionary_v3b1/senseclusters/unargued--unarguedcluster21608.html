unargued

adjective
not debated
7.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑrɡjud / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːɡjuːd / (BrE)
not discussed, debated, or presented as an argument, especially in a legal or formal context

Examples

An unargued point
The judge made a ruling on the unargued issue without hearing from either side.
Some points in the contract remained unargued during the negotiations.
The lawyer was surprised that the court addressed an unargued matter in its decision.
