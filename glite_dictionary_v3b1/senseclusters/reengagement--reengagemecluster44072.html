reengagement

noun
return to involvement
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
the act of becoming involved again in an activity, process, or relationship after a period of absence

Examples

Reengagement of staff
The company encouraged the reengagement of former employees through special programs.
After months away from school, her reengagement in classes was noticeable to her teachers.
Community leaders promoted reengagement in local events to strengthen neighborhood ties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disengagement - the act of withdrawing or becoming less involved
  • synonym: renewal - the process of resuming or starting again
