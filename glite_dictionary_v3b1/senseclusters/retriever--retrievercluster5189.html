retriever

noun
type of dog trained to retrieve game
Pronunciation: / rəˈtrivər / (AmE), / rɪˈtriːvə / (BrE)
a type of dog with a heavy, water-resistant coat, bred and trained to retrieve game for hunters

Examples

Golden retriever
During the competition, the retriever demonstrated impressive obedience and retrieving skills.
She adopted a golden retriever because of its friendly and gentle nature.
The retriever bounded across the field to fetch the duck for its owner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Labrador retriever - a popular retriever breed known for a gentle temperament
  • synonym: golden retriever - a retriever breed with a wavy golden coat
  • antonym: pointer - a hunting dog that indicates the presence of game
