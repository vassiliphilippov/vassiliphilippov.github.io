non-skilled

noun
untrained worker
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskɪld / (AmE), / nɒnˈskɪld / (BrE)
a person who does work that does not require special training, education, or professional skills

Examples

A non-skilled
Many non-skilled are employed in entry-level positions that require only basic training.
The company offers opportunities for non-skilled to learn new trades and advance their careers.
Programs were created to help non-skilled find stable employment in the manufacturing sector.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unskilled - a person lacking special training or qualifications
