biathlon

noun
sport with running and shooting
6.1
Pronunciation: / baɪˈæθlɑn / (AmE), / baɪˈæθlən / (BrE)
a sport in which competitors alternate between cross-country skiing (or running/roller skiing) and rifle shooting

Examples

Winter biathlon race
He trained for months to compete in his first biathlon this summer.
The biathlon requires both endurance for running and accuracy in shooting.
Many athletes enjoy the challenge of the biathlon because it tests multiple skills.
He trained for months to compete in the biathlon at the Winter Olympics.
The biathlon requires both endurance for skiing and accuracy in shooting.
Many spectators enjoy watching the biathlon because of its unique combination of skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duathlon - a race combining running and cycling
  • synonym: pentathlon - an athletic contest with five different events
  • synonym: triathlon - a race combining swimming, cycling, and running
