carboxyl

adjective
of or containing a carboxyl group
8.3
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈbɑksɪl / (AmE), / kɑːˈbɒksɪl / (BrE)
relating to or containing the carboxyl group (–COOH) or carboxyl radical in its structure.

Examples

Carboxyl compound
A carboxyl group consists of a carbon atom double-bonded to an oxygen atom and single-bonded to a hydroxyl group.
Adding a carboxyl group to the molecule increased its acidity.
Many amino acids have a carboxyl functional group in their structure.
