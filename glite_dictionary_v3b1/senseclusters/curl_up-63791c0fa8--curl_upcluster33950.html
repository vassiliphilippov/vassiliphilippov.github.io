curl up

verb
bend body into a curved position
2.8 4.19/m
Pronunciation: / k…ùl  åp / (AmE), / k…úÀêl  åp / (BrE)
to move your body so that your arms and legs are close to you, usually to feel comfortable, warm, or safe

Examples
