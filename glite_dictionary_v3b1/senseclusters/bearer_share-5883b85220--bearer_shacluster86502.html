bearer share

noun
share certificate without registered owner
8.3 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈber.ər ʃer/ /; / /ˈbeə.rər ʃer/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.rə ʃeə/ (BrE)
a share certificate that does not record the owner's name, so whoever physically possesses it is presumed to be the owner

Examples

A bearer share certificate
Because bearer shares are unregistered, they pose a higher risk of being used for money laundering.
In the past, many companies issued bearer shares for ease of transfer.
Several countries have enacted laws to restrict or eliminate bearer shares.

Related Terms

  • antonym: registered share - a share whose owner's name is registered with the company
