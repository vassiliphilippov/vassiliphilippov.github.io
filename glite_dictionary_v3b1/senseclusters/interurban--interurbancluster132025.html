interurban

noun
railway between cities
6.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈɜːr.bən/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈɜː.bən/ (BrE)
a passenger railway or vehicle that operates between towns or cities, especially for public transport

Examples

An interurban train
The old interurban connected several small towns before it was replaced by buses.
Many people relied on the interurban to travel to work in the nearby city.
Historic photos show the interurban passing through rural landscapes between communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commuter train - a train that carries passengers between suburbs and cities
  • antonym: streetcar - a rail vehicle that runs on tracks in city streets
