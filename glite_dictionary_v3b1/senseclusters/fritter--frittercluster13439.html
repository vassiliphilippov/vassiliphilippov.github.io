fritter

verb
waste money or time carelessly
7.6 5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪt̬.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfrɪt.ə / (BrE)
to waste time, money, or resources frivolously and unwisely, especially little by little (usually used as 'fritter away')

Examples

To fritter away money
He managed to fritter away most of his savings on unnecessary gadgets.
Over the years, they had frittered away the fortune their grandparents had left them.
She didn't want to fritter her time on trivial matters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conserve - to protect something from harm or destruction; to use carefully
  • synonym: squander - to waste something, especially money or time, in a reckless manner
