catchiness

noun
memorability or appeal
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈkætʃinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to remember or very appealing, especially in music or advertising

Examples

Catchiness of a song
The catchiness of the commercial jingle made it stick in everyone's mind.
Many pop songs owe their success to their catchiness and simple melodies.
Advertisers often look for slogans with strong catchiness to attract customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeal - the quality of being attractive or interesting
  • antonym: dullness - the quality of being uninteresting or hard to remember
  • synonym: memorability - the quality of being easily remembered
