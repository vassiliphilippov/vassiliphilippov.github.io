catchiness

noun
memorability or appeal
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkætʃ.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈkætʃ.iː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkætʃ.ɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈkætʃ.iː.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being easy to remember or very appealing, especially in music or advertising

Examples

Catchiness of a song

Many pop songs owe their success to their catchiness and simple melodies.
Advertisers often look for slogans with strong catchiness to attract customers.
