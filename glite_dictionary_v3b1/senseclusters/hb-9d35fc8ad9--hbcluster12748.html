An abbreviation for hemoglobin, a protein in red blood cells responsible for transporting oxygen from the lungs to body tissues.

Examples

Low Hb level
A low Hb reading may indicate a risk of insufficient oxygen transport in the body.
Doctors were concerned when his Hb count dropped significantly after surgery.
The laboratory measured her Hb levels to check for anemia.
