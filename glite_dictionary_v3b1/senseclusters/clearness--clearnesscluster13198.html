clearness

noun
the quality of being pure or transparent (of water)
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪr.nəs / (AmE), / ˈklɪə.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pure, transparent, or easy to see through, especially in reference to liquids like water

Examples

Water clearness
After filtration, the clearness of the sample improved dramatically.
Scientists measured the clearness of the water to monitor pollution levels.
The clearness of the lake allowed us to see fish swimming near the bottom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear; transparency; often used for liquids
