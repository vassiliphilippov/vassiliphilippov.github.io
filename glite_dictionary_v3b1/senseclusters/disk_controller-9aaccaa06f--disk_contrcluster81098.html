disk controller

noun
circuit controlling a hard disk drive
4.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /dɪsk kən.ˈtroʊ.lər/ (AmE), /dɪsk kən.ˈtrəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
a circuit or chip in a computer that translates commands into signals that control a hard disk drive

Examples

If the disk controller fails, the computer may not recognize any connected drives.
The disk controller interprets requests from the operating system to read or write data.
