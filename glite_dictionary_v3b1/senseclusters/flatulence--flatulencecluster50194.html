flatulence

noun
excessive gas in the digestive tract

Pronunciation: /ˈflæt.jʊ.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.jə.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.tʃʊ.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.tʃə.ləns/ (AmE), /ˈflæt.jʊ.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.jə.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.tʃʊ.ləns/ /; / /ˈflæt.tʃə.ləns/ (BrE)
the presence or release of gas in the stomach or intestines, often causing discomfort or the need to expel gas

Examples

Digestive flatulence
Doctors often ask patients about flatulence when diagnosing digestive problems.

Some medications can help reduce flatulence after eating heavy meals.
