flatulence

noun
excessive gas in the digestive tract
Pronunciation: / ˈflætʃələns / (AmE), / ˈflætjʊləns / (BrE)
the presence or release of gas in the stomach or intestines, often causing discomfort or the need to expel gas

Examples

Digestive flatulence
Doctors often ask patients about flatulence when diagnosing digestive problems.
She felt uncomfortable because of severe flatulence after eating beans.
Some medications can help relieve symptoms of flatulence and bloating.
Certain beans are known to cause flatulence in many people.
He felt embarrassed by his frequent flatulence during the meeting.
Some medications can help reduce flatulence after eating heavy meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloating - a swollen feeling in the abdomen caused by gas or fluid
  • synonym: constipation - a condition in which it is difficult to empty the bowels
  • synonym: gas - air or gas in the digestive tract
