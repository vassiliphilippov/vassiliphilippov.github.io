stockfish

noun
fish air-dried without salt
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈstɒkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
fish, especially cod, that has been split, cleaned, and air-dried without being salted.

Examples

Dried stockfish
He exported barrels of dried stockfish to countries across Europe.
The villagers relied on stockfish as a staple during the long winter months.
Traditional Norwegian cuisine often features stockfish prepared in various ways.
