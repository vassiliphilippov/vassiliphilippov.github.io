slice

noun
tool for spreading paint or ink
6.1
Pronunciation: / slaɪs / (AmE), / slaɪs / (BrE)
a flat, thin spatula used for spreading paint or ink, as in printmaking or painting.

Examples

A paint slice
After inking the plate, the printmaker wiped it carefully with a slice.
Every student received a slice for applying the ink evenly during the workshop.
The artist used a slice to smooth the thick layers of oil paint on the canvas.
