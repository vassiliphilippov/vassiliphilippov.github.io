multifamily

adjective
housing for multiple families
3.5
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.ˈfæ.mə.liː/ /; / /mʌl.taɪ.ˈfæ.mə.liː/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.ˈfæ.mə.liː/ /; / /mʌl.taɪ.ˈfæ.mə.liː/ (BrE)
describing a building or property designed to house more than one family in separate units

Examples

Multifamily apartment building

The city encourages the development of multifamily housing to address the growing population.
A multifamily property can provide more rental income than a single-family home.
