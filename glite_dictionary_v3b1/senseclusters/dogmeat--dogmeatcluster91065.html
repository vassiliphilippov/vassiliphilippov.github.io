dogmeat

noun
worthless person or thing
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌmit / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌmiːt / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing considered worthless, easily defeated, or in a hopeless situation

Examples

Dogmeat computer
After the new software update, my old laptop was basically dogmeat and couldn't run any programs.
He knew he was dogmeat the moment he saw his opponent in the final round.
The team was considered dogmeat after losing every game that season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: champion - a person who has defeated all rivals
  • synonym: loser - a person who is unsuccessful or regarded as a failure
  • synonym: underdog - a person or team expected to lose
