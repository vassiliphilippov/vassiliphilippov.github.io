microbicidal

adjective
kills microorganisms
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈbɪsɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈbɪsɪdəl / (BrE)
able to kill or destroy microorganisms such as bacteria or viruses

Examples

Microbicidal solutions
The laboratory tested several microbicidal solutions to ensure the equipment was free of harmful germs.
Doctors recommend using microbicidal hand sanitizers in hospitals to prevent infections.
A microbicidal coating was applied to the surfaces to reduce the spread of viruses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antimicrobial - able to kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms
  • synonym: bactericidal - able to kill bacteria
  • antonym: microbiostatic - inhibiting the growth of microorganisms without killing them
