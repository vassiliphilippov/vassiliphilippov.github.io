sanga

noun
African cattle breed
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsæŋ.ə/ (AmE), /ˈsæŋ.ə/ (BrE)
a type of African cattle breed, intermediate between humpless taurine and zebu, kept for milk and meat

The sanga breed is well adapted to the hot and dry climate of the region.
