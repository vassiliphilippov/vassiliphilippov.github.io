wagerer

noun
a person who bets
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪdʒərər / (AmE), / ˈweɪdʒərə / (BrE)
a person who makes or places a bet; someone who wagers money or something of value on the outcome of an event

Examples

A wagerer places bets
As a regular wagerer, she understood both the risks and rewards of gambling.
Every wagerer at the table waited anxiously as the dealer revealed the winning card.
The experienced wagerer studied the odds carefully before placing his bet on the racehorse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bettor - a person who bets
  • synonym: punter - a person who bets (primarily British)
