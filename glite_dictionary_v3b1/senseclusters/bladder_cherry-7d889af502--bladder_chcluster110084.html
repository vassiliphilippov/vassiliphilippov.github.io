bladder cherry

noun
ornamental plant with papery orange-red calyces
Pronunciation: / ˈblædər ˌtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈblædə ˌtʃɛri / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the nightshade family with distinctive balloon-like, orange-red papery calyces, often grown for ornamental purposes.

Examples

Bladder cherries plant
She planted a row of bladder cherries along the fence for their striking appearance in fall.
The bladder cherry adds a splash of color to autumn gardens with its bright orange husks.
The children were fascinated by the papery shells of the bladder cherry fruit in the backyard.
