reinscribe

verb
to draw inside again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈskraɪb / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈskraɪb / (BrE)
to draw or place a geometric figure inside another again, especially as part of a construction or proof

Examples

To reinscribe a square
The mathematician decided to reinscribe the square within the circle to check her calculations.
If you make a mistake, you can always reinscribe the triangle to ensure accuracy.
During the lesson, the teacher asked the students to reinscribe the polygon inside the given shape.
