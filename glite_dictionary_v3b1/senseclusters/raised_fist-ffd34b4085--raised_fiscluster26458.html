raised fist

noun
hand gesture meaning defiance or solidarity
5.9 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzd fɪst / (AmE), / ˈreɪzd fɪst / (BrE)
a hand gesture in which the fist is held up, symbolizing defiance or solidarity, especially in protests or demonstrations

Examples

A raised fist salute
He finished the race and saluted the cheering fans with a triumphant raised fist.
She stood on the stage, her raised fist a sign of unity with the crowd.
The photograph captured a sea of raised fists during the protest.
