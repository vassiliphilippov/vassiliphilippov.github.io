slide chart

noun
hand-held device for quick calculations or reference
4.2 4.8
Pronunciation: /slaɪd tʃɑːrt/ (AmE), /slaɪd tʃɑːt/ (BrE)
a portable device, typically made of paper, cardboard, or plastic, used for performing simple calculations or quickly looking up information by sliding panels or overlays

Examples

A slide chart device
Before smartphones, people often carried a slide chart to look up important medical data.
Many students found the slide chart helpful when converting between different units.
The nurse used a slide chart to determine the correct dosage for the patient.
