outpour

verb
to pour out
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpɔr / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌpɔː / (BrE)
to cause liquid or another substance to flow out from a container or place in a large amount

Examples

To outpour water
She outpoured the cup of water into the sink to clean the vase.
The scientist carefully outpoured the solution into the beaker for the experiment.
He outpours the contents of the bottle onto the ground during the ritual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pour - to make a liquid flow from a container
  • synonym: spill - to accidentally let liquid flow out of a container
