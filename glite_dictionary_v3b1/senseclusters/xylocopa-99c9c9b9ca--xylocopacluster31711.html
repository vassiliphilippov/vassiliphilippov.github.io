Xylocopa

proper noun
carpenter bee genus
Pronunciation: / zaɪˈlɑːkəpə / (AmE), / zaɪˈlɒkəpə / (BrE)
a genus of large bees commonly known as carpenter bees, noted for boring into wood to build nests

Examples

Xylocopa species name
The Xylocopa can often be seen hovering near wooden fences in the summer.
Scientists studied the nesting habits of Xylocopa in different types of wood.
Unlike honeybees, Xylocopa build their nests by tunneling into dead trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter bee - a large bee that bores into wood to make its nest
  • antonym: honeybee - a bee that produces honey and lives in large colonies
