unmaneuverable

adjective
difficult to steer
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnməˈnuːvərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnməˈnjuːvərəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be moved, steered, or handled easily or effectively

Examples

Unmaneuverable ship
The massive ship was almost unmaneuverable in the narrow harbor.
Because of its size, the old sofa was completely unmaneuverable through the apartment doorway.
Heavy snow made the small car nearly unmaneuverable on the icy road.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maneuverable - able to be moved or steered easily
  • synonym: unwieldy - difficult to handle or move because of size or shape
