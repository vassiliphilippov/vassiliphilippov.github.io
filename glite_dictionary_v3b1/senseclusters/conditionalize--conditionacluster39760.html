conditionalize

verb
to make dependent on conditions
7.9
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to make something dependent on a condition or set of conditions

Examples

To conditionalize payment
The contract will conditionalize payment on the completion of all tasks.
Teachers may conditionalize extra credit on students' attendance and participation.
The company decided to conditionalize bonuses based on yearly performance reviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condition - to make something subject to specific requirements
  • antonym: unconditional - not subject to any conditions
