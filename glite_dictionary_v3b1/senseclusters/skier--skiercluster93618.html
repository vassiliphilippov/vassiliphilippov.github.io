skier

noun
person who skis
Pronunciation: / ˈskiːər / (AmE), / ˈskiːə / (BrE)
a person who moves over snow on skis as a sport or recreation

Examples

A skier on slopes
As a beginner skier, he took lessons before trying the slopes on his own.
She has been a passionate skier since she was a child.
The Olympic gold medalist is a professional skier from Austria.

Related Terms

  • antonym: snowboarder - a person who rides a snowboard
