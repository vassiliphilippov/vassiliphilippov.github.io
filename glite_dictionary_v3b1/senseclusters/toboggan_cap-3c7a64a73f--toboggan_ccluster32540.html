toboggan cap

noun
close-fitting woolen winter hat
8.3
Pronunciation: / təˈbɑɡən kæp / (AmE), / təˈbɒɡən kap / (BrE)
A close-fitting knitted woolen hat, often with a tapering tail and a tassel, worn in cold weather.

Examples

A toboggan cap with tassel
During winter, you should always wear a toboggan cap to keep your head warm.
He pulled his toboggan cap down over his ears before stepping out into the snow.
Her red toboggan cap had a cheerful pom-pom that bounced as she walked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beanie - a small, close-fitting round cap, usually woolen
  • synonym: stocking cap - a long, close-fitting knitted cap, often with a tassel
