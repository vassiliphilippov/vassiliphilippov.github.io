pitter

noun
soft repeated sound
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɪtə / (BrE)
a light, soft, and repeated sound, especially made by rain or small feet

Examples

Pitter of rain
The pitter of raindrops on the roof made the evening feel cozy.
She listened to the pitter of her cat's paws as it ran across the floor.
The gentle pitter outside signaled the start of a spring shower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patter - a light, rapid, and repeated tapping sound
