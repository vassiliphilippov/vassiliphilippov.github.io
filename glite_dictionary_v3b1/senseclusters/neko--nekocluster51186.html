neko

noun
cat-like character
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈneːkəʊ / (BrE)
a cat, or a character with cat-like features, especially in Japanese anime and manga

Examples

A neko character
The main character in the show is a playful neko with big green eyes.
Many fans enjoy drawing neko characters with cute cat ears and tails.
She wore a neko costume to the anime convention last weekend.
