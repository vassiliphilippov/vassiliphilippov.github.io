littoral

noun
the region along a shore or coast
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərəl / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərəl / (BrE)
the part of the shore of a sea, lake, or ocean, especially the region lying between the high- and low-water marks

Examples

The littoral area
Erosion has dramatically changed the littoral in this region over the past decade.
Many species of birds nest in the littoral along the lake during spring.
The littoral was rich with tidal pools and marine life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coast - the land next to the sea; seashore
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the coast or shore
