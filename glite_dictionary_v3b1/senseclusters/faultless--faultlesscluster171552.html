faultless

adjective
perfect; without fault or error
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔltlɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːltlɪs / (BrE)
Free from mistakes, flaws, or imperfections; perfect.

Examples

A faultless record
He prided himself on maintaining a faultless attendance record at work.
Her faultless performance impressed both the judges and the audience.
The architect delivered a faultless design that met every requirement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flawless - without flaws or imperfections
  • synonym: impeccable - without any faults or errors; perfect
  • antonym: imperfect - not perfect; having faults or errors
