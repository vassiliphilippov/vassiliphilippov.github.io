reloader

noun
automatic refilling device
8.0
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈloʊ.dər/ /; / /rɪ.ˈloʊ.dər/ (AmE), /riː.ˈləʊ.də/ /; / /rɪ.ˈləʊ.də/ (BrE)
a machine or device that automatically refills magazines, cartridges, or other consumable supplies in equipment or on a production line

Examples

Automatic reloader machine
The technician installed a new reloader to keep the assembly line running smoothly.
A compact reloader automatically refills the cartridges in the printing press during each production cycle.
Without a reloader, the workers had to stop frequently to restock the machine.
