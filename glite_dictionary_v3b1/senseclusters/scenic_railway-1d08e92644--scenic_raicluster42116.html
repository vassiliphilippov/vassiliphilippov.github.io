scenic railway

noun
amusement park scenic train ride
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːnɪk ˈreɪlˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsiːnɪk ˈreɪl.weɪ / (BrE)
a small railway, especially in an amusement park, designed to give passengers a scenic or entertaining ride

Examples

A scenic railway ride
A classic scenic railway winds through artificial hills and tunnels, offering a pleasant view.
Many seaside resorts feature a scenic railway as one of their main attractions.
The children were excited to ride the scenic railway at the amusement park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roller coaster - track with sharp curves and steep slopes for amusement rides
