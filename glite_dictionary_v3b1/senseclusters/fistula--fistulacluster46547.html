fistula

noun
chronic inflammation of horse's withers
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪs.tʃə.lə / (AmE), / ˈfɪs.tjʊ.lə / (BrE)
A chronic inflammation, especially of the withers in horses, resulting in an abnormal passage or sore.

Examples

Horse fistula
Chronic fistula can cause significant discomfort and require extended treatment for horses.
Early intervention is important when a fistula starts to develop, to prevent infection spreading.
The veterinarian diagnosed the horse with a fistula after observing inflammation at its withers.
