satisfactory

adjective
good enough; meeting requirements
Pronunciation: / ˌsæt̬.ɪsˈfæk.tɚ.i /; / ˌsæ.tɪsˈfæk.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌsæt.ɪsˈfæk.tər.i / (BrE)
good enough to meet a need or requirement; acceptable, though not excellent

Examples

A satisfactory result
After reviewing the repairs, Julia found the results to be satisfactory.
His performance on the test was satisfactory, but there is still room for improvement.
The inspector confirmed that the restaurant's cleanliness was satisfactory.
Her work performance this year has been satisfactory, but there is room for improvement.
Is the quality of this product satisfactory for your needs?
The solution provided a satisfactory answer to the client's problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acceptable - good enough for a purpose or requirement
  • synonym: adequate - sufficient for a specific need; acceptable but not impressive
  • synonym: satisfying - giving pleasure or fulfillment
  • antonym: unsatisfactory - not good enough; not meeting requirements
