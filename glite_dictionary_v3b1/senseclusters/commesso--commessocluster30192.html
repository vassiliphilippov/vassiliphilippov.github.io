commesso

noun
shop assistant
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈmɛsoʊ / (AmE), / kɒˈmɛsəʊ / (BrE)
a shop assistant or salesperson, especially in a retail store, originally from Italian

Examples

A friendly commesso
The commesso greeted every customer with a warm smile as they entered the shop.
She asked the commesso for advice on choosing a gift for her friend.
A helpful commesso quickly found the right size for the customer.
