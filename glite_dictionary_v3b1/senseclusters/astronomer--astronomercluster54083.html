astronomer

noun
person who studies astronomy
3.4 29/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàstr…ëÀê.n…ô.m…ö / (AmE), / …ôÀàstr…ín.…ô.m…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job or hobby is the scientific study of stars, planets, and other objects in outer space

Examples

Professional astronomer
As a child, she dreamed of becoming an astronomer and exploring the universe.
Caroline Herschel was a pioneering astronomer in the 18th century.
The astronomer observed a new comet through the powerful telescope.
