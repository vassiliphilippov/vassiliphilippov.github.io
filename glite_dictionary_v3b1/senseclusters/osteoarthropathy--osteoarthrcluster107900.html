osteoarthropathy

noun
bone and joint disease
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstioʊɑrˈθɑpəθi / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊɑːˈθɒpəθi / (BrE)
a disease or disorder that affects both bones and joints, often causing pain, swelling, or other symptoms

Examples

Osteoarthropathy disease

Early diagnosis of osteoarthropathy is important for effective treatment and management.
Researchers are studying new medications to help patients with osteoarthropathy improve their quality of life.
