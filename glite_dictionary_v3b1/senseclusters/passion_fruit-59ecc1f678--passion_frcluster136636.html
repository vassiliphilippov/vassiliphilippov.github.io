passion fruit

noun
tropical fruit with sweet-tart flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈpæʃən fruːt /; / ˈpæʃən frut / (AmE), / ˈpæʃən fruːt / (BrE)
a small, round or egg-shaped tropical fruit with a tough rind and sweet-tart, aromatic pulp, used in desserts, drinks, and other foods

Examples

Fresh passion fruit
Many sorbets use passion fruit to achieve a vibrant, tangy flavor.
She decorated the cake with slices of passion fruit for a tropical touch.
The juice from a ripe passion fruit adds a refreshing zing to cocktails.
She bought a basket of ripe passion fruits at the exotic fruit market.
Some people find the taste of passion fruit juice too sour, but I enjoy it.
The cheesecake was topped with fresh passion fruit for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granadilla - another name for certain types of passion fruit, especially in South America
