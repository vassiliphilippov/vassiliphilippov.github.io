lomilomi

noun
Hawaiian massage practitioner
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊmiˈloʊmi / (AmE), / ˌlɒmiˈlɒmi / (BrE)
a practitioner of lomilomi, the traditional Hawaiian massage technique involving rhythmic movements and healing touch

Examples

A skilled lomilomi
The lomilomi used gentle, flowing strokes to help relax her muscles.
After a long hike, he booked a session with a skilled lomilomi for relief.
Many visitors seek out a traditional lomilomi when traveling to Hawaii.

  • synonym: masseur - a person who gives massages
