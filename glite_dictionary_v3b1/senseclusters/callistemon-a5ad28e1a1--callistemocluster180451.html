Callistemon

proper noun
Australian bottlebrush shrub
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪstəˌmɒn / (AmE), / kəˈlɪstɪmən / (BrE)
a genus of shrubs native to Australia, commonly known as bottlebrushes for their cylindrical, brush-like flowers

Examples

Callistemon shrubs
The bright red flowers of the Callistemon attract many birds to the garden.
Gardeners often plant Callistemon for its striking, brush-like blooms.
Native to Australia, Callistemon thrives in warm, sunny climates.
