hehe

noun
soft laugh in text
7.8
Pronunciation: / hiːˈhiː / (AmE), / hiːˈhiː / (BrE)
a short, quiet laugh or giggle, often used in text messages to show mild amusement or playfulness

Examples

Send a hehe
She sent a quick hehe after reading his funny message.
A soft hehe could be heard as the children watched the cartoon.
He replied with a hehe to show he was only joking.
