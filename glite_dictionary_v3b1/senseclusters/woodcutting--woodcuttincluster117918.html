woodcutting

noun
printmaking technique
3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊd.kʌt.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwʊd.kʌt.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the art or process of carving designs into wood blocks to create images for printing

Examples

Traditional woodcutting art
The artist demonstrated traditional woodcutting techniques to the students during the workshop.
Many famous prints from the Renaissance were created using woodcutting methods.
She studied woodcutting to learn how to make her own illustrated books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engraving - the process of cutting designs into a hard surface for printing
  • synonym: etching - a printmaking technique using acid to cut into a metal surface
