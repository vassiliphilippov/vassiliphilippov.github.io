tabasco

noun
a very hot chili pepper
3.5
Pronunciation: / təˈbæskoʊ / (AmE), / təˈbæs.kəʊ / (BrE)
A type of very hot, red chili pepper that is usually long and thin, often used in spicy foods and sauces.

A tabasco pepper
