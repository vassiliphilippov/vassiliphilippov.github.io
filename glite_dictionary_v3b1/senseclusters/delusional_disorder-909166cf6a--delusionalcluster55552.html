delusional disorder

noun
mental disorder marked by delusions
Pronunciation: / dəˈluːʒənəl dɪsˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈluːʒənəl dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)
A mental disorder characterized by the presence of persistent delusions with little or no accompanying hallucinations, mood disturbance, or disorganized behavior.

Examples

Delusional disorder symptoms
Effective treatment for delusional disorder may include therapy and medication.
Family members often struggle to understand the impact of delusional disorder on daily life.
The patient was diagnosed with delusional disorder after months of persistent, unshakeable beliefs.
