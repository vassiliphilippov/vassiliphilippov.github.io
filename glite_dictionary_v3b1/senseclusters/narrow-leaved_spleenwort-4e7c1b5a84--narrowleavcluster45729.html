narrow-leaved spleenwort

noun
North American fern with narrow fronds
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛroʊ liːvd ˈspliːnwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ liːvd ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
A North American fern species with narrow fronds borne on yellowish leafstalks.

Examples

The narrow-leaved spleenwort fern
Botanists identified the narrow-leaved spleenwort by its distinct yellowish leafstalks.
The narrow-leaved spleenwort thrives in rocky crevices and shaded forest areas.
We discovered a patch of narrow-leaved spleenwort growing along the mountain trail.
