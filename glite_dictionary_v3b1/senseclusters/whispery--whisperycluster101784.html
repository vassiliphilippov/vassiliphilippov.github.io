whispery

adjective
soft and quiet
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspəri / (AmE), / ˈwɪspəri / (BrE)
soft and quiet in a way that resembles a whisper

Examples

A whispery voice
The singer's whispery tone gave the song a gentle, emotional feel.
A whispery breeze moved through the trees, barely making a sound.
He told the story in a whispery voice so no one else would hear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hushed - very quiet or silent
  • antonym: loud - making a lot of noise
