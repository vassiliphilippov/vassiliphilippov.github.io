amma

noun
South Asian mother
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈʌmə / (BrE)
a respectful or affectionate term for mother, especially used in South Asian families

Examples

My amma cooks dinner
He called out to his amma for help with his homework.
During the festival, everyone gathered around their amma to receive blessings.
She missed her amma dearly after moving to another country.
