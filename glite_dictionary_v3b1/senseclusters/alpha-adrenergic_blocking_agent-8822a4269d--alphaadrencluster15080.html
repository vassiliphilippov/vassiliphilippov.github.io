alpha-adrenergic blocking agent

noun
drug that blocks alpha-adrenergic receptors
5.1 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.fə æd.rə.ˈne.r.dʒɪk ˈblɑː.kɪŋ ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (AmE), /ˈæl.fə æd.rə.ˈneə.dʒɪk ˈblɒk.ɪŋ ˈeɪ.dʒənt/ (BrE)
Any of a class of drugs that block alpha-adrenergic receptors, used to treat conditions such as benign prostatic hyperplasia by relaxing the muscles of the prostate and bladder.
