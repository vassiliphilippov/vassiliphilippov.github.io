comebacker

noun
person returning to success
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.bæk.ər/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.bæk.ə/ (BrE)
a person or team that returns to success or prominence after a period of decline or poor performance

Examples

A true comebacker
After a long injury, the star player became a true comebacker by leading his team to the finals.
The company was considered a comebacker after launching a product that revived its reputation.
She earned the title of comebacker when she returned to the top of the charts after years away.

  • synonym: resurgent - becoming strong or successful again
  • synonym: underdog - a person or team expected to lose but striving for success
