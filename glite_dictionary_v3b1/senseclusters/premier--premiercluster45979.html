premier

adjective
first in rank or importance
4.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈmɪr / (AmE), / prɪˈmɪə / (BrE)
Best, most important, or first in rank or degree.

Examples

Premier league team
She was the premier scientist in her field, winning numerous awards for her research.
The company strives to offer premier customer service to distinguish itself from competitors.
This is the city's premier art gallery, attracting visitors from around the world.
