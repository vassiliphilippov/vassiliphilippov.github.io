corvina

noun
Italian red wine
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈviːnə / (AmE), / kɔːˈviːnə / (BrE)
a type of red or rosé wine made from the Corvina grape, mainly produced in northern Italy

Examples

A glass of corvina
They enjoyed a glass of corvina with their pasta at the Italian restaurant.
The sommelier recommended corvina for its light, fruity flavor and smooth finish.
During the wine tasting, she preferred the corvina over the other red wines offered.
