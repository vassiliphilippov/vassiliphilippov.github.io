Castile soap

noun
mild hard soap made from olive oil
9.5
Pronunciation: / kæˈstil soʊp / (AmE), / kaˈstiːl səʊp / (BrE)
a hard, mild soap made with olive oil and sodium hydroxide, originally from the Castile region of Spain
