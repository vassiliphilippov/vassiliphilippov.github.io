tie

verb
connect musical notes with a curved line
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
(in music) to connect two or more notes of the same pitch by a curved line, indicating they should be played as one continuous note.

Examples

To tie two notes
In this measure, you should tie the two half notes so they are sustained.
She carefully marked where to tie the notes in each phrase.
The composer instructed the pianist to tie the final two notes for a smooth finish.
