industriousness

noun
diligence; quality of working hard
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of working hard and steadily; diligence and perseverance in completing tasks

Examples

Hard industriousness
Her industriousness impressed her supervisors and peers alike.
The team's industriousness was evident in the speed with which they finished the project.
Through sheer industriousness, she overcame the obstacles in her career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligence - careful and persistent work or effort
  • antonym: laziness - the quality of being unwilling to work or use energy
