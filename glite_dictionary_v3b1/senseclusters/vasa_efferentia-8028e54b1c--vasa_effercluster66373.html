vasa efferentia

noun
tubules connecting rete testis to epididymis
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪsə ɛfəˈrɛnʃiə / (AmE), / ˈvɑːsə ˌɛfəˈrɛnʃɪə / (BrE)
the highly coiled ducts that transport sperm from the rete testis to the vas deferens, forming the head of the epididymis

Examples

The vasa efferentia ducts
Blockages in the vasa efferentia can lead to male infertility.
During dissection, the anatomy students carefully identified the vasa efferentia near the head of the epididymis.
The vasa efferentia play a crucial role in transporting sperm from the testis to the epididymis.
