lanceolate spleenwort

noun
a species of spleenwort fern
Pronunciation: / ˈlænsiəˌleɪt ˈspliːnwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈlɑːnsiəˌleɪt ˈspliːnwɜːt / (BrE)
