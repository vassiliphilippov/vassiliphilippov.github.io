emp

noun
electromagnetic pulse
Pronunciation: / iː ɛm piː / (AmE), / iː ɛm piː / (BrE)
a short, intense burst of electromagnetic energy that can damage or disrupt electronic equipment and communications

Examples

A massive emp attack
A powerful emp could knock out all the electronics in a city within seconds.
The science fiction novel described a weapon that used an emp to disable enemy defenses.
After the emp, none of the computers or phones would turn on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromagnetic pulse - a burst of electromagnetic radiation that can disrupt electronics
