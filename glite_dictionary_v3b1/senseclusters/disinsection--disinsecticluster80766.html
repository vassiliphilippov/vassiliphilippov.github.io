disinsection

noun
insect elimination process
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛkʃən / (BrE)
the process of eliminating or controlling insects, especially to maintain hygiene or prevent disease

Examples

Disinsection of planes
International flights often require disinsection to prevent the spread of insects between countries.
The hotel room underwent disinsection before new guests arrived to ensure a pest-free environment.
Proper disinsection is essential in hospitals to reduce the risk of insect-borne diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfestation - the process of removing or destroying pests such as insects or rodents
  • synonym: fumigation - the use of smoke or gas to disinfect or destroy pests
