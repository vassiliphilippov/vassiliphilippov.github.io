three-dimensional

adjective
having length, width, and depth
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌθriː.dɪˈmɛn.ʃə.nəl /; / ˌθriː.dɪˈmɛn.ʃən.əl / (AmE), / ˌθriː.dɪˈmɛn.ʃən.əl / (BrE)
having or appearing to have length, width, and depth; not flat
