blading

noun
machine blade system
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbleɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the blades of a machine, such as a turbine or fan, considered as a group or system

Examples

Turbine blading
The engineer inspected the blading for signs of wear after the test run.
Proper maintenance of the blading is essential for efficient turbine operation.
Salt in the air can cause corrosion on the blading of coastal wind turbines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotor - the rotating part of a machine, especially in turbines or motors
  • antonym: stator - the stationary part of a machine, especially in turbines or motors
