chiro

noun
informal chiropractor
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkaɪrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a chiropractor, a healthcare professional who treats problems of the bones and muscles, especially the spine
