slothfully

adverb
lazily or slowly
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsloʊθfəli / (AmE), / ˈsləʊθfəli / (BrE)
in a lazy, slow, or unwilling manner

Examples

To slothfully move
He slothfully dragged himself out of bed on Sunday morning.
The students slothfully completed their assignments just before the deadline.
She slothfully wandered around the house, doing nothing in particular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetically - in a way that shows a lot of energy or enthusiasm
  • synonym: lazily - in a way that shows a lack of effort or energy
