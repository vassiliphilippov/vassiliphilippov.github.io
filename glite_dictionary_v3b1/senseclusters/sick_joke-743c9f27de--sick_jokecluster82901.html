sick joke

noun
offensive or tasteless joke
Pronunciation: / sɪk doʊk / (AmE), / sɪk dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a joke that is considered offensive or in very bad taste, often because it deals with disturbing or taboo subjects

Examples

A sick joke
He was criticized for telling a sick joke at the funeral.
She has no patience for anyone who laughs at a sick joke.
Sometimes people use sick jokes to shock an audience and get attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offensive joke - a joke that offends people due to its content
  • antonym: practical joke - a trick played on someone for humorous effect
