loop

noun
repeated musical sample
3.9
Pronunciation: /luːp/ (AmE), /luːp/ (BrE)
A short recorded piece of sound or music that is repeated continuously or several times to form a rhythmic or melodic pattern, often used in music production and performance.

Examples

A drum loop
The producer added a catchy drum loop to the track, then layered synths over it.
She built the beat from several short loops and arranged them into a longer groove.
During the performance, the guitarist used a pedal to record and repeat a melodic loop live for harmony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riff - a repeated musical phrase or pattern often used as a motif in a song.
  • synonym: sample - a short recorded sound used in music production, which can be repeated or altered.
