hooding

noun
covering a bird's head
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhʊd.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhʊd.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of placing a hood over a bird of prey's head to keep it calm, especially in falconry

Examples

Proper hooding is essential for safely transporting birds of prey to new locations.
During the demonstration, the trainer explained the importance of hooding in falconry.
