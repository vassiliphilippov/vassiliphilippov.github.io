muzzle velocity

noun
speed of projectile leaving gun
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəl vəˈlɑsəti / (AmE), / ˈmʌzəl vəˈlɒsəti / (BrE)
the speed of a projectile as it leaves the muzzle of a firearm

Examples

Muzzle velocity of bullet
Ballistics experts need to know the muzzle velocity to calculate a bullet's trajectory.
Old cannons had a much lower muzzle velocity compared to modern artillery.
The muzzle velocity of the new rifle exceeds 900 meters per second.
