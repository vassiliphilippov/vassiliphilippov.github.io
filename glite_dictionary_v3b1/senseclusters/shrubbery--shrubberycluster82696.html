shrubbery

noun
area planted with shrubs
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃrʌbəri / (AmE), / ˈʃrʌbəri / (BrE)
an area in a garden or park where shrubs are grown or planted together, often for decoration

Examples

Dense shrubbery area
A family of birds had made their home in the dense shrubbery behind the fence.
He spent the afternoon trimming the shrubbery at the edge of the garden path.
The old mansion was surrounded by an overgrown shrubbery that blocked the windows.
Children were hiding in the shrubbery during their game of hide and seek.
The gardener trimmed the shrubbery to keep it neat and attractive.
We walked past the shrubbery at the front of the mansion.
