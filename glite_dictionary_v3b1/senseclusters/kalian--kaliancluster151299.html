kalian

noun
oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco
Pronunciation: / ˈkæliˌɑːn / (AmE), / ˈkælɪən / (BrE)
an oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco, with a long flexible tube and a container where smoke is cooled by passing through water
