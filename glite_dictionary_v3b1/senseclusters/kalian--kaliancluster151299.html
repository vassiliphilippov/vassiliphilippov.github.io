kalian

noun
oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco
Pronunciation: / ˈkæliˌɑːn / (AmE), / ˈkælɪən / (BrE)
an oriental water pipe for smoking tobacco, with a long flexible tube and a container where smoke is cooled by passing through water

Examples

A kalian pipe
At the market, we watched craftsmen assemble a traditional kalian for tourists.
She enjoyed smoking sweetly flavored tobacco from a kalian during her trip to Central Asia.
The ornate kalian stood as a centerpiece on the table at the cafe.
