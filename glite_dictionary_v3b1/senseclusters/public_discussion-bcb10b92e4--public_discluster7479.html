public discussion

noun
open debate about public issues
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
an open and free exchange of views or debate about an issue of public interest

Examples

Public discussion on issues
The mayor opened the floor to public discussion on the new zoning regulations.
The proposed policy changes have sparked a lively public discussion among citizens and experts.
We encourage public discussion to ensure transparency in government decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
  • antonym: private discussion - a discussion not open to the general public
