patrol

noun
regular security activity in an area
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊl / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the act of regularly watching or traveling through an area to maintain order or security

Examples

Morning patrol
Forest rangers conduct a patrol each morning to check for signs of fire.
He volunteered for nightly patrol to help keep the neighborhood safe.
The security team made a patrol of the building every two hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guard - to watch over for protection or security
