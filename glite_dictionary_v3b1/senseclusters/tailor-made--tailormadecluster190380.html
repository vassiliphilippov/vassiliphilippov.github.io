Examples

Synonyms

  • tailored
    3.7 0.05/m
    tailored describes clothing made to fit a person's measurements, while tailor-made refers to any item made to suit particular needs.
  • bespoke
    6.9 0.55/m
    bespoke is used for high quality clothing made to measure in British English, while tailor-made is neutral and used for many items.
  • custom-made
    3.8 1.35/m
    custom-made is an ordinary phrase for items made for a customer, while tailor-made often implies an exact fit to specific needs.
  • made-to-order
    3.8 0.38/m
    made-to-order items are produced after a customer asks for them, while tailor-made stresses a close fit to an individual's measurements.
  • to order
    3.8 1.30/m
    to order means made according to a customer's instructions, while tailor-made highlights a precise personal fit for measurements.
  • custom
    4.4 14/m
    custom describes goods made to a customer's wishes or needs, while tailor-made stresses careful fit to measurements.
  • personalized
    4.1 0.04/m
    personalized is changed to match a person's taste or name, while tailor-made means made to fit body measurements or needs.
  • custom-built
    4.1 0.63/m
    custom-built usually refers to large objects like houses or machines built to order, while tailor-made focuses on fitted garments or small items.
  • individualized
    4.1 3.12/m
    individualized is adjusted to suit a person's special needs, while tailor-made usually means made to exact physical measurements.
