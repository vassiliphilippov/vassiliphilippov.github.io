applesauce

noun
sweet purée of cooked apples
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əlˌsɔs / (AmE), / ˈap.əl.sɔːs / (BrE)
A sweetened purée made from cooked apples, often flavored with sugar and spices.

Examples

Homemade applesauce
Homemade applesauce tastes much better than the store-bought kind.
She served pork chops with a side of applesauce.
You can use applesauce as a substitute for oil in many baking recipes.
