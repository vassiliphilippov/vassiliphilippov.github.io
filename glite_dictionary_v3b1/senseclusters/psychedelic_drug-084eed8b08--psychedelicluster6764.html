psychedelic drug

noun
hallucinogenic drug
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪk drʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəˈdɛlɪk drʌɡ / (BrE)
a drug that causes hallucinations and strong changes in how you see, feel, or think

Researchers are studying the effects of psychedelic drugs on depression and anxiety.
The use of psychedelic drugs has influenced many artists and musicians over the decades.
