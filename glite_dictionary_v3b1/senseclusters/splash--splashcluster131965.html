splash

noun
small amount of liquid or moisture
3.4
Pronunciation: / splæʃ / (AmE), / splæʃ / (BrE)
A small quantity of a liquid or moist substance, often added to something else.

Examples

A splash of milk
Could you add a splash of milk to my coffee, please?
The recipe calls for just a splash of vanilla extract.
There was a splash of mud on her new shoes after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dash - a small amount of liquid added to a drink or food
  • degree lower: drop - a very small round amount of liquid
