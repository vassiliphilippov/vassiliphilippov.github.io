unbreathed

adjective
not breathing
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbriːðd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbriːðd / (BrE)
not breathing or showing any signs of life, especially referring to a person or animal.

Examples

Unbreathed body
The rescuers found the puppy unbreathed after the accident, but quickly began CPR.
She stared in shock at the unbreathed figure lying motionless on the ground.
The doctor checked for a pulse on the unbreathed patient before starting emergency treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lifeless - showing no signs of life
