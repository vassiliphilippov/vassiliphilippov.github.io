galette

noun
buckwheat pancake
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ɡaˈlɛt / (BrE)
a thin, round, savory pancake made from buckwheat flour, especially popular in Brittany, France, and served with various fillings

Examples

Buckwheat galette pancake
She enjoyed a traditional Breton galette filled with mushrooms and cheese for dinner.
The restaurant specializes in savory galettes made with fresh local ingredients.
For a taste of France, try making a buckwheat galette at home with your favorite fillings.
