noun

An oil that easily evaporates at room temperature and has the characteristic odor or flavor of the plant from which it is derived; used in perfumes and flavorings.

Examples

volatile oil from plants
Citrus fruits are rich in volatile oil that gives them their distinctive aroma.
Peppermint is a popular source of volatile oil used in candies and chewing gum.
The laboratory extracted volatile oil from lavender for use in aromatherapy products.
