bootboy

noun
boy who cleans boots
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbuːt.bɔɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbuːt.bɔɪ/ (BrE)
a boy or young man employed to clean and care for boots, especially in a household or military setting

Examples

Household bootboy

In the grand house, the bootboy was responsible for all the footwear.
