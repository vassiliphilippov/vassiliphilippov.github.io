quantitative chemical analysis

noun
determining amounts of chemical components
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɑn.t̬əˌteɪ.t̬ɪv ˌkɛm.ɪ.kəl əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌkwɒn.tɪˌteɪ.tɪv ˌkɛm.ɪ.kəl əˈnæl.ə.sɪs / (BrE)
the process of determining the amount or concentration of each element or compound present in a substance through chemical methods

Examples
