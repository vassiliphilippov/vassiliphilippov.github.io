schizo

x
prefix meaning split
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪtsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈskɪtsəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'split' or 'division', used in technical or academic terms to indicate separation or fragmentation

Examples

Schizo-phrenia term
The term schizo-phrenia refers to a mental disorder characterized by a split between thought and emotion.
In literary theory, schizo-analysis explores fragmented identities and desires.
The prefix schizo- is often used in academic discussions about division or separation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: schism - a split or division between strongly opposed parties
