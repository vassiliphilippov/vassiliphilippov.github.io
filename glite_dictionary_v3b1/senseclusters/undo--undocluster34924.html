undo

verb
loosen or unfasten something tied
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdu / (AmE), / ʌnˈduː / (BrE)
to loosen, unfasten, or unwrap something that has been tied, fastened, or covered

Examples

To undo a knot
Don't forget to undo your seatbelt before getting out of the car.
It took him a few minutes to undo the buttons on his coat.
She tried to undo the knot in the ribbon but it was too tight.
Could you undo the package so I can see what’s inside?
He struggled to undo the tightly wrapped birthday present.
She carefully undid the ribbon and opened the box.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fasten - to attach or secure something
  • synonym: loosen - to make something less tight or firmly fixed
  • synonym: open - to make something no longer closed, especially a container or wrap
  • synonym: unwrap - to remove paper or covering from something
  • antonym: wrap - to cover something by winding or folding something round it
