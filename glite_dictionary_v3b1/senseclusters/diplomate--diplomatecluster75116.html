diplomate

noun
certified medical specialist
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpləˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪpləʊmeɪt / (BrE)
A medical specialist whose qualifications and competence have been certified by a diploma granted by an appropriate professional board or association.

Examples

A diplomate doctor
After years of training, Dr. Smith became a diplomate of the American Board of Cardiology.
Being a board-certified diplomate means meeting rigorous professional standards.
The panel included several diplomates in different medical fields to review the new treatment protocols.
