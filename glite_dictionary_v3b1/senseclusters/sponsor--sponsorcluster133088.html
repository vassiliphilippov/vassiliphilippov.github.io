sponsor

verb
to officially support or back a proposal or project
5.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑnsər / (AmE), / ˈspɒnsə / (BrE)
to support or take responsibility for something or someone, often by providing money or presenting an idea, project, or person

Examples

To sponsor an event

Several major brands sponsor the annual music festival.
She decided to sponsor a child in need through a charitable organization.
The company agreed to sponsor the local football team for the next season.
