opercular

adjective
covering or lid-like
Pronunciation: / oʊˈpɜːrkjələr / (AmE), / əʊˈpɜːkjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a covering or lid, especially in biological contexts

Examples

Opercular covering
The opercular structure in snails helps protect their soft bodies from predators.
Scientists studied the opercular bones to understand how the fish's gills are shielded.
The plant's opercular cap falls off when the seeds are ready to disperse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: covering - something that covers or protects
  • synonym: lid - a cover for the top of a container
