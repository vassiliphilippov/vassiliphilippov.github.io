one-ten-thousandth

noun
1 in 10,000; 0.0001 fraction
Pronunciation: / wʌn tɛn θaʊzəndθ / (AmE), / wʌn tɛn ˈθaʊzəndθ / (BrE)
One part out of ten thousand equal parts; the fraction 1⁄10,000 or 0.0001.

Examples
