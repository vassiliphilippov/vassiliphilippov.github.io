creeper

noun
Minecraft hostile, exploding mob
8.1 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8krip\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8kri\u02d0p\u0259 / (BrE)
(in the video game Minecraft) a hostile, green, silent creature that approaches players and explodes, causing damage to terrain and structures.

Examples

Minecraft creeper explodes
In Minecraft, a creeper sneaks up and explodes near the base.
She screamed when a creeper appeared behind the village and blew a hole in the wall.
