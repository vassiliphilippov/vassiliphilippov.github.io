Synonyms

  • flexure
    8.7 0.00/m
    flexure refers to bending of a joint in technical use, while flexion means the act or position of that bend.
  • flection
    9.9 0.00/m
    flection covers bending of joints or objects in general, while flexion focuses on a limb or joint and its bent position.
  • bending
    2.1 2.18/m
    bending means making something curve or change shape in general, while flexion is the bending of a limb or joint.
