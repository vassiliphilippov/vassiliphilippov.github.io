flexion

noun
bending of a joint
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛkʃən /; / ˈflɛk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈflɛkʃən /; / ˈflɛk.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of bending a limb or joint, or the position in which it is bent

Examples
