teasing

adjective
arousing sexual desire without intent to satisfy
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtiːzɪŋ / (BrE)
Behaving in a way that arouses sexual desire without intending to satisfy it.

Examples

Teasing behavior
His teasing tone made her blush, though she knew he wasn't serious.
She gave him a teasing glance before looking away.
The ad featured a teasing smile that hinted at flirtation.
