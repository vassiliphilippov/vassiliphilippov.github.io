threepenny

adjective
of little value or trivial importance
Pronunciation: / ˈθriːˌpɛni / (AmE), / ˈθriːpəni / (BrE)
Of little value; cheap or trivial.

Examples

Threepenny story
He dismissed their threepenny complaints without a second thought.
She wore a threepenny trinket she had found at the market.
The play felt like a threepenny imitation of classic works.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
  • antonym: valuable - worth a lot of money or importance
  • degree higher: worthless - having no value or use
