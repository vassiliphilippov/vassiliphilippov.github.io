fluidized bed boiler

noun
boiler with suspended burning fuel bed
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfluː.ɪd.aɪzd bɛd ˈbɔɪ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈfluː.ɪd.aɪzd bɛd ˈbɔɪ.lə / (BrE)
a type of boiler in which fuel is burned while suspended in a fluidized bed of solid particles, improving combustion efficiency and reducing emissions

Examples

A fluidized bed boiler
Engineers prefer a fluidized bed boiler for its high efficiency and fuel flexibility.
Maintaining a fluidized bed boiler requires specialized knowledge of combustion dynamics.
The new power plant installed a fluidized bed boiler to meet stricter emission regulations.
