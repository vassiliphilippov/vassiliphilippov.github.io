At exactly the appointed or expected time; on time.

Examples

Arrive punctually at work
If you finish your work punctually, you will have more free time.
She always makes sure to arrive punctually for her appointments.
The train departed punctually at 9 a.m., exactly as scheduled.

Synonyms

  • on time
    1.0 12/m
    on time is the common everyday phrase for being at the expected time, while punctually sounds more formal and precise.
  • right on time
    1.2 0.87/m
    right on time is a casual way to say you meet a schedule, while punctually sounds more formal and precise.
  • on the dot
    2.8 0.29/m
    on the dot means exactly at the minute, while punctually means arriving on time but not always to the exact minute.
