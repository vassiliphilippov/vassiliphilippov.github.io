whirling dervish

noun
Sufi dancer performing spinning ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrlɪŋ ˈdɜrvɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːlɪŋ ˈdɜːvɪʃ / (BrE)
a member of the Mevlevi Sufi order whose religious practice includes spinning in circles as a form of meditative dance

Examples

A whirling dervish dance
During the festival, a whirling dervish captivated the audience with his mesmerizing dance.
She likened his energetic movement across the stage to that of a whirling dervish.
The ceremony featured several whirling dervishes performing in their traditional flowing garments.
