insupportable

adjective
unbearable; intolerable
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnsəˈpɔrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnsəˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
too difficult, unpleasant, or extreme to be endured or accepted

Examples

Insupportable pain
The heat in the small room was insupportable by midday.
She found the constant noise from the construction site insupportable after a few weeks.
His arrogance became insupportable to everyone in the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bearable - able to be endured or tolerated
  • synonym: intolerable - impossible to accept or endure
  • synonym: unbearable - too painful or difficult to be endured
