tump

noun
small hill or mound
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʌmp / (AmE), / tʌmp / (BrE)
a small hill, mound, or clump, especially used in regional or dialectal English

Examples

A grassy tump
The children climbed to the top of the grassy tump to get a better view of the valley.
A lone tree grew on the tump in the middle of the field.
They sat on a tump and watched the sunset over the hills.
