Michelangelo Buonarroti

proper noun
Italian Renaissance artist and sculptor
6.0
Pronunciation: /maɪ.kəl.ˈæn.dʒə.loʊ bwoʊ.nə.ˈroʊ.tiː/ /; / /mɪ.kəl.ˈæn.dʒə.loʊ bwoʊ.nə.ˈroʊ.tiː/ (AmE), /maɪ.kəl.ˈæn.dʒə.ləʊ bwəʊ.nə.ˈrəʊ.tiː/ /; / /mɪ.kəl.ˈæn.dʒə.ləʊ bwəʊ.nə.ˈrəʊ.tiː/ (BrE)
(1475–1564) An Italian sculptor, painter, and architect, renowned as one of the greatest figures of the Renaissance for works such as the Sistine Chapel ceiling and the statue of David.
