chimbley

noun
dialect word for chimney
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪmbli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪmbli / (BrE)
a nonstandard or dialectal word for a chimney, a structure that carries smoke from a fire out of a building

Examples

Old chimbley stone
The old cottage had a stone chimbley that was always covered in soot.
Grandpa used to tell stories by the warm fire near the chimbley.
Smoke curled lazily from the chimbley on cold winter mornings.
