demy

noun
Magdalen College scholar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmi / (AmE), / ˈdɛmi / (BrE)
a scholar or student at Magdalen College, Oxford, who holds a demyship, historically a type of half-fellowship.

Examples

Oxford demy
She was named a demy at Magdalen College after excelling in her entrance exams.
As a demy, he received financial support to continue his studies at Oxford.
The annual dinner honored every current demy for their academic achievements.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fellow - a senior member of a college with certain privileges
  • synonym: scholar - a student who receives a scholarship for academic achievement
