blob

noun
a shapeless mass or lump
3.5 3.26/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘b / (AmE), / blÉ’b / (BrE)
a shapeless, indistinct mass or lump, often of a soft or thick substance

Examples

A blob of paint
A blob of paint landed on the floor, staining the wood.
From far away, the island looked like a dark blob on the horizon.
We watched the blob of jelly slowly spread across the plate.

Synonyms

  • glob
    4.8 0.74/m
    a glob is a small, often sticky drop of material that oozes, while a blob is a more shapeless, thicker mass.
  • glop
    9.0 0.20/m
    glop is a wet, messy, and usually unpleasant goo, while a blob is any shapeless soft mass not always disgusting.
  • lump
    2.0 5.58/m
    a lump is a firmer piece or mass you can feel or hold, while a blob is softer and more shapeless.

Related Terms
