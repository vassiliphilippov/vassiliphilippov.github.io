traiteur

noun
French food caterer
Pronunciation: / tʁɛ.tœʁ / (AmE), / tʁɛ.tœʁ / (BrE)
a person or business that prepares and sells ready-to-eat food, especially in the style of French cuisine

Examples

Local traiteur service
We ordered a selection of dishes from the local traiteur for our dinner party.
The traiteur offered a variety of ready-to-eat French specialties in the shop window.
For the picnic, they picked up salads and pastries from a nearby traiteur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caterer - a person or company providing food and drink for events
  • synonym: delicatessen - a shop selling cooked meats, cheeses, and prepared foods
