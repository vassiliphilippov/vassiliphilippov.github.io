thermal printer

noun
printer using heat-sensitive paper
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜr.məl ˈprɪn.tər / (AmE), / ˈθɜː.məl ˈprɪn.tə / (BrE)
A printer that produces printed images or text by applying heat to specially coated, heat-sensitive paper.

Examples

A thermal printer device
A thermal printer is often found in point-of-sale systems and portable label makers.
If the special paper runs out, the thermal printer won’t be able to print any more tickets.
Our receipt was printed using a thermal printer, which explained the slick, glossy paper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dot matrix printer - printer using pins to create images on paper
  • antonym: inkjet printer - printer spraying ink onto paper
  • antonym: laser printer - printer using laser and toner to produce images
