subtheme

noun
customized child theme
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌθiːm / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌθiːm / (BrE)
a website or software theme that is derived from a parent theme and customizes or overrides its design and features

Examples

Website subtheme
The designer built a subtheme to give the website a unique look while keeping the main structure.
By using a subtheme, you can update the parent theme without losing your custom changes.
Developers often create a subtheme to experiment with new layouts and color schemes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parent theme - the original theme from which a subtheme is derived
  • synonym: theme - the overall design and appearance of a website or software
