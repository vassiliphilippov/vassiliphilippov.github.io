piddle

verb
waste time idly
7.4 6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪd.əl / (AmE), / ˈpɪd.əl / (BrE)
to spend time idly or aimlessly, often without accomplishing anything important

Examples

To piddle around
He tends to piddle away his mornings doing nothing in particular.
Instead of making progress, we just piddled most of the afternoon.
Stop piddling around and get to work on your assignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawdle - to waste time or move slowly
  • synonym: fritter - to waste time, money, or energy on trifling matters
  • antonym: rush - to do something with great speed, opposite of wasting time
