megakaryocyte

noun
large bone marrow cell producing platelets
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡəˈkæri.oʊˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡəˈkæri.əˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a large cell in the bone marrow that produces platelets, which are essential for blood clotting

Examples
