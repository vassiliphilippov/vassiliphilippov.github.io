surfriding

noun
riding waves on a surfboard
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːfˌraɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfˌraɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport of riding a surfboard toward the shore on the crest of a wave; surfing

Examples

To surfride a wave
Early mornings are best for surfriding because the waves are usually calmer and more consistent.
Many beach towns host annual surfriding competitions that attract athletes from around the world.
She took lessons to improve her surfriding skills during their Hawaiian vacation.
