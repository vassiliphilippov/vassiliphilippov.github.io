automatic

adjective
operating without manual control

(of a device or process) operating with minimal human intervention; able to function independently, without the need for manual control.

Examples

Automatic washing machine
Many cars now come with automatic transmissions, making them easier to drive.
The doors to the building are automatic and open when someone approaches.
The factory uses automatic machines to increase productivity and reduce errors.

Synonyms

  • automated
    4.0 5.96/m
    automated is used for systems run by machines or computers, while automatic describes things that work without human control.
  • machine-controlled
    4.8 0.01/m
    machine-controlled means managed by machines in a system, while automatic refers to devices that operate without human control.
  • machine-driven
    4.2 0.01/m
    machine-driven describes something powered or guided by machines, while automatic emphasizes working independently without human control.
  • self-activating
    6.7 0.00/m
    self-activating starts to work by itself at a set moment, while automatic means it runs without human control generally.
