swoop

noun
a rapid, sudden attack or raid
Pronunciation: / swuːp / (AmE), / swuːp / (BrE)
a sudden and swift attack or raid, especially by police or military forces

Examples

A police swoop
In a dramatic swoop, the authorities arrested ten suspects involved in the robbery.
The army launched a surprise swoop on the enemy camp at dawn.
The police organized a swoop on the illegal gambling ring early this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raid - a sudden attack, typically a surprise one, by police or military forces
