a prefix meaning 'child' or 'relating to children', used in words like 'pediatric' and 'paediatric'

Examples

Ped-iatric doctor
The prefix ped- appears in many medical terms related to children.
Words like 'pediatrician' and 'pedagogy' both use the ped- prefix, though with different meanings.
When you see ped- at the start of a word, it often refers to children or childhood.
