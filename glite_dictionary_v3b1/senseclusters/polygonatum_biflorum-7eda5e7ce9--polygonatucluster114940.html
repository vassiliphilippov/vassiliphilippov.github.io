Polygonatum biflorum

noun
species of herbaceous plant (Solomon's seal)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈgɑːnətəm baɪˈflɔːrəm / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈɡeɪnətəm baɪˈflɔːrəm / (BrE)
