Synonyms

  • substitutable
    5.9 0.09/m
    substitutable is used when one thing can replace another without changing function, while interchangeable is a general, neutral word for that idea.
  • exchangeable
    4.0 0.18/m
    exchangeable suggests items can be traded or swapped in value or use, while interchangeable focuses on equal function or meaning.
  • replaceable
    3.3 0.97/m
    replaceable often means something can be put in place of another, while interchangeable stresses equal function without losing suitability.
  • fungible
    9.2 0.41/m
    fungible describes goods that legally count as identical for trade or debt, while interchangeable is a broader general term.
  • swappable
    3.9 0.04/m
    swappable is a more casual or technical word for parts you can exchange, while interchangeable is the wider, standard term.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replaceable - able to be replaced by something else
