criminal offense

noun
an act punishable as a crime
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns /; / ˈkrɪmɪnl əˈfɛns / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəl əˈfɛns /; / ˈkrɪmɪnl əˈfɛns / (BrE)
An act that is forbidden by law and punishable as a crime; usually regarded as morally wrong or harmful to society.

The new law aims to make cyberbullying a criminal offence.
