criminal offense

noun
an act punishable as a crime
1.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl əˈfens/ /; / /ˈkrɪm.ən.əl əˈfens/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪm.ɪ.nəl əˈfens/ /; / /ˈkrɪm.ən.əl əˈfens/ (BrE)
