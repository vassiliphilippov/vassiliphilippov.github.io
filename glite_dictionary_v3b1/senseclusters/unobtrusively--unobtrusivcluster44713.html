unobtrusively

adverb
in a manner not attracting attention
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əbˈtru.sɪv.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əbˈtruː.sɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that does not attract attention or cause disruption.

Examples

To unobtrusively enter
He entered the meeting unobtrusively, hoping not to interrupt the ongoing discussion.
She listened unobtrusively from the back of the classroom, taking detailed notes.
The security cameras were installed unobtrusively throughout the building.
