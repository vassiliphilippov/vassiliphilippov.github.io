main event

noun
most important or central event
Pronunciation: / meɪn ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / meɪn ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
the most important or central event in a series of events, especially in entertainment, sports, or public gatherings

Examples

The main event starts

The crowd eagerly awaited the main event of the wrestling match.
Tickets for the main event sold out in just a few hours.
