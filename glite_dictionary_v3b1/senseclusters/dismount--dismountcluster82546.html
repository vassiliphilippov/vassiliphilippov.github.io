Synonyms

  • unmount
    5.8 0.00/m
    unmount is used to get off a riding animal or vehicle, while dismount is the general term for leaving.
  • get off
    2.5 5.31/m
    get off is the everyday phrasal verb for leaving vehicles or animals, while dismount sounds more formal and specific.
  • alight
    4.1 0.32/m
    alight is a more formal word used when stepping down from a vehicle or horse, while dismount is common.
  • get down
    3.5 6.50/m
    get down means move down or leave a horse or similar animal, while dismount is the standard riding term.
