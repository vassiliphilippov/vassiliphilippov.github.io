sala

noun
large hall or room
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈsɑːlə / (BrE)
a large room or hall, especially in a Spanish or Italian building, used for gatherings or events

Examples

Guests gathered in the spacious sala before the concert began.
The hotel’s sala offered a beautiful view of the city skyline.
