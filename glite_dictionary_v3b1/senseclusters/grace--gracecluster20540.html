grace

noun
polite and considerate behavior
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪs / (AmE), / ɡreɪs / (BrE)
elegance, beauty, or politeness in movement, behavior, or expression

Examples

Move with grace

He accepted the disappointing news with good grace.
He accepted the award with grace and humility.
The dancer moved with such grace that the audience was mesmerized.
The poem captured the grace of the swan gliding across the lake.
