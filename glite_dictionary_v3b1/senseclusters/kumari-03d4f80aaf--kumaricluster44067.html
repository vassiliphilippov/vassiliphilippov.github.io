Kumari

noun
title for unmarried woman
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈmɑːri / (AmE), / kuːˈmɑːri / (BrE)
a title or form of address for an unmarried woman, especially in parts of South Asia

Examples

Kumari Sharma
At the ceremony, they respectfully called her Kumari before announcing her full name.
In some regions, the title Kumari is used to address young, unmarried women.
She was introduced as Kumari Sharma during the formal event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Miss - a title for an unmarried woman
  • antonym: Mrs - a title for a married woman
