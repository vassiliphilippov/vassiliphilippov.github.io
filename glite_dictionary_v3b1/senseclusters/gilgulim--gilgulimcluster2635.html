gilgulim

noun
reincarnated souls
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɪlˈɡuːlɪm / (AmE), / ɡɪlˈɡuːlɪm / (BrE)
in Jewish mysticism, the plural of 'gilgul', referring to the cycles or instances of reincarnation of souls

Examples

The gilgulim of souls
The Kabbalistic tradition teaches about the journey of souls through multiple gilgulim.
Some believe that unresolved actions in one life affect the next gilgulim of the soul.
Stories of gilgulim are often discussed in Jewish mystical texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reincarnation - the belief that souls are reborn in new bodies after death
