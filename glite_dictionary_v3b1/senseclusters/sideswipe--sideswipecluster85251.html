sideswipe

verb
strike from the side
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌswaɪp / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌswaɪp / (BrE)
to strike or hit someone or something from the side, especially while moving past

Examples

To sideswipe a vehicle
He accidentally sideswiped the cyclist as he turned the corner too sharply.
The bus sideswiped a parked car, leaving a long scratch down its length.
Their car was sideswiped by a truck on the highway, but fortunately no one was hurt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swipe - to strike or hit with a sweeping motion
