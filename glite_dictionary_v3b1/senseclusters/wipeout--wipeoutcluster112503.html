wipeout

noun
total destruction; complete elimination
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪpˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪpˌaʊt / (BrE)
an event that causes the total destruction or elimination of something

Examples

Total wipeout
After the hurricane, the village was nothing short of a wipeout.
The financial crash led to a wipeout of many small businesses in the area.
The team's defeat in the championship was a complete wipeout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annihilation - complete destruction or obliteration
  • synonym: devastation - severe and widespread destruction or damage
  • antonym: survival - the state of continuing to exist
