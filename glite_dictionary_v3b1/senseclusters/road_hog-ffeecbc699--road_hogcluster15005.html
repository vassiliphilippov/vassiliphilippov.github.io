road hog

noun
driver who obstructs others
Pronunciation: / roʊd hɑɡ /; / ˈroʊd hɑg / (AmE), / rəʊd hɒɡ /; / ˈrəʊd hɒg / (BrE)
a driver who selfishly takes up more than their fair share of the road, making it difficult for other vehicles

Examples

A road hog driver
Please don’t be a road hog; let other cars merge ahead of you.
That road hog nearly ran me off the highway this morning.
The police pulled over a road hog who was blocking both lanes.
Don’t be a road hog; let others merge!
My dad called the guy who cut him off a road hog during our road trip.
The highway was jammed because of a road hog blocking both lanes.
