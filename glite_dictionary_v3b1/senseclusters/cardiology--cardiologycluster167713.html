cardiology

noun
medical study of the heart
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑr.diˈɑ.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌkɑː.diˈɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine that deals with the study, diagnosis, and treatment of the heart and its diseases.

Examples

Cardiology department
Advances in cardiology have greatly improved treatments for heart disease.

The conference featured international experts in cardiology who discussed new research findings.
