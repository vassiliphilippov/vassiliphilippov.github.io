Calla

noun
genus of aquatic plant (water arum)
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkæl.ə/ (AmE), /ˈkæl.ə/ (BrE)
A genus of perennial, aquatic or semi-aquatic plants in the arum family, Araceae, with a single species commonly known as the water arum.

Examples
