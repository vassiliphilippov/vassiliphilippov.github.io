Calla

noun
genus of aquatic plant (water arum)
Pronunciation: / ˈkæ.lə / (AmE), / ˈka.lə / (BrE)
A genus of perennial, aquatic or semi-aquatic plants in the arum family, Araceae, with a single species commonly known as the water arum.

Examples

A Calla flower
A botanist pointed out a cluster of Calla growing beside the pond.
The Calla genus is known for its striking white spathes and wetland habitat.
The Calla is often confused with calla lilies, but they belong to different genera.
