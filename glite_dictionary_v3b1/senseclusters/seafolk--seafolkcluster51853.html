seafolk

noun
sea-dwelling creatures
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌfoʊk / (AmE), / ˈsiːfəʊk / (BrE)
all animals and other living creatures that inhabit the sea, considered as a group

Examples

Deep ocean seafolk
The mysterious seafolk of the deep ocean remain largely unknown to scientists.
Legends often speak of seafolk who protect the coral reefs from harm.
Children were fascinated by the colorful seafolk swimming in the aquarium.
