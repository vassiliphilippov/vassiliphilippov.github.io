crazyweed

noun
toxic leguminous plant of western North America
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪziˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈkreɪziˌwiːd / (BrE)
any of several leguminous plants native to western North America that can cause locoism (nervous disorder) in livestock when eaten

Examples

Crazyweed plant
Horses affected by crazyweed show symptoms of nervousness and abnormal behavior.
Scientists are researching methods to control crazyweed and prevent livestock poisoning.
The rancher lost several cattle after they grazed on crazyweed in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loco-weed - another term for toxic plants causing locoism in livestock
