jojoba

noun
oil-producing shrub
6.2
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈhoʊbə / (AmE), / həˈhəʊbə / (BrE)
a desert shrub whose seeds produce oil commonly used in cosmetics and skin care products

Examples

Jojoba plant
Many natural lotions include jojoba for its moisturizing properties.
Farmers in arid regions often grow jojoba because it thrives in dry climates.
The face cream is enriched with jojoba to help soften your skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argan - a tree whose kernels produce oil used in skin and hair care
  • synonym: shea - a tree whose nuts produce a butter used in cosmetics
