railcar

noun
vehicle for running on railroad tracks
3.7 0.28/m

A railway vehicle with wheels, designed to run on railroad tracks, often used to transport freight or passengers.

Examples

A railcar on tracks
A single railcar can transport dozens of containers across the country.
Each railcar in the train carried a different type of cargo.
The engineer inspected the railcar before allowing passengers to board.
