tanier

adjective
more tan in color
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtæniər / (AmE), / ˈtæniə / (BrE)
having a more tan color or appearance than something else.

Examples

A tanier skin
After a week at the beach, she was noticeably tanier than before.
His arms looked tanier than his face after working outside all summer.
The new paint color is tanier than the old one, giving the room a warmer feel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paler - having a lighter or less tan color
  • synonym: tanner - having a more tan color or appearance
