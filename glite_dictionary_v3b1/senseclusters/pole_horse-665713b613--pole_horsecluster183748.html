pole horse

noun
draft horse next to the vehicle's pole
Pronunciation: / poʊl hɔrs / (AmE), / pəʊl hɔːs / (BrE)
A draft horse harnessed beside the shaft or pole of a vehicle, typically as part of a team pulling a wagon or carriage.

Examples

Draft pole horse
During the parade, the pole horse maintained a steady pace alongside the others.
He explained how to harness the pole horse properly for safety.
The farmer hitched a strong pole horse to the wagon for the day's work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheel horse - a horse harnessed nearest the wheels of a wagon or carriage
