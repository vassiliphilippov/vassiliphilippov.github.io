mucoidal

adjective
relating to or resembling mucus
9.9
Pronunciation: / mjuˈkɔɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / mjuˈkɔɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or resembling mucus; having the characteristics of mucus.

Examples

Mucoidal discharge
Some infections result in a mucoidal discharge from the affected area.
The laboratory observed a mucoidal layer forming on the bacterial colonies.
Under the microscope, the tissue appeared mucoidal in consistency.
