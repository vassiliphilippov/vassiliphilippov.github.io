revaccination

noun
repeat vaccination
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌvæksɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌvæksɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of giving a vaccine again to increase or renew immunity

Examples

To revaccinate a patient
The doctor recommended revaccination after several years to maintain protection.
Many countries require revaccination for certain diseases every ten years.
After traveling abroad, she scheduled a revaccination to ensure her immunity was up to date.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booster - an additional dose of a vaccine to maintain immunity
  • synonym: vaccination - the act of giving a vaccine to produce immunity
