furan

noun
a toxic, flammable chemical compound
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfjʊr.ən/ (AmE), /ˈfjʊə.ræn/ (BrE)
A colorless, toxic, flammable liquid with a distinctive ether-like odor, used in chemical synthesis including the production of nylon.

Examples

Furan chemical liquid
Furan is commonly used as a starting material in the manufacture of nylon and other synthetic fibers.
Due to its toxic and flammable properties, furan must be handled with extreme care in laboratories.
Researchers identified traces of furan in heated food products, raising concerns about safety.
