co-author

verb
write jointly with another
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈɔːθər / (AmE), / ˌkəʊˈɔːθə / (BrE)
to write (a book, article, etc.) jointly with one or more other people; to serve as a joint author.

Examples

To co-author a book
She co-authored a best-selling science book with her colleague.
The novel was co-authored by two writers from different countries.
We are currently co-authoring an article for a medical journal.
