submental

noun
area under chin
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
the area or space located beneath the chin, especially under the lower jaw.

Examples

The submental area
The doctor examined the submental for any signs of swelling or infection.
A lump was detected in the patient's submental during the routine checkup.
Ultrasound imaging revealed a cyst in the submental region.
