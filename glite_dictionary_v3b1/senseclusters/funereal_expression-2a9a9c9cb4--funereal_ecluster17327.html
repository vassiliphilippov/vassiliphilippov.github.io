funereal expression

noun
a very serious or sad look
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɪriəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / fjʊˈnɪəriəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a very serious or sad look, especially one suggestive of mourning or great solemnity

Examples

Funereal expression on face
Despite the festive atmosphere, his funereal expression stood out among the smiling faces.
He entered the room with a funereal expression, making everyone fall silent.
Her funereal expression revealed the depth of her sorrow at the news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful expression - a happy or positive look
  • synonym: solemn expression - a serious or earnest facial expression
