stifle individuality

verb
suppress personal expression
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪfəl ˌɪndɪvəˈdjuːəlɪti / (AmE), / ˈstaɪfəl ˌɪndɪvɪˈdjʊə.lɪ.ti / (BrE)
to suppress or restrain the unique character or self-expression of an individual or group

Examples

To stifle individuality in students
Some schools fear that uniforms stifle individuality and discourage creativity in students.
The company's strict policies may unintentionally stifle individuality among employees.
True innovation thrives in environments that do not stifle individuality but celebrate diversity of thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: encourage individuality - to support or foster personal expression
  • synonym: repress individuality - to restrain or inhibit personal expression
