cincture

noun
band worn around the waist
9.9 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪŋk.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈsɪŋk.tʃə/ (BrE)
a band of material worn around the waist, often to fasten or strengthen a skirt, trousers, or a robe

Examples

An embroidered cincture
Her dress was fitted with an embroidered cincture that accentuated her waist.
Tailors often add a cincture to garments for both function and decoration.
The monk tied his robe with a simple cincture before morning prayers.
