Cannabis sativa

noun
species of cannabis plant with psychoactive uses
Pronunciation: / ˈkænəbɪs səˈtiːvə / (AmE), / ˈkanəbɪs səˈtiːvə / (BrE)
a tall, annual plant native to Asia, with pungent-smelling leaves, cultivated for its fibers and for its psychoactive and medicinal properties
