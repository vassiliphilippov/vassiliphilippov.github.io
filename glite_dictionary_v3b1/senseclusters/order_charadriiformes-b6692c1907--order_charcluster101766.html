order Charadriiformes

noun
order of aquatic birds (shorebirds, etc.)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər kəˌreɪdrioʊˈfɔrˌmiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kəˌreɪdrɪɒˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
