interword

adjective
between words
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈwɜrd / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈwɜːd / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between words, especially in the context of language or text analysis

Examples

Interword spacing analysis
The linguist studied interword spacing to understand how people read sentences.
Accurate interword recognition is essential for effective speech-to-text software.
Typographical errors often occur in interword spaces, making text harder to process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interlinear - written or inserted between lines of text
  • antonym: intraword - occurring within a single word
