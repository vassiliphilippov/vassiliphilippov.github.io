deciduous plant

noun
plant that sheds leaves annually
7.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɪdʒuəs plænt / (AmE), / dɪˈsɪdʒuːəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that sheds its leaves annually at the end of the growing season

Examples

Deciduous plants lose leaves
Many gardens feature both evergreen and deciduous plants for year-round interest.
Maple trees are deciduous plants, turning brilliant colors before shedding their foliage.
The oak is a classic example of a deciduous plant losing its leaves every autumn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deciduous tree - a tree that sheds its leaves annually
  • antonym: evergreen plant - a plant that retains its leaves throughout the year
