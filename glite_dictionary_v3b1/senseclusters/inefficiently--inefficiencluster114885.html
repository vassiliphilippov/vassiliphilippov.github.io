inefficiently

adverb
in a manner lacking efficiency
1.7 3.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃənt.liː/ /; / /ɪn.ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃənt.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃənt.liː/ /; / /ɪn.ɪ.ˈfɪ.ʃənt.lɪ/ (BrE)
