wide wale

noun
corduroy with wide ribs
Pronunciation: / waɪd weɪl / (AmE), / waɪd weɪl / (BrE)
Corduroy fabric characterized by wide parallel ribs or ridges.

Examples

Wide wale corduroy fabric
He preferred wide wale over pinwale because it gave his trousers a more rugged look.
Interior designers often use wide wale fabrics for accent pillows and upholstery.
The jacket was made from a soft wide wale corduroy that felt luxurious to the touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corduroy - a thick cotton fabric with raised ribs
  • degree lower: pinwale - corduroy with very narrow ribs
