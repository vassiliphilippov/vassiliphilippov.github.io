hammerhead shark

noun
a shark with a hammer-shaped head
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərhɛd ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈhæməhɛd ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a large shark with a distinctive hammer-shaped head, found in warm seas worldwide; some species may be dangerous to humans

Examples

A large hammerhead shark
Some fishermen avoid areas where hammerhead sharks are commonly found.
The hammerhead shark is known for its unique head shape and excellent sense of smell.
We spotted a hammerhead shark swimming near the coral reef during our dive.
