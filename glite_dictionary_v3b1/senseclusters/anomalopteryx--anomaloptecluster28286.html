anomalopteryx

noun
a small, extinct New Zealand moa
Pronunciation: / əˌnɑːməˈlɒptəˌrɪks / (AmE), / əˌnɒməˈlɒptərɪks / (BrE)
an extinct genus of small, slender moas from New Zealand, about the size of a large turkey

Examples

The anomalopteryx bird
Fossil remains of the anomalopteryx have been found throughout New Zealand.
Researchers believe the anomalopteryx went extinct several centuries ago.
The anomalopteryx was about the size of a large turkey, much smaller than other moas.
