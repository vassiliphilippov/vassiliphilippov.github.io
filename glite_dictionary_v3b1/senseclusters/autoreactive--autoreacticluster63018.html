autoreactive

adjective
reacting to own tissues
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtoʊriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊriˈæktɪv / (BrE)
describing immune cells or responses that react against the body's own tissues

Examples

Autoreactive immune cells
Researchers found that autoreactive T cells play a key role in the development of type 1 diabetes.
The presence of autoreactive antibodies can indicate an autoimmune disorder.
Doctors are studying how autoreactive immune responses contribute to chronic inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autoimmune - relating to a response of the immune system against the body's own cells
