hard shoulder

noun
paved emergency stopping area on a motorway
8.1
Pronunciation: / hɑrd ˈʃoʊldɚ / (AmE), / hɑːd ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
a paved area at the side of a motorway or major road where vehicles can stop in an emergency

Examples

The hard shoulder lane
Drivers must not stop on the hard shoulder except in emergencies.
Repairs to the hard shoulder caused delays on the motorway this morning.
The car broke down, so he pulled over onto the hard shoulder.
