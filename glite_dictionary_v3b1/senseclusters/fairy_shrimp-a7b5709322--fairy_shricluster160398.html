fairy shrimp

noun
small freshwater crustacean
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛri ʃrɪmp / (AmE), / ˈfeəri ʃrɪmp / (BrE)
A small freshwater crustacean with a transparent body and many legs, swimming on its back.

Examples

Small fairy shrimp
Scientists study fairy shrimp to understand how such delicate creatures survive in changing environments.
The children watched as the translucent fairy shrimp swam gracefully on their backs under the water's surface.
The temporary pond was teeming with fairy shrimp during the early spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brine shrimp - a similar small aquatic crustacean living in salty water
