blurp

interjection
comic sound effect
9.9
Pronunciation: / blɜːrp / (AmE), / blɜːp / (BrE)
an interjection imitating a brief silly sound, used to express mild surprise, embarrassment, or for comic effect

Examples

Blurp! Oops
He dropped his phone and said, "Blurp! That was clumsy of me."
When the teacher called on her unexpectedly, she muttered, "Blurp, I wasn't ready!"
The comedian made a funny face and added a loud blurp to make the audience laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oops - an exclamation used after a minor mistake or accident
  • synonym: whoops - an exclamation expressing surprise or a minor error
