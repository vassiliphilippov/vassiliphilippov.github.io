goddess

noun
female deity; divine being
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːd.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.ɪs / (BrE)
A female deity or divine being worshipped in various religions.

Examples

A goddess of love
In Hinduism, Durga is revered as a fierce goddess who protects her devotees.
Many cultures have a fertility goddess at the center of their mythology.
The ancient Greeks worshipped Athena as their most powerful goddess.
