preventionist

noun
advocate of prevention
6.7
Pronunciation: / prɪˈvɛnʃənɪst / (AmE), / prɪˈvɛnʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or works to prevent problems such as disease or crime

Examples

A preventionist doctor
As a dedicated preventionist, she organizes workshops to educate people about healthy habits.
The city hired a preventionist to help reduce crime in local neighborhoods.
Many preventionists believe that early education is key to stopping problems before they start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports a cause or policy
