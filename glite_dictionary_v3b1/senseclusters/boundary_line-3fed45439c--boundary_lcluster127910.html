boundary line

noun
line marking a boundary
2.8 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊndəri laɪn / (AmE), / ˈbaʊndəri laɪn / (BrE)
a line that marks the limit or edge of an area or a division between two regions

Examples

The boundary line of a field
During the match, the ball landed just outside the boundary line.
The fence was built exactly on the boundary line between the two properties.
The surveyor marked the boundary line to settle the dispute over the land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - the edge or boundary of something, especially a country or region
  • antonym: center line - a line marking the exact middle of something, especially a road or sports field
  • synonym: frontier - a line or border separating two countries
