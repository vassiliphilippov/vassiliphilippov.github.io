respawn

noun
game reappearance event
7.2
Pronunciation: / riˈspɔn / (AmE), / riˈspɔːn / (BrE)
the event or process in a video game when a player, character, or item reappears after being eliminated or removed

Examples

A quick respawn
After being defeated, the character's respawn allowed the player to continue the game quickly.
The respawn point was located near the center of the map for easy access.
Players waited for the respawn of rare items before exploring the next area.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elimination - the act of being removed or defeated in a game
  • synonym: revival - the act of coming back to life or activity
