furket

noun
forked pole for supporting muskets
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝː.kɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.kɪt / (BrE)
A forked pole, about four feet long, used as a support for holding a musket while aiming or firing.

Examples

A furket pole
He learned to steady his weapon using a furket during his training.
In the 17th century, soldiers rested their heavy muskets on a furket for better aim.
The museum display included a furket alongside early firearms and powder flasks.
