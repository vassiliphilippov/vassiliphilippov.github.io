garment worker

noun
person employed to make garments
2.5 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑːr.mənt ˈwɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /ˈgɑː.mənt ˈwɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a person who is employed to make garments, typically in a factory or workshop

Examples

Garment worker sewing clothes
As a garment worker, she worked long hours sewing shirts and trousers.
The factory employed hundreds of garment workers to meet international demand.
The rights of garment workers have become an important topic in the fashion industry.
