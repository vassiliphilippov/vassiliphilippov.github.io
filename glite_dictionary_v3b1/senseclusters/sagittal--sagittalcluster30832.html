sagittal

adjective
relating to a plane dividing body into left and right
Pronunciation: / ˈsædʒ.ɪ.t̬əl / (AmE), / ˈsædʒ.ɪ.təl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in a plane parallel to the sagittal suture, especially dividing the body into left and right parts

Examples

Sagittal plane
In anatomy class, we learned how the body is divided by the sagittal plane.
The sagittal suture runs along the top of the skull, separating the two parietal bones.
The doctor showed us a sagittal view of the brain on the MRI scan.
