printer's ink

noun
ink used in printing presses
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪntərz ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈprɪntəz ɪŋk / (BrE)
a semisolid, quick-drying ink specially formulated for use in printing presses

Examples

Printer's ink for presses
Old bills showed traces of printer's ink even after years in storage.

The newspaper's pages were smudged with printer's ink.
