unpreparedness

noun
lack of readiness
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprɪˈpɛrdnəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnprɪˈpeədnəs / (BrE)
the state of not being ready or properly equipped for something

Examples

Her unpreparedness for interview
Her unpreparedness for the interview was obvious when she could not answer basic questions.
The team's unpreparedness led to confusion during the emergency drill.
He regretted his unpreparedness after realizing he had forgotten important documents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: preparedness - the state of being ready or equipped for something
  • antonym: readiness - the state of being fully prepared for something
