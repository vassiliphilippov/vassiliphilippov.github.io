duxelles

noun
mushroom mixture
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʌkˈsɛlz / (AmE), / dʌkˈsɛlz / (BrE)
a finely chopped mixture of mushrooms, onions, and herbs sautéed in butter, used as a filling or flavoring in cooking

Examples

A duxelles mixture
The chef prepared a rich duxelles to use as a filling for the beef Wellington.
You can add duxelles to omelets for extra flavor and texture.
A layer of duxelles gives the pastry a delicious, earthy taste.
