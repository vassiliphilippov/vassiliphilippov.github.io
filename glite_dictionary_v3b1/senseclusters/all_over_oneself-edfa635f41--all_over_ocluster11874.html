all over oneself

adverb
excessively self-conscious or awkward
9.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / …îl Àào äv…ö w ånÀàs…õlf / (AmE), / …îÀêl Àà…ô äv…ô w ånÀàself / (BrE)
behaving in an excessively self-conscious, awkward, or eager manner, often drawing excessive attention to oneself

Examples

All over himself nervously
After receiving the compliment, he became all over himself and started blushing awkwardly.
He was all over himself trying to impress the new manager, barely able to sit still.
She gets all over herself with excitement whenever she talks about her favorite band.

Synonyms

  • make a show of oneself
    7.5 0.01/m
