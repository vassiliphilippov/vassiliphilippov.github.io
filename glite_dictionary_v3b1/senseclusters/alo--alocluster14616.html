alo

interjection
telephone greeting
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈloʊ / (AmE), / ɑːˈləʊ / (BrE)
used as a greeting when answering the telephone, especially in some countries

alo on phone

In some countries, people often say alo instead of 'hello' when answering the phone.
