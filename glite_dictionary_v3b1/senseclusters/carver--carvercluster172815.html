carver

noun
an artist who sculpts by cutting or shaping
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrvər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvə / (BrE)
an artist who creates sculptures by cutting or shaping materials such as wood, stone, or ivory

Examples

Stone carver
A skilled carver can transform a simple block of wood into a work of art.
Many museums feature the work of a master carver from the early 20th century.
The talented carver spent months perfecting the statue’s delicate features.
