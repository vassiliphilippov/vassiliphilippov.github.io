ethologist

noun
scientist who studies animal behavior
Pronunciation: / ɪˈθɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈθɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a biologist who studies animal behavior, especially in natural environments

Examples

An ethologist studies animals
As an ethologist, she carefully recorded the birds’ mating dances.
Many ethologists use video to document animal behaviors without disturbing them.
The ethologist spent months observing the wolf pack in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals
