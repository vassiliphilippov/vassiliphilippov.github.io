distantly

adverb
in a faraway manner
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstəntli / (AmE), / ˈdɪstəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is far away in space, time, or relation.

Examples

Heard distantly far away
She could hear the music distantly through the walls.
The mountains appeared distantly on the horizon, shrouded in mist.
They are only distantly related, sharing a common ancestor several generations ago.
