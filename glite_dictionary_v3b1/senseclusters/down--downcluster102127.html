down

noun
period of difficulty
6.3
Pronunciation: / daʊn / (AmE), / daʊn / (BrE)
a period of difficulty, sadness, or reduced success in life or activities

Examples

Life's downs
After losing his job, he went through several downs before finding new work.
Life is full of downs as well as moments of joy and success.
Even the most successful people experience downs from time to time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: low point - a time when someone feels least successful or happy
  • synonym: setback - an event that delays progress or causes difficulty
  • antonym: up - a period of happiness, success, or improvement
