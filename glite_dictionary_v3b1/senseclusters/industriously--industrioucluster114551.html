industriously

adverb
in a hardworking or diligent way
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəsli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows hard work and diligence.

Examples

Work industriously
She cleaned the entire house industriously while everyone else was away.
The students worked industriously to complete the group project before the deadline.
They industriously researched the topic to ensure their report was accurate and thorough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diligently - in a way that shows care and conscientiousness in one's work
