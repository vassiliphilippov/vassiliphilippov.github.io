cruzeiro

noun
stone cross monument
Pronunciation: / kruːˈzeɪroʊ / (AmE), / kruːˈzeɪərəʊ / (BrE)
a stone cross or monument, especially found in Portugal and former Portuguese colonies, marking religious or historical sites

Examples

Stone cruzeiro monument
The old cruzeiro at the crossroads is a symbol of the village's history.
Pilgrims stopped to pray at the cruzeiro before continuing their journey.
Many towns in Portugal have a central cruzeiro dating back centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross - a structure in the shape of a cross, often with religious significance
  • synonym: monument - a structure built to commemorate a person or event
