Elisabethville

proper noun
former name of Lubumbashi, Congo
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlɪzəbɛθˌvɪl / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪzəˌbɪl / (BrE)
the former name (until 1966) for the city of Lubumbashi in southeastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, known as a copper mining center near the Zambian border.
