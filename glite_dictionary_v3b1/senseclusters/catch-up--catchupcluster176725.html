catch-up

noun
informal meeting
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈkætʃˌʌp / (BrE)
an informal meeting or period when people update each other on news or progress

Examples

A catch-up chat
We scheduled a catch-up to talk about everything that happened over the summer.
After months apart, the friends enjoyed a long catch-up at their favorite cafe.
Let's arrange a catch-up next week so we can share our latest news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meeting - an event where people come together to discuss or share information
  • synonym: reunion - a gathering of people who have not seen each other for some time
