low-IQ remark

noun
unintelligent or foolish comment
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊ aɪ ˈkju rɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˌləʊ aɪ ˈkjuː rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment or statement that shows a lack of intelligence or poor judgment

Examples

A low-IQ remark
Her low-IQ remark during the debate made the audience groan in disbelief.
It's better to think before making a low-IQ remark in front of your colleagues.
The teacher ignored his low-IQ remark and continued with the lesson.
