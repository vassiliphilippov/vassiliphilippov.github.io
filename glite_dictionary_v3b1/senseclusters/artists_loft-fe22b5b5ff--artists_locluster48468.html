artist's loft

noun
converted industrial studio and living space for artists
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːr.tɪsts lɔft / (AmE), / ˈɑː.tɪsts lɒft / (BrE)
A large, open space in a former industrial building, converted for use as both a studio and living area by an artist.

Examples

An artist's loft apartment
After years of saving, they finally moved into a sunlit artist's loft in a converted warehouse.
Many creative people are drawn to the open floor plans typical of an artist's loft.
The painter spent her days creating sculptures in her spacious artist's loft downtown.
