Diospyros kaki

noun
oriental persimmon tree species
Pronunciation: / diˈɑːspɪˌrɒs ˈkɑːki / (AmE), / daɪˈɒspɪˌrɒs ˈkɑːki / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree native to Asia, cultivated for its large, red or orange edible but astringent fruit, commonly known as the persimmon.

Examples

The Diospyros kaki tree

The Diospyros kaki is widely grown in East Asia for its delicious fruit.
