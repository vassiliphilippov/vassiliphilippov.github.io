girlie

adjective
weak or effeminate
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli / (BrE)
informal, often derogatory: weak, cowardly, or lacking toughness, especially in a way considered effeminate

Examples

Girlie insult

The coach warned the team not to act girlie during the intense match.
Some people find the term girlie offensive and outdated in modern conversations.
