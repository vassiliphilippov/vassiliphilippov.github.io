prenegotiation

adjective
before negotiations
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːnəˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːnɪˌɡəʊʃiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
occurring or existing before formal negotiations, especially referring to meetings, documents, or processes that happen beforehand

Examples

Prenegotiation meeting
The team organized a prenegotiation session to clarify their goals before meeting with the other company.
A prenegotiation document was drafted to outline the main discussion points.
They found the prenegotiation phase helpful for building trust before the official talks began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postnegotiation - occurring after formal negotiations
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before the main event or activity
