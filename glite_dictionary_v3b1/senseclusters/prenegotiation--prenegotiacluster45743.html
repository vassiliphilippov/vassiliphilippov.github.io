prenegotiation

adjective
before negotiations
7.1
Pronunciation: /priː.nə.goʊ.ʃɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prə.nə.goʊ.ʃɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /priː.nə.gəʊ.ʃɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prə.nə.gəʊ.ʃɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
occurring or existing before formal negotiations, especially referring to meetings, documents, or processes that happen beforehand

Examples

A prenegotiation document was drafted to outline the main discussion points.
They found the prenegotiation phase helpful for building trust before the official talks began.
