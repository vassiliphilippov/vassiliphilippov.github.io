cancer of the blood

noun
malignant blood disease (leukemia)
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.sɚ əv ðə blʌd / (AmE), / ˈkæn.sə əv ðə blʌd / (BrE)
A malignant disease affecting blood-forming tissues, resulting in the abnormal growth of white blood cells; commonly known as leukemia.
