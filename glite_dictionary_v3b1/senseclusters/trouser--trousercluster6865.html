trouser

noun
part of a garment covering the leg
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊzər / (AmE), / ˈtraʊzə / (BrE)
the part of a garment that covers the leg, usually referring to either one leg of a pair of trousers or to trousers as a whole, chiefly in British English or in tailoring contexts

Examples

A trouser pocket
He kept his wallet in his trouser pocket for easy access.
She noticed a tear at the bottom of her right trouser.
The tailor carefully adjusted the left trouser leg to fit perfectly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trousers - an outer garment covering the body from the waist to the ankles with a separate part for each leg
