fitly

adverb
in an appropriate or suitable manner
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪt.li / (AmE), / ˈfɪt.li / (BrE)

Examples

Her speech was fitly timed, coming just when encouragement was needed.
The ceremony was fitly conducted with dignity and respect.
