illest

adjective
most unwell
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪlɪst / (AmE), / ˈɪlɪst / (BrE)
most sick or unwell; superlative form of ill.

Examples

The illest patient
After the long voyage he was the illest among the crew, barely able to stand.
Of all the patients in the ward, she looked the illest and needed immediate attention.
He became the illest in his family after catching the flu last winter.
