Cucumis melo cantalupensis

noun
variety of muskmelon (cantaloupe)
Pronunciation: / kuːˈkjuːmɪs ˈmɛloʊ kæntəˌluːˈpɛnsɪs / (AmE), / kjuːˈkjuːmɪs ˈmiːləʊ kæntəˌluːˈpensɪs / (BrE)
