hoary

adjective
having grey or white hair with age
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔri / (AmE), / ˈhɔːri / (BrE)
having grey or white hair, especially as a sign of age; showing signs of old age

Examples

Hoary hair
Across the table sat a hoary professor, respected for both his age and his knowledge.
Her hoary hair glistened in the morning sunlight, marking her many years of wisdom.
The old man stroked his hoary beard as he recalled tales from his youth.
