lawbreaker

noun
someone who breaks the law
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔːˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person who does not obey the law; someone who commits a crime or an illegal act

Examples

A lawbreaker commits crime
Strict laws do little to deter a determined lawbreaker.
The judge sentenced the young lawbreaker to community service.
The police were determined to catch the lawbreaker before he committed another crime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: criminal - a person who has committed a crime
  • antonym: law-abider - a person who obeys the law
  • synonym: offender - someone who commits an illegal act
