catechetic

adjective
relating to religious instruction (catechesis)
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈkɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈkɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to instruction by question and answer, especially in teaching the principles of the Christian religion

Examples

Catechetic teaching method
He specialized in catechetic methods for teaching new converts.

Their catechetic studies were essential for understanding Christian doctrine.
Her catechetic questioning guided the discussion toward deeper reflection.
Many catechisms use catechetic dialogue to teach religious doctrines.
The teacher employed a catechetic approach to ensure the students understood every concept thoroughly.
