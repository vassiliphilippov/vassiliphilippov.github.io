whiteman's foot

noun
a perennial weed, Plantago major
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtmənz fʊt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtmənz fʊt / (BrE)
A common European perennial weed (Plantago major), naturalized in many parts of the world and often found in lawns and roadsides.

Examples

Common whiteman's foot weed
Early settlers called Plantago major whiteman's foot because it spread along their paths.
