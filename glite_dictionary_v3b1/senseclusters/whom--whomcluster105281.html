whom

pronoun
object pronoun for people
1.0
Pronunciation: / huːm / (AmE), / huːm / (BrE)
used as the object of a verb or preposition to refer to a person or people previously mentioned or easily identified

Examples

To whom it may concern
To whom should I send this invitation for the event?
The person with whom I spoke was very helpful and knowledgeable.
She is the candidate whom the committee selected for the position.
