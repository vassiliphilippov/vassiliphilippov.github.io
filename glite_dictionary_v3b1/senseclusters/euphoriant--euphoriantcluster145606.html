euphoriant

noun
drug causing euphoria
6.6
Pronunciation: / juˈfɔriənt / (AmE), / juˈfɔːriənt / (BrE)
A psychoactive drug that tends to produce feelings of elation and euphoria.

Examples

A euphoriant drug
Doctors warn that repeated use of a euphoriant can lead to addiction.
Some herbal supplements are marketed as natural euphoriants with mood-lifting effects.
The patient became dependent on the euphoriant prescribed to manage his pain.
