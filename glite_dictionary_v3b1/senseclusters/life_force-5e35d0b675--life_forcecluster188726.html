life force

noun
game energy/health resource

(in games and fiction) a fictional energy or vitality resource that characters can lose, regain, or use to power abilities; often represented by hit points or a vitality meter.

Examples

A life force meter
In the dungeon, the thief drained the boss's life force to heal her own wounds.
Players gathered ancient life force crystals to restore their party before the final battle.
The game's mechanics let you sacrifice your life force to power forbidden spells at a cost.
