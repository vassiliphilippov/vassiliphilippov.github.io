nondiscriminatory

adjective
treating equally
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈskrɪmɪnəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪmɪnətəri / (BrE)
treating all people or groups equally and fairly, without showing bias or preference

Examples

Nondiscriminatory policy
The school has a nondiscriminatory admissions policy that welcomes students from all backgrounds.
A nondiscriminatory approach ensures everyone receives the same opportunities regardless of their identity.
The law requires companies to maintain nondiscriminatory practices in hiring and promotion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discriminatory - showing unfair difference in treatment toward certain people or groups
  • synonym: fair - treating people in a way that does not favor some over others
  • synonym: impartial - treating all sides or people equally; unbiased
