Tswana

noun
language of the Tswana people
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈtwɑːnə / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken chiefly by the Tswana people in Botswana and South Africa.

Examples

Tswana language
He used a traditional greeting in Tswana during the ceremony.
She is fluent in both English and Tswana.
The Tswana language is taught in many schools in Botswana.
