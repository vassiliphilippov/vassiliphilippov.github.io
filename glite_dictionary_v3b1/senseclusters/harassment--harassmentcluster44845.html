harassment

noun
intense annoyance from persistent torment
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / həˈræs.mənt, ˈhɛr.əs.mənt /; / həˈræsmənt, ˈhærəsmənt / (AmE), / ˈhær.əsmənt, həˈrɑːs.mənt /; / həˈrɑːsmənt, ˈhærəsmənt / (BrE)
the act of persistently attacking, annoying, or criticizing someone in a way that upsets or intimidates them

Examples

Persistent harassment
After weeks of harassment, she finally found peace when the bullying stopped.
He was driven nearly mad by the constant harassment from the noisy neighbors.
The harassment left him feeling anxious and unable to concentrate on his work.
Online harassment can have serious emotional effects on its victims.
Several employees complained about harassment in the workplace and reported it to management.
The company introduced new policies to prevent harassment and support those affected by it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullying - the act of intimidating or mistreating someone repeatedly
  • synonym: persecution - hostile and unfair treatment, often over a period of time
  • antonym: protection - the act of keeping someone safe from harm or harassment
  • antonym: relief - a feeling of reassurance and relaxation after distress
  • synonym: torment - severe physical or mental suffering; anguish
