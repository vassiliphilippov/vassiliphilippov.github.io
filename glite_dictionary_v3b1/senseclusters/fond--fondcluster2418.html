fond

noun
pan drippings
8.6 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / fÉ‘nd / (AmE), / fÉ’nd / (BrE)
the browned bits and juices left in a pan after cooking meat, used as a base for sauces.

Examples

Pan fond
After searing the steak, she used the fond to make a rich pan sauce.
Deglazing the fond with broth adds deep flavor to the gravy.
The chef explained that the secret to great sauces is building on the fond left in the pan.
