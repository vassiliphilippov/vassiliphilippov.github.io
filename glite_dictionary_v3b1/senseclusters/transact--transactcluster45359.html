transact

verb
conduct business or negotiations
4.0
Pronunciation: / trænˈsækt / (AmE), / trænˈzækt / (BrE)
to conduct or carry out business, especially financial or official business

Examples

To transact business
He had to transact with several foreign suppliers to complete the deal.
Many customers now prefer to transact online rather than visiting a physical branch.
The company must transact business in accordance with international regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deal - to do business or trade, especially formally
  • synonym: negotiate - to discuss something in order to reach an agreement
  • synonym: transact - to interact or engage in business, especially with someone else
