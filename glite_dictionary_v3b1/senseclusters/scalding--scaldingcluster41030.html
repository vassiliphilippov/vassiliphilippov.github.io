scalding

adjective
extremely hot
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːldɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely hot and capable of causing burns

Examples

Scalding water
She accidentally touched the scalding water and quickly pulled her hand away.
The soup was still scalding when it was served, so I waited before tasting it.
Be careful with the scalding steam coming from the kettle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boiling - very hot, at or near the boiling point
  • antonym: freezing - very cold, at or below the freezing point
  • degree lower: lukewarm - slightly warm, not hot
