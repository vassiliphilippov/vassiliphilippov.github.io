Qaddafi

proper noun
Libyan leader (1942–2011), ruled 1969–2011
7.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈdɑːfi / (AmE), / kaˈdæfi / (BrE)
Muammar Qaddafi (1942–2011), Libyan leader who ruled from 1969 to 2011 after seizing power in a coup and establishing a socialist state based on his interpretation of Islamic principles.
