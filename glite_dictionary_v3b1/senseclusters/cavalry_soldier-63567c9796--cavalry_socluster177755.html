cavalry soldier

noun
soldier mounted on horseback
Pronunciation: / ˈkævəlri ˌsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / ˈkævəlri ˌsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
a soldier who fights mounted on horseback, typically as part of a cavalry unit

Examples

A cavalry soldier on horseback
In historical reenactments, a cavalry soldier often wears authentic uniforms and rides trained horses.
The cavalry soldier drew his saber as he charged into battle.
The museum displayed armor and weapons used by a typical cavalry soldier from the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cavalryman - a soldier in a cavalry unit
  • antonym: infantryman - a soldier who fights on foot
