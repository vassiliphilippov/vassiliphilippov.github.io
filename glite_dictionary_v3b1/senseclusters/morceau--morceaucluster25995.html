morceau

noun
short musical or literary piece
9.1
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈsoʊ / (AmE), / mɔːˈsəʊ / (BrE)
A short literary or musical composition, often used to refer to a brief piece of music.

Examples

A morceau of music
She performed a beautiful morceau on the piano at the recital.
The anthology includes a morceau by each author featured in the collection.
The violinist delighted the audience with a charming morceau between the main symphonic works.

Related Terms

  • synonym: movement - a self-contained section of a musical composition
  • synonym: piece - a musical or literary composition
