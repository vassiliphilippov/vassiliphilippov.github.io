jerky

noun
strips of dried meat
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːki / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːki / (BrE)
meat, especially beef, that is cut into strips and dried, often by sun or low heat, to preserve it.

Examples

Beef jerky
Beef jerky is a popular snack among travelers and campers.
She offered me a piece of homemade jerky seasoned with pepper and garlic.
We packed some jerky for our hiking trip because it lasts a long time without refrigeration.
