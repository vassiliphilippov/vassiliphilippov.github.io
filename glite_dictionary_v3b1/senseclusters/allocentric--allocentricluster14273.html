allocentric

adjective
adventure-seeking
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæloʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
describing a person who prefers novel, adventurous, and culturally unfamiliar experiences, especially in travel or tourism

Examples

Allocentric traveler
The allocentric tourist chose to explore remote islands rather than visit popular attractions.
Her allocentric nature led her to try foods and customs unfamiliar to most travelers.
Travel companies often design special packages for allocentric clients seeking unique adventures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventurous - willing to try new or exciting experiences
  • antonym: psychocentric - preferring familiar and safe experiences, especially in travel
