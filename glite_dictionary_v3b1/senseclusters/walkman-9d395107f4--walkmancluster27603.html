Walkman

noun
portable personal stereo device
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkˌmæn /; / ˈwɔk.mən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkmən /; / ˈwɔːk.mən / (BrE)
a portable cassette or CD player with headphones, originally made by Sony, used for listening to music while walking

Examples

A Walkman player
During the 1980s, owning a Walkman was a symbol of coolness among teenagers.

Many people associate the Walkman with the rise of portable music listening.
She dusted off her Walkman from the 1980s and played her favorite mixtape.
