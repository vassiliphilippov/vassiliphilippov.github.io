Hypocreaceae

noun
family of brightly colored fungi
Pronunciation: / haɪˌpoʊkriˈeɪsi.i /; / haɪˌpoʊkriˈeɪsiˌaɪ / (AmE), / hʌɪˌpəʊkriˈiːsi.iː /; / hɪˌpəʊkriːˈiːsiːaɪ / (BrE)
a family of fungi with brightly colored, fleshy or membranous fruiting bodies, sometimes classified in the order Hypocreales
