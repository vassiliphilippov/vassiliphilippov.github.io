liquid ecstasy

noun
street name for the drug GHB
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈɛkstəsi / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˈɛkstəsi / (BrE)
A street name for gamma hydroxybutyrate (GHB), a drug often used recreationally for its euphoric and sedative effects.
