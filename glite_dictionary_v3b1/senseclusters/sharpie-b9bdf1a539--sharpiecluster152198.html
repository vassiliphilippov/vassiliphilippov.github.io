Sharpie

proper noun
permanent marker brand
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
a brand name for a permanent marker pen commonly used for writing or drawing on various surfaces

Examples

Sharpie brand marker
She used a Sharpie to sign her name on the poster.
Please label your lunch container with a Sharpie so it does not get lost.
Artists sometimes use a Sharpie for bold outlines in their sketches.
