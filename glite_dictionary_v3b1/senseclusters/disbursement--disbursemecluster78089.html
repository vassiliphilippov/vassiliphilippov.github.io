disbursement

noun
the act of paying out money
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈbɜrsmənt / (AmE), / dɪsˈbɜːsmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of paying out money, especially from a fund or account

Examples

A disbursement of funds
Each disbursement must be documented for auditing purposes.
Please submit all receipts to ensure proper disbursement to staff members.
The disbursement of funds will occur at the end of the fiscal year.
Before making any disbursement, the accountant double-checked all invoices.
She handled the disbursement of grants to all successful applicants.
The quarterly disbursement of funds to the departments occurs in April.
