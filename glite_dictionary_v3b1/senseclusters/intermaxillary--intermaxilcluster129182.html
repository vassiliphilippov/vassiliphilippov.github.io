intermaxillary

noun
front upper jaw bone
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmæksɪˌlɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmæksɪləri / (BrE)
a small bone at the front of the upper jaw, also known as the premaxilla, found in many vertebrates

Examples

Intermaxillary bone
The paleontologist identified an unusually shaped intermaxillary in the fossilized skull.
Damage to the intermaxillary can affect the alignment of the upper teeth.
In some animals, the intermaxillary bone is fused with the maxilla.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: maxilla - the main bone of the upper jaw
  • synonym: premaxilla - the bone at the very front of the upper jaw
