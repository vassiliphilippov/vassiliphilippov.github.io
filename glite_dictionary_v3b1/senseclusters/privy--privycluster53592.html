privy

adjective
hidden or secluded; private
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪvi / (AmE), / ˈprɪvi / (BrE)
(of a place or thing) hidden from general view or use; private or secluded.

Examples

Privy room
She found a privy alcove in the library to study without interruption.
The old monastery had a privy garden where the monks would meditate in peace.
They met at a privy location known only to a few close friends.
