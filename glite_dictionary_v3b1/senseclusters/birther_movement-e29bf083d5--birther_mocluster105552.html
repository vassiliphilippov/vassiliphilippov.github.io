birther movement

noun
political group questioning someone's birthplace
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.θər ˈmuːv.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.θə ˈmuːv.mənt/ (BrE)
a political campaign or group asserting that a public figure, especially a president, was not born in the country they serve, thereby questioning their eligibility for office
