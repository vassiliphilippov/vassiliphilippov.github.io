representationalism

noun
realistic art style
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
an approach in visual art that seeks to depict real objects, people, or scenes as they appear in life

Examples

Representationalism in painting
The artist's commitment to representationalism is evident in her lifelike landscapes and portraits.
Many critics praised the exhibition for its return to representationalism after years of abstract trends.
Students learned about representationalism by studying paintings that closely resemble real-life scenes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstraction - an artistic style that does not attempt to represent reality accurately
  • synonym: realism - an artistic style focused on representing subjects truthfully
