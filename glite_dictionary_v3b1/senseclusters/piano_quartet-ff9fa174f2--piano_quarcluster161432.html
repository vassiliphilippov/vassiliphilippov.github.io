piano quartet

noun
ensemble of piano and three string instruments
3.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /piː.ˈæn.oʊ kwɔːr.ˈtet/ /; / /piː.ˈæn.ə kwɔːr.ˈtet/ (AmE), /pi.ˈæn.əʊ kwɔː.ˈtet/ /; / /pɪ.ˈæn.əʊ kwɔː.ˈtet/ (BrE)
A group of four musicians consisting of a pianist, a violinist, a violist, and a cellist, or a piece of music composed for such a group.

Examples

A piano quartet group
A typical piano quartet features piano, violin, viola, and cello.
She is composing a new piano quartet for the upcoming festival.
The piano quartet gave an outstanding performance of Brahms last night.
