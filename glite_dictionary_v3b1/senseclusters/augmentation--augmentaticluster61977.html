augmentation

noun
an increase in amount or size
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːɡmənˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡmənˈteɪʃən /; / ˌɔːɡmɛnˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or amount of making something greater in size, amount, or degree; an increase or enhancement

Examples

Population augmentation

There has been a significant augmentation in the company's profits this year.
Medical researchers are exploring brain augmentation to enhance human memory and cognition.
The augmentation of staff numbers helped improve customer service at the help desk.
The company invested in the augmentation of its production capacity to meet rising demand.

  • antonym: reduction - the amount by which something is made smaller or less
