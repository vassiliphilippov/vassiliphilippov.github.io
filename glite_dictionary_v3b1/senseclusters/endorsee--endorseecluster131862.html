endorsee

noun
recipient of endorsement
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndɔrˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌɛndɔːˈsiː / (BrE)
a person to whom a check, right, document, or property is legally transferred by endorsement

Examples
