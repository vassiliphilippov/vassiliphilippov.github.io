elevated

adjective
raised above ground level
Pronunciation: / ˈɛl.ə.veɪ.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛl.ɪ.veɪ.tɪd / (BrE)
raised above the surrounding area or ground level; situated higher than its surroundings.

Examples

Elevated platform
Many train lines in the city run on elevated tracks above the streets.
She stood on an elevated platform to address the crowd.
The ceremony was held on an elevated stage so everyone could see.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowered - moved to a lower position
  • synonym: raised - lifted or moved to a higher position
