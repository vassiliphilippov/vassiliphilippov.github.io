Sir Lancelot

proper noun
legendary Knight of the Round Table
Pronunciation: / sɚ ˈlænsəlɑt / (AmE), / sə ˈlæn.sə.lɒt / (BrE)
In Arthurian legend, one of the most famous Knights of the Round Table; renowned for his bravery, chivalry, and his romance with Queen Guinevere.
