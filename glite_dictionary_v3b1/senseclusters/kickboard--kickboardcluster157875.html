kickboard

noun
two-wheeled scooter
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪk.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈkɪk.boʊrd/ (AmE), /ˈkɪk.bɔːd/ (BrE)

Examples

Wearing a helmet is important when riding a kickboard in busy areas.
