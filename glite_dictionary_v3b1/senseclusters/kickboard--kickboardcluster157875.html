kickboard

noun
two-wheeled scooter
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɪkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a small two-wheeled vehicle with a platform that a person rides by pushing one foot against the ground

Examples

Ride a kickboard
Children raced their kickboards down the sidewalk after school.
He bought a new kickboard to commute short distances in the city.
Wearing a helmet is important when riding a kickboard in busy areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scooter - a light two-wheeled vehicle ridden by standing and pushing with one foot
  • degree lower: skateboard - a board with wheels ridden by standing and pushing with one foot
