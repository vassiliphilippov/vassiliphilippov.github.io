hustle of city life

noun
the busy, chaotic nature of city life
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌsəl əv ˈsɪti laɪf / (AmE), / ˈhʌsəl əv ˈsɪti laɪf / (BrE)
the energetic, busy, and often chaotic activity characteristic of living in a city

Examples

The hustle of city life pace
He missed the quiet evenings back home, far from the hustle of city life.
She enjoyed returning to the countryside after experiencing the hustle of city life for years.
Some people thrive on the hustle of city life, while others find it exhausting.
