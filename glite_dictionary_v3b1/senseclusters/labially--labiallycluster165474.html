labially

adverb
using the lips
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbiəli / (AmE), / ˈleɪbiəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves the lips, especially in the production of speech sounds

Examples

Sound made labially
The consonant is pronounced labially by bringing both lips together.
Some languages use sounds that are formed labially rather than with the tongue.
The teacher explained how to produce the sound labially for correct pronunciation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orally - in a way that involves the mouth
