crabber

noun
crab fishing boat
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbər / (AmE), / ˈkræbə / (BrE)
a boat or ship designed and used for catching crabs, especially in commercial fishing

Examples

A crabber boat
The crabber returned to the harbor loaded with fresh crabs after a long night at sea.
Each season, the crew repairs their crabber to prepare for the busy months ahead.
A modern crabber is equipped with advanced navigation and safety equipment for deep-sea fishing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trawler - a fishing boat that uses a large net to catch fish or seafood
