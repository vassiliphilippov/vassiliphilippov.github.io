small wonder

noun
not surprising; to be expected
8.9
Pronunciation: / smɔl ˈwʌndɚ / (AmE), / smɔːl ˈwʌndə / (BrE)
used to say that something is not surprising or is to be expected in the circumstances

Examples

It is small wonder
After such a long trip, it's small wonder that they're tired.
Given his dedication, it's small wonder he succeeded.
With all the training she's had, it's small wonder she performs so well.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no wonder - used to show that something is not surprising; similar meaning
