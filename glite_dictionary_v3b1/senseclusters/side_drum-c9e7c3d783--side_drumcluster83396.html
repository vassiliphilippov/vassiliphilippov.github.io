side drum

noun
small drum with snare used in bands
3.8
Pronunciation: / saɪd drʌm / (AmE), / saɪd drʌm / (BrE)
a small drum with two heads and a snare stretched across the lower head, commonly used in orchestras and marching bands

Examples

A side drum player
He learned to play the side drum before moving on to other percussion instruments.
The crisp sound of the side drum echoed through the concert hall.
The marching band used a side drum to keep a steady rhythm during the parade.
