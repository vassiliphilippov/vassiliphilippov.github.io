Prunus maritima

noun
a North American seacoast shrub with edible purple fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs məˈrɪtəmə / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs məˈrɪtɪmə / (BrE)
a seacoast shrub native to northeastern North America, known for its showy white blossoms and edible purple fruit, commonly called the beach plum

Examples

Prunus maritima beach plum
Gardeners often cultivate Prunus maritima for its attractive flowers and fruit.
Jams made from Prunus maritima are popular delicacies in some coastal communities.
The Prunus maritima flourishes along the sandy dunes of the northeastern coastline.
