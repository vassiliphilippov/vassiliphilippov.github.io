coupling

noun
device that connects two things mechanically
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌplɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌplɪŋ / (BrE)
a device or mechanism that connects two things so they move or work together, especially in machinery

Examples

Machine coupling
A flexible coupling is used to absorb vibrations between connected machines.
The engineer installed a new coupling to link the two shafts together.
When the coupling broke, the train cars separated unexpectedly.
A faulty coupling caused the machinery to vibrate excessively during operation.
The engineer installed a new coupling to connect the two sections of the pipe.
The technician checked the coupling between the engine and the drive shaft for any signs of wear.

  • synonym: fastener - device used to hold two objects together
