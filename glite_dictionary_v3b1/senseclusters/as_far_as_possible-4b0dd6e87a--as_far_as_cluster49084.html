as far as possible

adverb
to the feasible or possible extent
2.7 0.99/m
Pronunciation: / …ôz f…ër …ôz Ààp…ës…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / …ôz f…ëÀê …ôz Ààp…ís…ôbl / (BrE)
to the extent that is feasible or achievable; as much as can be done.

Examples

Avoid noise as far as possible
Please avoid making noise as far as possible during the ceremony.
The company aims to reduce waste as far as possible for environmental reasons.
We tried to maintain normal routines as far as possible despite the changes.

Synonyms

  • as much as possible
    1.4 6.10/m
    as much as possible asks you to do something to the greatest feasible extent, while as far as possible stresses what can actually be achieved.
  • to the limit
    3.9 1.13/m
    to the limit means doing something as fully or intensely as the boundary allows, while as far as possible means what is achievable.
