skid mark

noun
mark left by a slipping tire
6.8 2.7
Pronunciation: /skɪd mɑːrk/ (AmE), /skɪd mɑːk/ (BrE)
a mark or streak left on a road or other surface by a tire sliding or slipping, usually as a result of sudden braking or loss of control

Examples

Skid marks on road
Investigators measured the skid marks to estimate how fast the vehicle was traveling.
The car left a long skid mark on the wet pavement after braking suddenly.
There was a visible skid mark where the truck tried to avoid the accident.
