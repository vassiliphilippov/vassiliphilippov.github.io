all for the plot

x
done to serve the plot of a story
Pronunciation: / ɔl fər ðə plɑt / (AmE), / ɔːl fə ðə plɒt / (BrE)
used to indicate that actions or events occur primarily to advance the storyline, often at the expense of logic or character consistency

Examples

All for the plot twist
Her miraculous recovery was clearly all for the plot to keep the show moving.
Some storylines seem unbelievable, but fans accept them as being all for the plot.
The villain suddenly switched sides, but it was all for the plot and didn't fit his character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plot armor - unrealistic protection of characters due to their importance to the story
