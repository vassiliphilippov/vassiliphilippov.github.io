separateness

noun
state of being separate or distinct
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛpɚrətnɪs /; / ˈsɛp.ɚ.ət.nəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛp(ə)rətnɪs /; / ˈsɛp.ər.ət.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being separate or distinct from others

Examples

Separateness of ideas
He valued the separateness of his work and personal life.
Modern technology can make the separateness of individuals less pronounced.
The separateness between the two communities was evident in their cultural practices.
A feeling of separateness can sometimes lead to loneliness.
Some people value separateness in their personal and professional lives.
The separateness of the two cultures was evident in their traditions and daily practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distinctness - the quality of being clearly different or separate
  • synonym: separation - the act or process of separating or the state of being separated
  • antonym: unity - the state of being united or joined as a whole
