libidinal

adjective
of or pertaining to the libido
Pronunciation: / lɪˈbɪdənəl / (AmE), / lɪˈbɪdɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the libido, especially sexual drives or desires.

Examples

Libidinal energy
Freud's theory discusses the influence of libidinal energy on human behavior.
Many artists have explored libidinal themes in their work.
The psychologist analyzed the patient's libidinal impulses during therapy.
