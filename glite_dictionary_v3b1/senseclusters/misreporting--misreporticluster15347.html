misreporting

noun
giving false information
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving false or incorrect information, especially in official reports or records

Examples

Misreporting financial results
The company was fined for misreporting its annual earnings to investors.
Accidental misreporting of data can affect the results of a scientific study.
Journalists must avoid misreporting facts to maintain public trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falsification - the act of making something false or misleading
  • antonym: reporting - the act of giving an account or statement of facts
