bewilderedly

adverb
in a confused or puzzled way
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪldərɪdli / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪldərɪdli / (BrE)
In a confused or puzzled manner; showing bewilderment.

Examples

Look bewilderedly around
He glanced bewilderedly around the room, unsure of what had just happened.
She bewilderedly tried to follow the complicated instructions.
The tourists looked bewilderedly at the unfamiliar street signs.
