Hatti

proper noun
ancient Anatolian region
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːti / (AmE), / ˈhæti / (BrE)
an ancient region and the people who lived in central Anatolia, in what is now Turkey

Examples

Hatti ancient region
Archaeologists have discovered artifacts from Hatti in central Turkey.
The ancient kingdom of Hatti played a significant role in early Anatolian history.
