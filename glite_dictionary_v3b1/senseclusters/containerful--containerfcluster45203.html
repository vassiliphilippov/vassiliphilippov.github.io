containerful

noun
the quantity a container will hold
9.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪnɚˌfʊl / (AmE), / kənˈteɪnəˌfʊl / (BrE)
An amount that fills a container; the quantity a container can hold.

Examples

A containerful of paint
After finishing a containerful of paint, she realized she needed to buy more for the second coat.
He brought in a containerful of fresh apples from the orchard.
The mixture requires exactly one containerful of water for every two scoops of powder.
