belong

verb
be rightly classified in a group
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɔŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒŋ / (BrE)
to be in the right or suitable place or category

Examples

Belong in a museum
Do you think this painting belongs in a modern art museum?
These files belong in the archive, not in the active folder.
Whales belong among the mammals due to their biological characteristics.
She finally felt that she belonged in her new school.
Some plants belong in the shade, not in direct sunlight.
These documents don't belong in this drawer; they should be filed in the cabinet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fit - be suitable or appropriate for a particular group or category
  • antonym: misfit - to be in the wrong place or situation
