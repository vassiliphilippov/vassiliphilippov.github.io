relaxed

adjective
free from tension or anxiety
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlækst / (AmE), / rɪˈlakst / (BrE)
free from tension, strain, or anxiety; calm and at ease.

Examples

A relaxed attitude
His relaxed attitude made everyone feel comfortable at the meeting.
She felt relaxed after spending the evening with friends.
The hotel has a relaxed atmosphere that helps guests unwind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • antonym: tense - feeling nervous or anxious, not relaxed
