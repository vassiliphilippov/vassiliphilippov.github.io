bullhide

noun
whip made from bull skin
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌhaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌhaɪd / (BrE)
a whip, strap, or similar implement made from the tough skin of a bull, used for driving animals or punishment

Examples

A bullhide whip
The cowboy carried a sturdy bullhide to help manage the herd.
She watched as the old bullhide hung on the barn wall, worn from years of use.
A crack of the bullhide echoed across the open field as the cattle moved forward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leather strap - a strip of leather used for fastening or whipping
  • synonym: rawhide - untanned animal skin, often used for whips or straps
