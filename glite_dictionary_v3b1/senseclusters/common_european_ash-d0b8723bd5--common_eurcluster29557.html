common European ash

noun
a tall European ash tree species
4.7 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑm.ən ˌjʊr.əˈpi.ən ˈæʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ən ˌjʊə.rəˈpiː.ən ˈæʃ / (BrE)
a tall tree native to Europe and parts of Asia, with dark-green, shiny leaves above and pale, downy leaves beneath; commonly known as the European ash (Fraxinus excelsior)
