representationalist

noun
realistic artist

Pronunciation: /rep.rɪ.zen.ˈteɪ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ /; / /rə.prɪ.zen.ˈteɪ.ʃə.nəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /rep.rɪ.zen.ˈteɪ.ʃən.əl.ɪst/ /; / /rə.prɪ.zen.ˈteɪ.ʃən.əl.ɪst/ (BrE)
an artist who creates art that depicts recognizable people, places, or objects rather than abstract forms

Examples

Representationalist painter
As a representationalist, he focused on painting landscapes that looked true to life.
The gallery features works by a renowned representationalist known for her detailed portraits.
Many representationalists believe that art should reflect the real world as closely as possible.
