steeliness

noun
cold gray color
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːlinəs / (AmE), / ˈstiːlinɪs / (BrE)
a cold gray color or metallic shine that resembles the surface of steel

Examples

Steeliness of the sky
The steeliness of the winter sky made the morning feel especially cold and distant.
Her painting captured the steeliness of the city skyline at dawn.
A faint steeliness colored the clouds as the storm approached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grayness - the quality of being gray in color
  • antonym: warmth - a quality of being warm in color or feeling
