automated teller machine

noun
machine for banking transactions, such as cash withdrawal
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔtəˌmeɪtɪd ˈtɛlər məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˌmeɪtɪd ˈtɛlə məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a machine, typically located outside banks or other public places, that allows bank customers to perform basic financial transactions such as withdrawing cash using a card and a PIN, without the need for a human teller

Examples

An automated teller machine outside
Many people prefer using an automated teller machine for quick banking transactions.
She withdrew cash from the automated teller machine outside the supermarket.
The automated teller machine was temporarily out of service due to maintenance work.
