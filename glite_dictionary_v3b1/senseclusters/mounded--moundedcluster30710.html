mounded

verb
to pile up
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊndɪd / (AmE), / ˈmaʊndɪd / (BrE)
formed into a mound or heap; piled up in a raised mass.

Examples

To mound the soil
The gardener mounded the earth around the base of the rose bushes for winter protection.
After the harvest, workers mounded the potatoes in the storage shed.
Snow was mounded along the sides of the road after the plows passed.

Related Terms
