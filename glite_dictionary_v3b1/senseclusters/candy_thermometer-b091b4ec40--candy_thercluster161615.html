candy thermometer

noun
temperature gauge for candy making
Pronunciation: / ˈkændi θərˌmɑmətɚ / (AmE), / ˈkændi θəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a cooking thermometer used to measure the temperature of sugar syrups when making candy or other confections

Examples

A candy thermometer
A candy thermometer is essential when you want to make fudge or toffee at home.
She used a candy thermometer to make sure the caramel reached the right temperature.
Without a candy thermometer, it's difficult to determine the exact stage of your sugar syrup.
