vexation

noun
a cause of annoyance or distress
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɛkˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / vɛkˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
A person or thing that causes annoyance, worry, or unhappiness.

Examples

A daily vexation
For years, paperwork was a constant vexation in her job as a manager.
His lateness was more than a minor vexation to his colleagues.
The loud construction next door has become a daily vexation for the residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyance - something that causes irritation or bother
  • antonym: pleasure - a feeling or source of enjoyment; happiness
