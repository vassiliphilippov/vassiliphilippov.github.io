rehab

verb
to restore condition
3.7
Pronunciation: / riˈhæb /; / ˈriːˌhæb / (AmE), / riˈhæb /; / ˈriːˌhæb / (BrE)
to repair, restore, or renovate something to bring it back to a good or original condition

Examples

  • synonym: renovate - to restore something to a good state of repair
