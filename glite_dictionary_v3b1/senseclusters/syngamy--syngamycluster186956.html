syngamy

noun
fusion of nuclei
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəmi / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡəmi / (BrE)
the fusion of two reproductive cells (gametes or nuclei) during sexual reproduction to form a new cell (zygote or diploid cell)
