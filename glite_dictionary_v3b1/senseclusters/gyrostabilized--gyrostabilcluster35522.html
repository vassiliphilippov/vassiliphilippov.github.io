gyrostabilized

adjective
stabilized by gyroscope
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒaɪroʊˈsteɪbəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪərəʊˈsteɪbɪlaɪzd / (BrE)

Examples

Gyrostabilized camera
The drone's gyrostabilized camera allows for smooth video footage even in windy conditions.
A gyrostabilized platform helps keep the telescope steady while observing the stars.
Many modern ships use gyrostabilized systems to reduce rolling in rough seas.
