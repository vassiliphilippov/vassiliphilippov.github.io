centon

noun
patchwork composition
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.tən/ (AmE), /ˈsen.tən/ /; / /ˈsen.tɒn/ (BrE)
a musical or artistic composition created by assembling parts taken from other works, forming a medley or patchwork

Examples

A musical centon
The composer created a centon by blending melodies from several classical pieces.
Her latest painting is a centon of styles borrowed from famous artists.
The festival featured a centon of folk songs from different regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mashup - a creative work combining elements from different sources
  • synonym: medley - a musical piece made from parts of different songs
  • antonym: original - a work created entirely new, not from existing parts
