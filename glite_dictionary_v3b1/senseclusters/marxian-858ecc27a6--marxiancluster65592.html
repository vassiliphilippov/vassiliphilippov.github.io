Marxian

noun
follower of Marx
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrksiən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksiən / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the theories and political ideas of Karl Marx

Examples

A Marxian thinker
As a dedicated Marxian, he often discussed the importance of class struggle in society.
The conference attracted many Marxians interested in economic theory and social change.
Her research was influenced by Marxian perspectives on labor and inequality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Marxist - a person who believes in the theories of Karl Marx
  • antonym: capitalist - a person who supports capitalism
