nontariff

adjective
exempt from tariffs
6.3
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈtærɪf / (AmE), / nɒnˈtærɪf / (BrE)
not involving tariffs; relating to trade restrictions that are not taxes on imports or exports

Examples

Nontariff trade barriers
The government created a nontariff policy to encourage more international trade.
Certain products are classified as nontariff and can enter the country without extra charges.
Exporters prefer nontariff goods because they are more competitive in foreign markets.
The government introduced nontariff measures to control the flow of foreign goods.
Many countries use nontariff barriers such as quotas and licensing requirements.
Exporters faced new nontariff obstacles after the trade agreement changed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duty-free - not subject to import taxes or duties
  • antonym: tariff - a tax or duty on imported or exported goods
  • antonym: tariffed - subject to tariffs or import duties
