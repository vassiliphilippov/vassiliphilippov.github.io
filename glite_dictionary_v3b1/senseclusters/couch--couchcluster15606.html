couch

verb
to crouch down
Pronunciation: / kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
to lower your body close to the ground, often to hide or protect yourself

Examples

To couch in the grass
The rabbit began to couch in the tall grass to avoid being seen.
Soldiers couched behind the wall as the enemy approached.
The cat couches low before pouncing on its toy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crouch - to bend your knees and lower your body close to the ground
  • antonym: stand - to be in an upright position on your feet
