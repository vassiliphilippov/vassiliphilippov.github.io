tomahawk

noun
bone-in ribeye steak
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑː.mə.hɔːk/ (AmE), /ˈtɒ.mə.hɔːk/ (BrE)
a large, bone-in ribeye steak served with the long rib bone attached, named for its resemblance to a tomahawk axe.

Examples

A tomahawk steak
For his birthday, he ordered a massive tomahawk cooked medium-rare and shared it with friends.
The restaurant's signature dish is a grilled tomahawk served with roasted potatoes and chimichurri sauce.
At the barbecue they offered several tomahawks, each seared on the outside and tender inside.

Related Terms
