abater

noun
person who reduces
8.2
Pronunciation: / əˈbeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈbeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that reduces, removes, or lessens something, especially a nuisance or hazard

Examples

An abater of hazards
The city hired an abater to deal with the hazardous waste.
A professional abater was called to remove the mold from the building.
Noise complaints dropped after the abater installed new soundproofing panels.
