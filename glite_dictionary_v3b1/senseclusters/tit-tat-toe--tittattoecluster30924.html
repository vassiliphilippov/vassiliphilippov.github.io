tit-tat-toe

noun
a pencil-and-paper game of Xs and Os
7.3 3.7
Pronunciation: /tɪt.tæt.ˈtoʊ/ (AmE), /tɪt.tæt.ˈtəʊ/ /; / /tɪt.tɑːt.ˈtəʊ/ (BrE)
a game for two players who take turns marking spaces in a 3×3 grid with Xs and Os, aiming to place three of their marks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally

Examples

Play tit-tat-toe game
Although it's a simple game, tit-tat-toe can be surprisingly strategic if both players pay close attention.
During the thunderstorm, the children played tit-tat-toe by candlelight to pass the time.
He quickly drew a grid on the napkin and challenged me to a game of tit-tat-toe.
