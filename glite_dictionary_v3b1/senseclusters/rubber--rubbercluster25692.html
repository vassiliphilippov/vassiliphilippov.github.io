rubber

adjective
(of a cheque) returned for insufficient funds
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ə / (BrE)
(of a cheque) dishonored or returned because there are not enough funds in the account.

Examples

Rubber check
Businesses often warn customers that presenting a rubber check may result in additional fees.
He tried to pay his rent with a rubber check, but the landlord refused to accept it.
The store manager returned the rubber cheque to the customer, demanding cash instead.
