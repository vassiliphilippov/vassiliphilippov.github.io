bricole

noun
historical lever-operated catapult
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɪˈkoʊl / (AmE), / brɪˈkəʊl / (BrE)
A type of historical catapult operated by manpower, using cords attached to a lever and sling to launch projectiles.
