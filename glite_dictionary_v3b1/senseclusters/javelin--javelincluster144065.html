javelin

noun
athletic event: spear-throwing
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒævəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒævəlɪn / (BrE)
an athletic event in which competitors throw a long, slender spear as far as possible

Examples

Javelin competition
During the javelin, the stadium fell silent as the competitor prepared to throw.
She set a new record in the javelin at the national championships.
The javelin requires both strength and precise technique from the athletes.
