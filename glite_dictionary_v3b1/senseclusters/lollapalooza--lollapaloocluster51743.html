lollapalooza

proper noun
music festival
Pronunciation: / ˌlɑːləpəˈluːzə / (AmE), / ˌlɒləpəˈluːzə / (BrE)
an annual multi-day music festival featuring rock, pop, hip-hop, and electronic artists, held in various cities
