level the ground

verb
make the ground flat or even
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəl ðə ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəl ðə ɡraʊnd / (BrE)
to make the surface of the ground flat or even, especially as preparation for construction or landscaping

Examples

To level the ground carefully

Before laying down the patio stones, we had to level the ground to prevent uneven surfaces.
Heavy machinery was brought in to level the ground for the new sports field.
