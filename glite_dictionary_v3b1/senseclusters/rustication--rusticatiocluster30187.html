rustication

noun
the act of moving to live in the country
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌstəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrʌstɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of living in the countryside, especially for relaxation or to escape city life; rural life or manners
