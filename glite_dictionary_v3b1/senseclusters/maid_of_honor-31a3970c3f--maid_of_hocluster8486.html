maid of honor

noun
chief unmarried female attendant at a wedding
1.0
Pronunciation: / meɪd əv ˈɑnɚ / (AmE), / meɪd əv ˈɒnə / (BrE)
an unmarried woman who serves as the chief attendant to the bride at a wedding ceremony

Examples

The maid of honor speech
As maid of honor, she gave a heartfelt speech at the reception.
My sister was honored to be the maid of honor at my wedding.
The maid of honor helped organize the bridal shower and rehearsal dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridesmaid - a woman who attends the bride at a wedding
  • antonym: matron of honor - a married woman who serves as chief attendant to the bride
