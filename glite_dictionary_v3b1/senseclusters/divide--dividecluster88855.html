divide

verb
to separate into parts or groups
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪd / (BrE)
to separate or split something into parts, groups, or sections

Examples

To divide a cake
After the disagreement, the group divided into two factions.
The river divides the city into two distinct areas.
We need to divide the cake equally among the children.
Let's divide the pizza so everyone gets a fair share.
She used a knife to divide the orange into neat slices.
The teacher asked us to divide into small groups for the activity.
A busy highway divides the park from the residential area.
A tall fence divides the two neighboring gardens.
The river divided the ancient city into eastern and western parts.
