un-

prefix for reversal
Pronunciation: / ʌn / (AmE), / ʌn / (BrE)
a prefix added to verbs, adjectives, or nouns to indicate reversal, removal, or the opposite of an action or quality

Examples

Un-tie laces
When you untie your shoes, you reverse the action of tying them.
The company decided to undo the changes after receiving negative feedback.
She tried to uncover the truth behind the mysterious disappearance.
