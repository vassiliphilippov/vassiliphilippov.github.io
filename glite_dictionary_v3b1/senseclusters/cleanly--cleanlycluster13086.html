cleanly

adjective
habitually clean; regularly clean
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklinli / (AmE), / ˈkliːnli / (BrE)
(of a person, animal, or place) habitually clean; characterized by regular cleanliness.

Examples

Cleanly habits
He is a cleanly man who takes pride in his appearance and environment.
The animals are surprisingly cleanly, regularly grooming themselves throughout the day.
The children grew up in a cleanly household where hygiene was always valued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clean - free from dirt or impurities
  • antonym: uncleanly - not habitually clean
