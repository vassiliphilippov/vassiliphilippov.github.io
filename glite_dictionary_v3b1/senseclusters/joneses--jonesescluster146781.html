joneses

verb
to crave intensely
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊnzɪz / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊnzɪz / (BrE)
has a strong craving or desire for something, especially a substance or experience, often used informally or in slang.

Examples

She joneses chocolate
Whenever she sees chocolate, she joneses for a piece even if she is not hungry.
He often joneses for adventure when life feels too routine.
After quitting coffee, she still joneses for a cup every morning.
