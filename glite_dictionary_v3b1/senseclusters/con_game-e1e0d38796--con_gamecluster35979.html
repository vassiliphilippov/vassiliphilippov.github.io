con game

noun
a dishonest scam or swindle
6.7 5.3
Pronunciation: /kɑːn geɪm/ (AmE), /kɒn geɪm/ (BrE)
a dishonest trick in which someone cheats others, especially by persuading them to believe something false or to buy worthless property; a scam or swindle

Examples

An elaborate con game
He fell for an elaborate con game run by professional scammers.
Many con games target vulnerable people who are easily persuaded.
The police uncovered a con game that cheated victims out of millions of dollars.
