metharbital

noun
anticonvulsant drug for epilepsy
Pronunciation: / mɛˈθɑːrbɪtəl / (AmE), / mɛˈθɑːbɪtəl / (BrE)
an anticonvulsant drug used in the treatment of epilepsy, also known by the trade name Gemonil

Examples

metharbital drug
Patients with epilepsy may respond well to metharbital as part of their treatment regimen.
Researchers are studying the long-term effects of metharbital in managing convulsive disorders.
The doctor prescribed metharbital to help control her epileptic seizures.
