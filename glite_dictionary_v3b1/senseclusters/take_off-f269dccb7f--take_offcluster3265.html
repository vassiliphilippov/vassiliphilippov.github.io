Synonyms

  • pull off
    2.7 4/m
    pull off removes something by pulling it away from where it is attached, while take off simply means to take something from your body or an object.
  • throw off
    3.8 0.2/m
    throw off removes or gets rid of things quickly, often in a strong or sudden way, while take off is a neutral removal action.
  • remove
    1.5 160/m
    remove is a broad, often formal verb for taking something away or dismissing it, while take off is an everyday two-word verb for removing things.
