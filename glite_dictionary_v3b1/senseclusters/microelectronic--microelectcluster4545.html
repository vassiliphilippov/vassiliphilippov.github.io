microelectronic

adjective
involving tiny electronic components or circuits
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or consisting of extremely small electronic components and circuits, especially at the microscopic scale used in computers and other digital devices

Examples

Microelectronic technology
Advancements in microelectronic technology have enabled the development of faster and smaller computers.
He is pursuing a degree in microelectronic engineering, focusing on miniaturized circuit design.
The company specializes in microelectronic devices used in medical equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electronic - relating to devices or systems that use the flow of electrons in circuits
