successional

adjective
in sequence; ecological change
6.7
Pronunciation: /sək.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ /; / /sʌk.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (AmE), /sək.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ /; / /sʌk.ˈseʃ.ən.əl/ (BrE)

Examples

Successional changes in nature

Ecologists study successional patterns to understand how ecosystems recover after disturbances.
Each successional stage in a forest brings different animals and plants to the area.
