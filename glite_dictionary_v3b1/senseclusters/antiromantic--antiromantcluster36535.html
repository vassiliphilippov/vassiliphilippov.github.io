antiromantic

adjective
opposed to Romanticism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiɹoʊˈmæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiɹəʊˈmæntɪk / (BrE)
rejecting or critical of Romanticism or its principles, especially in art or literature

Examples

Antiromantic art style
The critic described the novel as antiromantic because it avoided emotional exaggeration and idealized love.
Her antiromantic views were clear in her essays, which challenged the values of the Romantic poets.
Many modern artists adopted an antiromantic style, focusing on everyday reality instead of imagination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: realist - favoring a realistic approach in art or literature
  • antonym: romantic - relating to or characteristic of Romanticism
