white admiral

noun
a Eurasian butterfly with white-marked wings
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈædmərəl / (AmE), / waɪt ˈædmɪrəl / (BrE)
a Eurasian butterfly with brown wings marked by white bands or patches, commonly found in woodlands

Examples

Eurasian white admiral
Many wildlife photographers hope to capture a close-up of a white admiral during the summer months.
The white admiral is known for its distinctive white bands across its brown wings.
