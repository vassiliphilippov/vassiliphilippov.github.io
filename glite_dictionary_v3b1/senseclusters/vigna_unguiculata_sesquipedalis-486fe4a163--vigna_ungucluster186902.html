Vigna unguiculata sesquipedalis

noun
tropical bean plant with long pods
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪɡnə ˌʌŋɡwɪˈkjuleɪtə sɛsˌkwɪpɪˈdeɪlɪs / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡnə ˌʌŋɡwɪˈkjʊlətə sɛsˌkwɪpɪˈdeɪlɪs / (BrE)
A tropical plant of the legume family, known for its very long, edible pods, commonly called yardlong bean or asparagus bean.
