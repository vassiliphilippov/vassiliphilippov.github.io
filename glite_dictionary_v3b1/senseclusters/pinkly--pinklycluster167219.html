pinkly

adverb
in a pink way
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪŋkli / (AmE), / ˈpɪŋkli / (BrE)
in a pink manner or with a pink color.

Examples

Glowing pinkly clouds
The clouds reflected the sunrise and glowed pinkly over the mountains.
Her cheeks blushed pinkly when she received the compliment.
The flowers bloomed pinkly in the garden, brightening the spring morning.
