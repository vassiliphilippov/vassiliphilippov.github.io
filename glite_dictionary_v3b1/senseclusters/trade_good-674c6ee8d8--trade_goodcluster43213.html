trade good

noun
raw material or product sold in bulk for manufacturing
Pronunciation: / treɪd ɡʊd / (AmE), / treɪd ɡʊd / (BrE)
a raw material or product that is sold in large quantities, typically to other businesses for manufacturing or production purposes

Examples

A trade good product
Before becoming a finished product, the material is first purchased as a trade good.
Cotton is a common trade good exported from this region to international markets.
The company imports various trade goods to supply local manufacturers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commodity - a raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold
