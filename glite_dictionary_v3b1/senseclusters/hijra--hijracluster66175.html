hijra

noun
South Asian third gender
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdʒrə / (AmE), / ˈhɪdʒrə / (BrE)
a member of a traditional third-gender community in South Asia, often recognized as neither male nor female

Examples

Hijra community
The hijra community has a long history in India and Pakistan.
Many hijra are respected for their cultural and religious roles in South Asian society.
A hijra may be invited to bless a newborn child at traditional ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: third gender - a gender category outside the traditional male and female
  • synonym: transgender - a person whose gender identity differs from their assigned sex at birth
