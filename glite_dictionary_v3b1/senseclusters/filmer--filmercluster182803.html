filmer

noun
person recording video
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlmər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlmə / (BrE)
a person who records video or makes movies, especially using a camera

Examples

The filmer records video
The filmer set up his camera to capture the sunrise over the mountains.
During the concert, a professional filmer moved through the crowd to get the best shots.
She worked as a filmer for several independent documentaries last year.
