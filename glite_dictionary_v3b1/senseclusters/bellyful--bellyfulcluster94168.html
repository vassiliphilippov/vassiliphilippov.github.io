bellyful

noun
amount that fills stomach
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌfʊl / (BrE)
an amount of food or something else that completely fills the stomach or is more than enough

Examples

A bellyful of food
After the feast, everyone had a bellyful and wanted to rest.
He complained that he had a bellyful of bad news lately.
She ate a bellyful of pasta and skipped dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glut - an excessively abundant supply of something
  • antonym: scarcity - a lack or shortage of something
  • synonym: surfeit - an excessive amount of something, especially food or drink
