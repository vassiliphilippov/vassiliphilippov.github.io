adobe

noun
sun-dried brick used for building
3.3 4.2
Pronunciation: / əˈdoʊbi / (AmE), / əˈdəʊbi / (BrE)
a brick or building material made from a mixture of sun-dried earth, clay, sand, and straw, used especially in hot, dry climates

Examples

The builders mixed adobe with straw to create sturdy bricks.
