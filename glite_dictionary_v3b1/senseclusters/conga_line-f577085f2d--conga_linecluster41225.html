conga line

noun
line of dancers performing the conga
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡə laɪn /; / ˈkɑːŋɡə laɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡə laɪn / (BrE)
a line of people, one behind another, dancing the conga by moving around together to music

Examples

A conga line dance

She started a conga line at her birthday celebration, and soon almost every guest was dancing.
The conga line snaked through the room, with laughter and singing filling the air.
