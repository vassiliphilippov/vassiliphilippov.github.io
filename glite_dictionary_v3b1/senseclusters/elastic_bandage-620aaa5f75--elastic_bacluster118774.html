elastic bandage

noun
stretchable support bandage
2.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæstɪk ˈbændɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈlæstɪk ˈbændɪdʒ / (BrE)
a long strip of stretchable fabric used to wrap and support injured joints or muscles, applying pressure to reduce swelling

Examples

An elastic bandage wrap
After twisting his ankle, the nurse wrapped an elastic bandage around it to reduce swelling.
She used an elastic bandage to support her injured wrist during recovery.
You should always keep an elastic bandage in your first aid kit for emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compression bandage - a bandage designed to apply pressure to a specific area of the body
