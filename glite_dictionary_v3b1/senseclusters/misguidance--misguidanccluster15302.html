misguidance

noun
wrong advice
7.1
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈgaɪ.dəns/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈgaɪ.dəns/ (BrE)
the act or result of giving incorrect or misleading advice or information that leads someone to make a wrong decision

Examples

Due to misguidance from sources
Due to misguidance from unreliable sources, the team made a costly mistake.
He realized too late that his actions were the result of misguidance from his peers.
The project failed because of misguidance during the planning stage.
The team suffered from misguidance and made several costly mistakes.
Without proper leadership, misguidance can easily derail a project.
He realized too late that he had acted under misguidance from unreliable sources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guidance - help or advice that tells you what to do
  • synonym: misdirection - the act of giving wrong or misleading directions
