unhandcuffed

adjective
not wearing handcuffs
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhændˌkʌft / (AmE), / ʌnˈhændˌkʌft / (BrE)
not wearing handcuffs or not having been restrained with handcuffs

Examples

Unhandcuffed suspect
The police allowed the unhandcuffed witness to leave the station after questioning.
He was brought into the courtroom unhandcuffed, showing the judge he was not a threat.
Unlike the other suspects, she remained unhandcuffed during the entire interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handcuffed - wearing or restrained with handcuffs
