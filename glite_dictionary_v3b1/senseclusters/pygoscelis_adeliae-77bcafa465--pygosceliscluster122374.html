Pygoscelis adeliae

noun
species of Antarctic penguin
Pronunciation: / paɪɡəˈsiːlɪs ˌædəˈliːiː / (AmE), / paɪɡəˈsiːlɪs ˌædəˈliːiː / (BrE)
