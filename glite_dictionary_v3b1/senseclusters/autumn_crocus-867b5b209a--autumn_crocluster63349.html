autumn crocus

noun
flowering plant with autumn blooms
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtəm ˈkroʊkəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəm ˈkrəʊkəs / (BrE)
a bulbous plant, native to western and central Europe, that produces white, purple, or lavender-and-white flowers in the autumn

Examples

The autumn crocus flower
