spacefaring

adjective
able to travel in space
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsˌfɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌfeərɪŋ / (BrE)
capable of traveling or operating in outer space using spacecraft

Examples

Spacefaring country
A spacefaring nation invests heavily in technology to explore the solar system.
The movie imagines a future where humanity becomes a truly spacefaring civilization.
Scientists debate when Earth will become fully spacefaring and able to reach other planets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: terrestrial - relating to the Earth or land
