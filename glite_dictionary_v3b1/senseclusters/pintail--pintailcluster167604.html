pintail

noun
a duck with a long, pointed tail
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌteɪl / (BrE)
a long-necked duck found in many parts of the world, known for its long, pointed central tail feathers

Examples

A pintail duck
A male pintail in breeding plumage displays striking white and brown colors.
Flocks of pintails migrate south every winter.
The pintail is easily recognized by its slender neck and long tail feathers.
