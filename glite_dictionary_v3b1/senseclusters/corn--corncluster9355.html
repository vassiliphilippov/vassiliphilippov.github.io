corn

noun
something sentimental or clichéd
Pronunciation: / kɔrn / (AmE), / kɔːn / (BrE)
Something that is overly sentimental, clichéd, or lacking in originality, often used to describe entertainment or art.

Examples

Corny jokes
His speech was full of corn and empty platitudes.
I can't stand the corn in those old romantic movies.
That commercial was pure corn and made the whole audience groan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sentimentality - excessive emotion or sentiment
