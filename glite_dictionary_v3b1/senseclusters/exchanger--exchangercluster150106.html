exchanger

noun
person or device that swaps
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒər / (AmE), / ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒə / (BrE)
a person or device that exchanges or swaps things, such as money, items, or energy

Examples

Heat exchanger
The exchanger at the airport helped us convert our currency before traveling.
A heat exchanger transfers energy from one fluid to another in many industrial systems.
She worked as an exchanger at the market, swapping goods between vendors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: converter - a device that changes something into a different form
  • synonym: trader - a person who buys and sells goods or assets
