uneatable

adjective
not fit to be eaten
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈiːtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈiːtəbl̩ / (BrE)
not fit or suitable for being eaten; not edible

Examples

An uneatable meal
After sitting out all night, the pizza was cold and uneatable.
Many wild mushrooms are uneatable and can even be poisonous.
The fish was so overcooked that it became completely uneatable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inedible - not fit or suitable for eating
