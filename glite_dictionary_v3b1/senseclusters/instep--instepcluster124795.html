instep

noun
the arch of the foot
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈɪn.stɛp / (BrE)
the curved upper part of the foot between the ball and the ankle, or the part of a shoe or stocking that covers this area

Some boots have extra padding in the instep for added comfort.
