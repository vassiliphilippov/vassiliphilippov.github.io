undesirably

adverb
in an undesirable way
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzaɪərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is not wanted or is likely to cause problems; in an undesirable manner.

Examples

Act undesirably often
His comments were undesirably critical during the meeting, making everyone uncomfortable.
The results of the experiment were undesirably inconsistent, prompting further investigation.
The water in the region is undesirably high in lead.
