Geminid

noun
meteor in Geminids shower
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmɪnɪd / (BrE)
a meteor that is part of the annual Geminids meteor shower, usually visible in December

Examples

A bright Geminid
We saw a bright Geminid streak across the sky during the meteor shower.
Every December, stargazers look forward to spotting a Geminid in the night sky.
The Geminid meteor shower is known for its many visible meteors each year.
