Phocoena

noun
genus of small porpoises
9.9
Pronunciation: / foʊˈsiːnə / (AmE), / fəˈsiːnə / (BrE)
A genus of small, blunt-nosed porpoises found in the Northern Hemisphere.

Examples

The genus Phocoena
Harbor porpoises belong to the genus Phocoena and are found in coastal waters.
Researchers identified the stranded animal as a member of the genus Phocoena.
The Phocoena's blunt snout distinguishes it from other marine mammals.
