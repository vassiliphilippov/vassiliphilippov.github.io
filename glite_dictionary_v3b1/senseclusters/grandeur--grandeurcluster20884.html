grandeur

noun
impressive beauty or magnificence
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræn.dʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡræn.djʊə / (BrE)
impressive beauty, power, or size that inspires admiration or awe

Examples

Natural grandeur
Even the simplest ceremony was marked by a sense of grandeur and tradition.
He tried to recapture the lost grandeur of his family's estate.
The palace was renowned for its grandeur and breathtaking architecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humbleness - the quality of being modest or unpretentious
  • synonym: magnificence - the quality of being magnificent
  • synonym: splendor - great beauty or impressive appearance
