retire

verb
cause someone to leave a job or position
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪə / (BrE)
to cause someone to leave their job or position, especially because of age or after a particular event

Examples

To retire an employee
After the scandal, the board decided to retire the director from her position.
Several senior managers were retired early as part of the reorganization plan.
The company quietly retired him following the investigation.
