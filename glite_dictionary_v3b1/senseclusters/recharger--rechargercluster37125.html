recharger

noun
device for charging batteries
3.5
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈtʃɑːr.dʒər/ /; / /rə.ˈtʃɑːr.dʒər/ (AmE), /riː.ˈtʃɑː.dʒə/ /; / /rə.ˈtʃɑː.dʒə/ (BrE)
