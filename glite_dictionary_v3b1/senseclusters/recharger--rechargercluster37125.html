recharger

noun
device for charging batteries
Pronunciation: / riˈtʃɑrdʒər / (AmE), / riˈtʃɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a device (or sometimes a person) that restores electrical energy or credit to something, such as batteries or prepaid cards

Examples

Phone recharger
I always keep a spare recharger in my backpack for emergencies.
The new phone comes with a fast recharger that can power up the battery in under an hour.
He borrowed my recharger because he left his at home.
I forgot my recharger at home and my phone battery is almost dead.
The technician acted as a recharger for the team's equipment during the event.
Many electric cars come with a portable recharger for convenience on long trips.

Related Terms

  • antonym: battery - a device that stores electrical energy for use
  • synonym: charger - a device for supplying power to a battery
  • antonym: discharger - a device or process that removes electrical charge
