come

verb
to emerge or be emitted
Pronunciation: / kʌm / (AmE), / kʌm / (BrE)
To move out or appear from a source or origin; to be produced or emitted.

Examples

A sharp cry came from the baby in the next room.
As the door creaked open, a faint light came from the hallway.
When the truth finally came out, everyone was shocked.
