autocephalous

adjective
self-governing (church)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
governing itself independently, especially used of a church that is self-governing and not under external authority

Examples

An autocephalous church
The national church declared itself autocephalous after years of foreign oversight.
Several Orthodox churches are recognized as autocephalous by their peers.
An autocephalous church can appoint its own leaders without external approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not controlled by others; self-governing
  • antonym: subordinate - under the authority or control of another
