elevated

adjective
of noble or lofty character or style
Pronunciation: / ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪveɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of ideas, style, or thought) of high moral, intellectual, or artistic value; noble or lofty.

Examples

Elevated style
Her speech had an elevated tone, inspiring everyone who listened.
The novel explores elevated themes such as justice and compassion.
They strove for an elevated standard of conduct in their daily lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: base - lacking moral principles; ignoble
  • synonym: exalted - of high moral or intellectual value
  • synonym: lofty - of imposing height or of high moral or intellectual value
