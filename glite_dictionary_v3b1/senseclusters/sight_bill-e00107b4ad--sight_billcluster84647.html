sight bill

noun
bill payable on presentation
Pronunciation: / saɪt bɪl / (AmE), / saɪt bɪl / (BrE)
a bill of exchange payable immediately upon presentation to the drawee.

Examples

A sight bill payment
Many international transactions are settled through the use of a sight bill.
Payment is required upon presentation of the sight bill at the bank.
The exporter opted to use a sight bill to reduce the risk of non-payment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill of exchange - a written order to pay a stated sum on demand or at a set time
