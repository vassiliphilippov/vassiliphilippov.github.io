durable press

noun
wrinkle-resistant treated fabric
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrəbəl ˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərəbəl ˌprɛs / (BrE)
A fabric that has been chemically treated to resist wrinkling and retain its shape after washing and wearing.

Examples

Clothes labeled as durable press are popular among travelers for their convenience.

The shirt's durable press finish means less ironing for busy professionals.
