Lovecraftian

noun
cosmic horror work
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌvˈkræftiən / (AmE), / ˌlʌvˈkrɑːftiən / (BrE)
a story, film, game, or other work that strongly resembles the themes or style of H. P. Lovecraft, especially cosmic horror; also, a person who creates or admires such works

Examples

A true Lovecraftian
The film was praised as a true Lovecraftian that captured the unsettling atmosphere of cosmic horror.
Many fans of horror literature consider the novel a modern Lovecraftian with its ancient gods and existential dread.
As a devoted Lovecraftian, she collects rare editions of H. P. Lovecraft's stories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmic horror - a genre of horror emphasizing the insignificance of humanity in the universe
  • synonym: weird fiction - fiction blending horror, fantasy, and the supernatural
