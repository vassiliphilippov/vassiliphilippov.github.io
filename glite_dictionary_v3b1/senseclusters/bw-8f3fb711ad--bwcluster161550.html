BW

noun
abbreviation: bandwidth (network capacity)
Pronunciation: / biː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (AmE), / biː ˈdʌbəl.juː / (BrE)
Abbreviation for bandwidth: the capacity of a network connection to transfer data, typically measured in bits per second.

Examples

BW limit
We measured the BW of the connection at 1 Gbps during the test.
The video kept buffering because the apartment's BW couldn't handle simultaneous streaming and downloads.
To improve performance, the IT team increased the server's BW and adjusted QoS settings.
