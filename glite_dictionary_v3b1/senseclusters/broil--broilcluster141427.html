broil

verb
to cook under direct heat
Pronunciation: / brɔɪl / (AmE), / brɔɪl / (BrE)
to cook food by exposing it to direct high heat, usually from above, such as under a broiler or grill

Examples

To broil chicken
It's better to broil the fish for just a few minutes on each side.
Many recipes tell you to broil vegetables to bring out their natural sweetness.
She decided to broil the chicken to give it a crispy skin.
I like to broil salmon with a sprinkle of lemon and dill.
Set the oven to broil and watch the cheese melt and bubble.
She decided to broil the vegetables instead of roasting them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grill - to cook food over direct heat, usually above the heat source
