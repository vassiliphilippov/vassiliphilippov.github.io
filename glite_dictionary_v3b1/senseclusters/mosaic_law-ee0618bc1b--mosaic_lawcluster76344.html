Mosaic law

noun
biblical laws given through Moses
7.1 9.8
Pronunciation: / moʊˈzeɪɪk lɔ / (AmE), / məʊˈzeɪɪk lɔː / (BrE)
the body of laws given by God to the Israelites through Moses, chiefly detailed in the first five books of the Old Testament, including the Ten Commandments and other rules of religious observance
