Examples

Insufficient funds
He was unable to attend the trip due to insufficient funds in his account.
The water supply is insufficient to meet the city's needs during a drought.

    1.8 0.00/m

  • wanting
    4.4 0.00/m

  • scant
    5.5 4.14/m
    scant is used for amounts that are barely enough or slightly less, while insufficient covers any lack in amount, degree, or quality.
  • scanty
    6.6 0.41/m

  • meager
    4.8 4.95/m

  • scarce
