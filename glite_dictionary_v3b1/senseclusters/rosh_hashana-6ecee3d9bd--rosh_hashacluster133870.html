Rosh Hashana

proper noun
Jewish New Year festival
7.2 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ro ä É h…ôÀà É…ëÀên…ô / (AmE), / r…í É h…ôÀà É…ëÀên…ô / (BrE)
The Jewish New Year festival, observed on the first (and often second) day of Tishri, marked by prayer, reflection, and the sounding of the shofar.

Examples

Rosh Hashana festival
Rosh Hashana marks the beginning of the High Holy Days in Judaism.
Many Jewish families gather together to celebrate Rosh Hashana with traditional foods and prayers.
The synagogue was filled with the sound of the shofar on Rosh Hashana.
