Synonyms

  • improbable
    4.9 5/m
    improbable sounds a bit more formal and appears often in writing, while unlikely is the everyday word for a low chance.
  • implausible
    7.0 2/m
    implausible sounds hard to believe because it lacks reason, while unlikely says something probably will not happen.
  • doubtful
    3.0 4/m
    doubtful suggests uncertainty or suspicion about truth, while unlikely focuses on the low probability that something will happen.
