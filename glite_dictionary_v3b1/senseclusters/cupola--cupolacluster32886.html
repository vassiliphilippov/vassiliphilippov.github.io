cupola

noun
spacecraft observation module
8.6 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkjuÀêp…ôl…ô / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêp…ôl…ô / (BrE)
(in spacecraft and space stations) a small, dome-shaped observation module with multiple windows that provides a panoramic external view.

Examples

Space station cupola
During the spacewalk, the commander took photos from the station's cupola to document the docking.
Astronauts often use the cupola to observe Earth and guide robotic arm operations during missions.
The mission team scheduled a video call so the family could see their daughter inside the cupola.
