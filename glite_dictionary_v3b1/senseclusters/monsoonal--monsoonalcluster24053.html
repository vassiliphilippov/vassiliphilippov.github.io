monsoonal

adjective
of a monsoon
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈsuːnəl / (AmE), / mɒnˈsuːnəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a monsoon or the monsoon season

Examples

Monsoonal weather patterns
The region experiences monsoonal weather patterns with heavy rainfall every summer.
Farmers depend on monsoonal rains to water their crops during the growing season.
The monsoonal climate brings both benefits and challenges to the local communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monsoon - a seasonal wind and heavy rain period in South and Southeast Asia
