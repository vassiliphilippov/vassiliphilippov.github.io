style

noun
editorial conventions for writing and publishing
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /staɪl/ (AmE), /staɪl/ (BrE)
a set of rules or conventions for writing, formatting, or presenting information, especially in publishing or digital documents

Examples

Style guide
Changing the company’s style improved the professional appearance of all written communications.
Please check the house style before submitting your manuscript for publication.
The newspaper follows a strict style guide to ensure consistency in its articles.
The designers updated the website style to align with the new branding.
You can change a page's style by editing the site's CSS stylesheet directly.
The blog uses a consistent style to ensure headings and lists look the same across posts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: format - the way in which something is arranged or set out, especially in publishing
  • synonym: stylesheet - a file or collection of rules (often CSS) that defines how HTML or other digital content is presented visually
