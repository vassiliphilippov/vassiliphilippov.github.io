Luschka's tonsil

noun
lymphatic tissue in the nasopharynx (adenoid)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊʃkəz ˈtɑnsəl / (AmE), / ˈlʊʃkəz ˈtɒnsəl / (BrE)
a collection of lymphatic tissue located in the midline of the nasopharynx, also known as the pharyngeal tonsil or adenoid

Examples

Luschka's tonsil enlargement
Enlargement of the Luschka's tonsil can lead to breathing difficulties, especially in young children.
Obstruction caused by the Luschka's tonsil is sometimes treated with adenoidectomy.
The surgeon noted inflammation of the Luschka's tonsil during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adenoid - the pharyngeal tonsil, enlarged in some children
  • degree lower: palatine tonsil - tonsils located on either side at the back of the throat
