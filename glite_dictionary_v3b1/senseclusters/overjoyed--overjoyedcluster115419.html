overjoyed

adjective
extremely happy or delighted
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdʒɔɪd / (BrE)
extremely happy or delighted; filled with great joy.

Examples

To feel overjoyed
He looked overjoyed as he hugged his friends after the big win.
She was overjoyed when she heard the good news about her promotion.
The children were overjoyed to see the puppy waiting for them at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delighted - greatly pleased or very happy
  • synonym: ecstatic - feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joy
  • antonym: miserable - extremely unhappy or wretched
