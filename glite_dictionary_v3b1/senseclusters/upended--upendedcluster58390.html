Synonyms

  • upturned
    4.5 0.01/m
    upturned has its lower side pointing upward and often looks tilted or set on end, while upended suggests being suddenly turned over.
  • upside down
    2.0 6/m
    upside down describes something with its top where the bottom should be, while upended can also mean set on end.
  • overturned
    3.6 0.00/m
    overturned means flipped so the top becomes the bottom, while upended can also mean set on its end rather than fully flipped.
  • inverted
    4.8 0.00/m
    inverted is a more formal word for having top and bottom swapped, while upended is common and may mean set on end.
  • overset
    7.0 0.00/m
    overset usually describes boats capsizing or objects tipping over in water, while upended is a general word for something turned so its end faces upward.
