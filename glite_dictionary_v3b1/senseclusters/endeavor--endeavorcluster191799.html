endeavor

noun
serious effort to achieve something
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɛvər /; / ɪnˈdɛvɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɛvə /; / ɪnˈdevə / (BrE)
an attempt or effort to achieve something, especially one that requires determination

Examples

Global cooperation is vital in the endeavor to tackle climate change.
Her latest endeavor to write a novel took nearly three years to complete.
The scientific endeavor to cure the disease continues despite setbacks.
Despite many failures, their endeavour finally met with success.
The restoration project was a challenging endeavour requiring many volunteers.
This scientific endeavour could revolutionize medicine in the next decade.
