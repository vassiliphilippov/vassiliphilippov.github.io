borate

noun
salt or ester of boric acid
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɔːreɪt / (BrE)
a salt or ester derived from boric acid, commonly used in chemistry and industry

Examples

A borate salt
Adding borate to the solution helped prevent the growth of fungi.
Sodium borate is often used as a cleaning agent and antiseptic.
The laboratory synthesized several types of borate compounds.
