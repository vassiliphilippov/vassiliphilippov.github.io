freeze

verb
anesthetize by cold
1.0 7/m
Pronunciation: / friÀêz / (AmE), / friÀêz / (BrE)

Examples

To freeze a tooth
Dentists often freeze a tooth with a cold spray before working on it.
The doctor will freeze the mole before removing it to numb the area.
They used ice to freeze his finger and reduce the pain after the injury.
