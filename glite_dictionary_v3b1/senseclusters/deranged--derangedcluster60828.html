Mentally disturbed or insane; having lost the ability to think or act in a normal or rational way.

Examples

A deranged person
After years of isolation, he became increasingly deranged and unpredictable.
The movie's villain is portrayed as a deranged mastermind plotting chaos.
The police described the suspect as a deranged individual acting without reason.

Synonyms

  • insane
    4.0 15/m
    insane is used for severe mental illness or extreme irrationality, while deranged suggests loss of normal thinking and behavior.
  • unhinged
    7.8 1.13/m
    unhinged describes someone mentally unbalanced and unpredictable, while deranged implies deep loss of normal thinking or control.
  • crazed
    5.0 0.10/m
    crazed shows wild, out-of-control actions and panic, while deranged refers to a general loss of rational thought and behavior.
  • demented
    7.9 2.38/m
    demented often describes long-term brain illness causing memory and thought loss, while deranged implies sudden or full loss of normal reasoning.
  • crazy
    4.1 32/m
    crazy is a casual, informal word for odd or wild behavior, while deranged feels stronger and more clinical.
  • mad
    4.8 8.23/m
    mad can mean angry or mentally ill in casual speech, while deranged specifically describes serious loss of rational thought.
