woodturner

noun
person shaping wood
5.4 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw ädÀåt…úrn…ôr / (AmE), / Ààw ädÀåt…úÀên…ô / (BrE)

Examples

Many hobbyists become woodturners to create custom pens and decorative items.
