woodturner

noun
person shaping wood
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌtɜrnər / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌtɜːnə / (BrE)
a person who shapes wood on a lathe to create objects such as bowls, spindles, or furniture parts

Examples

A woodturner shapes wood
The woodturner crafted beautiful bowls from maple and oak.
A skilled woodturner can transform a simple block of wood into a work of art.
Many hobbyists become woodturners to create custom pens and decorative items.
