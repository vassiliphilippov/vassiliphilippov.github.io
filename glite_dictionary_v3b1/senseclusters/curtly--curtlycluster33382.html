curtly

adverb
in a curt or abrupt manner
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːtli / (AmE), / ˈkɜːtli / (BrE)
In a curt, brief, and often rude or abrupt manner.

Examples

To curtly reply
He nodded curtly and walked away without another word.
She responded curtly to my question, making it clear she was annoyed.
The manager dismissed the suggestion curtly, ending the discussion immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abruptly - in a sudden or unexpected manner
  • antonym: politely - in a courteous and respectful manner
