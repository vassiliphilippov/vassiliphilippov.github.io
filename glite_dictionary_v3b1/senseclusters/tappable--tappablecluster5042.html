tappable

adjective
able to be wiretapped
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtæpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be monitored or accessed by electronic surveillance, especially by wiretapping.

Examples

Tappable phone line
The judge determined that the phone line was tappable under the current warrant.
Sensitive conversations should not be held on a tappable device.
The company upgraded its systems to prevent them from being easily tappable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secure - protected from unauthorized access or surveillance
  • synonym: surveillable - able to be watched or monitored by surveillance
