kumquat tree

noun
tree bearing small edible kumquats
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmˌkwɑt tri / (AmE), / ˈkʌmkwɒt triː / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub of the genus Fortunella, bearing small orange-colored edible fruits with thick sweet-flavored skin and sour pulp

Examples

The kumquat tree fruit
Gardeners often admire the kumquat tree for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.
She planted a kumquat tree near the patio for its fragrant blossoms.
The kumquat tree in our backyard produces fruit every winter.
