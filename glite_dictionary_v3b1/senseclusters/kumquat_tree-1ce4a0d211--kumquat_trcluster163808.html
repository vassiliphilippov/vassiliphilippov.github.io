kumquat tree

noun
tree bearing small edible kumquats
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmˌkwɑt tri / (AmE), / ˈkʌmkwɒt triː / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub of the genus Fortunella, bearing small orange-colored edible fruits with thick sweet-flavored skin and sour pulp
