epicrisis

noun
critical commentary
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈkraɪsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈkraɪsɪs / (BrE)
a critical commentary or formal review of a work, event, or situation, often summarizing and judging its merits

Examples

Literary epicrisis
The professor wrote an insightful epicrisis of the novel for the literary journal.
After the conference, attendees received an epicrisis summarizing the main discussions and outcomes.
Her epicrisis of the film highlighted both its strengths and its weaknesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critique - a detailed analysis and assessment of something
  • antonym: praise - an expression of approval or admiration
  • synonym: review - an evaluation or assessment of a work or performance
