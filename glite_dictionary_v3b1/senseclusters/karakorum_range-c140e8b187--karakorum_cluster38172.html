Karakorum Range

proper noun
mountain range in northern Kashmir
Pronunciation: / ˌkærəˈkɔːrəm reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˌkærəˈkɔːrəm reɪndʒ / (BrE)
a mountain range in northern Kashmir, forming an extension of the Hindu Kush, and containing K2, the world's second highest peak.

Examples

The Karakorum Range mountains
Glaciers in the Karakorum Range are among the largest outside the polar regions.
Many explorers have been fascinated by the remote beauty of the Karakorum Range.
The Karakorum Range is known for its dramatic peaks and challenging climbing routes.
