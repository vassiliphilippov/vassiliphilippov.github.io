Synonyms

  • relapse
    6.5 2.13/m
    relapse often happens when someone returns to old harmful habits after improvement, while backsliding is broader moral or behavioral falling-back.
  • regression
    7.6 1.92/m
    regression means moving back to a less developed or earlier stage, while backsliding focuses on returning to worse moral or behavior states.
  • reversion
    6.9 0.43/m
    reversion is a return to a previous state in general, while backsliding describes falling back into worse moral or behavioral habits.
  • retrogression
    8.9 0.10/m
