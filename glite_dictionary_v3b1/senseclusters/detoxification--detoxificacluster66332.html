detoxification

noun
removal of toxins, especially medically
3.3 4.0
Pronunciation: /dɪ.tɑːk.sɪ.fɪk.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.tɑːk.sɪ.fɪk.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.tɒk.sɪ.fɪk.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.tɒk.sɪ.fɪk.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of removing or neutralizing harmful substances from the body, especially as a medical treatment for poisoning or addiction

Examples
