manticora

noun
mythical monster with human, lion, and scorpion features
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn.tɪˈkɔr.ə / (AmE), / ˌmæn.tɪˈkɔː.rə / (BrE)
a mythical creature said to have the head of a man (with horns), the body of a lion, and the tail of a scorpion

Examples

The manticora beast
Artists have depicted the manticora in tapestries alongside other mythical beasts.
In the ancient text, a manticora guarded the entrance to the hidden city.
Legends describe the fierce manticora as a fearsome predator with a scorpion's deadly sting.
