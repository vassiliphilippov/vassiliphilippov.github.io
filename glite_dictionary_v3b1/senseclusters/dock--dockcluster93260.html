dock

noun
platform for loading or unloading vehicles
Pronunciation: / dɑk / (AmE), / dɒk / (BrE)
a platform at which trucks or trains are loaded or unloaded, typically at a warehouse or station

Examples

Loading dock
Due to repairs, the loading dock is temporarily closed.
Pallets were stacked near the warehouse dock waiting for the morning shift.
The delivery driver backed his truck up to the dock to unload the goods.
