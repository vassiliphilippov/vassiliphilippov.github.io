approach

verb
make a proposal or suggestion to someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
to speak to someone, often in order to make a request, offer, or suggestion

Examples

To approach a stranger
He nervously approached his neighbor to ask for a favor.
Several companies have approached us about a possible partnership.
The manager decided to approach her colleague with a new business proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propose - to suggest a plan or idea to someone for consideration
  • synonym: suggest - to mention an idea or plan for consideration
