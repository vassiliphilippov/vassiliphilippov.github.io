Ilmen

proper noun
lake in northwestern Russia
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɪlmɛn / (AmE), / ˈɪlmən / (BrE)
a lake in northwestern Russia that drains through the Volkhov River into Lake Ladoga.

Examples
