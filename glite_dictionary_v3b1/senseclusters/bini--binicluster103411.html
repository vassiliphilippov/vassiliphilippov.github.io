bini

noun
language of Edo people
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːni / (AmE), / ˈbiːni / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Bini (Edo) people in southern Nigeria

Examples

Bini language
Many children in Benin City grow up speaking both English and bini at home.
Researchers are working to document traditional folktales in the bini language.
He is proud of his heritage and enjoys teaching bini to younger generations.
