jostle

noun
an act of pushing (in a crowd)
4.8 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ëÀês…ôl / (AmE), / Ààd í…ís…ôl / (BrE)
an act of pushing or shoving, especially in a crowd to make your way through

Examples

A quick jostle
He gave me a friendly jostle as we moved through the busy crowd.
The commuters experienced the usual jostle during rush hour on the subway.
There was a sudden jostle at the entrance as people tried to get out.

Synonyms

  • elbowing
    4.4 0.03/m
    elbowing is pushing with your elbow in tight spaces, while jostle is general pushing or shoving to get through a crowd.
  • thrust
    4.2 2.53/m
    thrust is a strong sudden push often aimed forward, while jostle is a milder shove in a crowd to move past people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shove - an act of pushing someone or something forcefully
