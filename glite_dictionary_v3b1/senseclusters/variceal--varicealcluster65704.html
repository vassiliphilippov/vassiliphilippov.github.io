variceal

adjective
of swollen veins
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌværɪˈsiːəl / (AmE), / ˌværɪˈsiːəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by swollen or enlarged veins, especially varices

Examples

Variceal bleeding
The doctor diagnosed the patient with variceal bleeding in the esophagus.
Treatment for variceal hemorrhage often requires urgent medical attention.
Regular monitoring is important for patients at risk of variceal complications.
