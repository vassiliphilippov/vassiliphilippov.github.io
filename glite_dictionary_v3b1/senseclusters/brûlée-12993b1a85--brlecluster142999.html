brûlée

noun
dessert with caramelized sugar
Pronunciation: / bruːˈleɪ / (AmE), / bruːˈleɪ / (BrE)
a dessert with a layer of caramelized sugar on top, especially as in crème brûlée

Examples

A classic brulee dessert
For dessert, he chose a classic brûlée with a perfectly crisp sugar topping.
The chef used a torch to finish the brûlée right at the table.
She learned how to make a delicious vanilla brûlée in her cooking class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crème brûlée - a custard dessert with a caramelized sugar top
  • synonym: pudding - a sweet dessert, often creamy or baked
