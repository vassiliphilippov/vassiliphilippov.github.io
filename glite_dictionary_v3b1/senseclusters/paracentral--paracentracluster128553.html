paracentral

adjective
near the center
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌparəˈsɛntrəl / (BrE)
located near or beside the center, especially in anatomical or medical contexts

Examples

Paracentral vision loss
The ophthalmologist identified a paracentral scotoma during the eye examination.
A paracentral tumor was discovered close to the brain's midline.
Damage to the paracentral area of the retina can affect vision near the center.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: central - located at or near the center
  • degree lower: peripheral - located on or near the outer edge
