sprinkle

noun
act of scattering drops or particles
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋkəl / (BrE)
an act of scattering small drops of liquid or particles over a surface, often water

Examples

A sprinkle of water
A sprinkle of rain was enough to refresh the garden in the morning.
The ceremony began with a gentle sprinkle of holy water on the congregation.
There was a light sprinkle of water on the dusty floor after cleaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: splash - act of scattering liquid, especially water, in drops
