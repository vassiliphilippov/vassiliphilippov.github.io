hospitalman

noun
hospital attendant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtəlmæn / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtəlmən / (BrE)
an archaic term for a person employed to care for the sick in a hospital, such as an attendant or orderly

Examples

A hospitalman assists patients
In the early 1900s, a hospitalman would assist doctors with basic patient care.
The hospitalman made sure the patients were comfortable and had everything they needed.
Before modern nursing roles, a hospitalman was often responsible for cleaning and feeding patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick or infirm
  • synonym: orderly - a hospital worker who assists nurses and cares for patients
