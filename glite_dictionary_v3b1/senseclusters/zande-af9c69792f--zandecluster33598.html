Zande

proper noun
Central African people or language
Pronunciation: / ˈzændeɪ / (AmE), / ˈzændeɪ / (BrE)
a member of a Central African ethnic group or the language spoken by this group in South Sudan, Central African Republic, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo

Examples
