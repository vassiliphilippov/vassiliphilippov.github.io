digital artery

noun
artery supplying a finger or toe
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬l ˈɑːr.t̬ɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒ.ɪ.təl ˈɑː.tər.i / (BrE)
An artery in the hand or foot that supplies blood to a finger or toe.

Examples

A digital artery in hand
A blockage in a digital artery can cause numbness in the affected finger.
Surgeons must carefully avoid damaging the digital arteries during hand operations.
The patient suffered an injury that severed a digital artery in his toe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artery - a blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart
