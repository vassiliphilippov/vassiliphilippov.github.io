howler

noun
extremely funny joke or remark
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhaʊlə / (BrE)
A joke or remark that is extremely funny and causes loud laughter, often used informally.

Examples

A real howler joke
He told a howler at the party that made even the serious guests laugh out loud.
Last night's comedy show was full of howlers that had everyone in stitches.
That was such a howler—I couldn't stop laughing for minutes afterward.
