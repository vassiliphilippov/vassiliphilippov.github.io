salvational

adjective
relating to salvation
Pronunciation: / sælˈveɪʃənəl / (AmE), / sælˈveɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or capable of bringing salvation or deliverance, especially in a spiritual or religious sense

Examples

The salvational message
The salvational message of the sermon inspired many people in the congregation.
She found salvational hope in her new beliefs during a difficult time.
Many religions offer salvational paths to guide followers toward spiritual fulfillment.
