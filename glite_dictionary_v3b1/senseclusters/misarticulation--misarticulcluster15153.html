misarticulation

noun
incorrect pronunciation
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
incorrect or abnormal pronunciation of speech sounds, especially in children or speech disorders

Examples

Speech misarticulation
The speech therapist identified several misarticulations in the child's spoken words.
Early intervention can help reduce misarticulation and improve communication skills.
Teachers are trained to recognize misarticulation and refer students for further assessment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: articulation - the clear and correct pronunciation of speech sounds
  • synonym: mispronunciation - the act of pronouncing a word incorrectly
