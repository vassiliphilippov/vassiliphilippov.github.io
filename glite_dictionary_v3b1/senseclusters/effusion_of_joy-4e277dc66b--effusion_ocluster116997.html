effusion of joy

noun
an outpouring of happiness or enthusiasm
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfjuʒən əv dʒɔɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈfjuːʒən əv dʒɔɪ / (BrE)
an overwhelming expression or outpouring of happiness or enthusiasm

Examples

A sudden effusion of joy
At the news of her acceptance, an effusion of joy swept over her entire family.
His speech was met with an effusion of joy from the audience.
The children's effusion of joy filled the house when their mother returned.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gloom - a state or feeling of sadness or depression
  • synonym: jubilation - a feeling or expression of great happiness and triumph
