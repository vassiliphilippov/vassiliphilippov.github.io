Examples

Law of common fate principle
According to the law of common fate, cars moving together in a convoy appear as a unified collection rather than separate vehicles.
Designers use the law of common fate to help users perceive moving objects as related on digital interfaces.
When birds fly in a V-formation, the law of common fate leads us to perceive them as a single group.
