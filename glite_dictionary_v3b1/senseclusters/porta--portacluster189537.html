porta

noun
an anatomical opening or entrance
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔr.tə / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.tə / (BrE)
An anatomical opening or entrance into a bodily cavity, especially in reference to organs such as the heart or liver.

Examples

The porta of liver
A blockage at the porta can lead to serious medical complications.
Surgeons carefully examined the porta before proceeding with the transplant.
The doctor explained that the porta allows blood to enter the chamber of the heart.
