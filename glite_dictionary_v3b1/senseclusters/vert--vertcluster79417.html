vert

noun
heraldic green
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜrt / (AmE), / vɜːt / (BrE)
the color green as used in coats of arms and heraldic designs

Examples

Field of vert
The family coat of arms features a lion on a field of vert.
In heraldry, vert often symbolizes growth, hope, and loyalty in love.
The shield was divided into sections of vert and gold to represent the region's forests and wealth.
