kapa

noun
Hawaiian bark cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
a traditional Hawaiian cloth made by beating the bark of certain trees, often decorated with natural dyes

Examples

Traditional Hawaiian ceremonies often feature garments made from kapa.
