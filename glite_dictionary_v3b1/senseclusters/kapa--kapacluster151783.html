kapa

noun
Hawaiian bark cloth
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
a traditional Hawaiian cloth made by beating the bark of certain trees, often decorated with natural dyes

Examples

A piece of kapa
The museum displayed a beautiful piece of kapa decorated with natural dyes.
Artisans demonstrated how kapa is made from the bark of the wauke tree.
Traditional Hawaiian ceremonies often feature garments made from kapa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloth - a material made by weaving or felting fibers
  • synonym: tapa - bark cloth made in Polynesia and other Pacific islands
