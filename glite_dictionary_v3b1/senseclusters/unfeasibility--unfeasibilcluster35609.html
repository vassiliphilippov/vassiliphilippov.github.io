unfeasibility

noun
impossibility of being done
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌfiːzəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌnˌfiːzəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being impossible to do or carry out.

Examples

The unfeasibility of plans
After reviewing the budget, the team concluded the project’s unfeasibility was due to insufficient funding.
Environmental concerns led to the unfeasibility of building the dam at that location.
The unfeasibility of the plan became apparent once more details were revealed.
