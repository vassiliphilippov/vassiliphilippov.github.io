malaria mosquito

noun
mosquito that transmits malaria
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛriə məˈskiːtoʊ / (AmE), / məˈleəriə məˈskiːtəʊ / (BrE)
a mosquito of the genus Anopheles, especially those species that transmit the parasite responsible for malaria in humans
