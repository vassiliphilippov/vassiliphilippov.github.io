October 24

proper noun
United Nations Day; UN founding anniversary
Pronunciation: / ˌɑkˈtoʊbɚ ˈtwɛnti ˈfɔr / (AmE), / ɒkˈtəʊbə ˈtwɛnti ˈfɔː / (BrE)
The day each year that marks United Nations Day, celebrating the founding of the United Nations in 1945.

Examples
