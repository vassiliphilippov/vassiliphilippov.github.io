Mithraism

noun
ancient Persian-Roman mystery religion
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪθ.reɪˌɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmɪθ.rəˌɪz.əm / (BrE)
An ancient Persian religion centered around the worship of Mithras, which became popular among Romans during the first three centuries AD.

Examples

Ancient Mithraism religion
Archaeologists have uncovered temples dedicated to Mithraism across the former Roman Empire.
Many ancient Roman soldiers were followers of Mithraism, drawn to its secretive rituals.
The decline of Mithraism coincided with the rise of Christianity in Rome.
