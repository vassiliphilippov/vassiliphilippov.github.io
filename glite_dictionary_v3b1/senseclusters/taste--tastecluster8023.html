taste

noun
brief experience; sample
Pronunciation: / teɪst / (AmE), / teɪst / (BrE)
a small amount or sample of something, or a brief experience of something

Examples

A taste of freedom
He got his first taste of freedom when he traveled alone.
Her summer internship gave her a taste of the business world.
The concert offered a taste of the band's new musical direction.
He offered me a taste of his wine at the party.
She just had a taste of the cake to see if it was too sweet.
Would you like a taste of my soup before you order?

Related Terms

  • synonym: sample - a small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like
