heathery

adjective
like heather
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːðəri /; / ˈhɛðəri / (AmE), / ˈhiːðəri / (BrE)
having the appearance, color, or pattern of heather plants; soft, muted, or flecked like heather

Examples

Heathery landscape
The landscape was dotted with heathery patches stretching as far as the eye could see.
She wore a scarf in a soft, heathery shade of purple.
We hiked across the heathery hills under a bright blue sky.
She wore a heathery scarf that blended shades of purple and gray.
The living room was decorated in heathery tones, giving it a calm and cozy feel.
He admired the heathery texture of the fabric, which reminded him of wild moorlands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - not covered with vegetation
  • synonym: heathered - having a mottled appearance like heather
  • synonym: mottled - marked with spots or smears of color
  • antonym: plain - having a single, unvaried color or appearance
