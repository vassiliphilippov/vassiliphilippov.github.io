scarus

noun
brightly colored parrotfish
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛrəs / (AmE), / ˈskɑːrəs / (BrE)
a type of brightly colored parrotfish found in warm seas, often near coral reefs

A scarus fish

A single scarus can help keep coral reefs healthy by eating algae.
