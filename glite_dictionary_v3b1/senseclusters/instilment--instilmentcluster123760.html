instilment

noun
introduction of a liquid drop by drop
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪlmənt / (BrE)
the process of introducing a liquid drop by drop, especially by pouring or injection

Examples

Daily instilment of antibiotics
Her treatment involved the daily instilment of antibiotics into the ear canal.
Proper instilment of the solution ensures the medication reaches the intended site.
The instilment of eye drops should be performed carefully to avoid contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instillation - the process of dripping a liquid into a body part, especially for medical purposes
