frozen

adjective
lacking warmth or friendliness
2.6 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfroʊ.zən/ (AmE), /ˈfrəʊ.zən/ (BrE)
(of a person's expression or manner) lacking warmth or friendliness; showing unfriendliness or disdain.

Examples

Frozen smile
His frozen smile made it clear I wasn't welcome.
She gave me a frozen stare when I walked into the room.
There was a frozen silence between the two old rivals.
