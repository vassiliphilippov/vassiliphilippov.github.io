uremic

adjective
relating to excess waste in blood from kidney failure
5.0 7.9
Pronunciation: /juː.ˈriː.mɪk/ /; / /jʊə.ˈriː.mɪk/ (AmE), /jʊə.ˈriː.mɪk/ /; / /juː.ˈriː.mɪk/ (BrE)
