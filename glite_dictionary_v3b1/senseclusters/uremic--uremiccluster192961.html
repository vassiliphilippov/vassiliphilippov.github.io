uremic

adjective
relating to excess waste in blood from kidney failure
Pronunciation: / juˈrimɪk /; / jʊˈriːmɪk / (AmE), / jʊəˈriːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by an accumulation of waste products in the blood due to impaired kidney function.

Examples

Uremic odor
A uremic odor on the breath can be a sign of severe kidney dysfunction.
He developed uremic symptoms, including confusion and fatigue, due to advanced renal disease.
The patient was diagnosed as uremic after her kidneys failed to filter waste from her blood.
A uraemic odor on the breath can be a sign of advanced renal failure.
Doctors monitored the uraemic complications during the course of treatment.
The patient developed uraemic symptoms as his kidney function deteriorated.
