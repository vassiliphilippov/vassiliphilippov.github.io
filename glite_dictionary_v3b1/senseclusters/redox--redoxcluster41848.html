redox

noun
oxidation-reduction chemical reaction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌdɑks / (AmE), / ˈriː.dɒks / (BrE)
a chemical reaction in which one substance is oxidized and another is reduced, often occurring together as a single process

Examples

Redox chemical reaction
Scientists analyzed the redox processes involved in cellular respiration.
The redox reaction between hydrogen and oxygen releases energy as water is formed.
Understanding redox mechanisms is crucial in developing efficient batteries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oxidation - a process in which a substance loses electrons
  • synonym: reduction - a process in which a substance gains electrons
