runaround

adjective
evasive or delaying
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (BrE)
evasive or unhelpful, especially by giving answers or responses that avoid a direct solution or delay action

Examples

Runaround answers
Whenever I asked for help, I only got runaround responses that never solved my problem.
The customer was frustrated by the runaround attitude of the support team.
Instead of a clear answer, she received a runaround reply that left her confused.

Related Terms

  • antonym: direct - straightforward and clear, not avoiding the issue
  • synonym: evasive - not giving a direct answer or clear response
