family Pteridaceae

noun
a family of ferns
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌtɛrɪˈdeɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌtɛrɪˈdeɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of ferns in some classification systems, historically treated as part of or subdivided from the Polypodiaceae family

Examples

family Pteridaceae ferns
Many ornamental ferns grown in gardens belong to the family Pteridaceae.
Some taxonomists further subdivide the family Pteridaceae based on genetic studies.
The family Pteridaceae includes a wide range of fern species found in tropical regions.
