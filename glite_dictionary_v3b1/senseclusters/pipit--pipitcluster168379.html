pipit

noun
small ground-dwelling songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪpɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɪpɪt / (BrE)
A small songbird that lives mainly on the ground in open country, with streaked brown feathers.

Examples

A small pipit bird
Many pipits build their nests directly on the ground in grassy fields.
The pipit is noted for its subtle, streaky plumage and melodious song.
We spotted a pipit hopping along the roadside during our walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lark - a ground-dwelling songbird of open country
