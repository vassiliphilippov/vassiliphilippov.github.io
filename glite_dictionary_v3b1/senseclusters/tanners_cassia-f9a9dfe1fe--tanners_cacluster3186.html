tanner's cassia

noun
Indian shrub used in tanning
Pronunciation: / ˈtænərz ˈkæsiə / (AmE), / ˈtanəz ˈkæsiə / (BrE)
an evergreen Indian shrub with bright yellow flowers, whose bark is used in tanning; sometimes placed in the genus Cassia

Examples

Tanner's cassia shrub
Gardeners admire tanner's cassia for its vivid display of yellow flowers.
The bark of tanner's cassia is highly valued in the leather industry.
Traditional practices often use tanner's cassia as a source of natural dye and tanning agent.
