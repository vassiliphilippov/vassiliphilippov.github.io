chrome-tungsten steel

noun
steel alloy with chromium and tungsten
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊm ˈtʌŋstən stil / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊm ˈtʌŋstən stiːl / (BrE)
a type of steel alloy that contains both chromium and tungsten, giving it high strength and resistance to wear and heat

Examples

Chrome-tungsten steel alloy
Many cutting tools are manufactured from chrome-tungsten steel for enhanced durability.
The addition of chromium and tungsten makes chrome-tungsten steel suitable for high-temperature environments.
The company specializes in producing chrome-tungsten steel for industrial machinery.

Related Terms
