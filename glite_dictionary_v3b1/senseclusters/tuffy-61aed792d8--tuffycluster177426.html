Tuffy

proper noun
cartoon mouse character
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌfi / (AmE), / ˈtʌfi / (BrE)
a small cartoon mouse character, also known as Nibbles, who appears in the Tom and Jerry animated series

Examples

Tuffy cartoon mouse

Children love watching Tuffy outsmart the cat in Tom and Jerry episodes.
Tuffy is sometimes called Nibbles in different versions of the show.
