Examples

A parade of stars

Synonyms

  • procession
    4.1 6.89/m
    a procession is an orderly, often quiet line of people in a ceremony, while a parade is a more showy public display.
  • cavalcade
    7.9 0.54/m
    a cavalcade is a formal procession of riders or vehicles in celebration, while a parade is a broader, more common public show.
  • march
    2.6 7.18/m
    a march is a walk by people often to support or protest a cause, while a parade is usually a showy, festive event.
  • display
    1.6 16/m
    a display is a static arrangement of objects shown to the public, while a parade is moving and focused on people or floats.
  • exhibition
    3.3 23/m
    an exhibition is a public show of art or goods inside a venue, while a parade is an outdoor moving display of people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - an act of showing something, usually for people to look at or admire
  • synonym: procession - an organized group or line moving forward in an orderly way, especially for a ceremony
  • synonym: show - a display or exhibition; a visible presentation
