show bill

noun
poster advertising a show or play
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊ bɪl / (AmE), / ʃəʊ bɪl / (BrE)
a poster that advertises a theatrical show or play, typically displayed to attract audiences

Examples

Bright show bills
Brightly colored show bills were plastered all over the city to promote the new musical.
Collectors sometimes frame vintage show bills as pieces of theatrical history.
He stopped to read a show bill outside the theater before deciding to see the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playbill - a poster or printed notice announcing a play
