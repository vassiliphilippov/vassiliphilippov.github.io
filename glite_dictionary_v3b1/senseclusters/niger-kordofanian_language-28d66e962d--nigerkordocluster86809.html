Niger-Kordofanian language

noun
African language family south of the Sahara
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈnaɪ.dʒər kɔːr.də.ˈfeɪ.niː.ən ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈnaɪ.dʒə kɔː.də.ˈfeɪ.niː.ən ˈlæŋ.gwɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a family of languages spoken primarily in sub-Saharan Africa, characterized by tonal languages and including major languages such as Swahili and Fula
