draftsperson

noun
person who makes technical drawings
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftsˌpɜrsən / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftsˌpɜːsən / (BrE)
a skilled worker who prepares detailed technical drawings or plans, especially of buildings or machinery

Examples

A draftsperson draws plans
After completing her degree in architecture, she worked as a draftsperson for a major construction firm.
As a skilled draftsperson, he transformed the engineers' ideas into clear technical drawings.
The company hired a draftsperson to create precise blueprints for the new factory layout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drafter - someone who draws technical plans or designs
  • synonym: draftsman - a person who makes detailed drawings or plans
