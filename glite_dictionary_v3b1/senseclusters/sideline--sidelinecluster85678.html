sideline

noun
secondary line of business or merchandise
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌlaɪn /; / ˈsaɪd.laɪn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌlaɪn /; / ˈsaɪd.laɪn / (BrE)
an activity or business that someone does in addition to their main job, especially to earn extra income
