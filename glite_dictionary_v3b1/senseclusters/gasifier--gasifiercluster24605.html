gasifier

noun
fuel-to-gas device
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈɡæsɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a machine or device that converts solid or liquid fuel into gas for use as energy

Examples

Modern gasifiers machine
Modern gasifiers can turn wood chips into usable fuel gas.

Engineers installed a gasifier to convert coal into a cleaner-burning gas.
A small-scale gasifier can provide fuel for remote communities without access to natural gas.
