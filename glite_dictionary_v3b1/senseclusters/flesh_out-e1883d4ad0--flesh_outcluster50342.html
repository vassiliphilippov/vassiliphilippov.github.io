flesh out

verb
add details or substance; make more complete
8.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /fleʃ aʊt/ (AmE), /fleʃ aʊt/ (BrE)
to add more details or substance to something (such as an idea, plan, or argument) to make it more complete or clear
