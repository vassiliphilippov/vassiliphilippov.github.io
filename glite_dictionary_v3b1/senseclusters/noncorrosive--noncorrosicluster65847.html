noncorrosive

adjective
not damaging materials
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈroʊsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊsɪv / (BrE)
not causing damage or destruction to materials through chemical action

Examples

Noncorrosive material
The laboratory uses noncorrosive containers to store sensitive chemicals safely.
It is important to choose noncorrosive cleaning products for delicate surfaces.
The pipes are made from a noncorrosive material to prevent rust and damage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corrosive - causing damage to materials by chemical action
