folklorist

noun
expert in folklore
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊkˌlɔːrɪst / (AmE), / ˈfəʊkˌlɒrɪst /; / ˈfəʊkˌlɔːrɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies, collects, and analyzes traditional stories, customs, and beliefs of a community

Examples

The folklorist studies stories
The folklorist traveled to remote villages to collect ancient myths and legends.
As a folklorist, she published several books on regional customs and oral traditions.
Many folklorists work closely with local communities to preserve their cultural heritage.
The folklorist traveled to remote villages to document ancient myths and legends.
As a folklorist, she published several books on traditional songs and dances.
A skilled folklorist can reveal much about a culture through its stories and rituals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropologist - a person who studies human societies and cultures
  • synonym: historian - a person who studies or writes about history
