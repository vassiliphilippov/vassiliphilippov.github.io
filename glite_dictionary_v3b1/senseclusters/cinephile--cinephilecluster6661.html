cinephile

noun
movie lover
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɪnɪfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who loves or is very passionate about movies and cinema

Examples

A cinephile loves movies
As a dedicated cinephile, he collects rare films from around the world.
The local club attracts many cinephiles who enjoy discussing classic movies.
Being a cinephile means she always knows the latest releases and directors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: film buff - a person who is very interested in movies
  • synonym: moviegoer - a person who goes to see movies, especially regularly
