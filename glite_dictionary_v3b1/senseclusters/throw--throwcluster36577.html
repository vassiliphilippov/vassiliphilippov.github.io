throw

verb
(of an animal) cause rider to fall
1.5
Pronunciation: / θroʊ / (AmE), / θrəʊ / (BrE)
(of an animal) to cause a person riding it to fall off suddenly.

Examples

A horse throws a rider
He was thrown from the bull after only a few seconds.
The horse threw its rider during the jump.
The wild camel threw her off when she tried to climb on.
