disembarrassment

noun
relief from embarrassment or difficulty
9.5 8.9
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ɪm.ˈbær.ə.smənt/ /; / /dɪz.ɪm.ˈbær.ə.smənt/ (AmE), /dɪs.ɪm.ˈbær.ə.smənt/ /; / /dɪz.ɪm.ˈbær.ə.smənt/ (BrE)
something that relieves or removes embarrassment or difficulty; a means of extricating someone from an awkward situation.
