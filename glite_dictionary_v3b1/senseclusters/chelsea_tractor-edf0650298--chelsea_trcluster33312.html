Chelsea tractor

noun
large luxury SUV, esp. in cities
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛl.si ˈtræk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛl.si ˈtræk.tə / (BrE)
A large luxury sport utility vehicle (SUV), especially one used in city or suburban settings rather than for off-road driving.
