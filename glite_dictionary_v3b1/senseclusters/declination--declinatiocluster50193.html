declination

noun
downward slope or bend
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛklɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a downward slope or bend; the state or position of sloping downward

Examples

Declination of the slope
Erosion gradually increased the declination of the hillside over the years.
The mountain path became more difficult after the sharp declination near the summit.
They set up camp at the bottom of a gentle declination overlooking the valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascent - an upward slope or climb
  • synonym: incline - an upward or downward slope
