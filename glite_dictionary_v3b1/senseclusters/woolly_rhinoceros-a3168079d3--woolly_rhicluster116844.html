woolly rhinoceros

noun
extinct, thick-haired prehistoric rhinoceros
7.1 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwʊ.lɪ raɪ.ˈnɑː.sə.rəs/ (AmE), /ˈwʊ.lɪ raɪ.ˈnɒ.sə.rəs/ (BrE)
an extinct species of rhinoceros with a thick, hairy coat that lived during the Ice Age in Arctic and subarctic regions
