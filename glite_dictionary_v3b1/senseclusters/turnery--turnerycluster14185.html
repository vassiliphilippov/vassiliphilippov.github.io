turnery

noun
workshop for shaping objects on a lathe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːnəri / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnəri / (BrE)
a workshop or establishment where objects, especially of wood, are shaped on a lathe

Examples
