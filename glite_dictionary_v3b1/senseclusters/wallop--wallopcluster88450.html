wallop

verb
strike hard
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːləp / (AmE), / ˈwɒləp / (BrE)
to strike someone or something very hard

Examples

To wallop the opponent
He raised his fist and walloped his opponent in the jaw.
The little boy walloped the ball over the fence with his bat.
When the piñata fell, everyone rushed to wallop it for the candy inside.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: tap - to strike lightly
