maharani

noun
Indian queen or princess
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːni / (AmE), / ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːni / (BrE)
a queen or princess in India, especially the wife of a maharaja

Examples

The maharani of India
In the palace museum, several portraits of the former maharani are displayed.
The maharani attended the festival dressed in a beautifully embroidered sari.
The maharani was renowned for her charitable work in the region.
