Synonyms

  • turbofan engine
    5.1 0.04/m
    turbofan engine is the full technical name for this jet engine, while turbofan is the shorter, common term used in speech.
  • fanjet engine
    6.8 0.00/m
    fanjet engine describes a turbine-driven fan supplying extra air to the burner, while turbofan names the general jet engine type.
  • fanjet
    6.7 0.02/m
    fanjet can mean either the engine or an aircraft powered by it, while turbofan refers specifically to the engine type.
  • fan-jet
    6.4 0.00/m
    fan-jet is a hyphenated variant that stresses the fan's role in thrust, while turbofan is the standard modern name.
