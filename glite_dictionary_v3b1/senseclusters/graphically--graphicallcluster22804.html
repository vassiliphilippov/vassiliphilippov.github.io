graphically

adverb
in relation to visual or graphic aspects
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪkli /; / ˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses or relates to visual or graphic representations, such as charts, diagrams, or illustrations

This concept can be represented graphically to make it easier to understand.
