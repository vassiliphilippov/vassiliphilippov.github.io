interview

verb
formally question for evaluation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.tərˌvju /; / ˈɪntərˌvju / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.vjuː /; / ˈɪntəvjuː / (BrE)
to formally ask someone questions to obtain information, especially for a job, position, or for media (to be the interviewer)

Examples

To interview a candidate
She interviewed several candidates for the manager position last week.
They are interviewing potential interns throughout the month of June.
We plan to interview all the applicants before making a decision.
She has interviewed dozens of experts for her latest documentary.
The journalist will interview the mayor about the recent policy changes.
We are interviewing several candidates for the television segment tonight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interrogate - ask questions closely, aggressively, or formally
  • synonym: question - ask questions to obtain information
