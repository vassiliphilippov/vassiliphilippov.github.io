hydrofracking

noun
oil/gas extraction method
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌfrækɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌfrækɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of extracting oil or gas from underground rock by injecting high-pressure water to create fractures

Examples

Hydrofracking method
The debate over hydrofracking centers on its environmental impact and potential risks to water supplies.
Many energy companies have adopted hydrofracking to increase oil and gas production in shale formations.
Some communities have banned hydrofracking due to concerns about groundwater contamination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fracking - the process of injecting liquid at high pressure to extract oil or gas from rock
  • synonym: hydraulic fracturing - the technical term for extracting oil or gas by injecting high-pressure fluid into rock
