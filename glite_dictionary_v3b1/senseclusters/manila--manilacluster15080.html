manila

noun
strong light brown paper or cardboard
7.7 4.7
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈnɪ.lə/ (AmE), /mə.ˈnɪ.lə/ (BrE)
A strong paper or thin cardboard with a smooth light brown finish, typically made from Manila hemp, used for folders, envelopes, and other stationery.

Examples

A manila folder
