manila

noun
strong light brown paper or cardboard
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪlə / (AmE), / məˈnɪlə / (BrE)
A strong paper or thin cardboard with a smooth light brown finish, typically made from Manila hemp, used for folders, envelopes, and other stationery.

Examples

A manila folder
A stack of manila folders sat on the corner of his desk, waiting to be sorted.
She placed the documents neatly in a manila folder before the meeting.
The office uses manila envelopes to mail important papers.
