ness

noun
strip of land projecting into water
Pronunciation: / nɛs / (AmE), / nɛs / (BrE)
a narrow piece of land that extends into a sea, lake, or other body of water

Examples

A rocky ness
Birds often nest along the rocky ness jutting into the North Sea.
The small coastal town was built on a ness overlooking the bay.
You can see the old lighthouse at the tip of the ness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peninsula - a piece of land almost surrounded by water or projecting out into a body of water
  • synonym: promontory - a high point of land that juts out into water
