buprestid

adjective
of jewel beetles
Pronunciation: / bjuˈprɛstɪd / (AmE), / bjuˈprɛstɪd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of beetles of the family Buprestidae, known as jewel beetles.

Examples

Buprestid beetle larvae
The buprestid larvae bore into tree trunks, sometimes damaging valuable timber.
Collectors prize the metallic colors of buprestid beetles from tropical regions.
A buprestid species was recently discovered in the rainforest, dazzling scientists with its iridescent wings.

Related Terms
