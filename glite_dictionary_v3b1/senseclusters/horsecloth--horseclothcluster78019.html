horsecloth

noun
cloth covering for a horse
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsklɒθ / (BrE)
A cloth used to cover or protect a horse, especially for warmth or as part of its trappings.

Examples

A horsecloth cover
The decorative horsecloth at the parade was embroidered with the owner's initials.
The stable master ensured each horsecloth was clean and dry for the animals.
When the weather turned cold, she placed a horsecloth over her mare before riding home.
