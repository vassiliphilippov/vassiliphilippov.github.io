tartlet

noun
small filled pastry shell
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrt.lɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɑːt.lɪt / (BrE)
A small pastry shell filled with sweet or savory ingredients, often served as a canapé or dessert.

Examples

A tartlet with fruit
Each tartlet was filled with lemon curd and topped with fresh fruit.
She arranged the mini tartlets on a tray for the party guests.
The chef prepared a savory mushroom tartlet as an appetizer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tart - a pastry dish with a filling over a pastry base and open top
