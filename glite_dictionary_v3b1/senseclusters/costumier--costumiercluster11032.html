costumier

noun
someone who designs or supplies costumes
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːstuˈmiːər / (AmE), / ˌkɒstjʊˈmɪə / (BrE)
a person who designs, makes, or supplies costumes, especially for theatrical productions or events

Examples

The costumier designs costumes
After years as a costumier, she opened her own costume rental shop.
The costumier created elaborate outfits for each character in the play.
The film's authentic period clothing was sourced from a renowned costumier in London.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costume designer - a person responsible for creating costumes for a production
