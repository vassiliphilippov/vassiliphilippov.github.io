nonet

noun
group of nine musicians
7.2
Pronunciation: / noʊˈnɛt / (AmE), / nɒˈnɛt / (BrE)
a group of nine musicians or a musical composition written for nine parts or instruments

Examples

A string nonet
The orchestra performed a beautiful nonet composed for strings and woodwinds.
She joined a nonet that specialized in contemporary chamber music.
The new nonet was praised for its harmonious blend of instruments.
