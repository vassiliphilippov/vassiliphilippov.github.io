collegially

adverb
in a cooperative manner
6.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈliːdʒiəli / (AmE), / kəˈliːdʒiəli / (BrE)
in a friendly, cooperative, and equal way among colleagues or group members

Examples

Discuss collegially together
The committee members discussed the proposal collegially and reached a consensus quickly.
She prefers to work collegially rather than compete with her teammates.
Decisions were made collegially to ensure everyone felt included in the process.
The board members discussed the proposal collegially before making a final decision.
The team worked collegially to solve the complex problem together.
All departments met collegially to ensure everyone had input on the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperatively - in a way that involves working together with others
  • antonym: individually - in a way that is done alone or separately
