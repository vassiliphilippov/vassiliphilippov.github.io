on tap

adjective
available from a tap or faucet
2.8
Pronunciation: / ɑn tæp / (AmE), / ɒn tæp / (BrE)
(of liquids, especially drinks such as beer, or water) available to be served directly from a tap or faucet.

Examples

Beer on tap
Hot and cold running water are on tap in every room of the hotel.
In our new kitchen, we have filtered water on tap at all times.
The pub has several different beers on tap for customers to try.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on draft - of beer, served directly from a barrel or tank rather than from a bottle or can
