American mastodon

noun
extinct North American mastodon species
5.0
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈmæs.təˌdɑn / (AmE), / əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən ˈmæs.təˌdɒn / (BrE)
an extinct species of mastodon (Mammut americanum) that lived in North America during the late Pleistocene epoch

Examples

Unlike mammoths, the American mastodon had distinctive cone-shaped teeth.
