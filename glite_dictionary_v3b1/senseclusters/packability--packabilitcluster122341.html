packability

noun
ease of packing
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpækəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌpækəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being easy to pack or fit into a space for storage or transport

Examples

Jacket packability
The packability of this jacket makes it perfect for travelers with limited luggage space.
When choosing camping gear, high packability is often just as important as durability.
Thanks to its excellent packability, the sleeping bag fits easily into a small backpack.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bulkiness - the quality of being large and difficult to handle or pack
  • synonym: portability - the quality of being easy to carry or move
