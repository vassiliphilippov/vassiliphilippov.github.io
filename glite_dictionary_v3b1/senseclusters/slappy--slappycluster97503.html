slappy

adjective
playfully hitting
Pronunciation: / ˈslæpi / (AmE), / ˈslæpi / (BrE)
playful or characterized by giving light, quick slaps or hits

Examples

A slappy puppy
The puppy was slappy and full of energy.
Their slappy game made everyone in the room laugh.
He gave his friend a slappy high five after the win.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playful - full of fun and high spirits
  • antonym: rough - not gentle or careful in action
