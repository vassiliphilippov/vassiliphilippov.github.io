metering

noun
measuring usage
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmiːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of measuring, recording, or controlling the amount or flow of something, such as water, gas, electricity, or other resources

Examples

Water metering
Accurate metering helps households monitor their energy consumption.
The city installed new water metering systems to track usage more efficiently.
Modern metering technology allows companies to bill customers based on actual usage.
The city introduced metering to control water usage during the drought.
Traffic metering at the highway on-ramp helps reduce congestion during rush hour.
The new electricity metering system allows customers to track their energy consumption in real time.

  • synonym: regulation - the act of controlling or managing something
  • synonym: restriction - the act of limiting or controlling something
