bond

noun
force linking atoms
Pronunciation: / bɑnd / (AmE), / bɒnd / (BrE)
A strong force of attraction that holds atoms together in a molecule or crystal.

Examples

A chemical bond
Breaking the chemical bond between atoms requires energy.
In a water molecule, each hydrogen atom forms a bond with the oxygen atom.
The strength of a bond affects the stability of the compound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemical bond - a force that holds atoms or ions together in a molecule or crystal
