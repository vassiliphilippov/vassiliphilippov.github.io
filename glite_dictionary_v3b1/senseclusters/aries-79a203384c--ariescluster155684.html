Aries

proper noun
zodiac sign
Pronunciation: / ˈɛriˌiz / (AmE), / ˈɛəriːz / (BrE)
the first sign of the zodiac, representing people born between March 21 and April 19

Examples

Aries horoscope sign
My sister is an Aries and enjoys new challenges.
People born under Aries are often described as energetic and adventurous.
The horoscope said that Aries would have a lucky week ahead.
