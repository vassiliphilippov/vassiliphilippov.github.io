maya

noun
illusion of reality
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːjə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːjə / (BrE)
the illusion or appearance of the world, especially as understood in certain spiritual or philosophical traditions

Examples

The maya illusion
In some Eastern philosophies, the world is considered maya, hiding the ultimate truth.
The concept of maya suggests that what we perceive is not the true reality.
He studied ancient texts to better understand the meaning of maya in spiritual teachings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illusion - something that appears real but is not
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
