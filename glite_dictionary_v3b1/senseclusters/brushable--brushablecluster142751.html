brushable

adjective
can be brushed
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be brushed or suitable for brushing

Examples

Brushable hair
After using the new conditioner, her hair was soft and brushable.
The paint is brushable, making it easy to apply with a standard brush.
He prefers brushable fabrics that do not get matted easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combed - able to be combed
  • antonym: tangled - difficult or impossible to brush
