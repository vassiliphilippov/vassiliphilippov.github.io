Synonyms

    4.4 5.01/m
    relish means enjoying something with strong pleasure and often slowly, while savor means taking time to fully appreciate it.
  • delight
    1.0 4.47/m
    delight is strong joy or pleasure from something, while savor focuses on slowly enjoying and appreciating it.
  • enjoy
    1.0 170/m
    enjoy means take pleasure in something in general, while savor means to slow down and fully appreciate its taste or the moment.
