progressivist

adjective
favoring reform
7.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈɡrɛsɪvɪst / (AmE), / prəˈɡrɛsɪvɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to progressive ideas, policies, or reforms, especially in politics or education

Examples

Progressivist policies
The mayor's progressivist approach led to new policies on renewable energy and public transportation.
Many educators support progressivist methods that encourage creativity and critical thinking in the classroom.
The party's progressivist platform attracted young voters who wanted significant social change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - opposed to change; favoring traditional views
  • synonym: progressive - favoring or implementing social reform or new ideas
