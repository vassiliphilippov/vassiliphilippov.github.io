anterolaterally

adverb
toward front and side
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊˈlætərəli / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊˈlætərəli / (BrE)
in a direction that is both toward the front and toward the side

Examples

Runs anterolaterally from cord
The nerve runs anterolaterally from the spinal cord to reach the limb.
The tumor was located anterolaterally to the main artery, making surgery challenging.
The muscle fibers extend anterolaterally from the center of the chest.
