monocyclic

adjective
having one cycle
9.0
Pronunciation: /mə.noʊ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk/ /; / /mɑː.nə.ˈsaɪ.klɪk/ (AmE), /mə.nɒ.ˈsaɪ.klɪk/ /; / /mɒ.nə.ˈsaɪ.klɪk/ (BrE)
having exactly one cycle, such as a single closed path in a graph or a single life or disease cycle in a season or year
