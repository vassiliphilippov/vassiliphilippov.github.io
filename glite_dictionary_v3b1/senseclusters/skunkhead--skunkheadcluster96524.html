skunkhead

noun
person with streaked hair
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈskʌŋkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈskʌŋkˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person whose hair is streaked with white or another color, resembling the pattern of a skunk

Examples

White-striped skunkhead
After dyeing a white stripe down the middle, his friends called him a skunkhead.
The teenager embraced her new look as a proud skunkhead at school.
People sometimes stare when they see a skunkhead walking down the street.
