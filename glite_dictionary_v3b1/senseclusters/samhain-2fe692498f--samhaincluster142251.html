Samhain

proper noun
Gaelic month name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊwɪn / (AmE), / ˈsaʊwɪn / (BrE)
the name of the month corresponding roughly to November in the traditional Irish and Scottish Gaelic calendars

Examples

Samhain month
In ancient Ireland, Samhain marked the end of the harvest season and the beginning of winter.
Many traditional festivals are still celebrated during Samhain in parts of Scotland and Ireland.
The calendar month of Samhain was important for planning agricultural activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: November - the eleventh month of the year in the Gregorian calendar
