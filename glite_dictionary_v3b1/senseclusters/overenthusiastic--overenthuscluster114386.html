overenthusiastic

adjective
showing excessive enthusiasm
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˌθuziˈæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˌθjuːziˈæstɪk / (BrE)
Excessively enthusiastic to an unreasonable or unnecessary degree.

Examples

An overenthusiastic greeting
Her overenthusiastic greeting startled the new employees on their first day.
If you are overenthusiastic about your ideas, colleagues may find you overwhelming.
The overenthusiastic fans rushed onto the field before the game had even ended.
