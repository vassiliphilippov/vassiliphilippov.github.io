universal

noun
mechanical coupling allowing multidirectional movement
Pronunciation: / juːnɪˈvɜrsəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl / (BrE)
a coupling for connecting two rotating shafts at an angle, allowing movement in all directions; also known as a universal joint

Examples

A universal joint
A faulty universal can cause vibrations while driving up steep roads.
Mechanics inspected the universal to ensure the driveshaft could rotate smoothly.
The car's universal was replaced after it started making unusual noises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: universal joint - A joint or coupling that transmits rotary motion between shafts not in line with each other
