petrified

adjective
turned to stone
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛtrəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɛtrɪfaɪd / (BrE)
changed into stone or a stony substance, especially through a natural process over a long time

Examples

Petrified wood
The museum displayed a petrified log that was millions of years old.
Scientists studied the petrified remains of ancient plants found in the valley.
We visited a forest where the trees had become petrified over centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fossilized - preserved in the form of a fossil
