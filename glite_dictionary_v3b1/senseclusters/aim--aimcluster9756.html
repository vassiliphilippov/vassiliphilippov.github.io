aim

verb
to direct towards a target
Pronunciation: / eɪm / (AmE), / eɪm / (BrE)
to point or direct a weapon, object, or device towards a target or direction

Examples

To aim a gun
It's dangerous to aim a loaded gun at anyone, even as a joke.
She aimed the water hose at the flowers to give them a good soaking.
The photographer carefully aimed his camera at the hummingbird.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fire - to discharge a weapon at a target
  • synonym: point - to direct or aim an object towards something
