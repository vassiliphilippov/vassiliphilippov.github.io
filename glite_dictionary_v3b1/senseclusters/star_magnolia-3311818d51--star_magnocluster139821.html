star magnolia

noun
ornamental shrub with star-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / stɑr məgˈnoʊliə / (AmE), / stɑː məgˈnəʊliə / (BrE)
A small, deciduous shrub or tree native to Japan, known for its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom before the leaves appear; widely grown as an ornamental plant.
