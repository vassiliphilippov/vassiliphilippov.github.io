bastion

noun
defender of a principle or cause
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstʃən / (AmE), / ˈbæstɪən / (BrE)
an organization or group strongly defending or upholding particular principles, attitudes, or activities

Examples

Bastion of freedom
For decades, the school was regarded as a bastion of academic excellence.
The newspaper became a bastion of free speech during the political crisis.
This organization remains the last bastion of traditional values in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulwark - a strong support or protection for something
  • synonym: stronghold - a place or group that is strongly defended or upholds something
