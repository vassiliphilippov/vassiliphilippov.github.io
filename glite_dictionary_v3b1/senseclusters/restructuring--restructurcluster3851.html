Synonyms

  • reorganization
    3.7 3.84/m
    reorganization makes major changes to an organization's structure, while restructuring is a broader process of reorganizing to improve how it works.
  • reconfiguration
    5.6 0.66/m
    reconfiguration arranges parts or systems in a new way, while restructuring refers to larger organizational change in companies or systems.
  • shake-up
    4.3 0.64/m
    a shake-up is a sudden, often drastic reorganization of staff or rules, while restructuring is a formal process to improve organization.
  • realignment
    4.6 0.43/m
    realignment changes positions or partnerships to better match goals, while restructuring covers larger reorganization of systems or departments.
  • revamp
    4.9 3.16/m
    revamp improves or renovates methods, products, or systems, while restructuring focuses on changing an organization's structure or operation.
