uncultivatable

adjective
not suitable for cultivation
2.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌkʌltɪˈveɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˌkʌltɪˈveɪtəbəl / (BrE)
(of land) not suitable for cultivation or farming.

Examples

Uncultivatable land
Government programs focus on reforesting uncultivatable land for environmental restoration.
Much of the desert is considered uncultivatable due to its dryness and poor soil.
The steep, rocky hillside was completely uncultivatable by traditional farming methods.
