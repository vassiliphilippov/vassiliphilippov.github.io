Judaistic

adjective
relating to Judaizers
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒuːdiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒuːdiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to Judaizers or to doctrines that emphasize strict observance of Mosaic law, especially in Christian theological contexts

Examples

Judaistic practices
The council debated whether the Judaistic practices should be required for new members.
Some early Christian communities held Judaistic beliefs about dietary laws and rituals.
His writings often challenge Judaistic interpretations of religious texts.
