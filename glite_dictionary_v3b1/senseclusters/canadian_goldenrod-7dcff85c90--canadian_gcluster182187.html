Canadian goldenrod

noun
tall North American plant with yellow flower clusters
5.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɡoʊldə(n)ˌrɑd / (AmE), / kəˈneɪdiən ˈɡəʊldə(n)ˌrɒd / (BrE)
a tall North American plant with arching branches and large clusters of small yellow flowers, often considered a weed

Examples

Canadian goldenrod flowers
Bees are attracted to Canadian goldenrod during late summer and autumn.
Many gardeners remove Canadian goldenrod because it can quickly become invasive.
