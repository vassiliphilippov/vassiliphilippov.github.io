yamp

noun
edible wild plant
Pronunciation: / jæmp / (AmE), / jæmp / (BrE)
a North American wild plant with an edible root, also known as biscuitroot, traditionally used as food by Native Americans

Examples

Wild yamp root
Early settlers learned to eat yamp from local Native American tribes.
The root of the yamp can be cooked or eaten raw in salads.
Foraging for yamp was an important tradition in some indigenous communities.
