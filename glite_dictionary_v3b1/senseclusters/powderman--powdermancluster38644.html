powderman

noun
explosives handler
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.dər.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.də.mæn/ (BrE)
a person who handles, uses, makes, or sells explosives or gunpowder, especially in mining or construction

Examples

Mining powderman
The experienced powderman ensured all safety procedures were followed before the blast.
During the tunnel construction, the powderman played a crucial role in breaking through rock.
Every powderman must be trained to handle explosives with extreme caution.
The powderman carefully measured the gunpowder before loading it into the mine's blasting holes.
In the old days, every army relied on a skilled powderman to supply their cannons.
After years of dangerous work, the experienced powderman retired from the quarry.
