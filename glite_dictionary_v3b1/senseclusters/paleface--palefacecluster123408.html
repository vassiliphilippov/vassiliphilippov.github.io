paleface

noun
derogatory slang for a white person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪlˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlfeɪs / (BrE)
(slang, often offensive) A derogatory or mocking term, historically used by some North American Indigenous peoples to refer to a white person.

Examples

The paleface stranger
He was called a paleface by the locals in the story.
Some old Western movies use the term paleface to refer to European settlers.
The chief glared at the paleface who had wandered into their camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: white person - a person of European descent with light skin color
