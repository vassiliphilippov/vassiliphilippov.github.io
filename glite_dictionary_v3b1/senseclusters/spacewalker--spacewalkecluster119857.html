spacewalker

noun
astronaut active outside a spacecraft
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
an astronaut who carries out activities outside a spacecraft in outer space

Examples

A spacewalker in suit
Each spacewalker must wear a special suit to survive in the vacuum of space.
The spacewalker skillfully repaired the satellite during her time outside the station.
Two spacewalkers floated above the Earth as they installed new equipment on the ISS.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronaut - a person trained to travel in a spacecraft
  • synonym: cosmonaut - a Russian astronaut
