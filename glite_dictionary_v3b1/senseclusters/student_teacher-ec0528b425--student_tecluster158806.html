student teacher

noun
trainee teacher working under supervision
4.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstuː.dənt ˈtiː.tʃər/ /; / /ˈstjuː.dənt ˈtiː.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈstjuː.dənt ˈtiː.tʃə/ /; / /ˈstʃuː.dənt ˈtiː.tʃə/ (BrE)
a college or university student who teaches in a school under the supervision of a certified teacher as part of their training to become a qualified teacher
