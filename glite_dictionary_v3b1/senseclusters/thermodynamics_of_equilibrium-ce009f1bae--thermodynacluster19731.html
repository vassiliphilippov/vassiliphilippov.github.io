thermodynamics of equilibrium

noun
study of thermodynamics in equilibrium states
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪks əv ˌiːkwəˈlɪbrɪəm / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks əv ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbrɪəm / (BrE)
the branch of thermodynamics concerned with the study and characterization of systems in thermal equilibrium

Examples

Thermodynamics of equilibrium study
Advanced students learn how the thermodynamics of equilibrium differs from that of rapidly changing processes.
Much of classical physics relies on the thermodynamics of equilibrium to describe stable systems.
