gift from above

noun
unexpected blessing or good fortune
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪft frəm əˈbʌv / (AmE), / ɡɪft frəm əˈbʌv / (BrE)
an unexpected blessing or piece of good fortune, often attributed to divine or supernatural intervention

Examples

A gift from above
After years of trying, their baby felt like a true gift from above.
Finding the lost dog was a gift from above for the worried family.
The unexpected funding was a gift from above for the struggling project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blessing - a beneficial thing for which one is grateful
  • antonym: curse - something considered to bring bad luck or harm
