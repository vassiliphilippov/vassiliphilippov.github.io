corbiestep

noun
step-shaped feature atop a gable wall
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.biˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.bi.stɛp / (BrE)
(architecture) A step-like projection forming the top edge of a gable wall, characteristic of some traditional buildings.

A corbiestep wall
