procedural

adjective
generated by algorithms
6.4
Pronunciation: /prə.ˈsiː.dʒə.rəl/ (AmE), /prə.ˈsiː.dʒə.rəl/ (BrE)
(in computing and digital media) produced, controlled, or generated by an automated algorithmic procedure rather than created manually.

Examples

Procedural generation
The game's vast landscapes were built using procedural generation to give players unique maps every session.
Artists often combine hand-made models with procedural textures to speed up asset production for films.
Researchers trained an algorithm that produces procedural music adapting to the player's actions in real time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: algorithmic - relating to or using a set of rules or calculations to solve problems or produce results
  • antonym: handcrafted - made or created by hand rather than by automated processes
