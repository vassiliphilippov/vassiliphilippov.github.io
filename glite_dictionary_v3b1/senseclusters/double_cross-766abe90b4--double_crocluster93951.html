double cross

noun
an act of betrayal
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl krɔs / (AmE), / ˈdʌbl krɒs / (BrE)
an act of betraying someone, especially after pretending to support or work with them

Examples

A double cross act
After the double cross, no one in the organization trusted him again.
She planned a double cross that would leave her rivals out of the deal.
The final double cross shocked everyone involved in the scheme.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betrayal - the act of betraying someone or something
  • antonym: loyalty - the quality of being loyal to someone or something
