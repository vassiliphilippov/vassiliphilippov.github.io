spaghetti and meatballs

noun
Italian-American pasta dish with meatballs
Pronunciation: / spəˈɡɛti ənd ˈmitˌbɔlz / (AmE), / spəˈɡɛti ənd ˈmiːtbɔːlz / (BrE)
An Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti served with meatballs in a tomato sauce.

Examples

Spaghetti and meatballs dinner
Every Sunday, my grandmother would cook spaghetti and meatballs for the whole family.
