encrustation

noun
hard outer layer covering something
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.krəˈsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛn.krʌsˈteɪ.ʃən /; / ˌɛn.krʌˈsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a hard outer layer that forms on something, or the process of forming such a layer
