inapprehensive

adjective
not anxious or fearful
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv / (BrE)
not feeling anxious, fearful, or worried about something

Examples

Inapprehensive about danger
Despite the loud thunder, he remained inapprehensive and continued reading his book.
The team was surprisingly inapprehensive before the big match, showing no signs of nerves.
She walked into the interview inapprehensive, confident in her preparation and skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apprehensive - anxious or fearful that something bad will happen
  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anxiety
  • synonym: unconcerned - not worried or anxious
