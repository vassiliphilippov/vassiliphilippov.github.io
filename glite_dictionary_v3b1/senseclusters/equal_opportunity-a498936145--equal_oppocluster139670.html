equal opportunity

noun
fairness in access to opportunities
3.1 2.25/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàiÀêkw…ôl …ëÀêp…ôrÀàtun…ôti / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêkw…ôl …íp…ôÀàtjuÀên…ôti / (BrE)
