companionable

adjective
friendly; pleasant company
4.8
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpænjənəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈpænjənəbl / (BrE)
friendly and pleasant to be with; suggestive of companionship or suitability for company

Examples

A companionable silence
A companionable silence filled the room as they read their books together.
She found her new roommate to be warm, funny, and highly companionable.
Though shy at first, the dog soon became companionable and enjoyed being around people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociable - enjoying the company of others; friendly
  • antonym: unsociable - not enjoying the company of others; unfriendly
