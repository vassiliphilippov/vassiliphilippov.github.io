journalist's privilege

noun
legal right of journalists to keep sources confidential
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːnəlɪsts ˈprɪvəlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːnəlɪsts ˈprɪvɪlɪdʒ / (BrE)
the legal right of a journalist to refuse to reveal confidential information or sources in certain circumstances
