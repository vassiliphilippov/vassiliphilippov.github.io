permissively

adverb
in a permissive manner
1.0
Pronunciation: / pərˈmɪsɪvli / (AmE), / pəˈmɪsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that allows or tolerates a great deal of freedom or leniency.

Examples

Raised permissively
He was raised permissively, with few rules or restrictions imposed at home.
The company interpreted the regulations permissively, opting for the most flexible approach allowed.
The teacher managed her classroom permissively, allowing students to express themselves freely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leniently - in a lenient manner
  • antonym: strictly - in a strict manner
