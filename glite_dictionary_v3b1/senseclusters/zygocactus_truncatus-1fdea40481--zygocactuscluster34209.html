Zygocactus truncatus

noun
South American houseplant cactus with jointed stems
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌzaɪɡoʊˈkæktəs trʌŋˈkeɪtəs / (AmE), / ˌzaɪɡəʊˈkaktəs trʌŋˈkeɪtəs / (BrE)
A South American cactus with jointed stems and usually red flowers, commonly grown as a houseplant; sometimes classified under the genus Schlumbergera.
