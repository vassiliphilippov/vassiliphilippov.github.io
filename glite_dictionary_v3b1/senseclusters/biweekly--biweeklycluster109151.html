biweekly

adjective
happening two times a week
Pronunciation: / baɪˈwikli / (AmE), / baɪˈwiːkli / (BrE)
Occurring twice each week; happening two times in a week.

Examples

Biweekly meetings
He receives biweekly updates from his project team to stay informed.
Our gym classes are biweekly, so you can attend on both Tuesdays and Fridays.
The committee holds biweekly meetings every Monday and Thursday.
