preprocessing

noun
initial data processing
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈprɑːsɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈprɒsɛsɪŋ /; / ˌpriːˈprɒsɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of preparing or processing data or material before it is analyzed or used

Examples

Data preprocessing
Image preprocessing is essential to improve the quality of photos before applying filters.
In machine learning, preprocessing helps clean and organize data for better model performance.
The brain performs preprocessing of sounds to identify pitch and volume before we understand speech.
Effective preprocessing can greatly improve the accuracy of machine learning models.
The preprocessing of raw images is essential before they are analyzed by the software.
Data preprocessing often involves cleaning and organizing information for better results.
