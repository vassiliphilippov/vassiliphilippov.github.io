determinant

noun
site on antigen where antibody binds
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɝmɪnənt / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnənt / (BrE)
the specific region on the surface of an antigen molecule to which an antibody binds

Examples

Antigen determinant
Each determinant on the antigen can elicit a specific reaction from the immune cells.
Researchers mapped several determinants involved in the immune response to the protein.
The antibody recognizes a unique determinant on the virus's surface.
