plasma

noun
liquid part of blood or lymph

the pale, liquid component of blood and lymph, in which blood cells are suspended and which does not contain cells itself

Examples

Blood plasma
Plasma makes up more than half of the blood's volume in the human body.
After donating blood, only the plasma is separated for medical use.
The doctor collected a sample of plasma to test for antibodies.
