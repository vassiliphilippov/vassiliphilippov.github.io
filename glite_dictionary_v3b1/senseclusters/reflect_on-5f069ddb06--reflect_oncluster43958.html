reflect on

verb
think deeply about
Pronunciation: / rɪˈflɛkt ɑn / (AmE), / rɪˈflɛkt ɒn / (BrE)
to think carefully and deeply about something

Examples

To reflect on feedback
After the meeting, she took some time to reflect on the feedback she had received.
He often reflects on his childhood when making important decisions.
Traveling allows people to step back and reflect on their lives from a new perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contemplate - to think about something deeply and thoroughly
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
