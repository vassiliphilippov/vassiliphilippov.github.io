constitutionality

noun
legality under constitution
Pronunciation: / kənˌstɪtuːʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / kənˌstɪtjuːʃəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being in accordance with a constitution, especially a national constitution

Examples

The constitutionality of law
The Supreme Court will decide the constitutionality of the proposed amendment.
Lawyers questioned the constitutionality of the government's surveillance program.
Debates about the constitutionality of the new tax law continued for months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legality - the state of being legal or lawful
  • antonym: unconstitutionality - the state of not being allowed by a constitution
