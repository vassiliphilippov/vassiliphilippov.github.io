baking soda

noun
white powder used in baking and cleaning
2.8 1.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪ.kɪŋ ˈsoʊ.də/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪ.kɪŋ ˈsəʊ.də/ (BrE)
a white powder (sodium bicarbonate) used in baking, cleaning, or as an antacid

Examples

Add baking soda to batter
Add one teaspoon of baking soda to the cake batter for a lighter texture.
Many people use baking soda as a natural remedy for heartburn.
She cleaned the stained sink by scrubbing it with baking soda and water.
If your recipe calls for baking soda, be sure not to confuse it with baking powder.
Many people keep a box of baking soda in the fridge to absorb odors.
You can use baking soda to clean tough stains from kitchen surfaces.
