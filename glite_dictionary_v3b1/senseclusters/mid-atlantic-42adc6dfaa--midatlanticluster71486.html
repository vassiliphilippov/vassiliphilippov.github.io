Mid-Atlantic

proper noun
region in eastern United States
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪd əˈtlæntɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪd ətˈlæntɪk / (BrE)
a geographical region in the eastern United States, typically including states such as New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, and sometimes others

Examples

The Mid-Atlantic region
Many universities in the Mid-Atlantic attract students from across the country.
She moved from California to the Mid-Atlantic for her new job.
The climate in the Mid-Atlantic region can vary significantly from north to south.
