damn fool

interjection
insulting exclamation
Pronunciation: / dæm fuːl / (AmE), / dæm fuːl / (BrE)
an exclamation used to insult or criticize someone for acting stupidly or carelessly

Examples

Damn fool! shouted angrily
He called his friend a damn fool after she forgot her keys for the third time.
When the plan failed, the manager shouted, 'Damn fool! You should have double-checked the details.'
She muttered 'damn fool' under her breath when the cyclist ran the red light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
  • synonym: idiot - a person who acts in a very stupid way
