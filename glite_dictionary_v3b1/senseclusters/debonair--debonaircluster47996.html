debonair

adjective
charming and sophisticated (of a man)
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛbəˈnɛr / (AmE), / ˌdɛbəˈneə /; / ˌdɛb.əˈneə / (BrE)
(of a man) confident, stylish, and charming, especially in a sophisticated and relaxed way
