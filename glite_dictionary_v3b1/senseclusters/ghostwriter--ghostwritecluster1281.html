ghostwriter

noun
someone who writes for another credited as author
3.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgoʊst.raɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈgəʊst.raɪ.tə/ (BrE)

Examples

Many bestselling books are actually written by a ghostwriter, not the person credited.
