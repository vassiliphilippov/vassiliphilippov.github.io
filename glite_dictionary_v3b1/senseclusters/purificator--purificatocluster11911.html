purificator

noun
white linen cloth for wiping the chalice
5.1 9.9
Pronunciation: /pjʊr.ɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.tər/ /; / /pɜːr.ɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /pjuː.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.tə/ /; / /pjʊə.rɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
