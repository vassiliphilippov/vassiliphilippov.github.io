cow

noun
unpleasant or disliked woman (offensive, informal)
Pronunciation: / kaʊ / (AmE), / kaʊ / (BrE)
(informal, offensive) An unpleasant or disliked woman, especially one who is large or seen as difficult.

Examples

A mean cow
He called his boss a cow after she yelled at him in front of everyone.
I can't believe she said that—what a cow!
Stop being such a cow and help us for once.
