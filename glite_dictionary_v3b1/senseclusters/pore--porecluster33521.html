pore

noun
small opening in plant leaves or stems
1.2 5.8
Pronunciation: / pɔr / (AmE), / pɔː / (BrE)
a minute opening in the epidermis of a leaf or stem, through which gases and water vapor pass

Examples

Leaf pore
Scientists examined the pores under a microscope to study how the leaves breathe.
The plant's stem contains specialized pores that allow gas exchange with the atmosphere.
Water vapor escapes through each pore on the leaf surface during transpiration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stoma - a microscopic opening in the epidermis of plants, similar to a pore
