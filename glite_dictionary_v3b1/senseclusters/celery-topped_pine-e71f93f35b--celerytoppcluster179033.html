celery-topped pine

noun
Tasmanian evergreen coniferous tree
7.1 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈse.lə.riː.tɑːpt paɪn/ (AmE), /ˈse.lə.riː.tɒpt paɪn/ (BrE)
