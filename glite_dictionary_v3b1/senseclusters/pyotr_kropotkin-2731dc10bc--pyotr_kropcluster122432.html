Pyotr Kropotkin

proper noun
Russian anarchist and philosopher
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpjoʊtər kroʊˈpɒtkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpjɒtə kropˈɒtkɪn / (BrE)
(1842–1921) Russian anarchist, geographer, and philosopher, known for his advocacy of anarchist communism and influential writings on mutual aid.
