guarani

noun
Paraguayan unit of currency
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡwɑːrəˈniː / (AmE), / ɡwɑːˈrɑːni / (BrE)
the basic unit of currency in Paraguay, equal to 100 centimos.

Examples

The guarani currency
He exchanged his dollars for guarani at the airport.
Prices in Paraguay are usually listed in guarani rather than centimos.
The shopkeeper handed me my change in guarani.
