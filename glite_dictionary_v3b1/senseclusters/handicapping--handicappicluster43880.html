handicapping

noun
predicting outcomes
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhændiˌkæpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændikæpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of assessing or predicting the likely outcome of an event, especially in sports or politics, often for betting purposes

Examples

Handicapping election results
He spent the afternoon handicapping the upcoming horse races for his friends.
Political analysts are busy handicapping the chances of each candidate in the election.
Many fans enjoy handicapping games before placing their bets online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betting - the act of risking money on the outcome of an event
  • synonym: forecasting - predicting future events or results
