weatherization

noun
protection from weather
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛðərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌwɛðəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of protecting vehicles, equipment, or other objects from damage caused by weather or water

Examples

Vehicle weatherization
The city offered free weatherization services to help protect public buses from harsh winter conditions.
Proper weatherization of outdoor machinery can extend its lifespan and reduce maintenance costs.
Before hurricane season, the team completed the weatherization of all critical communication equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulation - material or process used to prevent heat loss or gain
  • synonym: waterproofing - making something resistant to water
