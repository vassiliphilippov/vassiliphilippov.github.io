makeup

noun
composition of someone or something
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪkʌp / (BrE)
the combination of qualities or components that form or constitute a person, group, or thing

Examples

The makeup of a team
Genetic factors play a large role in determining the makeup of an individual.
The cultural makeup of the city has changed significantly over the last few decades.
The team’s makeup included both experienced professionals and recent graduates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - the way in which something is put together or arranged
  • synonym: structure - the arrangement and relationship of parts in something complex
