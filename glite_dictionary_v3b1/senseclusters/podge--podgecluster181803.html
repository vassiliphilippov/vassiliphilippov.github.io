podge

noun
plump person or thing
Pronunciation: / pɑdʒ / (AmE), / pɒdʒ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing that is plump or chubby

Examples

A chubby podge
As a child, he was often called podge by his classmates.
The kitten grew into a little podge after being spoiled with treats.
She laughed and hugged her podge of a dog tightly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skinny - very thin
