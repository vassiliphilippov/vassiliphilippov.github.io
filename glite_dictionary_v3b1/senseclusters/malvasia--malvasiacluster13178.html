malvasia

noun
sweet wine grape variety
Pronunciation: / mælˈveɪziə / (AmE), / malˈvasɪə / (BrE)
A variety of grape used in the production of sweet wines, especially malmsey.

Examples

The malvasia grape
Many dessert wines are crafted from the aromatic malvasia grape.
She toured the winery and tasted a rare bottle made exclusively from malvasia.
The vineyard is famous for its centuries-old malvasia vines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malmsey - a sweet fortified wine made chiefly from malvasia grapes
