ally with

verb
formally unite with someone or a group
Pronunciation: / əˈlaɪ wɪθ / (AmE), / əˈlaɪ wɪð / (BrE)
to unite or cooperate formally with another person, group, or country, usually for a common purpose

Examples

To ally with a country
During the conflict, she allied with former competitors to achieve her goal.
Several small nations decided to ally with their neighbors for mutual protection.
The company chose to ally with a local supplier to expand its reach.
