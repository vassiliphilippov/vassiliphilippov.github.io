trivet

noun
three-legged metal stand for hot pots or pans
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪvət /; / ˈtrɪvɪt / (AmE), / ˈtrɪvɪt / (BrE)
a small stand used to support a hot dish or pot, either to protect a surface or for cooking over a fire
