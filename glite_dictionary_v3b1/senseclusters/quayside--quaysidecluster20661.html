quayside

noun
area beside quay
4.8 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀåsa…™d /; / ÀàkiÀêsa…™d / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀêsa…™d / (BrE)
the area or walkway next to a quay, often used for loading ships, walking, shopping, or relaxing by the water
