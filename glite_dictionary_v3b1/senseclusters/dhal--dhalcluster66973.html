dhal

noun
tropical leguminous herb
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑl / (AmE), / dɑːl / (BrE)
A tropical woody herb (Cajanus cajan) with showy yellow flowers and flat pods, widely cultivated in tropical regions.

Examples

A dhal plant
Farmers rotate dhal crops with other legumes to enrich the soil.
Showy yellow flowers make the dhal particularly attractive during the summer season.
The dhal plant is often grown for its nutritious seeds in South Asia.

Related Terms
