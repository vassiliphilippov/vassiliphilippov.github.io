chokingly

adverb
in a suffocating way
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes or resembles choking, or makes it hard to breathe or speak (due to physical or emotional reasons)

Examples

Air chokingly thick
The air in the tunnel was chokingly thick with dust after the collapse.
She found the perfume in the elevator chokingly strong and had to step out.
The restaurant became chokingly smoky when the grill caught fire.
He spoke chokingly as he tried to hold back his tears during the farewell.
The announcement was made chokingly, with emotion clear in her trembling voice.
She laughed chokingly, unable to catch her breath after hearing the good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathlessly - in a way that leaves someone short of breath
  • synonym: brokenly - in a way that is interrupted or not smooth, often due to emotion
  • antonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed manner
  • synonym: suffocatingly - in a way that makes breathing difficult
