cry

verb
announce publicly by calling out
8.2
Pronunciation: / kraɪ / (AmE), / kraɪ / (BrE)
to make a public announcement, especially by calling out loudly in a street or marketplace

Examples

To cry the news
Before print media, officials would cry royal proclamations in the town square.
In the old days, the town crier would cry important news for everyone to hear.
Merchants used to cry their wares in the bustling city market.
