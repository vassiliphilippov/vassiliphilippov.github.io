trio

noun
group of three people or things
2.7 5.04/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtriÀê.o ä /; / ÀàtriÀêo ä / (AmE), / ÀàtriÀê.…ô ä /; / ÀàtriÀê…ô ä / (BrE)
a group of three people or things considered as a unit, especially a group of performers or musicians

Examples
