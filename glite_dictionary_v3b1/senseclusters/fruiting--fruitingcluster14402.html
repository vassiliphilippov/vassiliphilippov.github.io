fruiting

adjective
capable of bearing fruit (plants)
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfruːtɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant or tree) capable of bearing fruit; producing or likely to produce fruit.

Examples

Fruiting plants
Farmers monitor the fruiting stage closely to ensure a good yield.
The fruiting trees in the orchard will be ready for harvest by late summer.
This variety is not fruiting in its first year, but will do so next season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sterile - (of a plant) unable to produce fruit or offspring
