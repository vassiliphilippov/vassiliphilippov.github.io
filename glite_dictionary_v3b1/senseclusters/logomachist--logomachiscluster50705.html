logomachist

noun
one who argues about words
Pronunciation: / loʊˈgɑːməkɪst / (AmE), / ləʊˈgɒməkɪst / (BrE)
a person who engages in disputes or arguments about words or their meanings

Examples

A logomachist argues words
In literary circles, a logomachist may be both admired and avoided.
She was labeled a logomachist for her habit of correcting trivial word choices.
The debate turned tedious, as the two logomachists argued endlessly over semantics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quibbler - a person who raises trivial objections or distinctions
