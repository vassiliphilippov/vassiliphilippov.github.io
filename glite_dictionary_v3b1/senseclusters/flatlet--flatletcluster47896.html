flatlet

noun
a very small flat or apartment
Pronunciation: / ˈflæt.lət / (AmE), / ˈflæt.lɪt / (BrE)
a very small self-contained flat, typically consisting of one or two rooms

Examples

A small flatlet
She rented a flatlet close to the university campus.
The old mansion was divided into several flatlets for single tenants.
They converted the basement into a small flatlet for guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: studio - a small apartment consisting of one main room
