mystifyingly

adverb
in a confusing way
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəˌfaɪɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəˌfaɪɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is confusing or difficult to understand

Examples

A mystifyingly complex problem
The solution was mystifyingly complex for such a basic problem.
He mystifyingly refused to answer any of the reporter's questions.
The movie's ending was mystifyingly abrupt, leaving the audience confused.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to understand
  • synonym: puzzlingly - in a way that causes confusion or puzzlement
