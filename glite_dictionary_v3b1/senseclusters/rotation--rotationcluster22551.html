Synonyms

  • rotary motion
    3.6 0.07/m
    rotary motion is the technical term for continuous movement around an axis in machines, while rotation is the general act of turning.
  • turn
    1.0 40/m
    turn is a single change of direction or one short movement, while rotation is a steady circular movement around an axis.
  • spin
    1.1 9/m
    spin means turning quickly with speed or force, while rotation is a neutral term for turning around an axis.
