tsp

noun
teaspoon abbreviation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ti ɛs pi / (AmE), / tiː ɛs piː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for teaspoon, a small unit of measurement used in cooking and recipes

Examples

1 tsp of salt
The recipe calls for 1 tsp of salt and 2 tbsp of sugar.
Please add half a tsp of vanilla extract to the mixture.
He measured out a tsp of honey for his tea.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: tbsp - abbreviation for tablespoon, a larger unit of measurement in cooking
  • synonym: teaspoon - a small spoon used for stirring tea or measuring small amounts in cooking
