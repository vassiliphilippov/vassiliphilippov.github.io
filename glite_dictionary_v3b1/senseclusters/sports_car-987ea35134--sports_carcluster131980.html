sports car

noun
a fast, low two-seater car
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrts ˌkɑr /; / spɔrts kɑr / (AmE), / ˈspɔːts ˌkɑː /; / spɔːts kɑː / (BrE)
a small, fast car designed for high speed and performance, usually with two seats

Examples

A sports car drives fast
He dreamed of driving a bright red sports car along the coast.
Her new sports car accelerates from zero to sixty in just a few seconds.
Many collectors admire classic sports cars for their design and speed.
He always dreamed of owning a bright red sports car when he grew up.
Many sports cars are designed with advanced safety features and aerodynamic shapes.
The new sports car accelerates from zero to sixty in under five seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convertible - a car with a folding or detachable roof
  • synonym: roadster - an open two-seater car, often sporty
  • antonym: sedan - a car with a separate trunk and seating for more passengers
