actual

adjective
real; existing in fact
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.tʃu.əl /; / ˈæktʃuəl / (AmE), / ˈæk.tʃu.əl /; / ˈæktʃuəl / (BrE)
real or existing in fact; not imagined, supposed, or theoretical

Examples
