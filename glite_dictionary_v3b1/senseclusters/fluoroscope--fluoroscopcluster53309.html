fluoroscope

noun
X-ray machine with fluorescent viewing screen
6.0 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈflʊr.ə.skoʊ/ /; / /ˈflʊə.rə.skoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈflʊə.rə.skəʊ/ /; / /ˈflɔː.rə.skəʊ/ (BrE)
