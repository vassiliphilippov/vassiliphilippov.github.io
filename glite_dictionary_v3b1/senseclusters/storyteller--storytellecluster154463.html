storyteller

noun
person who tells stories
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔriˌtɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːriˌtɛlə / (BrE)
a person who tells stories, especially as a profession or as a form of entertainment

Examples

A storyteller on stage
As a skilled storyteller, she could make even the most ordinary events sound magical.
The children sat quietly, captivated by the storyteller's voice.
The festival featured a famous storyteller who shared traditional folk tales.
