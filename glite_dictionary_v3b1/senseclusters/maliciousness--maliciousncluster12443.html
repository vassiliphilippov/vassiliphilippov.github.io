maliciousness

noun
the desire to harm others
6.0
Pronunciation: / məˈlɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / məˈlɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the desire to harm others or see them suffer; the quality of being malicious

Examples

Hidden maliciousness
Despite his outward charm, there was a hidden maliciousness in his actions.
Her maliciousness was evident in the way she spread false rumors about her colleagues.
The villain's maliciousness made him a truly frightening character in the novel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolence - the quality of being well meaning and kind
  • synonym: malevolence - the state of wishing evil or harm to others
