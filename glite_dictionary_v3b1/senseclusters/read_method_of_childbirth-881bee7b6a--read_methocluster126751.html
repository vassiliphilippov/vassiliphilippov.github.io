Read method of childbirth

noun
method of natural childbirth using relaxation and education
7.0
Pronunciation: / riːd ˈmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜrθ / (AmE), / riːd ˈmɛθəd əv ˈtʃaɪldˌbɜːθ / (BrE)
a method of natural childbirth that considers labor a normal process, emphasizing education, relaxation, and breathing techniques to reduce pain, which is seen mainly as psychological.

Examples

The Read method of childbirth approach
Many parents choose the Read method of childbirth to promote a natural and less stressful birth experience.
She found that the Read method of childbirth helped her manage labor without medication.
The hospital offers classes in the Read method of childbirth for expectant mothers and their partners.
