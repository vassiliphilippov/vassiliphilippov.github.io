canine distemper

noun
infectious viral disease of dogs
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnaɪn dɪˈstɛmpər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnaɪn dɪˈstɛmpə / (BrE)
a contagious viral disease affecting dogs, especially puppies, characterized by high fever, respiratory inflammation, and often neurological symptoms
