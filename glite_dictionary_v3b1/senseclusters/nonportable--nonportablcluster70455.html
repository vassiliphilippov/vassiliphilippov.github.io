nonportable

adjective
not easily moved
6.1
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpɔrtəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be easily moved, carried, or transferred from one place or system to another

Examples

A nonportable device
The old computer is nonportable and must remain in the office.
Because the application is nonportable, it only works on one type of device.
Heavy, nonportable equipment can be difficult to use in different locations.
