purifier

noun
device for removing impurities
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊr.əˌfaɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpjʊə.rɪ.faɪ.ə / (BrE)
a device or apparatus that removes impurities from air, water, or other substances

Examples

A purifier device
A water purifier is essential when traveling in remote areas.
The new air purifier made a noticeable difference in the room’s air quality.
They installed a purifier to remove contaminants from the laboratory’s ventilation system.
