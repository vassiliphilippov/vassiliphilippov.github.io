knot

noun
unit of speed (nautical mile per hour)
5.3 4.53/m
Pronunciation: / nÉ‘t / (AmE), / nÉ’t / (BrE)
a unit of speed used in navigation, equal to one nautical mile (1.852 km) per hour, commonly used for ships and aircraft

Examples

Speed in knots
Aircraft often report their speed in knots when flying over oceans.
The ship was sailing at 20 knots across the open sea.
Wind speeds reached 35 knots during the storm.
