excimer

noun
ultraviolet laser type
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksɪmər / (AmE), / ˈɛksɪmə / (BrE)
a type of laser that uses excimer molecules to generate ultraviolet light, often used in eye surgery and microfabrication

Examples

Excimer laser
The surgeon used an excimer to reshape the patient's cornea during the procedure.
Advances in excimer technology have improved the precision of eye surgeries.
Photolithography processes in microchip manufacturing often rely on excimer lasers for fine patterning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laser - a device that emits a concentrated beam of light
