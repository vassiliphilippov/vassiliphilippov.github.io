hatched

adjective
emerged from an egg
1.3
Pronunciation: / hætʃt / (AmE), / hætʃt / (BrE)
(of a young animal) having emerged from an egg.

Examples

Newly hatched chicks
Most reptiles are independent soon after they are hatched.
The newly hatched chicks huddled together under the heat lamp.
These birds remain in their nests for several days after being hatched.

  • antonym: unhatched - not yet emerged from an egg
