sesquiterpene

adjective
containing sesquiterpenes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛskwɪˈtɜːrpiːn / (AmE), / ˌsɛskwɪˈtɜːpiːn / (BrE)
relating to or containing sesquiterpenes, which are a class of terpenes made of three isoprene units.

Examples

Sesquiterpene structure
The essential oil contains a high concentration of sesquiterpene compounds.
Scientists studied the sesquiterpene profile of the plant to understand its medicinal properties.
A sesquiterpene structure can influence the aroma and biological activity of natural products.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: monoterpene - a terpene made of two isoprene units
