paunchy

adjective
having a large belly
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔn(t)ʃi / (AmE), / ˈpɔːn(t)ʃi / (BrE)
Having a large, protruding belly, often as a result of being overweight.

Examples

A paunchy belly
Despite his paunchy appearance, he was surprisingly agile on the basketball court.
The paunchy businessman struggled to button his suit jacket.
Years of hearty eating had left him distinctly paunchy around the middle.
