paunchy

adjective
having a large belly
7.4 0.23/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…în(t) Éi / (AmE), / Ààp…îÀên(t) Éi / (BrE)
Having a large, protruding belly, often as a result of being overweight.

Examples

A paunchy belly
Despite his paunchy appearance, he was surprisingly agile on the basketball court.
The paunchy businessman struggled to button his suit jacket.
Years of hearty eating had left him distinctly paunchy around the middle.

Synonyms

  • potbellied
    3.7 0.32/m
    potbellied is a rounded, sticking-out belly often seen in older people, while paunchy means a large protruding belly from being overweight.
  • big-bellied
    3.5 0.10/m
    big-bellied describes someone with a clearly large belly in neutral terms, while paunchy suggests being overweight with a bulging stomach.
