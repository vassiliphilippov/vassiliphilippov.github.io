bone of contention

noun
subject of dispute or disagreement
Pronunciation: / boʊn əv kənˈtɛnʃən / (AmE), / bəʊn əv kənˈtɛnʃən / (BrE)
an issue or subject that causes disagreement or argument between people

Examples

A bone of contention between neighbours
Parking spaces in the building have long been a bone of contention for residents.
The inheritance quickly became a bone of contention among family members.
The new policy is likely to be a bone of contention between management and staff.
Parking spaces have always been a bone of contention in this neighborhood.
The new company policy quickly became a bone of contention among the employees.
Their father's will was a bone of contention between the siblings for years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - a state of harmony or accord
  • synonym: matter of dispute - a subject that people are arguing about
  • synonym: sticking point - an obstacle causing disagreement in negotiations
