copyreader

noun
editor who checks written material for publication
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpiˌriːdər / (AmE), / ˈkɒpiˌriːdə / (BrE)
an editor whose job is to check and correct written material before it is published

Examples

A copyreader checks text
As a skilled copyreader, she ensures that every document is polished and accurate.
Our magazine relies on a dedicated copyreader to maintain quality and consistency.
The copyreader found several grammatical errors in the article before it went to print.
