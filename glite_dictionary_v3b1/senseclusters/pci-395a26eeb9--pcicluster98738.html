PCI

noun
payment card standards
Pronunciation: / ˌpiː siː ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌpiː siː ˈaɪ / (BrE)
the set of industry standards and practices for processing credit and debit card payments securely, especially regarding data protection

Examples

PCI compliance
All companies that handle card payments must follow PCI requirements to protect customer data.
The new software update helps businesses meet PCI compliance more easily.
Failure to adhere to PCI standards can result in heavy fines for merchants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PCI DSS - the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard, a set of security requirements for card payments
  • synonym: compliance - the act of following rules or standards, especially in business or law
