shoulder

verb
lift or carry onto one's shoulders
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
to lift or carry (something) onto one's shoulders, especially as a way to transport it

Examples

To shoulder a bag
After winning the match, the players shouldered their coach in celebration.
He shouldered the heavy bag and walked up the hill.
She shouldered her backpack and headed for the bus stop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heave - to lift or haul with effort
  • synonym: hoist - to raise or lift something, often with mechanical help
