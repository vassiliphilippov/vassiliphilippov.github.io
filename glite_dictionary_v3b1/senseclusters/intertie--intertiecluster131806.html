intertie

verb
to connect power networks
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɪntətaɪ / (BrE)
to connect two or more power systems or networks so they can exchange electricity

Examples

To intertie power grids
The engineers will intertie the two power grids to improve reliability during peak demand.
Plans are underway to intertie several regional networks for better energy distribution.
By intertieing the systems, the utility can share electricity more efficiently across the state.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disconnect - to separate or break the connection between things
  • synonym: link - to connect or join together
