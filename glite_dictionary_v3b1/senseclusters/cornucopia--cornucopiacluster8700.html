cornucopia

noun
an abundance; great supply
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔrnjuˈkoʊpiə / (AmE), / ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiə / (BrE)
an abundant supply of good things; a state or source of great plenty

Examples

Cornucopia of ideas
Her backyard was a cornucopia of wildflowers in the spring.
The festival offered a cornucopia of foods from around the world.
There is a cornucopia of information available online for new learners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something
  • antonym: scarcity - the state of being in short supply; shortage
