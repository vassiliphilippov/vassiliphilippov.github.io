pandemonium

noun
state of wild disorder or chaos
Pronunciation: / ˌpæn.dəˈmoʊ.ni.əm / (AmE), / ˌpan.dɪˈməʊ.ni.əm / (BrE)
a situation in which there is wild and noisy disorder, confusion, or chaos

Examples

Wild pandemonium erupted
After the final whistle, pandemonium erupted among the fans in the stadium.
The news caused pandemonium in financial markets around the world.
When the fire alarm went off, pandemonium broke out in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chaos - a state of complete disorder and confusion
  • antonym: order - an organized or orderly condition
