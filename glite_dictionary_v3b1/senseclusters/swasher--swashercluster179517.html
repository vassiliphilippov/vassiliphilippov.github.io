swasher

noun
boastful person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːʃər / (AmE), / ˈswɒʃə / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a swaggering or boastful person

Examples

A swasher boasts
In the old novel, the main villain was a notorious swasher who loved to brag about his adventures.
The townspeople quickly grew tired of the swasher and his endless stories of supposed heroism.
She dismissed him as a mere swasher, unimpressed by his exaggerated claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braggart - a person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - someone who does not boast or show off
  • synonym: swaggerer - someone who walks or behaves in a very confident and arrogant way
