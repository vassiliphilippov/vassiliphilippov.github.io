baring

noun
removal of a covering
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛːrɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing a covering; the exposure of something hidden or protected

Examples

The baring of secrets
His sudden baring of personal secrets surprised everyone present.
Spring cleaning often involves the baring of floors that are usually covered by rugs.
The baring of the ancient mural revealed vibrant colors hidden for centuries.
