maniac

noun
person with obsessive enthusiasm
7.9 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪniˌæk / (AmE), / ˈmeɪniˌak / (BrE)
a person who shows extreme enthusiasm or obsession for something, often in a way that seems excessive or unreasonable

Examples

A sports maniac
He's a football maniac who never misses a game.
My brother is a fitness maniac; he goes to the gym twice a day.
That guy drives like a maniac when he's late for work.
