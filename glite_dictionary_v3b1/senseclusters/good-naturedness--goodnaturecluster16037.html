good-naturedness

noun
quality of being friendly and helpful
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈneɪtʃərdnəs / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈneɪtʃədnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant, friendly, and willing to help others

Examples

Good-naturedness and kindness
Her good-naturedness made her popular among colleagues and friends alike.
His good-naturedness was evident even when faced with difficult customers.
I appreciate your good-naturedness in helping me move on such short notice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amiability - the quality of having a friendly and pleasant manner
  • antonym: ill-naturedness - the quality of being unpleasant or unkind
