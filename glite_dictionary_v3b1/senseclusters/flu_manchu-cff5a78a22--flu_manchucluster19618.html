Flu Manchu

noun
humorous term for looking ill from the flu
Pronunciation: / flu mænˈtʃuː / (AmE), / flu mænˈtʃuː / (BrE)
a humorous term for a person who looks ill from the flu, or for a widespread flu outbreak

Examples

Looking like Flu Manchu
After a week in bed, he emerged looking like Flu Manchu.
The whole class caught the flu, and parents joked it was a Flu Manchu situation.
Whenever flu season comes, she calls herself Flu Manchu to make her coworkers laugh.
