holographically

adverb
using holography
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊləˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɒləˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or uses holography, especially by creating or displaying holograms

Examples

Appeared holographically on stage
The concert featured a singer who appeared holographically on stage, amazing the audience.
Instructions were displayed holographically above the device for easy reference.
The museum allows visitors to interact with artifacts holographically using special glasses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitally - in a way that uses digital technology
  • antonym: physically - in a way that involves real, tangible objects
