stranding

noun
animal left ashore
5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstræn.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈstræn.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the event or state of being left somewhere with no way to leave or return, especially for animals or people

Examples

Whale stranding event
The rescue team arrived quickly after the whale stranding was reported on the coast.
Scientists study each stranding to learn more about marine animal behavior and health.
A mass stranding of dolphins drew attention from environmental groups and local media.
The sudden storm led to the stranding of several hikers on the mountain.
A mass stranding of whales occurred along the coastline last year.
After the bus broke down, the stranding of commuters caused major delays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of leaving someone or something behind
  • synonym: beaching - the act of an animal coming onto land and being unable to return to water
  • antonym: rescue - the act of saving someone from a dangerous or difficult situation
