request

verb
to politely ask someone to do something
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkwɛst / (AmE), / rɪˈkwɛst / (BrE)
to politely or formally ask for something to be done, given, or allowed

Examples

To request help
She requested a meeting with her supervisor to discuss her progress.
The teacher requested that all assignments be submitted by Friday.
You are kindly requested to turn off your mobile phones during the performance.
If you need more details, you can request them by email.
She requested a copy of the report from the manager.
They requested permission to enter the restricted area.
If you need assistance, simply request help at the front desk.
She politely requested a glass of water from the waiter.
We request that all mobile phones be switched off during the performance.
