yardful

noun
amount filling a yard
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑrdˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈjɑːdˌfʊl / (BrE)
an amount that fills or is contained in a yard, especially a yard-sized outdoor area

Examples

A yardful of leaves
After raking all afternoon, they had a yardful of leaves to bag.
The children played in a yardful of fresh snow after the storm.
We found a yardful of wildflowers blooming behind the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handful - an amount that fills a hand
  • synonym: roomful - an amount that fills a room
