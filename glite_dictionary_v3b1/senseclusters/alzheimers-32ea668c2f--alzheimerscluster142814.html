Alzheimer's

noun
progressive brain disorder causing memory loss
1.6
Pronunciation: / ælˈzaɪmɚz /; / ˈælzˌhaɪmərz / (AmE), / ælˈzaɪməz /; / ˈaltshaɪməz / (BrE)
a progressive brain disease that causes memory loss, confusion, and difficulty with thinking and daily activities, usually starting in middle or old age
