hospital occupancy

noun
proportion of beds occupied in a hospital
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːspɪtl ɑːˈkjuːpənsi / (AmE), / ˌhɒspɪtl ɒˈkjuːpənsi / (BrE)
the proportion of hospital beds that are occupied by patients at a given time

Examples

Low hospital occupancy rates
Low hospital occupancy rates can indicate unused capacity and financial challenges for healthcare providers.
Public health agencies track hospital occupancy to monitor the impact of infectious disease outbreaks.
The hospital occupancy reached an all-time high during the flu season last winter.
