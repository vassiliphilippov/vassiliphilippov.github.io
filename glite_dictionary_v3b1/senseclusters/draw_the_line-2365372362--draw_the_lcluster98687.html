draw the line

verb
set a limit to what is acceptable
7.2
Pronunciation: / drɔ ðə laɪn / (AmE), / drɔː ðə laɪn / (BrE)
set a definite limit to what one is willing to accept or do, especially to avoid unacceptable behavior or situations

Examples

To draw the line at overtime
I have to draw the line at working overtime every weekend.
She drew the line when her coworkers started asking personal questions.
We need to draw the line somewhere before this project becomes unmanageable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cross the line - to go beyond what is acceptable or permissible
  • synonym: set a boundary - to establish a limit or border
