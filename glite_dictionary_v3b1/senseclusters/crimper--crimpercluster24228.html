crimper

noun
device for curling hair
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmpɚ /; / ˈkrɪmpər / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmpə / (BrE)
a device used to curl or create waves in hair

Examples

Hair crimper

She carefully placed each section of hair into the crimper before getting ready for her party.
Using a crimper can give your hair tight, bouncy curls in just a few minutes.
She used a crimper to style her hair with waves for the party.
The baker grabbed a crimper to seal the edges of the pie crust.
Electricians often use a crimper to attach connectors to wires securely.
