resort

noun
action of seeking help as a last option
2.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːt / (BrE)
an action of turning to someone or something for help, especially as a final option

Examples

Last resort
She viewed asking for financial help as a last resort.
Violence must never be a first resort in resolving disputes.
When negotiations failed, their only resort was legal action.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeal - an earnest request or plea for help or support
  • synonym: recourse - the use of someone or something as a source of help
