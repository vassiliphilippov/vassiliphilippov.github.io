Brama

noun
genus of marine fishes in Bramidae family
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːmə / (BrE)
A genus of marine fishes (family Bramidae) known for deep-bodied forms found in warm and temperate seas.

Examples

The Brama fish
Marine biologists recently discovered a new species within the Brama genus.

The genus Brama includes several species commonly known as pomfrets.
