narrow-mindedly

adverb
in a narrow-minded manner
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪdli / (AmE), / ˌnærəʊˈmaɪndɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows unwillingness to accept or consider different ideas, opinions, or cultures.

Examples

To narrow-mindedly reject ideas
He responded narrow-mindedly to any suggestion that challenged his beliefs.
The committee narrow-mindedly rejected all proposals from outside their group.
They narrow-mindedly refused to allow any foreign cuisine at the festival.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open-mindedly - in a way that is willing to consider new ideas and perspectives
