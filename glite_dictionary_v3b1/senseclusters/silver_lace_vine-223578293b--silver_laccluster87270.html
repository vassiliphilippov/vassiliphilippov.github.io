silver lace vine

noun
ornamental climbing vine with fragrant greenish flowers
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ leɪs vaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə leɪs vaɪn / (BrE)
A twining perennial vine with racemes of fragrant greenish flowers, native from western China to Russia, often grown for ornament.

Examples

The silver lace vine flower
Gardeners often choose the silver lace vine to create natural shade on pergolas and trellises.
In spring, clusters of fragrant greenish blooms adorned the silver lace vine in the garden.
The silver lace vine climbed quickly and covered the entire fence within a year.
