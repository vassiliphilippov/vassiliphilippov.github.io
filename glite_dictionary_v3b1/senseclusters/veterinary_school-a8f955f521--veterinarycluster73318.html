veterinary school

noun
institution teaching veterinary medicine
Pronunciation: / vəˈtɛrəˌnɛri skuːl / (AmE), / vəˈtɛrɪnəri skuːl / (BrE)
an institution of higher education specializing in the teaching and training of veterinary medicine and surgery.
