Synonyms

  • inevitable
    5.3 0.01/m
    inevitable is certain to happen and widely used in everyday speech, while unavoidable stresses that it cannot be stopped.
  • inescapable
    5.4 3.39/m
    inescapable feels like you cannot get away from it physically or mentally, while unavoidable focuses on being unable to prevent it.
  • unpreventable
    6.3 0.06/m
    unpreventable says something cannot be stopped, but unavoidable is the usual, more natural word in spoken and written English.
  • ineluctable
    9.9 0.31/m
    ineluctable is rare and literary, used in formal texts, whereas unavoidable is common everyday language for things you cannot avoid.
