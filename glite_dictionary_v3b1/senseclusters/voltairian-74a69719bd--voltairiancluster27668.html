Voltairian

noun
follower of Voltaire
9.9
Pronunciation: /vɑːl.ˈter.ɪ.ən/ /; / /voʊl.ˈter.ɪ.ən/ (AmE), /vɒl.ˈteə.rɪ.ən/ /; / /vəʊl.ˈteə.rɪ.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the ideas of Voltaire, especially skepticism, freedom of speech, and opposition to religious authority

Examples

A Voltairian thinker
As a proud Voltairian, he often spoke out against censorship and defended individual rights.

Her essays revealed her as a true Voltairian, always questioning authority and promoting free speech.
