artificial lake

noun
man-made body of water
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl leɪk / (AmE), / ˌɑːtɪˈfɪʃəl leɪk / (BrE)
a man-made body of water created to store water for community use or other purposes

Examples

An artificial lake
A new artificial lake was constructed to provide water for the nearby town.
Drought conditions have made the artificial lake shrink noticeably this summer.
The city park features an artificial lake where people can go boating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reservoir - a large natural or artificial lake used as a source of water supply
