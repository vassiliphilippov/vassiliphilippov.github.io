unpucker

verb
to smooth wrinkles
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌkər / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌkə / (BrE)
to smooth out or release something from a puckered or wrinkled state

Examples

To unpucker lips
She tried to unpucker her lips after making a funny face.
He gently unpuckered the fabric to remove the wrinkles before ironing.
The child laughed as he unpuckered his brow after being surprised.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pucker - to gather or contract into wrinkles or folds
  • synonym: smooth - to make a surface even or free from wrinkles
