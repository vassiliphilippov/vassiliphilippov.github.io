crystallize

verb
form crystals or cause to crystallize
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪz /; / ˈkrɪs.təˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəlaɪz /; / ˈkrɪs.tə.laɪz / (BrE)
to form crystals or take on a crystalline structure (as in a physical or chemical process)

Examples

Salt will crystallize from seawater as the water evaporates under the sun.

The solution was left overnight to allow the salt to crystallize naturally.
