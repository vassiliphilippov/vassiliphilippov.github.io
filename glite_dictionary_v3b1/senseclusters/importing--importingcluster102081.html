importing

noun
bringing goods into a country for sale
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɔrtɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɔːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of bringing goods or services into a country from abroad, typically for sale

Examples

The importing of goods
His business expanded rapidly after he began importing electronics from Asia.
Strict regulations govern the importing of dairy products into the country.
The company specializes in importing exotic fruits from South America.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exporting - the act of sending goods to another country for sale
