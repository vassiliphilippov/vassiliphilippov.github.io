hibiscus

noun
type of flowering plant
Pronunciation: / haɪˈbɪskəs / (AmE), / hɪˈbɪskəs / (BrE)
A plant of the genus Hibiscus, typically having large, colorful flowers and often grown as ornamentals or for their edible parts.

Examples

A hibiscus flower
Hibiscus plants require plenty of sunlight and well-drained soil to thrive.
He brewed a refreshing tea from dried hibiscus petals.
The hibiscus in her garden blooms with bright red flowers every summer.
