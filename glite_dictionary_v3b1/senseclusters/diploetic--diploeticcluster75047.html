diploetic

adjective
of the skull's diploe
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪploʊˈiːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləʊˈiːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the diploe, the spongy bone layer between the inner and outer layers of the skull

Examples

Diploetic veins
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the diploetic veins during the procedure.
A fracture in the diploetic layer can sometimes be seen on specialized skull X-rays.
Researchers studied the diploetic spaces to understand blood flow within the skull.
