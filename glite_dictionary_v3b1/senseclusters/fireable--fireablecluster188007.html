fireable

adjective
able to be fired
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərəbl / (BrE)
able to be fired or discharged, especially referring to a weapon or firearm that is in working condition.

Examples

Fireable weapon
The old cannon was restored and made fireable for the historical reenactment.
Before using any equipment, check if it is still fireable and safe to operate.
Collectors were surprised to find the antique pistol was actually fireable after all these years.
