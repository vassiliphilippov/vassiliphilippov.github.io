effort

noun
earnest attempt to accomplish something
1.6 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈe.fərt/ (AmE), /ˈe.fət/ (BrE)
the use of physical or mental energy to try to do something; an attempt

Examples

Make an effort
Even a small effort can make a big difference in someone's life.
She made a tremendous effort to finish the project on time.
Winning the championship was the result of years of effort and dedication.
Despite her best effort, she could not solve the complex puzzle.
The project was a group effort that demanded everyone's dedication.
Winning the championship took a lot of effort from the entire team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endeavor - an attempt to achieve something, especially a long or difficult one
  • synonym: exertion - vigorous physical or mental effort
  • antonym: idleness - the state of being inactive or not making any effort
  • antonym: laziness - the quality of being unwilling to work or use energy
  • synonym: try - an attempt or effort to do something
