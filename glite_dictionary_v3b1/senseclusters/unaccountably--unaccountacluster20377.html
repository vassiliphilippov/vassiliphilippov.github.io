unaccountably

adverb
in an inexplicable manner
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈkaʊn.tə.bli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈkaʊn.tə.bli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be explained or understood; for reasons not clear or apparent.

Examples

Feeling unaccountably anxious
He felt unaccountably anxious before the meeting, despite being well-prepared.
She unaccountably forgot her keys, even though she never does.
The painting unaccountably vanished from the gallery overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexplicably - in a way that cannot be explained or is very strange
