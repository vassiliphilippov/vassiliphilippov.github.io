daycare

noun
childcare service during the day
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌkeə / (BrE)
A service or facility that provides care and supervision for infants or young children during the day, typically while parents are at work.

Examples

A daycare center
It's important to choose a reputable daycare provider for your child.
My daughter goes to daycare while I am at work.
The company offers on-site daycare for its employees' children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysitting - looking after children temporarily
  • synonym: nursery - a place where young children are cared for during the day
