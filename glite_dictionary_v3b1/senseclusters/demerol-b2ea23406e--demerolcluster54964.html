Demerol

noun
Synthetic narcotic painkiller (meperidine)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛmərˌɔl / (AmE), / ˈdɛmərɒl / (BrE)
A synthetic narcotic drug, used to treat moderate to severe pain, especially in medical settings; trade name for meperidine.
