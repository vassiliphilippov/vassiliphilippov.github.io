nonstatistical

adjective
not using statistics
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.stəˈtɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.stəˈtɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
not involving, based on, or related to statistics or statistical methods

Examples

A nonstatistical method
The study used a nonstatistical approach to evaluate the effectiveness of the program.
Her conclusions were drawn from nonstatistical evidence such as interviews and observations.
We need both statistical and nonstatistical methods to fully understand the data.

Related Terms

  • antonym: statistical - involving or based on statistics
