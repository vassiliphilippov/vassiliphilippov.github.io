jargonistic

adjective
full of technical terms
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒɑːɡəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
using or characterized by specialized or technical language that is difficult for most people to understand

Examples

A jargonistic style
The instructions were so jargonistic that most new employees struggled to follow them.
Her jargonistic explanation confused the audience instead of clarifying the topic.
Try to avoid jargonistic language when writing for a general audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - easy to understand; not complicated or technical
  • synonym: technical - using language specific to a particular subject or profession
