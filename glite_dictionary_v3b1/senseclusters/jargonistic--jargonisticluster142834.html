jargonistic

adjective
full of technical terms
9.3
Pronunciation: /dʒɑːr.gə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /dʒɑː.ɡə.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples

A jargonistic style

Her jargonistic explanation confused the audience instead of clarifying the topic.
Try to avoid jargonistic language when writing for a general audience.
