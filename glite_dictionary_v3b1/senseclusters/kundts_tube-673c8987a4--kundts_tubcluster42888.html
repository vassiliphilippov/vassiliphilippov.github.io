Kundt's tube

noun
apparatus for measuring sound speed
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / kÊŠnts tuËb / (AmE), / kÊŠnts tjuËb / (BrE)
an apparatus used to determine the speed of sound in a gas or solid by observing the positions of stationary waves in a tube
