here

adverb
at this point or in this respect
Pronunciation: / hɪr / (AmE), / hɪə / (BrE)
at this point, in this circumstance, or with regard to this detail or subject.

Examples

Here in the text
Here the author clearly disagrees with the previous interpretation.
Here, I must point out an important exception to the rule.
We will stop the presentation here and continue after lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: there - at that point, situation, or detail (often contrasted with 'here')
