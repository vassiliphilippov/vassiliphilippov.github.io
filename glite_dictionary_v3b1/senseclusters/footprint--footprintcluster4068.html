footprint

noun
digital trace of online activity
Pronunciation: / c8f8atccpr6ant / (AmE), / c8f8at.pr6ant / (BrE)
the record or traces of a person's or device's activity on the internet or digital systems, which can be collected to build profiles or target advertisements; also called a 'digital footprint'.

Examples

Digital footprint
Advertisers map a user's footprint across websites to tailor online ads.
Students should manage their digital footprint because employers often check online profiles.
She deleted old posts to reduce her social media footprint and protect privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital trail - a sequence of records or logs created by online actions
  • synonym: user profile - a collection of data about a person, often constructed from their online activity
