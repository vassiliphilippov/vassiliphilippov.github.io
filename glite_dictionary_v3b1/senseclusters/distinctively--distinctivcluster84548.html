distinctively

adverb
in a way that is distinctive or characteristic
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstɪŋktɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈstɪŋktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is characteristic of something or someone and makes them clearly different from others.

Examples

To dress distinctively
He dressed distinctively, making him easy to spot in any crowd.
The artist used colors distinctively to set her paintings apart from others in the gallery.
The chef prepares dishes distinctively, always adding a personal twist to each meal.
