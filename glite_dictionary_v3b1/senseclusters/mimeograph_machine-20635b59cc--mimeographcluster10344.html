mimeograph machine

noun
duplicating machine using stencils
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪmiəˌɡræf məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈmaɪmɪəˌɡrɑːf məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a device for making copies of documents by pressing ink through a stencil onto paper, widely used before modern photocopiers.

Examples

The mimeograph machine copies
Before photocopiers became common, offices relied on the mimeograph machine to duplicate documents.
He remembers the distinctive ink smell that filled the room after running the mimeograph machine.
The school secretary used the mimeograph machine to print worksheets for every class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duplicator - a device used for making copies of documents
  • degree higher: photocopier - a machine that makes paper copies of documents and images
