Examples

The mimeograph machine copies

Synonyms

  • mimeograph
    9.8 0.11/m
    mimeograph is the standard name for the copying device used with stencils, while mimeograph machine is the longer descriptive phrase.
  • mimeo
    9.9 0.10/m
    mimeo often means either the quick informal copy or the machine, while mimeograph machine refers specifically to the actual copying device.
