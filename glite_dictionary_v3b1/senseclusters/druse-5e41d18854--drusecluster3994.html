Druse

noun
member of a Middle Eastern religious sect
9.9
Pronunciation: / druːz / (AmE), / druːz / (BrE)
a member of an esoteric monotheistic religious sect, mainly living in Syria and Lebanon, whose followers believe Al-hakim was an incarnation of God
