ovarian cyst

noun
benign fluid-filled sac on the ovary
6.5 3.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.ˈver.ɪ.ən sɪst/ /; / /əʊv.ˈe.riː.ən sɪst/ (AmE), /əʊ.ˈveə.ri.ən sɪst/ (BrE)
a fluid-filled sac that develops on or inside an ovary, usually benign but sometimes requiring medical attention

Examples

An ovarian cyst grows
Many women with an ovarian cyst experience no symptoms at all.
Surgery is rarely required to remove an ovarian cyst, unless it causes pain or complications.
The ultrasound revealed an ovarian cyst that needed to be monitored over several months.
