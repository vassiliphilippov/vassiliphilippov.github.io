umbel-like

adjective
resembling an umbel in form
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmbəl laɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌmbəl laɪk / (BrE)
Resembling or having the form of an umbel (a type of cluster in which stems or stalks arise from a common point, like the ribs of an umbrella), especially in plants.

Examples

Umbel-like flower cluster
Many members of the carrot family bear umbel-like inflorescences that attract pollinators.
The botanist noted the plant’s umbel-like structure, which distinguished it from similar species.
The tiny white flowers were arranged in a umbel-like cluster at the top of the stem.
