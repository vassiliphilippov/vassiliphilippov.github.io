spout

noun
an opening for pouring liquids or grain
2.7 1.24/m
Pronunciation: / spaÊŠt / (AmE), / spaÊŠt / (BrE)
a tube or opening through which a liquid or grain can be poured or flows out, especially as part of a container

Examples

A teapot spout
A steady stream of grain flowed out of the silo's spout.
Make sure the spout is clean before filling the watering can.
She poured the tea carefully from the spout of the teapot.

Synonyms

  • nozzle
    4.1 2.82/m
    nozzle is a short tube that directs and often controls liquid flow, while spout is a simple pouring opening on a container.
  • outlet
    2.8 2.00/m
    outlet is a general opening where things can escape or flow out, while spout is a container's pouring opening for liquids or grain.
