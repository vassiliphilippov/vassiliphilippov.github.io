parcel bomb

noun
explosive device hidden in a package
5.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêr.s…ôl b…ëÀêm / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀê.s…ôl b…ím / (BrE)
An explosive device concealed within a package or envelope, designed to detonate when opened.

Examples

Parcel bomb threat
Security measures were increased after a parcel bomb was found at the office.
She received a suspicious package, which was later confirmed to be a parcel bomb.
The police safely defused the parcel bomb before it could cause any harm.

Synonyms

  • package bomb
    5.6 0.02/m
    package bomb is the common term for an explosive hidden in a parcel or box, while parcel bomb names the same device.
  • letter bomb
    7.8 0.04/m
    letter bomb is an explosive placed in a small envelope and sent to someone, while parcel bomb is hidden in a larger package.
