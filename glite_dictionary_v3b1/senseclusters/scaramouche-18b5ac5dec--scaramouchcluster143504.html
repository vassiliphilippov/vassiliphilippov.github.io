Scaramouche

noun
boastful cowardly commedia dell'arte character
Pronunciation: / ˌskærəˈmuːʃ / (AmE), / ˌskærəˈmuːʃ / (BrE)
a boastful but cowardly character in commedia dell'arte, often depicted as a comic figure who blusters and is easily frightened
