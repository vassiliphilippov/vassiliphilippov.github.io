repo

noun
repossessed property
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.poʊ/ /; / /ˈre.poʊ/ (AmE), /ˈriː.pəʊ/ /; / /ˈre.pəʊ/ (BrE)
the act of repossessing property by a lender when a borrower fails to make payments, or an item that has been repossessed

Examples

A car repo
After missing several car payments, he faced a repo of his vehicle.
The bank scheduled a repo for the house after the mortgage default.
She bought a used laptop that turned out to be a repo from an electronics store.

  • synonym: repossession - the act of taking back property after a default on payments
