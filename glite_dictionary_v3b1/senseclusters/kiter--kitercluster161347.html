kiter

noun
video game tactician
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkaɪtə / (BrE)
a player in video games who uses hit-and-run or distance tactics to keep an enemy at range

Examples

Game kiter
The kiter led the monster around the arena while the rest of the team attacked from behind.
In this strategy, the kiter must keep moving to avoid getting caught by the boss.
A skilled kiter can make difficult battles much easier for the group.
