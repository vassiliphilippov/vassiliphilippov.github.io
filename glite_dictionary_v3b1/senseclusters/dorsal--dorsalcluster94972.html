dorsal

noun
back or upper side
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrsəl / (AmE), / ˈdɔːsəl / (BrE)
the back or upper side of an animal, especially referring to a fin on the back of a fish or marine animal

Examples

Dorsal fin
The dolphin's dorsal helps it stay balanced as it swims through the water.
Scientists studied the dorsal of the fish to learn more about its movement.
The large dorsal of the shark was visible above the surface of the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: back - the rear surface of the body, especially of a vertebrate
  • antonym: ventral - the underside or abdominal part of an animal
