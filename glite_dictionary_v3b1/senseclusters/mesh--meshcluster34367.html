mesh

noun
the act of interlocking or meshing
Pronunciation: / mɛʃ / (AmE), / mɛʃ / (BrE)
the act or state of fitting or locking together, especially in machines or organizations

Examples

A tight mesh between departments led to a successful project outcome.

The mesh of gears must be exact for the engine to run smoothly.
A grinding noise may indicate a problem with the mesh of the transmission components.
Technicians checked the mesh before restarting the machine to ensure all parts were properly aligned.
The mesh of the gears must be precise for the engine to function smoothly.

  • synonym: engagement - the condition of parts interlocking or being in contact, especially in machinery
  • synonym: interlock - to join or fit together firmly
