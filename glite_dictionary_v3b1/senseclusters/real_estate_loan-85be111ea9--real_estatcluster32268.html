real estate loan

noun
loan for buying real property
Pronunciation: / ˈriːəl ɪˌsteɪt loʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɪəl ɪˌsteɪt ləʊn / (BrE)
a loan for purchasing or developing real property, typically secured by a mortgage on the property

Examples

A real estate loan application
Due to his poor credit, he struggled to qualify for a real estate loan.
Many first-time homebuyers rely on a real estate loan to afford their new home.
The bank offers a variety of real estate loan options with competitive interest rates.
