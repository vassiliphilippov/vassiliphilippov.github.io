Ovid

proper noun
famous Roman poet (43 BC – AD 17)
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vɪd/ /; / /ˈɑː.vɪd/ (AmE), /ˈɒ.vɪd/ /; / /ˈəʊ.vɪd/ (BrE)
