para I

noun
woman who has given birth once
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrə wʌn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːrə wʌn / (BrE)
A woman who has given birth to a child once; also called primipara.

Examples

A para I mother
As a para I, her case was closely monitored by the obstetrician.
The medical record noted that she is a para I following her recent delivery.
The study examined the postpartum experiences of para I women in urban hospitals.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: para II - woman who has given birth two times
  • synonym: primipara - woman who has given birth for the first time
