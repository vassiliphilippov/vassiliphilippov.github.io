paintbrush

noun
brush for applying paint
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪntˌbrʌʃ / (BrE)
A brush with bristles used for applying paint to a surface.

Examples

A wide paintbrush
A wide paintbrush is perfect for covering large areas with color quickly.
He forgot to clean the paintbrush after finishing the mural, so the bristles became stiff.
She dipped her paintbrush into the bright red paint and began to work on the canvas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brush - a tool with bristles, used for cleaning, painting, etc.
