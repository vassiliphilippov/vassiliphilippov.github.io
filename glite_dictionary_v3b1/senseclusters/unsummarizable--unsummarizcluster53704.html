unsummarizable

adjective
cannot be summarized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsʌmərˌaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌmərˌaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to summarize briefly or clearly

Examples

An unsummarizable story
The documentary was so detailed that it was nearly unsummarizable in a short review.
Her explanation was so complex and lengthy that it became unsummarizable for the audience.
Some works of literature are considered unsummarizable because of their depth and nuance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incomprehensible - impossible to understand
  • antonym: summarizable - able to be summarized
