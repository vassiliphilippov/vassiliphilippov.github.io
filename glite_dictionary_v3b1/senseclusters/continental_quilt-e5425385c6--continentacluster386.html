continental quilt

noun
thick bed covering; duvet (British)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.t̬ɪˈnen.t̬l kwɪlt / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.tɪˈnen.tl kwɪlt / (BrE)
a thick, soft bed covering filled with down, feathers, or synthetic fibers, used instead of sheets and blankets, commonly known as a duvet in British English

Examples

A continental quilt cover
On cold nights, I love snuggling under my continental quilt.
She prefers a continental quilt because it is lighter and easier to make the bed.
We replaced all the sheets and blankets with a new continental quilt for extra warmth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blanket - a large piece of soft material used as a covering on a bed
  • synonym: duvet - a soft quilt filled with down or feathers; used instead of an upper sheet and blankets
