tennis elbow

noun
painful elbow tendon inflammation from overuse
5.8 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnɪs ˌɛlˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪs ˌɛl.bəʊ / (BrE)
Painful inflammation of the tendon at the outer side of the elbow, caused by overuse of arm muscles, especially from activities like racket sports or repetitive wrist movements.

Examples

Tennis elbow pain
After months of playing squash, she was diagnosed with tennis elbow by her doctor.
He wore a special brace on his arm to help manage his tennis elbow.
Typing for long periods can sometimes trigger symptoms similar to tennis elbow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: golfer's elbow - painful tendon inflammation at the inner elbow
  • synonym: lateral epicondylitis - medical term for tennis elbow
