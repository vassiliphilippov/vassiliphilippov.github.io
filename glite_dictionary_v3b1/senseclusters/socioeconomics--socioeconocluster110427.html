socioeconomics

noun
study of society and economy
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊˌɛkəˈnɑmɪks / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks / (BrE)
the study of how social and economic factors interact and influence each other in society or a community

Examples

Socioeconomics studies society
Socioeconomics examines how income and education levels affect people's opportunities in life.
Researchers in socioeconomics analyze the impact of social class on economic behavior.
Understanding socioeconomics can help governments create better policies for reducing poverty.
Researchers analyzed the socioeconomics of the city to understand patterns of inequality.
Changes in socioeconomics can affect access to education and healthcare in rural areas.
The report highlights how socioeconomics influences people's opportunities and quality of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economics - the study of production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services
  • synonym: sociology - the study of society, social relationships, and institutions
