socioeconomics

noun
study of society and economy
5.6
Pronunciation: /soʊ.si.oʊ.iː.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪks/ /; / /soʊ.si.oʊ.e.kə.ˈnɑː.mɪks/ (AmE), /səʊ.si.əʊ.iː.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪks/ /; / /səʊ.si.əʊ.e.kə.ˈnɒm.ɪks/ (BrE)
