woodwork

noun
parts made of wood in buildings or furniture
3.3 0.63/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw ädÀåw…ùk / (AmE), / Ààw ädÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
the parts made of wood in a building or piece of furniture, especially the moldings, staircases, doors, or trim

Examples

Polished woodwork
He carefully polished the woodwork on the staircase until it gleamed.
The craftsmen paid special attention to the details of the woodwork in the new library.
The original woodwork in the house has been beautifully restored.

Synonyms

  • timberwork
    4.2 0.01/m
    timberwork refers to large structural wooden parts used in building frames and repairs, while woodwork means the interior moldings, doors, and trim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpentry - the activity or skill of making things from wood
