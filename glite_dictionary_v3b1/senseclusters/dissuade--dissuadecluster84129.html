Synonyms

  • talk out of
    4.0 3.10/m
    talk out of is an informal phrasal verb used to persuade someone not to act, while dissuade is more formal and general.
  • discourage
    3.5 21/m
    discourage means making someone less confident or hopeful to prevent action, while dissuade focuses on persuading them not to act.
  • deter
    5.6 8.09/m
    deter tries to discourage or stop action by raising fear of bad results, while dissuade uses direct persuasion or advice.
