Equisetum palustre

noun
a species of horsetail plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkwɪˈsiːtəm pəˈlʌstri / (AmE), / ˌiːkwɪˈsiːtəm pəˈlɑːstri / (BrE)
A species of horsetail found widely in wet or boggy areas of the Northern Hemisphere, known as the scouring-rush horsetail.
