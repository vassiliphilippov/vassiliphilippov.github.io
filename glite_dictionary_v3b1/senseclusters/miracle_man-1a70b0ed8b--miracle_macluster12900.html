miracle man

noun
man reputed or claimed to perform miracles
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrəkəl mæn / (AmE), / ˈmɪrəkəl mæn / (BrE)
a man who is believed to perform miracles or achieve extraordinary results, sometimes supernaturally

Examples

The miracle man coach
Daily headlines praised the new coach as their team's miracle man following an unbelievable victory.
Some call the brilliant surgeon a miracle man for saving patients thought beyond hope.
The villagers regarded him as a miracle man after he healed several people.
