separation

noun
ending of employment
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of ending someone's employment, either by resignation or dismissal

Examples

Job separation
A voluntary separation package was offered to encourage employees to leave.
Her separation from the firm was handled professionally by HR.
The company announced the separation of several employees due to restructuring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - termination of employment by the employer
  • synonym: resignation - voluntary act of leaving a job
