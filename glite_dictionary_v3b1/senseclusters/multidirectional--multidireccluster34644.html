multidirectional

adjective
in many directions
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪdaɪˈrɛkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
moving, operating, or affecting in many different directions at the same time

Examples

Multidirectional movement
The new security camera has a multidirectional lens to cover the entire room.
Engineers designed a multidirectional robot that can move smoothly across any surface.
The multidirectional flow of information helps teams collaborate more effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: omnidirectional - able to move or receive equally in all directions
  • antonym: unidirectional - moving or operating in only one direction
