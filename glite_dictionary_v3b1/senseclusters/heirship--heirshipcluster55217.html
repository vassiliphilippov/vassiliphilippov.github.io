heirship

noun
status of being an heir
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈeəʃɪp / (BrE)
the condition or legal right of being an heir to property, a title, or an estate

Examples

Legal heirship rights
The court recognized his heirship to the family estate.
Questions about heirship can complicate the distribution of property after someone dies.
She proved her heirship with official documents and family records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inheritance - the act of receiving property or title from someone after their death
  • synonym: succession - the process of inheriting a title, office, or property
