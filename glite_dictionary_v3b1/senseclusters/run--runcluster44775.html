run

verb
(of colors/dyes) spread or become diffused
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
to flow or move like a liquid, or for a liquid, color, or substance to spread

Examples

Colors run
Be careful, or the colors will run and ruin your new tablecloth.
I always wash new clothes separately in case the dye decides to run.
The red dye may run when you wash the shirt for the first time.
A narrow stream runs behind their cottage, winding through the fields.
After the dam broke, muddy water ran rapidly through the valley.
During the heavy rain, water began to run down the street and into the drains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bleed - (of dye or ink) to spread or run into adjacent areas
  • synonym: flow - (of fluids) to move steadily and continuously in a current or stream
