hogtie

verb
to tie legs together
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːɡˌtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɒɡˌtaɪ / (BrE)
to tie together the legs of a person or animal so that movement is impossible

Examples

To hogtie an animal
The cowboys had to hogtie the steer before loading it onto the truck.
During the demonstration, the trainer showed how to hogtie a horse safely.
The thief was hogtied by security until the police arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bind - to tie or fasten something tightly
  • antonym: untie - to release something that has been tied
