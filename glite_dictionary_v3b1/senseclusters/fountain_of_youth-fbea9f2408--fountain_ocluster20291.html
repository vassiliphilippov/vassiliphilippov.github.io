Fountain of Youth

noun
legendary spring restoring youth
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊntən əv ˈjuθ / (AmE), / ˈfaʊntɪn əv ˈjuːθ / (BrE)
A mythical spring said to restore youth to anyone who drinks or bathes in its waters, featured in folklore and legends.

Examples

The Fountain of Youth
Many explorers throughout history have searched for the Fountain of Youth in hopes of gaining immortality.
She joked that her skincare routine was like discovering the Fountain of Youth.
The legend of the Fountain of Youth continues to inspire writers and filmmakers today.
