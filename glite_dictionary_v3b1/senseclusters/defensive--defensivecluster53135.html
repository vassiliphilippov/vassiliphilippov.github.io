defensive

adjective
protecting against attack or harm
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfen.sɪv/ /; / /də.ˈfen.sɪv/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfen.sɪv/ /; / /də.ˈfen.sɪv/ (BrE)
intended to protect against attack, danger, or harm, or related to defending against aggression.

Examples

Defensive measures
Modern locks and alarms are examples of defensive measures to secure a building.

The army maintained a defensive position throughout the conflict.
