parky

adjective
noticeably cold (informal, British)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈpɑːki / (BrE)
(informal, British) Uncomfortably or noticeably cold, especially with reference to the weather.

Examples

The parky weather
Even the dog didn't want to go out in the parky weather.
It's a bit parky outside—better take a coat.
The mornings have been rather parky this week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilly - uncomfortably cool or cold
  • antonym: warm - having a comfortably high temperature
