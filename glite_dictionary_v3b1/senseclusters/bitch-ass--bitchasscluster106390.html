bitch-ass

noun
slang, offensive term for a coward
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪtʃ-æs / (AmE), / bɪtʃ-ɑːs / (BrE)
A highly offensive slang term for a cowardly, contemptible, or weak person.

Examples

A bitch-ass coward
He called his friend a bitch-ass when he refused to help.
No one wants to be seen as a bitch-ass in front of their peers.
She told him to stop being such a bitch-ass and stand up for himself.
