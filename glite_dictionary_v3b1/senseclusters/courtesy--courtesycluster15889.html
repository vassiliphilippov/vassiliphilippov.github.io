Examples

Basic courtesy
Basic courtesy is expected in all professional settings.
He spoke to the elderly woman with courtesy and respect.
She accepted the invitation with great courtesy and warmth.
His kind courtesy made everyone feel welcome at the meeting.
It is a common courtesy to greet your neighbor with a smile.
She thanked the host with a simple courtesy before leaving the party.
It was a simple courtesy to hold the door open for the person behind me.
Sending a thank-you note is considered a common courtesy after receiving a gift.
The manager offered us coffee as a courtesy when we arrived for the meeting.

Synonyms

  • politeness
    1.3 2.03/m
    politeness is the general quality of good manners and respect, while courtesy names specific polite acts or small remarks.
  • courteousness
    3.5 0.03/m
    courteousness is the steady habit of being polite and respectful, while courtesy often means a single polite action or remark.
  • good manners
    1.0 1.35/m
    good manners are everyday polite actions people show around others, while courtesy can mean a small formal or helpful act.
  • civility
    4.2 5.15/m
    civility is polite, public or formal speech and behavior, while courtesy is often small, personal acts of respect and consideration.
  • graciousness
    4.1 0.45/m
    graciousness is warm, kind, and generous behavior, while courtesy focuses more on polite acts or brief respectful words.
  • consideration
    3.2 4.10/m
    consideration is careful thought for others' feelings and needs, while courtesy is expressed as polite acts or short respectful remarks.
