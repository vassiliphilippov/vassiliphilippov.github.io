turning

noun
act of changing in practice or custom
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
an act or instance in which there is a change in practice, policy, or custom

Examples

A political turning
His leadership marked a major turning in the political party's attitudes.
The company underwent several turnings in its approach to employee benefits.
Tradition makes allowance for rare turnings in established customs.
