unabsorbable

adjective
not able to be absorbed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəbˈzɔːrbəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəbˈzɔːbəbəl / (BrE)
not capable of being absorbed or taken in by another substance or material

Examples

Unabsorbable surface
The plastic coating makes the surface unabsorbable to water and stains.
Doctors use unabsorbable sutures for wounds that need long-term support.
Some minerals are unabsorbable by the human digestive system and pass through unchanged.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absorbable - capable of being absorbed or taken in
  • synonym: impermeable - not allowing liquid or gas to pass through
