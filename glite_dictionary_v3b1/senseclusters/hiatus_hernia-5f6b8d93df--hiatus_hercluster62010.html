hiatus hernia

noun
protrusion of the stomach through the diaphragm
9.9 5.0
Pronunciation: / haɪˈeɪtəs ˈhɜrniə / (AmE), / hʌɪˈeɪtəs ˈhɜːnɪə / (BrE)
A condition in which part of the stomach protrudes upward through the diaphragm into the chest cavity.

Examples

A hiatus hernia condition
Lifestyle changes can help manage the discomfort from a hiatus hernia.
Surgery may be required if a hiatus hernia causes severe symptoms.
The doctor diagnosed her with a hiatus hernia after she complained of persistent heartburn.
