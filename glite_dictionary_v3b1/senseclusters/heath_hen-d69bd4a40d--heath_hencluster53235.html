heath hen

noun
extinct North American prairie chicken
Pronunciation: / hiːθ hɛn / (AmE), / hiːθ hɛn / (BrE)
an extinct species of prairie chicken (Tympanuchus cupido cupido) once found in eastern North America

Examples

The heath hen bird
Efforts to save the heath hen came too late to prevent its extinction.
Naturalists once observed large flocks of heath hens on the heathlands of Massachusetts.
The heath hen became extinct in the early twentieth century due to hunting and habitat loss.
