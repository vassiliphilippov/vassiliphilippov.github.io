nonhostile

adjective
peaceful or neutral
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈhɑstaɪl / (AmE), / nɒnˈhɒstaɪl / (BrE)
not aggressive, unfriendly, or threatening; peaceful or neutral in attitude or behavior

Examples

A nonhostile environment
The negotiations took place in a nonhostile environment, allowing both sides to speak openly.
Despite their past disagreements, their conversation remained nonhostile and respectful.
The company aims to create a nonhostile workplace where everyone feels safe and valued.
The soldiers approached the village, relieved to find the residents were nonhostile and welcoming.
During the negotiation, both parties maintained a nonhostile attitude to reach an agreement.
The animal appeared nonhostile, calmly observing the hikers from a distance.
