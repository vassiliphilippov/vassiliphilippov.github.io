nigh

adjective
on the left side (team of horses/oxen)
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / naɪ / (AmE), / naɪ / (BrE)
(in equestrian and agricultural contexts) on or relating to the left side of a team or pair of horses or oxen.

Examples

Nigh horse
In traditional ploughing, the nigh horse was always placed on the left.
Instructions said to hitch the nigh animal on the left, and the off animal on the right.
The farmer kept the nigh ox closer to the furrow for better control.
