bounce-back

noun
quick recovery
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnsˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnsˌbæk / (BrE)
a quick recovery after a setback, difficulty, or failure

Examples

A strong bounce-back
After losing the first game, the team's bounce-back in the second match was remarkable.
Her bounce-back from illness showed her determination and strength.
The company’s financial bounce-back surprised many industry experts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collapse - a sudden failure or breakdown
  • synonym: recovery - the process of returning to a normal state after a setback
  • synonym: resilience - the ability to recover quickly from difficulties
