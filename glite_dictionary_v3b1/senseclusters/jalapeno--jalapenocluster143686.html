jalapeno

noun
type of chili pepper
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑː.ləˈpeɪ.njoʊ /; / ˌhɑːləˈpeɪnjoʊ / (AmE), / ˌhæ.ləˈpeɪ.njəʊ /; / ˌhæl.əˈpeɪ.njəʊ / (BrE)
