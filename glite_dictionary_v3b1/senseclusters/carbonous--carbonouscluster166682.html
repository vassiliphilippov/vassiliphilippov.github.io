carbonous

adjective
relating to carbon or its compounds
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrbənəs / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbənəs / (BrE)
relating to, consisting of, or yielding carbon, especially in compounds where carbon is in a lower oxidation state than in carbonic compounds.

Examples

Carbonous oxide
Older chemistry texts refer to carbon monoxide as a carbonous oxide.
Some metals can form both carbonous and carbonic salts, depending on the oxidation state of the carbon.
The solution contains several carbonous compounds derived from fossil fuels.
