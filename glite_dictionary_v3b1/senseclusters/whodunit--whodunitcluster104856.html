whodunit

noun

7.7 0.51/m

A novel, play, film, or story that focuses on solving a crime, especially a murder, and often centers on finding out who committed it.

Examples

A whodunit story
Agatha Christie's stories are famous examples of the whodunit genre.
The detective novel was a classic whodunit that kept readers guessing until the final page.
We watched a gripping whodunit last night, and the ending was completely unexpected.
