photoresist

noun
light-sensitive material
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊrɪˌzɪst / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊrɪˌzɪst / (BrE)
a light-sensitive material used in electronics manufacturing to create patterns on surfaces during processes like photolithography

Examples

Photoresist layer
The technician carefully applied photoresist to the silicon wafer before the next step.
After exposure to ultraviolet light, the photoresist revealed the desired circuit pattern.
Removing the photoresist left behind the intricate design needed for the microchip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a material used to protect parts of a surface during processing
