monaural

adjective
pertaining to one ear only
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈnɔrəl / (AmE), / məˈnɔːrəl / (BrE)
Relating to or involving one ear only, or hearing with a single ear.

Examples

Monaural sound system
Early radio broadcasts were transmitted in monaural rather than stereo sound.
Some people with monaural hearing find it difficult to locate the source of sounds.
Tests revealed she had monaural deafness in her right ear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: binaural - relating to both ears
