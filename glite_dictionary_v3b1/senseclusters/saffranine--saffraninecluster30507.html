saffranine

noun
red synthetic organic dye
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfrəˌnaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsæfrəniːn / (BrE)
any of a class of chiefly red synthetic dyes used in staining and as coloring agents, especially in biological and chemical applications
