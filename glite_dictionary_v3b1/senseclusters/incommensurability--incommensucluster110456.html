incommensurability

noun
impossible to compare
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəˌmɛnsjʊrəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəˌmɛnsjʊərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to compare or measure by a common standard

Examples

Incommensurability of cultures
The incommensurability of the two cultures made meaningful comparison difficult.
Philosophers often debate the incommensurability of scientific paradigms.
The incommensurability between their values led to frequent misunderstandings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: commensurability - the quality of being measurable or comparable by a common standard
  • synonym: incomparability - the state of being impossible to compare
