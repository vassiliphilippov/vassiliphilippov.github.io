concept album

noun
album unified by a common theme
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.sɛpt ˈæl.bəm / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.sɛpt ˈæl.bəm / (BrE)
An album in which all tracks are unified by a common theme, narrative, or idea, either musically or lyrically.

Examples

A concept album theme
He prefers listening to a concept album in order to appreciate its overarching narrative.
Pink Floyd's 'The Wall' is a classic example of a concept album that explores complex themes.
The band's latest concept album tells a continuous story from beginning to end.
