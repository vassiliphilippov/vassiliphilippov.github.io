foraminiferal

adjective
of foraminifera
Pronunciation: / fəˌræməˈnɪfərəl / (AmE), / fəˌræmɪˈnɪfərəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of foraminifera, a group of single-celled marine organisms with shells
