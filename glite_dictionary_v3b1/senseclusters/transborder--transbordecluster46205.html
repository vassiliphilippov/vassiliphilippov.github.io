transborder

adjective
across national borders
6.3
Pronunciation: / trænsˈbɔrdər / (AmE), / tranzˈbɔːdə /; / trænzˈbɔːdə / (BrE)
existing on, extending across, or relating to activities that cross a national border or involve neighboring countries

Examples

A transborder agreement
The two governments signed a transborder agreement to manage the shared water resources.
A transborder highway connects the cities on either side of the frontier.
Environmental groups are working on transborder conservation projects in the region.
The government signed a transborder agreement to improve transportation between the two countries.
Environmental issues often require transborder cooperation to be effectively addressed.
A new transborder railway will connect several major cities across the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cross-border - involving or extending across a border between countries
  • antonym: domestic - existing or occurring within one country
  • synonym: international - involving two or more countries
