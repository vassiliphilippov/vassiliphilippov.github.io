U.S. House

noun
lower chamber of U.S. Congress
Pronunciation: / ˌju ˈɛs ˈhaʊs / (AmE), / ˌjuː ˈes ˈhaʊs / (BrE)
the lower legislative chamber of the United States Congress, formally known as the House of Representatives

Examples

The U.S. House of Representatives
After the election, control of the U.S. House shifted to a different party.
Several members of the U.S. House spoke out in favor of the proposal.
The U.S. House passed a new budget bill yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: House of Representatives - the official name of the lower chamber of the U.S. Congress
  • antonym: U.S. Senate - the upper legislative chamber of the United States Congress
