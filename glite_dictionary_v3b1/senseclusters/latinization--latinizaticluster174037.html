latinization

noun
adoption of Latin style
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌlætənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌlætɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something conform to the Latin language, style, or culture

Examples

Latinization of culture
The latinization of scientific names helps standardize species identification worldwide.
During the Renaissance, the latinization of scholars' names was a common practice.
The latinization of local customs often occurred as the Roman Empire expanded.
