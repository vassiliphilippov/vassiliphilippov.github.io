blow out

verb
extinguish a flame or light by blowing
Pronunciation: / bloʊ aʊt / (AmE), / bləʊ aʊt / (BrE)
to extinguish a flame, light, or fire by blowing air on it

Examples

To blow out candles
Please blow out the lantern before you leave the tent.
She leaned over to blow out the birthday candles on the cake.
The wind was so strong that it blew out the campfire instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extinguish - to make something (a fire, light, etc.) stop burning or shining
  • synonym: quench - to put out a fire or light; to extinguish
