graphicness

noun
vivid explicit detail
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪknəs / (BrE)
the quality of being very clear, vivid, or explicit in detail, especially in describing something unpleasant or shocking

Examples

The graphicness of violence
The graphicness of the crime scene photos shocked the jury during the trial.
Some viewers were disturbed by the graphicness of the war documentary's footage.
The author was criticized for the graphicness in her descriptions of violence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explicitness - the quality of being stated clearly and in detail
  • antonym: subtlety - the quality of being delicate or not obvious
  • synonym: vividness - the quality of being very clear and detailed
