handclasp

noun
act of shaking hands
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌklæsp / (AmE), / ˈhændˌklɑːsp / (BrE)
the act of grasping and shaking another person's hand, often as a greeting, sign of agreement, or introduction

Examples

A handclasp greeting
The deal was sealed with a simple handclasp between the business partners.
The two leaders exchanged a firm handclasp at the start of the meeting.
With a warm handclasp, she welcomed her old friend back home.
