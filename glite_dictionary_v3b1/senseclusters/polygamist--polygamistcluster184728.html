polygamist

noun
person with multiple spouses
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪɡəmɪst / (AmE), / pəˈlɪɡəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who is married to more than one spouse at the same time

Examples

A polygamist man
In some cultures, a polygamist may have several wives at the same time.
The documentary follows the daily life of a polygamist family in rural Utah.
The law prohibits anyone from becoming a polygamist in this country.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bigamist - a person who is married to two people at the same time
  • antonym: monogamist - a person married to only one spouse at a time
