doubling

noun
raising stakes by 2x in a card game
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌblɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌblɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of raising the stakes in a card game by double the previous amount, often as a strategic move to challenge opponents

Examples

A doubling in poker
He surprised everyone at the table with a bold doubling in the second round.
Her doubling forced the opponents to reconsider their strategy.
In bridge, doubling can be a powerful way to signal confidence in your hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: double - to increase by twofold; to become twice as great
