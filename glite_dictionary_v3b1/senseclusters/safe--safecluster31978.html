safe

noun
strongbox for valuables
1.3
Pronunciation: / seɪf / (AmE), / seɪf / (BrE)
A strong container, typically made of metal, used for keeping money, documents, or other valuables secure against theft or damage.

Examples

A bank safe
After the robbery, police found the safe empty and its door open.
The jewelry was locked in the safe overnight for protection.
We keep important documents in a fireproof safe at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vault - a secure room or compartment for storing valuables
