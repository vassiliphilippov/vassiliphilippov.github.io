Bufo canorus

noun
Yosemite toad species
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːfoʊ kəˈnoʊrəs / (AmE), / ˈbjuːfəʊ kəˈnɔːrəs / (BrE)
A species of toad, commonly known as the Yosemite toad, found in high meadows and forest edges of the Sierra Nevada mountains.

Examples

Bufo canorus toad species
Biologists monitor populations of Bufo canorus to assess the health of Sierra Nevada wetlands.

Unlike other amphibians, Bufo canorus is adapted to the cold, high-altitude meadows.
