shawarma

noun
Middle Eastern meat wrap
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈwɑrmə / (AmE), / ʃəˈwɑːmə / (BrE)
a Middle Eastern dish of spiced meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served in flatbread or pita, often with vegetables and sauces
