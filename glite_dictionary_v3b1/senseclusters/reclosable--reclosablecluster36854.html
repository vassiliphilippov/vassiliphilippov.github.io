reclosable

adjective
can be closed again
3.6
Pronunciation: / riˈkloʊzəbəl / (AmE), / riˈkləʊzəbəl / (BrE)

Examples

Reclosable bag

The new coffee jar comes with a reclosable lid for convenience.
