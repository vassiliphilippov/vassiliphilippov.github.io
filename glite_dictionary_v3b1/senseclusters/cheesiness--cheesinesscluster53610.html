cheesiness

noun
cheapness or tackiness
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cheap, corny, overly sentimental, or lacking sophistication

Examples

Cheesiness of a movie
The cheesiness of the movie made it hard to take the story seriously.
He laughed at the cheesiness of the old love songs on the radio.
Some people enjoy the cheesiness of holiday decorations, while others find them too much.
The cheesiness of the commercial made it hard to take seriously.
He laughed at the cheesiness of the romantic dialogue in the film.
Some people enjoy the cheesiness of old pop songs for their nostalgic charm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corniness - the quality of being silly or overly sentimental
  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being stylish and graceful
  • synonym: sentimentality - excessive tenderness or emotion
  • antonym: sophistication - the quality of being refined or cultured
  • synonym: tackiness - the quality of being in poor taste or cheaply made
