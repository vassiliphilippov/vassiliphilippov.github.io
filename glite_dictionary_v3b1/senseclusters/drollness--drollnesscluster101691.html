drollness

noun
amusing oddity
Pronunciation: / ˈdroʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being amusing or funny in an odd, whimsical, or unexpected way

Examples

The drollness of stories
The drollness of his stories always made the children laugh.
She appreciated the drollness in his comments during the meeting.
His drollness brought a lighthearted mood to the otherwise tense discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing or entertaining
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being solemn or not funny
