stupidhead

noun
childish insult
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːpɪdhɛd / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpɪdhɛd / (BrE)
a childish or playful insult for someone considered silly or foolish

Examples

Call him a stupidhead
During the game, she laughed and called her friend a stupidhead for making a silly mistake.
He shouted 'You stupidhead!' when his brother accidentally spilled the juice.
Kids sometimes tease each other by saying things like stupidhead on the playground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dummy - a silly or foolish person, often used as a mild insult
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence or ability
