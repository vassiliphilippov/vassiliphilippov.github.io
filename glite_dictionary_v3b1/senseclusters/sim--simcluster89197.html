sim

noun
simulation instance
Pronunciation: / sɪm / (AmE), / sɪm / (BrE)
an instance of a simulation, especially a computer model or run used to study or predict a process

Examples

Run a sim test
The team ran a sim to test how the new bridge design would handle heavy traffic.
Before building the prototype, they created a sim to predict possible failures.
In the gaming industry, a sim can help developers balance complex systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: experiment - a scientific test to discover how something works
  • synonym: model - a representation used to study or predict how something works
  • synonym: simulation - a model or imitation of a real-world process or system
