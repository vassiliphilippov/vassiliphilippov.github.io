reemergence

noun
return to prominence
5.5
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪ.ˈmɜːr.dʒəns/ (AmE), /riː.ɪ.ˈmɜː.dʒəns/ (BrE)
the act of appearing or becoming active or important again after being absent or having disappeared

Examples
