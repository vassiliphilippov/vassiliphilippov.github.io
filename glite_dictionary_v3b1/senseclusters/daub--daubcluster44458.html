daub

noun
an unskillful painting
9.0
Pronunciation: / dɔb / (AmE), / dɔːb / (BrE)
a painting or picture done in a careless or unskilled way

Examples

A messy daub painting
He was disappointed when his painting was dismissed as a mere daub.
The critics called the work a childish daub rather than true art.
The gallery displayed a daub that barely resembled a portrait.

Related Terms

  • antonym: masterpiece - an outstanding work of art or craftsmanship
  • degree higher: sketch - a rough or unfinished drawing or painting
