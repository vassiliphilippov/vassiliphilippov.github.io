jumpmaster

noun
jumping activity supervisor
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌmpˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌmpˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who supervises and is responsible for the safety of people during jumping activities such as skydiving, parachuting, or bungee jumping

Examples

The jumpmaster checks safety
The jumpmaster checked everyone's harnesses before the group boarded the plane for their first skydive.
Before the bungee jump, the jumpmaster explained the safety procedures to all participants.
A skilled jumpmaster can help nervous jumpers feel more confident before taking the leap.
The jumpmaster gave the final safety briefing before the team boarded the plane.
Each parachutist waited for the jumpmaster to signal when to exit the aircraft.
During training, the jumpmaster carefully inspected all the equipment for defects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instructor - a person who teaches or supervises an activity
  • antonym: paratrooper - a soldier trained to parachute from aircraft
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
