futuristically

adverb

Pronunciation: / fjuːˈtʃɚɪstɪkli /; / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪkli / (AmE), / fjuːˈtʃʊərɪstɪkli /; / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪkli / (BrE)
