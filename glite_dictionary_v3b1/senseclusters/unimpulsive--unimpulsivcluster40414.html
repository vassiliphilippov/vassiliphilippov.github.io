unimpulsive

adjective
not acting on impulse
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈpʌlsɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈpʌlsɪv / (BrE)
not acting on sudden urges or without thinking; showing careful or deliberate behavior

Examples

Unimpulsive decision maker
Because of his unimpulsive approach, he rarely made mistakes in important situations.
Her unimpulsive decisions often led to better outcomes for the whole team.
Being unimpulsive helped him save money instead of spending it on unnecessary things.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautious - careful to avoid risks or mistakes
  • synonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • antonym: impulsive - acting suddenly without thinking
