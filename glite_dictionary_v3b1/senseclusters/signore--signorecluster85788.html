signore

noun
Italian title of respect for a man
4.3
Pronunciation: / siˈnjɔreɪ / (AmE), / siˈnjɔːreɪ / (BrE)
an Italian title of respect used to address a man, equivalent to 'sir' in English, used separately and not prefixed to a name

Examples

The signore said
At the café, he overheard someone calling, "Signore, your coffee is ready!"
The guide addressed the tourist as signore, out of courtesy.
When the signore entered the restaurant, the staff greeted him warmly.
