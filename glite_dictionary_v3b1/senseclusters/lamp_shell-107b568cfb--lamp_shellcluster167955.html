lamp shell

noun
marine animal with a bivalve shell (brachiopod)
Pronunciation: / læmp ʃɛl / (AmE), / læmp ʃɛl / (BrE)
a marine animal with a bivalve shell and a pair of arms bearing tentacles for feeding; belonging to the phylum Brachiopoda

Examples

A lamp shell fossil
Fossils of the lamp shell provide insight into ancient marine environments.
Scientists discovered a rare species of lamp shell on the ocean floor.
The lamp shell is often mistaken for a clam due to its bivalve shell.
