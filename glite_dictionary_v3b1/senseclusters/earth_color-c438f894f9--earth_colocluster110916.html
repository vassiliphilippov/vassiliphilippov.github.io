earth color

noun
naturally occurring pigment mineral
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɜrθ ˈkʌlɚ / (AmE), / ɜːθ ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
A naturally occurring mineral pigment, typically brown, yellow, or red, used in art and decoration.

Examples

Earth color pigment
Artists have used earth color pigments for centuries to achieve natural tones in their paintings.
She preferred to use earth color in her pottery glaze for a rustic look.
The paint was made from a local earth color found near the riverbank.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ochre - an earthy pigment containing ferric oxide, typically with clay, varying from light yellow to brown or red
