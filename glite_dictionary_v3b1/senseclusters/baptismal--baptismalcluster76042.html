baptismal

adjective
relating to baptism
Pronunciation: / bæpˈtɪzməl / (AmE), / bæpˈtɪzməl / (BrE)
of or relating to baptism or the ceremony of baptizing

Examples

Baptismal font
She carefully cleaned the baptismal font before the service began.
The baptismal certificate was kept as a family keepsake.
The child wore a white baptismal gown during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baptism - the Christian religious rite of sprinkling water on someone to admit them to the church
