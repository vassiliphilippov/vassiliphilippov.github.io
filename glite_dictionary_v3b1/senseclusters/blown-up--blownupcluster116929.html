blown-up

adjective
made larger (of a photo or image)
Pronunciation: / bloʊn ˈʌp / (AmE), / bləʊn ˈʌp / (BrE)
(of a photograph or image) made larger than the original size.

Examples

Blown-up photo
I hung a blown-up photograph of my grandparents in the living room.
The blown-up image showed details that were impossible to see in the original.
The blown-up map on the wall helped everyone find their way more easily.
