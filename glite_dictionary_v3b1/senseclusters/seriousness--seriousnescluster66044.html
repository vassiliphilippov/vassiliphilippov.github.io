seriousness

noun
the quality of being serious
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being serious, earnest, or sincere in attitude or manner

Examples

His seriousness at work
Despite his usual jokes, he spoke with great seriousness about the issue.
His seriousness impressed the interview panel and helped him get the job.
There was a seriousness in her voice that made everyone pay attention.
Despite the laughter, there was a certain seriousness in his tone.
His seriousness during the meeting showed how important the issue was to him.
She always approaches her studies with seriousness and dedication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earnestness - the quality of being earnest and honest
  • synonym: gravity - seriousness or solemnity of manner
  • antonym: levity - lack of seriousness
  • synonym: solemnity - the state or quality of being serious and dignified
