take to task

verb
criticize or reprimand severely
Pronunciation: / teɪk tə tæsk / (AmE), / teɪk tə tɑːsk / (BrE)
to criticize or reprimand someone severely for a fault or mistake

Examples

To take to task severely
She was taken to task by her parents for staying out too late.
The editor takes to task writers who submit careless work.
The manager took to task the employees for missing the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reprimand - to express sharp disapproval or criticism
  • synonym: scold - to reprimand angrily
