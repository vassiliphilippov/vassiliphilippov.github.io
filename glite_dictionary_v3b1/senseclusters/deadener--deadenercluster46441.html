deadener

noun
mood dampener
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdənər / (AmE), / ˈdɛdənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that reduces emotional intensity, excitement, or interest

Examples

A deadener for excitement
The negative review acted as a real deadener for the team's motivation.
Lack of audience response can be a deadener for any performer.
His constant criticism was a deadener to the group's enthusiasm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: booster - something that increases enthusiasm or energy
  • synonym: dampener - something that lessens excitement or enthusiasm
  • synonym: killjoy - a person who spoils the enjoyment of others
