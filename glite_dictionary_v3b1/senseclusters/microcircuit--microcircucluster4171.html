microcircuit

noun
tiny electronic circuit on a chip
7.3 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Àåma…™kro äÀàs…úÀêrk…™t / (AmE), / Àåma…™kr…ô äÀàs…úÀêk…™t / (BrE)
A very small electronic circuit, typically built into a semiconductor chip, which forms a complete system rather than a single component.

Examples

A microcircuit chip
Engineers tested each microcircuit for faults before assembling the final device.
Recent advances in microcircuit design have significantly reduced the size of electronic gadgets.
The new smartphone uses a highly advanced microcircuit to manage power consumption efficiently.

Synonyms

  • integrated circuit
    4.2 0.81/m
    an integrated circuit contains many electronic components made on one chip to work as a whole circuit, while a microcircuit is a very small circuit.
  • microchip
    3.7 1.88/m
    a microchip is a small piece of silicon holding an integrated circuit used in devices, while a microcircuit is the small circuit itself.
