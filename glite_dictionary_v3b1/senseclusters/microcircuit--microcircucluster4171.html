microcircuit

noun
tiny electronic circuit on a chip
5.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.sɜːr.kɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.sɜː.kɪt/ (BrE)
