wunderkind

noun
talented young person
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvʊndɚˌkɪnd / (AmE), / ˈvʊndəˌkɪnd / (BrE)
a young person who is exceptionally talented or successful in a particular field

Examples

A young wunderkind
The chess wunderkind defeated much older and more experienced players in the tournament.
She became a tech wunderkind after launching her first successful app at age fifteen.
Many people called him a wunderkind because of his early achievements in mathematics.
