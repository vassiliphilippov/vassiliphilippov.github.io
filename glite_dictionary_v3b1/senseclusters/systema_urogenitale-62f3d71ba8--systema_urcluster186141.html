systema urogenitale

noun
combined reproductive and urinary organ system
Pronunciation: / sɪˈstɛm.ə juː.roʊ.dʒɪˈnæ.tə.li / (AmE), / sɪˈstɛ.mə juː.rə.dʒɪˈnæ.tə.li / (BrE)
the bodily system that includes all organs involved in reproduction and in the formation and voidance of urine; also known as the urogenital system

Examples

The systema urogenitale organs
Any disorders of the systema urogenitale can affect either urinary or reproductive health.
Medical students must learn the anatomy of the systema urogenitale in their first year.
The systema urogenitale is essential for both excretion and reproduction in mammals.
