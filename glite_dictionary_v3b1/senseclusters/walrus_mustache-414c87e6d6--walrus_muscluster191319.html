walrus mustache

noun
thick, drooping mustache style
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔl.rəs məˈstæʃ /; / ˈwɑl.rəs məˈstæʃ / (AmE), / ˈwɔːl.rəs məˈstɑːʃ /; / ˈwɔːl.rəs ˈmʌstæʃ / (BrE)
A thick, bushy mustache that droops over the mouth, resembling the whiskers of a walrus.

Examples

A walrus mustache
After decades, his trademark walrus mustache was as grand as ever.
He sported a massive walrus mustache that drooped well below his lip.

He was famous for his walrus moustache, which covered his upper lip completely.
In the photograph, her grandfather sports a thick walrus moustache and a friendly smile.
The old professor twirled his walrus moustache thoughtfully while pondering the question.
