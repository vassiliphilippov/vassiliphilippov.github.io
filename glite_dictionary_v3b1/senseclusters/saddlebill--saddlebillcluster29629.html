saddlebill

noun
African stork with striking red and black bill
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæd.əl.bɪl / (AmE), / ˈsæd.əl.bɪl / (BrE)
a large black-and-white stork of tropical Africa, notable for its striking red bill with a black band around the middle

Examples

A saddlebill stork
A pair of saddlebills built their nest in the reeds by the river.
The saddlebill is easily recognized by the black band across its bright red bill.
Wildlife enthusiasts traveled to the Okavango Delta to photograph the rare saddlebill.
