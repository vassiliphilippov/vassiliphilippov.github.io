typologically

adverb
by type classification
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌtaɪpəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtaɪpəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the classification of types, especially in language or science

Examples

To compare typologically
The two languages are typologically similar, sharing many grammatical features.
Researchers compared the species typologically to understand their evolutionary relationships.
This approach analyzes cultures typologically rather than focusing on individual differences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: randomly - in a way that lacks any definite plan or order
  • synonym: structurally - in a way that relates to structure or organization
