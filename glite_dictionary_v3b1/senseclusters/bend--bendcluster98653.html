bend

noun
curved segment; road or river curve
4.7 1.1
Pronunciation: /bend/ (AmE), /bend/ (BrE)
a curved or angled part or shape in something that is not straight, such as a road, river, pipe, or body part

Examples

A river bend
Mist rose from the river at each bend through the valley.
The car slowed down as it approached the bend in the road.
The train derailed at a sharp bend just outside the station.
She slowed her bicycle to take the bend safely.
The road made a sharp bend just past the old barn.
There was a noticeable bend in the river after the heavy rains.
The car skidded as it approached a bend in the icy road.
There was a sharp bend in the river where the boats slowed down.
You can't see what's coming until you go around the bend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curve - a shape or line with no straight parts
  • synonym: fold - a line or shape where something is bent over on itself
  • antonym: straight - not bending or curving; without an angle
