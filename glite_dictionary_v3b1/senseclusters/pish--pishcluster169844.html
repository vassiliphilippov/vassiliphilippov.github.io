pish

noun
bird-attracting sound
9.9
Pronunciation: /pɪʃ/ (AmE), /pɪʃ/ (BrE)
a short, sharp hissing or chattering sound made to attract small birds, especially by birdwatchers.

Examples

A soft pish

A quick pish often brings curious chickadees closer for a better look.
He practiced his pish to improve his chances of spotting rare birds.
