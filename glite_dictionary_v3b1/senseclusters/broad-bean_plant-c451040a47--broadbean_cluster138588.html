broad-bean plant

noun
upright plant grown for edible seeds
Pronunciation: / brɔd bin plænt / (AmE), / brɔːd biːn plɑːnt / (BrE)
an upright plant (Vicia faba) of the Old World, grown especially for its large, flat, edible seeds and sometimes as animal fodder

Examples

The broad-bean plant grows
Farmers often grow the broad-bean plant for both human consumption and as animal fodder.
In early spring, we planted rows of broad-bean plants in our vegetable garden.
The broad-bean plant thrives in cool temperatures and is commonly cultivated in Mediterranean regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broad bean - the plant or its edible seed, another term for fava bean
  • synonym: fava bean - the edible seed of the broad-bean plant
