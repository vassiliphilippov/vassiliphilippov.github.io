edge of the abyss

noun
a critical or dangerous turning point
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ əv ði əˈbɪs / (AmE), / ɛdʒ əv ði əˈbɪs / (BrE)
a point or situation where disaster, catastrophe, or crisis is imminent; a critical or dangerous moment

Examples

On the edge of the abyss moment
He felt as if his personal life was teetering on the edge of the abyss.
Many believe climate change has brought humanity to the edge of the abyss.
The company was on the edge of the abyss during the economic recession.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brink - the extreme edge or margin of something, often used in 'on the brink' for crisis
  • synonym: point of no return - the stage at which one must continue on a course of action
  • synonym: verge - the edge or border of something, often used idiomatically for an impending event
