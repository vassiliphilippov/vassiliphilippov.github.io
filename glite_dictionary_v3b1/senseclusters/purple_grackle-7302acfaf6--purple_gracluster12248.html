purple grackle

noun
a North American bird with glossy dark feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈɡrækəl / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈɡrækəl / (BrE)
A type of grackle found in the eastern United States, noted for its glossy purplish-black plumage.

Examples

A purple grackle bird
A flock of purple grackles swooped down to feast on the spilled grain.
The purple grackle is often seen foraging in suburban gardens during spring.
We spotted a purple grackle by the pond, its feathers shimmering in the sunlight.
