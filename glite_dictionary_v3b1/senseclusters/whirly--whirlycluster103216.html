whirly

adjective
dizzy or giddy
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːli / (BrE)
feeling dizzy, light-headed, or mentally confused, as if things are spinning around you

Examples

Feeling whirly after spinning
After spinning in circles, the children felt whirly and had to sit down.
She stood up too quickly and became a bit whirly for a moment.
The heat made him feel whirly and unable to concentrate on his work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear-headed - able to think clearly; not confused or dizzy
  • synonym: dizzy - feeling as if you or everything around you is spinning
  • synonym: giddy - feeling light-headed or unsteady
