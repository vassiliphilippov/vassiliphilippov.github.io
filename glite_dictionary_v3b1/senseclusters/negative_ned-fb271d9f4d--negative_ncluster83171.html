Negative Ned

noun
a persistently pessimistic person
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡətɪv nɛd / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡətɪv nɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for someone who is always pessimistic or only sees the negative side of things

Examples

Negative Ned attitude
Don't be such a Negative Ned; try to see the positives in this situation.
It's hard to get excited about changes in the office with a Negative Ned around.
Whenever someone suggests a new idea, Negative Ned immediately points out all the potential problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Negative Nancy - informal term for a persistently pessimistic person (often female)
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • synonym: pessimist - a person who always expects the worst
