romantically

adverb
in a manner relating to love or romance
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæntɪkli / (AmE), / rəˈmantɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that expresses or relates to love or romance.

Examples

Romantically give flowers
He surprised her romantically with flowers and a candlelit dinner.
She always dreamed of being whisked away romantically to Paris.
They were romantically involved for over a year before getting married.

Related Terms

  • antonym: platonically - in a manner characterized by friendship, not romance
