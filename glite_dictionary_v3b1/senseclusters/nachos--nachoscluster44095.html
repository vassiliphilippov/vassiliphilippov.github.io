nachos

noun
tortilla chip dish
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːtʃoʊz / (AmE), / ˈnatʃəʊz / (BrE)
a dish consisting of tortilla chips topped with melted cheese and often other ingredients such as beans, salsa, or meat

Examples

Cheesy nachos platter
We shared a large plate of nachos at the party before the main meal arrived.
The restaurant serves nachos with spicy salsa and guacamole on the side.
She made homemade nachos by layering cheese, beans, and jalapeños over tortilla chips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quesadilla - a Mexican dish of a tortilla filled with cheese and sometimes other ingredients
  • synonym: tacos - a Mexican dish with a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various mixtures
