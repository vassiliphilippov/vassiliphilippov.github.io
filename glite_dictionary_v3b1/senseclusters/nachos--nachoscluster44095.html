nachos

noun
tortilla chip dish
1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnɑː.tʃoʊz/ /; / /ˈnæ.tʃoʊz/ (AmE), /ˈnæ.tʃəʊz/ /; / /ˈnɑː.tʃəʊz/ (BrE)
