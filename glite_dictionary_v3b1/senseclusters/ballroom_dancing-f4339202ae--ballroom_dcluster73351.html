ballroom dancing

noun
partner dance performed in a ballroom
4.8 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌrum ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlruːm ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
Any of a variety of partner dances, such as the waltz or tango, traditionally performed by couples in a ballroom setting, often socially or competitively.

Examples

Competitive ballroom dancing
Competitive ballroom dancing demands precise technique and excellent coordination between partners.

They enjoy ballroom dancing together every Friday at the local community center.
