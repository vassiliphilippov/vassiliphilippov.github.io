sour-gum family

noun
botanical family including sour gum trees
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊr ˌɡʌm ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈsaʊə ˌɡʌm ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of flowering trees in the order Myrtales, including the sour gum (Nyssa) and related species
