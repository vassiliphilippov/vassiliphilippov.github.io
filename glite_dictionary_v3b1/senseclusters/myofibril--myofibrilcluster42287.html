myofibril

noun
contractile thread in muscle fiber
7.8 8.0
Pronunciation: /maɪ.oʊ.ˈfaɪ.brəl/ (AmE), /maɪ.əʊ.ˈfaɪ.brɪl/ (BrE)
any of the elongated contractile threads found in striated muscle cells, responsible for muscle contraction

Examples

Myofibrils in muscle
