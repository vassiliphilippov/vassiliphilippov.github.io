tagger

noun
person who attaches or adds to something
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtæɡər / (AmE), / ˈtæɡə / (BrE)
a person who appends, attaches, or joins one thing to another, often by adding labels or supplementary material

Examples

Content tagger
A content tagger helps organize large databases by attaching relevant labels to each item.
During the survey, each sample was recorded by a designated tagger to ensure accuracy.
The anonymous tagger added several footnotes to clarify the original manuscript.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attacher - a person who joins or fixes something to another thing
