backwood

noun
remote forest area
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbækwʊd / (AmE), / ˈbækwʊd / (BrE)
a remote or undeveloped forested area, usually far from towns or cities

Examples

A backwood cabin
They built a cabin deep in the backwood to escape city life.
Hiking through the backwood can be both peaceful and challenging.
The old legend tells of a hidden treasure somewhere in the backwood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backwoods - remote, undeveloped, or rural forested regions
  • antonym: city - a large and densely populated urban area
  • synonym: wilderness - an area of land that has not been developed or cultivated
