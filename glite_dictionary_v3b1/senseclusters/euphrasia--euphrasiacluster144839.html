euphrasia

noun
herb for eye health
Pronunciation: / juːˈfreɪʒiə / (AmE), / juːˈfreɪziə / (BrE)
a genus of small flowering plants, commonly called eyebright, used in herbal medicine for eye conditions

Examples

euphrasia plant
Herbalists often recommend euphrasia for soothing irritated eyes.
