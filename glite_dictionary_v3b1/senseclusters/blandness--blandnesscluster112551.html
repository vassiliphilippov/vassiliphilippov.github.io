blandness

noun
lack of emotion or concern
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblænd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈblænd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of showing no emotion, embarrassment, or concern in a situation where some is expected

Examples

Blandness under pressure
Despite the difficult questions, he maintained a remarkable blandness throughout the interview.
The blandness of her response left everyone confused about her true feelings.
The blandness of his confession enraged the judge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotion - a strong feeling deriving from one's circumstances, mood, or relationships
  • synonym: impassivity - lack of emotion or expression
