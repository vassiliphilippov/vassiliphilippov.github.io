quinolone

noun
chemical compound scaffold
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪnəˌloʊn / (AmE), / ˈkwɪnələʊn / (BrE)
a bicyclic organic compound related to quinoline, containing a ketone group, used as a chemical scaffold in research and drug development
