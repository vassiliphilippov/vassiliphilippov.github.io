ripe

adjective
fully matured and ready to eat or use
3.9 1.0
Pronunciation: /raɪp/ (AmE), /raɪp/ (BrE)
(of fruit, food, or crops) fully grown and developed, ready to be eaten, harvested, or used

Examples

Ripe fruit
The ripe tomatoes were picked and used in the salad.

Once the berries turn dark purple, they are considered ripe and ready to eat.
The peaches in the basket are finally ripe and sweet.
You should wait until the tomatoes are ripe before picking them from the vine.
