cut of mutton

noun
slice or portion of mature sheep meat
7.2
Pronunciation: / kʌt əv ˈmʌtən / (AmE), / kʌt əv ˈmʌtən / (BrE)
A portion or slice of meat taken from a mature sheep, especially as prepared for cooking or serving.

Examples

A cut of mutton slice
Each traditional curry used a different cut of mutton for the best flavor.
The chef recommended a juicy cut of mutton for the Sunday roast.
They selected a thick cut of mutton to slow-cook over the open fire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cut of lamb - a portion or slice of meat from a young sheep
