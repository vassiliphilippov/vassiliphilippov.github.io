bristle brush

noun
brush with stiff animal/plant hairs
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪsəl brʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈbrɪsəl brʌʃ / (BrE)
A type of brush with short, stiff hairs made from animal or plant bristles, used for cleaning or painting.

Examples

A bristle brush for painting
A bristle brush works well for painting rough surfaces like wood or stone.
She used a bristle brush to scrub the mud off her boots.
The artist preferred a natural bristle brush for her oil paintings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brush - an implement with bristles for cleaning, painting, etc.
