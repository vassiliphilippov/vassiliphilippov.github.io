weightiness

noun
the condition of being heavy
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪ.ti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈweɪ.ti.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being heavy or having considerable weight

Examples

Weightiness of the metal
Lead is notable for its extreme weightiness compared to other metals.
The weightiness of the box surprised everyone who tried to lift it.
The sculpture's weightiness required a reinforced platform for display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heaviness - the quality of being heavy in weight
  • antonym: lightness - the quality of having little weight
