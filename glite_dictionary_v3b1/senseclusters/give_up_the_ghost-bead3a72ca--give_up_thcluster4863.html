give up the ghost

verb
to die; to cease functioning
8.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /gɪv ʌp ðə goʊst/ /; / /gɪv ʌp ðiː goʊst/ (AmE), /gɪv ʌp ðə gəʊst/ /; / /gɪv ʌp ðiː gəʊst/ (BrE)
(informal, often humorous) To die; to stop functioning or come to an end, especially after a struggle or long period of decline.

Examples

To give up the ghost suddenly
After years of loyal service, my old laptop finally gave up the ghost yesterday.
She fought bravely against illness for months before eventually giving up the ghost.
The engine gave up the ghost halfway through our road trip, forcing us to call for help.

  • synonym: perish - to die, usually in a sudden or untimely way
