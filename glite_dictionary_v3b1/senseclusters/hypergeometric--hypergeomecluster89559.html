hypergeometric

adjective
mathematical distribution
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving a specific mathematical function or probability distribution used in statistics and combinatorics

Examples

The hypergeometric distribution
The hypergeometric distribution is important when sampling without replacement from a finite population.
A hypergeometric function can be used to solve certain types of differential equations in mathematics.
In statistics, hypergeometric probabilities are calculated when the sample size is a significant fraction of the population.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - relating to the normal distribution in statistics
