New Year's Day

noun
first day of the year (1 January)
Pronunciation: / ˌnuː ˈjɪrz deɪ / (AmE), / ˌnjuː ˈjɪəz deɪ / (BrE)
the first day of the year in the modern Western calendar, usually celebrated as a public holiday on 1 January.

Examples

New Year's Day holiday
New Year's Day is a public holiday in many countries around the world.
She made a resolution to exercise more starting on New Year's Day.
We always gather as a family to celebrate New Year's Day together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: New Year - the time at which a new calendar year begins
