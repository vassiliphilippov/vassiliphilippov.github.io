quadrillion

noun
the number 1,000,000,000,000,000; 10¹⁵
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɪljən / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɪljən / (BrE)
The number 1,000,000,000,000,000; one thousand trillion or 10 to the power of 15.

Examples

A quadrillion dollars
If everyone on earth had a billion dollars, the total wealth would still be less than one quadrillion.
It would take thousands of computers working together to solve a quadrillion calculations in a minute.
Scientists estimate that there are over a quadrillion individual ants on the planet.
