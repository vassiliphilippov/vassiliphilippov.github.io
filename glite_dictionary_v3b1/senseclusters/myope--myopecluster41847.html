myope

noun
person with nearsightedness
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪˌoʊp / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪəʊp / (BrE)
a person who is nearsighted; someone affected by myopia

Examples

A myope with glasses
As a child, she was a myope and wore glasses to correct her vision.
Reading small print was never a problem for the myope in the class, but he struggled to see the board.
The doctor explained that a myope can see nearby objects clearly but distant ones appear blurry.

  • synonym: myopic - having myopia; nearsighted
