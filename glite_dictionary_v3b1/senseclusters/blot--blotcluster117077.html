blot

verb
to dry ink with blotting paper
4.1 0.5/m
Pronunciation: / blÉ‘t / (AmE), / blÉ’t / (BrE)
to absorb or dry up liquid from a surface by pressing something soft, like paper or cloth, against it

Synonyms

  • sponge
    2.1 0.1/m
    sponge means wiping or soaking up liquid with a sponge or cloth, while blot means pressing paper or cloth to dry a surface.
  • soak up
    2.6 1/m
