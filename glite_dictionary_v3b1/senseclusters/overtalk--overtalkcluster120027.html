overtalk

verb
to dominate conversation
6.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàt…îk / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàt…îÀêk / (BrE)
to talk so much that you dominate a conversation or prevent others from speaking

Examples

To overtalk the group
She tends to overtalk in group discussions, making it difficult for quieter people to share their ideas.
If you overtalk during interviews, you might miss important questions from the interviewer.
He realized he would often overtalk when he was nervous or excited.
