elbowing

noun
the act of pushing with the elbow
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlˌboʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛlbəʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pushing or jostling someone with one's elbow, especially deliberately or in competition

Examples

Aggressive elbowing in queue
Aggressive elbowing in the queue annoyed those waiting patiently.
He was penalized for elbowing his opponent during the hockey match.
In the crowded subway, some passengers resorted to elbowing to get a better position.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: nudging - the act of gently pushing someone or something to attract attention
  • synonym: shoving - the act of pushing someone or something forcefully
