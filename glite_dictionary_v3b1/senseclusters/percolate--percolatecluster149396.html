percolate

verb
prepare coffee or similar in a percolator
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.kə.leɪt / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.kə.leɪt / (BrE)
to prepare (a beverage, especially coffee) in a percolator by allowing water to pass through the grounds repeatedly

Examples

To percolate coffee
He likes to percolate his coffee every morning for a richer flavor.
Some people still prefer to percolate their tea instead of using a kettle.
The aroma fills the kitchen as the coffee begins to percolate.
