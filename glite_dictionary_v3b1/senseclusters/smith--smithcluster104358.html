smith

verb
to forge metal
7.3
Pronunciation: / smɪθ / (AmE), / smɪθ / (BrE)
to shape or form metal by heating it and hammering, especially using traditional methods

Examples

To smith a sword
The blacksmith spent hours smithing a new horseshoe for the mare.
She enjoys smithing decorative ironwork in her workshop.
Apprentices learn the basics of smithing before attempting more complex projects.
