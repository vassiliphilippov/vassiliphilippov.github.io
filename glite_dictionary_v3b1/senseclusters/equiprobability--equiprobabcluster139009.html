equiprobability

noun
equal likelihood
9.9
Pronunciation: /iː.kwɪ.prə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪ.kwɪ.prə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪ.kwɪ.prə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /iː.kwɪ.prə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the state or condition in which all possible outcomes have an equal chance of occurring

Examples

Equal equiprobability outcomes
In a fair dice game, equiprobability means each number has the same chance of appearing.
The concept of equiprobability is fundamental in basic probability theory.
Without equiprobability, some outcomes would be more likely than others, making the game unfair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bias - a tendency for some outcomes to be favored over others
  • synonym: randomness - the quality of lacking any predictable order or plan
  • synonym: uniformity - the quality of being the same in all cases
