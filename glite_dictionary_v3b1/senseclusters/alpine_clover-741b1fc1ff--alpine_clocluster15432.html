alpine clover

noun
mountain clover with pink flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈælpaɪn ˈkloʊvər / (AmE), / ˈælpaɪn ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
A European clover plant found in mountainous regions, typically with fragrant pink flowers.
