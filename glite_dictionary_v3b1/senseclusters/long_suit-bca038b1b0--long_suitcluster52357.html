long suit

noun
one's strong point or skill
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ suːt / (AmE), / lɒŋ suːt / (BrE)
a particular skill, ability, or area in which a person excels; a strong point

Examples

Her long suit is math
Her long suit has always been creative problem-solving.
Public speaking is not his long suit, but he excels at writing.
Teamwork proved to be the company's long suit during the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forte - a person's strong point; what someone does best
  • antonym: weakness - a lack of strength or a disadvantage
