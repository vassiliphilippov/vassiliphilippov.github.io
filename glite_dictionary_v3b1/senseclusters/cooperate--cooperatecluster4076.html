Synonyms

  • collaborate with
    4.2 2/m
    collaborate with means joining efforts on a shared project in a focused way, while cooperate covers working together more generally.
  • work together
    2.7 30/m
    work together means doing tasks with others in a direct, practical way, while cooperate is slightly more formal and wider in use.
  • collaborate
    4.6 4/m
    collaborate means working jointly, often on creative or intellectual work, while cooperate is more general across many situations.
  • join forces
    3.4 3/m
    join forces means combining power or effort, often for one clear aim, while cooperate is a general word for working together.
  • team up
    2.5 4/m
    team up means joining with others, often briefly or informally, while cooperate describes working together more steadily or formally.
  • come together
    2.7 13/m
