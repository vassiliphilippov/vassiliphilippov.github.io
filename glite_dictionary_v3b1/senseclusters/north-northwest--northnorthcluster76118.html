north-northwest

noun
compass point between north and northwest
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˌnɔrθˈwɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˌnɔːθˈwɛst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point lying halfway between north and northwest, at an angle of 337.5° clockwise from due north

Examples

North-northwest direction
If you head north-northwest from here, you'll reach the coast by sunset.
The ship was sailing north-northwest to avoid the storm.
Winds are expected from the north-northwest at ten miles per hour.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: northwest - the compass point between north and west
