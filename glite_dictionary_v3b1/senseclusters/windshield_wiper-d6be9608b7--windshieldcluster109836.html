windshield wiper

noun
device for clearing a vehicle's windshield
2.3 0.70/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…™ndÀå Éild Ààwa…™p…ôr / (AmE), / Ààw…™nd ÉiÀêld Ààwa…™p…ô / (BrE)
A mechanical device, usually a blade attached to a hinged arm, that moves across a vehicle's windshield to clear rain, snow, or debris.

Examples

A windshield wiper blade
During the snowstorm, the windshield wiper struggled to keep the glass clear.
She turned on the windshield wiper as the rain started pouring down.
The windshield wiper was making a squeaking noise and needed to be replaced.

Synonyms

  • windscreen wiper
    2.3 0.02/m
    windscreen wiper is the British name for the same car blade, while windshield wiper is the American term drivers use.
  • wiper
    2.1 1.72/m
    wiper is a shorter, more general name for the blade, while windshield wiper is the common, specific name for the car's front window device.
