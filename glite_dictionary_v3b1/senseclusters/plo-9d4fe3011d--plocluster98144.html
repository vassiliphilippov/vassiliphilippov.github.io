PLO

noun
Palestine Liberation Organization
5.6 12/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê …õl o ä / (AmE), / piÀê …õl …ô ä / (BrE)
an acronym for the Palestine Liberation Organization, a political movement representing Palestinian Arabs, formed in 1964 to establish an independent state of Palestine

Examples

PLO peace agreement
The PLO signed a peace agreement with Israel in the early 1990s.
The United Nations granted the PLO observer status in 1974.
Yasser Arafat was the longtime leader of the PLO.
