psychosocially

adverb
involving mind and society
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈsoʊʃəli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈsəʊʃəli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves both psychological and social factors

Examples

To be psychosocially affected
The children were psychosocially impacted by the changes in their family environment.
Long-term illness can affect a person psychosocially as well as physically.
Support groups help patients adjust psychosocially after major life events.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: socially - in a way that relates to society or social relations
