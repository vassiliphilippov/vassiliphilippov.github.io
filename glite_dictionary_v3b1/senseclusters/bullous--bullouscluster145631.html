bullous

adjective
with large blisters
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊləs / (AmE), / ˈbʊləs / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by large fluid-filled blisters on the skin or mucous membranes

Examples

Bullous skin disorder
The doctor diagnosed the patient with a bullous skin disorder after examining the blisters.
A bullous eruption appeared on her arm following exposure to the chemical.
Treatment for bullous diseases often involves managing the blisters and preventing infection.
