floorwalker

noun
supervisor in a retail store
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrˌwɑːkər / (AmE), / ˈflɔːˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
an employee in a retail store who supervises sales staff and assists customers with their needs and complaints

Examples

The floorwalker helps customers
Each new employee was introduced to the floorwalker on their first day at the store.
If you have any complaints, please speak with the floorwalker near the entrance.
The floorwalker quickly assisted the customer who was upset about a missing item.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shopwalker - British term for a floorwalker, a retail supervisor
