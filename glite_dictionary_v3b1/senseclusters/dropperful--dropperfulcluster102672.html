dropperful

noun
amount in one dropper
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑːpərfʊl / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpəfʊl / (BrE)
the amount of liquid that fills a dropper once, typically used as a measure for medicine or chemicals

Examples

One dropperful medicine
The instructions say to add one dropperful of the solution to your drink each morning.
She measured a dropperful of medicine and gave it to her child before bedtime.
For best results, use a full dropperful under your tongue every evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dose - a measured amount of medicine to be taken at one time
  • synonym: teaspoonful - the amount that fits in a teaspoon
