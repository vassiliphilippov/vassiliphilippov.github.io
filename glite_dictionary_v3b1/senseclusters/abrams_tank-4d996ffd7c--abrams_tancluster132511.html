Abrams tank

noun
modern US battle tank
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪbrəmz tæŋk / (AmE), / ˈeɪbrəmz tæŋk / (BrE)
a modern American main battle tank used by the military, known for its advanced armor and firepower

Examples

The Abrams tank model
The Abrams tank is equipped with advanced targeting systems for battlefield superiority.
Soldiers trained for months to operate the powerful Abrams tank in combat situations.
An Abrams tank led the convoy through the desert during the military exercise.
