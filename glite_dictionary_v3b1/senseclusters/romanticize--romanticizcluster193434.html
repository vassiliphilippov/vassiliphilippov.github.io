romanticize

verb
give romantic style or character
6.3
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæn.tɪ.saɪz / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæn.tɪ.saɪz / (BrE)
to give something a romantic or sentimental style or character, especially in appearance or presentation

Examples

To romanticize city life
Artists often romanticize city life, giving it an air of mystery and charm.
She loves to romanticize her apartment with vintage furnishings and soft lighting.
The fashion house sought to romanticize traditional rural clothing in its new collection.
