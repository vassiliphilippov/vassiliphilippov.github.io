grainfield

noun
field for growing grain
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪnˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪnfiːld / (BrE)
a field where grain such as wheat, barley, or oats is grown

Examples

A grainfield of wheat
After the rain, the grainfield glistened under the morning sun.
From the window, you could see a vast grainfield stretching to the horizon.
The workers moved through the grainfield at dawn, their sickles ready for harvest.
