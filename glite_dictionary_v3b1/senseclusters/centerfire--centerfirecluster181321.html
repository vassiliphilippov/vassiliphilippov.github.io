centerfire

adjective
cartridge with central primer
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntərˌfaɪr / (AmE), / ˈsɛntəˌfaɪə / (BrE)
describing a cartridge in which the primer is located at the center of the base

Examples

Centerfire cartridge
Most modern rifles use centerfire cartridges for greater reliability and power.
The instructor explained the difference between centerfire and rimfire ammunition to the class.
He purchased a box of centerfire rounds for his hunting trip.
