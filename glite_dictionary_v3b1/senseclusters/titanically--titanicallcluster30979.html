titanically

adverb
extremely powerfully
7.1
Pronunciation: / taɪˈtænɪkli / (AmE), / taɪˈtænɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely large, powerful, or impressive

Examples

To act titanically strong
The skyscraper rose titanically above the city skyline, dominating the view for miles.
She worked titanically to complete the project before the deadline, impressing everyone with her effort.
The river flowed titanically after the heavy rains, sweeping away everything in its path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immensely - to a very great extent
  • antonym: modestly - in a limited or moderate way
