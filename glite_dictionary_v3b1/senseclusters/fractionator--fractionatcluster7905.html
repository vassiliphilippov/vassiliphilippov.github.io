fractionator

noun
separating device
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfrækʃəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfrækʃəneɪtə / (BrE)
a device or apparatus used to separate mixtures into their individual components by fractionation

Examples

A fractionator device
The chemical plant installed a new fractionator to improve the efficiency of its separation process.
A fractionator is essential in oil refineries for dividing crude oil into useful products.
Technicians monitor the fractionator closely to ensure each component is collected correctly.

  • synonym: separator - a device that divides substances into separate parts
