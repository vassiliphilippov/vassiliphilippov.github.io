in memoriam

noun
tribute for the deceased
Pronunciation: / ɪn məˈmɔriəm / (AmE), / ɪn mɪˈmɔːriəm / (BrE)
a printed notice or short tribute honoring someone who has died, often published in a newspaper or program

Examples

Newspaper in memoriam
The family published an in memoriam in the local newspaper to honor their grandmother.
At the end of the concert program, there was an in memoriam for the late conductor.
Many people write an in memoriam each year to remember loved ones who have passed away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obituary - a published notice of a person's death, often with a brief biography
  • synonym: tribute - an act, statement, or gift that shows respect or admiration
