osteotome

noun
bone-cutting tool
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstiəˌtoʊm / (AmE), / ˈɒstɪəˌtəʊm / (BrE)
a surgical instrument used for cutting or preparing bone during medical procedures

Examples

Surgical osteotome
The surgeon selected an osteotome to carefully cut through the patient's bone.
An osteotome is often used in orthopedic surgery to shape or remove bone.
Before inserting the implant, the doctor used an osteotome to prepare the site.

  • synonym: saw - a tool for cutting through materials such as wood or bone
