popper

noun
party noise device
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpər / (AmE), / ˈpɒpə / (BrE)
a small device that makes a loud noise and often releases confetti when activated, typically used at celebrations

Examples

A party popper
Each guest received a colorful popper to use when the clock struck midnight.
The children laughed as confetti burst from the popper during the birthday celebration.
She pulled the string on the popper, filling the room with a loud bang and streamers.
