bookmaking

noun
betting business
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkˌmeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊkˌmeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Sports bookmaking
Bookmaking is strictly regulated in many countries to prevent illegal gambling.
He made a fortune through bookmaking before the government tightened the laws.
Online bookmaking has become increasingly popular with the rise of sports betting websites.
