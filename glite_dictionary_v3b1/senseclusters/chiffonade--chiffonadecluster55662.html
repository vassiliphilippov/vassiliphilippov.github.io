chiffonade

noun
thinly cut herbs
7.8
Pronunciation: / ʃɪfəˈnɑd / (AmE), / ʃɪfəˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a way of cutting leafy vegetables or herbs into thin, ribbon-like strips, or the strips themselves, used in cooking
