Syracusan

noun
person from Syracuse
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪrəˈkjuːzən / (AmE), / ˌsɪrəˈkjuːzən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of Syracuse, especially the city in Sicily, Italy

Examples

An ancient Syracusan
The ancient Syracusan navy was famous for its strength.
A Syracusan merchant brought goods from the Mediterranean to the city market.
Many artifacts from the Syracusan civilization are displayed in the local museum.
