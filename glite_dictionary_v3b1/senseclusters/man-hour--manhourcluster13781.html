man-hour

noun
unit of work time
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈmænˌaʊə / (BrE)
a unit of measurement equal to the work done by one person in one hour

Examples

One man-hour
The construction of the bridge took over 10,000 man-hours to finish.
We estimated that the repairs would require at least 200 man-hours.
By dividing the tasks, the team reduced the total man-hours needed for the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: person-hour - a unit of work equal to one person working for one hour
  • synonym: workload - the amount of work to be done
