cowboy hat

noun
wide-brimmed hat worn by cowboys
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊbɔɪ hæt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊbɔɪ hæt / (BrE)
a hat with a wide brim and a high crown, typically made of felt or straw, associated with North American cowboys

Examples

A cowboy hat
He wore a cowboy hat and boots to the party, making everyone smile.
The iconic cowboy hat shielded the rancher from the blazing desert sun.
Tourists often buy a cowboy hat as a souvenir when visiting Texas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Stetson - brand name often used synonymously with cowboy hat
