'bout time

x
impatient expression for overdue action
2.4
Pronunciation: / baʊt taɪm / (AmE), / baʊt taɪm / (BrE)
Used to express impatience or the feeling that something should have happened already.

Examples

'Bout time you arrived
'Bout time you showed up—I've been waiting for an hour!
He finally finished the report; it's 'bout time he did something useful.
Well, it's 'bout time we heard some good news from the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about time - used to express impatience when a delayed event finally happens
