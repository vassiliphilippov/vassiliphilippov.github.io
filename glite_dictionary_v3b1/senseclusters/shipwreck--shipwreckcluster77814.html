shipwreck

noun
irretrievable loss; total ruin
7.1 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌrɛk / (AmE), / ˈʃɪprɛk / (BrE)
A situation of complete destruction or irretrievable loss, especially as an abrupt end to something important or valued.

Examples

The shipwreck of hope
Her dreams of becoming a dancer ended in the shipwreck of disappointment.
Many relationships suffer the shipwreck of trust after betrayal.
The financial scandal resulted in the shipwreck of his years of hard work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catastrophe - a sudden event causing great and usually sudden damage or suffering
  • synonym: ruin - the complete destruction or collapse of something
  • antonym: salvation - preservation or deliverance from harm or loss
