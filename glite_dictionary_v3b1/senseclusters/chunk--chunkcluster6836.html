chunk

verb
to divide into pieces
Pronunciation: / tʃʌŋk / (AmE), / tʃʌŋk / (BrE)
to break or divide something into smaller pieces or parts

Examples

To chunk a file
To process the large file, we chunked it into smaller sections.
The teacher asked the students to chunk the text into paragraphs for easier reading.
You can chunk your study material to help remember it better.

  • synonym: split - to divide or separate into parts
