naked

adjective
not wearing any clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪkɪd / (AmE), / ˈneɪkɪd / (BrE)
not wearing any clothes or having no covering on the body

Examples

Completely naked
He woke up to realize he was completely naked in a strange bed.
She posed naked for the artist's latest painting.
The toddler ran through the sprinklers naked, laughing with delight.
The children ran naked through the sprinklers in the backyard.
He felt naked without his glasses in the crowded room.
The tree stood naked after all its leaves had fallen in autumn.

  • antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; not naked
  • synonym: nude - not wearing clothes; naked, especially in art
