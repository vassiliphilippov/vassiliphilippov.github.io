Examples

Synonyms

  • airflow
    3.5 2.46/m
    airflow is the movement of air through or around objects, while air flow emphasises measured or controlled movement in a system.
  • flow of air
    2.6 0.13/m
    flow of air means the circulation of air by wind or machines, while air flow focuses on controlled or measured movement.
  • air current
    3.3 0.43/m
    air current is a moving stream of air with noticeable speed, while air flow highlights measured or controlled movement in a space.
  • current of air
    3.5 0.10/m
    current of air is a flow from higher to lower pressure that can be strong, while air flow often means measured movement in a system.
  • airstream
    4.2 0.02/m
    airstream is a steady prevailing wind or defined air flow over distances, while air flow usually refers to measured movement in a system.
