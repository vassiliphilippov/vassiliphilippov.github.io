festivity

noun
joyous celebration or event
Pronunciation: / fɛˈstɪvɪti / (AmE), / fɛˈstɪvɪti / (BrE)
a celebration or enjoyable event marked by lively and cheerful activities

Examples

A festivity event
A sense of festivity filled the air as people gathered for the parade.
Decorations and music added to the festivity of the evening.
The city was alive with festivity during the New Year celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebration - the action of marking one's pleasure at an important event or occasion
  • antonym: solemnity - a formal, dignified rite or ceremony
