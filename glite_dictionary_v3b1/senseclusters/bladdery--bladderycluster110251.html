bladdery

adjective
resembling a bladder
Pronunciation: / ˈblædəri / (AmE), / ˈblædəri / (BrE)
resembling or having the characteristics of a bladder, especially in appearance or texture

Examples

bladdery sacs
Some seaweeds possess bladdery sacs that help them float near the water's surface.
The fungus produced bladdery growths that stood out against the bark of the tree.
The leaves of the plant had a bladdery texture, thin and inflated like tiny balloons.
