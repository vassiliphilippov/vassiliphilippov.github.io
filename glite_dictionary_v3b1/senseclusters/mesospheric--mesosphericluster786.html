mesospheric

adjective
of the mesosphere
9.7
Pronunciation: /me.zə.ˈsfɪr.ɪk/ /; / /miː.zə.ˈsfɪr.ɪk/ (AmE), /me.zə.ˈsfɪə.rɪk/ /; / /miː.zə.ˈsfɪə.rɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the mesosphere, the atmospheric layer above the stratosphere and below the thermosphere

Examples

Mesospheric clouds
Researchers observed unusual mesospheric clouds forming at high altitudes during the summer months.
The satellite collected data on mesospheric winds to help improve weather prediction models.
Changes in mesospheric temperatures can indicate shifts in global atmospheric patterns.
