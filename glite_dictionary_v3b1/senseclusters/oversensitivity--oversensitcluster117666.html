oversensitivity

noun
excessive sensitivity
5.8
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.sen.sɪ.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.sen.sɪ.ˈtɪv.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of reacting too strongly or emotionally to stimuli, criticism, or situations

Examples

Oversensitivity to criticism
Her oversensitivity to criticism made it difficult for her to work in a team environment.
Some people show oversensitivity to loud noises or bright lights.
His oversensitivity often led to misunderstandings with friends and colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypersensitivity - an abnormally high sensitivity to stimuli
  • antonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity or concern for others
