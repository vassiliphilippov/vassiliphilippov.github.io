antipatriotic

adjective
opposed to one's country
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌpætriˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
showing opposition to or lack of loyalty toward one's own country

Examples

Antipatriotic actions
The government labeled the protest as antipatriotic and called for unity.
Some people viewed his refusal to stand for the anthem as antipatriotic behavior.
Critics accused the artist of making antipatriotic statements in her latest interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: patriotic - showing love and loyalty to one's country
  • synonym: unpatriotic - not showing love or loyalty to one's country
