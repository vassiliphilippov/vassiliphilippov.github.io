sleepwalker

noun
person who walks while asleep
2.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈsliːpˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who walks about while still asleep, typically without remembering it afterwards

Examples

A sleepwalker at night
As a sleepwalker, he sometimes found himself in the kitchen at night without remembering how he got there.
Parents of a sleepwalker should take precautions to ensure their child's safety during the night.
The story of the famous sleepwalker fascinated everyone at the party.
