T. E. Lawrence

proper noun
British soldier and writer; led Arab revolt, 1916–18
8.2
Pronunciation: /tiː iː ˈlɔːr.əns/ /; / /tiː iː ˈlɑːr.əns/ (AmE), /tiː iː ˈlɒr.əns/ /; / /tiː iː ˈlɔːr.əns/ (BrE)
(1888–1935) A British archaeologist, army officer, and writer known for his role in organizing the Arab revolt against the Ottoman Empire during World War I, and for his writings about his experiences in the Middle East.

Examples

T. E. Lawrence memoir
In his memoir, T. E. Lawrence vividly describes his experiences during the Arab Revolt.
Many historians study T. E. Lawrence for his influential role in Middle Eastern history.
The film 'Lawrence of Arabia' is based on the life of T. E. Lawrence.
