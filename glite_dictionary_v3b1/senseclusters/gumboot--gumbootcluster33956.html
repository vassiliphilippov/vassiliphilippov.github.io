gumboot

noun
large marine mollusc
9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌm.buːt/ (AmE), /ˈgʌm.buːt/ (BrE)
a large marine mollusc of the North Pacific, also known as the gumboot chiton, with a leathery reddish-brown outer surface

Examples

A gumboot chiton
The tide pool revealed a large gumboot clinging to the rock at low tide.
Marine biologists studied the gumboot to learn more about its unique leathery covering.
Children were excited to spot a gumboot during their field trip to the rocky shore.
