alkaptonuria

noun
rare metabolic disorder with dark urine
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /æl.kæp.tə.n.juː.ˈrɪ.ə/ /; / /æl.kæp.tə.n.jʊə.ˈrɪ.ə/ (AmE), /æl.kæp.tə.n.jʊə.ˈrɪ.ə/ /; / /æl.kæp.tə.n.ʊə.ˈrɪ.ə/ (BrE)
A rare inherited metabolic disorder characterized by the presence of alkapton in the urine and by darkening of connective tissues (ochronosis).

Examples

Alkaptonuria genetic disorder
Because alkaptonuria is genetic, family members were also tested for the disorder.
Doctors diagnosed her with alkaptonuria after noticing her urine turned dark upon standing.
People with alkaptonuria may develop joint problems due to ochronosis.
