natural spring

noun
place where groundwater flows out
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ·ɚ·əl sprɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ·ər·əl sprɪŋ / (BrE)
A place where groundwater flows naturally to the earth’s surface.

Examples

A natural spring source
After a long hike, they were relieved to find a cool natural spring in the woods.
Bottled water companies often claim their products are sourced from a natural spring.
The old village was built near a natural spring that provided fresh water.
