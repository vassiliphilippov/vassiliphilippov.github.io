Copernicus

proper noun
prominent crater on the Moon
Pronunciation: / koʊˈpɜrnɪkəs / (AmE), / kəʊˈpɜːnɪkəs / (BrE)
a large and prominent lunar crater on the Moon, located in the eastern part of the Oceanus Procellarum.

Examples

Copernicus crater
Astronomers often study the Copernicus crater to learn about the Moon's geological history.
Many lunar maps highlight Copernicus as one of the most remarkable features of the Moon.
Through my telescope, I could clearly see Copernicus shining on the lunar surface.
