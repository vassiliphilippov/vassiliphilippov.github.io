vizsla

noun
Hungarian hunting dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈviːʒ.lə / (AmE), / ˈvɪz.lə / (BrE)
A Hungarian hunting dog breed known for its short, deep reddish coat, lean build, and keen sense of smell and sight.

Examples

A vizsla dog
After much research, they decided a vizsla would suit their active lifestyle.
The vizsla is prized for its versatility and loyalty by hunters and families alike.
The dog's short, rust-colored coat is a distinguishing feature of the vizsla.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Weimaraner - German breed of large hunting dog, usually with a gray coat
