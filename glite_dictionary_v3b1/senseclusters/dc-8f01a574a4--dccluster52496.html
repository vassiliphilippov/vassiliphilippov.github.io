Synonyms

  • D.C.
    2.1 0.00/m
    D.C. is the abbreviation with periods used in formal writing, while DC is the version without dots often seen on signs and headlines.
  • District of Columbia
    3.5 2.03/m
    District of Columbia is the full official name for the federal district, while DC is its short, commonly used abbreviation.
  • Washington D.C.
    1.0 24/m
    Washington D.C. names the capital city and sounds like a place name, while DC refers mainly to the district abbreviation.
  • Washington
    1.0 167/m
    Washington usually refers to the city in speech, while DC is the abbreviation that points to the federal district in official contexts.
