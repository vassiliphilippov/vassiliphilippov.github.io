waah

noun
cry of distress
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / w…ëÀê / (AmE), / w…ëÀê / (BrE)
a long, high-pitched cry or wail expressing distress, complaint, or unhappiness, especially by a baby or child

Examples

A baby's waah

He let out a loud waah when he dropped his ice cream on the ground.
You could hear her waah from the next room after she fell.
