Sub-Saharan Africa

proper noun
region of Africa south of the Sahara
Pronunciation: / sʌb səˈhɛrən ˈæfrɪkə / (AmE), / sʌb səˈheərən ˈæfrɪkə / (BrE)
the area of Africa located south of the Sahara Desert, typically distinguished culturally and geographically from North Africa.

Examples

Sub-Saharan Africa region
Climate change has disproportionately affected Sub-Saharan Africa compared to other parts of the world.
International aid organizations often focus their efforts on Sub-Saharan Africa due to ongoing development challenges.
Many languages are spoken throughout Sub-Saharan Africa, reflecting its great cultural diversity.
