consolamentum

noun
act of consolation
9.9
Pronunciation: / kənˌsoʊləˈmɛntəm / (AmE), / kənˌsɒləˈmɛntəm / (BrE)
an act of giving comfort or consolation, especially in a spiritual or emotional sense

Examples

Spiritual consolamentum

The old text described a ritual of consolamentum for those in mourning.
She offered consolamentum to her grieving neighbor through daily visits and prayers.
