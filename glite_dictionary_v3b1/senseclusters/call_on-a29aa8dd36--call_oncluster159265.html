call on

verb
to visit briefly
3.1 2.23/m
Pronunciation: / k…îl …ën / (AmE), / k…îÀêl …ín / (BrE)
to visit someone, usually briefly, often for a specific purpose

Examples

To call on a friend
I plan to call on my grandmother this afternoon to see how she's doing.
The doctor will call on several patients at their homes today.
We decided to call on our neighbors to invite them to the party.
Diplomats often call on local officials when visiting a new city.
She decided to call on her neighbor after work to check in.
The professor will call on several students during office hours.
