Cosa Nostra

noun
Italian-American crime syndicate
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊsə ˈnɔstrə / (AmE), / ˌkəʊsə ˈnɒstrə / (BrE)
A criminal syndicate in the United States, organized into families and linked to the Sicilian Mafia.

Examples

Cosa Nostra family
He was suspected of having links to the Cosa Nostra in New York.
Many movies portray the traditions and code of silence within the Cosa Nostra.
The FBI launched an investigation into the Cosa Nostra after several high-profile crimes came to light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mafia - the Sicilian-based organized crime syndicate
