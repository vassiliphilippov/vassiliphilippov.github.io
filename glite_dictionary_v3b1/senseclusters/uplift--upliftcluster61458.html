uplift

verb
raise land by geological forces
5.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʌ.plɪft/ (AmE), /ˈʌ.plɪft/ (BrE)
(of land) to raise or be raised to a higher position or level, especially as a result of geological processes.

Examples

To uplift the land
Geological processes over millions of years uplifted the entire mountain range.
Seismic activity has uplifted parts of the valley, exposing ancient rock layers.
The rapid movement of tectonic plates can uplift coastal regions, changing the landscape dramatically.
