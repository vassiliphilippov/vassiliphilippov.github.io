meritoriousness

noun
quality of being deserving or worthy
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔriəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being deserving praise or reward; worthiness or merit.

Examples

The meritoriousness of work
Debates continue over the meritoriousness of granting awards for participation.
His essay was praised for its originality and meritoriousness by the professor.
The meritoriousness of her actions was recognized by the entire committee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undeserving - not worthy of reward or praise
  • synonym: worthiness - the quality of being worthy or deserving
