whichever

conjunction
no matter which
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃˈɛvər / (AmE), / wɪtʃˈɛvə / (BrE)
used to introduce a clause meaning 'no matter which one' when presenting a choice among alternatives

Examples

Whichever bus comes first
You can take whichever bus arrives first to get to the city center.
She will support you, whichever decision you make.
Choose whichever color you like best for your new room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - used to refer to anything or everything that
