sentiment

noun
emotional polarity of text
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsen.tɪ.mənt/ /; / /ˈsen.tə.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈsen.tɪ.mənt/ (BrE)
(in computational linguistics and data analysis) the emotional tone, polarity (positive, negative, neutral), or expressed attitude in a piece of text; the property measured in sentiment analysis.

Examples

Sentiment analysis
Researchers analyzed customer reviews by measuring their sentiment to gauge overall satisfaction.
The social media team tracked daily sentiment to see how followers reacted to the announcement.
An algorithm assigned a positive or negative sentiment score to each product review automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opinion - a view or judgment expressed by someone; similar to sentiment when expressed in text
  • synonym: polarity - the positive, negative, or neutral orientation often assigned to text in sentiment analysis
