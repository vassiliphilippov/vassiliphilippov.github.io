unspeakably

adverb
to an inexpressible degree
2.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspiːkəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈspiːkəbli / (BrE)
to a degree that cannot be expressed in words; indescribably.

Examples

To be unspeakably grateful
She was unspeakably grateful to everyone who helped her during the crisis.
The loss of his childhood pet made him unspeakably sad for weeks.
The view from the mountain summit was unspeakably beautiful at sunrise.

Related Terms
