wheel spoke

noun
rod connecting wheel hub to rim
7.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /wiːl spoʊk/ /; / /hwiːl spoʊk/ (AmE), /wiːl spəʊk/ /; / /hwiːl spəʊk/ (BrE)
