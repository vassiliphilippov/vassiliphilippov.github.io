wheel spoke

noun
rod connecting wheel hub to rim
Pronunciation: / wiːl spoʊk / (AmE), / wiːl spəʊk / (BrE)
one of the bars or rods radiating from the center of a wheel to its outer edge, providing structural support

Examples

A wheel spoke
A missing wheel spoke can make a wheel unstable and dangerous.
She carefully cleaned each wheel spoke to restore the old carriage.
The bicycle's wheel spokes were bent after the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoke - a bar or rod of a wheel connecting the hub with the rim
