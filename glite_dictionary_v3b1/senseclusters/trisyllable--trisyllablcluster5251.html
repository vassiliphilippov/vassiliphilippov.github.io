trisyllable

noun
word with three syllables
Pronunciation: / traɪˈsɪləbəl / (AmE), / traɪˈsɪləb(ə)l / (BrE)
A word consisting of three syllables.

Examples

A trisyllable word
In poetry, a trisyllable can fit neatly into a single metrical foot.
Teachers often ask students to count the syllables in a trisyllable.
The word trisyllable itself is a good example because it has exactly three syllables.
