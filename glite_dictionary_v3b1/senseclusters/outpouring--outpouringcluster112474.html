outpouring

noun
a flowing out of a fluid
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpɔrɪŋ /; / aʊtˈpɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌpɔːrɪŋ /; / aʊtˈpɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden and large flow or expression of something, such as liquid, emotion, or response

Examples

A sudden outpouring of lava
A sudden outpouring of lava covered the hillside after the eruption.
The heavy rain caused an outpouring of water onto the streets.
There was an outpouring of champagne from the bottle as it was opened.
A sudden outpouring of lava threatened the nearby villages.
The storm caused an outpouring of water down the hillside within minutes.
There was an outpouring of tears when the sad news was announced.
An outpouring of emotion swept through the audience after the announcement.
The accident triggered an outpouring of support for the victims' families.
There was an outpouring of sympathy from people around the world.
