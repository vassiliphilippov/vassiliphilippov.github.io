subthalamic

adjective
beneath the thalamus
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbθəˈlæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌsʌbθəˈlæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the area beneath the thalamus in the brain

Examples

Subthalamic nucleus
The subthalamic nucleus is important in regulating movement in the brain.
Researchers studied the effects of stimulation in the subthalamic region for Parkinson's disease.
Damage to subthalamic structures can lead to motor control problems.
