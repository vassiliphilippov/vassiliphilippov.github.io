unconceal

verb
to reveal
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsiːl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsiːl / (BrE)

Examples

To unconceal the truth
The investigation helped to unconceal the truth behind the rumors.
Her expression did little to unconceal her true feelings about the decision.
The new evidence will unconceal what really happened that night.

Related Terms
