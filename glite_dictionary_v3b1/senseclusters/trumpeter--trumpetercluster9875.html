trumpeter

noun
large South American bird with loud cry
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpətər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtə / (BrE)
a large, social bird of South American forests, with glossy black feathers and a loud, prolonged call, often kept as a domestic animal

Examples

Amazon trumpeter
A flock of trumpeters was spotted near the riverbank, searching for berries.
Local villagers often keep a trumpeter as a watchdog, thanks to its loud cries.
The trumpeter is known for its resonant call echoing through the Amazon rainforest.
