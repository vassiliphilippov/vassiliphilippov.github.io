heatmap

verb
to visualize data intensity
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtˌmæp / (AmE), / ˈhiːtˌmæp / (BrE)
to create or analyze a visual representation of data using colors to show intensity or frequency

Examples

To heatmap data
The team decided to heatmap user activity to identify the most popular sections of the website.
After collecting the data, we will heatmap the results to highlight areas of high engagement.
Designers often heatmap app interfaces to improve user experience based on click patterns.
