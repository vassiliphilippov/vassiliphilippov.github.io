taxi

verb
(of an aircraft) move on the ground
9.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtæk.siː/ /; / /ˈtæk.sɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtæk.siː/ /; / /ˈtæk.sɪ/ (BrE)
(of an aircraft) to move slowly along the ground under its own power before takeoff or after landing.

Examples

To taxi to runway
After takeoff clearance, the pilot taxied the jet onto the runway.
Passengers remained seated while the aircraft taxied to the gate.
The plane taxied slowly towards the terminal after landing.
