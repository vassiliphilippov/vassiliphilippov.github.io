cloud of uncertainty

noun
state of doubt or indecision
5.4
Pronunciation: / klaʊd əv ənˈsɝːtnti / (AmE), / klaʊd əv ʌnˈsɜːtnti / (BrE)
a state or feeling of doubt and lack of confidence about what will happen

Examples

A cloud of uncertainty
A cloud of uncertainty hangs over the company's future after the recent merger talks.
Despite the announcement, a cloud of uncertainty still lingered in the community.
There was a cloud of uncertainty surrounding the outcome of the negotiations.
