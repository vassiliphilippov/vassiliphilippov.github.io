cloud of uncertainty

noun
state of doubt or indecision
6.7 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / kla äd …ôv …ônÀàs…ùÀêtnti / (AmE), / kla äd …ôv  ånÀàs…úÀêtnti / (BrE)

Examples

A cloud of uncertainty

There was a cloud of uncertainty surrounding the outcome of the negotiations.
