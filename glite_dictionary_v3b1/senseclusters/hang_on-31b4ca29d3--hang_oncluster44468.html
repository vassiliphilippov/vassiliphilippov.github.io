hang on

verb
wait on the telephone line
Pronunciation: / hæŋ ɑn / (AmE), / hæŋ ɒn / (BrE)
to remain connected on a telephone line; to wait without ending the call

Examples

To hang on the phone
Could you hang on a minute while I transfer your call?
He asked the caller to hang on while he checked the schedule.
If you hang on, I'll find out if she's available to speak with you.
