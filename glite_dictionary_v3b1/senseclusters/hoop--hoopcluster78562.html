hoop

noun
rigid circular band for fastening or holding
1.5 2.46/m
Pronunciation: / huÀêp / (AmE), / huÀêp / (BrE)
a rigid circular band, typically made of metal, wood, or other material, used for holding together or fastening objects, or for hanging or pulling things

Examples

Metal hoop
A metal hoop stuck out from the wall for hanging tools.
He attached the bucket to the hoop and lowered it into the well.
The old wooden barrel was held together by iron hoops.

Synonyms

  • ring
    1.0 45/m
    ring is a small metal band often worn as jewelry or used for small fastenings, while hoop is a larger rigid band for holding or hanging things.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loop - a shape produced by a curve that bends around and crosses itself
  • synonym: ring - a small circular band, typically of metal, worn on a finger or used as a fastening
