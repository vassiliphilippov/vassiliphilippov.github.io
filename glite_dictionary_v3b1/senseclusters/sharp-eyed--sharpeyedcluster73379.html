sharp-eyed

adjective
having keen eyesight
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp aɪd /; / ʃɑrp ˈaɪd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp aɪd /; / ʃɑːp ˈaɪd / (BrE)
