blanker

adjective
more expressionless
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblæŋkə / (BrE)
more expressionless, empty, or lacking detail or content than something else.

Examples

Blanker expression
Her eyes became blanker as she listened to the disappointing news.
The wall looked even blanker after they removed the last painting.
His response was blanker than I expected, showing no emotion at all.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blank - expressionless or empty
  • synonym: emptier - having less content or detail
  • antonym: fuller - having more content or detail
