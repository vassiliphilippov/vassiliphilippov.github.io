grade

noun
mark showing student performance
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ɡreɪd / (BrE)
a number or letter given to show how well a student has done in an exam or course

Examples

A grade on a test
His final grade in history was lower than expected.
She was thrilled to receive an A grade on her math test.
Universities often require a minimum grade for admission to their programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: score - the number of points achieved in a test or game
