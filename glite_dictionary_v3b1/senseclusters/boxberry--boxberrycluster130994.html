boxberry

noun
creeping North American plant with red berries
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈbɒksˌbɛri / (BrE)
a creeping evergreen plant native to eastern North America, with glossy leaves and bright red, spicy berries (the source of wintergreen oil)

Examples

Bright boxberry berries
Birds are known to feast on the bright scarlet berries of the boxberry during autumn.
She planted a boxberry in her shade garden to add year-round greenery.
The forest floor was dotted with boxberry plants, their red fruit standing out against the green leaves.
Birds are often attracted to the bright red boxberries in late autumn.
She recognized the spicy scent of boxberry while hiking through the forest.
The boxberry is used to produce oil of wintergreen for flavoring and medicinal products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wintergreen - an aromatic plant whose leaves or berries yield wintergreen oil
