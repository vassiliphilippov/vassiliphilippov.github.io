untraversed

adjective
not traveled over or explored
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌntrəˈvɜrsd / (AmE), / ˌʌntrəˈvɜːst / (BrE)
(of a place) not traveled over or through; not explored or crossed by people.

Examples

An untraversed path
Much of the dense jungle remains untraversed by humans due to its difficult terrain.
The documentary focuses on the challenges of exploring untraversed regions of the Arctic.
The hikers ventured into an untraversed mountain pass, relying only on their maps and instincts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: explored - having been investigated or traversed
  • synonym: untrodden - not having been walked on
