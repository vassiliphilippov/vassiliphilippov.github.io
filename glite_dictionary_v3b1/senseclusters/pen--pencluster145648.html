pen

noun
an enclosure for livestock or animals
Pronunciation: / pɛn / (AmE), / pɛn / (BrE)
a small enclosure for confining animals, especially livestock such as pigs, sheep, or poultry.

Examples

A pig pen
Please make sure the gate to the pig pen is properly closed.
The chickens clucked happily inside their pen in the backyard.
The farmer built a sturdy pen to keep his sheep safe at night.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cage - a structure of bars or wires for confining animals or birds
  • synonym: corral - an enclosure for confining livestock, especially cattle or horses
