infant

noun
very young child (under one year)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.fənt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fənt / (BrE)
a very young child, especially one under one year old who has not yet begun to walk or talk

Examples

A infant baby
An infant requires constant attention and care from adults.
She enrolled her infant in a parenting class for new mothers.
The hospital provides special care for premature infants.
