navigational

adjective
relating to navigation
Pronunciation: / ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌnavɪˈɡeɪʃ(ə)nəl / (BrE)
relating to the process or activity of navigating, especially the movement of ships, aircraft, or vehicles.

Examples

Modern navigational systems
Modern ships use sophisticated navigational systems for precise positioning at sea.
The hikers brought a compass as a vital navigational tool in the dense forest.
The pilot relied on navigational charts to safely guide the plane through the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigation - the process or activity of accurately ascertaining one's position and planning and following a route
