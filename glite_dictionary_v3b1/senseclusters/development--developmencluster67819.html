development

noun
photographic processing to reveal an image
3.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpmənt / (BrE)
the process of treating a photosensitive material, such as photographic film, to make an image visible

Examples

Film development
Advances in digital photography have greatly reduced the need for traditional development.
Proper development techniques ensure sharp and vivid photographic images.
The development of black-and-white film requires careful temperature control.
