demonstrativeness

noun
quality of openly showing emotions
Pronunciation: / dɪˌmɑn.strəˈtɪv.nəs / (AmE), / dɪˌmɒn.strəˈtɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of openly and freely expressing one's feelings or emotions

Examples

Open demonstrativeness
He admired his friend's demonstrativeness, which contrasted with his own reserved nature.
Her demonstrativeness made everyone feel instantly comfortable at the party.
In some cultures, demonstrativeness is encouraged, while in others, restraint is valued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressiveness - the quality of effectively conveying thoughts or feelings
  • antonym: reserve - the quality of not expressing one's feelings openly
