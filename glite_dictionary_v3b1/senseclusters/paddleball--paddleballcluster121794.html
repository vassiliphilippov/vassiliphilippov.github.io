paddleball

noun
sport with paddles
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a sport in which players use solid paddles to hit a small rubber ball against a wall or over a net

Examples

Paddleball match
They joined a local league that plays paddleball every Tuesday at the community sports center.
The children spent the afternoon learning how to play paddleball on the new court.
Many people enjoy paddleball because it is easy to learn and a great workout.

Related Terms

  • synonym: racquetball - a sport played with rackets and a small ball in an indoor court
  • synonym: tennis - a sport where players use rackets to hit a ball over a net
