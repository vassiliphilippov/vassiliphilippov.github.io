mote

verb
to cover with specks
9.9
Pronunciation: / moʊt / (AmE), / məʊt / (BrE)
to cover or become covered with small particles or specks, especially dust (archaic or rare)

Examples

To mote a surface
By evening the freshly sanded floor was moted with fine dust from the workshop.
The old windows were moted with particles that danced in the sunlight.
After the storm, the leaves were moted with tiny bits of debris.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - to remove dirt or particles from something
  • synonym: speckle - to mark with small spots or specks
