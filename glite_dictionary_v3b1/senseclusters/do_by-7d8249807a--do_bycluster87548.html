do by

verb
treat or deal with someone
Pronunciation: / du ˈbaɪ / (AmE), / duː ˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to treat or deal with someone in a particular way

Examples

To do by someone
He tried to do by his employees as fairly as possible.
She hopes her children will do by her with respect and kindness.
You should always do by others as you would have them do by you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mistreat - to treat someone badly
  • synonym: treat - to behave toward or deal with in a particular way
