forkless

adjective
without a fork
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrkləs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkləs / (BrE)
not having a fork or lacking a fork as part of something

Examples

Forkless lunch set
He improvised with chopsticks because his lunch set was forkless.
The picnic kit was completely forkless, so we had to eat with our hands.
She noticed the cutlery drawer was forkless after the party.
