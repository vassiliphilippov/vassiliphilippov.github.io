metal money

noun
money in the form of coins
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛt̬l ˈmʌni / (AmE), / ˈmɛtl ˈmʌni / (BrE)
Money in the form of coins, as opposed to paper money.

Examples

Metal money coins
Many ancient civilizations used metal money for trade and daily transactions.
She emptied her purse to find enough metal money for the bus fare.
The vending machine only accepts metal money, so you'll need coins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coin - a flat, typically round piece of metal used as money
  • antonym: paper money - money in the form of banknotes, not coins
