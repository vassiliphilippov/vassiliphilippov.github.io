recycling

noun
reuse of digital content
3.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈsaɪ.kəl.ɪŋ/ /; / /riː.ˈsaɪ.klɪŋ/ (AmE), /riː.ˈsaɪ.kəl.ɪŋ/ /; / /riː.ˈsaɪ.klɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of reusing or repurposing existing digital content (such as articles, posts, videos, or images) for new formats or audiences.

Examples

Digital recycling
Many online publishers practice recycling by repurposing old posts as new articles.
The marketing team proposed a recycling plan to turn webinars into short social videos.
She complained about the constant recycling of memes instead of producing original material.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repurpose - to adapt existing content or material for a new use or audience
  • synonym: reuse - to use something again in its current form or with minimal change
