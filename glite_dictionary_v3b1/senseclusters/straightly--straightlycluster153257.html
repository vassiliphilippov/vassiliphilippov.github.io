straightly

adverb
directly, without deviation
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪtli / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtli / (BrE)
in a straight or direct manner, without deviation or curve

Examples

Walk straightly forward
He walked straightly down the narrow path, never turning his head.
The river flows straightly through the valley for several miles.
She answered the question straightly, without hesitation or evasion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directly - in a straight line or without delay
  • antonym: indirectly - not in a straight or direct way
