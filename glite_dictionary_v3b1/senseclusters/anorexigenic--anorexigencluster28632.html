anorexigenic

adjective
causing loss of appetite
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˌrɛksɪˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌan.ə.rɛk.sɪˈdʒɛn.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance or action) causing a loss of appetite or suppressing appetite.

Examples

Anorexigenic effect
Researchers are studying new compounds with anorexigenic properties for possible use in weight management.
Some stimulants can have anorexigenic effects, reducing the desire to eat.
The doctor prescribed an anorexigenic medication to help manage the patient's obesity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetite suppressant - a drug or agent that reduces appetite
  • antonym: orexigenic - stimulating or increasing appetite
