late-flowering

adjective
blooming in autumn or late season
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪt ˌflaʊərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈleɪt ˌflaʊə.rɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming or flowering in the late part of the growing season, typically in autumn.

Examples

Late-flowering plants
The garden looks beautiful in September, thanks to several late-flowering perennials.
This variety of chrysanthemum is known for being late-flowering compared to others.
We added some late-flowering asters to ensure color lasts into autumn.

Related Terms
