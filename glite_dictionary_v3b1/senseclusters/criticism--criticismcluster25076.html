Examples

Synonyms

  • critique
    5.6 15/m
    a critique gives a focused, detailed analysis of a work, while criticism is a broader spoken or written judgment of merits and faults.
  • critical analysis
    5.1 0.90/m
    a critical analysis examines a work closely to judge techniques and meaning, while criticism is any spoken or written evaluation.
  • critical review
    4.1 0.12/m
    a critical review is an article that evaluates a work for readers, while criticism can be any spoken or written judgment.
  • review
    3.0 39/m
    a review is an article that gives an opinion about a book or film, while criticism focuses more on analysis and judgment.
