criticism

noun
evaluation of a creative work
1.5 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪt.ɪ.sɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a written or spoken evaluation of a literary, artistic, or other creative work, especially one that analyzes and judges its merits and faults

Examples

Art criticism
He specialized in art criticism and wrote many essays on modern painters.
Her criticism of the new novel was published in the literary magazine.
The play received glowing criticism from several well-known reviewers.
