abominator

noun
person who hates or detests
Pronunciation: / əˈbɑːməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / əˈbɒmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person who hates or strongly detests someone or something

Examples

An abominator of injustice
As an outspoken abominator of injustice, she never hesitated to speak her mind.
He earned a reputation as an abominator of modern art, refusing to visit any galleries.
The villain in the novel is an abominator of all things good and pure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hater - person who intensely dislikes something or someone
