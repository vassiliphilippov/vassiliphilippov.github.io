undressed

adjective
without clothing; not dressed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɛst / (BrE)
having removed all or some clothing; not wearing clothes

Examples

Completely undressed
After the party, he quickly undressed and got into bed.
She felt embarrassed when she realized she was undressed in front of the window.
The doctor asked him to get undressed before the examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dressed - wearing clothes
  • synonym: naked - without any clothes on
