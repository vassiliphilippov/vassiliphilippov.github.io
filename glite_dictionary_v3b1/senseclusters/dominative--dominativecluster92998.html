dominative

noun
controlling person
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmɪˌneɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
a person who tends to dominate or control others in a group or situation

Examples

A dominative in meetings
The new manager quickly gained a reputation as a dominative who rarely listened to feedback.
In group projects, a dominative often tries to take charge of every decision.
Some people find it difficult to work with a dominative because of their controlling nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominator - a person who exercises control or power over others
  • antonym: subordinate - a person under the authority or control of another
