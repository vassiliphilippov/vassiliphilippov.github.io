Austrian winter pea

noun
forage variety of pea plant
Pronunciation: / ˈɔstriən ˈwɪntər pi / (AmE), / ˈɒstriən ˈwɪntə piː / (BrE)
A variety of pea plant (Pisum sativum subsp. arvense), native to the Mediterranean region and North Africa, widely grown as a forage crop, especially in cooler climates.

Examples

Austrian winter pea plant
Adding Austrian winter pea to the rotation improves forage quality for livestock.
Farmers often plant Austrian winter pea as a cover crop to enrich the soil.
The field was lush with blooming Austrian winter pea in early spring.
