rangy

adjective
adapted to wandering or roaming
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒi / (BrE)
(of an animal) adapted to wandering or roaming; suited to wide-ranging movement over large areas.

Examples

Rangy wild horse
Mustangs are known for their rangy build and ability to cover great distances in search of food.
She adopted a rangy dog that loved nothing more than exploring the countryside.
The rangy cattle grazed freely across the vast prairie, hardly stopping in one place for long.
