armory

noun
collection of resources or skills
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrməri /; / ˈɑːrˌmɛri / (AmE), / ˈɑːməri / (BrE)
a collection of resources or skills that are available for use, especially to help achieve something

Examples

Armory of skills
A good leader possesses an armory of strategies to handle different situations.
She drew upon her vast armory of negotiation skills to resolve the dispute.
Writers often dip into their armory of experiences when creating new stories.
He drew upon his armoury of persuasive techniques to win the debate.
She considered patience one of the greatest tools in her armoury as a teacher.
The company's vast armoury of patents gives it an advantage over competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arsenal - a collection of resources, weapons, or tools available for use
