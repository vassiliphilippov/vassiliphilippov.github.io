wedge

noun
type of golf club with high loft
Pronunciation: / wɛdʒ / (AmE), / wɛdʒ / (BrE)
a type of golf club with a high loft and a broad sole, designed for short approach shots and for playing out of sand bunkers

Examples

A golf wedge
Golfers often carry more than one wedge to handle different course conditions.
She pulled out her wedge to chip the ball onto the green.
The bunker shot was made easier with a sand wedge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iron - a type of golf club with a metal head, generally used for mid-range shots
