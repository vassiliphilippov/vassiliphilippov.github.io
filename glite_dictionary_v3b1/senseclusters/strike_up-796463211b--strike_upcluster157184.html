strike up

verb
to begin something, such as a conversation
Pronunciation: / straɪk ʌp / (AmE), / straɪk ʌp / (BrE)
to begin a conversation, relationship, or activity, often in a sudden or informal way

Examples

To strike up a conversation
It can be hard to strike up a conversation with strangers at a party.
They quickly struck up a friendship after meeting at university.
Whenever the band strikes up a lively tune, everyone starts to dance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: end - to bring to a conclusion or finish
  • synonym: start - to begin something
