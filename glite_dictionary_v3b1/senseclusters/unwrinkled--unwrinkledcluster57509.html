unwrinkled

adjective
smooth, not wrinkled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪŋkəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋkəld / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially skin or fabric) smooth and free from wrinkles or creases.

Examples

An unwrinkled shirt
He wore a freshly ironed, unwrinkled shirt to the interview.
Her face remained remarkably unwrinkled despite her advancing age.
The fabric feels smooth and unwrinkled after using the new laundry product.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • antonym: wrinkled - having small lines or folds on the surface
