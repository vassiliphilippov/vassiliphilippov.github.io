nonfebrile

adjective
without fever
8.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfiːbraɪl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfiːbraɪl / (BrE)
not having a fever or not related to fever

Examples

Nonfebrile seizure
The patient experienced a nonfebrile seizure, which was not caused by an infection.
Doctors noted that the illness was nonfebrile, as the child's temperature remained normal.
A nonfebrile episode can sometimes be more difficult to diagnose than one with a fever.
The patient remained nonfebrile throughout the observation period.
Despite the infection, she was nonfebrile and showed no other symptoms.
Doctors noted that most children with the condition were nonfebrile at admission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afebrile - not having a fever
  • antonym: febrile - having or showing symptoms of a fever
