sharecrop

noun
tenant farmer
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃer.krɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈʃeə.krɒp/ (BrE)
a person who farms land owned by someone else and pays the owner with a share of the crops produced

Examples

A sharecrop worker
He worked as a sharecrop for many years after moving to the rural South.
During the 1930s, many families survived by becoming sharecrops on large plantations.
The life of a sharecrop was often difficult and uncertain due to poor harvests.

  • synonym: sharecropper - a farmer who works land for a share of the crop
  • synonym: tenant farmer - a person who farms rented land
