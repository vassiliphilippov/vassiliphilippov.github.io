abdominal pregnancy

noun
pregnancy occurring in the abdomen
7.0 9.2
Pronunciation: / əbˈdɑmɪnəl ˈprɛɡnənsi / (AmE), / æbˈdɒmɪnəl ˈprɛɡnənsi / (BrE)
A rare type of ectopic pregnancy in which the fertilized egg implants and develops in the abdominal cavity instead of the uterus.

Examples

An abdominal pregnancy case
An abdominal pregnancy can be life-threatening if not detected and managed promptly.
