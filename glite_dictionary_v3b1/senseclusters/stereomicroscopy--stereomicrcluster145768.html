stereomicroscopy

noun
3D microscope use
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrioʊmaɪˈkrɑːskəpi / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəʊmaɪˈkrɒskəpi / (BrE)
the use of a microscope that gives a three-dimensional view of a specimen

Examples

Stereomicroscopy view
The scientist used stereomicroscopy to observe the surface structure of the leaf in detail.
With stereomicroscopy, students could see the three-dimensional features of the mineral sample.
Advances in stereomicroscopy have improved the study of insects and small organisms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopy - examination of objects visible to the naked eye
  • synonym: microscopy - the use of microscopes to view small objects
