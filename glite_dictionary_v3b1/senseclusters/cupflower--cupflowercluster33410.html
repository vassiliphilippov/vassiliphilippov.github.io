cupflower

noun
a Mexican climbing plant with yellow, funnel-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌpˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈkʌpˌflaʊə / (BrE)
A Mexican evergreen climbing plant with large, solitary, funnel-shaped, fragrant yellow flowers marked with purple-brown ridges in the throat.

Examples

Yellow cupflower vine
Hummingbirds are attracted to the vibrant cupflower in our greenhouse.
She planted a cupflower beside the trellis to let it climb and add color to her patio.
The cupflower bloomed early this year, filling the garden with its sweet fragrance.
