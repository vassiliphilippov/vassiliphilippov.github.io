junker

noun
old, unreliable car
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋkə / (BrE)
an old, worn-out, or unreliable vehicle, especially a car that is no longer in good condition

Examples

An old junker car
He finally sold his old junker and bought a new car.
The engine in that junker barely starts on cold mornings.
She drove her junker across the country despite its many problems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classic car - an old car in good condition, often considered valuable
  • synonym: clunker - an old or badly working car
  • synonym: lemon - a car that is found to be defective only after it has been bought
