artifact

noun
a man-made object
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrtəˌfækt /; / ˈɑr.t̬ə.fækt / (AmE), / ˈɑːtɪfɑːkt /; / ˈɑː.tɪ.fækt / (BrE)
an object made by a human being, typically one of cultural or historical interest

Examples

Each artefact in the collection has its own unique story.
Researchers carefully cleaned the fragile artefact for further analysis.
The museum displayed an ancient artefact that was unearthed during the excavation.
