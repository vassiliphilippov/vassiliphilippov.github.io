psychometrically

adverb
relating to psychometry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to psychometry, the supposed psychic ability to learn about people or events by touching objects linked to them

Examples

Psychometrically sense objects
The medium claimed she could psychometrically sense the history of the old photograph.
Artifacts in the museum were psychometrically analyzed by those with alleged psychic abilities.
He attempted to psychometrically read the ring to uncover its mysterious past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairvoyantly - in a manner relating to perceiving things beyond normal senses
