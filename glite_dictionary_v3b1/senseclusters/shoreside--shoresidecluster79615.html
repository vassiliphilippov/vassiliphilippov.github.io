shoreside

noun
area beside water
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɔːr.saɪd/ (AmE), /ˈʃɔː.saɪd/ (BrE)
the land or area next to a sea, lake, or river, often used for buildings, storage, or services related to water activities

Examples

Shoreside storage
The fishermen stored their nets in a shoreside shed after returning from the lake.
We enjoyed a picnic on the shoreside before going for a swim in the river.
A new restaurant opened on the shoreside, offering beautiful views of the harbor.
