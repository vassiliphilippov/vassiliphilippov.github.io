recreationally

adverb
for enjoyment
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that is done for enjoyment, leisure, or pleasure rather than for work or duty

Examples

To recreationally hike
Many people hike recreationally to enjoy nature and stay active.
He plays chess recreationally, not in official tournaments.
Some adults take art classes recreationally after work to relax.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leisurely - in a relaxed or unhurried way
  • antonym: professionally - in a way that relates to a job or career
