snacky

adjective
like a snack
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæki / (AmE), / ˈsnæki / (BrE)
suitable for or consisting of snacks; small, light, and intended to be eaten between meals

Examples

Snacky food
We ordered a few snacky dishes to share before the main course arrived.
Her lunch was quite snacky, just some fruit and a handful of nuts.
The party featured a snacky spread of chips, dips, and finger foods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hearty - large and filling, especially of food
  • synonym: light - not heavy or filling; easy to eat
