pâté

noun
rich savory meat paste or spread
3.3
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈteɪ /; / pæˈteɪ / (AmE), / pæˈteɪ /; / ˈpæteɪ / (BrE)
a soft, savory paste made from finely chopped or pureed meat, fish, or vegetables, usually served as a spread or appetizer

Examples

Duck liver pate
Duck liver pâté is a popular appetizer in French cuisine.
He impressed his guests by making homemade chicken pâté for the party.
She spread a thick layer of pâté on a slice of toasted baguette.
They enjoyed slices of toasted bread topped with rich pâté before dinner.
The chef prepared a vegetarian pâté using mushrooms and lentils for the appetizer course.
At the party, guests spread chicken pâté on crackers as a savory snack.
