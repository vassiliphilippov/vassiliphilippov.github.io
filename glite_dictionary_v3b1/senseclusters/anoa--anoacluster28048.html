anoa

noun
small wild buffalo from Sulawesi
8.3 9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnoʊ.ə / (AmE), / əˈnəʊ.ə / (BrE)
A small wild buffalo from the island of Sulawesi (Celebes), Indonesia, distinguished by its short, straight horns.

Examples

The anoa buffalo
Conservationists are working to protect the endangered anoa from poaching and habitat loss.
The anoa is rarely seen outside its native forests on Sulawesi.
Unlike other buffaloes, the anoa is notably smaller and more reclusive.
