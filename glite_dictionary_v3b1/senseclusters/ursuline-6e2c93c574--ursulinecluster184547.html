Ursuline

proper noun
Ursuline institution
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrsəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːsjuːˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a school, convent, or institution run by the Ursuline order, often used as a proper name for such places

Examples

Many alumni of the Ursuline return to visit during annual celebrations.
