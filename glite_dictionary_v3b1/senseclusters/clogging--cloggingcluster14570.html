clogging

adjective
causing obstruction or blockage
3.1 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑgɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklɒgɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something) causing an obstruction or hindrance, especially by blocking movement or flow.

Examples

Clogging mud
After the rainfall, clogging mud slowed down traffic on the rural road.
Medical professionals warn against clogging food particles in small airways.
The plumber removed the clogging debris from the kitchen drain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blocking - stopping movement through an obstruction
  • antonym: unclogging - removing an obstruction; restoring flow or movement
