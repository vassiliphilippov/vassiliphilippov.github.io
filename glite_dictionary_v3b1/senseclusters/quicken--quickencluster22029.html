quicken

verb
(of fetus) show first sign of life
6.8 9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkən / (BrE)
(of a fetus) to begin to show signs of life, especially movement, in the womb.

Examples

A fetus quickens
Doctors once relied on the point at which a fetus would quicken to determine pregnancy stages.
During her pregnancy, she felt the baby quicken at around twenty weeks.
It was a moment of joy when the fetus first quickened and she sensed life within her.
