Kuiper belt

noun
region past Neptune with icy bodies
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkaɪ.pər belt/ /; / /ˈkuː.pər belt/ (AmE), /ˈkaɪ.pə belt/ /; / /ˈkuː.pə belt/ (BrE)
A region of the Solar System beyond Neptune, containing many small, icy bodies and dwarf planets.

Examples

The Kuiper belt region
Many astronomers believe the Kuiper belt is the source of some short-period comets.
