transpiration

noun
release of water vapor through skin or leaves
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænspəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtrænspɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process by which water vapor is given off through the skin, mucous membranes, or the surface of leaves

Examples

Human transpiration
During exercise, transpiration can help cool the human body.
Humidity affects the rate of transpiration in both plants and animals.
Plants regulate water loss through the process of transpiration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perspiration - the process of sweating; excretion of moisture by the skin
