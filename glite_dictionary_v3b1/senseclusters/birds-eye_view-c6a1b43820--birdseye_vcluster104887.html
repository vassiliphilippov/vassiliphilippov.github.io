bird's-eye view

noun
view from above showing broad area
3.5 0.18/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ùdz a…™ Àåvju / (AmE), / Ààb…úÀêdz a…™ ÀåvjuÀê / (BrE)
A view of something from a high position, giving a broad and comprehensive perspective of the whole area.

Examples

A bird's-eye view map

With satellite images, scientists can study a bird's-eye view of environmental changes over time.
