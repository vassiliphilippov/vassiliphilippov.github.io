Synonyms

  • come up
    3.5 12/m
    come up is used when equipment or lights begin operating suddenly and become active, while come on is more general.
  • come online
    3.7 1/m
    come online refers to systems or services becoming available after setup, while come on applies to lights or devices starting to work.
  • start up
    3.0 2/m
    start up often means turning a machine on or causing it to begin, while come on usually describes things switching on by themselves.
  • go on
    1.1 3/m
    go on can mean a machine begins working or continues, while come on specifically means that something switches on or becomes available.
