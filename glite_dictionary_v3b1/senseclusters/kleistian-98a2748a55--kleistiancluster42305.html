Kleistian

noun
follower of Kleist
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪstiən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪstiən / (BrE)
a person who admires, follows, or studies the works and ideas of Heinrich von Kleist

Examples

A famous Kleistian
The conference attracted several renowned Kleistians from universities across Europe.
As a dedicated Kleistian, she has read all of Kleist's plays and essays.
The literary journal published an article by a prominent Kleistian on the influence of Kleist's work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kantian - a follower or scholar of Immanuel Kant
  • synonym: Shakespearean - a follower or scholar of William Shakespeare
