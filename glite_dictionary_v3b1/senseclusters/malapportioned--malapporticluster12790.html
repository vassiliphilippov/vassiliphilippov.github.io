malapportioned

adjective
unfairly distributed
9.8
Pronunciation: /mæ.lə.ˈpɔːr.ʃənd/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈpɔːr.ʃənd/ (AmE), /mæ.lə.ˈpɔː.ʃənd/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈpɔː.ʃənd/ (BrE)
distributed in an unfair or unequal way, especially referring to voting districts or representation.

Examples

Malapportioned districts
The court found that the state’s legislative districts were malapportioned and ordered them to be redrawn.
A malapportioned system can lead to some groups having more political power than others.
Critics argued that the council was malapportioned, giving rural areas more influence than urban ones.
