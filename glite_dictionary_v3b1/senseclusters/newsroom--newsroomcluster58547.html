newsroom

noun
room for reading newspapers and periodicals
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzruːm / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzruːm / (BrE)
a room, especially in a library or club, where newspapers and periodicals are available to be read

Examples

Library newsroom
A new newsroom was established to provide easy access to daily and weekly newspapers for visitors.
She spent her afternoons quietly reading the latest magazines in the newsroom of the public library.
The club's newsroom was popular among members who wanted to keep up with current events.
