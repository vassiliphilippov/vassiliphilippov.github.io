quadratics

noun
branch of algebra about quadratic equations
Pronunciation: / kwɑˈdrætɪks / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrætɪks / (BrE)
the branch of algebra that deals with quadratic equations and their properties

Examples

Study of quadratics
Her favorite topic in mathematics was quadratics, especially solving equations by factoring.
Students often struggle when they are first introduced to quadratics in high school algebra.
Understanding the properties of quadratics is essential for solving many types of equations.
