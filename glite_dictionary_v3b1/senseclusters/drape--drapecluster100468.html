drape

noun
hanging cloth used as a window covering
3.1 2.40/m
Pronunciation: / dreÉªp / (AmE), / dreÉªp / (BrE)
A piece of cloth, especially one used to cover a window as a curtain or blind.

Examples

Window drape
He closed the drapes to block out the afternoon glare from the window.
She bought new drapes to match the color of her living room walls.
The sunlight filtered softly through the heavy drapes in the bedroom.

Synonyms
