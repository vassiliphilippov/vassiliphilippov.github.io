fundholder

noun
investment fund owner
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person or organization that owns shares or units in an investment fund, such as a mutual fund

Examples

Fundholder owns shares
Each fundholder received an annual statement showing the performance of their investment last year.
The rights of a fundholder are protected by strict financial regulations.
As a fundholder, she regularly reviews the fund's reports to track her returns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: investor - a person who puts money into financial schemes or assets
  • synonym: shareholder - a person who owns shares in a company
