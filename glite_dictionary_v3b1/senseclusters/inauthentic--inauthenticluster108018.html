inauthentic

adjective
not genuine; fake
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɔˈθɛntɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪnɔːˈθɛntɪk / (BrE)
not genuine or real; false or fake, not what it appears to be

Examples

An inauthentic artifact
Collectors are wary of buying inauthentic artifacts from unreliable dealers.
Many people can sense when an apology feels inauthentic rather than sincere.
The museum discovered that the painting was inauthentic after a thorough analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic - genuine; real; not false or copied
  • synonym: spurious - not being what it purports to be; false or fake
