mV

noun
unit equal to one thousandth of a volt
Pronunciation: / ɛm viː / (AmE), / ɛm viː / (BrE)
a unit of electrical potential equal to one thousandth of a volt

Examples

One mV unit
He calibrated the device to respond to changes of one mV at a time.
The voltage measured across the resistor was just 10 mV, indicating a very small current.
This amplifier can detect signals as low as a few mV.
