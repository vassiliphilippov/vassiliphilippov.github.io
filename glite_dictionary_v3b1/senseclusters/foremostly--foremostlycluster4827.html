foremostly

adverb
at the very front
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌmoʊstli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməʊstli / (BrE)
in the foremost position; at or near the very front or leading place.

Examples

Foremostly at the front
The scouts rode foremostly along the ridge to spot any approaching enemy troops before the main force.
During the parade, the band marched foremostly ahead of all the other groups.
The rescue team advanced foremostly into the disaster zone to assess the situation quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontmost - in the position furthest to the front
  • antonym: rearmost - in the position furthest to the back
