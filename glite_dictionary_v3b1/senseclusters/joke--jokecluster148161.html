joke

noun
something not to be taken seriously
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊk / (AmE), / dʒəʊk / (BrE)
a person or thing regarded as foolish, insignificant, or not to be taken seriously

Examples

A joke team
After his disastrous performance, the whole team was treated like a joke in the league.
Many considered his proposal for the new law a joke rather than a viable plan.
The so-called security measures are a joke and won't stop anyone determined.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriousness - quality of being serious or solemn
