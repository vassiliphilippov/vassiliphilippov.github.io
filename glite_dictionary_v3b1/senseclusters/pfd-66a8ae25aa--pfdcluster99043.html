PFD

noun
cockpit flight display
8.7
Pronunciation: / pi ɛf di / (AmE), / piː ɛf diː / (BrE)
the main electronic cockpit display in an aircraft that shows essential flight information such as attitude, altitude, airspeed, and heading

Examples

PFD screen
The pilot glanced at the PFD to monitor the aircraft's altitude and heading.
Modern airplanes are equipped with a PFD that displays critical flight data in real time.
During training, students learn how to interpret information shown on the PFD.
