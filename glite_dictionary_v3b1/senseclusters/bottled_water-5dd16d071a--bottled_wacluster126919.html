bottled water

noun
water sold in bottles for drinking
3.3 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑt̬.əld ˈwɔt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əld ˈwɔː.tə / (BrE)
water that is sold in bottles, often sourced from a spring or purified for drinking

Examples

Many people prefer bottled water when traveling to places with questionable tap water quality.
The restaurant offered both tap and bottled water to its customers.
