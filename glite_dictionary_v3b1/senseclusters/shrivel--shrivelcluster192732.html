Examples

Synonyms

  • wither
    4.4 6.80/m
    wither describes plants or things losing moisture and strength and going limp, while shrivel means drying into small, wrinkled form.
  • shrink
    1.8 5.84/m
    shrink means becoming smaller in size, often without wrinkling, while shrivel means drying and forming wrinkles as it gets smaller.
