vestibular

adjective
relating to balance or equilibrium
Pronunciation: / vɛˈstɪbjələr / (AmE), / vɛˈstɪbjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to the sense of balance or equilibrium, especially as mediated by the structures of the inner ear

Examples

Vestibular sense
Physical therapy can help treat vestibular disorders in patients experiencing balance issues.
Problems with the vestibular apparatus can cause dizziness and vertigo.
The vestibular system helps you maintain your balance while walking or running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: equilibrium - a state of physical balance
