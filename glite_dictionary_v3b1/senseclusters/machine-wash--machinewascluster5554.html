machine-wash

verb
wash by machine, not by hand
3.5
Pronunciation: / məˈʃin wɑʃ / (AmE), / məˈʃiːn wɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash (clothes or fabrics) using a washing machine rather than by hand

Examples

To machine-wash clothes
Always check if a garment can be machine-washed before putting it in the laundry.
Do not machine-wash delicate silk fabrics, as they may get damaged.
These curtains are easy to machine-wash and require minimal care.
