tube

noun
London underground railway system
2.4 4.58/m
Pronunciation: / tuÀêb / (AmE), / tjuÀêb / (BrE)
(the Tube) the underground railway system in London.

Examples

Take the tube
Many Londoners rely on the tube for their daily commute.
The tube was delayed due to signal problems this morning.
We took the tube to Piccadilly Circus during rush hour.

Synonyms

  • underground
    3.6 0.44/m
    underground is the common British term for a city's electric rail network, while tube is the informal London name for it.
  • subway
    1.0 12/m
    subway is the American word for a city's underground rail, while tube is the London nickname used more informally.
  • subway system
    2.4 1.00/m
    subway system describes the full network of underground trains in a city, while tube is the London name for that system.
  • metro
    1.8 2.16/m
    metro is a general city rapid transit term often used outside Britain, while tube is the specific informal London name.
