tedder

noun
hay spreader
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛdər / (AmE), / ˈtɛdə / (BrE)
a person who spreads and turns cut hay in a field to help it dry

Examples

A skilled tedder
The tedder worked from dawn to dusk, ensuring the hay dried evenly in the summer sun.
After the grass was cut, the tedder carefully turned it to prevent mold from forming.
In the old days, a skilled tedder was essential for a successful hay harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haymaker - a person or machine that makes hay
