agromania

noun
intense desire to be alone or outdoors
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌaɡrəˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
An unusually intense desire to be alone or to spend time outdoors in open spaces.

Examples

Agromania for open spaces
After months in the crowded city, he developed agromania and craved solitude in the countryside.
Her agromania made city living nearly impossible, so she moved to a rural area.
The character in the novel displays signs of agromania, preferring open fields to crowded rooms.
