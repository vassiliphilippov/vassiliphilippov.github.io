ethnicist

noun
person biased by ethnicity
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθnɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈɛθnɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who promotes, studies, or shows bias toward a particular ethnic group

Examples

An ethnicist leader
The historian was criticized as an ethnicist for favoring one group in his research.
Some policies were influenced by an ethnicist perspective, leading to social tensions.
An ethnicist may focus on promoting the interests of a single cultural community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in equal rights for all people
  • synonym: racist - a person who believes one race is superior to others
