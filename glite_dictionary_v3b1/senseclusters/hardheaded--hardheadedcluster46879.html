hardheaded

adjective
stubborn; unwilling to change mind
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːrdˈhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌhɑːdˈhɛdɪd / (BrE)
unwilling to change one's mind or be influenced by others, especially in an unreasonable or stubborn way

Examples

Hardheaded refusal
Despite everyone's advice, she remained hardheaded and refused to reconsider her decision.
Even when the evidence was clear, Mark was too hardheaded to admit he was wrong.
His hardheaded approach made it difficult to negotiate any compromises.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obstinate - refusing to change one's opinion or course of action
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas
  • synonym: stubborn - unwilling to change one's mind or attitude
