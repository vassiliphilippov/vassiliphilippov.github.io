austere

adjective
strict or stern in appearance or manner
Pronunciation: / ɔˈstɪr / (AmE), / ɔːˈstɪə / (BrE)
having a stern or strict appearance or manner; severe or unfriendly in aspect

Examples

Austere expression
He wore an austere expression, giving no hint of warmth or amusement.
Her austere manner kept the children on their best behavior.
The headmaster entered with an austere look that silenced the entire classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forbidding - unfriendly or threatening in appearance
  • antonym: gentle - mild in temperament or manner
  • synonym: stern - serious and unrelenting in attitude or manner
