noncapitalist

adjective
not capitalist
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
not based on, supporting, or relating to capitalism or capitalist principles

Examples

Noncapitalist economy
The government implemented noncapitalist policies to reduce economic inequality.
Some countries have developed noncapitalist models that focus on collective ownership.
The new constitution established a noncapitalist framework for the national economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticapitalist - opposed to capitalism
  • antonym: capitalist - supporting or relating to capitalism
