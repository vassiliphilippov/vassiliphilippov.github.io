slam-dunk

verb
score in basketball by dunking forcefully
Pronunciation: / slæm dʌŋk / (AmE), / slæm dʌŋk / (BrE)
to score by forcefully putting the ball directly through the basketball hoop with one or both hands

Examples

To slam-dunk a basketball
Every time Jessica slam-dunks, the crowd goes wild.
He dreams of slam-dunking in a real basketball game someday.
Rasheed slam-dunked the ball over two defenders in the final seconds.
