conformationally

adverb
relating to structure
9.5
Pronunciation: / kənˌfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / kənˌfɔːˈmeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the shape or structure of a molecule or object

Examples

Acts conformationally on shape
The enzyme acts conformationally to change the shape of the substrate.
Some drugs work conformationally by altering the structure of proteins.
The molecule is conformationally flexible, allowing it to bind to different targets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: structurally - in a way that relates to structure
