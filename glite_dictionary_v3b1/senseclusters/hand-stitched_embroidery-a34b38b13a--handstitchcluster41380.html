hand-stitched embroidery

noun
embroidery sewn by hand
2.2
Pronunciation: / hænd stɪtʃt ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri / (AmE), / hænd stɪtʃt ɪmˈbrɔɪdəri / (BrE)
Embroidery that is sewn by hand rather than by machine.

Examples

Hand-stitched embroidery on fabric
Collectors often prize garments featuring hand-stitched embroidery for their craftsmanship and uniqueness.
Her wedding dress was adorned with exquisite hand-stitched embroidery along the hem.
The artisan spent weeks completing the hand-stitched embroidery on the decorative pillows.

Related Terms

  • antonym: machine embroidery - embroidery created using a sewing machine or embroidery machine
