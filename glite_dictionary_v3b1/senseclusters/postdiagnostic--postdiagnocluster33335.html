postdiagnostic

adjective
after a diagnosis
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstdaɪəgˈnɑstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstdaɪəgˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the period or events that occur after a diagnosis has been made

Examples

Postdiagnostic counseling
The hospital offers postdiagnostic counseling to help patients adjust to their new condition.
Effective postdiagnostic care can improve a patient's quality of life after receiving a diagnosis.
Support groups play an important role in the postdiagnostic phase for many individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postoperative - relating to the period after a surgical operation
  • antonym: prediagnostic - relating to the period before a diagnosis is made
