interrater

adjective
between judges
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈreɪtər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈreɪtə / (BrE)
relating to the level of agreement or consistency between different judges, raters, or observers

Examples

Interrater reliability
The researchers calculated interrater reliability to ensure the results were consistent across all observers.
High interrater agreement is essential when multiple people are scoring the same test.
Training sessions improved interrater consistency among the team of evaluators.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intrarater - relating to the consistency of ratings by the same judge or observer
