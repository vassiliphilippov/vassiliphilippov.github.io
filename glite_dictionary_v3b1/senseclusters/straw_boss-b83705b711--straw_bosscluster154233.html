straw boss

noun
minor supervisor within a work group
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /strɔː bɔːs/ /; / /strɑː bɑːs/ (AmE), /strɔː bɒs/ (BrE)
a worker who supervises other workers within a group, often holding minor authority below that of a foreman

Examples

The straw boss supervises workers
As the straw boss, he ensured everyone stayed on task, but major decisions went to the foreman.
She was promoted from laborer to straw boss after only six months on the crew.
The workers respected the straw boss for listening to their concerns and relaying them to management.
