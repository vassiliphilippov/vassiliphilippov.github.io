gas line

noun
pipe carrying gasoline to engine
Pronunciation: / ɡæs laɪn / (AmE), / ɡas laɪn / (BrE)
a pipe that carries gasoline from a fuel tank to a gasoline engine

Examples

Engine gas line
Always check the gas line for cracks before starting the engine after long storage.
He replaced the clogged gas line to restore the car's performance.
The mechanic discovered a leak in the gas line that needed urgent repair.
