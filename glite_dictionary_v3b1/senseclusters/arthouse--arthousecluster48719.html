arthouse

noun
artistic cinema or film
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɑːthaʊs / (BrE)
a cinema or film that focuses on artistic, experimental, or independent movies rather than mainstream productions

Examples

An arthouse cinema
They visited an arthouse to see a documentary that was not shown in regular theaters.
The director is known for making arthouse films that explore complex social themes.
Many film students prefer watching arthouse movies for their unique storytelling styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent film - a movie produced outside the major film studio system
  • antonym: mainstream cinema - films produced for mass audiences by major studios
