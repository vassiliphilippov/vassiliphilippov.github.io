cardinal

adjective
essential or fundamental
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪnəl / (BrE)
of the greatest importance; fundamental or essential.

Examples

Cardinal rule
Breaking the cardinal rule of safety could result in serious consequences.
Honesty is a cardinal virtue in any relationship.
The cardinal principle of the program is fairness to all participants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central - of the greatest importance; principal or essential
  • synonym: fundamental - forming a necessary base or core; of central importance
  • antonym: minor - less important or not essential
