mountainous terrain

noun
land with many mountains
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntn̩əs təˈreɪn / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪnəs tɛˈreɪn / (BrE)
An area of land characterized by the presence of many mountains or significant elevation changes.

Examples

Mountainous terrain areas
Plants that thrive in mountainous terrain are often resistant to cold and wind.
The region's mountainous terrain makes road construction challenging.
Travelers were warned to prepare for mountainous terrain along the hiking trail.
