flapdoodle

noun
foolish person
Pronunciation: / ˈflæpˌduːdəl / (AmE), / ˈflæpˌduːdəl / (BrE)
a foolish or silly person, especially someone who says or does things that make no sense

Examples

A silly flapdoodle
He acted like a complete flapdoodle during the meeting, making everyone laugh at his silly ideas.
Don't pay attention to that flapdoodle; he never knows what he's talking about.
Whenever she tries to fix the computer, she ends up looking like a flapdoodle.

  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense or intelligence
