eye shield

noun
protective eye cover
Pronunciation: / aɪ ʃiːld / (AmE), / aɪ ʃiːld / (BrE)
a protective cover worn over the eyes to prevent injury or block light

Examples

An eye shield cover
The patient was given an eye shield to wear after the surgery.
Welders must use an eye shield to protect themselves from sparks and bright light.
Children playing certain sports are required to wear an eye shield for safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goggles - protective eyewear that covers the eyes
  • synonym: visor - a shield for the eyes, often attached to a helmet or cap
