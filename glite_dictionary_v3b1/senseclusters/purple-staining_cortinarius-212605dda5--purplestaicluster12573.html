purple-staining Cortinarius

noun
a purple-staining species of mushroom
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˌsteɪnɪŋ kɔːrˈtɪnəriəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˌsteɪnɪŋ kɔːˈtɪnəriəs / (BrE)
a species of mushroom with a smooth, slimy, reddish-purple cap, close violet gills, and all parts staining dark purple when bruised
