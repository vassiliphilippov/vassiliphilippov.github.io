wrinkle one's nose

verb
make a facial gesture of dislike or disapproval
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋkəl wʌnz noʊz / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋkəl wʌnz nəʊz / (BrE)
to make lines appear on the nose, especially to show disgust or disapproval

Examples

To wrinkle his nose
He wrinkled his nose at the idea of eating snails for dinner.
The children wrinkled their noses when they walked past the rubbish bin.
