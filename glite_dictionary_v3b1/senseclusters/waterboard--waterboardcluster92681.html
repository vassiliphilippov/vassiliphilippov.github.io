waterboard

noun
torture by simulated drowning
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑː.tər.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈwɔː.tər.bɔːrd/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə.bɔːd/ (BrE)
