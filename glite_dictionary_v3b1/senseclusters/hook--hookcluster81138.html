hook

noun
boxing punch delivered from the side
6.3 4.7
Pronunciation: / hʊk / (AmE), / hʊk / (BrE)
A short, swinging punch delivered from the side with the elbow bent, commonly used in boxing.

Examples

A left hook
He dodged the punch and countered with a swift hook to the body.
She practiced her left hook repeatedly in training before the big match.
The boxer knocked out his opponent with a powerful hook to the jaw.
