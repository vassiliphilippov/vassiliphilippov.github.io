adhocracy

noun
flexible, informal organization structure
9.4 9.8
Pronunciation: / ædˈhɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ædˈhɒkrəsi / (BrE)
An organization or management structure characterized by flexibility, lack of formal rules, and decentralized authority, often formed to address new or complex problems.

Examples

An adhocracy organization
As the project evolved rapidly, the team shifted towards an adhocracy to remain agile.
Many creative industries rely on an adhocracy rather than a traditional hierarchy.
The startup embraced an adhocracy to encourage innovation and quick decision-making.
