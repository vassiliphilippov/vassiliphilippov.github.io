super-secret

adjective
extremely confidential
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌsiːkrɪt / (BrE)

Examples

She whispered the super-secret password to her friend so no one else could hear.
Access to the super-secret files was restricted to only a handful of trusted employees.
