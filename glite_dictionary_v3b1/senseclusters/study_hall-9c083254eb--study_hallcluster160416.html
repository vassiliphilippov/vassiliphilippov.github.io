study hall

noun
supervised school study period
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdi hɔl / (AmE), / ˈstʌdi hɔːl / (BrE)
a period or place in a school where students can study or do homework under supervision

Examples

Study hall period
During his study hall, Tom finished all his math homework before lunch.
Many students gather in the library during study hall to work on group projects.
She used her study hall to prepare for the upcoming biology test.
During study hall, students are expected to work independently and remain quiet.
She spent her study hall finishing homework for her next class.
The library is open to anyone who wants a quiet study hall during lunch.
