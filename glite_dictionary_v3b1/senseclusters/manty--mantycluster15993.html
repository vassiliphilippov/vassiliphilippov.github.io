manty

noun
Central Asian dumpling
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnti / (AmE), / ˈmanti / (BrE)
a type of steamed dumpling filled with meat, popular in Central Asian cuisine

Examples

A plate of manty
We ordered a plate of manty at the Uzbek restaurant.
The chef prepared traditional manty filled with spiced lamb and onions.
At the festival, visitors enjoyed tasting homemade manty from different Central Asian countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baozi - a Chinese steamed bun with filling
  • synonym: dumpling - a small mass of dough cooked by boiling or steaming, often with a filling
