mate

noun
one of a matching pair
Pronunciation: / meɪt / (AmE), / meɪt / (BrE)
either one of a matching pair of things, especially when considered as a counterpart or companion

Examples

A mate for a sock
He searched everywhere but never found the mate to his earring.
The mate to this chess piece was missing from the set.
When you buy gloves, always make sure each mate fits well.
