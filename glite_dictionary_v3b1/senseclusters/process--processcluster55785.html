process

noun
anatomical projection or outgrowth
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.ses / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.ses / (BrE)
a projection or outgrowth from a part of an animal or plant body, especially a natural anatomical prominence

Examples

Bone process
Several plants have a thorn-like process on their stems.
The surgeon carefully avoided the process extending from the vertebra.
The zygomatic process forms a prominent part of the cheekbone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protuberance - a thing that protrudes from something else, especially from a surface of the body
