rearing

adjective
standing erect on hind leg (heraldic)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially in heraldry) standing on the left hind leg with the forelegs elevated and the head usually in profile.

Examples

Rearing horse
In heraldry, a rearing unicorn often symbolizes purity and bravery.
The family crest displays a rearing lion with its paw ready to strike.
The statue portrays a horse rearing, reminiscent of classic heraldic images.
