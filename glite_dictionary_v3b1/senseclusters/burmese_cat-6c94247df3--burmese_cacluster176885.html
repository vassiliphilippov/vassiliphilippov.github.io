Burmese cat

noun
a short-haired breed of domestic cat
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːmiːz kæt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːmiːz kæt / (BrE)
A breed of domestic cat with short hair, a body type similar to the Siamese, and a solid dark brown or grey coat.

Examples

A Burmese cat breed
My friend adopted a Burmese cat from the animal shelter last year.
The Burmese cat is known for its affectionate nature and distinctive solid-colored coat.
Unlike the Siamese, the Burmese cat has a rounded head and compact body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Siamese cat - a breed of cat with a slender body and pointed coat pattern
