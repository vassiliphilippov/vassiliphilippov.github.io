receptacle

noun
container for holding things
Pronunciation: / rəˈsɛptəkəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛptəkl̩ / (BrE)
a container used to hold or store things

Examples

A trash receptacle
Please use the designated receptacle for recycling plastics.
She emptied the contents into a small glass receptacle and sealed it with a lid.
The kitchen was full of jars and other receptacles for storing spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: container - an object that can be used to hold or transport something
