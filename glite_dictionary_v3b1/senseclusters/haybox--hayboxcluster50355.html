haybox

noun
container for hay
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box or container used to store hay for feeding livestock or horses

Examples

Horse haybox
The farmer filled the haybox with fresh hay every morning for the horses.
During winter, the cows gathered around the large wooden haybox in the barn.
He built a sturdy haybox to keep the feed dry and organized.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manger - an open box for animals to eat from
  • synonym: trough - a long, narrow container for animals to eat or drink from
