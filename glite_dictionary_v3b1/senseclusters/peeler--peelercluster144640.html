peeler

noun
person who peels fruits or vegetables
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːlər / (AmE), / ˈpiːlə / (BrE)
a worker whose job is to remove the skins from fruits and vegetables

Examples

Fruit peeler at work
Each peeler was assigned a specific fruit for the day's work.
She worked as a peeler during the busy harvest season.
The factory hired a new peeler to help process the potatoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peel - to remove the outer covering or skin from something, especially food
