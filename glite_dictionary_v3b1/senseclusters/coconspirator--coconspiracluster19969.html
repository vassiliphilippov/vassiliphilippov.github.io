coconspirator

noun
a fellow member in a conspiracy
5.9
Pronunciation: / koʊ.kənˈspɪr.ə.tər / (AmE), / kəʊ.kənˈspɪr.ə.tə / (BrE)

Examples

The police arrested every coconspirator involved in the elaborate heist.
