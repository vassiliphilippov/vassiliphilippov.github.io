drone on

verb
to talk in a monotonous voice
9.0
Pronunciation: / droʊn ɑn / (AmE), / drəʊn ɒn / (BrE)
to speak repeatedly in a dull, monotonous voice, especially for a long time

Examples

To drone on about work
He tends to drone on about his work at every dinner party.
She realized she was starting to drone on and quickly changed the subject.
The professor would often drone on for hours, making it hard to stay awake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ramble - to talk or write in a long-winded, confused way
