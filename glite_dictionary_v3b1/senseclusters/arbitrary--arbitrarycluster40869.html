arbitrary

adjective
based on random choice or personal whim
8.0 6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːr.bɪˌtrɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈɑː.bɪ.trə.ri / (BrE)
Based on random choice, personal whim, or individual will rather than any reason or system.

Examples

An arbitrary choice
She objected to the arbitrary division of the class into two groups.
The law was criticized for its arbitrary enforcement and lack of fairness.
The manager made an arbitrary decision that confused everyone on the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capricious - given to sudden changes of mood or behavior
  • antonym: systematic - done according to a fixed plan or system
