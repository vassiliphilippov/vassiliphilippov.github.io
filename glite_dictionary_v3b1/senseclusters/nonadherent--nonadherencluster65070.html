nonadherent

noun
person not supporting group
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnədˈhɪrənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnədˈhɪərənt / (BrE)
a person who does not belong to or support a particular group, religion, or movement

Examples

A nonadherent in church
As a nonadherent, she chose not to participate in the religious ceremony.
The organization welcomed both members and nonadherents to the public event.
He was the only nonadherent present at the meeting, but he listened respectfully.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adherent - a person who supports or follows a particular group or belief
  • synonym: dissenter - a person who disagrees with or refuses to support a group or belief
