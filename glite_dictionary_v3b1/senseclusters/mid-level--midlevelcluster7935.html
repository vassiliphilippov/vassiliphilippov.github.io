mid-level

noun
intermediate position
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.le.vəl/ /; / /mɪd ˈle.vəl/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.le.vəl/ /; / /mɪd ˈle.vəl/ (BrE)
a person or position that is intermediate in rank, responsibility, or authority between junior and senior levels

Examples

A mid-level in the team
After several years as a junior analyst, he was promoted to a mid-level in the company.
Many mid-level employees are responsible for supervising teams and reporting to upper management.
The training program is designed specifically for mid-level professionals seeking to advance their careers.
