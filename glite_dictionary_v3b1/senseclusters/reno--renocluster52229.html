reno

verb
to renovate
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈrɛnəʊ / (BrE)

Examples

To reno a kitchen

After buying the old house, she planned to reno every room.
We need to reno the bathroom before we can move in.
