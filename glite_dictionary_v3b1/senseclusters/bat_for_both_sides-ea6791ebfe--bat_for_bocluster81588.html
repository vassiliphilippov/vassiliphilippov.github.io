bat for both sides

verb
support both opposing parties or sides

Pronunciation: /bæt fɔːr boʊθ saɪdz/ /; / /bæt fər boʊθ saɪdz/ /; / /bæt fɔːr bəʊθ saɪdz/ (AmE), /bæt fɔː bəʊθ saɪdz/ /; / /bæt fə bəʊθ saɪdz/ (BrE)
