OK

noun
approval or authorization
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
An official approval or authorization to proceed with something.

Examples

Manager's OK
Before we start the project, we need the manager's OK.
She gave her OK for the team to proceed with the plan.
The construction can't begin without the city's OK.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approval - the act of agreeing with or accepting something
  • synonym: permission - the official consent to do something
  • antonym: refusal - the act of saying no or rejecting a request
