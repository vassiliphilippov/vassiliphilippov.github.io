An official approval or authorization to proceed with something.

Examples

Manager's OK
Before we start the project, we need the manager's OK.
She gave her OK for the team to proceed with the plan.
The construction can't begin without the city's OK.

Synonyms

  • approval
    1.6 26/m
    approval is an official agreement or permission, while OK is a shorter, informal signal that lets someone proceed.
  • go-ahead
    3.4 1.07/m
    go-ahead is an official signal to begin or continue work, while OK is a casual approval people say in many situations.
  • authorization
    4.1 2.44/m
    authorization is a formal written permission or order used in rules or papers, while OK is a simpler, everyday approval to act.
