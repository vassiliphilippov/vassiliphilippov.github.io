ethanoyl group

noun
the CH3CO– functional group in chemistry
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθəˌnɔɪl ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˌiːθəˌnɔɪl ɡruːp / (BrE)
the functional group with the structure CH3CO–, derived from acetic acid; also known as the acetyl group.
