climate change solution

noun
strategy to address climate change
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ səˈluːʃən / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ səˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a method or strategy for reducing the causes or impacts of climate change

Examples

A climate change solution method
Many scientists advocate reforestation as a powerful climate change solution.
Switching to electric vehicles is considered an effective climate change solution.
The government invested heavily in renewable energy as a climate change solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptation - strategies to adjust to climate change effects
  • synonym: mitigation - actions taken to reduce the severity of climate change
