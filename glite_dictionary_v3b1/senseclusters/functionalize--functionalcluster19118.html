functionalize

verb
to add functional group
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃənəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃənəlaɪz / (BrE)
to modify something (especially in science or engineering) so that it serves a specific function or has new properties
