antipathetical

adjective
strongly opposed or hostile
Pronunciation: / ænˌtɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl /; / ˌæn.tɪ.pəˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ænˌtɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl /; / ˌæn.tɪ.pəˈθɛt.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
having or showing strong dislike, aversion, or opposition to something or someone

Examples

Antipathetical attitude
His antipathetical attitude towards modern art was well known among his friends.

He is antipathetical to modern art and prefers classical paintings instead.

The CEO was openly antipathetical to changing the company’s long-standing policies.
