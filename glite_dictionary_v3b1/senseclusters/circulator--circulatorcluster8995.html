circulator

noun
device that moves fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that moves something around or keeps it flowing continuously, especially in a system

Examples

Water circulator
The aquarium's circulator ensures the water stays clean and oxygenated for the fish.
A skilled circulator in the operating room manages the flow of instruments and supplies.
The heating system uses a circulator to move hot water throughout the building.
The heating system relies on a circulator to keep hot water moving through the pipes.
A skilled circulator ensured that information reached every department quickly.
Modern aquariums often use a circulator to maintain water flow for the fish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blocker - something that stops or prevents movement
  • synonym: distributor - a device or person that supplies or spreads something
  • synonym: pump - a device that moves liquids or gases by pressure
