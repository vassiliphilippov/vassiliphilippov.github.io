negus

noun
hot drink of wine, sugar, and spices
Pronunciation: / ˈniːɡəs / (AmE), / ˈniːɡəs / (BrE)
A hot drink made from wine mixed with hot water, sugar, lemon juice, and nutmeg.

Examples

A cup of negus
After dinner, the host served a warming cup of negus to the guests.
On cold evenings, she enjoyed making negus as a comforting treat.
The recipe called for red wine, lemon juice, and nutmeg to create a traditional negus.
