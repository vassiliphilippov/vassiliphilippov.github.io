nonbreeding

adjective
not reproducing
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈbriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈbriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
not involved in reproduction or not in the period when breeding occurs.

Examples

The nonbreeding season
During the nonbreeding season, the birds travel to warmer regions.
Some animals change their appearance in the nonbreeding period.
Researchers observed the flock's behavior outside the nonbreeding months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breeding - involved in reproduction or producing offspring
