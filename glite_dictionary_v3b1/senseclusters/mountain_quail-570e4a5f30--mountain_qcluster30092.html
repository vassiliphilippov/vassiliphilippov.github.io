mountain quail

noun
a quail species of western US mountains
4.7 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊn.tən kweɪl/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tən ˈkweɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊn.tɪn kweɪl/ /; / /ˈmaʊn.tɪn ˈkweɪ.əl/ (BrE)
