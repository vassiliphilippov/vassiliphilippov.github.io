mountain quail

noun
a quail species of western US mountains
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntən kweɪl / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪn kweɪl / (BrE)
a North American quail species (Oreortyx pictus) of mountainous regions, slightly larger than the California quail, known for its long head plume

Examples

The mountain quail bird
The mountain quail inhabits the dense brushy slopes of the western United States.
Unlike other quails, the mountain quail has a distinctive long plume on its head.
We spotted a pair of mountain quail foraging near the river's edge this morning.
