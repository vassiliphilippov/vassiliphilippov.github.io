valorize

verb
to set commodity value
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvæləˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈvæləˌraɪz / (BrE)
to increase, fix, or maintain the price or value of a commodity, often through official or government action

Examples

To valorize coffee prices
The government decided to valorize coffee to support local farmers during the economic crisis.
International agreements sometimes aim to valorize certain commodities to prevent price collapses.
Efforts to valorize agricultural products can help stabilize rural economies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: devalue - to reduce the value or price of something
  • synonym: stabilize - to make something steady or unlikely to change
