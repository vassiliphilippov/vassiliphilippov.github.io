bumblebee

noun
large hairy social bee
3.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌm.bəl.biː/ (AmE), /ˈbʌm.bəl.biː/ (BrE)
a large, hairy, social bee found in temperate regions, known for its loud buzzing and important role in pollination

Examples

A bumblebee buzzing
A bumblebee buzzed loudly as it hovered over the blooming lavender.
Children watched a bumblebee collect pollen from the garden flowers.
Unlike honeybees, a bumblebee can sting more than once if threatened.
