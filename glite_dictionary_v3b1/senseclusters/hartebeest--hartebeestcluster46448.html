hartebeest

noun
large African antelope with curved horns
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrtəˌbist / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtɪˌbiːst / (BrE)
a large African antelope with a sloping back and lyre-shaped horns that curve backward

Examples

A hartebeest antelope
A group of hartebeest moved quickly across the plains, their distinctive horns easy to spot.
Scientists are studying the migration patterns of the hartebeest in East Africa.
The hartebeest grazed on the open savannah, alert for predators.
