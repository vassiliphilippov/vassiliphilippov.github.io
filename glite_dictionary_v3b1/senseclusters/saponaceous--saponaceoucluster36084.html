saponaceous

adjective
soapy or soap-like
Pronunciation: / ˌsæpəˈneɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsapəˈneɪsiəs / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of soap, especially in being slippery or soapy to the touch

Examples

A saponaceous feel
Her hands were covered with a saponaceous film after handling the substance.
Some plants have saponaceous leaves that feel slippery when touched.
The chemist described the liquid as having a saponaceous texture.
