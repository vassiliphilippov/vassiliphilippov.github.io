strike

noun
a military attack or assault
Pronunciation: / straɪk / (AmE), / straɪk / (BrE)
a military attack, especially one intended to seize, damage, or destroy a specific objective

Examples

A missile strike
A missile strike was carried out to destroy the supply depot.
The air force launched a surprise strike on enemy positions before sunrise.
The general ordered a precision strike to neutralize the radar installation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack - an aggressive action against a target
  • antonym: defense - the act of protecting from attack
