Tunisian dinar

noun
currency of Tunisia
Pronunciation: / tuːˈnɪʒən dɪˈnɑr / (AmE), / tjuːˈnɪʒən dɪˈnɑː / (BrE)
the official currency and basic unit of money in Tunisia

Examples

The Tunisian dinar

The price of the souvenir was twenty Tunisian dinars.
