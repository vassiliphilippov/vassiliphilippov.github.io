possessory

adjective
relating to ownership
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛsəri / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsəri / (BrE)
relating to having ownership, control, or the right to possess something, especially in a legal or property context

Examples

Possessory rights
The court recognized her possessory rights to the land.
A possessory interest gives someone the legal authority to use the property.
They disputed the possessory claim over the valuable artifact.
The court granted a possessory order to help the landlord recover the property.
A possessory lien allows someone to keep goods until a debt is paid.
She filed a possessory claim to regain access to her apartment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonproprietary - not relating to ownership or property
  • synonym: ownership - the state or fact of owning something
  • synonym: proprietary - relating to ownership or property
