leafy

adjective
having many leaves or much foliage
Pronunciation: / ˈliːfi / (AmE), / ˈliːfi / (BrE)
having an abundance of leaves or covered with leaves; full of foliage.

Examples

Leafy green vegetables
Spinach and kale are both highly nutritious leafy vegetables.
The park was filled with leafy trees that provided cool shade in the summer.
They bought a house in a quiet, leafy suburb.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leafless - lacking leaves
