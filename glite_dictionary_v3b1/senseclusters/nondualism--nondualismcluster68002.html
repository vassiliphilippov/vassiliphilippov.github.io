nondualism

noun
unity of all things
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈduːəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / nɒnˈdjuːəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or philosophical view that everything is fundamentally one and that separations or opposites are illusions
