breakbone

adjective
severe bone pain
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌbəʊn / (BrE)
causing or associated with severe pain in the bones, especially as a symptom of certain fevers

The patient described the fever as breakbone because of the intense aches throughout his body.

She was hospitalized with a breakbone fever that left her unable to move comfortably.
