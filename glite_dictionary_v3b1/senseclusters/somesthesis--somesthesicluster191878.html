somesthesis

noun
the sense of bodily feeling and position
Pronunciation: / soʊˈmɛsθəsɪs /; / ˌsoʊməˈɛsθəsɪs / (AmE), / səʊˈmɛsθəsɪs /; / ˌsəʊməˈiːsθəsɪs / (BrE)
the faculty of bodily perception encompassing sensations from the skin, muscles, joints, and internal organs, including touch, temperature, pain, and proprioception
