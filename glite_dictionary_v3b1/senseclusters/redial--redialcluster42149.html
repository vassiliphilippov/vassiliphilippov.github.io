redial

verb
to call again
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdaɪəl / (BrE)
to call a phone number again, especially by using a phone's redial function

Examples

To redial a number
If the number is busy, you can simply redial after a few minutes.
She pressed the button to redial her friend's number automatically.
Many modern phones allow you to redial the last number you called with one touch.
