headboard

noun
board at the head of a bed
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A board or panel that forms the head of a bed, typically rising above the mattress and supporting pillows.

Examples

The headboard of bed
She leaned back against the headboard and started reading her book.
The wooden headboard gave the bed a classic, elegant look.
