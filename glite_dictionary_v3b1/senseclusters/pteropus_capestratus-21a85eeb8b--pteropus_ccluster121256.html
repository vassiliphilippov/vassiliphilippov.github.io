Pteropus capestratus

noun
a species of fruit bat
Pronunciation: / tɛˈroʊpəs kæˌpɛstrətəs / (AmE), / tɛˈrəʊpəs kaˈpɛstrətəs / (BrE)
A species of fruit bat native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, known for its large size and nocturnal habits.
