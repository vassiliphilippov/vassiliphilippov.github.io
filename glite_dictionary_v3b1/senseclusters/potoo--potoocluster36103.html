potoo

noun
camouflaged nocturnal bird
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtuː / (AmE), / pəˈtuː / (BrE)
a nocturnal bird of Central and South America, known for its excellent camouflage and haunting calls

Examples

The potoo bird
The potoo is almost invisible during the day because it looks like a broken branch.
We heard the eerie call of a potoo echoing through the rainforest at night.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a potoo perched motionless on a tree stump.
