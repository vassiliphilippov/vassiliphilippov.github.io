caraway

noun
leaves of the caraway plant used as herb
Pronunciation: / ˈkæɹəˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈkærəweɪ / (BrE)
The leaves of the caraway plant, used sparingly as an herb in soups and stews.

Examples

Caraway leaves in soup
She added a few caraway leaves to the stew to enhance its flavor.
The soup was garnished with finely chopped caraway for an aromatic touch.
Using fresh caraway in salads gives a subtle hint of spice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caraway seed - the seeds of the caraway plant, used as spice
