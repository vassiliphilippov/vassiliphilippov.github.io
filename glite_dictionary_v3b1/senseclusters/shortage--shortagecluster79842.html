Synonyms

  • scarcity
    4.4 1/m
    scarcity describes things being in short supply over time or area, while shortage describes a specific lack when people need something.
  • dearth
    8.5 0.7/m
    dearth feels like a strong, often serious lack of something, while shortage is a neutral word for not having enough.
  • shortfall
    5.5 2/m
    shortfall names the exact amount by which something is less than needed, while shortage names the general situation of not enough.
  • deficit
    4.3 30/m
    deficit is an amount lacking, often in money or resources, while shortage is the broader situation of not having enough.
  • undersupply
    5.1 0.01/m
    undersupply is a market condition where demand exceeds supply, while shortage means there is not enough of something needed.
  • insufficiency
    6.5 0.07/m
    insufficiency is a formal term for not having enough of something, while shortage is more common in everyday speech about needs.
  • deficiency

  • inadequacy

  • paucity
