design

noun
arrangement or plan of something
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a plan, drawing, or the process of planning how something will look, function, or be built

Examples

A design plan
The awkward design of the keyboard made it uncomfortable to type for long periods.
This house was built according to a classic design from the 1920s.
We admired the clever design of the new seating arrangement at the wedding.
Engineers met to discuss the design before beginning construction.
She was praised for her innovative design of the company logo.
The design of the new building incorporates energy-saving features.
Before starting construction, the team reviewed the design thoroughly.
She spent weeks perfecting the design for her fashion collection.
The architect presented a detailed design for the new library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a detailed technical plan or model for construction or design
  • synonym: conception - the forming or devising of a plan or idea in the mind
  • antonym: disorder - lack of order or arrangement
  • synonym: draft - a preliminary version or sketch
  • antonym: execution - the carrying out or putting into effect of a plan
  • synonym: layout - the way in which the parts of something are arranged
  • synonym: pattern - a model or design used as a guide for making things
  • synonym: plan - a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something
  • synonym: sketch - a simple drawing giving the essential features without detail
