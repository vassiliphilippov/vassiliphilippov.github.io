seat

noun
supporting part of a machine
7.6
Pronunciation: / siːt / (AmE), / siːt / (BrE)
a part of a machine that provides support or guidance for another component, often by acting as a base or housing

Examples

Valve seat
A cracked seat can compromise the function of the entire assembly.
In this design, the rotating shaft fits snugly into its seat for stability.
The valve seat was worn out, causing leaks in the engine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bearing - a machine part that supports another moving part and reduces friction
