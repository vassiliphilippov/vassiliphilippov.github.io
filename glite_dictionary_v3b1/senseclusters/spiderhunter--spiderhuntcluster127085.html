spiderhunter

noun
person who removes spiders
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərˌhʌntər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəˌhʌntə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to hunt, catch, or remove spiders, often as a form of pest control

Examples

Professional spiderhunter
They called a spiderhunter to deal with the infestation in their basement.
The spiderhunter carefully removed the webs from every corner of the attic.
After seeing a large spider in the kitchen, she contacted a local spiderhunter for help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exterminator - a person whose job is to remove or destroy pests
  • synonym: pest controller - a person who manages or removes unwanted animals or insects
