corslet

noun
armor for the trunk of the body
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrs.lət / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.slɪt / (BrE)
A piece of body armor covering the trunk, typically comprising a breastplate and back piece.

Examples

Historical reenactors often wear a replica corslet as part of their costumes.
The knight donned his corslet before heading into battle.
