recolonize

verb
to restore colonial rule
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkɑːləˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkɒlənaɪz / (BrE)
to bring a territory or people back under colonial rule or external political control after a period of independence

Examples

To recolonize a country
After the conflict, there were attempts to recolonize the region by the former imperial power.
International organizations condemned efforts to recolonize territories that had gained independence.
The treaty raised concerns that it might enable one country to recolonize its neighbor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colonize - to establish control over a foreign territory or people
  • antonym: decolonize - to free a country or region from colonial rule
