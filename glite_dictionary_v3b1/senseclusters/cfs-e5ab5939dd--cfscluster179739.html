CFS

noun
child welfare agency
5.5
Pronunciation: / si ɛf ɛs / (AmE), / siː ɛf ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Child and Family Services, a government or local agency that provides protection and support to children and families

Examples

CFS caseworker visit

Families in crisis can receive support from CFS to ensure children's safety and well-being.
