installment

noun
regular part-payment of a debt
1.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈstɑːl.mənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈstɔːl.mənt/ (BrE)
a sum of money paid as part of a series of payments for something, especially a debt, usually at regular intervals

Examples

Monthly installment payment
Each month, she paid an installment on her student loans.
The car was finally his after he made the last installment.
You can buy the sofa now and pay in twelve easy installments.
She paid for the furniture in monthly instalments.
The novel was published in weekly instalments in the magazine.
You can choose to pay the total amount in three instalments over the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: installment - alternative spelling, especially in American English
  • antonym: lump sum - a single payment of the entire amount
  • synonym: payment - the act of paying money
