squinched

adjective
with eyes half-closed to see better
6.9
Pronunciation: / skwɪntʃt / (AmE), / skwɪntʃt / (BrE)
(of eyes) half-closed in order to see better; narrowed to focus or exclude bright light.

Examples

Squinched eyes
His squinched expression told me the sun was in his eyes.
She looked at the distant sign with squinched eyes, trying to make out the words.
Through squinched eyelids, the world appeared bright and blurry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squinting - narrowing the eyes to see better
