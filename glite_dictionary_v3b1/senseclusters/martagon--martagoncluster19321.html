martagon

noun
a lily with purple turk's-cap flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtəˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːtəɡən / (BrE)
a lily (Lilium martagon) native to northwestern Europe and northwestern Asia, having small, dull purple, turk's-cap-shaped flowers
