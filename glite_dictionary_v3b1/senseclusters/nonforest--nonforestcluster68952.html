nonforest

adjective
not forested
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈfɔrɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈfɒrɪst / (BrE)
not consisting of or relating to forested land or areas covered by forest

Examples

Nonforest land
The researchers studied the differences between nonforest and forested habitats in the region.
Many animals migrate from nonforest areas to forests during the dry season.
The government plans to reforest some nonforest land to combat climate change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forested - covered with or relating to forest
  • antonym: wooded - covered with trees or woods
