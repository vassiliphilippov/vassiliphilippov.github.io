Styphelia triflora

noun
Australian shrub with scented foliage and pink flowers
Pronunciation: / staɪˈfili.ə traɪˈflɔrə / (AmE), / stɪˈfɛli.ə trɪˈflɔːrə / (BrE)
A heathlike shrub native to southwestern Australia, known for its sharply scented leaves, pink flowers, and pentagonal fruit.
