microplate

noun
lab plate with wells
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a flat laboratory plate with multiple small wells used to hold samples for experiments or tests

Examples

Lab microplate wells
The technician placed each sample into a separate well of the microplate for analysis.
Using a microplate allows scientists to test many samples at the same time.
After adding the reagent, the color change in the microplate indicated a positive result.

Related Terms

  • antonym: test tube - a small glass tube used to hold samples in laboratories
  • synonym: well plate - a laboratory plate with multiple wells for holding samples
