yardarm

noun
end of a yard on a square-rigged ship
7.2 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈjɑːrd.ɑːrm/ (AmE), /ˈjɑːd.ɑːm/ (BrE)
either end of the horizontal spar (yard) on a square-rigged ship, from which sails are set

Examples

The yardarm of a ship
A flag was hoisted to the yardarm as a signal to the other ships.
The sailor stood beneath the yardarm to secure the rigging during the storm.
Traditionally, executions at sea were sometimes carried out by hanging from the yardarm.
