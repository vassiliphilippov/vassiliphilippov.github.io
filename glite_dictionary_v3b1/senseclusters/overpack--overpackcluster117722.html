overpack

noun
outer shipping container
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.pæk/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.pæk/ (BrE)
a large outer container or extra packaging used to hold and protect one or more packages during transport or storage

Examples

A shipping overpack
The fragile items were placed in an overpack to ensure they arrived safely.
Each overpack was labeled with handling instructions for the shipping company.
The warehouse stores chemicals in a special overpack to prevent leaks during transport.
