unmagnified

adjective
not enlarged
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæɡnɪfaɪd /; / ʌnˈmæɡnɪˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmæɡnɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not made to appear larger than it is, especially by a lens or device
