noncredible

adjective
not believable
7.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkrɛdəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈkrɛdəbəl / (BrE)
not believable or lacking credibility; not able to be trusted as true or reliable

Examples

Noncredible report
The report was dismissed as noncredible due to a lack of supporting evidence.
She found the explanation so noncredible that she refused to believe it.
A noncredible source can damage the reputation of a news outlet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: credible - able to be believed; convincing
  • synonym: incredible - impossible or very difficult to believe
  • synonym: unreliable - not able to be trusted or depended on
