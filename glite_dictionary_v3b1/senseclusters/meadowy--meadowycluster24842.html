meadowy

adjective
like a meadow
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdoʊi / (AmE), / ˈmɛdəʊi / (BrE)
having qualities (such as appearance, smell, or taste) that are like a meadow, for example grassy, floral, or filled with wildflowers

Examples

Meadowy floral scent
The tea had a delicate, meadowy flavor that reminded her of spring mornings.
A meadowy scent drifted through the open window, filling the room with freshness.
He described the wine as light and meadowy, with hints of wildflowers and grass.
The cottage was surrounded by meadowy fields full of wildflowers and tall grass.
A gentle breeze swept across the meadowy landscape, making the grass ripple like waves.
We enjoyed a picnic on the meadowy hillside under the clear blue sky.
