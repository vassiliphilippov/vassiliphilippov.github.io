saddleback

noun
a ridge or pass between two peaks
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsæd.əlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsæd.əlˌbæk / (BrE)
a low pass or ridge between two peaks, shaped like a saddle

Examples

Mountain saddleback
From the summit, you could see a distinct saddleback connecting the two mountains.
The hikers reached the saddleback before stopping for lunch.
The trail follows the saddleback between the higher ridges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: col - a low point on a ridge between two peaks
