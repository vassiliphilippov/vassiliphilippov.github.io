rectify

verb
convert AC to DC (electricity)
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛk.təˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈrɛk.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
(in electricity) To convert alternating current into direct current.

Examples

To rectify current
A transformer steps up the voltage, and then the system rectifies the AC to DC.
The circuit uses a diode bridge to rectify the incoming current.
This device can rectify alternating current to supply stable power for the equipment.
