phencyclidine

noun
synthetic anesthetic and hallucinogenic drug
5.4 9.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfen.saɪ.klɪ.diːn/ (AmE), /ˈfen.saɪ.klɪ.diːn/ (BrE)
