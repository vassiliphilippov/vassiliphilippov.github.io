egg cream

noun
a soda fountain drink of milk, syrup, and soda water
Pronunciation: / ɛɡ kriːm / (AmE), / ɛɡ kriːm / (BrE)
a sweet beverage made with milk, flavored syrup (usually chocolate), and soda water, typically without egg or cream

Examples

A egg cream drink
Many people are surprised to learn that an egg cream contains neither egg nor cream.
On hot summer afternoons, nothing beats an egg cream as a refreshing treat.
She ordered a cold egg cream from the old-fashioned soda fountain.
