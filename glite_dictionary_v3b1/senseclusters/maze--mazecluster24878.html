maze

verb
to wander confusingly
5.6
Pronunciation: /meɪz/ (AmE), /meɪz/ (BrE)
to move through or lead someone through a confusing network of paths or passages, often in a winding or perplexing way

Examples

To maze through paths
They spent the afternoon mazing through the hedge maze, laughing and getting lost repeatedly.
The children enjoyed mazing around the cornfield, trying to find the exit.
We were mazed by the winding streets of the old city, unsure of which way to go.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guide - to lead or show the way
  • synonym: wander - to move about without a fixed course or purpose
