oboe d'amore

noun
a lower-pitched type of oboe
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊboʊ də ˈmɔːreɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊbəʊ dɑːˈmɔːreɪ / (BrE)
an oboe pitched a minor third lower than the standard oboe, used especially in baroque music
