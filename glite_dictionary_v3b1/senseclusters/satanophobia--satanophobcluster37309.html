satanophobia

noun
abnormal fear of Satan
4.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /seɪ.tən.ə.ˈfoʊ.biː.ə/ (AmE), /seɪ.tən.ə.ˈfəʊ.biː.ə/ (BrE)
An abnormal or extreme fear of Satan or the devil.

Examples

Severe satanophobia

People with satanophobia may experience anxiety when exposed to religious imagery related to the devil.
