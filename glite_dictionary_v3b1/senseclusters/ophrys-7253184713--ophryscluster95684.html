Ophrys

proper noun
orchid genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfrɪs / (AmE), / ˈɒfrɪs / (BrE)
a genus of orchids with distinctive flowers that often resemble insects, especially bees or flies

Examples

Ophrys bee orchid
The Ophrys genus includes the well-known bee orchid found in Europe.
Many Ophrys species have flowers that mimic the appearance of insects to attract pollinators.
Botanists are fascinated by the unique pollination strategies of Ophrys orchids.
