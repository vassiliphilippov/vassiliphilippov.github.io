trade magazine

noun
magazine for a particular industry or trade
Pronunciation: / treɪd məˈɡæz.iːn / (AmE), / treɪd məˈɡæz.iːn / (BrE)
a magazine aimed at members of a particular industry or trade, providing news, information, and trends relevant to that field

Examples

A trade magazine article
Advertising in a trade magazine can help companies reach their target audience more effectively.
Many professionals subscribe to a trade magazine to stay informed about the latest developments in their field.
The article was originally published in a respected trade magazine for healthcare administrators.
