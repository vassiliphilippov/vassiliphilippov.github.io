wildcard

noun
substitute game piece
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a playing card or game piece that can represent any other card or value in a game

Examples

Wildcard card
In poker, the wildcard can stand in for any card the player needs.
The game became unpredictable when a wildcard was introduced into the deck.
She drew a wildcard and used it to complete her winning hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joker - a special card in a deck often used as a wildcard
  • synonym: trump - a card that can beat others in certain games
