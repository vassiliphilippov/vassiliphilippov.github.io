digestive

adjective
relating to digestion
1.0
Pronunciation: / daɪˈdʒɛstɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛstɪv / (BrE)
relating to or having the power to cause or promote the process of digestion

Examples

Digestive system
He was prescribed medicine to help with his digestive problems.
The digestive system includes the stomach, intestines, and other organs.
The doctor explained how digestive enzymes break down food in the stomach.

Related Terms
