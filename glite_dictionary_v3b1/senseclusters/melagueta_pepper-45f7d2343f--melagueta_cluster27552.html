melagueta pepper

noun
West African plant yielding pungent seeds
6.9 8.1
Pronunciation: / mɛləˈɡɛtə ˈpɛpər / (AmE), / mɛləˈɡiːtə ˈpɛpə / (BrE)
A West African plant (Aframomum melegueta) of the ginger family, valued for its pungent, peppery seeds used as a spice.
