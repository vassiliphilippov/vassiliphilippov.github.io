paddleboarding

noun
water sport with paddle
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a water sport in which a person stands or kneels on a board and moves across the water using a paddle

Examples

Go paddleboarding on a lake
We spent the afternoon paddleboarding along the calm river near our campsite.
She learned paddleboarding quickly and now enjoys exploring different lakes.
Many tourists try paddleboarding for the first time during their summer holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kayaking - traveling in a small boat using a double-bladed paddle
  • synonym: surfing - riding on ocean waves while standing or lying on a board
