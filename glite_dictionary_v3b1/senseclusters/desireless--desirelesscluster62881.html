desireless

adjective
without desires
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪərləs / (AmE), / dɪˈzaɪəlɪs / (BrE)
having no desires, wishes, or longings; free from wanting anything

Examples

A desireless mind
After years of searching, she finally felt desireless and at peace.
The monk lived a desireless existence, detached from material things.
He appeared desireless, unaffected by the temptations around him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content - satisfied with what one has; not wanting more
  • antonym: longing - a strong feeling of wanting something
