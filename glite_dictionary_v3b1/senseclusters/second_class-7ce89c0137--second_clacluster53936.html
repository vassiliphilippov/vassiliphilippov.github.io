second class

adverb
in the second tier for travel or mail
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛk.ənd ˈklæs / (AmE), / ˌsɛk.ənd ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
using a mode of travel, transport, or postal service that is ranked as the second tier in terms of quality or priority, below first class.

Examples

Travel second class
He usually flies second class rather than paying more for first class.
The letter was sent second class, so it took a few extra days to arrive.
We chose to travel second class to save money on the train tickets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: first class - in the highest category of service for travel or mail
  • degree lower: third class - in the third category, below first and second, for travel or mail
