sick person

noun
person suffering from illness
Pronunciation: / sɪk ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / sɪk ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is suffering from an illness or disease

Examples

A sick person
During the flu season, there are always many sick persons absent from work.
It is important to isolate a sick person to prevent the spread of infection.
The hospital was crowded with sick persons waiting for treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy person - a person who is not suffering from an illness
  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical care or treatment
