mass

verb
to gather or assemble into a mass
2.9
Pronunciation: / mæs / (AmE), / mæs / (BrE)
to assemble or cause to assemble into a large group or mass

Examples

To mass outside
Protesters began to mass outside the city hall during the demonstration.
Security forces massed at the border in response to the threat.
Storm clouds started to mass ominously on the horizon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disperse - to scatter or break up
  • synonym: gather - to come or bring together
