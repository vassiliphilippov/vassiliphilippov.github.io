motor torpedo boat

noun
small, fast torpedo-armed naval boat
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtər tɔrˈpiːdoʊ boʊt / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə tɔːˈpiːdəʊ bəʊt / (BrE)
a small, fast, unarmored naval boat equipped with torpedoes, used especially during the early and mid-20th century for patrol and attack missions

Examples

A fast motor torpedo boat
During World War II, the motor torpedo boat was famous for its speed and agility in coastal attacks.
He joined the navy and served aboard a motor torpedo boat in the Mediterranean Sea.
The museum is restoring a historic motor torpedo boat for public display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PT boat - abbreviation for 'patrol torpedo boat', synonymous naval vessel
