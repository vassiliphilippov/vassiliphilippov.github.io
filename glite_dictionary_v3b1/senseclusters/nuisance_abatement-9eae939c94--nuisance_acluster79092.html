nuisance abatement

noun
legal removal of a nuisance
9.9 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnuː.səns ə.ˈbeɪt.mənt/ /; / /ˈnjuː.səns ə.ˈbeɪt.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈnjuː.səns ə.ˈbeɪt.mənt/ /; / /ˈnuː.səns ə.ˈbeɪt.mənt/ (BrE)
(law) the removal, termination, or destruction of something that has been legally determined to be a nuisance.

Examples

Nuisance abatement order
The city issued a nuisance abatement order to address the hazardous conditions at the property.
The law allows for nuisance abatement actions to stop activities that endanger public health.
Through nuisance abatement, officials were able to clear the abandoned vehicles from the neighborhood.
