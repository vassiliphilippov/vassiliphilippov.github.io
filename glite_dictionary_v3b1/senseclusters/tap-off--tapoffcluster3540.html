tap-off

noun
basketball game start (jump ball)
Pronunciation: / ˈtæp ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈtæp ɒf / (BrE)
the act of starting a basketball game with a jump ball

Examples

The tap-off start
At the tap-off, both centers jumped high to gain possession for their team.
He missed the tap-off, but quickly recovered the ball on defense.
The crowd cheered as the tap-off signaled the beginning of the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jump ball - the method of putting the ball into play by tossing it up between two opponents
