procrustean standard

noun
rigidly enforced standard
6.7
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkrʌstiən ˈstændərd / (AmE), / prəˈkrʌstiːən ˈstandəd / (BrE)
A standard or rule that is rigidly applied to everyone or everything, irrespective of individual differences or circumstances.

Examples

A procrustean standard
Applying a procrustean standard to education ignores the unique needs of each student.
Many criticize bureaucracy for imposing a procrustean standard that stifles innovation.
The company's procrustean standard for employee behavior left no room for creativity or individuality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: one-size-fits-all - a solution or approach meant to fit everyone, often inappropriately
