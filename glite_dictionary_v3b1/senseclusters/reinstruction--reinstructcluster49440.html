reinstruction

noun
reprogramming process
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process of loading, changing, or reprogramming the instructions in a machine, device, or software system

Examples

Device reinstruction process
The software update required a complete reinstruction of the device to function properly.
Engineers performed a reinstruction of the control system to improve the robot's performance.
After the error, a quick reinstruction allowed the machine to resume normal operation.
