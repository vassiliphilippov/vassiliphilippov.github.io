bisect

verb
divide into two equal parts
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsɛkt, ˈbaɪsɛkt / (AmE), / baɪˈsɛkt, ˈbɪsɛkt / (BrE)
to divide something into two equal parts, especially by cutting or drawing a line through it

Examples

To bisect a line
In geometry, an angle bisector is a ray that bisects the angle into two congruent angles.
The chef used a sharp knife to bisect the cake, ensuring two equal portions.
To find the midpoint, you must bisect the line segment precisely.
