dark cloud

noun
metaphor for gloom or an ominous situation
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɑrk klaʊd / (AmE), / dɑːk klaʊd / (BrE)
A metaphorical expression for a feeling of gloom, sadness, or an ominous situation.

Examples

A dark cloud of sadness
After the accident, a dark cloud settled over the entire family.
The announcement cast a dark cloud on the celebration.
There has been a dark cloud hanging over the company since the layoffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shadow - a dark area or feeling representing sadness or threat
  • antonym: silver lining - a hopeful or comforting prospect in a gloomy situation
