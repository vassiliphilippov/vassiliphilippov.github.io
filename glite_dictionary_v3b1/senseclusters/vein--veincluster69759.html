vein

verb
to mark with veinlike lines or streaks
7.2
Pronunciation: / veɪn / (AmE), / veɪn / (BrE)
to mark or become marked with lines or streaks resembling veins

Examples

Frost veined glass
Frost veined the windowpane in delicate patterns overnight.
The artist veined the marble sculpture to give it a realistic appearance.
The autumn leaves were beautifully veined with red and gold lines.
