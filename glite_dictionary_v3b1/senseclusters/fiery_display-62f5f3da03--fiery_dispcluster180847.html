fiery display

noun
vivid, intense exhibition
5.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪr.iː dɪ.ˈspleɪ/ /; / /ˈfaɪ.ə.riː dɪ.ˈspleɪ/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.riː dɪ.ˈspləʊ/ /; / /ˈfaɪr.iː dɪ.ˈspləʊ/ (BrE)
