horse fancier

noun
person who breeds and cares for horses
Pronunciation: / hɔrs ˈfænsiər / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈfænsiə / (BrE)
A person who breeds, raises, and cares for horses, often as a hobby or for competitive purposes.

Examples

Horse fanciers attend shows
Many horse fanciers attend equestrian shows to showcase their prized animals.
She became a passionate horse fancier after inheriting her family's stable.
The local horse fancier was known for training thoroughbred stallions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horse breeder - a person who breeds horses
  • degree lower: horse owner - a person who owns a horse, not necessarily breeding them
