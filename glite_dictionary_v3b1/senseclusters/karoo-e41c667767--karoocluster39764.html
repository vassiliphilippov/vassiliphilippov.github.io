Karoo

proper noun
South African semi-desert
8.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈruː/ (AmE), /kə.ˈruː/ (BrE)
a large semi-desert region in South Africa, known for its dry landscape, unique wildlife, and rich fossil record

Examples

The Karoo region
The Karoo stretches for hundreds of kilometers across central South Africa.
