Karoo

proper noun
South African semi-desert
Pronunciation: / kəˈruː / (AmE), / kəˈruː / (BrE)
a large semi-desert region in South Africa, known for its dry landscape, unique wildlife, and rich fossil record

Examples

The Karoo region
The Karoo stretches for hundreds of kilometers across central South Africa.
Farmers in the Karoo have adapted to the region's dry climate and sparse vegetation.
Many tourists visit the Karoo to experience its wide open spaces and starry night skies.
Scientists discovered new dinosaur fossils in the Karoo during their latest expedition.
The Karoo landscape is famous for its wide open spaces and unique plant life.
Many important geological studies have focused on the rock layers of the Karoo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Kalahari - a large desert region in southern Africa
  • synonym: Namib - a coastal desert in Namibia, southern Africa
  • antonym: rainforest - a dense, wet forest with high rainfall
