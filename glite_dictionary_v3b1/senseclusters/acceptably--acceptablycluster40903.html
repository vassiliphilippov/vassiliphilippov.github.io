In a way that is good enough to be accepted, though not particularly impressive or outstanding.

Examples

To speak acceptably
His presentation was acceptably clear, but it could have been better organized.
She spoke acceptably in French, though she still made some mistakes.
The students performed acceptably on their final exams, meeting the basic requirements.

Synonyms

    3.0 11/m

  • tolerably
    4.9 0.27/m
    tolerably describes something done in a moderately acceptable way, while acceptably stresses being good enough but not impressive.
  • passably
    4.6 0.14/m
    passably means fairly well or just meeting a standard, while acceptably means good enough but not particularly impressive.
  • decently
    2.7 1.30/m

  • all right
    1.0 15/m
