share

noun
a unit of ownership in a company
Pronunciation: / ʃɛr / (AmE), / ʃeə / (BrE)
one of the equal parts into which a company's capital is divided, ownership of which is represented by a stock certificate

Examples

Buy shares in a company
He decided to invest by purchasing 50 shares in a technology company.
Owning shares entitles you to vote at the company's annual meetings.
She sold her shares for a significant profit after the stock price rose.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stock - the capital raised by a company through shares
