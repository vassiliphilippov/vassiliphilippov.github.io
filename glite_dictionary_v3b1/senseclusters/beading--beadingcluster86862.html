beading

noun
decorative molding with bead-like pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a narrow decorative molding resembling a row of beads, used to edge or ornament furniture or other objects

Examples

Furniture beading
Many Victorian fireplaces feature decorative beading around the mantel.
She carefully restored the beading on the antique cabinet.
The ornate table had beading along the edge for an elegant touch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: molding - a strip of material with a decorative profile used for ornament or finishing on furniture or walls
