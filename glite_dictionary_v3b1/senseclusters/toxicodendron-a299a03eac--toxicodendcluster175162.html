Toxicodendron

noun
genus of poisonous plants
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑksɪoʊˈdɛndrən /; / ˌtɑksɪkoʊˈdɛndrən / (AmE), / ˌtɒksɪəʊˈdɛndrən /; / ˌtɒksɪkəʊˈdɛndrən / (BrE)
a genus of shrubs and small trees, including poison ivy and poison oak, with poisonous foliage that can cause skin irritation

Examples

The Toxicodendron genus includes both shrubs and small trees native to North America.
