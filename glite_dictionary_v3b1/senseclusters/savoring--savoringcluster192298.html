savoring

noun
tasting and enjoying small amounts
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɚɪŋ /; / ˈseɪvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of tasting and enjoying a small amount of something (food or drink) to appreciate or assess its flavor or quality

Examples

The savoring of food
He took his time with the savoring of the first ripe strawberry from his garden.
Her savoring of the aged wine impressed even the most experienced connoisseur.
The art of savoring each bite can transform an ordinary meal into a memorable experience.
He lingered over dinner, prolonging the savouring of each delicious bite.
Her savouring of the chocolate was so intense that she closed her eyes to focus on the taste.
The chef took pleasure in the careful savouring of each ingredient in his creation.
