nobody

noun
person of no importance or influence
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈnəʊbɒdi / (BrE)
a person who is not important or influential; someone of no social standing or significance

Examples

A nobody in school
After years of hard work, she went from being a nobody to a celebrated author.
In high school, he often felt like a nobody among his more popular classmates.
They dismissed his ideas, thinking he was just a nobody with nothing to offer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: somebody - a person of importance or influence
