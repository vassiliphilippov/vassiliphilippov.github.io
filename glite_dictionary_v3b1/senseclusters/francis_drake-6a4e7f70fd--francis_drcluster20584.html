Francis Drake

proper noun
English explorer and admiral (1540–1596)
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfræn.sɪs dreɪk / (AmE), / ˈfræn.sɪs dreɪk / (BrE)
(1540–1596) English explorer and admiral, the first Englishman to circumnavigate the globe and a leader in the defeat of the Spanish Armada.

Examples

Francis Drake explorer
Francis Drake is renowned for leading the second successful circumnavigation of the world.
