water moccasin

noun
a venomous North American water snake
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˈmɑkəsɪn /; / ˈwɔtɚ ˈmɑkəsən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˈmɒkəsɪn / (BrE)
a large, venomous North American snake (also called cottonmouth) that lives in or near freshwater habitats such as swamps and streams
