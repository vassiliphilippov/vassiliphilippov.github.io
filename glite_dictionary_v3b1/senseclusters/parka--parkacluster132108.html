parka

noun
warm hooded jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrkə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkə / (BrE)
a large, warm jacket with a hood, often lined with fur or faux fur, designed for cold weather

Examples

A warm parka
Before going hiking in Alaska, he bought a new parka for extra insulation.
His red parka made him easy to spot on the ski slope.
She wore a thick parka to keep warm during the snowstorm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorak - a waterproof jacket, typically with a hood, used for bad weather
