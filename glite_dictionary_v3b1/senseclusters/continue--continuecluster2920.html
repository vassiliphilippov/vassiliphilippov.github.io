continue

verb
resume after interruption
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɪn.ju / (AmE), / kənˈtɪn.juː / (BrE)
to start again or resume after a pause or interruption

Examples

To continue after a break
After the rain stopped, the players continued the match.
She paused to answer the phone and then continued cooking dinner.
Work continued on the bridge after a short delay.
Despite the interruption, she continued speaking as if nothing had happened.
He continued, 'Let me clarify what I meant by that statement.'
She paused to collect her thoughts, then continued with her explanation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cease - to stop doing something
  • synonym: proceed - to go on with a course of action, especially after a pause
  • synonym: resume - to begin again after stopping
  • antonym: stop - to cease from an action, especially speaking
