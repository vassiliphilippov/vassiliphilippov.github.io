procure

verb
obtain with special effort
7.1
Pronunciation: / prəˈkjʊr / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊə / (BrE)
to obtain something, especially with effort or by special means

Examples

To procure supplies
It can be difficult to procure tickets for a popular concert at the last minute.
She managed to procure a rare book that was out of print for years.
The agents were tasked to procure vital information from the informant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acquire - to come into possession of something
  • synonym: obtain - to get something, especially by effort
