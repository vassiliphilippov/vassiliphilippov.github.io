richness

noun
abundant wealth
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃ.nəs / (BrE)
the state of having abundant wealth or resources

Examples

Richness and luxury
He was unprepared for the richness and luxury displayed at the banquet.
Her paintings depict the richness and prosperity of ancient civilizations.
The sudden richness of the region transformed it into a bustling economic hub.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poverty - the state of being extremely poor
  • synonym: wealth - a large amount of valuable possessions or money
