electrodynamics

noun
physics of moving charges
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊdaɪˈnæmɪks / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks / (BrE)
the branch of physics that studies the behavior and effects of moving electric charges
