rodeo

noun
competition of cowboy skills
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.di.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.di.əʊ / (BrE)
A public event where cowboys compete in skills such as riding broncos and roping cattle.

Examples

A rodeo event
Every summer, thousands of people attend the local rodeo to watch bull riding and barrel racing.
She dreamed of becoming a champion at the national rodeo.
The rodeo featured contests that highlighted both horsemanship and teamwork.
