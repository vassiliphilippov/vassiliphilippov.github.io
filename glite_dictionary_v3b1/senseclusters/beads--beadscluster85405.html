beads

noun
strand of small items threaded together
Pronunciation: / bidz / (AmE), / biːdz / (BrE)
A string or strand consisting of several small, usually round objects (beads) threaded together, often used as jewelry or decoration.

Examples

A beads necklace
He bought a strand of wooden beads at the market as a souvenir.
She wore a beautiful set of beads around her neck to the party.
The children made colorful beads to give as gifts to their friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necklace - a piece of jewellery worn around the neck
  • synonym: rosary - a string of beads used in prayer
