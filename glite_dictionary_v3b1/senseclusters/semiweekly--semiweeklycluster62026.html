semiweekly

adjective, adverb
occurring twice a week
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈwiːkli / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈwiːkli / (BrE)
happening or appearing twice a week

Examples

Semiweekly meetings
She attends semiweekly meetings to stay updated on the project's progress.
The company publishes a semiweekly newsletter that goes out every Tuesday and Friday.
The medication must be taken on a semiweekly basis to be effective.
Our team meets semiweekly to discuss project updates and goals.
She attends yoga classes semiweekly to maintain her flexibility and balance.
The magazine is published semiweekly, so subscribers receive two issues each week.

  • degree lower: weekly - occurring once a week
