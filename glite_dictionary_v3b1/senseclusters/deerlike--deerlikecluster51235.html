deerlike

adjective
resembling a deer
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪrˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɪəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or similar to a deer in appearance, movement, or behavior

Examples

Deerlike movement
The creature had a deerlike elegance as it moved through the tall grass.
Her deerlike eyes gave her a gentle and innocent appearance.
With its slender legs and deerlike features, the animal blended into the forest.
