hypostatically

adverb
relating to hypostasis
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to hypostasis, especially the settling of blood or fluids in body tissues after death
