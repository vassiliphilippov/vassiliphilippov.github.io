pulsatility

noun
rhythmic pulsing quality
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌlˌsəˈtɪlɪti /; / ˌpʌl.səˈtɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌpʌlˈsætɪlɪti /; / ˌpʌl.səˈtɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
