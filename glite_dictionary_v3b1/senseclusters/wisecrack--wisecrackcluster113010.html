wisecrack

noun
clever or witty remark
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪzˌkræk / (AmE), / ˈwaɪz.kræk / (BrE)
a clever or witty remark, often intended to be humorous or sarcastic

Examples

A funny wisecrack
His constant wisecracks made him popular among his friends.
Instead of taking offense, the teacher laughed at the student's wisecrack.
She couldn't resist making a wisecrack during the boring meeting.
