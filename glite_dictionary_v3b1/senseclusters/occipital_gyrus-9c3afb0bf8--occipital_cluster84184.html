occipital gyrus

noun
ridge of the occipital lobe in the brain
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɪp.ɪ.təl ˈdʒaɪ.rəs / (AmE), / ɒkˈsɪp.ɪ.təl ˈdʒʌɪ.rəs / (BrE)
any of the convoluted ridges on the outer surface of the occipital lobe in the brain

Examples

The occipital gyrus in brain
Damage to the occipital gyrus can affect a person's ability to process visual information.
Researchers examined brain scans focusing on changes in the right occipital gyrus.
The visual cortex is located in the occipital gyrus of the brain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gyrus - a ridge on the cerebral cortex
  • antonym: sulcus - a groove or furrow on the surface of the brain, between gyri
