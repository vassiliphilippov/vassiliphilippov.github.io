multiscreen

noun
cinema with many screens
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌskriːn / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌskriːn / (BrE)
a cinema complex with several separate auditoriums or screens showing different films at the same time

Examples

Multiscreen cinema
The new multiscreen in town offers a wide variety of movies every night.
We visited a multiscreen so everyone could watch the film they wanted.
Many modern shopping centers include a large multiscreen for entertainment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multiplex - a cinema with multiple screens in one building
  • antonym: single-screen - a cinema with only one screen
