alderman

noun
member of a city council
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔldɚmən / (AmE), / ˈɔːldəmən / (BrE)
a member of a municipal legislative body, such as a city council, traditionally a senior or elected official

Examples

City alderman
He has served as an alderman for ten years, representing his district.
The city appointed an alderman to chair the committee on public safety.
Voters elected her as their first female alderman in the town's history.
