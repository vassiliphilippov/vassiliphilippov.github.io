briolette

noun
pear-shaped gemstone cut
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbriːoʊˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌbriːəˈlɛt / (BrE)
a gemstone cut in an elongated, faceted pear shape, often used in jewelry

Examples

A briolette pendant
The pendant was set with a sparkling briolette that caught the light beautifully.
She admired the delicate earrings, each featuring a single sapphire briolette.
A diamond briolette hung elegantly from the center of the necklace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cabochon - a gemstone polished but not faceted, with a smooth, rounded surface
  • synonym: faceted - cut with many flat surfaces to enhance brilliance
