colonialize

verb
to be colonized by microbes
8.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈloʊ.nɪ.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /kə.ˈləʊ.nɪ.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to become occupied or established by colonies of microorganisms, such as bacteria or fungi, especially on tissue or surfaces.

Examples

Bacteria colonialize a wound
The wound was quickly colonialized by bacteria, requiring immediate medical attention.
Surfaces in hospitals can become colonialized by antibiotic-resistant microorganisms if not properly cleaned.
Doctors monitored whether the catheter site had been colonialized by any harmful microbes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infect - to contaminate with harmful microorganisms
