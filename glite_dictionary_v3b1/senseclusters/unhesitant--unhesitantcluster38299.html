unhesitant

adjective
without hesitation
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛzɪtənt / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛzɪtənt / (BrE)
acting or speaking without hesitation, pause, or doubt

Examples

An unhesitant answer
Her unhesitant response impressed everyone in the meeting.
He made an unhesitant decision to accept the job offer.
With unhesitant steps, she walked onto the stage to give her speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confident - sure of oneself; having no doubt
  • synonym: decisive - able to make decisions quickly and confidently
  • antonym: hesitant - uncertain or slow to act because of doubt
