Synonyms

  • take part
    1.4 13/m
    take part is a common phrasal verb for joining an activity with others, while participating focuses on being involved.
  • engage
    3.4 80/m
    engage is more formal and often means being actively involved in something, while participating simply means taking part.
  • join in
    1.1 9/m

  • partake
    5.5 2/m
