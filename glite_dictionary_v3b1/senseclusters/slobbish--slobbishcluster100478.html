slobbish

adjective
untidy or lazy
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑːbɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈslɒbɪʃ / (BrE)
untidy, lazy, or behaving in a way that resembles a slob

Examples

Slobbish habits
His slobbish habits made his room very messy.
She felt embarrassed by her slobbish appearance at the party.
After a long week, he became quite slobbish and left dishes everywhere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neat - tidy and orderly in appearance or habits
  • synonym: slovenly - messy and careless in appearance or habits
