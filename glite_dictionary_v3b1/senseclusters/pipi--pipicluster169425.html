pipi

noun
New Zealand clam
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːpiː / (AmE), / ˈpiːpiː / (BrE)
a small edible saltwater clam (Paphies australis) native to New Zealand, commonly eaten as seafood

Examples

A pipi shell
We found several pipi on the beach and took them home to cook.
Local markets often sell fresh pipi for making traditional seafood dishes.
Children enjoy digging for pipi in the wet sand during low tide.
