service

noun
formal legal delivery of a document
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.vɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vɪs / (BrE)
the formal delivery of a writ, summons, or other legal notice to a person, particularly as required by law

Examples

Legal service

Proper service of the court order is required for the case to proceed.
The service of legal documents must follow strict guidelines to be valid.
