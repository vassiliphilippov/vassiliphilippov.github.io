unfortified

adjective
without protection
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔːrtɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not protected by defensive walls, structures, or added strengthening substances

Examples

An unfortified village
The invaders marched into the unfortified village without facing any resistance.
This juice is unfortified, so it does not contain added vitamins or minerals.
The unfortified border made it easy for people to cross between the two countries.
