christening

noun
ceremony of giving a Christian name at baptism
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsənɪŋ / (BrE)
the ceremony of giving a Christian name to someone, typically an infant, at baptism

Examples

The christening ceremony
After the christening, everyone gathered for a celebratory meal.
The baby's christening was attended by family and close friends.
They chose a traditional church for the christening ceremony.
