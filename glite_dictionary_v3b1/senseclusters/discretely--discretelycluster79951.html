discretely

adverb
in separate units
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskritli / (AmE), / dɪˈskriːtli / (BrE)
in a way that is separate, distinct, or individual; not together or continuous

Examples

Recorded discretely at intervals
The data was recorded discretely at regular time intervals for analysis.
Each stage of the process operates discretely to ensure accuracy and control.
The lights can be adjusted discretely rather than all at once.
The sensors operate discretely to monitor different areas of the building.
Each chapter in the book can be read discretely without losing understanding.
The tasks were assigned discretely to ensure no overlap between team members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuously - in an unbroken or uninterrupted way
  • synonym: individually - one at a time or separately
  • synonym: separately - in a way that is not together or combined
  • antonym: together - with or in proximity to another person or people
