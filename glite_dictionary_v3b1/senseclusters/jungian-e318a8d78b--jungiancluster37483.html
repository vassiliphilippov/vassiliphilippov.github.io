Jungian

adjective
relating to Carl Jung's psychology
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʊŋiən / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊŋiən / (BrE)
Relating to the theories or ideas of the Swiss psychologist Carl Jung, especially his concepts of the collective unconscious and archetypes.

Examples

Jungian theory
A Jungian analysis often involves interpreting myths and cultural stories.
Her artwork is clearly influenced by Jungian archetypes and symbolism.
Many therapists use Jungian principles to explore their patients' dreams.
