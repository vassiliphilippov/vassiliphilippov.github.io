engorged

adjective
swollen and overfull, esp. with blood
3.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈgɔːrdʒd/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈgɔːdʒd/ (BrE)
(of a body part or vessel) swollen with fluid, especially blood, to the point of being overfull.

Examples

Engorged blood vessels
After the injury, her ankle became red and engorged with fluid.
During the allergic reaction, his lips grew painfully engorged.
The veins in his arms looked engorged after an intense workout.
