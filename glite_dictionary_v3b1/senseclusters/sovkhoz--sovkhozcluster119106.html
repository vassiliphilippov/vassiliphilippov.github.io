sovkhoz

noun
state-owned Soviet farm
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑːv.kɑːz/ /; / /ˈsɑːv.kəz/ (AmE), /ˈsɒv.kɒz/ /; / /ˈsɒv.kəz/ (BrE)
a large state-owned farm in the former Soviet Union where workers were employed by the government

Examples

A sovkhoz farm
During the Soviet era, many people worked on a sovkhoz in rural areas.
The government managed every aspect of the sovkhoz, from planting to distribution.
After the collapse of the Soviet Union, the sovkhoz system was largely dismantled.
