Acular

noun
trade name for an anti-inflammatory drug
9.1
Pronunciation: / əˈkjuːlər / (AmE), / əˈkjuːlə / (BrE)
A trade name for ketorolac, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used especially as an eye drop to relieve itching or inflammation.

Examples

Acular eye drops
Some patients experience mild irritation after applying Acular to the affected eye.
The doctor prescribed Acular to reduce inflammation in my eyes after surgery.
You should use Acular drops exactly as directed by your ophthalmologist.
