schlimazel

noun
chronically unlucky person
Pronunciation: / ʃlɪˈmɑːzəl / (AmE), / ʃlɪˈmɑːzəl / (BrE)
A very unlucky or chronically unfortunate person, especially one who seems destined to fail repeatedly.

Examples

A schlimazel person
In stories, the schlimazel is the character who gets caught in every mishap.
No matter how hard he tries, he's always a schlimazel, tripping over his own feet.
You don't want to be the schlimazel at the poker table tonight.
