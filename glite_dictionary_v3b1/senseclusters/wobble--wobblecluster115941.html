wobble

noun
an unsteady rocking movement
2.8 0.96/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆwÉ‘bÉ™l / (AmE), / ËˆwÉ’bÉ™l / (BrE)
an unsteady or rocking movement back and forth or from side to side

Examples

A wobble in the wheel
Her bike developed a wobble when she hit the gravel road.
The plane's wings had a slight wobble during the turbulence.
There was a noticeable wobble in the table after it lost a leg.

Synonyms

  • jiggle
    3.9 0.34/m
    jiggle is a small, irregular shaking that moves something quickly, while wobble is a slower, unsteady rocking movement.
  • rocking
    1.1 0.03/m
    rocking is a gentle, steady back-and-forth motion used to calm or sway, while wobble is an unsteady, shaky rocking movement.
  • wiggle
    1.0 2.02/m
    wiggle is a small, quick side-to-side movement often done on purpose, while wobble is an unsteady, rocking motion.
