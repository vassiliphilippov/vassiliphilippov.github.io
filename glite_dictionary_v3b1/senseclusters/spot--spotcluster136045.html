spot

noun
playing card with pips
Pronunciation: / spɑt / (AmE), / spɒt / (BrE)
a small round mark, stain, or area that is different from what surrounds it (such as on a surface, card, or dice)

Examples

A red spot
He was dealt a seven spot and a king in the first round.
She drew a four spot and won the hand with it.
The dealer placed an eight spot face up on the table.
She noticed a red spot forming on the white ceiling.
The leopard is known for the black spots covering its golden fur.
There was a large spot of oil on his shirt after lunch.
Count the spots on the card to see what number you have.
I rolled a die and it landed with a single spot facing up.
The ace of spades has just one spot in the center of the card.
