tempter

noun
a person who tempts others
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmp.tər / (AmE), / ˈtɛmp.tə / (BrE)
a person or thing that tempts someone to do something wrong or unwise

Examples

A tempter of friends
He saw himself as a tempter, always encouraging his friends to take risks.
In the novel, the mysterious tempter lured the hero away from his moral path.
Many religions warn of a tempter who tries to lead people astray.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discourager - a person who tries to prevent someone from doing something
  • synonym: seducer - a person who entices someone into sexual activity or wrongdoing
  • synonym: temptress - a woman who tempts someone, especially sexually
