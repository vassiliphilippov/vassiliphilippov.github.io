jellybean

noun
insignificant person
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛliˌbin / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛliˌbiːn / (BrE)
an informal term for a person or thing considered weak, unimportant, or lacking substance

Examples

A weak jellybean
He was dismissed as a jellybean by his more assertive colleagues in the meeting.
Don't let anyone call you a jellybean just because you have a gentle approach.
The proposal was seen as a jellybean and quickly ignored by the committee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lightweight - a person or thing of little importance or influence
  • synonym: nobody - a person of no importance or influence
  • antonym: somebody - a person of importance or influence
