swine flu

noun
influenza in pigs, sometimes infecting humans
8.7
Pronunciation: / swaɪn flu / (AmE), / swaɪn fluː / (BrE)
an infectious respiratory disease in pigs caused by influenza viruses, which can sometimes spread to humans

Examples

Swine flu outbreak
During the 2009 outbreak, many people contracted swine flu in several countries.
Some health officials monitor pig farms to prevent the spread of swine flu to humans.
Vaccines were quickly developed to combat the most severe cases of swine flu.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influenza - a viral infection that attacks your respiratory system
