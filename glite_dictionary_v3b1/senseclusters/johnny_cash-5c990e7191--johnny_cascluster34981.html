Johnny Cash

proper noun
American country singer-songwriter (1932–2003)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑni kæʃ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒni kæʃ / (BrE)
(1932–2003) An American country music singer and songwriter known for his deep, distinctive voice and songs exploring themes of sorrow, morality, and redemption.
