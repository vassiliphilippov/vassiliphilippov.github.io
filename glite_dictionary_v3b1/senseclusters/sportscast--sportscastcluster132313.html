sportscast

noun
broadcast of sports news or events
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔːrtˌskæst / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtskɑːst / (BrE)
a radio or television broadcast that presents sports news, commentary, or live coverage of sports events

Examples

Live sportscast
I listened to the sportscast on the radio while driving home.
She started her career as a sportscast reporter for a local TV station.
The network will air a live sportscast of tonight's basketball game.
