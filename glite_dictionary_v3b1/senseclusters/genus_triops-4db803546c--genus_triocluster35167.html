genus Triops

noun
type genus of Triopidae; small crustaceans
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtraɪɒps / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈtraɪɒps / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Triopidae, comprising small freshwater crustaceans with a small third median eye.
