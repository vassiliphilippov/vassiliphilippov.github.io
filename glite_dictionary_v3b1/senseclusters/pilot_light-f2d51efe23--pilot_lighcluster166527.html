pilot light

noun
indicator light showing power is on
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət laɪt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət laɪt / (BrE)
a small light on a device or control panel that indicates whether power is on or a motor is operating

Examples

Device pilot light
If the pilot light goes out, you may not realize the appliance is still off.
She checked the pilot light before starting the motor to make sure everything was functioning.
The pilot light on the front panel glowed steadily to show the machine was running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicator light - a light that shows whether a machine or system is working
