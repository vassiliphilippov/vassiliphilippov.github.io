theologoumenon

noun
unofficial theological opinion
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiˌɑləˈɡuːmənɑn / (AmE), / θiˌɒləˈɡuːmənɒn / (BrE)
a theological statement or opinion that is not an official doctrine of a religion

Examples

A theologoumenon opinion
The concept of purgatory is sometimes regarded as a theologoumenon rather than official doctrine.
Scholars debated whether the story was a theologoumenon or a core teaching of the church.
Many religious traditions contain at least one theologoumenon that is widely discussed but not required to believe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doctrine - an official teaching or set of beliefs held by a religious group
  • antonym: dogma - an official system of principles or beliefs, especially in religion
