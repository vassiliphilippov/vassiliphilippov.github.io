mannikin

noun
medical training dummy
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.ɪ.kɪn/ /; / /ˈmæn.ə.kɪn/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.ɪ.kɪn/ /; / /ˈmæn.ə.kɪn/ (BrE)
a life-like model of a human, often with simulated physiological functions, used in medical training to practice clinical procedures and simulate patient care.

Examples

Medical mannikin
Medical students practiced CPR on a high-fidelity mannikin in the simulation lab.
The instructor programmed the mannikin to simulate irregular breathing and a weak pulse for training.
Hospitals use advanced mannikins to rehearse rare emergencies without risk to real patients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mannequin - a life-size model of a human used for display or demonstration; related term used for non-medical dummies
  • synonym: patient simulator - a device that reproduces human physiological responses for healthcare training and assessment
