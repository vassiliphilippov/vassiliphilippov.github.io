ill

noun
harmful problem
Pronunciation: / ɪl / (AmE), / ɪl / (BrE)
a problem, difficulty, or harmful condition that causes suffering or trouble, especially in society.

Examples

Social ills
The new policy aims to solve the social ills that have plagued the city for years.
Many organizations work to address the ills of poverty and inequality.
Education is often seen as a way to cure the ills of society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benefit - something that is helpful or good
  • synonym: evil - something morally wrong or harmful
  • synonym: trouble - a situation that causes difficulty or worry
