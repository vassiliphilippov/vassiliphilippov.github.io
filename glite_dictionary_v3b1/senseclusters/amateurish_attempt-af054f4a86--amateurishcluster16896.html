amateurish attempt

noun
inexperienced or unskilled attempt
5.4 4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæm.ə.tʃɜːr.ɪʃ ə.ˈtempt/ (AmE), /ˈæm.ə.tjʊə.rɪʃ ə.ˈtempt/ /; / /ˈæm.ə.tʃʊə.rɪʃ ə.ˈtempt/ (BrE)
