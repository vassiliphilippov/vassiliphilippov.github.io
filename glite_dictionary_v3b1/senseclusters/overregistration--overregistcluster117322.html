overregistration

noun
excess registration
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˌrɛdʒɪˈstreɪʃən / (BrE)
the situation in which more people or entries are registered than the available capacity or allowed number

Examples

Class overregistration
Due to overregistration, the class had to turn away several interested students.
The event organizers were surprised by the overregistration and had to find a larger venue.
To avoid overregistration, the system automatically closes when the maximum number is reached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overbooking - registering more people than there is space for
  • antonym: underregistration - having fewer people registered than needed or expected
