velours

noun
heavy, velvet-like fabric
Pronunciation: / vəˈlʊərz / (AmE), / vəˈlʊəz / (BrE)
A heavy fabric with a soft, plush surface that closely resembles velvet, often used for upholstery, clothing, and curtains.

Examples

A velours fabric
She chose a dress made of velours for the winter gala, appreciating its warmth and texture.
The curtains in the theater were made from a rich, blue velours that added to the opulent decor.
The sofa was upholstered in a deep red velours that felt incredibly soft to the touch.
