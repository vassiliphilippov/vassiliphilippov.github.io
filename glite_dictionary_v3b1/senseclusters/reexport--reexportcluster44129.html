reexport

noun
exported imported goods
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛkspɔrt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛkspɔːt / (BrE)
the export of goods that were previously imported into a country, often without significant alteration

Examples

A reexport of goods
The port city is a major hub for the reexport of electronics to neighboring countries.
Many luxury goods arrive as imports and leave again as reexport shipments.
Government statistics track the value of reexport to monitor international trade flows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: export - the act of sending goods to another country for sale
  • antonym: reimport - to import goods that were previously exported
