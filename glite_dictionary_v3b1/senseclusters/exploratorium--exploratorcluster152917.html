exploratorium

noun
interactive learning center
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌsplɔːrəˈtɔːriəm / (AmE), / ɪkˌsplɒrəˈtɔːriəm / (BrE)
a place, often a museum, where people learn by exploring hands-on exhibits and interactive activities

Examples

School groups often visit the exploratorium to experience educational activities outside the classroom.
