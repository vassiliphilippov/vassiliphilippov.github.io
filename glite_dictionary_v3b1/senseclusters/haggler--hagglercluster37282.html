haggler

noun
person who bargains persistently
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhæɡlər / (AmE), / ˈhæɡlə / (BrE)
a person who bargains persistently and often aggressively, especially over the price of goods or services

Examples

A haggler at market
Despite the fixed prices, the determined haggler managed to get a discount.
She was known as a stubborn haggler who never accepted the first offer.
The haggler spent nearly an hour negotiating the price down in the busy marketplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bargainer - a person who negotiates the terms of a purchase or agreement
