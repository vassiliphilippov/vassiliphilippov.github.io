ace

noun
person who is highly skilled
5.0
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪs / (BrE)
a person who excels at a particular activity or is notably skilled in any field

Examples

An ace pilot
After years of training, he became an ace pilot with a flawless record.
She is an ace at solving complex mathematical problems.
The company hired an IT ace to improve their computer systems.
