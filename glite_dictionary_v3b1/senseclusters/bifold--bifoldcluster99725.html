bifold

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈbaɪ.foʊld/ (AmE), /ˈbaɪ.fəʊld/ /; / /ˈbaɪ.foʊld/ (BrE)

Examples

Cleaning the bifold regularly helps it open and close smoothly.
