bifold

noun
two-panel folding door
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈbaɪfəʊld / (BrE)
a door or partition made of two panels that fold together when opened

Examples

A bifold closet
The closet was fitted with a bifold to save space in the small bedroom.
He installed a new bifold between the living room and the study.
Cleaning the bifold regularly helps it open and close smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folding door - a door that opens by folding in sections
  • antonym: sliding door - a door that opens by sliding horizontally
