bipod

noun
two-legged support
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪpɑd / (AmE), / ˈbaɪpɒd / (BrE)
a two-legged support used to steady equipment such as a rifle or camera

Examples

A bipod support
The hunter attached a bipod to his rifle for greater stability while aiming.
A bipod can help keep a camera steady during long exposure shots.
Engineers designed a lightweight bipod for use with surveying equipment.
The sniper rested his rifle on a bipod to improve his aim.
A bipod can make it easier to take steady photos in low light conditions.
She attached a lightweight bipod to her camera for added stability during the shoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stand - a device used to support something in a steady position
  • antonym: tripod - a support with three legs, often used for cameras
