cartop

noun
top of a cart
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtɒp / (BrE)
the upper surface or top part of a cart, used for carrying goods or loads

Examples

Hay on cartop
The peasants set the haybales carefully upon the cartop before the journey home.
Rainwater collected on the cartop after the storm passed through the village.
He climbed onto the cartop to secure the barrels before they left the farm.
