freakiness

noun
strangeness or oddness
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈfriːkinəs / (BrE)

Examples

The freakiness of the weather surprised everyone in the city.

Some people are drawn to the freakiness of experimental art.
