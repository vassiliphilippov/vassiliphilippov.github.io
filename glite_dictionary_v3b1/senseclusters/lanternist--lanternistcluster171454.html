lanternist

noun
magic lantern operator
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæntərnɪst / (AmE), / ˈlæntənɪst / (BrE)
a person who operates a magic lantern to project images for an audience

Examples

Magic lanternist
In the 19th century, a traveling lanternist would visit villages to show magical images.
The children were amazed as the lanternist projected colorful scenes onto the wall.
Before cinemas existed, a lanternist was a popular entertainer at fairs and gatherings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: audience - a group of people watching a performance or show
  • synonym: projectionist - a person who operates a film or slide projector
