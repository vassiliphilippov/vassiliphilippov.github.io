parking crater

noun
large pothole in parking area
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrkɪŋ ˌkreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ ˌkreɪtə / (BrE)
a large pothole or depression in a parking area, often humorously likened to the impact crater of a meteorite

Examples

A parking crater hole
After the winter snow melted, a new parking crater appeared next to my usual spot.
Be careful not to damage your tires on that parking crater by the entrance.
The city had to close half the lot to fix a massive parking crater near the building.
