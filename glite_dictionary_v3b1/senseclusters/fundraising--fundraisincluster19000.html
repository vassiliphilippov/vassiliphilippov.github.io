fundraising

adjective
raising money
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to events, activities, or efforts intended to collect money for a cause or organization

Examples

Fundraising dinner
The school organized a fundraising event to buy new computers for the students.
They launched a fundraising campaign to support local animal shelters.
A fundraising dinner was held to collect money for disaster relief efforts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charitable - relating to giving money or help to those in need
  • synonym: donation - something that is given to help a person or organization
