webber

noun
net maker
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbər / (AmE), / ˈwɛbə / (BrE)
a person who makes or repairs nets or webs, especially in historical contexts

Examples

A skilled webber
In medieval villages, a skilled webber was vital for providing fishing nets.
The old records mention a webber who supplied nets to the entire town.
Few people today know what a webber did in the past.
