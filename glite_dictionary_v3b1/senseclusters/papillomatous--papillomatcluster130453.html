papillomatous

adjective
covered with papillae
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpɪˈloʊmətəs / (AmE), / ˌpæpɪˈləʊmətəs / (BrE)
having or relating to small, nipple-like projections (like a papilloma, a benign wart-like growth)

Examples

Papillomatous skin growth
The doctor noted a papillomatous growth on the patient's skin during the examination.
Under the microscope, the tissue appeared papillomatous with many tiny projections.
Some plant leaves have a papillomatous texture that helps them retain water.
The doctor noted several papillomatous lesions on the patient's vocal cords.
A papillomatous surface can indicate the presence of a benign growth.
Microscopic examination showed papillomatous changes in the tissue sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benign - not harmful; not cancerous
  • synonym: papillose - having small, nipple-like projections
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without projections
  • synonym: tumorous - relating to or resembling a tumor
