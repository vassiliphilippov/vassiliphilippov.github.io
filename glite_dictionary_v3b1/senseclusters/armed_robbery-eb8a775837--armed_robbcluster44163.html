armed robbery

noun
theft involving use of a weapon
Pronunciation: / ɑrmd ˈrɑbəri / (AmE), / ɑːmd ˈrɒbəri / (BrE)
the crime of stealing from someone or somewhere using a weapon, especially a gun

Examples

Armed robbery with a gun
He was sentenced to ten years in prison for armed robbery.
The police are investigating a string of armed robbery incidents in the city.
Wearing masks, the suspects committed armed robbery at the local convenience store.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: robbery - the act of taking property illegally from a person or place, often using force
