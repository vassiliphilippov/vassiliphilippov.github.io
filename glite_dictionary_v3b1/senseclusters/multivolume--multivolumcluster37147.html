multivolume

noun
set of several books
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈvɑːljuːm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈvɒljuːm / (BrE)
a set of books or a work published in more than one separate volume

Examples

Multivolume set

Collectors often seek out rare multivolume editions of classic literature.
