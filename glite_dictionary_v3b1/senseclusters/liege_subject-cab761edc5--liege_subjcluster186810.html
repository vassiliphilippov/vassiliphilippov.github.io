liege subject

noun
vassal owing service to a lord
9.9
Pronunciation: / liːdʒ ˈsʌbdʒɛkt / (AmE), / liːdʒ ˈsʌbdʒɪkt / (BrE)
a person who owes loyalty and service to a feudal lord, typically as the holder of land under the feudal system

Examples

A liege subject swears loyalty
As a liege subject, he swore an oath of loyalty in the grand hall of the castle.
In medieval times, every liege subject was expected to provide military service to his lord.
The king relied on his liege subjects to maintain order throughout the realm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vassal - a person in the feudal system who owes allegiance and service to a lord
