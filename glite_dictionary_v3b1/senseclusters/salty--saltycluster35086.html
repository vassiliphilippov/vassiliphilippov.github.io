Synonyms

  • saline
    4.6 0.28/m
    saline is a formal word for something that contains salt, while salty is the common everyday adjective for that taste.
  • briny
    6.4 0.33/m
    briny describes a taste often linked to seawater, while salty is a general word for foods that taste of salt.
