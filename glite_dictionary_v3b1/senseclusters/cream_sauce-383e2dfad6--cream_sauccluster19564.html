cream sauce

noun
a white sauce made with cream
3.9 1.5
Pronunciation: /kriːm sɔːs/ /; / /kriːm sɑːs/ (AmE), /kriːm sɔːs/ (BrE)
a white sauce made primarily with cream, often used as a base or accompaniment in various dishes

Examples

Cream sauce for pasta
Many pasta dishes use cream sauce as a delicious alternative to tomato-based sauces.
The chicken was served with a rich cream sauce seasoned with garlic and herbs.
You can make a simple cream sauce by simmering heavy cream with butter and cheese.
