urushiol

noun
plant allergen oil
Pronunciation: / jʊˈruːʃiˌɔl / (AmE), / jʊˈruːʃiˌɒl / (BrE)
an oily organic substance found in certain plants, such as poison ivy, that causes allergic skin reactions

Examples

Urushiol from poison ivy
Contact with urushiol from poison ivy can result in an itchy rash.
Washing your skin quickly after touching urushiol may help prevent an allergic reaction.
Scientists study urushiol to better understand how it triggers skin inflammation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allergen - a substance that causes an allergic reaction
  • synonym: poison ivy - a plant that contains urushiol and causes skin irritation
