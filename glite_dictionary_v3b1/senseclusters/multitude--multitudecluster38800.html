multitude

Examples

Synonyms

  • masses
    2.3 0.26/m
    masses are the ordinary people contrasted with the elite and often imply social class, while multitude simply stresses a large number.
  • mass
    3.0 15/m
    mass refers to ordinary people as a social class, often used in political talk, while multitude highlights sheer size rather than class.
  • populace
    5.7 5.52/m
    populace means the people of a place in general and is neutral in tone, while multitude suggests a large crowd of common people.
