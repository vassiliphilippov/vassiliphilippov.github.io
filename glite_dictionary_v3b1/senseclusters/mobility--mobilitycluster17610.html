mobility

noun
ability to move freely
1.0
Pronunciation: / moʊˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / məʊˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability or quality of moving or being moved freely and easily

Examples

Full mobility
After her surgery, she worked hard to regain full mobility in her leg.
Economic mobility is considered an important measure of social progress.
Improvements in public transport have increased mobility for many city residents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immobility - the state of being unable to move
