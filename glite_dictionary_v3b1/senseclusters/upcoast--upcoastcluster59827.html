upcoast

noun
region farther along coast
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌkoʊst / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌkəʊst / (BrE)
the area or region farther along a coast, often toward the head or in a northward direction

Examples

The upcoast region
They decided to explore the upcoast for hidden beaches and quiet coves.
Many fishing villages are scattered along the upcoast beyond the main harbor.
After lunch, we drove upcoast to see the famous lighthouse on the cliffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coastline - the outline or boundary of a coast
  • antonym: downcoast - the area or region farther down a coast, often toward the south or mouth
