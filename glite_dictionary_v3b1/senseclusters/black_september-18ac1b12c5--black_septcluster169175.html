Black September

noun
Palestinian militant group (1970s)
Pronunciation: / blæk sɛpˈtɛmbɚ / (AmE), / blæk sɛpˈtɛmbə / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization, active mainly in the early 1970s, known for carrying out international terrorist attacks after splitting from the PLO in 1974.

Examples

Black September group
Members of Black September operated under various aliases in different countries.
The Black September organization was responsible for the 1972 Munich Olympics attack.
The activities of Black September drew international condemnation in the 1970s.
