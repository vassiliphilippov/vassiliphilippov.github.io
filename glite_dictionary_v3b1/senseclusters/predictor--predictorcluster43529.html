predictor

noun
person who forecasts the future
1.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktɚ / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktə / (BrE)
a person who forecasts future events, often using expert knowledge or data

Examples

Expert predictor of trends
As an experienced predictor of economic trends, she is often asked for her opinion on the market.
Many people regard him as a reliable predictor of political outcomes.
The weather predictor gave a surprisingly accurate forecast for the entire month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forecaster - a person who predicts, especially about the weather or other future events
