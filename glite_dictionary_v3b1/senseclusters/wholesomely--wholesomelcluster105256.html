wholesomely

adverb
in a wholesome manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊl.səm.li / (AmE), / ˈhəʊl.səm.li / (BrE)
In a wholesome, morally good, or beneficial manner.

Examples

To live wholesomely
She always tries to live wholesomely, making healthy choices every day.
The discussion was conducted wholesomely and respectfully, focusing on honest opinions.
The film portrays family life wholesomely, without sensationalism or negativity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wholesome - characterized by moral goodness or promoting well-being
