Nestorius

proper noun
Syrian Christian patriarch, namesake of Nestorianism
9.9
Pronunciation: /ne.ˈstɔː.rɪ.əs/ /; / /nə.ˈstɔː.rɪ.əs/ (AmE), /ne.ˈstɔː.rɪ.əs/ /; / /nə.ˈstɔː.rɪ.əs/ (BrE)
(c. 386–451) Syrian Christian bishop and Patriarch of Constantinople whose teachings led to the theological doctrine known as Nestorianism.
