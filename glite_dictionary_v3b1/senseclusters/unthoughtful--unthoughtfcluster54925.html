unthoughtful

adjective
lacking careful thought; inconsiderate
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɔtfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɔːtfəl / (BrE)
not exhibiting or characterized by careful thought; lacking consideration or reflection

Examples

Unthoughtful comments
Her unthoughtful comments hurt the feelings of those around her.
It was unthoughtful of him to leave without saying goodbye.
Making unthoughtful decisions can lead to unnecessary problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inconsiderate - not caring about the feelings or needs of others
  • antonym: thoughtful - showing careful thought or consideration
