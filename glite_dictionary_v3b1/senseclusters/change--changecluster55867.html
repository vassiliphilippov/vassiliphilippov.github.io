Synonyms

  • turn
    1.0 258/m
    turn means to become or cause a new state, while change is a general making or becoming different.
  • transform
    3.4 48/m
    transform means to change something in a deep or complete way, while change covers small or large differences.
  • turn into
    2.2 39/m
    turn into means to become something else or change form, while change describes becoming different in any way.
  • alter
    2.5 41/m
    alter means to make a small or partial change in form or detail, while change can mean any difference.
  • modify
    3.2 26/m
    modify means to make small adjustments to suit a purpose or improve something, while change is more general.
  • vary
    2.6 23/m
    vary means to differ or to make several different versions, while change usually means one act of becoming different.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alter - to make a small change or modification to something
  • antonym: preserve - to keep something in its original state
  • antonym: remain - to continue unchanged, to stay the same
  • synonym: transform - to make a thorough or dramatic change in form, appearance, or character
  • synonym: vary - to become different; to change within limits
