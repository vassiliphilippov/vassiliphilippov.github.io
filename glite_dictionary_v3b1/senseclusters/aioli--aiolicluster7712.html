aioli

noun
garlic-flavored mayonnaise sauce
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈoʊli / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊli / (BrE)
a sauce made from garlic and olive oil, often with egg yolk, similar to mayonnaise and used as a condiment or dip

Examples

Garlic aioli sauce
Many restaurants offer aioli as a flavorful substitute for mayonnaise on sandwiches.
She whisked fresh garlic into the aioli to enhance its taste.
The grilled vegetables were served with a side of aioli for dipping.
For extra flavor, try adding some lemon juice to your homemade aioli.
He served roasted potatoes with a side of aioli for dipping.
The grilled fish was topped with a generous dollop of aioli.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mayonnaise - a thick, creamy sauce made of egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice
