standard procedure

A prescribed or established way of doing something that is routinely followed in a particular situation.

Examples

The standard procedure
During emergencies, staff are expected to adhere to the standard procedure without hesitation.
Following the standard procedure ensured that the experiment was conducted correctly and safely.
It is standard procedure to check identification before allowing entry into the building.

Synonyms

  • standard operating procedure
    4.3 0.60/m
    standard operating procedure gives a formal, detailed set of steps for operations, while standard procedure is a more general prescribed way to do tasks.
  • SOP
    4.7 0.33/m
    SOP is an abbreviation used in organizations for routine methods, while standard procedure names a general prescribed way to act.
  • standing operating procedure
    3.9 0.00/m
    standing operating procedure is a phrase meaning a continuously in-force method used in organizations, while standard procedure is any established way routinely followed.
