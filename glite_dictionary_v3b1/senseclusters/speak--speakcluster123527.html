speak

verb
give a speech or talk
3.2 80/m
Pronunciation: / spiÀêk / (AmE), / spiÀêk / (BrE)
to give a speech or formal talk to an audience

Examples

To speak at a meeting
Many guests were asked to speak about their experiences during the conference.
She will speak to the shareholders at tomorrow's meeting.
The mayor was invited to speak at the graduation ceremony.

Synonyms

  • address
    4.4 17/m
    address is a formal verb people use to talk directly to a group or audience, while speak means to give a speech to listeners.
  • deliver a speech
    3.3 0.45/m
    deliver a speech means giving a formal talk to an audience, while speak is the general term for making such talks.
  • make a speech
    2.8 1.19/m
    make a speech is for delivering a formal talk, often at public ceremonies, while speak means giving a talk in general.
  • speak in public
    3.9 0.20/m
    speak in public means talking before people outside private settings, while speak means to give a formal talk to an audience.
