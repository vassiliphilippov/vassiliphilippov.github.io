tight deadline

noun
short or strict time limit
7.4
Pronunciation: / taɪt ˈdɛdlaɪn / (AmE), / taɪt ˈdɛdlaɪn / (BrE)
a time limit for completing a task that is short or allows little flexibility

Examples

A tight deadline approaches
Due to the tight deadline, the team decided to divide the work among more people.
Journalists often work with tight deadlines to deliver news quickly.
She had to finish the project under a tight deadline and barely made it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strict deadline - a deadline with little or no flexibility
