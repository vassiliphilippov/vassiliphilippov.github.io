thenceforth

adverb
from that time onward
Pronunciation: / ðɛnsˈfɔrθ / (AmE), / ðɛnsˈfɔːθ / (BrE)
From that time onward; starting from a specific point in time and continuing after that.

Examples

From thenceforth onward
Thenceforth, their relationship was never the same.
He resigned from the board, and thenceforth had no involvement in company affairs.
The policy was updated, and thenceforth all employees followed the new guidelines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thereafter - after that time
