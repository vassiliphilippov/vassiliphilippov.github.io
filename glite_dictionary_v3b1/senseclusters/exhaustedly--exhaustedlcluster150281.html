exhaustedly

adverb
with great tiredness
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔstɪdli / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːstɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows extreme tiredness or weariness

Examples

Speak exhaustedly
He spoke exhaustedly after running the marathon without stopping.
The workers slumped exhaustedly into their chairs at the end of the shift.
She smiled exhaustedly as she finished the last page of her assignment.
