Synonyms

  • come around
    2.7 1.44/m
    come around is used for regular events that occur again in a cycle, while roll around is a casual phrasal for the same idea.
  • recur
    5.7 5.84/m
    recur means something happens again at intervals and often repeatedly, while roll around focuses on a regular time arriving again.
  • reoccur
    3.8 0.18/m
    reoccur simply means to happen again after some time, while roll around implies a regular, returning time or event.
