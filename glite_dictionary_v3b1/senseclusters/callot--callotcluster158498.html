callot

noun
small close-fitting cap
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkælɒt / (BrE)
a small, close-fitting cap, especially one worn by clerics or artists in the past

Examples

A callot cap
The old portrait showed a priest wearing a black callot on his head.
In the studio, the artist adjusted his callot before starting to paint.
During the ceremony, each cleric wore a traditional callot as part of their attire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beret - a round, flat cap often worn by artists
  • synonym: skullcap - a small, rounded cap fitting closely to the head
