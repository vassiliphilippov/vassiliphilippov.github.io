Pyotr Tchaikovsky

proper noun
Russian composer (1840–1893)
6.3
Pronunciation: / piˈjɔtər tʃaɪˈkɒfski / (AmE), / pɪˈɒtə tʃaɪˈkɒfski / (BrE)
