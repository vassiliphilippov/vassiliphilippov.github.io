glockenspiel

noun
metal percussion instrument played with hammers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑk.ənˌspiːl / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒk.ənˌspiːl / (BrE)
a percussion instrument with a set of tuned metal bars arranged in a frame, played by striking with small hammers

Examples

A glockenspiel instrument
I bought a small glockenspiel for my nephew to encourage his interest in music.
The child played the glockenspiel during the school concert.
The distinctive sound of the glockenspiel added brightness to the orchestra's performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: xylophone - a percussion instrument with wooden bars played by striking with mallets
