Spartina

noun
a grass genus in salt marshes and swamps
Pronunciation: / spɑːrˈtaɪnə /; / spɑːrˈtiːnə / (AmE), / spɑːˈtiːnə / (BrE)
a genus of grasses found in freshwater swamps and salt marshes, commonly known as cordgrasses
