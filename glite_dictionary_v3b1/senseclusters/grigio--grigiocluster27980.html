grigio

noun
Italian white wine
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːdʒioʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːdʒiəʊ / (BrE)
a type of white wine, especially Pinot Grigio, originally from Italy

Examples

A glass of grigio
She ordered a glass of grigio with her dinner.
The restaurant offers several varieties of grigio on its wine list.
On a hot day, a chilled grigio is especially refreshing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chardonnay - a widely grown white wine grape and the wine made from it
  • synonym: Pinot Grigio - a popular Italian white wine made from the Pinot Grigio grape
  • antonym: red wine - wine made from dark-colored grape varieties
