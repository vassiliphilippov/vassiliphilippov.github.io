stud mare

noun
female horse used for breeding
4.5
Pronunciation: / stʌd mɛr / (AmE), / stʌd meə / (BrE)
A female horse kept for breeding purposes.

Examples

A stud mare for breeding
After retiring from racing, she became a valuable stud mare at the breeding farm.
The stable is renowned for its champion stud mare that has produced several race winners.
They brought in a new stud mare to improve the bloodline of their horses.
