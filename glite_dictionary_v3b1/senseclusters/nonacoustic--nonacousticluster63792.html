nonacoustic

adjective
not involving sound
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈkuːstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈkuːstɪk / (BrE)
not relating to, produced by, or involving sound or hearing

Examples

Nonacoustic methods
The laboratory used nonacoustic methods to detect movement in the water.
A nonacoustic alarm system relies on light instead of sound to alert people.
Researchers are developing nonacoustic communication devices for use in noisy environments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acoustic - relating to sound or the sense of hearing
