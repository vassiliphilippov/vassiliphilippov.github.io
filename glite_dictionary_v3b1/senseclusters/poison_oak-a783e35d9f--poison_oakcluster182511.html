poison oak

noun
plant causing skin irritation
8.7 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪzən oʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪzən əʊk / (BrE)
a North American climbing or spreading plant with leaves that resemble oak leaves, producing an oil that causes skin irritation and rash upon contact

Examples

Poison oak plant
He developed a rash after brushing against poison oak on the hiking trail.
It is important to recognize poison oak to avoid skin irritation during outdoor activities.
The park ranger warned visitors about the presence of poison oak near the campsite.
