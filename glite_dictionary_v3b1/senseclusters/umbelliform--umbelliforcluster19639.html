umbelliform

adjective
shaped like an umbel (flower cluster)
Pronunciation: / ʌmˈbɛl.ɪ.fɔrm / (AmE), / ʌmˈbɛl.ɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of an umbel; resembling an umbel, especially as seen in certain flowers or inflorescences.

Examples

Umbelliform flower shape
Several members of the carrot family display umbelliform inflorescences.
The umbelliform arrangement of the blossoms is a distinguishing feature of this species.
The plant produces umbelliform clusters of tiny white flowers in midsummer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: umbel - a flat-topped or rounded flower cluster with stalks of nearly equal length from a common point
