circuit

verb
to connect in a loop
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkɪt / (BrE)
to connect or arrange components in a closed path or loop for electricity or signals to flow

Examples

To circuit devices

Before testing, make sure you have circuited all the necessary components together.
Students learned how to circuit a simple light bulb and battery in science class.

Related Terms
