ultimate

adjective
last or final in a series
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltəmɪt /; / ˈʌltəmət / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət / (BrE)
being the last, final, highest, or most extreme in a series or degree

Examples

The ultimate goal

Climbing Mount Everest is considered the ultimate challenge for many mountaineers.
Their ultimate goal is to build a sustainable business that helps the community.
