pose

verb
pretend to be someone else, often deceptively
2.2
Pronunciation: / poʊz / (AmE), / pəʊz / (BrE)
to pretend to be someone or something you are not, often with the intention to deceive

Examples

To pose as a doctor
He was arrested for posing as a government official.
She tried to pose as a reporter in order to get access to the event.
The criminal posed as a police officer to gain entry to the house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impersonate - to pretend to be another person for entertainment or fraud
  • synonym: masquerade - to pretend to be someone else, especially to deceive
