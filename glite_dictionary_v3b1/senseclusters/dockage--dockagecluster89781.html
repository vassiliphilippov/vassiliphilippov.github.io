dockage

noun
place for docking ships in a harbor
9.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑː.kɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈdɒk.ɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a place in a harbor where a ship is docked for loading, unloading, or repairs, sometimes equipped with gates to control water levels
