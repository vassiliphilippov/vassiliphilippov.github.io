garlicky

adjective
tasting or smelling of garlic
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪki / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪki / (BrE)
Having the taste or smell of garlic; resembling or containing garlic.

Examples

Garlicky bread
After eating the garlicky bread, his breath smelled strongly of garlic.
She made a garlicky dip that everyone enjoyed at the party.
The pasta had a delicious, garlicky flavor that lingered after each bite.
