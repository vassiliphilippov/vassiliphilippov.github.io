self-destruct

verb
destroy itself automatically
1.0 5.4
Pronunciation: /self dɪ.ˈstrʌkt/ /; / /self.dɪ.ˈstrʌkt/ (AmE), /self dɪ.ˈstrʌkt/ /; / /self.dɪ.ˈstrʌkt/ (BrE)

Examples
