cuddling

noun
holding someone affectionately; close embrace
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌd.əl.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌd.əl.ɪŋ / (BrE)
holding someone close in one's arms as a sign of affection; the act of embracing or being physically close for comfort or warmth, often in a gentle or loving manner.

Examples

Cuddling with partner
He missed the warmth and comfort of cuddling with his partner before bed.
Sometimes a little cuddling is all you need after a stressful day.
They spent the whole morning cuddling on the couch, enjoying each other's company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hugging - embracing someone by putting your arms around them
  • synonym: snuggling - settling or moving into a warm, comfortable position close to someone
