gambling ring

noun
group involved in illegal gambling
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmbəlɪŋ rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡæmblɪŋ rɪŋ / (BrE)
a group of people who organize or participate in illegal betting activities, especially as a coordinated operation

Examples

A gambling ring operation
He was suspected of running a gambling ring from the back of his bar.
Police arrested several members of a gambling ring during the raid last night.
The documentary exposed how the gambling ring operated across multiple cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bookmaking - the activity of accepting and paying off bets, usually illegally
  • synonym: gambling syndicate - a group that organizes large-scale gambling operations, often illegally
  • antonym: police raid - a sudden police operation to search or arrest people, often used in connection with illegal gambling
