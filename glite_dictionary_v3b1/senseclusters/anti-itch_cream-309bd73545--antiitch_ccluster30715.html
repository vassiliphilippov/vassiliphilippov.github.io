anti-itch cream

noun
cream that relieves itching
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈɪtʃ ˌkɹim / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈɪtʃ ˌkɹiːm / (BrE)
a cream applied to the skin to relieve or reduce itching

Examples
