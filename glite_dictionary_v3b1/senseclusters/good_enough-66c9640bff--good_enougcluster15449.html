good enough

adjective
adequate; sufficiently satisfactory
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf / (AmE), / ɡʊd ɪˈnʌf / (BrE)
adequate or satisfactory for a particular purpose or situation.

Examples

Good enough to pass
He didn't study much, but his score was good enough to pass the exam.
She wondered if her skills were good enough to get the job.
The meal wasn't perfect, but it was good enough for a quick lunch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inadequate - not sufficient or satisfactory
  • synonym: sufficient - enough; adequate for the purpose
