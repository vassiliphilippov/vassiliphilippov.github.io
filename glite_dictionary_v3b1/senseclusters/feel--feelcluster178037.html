feel

noun
general atmosphere or impression
3.4 12/m
Pronunciation: / fil / (AmE), / fiÀêl / (BrE)
the general atmosphere or impression of a place, event, or situation, and the effect it has on people

Examples

The feel of a place
I love the relaxed feel of small coastal towns during the off-season.
The restaurant has a warm, welcoming feel that makes customers want to stay longer.
There was a tense feel to the meeting after the argument.
