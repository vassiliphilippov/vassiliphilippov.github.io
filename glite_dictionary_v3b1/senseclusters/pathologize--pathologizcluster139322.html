pathologize

verb
to analyze for disease
9.2
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈθɑː.lə.dʒaɪz/ /; / /pə.ˈθɔː.lə.dʒaɪz/ (AmE), /pə.ˈθɒl.ə.dʒaɪz/ (BrE)
to examine tissue or biological samples in order to determine the presence or nature of disease

Examples

To pathologize a sample
The laboratory will pathologize the tissue sample to check for any signs of infection.
Doctors often pathologize unusual growths to determine if they are cancerous.
After surgery, the removed organ was pathologized for further analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyze - to examine something in detail to understand it
  • synonym: diagnose - to identify a disease or condition from its signs and symptoms
