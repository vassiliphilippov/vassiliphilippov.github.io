Synonyms

  • Kepler's law of planetary motion
    0.00/m7.3
    Presents the three scientific rules describing planetary motion: ellipses, equal area speeds, period-distance relation
    vsKepler's law: one of the three scientific laws formulated by Johannes Kepler describing the motion of planets around the Sun, outlining their elliptical orbits, area speeds, and the relationship between orbital period and distance from the Sun.

Etymology

Named after Johannes Kepler, a German astronomer.

Related Terms
