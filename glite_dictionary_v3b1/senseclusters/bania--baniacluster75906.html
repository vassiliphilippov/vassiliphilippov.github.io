bania

noun
Indian merchant caste
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːniə / (AmE), / ˈbɑːniə / (BrE)
a member of a traditional Indian merchant or trading caste, known for involvement in commerce and business

Examples

Many banias are known for their expertise in business and finance.
