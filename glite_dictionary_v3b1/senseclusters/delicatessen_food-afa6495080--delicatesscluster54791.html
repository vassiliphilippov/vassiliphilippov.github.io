delicatessen food

noun
ready-to-eat foods (often at a delicatessen)
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən fuːd / (AmE), / ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən fuːd / (BrE)
Ready-to-eat food products, especially those sold in a delicatessen, such as cured meats, cheeses, and salads.

Examples

Many supermarkets have a counter dedicated to delicatessen food such as cold cuts and specialty cheeses.

We stopped by the market to pick up some delicatessen food for the picnic.
