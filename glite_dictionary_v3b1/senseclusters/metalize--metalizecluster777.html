metalize

verb
to coat with a layer of metal
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to coat or cover (an object) with a thin layer of metal, especially by chemical or physical means

Examples

To metalize a surface
After you metalize the object, it gains a shiny, reflective appearance.
The manufacturer decided to metalize the plastic parts to improve their durability.
We will metalize the surface using a special electroplating process.
