sheet of paper

noun
a single piece of paper
1.0 2.84/m
Pronunciation: /  Éit …ôv Ààpe…™p…ö / (AmE), /  ÉiÀêt …ôv Ààpe…™p…ô / (BrE)
A single, flat, usually rectangular piece of paper, used for writing or printing on.

Examples

A sheet of paper
Please hand me a sheet of paper so I can take notes.
She wrote her ideas on a blank sheet of paper during the meeting.
The printer jammed because it tried to feed two sheets of paper at once.

Synonyms

  • piece of paper
    1.0 8.50/m
    piece of paper is any single loose paper used for notes or printing, while sheet of paper is the common phrase for a single flat rectangular paper.
  • sheet
    1.0 19/m
    sheet is a short word people use for a single paper, while sheet of paper is the full phrase that clearly means one flat page.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piece of paper - a separate part or portion of paper
