fingerless

adjective
without fingers
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚləs / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡələs / (BrE)
lacking fingers, either naturally or as a result of removal

Examples

Fingerless gloves
She wore fingerless gloves to keep her hands warm while using her phone.
The kitten was born fingerless, with only small stubs for paws.
The statue had a fingerless hand after years of weathering.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fingered - having fingers
