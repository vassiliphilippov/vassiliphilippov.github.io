Aralia stipulata

noun
Chinese aralia species, less prickly than angelica tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.li.ə ˌstɪ.pjəˈleɪ.tə / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.lɪ.ə ˌstɪ.pjʊˈleɪ.tə / (BrE)
A species of aralia native to China, similar to the American angelica tree but less prickly.

Examples
