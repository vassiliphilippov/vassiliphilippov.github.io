talpid

noun
mole family mammal
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtælpɪd / (AmE), / ˈtælpɪd / (BrE)
a small, burrowing mammal of the family Talpidae, such as a mole

Examples

A talpid mammal
The biologist studied the talpid to learn more about its underground habits.
A rare species of talpid was discovered in the remote forest region.
Farmers sometimes encounter a talpid while digging in their fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mole - a small, burrowing mammal with velvety fur and spade-like forefeet
  • antonym: rodent - a small mammal such as a mouse or rat, not closely related to moles
