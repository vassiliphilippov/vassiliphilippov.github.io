quill

verb
to shape paper art
9.0
Pronunciation: /kwɪl/ (AmE), /kwɪl/ (BrE)
to make decorative designs by rolling and shaping narrow strips of paper into various forms.

Examples

To quill paper shapes
She spent the afternoon quilling colorful paper strips into flowers for the invitation.
He enjoys quilling intricate patterns to decorate greeting cards.
Many artists have started quilling as a relaxing and creative hobby.
