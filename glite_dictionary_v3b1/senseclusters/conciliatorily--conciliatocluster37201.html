conciliatorily

adverb
peace-making manner
Pronunciation: / kənˌsɪliˈeɪtərəli / (AmE), / kənˌsɪlɪˈeɪtərɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is intended to make peace or reduce hostility

Examples

To speak conciliatorily
He responded conciliatorily to the criticism, hoping to avoid further conflict.
The manager spoke conciliatorily to resolve the disagreement between team members.
She acted conciliatorily after realizing her words had upset her friend.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressively - in a forceful or hostile way
  • synonym: peacefully - in a calm or non-violent way
