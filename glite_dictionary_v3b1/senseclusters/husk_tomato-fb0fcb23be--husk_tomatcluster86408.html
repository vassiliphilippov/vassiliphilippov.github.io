husk tomato

noun
edible berry of Physalis genus with papery husk
3.2
Pronunciation: / hʌsk təˈmeɪɾoʊ /; / hʌsk təˈmeɪtoʊ / (AmE), / hʌsk təˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
an edible fruit (berry) of the genus Physalis, such as the tomatillo, that is enclosed in a thin, papery husk
