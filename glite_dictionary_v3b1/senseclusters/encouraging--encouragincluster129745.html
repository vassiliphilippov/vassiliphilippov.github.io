encouraging

adjective
giving support or hope
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɝrɪdʒɪŋ /; / ɪnˈkɝːɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
giving someone support, confidence, or hope; making someone more likely to do something or feel positive

Examples

An encouraging smile
Her teacher gave her an encouraging smile after she answered the question.
It is encouraging to see so many people interested in volunteering.
The initial results of the experiment are very encouraging.
Her words were encouraging and helped me regain my confidence before the exam.
The team's recent performance is an encouraging sign for future success.
We received several encouraging letters from friends during our difficult time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraging - making someone lose confidence or enthusiasm
  • synonym: supportive - giving help or encouragement
