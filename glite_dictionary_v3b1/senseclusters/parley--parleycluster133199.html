parley

noun
negotiation between enemies
8.1 0.22/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêrÀåli / (AmE), / Ààp…ëÀêli / (BrE)
a discussion or conference held to negotiate terms, especially between enemies or opposing sides during a conflict

Examples

A parley between enemies
After hours of fierce fighting, both sides called for a parley to avoid further bloodshed.
The pirates held a parley with the merchant crew before deciding their fate.
The two commanders agreed to a parley to discuss the terms of a possible truce.

Synonyms

  • talks
    2.3 0.19/m
    talks are formal discussions between groups to reach an agreement, while parley is a short meeting often held between enemies during conflict.
  • negotiation
    3.5 44/m
    negotiation is the longer process of discussing terms to reach agreement, while parley is a single, often urgent meeting between opposing sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negotiation - formal discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
