jurisprudence

noun
theory or philosophy of law
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒʊrɪsˈpruːdəns /; / ˌdʒʊrɪsˈprudns / (AmE), / ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːdəns /; / ˌdʒʊərɪsˈpruːd(ə)ns / (BrE)
the theory, philosophy, or science of law; the study of legal principles and systems

Examples

Study of jurisprudence
Many legal scholars have contributed to the field of jurisprudence with influential theories.
