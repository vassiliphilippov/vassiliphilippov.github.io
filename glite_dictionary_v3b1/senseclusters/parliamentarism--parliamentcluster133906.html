parliamentarism

noun
parliament-based government
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrləˈmɛntəˌrɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpɑːlɪəˈmɛntərɪzəm / (BrE)
a system of government in which parliament holds the main power and the executive branch is chosen by and accountable to parliament
