acute

adjective
ending in a sharp point
2.4 3.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkjuːt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkjuːt/ (BrE)
sharp or pointed in shape, or forming a sharp angle (less than 90 degrees)

Examples

Acute angle
The bird's beak was long and acute, perfectly suited for probing into flowers.
The cactus has many acute spines along its surface.
The mathematician drew an acute angle that measured less than ninety degrees.
An acute angle measures less than a right angle.
The teacher drew an acute angle on the board to illustrate the concept.
The triangle has three acute angles.
