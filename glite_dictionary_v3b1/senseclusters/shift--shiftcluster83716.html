shift

noun
a group of workers on duty at the same time
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃɪft / (AmE), / ʃɪft / (BrE)
a period of work or a group of workers working during a particular time

Examples

The night shift starts at 11 p.m. and ends at 7 a.m.

I'm working the night shift at the hospital this week.
She swapped her shift so she could attend her son's recital.
