moniliasis

noun
fungal infection by Monilia or Candida
9.3
Pronunciation: / mɑːnɪˈlaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / mɒnɪˈlaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
A fungal infection caused by fungi of the genus Monilia or Candida, especially Candida albicans.

Examples

Moniliasis infection
Prolonged antibiotic use can increase the risk of developing moniliasis.
The patient was diagnosed with moniliasis after presenting with persistent oral lesions.
Treatment for moniliasis typically involves antifungal medications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candidiasis - fungal infection caused by Candida species, especially Candida albicans
