aerospace engineer

noun
engineer of aircraft and spacecraft
Pronunciation: / ˈɛroʊˌspeɪs ɛnˈdʒɪnɪr / (AmE), / ˈeərəʊˌspeɪs enˈdʒɪnɪə / (BrE)
an engineer who designs, develops, and tests aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems and equipment

Examples

Aerospace engineer designs aircraft
After graduating, she became an aerospace engineer at a major aircraft manufacturer.
As an aerospace engineer, he is involved in designing environmentally friendly propulsion systems.
The company hired a team of aerospace engineers to work on the new satellite project.
