sperm oil

noun
oil from the sperm whale, used as lubricant and fuel
Pronunciation: / spɝm oʊɪl / (AmE), / spɜːm ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellowish oil obtained from the head cavity and blubber of the sperm whale, formerly used as a lubricant and in lamps

Examples

Pure sperm oil
Before electricity, sperm oil was prized for use in lamps because it burned brightly and cleanly.
The whaling industry relied heavily on sperm oil for lubricating machinery in the 19th century.
With conservation measures in place, the commercial extraction of sperm oil has ceased.
