bullyingly

adverb
in an intimidating way
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊliɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbʊliɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that intimidates, harasses, or threatens others

Examples

To speak bullyingly
The manager spoke bullyingly to his team, making everyone feel uneasy.
She glared bullyingly at her opponent during the debate.
He bullyingly demanded that everyone follow his instructions without question.
