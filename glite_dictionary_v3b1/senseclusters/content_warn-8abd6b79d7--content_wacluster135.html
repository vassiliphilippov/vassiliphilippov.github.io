content warn

verb
give a notice about sensitive content
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.tɛnt wɔrn / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛnt wɔːn / (BrE)
to give a notice preceding material that may be sensitive or distressing to some audiences, especially online or in media.

Examples

To content warn viewers
Before sharing graphic images, please content warn so viewers can decide if they want to see them.
