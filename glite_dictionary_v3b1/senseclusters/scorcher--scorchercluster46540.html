scorcher

noun
a very hard-hit ball (sports)
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrtʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːtʃə / (BrE)
a ball that is hit or kicked with great force, especially in sports, moving very fast

Examples

A scorcher goal
He hit a scorcher down the first base line and made it to second.
That shot was a real scorcher and gave the goalkeeper no chance.
The crowd erupted as she sent a scorcher over the boundary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blaster - a ball hit with tremendous force (sports slang)
  • antonym: softie - a ball hit gently or weakly (sports slang)
