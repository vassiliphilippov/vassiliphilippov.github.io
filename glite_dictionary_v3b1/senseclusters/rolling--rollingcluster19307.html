rolling

noun
deep, prolonged sound (as of thunder or bells)
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
a deep, continuous, and prolonged sound, especially of thunder or large bells

Examples

Rolling thunder
A distant rolling of thunder echoed across the valley.
The rolling of the church bells could be heard throughout the town.
We listened to the low rolling that signaled an approaching storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peal - a loud, prolonged ringing sound, especially of bells
  • synonym: rumble - a continuous deep, resonant sound
  • antonym: silence - the complete absence of sound
