lodge

verb
formally accuse; make an official complaint
3.6
Pronunciation: / lɑdʒ / (AmE), / lɒdʒ / (BrE)
to make a formal statement accusing someone of a crime to the police or a court

Examples

To lodge a complaint
After discovering the breach, he wanted to lodge a police report immediately.
She lodged formal charges of fraud against her employer last year.
The victim decided to lodge a complaint against the suspect with the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: file - to officially present a document to an authority, often used for complaints or charges
