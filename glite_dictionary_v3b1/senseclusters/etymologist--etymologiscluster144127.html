etymologist

noun
person who studies word origins
Pronunciation: / ˌɛtəˈmɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies the origin, history, and meaning of words.

Examples

An etymologist studies words
As an etymologist, she can trace the history of almost any English word.
Aspiring to become an etymologist, he spent years studying ancient texts and languages.
The documentary featured an etymologist who explained how certain phrases entered the language.
