undetectably

adverb
invisibly; unnoticeably
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈtɛktəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be noticed, discovered, or detected

Examples

Changed undetectably
The flavor was changed so undetectably that no one noticed the difference.
The spy moved undetectably through the crowded market, blending in with the locals.
Tiny errors were introduced undetectably into the data, making them hard to find.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperceptibly - in a way that is impossible to perceive
  • antonym: noticeably - in a way that is easy to see or detect
