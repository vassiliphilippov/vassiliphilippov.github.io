cereal oat

noun
edible grain-producing grass (Avena sativa)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪə.ri.əl oʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪə.ri.əl əʊt / (BrE)
a species of grass (Avena sativa) cultivated in temperate regions for its edible grains, used especially for food and livestock feed

Examples

Cereal oat grains
Breakfast foods like oatmeal are made from cereal oat grains.
Farmers in northern Europe often grow cereal oat as a staple crop for both people and animals.
The cereal oat thrives in cooler climates compared to other cereal crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oat - common name for members of the genus Avena, especially their edible seeds
