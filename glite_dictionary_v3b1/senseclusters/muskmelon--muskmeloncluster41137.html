muskmelon

noun
sweet, fragrant melon fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌskˌmɛlən /; / ˈmʌskˌmɛl.ən / (AmE), / ˈmʌskˌmɛlən /; / ˈmʌskˌmɛl.ən / (BrE)
a type of sweet, fragrant melon with edible flesh, often with a netted rind, including varieties such as cantaloupe
