porterage

noun
the carrying of goods by porters
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔrtərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtərɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or service of carrying goods, luggage, or other burdens, especially by porters or for a fee

Examples

Porterage of luggage
Arrangements for porterage were made at each stop along the hiking trail.
He included the cost of porterage in the travel expenses for the expedition.
The hotel charged a small fee for porterage to bring our bags to the room.
