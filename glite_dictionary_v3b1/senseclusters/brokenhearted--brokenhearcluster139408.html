brokenhearted

adjective
deeply sorrowful; grieving
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˌbroʊkənˈhɑrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌbrəʊkənˈhɑːtɪd / (BrE)
overwhelmed by grief or sorrow, especially due to loss or disappointment

Examples

A brokenhearted person
After the sudden loss of her dog, she felt utterly brokenhearted for weeks.
Despite being brokenhearted, she tried to put on a brave face for her friends.
He looked brokenhearted when he heard the disappointing news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - noticeably happy and optimistic
  • synonym: heartbroken - affected by overwhelming sorrow or grief
