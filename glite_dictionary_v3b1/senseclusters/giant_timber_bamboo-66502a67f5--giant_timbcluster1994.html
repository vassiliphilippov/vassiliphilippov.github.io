giant timber bamboo

noun
large species of bamboo plant
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɪmbɚ bæmˈbu / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɪmbə bæmˈbuː / (BrE)
a very large species of bamboo with thick-walled stems, native to China and grown in many other regions

Examples

Tall giant timber bamboo
Many gardeners seek out giant timber bamboo for its fast growth and tall, dense canes.
The craftsman built a sturdy fence using giant timber bamboo harvested from his garden.
The lush grove of giant timber bamboo created a natural privacy screen around the villa.
