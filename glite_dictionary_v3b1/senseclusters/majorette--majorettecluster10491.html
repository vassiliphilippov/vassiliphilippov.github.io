majorette

noun
female baton twirler in marching bands
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmeɪdʒəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌmædʒəˈrɛt /; / ˌmadʒəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a girl or woman who twirls a baton and performs choreographed routines, usually as part of a marching band or parade
