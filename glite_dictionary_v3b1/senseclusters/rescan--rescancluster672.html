rescan

noun
repeat scan
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈskæn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈskæn / (BrE)
an act of scanning something again to obtain clearer, corrected, or updated results

Examples

A rescan of the file
The technician performed a rescan of the document to improve the image quality.
After fixing the error, a quick rescan confirmed that all files were now safe.
If the barcode is unreadable, try a rescan to capture the correct information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scan - an act of examining something closely or systematically
