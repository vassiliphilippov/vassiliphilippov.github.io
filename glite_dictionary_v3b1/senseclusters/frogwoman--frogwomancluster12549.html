frogwoman

noun
female underwater diver (e.g., military or technical)
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔɡˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfrɒɡˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who works underwater as a diver, especially for military, technical, or rescue purposes, often using special equipment.

Examples

Frogwoman diver
As a navy frogwoman, she participated in several high-risk underwater operations.
The frogwoman emerged from the water, carrying vital equipment for her team.
The seasoned frogwoman prepared her diving gear before the deep-sea rescue mission.
