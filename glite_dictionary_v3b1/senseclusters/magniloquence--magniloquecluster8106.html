magniloquence

noun
grand or bombastic language
Pronunciation: / mægˈnɪl.ə.kwəns / (AmE), / mæɡˈnɪl.ə.kwəns / (BrE)
The use of grand or bombastic language, often with excessive verbal ornamentation.

Examples

Grand magniloquence
Critics accused the author of magniloquence in his latest novel.
She recognized the magniloquence in his writing and preferred a simpler style.
The politician's magniloquence impressed some but left others unconvinced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grandiloquence - lofty, pompous, or extravagant language
  • antonym: plainness - simple or unadorned style of language
