sutura

noun
immovable skull joint
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / suÀêÀàt är.…ô / (AmE), / suÀêÀàtj ä…ô.r…ô / (BrE)
An immovable joint, especially one between the bones of the skull, where the bones are fused together.

Examples

The sutura of skull
A fracture at the lambdoid sutura can indicate severe head trauma.
Craniosynostosis is a condition where a sutura closes prematurely.
The coronal sutura separates the frontal bone from the parietal bones.

Synonyms

  • suture
    8.2 0.2/m
    suture is the standard English name for an immovable skull joint, while sutura is a variant form of the same anatomical term.
  • fibrous joint
    7.9 0.00/m
    fibrous joint is an immovable joint joined by fibrous tissue, while sutura refers specifically to skull bones fused together.
