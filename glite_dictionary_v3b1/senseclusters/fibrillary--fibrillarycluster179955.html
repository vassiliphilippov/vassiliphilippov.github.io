fibrillary

adjective
irregular muscle contractions
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbrəˌlɛri / (AmE), / ˈfɪbrɪləri / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by rapid, irregular contractions or electrical activity in muscle tissue, especially the heart

Examples

Fibrillary heart activity
The doctor noted fibrillary movements in the patient's heart muscle during the examination.
An ECG can detect fibrillary activity that may indicate a serious cardiac condition.
Researchers studied the effects of fibrillary contractions on overall heart function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibrillation - rapid, irregular, and unsynchronized contraction of muscle fibers
