orchestration

noun
musical arrangement for orchestra or band
5.5 5.4
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.kə.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɔː.kə.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the arrangement of a musical composition for performance by an orchestra or band, specifying which instruments play which parts
