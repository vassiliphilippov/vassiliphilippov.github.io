codification

noun
process of systematizing laws or rules
7.8 6.0
Pronunciation: /kɑː.də.fə.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /koʊ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəʊ.dɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
