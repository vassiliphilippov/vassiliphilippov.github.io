responsory

adjective
relating to a response
Pronunciation: / rɪˈspɑːnsəri / (AmE), / rɪˈspɒnsəri / (BrE)
relating to or serving as a response, especially as a short chant or passage in a religious service.

Examples

Responsory passage
The responsory chant echoed through the cathedral during the evening service.
A responsory hymn was sung after each reading in the ceremony.
The choir performed a responsory piece that alternated between soloist and group.
