Synonyms

  • buccaneer
    9.5 1.12/m
    a buccaneer attacks ships and takes their goods at sea, while a pirate is the general name for such robbers.
  • sea robber
    8.6 0.00/m
    a sea robber loots ships or raids coasts without permission, while a pirate is the standard word for someone who does this.
  • sea rover
    9.9 0.00/m
    a sea rover plunders ships and coastal areas without authorization, while a pirate is the common term for that unlawful sailor.
  • freebooter
    9.9 0.09/m
    a freebooter takes spoils during raids and may act as an adventurer, while a pirate specifically raids ships at sea.
