nonculturally

adverb
not involving culture
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkʌltʃɚəli / (AmE), / nɒnˈkʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not related to or influenced by culture

Examples

To act nonculturally
The new guidelines were implemented nonculturally to ensure fairness for everyone.
Decisions were made nonculturally, focusing only on economic factors.
The assessment was designed to be nonculturally biased, treating all backgrounds equally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: culturally - in a way that relates to culture
