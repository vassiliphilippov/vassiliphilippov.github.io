standpoint

noun
a particular point of view or perspective
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstændˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈstæn(d)ˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
an attitude or point of view from which something is considered or evaluated

Examples

An economic standpoint

She considered the proposal from every possible standpoint before making her choice.
The critics examined the film from a feminist standpoint.
