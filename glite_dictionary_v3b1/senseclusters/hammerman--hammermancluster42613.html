hammerman

noun
machine hammer operator
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhæməmæn / (BrE)
a worker who operates a heavy hammering machine, such as a pile driver or power hammer, in construction or manufacturing

Hammerman runs machines
