transsexual

noun
person who has undergone sex reassignment
3.2
Pronunciation: / trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / trænˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
a person whose gender identity is different from their sex assigned at birth, often involving medical transition to live as the other gender

Examples

A transsexual woman

Many transsexual people advocate for equal rights and acceptance in society.
The clinic provides medical support for transsexual individuals seeking gender-affirming treatments.
As a transsexual, she faced many challenges but found support in the community.
Some transsexual individuals choose to undergo medical procedures to align their bodies with their gender identity.
The film explores the life of a transsexual protagonist struggling for acceptance.
