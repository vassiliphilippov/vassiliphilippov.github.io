grandstand

noun
main seating area for spectators
2.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgrænd.stænd/ (AmE), /ˈgrænd.stænd/ (BrE)
the main seating area or structure for spectators at a stadium, racetrack, or similar venue, often with multiple tiers and a roof

Examples

A stadium grandstand
Fans filled every seat in the grandstand for the championship game.
She waved to her family as she walked past the crowded grandstand.
The roar from the grandstand could be heard for miles.
Rain poured down, but those in the grandstand stayed dry under its wide roof.
She waved to her family, who were seated high up in the grandstand overlooking the finish line.
The fans filled the grandstand long before the match began to get the best views.
