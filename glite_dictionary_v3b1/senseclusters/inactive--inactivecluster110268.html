inactive

adjective
not active; idle or unused
4.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈæktɪv / (BrE)
not engaged in any activity; idle, unused, or not in operation.

Examples

Inactive factory
Many inactive factories have fallen into disrepair over time.
The company closed several inactive accounts last year.
The volcano has remained inactive for over a century.
