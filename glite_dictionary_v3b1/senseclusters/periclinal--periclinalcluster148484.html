periclinal

adjective
parallel to surface
9.9
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.ˈklɪ.nəl/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.ˈklɪ.nəl/ (BrE)
parallel to the surface, especially describing the orientation of cell walls or divisions in plant tissues

Examples

Periclinal cell walls
The periclinal arrangement of the cells helps the plant maintain its structure.
Botanists observed a periclinal division that contributed to the thickening of the root.
A periclinal wall is oriented parallel to the surface of the plant organ.
