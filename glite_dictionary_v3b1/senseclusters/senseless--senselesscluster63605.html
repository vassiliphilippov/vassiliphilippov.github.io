senseless

adjective
unconscious; unresponsive to stimuli
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛns.ləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.sləs / (BrE)
unconscious; not able to feel or respond to physical or mental stimuli

Examples

Senseless body
After the fall, he lay senseless on the ground until help arrived.
He was found senseless after inhaling the toxic fumes.
The boxer was knocked senseless by his opponent in the second round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insensible - not able to feel or respond to stimulation; unconscious
  • synonym: unconscious - not awake, especially after being knocked out or fainting
