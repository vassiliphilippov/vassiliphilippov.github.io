refrigerator car

noun
railway car with refrigeration for goods
7.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɚ kɑr / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtə kɑː / (BrE)
A railway freight car equipped with a refrigeration system for transporting perishable goods.

Examples

A refrigerator car transports meat
Meat was transported across the country in a refrigerator car during the summer.
Modern refrigerator cars use advanced cooling technology for long-distance journeys.
The shipment of fresh produce arrived in a refrigerator car to ensure it stayed cool.
