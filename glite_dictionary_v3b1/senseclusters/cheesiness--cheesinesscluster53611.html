cheesiness

noun
strong cheese flavor
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzinəs / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzinəs / (BrE)
the quality of containing, resembling, or tasting strongly of cheese

Examples

Cheesiness of pizza
The cheesiness of the pizza made it a favorite among the children at the party.
She loved the rich cheesiness of the homemade macaroni and cheese.
The cheesiness in the sauce was so strong that it overpowered the other flavors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandness - the quality of lacking strong flavor
  • synonym: creaminess - the quality of being smooth and rich like cream
