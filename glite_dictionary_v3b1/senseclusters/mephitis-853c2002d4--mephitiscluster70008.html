Mephitis

noun
genus of skunks
Pronunciation: / məˈfaɪtɪs / (AmE), / mɪˈfɪtɪs / (BrE)
the genus of skunks (type genus of Mephitinae), including North American species, known for their strong-smelling defensive spray
