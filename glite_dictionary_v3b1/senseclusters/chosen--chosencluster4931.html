chosen

noun
an exclusive or privileged group
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊzən / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊzən / (BrE)
a group of people who are selected or regarded as specially privileged or set apart from others; the elect

Examples

The chosen few
Access to the inner circle was granted solely to the political chosen.
Only a handful of the chosen were invited to the private ceremony.
Throughout history, rulers often believed they ruled as part of a divine chosen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elect - a group of people specially chosen or favored
