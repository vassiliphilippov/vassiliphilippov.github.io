atmospheric phenomenon

noun
event or process in the atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˌætməsˈfɪrɪk fəˈnɑmɪˌnɑn / (AmE), / ˌætməsˈfɪərɪk fɪˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
a physical event or process that takes place in or is caused by the atmosphere

Examples

An atmospheric phenomenon like rainbow
A rainbow is an example of an atmospheric phenomenon caused by the refraction of sunlight.
Hurricanes are dangerous atmospheric phenomena that can cause widespread destruction.
The Northern Lights are a spectacular atmospheric phenomenon visible in polar regions.
