broadbill

noun
tropical American heron
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdbɪl / (BrE)
A tropical American heron related to night herons, known for its broad bill.

Examples

Tropical broadbill heron
Scientists are studying the unique feeding habits of the broadbill.
The broadbill is often seen wading in shallow waters at dusk.
The broadbills build their nests among tall reeds by the river.
