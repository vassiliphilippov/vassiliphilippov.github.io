Fiji Islands

proper noun
group of islands in the Pacific forming Fiji
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːdʒi ˈaɪləndz / (AmE), / ˈfiːdʒi ˈaɪləndz / (BrE)
a group of over 800 islands in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, with about 100 inhabited, forming the country of Fiji

Examples

The Fiji Islands group
