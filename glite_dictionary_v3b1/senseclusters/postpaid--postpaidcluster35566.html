postpaid

adjective
paid for in advance (mail/services)
6.8
Pronunciation: / poʊstˈpeɪd / (AmE), / pəʊstˈpeɪd / (BrE)
(of mail or services) paid for in advance, especially so that the recipient does not need to pay.

Examples

Postpaid shipping
All books ordered will be shipped postpaid to your address.
My mobile plan is a postpaid service with monthly billing.
The package was sent postpaid, so the recipient didn't need to pay anything on delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collect - (of mail or services) to be paid for by the recipient, not in advance
  • synonym: prepaid - paid for before use, especially relating to services or mail
