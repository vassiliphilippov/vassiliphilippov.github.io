failure

noun
omission; neglect of duty; not doing what is expected
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪljɚ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪljə / (BrE)
an act of not doing something expected or required; a lack of action or omission

Examples

Failure to act
Her failure to submit the report on time caused a delay in the project.
The company's failure to comply with regulations resulted in heavy fines.
We discussed the failure to follow up with clients after their purchases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neglect - failure to care for something properly
  • synonym: omission - the action of leaving out or neglecting
  • antonym: success - the accomplishment of an aim or purpose
