mansplaining

noun
condescending explanation
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌspleɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmænˌspleɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
when a man explains something to a woman in a condescending or patronizing way, often assuming she knows less

Examples

Annoying mansplaining
She grew frustrated with his constant mansplaining during the discussion.
Many people find mansplaining disrespectful and unhelpful in the workplace.
He apologized after realizing his comment was an example of mansplaining.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condescending - showing a superior attitude toward others
  • synonym: patronizing - treating someone as if they are less intelligent or capable
