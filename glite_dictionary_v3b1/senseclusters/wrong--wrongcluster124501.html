wrong

noun
harm or injury from violation of a legal right
1.3
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ / (AmE), / rɒŋ / (BrE)
an act or occurrence that harms or injures someone; something that is unjust or unfair

Examples

A great wrong

In court, she testified about the wrong committed against her property rights.
The company sued for the wrong done to its reputation by false accusations.
He apologized when he realized he was wrong in accusing her.
She admitted her wrong and tried to make amends.
The court decided who was in the wrong in the dispute.
