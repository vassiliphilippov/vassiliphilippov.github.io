riot act

noun
vigorous reprimand; severe warning
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪət ækt / (AmE), / ˈraɪət ækt / (BrE)
a severe or forceful warning or reprimand to stop unacceptable behavior

Examples

Read the riot act
If you show up late again, I’ll have to read you the riot act.
The manager threatened to read the riot act to the team after their poor performance.
When the children broke the vase, their mother read them the riot act.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reprimand - a formal expression of disapproval
  • synonym: scolding - an angry or critical rebuke or reprimand
