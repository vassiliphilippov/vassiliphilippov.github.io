pillow

noun
soft cushion for the head (sleeping)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ / (BrE)
A soft cushion used to support the head, especially while sleeping.

Examples

A soft pillow
Feathers often spill out if a pillow's seam comes undone.
He hugged his pillow tightly during the thunderstorm.
She placed her tired head on the soft pillow and quickly fell asleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cushion - a soft bag of material used to make a seat or other object more comfortable
