black tree fern

noun
a tree fern from New Zealand and Australia
Pronunciation: / blæk tri fɝn / (AmE), / blæk tri fɜːn / (BrE)
a large and ornamental tree fern native to New Zealand and Australia, noted for its crown of pinnate fronds with whitish undersides.
