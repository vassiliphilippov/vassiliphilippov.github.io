preprimary

adjective
before primary school
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈpraɪˌmɛri / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈprʌɪməri / (BrE)
relating to the stage of education for young children before they enter primary school

Examples

Preprimary education
Many children attend preprimary classes before starting elementary school.
The government increased funding for preprimary education programs this year.
Teachers in preprimary settings focus on play-based learning and social skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preschool - relating to education before primary school
  • degree higher: primary - relating to the first stage of formal education for children
