pot metal

noun
cast iron for cooking wares
Pronunciation: / pɑt ˈmɛtəl /; / ˈpɑt ˌmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / pɒt ˈmɛtəl /; / ˈpɒt ˌmɛtl̩ / (BrE)
a cheap metal alloy (such as cast iron or a mix of copper and lead) used for making pots, cookware, and inexpensive items
