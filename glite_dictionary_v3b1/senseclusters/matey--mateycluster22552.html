matey

adjective
friendly; acting as friends
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪti / (AmE), / ˈmeɪti / (BrE)
(informal) Friendly in a way that suggests a close friendship or camaraderie; behaving as friends or pals.

Examples

A matey attitude
He's always very matey with new colleagues to make them feel welcome.
The two boys quickly became matey after sitting together in class.
They grew more matey as the project progressed, sharing jokes and stories.
