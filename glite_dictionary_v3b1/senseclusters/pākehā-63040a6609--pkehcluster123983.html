Pākehā

noun
non-Māori New Zealander
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːkɛhɑː / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɛhɑː / (BrE)
a person in New Zealand who is not of Māori descent, especially someone of European origin

Examples

A Pakeha person
