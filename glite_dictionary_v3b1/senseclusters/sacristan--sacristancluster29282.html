sacristan

noun
person in charge of sacred church objects
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsækrɪstən / (AmE), / ˈsækrɪstən / (BrE)
a person in a church who is responsible for looking after the sacred objects and the sacristy

Examples

The sacristan prepares vestments
As sacristan, Maria ensures all the vestments and vessels are ready for each service.
The sacristan carefully arranged the chalices and candles for the upcoming mass.
The priest thanked the sacristan for preparing the altar so beautifully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sexton - a church officer who looks after the church building and grounds
