Juglans

noun
genus of walnut trees
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌɡ.lænz /; / ˈdʒʌɡˌlænz / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌɡ.lænz / (BrE)
the genus of trees (including walnuts and butternuts) that is the type genus of the walnut family (Juglandaceae)

Botanists classify several species of walnuts under the genus Juglans.

The genus Juglans includes both the English walnut and the black walnut.
