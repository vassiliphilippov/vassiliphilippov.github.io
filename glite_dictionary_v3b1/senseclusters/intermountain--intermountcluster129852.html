intermountain

adjective
between mountains
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmaʊntən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmaʊntən / (BrE)
located or occurring between mountains or mountain ranges

Examples

Intermountain valley
The intermountain valley receives less rainfall than the surrounding peaks.
Many unique plant species thrive in the intermountain regions of the western United States.
Travelers crossed the intermountain area to reach the next town beyond the mountains.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coastal - located on or near a coast
  • synonym: mountainous - full of or covered with mountains
