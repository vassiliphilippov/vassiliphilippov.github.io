nonconsumption

noun
refusal to buy
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
the deliberate refusal to buy or use certain goods or services, often as a form of protest or political pressure

Examples

Nonconsumption of products
The activists promoted nonconsumption of fast fashion brands to raise awareness about environmental issues.
A nationwide nonconsumption of certain products pressured the company to change its labor practices.
Through organized nonconsumption, the community sent a strong message to the government about their dissatisfaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boycott - the act of refusing to buy, use, or participate as a protest
  • antonym: consumption - the act of buying or using goods and services
