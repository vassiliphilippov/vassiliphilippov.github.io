grip

noun
small travel bag or suitcase
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪp / (AmE), / ɡrɪp / (BrE)
a small portable bag or suitcase used for carrying clothes and personal items, typically rectangular in shape

Examples

Travel grip
He kept a spare set of toiletries in his grip for last-minute business trips.
She packed her clothes carefully in her old leather grip before leaving for the weekend.
The porter loaded the heavy grip onto the carriage roof.
