grindable

adjective
can be farmed repeatedly
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndəbəl / (BrE)
able to be repeatedly played or farmed in a video game to gain experience, items, or resources.

Examples

Grindable dungeon
This dungeon is very grindable, so players often return to collect rare loot.
Many gamers look for grindable areas to level up their characters quickly.
The event features several grindable quests that reward special items.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ungrindable - not able to be repeatedly played for resources
