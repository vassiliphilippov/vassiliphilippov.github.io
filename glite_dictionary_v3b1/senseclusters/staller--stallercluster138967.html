staller

noun
market stall seller
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔlər / (AmE), / ˈstɔːlə / (BrE)
a person who sells goods from a stall, especially at a market or fair

Examples

Market staller
The staller greeted every passerby with a friendly smile and a special offer.
Each staller at the fair decorated their booth to attract more customers.
After years as a staller, she knew exactly what products would sell best in the market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services
  • synonym: merchant - a person who buys and sells goods in large quantities
  • synonym: vendor - a person who sells goods, especially in the street
