aftermarket

adjective
non-original parts
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
describing parts or accessories made by a company other than the original manufacturer, especially for vehicles

Examples

Aftermarket car parts
He bought an aftermarket exhaust system to improve his car's performance.
Many drivers choose aftermarket wheels for a more personalized look.
The mechanic recommended using aftermarket parts to save money on repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: original equipment - parts made by the original manufacturer
  • synonym: replacement - something that takes the place of another item
