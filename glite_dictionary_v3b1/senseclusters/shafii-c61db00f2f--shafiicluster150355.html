Shafi'i

adjective
of Shafi'i school
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈfiː.i / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈfiː.i / (BrE)
relating to the Shafi'i school of Islamic law or its teachings and followers

Shafi'i legal tradition

She studied Shafi'i jurisprudence to better understand the differences among Islamic legal schools.
