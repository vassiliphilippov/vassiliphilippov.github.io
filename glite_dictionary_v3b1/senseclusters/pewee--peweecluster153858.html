pewee

noun
small North American woodland flycatcher
Pronunciation: / ˈpiˌwi / (AmE), / ˈpiːwiː / (BrE)
A small olive-colored woodland flycatcher found in eastern North America, known for its plaintive call.

Examples

A pewee bird
During migration, several pewees can be seen flitting among the treetops.
The distinctive call of the pewee echoed through the woods on a summer morning.
We spotted a pewee perched quietly on a branch near the forest path.
