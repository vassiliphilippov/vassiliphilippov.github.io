tradesperson

noun
merchant or shopkeeper
3.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtre…™dzÀåp…úrs…ôn / (AmE), / Ààtre…™dzÀåp…úÀês…ôn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to buy and sell goods, especially as a merchant or shopkeeper

Examples

A tradesperson sells goods
The tradesperson greeted every customer with a friendly smile at the market stall.
As a skilled tradesperson, she knew how to negotiate the best prices for her goods.
Many local businesses rely on a trusted tradesperson to supply their daily needs.
