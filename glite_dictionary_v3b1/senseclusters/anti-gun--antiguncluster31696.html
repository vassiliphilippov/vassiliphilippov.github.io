anti-gun

adjective
opposed to guns
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌɡʌn / (BrE)
opposed to the ownership, use, or presence of guns

Examples

Anti-gun laws
The city council passed several anti-gun laws to improve public safety.
Many activists attended the anti-gun rally in the capital last weekend.
Her anti-gun stance was clear during the debate on firearm regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-gun - supporting the ownership or use of guns
