vena epigastrica inferior

noun
abdominal vein draining into external iliac vein
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪ.kə ɪn.ˈfɪr.ɪ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə ep.ɪ.ˈgæs.trɪ.kə ɪn.ˈfɪə.rɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a vein in the lower abdomen that drains into the external iliac vein

Examples

The vena epigastrica inferior vein
