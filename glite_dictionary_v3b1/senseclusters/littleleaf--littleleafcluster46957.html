littleleaf

noun
plant with small leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬lˌlif / (AmE), / ˈlɪtlˌliːf / (BrE)
a plant or tree with small leaves, often used in the names of certain species

Examples

Littleleaf linden
The littleleaf linden is popular for city landscaping because of its compact size.
Gardeners often choose littleleaf species for their neat appearance and easy maintenance.
Some littleleaf plants are better suited to dry climates due to their small leaves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadleaf - a plant or tree with wide, flat leaves
