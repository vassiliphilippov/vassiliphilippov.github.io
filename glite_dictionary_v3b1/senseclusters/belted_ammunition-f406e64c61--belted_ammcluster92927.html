belted ammunition

noun
ammunition in linked strips for machine guns
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛltɪd ˌæmjuˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɛltɪd ˌamjʊˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
Ammunition, usually of small caliber, that is loaded in flexible linked strips for use in a machine gun.

Examples

Belted ammunition belt
After several minutes of continuous use, the belted ammunition ran out and had to be replaced.
Soldiers checked the belted ammunition for any damaged links before the mission.
The gunner fed the belted ammunition into the machine gun before opening fire.

  • synonym: cartridge belt - a belt used to carry cartridges or ammunition
