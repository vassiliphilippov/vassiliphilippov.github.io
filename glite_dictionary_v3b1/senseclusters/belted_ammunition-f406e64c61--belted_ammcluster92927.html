belted ammunition

noun
ammunition in linked strips for machine guns
2.1 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛltɪd ˌæmjuˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɛltɪd ˌamjʊˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
