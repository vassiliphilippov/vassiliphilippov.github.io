Amerind language

noun
indigenous language of the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈæmərɪnd ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈæmərɪnd ˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of the languages spoken by the indigenous peoples of the Americas, especially as classified in linguistic studies

Examples

Amerind languages spoken
Many Amerind languages have only a small number of speakers remaining today.
Scholars continue to debate the origins and relationships among Amerind languages.
This region is home to several distinct Amerind languages still spoken by local communities.
