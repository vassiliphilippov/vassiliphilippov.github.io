refill

noun
product for filling again
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌfɪl / (AmE), / ˈriːfɪl / (BrE)
a product or amount of something used to fill again a container with its original or suitable contents, especially when sold separately to replace what has been used

Examples

A refill pack
After using up the paper, she purchased a notebook refill to continue her notes.
I need to buy a refill for my water filter before it stops working.
The store sells ink refills for all major ballpoint pen brands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replacement - something that takes the place of another
