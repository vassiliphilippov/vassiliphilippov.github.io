about time

x
something happened later than expected
Pronunciation: / əˈbaʊt taɪm / (AmE), / əˈbaʊt tʌɪm / (BrE)
used to express that something has happened later than expected and should have happened sooner

Examples

It is about time
She apologized for her mistake, and I thought it was about time too.
They finally fixed the heating—it's about time we weren't freezing in the office.
You got the promotion? Well, it's about time!
