iodoprotein

noun
protein containing iodine
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪədoʊˈproʊtin / (AmE), / ˌaɪədəʊˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
a protein that contains iodine as part of its structure

Examples

An iodoprotein molecule
Researchers studied the effects of dietary iodine on the production of iodoproteins in animals.
The presence of an iodoprotein can be detected using specialized staining techniques.
Thyroglobulin is a well-known example of an iodoprotein found in the thyroid gland.
