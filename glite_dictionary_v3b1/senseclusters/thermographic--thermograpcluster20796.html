thermographic

adjective
heat imaging
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the recording or imaging of heat patterns using special equipment

Examples

Thermographic image analysis
The engineer analyzed the thermographic images to find areas of heat loss in the building.
A thermographic scan can reveal hidden electrical problems by showing hot spots.
Veterinarians sometimes use thermographic cameras to detect injuries in animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infrared - relating to or using infrared radiation
  • synonym: thermal - relating to heat
