anti-liberal

adjective
opposed to liberalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
opposed to liberal beliefs, policies, or individuals

Examples

Anti-liberal views
The candidate's anti-liberal rhetoric appealed to a specific segment of the population.
Some media outlets are known for their anti-liberal commentary on social issues.
The organization adopted an anti-liberal approach in its recent policy proposals.
