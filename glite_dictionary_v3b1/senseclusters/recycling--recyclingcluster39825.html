recycling

noun
materials collected for reuse
Pronunciation: / riˈsaɪklɪŋ / (AmE), / riˈsaɪklɪŋ / (BrE)
materials that have been set aside or collected so they can be processed and used again to make new products.

Examples

Household recycling
Please separate your recycling from regular household waste.
Residents are encouraged to take their recycling to the local collection center.
The city collects recycling and trash on different days of the week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: waste - unwanted or unusable materials, especially that which is discarded
