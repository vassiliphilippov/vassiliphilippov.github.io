blackie

noun
nickname for black pet
Pronunciation: / ˈblæki / (AmE), / ˈblæki / (BrE)
a common nickname for a black animal, especially a pet such as a cat or dog

A pet named blackie

The neighbors' cat, blackie, often sleeps on our porch in the afternoon sun.
We used to call every black rabbit we found in the garden blackie.
