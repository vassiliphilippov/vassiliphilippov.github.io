carbolic

adjective
containing phenol
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈbɑlɪk / (AmE), / kɑːˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing phenol, especially as used as a disinfectant

Examples

Carbolic soap
The doctor applied a carbolic lotion to disinfect the patient's wound.
Early hospitals often used carbolic sprays to reduce the risk of infection.
The strong smell of carbolic soap filled the old clinic.
