toiletry

noun
personal care item for washing/grooming
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔɪlətri / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlətri / (BrE)
An object or article used in washing or taking care of one's body, such as soap, shampoo, toothpaste, or deodorant.

Examples

Toiletries bag
I always pack my toiletries in a separate bag when I travel.
She bought new toiletries for her bathroom, including toothpaste and a fresh razor.
The hotel provides complimentary toiletries such as shampoo and soap.
