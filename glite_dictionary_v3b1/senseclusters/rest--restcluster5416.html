rest

noun
what remains after other parts are taken
Pronunciation: / rɛst / (AmE), / rɛst / (BrE)
the part or quantity that is left after the other parts or people have been taken or dealt with

Examples

The rest of the group
Of all the team, James stood out from the rest.
She finished most of her work and left the rest for tomorrow.
You can have the rest of the cake if you like.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance - the amount left over after other parts have been subtracted
  • synonym: remainder - the part left after the other parts have been used or taken away
  • antonym: whole - the entire amount, with nothing removed
