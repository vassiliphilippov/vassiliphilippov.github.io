beef

noun
cattle raised for meat
Pronunciation: / biːf / (AmE), / biːf / (BrE)
Cattle that are bred and raised for their meat.

Examples

Beef cattle farm
Farmers are working to improve the health and productivity of their beef herds.
Grass-fed beef are often preferred for their leaner meat.
The ranch specializes in raising beef for high-quality steaks and roasts.
