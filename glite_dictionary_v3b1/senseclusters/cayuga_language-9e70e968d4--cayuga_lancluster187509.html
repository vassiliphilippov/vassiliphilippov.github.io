Cayuga language

noun
Iroquoian language of the Cayuga people
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈjuːɡə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / kaɪˈjuːɡə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Iroquoian language spoken by the Cayuga people, indigenous to regions of Canada and the United States
