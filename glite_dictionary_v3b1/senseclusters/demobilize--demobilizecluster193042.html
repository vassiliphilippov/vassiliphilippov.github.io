Synonyms

  • demob
    9.1 0.00/m
    demob is the British informal short form soldiers use when leaving service, while demobilize is the standard formal term.
  • muster out
    7.2 0.10/m
    muster out means officially releasing soldiers at the end of their service, while demobilize often refers to releasing forces after a war.
  • discharge
    4.4 4.46/m
    discharge officially frees someone from a duty or service, while demobilize specifically returns soldiers to civilian life, often after war.
