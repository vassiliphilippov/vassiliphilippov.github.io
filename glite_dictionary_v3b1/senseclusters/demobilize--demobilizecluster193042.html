demobilize

verb
release from military service
Pronunciation: / diˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz /; / dɪˈmoʊ.bəˌlaɪz /; / dɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈməʊbɪlaɪz /; / diˈməʊbəlaɪz /; / dɪˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to release someone or soldiers from military service and return them to civilian life, especially after a war
