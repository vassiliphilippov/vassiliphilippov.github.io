honey do list

noun
list of household chores for a partner
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌni duː lɪst / (AmE), / ˈhʌni dʊ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of household tasks assigned by one partner to another, especially for completion at home, often on weekends or free time.

Examples

The honey do list tasks
After a long week at work, he dreaded facing the honey do list on Saturday.
She spent her Saturday morning working through the honey do list her wife had given her.
They added painting the fence to their growing honey do list this summer.
