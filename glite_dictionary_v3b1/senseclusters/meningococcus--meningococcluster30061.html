meningococcus

noun
bacterium causing meningitis
9.3
Pronunciation: / məˌnɪndʒəˈkɑkəs / (AmE), / mɪˌnɪŋɡəˈkɒkəs /; / məˌnɪndʒəˈkɒkəs / (BrE)
a bacterium that can cause meningitis and other serious infections in humans
