molly

noun
small aquarium freshwater fish
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːli / (AmE), / ˈmɒli / (BrE)
A small, livebearing freshwater fish of the genus Poecilia, often kept in home aquariums for its hardiness and colorful varieties.

Examples

A molly fish
Mollies are known for their peaceful temperament and adaptability to different water conditions.
The pet store had several types of molly fish, including black and dalmatian varieties.
We decided to add a bright orange molly to our aquarium.
