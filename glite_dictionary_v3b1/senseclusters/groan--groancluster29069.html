groan

verb
make a low sound expressing pain or displeasure
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊn / (AmE), / ɡrəʊn / (BrE)
to make a low, deep sound showing pain, discomfort, or displeasure

Examples

To groan in pain
He groaned loudly as he struggled to lift the heavy box.
She groaned in pain after twisting her ankle on the stairs.
The children groaned when they saw the amount of homework assigned for the weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moan - to make a low sound expressing pain or unhappiness
