decent

adjective
of satisfactory quality; adequate
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːsənt / (AmE), / ˈdiːsənt / (BrE)
good enough in quality or standard; meeting what is required or expected

Examples

A decent job
I finally found a decent place to stay during my business trip.
She made a decent effort on her homework, but there was still room for improvement.
The team offered him a decent salary for the new position.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adequate - sufficient for a specific requirement; acceptable
  • synonym: satisfactory - fulfilling requirements or expectations
  • antonym: unsatisfactory - not good enough; not meeting requirements
