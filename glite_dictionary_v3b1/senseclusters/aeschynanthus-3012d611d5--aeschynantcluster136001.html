Aeschynanthus

noun
a genus of tropical flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ˌiːskiːˈnænθəs / (AmE), / ˌiːskɪˈnænθəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical Asian plants with bright red or orange flowers and seeds bearing distinctive hairs at both ends.
