overprecise

adjective
excessively exact
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəprɪˈsaɪs / (BrE)
giving or requiring more exactness or detail than is necessary

Examples

An overprecise answer
Her overprecise answers made the simple interview seem much more complicated.
The instructions were so overprecise that they confused everyone trying to follow them.
He tends to be overprecise when describing even the smallest details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail
  • degree lower: precise - marked by exactness and accuracy
  • antonym: vague - not clearly or precisely expressed
