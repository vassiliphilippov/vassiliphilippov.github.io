auric

adjective
relating to an aura or energy field
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˈɔːrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of an aura — an energy field believed to surround a person or object, especially in spiritual, New Age, or holistic contexts.

Examples

Auric field
Practitioners say an auric field surrounds the body with protective energy.
He learned to sense changes in his partner's auric colours during distance healing sessions.
The therapist cleansed the client's auric layer to help balance physical and emotional symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aura - a subtle atmosphere or energy field that some people believe surrounds living beings
  • synonym: energy field - a non-physical field of energy thought by some traditions to surround people or objects
