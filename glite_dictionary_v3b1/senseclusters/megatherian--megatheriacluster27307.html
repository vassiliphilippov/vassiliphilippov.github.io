megatherian

noun
large extinct ground sloth
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛgəˈθɪriən / (AmE), / ˌmɛgəˈθɪəriən / (BrE)
Any of several large, extinct ground sloths of the family Megatheriidae that lived in South America during the Cenozoic era.

Examples

A megatherian sloth
A megatherian could reach the size of a modern elephant, making it one of the largest land mammals of its time.
Paleontologists study megatherian remains to understand their diet and behavior.
The fossilized bones of a megatherian were discovered in a cave in Argentina.
