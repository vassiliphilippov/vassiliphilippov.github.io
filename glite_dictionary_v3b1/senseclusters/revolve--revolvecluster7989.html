revolve

verb
cause to move in a circle or rotate
1.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlv / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlv / (BrE)
to cause something to move in a circular path or to rotate around a central point or axis

Examples

To revolve a wheel
Engineers revolve the wheel to check for balance.
She could revolve the sculpture with a gentle push.
The teacher used a model to revolve the planets around the sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rotate - to move or cause to move in a circle around an axis
  • synonym: spin - to turn around quickly on a central point
