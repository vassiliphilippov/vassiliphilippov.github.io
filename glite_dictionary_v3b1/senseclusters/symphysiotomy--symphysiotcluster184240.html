symphysiotomy

noun
pelvic widening surgery
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɪm.fɪ.siˈɑː.tə.miː/ /; / /sɪm.fɪ.ziˈɑː.tə.miː/ (AmE), /sɪm.fɪ.siˈɒt.ə.miː/ /; / /sɪm.fɪ.ziˈɒt.ə.miː/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the pubic symphysis is divided to widen the pelvis during childbirth

Examples
