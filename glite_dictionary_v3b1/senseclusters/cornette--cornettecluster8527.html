cornette

noun
starched nun's headdress
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈnɛt / (AmE), / kɔːˈnɛt / (BrE)
a headdress with starched, wing-like extensions, formerly worn by some nuns or women in the past

Examples

Nun's cornette
The museum displayed a traditional cornette as part of its exhibit on religious clothing.
She recognized the nurse's distinctive cornette from old photographs.
In the play, the actress wore a white cornette to portray a nun from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: veil - a piece of cloth worn over the head or face, often by women or nuns
  • synonym: wimple - a cloth headdress covering the head and neck, worn by some nuns
