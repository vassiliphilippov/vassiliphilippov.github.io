admonition

noun
firm warning or reprimand
8.0 5.9
Pronunciation: /æd.mə.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /æd.mə.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a warning or piece of advice, especially to correct or prevent something bad

Examples
