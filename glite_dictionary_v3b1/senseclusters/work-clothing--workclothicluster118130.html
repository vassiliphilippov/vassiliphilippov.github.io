work-clothing

noun
clothes for manual or physical work
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːk ˌkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːk ˌkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
Garments or attire worn for performing manual labor, designed to be durable and functional for work activities.

Examples

Work-clothing for labor
After changing into his work-clothing, he started repairing the machinery.
Durable work-clothing is essential for anyone working in construction or agriculture.
The factory provides work-clothing to its employees to ensure their safety and comfort.

Related Terms
