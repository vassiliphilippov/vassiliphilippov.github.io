superfly

noun
successful drug dealer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəflaɪ / (BrE)
(slang) a person who sells illegal drugs, especially one who is influential or successful in the trade

Examples

A superfly dealer
The police suspected that the new superfly in town was behind the recent surge in drug activity.
He lived a flashy lifestyle, earning a reputation as a superfly among his peers.
After years as a small-time dealer, he finally became known as a superfly in the city.

  • antonym: user - a person who consumes illegal drugs
