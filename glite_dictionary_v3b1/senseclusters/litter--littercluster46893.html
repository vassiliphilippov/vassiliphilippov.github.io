litter

noun
offspring born at one time (animals)
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə / (BrE)
a group of young animals born to an animal at one time

Examples

A litter of puppies
Each litter usually contains between four and eight puppies.
The cat gave birth to a litter of five kittens last night.
The sow cared for her litter diligently, keeping her piglets close.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brood - a group of young birds hatched at one time
