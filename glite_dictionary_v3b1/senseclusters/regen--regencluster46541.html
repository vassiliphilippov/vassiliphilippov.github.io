to regrow or restore

Examples

To regen damaged cells

Scientists study how certain animals can regen lost limbs.

Synonyms

    5.3 1.82/m

  • regrow
    3.7 0.41/m
    regrow means to grow again after damage, often for plants or parts, while regen refers to restoring tissue or function.
