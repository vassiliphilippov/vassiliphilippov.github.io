occidentalist

noun
Western culture scholar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌɒksɪˈdɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies or interprets Western culture, especially from a non-Western perspective

Examples

Occidentalist scholar
The occidentalist presented a unique analysis of European art from an Asian perspective.
As an occidentalist, she often compared Western and Eastern philosophies in her research.
The conference invited a renowned occidentalist to discuss Western influence on global literature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orientalist - a person who studies or interprets Eastern cultures
  • synonym: westernist - a person who supports or favors Western ideas or culture
