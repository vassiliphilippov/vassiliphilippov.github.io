murderous

adjective
extremely violent; capable of murder
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdərəs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdərəs / (BrE)
Having a tendency or capability to kill; extremely violent or likely to cause death.

Examples

A murderous look

The criminal gave her a murderous glare as she testified against him.
The film depicts the murderous violence of the gang's confrontations.
