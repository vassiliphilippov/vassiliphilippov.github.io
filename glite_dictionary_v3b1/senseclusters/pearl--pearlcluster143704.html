pearl

verb
to decorate with pearls
Pronunciation: / pɜrld / (AmE), / pɜːld / (BrE)
to decorate or cover something with pearls or make it look like it is covered with pearls.

Examples

To pearl a dress
The artist pearled the edges of the painting to give it a luxurious touch.
Her wedding veil was beautifully pearled with tiny white beads.
They pearl the costumes for the festival every year to make them sparkle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embellish - to decorate or add details to something
