saw log

noun
log large enough to be sawn into lumber
Pronunciation: / sɔ lɔɡ / (AmE), / sɔː lɒɡ / (BrE)
A log of sufficient size and quality to be cut into boards or lumber.

Examples

A saw log
Each saw log was carefully measured to ensure it met the mill's requirements.
It takes a large saw log to produce wide wooden boards for furniture.
The lumberjack rolled the saw log toward the mill for processing.
