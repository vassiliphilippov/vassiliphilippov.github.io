Rousseauist

adjective
of Rousseau's ideas
9.9
Pronunciation: /ruː.ˈsoʊ.ɪst/ /; / /rə.ˈsoʊ.ɪst/ (AmE), /ruː.ˈsəʊ.ɪst/ /; / /rə.ˈsəʊ.ɪst/ (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the political, educational, or philosophical ideas of Jean-Jacques Rousseau

Examples

Rousseauist philosophy
The school's Rousseauist philosophy encourages children to learn through natural experiences.
Her Rousseauist views shaped the curriculum to focus on freedom and self-expression.
Many critics described the movement as Rousseauist in its emphasis on returning to nature.
