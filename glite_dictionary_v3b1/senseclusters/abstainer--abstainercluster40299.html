abstainer

noun
person who refrains from indulgences
7.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈsteɪnɚ / (AmE), / əbˈsteɪnə / (BrE)
A person who deliberately refrains from certain pleasures or indulgences, especially as a spiritual or moral discipline.

Examples

A strict abstainer from sweets
As an abstainer, she refused to eat sweets during Lent.
Many monks are known to be lifelong abstainers from alcohol and rich foods.
The club encouraged its members to be abstainers from all worldly temptations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascetic - a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention
  • antonym: hedonist - a person who pursues pleasure as a goal
