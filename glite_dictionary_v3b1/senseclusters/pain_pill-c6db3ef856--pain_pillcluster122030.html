pain pill

noun
a pill for relieving pain
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪn pɪl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪn pɪl / (BrE)
a pill taken to relieve physical pain; an analgesic in tablet form

Examples

A pain pill tablet

Please do not drive after taking a strong pain pill.
The doctor prescribed a pain pill to help with her back injury.
