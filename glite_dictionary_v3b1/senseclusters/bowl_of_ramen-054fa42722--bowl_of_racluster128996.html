bowl of ramen

noun
serving of Japanese noodle soup
4.1
Pronunciation: / boʊl əv ˈrɑːmən / (AmE), / bəʊl əv ˈrɑːmən / (BrE)
A serving of Japanese noodle soup, typically consisting of wheat noodles, broth, and various toppings such as meat, vegetables, and eggs, served in a bowl.

Examples

A bowl of ramen soup
I ordered a steaming bowl of ramen for lunch at the new restaurant.
Nothing warms me up on a cold night like a hearty bowl of ramen.
She took a picture of the beautifully presented bowl of ramen before eating.
