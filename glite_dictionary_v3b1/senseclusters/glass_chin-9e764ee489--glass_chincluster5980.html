glass chin

noun
vulnerability to being knocked out (sports)
9.2 7.0
Pronunciation: /glæs tʃɪn/ /; / /glɑːs tʃɪn/ (AmE), /glɑːs tʃɪn/ /; / /glæs tʃɪn/ (BrE)
