sack

verb
dismiss from a job
Pronunciation: / sæk / (AmE), / sæk / (BrE)
to dismiss someone from their job; to terminate someone's employment

Examples

To sack an employee
After the mistake, the manager decided to sack him immediately.
Due to budget cuts, several employees were sacked last month.
If you arrive late again, the boss might sack you.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismiss - to remove someone from their job
  • synonym: fire - to dismiss from employment
  • antonym: hire - to employ someone for a job
