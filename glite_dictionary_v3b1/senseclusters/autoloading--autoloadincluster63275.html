Examples

Synonyms

  • self-loading
    7.4 0.04/m

  • semiautomatic
    5.5 0.92/m
    semiautomatic is a firearm that fires one shot per trigger pull and reloads automatically, while autoloading means automatic reloading for continuous or rapid fire.
