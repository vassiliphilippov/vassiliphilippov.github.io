gloss

verb
make shiny or glossy by rubbing
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑs / (AmE), / ɡlɒs / (BrE)
to make a surface shiny or glossy, typically by rubbing or polishing

Examples

To gloss shoes
He glossed his shoes with a soft cloth before the interview.
She carefully glossed the wooden table until it reflected the light.
To achieve a perfect manicure, they gloss each nail after applying the polish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - to make or become less shiny or bright
  • synonym: polish - to make smooth and shiny by rubbing
