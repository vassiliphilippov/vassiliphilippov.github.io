stovepipe hat

noun
tall, cylindrical formal hat
7.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈstoʊv.paɪp hæt/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊv.paɪp hæt/ (BrE)
a tall, cylindrical hat with straight sides, typically worn as formal dress, especially in the 19th century
