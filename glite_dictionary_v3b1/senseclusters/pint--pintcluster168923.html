pint

noun
US liquid measure, 16 fluid ounces
8.6 1.0
Pronunciation: / paɪnt / (AmE), / paɪnt / (BrE)
A unit for measuring liquids in the US, equal to 16 fluid ounces or one eighth of a gallon.

Examples

A pint of milk
A pint is equal to 16 fluid ounces in the US.
I bought a pint of strawberries at the store.
This recipe calls for one pint of milk.
