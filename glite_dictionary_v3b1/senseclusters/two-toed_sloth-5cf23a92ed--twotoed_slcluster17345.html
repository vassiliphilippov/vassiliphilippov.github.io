two-toed sloth

noun
Central/South American sloth with two forefoot claws
Pronunciation: / ˌtuː ˈtoʊd sloʊθ /; / tuː toʊd sloʊθ / (AmE), / ˌtuː ˈtəʊd sləʊθ /; / tuː təʊd slɒθ / (BrE)
a sloth from Central and South America with two long claws on each forefoot and three on each hindfoot
