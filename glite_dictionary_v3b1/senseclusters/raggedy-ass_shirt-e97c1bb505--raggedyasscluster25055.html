raggedy-ass shirt

noun
very worn-out or shabby shirt
8.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈræɡ.ə.diː æs ʃɜːrt/ /; / /ˈræɡ.ɪ.diː æs ʃɜːrt/ (AmE), /ˈræɡ.ɪ.diː æs ʃɜːt/ /; / /ˈræɡ.ə.diː æs ʃɜːt/ (BrE)
A very worn-out or shabby shirt, often used to emphasize how old, torn, or disreputable the shirt appears.

Examples

A raggedy-ass shirt full of holes
Don't show up to the meeting in that raggedy-ass shirt; it's full of holes!

She laughed when she saw the raggedy-ass shirt her brother tried to wear for dinner.
