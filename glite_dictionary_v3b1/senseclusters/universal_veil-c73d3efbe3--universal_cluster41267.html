Examples

The universal veil membrane
In the young Amanita mushroom, the universal veil covers the entire structure until it ruptures as the mushroom matures.
Mushroom hunters are taught to examine traces of the universal veil to help identify poisonous species.
Remnants of the universal veil can often be seen as a volva at the base of some mushroom stems.
