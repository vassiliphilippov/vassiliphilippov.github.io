prepatent

adjective
before detection
9.9
Pronunciation: / priːˈpeɪtənt / (AmE), / priːˈpeɪtənt / (BrE)
describing the period before a disease or parasite becomes detectable by standard tests

Examples

Prepatent period
The infection was still in its prepatent phase, so no symptoms had appeared.
Doctors monitor patients closely during the prepatent period of certain diseases.
A prepatent infection can be difficult to diagnose without advanced testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latent - present but not yet visible or active
  • antonym: patent - clearly visible or detectable
