anthurium

noun
a tropical plant with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / ænˈθjʊriəm / (AmE), / ænˈθjʊəriəm / (BrE)
a tropical American plant of the genus Anthurium, often grown for its brightly colored, showy flowers and decorative foliage

Examples

Bright anthurium flowers
Many florists use anthuriums in arrangements because of their unique heart-shaped flowers.
She gave him an anthurium as a housewarming gift, knowing he loved exotic plants.
The red anthurium brightened up the whole living room with its vivid hue.
