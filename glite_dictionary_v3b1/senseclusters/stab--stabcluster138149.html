stab

noun
sudden, brief sensation or feeling
6.5
Pronunciation: / stæb / (AmE), / stæb / (BrE)
a sudden, brief, and acute feeling or sensation, especially of pain or emotion

Examples

A stab of pain
A stab of fear shot through her when she heard the noise.
Every stab of disappointment made him more determined to succeed.
He felt a sudden stab of guilt as he remembered her words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pang - a sudden sharp feeling of pain or emotion
  • synonym: twinge - a sudden, brief pain or emotion
