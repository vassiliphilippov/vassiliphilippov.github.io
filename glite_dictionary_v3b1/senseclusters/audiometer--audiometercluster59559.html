audiometer

noun
instrument for measuring hearing sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːdiˈɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌɔːdiˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an electronic instrument used to measure a person's hearing sensitivity at different frequencies and intensities

Examples

An audiometer device
An audiometer is commonly found in audiology clinics for hearing assessments.
Modern audiometers can measure a wide range of sound frequencies and intensities.
The doctor used an audiometer to test the child's hearing sensitivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audiometry - the process of measuring hearing function using an audiometer
