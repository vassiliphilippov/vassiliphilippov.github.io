peppery

adjective
tasting or smelling of pepper
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpəri / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəri / (BrE)
Having a strong, pungent, and spicy flavour or smell like that of pepper.

Examples

A peppery taste
His salad dressing was too peppery for my taste.
She enjoys the peppery kick in her soup that comes from fresh black pepper.
The chef prepared a peppery sauce to accompany the grilled chicken.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bland - Lacking strong taste or flavour
  • synonym: spicy - Having a strong, pungent flavour from spices
