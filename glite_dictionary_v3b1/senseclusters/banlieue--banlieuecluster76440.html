banlieue

noun
French city suburb
Pronunciation: / bɑ̃ljø /; / bɑ̃ːlˈjɜː / (AmE), / bɑ̃ljø /; / bɑ̃ːlˈjɜː / (BrE)
a suburb on the outskirts of a French city (sometimes associated with social or economic challenges)

Examples

The banlieue area
Many families choose to live in the banlieue for more space and quieter surroundings.
The train from the banlieue to the city center runs every fifteen minutes.
She grew up in a banlieue just outside of Lyon.
Many families in the banlieue face challenges related to unemployment and limited public services.
The film explores the daily lives of teenagers growing up in a tough banlieue outside Paris.
Protests erupted in the banlieue after a controversial police incident.
