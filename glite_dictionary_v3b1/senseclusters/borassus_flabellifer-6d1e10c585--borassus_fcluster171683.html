Borassus flabellifer

noun
species of tall fan palm tree
9.9
Pronunciation: /bə.ˈræs.əs flə.ˈbel.ɪ.fər/ (AmE), /bə.ˈræs.əs flə.ˈbel.ɪ.fə/ (BrE)
a tall fan palm native to Africa, India, and Malaysia, yielding hard wood, sweet sap used for palm wine and sugar, and leaves for thatching and weaving.
