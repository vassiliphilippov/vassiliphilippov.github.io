unmount

verb
to get off a ride
5.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÊŒnËˆmaÊŠnt / (AmE), / ÊŒnËˆmaÊŠnt / (BrE)
to get off from a horse, bicycle, or other animal or vehicle used for riding

Examples

To unmount a horse
After the long ride she decided to unmount and rest beneath the old oak tree.
He carefully unmounted from his bicycle at the end of the trail.
The children were taught how to safely unmount their ponies after riding lessons.

Synonyms

  • dismount
    5.2 0.45/m
    dismount is the formal act of getting off a horse or bicycle, while unmount is the common verb for that action.
  • get off
    2.5 5.31/m
    get off is a casual phrasal verb for leaving a vehicle or animal, while unmount is used specifically for getting off rideable animals or vehicles.

Related Terms
