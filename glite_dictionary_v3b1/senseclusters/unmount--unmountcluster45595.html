unmount

verb
to get off a ride
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaʊnt / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaʊnt / (BrE)
to get off from a horse, bicycle, or other animal or vehicle used for riding

Examples

To unmount a horse
After the long ride she decided to unmount and rest beneath the old oak tree.
He carefully unmounted from his bicycle at the end of the trail.
The children were taught how to safely unmount their ponies after riding lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismount - to get off from a horse, bicycle, or similar vehicle
  • antonym: mount - to get on a horse, bicycle, or similar vehicle
