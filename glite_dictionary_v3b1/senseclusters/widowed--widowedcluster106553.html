widowed

adjective
having lost a spouse through death
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪdoʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɪd.əʊd / (BrE)
having lost one's spouse through death and not remarried.

Examples

Widowed person
After he was widowed, he moved closer to his children.
Many widowed people find support in groups with others who have similar experiences.
She has been widowed for several years but remains active in her community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: widow - a woman whose spouse has died and who has not remarried
  • synonym: widower - a man whose spouse has died and who has not remarried
