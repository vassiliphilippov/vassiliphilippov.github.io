overwater

adjective
above water location
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈwɔtər / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
located above or built directly over water, especially referring to buildings or structures supported on stilts above a body of water

Examples

Overwater bungalow
They booked an overwater villa for their honeymoon, enjoying direct access to the lagoon.
The resort features several overwater bungalows with stunning views of the ocean.
Guests can watch fish swim beneath their overwater suite through glass panels in the floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: underwater - located or occurring below the surface of water
  • synonym: waterside - situated beside or near water
