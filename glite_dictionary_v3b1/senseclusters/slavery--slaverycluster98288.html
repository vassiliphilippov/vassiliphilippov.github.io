  • wage slavery
    8.1 0.03/m
    wage slavery is work for very low pay and little choice, while slavery covers any harsh forced labour under control.
  • slave labour
    4.4 0.9/m
    slave labour is work forced on people often without pay or threat, while slavery names the full system of such abuse.
  • servitude
    8.9 1/m
