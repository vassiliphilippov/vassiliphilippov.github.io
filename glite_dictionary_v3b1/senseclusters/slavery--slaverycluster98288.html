Examples

Modern slavery at work
He compared his exhausting schedule to slavery, though he knew it wasn't truly the same.
Many factory workers endured slavery for years before labor reforms improved their conditions.

Synonyms

  • enslavement
    6.3 1.47/m
    enslavement is the state of being held as a slave, while slavery is the wider system of harsh, unpaid or low-paid forced work.
  • wage slavery
    8.1 0.07/m
    wage slavery is work for very low pay and little choice, while slavery covers any harsh forced labour under control.
  • slave labour
    4.4 1.07/m
    slave labour is work forced on people often without pay or threat, while slavery names the full system of such abuse.
  • servitude
    8.9 2.11/m
    servitude is being under another's power, sometimes as punishment, while slavery usually means long, forced work and loss of freedom.
  • bondage
    8.6 1.21/m
    bondage is being under control or held, often in a broad or metaphorical sense, while slavery refers to forced, harsh work and ownership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freedom - the state of being free, not under the control or power of another
  • synonym: servitude - the condition of being a servant or subject, often with limited freedom
