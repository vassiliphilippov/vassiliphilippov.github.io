turnip-shaped

adjective
having the shape of a turnip
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.nɪp ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape or form of a turnip; bulbous and rounded, narrowing toward the base.

Examples

Turnip-shaped lantern
Some old lanterns are distinctly turnip-shaped, giving them a unique appearance.
The architect designed a turnip-shaped dome for the new church.
The strange, turnip-shaped root was used in traditional medicine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulbous - rounded and swollen in shape
