Phanerozoic eon

noun
era of abundant life from 540 million years ago to now
Pronunciation: / ˌfænəroʊˈzoʊɪk iːˈɑn /; / ˌfænəroʊˈzoʊɪk ˈiːɒn / (AmE), / ˌfænərəʊˈzəʊɪk iːˈɒn /; / ˌfænərəʊˈzəʊɪk ˈiːɒn / (BrE)
the geological time period from about 540 million years ago to the present, marked by abundant and diverse life forms
