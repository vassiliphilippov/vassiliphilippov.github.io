frontlet

noun
ornamental band for the forehead
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntlət / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntlɪt / (BrE)
an ornamental band, jewel, or other adornment worn on the forehead, especially as part of traditional costume or ceremonial dress

Examples

A jeweled frontlet
In medieval times, a jeweled frontlet was often a sign of nobility.
She fastened the silver frontlet in place before the festival ceremony.
The dancer wore a delicate frontlet adorned with small pearls across her forehead.
