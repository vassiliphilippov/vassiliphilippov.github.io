salvation

noun
being saved from sin or evil
Pronunciation: / sælˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / sælˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
(Theology) The act of being saved from sin or its consequences, especially in a religious context.

Examples

Religious salvation
Many Christians believe that salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ.
She devoted her life to helping others find spiritual salvation.
The concept of salvation is central to most world religions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: redemption - the action of saving or being saved from sin, error, or evil
