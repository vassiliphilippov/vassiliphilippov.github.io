swamp milkweed

noun
a wetland perennial plant with colorful flowers
7.0 6.8
Pronunciation: / swɑmp ˈmɪlkˌwid / (AmE), / swɒmp ˈmɪlkˌwiːd / (BrE)
A perennial plant native to eastern North America, with dense branches and clusters of white to crimson or purple flowers, typically found in wetlands.
