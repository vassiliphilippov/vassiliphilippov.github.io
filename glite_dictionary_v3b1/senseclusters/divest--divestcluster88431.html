Synonyms

  • disinvest
    8.0 0.10/m
    disinvest means withdrawing or reducing investment, while divest means selling off assets or parts of a business to stop holding them.
  • liquidate
    7.3 2/m
    liquidate is selling assets to pay debts or close a company, while divest is a planned sale of investments or business parts.
  • sell off
    3.5 3/m
    sell off means selling quickly, often at low prices, while divest means formally getting rid of an asset or business part.
  • dispose
    3.4 8/m
    dispose usually means getting rid of something by throwing away or giving it away, while divest focuses on selling investments or assets.
