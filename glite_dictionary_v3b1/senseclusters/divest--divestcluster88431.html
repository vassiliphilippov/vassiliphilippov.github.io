divest

verb
to sell off or dispose of an investment
5.5 6.0
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈvest/ /; / /daɪ.ˈvest/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈvest/ /; / /daɪ.ˈvest/ (BrE)
to sell off or dispose of an asset, investment, or part of a business; to cease to hold an investment

Examples

To divest assets
After the merger, the company was required to divest some of its assets to comply with regulations.
She chose to divest her holdings in the tech sector before the market declined.
The organization decided to divest from fossil fuel companies in response to public pressure.
