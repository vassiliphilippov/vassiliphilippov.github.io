minutia

noun
fingerprint feature used for identification
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːʃiə / (AmE), / mɪˈnjuːʃɪə / (BrE)
In biometrics and forensics, any of the distinct features of a fingerprint that allow different prints to be compared.
