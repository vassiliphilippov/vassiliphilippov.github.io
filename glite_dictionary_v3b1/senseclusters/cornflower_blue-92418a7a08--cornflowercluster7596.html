cornflower blue

noun
a shade of medium-to-light blue
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌflaʊɚ blu / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌflaʊə bluː / (BrE)
a shade of medium-to-light blue, similar to the color of a cornflower, containing little green
