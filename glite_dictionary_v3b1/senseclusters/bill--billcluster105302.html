bill

verb
to send a request for payment
1.1
Pronunciation: / bɪl / (AmE), / bɪl / (BrE)
to send someone a request for payment for goods supplied or services rendered

Examples

To bill a customer
Please check your invoice to ensure you were billed correctly for all items.
The hotel will bill you for any additional services you use during your stay.
We were accidentally billed twice for the same meal at the restaurant.

Related Terms
