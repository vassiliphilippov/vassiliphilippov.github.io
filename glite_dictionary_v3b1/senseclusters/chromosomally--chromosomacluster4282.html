chromosomally

adverb
relating to chromosomes
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊməˈsoʊməli / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊməˈsəʊməli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or involves chromosomes

Examples

To be chromosomally inherited
The disorder is chromosomally inherited and can be detected through genetic testing.
Some diseases are chromosomally determined rather than caused by environmental factors.
The scientist explained how traits are passed on chromosomally from parents to children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetically - in a way that relates to genes or heredity
