bilaterally

adverb
involving two sides or parts
4.2 6.1
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlæt̬.ɚ.əl.i / (AmE), / bɪˈlæt.ər.əl.i / (BrE)
Involving or affecting two sides; in a way that concerns both sides or parts.

Examples

Bilaterally affected limbs
Patients often experience symptoms bilaterally, affecting both arms or both legs at the same time.
The agreement was reached bilaterally, with both countries participating equally in the negotiations.
The treaty must be accepted bilaterally to become effective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unilaterally - done by or affecting only one side
