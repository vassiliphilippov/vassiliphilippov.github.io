blue flag

noun
a blue-flowered iris native to eastern North America
Pronunciation: / bluː flæɡ / (AmE), / bluː flaɡ / (BrE)
a common iris of eastern North America with blue or blue-violet flowers; its root was formerly used medicinally

Examples

The blue flag flower
Botanists studied the medicinal properties of the blue flag root in the nineteenth century.
The blue flag blooms along streams and wet meadows from late spring to early summer.
We spotted several blue flags growing by the edge of the pond during our hike.
