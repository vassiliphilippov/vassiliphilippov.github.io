TOB

noun
best bid and ask prices
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ti o ä bi / (AmE), / tiÀê …ô ä biÀê / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'top of book', referring to the best available bid and ask prices in a financial market order book

Examples

TOB price check
The analyst checked the TOB before placing a large order on the exchange.
High-frequency traders monitor the TOB to react quickly to market changes.
A sudden shift in the TOB can indicate increased market activity.
