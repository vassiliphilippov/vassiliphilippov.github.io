remodeler

noun
protein altering chromatin
Pronunciation: / riˈmɑːdələr / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒdələ / (BrE)
a protein or molecular complex that changes the structure of chromatin or other cellular components, affecting gene expression

Examples

A chromatin remodeler
Scientists identified a new remodeler that plays a key role in gene regulation.
The malfunction of a chromatin remodeler can lead to various genetic diseases.
Researchers are studying how each remodeler affects the accessibility of DNA in the cell nucleus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chromatin remodeler - a protein complex that modifies chromatin structure
  • synonym: enzyme - a protein that speeds up chemical reactions in the body
