decentralist

noun
supporter of decentralization
7.3
Pronunciation: /diː.ˈsen.trəl.ɪst/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsen.trəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /diː.ˈsen.trəl.ɪst/ /; / /dɪ.ˈsen.trəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports the distribution of power or authority away from a central organization or government

Examples

A decentralist leader
The decentralist movement gained popularity as communities demanded more local decision-making.
As a proud decentralist, she advocated for regional governments to have greater autonomy.
Many decentralists believe that local control leads to more effective policies.
