arrival

noun
a person who has just arrived
4.6
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪvəl / (AmE), / əˈraɪvəl / (BrE)
a person who arrives at a place, especially one who has just come to join a group or community

Examples

A new arrival
As a recent arrival, she still felt unfamiliar with the local customs.
At the airport, friends greeted each arrival with flowers and smiles.
The party was excited to meet the latest arrival from abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newcomer - a person who has recently arrived in a place or group
  • antonym: old-timer - a person who has been in a place or group for a long time
