cap

verb
form the top or covering of something
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kæp / (BrE)
to lie at the top of or to form a covering or topping for something

Examples

To cap a tower
A golden dome caps the ancient temple, making it visible from miles away.
A layer of fog capped the city skyline at dawn.
The mountain peak was capped with glistening snow.
