population profile

noun
chart showing population by age
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃən ˈproʊfaɪl / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən ˈprəʊfaɪl / (BrE)
a chart that displays the distribution of a population according to age, often showing numbers or percentages by age groups

Examples

A population profile chart
Changes in birth rates can significantly alter the shape of a nation's population profile over time.
Demographers often use a population profile to analyze age distribution and plan for future needs.
The country's population profile revealed a large proportion of young people compared to the elderly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: population pyramid - a graphical illustration that shows the distribution of various age groups in a population, forming the shape of a pyramid
