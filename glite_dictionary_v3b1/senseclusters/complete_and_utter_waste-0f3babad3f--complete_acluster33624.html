complete and utter waste

noun
something completely pointless; no benefit
2.0
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplit ənd ˈʌtər weɪst / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt ənd ˈʌtə weɪst / (BrE)
something that is entirely pointless or provides no benefit whatsoever

Examples

A complete and utter waste of money
He thought the movie was a complete and utter waste of money.
Spending hours arguing over trivia is a complete and utter waste of time.
The meeting turned out to be a complete and utter waste of everyone's afternoon.
