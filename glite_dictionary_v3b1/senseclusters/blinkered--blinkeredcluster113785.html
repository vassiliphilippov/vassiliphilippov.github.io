blinkered

adjective
wearing side blinders
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈblɪŋ.kərd/ (AmE), /ˈblɪŋ.kəd/ (BrE)
wearing blinkers that limit side vision, especially referring to horses with restricted physical sight to the sides

Examples

Blinkered horse
The blinkered horse could not see anything except the path ahead.
Jockeys often use blinkered horses to help them focus during races.
Because it was blinkered, the animal was less distracted by the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blinded - unable to see because of a physical obstruction
