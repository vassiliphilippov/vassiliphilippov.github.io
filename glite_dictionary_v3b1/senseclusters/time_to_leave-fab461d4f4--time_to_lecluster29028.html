time to leave

noun
moment to depart
Pronunciation: / taɪm tə liv / (AmE), / taɪm tə liːv / (BrE)
the moment when it is appropriate or necessary to depart from a place or situation

Examples

The time to leave comes
As soon as the meeting ended, everyone agreed it was time to leave.
She checked her watch and realized it was time to leave for the airport.
When the lights started to dim, they knew it was time to leave the theater.

Related Terms

  • antonym: arrival - the act of coming to a place
  • synonym: departure - the act of leaving a place
