sewing machine

noun
machine for stitching fabric or materials
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊɪŋ məˌʃin / (AmE), / ˈsəʊɪŋ məˌʃiːn / (BrE)
A machine used for stitching fabric, leather, or other materials together with thread, especially as a household appliance or for garment-making.

Examples

Modern sewing machines
Modern sewing machines offer a variety of automatic stitching options.
She learned how to use the sewing machine to make her own clothes.
The old sewing machine in the attic still works perfectly.
