rit.

adjective
music: gradual decrease in tempo
Pronunciation: / ɹɪt / (AmE), / ɹɪt / (BrE)
(music) An abbreviation for 'ritardando', indicating a gradual decrease in tempo.

Examples

A rit. marking
Many classical pieces end with a rit. to slow the music dramatically.
The conductor signaled the orchestra to play rit. before the final chord.
The pianist noticed the rit. marking and gradually reduced her speed.
