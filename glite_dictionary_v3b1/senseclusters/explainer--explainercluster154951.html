explainer

noun
person or thing that explains
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspleɪnər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspleɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something clear or easy to understand, especially through explanation or demonstration

Examples

A helpful explainer
The animated explainer helped everyone understand the new policy.
She watched a short explainer video to learn how the app works.
A good explainer can make even complex topics easy to grasp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstrator - a person who shows how something works or is done
  • synonym: instructor - a person who teaches or explains something
