distributorship

noun
authorized seller business
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪbjətərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjʊtəʃɪp / (BrE)
a business or the position of being an authorized distributor for a supplier

Examples

An authorized distributorship
After years of negotiation, he finally secured a distributorship for the popular electronics brand.
The company expanded its distributorship network to reach more customers across the country.
Owning a distributorship can be profitable if you choose the right products to sell.
She acquired a distributorship for several popular electronics brands in her region.
The company expanded its distributorship network to cover more cities across the country.
Losing the distributorship meant they could no longer supply those products to local stores.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dealership - a business authorized to sell products, especially vehicles
  • synonym: distribution - the act of delivering or spreading products to various locations
  • synonym: franchise - a business licensed to operate under a brand and sell its products
  • antonym: manufacturer - a company that makes products, often supplying them to distributors
  • antonym: retail - the sale of goods to the public in small quantities
  • synonym: retailer - a business that sells goods directly to consumers
