stabilize

verb
make stable; prevent changes
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪbəˌlaɪz /; / ˈsteɪ.bəˌlaɪz /; / ˈsteɪ.bə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈsteɪbəlaɪz /; / ˈsteɪ.bɪ.laɪz / (BrE)
to make something steady or stop it from changing, moving, or falling
