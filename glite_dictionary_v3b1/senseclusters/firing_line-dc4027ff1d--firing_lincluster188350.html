firing line

noun
critical or leading position in an activity
9.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.rɪŋ laɪn/ /; / /ˈfaɪ.ə.rɪŋ laɪn/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.rɪŋ laɪn/ /; / /ˈfaɪ.ə.rɪŋ laɪn/ (BrE)
a position where someone is the focus of attention, pressure, or criticism, especially in a difficult situation

Examples
