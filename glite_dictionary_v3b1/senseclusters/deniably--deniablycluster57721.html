deniably

adverb
in a disputable way
7.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈnaɪəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈnaɪəbli / (BrE)
in a manner that can be denied or disputed as not true or not certain

Examples

To deny clearly
The company's involvement in the scandal was deniably unclear to the public.
His actions were deniably suspicious, but there was no concrete proof.
The report was deniably incomplete, leaving many questions unanswered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument
  • antonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be denied or disputed
