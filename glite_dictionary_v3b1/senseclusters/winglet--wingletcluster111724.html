winglet

noun
small wing projection
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋlət / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋlɪt / (BrE)
a small wing or wing-like projection, especially on an animal, plant, or young bird

Examples

A bird's winglet
The young bird stretched its tiny winglets as it learned to balance.
Some insects have small winglets that help them glide through the air.
The plant's seeds are equipped with winglets to aid in wind dispersal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appendage - a projecting part of an animal or plant body
  • degree higher: wing - a limb or structure used for flying
