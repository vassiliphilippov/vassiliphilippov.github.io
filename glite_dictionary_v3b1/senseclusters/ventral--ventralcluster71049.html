ventral

adjective
on or near the belly/front side
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˈvɛntrəl / (BrE)
relating to or located on the belly, underside, or front/lower part of the body (especially in animals and anatomy)

Examples

Ventral body side
In comparative anatomy, scientists use the term ventral to describe features on the underside of animals.
In humans, the navel is located on the ventral surface of the body.
The fish has a lighter color on its ventral side to help it blend with the sunlight above.
Certain injuries can damage the ventral roots of spinal nerves, affecting muscle movement.
Examination of the ventral side of the leaf revealed tiny hairs.
The ventral surface of the fish is usually lighter in color than its dorsal side.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dorsal - relating to the back or upper side
