old-womanish

adjective
excessively prim or fussily fastidious
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊldˈwʊmənɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊldˈwʊmənɪʃ / (BrE)
Excessively prim or fussily fastidious, in a way stereotypically associated with an old woman.

Examples

An old-womanish fuss
Don't be so old-womanish about keeping the kitchen spotless.
His old-womanish concern with manners made him unpopular with the students.
She has an old-womanish tendency to straighten everything on her desk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fastidious - very attentive to detail; difficult to please
  • synonym: fussy - excessively concerned with details; hard to please
