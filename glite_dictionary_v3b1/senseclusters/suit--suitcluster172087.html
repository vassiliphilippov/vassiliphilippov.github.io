suit

noun
businessman, esp. corporate or conventional type (slang)
Pronunciation: / sut / (AmE), / suːt / (BrE)
(slang) A businessman, especially one who is seen as conventional, corporate, or lacking individuality, typically dressed in a business suit.

Examples

Corporate suit
She always felt uncomfortable around the suits when she pitched new ideas.
The suits in the boardroom made all the final decisions without consulting the creative team.
They blamed the recent policy changes on pressure from the suits upstairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creative - a person who works in a creative profession, especially advertising; often contrasted with 'suit'
  • synonym: executive - a person in a high position in a company or organization
  • synonym: manager - a person who manages an organization or part of one
