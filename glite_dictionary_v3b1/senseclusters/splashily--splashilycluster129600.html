splashily

adverb
in a flashy way
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃɪli / (BrE)
in a showy, flashy, or attention-grabbing manner, often with bold or striking effects

Examples

Splashily decorated room
The designer decorated the room splashily, using bright patterns and bold furniture.
She dressed splashily for the party, wearing a sparkling red dress and large earrings.
The advertisement was created splashily to catch the attention of everyone passing by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashily - in a way that is bright, showy, or meant to attract attention
  • antonym: plainly - in a simple or unadorned manner
