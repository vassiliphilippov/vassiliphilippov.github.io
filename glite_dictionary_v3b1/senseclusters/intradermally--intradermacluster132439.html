intradermally

adverb
injected or applied into the skin
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈdɝː.məli / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈdɜː.məli / (BrE)
in a way that is injected or applied into the skin, rather than beneath or below it

Examples

Injected intradermally
For allergy testing, substances are often injected intradermally.
The medication is designed to be delivered intradermally for faster absorption.
The vaccine was administered intradermally rather than into the muscle.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subcutaneously - in a way that is injected or applied under the skin
