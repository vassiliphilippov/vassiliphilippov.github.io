postdoctorate

noun
doctoral researcher
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈdɑktərət / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈdɒktərət / (BrE)
a person who has completed a doctoral degree and is engaged in further academic or research work, usually on a temporary appointment.

Examples

A postdoctorate at university
After earning her PhD, she became a postdoctorate at a leading cancer research institute.
The university offers several fellowships for postdoctorates in the sciences.
As a postdoctorate, he published several influential papers in his field.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doctoral student - a person studying for a doctoral degree
  • synonym: postdoc - a person doing postdoctoral research
