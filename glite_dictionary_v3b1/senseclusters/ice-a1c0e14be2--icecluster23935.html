ICE

noun
internal combustion engine
6.5 3.93/m
Pronunciation: / a…™ si i / (AmE), / a…™ siÀê iÀê / (BrE)
An internal combustion engine; a heat engine in which fuel combustion occurs within the engine, producing expanding gases that move a piston or turn a turbine.

Examples

Car ICE
Most cars on the road still use an ICE rather than an electric motor.
New regulations aim to limit emissions produced by ICE cars.
The transition from ICE vehicles to electric vehicles is accelerating worldwide.

Synonyms

  • synonym: engine - machine that converts power into motion
