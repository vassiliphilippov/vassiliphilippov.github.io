pharmaceutically

adverb
in a drug-related way
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪkli /; / ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to pharmaceuticals, drugs, or medicines (including their preparation, use, manufacture, or effects)

This compound is pharmaceutically important for treating certain infections.
The drug must be pharmaceutically approved before it can be sold to the public.
