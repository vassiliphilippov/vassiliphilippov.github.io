TACAN

noun
aircraft navigation system
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæk.æn/ (AmE), /ˈtæk.æn/ (BrE)
a radio navigation system used by military aircraft to determine position, bearing, and distance from a ground or ship-based station
