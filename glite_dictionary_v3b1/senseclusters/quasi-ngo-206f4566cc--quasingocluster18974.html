quasi-NGO

noun
government-funded but independent organization
7.8 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkweɪ.zaɪ en.dʒiː.ˈoʊ/ /; / /ˈkweɪ.zaɪ ən.dʒiː.ˈoʊ/ /; / /ˈkwɑː.zi ən.dʒiː.ˈoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈkweɪ.zaɪ en.dʒiː.ˈəʊ/ /; / /ˈkweɪ.zaɪ ən.dʒiː.ˈəʊ/ /; / /ˈkwɑː.zi en.dʒiː.ˈəʊ/ (BrE)
An organization that is funded by the government but operates independently of it, resembling a non-governmental organization (NGO).

Examples

Several quasi-NGOs
Several quasi-NGOs played a crucial role in the disaster relief effort.
Some experts question whether a quasi-NGO can truly advocate against its funding government.
The new environmental group is a quasi-NGO, receiving government grants while maintaining operational independence.
