tubulation

noun
tubular structure
Pronunciation: / ˌtuːbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtjuːbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a tube or small tubular passage or structure, especially within a biological organ or part of a device

Examples

Kidney tubulation
The tubulation in the kidney helps filter waste from the blood.
Engineers designed a new tubulation system to improve fluid flow in the device.
Damage to the tubulation of the organ can affect its normal function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canal - a tubular passage or channel in the body
  • synonym: duct - a tube or channel in the body or a device for conveying fluids
