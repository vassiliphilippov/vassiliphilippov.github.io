talent scout

noun
someone who recruits talented people
6.3 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtælənt skaʊt / (AmE), / ˈtælənt skaʊt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to find and recruit talented people, especially in entertainment or sports

Examples

Talent scout finds players
As a talent scout, his job is to discover promising young athletes for the national team.
Many famous actors were first noticed by a talent scout early in their careers.
The talent scout offered her a contract after seeing her perform at the local theater.
