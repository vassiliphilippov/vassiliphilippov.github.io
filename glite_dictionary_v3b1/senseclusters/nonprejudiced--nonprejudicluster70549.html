nonprejudiced

adjective
without bias
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈprɛdʒədɪst / (AmE), / nɒnˈprɛdʒədɪst / (BrE)
not having or showing an unfair dislike, bias, or prejudice toward others

Examples

A nonprejudiced attitude
Her nonprejudiced approach made everyone feel welcome in the group.
A nonprejudiced judge is essential for a fair trial.
He was praised for his nonprejudiced views on cultural differences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impartial - treating all people and groups equally
  • antonym: prejudiced - having or showing an unfair dislike or bias
  • synonym: unbiased - not influenced by personal feelings or opinions
