kilted

verb
to gather into pleats
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪl.tɪd/ /; / /ˈkɪl.təd/ (AmE), /ˈkɪl.tɪd/ /; / /ˈkɪl.təd/ (BrE)
to tuck up, hitch, or gather a skirt or garment into pleats so that it resembles a kilt.
