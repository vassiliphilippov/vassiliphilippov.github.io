Douay Version

noun
English Catholic translation of the Vulgate Bible
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌduː.eɪ ˈvɜr.ʒən / (AmE), / ˌduː.eɪ ˈvɜː.ʒən / (BrE)
an English translation of the Latin Vulgate Bible, produced by Roman Catholic scholars at Douay and Rheims in the late 16th and early 17th centuries
