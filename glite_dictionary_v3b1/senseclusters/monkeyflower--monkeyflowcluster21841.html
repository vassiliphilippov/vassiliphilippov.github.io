monkeyflower

noun
flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkiˌflaʊər / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkiˌflaʊə / (BrE)
a plant with brightly colored flowers, some of which resemble a monkey's face, found in various regions

Examples

Bright monkeyflower plant
The monkeyflower added a splash of color to the edge of the pond.
Hikers admired the wild monkeyflower growing along the mountain trail.
She planted a monkeyflower in her garden to attract bees and butterflies.
