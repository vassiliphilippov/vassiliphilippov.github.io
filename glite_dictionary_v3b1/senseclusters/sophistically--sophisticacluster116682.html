sophistically

adverb
in a clever manner
9.7
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈfɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /sə.ˈfɪs.tɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /sə.ˈfɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /sə.ˈfɪs.tɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a manner that is sophisticated, clever, or subtly argued, often showing complex reasoning or style

She sophistically defended her position, using complex arguments to persuade the audience.
The report was sophistically written, impressing the committee with its depth and clarity.
