suprascapular

adjective
above the shoulder blade
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈskæpjələr / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈskæpjʊlə / (BrE)
situated above or relating to the area above the scapula (shoulder blade)

Examples

Suprascapular region
The suprascapular nerve can sometimes be compressed in athletes.
An injury to the suprascapular region may affect shoulder movement.
Doctors examined the suprascapular area for signs of inflammation.
