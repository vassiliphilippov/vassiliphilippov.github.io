outgive

verb
to give more
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈɡɪv / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈɡɪv / (BrE)

Examples

To outgive others

No matter how much he tried, he could not outgive his generous friend.
The two companies competed to outgive each other in their charitable donations.
