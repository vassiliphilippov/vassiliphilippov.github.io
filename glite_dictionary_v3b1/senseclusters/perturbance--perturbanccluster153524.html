perturbance

noun
small disturbance
9.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàt…úrb…ôns / (AmE), / p…ôÀàt…úÀêb…ôns / (BrE)
a small change or disturbance in the motion of a celestial body or physical system caused by an external force

Examples

Orbital perturbance
The astronomer noticed a slight perturbance in the planet's orbit due to a nearby asteroid.
Engineers must account for every perturbance when designing stable satellites.
A sudden perturbance in the system caused the experiment to yield unexpected results.

Synonyms
