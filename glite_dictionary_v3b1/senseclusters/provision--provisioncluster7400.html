provision

noun
planning for future needs or contingencies
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
the action of preparing or planning for future needs or possible events

Examples

Make provision for emergencies
Before traveling abroad, it is wise to make provision for health insurance.
Good leaders always make provision for unexpected emergencies.
She made careful provision for her children's education in her will.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neglect - failure to care for or anticipate
  • synonym: preparation - the action or process of making ready or being made ready for use
