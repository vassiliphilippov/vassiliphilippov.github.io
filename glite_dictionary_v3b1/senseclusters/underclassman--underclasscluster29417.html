underclassman

noun
student not yet a senior
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈklæsmən / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈklɑːsmən / (BrE)
a student in a school or college who is not yet a senior, typically a freshman or sophomore

Examples

An underclassman student
As an underclassman, he looked up to the older students on the football team.
Many dormitories are reserved for underclassmen during their first two years of college.
The orientation program is designed especially for underclassmen to help them adjust to campus life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: upperclassman - a student in a school or college who is a junior or senior
