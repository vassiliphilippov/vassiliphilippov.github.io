military posture

noun
state of a nation's military strength and readiness
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˈpɑstʃər / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˈpɒstʃə / (BrE)
the overall state of a country's armed forces in terms of strength, readiness, and capability to engage in warfare

Examples

The military posture of a country
After years of budget cuts, critics argued that the nation's military posture had weakened significantly.
Debates in Congress focused on whether to increase the country's military posture.
The government's decision improved the nation's military posture relative to its neighbors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: military readiness - the preparedness of military forces for action
  • synonym: military strength - the physical power and resources of a country's armed forces
