Makua

proper noun
ethnic group or language
Pronunciation: / məˈkuːə / (AmE), / məˈkuːə / (BrE)
a member of a Bantu-speaking ethnic group of Mozambique and Tanzania, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Makua people
The Makua have a long history of farming and trade in northern Mozambique.
She is studying the Makua language as part of her research on African linguistics.
Many Makua communities celebrate traditional festivals with music and dance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Yao - a Bantu-speaking ethnic group in southeastern Africa
