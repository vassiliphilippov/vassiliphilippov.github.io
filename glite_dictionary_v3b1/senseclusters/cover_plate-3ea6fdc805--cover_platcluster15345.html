cover plate

noun
plate used to cover an opening
2.1 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌ.vər pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.və pleɪt/ (BrE)
a flat, usually rigid plate used to cover or close off an opening in a chamber, receptacle, or piece of equipment

Examples

Metal cover plate
Make sure the cover plate is securely fastened to prevent dust from entering the machine.
The electrician replaced the broken cover plate on the light switch.
The metal cover plate protect the inner workings of the engine from debris.
