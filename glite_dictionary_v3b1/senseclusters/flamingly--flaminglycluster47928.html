flamingly

adverb
with visible flames
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈfleɪmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is burning with flames or appears to be on fire

Examples

Flamingly burning logs
The dry leaves caught fire and burned flamingly in the autumn breeze.
The torch was held high, shining flamingly in the darkness of the cave.
Logs in the fireplace glowed flamingly as the family gathered around for warmth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blazingly - in a way that burns very brightly or intensely
