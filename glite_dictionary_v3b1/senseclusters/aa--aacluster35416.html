aa

noun
rough, jagged lava type
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː ˌɑː / (AmE), / ˈɑː ˌɑː / (BrE)
A type of basaltic lava with a rough, broken surface of jagged, clinkery fragments.

Examples

Rough aa lava
Geologists identified the field as being primarily composed of aa lava rather than pahoehoe.
The volcano produced aa flows that covered the landscape with jagged rocks.
Walking on aa is difficult because of its sharp, irregular surface.
