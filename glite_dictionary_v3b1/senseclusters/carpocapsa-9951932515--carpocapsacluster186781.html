Carpocapsa

noun
former genus name for codling moths
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑːrpəˈkæpsə /; / ˌkɑːr.pəˈkæp.sə / (AmE), / kɑːpəˈkæpsə /; / ˌkɑː.pəˈkæp.sə / (BrE)
a former genus name for codling moths (now usually called Cydia), whose larvae are pests of apples and other fruit
