enrhythmic

adjective
synchronized rhythm
9.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ /; / /ən.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ /; / /ən.ˈrɪð.mɪk/ (BrE)
