enrhythmic

adjective
synchronized rhythm
9.2
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈrɪðmɪk / (AmE), / ɛnˈrɪðmɪk / (BrE)
having or showing rhythm, especially by matching or coordinating with a beat or rhythmic pattern (especially in music or movement)
