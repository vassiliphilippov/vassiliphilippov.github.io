Examples

-ish in 'English'
The word 'English' uses the suffix -ish to show both the language and the nationality.
Adding -ish to 'Spain' creates 'Spanish', referring to the people or language of Spain.
Many country names in English form adjectives by adding -ish, such as 'Danish' or 'Swedish'.
