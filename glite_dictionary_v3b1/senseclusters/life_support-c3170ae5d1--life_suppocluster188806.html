life support

noun
equipment that keeps people alive in deadly environments
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔːt / (BrE)
equipment or systems that keep a person alive in a dangerous or otherwise deadly environment, especially by providing air, water, or other vital needs.

Examples

Life support system in space
After the accident, the patient was kept alive by life support.
Modern submarines are equipped with advanced life support systems.
The crew depended on life support during their mission to Mars.
