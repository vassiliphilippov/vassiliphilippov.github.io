dunk

noun
downward basketball shot into the basket
Pronunciation: / dʌŋk / (AmE), / dʌŋk / (BrE)
A shot in basketball in which the ball is thrust downwards into the basket with one or both hands above the rim.

Examples

A slam dunk
He wowed the crowd with a spectacular dunk in the final seconds of the game.
Her first dunk during practice amazed her teammates.
The highlight reel featured an impressive dunk over two defenders.
