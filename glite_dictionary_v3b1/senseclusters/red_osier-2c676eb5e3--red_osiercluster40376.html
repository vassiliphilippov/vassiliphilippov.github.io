red osier

noun
a willow with reddish twigs and tannin-rich bark
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈoʊʒər / (AmE), / rɛd ˈəʊziə / (BrE)
A Eurasian willow with reddish or purple twigs and bark high in tannin, often used in basketry.

Examples

Red osier basket
Many baskets were woven from the supple branches of the red osier.
The bright stems of the red osier stood out against the winter snow.
The park's wetland restoration project included planting hundreds of red osier shrubs.
