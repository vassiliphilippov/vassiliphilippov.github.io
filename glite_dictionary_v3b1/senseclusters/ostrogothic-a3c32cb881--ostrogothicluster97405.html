Ostrogothic

adjective
of the Ostrogoths
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstrəˈɡɑθɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstrəˈɡɒθɪk / (BrE)
relating to the Ostrogoths, an ancient East Germanic people or their kingdom

Examples

Ostrogothic kingdom
The Ostrogothic kingdom was established in Italy after the fall of the Western Roman Empire.
Archaeologists discovered Ostrogothic artifacts during the excavation of the ancient site.
The Ostrogothic rulers played a significant role in shaping early medieval European history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gothic - relating to the Goths or their language
  • synonym: Visigothic - relating to the Visigoths, a branch of the Goths
