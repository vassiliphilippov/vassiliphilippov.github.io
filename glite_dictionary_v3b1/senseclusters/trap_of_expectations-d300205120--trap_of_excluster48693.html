trap of expectations

noun
pressure caused by others' expectations
Pronunciation: / træp ʌv ˌɛk.spɛkˈteɪ.ʃənz / (AmE), / træp əv ˌɛk.spɛkˈteɪ.ʃənz / (BrE)
a situation in which a person feels confined or pressured by the expectations of others or society

Examples

The trap of expectations pressure
Artists often feel stifled by the trap of expectations placed upon them by their audience.
Many students experience the trap of expectations when trying to meet their parents' goals.
She struggled for years to break free from the trap of expectations imposed by her community.
