modularly

adverb
using modular arithmetic
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdʒələrli / (AmE), / ˈmɒdjʊləli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or uses modular arithmetic or reductions modulo a number.

Examples

Modularly reduce numbers
The cryptographic algorithm processes data modularly to ensure security and efficiency.
In number theory, many problems are solved modularly by considering remainders after division.
He checked the results modularly, reducing each value by a prime number to simplify the calculations.
