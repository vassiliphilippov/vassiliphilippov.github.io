slipware

noun
pottery with slip decoration
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌweə / (BrE)
pottery that is decorated with slip, a liquid mixture of clay and water applied before firing

Examples

Slipware pottery
The artisan created beautiful slipware bowls with intricate patterns for the exhibition.
Many collectors value antique slipware for its unique decorative techniques.
She learned how to make slipware during her pottery class at the community center.
