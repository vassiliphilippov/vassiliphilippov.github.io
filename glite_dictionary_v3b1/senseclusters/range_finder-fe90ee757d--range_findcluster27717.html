range finder

noun
device for measuring distance to an object
6.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /reɪndʒ ˈfaɪn.dər/ (AmE), /reɪndʒ ˈfaɪn.də/ (BrE)
a device that measures the distance from the observer to a target, typically using acoustic, optical, or electronic means

Examples

A range finder device
A laser range finder can quickly measure the distance to faraway objects with high accuracy.
Modern golfers often carry a range finder to determine how far they are from the hole.
The photographer used a range finder to ensure the subject was perfectly in focus.
