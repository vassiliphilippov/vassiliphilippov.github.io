tolerationist

noun
supporter of tolerance
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːləˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌtɒləˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates for religious or social tolerance

Examples

A tolerationist leader
As a proud tolerationist, he welcomed people of all backgrounds into his community.
The debate included a well-known tolerationist who argued for greater acceptance of religious diversity.
Many tolerationists have played important roles in promoting peaceful coexistence throughout history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bigot - a person who is intolerant toward those with different opinions
  • synonym: liberal - a person open to different views and tolerant of others
