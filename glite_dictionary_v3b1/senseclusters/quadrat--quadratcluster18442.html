quadrat

noun
printing space block
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːdrət / (AmE), / ˈkwædrət / (BrE)
a piece of type or a metal or wooden block used in letterpress printing to create a space or blank area on the page

Examples

Printing quadrat
The printer placed a quadrat between the lines to separate the paragraphs clearly.
Each quadrat was carefully positioned to ensure even spacing in the final print.
He learned how to use a quadrat while training as a traditional typesetter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space - a blank area in printed text
  • synonym: type - a piece of metal or wood with a raised letter or character for printing
