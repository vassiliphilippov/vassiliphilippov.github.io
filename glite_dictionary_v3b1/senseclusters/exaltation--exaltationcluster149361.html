exaltation

noun
maximum planetary influence in astrology
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɛɡˌzɑlˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪɡˌzɔːlˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
(Astrology) The position of a planet in the zodiac where it is believed to exert its strongest influence.

Examples

Exaltation of a planet
According to astrologers, the exaltation of the Sun is in Aries.
Many believe that planetary exaltation enhances positive traits in a birth chart.
Venus reaches its exaltation in the sign of Pisces.
