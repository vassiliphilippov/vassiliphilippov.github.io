ching

verb
to make metallic sound
8.8
Pronunciation: /tʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /tʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
to make a short, sharp, metallic or ringing sound, like that of metal striking metal

Examples

Coins ching together
The coins chinged together as he dropped them into the metal tray.
She heard the bell ching softly when the door opened.
Every time the glass touched the fork, it would ching and catch everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clink - to make a light, sharp, ringing sound
