chicle gum

noun
natural gum from the sapodilla tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.əl ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.əl ɡʌm / (BrE)
a natural gum obtained from the sapodilla tree, traditionally used as the base for chewing gum

Examples

Chicle gum sap
Harvesters collect chicle gum by tapping the bark of the sapodilla tree.
The use of chicle gum declined with the arrival of cheaper artificial substitutes.
Traditional chewing gum was made from chicle gum before synthetic bases became common.
