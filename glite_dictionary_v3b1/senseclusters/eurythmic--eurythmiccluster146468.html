eurythmic

adjective
harmonious movement
Pronunciation: / juˈrɪðmɪk / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɪðmɪk / (BrE)
characterized by harmonious or rhythmically pleasing movement or arrangement

Examples

Eurythmic design
The eurythmic design of the garden paths created a sense of peace and balance.
Her eurythmic gestures captivated the audience during the performance.
Architects admired the eurythmic proportions of the historic building.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discordant - lacking harmony or agreement
  • synonym: rhythmic - having a regular repeated pattern of sounds or movements
