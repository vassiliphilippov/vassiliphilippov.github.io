obliviously

adverb
without awareness
Pronunciation: / əˈblɪviəsli / (AmE), / əˈblɪviəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of awareness of what is happening around oneself

Examples

She obliviously scrolled
She obliviously scrolled through her phone, missing the announcement.
The cat sat obliviously in the sun while the children played nearby.
He obliviously left his keys on the counter and walked out the door.
She walked obliviously past her friends, not noticing their waves.
The children played obliviously in the park, unaware of the approaching storm.
He scrolled through his phone obliviously while the meeting continued around him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consciously - in a way that shows awareness or intention
  • synonym: unawarely - in a manner showing lack of awareness
