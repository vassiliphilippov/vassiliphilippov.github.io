office staff

noun
employees working in an office
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfɪs stæf / (AmE), / ˈɒfɪs stɑːf / (BrE)
The employees who work in an office, especially those doing professional, administrative, or clerical duties.

Examples

The office staff team

The company hired two new people to join the office staff last month.
