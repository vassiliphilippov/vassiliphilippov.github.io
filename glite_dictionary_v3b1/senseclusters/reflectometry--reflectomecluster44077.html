reflectometry

noun
wave measurement technique
Pronunciation: / rɪˌflɛkˈtɑːmətri / (AmE), / rɪˌflɛkˈtɒmətri / (BrE)
a technique for measuring reflected waves to analyze the properties of materials or systems

Examples

Reflectometry technique
The laboratory used reflectometry to determine the thickness of the thin film.
Cable faults can be located precisely using reflectometry techniques.
Advances in reflectometry have improved the accuracy of material analysis in engineering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spectroscopy - the study of the interaction between matter and electromagnetic radiation
  • antonym: transmission - the passage of waves through a material
