reflectometry

noun
wave measurement technique
9.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.flek.ˈtɑː.mə.triː/ (AmE), /rɪ.flek.ˈtɒm.ə.triː/ (BrE)
a technique for measuring reflected waves to analyze the properties of materials or systems

Examples

Reflectometry technique
The laboratory used reflectometry to determine the thickness of the thin film.

Advances in reflectometry have improved the accuracy of material analysis in engineering.
