bitter end

noun
the final, often unpleasant, stage
1.7 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪt.ər end/ (AmE), /ˈbɪt.ə end/ (BrE)

The negotiations dragged on to the bitter end, with neither side willing to compromise.
