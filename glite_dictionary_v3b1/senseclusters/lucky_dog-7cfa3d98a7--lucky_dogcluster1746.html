lucky dog

noun
a person who is very lucky
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌki dɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈlʌki dɒɡ / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who has good fortune or is unusually lucky

Examples

A lucky dog
After finding fifty dollars on the street, he was called a lucky dog by his friends.
Winning the grand prize makes her the lucky dog of the evening.
You’re a lucky dog to get such a great opportunity!

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortune - chance or luck as an external, arbitrary force affecting human affairs
  • antonym: unlucky dog - an informal term for a particularly unfortunate or unlucky person
