jole

noun
sudden jolt
9.9
a sudden jerk, shake, or jolt, especially as felt in movement; dialectal or regional use.

Examples

A sudden jole
The old truck gave a jole as it hit the pothole on the country road.
She spilled her coffee after a sudden jole when the bus stopped quickly.
Passengers braced themselves for another jole as the roller coaster sped around the bend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jolt - a sudden, rough movement or shock
  • synonym: shake - a quick, short movement back and forth or up and down
