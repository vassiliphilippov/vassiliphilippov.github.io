auriculoventricular

adjective
of or relating to the atria and ventricles of the heart
Pronunciation: / əˌrɪk.jə.loʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jə.lɚ / (AmE), / ɔːˌrɪk.jʊ.ləʊ.vɛnˈtrɪk.jʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting both the atria and ventricles of the heart.
