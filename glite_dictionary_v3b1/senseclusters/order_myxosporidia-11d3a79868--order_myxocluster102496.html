order Myxosporidia

noun
order of spore-forming protozoan parasites
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌmɪksoʊspɔːrˈɪdiə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌmɪksəʊspɔːˈɪdɪə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of single-celled, spore-forming parasites in the subclass Cnidosporidia, characterized by complex spores with at least one infective amoeboid sporoplasm and one or more polar capsules containing coiled, extrusible filaments

Examples

The order Myxosporidia

The complex life cycle of the order Myxosporidia has challenged scientists for decades.
