consuetude

noun
custom with legal force
9.2 9.9
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈsuː.tuːd/ /; / /kən.ˈsjuː.tuːd/ (AmE), /kɒn.ˈsjuː.tjuːd/ /; / /kən.ˈsjuː.tjuːd/ (BrE)
