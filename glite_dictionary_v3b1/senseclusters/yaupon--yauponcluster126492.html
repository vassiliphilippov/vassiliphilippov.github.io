yaupon

noun
caffeinated herbal tea
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑːpɑn / (AmE), / ˈjɔːpɒn / (BrE)
a caffeinated herbal tea made by steeping the leaves of the yaupon plant, native to the southeastern United States

Examples
