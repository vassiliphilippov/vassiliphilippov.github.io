put one's darnedest

verb
do one's very best
Pronunciation: / pʊt wʌnz ˈdɑrn.dəst / (AmE), / pʊt wʌnz ˈdɑːn.dɪst / (BrE)

You have to put your darnedest into studying if you want to pass the exam.
