promote

verb
move pawn/piece to a superior one (in chess/checkers)
4.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊt / (AmE), / prəˈməʊt / (BrE)
(in chess or checkers) to change a pawn or piece reaching the opponent's end of the board into a more powerful piece
