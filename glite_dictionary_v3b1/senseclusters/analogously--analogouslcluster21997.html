analogously

adverb
in a similar or comparable way
Pronunciation: / əˈnæl.ə.ɡə.sli / (AmE), / əˈnæl.ə.ɡəs.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows a similarity between two situations, things, or processes

Examples

Used analogously
Just as water flows from high to low, energy can transfer analogously between systems.
The company expanded into new markets analogously to its previous strategy.
The laws of motion in physics can be applied analogously in other scientific fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: similarly - in a way that is like something else
