Rupicapra

noun
genus of goat-antelopes (chamois)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ruːpɪˈkæprə / (AmE), / ruːpɪˈkaprə / (BrE)
The genus of goat-antelopes that includes the chamois, native to mountainous regions of Europe.
