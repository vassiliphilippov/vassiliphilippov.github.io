wristlock

noun
joint lock on wrist
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪstˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈrɪstˌlɒk / (BrE)
a joint lock technique that involves twisting or bending the wrist to control or restrain an opponent

Examples

A wristlock hold
The martial arts instructor showed the class how to apply a wristlock safely.
During the match, she escaped a hold by countering with a quick wristlock.
Police officers are trained to use a wristlock to restrain suspects without causing injury.
