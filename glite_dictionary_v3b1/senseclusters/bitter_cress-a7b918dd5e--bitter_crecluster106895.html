bitter cress

noun
herbaceous plant of the genus Cardamine
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪt.ər kres/ (AmE), /ˈbɪt.ə kres/ (BrE)
any of several herbaceous plants of the genus Cardamine, typically with divided leaves and small white, pink or purple flowers, found worldwide except Antarctica.
