Nobelist

noun
winner of a Nobel Prize
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊbəˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈnəʊbəˌlɪst / (BrE)
a person who has been awarded a Nobel Prize in any category

Examples

A Nobelist winner
As a Nobelist, she travels the world speaking about her groundbreaking research.
Many aspiring scientists dream of becoming a Nobelist one day.
The Nobelist gave an inspiring lecture on the power of scientific discovery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laureate - a person honored with an award for outstanding achievement
