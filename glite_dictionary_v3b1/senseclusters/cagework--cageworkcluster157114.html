cagework

noun
making cage frameworks
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪdʒˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkeɪdʒˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the craft, skill, or process of making cages or cage-like frameworks from materials such as wire or metal

Examples
