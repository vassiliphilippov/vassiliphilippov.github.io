fiber bundle

noun
group of fibers, esp. nerve fibers
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪbɚ ˌbʌndl̩ /; / ˈfaɪbər ˈbʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪbə ˌbʌndl̩ /; / ˈfaɪbə ˈbʌndəl / (BrE)
a bundle of fibers, especially referring to groups of nerve fibers in the body
