unthanked

adjective
not appreciated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθæŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈθæŋkt / (BrE)
not having received thanks or gratitude for something done

Examples

Often unthanked work
His hard work often went unthanked by his colleagues.
Many volunteers feel unthanked despite their valuable contributions.
She completed the project, but her efforts remained unthanked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unappreciated - not recognized or valued for one's efforts
