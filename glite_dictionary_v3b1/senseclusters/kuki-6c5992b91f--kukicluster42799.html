Kuki

noun
Kamarupan language group of eastern South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːki / (AmE), / ˈkuːki / (BrE)
A group of Kamarupan languages spoken in western Myanmar (Burma), Bangladesh, and the easternmost region of India.

Examples

Kuki language group
Linguists have studied the similarities between Kuki and other Tibeto-Burman languages.
Many people in Manipur communicate in dialects of Kuki at home.
The Kuki languages are spoken by various communities along the Indo-Burmese border.
