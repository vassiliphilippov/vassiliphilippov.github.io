quietly

adverb
in a low or soft voice
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkwaɪətli / (AmE), / ˈkwaɪətli / (BrE)
in a way that is quiet, calm, or makes little or no noise

Examples

To quietly walk
He tiptoed quietly across the room to avoid attention.
She closed the door quietly so as not to wake anyone.
The teacher spoke quietly to the nervous student.
He quietly read his book in the corner, lost in thought.
She closed the door quietly behind her and tiptoed away.
The children crept quietly into the room so as not to wake their parents.
He quietly slipped out of the meeting without anyone noticing.
She closed the door quietly so she wouldn't wake the baby.
The students sat quietly during the exam, focused on their work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peacefully - in a peaceful or calm way
  • synonym: silently - without any sound at all
  • synonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet manner, with little noise
