pit-run gravel

noun
gravel found in natural deposits
3.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪt ˈrʌn ˈɡrævəl / (AmE), / ˌpɪt ˈrʌn ˈɡrævəl / (BrE)
Gravel as found in natural deposits, typically unprocessed and used in construction in its natural state.

Examples

pit-run gravel deposits
Many driveways in rural areas are made with pit-run gravel because it is readily available and inexpensive.

Unlike processed materials, pit-run gravel contains a natural mix of sand and stones.
