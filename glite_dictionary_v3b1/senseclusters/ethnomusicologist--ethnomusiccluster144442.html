ethnomusicologist

noun
music culture researcher
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˌmjuːzɪˈkɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
