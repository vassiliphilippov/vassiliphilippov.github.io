bandsaw

noun
power saw with a continuous toothed band
6.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbænd.sɑː/ /; / /ˈbænd.sɔː/ (AmE), /ˈbænd.sɔː/ (BrE)
A power saw with a long, continuous toothed metal band stretched between two wheels, used to cut wood, metal, or other materials.
