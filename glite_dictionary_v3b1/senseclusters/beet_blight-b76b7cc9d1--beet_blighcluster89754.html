beet blight

noun
disease affecting beet plants
9.6
Pronunciation: / bit blaɪt / (AmE), / biːt blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease affecting beet plants, typically caused by fungi or bacteria, resulting in damage or reduced growth

Examples

Beet blight disease

Symptoms of beet blight include yellowing leaves and wilting plants.
