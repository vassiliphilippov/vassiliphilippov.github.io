shapely

adjective
having a well-proportioned, attractive shape
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpli / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpli / (BrE)
having an attractive and well-proportioned shape, especially referring to the human body or body parts.

Examples

A shapely body
Despite his age, he maintained shapely arms through regular exercise.
She chose a dress that highlighted her shapely figure at the party.
The statue depicted a shapely goddess standing in the center of the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lanky - ungracefully thin and tall, not shapely
  • synonym: well-proportioned - having balanced and pleasing proportions
