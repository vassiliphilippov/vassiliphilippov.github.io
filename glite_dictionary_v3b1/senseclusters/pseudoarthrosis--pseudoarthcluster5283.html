pseudoarthrosis

noun
false bone joint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊɑːrˈθroʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊɑːˈθrəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a condition in which a fractured bone does not heal, resulting in a false joint at the break site

Examples

Pseudoarthrosis after fracture
The surgeon explained that pseudoarthrosis had developed after the initial bone fracture failed to heal.
Treatment for pseudoarthrosis often requires additional surgery to promote bone healing.
The patient experienced pain and limited movement due to pseudoarthrosis in his arm.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fracture - a break or crack in a bone
  • synonym: nonunion - failure of a fractured bone to heal
