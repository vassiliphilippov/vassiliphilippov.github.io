terahertz

noun
unit of frequency (one trillion hertz)
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəˌhɝts / (AmE), / ˈtɛrəˌhɜːts / (BrE)
A unit of frequency equal to one trillion cycles per second (10¹² hertz).

Examples

A terahertz signal
Scientists measured the molecular vibrations to be around two terahertz.
The new imaging device operates at frequencies in the terahertz range.
Wireless systems using terahertz waves promise incredibly high data transmission rates.
