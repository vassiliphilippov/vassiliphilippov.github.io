soddenly

adverb
in a soaked manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑdnli / (AmE), / ˈsɒdnli / (BrE)
in a way that is soaked with liquid or extremely wet

Examples

Shoes squelched soddenly
After walking through the storm, his shoes squelched soddenly with every step.
The picnic blanket lay soddenly on the grass after the sudden downpour.
She arrived home soddenly from her run in the heavy rain.

  • antonym: dryly - in a dry manner, without moisture
