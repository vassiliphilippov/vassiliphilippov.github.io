deesis

noun
prayer of supplication
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˈiːsɪs / (AmE), / diˈiːsɪs / (BrE)
a prayer or act of earnest request or intercession, especially in Christian liturgical tradition

Examples

A deesis prayer
