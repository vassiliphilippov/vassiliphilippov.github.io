bullshot

noun
misleading promotional game image
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8b\u028al\u02cc\u0283\u0251t / (AmE), / \u02c8b\u028al\u0283\u0252t / (BrE)
A promotional image or video still (especially for video games) that has been altered or staged to make the graphics or gameplay appear better than they actually are.

Examples

Game bullshot
The game's early trailers used a bullshot that made environments look unrealistically detailed.
Players complained after buying the game because the advertised bullshot did not match in-game visuals.
Journalists often compare promotional images to actual gameplay to spot any bullshot before release.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic gameplay footage - actual, unaltered video or images showing how a game looks and plays; opposite of a deliberately enhanced promotional image.
  • synonym: screenshot - an image captured from a computer or device display; promotional screenshots may be authentic or altered.
