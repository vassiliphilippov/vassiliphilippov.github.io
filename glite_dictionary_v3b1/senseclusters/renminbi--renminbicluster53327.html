renminbi

noun
Chinese currency
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛnˈmɪnˌbi / (AmE), / ˌrɛnˈmɪnˌbiː / (BrE)
the official currency of China, also called the yuan

Examples

Chinese renminbi
Tourists are required to exchange their money for renminbi when traveling in China.
The value of the renminbi has changed significantly over the past decade.
She paid for the souvenirs in renminbi at the local market.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dollar - the official currency of the United States and other countries
  • synonym: yuan - the basic unit of the renminbi, China's currency
