spring mattress

noun
mattress with internal springs
4.3
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈmætrɪs / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈmatrɪs / (BrE)
A mattress constructed with metal springs or coils inside to provide support and comfort.

Examples

A spring mattress
I replaced my old futon with a comfortable spring mattress.
Many hotels use spring mattresses for their guest rooms to ensure a good night's sleep.
The spring mattress provided excellent support for my back throughout the night.
