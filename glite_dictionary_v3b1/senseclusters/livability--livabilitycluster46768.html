livability

noun
suitability for living
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪvəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌlɪvəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a place or environment that makes it suitable, comfortable, or pleasant for people to live in
