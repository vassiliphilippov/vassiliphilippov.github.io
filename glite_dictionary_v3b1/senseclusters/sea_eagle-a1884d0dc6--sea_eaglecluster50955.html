sea eagle

noun
large fish-eating eagle
Pronunciation: / ˈsi ˌiɡəl /; / si ˈiɡəl / (AmE), / ˈsiː ˌiːɡl̩ /; / siː ˈiːɡl̩ / (BrE)
a large eagle that lives near water and mainly eats fish

Examples

The sea eagle hunts fish
Many conservationists are working to reintroduce the sea eagle to its former habitats.
The sea eagle soared above the rocky coastline searching for fish.
We spotted a pair of sea eagles nesting on the cliffs by the lake.
Many sea eagles nest on rocky cliffs near the ocean.
The sea eagle soared above the lake in search of fish.
We spotted a white-tailed sea eagle during our trip to Scotland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bald eagle - a North American species of sea eagle
  • synonym: osprey - a large fish-eating bird of prey
  • synonym: white-tailed eagle - a species of sea eagle native to Europe and Asia
