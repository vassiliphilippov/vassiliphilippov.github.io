predynastic

adjective
before a dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪˈnæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪˈnæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the period before the establishment of a dynasty, especially in ancient Egypt

Examples

Predynastic Egypt
The museum displays several predynastic artifacts from early Egyptian history.
Archaeologists study predynastic settlements to understand the origins of ancient civilizations.
Pottery from the predynastic period shows the development of early artistic styles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dynastic - relating to a dynasty or period ruled by a dynasty
  • synonym: prehistoric - relating to the time before written history
