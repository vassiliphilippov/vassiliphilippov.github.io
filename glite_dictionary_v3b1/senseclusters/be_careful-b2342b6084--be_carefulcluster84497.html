be careful

verb
warn to take precautions; avoid danger
Pronunciation: / bi ˈkɛr.fəl / (AmE), / bi ˈkeə.fəl / (BrE)
used to warn someone to take precautions in order to avoid danger or harm

Examples

To be careful walking
Be careful when walking on the icy sidewalk, as it can be very slippery.
Please be careful not to spill anything on the new carpet.
You need to be careful with that fragile vase, or it might break.

Related Terms

  • antonym: be careless - to fail to take proper care; to be negligent or reckless
  • synonym: be cautious - to act with care to avoid risk
