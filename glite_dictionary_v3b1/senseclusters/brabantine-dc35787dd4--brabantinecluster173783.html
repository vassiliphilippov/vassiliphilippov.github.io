Brabantine

noun
Dutch dialect
Pronunciation: / brəˈbæntaɪn / (AmE), / brəˈbæntaɪn / (BrE)
a variety of the Dutch language spoken in parts of the southern Netherlands and northern Belgium

Examples

Brabantine dialect speaker
She learned to speak Brabantine from her grandmother in the village.
The documentary explored the history and features of the Brabantine dialect.
Many people in northern Belgium still use Brabantine in everyday conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Brabantian - the dialect of Dutch spoken in the Brabant region
  • synonym: Flemish - the Dutch dialects spoken in northern Belgium
  • antonym: Standard Dutch - the official form of the Dutch language
