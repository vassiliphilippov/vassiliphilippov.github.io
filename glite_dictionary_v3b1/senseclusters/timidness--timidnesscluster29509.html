timidness

noun
quality of being timid or shy
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪmɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɪmɪdnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of lacking courage or confidence; being easily frightened or hesitant, especially in unfamiliar situations.

Examples

His timidness showed
Because of his timidness, he hesitated before approaching the unfamiliar dog.
Her timidness made it difficult for her to speak up during the meeting.
You can overcome timidness by gradually facing new situations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boldness - willingness to take risks; confidence
  • synonym: timidity - the quality of being easily frightened; lack of courage or confidence
