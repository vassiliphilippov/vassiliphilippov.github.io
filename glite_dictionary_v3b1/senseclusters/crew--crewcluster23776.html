crew

noun
group of friends (informal)
3.5 8.46/m
Pronunciation: / kruÀê / (AmE), / kruÀê / (BrE)
An informal group of friends who regularly associate with each other.

Examples

My crew hangs out
Jake is going out with his crew this weekend to see a movie.
She introduced me to her crew at the party last night.
Whenever there's a concert, our entire crew tries to get tickets together.
