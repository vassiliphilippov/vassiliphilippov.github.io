banana boat

noun
ship for transporting bananas
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə boʊt / (AmE), / bəˈnænə bəʊt / (BrE)
a ship specially designed or used for transporting bananas from tropical regions

Examples

A banana boat from Jamaica
He worked as an engineer on a banana boat plying the route between Jamaica and the United States.
Old banana boats were a common sight in Caribbean harbors during harvest season.
The banana boat arrived at the port carrying its load of fresh fruit.
