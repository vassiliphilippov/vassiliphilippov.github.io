sleety

adjective
characterized by sleet (frozen rain)
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːti / (AmE), / ˈsliːti / (BrE)
(of weather) characterized by or consisting of sleet; relating to frozen or partially frozen rain.

Examples

sleety weather
A sleety mix of rain and ice made driving hazardous this morning.
I stayed indoors all day to avoid the sleety weather outside.
The sky was grey and the wind was sleety as we hurried home.
