Synonyms

  • divine service
    7.4 0.03/m
    divine service is a formal public worship event led in church, while liturgy is the fixed set of rites used.
  • religious service
    2.8 0.2/m
    religious service is a public act of worship in a church or temple, while liturgy names the formal, prescribed words and actions.
  • service
    4.2 17/m
    service is any religious meeting for worship, while liturgy is the specific set of words and rites used in that meeting.
  • religious rite
    4.1 0.1/m
    religious rite is a single prescribed ceremony or act in a faith, while liturgy is the whole ordered service around such rites.
  • religious ceremony
    2.9 0.4/m
    religious ceremony is a formal event in a faith that marks an occasion, while liturgy means the established public worship form.
  • ritual
    4.6 19/m
    ritual is a set of repeated actions with symbolic meaning, while liturgy is the formal public worship that includes those actions.
  • ceremony
    2.5 40/m
    ceremony is any formal event that follows tradition or rules, while liturgy specifically means a public religious service with set rites.
