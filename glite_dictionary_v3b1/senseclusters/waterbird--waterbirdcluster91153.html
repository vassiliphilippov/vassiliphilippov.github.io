waterbird

noun
bird living on or near freshwater
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌbɝd / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəbɜːd / (BrE)
a bird that lives on or near freshwater, especially one that swims or wades

Examples

A waterbird swims
During migration, thousands of waterbirds gather at this wetland.
Researchers observed a rare waterbird feeding near the marshy shore.
The lake was home to many species of waterbird, including ducks and herons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterfowl - birds that live on or near water, especially ducks, geese, and swans
