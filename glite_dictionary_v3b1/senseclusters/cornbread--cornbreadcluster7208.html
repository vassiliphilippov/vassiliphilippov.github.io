cornbread

noun
bread made from cornmeal
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌbrɛd / (BrE)
A type of bread made chiefly from cornmeal, often associated with Southern U.S. cuisine.
