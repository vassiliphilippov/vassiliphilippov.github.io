copper sulfate

noun
blue crystalline chemical compound
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːpər ˈsʌlˌfeɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒpə ˈsʌl.feɪt /; / ˈkɒpə ˈsʌlfeɪt / (BrE)
a bright blue crystalline salt or compound used as a fungicide, algaecide, and in electroplating and chemistry, obtained by the action of sulfuric acid on copper oxide
