vice-regent

noun
deputy or representative of a regent
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪsˈriːdʒənt / (AmE), / ˌvaɪsˈriːdʒənt / (BrE)
a person who acts as the deputy or representative of a regent, exercising authority on their behalf

Examples

The vice-regent acts
Appointed as vice-regent, she carried out the duties of the regency with diligence.
Historical records mention several vice-regents administering the territory on behalf of the crown.
The vice-regent was responsible for governing the province during the king’s absence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deputy - a person who acts as a substitute with the power to act
  • degree higher: regent - a person appointed to administer a state because the monarch is a minor or absent
