colluder

noun
secret accomplice
8.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈluːdər / (AmE), / kəˈluːdə / (BrE)
a person who secretly works with others to do something dishonest or illegal

Examples

The colluder in crime
The authorities arrested the main colluder behind the financial fraud.
Investigators believe there is more than one colluder involved in the illegal operation.
She denied being a colluder in the scheme, claiming she acted alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accomplice - a person who helps another commit a crime
  • synonym: conspirator - a person who plans secretly with others to do something harmful or illegal
  • antonym: whistleblower - a person who exposes secret or illegal activities
