martyrological

adjective
relating to martyrdom
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrtiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmɑːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
showing qualities of martyrdom, such as suffering, self-sacrifice, or the attitude of a martyr.

Examples

Martyrological attitude
Her martyrological attitude made her colleagues feel guilty for not working as hard.
The film portrayed the hero in a martyrological light, emphasizing his suffering for others.
He gave a martyrological account of his struggles, highlighting every sacrifice he had made.

Related Terms

  • synonym: martyr-like - having the qualities or attitude of a martyr
  • synonym: self-sacrificing - willing to give up one's own interests for others
  • antonym: selfish - lacking consideration for others; concerned only with oneself
