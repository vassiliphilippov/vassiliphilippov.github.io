magnetic

adjective
having properties of a magnet
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /mæg.ˈne.tɪk/ (AmE), /mæg.ˈne.tɪk/ (BrE)
having the properties of a magnet or relating to magnetism; able to attract certain metals

Examples
