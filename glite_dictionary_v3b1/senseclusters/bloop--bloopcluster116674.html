bloop

verb
to make bubbling sound
6.7 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / bluÀêp / (AmE), / bluÀêp / (BrE)
to make a short, soft, bubbling or popping sound, especially in a liquid

Examples

Soup blooped gently

We listened to the mud pots bloop and bubble in the geothermal field.
As the diver exhaled, air bubbles blooped to the surface of the water.

We listened to the mud bloop under our boots as we walked through the swamp.
Tiny air pockets would bloop to the surface of the pond every few seconds.
