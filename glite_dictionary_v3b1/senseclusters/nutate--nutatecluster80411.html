nutate

verb
to sway or nod repeatedly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːteɪt / (AmE), / ˈnjuːteɪt / (BrE)
to rock, sway, or nod, especially involuntarily or in a periodic manner

Examples

To nutate slightly
After receiving the news, he unconsciously began to nutate while standing in place.
Some plants nutate as their stems grow, tracing subtle circular patterns in the air.
The flower heads nutate gently in the wind, swaying back and forth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oscillate - to move or swing back and forth in a regular rhythm
