hui

noun
Chinese Muslim ethnic group
Pronunciation: / hweɪ / (AmE), / hweɪ / (BrE)
a predominantly Muslim ethnic group in China, known for their shared cultural and religious traditions

Examples

The hui people
The hui have a long history of blending Islamic practices with Chinese customs.
Many hui communities celebrate both Chinese and Islamic holidays throughout the year.
Researchers are studying the unique traditions of the hui in northwest China.
