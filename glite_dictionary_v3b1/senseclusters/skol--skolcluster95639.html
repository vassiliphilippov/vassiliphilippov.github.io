skol

interjection
Scandinavian toast
Pronunciation: / skoʊl / (AmE), / skəʊl / (BrE)
an exclamation used in Scandinavian countries to wish good health before drinking, similar to 'cheers'

Examples

Skol before drinking
He raised his glass and shouted 'skol!' before taking a sip.
At the wedding, everyone joined in with a loud 'skol' before the first toast.
The group clinked their glasses and said 'skol' to celebrate their reunion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salud - Spanish exclamation wishing good health when drinking
