halvah

noun
sweet sesame confection
7.4
Pronunciation: / hɑlˈvɑ / (AmE), / hælˈvɑː / (BrE)
a sweet confection made from sesame paste or other nut butters mixed with sugar

Examples

Sesame halvah dessert
She offered me a piece of homemade halvah with tea.
At the market, I bought pistachio halvah to share with my family.
Many Middle Eastern bakeries sell different varieties of halvah throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fudge - a soft, sweet candy made from sugar, butter, and milk
  • synonym: nougat - a sweet made from sugar or honey with nuts and egg white
