Corallorhiza maculata

noun
a species of North American orchid
Pronunciation: / kəˌræl.əˈraɪ.zə ˌmæk.jʊˈleɪ.tə / (AmE), / kɒˌral.əˈraɪ.zə ˌmak.juˈleɪ.tə / (BrE)
A species of North American orchid with yellowish, reddish, or purplish-brown leafless stems and loose racemes of similar-colored flowers with white purple-spotted lips.
