Tuberales

noun
order of fungi including truffles
9.9
Pronunciation: /tuː.ˈbər.ə.liːz/ /; / /tuː.ˈbeɪ.rə.liːz/ (AmE), /tjuː.ˈbə.rə.liːz/ /; / /tuː.ˈbə.rə.liːz/ (BrE)
An order of fungi in the subdivision Ascomycota, characterized by closed, underground fruiting bodies (ascocarps), including truffles and their relatives.

Examples

Tuberales fungi order
Many species within the Tuberales form symbiotic relationships with trees.
Mycologists study the Tuberales to understand their unique underground fruiting bodies.
The Tuberales are well known for producing highly prized edible truffles.
