referable

adjective
warranting referral to a specialist
7.0
Pronunciation: /rə.ˈfɜːr.ə.bəl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfɜːr.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /rə.ˈfɜː.rə.bəl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈfɜː.rə.bəl/ (BrE)
(medicine) appropriate for, or requiring, referral to a specialist or another service for further assessment or management.

Examples

Referable to a specialist
The GP noted that the persistent neck swelling was referable to an ENT clinic for investigation.
Any child with unexplained weight loss should be considered referable to paediatric oncology for further tests.
If symptoms persist after standard treatment, the patient is referable to secondary care for specialist review.

Related Terms
