lobulated

adjective
divided into lobes
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːbjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɒbjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
divided into small rounded sections or lobes, often used to describe organs or tumors

Examples

lobulated tumor

A lobulated liver can be a sign of certain medical conditions.
The plant's leaves are lobulated, giving them a unique appearance.
