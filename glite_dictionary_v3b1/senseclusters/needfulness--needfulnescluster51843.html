needfulness

noun
state of being necessary
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈniːdfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being necessary or required

Examples

The needfulness of rules
The needfulness of regular exercise is often emphasized by health experts.
She questioned the needfulness of such strict rules in the classroom.
Many people underestimate the needfulness of clear communication in teamwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessity - the state of being needed or required
  • antonym: redundancy - the state of being unnecessary or not required
