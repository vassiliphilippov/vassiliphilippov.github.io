spearer

noun
one who uses a spear
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrər / (AmE), / ˈspɪərə / (BrE)
a person or animal that uses a spear to hunt, catch, or attack

Examples

A skilled spearer
The experienced spearer waited patiently by the riverbank for fish to appear.
In some cultures, a skilled spearer is highly respected for providing food.
The animal acted as a natural spearer, using its sharp horn to catch prey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisher - a person who catches fish
  • synonym: hunter - a person or animal that hunts
