congenial

adjective
compatible for grafting or fertilization (plants)
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒiniəl / (AmE), / kənˈdʒiːniəl / (BrE)
(of plants) capable of being cross-fertilized or grafted together due to compatibility.

Examples

Congenial plants
Choosing congenial rootstocks is vital for successful fruit tree propagation.
The plants proved surprisingly congenial, allowing for efficient cross-breeding.
These two rose species are congenial, making them ideal for grafting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compatible - able to exist or work together without conflict
