tragicomically

adverb
tragic and comic way
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtrædʒɪˈkɑːmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtrædʒɪˈkɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that combines both tragic and comic elements

Examples

Ends tragicomically
The story ended tragicomically, leaving the audience unsure whether to laugh or cry.
He tragicomically recounted his misadventures, making everyone both sympathize and chuckle.
Their attempt to fix the car failed tragicomically, resulting in both a mess and a good laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ironically - in a way that is opposite to what is expected or intended
  • antonym: seriously - in a solemn or earnest manner
