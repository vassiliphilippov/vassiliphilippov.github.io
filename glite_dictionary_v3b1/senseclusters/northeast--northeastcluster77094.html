northeast

noun
compass direction between north and east
Pronunciation: / ˌnɔrθˈist / (AmE), / ˌnɔːθˈiːst / (BrE)
the direction or compass point halfway between north and east (at a 45-degree angle)

Examples

Northeast on the compass
A strong wind was blowing from the northeast all day.
Snow is expected to move in from the northeast this evening.
The garden faces northeast, getting the morning sun.
A strong wind was blowing from the northeast all morning.
The city is located to the northeast of the capital.
They traveled in a northeast direction across the open plain.
