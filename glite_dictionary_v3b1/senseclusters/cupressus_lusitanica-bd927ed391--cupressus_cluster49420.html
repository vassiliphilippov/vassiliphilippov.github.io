Cupressus lusitanica

noun
a Mexican evergreen conifer tree
9.8
Pronunciation: / kuːˈprɛsəs luːsɪˈtænɪkə / (AmE), / kjuːˈprɛsəs luːsɪˈtænɪkə / (BrE)
a tall, spreading evergreen conifer native to Mexico, with drooping branches, commonly planted as an ornamental tree; also known as the Mexican cypress
