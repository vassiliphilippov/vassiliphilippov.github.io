unyoked

verb
to remove a yoke
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈjoʊkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈjəʊkt / (BrE)
removed a yoke from an animal or object; released or separated something that had been harnessed or joined

Examples

To unyoke the horses
After a long day in the fields, the farmer unyoked the horses and led them to water.
The oxen were unyoked as soon as the plowing was finished for the day.
She unyoked the cart from the donkey before feeding it some hay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: release - to set free or let go
  • antonym: yoke - to join or harness together, especially animals
