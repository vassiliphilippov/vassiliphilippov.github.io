Swedenborgian

noun
follower of Swedenborg
Pronunciation: / ˌswiːdənˈbɔːrɡiən / (AmE), / ˌswiːdənˈbɔːɡiən / (BrE)
a member of a Christian religious group that follows the teachings of Emanuel Swedenborg

Examples

A Swedenborgian churchgoer
The Swedenborgian community gathers every Sunday to discuss spiritual teachings.
As a Swedenborgian, she believes in a unique interpretation of the afterlife.
The church was founded by a group of devoted Swedenborgians in the 19th century.
