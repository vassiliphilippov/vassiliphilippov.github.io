grappling hook

noun
hooked tool used with rope to grasp objects
9.0 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈgræp.lɪŋ hʊk/ /; / /ˈgræp.əl.ɪŋ hʊk/ (AmE), /ˈgræp.lɪŋ hʊk/ /; / /ˈgræp.əl.ɪŋ hʊk/ (BrE)
A tool with multiple hooks, typically attached to a rope, used for grasping or holding objects, especially in climbing or retrieval.
