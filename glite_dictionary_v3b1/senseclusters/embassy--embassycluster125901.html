embassy

noun
the ambassador and staff representing a country
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.bə.si /; / ˈɛmbəsi / (AmE), / ˈɛm.bə.si /; / ˈɛmbəsi / (BrE)
the official group of people and the building where a country's representatives (the ambassador and staff) work in a foreign country

Examples

The French embassy
She was invited to a reception hosted by the French embassy.
The embassy organized cultural exchange programs in the host country.
The American embassy worked tirelessly to assist citizens during the crisis.
Security has increased around the embassy following recent political unrest.
She went to the embassy to renew her visa before it expired.
The American embassy in Paris is located near the river Seine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consulate - the office or staff of a consul, a lower rank diplomatic official
