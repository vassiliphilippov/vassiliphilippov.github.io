  • subterraneous

    subterraneous is a formal word for things below the earth's surface, while subsurface means beneath any object's surface.
  • subjacent
    9.9 0.01/m
    subjacent means directly under something in a strict spatial sense, while subsurface is a general term for beneath a surface.
  • underground
