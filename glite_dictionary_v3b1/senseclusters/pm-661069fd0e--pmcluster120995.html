p.m.

noun
the time between noon and midnight
1.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê Àà…õm / (AmE), / piÀê Àà…õm / (BrE)
the period of time from noon until midnight, used to specify times after 12:00 noon.

Examples

6:00 p.m.
Our flight departs at 6:45 p.m. sharp.
She doesn't usually eat lunch until 2 p.m..
The meeting is scheduled for 3:00 p.m. tomorrow.

Synonyms

  • post meridiem
    5.3 0.01/m
    post meridiem is the full Latin phrase used in formal time notation, while p.m. is the common punctuated abbreviation.
  • PM
    1.0 0.00/m
    PM is the capitalized abbreviation often shown on digital clocks, while p.m. is the punctuated, written form common in print.
