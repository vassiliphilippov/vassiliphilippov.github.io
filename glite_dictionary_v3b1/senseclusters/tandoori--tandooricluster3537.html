tandoori

noun
spiced clay-oven dish
Pronunciation: / tænˈdʊri / (AmE), / tænˈdʊəri / (BrE)
a dish, usually meat or vegetables, marinated with spices and cooked in a tandoor (clay oven).

Examples

A tandoori with naan
The restaurant is famous for its delicious tandoori served with fresh naan bread.
She ordered a vegetarian tandoori that was full of flavor and spices.
At the party, everyone enjoyed the smoky taste of the tandoori cooked in a traditional oven.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue - meat or vegetables cooked over an open fire or grill
  • synonym: roast - to cook food, especially meat, using dry heat in an oven or over a fire
