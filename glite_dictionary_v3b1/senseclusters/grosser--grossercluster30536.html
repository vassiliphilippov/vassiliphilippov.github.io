grosser

noun
wholesale dealer
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊsər / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊsə / (BrE)
a wholesaler or dealer who sells goods in large quantities, especially to retailers rather than individual customers

Examples

A spice grosser
The restaurant bought bulk spices from the grosser to supply its busy kitchen.
In the past, shopkeepers would visit the grosser to purchase goods for their stores.
The grosser offered discounts to customers who ordered large quantities of merchandise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods directly to consumers
  • synonym: wholesaler - a person or company that sells goods in large quantities to retailers
