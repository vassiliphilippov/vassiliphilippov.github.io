alif

noun
Arabic letter; number one
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪf / (AmE), / ˈælɪf / (BrE)
the first letter of the Arabic alphabet, written as 'ا', representing a long 'a' sound and sometimes the number one
