Cephalotaceae

noun
plant family containing Cephalotus
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛfələˈteɪsi.i /; / ˌsɛfəloʊˈteɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌsɛfələˈteɪsɪ.i /; / ˌsɛfəˌlɒtəˈsiːʌiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants in the order Rosales, consisting only of the genus Cephalotus (the Australian pitcher plant)

Botanists classified the Australian pitcher plant within the family Cephalotaceae.
