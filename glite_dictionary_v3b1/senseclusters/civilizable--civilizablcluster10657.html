civilizable

adjective
able to be civilized
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvələˌzaɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪvɪlaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made more civilized, advanced, or suitable for living or polite society

Examples

The explorers believed the remote island was civilizable with enough resources and effort.

He argued that every society is civilizable with patience and understanding.
