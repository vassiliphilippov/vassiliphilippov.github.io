Salsola

proper noun
a genus of saltwort plants
Pronunciation: / sælˈsoʊlə / (AmE), / salˈsəʊlə / (BrE)
A genus of mostly Old World herbs or shrubs, commonly known as saltworts, some species of which are adapted to saline soils.
