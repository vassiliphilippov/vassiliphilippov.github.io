Amoy language

noun
variety of Chinese spoken in Fujian, China
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈmɔɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a variety of Chinese, part of the Southern Min group, spoken primarily in the Fujian province of China and in Taiwan
