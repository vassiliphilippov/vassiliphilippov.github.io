Amoy language

noun
variety of Chinese spoken in Fujian, China
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔɪ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈmɔɪ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
a variety of Chinese, part of the Southern Min group, spoken primarily in the Fujian province of China and in Taiwan

Examples

Amoy language speakers
Many overseas Chinese communities continue to use Amoy language in their daily interactions.
She decided to learn Amoy language to communicate with her relatives in southern Fujian.
The Amoy language is distinct from Mandarin and Cantonese in pronunciation and vocabulary.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Mandarin Chinese - the most widely spoken Chinese language, distinct from Amoy
  • synonym: Southern Min - a group of Chinese dialects including Amoy
