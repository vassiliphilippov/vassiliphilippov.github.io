queen mole rat

noun
dominant reproductive female naked mole rat
Pronunciation: / kwiːn moʊl ræt / (AmE), / kwiːn məʊl ræt / (BrE)
the dominant female naked mole rat in a colony, which is larger than others and the only individual that produces offspring, typically mating with only a few males

Examples

The queen mole rat colony
In each colony, the queen mole rat is responsible for bearing all the young.
Researchers observed how the queen mole rat asserts dominance over the other females.
The queen mole rat grew significantly larger than the worker mole rats.
