beckoningly

adverb
invitingly
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛkənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbɛkənɪŋli / (BrE)
in an inviting or tempting way, as if signaling someone to come closer or follow

Examples

Smile beckoningly
She smiled beckoningly at him from across the room.
The open door stood beckoningly, inviting us to step inside.
He waved beckoningly to his friends to join him at the table.
The open door glowed beckoningly in the darkness, inviting us to step inside.
She smiled beckoningly at her friends, encouraging them to join her on the dance floor.
The aroma of fresh bread drifted beckoningly from the bakery on the corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alluringly - in a way that attracts or tempts
  • synonym: invitingly - in a way that encourages someone to approach or join
  • synonym: temptingly - in a way that arouses desire or interest
  • antonym: uninvitingly - in a way that is not attractive or welcoming
