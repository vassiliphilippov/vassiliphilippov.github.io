hydroform

verb
to shape metal with fluid
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌfɔːm / (BrE)
to shape metal or hollow components by using high-pressure fluid to force them into a die.

Examples

Modern manufacturing often uses machines to hydroform complex metal parts efficiently.
