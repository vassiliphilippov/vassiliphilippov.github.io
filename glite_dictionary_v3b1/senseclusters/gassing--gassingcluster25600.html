gassing

verb
to chat at length
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡæsɪŋ / (BrE)
to talk a lot, especially about unimportant or trivial things, often in a casual or chatty way

Examples

Gassing about nothing
They were gassing for hours about their favorite TV shows.
We spent the afternoon gassing in the café instead of working.
He was still gassing on the phone when I left the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babbling - talking rapidly and continuously in a foolish, excited, or incomprehensible way
  • synonym: chattering - talking quickly and continuously about trivial matters
  • antonym: listening - paying attention to what someone is saying
