secret

adjective
kept hidden; not openly disclosed
2.0 41/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀê.kr…ôt /; / ÀàsiÀêkr…ôt / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀê.kr…™t /; / ÀàsiÀêkr…™t / (BrE)
kept hidden or not meant to be known or seen by others; done or made in a way that is meant to be hidden

Examples

Secret plan
She kept her ambitions secret from her colleagues.

Synonyms

  • confidential
    5.3 8.68/m
    confidential is used for private information shared with trust, while secret is a broader word for anything kept hidden.
  • undisclosed
    5.8 1.65/m
    undisclosed means not revealed in reports or statements, while secret is a general hidden fact or action.
  • covert
    6.8 6.39/m
    covert describes actions done quietly or in secret, often in military or spy work, while secret is more general.
  • concealed
    3.7 0.27/m
    concealed is physically kept out of sight or covered, while secret can refer to hidden ideas, plans, or things.
  • hidden
    1.1 7.26/m
    hidden stays out of sight or is hard to find, often by accident, while secret is usually meant to be kept hidden.
