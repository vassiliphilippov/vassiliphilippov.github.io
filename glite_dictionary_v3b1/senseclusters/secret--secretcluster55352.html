secret

adjective
kept hidden; not openly disclosed
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.krət/ /; / /ˈsiː.krɪt/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.krət/ /; / /ˈsiː.krɪt/ (BrE)

Examples
