head louse

noun
parasitic insect living on human scalp
Pronunciation: / hɛd laʊs / (AmE), / hɛd laʊs / (BrE)
a small parasitic insect that lives on the scalp and hair of humans, feeding on blood

Examples

A head louse egg
A single head louse can lay dozens of eggs, called nits, in one week.
Children in schools are often checked for head louse infestations.
She used a special shampoo to remove head louse from her daughter's hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body louse - a species of louse infesting the body and clothing of humans
  • synonym: lice - plural form for louse, used for various louse species
