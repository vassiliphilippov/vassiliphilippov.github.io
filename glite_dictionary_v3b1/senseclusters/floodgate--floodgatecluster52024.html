floodgate

noun
gate or valve controlling water flow
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌd.geɪt/ (AmE), /ˈflʌd.geɪt/ (BrE)
a gate or valve used to control the flow of water, especially through a sluice or a dam

Examples

Dam floodgate
Authorities monitored the floodgate closely during the storm to manage potential flooding.
Heavy rainfall forced workers to open the floodgate to prevent the dam from overflowing.
The old mill was built next to a stream, with a floodgate to control the water supply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sluice gate - a sliding gate for regulating water flow in a sluice
