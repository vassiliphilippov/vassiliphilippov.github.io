singability

noun
ease of singing
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a song or melody that makes it easy or pleasant to sing

Examples

The singability of a song
The singability of the chorus made it a favorite at school assemblies.
Composers often consider singability when writing music for amateur choirs.
Her new song lacks the singability that made her earlier hits so popular.
The singability of the chorus made it popular at karaoke nights.
When writing for children, composers often consider the singability of their melodies.
She praised the singability of the new anthem, saying everyone could join in easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: difficulty - the quality of being hard to do or perform
  • synonym: melodiousness - the quality of having a pleasant melody
  • synonym: tunefulness - the quality of being tuneful or musical
