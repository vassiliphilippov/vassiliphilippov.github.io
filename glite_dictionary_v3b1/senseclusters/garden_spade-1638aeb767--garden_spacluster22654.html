garden spade

noun
spade used in gardening
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn̩ speɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn speɪd / (BrE)
a spade with a flat, typically rectangular blade used by gardeners for digging and edging soil
