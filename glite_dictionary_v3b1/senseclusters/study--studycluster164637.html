study

noun
room used for reading and writing
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌdi / (AmE), / ˈstʌdi / (BrE)
a room in a house set aside for reading, writing, or academic work

Examples

A quiet study
He spent every morning in the study working on his novel.
She converted the spare bedroom into a comfortable study.
The shelves in the study were lined with old books and letters.
