shooting star

noun
meteoroid burning up in sky
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːɾɪŋ stɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtɪŋ stɑː / (BrE)
A bright streak of light in the night sky caused by a meteoroid burning up as it enters the Earth's atmosphere.

Examples

A shooting star streak
A shooting star is actually a small rock from space burning up in Earth's atmosphere.
Many people make a wish when they see a shooting star.
We saw a shooting star streak across the sky during the meteor shower last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteor - A small body from space that enters Earth's atmosphere, producing a streak of light.
  • degree lower: meteorite - A meteoroid that survives its passage through the atmosphere and reaches the Earth's surface.
