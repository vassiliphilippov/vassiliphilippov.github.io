grass-covered

adjective
covered with grass
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræs ˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈɡrɑːs ˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
covered with grass; having a surface largely or entirely covered by grass.

Examples

Grass-covered hills

The hills were grass-covered and shone in the morning light.
They picnicked on a grass-covered field near the river.
