iPad kid

noun
child adept with or reliant on iPads/tablets
1.4
Pronunciation: / aɪˌpæd kɪd / (AmE), / aɪˌpæd kɪd / (BrE)
A child who is accustomed to using iPads or similar tablets, often to the point of being very knowledgeable or dependent on them.

Examples

iPad kid at home
Her nephew is such an iPad kid, always teaching his parents how to use new apps.
Some worry that becoming an iPad kid can limit outdoor play and social interaction.
The classroom is full of iPad kids who master digital tools quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screenager - a teenager who spends much time using electronic devices
  • synonym: tablet kid - child who uses tablets frequently
