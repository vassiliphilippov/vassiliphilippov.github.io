iPad kid

noun
child adept with or reliant on iPads/tablets
1.4 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.pæd kɪd/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.pæd kɪd/ (BrE)
A child who is accustomed to using iPads or similar tablets, often to the point of being very knowledgeable or dependent on them.
