sim

noun
mobile phone card
4.5
Pronunciation: / sɪm / (AmE), / sɪm / (BrE)
a small removable smart card in a mobile phone that stores network and subscriber information

Examples

Insert a sim card
You need to insert a sim into your phone before you can make calls.
She bought a new sim when she arrived in the country to use local services.
If your sim is damaged, you might lose access to your contacts and messages.
