sidesaddle

adverb
riding a horse with legs on one side
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌsædəl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌsædəl / (BrE)
in a manner of riding a horse with both legs on the same side of the animal, as is traditional for women in certain periods

Examples

To ride sidesaddle
During the parade, the rider performed tricks while seated sidesaddle.
She preferred to ride sidesaddle during the historical reenactment.
The queen made her entrance on horseback, sitting gracefully sidesaddle.
