Greek chorus

noun
group of performers commenting in Greek drama
Pronunciation: / ɡrik ˈkɔrəs /; / ɡrik ˈkɔɹ.əs / (AmE), / ɡriːk ˈkɔːrəs /; / ɡriːk ˈkɔː.rəs / (BrE)
a group of people, especially in ancient Greek drama, who comment on or react to the main events, or any group that comments on events as observers
