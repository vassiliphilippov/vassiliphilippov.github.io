monandrous

adjective
having only one husband or male mate
Pronunciation: / moʊˈnændrəs / (AmE), / məˈnændrəs / (BrE)
(of a female or species) having only one husband or male mate at a time.

Examples

A monandrous marriage
A monandrous marriage system is common in many human societies.
In some butterfly species, the females are strictly monandrous throughout their lives.
Scientists noted that the queen ant appeared to be monandrous, mating with only one male in her lifetime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polyandrous - having more than one husband or male mate at a time
