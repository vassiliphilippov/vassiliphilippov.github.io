stoa

noun
Greek covered walkway
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊə / (AmE), / ˈstəʊə / (BrE)
a covered walkway or portico, especially one used for public gatherings in ancient Greek architecture

Examples

Ancient stoa building
The ancient marketplace was lined with a grand stoa where citizens gathered.
Visitors admired the columns and high ceilings of the restored stoa in Athens.
Philosophers would meet in the stoa to discuss ideas and debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colonnade - a row of columns supporting a roof or entablature
  • synonym: portico - a structure consisting of a roof supported by columns at regular intervals
