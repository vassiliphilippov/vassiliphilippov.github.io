prideful

adjective
having strong pride, especially from success
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪd.fəl / (AmE), / ˈpraɪd.fəl / (BrE)
feeling or showing strong pride, especially as a result of achievement or success; having an attitude of self-respect and satisfaction.

Examples

Prideful achievement
Despite her prideful attitude, she remained grateful for her team's support.
She gave her parents a prideful smile after receiving the award.
The prideful winner raised his trophy high before an applauding crowd.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
  • synonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction as a result of one's own achievements
