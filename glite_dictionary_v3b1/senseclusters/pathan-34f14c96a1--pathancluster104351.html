Pathan

noun
member of a Pashto-speaking ethnic group
9.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈtɑn /; / pəˈtɑːn / (AmE), / pəˈtɑːn / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group (also called Pashtuns) living mainly in northwestern Pakistan and southeastern Afghanistan, who speak Pashto
