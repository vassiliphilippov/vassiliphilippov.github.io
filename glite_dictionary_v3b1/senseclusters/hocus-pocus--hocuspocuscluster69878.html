hocus-pocus

noun
meaningless magic chant
8.9 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.kəs ˈpoʊ.kəs/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.kəs ˈpəʊ.kəs/ (BrE)
words or actions used to trick or deceive people, often by making something seem magical or mysterious
