acetaminophen

noun
mild pain reliever and fever reducer
8.3 3.3
Pronunciation: /ə.siː.tə.ˈmɪn.ə.fən/ /; / /ə.sɪ.tə.ˈmɪn.ə.fən/ (AmE), /ə.siː.tə.ˈmɪn.ə.fən/ /; / /ə.sɪ.tə.ˈmɪn.ə.fən/ (BrE)
A common drug used to relieve mild pain and reduce fever, not effective against inflammation.

Examples
