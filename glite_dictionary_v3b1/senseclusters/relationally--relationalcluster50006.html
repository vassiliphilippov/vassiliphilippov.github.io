relationally

adverb
by relationships
Pronunciation: / rɪˈleɪʃənəli / (AmE), / rɪˈleɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that is based on or organized by relationships between data items or elements, especially in computing or mathematics

Examples

Relationally linked data
The tables in the database are connected relationally to allow for complex queries.
Data is often organized relationally to improve efficiency and consistency in information systems.
In mathematics, sets can be compared relationally to determine how their elements interact.
