black carpet beetle

noun
an all-black species of carpet beetle
7.5
Pronunciation: / blæk ˈkɑr.pɪt ˈbiː.təl / (AmE), / blæk ˈkɑː.pɪt ˈbiː.tl̩ / (BrE)
a species of carpet beetle that is entirely black in color, often found as a household pest damaging fabrics and stored products
