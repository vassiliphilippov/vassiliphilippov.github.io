puff

noun
soft pad for applying powder
3.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /pʌf/ (AmE), /pʌf/ (BrE)
a soft, padded object, such as a cosmetic pad, cushion, or quilted bedding

Examples

Makeup puff
After washing the puff, let it air dry before using it again.
She dabbed her face with a puff to apply the new face powder.
The makeup kit came with a tiny pink puff for easy application.
He bought a blue velvet puff to match the new sofa.
She placed her feet on the puff while reading a book in the living room.
The children fought over who got to sit on the soft puff during movie night.
After washing the puff, she hung it in the sun to dry.
She pulled the thick puff up to her chin to stay warm during the winter night.
The guest room was always prepared with a soft puff and extra pillows for visitors.

  • synonym: ottoman - a padded, upholstered seat without arms or a back, often used for sitting or as a footrest
  • synonym: quilt - bedding made of two layers of fabric filled with padding and stitched together
