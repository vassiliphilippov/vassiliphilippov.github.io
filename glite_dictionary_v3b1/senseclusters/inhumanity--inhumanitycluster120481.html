inhumanity

noun
cruelty; lack of compassion
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.hjuˈmæn.ə.t̬i /; / ɪn.hjuˈmæn.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.hjuːˈmæn.ə.ti /; / ɪn.hjuːˈmæn.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
cruel behaviour or a very cruel act; great lack of compassion or humanity

Examples

Acts of inhumanity
History is filled with examples of inhumanity during times of war.
Many activists work tirelessly to fight against inhumanity in its various forms.
The inhumanity of their actions shocked everyone who heard the story.
He was shocked by the sheer inhumanity of the attack on civilians.
The reports documented the inhumanity inflicted upon prisoners during the war.
Throughout history, acts of inhumanity have been condemned by society.

  • synonym: cruelty - callousness and causing pain to others
  • antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
