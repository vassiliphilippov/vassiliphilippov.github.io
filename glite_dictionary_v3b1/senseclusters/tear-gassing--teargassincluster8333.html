tear-gassing

verb
to use tear gas
6.4
Pronunciation: /tɪər ˈgæs.ɪŋ/ /; / /teər ˈgæs.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /tɪə ˈgæs.ɪŋ/ /; / /teə ˈgæs.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
to use tear gas on people or in an area, usually to disperse a crowd or control a protest

Examples

To tear-gas a crowd
The police started tear-gassing the crowd when the protest became violent.
Authorities were criticized for tear-gassing peaceful demonstrators in the city center.
After several warnings, officers began tear-gassing the area to clear the streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disperse - to cause a group of people to separate and go in different directions
  • synonym: gas - to attack or control people using gas
