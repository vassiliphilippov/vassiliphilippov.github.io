Iguanidae

noun
family of New World lizards (iguanas)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈɡwɑːnɪˌdiː /; / ɪˌɡwɑːnɪˈdiː / (AmE), / ɪˈɡwɑːnɪˌdiː /; / ɪˌɡwɑːnɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a family of New World lizards that includes iguanas and their close relatives, mainly found in Central and South America
