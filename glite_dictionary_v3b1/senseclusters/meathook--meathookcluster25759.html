meathook

noun
butcher's hook
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːthʊk / (AmE), / ˈmiːthʊk / (BrE)
a large, strong hook used for hanging meat, especially in a butcher's shop or slaughterhouse

Examples

A meathook in the butcher's shop
The butcher hung the fresh beef on a meathook in the cold storage room.
She was startled to see several large meathooks hanging from the ceiling of the old shop.
Each meathook was carefully cleaned after use to maintain hygiene standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hook - a curved piece of metal or other material used for hanging things
