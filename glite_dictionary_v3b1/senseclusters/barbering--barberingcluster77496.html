barbering

noun
excessive animal grooming
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbərɪŋ / (BrE)
the removal of hair or whiskers from other animals by excessive grooming, especially observed in laboratory rodents

Examples

Animal barbering
The researcher observed barbering in several cages, with mice missing patches of fur.
Veterinarians study barbering to understand stress behaviors in laboratory animals.
Signs of barbering include bald spots and shortened whiskers on affected rodents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grooming - the act of cleaning or maintaining the appearance of oneself or another animal
