heterophobia

noun
fear of difference
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtərəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
fear or strong dislike of people or things that are different or unfamiliar

Examples

Heterophobia towards outsiders
The psychologist discussed the impact of heterophobia on social integration.
Some communities struggle with heterophobia when faced with unfamiliar cultures.
Educational programs aim to reduce heterophobia by promoting understanding and acceptance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homophobia - fear or dislike of homosexual people
  • synonym: xenophobia - fear or dislike of foreigners or strangers
