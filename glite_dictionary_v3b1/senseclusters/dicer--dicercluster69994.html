dicer

noun
mechanical kitchen device for dicing food
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪsər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪsə / (BrE)
a mechanical device used to cut food into small cubes or pieces

Examples

Vegetable dicer
He bought a new dicer to replace his old, broken one.
She used a dicer to quickly prepare vegetables for the salad.
The kitchen was equipped with a professional dicer that made food prep easier.
