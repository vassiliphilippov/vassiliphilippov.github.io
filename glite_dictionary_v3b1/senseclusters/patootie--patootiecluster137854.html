patootie

noun
affectionate nickname
Pronunciation: / pəˈtuːti / (AmE), / pəˈtuːti / (BrE)
a playful or affectionate term for a person's buttocks or for a cute or attractive person

Examples

Cute patootie
She jokingly called her baby sister a little patootie.
He slipped and landed right on his patootie during the game.
Everyone at the party thought the puppy was such a patootie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cutie - an attractive or endearing person
  • synonym: sweetie - a person regarded with affection
