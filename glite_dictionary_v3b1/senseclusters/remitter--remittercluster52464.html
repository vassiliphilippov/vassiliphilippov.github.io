remitter

noun
person sending payment
5.7
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈmɪtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that sends money or makes a payment, especially by bank transfer or mail

Examples

Remitter of funds
The remitter must provide identification when transferring funds overseas.
Banks require the remitter to fill out a form before sending money abroad.
If the remitter enters the wrong account number, the payment may not reach the recipient.
The remitter must provide accurate account details to ensure the funds are transferred correctly.
Banks often verify the identity of the remitter before processing international payments.
If the remitter enters the wrong information, the money may not reach the intended recipient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: payer - a person who pays money
  • antonym: recipient - a person who receives something
