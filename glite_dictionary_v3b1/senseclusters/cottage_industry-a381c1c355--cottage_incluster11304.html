cottage industry

noun
small-scale home-based industry
5.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.tɪdʒ ˈɪn.də.striː/ /; / /ˈkɑː.tə.dʒ ˈɪn.də.striː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒt.ɪdʒ ˈɪn.də.striː/ /; / /ˈkɒt.ə.dʒ ˈɪn.də.striː/ (BrE)
A small-scale business or manufacturing activity carried out at home by family members using their own equipment.

Examples

A cottage industry business
Before the rise of factories, many families relied on the cottage industry to produce textiles and goods.
Handmade soaps are just one example of a successful cottage industry in the area.
With the increase in remote work, the cottage industry is seeing a resurgence in some communities.
