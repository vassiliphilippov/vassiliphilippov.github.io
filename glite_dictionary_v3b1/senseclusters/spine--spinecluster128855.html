spine

noun
narrow edge of a book's cover binding the pages
4.6 1.74/m
Pronunciation: / spaÉªn / (AmE), / spaÉªn / (BrE)
the part of a book's cover that encloses the bound edge of its pages and displays information such as the title and author when the book is shelved upright

Examples

Book spine
She wrote her name in ink on the spine of her textbook so it wouldn't be lost.
The spine of this old novel is cracked and barely holds the pages together.
When organizing his library, he arranged the books so the titles on each spine lined up neatly.
