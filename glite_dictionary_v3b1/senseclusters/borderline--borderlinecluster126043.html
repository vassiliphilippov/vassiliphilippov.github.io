borderline

noun
a boundary line
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːrdərˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdəˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a line marking the boundary between two places, areas, or countries

Examples

National borderline
The dispute was about where the exact borderline lay between the two provinces.
The river acts as a natural borderline between the two countries.
They planted trees along the borderline to clearly mark their property.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - the edge or boundary of something, or the dividing line between two areas
  • synonym: boundary - a real or imaginary line that marks the edge of something
