silver medal

noun
medal for second place in competition
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvɚ ˈmɛdəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈmɛdəl / (BrE)
a medal made of silver or resembling silver, awarded to the second place finisher in a competition or event

Examples

A silver medal
He narrowly missed first place but still took home a silver medal.
She proudly displayed her silver medal after finishing second in the race.
Winning a silver medal at the Olympics is a remarkable achievement.
