stage

noun
scene or situation for events
3.9
Pronunciation: / steɪdʒ / (AmE), / steɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place or situation regarded as a setting for events or actions, especially in a metaphorical sense

Examples

World as a stage
All the world's a stage, and we each play our part.
The invention of the internet set the stage for a new era of communication.
The summit provided a stage for leaders to discuss global challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backdrop - The setting or background for an event or situation
  • synonym: scene - The place where an action or event occurs
