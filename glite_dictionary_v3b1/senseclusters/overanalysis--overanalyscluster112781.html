overanalysis

noun
excessive examination
7.1
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.rə.ˈnæl.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
the act of thinking about or examining something too much or in excessive detail, often making it more complicated than necessary

Examples

An overanalysis of details
His overanalysis of the situation led to unnecessary worry and confusion.
Sometimes overanalysis can prevent you from making quick and effective decisions.
The team's overanalysis of the data delayed the start of the project.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: analysis - the process of examining something in detail
  • antonym: neglect - failure to give proper attention or consideration
  • synonym: overthinking - thinking about something too much or for too long
