acclimatize

verb
adapt to a new climate or environment
Pronunciation: / əˈklaɪ.məˌtaɪz /; / əˈklaɪməˌtaɪz / (AmE), / əˈklaɪ.mə.taɪz /; / əˈklaɪmətaɪz / (BrE)
to adapt or become accustomed to a new climate or environment

Examples

To acclimatize to weather
After a few weeks, he finally acclimatized to the humid weather.
It can take several days to acclimatize to the high altitude of the mountains.
New employees need time to acclimatize to the company's culture and routines.
After a few days, the team began to acclimatise to the high altitude.
It took her months to fully acclimatise to life in the tropics.
Plants may struggle to acclimatise when moved to a very different environment.
