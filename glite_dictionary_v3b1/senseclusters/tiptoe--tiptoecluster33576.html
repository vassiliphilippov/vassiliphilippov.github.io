tiptoe

noun
front of foot raised
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɪptəʊ / (BrE)
the front part of the foot, used when walking quietly or reaching for something high by raising the heels.

Examples

On your tiptoes
She reached the top shelf by standing on her tiptoes.
The children walked on tiptoe so they would not wake the baby.
He balanced carefully on tiptoe to peek over the fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ball of the foot - the rounded part of the foot just behind the toes
  • antonym: heel - the back part of the foot below the ankle
