territorialize

verb
to extend by adding territory
4.2 7.7
Pronunciation: /ter.ɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /ter.ɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.əl.aɪz/ (AmE), /ter.ɪ.ˈtɒr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /ter.ɪ.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to make something into a territory or organize it based on territorial divisions, especially for political or administrative purposes

Related Terms

  • synonym: annex - to add territory to one's own territory by appropriation
  • antonym: deterritorialize - to remove from a territory or reverse territorial organization
  • synonym: regionalize - organize into regions rather than territories
