transhumanism

noun
enhancing humans with technology
Pronunciation: / trænzˈhjuːmənɪzəm / (AmE), / trænzˈhjuːmənɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or movement that supports using advanced technology to enhance or evolve human abilities and extend lifespan
