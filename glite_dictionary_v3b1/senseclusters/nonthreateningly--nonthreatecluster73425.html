nonthreateningly

adverb
without seeming dangerous
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈθrɛtənɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that does not appear dangerous or likely to cause harm

Examples

To smile nonthreateningly
The teacher smiled nonthreateningly to help the nervous student feel at ease.
He spoke nonthreateningly, making sure not to alarm anyone in the room.
The animal approached the group nonthreateningly, showing it meant no harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmlessly - in a way that causes no harm
  • antonym: menacingly - in a way that seems threatening or dangerous
