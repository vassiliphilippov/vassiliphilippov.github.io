unconscious

noun
part of the mind outside awareness
7.8 3.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈkɑːn.ʃəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈkɒn.ʃəs/ (BrE)
the part of the mind that contains thoughts, feelings, and memories of which a person is not aware

Examples

Unconscious mind
Dreams are often thought to reveal hidden desires from the unconscious mind.
Fears and anxieties can sometimes be rooted deep within the unconscious.
Some psychologists believe that childhood experiences remain buried in the unconscious.
