quadruple

verb
increase to four times as much
5.4
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdruːpəl / (AmE), / kwɒˈdruːpəl / (BrE)
to become or make four times as great or as many; to increase fourfold

Examples

To quadruple profits
Home prices in that region quadrupled between 2000 and 2010.
If you quadruple the recipe, you'll have enough for the entire party.
The company's profits quadrupled in just two years.
