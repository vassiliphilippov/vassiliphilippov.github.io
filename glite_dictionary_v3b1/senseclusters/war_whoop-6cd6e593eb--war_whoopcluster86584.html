war whoop

noun
battle cry; loud yell in warfare
Pronunciation: / wɔɹ ˌwuːp / (AmE), / wɔː ˌwuːp / (BrE)
A loud yell or cry, especially one uttered by warriors to rally troops or intimidate the enemy.

Examples

A war whoop yell
Suddenly, a war whoop rang out, signaling the attack had begun.
The warriors let out a piercing war whoop as they charged toward the enemy.
With each war whoop, the soldiers' courage seemed to multiply on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle cry - a cry or shout uttered by soldiers as they go into battle
