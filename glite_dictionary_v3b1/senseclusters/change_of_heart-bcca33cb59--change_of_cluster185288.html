change of heart

noun
reversal of opinion or feelings
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv hɑrt / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒ əv hɑːt / (BrE)
a complete change in one's opinion, feelings, or intentions about something

Examples

A change of heart
After a long discussion, she had a change of heart and decided to forgive him.
He experienced a change of heart just before signing the contract.
The manager's change of heart took everyone by surprise at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about-face - a sudden and complete change of opinion or behavior
  • synonym: change of mind - an act of changing one's decision or opinion
  • antonym: resolve - firm determination to do something (opposite of a reversal)
