knurled

adjective
with ridged surface
8.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / n…úrld / (AmE), / n…úÀêld / (BrE)
having a surface covered with small ridges or bumps, typically to improve grip

Examples

A knurled handle
The screwdriver has a knurled handle to prevent slipping during use.
She gripped the knurled knob and turned it easily, even with wet hands.
Many metal tools feature knurled surfaces for better control and safety.

Synonyms

  • reeded
    7.2 0.00/m

  • ridged
    5.0 0.09/m
    ridged has one or more raised bands that can be large or decorative, while knurled means many small bumps for a firm grip.
