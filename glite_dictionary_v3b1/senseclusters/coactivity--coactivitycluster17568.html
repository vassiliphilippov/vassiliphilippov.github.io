coactivity

noun
joint or simultaneous action
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˌkəʊækˈtɪvəti / (BrE)
the state or process of acting together or at the same time, especially in a coordinated way

Examples

The coactivity of regions
The coactivity of different brain regions is essential for complex thought processes.
Successful projects often depend on the coactivity of team members from various departments.
Scientists measured the coactivity of muscles during coordinated movement tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others to achieve a goal
  • synonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common purpose
  • antonym: independence - the state of acting alone or separately
