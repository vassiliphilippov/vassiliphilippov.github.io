long plane

noun
carpenter's hand tool for edging boards
2.3 8.1
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ pleɪn / (AmE), / lɒŋ pleɪn / (BrE)
a long carpenter's hand tool used to shape and flatten the edges of boards so they fit together smoothly

Examples

A long plane tool
A long plane is essential when joining two pieces of wood seamlessly.
Before gluing the boards, he carefully worked them with a long plane for a perfect fit.
The carpenter used a long plane to smooth the edge of the oak board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jointer plane - a similar long carpenter's plane for jointing edges
