legal blindness

noun
vision severely limited by legal definition
4.8 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈliːɡəl ˈblaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl ˈblaɪndnəs / (BrE)
Visual impairment defined by law, usually as vision of 20/200 or worse in the better eye with the best possible correction, or a severely limited field of vision.
