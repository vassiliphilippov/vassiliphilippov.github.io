seriously folks

x
expression to signal earnestness or seriousness
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəsli foʊks / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəsli fəʊks / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate that the speaker is shifting to a more serious topic or is emphasizing sincerity, often following a humorous or light remark.

Examples

Seriously folks listen up
Seriously folks, let's focus on the main point of the discussion now.
The show was fun, but seriously folks, please drive safely on your way home.
You might laugh, but seriously folks, climate change is a real threat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seriously - in a serious manner; earnestly
