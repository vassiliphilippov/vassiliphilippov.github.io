jury-rigged

adjective
made or fixed with improvised materials
8.1 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːi rɪɡd / (AmE), / ˈdʒʊəri rɪɡd / (BrE)
built or repaired in a makeshift way using whatever materials are available, often temporarily and not professionally done

Examples

A jury-rigged repair
Facing an urgent deadline, the team put together a jury-rigged solution that worked just well enough.
The broken antenna was replaced with a jury-rigged wire to restore the radio signal temporarily.
They made a jury-rigged shelter from tree branches and plastic sheets after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvised - created and performed or made with whatever materials are immediately available
  • synonym: makeshift - used as a temporary or substitute solution
