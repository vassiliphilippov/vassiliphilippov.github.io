machining center

noun
automated CNC milling machine for manufacturing
1.5 7.2
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈʃiː.nɪŋ ˈsen.tər/ (AmE), /mə.ˈʃiː.nɪŋ ˈsen.tə/ (BrE)
A computer-controlled milling machine equipped with automatic tool changers, tool magazines or carousels, coolant systems, enclosures, and other features, used for precision manufacturing and metalworking.

Examples

A machining center machine
Our company invested in several machining centers to handle complex parts for the aerospace industry.
Skilled operators are needed to program and maintain each machining center on the assembly line.
The new factory was equipped with a state-of-the-art machining center to improve production efficiency.

  • synonym: milling machine - a machine tool used to shape solid materials by cutting
