consider

verb
regard as; view in a particular way
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɪdɚ / (AmE), / kənˈsɪdə / (BrE)
to think of or regard someone or something in a particular way; to have an opinion about

Examples

To consider someone important
Do you consider this behavior acceptable in the workplace?

The committee considered his proposal as an important step forward for the project.
He is generally considered a reliable expert in his field.
Many people consider her one of the best painters of our time.
The committee will consider your proposal carefully before making a decision.
