nonattainment

noun
failure to meet standard
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.əˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.əˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the state of not meeting a required standard, especially in relation to environmental or air quality regulations

Examples

Nonattainment of air quality
The region was classified as a nonattainment area due to high levels of air pollution.
New regulations were introduced to help cities move out of nonattainment status.
Industries in nonattainment zones must take extra steps to reduce emissions.

  • synonym: violation - the act of breaking or failing to comply with a rule or standard
