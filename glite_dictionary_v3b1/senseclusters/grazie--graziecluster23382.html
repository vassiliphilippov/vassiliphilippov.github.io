grazie

x
Italian for thank you
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɑːtsi.eɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡrat.si.eɪ / (BrE)
an Italian word meaning 'thank you', sometimes used in English conversation to express gratitude

Examples

grazie for dinner
After the meal, he looked at the chef and said grazie with a smile.
She handed me the book I had dropped, and I replied, 'grazie!'
Tourists often say grazie when visiting Italian restaurants abroad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thank you - a polite expression of gratitude
  • synonym: thanks - an informal way to express gratitude
