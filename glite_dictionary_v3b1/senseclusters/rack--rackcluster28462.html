rack

noun
rib section of veal, pork, lamb, or mutton
Pronunciation: / ræk / (AmE), / ræk / (BrE)
a rib section of the forequarter of veal, pork, lamb, or mutton, often including several ribs and prepared as a cut of meat

Examples

Rack of lamb
At the butcher, she ordered a rack of veal for the special occasion.
He seasoned the rack of pork before roasting it in the oven.
The chef prepared a succulent rack of lamb for the dinner party.
