one-sidedly

adverb
in a manner involving only one side
Pronunciation: / wʌnˈsaɪdɪdli / (AmE), / wʌnˈsaɪdɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that involves or favors only one side, person, or group.

Examples

To think one-sidedly
If we approach the problem one-sidedly, we may overlook important solutions.
The journalist reported one-sidedly on the issue, ignoring key facts from the other perspective.
The referee was accused of judging the match one-sidedly, favoring only the home team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unilaterally - in a way that is done by or affects only one side, person, or group
