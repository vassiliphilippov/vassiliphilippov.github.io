(in IT and customer support) to create or log a ticket in an issue-tracking or service-desk system for a bug, request, or task so it can be tracked and resolved.

Examples

Support issues a ticket
When a user reports a crash, the support team will issue a ticket and start investigating the problem.
She issued a ticket yesterday after finding a security vulnerability in the web application.
Our SLA requires us to begin work within four hours of issuing a ticket for a critical outage.
