ontogenetically

adverb
relating to individual development
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑntəˌdʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɒntəˌdʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the development of an individual organism from early stages to maturity

Examples

Develop ontogenetically
The immune system matures ontogenetically as the child grows.
Certain behaviors appear ontogenetically rather than being learned from others.
The disease manifests ontogenetically during adolescence in most cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: developmentally - in a way that relates to growth or development
  • antonym: phylogenetically - in a way that relates to the evolutionary development of a species
