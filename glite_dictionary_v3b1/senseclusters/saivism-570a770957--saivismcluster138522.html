Saivism

noun
Hindu tradition worshipping Shiva
Pronunciation: / ˈshaɪˌvɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪvɪzəm / (BrE)
one of the major traditions within Hinduism, centered on the worship of the god Shiva as the supreme being

Examples

Saivism worship of Shiva
He spent years studying Saivism and its philosophical texts.
Many temples across South India are dedicated to Saivism, reflecting its long history in the region.
Pilgrimages to sacred sites are an important aspect of Saivism for many followers.
