dragon

noun
small gliding Asian lizard
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡən / (AmE), / ˈdræɡən / (BrE)
a small tropical Asian lizard capable of gliding by spreading winglike membranes on each side of the body

Examples

Flying dragon lizard
Biologists discovered a new species of dragon in the dense forests of Borneo.
The dragon launched itself from the tree, gliding gracefully between branches.
Unlike other lizards, the dragon can escape predators by gliding through the air.
