spread-out

adjective
extended to cover a wide area
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛd ˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈsprɛd ˌaʊt / (BrE)
arranged or extended so as to cover a wide area or to resemble a fan

Examples

The spread-out feathers
She admired the spread-out feathers of the majestic peacock.
The reports were arranged in a spread-out fashion on the table.
We looked over the spread-out map to plan our journey.
