aristocratical

adjective
relating to nobility or aristocracy
Pronunciation: / əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌrɪstəˈkrætɪkəl / (BrE)
Belonging to, characteristic of, or typical of the nobility or aristocracy.

Examples

An aristocratical family
He grew up in an aristocratical household surrounded by traditional customs.
Her aristocratical manner set her apart from her peers.
The aristocratical style of the mansion impressed all who visited.

Related Terms
