Ernesto Guevara

proper noun
Argentine revolutionary and Cuban Revolution leader
Pronunciation: / ɛrˈnɛstoʊ ɡɛˈvɑrə / (AmE), / ɛˈnɛstəʊ ɡɛˈvɑːrə / (BrE)
(1928–1967) An Argentine Marxist revolutionary and major figure in the Cuban Revolution, known as Che Guevara; played key roles in Latin American revolutionary movements.
