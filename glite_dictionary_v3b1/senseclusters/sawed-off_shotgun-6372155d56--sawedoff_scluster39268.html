sawed-off shotgun

noun
shotgun with shortened barrels
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔd ɔf ˈʃɑtˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsɔːd ɒf ˈʃɒtɡʌn / (BrE)
A shotgun with shortened barrels, often used for increased portability and a wider shot spread at close range.

Examples

A sawed-off shotgun
He customized his weapon into a sawed-off shotgun for use in close-quarters combat.
The police found a sawed-off shotgun hidden under the seat of the suspect's car.
Using a sawed-off shotgun is illegal in many countries due to its increased risk and concealability.
