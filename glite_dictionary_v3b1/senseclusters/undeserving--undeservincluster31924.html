undeserving

adjective
not deserving of something
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈzɜrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈzɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not deserving of something, especially not deserving of help, reward, or sympathy.

Examples

Undeserving of kindness
He thought himself undeserving of such kindness from strangers.
Many felt the charity was wasting money on the undeserving rich.
The policy aims to distinguish between the deserving and the undeserving poor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deserving - worthy of being given something, especially help or praise
