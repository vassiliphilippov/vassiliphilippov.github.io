procrustean bed

noun
uniform standard ignoring individuality
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkrʌstiən bɛd / (AmE), / prəˈkrʌstiən bɛd / (BrE)
a rigid standard or system that ignores individual differences and forces conformity

Examples

A procrustean bed system
Critics argue that the new uniform policy is a procrustean bed stifling student creativity.
Imposing a procrustean bed of standardized testing can overlook individual learning needs.
Not all companies thrive under a procrustean bed of rigid corporate rules.

Related Terms

  • antonym: custom-made - made or tailored to individual order; allowing individuality
  • synonym: straightjacket - a constraint or restriction, often figurative
