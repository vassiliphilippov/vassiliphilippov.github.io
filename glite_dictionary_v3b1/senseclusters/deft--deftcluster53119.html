deft

adjective
skillful and quick; especially with the hands
6.0
Pronunciation: / dɛft / (AmE), / dɛft / (BrE)
Skillful and quick in one's movements, especially with the hands.

Examples

A deft hand
The artist moved the brush in deft strokes across the canvas.
The surgeon's deft hands made the operation look effortless.
With a deft flick of his wrist, he caught the falling glass.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement or lacking skill
  • synonym: dexterous - showing or having skill, especially with the hands
