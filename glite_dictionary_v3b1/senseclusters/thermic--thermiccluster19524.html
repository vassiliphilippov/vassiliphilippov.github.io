thermic

adjective
relating to heat
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜrmɪk / (AmE), / ˈθɜːmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by heat.

Examples

A thermic reaction
A thermic reaction releases energy in the form of heat.
Scientists measured the thermic effect of the new alloy under high temperatures.
The thermic properties of these materials make them suitable for insulation.

Related Terms
