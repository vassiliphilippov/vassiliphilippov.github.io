bungler

noun
an incompetent or clumsy person
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who frequently makes mistakes due to lack of skill or competence

Examples

A bungler at work
Don’t hand that delicate machinery to a bungler like Tom.
He was such a notorious bungler that no one trusted him with important tasks.
The new employee quickly gained a reputation as a bungler after several costly mistakes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botcher - a person who does work badly or makes mistakes
  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill
