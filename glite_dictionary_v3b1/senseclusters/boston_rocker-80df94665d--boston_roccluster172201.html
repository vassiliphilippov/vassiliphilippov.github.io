Boston rocker

noun
rocking chair with high spindle back and decorative top
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑstən ˈrɑkər / (AmE), / ˈbɒstən ˈrɒkə / (BrE)
a type of rocking chair with a high spindle back and a decorative top panel, often associated with 19th-century American design

Examples

A Boston rocker chair

The museum displayed a beautiful 19th-century Boston rocker among other period furniture.
