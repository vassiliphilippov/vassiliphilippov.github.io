defyingly

adverb
in a challenging way
8.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /də.ˈfaɪ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfaɪ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /də.ˈfaɪ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that resists, challenges, or boldly opposes authority, rules, or expectations

Examples

To act defyingly
The artist painted defyingly outside the accepted styles of her time.
The river flowed defyingly through the rocky landscape, refusing to be tamed.
He smiled defyingly at the strict rules, determined to do things his own way.
He stared defyingly at the teacher after being scolded for his behavior.
The protester raised her sign defyingly despite the warnings from the police.
She smiled defyingly as she refused to follow the unfair rule.
