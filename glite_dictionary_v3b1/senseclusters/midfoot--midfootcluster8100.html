midfoot

adjective
middle of foot
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌfʊt / (BrE)
relating to or located in the middle part of the foot, between the heel and the toes

Examples

Midfoot injury
The doctor examined the midfoot area for signs of swelling after the injury.
A fracture in the midfoot can make walking very painful and difficult.
Special insoles can help relieve midfoot discomfort caused by overuse.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forefoot - the front part of the foot near the toes
  • antonym: hindfoot - the back part of the foot near the heel
