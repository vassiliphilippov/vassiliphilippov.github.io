harlequin

verb
variegate with spots or marks
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrləˌkwɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːlɪkwɪn / (BrE)
to variegate or decorate with patches, spots, or marks of different colors

Examples

Harlequined with colors
After playing in the mud, the puppy's fur was harlequined with brown spots.
Large clouds harlequined the autumn sky with shifting patterns of light and shadow.
The artist harlequined the wall with bold, irregular patches of color.

  • synonym: spotted - marked with spots of color or light
