heat

noun
energy transferred due to temperature difference
Pronunciation: / hit / (AmE), / hiːt / (BrE)
a form of energy that is transferred between systems or objects with different temperatures, usually moving from the hotter to the cooler body

Examples

Heat transfer
Metal conducts heat more efficiently than wood.
The heat from the engine can be felt even several feet away.
When water boils, heat is transferred from the stove to the pot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temperature - the degree of hotness or coldness measured on a definite scale
