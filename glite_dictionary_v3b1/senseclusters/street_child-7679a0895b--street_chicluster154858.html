street child

noun
homeless or abandoned child living on the street
4.4 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / striÀêt t Éa…™ld / (AmE), / striÀêt t Éa…™ld / (BrE)
A homeless child, especially one who has been abandoned or orphaned and lives or works in the streets, often without adult supervision.

Examples

A street child alone

Many organizations are dedicated to helping street children find shelter and education.
The documentary tells the story of a street child struggling to survive in the city.
