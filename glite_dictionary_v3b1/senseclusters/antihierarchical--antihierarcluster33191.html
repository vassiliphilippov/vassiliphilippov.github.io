antihierarchical

adjective
opposed to hierarchy
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌhaɪəˈrɑːrkɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌhaɪəˈrɑːkɪkəl / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting hierarchical structures or systems

Examples

Antihierarchical organization
The company implemented an antihierarchical approach to encourage open communication among all employees.
Her antihierarchical beliefs led her to support flat organizational structures.
Many modern startups prefer antihierarchical systems to promote creativity and collaboration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hierarchical - organized in a ranked or graded order
