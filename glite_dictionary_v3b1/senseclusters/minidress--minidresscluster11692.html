minidress

noun
very short dress
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌdrɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌdrɛs / (BrE)
a very short dress that usually ends well above the knees

Examples

A minidress style
She wore a sparkling minidress to the concert and received many compliments.
The new fashion collection features a bold yellow minidress perfect for summer parties.
In the 1960s, the minidress became a symbol of youth and freedom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maxidress - a long dress that reaches the ankles
  • synonym: miniskirt - a very short skirt ending well above the knees
