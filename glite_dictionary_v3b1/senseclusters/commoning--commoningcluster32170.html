commoning

noun
collective resource management
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of managing and sharing resources collectively by a community for mutual benefit

Examples

Commoning of land
The village practiced commoning to ensure everyone had access to water and grazing land.
Modern commoning initiatives include community-run libraries and tool-sharing programs.
Through commoning, neighbors worked together to maintain the local park for all residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communalism - the principle or practice of shared ownership and management by a community
  • antonym: privatization - the transfer of ownership or control from public or community to private hands
