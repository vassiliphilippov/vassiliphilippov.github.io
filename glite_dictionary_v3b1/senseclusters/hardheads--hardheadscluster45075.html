hardheads

noun
a perennial plant with purple flowers; black knapweed
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌhɛdz / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌhɛdz / (BrE)
A weedy perennial plant with tough, wiry stems and purple flowers, native to Europe but widely naturalized; also known as black knapweed (Centaurea nigra).

Examples

The hardheads plant
Gardeners often remove hardheads because they can crowd out native wildflowers.
She picked a handful of hardheads to add purple color to her bouquet.
The field was dotted with hardheads swaying in the summer breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black knapweed - another common name for the same plant species (Centaurea nigra)
