Examples

Centum languages branch
In comparative linguistics, Centum languages are contrasted with Satem languages due to their sound changes.
Latin is an example of a Centum language, preserving original velar consonants from Proto-Indo-European.
Tocharian, once spoken in Central Asia, is classified as a Centum language despite its eastern location.
