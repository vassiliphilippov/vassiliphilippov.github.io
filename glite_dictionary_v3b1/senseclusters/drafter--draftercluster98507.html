drafter

noun
technical drawing specialist
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftər / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftə / (BrE)
a person who creates detailed technical drawings or plans, especially for buildings, machines, or engineering projects

Examples

Technical drafter for buildings
The drafter worked closely with the engineers to finalize the bridge design.
As a skilled drafter, she produced accurate plans for the construction team.
Every drafter in the firm uses advanced software to create detailed blueprints.
The drafter created precise blueprints for the new office building.
As a skilled drafter, she worked closely with engineers to finalize the machine's design.
Many companies hire a drafter to prepare technical drawings for construction projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: architect - a person who designs buildings
  • synonym: draftsman - a person skilled in drawing plans or technical drawings
  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs and builds complex products or structures
