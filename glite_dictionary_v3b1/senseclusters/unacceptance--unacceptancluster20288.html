unacceptance

noun
lack of acceptance
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəkˈsɛptəns / (AmE), / ˌʌnəkˈsɛptəns / (BrE)
the state or condition of not being accepted or not accepting something

Examples

The unacceptance of change
The team's unacceptance of the new member made it difficult for her to fit in.
His unacceptance of criticism prevented him from improving his work.
Cultural differences sometimes lead to unacceptance in diverse communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptance - the act of agreeing to or approving something
  • synonym: rejection - the act of refusing to accept or approve
