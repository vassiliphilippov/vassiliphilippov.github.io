miffy

noun
Miffy character toy
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪfi / (AmE), / ˈmɪfi / (BrE)
a toy, picture, or other product representing the Miffy character, especially a small stuffed rabbit

Examples

A soft miffy
She gave her niece a soft miffy to cuddle when she started nursery.
The child carried his favorite miffy everywhere he went.
A collection of miffy toys was displayed on the shelf in her bedroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plush toy - a soft toy made of fabric and stuffed with a soft material
  • synonym: stuffed animal - a toy animal made from cloth and filled with soft material
