coconut meat

noun
the edible white flesh of a coconut
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkəˌnʌt ˌmiːt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkənʌt ˌmiːt / (BrE)
the firm, white, edible flesh found inside a coconut shell, commonly used in cooking and baking

Examples

Fresh coconut meat
Fresh coconut meat can be eaten as a snack or added to curries.
She sprinkled coconut meat on top of the cake for extra flavor.
They dried the coconut meat before using it in homemade granola.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coconut - the large, brown, hard-shelled fruit of the coconut palm
