cross-link

noun
digital cross-reference or hyperlink
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɔːs.lɪŋk/ /; / /ˈkrɑːs.lɪŋk/ (AmE), /ˈkrɒs.lɪŋk/ /; / /ˈkrɔːs.lɪŋk/ (BrE)
a link or cross-reference that connects two digital documents, web pages, or datasets so users or systems can navigate between or integrate them.
