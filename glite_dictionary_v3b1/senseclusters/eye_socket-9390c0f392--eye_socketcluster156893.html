eye socket

noun
bony cavity containing the eyeball
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ ˌsɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌsɒkɪt / (BrE)
the bony cavity in the skull in which the eyeball sits

Examples

The eye socket bone
A blow to the face fractured his eye socket.
She wore sunglasses to hide the bruise around her eye socket.
The doctor examined the injury near his eye socket carefully.
