objectivize

verb
to treat as object
Pronunciation: / əbˈdʒɛktɪˌvaɪz / (AmE), / əbˈdʒɛktɪvaɪz / (BrE)
to treat someone or something as an object, ignoring their individuality or humanity

Examples

To objectivize people
The documentary explores how advertising can objectivize people by focusing only on their appearance.
Critics argue that certain films objectivize women, reducing them to mere symbols.
Teachers should avoid language that might objectivize students or ignore their unique perspectives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dehumanize - to deprive someone of human qualities or dignity
  • antonym: respect - to treat someone with consideration and honor
