air lane

noun
designated route for airplanes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌleɪn / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌleɪn / (BrE)
a designated route that airplanes follow between airports

Examples

The air lane route
Pilots must stay within the air lane specified in their flight plan.
Several planes share the same air lane on the route from New York to London.
Strict regulations govern traffic in each air lane to ensure safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airway - a designated route in the sky used by aircraft
