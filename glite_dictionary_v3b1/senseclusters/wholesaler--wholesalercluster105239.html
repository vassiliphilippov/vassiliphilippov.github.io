wholesaler

noun
person or business selling goods in bulk to retailers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊlˌseɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊlˌseɪlə / (BrE)
a person or company that buys goods in large quantities from producers or importers and sells them to retailers, not directly to the public

Examples

The wholesaler sells goods
After the manufacturer, the wholesaler is the next step in the supply chain.
The wholesaler supplied hundreds of stores with fresh fruit every week.
We buy our products from a local wholesaler to get better prices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retailer - a person or business that sells goods to the public in relatively small quantities
