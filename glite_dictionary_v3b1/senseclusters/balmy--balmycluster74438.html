balmy

adjective
pleasantly warm and mild (weather)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈbɑːmi / (BrE)
(of weather) pleasantly warm and mild.

Examples

Balmy summer night
After a week of rain, the balmy weather was a welcome change.
The city is known for its balmy climate year-round, attracting tourists from colder regions.
We enjoyed several balmy evenings on the beach during our vacation.
