block

noun
city area surrounded by streets
Pronunciation: / blɑk / (AmE), / blɒk / (BrE)
A rectangular area in a city surrounded by streets, usually containing several buildings.

Examples

A city block
She walked three blocks to reach the nearest store.
The parade will pass through every block in the downtown area.
Their apartment is in the next block over from the school.
