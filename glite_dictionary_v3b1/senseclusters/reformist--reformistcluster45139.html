reformist

adjective
supporting reform or change
1.0 5.3
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfɔːr.mɪst/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈfɔː.mɪst/ (BrE)

Examples

Reformist policies

Many reformist politicians push for changes to improve social welfare systems.
The government's reformist agenda met with both support and resistance from voters.
