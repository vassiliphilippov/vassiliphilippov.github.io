banana

noun
long curved yellow fruit
1.1
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
A long, curved fruit with yellow skin and soft, sweet flesh, growing in clusters on tropical plants.

Examples

A ripe banana
Monkeys are often shown eating a banana in cartoons.
She peeled a banana for her breakfast smoothie.
The banana ripened quickly in the warm kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plantain - a starchy banana variety, often cooked rather than eaten raw
