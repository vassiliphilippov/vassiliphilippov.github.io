fall-flowering

adjective
blooming in autumn (of a plant)
8.6
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈflaʊɚɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈflaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) producing flowers in the autumn season.

Examples

Fall-flowering plants
A fall-flowering crocus adds a splash of color to autumn borders.
Gardeners often look for fall-flowering species to extend the blooming season.
The fall-flowering chrysanthemums brighten up the garden when most other plants have faded.
