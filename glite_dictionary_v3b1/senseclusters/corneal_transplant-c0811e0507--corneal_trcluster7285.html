corneal transplant

noun
surgery to replace corneal tissue
Pronunciation: / kɔrniəl ˈtrænsplænt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪəl ˈtrænsplɑːnt / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which part or all of a damaged or diseased cornea is replaced by healthy corneal tissue from a donor

Examples

Corneal transplant surgery
Advances in medicine have improved the success rate of corneal transplant procedures.
After her accident, a corneal transplant was the only way to restore her vision.
The patient was placed on a waiting list for a corneal transplant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keratoplasty - surgical repair or replacement of the cornea
