common cold

noun
mild viral infection of nose and throat
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən koʊld / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən kəʊld / (BrE)
a mild viral infection of the nose and throat, causing a runny nose, sneezing, and sore throat; does not affect the lungs

Examples

Catch a common cold
Many people catch a common cold several times each year, especially during winter.
She stayed home from work to recover from a common cold.
There is no effective vaccine against the common cold yet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - an illness causing sneezing and a runny nose
