kinetically

adverb
relating to reaction rates
Pronunciation: / kɪˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / kɪˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the rate or speed of a chemical or physical process, rather than its final state.

Examples

React kinetically faster
The reaction proceeds kinetically faster at higher temperatures, even if the final product is unchanged.
Some products are kinetically favored but not the most stable in the long run.
Scientists studied how the process changed when it was controlled kinetically rather than by equilibrium.

Related Terms

  • antonym: thermodynamically - in a way relating to the energy and equilibrium of a process
