associator

noun
one who connects
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊsiˌeɪtər / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiˌeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that connects or joins with others or forms associations

Examples

Associator of ideas
As an associator, she brought people from different backgrounds together.
The new manager acted as an associator to help the teams collaborate more effectively.
In group projects, an associator often helps everyone stay connected and focused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connector - a person or thing that links or joins others
  • antonym: isolator - a person or thing that separates or keeps apart
  • synonym: unifier - someone who brings people or things together
