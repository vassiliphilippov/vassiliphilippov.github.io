kleptocrat

noun
corrupt leader
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛptəˌkræt / (AmE), / ˈklɛptəkræt / (BrE)
a person in power, especially a government official or ruler, who uses their position to steal money or resources for personal gain

Examples

A kleptocrat in power
The country suffered under a kleptocrat who enriched himself at the expense of the people.
Journalists exposed the kleptocrat's secret bank accounts filled with stolen public funds.
Many citizens protested against the kleptocrat and demanded honest leadership.
The country was ruled for decades by a kleptocrat who drained its wealth.
Many citizens protested against the kleptocrat and his corrupt government.
International organizations imposed sanctions on the kleptocrat for stealing public funds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrupt official - a person in authority who acts dishonestly for personal benefit
  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power, often ruling oppressively
  • synonym: embezzler - someone who steals money they are responsible for
  • antonym: reformer - a person who works to change and improve a system
