micropaleontology

noun
study of microfossils in paleontology
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˌpeɪ.li.ɑnˈtɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˌpæ.lɪ.ɒnˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The branch of paleontology that studies microfossils, which are fossils of microscopic organisms.

Examples

Micropaleontology studies microfossils
Advances in micropaleontology have helped researchers understand ancient climate changes.

She published a groundbreaking paper in micropaleontology focusing on the diversity of foraminifera.
