tempestuous relationship

noun
volatile, stormy relationship
4.8
Pronunciation: / tɛmˈpɛs.tʃu.əs rɪˈleɪ.ʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / tɛmˈpɛs.tjʊ.əs rɪˈleɪ.ʃənˌʃɪp / (BrE)
a relationship marked by frequent conflict, intense emotions, and volatility

Examples

A tempestuous relationship with conflict
After years of a tempestuous relationship, they finally decided to part ways.
Despite their deep connection, the couple's tempestuous relationship often left them emotionally exhausted.
Their tempestuous relationship was the talk of the office due to its constant ups and downs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmonious relationship - relationship characterized by harmony and agreement
  • synonym: turbulent relationship - relationship involving disorder or conflict
