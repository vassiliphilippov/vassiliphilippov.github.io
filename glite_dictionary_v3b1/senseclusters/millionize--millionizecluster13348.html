millionize

verb
to reach a million
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪljənaɪz / (BrE)
to increase something so that it reaches a total of one million

Examples

To millionize donations
The charity hopes to millionize its donations by the end of the year.
They set an ambitious goal to millionize their social media followers within twelve months.
The company plans to millionize its customer base through a new marketing strategy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reduce - to make something smaller in amount or number
