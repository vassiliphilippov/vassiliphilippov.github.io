anhedral

adjective
irregular crystal or wing angle
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.ˈhe.drəl/ /; / /ə.ˈhe.drəl/ (AmE), /æn.ˈhe.drəl/ /; / /ə.ˈhe.drəl/ (BrE)
lacking well-formed crystal faces, or having a downward angle of an aircraft wing relative to the horizontal

Examples

Aircraft designers sometimes use anhedral wings to improve roll performance.
