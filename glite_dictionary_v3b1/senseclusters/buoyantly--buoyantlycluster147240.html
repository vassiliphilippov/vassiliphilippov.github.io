buoyantly

adverb
in a cheerful or lively manner
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.ənt.li / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.ənt.li / (BrE)

Examples

To move buoyantly

She floated buoyantly into the meeting, full of positive energy and fresh ideas.
