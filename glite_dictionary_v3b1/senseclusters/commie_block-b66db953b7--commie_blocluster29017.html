commie block

noun
utilitarian communist-era apartment building
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmi blɑk / (AmE), / ˈkɒmi blɒk / (BrE)
A large, utilitarian apartment building, typically associated with communist-era architecture, characterized by a stark, functional design.

Examples

A commie block building
Many immigrants were first housed in a commie block after arriving in the country.
My grandparents still live in a commie block on the outskirts of the city.
The new film features scenes set inside a dilapidated commie block.
