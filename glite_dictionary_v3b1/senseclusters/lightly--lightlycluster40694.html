lightly

adverb
in moderation; with restraint
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtli / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtli / (BrE)
In a way that involves only a small amount of food, drink, or indulgence; with moderation or temperance.

Examples

Eat lightly
Doctors recommend eating lightly before intense exercise.
During the holidays, he tried to drink lightly despite the many parties.
She prefers to eat lightly in the evenings to avoid feeling too full.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moderately - in a moderate way; not excessively
