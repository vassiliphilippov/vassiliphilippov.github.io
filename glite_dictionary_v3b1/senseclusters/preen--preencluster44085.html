preen

verb
clean and arrange feathers with the beak (bird)
8.6
Pronunciation: / priːn / (AmE), / priːn / (BrE)
(of a bird) to clean and arrange its feathers with its beak.

Examples

A bird preens feathers
The parrot sat on its perch and preened for hours.
The starling preened its wings after a bath.
Water birds often preen their feathers to keep them waterproof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groom - to clean or maintain the appearance of (oneself or another animal)
