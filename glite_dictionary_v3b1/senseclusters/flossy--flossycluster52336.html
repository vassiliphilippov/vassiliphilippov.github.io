flossy

adjective
soft and down-like in texture
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑsi / (AmE), / ˈflɒsi / (BrE)
having a soft, fluffy, or down-like texture; resembling floss or down

Examples

Flossy sweater
She wore a sweater so flossy that it felt like a gentle cloud.
The flossy texture of the blanket was perfect for a cold night.
The chick's flossy feathers made it feel incredibly delicate in my hands.
