subplate

noun
secondary underlying plate
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a secondary plate or layer located beneath a main plate in a structure or system

Examples

Metal subplate
The technician carefully removed the subplate to access the wiring underneath.
A damaged subplate can compromise the stability of the entire assembly.
Engineers designed the subplate to support the main structural components.

Related Terms

  • antonym: main plate - the primary plate in a structure or system
  • synonym: substrate - a layer that underlies something else
