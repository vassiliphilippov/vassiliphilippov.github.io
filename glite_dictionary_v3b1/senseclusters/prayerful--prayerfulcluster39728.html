prayerful

adjective
given to praying; devout
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛr.fəl / (AmE), / ˈpreə.fəl / (BrE)
given to or characterized by prayer; showing or expressing reverence or devotion in prayer

Examples

A prayerful attitude
His prayerful attitude inspired those around him during the difficult time.
She sat in a prayerful silence, her hands folded in her lap.
The church was filled with a prayerful atmosphere as the congregation gathered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devout - deeply religious or pious
  • antonym: irreverent - showing a lack of respect for things generally taken seriously, especially religion
