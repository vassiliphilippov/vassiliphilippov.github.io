Synonyms

  • pious
    6.3 3.49/m
    pious expresses steady devotion and religious respect in daily life, while prayerful focuses on regular, active prayer and private reverence.
  • devout
    5.2 0.32/m
    devout is deeply religious and shows strong faith in beliefs, while prayerful is shown by someone who spends time praying.
  • godly
    5.1 1.32/m

  • worshipful
    7.1 0.31/m
    worshipful expresses strong adoration and public praise, while prayerful focuses on private, ongoing prayer and quiet reverence.
