airwash

noun
cleaning with air
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈeəˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
a process that uses a stream of air to clean or remove particles from a surface or object

Examples

An airwash process
The technician used an airwash to remove dust from the computer components.
Many modern fireplaces include an airwash system to keep the glass clean.
Regular airwash maintenance helps extend the life of industrial filters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow-off - a method of cleaning by blowing air to remove dust or particles
  • antonym: wash - to clean something using water or another liquid
