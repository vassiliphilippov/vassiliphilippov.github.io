Ovimbundu

proper noun
Bantu ethnic group of western Angola
Pronunciation: / oʊˌvɪmˈbʊnduː / (AmE), / əʊˌvɪmˈbʊnduː / (BrE)
a Bantu ethnic group native to western Angola, traditionally speaking the Umbundu language
