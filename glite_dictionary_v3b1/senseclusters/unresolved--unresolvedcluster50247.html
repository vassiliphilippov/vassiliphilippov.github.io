unresolved

adjective
musical: dissonant or unresolved
4.7 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɑːlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a musical sound or chord) characterized by dissonance and lacking a final harmonic resolution.

Examples

Unresolved chord
Some jazz pieces deliberately leave progressions unresolved to evoke a sense of ambiguity.
The unresolved dissonance at the end leaves listeners wanting a clear conclusion.
The symphony's opening features an unresolved chord that creates tension and expectation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissonant - lacking harmony; clashing in sound, especially in music
  • antonym: resolved - (in music) having harmonic resolution; not dissonant
