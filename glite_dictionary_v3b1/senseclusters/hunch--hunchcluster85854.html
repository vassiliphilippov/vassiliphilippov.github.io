hunch

verb
bend forward with rounded shoulders
5.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /hʌntʃ/ (AmE), /hʌntʃ/ (BrE)
to bend forward and draw the shoulders forward, causing the back to round

Examples

To hunch your shoulders
He hunched over his desk, concentrating on the paperwork in front of him.
She hunched forward to get a better look at the tiny writing on the page.
The old man hunched his shoulders against the cold wind as he walked down the street.
