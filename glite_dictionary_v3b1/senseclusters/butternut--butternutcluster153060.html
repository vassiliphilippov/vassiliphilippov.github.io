butternut

noun
edible nut of the white walnut tree
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ərˌnʌt / (AmE), / ˈbʌtənʌt / (BrE)
a North American tree (Juglans cinerea), also called white walnut, or its edible nut or wood

Examples

The butternut tree produces nuts that are valued for their rich, oily flavor.
