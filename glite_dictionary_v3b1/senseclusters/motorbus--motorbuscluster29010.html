motorbus

noun
large passenger vehicle for public transport
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌbʌs / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəbʌs / (BrE)
a large road vehicle designed to carry many passengers, especially as part of a public transport service

Examples

A motorbus service
In the early 20th century, the motorbus replaced horse-drawn carriages in many towns.
She caught the morning motorbus to reach her office on time.
The motorbus stopped at every main street along its route through the city.
