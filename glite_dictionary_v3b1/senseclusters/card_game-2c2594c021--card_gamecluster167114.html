card game

noun
game played with playing cards
Pronunciation: / kɑrd ɡeɪm / (AmE), / kɑːd ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played with a set of playing cards, following specific rules and often involving skill, chance, or both.

Examples

A card game of skill
Bridge is a card game that requires skill, strategy, and cooperation.
Poker is a popular card game enjoyed around the world.
She invited her friends over for a night of card games and snacks.
