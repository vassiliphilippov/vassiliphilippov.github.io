Mount Pinatubo

proper noun
volcano in the Philippines, erupted in 1991
Pronunciation: / maʊnt pɪˈnɑːtuboʊ / (AmE), / maʊnt pɪˈnætʊbəʊ / (BrE)
a volcano located on Luzon island in the Philippines, northwest of Manila, notable for its major eruption in 1991 after centuries of dormancy

Examples

Mount Pinatubo eruption
Ash from Mount Pinatubo blanketed nearby towns and affected global weather patterns.
Scientists continue to study Mount Pinatubo to better understand volcanic eruptions and their impacts.
The eruption of Mount Pinatubo in 1991 was one of the largest volcanic events of the 20th century.
