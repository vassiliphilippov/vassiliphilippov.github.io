sack

noun
dismissal from employment
7.1
Pronunciation: / sæk / (AmE), / sæk / (BrE)
the act of dismissing someone from their job; termination of employment

Examples

He got the sack
After the restructuring, several employees got the sack without warning.
Many workers fear the sack during periods of economic downturn.
She was devastated to receive the sack after only six months at the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from employment
  • synonym: firing - the act of dismissing an employee from a job
  • antonym: hiring - the act of employing someone for a job
