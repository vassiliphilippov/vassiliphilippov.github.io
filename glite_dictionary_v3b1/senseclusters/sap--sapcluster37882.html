sap

verb
to weaken or deplete gradually
Pronunciation: / sæp / (AmE), / sap / (BrE)
to gradually weaken or deplete the strength, energy, or effectiveness of someone or something

Examples

To sap your energy
Constant criticism can sap your confidence over time.
Her illness has slowly sapped her strength, leaving her exhausted.
The long hours at work began to sap his enthusiasm for the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strengthen - make or become stronger
  • synonym: weaken - make or become less strong
