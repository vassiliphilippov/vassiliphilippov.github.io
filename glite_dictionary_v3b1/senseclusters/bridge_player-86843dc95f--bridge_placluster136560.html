bridge player

noun
person who plays bridge (card game)
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪdʒ ˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪdʒ ˌpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the card game bridge, either professionally or for recreation

Examples

Bridge player club
As a dedicated bridge player, he spends hours each week practicing with his club.
She is an experienced bridge player who has competed in tournaments worldwide.
They needed a fourth bridge player to start the game.
