inviter

noun
one who invites
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that invites or encourages others to go somewhere or do something

Examples

A friendly inviter
The colorful poster served as an effective inviter to the art exhibition.
As the inviter, she made sure all her guests felt welcome at the party.
The aroma of fresh bread was a strong inviter for customers passing by the bakery.
The inviter sent out colorful invitations to all her friends for the party.
As the inviter, he made sure everyone felt welcome at the event.
Each inviter was responsible for greeting their guests at the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attractor - something that draws attention or interest
  • antonym: guest - a person who is invited to an event or place
  • synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
