windsurfer

noun
person using sailboard
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌsɝːfər / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌsɜːfə / (BrE)
a person who rides on water using a board with an attached sail, controlling direction and speed by adjusting the sail

Examples

Skilled windsurfer
The experienced windsurfer caught the wind and sped across the bay.
As a beginner windsurfer, she practiced balancing on the board near the shore.
Dozens of windsurfers gathered at the lake for the annual competition.
The experienced windsurfer glided smoothly across the lake on a windy afternoon.
Many windsurfers gather at the beach each summer to enjoy the strong sea breeze.
She became a skilled windsurfer after taking lessons for just a few weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sailor - a person who operates or travels in a boat with sails
  • synonym: surfer - a person who rides on ocean waves using a surfboard
