Sudeten

proper noun
Central European mountains
Pronunciation: / suːˈdeɪtən / (AmE), / suːˈdeɪtən / (BrE)
a mountain range and region in Central Europe, mainly located in the Czech Republic and parts of Poland and Germany

Examples

The Sudeten mountains
The Sudeten are known for their scenic beauty and hiking trails.
Many tourists visit the Sudeten each year to enjoy winter sports.
The border between Poland and the Czech Republic runs through the Sudeten mountains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Alps - a large mountain range in Central Europe
  • synonym: Carpathians - a major mountain range in Central and Eastern Europe
