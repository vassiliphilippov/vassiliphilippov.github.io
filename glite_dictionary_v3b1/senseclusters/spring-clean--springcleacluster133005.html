spring-clean

verb
clean the whole house thoroughly
5.2
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈklin / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈkliːn / (BrE)
to thoroughly clean the entire house, typically carried out once a year, especially in spring

Examples

To spring-clean the house
Every March, we spring-clean the entire house from top to bottom.
She decided to spring-clean before the in-laws arrived for their visit.
They spent the whole weekend spring-cleaning and organizing the garage.
