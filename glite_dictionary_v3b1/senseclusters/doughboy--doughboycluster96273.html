doughboy

noun
deep-fried bread lump
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈdəʊˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a small rounded lump of dough that is deep-fried and served hot as bread

Examples

Hot doughboy with jam
At breakfast, locals often enjoy a doughboy with jam or honey.
She ordered a fresh doughboy dusted with sugar at the fair.
The aroma of a hot doughboy filled the kitchen as it was taken from the fryer.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: doughnut - a small, sweet fried cake of dough, typically ring-shaped
