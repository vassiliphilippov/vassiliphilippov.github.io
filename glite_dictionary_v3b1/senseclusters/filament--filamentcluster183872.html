filament

noun
slender stalk of a stamen
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪləmənt / (AmE), / ˈfɪləmənt / (BrE)
the slender stalk of a stamen in a flower, supporting the anther

Examples

Flower filament
Each stamen consists of a filament and an anther where pollen is produced.
The filament supports the anther at the top of the stamen.
The length of the filament affects how the flower disperses pollen.
