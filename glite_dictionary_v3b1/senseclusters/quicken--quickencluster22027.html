quicken

verb
make or become faster
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪkən / (AmE), / ˈkwɪkən / (BrE)
to make or become faster; to move more quickly

Examples

To quicken the pace
Her heartbeat quickened when she heard the unexpected news.
The pace of the runners began to quicken as they neared the finish line.
With the wind at its back, the boat seemed to quicken across the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accelerate - to begin to move more quickly
  • antonym: slow - to move at a slower speed
