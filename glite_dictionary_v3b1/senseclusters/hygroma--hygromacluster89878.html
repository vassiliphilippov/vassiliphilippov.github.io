hygroma

noun
congenital cystic swelling
Pronunciation: / haɪˈɡroʊmə / (AmE), / haɪˈɡrəʊmə / (BrE)
a swelling or cyst filled with fluid that can occur in different parts of the body, such as the neck, head, brain, or near a joint
