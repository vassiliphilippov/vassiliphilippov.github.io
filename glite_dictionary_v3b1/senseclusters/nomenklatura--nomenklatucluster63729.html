nomenklatura

noun
system of patronage in communist countries
9.5 9.8
Pronunciation: /nəʊ.meŋ.klə.ˈtʃʊ.rə/ (AmE), /nɒ.men.klə.ˈtʃʊə.rə/ /; / /nɒ.men.klə.ˈtjʊə.rə/ (BrE)
A system in communist countries whereby key positions in government and industry are filled by Communist Party appointees, managed through lists or committees.

Examples

Soviet nomenklatura system
Academic studies often analyze the role of the nomenklatura in sustaining Soviet political power.
Access to elite privileges was controlled by one's place within the nomenklatura.
After joining the Party, he was added to the nomenklatura and given a senior position in the ministry.
