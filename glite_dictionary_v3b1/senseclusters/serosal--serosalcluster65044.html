serosal

adjective
of serous membranes
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈroʊzəl / (AmE), / sɪˈrəʊzəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a serous membrane that lines certain internal cavities of the body

Examples

Serosal membrane layer
The doctor noted inflammation on the serosal layer of the patient's stomach.
During surgery, the serosal surface of the organ was carefully inspected for any abnormalities.
A tear in the serosal membrane can lead to serious complications if not treated promptly.
