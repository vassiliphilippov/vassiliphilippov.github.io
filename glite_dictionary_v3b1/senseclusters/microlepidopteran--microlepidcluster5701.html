microlepidopteran

adjective
about tiny moths
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of microlepidoptera, a group of very small moths.
