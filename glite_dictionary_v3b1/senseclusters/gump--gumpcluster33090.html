gump

noun
slang for fool
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌmp / (AmE), / ɡʌmp / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who is foolish, naive, or easily tricked

Examples

A gump person
Everyone laughed when he called himself a gump for forgetting his keys again.
Don't be such a gump—check the facts before you believe everything you hear.
She felt like a gump after falling for the obvious joke.

Related Terms
