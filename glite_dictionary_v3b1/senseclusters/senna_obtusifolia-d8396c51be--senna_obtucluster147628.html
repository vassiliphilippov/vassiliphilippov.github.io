Senna obtusifolia

noun
tropical weed with yellow flowers and curved pods
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.ə əbˌtu.səˈfoʊ.li.ə / (AmE), / ˈsɛ.nə ɒbˌtjuː.sɪˈfəʊ.li.ə / (BrE)
A tropical herb or subshrub with yellow flowers and slender curved pods, commonly found as a weed; sometimes classified as Cassia obtusifolia.

Examples

Senna obtusifolia plant
Botanists identified the plant as Senna obtusifolia due to its characteristic yellow flowers and curved pods.
Farmers try to control the spread of Senna obtusifolia because it competes with crops.
The fields were overrun with Senna obtusifolia after the rainy season.
