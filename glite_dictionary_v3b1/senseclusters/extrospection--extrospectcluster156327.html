extrospection

noun
observation of external things
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈspɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈspɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of examining or observing things outside oneself, as opposed to introspection.

Examples

The extrospection of nature
Her extrospection led her to notice patterns in nature that she might have otherwise missed.
Scientific discovery often relies on extrospection rather than just internal reflection.
The philosopher compared the value of extrospection with that of introspection in personal growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: introspection - examination of one's own thoughts and feelings
  • synonym: observation - the act of watching or monitoring something carefully
