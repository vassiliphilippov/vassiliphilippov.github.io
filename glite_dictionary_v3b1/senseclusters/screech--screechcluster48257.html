screech

noun
sharp, piercing cry or sound
4.7
Pronunciation: / skritʃ / (AmE), / skriːtʃ / (BrE)
a loud, high-pitched, harsh or piercing sound or cry

Examples

A loud screech
Her screech startled everyone in the quiet library.
The distant screech of an owl echoed through the forest at night.
There was a sudden screech as the car's brakes were slammed.
A sudden screech from the brakes startled everyone on the bus.
Children ran away at the screech of the microphone feedback.
The barn owl let out a chilling screech in the darkness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scream - a loud, high-pitched, and usually long cry
  • synonym: shriek - a loud, sharp, high-pitched cry
  • antonym: whisper - a soft or low voice, the opposite of making a loud sound
