screech

noun
sharp, piercing cry or sound
4.7 2.5
Pronunciation: /skriːtʃ/ /; / /skrɪtʃ/ (AmE), /skriːtʃ/ /; / /skrɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a loud, high-pitched, harsh or piercing sound or cry

Examples

A loud screech
