muffin cup

noun
small baking container for muffins or cupcakes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌfɪn ˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈmʌfɪn ˌkʌp / (BrE)
a small, round container of paper, foil, or silicone with scalloped edges, used to hold and shape muffins or cupcakes during baking

Examples

A muffin cup holder
After baking, she peeled the paper muffin cup away from the warm blueberry muffin.
She poured the batter into each muffin cup before putting the tray in the oven.
The recipe recommends using silicone muffin cups for easy removal.
