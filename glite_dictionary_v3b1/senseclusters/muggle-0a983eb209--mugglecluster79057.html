Muggle

noun
non-magical person
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌɡəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌɡəl / (BrE)
a person who does not have magical powers, especially in the fictional world of Harry Potter

Examples

A Muggle person
In the Harry Potter series, Muggle refers to anyone who cannot perform magic.
Hermione was born to Muggle parents who had no idea about the wizarding world.
Some wizards prefer to live among Muggles and keep their magic a secret.

Related Terms

  • synonym: non-magical - lacking magical abilities
  • antonym: wizard - a person with magical powers
