wealth

noun
abundance of money or possessions
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɛlθ / (AmE), / wɛlθ / (BrE)
an abundance of money, valuable possessions, or resources; the state of being rich

Examples

Personal wealth
He accumulated great wealth through years of hard work.
Her wealth allowed her to travel to every continent.
Nations with abundant natural resources often have substantial wealth.
Despite his great wealth, he chose to live a simple life.
Her family's wealth allowed her to travel the world from a young age.
Increasing wealth in the region has improved the quality of life for many people.
Education is often regarded as a key to achieving future wealth.
Her family's wealth was built on generations of successful business ventures.
The distribution of wealth in that country is highly unequal.
