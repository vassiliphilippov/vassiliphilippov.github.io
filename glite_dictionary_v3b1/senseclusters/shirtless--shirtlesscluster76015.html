shirtless

adjective
without a shirt
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɜːrtləs / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtləs / (BrE)
not wearing a shirt or any clothing on the upper body

Examples

Shirtless man running
He jogged shirtless through the park on a hot day.
The actor appeared shirtless in several scenes of the movie.
Many people at the beach were shirtless to enjoy the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clothed - wearing clothes
