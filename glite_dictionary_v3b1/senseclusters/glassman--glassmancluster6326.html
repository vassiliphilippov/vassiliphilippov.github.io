glassman

noun
glass worker
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make, install, or repair glass items such as windows or mirrors

Examples

The glassman repairs windows
The glassman arrived early to fix the shattered shop window before customers arrived.
After the storm, a skilled glassman was needed to replace several broken panes in the office building.
If your mirror cracks, you should call a professional glassman to repair it safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glazier - a person who fits glass into windows and doors
