au naturel

adjective
naked; in a natural state
Pronunciation: / oʊ nætʃəˈrɛl / (AmE), / əʊ na.tʃʊəˈrɛl / (BrE)
with no clothes on; naked; in a natural, unadorned state.

Examples

Au naturel appearance
He appeared au naturel in the scene to emphasize vulnerability.
Many people enjoy swimming au naturel at secluded beaches.
The artist painted the model au naturel for his latest series of portraits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clothed - wearing clothes; dressed
  • synonym: naked - having no clothes on the body
