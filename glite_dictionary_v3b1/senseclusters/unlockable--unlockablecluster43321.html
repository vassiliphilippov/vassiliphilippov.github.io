unlockable

adjective
cannot be locked
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɑːkəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒkəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be locked or secured with a lock

Examples

Unlockable box
The old cabinet was unlockable, so we could not secure our important documents inside.
Because the door is unlockable, it is not suitable for storing valuable items.
They replaced the unlockable window with one that could be locked for better security.
