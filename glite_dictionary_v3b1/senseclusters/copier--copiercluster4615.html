copier

noun
a machine for making copies of documents
1.0 1.48/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀê.pi.…ö / (AmE), / Ààk…íp.i.…ô / (BrE)
a machine that makes copies of documents, drawings, or other visual materials, usually onto paper

Examples

Office copier machine
Our copier can print both color and black-and-white copies.
She placed the report in the copier and pressed the green button.
The office bought a new copier to improve productivity.

Synonyms

  • photocopier
    2.0 0.38/m
    photocopier is the office machine that makes paper copies with light or digital scanners, while copier means any copy-making device.
  • Xerox machine
    2.9 0.29/m
    Xerox machine is a brand name often used for photocopiers in everyday speech, while copier is the neutral general term.
  • duplicator
    4.9 0.05/m
    duplicator is an older or broader device that makes many copies by different methods, while copier is the common term for modern copy machines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photocopier - machine that copies documents using light and electrostatic process
