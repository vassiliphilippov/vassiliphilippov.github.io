copier

noun
a machine for making copies of documents
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.pi.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒp.i.ə / (BrE)
a machine that makes copies of documents, drawings, or other visual materials, usually onto paper

Examples

Office copier machine
Our copier can print both color and black-and-white copies.
She placed the report in the copier and pressed the green button.
The office bought a new copier to improve productivity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photocopier - machine that copies documents using light and electrostatic process
