Rabindranath Tagore

proper noun
Indian writer, poet, and philosopher (1861–1941)
Pronunciation: / rəˌbɪndrənɑːθ təˈɡɔr / (AmE), / ˌræbɪndrənʌθ təˈɡɔː / (BrE)
(1861–1941) Indian writer, poet, and philosopher known for pioneering modern Bengali literature and composing India's national anthem.
