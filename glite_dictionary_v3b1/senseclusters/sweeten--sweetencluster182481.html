sweeten

verb
make sweeter in taste
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈswiː.tən/ (AmE), /ˈswiː.tən/ (BrE)
to make something taste sweeter by adding sugar or a similar substance, or to make a situation or offer more pleasant or attractive

Examples

To sweeten food
She likes to sweeten her oatmeal with brown sugar.
The chef decided to sweeten the sauce to balance the sour flavors.
You can sweeten your tea with a spoonful of honey.
A little sugar will sweeten the sauce and balance its acidity.
She added honey to sweeten her tea.
They tried to sweeten the deal by offering free delivery.
