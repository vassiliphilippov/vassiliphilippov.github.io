podcaster

noun
creator of podcasts
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑdˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˈpɒdˌkɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who creates and publishes podcasts (audio programs) on the internet

Many podcasters record episodes from their own homes using simple equipment.
As a successful podcaster, he has built a loyal audience over the years.
