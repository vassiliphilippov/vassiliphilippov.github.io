partita

noun
18th-century instrumental music suite
Pronunciation: / pɑrˈtiːtə / (AmE), / pɑːˈtiːtə / (BrE)
A suite of instrumental music, especially as commonly composed in the 18th century.

Examples

A partita for keyboard
Bach composed several famous partitas for keyboard instruments.
Many partitas from the Baroque period are still widely played today.
The violinist performed a partita by Telemann during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suite - a set of instrumental or orchestral pieces to be played in succession
