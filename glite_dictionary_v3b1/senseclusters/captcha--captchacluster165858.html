captcha

verb
to require a CAPTCHA
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkæptʃə / (AmE), / ˈkæptʃə / (BrE)
to require someone to complete a CAPTCHA test to prove they are human, especially on a website.

Examples

Websites captcha users
Many websites now captcha users before allowing them to post comments.
If you try to log in too many times, the system will captcha you to prevent bots.
The forum started to captcha every registration to reduce spam accounts.
