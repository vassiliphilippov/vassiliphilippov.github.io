no rest for the wicked

x
used humorously to mean being busy, no time to relax
Pronunciation: / noʊ rɛst fər ðə ˈwɪkɪd / (AmE), / nəʊ rɛst fə ðə ˈwɪkɪd / (BrE)
used to say that you have a lot of work or problems and get little or no time to relax, often spoken humorously

Examples

There is no rest for the wicked
"Even on vacation, I still get work emails; truly, there's no rest for the wicked."
"I'm working all weekend again—no rest for the wicked."
She smiled after a long day, saying, "Well, no rest for the wicked!"
