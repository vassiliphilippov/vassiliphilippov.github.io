statelessness

noun
lack of citizenship
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtləsnəs / (BrE)
the condition of not being recognized as a citizen by any country or not having a functioning government or state authority

Examples

Refugee statelessness
After the war, thousands of people faced statelessness and struggled to find a new home.
International organizations work to reduce statelessness and help affected individuals gain legal rights.
Children born to refugees are at risk of statelessness if their birth is not officially registered.
