women's liberationist

noun
supporter of the women's liberation movement
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmɪnz ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɪmɪnz ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who advocates for the women's liberation movement and supports feminism, especially in the context of campaigning for equal rights for women

Examples

A women's liberationist activist
As a dedicated women's liberationist, she organized protests and educational forums.
She has long been a prominent women's liberationist who campaigns for equal pay.
The women's liberationist movement of the 1970s challenged traditional gender roles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminist - a person who supports feminism
