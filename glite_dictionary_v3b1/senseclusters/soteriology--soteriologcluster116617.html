soteriology

noun
theology of salvation
7.4
Pronunciation: / soʊˌtɪriˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / səʊˌtɪəriˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of Christian theology that deals with the study of salvation, particularly as achieved through divine agency.

Examples

Christian soteriology study
Her doctoral thesis focused on the development of soteriology in early Christian writings.
In his lecture on soteriology, the professor discussed various interpretations of salvation in Christianity.
The church council debated issues of soteriology for months before reaching a consensus.
