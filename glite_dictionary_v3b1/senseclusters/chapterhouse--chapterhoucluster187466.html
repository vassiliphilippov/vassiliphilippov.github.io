chapterhouse

noun
fraternity residence house
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæp.tɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈtʃæp.təˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A house used as a residence by a chapter of a fraternity or similar organization.

Examples

Fraternity chapterhouse
After joining the fraternity, he moved into the chapterhouse with his new brothers.
Many events and fundraisers are held at the chapterhouse throughout the academic year.
The university's oldest chapterhouse stands at the corner of Fifth and Maple.
