chop the wood

verb
cut wood into pieces for burning
Pronunciation: / tʃɑp ðə wʊd / (AmE), / tʃɒp ðə wʊd / (BrE)
to cut wood into pieces, typically for use as firewood

Examples

To chop the wood daily

She asked her brother to chop the wood before it started raining.
They took turns chopping the wood to make enough kindling for the night.
