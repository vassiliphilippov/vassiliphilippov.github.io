unconcentrated

adjective
not focused
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsɛntrəˌteɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɛntrətɪd / (BrE)
not mentally focused or lacking concentration on a task or subject

Examples

Unconcentrated student
He was so unconcentrated that he forgot to answer the teacher's question.
After a poor night's sleep, I felt unconcentrated at work all morning.
Her unconcentrated mind kept wandering during the important lecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because of other thoughts or activities
  • antonym: focused - paying close attention to something
