scratchiness

noun
rough, abrasion-causing quality
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being rough or likely to cause abrasions to the skin or surface

Examples

The scratchiness level
Abrasive cleaners are often rated based on their scratchiness to prevent damage to delicate surfaces.
He complained about the scratchiness of the cheap toilet paper.
The scratchiness of the wool sweater made it uncomfortable to wear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roughness - the quality of being rough to the touch
