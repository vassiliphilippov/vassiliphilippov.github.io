toxicology

noun
study of poisons and their effects
5.1 5.1
Pronunciation: /tɑːk.sɪ.ˈkɑː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /tɒk.sɪ.ˈkɒ.lə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
the branch of science concerned with the study of the nature, effects, detection, and treatment of poisons and toxins

Examples

Study of toxicology
Experts in toxicology testified at the trial about the effects of the poison.
She is studying toxicology to learn how different chemicals affect living organisms.
The toxicology report revealed the presence of various substances in the patient’s system.
