perfectness

noun
state of perfection
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrfɪktnəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːfɪktnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being perfect or without flaws

Examples

The perfectness of art
The perfectness of the sculpture amazed everyone at the gallery.
He admired the perfectness of the plan and how smoothly it worked.
Many strive for perfectness in their work, but few achieve it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perfection - the condition of being perfect
