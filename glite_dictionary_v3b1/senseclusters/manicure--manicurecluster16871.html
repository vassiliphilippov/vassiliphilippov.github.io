manicure

noun
treatment for hands and fingernails
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌkjʊr / (AmE), / ˈmænɪˌkjʊə / (BrE)
a professional or cosmetic treatment for the hands and fingernails, including cleaning, shaping, and polishing the nails

Examples

A manicure treatment
After a stressful week, a relaxing manicure was just what she needed.
She scheduled a manicure before the wedding to make sure her nails looked perfect.
The salon offers both manicure and pedicure services for their customers.
