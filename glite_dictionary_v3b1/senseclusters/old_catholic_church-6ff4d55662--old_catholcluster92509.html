Old Catholic Church

proper noun
Catholic churches separated from Rome in the 18th century
7.8
Pronunciation: / oʊld ˈkæθlɪk tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / əʊld ˈkæθlɪk tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A group of Catholic churches which separated from the Roman Catholic Church in the 18th century, mainly over issues of doctrine and church authority.
