alpinist

noun
mountain climber specializing in difficult climbs
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.pə.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈal.pɪ.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who climbs mountains, especially those involving challenging or technical ascents

Examples

Experienced alpinist climbs
During the expedition, each alpinist was responsible for carrying their own gear up the steep slopes.
Reinhold Messner is a legendary alpinist known for his remarkable high-altitude ascents.
The experienced alpinist prepared for weeks before attempting to scale the north face of the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mountaineer - a person who climbs mountains, especially as a sport or occupation
