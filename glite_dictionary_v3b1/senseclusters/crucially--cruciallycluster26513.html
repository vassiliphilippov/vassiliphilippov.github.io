crucially

adverb
in a crucial or decisive way
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːʃəli / (AmE), / ˈkruːʃəli / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely important or decisive.

Examples

To crucially affect results
He crucially contributed to the team's success with his leadership skills.
It is crucially important that all safety procedures are followed.
Most crucially, the new evidence changed the outcome of the trial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trivially - in a way that is of little importance
  • synonym: vitally - in a way that is absolutely necessary or essential
