spray

verb
cover with a liquid in fine drops
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / spreɪ / (AmE), / spreɪ / (BrE)
to send out liquid in small drops, often to cover or treat a surface

Examples

He sprayed the garden with water to keep the plants cool.
