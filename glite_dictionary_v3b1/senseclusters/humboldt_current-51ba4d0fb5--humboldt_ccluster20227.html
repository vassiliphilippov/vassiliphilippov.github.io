Humboldt current

noun
cold ocean current off South America
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmˌboʊlt ˈkʌrənt / (AmE), / ˈhʌmbəʊlt ˈkʌrənt / (BrE)
a cold ocean current that flows northward along the Pacific coast of South America before veering westward into the Pacific Ocean
