Examples

A beautiful landscape

Synonyms

  • scenery
    1.0 5.66/m
    scenery is the natural features people notice as a whole, while landscape names the whole area of land seen in one view.
  • prospect
    3.9 2.06/m
    prospect refers to a wide, often distant view across land, while landscape focuses on the land area seen in a single view.
  • panorama
    4.8 0.89/m
    panorama is an unbroken wide view seen from high places, while landscape is the land area you see in one frame.
  • view
    1.0 68/m
    view is what you can see from a place, often a single spot, while landscape names the whole land area visible at once.
  • scene
    1.9 19/m
    scene is any visual setting or moment, in nature or art, while landscape specifically means an area of land seen at once.
