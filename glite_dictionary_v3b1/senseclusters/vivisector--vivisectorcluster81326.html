vivisector

noun
merciless critic
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪvəˈsɛktər / (AmE), / ˌvɪvɪˈsɛktə / (BrE)
a person who examines, analyzes, or criticizes something in a cruelly thorough or merciless way, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Art vivisector
The harsh vivisector left the artist feeling exposed and vulnerable after the review.
As a literary vivisector, she dissected every weakness in the author's argument.
He became known as a vivisector for his relentless and unforgiving analysis of political speeches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critic - a person who judges or evaluates something
  • antonym: supporter - a person who approves of or defends something
