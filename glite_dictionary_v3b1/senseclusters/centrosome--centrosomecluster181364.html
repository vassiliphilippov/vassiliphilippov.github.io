A small region of the cytoplasm near the nucleus of a cell, containing the centrioles and serving as the main center for organizing microtubules.

Examples

Animal centrosome near nucleus
Each animal cell typically contains a single centrosome near its nucleus.
The centrosome plays a crucial role in organizing microtubules during cell division.
When the centrosome duplicates, it helps ensure proper chromosome separation.
