nyau

interjection
cat imitation sound
Pronunciation: / njaʊ / (AmE), / njaʊ / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a cat's meow, used playfully or to attract a cat's attention

Examples

Nyau to a cat
She called out nyau to get the kitten's attention in the garden.
With a cheerful nyau, he tried to make the cat come closer.
Children often say nyau when pretending to be cats during playtime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meow - the sound a cat makes; an exclamation imitating this sound
