logicist

noun
expert in logic
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːdʒɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled in or studies formal logic, or who supports the philosophy that mathematics can be reduced to logic

As a dedicated logicist, she believed that all mathematical truths are logical truths.
