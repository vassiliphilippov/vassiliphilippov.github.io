large

adjective
generous and magnanimous
Pronunciation: / lɑrdʒ / (AmE), / lɑːdʒ / (BrE)
(of a person, spirit, or action) showing generosity, understanding, or magnanimity.

Examples

Large spirit
He was known for his large and generous spirit throughout the community.
Her large heart was evident in the way she forgave those who wronged her.
It was very large of him to accept their apology so graciously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generous - showing a readiness to give more than is strictly necessary or expected
  • synonym: magnanimous - very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful
  • antonym: petty - small-minded; showing mean or spiteful behavior
