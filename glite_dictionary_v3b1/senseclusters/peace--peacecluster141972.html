peace

noun
treaty or agreement ending war
3.8
Pronunciation: / pis / (AmE), / piːs / (BrE)
a formal agreement or treaty that ends a state of war between parties

Examples

Sign a peace
After years of conflict, the two countries finally signed a peace in Geneva.
The historic peace between the warring nations was celebrated worldwide.
The leaders negotiated a peace that brought an end to decades of hostility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truce - an agreement between enemies or opponents to stop fighting for a certain time
  • antonym: war - a state of armed conflict between countries or groups
