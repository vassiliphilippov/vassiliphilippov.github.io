nightlife

noun
entertainment and activities at night
3.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈnaɪt.laɪf/ (AmE), /ˈnaɪt.laɪf/ (BrE)
entertainment and social activities that happen at night, especially in bars, clubs, theaters, or restaurants
