willingly

adverb
readily or voluntarily
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows you are happy or eager to do something; readily or voluntarily.

Examples

To willingly help
He willingly took on extra work to support his team.
She willingly agreed to help with the project despite her busy schedule.
The children willingly shared their toys with the new student.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reluctantly - in a way that shows unwillingness or hesitation
