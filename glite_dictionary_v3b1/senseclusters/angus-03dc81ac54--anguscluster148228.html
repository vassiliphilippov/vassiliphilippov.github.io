Angus

proper noun
Celtic god of love and beauty
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəs / (BrE)
a god of love and beauty in Celtic mythology, regarded as a patron of young men and women

Examples

Celtic god Angus
Angus was celebrated in festivals dedicated to love and youth.
Many legends tell of Angus transforming into a swan to pursue his beloved.
The ancient Celts believed that Angus would help young lovers find happiness.
