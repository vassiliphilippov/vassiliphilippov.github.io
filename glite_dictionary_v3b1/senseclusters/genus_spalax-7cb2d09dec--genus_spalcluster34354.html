genus Spalax

noun
genus of blind mole rats
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈspeɪ.læks / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈspa.laks / (BrE)
The type genus of the Spalacidae family, comprising the blind mole rats.

The genus Spalax includes several species of blind mole rats found in southeastern Europe and the Middle East.
