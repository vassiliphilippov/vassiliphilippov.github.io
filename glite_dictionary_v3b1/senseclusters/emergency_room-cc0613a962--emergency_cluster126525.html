emergency room

noun
hospital room for emergency treatment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɝːdʒənsi ruːm /; / ɪˈmɚdʒənsi ruːm / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒənsi ruːm / (BrE)
the part of a hospital where people with serious or urgent medical problems are treated immediately
