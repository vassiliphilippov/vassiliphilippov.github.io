Synonyms

  • waste

    waste is unwanted or unusable material commonly thrown away, while waste material emphasizes the substance being discarded.
  • waste matter
    1.9 0.01/m
    waste matter refers to unwanted substances rejected or thrown away, while waste material names any discarded material in general.
  • waste product
    2.9 0.3/m
    waste product is a byproduct produced and discarded from a process, while waste material covers any discarded substance.
  • rubbish
    1.0 1/m

  • trash
