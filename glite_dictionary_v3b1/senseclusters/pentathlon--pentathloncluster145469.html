pentathlon

noun
athletic contest of five events
Pronunciation: / pɛnˈtæθlɑn / (AmE), / pɛnˈtæθlən / (BrE)
an athletic contest in which each competitor takes part in five different events.

Examples

The pentathlon event
She won a gold medal in the pentathlon at the Olympic Games.
The modern pentathlon includes fencing, swimming, equestrian, shooting, and running.
Training for a pentathlon requires proficiency in several different sports disciplines.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: triathlon - athletic contest with three events
