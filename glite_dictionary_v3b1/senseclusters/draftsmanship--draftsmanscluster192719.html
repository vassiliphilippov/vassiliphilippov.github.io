draftsmanship

noun
skill in drawing or making diagrams
Pronunciation: / ˈdræftsmənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːftsmənˌʃɪp /; / ˈdrɑːftsmənʃɪp / (BrE)
the skill or practice of drawing artistic pictures or diagrams, especially with precision and technical accuracy

Examples

Technical draftsmanship
He won an award for his exceptional draftsmanship in technical illustration.
Her draftsmanship impressed the instructors at the art school.
The architectural plans displayed remarkable draftsmanship.
Her draughtsmanship impressed the judges at the art competition.
The architect's draughtsmanship was evident in the detailed blueprints he produced.
Improving your draughtsmanship requires regular practice and careful observation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draftsmanship - the skill of drawing, especially technical or artistic drawings
  • synonym: drawing - the act or skill of making pictures with a pen or pencil
  • antonym: painting - the art of using paint to create pictures
