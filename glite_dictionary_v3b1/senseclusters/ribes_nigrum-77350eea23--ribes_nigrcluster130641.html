Ribes nigrum

noun
species of black currant plant
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪbiːz ˈnaɪɡrəm / (AmE), / ˈraɪbiːz ˈnɪɡrəm / (BrE)
A species of currant plant widely cultivated for its edible black aromatic berries, commonly known as black currant.

Examples

Ribes nigrum berries
Ribes nigrum is cultivated in many countries for its distinctive black berries.
Some gardeners prefer to plant Ribes nigrum because of its hardiness and high fruit yield.
The berries of Ribes nigrum are rich in vitamin C and used in jams and juices.
