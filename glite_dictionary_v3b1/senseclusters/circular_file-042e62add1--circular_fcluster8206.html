circular file

noun
wastebasket (humorous)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrkjələr faɪl /; / ˈsɜrkjələr faɪl / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə faɪl / (BrE)
a humorous or ironic term for a wastebasket or trash can, especially when talking about throwing away something unimportant
