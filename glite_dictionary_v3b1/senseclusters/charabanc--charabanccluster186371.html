charabanc

noun
large excursion vehicle (historical)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃær.ə.bæŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃær.ə.bæŋ / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle with seats arranged in rows, used especially in the past for carrying groups of people on excursions

Examples

A charabanc ride
During the early 20th century, people enjoyed seaside outings in a charabanc.
In old photographs, holidaymakers are often seen smiling from the seats of a charabanc.
The group boarded the charabanc for a day trip to the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus - a large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically along a regular route
