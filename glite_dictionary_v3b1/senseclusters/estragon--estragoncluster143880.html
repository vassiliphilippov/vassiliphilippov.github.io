estragon

noun
aromatic culinary herb; tarragon
Pronunciation: / ˈɛstrəˌgɒn /; / ˈɛstrəˌɡɒn / (AmE), / ˈɛstrəgən /; / ˈɛstrəɡən / (BrE)
an aromatic herb (also called tarragon), whose leaves are used as a seasoning in cooking, especially in French cuisine
