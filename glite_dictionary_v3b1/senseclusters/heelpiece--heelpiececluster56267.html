heelpiece

noun
shoe heel part
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːlˌpiːs / (AmE), / ˈhiːlˌpiːs / (BrE)
the part of a shoe or boot that covers, supports, or reinforces the heel

Examples

Shoe heelpiece
The heelpiece of his boot wore out after months of hiking.
She replaced the worn heelpiece to make her shoes last longer.
A sturdy heelpiece can provide extra support and comfort for your feet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heel - the back part of a shoe under the foot's heel
  • antonym: insole - the inner sole of a shoe
