nomadically

adverb
in a wandering way
6.9
Pronunciation: /nə.ˈmæd.ɪ.kliː/ /; / /noʊ.ˈmæd.ɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /nə.ˈmæd.ɪ.kliː/ /; / /nəʊ.ˈmæd.ɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a manner that involves moving from place to place without a permanent home

Examples

Travel nomadically
After losing their home, the family traveled nomadically across the country for several months.
Some artists choose to live nomadically, finding inspiration in new places.
For years, he moved nomadically from city to city, never settling down.
