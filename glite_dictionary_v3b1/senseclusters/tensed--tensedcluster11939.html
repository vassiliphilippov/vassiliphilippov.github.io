tensed

adjective
indicating grammatical tense

(of a verb) having inflections that show grammatical tense, such as past or present.

Examples

Tensed verb form
In English, most regular verbs become tensed by adding -ed for the past tense.
Some languages have a complex system of tensed and untensed verb forms.
The verb 'walk' can appear in both a tensed and a non-tensed form.
