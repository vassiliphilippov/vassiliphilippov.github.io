inscrutability

noun
impossibility of being understood
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌskruːtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪnˌskruːtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being impossible to understand or interpret

Examples

The inscrutability of faces
Despite her friendly demeanor, there was an inscrutability about her that made others wary.
Philosophers have long debated the inscrutability of the universe and its laws.
The inscrutability of his motives left everyone puzzled.
