bring to light

verb
reveal or make known
2.3
Pronunciation: / brɪŋ tə laɪt / (AmE), / brɪŋ tə laɪt / (BrE)
to reveal or make something known, especially facts that were previously hidden or secret

Examples

To bring to light facts
A thorough review will bring to light any mistakes that were overlooked before.
The journalist's investigation helped bring to light several irregularities in the company's finances.
The letter finally brought to light the real reason for her sudden resignation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conceal - to keep something hidden or secret
  • synonym: expose - to make something visible or reveal something hidden
