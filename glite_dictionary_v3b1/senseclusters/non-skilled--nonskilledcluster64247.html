non-skilled

adjective
without skill or training
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈskɪld / (AmE), / nɒn ˈskɪld / (BrE)
lacking ability, training, or competence in a particular activity or job

Examples

Non-skilled worker
Many non-skilled workers find it difficult to get higher-paying jobs.
The company offers training programs for non-skilled employees to improve their abilities.
He was assigned to a non-skilled position that did not require previous experience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skilled - having the ability or training to do something well
  • synonym: unskilled - not having special skill or training
