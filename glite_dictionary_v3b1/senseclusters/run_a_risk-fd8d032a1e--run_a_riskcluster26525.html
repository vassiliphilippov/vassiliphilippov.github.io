run a risk

verb
take a chance with possible bad outcome
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /rʌn ə rɪsk/ (AmE), /rʌn ə rɪsk/ (BrE)
to do something that might lead to danger, harm, or loss; to take a chance that something bad could happen

Examples

If you don't wear a helmet while biking, you run a risk of injury.
They knew they would run a risk by investing in a new business.
