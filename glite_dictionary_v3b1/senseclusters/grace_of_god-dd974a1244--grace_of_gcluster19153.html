grace of God

noun
God's unearned favor or goodwill
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪs əv ɡɑd /; / ˈɡreɪs əv ɡɑd / (AmE), / ɡreɪs əv ɡɒd /; / ˈɡreɪs əv ɡɒd / (BrE)
the unearned favor, help, or goodwill given by God; divine assistance or blessing

Examples

The grace of God
He survived the accident by the grace of God, according to his family.
Many believers trust in the grace of God to guide them through difficult times.
She attributed her recovery from illness to the grace of God.
By the grace of God, peace was restored to the community.
She believed her recovery was due to the grace of God.
They said it was only by the grace of God that everyone survived the accident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divine grace - free and unmerited favor from God, especially in Christian theology
