Synonyms

  • voting right
    3.3 0.9/m
    voting right refers to the legal permission to take part in elections by casting a ballot, while right to vote names that entitlement.
  • suffrage
    7.6 0.9/m
    suffrage is a formal, historical word for the legal right to vote in elections, while right to vote is everyday phrasing.
  • franchise
    6.6 0.7/m
    franchise is a legal government term for a statutory right to vote, while right to vote is the plain everyday phrase.
  • vote
    1.0 17/m
    vote often means the act of casting a ballot, while right to vote specifically names the legal entitlement to participate in elections.
