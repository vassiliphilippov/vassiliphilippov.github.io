apple jelly

noun
jelly made from apple juice
Pronunciation: / ˈæpəl ˌdʒɛli / (AmE), / ˈæpəl ˌdʒɛli / (BrE)
A sweet, clear jelly made from apple juice, commonly used as a spread or in cooking.

Examples

Homemade apple jelly
Homemade apple jelly has a delicate flavor and a beautiful golden color.
She spread apple jelly on her toast for breakfast.
The recipe calls for a spoonful of apple jelly to add sweetness to the sauce.
