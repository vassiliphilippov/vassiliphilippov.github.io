California bay tree

noun
Pacific coast tree with aromatic leaves and hard wood
6.0
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɪfɚniə beɪ triː / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəniə beɪ triː / (BrE)
A tree native to the Pacific coast of North America, with aromatic leaves, small clustered flowers, olive-like fruit, and hard, tough wood.

Examples

The California bay tree wood
Local artisans often use the wood from the California bay tree to craft furniture and utensils.
The California bay tree thrives in coastal forests and is prized for its fragrant leaves.
Wildlife is attracted to the olive-like fruit produced by the California bay tree.
