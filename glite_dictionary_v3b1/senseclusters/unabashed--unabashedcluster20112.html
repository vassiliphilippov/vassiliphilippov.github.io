unabashed

adjective
not embarrassed or ashamed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈbæʃt / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈbæʃt / (BrE)
not embarrassed, ashamed, or affected by others' opinions; confident and unashamed

Examples

Unabashed confidence
Despite the criticism, the artist remained unabashed in expressing her unique style.
His unabashed curiosity sometimes caught people off guard, but he never apologized for it.
She made an unabashed declaration of her feelings in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ashamed - feeling guilt or embarrassment; opposite of unabashed
