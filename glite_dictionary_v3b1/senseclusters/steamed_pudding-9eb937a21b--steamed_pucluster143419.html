steamed pudding

noun
pudding cooked by steaming
Pronunciation: / stimd ˈpʌdɪŋ / (AmE), / stiːmd ˈpʊdɪŋ / (BrE)
a sweet or savory pudding cooked by steaming, often made with flour, eggs, and suet or butter

Examples

Traditional steamed pudding
For the holidays, my grandmother always makes her famous steamed pudding with dried fruit and spices.
The menu offers a traditional British steamed pudding served with custard.
They experimented with different ingredients to create a vegan steamed pudding.
