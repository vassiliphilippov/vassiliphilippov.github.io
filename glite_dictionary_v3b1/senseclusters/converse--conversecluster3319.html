converse

verb
talk in conversation
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːs / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːs / (BrE)
to engage in conversation; to talk informally with someone

Examples

To converse with friends
She enjoys conversing with people from different cultures.
The two scientists conversed for hours about their research.
We conversed quietly while waiting for the train.

Related Terms
