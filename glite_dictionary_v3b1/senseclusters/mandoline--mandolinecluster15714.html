mandoline

noun
kitchen slicer tool
Pronunciation: / ˈmændəˌliːn / (AmE), / ˌmændəˈliːn / (BrE)
a kitchen tool with a flat surface and adjustable blades used to slice fruits and vegetables thinly and evenly

Examples

Kitchen mandoline slicer
She carefully used the mandoline to slice cucumbers for the salad.
A mandoline can help you achieve perfectly even potato chips at home.
Always use the hand guard when operating a mandoline to avoid injury.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: knife - a tool with a sharp blade used for cutting food
  • synonym: slicer - a tool or machine for cutting food into thin pieces
