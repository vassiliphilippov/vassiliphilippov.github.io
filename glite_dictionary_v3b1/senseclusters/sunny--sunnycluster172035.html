sunny

adjective
bright with sunlight
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌni / (AmE), / ˈsʌni / (BrE)
bright with sunlight, or cheerful and pleasant like sunshine

Examples

A sunny day
She wore sunglasses on the sunny beach.
The room feels warmer and more cheerful when it's sunny outside.
We decided to have a picnic because it was a sunny afternoon.
After days of rain, the sunny weather lifted everyone's spirits.
His sunny disposition made him popular among his colleagues.
She greeted everyone with a sunny smile that brightened up the whole room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerful - happy and showing it by the way you behave
  • antonym: cloudy - covered with or resembling clouds; overcast
  • antonym: gloomy - dark or poorly lit, especially giving a feeling of depression or melancholy
