girt

noun
horizontal wall support
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɜrt / (AmE), / ɡɜːt / (BrE)
a horizontal structural member in a framed wall that connects columns and supports wall cladding or sheathing

Examples

Steel girt support
The workers attached each girt to the steel columns before installing the wall panels.
A damaged girt can weaken the entire structure of a warehouse wall.
Engineers specified the spacing of every girt to ensure the cladding would be properly supported.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beam - a main horizontal support in construction
  • antonym: stud - a vertical framing member in a wall
