riding bitt

noun
large post for securing an anchor cable
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪdɪŋ bɪt / (AmE), / ˈraɪdɪŋ bɪt / (BrE)
one of the large sturdy posts on a ship's deck used to secure the cable of a dropped anchor
