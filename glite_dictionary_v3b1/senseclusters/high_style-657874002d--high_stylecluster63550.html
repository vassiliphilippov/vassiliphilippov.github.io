high style

noun
trend-setting fashion or design
1.7
Pronunciation: / haɪ staɪl / (AmE), / haɪ staɪl / (BrE)
Fashion or design that is considered trend-setting, sophisticated, and of the highest quality or elegance.

Examples

High style fashion
She is always dressed in high style at every gala she attends.
The designer's new collection defines high style for the modern woman.
They redecorated their office in high style, with sleek furniture and contemporary artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haute couture - exclusive, custom-fitted high-end fashion design
  • synonym: high fashion - fashion that sets the trend or is very sophisticated
  • antonym: street style - fashion inspired by urban streetwear, typically less formal
