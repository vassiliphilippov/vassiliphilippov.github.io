dark

adjective
difficult to understand in style or expression
3.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /dɑːrk/ (AmE), /dɑːk/ (BrE)
(of writing, speech, etc.) difficult to understand, often because of complexity or obscurity of style or expression.

Examples

Dark writing style
Her latest novel is so dark that many readers struggle to grasp its meaning.
Some students found the poem's language too dark to interpret without guidance.
The philosopher's dark writing made his arguments inaccessible to most people.
