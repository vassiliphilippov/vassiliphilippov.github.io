hibernator

noun
animal that hibernates
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪbəneɪtə / (BrE)
an animal that spends the winter in a state of deep sleep or inactivity to survive cold conditions

Examples
