bond servant

noun
person bound to work without pay
4.0
Pronunciation: / bɑnd ˈsɜrvənt / (AmE), / bɒnd ˈsɜːvənt / (BrE)
a person who is bound to work for another without wages, typically as a form of servitude or slavery

Examples

A bond servant works
Her ancestor arrived in the New World as a bond servant seeking freedom after years of labor.
In colonial America, thousands of bond servants worked on plantations without pay.
The story explores the harsh lives of bond servants in the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indentured servant - a person bound by contract to work for another for a specified period, usually without pay but in exchange for accommodation, food, etc.
  • synonym: slave - a person who is legally owned by someone else and forced to work for them
