chickie

noun
young chicken (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪki / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪki / (BrE)
an informal or affectionate term for a small or young chicken

Examples

A baby chickie
The farmer showed the children a tiny chickie just hatched from its egg.
She gently held the yellow chickie in her hands and smiled.
Every spring, the barn is filled with the sound of chickies peeping.
