trailing windmills

noun
trailing plant with deep pink flowers, southwestern US/Mexico
6.6 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ ˈwɪnd.mɪlz/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪ.lɪŋ ˈwɪnd.mɪlz/ (BrE)
A trailing plant native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, noted for its crowded clusters of three brilliant deep pink flowers that resemble a single large flower and typically bloom near the ground in dry, sandy, or gravelly soils.
