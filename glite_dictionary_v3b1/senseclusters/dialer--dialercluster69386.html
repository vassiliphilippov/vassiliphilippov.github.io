dialer

noun
person or device dialing
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪlər / (AmE), / ˈdaɪlə / (BrE)
a person who dials telephone numbers, especially as part of their job, or a device that automatically dials numbers

Examples

A dialer at work
The company hired a new dialer to help with customer outreach during the busy season.
She worked as a dialer in the office, entering numbers by hand during the campaign.
The old phone system used a mechanical dialer to connect calls automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caller - a person who makes a telephone call
  • synonym: operator - a person who operates a telephone switchboard
