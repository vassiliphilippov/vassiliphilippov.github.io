printable

adjective
suitable for printing
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈprɪntəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be printed easily on paper or another medium

Examples

Printable worksheet
The teacher provided a printable worksheet for the students to complete at home.
You can find a printable map of the city on the official tourism website.
Please make sure the document is printable before sending it to the office printer.
