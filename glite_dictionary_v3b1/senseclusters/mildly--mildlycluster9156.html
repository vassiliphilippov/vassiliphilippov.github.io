mildly

adverb
to a moderate or slight degree
3.9 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪld.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪld.lɪ/ (BrE)
to a moderate or slight degree; in a gentle or not extreme way

Examples

To mildly react
He reacted mildly to the criticism, choosing not to argue.
She was mildly amused by the unexpected comment.
The soup was mildly spicy, making it suitable for most people.
He was mildly surprised by the unexpected news.
She spoke mildly to the children to calm them down.
The weather was mildly unpleasant but not unbearable.
