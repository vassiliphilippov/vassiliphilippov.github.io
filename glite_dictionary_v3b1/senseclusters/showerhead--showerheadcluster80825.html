showerhead

noun
bathroom device for spraying water
2.3 1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊəˌhɛd / (BrE)
A device with a perforated nozzle designed to distribute water in the form of a shower, typically fitted at the end of a pipe in a bathroom.

Examples

Bathroom showerhead
Make sure the showerhead is tightly screwed on to avoid leaks.
She installed a new high-pressure showerhead in her bathroom.
The old showerhead was clogged and needed to be replaced.
