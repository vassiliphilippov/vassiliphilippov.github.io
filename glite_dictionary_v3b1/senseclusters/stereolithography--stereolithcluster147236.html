stereolithography

noun
3D printing method
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrioʊlɪˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəʊlɪˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a method of creating three-dimensional objects by hardening layers of liquid resin with light, often used in 3D printing

Examples

Stereolithography 3D printing
The engineer used stereolithography to quickly produce a detailed prototype of the new device.
Advances in stereolithography have made it possible to create complex medical models for surgery planning.
Many jewelry designers rely on stereolithography to craft intricate molds for their pieces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: 3D printing - the process of making three-dimensional objects from a digital file
  • synonym: additive manufacturing - manufacturing objects by adding material layer by layer
