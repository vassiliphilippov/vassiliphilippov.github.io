celandine

noun
North American plant with yellow or orange flowers
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlənˌdaɪn / (AmE), / ˈsiːləndaɪn / (BrE)
A North American annual plant, typically with yellow or orange flowers, that grows chiefly on wet, acidic soil.

Examples

Wild celandine flowers
