nondrinker

noun
person who abstains from alcohol
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈdrɪŋkər / (AmE), / nɒnˈdrɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person who does not drink alcoholic beverages

Examples

A nondrinker person
As a nondrinker, she always volunteered to be the designated driver for her friends.
Company parties can be challenging for a nondrinker, but he never felt left out.
The restaurant offers several refreshing options for the nondrinker on the menu.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drinker - a person who drinks alcoholic beverages
  • synonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
