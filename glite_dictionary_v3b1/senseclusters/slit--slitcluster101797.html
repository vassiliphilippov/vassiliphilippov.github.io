slit

adjective
with narrow openings
Pronunciation: / slɪt / (AmE), / slɪt / (BrE)
having one or more long, narrow openings cut into the surface

Examples

Slit sleeves
The dress had a slit sleeve that revealed part of her arm.
He wore a mask with slit eyes to complete his costume.
The envelope was slit open along the top edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cut - having an opening or incision made by cutting
  • antonym: sealed - closed tightly so that nothing can enter or leave
