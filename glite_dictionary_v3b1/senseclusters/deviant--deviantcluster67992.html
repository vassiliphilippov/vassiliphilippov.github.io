deviant

noun
person whose behavior is abnormal or unacceptable
3.1 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdiː.vi.ənt/ (AmE), /ˈdiː.vi.ənt/ (BrE)
a person whose behavior or morals differ markedly from what is considered normal or acceptable, especially in a sexual context

Examples

The novel explores the mind of a social deviant ostracized by the community.
