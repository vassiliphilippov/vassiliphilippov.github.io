sojourn

noun
a temporary stay
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.dʒɝn / (AmE), / ˈsɒdʒ.ɜːn / (BrE)
a temporary stay in a place, especially as a visitor or guest

Examples

A short sojourn
During his sojourn in Paris, he visited many famous art museums.
The writer described her brief sojourn at the seaside cottage in her memoir.
Their mountain sojourn lasted only two weeks but was unforgettable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: residence - the act or period of living in a place
  • synonym: visit - go to see or spend time in a place as a guest
