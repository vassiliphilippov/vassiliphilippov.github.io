sojourn

noun
a temporary stay
7.5 1.38/m
Pronunciation: / Ààso ä.d í…ùn / (AmE), / Ààs…íd í.…úÀên / (BrE)
a temporary stay in a place, especially as a visitor or guest

Examples

A short sojourn
During his sojourn in Paris, he visited many famous art museums.
The writer described her brief sojourn at the seaside cottage in her memoir.
Their mountain sojourn lasted only two weeks but was unforgettable.

Synonyms

  • tarriance
    9.9 0.00/m
    tarriance is lingering or delaying in a place, while sojourn is a temporary stay as a visitor for a time.
  • lodging
    3.4 4.91/m
    lodging is a place to stay temporarily, while sojourn is the act of staying somewhere as a guest.
  • visit
    1.0 66/m
    visit is an occasion to go see someone or a place briefly, while sojourn is a longer temporary stay as a guest.

Related Terms
