trialogue

verb
to discuss as three
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəlɒɡ / (BrE)
to take part in or conduct a discussion or exchange involving three people or groups

Examples

It is often more productive to trialogue when three perspectives are involved.
