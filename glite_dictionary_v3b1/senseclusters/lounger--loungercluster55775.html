lounger

noun
comfortable garment for leisure wear
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊndʒər / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒə / (BrE)
a loose-fitting piece of clothing worn for comfort, usually at home or while relaxing

Examples

A soft lounger
After a long day at work, she changed into her favorite lounger to relax on the sofa.
He bought a matching lounger and robe set for his wife as a birthday gift.
The store's new collection includes a soft cotton lounger perfect for weekend mornings.
