truffle

verb
to hunt truffles
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌfəl / (BrE)
to search for or hunt truffles, usually with the help of trained animals such as dogs or pigs

Examples

To truffle with dogs
Early in the morning, they truffled through the woods with their trained dog.
Farmers in the region have truffled for generations, passing down their knowledge.
She truffles every autumn, hoping to find rare black truffles in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forage - to search widely for food or provisions
  • synonym: hunt - to search for something, especially animals or food
