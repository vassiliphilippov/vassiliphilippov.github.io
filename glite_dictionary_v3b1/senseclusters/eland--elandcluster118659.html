eland

noun
large African antelope with twisted horns
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiː.lænd / (AmE), / ˈiː.lənd / (BrE)
either of two large African antelopes (genus Taurotragus) with short, spirally twisted horns in both males and females

Examples

The eland antelope
Both male and female eland have distinctive spiraled horns.
The eland is the largest species of antelope found in Africa.
We spotted a herd of eland grazing on the open plains.
