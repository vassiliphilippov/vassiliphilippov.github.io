Dalbergia cearensis

noun
Brazilian tree yielding valuable cabinet wood
Pronunciation: / dalˈbɜr.dʒi.ə si.əˈrɛn.sɪs / (AmE), / dalˈbɜː.dʒi.ə si.əˈrɛn.sɪs / (BrE)
