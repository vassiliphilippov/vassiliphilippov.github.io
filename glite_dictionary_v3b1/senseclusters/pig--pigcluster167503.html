pig

noun
domesticated mammal; swine
Pronunciation: / pɪɡ / (AmE), / pɪɡ / (BrE)
a domesticated mammal kept for its meat (pork) and other products, typically having a stout body, short legs, and a snout.

Examples

A pig on a farm
Bacon comes from the meat of the pig.
Children squealed with delight as they watched the pig roll in the mud.
The farmer raised dozens of pigs on his smallholding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boar - a wild pig, or an uncastrated male pig
  • synonym: hog - a domesticated pig, especially one reared for slaughter
  • synonym: sow - an adult female pig
