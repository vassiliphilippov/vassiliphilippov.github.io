to the lions

x
expose to a harsh situation
Pronunciation: / tə ðə ˈlaɪ.ənz / (AmE), / tə ðə ˈlaɪ.ənz / (BrE)
to put someone in a situation where they are likely to face severe criticism, difficulty, or danger, often without adequate preparation or support

Examples

The manager sent the intern to the lions during the board meeting without any warning.
