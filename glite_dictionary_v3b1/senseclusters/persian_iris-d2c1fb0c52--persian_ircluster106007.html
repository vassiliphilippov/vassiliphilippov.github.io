Persian iris

noun
bulbous iris plant with pale lilac flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜːrʒən ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʃən ˈaɪrɪs / (BrE)
a bulbous iris native to Asia Minor, cultivated for its pale lilac-colored flowers
