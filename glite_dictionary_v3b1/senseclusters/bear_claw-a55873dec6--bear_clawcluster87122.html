bear claw

noun
almond-flavored pastry shaped like a bear's claw
5.3
Pronunciation: / bɛr klɔ / (AmE), / beə klɔː / (BrE)
A sweet, yeast-raised pastry, often almond-flavored, shaped in a semicircle with slits to resemble a bear's claw.

Examples

A sweet bear claw
Every morning, he buys a bear claw and coffee from the bakery across the street.
My grandmother taught me how to make a homemade bear claw for special family breakfasts.
The bear claw is her favorite pastry because of its sweet almond filling and unique shape.
