fall all over

verb
show excessive affection or gratitude
6.2
Pronunciation: / fɔl ɔl ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / fɔːl ɔːl ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to show excessive affection, admiration, or gratitude towards someone, often to an exaggerated degree

Examples

To fall all over someone
She fell all over her boss after getting her promotion, showering him with praise.
The guests were falling all over the host to thank her for the wonderful dinner.
Whenever he sees a celebrity, he falls all over them and asks for autographs.

Related Terms
