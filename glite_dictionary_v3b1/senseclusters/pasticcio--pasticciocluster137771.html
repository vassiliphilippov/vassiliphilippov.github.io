pasticcio

noun
Italian baked dish
8.3
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈstiːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / paˈstɪtʃəʊ / (BrE)
an Italian baked savory dish, often made with pasta, meat, and sauce, similar to a casserole or pasta bake

Examples

Baked pasticcio
For Sunday dinner she prepared a cheesy baked pasticcio of pasta and béchamel sauce that everyone loved.
The restaurant's signature pasticcio combines layers of pasta, meat, and creamy sauce.
He learned to make a traditional pasticcio from his grandmother's old recipe book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lasagna - an Italian baked pasta dish with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese
