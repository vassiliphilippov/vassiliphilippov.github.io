semaphore flag

noun
flag for visual signaling
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛməˌfɔr flæɡ / (AmE), / ˈsɛm.ə.fɔː flaɡ / (BrE)
a flag used to transmit messages at a distance by means of visual signals, often by waving or positioning the flag in particular patterns

Examples

A pair of semaphore flags
A pair of semaphore flags is essential equipment for maritime signaling.
During the exercise, crew members practiced sending messages with semaphore flags.
The ship communicated with the shore using semaphore flags.
