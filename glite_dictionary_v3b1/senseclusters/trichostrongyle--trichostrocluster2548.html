trichostrongyle

noun
parasitic roundworm
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪkoʊˈstrɒndʒaɪl / (AmE), / ˌtrɪkəʊˈstrɒndʒaɪl / (BrE)
a parasitic roundworm that infects the intestines of animals, especially livestock

Examples

A trichostrongyle worm
The veterinarian found a trichostrongyle infection in the sheep during a routine check.
Farmers use deworming treatments to protect cattle from trichostrongyle infestations.
A microscope is needed to identify a trichostrongyle in animal fecal samples.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nematode - a type of roundworm
  • antonym: tapeworm - a flat, parasitic worm
