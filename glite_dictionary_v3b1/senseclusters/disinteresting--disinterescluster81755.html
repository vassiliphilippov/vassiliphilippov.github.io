disinteresting

adjective
not engaging
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɪnˈtrɛstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɪnˈtrɛstɪŋ / (BrE)
not interesting; causing boredom or making people lose interest

Examples

A disinteresting lecture
The lecture was so disinteresting that several students fell asleep halfway through.
Her disinteresting presentation failed to capture the audience's attention.
A disinteresting book can make even a rainy afternoon feel long and tedious.
The documentary was so disinteresting that I stopped watching after ten minutes.
He found the book disinteresting and struggled to finish it.
Despite the hype, the movie turned out to be rather disinteresting for most viewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - not interesting; dull or tedious
  • antonym: interesting - holding attention; engaging or exciting
