sirih

noun
chewed tropical leaf
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːri / (AmE), / ˈsiːri / (BrE)
a tropical vine of Southeast Asia whose leaves are chewed, often with betel nut, as a mild stimulant

Examples

Sirih vine leaves
In many traditional ceremonies, guests are offered sirih as a sign of respect.
Elders in the village still enjoy chewing sirih with betel nut every afternoon.
The market stall sold bundles of fresh sirih leaves for local customers.
