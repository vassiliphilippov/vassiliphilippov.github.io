gigger

noun
gig carriage driver
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪɡər / (AmE), / ˈɡɪɡə / (BrE)
a person who drives or operates a gig, a light two-wheeled carriage

Examples

A gigger with a carriage
The gigger waited patiently as the horses were prepared for the afternoon outing.
In Victorian times, a skilled gigger was highly valued by wealthy families.
The old photograph showed a proud gigger standing beside his polished gig.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coachman - a person who drives a horse-drawn carriage
  • antonym: passenger - a person who rides in a vehicle but does not drive it
