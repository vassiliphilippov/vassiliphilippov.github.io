dogbane

noun
a poisonous plant of the genus Apocynum
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌbeɪn / (BrE)
A poisonous perennial plant of the genus Apocynum, with acrid milky sap, bell-shaped white or pink flowers, and a very bitter root.

Examples

The dogbane plant
Botanists studied the milky sap of dogbane for its chemical properties.
Some traditional herbal remedies included dogbane, despite its known toxicity.
The fields were covered in dogbane, warning hikers to keep their pets away.
