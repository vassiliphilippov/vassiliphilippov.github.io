carving

noun
the act or result of shaping by cutting
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or art of shaping something by cutting away parts of it, or an object made in this way (especially from wood, stone, or similar materials)
