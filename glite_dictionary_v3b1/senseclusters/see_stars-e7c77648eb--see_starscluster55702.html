see stars

verb
experience flashes of light (dizziness)
Pronunciation: / si stɑrz / (AmE), / siː stɑːz /; / si stɑːz / (BrE)
to experience flashes of light or bright spots in your vision, usually after being hit on the head or feeling faint

Examples

To see stars after hit
After the ball hit him on the head, he saw stars for several minutes.
He was so dizzy that it felt like he was seeing stars after spinning around.
She sees stars whenever she stands up too quickly due to her low blood pressure.
After tripping and hitting his head on the pavement, he began to see stars for a moment.
She accidentally bumped her forehead on the low shelf and immediately began seeing stars.
The boxer was punched so hard that he started to see stars in the ring.

  • synonym: dizzy - feeling unsteady or light-headed
