watchword

noun
secret password or code word
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃ.wɝd / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃ.wɜːd / (BrE)
a secret word or phrase used to gain admission or indicate membership in a group; a password or code word

Examples

Secret watchword
Each night, a new watchword was distributed to the soldiers for security.
The guard demanded the watchword before letting anyone enter the building.
Without the correct watchword, the door would remain locked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: password - a secret word or phrase used to gain access to something
