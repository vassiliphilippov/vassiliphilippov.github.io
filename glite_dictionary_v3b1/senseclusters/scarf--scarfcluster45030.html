scarf

noun
piece of fabric worn around neck/head
1.0 8.04/m
Pronunciation: / sk…ëÀêrf / (AmE), / sk…ëÀêf / (BrE)
A long piece of fabric worn around the head, neck, or shoulders for warmth, protection, or decoration.

Examples

A warm scarf
At the party, his silk scarf added a dash of elegance to his outfit.
During winter, children often lose their scarves while playing outside.
She wrapped a red scarf around her neck to keep warm.

Synonyms

  • muffler
    2.8 0.43/m
    a muffler is a close-fitting neck wrap used mainly for warmth, while a scarf can be worn more widely for warmth, protection, or style.
  • shawl
    1.0 3.66/m
    a shawl is a larger, often rectangular cloth worn over shoulders or head for warmth or dress, while a scarf is narrower and more versatile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shawl - a piece of fabric worn over the shoulders or head, often larger and not wrapped as tightly
