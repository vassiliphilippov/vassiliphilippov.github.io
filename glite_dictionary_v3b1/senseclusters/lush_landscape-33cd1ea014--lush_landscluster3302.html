lush landscape

noun
area rich in green plant life
Pronunciation: / lʌʃ ˈlænd.skeɪp / (AmE), / lʌʃ ˈlan.dskeɪp / (BrE)
an area of land that is rich in plant life and appears very green and fertile

Examples

A lush landscape
After months of rain, the valley had transformed into a lush landscape.
The island is famous for its lush landscape and exotic wildlife.
They stopped to admire the lush landscape spread out before them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: verdant - green with grass or other rich vegetation
