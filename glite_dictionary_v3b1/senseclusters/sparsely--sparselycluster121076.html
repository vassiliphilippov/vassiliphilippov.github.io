sparsely

adverb
thinly or unevenly distributed
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrsli / (AmE), / ˈspɑːsli / (BrE)
In a way that is thinly scattered or distributed; in small amounts over a large area or space.

Examples

A sparsely furnished room
Her apartment was sparsely furnished with just a table and two chairs.
Only a sparsely populated village lies beyond the river.
The hills were sparsely covered with trees, making the area look open and empty.

Related Terms

  • antonym: densely - in a dense or compact way
