boink

interjection
comic impact sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɔɪŋk / (AmE), / bɔɪŋk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to represent a short, bouncing, or comic impact noise

Examples

Boink! on impact
With a sudden boink, the rubber ball bounced off the wall and rolled away.
The cartoon character landed on a spring with a loud boink that made everyone laugh.
Every time the toy hit the floor, it made a funny boink sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boing - an exclamation imitating a springy or bouncing sound
  • antonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
