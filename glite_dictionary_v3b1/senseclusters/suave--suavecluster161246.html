suave

adjective
charming, confident, and elegant
5.7
Pronunciation: / swɑv / (AmE), / swɑːv / (BrE)
charming, confident, and elegant in a smooth and sophisticated way (especially describing a man)

Examples

A suave man
He tried to sound suave, but his nervousness showed through.
James Bond is often portrayed as a suave and sophisticated secret agent.
The suave host welcomed each guest with a charming smile.
He gave a suave smile as he confidently entered the room.
Her boss is known for his suave manner and impeccable dress sense.
The actor played a suave spy who charmed everyone he met.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkward - lacking grace or social polish
  • synonym: charming - pleasant and attractive in manner
  • synonym: debonair - charming, confident, and elegant (usually of a man)
  • synonym: sophisticated - having a refined knowledge of the world or culture
  • synonym: urbane - courteous and refined in manner, typically of a man
