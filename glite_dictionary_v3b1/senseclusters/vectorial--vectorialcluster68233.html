vectorial

adjective
relating to vectors
8.5
Pronunciation: /vek.ˈtɔː.rɪ.əl/ (AmE), /vek.ˈtɔː.rɪ.əl/ (BrE)
relating to or caused by a biological vector, such as an organism that transmits disease.

Examples

Vectorial disease transmission
The vectorial spread of malaria depends on the presence of certain mosquito species.
Researchers are studying the vectorial capacity of ticks to transmit new diseases.
Climate change may increase the vectorial transmission of viruses in some regions.

Related Terms
