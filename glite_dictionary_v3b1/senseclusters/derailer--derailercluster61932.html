derailer

noun
train or bike device
Pronunciation: / dɪˈreɪlər / (AmE), / dɪˈreɪlə / (BrE)
a device that causes a train to leave the tracks or shifts a bicycle chain between gears

Examples

Bicycle derailer
The mechanic checked the derailer to ensure the bike shifted gears smoothly.
A faulty derailer can cause a train to leave the tracks in an emergency.
He replaced the old derailer on his mountain bike to improve gear changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: derailleur - a mechanism for shifting the chain on a bicycle
  • synonym: switch - a device for changing the direction of a train on tracks
