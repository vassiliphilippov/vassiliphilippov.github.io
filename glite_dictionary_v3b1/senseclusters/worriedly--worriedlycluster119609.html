worriedly

adverb
in a worried manner
2.0 0.53/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…úÀêr…™dli / (AmE), / Ààw år…™dli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows worry or anxiety.

Examples

To speak worriedly
He spoke worriedly about the results of the upcoming exam.
She glanced worriedly at the clock as the minutes ticked by.
The parents waited worriedly for news from the hospital.

Synonyms

  • anxiously
    3.2 4.65/m
    anxiously shows strong nervous worry about what might happen, while worriedly means showing general worry or anxiety.
  • nervously
    2.3 10/m
    nervously shows physical signs like shaking or a fast heartbeat, while worriedly focuses on feeling anxious or troubled.
  • apprehensively
    6.0 0.31/m
    apprehensively shows fearful expectation that something bad will happen, while worriedly is broader worry without strong fear.
  • uneasily
    4.2 3.13/m
    uneasily describes feeling uncomfortable and tense in a situation, while worriedly highlights clear worry or anxious thought.
  • concernedly
    4.9 0.00/m
    concernedly shows care or interest as well as worry, while worriedly focuses mainly on feeling anxious.
