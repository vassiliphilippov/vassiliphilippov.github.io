left-wing

adjective
supporting progressive or socialist politics
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛftˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
believing in or supporting political or social ideas that favor progress, reform, or equality, typically associated with socialism or liberalism

Examples

Left-wing politics
Many young voters are attracted to left-wing policies that promote social justice.
Newspapers sometimes display a left-wing bias in their political reporting.
The candidate's left-wing views set her apart from her more conservative opponents.
