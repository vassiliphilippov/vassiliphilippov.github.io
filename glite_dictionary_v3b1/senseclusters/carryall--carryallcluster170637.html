carryall

noun
large bag or basket for carrying items
Pronunciation: / ˈkæriˌɔl / (AmE), / ˈkæriˌɔːl / (BrE)
a large, capacious bag or basket used for carrying things.

Examples

A large carryall bag
He slung his carryall over his shoulder and headed for the train station.
She packed her groceries into a large carryall before leaving the store.
The beach was crowded with families lugging colorful carryalls filled with towels and snacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tote bag - a large bag with parallel handles for carrying items
