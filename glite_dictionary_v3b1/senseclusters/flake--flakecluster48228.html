flake

noun
eccentric or unreliable person
8.2
Pronunciation: / fleɪk / (AmE), / fleɪk / (BrE)
a person regarded as strange, eccentric, or unreliable

Examples

He's a flake
Everyone thinks he's a bit of a flake because of his strange ideas.
The new team member seemed nice, but turned out to be a total flake after missing several meetings.
You can't rely on her; she's such a flake when it comes to keeping promises.

  • antonym: reliable - dependable; can be trusted
  • synonym: weirdo - person who is very strange
