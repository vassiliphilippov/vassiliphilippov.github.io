stemmer

noun
device for removing stems from fruit
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmər / (AmE), / ˈstɛmə / (BrE)
a device or machine used to remove stems from fruit, such as grapes or apples, during processing

Examples

Fruit stemmer
After passing through the stemmer, the apples were ready for further preparation.
Technicians maintain the stemmer regularly to prevent mechanical failure during harvest.
The winery invested in a new stemmer to improve grape processing efficiency.
