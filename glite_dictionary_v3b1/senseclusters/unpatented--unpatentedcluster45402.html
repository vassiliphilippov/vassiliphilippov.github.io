unpatented

adjective
not protected by a patent
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpæt.əntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpæt.əntɪd / (BrE)
(of a device or process) not protected by a patent; not having received official patent rights.

Examples

An unpatented device
Some inventors choose to keep their unpatented ideas as trade secrets.
The company focused on developing unpatented technologies to avoid legal issues.
The device is unpatented, allowing other manufacturers to create similar products.
