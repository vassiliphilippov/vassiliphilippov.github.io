washhouse

noun
building for doing laundry
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a building or outbuilding where laundry is washed, especially one used for this purpose in the past

Examples

Old washhouse building
During the rainy season, the washhouse was always busy with neighbors washing clothes together.
She remembered helping her grandmother carry baskets to the washhouse every Monday morning.
The old farmhouse still had a separate washhouse behind it for doing laundry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laundry - a room or building where clothes are washed and ironed
