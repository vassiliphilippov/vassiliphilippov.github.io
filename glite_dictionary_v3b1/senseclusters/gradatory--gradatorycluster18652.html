gradatory

adjective
proceeding by degrees
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdət(ə)ri / (BrE)
Taking place or proceeding step by step, or by degrees.

Examples

A gradatory process
Her recovery has been gradatory rather than immediate, improving in small steps over months.
The gradatory transformation of the landscape was barely noticeable at first.
The evolution of languages is a gradatory process, rarely marked by sudden change.
