pin cherry

noun
North American wild cherry with red fruit
Pronunciation: / pɪn ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / pɪn ˈtʃɛəri / (BrE)
A small North American shrub or tree (Prunus pensylvanica) with bright red, sour fruit.

Examples

Bright red pin cherry
After a fire, pin cherry shrubs are among the first plants to regrow in the area.
Many birds feed on the sour red fruit of the pin cherry in late summer.
The pin cherry is often found growing in clearings and along forest edges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherry - a small, round stone fruit of various species of Prunus
