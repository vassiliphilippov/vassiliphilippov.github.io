pharmacotherapy

noun
treatment with drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːrməkəˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməkəˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the treatment of disease or medical conditions using medicines or drugs

Examples

Pharmacotherapy treatment
The doctor recommended pharmacotherapy to help manage her high blood pressure.
Some mental health conditions are treated with a combination of pharmacotherapy and counseling.
Advances in pharmacotherapy have improved outcomes for many chronic diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chemotherapy - treatment of disease, especially cancer, using chemical substances
  • antonym: psychotherapy - treatment of mental disorders by psychological methods
