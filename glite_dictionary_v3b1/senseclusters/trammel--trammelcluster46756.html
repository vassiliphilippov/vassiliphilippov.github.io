trammel

noun
adjustable hearth pothook
Pronunciation: / ˈtræməl / (AmE), / ˈtræməl / (BrE)
an adjustable metal hook or bar in a fireplace, used to suspend pots at different heights over the fire

Examples

Fireplace trammel hook
Antique collectors prize the iron trammel as a symbol of early domestic life.
She adjusted the trammel to raise the pot higher above the flames.
The old kitchen still had a trammel hanging above the hearth for cooking stews.
