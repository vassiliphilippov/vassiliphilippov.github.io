wacker

noun
construction compactor
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwækər / (AmE), / ˈwækə / (BrE)
a handheld or plate compactor used to press and firm soil or gravel for construction work

Examples

A wacker machine
The workers used a wacker to compact the soil before pouring the concrete.
After spreading the gravel, he ran the wacker over it several times to make it firm.
You need a wacker if you want the ground to be stable for paving stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compactor - a machine used to press and firm soil or materials
  • synonym: roller - a heavy machine used to flatten surfaces in construction
