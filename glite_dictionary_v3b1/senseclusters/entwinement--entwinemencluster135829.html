entwinement

noun
close involvement
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtwaɪnmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈtwaɪnmənt / (BrE)
a close and often complicated connection or involvement between people, groups, ideas, or events

Examples

Entwinement of lives
The entwinement of their personal and professional lives made things complicated.
Historians study the entwinement of cultural traditions across different regions.
The entwinement of technology and education is shaping the future of learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interconnection - a mutual connection between two or more things
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving or being moved apart
