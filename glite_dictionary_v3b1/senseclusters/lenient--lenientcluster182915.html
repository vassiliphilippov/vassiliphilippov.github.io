  • permissive
    4.9 0.6/m
    permissive allows a lot of freedom in rules or behavior, while lenient focuses on giving softer punishment or fewer penalties.
  • indulgent
    6.1 2/m
    indulgent lets people have what they want and can spoil them, while lenient means not strict in punishment or rules.
  • lax
