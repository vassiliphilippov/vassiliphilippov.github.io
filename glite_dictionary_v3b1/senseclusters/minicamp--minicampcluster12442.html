minicamp

noun
short training camp
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌkæmp / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌkæmp / (BrE)
a short, intensive training camp or session for sports teams, usually held before the main season

Examples

A minicamp session
The coach organized a minicamp to help new players learn the team's strategies.
Before the official season began, the basketball team attended a week-long minicamp.
Many athletes use a minicamp to improve their skills and fitness before competitions.
The football team attended a week-long minicamp to get ready for the season.
Rookies are invited to a special minicamp before joining the main squad.
Coaches use the minicamp to evaluate new players and teach basic strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: training camp - a period of intensive training for athletes or teams
