imprint

noun
a distinctive influence or mark
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmˌprɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɪm.prɪnt / (BrE)
a lasting effect or distinctive mark left by an event, influence, or person

Examples

A lasting imprint
Her teaching left an indelible imprint on her students' lives.
The architecture of the city still bears the imprint of its colonial past.
The artist's work carries the imprint of several different styles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mark - a visible impression or trace left by something
  • synonym: trace - a sign or evidence of some past influence, event, or action
