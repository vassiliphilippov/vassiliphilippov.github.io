multivocality

noun
multiple perspectives
9.5
Pronunciation: /mʌl.tɪ.voʊ.ˈkæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /mʌl.tɪ.vəʊ.ˈkæl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the presence or use of multiple voices, perspectives, or meanings within a text, statement, or situation

Examples
