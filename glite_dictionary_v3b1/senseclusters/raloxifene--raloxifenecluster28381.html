raloxifene

noun
osteoporosis medication
Pronunciation: / rəˈlɑksɪˌfiːn /; / rəˈlɑksəˌfiːn / (AmE), / rəˈlɒksɪˌfiːn /; / rəˈlɒksɪfiːn / (BrE)
a drug used to prevent and treat osteoporosis and reduce the risk of breast cancer in postmenopausal women
