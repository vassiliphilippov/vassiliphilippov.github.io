collectible

noun
an item worth collecting
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktəbl̩ / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
an object that people collect because it is interesting or valuable, not necessarily old or antique

Examples

A rare collectible
She has an impressive display of baseball cards and other collectibles in her living room.
Some collectibles become more valuable over time, while others simply hold sentimental value.
The shop specializes in rare collectibles from the 1980s and 1990s.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: antique - an old and valuable object
  • synonym: curio - an unusual object valued as a curiosity
  • degree lower: souvenir - an object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event
