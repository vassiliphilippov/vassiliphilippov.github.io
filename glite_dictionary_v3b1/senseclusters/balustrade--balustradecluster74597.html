Examples

Stone balustrade

Synonyms

  • banister
    2.7 1.33/m
    a banister is the handrail people hold on stairs, while a balustrade is a full row of posts and rails forming the side barrier.
  • railing
    1.6 1.36/m
    a railing is a simple barrier of bars and posts to prevent falls, while a balustrade uses vertical balusters and looks more decorative.
