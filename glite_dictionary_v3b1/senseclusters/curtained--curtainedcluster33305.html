curtained

adjective
furnished with curtains
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.tənd / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.tənd / (BrE)
furnished or concealed with curtains or draperies.

Examples

Curtained window
The curtained window kept the bright sunlight out of the room.
The dining room was beautifully curtained, enhancing the warm and intimate atmosphere.
We sat in a curtained booth in the quiet restaurant.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bare - without covering or adornment, especially curtains or drapery
  • synonym: draped - covered, adorned, or arranged with drapery
