jug band

noun
band with improvised instruments
Pronunciation: / dʒʌɡ bænd / (AmE), / dʒʌɡ band / (BrE)
a band that plays music using jugs, washboards, kazoos, and other improvised instruments

Examples

A jug band group
He learned to play the washboard in a jug band during college.
Many early blues musicians got their start in jug bands.
The local museum is hosting a performance by a famous jug band this Saturday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skiffle band - a group that plays folk, jazz, or blues with improvised instruments
