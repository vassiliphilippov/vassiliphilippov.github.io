screwdriver

noun
tool for turning screws
2.7 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskruːˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈskruːˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
A hand tool with a handle and a metal shaft, used for turning screws and typically having a tip that fits into the head of a screw.

Examples

Flat-head screwdriver
Always use the correct screwdriver to avoid damaging the screw or the tool.
He reached for a screwdriver to fix the loose hinge on the door.
This set includes both flat-head and Phillips screwdrivers for different types of screws.
