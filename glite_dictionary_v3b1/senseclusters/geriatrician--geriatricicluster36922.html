geriatrician

noun
doctor specializing in care of elderly
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
A medical doctor who specializes in the care and treatment of older adults.

Examples

Geriatrician doctor
After turning seventy-five, my grandmother decided to see a geriatrician for her annual checkups.
Dr. Smith is a renowned geriatrician specializing in memory disorders among elderly patients.
The hospital recently hired a new geriatrician to expand their elderly care services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gerontologist - a person who studies the process of aging and the problems of elderly people
