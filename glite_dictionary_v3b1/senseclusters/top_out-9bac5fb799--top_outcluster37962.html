top out

verb
leave career at peak success
6.8
Pronunciation: / tɑp aʊt / (AmE), / tɒp aʊt / (BrE)
to leave or abandon one's career just as one achieves a high level of success

Examples

To top out early
After years of hard work, she decided to top out and focus on her family instead.
He surprised everyone when he topped out at the very peak of his acting career.
It's rare for athletes to top out when they're still winning championships.
