interbody

adjective
between body structures
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈbɑːdi / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈbɒdi / (BrE)
situated or occurring between the bodies of two structures, especially between vertebrae in the spine

Examples

An interbody procedure
The doctor recommended an interbody procedure to help relieve the patient's back pain.
An interbody implant was placed between the vertebrae to support the spine.
Advances in interbody fusion techniques have improved recovery times for spinal surgery patients.
