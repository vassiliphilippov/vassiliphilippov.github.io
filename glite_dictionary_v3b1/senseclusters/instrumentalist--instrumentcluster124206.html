instrumentalist

noun
person who plays a musical instrument
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.strəˈmɛn.tə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪn.strəˈmen.tə.lɪst / (BrE)

Examples

Professional instrumentalist

She is a talented instrumentalist who plays both the piano and the violin.
