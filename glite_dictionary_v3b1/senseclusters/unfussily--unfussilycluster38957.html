unfussily

adverb
simply or plainly
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfʌsɪli / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌsɪli / (BrE)
in a simple, plain, or unpretentious manner, without unnecessary attention or decoration

Examples

Unfussily decorated room
She decorated her apartment unfussily, choosing only a few simple pieces of furniture.
The chef presented the meal unfussily, letting the fresh ingredients speak for themselves.
He always dresses unfussily, preferring comfort over fashion.
