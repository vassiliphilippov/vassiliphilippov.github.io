injuriously

adverb
in a harmful or damaging way
3.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdʒʊr.iː.əs.lɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdʒʊr.ɪ.əs.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdʒʊə.riː.əs.lɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdʒʊə.rɪ.əs.lɪ/ (BrE)
In a manner that causes or is likely to cause harm or injury; harmfully.

Examples

Used injuriously in speech
