quint

noun
group of five
7.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / kwÉªnt / (AmE), / kwÉªnt / (BrE)
a group or set of five people or things considered as a unit

Examples

A music quint

She joined a quint of artists to collaborate on the mural.
