quint

noun
group of five
7.1
Pronunciation: / kwɪnt / (AmE), / kwɪnt / (BrE)
a group or set of five people or things considered as a unit

Examples

A music quint
The jazz group performed as a quint for the first time last night.
Each quint in the competition had to present a unique project.
She joined a quint of artists to collaborate on the mural.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: quartet - a group of four people or things
  • synonym: quintet - a group of five musicians or singers
  • degree higher: sextet - a group of six people or things
