pre-wrapped

adjective
already packaged
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈræpt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈræpt / (BrE)
wrapped in packaging before being sold or used

Examples

A pre-wrapped sandwich
She bought a pre-wrapped sandwich for lunch at the convenience store.
The bakery sells pre-wrapped cookies to keep them fresh for customers.
He grabbed a pre-wrapped gift from the shelf to save time on wrapping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packaged - placed in a container or wrapping for sale
  • antonym: unwrapped - not covered or enclosed in packaging
