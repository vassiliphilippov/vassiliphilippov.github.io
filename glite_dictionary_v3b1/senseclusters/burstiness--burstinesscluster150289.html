burstiness

noun
variation in language
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.stɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.stɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of having noticeable variation in sentence length, rhythm, or word choice that makes language feel lively or natural
