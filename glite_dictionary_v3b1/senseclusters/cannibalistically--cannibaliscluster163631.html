cannibalistically

adverb
in a self-competing way
9.5
Pronunciation: /kæn.ɪ.bə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /kæn.ɪ.bə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that takes customers, sales, or resources from one's own products or services, especially in business.

Examples

Sell cannibalistically in business
The new product was launched cannibalistically, taking market share from the company's existing line.
Some companies grow cannibalistically by introducing cheaper models that reduce sales of their premium products.
The brand expanded cannibalistically, causing its own older services to lose customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitively - in a way that involves competition
  • antonym: cooperatively - in a way that involves working together
