clubbing

noun
swelling of fingers or toes due to disease
5.4 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈklʌb.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈklʌb.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
a condition in which the ends of the fingers and toes become enlarged and rounded, often as a sign of heart or lung disease

Examples

Clubbing of the fingers
Clubbing of the toes is an early indicator of some chronic lung diseases.
Long-term oxygen deficiency can lead to clubbing in the extremities.
The doctor noticed clubbing of the patient's fingers, which prompted further heart tests.
