Calvert Vaux

proper noun
US landscape architect, co-designer of Central Park
Pronunciation: / ˈkæl.vɚt vɔːks / (AmE), / ˈkæl.vət vɔːks / (BrE)
(1824–1895) United States landscape architect, born in England, known for co-designing Central Park in New York City.
