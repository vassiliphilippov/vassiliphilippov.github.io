Orthodox Catholic Church

proper noun
Christian church of Byzantine tradition
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˌdɑks ˈkæθəlɪk tʃɝtʃ / (AmE), / ˌɔːθədɒks ˈkæθlɪk tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A Christian church derived from the Byzantine Church and following Byzantine rites; also known as the Eastern Orthodox Church.

Examples

Orthodox Catholic Church traditions
He studied the art and architecture associated with the Orthodox Catholic Church in Eastern Europe.
Many traditions in the Orthodox Catholic Church date back to the early centuries of Christianity.
The Orthodox Catholic Church celebrates Easter according to a different calendar than Western churches.
