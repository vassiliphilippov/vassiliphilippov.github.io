cynophobia

noun
irrational fear of dogs
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
an abnormal or irrational fear of dogs

Examples

Fear of dogs
Despite her cynophobia, she agreed to attend the dog-friendly park event with her friend.
Her cynophobia made it impossible for her to visit friends who owned dogs.
The therapist specialized in treating severe cases of cynophobia.
