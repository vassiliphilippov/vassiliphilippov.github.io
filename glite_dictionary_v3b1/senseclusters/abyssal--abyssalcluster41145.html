abyssal

adjective
extremely deep; like an abyss
2.1
Pronunciation: / əˈbɪsəl / (AmE), / əˈbɪsəl / (BrE)
extremely deep or unfathomable; relating to or resembling an abyss, especially in depth

Examples

The submarine dived into the abyssal depths, far below the reach of sunlight.
