nationalization

noun
making something national in character
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnæʃənələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌnæʃ.ə.nəl.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌnaʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌnæʃ.ə.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something national in character or scope

Examples
