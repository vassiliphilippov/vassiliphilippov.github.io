spontaneous trip

noun
an unplanned or impulsive journey
Pronunciation: / spɑnˈteɪniəs trɪp / (AmE), / spɒnˈteɪniəs trɪp / (BrE)
a journey or excursion that is undertaken suddenly and without prior planning or arrangement

Examples

A spontaneous trip with friends
Her most memorable memories were made during a spontaneous trip with friends after finals.
Last year, a spontaneous trip to Paris changed the course of his summer plans.
On Friday, we decided to take a spontaneous trip to the mountains just for fun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impromptu trip - a journey taken without preparation or planning
  • antonym: planned trip - a journey that is organized or arranged in advance
