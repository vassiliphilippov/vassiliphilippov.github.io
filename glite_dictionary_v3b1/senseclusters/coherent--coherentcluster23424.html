coherent

adjective
clear and logical in speech or thought
2.6 5.8
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈhɪr.ənt/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈhɪə.rənt/ (BrE)
clear, logical, and well-connected; easy to understand or follow (about people, speech, writing, or arguments)

Examples
