buccal cavity

noun
space inside the mouth
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌk.əl ˈkæv.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˈbʌk.əl ˈkæv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the space inside the mouth between the jaws and the cheeks

Examples

The buccal cavity space
A dentist will often examine the buccal cavity for signs of infection or disease.
Food is first broken down in the buccal cavity by the action of chewing and saliva.
The hamster stores seeds in its buccal cavity before transporting them to its burrow.
