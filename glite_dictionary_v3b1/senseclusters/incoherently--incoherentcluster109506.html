incoherently

adverb
in a confused or unclear way
5.6 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkoʊˈhɪrəntli / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is not clear, logical, or easy to understand; without coherence

Examples

Speak incoherently
After the accident, she explained what happened incoherently because she was still in shock.
During the heated debate, he argued incoherently and failed to convince anyone.
The witness spoke incoherently, making it difficult for the jury to understand his account.
