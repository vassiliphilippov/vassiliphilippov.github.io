Dasypus

proper noun
genus of long-nosed armadillos
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdæ.sɪ.pəs / (AmE), / ˈdæsɪpəs / (BrE)
A genus of armadillos, commonly known as the long-nosed armadillos, and the type genus of the family Dasypodidae.

Examples

Dasypus genus
Among the various species of Dasypus, the nine-banded armadillo is the most widespread in the Americas.
Researchers discovered a new member of the genus Dasypus in South America.
The Dasypus genus is notable for its long snout and protective armor.
