Synonyms

  • blocking agent
    5.8 0.07/m
    blocking agent is a formal scientific term for a drug that inhibits a process, while blocker is a shorter, more common name used broadly.
  • inhibitor
    6.0 4.28/m
    inhibitor is a substance that slows or stops a chemical or biological process, while blocker names drugs that prevent other substances acting in the body.
  • antagonist
    7.3 0.70/m
    antagonist blocks or reduces another drug's effect at receptors, while blocker is a broader term for drugs that stop biological actions.
