Morus rubra

noun
North American mulberry tree with dark fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔr.əs ˈruː.brə / (AmE), / ˈmɔː.rəs ˈruː.brə / (BrE)
a species of mulberry tree native to North America, known for its dark purple edible fruit

Examples

Morus rubra tree
Gardeners often plant Morus rubra for its ornamental value and edible berries.
The Morus rubra produces fruit that is sweet and juicy when fully ripe.
Unlike the white mulberry, Morus rubra is native to the eastern United States.
