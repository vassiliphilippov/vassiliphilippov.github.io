unconstraining

noun
removal of restrictions
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈstreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of removing constraints or restrictions from something.

Examples

Unconstraining of rules
The unconstraining of regulations encouraged innovation in the industry.
Many teachers supported the unconstraining of traditional classroom rules.
The unconstraining of the schedule allowed for more creative activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liberation - the act of setting someone or something free
  • antonym: restriction - a limitation or control placed on something
