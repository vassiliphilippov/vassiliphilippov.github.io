Examples

A congregation of birds
A congregation of geese gathered by the lake at sunset.

Synonyms

  • assembly
    2.5 17/m
    an assembly is a formal meeting of people for a purpose, while congregation often refers to a gathered group, often for worship.
  • gathering
    1.0 14/m
    a gathering is a casual meeting or social group, while congregation usually means a more organized or worshiping group.
  • assemblage
    6.6 0.69/m
    an assemblage is a loose collection of people or things in one place, while congregation implies a settled, often religious group.
  • flock
    2.0 3.46/m
    a flock is a group gathered under a leader or of animals, while congregation names a general gathered group often for worship.
  • collectivity
    6.6 0.45/m
    a collectivity is a group seen as a single unit acting together, while congregation describes people gathered physically or socially.
