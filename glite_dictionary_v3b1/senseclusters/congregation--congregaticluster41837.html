congregation

noun
group gathered together
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən /; / ˌkɑːŋɡrəˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of people, animals, or things gathered together, or the act of gathering together

Examples

A congregation of birds
A congregation of geese gathered by the lake at sunset.
A large congregation filled the hall for the ceremony.
As winter neared, a congregation of students studied together in the library.
Birds formed a large congregation on the wire as dusk approached.
During the festival, there was a notable congregation of residents in the main square.
The sudden congregation of people in the park surprised the local authorities.
