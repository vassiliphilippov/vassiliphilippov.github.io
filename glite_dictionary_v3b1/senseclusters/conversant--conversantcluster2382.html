conversant

adjective
familiar or knowledgeable about something
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrsənt / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːsənt / (BrE)
having knowledge about something; familiar with a subject or activity, especially through experience or study (usually followed by 'with').

Examples

Conversant with customs
After living in France for years, he became conversant with local customs.
Applicants must be conversant with current safety regulations.
She is fully conversant with computer programming languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: familiar - well acquainted with something
