northern bog lemming

noun
rodent native to northern North America
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrðərn bɑɡ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɔːðən bɒɡ ˈlɛmɪŋ / (BrE)
A small rodent (Synaptomys borealis) found in wet alpine and subalpine meadows of Canada and Alaska.

Examples

The northern bog lemming rodent
Researchers studied the impact of climate change on the habitat of the northern bog lemming.
The northern bog lemming is rarely seen due to its secretive habits in dense vegetation.
Unlike other rodents, the northern bog lemming thrives in wet, cool environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemming - any of various small, short-tailed rodents found in the Arctic
