full bloom

noun
peak stage of flowering
3.0 0.60/m
Pronunciation: / f äl bluÀêm / (AmE), / f äl bluÀêm / (BrE)
the state of a flower or plant when it is at its peak of flowering and most beautiful or mature

Examples

Full bloom flowers
Her roses reached full bloom earlier this year due to the warm weather.
The beauty of the valley in full bloom is truly breathtaking.
The cherry trees were in full bloom, attracting tourists from all over the city.
