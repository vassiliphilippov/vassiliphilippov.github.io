legacy of the Soviet Union

noun
historical and cultural effects of Soviet rule
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ðə ˌsoʊviət juˈniən / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡəsi əv ðə ˌsəʊviət juːˈniːən / (BrE)
the lasting historical, political, and cultural effects resulting from the existence and policies of the Soviet Union.
