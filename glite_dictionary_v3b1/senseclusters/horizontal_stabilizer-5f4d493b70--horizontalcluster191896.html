Synonyms

  • tailplane
    8.8 0.01/m
    a tailplane is the fixed horizontal surface at an aircraft's tail that carries the elevator, while a horizontal stabilizer is the formal term for that fixed surface.
  • horizontal tail
    4.7 0.01/m
    a horizontal tail often includes both the stabilizer and the elevator to control pitch, while a horizontal stabilizer is only the fixed surface.
