duster

noun
baseball pitch close to the batter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstər / (AmE), / ˈdʌstə / (BrE)
(Baseball) A pitch thrown intentionally close to the batter to intimidate or force them away from the plate.

Examples

A fastball duster
After that duster, the batter stepped back nervously.
The pitcher threw a duster to move the hitter off the plate.
The umpire warned the pitcher for a dangerous duster in the second inning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beanball - a pitch thrown at or near the batter's head
