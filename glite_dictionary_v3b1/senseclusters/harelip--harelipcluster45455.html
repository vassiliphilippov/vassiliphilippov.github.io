harelip

noun
congenital split in the upper lip
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌlɪp / (AmE), / ˈheə.lɪp / (BrE)
a congenital cleft in the middle of the upper lip; now usually referred to as a 'cleft lip' and often considered offensive

Examples

A harelip condition
Early literature often used the word harelip to describe this congenital condition.
He was born with a harelip, which was corrected through surgery in infancy.
The term harelip is now considered offensive and has been replaced by 'cleft lip' in medical contexts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleft lip - a congenital split in the upper lip; the current medical term
