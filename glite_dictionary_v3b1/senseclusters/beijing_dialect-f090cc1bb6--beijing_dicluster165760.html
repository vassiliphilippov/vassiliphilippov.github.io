Beijing dialect

noun
the dialect of Chinese spoken in Beijing
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪˌdʒɪŋ ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (AmE), / ˈbeɪˌdʒɪŋ ˈdaɪ.əˌlɛkt / (BrE)
the variety of Chinese spoken in Beijing, which forms the basis of Standard Mandarin, the official language of China

Examples

Beijing dialect speech
Linguists often study the Beijing dialect to better understand the development of modern Standard Chinese.
Many television programs in China use the Beijing dialect for their broadcasts.
The Beijing dialect is considered the standard for Mandarin pronunciation in China.
