mitred

adjective
wearing a mitre
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtərd / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtəd / (BrE)
wearing, or entitled to wear, a mitre, especially referring to a bishop or church official.

Examples

Mitred bishop
The mitred archbishop led the procession through the ancient church.
A mitred figure stood at the altar, ready to begin the ceremony.
Only mitred clergy were allowed to participate in the special service.
