ghost

verb
haunt persistently like a ghost
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊst / (AmE), / ɡəʊst / (BrE)
(of a fear, memory, thought, etc.) to appear or persist in the mind in a way that is difficult to escape, like a haunting presence.

Examples

Bad memories ghost him
Memories of the accident continued to ghost him for years.
Old regrets still ghost his mind late at night.
The thought of failure would sometimes ghost her dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haunt - to repeatedly trouble, appear, or linger in the mind or memory
