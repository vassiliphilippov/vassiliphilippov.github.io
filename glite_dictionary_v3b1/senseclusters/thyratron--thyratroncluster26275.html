thyratron

noun
gas-filled switch tube
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθaɪrəˌtrɑn / (AmE), / ˈθaɪrəˌtrɒn / (BrE)
a gas-filled electronic tube used as a high-speed switch in circuits, especially for controlling large currents
