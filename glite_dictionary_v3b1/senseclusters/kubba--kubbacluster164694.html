kubba

noun
Middle Eastern dish
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːbə / (AmE), / ˈkuːbə / (BrE)
a Middle Eastern dish made of bulgur wheat and minced meat, often shaped into balls or patties and cooked by frying or baking

Examples

Fried kubba balls
We ordered kubba as an appetizer at the Iraqi restaurant.
My grandmother taught me how to make traditional kubba for family gatherings.
The chef served spicy kubba with a side of yogurt sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falafel - a deep-fried ball made from ground chickpeas or fava beans
  • synonym: kibbeh - a similar Middle Eastern dish of bulgur and meat
