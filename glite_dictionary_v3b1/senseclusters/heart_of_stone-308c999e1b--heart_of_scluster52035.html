heart of stone

noun
emotionally unfeeling; showing no compassion
2.1
Pronunciation: / hɑrt əv stoʊn / (AmE), / hɑːt əv stəʊn / (BrE)
an expression describing a person who is emotionally unfeeling, showing no compassion, pity, or empathy

Examples

A cold heart of stone
Despite his heart of stone, he agreed to help the orphaned child.
His heart of stone made working with him especially difficult during tough times.
People said she had a heart of stone when she refused to listen to their pleas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold-hearted - lacking in compassion or sympathy; unfeeling
  • synonym: heartless - completely lacking in compassion or kindness
