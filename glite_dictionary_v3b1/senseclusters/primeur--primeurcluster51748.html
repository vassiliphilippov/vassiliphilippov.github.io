primeur

noun
early produce seller
9.9
Pronunciation: / priːˈmɜr / (AmE), / priːˈmɜː / (BrE)
a seller or dealer of early-season fruit and vegetables, especially in French contexts

Examples

Local primeur shop
The local primeur always has the freshest strawberries at the start of the season.
We stopped by the primeur to buy some early peas and carrots for dinner.
In Paris, a primeur is known for offering the first fruits and vegetables of the year.
