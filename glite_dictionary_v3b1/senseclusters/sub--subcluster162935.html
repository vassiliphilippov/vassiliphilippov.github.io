sub

noun
long sandwich with various fillings
Pronunciation: / sʌb / (AmE), / sʌb / (BrE)
a sandwich made from a long split roll filled with meats, cheese, and other ingredients such as lettuce, tomato, and onion; commonly called a submarine sandwich

Examples

A turkey sub
I ordered a turkey sub with lettuce, tomato, and extra cheese for lunch.
She prefers a veggie sub over any other type of sandwich.
The deli around the corner makes the best Italian subs in town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grinder - Northeastern US term for a similar sandwich
  • synonym: hoagie - another American English term for a similar sandwich
  • degree lower: sandwich - two or more slices of bread with a filling
