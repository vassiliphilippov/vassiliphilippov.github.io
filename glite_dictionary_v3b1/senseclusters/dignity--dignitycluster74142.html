dignity

noun
worthiness of respect or honor
1.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪg.nɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈdɪg.nə.ti/ (AmE), /ˈdɪg.nɪ.ti/ /; / /ˈdɪg.nə.ti/ (BrE)
