uprise

verb
stand up on end (of hair, fur, etc.)
7.0 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈʌp.raɪz/ /; / /ə.ˈpraɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpraɪz/ /; / /ˈʌp.raɪz/ (BrE)
(of hair, fur, or similar) to stand up on end, especially as a reaction to fear or excitement.

Examples

Hair uprises in fear
A sudden gust of cold wind caused his hair to uprise in a strange, unsettling way.
The chilling story made the hairs on my arms uprise involuntarily.
When startled, the cat's fur would uprise along her spine, making her appear much larger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bristle - (of hair or fur) to stand up stiffly, as from fear or anger
