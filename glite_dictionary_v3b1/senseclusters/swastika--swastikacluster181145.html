swastika

noun
ancient cross-shaped symbol; Nazi emblem
5.3 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈswɑː.stɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈswɑː.stə.kə/ (AmE), /ˈswɒs.tɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈswɒs.tə.kə/ (BrE)
an ancient symbol consisting of a cross with arms bent at right angles, used as a religious sign in various cultures and, since the 20th century, as an emblem associated with Nazism

Examples

Ancient swastika symbol
Many ancient temples are decorated with the swastika symbol, signifying good fortune.
Some cultures continue to use the swastika in religious ceremonies, despite its modern associations.
The Nazis adopted the swastika as their official emblem, forever changing its perception in the West.
