size

noun
a substance for stiffening or filling pores in surfaces
Pronunciation: / saɪz / (AmE), / saɪz / (BrE)
a glutinous or gelatinous substance used to fill the pores of surfaces such as paper or textile, or to stiffen fabrics

Examples

Fabric size
Certain types of size are used to stiffen fine fabrics in tailoring.
