moneyless

adjective
not involving money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌləs / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌləs / (BrE)
Not using or involving money; not based on the possession or use of money.

Examples

Moneyless society
In a moneyless system, goods are exchanged through bartering rather than purchase.
Some communities experiment with moneyless living to promote sustainability and cooperation.
The documentary explored a moneyless society where resources are shared freely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cashless - not using cash for transactions
