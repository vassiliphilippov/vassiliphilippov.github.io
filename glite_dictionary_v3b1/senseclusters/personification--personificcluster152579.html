personification

noun
representation of ideas as people
8.3 6.0
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.sə.nɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /pər.sɑː.nə.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /pɜː.sə.nɪ.fɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
