shot

noun
an injection or vaccination
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt / (AmE), / ʃɒt / (BrE)
an injection of a drug or vaccine given by means of a syringe

Examples

A flu shot
He took his son to the clinic for a flu shot before winter started.
Many children are afraid of getting a shot at the pediatrician's office.
The doctor recommended that everyone get a tetanus shot every ten years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injection - the act of putting a liquid into the body by means of a syringe
  • synonym: vaccination - an injection to protect against disease
