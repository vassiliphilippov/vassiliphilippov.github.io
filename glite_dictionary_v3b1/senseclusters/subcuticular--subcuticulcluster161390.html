subcuticular

adjective
beneath the cuticle
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbkjuːˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkjuːˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring just beneath the outer layer of skin or a cuticle

Examples

Subcuticular stitch
The doctor recommended a subcuticular stitch for a cleaner scar.
A subcuticular injection is given just below the surface of the skin.
The wound was closed using a subcuticular suture technique to minimize scarring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subcutaneous - situated or applied under the skin
