quotient

noun
the result of division
Pronunciation: / ˈkwoʊʃənt / (AmE), / ˈkwəʊʃənt / (BrE)
the result of dividing one number or quantity by another

Examples

The quotient of numbers
In the exam, she calculated the quotient quickly and moved on to the next question.
The quotient of 20 divided by 4 is 5.
To solve the problem, find the quotient of the two numbers.
She explained how to find the quotient using long division.
The teacher asked us to write both the quotient and the remainder.
When you divide 12 by 4, the quotient is 3.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dividend - the number to be divided in a division operation
  • synonym: divisor - the number by which another number is divided
  • antonym: product - the result of multiplying two numbers
  • synonym: remainder - the amount left over after division
