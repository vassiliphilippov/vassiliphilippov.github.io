humaniform

adjective
having humanlike qualities
9.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhjuː.mə.nɪ.fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈhjuː.mə.nə.fɔːrm/ (AmE), /ˈhjuː.mə.nɪ.fɔːm/ /; / /ˈhjuː.mə.nə.fɔːm/ (BrE)

Examples

Humaniform behavior
