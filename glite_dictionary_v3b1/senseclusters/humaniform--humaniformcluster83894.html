humaniform

adjective
having humanlike qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmənɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmənɪˌfɔːm / (BrE)
having qualities of behavior, thought, or emotion that are similar to those of a human being

Examples

Humaniform behavior
The artificial intelligence responded in a humaniform way, showing empathy and understanding.
Her conversational robot displayed surprisingly warm responses, making its interactions feel oddly humaniform to users.
Writers often create humaniform characters to help readers relate to non-human beings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropomorphic - attributing human characteristics to non-human things
  • antonym: robotic - resembling or relating to a robot, often lacking human qualities
