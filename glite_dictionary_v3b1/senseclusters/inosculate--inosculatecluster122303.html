inosculate

verb
join or merge together (esp. of vessels)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑskjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒskjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to join or merge together, especially of tubes, blood vessels, or branches becoming interconnected
