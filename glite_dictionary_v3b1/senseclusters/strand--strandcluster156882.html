strand

noun
a single-string necklace (e.g. of pearls or beads)
Pronunciation: / strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
a necklace made by stringing together beads, pearls, or similar objects in a single line

Examples

A strand of pearls
My grandmother left me her favorite strand of amber beads.
She wore a beautiful strand of pearls around her neck.
The jeweler displayed a delicate strand with tiny silver charms.
