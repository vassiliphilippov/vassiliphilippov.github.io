genus Martes

noun
genus of mustelids including martens
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɑːr.tiːz / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈmɑː.tiːz / (BrE)
A genus of carnivorous mammals in the family Mustelidae, including the martens.
