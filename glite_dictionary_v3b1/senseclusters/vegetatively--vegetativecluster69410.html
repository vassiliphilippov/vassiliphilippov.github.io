vegetatively

adverb
by plant growth
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛdʒəˈteɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to plant growth or asexual reproduction, without seeds or spores

Examples

Plants grow vegetatively
Some plants spread vegetatively by producing new shoots from their roots.
The strawberry plant can reproduce vegetatively through its runners.
Gardeners often propagate certain species vegetatively to maintain desirable traits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asexually - without sexual reproduction
