jostling

noun
the act of pushing to make way
8.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɑː.slɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdʒɑː.sə.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɒ.slɪŋ/ /; / /ˈdʒɒ.sə.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the action of pushing, elbowing, or bumping against someone, typically in a crowd, to make one's way

Examples

Crowd jostling
After all the jostling at the entrance, she finally managed to get inside.
The jostling at the busy station made it hard to walk without bumping into someone.
There was a lot of jostling in the crowd as people tried to get closer to the stage.
