blowhard

noun
boastful, talkative person
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌhɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌhɑːd / (BrE)
A person who brags a lot or talks in an excessively boastful way.

Examples

A blowhard person
He comes across as a real blowhard at meetings, always bragging about his success.
Nobody likes working with a blowhard who never listens to others.
The politician's speech confirmed his reputation as a blowhard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boaster - Someone who boasts
  • synonym: braggart - A person who boasts about achievements or possessions
  • antonym: modest person - A person who does not boast
