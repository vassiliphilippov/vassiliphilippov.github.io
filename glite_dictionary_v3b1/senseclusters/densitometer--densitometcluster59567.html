densitometer

noun
instrument for measuring optical density
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛn.səˈtɑː.mə.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌdɛn.sɪˈtɒm.ɪ.tə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the optical or photographic density of a material

Examples

Photo densitometer
A densitometer can be used to ensure proper exposure in photographic prints.
In quality control, a densitometer helps monitor the consistency of printed materials.
The technician calibrated the densitometer before measuring the film's density.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spectrophotometer - an instrument used to measure the intensity of light at different wavelengths
