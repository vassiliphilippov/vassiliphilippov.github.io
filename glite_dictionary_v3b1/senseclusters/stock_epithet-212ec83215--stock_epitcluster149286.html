stock epithet

noun
repeated descriptive phrase in literature
9.1
Pronunciation: / stɑk ˈɛpɪˌθɛt / (AmE), / stɒk ˈɛpɪˌθɛt / (BrE)
a descriptive word or phrase, often repeated, that is used by an author to characterize a person or thing, especially in epic poetry or oral storytelling

A stock epithet phrase
