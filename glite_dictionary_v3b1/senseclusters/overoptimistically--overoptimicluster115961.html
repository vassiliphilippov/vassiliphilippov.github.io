overoptimistically

adverb
unrealistically positive
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˌɑptɪˈmɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows more hope or positivity than is realistic or reasonable

Examples

To overoptimistically predict results
He overoptimistically predicted that the repairs would only take a day.
The team overoptimistically assumed they would win every match this season.
Investors overoptimistically expected the new product to double profits within a month.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: optimistically - in a hopeful or positive way
  • antonym: realistically - in a way that accepts the true situation
