butcher's thumb

noun
method for testing meat quality by thumb
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃərz θʌm / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃəz θʌm / (BrE)
a method of skillfully measuring or assessing, especially used by butchers to test meat quality by pressing with the thumb

Examples

The butcher's thumb method
Old-school butchers trust the butcher's thumb method for grading meat quality.
The chef used a butcher's thumb to check the tenderness of the steak.
With years of experience, she relied on her butcher's thumb rather than a thermometer.
