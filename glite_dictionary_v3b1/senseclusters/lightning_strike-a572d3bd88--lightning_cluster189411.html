lightning strike

noun
sudden electrical discharge or burst of inspiration
4.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.nɪŋ straɪk/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.nɪŋ straɪk/ (BrE)
A sudden discharge of electricity in the atmosphere, usually during a thunderstorm; also, a sudden and powerful burst of creative thought or inspiration.

Examples

A lightning strike during storm
After a lightning strike, the house's electronics were all damaged.
Her artistic breakthrough was like a lightning strike of genius.
The forest was set ablaze by a lightning strike during last night's storm.
