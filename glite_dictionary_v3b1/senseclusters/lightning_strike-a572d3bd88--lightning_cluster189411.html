A sudden discharge of electricity in the atmosphere, usually during a thunderstorm; also, a sudden and powerful burst of creative thought or inspiration.

Examples

A lightning strike during storm
After a lightning strike, the house's electronics were all damaged.
Her artistic breakthrough was like a lightning strike of genius.
The forest was set ablaze by a lightning strike during last night's storm.

Synonyms

  • bolt of lightning
    2.1 0.48/m
    a bolt of lightning is a sudden visible flash crossing the sky, while a lightning strike focuses on the electrical discharge and where it hits.
  • bolt
    1.7 2.28/m
    a bolt is a quick flash or discharge seen in the sky, while a lightning strike refers to the actual electrical hit on an object.
  • lightning
    1.0 24/m
    lightning is the general bright electrical flash during storms, while a lightning strike names a single discharge that may hit the ground or something.
  • thunderbolt
    3.5 0.30/m
    a thunderbolt is a dramatic lightning flash often thought of with thunder, while a lightning strike is the electrical discharge itself.
