bearberry willow

noun
a low Arctic/alpine shrub (Salix reticulata)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrˌbɛri ˈwɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbɛəˌbɛri ˈwɪləʊ / (BrE)
A low, mat-forming shrub (Salix reticulata) found in Arctic and alpine regions of North America and Greenland, with deep green, elliptic leaves that taper at the base.

Examples

A bearberry willow shrub

The bearberry willow thrives in harsh Arctic environments, forming dense mats close to the ground.
