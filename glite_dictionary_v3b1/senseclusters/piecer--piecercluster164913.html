piecer

noun
assembler of parts
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.sər/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.sə/ (BrE)
a person who gathers separate facts or parts and puts them together to form a complete whole, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

Piecer of clues
As an investigative piecer, she was skilled at connecting scattered clues into a clear story.
The journalist acted as a piecer, gathering bits of information from various sources.
He became known as a piecer for his ability to reconstruct events from fragmented evidence.

  • synonym: compiler - a person who collects and organizes information
