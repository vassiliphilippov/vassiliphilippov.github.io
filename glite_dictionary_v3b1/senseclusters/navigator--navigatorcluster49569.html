navigator

noun
aircrew member who directs aircraft's course
2.2 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnævəˌɡeɪtər /; / ˈnævɪˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈnævɪɡeɪtə /; / ˈnavɪɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who plans and directs the route or course of a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle
