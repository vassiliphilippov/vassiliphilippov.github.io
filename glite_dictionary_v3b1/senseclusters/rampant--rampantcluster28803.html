rampant

adjective
(of a plant) growing unchecked and vigorously
8.3 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈræm.pənt/ (AmE), /ˈræm.pənt/ (BrE)
(of a plant) growing vigorously and without restraint, especially so as to spread quickly and become difficult to control.

Examples

Rampant weeds
A rampant growth of kudzu quickly covered the abandoned house.
He struggled to keep the rampant weeds from taking over his vegetable patch.
The garden was overrun with rampant ivy climbing the fences and trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invasive - (of a plant) tending to spread prolifically and undesirably
  • synonym: unchecked - not controlled or restrained
