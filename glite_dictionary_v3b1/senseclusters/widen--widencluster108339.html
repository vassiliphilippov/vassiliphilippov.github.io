Synonyms

  • broaden
    3.9 9.58/m
    broaden means to make something more extensive or inclusive, while widen focuses on increasing physical or metaphorical width.
  • expand
    2.5 74/m
    expand means to grow in size, amount, or range, while widen usually refers to making things broader in width or scope.
  • dilate
    6.1 1.49/m
    dilate means to become larger or more open, often in medical or formal settings, while widen is more general.
  • stretch
    1.0 26/m
    stretch means to pull something longer or wider by force, while widen simply means to increase width or range.
