widen

verb
make clothing larger by altering
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdən /; / ˈwaɪdn̩ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdən /; / ˈwaɪdn̩ / (BrE)
to make or become wider or broader; to increase the width, size, or scope of something

Examples

To widen a road
