brush

verb
touch or move lightly and briefly
1.3
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ / (AmE), / brʌʃ / (BrE)
to touch something lightly and briefly, especially in passing

Examples

To brush past
As she walked by, her skirt brushed his arm for an instant.
He brushed against the wet paint without noticing.
The bird brushed the top of the hedge with its wings as it flew past.
A sense of relief brushed over him when he heard the good news.
Her long skirt brushed the floor as she walked through the room.
The tree branches brushed against the windows during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graze - to touch or scrape lightly in passing
  • synonym: skim - to pass over lightly or rapidly
