compositional

adjective
relating to arrangement or grouping
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən.əl / (AmE), / ˌkɒm.pəˈzɪʃ.ən.əl / (BrE)
relating to the arrangement or grouping of elements in a work, such as in art, music, or other domains

Examples

Compositional techniques
She studied the compositional techniques used by classical composers.
The painting's compositional balance draws the viewer's eye to the central figure.
Their research focuses on the compositional structure of different minerals.

Related Terms
