pound

verb
move with heavy, noisy steps
1.3
Pronunciation: / paʊnd / (AmE), / paʊnd / (BrE)
to move with heavy, noisy, or clumsy steps, especially repeatedly or forcefully

Examples

To pound down the hall
He pounded across the gym floor in his heavy boots.
She pounded up the stairs, shaking the whole house with each step.
The soldiers pounded through the muddy field to reach their positions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lumber - to move in a slow, heavy, awkward way
  • synonym: trudge - to walk slowly and with heavy steps, typically from exhaustion
