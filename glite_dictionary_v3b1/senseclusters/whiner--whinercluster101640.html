whiner

noun
person who complains a lot
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnə / (BrE)
a person who complains a lot, especially in an annoying or petty way

Examples

A whiner complains

The coach told the team there was no room for a whiner on the field.
