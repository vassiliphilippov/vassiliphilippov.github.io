order Aspergillales

noun
order of fungi with closed ascocarps
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.dər æs.pə.rɪ.ˈgeɪ.liːz/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.də æs.pə.rɪ.ˈgeɪ.liːz/ (BrE)
A taxonomic order of fungi characterized by a closed ascocarp (cleistothecium) with asci scattered rather than arranged in a hymenium.
