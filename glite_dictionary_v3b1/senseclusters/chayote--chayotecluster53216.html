chayote

noun
pear-shaped vegetable
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈoʊteɪ / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈəʊti / (BrE)
a green, pear-shaped vegetable (the fruit of the chayote plant) commonly used in cooking for its mild flavor and crisp texture
