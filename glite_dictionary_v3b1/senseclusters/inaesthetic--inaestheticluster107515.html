inaesthetic

adjective
lacking beauty or aesthetic value
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪniːsˈθetɪk / (BrE)
not pleasing to the senses or lacking in beauty; violating standards of aesthetic taste

Examples

An inaesthetic color
His inaesthetic choice of colors clashed terribly with the elegant surroundings.
She found the painting inaesthetic and uninspiring compared to the others in the gallery.
The building's inaesthetic design drew criticism from the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aesthetic - pleasing in appearance or beauty
  • synonym: unaesthetic - not aesthetic; lacking in beauty or taste
