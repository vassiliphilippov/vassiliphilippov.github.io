church member

noun
a regular attendee of a church
Pronunciation: / tʃɝtʃ ˈmɛmbɚ / (AmE), / tʃɜːtʃ ˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
a person who regularly attends church services and is considered part of a church community

Examples

A church member volunteers
As a dedicated church member, she volunteers every weekend at the local food pantry.
Every church member was invited to participate in the annual fundraising event.
The new pastor made an effort to meet with each church member individually.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parishioner - a member or inhabitant of a parish, especially one who attends church
