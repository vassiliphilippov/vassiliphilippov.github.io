body

noun
main part of a communication
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
the main part of a written, spoken, or digital communication, not including the introduction, conclusion, or supplementary sections

Examples

Body of a letter
After reading the body of the letter, she finally understood the writer's concerns.
Please keep your questions focused on the body of the report.
The body of his email explained the new policy changes in detail.
