visionless

adjective
without foresight
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒənləs / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒənləs / (BrE)
lacking the ability to see or to plan for the future

Examples

A visionless leader
The leader's visionless approach failed to inspire confidence in the team.
A visionless government often struggles to address long-term challenges.
Critics described the proposal as visionless and lacking innovation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind - unable to see; lacking perception or awareness
  • synonym: shortsighted - not considering the future or long-term effects
  • antonym: visionary - having clear ideas about what should happen in the future
