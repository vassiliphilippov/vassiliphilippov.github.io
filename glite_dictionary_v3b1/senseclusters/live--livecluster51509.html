Synonyms

  • experience
    2.1 140/m
    experience means undergoing or feeling an event as it happens, while live means having a way of life or going through situations.
  • live on
    3.2 3.72/m
    live on means to continue by using a particular resource or income, while live is a general verb for supporting oneself.
  • survive
    2.7 21/m
    survive means managing to stay alive, often with hardship, while live is a neutral word for having life or living.
  • subsist
    7.2 1.75/m
    subsist means to stay alive on very little money or food, while live is more general and does not imply extreme lack.
  • exist
    1.9 38/m
    exist means to be alive or to continue in a basic state, while live suggests having a life with activity or support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subsist - to manage to live, especially with difficulty
  • degree higher: thrive - to grow, develop, or be successful; to flourish
