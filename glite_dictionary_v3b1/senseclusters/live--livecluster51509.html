live

verb
lead life in a certain style
1.9
Pronunciation: / lɪv / (AmE), / lɪv / (BrE)
to have a particular way of life, to experience or go through a situation, or to support oneself

Examples

After losing his job, he had to live very simply for several months.
During college, she learned how to live on a tight budget.
They live in luxury, traveling the world and staying in five-star hotels.
He felt as though he had lived a nightmare during that difficult period.
She has lived many hardships in her journey to success.
Some people never truly live their dreams, but she made hers come true.
After his illness, he realized he needed to live with more intention and joy.
She wants to live her life to the fullest, embracing every opportunity that comes her way.
Some people travel the world in search of the best way to live.
