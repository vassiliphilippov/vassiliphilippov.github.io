unfettered

adjective
not limited or restricted
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɛtɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɛtəd / (BrE)
Not limited or restricted; free and unrestrained.

Examples

Unfettered freedom
She valued her unfettered creativity as an independent artist.
The journalists reported on the issue with unfettered honesty.
The new law gives businesses unfettered access to the national market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free - not constrained or restricted; at liberty
  • antonym: restricted - limited in amount, extent, or range
