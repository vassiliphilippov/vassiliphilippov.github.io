microbrachia

noun
abnormally small arms (medical term)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.kroʊˈbrækiə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪ.krəʊˈbrækiə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by abnormally small arms.

Examples

A microbrachia condition
Cases of microbrachia are extremely rare and often associated with other congenital abnormalities.
Pediatricians monitor for signs of microbrachia during early childhood development screenings.
The genetic disorder resulted in microbrachia in both of the patient's arms.
