metope

noun
Doric frieze panel
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəpi / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəpi / (BrE)
a rectangular architectural panel between two triglyphs in a Doric frieze, often decorated with sculpture or relief

Examples

A metope panel
The sculptor carved mythological scenes into each metope of the ancient temple.
Many museums display fragments of metopes from classical Greek architecture.
The restoration project focused on cleaning the weathered metope panels above the columns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frieze - a horizontal band of decoration on a wall or building
  • antonym: triglyph - a tablet with three vertical grooves in a Doric frieze
