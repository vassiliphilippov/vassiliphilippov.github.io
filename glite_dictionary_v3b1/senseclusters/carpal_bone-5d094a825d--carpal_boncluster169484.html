carpal bone

noun
one of the small wrist bones
4.8 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ër.p…ôl bo än / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀê.p…ôl b…ô än / (BrE)
any of the eight small bones forming the wrist in primates, located between the forearm and the hand

Examples

A carpal bone fracture

Arthritis may develop in the carpal bones and cause pain or stiffness.
The human wrist contains eight carpal bones arranged in two rows.
