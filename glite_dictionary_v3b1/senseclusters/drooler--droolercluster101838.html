drooler

noun
person who dribbles saliva
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdruːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈdruːlə / (BrE)
a person, especially a baby, who lets saliva flow from the mouth

Examples

A drooler baby
As a teething baby, she was a constant drooler who needed plenty of bibs.
He joked that his grandfather was a bit of a drooler in his old age.
The new puppy is such a drooler that we have to wipe his mouth constantly.
