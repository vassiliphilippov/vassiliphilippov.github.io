inbetweener

noun
person in transition
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.bɪˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˌɪn.bɪˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that is in an intermediate or transitional stage between two groups, categories, or states

Examples

A social inbetweener
As an inbetweener, she didn't feel like she belonged to either group.
The company hired an inbetweener to bridge the gap between the two departments.
Teenagers often feel like inbetweeners as they move from childhood to adulthood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermediate - a person or thing situated between two stages or groups
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a particular group
