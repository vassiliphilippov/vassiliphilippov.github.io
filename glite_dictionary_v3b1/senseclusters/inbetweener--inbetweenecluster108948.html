inbetweener

noun
person in transition
7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.bɪ.ˈtwiː.nər/ /; / /ɪn.bə.ˈtwiː.nər/ (AmE), /ɪn.bɪ.ˈtwiː.nə/ /; / /ɪn.bə.ˈtwiː.nə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that is in an intermediate or transitional stage between two groups, categories, or states

Examples

Teenagers often feel like inbetweeners as they move from childhood to adulthood.
