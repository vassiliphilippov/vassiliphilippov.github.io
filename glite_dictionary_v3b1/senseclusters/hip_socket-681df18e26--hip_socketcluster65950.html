hip socket

noun
socket in the pelvis for the thigh bone
Pronunciation: / hɪp ˈsɑk.ɪt / (AmE), / hɪp ˈsɒk.ɪt / (BrE)
the cup-shaped cavity in the pelvis into which the head of the femur fits to form the hip joint

Examples

The hip socket joint
Arthritis can cause pain and stiffness in the hip socket.
The athlete suffered a dislocation of her hip socket during the game.
The surgeon examined the X-ray to check for damage to the hip socket.

  • synonym: acetabulum - the anatomical name for the hip socket
