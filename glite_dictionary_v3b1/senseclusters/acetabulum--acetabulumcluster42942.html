acetabulum

noun
socket in the hip bone for the femur
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /æ.sɪ.ˈtæb.jʊ.ləm/ /; / /ə.sə.ˈtæb.jə.ləm/ (AmE), /æ.sɪ.ˈtæb.jʊ.ləm/ /; / /ə.sɪ.ˈtæb.jə.ləm/ (BrE)
the cup-shaped socket in the hip bone that receives the head of the femur to form the hip joint
