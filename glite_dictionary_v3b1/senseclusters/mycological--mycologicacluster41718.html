mycological

adjective
relating to fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl /; / ˌmʌɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to fungi or the scientific study of fungi

Examples

Mycological study
The scientist published a mycological study on rare mushrooms found in the rainforest.
She joined a mycological society to learn more about identifying wild fungi.
The laboratory specializes in mycological testing for fungal infections.
The laboratory specializes in mycological research on rare species of mushrooms.
She published a mycological paper on the effects of fungi in agriculture.
A mycological survey revealed several new types of molds in the forest.
