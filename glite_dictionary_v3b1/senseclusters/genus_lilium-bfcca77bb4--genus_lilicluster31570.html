genus Lilium

noun
genus of true lilies (Liliaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈlɪli.əm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˈlɪlɪ.əm / (BrE)
The type genus of the lily family (Liliaceae), comprising the true lilies, widely cultivated for their large, showy flowers.
