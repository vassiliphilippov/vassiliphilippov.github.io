knotless

adjective
without knots (hair)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑtləs / (AmE), / ˈnɒtləs / (BrE)
describing braids or hair extensions made without tying small knots at the base, resulting in a smoother appearance

Examples

Knotless braids style
She prefers knotless braids because they are more comfortable and look natural.
The stylist recommended knotless extensions to reduce tension on the scalp.
Many people choose knotless styles for their lightweight feel and smooth finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braided - formed by interweaving three or more strands of hair
  • antonym: knotted - tied or fastened with a knot
