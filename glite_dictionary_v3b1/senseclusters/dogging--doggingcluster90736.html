dogging

noun
British slang for public sexual activity
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
(in British slang) the practice of engaging in or watching sexual activity in a public or semi-public place, often involving strangers and cars or outdoor locations.

Examples

Public dogging event
The police dispersed the crowd after reports of dogging in the park.
Local residents complained about cars arriving late at night for dogging near the riverbank.
He refused to discuss his involvement in dogging, saying it was a private matter.
