raconteur

noun
skilled storyteller
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌrækɑnˈtɜr / (AmE), / ˌrækɒnˈtɜː / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at telling stories or anecdotes in an entertaining way

Examples

A raconteur at parties
He was known as a brilliant raconteur at dinner parties, always ready with a captivating tale.
Her reputation as a raconteur made her the center of attention at every gathering.
The famous actor is as much a raconteur as he is a performer.
