addressability

noun
targeted reach capability
Pronunciation: / əˌdrɛsəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌdrɛsəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the ability of a system or platform to identify and reach specific individuals, households, or devices for targeted communication or advertising

Examples

Digital addressability
Digital marketers rely on addressability to deliver personalized ads to the right consumers.
The addressability of modern smart TVs allows advertisers to target specific households with relevant content.
Improved addressability in online platforms has changed how companies approach their advertising strategies.
