joker of the group

noun
person who entertains others with jokes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒoʊkɚ əv ðə ɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈdʒəʊkə əv ðə ɡruːp / (BrE)
a person in a group who frequently jokes or amuses others by making them laugh

Examples

The joker of the group
Ben is always the joker of the group and makes everyone feel at ease.
She became known as the joker of the group after her hilarious impressions.
Whenever there's an awkward silence, the joker of the group chimes in with a funny story.
