renderer

noun
animal byproduct processor
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛndərər / (AmE), / ˈrɛndərə / (BrE)
a person or company that processes animal carcasses or waste to extract fat and other byproducts

Examples

Animal renderer
The renderer collected animal waste from farms to produce tallow and other useful materials.
After the animals were processed, the remains were sent to a renderer for further treatment.
Local regulations require the renderer to follow strict environmental guidelines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: butcher - a person who slaughters animals for meat
  • synonym: processor - a person or company that treats materials to produce something
