royal yard

noun
yard on a ship's royalmast above the topgallant
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl jɑrd / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl jɑːd / (BrE)
the horizontal spar on the royalmast of a sailing ship, from which the royal sail is set, located above the topgallant yard.

Examples

The royal yard spar
During storms, it was risky work to furl the sails on the royal yard.
The royal yard must be hoisted carefully above the topgallant before setting the royal sail.
The sailors climbed up to the royal yard to adjust the highest sail.
