bonnethead

noun
small hammerhead shark species
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑnɪtˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbɒnɪtˌhɛd / (BrE)
a small, harmless species of hammerhead shark with a spade-shaped head, commonly found in bays and estuaries

Examples

A bonnethead shark
Scientists tagged several bonnetheads to study their migration patterns along the coastline.
The bonnethead is often seen swimming in shallow coastal waters during the summer months.
Unlike other hammerheads, the bonnethead has a distinctly spade-shaped head.
