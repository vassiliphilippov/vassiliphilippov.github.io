barbecuer

noun
outdoor cooking device
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjuːər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuːə / (BrE)

After cleaning the barbecuer, he was ready to cook steaks for his friends.
