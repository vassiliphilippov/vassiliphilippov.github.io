barbecuer

noun
outdoor cooking device
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbɪˌkjuːər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbɪˌkjuːə / (BrE)
a device or appliance used for cooking food over an open flame or on a grill outdoors

Examples

A barbecuer grill
They bought a new barbecuer with side burners and a large cooking surface for summer parties.
The family gathered around the barbecuer to grill burgers and vegetables on a sunny afternoon.
After cleaning the barbecuer, he was ready to cook steaks for his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbecue grill - a grill used for cooking food outdoors over a fire
  • antonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for cooking food with heat
