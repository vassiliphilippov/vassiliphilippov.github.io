jaguar

noun
large spotted wild cat of the Americas
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæɡwɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒæɡwɑː / (BrE)
a large, heavily built, spotted wild cat (Panthera onca) of Central and South America, closely related to the leopard

Examples

The jaguar cat
Conservationists are working hard to protect the jaguar from habitat loss.
The jaguar is known for its powerful jaws and beautiful spotted coat.
We saw a jaguar resting in the shade during our trip to the Brazilian rainforest.
