hormonally

adverb
involving hormones
Pronunciation: / hɔrˈmoʊnəli / (AmE), / hɔːˈməʊnəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is affected by hormones

Examples

To be hormonally regulated
Some medical conditions are hormonally regulated and require careful monitoring.
Teenagers can be hormonally sensitive during puberty, leading to mood changes.
The treatment works hormonally to balance the body's natural processes.
