parade

noun
ceremonial procession with marching
Pronunciation: / pəˈreɪd / (AmE), / pəˈreɪd / (BrE)
a ceremonial procession, often involving people marching in celebration, commemoration, or display

Examples

A city parade
A festive parade takes place every year to celebrate the town's founding.
The soldiers led the parade with their colorful uniforms and marching band.
We went to watch the Thanksgiving Day parade as it marched through the city streets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procession - a group of people moving forward in an orderly way, usually as part of a ceremony
