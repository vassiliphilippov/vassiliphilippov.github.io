opposing

adjective
actively competing or hostile
4.7
Pronunciation: / əˈpoʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpəʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
Actively competing against or hostile to someone or something; in conflict or opposition.

Examples

Opposing sides
The agreement was reached after long negotiations between opposing sides.
The two opposing teams shook hands before the match began.
There was a tense debate between the opposing political parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competing - striving against another for superiority or victory
  • antonym: supporting - giving support or approval to someone or something
