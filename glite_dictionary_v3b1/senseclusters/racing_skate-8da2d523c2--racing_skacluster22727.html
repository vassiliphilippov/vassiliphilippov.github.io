racing skate

noun
ice skate with long blade for racing
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.sɪŋ skeɪt / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.sɪŋ skeɪt / (BrE)
An ice skate with a long, thin blade, designed specifically for speed skating races.

Examples

A pair of racing skates
He switched to his racing skates before lining up for the 500-meter race.
Professional speed skaters always use racing skates for their competitions.
The long blades of racing skates provide greater stability and speed on the ice.
