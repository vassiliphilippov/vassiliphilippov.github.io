keep out

verb
prevent entry; shut out
1.3 7/m
Pronunciation: / kip a ät / (AmE), / kiÀêp a ät / (BrE)
to prevent someone or something from entering a place; to shut out

Examples

To keep out animals
Please keep out of the construction area for your safety.
Strict laws are used to keep out invasive species from the country.
The thick curtains kept out most of the noise from the street.
