rec room

noun
room for recreation or informal entertaining
Pronunciation: / rɛk ruːm / (AmE), / rɛk ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house equipped for relaxation or informal entertaining, often containing games or recreational equipment

Examples

A rec room with games
Our new house has a finished basement with a spacious rec room.
The kids spend most of their weekends hanging out in the rec room.
We put a pool table and a dartboard in the rec room.
