diagnostically

adverb
in a diagnostic way
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the process of identifying a problem or disease

Examples

Used diagnostically in medicine
The doctor used the scan diagnostically to determine the cause of the symptoms.
Blood tests are often performed diagnostically to rule out certain conditions.
The new software analyzes patient data diagnostically to assist in early detection of diseases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinically - in a way that relates to the observation and treatment of patients
