Hussein

proper noun
Iraqi president, 1979–2003
5.6
Pronunciation: /huː.ˈseɪn/ /; / /hʊ.ˈseɪn/ (AmE), /huː.ˈseɪn/ /; / /hjuː.ˈseɪn/ (BrE)
(Saddam Hussein) The Iraqi political leader (1937–2006) who was president of Iraq from 1979 to 2003, notable for leading Iraq in wars against Iran and Kuwait.

Examples

Hussein president Iraq
Hussein was the president of Iraq for almost 24 years.
Many books have been written about the regime of Hussein and its impact on Iraq.
The invasion of Kuwait by Hussein sparked the Gulf War.
