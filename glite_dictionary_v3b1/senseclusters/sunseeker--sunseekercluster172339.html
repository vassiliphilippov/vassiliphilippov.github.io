sunseeker

noun
person who loves sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌsiːkər / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌsiːkə / (BrE)
a person who enjoys spending time in sunny weather or traveling to sunny places

Examples

A true sunseeker
As a true sunseeker, she always vacations in tropical destinations.
Many sunseekers flock to the Mediterranean coast during the summer months.
The resort is popular among sunseekers looking for warm weather and clear skies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beachgoer - a person who frequently visits the beach
  • synonym: traveler - a person who travels to different places
