heave

verb
attempt to vomit, retch
6.7 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / hiv / (AmE), / hiÀêv / (BrE)
to make an unsuccessful effort to vomit; to strain as if to vomit, often producing a retching sound

Examples

To heave overboard
I watched the boatman suddenly heave over the side, pale and sweating.
She tried to heave after coughing so hard, but nothing came up.
The smell of rotten food made him heave in the kitchen.

Synonyms

  • retch
    7.2 0.73/m
    retch is a strong, involuntary effort that may bring up stomach contents, while heave is an unsuccessful strain that only makes a retching sound.
  • gag
    5.1 0.68/m
    gag is a throat reflex that makes you choke or feel like vomiting, while heave is a weaker strain that fails to bring up contents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retch - to make the sound and movement of vomiting without bringing anything up
