kino gum

noun
astringent gum from tropical trees
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːnoʊ ɡʌm / (AmE), / ˈkiːnəʊ ɡʌm / (BrE)
a reddish or black resin (gum) obtained from certain tropical trees, used in tanning leather and medicine
