Isthmus of Suez

proper noun
land bridge connecting Africa and Asia
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsθməs əv suˈɛz, ˈsuˌzɛz / (AmE), / ˈɪsθməs əv ˈsjuːɛz, ˈsuːɛz / (BrE)
the narrow land bridge in northeastern Egypt that connects the continents of Africa and Asia

Examples
