denizen

noun
inhabitant of a particular place
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnɪzən /; / ˈdɛn.ɪ.zən / (AmE), / ˈdɛnɪzən /; / ˈdɛn.ɪ.zən / (BrE)
a person, animal, or plant that lives in or is found in a particular place

Examples

A denizen of city
As a lifelong denizen of the city, she knows every hidden alley and café.
Many denizens of the forest remain unseen by human visitors.
The museum is a popular meeting place for the denizens of the local art community.
Cacti are denizens of arid landscapes, thriving where few other plants survive.
Some birds, once rare, have become regular denizens of local parks.
The coral reefs are home to many unusual denizens of the sea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreigner - a person who comes from a foreign country
  • synonym: inhabitant - a person or animal that lives in a particular place
  • synonym: resident - a person who lives somewhere permanently or on a long-term basis
