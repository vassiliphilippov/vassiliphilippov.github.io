complaining

adjective
habitually expressing dissatisfaction
1.2 3.1
Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpleɪ.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpleɪ.nɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples

The complaining employee was never satisfied with any decision made by management.
