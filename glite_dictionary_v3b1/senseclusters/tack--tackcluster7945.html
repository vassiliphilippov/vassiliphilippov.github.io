tack

noun
equipment for horses (saddles, bridles, etc.)
Pronunciation: / tæk / (AmE), / tæk / (BrE)
the equipment or harness used for riding or working horses, such as saddles, bridles, and reins

Examples

Horse tack set
It is important to check all your horse's tack before heading out.
She stored her riding tack in a neat, organized cupboard.
The stable hand cleaned the tack thoroughly after every ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harness - a set of straps and fittings by which a horse is fastened to a cart, plow, etc.
