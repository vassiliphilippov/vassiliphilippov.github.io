wheelie

noun
wheeled rubbish bin
4.5 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàwiÀêli / (BrE)
a large plastic bin with wheels, used for collecting household rubbish and easily moved by tilting and rolling it

Examples

A wheelie bin
The council collects the wheelie from every house once a week.
She struggled to move the heavy wheelie down the driveway on collection day.
Please remember to put your wheelie out on Thursday mornings.

Synonyms

  • toter
    8.0 0.51/m
    a toter is a wheeled plastic bin used to take household garbage to the curb, while a wheelie is moved by tilting and rolling.
