manipulator

noun
3D‑modelling UI control (move/rotate/scale)
7.7
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈnɪp.jə.leɪ.tər/ /; / /mə.ˈnɪp.jʊ.leɪ.tər/ (AmE), /mə.ˈnɪp.jə.leɪ.tə/ /; / /mə.ˈnɪp.jʊ.leɪ.tə/ (BrE)
(in computer graphics and 3D modelling) a graphical user-interface control (also called a gizmo) that lets a user move, rotate, or scale a digital object along one or more axes.

Examples

3D manipulator
During modeling, the manipulator lets you drag and rotate objects in 3D space.
To scale the character, she enabled the manipulator and pulled the blue axis handle upward.
Many beginners find the manipulators confusing until they learn each axis and snapping option.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gizmo - a small device or control; in graphics, an on-screen widget used to transform objects
  • synonym: transform tool - a software tool that moves, rotates, or scales objects in a digital scene
