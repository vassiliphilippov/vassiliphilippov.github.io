fracture

verb
break into pieces
4.3 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈfræk.tʃər/ /; / /ˈfræk.tjər/ (AmE), /ˈfræk.tʃə/ /; / /ˈfræk.tjə/ (BrE)
to break or crack something hard, such as a bone or a physical object

Examples

To fracture a bone
Repeated stress can fracture metal over time.
