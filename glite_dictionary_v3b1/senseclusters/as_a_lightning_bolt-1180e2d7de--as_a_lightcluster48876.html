as a lightning bolt

adverb
suddenly and unexpectedly
Pronunciation: / æz ə ˈlaɪtnɪŋ boʊlt / (AmE), / az ə ˈlaɪtnɪŋ bəʊlt / (BrE)
very suddenly and unexpectedly; with great surprise or shock.

Examples

Arrive as a lightning bolt
For many viewers, the unexpected plot twist arrived as a lightning bolt.
Her decision to leave the company came as a lightning bolt to her coworkers.
The news of the merger hit the staff as a lightning bolt, leaving everyone speechless.
