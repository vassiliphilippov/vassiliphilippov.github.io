strake

verb
to add planks to hull
9.9
Pronunciation: / streɪk / (AmE), / streɪk / (BrE)
to provide or mark with strakes, which are planks or strips fixed to a ship's hull.

Examples

To strake a hull
The workers carefully straked the wooden hull to ensure it was watertight.
Before painting, the team strakes the boat with new planks for extra strength.
Shipbuilders have straked vessels this way for centuries to improve durability.
