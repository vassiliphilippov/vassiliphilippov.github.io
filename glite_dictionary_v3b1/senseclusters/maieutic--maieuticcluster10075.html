maieutic

adjective
Socratic questioning method
9.9
Pronunciation: /maɪ.ə.ˈjuː.tɪk/ /; / /meɪ.ə.ˈjuː.tɪk/ (AmE), /maɪ.ə.ˈjuː.tɪk/ /; / /meɪ.ə.ˈjuː.tɪk/ (BrE)
