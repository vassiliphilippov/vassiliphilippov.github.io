maieutic

adjective
Socratic questioning method
Pronunciation: / maɪˈjuːtɪk / (AmE), / maɪˈjuːtɪk / (BrE)
relating to a method of teaching that helps someone discover knowledge by asking questions, especially as in the Socratic method

Examples

Maieutic teaching method
The professor's maieutic style encouraged students to think deeply and find answers themselves.
Using a maieutic approach, the mentor guided her mentee to new insights through careful questioning.
Many educators value maieutic techniques for promoting independent learning and critical thinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Socratic - relating to the teaching method of Socrates, using questions to stimulate critical thinking
  • antonym: didactic - intended to teach, especially in a moralizing way
