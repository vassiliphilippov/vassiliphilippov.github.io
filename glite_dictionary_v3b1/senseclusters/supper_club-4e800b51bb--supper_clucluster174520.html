supper club

noun
small luxurious nightclub or restaurant with entertainment
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌpər klʌb / (AmE), / ˈsʌpə klʌb / (BrE)
A small, often luxurious nightclub or restaurant where guests can dine and enjoy entertainment, typically in the evening.

Examples

A supper club dinner
Many supper clubs in the city are renowned for their gourmet cuisine and nightly entertainment.
The supper club offered an intimate atmosphere with velvet chairs and candlelit tables.
They spent Saturday night at a popular supper club downtown, enjoying live jazz and dinner.
