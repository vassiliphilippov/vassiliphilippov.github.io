henotheistic

adjective
belief in one main god
9.9
Pronunciation: /hen.ə.ˈθiː.ɪs.tɪk/ /; / /hiː.nə.ˈθiː.ɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /hen.ə.ˈθiː.ɪs.tɪk/ /; / /hiː.nə.ˈθiː.ɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)
believing in or worshipping one god while accepting the existence of other gods

Examples

Henotheistic belief system
The ancient tribe was henotheistic, honoring one primary god but acknowledging several others.
A henotheistic religion may focus worship on a single deity without denying the existence of others.
Scholars debate whether early Vedic traditions were truly henotheistic or simply polytheistic.
