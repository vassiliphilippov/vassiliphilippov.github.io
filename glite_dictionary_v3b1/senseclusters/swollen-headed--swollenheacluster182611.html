swollen-headed

adjective
conceited or arrogant
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈswoʊlən ˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈswəʊlən ˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
Having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance; conceited or arrogant.

Examples

A swollen-headed person
After winning the award, he became so swollen-headed that even his friends avoided him.
Don't get swollen-headed just because you received a compliment.
Her swollen-headed attitude made her unpopular among coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceited - having an excessively high opinion of oneself
  • synonym: egotistical - excessively self-centered or self-important
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest opinion of one's importance
