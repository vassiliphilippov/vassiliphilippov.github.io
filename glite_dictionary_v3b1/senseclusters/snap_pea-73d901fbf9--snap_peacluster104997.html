snap pea

noun
variety of edible pea with crisp pods
5.5
Pronunciation: / snæp pi / (AmE), / snæp piː / (BrE)
A variety of pea with edible, crisp, rounded pods that are eaten whole, both raw and cooked.

Examples

Fresh snap pea pods
Fresh snap pea pods add a sweet crunch to salads and stir-fries.
She picked a handful of snap peas from the garden for dinner.
The recipe calls for blanched snap peas tossed with sesame oil.
