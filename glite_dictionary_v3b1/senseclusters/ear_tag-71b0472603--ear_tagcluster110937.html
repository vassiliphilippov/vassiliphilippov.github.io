ear tag

noun
animal identification tag
Pronunciation: / ɪr tæɡ / (AmE), / ɪə tæɡ / (BrE)
a small tag attached to an animal's ear to identify and track it

Examples

A sheep ear tag
Each sheep received a yellow ear tag to help the farmer keep accurate records.
The veterinarian checked the ear tag to confirm the animal's identity before treatment.
Modern ear tags often include barcodes or electronic chips for easier tracking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branding - marking livestock with a symbol for identification
  • synonym: microchip - an electronic device implanted in animals for identification
