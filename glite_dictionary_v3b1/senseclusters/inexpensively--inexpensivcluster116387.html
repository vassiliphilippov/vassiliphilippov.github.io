inexpensively

adverb
at little cost; cheaply
6.1 2.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ɪk.ˈspen.sɪv.liː/ /; / /ɪn.ɪk.ˈspen.sɪv.ɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ɪk.ˈspen.sɪv.ɪ/ /; / /ɪn.ɪk.ˈspen.sɪv.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
