inexpensively

adverb
at little cost; cheaply
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.ɪkˈspɛn.sɪv.li /; / ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌɪn.ɪkˈspen.sɪv.li /; / ˌɪnɪkˈspensɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that does not cost much money; cheaply

Examples

Done inexpensively
The renovation was done inexpensively, but the results were impressive.
We managed to travel inexpensively by staying in hostels and taking local buses.
You can eat very inexpensively in this part of the city.
Many students eat inexpensively by shopping at local markets and preparing simple dishes.
The family managed to travel inexpensively by staying in hostels and cooking their own meals.
You can decorate your home inexpensively if you reuse and repurpose old furniture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheaply - at a low price; not costing much money
  • antonym: expensively - at a high price; costing a lot of money
