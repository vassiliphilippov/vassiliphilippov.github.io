condominial

adjective
of shared ownership
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːndəˈmɪniəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a condominium or shared property ownership

Examples

Condominial property ownership
The condominial model encourages residents to participate in building management decisions.
A condominial agreement outlines the rules for shared spaces in the complex.
Many urban developments now use a condominial approach to property ownership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: communal - shared by a group of people
  • antonym: private - belonging to or for the use of one person or group only
