haute couture

noun
high fashion; custom-fitted designer clothing
8.8 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / o ät kuÀêÀàt är / (AmE), / …ô ät kuÀêÀàtj ä…ô / (BrE)
the designing and making of high-end, custom-fitted fashion clothing, or the fashions produced in this way

Examples

Haute couture fashion
Many celebrities attend fashion week to see the latest haute couture collections.
She wore a stunning haute couture gown to the gala, custom-made by a top Parisian designer.
The art of haute couture requires thousands of hours of skilled craftsmanship.

Synonyms

  • couture
    9.1 3.27/m
    couture is the design and making of fashionable, high-quality clothes by leading fashion houses, while haute couture is custom and made-to-measure luxury clothing.
  • high fashion
    4.1 0.65/m
    high fashion is exclusive, trend-setting clothing often sold by luxury brands, while haute couture is custom-made, fitted clothing for individual clients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: high fashion - exclusive, trend-setting fashion, especially as produced by top designers
  • antonym: ready-to-wear - clothing made in standard sizes, not custom-fitted
