Skagerrak

proper noun
strait between Denmark and Norway
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskæɡəˌræk / (AmE), / ˈskæɡərak / (BrE)
a broad strait of the North Sea separating the Jutland Peninsula of Denmark and the southern coast of Norway.

Examples

The Skagerrak strait
During World War I, a major naval battle occurred in the waters of the Skagerrak.
Many ferries cross the Skagerrak each day, linking Denmark and Norway.
The Skagerrak is an important shipping route connecting the North Sea and the Baltic Sea.
