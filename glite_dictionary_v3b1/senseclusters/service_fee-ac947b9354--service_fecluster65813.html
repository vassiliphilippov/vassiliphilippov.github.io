service fee

noun
additional charge for service
2.7 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs…ùÀêv…™s fiÀê / (AmE), / Ààs…úÀêv…™s fiÀê / (BrE)
an additional charge, usually a percentage of the bill, added to cover the cost of service, especially in hotels and restaurants

Examples

A service fee added
Be sure to check if the service fee is included before leaving an additional tip.
Some restaurants automatically add a 15% service fee to large parties.
The hotel bill included a service fee for room cleaning and staff gratuity.

Synonyms

    3.2 0.21/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: tip - a voluntary amount of money given to someone for their service, especially in hospitality
