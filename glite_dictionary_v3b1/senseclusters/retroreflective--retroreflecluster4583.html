retroreflective

adjective
reflects light back
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊrɪˈflɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊrɪˈflɛktɪv / (BrE)
reflecting light back toward its source, especially for increased visibility or safety

Examples

Retroreflective vest
The cyclist wore a retroreflective vest to stay visible to drivers at night.
Road signs with retroreflective surfaces are easier to see in headlights.
Emergency vehicles often use retroreflective markings for safety in low light.
