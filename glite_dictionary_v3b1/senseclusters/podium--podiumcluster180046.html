A small raised platform on which a person stands to be seen by an audience, often when giving a speech or receiving an award.

Examples

A podium for speeches
Officials placed the medals on the podium before the ceremony began.
She approached the podium to deliver her acceptance speech.
The winner stood proudly on the podium as the crowd cheered.

Synonyms

  • rostrum
    7.1 0.20/m
    a rostrum is a formal raised platform that highlights a speaker at events, while a podium is usually a smaller stand for one person.
  • dais
    7.6 1.36/m
    a dais is a front platform where officials sit or stand, while a podium is a smaller standing platform used by one speaker.
