moonwalker

noun
moonwalk performer
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who performs the moonwalk, a dance move where the dancer appears to glide backward smoothly

Examples

Skilled moonwalker
The crowd cheered as the skilled moonwalker performed on stage during the talent show.
He became a popular moonwalker after posting his dance videos online.
Every aspiring moonwalker tries to master the smooth backward glide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moonwalk - a dance move where the dancer glides backward
