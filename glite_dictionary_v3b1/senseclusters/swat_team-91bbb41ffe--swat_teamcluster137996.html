SWAT team

noun
rapid cross-functional crisis team
Pronunciation: / swɑt tim / (AmE), / swɒt tiːm / (BrE)
a temporary, cross-functional group assembled in a business or organizational setting to respond rapidly to a serious or urgent problem and resolve it quickly.

Examples

IT SWAT team
The IT department formed a SWAT team to stop the data breach within hours.
When the app crashed worldwide, management sent a SWAT team to fix servers and communications.
They assembled several SWAT teams across regions to contain the supply-chain disruption quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incident response team - a group (often in IT or security) that manages and resolves incidents such as breaches or outages
  • synonym: task force - a temporary group assembled to work on a specific task or project, often across departments
