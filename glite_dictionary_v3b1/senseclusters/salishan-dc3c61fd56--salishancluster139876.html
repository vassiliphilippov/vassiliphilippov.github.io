Salishan

proper noun
Native peoples and languages
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪlɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈseɪlɪʃən / (BrE)
a group of related Native American peoples and languages of the Pacific Northwest region of North America

Examples

Salishan peoples
The Salishan languages are spoken by several tribes in the Pacific Northwest.
Anthropologists have documented the traditions of Salishan peoples for many years.
Many Salishan communities are working to preserve their native languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Salish - a specific Native American people and their language in the Pacific Northwest
