obsession

noun
unhealthy, persistent preoccupation
1.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /əb.ˈseʃ.ən/ (AmE), /əb.ˈseʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a strong and persistent preoccupation or interest with something or someone that dominates your thoughts or actions, sometimes in an unhealthy or irrational way

Examples

An obsession with winning
He developed an obsession with winning, which affected his relationships.
Her obsession with cleanliness led her to clean the house every day.
The detective's obsession with solving the case drove him to work late every night.
Despite therapy, his obsession with orderliness affected his daily routine.
His cleaning obsession led him to scrub the kitchen multiple times a day.

His obsession with video games sometimes made him forget to do his homework.

She talked about her new hobby with such obsession that her friends grew concerned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addiction - the condition of being dependent on a substance or activity
  • synonym: compulsion - an irresistible urge to behave in a certain way
  • synonym: fixation - an obsessive interest or feeling that dominates one's thoughts
  • antonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
  • synonym: preoccupation - the state of being absorbed or engrossed with something
