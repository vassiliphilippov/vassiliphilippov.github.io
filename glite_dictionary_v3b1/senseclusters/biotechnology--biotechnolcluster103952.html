biotechnology

noun
the use of biological systems for industrial purposes
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊ.tɛkˈnɑl.ə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌbʌɪ.əʊ.tɛkˈnɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
the use of living organisms or biological systems, especially microorganisms, to develop or manufacture products for specific industrial purposes

Examples

Biotechnology industry
Biotechnology has revolutionized the way we produce medicines and vaccines.
Many environmental solutions now rely on advances in biotechnology.
The government invested heavily in biotechnology to enhance agricultural yields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genetic engineering - the direct manipulation of an organism's genes using biotechnology
