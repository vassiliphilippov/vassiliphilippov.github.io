tropology

noun
study of figurative language
Pronunciation: / troʊˈpɑlədʒi / (AmE), / trəʊˈpɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study or use of figurative language, especially tropes such as metaphors and similes in literature

Examples

Tropology in poetry
She wrote her thesis on tropology and its influence on modern poetry.
A deep understanding of tropology can enhance literary analysis skills.
The professor's lecture on tropology explored the use of metaphor in classic novels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: literalism - adherence to the exact letter or meaning without figurative interpretation
  • synonym: rhetoric - the art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing
