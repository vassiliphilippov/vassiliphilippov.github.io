transvaginal

adjective
through the vagina
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzvəˈdʒaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌtranzvəˈdʒaɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or performed through the vagina, especially in medical procedures

Examples

Transvaginal ultrasound
The doctor recommended a transvaginal ultrasound to get a clearer image of the uterus.
A transvaginal procedure may be less invasive than traditional surgery.
Many gynecological exams include a transvaginal scan for better diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: transabdominal - relating to or performed through the abdominal wall
