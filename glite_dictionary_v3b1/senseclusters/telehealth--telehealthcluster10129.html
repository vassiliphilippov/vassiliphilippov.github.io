telehealth

noun
remote health care
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.helθ/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.helθ/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.helθ/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.helθ/ (BrE)

Examples

Telehealth appointment
