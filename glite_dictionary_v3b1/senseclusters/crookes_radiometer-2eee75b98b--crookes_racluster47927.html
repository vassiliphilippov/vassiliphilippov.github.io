Crookes radiometer

noun
light-driven scientific instrument
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊks ˌreɪdiˌɒˈmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈkrʊks ˌreɪdiˈɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a scientific instrument with lightweight vanes that spin when exposed to light, demonstrating radiant energy
