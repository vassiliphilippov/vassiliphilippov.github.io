brahmanical

adjective
of Brahmanism or Brahmins
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈmænɪkəl / (AmE), / brɑːˈmænɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to Brahmanism, the Brahmin caste, or their traditions and ancient texts

Examples

Brahmanical rituals
The temple follows brahmanical rituals and customs.
Many ancient texts were preserved by brahmanical scholars in India.
The festival is celebrated in a distinctly brahmanical manner in this region.
The brahmanical rituals described in the ancient texts are still practiced in some regions today.
Her research focused on the influence of brahmanical traditions in early Indian society.
Many brahmanical teachings emphasize the importance of ritual purity and social order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brahmin - a member of the highest Hindu caste
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
  • synonym: vedic - relating to the Vedas, ancient Hindu scriptures
