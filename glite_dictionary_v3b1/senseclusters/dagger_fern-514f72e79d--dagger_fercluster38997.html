dagger fern

noun
North American evergreen fern
Pronunciation: / ˈdæɡər fɝn / (AmE), / ˈdæɡə fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American evergreen fern with pinnate leaves and dense clusters of lance-shaped fronds.
