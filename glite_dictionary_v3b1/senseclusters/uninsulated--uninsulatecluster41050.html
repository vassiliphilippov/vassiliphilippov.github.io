uninsulated

adjective
without insulation
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈɪnsjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈɪnsjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not protected or covered with insulating material, allowing heat, electricity, or sound to pass through easily

Examples

Uninsulated walls
The old attic had uninsulated walls, making it very cold in winter.
Touching uninsulated wires can be extremely dangerous due to the risk of electric shock.
They replaced the uninsulated pipes to prevent heat loss in the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or protected; exposed
  • antonym: insulated - protected or covered with insulating material
