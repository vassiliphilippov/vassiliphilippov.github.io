effectively

adverb
in effect; in reality
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛktɪvli / (BrE)
In actuality; in effect or in reality, even if not officially so.

Examples

Effectively in charge
All flights were canceled, so the city was effectively cut off from the outside world.
Although they were not officially partners, she was effectively running the business.
He ignored the warning, effectively risking his job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actually - in fact; really
  • antonym: nominally - in name only; not in fact
