Lethean

adjective
relating to forgetfulness
Pronunciation: / ˈliːθiən / (AmE), / ˈliːθiən / (BrE)
causing or relating to forgetfulness or oblivion, especially as in Greek mythology

Examples

Lethean effect
The old house had a Lethean atmosphere, making visitors forget their worries.
He fell into a Lethean sleep, waking with no memory of the night before.
The novel's ending was shrouded in a Lethean sense of loss and oblivion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forgetful - tending to forget things
  • synonym: oblivious - not aware of or not remembering something
  • antonym: remembering - able to recall or keep in mind
