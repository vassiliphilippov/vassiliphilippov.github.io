unfirm

adjective
not stable; loose or shifting
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɜrm /; / ʌnˈfɝːm / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɜːm / (BrE)
not firm, stable, or solid; loose or likely to move or change position

Examples
