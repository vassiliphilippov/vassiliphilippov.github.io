drifty

adjective
absent-minded
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪfti / (AmE), / ˈdrɪfti / (BrE)
tending to be distracted, absent-minded, or not focused

Examples

Drifty thoughts
He gave a drifty answer, as if his mind was somewhere else.
During the long lecture, I started to feel drifty and lost track of the topic.
Her drifty manner made it hard for her to finish her assignments on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absent-minded - forgetful or inattentive
  • synonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because of other thoughts
  • antonym: focused - able to concentrate on one thing
