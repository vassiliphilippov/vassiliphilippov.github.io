overproof

noun
strong alcoholic spirit
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈpruːf / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpruːf / (BrE)
an alcoholic spirit that has a higher alcohol content than the standard proof level.

A bottle of overproof

Some people find overproof too strong to drink straight.
