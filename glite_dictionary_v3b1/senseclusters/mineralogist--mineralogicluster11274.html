mineralogist

noun
scientist specializing in minerals
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnərəˈlɑdʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌmɪnərəˈlɒdʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who specializes in the study of minerals and their properties, classification, and formation

Examples

A mineralogist studies minerals
As a mineralogist, she spent years researching the crystal structures of rare minerals.
Museums often employ a mineralogist to catalog and care for their mineral collections.
The mineralogist examined the rock sample to determine its exact composition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geologist - a scientist who studies the Earth's physical structure and substances
  • synonym: petrologist - a scientist who studies rocks
