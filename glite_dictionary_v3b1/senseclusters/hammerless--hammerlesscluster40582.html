hammerless

adjective
without external hammer
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərləs / (AmE), / ˈhæmələs / (BrE)
describing a firearm that does not have an external hammer mechanism

Examples

A hammerless revolver
The police officer carried a hammerless revolver for added safety.
Many hunters choose hammerless shotguns because they are less likely to snag on clothing.
A hammerless design can make a firearm easier to operate quickly in emergencies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hammered - having an external hammer mechanism
