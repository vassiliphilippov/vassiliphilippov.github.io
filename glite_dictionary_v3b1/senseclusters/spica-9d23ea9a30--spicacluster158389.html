Acronymic name for the planned space observatory Space Infrared Telescope for Cosmology and Astrophysics (SPICA), an infrared telescope mission intended to study the cold universe.

Examples

Spica space telescope
The planned space telescope Spica aimed to study the cold universe in infrared wavelengths.
Scientists proposed Spica as a successor to earlier infrared observatories for deep cosmology surveys.
Funding uncertainties delayed Spica, but researchers continued designing its sensitive detectors and instruments.
