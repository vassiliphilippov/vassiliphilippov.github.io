sanctity

noun
holiness; sacredness
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋktəti / (AmE), / ˈsaŋktɪti / (BrE)
The quality or state of being holy, sacred, or saintly.

Examples

The sanctity of marriage
Many people believe in the sanctity of marriage.
She spoke reverently about the sanctity of human life.
The sanctity of the temple was respected by all who entered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiness - the state of being holy
  • antonym: profane - not sacred or holy
  • synonym: sacredness - the quality of being sacred or revered
