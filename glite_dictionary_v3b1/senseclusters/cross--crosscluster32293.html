cross

adjective
lying across; at right angles
Pronunciation: / krɔs / (AmE), / krɒs / (BrE)
lying or extending across something; situated at right angles to the main axis

Examples

Cross beams
A cross brace enhances the stability of the framework.
The engineer installed a cross beam to support the structure.
The staircase is located in the cross hall, running across the main axis of the house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: longitudinal - running lengthwise rather than across; parallel to the long axis
  • synonym: transverse - lying or placed across something; crosswise
