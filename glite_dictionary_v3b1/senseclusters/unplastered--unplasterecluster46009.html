unplastered

adjective
not covered with plaster
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplæstərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɑːstəd / (BrE)
not covered or coated with plaster, especially referring to walls or ceilings

Examples

Unplastered walls
The old cottage had unplastered walls that revealed the original stonework.
They decided to leave one room unplastered to create a rustic look.
During renovations, several unplastered ceilings exposed the wooden beams above.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered or decorated; exposed
  • antonym: plastered - covered with a layer of plaster
