exarch

noun
bishop below a patriarch in Eastern Christianity
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…õ…°.z…ërk /; / Àà…õk.s…ërk / (AmE), / Àà…õk.s…ëÀêk / (BrE)
