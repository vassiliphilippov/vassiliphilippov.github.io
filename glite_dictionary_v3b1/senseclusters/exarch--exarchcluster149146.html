exarch

noun
bishop below a patriarch in Eastern Christianity
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈek.sɑːrk/ (AmE), /ˈek.sɑːk/ (BrE)
a senior bishop in Eastern Christianity, ranking below a patriarch and often with authority over other bishops in a region
