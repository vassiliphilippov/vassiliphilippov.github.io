gift

noun
the act of giving
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɪft / (AmE), / ɡɪft / (BrE)
the act of giving something to someone without expecting payment or return

Examples

The gift of time
He was honored for the gift of his time and talent to the community.
Her generous gift to the foundation made a lasting impact.
The annual gift of food to the shelter helps many families in need.
