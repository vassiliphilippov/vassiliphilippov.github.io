tam-tam

noun
large flat percussion instrument (like gong)
9.7 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtæm.tæm/ (AmE), /ˈtæm.tæm/ (BrE)
a percussion instrument consisting of a flat metal disc or plate, similar to a gong, that is struck with a soft-headed mallet to produce a resonant sound

Examples

A tam-tam instrument
A deep, shimmering sound echoed as the percussionist played the tam-tam at the back of the stage.
He carefully suspended the tam-tam before the performance began.
The orchestra featured a dramatic tam-tam strike during the climax of the piece.
