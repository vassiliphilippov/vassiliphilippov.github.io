lady

noun
polite term for a woman
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
A polite or respectful way of referring to a woman.

Examples

A nice lady
A kind lady offered to help me carry the groceries.
Please allow the lady to go ahead of you in line.
The lady at the front desk answered all my questions politely.
