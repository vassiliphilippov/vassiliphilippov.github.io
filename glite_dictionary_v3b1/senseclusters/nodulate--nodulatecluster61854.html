nodulate

verb
to form nodules
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdʒəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɒdjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to form or develop small lumps or nodules, especially on plant roots as part of a biological process

Examples

To nodulate roots
Legume roots often nodulate when they interact with certain soil bacteria.
Scientists study how plants nodulate to improve crop yields and soil health.
If the soil lacks the right bacteria, the plants may not nodulate properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nodule - a small lump or swelling, especially on a plant root
  • antonym: smooth - having an even surface without lumps or nodules
