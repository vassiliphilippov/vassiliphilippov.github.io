to go to see a person or place, either as a guest, for pleasure, for official or professional reasons, or for a short time

Examples

To visit a friend
He visited his cousin in New York and stayed for the entire summer.
We visited my grandparents for two weeks during the holidays.
Whenever she travels abroad, she always visits friends who live there.
Many tourists visit the Grand Canyon every year to admire its beauty.
Next summer, I want to visit all the famous landmarks in Rome.
We decided to visit the national museum during our city trip.
The health inspector will visit the restaurant tomorrow to ensure standards are met.
The principal often visits classrooms to observe teachers and speak with students.
The senator visited the community center as part of her official duties.

Synonyms

  • pay a visit
    2.3 1.53/m
    pay a visit means going briefly to someone or a place, while visit covers both short, formal, or longer trips.
  • call on
    3.1 2.23/m
    call on means visiting someone briefly for a particular purpose, while visit is more general for pleasure or official reasons.
  • call in
    2.8 0.03/m
    call in is to stop by briefly, often without arrangement, while visit can be planned and may be longer or formal.
  • see
    1.0 164/m
    see is often used for visiting places or events for entertainment, while visit includes meeting people or official purposes too.
