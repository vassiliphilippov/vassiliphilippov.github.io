Hyperstat

noun
trade name for a vasodilator drug
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.pər.stæt / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.pə.stæt / (BrE)
A trade name for diazoxide, a vasodilator formerly used to treat severe hypertension.

Examples

Hyperstat medication
After the introduction of newer medications, Hyperstat became less common in the treatment of hypertension.
Because Hyperstat acts as a potent vasodilator, it was administered in emergency situations.
The doctor prescribed Hyperstat to quickly reduce the patient's dangerously high blood pressure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diazoxide - a drug used to lower blood pressure
