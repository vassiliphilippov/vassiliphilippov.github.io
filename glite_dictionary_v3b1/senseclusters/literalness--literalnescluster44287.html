literalness

noun
quality of being literal
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.ɚ.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.ər.əl.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being literal, especially in taking words or actions at their most basic and concrete meaning without interpreting or embellishing.

Examples

The literalness of words
Legal documents require a certain literalness to avoid ambiguity.
She criticized the literalness with which he read the poem, missing its deeper meaning.
The literalness of his interpretation left no room for imagination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: figurative - departing from a literal use of words; metaphorical
  • synonym: literal - taking words in their usual or most basic sense
