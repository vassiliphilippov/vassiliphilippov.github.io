wedgie

noun
shoe with a wedge heel
6.8 8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛdʒ.i / (AmE), / ˈwɛdʒ.i / (BrE)
A women's shoe with a heel that forms a continuous wedge-shaped sole from the back to the middle or front of the foot.

Examples

Wedgie sandals
Many sandals come in both flat and wedgie styles.
She bought a pair of stylish wedgies for the summer party.
The wedgie added extra height without sacrificing comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wedge heel - a heel in the form of a wedge that runs from the heel to the mid or forefoot
