vibrant palette

noun
bright and lively selection of colors
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈpælət / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrənt ˈpalət / (BrE)
a selection of colors that are bright, vivid, and full of energy, often used in painting or design

Examples

A vibrant palette of colors
Many designers prefer a vibrant palette to attract attention to their creations.
The artist was known for using a vibrant palette in all of her work.
The living room was decorated with a vibrant palette to create a lively atmosphere.
