desexualize

verb
make infertile; remove reproductive ability
Pronunciation: / diˈsɛkʃuəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / diˈsɛksjʊəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make infertile or incapable of reproduction, often by removing or altering reproductive organs or functions
