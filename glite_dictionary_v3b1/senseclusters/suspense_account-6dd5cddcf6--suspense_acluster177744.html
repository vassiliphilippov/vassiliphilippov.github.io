suspense account

noun
temporary account for uncertain transactions
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛns əˌkaʊnt / (AmE), / səˈspɛns əˌkaʊnt / (BrE)
an account in which uncertain or questionable receipts and payments are temporarily recorded until their correct classification is determined

Examples

The suspense account
An audit revealed several entries lingering in the suspense account from previous months.
At the end of the year, all balances in the suspense account must be investigated and resolved.
The company placed the ambiguous transaction into a suspense account until it could be properly classified.
