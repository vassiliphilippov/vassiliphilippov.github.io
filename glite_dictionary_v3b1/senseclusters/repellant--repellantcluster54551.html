repellant

noun
the power to repel
5.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɛlənt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɛlənt / (BrE)
the power or quality of driving something away or causing aversion

Examples

Repellant effect
His arrogance had a strong repellant effect on those around him.
Plants sometimes produce natural repellants to deter insects and animals.
The repellant she used kept the mosquitoes away all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repellent - the power to repel; something repulsive or serving to drive away
