nonacute

adjective
not sharp-angled
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈkjuːt / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈkjuːt / (BrE)
not acute; in geometry, describing an angle or triangle that has at least one angle equal to or greater than 90 degrees.

Examples

Nonacute angle
In the diagram, the nonacute angle was clearly marked with a square symbol.
A triangle with a nonacute angle cannot be classified as an acute triangle.
The teacher asked the class to find a nonacute angle among the shapes on the board.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acute - having an angle less than 90 degrees
  • synonym: obtuse - having an angle greater than 90 degrees and less than 180 degrees
  • synonym: right - having an angle of exactly 90 degrees
