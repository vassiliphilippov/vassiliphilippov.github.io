groundlessness

noun
lack of valid basis or substance
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraʊndləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈɡraʊndləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking a valid basis, substance, or justification

Examples

The groundlessness of claims
His argument collapsed under the groundlessness of the assumptions.
She was frustrated by the groundlessness of the accusations against her.
The committee quickly pointed out the groundlessness of the rumors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baselessness - the state of being without foundation or support
  • antonym: validity - the quality of being logically or factually sound
