cultural movement

noun
group working to advance cultural goals
5.5 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.tʃər.əl ˈmuːv.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.tʃə.rəl ˈmuːv.mənt/ /; / /ˈkʌl.tʃʊə.rəl ˈmuːv.mənt/ (BrE)
a group effort by people aiming to promote specific cultural values, ideas, or practices, often leading to widespread change in society

Examples

The cultural movement
Many artists contributed to the cultural movement that redefined modern art.
The Harlem Renaissance was an influential cultural movement in the early twentieth century.
The rise of social media has fueled new cultural movements around the world.
