Frost's bolete

noun
a North American red-capped fungus
9.9
Pronunciation: / frɔsts boʊˈlit / (AmE), / frɒsts bəʊˈliːt / (BrE)
a type of fungus with a red cap and a red, coarsely netted stalk, found in North America

Examples

Frost's bolete mushroom
Many mushroom hunters search the woods in autumn for the distinctive Frost's bolete.
The bright red stalk of the Frost's bolete sets it apart from other boletes.
You can identify a Frost's bolete by its red, coarsely reticulate stem and cap.
