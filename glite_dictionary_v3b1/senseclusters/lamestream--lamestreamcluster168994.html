lamestream

noun
derogatory for mainstream media
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪmstriːm / (AmE), / ˈleɪmstriːm / (BrE)
an informal, derogatory term for mainstream media, especially when considered biased, dull, or untrustworthy

Examples

The lamestream media
Some critics accuse the lamestream of ignoring important stories that do not fit their agenda.
He prefers independent blogs over the lamestream because he finds them more trustworthy.
The politician used the term lamestream to dismiss negative coverage from major news outlets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alternative media - media sources outside the mainstream
  • synonym: mainstream media - the large, established news organizations
