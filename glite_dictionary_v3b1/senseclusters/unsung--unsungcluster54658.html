unsung

adjective
not celebrated or recognized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈsʌŋ / (BrE)
not celebrated, praised, or recognized, especially when deserving it

Examples

An unsung hero
Many unsung individuals make important contributions behind the scenes.
The teacher was one of the unsung heroes of the community.
Their efforts remained unsung for years until they were finally acknowledged.
Many of the team's most important members remain unsung despite their hard work.
She was an unsung hero who quietly made an enormous difference in the community.
The museum's collection contains many unsung masterpieces rarely seen by the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: celebrated - famous or widely praised; opposite of 'unsung'
  • synonym: unheralded - not previously announced, recognized, or celebrated
