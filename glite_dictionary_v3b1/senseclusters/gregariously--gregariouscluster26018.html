gregariously

adverb
in a sociable manner
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈɡɛriəsli / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈɡɛːrɪəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that is sociable and enjoys the company of others; in a gregarious way.

Examples

To gregariously join groups
He gregariously introduced himself at the meeting, eager to make new contacts.
She moved gregariously among the guests, making everyone feel at home.
The children played gregariously in the park, laughing and calling out to one another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociably - in a way that is friendly or enjoys the company of others
  • antonym: solitarily - in a solitary or alone manner
