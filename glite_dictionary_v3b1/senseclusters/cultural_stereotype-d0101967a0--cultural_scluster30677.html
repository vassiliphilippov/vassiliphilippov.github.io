cultural stereotype

noun
oversimplified belief about a culture
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltʃɚəl ˈstɛriəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈkʌltʃərəl ˈstɛriətaɪp / (BrE)
a widely held but oversimplified and generalized belief or idea about the characteristics of a particular cultural group

Examples

Many people are trying to break free from the cultural stereotype associated with their background.
Teachers must be careful not to reinforce cultural stereotypes in the classroom.
The film challenges the cultural stereotype that all artists are unconventional.
