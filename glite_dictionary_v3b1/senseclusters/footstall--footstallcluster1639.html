footstall

noun
architectural base or support
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌstɔl / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌstɔːl / (BrE)
an architectural base or support for a column or statue

Examples

A footstall base
Each column rose from a solid granite footstall to support the roof of the temple.
The architect specified that every footstall should be carved from limestone to match the building’s façade.
The sculptor placed the statue on a marble footstall for a more impressive display.
