busy as a bee

adjective
very busy or industrious
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪzi æz ə bi / (AmE), / ˈbɪzi æz ə biː / (BrE)
extremely busy or industrious, working hard and continuously

Examples

Busy as a bee working hard
During tax season, accountants are busy as a bee helping their clients.
Even on Saturdays, she's always busy as a bee organizing community events.
With all the preparations for the wedding, he was busy as a bee from morning till night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard-working - diligent; putting a lot of effort into work
  • antonym: idle - not active or working; doing nothing
