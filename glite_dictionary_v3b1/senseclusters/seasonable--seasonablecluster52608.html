seasonable

adjective
occurring at an appropriate time
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzənəbəl / (AmE), / ˈsiːzənəbl / (BrE)
occurring or done at an appropriate or proper time; timely

Examples

Seasonable advice
A seasonable rain helped the crops grow after a long drought.
Her advice proved seasonable as the situation quickly escalated.
We waited until a seasonable hour to begin the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timely - done at a suitable or opportune moment
  • antonym: untimely - occurring at an inappropriate or unsuitable time
