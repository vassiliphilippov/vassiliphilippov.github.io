polydrug

noun
use of multiple drugs
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
the use of more than one drug, either simultaneously or over a period of time, often leading to increased health risks

Examples

Polydrug use disorder
The clinic offers special programs for people with polydrug use disorders.
Researchers are studying the effects of polydrug abuse on long-term health outcomes.
Cases of polydrug overdose have become more common in recent years.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monodrug - the use of a single drug
  • synonym: polypharmacy - the use of multiple medications by a patient, especially in older adults
