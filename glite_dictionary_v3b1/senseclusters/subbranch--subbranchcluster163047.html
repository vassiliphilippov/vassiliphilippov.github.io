subbranch

noun
smaller division
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌbræntʃ / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌbrɑːntʃ / (BrE)
a smaller division or section that extends from a main branch within an organization, system, or structure

Examples

Bank subbranch
The bank opened a new subbranch to serve customers in the rural area.
Each subbranch of the library system has its own collection and staff.
The organization restructured, creating several subbranches to handle different regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branch - a main division or section of an organization or system
  • synonym: division - a part or section of a larger organization
  • antonym: headquarters - the main office or center of operations
