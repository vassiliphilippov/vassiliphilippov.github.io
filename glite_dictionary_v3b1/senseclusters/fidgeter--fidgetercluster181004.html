fidgeter

noun
restless person
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪdʒɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfɪdʒɪtə / (BrE)
a person who moves restlessly or cannot stay still, often because of nervousness or boredom

Examples

A fidgeter in class
The teacher noticed a fidgeter in the back row who kept tapping his pencil.
During long meetings, my brother is always the biggest fidgeter in the room.
Even as a child, she was known as a fidgeter who could never sit still for long.
The teacher noticed a fidgeter tapping his pencil during the exam.
As a child, she was always a fidgeter and found it hard to sit still.
During long meetings, the fidgeter kept shifting in his seat and playing with his pen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing nervousness or restlessness
  • synonym: restless - unable to stay still or relax
