self-starter

noun
motor that starts an engine automatically
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈstɑrtər / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a motor that automatically starts an internal-combustion engine, especially in cars or other vehicles

Examples

Car self-starter
Most modern vehicles are equipped with a self-starter to simplify ignition.
The mechanic replaced the faulty self-starter in my car last week.
Without a functioning self-starter, the engine would not turn over.
