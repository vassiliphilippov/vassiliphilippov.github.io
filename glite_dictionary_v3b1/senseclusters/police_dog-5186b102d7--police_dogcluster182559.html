police dog

noun
dog trained to assist police
3.2
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis dɔɡ / (AmE), / pəˈliːs dɒɡ / (BrE)
A dog that is trained to assist police, especially in tracking criminals or detecting drugs and explosives.

Examples

Police dog sniffs luggage
At the airport, a police dog sniffed the luggage for illegal substances.
Officers rely on their police dogs to help track down suspects.
The police dog quickly found the missing child in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniffer dog - a dog trained to detect substances such as drugs or explosives by smell
