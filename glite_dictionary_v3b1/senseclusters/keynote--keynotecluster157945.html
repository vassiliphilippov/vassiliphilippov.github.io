keynote

noun
main conference speech
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈkiːnəʊt / (BrE)
the main speech or presentation at a meeting or conference, setting the central theme or tone

Examples

Keynote speech
The conference opened with a powerful keynote delivered by a renowned scientist.
She was honored to give the keynote at the annual technology summit.
Attendees were inspired by the keynote that set the tone for the entire event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: address - a formal speech delivered to an audience
  • synonym: lecture - an educational talk to an audience
