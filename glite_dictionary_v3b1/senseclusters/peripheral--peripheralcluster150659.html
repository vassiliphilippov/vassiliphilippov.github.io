peripheral

adjective
not central; minor or secondary
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɪfərəl / (AmE), / pəˈrɪfərəl / (BrE)
Not as important as the main subject; relating to or situated on the edge or periphery of something.

Examples

Peripheral details
His comments were interesting but largely peripheral to the main topic.
The committee discussed several peripheral issues before tackling the core problem.
While technology is important, it remains peripheral to the main objectives of the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: central - of primary importance; focused on the main point
