unarousing

adjective
not exciting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈraʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈraʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing excitement, interest, or emotional stimulation

Examples

Unarousing speech
The lecture was so unarousing that several students struggled to stay awake.
Despite the beautiful setting, the play felt unarousing and failed to capture the audience's attention.
He found the book unarousing and quickly lost interest after the first few chapters.

  • antonym: stimulating - causing excitement or interest
