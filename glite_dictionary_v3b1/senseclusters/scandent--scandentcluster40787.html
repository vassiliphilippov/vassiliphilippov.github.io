scandent

adjective
(botany) climbing or ascending (of plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈskændənt / (AmE), / ˈskændənt / (BrE)
(of a plant) having a tendency to climb or grow upwards by clinging to other objects.

Examples

Scandent plant growth
Clematis is a popular scandent plant often grown on garden fences.
Some species in the rainforest have developed scandent stems to access the upper canopy.
The scandent vines covered the trellis, reaching towards the sunlight.
