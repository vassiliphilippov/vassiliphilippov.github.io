model

noun
female fashion model
3.9 16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëd…ôl /; / Ààm…ëÀêd…ôl / (AmE), / Ààm…íd…ôl / (BrE)
a person whose job is to display clothes or pose for artists or photographers

Examples

A fashion model
Many magazines feature a model on the cover to attract readers.
She dreamed of becoming a model and traveling to Paris for fashion shows.
The model walked gracefully down the runway, showcasing the designer's new collection.
Aspiring to become a model, she started working with several local painters.
The model held her pose for the art class as students sketched her silhouette.
The photographer hired a model to showcase the latest collection.

Synonyms

  • fashion model
    2.7 0.43/m
    fashion model wears and shows clothes on runways or in photos, while model can also pose for artists or photographers.
  • mannequin
    4.1 0.42/m
    mannequin models clothes for designers or on catwalks, while model is the general word for someone who displays clothing or poses.
  • photographer's model
    3.9 0.01/m
    photographer's model poses mainly for photographers in studios or shoots, while model covers posing for artists and showing clothes too.
  • artist's model
    3.4 0.11/m
    artist's model poses for painters and sculptors in studios, while model also includes people who wear clothes for fashion shows or photos.
  • poser
    7.2 0.02/m
    poser poses for a camera or artist but often sounds informal or negative, while model is the neutral professional term for that job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mannequin - a person employed to display clothes by wearing them, especially in fashion shows
  • synonym: subject - the person or thing that is being studied, described, or dealt with
  • degree higher: supermodel - a highly paid, internationally famous fashion model
