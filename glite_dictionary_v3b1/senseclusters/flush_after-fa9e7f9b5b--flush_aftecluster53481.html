flush after

verb
flush a toilet after use
1.0
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / flʌʃ ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to flush the toilet after using it, by causing water to flow through and clean it

Examples

To flush after toilet
It's important to teach children to flush after every visit to the toilet.
Please remember to flush after you use the restroom.
Signs in the public bathroom ask patrons to flush after themselves.

Related Terms
