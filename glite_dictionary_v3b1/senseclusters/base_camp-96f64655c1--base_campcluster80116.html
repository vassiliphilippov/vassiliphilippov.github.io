base camp

noun
main starting camp
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪs kæmp /; / beɪs kæmp / (AmE), / ˈbeɪs kæmp /; / beɪs kæmp / (BrE)
a main camp used as a starting point and support center for activities like climbing, hiking, or exploration

Examples

A base camp for climbing
The expedition team set up a base camp before attempting to reach the mountain summit.
After a long day of hiking, everyone returned to base camp to rest and prepare for tomorrow.
Supplies were delivered to the base camp at the foot of the glacier.
The climbers returned to base camp to rest before attempting the summit.
All supplies and communications were managed from the base camp during the expedition.
After a long day of research, the scientists gathered at base camp to share their findings.
