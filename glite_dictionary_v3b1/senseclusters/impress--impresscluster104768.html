Examples

Synonyms

  • imprint
    3.8 0.98/m
    imprint makes a clear, lasting image by pressing into soft material, while impress means to press a design into a surface.
  • stamp
    2.2 2.88/m
    stamp presses a shape or mark into something using a tool or machine, while impress refers to making a pressed design on a surface.
