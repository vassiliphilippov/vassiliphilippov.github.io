impress

verb
reproduce by printing or stamping
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈprɛs / (AmE), / ɪmˈprɛs /; / ɪmˈpres / (BrE)
to mark or stamp a surface with a design, image, or pattern by pressing

Examples

To impress a seal
A company seal was impressed at the bottom of the certificate.
Each coin has its own design impressed upon it during minting.
The printer impressed the letterhead onto the paper with great precision.
Coins are often made by impressing images onto metal blanks.
She carefully impressed a floral pattern onto the soft clay.
The craftsman impressed his initials into the base of the pottery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emboss - to carve, mold, or stamp a design so that it stands out in relief
  • synonym: engrave - to cut or carve a design into the surface of something
  • synonym: print - to produce text or images on paper
  • synonym: stamp - to imprint a mark or design using pressure
