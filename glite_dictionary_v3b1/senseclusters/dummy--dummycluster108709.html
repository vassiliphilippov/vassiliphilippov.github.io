dummy

noun
figure representing a human form
4.5 2.71/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÊŒmi / (AmE), / ËˆdÊŒmi / (BrE)
an object made to resemble a person, used for displaying clothes, practicing medical procedures, testing accidents, or as a decoy

Examples

A dummy mannequin
During the crash test, engineers used a dummy to simulate a real person.
Medical students practiced CPR on a training dummy before working with real patients.
The shop window was filled with dummies dressed in the latest fashion.
