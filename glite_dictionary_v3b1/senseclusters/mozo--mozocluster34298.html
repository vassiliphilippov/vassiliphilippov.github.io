mozo

noun
restaurant server
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈməʊzəʊ / (BrE)
a waiter or server in a restaurant or café, especially in Spanish-speaking countries

Examples

Restaurant mozo
The mozo greeted us with a smile and handed us the menus.
After we finished our meal, the mozo quickly cleared the table.
In many cafés, the mozo also prepares simple drinks for customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waiter - a person who serves food and drinks in a restaurant
