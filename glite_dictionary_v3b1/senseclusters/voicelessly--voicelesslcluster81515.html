voicelessly

adverb
without vocal sound
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪsləsli / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪsləsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not use the voice or vocal sound

Examples

Whisper voicelessly
She mouthed the answer voicelessly so the teacher would not hear.
The actor moved his lips voicelessly during the rehearsal to practice his lines.
He apologized voicelessly, unable to find the strength to speak out loud.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aloud - using the voice so as to be heard
  • synonym: silently - in a quiet or noiseless manner
