whizzle

noun
whizzing sound
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪz.əl/ /; / /wɪz/ (AmE), /ˈwɪz.əl/ /; / /wɪz/ (BrE)
a whizzing or hissing sound, especially from a sudden rush of air or rapid movement

Examples

A whizzle of air
The sudden whizzle of tires warned him that the car had skidded past.
A whizzle filled the air as the arrow shot past the target.
She heard a faint whizzle as the wind rushed through the narrow alley.

Related Terms
