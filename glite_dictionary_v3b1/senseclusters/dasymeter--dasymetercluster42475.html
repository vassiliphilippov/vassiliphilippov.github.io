dasymeter

noun
device for measuring gas density
Pronunciation: / ˈdæsɪˌmiːtər / (AmE), / ˈdæsɪˌmiːtə / (BrE)
A device consisting of a thin glass globe used to measure the density of a gas by weighing it.

Examples

A dasymeter device
A dasymeter can provide precise measurements in studies of atmospheric composition.
Careful calibration of the dasymeter is essential for accurate results.
The scientist used a dasymeter to determine the density of the experimental gas.
