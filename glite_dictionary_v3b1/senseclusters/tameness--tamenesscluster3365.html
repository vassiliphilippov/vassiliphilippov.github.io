tameness

noun
dullness; lack of excitement or originality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪmnəs / (AmE), / ˈteɪmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being dull, unexciting, or lacking in sophistication or originality

Examples

Film's tameness
Critics complained about the tameness of the film, expecting something more bold and original.
Despite the big budget, there was a certain tameness in the musical that left audiences underwhelmed.
The tameness of the speech made it forgettable for most listeners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insipidity - the quality of lacking flavor or interest
  • antonym: vitality - energy, liveliness, or excitement
