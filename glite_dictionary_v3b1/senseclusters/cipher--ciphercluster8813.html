cipher

noun
a message in secret code
4.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.fər/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.fə/ (BrE)
a message written using a secret code, especially one that is intended to be difficult to understand without the key

Examples

A cipher message
Breaking the cipher took the codebreakers several hours of intense work.
The general issued orders in a cipher to ensure they remained secret.
The spy sent a cipher to his contact, hiding the true meaning from anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: code - a system of words, letters, or symbols used to represent others, especially for secrecy
  • antonym: decipher - to succeed in understanding, interpreting, or identifying something (especially a coded message)
