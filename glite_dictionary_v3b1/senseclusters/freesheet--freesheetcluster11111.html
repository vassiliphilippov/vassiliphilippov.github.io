freesheet

noun
free newspaper
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈfriːʃiːt / (BrE)
a newspaper, magazine, leaflet, or flyer that is given away free of charge, often for advertising or information

Examples

A freesheet newspaper
Every morning, commuters grab a freesheet from the stand at the subway entrance.
The city launched a new freesheet to keep residents informed about local events.
She read the freesheet on her way to work, enjoying the short articles and puzzles.
The local supermarket distributes a freesheet every week with special offers and coupons.
I picked up a freesheet at the train station that listed upcoming community events.
Many businesses use a freesheet to advertise their latest products to potential customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: broadsheet - a large-format newspaper, usually more serious in content
  • synonym: brochure - a small booklet or pamphlet containing information or advertising
  • synonym: flyer - a small printed sheet used to advertise or inform
  • synonym: magazine - a periodical publication with articles and illustrations
  • synonym: newspaper - a publication containing news, articles, and advertisements
