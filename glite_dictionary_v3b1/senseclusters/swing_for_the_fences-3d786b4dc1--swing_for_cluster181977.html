swing for the fences

verb
take a big risk for a large reward
8.2
Pronunciation: / swɪŋ fər ðə ˈfɛnsɪz / (AmE), / swɪŋ fə ðə ˈfɛnsɪz / (BrE)
to attempt something ambitious or daring, especially by taking a significant risk for a potentially large reward
