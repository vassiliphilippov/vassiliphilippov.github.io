vinification

noun
the process of making wine from grapes
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪnəfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌvɪnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of fermenting grape juice to produce wine

Examples

Vinification process
Modern wineries often use temperature-controlled tanks during vinification to ensure the best results.
Red and white wines undergo different methods of vinification to bring out their unique flavors.
The art of vinification has evolved significantly over the centuries with the development of new techniques.
