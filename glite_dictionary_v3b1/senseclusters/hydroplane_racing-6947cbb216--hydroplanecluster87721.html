hydroplane racing

noun
racing with high-speed motorboats
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdroʊˌpleɪn ˈreɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhaɪdrəʊˌpleɪn ˈreɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
