automobile insurance

noun
insurance for vehicles against accidents or theft
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɑː.t̬ə.moʊˌbiːl ɪnˈʃʊr.əns / (AmE), / ˌɔː.tə.məˌbiːl ɪnˈʃʊə.rəns / (BrE)
