(in Christian belief) the day at the end of time when God judges everyone according to their actions; any moment of final judgment or accountability.

Examples

The final day of reckoning

For many believers, the day of reckoning is a time to account for one's actions.
Some view environmental crises as humanity's day of reckoning for misusing natural resources.
