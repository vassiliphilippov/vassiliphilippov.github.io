eureka

interjection
triumph on discovery
Pronunciation: / juˈriːkə / (AmE), / jʊəˈriːkə / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express triumph or excitement upon making a sudden discovery or finding a solution

Examples

Shout eureka
When the scientist saw the results, he exclaimed eureka in excitement.
After solving the difficult puzzle, she shouted eureka and smiled broadly.
He ran into the room yelling eureka after discovering the missing piece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aha - an exclamation used when something is suddenly understood
  • synonym: finally - an exclamation expressing relief at reaching a result
