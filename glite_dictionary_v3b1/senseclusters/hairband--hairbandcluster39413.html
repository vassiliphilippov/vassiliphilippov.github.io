hairband

noun
1980s glam rock band
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈheəˌbænd / (BrE)
a rock band, especially from the 1980s, known for flashy clothing, big hair, and a glam metal style

Examples

An 80s hairband
The 1980s were famous for hairbands with wild hairstyles and energetic stage shows.
Many hairbands became known for their catchy songs and dramatic fashion sense.
He dressed up as the lead singer of a hairband for the retro party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glam metal - a style of rock music with flashy looks and theatrical performances
  • antonym: grunge band - a rock band with a raw, unpolished sound popular in the 1990s
