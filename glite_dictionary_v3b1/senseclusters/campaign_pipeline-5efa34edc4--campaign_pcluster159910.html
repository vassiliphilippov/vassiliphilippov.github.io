campaign pipeline

noun
planned marketing sequence
Pronunciation: / kæmˈpeɪn ˈpaɪplaɪn / (AmE), / kæmˈpeɪn ˈpaɪplaɪn / (BrE)
a planned sequence of marketing activities or strategies designed to reach specific business goals

Examples

A campaign pipeline plan
A well-organized campaign pipeline helps teams monitor progress and stay on schedule.
The company invested in new tools to streamline its campaign pipeline and improve efficiency.
Their campaign pipeline ensures that marketing initiatives launch seamlessly one after another.
