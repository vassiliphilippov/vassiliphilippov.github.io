forbidden

adjective
not allowed; prohibited
2.8
Pronunciation: / fərˈbɪdn̩ / (AmE), / fəˈbɪdn̩ / (BrE)
not allowed by law, rules, or custom; excluded from use or mention

Examples

Forbidden by law
Certain topics remain forbidden in polite conversation.
Smoking is strictly forbidden in this building.
The children were curious about the forbidden room at the end of the hallway.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permitted - allowed, not forbidden
  • synonym: prohibited - formally forbidden, especially by law
