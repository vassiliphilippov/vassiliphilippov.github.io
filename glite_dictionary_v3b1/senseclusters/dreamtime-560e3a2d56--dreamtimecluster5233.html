Dreamtime

noun
Aboriginal creation era
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdriːm.taɪm/ (AmE), /ˈdriːm.taɪm/ (BrE)
the mythological period or timeless spiritual realm in Australian Aboriginal belief, when ancestral beings created the world and established laws and traditions

Examples

Aboriginal Dreamtime stories
Many Aboriginal artworks depict stories from the Dreamtime and their connection to the land.
Elders pass down Dreamtime stories to teach younger generations about their culture and origins.
The concept of the Dreamtime is central to understanding Aboriginal spirituality and traditions.
Many Aboriginal stories describe how the world was shaped during the Dreamtime by powerful ancestral beings.
The laws and customs of the community are believed to come from the Dreamtime.
Ceremonies often retell events from the Dreamtime to connect people with their spiritual heritage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestral beings - spiritual ancestors in Aboriginal belief
  • synonym: creation myth - a traditional story explaining the origin of the world or people
  • synonym: legend - a traditional story sometimes regarded as historical but unauthenticated
