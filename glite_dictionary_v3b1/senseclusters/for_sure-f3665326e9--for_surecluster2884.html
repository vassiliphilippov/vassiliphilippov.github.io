for sure

adverb
without doubt; certainly
1.0
Pronunciation: / fər ʃʊr / (AmE), / fə ʃɔː /; / fə ˈʃɔː / (BrE)
without any doubt; certainly; definitely

Examples

Yes, for sure

Are you going to the party tonight? Yes, I'll be there for sure.
I don't know if he's coming, but he'll call me for sure if he does.
This is the best movie I've seen this year, for sure.
