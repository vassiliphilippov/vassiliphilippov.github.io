sublimator

noun
device for sublimation
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈlɪmeɪtər / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪmeɪtə / (BrE)
a device or substance that causes or facilitates the process of sublimation, turning a solid directly into vapor

Examples

Laboratory sublimator
The laboratory technician used a sublimator to purify the chemical sample.
A sublimator is essential in processes where direct solid-to-vapor conversion is needed.
Engineers designed a new sublimator for use in spacecraft life support systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: condenser - a device that turns vapor into liquid
  • synonym: sublimate - to change directly from a solid to a vapor without becoming liquid
