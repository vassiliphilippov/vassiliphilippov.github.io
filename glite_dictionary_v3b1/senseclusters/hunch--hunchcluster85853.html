hunch

noun
the act of bending into a stooped position
2.4
Pronunciation: / hʌntʃ / (AmE), / hʌntʃ / (BrE)
the act or posture of bending one's body into a humped or stooped position

Examples

A hunch in his back
After hours of gardening, his hunch became more pronounced.
She sat with a hunch in her back, staring at the floor.
The old man walked with a hunch as he crossed the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stoop - the act of bending one's head or body forward and downward
  • antonym: straighten - to make or become straight, the opposite of hunching
