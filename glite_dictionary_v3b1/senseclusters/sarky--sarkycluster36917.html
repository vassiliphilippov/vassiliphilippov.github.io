sarky

adjective
sarcastic, mocking
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːki / (BrE)
informal and British; using sarcasm or mocking in a slightly rude or teasing way

Examples

A sarky reply
She gave me a sarky reply when I asked about her weekend plans.
His sarky sense of humor sometimes annoys his friends.
Try not to be so sarky during serious discussions at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ironic - using words to express something different from the literal meaning
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
  • antonym: sincere - genuine and without pretending or sarcasm
