physical restoration

noun
process of removing or reducing disabilities
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪkəl ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of helping disabled persons by removing or reducing physical disabilities; rehabilitation to restore physical function
