naphthenic

adjective
from naphthenes
Pronunciation: / næfˈθɛnɪk / (AmE), / næfˈθɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from naphthenes, a type of hydrocarbon found in petroleum

Examples

Naphthenic acids
The refinery processes naphthenic acids to produce lubricants.
Certain oils with a naphthenic base are preferred for their stability at low temperatures.
The chemist analyzed the naphthenic content of the crude oil sample.
