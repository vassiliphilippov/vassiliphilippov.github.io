Fulani

noun
member of a West African ethnic group
Pronunciation: / fuːˈlɑːni / (AmE), / fuːˈlɑːni / (BrE)
a member of a pastoral and nomadic people of western Africa, traditionally Muslim cattle herders

Examples

A Fulani herder
Many Fulani have settled in urban areas, though some still maintain their pastoral lifestyle.
She married into a Fulani family that followed traditional nomadic customs.
The Fulani are renowned for their expertise in cattle herding across vast regions of West Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Fula - another name for the Fulani people
