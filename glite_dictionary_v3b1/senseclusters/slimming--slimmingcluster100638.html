slimming

verb
to lose weight
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈslɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
becoming thinner or causing someone to become thinner by losing weight, especially through diet or exercise

Examples

She is slimming
She is slimming by cutting out sugary snacks and exercising every morning.
Many people join gyms when they start slimming for health reasons.
He found that slimming was easier with the support of his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dieting - restricting food intake to lose weight
  • antonym: fattening - causing to become fatter
