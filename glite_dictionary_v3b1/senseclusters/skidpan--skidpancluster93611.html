skidpan

noun
surface for practicing car skids
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪdˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈskɪdˌpan / (BrE)
a specially constructed paved area designed to allow vehicles to skid safely, used for driver training and practicing handling skills in slippery conditions

Examples

A skidpan area
Police officers underwent advanced training on the skidpan during their defensive driving course.
Practicing on a skidpan helps drivers become more confident in winter conditions.
The driving school used a skidpan to teach students how to control a car in a slide.
