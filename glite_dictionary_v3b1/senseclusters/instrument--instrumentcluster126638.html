instrument

noun
legal document stating contract or rights
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrʊmənt / (BrE)
(Law) A formal legal document that records a contractual relationship or grants a right.

Examples

A legal instrument
A will is a type of instrument that distributes property upon death.
All terms and conditions are clearly stated in the legal instrument.
The parties signed the instrument before a notary public to make the agreement official.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contract - a legally binding agreement between parties
  • synonym: deed - a legal document conveying or transferring property
