thrombasthenia

noun
a rare blood disorder causing excessive bleeding
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌθrɑːmbəˈsθiːniə / (AmE), / ˌθrɒmbəˈsθiːniə / (BrE)
a rare inherited blood disorder in which platelets fail to function normally, leading to excessive bleeding

Examples

Thrombasthenia blood disorder
