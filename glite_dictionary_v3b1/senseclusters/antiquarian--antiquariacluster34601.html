antiquarian

noun
person dealing with antiques or antiquities
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tɪˈkwer.i.ən / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən / (BrE)
a person who studies, collects, or deals in antiques or ancient objects

Examples

An antiquarian collector
As a noted antiquarian, she is often consulted by museums about ancient ceramics.
Many antiquarian societies preserve the history and artifacts of their regions.
The antiquarian spent years restoring medieval manuscripts in his private collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antique dealer - a person who buys and sells antiques
  • antonym: modernist - a person who favors modern ideas over traditional ones
