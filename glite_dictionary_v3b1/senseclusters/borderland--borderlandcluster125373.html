borderland

noun
area near the border of a country or region
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɚˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdəˌlænd / (BrE)
an area of land near or along the border or boundary between two countries or regions

Examples

The borderland area
Cultural traditions in the borderland reflect influences from both neighboring countries.
The ancient castle was built in the borderland to guard against invasions.
Wildlife thrives in the borderland, where forests and rivers cross political boundaries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frontier - the extreme limit of settled land; a border between countries
