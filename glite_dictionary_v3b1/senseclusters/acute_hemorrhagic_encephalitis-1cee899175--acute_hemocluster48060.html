acute hemorrhagic encephalitis

noun
severe brain inflammation with bleeding
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈkjuːt hem.ə.ˈrædʒ.ɪk en.sef.ə.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (AmE), /ə.ˈkjuːt hem.ə.ˈrædʒ.ɪk en.sef.ə.ˈlaɪ.tɪs/ (BrE)
A rare and severe type of encephalitis characterized by sudden inflammation of the brain with bleeding into brain tissue.

Examples

Severe acute hemorrhagic encephalitis
Due to its severity, acute hemorrhagic encephalitis requires immediate hospitalization and intensive treatment.
Recent studies have shown that acute hemorrhagic encephalitis can be triggered by certain viral infections.
The patient was diagnosed with acute hemorrhagic encephalitis after presenting rapid neurological decline.
