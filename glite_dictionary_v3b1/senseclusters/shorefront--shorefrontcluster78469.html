shorefront

noun
land by a shore
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
the land or area directly along the edge of a shore, especially where it meets water

Examples

The shorefront area
The city plans to develop the shorefront into a public park with walking paths.
Many expensive homes are located along the shorefront for their beautiful views.
Tourists enjoyed dining at restaurants right on the shorefront during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beach - a sandy or pebbly area by the edge of a sea or lake
  • antonym: inland - away from the coast or shore
  • synonym: waterfront - the land along the edge of a body of water
