overtired

adjective
extremely tired
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈtaɪərd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
extremely tired, often so much that it becomes difficult to rest or sleep

Examples

Feeling overtired
After working late all week, she felt overtired and unable to concentrate.
The overtired toddler cried for an hour before finally falling asleep.
If you become overtired, it can actually make it harder to get a good night's sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - very tired; completely worn out
  • antonym: rested - having had enough rest; not tired
