twizzle

noun
figure skating spin
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪzəl / (AmE), / ˈtwɪzəl / (BrE)
a rapid spin performed on one foot by a figure skater or dancer, often as part of a routine

Examples

A skating twizzle
The skater executed a perfect twizzle during her ice dance routine.
Learning to perform a twizzle can be challenging for beginner figure skaters.
His twizzles were fast and controlled, impressing both the judges and the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pirouette - a full turn on one foot, especially in ballet
  • synonym: spin - a quick turning movement around a central point
