irredentist

noun
an advocate of restoring lost territory
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ɪ.rɪ.ˈden.tɪst/ /; / /ɪ.rə.ˈden.tɪst/ (AmE), /ɪ.rɪ.ˈden.tɪst/ /; / /ɪ.rə.ˈden.tɪst/ (BrE)
