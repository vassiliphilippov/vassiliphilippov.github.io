transumption

noun
taking possession
9.9
Pronunciation: / trænˈsʌmpʃən / (AmE), / trænˈsʌmpʃən / (BrE)
the act of taking possession of something or formally assuming an office, role, or responsibility

Examples

Transumption of office
The transumption of leadership occurred smoothly after the election results were announced.
Upon the CEO's retirement, the transumption of duties was handled by the board.
The ceremony marked the official transumption of the new governor's responsibilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assumption - the act of taking on a role or responsibility
  • antonym: relinquishment - the act of giving up or abandoning something
