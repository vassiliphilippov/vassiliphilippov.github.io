Principen

noun
trade name for ampicillin antibiotic
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪnsɪpən / (AmE), / ˈprɪnsɪpən / (BrE)
A trade name for ampicillin, a semisynthetic penicillin antibiotic used to treat a variety of bacterial infections.

Examples

Principen antibiotic medicine
Some patients experience allergic reactions to Principen just as with other penicillins.
The doctor prescribed Principen to treat her bacterial infection.
You should complete the full course of Principen even if your symptoms improve.
