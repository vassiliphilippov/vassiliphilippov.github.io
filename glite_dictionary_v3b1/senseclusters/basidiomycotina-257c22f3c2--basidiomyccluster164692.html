Basidiomycotina

noun
subdivision of fungi with basidium-born spores
Pronunciation: / bəˌsɪdi.oʊmaɪˈkoʊtɪnə /; / bəˌsɪdioʊmaɪˈkoʊtɪnə / (AmE), / bəˌsɪdi.əʊmaɪˈkəʊtɪnə /; / bæsɪdɪˌəʊmaɪˈkəʊtɪnə / (BrE)
a former subdivision of fungi whose spores develop on a structure called a basidium, including mushrooms, puffballs, smuts, rusts, and similar fungi
