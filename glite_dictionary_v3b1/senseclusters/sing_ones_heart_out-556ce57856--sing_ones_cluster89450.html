sing one's heart out

verb
sing with passion and energy
1.3
Pronunciation: / sɪŋ wʌnz hɑrt aʊt / (AmE), / sɪŋ wʌnz hɑːt aʊt / (BrE)
to sing with great passion, energy, or enthusiasm, often regardless of skill or outcome

Examples

To sing her heart out
At the school talent show, Emma decided to sing her heart out in front of the audience.
Despite losing her voice, she continued to sing her heart out until the very end.
Even though he was nervous, Tom sang his heart out and impressed everyone.
