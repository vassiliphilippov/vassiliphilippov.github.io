heterocentric

adjective
focused on heterosexual norms
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
describing a perspective, system, or attitude that centers on heterosexual norms or values, often ignoring other sexual orientations

Examples

Heterocentric views
The documentary challenges the heterocentric views often found in traditional education systems.
Critics argue that the policy is heterocentric and fails to address the needs of LGBTQ+ individuals.
Many advertisements still use heterocentric imagery, overlooking diverse relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heteronormative - assuming heterosexuality as the default or norm
  • antonym: inclusive - including people of all sexual orientations
