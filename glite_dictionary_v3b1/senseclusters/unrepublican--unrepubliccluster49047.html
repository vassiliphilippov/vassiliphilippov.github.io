unrepublican

adjective
against republican principles
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpʌblɪkən / (BrE)
not in accordance with the principles of a republic or the Republican party

Examples

An unrepublican action
Many critics argued that the new law was unrepublican and undermined democratic values.
His actions were seen as unrepublican by members of his own party.
The proposal was rejected for being too unrepublican in its approach to governance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antirepublican - opposed to the principles of a republic
  • antonym: republican - supporting the principles of a republic or the Republican party
