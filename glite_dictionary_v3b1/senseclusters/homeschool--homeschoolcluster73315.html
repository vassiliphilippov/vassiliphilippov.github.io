homeschool

noun
education at home
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌskuːl / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌskuːl / (BrE)
a system or practice of educating children at home instead of in a traditional school setting

Examples

A homeschool program
Many families choose homeschool to provide a more personalized education for their children.
The local community center offers resources for parents who homeschool their kids.
After moving to a rural area, they decided to try homeschool for the first time.
