shoulders sag

verb
shoulders droop with disappointment or fatigue

Pronunciation: /ˈʃoʊl.dərz sæɡ/ (AmE), /ˈʃəʊl.dəz sæɡ/ (BrE)
(of a person's shoulders) to droop or slump as a sign of disappointment, fatigue, or dejection.
