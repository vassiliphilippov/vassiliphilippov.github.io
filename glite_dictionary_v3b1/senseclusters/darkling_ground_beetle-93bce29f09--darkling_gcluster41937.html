darkling ground beetle

noun
a black terrestrial beetle; larvae feed on decaying matter
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːr.klɪŋ graʊnd ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈdɑː.klɪŋ graʊnd ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
