dedicant

noun
person being dedicated
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈdɛdɪkənt / (BrE)
a person who is the subject of a dedication, especially during a ceremony or ritual

Examples

Honored dedicant
The dedicant received blessings from the community during the special ceremony.
As the dedicant, she felt honored to be the focus of the ritual.
Each dedicant was called forward to accept their new role in the group.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dedicator - a person who dedicates something to someone else
  • synonym: honoree - a person who receives an honor or is recognized in a ceremony
