exposure

noun
a photographic image or print
5.0 3.6
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
the process in photography of allowing light to reach film or a sensor, or the photograph created by this process

Examples

Camera exposure
Each exposure was carefully catalogued in her digital archive.
