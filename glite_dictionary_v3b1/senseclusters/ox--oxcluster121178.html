ox

noun
a large bovine animal, often domesticated
1.0 0.41/m
Pronunciation: / É‘ks / (AmE), / É’ks / (BrE)
A large domesticated or wild bovine animal, especially a castrated male used for draught work or a member of the genera Bos or Bibos.

Examples

A wild ox
A wild ox is rarely seen in this region today.
The ancient farmers used an ox to plow their fields.
The museum displayed the skull of a prehistoric ox.
