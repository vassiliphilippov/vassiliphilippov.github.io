VSS

sym
electronics symbol
Pronunciation: / vi ɛs ɛs / (AmE), / vi ɛs ɛs / (BrE)
a symbol used in electronics to represent the voltage source supply or ground connection in a circuit

Examples

VSS pin connection
The engineer connected the microcontroller's pin to VSS to complete the circuit.
Always check that VSS is properly connected before powering the device.
In the schematic, VSS indicates the ground terminal for the power supply.

Related Terms

  • antonym: VDD - symbol for the positive voltage supply in a circuit
  • synonym: ground - reference point in an electrical circuit from which voltages are measured
