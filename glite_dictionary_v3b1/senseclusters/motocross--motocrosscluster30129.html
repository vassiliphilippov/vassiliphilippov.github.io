motocross

noun
off-road motorcycle racing
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtəˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəˌkrɒs / (BrE)
a sport in which riders race motorcycles on rough, off-road tracks with obstacles

Examples

Motocross race
He has been training for the national motocross championship all year.
Many fans gathered to watch the exciting motocross race on the muddy track.
She enjoys the thrill and challenge of competing in motocross events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enduro - long-distance off-road motorcycle racing
  • synonym: supercross - indoor motorcycle racing on artificial dirt tracks
