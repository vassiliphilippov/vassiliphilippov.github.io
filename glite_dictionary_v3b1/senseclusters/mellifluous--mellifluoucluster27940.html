mellifluous

adjective
smooth and pleasant-sounding (of a sound or voice)
8.6 6.8
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈlɪ.fl.uː.əs/ /; / /mɛ.ˈlɪ.fl.uː.əs/ (AmE), /mə.ˈlɪ.fl.ʊə.s/ /; / /mɛ.ˈlɪ.fl.ʊə.s/ (BrE)
(of a sound) pleasingly smooth and musical to hear.

Examples

A mellifluous voice
His mellifluous tone made even the most boring speech enjoyable.
She found the mellifluous notes of the flute relaxing after a long day.
The mellifluous voice of the singer captivated the entire audience.
