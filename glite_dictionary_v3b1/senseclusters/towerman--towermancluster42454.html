towerman

noun
watchtower guard
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtaʊərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈtaʊəˌmæn / (BrE)
a guard or sentry stationed in a watchtower, especially for observing and reporting danger in historical or military settings

Examples

Castle towerman
The towerman sounded the alarm when he spotted movement near the castle walls.
Every night, a towerman kept watch over the sleeping village from the highest point.
During the siege, a lone towerman scanned the valley for any approaching soldiers at dusk.
