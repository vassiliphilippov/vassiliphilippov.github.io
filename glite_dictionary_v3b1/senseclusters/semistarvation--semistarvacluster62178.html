semistarvation

noun
partial deprivation
8.3
Pronunciation: /se.mi.stɑːr.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sə.mi.stɑːr.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /se.mi.stɑː.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ /; / /sə.mi.stɑː.ˈveɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
