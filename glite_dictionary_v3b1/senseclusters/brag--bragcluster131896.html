brag

verb
talk boastfully about achievements
4.5
Pronunciation: / bræɡ / (AmE), / bræɡ / (BrE)
to talk with excessive pride about oneself or one's achievements, possessions, or abilities; to show off

Examples

To brag about money
He loves to brag about his new car whenever he gets the chance.
Instead of being modest, he always brags about his accomplishments.
She couldn't help but brag after winning first place in the competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boast - to talk with excessive pride about oneself
  • antonym: humble - modest or not showing pride
