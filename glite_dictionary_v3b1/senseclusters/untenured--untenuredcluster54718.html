untenured

adjective
without permanent status
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛnjɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛnjəd / (BrE)
not having been granted permanent employment status, especially in a university or academic position

Examples

Untenured faculty members
The university hired several untenured faculty members to fill temporary teaching positions.
As an untenured lecturer, she had to reapply for her job every year.
Many untenured professors work hard to secure a permanent position at the institution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probationary - serving a trial period before permanent status is granted
  • antonym: tenured - having permanent employment status, especially at a university
