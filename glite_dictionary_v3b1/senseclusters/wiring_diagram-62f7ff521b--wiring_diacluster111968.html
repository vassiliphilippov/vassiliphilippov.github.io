wiring diagram

noun
schematic showing electrical wiring layout
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪrɪŋ ˌdaɪəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈwaɪərɪŋ ˌdaɪəɡræm / (BrE)
A schematic drawing showing the connections and layout of the electrical wiring in a system or device.

Examples

A wiring diagram drawing
Before repairing the control panel, make sure you understand the wiring diagram.
Each appliance included a wiring diagram to guide proper installation.
The electrician referred to the wiring diagram to install the new lighting system.
