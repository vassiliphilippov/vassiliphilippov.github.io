borrow pit

noun
excavation for earth used elsewhere
8.1 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêro ä p…™t / (AmE), / Ààb…ír…ô ä p…™t / (BrE)
An excavation or pit from which soil, gravel, or other material has been taken for use as fill at another location, especially in construction or civil engineering.

Examples

Regulations require contractors to restore the land after using a borrow pit for road works.
The construction team dug a borrow pit nearby to supply soil for the highway embankment.
