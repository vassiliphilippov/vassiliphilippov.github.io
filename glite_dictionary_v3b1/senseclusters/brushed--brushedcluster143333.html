brushed

adjective
groomed with a brush (hair/clothes)
Pronunciation: / brʌʃt / (AmE), / brʌʃt / (BrE)
(of hair or clothing) having been groomed or made neat with a brush.

Examples

Brushed hair
His suit looked immaculate, with every part carefully brushed before the meeting.
She arrived with her brushed hair gleaming in the sunlight.
The brushed coat on the dog made it appear soft and shiny.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combed - (of hair) arranged using a comb
  • antonym: unbrushed - not groomed or made neat with a brush
