Examples

Foaming beer
She poured a glass of foaming beer for her friend.

The river was foaming at the base of the waterfall after the heavy rain.

Synonyms

  • frothy
    3.2 0.80/m
    frothy is covered in light bubbles and used to describe texture, while foaming means actively making a thick mass of bubbles.
  • bubbly
    3.2 1.48/m
    bubbly has many small bubbles from carbonation and often describes drinks or people, while foaming means actively forming thick surface foam.
