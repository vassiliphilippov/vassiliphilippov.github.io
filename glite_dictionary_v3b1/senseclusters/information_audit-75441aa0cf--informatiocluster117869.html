information audit

noun
systematic examination of organizational information use
5.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.fɔːr.ˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈɔː.dɪt/ /; / /ɪn.fər.ˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈɔː.dɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.fə.ˈmeɪ.ʃən ˈɔː.dɪt/ (BrE)
