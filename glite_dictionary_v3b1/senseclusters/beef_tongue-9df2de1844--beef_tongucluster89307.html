beef tongue

noun
edible tongue of a cow
Pronunciation: / bif tʌŋ / (AmE), / biːf tʌŋ / (BrE)
The tongue of a cow prepared and eaten as meat.

Examples

Cooked beef tongue
He slow-cooked the beef tongue until it was tender and flavorful.
In some cuisines, beef tongue is served with a spicy sauce.
The deli offers beef tongue sandwiches as a specialty.
