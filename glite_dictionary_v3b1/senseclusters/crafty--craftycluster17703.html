crafty

adjective
cunning, skilled in deception
Pronunciation: / ˈkræfti / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːfti / (BrE)
skilled at achieving one's aims by deceitful or clever methods; cunning.

Examples

A crafty plan
His crafty negotiation tactics earned him a better contract than anyone expected.
She gave him a crafty smile, hinting that she knew more than she was letting on.
The crafty fox managed to outwit the hunters and escape into the woods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cunning - having or showing skill in achieving one's ends by deceit
  • antonym: honest - free of deceit; truthful and sincere
  • synonym: wily - skilled at gaining an advantage, especially deceitfully
