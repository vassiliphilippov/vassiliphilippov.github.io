supersocial

adjective
extremely social
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)

Examples

A supersocial animal

Her supersocial nature made her popular at every event she attended.
Certain primates are considered supersocial due to their strong group cooperation and communication.
