sterile

adjective
lacking creativity or originality
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛrəl / (AmE), / ˈstɪərʌɪl / (BrE)
lacking originality, creativity, or imaginative power; unproductive or uninspired

Examples

Sterile writing style
Critics described the play as sterile and lacking imaginative spark.
His lecture was informative but ultimately sterile, failing to inspire the audience.
The committee rejected the sterile proposal, calling for more innovative ideas.
