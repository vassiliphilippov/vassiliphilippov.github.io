hoot

noun
something of little value (used in negatives)
5.5 1.07/m
Pronunciation: / huÀêt / (AmE), / huÀêt / (BrE)
A thing of little or no value (used especially in the negative, as in 'not worth a hoot').

Examples

Not worth a hoot
He didn't care a hoot about what people thought of his decision.
She doesn't give a hoot about fashion trends, and wears whatever she likes.
The old car isn't worth a hoot anymore, so we might as well sell it for scrap.
