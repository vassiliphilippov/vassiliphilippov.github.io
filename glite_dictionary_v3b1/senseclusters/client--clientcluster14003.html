client

noun
person receiving goods or services
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈklaɪənt /; / ˈklaɪ.ənt / (BrE)
a person or organization that receives goods, services, or professional advice from a company or professional

Examples

A client meeting
As a financial advisor, she meets with new clients every week.
The law firm has many important clients in the banking industry.
The salon offers special discounts to regular clients.
Every client is entitled to confidential legal advice.
The client met with her attorney to discuss the case.
The law firm represents several high-profile clients in corporate matters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: provider - a person or organization that supplies goods or services
