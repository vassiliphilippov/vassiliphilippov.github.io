monkey business

noun
mischievous or deceitful behavior
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ˌbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ˌbɪznɪs /; / ˈmʌŋki ˌbɪznəs / (BrE)
playful, mischievous, or slightly dishonest behavior (often meant to deceive or amuse)

Examples

Monkey business at work
Stop your monkey business and get back to work!

The principal warned the students that any monkey business during the trip would not be tolerated.
Their accountant discovered some financial monkey business in the records.
Whenever they get together, you can expect a bit of monkey business and laughter.
