gate

verb
restrict access to online content
7.0
Pronunciation: /geɪt/ (AmE), /geɪt/ (BrE)
to restrict access to online content, features, or services so that they are available only after authentication, payment, or meeting certain conditions.

Examples

To gate content
Many websites gate premium articles behind a login to encourage readers to subscribe.
The company gated its research reports, requiring email addresses to download the PDFs.
Instead of gating all tutorials, they offered some free previews to attract new users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paywall - a system that requires payment to access online content
  • synonym: restrict - to limit or control access, use, or availability
