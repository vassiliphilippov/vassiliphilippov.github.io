pious

adjective
showing religious devotion
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪəs / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ.əs / (BrE)
having or showing deep respect and devotion toward a deity or religion

Examples

A pious person
His grandmother was known for her pious nature and daily prayers.
She donated generously to charity out of a pious sense of duty.
The ceremony included several pious readings from sacred texts.
