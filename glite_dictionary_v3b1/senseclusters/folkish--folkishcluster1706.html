folkish

adjective
simple and traditional
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.kɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.kɪʃ/ (BrE)
having qualities that are simple, traditional, or characteristic of rural or folk culture, including traditional stories, music, customs, or arts

Examples

Folkish music style
The artist's folkish style draws inspiration from rural traditions and simple living.
They decorated the cottage with folkish patterns and handmade crafts.
Her music has a folkish quality that reminds listeners of old village songs.
The museum displayed a collection of folkish crafts from rural communities.
Her music has a folkish quality that reminds listeners of old ballads.
They decorated the hall with folkish patterns inspired by traditional embroidery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folkloric - relating to folklore or traditional culture
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, not traditional
  • synonym: rustic - having a simple, rural, or country style
  • synonym: traditional - following customs or ways of life handed down from the past
