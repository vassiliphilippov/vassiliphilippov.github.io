town hall

noun
building for town government administration
Pronunciation: / taʊn hɑl / (AmE), / taʊn hɔːl / (BrE)
A building used for the administration of a town's government, often housing official offices and sometimes used for public meetings or events.

Examples

The town hall building
Many important decisions are made inside the town hall each month.
The mayor gave a speech at the town hall during the annual festival.
The old town hall was renovated to preserve its historic architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: city hall - a government building that houses administrative offices of a city government
