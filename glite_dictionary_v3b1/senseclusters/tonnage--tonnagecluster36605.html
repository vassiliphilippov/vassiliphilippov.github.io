tonnage

noun
weight in tons
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtʌnɪdʒ / (BrE)
the total weight or carrying capacity of a ship, measured in tons

Examples

Ship's tonnage
The ship's tonnage determines how much cargo it can carry.
New regulations limit the tonnage of trucks allowed on this bridge.
The port reported a record tonnage of goods handled this year.
The tonnage of the vessel determines how much cargo it can legally carry.
Port fees are often based on the tonnage of each arriving ship.
The navy increased its total tonnage to strengthen its fleet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capacity - the maximum amount that something can contain
  • synonym: weight - the amount something weighs
