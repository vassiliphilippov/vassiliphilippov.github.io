redbone

noun
breed of American hound dog
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈrɛd.bəʊn / (BrE)
A breed of American hound with a red or red-and-tan coat, known for speed and used particularly in hunting.

Examples

Redbone coonhound
As a puppy, the redbone was full of energy and curiosity.
My neighbor owns a redbone that excels at tracking raccoons in the woods.
The redbone is known for its endurance and distinctive reddish coat.
