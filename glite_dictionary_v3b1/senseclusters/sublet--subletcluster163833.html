sublet

noun
property rented by a tenant to someone else
8.1 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.lɛt / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.lɛt / (BrE)
a property or unit that a tenant rents to another person for a period within the term of the original lease

Examples

A summer sublet
I'm looking for a summer sublet close to campus.
The sublet will expire at the end of July.
They found a sublet while their apartment was being renovated.
