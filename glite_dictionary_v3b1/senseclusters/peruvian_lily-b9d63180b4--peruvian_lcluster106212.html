Peruvian lily

noun
flowering Andean plant with showy flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈruːviən ˈlɪli / (AmE), / pəˈruːviən ˈlɪli / (BrE)
A plant native to the Andes, known for its clusters of showy pinkish-purple lily-like flowers; often grown as an ornamental.

Examples

The Peruvian lily flower
Florists often use the Peruvian lily in bouquets for its long-lasting blooms.

The Peruvian lily adds a splash of vibrant color to the garden each spring.
