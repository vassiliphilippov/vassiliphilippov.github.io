Epistle of Paul the Apostle to the Colossians

noun
a New Testament letter to the Colossians
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪsəl əv pɔl ði əˈpɑsəl tu ðə kəˈlɑʃənz / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪsəl ɒv pɔːl ði əˈpɒsl tuː ðə kəˈlɒʃənz / (BrE)
a book of the New Testament, presented as a letter written by Saint Paul to the Christians in Colossae (in ancient Phrygia)
