closeness

noun
state of being poorly ventilated
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊsnəs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stuffy or poorly ventilated due to lack of air circulation

Examples

Stuffy closeness
After hours in the crowded, windowless room, the closeness became almost unbearable.
She complained about the closeness of the packed train carriage.
The closeness of the small attic made it hard to breathe during summer.
