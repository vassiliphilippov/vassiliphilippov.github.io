jacket

noun
short coat; garment to the waist or hips
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk.ɪt / (BrE)
a short coat, typically extending to the waist or hips, usually fastened down the front

Examples

A leather jacket
He put on his jacket before leaving the house.
Her leather jacket made her look stylish and confident.
Please hang your jacket on the coat rack by the door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coat - an outer garment worn outdoors, usually longer than a jacket
