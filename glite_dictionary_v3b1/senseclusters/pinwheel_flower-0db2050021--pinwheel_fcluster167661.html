pinwheel flower

noun
tropical shrub with scented, wavy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌwiːl ˈflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnwiːl ˈflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
A tropical shrub with glossy leaves and fragrant, night-blooming flowers with crimped or wavy corollas, native to northern India and Thailand.

Examples

The pinwheel flower plant
Botanists collected samples of the pinwheel flower during their expedition in Thailand.

The glossy leaves of the pinwheel flower add an elegant touch to tropical landscapes.
