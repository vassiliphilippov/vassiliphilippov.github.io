hoster

noun
show presenter
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstə / (BrE)
a person who presents or leads a television or radio program; a show presenter

Examples

TV hoster
The hoster welcomed the audience and explained the rules of the game show.
As a popular hoster, she is known for her engaging interviews with celebrities.
Each week, the hoster introduces new topics and leads discussions with experts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guest - a person invited to appear on a show
  • synonym: host - a person who introduces and leads a television or radio show
  • synonym: presenter - someone who presents a television or radio program
