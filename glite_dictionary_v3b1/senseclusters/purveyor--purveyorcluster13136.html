purveyor

noun
person or business that supplies goods
Pronunciation: / pərˈveɪ.ər / (AmE), / pəˈveɪ.ə / (BrE)
a person, organization, or company that supplies goods, especially food or provisions

Examples

A purveyor of food
As the main purveyor of gourmet cheese in the city, she built a loyal customer base.
The hotel uses a local purveyor to source all its fresh fruits and vegetables.
They pride themselves on being the finest purveyors of Italian delicacies in the region.
