brassiness

noun
yellow metallic sheen
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsinəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsinəs / (BrE)
a yellowish metallic color or shine resembling that of brass

Examples

Brassiness of the lamp
The brassiness of the new lamp gave the living room a vintage feel.
After years of polishing, the doorknob's brassiness was still impressive.
Sunlight brought out the brassiness in the old picture frame on the wall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - lack of brightness or shine
  • synonym: luster - a gentle sheen or soft glow
