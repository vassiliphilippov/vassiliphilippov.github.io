Synonyms

  • dig out
    3.2 0.7/m
    dig out is used to remove something from under earth or snow, while dig up means to find or recover it by digging.
  • unearth
    4.2 0.8/m
    unearth is used to bring buried things to light, while dig up means to find or recover them by digging in the ground.
  • excavate
    5.8 1/m
    excavate is the formal act of digging, often by archaeologists at a site, while dig up is a general idea of finding by digging.
  • dig out
    3.4 1.0/m
    dig out can mean to find something forgotten or free something trapped, while dig up focuses on physically recovering something from the ground.
