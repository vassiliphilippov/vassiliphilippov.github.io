Examples

Disabling injury

The accident left him with a disabling condition affecting his mobility.

Synonyms

  • disable
    3.5 3.42/m
    disable is the basic verb for making something unable to work or act, while disabling describes that effect or action.
  • incapacitate
    7.5 1.96/m
    incapacitate means to make someone unable to function, often in medical or legal contexts, while disabling is a general everyday term.
  • crippling
    5.8 0.39/m
    crippling causes severe, often long-lasting damage that stops normal life, while disabling covers loss of ability without implying severity.
  • paralyzing
    5.1 0.00/m
    paralyzing stops movement or action completely, often physically, while disabling is a broader term for reduced ability or function.
