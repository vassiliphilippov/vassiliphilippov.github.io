wage earner

noun
person earning wages for work
Pronunciation: / weɪdʒ ˈɜrnər / (AmE), / weɪdʒ ˈɜːnə / (BrE)
A person who earns wages by working, typically as an employee receiving payment based on hours worked or output produced.

Examples

The wage earner works
After losing his job, the wage earner struggled to pay the bills.
As the sole wage earner in the family, he felt a lot of pressure to provide.
Government policies often focus on supporting the average wage earner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: employee - a person employed for wages or salary
  • antonym: employer - a person or organization that hires others for wages or salary
