oppositional

adjective
resisting or opposing
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːpəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
showing opposition, resistance, or disagreement to something or someone (such as a group, person, or authority)
