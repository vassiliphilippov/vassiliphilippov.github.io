meridionality

noun
southern orientation
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˌrɪdiəˈnæləti / (AmE), / məˌrɪdiəˈnælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being oriented toward the south or relating to meridians

Examples

Meridionality of a region
The meridionality of the region affects its climate patterns.
Scientists studied the meridionality of ocean currents to understand temperature changes.
The city's meridionality gives it a warmer climate than neighboring areas.
