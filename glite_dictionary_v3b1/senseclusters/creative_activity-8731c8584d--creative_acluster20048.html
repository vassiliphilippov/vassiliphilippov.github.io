creative activity

noun
act of creating something new
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪtɪv ækˈtɪvɪti / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the process of using imagination or artistic skill to produce something new or original

Examples

Creative activity in art
Her weekends are filled with creative activity, from painting to composing music.
The art class encourages creative activity among students of all ages.
Writing poetry is just one form of creative activity that can stimulate the mind.
