inoculator

noun
one who administers inoculations
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɑkjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person, especially a medical practitioner, who administers inoculations to protect against disease

Examples

An inoculator gives shots
As an experienced inoculator, she traveled to several countries during the immunization campaign.
Every inoculator must follow strict hygiene protocols to prevent infection.
The inoculator administered vaccines to hundreds of children in the remote village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vaccinator - a person who administers vaccines
