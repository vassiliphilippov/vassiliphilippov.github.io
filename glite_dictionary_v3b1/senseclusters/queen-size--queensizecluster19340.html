queen-size

adjective
bed size between full and king
3.2 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / kwiÀên sa…™z / (AmE), / kwiÀên sa…™z / (BrE)
(of a bed, mattress, etc.) of a standard size larger than full-size but smaller than king-size, typically measuring about 60 inches by 80 inches (152 cm by 203 cm).

Examples

Queen-size bed

She found the queen-size sheets she needed in the linen section.
We decided to buy a queen-size mattress for our guest room.
