frisé

noun
curly fabric or hairstyle
9.9
Pronunciation: / friˈzeɪ / (AmE), / friˈzeɪ / (BrE)
a type of fabric with a curly or looped surface, or a hairstyle featuring tight curls

Many antique chairs feature upholstery made from frisé fabric.
