impolite

adjective
not polite; rude
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpəˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈlaɪt / (BrE)
not having or showing good manners; rude or discourteous.

Examples

Impolite behavior
It is considered impolite to interrupt someone while they are speaking.
She felt impolite for not saying thank you after receiving the gift.
The waiter was so impolite that we decided not to leave a tip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polite - having or showing good manners and respect for others
  • synonym: rude - having or showing a lack of respect or manners
