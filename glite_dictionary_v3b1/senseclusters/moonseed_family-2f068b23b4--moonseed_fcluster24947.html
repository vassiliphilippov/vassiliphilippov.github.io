moonseed family

noun
family of climbing plants (Menispermaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌsiːd ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈmuːnsiːd ˈfæmɪli / (BrE)
A family (Menispermaceae) of herbaceous or woody climbing plants, many of which are tropical with characteristic crescent-shaped seeds.

The moonseed family plants
Botanists often classify these vines as part of the moonseed family due to their distinctive seeds.
Some members of the moonseed family are used in traditional medicine for their alkaloid content.
The moonseed family includes a variety of tropical climbers found in Asia and Africa.
