El Cid

proper noun
heroic Spanish nobleman and epic hero
Pronunciation: / ɛl sɪd / (AmE), / ɛl sɪd / (BrE)
the legendary Spanish nobleman and military leader, celebrated as the hero of a 12th-century Spanish epic poem
