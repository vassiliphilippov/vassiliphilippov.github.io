compulsively

adverb
in a compulsive manner
8.3
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpʌlsɪvli / (AmE), / kəmˈpʌlsɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that involves or shows compulsion; driven by an irresistible urge, especially one that is against one's conscious wishes.

Examples

To compulsively eat
After quitting smoking, she began to eat compulsively to cope with the cravings.
He checked his phone compulsively, unable to concentrate on anything else.
She washed her hands compulsively whenever she touched something outside her home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsessively - in an obsessive manner; with excessive preoccupation
