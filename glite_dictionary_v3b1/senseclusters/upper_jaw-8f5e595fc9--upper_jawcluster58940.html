upper jaw

noun
upper part of the jaw in vertebrates
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpɚ dʒɑ / (AmE), / ˈʌpə dʒɔː / (BrE)
the upper part of the jaw in vertebrates, which is fused to the cranium and holds the upper teeth

Examples

The upper jaw bone
Fractures of the upper jaw can make eating and speaking difficult.
In many fish, the upper jaw is fixed and does not move like the lower one.
The dentist examined my upper jaw to check for any cavities near the gum line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lower jaw - the lower movable part of the jaw in vertebrates
  • synonym: maxilla - the upper jawbone in vertebrates
