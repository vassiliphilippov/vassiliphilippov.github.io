Flodden

proper noun
hill in Northumberland, site of 1513 battle
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑːdən / (AmE), / ˈflɒdən / (BrE)
a hill in Northumberland, England, notable as the site of the Battle of Flodden in 1513, where the Scots were defeated by the English.

Examples
