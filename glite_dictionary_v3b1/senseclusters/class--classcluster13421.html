class

noun
a rank or league by quality
2.6
Pronunciation: / klæs / (AmE), / klɑːs / (BrE)
a group or category of people or things that share a common characteristic, quality, or status (such as in society, science, or competitions)

Examples

Social class
After winning the tournament, the team moved up to a higher class next season.
She competed in the lightweight class at the world championships.
The baseball player was promoted from Class C to a higher class after an impressive season.
Political leaders often talk about the needs of the middle class during elections.
She was born into a wealthy class and attended the best schools in the city.
There has been increasing tension between different class groups in recent years.
Detergents can be divided into two major classes according to their chemical properties.
Each animal belongs to a specific class based on its characteristics.
We studied several classes of antibiotics in our biology course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caste - hereditary social group, especially in India
  • synonym: category - A group of people or things sharing a common type or classification.
  • synonym: division - a group or level in a sports competition
  • degree lower: order - taxonomic rank below class in biology
  • degree higher: phylum - taxonomic rank above class in biology
  • synonym: rank - a position in the hierarchy of an organization or society
  • synonym: type - A category of people or things having common characteristics.
