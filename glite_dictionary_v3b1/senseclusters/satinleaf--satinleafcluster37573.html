satinleaf

noun
tropical American timber tree
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsætɪnˌlif / (AmE), / ˈsætɪnˌliːf / (BrE)
A tropical American tree with dark, hard, heavy wood and small purple plum-like fruit, grown for its timber and ornamental value.

Examples

The satinleaf tree
Carpenters value the wood from the satinleaf for furniture making.
The satinleaf tree is prized for its striking copper-colored leaves.
The small purple fruit of the satinleaf attracts many birds.
