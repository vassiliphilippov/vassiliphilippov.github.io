governor

noun
software regulator of CPU frequency
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌ.və.nər/ /; / /ˈgʌ.vər.nər/ (AmE), /ˈgʌ.və.nə/ (BrE)
(in computing) a software component or policy that automatically manages a processor's (CPU) frequency and power use to balance performance and energy efficiency.

Examples

CPU governor
The governor on the cloud server lowered CPU frequency to save energy during low traffic.
System administrators chose a conservative governor to prioritize battery life on the laptops.
Changing the CPU governor from 'performance' to 'ondemand' reduced temperatures and power use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: power management - the set of policies and mechanisms that control energy use in a computer system
  • synonym: regulator - a device or mechanism that controls the operation or speed of a system
