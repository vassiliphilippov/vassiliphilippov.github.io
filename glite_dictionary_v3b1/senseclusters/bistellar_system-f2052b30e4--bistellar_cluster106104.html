bistellar system

noun
star system with two stars
Pronunciation: / baɪˈstɛlɚ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / baɪˈstelə ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A star system composed of exactly two stars orbiting each other.

Examples

A bistellar system with two stars
A newly found bistellar system challenges existing theories about star formation.
