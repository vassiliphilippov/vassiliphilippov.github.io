urbanicity

noun
degree of urban character
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɜrbəˈnɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌɜːbəˈnɪsəti / (BrE)
the degree to which a place or area has urban or city-like characteristics

Examples

The urbanicity level
The urbanicity of a neighborhood can affect the types of services available to residents.
Researchers are examining how urbanicity impacts mental health outcomes in different populations.
High urbanicity often means better access to public transportation and cultural events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rurality - the degree to which an area is rural or countryside-like
  • synonym: urbanization - the process by which an area becomes more urban
