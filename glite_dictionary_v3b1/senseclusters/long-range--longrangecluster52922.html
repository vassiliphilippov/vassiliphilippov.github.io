long-range

adjective
effective or suitable over long distances
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ reɪndʒ / (AmE), / lɒŋ reɪndʒ / (BrE)
Able to operate, be used, or be effective over long distances.

Examples

Long-range missile
Scientists developed a long-range communication device for deep-sea exploration.
The missile is equipped with long-range targeting systems for precision strikes.
They invested in a long-range wireless network to connect rural communities.
