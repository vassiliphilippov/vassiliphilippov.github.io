hemmer

noun
person who hems
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmər / (AmE), / ˈhɛmə / (BrE)
a person whose job or task is to fold and sew hems on garments or fabric items

Examples

Skilled hemmer at work
The skilled hemmer worked quickly to finish the dresses before the event.
As a hemmer, she takes pride in making every seam neat and even.
The factory hired an experienced hemmer to improve the quality of their clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seamstress - a woman who sews, especially as a job
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing
