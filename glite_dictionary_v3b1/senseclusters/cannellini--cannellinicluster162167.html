cannellini

noun
white kidney bean
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəˈliːni / (AmE), / ˌkanɛˈliːni / (BrE)
a type of large white kidney bean commonly used in Italian cooking

Examples

White cannellini beans
She added cannellini to the salad for extra protein and texture.
The recipe calls for a can of cannellini beans, drained and rinsed.
He made a hearty stew with tomatoes, spinach, and cannellini.
