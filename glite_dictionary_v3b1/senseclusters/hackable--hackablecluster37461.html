hackable

adjective
vulnerable to hacking
Pronunciation: / ˈhækəbəl / (AmE), / ˈhækəbəl / (BrE)
able to be accessed, broken into, or manipulated by unauthorized users, especially in computing

Examples

Hackable computer system
Outdated software can make your computer more hackable.
The company fixed several bugs that had left their app hackable by cybercriminals.
Experts warn that smart home devices are often hackable if not properly secured.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secure - protected against unauthorized access or attack
  • synonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or attacked
