silky elm

noun
Australasian tree with soft leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki ɛlm / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki ɛlm / (BrE)
a large tree native to Australasia, known for its soft, silky leaves and bark

Examples

The silky elm tree

The silky elm thrives in moist, temperate forests across southeastern Australia.
