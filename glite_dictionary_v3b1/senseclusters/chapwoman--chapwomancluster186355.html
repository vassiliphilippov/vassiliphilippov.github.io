chapwoman

noun
female itinerant peddler
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtʃapˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who travels from place to place selling goods; a female itinerant peddler.

Examples

A chapwoman selling goods
During the fair, a chapwoman offered ribbons and buttons to passersby.
In the old market records, a chapwoman is listed among the traders.
The traveling chapwoman made her living selling wares from village to village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chapman - an itinerant peddler or hawker (typically male)
