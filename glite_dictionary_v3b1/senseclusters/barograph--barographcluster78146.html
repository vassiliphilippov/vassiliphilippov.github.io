barograph

noun
recording instrument for atmospheric pressure
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɹəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈbɑːrəˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
an instrument that automatically records changes in atmospheric pressure on paper

Examples

A barograph instrument
A sudden drop on the barograph indicated the approach of a storm.
Early explorers often relied on a barograph during their expeditions to track weather changes.
The meteorologist checked the barograph to analyze pressure trends over the last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barometer - instrument for measuring atmospheric pressure
