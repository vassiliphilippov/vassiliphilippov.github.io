superorder Malacopterygii

noun
group of fishes with soft-rayed fins
Pronunciation: / suː.pɚˌɔːr.dɚ ˌmæl.əˌkɒp.təˈrɪdʒ.i.aɪ / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pərˌɔː.də ˌmæl.əˌkɒp.təˈrɪdʒ.ɪ.aɪ / (BrE)
