vigneron

noun
grapevine grower
Pronunciation: / ˌviːnjəˈrɒn /; / viːnˈjɛrɒn / (AmE), / ˌviːnjəˈrɒ̃ /; / viːnˈjɛrɒn / (BrE)
a person who grows grapevines, especially for making wine

Examples

Experienced vigneron tending vineyard
The experienced vigneron carefully tended his vineyard throughout the growing season.
Many vignerons sell their grapes to local wineries instead of producing wine themselves.
As a young vigneron, she learned traditional methods of grape cultivation from her family.
The vigneron inspects the grapes daily to ensure the best quality for the harvest.
Many French villages celebrate the work of the local vigneron during wine festivals.
Becoming a skilled vigneron requires years of experience and knowledge of the land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: viticulturist - a person skilled in the science of grape growing
  • synonym: winemaker - a person who makes wine
