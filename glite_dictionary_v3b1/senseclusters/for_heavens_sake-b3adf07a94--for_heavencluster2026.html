for heaven's sake

interjection
exclamation of impatience or urgency
Pronunciation: / fər ˈhevənz seɪk / (AmE), / fə ˈhevənz seɪk / (BrE)
used to express impatience, annoyance, urgency, or emphasis.

Examples

For heaven's sake stop
For heaven's sake, don't forget your own birthday!
For heaven's sake, lock the door when you leave the house.
Will you please be quiet, for heaven's sake?

Related Terms

  • synonym: for God's sake - exclamation used to show anger or exasperation
  • synonym: for goodness' sake - expression used to emphasize annoyance or urgency
