erucic

noun
fatty acid in oils
Pronunciation: / ɪˈruːsɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈruːsɪk / (BrE)
erucic acid, a long-chain monounsaturated omega-9 fatty acid found in certain plant oils such as mustard and rapeseed
