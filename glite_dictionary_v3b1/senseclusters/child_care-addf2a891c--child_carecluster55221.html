Synonyms

  • childcare
    2.1 2.28/m
    childcare is the common single-word term for paid care services, while child care often appears as a two-word phrase in texts.
  • day care
    1.2 3.83/m
    day care cares for children during working hours at a facility, while child care covers care in varied settings and times.
  • creche
    5.7 0.10/m

  • nursery
