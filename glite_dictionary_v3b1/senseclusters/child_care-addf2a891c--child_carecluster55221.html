child care

noun
supervision and care of children as a service
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪld ˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪld ˌkeə / (BrE)
the care and supervision of children, especially by someone other than their parents or guardians, typically as a paid service

Examples

Reliable child care
After returning to work, Mary looked for reliable child care for her infant daughter.
Many employers now offer on-site child care as a benefit to their staff.
The cost of child care has increased significantly in recent years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysitting - the act of taking care of children temporarily, usually in their own home
  • synonym: nursery - a place where young children are cared for during the day
