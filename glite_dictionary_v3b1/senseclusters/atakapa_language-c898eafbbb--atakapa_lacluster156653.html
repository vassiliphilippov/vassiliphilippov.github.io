Atakapa language

noun
extinct language of the Atakapa people
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑːkəpə ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / əˈtækəpə ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
an extinct language formerly spoken by the Atakapa people of the Gulf Coast region of Louisiana and Texas

Examples

Atakapa language extinct
Few written records exist for the Atakapa language, making reconstruction difficult.
Linguists have studied the grammar and vocabulary of the Atakapa language.
The Atakapa language became extinct in the early twentieth century.
