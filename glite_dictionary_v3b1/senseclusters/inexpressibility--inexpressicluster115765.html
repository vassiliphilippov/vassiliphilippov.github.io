inexpressibility

noun
impossibility to express
8.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ɪk.spre.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ɪk.spre.sə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the quality of being impossible or extremely difficult to express in words

Examples

The inexpressibility of feelings
The inexpressibility of his gratitude was clear from the tears in his eyes.
She struggled with the inexpressibility of the beauty she witnessed during her travels.
Poets often write about the inexpressibility of deep emotions and experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineffability - the quality of being too great or extreme to be described in words
