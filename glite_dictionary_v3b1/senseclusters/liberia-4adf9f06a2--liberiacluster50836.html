Liberia

proper noun
country in West Africa
Pronunciation: / laɪˈbɪriə / (AmE), / laɪˈbɪəriə / (BrE)
a country in West Africa, founded in the 19th century by formerly enslaved people from the United States; its capital is Monrovia.

Examples

Liberia country
Monrovia is the bustling capital city of Liberia.
