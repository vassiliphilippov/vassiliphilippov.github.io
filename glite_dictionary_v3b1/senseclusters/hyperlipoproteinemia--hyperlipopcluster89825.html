hyperlipoproteinemia

noun
abnormally high blood lipoproteins and cholesterol
9.9
Pronunciation: /haɪ.pər.lɪp.ə.proʊ.tiː.ˈniː.ə/ /; / /haɪ.pər.ˈlɪp.ə.proʊ.tiː.niː.ə/ (AmE), /haɪ.pə.lɪp.ə.prəʊ.tiː.ˈniː.ə/ /; / /haɪ.pə.ˈlɪp.ə.prəʊ.tiː.niː.ə/ (BrE)
any of several disorders marked by abnormally high levels of lipoproteins and cholesterol in the blood

Examples

High hyperlipoproteinemia levels

Treating hyperlipoproteinemia often involves both dietary changes and medication.
