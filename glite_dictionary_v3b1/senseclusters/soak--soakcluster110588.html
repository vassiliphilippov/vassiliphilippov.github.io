soak

verb

to place or immerse something in liquid for a period of time, usually to soften or clean it

Examples

To soak clothes

Let the beans soak overnight before cooking them.

Synonyms

  • macerate
    8.5 0.10/m
    macerate is a technical process that softens material by long soaking, while soak is general immersion to clean or soften.
  • immerse
    4.2 1.15/m
    immerse means putting something fully under liquid for a time, while soak often implies leaving it to soften or clean.
  • steep
    3.5 0.96/m

  • sop
    8.3 0.07/m
