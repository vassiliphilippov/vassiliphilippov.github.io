soak

verb
immerse in liquid for a time
2.2
Pronunciation: / soʊk / (AmE), / səʊk / (BrE)
to place or immerse something in liquid for a period of time, usually to soften or clean it

Examples

To soak clothes
I soaked my shirt in cold water to remove the stain.
Let the beans soak overnight before cooking them.
She likes to soak her feet in warm water after a long day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - to remove moisture from; not wet
  • synonym: immerse - to plunge or dip into a liquid completely
