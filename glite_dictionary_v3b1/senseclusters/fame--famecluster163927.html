fame

noun
being widely known or honored
Pronunciation: / feɪm / (AmE), / feɪm / (BrE)
the state of being widely known or recognized by many people, often for achievements

Examples

To gain fame
After years of hard work, she finally achieved fame as a novelist.
His search for fame led him to move to Hollywood.
The artist's fame continued to grow long after his death.
His fame grew rapidly thanks to his impressive performances on stage.
Many people seek fame, but not everyone can handle public attention.
She achieved fame after winning the international writing competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obscurity - the state of not being well known
  • synonym: renown - the condition of being known and admired by many people
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions that are generally held about someone or something
