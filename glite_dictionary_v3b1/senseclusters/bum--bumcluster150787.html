bum

verb
ask for and get something for free
Pronunciation: / bʌm / (AmE), / bʌm / (BrE)
to ask for and get something for free, usually in a casual way

Examples

To bum a ride
Can I bum a ride home from you tonight?
He managed to bum a cigarette off a passerby.
Stop trying to bum money every time we meet.
He bummed a cigarette from a man waiting at the bus stop.
Can I bum a dollar for the vending machine?
She often bummed rides from her friends when her car was in the shop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: borrow - to take and use something belonging to someone else with the intention of returning it
  • antonym: give - to provide something to someone without expecting payment
  • synonym: mooch - to ask for and receive something for free
  • synonym: scrounge - to seek to obtain something, typically by stealth or persuasion, without paying for it
