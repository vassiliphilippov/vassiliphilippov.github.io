index

noun
the finger next to the thumb
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌdɛks / (AmE), / ˈɪndɛks / (BrE)
the finger next to the thumb; the second finger of the hand, also called the index finger or forefinger

Examples

Index finger
He pointed at the map with his index finger.
She wore a beautiful ring on her index finger.
The doctor checked my pulse by placing two fingers, including the index, on my wrist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: index finger - the finger next to the thumb; synonym for this sense of 'index'
  • degree higher: middle finger - the third finger, next to the index finger
  • degree lower: thumb - the first and thickest finger of the hand
