implant

noun
permanently inserted medical device
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.plænt / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɑːnt / (BrE)
A device or tissue that is inserted permanently into the body surgically, especially as a medical prosthesis.

Examples

A dental implant
After the accident, she required a knee implant to regain mobility.
The doctor explained the risks and benefits of having a cochlear implant.
The surgeon inserted a dental implant to replace the missing tooth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosthesis - an artificial device that replaces a missing body part
