radio drama

noun
audio play performed on radio
3.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààre…™dio ä Ààdr…ëm…ô / (AmE), / Ààre…™di…ô ä Ààdr…ëÀêm…ô / (BrE)
A play or story performed by actors and broadcast on radio, relying solely on sound without visual elements.

Examples

A radio drama play
Many classic mystery stories were adapted into radio drama before being made into movies.
She grew up listening to radio drama every Sunday night with her family.
The BBC has a long tradition of producing high-quality radio drama for its listeners.

Synonyms

  • radio theater
    4.4 0.01/m
    radio theater is a full audio performance made for radio with actors, music, and sound effects, while radio drama is any play broadcast on radio.
  • audio drama
    4.2 0.01/m
    audio drama is a sound-only performance distributed on audio media or radio, while radio drama is a play specifically broadcast on radio.
