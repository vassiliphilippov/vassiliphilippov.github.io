sigmoid colon

noun
S-shaped section of the large intestine
5.6 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪg.mɔɪd ˈkoʊ.lən/ (AmE), /ˈsɪg.mɔɪd ˈkəʊ.lən/ (BrE)
