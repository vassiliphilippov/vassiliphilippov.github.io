sprezzatura

noun
studied nonchalance
Pronunciation: / ˌsprɛtsəˈtʊrə / (AmE), / ˌsprɛtsəˈtjʊərə / (BrE)
an appearance of effortless grace, style, or elegance, where skill or effort is hidden to make things look natural and unforced
