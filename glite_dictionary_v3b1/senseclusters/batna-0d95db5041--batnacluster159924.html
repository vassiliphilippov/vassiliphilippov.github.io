BATNA

noun
best alternative if negotiations fail
9.7
Pronunciation: / biː eɪ tiː ɛn eɪ / (AmE), / biː eɪ tiː ɛn eɪ / (BrE)
An acronym for 'Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement': the most advantageous course of action a party can take if negotiations fail.

Examples

Calculate BATNA
Before the meeting, she calculated her BATNA so she would know when to walk away.
Negotiators with a strong BATNA often have more leverage and can demand better terms.
They discussed alternatives until each party understood their BATNA and its practical limits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: best alternative - the most favourable option available outside the current negotiation
  • synonym: walk‑away option - an option that allows a negotiator to end talks without agreeing to a bad deal
