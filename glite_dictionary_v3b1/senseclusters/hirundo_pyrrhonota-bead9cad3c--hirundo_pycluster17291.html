Hirundo pyrrhonota

noun
species of North American swallow
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɪˈrʌndoʊ pɪˌroʊˈnoʊtə / (AmE), / hɪˈrʌndəʊ pɪˌrɒˈnəʊtə / (BrE)
A North American swallow that nests in colonies and builds bottle-shaped mud nests on cliffs and walls.
