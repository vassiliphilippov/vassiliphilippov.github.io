bad check

noun
check not honored for insufficient funds
5.5 3.5
Pronunciation: / bæd tʃɛk / (AmE), / bæd tʃɛk / (BrE)
a check that cannot be honored by a bank because the account on which it is drawn lacks sufficient funds

Examples

A bad check
Businesses may refuse service to customers who have given them a bad check in the past.
He was arrested after writing a bad check at the grocery store.
Issuing a bad check is considered a criminal offense in many states.
Banks charge a penalty if you deposit a bad cheque.
He was arrested for writing a bad cheque at the grocery store.
Passing a bad cheque can be considered a criminal offense in many countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounced check - a check returned by the bank due to insufficient funds
  • synonym: dishonored cheque - a cheque not accepted by a bank, usually due to insufficient funds, a synonym
  • antonym: good cheque - a cheque honored by the bank, an antonym
  • antonym: valid check - a check that can be honored as funds are available
