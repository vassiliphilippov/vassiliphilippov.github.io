heterodactyl foot

noun
bird's foot with two toes back, two forward
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈdæktaɪl fʊt / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈdæktaɪl fʊt / (BrE)
A bird's foot in which the first and second toes point backward, and the third and fourth toes point forward.
