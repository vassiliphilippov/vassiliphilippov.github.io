flare

noun
unwanted optical reflection or image fogging
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
an unwanted bright spot or streak in a photograph or optical image, caused by stray light reflections within the optical system

Examples

Lens flare in photos
The photographer noticed a flare in the corner of his image due to sunlight hitting the lens.
The unwanted flare made part of the photograph look hazy and washed out.
Using a lens hood can reduce the risk of flare when shooting outdoors.
