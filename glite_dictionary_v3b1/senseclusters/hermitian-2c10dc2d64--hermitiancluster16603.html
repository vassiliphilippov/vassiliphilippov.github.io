Hermitian

adjective
matrix equal to conjugate transpose
Pronunciation: / hɜrˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / hɜːˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
relating to a matrix, operator, or form that is equal to its own conjugate transpose or adjoint (especially in mathematics or physics)
