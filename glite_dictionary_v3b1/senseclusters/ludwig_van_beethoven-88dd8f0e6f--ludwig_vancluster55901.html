Ludwig van Beethoven

famous German composer (1770–1827)

Pronunciation: /ˈluːd.vɪg væn ˈbeɪ.toʊ.vən/ /; / /ˈlʊd.vɪg væn ˈbeɪ.toʊ.vən/ /; / /ˈluːd.vɪg væn ˈbeɪ.tə.vən/ (AmE), /ˈluːd.vɪg væn ˈbeɪ.təʊ.vən/ /; / /ˈlʊd.vɪg væn ˈbeɪ.təʊ.vən/ /; / /ˈlʌd.wɪg væn ˈbeɪ.təʊ.vən/ (BrE)
(1770–1827) A German composer and pianist, regarded as one of the greatest figures in Western classical music, known especially for his symphonies and chamber music, and for continuing to compose after he became deaf.

Examples
