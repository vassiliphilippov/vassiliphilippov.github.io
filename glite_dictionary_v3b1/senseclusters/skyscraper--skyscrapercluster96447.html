skyscraper

noun
logic puzzle with visible-building clues
Pronunciation: / c8ska6acckre6ap59r / (AmE), / c8ska6acckre6ap59 / (BrE)
a logic puzzle played on an n×n grid in which numbers represent building heights and clues at the grid edges show how many buildings are visible from each side.

Examples

Skyscraper puzzle game
He spent the afternoon solving a skyscraper puzzle from the weekend puzzle book.
At the club meeting she showed us how to complete small skyscrapers using the edge clues.
To finish the hardest skyscraper, you must deduce each row's building heights from clues.
