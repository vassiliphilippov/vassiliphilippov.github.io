comparatist

adjective
comparative study
9.3
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpærətɪst / (AmE), / kəmˈpærətɪst / (BrE)
relating to the study or method of comparing different languages, literatures, cultures, or systems.

Examples

Comparatist approach
The comparatist perspective helps scholars find connections between different literary traditions.
Her comparatist analysis revealed similarities between Eastern and Western philosophies.
A comparatist approach is often used in cultural studies to highlight global influences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comparative - involving the act of comparing
