parthenogenetically

adverb
by asexual reproduction
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrθənoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpɑːθənəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner involving reproduction without fertilization, as by parthenogenesis

Examples

Reproduce parthenogenetically
Certain insects can reproduce parthenogenetically when no males are present in the population.
The scientist observed that the eggs developed parthenogenetically in the laboratory environment.
Some plants are able to propagate parthenogenetically under specific conditions.
