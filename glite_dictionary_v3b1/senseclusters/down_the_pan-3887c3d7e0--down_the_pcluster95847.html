down the pan

adverb
became ruined or wasted
7.6
Pronunciation: / daʊn ðə pæn / (AmE), / daʊn ðə pan / (BrE)
used to say that something is ruined, wasted, or fails completely

Examples

Down the pan completely
Her chances of winning the competition went down the pan when she missed the final round.
If we lose this contract, all our investments could go down the pan.
The company’s years of hard work went down the pan after the new regulations were introduced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: down the drain - used to say something is wasted or ruined
