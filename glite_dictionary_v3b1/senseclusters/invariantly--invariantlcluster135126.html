invariantly

adverb
without changing

Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈver.ɪ.ənt.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈveə.rɪ.ənt.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Gravity acts invariantly on all objects regardless of their mass.

He invariantly takes a walk after dinner, no matter the weather.
The store invariantly opens at 9 a.m. every day, including holidays.
