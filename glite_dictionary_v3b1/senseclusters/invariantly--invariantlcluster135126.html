invariantly

adverb
without changing
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛriəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛːrɪəntli / (BrE)
in a way that does not change; always the same or constant

Examples

Invariantly the same
The results of the experiment remained invariantly the same despite different starting points.
Gravity acts invariantly on all objects regardless of their mass.
The rules apply invariantly to every participant in the competition.
The results of the experiment invariantly matched the predictions, regardless of the conditions.
He invariantly takes a walk after dinner, no matter the weather.
The store invariantly opens at 9 a.m. every day, including holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: always - at all times; on every occasion
  • synonym: consistently - in a way that stays the same over time
  • antonym: never - at no time; not ever
  • antonym: variably - in a way that changes or is not constant
