dematerialize

verb
convert physical certificates into electronic form
8.1
Pronunciation: /diː.mə.ˈtɪr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ /; / /dɪ.mə.ˈtɪr.ɪ.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /diː.mə.ˈtɪə.riː.ə.laɪz/ /; / /dɪ.mə.ˈtɪə.riː.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
(in finance and records management) to convert physical securities, certificates, or documents into electronic form so they are held, transferred, and recorded electronically.

Examples

To dematerialize documents
Investors were required to dematerialise their share certificates and register them in the central depository.
The bank helped customers dematerialise old bond certificates so they could trade them electronically.
After dematerialising its stock, the company found shareholder communications became faster and cheaper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: convert - to change something into a different form, here physical documents into electronic records
  • synonym: dematerialize - US spelling variant meaning to convert physical certificates or documents into electronic form
