sticker

noun
sharp-pointed projection on a plant
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkər / (AmE), / ˈstɪkə / (BrE)
a small, sharp-pointed projection, resembling a spike or thorn, found on a plant stem or leaf

Examples

A plant sticker
Be careful of the sticker on that cactus; it can prick your finger.
Some weeds in the garden produce stickers that can get stuck in clothing.
The dog's fur was full of stickers after running through the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prickle - a small, sharp outgrowth on a plant stem or leaf
  • synonym: thorn - a stiff, sharp-pointed projection on a plant
