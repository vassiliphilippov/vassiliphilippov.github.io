Synonyms

    5.6 0.67/m
    antiaircraft refers to weapons or artillery that attack aircraft, while flak names the bursting shells and the guns used to hit planes.
  • antiaircraft gun
    5.3 0.15/m

  • ack-ack
    9.9 0.04/m

  • antiaircraft fire
