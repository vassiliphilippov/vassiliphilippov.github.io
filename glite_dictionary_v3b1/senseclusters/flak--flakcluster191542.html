flak

noun
antiaircraft artillery fire
9.9
Pronunciation: / flæk / (AmE), / flæk / (BrE)
antiaircraft artillery or bursting shells fired from it, used to attack aircraft in flight

Examples

Enemy flak hits planes
Puffs of black flak dotted the sky during the air raid.
The bombers faced heavy flak as they crossed enemy lines.
The pilot managed to fly through intense flak and return safely.
