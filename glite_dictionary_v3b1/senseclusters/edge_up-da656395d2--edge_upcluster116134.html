edge up

verb
increase slightly
Pronunciation: / ɛdʒ ʌp / (AmE), / ɛdʒ ʌp / (BrE)
to increase slowly or slightly, especially used for prices, amounts, or rates.

Examples

Prices edge up

Temperatures are expected to edge up a few degrees by the weekend.
Sales began to edge up slowly after the new advertising campaign launched.
