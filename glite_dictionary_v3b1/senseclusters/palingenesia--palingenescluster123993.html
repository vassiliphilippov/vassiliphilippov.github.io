palingenesia

noun
rebirth or renewal
Pronunciation: / ˌpælɪndʒəˈniːziə / (AmE), / ˌpælɪndʒɪˈniːziə / (BrE)
a rare term meaning rebirth or regeneration, especially spiritual or philosophical renewal

Examples

The palingenesia of society
The philosopher wrote about the palingenesia of society after a period of crisis.
In ancient texts, palingenesia was used to describe the soul's spiritual rebirth.
The poet believed in the palingenesia of nature each spring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destruction - the act of causing something to end or be ruined
  • synonym: palingenesis - rebirth or regeneration, especially spiritual renewal
  • synonym: regeneration - the process of renewal or restoration
