Dadaist

adjective
of Dada art movement
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːdəˌɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɑːdəɪst / (BrE)
relating to the Dada art movement, especially showing absurd, anti-art, or nonsensical qualities

Examples

Dadaist artwork
The artist's Dadaist approach challenged viewers to question the meaning of art.
Her latest play is filled with Dadaist humor and unexpected absurdities.
Critics described the performance as a bold and Dadaist statement against tradition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional - following traditional or accepted standards
  • synonym: surrealist - relating to the Surrealism art movement, often dreamlike or illogical
