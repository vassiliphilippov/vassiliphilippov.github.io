headedness

noun
dizzy sensation
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛdɪdnəs / (BrE)
a state of feeling dizzy, light-headed, or unsteady, often leading to faintness

Examples

Sudden headedness
After standing quickly she felt a sudden headedness that made her sit down immediately.
The medication caused mild headedness in some patients, especially when they stood up too fast.
He complained of headedness and weakness after skipping breakfast that morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dizziness - a feeling of unsteadiness or loss of balance
  • antonym: stability - the state of being steady and not likely to fall
  • synonym: vertigo - a sensation of spinning or losing balance
