vaccenic

adjective
from vaccenic acid
9.8
Pronunciation: / vækˈsiːnɪk / (AmE), / vækˈsiːnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from vaccenic acid, a type of unsaturated fatty acid found in animal fats and dairy products

Examples

Vaccenic acid content
The researchers analyzed the vaccenic content in various dairy products.
Milk from grass-fed cows tends to have higher vaccenic acid levels.
Scientists are studying the health effects of vaccenic acid in human diets.
