boron nitride

noun
a chemical compound of boron and nitrogen
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɹɑn ˈnaɪˌtraɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrɒn ˈnaɪtraɪd / (BrE)
a chemical compound (BN) consisting of equal numbers of boron and nitrogen atoms, with applications in industry and nanotechnology

Examples

Hexagonal boron nitride
Hexagonal boron nitride is often used as a lubricant at high temperatures.
Scientists are researching boron nitride nanotubes for use in advanced materials.
The structure of boron nitride is similar to that of graphite, making it useful in many applications.
