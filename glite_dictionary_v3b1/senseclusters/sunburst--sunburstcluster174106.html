sunburst

noun
radial hierarchical chart
7.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / c8s8cnccb5dst / (AmE), / c8s8cnccb5cd0st / (BrE)
(in data visualization) a radial chart that shows hierarchical data as concentric rings or segmented wedges, where each ring represents a level of the hierarchy.

Examples

Sunburst chart
The marketing team presented a colorful sunburst that showed customer segments and subcategories clearly.
On the dashboard, the interactive sunburst lets users click rings to explore nested category data.
To compare proportions, she converted the table into a radial sunburst this morning for the report.
