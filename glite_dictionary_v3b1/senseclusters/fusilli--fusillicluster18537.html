fusilli

noun
spiral pasta
Pronunciation: / fuˈsili / (AmE), / fuˈzɪli / (BrE)
a type of pasta shaped like short, twisted spirals, often used in salads or with sauces

Examples

A fusilli pasta
She made a delicious salad with fusilli and fresh vegetables.
The chef tossed the cooked fusilli with a creamy tomato sauce.
I prefer fusilli because its spiral shape holds more sauce than other pastas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: penne - a type of pasta shaped like short tubes
  • synonym: rotini - a similar spiral-shaped pasta
