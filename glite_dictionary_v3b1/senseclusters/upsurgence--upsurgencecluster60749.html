upsurgence

noun
sudden upward movement
8.8
Pronunciation: /ʌp.ˈsɜːr.dʒəns/ (AmE), /ʌp.ˈsɜː.dʒəns/ (BrE)
a sudden upward movement or emergence, especially of water, gas, or another substance from below the surface

Examples

Water upsurgence
The upsurgence of groundwater after the heavy rains flooded the lower fields.
Scientists observed an upsurgence of volcanic gases escaping through new cracks in the earth.
There was a sudden upsurgence of bubbles as the diver disturbed the lake bed.

  • synonym: upwelling - an upward flow of liquid or gas from below the surface
