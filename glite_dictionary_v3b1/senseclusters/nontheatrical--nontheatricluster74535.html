nontheatrical

adjective
not for theater
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnθiˈætrɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnθiˈætrɪkəl / (BrE)
not intended for or related to performance in a theater or theatrical setting

Examples

Nontheatrical film release
The film was distributed through nontheatrical channels like educational institutions and libraries.
Many documentaries have a nontheatrical release, reaching audiences outside of cinemas.
He specializes in nontheatrical productions, such as online videos and corporate presentations.
