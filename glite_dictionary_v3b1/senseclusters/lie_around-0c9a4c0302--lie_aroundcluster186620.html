lie around

verb
be idle; do nothing productive
6.4 3.1
Pronunciation: /laɪ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /laɪ ə.ˈraʊn/ (AmE), /laɪ ə.ˈraʊnd/ /; / /laɪ ə.ˈraʊn/ (BrE)
