lie around

verb
be idle; do nothing productive
6.4
Pronunciation: / laɪ əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / laɪ əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to spend time without doing anything useful or productive; to be idle or inactive

Examples

To lie around at home
After surgery, she had to lie around for a few days to recover.
He likes to lie around on weekends and watch TV.
Stop lying around all day and help me with the chores!

Related Terms

  • synonym: hang around - to spend time doing little or nothing, often waiting
  • synonym: laze - to spend time in a relaxed or lazy way, doing nothing much
  • antonym: work - to do tasks or be productive
