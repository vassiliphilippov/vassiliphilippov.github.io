PIR

noun
post-project review
9.3
Pronunciation: /pɪr/ /; / /ˈpiː.aɪ.ɑːr/ (AmE), /pɪə/ /; / /ˈpiː.aɪ.ɑː/ (BrE)
a formal evaluation conducted after a project or system is implemented to assess results and identify lessons learned

Examples

PIR review
The company held a PIR to evaluate the success of the new software rollout.
After every major project, a PIR helps the team improve future performance.
Lessons learned during the PIR were shared with all departments to avoid repeating mistakes.
