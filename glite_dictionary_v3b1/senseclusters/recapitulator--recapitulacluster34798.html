recapitulator

noun
one who summarizes
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkəˈpɪtʃəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ˌriːkəˈpɪtjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that summarizes or restates the main points of something

Examples

The recapitulator summarizes points
At the end of the conference, the recapitulator provided a concise overview of the key discussions.
The teacher acted as a recapitulator, restating the main ideas before the exam.
A good recapitulator helps everyone remember the most important points from a meeting.
