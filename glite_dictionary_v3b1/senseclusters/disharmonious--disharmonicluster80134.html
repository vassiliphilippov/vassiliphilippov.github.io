Synonyms

  • inharmonious
    8.3 0.03/m
    inharmonious feels formal and calls attention to parts that do not fit together, while disharmonious names a general lack of harmony.
  • unharmonious
    7.3 0.01/m
    unharmonious describes things that do not form a pleasing whole, while disharmonious is a broader word for lack of agreement or harmony.
  • discordant
    7.4 1.16/m
    discordant often shows a clear clash in sound or opinion, while disharmonious describes a more general absence of harmony.
  • dissonant
    7.1 0.15/m
    dissonant usually refers to clashing sounds or ideas and feels stronger, while disharmonious is a neutral term for lack of harmony.
