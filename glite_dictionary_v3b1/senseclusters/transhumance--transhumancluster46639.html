transhumance

noun
seasonal livestock migration
9.9
Pronunciation: /trænz.ˈhjuː.məns/ /; / /trænz.ˈjuː.məns/ (AmE), /trænz.ˈhjuː.məns/ /; / /trænz.ˈjuː.məns/ (BrE)
the seasonal movement of livestock between different grazing areas, usually between lowlands and highlands

Examples

Seasonal transhumance of livestock
The tradition of transhumance helps maintain the health of mountain pastures.
Many rural communities depend on transhumance to support their herds throughout the year.
Climate change is affecting the routes used for transhumance in several countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomadism - the practice of moving from place to place without a permanent home
  • antonym: sedentarism - the practice of living in one place without moving
