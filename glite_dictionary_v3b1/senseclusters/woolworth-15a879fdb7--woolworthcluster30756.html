Woolworth

proper noun
surname; retail founder
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlˌwɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈwʊlwəθ / (BrE)
a surname, especially referring to Frank Winfield Woolworth (1852–1919), the American entrepreneur who founded the Woolworth company

Examples

Frank Woolworth
Frank Winfield Woolworth revolutionized retail by introducing the five-and-dime store concept.
The Woolworth building in New York City was once the tallest skyscraper in the world.
Many people remember shopping at Woolworth stores during their childhood.
