freezer

noun
appliance for freezing and storing food
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːzər / (AmE), / ˈfriːzə / (BrE)
A device or compartment for freezing and storing food at very low temperatures for extended periods.

Examples

A freezer compartment
After the power outage, everything in the freezer began to thaw.
She opened the freezer to get some ice for her drink.
We keep homemade soup in the freezer for easy meals during the week.
