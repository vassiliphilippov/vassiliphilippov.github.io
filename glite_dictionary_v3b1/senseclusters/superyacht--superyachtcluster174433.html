superyacht

noun
luxury private yacht
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌjɑt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌjɒt / (BrE)
a very large and luxurious private yacht, typically owned by wealthy individuals

Examples

A superyacht party
The billionaire hosted a lavish party on his superyacht anchored off the coast of Monaco.
Many celebrities choose to spend their holidays traveling the world on a superyacht.
A superyacht can have its own swimming pool, helipad, and even a cinema.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: yacht - a medium-sized boat used for pleasure or racing
