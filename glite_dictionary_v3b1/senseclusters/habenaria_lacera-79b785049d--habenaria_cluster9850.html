Habenaria lacera

noun
a fringed orchid species of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / həˌbænəriə ləˈsɛrə / (AmE), / həˌbænəriə ləˈsɛrə / (BrE)
A species of fringed orchid native to eastern North America, with greenish flowers and a distinctly cut or fringed lip.

Examples

Habenaria lacera orchid
Botanists observed the unique, fringed lip of the Habenaria lacera during their summer field study.
Conservation efforts focus on protecting habitats vital for rare orchids such as Habenaria lacera.
The Habenaria lacera thrives in moist meadows and boggy areas across the eastern United States.
