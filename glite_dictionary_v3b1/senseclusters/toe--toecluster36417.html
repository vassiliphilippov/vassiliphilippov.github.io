toe

noun
part of a golf clubhead farthest from the shaft
6.1
Pronunciation: / toʊ / (AmE), / təʊ / (BrE)
(Golf) The part of a clubhead that is farthest from the shaft.

Examples

Club toe
If you hit the toe of the club, the ball may veer off to the right.
Many beginners have a tendency to strike shots toward the toe of the clubhead.
The new design places more weight toward the toe for better balance.
