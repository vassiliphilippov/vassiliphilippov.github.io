Archeopteryx

noun
extinct early bird genus from the Jurassic
Pronunciation: / ˌɑrkioʊˈtɜːrɪks /; / ˌɑrkiˈɑptərɪks / (AmE), / ˌɑːkiˈɒptərɪks / (BrE)
an extinct genus of early bird-like creatures from the Jurassic period, seen as a link between dinosaurs and modern birds
