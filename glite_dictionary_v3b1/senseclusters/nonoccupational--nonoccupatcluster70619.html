nonoccupational

adjective
not work-related
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɑːkjəˈpeɪʃənəl /; / ˌnɑnˌɑkjəˈpeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɒkjʊˈpeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not related to one's job or paid work

Examples

Nonoccupational injuries
The insurance policy covers only nonoccupational injuries that occur outside of work.
She developed a nonoccupational illness while traveling abroad on vacation.
Benefits are available for nonoccupational accidents as well as those that happen at work.
The doctor asked if his injury was nonoccupational or related to his work.
She participates in nonoccupational sports leagues to stay active after office hours.
Many people pursue nonoccupational activities like painting or gardening for relaxation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occupational - related to one's job or occupation
  • synonym: personal - relating to an individual or private life
  • synonym: recreational - done for enjoyment or relaxation rather than as a job
