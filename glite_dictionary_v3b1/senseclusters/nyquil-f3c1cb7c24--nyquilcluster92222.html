NyQuil

noun
cold and flu medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪkwɪl / (AmE), / ˈnaɪkwɪl / (BrE)
a brand-name over-the-counter medicine used to relieve cold and flu symptoms, often taken to help induce sleep

Examples

The pharmacist recommended NyQuil for nighttime relief of her cough and fever.
