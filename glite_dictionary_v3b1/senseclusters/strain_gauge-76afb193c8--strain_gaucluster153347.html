strain gauge

noun
device to measure surface strain
8.0
Pronunciation: / streɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / streɪn ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a device used to measure the amount of strain (deformation) on a surface

Examples

A strain gauge device
A strain gauge helps detect structural weaknesses by recording minute deformations in a material.
Researchers used a strain gauge to precisely measure the stretch in the metal rod under load.
The engineer attached a strain gauge to the bridge girder to monitor stress during testing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sensor - device that detects or measures a physical property
  • synonym: strain - deformation caused by stress on an object
