hoagy

noun
large sandwich on a long roll
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊ.giː/ /; / /ˈhoʊ.dʒiː/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊ.ɡiː/ /; / /ˈhəʊ.dʒiː/ (BrE)
A large sandwich made from a long, crusty roll filled with meats, cheese, and various condiments; also known as a sub or hero in different regions of the United States.

Examples

An Italian hoagy
I ordered an Italian hoagy for lunch at the deli.
She couldn't finish her foot-long hoagy, so she saved half for later.
The restaurant is famous for its enormous hoagies stuffed with fresh ingredients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - another regional term for a large sandwich of this type
  • synonym: sub - a similar large sandwich, also called 'submarine sandwich'
