sego

noun
North American lily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈsiːɡəʊ / (BrE)
a type of lily native to western North America, especially Utah, known for its white or yellow flowers

Examples

The sego lily
The sego is admired for its delicate petals and striking appearance in the wild.
Many people visit Utah in spring to see fields of blooming sego flowers.
The sego was chosen as the state flower because of its beauty and historical significance.
