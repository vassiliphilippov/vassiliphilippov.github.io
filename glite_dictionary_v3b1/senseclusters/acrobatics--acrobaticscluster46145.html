acrobatics

noun
skilled gymnastic feats or stunts
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌækrəˈbætɪks / (AmE), / ˌakrəˈbatɪks / (BrE)
the performance of skilled gymnastic or physical feats, especially those involving agility, balance, and coordination, as practiced by acrobats

Examples

Gymnastic acrobatics
She trained for years to perfect her acrobatics before joining the dance company.
The circus amazed the audience with breathtaking acrobatics performed high above the ground.
The film's action scenes feature impressive acrobatics and daring stunts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gymnastics - the practice or sport of exercises and performances requiring physical strength, flexibility, and coordination
