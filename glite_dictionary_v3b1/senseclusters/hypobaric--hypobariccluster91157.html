hypobaric

adjective
low atmospheric pressure
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈbærɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəʊˈbærɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving lower than normal atmospheric pressure

Examples

Hypobaric chamber
The scientists used a hypobaric chamber to study the effects of altitude on the human body.
Pilots must be aware of hypobaric conditions when flying at high elevations.
Training in a hypobaric environment helps athletes prepare for competitions at high altitudes.
