niggardliness

noun
extreme stinginess; excessive unwillingness to give
9.9 9.4
Pronunciation: /ˈnɪg.ərd.lɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈnɪg.əd.lɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
Extreme stinginess or reluctance to give or spend; excessive meanness in money matters.

Examples

Extreme niggardliness
Despite his wealth, his niggardliness was obvious in even the smallest matters.
His niggardliness made him unpopular among friends and family.
The charity suffered because of the government’s niggardliness in funding social programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - willingness to give or share; opposite of stinginess
  • synonym: parsimony - extreme unwillingness to spend money
  • synonym: stinginess - unwillingness to spend money or give
