lashkar

noun
armed militia group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlæʃkɑr / (AmE), / ˈlæʃkɑː / (BrE)
a group of armed people or a militia, especially in South Asian regions

Examples

A local lashkar was formed to protect the community from bandits.
The government negotiated with the lashkar to restore peace in the region.
