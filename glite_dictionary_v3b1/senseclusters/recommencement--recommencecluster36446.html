recommencement

noun
the act of beginning again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːkəˈmɛnsmənt / (AmE), / ˌriːkəˈmɛnsmənt / (BrE)
The act or process of beginning something again after it has stopped or been interrupted.

Examples

The recommencement of work
Following a long pause, the recommencement of the meeting was announced.
The recommencement of negotiations brought hope for a peaceful solution.
The city celebrated the recommencement of the annual festival after a two-year hiatus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resumption - the act of starting something again after a pause
