pichi

noun
Peruvian medicinal shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈpiː.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈpiː.tʃi / (BrE)
A Peruvian shrub with small pink to lavender tubular flowers; its leaves are used as a tonic and diuretic.

Examples

The pichi shrub
Local farmers showed us the delicate pichi shrubs growing along the riverbanks in Peru.
Some herbal supplements contain extracts of pichi for their purported medicinal effects.
The herbalist prepared a tea from the dried pichi leaves to use as a gentle diuretic.
