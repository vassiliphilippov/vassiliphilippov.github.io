pichi

noun
Peruvian medicinal shrub
6.1 9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.tʃiː/ /; / /pɪ.ˈtʃiː/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.tʃiː/ /; / /pɪ.ˈtʃiː/ (BrE)
