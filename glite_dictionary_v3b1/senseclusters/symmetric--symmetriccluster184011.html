symmetric

adjective
having balanced, corresponding parts
3.7 3.5
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /ˈsɪm.ə.trɪk/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈme.trɪk/ /; / /ˈsɪm.ə.trɪk/ (BrE)
having similarity or correspondence in size, shape, and relative position of parts on opposite sides of a dividing line or plane; exhibiting symmetry

Examples
