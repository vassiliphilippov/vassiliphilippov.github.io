cetacean

adjective
relating to whales and dolphins
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / sɪˈteɪsɪən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of whales, dolphins, and porpoises

Examples

Cetacean research
Many cetacean species are protected due to concerns about their declining populations.
Recent studies have revealed new information about cetacean intelligence and social behaviors.
The museum features a vast collection of cetacean skeletons, including those of whales and dolphins.
