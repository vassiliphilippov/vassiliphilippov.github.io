vicinity

noun
area nearby or surrounding
Pronunciation: / vəˈsɪnəti / (AmE), / vɪˈsɪnɪti / (BrE)
the area immediately surrounding a particular place; a nearby region or neighborhood

Examples

The vicinity of a hotel
Several restaurants can be found in the vicinity of the hotel.
The police established a perimeter in the vicinity of the crime scene.
Wild animals are rarely seen in this vicinity during the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neighborhood - the area surrounding a particular place
  • synonym: proximity - closeness in space, time, or relationship
