hydroponically

adverb
using water to grow plants
6.7
Pronunciation: /haɪ.drə.ˈpɑː.nɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /haɪ.drə.ˈpɒn.ɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that involves growing plants in water with added nutrients instead of soil

Examples

To grow hydroponically
Lettuce is often grown hydroponically to save space and water in urban farms.
Some restaurants use herbs that are cultivated hydroponically in their own kitchens.
The scientist explained how strawberries can be produced hydroponically all year round.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soilless - without using soil
  • antonym: traditionally - in the usual or conventional way, often using soil for plants
