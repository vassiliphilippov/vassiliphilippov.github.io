peaky

adjective
having pronounced peaks (esp. in sound/signals)
7.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpiÀêki / (AmE), / ÀàpiÀêki / (BrE)
(of a signal, sound, or visual display) having sharp or pronounced peaks, especially in high-frequency ranges.

Examples

Peaky signal
Engineers adjusted the monitor to reduce its peaky response at high frequencies.
Her voice can become rather peaky on certain notes when the volume is high.
The audio track sounded too peaky for my taste and needed some equalization.
