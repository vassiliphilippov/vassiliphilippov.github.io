active

adjective
serving in the military; ready for operations
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˈæk.tɪv / (BrE)
(of military personnel) engaged in or ready for military or naval operations; currently serving and available for duty.

Examples

Active duty
After graduating from the academy, she served as an active officer in the Navy for five years.
He was placed on active duty after the unit received new orders.
Only active members of the armed forces are eligible for this deployment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inactive - not currently engaged in military or naval operations; not on active duty
