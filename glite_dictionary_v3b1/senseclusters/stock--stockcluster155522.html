stock

noun
ancestry; family lineage
5.8 2/m
Pronunciation: / stÉ‘k / (AmE), / stÉ’k / (BrE)
the ancestry or line of descent from which a person or group originates; lineage or family background.

Examples

Noble stock
He comes from noble stock with a long history of leadership.
Many believe that talent runs in the stock of that family.
Their stock can be traced back to early settlers in the region.
