Adirondack

proper noun
New York mountain range
Pronunciation: / ˌædəˈrɑndæk / (AmE), / ˌædəˈrɒndæk / (BrE)
a mountain range in northeastern New York State, or something named after it, such as a style of outdoor chair

Examples

Adirondack Mountains
We spent our vacation hiking in the Adirondack Mountains.
The classic Adirondack chair is popular for outdoor seating on porches and patios.
Many tourists visit the Adirondack region each fall to see the colorful leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Catskills - a mountain range in southeastern New York State
