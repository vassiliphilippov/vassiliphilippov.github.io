lightness

noun
cheerful or carefree attitude
5.9 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being cheerful, lighthearted, or lacking seriousness; a carefree or joyful attitude or feeling

Examples

Lightness of mood
He approached the stressful situation with admirable lightness and humor.
Her lightness at the meeting helped everyone relax and enjoy the discussion.
There was a lightness in her voice that made the conversation delightful.
He spoke with a lightness in his voice that hadn't been there before.
The team's victory brought a sense of lightness to the entire community.
When she received the good news, a wave of lightness washed over her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elation - a feeling of great joy or pride
  • antonym: joylessness - a lack of joy or happiness
  • synonym: levity - lack of seriousness; lightness of manner
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious; lack of playfulness
