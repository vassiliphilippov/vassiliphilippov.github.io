heartless

adjective
cruel; lacking compassion or sympathy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtləs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtləs / (BrE)
showing no kindness, sympathy, or compassion; cruel or unfeeling.

Examples

A heartless act
Her heartless remark left everyone in the room stunned and silent.
It's heartless to abandon a pet when it becomes inconvenient.
The company made a heartless decision to lay off hundreds of workers before the holidays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • synonym: cruel - showing a willingness to cause pain or suffering
