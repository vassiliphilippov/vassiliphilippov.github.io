spotted sunfish

noun
a type of small freshwater fish of southeastern U.S.
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːtɪd ˈsʌnˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈspɒtɪd ˈsʌnˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish (Lepomis punctatus) of the sunfish family, native to streams in the southeastern United States, notable for its dark spots and valued as a panfish
