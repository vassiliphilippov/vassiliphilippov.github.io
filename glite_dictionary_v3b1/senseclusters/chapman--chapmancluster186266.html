chapman

noun
historical peddler or traveling salesman
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæpmən / (AmE), / ˈtʃapmən / (BrE)
a man who travels from place to place selling goods; an itinerant peddler, especially in the past

Examples

A chapman sells goods
A chapman was often a welcome sight in rural communities starved of supplies.
In the 17th century, a chapman would travel from village to village selling his wares.
The old chapman offered ribbons and trinkets to the townsfolk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peddler - someone who travels to sell goods
