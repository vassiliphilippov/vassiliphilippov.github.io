edible-podded pea

noun
pea with edible pods
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdəbl ˈpɑdɪd pi / (AmE), / ˌɛdəbl ˈpɒdɪd piː / (BrE)
A type of pea plant whose pods are thick, soft, and can be eaten along with the peas, unlike common peas with inedible pods.

Examples

Fresh edible-podded peas
Farmers planted several rows of edible-podded peas to sell at the local market.
Stir-fries often include edible-podded pea for a fresh, sweet crunch.
You can eat the entire edible-podded pea, including the pod, which makes them convenient for snacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snow pea - a variety of edible-podded pea with flat pods
  • synonym: sugar snap pea - a variety of edible-podded pea with rounded, crisp pods
