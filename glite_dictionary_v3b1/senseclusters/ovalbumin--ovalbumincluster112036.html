ovalbumin

noun
main protein in egg white
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvæl.bju.mɪn / (AmE), / əʊˈvæl.bjuː.mɪn / (BrE)
the main protein found in egg white, forming the bulk of its structure and giving it its characteristics

Examples

Ovalbumin protein
Allergic reactions can be caused by ovalbumin present in food products.
During baking, ovalbumin helps to stabilize the structure of meringues.
The laboratory isolated ovalbumin to study its properties as a protein.

Related Terms

  • synonym: albumin - any of a group of simple proteins, found especially in egg white, blood, and milk
