Examples

Elderly shut-in
After her accident, she became a shut-in and rarely saw anyone except close friends.
The charity delivers meals daily to local shut-ins who cannot leave their homes.
Volunteers provide companionship and assistance to elderly shut-ins in the community.

Synonyms

    6.7 0.03/m

    6.0 0.25/m

  • bedridden
    4.5 0.73/m
