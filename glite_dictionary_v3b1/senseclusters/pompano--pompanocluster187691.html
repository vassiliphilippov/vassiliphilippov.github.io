pompano

noun
a warm-water fish, or its edible flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːmpəˌnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɒmpənəʊ / (BrE)
a type of warm-water marine fish, or its flesh used as food

Examples
