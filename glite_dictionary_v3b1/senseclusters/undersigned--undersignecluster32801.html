undersigned

noun
person signing document
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərsaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəsaɪnd / (BrE)
the person or people whose name or names are signed at the end of a document

Examples

The undersigned confirms
The undersigned hereby certifies that all information provided is accurate.
Please contact the undersigned if you have any questions about this agreement.
The contract must be returned with the undersigned for it to be valid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: signatory - a person who has signed a document
