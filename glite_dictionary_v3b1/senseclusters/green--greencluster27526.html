green

noun
golf course area around the hole
Pronunciation: / ɡrin / (AmE), / ɡriːn / (BrE)
an area of smooth, closely-cropped grass on a golf course, surrounding the hole

Examples

Golf green
Golfers must read the slope of the green to judge their putts correctly.
He sank the putt from the edge of the green.
The groundskeepers mow the greens every morning to keep them perfect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairway - the part of a golf course between the tee and the green
