shlepper

noun
an awkward or inept person
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɛpər / (AmE), / ˈʃlɛpə / (BrE)
an awkward, clumsy, or inept person, especially one who is regarded with mild contempt or pity

Examples

A clumsy shlepper
Although she's a talented artist, she's a complete shlepper when it comes to organizing her studio.
Everyone said I was a shlepper for spilling soup all over the table.
He always looks like a bit of a shlepper in his wrinkled clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bungler - a person who does things badly or awkwardly
