use

noun
purpose or function of something
1.0 302/m
Pronunciation: / jus / (AmE), / juÀês / (BrE)
the purpose, function, benefit, or act of using something

Examples

A use for something
I see no practical use for this old equipment.
Is there any use in trying to change his mind?
She questioned the use of continuing the discussion any further.
Rising use of energy is a concern for environmentalists.
Some factories have reduced their use of water to lower costs.
The use of raw materials in this sector is closely monitored.
She found another use for the empty bottles in her workshop.
The old building has been put to good use as a community center.
With careful use, this tool can last for many years.

Synonyms

  • utilization
    4.3 6.91/m
    utilization is a formal word for the act or process of using something efficiently, while use is broader and everyday.
  • application
    3.1 34/m
    application means putting something into operation for a task or problem, while use covers any purpose or way it is used.
  • employment
    2.1 2.90/m
    employment means using something for a particular purpose, often in formal or technical writing, while use is general and common.
  • purpose
    1.6 152/m
    purpose is the reason or intended aim for doing something, while use focuses on the act or practical way it is used.
  • usage
    1.8 15/m
    usage shows the usual manner or habit of using something in speech or practice, while use covers both purpose and action.
  • function
    2.9 52/m
    function is the role or job something performs within a system, while use can mean both purpose and the act of using it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consumption - the act of using up a resource
  • antonym: disuse - the state of not being used or no longer used
  • synonym: function - the practical use or purpose of something
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
  • synonym: utility - the state of being useful or beneficial
  • synonym: utilization - the act of making practical or effective use of something
