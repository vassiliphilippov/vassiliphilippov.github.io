anatomize

verb
analyze in minute detail
6.4 8.8
Pronunciation: / əˈnætəˌmaɪz /; / əˈnæt̬.əˌmaɪz /; / əˈnæt̬.ə.maɪz / (AmE), / əˈnætəmaɪz /; / əˈnæt.ə.maɪz / (BrE)
to examine or analyze something in great detail, especially by breaking it down into its parts

Examples
