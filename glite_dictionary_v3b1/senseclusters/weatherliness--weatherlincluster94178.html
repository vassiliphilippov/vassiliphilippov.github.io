weatherliness

noun
ability of a vessel to sail close to the wind
8.7 9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈweð.ər.lɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈweð.ər.liː.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈweð.ə.lɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈweð.ə.liː.nəs/ (BrE)
