favicon

noun
website icon
Pronunciation: / ˈfævɪˌkɑn / (AmE), / ˈfævɪˌkɒn / (BrE)
a small icon that represents a website, shown in the browser tab, bookmarks, or address bar

Examples

Website favicon
The website's favicon appears next to its name in the browser tab.
She designed a new favicon to make the site more recognizable in bookmarks.
If your favicon is missing, some browsers will show a default icon instead.
