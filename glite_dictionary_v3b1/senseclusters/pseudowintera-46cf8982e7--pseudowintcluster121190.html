Pseudowintera

noun
genus of evergreen shrubs or trees
Pronunciation: / suːˌdoʊˈwɪntərə /; / ˌsuːdoʊˈwɪntərə / (AmE), / sjuːˌdəʊˈwɪntərə /; / ˌsjuːdəʊˈwɪntərə / (BrE)
a genus of evergreen shrubs or small trees native to Australia and New Zealand, known for aromatic leaves; includes the horopito; belongs to the family Winteraceae
