broadcaster

noun
device for scattering material
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæstər / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌkɑːstə / (BrE)
a mechanical device for scattering seeds, fertilizer, sand, or similar materials evenly over a wide area

Examples

Seed broadcaster
A handheld broadcaster is ideal for spreading salt on icy sidewalks during winter.
After filling the broadcaster with grass seed, she walked slowly to cover the entire lawn.
The farmer used a broadcaster to distribute fertilizer evenly across the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spreader - a device used for spreading things such as fertilizer or seeds
