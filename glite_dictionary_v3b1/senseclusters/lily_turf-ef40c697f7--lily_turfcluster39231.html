lily turf

noun
tufted ground-covering plant with purple flowers
2.9 6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪli tɝːf / (AmE), / ˈlɪli tɜːf / (BrE)
An Asiatic perennial herb (genus Liriope) with grasslike evergreen leaves and clusters of dark mauve, grapelike flowers, commonly grown as ground cover.

Examples

Purple lily turf flowers
Clusters of purple flowers appeared on the lily turf in late summer, adding color to the landscape.
The lily turf thrived in the shady areas of the garden where grass wouldn't grow.
We planted lily turf along the pathway to create a neat, green border.
