frostbiting

noun
damage from freezing
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɔstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstˌbaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of tissue or plant damage caused by exposure to freezing temperatures

Examples

Severe frostbiting
The unexpected late freeze caused severe frostbiting of the orchard's young blossoms.
Farmers worry about frostbiting when temperatures drop suddenly in early spring.
Prolonged exposure to cold winds led to frostbiting of the hikers' fingers and toes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freezing - the process of becoming frozen due to low temperatures
  • synonym: frostbite - injury to body tissue caused by freezing
