woofy

adjective
dog-like or burly
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːfi / (AmE), / ˈwuːfi / (BrE)
informal term describing someone or something as resembling a dog, especially in a friendly, strong, or burly way

Examples

Woofy personality
The new coach has a woofy charm that makes everyone feel welcome.
Fans described the actor as woofy because of his rugged looks and friendly attitude.
Her woofy dog greeted everyone at the park with a wagging tail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burly - strong and heavily built
  • synonym: doggy - resembling or characteristic of a dog
