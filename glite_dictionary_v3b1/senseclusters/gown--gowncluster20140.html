gown

noun
long ceremonial or official garment
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, formal or ceremonial garment, such as an academic, judicial, or evening dress

Examples

Academic gown
Graduates received their diplomas while wearing academic caps and gowns.
Her ceremonial gown was adorned with gold embroidery for the special occasion.
The judge entered the courtroom dressed in a traditional black gown.
Many celebrities walked the red carpet in designer gowns.
She wore a stunning evening gown to the charity ball.
The bride chose a simple white gown for her wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dress - a one-piece garment for a woman or girl
  • synonym: robe - a long, loose outer garment, often worn as formal or ceremonial dress
