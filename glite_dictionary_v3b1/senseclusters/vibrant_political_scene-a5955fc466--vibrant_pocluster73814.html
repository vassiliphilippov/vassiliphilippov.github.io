vibrant political scene

noun
dynamic and lively political environment
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrənt pəˈlɪtɪkəl siːn / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrənt pəˈlɪtɪkəl siːn / (BrE)
a dynamic and energetic environment in which political activities, debates, and events are lively and frequently occurring
