poor decision

noun
unwise or bad choice
Pronunciation: / pʊr dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / pɔː dɪˈsɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a choice or judgment that is not wise or is likely to lead to negative consequences

Examples

A poor decision
He admitted that taking the job offer without thinking was a poor decision.
Making a poor decision early in the project caused many problems later.
The company suffered losses due to several poor decisions by its leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bad decision - an unwise or harmful choice
  • antonym: good decision - a wise or beneficial choice
