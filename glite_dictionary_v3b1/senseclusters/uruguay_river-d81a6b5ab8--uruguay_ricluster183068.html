Uruguay River

proper noun
river forming part of boundaries in South America
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊrəˌɡwaɪ ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəˌɡwaɪ ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in South America rising in southern Brazil and flowing south to the Río de la Plata, forming part of the boundary between Argentina and Brazil, and then between Argentina and Uruguay

Examples

The Uruguay River banks
Fishing is a major activity along the banks of the Uruguay River.
Heavy rainfall caused flooding along the Uruguay River last year.
The Uruguay River is an important waterway for transporting goods between Brazil, Argentina, and Uruguay.
