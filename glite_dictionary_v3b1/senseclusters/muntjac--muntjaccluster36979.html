muntjac

noun
a small Asian deer
5.2 8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌnt.dʒæk / (AmE), / ˈmʌnt.dʒæk / (BrE)
a small Asian deer with short antlers and a barking call, native to South and Southeast Asia

Examples

A muntjac deer

In parts of England, the muntjac has become an invasive species, thriving in woodlands and gardens.
The muntjac is often heard before it is seen, thanks to its distinctive barking call.
