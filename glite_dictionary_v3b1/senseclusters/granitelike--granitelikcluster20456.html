granitelike

adjective
resembling granite in hardness or appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrænɪtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡrænɪtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Very hard or tough, resembling granite in hardness or appearance.

Examples

Granitelike hardness
Her granitelike resolve inspired everyone around her.
His determination was granitelike, refusing to bend under pressure.
The ancient statue had a granitelike exterior that endured centuries of weather.
