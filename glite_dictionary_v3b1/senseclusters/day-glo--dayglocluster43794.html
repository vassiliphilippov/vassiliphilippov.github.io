day-glo

adjective
fluorescent color
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌɡləʊ / (BrE)
very bright and fluorescent in color, often appearing to glow in daylight

Examples

Day-glo orange paint
He painted his bicycle with day-glo orange to make it more visible at night.
The artist used day-glo colors to create a striking, modern look in her mural.
At the festival, many people wore day-glo accessories that glowed under the lights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or vividness
  • synonym: neon - extremely bright or fluorescent in color
