antiscience

adjective
opposed to science
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
opposed to or rejecting the principles, methods, or findings of science

Examples

Antiscience views
The politician's antiscience statements alarmed many experts in the medical community.
Some groups promote antiscience views that challenge well-established research.
Education programs aim to counter antiscience attitudes with accurate information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pseudoscientific - falsely or mistakenly claimed to be scientific
  • antonym: scientific - relating to or based on science
