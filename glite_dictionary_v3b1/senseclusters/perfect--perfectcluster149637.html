perfect

verb
to make perfect or improve fully
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛkt / (AmE), / pəˈfɛkt / (BrE)
to make something as good, complete, or effective as possible; to improve so that it is perfect

Examples

To perfect a skill
She spent years trying to perfect her painting technique.
The chef is always looking for ways to perfect his recipes.
You can perfect your language skills by practicing every day.

  • synonym: refine - to improve something by making small changes
