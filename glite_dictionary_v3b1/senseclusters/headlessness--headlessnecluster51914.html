headlessness

noun
absence of leadership
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.lə.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈhed.lə.nəs/ (BrE)
the state of lacking a leader or person in charge, resulting in absence of direction or guidance

Examples

Team headlessness
The sudden resignation of the manager left the team in a state of headlessness.
Periods of headlessness can cause confusion and slow progress in any organization.
After the principal retired, the school experienced months of headlessness before a replacement was found.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anarchy - a state of disorder due to lack of authority
  • synonym: leaderless - without a leader or person in charge
  • antonym: leadership - the action or position of leading a group or organization
