multiplayer

adjective
for several players
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌpleɪər / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌpleɪə / (BrE)
allowing or involving several people to play a game together at the same time

Examples

Multiplayer game mode
The new racing game has a multiplayer mode where friends can compete online.
We spent hours enjoying the multiplayer features of the adventure game together.
Many popular games now offer both single-player and multiplayer options.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperative - involving players working together towards a common goal
  • antonym: single-player - designed for one person to play alone
