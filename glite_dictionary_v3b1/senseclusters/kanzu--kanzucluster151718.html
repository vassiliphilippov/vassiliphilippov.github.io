kanzu

noun
East African men's long robe
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnzuː / (AmE), / ˈkænzuː / (BrE)
a long, usually white robe with long sleeves traditionally worn by men in East Africa

Examples

A white kanzu
He wore a crisp white kanzu to the wedding ceremony.
Many men don a kanzu during religious festivals in Tanzania.
The kanzu is often paired with a cap called a 'kofia'.
