seeding

noun
removing seeds
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of removing seeds from fruit or vegetables before cooking or serving

Examples

Seeding tomatoes
She spent the afternoon seeding the tomatoes before making the fresh salsa.
After seeding the peppers, he chopped them for the salad.
The recipe recommends seeding the cucumbers to avoid extra moisture in the dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitting - the act of removing pits or stones from fruit
