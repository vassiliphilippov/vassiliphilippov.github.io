pastel

noun
a soft, pale color
2.1
Pronunciation: / pæˈstɛl / (AmE), / pæˈstɛl / (BrE)
a soft, pale color; any of various light shades, especially as used in art, fashion, or design

Examples

A pastel shade
She prefers decorating her room with pastel shades for a calming effect.
Spring fashion collections often feature clothes in cheerful pastel tones.
The artist used soft pastel colors to create a delicate landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pale - light in color or shade; lacking intensity or brightness
  • antonym: vivid - having strong, bright colors
