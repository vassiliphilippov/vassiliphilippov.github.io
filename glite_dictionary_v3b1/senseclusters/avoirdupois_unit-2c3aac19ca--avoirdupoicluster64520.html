avoirdupois unit

noun
unit of weight in the avoirdupois system
Pronunciation: / ˌævɚdəˈpwɑɪz ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / ˌævədəˈpɔɪz ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
any of the units of weight used in the avoirdupois system, such as the pound or ounce, chiefly for measuring mass in the imperial and US customary systems.
