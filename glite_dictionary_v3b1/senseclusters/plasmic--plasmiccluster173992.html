plasmic

adjective
of plasma (gas)
Pronunciation: / ˈplæzmɪk / (AmE), / ˈplæzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to plasma, especially the ionized gas found in physics or phenomena produced by such a gas

Examples

Plasmic arc
The scientist observed a plasmic arc forming between the electrodes during the test.
Auroras are beautiful plasmic displays caused by charged particles in the Earth's atmosphere.
The plasmic state of matter is often found in stars and lightning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plasma - an ionized gas consisting of charged particles
