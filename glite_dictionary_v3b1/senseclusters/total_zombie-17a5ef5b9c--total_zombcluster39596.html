total zombie

noun
utterly unresponsive or disengaged person
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊt̬l ˈzɑːmbi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtəl ˈzɒmbi / (BrE)
a person who is completely unresponsive, disengaged, or mentally absent, often due to exhaustion or distraction

Examples

A total zombie at work
After staying up all night to finish her project, she felt like a total zombie at work.
He was a total zombie during the meeting and didn't hear a word that was said.
If I don't get my morning coffee, I'm a total zombie for hours.
