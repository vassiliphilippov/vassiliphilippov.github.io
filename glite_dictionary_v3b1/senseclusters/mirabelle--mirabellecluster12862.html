mirabelle

noun
small sweet plum
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪrəˈbɛl / (AmE), / ˌmɪrəˈbɛl / (BrE)
a small, sweet yellow or red plum, often used for making preserves and desserts

Examples

A mirabelle plum
She made a delicious jam from fresh mirabelle picked in the countryside.
The market stall offered baskets of golden mirabelle during the summer season.
A slice of tart filled with mirabelle is a popular dessert in parts of France.
