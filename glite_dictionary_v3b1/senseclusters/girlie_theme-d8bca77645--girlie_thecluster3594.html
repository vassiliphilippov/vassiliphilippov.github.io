girlie theme

noun
motif with feminine elements
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝːli θim / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːli θiːm / (BrE)
A decorative or stylistic motif featuring elements commonly associated with femininity, such as pink, lace, or florals.

Examples

A girlie theme party
He surprised his daughter with a girlie theme party full of glitter and flowers.
Her bedroom has a girlie theme with butterflies and pink curtains.
The event planners chose a girlie theme for the baby shower.
