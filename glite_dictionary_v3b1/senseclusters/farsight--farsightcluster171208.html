farsight

noun
maximum visible distance
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːrˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɑːsaɪt / (BrE)
the maximum distance at which something can be seen, detected, or targeted, especially in technical, military, or gaming contexts

Examples

Radar farsight
The radar system's farsight allows it to detect aircraft from many miles away.
In the strategy game, each unit has a different farsight that affects how much of the map they reveal.
Fog can greatly reduce your farsight when driving in the countryside at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: range - the distance over which something can be seen or detected
  • synonym: visibility - the distance one can see under certain conditions
