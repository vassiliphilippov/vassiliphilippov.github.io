tanha

noun
Buddhist craving
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːn.hɑː / (AmE), / ˈtʌn.hɑː / (BrE)
in Buddhism, the craving or desire for things or experiences that leads to suffering

Examples

Meditation practices are designed to help people overcome tanha and find inner peace.
