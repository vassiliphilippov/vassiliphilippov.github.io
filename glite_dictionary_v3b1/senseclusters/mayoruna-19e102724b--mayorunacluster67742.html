Mayoruna

proper noun
Amazonian indigenous group
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪəˈruːnə / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəˈruːnə / (BrE)
an indigenous people living in the Amazon region of South America, known for their unique culture and traditions

Examples

The Mayoruna people
The Mayoruna have a rich tradition of body art and rituals.
Researchers visited the Mayoruna to learn about their knowledge of Amazonian plants.
The Mayoruna community works to preserve their language and customs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Matsés - another name for the Mayoruna people
  • synonym: Yanomami - an indigenous people of the Amazon rainforest
