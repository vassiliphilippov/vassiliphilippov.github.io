rogation

noun
solemn church supplication ceremony
Pronunciation: / roʊˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / rəʊˈɡeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a solemn supplication ceremony, especially in the Christian church, usually involving prayers for divine blessing, often before planting crops

Examples

A rogation ceremony
During the rogation, the congregation walked through the fields singing hymns.
The history of rogation days dates back to early medieval Europe.
The parish held a traditional rogation to pray for a good harvest.
