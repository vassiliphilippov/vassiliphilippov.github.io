Examples

Synonyms

  • deferral
    5.8 0.98/m
    deferral is a formal act of delaying something to a later set time, while postponement is a general delay that moves an event later.
  • deferment
    6.2 1.19/m
    deferment is the legal or formal action of moving a date to later, while postponement is the general act of delaying an event to a new time.
  • delay
    1.3 36/m
    delay is a period when something is late or held up, sometimes by accident, while postponement means moving an event to a later planned time.
  • hold
    2.0 10/m
    hold is a short pause when action is stopped temporarily, often without a new date, while postponement sets a later time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: advancement - the act of bringing something forward to an earlier time
  • synonym: delay - the period of causing something to happen later than planned
