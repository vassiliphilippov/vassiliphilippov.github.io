tethering

noun
fastening to a point
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈte.ðər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈte.ðə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of fastening an animal or object to a fixed point with a rope, chain, or similar device to restrict movement
