Spinozist

adjective
of Spinoza's philosophy
Pronunciation: / spɪˈnoʊzɪst / (AmE), / spɪˈnəʊzɪst / (BrE)
relating to the philosophy, doctrines, or ideas of Baruch Spinoza, a Dutch philosopher.

Examples

Spinozist philosophy
The professor gave a Spinozist analysis of the relationship between mind and body.
Her worldview is deeply influenced by Spinozist ideas about nature and God.
Many modern philosophers have debated the relevance of Spinozist ethics in contemporary society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Cartesian - relating to the philosophy of René Descartes
  • synonym: Spinozism - the philosophical system of Baruch Spinoza
