emo

noun
member of emo subculture
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈiːmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈiːməʊ / (BrE)
a person who is part of the emo subculture, known for its distinctive music, fashion, and emotional expression

Examples

An emo kid
He dressed like an emo and listened to bands with emotional lyrics.
Many teenagers went through an emo phase in the early 2000s.
The group of emos at school often shared their favorite music and fashion tips.
