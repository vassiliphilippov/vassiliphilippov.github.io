town and gown

noun
university and local community relationship
Pronunciation: / taʊn ənd ɡaʊn / (AmE), / taʊn ənd ɡaʊn / (BrE)
the relationship, often marked by tension or cooperation, between a university and the local residents of the town where it is located

Examples

Town and gown relations
Efforts to improve town and gown relations included joint festivals and discussion panels.
Protests sometimes arise from misunderstandings between town and gown in university cities.
The university set up a liaison office to address town and gown issues more effectively.
