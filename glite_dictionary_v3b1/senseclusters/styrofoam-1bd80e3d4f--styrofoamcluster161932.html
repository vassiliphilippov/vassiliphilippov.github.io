Styrofoam

noun
lightweight polystyrene foam material
5.2 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪrəˌfoʊm / (AmE), / ˈstaɪərəfəʊm / (BrE)
A type of lightweight, resilient foam made from polystyrene, commonly used for insulation and packing materials.

Examples

A sheet of Styrofoam
A sheet of Styrofoam was used as insulation under the roof.
Cups made from Styrofoam are not biodegradable and harm the environment.
They packed the electronics in Styrofoam to prevent damage during shipping.
