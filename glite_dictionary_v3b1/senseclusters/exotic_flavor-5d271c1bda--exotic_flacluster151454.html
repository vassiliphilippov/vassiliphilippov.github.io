exotic flavor

noun
distinctive, unusual taste (esp. foreign)
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɑtɪk ˈfleɪvɚ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɒtɪk ˈfleɪvə / (BrE)
a taste that is distinctive and unusual, often associated with foreign or unfamiliar foods

Examples

An exotic flavor in food
Adding saffron gives the dish an exotic flavor that sets it apart from traditional recipes.
Many teas from Asia are celebrated for their exotic flavor and aroma.
The new restaurant is known for its exotic flavor combinations that surprise the palate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary flavor - a flavor that is typical and not unusual
  • synonym: unusual flavor - a flavor that is not commonly found or expected
