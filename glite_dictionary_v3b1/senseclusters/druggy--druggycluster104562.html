druggy

adjective
related to drug culture
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈdrʌɡi / (BrE)
having qualities, atmosphere, or appearance associated with drug use or drug culture

Examples

Druggy style
The artist's new collection has a distinctly druggy style with swirling colors and surreal shapes.
Some people avoid the club because of its druggy atmosphere and loud music.
His druggy appearance made it hard for him to find a job in a conservative office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychedelic - having vivid colors or patterns, often associated with drug use
  • antonym: sober - not affected by drugs or alcohol
