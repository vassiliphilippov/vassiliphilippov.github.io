insp

noun
inhalation abbreviation
Pronunciation: / ɪnsp / (AmE), / ɪnsp / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'inspiration', meaning the act of inhaling or taking air into the lungs

Examples

Insp value reading
The doctor noted the patient's insp and exp values during the examination.
On the chart, insp refers to the phase when the patient inhales.
Nurses monitor both insp and exp rates to assess respiratory health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expiration - the act of breathing out air from the lungs
  • synonym: inspiration - the act of drawing air into the lungs
