rosily

adverb
optimistically
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzɪli / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzɪli / (BrE)
in an optimistic or hopeful manner, suggesting a positive outcome

Examples

Describe rosily
He described their chances of winning the contract rosily, despite the tough competition.
The report painted the future of the industry rosily, focusing on growth and innovation.
Even after the setback, she continued to speak rosily about their prospects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimistically - in a hopeful or positive way
  • antonym: pessimistically - in a negative or hopeless way
