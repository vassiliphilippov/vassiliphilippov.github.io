Julia Ward Howe

proper noun
American feminist and women's rights activist
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒuː.lɪ.ə wɔːrd haʊ/ /; / /ˈdʒuːl.jə wɔːrd haʊ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒuː.lɪ.ə wɔːd haʊ/ /; / /ˈdʒuːl.jə wɔːd haʊ/ (BrE)
(1819–1910) An American feminist, writer, and social reformer known for her work in the women's suffrage movement and for writing 'The Battle Hymn of the Republic.'
