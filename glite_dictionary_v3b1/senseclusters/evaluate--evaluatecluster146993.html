evaluate

verb
form a critical opinion or judgment
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæljuˌeɪt /; / ɪˈvæl.ju.eɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈval.ju.eɪt /; / ɪˈvæl.ju.eɪt / (BrE)
to judge or assess the value, quality, or significance of something

Examples

To evaluate the results

The committee will evaluate each application before making a decision.
She evaluated the quality of the painting before making an offer.
Teachers often evaluate students' progress using a variety of methods.
The board will evaluate each proposal before making a decision.
