subfossil

adjective
partially fossilized
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbˈfɑsəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈfɒsəl / (BrE)
partially fossilized and still retaining some organic material, not fully turned into stone

Examples

Subfossil bone fragment

The cave contained subfossil remains of animals that lived thousands of years ago.
