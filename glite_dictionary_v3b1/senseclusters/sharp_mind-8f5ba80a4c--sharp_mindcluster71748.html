sharp mind

noun
intelligent, quick-witted person
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp maɪnd / (AmE), / ʃɑːp maɪnd / (BrE)
an intelligent and quick-witted person; the quality of thinking quickly and clearly

Examples

A sharp mind
During the interview, his sharp mind impressed everyone at the table.
Even in old age, he retained a sharp mind and a keen sense of humor.
Her sharp mind allowed her to excel in competitive chess tournaments.
