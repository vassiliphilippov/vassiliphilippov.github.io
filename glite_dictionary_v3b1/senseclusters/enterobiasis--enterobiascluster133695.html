enterobiasis

noun
pinworm infection
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.tə.roʊˈbaɪ.əsɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛn.tərəʊˈbaɪ.əsɪs / (BrE)
An infestation or infection caused by the pinworm Enterobius vermicularis, especially common in children.

Examples

Enterobiasis infection
Most cases of enterobiasis occur in children and are easily treated with medication.
Public health officials monitor schools for outbreaks of enterobiasis to prevent further spread.
The symptoms of enterobiasis often include itching around the anus, especially at night.
