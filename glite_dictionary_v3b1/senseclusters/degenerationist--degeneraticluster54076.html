degenerationist

adjective
supporting decline theory
9.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / dɪˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the theory that a society, group, or organism is in decline or deteriorating.

Examples

Degenerationist theory
The historian took a degenerationist view of the empire's final years.
Some critics labeled the novel's themes as overly degenerationist and negative.
His degenerationist arguments were challenged by those who believed in social progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pessimistic - expecting the worst or believing things are getting worse
  • antonym: progressive - favoring or promoting progress and improvement
