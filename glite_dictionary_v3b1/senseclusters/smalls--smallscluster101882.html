smalls

noun
underwear; small items
Pronunciation: / smɔlz / (AmE), / smɔːlz / (BrE)
informal term for small personal items, especially underwear or sometimes small coins

Examples

Wash smalls separately
She always washes her smalls separately from the rest of her laundry.
He emptied his pockets and found only a few smalls left for the bus fare.
When traveling, it's helpful to pack your smalls in a separate pouch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: change - coins of low value
  • synonym: underwear - clothes worn next to the skin under other clothes
