autoharp

noun
chorded string instrument
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.toʊ.hɑːrp/ /; / /ˈɔː.toʊ.hɑːrp/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.təʊ.hɑːp/ (BrE)
