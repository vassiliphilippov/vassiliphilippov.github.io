autoharp

noun
chorded string instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːtoʊˌhɑːrp / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌhɑːp / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument with buttons that mute certain strings to form chords when strummed or plucked

Examples

Play the autoharp
She learned to play folk songs on her autoharp in just a few weeks.
The musician brought an autoharp to the concert, adding a unique sound to the performance.
He strummed the autoharp while singing a traditional ballad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guitar - a stringed instrument played by plucking or strumming
  • synonym: zither - a stringed instrument with a flat body and many strings
