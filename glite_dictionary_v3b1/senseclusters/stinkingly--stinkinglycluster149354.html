stinkingly

adverb
very; extremely (informal)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
used informally as an intensifier meaning very or extremely, especially before adjectives.

Examples

Stinkingly rich
He was stinkingly rich and never worried about money.
The soup was stinkingly hot, so I had to wait before eating.
She found the exam stinkingly difficult despite all her preparation.
