bash

verb
hit something with force
5.5
Pronunciation: / bæʃ / (AmE), / bæʃ / (BrE)
to strike something or someone with great force; to hit hard

Examples

To bash the door
He bashed his elbow against the doorframe and winced in pain.
If you bash that computer any harder, you'll break it.
The two wrestlers bashed each other with steel chairs during the match.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: pummel - to strike repeatedly, typically with fists
  • synonym: smash - to break or hit forcibly and noisily
  • synonym: strike - to hit forcefully
  • degree lower: tap - to strike lightly
