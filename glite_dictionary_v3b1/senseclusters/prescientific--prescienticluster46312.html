prescientific

adjective
before modern science
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsaɪənˈtɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːsaɪənˈtɪfɪk / (BrE)
relating to a period, ideas, or methods that existed before the development of modern science

Examples

A prescientific period
Many prescientific explanations for disease involved supernatural causes rather than germs.
The book explores prescientific methods of navigation used by ancient sailors.
Some prescientific beliefs about the universe persisted for centuries before being disproved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preindustrial - relating to the time before industrialization
  • antonym: scientific - relating to or based on science
