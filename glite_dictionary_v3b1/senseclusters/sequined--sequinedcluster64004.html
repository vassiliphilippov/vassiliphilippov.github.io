sequined

adjective
decorated with sequins
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkwɪnd / (AmE), / ˈsiːkwɪnd / (BrE)
decorated or covered with sequins, which are small, shiny, disk-shaped beads sewn onto fabric for decoration

Examples

Sequined dress
Her bag, sequined and colorful, caught everyone's attention at the party.
She wore a stunning sequined dress that sparkled under the lights.
The sequined costumes made the performers stand out on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - not decorated or adorned
