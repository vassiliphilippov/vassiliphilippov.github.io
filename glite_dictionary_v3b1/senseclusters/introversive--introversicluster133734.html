introversive

adjective
having inward-focused thoughts or feelings
6.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈvɝː.sɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈvɜː.sɪv / (BrE)
Characterized by inward direction of thought, interest, or feeling toward oneself rather than toward external objects or people.

Examples

Introversive personality traits
Her introversive tendencies made her prefer journaling and quiet reflection over large social gatherings.
Psychologists often distinguish between introversive and extroversive personality traits in their research.
The artist's introversive style was evident in his deeply personal and introspective paintings.
