applaud

verb
express approval or praise
3.7
Pronunciation: / əˈplɔd / (AmE), / əˈplɔːd / (BrE)
to express approval, praise, or admiration for someone or something, especially publicly

Examples

To applaud good work
I must applaud your dedication to helping others in your community.
Many experts applaud the new policy for its positive environmental impact.
The critics applauded the author’s courage in telling her story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: criticize - to express disapproval of someone or something
  • synonym: praise - express strong approval or admiration
