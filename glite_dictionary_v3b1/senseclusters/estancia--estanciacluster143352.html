estancia

noun
temporary stay
Pronunciation: / ɛˈstɑːnsiə / (AmE), / ɛˈstænsiə / (BrE)
a period of staying in a place, especially as a temporary residence or visit

Examples

Her estancia abroad
Her estancia in Argentina lasted for six months while she studied Spanish.
During his estancia in Paris, he visited many famous museums and landmarks.
The writer described his estancia at the mountain retreat as peaceful and inspiring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: residence - the act of living in a place for some time
  • synonym: sojourn - a temporary stay in a place
  • synonym: visit - a short stay at a place for a particular purpose
