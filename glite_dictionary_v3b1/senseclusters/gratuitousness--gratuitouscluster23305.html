gratuitousness

noun
state of being free
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈtuːɪtəsnəs / (AmE), / ɡrəˈtjuːɪtəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being given or done without charge, payment, or necessity

Examples

Gratuitousness of a gift
The gratuitousness of the meal surprised everyone, as no one expected it to be free.
She appreciated the gratuitousness of the advice, which was offered without any expectation of reward.
Many guests commented on the gratuitousness of the hotel's welcome gifts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generosity - the quality of being kind and giving
  • antonym: necessity - the state of being needed or required
