braid

verb
decorate with narrow bands or ribbons
7.2
Pronunciation: / breɪd / (AmE), / breɪd / (BrE)
to decorate a piece of clothing or fabric with narrow bands, ribbons, or braided trims

Examples

To braid a dress
She learned how to braid curtains with decorative cords for a traditional look.
The seamstress decided to braid the cuffs of the jacket with gold ribbon.
They braided the collar of the uniform to give it a more formal appearance.
