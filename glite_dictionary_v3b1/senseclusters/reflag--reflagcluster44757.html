reflag

verb
to reset status flag
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈflæɡ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈflæɡ / (BrE)
to change or reset the value of a flag or status marker in a computer system, file, or dataset

Examples

To reflag a file
The technician needed to reflag the files after the system update changed their status.
If you find incorrect data, please reflag the affected entries for review.
After fixing the bug, the developer had to reflag several records in the database.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flag - to mark or indicate something with a flag or status marker
  • synonym: reset - to set something back to its original state
