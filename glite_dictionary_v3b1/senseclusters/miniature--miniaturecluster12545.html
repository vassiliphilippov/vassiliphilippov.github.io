miniature

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.niː.ə.tʃər/ /; / /ˈmɪ.nɪ.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.nɪ.tʃə/ /; / /ˈmɪ.nə.tʃə/ (BrE)

Examples

A miniature car model

She collects miniatures of famous landmarks from around the world.
