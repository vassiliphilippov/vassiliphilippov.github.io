fluorescent

adjective
emitting light under radiation
3.7 4.6
Pronunciation: /flʊ.ˈres.ənt/ /; / /flɔː.ˈres.ənt/ (AmE), /flʊə.ˈres.ənt/ (BrE)
emitting visible light when exposed to radiation from an external source, such as ultraviolet light

Examples

Fluorescent minerals glow
Some minerals are naturally fluorescent and glow brightly under ultraviolet light.
The jellyfish appeared fluorescent under the blue lights of the aquarium.
They used fluorescent markers to stain the cells for microscopic examination.
