threefold

adjective
having three parts or aspects
Pronunciation: / ˈθriˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈθriːfəʊld / (BrE)
having three parts, elements, or aspects; triple in character

Examples

Our responsibilities are threefold, covering safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
The committee's mission is threefold: research, implementation, and review.
The problem has a threefold nature involving social, economic, and political elements.
