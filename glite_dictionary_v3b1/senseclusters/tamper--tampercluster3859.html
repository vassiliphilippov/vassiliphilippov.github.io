tamper

noun
tool for pressing down material
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmpər / (AmE), / ˈtæmpə / (BrE)
a tool used for pressing down material, such as tobacco in a pipe bowl or charges in a drill hole.

Examples

A soil tamper
Gardeners sometimes employ a hand tamper to compact soil evenly.
He used a tamper to press the tobacco firmly into the pipe.
The explosives expert reached for his tamper before sealing the drill hole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tamping iron - an iron tool for tamping explosive charges in drill holes
