draw

noun
type of poker game
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
A form of poker in which players may discard unwanted cards and receive replacements from the dealer.

Examples

Five-card draw
He prefers to play draw rather than stud poker at the casino.
Many beginners start with five-card draw because of its simple rules.
The tournament featured several rounds of draw, followed by a final stud game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stud - a type of poker in which some cards are dealt face up and others face down
