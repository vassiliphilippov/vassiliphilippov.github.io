orthoptist

noun
professional treating eye movement disorders
5.7 9.3
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑptɪst / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒptɪst / (BrE)
A medical professional trained to diagnose and treat defects in eye movement and problems with binocular vision.

Examples

Orthoptist eye specialist
After experiencing double vision, she scheduled an appointment with a orthoptist.
The orthoptist worked closely with the ophthalmologist to monitor the child's strabismus.
The hospital hired a new orthoptist to help manage patients with eye coordination problems.
