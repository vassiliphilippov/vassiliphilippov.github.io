firefighter

noun
person who fights fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚˌfaɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌfaɪtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to put out fires and rescue people from dangerous situations

Examples

A firefighter puts out fires
After years of training, she finally became a qualified firefighter.
Children love visiting the station and meeting a real firefighter.
The firefighter rushed into the burning building to save the tenants.
After the earthquake, firefighters worked nonstop to rescue people from the rubble.
Becoming a firefighter requires physical fitness, courage, and extensive training.
The firefighter bravely entered the burning building to save the family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireman - a person whose job is to extinguish fires (now often used as a synonym but less gender-neutral)
  • antonym: police officer - a member of the police force, responsible for maintaining law and order
