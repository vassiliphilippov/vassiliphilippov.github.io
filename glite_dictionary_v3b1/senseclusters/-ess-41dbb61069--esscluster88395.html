-ess

x
female suffix
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɛs / (AmE), / ɛs / (BrE)
a suffix added to nouns to indicate a female person or animal

Examples

The suffix -ess
The word 'waitress' uses the suffix -ess to show a female server.
In 'lioness', the -ess ending indicates a female lion.
Many older English words use -ess to mark the feminine form, such as 'princess'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -ette - a suffix sometimes used to form feminine nouns
