kneeling

noun
the act of being on one's knees
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈniːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈniːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or position of being on one's knees, especially as a sign of respect, worship, or submission

Examples

A moment of kneeling
During the ceremony, the crowd observed a solemn moment of kneeling in remembrance of the fallen.
His kneeling before the altar was a gesture of deep devotion.
The monk spent hours in kneeling meditation each day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prostration - the act of lying stretched out on the ground, often as an act of submission or worship
