limitedly

adverb
in a restricted way
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a limited or restricted way; not extensively or fully

Examples

Only limitedly used
The new law was only limitedly enforced in rural areas.
Her knowledge of French is limitedly useful for this project.
The medicine was limitedly available during the shortage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extensively - in a thorough or wide-ranging way
  • synonym: partially - to some extent but not completely
