maple family

noun
family of trees and shrubs including maples
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪpəl ˌfæməli / (AmE), / ˈmeɪpəl ˌfæmɪli / (BrE)
a family of trees and shrubs (Aceraceae) in the order Sapindales, including the maples and their close relatives
