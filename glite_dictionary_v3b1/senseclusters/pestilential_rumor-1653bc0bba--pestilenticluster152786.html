pestilential rumor

noun
highly harmful or damaging rumor
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛstəˈlɛnʃəl ˈruːmɚ / (AmE), / ˌpɛstɪˈlɛnʃəl ˈruːmə / (BrE)
a rumor that is highly damaging, harmful, or likely to spread harm or discord

Examples

A pestilential rumor
Despite her efforts to clarify the truth, a pestilential rumor continued to haunt her career.
Nothing was more damaging than a pestilential rumor whispered in the corridors of power.
The pestilential rumor quickly spread throughout the small town, sowing distrust among neighbors.
