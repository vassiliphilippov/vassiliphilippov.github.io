daybed

noun
an armless couch that doubles as a bed
3.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.bed/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.bed/ (BrE)
a piece of furniture like a couch or sofa that can be used for sitting or reclining during the day and as a bed at night
