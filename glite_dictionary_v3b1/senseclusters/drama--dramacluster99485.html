drama

noun
a play intended for stage performance
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑː.mə / (AmE), / ˈdrɑː.mə / (BrE)
a play or theatrical work, or the genre of plays performed by actors on stage

Examples

A drama on stage
His first drama was performed on Broadway and received critical acclaim.
She has always loved reading and performing in drama since high school.
The local theatre is staging a modern drama about family relationships.
Shakespeare's most famous drama is often considered to be Hamlet.
She prefers reading drama to novels or poetry.
The school is putting on a modern drama about teenage life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comedy - a humorous play intended to entertain
  • degree lower: farce - a light, humorous dramatic work with exaggerated situations
  • synonym: play - a literary work written to be performed by actors
  • antonym: tragedy - a play dealing with tragic events and having an unhappy ending
