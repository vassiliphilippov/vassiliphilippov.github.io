drama

noun
a play intended for stage performance
1.8 14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààdr…ëÀê.m…ô / (AmE), / Ààdr…ëÀê.m…ô / (BrE)
a play or theatrical work, or the genre of plays performed by actors on stage

Examples

A drama on stage

Synonyms

  • dramatic play
    4.6 0.06/m
    a dramatic play is a script made for live stage performance, while drama refers to the larger genre of those staged works.
  • dramatic work
    4.1 0.11/m
    a dramatic work is any script for stage, television, or film, while drama usually means stage plays and their genre.
  • dramatic composition
    6.5 0.01/m

  • dramatic art
    4.4 0.06/m
    a dramatic art is the craft of writing and acting for the theatre, while drama is the plays and the stage genre made by that craft.
