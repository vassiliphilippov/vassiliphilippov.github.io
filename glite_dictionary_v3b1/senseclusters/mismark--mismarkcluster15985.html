mismark

verb
to label incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈmɑrk / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈmɑːk / (BrE)
to mark something incorrectly or with the wrong label

Examples

To mismark a package
The shipment was mismarked, causing confusion at the warehouse.
If you mismark the test papers, students may receive the wrong grades.
Several products were mismarked and placed on the wrong shelf.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mark - to make a visible sign or label on something
  • synonym: mislabel - to attach the wrong label to something
