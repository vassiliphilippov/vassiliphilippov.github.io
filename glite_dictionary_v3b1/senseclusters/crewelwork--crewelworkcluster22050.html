crewelwork

noun
embroidery with worsted wool yarn
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːəlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈkruːəlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
embroidery made using loosely twisted worsted wool yarn, often featuring elaborate designs.

Examples

Crewelwork embroidery patterns
She learned to stitch crewelwork patterns during her textile art course.
The antique cushion was decorated with beautiful crewelwork featuring flowers and birds.
Traditional crewelwork uses wool yarns to create raised, textured designs on linen.
