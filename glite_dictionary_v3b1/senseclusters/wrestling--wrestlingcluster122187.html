wrestling

noun
close hand-to-hand combat
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛs.lɪŋ /; / ˈrɛslɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛs.lɪŋ /; / ˈrɛslɪŋ / (BrE)
a sport or activity where two people fight by holding, pushing, or throwing each other, trying to force their opponent’s shoulders to the ground

Examples

The Olympic Games include several styles of wrestling in their competitions.
