African love grass

noun
a perennial South African grass species
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪkən ˈlʌv ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˌaf.rɪ.kən ˈlʌv ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a perennial grass native to South Africa, with densely clumped flimsy stems, often introduced elsewhere for erosion control
