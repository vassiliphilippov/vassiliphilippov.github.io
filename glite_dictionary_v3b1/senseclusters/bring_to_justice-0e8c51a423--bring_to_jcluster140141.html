bring to justice

verb
hold accountable or punish for wrongdoing
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌs.tɪs/ /; / /brɪŋ tuː ˈdʒʌs.tɪs/ /; / /brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌs.təs/ (AmE), /brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌs.tɪs/ /; / /brɪŋ tə ˈdʒʌs.təs/ /; / /brɪŋ tuː ˈdʒʌs.tɪs/ (BrE)
to make sure someone who committed a crime is found and punished by the law

Examples

  • synonym: punish - to subject someone to a penalty for an offense
