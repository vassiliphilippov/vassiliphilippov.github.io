hypostatically

adverb
relating to substance
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˈstætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈstætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the underlying reality or substance, especially in theological contexts

Examples

Hypostatically united natures
The theologian described the union of Christ's natures hypostatically in his lecture.
In some doctrines, the divine and human are joined hypostatically rather than merely symbolically.
The concept was explained hypostatically to clarify its deeper philosophical meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: substantially - in a way that relates to substance or essence
