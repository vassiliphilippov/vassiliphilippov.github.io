Baathist

adjective
of the Baath party
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːθɪst / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθɪst / (BrE)
relating to the Baath party, its ideology, policies, or members, especially in Syria or Iraq

Examples

Baathist government
The new government implemented several Baathist reforms after coming to power.
Many officials in the region were loyal to the Baathist ideology.
International observers criticized the Baathist policies for limiting freedom of speech and political opposition.
