citizenry

noun
the citizens of a country or state
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪt̬.ə.zən.ri / (AmE), / ˈsɪt.ɪ.zən.ri / (BrE)
all the people who live in a particular country or state and have the legal rights and responsibilities of citizenship; the body of citizens collectively.

Examples

The citizenry of a country
A well-educated citizenry is vital to the health of a democracy.
The citizenry voted overwhelmingly in favor of the new policy.
The mayor addressed the citizenry in his weekly broadcast.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreigner - a person who is not a citizen of the country in question
  • synonym: population - all the inhabitants of a particular country or area
