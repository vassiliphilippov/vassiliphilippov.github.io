sacrotuberous

adjective
of sacrum and ischium
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌseɪkroʊˈtuːbərəs / (AmE), / ˌsakrəʊˈtjuːbərəs / (BrE)
relating to or connecting the sacrum and the ischial tuberosity in the pelvis

Examples

Sacrotuberous ligament
The sacrotuberous ligament is important for stabilizing the pelvis during movement.
Injury to the sacrotuberous ligament can cause pain in the lower back and hip.
The surgeon carefully examined the sacrotuberous area before proceeding with the operation.
