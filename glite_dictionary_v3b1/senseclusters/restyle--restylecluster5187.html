restyle

noun
design update
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈriːstaɪl / (AmE), / ˈriːstaɪl / (BrE)
an act of changing the design or appearance of something, especially as a cosmetic update or revision.

Examples

A restyle of a phone
The latest restyle of the smartphone features a slimmer body and a new color option.
After the restyle, the restaurant attracted more customers with its modern look.
The car manufacturer announced a restyle for its popular sedan, updating the front and rear design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: makeover - a complete transformation or remodeling of appearance
  • antonym: original - the first or initial version before any changes
  • synonym: redesign - an act of designing something again or differently
