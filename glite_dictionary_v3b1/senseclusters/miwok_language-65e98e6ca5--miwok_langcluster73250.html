Miwok language

noun
Penutian language of the Miwok people
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːˌwɑk ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈmiːˌwɒk ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language of the Miwok people, belonging to the Penutian language family and traditionally spoken in central California

Examples

The Miwok language
A dictionary of the Miwok language was published to help preserve traditional stories.
Many elders still speak the Miwok language and are working to teach it to younger generations.
Research into the Miwok language has provided insight into the history of central California.
