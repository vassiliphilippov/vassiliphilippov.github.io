bivouac

noun
temporary camping site
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪvuˌæk / (AmE), / ˈbɪvuæk /; / ˈbɪv.u.æk / (BrE)
a temporary camp or shelter, especially outdoors, used by travelers, soldiers, or mountaineers

Examples

A mountain bivouac
After a long hike, the group set up their bivouac in a clearing in the woods.
The weather was kind, making the bivouac a pleasant and memorable experience.
We found a perfect bivouac near the lake for our overnight stay.
After a long day's climb, the team established a bivouac on the mountain slope.
The refugees constructed a makeshift bivouac using tarpaulins and sticks.
The soldiers set up a bivouac in the forest before nightfall.
