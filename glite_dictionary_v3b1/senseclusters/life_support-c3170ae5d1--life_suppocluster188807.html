life support

noun
essential assistance for survival or well-being
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf səˌpɔːt / (BrE)
assistance that is essential for someone's survival or well-being, especially in difficult circumstances; often used figuratively to refer to crucial emotional or practical help.

Examples

Emotional life support
After losing his job, his friends became his life support system.
During her illness, her family acted as emotional life support.
Her mentorship was the life support I needed to rebuild my confidence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lifeline - something that provides help or protection in a difficult situation
  • synonym: support - practical or emotional help
