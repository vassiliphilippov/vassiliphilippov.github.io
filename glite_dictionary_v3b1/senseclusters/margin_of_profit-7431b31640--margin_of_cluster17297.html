margin of profit

noun
percentage of profit from sales
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒən əv ˈprɑfɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪn əv ˈprɒfɪt / (BrE)
The amount by which revenue from sales exceeds costs, expressed as a percentage of net sales.

Examples

The margin of profit percentage
Even a small increase in sales can significantly boost the margin of profit.
Retailers often aim for a high margin of profit to maximize their earnings.
The company improved its margin of profit by cutting manufacturing expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profit margin - percentage of sales that is profit; same financial concept
