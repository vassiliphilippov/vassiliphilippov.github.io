ashrama

noun
Hindu life stage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːʃrəmə / (AmE), / ˈɑːʃrəmə / (BrE)
one of the four traditional stages of life in classical Hinduism, such as student, householder, forest-dweller, or renunciate

Examples

Life's ashrama stages
In Hindu tradition, each ashrama represents a different phase of a person's life.
The concept of ashrama guides individuals through spiritual and social responsibilities.
He entered the third ashrama, choosing a life of contemplation in the forest.
