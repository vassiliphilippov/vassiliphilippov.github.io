carpet pad

noun
a layer under a carpet for cushioning or support
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːr.pət pæd / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.pɪt pæd / (BrE)
A layer of material placed under a carpet to provide cushioning, insulation, or support.

Examples

A carpet pad layer
Replacing the carpet pad extended the life of the living room carpet.
The carpet pad helps reduce noise and insulate the floor.
We installed a thick carpet pad to make the new carpet feel extra soft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underlay - a material laid under carpet for insulation or cushioning; chiefly British English
