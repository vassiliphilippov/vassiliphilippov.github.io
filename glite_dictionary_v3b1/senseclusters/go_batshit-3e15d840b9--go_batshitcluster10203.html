go batshit

verb
become extremely angry or crazy
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ˈbæt.ʃɪt / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ˈbæt.ʃɪt / (BrE)
to become extremely angry, irrational, excited, or crazy

Examples

To go batshit angry
If you try to mess with her stuff, she'll go batshit for sure.
The crowd went batshit when the band appeared on stage.
When the boss heard about the mistake, he went batshit and started yelling at everyone.
