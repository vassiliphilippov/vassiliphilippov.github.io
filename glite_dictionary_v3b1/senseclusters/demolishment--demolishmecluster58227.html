demolishment

noun
complete destruction
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑlɪʃmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒlɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of completely destroying or ruining something, not limited to buildings

Examples

Complete demolishment of plans
The sudden demolishment of the old stadium surprised many local residents.
Years of neglect led to the demolishment of the once-beautiful theater.
The company faced demolishment after a series of financial scandals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: construction - the act of building or creating something
  • synonym: destruction - the act of causing so much damage that something no longer exists
  • synonym: ruin - the state of being destroyed or severely damaged
