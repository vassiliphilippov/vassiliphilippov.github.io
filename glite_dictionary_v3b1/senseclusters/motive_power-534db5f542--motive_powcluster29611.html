motive power

noun
energy or force causing motion
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.tɪv ˌpaʊ.ər /; / ˈmoʊtɪv ˈpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tɪv ˌpaʊ.ə /; / ˈməʊtɪv ˈpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
