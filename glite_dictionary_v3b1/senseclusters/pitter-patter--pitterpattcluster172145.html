Examples

The pitter-patter of rain
He smiled when he heard the pitter-patter of his dog's paws racing across the floor.
The pitter-patter of children running through the hallway filled the house with joy.
The pitter-patter of rain on the window soothed her to sleep.

Synonyms

  • pit-a-pat
    8.9 0.01/m
    pit-a-pat is a quick, light, repeated sound used for heartbeats or footsteps, while pitter-patter describes many small taps like rain.
  • pitter
    6.3 0.28/m
    pitter is a single soft, repeated sound heard from rain or small feet, while pitter-patter refers to a series of many quick taps.
