fieldwork

noun
temporary military fortification
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːldˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈfiːldˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a temporary fortification constructed by troops in the field, especially during military operations

Examples

Military fieldwork
During the siege, several fieldworks were erected to guard the supply lines.
General Smith ordered the men to reinforce the existing fieldwork before nightfall.
The soldiers hastily constructed a fieldwork to protect themselves from enemy fire.
