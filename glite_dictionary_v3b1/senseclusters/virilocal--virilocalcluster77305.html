virilocal

adjective
living with husband's family
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪrəˈloʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌvɪrɪˈləʊkəl / (BrE)
describing a social system in which a married couple lives with or near the husband's family

Examples

A virilocal family
In some traditional societies, families follow a virilocal pattern after marriage.
Anthropologists study how virilocal residence affects family relationships and inheritance.
A virilocal arrangement often means the bride moves into her husband's family home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matrilocal - describing residence with or near the wife's family after marriage
  • synonym: patrilocal - describing residence with or near the husband's family after marriage
