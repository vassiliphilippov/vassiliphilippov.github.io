disreputable

adjective
having a bad reputation
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsˈrɛpjətəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsˈrɛpjʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Not considered to be respectable in character, appearance, or behavior; having a bad reputation.

Examples

A disreputable person
His disreputable appearance made it hard for him to find a job.
She avoided making deals with any disreputable companies.
The police warned us about the disreputable bar at the end of the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notorious - famous or well-known for some bad quality or deed
  • antonym: respectable - regarded by society to be good, proper, or correct
