reverse gear

noun
gear for moving backwards
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɜrs ɡɪr / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs ɡɪə / (BrE)
a gear in a vehicle or machine that enables it to move backwards

Examples

Put reverse gear on
Put the car into reverse gear before backing out of the driveway.
She accidentally shifted into reverse gear instead of drive.
The old tractor's reverse gear sometimes gets stuck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drive - the gear in a vehicle that moves it forward
