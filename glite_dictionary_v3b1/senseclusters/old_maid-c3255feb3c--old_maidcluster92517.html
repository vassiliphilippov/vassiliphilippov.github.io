old maid

noun
elderly unmarried woman (outdated, sometimes derogatory)
4.1 7.1
Pronunciation: /oʊld meɪd/ (AmE), /əʊld meɪd/ (BrE)
an older woman who has never married, often with a negative or old-fashioned meaning

Examples

The term old maid is rarely used today and is considered outdated and offensive by many.
