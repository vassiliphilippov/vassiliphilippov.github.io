shaft encoder

noun
device for measuring shaft position or motion
9.2 6.4
Pronunciation: /ʃæft ɪn.ˈkoʊ.dər/ /; / /ʃæft ɛn.ˈkoʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ʃɑːft ɪn.ˈkəʊ.də/ /; / /ʃɑːft ɛn.ˈkəʊ.də/ (BrE)
an electro-mechanical device that converts the angular position or motion of a shaft or axle into an analog or digital code

Examples

A shaft encoder device
Engineers installed a shaft encoder to monitor the rotation of the conveyor belt.
If the shaft encoder fails, the system may lose track of the motor’s position.
The shaft encoder provided real-time position feedback to the robotic arm.
