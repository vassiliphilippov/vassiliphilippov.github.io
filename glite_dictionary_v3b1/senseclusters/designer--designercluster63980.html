designer

noun
person who devises secret plots
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə / (BrE)
a person who devises secret plans or schemes, often with a negative or conspiratorial purpose

Examples

Plot designer
Authorities arrested the alleged designer of the plot before it could be carried out.
She acted as the designer behind the elaborate scheme to defraud investors.
The investigation revealed that he was the chief designer of the complex financial scam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conspirator - person who takes part in a secret plan to do something unlawful or harmful
  • synonym: plotter - person who secretly plans something, especially something illegal or harmful
  • antonym: victim - person harmed by a plot or scheme
