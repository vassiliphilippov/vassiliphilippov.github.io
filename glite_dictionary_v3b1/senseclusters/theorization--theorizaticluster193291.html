theorization

noun
the process of creating or applying theories
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˌθiəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌθɪəraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌθɪərəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or act of forming, developing, applying, or using theories, especially in a formal or systematic way, to explain or understand something
