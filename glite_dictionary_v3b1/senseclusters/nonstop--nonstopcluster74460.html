nonstop

noun
direct flight with no stops
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌstɑp / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌstɒp / (BrE)
a flight or journey made without any stops between the starting point and the final destination

Examples

A nonstop flight
Are there any nonstops available to San Francisco tomorrow?
I prefer to book a nonstop whenever I fly cross-country.
The airline added two new nonstops to Florida this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: direct - a flight that goes straight to its destination, sometimes with stops but no change of planes
