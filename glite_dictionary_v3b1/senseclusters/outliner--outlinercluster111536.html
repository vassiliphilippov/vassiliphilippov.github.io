outliner

noun
tool for outlining
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a pen, marker, or tool used to draw or emphasize the outer lines of a shape or image

Examples

Black outliner pen
She used a black outliner to make the edges of her drawing stand out.
Before coloring, he traced the shapes with an outliner for a cleaner look.
The art teacher recommended using an outliner to define the main features of the sketch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eraser - a tool used to remove pencil or pen marks
  • synonym: highlighter - a pen used to emphasize text with translucent color
  • synonym: marker - a pen with a broad tip for writing or drawing
