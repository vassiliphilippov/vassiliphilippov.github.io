unrisen

adjective
not increased in volume
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪzən / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪzən / (BrE)
not having risen, especially referring to dough or bread that has not increased in volume as expected

Examples

Unrisen dough
The unrisen dough was left on the counter overnight.
If the yeast is old, your bread may remain unrisen after hours of waiting.
She was disappointed to find the cake batter still unrisen in the oven.

Related Terms

  • antonym: risen - having increased in volume, especially of dough or bread
