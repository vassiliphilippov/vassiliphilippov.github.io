pixelated

adjective
blocky image quality
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪksəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪksɪleɪtɪd /; / ˈpɪksəleɪtɪd / (BrE)
appearing blocky or made up of visible pixels, usually because of low resolution or technical problems

Examples

Pixelated photo
The enlarged photo became pixelated and lost its original detail.
When the internet connection is slow, videos often appear pixelated on the screen.
He used a filter to make his profile picture look intentionally pixelated for a retro effect.
The video call became pixelated when the internet connection was weak.
After zooming in, the picture looked pixelated and hard to recognize.
Old computer games often have pixelated graphics compared to modern ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blurry - not clear or sharp in appearance
  • antonym: clear - easy to see or distinguish; sharp
