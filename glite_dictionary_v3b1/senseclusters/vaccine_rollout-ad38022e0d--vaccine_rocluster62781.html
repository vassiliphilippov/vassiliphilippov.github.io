vaccine rollout

noun
distribution and administration of vaccines
1.8
Pronunciation: / vækˈsiːn ˈroʊˌlaʊt / (AmE), / vækˈsiːn ˈrəʊˌlaʊt / (BrE)
the organized distribution and administration of vaccines to a population, typically as part of a public health campaign

Examples

The vaccine rollout plan
Concerns were raised about the speed and efficiency of the vaccine rollout in rural areas.
Effective communication strategies greatly improved public participation in the vaccine rollout.
The vaccine rollout across the country helped reduce infection rates significantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immunization campaign - organized effort to immunize a population against disease
  • synonym: mass vaccination - widespread administration of vaccines to large numbers of people
