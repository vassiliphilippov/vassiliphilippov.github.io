curve

verb
slang: reject or snub someone
Pronunciation: / kɜrv / (AmE), / kɜːv / (BrE)
(slang) to deliberately reject or ignore someone’s romantic or social advances; to snub someone, often in dating contexts.

Examples

To curve a person
After a few texts, he realised she'd curved him and stopped replying politely.
She tried to flirt, but he was clearly curving her in front of his friends.
People say it's rude to openly curve someone at a party without explanation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accept - to agree to receive someone's advances or invitation; opposite of curve (in this sense)
  • synonym: reject - to refuse the advances or offer of someone
  • synonym: snub - to ignore or treat someone coldly or with contempt
