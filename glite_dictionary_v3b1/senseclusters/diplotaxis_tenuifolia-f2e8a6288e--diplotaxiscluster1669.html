Diplotaxis tenuifolia

noun
yellow-flowered European plant; wild rocket
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪpləˈtæksɪs ˌtɛnjuɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈtæksɪs ˌtɛnjʊɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered European plant, commonly known as wild rocket, that grows on old walls and in waste places and is considered a weed in parts of North America.

Examples

Diplotaxis tenuifolia plant
Botanists conducted a survey of Diplotaxis tenuifolia growing on the ancient city walls.
In some regions, Diplotaxis tenuifolia has become a troublesome invasive plant along roadsides and in gardens.
The fresh leaves of Diplotaxis tenuifolia are often added to salads for their peppery flavor.
