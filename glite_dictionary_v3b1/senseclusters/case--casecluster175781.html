case

verb
to enclose in a case or container
Pronunciation: / keɪs / (AmE), / keɪs / (BrE)
to enclose something in a case or container, or as if in a case

Examples

To case books
Books were carefully cased in cardboard boxes for the move.
Delicate instruments must be cased securely to avoid damage during transport.
The jeweler will case each watch before shipping it to prevent scratches.
