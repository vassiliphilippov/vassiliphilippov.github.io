tempestuous weather

noun
stormy or violent weather conditions
Pronunciation: / tɛmˈpɛstʃuəs ˈwɛðər / (AmE), / tɛmˈpɛstjuːəs ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
very stormy or turbulent atmospheric conditions, often marked by strong winds, heavy rain, or violent storms.

Examples

Tempestuous weather with strong winds
Farmers worry that tempestuous weather could damage this year's crops.
The tempestuous weather battered the coastline for hours.
We stayed inside all day due to the tempestuous weather.
