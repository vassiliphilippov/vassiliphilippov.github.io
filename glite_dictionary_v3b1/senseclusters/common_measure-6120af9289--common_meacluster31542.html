common measure

noun
number dividing others exactly
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈmɛʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈmɛʒə / (BrE)
a number that divides two or more other numbers exactly, with no remainder

Examples

Common measure of two numbers
6 is a common measure of 12 and 18.
The students were asked to identify a common measure for the pair of numbers.
To simplify the fractions, find a common measure for the denominators.
