celeste

noun
keyboard percussion instrument
8.4
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈlest/ (AmE), /sə.ˈlest/ (BrE)
a small keyboard percussion instrument that produces a soft, bell-like sound, often used in orchestral and chamber music

Examples

Orchestra celeste
The delicate sound of the celeste added a magical quality to the orchestra's performance.
She learned to play the celeste for her role in the chamber music ensemble.
A famous use of the celeste is in Tchaikovsky's 'Dance of the Sugar Plum Fairy.'
