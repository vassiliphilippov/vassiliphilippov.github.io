hobbyist

noun
someone who enjoys a hobby for pleasure
3.5 1.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêbi…™st / (AmE), / Ààh…íb…™…™st / (BrE)
a person who pursues a particular activity or hobby for pleasure, not as a profession

Examples

A hobbyist painter
As a dedicated hobbyist, she spends her weekends painting landscapes and attending art workshops.
Many hobbyists share their knowledge and projects online through communities and forums.
Though John is a talented woodworker, he still considers himself a hobbyist rather than a professional.

Synonyms

  • amateur
    2.8 8.26/m
    an amateur takes part in an activity for nonprofessional reasons and may lack skill, while a hobbyist simply enjoys a pastime for pleasure.
  • enthusiast
    3.4 6.63/m
    an enthusiast is very passionate and invests time and energy, while a hobbyist takes part in a pastime mainly for simple personal pleasure.
  • fancier
    7.4 1.06/m
    a fancier has a special, often expert interest in a particular hobby or item, while a hobbyist enjoys a pastime without doing it professionally.
