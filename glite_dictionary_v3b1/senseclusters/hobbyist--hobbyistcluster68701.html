hobbyist

noun
someone who enjoys a hobby for pleasure
7.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.biː.ɪst/ /; / /ˈhɑː.bɪst/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.bɪst/ /; / /ˈhɒ.bɪ.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who pursues a particular activity or hobby for pleasure, not as a profession

Examples

A hobbyist painter
As a dedicated hobbyist, she spends her weekends painting landscapes and attending art workshops.
Many hobbyists share their knowledge and projects online through communities and forums.
Though John is a talented woodworker, he still considers himself a hobbyist rather than a professional.

Related Terms
