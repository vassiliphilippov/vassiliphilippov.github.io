breastband

noun
chest support band
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛstˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈbrɛstˌbænd / (BrE)
a band of fabric or elastic worn around the chest to support, compress, or shape the breasts, often used in garments or for medical support

Examples

Medical breastband
The doctor recommended wearing a breastband after the operation to aid recovery.
Athletes sometimes use a breastband for extra support during intense physical activity.
She adjusted her breastband to ensure it fit comfortably under her dress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binder - a tight garment used to compress the chest
  • synonym: bra - an undergarment worn to support the breasts
