hurdles

noun
footrace with barriers to jump
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝdəlz / (AmE), / ˈhɜːdlz / (BrE)
a race in which runners must jump over a series of barriers set at fixed distances

Examples

The hurdles race
During practice, he focused on improving his speed between the hurdles.
She won gold in the women's 100-meter hurdles at the Olympics.
The coach specializes in training athletes for hurdles and sprints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steeplechase - a race in which competitors must jump over obstacles including water jumps and fences
