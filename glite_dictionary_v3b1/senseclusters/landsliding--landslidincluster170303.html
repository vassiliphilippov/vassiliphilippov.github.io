Examples

Synonyms

  • landslide
    3.3 2.30/m
    landslide is the common noun for a large, fast fall of earth or a big election win, while landsliding names the action.
  • landslip
    7.5 0.00/m
    landslip is a sudden movement of earth down a mountain or cliff, while landsliding refers to that movement in general.
  • slide
    3.4 2.74/m
    slide is a general fall of earth, rock, or snow down a slope, while landsliding usually implies a large, destructive movement.
  • downslide
    6.5 0.00/m
    downslide is a sudden downward flow of rocks or debris, while landsliding often means the full event or process in geology or elections.
