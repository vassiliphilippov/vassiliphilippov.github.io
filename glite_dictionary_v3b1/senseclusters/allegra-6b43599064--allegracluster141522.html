Allegra

proper noun
allergy medication brand
4.8
Pronunciation: / əˈlɛɡrə / (AmE), / əˈlɛɡrə / (BrE)
a brand name for a medication used to relieve allergy symptoms such as sneezing, runny nose, and itchy eyes

Examples

Allegra allergy medicine

You should ask your pharmacist if Allegra is right for your allergy symptoms.
