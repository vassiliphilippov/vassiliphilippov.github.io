wisen

verb
to become wiser
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪzən / (AmE), / ˈwaɪzən / (BrE)
to make or become wiser or more knowledgeable through experience or learning

Examples

To wisen from experience
Traveling to new countries can wisen you in unexpected ways.
Over the years, difficult challenges have helped her wisen and grow.
Parents hope that their advice will wisen their children as they face life's choices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: educate - to give knowledge or skill to someone
  • synonym: enlighten - to give someone greater knowledge or understanding
  • antonym: fool - to make someone act unwisely or without knowledge
