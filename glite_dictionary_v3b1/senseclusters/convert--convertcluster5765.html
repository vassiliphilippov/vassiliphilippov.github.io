convert

verb
exchange or replace with another of the same category
3.7 22/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàv…ùÀêt / (AmE), / k…ônÀàv…úÀêt / (BrE)
to exchange or replace something with another thing of the same or a related kind, especially currency, units, or forms

Examples

To convert money
I asked the bank to convert my euros into dollars for my trip.
The company plans to convert its assets into shares for easier trading.
You need to convert these measurements from inches to centimeters before baking.

Synonyms

  • change
    1.0 22/m
    change covers many kinds of replacement or making something different in daily use, while convert focuses on changing form, units, or currency.
  • exchange
    1.8 20/m
    exchange is giving and receiving between two parties or swapping items, while convert often means changing something into a different form or unit.
  • interchange
    4.1 0.26/m
    interchange means things can be swapped or used in each other's place, while convert means changing one thing into a different form or unit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: change - to make someone or something become different; to exchange something for something else
  • synonym: exchange - to give something and receive something of the same kind in return
