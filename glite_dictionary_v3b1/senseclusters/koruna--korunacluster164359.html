koruna

noun
Slovakia's former currency unit
Pronunciation: / kɔːˈruːnə / (AmE), / kɔːˈruːnə / (BrE)
the former basic unit of currency in Slovakia, equal to 100 heller, replaced by the euro in 2009.

Examples

Slovak koruna notes
Prices in Slovakia used to be listed in koruna before the currency changed.
She found some old koruny in a drawer while cleaning.
The koruna was replaced by the euro in Slovakia in 2009.
