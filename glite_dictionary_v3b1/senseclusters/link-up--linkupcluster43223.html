link-up

noun
connection or meeting
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkˌʌp / (BrE)
a meeting, connection, or collaboration between people, groups, or systems for a common purpose

Examples

Business link-up
The two organizations held a link-up to discuss future projects.
A link-up between the schools allowed students to share resources.
The link-up of the computer systems improved communication across departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others
  • synonym: partnership - a relationship where people or groups work together
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving apart or ending a connection
