cooldown

noun
waiting period
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (AmE), /ˈkuːl.daʊn/ (BrE)
a period of enforced waiting before an action can be repeated, especially in games or technology

Examples

Game cooldown timer
After using the special attack, there is a cooldown before you can use it again.
The app has a five-minute cooldown to prevent users from sending too many requests.
Players must wait for the cooldown to finish before performing the action again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delay - a period of time when something is postponed
  • antonym: immediacy - the quality of happening without delay
  • synonym: pause - a temporary stop or break in activity
