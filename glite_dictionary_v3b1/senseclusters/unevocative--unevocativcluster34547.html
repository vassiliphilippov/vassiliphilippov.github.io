unevocative

adjective
not emotionally stirring
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈvɑːkətɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈvɒkətɪv / (BrE)
not bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind

Examples

An unevocative story
Although the novel was well-written, many readers found it unevocative and hard to connect with emotionally.
The speech was clear but rather unevocative, leaving the audience unmoved.
Critics described the film as visually impressive but ultimately unevocative in its storytelling.

Related Terms

  • antonym: evocative - bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind
  • synonym: uninspiring - not making you feel excited or interested
