herpes zoster

noun
viral infection causing painful skin rash (shingles)
7.5 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈhɜːr.piːz ˈzɑː.stər/ (AmE), /ˈhɜː.piːz ˈzɒs.tə/ (BrE)
