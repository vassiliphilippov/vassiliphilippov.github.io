hatpin

noun
long pin to secure hat to hair
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhætˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˈhætˌpɪn / (BrE)
A long, sturdy pin used by women to fasten a hat to their hair.

Examples

A hatpin for hats
She displayed her collection of ornate hatpins from the Victorian era.
The wind was so strong that only a hatpin kept her hat in place.
Vintage hatpins are now considered valuable antique accessories.
