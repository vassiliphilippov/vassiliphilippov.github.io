endogamic

adjective
relating to marriage within a group
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.doʊˈɡæ.mɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dəʊˈɡæ.mɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by the custom of marrying only within a specific clan, tribe, or social group.

Examples

Endogamic marriage
Membership in certain castes was maintained through strictly endogamic marriages.
The tribe has long upheld endogamic traditions to preserve its cultural heritage.
Their community is known for its endogamic practices, marrying only within their own social group.
