Guiana Highlands

proper noun
mountainous plateau in northern South America
Pronunciation: / giˈænə ˈhaɪləndz / (AmE), / giˈɑːnə ˈhaɪləndz / (BrE)
a mountainous plateau region in northern South America, extending from Venezuela into Guyana and northern Brazil
