audiophile

noun
hi-fi enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈɑdiəˌfaɪl /; / ˈɔdiəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːdiəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who is very interested in high-quality sound and audio equipment

Examples

An audiophile loves music
As an audiophile, she spends hours comparing different headphones before making a purchase.
The audiophile community often discusses the best ways to improve sound quality at home.
He became an audiophile after hearing the difference high-end speakers could make.
As an audiophile, she spends hours comparing different headphones for the best sound quality.
Many audiophiles invest in premium speakers and amplifiers to enhance their listening experience.
The store caters to audiophiles looking for the latest high-fidelity audio equipment.
