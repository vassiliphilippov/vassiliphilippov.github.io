germ

noun
microorganism that may cause disease
1.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɝm / (AmE), / dʒɜːm / (BrE)
a very small living thing, especially one that can cause disease

Examples

Dangerous germ
Doctors recommend covering your mouth when you sneeze to prevent the spread of germs.
It's important to wash your hands regularly to get rid of germs.
Many parents worry about their children catching germs at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacterium - microscopic, single-celled organism
  • synonym: virus - infectious agent that can cause disease
