pesterer

noun
persistently annoying person
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛstərər / (AmE), / ˈpɛstərə / (BrE)
a person who persistently annoys or bothers others

Examples

The pesterer annoys
Even the teacher grew tired of the pesterer in the back row, who always made distracting remarks.
She called her ex-boyfriend a pesterer because he wouldn't stop texting her.
The little pesterer kept interrupting our conversation with endless questions.
