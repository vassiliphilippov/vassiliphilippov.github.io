cookie jar reserve

noun
hidden reserve for earnings management
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊki dʒɑr rɪˈzɜrv / (AmE), / ˈkʊki dʒɑː rɪˈzɜːv / (BrE)
an amount of money secretly kept by a company, used to artificially smooth or adjust quarterly earnings reports
