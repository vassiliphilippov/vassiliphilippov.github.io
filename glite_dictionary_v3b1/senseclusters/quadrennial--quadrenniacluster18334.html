quadrennial

adjective
every four years
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɛniəl / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɛniəl / (BrE)
happening or recurring every four years, or lasting for four years

Examples

Quadrennial event
The Olympic Games are a quadrennial event that brings together athletes from around the world.
The committee holds a quadrennial review to assess the progress of the project.
Her scholarship is quadrennial, covering the entire four-year undergraduate program.
The World Cup is a quadrennial tournament that attracts fans from around the globe.
Elections for the presidency are held on a quadrennial basis in many countries.
The committee meets for a quadrennial review of its long-term goals.
