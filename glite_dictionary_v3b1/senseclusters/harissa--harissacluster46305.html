harissa

noun
spicy chili paste
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈrisə / (AmE), / həˈriːsə / (BrE)
a spicy North African paste made from chili peppers, garlic, and various spices, used to flavor food

Examples

Spicy harissa paste
She added a spoonful of harissa to the soup to give it a spicy kick.
The grilled chicken was served with a side of harissa for extra flavor.
Many North African dishes use harissa as a key ingredient in their sauces.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mayonnaise - a creamy sauce made from eggs and oil
  • synonym: sriracha - a spicy chili sauce from Thailand
