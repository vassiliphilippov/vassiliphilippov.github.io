terroristic

adjective
causing extreme fear
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
intended to cause extreme fear or very frightening in effect, not necessarily related to political terrorism

Examples

Terroristic threats
The villain's terroristic threats made the entire city panic.
Some horror movies rely on terroristic scenes to shock the audience.
The terroristic atmosphere of the abandoned house kept everyone on edge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frightening - causing fear or alarm
  • antonym: reassuring - making someone feel less afraid or worried
  • synonym: terrifying - causing intense fear
