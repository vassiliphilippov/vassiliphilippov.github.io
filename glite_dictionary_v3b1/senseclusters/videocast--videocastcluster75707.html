videocast

noun
online video broadcast
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪd.ɪ.oʊ.kæst/ (AmE), /ˈvɪd.ɪ.əʊ.kɑːst/ (BrE)
a broadcast of video content over the internet, often available live or on demand

Examples

A weekly videocast

You can watch the entire lecture as a videocast on the university website.
Many conferences now offer a live videocast for people who cannot attend in person.
