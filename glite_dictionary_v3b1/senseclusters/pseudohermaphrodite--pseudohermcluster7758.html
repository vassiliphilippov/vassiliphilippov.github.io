pseudohermaphrodite

adjective
person with mismatched internal and external sex characteristics
9.9
Pronunciation: /suː.doʊ.hɜːr.ˈmæ.frə.daɪt/ (AmE), /sjuː.dəʊ.hɜː.ˈmæ.frə.daɪt/ (BrE)
a person or animal whose internal reproductive organs are of one sex but who has external sexual characteristics of the other sex

Examples

Medical literature now rarely uses the term pseudohermaphrodite due to its outdated and potentially confusing nature.
The doctor explained that the patient was a pseudohermaphrodite because their internal reproductive organs did not match their outward appearance.
