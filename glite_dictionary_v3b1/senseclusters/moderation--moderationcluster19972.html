moderation

noun
oversight of online content
4.0 0.84/m
Pronunciation: / ccm51d59c8re6a8359n / (AmE), / ccm52d59c8re6a8359n / (BrE)
(in online platforms and social media) the activity of monitoring, reviewing, and managing user-generated content and behaviour to enforce rules, remove harmful material, and uphold community standards.

Examples

Online moderation
The platform's moderation team removed dozens of abusive comments yesterday.
Effective moderation helps online communities stay welcoming and prevents harassment from spreading.
They updated their moderation policies to include clearer rules about misinformation and spam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: censorship - the suppression or prohibition of speech or content; sometimes contrasted with moderation when removal is broader or politically motivated
  • synonym: content moderation - the specific practice of reviewing and applying platform rules to user posts, images, and comments
