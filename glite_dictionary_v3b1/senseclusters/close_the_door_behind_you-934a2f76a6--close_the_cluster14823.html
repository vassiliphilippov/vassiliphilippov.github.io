close the door behind you

verb
shut the door after passing through
Pronunciation: / kloʊz ðə dɔr bɪˈhaɪnd ju / (AmE), / kləʊz ðə dɔː bɪˈhaɪnd juː / (BrE)
to shut the door after you have gone through or out of a room or building, ensuring it is not left open

Examples

To close the door behind you
He forgot to close the door behind him, and the cold air came rushing in.
Please close the door behind you when you come in from the garden.
Remember to close the door behind you so the cat doesn't get out.
