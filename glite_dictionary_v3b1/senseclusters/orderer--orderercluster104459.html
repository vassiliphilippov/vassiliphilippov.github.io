orderer

noun
one who arranges or organizes
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdərər / (AmE), / ˈɔːdərə / (BrE)
a person who arranges or organizes things in an orderly way

Examples

Main orderer of files
As the chief orderer, she made sure every file was in its right place.
He became known as an orderer of chaos in his workplace.
The famous philosopher was an orderer of complex ideas into clear theories.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disorganizer - a person who makes things disordered or chaotic
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates things
