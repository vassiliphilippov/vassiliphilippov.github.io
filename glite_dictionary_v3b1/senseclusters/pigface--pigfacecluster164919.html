pigface

noun
coastal succulent plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌfeɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡˌfeɪs / (BrE)
a succulent plant with thick, fleshy leaves and bright flowers, commonly found in coastal areas

Examples

Coastal pigface plant
The sand dunes were covered in vibrant pigface during the spring.
Gardeners often plant pigface to prevent soil erosion along the coast.
The bright pink flowers of pigface attract many pollinators in the summer.
