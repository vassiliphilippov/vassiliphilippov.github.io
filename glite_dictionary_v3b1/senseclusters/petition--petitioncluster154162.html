Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
A solemn or reverent request, especially one addressed to a deity or divine being.

Examples

A petition to God
His nightly petition was for the health and happiness of his family.
Many religions include some form of petition in their rituals.
The ancient people offered a petition to the gods for rain.
