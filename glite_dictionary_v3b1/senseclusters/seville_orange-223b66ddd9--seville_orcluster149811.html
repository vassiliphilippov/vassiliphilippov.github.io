Seville orange

noun
bitter orange used in marmalade
8.5 6.5
Pronunciation: / səˈvɪl ˈɔːrɪndʒ /; / səˈvɪl ˈɔrɪndʒ / (AmE), / sɪˈvɪl ˈɒrɪndʒ /; / səˈvɪl ˈɒrɪndʒ / (BrE)
