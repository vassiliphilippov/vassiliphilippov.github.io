a nice touch

noun
enhancing or pleasing detail
Pronunciation: / ə naɪs tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ə naɪs tʌtʃ / (BrE)
an element or detail that improves or enhances something, making it more attractive or appealing

Examples

A nice touch to dessert
The cinnamon sprinkled on top was a nice touch to the dessert.
The flowers on the tables were a nice touch at the wedding reception.
Your handwritten note was a nice touch and really made my day.
