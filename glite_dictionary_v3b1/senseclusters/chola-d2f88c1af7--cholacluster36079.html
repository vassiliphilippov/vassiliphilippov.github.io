Chola

proper noun
South Indian dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊlə / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊlə / (BrE)
a powerful South Indian dynasty (c. 9th–13th centuries) known for its rule over southern India and contributions to art and architecture

Examples

The Chola dynasty
The Chola dynasty built some of the most impressive temples in southern India.
During the Chola period, trade flourished between India and Southeast Asia.
Historians admire the Chola navy for its strength and influence across the Indian Ocean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Pallava - an earlier South Indian dynasty known for its architecture
  • synonym: Pandya - a contemporary South Indian dynasty
