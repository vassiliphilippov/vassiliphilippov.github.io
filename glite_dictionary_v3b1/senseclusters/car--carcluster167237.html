car

noun
compartment of a lift or cable railway
5.3 1.58/m
Pronunciation: / k…ër / (AmE), / k…ëÀê / (BrE)
the part of a lift (elevator), cable railway, or similar transport system in which people or goods travel up and down

Examples

Elevator car
The elevator car ascended smoothly to the fifth floor.
The old mine uses a sturdy car to carry workers underground.
We waited for the car to arrive before entering the building.

Synonyms
