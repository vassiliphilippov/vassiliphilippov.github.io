journeyman

noun
qualified skilled worker in a trade
7.0 0.83/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ùÀên…™Àåm√¶n / (AmE), / Ààd í…úÀên…™m…ôn / (BrE)
a skilled worker who has completed an apprenticeship and works in a trade or craft, usually under an employer or as a qualified employee, but not yet a master

Examples

A journeyman carpenter
After years as an apprentice, he became a journeyman carpenter and started taking on more complex projects.
He worked as a journeyman blacksmith, traveling from town to town in search of employment.
The journeyman electrician was hired to rewire the historic building.

Synonyms

  • tradesperson
    3.6 0.01/m
    tradesperson works in a skilled manual trade, often in construction or repairs, while journeyman names a trained worker who is not yet a master.
  • tradesman
    3.2 0.11/m
    tradesman refers to a skilled worker, often male, in building or repair, while journeyman is a trained worker not yet a master.
  • skilled workman
    3.0 0.00/m
    skilled workman is a general term for a trained male worker in a job, while journeyman is a specific qualified rank after apprenticeship.
