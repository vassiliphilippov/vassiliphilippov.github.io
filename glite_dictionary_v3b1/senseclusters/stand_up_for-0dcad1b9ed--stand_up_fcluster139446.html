stand up for

verb
defend or support someone or something
1.0
Pronunciation: / stænd ʌp fɔr /; / stænd ʌp fər / (AmE), / stænd ʌp fə / (BrE)
to defend or support someone or something, especially when they are being criticized or treated unfairly
