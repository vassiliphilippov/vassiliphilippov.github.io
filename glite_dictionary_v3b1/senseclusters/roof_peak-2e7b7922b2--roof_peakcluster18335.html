roof peak

noun
highest point of a roof
5.4
Pronunciation: / ruːf piːk / (AmE), / ruːf piːk / (BrE)
the highest point or ridge of a roof, where the two sloping sides meet

Examples

The roof peak ridge
A weather vane was installed on the roof peak of the barn.
Snow accumulated along the roof peak after the storm.
The bright sunlight shone directly on the roof peak at noon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roof ridge - the horizontal line at the top of a roof, where the sloping sides meet
