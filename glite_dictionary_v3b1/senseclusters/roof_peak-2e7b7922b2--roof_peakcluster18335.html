roof peak

noun
highest point of a roof
3.4 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / ruÀêf piÀêk / (AmE), / ruÀêf piÀêk / (BrE)
the highest point or ridge of a roof, where the two sloping sides meet

Examples

The roof peak ridge
