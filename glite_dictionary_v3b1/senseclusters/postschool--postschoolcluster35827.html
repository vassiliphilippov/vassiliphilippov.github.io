postschool

noun
life after schooling
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈskuːl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈskuːl / (BrE)
the period of life or activities that occur after someone has completed formal schooling

Examples

Postschool years
Many young adults face new challenges during the postschool years as they start their careers.
The postschool transition can be both exciting and overwhelming for recent graduates.
She joined a support group to help her adjust to postschool life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postgraduate - relating to studies or activities after graduation
  • antonym: preschool - the period before formal schooling begins
