magnetic bottle

noun
device using magnetic fields to confine plasma
8.1
Pronunciation: / mæɡˈnɛtɪk ˈbɑtəl / (AmE), / mæɡˈnetɪk ˈbɒtəl / (BrE)
a device that uses a configuration of magnetic fields to confine plasma, especially in controlled thermonuclear fusion experiments

Examples

A magnetic bottle device
Scientists use a magnetic bottle to contain the hot plasma in fusion reactors.
The design of the magnetic bottle determines how efficiently plasma can be confined.
Without a magnetic bottle, the plasma would escape and the reaction would stop.
