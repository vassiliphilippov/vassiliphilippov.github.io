retighten

verb
to make tight again
3.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈtaɪ.tən/ /; / /riː.ˈtaɪ.tən/ /; / /rə.ˈtaɪ.tən/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈtaɪ.tən/ /; / /riː.ˈtaɪ.tən/ /; / /rə.ˈtaɪ.tən/ (BrE)
to make something tight again or tighter than it was before, especially after it has become loose (e.g., bolts, nuts, screws)
