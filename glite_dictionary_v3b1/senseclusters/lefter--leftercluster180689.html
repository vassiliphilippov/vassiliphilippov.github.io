lefter

adjective
more left-wing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftər / (AmE), / ˈlɛftə / (BrE)
having more left-wing or progressive political views or policies than someone or something else.

Examples

Lefter politician
The new candidate is considered lefter than her predecessor on economic issues.
Some members wanted the party to take an even lefter stance on social policies.
After the debate, it was clear his views were lefter than most of the panel.
