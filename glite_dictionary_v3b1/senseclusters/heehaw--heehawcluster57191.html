heehaw

noun
loud foolish person
Pronunciation: / ˈhiˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːhɔː / (BrE)
an informal term for a loud, foolish, or boorish person, often used in a derogatory way

Examples

A noisy heehaw
Everyone was embarrassed by the heehaw who kept shouting during the performance.
Don't act like a heehaw at the dinner table; show some manners.
The group laughed at the heehaw making silly jokes in the back row.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a polite and well-mannered man
  • synonym: lout - an uncouth or aggressive person
