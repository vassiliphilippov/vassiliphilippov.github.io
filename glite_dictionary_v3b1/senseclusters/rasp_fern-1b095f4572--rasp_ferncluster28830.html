rasp fern

noun
a fern of the genus Doodia with toothed fronds
8.7
Pronunciation: / ræsp fɜrn / (AmE), / rɑːsp fɜːn / (BrE)
Any fern of the genus Doodia, characterized by pinnate fronds with sharply toothed leaflets.

A rasp fern plant
