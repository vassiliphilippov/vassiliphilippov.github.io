P

noun
chemical symbol for phosphorus
4.8
Pronunciation: / piː / (AmE), / piː / (BrE)
the symbol for phosphorus, a multivalent nonmetallic chemical element of the nitrogen family, commonly found in inorganic phosphate rocks and organic phosphates in living cells

Examples

Fertilizers often contain high amounts of P to help plants grow.

White P is highly reactive and needs to be stored under water.
