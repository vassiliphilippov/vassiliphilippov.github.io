great power

noun
powerful, globally influential country
1.2
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈpaʊ.ɚ / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈpaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a country or state that has the ability to influence international events and project power on a global scale

Examples

A great power country
China is widely regarded as a great power in the twenty-first century.
The summit brought together leaders from every great power to discuss global security.
Throughout history, great powers have often competed for influence and territory.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: superpower - a state with dominant influence and military strength surpassing that of other great powers
