Hitlerite

noun
follower of Hitler
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtləˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɪtləˌraɪt / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows Adolf Hitler, his policies, or the Nazi movement

Examples

A Hitlerite follower
During the war, anyone suspected of being a Hitlerite was closely monitored by the authorities.
The documentary explored how some Hitlerites continued to spread Nazi ideology after World War II.
He denied being a Hitlerite, insisting he did not support any extremist views.
