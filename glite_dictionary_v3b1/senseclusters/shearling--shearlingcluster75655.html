shearling

adjective
sheepskin with wool
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪrlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪəlɪŋ / (BrE)
made from sheepskin with the wool still attached, especially used for clothing or trim

Examples

Shearling coat
He wore a shearling coat to stay warm during the winter months.
The boots were lined with shearling for extra comfort and insulation.
She chose a shearling collar to add a touch of luxury to her jacket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lambskin - leather made from the skin of a young sheep
  • antonym: leather - material made from animal skin, usually without wool or hair
