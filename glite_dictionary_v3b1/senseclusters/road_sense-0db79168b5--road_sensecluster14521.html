road sense

noun
good judgment for safety on roads
1.9
Pronunciation: / roʊd sɛns / (AmE), / rəʊd sɛns / (BrE)
the ability to judge and avoid danger on the road, especially through awareness of traffic and potential hazards

Examples

Road sense skills
It is important for children to develop road sense at an early age.
Teenagers often lack road sense when they first start cycling in busy areas.
Thanks to her excellent road sense, she avoided several potential accidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: road safety - the condition of being protected from risk or injury on the road
