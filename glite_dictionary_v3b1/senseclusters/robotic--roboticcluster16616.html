robotic

adjective
mechanical or unfeeling in manner
5.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /roʊ.ˈbɑː.tɪk/ /; / /rəʊ.ˈbɑː.tɪk/ (AmE), /rəʊ.ˈbɒt.ɪk/ /; / /rə.ˈbɒt.ɪk/ (BrE)
behaving in a mechanical, unfeeling, or automatic way, as if controlled by a machine rather than by thought or emotion

Examples

Robotic behavior
He moved through his daily routine in a robotic manner, showing no enthusiasm.
Her robotic responses made it hard to know how she really felt.
The customer service agent's robotic tone frustrated the caller.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - done without conscious thought, by reflex
  • antonym: human - relating to or characteristic of people, especially showing emotion or warmth
  • synonym: mechanical - operating like a machine, lacking emotion or spontaneity
