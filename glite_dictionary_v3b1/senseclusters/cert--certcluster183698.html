cert

noun
something certain to happen; a sure thing
6.5
Pronunciation: / sɜrt / (AmE), / sɜːt / (BrE)
a person or thing that is certain to win or succeed; a sure thing

Examples

A cert to win
Everyone thought the champion was a cert to win the race.
It's a cert that the show will sell out within minutes.
Winning the award was a cert after her outstanding performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certainty - something that is certain
  • antonym: long shot - something with little chance of success
