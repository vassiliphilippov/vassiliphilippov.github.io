preorder

verb
to order in advance
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈɔrdər /; / priːˈɔrdər / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈɔːdə /; / priːˈɔːdə / (BrE)
to order or request a product before it is officially available for sale or delivery

Examples

If you preorder the book now, you will get a special discount and early delivery.
