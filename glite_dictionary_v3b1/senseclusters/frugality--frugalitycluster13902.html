frugality

noun
economical use of resources
2.3
Pronunciation: / fruˈɡæləti / (AmE), / fruːˈɡælɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being economical with money or resources; careful management to avoid waste.

Examples

Practice frugality daily
Her frugality allowed her to save enough money to travel the world.
Practicing frugality can help you avoid unnecessary expenses and live a simpler life.
The family's frugality was evident in their habit of reusing and recycling household items.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagance - lack of restraint in spending money or using resources
  • synonym: thrift - the quality of using resources carefully and not wastefully
