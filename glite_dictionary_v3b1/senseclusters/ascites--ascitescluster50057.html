ascites

noun
accumulation of fluid in the abdomen
Pronunciation: / əˈsaɪtiz / (AmE), / əˈsaɪtiːz / (BrE)
an abnormal accumulation of serous fluid in the abdominal (peritoneal) cavity, often causing swelling

Examples

Abdominal ascites swelling
Doctors performed a procedure to drain the ascites from her abdomen.
Large volumes of ascites can be uncomfortable and require medical intervention.
The patient was diagnosed with ascites as a result of chronic liver disease.
