neat's-foot oil

noun
oil from cattle feet for leather care
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnitsˌfʊt ˌɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈniːtsˌfʊt ˌɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow oil made from the feet and legs of cattle, used especially for conditioning and dressing leather

Examples

Neat's-foot oil for leather
