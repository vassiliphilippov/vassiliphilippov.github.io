bandy legs

noun
legs curving outward at knees
Pronunciation: / ˈbændi lɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈbændi lɛɡz / (BrE)
Legs that curve outward at the knees, giving a bowed appearance.

Examples

Bandy legs condition
As a child, he was teased because of his bandy legs.
She developed bandy legs as a result of a vitamin deficiency.
The old cowboy's bandy legs were a testament to years spent riding horses.
