checkmate

noun
complete defeat; decisive victory
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkˌmeɪt / (BrE)
A complete defeat or decisive victory, especially one that leaves an opponent with no possible response.

Examples

Business checkmate
After months of negotiations, the company's acquisition of its rival was a business checkmate.
His brilliant strategy in the debate led to a rhetorical checkmate over his opponent.
With this final piece of evidence, the prosecution delivered a legal checkmate in the courtroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defeat - the act of overcoming or being overcome in a contest or struggle
  • synonym: victory - the act of defeating an opponent or enemy; success in a contest
