metalwork

noun
the metal parts of something
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtlˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈmɛtlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the parts of an object that are made of metal

Examples

Antique metalwork
During restoration, specialists carefully examined the antique clock's metalwork.
The old gate's metalwork was rusted and badly in need of repair.
You should polish the metalwork on the car to keep it shiny and clean.

Related Terms
