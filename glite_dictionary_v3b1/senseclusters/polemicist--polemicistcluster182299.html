polemicist

noun
a person engaged in controversial debate
Pronunciation: / poʊˈlɛmɪsɪst / (AmE), / pəˈlɛmɪsɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes or speaks in support of a controversial argument, especially one who opposes others in public debate, particularly in theology or intellectual topics

Examples

A polemicist debates ideas
As a well-known polemicist, she frequently engaged in heated debates with her philosophical rivals.
Historians remember him as a sharp-tongued polemicist in the religious debates of his era.
The article was written by a fierce polemicist who never shied away from controversy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controversialist - a person who engages in controversy, especially in writing
