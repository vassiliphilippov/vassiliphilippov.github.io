registrar

noun
official in charge of records
5.3 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / Ààr…õd í.…ôÀåstr…ër / (AmE), / Ààr…õd í.…™str…ëÀê / (BrE)
an official responsible for keeping records, especially of births, deaths, marriages, students, or other formal registrations

Examples

Birth registrar
Please submit your documents to the registrar to complete your registration.
The registrar is responsible for issuing birth certificates in this district.
The registrar updated the list of new students before the semester began.

Synonyms

  • recorder
    3.3 0.40/m
    a recorder is a person who keeps official accounts in an organization, while a registrar is a formal official who records vital or institutional registrations.
  • record-keeper
    3.8 0.06/m
    a record-keeper is someone who maintains official lists and files at many places, while a registrar is specifically the named official for legal registrations.
  • recordist
    6.2 0.00/m
    a recordist is a less common label for a person who keeps records in an organization, while a registrar is an official for legal registrations.
