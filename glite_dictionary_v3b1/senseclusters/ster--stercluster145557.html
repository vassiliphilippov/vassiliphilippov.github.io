Pronunciation: / stər / (AmE), / stə / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns indicating a person who does a particular activity or is associated with something

Examples

The -ster suffix
The word 'youngster' uses the -ster suffix to mean a young person.
In 'trickster', the -ster ending shows someone who plays tricks.
The suffix -ster is also found in words like 'gangster' and 'hipster'.
