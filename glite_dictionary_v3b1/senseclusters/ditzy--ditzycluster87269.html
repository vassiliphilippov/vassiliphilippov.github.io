ditzy

noun
silly or scatterbrained person
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪtsi / (AmE), / ˈdɪtsi / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is silly, scatterbrained, or not very serious

Examples

She is a ditzy
People sometimes call her a ditzy because she forgets things so easily.
He played the role of a lovable ditzy in the comedy film.
Despite being a bit ditzy, she always brings joy to her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airhead - a person who is not very intelligent or is easily distracted
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: scatterbrain - a person who is forgetful or disorganized
