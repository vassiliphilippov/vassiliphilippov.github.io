vermeil

adjective
gold-plated
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜrˈmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈvɜːmaɪl / (BrE)
covered or coated with a thin layer of gold, especially referring to silver that has been gold-plated

Examples

Vermeil finish
The antique box was vermeil, giving it a rich golden appearance despite being made of silver.
She admired the vermeil frame that surrounded the old family portrait in the hallway.
Many royal artifacts are vermeil, combining the beauty of gold with the strength of silver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gilded - covered with a thin layer of gold
  • antonym: silver - made of or containing silver, not gold-plated
