sprawl

verb
sit or lie with limbs spread out
7.1
Pronunciation: / sprɔl / (AmE), / sprɔːl / (BrE)
to sit or lie with arms and legs spread out in an ungainly or relaxed way

Examples

To sprawl on the sofa
He likes to sprawl on the sofa after work, taking up half the cushions.
She was sprawling in an armchair, completely at ease.
The children sprawled on the grass, exhausted after their game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curl up - to arrange the body in a curled or compact position
  • synonym: recline - to lean or lie back in a relaxed position
