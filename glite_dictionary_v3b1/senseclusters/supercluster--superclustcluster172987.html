supercluster

noun
large galaxy group
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌklʌstər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌklʌstə / (BrE)
an extremely large group of galaxies or galaxy clusters held together by gravity, forming one of the largest structures in the universe
