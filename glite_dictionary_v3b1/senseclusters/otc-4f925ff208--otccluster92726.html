OTC

adjective
non-prescription medicine
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ tiː ˈsiː / (AmE), / ˌəʊ tiː ˈsiː / (BrE)
available for purchase without a doctor's prescription, especially referring to medicines

Examples

OTC medicine
Many pain relievers are sold as OTC drugs in most pharmacies.
You should always read the label before taking any OTC medication.
Some allergy medicines are only available by prescription, while others are OTC.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonprescription - not requiring a doctor's prescription
  • antonym: prescription - medicine that requires a doctor's authorization
