profiterole

noun
small cream-filled pastry
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɪt̬.əˌroʊl / (AmE), / prəˈfɪt.ər.əʊl / (BrE)
A small round hollow pastry, typically filled with cream and often topped with chocolate sauce.

Examples

A profiterole with cream
At the bakery, I ordered a profiterole filled with rich vanilla cream and drizzled with chocolate.
She made a tower of profiteroles for dessert at the party.
The waiter recommended the profiterole with raspberry sauce as a sweet treat.
