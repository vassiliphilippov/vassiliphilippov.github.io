diapause

noun
suspended development period
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌpɔz / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəpɑːz /; / ˈdaɪəˌpɔːz / (BrE)
a period when an animal (especially an insect) temporarily stops developing to survive difficult environmental conditions
