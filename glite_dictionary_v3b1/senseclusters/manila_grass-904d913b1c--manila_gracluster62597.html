Manila grass

noun
tropical lawn grass from the Philippines
8.7
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪlə ɡræs / (AmE), / məˈnɪlə ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
A type of lawn grass native to the Philippines, also grown in the United States, known for its fine texture and tolerance to heat.

Examples

Lush Manila grass
After replacing the old turf, the lawn now features lush Manila grass.
Golf courses in tropical regions often use Manila grass because of its fine texture.
The landscaper suggested planting Manila grass for its durability in hot climates.
