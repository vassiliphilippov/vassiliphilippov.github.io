annotating

noun
the act of adding explanatory notes
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈænəˌteɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈanəˌteɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of adding explanatory notes or comments to a text, diagram, or other piece of information

Examples

Annotating a book
Annotating the manuscript helped the editor track important changes and suggestions.
He found that annotating his lecture notes improved his understanding of the material.
Students spent the afternoon annotating the assigned article for tomorrow's discussion.
