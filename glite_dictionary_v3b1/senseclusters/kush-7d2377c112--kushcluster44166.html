Kush

proper noun
ancient African kingdom
Pronunciation: / kʊʃ / (AmE), / kʊʃ / (BrE)
an ancient African kingdom located south of Egypt, known for its rich culture and history

Examples

Kingdom of Kush
The ancient kingdom of Kush was located south of Egypt.
Archaeologists have discovered impressive pyramids built by the rulers of Kush.
Trade between Kush and Egypt influenced both cultures for centuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Egypt - an ancient civilization in northeastern Africa
  • synonym: Nubia - a region along the Nile south of Egypt, closely linked to Kush
