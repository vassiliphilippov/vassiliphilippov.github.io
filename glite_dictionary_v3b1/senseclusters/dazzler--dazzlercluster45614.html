dazzler

noun
blinding light device
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdæz.lər/ (AmE), /ˈdæz.lə/ (BrE)
a device that emits a powerful light or laser to temporarily blind, disorient, or distract people or animals

Examples

Laser dazzler
The security team used a dazzler to prevent intruders from approaching the restricted area.
During the demonstration, police officers pointed a dazzler at the crowd to disperse them safely.
Soldiers carried a portable dazzler to temporarily blind enemy personnel without causing permanent harm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flashbang - a device that creates a bright flash and loud noise to disorient people
  • synonym: strobe - a device that produces regular flashes of light
