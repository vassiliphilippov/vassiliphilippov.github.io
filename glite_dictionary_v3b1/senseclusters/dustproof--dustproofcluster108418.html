dustproof

adjective
resistant to dust
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈdʌstˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent dust from entering or affecting something

Examples

Dustproof phone case
This phone is dustproof, so you can use it at the beach without worry.
The laboratory uses dustproof containers to protect sensitive equipment.
He bought a dustproof cover for his computer to keep it clean.
