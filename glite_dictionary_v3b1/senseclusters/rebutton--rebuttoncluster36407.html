rebutton

verb
to sew button back on
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbʌtən / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbʌtən / (BrE)
to attach or sew a button back onto a piece of clothing after it has come off or become loose

Examples

To rebutton a jacket
After losing a button at work, she had to rebutton her jacket before the meeting.
He learned how to rebutton his shirt when a button came loose.
If you notice a missing button, you should rebutton your coat as soon as possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sew - to join or repair something using a needle and thread
  • antonym: unbutton - to undo the buttons of a piece of clothing
