gain

noun
an advantage or benefit
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪn / (AmE), / ɡeɪn / (BrE)
an advantage, benefit, profit, or increase in amount, value, or weight

Examples

A gain in profit
She realized the gain outweighed the potential risks.

There was little gain from their risky investment.

The company's quarterly report showed a significant gain compared to last year.
Technological advances led to a rapid gain in productivity.
The company reported a significant gain in profits this quarter.
There was a gain of five pounds in her weight over the winter.
