stay-at-home

adjective
preferring to stay at home
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsteɪ æt ˈhoʊm / (AmE), / ˌsteɪ æt ˈhəʊm / (BrE)
(of a person) preferring to remain at home rather than travel or go out socially.

Examples

Stay-at-home person
Although her friends traveled often, Emily was a true stay-at-home who preferred her own company.
Being a stay-at-home type, he rarely went out to parties or events.
Her stay-at-home nature made her the go-to person whenever someone needed house-sitting.
