animalculous

adjective
microscopic organism-like
Pronunciation: / ˌænɪˈmælkjələs / (AmE), / ˌænɪˈmalkjʊləs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a very small animal or microscopic organism

Examples

Animalculous creatures
Under the microscope, the water revealed an animalculous world teeming with life.
The scientist described the animalculous particles she observed in the laboratory sample.
Early researchers were fascinated by the animalculous creatures found in a single drop of pond water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: macroscopic - large enough to be visible to the naked eye
  • synonym: microscopic - so small as to be visible only with a microscope
