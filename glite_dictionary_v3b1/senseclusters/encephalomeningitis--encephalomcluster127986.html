encephalomeningitis

noun
inflammation of brain and spinal cord meninges
Pronunciation: / ɛnˌsɛfəloʊməˌnɪndʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ɛnˌsɛfələʊmɛnɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the brain and spinal cord together with their meninges.

Examples
