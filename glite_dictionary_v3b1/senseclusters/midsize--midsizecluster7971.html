midsize

noun
medium-sized vehicle
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌsaɪz / (BrE)
a vehicle or object that is medium in size, especially compared to smaller or larger types in the same category

Examples

A midsize sedan

The rental agency offered us a midsize for our road trip across the country.
He prefers driving a midsize because it is easier to park than a large SUV.
