Synonyms

  • disperse
    4.4 10/m
    disperse moves people or things widely and evenly, while scatter suggests a quick, messy flight in many directions.
  • spread out
    2.8 5.25/m
    spread out means people or things move to cover a wider area, while scatter suggests sudden, rapid separation.
  • dissipate
    6.8 4.56/m
    dissipate makes something vanish slowly or fade away, while scatter means to send things apart quickly and visibly.
  • break up
    3.4 4.76/m
    break up ends a meeting or group and causes people to leave, while scatter means people run apart in different directions.
