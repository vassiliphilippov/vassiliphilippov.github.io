tradesman

noun
skilled manual worker
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdzmən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdzmən / (BrE)
a skilled worker who practices a particular trade or craft, especially in building, repairs, or manual work

Examples

Skilled tradesman repairs
The tradesman arrived early to install the new kitchen cabinets.
As a skilled tradesman, he took great pride in his woodworking.
Many homeowners rely on a trusted tradesman for repairs and renovations.
The tradesman arrived early to repair the broken window in the living room.
Many tradesmen are self-employed and work on various construction projects.
She hired a skilled tradesman to renovate her old kitchen cabinets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artisan - a worker skilled in making things by hand
  • synonym: craftsman - a person skilled in a particular craft
  • antonym: laborer - a person doing unskilled manual work
