tradesman

noun
skilled manual worker
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪdz.mən/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪdz.mən/ (BrE)
a skilled worker who practices a particular trade or craft, especially in building, repairs, or manual work

Examples
