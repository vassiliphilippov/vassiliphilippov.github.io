bundle up

verb
dress warmly
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌndəl ʌp /; / ˈbʌnd(ə)l ˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈbʌndl ʌp /; / ˈbʌn.dl ʌp / (BrE)
to put on warm clothes, especially several layers, to protect yourself or someone else from cold weather
