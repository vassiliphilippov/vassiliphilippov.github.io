Pyrex

noun
strong, heat-resistant glass; glassware brand
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌrɛks / (AmE), / ˈpaɪrɛks / (BrE)
a type of strong, heat-resistant glass, or glassware made from it, commonly used in laboratories and kitchens

Examples

Pyrex glassware
He poured the boiling liquid carefully into a Pyrex measuring cup.
Most laboratory beakers are made from Pyrex to withstand high temperatures.
She baked the casserole in a Pyrex dish so it could go straight from the oven to the table.
