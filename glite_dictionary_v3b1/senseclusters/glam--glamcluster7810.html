glam

verb
to make stylish
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɡlæm / (AmE), / ɡlæm / (BrE)
to make someone or something look stylish and attractive, often by adding makeup, fashionable clothes, or accessories

Examples

To glam up
She glammed herself up for the party with a sparkling dress and bold makeup.
The stylist glammed the models before the big fashion show.
He glammed his old car with new paint and shiny wheels.
