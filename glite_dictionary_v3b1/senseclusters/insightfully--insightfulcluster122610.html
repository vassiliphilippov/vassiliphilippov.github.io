insightfully

adverb
with deep understanding
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsaɪtfəli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsaɪtfəli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows deep understanding or clear perception of a subject or situation

Examples

To insightfully analyze
The professor insightfully analyzed the novel's themes during the lecture.
He insightfully predicted the outcome based on his experience in the industry.
Her comments were insightfully crafted, revealing a deep understanding of the issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perceptively - in a way that shows keen observation or understanding
  • antonym: superficially - in a way that lacks depth or thoroughness
  • synonym: thoughtfully - in a way that shows careful consideration or attention
