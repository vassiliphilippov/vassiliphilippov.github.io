Demodex

noun
hair follicle mite
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛməˌdɛks / (AmE), / ˈdiːməˌdɛks / (BrE)
a genus of tiny mites that live in or near the hair follicles of mammals, sometimes causing skin irritation

Examples

Demodex mites
A dermatologist identified Demodex mites as the cause of the patient's skin irritation.
Some people have a higher number of Demodex living on their skin without any symptoms.
Researchers study Demodex to understand its role in certain skin conditions.
