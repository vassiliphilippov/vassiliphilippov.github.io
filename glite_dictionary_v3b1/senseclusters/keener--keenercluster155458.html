keener

adjective
more sharp or eager
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːnər / (AmE), / ˈkiːnə / (BrE)
having more sharpness, eagerness, or intensity in perception, feeling, or interest than something else.

Examples

Keener senses
After years of training, his instincts became keener than ever before.
The detective's keener sense of observation helped solve the difficult case.
Her interest in science grew keener as she learned more about the subject.

Related Terms

  • antonym: duller - less sharp or less intense in feeling or perception
  • degree lower: keen - sharp, eager, or intense in perception or feeling
  • degree higher: keener - comparative form of keen
  • synonym: sharp - having a fine edge or being quick to notice things
