Synonyms

  • rerun
    2.3 0.10/m
    rerun is a repeated TV or film showing scheduled on a channel, while rebroadcast is simply broadcasting the program again.
  • retransmit
    6.0 0.06/m
    retransmit is sending a signal or program again, often between stations, while rebroadcast focuses on airing the program again for listeners or viewers.
  • reshow
    3.4 0.01/m
    reshow is to present a film or object again at a place like a cinema, while rebroadcast means airing it again on radio or TV.
  • replay
    2.1 1.76/m
    replay is playing a recorded event again for viewers to watch moments, while rebroadcast is airing the original program again for the whole audience.
