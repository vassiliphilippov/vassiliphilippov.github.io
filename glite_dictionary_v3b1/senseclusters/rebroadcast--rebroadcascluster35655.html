Synonyms

  • rerun
    2.3 0.2/m

  • retransmit
    6.0 0.04/m
    retransmit is sending a signal or program again, often between stations, while rebroadcast focuses on airing the program again for listeners or viewers.
  • reshow
    3.4 0.01/m
