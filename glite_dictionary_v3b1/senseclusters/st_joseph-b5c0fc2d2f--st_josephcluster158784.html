St. Joseph

proper noun
city in northwest Missouri, USA
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒoʊzəf / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈdʒəʊzɪf / (BrE)
A city in northwest Missouri, USA, on the Missouri River, historically the eastern terminus of the Pony Express in the 19th century.

Examples

St. Joseph city
He grew up in St. Joseph, Missouri, where his family still lives today.
Historic St. Joseph attracts tourists interested in the Old West and the Pony Express.
The Pony Express began in St. Joseph and carried mail all the way to California.
