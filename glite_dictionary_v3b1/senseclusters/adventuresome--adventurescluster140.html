adventuresome

adjective
willing to seek daring experiences
5.6
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃərsəm / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃəsəm / (BrE)
Willing to undertake or seeking out new and daring experiences; adventurous.

Examples

An adventuresome person
He has always been adventuresome, trying activities others might find too risky.
Her adventuresome spirit led her to travel alone through South America.
The company is known for its adventuresome approach to investing in new technologies.
