unconcerted

adjective
not single-step (chemistry)
8.6
Pronunciation: /ʌn.kən.ˈsɜːr.tɪd/ /; / /ən.kən.ˈsɜːr.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.kən.ˈsɜː.tɪd/ /; / /ən.kən.ˈsɜː.tɪd/ (BrE)
