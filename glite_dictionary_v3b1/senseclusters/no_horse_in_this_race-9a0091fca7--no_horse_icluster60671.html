no horse in this race

x
no personal stake in an outcome
Pronunciation: / noʊ hɔrs ɪn ðɪs reɪs / (AmE), / nəʊ hɔːs ɪn ðɪs reɪs / (BrE)
Having no personal interest, involvement, or stake in the outcome of a particular situation or contest.

Examples

It's easy for him to be objective, since he really has no horse in this race.
