brr

noun
vibrating sound
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / b…úr / (AmE), / b…úÀê / (BrE)
a low, vibrating or buzzing sound, often imitating a motor or similar noise

Examples

A loud brr
The old refrigerator made a constant brr that filled the quiet kitchen.
As the engine started, a faint brr echoed through the garage.
We heard a soft brr coming from the heater as it turned on.

Synonyms

  • whirr
    6.7 1.11/m
    whirr is a steady, low mechanical sound from fast-moving parts, while brr is a short buzzing imitation of a motor.
  • buzz
    1.0 3.95/m
    buzz is a continuous humming like an insect or small machine, while brr is a simpler short buzzing that mimics a motor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hum - a steady, low sound like that of machinery
