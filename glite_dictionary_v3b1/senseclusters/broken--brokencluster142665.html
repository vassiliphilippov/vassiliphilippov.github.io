broken

adjective
financially ruined
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
Having suffered severe financial ruin or loss; no longer financially sound.

Examples

Broken businessman
After years of risky investments, he was left broken and penniless.
The economic crisis left many families broken and homeless.
Their broken business empire was once the pride of the city.
