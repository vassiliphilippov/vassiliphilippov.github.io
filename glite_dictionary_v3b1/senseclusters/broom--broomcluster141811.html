broom

noun
cleaning tool for sweeping
3.1
Pronunciation: / bruːm / (AmE), / bruːm / (BrE)
A cleaning tool with a bundle of straws or twigs fixed to a long handle, used for sweeping floors.

Examples

A broom for sweeping
After breaking the glass, he quickly grabbed a broom to clean up the mess.
She used a broom to sweep the kitchen floor every morning.
The school's janitor replaced the old broom with a new one.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brush - a tool with bristles or stiff fibers, used for cleaning or painting
