incarceration

noun
imprisonment; state of being in prison
2.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌkɑr.səˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌkɑː.səˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the state of being confined in prison; imprisonment.

Examples

Prison incarceration
After his incarceration, he became an advocate for prison reform.
Long-term incarceration can have serious effects on mental health.
The judge decided that incarceration was necessary for the protection of the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imprisonment - the act of putting someone in prison or the state of being imprisoned
  • antonym: release - the act of letting someone go free from prison
