immortalize

verb
make famous forever
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔːr.t̬ə.laɪz /; / ɪˈmɔrtəˌlaɪz /; / ɪˈmɔːrdəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔː.tə.laɪz /; / ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make someone or something remembered forever, often by making them famous or honoring them with a lasting tribute
