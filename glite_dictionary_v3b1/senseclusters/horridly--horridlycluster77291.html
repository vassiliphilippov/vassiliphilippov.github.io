horridly

adverb
in a hideous or dreadful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrɪdli / (AmE), / ˈhɒrɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes horror, disgust, or intense displeasure; hideously or dreadfully.

Examples

To be horridly late
He was horridly late to the meeting, causing everyone to worry.
Her plan failed horridly, resulting in utter chaos.
The food at the new restaurant was horridly overcooked and nearly inedible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delightfully - in a way that causes delight or great pleasure
  • synonym: horribly - in a way that causes horror or disgust
