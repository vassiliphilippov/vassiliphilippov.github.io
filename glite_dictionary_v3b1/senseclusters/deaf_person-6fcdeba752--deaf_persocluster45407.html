deaf person

noun
person with severe hearing loss
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /def ˈpɜːr.sən/ (AmE), /def ˈpɜː.sən/ (BrE)
A person who has a severe or total hearing loss and cannot hear most sounds.

Examples

Deaf person with sign language
As a deaf person, she relies on sign language to communicate with her friends.
Technology has greatly improved accessibility for the average deaf person in the workplace.
The school provides special resources to help any deaf person succeed academically.
