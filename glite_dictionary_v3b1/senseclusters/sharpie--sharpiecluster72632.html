sharpie

noun
shallow-draft sailboat with triangular sail
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpi / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpi / (BrE)
a shallow-draft sailboat with a sharp prow, flat bottom, and triangular sail, formerly used along the northern Atlantic coast of the United States

Examples

A sharpie sailboat
Designed for shallow waters, the sharpie easily navigated the tidal flats.
She restored an old sharpie and sailed it along the Connecticut coast.
The fishermen took their sharpie out into the sheltered bay to check their lobster traps.
