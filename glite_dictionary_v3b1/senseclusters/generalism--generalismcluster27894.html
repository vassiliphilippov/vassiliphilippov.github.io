generalism

noun
broad knowledge approach
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛnərəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛnərəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the practice or principle of having a broad range of knowledge or skills rather than specializing in one area

Examples

Generalism in the workplace
Her generalism allowed her to adapt quickly to different roles in the company.
In today's job market, generalism can be as valuable as deep expertise.
The organization valued generalism because it encouraged employees to learn new skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalist - a person with broad knowledge or skills in many areas
  • antonym: specialism - the practice of focusing on a specific area or field
