eggcup

noun
small cup for holding a boiled egg
Pronunciation: / ˈɛgˌkʌp / (AmE), / ˈɛgˌkʌp / (BrE)
a small cup designed to hold a boiled egg upright while it is being eaten

Examples

A small eggcup
He placed the soft-boiled egg carefully into the eggcup on his plate.
Her grandmother gave her a set of antique eggcups as a gift.
The eggcup kept the egg steady while he tapped off the top with a spoon.
