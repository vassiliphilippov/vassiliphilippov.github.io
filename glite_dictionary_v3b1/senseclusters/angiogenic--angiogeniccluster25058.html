angiogenic

adjective
forming blood vessels
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒiəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌandʒiəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
causing or relating to the formation of new blood vessels

Examples

angiogenic factors
Researchers are studying angiogenic factors that could improve recovery after heart attacks.
The tumor released angiogenic signals to stimulate new blood vessel growth.
Some therapies use angiogenic drugs to help heal chronic wounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antiangiogenic - preventing the formation of new blood vessels
  • synonym: vasculogenic - relating to the formation of blood vessels, especially in embryos
