systems theory

noun
interdisciplinary study of systems and their principles
4.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪs.təmz ˈθiː.ə.riː/ /; / /ˈsɪs.təmz ˈθɪr.iː/ (AmE), /ˈsɪs.təmz ˈθɪə.riː/ /; / /ˈsɪs.təmz ˈθiː.ə.riː/ (BrE)
