club

noun
playing card suit marked with black trefoil
Pronunciation: / klʌb / (AmE), / klʌb / (BrE)
one of the four suits in a standard deck of playing cards, marked with a black trefoil symbol

Examples

Ace of clubs
He drew the ace of clubs from the deck.
If clubs are trumps, you need to play carefully.
She played a club to win the trick.
