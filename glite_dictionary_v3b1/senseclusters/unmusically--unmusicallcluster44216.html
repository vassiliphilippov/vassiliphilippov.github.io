unmusically

adverb
in an unmusical manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmjuːzɪkli / (AmE), / ʌnˈmjuːzɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that lacks musical quality or skill.

Examples

Play unmusically offbeat
Although he tried hard, his efforts at the piano sounded unmusically offbeat.
She always sings unmusically, missing every note in the song.
The violin squeaked unmusically during the difficult passage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: musically - in a musical manner; with musical quality
