inconsequentially

adverb
in an unimportant or irrelevant way
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌkɑːnsəˈkwɛnʃəli / (AmE), / ɪnˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks importance or consequence; without significant effect or relevance.

Examples

To inconsequentially mention
He inconsequentially mentioned his new job, not expecting anyone to be interested.
She changed the subject inconsequentially, as if the previous discussion did not matter.
The committee moved from one topic to another inconsequentially, without reaching any decisions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consequentially - in a way that has important effects or consequences
  • synonym: irrelevantly - in a way that is not relevant
