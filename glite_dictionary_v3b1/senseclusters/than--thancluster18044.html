than

conjunction
immediate sequence
1.0
Pronunciation: / ðæn / (AmE), / ðæn / (BrE)
used after expressions like 'no sooner' to show that one event happened immediately after another

Examples

No sooner than
No sooner had I sat down than the phone rang loudly in the next room.
She had barely finished her sentence than the lights went out unexpectedly.
No sooner did the train arrive than the passengers rushed to board.
