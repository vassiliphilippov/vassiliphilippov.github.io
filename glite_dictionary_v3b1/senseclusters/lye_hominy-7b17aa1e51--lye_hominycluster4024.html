lye hominy

noun
hominy prepared with lye
Pronunciation: / laɪ ˈhɑːməˌni / (AmE), / laɪ ˈhɒmɪni / (BrE)
Hominy that has been prepared by soaking corn kernels in a solution of lye to remove the hulls and bleach them.

Examples

Lye hominy preparation
My grandmother used to make lye hominy from scratch every winter.
The recipe calls for a cup of cooked lye hominy as a side dish.
Traditionally, lye hominy is served with butter and salt for breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hominy - hulled corn kernels that have been boiled and may be eaten as food
