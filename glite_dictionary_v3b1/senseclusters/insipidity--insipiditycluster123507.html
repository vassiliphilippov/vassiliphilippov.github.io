insipidity

noun
dullness; lack of distinctive taste or interest
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.sɪˈpɪdəti /; / ˌɪn.səˈpɪd.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.sɪˈpɪdəti /; / ˌɪn.sɪˈpɪd.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of being dull, bland, or lacking in interest, spirit, or flavor
