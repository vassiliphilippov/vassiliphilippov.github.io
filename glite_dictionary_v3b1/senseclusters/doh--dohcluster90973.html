doh

interjection
exclamation of frustration
Pronunciation: / doʊ / (AmE), / dəʊ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express frustration or embarrassment after realizing a mistake or something obvious

Examples

Oops, doh!
I forgot to bring my wallet to the store—doh!
He realized he had sent the email to the wrong person and muttered, doh.
She left her keys in the car again. Doh!

Related Terms

  • synonym: duh - an exclamation used to indicate something is obvious
  • synonym: oops - an exclamation used after making a mistake
