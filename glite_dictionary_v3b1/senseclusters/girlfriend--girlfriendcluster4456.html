girlfriend

noun
female friend (platonic sense)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɝːlˌfrɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡɜːlˌfrɛnd / (BrE)
A female friend, especially one with whom a woman has a close or platonic relationship.

Examples

Lunch with girlfriends
After years apart, the two girlfriends reunited over lunch.
Emma considers Sarah her oldest girlfriend from college.
She went shopping with her girlfriend on Saturday afternoon.
