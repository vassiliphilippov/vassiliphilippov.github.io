face mask

noun
protective facial covering
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪs mæsk / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs mɑːsk / (BrE)
a covering worn over the nose and mouth to protect against dust, germs, or pollution

Examples

Medical face mask
During the pandemic, everyone was required to wear a face mask in public places.
He put on a face mask before entering the construction site to avoid inhaling dust.
Some people decorate their face masks with colorful patterns and designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balaclava - a close-fitting covering for the whole head and neck, leaving only parts of the face exposed
  • synonym: respirator - a mask designed to protect the wearer from inhaling harmful substances
