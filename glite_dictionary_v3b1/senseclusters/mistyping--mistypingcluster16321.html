mistyping

noun
typing error
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪsˈtaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of pressing the wrong keys or making errors while typing on a keyboard

Examples

A mistyping error
A single mistyping in your email address can prevent you from receiving important messages.
He blamed the error on a mistyping rather than a misunderstanding of the instructions.
Frequent mistyping can slow down your work and cause frustration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correction - the act of fixing a mistake
  • synonym: typo - a small mistake made in typed or printed text
