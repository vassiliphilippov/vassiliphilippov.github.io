Synonyms

  • worldwide
    2.1 16/m
    worldwide describes something happening everywhere across the world, while globally sounds more formal and often appears in reports.
  • all over the world
    1.0 11/m
    all over the world is a common spoken phrase for many countries, while globally is a shorter, more formal adverb.
  • around the world
    1.0 33/m
    around the world often suggests movement or many countries in casual talk, while globally focuses on wide, formal effect.
  • world over
    3.8 2.04/m
    world over sounds slightly old-fashioned or literary, while globally is modern and common in international or scientific language.
  • planetwide
    3.7 0.07/m
    planetwide feels rare and technical, while globally is standard and widely used to talk about effects across the world.
