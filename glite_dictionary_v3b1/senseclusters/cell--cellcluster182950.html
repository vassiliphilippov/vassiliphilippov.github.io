cell

verb
to divide into compartments
Pronunciation: / sɛl / (AmE), / sɛl / (BrE)
to divide or partition something into cells or small enclosed compartments

Examples

To cell a space
The architect celled the open-plan workshop into smaller studios to give artists private spaces.
To improve organization, the warehouse manager celled the storage area into separate sections.
The old building was celled to create private offices for each employee.

Related Terms

  • antonym: combine - to join or merge together
  • synonym: partition - to divide into parts or sections
  • synonym: separate - to set or keep apart
