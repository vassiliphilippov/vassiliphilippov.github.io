pro-Beijing

adjective
supporting the Chinese Communist Party
Pronunciation: / proʊ bəˈdʒeɪ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / prəʊ bɪˈdʒeɪ.ɪŋ / (BrE)
politically supporting or aligned with the Chinese Communist Party, especially in the context of Hong Kong and China.

Examples

Pro-Beijing groups
Many pro-Beijing groups organized rallies in support of the proposed legislation.
She is widely regarded as one of the most influential pro-Beijing politicians in the region.
The government was criticized for its appointment of several pro-Beijing officials.
