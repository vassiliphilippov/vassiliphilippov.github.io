vectorial

adjective
involving vectors
Pronunciation: / vɛkˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / vɛkˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving vectors, which have both direction and magnitude

Examples

Vectorial force analysis
The vectorial representation of force helps students understand its direction and strength.
In physics, many problems require a vectorial analysis rather than a simple calculation.
The vectorial sum of the two velocities determines the object's final movement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: scalar - relating to quantities that have only magnitude, not direction
