anglewing

noun
a butterfly with angular notched forewings
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəlˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡəlˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
A nymphalid butterfly characterized by angular notches on the outer edges of its forewings.

Examples

An anglewing butterfly
Certain types of anglewing butterflies are commonly found in North American forests.
The anglewing is easily recognized by the sharp notches on its forewings.
We spotted an anglewing resting on a tree trunk during our hike.
