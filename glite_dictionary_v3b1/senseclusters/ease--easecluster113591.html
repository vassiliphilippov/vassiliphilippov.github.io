ease

verb
move gently or carefully
2.4 8.67/m
Pronunciation: / iÀêz / (AmE), / iÀêz / (BrE)
to move gently or carefully, often to avoid sudden movement or to decrease discomfort

Examples

To ease into place
He eased himself into the chair after a long day.
She slowly eased the door open, trying not to make a sound.
The nurse eased the patient onto the bed to prevent pain.

Related Terms
