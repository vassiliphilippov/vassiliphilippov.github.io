archvillain

noun
main enemy
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtʃˌvɪlən / (AmE), / ˈɑːtʃˌvɪlən / (BrE)
the main or most powerful enemy, especially in stories, comics, or films

Examples

The archvillain appears

Every superhero needs an archvillain to test their strength and courage.
