headhunt

noun
recruiting for top jobs
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌhʌnt / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌhʌnt / (BrE)
the act or process of actively seeking and recruiting someone, especially for a senior or specialist position

Examples

Executive headhunt
The company launched a headhunt to find a new chief executive officer.
After a global headhunt, they finally hired the perfect candidate for the role.
A successful headhunt can bring fresh talent and ideas to an organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headhunting - the practice of finding and recruiting suitable people for senior jobs
  • synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and hiring new employees
