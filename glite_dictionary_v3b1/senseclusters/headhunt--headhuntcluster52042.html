headhunt

noun
recruiting for top jobs
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.hʌnt/ (AmE), /ˈhed.hʌnt/ (BrE)
the act or process of actively seeking and recruiting someone, especially for a senior or specialist position

Examples

Executive headhunt

After a global headhunt, they finally hired the perfect candidate for the role.
A successful headhunt can bring fresh talent and ideas to an organization.
