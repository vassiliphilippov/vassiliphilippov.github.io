sheesh

interjection
exclamation of amazement
Pronunciation: / ʃiːʃ / (AmE), / ʃiːʃ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to show strong feelings such as surprise, amazement, admiration, or annoyance

Examples

Sheesh expression
When he saw the final score, he just said, "sheesh, that was a close game!"
After seeing her friend's new car, she exclaimed, "sheesh, that's amazing!"
He looked at the mountain of paperwork and muttered, "sheesh, this is going to take all day."
Sheesh, I can't believe how much homework we have tonight.
He forgot his keys again? Sheesh, that's the third time this week.
When I saw the price of the tickets, all I could say was sheesh.
