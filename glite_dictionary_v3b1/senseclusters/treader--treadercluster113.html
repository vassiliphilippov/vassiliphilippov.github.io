treader

noun
one who steps
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛdər / (AmE), / ˈtrɛdə / (BrE)
a person or thing that treads or steps on something, especially by walking over it

Examples

A treader on path
The muddy path showed the marks of every treader who passed by.
Each treader left footprints in the fresh snow along the trail.
The old floor creaked under the weight of a heavy treader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stomper - a person who steps heavily
  • synonym: walker - a person who walks
