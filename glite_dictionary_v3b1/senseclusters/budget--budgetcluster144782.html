budget

noun
plan of income and expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌdʒət /; / ˈbʌdʒ.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʌdʒɪt /; / ˈbʌdʒ.ɪt / (BrE)
a plan for how much money will be spent and received over a period of time, or the amount of money available for a particular purpose

Examples

A family budget
Every household should have a budget to manage their expenses efficiently.
The finance committee approved the budget for the coming year after much debate.
The school's budget was reduced due to funding cuts.
Our research department operates on a tight budget to limit expenses.
The festival was canceled because they could not secure the necessary budget.
You need to prepare a monthly budget for your household expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allowance - an amount of money set aside regularly for a purpose
  • synonym: fund - a sum of money saved or made available for a particular purpose
  • antonym: overspend - to spend more money than is available in a budget
  • synonym: spending plan - a plan for how money will be spent
