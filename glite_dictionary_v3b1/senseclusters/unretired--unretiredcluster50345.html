unretired

adjective
still working
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈtaɪrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
not having retired; still active in work or a particular role, especially after the usual retirement age.

Examples

Unretired business owner
Despite his age, he remained unretired and continued to run his business.
Many athletes become unretired when they return to competition after announcing retirement.
She is one of the few professors who are still unretired at seventy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: active - still engaged in work or activity
  • antonym: retired - having left one's job or profession permanently
