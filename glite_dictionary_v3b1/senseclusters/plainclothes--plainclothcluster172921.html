plainclothes

noun
undercover police officer
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnˌkloʊðz / (AmE), / ˈpleɪnˌkləʊðz / (BrE)
a police officer or detective who does not wear a uniform in order to avoid being recognized

Examples

A plainclothes detective
A plainclothes followed the suspect through the crowded market without drawing attention.
The plainclothes blended in with the crowd to observe the situation discreetly.
After the incident, several plainclothes arrived to question witnesses at the scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undercover - working secretly to gather information
  • antonym: uniformed officer - a police officer wearing an official uniform
