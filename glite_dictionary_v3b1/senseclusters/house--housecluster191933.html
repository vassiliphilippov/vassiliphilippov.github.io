house

noun
a building where people live
1.0 436/m
Pronunciation: / ha äs /; / hus / (AmE), / ha äs /; / huÀês / (BrE)
A building for people to live in, especially one that is intended for a single family.

Examples

A family house
Jane invited all her friends to a party at her house.
The house was painted a bright yellow color.
They finally bought a new house in the suburbs.
They invited us into their cozy hoose for tea and biscuits.
The old stone hoose stood at the end of the narrow lane.
Every summer, the family gathered at the ancestral hoose in the Highlands.

Synonyms

  • dwelling
    2.9 7.92/m
    a dwelling is any place where people live, including apartments or flats, while a house is a single-family building.
  • residence
    2.5 14/m
    a residence is a formal or long-term home often named in papers, while a house is an ordinary single-family building.
