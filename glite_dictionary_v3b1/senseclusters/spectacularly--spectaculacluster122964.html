spectacularly

adverb
in an impressive or dramatic manner
Pronunciation: / spɛkˈtækjələrli / (AmE), / spɛkˈtakjʊləli / (BrE)
In a way that is very impressive, exciting, or dramatic.

Examples

To perform spectacularly
Her performance was spectacularly good, earning a standing ovation from the entire audience.
The fireworks exploded spectacularly above the city skyline.
The plan failed spectacularly, costing the company millions in losses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impressively - in an impressive manner
  • antonym: modestly - in a modest manner, not spectacularly
