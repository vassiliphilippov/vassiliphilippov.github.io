overflowing

adjective
spilling over with liquid; flooded
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈfloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈfləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a place or container) so full that the contents, especially liquid, spill over or cover the inside and surroundings.

Examples

Overflowing with water
After the storm, several roads were closed because they were overflowing with water.
The bathtub was overflowing, and water spilled onto the floor.
The river was overflowing after several days of heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - free from moisture or liquid
  • synonym: flooded - covered or filled with water, especially in excess
