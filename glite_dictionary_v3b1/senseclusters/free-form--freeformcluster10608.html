free-form

adjective
without fixed structure
Pronunciation: / ˈfriː fɔːrm / (AmE), / ˈfriː fɔːm / (BrE)
not following fixed rules, structure, or patterns; open and creative in style or form

Examples

Free-form dance
The dancers performed a free-form routine that allowed for personal expression.
Her free-form writing style breaks away from traditional grammar and structure.
In the workshop, participants created free-form designs using clay and paint.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rigid - not flexible or adaptable; strictly fixed
  • synonym: unstructured - not organized in a fixed or regular way
