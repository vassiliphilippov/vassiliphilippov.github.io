overt

adjective
openly visible; not hidden
5.1 7.00/m
Pronunciation: / o äÀàv…úrt / (AmE), / …ô äÀàv…úÀêt / (BrE)
done or shown openly; not hidden, secret, or concealed

Examples

An overt action
She showed overt affection for her children, hugging them in public.
The company made an overt attempt to seek public approval for its new policy.
There was overt hostility between the two rival teams during the match.

Synonyms

  • undisguised
    4.7 0.34/m
    undisguised is clearly obvious and shows no attempt to hide, while overt is a more formal word for actions done openly.
  • open
    1.0 42/m
    open describes a person who is honest or ready to share, while overt describes actions shown openly, not hidden.
