Ionic dialect

noun
ancient Greek dialect of Ionia and Attica
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈɑnɪk ˈdaɪəˌlɛkt / (AmE), / aɪˈɒnɪk ˈdaɪəlɛkt / (BrE)
the ancient Greek dialect spoken and written in Ionia and later in Athens and Attica

Examples

Ionic dialect of Greece
Homer's epics were written in a form of the Ionic dialect.
Many inscriptions from ancient Asia Minor are in the Ionic dialect.
Scholars often study the Ionic dialect to better understand early Greek literature.
