toe box

noun
front part of a shoe for the toes
2.6
Pronunciation: / toʊ bɑks / (AmE), / təʊ bɒks / (BrE)
the section at the front of a shoe or boot that encloses and protects the toes

Examples

A toe box section
A reinforced toe box helps protect your feet during long hikes.
If your toe box is too narrow, it can cause blisters and discomfort.
Some running shoes have a wide toe box to allow toes more movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: upper - the part of a shoe covering the foot above the sole
