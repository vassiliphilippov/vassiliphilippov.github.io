Ursuline

adjective
of Ursuline order
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrsəˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːsjuːlaɪn / (BrE)
relating to the Ursuline religious order, its members, institutions, or traditions

Examples

Ursuline tradition
The Ursuline school was known for its high academic standards and community service.
She attended an Ursuline convent during her childhood in France.
Many Ursuline institutions focus on the education of young women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
