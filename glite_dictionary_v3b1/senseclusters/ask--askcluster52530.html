Synonyms

  • question
    1.0 22/m
    question means putting specific questions to someone to get facts or test knowledge, while ask is the general action of seeking an answer.
  • inquire
    3.0 9.46/m
    inquire sounds more formal and is used in polite or official situations to request information, while ask is more general everyday speech.
  • query
    3.7 2.54/m
    query is often used to show doubt or in technical requests for data, while ask is a plain, general way to seek information.
