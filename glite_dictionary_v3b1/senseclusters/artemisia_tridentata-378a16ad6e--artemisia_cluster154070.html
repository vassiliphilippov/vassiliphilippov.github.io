Artemisia tridentata

noun
aromatic shrub of western North America
Pronunciation: / ɑːr.təˈmɪz.i.ə traɪˈdɛn.teɪ.tə / (AmE), / ˌɑː.tɪˈmɪz.i.ə traɪˈden.teɪ.tə / (BrE)
a large, aromatic shrub native to arid regions of western North America, with silvery-grey, three-lobed leaves, commonly known as big sagebrush
