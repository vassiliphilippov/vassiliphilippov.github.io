powder-post termite

noun
highly destructive dry-wood termite
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊ.dɚ poʊst ˈtɝː.maɪt / (AmE), / ˈpaʊ.də pəʊst ˈtɜː.maɪt / (BrE)
a species of dry-wood termite that causes severe damage to wood structures in warm regions by boring into and consuming dry wood

Examples

A powder-post termite infestation
A powder-post termite infestation can quickly weaken wooden beams in houses.
Pest control experts found evidence of powder-post termites inside the antique furniture.
Residents in tropical regions often protect their homes from powder-post termite damage.
