nonpunctual

adjective
habitually late
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpʌŋktʃuəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈpʌŋktjʊəl / (BrE)
not arriving, happening, or doing something at the agreed or expected time; habitually late

Examples

Nonpunctual student
His nonpunctual behavior often caused meetings to start late.
Being nonpunctual can create a bad impression at work.
The train service is known for its nonpunctual arrivals during winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: late - arriving or happening after the expected time
  • antonym: punctual - arriving or happening at the expected or agreed time
