unfabulous

adjective
not impressive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæbjələs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfæbjʊləs / (BrE)
not wonderful, impressive, or exciting; ordinary or unremarkable

Examples

An unfabulous party
The party was rather unfabulous and ended much earlier than we expected.
Her performance was surprisingly unfabulous given her usual talent and energy.
Despite the hype, the new restaurant turned out to be quite unfabulous.
The documentary gave an unfabulous account of the scientist's daily routine.
Her explanation was refreshingly unfabulous, sticking closely to the facts.
He preferred an unfabulous lifestyle, avoiding drama and extravagance.
