scratcher

noun
device used for scratching
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskrætʃər / (AmE), / ˈskrætʃə / (BrE)
a device used for scratching a surface, especially the body or an animal's object

Examples

A back scratcher
A back scratcher is handy when you can't reach an itch.

The store sells several types of scratchers for pets.
