onsen

noun
Japanese hot spring
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnˌsɛn / (AmE), / ˈɒn.sen / (BrE)
a Japanese hot spring, often with bathing facilities or inns built around it, used for public bathing and relaxation

Examples

A onsen hot spring
During their trip to Japan, they relaxed in a traditional onsen surrounded by mountains.
Many tourists visit an onsen to experience the soothing natural hot-spring waters.
The onsen offered both indoor and outdoor baths with beautiful views of the garden.
After a long day of travel, we relaxed in the outdoor onsen overlooking the mountains.
Many tourists visit Japan to experience a traditional onsen bath.
The hotel offers private onsen rooms for guests seeking extra privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hot spring - a natural spring of hot water
  • synonym: sauna - a small room used as a hot-air or steam bath
  • synonym: spa - a place with mineral springs used for bathing and relaxation
