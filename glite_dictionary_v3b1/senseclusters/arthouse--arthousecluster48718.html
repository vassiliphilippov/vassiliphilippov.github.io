arthouse

adjective
experimental or artistic
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrtˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɑːtˌhaʊs / (BrE)
relating to films or works that are unconventional, experimental, or intended for a limited, artistic audience rather than mass commercial appeal

Examples

Arthouse film festival
The festival featured several arthouse films that challenged traditional storytelling.
She prefers arthouse cinema because it often explores deeper themes and emotions.
The director is known for his arthouse style, which appeals to a niche audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not produced by a major studio; outside the mainstream
  • antonym: mainstream - intended for a wide, general audience
