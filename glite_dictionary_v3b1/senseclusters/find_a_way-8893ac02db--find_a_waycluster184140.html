find a way

verb
devise a solution or method
1.0
Pronunciation: / faɪnd ə weɪ / (AmE), / faɪnd ə weɪ / (BrE)
to discover or devise a method or solution for overcoming a problem or achieving something, especially when it is difficult

Examples

To find a way forward
Despite many setbacks, she was determined to find a way to finish the project.
Scientists are racing to find a way to treat the new disease.
When the main entrance was blocked, we had to find a way around it.
