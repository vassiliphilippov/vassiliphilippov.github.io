adventitiously

adverb
by chance
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.vɛnˈtɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌæd.vɛnˈtɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)

Examples

Seeds spread adventitiously

Some traditions develop adventitiously rather than through careful planning.
The new species was introduced adventitiously to the island by visiting ships.
