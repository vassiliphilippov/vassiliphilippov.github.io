adventitiously

adverb
by chance
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæd.vɛnˈtɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌæd.vɛnˈtɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
in a way that happens by chance or comes from an external or unexpected source

Examples

Seeds spread adventitiously
The wildflowers appeared adventitiously in the garden after a storm scattered their seeds.
Some traditions develop adventitiously rather than through careful planning.
The new species was introduced adventitiously to the island by visiting ships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accidentally - in a way that is not planned or intended
  • antonym: deliberately - in a way that is planned or intended
