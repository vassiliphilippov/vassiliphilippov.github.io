unfarmed

adjective
not cultivated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɑrmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɑːmd / (BrE)
not used for farming or agricultural purposes

Examples

An unfarmed field
Much of the land in the region remains unfarmed.
The unfarmed fields were covered in wildflowers and tall grass.
They purchased several acres of unfarmed land to start a new project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cultivated - prepared and used for growing crops
  • synonym: untouched - not changed or affected by people
