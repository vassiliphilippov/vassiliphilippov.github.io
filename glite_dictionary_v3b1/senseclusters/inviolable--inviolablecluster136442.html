Synonyms

    9.1 1.02/m
    sacrosanct is treated as sacred and not to be touched in belief or practice, while inviolable describes something that must not be broken.
  • inviolate
    9.6 0.34/m
    inviolate means kept pure, left untouched, or unharmed, while inviolable means protected as too important to be broken.
  • inalienable
    8.0 2.22/m
    inalienable refers to rights that cannot be taken away, while inviolable covers sacred rules or things that must not be infringed.
  • infrangible
    9.9 0.00/m
    infrangible means literally impossible to break or damage, while inviolable often means morally or legally protected from being broken.
