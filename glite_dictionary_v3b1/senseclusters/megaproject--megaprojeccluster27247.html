megaproject

noun
large-scale project
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡəˌprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
a very large and complex project, typically involving significant investment, resources, and long-term planning

Examples

A megaproject plan
The government announced a new megaproject to build a high-speed rail network across the country.
Many megaprojects face delays and cost overruns due to their complexity.
The construction of the massive dam was considered the region's most ambitious megaproject in decades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiative - a new plan or process to achieve something or solve a problem
  • antonym: minor project - a small or less complex project
  • synonym: undertaking - a task or project, especially one that is large or difficult
