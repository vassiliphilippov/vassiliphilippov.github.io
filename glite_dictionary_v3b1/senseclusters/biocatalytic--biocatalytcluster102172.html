biocatalytic

adjective
relating to biocatalysts or their action
3.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.kæt.ə.ˈlɪt.ɪk/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.kæt.ə.ˈlɪt.ɪk/ (BrE)
