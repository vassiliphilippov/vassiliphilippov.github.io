contagious laugh

noun
a laugh that spreads to others
4.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəs læf / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs lɑːf / (BrE)
A laugh that easily causes others to start laughing or feel joyful.

Examples

A contagious laugh
Her contagious laugh made even the shyest people in the room start giggling.
The comedian's contagious laugh filled the entire theater with joy.
You can't help but smile when you hear his contagious laugh.
