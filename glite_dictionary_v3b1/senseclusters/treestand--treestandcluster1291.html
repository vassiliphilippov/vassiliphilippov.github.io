treestand

noun
hunting platform in tree
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːˌstænd / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌstænd / (BrE)
a platform attached high in a tree, used by hunters to observe or hunt wildlife from above

Examples

Hunting treestand
The hunter climbed into his treestand before sunrise to wait for deer.
From the treestand, she had a clear view of the entire clearing below.
He spent hours in the treestand, hoping to spot a wild turkey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blind - a shelter used by hunters to conceal themselves from wildlife
