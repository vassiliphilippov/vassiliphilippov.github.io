mushroom party

noun
party where people take psychedelic mushrooms
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌ.ʃruːm ˈpɑːr.tiː/ /; / /ˈmʌ.ʃrʊm ˈpɑːr.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈmʌ.ʃrʊm ˈpɑː.tiː/ /; / /ˈmʌ.ʃruːm ˈpɑː.tiː/ (BrE)
