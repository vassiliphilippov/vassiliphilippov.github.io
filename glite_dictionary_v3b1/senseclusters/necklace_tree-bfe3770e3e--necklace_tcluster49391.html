necklace tree

noun
tree with seeds used as beads
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛk.ləs triː / (AmE), / ˈnɛk.lɪs triː / (BrE)
a tree of the genus Ormosia, whose brightly colored seeds are often used as beads in necklaces and other jewelry

The necklace tree seeds
