beefy

adjective
muscular and heavily built
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːfi / (AmE), / ˈbiːfi / (BrE)
having a large, muscular, and heavily built body; strong and robust in appearance.

Examples

A beefy man
Despite his beefy appearance, he was incredibly gentle with small children.
She asked her beefy brother to help move the heavy table across the room.
The beefy security guard stood by the entrance, watching everyone who walked in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burly - large and strong; heavily built
  • antonym: scrawny - unattractively thin and bony
