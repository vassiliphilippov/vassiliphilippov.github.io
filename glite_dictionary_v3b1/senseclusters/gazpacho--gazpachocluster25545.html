gazpacho

noun
cold Spanish vegetable soup
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈspɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ɡæzˈpætʃəʊ / (BrE)
A cold Spanish soup made from blended raw vegetables, especially tomatoes, onions, cucumbers, and peppers.

Examples

A bowl of gazpacho
On a hot day, nothing is more refreshing than a bowl of gazpacho.
The chef added extra herbs to the gazpacho for a unique and vibrant flavor.
We enjoyed homemade gazpacho as an appetizer before the main course.
