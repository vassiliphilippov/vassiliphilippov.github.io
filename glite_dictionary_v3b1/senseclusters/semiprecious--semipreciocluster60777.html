semiprecious

adjective
of lesser-value gemstones
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈprɛʃəs, ˌsɛmaɪˈprɛʃəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈprɛʃəs, ˌsɛmaɪˈprɛʃəs / (BrE)
(of a gemstone) having less commercial value than precious stones such as diamonds, rubies, sapphires, and emeralds.

Examples

Semiprecious gemstones
Many artisans create jewelry from semiprecious gems like garnet and tourmaline.
She wore a necklace made of semiprecious stones such as amethyst and jade.
The museum displays featured both semiprecious and precious stones from around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: precious - (of stones) of high value and rarity
