potage

noun
thick, creamy soup (often French)
8.2
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtɑːʒ / (AmE), / pɒˈtɑːʒ / (BrE)
a thick, often creamy soup, especially one of French origin made from meat, vegetables, or both, pureed or left chunky

Examples

A potage soup
She learned to prepare a classic French potage during her culinary studies.
The restaurant served a delicious potage made with leeks and potatoes.
Would you like a bowl of potage before the main course?

Related Terms

  • degree lower: broth - liquid in which meat, fish, or vegetables have been cooked
  • synonym: soup - liquid food made by boiling meat, vegetables, etc.
