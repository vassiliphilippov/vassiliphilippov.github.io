cosmographist

noun
expert in cosmography
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːzməˈɡræfɪst / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməˈɡræfɪst / (BrE)
a person who is knowledgeable about or specializes in cosmography, the science that describes the general features of the universe.

Examples

A cosmographist studies universe
As a cosmographist, she contributed greatly to early maps and models of the cosmos.
Centuries ago, every royal court sought the advice of a skilled cosmographist for navigation and exploration.
The renowned cosmographist presented new theories about the structure of the universe at the conference.
