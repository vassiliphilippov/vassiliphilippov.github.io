heritability

noun
genetic transmission extent
8.1
Pronunciation: /hə.rɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /he.rɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /hə.rɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /he.rɪ.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the extent to which a trait or characteristic can be passed from parents to offspring through genes
