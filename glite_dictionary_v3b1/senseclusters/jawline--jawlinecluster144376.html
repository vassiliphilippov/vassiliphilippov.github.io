jawline

noun
lower jaw contour
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːlaɪn / (BrE)
the outline or shape formed by the lower edge of the jaw, especially as seen from the side

Examples

The model's strong jawline made her stand out in the photographs.

Artists often emphasize the jawline when drawing portraits to capture a person's features.
Many models are admired for their sharp jawline and defined facial features.
