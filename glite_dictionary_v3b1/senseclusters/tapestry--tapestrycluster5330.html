tapestry

noun
decorative woven wall hanging
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpəstri /; / ˈtæp.ə.stri / (AmE), / ˈtæpɪstri /; / ˈtæp.ɪ.stri / (BrE)
a thick, decorative fabric with designs or pictures woven or embroidered into it, often used as a wall hanging or for furniture
