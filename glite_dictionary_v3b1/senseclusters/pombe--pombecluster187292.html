pombe

noun
East African beer
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊmbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɒmbeɪ / (BrE)
a traditional East African alcoholic drink made by fermenting grains or fruit

Examples

Homemade pombe drink
During the celebration, villagers shared homemade pombe with their guests.
He learned how to brew pombe using millet and bananas from his grandmother.
Tourists were invited to taste the local pombe at the cultural festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beer - an alcoholic drink made from fermented grains
  • synonym: wine - an alcoholic drink made from fermented fruit, usually grapes
