metafilter

noun
filter of filters
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌfɪltə / (BrE)
a tool or method that processes or combines the outputs of other filters to produce a final result

Examples

A metafilter tool
The metafilter improved the quality of the processed image by combining several filtering techniques.
Developers used a metafilter to refine the search results from multiple sources.
By applying a metafilter, the system was able to remove more noise from the data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregator - a tool that collects and combines information from multiple sources
  • degree lower: filter - a device or process that removes unwanted parts from something
