rue

noun
French word for a street or road
Pronunciation: / ruː / (AmE), / ruː / (BrE)
A street or road, especially in France or French-speaking countries, often used in the names of streets.

Examples

A Paris rue
She strolled along the charming rue, admiring the old buildings.
The café sits on a busy rue in the center of Paris.
We stayed in a small hotel on the rue de Rivoli.
