luminist

adjective
emphasizing light effects
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈluː.mɪ.nɪst/ /; / /ˈluː.mə.nɪst/ (AmE), /ˈluː.mɪ.nɪst/ /; / /ˈluː.mə.nɪst/ (BrE)
relating to or characterized by an emphasis on the effects of light, especially in art or photography

Examples

Luminist style painting
The artist's luminist paintings capture the delicate changes of sunlight on water.
Critics praised the luminist style of her landscape photographs.
He adopted a luminist approach to highlight the interplay of light and shadow in his work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impressionist - relating to Impressionism, an art style focusing on light and color
  • synonym: luminous - emitting or reflecting light
