flag smut

noun
fungal disease of cereals and grasses
Pronunciation: / flæɡ smʌt / (AmE), / flæɡ smʌt / (BrE)
A fungal disease affecting the leaves and stems of cereals and other grasses, characterized by dark, powdery streaks or patches.

Examples

Flag smut disease
Farmers are advised to use resistant varieties to prevent flag smut.
Symptoms of flag smut include black streaks on the leaves and stems.
The wheat crop was badly damaged by flag smut this season.
