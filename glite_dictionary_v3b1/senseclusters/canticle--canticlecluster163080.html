canticle

noun
hymn or song from the Bible
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈkæn.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a hymn or song of praise taken from the Bible, often used in Christian liturgical services.

Examples

A canticle of praise
During the service, the choir sang a beautiful canticle from the Book of Psalms.
Many Christian traditions include a canticle as part of their daily worship.
The priest selected a canticle to conclude the morning prayers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hymn - a religious song or poem of praise to God or a god
