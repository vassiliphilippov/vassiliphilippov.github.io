bad fairy

noun
harmful or malicious fairy
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈfɛri / (AmE), / bæd ˈfeəri / (BrE)
a fairy in stories or folklore who intends to cause harm or mischief

Examples

A bad fairy story
Children dressed as a bad fairy for the costume contest at school.
In the tale, a bad fairy cast a curse on the newborn princess.
The presence of a bad fairy changed the mood of the magical forest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good fairy - a fairy that brings good fortune or helps people
