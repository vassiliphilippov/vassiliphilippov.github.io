supplicator

noun
person who begs humbly
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌplɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsʌplɪˌkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who humbly asks for something, especially in prayer or as a plea

Examples

Praying supplicator
The supplicator knelt before the altar, whispering a quiet prayer for forgiveness.
As a humble supplicator, she pleaded for help from the village elders.
The king listened patiently to each supplicator who came to his court seeking justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beggar - a person who asks others for money or help
  • synonym: petitioner - a person who formally asks for something
