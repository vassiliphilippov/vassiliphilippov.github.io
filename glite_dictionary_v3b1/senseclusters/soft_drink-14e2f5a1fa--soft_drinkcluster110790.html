soft drink

noun
nonalcoholic carbonated beverage
Pronunciation: / sɑft drɪŋk / (AmE), / sɒft drɪŋk / (BrE)
a nonalcoholic drink, typically one that is carbonated, such as soda or lemonade

Examples

A soft drink bottle
Could I get a soft drink with my meal, please?
She prefers to order a soft drink rather than coffee at lunch.
We stocked the fridge with several types of soft drinks for the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soda - a sweet carbonated soft drink
