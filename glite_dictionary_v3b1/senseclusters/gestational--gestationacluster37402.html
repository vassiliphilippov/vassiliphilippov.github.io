gestational

adjective
relating to pregnancy or gestation
Pronunciation: / dʒɛˈsteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / dʒɛˈsteɪʃ(ə)nəl / (BrE)
relating to the period during which an embryo develops within the womb; pertaining to gestation

Examples

Gestational diabetes
Certain conditions, such as gestational diabetes, can affect women during pregnancy.
Her gestational progress was tracked with regular ultrasounds.
The doctor monitored the baby's gestational age to ensure healthy development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gestation - the process of carrying or being carried in the womb between conception and birth
