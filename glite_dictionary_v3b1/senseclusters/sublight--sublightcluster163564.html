sublight

adjective
slower than light
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌlaɪt / (BrE)
relating to speeds that are slower than the speed of light

Examples

Sublight travel
The probe was designed for sublight travel between planets in our solar system.
At sublight speeds, journeys to distant stars would take many years.
Scientists discussed the challenges of maintaining life support on a sublight spacecraft.

Related Terms

  • antonym: superluminal - faster than the speed of light
