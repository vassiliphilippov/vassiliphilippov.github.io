planum

noun
planetary plateau
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪnəm / (AmE), / ˈplɑːnəm / (BrE)
a broad, flat plain or plateau on the surface of a planet or moon, especially in planetary geology

Examples

Martian planum
The rover landed safely on the vast planum near the planet's equator.
Scientists studied the geological features of the icy planum on Jupiter's moon.
A new crater was discovered on the southern edge of the Martian planum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - a large area of flat land with few trees
  • synonym: plateau - an area of flat, elevated land
