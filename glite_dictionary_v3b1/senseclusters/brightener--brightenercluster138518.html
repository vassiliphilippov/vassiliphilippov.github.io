brightener

noun
chemical for shiny finish
6.5

a chemical additive used in metal finishing or plating to create a smooth, shiny, and reflective surface

Examples

Metal brightener
The technician added a brightener to the solution to improve the appearance of the metal parts.
Using a brightener in the plating process results in a more reflective and attractive finish.
Some brighteners are specially formulated for use with chrome to achieve a mirror-like surface.
