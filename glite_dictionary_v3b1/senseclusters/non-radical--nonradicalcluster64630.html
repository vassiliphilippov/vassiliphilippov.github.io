non-radical

adjective
not extreme
4.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrædɪkəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈrædɪkəl / (BrE)
not extreme or not advocating for major or fundamental change

Examples

Non-radical reform
The committee chose a non-radical approach to reform, preferring gradual changes.
His non-radical opinions made him popular among more traditional members of the group.
Many voters are looking for non-radical solutions to the country's economic problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moderate - not extreme in views or actions
  • antonym: radical - favoring major or fundamental change
