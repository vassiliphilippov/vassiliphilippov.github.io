fail

noun
embarrassing mistake (slang)
8.2
Pronunciation: /feɪl/ (AmE), /feɪl/ (BrE)
(informal, slang) a conspicuous, often embarrassing mistake, error, or unsuccessful action, especially as noted or shared online.

Examples

A big fail
The magician's trick was a total fail when the rabbit didn't appear on stage.
They posted the video and the internet laughed at the epic fail within hours.
After the press conference, reporters called the policy rollout a public fail.
