convinced

adjective
completely certain or persuaded
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɪnst / (AmE), / kənˈvɪnst / (BrE)
completely certain or having a strong belief about something

Examples

Fully convinced of truth
After reading all the evidence, the jury was finally convinced of his innocence.
I am not convinced by his explanation.
She seemed absolutely convinced that her plan would succeed.
After years of research, he became convinced of the theory's truth.
She is a convinced supporter of environmental causes.
The convinced atheist refused to participate in religious ceremonies.
