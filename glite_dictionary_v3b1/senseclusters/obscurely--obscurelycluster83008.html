obscurely

adverb
in an unclear or hard-to-understand way
6.1
Pronunciation: / əbˈskjʊrli / (AmE), / əbˈskjʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that is not clear or is difficult to understand; in an unclear or ambiguous manner.

Examples

To speak obscurely
Many passages in the play are obscurely constructed and open to interpretation.
The instructions were obscurely worded, making them difficult to follow.
The message was obscurely conveyed, so few people understood its true meaning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a clear and easily understandable manner
