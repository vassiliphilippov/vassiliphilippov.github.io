countergift

verb
to reciprocate gift
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌɡɪft / (BrE)
to give a gift in return for one that has been received from someone else

Examples

To countergift a friend
After receiving a beautiful painting, he decided to countergift his friend with a handmade sculpture.
It is common in some cultures to countergift after being given something special.
She wanted to countergift her colleague to show her appreciation for the thoughtful present.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: gift - to give something as a present
  • synonym: reciprocate - to give or do something in return for what has been received
