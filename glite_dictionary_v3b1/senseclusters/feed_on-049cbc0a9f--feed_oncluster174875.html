feed on

verb
be sustained or nourished by
2.1 2.5
Pronunciation: /fiːd ən/ /; / /fiːd ɑːn/ (AmE), /fiːd ɒn/ /; / /fiːd ən/ (BrE)
to eat or be sustained by something, either as food (for animals) or as a source of support or inspiration (figurative)

Examples
