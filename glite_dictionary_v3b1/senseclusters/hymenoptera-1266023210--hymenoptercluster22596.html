Hymenoptera

noun
order of insects including bees, wasps, and ants
Pronunciation: / haɪməˈnɑptərə /; / ˌhaɪməˈnɑptərə / (AmE), / haɪməˈnɒptərə /; / ˌhɪmɪˈnɒptərə / (BrE)
a large order of insects that includes bees, wasps, ants, and related groups, usually with two pairs of thin wings and often a stinger
