stout

adjective
somewhat fat or heavily built (euphemistic)
Pronunciation: / staʊt / (AmE), / staʊt / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a woman) somewhat fat or heavily built, often used as an inoffensive or euphemistic term.

Examples

A stout lady
He described his neighbor as a kindly, stout lady with a warm smile.
In her youth she had been slim, but now she was rather stout.
Though she was a stout woman, she moved with surprising grace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plump - slightly fat in a pleasant way
  • antonym: thin - having little fat on the body
