primogenitor

noun
direct ancestor; earliest known forebear
Pronunciation: / ˌpraɪmoʊˈdʒɛnɪtər / (AmE), / ˌprɪməʊˈdʒɛnɪtə / (BrE)
an ancestor in the direct line, especially the earliest known ancestor of a family

Examples

The primogenitor ancestor
Genealogists traced the primogenitor of the royal line to a medieval nobleman.
In the clan's oral tradition, their primogenitor is always honored at the annual festival.
Legend says that our family's primogenitor arrived on these shores centuries ago.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestor - a person, typically one more remote than a grandparent, from whom one is descended
