Solea

noun
genus of flatfishes (soles)
Pronunciation: / soʊˈliːə / (AmE), / səʊˈliːə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Soleidae, comprising certain species of flatfish known as soles.

Solea flatfish species

The genus Solea includes several important species of flatfish consumed as seafood.
