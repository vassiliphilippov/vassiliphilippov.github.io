viscidity

noun
state of being sticky; stickiness
Pronunciation: / vɪˈsɪdəti / (AmE), / vɪˈsɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being sticky or cohesive; stickiness.

Examples

The viscidity of glue
Certain plants secrete a substance known for its remarkable viscidity.
The viscidity of honey makes it difficult to pour quickly.
The viscidity of the glue ensures a strong bond between surfaces.
