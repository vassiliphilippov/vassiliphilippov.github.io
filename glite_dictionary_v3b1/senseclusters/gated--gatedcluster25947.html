gated

adjective
protected by a gate
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
protected or restricted by a gate or barrier so that only certain people can enter (especially for places like communities or buildings)

Examples

Gated community
They moved into a gated community for extra security and privacy.
The school is gated to ensure that only students and staff can enter.
Access to the park is gated after dark to prevent vandalism.
The community is gated to ensure the safety and privacy of its residents.
Access to the premium content is gated for subscribers only.
They live in a gated neighborhood with security at the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclosed - surrounded or closed off on all sides
  • synonym: exclusive - limited to a particular group and not open to everyone
  • antonym: open - not closed or restricted by a barrier
