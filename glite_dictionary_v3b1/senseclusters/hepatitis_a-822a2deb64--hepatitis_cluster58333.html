A contagious viral infection of the liver, caused by hepatitis A virus, typically spread through ingestion of contaminated food or water, and usually resulting in acute but mild illness.

Examples

Hepatitis A infection
Doctors recommend handwashing to reduce the risk of hepatitis A infection.
Many travelers get vaccinated against hepatitis A before visiting new countries.
Outbreaks of hepatitis A are often linked to contaminated water supplies.
