hunky

noun
offensive ethnic slur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋki / (BrE)
an offensive, dated term for a person from Hungary or of Hungarian descent, especially used as a slur against Eastern European immigrants

Examples

An old hunky slur

Historians note that hunky reflected prejudice against Hungarian and other Eastern European workers.
