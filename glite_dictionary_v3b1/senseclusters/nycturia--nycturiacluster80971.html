nycturia

noun
excessive urination at night
Pronunciation: / nɪkˈtʊriə / (AmE), / nɪkˈtjʊəriə / (BrE)
A medical condition characterized by excessive urination at night, especially common in older adults.

Examples

Nycturia at night
Older men are particularly susceptible to nycturia due to prostate issues.
The patient reported frequent episodes of nycturia, waking up several times each night to urinate.
Treating underlying conditions like diabetes can help reduce nycturia in some patients.
