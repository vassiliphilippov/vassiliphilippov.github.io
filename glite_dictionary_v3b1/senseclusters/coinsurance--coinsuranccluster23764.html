coinsurance

noun

In health insurance, the portion of covered medical costs that the insured must pay, usually expressed as a percentage, after any deductible has been met.

Examples

Health coinsurance payment
Her coinsurance for hospital bills was 20% after meeting the deductible.
Before surgery he checked that his coinsurance rate would not make treatment unaffordable.
The plan's high deductible and 30% coinsurance meant she paid much more out-of-pocket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cost-sharing - an arrangement in which patients and insurers split health-care costs
  • related: deductible - the amount a policyholder must pay before the insurer begins to pay
