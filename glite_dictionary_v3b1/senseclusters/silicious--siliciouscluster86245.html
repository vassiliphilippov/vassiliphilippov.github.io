silicious

adjective
containing or resembling silica
3.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈlɪʃəs / (AmE), / sɪˈlɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or resembling silica; composed mainly of silica.

Examples

Silicious rock
Many forms of sandstone are considered silicious because they are composed primarily of quartz grains.
Some marine sponges have silicious skeletons that give them structural strength.
The rock sample was identified as silicious due to its high silica content.
