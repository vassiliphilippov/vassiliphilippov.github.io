challengingly

adverb
in a difficult way
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that provokes a response, dares someone, or presents a challenge

Examples

Look challengingly
She stared challengingly at her opponent, refusing to back down.
The teacher spoke challengingly to encourage the students to think more deeply.
He completed the puzzle challengingly, determined to prove his skills.
She looked at him challengingly, daring him to disagree with her decision.
The student answered the teacher's question challengingly, not afraid to express his own opinion.
He stood challengingly in front of the crowd, ready to defend his beliefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defiantly - in a way that shows resistance or bold disobedience
  • synonym: difficultly - in a way that is hard to do or understand
  • antonym: easily - in a way that is not difficult
  • synonym: provocatively - in a way that provokes a reaction or response
  • antonym: submissively - in a way that shows willingness to obey or yield
