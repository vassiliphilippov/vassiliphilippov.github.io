pyriform

adjective
pear-shaped
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪrɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈpɪrɪfɔːm / (BrE)
having the shape of a pear; pear-shaped

Examples

Pyriform shape
The scientist described the egg as pyriform due to its distinctive shape.
Some fruits, like avocados, are naturally pyriform rather than round.
The vase had a pyriform body that widened at the base and narrowed at the top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pear-shaped - having the shape of a pear
  • antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere
