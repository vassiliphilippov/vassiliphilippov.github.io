estimate

noun
cost statement; likely cost of a job or service
4.3 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈes.tə.mət/ /; / /ˈes.tə.meɪt/ (AmE), /ˈes.tɪ.mət/ /; / /ˈes.tɪ.meɪt/ (BrE)
a written or formal statement of the likely cost or value of something, especially for a job, service, or insurance

Examples

A written estimate
His estimate for painting the house was surprisingly affordable.
The mechanic provided an estimate before starting any repairs.
We asked three contractors for an estimate on the kitchen remodel.
I requested an estimate from the auto shop to submit to my insurance company.
The contractor gave us an estimate for fixing the roof before starting the work.
The tax officer reviewed the estimate to assess the property's value.
