nonsocialist

adjective
not socialist
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈsoʊʃəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəlɪst / (BrE)
not supporting, relating to, or based on socialism or socialist principles

Examples

Nonsocialist government
The new government introduced nonsocialist policies to encourage private business.
Many voters preferred a nonsocialist candidate in the recent election.
The organization is known for its nonsocialist stance on economic issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capitalist - supporting or relating to capitalism
  • antonym: socialist - supporting or relating to socialism
