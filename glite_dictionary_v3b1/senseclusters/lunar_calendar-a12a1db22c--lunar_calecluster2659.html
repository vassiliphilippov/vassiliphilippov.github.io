lunar calendar

noun
calendar based on the moon's cycles
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnɚ ˈkælənˌdɚ / (AmE), / ˈluːnə ˈkælɪndə / (BrE)
A calendar system in which months are determined by the cycles of the moon.

Examples

The lunar calendar system
Farmers in ancient societies often relied on the lunar calendar to plan their agricultural activities.
Many traditional holidays are set according to the lunar calendar rather than the solar one.
The Chinese New Year date changes each year because it follows the lunar calendar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solar calendar - a calendar based on the solar year
