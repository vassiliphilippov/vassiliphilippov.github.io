bolshy

adjective
deliberately combative or defiant
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlʃi /; / ˈbɒlʃi / (AmE), / ˈbɒlʃi / (BrE)
deliberately combative, uncooperative, or defiant, especially towards authority

Examples

He's always so bolshy in meetings, never willing to go along with the group.

Don't get bolshy with your manager just because you disagree with her.
The team became bolshy when asked to work overtime for the third night in a row.
