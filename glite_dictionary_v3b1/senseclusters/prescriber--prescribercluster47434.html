prescriber

noun
one who sets rules
5.6
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈskraɪ.bər/ /; / /prɪ.ˈskraɪ.bər/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈskraɪ.bə/ /; / /priː.ˈskraɪ.bə/ (BrE)
a person or organization that formally specifies or authorizes rules, standards, or procedures to be followed

Examples

Official prescriber of rules
The committee acted as the main prescriber of safety protocols in the factory.
As a prescriber of company policy, she ensured all employees understood the new guidelines.
Government agencies often serve as prescribers of industry standards and regulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enforcer - a person who ensures that rules or laws are obeyed
  • antonym: follower - a person who accepts and follows rules set by others
  • synonym: regulator - a person or body that controls or supervises something by rules
