self-confidence

noun
belief in your own abilities
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈkɑːn.fə.dəns / (AmE), / ˌselfˈkɒn.fɪ.dəns / (BrE)
a feeling or belief in your own abilities and worth; freedom from doubt about yourself

Examples

Building self-confidence
Building self-confidence can help you succeed in many areas of life.
His self-confidence grew as he completed each new challenge.
She showed remarkable self-confidence during the job interview.
