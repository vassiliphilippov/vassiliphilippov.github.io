sit up

verb
stay awake past usual bedtime
Pronunciation: / sɪt ʌp / (AmE), / sɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to stay awake and not go to bed, often later than usual or expected

Examples

Sit up late
He sat up all night to finish his assignment.
She often sits up late reading her favorite books.
We decided to sit up and wait for our daughter to come home.
