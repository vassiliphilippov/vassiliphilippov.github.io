road game

noun
sports game played away from home
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊd ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈrəʊd ɡeɪm / (BrE)
a sports game played at an opponent's venue, not at home

Examples

To play a road game
Playing a road game is often more challenging for athletes due to travel and unfamiliar settings.
The team won their last road game despite a hostile crowd.
Tonight's road game will be broadcast live on national television.

Related Terms

  • antonym: home game - a sports game played at one's own venue
