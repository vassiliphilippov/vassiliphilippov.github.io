ruff

noun
Eurasian wading bird with male neck ruff
Pronunciation: / rʌf / (AmE), / rʌf / (BrE)
A large wading bird (Calidris pugnax) found in Eurasia, with the male distinguished by a conspicuous erectile neck ruff during the breeding season.

Examples

A male ruff bird
During mating season, the male ruff displays a spectacular collar of feathers to attract females.
The unique breeding plumage of the ruff makes it a favorite among birdwatchers.
We spotted a ruff feeding at the edge of the marsh with other wading birds.
