multitudinously

adverb
in great numbers
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈtuːdənəsli / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdənəsli / (BrE)
in a manner involving or consisting of a great many things or people

Examples

Flowers bloom multitudinously
The flowers bloomed multitudinously across the entire field in early spring.
Supporters gathered multitudinously in the city square to celebrate the victory.
Birds arrived multitudinously at the lake during their annual migration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundantly - in large quantities; plentifully
  • antonym: sparsely - in small numbers or amounts; thinly scattered
