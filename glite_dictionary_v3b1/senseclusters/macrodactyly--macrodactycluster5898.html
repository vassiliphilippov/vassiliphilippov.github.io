macrodactyly

noun
enlarged fingers or toes
9.9
Pronunciation: /mæ.krə.ˈdæk.tɪ.liː/ (AmE), /mæ.krə.ˈdæk.tɪ.liː/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which one or more fingers or toes are abnormally large due to overgrowth of bone and soft tissue

Examples

Child with macrodactyly
The surgeon explained the treatment options for the child's macrodactyly affecting her left foot.
People with macrodactyly may require surgery to improve hand or foot function.
Early diagnosis of macrodactyly can help prevent complications as the child grows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gigantism - abnormal growth resulting in excessive size of the entire body or parts
  • antonym: microdactyly - a condition in which fingers or toes are abnormally small
