Mandaean language

noun
form of Aramaic used by the Mandaeans
Pronunciation: / mænˈdiːən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / manˈdiːən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the variety of Aramaic used by the Mandaeans, primarily for religious and literary purposes

Examples

Mandaean language texts
Many ancient religious texts are written in Mandaean language.
Scholars have studied the Mandaean language to better understand Gnostic traditions.
The Mandaean language preserves unique aspects of Eastern Aramaic dialects.
