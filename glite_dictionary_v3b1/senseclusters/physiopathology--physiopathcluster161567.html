physiopathology

noun
study of disease effects
9.9
Pronunciation: /fɪz.i.oʊ.pə.ˈθɑː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /fɪz.i.əʊ.pə.ˈθɒl.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
the study of how diseases or injuries change the normal functions of the body
