halter

noun
rope used for hanging execution
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔltər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːltə / (BrE)
a rope or noose used by a hangman to execute a condemned person by hanging

Examples

The halter of death
He narrowly escaped the halter thanks to a last-minute pardon.
Stories of outlaws often ended with the halter claiming another life.
The condemned man shuddered at the sight of the halter beside the gallows.
