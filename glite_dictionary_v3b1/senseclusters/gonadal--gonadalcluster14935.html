gonadal

adjective
relating to the gonads
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈnædəl / (AmE), / ɡəʊˈnædəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving the gonads (the organs that produce eggs or sperm, such as the ovaries or testes).

Examples

Gonadal function
Abnormal gonadal function may lead to infertility in both men and women.
Some genetic disorders can affect gonadal development during puberty.
The researchers studied the effects of stress on gonadal hormones in mammals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gonad - an organ that produces gametes; an ovary or testis
