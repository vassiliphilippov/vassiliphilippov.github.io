torpedoman

noun
naval torpedo specialist
8.9
Pronunciation: / tɔrˈpidəˌmæn / (AmE), / tɔːˈpiːdəʊˌmæn / (BrE)
a member of a navy responsible for handling, maintaining, and operating torpedoes on a ship or submarine

Examples

The torpedoman inspects torpedoes
The experienced torpedoman carefully inspected each torpedo before the submarine left port.
During the drill, the torpedoman demonstrated how to load and launch a torpedo safely.
As a torpedoman, he was responsible for maintaining all the submarine's underwater weapons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engineer - a person responsible for operating and maintaining machinery on a ship
  • synonym: gunner - a person in the military responsible for operating guns or artillery
