muchness

noun
greatness of quantity or extent
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌtʃ.nɪs / (BrE)
a large amount or quantity; the state or quality of being great in extent, degree, or measure

Examples

The muchness of noise
He was taken aback by the muchness of the noise in the city at night.
Sometimes the muchness of information online can feel overwhelming.
The muchness of her generosity impressed everyone who knew her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundance - a very large quantity of something
  • antonym: scarcity - the state of being in short supply
