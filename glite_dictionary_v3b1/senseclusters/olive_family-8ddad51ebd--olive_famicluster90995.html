olive family

noun
family of trees and shrubs including olive and jasmine
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.lɪv ˈfæm.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈɒl.ɪv ˈfæm.ɪ.li / (BrE)
a family of flowering trees and shrubs, including olives, ashes, jasmines, privets, and lilacs, usually producing berries, drupes, or capsules as fruit
