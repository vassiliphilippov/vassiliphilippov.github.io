chicken stock

noun
liquid from simmered chicken and vegetables
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən stɑk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn stɒk / (BrE)
a savory liquid made by simmering chicken, bones, and vegetables in water, used as a base for soups, sauces, and other dishes

Examples

A cup of chicken stock
She added a cup of chicken stock to the soup to enhance its flavor.
The chef simmered vegetables in chicken stock for a rich and hearty stew.
You can substitute water for chicken stock, but the dish may be less flavorful.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broth - a savory liquid in which meat or vegetables have been simmered
