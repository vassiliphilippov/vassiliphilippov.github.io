endothermy

noun
internal heat regulation
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈθɜːrmi / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈθɜːmi / (BrE)
the ability or process in animals of keeping a constant body temperature by producing heat inside the body
