painted cup

noun
plant of the genus Castilleja with bright bracts
9.0 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪn.tɪd kʌp/ /; / /ˈpeɪn.təd kʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪn.tɪd kʌp/ /; / /ˈpeɪn.təd kʌp/ (BrE)
