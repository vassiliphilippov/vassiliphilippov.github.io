energetically

adverb
with energy and enthusiasm
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛnɚˈdʒɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛnəˈdʒɛtɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that shows or involves a lot of energy and enthusiasm.

Examples

To wave energetically
He waved energetically as the train pulled away from the station.
She energetically defended her proposal during the meeting.
The children played energetically in the park all afternoon.
