benchtop

adjective
for use on a bench
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbentʃ.tɑːp/ (AmE), /ˈbentʃ.tɒp/ (BrE)
small enough or designed to be used on a workbench or countertop, especially for laboratory or kitchen equipment

Examples

Benchtop equipment
The scientist placed the benchtop microscope next to the samples for easy access.
A benchtop oven is ideal for small-scale baking in a home kitchen.
They upgraded to a larger machine because the benchtop model was too limited for their needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: floor-standing - designed to stand on the floor rather than a bench or table
  • synonym: portable - able to be easily carried or moved
