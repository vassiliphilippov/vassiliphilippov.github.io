readmittance

noun
being allowed back
Pronunciation: / ˌriːədˈmɪtəns / (AmE), / ˌriːədˈmɪtəns / (BrE)
the act of being allowed to enter or join again after having been excluded or removed

Examples

The readmittance to group
After completing the required training, his readmittance to the organization was approved.
The school board discussed the readmittance of students who had been suspended.
She was grateful for her readmittance to the club after resolving the misunderstanding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admission - the act of being allowed to enter
  • antonym: exclusion - the act of preventing someone from entering
  • synonym: reentry - the act of entering again
