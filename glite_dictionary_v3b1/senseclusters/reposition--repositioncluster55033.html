reposition

verb
move something to another position
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːpəˈzɪʃən /; / ˌriː.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌriːpəˈzɪʃən /; / ˌriː.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
to move something to a different place or position
