intercaste

noun
individual of mixed caste
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈkæst/ /; / /ɪn.tər.ˈkɑːst/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈkɑːst/ (BrE)
an individual, especially in social insects, that displays characteristics of more than one caste or is of intermediate caste

Examples

An intercaste ant
The scientist discovered an unusual intercaste among the colony's ants.
Some bees develop as intercaste individuals, showing traits of both workers and queens.
Researchers are studying how environmental factors can lead to the appearance of an intercaste in insect populations.
