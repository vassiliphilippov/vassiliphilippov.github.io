civil service

noun
government administration employees
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɜːr.vɪs/ /; / /ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɜːr.vəs/ (AmE), /ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɜː.vɪs/ /; / /ˈsɪv.əl ˈsɜː.vəs/ (BrE)
