hack

noun
taxi or vehicle for hire
Pronunciation: / hæk / (AmE), / hæk / (BrE)
(dated, chiefly US) A taxi or a vehicle for hire, especially one driven by a professional driver for transporting passengers for a fare.

Examples

A hack cab
He hailed a hack at the curb to get across town quickly.
In old movies, you often see characters climbing into a hack late at night.
It was raining, so she decided to take a hack rather than wait for the bus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taxi - a car licensed to transport passengers for hire
