stuffer

noun
filling item
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌfər / (AmE), / ˈstʌfə / (BrE)
an item used to fill space or add volume to something, often for protection or appearance

Examples

Foam stuffer
The package contained foam stuffers to keep the fragile items from moving.
She used tissue paper stuffers to make the gift bag look fuller.
Many companies include promotional stuffers in their mailings to advertise new products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: filler - a material or object used to fill empty space
  • synonym: padding - soft material used to protect or fill space
