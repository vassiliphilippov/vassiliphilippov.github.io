trailer

noun
vehicle designed to be lived in, pulled by another
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪlɚ / (AmE), / ˈtreɪlə / (BrE)
a wheeled vehicle that is pulled by a car or truck and is equipped for people to live or sleep in

Examples

A trailer home

The trailer was spacious enough for a family of four to sleep comfortably.
We spent the weekend camping in our trailer by the lake.
