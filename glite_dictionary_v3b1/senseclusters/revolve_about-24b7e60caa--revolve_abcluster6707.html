revolve about

verb
be primarily focused on something
4.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑlv əˈbaʊt / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒlv əˈbaʊt / (BrE)
(of an activity, life, etc.) to be mainly concerned with or focused on a particular person, subject, or thing.

Examples

To revolve about a topic
Her daily schedule tends to revolve about her children's activities.
Much of the conversation seemed to revolve about the upcoming holiday.
The storylines in the show revolve about the lives of the main characters.
