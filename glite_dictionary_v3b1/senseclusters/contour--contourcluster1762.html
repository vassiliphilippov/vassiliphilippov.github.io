contour

verb
to form the outline or shape of
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑnˌtʊr / (AmE), / ˈkɒntʊə / (BrE)
to shape or form the outline or surface of something, especially so as to follow its natural curves or features

Examples

To contour the land
Architects often contour the land to blend buildings with the natural surroundings.
She used makeup to contour her cheekbones for a more defined look.
The hills have been carefully contoured to prevent soil erosion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outline - to draw or mark the outer edge of something
  • synonym: shape - to give a particular form or shape to something
