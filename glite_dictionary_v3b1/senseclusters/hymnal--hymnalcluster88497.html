hymnal

noun
book of hymns for worship
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪmnəl / (AmE), / ˈhɪmnəl / (BrE)
a book containing a collection of hymns, typically used in church services

Examples

A hymnal book
Old church pews often hold a hymnal in the shelf below the seat.
She picked up the hymnal and turned to the page for the next song.
The choir rehearsed every song in the new hymnal before Sunday service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hymn - a religious song of praise
