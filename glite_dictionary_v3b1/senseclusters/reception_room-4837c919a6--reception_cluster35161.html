reception room

noun
room for receiving visitors
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛp.ʃən rum / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛp.ʃən ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house or hotel where visitors are received and entertained

Examples

The reception room welcomes guests
Guests were shown into the reception room while dinner was being prepared.
The hotel features an elegant reception room for special events and gatherings.
We waited in the reception room until the host arrived to greet us.
