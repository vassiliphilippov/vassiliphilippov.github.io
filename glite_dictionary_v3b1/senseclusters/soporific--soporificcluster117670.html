soporific

adjective
inducing sleepiness or mental dullness
8.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /soʊ.pə.ˈrɪf.ɪk/ /; / /sɑː.pə.ˈrɪf.ɪk/ (AmE), /səʊ.pə.ˈrɪf.ɪk/ /; / /sɒ.pə.ˈrɪf.ɪk/ (BrE)
