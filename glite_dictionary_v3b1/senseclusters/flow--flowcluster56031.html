flow

verb
undergo menstruation
Pronunciation: / floʊ / (AmE), / fləʊ / (BrE)
(of a woman) to have a menstrual period; to menstruate.

Examples

To flow during menstruation
She was embarrassed because she began to flow unexpectedly at school.
She wasn't feeling well because she had just started to flow.
Some women flow heavily during their menstrual cycles, while others have lighter periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menstruate - to have a menstrual period; to discharge blood monthly as part of the menstrual cycle
