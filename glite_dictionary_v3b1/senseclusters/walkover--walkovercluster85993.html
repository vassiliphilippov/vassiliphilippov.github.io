walkover

noun
gymnastic backbend passing through handstand
6.1 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔːk.ə.vər/ /; / /ˈwɑːk.ə.vər/ (AmE), /ˈwɔːk.ə.və/ (BrE)
A gymnastic movement in which a person performs a backbend followed by passing the legs over the body until standing again, often incorporating a handstand position.

Examples

A back walkover
During practice, he hurt his wrist attempting a one-handed walkover.
Learning the walkover is often a milestone for young gymnasts.
She flawlessly executed a back walkover in her floor routine.
