blue book

noun
exam booklet with blue cover
Pronunciation: / blu bʊk / (AmE), / bluː bʊk / (BrE)
a blue-covered booklet used by students to write examination answers, commonly at universities and colleges

Examples

An exam blue book
I wrote my essay answers in the blue book provided by the university.
She bought a blue book from the bookstore before the final exam.
The professor reminded students to bring a blue book for the midterm test.
