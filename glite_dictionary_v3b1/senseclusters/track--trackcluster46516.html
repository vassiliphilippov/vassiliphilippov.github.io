track

noun
individual song or piece on an album
1.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /træk/ (AmE), /træk/ (BrE)
a song or piece of music on an album, or the groove on a record that contains the music

Examples

A music track
She skipped to the next track because she had already heard the previous one.
The fourth track on this album is my favorite ballad.
They released a bonus track for the digital edition of the album.
Dust in the track of the record can cause crackling noises during playback.
Each track must be carefully pressed to ensure accurate sound quality.
The stylus follows the track on the vinyl to reproduce the original sound.
