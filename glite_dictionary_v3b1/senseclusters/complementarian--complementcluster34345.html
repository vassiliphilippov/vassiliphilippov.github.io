complementarian

noun
believer in gender roles
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːmpləˌmɛnˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌkɒmplɪmɛnˈteəriən / (BrE)
a person who believes that men and women have different but complementary roles, especially in family and society

Examples

A complementarian leader
As a complementarian, he supports traditional roles for men and women in marriage.
The debate featured a complementarian and an egalitarian discussing gender equality.
Many complementarians interpret religious texts as supporting different roles for men and women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egalitarian - a person who believes in equal rights and roles for all genders
