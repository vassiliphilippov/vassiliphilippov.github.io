Pisces

noun
constellation between Aquarius and Aries
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪsiːz / (AmE), / ˈpɪsiːz / (BrE)
a large and faint constellation of the zodiac, situated between Aquarius and Aries

Examples

Pisces constellation
Pisces lies between Aquarius to the west and Aries to the east in the zodiac.
Ancient astronomers identified Pisces as two fishes swimming in opposite directions.
The constellation Pisces is best seen in the night sky during autumn.
