inclinator

noun
diagonal lift
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkleɪnəˌtɔr / (AmE), / ɪnˈkleɪnətə / (BrE)
a mechanical lift that moves diagonally along an incline to transport people or goods between different levels

Examples

Hotel inclinator lift
The hotel installed an inclinator to help guests reach the hillside suites easily.
Engineers recommended an inclinator for the steep path between the parking lot and the main building.
The museum's inclinator provides a unique view as it moves up the sloped gallery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elevator - a vertical lift for people or goods
  • synonym: funicular - a cable railway on a steep incline
