bulk mail

noun
mass email; spam or marketing messages
3.3
Pronunciation: /bʌlk meɪl/ (AmE), /bʌlk meɪl/ (BrE)
large quantities of email messages sent at once to many recipients, often for marketing or unsolicited (spam) purposes.
