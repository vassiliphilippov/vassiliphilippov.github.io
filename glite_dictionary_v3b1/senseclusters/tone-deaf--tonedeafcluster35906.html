tone-deaf

adjective
insensitive to social or emotional cues
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtoʊn.dɛf/ (AmE), /ˈtəʊn.dɛf/ (BrE)
Insensitive to or unaware of emotional nuances or social cues, often resulting in inappropriate or unperceptive responses.

Examples

Tone-deaf comment
His tone-deaf remarks during the meeting upset several colleagues.
She is sometimes tone-deaf to the mood of the room, making jokes at the wrong moment.
The company's tone-deaf advertisement was widely criticized for ignoring public sentiment.
