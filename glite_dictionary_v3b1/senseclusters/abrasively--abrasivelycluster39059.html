abrasively

adverb
with abrasive action
6.6
Pronunciation: / əˈbreɪsɪvli / (AmE), / əˈbreɪsɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that scrapes, wears away, or removes material from a surface by rubbing or friction

Examples

Clean abrasively
The cleaner scrubbed the tiles abrasively to remove the stubborn stains.
If you polish the wood abrasively, you might damage its surface.
The sandpaper worked abrasively to smooth out the rough edges of the board.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roughly - in a harsh or coarse manner
  • antonym: smoothly - in a gentle or even manner
