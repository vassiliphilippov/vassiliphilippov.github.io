presser

noun
informal press conference
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛsər / (AmE), / ˈprɛsə / (BrE)
an informal term for a press conference where officials or organizations speak to journalists

Examples

A team presser
The coach called a presser after the game to discuss the team's performance.
Journalists gathered at city hall for the mayor's morning presser.
She announced her resignation during a brief presser on Monday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: press conference - a meeting where officials give information to journalists
