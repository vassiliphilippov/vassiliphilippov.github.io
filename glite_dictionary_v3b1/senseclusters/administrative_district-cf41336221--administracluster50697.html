administrative district

noun
area set for government administration
Pronunciation: / ədˌmɪnəˈstreɪtɪv ˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪtɪv ˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
A district established and defined by authorities for the purpose of administration, governance, or public services.

Examples

Local administrative district
Each administrative district has its own local government office.
Schools in the administrative district must adhere to the regulations set by regional authorities.
The city council decided to reorganize the administrative district boundaries to improve public services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district - an area of a country or city designated for a particular purpose
