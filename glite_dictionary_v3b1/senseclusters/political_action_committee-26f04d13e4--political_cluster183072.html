political action committee

noun
group raising funds for political campaigns
2.7 4.4
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪt̬ɪkəl ˈækʃən kəˌmɪti / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈækʃən kəˌmɪti / (BrE)
an organization formed by a special-interest group to raise and spend money in support of political candidates and causes

Examples

Candidates often receive contributions from more than one political action committee during an election cycle.
