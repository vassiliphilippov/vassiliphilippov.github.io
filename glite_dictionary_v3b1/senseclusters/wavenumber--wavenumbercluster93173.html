wavenumber

noun
spatial frequency measure
8.2
Pronunciation: /weɪv ˈnʌm.bər/ (AmE), /weɪv ˈnʌm.bə/ (BrE)
a measure of spatial frequency; the number of waves per unit distance, used in physics and spectroscopy

Examples

The wavenumber measure
