crawl

verb
swim using the crawl stroke
5.3 3.2
Pronunciation: / krɔl / (AmE), / krɔːl / (BrE)
to swim using the crawl stroke, a style in which the swimmer moves arms alternately over the water and kicks with the legs

Examples

To crawl across water
After mastering the breaststroke, he began to crawl across the pool effortlessly.
Most competitive swimmers prefer to crawl because it's the fastest stroke.
She learned how to crawl during her first swimming lessons.

Related Terms
