apprehension

noun
the act of capturing or arresting someone
Pronunciation: / ˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən / (BrE)
the act of capturing or arresting someone, especially a criminal

Examples

Police apprehension
After weeks of investigation, his apprehension was finally announced to the public.
The police worked together to ensure the apprehension of the armed robber.
The swift apprehension of the suspect prevented further crimes.
