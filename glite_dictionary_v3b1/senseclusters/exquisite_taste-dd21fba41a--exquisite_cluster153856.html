exquisite taste

noun
refined and superior aesthetic preference
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskwɪzət teɪst / (AmE), / ɛkˈskwɪzɪt teɪst / (BrE)
the ability to choose or appreciate things of exceptional beauty or quality, especially in art, fashion, or food

Examples

Exquisite taste in art
His exquisite taste in music impresses everyone at the party.
She has exquisite taste in interior design, choosing pieces that are both elegant and unique.
The gallery was curated by an expert with exquisite taste for contemporary art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good taste - the ability to judge what is attractive or appropriate
  • antonym: poor taste - lack of ability to judge what is attractive or appropriate
