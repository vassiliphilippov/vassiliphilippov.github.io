nonvoter

noun
person who does not vote
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈnɑːn.voʊ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈnɒn.vəʊ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who does not vote in an election, usually someone who is eligible but chooses not to participate

Examples

Young nonvoter
A large number of nonvoters can affect the outcome of an election.
The campaign aimed to encourage nonvoters to participate in the democratic process.
Many young people remain nonvoters despite being eligible to vote.
The number of nonvoters increased significantly compared to the previous election.
Campaigns often try to encourage nonvoters to participate in the democratic process.
Many nonvoters said they felt their vote would not make a difference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstainer - a person who deliberately does not take part in something, especially voting
  • antonym: voter - a person who votes in an election
