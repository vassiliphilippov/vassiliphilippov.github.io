cheesy

adjective
overly sentimental
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃiː.zi/ (AmE), /ˈtʃiː.zi/ (BrE)
overly sentimental, silly, or lacking in good taste, often in a way that seems insincere or cheap

Examples

Cheesy love story
The movie was so cheesy that we couldn't stop laughing at the dramatic scenes.
He gave her a cheesy card with hearts and glitter for Valentine's Day.
Some people love cheesy romantic comedies, while others find them hard to watch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corny - trying too hard to be sentimental or funny, and not seeming genuine
  • antonym: genuine - real and sincere, not fake or insincere
  • synonym: tacky - showing poor taste or quality
