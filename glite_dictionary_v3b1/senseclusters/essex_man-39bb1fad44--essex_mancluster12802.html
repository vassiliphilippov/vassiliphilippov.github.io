Essex man

noun
stereotypical aspirational man from Essex, UK
Pronunciation: / ˈɛsɪks mæn / (AmE), / ˈɛsɪks mæn / (BrE)
(in Britain) a stereotype of a man from Essex, typically portrayed as materialistic, aspirational, and fond of conspicuous consumption.

Examples

An Essex man stereotype
He jokes about being an Essex man, but he's actually quite modest.
Many commentators discussed the influence of the Essex man on election results in the 1980s.
The Essex man is often portrayed as someone who enjoys flashy cars and designer clothes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yuppie - a young urban professional, especially one seen as materialistic and career-focused
