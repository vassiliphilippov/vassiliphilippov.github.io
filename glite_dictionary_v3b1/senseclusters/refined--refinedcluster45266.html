refined

adjective
freed from impurities by processing
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈfaɪnd / (BrE)
(of a substance) made pure or freed from unwanted elements by processing

Examples

Refined sugar
He refused to eat foods that were excessively refined or processed.
The factory specializes in producing refined sugar from raw cane.
This soap is made using refined coconut oil for a smoother texture.
Olive oil can be either extra-virgin or refined, depending on its processing.
The factory produces refined sugar for use in baking and confectionery.
This machine turns crude oil into refined gasoline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crude - not processed or purified; in a natural state
  • synonym: pure - not mixed with any other substance; free from contamination
  • antonym: raw - in its natural, unprocessed state
  • antonym: unrefined - not made pure or processed; containing impurities
