lodge

noun
formal association or society (e.g., Freemasons)
7.6 2.49/m
Pronunciation: / lÉ‘dÊ’ / (AmE), / lÉ’dÊ’ / (BrE)
a formal association or society of people with shared interests or aims, especially one with secret rites or membership such as a fraternal order

Examples

A Masonic lodge
He became a member of the local Masonic lodge after his initiation ceremony.
Many traditions and rituals are upheld by members of the fraternal lodge.
The lodge meets every Thursday to organize charitable activities for the community.

Synonyms

  • secret society
    6.3 0.98/m
    secret society hides membership and rituals from outsiders, while lodge is a formal association often with secret rites and ceremonies.
  • order
    6.7 13/m
    order often names a formal religious or knightly community, while lodge is a broader fraternal association with membership rituals.
