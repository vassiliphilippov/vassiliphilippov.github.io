impendency

noun
state of being imminent
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛndənsi / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛndənsi / (BrE)
the state of being about to happen; imminence

Examples

The impendency of danger
Her voice betrayed a sense of impendency as the storm approached.
The committee discussed the impendency of an economic crisis during their meeting.
The growing tension signaled the impendency of conflict in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imminence - the state of being about to occur
  • antonym: remoteness - the state of being far away in time
