polygraphy

noun
recording body signals
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪɡrəfi / (BrE)
the simultaneous recording of several physiological signals, such as breathing, oxygen levels, and heart rate, for medical monitoring

Examples

Medical polygraphy
The doctor recommended polygraphy to assess the patient's breathing patterns overnight.
Modern sleep clinics often use polygraphy to monitor several body functions at once.
During the test, polygraphy recorded changes in heart rate and oxygen saturation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: monitoring - the regular observation and recording of activities or signals
  • synonym: polysomnography - the recording of multiple physiological signals during sleep
