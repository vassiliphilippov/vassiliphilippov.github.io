A strong railing placed at the edge of a stairway, road, or bridge to prevent people or vehicles from falling off.

Examples

A guardrail on road
Highway workers installed a new guardrail after the curve to improve safety.
Please make sure to hold the guardrail when using the stairs.
The car skidded off the road and crashed into the guardrail.

Synonyms

    2.3 0.03/m

  • railing
    1.6 1.36/m
    a railing is often a lighter, sometimes decorative barrier on stairs or balconies, while a guardrail is stronger and protects vehicles.
