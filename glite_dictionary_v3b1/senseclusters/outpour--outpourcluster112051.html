outpour

verb
to express emotions suddenly
6.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààa ätÀåp…îr / (AmE), / Ààa ätÀåp…îÀê / (BrE)
to express strong feelings, emotions, or words openly and suddenly, often in large amounts.

Examples

To outpour feelings
After the announcement, fans outpoured their excitement on social media.
She outpoured her feelings in a heartfelt letter to her friend.
The community outpoured support for the family after the tragedy.
