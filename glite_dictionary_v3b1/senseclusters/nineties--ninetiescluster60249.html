nineties

noun
ages 90 to 100 in life
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪn.tiz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪn.tiz / (BrE)
The period of a person's life between the ages of 90 and 100.

Examples

In his nineties
Despite being in his nineties, he still enjoyed daily walks around the park.
Many people today are living healthier lives into their nineties.
She remained active well into her nineties, gardening and volunteering in her community.
