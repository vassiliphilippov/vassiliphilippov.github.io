ethnomusicology

noun
study of world music
Pronunciation: / ˌɛθnoʊˌmjuːzɪˈkɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɛθnəʊˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the academic study of music from different cultures, especially focusing on its social and cultural context

Examples

Study of ethnomusicology
She decided to specialize in ethnomusicology to better understand the music of indigenous peoples.
The university offers a course in ethnomusicology that explores music traditions from around the world.
His research in ethnomusicology focuses on the role of music in African societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropology - the study of human societies and cultures
  • synonym: musicology - the scholarly study of music
