washerman

noun
man who operates or washes clothes
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃɚˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃəmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to wash clothes, especially in an industrial or commercial setting

Examples

The washerman cleans clothes
After years of working as a washerman, he knew the best way to remove stubborn stains from clothing.
The washerman arrived early each morning to ensure the laundry was cleaned on time.
The hotel employed a skilled washerman to handle all the bed linens and staff uniforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: launderer - person who washes laundry, especially professionally
  • synonym: washerwoman - woman who washes clothes, especially professionally
