alterity

noun
state of otherness
9.9
Pronunciation: / ælˈtɛrəti / (AmE), / ælˈtɛrɪti / (BrE)
the state of being different or the quality of otherness, especially in relation to identity or culture

Examples

Cultural alterity
The philosopher discussed the concept of alterity in relation to cultural identity.
Understanding alterity can help promote empathy between people from different backgrounds.
The exhibition highlights the alterity of marginalized communities through art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: otherness - the quality of being different or distinct
  • antonym: sameness - the quality of being the same or identical
