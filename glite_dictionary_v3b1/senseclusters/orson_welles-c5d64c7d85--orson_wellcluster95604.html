Orson Welles

proper noun
American actor, director, and filmmaker (1915–1985)
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrsən ˈwɛlz / (AmE), / ˈɔːsən ˈwɛlz / (BrE)
(1915–1985) a celebrated American actor, director, writer, and producer known for films such as 'Citizen Kane' and his innovative contributions to theatre, radio, and cinema.
