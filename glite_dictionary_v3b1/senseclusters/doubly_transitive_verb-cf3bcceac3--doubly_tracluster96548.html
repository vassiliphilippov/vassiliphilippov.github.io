doubly transitive verb

noun
verb taking direct and indirect objects
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbli trænˈzɪtɪv vɝb /; / ˈdʌbli trænsˈɪtɪv vɜrb / (AmE), / ˈdʌbli trænˈzɪtɪv vɜːb /; / ˈdʌbli trænˈsɪtɪv vɜːb / (BrE)
a verb that takes both a direct object and an indirect object
