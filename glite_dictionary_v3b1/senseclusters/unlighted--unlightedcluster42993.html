unlighted

adjective
not set on fire or burning
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of a candle, lamp, cigarette, etc.) not set on fire or burning; not lit.

Examples

Unlighted candle
She put the unlighted cigarette back into the pack.
The street remained dark, as the lamps were still unlighted.
The table was bare, with the unlighted candles casting no glow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unlit - (of something that can be lit) not burning or shining
