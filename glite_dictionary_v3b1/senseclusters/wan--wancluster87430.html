wan

adjective
pale and faint (of light or color)
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɑn / (AmE), / wɒn / (BrE)
(of light or color) pale and weak; lacking intensity or brightness.

Examples

Wan light
A wan moon hung silently above the misty fields.
The wan glow of dawn barely illuminated the empty street.
Through the dusty curtains, only a wan beam of sunlight entered the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bright - having a lot of light or strong color
  • synonym: pale - lacking strong color or brightness
