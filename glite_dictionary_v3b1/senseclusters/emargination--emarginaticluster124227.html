emargination

noun
notched edge or tip
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmɑrdʒɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌmɑːdʒɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a notch or indentation at the edge or tip of a structure, especially in biological contexts

Small emargination edge

The shell's emargination was visible even without a magnifying glass.
