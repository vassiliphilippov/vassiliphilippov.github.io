bibliotherapy

noun
therapy using books
Pronunciation: / ˌbɪblioʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌbɪbliəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
the use of books or reading as a method to support mental health or emotional well-being

Examples

Bibliotherapy for healing
Many therapists recommend bibliotherapy to help patients process their emotions.
Through bibliotherapy, children can learn to cope with difficult life changes.
She turned to bibliotherapy as a way to manage her anxiety and stress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychotherapy - the treatment of mental disorders by psychological means
  • synonym: therapy - treatment intended to relieve or heal a disorder
