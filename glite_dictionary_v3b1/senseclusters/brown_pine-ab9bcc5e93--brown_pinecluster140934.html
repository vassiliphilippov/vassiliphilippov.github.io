brown pine

noun
Australian tree with wood turning brown
Pronunciation: / braʊn paɪn / (AmE), / braʊn paɪn / (BrE)
a large Australian tree (Podocarpus elatus) with straight-grained yellow wood that turns brown when exposed to air

Examples

The brown pine tree
After being cut, the yellow wood of the brown pine gradually turns a deep brown.
Many antique Australian furniture pieces were crafted from brown pine.
The straight-grained wood of the brown pine is prized for cabinetry.
