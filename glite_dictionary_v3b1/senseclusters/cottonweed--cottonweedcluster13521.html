cottonweed

noun
woolly-flowered plant of genus Froelichia
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːtənˌwid /; / ˈkɑtnˌwid / (AmE), / ˈkɒtənˌwiːd /; / ˈkɒtnˌwiːd / (BrE)
a North American plant with soft, woolly, white flowers or leaves
