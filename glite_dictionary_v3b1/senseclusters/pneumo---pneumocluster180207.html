a combining form meaning 'spirit' or 'spiritual', used in theological and philosophical terms

Examples

Pneumo-philosophy
The prefix pneumo- is used in words related to spirit or breath in philosophical texts.
In theology, pneumo- appears in terms like 'pneumatology' to refer to the study of the spirit.
Scholars often discuss the meaning of pneumo- in ancient Greek philosophy.
