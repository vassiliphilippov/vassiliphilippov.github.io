OTC stock

noun
stock traded outside exchanges
6.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo ä ti Ààsi st…ëk / (AmE), / Àå…ô ä tiÀê ÀàsiÀê st…ík / (BrE)
A stock that is traded over the counter (OTC), rather than on an organized exchange.

Examples

OTC stock trading
Because OTC stock trading is less regulated, investors face higher risks.
Many technology startups begin as OTC stocks before qualifying for a major exchange.
She researched the financials carefully before buying shares in an OTC stock.

Synonyms

  • over-the-counter stock
    6.5 0.07/m
    over-the-counter stock names shares traded through a decentralized dealer network, while OTC stock is the common abbreviated term for that market.
  • unlisted stock
    5.8 0.00/m
    unlisted stock is not listed on an exchange and may trade privately or through informal markets, while OTC stock specifically trades through dealer networks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: listed stock - stock traded on an official exchange
  • synonym: over-the-counter - not traded on an organized exchange
