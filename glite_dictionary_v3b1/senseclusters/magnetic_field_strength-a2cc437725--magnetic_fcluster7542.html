Examples

The magnetic field strength

Synonyms

  • magnetic flux density
    7.2 0.00/m
    magnetic flux density gives the magnetic flux through a unit area and is often measured in teslas, while magnetic field strength describes field intensity.
  • magnetic intensity
    6.7 0.02/m
    magnetic intensity names the field's strength over an area and sounds more general, while magnetic field strength is the precise intensity measure.
