Bucerotidae

noun
family of birds including hornbills
Pronunciation: / ˌbjuːsəˈrɑːtəˌdi /; / ˌbjuːsəˌroʊˈtɪdi / (AmE), / ˌbjuːsəˈrɒtɪdiː /; / ˌbjuːsərəˈtɪːdiː / (BrE)
the family of large tropical birds called hornbills, known for their long, curved bills and a casque (a growth) on the upper part of the bill
