overripe

adjective
too ripe and becoming soft or decayed
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈraɪp / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈraɪp / (BrE)
(of fruit or vegetables) too ripe and starting to become soft or decay.

Examples

An overripe fruit
Some cheeses are made with overripe milk to enhance their flavor.
The bananas had turned overripe and started to attract fruit flies.
You should avoid buying overripe tomatoes as they spoil quickly.
