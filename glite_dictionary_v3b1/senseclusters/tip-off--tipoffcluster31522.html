tip-off

noun
start of a basketball game, jump ball
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪp ˌɔf / (AmE), / ˈtɪp ɒf / (BrE)
the act of starting a basketball game by tossing the ball upward between two opposing players for a jump ball

Examples

Basketball tip-off
After the tip-off, the home team quickly took possession of the ball.
Fans cheered loudly as the players leaped for the tip-off at center court.
The referee blew the whistle and prepared for the tip-off.
