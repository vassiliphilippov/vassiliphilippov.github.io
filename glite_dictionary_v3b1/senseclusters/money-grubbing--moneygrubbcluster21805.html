money-grubbing

adjective
greedy for money
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌniˌɡrʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
greedy for money or overly concerned with making money

Examples

Money-grubbing businessman
Many people saw the new policy as a money-grubbing attempt to increase profits.
The money-grubbing landlord raised the rent every year without making improvements.
Critics accused the company of money-grubbing behavior at the expense of its workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avaricious - having an extreme desire for wealth
  • antonym: generous - willing to give money or help freely
  • synonym: greedy - wanting more than is needed, especially money or food
