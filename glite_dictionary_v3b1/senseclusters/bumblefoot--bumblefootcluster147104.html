bumblefoot

noun
bird foot infection
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌmbəlˌfʊt / (AmE), / ˈbʌmbəlˌfʊt / (BrE)
a painful infection or swelling of the foot in animals, especially birds, mammals, or reptiles

Examples

Chicken bumblefoot
The veterinarian diagnosed the chicken with bumblefoot after noticing swelling and redness.
Farmers are advised to check their birds regularly for signs of bumblefoot to prevent serious infections.
Treatment for bumblefoot often includes cleaning the wound and applying antibiotics.
