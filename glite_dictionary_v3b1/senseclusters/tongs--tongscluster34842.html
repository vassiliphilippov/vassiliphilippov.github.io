tongs

noun
device for gripping objects
5.2
Pronunciation: / tɑŋz / (AmE), / tɒŋz / (BrE)
a tool with two movable arms joined at one end, used for picking up and holding objects

Examples

Kitchen tongs
Please use the tongs to pick up the ice cubes, not your hands.
She used a pair of tongs to serve the salad.
The blacksmith gripped the hot metal with tongs and placed it on the anvil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forceps - a medical instrument similar to tongs, used for grasping
  • synonym: pincers - a tool similar to tongs for gripping or pulling
