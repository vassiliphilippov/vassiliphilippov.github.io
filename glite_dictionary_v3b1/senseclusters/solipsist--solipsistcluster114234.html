solipsist

noun
self-centered person
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.lɪp.sɪst/ /; / /ˈsoʊ.lɪp.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈsɒ.lɪp.sɪst/ /; / /ˈsəʊ.lɪp.sɪst/ (BrE)
