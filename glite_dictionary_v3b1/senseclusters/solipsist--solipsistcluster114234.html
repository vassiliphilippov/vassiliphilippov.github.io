solipsist

noun
self-centered person
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪpsɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪpsɪst / (BrE)
a person who is extremely self-centered and disregards the thoughts or feelings of others

Examples

Selfish solipsist
The CEO was criticized as a solipsist who rarely listened to his team.
Her friends grew frustrated with her solipsist attitude during group discussions.
It is difficult to work with a solipsist who ignores everyone else's opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: altruist - a person who cares about the well-being of others
  • synonym: egotist - a person who is excessively self-absorbed
