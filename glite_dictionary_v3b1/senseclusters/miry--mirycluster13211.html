miry

adjective
very muddy or boggy
8.6 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.rɪ/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.ə.rɪ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.ə.rɪ/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.rɪ/ (BrE)
(of ground or a surface) very muddy, boggy, or wet and soft so that it is difficult to walk on.

Examples

A miry road
Heavy wagons often got stuck in the miry roads during the wet season.
The miry banks of the river were dotted with reeds and wildflowers.
The hiker's boots sank deep into the miry trail after last night's rain.
