midweight

adjective
moderate weight
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌweɪt / (BrE)
having a weight that is between light and heavy, especially used to describe clothing or equipment

Examples

Midweight jacket
A midweight jacket is ideal for spring when the weather is unpredictable.
He chose a midweight sleeping bag for the camping trip in mild temperatures.
These midweight hiking boots offer a good balance between comfort and durability.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: heavyweight - having a lot of weight; very heavy
  • degree lower: lightweight - having little weight; not heavy
