tipu

noun
South American ornamental tree
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːpuː / (AmE), / ˈtiːpuː / (BrE)
A semi-evergreen tree native to South America, known for its odd-pinnate leaves and golden yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental.

Examples

A tipu tree
A tipu can provide welcome shade in hot climates thanks to its broad canopy.
The city park is lined with tipu trees that burst into golden bloom every spring.
We planted a tipu in our garden for its beautiful flowers and attractive foliage.
