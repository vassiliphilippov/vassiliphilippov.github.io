A pair consisting of a hammer and an anvil, especially as tools used together in metalworking; also used metaphorically to describe a relationship in which one party or force shapes or influences the other, often through repeated interaction.

Examples

Their discussions acted as a hammer and anvil, refining each other's ideas.
