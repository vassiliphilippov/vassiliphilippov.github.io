cobb

noun
rounded mass or wall
9.8
Pronunciation: / kɑb / (AmE), / kɒb / (BrE)
a rounded mass, especially of earth or clay, or a type of wall made from earth and straw

Examples

Cobb wall
The workers shaped each cobb by hand before adding it to the wall.
Traditional houses in the region often feature thick cobb walls for insulation.
A large cobb of clay was left to dry in the sun before use.
