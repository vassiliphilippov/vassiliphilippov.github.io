gonion

noun
craniometric point at jaw angle
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊniən / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊniən / (BrE)
the craniometric point located at the angle or apex of the lower jaw on either side

Examples

The gonion point
A fracture near the gonion can affect the shape of the jawline.
In anthropology, the gonion is used as a reference point for facial measurements.
The surgeon marked the gonion before performing the mandibular procedure.
