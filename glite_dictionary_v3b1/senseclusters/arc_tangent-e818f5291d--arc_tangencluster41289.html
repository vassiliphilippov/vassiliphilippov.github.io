arc tangent

noun
inverse of the tangent function

Pronunciation: /ɑːrk ˈtæn.dʒənt/ (AmE), /ɑːk ˈtæn.dʒənt/ (BrE)

Examples

The arc tangent function
Engineers frequently use arc tangent when analyzing circuits with phase differences.
The calculator's arc tangent function is often labeled as arctan or tan⁻¹.
To find the angle, take the arc tangent of the ratio of the sides.
