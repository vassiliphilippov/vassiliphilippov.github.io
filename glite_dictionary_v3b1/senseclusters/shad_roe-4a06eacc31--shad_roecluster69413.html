shad roe

noun
eggs of the shad fish used as food
Pronunciation: / ʃæd roʊ / (AmE), / ʃad rəʊ / (BrE)
The eggs of the shad fish, used as food and typically parboiled, baked, or sautéed.

Examples

Fresh shad roe
Each spring, chefs look forward to cooking fresh shad roe during its short season.
In upscale restaurants, shad roe is often served with toast points and lemon.
My grandmother prepared shad roe by gently sautéing it with bacon and herbs.
