changeability

noun
the quality of being changeable
Pronunciation: / ˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌtʃeɪndʒəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to change or be changed easily or frequently.

Examples

The changeability of weather
Farmers complain about the changeability of the region's climate.
His career path reflected the changeability of his interests over time.
The changeability of her moods made her difficult to predict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: changeableness - the tendency to change; being changeable
  • antonym: immutability - the quality of being unchangeable
