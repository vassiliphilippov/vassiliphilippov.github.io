swabber

noun
ship cleaner or tool
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːbər / (AmE), / ˈswɒbə / (BrE)
a person or tool used for cleaning surfaces, especially the decks of ships

Examples

Ship swabber
The swabber cleaned the deck every morning before sunrise.
A long-handled swabber is used to scrub the ship's floors.
During the voyage, the swabber made sure the decks stayed spotless.
