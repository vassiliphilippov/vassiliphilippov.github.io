tea tortrix

noun
moth pest of tea and coffee plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ti tɔr.trɪks / (AmE), / ti tɔː.trɪks / (BrE)
a small Indian moth (especially Homona coffearia) that infests tea and coffee plants, causing damage to the crops

Examples

The tea tortrix moth
Infestation by the tea tortrix can significantly reduce crop yields.
Researchers are studying ways to control tea tortrix populations in coffee-growing regions.
The tea tortrix is a serious pest in many Asian tea plantations.
