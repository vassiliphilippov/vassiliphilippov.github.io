tin hat

noun
protective helmet worn by construction workers
6.7
Pronunciation: / tɪn hæt / (AmE), / tɪn hæt / (BrE)
a lightweight helmet, typically made of plastic or metal, worn by construction workers for protection

Examples

Wear a tin hat
All workers must wear a tin hat when entering the construction area.

The company supplied new tin hats to ensure everyone's safety.
