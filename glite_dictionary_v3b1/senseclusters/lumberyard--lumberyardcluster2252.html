lumberyard

noun
place where lumber is sold
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌmbɚˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈlʌmbəˌjɑːd / (BrE)
A place where lumber is stored and sold.

Examples

The lumberyard sells wood

I stopped by the lumberyard to pick up some wooden planks for the project.
The old lumberyard at the edge of town is being converted into a community center.
