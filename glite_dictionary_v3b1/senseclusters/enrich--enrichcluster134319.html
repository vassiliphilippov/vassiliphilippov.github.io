enrich

verb
make wealthier; increase riches
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈrɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪnˈrɪtʃ / (BrE)
to make someone or something wealthier or richer

Examples

To enrich a family
Many believe that wise investments will enrich their future.
The discovery of gold mines helped to enrich the entire region.
Trade with foreign countries can enrich a nation's economy.
