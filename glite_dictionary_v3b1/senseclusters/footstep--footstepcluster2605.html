footstep

noun
distance covered by a single step
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌstɛp / (AmE), / ˈfʊtstɛp /; / ˈfʊt.stɛp / (BrE)
an act of taking a step or the distance covered in one step while walking

Examples

Each footstep forward
Every footstep brought him closer to the mountain peak.
He measured the length of the garden using each footstep.
The ancient stones were placed a footstep apart to help travelers cross the stream.
Each footstep left a faint mark in the freshly fallen snow.
He measured the distance in careful footsteps, counting as he walked.
She heard a footstep behind her in the hallway, making her turn quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pace - a single step taken when walking; also a measure of distance equal to one step
  • synonym: step - the act of moving by lifting the foot and setting it down again
