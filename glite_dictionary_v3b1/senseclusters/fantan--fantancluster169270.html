fantan

noun
a card game played by laying sevens and sequences
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌtæn / (AmE), / ˈfæn.tæn / (BrE)
A card game in which players lay down sevens and then other cards in sequence in the same suit, aiming to be the first to use all their cards.

Examples

Fantan card game
At the party, they started a lively game of fantan that lasted for hours.
Every Friday night, the family would gather around the table to play fantan.
He taught his friends how to play fantan with a standard deck of cards.
