regrease

verb
to apply grease again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɡriːs / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɡriːs / (BrE)
to apply grease to something again, especially machinery or equipment, to ensure proper function or maintenance

Examples

To regrease the gears
It is important to regrease the gears after every cleaning to prevent wear.
The technician advised us to regrease the moving parts every few months.
Before reassembling the engine, make sure to regrease all the bearings thoroughly.

  • synonym: lubricate - to apply a substance to reduce friction between surfaces
