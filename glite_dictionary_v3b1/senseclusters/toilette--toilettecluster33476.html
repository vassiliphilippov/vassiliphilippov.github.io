toilette

noun
act of dressing and grooming oneself
3.1
Pronunciation: / twɑɪˈlɛt / (AmE), / twɑːˈlɛt / (BrE)
the act of dressing and preparing yourself, especially in the morning or before an event

Examples

Morning toilette
After her lengthy toilette, she emerged elegantly dressed and ready for the soirée.
He devoted an hour each morning to his toilette before going to work.
In the 18th century, a lady's toilette was a social occasion involving her maids and companions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toilet - the act or process of washing and dressing oneself; also a fixture in a bathroom
