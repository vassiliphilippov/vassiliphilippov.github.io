trow

verb
to believe or suppose
Pronunciation: / troʊ / (AmE), / trəʊ / (BrE)
to believe, think, or suppose, used in archaic or literary English

Examples

I trow not
I trow the journey will be long and difficult.
She will succeed, I trow, for she is determined.
He did not, I trow, expect such a warm welcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: believe - to accept something as true
  • antonym: doubt - to feel uncertain about something
