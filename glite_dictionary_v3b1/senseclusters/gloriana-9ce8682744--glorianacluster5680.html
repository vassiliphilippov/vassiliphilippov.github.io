Gloriana

proper noun
Elizabeth I as symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡlɔːriˈænə / (AmE), / ɡlɒriˈɑːnə / (BrE)
a poetic or literary name for Queen Elizabeth I, often used as a symbol of England or English greatness

Examples

Gloriana as Queen Elizabeth
The poet referred to Queen Elizabeth I as Gloriana in his epic work.
In literature, Gloriana often represents the spirit and power of England.
The opera Gloriana celebrates the reign of Elizabeth I.
