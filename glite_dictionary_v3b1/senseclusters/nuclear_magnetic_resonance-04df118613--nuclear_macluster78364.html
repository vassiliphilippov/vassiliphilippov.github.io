nuclear magnetic resonance

noun
phenomenon where nuclei absorb radiation in a magnetic field
4.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnuː.klɪ.ər mæɡ.ˈnet.ɪk ˈrez.ə.nəns/ (AmE), /ˈnjuː.klɪ.ə mæɡ.ˈnet.ɪk ˈrez.ə.nəns/ (BrE)
a physical phenomenon in which atomic nuclei in a magnetic field absorb and re-emit electromagnetic radiation, especially used in imaging and chemical analysis
