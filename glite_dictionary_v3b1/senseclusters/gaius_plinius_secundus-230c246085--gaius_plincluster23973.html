Gaius Plinius Secundus

proper noun
Roman naturalist and author; died at Vesuvius
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ.əs plɪˈniː.əs sɪˈkʌn.dəs / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ.əs plɪˈniː.əs sɪˈkʌn.dəs / (BrE)
A Roman author and naturalist (AD 23–79), known for writing an encyclopedic work on natural history and for his death during the eruption of Vesuvius.

Examples

Gaius Plinius Secundus natural history
Gaius Plinius Secundus documented much of what was known about the natural world in his time.
Many historians refer to Gaius Plinius Secundus when discussing ancient Roman science.
The eruption of Vesuvius in 79 AD claimed the life of Gaius Plinius Secundus.
