rock rose

noun
a shrub with showy flowers and aromatic leaves
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑk ˌroʊz / (AmE), / ˈrɒk ˌrəʊz / (BrE)
any of several small evergreen shrubs of the genus Cistus, native to southern Europe and North Africa, grown for their showy flowers and aromatic foliage

Examples

Garden rock rose shrub
Gardeners appreciate the aromatic foliage of the rock rose in Mediterranean-style landscapes.
She admired the delicate pink blooms of the rock rose along the hillside trail.
The rock rose thrives in dry, sunny gardens where few other plants will survive.
