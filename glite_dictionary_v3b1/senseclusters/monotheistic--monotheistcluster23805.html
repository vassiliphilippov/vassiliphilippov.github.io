monotheistic

adjective
belief in one god
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnoʊθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəʊθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Believing that there is only one god.

Examples

Monotheistic religion
His monotheistic beliefs set him apart from his polytheistic neighbors.
Judaism, Christianity, and Islam are all considered monotheistic religions.
The ancient Hebrews were among the first monotheistic peoples in recorded history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: polytheistic - believing in more than one god
