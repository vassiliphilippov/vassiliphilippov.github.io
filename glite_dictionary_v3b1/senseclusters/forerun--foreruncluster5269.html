forerun

verb
to go ahead
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌrʌn / (AmE), / ˈfɔːrˌrʌn / (BrE)
to go or run ahead of someone or something, often to lead the way or act as a scout

Examples

Scouts forerun the group
The scouts will forerun the main group to ensure the path is clear.
A small team was sent to forerun the expedition and report any dangers.
He foreran the others, checking the trail for obstacles before they arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follow - to come or go after someone or something
  • synonym: precede - to come or go before in time or place
