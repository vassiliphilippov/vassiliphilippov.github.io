war power

noun
special government powers during war
7.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /wɔːr ˈpaʊ.ər/ /; / /wɔːr paʊr/ /; / /wɑːr ˈpaʊ.ər/ (AmE), /wɔː ˈpaʊ.ə/ /; / /wɔː pɑː/ (BrE)
