tetra-

x
prefix meaning four
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtrə / (AmE), / ˈtɛtrə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'four' or 'having four parts'

Examples

tetra- means four
A tetra-hedron is a geometric solid with four triangular faces.
The word tetra-chloride refers to a compound containing four chlorine atoms.
Scientists use the prefix tetra- to describe molecules with four similar parts.
