superset

noun
exercise sequence
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəsɛt / (BrE)
a set of two or more different exercises performed one after another without rest, usually targeting different muscle groups

Examples

A superset workout
The trainer recommended adding a superset of squats and lunges to increase intensity.
By performing a superset, she saved time and kept her heart rate up during the workout.
He likes to finish his gym session with a superset of bicep curls and tricep dips.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circuit - a series of exercises performed in sequence with minimal rest
  • degree lower: set - a group of repetitions of a single exercise
