bay

noun
partly enclosed coastal water area
7.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /beɪ/ (AmE), /beɪ/ (BrE)
an area of the sea or a large lake that is partly enclosed by a wide curve of the land, larger than a cove but smaller than a gulf

Examples

A seaside bay
Many picturesque villages are found along the bay where the river meets the sea.
The hotel offers stunning views overlooking the tranquil bay and its sandy beaches.
The sailors anchored their boat in the sheltered bay overnight.

Related Terms
