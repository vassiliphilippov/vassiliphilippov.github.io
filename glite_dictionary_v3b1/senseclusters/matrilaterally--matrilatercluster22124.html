matrilaterally

adverb
through mother's line
Pronunciation: / ˌmætrɪˈlæt̬ɚəli / (AmE), / ˌmætrɪˈlætərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or is traced through the mother's side of the family

Examples

Inherited matrilaterally
In some cultures, property is inherited matrilaterally rather than through the father's line.
The family traced their ancestry matrilaterally to honor their maternal heritage.
Citizenship in that society was determined matrilaterally, following the mother's lineage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: patrilaterally - in a way that relates to or is traced through the father's side of the family
