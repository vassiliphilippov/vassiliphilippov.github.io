fwd

verb
to forward a message
Pronunciation: / ɛf ˈdʌbəl.juː ˈdiː / (AmE), / ɛf ˈdʌbəl.juː ˈdiː / (BrE)
to send a message or document to another person, especially by email, by forwarding it

Examples

To fwd an email
Could you fwd me the latest project update when you have a moment?
I will fwd the invitation to everyone in the team.
She forgot to fwd the important email to her manager.
