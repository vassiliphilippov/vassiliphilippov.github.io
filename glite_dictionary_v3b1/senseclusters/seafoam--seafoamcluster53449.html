seafoam

adjective
pale greenish-blue
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːˌfoʊm / (AmE), / ˈsiːfəʊm / (BrE)
having a pale greenish-blue color similar to the foam on the sea

Examples

Seafoam green walls

The designer chose seafoam accents to give the living room a fresh, coastal feel.
His tie was a subtle seafoam that complemented his navy suit perfectly.
