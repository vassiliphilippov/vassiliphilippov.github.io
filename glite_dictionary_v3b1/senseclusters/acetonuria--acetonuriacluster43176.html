acetonuria

noun
excess ketone bodies in urine
Pronunciation: / ˌæsətəˈnʊriə / (AmE), / ˌæsɪtəˈnjʊəriə / (BrE)
The presence of excessive ketone bodies, especially acetone, in the urine, often seen in diabetes mellitus or starvation.

Examples

Excessive acetonuria in urine
Starvation or prolonged fasting increases the risk of developing acetonuria.
The doctor diagnosed acetonuria after detecting high amounts of ketone bodies in the patient's urine sample.
Uncontrolled diabetes can lead to complications such as acetonuria and dehydration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ketonuria - the presence of ketone bodies in the urine
