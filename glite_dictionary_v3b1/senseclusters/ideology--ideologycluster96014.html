Synonyms

  • belief system
    3.8 2/m
    a belief system is a connected set of principles guiding a group's actions, while ideology often names a wider political or social framework.
  • ism
    8.6 1/m
    an ism is a named system or doctrine that labels a movement or theory, while ideology describes the whole set of guiding beliefs.
  • doctrine
    6.0 9/m
    a doctrine is a specific teaching or formal rule taught within a group, while ideology is a broader system of political or social beliefs.
