H-bomb

noun

Examples

Synonyms

  • hydrogen bomb
    4.9 0.35/m
    a hydrogen bomb is the full technical name used in reports and writing, while an H-bomb is the short, common spoken term.
  • thermonuclear bomb
    6.9 0.08/m
    a thermonuclear bomb uses fusion at very high temperatures and formal language, while an H-bomb is the common short name for the hydrogen-fusion weapon.
  • fusion bomb
    6.3 0.03/m
    a fusion bomb fuses light atomic nuclei such as hydrogen and can mean any fusion device, while an H-bomb names the hydrogen-fusion weapon.
