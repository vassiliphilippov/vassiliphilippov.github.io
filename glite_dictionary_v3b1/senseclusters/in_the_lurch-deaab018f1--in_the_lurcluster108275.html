in the lurch

adverb
abandoned in a difficult situation
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn ðə lɜːrtʃ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː lɜːrtʃ/ /; / /ən ðə lɜːrtʃ/ (AmE), /ɪn ðə lɜːtʃ/ /; / /ɪn ðiː lɜːtʃ/ /; / /ən ðə lɜːtʃ/ (BrE)
