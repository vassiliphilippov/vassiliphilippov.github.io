outrageous

adjective
grossly offensive or shocking
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈreɪdʒəs / (AmE), / aʊtˈreɪdʒəs / (BrE)
Shockingly bad, immoral, or offensive to accepted standards of decency or morality.

Examples

Outrageous behavior
She was shocked by the outrageous behavior of the corrupt officials.
The judge described the attack as an outrageous act of cruelty.
The living conditions in the prison were outrageous and inhumane.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptable - regarded as normal or proper
  • synonym: appalling - causing horror or dismay
  • synonym: hideous - extremely unpleasant or shocking
