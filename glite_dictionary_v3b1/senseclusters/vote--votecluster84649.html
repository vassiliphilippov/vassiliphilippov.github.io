vote

noun
total number of voters
1.0
Pronunciation: / voʊt / (AmE), / vəʊt / (BrE)
the act of voting, the result of a vote, or the total number of votes in an election or poll

  • synonym: poll - the process of voting in an election
  • synonym: turnout - the number of people who vote in an election
