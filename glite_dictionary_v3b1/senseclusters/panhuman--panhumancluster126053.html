panhuman

adjective
affecting all humans
7.5
Pronunciation: / pænˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / pænˈhjuːmən / (BrE)

Examples

The search for meaning is often considered a panhuman quest across cultures.
Access to clean water is a panhuman right that should be protected everywhere.
