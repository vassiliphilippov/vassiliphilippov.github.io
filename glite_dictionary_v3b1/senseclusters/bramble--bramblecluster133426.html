bramble

verb
to become overgrown
Pronunciation: / ˈbræmbəl / (AmE), / ˈbræmbəl / (BrE)
to become covered with or tangled in thorny bushes, especially brambles.

Examples

The path brambled over
The abandoned garden slowly brambled until it was impossible to walk through.
After years of neglect, the path had brambled and was hidden from view.
If you do not maintain the land, it will quickly bramble and become wild.
