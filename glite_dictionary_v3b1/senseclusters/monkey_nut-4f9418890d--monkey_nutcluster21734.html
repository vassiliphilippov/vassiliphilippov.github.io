monkey nut

noun
peanut pod (British English)
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki nʌt / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki nʌt / (BrE)
the pod of the peanut plant, usually containing two nuts or seeds; chiefly British term for a peanut in its shell

Examples

A monkey nut shell
Children love cracking open the shells of monkey nuts at Christmas.
She bought a bag of monkey nuts to feed the squirrels in the park.
You can find monkey nuts in most British supermarkets during the winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groundnut - another British term for peanut
  • synonym: peanut - edible seed of the peanut plant
