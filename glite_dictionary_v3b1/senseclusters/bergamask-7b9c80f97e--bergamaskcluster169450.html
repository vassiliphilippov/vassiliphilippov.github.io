Bergamask

noun
rustic country person
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrɡəˌmæsk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡəˌmɑːsk / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a rustic, clumsy, or unsophisticated person from the countryside

Examples

A Bergamask villager
The villagers laughed as the Bergamask stumbled through the dance with exaggerated clumsiness.
In the old play, a Bergamask character provided comic relief with his awkward manners.
He was called a Bergamask because of his rustic accent and simple ways.
