butcherly

adjective
savage; involving bloodshed and brutality
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃərli / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃəli / (BrE)
involving or characterized by great bloodshed or brutality; savage or cruel

Examples

Butcherly violence
His butcherly actions during the conflict were condemned by the international community.
The film was criticized for its butcherly violence and lack of remorse.
The general's butcherly tactics left entire villages in ruins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bloodthirsty - eager to shed blood; violent or cruel
  • antonym: merciful - showing compassion or forgiveness
  • synonym: sanguinary - involving or causing much bloodshed
