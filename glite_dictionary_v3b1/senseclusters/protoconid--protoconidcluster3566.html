protoconid

noun
main lower molar cusp
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈkoʊnɪd / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈkəʊnɪd / (BrE)
the main cusp on the chewing surface of a lower molar tooth in mammals

Examples

The protoconid cusp
The paleontologist identified the protoconid as a key feature of the ancient mammal's tooth.
Damage to the protoconid can affect how efficiently an animal chews its food.
Researchers compared the size of the protoconid across several extinct species.
