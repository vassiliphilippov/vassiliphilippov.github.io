hahahahaha

noun
written laughter
Pronunciation: / hɑhɑhɑhɑhɑhɑhɑ / (AmE), / hɑːhɑːhɑːhɑːhɑː / (BrE)
a written representation of laughter, often used in messages or online posts to show amusement

Examples

A long hahahahaha
When she read the joke, she replied with a long hahahahaha in the group chat.
His comment was so funny that my response was just hahahahaha.
You could tell he found it hilarious by the hahahahaha he posted under the video.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haha - a shorter written form of laughter in text
  • synonym: lol - an abbreviation for 'laugh out loud', used to show amusement in messages
