company

noun
business organization
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a business organization that makes, buys, or sells goods or provides services in exchange for money

Examples

A tech company
Many people dream of working for a tech company in Silicon Valley.
She started her own company at the age of twenty-five.
The company is planning to expand into international markets next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a large business or company that is legally separate from its owners
  • synonym: firm - a business, especially one involving a partnership of owners
