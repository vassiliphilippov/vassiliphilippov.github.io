Synonyms

  • business organization
    2.9 0.1/m
    business organization is a formal legal entity that runs commercial activities, while company is the everyday word for a business that sells goods or services.
  • business firm
    2.7 0.05/m
    business firm is an organization owned by partners and employees that trades for profit, while company is the broader term for any business.
  • firm
    1.9 70/m
    firm often names a professional or small business like a law office, while company covers any business that makes or sells goods or services.
  • business enterprise
    3.2 0.1/m
    business enterprise sounds formal and often refers to large or complex commercial activities, while company is a common word for any business.
  • commercial enterprise
    3.4 0.2/m
    commercial enterprise focuses on buying and selling goods to make profit, while company is the general name for any business organization.
  • operation
    3.7 30/m
    operation refers to the active running or a large-scale business project, while company names the legal business that makes or sells goods.
