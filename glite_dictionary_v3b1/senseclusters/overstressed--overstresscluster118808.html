overstressed

adjective
excessively stressed
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈstrɛst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈstrɛst / (BrE)
subjected to more physical force, pressure, or stress than is safe, risking damage or failure

Examples

Overstressed cable
The bridge was declared unsafe because several overstressed cables showed signs of wear.
If a machine is overstressed, it may break down unexpectedly during operation.
Engineers warned that the building's foundation was overstressed after the heavy storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overloaded - carrying more weight or load than is safe
  • antonym: unstressed - not subjected to stress or pressure
