overkindness

noun
excessive kindness
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkaɪndnəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkaɪndnəs / (BrE)
the quality or act of being excessively kind, often to a fault or beyond what is appropriate

Examples

His overkindness caused problems
His overkindness sometimes led others to take advantage of his generosity.
She realized that her overkindness was making it hard to set healthy boundaries.
People may mistake overkindness for weakness, but it often comes from a place of strength.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harshness - the quality of being cruel or unkind
  • degree lower: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
