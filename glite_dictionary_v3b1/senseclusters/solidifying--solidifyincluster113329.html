solidifying

noun
process of becoming solid
2.0
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪdəˌfaɪɪŋ / (AmE), / səˈlɪdɪfaɪɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of becoming hard or solid by cooling, drying, or crystallization

Examples

The solidifying process
As temperatures dropped, the solidifying process in the pipes began quickly.
Proper solidifying of the mixture is essential for achieving the correct texture.
The solidifying of the molten metal took several minutes.
