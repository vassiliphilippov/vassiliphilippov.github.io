spined

adjective
having spines
4.1
Pronunciation: / spaɪnd / (AmE), / spaɪnd / (BrE)
having a spine or spines, such as a backbone or sharp, pointed projections

Examples

Spined animal
The hedgehog is a small, spined animal that curls up for protection.
Many desert plants are spined to help defend against herbivores.
The fish has a spined dorsal fin that deters predators.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spineless - lacking a spine or spines
  • synonym: spiny - covered with or having many spines or thorns
