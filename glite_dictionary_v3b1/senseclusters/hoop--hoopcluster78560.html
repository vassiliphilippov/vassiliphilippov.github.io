hoop

noun
basketball ring with net
1.7 2.49/m
Pronunciation: / huÀêp / (AmE), / huÀêp / (BrE)
In basketball, a horizontal circular metal ring supporting a net, through which players attempt to score points by throwing the ball.

Examples

Basketball hoop
He made an impressive shot through the hoop to win the game.
Kids spent hours practicing their throws at the basketball hoop in the park.
The hoop was slightly bent after the match, making shots more difficult.

Synonyms

  • basketball hoop
    1.4 0.14/m
    basketball hoop is the full metal ring with an attached net used in the game, while hoop is the shorter general name.
  • rim
    2.4 2.87/m
    rim is the metal ring that holds the net, while hoop is a general word for the ring and its net.
  • basket
    1.1 2.49/m
    basket is the whole scoring structure or the scored point, while hoop is the physical metal ring with a net.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basket - the entire basketball goal structure, including the hoop and net
