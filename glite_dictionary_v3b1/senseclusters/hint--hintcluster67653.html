hint

verb
suggest or imply indirectly
2.0 11/m
Pronunciation: / hÉªnt / (AmE), / hÉªnt / (BrE)
to suggest or indicate something indirectly; to make a statement or gesture that implies something without saying it directly

Examples

To hint at news
During the meeting, she hinted that a major change was coming.
He tried to hint at his feelings without being too obvious.
If you want a raise, you need to hint it more clearly to your boss.
