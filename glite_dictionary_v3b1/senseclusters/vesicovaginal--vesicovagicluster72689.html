vesicovaginal

adjective
bladder and vagina
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛsɪkoʊvəˈdʒaɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌvɛsɪkəʊvəˈdʒaɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving both the bladder and the vagina

Examples

Vesicovaginal fistula
A vesicovaginal fistula is a serious medical condition requiring surgery.
The doctor explained the risks of vesicovaginal complications after the procedure.
Specialists in urology and gynecology often treat vesicovaginal disorders together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urogenital - relating to the urinary and genital organs
