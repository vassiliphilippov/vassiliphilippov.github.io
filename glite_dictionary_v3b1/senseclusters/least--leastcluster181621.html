least

adjective
smallest in amount or degree
1.0 491/m
Pronunciation: / list / (AmE), / liÀêst / (BrE)
smallest in amount, extent, or degree; the superlative form of 'little' used with mass nouns, often preceded by 'the'.

Examples

Least interest
He showed the least interest in the project among his colleagues.
Of all the candidates, she had the least experience but showed great potential.
This route involves the least amount of traffic during rush hour.

Synonyms

  • minimum
    1.0 58/m
    minimum refers to the smallest allowed amount in rules or measurements, while least is the superlative form used with mass nouns.
  • minimal
    3.3 21/m
    minimal describes something very small or almost no size or effect, while least names the smallest amount or degree.
