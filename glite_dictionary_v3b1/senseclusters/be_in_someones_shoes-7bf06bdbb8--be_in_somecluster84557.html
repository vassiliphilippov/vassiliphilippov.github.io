be in someone's shoes

verb
imagine oneself in another’s situation
2.1 4.2
Pronunciation: / bi ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ʃuz / (AmE), / biː ɪn ˈsʌmwʌnz ʃuːz / (BrE)

Examples
