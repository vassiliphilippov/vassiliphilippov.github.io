murid

noun
mouse or rat family rodent
Pronunciation: / ˈmjʊrɪd / (AmE), / ˈmjʊərɪd / (BrE)
a rodent of the family Muridae, which includes mice, rats, and related species

Examples

A murid mouse
The scientist observed the murid as it explored the maze in the laboratory.
Many murids are used in research because of their genetic similarities to humans.
Farmers often try to keep murids away from their stored grain to prevent damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouse - a small rodent with a pointed snout and long tail
  • synonym: rat - a medium-sized rodent with a long tail
  • synonym: rodent - a small mammal with continuously growing front teeth
