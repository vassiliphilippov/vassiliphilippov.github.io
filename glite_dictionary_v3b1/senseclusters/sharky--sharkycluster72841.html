sharky

adjective
predatory or aggressive
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːki / (BrE)
behaving in a predatory, aggressive, or ruthless manner, especially in business or competition

Examples

Sharky business tactics
The new manager's sharky tactics quickly intimidated the rest of the team.
Her sharky approach to negotiations helped the company secure a better deal.
People described the investor as sharky because he always looked for weaknesses to exploit.

Related Terms
