litter lout

noun
person who drops litter in public
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtər laʊt / (AmE), / ˈlɪtə laʊt / (BrE)
a person who carelessly drops litter in public places

Examples

litter lout in public
If you see a litter lout in action, you should report them to the authorities.
Schools often run campaigns to discourage students from becoming a litter lout.
The park was left in a terrible state by a litter lout who abandoned bottles and wrappers everywhere.
