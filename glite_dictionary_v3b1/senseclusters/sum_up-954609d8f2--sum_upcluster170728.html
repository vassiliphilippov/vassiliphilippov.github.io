sum up

verb
add together to find a total
Pronunciation: / sʌm ʌp / (AmE), / sʌm ʌp / (BrE)
to find the total of several numbers or amounts by adding them together

Examples

To sum up the numbers

If you sum up these expenses, you'll see how much we spent on the trip.
They asked her to sum up the votes from each department.
