astraphobia

noun
morbid fear of thunder and lightning
Pronunciation: / ˌæstrəˈfoʊbiə / (AmE), / ˌæstrəˈfəʊbiə / (BrE)
An abnormal and persistent fear of thunder and lightning.

Examples

Severe astraphobia
His severe astraphobia made it impossible for him to relax during summer storms.
Many children develop astraphobia after experiencing particularly violent thunderstorms.
Those suffering from astraphobia may seek shelter at the slightest hint of thunder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phobia - an extreme or irrational fear of something
  • synonym: thunderstorm - a storm with thunder and lightning
