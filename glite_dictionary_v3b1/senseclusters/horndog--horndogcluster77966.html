horndog

adjective
sexually eager (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌdɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːndɒɡ / (BrE)
informal; showing strong or excessive sexual desire, often in a way that is obvious or inappropriate

Examples

Horndog behavior

She rolled her eyes at his horndog comments during the party.
The movie features a horndog character who is always chasing after women.
