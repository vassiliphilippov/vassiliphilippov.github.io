overgorge

verb
eat excessively or immoderately

Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɡɔrdʒ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɡɔːdʒ / (BrE)

Be careful not to overgorge during the festival; you'll regret it later.
He overgorged himself at the holiday buffet and felt ill all evening.
