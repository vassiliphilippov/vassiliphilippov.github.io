slopshop

noun
store selling cheap ready-made clothing
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑpˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈslɒpˌʃɒp / (BrE)
A shop or store that sells cheap, ready-made clothing.

Examples

A slopshop store
During the 19th century, many laborers bought their clothes at the local slopshop.
He worked in a slopshop before opening his own clothing business.
