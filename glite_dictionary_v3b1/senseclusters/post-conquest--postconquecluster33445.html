post-conquest

adjective
after a conquest
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈkɑːŋkwɛst / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈkɒŋkwɛst / (BrE)
relating to the period after a conquest, especially following a significant historical takeover

Examples

Post-conquest society
The post-conquest government introduced new laws to control the region.
Historians study post-conquest art to understand cultural changes after the invasion.
Many traditions were lost in the post-conquest era as new rulers imposed their customs.
