known

adjective
recognized or identified as existing or true
Pronunciation: / noʊn / (AmE), / nəʊn / (BrE)
recognized, identified, or established as existing or true; apprehended with certainty.

Examples

Known expert
He is a known expert in his field and is frequently invited to speak at conferences.
The police are searching for a known associate of the suspect.
This bakery is known for its delicious sourdough bread.
