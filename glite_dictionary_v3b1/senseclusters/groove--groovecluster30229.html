groove

verb
to dance to music
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɡruːv / (AmE), / ɡruːv / (BrE)
to enjoy music and move your body in time with its rhythm, often by dancing or swaying

Examples

To groove to music
Everyone was grooving to the beat at the concert.
She loves to groove when her favorite song comes on.
The crowd started grooving as soon as the band began to play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dance - to move your body to music
  • antonym: stand still - to remain without moving
  • synonym: sway - to move slowly or rhythmically from side to side
