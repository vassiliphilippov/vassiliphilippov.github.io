accent

verb
emphasize; give special importance to
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈækˌsɛnt / (AmE), / ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)

Examples

Good lighting can accent the beauty of a room.
The decorator chose curtains that accent the colors of the furniture.
