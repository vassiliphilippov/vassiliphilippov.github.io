vexed

adjective
annoyed or distressed
7.6
Pronunciation: / vɛkst / (AmE), / vɛkst / (BrE)
feeling or showing irritation, annoyance, or distress, especially from petty annoyances or persistent problems

Examples

Vexed face
His vexed expression told me he was bothered by more than just the noise outside.
Many citizens remain vexed about the lack of clear information from local government officials.
The vexed manager struggled to resolve the ongoing conflict among her employees.
