Maliki

noun
Islamic legal scholar
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmælɪki / (AmE), / ˈmælɪki / (BrE)
a follower or scholar of the Maliki school, one of the four main Sunni Islamic legal schools

Examples

Maliki scholar
The Maliki tradition is especially influential in North and West Africa.
She studied under a renowned Maliki jurist at the university.
Many communities in Morocco follow the teachings of the Maliki school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hanafi - a follower of the Hanafi school of Sunni Islamic law
  • synonym: Hanbali - a follower of the Hanbali school of Sunni Islamic law
  • synonym: Shafi'i - a follower of the Shafi'i school of Sunni Islamic law
