spiral

noun
curve winding around an axis at a constant angle
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪrəl /; / ˈspaɪ.rəl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪərəl /; / ˈspaɪ.rəl / (BrE)
a curve, shape, or object that winds around a central point, moving gradually away from or toward it (such as in geometry, nature, or decoration)
