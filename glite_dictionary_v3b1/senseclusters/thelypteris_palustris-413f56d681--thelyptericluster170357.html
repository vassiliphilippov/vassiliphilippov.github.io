Thelypteris palustris

noun
a wetland fern species
Pronunciation: / θəˈlɪptərɪs pəˈlʌstrɪs / (AmE), / θəˈlɪptərɪs pəˈlʌstrɪs / (BrE)
A fern species with deeply divided fronds, typically found in wetlands across the northern temperate regions.
