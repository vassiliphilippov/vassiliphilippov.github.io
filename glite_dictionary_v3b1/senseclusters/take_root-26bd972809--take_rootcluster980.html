take root

verb
begin to develop or become established
2.2
Pronunciation: / teɪk ruːt / (AmE), / teɪk ruːt / (BrE)
to begin to develop, exist, or become firmly established in a place, mind, or situation

Examples

New ideas take root
Environmental awareness is starting to take root among young people.
It took years for the new traditions to take root in the village.
The artist's influence slowly began to take root in the community.
