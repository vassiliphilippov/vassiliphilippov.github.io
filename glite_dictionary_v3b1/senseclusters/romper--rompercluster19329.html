romper

noun
one-piece play garment for children
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːmpər / (AmE), / ˈrɒmpə / (BrE)
A one-piece garment for young children, typically used for play, with short legs and sometimes sleeves, resembling a combination of shirt and shorts.

Examples

A baby romper
Many parents choose rompers for their convenience and comfort during play.
She dressed her baby in a soft cotton romper for nap time.
The toddler wore a brightly colored romper to the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: onesie - a one-piece garment for infants
