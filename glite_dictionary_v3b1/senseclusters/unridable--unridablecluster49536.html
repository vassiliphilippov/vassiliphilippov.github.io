unridable

adjective
not able to be ridden
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪdəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪdəbəl / (BrE)
not suitable or possible to be ridden, especially by a person or animal

Examples

An unridable bicycle
After the accident, the bicycle was completely unridable until it was repaired.
The wild stallion remained unridable despite the trainer's best efforts.
Heavy snow made the mountain trail unridable for even the most experienced cyclists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rideable - suitable or possible to be ridden
