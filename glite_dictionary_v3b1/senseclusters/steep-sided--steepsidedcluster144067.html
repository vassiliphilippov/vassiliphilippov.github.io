steep-sided

adjective
having very steep sides
6.3 3.5
Pronunciation: / stip ˈsaɪdɪd / (AmE), / stiːp ˈsaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of a slope, cliff, or object) having very steep or nearly vertical sides.

Examples

A steep-sided hill
A steep-sided hill dominated the landscape, making it difficult for vehicles to ascend.
The hikers climbed a steep-sided valley that was challenging to traverse.
The river flows through a steep-sided canyon formed over millions of years.
