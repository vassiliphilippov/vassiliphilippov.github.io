slug cutter

noun
printing tool for cutting lead spacers
Pronunciation: / slʌɡ ˈkʌtɚ / (AmE), / slʌɡ ˈkʌtə / (BrE)
a tool used in printing to cut leading and slugs—strips of lead used for spacing and support in assembled type forms

Examples

A slug cutter tool
A slug cutter is essential for setting up traditional letterpress type.
Historic print shops often display a slug cutter alongside type cases and composing sticks.
The printer used a slug cutter to prepare the necessary lead strips.
