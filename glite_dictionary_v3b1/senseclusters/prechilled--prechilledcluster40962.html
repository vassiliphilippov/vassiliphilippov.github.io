prechilled

adjective
cooled in advance
Pronunciation: / priːˈtʃɪld / (AmE), / priːˈtʃɪld / (BrE)
cooled to a low temperature before being used or served

Examples

A prechilled glass
For the best taste, serve the dessert in a prechilled glass.
The chef recommends using prechilled plates to keep the salad crisp.
They stored the drinks in a prechilled cooler before the picnic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilled - cooled to a low temperature
  • antonym: warm - having a moderately high temperature
