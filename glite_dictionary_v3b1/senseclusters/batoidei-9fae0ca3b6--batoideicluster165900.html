Batoidei

noun
superorder of cartilaginous fish (rays, skates)
Pronunciation: / ˌbeɪtoʊˈaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˌbeɪtəʊˈiːdaɪ /; / ˌbætɔɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a group of cartilaginous fish with flattened bodies, including rays, skates, guitarfishes, and sawfishes
