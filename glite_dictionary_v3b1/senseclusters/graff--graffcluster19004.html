graff

noun
informal graffiti
Pronunciation: / ɡræf / (AmE), / ɡræf / (BrE)
informal term for graffiti, especially street art or tagging on public surfaces

Examples

Colorful graff art
The abandoned building was covered in colorful graff from local artists.
She admired the creative graff that brightened up the city walls.
Many people see graff as a form of urban expression rather than vandalism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graffiti - writing or drawings scribbled or sprayed on a wall or other surface in a public place
  • synonym: street art - visual art created in public locations, usually unsanctioned
