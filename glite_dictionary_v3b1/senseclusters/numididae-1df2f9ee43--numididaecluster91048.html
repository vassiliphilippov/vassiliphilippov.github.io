Numididae

noun
family of birds including guinea fowl
Pronunciation: / nuːˈmɪdəˌdiː / (AmE), / njuːˈmɪdɪdiː / (BrE)
a group of birds including guinea fowl and their relatives, native to Africa and Madagascar
