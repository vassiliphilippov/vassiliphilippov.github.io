paddleboarding

verb
to stand or kneel on board
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
to take part in the activity of standing or kneeling on a board and moving across water using a paddle

Examples

To paddleboard on water
We spent the afternoon paddleboarding along the river, enjoying the peaceful scenery.
She tried paddleboarding for the first time during her vacation in Hawaii.
Many people enjoy paddleboarding as a way to exercise and relax on the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kayaking - to travel in a small boat using a double-bladed paddle
  • synonym: surfing - to ride on ocean waves using a board
