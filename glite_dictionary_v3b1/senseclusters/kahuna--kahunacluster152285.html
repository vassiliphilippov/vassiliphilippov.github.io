kahuna

noun
Hawaiian priest or expert
Pronunciation: / kəˈhuːnə / (AmE), / kəˈhuːnə / (BrE)
a Hawaiian priest, healer, or expert who performs religious ceremonies or traditional practices

Hawaiian kahuna ceremony
