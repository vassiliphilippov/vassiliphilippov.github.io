kahuna

noun
Hawaiian priest or expert
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈhuːnə / (AmE), / kəˈhuːnə / (BrE)
a Hawaiian priest, healer, or expert who performs religious ceremonies or traditional practices

Examples

Hawaiian kahuna ceremony
The villagers gathered to watch the kahuna perform a sacred ritual at sunrise.
As a respected kahuna, she was often asked for advice on health and spiritual matters.
Tourists learned about the important role of the kahuna in Hawaiian culture during the museum tour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: priest - a person authorized to perform religious ceremonies
  • synonym: shaman - a person regarded as having access to spiritual or healing powers
