behalf

noun
benefit or interest (esp. in set phrases)
4.2 2.5
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhæf / (AmE), / bɪˈhɑːf / (BrE)
for the benefit of someone or as the representative of someone (usually in the phrases 'on behalf of' or 'in behalf of')

Examples

On behalf of someone
Donations were made in behalf of the disaster victims to support relief efforts.

The lawyer spoke on behalf of the defendant during the trial.
She accepted the award on behalf of her colleagues who could not attend.
Thank you for your generous donation on behalf of the entire organization.
The lawyer spoke on behalf of the defendant in court.
