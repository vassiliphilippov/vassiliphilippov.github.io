worldless

adjective
spiritually detached
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldləs / (BrE)
not concerned with or attached to worldly or material matters; spiritual or otherworldly in character

Examples

Worldless peace
Her worldless calm suggested she cared little for money or social status.
The monk lived a worldless life, devoted entirely to meditation and prayer.
His worldless attitude made him uninterested in fame or material success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: materialistic - focused on material possessions and physical comfort
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or soul rather than material things
