hermeneut

noun
text interpreter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrməˌnut / (AmE), / ˈhɜːmɪˌnjuːt / (BrE)

Examples

The hermeneut interprets texts

As a skilled hermeneut, she was often consulted for her insights into philosophical texts.
The conference brought together every leading hermeneut in the field of theology.
