cyberbullying

noun
online harassment
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪbərˌbʊliɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪbəˌbʊliɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of harassing, threatening, or intimidating someone using digital devices or online platforms

Examples

Online cyberbullying cases
Schools are taking steps to prevent cyberbullying among students on social media platforms.
Victims of cyberbullying often feel isolated and afraid to speak out.
Many countries have introduced laws to address the problem of cyberbullying online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullying - the act of intimidating or mistreating someone repeatedly
  • synonym: harassment - persistent unwanted behavior that causes distress
  • antonym: support - help or assistance given to someone in need
