noncomprehension

noun
lack of understanding
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkɑmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən / (BrE)
a lack of understanding or inability to comprehend something

Examples

Expression of noncomprehension
Her expression of noncomprehension made it clear that the instructions were too complicated.
The teacher noticed the students' noncomprehension and decided to explain the topic again.
He responded with noncomprehension when asked about the new company policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comprehension - the ability to understand something
  • synonym: misunderstanding - a failure to understand something correctly
