homeopathy

noun
alternative medical system using minute doses
3.4
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmiːəˌpæθi /; / ˌhoʊ.miˈɑː.pə.θi / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi /; / ˌhəʊ.miˈɒp.ə.θi / (BrE)
a system of alternative medicine based on treating diseases with minute doses of substances that, in larger amounts, would produce symptoms of the disease in healthy people
