stiffener

noun
something that adds rigidity or support
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪfənər /; / ˈstɪfənɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɪfənə / (BrE)
a material or device added to make something stronger, more rigid, or to prevent it from bending or buckling

Examples

A stiffener material
Carpenters often use plywood as a stiffener in door construction.
His shirt had a plastic stiffener sewn into the collar to keep it sharp.
The engineers installed a steel stiffener to reinforce the bridge beam.
Steel stiffeners were welded to the web of the bridge girder.
The engineer recommended installing a stiffener to support the weakened beam.
Without a proper stiffener, the panel could buckle under heavy loads.
