creepy-crawly

noun
small crawling creature (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈkripi ˌkrɔli /; / ˈkripi ˈkrɔli / (AmE), / ˈkriːpi ˌkrɔːli /; / ˈkriːpi ˈkrɔːli / (BrE)
an informal word for a small creature that crawls, such as an insect, worm, or spider, especially one that makes people feel uncomfortable or afraid

Examples

A creepy-crawly in the room
I brushed a creepy-crawly off my arm while hiking through the forest.
My daughter screamed when she saw a creepy-crawly under her bed.
Some people love collecting creepy-crawlies from the backyard to observe them.
After turning over the log, we found several creepy-crawlies scurrying away.
He hates camping because of all the creepy-crawlies that come out at night.
My daughter screamed when she saw a creepy-crawly in the bathtub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arachnid - animal such as a spider or scorpion
  • synonym: bug - informal term for an insect
  • synonym: insect - small arthropod animal
