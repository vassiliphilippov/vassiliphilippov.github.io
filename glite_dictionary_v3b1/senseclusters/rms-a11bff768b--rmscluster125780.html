RMS

noun
records management system
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɑr ɛm ɛs / (AmE), / ɑː ɛm ɛs / (BrE)
a computer system or software used to record, store, organize, and manage business documents and records

Examples

Company RMS software
The company upgraded its RMS to handle a larger volume of digital records.
An effective RMS helps organizations comply with legal requirements for document retention.
Employees received training on how to use the new RMS for managing files.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DMS - a document management system for storing and organizing digital documents
  • synonym: database - an organized collection of data stored electronically
