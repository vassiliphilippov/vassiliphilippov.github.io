spinster

noun
an older unmarried woman
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪn.stɚ / (AmE), / ˈspɪn.stə / (BrE)
an unmarried woman, typically one who is older and beyond the usual age for marriage; often used with disapproving or old-fashioned connotations

Examples

An old spinster
In Victorian novels, a spinster was often portrayed as lonely or eccentric.
She lived alone with her cats, embracing the life of a spinster without regret.
The villagers viewed the spinster with a mix of curiosity and pity.

  • antonym: bachelor - an unmarried man
