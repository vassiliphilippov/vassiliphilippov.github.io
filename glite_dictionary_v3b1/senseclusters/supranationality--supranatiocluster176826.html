supranationality

noun
authority above nations
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˌnæʃəˈnæləti / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˌnæʃəˈnæləti / (BrE)
the principle or quality of having authority or influence that goes beyond individual nation-states, especially in organizations or policies
