Pinguicula

proper noun
carnivorous plant genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪŋˈɡwɪkjʊlə / (AmE), / pɪŋˈɡwɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
a genus of carnivorous plants, commonly called butterworts, that trap insects on their sticky leaves

Examples

Pinguicula traps insects
The leaves of Pinguicula secrete a sticky substance to trap small insects.
Many gardeners grow Pinguicula for its unique appearance and insect-catching ability.
Pinguicula species are found in both Europe and the Americas.
