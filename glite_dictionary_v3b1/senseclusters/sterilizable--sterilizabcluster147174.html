sterilizable

adjective
able to be made infertile
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr.ə.laɪ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be made incapable of producing offspring, especially by a medical or surgical procedure

Examples

Sterilizable animals
The veterinarian confirmed that the stray dog was sterilizable before scheduling the procedure.
Many animal shelters focus on finding homes for sterilizable pets to help control the population.
Before adoption, all sterilizable animals at the clinic undergo a routine surgery.
