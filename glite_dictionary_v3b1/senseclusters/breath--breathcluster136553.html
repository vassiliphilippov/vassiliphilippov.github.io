breath

noun
a brief pause or respite
2.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /breθ/ (AmE), /breθ/ (BrE)
a brief period of rest or pause from activity, allowing time to recover or regroup

Examples

He worked tirelessly for hours without so much as a breath between tasks.
The meeting was intense, but the coffee break gave everyone a much-needed breath.
