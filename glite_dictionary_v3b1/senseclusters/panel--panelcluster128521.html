panel

noun
temporary user interface window (computing)
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəl / (AmE), / ˈpænəl / (BrE)
In computing, a small temporary window in a graphical user interface that appears to request information from the user, typically dismissed with 'OK' or 'Cancel.'

Examples

A pop-up panel
She closed the confirmation panel after submitting the form.
The settings panel provides options to customize your preferences.
When you click the button, a panel will appear asking for your password.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialog box - a small window that prompts the user to enter information or make a choice
