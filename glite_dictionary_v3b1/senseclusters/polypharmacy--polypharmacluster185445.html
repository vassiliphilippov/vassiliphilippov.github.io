polypharmacy

noun
multiple medications
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈfɑrməsi / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈfɑːməsi / (BrE)
the use of multiple medications by a single patient, especially to treat complex or chronic conditions

Examples

Risk of polypharmacy
Elderly patients are at higher risk of adverse effects due to polypharmacy.
Doctors must carefully review medication lists to avoid problems caused by polypharmacy.
Managing polypharmacy is a key challenge in treating people with multiple chronic diseases.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monotherapy - treatment using a single medication or therapy
  • synonym: polytherapy - treatment using more than one therapy or medication
