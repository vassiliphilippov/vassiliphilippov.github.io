fisherwoman

noun
female professional fisher
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃərˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃəˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman whose occupation is catching fish.

Examples

A fisherwoman at sea
As a fisherwoman, she often wakes up before sunrise to begin her day at sea.
Many coastal villages depend on the work of skilled fisherwomen.
The fisherwoman returned to shore with a boat full of fresh catch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fisher - a person whose occupation is catching fish, regardless of gender
  • synonym: fisherman - a man whose occupation is catching fish
