tuna fish salad

noun
salad made with canned tuna
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːnə fɪʃ səˈlæd / (AmE), / ˈtjuːnə fɪʃ səˈlæd / (BrE)
A salad made mainly with chopped canned tuna, often mixed with mayonnaise and other ingredients.

Examples

A tuna fish salad sandwich
For lunch, he made a quick tuna fish salad with celery and onions.
She brought a bowl of tuna fish salad to the picnic.
The deli offers several varieties, but I always choose the tuna fish salad.
