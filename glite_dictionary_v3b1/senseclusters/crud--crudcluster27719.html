crud

noun
disgusting or dirty substance
Pronunciation: / krʌd / (AmE), / krʌd / (BrE)
a substance that is foul, dirty, or unpleasant

Examples

Sticky crud
After cleaning the attic, she was covered in crud from head to toe.
Grease and crud had built up in the old stove.
He wiped the crud off his shoes before coming inside.
