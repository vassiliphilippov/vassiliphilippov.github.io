cunny

noun
rabbit (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌni / (AmE), / ˈkʌni / (BrE)
an archaic or dialectal term for a rabbit

Examples

A clever cunny
The old tale spoke of a clever cunny who outwitted the fox.
In some rural dialects, people still refer to a rabbit as a cunny.
A small cunny darted across the meadow at dawn yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coney - an old term for a rabbit
  • synonym: rabbit - a small, furry mammal with long ears
