neurospicy approach

noun
unconventional, neurodivergent way of thinking
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈspaɪsi əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈspaɪsi əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
an informal and unique way of thinking or problem-solving, often associated with neurodivergence such as autism or ADHD

Examples

A neurospicy approach to thinking
Her neurospicy approach to organizing the event led to some creative and unexpected results.
In the meeting, he explained that his neurospicy approach often helps him see solutions others miss.
Teachers are learning to embrace each student's neurospicy approach rather than insisting on one way of thinking.

  • synonym: neurodivergent - relating to or showing neurodivergence
