sadistic tendency

noun
desire to cause others pain
5.2
Pronunciation: / səˈdɪstɪk tɛnˈdɛnsi / (AmE), / səˈdɪstɪk tɛnˈdɛnsi / (BrE)
a tendency or inclination to derive pleasure from inflicting pain or suffering on others

Examples

Counselors discussed ways to help patients manage a sadistic tendency in therapeutic settings.

The detective suspected the criminal had a sadistic tendency that drove his violent crimes.
