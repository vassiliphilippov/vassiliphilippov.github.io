wartlike

adjective
resembling a wart; warty
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrtˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtˌlaɪk / (BrE)
(of skin or surface) resembling a wart or covered with warty projections.

Examples

Wartlike skin bumps
Her skin showed several wartlike lesions that required examination by a dermatologist.
Some plant stems develop wartlike growths when infected by certain fungi.
The toad's wartlike bumps help it blend into rough terrain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: warty - covered with or resembling warts
