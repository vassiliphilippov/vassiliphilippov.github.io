astigmia

noun
optical defect affecting image clarity
Pronunciation: / əˈstɪɡmiə /; / əˈstɪɡ.mi.ə / (AmE), / əˈstɪɡmiə /; / əˈstɪɡ.mi.ə / (BrE)
a defect in the eye or an optical system that causes blurred or distorted images
