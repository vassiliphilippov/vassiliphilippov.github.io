Navajo

noun
Athabaskan language of the Navajo
Pronunciation: / ˈnævəˌhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈnavəhəʊ / (BrE)
the native Athabaskan language spoken by the Navajo people, primarily in the southwestern United States
