designer bag

noun
handbag by a famous designer
1.6
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnər bæɡ / (AmE), / dɪˈzʌɪnə bæɡ / (BrE)
A handbag made by a well-known or prestigious fashion designer, often expensive and seen as a status symbol.

Examples

A designer bag
His collection includes several rare designer bags from luxury brands.
She treated herself to a new designer bag after her big promotion.
The designer bag she was carrying caught everyone's attention at the party.
