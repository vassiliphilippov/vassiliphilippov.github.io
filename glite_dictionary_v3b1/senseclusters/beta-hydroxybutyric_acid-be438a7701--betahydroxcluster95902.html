A beta derivative of hydroxybutyric acid that is produced during the metabolism of fats and is found in the blood and urine, especially in cases of impaired metabolism such as diabetes or fasting.

Elevated beta-hydroxybutyric acid is a hallmark of diabetic ketoacidosis.
