cryocautery

noun
instrument for destroying tissue by freezing
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪoʊˈkɔːtəɹi / (AmE), / ˌkraɪəʊˈkɔːtəri / (BrE)
a medical instrument used to destroy tissue by freezing it, typically for surgical or dermatological procedures

Examples

Cryocautery device
Advances in cryocautery have made certain treatments less invasive than traditional surgery.
During the procedure, the surgeon relied on cryocautery to minimize tissue damage.
The dermatologist used a cryocautery to remove the wart from the patient's hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautery - instrument or process of burning tissue for medical reasons
  • synonym: cryosurgery - surgical technique using freezing to destroy tissue
