perambulatory

adjective
official walking inspection
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈræmbjəˌlɔːtɔri / (AmE), / pəˈræmbjʊlətəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving an official walk or inspection, especially to examine boundaries or property on foot

Examples

Perambulatory inspection
The committee conducted a perambulatory review of the park's boundaries before approving the new fence.
During the perambulatory survey, officials noted several discrepancies in the property lines.
A perambulatory inspection was required to confirm the estate's legal borders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perambulate - to walk through or travel over, especially for inspection
  • antonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved
