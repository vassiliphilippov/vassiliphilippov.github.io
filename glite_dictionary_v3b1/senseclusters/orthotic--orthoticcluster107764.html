orthotic

adjective
relating to body supports
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑtɪk / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to devices or supports designed to correct or assist the function of body parts, especially the limbs or spine

Examples

Orthotic support device
The doctor recommended an orthotic device to help support her injured ankle.
Many athletes use orthotic insoles to improve comfort and prevent foot pain.
An orthotic brace can assist with proper posture and spinal alignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orthopedic - relating to the correction of deformities of bones or muscles
