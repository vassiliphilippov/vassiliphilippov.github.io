cup holder

noun
current titleholder
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌp ˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkʌp ˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person, team, or organization that currently holds a trophy or championship title, especially in sports competitions

Examples

World cup holder
The cup holder will travel to the final to defend their title next month.
As the reigning cup holder, the team faces high expectations from their fans.
Every challenger wants to defeat the cup holder and claim the trophy for themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: champion - the winner of a competition or contest
  • antonym: contender - a person or team competing to win a title
