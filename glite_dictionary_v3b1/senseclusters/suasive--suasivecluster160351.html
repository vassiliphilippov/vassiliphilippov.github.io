suasive

adjective
persuasive quality
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈswesɪv / (AmE), / ˈswɑːsɪv / (BrE)

Examples

A suasive manner
Her suasive manner made everyone agree with her proposal.
The lawyer's suasive speech changed the jury's opinion.
He used suasive arguments to support his point during the debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unconvincing - not able to persuade or convince someone
