tightfistedness

noun
extreme stinginess
Pronunciation: / ˌtaɪtˈfɪstɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌtaɪtˈfɪstɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being extremely unwilling to spend money; extreme stinginess

Examples

Extreme tightfistedness
Because of his tightfistedness, the party was held in a cramped room with no decorations.
Her tightfistedness was legendary, as she would never part with a single coin unnecessarily.
The company suffered from the owner's tightfistedness, refusing to invest in even basic equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - the quality of being generous
  • synonym: stinginess - unwillingness to spend money
