SAR

noun
energy absorption rate
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs eɪ ˈɑːr / (AmE), / ˌɛs ɑː ˈɑː / (BrE)
the rate at which the body absorbs energy from an electromagnetic field, especially from devices like mobile phones

Examples

SAR value phone
The SAR value of a mobile phone indicates how much radiation your body absorbs during use.
Regulations require that all new devices have a SAR below a certain safety limit.
Consumers often check the SAR before purchasing electronic gadgets to ensure they are safe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiation dose - the amount of radiation absorbed by an object or person
