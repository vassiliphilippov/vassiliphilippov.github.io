logogrammatically

adverb
by means of logograms
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌloʊɡəˌɡræməˈtɪkli / (AmE), / ˌlɒɡəˌɡræməˈtɪkli / (BrE)
by means of logograms; in a way that uses or relates to logograms in writing.

Examples

Written logogrammatically
Chinese is written logogrammatically, using individual symbols to represent whole words or morphemes.
Some ancient languages conveyed meaning logogrammatically instead of using an alphabetic system.
The inscription was interpreted logogrammatically, identifying the logograms to determine the text's meaning.
