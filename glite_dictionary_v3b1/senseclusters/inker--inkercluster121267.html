inker

noun
printing device part
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkə / (BrE)
a device or machine part that applies ink to a surface, especially in printing equipment

Examples

Printing press inker
The technician replaced the worn inker to stop streaks appearing on the printed posters.
If the inker is not working properly, the images may come out faded or uneven.
Modern printers often have a removable inker for easy maintenance and cleaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printer - a machine that produces printed material
  • synonym: roller - a cylindrical tool or part that moves over a surface
