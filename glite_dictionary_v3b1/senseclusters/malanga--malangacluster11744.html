malanga

noun
tropical plant with edible tuber
Pronunciation: / məˈlɑːŋɡə / (AmE), / məˈlæŋɡə / (BrE)
A tropical American plant with starchy edible tubers that are cooked and eaten like yams or potatoes.

Examples

Cooked malanga tubers
Many Caribbean dishes use malanga as a staple ingredient, similar to potatoes.
She bought fresh malanga at the market to make a traditional soup.
The chef added diced malanga to the stew for extra texture and flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taro - tropical plant with starchy edible root
  • synonym: yam - edible starchy tuber of plants from the genus Dioscorea
