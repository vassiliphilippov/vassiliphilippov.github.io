root about

verb
search or rummage thoroughly

Pronunciation: / rut …ôÀàba ät / (AmE), / ruÀêt …ôÀàba ät / (BrE)
to search through or rummage in a place or container, especially in a thorough or disorderly way

Examples

To root about the garden

He had to root about in his desk to find the missing receipt.
She spent the afternoon rooting about in the garage for her old bike.
