comp

verb
to give for free
7.9
Pronunciation: /kɑːmp/ (AmE), /kɒmp/ (BrE)
to provide something, such as a meal, service, or item, free of charge as a courtesy or compensation

Examples

To comp a meal
The hotel comped our room after we complained about the noise.
Because it was my birthday, the manager comped my dessert.
They comped our tickets to the show as a special promotion.
The manager comped our dessert because our food arrived late.
After the show, the band was comped a meal at the restaurant.
They comped his hotel room as an apology for the inconvenience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charge - to ask for payment for something
  • synonym: cover - to pay for something on someone else's behalf
  • synonym: give - to provide something to someone
