sloppiness

noun
wet, muddy ground
Pronunciation: / ˈslɑpiˌnəs / (AmE), / ˈslɒpɪnɪs / (BrE)
the state of ground being wet, muddy, or soaked with water

Examples

Sloppiness of the field
After the heavy rain, the sloppiness of the football pitch made playing difficult.
The hikers complained about the sloppiness along the trail after last night's downpour.
We canceled our picnic because of the sloppiness in the park following the storm.
