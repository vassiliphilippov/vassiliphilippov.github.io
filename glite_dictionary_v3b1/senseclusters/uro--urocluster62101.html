uro

noun
urine-related prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊəroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix used in medical terms to refer to urine or the urinary system

Examples

Urogenital system
The term urogenital refers to both the urinary and reproductive organs.
In medical vocabulary, uro is often used as a prefix for words about urine.
The specialist recommended a urodynamic study to assess bladder function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urinary - relating to urine or the organs that produce and carry it
