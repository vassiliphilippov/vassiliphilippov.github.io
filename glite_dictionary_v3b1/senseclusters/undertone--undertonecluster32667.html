undertone

noun
quiet or hushed tone of voice
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌtoʊn / (AmE), / ˈʌndətəʊn / (BrE)
a quiet or hushed tone of voice that is not intended to be heard by everyone

Examples

A quiet undertone
He delivered his warning in an urgent undertone just before the meeting started.
She spoke in a low undertone so that no one else would hear.
There was a nervous undertone in their conversation as they discussed the secret.
