unscented

adjective
without fragrance
3.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛntɪd / (BrE)
having no added smell or fragrance

Examples

Unscented laundry detergent
Many people with allergies prefer unscented laundry detergent to avoid irritation.
The hotel provided unscented candles in the rooms for guests with sensitivities.
She chose an unscented lotion because strong perfumes give her headaches.
