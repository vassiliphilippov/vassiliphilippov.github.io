Lou Gehrig's disease

noun
progressive neurological disease (ALS)
4.1 0.35/m
Pronunciation: / luÀê Àà…°…õr…™…°z d…™Àåziz / (AmE), / luÀê Àà…°…õr…™…°z d…™ÀåziÀêz / (BrE)
A progressive neurological disease in which nerve cells controlling voluntary muscles deteriorate and die, leading to muscle weakness and atrophy; also known as amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS).

Examples

Lou Gehrig's disease symptoms
After being diagnosed with Lou Gehrig's disease, he became an advocate for increased awareness.
Doctors explained that Lou Gehrig's disease can lead to the gradual loss of muscle control.
The charity raises funds for research into Lou Gehrig's disease each year.
