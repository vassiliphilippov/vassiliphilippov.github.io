business activity

noun
action within a commercial enterprise
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ækˌtɪvəti / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
an action or set of actions undertaken as part of running a commercial enterprise, such as buying, selling, or providing services

Examples

Accounting auditors review all business activities to ensure compliance with the law.

The company is expanding its business activity into new international markets.
