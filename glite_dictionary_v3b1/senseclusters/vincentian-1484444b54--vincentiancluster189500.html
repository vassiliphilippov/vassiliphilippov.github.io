Vincentian

adjective
of Saint Vincent
8.1
Pronunciation: / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (AmE), / vɪnˈsɛnʃən / (BrE)
relating to Saint Vincent, the Vincentian religious order, or to Saint Vincent and the Grenadines

Examples

Vincentian values
The Vincentian tradition emphasizes service to the poor and marginalized.
She attended a Vincentian school known for its strong community values.
Many Vincentian customs are celebrated during the national festival.
