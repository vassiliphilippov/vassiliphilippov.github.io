crystallized

adjective
preserved with sugar (esp. fruits)
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstəlaɪzd / (BrE)
(of fruits) preserved by coating with or absorbing sugar, resulting in a sugary, crystalline texture.

Examples

Crystallized fruit pieces
I enjoy snacking on crystallized pineapple during the holidays.
She used crystallized ginger in the cake for an extra sweet flavor.
The dessert was decorated with crystallized violets for a touch of elegance.
She decorated the cake with crystallised violets and orange peel.
The crystallised fruit added a sweet and festive touch to the dessert.
You can buy crystallised ginger in most supermarkets.
