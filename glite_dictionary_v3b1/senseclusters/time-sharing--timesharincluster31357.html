time-sharing

noun
shared property use
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmˌʃɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmˌʃɛərɪŋ / (BrE)
an arrangement in which several people share the right to use a property, such as a vacation home, each for a specific period every year

Examples

Time-sharing vacation home
They invested in time-sharing so they could visit the mountain resort every summer.
The couple attended a presentation about time-sharing opportunities at a beachfront property.
Many families choose time-sharing to enjoy vacations without buying a second home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timeshare - a property with shared ownership or usage rights for set periods
