musk duck

noun
large wild duck, widely domesticated
5.9
Pronunciation: / mʌsk dʌk / (AmE), / mʌsk dʌk / (BrE)
a large, crested wild duck native to Central and South America, widely domesticated for its meat and eggs

Examples

A musk duck swimming
A pair of musk ducks glided silently along the edge of the wetland.
Farmers raise musk ducks for both their eggs and flavorful meat.
The musk duck is often seen in large ponds and lakes throughout South America.
