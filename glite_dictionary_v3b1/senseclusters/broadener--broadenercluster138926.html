broadener

noun
one who expands
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔːdənər / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdənə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something wider, more inclusive, or more extensive

Examples

A broadener of mind
Travel can be a real broadener of the mind.
Education is often seen as a powerful broadener of opportunities for young people.
Meeting people from different cultures is a great broadener of perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expander - a person or thing that increases the size or scope of something
  • antonym: narrower - a person or thing that makes something less wide or more limited
