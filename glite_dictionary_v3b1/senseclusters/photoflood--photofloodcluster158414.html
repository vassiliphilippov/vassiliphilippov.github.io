photoflood

noun
intense lamp for photography
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌflʌd / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌflʌd / (BrE)
a lamp that produces an intense, broad beam of artificial light, used especially in photography and filming

Examples

A photoflood lamp
A photoflood can quickly brighten up a dimly lit room for photography.
The studio used a photoflood to create even lighting for the portrait session.
You should replace the old photoflood bulbs for better illumination during your shoots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: floodlight - a powerful broad-beamed electric light
