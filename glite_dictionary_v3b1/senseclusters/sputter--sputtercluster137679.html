sputter

verb
make explosive spitting sounds
6.6 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈspʌt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈspʌt.ə/ (BrE)
to make a series of soft, explosive, or spitting sounds, often as a result of running unevenly or when failing to function smoothly

Examples

To sputter and stop
The candle sputtered and went out during the storm.
The engine sputtered before finally roaring to life.
The old car began to sputter as it climbed the steep hill.
