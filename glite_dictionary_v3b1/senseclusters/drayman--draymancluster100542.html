drayman

noun
driver of a dray
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪmən / (AmE), / ˈdreɪmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a dray (a low cart for transporting goods, especially beer)

Examples

The brewery hired a new drayman to transport kegs to local pubs.
