anaphrodisiac

adjective
reducing sexual desire
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ə.froʊˈdɪz.i.æk / (AmE), / ˌan.ə.frəʊˈdɪz.i.ak / (BrE)
(of a drug, substance, or agent) tending to diminish or suppress sexual desire.

Examples

An anaphrodisiac drug
He avoided foods rumored to be anaphrodisiac due to concerns about reduced desire.
Some medications act as an anaphrodisiac and may lower libido as a side effect.
The herb was historically considered an anaphrodisiac by practitioners of traditional medicine.
