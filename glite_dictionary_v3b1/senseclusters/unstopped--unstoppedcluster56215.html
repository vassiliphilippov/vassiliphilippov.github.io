unstopped

adjective
not blocked
4.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpt / (BrE)
not blocked, prevented, or brought to a stop; continuing without interruption or obstruction

Examples

With the road unstopped, traffic moved smoothly through the city.
His determination remained unstopped despite many setbacks.
