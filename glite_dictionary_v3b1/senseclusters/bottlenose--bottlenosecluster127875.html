bottlenose

noun
northern Atlantic beaked whale
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːt.əlˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈbɒt.əl.nəʊz / (BrE)
A species of beaked whale found in the northern Atlantic, distinguished by a bulbous forehead.

Examples

Bottlenose whale
Researchers have observed pods of bottlenose whales migrating along the northern Atlantic coastline.
The bottlenose is well adapted to deep-sea diving and can reach great depths in search of food.
Unlike dolphins, the bottlenose whale has a distinctive bulbous forehead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bottlenose dolphin - a common dolphin species with a bottle-shaped snout
