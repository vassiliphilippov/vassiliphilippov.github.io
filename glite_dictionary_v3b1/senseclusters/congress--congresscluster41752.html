congress

noun
formal meeting of representatives
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋɡrəs /; / ˈkɑːŋ.ɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrəs /; / ˈkɒŋ.ɡrɛs / (BrE)
a formal meeting or assembly of people, especially for discussion or decision-making
