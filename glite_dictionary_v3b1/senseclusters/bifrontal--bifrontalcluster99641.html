bifrontal

adjective
involving both frontal lobes
Pronunciation: / baɪˈfrʌntəl / (AmE), / baɪˈfrʌntəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting both frontal regions, especially the two frontal lobes of the brain

Examples

Bifrontal brain lesion
The MRI showed a bifrontal lesion that required immediate attention.
Doctors discussed the possible causes of the patient's bifrontal swelling.
A bifrontal approach was used during the neurosurgical procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bilateral - affecting or relating to both sides
  • antonym: unilateral - affecting or relating to one side only
