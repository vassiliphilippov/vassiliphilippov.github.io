pull

noun
act of exerting force to move something
1.0 3.35/m
Pronunciation: / pÊŠl / (AmE), / pÊŠl / (BrE)

Examples

A strong pull

The car needed an extra pull to get up the steep driveway.
After a long pull, the team finally reached the summit.
Completing the marathon was a real pull for everyone involved.
