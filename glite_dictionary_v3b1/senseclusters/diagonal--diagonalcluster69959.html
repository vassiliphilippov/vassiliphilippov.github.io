diagonal

noun
the slash punctuation mark (‘/’)
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæɡənəl / (AmE), / daɪˈaɡənl / (BrE)
a punctuation mark (‘/’) used to separate related items of information

Examples

A diagonal mark
In fractions, the numerator and denominator are divided by a diagonal.
Please use a diagonal to separate your choices on the form.
The website address included a diagonal between the folders.
