wimpled

adjective
wearing a wimple
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmpəld / (AmE), / ˈwɪmpəld / (BrE)
wearing or covered with a wimple, a cloth that covers the head and neck

Examples

Wimpled nun
The wimpled nun walked quietly through the cloister.
In the painting, a wimpled woman gazes serenely into the distance.
Medieval queens were often depicted as wimpled figures in elaborate robes.
