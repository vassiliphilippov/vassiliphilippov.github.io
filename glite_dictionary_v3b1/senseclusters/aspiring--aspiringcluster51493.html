aspiring

adjective
having ambitions or striving for success
4.5
Pronunciation: / əˈspaɪɹɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈspaɪəɹɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a strong desire to achieve a particular goal, especially recognition or advancement.

Examples

Aspiring entrepreneurs
He attended the workshop for aspiring entrepreneurs looking to start their own business.
Many aspiring musicians move to the city to pursue their dreams.
She is an aspiring writer hoping to publish her first novel soon.
