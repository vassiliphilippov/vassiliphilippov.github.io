guest bedroom

noun
bedroom for visitors
Pronunciation: / ɡɛst ˈbɛdˌruːm / (AmE), / ɡɛst ˈbɛdruːm / (BrE)
a bedroom in a home intended for use by visitors

Examples

A guest bedroom bed
The guest bedroom has a comfortable queen-sized bed.
They redecorated the guest bedroom before their relatives arrived.
We keep fresh towels in the guest bedroom for anyone who stays over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spare room - an extra room in a home, often for guests
