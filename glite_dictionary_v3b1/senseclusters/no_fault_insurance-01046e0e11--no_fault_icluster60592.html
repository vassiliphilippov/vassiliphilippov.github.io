A type of automobile insurance in which an injured party recovers damages from their own insurer, regardless of who was at fault for the accident.

Examples

No fault insurance system
No fault insurance systems aim to reduce legal disputes over minor traffic accidents.
Because she had no fault insurance, her medical costs after the crash were covered by her own policy.
Some states in the US require no fault insurance for all drivers.
