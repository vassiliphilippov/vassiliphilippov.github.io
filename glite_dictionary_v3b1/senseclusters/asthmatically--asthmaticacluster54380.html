asthmatically

adverb
with wheezing or sputtering
Pronunciation: / æzˈmæ tɪk li / (AmE), / æzˈmæt ɪk li / (BrE)
in a way that shows or resembles the symptoms of asthma, such as wheezing or difficulty breathing

Examples

To move asthmatically
The old bus climbed the hill asthmatically, its engine coughing and sputtering all the way.
He spoke asthmatically, struggling to catch his breath after running up the stairs.
The generator started asthmatically before finally running at full power.
He climbed the hill asthmatically, struggling to catch his breath at the top.
The old dog panted asthmatically after chasing the ball across the yard.
She spoke asthmatically, her words interrupted by short, shallow breaths.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without interruption
  • synonym: wheezily - in a way that produces a wheezing sound
