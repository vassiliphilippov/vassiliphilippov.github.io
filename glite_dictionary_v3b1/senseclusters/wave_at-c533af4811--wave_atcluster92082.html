wave at

verb
greet by moving hand
1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪv æt / (AmE), / weɪv at / (BrE)
to greet someone or attract their attention by moving your hand from side to side

Examples

To wave at someone
Every morning, she waves at her neighbor as she leaves for work.
He waved at me from across the crowded street.
The children excitedly waved at the passing train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greet - to give a polite word or sign of welcome or recognition
  • antonym: ignore - to deliberately pay no attention to someone
