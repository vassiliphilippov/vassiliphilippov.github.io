shootdown

noun
forceful rejection
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuːtˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈʃuːtˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a forceful rejection or public dismissal of a suggestion, idea, proposal, or claim

Examples

A shootdown of an idea
The committee's quick shootdown of the proposal surprised everyone in the meeting.
After the public shootdown of his idea, he decided to revise his approach.
Her suggestion received an immediate shootdown from the rest of the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acceptance - the act of agreeing to or approving something
  • synonym: dismissal - the act of rejecting or refusing something
