futurology

noun
study of predicting future trends
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of or systematic attempt to predict future developments, trends, or events, especially based on current conditions and data

Examples

Study of futurology
Interest in futurology has increased with rapid advancements in artificial intelligence.
She is an expert in futurology, advising companies about coming technological changes.
The conference brought together leaders in futurology to discuss the challenges of the next decade.
