Spenserian

noun
imitator of Spenser

Pronunciation: /spen.ˈsɪ.rɪ.ən/ /; / /spen.ˈsɪə.rɪ.ən/ (AmE), /spen.ˈsɪə.rɪ.ən/ /; / /spen.ˈsɪ.rɪ.ən/ (BrE)
