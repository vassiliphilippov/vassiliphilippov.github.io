go on

verb
continue speaking or talking
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɑn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɒn / (BrE)
to continue speaking, often after a pause or when encouraged to do so

Examples

To go on talking
After a brief pause, she went on explaining her point.
He sighed, then went on talking as if nothing had happened.
Please go on; I want to hear the rest of your story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continue - to persist in an activity or process
  • antonym: stop - to cease an activity or action
