comte

noun
French cheese or noble title
8.8
Pronunciation: / kɒmˈteɪ / (AmE), / kɒ̃ˈteɪ / (BrE)
a hard French cheese made from cow's milk, or a French noble title equivalent to count

Examples

A plate of comte
He ordered a plate of comte with bread and fruit at the café.
The comte greeted his guests with a formal speech at the castle.
She prefers comte over cheddar when making a cheese platter.

Related Terms
