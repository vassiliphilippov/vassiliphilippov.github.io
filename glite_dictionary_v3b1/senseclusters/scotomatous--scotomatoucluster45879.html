scotomatous

adjective
relating to or affected by a scotoma
9.9
Pronunciation: / skoʊˈtoʊmətəs / (AmE), / skəʊˈtɒmətəs / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by a scotoma; characterized by the presence of a scotoma.

Examples

Scotomatous eye defect
A scotomatous defect may indicate a problem with the optic nerve.
Researchers studied the size and shape of scotomatous areas in people with glaucoma.
The patient's visual field test revealed a scotomatous region in the right eye.

Related Terms
