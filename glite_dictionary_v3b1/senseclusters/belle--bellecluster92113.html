belle

noun
the most admired young woman
6.9
Pronunciation: / bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
A beautiful and charming young woman, especially one who is the most admired at a particular event or in a specific group.

Examples

The belle of the ball
At the party, she was undoubtedly the belle that everyone wanted to talk to.
In her youth, she was considered the belle of her small southern town.
The debutante arrived at the ball expecting to be the belle of the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beauty - a beautiful woman, especially one who is admired for her looks
