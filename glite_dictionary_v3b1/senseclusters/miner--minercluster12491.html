miner

noun
person who works in a mine
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə / (BrE)
a person who works in a mine, extracting minerals, coal, or other materials from the earth

Examples

Coal miner
In the 19th century, miner strikes were common in coal-producing regions.
Many miners risk their lives daily working underground for precious minerals.
The miner descended into the shaft before sunrise to start his shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mining - the process or industry of extracting minerals from the ground
