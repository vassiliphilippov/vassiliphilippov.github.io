tropospheric

adjective
of the troposphere
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌtroʊpəˈsfɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌtrɒpəˈsfɛrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the troposphere, the lowest layer of Earth's atmosphere where most weather occurs

Examples

Tropospheric layer
Scientists study tropospheric changes to better understand climate patterns.
Most commercial airplanes fly just above the tropospheric layer.
The tropospheric temperature can vary greatly depending on location and season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atmospheric - relating to the atmosphere
  • antonym: stratospheric - relating to the stratosphere, the layer above the troposphere
