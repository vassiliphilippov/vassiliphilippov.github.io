beetleweed

noun
a perennial herb with glossy, heart-shaped leaves
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêt…ôlÀåwiÀêd / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêt…ôlÀåwiÀêd / (BrE)
A tufted evergreen perennial herb with spikes of tiny white flowers and glossy, round to heart-shaped leaves, which turn coppery, maroon, or purplish in autumn.

The beetleweed plant
