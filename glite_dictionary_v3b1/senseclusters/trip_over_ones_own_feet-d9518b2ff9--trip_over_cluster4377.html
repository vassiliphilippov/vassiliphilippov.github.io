trip over one's own feet

verb
to stumble clumsily
6.3
Pronunciation: / trɪp ˈoʊvɚ wʌnz oʊn fit / (AmE), / trɪp ˈəʊvə wʌnz əʊn fiːt / (BrE)
to stumble or fall awkwardly due to one's own clumsiness

Examples

To trip over his own feet awkwardly
He tripped over his own feet and spilled coffee everywhere in front of his colleagues.
I was so nervous during my speech that I nearly tripped over my own feet walking to the stage.
She always seems to trip over her own feet when there's something important happening.
