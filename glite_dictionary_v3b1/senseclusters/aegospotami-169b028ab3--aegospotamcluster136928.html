Aegospotami

proper noun
ancient creek and battle site in Thrace
Pronunciation: / iːˌɡɑːspəˈteɪmaɪ /; / iːˌɡoʊspəˈteɪmaɪ / (AmE), / iːˌɡɒspəˈteɪmaɪ /; / iːˌɡɒspəˈtæmɪ / (BrE)
a creek or river in ancient Thrace (now in Turkey), famous as the site where the Spartan fleet defeated the Athenians in the final battle of the Peloponnesian War
