indiv.

adjective
abbreviation; single entity
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪv / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪv / (BrE)
abbreviation of 'individual', used to refer to a single person or thing rather than a group

Examples

Indiv. assignment
Each student must submit an indiv. assignment by the end of the week.
The survey results are based on indiv. responses rather than group data.
Please provide an indiv. report for every team member involved in the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collective - relating to a group rather than a single person or thing
  • synonym: individual - referring to a single person or thing, not collective
