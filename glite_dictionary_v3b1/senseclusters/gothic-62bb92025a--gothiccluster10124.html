Gothic

adjective
relating to the ancient Goths' language
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪk / (BrE)
of or relating to the language of the ancient Goths, an extinct East Germanic language.

Examples

Gothic script
Few modern scholars can read ancient Gothic manuscripts.
He specializes in the structure of Gothic grammar for comparative linguistics.
The Gothic Bible translation is one of the oldest Germanic texts.
