ingestible

noun
swallowable device
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛstɪbəl / (BrE)
a substance or device designed to be swallowed, especially for medication or internal monitoring purposes

Examples

A medical ingestible
The doctor prescribed an ingestible that would monitor the patient's digestive system.
Researchers are developing new ingestibles to deliver medication more effectively.
Before the procedure, she was given an ingestible camera to swallow for internal imaging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capsule - a small container, often for medicine, that is swallowed
  • antonym: injectable - a substance or device designed to be injected
  • synonym: tablet - a small, solid dose of medicine meant to be swallowed
