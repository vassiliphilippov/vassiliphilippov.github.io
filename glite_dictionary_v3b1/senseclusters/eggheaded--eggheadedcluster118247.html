eggheaded

adjective
overly academic
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈeɡ.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈeɡ.hed.ə/ (AmE), /ˈeɡ.hed.ɪd/ /; / /ˈeɡ.hed.ə/ (BrE)
very academic or intellectual, often in a way that seems impractical or out of touch with reality

Examples

An eggheaded professor
The professor's eggheaded explanation confused the students who wanted simple answers.
Some critics called the new policy too eggheaded to work in real life.
He tends to make eggheaded arguments that ignore everyday experience.
Her eggheaded approach to solving problems sometimes made her seem out of touch with reality.
The movie was criticized for its eggheaded dialogue that few people could understand.
He was teased for being eggheaded but was proud of his academic achievements.

  • synonym: intellectual - showing a high level of intelligence or academic ability
  • antonym: practical - focused on real situations and actions
  • synonym: theoretical - concerned with ideas rather than practical use
