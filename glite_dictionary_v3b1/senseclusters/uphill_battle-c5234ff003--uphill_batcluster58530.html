uphill battle

noun
very difficult struggle or challenge
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌhɪl ˈbætl̩ / (AmE), / ˈʌphɪl ˈbætl / (BrE)
a task, challenge, or struggle that is very difficult to achieve or overcome, often requiring significant effort against great odds

Examples

An uphill battle
For many minorities, obtaining equal rights has long been an uphill battle.
Getting the new policy implemented has been an uphill battle from the start.
Securing funding for the initiative turned into an uphill battle over several months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cakewalk - something very easy to accomplish
  • synonym: struggle - a forceful or violent effort to get free of restraint or resist attack
