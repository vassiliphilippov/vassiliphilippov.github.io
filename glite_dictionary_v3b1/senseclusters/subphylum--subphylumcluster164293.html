subphylum

noun
taxonomic group below phylum
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈfaɪləm / (AmE), / sʌbˈfʌɪləm / (BrE)
(Biology) A taxonomic group ranking below a phylum and above a class.

Examples

A subphylum group
Crustaceans are categorized within a subphylum distinct from insects.
The animal kingdom contains several subphyla that classify organisms with shared characteristics.
The newly discovered species was placed in a new subphylum due to its unique features.
