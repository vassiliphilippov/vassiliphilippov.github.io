Mount Athos

proper noun
autonomous monastic region in Greece
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ˈæθɒs / (AmE), / maʊnt ˈæθɒs / (BrE)
an autonomous peninsula in northeastern Greece, known for its many Greek Orthodox monasteries and spiritual heritage

Examples

Mount Athos peninsula
