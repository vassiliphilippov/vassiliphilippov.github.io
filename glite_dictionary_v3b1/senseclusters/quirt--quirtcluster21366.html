quirt

noun
whip with a leather thong, for riding
Pronunciation: / kwɝt / (AmE), / kwɜːt / (BrE)
a whip with a short handle and a leather thong at the end, traditionally used in horseback riding

Examples

A leather quirt
A leather quirt hung on the wall of the stable.
She skillfully flicked the quirt to guide the horse.
The cowboy carried a quirt attached to his saddle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whip - a flexible instrument for striking, often made of a strip of leather
