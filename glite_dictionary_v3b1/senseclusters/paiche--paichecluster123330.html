paiche

noun
edible fish meat
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪtʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪtʃeɪ / (BrE)
the flesh of the paiche fish when prepared or served as food, used as a culinary ingredient or dish

Examples

Grilled paiche dish

At the market, vendors offered fresh paiche fillets for sale.
Many restaurants in the region feature paiche as a specialty on their menus.
