natural resource

noun
material or energy source from nature
3.1 2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl ˈriː.sɔːrs/ /; / /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl ˈrɪ.zɔːrs/ (AmE), /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl ˈrɪ.sɔːs/ /; / /ˈnæ.tʃə.rəl ˈrɪ.zɔːs/ (BrE)
A material or source of energy found in nature, such as water, minerals, forests, or fossil fuels, that is valuable to humans or can be exploited for economic gain.

Examples

A natural resource like water
Conserving water as a natural resource is essential for future generations.
Forests are a vital natural resource that provide habitats for wildlife.
The country's economy relies heavily on the export of natural resources such as oil and minerals.
