Caesalpinioideae

noun
subfamily of leguminous plants (Caesalpinioideae)
9.9
Pronunciation: /siː.zæl.pɪ.nɪ.ˈɔɪ.diː/ /; / /keɪ.zæl.pɪ.nɪ.ˈɔɪ.diː/ (AmE), /siː.zæl.pɪ.nɪ.ˈɔɪ.diː/ /; / /keɪ.zæl.pɪ.nɪ.ˈɔɪ.diː/ (BrE)
a subfamily of flowering plants in the legume family, sometimes treated as a separate family (Caesalpiniaceae) in certain classification systems, including trees, shrubs, and lianas mostly with pinnate leaves
