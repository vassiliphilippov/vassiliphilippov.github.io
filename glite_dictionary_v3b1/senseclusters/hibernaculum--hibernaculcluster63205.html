hibernaculum

noun
winter survival bud
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪbərˈnækjələm / (AmE), / ˌhaɪbəˈnækjʊləm / (BrE)
a specialized bud or structure in certain plants that enables survival during winter dormancy

Examples

Plant hibernaculum
The aquatic plant forms a hibernaculum to survive the freezing winter months.
Scientists studied how the hibernaculum protects the plant's vital tissues during dormancy.
Each spring, new shoots emerge from the hibernaculum after the ice melts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bud - a small growth on a plant that can develop into a flower, leaf, or shoot
  • synonym: dormancy - a period when growth or activity stops in plants or animals
