horned viper

noun
venomous viper with horn-like spines
7.3
Pronunciation: / hɔrn(d) ˈvaɪpər / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈvaɪpə / (BrE)
a highly venomous viper of northern Africa and southwestern Asia, characterized by a horny spine above each eye

Examples

The horned viper snake
Hikers in northern Africa are advised to watch for the horned viper among the rocks.
The horned viper is easily recognized by the prominent spikes above its eyes.
The zoo's reptile house features a rare horned viper in its desert exhibit.
