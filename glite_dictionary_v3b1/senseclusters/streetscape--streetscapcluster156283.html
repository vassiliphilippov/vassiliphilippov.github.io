streetscape

noun
street scene image
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstriːt.skeɪp/ (AmE), /ˈstriːt.skeɪp/ (BrE)
a view, image, or artistic representation of a street and its surrounding features

Examples

A painted streetscape
The artist painted a vibrant streetscape showing the busy market in the morning.
Tourists admired the old town's unique streetscape with its colorful buildings and cobblestone roads.
A photographer spent hours waiting for the perfect light to capture the evening streetscape.
