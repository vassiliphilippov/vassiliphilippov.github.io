peacock flower

noun
tropical tree/shrub with bright red-orange flowers
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpiː.kɑk ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈpiː.kɒk ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a showy tropical tree or shrub native to Madagascar, widely grown in warm regions for its large clusters of scarlet and orange flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Poinciana

Examples

The peacock flower tree
Gardeners often plant peacock flower along avenues for its colorful canopy of flowers.
In full bloom, the peacock flower attracts many butterflies to the park.
The peacock flower is admired for its brilliant blossoms in tropical gardens.
