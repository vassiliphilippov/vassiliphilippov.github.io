drum

verb
make a rhythmic sound
1.3 1.46/m
Pronunciation: / drÊŒm / (AmE), / drÊŒm / (BrE)
to make a continuous rhythmic sound, often by repeatedly hitting a surface

Examples

To drum fingers
A woodpecker drummed on the tree outside my window.
He absentmindedly drummed his fingers on the armrest during the meeting.
The rain drummed steadily on the roof all night long.
