Muhammad

proper noun
the prophet and founder of Islam
3.5
Pronunciation: / muˈhɑːməd / (AmE), / mʊˈhɑːməd / (BrE)
The Arab prophet (570–632) who, according to Islam, was the last messenger of Allah and the founder of Islam.
