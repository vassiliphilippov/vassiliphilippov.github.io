slate pencil

noun
pencil for writing on slate
7.1 7.8
Pronunciation: /sleɪt ˈpen.səl/ /; / /sleɪt ˈpen.sɪl/ (AmE), /sleɪt ˈpen.səl/ /; / /sleɪt ˈpen.sɪl/ (BrE)
A slender rod made of soft slate or soapstone, used for writing on slate boards, especially in classrooms before the widespread use of paper.

Examples

A slate pencil for writing
His grandmother still keeps her childhood slate pencil as a treasured keepsake.
In old schools, children used a slate pencil to practice their handwriting on small slates.
The museum displayed a slate pencil alongside other historical classroom tools.
