camera operator

noun
person who operates a movie or TV camera
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmərə ˈɑpəˌreɪtər / (AmE), / ˈkæmərə ˈɒpəreɪtə / (BrE)
a person responsible for operating a movie or television camera during filming or broadcasting

Examples

A camera operator films scenes
A good camera operator needs to anticipate the director's instructions and move the camera fluidly.
During the live event, the camera operator skillfully tracked the main speaker across the stage.
The camera operator adjusted the focus to capture the actors' expressions perfectly.
