gung

verb
to clog with grime
Pronunciation: / ɡʌŋ / (AmE), / ɡʌŋ / (BrE)
to cover, clog, or make dirty with a sticky or greasy substance.

Examples

To gung up pipes
If you don't change the oil regularly, sludge can gung up the engine parts.
The drain was gunged with hair and soap residue after months of use.
Be careful not to gung the gears with too much grease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clog - to block or obstruct with a thick or sticky substance
