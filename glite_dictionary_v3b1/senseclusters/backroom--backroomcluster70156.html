backroom

noun
private storage room
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈbækˌruːm / (BrE)
a room at the back of a building, often used for storage, private work, or activities not meant for the public

Examples

Store backroom
The manager kept important documents locked in the backroom of the store.
Employees gathered in the backroom to discuss the new project privately.
Old furniture and unused supplies were stacked in the dusty backroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: office - a room where people work, often privately
  • antonym: showroom - a room where goods are displayed to the public
  • synonym: storeroom - a room used for storing things
