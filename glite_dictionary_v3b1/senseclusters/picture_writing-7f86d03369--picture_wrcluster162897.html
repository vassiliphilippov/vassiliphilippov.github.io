picture writing

noun
writing using pictorial symbols
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪktʃɚ ˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɪktʃə ˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
a system of writing that uses pictorial symbols to represent words, ideas, or sounds, rather than alphabetic or phonetic symbols.

Examples

Picture writing system
Ancient Egyptians used picture writing called hieroglyphics to record their language.
Researchers study how children interpret picture writing in educational settings.
The museum displays artifacts covered in picture writing from early Native American cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ideograph - a written character symbolizing the idea of a thing without indicating the sounds used to say it
  • synonym: pictograph - a pictorial symbol for a word or phrase
