undistilled

adjective
not purified by distillation
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈstɪld / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈstɪld / (BrE)
not purified or concentrated by distillation; in its original or natural state

Examples

Undistilled water
The chemist specified that only undistilled water should be used for this procedure.
Some traditional recipes call for undistilled vinegar to preserve the natural flavors.
The sample contained undistilled alcohol, which affected the results of the experiment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distilled - purified or concentrated by distillation
  • antonym: pure - not mixed with anything else; clean
  • synonym: raw - in a natural, unprocessed state
