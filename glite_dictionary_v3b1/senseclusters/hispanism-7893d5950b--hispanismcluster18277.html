Hispanism

noun
Spanish borrowing
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪspəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈhɪspənɪzəm / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or cultural element borrowed from Spanish into another language

Examples

A Hispanism word
The word 'siesta' is a well-known Hispanism used in many languages.
Many American cities have names that are Hispanisms reflecting Spanish influence.
The tradition of eating tapas is a cultural Hispanism adopted in several countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gallicism - a word or expression borrowed from French into another language
