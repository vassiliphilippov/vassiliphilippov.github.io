icicle plant

noun
Old World annual plant with icy-looking foliage
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsɪkəl ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪkəl ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
An Old World annual plant widely naturalized in warm regions, with white flowers and fleshy, hairy foliage that looks as if covered with ice.

Examples

The icicle plant flower
Gardeners appreciate the distinctive appearance of the icicle plant in rock gardens and borders.
