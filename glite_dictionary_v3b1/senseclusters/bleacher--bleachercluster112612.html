bleacher

noun
outdoor spectator seating
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbliːtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbliːtʃə / (BrE)
a tiered bench or set of benches for spectators at a sports event, usually outdoors and uncovered

Examples

Crowded bleachers
The crowd in the bleachers erupted in cheers as the home team scored a goal.
We arrived early to get good seats in the bleachers before the baseball game started.
During the summer, sitting on the bleachers can get very hot under the sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: field - the area where a sports event takes place
  • synonym: grandstand - a large, covered seating area for spectators at a sports event
