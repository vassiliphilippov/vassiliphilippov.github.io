cable television

noun
television delivered by cable
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌtɛl.əˈvɪʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl ˌtɛl.ɪˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a system for delivering television programs to viewers through cables (not by broadcast or satellite)

Examples

Cable television channels

She subscribed to cable television to watch her favorite sports channels.
The popularity of cable television began to surge in the 1980s.
