secretarial assistant

noun
person doing clerical and secretarial work
Pronunciation: / sɪˌkrɛˈtɛriəl əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / sɪˌkrɪˈtɛəriəl əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a person who handles correspondence and clerical work for a supervisor or organization

Examples

As a secretarial assistant, he supported several managers with their daily administrative tasks.
Her duties as a secretarial assistant included filing paperwork and preparing reports.
The company hired a new secretarial assistant to manage phone calls and schedule meetings.
