fluorescently

adverb
using fluorescence
7.2
Pronunciation: / flʊˈrɛsəntli / (AmE), / flʊəˈrɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that uses or involves fluorescence, especially for detection or imaging purposes

Examples

Fluorescently labeled cells
The sample was fluorescently labeled to make the cells visible under the microscope.
Researchers tracked the movement of proteins by tagging them fluorescently in the laboratory.
The bacteria were fluorescently stained to help scientists identify them more easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luminescently - in a way that emits light not caused by heat
