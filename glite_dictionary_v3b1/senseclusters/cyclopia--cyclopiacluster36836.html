cyclopia

noun
congenital disorder with one eye
9.4
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkloʊpiə / (AmE), / sɪˈkləʊpiə / (BrE)
A rare congenital disorder characterized by the presence of a single eye located in the area normally occupied by the root of the nose.

Examples

Cyclopia birth defect
Cyclopia is extremely rare and usually results in death shortly after birth.
The condition known as cyclopia is named after the mythical Cyclops.
The medical team studied a case of cyclopia in a newborn lamb.
