nobby

adjective
bumpy or knobbly
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑbi / (AmE), / ˈnɒbi / (BrE)
having knobs, lumps, or rounded bumps on the surface; knobbly or bumpy in texture

Examples

The sculpture was intentionally nobby to give it a more natural appearance.
