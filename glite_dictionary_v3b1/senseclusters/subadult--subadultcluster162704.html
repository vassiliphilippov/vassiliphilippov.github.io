subadult

noun
immature animal stage
7.1
Pronunciation: /sʌb.ˈæd.ʌlt/ /; / /səb.ˈæd.ʌlt/ (AmE), /səb.ˈæd.ʌlt/ /; / /sʌb.ˈæd.ʌlt/ (BrE)
