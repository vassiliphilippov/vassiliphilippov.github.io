subadult

noun
immature animal stage
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbəˈdʌlt / (AmE), / ˌsʌbˈædʌlt / (BrE)
an animal or person that is not fully grown but is no longer a juvenile

Examples

A subadult animal
The biologist observed a subadult crab that had not yet reached full size.
Many subadults in the population are difficult to distinguish from adults except for their smaller size.
The zoo received a subadult eagle that will be released into the wild once it matures.
The wildlife reserve released several subadult turtles into the sea.
A subadult bear may look large but is not yet fully mature.
Researchers observed a group of subadult lions learning to hunt together.
The wildlife reserve is home to several subadult lions.
Researchers observed a group of subadults learning to hunt on their own.
A subadult may display some adult behaviors but is not yet fully mature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adolescent - a young person or animal developing into an adult
  • degree higher: adult - a fully mature animal
  • degree lower: juvenile - an animal that is not yet mature
