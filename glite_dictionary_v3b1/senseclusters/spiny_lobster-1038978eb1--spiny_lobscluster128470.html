spiny lobster

noun
warm-water lobster species without large claws
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪni ˈlɑbstɚ /; / ˈspaɪni ˈlɑːbstər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪni ˈlɒbstə / (BrE)
a large edible sea animal with a spiny shell and no large claws, found in warm or tropical seas
