entitled

adjective
expecting special treatment
5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtaɪ.təld/ /; / /en.ˈtaɪ.təld/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtaɪ.təld/ /; / /en.ˈtaɪ.təld/ (BrE)

Examples

Entitled attitude

Some students act entitled and expect good grades without putting in the effort.
His entitled attitude made it difficult for him to work well with others.
