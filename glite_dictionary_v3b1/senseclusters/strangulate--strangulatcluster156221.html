strangulate

verb
kill by blocking the airway by squeezing
Pronunciation: / ˈstræŋɡjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈstræŋɡjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to kill by squeezing or compressing someone's throat so as to cut off their air supply

Examples

To strangulate the neck
Doctors confirmed the cause of death was that the woman had been strangulated.
The murderer attempted to strangulate his victim during the robbery.
The suspect confessed to strangulating his opponent after a heated argument.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strangle - kill by squeezing the throat and cutting off air
  • degree lower: suffocate - die or kill through lack of air
