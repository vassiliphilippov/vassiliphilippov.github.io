runaway robin

noun
trailing mint plant with purplish flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnəˌweɪ ˈrɑːbɪn / (AmE), / ˈrʌnəˌweɪ ˈrɒbɪn / (BrE)
A trailing aromatic plant of the mint family with rounded leaves and small purplish flowers, often grown in hanging baskets; native to Europe and naturalized in North America.

Examples

Runaway robin plant
Runaway robin is a popular choice for hanging baskets due to its delicate trailing stems.
Gardeners often plant runaway robin as a ground cover in shady areas.
The small purplish flowers of runaway robin add a touch of color to early spring gardens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ground ivy - another common name for the same plant species (Glechoma hederacea)
