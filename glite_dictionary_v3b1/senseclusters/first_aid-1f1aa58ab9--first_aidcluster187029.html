first aid

noun
emergency care before medical help
Pronunciation: / fɜrst eɪd / (AmE), / fɜːst eɪd / (BrE)
emergency care or treatment given to an ill or injured person before regular medical services can be obtained.

Examples

Basic first aid
Everyone should learn basic first aid in case of an emergency.
She gave him first aid before the ambulance arrived.
The camp counselor took a first aid course over the summer.
