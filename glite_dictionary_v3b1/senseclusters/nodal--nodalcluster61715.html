nodal

adjective
at a node
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊdəl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊdəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated at a node or central point, especially in a network or system

Examples

The nodal point
The nodal region of the plant is where new branches often grow.
Engineers analyzed the nodal connections to improve the stability of the bridge.
In the diagram, each nodal point represents a key intersection in the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central - located at or forming the center
  • antonym: peripheral - relating to the outer edges or periphery
