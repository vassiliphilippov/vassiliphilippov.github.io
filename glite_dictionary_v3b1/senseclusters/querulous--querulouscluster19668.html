querulous

adjective
habitually complaining or whining
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɛr.yə.ləs / (AmE), / ˈkwɛr.ʊ.ləs / (BrE)
complaining in a petulant or whining manner, especially habitually

Examples

A querulous voice
Even his friends found his querulous complaints exhausting after a while.
He became increasingly querulous as the day wore on, grumbling about every little inconvenience.
The querulous tone in her voice made it clear she was dissatisfied with the plan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complaining - expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance about something
  • antonym: contented - feeling or showing satisfaction with one's possessions, status, or situation
