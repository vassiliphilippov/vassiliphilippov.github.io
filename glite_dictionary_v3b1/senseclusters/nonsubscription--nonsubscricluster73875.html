nonsubscription

noun
refusal to sign
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səbˈskrɪp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səbˈskrɪp.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of not signing or not agreeing to a formal statement, creed, or document

Examples

Nonsubscription to a statement
His nonsubscription to the agreement surprised the rest of the committee.
The church faced division over the issue of nonsubscription to its official creed.
Public nonsubscription to the new policy led to further debate among the members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissent - refusal to agree with an official opinion or decision
  • antonym: subscription - the act of signing or agreeing to a statement or document
