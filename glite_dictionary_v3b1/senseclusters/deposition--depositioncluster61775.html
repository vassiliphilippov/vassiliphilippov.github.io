deposition

noun
legal pretrial witness statement

Pronunciation: /dep.ə.ˈzɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /dep.ə.ˈzɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
(law) a formal statement made by a witness under oath, taken outside of court before a trial.

Examples

Court deposition

The lawyer scheduled the deposition for next Friday afternoon.
