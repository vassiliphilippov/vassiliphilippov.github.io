fit

adjective
ready or strongly disposed to do
7.2 3.1
Pronunciation: / fɪt / (AmE), / fɪt / (BrE)
(usually followed by 'to' or 'for') on the point of or strongly disposed to do something; ready or inclined to a particular action, often used in set phrases.

Examples

Fit to drop
After working all night, he was fit to drop from exhaustion.
She was so angry she looked fit to scream at anyone who bothered her.
The audience was laughing fit to burst at the comedian's jokes.
