recordless

adjective
not recorded
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkərdləs / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɔːdləs / (BrE)
not captured, preserved, or released as an audio or visual recording

Examples

Recordless performance
The artist's early performances were recordless, so fans can only imagine what they were like.
Many important historical events remain recordless because no one documented them at the time.
The meeting was intentionally kept recordless to encourage open and honest discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recorded - captured or preserved in an audio or visual recording
  • synonym: unrecorded - not captured or preserved in a recording
