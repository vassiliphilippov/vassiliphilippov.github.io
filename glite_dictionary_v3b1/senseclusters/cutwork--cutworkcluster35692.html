cutwork

noun
type of openwork embroidery
9.8 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk åtÀåw…ùk / (AmE), / Ààk åtÀåw…úÀêk / (BrE)
A type of embroidery in which the design is outlined in buttonhole stitch and the fabric between the outlines is cut away.

Examples

Intricate cutwork embroidery
Antique linens with intricate cutwork are highly valued by collectors.
She learned how to do cutwork embroidery from her grandmother.
The tablecloth was decorated with delicate cutwork along its edges.
