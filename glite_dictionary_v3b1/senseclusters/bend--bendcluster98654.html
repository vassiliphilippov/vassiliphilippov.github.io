bend

noun
diagonal band on a shield (heraldry)
Pronunciation: / bɛnd / (AmE), / bɛnd / (BrE)
A wide diagonal band running from the top right to the bottom left of a shield in heraldry.

Examples

A red bend on a shield
A red bend on the shield symbolizes military strength in heraldry.

The family's coat of arms features a silver bend across a blue field.
