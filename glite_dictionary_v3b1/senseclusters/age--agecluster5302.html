age

noun
a long or indefinite period of time
Pronunciation: / eɪdʒ / (AmE), / eɪdʒ / (BrE)
A very long or indefinite period of time.

Examples

For ages
I've been waiting for ages for your reply to my email.
It seemed to take ages for the train to arrive this morning.
We haven't seen each other in ages and have a lot to catch up on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epoch - a particular period of time in history or a person's life
  • synonym: era - a long and distinct period of history
