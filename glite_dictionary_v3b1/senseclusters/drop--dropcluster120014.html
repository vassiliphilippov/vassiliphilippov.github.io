drop

noun
steep high rock face or descent
5.3 3/m
Pronunciation: / drÉ‘p / (AmE), / drÉ’p / (BrE)
a steep, high face of rock or a sudden vertical descent, especially in a landscape or along a path

Examples

A steep drop
Climbers must always be aware of the steep drop on the north side of the peak.
He peered over the drop and felt his stomach lurch at the dizzying height.
The narrow trail ran right along the edge of a sheer drop.
