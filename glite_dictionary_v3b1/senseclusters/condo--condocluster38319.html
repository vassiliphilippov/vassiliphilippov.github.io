condo

noun
individually owned apartment unit
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.doʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dəʊ / (BrE)
a private apartment or unit within a building or complex in which each unit is individually owned

Examples

A condo apartment
After selling their house, they decided to move into a downtown condo.
She recently purchased a condo overlooking the ocean.
The condo comes with access to a swimming pool and gym.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment - a set of rooms for living in, typically on one floor of a building
