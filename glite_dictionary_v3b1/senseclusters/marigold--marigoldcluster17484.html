marigold

noun
bright yellow or orange flowering plant
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmærɪˌgoʊld / (AmE), / ˈmærɪɡəʊld / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Tagetes, widely cultivated for their brightly colored yellow or orange flowers

Examples

A marigold flower
A bouquet of marigold flowers brightened up the kitchen table.
She planted marigolds along the edge of the vegetable patch to keep pests away.
The garden was filled with bright marigold blossoms that glowed in the sunlight.
