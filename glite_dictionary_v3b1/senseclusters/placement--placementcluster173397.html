placement

noun
finding a job or position for someone
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
the act or process of finding a suitable job or position for someone, especially through an agency

Examples

Job placement
His placement in the marketing department was arranged by a recruiter.
Many universities have placement services to help students find internships.
The agency offers placement assistance to recent graduates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recruitment - the process of finding and hiring employees
