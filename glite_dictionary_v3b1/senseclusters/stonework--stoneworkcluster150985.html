stonework

noun
masonry or structures made with stone
4.2 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsto änÀåw…ùk / (AmE), / Ààst…ô änw…úÀêk / (BrE)
the parts of a building or structure made from stone, or the skill or work of building with stone

Examples

Restorers carefully cleaned the intricate stonework on the cathedral's facade.

The ancient castle's stonework has stood for centuries despite harsh weather.
