Atromid-S

noun
trade name for a lipid-lowering drug
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌætrəˌmɪd ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌatrəˌmɪd ˈɛs / (BrE)
A trade name for a drug (clofibrate) used to reduce lipid levels in the blood, often prescribed to treat certain cardiovascular diseases.

Examples

Atromid-S drug
After several months on Atromid-S, her blood lipid readings improved significantly.
Many older studies reference the use of Atromid-S in cardiovascular treatment.
The doctor prescribed Atromid-S to help lower his cholesterol levels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clofibrate - a drug used to lower cholesterol and triglyceride levels in the blood
