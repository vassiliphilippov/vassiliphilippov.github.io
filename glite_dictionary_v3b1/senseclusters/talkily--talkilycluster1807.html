talkily

adverb
in a chatty or talkative manner
9.3 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑː.kɪ.li/ /; / /ˈtɑː.kə.li/ /; / /ˈtɔː.kɪ.li/ (AmE), /ˈtɔː.kɪ.li/ /; / /ˈtɔː.kə.li/ (BrE)
In a chatty or talkative manner; speaking in a loquacious or garrulous way.

Examples

Speak talkily all evening
She spoke talkily, filling every silence with stories and jokes.
The meeting grew increasingly talkily as everyone shared their weekend plans.
Whenever he's nervous, he tends to act talkily around strangers.
