smooth crabgrass

noun
a low-growing annual weed (Digitaria ischaemum)
Pronunciation: / smuð ˈkræbˌɡræs / (AmE), / smuːð ˈkrabˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a low-growing annual weed (Digitaria ischaemum) with smooth stems and leaves that spreads by seeds and by rooting at the lower joints
