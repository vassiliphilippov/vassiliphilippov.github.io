isoprene

noun
synthetic rubber chemical
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsəˌpriːn / (AmE), / ˈaɪsəˌpriːn / (BrE)
a colorless liquid hydrocarbon (C5H8) used in making synthetic rubber and as a building block of natural rubber and other molecules
