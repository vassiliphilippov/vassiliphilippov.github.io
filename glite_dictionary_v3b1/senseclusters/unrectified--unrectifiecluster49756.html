unrectified

adjective
not converted to DC
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛktɪfaɪd / (BrE)
not converted from alternating current to direct current, especially referring to an electrical signal that has not passed through a rectifier

Examples

Unrectified current
The oscilloscope displayed an unrectified waveform before the signal entered the power supply circuit.
Engineers checked the unrectified current to ensure the rectifier was functioning properly.
An unrectified voltage can damage sensitive electronic components if not properly managed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rectified - converted from alternating current to direct current
