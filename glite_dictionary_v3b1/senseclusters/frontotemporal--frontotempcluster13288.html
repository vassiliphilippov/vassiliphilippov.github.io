frontotemporal

adjective
frontal and temporal lobes
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfrʌntoʊˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˌfrʌntəʊˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
relating to both the frontal and temporal lobes of the brain

Examples

Frontotemporal brain area

Certain types of dementia are classified as frontotemporal because they affect specific brain regions.
Researchers are studying frontotemporal changes to better understand language disorders.
