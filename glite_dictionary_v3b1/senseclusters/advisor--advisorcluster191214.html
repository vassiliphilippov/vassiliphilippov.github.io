advisor

noun
automated financial advice service
3.6
Pronunciation: /æd.ˈvaɪ.zər/ /; / /ə.ˈvaɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈvaɪ.zə/ /; / /æd.ˈvaɪ.zə/ (BrE)
(in finance and technology) an automated software service or app that provides personalized financial advice or manages investments using algorithms and little or no human intervention.

The startup launched an advisor that builds low-cost portfolios based on users' risk profiles.
After the market drop, she checked the advisor app to rebalance her ETFs automatically.
