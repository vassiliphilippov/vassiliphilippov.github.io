natural

noun
person innately suited to succeed
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl / (BrE)
a person who is innately gifted or regarded as certain to succeed at a particular activity

Examples

A natural at chess
Ever since childhood, everyone could see that he was a natural on the piano.
They hired him right away—he was a natural for the role.
With her athleticism and quick learning, she’s a natural at basketball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prodigy - a young person with exceptional qualities or abilities
  • synonym: talent - a person with a natural aptitude or skill
