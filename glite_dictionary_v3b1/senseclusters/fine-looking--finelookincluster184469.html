fine-looking

adjective
attractive; pleasing in appearance
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /faɪn ˈlʊk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /faɪn ˈlʊk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
Attractive or pleasing in appearance, especially because of conformity to ideals of form and proportion.

Examples

A fine-looking man
He was a fine-looking young man with a bright smile and kind eyes.
She wore a simple dress that made her look especially fine-looking at the party.
The fine-looking garden drew many compliments from visitors.
