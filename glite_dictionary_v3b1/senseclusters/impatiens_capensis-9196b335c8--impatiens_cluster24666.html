Impatiens capensis

noun
North American flowering plant (jewelweed)
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpeɪʃənz kəˈpɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ɪmˈpeɪ.ʃənz kəˈpɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A North American annual plant with orange or yellow flowers, commonly growing in moist, acidic soils; also known as jewelweed or spotted touch-me-not.
