powder-post beetle

noun
wood-boring beetle damaging timber
7.3 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.dər poʊst ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.də pəʊst ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
any of several wood-boring beetles whose larvae feed on and can damage timber, reducing it to a fine powder

Examples

Powder-post beetles damage wood
Furniture makers take precautions against powder-post beetles when storing timber.
He discovered a small pile of dust, a sign that powder-post beetles had attacked the wood.
The old barn was infested with powder-post beetles that weakened its beams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodworm - larval stage of wood-boring beetles, often causing timber damage
