refile

noun
repeat submission
6.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêfa…™l / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêfa…™l / (BrE)
an act of filing something again, especially the resubmission of a legal or official document

Examples

A refile of documents
The court required a refile of the application due to missing information.
After correcting the errors, the company prepared a refile of the annual report.
A refile was necessary when the original documents were lost in transit.

Synonyms

  • resubmission
    4.1 0.07/m
    resubmission is sending a corrected document again for approval or review, while refile is the formal legal act of filing it once more.
