tankless

adjective
without a storage tank
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋkləs / (AmE), / ˈtæŋkləs / (BrE)
not having a storage tank, especially referring to devices that heat water only when needed

Examples

Tankless water heater
They decided to buy a tankless water heater to save space in their small apartment.
A tankless system provides hot water only when you need it, making it more energy efficient.
Many modern homes are switching to tankless solutions for both convenience and cost savings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: storage - the act of keeping or storing something for future use
