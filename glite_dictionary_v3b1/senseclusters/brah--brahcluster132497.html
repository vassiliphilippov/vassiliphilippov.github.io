brah

interjection
informal greeting
9.9
Pronunciation: / brɑ / (AmE), / brɑː / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to address a friend, attract attention, or express surprise or emphasis

Examples

Brah, listen
Brah, you won't believe what happened at the party last night.
Hey brah, can you help me carry this box to the car?
Brah, that was an amazing goal you just scored!

Related Terms

  • synonym: bro - an informal term for a male friend
  • synonym: dude - an informal way to address a man or friend
