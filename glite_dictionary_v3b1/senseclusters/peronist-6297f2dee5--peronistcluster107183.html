Peronist

adjective
of Peronism
8.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈroʊnɪst / (AmE), / pəˈrəʊnɪst / (BrE)
relating to Peronism or the political ideas and policies of Juan Perón and his followers in Argentina

Examples

Peronist policies
The new administration introduced several Peronist reforms to support workers and the poor.
Many voters were drawn to the Peronist candidate because of promises to increase social spending.
The country's history has been shaped by decades of Peronist influence in politics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-Peronist - opposed to Peronism or its policies
  • synonym: populist - relating to political movements that claim to represent ordinary people
