Pronunciation: / eɪnt heɪ / (AmE), / eɪnt heɪ / (BrE)
(informal) Not insignificant or unimportant; of considerable value or amount.

Examples

ain't hay for scientists
Getting published in a major journal ain't hay for a young scientist.
She got a 10% raise, which ain't hay in this economy.
Winning that much money ain't hay for a weekend's work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: trivial - of little value or importance
