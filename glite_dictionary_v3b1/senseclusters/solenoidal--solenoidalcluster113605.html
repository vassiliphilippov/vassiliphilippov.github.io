Examples

Solenoidal vector field
In fluid dynamics, an incompressible flow is described as solenoidal because its divergence is zero.
The magnetic field around a current-carrying wire is a classic example of a solenoidal vector field.
Engineers often analyze solenoidal fields when studying electromagnetic phenomena.
