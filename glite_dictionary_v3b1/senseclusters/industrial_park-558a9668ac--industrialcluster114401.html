industrial park

noun
area for factories and businesses
3.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəl pɑːrk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl pɑːk / (BrE)
an area of land specially planned for factories and businesses, usually situated outside the city centre.

Examples

An industrial park area
Many factories in the industrial park shared facilities and infrastructure to reduce costs.
The new industrial park has attracted several multinational companies to the region.
Traffic increased near the industrial park after two large warehouses opened last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: business park - an area with offices and commercial businesses, typically with few or no manufacturing plants
