coworking

noun
shared workspace
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
a shared office space or the practice of working in a shared office with people from different companies or professions

Examples

Coworking office
Many freelancers prefer coworking because it offers flexibility and a sense of community.
The city has seen a rise in coworking spaces catering to startups and remote workers.
She found her new job through a contact she met at a coworking event.
Many freelancers prefer coworking because it offers both flexibility and a sense of community.
The city has opened several new coworking spaces to support small businesses and startups.
After months of working from home, she decided to try coworking for a change of environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: private office - an office space used by only one person or company
  • antonym: remote work - working from a location outside a traditional office
  • synonym: shared office - an office space used by multiple people or companies
  • synonym: shared workspace - an office space used by people from different organizations
  • synonym: workspace - a place where people do their work
