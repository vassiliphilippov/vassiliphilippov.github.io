incl.

x
abbreviation for 'including'
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ / (BrE)
abbreviation for 'including', used to show that something is part of a group or total

Examples

Price incl. tax
The total cost, incl. shipping, will be sent to your email.
Lunch is provided, incl. drinks and dessert, at the conference.
The package, incl. all accessories, arrived yesterday.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excluding - not containing as part of the whole
  • synonym: including - containing as part of the whole
