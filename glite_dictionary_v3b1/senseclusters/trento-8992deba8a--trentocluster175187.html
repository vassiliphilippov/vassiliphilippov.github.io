Trento

proper noun
city in northern Italy; Council of Trent site
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛn.toʊ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛn.təʊ / (BrE)
A city in northern Italy on the River Adige, known as the site of the Council of Trent.

Examples

Trento city in Italy
