browning

noun
cooking food until it turns brown and crisp
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of cooking food until it turns brown and often develops a crisp surface, typically over a fire or on a grill

Examples

Proper browning of food
Proper browning adds both flavor and texture to grilled vegetables.
She monitored the browning of the bread to make sure it didn't burn.
The browning of the chicken gave it a delicious, crispy skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toasting - the process of browning food, especially bread, by exposure to heat
