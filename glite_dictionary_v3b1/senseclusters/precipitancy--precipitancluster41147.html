precipitancy

noun
sudden or rash haste
8.7 8.6
Pronunciation: /prɪ.ˈsɪp.ɪ.tən.siː/ /; / /prə.ˈsɪp.ɪ.tən.siː/ (AmE), /prɪ.ˈsɪp.ɪ.tən.siː/ /; / /prə.ˈsɪp.ɪ.tən.siː/ (BrE)
The quality of acting or occurring with sudden haste or without due consideration; rashness.

Examples

She blamed the accident on his precipitancy rather than a genuine error.
The precipitancy of their retreat surprised even their allies.
