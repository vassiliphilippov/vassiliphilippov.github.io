waste product

noun
unwanted byproduct of a process
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪst ˌprɑː.dʌkt / (AmE), / ˈweɪst ˌprɒd.ʌkt / (BrE)
a substance or material that is produced and discarded as useless or unwanted from a process, organism, or system

Examples

Chemical waste products
Chemical factories must safely dispose of their waste products to prevent environmental damage.
During digestion, the body generates several waste products that are eventually excreted.
Recycling reduces the amount of waste product that ends up in landfills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: byproduct - secondary product derived from a manufacturing process or chemical reaction
  • synonym: refuse - waste or discarded material
