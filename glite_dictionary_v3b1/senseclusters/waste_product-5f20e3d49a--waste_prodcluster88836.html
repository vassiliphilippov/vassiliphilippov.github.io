waste product

noun
unwanted byproduct of a process
1.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /weɪst ˈprɑː.dəkt/ /; / /weɪst ˈprɑː.dʌkt/ (AmE), /weɪst ˈprɒ.dəkt/ /; / /weɪst ˈprɒ.dʌkt/ (BrE)
