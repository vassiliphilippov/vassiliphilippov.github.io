Stenopterygius

noun
genus of extinct ichthyosaurs
Pronunciation: / stɛˌnɑːp.təˈrɪdʒ.i.əs /; / stɛˌnɒptəˈrɪdʒiəs / (AmE), / stɛnˌɒp.təˈrɪdʒ.i.əs /; / stɛˌnɒptəˈrɪdʒɪəs / (BrE)
a genus of extinct marine reptiles (ichthyosaurs) from the Jurassic period, known for their streamlined, fish-like bodies
