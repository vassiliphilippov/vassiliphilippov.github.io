hot up

verb
make a machine more powerful
6.2
Pronunciation: / hɑt ʌp / (AmE), / hɒt ʌp / (BrE)
to make a machine, especially an engine, more powerful or efficient, often by changing its parts

Examples

To hot up an engine
He decided to hot up his car so it would go faster on the track.
Some mechanics specialize in hotting up old motorcycles for racing purposes.
The engine had been hotted up to deliver extra horsepower.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soup up - to modify a vehicle or machine to increase its power or performance
