condom

noun
contraceptive sheath worn on penis
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.dəm / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dəm / (BrE)
A thin sheath, usually made of rubber or latex, worn over the penis during sexual intercourse as a contraceptive or to prevent sexually transmitted infections.

Examples

A condom use
A condom can help prevent the transmission of many sexually transmitted infections.
He always uses a condom to ensure safe sex.
She found a box of condoms in the bathroom drawer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prophylactic - contraceptive device to prevent disease or pregnancy; synonym for condom
