parabiotic

adjective
joined organisms
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəbaɪˈɑtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpærəbaɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the anatomical or physiological joining of two organisms, especially for scientific study

Examples

Parabiotic mouse experiment
The scientists created parabiotic pairs of mice to study the effects of shared blood circulation.
A parabiotic experiment can reveal how hormones travel between two connected animals.
Researchers observed changes in metabolism in parabiotic rats joined at the skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: symbiotic - involving interaction between two different organisms living together
