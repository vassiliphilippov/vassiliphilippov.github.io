trauma

noun
serious bodily injury
4.4 8.41/m
Pronunciation: / Ààtr…îm…ô, Ààtra äm…ô / (AmE), / Ààtr…îÀêm…ô, Ààtra äm…ô / (BrE)
A serious physical injury or wound caused by an external force like violence, accident, or fracture.

Examples

Severe trauma
Emergency surgery was required to treat internal trauma caused by the accident.
Medical staff are trained to respond quickly to cases of blunt force trauma.
The victim suffered severe trauma to his legs after the car crash.

Synonyms

  • injury
    1.4 78/m
    injury is any physical harm to the body, while trauma is a serious injury caused by strong external force like accidents or violence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injury - harm or damage to the body
  • synonym: wound - an injury to body tissue, typically one in which the skin is cut or broken
