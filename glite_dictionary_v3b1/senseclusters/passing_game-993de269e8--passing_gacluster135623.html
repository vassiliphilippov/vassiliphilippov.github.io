passing game

noun
football plays involving passes
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋ ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋ ɡeɪm / (BrE)
(American football) a strategy or series of plays that involve advancing the ball by throwing it to teammates.

Examples

A strong passing game
A strong passing game can keep the defense off balance.
The team's passing game was especially effective in the second half.
They focused on improving their passing game during practice this week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: running game - (American football) plays that advance the ball by running rather than passing
