go

verb
stop working; fail (of a device or body part)
6.5 4.5
Pronunciation: /goʊ/ /; / /gəʊ/ (AmE), /gəʊ/ /; / /goʊ/ (BrE)
to stop functioning, to end, to be removed, or to die

Examples

With the renovation, all the outdated fixtures had to go.
