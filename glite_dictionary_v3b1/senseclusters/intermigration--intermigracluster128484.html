intermigration

noun
migration between groups
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərmaɪˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəməˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the movement of people or groups from one region, group, or population to another

Examples

The intermigration of workers
The intermigration of workers between urban and rural areas has increased in recent years.
Cultural diversity in the region grew due to ongoing intermigration among neighboring communities.
Government policies can influence the rate of intermigration across different provinces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emigration - the act of leaving one's own country to settle in another
  • synonym: immigration - the act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country
  • synonym: migration - the movement of people from one place to another
