hank

noun
coil of rope, wool, or yarn
Pronunciation: / hæŋk / (AmE), / hæŋk / (BrE)
a length of rope, wool, or yarn wound into a loose coil or loop for storage or use

Examples

A hank of yarn
A large hank of rope was hanging from the hook in the garage.
She bought a hank of wool to knit a scarf.
The weaver untangled a hank of yarn before starting her work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skein - a loosely coiled length of yarn or thread
