minium

noun
red lead pigment; lead oxide
9.9 9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪ.niː.əm/ /; / /ˈmɪ.nɪ.əm/ (AmE), /ˈmɪ.nɪ.əm/ /; / /ˈmɪ.niː.əm/ (BrE)
