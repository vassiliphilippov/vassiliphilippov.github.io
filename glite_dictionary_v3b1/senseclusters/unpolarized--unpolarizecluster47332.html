unpolarized

adjective
not divided; neutral
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpoʊləˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpəʊləraɪzd / (BrE)
not divided into opposing groups or opinions; neutral or lacking polarization

Examples

Unpolarized debate
The discussion remained unpolarized, with participants listening respectfully to all viewpoints.
In some countries, the media landscape is relatively unpolarized compared to others.
Voters in the region are largely unpolarized, making election outcomes difficult to predict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral - not supporting any side in a conflict or disagreement
  • antonym: polarized - divided into two sharply contrasting groups or opinions
