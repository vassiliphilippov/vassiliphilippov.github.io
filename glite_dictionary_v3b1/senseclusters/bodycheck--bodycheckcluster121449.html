bodycheck

noun
security pat-down
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdiˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
a physical search of a person's body or clothing, especially a pat-down for security or policing purposes

Examples

Airport bodycheck
The security guard performed a bodycheck before allowing anyone into the concert hall.
Passengers must undergo a bodycheck at the airport for safety reasons.
After the alarm sounded, the officer conducted a thorough bodycheck of everyone in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frisk - a quick search of a person's clothing for concealed items
  • synonym: search - an act of looking for something hidden, especially by the police
