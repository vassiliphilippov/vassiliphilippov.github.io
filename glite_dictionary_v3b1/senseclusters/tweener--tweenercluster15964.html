tweener

noun
player between positions
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a basketball player whose size and skills are between those of a guard and a forward, making them suitable for both positions

Examples

Basketball tweener player
The coach valued having a tweener on the team for added flexibility in the lineup.
As a tweener, she could defend smaller guards or match up against taller forwards.
Many modern teams look for a tweener who can adapt to different roles on the court.

Related Terms

  • antonym: specialist - a player who focuses on a single position or skill
  • synonym: swingman - a basketball player who can play both guard and forward positions
