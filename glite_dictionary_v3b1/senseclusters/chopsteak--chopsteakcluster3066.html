chopsteak

noun
patty of ground cooked beef
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑpˌsteɪk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒpˌsteɪk / (BrE)
a patty made of ground cooked beef, often served as a main dish

Examples

A chopsteak patty
For dinner, she ordered a chopsteak with mashed potatoes and gravy.
He prefers chopsteak to traditional cuts because it's easy to cook and full of flavor.
The restaurant's signature dish is a juicy chopsteak served with sautéed onions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hamburger - a patty of ground beef served in a bun or by itself
