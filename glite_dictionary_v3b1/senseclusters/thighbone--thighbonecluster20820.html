thighbone

noun
the main bone of the thigh
1.4 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈθaɪ.boʊn/ (AmE), /ˈθaɪ.bəʊn/ (BrE)
the long, thick bone in the human thigh, extending from the pelvis to the knee; also called the femur

Examples
