crunchiness

noun
quality of being crisp
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkrʌn.tʃiː.nəs/ /; / /ˈkrʌn.tʃɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈkrʌn.tʃiː.nəs/ /; / /ˈkrʌn.tʃɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being firm and making a crisp, crackling sound when bitten or chewed

Examples

Apple crunchiness
The crunchiness of the apple made it especially refreshing on a hot day.
She loves the crunchiness of toasted bread with her morning coffee.
The crunchiness of the cereal was lost after it sat in milk for too long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crispness - the quality of being pleasantly firm and brittle
  • antonym: softness - the quality of being easy to bite or chew without resistance
