Psithyrus

noun
a parasitic genus of large bees
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪθɪrəs /; / ˈsɪθɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪθɪrəs / (BrE)
a genus of large bees related to bumblebees, which do not collect pollen or have a worker caste, and are parasitic on other bees
