3-D

noun
three-dimensional movie or image
1.7 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈθriː.diː/ /; / /θriː.ˈdiː/ (AmE), /ˈθriː.diː/ /; / /θriː.ˈdiː/ (BrE)
A movie or image that has or appears to have three-dimensional form, often created or viewed with special technology to give a sense of depth.

Examples

A 3-D movie
Children love watching animated films in 3-D because the characters seem to jump off the screen.
The new action movie is being released in 3-D for a more immersive experience.
We had to wear special glasses to enjoy the 3-D effects at the cinema.

Related Terms

  • synonym: three-dimensional - having or appearing to have height, width, and depth
