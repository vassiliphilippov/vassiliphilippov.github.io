champa

noun
fragrant floral product
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌmpə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌmpə / (BrE)
a fragrant product, such as incense, soap, or perfume, made from or scented with the flowers of the champa tree

Examples

Champa incense sticks
She bought a bar of champa soap scented with warm floral oils.
The room was filled with the sweet aroma of burning champa incense.
He prefers champa perfume for its unique and exotic fragrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incense - a substance burned for its pleasant smell
  • synonym: perfume - a liquid with a pleasant smell worn on the body
