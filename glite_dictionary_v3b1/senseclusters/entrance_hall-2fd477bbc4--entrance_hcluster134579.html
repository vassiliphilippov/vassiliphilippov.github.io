entrance hall

noun
large entry room near the main door
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrəns hɔl / (AmE), / ˈɛntrəns hɔːl / (BrE)
a large room or area just inside the main entrance of a building, used for entry and sometimes for greeting or waiting

Examples

The entrance hall area
Guests left their coats in the entrance hall before heading into the main room.
She paused in the entrance hall to check her reflection in the mirror.
The grand entrance hall was adorned with marble columns and a sweeping staircase.

  • synonym: lobby - a large entrance or reception area in a building
  • synonym: vestibule - a small entry hall or antechamber to a building
