hallway

noun
interior passage or corridor
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑlˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːlˌweɪ / (BrE)
A long narrow passage inside a building, onto which rooms open.

Examples

Long hallway inside
Paintings lined both sides of the hallway on the upper floor.
She waited for her friend in the hallway outside the classroom.
The children ran down the hallway laughing and shouting.
