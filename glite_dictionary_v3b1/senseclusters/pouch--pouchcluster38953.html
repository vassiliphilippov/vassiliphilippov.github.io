pouch

noun
small soft bag or container
Pronunciation: / paʊtʃ / (AmE), / paʊtʃ / (BrE)
a small or medium-sized bag or container used for holding or carrying things, typically made of soft material and often closed with a drawstring, flap, or zipper.

Examples

A pouch of coins
He handed me a pouch containing dried herbs and spices.
She kept her coins in a small leather pouch in her purse.
The kangaroo carries its baby in its abdominal pouch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bag - a flexible container used for carrying things
  • synonym: sack - a large bag made of rough material used for storage or carrying
