Examples

Synonyms

  • daily
    1.0 94/m
    daily is used for things that happen every day in ordinary speech, while diurnal sounds more formal and scientific.
  • circadian
    7.0 3.54/m
    circadian refers to biological rhythms tied to roughly 24 hours and body clocks, while diurnal means happening each day in general.
  • diel
    8.7 0.00/m
    diel relates to a full 24-hour day-night cycle in scientific contexts, while diurnal is used more widely for daily events.
