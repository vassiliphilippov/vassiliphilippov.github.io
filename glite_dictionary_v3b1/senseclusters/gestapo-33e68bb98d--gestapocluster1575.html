Gestapo

noun
secret Nazi police force
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈstɑpoʊ / (AmE), / ɡɛˈstɑːpəʊ / (BrE)
the secret state police of Nazi Germany, known for using terror and violence to suppress opposition

Examples

The Gestapo secret police
During World War II, the mere mention of the Gestapo inspired fear among the population.
He was interrogated by the Gestapo after being suspected of helping fugitives.
The Gestapo conducted raids throughout the city in search of political dissidents.
