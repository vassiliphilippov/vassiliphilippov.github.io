jack

noun
target ball in lawn bowling
Pronunciation: / dʒæk / (AmE), / dʒak / (BrE)
a small white ball used as a target in lawn bowling and similar games, at which the players aim their balls

Examples

In bocce, the jack is thrown first to set the target for the round.
The players aimed their balls as close as possible to the jack.
