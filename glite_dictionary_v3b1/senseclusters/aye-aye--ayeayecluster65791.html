aye-aye

noun
a nocturnal Madagascan lemur
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ ˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌaɪ / (BrE)
A nocturnal lemur native to Madagascar, distinguished by its long bony fingers and rodent-like incisor teeth.

Examples

Many people mistakenly believe the aye-aye brings bad luck because of its unusual appearance.

The aye-aye uses its elongated middle finger to extract insects from tree bark.
