whorish

adjective
lacking moral principles
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːrɪʃ / (BrE)
showing a willingness to abandon principles or dignity for money, approval, or personal gain, often used as an insult.

Examples

Whorish ambition
The politician's whorish behavior shocked even his closest supporters.
Critics described the advertising campaign as whorish and lacking in integrity.
She refused to adopt a whorish attitude just to gain popularity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrupt - willing to act dishonestly for personal gain
  • antonym: honorable - showing high moral standards
  • synonym: unprincipled - not acting according to moral principles
