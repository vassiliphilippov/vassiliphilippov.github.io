whorish

adjective
lacking moral principles
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɔːr.ɪʃ/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈhɔː.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
showing a willingness to abandon principles or dignity for money, approval, or personal gain, often used as an insult.
