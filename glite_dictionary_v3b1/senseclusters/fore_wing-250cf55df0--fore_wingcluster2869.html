fore wing

noun
front wing of a four-winged insect
Pronunciation: / fɔr wɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔː wɪŋ / (BrE)
either of the front pair of wings on an insect that has four wings

Examples

The fore wings of insects
During flight, a butterfly uses both its fore wings and hind wings for balance.
Many beetles have hardened fore wings that protect their delicate hind wings underneath.
The dragonfly's fore wings are usually narrower than its hind wings.
