frequent flyer

noun
person who flies often by plane
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkwənt ˈflaɪər / (AmE), / ˈfriːkwənt ˈflaɪə / (BrE)
a person who travels by air very often, especially one who is a member of an airline's loyalty program

Examples

A frequent flyer member
As a frequent flyer, she accumulated enough miles for free tickets.
He became a frequent flyer after his job required weekly business trips.
The airline offers special perks to frequent flyers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occasional flyer - a person who flies by plane only rarely
  • synonym: traveler - person who travels, especially to distant places
