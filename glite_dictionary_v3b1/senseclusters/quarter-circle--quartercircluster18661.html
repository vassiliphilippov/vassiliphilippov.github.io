quarter-circle

noun
one fourth of a circle's circumference
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtɚ ˌsɝkəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtə ˌsɜːkl / (BrE)
A quarter of the circumference of a circle; an arc or sector forming one fourth of a circle.

Examples

A quarter-circle arc
He used a protractor to measure the quarter-circle on the diagram.
The garden path curved gracefully along the quarter-circle at the corner of the yard.
The staircase was designed to follow a quarter-circle, creating a smooth, sweeping effect.
