opioid

adjective
relating to opiates
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpiˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈəʊpiˌɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to substances that act on opioid receptors or to the body's opioid system

Examples

Opioid medication
The doctor prescribed an opioid medication to manage the patient's severe pain.
Researchers are studying the opioid system to develop new treatments for addiction.
Some painkillers have opioid properties and can be highly addictive if misused.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narcotic - relating to drugs that dull the senses and relieve pain
  • antonym: non-opioid - not related to opioid substances or effects
