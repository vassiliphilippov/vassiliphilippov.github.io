implementer

noun
one who implements
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmpləˌmɛntər / (AmE), / ˈɪmplɪˌmɛntə / (BrE)
a program, module, device, or person that carries out a particular function by implementing an algorithm or specification

Examples

Software implementer
The software implementer ensured that the new protocol was correctly integrated into the system.
Each implementer must follow the official specification to guarantee compatibility.
A skilled implementer can turn a complex algorithm into an efficient working program.

Related Terms

  • antonym: designer - someone who creates the plan or specification for something
  • synonym: executor - someone or something that carries out a task or plan
