nonrecognition

noun
lack of acknowledgment
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛkəɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
the failure to acknowledge, reward, or give credit to someone's efforts, achievements, or contributions

Examples

Nonrecognition of effort
The team's hard work was met with complete nonrecognition from upper management.
She felt discouraged by the nonrecognition of her contributions to the project.
Long-term nonrecognition can lead to low morale among employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disregard - lack of attention or respect
  • antonym: recognition - acknowledgment or appreciation of someone's efforts or achievements
