neurologically

adverb
in terms of the nervous system
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊrəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the nervous system or the brain

Examples

To be neurologically stable
The patient was found to be neurologically stable after the surgery.
Some disorders can affect people neurologically even if their physical health seems normal.
The study examined how children develop neurologically during their first five years.
