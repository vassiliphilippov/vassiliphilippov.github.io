nautically

adverb
in a maritime manner
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔtɪkli / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to ships, navigation, or sailors

Examples

A nautically important coast
The coastline is nautically important because of its many safe harbors.
He decorated his office nautically, with model ships and maps of the sea.
The event was nautically themed, featuring sailors and maritime flags.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maritime - relating to the sea, ships, or navigation
