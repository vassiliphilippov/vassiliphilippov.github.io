phrasing

noun
grouping of musical phrases
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the way in which a sequence of musical notes is grouped and articulated to form musical phrases in a melodic line

Examples

Musical phrasing
Good phrasing can transform a simple tune into a moving musical performance.
The conductor praised her excellent phrasing during the slow movement.
The violinist's phrasing brought out the emotional depth of the melody.

Related Terms

  • synonym: articulation - the clarity in the production of musical notes and phrases
