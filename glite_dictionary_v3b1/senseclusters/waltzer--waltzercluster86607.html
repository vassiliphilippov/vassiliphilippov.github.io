waltzer

noun
spinning fairground ride
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔltsər / (AmE), / ˈwɒltsə / (BrE)
a fairground ride with spinning seats that move in circles, often accompanied by music

Examples

A spinning waltzer ride
The children screamed with excitement as the waltzer spun them around at the fair.
We waited in line for the waltzer because it was the most popular ride at the carnival.
After riding the waltzer, she felt dizzy but wanted to go again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carousel - a rotating amusement ride with seats for riders
  • antonym: roller coaster - an amusement ride with fast, steep tracks
