bollocks

noun
vulgar nonsense
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑləks / (AmE), / ˈbɒləks / (BrE)
vulgar slang for nonsense, lies, or something considered worthless or untrue

Examples

Utter bollocks
He said the story was complete bollocks and told us not to believe it.
Don't listen to that; it's just a load of bollocks made up to scare people.
She called his excuse bollocks and demanded the real reason for his absence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are foolish or untrue
  • antonym: truth - something that is true or factual
