Mysidacea

noun
order of shrimp-like crustaceans
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪsɪˈdeɪsiə / (AmE), / ˌmɪsɪˈdeɪsɪə / (BrE)
An order of small, shrimp-like crustaceans commonly known as opossum shrimp, found in marine and freshwater environments.
