capacitively

adverb
involving capacitance
9.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.tɪv.liː/ /; / /kə.ˈpæs.tɪv.liː/ (AmE), /kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.tɪv.liː/ /; / /kə.ˈpæs.tɪv.liː/ (BrE)
in a manner that involves or relates to electrical capacitance or capacitive effects

Examples

To couple capacitively
The two circuits are capacitively coupled to allow signal transfer without direct connection.
Sensors can detect touch capacitively by measuring changes in electrical charge.
The filter works capacitively to block unwanted frequencies in the signal.
