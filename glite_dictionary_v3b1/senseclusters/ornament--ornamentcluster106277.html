ornament

verb
decorate or embellish
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrnəˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈɔːnəˌmɛnt /; / ˈɔːnəmənt / (BrE)
to decorate or make something more attractive by adding decorative details or objects

Examples

To ornament a room
Artists often ornament their manuscripts with colorful illustrations.

She carefully ornamented the cake with fresh flowers before serving it.
The walls were ornamented with beautiful tapestries and paintings.
Thousands of tiny lights ornamented the city streets during the holiday season.
