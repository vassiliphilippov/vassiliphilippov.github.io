Great Smoky Mountains National Park

proper noun
national park in Tennessee and North Carolina
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt ˈsmoʊki ˈmaʊntənz ˈnæʃənl pɑrk / (AmE), / ɡreɪt ˈsməʊki ˈmaʊntənz ˈnæʃnəl pɑːk / (BrE)
