Stambul

proper noun
historical name for Istanbul, Turkey
Pronunciation: / stɑmˈbul / (AmE), / stamˈbuːl / (BrE)
A historical or alternative name for Istanbul, the largest city in Turkey, formerly known as Byzantium and later Constantinople.

Examples

The city Stambul
He became fascinated by the history of Stambul during his studies on the Byzantine Empire.
In nineteenth-century travel literature, the city is often referred to as Stambul.
Many maps from the Ottoman period label the region as Stambul rather than Istanbul.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Istanbul - the modern name for the city formerly known as Constantinople and Stambul
