socialize

verb
bring under socialist or collective control
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪz /; / ˈsoʊʃəlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something conform to socialist ideas or bring under collective or governmental control

Examples

To socialize medicine
Healthcare advocates argue that it is time to fully socialize medicine in the country.
Some oppose efforts to socialize higher education, fearing a loss of quality.
The new government plans to socialize key industries to ensure universal access.
Many progressives advocate to socialise health care for better public access.
The government decided to socialise the banks during the economic crisis.
There was fierce debate over whether to socialise the energy sector.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalize - transfer from private to state ownership or control
  • antonym: privatize - transfer from public or state control to private ownership
