shockproof

adjective
emotionally resistant
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑkˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkˌpruːf / (BrE)
not easily surprised, upset, or affected by shocking or disturbing events

Examples

Shockproof attitude
After years in emergency medicine, she became emotionally shockproof and stayed calm in crises.
Journalists covering disasters often need to be shockproof to handle difficult situations.
His shockproof attitude helped him remain composed during the unexpected announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sensitive - easily affected by emotions or events
  • synonym: unflappable - calm and not easily upset or disturbed
