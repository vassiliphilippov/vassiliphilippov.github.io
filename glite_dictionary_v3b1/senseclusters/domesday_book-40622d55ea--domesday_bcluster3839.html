Domesday Book

proper noun
record of an English land and property survey, 1086
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊmz.deɪ ˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈdəʊmz.deɪ ˌbʊk /; / ˈdʊmz.deɪ ˌbʊk / (BrE)
the official record of land and property in England, compiled in 1086 by order of William the Conqueror
