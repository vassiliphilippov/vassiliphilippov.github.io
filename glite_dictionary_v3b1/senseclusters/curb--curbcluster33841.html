curb

noun
raised edge by sidewalk and street
2.1 8.22/m
Pronunciation: / k…úrb / (AmE), / k…úÀêb / (BrE)
the raised edge between a sidewalk and a roadway, often made of stone or concrete, forming part of a gutter

Examples

A street curb
She tripped and fell off the curb while crossing the road.
The car stopped just before hitting the curb at the end of the street.
Workers were repairing the curb outside our house after the storm.
