resist

verb
to avoid temptation
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɪst / (AmE), / rɪˈzɪst / (BrE)
to stop yourself from doing something that you want to do or feel tempted to do

Examples

To resist temptation
He could not resist the urge to check his phone during the meeting.
It is hard to resist fresh bread when you are hungry.
They tried to resist laughing at the funny story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: succumb - to fail to resist pressure or temptation
  • synonym: withstand - to not be affected by something; to endure
