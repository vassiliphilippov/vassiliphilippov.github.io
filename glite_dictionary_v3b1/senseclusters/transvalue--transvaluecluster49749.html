transvalue

noun
reinterpretation of value
Pronunciation: / trænsˈvæljuː / (AmE), / tranzˈvaljuː / (BrE)
the act or process of reinterpreting or assigning a new value or meaning to something, or the new value or meaning itself

Examples

A transvalue of beliefs
The philosopher argued for a transvalue of traditional moral concepts in modern society.
Art movements often lead to the transvalue of what is considered beautiful or meaningful.
The transvalue of money in times of crisis can change how people view wealth and security.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinterpretation - the act of interpreting something in a new or different way
  • synonym: revaluation - the process of assessing or assigning a new value to something
