pluggy

adjective
awkward or stubby
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈplʌɡi / (BrE)
looking or feeling like a plug, often appearing awkward, stubby, or unnatural

Examples

Pluggy hairline
After the hair transplant, his hairline looked a bit pluggy and uneven.
The new bushes in the garden appeared pluggy compared to the older, fuller plants.
Her attempt at trimming the bangs left them looking rather pluggy and uneven.

Related Terms

  • antonym: natural - appearing normal or not artificial
  • synonym: stubby - short and thick or blunt in appearance
