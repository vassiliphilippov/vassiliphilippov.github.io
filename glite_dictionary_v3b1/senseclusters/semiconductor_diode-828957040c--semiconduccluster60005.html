semiconductor diode

noun
electronic component with p-n junction
Pronunciation: / sɛˌmɪkənˈdʌktɚ ˈdaɪoʊd / (AmE), / sɛmɪkənˈdʌktə ˈdaɪəʊd / (BrE)
An electronic device made from a semiconductor material that allows current to flow in one direction only, typically consisting of a p-n junction.

Examples

A semiconductor diode device
A semiconductor diode is often used in power supplies to convert alternating current to direct current.
Light-emitting diodes, or LEDs, are a common type of semiconductor diode used for illumination.
The circuit failed because the semiconductor diode was installed backward.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diode - an electronic device that allows current to flow more easily in one direction
