liquified

adjective
converted to liquid by heating
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪd /; / ˈlɪkwəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪfaɪd /; / ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance) changed into liquid form, usually by heating or pressure
