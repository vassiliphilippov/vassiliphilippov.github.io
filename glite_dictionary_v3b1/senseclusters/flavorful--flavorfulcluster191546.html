flavorful

adjective
tasting strong or pleasant
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvɚfəl / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvəf(ə)l / (BrE)
having a strong, pleasant, or distinctive taste or flavor.

Examples

A flavorful meal
She loves preparing flavorful meals that surprise her family.
The soup was especially flavorful thanks to the fresh herbs and spices.
This cheese is more flavorful than the mild version we tried last week.
Roasting the vegetables made them much more flavourful than boiling.
She prefers flavourful cheeses to those with a milder taste.
The soup was incredibly flavourful, with layers of spices and fresh herbs.
