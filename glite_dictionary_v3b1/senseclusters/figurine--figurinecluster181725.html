figurine

noun
small carved or molded model figure
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪɡjəˈrin / (AmE), / ˌfɪɡjʊˈriːn / (BrE)
a small model of a human, animal, or mythical figure, typically made of ceramic, metal, or plastic

Examples

A porcelain figurine
He gave her a porcelain figurine as a souvenir from his trip.
She placed a delicate figurine of a dancer on the shelf.
The museum has a collection of ancient bronze figurines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statuette - a small statue or carved figure
