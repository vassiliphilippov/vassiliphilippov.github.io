serial

adjective
relating to serial technique in music
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriəl / (BrE)
relating to or composed using the serial technique, especially in music, where a fixed sequence of elements is used as the basis for a composition

Examples

Serial music
He studied the principles of serial composition as part of his music degree.
Many 20th-century pieces feature serial structures that depart from traditional harmony.
The composer is known for her serial works, which use a strict sequence of tones.
