Cyanea

proper noun
jellyfish genus
Pronunciation: / saɪˈæniə / (AmE), / saɪˈiːniə / (BrE)
a genus of large jellyfish known for their long, stinging tentacles, including the lion's mane jellyfish

Examples

Cyanea jellyfish species
The lion's mane jellyfish, a member of the genus Cyanea, is one of the largest jellyfish in the world.
Scientists studied several species within Cyanea to understand their unique stinging cells.
Divers are often cautious around Cyanea because of their long, powerful tentacles.
