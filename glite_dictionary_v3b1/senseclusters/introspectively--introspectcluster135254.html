introspectively

adverb
inwardly reflective
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈspɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves examining or reflecting on one's own thoughts or feelings

Examples

He thinks introspectively
He answered the question introspectively, pausing to consider his true feelings.
During the retreat, participants were encouraged to think introspectively about their personal goals.
She often writes introspectively in her journal to better understand herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outwardly - in a way that focuses on external things rather than oneself
  • synonym: reflectively - in a thoughtful or contemplative way
