stonecutter

noun
tool or machine for cutting stone
Pronunciation: / c8sto8ancck8ct59r / (AmE), / c8st598ancck8ct59 / (BrE)
a machine or handheld tool used for cutting, shaping, or slicing stone in masonry or sculpture work.

Examples

Electric stonecutter tool
The workshop installed a powerful stonecutter to shape granite blocks for the facade.
He used the stonecutter to make precise grooves in the marble for the fountain.
Modern stonecutters can slice slabs far faster than traditional hand tools.
