strawstack

noun
pile of straw
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔˌstæk / (AmE), / ˈstrɔːˌstak / (BrE)
a large pile or heap of straw, typically found on a farm for storage or animal bedding

Examples

A large strawstack
After the harvest, the workers built a massive strawstack near the edge of the field.
Children loved to play hide-and-seek around the old strawstack every summer.
The cows sheltered beside the strawstack during the cold winter nights.
