pink paper daisy

noun
Australian daisy with bright pink papery flowers
Pronunciation: / pɪŋk ˈpeɪpɚ ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / pɪŋk ˈpeɪpə ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a flowering plant of southwestern Australia, noted for its bright pink, papery, daisy-like flowers that are often grown for drying
