beneficiary

adjective
relating to a benefice; having rights from a benefice
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃiˌɛri / (AmE), / ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃiəri / (BrE)
possessing, or relating to, a benefice; having rights or privileges arising from the holding of a benefice, especially in ecclesiastical or historical contexts.

Examples

Beneficiary priest
