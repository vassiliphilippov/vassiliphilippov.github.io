highlight reel

noun
compilation of best moments
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt riːl / (AmE), / ˈhaɪˌlaɪt riːl / (BrE)
a short video or recording that shows the most exciting or important moments of an event or performance

Examples

A highlight reel video
After the ceremony, they posted a highlight reel of the wedding on social media.
She made a highlight reel of her dance performances for her audition tape.
The team's highlight reel featured all the most spectacular goals of the season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blooper reel - a compilation of funny or mistaken moments from filming
  • synonym: montage - a sequence of short shots edited together
