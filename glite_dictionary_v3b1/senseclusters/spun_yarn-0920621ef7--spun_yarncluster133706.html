spun yarn

noun
lightweight rope of loosely twisted yarns (nautical)
4.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /spʌn jɑːrn/ (AmE), /spʌn jɑːn/ (BrE)
(Nautical) A lightweight rope made by loosely twisting several rope yarns together, used especially for small lashing or seizings.

Examples

A spun yarn rope
The sailor used spun yarn to secure the rigging on the ship’s deck.
They carried a coil of spun yarn as part of their standard maintenance kit on board.
Traditional ship repairs often relied on spun yarn for making seizings and serving lines.
