apple pud

noun
informal term for apple dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈæp.əl pʊd / (AmE), / ˈæp.əl pʊd / (BrE)
an informal term for a dessert made with apples, such as apple pudding or similar sweet dishes

Examples

Homemade apple pud
After Sunday dinner, we enjoyed some homemade apple pud with custard.
She always asks for apple pud when she visits her grandmother's house.
There's nothing like a warm apple pud on a cold evening.
