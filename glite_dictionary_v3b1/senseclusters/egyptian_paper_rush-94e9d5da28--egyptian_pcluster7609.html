Egyptian paper rush

noun
tall Nile sedge used for papyrus
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈdʒɪp.ʃən ˈpeɪ.pər rʌʃ/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈdʒɪp.ʃən ˈpeɪ.pə rʌʃ/ (BrE)
