uninterestedly

adverb
without interest
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɪntrəstɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɪntrəstɪdli / (BrE)

Examples

Look uninterestedly

The students listened uninterestedly as the teacher repeated the instructions for the third time.
She scrolled uninterestedly through her messages, hoping for something more exciting to appear.
