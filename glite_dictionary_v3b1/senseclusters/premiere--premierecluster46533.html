premiere

noun
first public performance or showing
Pronunciation: / prəˈmɪr / (AmE), / prɪˈmɪə / (BrE)
the first public performance or showing of a play, film, or piece of music

Examples

Movie premiere
She wore a stunning dress to the Broadway premiere of the musical.
The film had its premiere at the Cannes Film Festival.
Tickets for the premiere sold out within hours of being released.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opening night - the first performance of a play
