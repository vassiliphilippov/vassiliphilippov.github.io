shapeshifter

noun
creature that changes form
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftər / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpˌʃɪftə / (BrE)
a being or creature that can change its physical form or appearance at will

Examples

A shapeshifter turns into a wolf
The legend describes a shapeshifter who could become any animal it wished.
In many fantasy novels, a shapeshifter uses its powers to hide from enemies.
The movie's villain was revealed to be a shapeshifter in disguise.
