stuffed grape leaves

noun
grape leaves stuffed with seasoned rice and/or meat
5.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /stʌft greɪp liːvz/ (AmE), /stʌft greɪp liːvz/ (BrE)
A dish of grape leaves stuffed with a mixture of seasoned rice, nuts, currants, or minced meat, often simmered or braised in stock.

Examples

A plate of stuffed grape leaves
At the party, we served stuffed grape leaves as an appetizer alongside hummus and pita bread.
He ordered a plate of warm stuffed grape leaves at the Mediterranean restaurant.
My grandmother taught me how to make stuffed grape leaves with her special blend of spices and rice.
