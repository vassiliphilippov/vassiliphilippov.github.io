leathery

adjective
tough and flexible like leather
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðəri / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəri / (BrE)
having a tough but flexible texture, similar to leather; resembling or made to resemble leather.

Examples

Leathery skin texture
His skin became leathery after years of working in the sun.
She wore a leathery jacket that looked both sturdy and stylish.
The leathery leaves of the plant help it retain water in harsh climates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leatherlike - having a texture or appearance similar to leather
  • antonym: soft - having a smooth or yielding texture, opposite of tough or leathery
