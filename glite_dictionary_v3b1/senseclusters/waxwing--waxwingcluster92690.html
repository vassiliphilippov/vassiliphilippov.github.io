waxwing

noun
a type of songbird with waxy wing tips
9.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwæks.wɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwæks.wɪŋ/ (BrE)
A songbird of the Northern Hemisphere with brown velvety plumage, a crested head, and red wax-like tips on its wings.

Examples

A waxwing bird
A flock of waxwings gathered in the trees to feed on the berries.
The park is home to both cedar waxwings and Bohemian waxwings in the winter months.
We spotted a beautiful waxwing with its distinct red-tipped wings during our hike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bohemian waxwing - a larger species of waxwing found in northern Europe and Asia
  • synonym: cedar waxwing - a species of waxwing with yellow-tipped tail found in North America
