bergschrund

noun
glacial crevasse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛrɡˌʃrʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbɛːɡˌʃrʊnd / (BrE)
a deep crevasse that forms at the upper end of a glacier, close to the mountain wall

Examples

A bergschrund crevasse
The climbers had to cross a wide bergschrund before reaching the steep ice slope.
A hidden bergschrund can pose a serious danger to mountaineers on high-altitude glaciers.
They set up camp just below the bergschrund to prepare for the next day's ascent.
