unchinked

adjective
not sealed with chinking
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪŋkt / (BrE)
not filled or sealed with chinking, especially referring to gaps between logs in a wall

Examples

An unchinked log wall
The cold wind blew through the unchinked spaces between the logs.
They decided to repair the unchinked cabin walls before winter arrived.
An unchinked log house can be very drafty and uncomfortable in cold weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chinked - filled or sealed with chinking between logs
  • synonym: unsealed - not closed or made tight against passage of air or water
