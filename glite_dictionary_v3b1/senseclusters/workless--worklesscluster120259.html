workless

adjective
unemployed; jobless
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrkləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkləs / (BrE)
without a job or employment; unemployed

Examples

Workless people
After the factory closed, many residents became workless and struggled to find new jobs.
The government introduced new programs to support workless families in the community.
He felt anxious about being workless for the first time in his adult life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employed - having a paid job
  • synonym: jobless - without a job; unemployed
  • synonym: unemployed - not having a job or paid work
