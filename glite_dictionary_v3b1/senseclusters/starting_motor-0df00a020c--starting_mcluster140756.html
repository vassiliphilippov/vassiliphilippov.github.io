starting motor

noun
electric motor for starting an engine
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrtɪŋ ˌmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ ˌməʊtə / (BrE)
an electric motor used to start an internal combustion engine by turning the engine's crankshaft until it begins to operate under its own power

Examples

The starting motor
Mechanics often check the starting motor when diagnosing ignition problems.
Older vehicles are more likely to have issues with the starting motor during cold weather.
When the starting motor failed, the car wouldn't start at all.
