hic

noun
minor problem
Pronunciation: / hɪk / (AmE), / hɪk / (BrE)
an informal term for a small problem, fault, or temporary interruption in a process or event

Examples

A small hic
There was a small hic in the schedule, but the meeting continued as planned.
We encountered a hic with the software update, but it was quickly fixed.
Apart from one hic, the event ran smoothly and everyone enjoyed themselves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glitch - a small fault or problem, especially in a machine or system
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being without problems or interruptions
  • synonym: snag - a small difficulty or problem
