hoer

noun
person using a hoe
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊə / (BrE)
a person who uses a hoe to dig, weed, or cultivate soil, especially in farming or gardening

Examples

Skilled hoer
The experienced hoer worked quickly to clear the weeds from the vegetable rows.
Each hoer was given a section of the field to cultivate by hand.
During the planting season, the farm relied on a skilled hoer to prepare the soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmer - a person who owns or manages a farm
  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
