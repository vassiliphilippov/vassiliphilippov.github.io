rushed

adjective
done quickly or under pressure
Pronunciation: / rʌʃt / (AmE), / rʌʃt / (BrE)
done quickly or under pressure, often without enough care or time

Examples

A rushed decision
Because the meeting was rushed, we missed some important details.
The report was clearly a rushed effort, full of errors and omissions.
We had a rushed breakfast before catching the early train.
