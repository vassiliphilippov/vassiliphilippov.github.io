unkept

adjective
not fulfilled or honored (esp. promises or contracts)
2.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɛpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɛpt / (BrE)
(especially of promises or contracts) not fulfilled or honored; violated or disregarded.

Examples

Unkept promises
His apology did little to make up for the unkept promises from last year.
Many contracts include penalties for unkept terms or conditions.
She was disappointed by his long list of unkept commitments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broken - not kept; violated or disregarded
  • antonym: fulfilled - carried out or honored as promised
