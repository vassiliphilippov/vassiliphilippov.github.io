own worst enemy

noun
someone who sabotages their own success
Pronunciation: / oʊn wɜrst ˈɛnəmi / (AmE), / əʊn wɜːst ˈɛnəmi / (BrE)
someone who causes their own problems or prevents their own success by their actions or attitude

Examples

Be own worst enemy
By doubting his abilities at every turn, he became his own worst enemy.
If you don't start trusting your team, you'll be your own worst enemy at work.
She admits that procrastination often makes her her own worst enemy when studying for exams.
