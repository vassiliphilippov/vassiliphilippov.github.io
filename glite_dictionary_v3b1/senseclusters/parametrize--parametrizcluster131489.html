parametrize

verb
to use parameters
Pronunciation: / pəˈræməˌtraɪz / (AmE), / pəˈræmətraɪz / (BrE)
to express or define something using parameters, especially in mathematics, science, or programming

Examples

To parametrize a function
To make the function reusable, you should parametrize its input values.
The scientist decided to parametrize the model to test different scenarios.
By parametrizing the equation, the team could analyze a wider range of cases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generalize - to make something more general or flexible
  • synonym: parameterize - to express in terms of parameters; alternative spelling
