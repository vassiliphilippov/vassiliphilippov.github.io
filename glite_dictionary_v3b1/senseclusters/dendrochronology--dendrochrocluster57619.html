dendrochronology

noun
tree ring dating
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛndroʊkrəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌdɛndrəʊkrəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific method of dating events and environmental changes by analyzing tree ring patterns

Examples

Dendrochronology tree rings
Scientists used dendrochronology to determine the exact year the ancient ship was built.
Through dendrochronology, researchers can study past climate changes recorded in tree rings.
The age of the wooden beams was confirmed by dendrochronology in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radiocarbon dating - a method of dating organic material by measuring carbon-14
  • synonym: tree-ring dating - the process of determining age by counting tree rings
