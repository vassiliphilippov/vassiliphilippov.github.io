willow aster

noun
a North American species of aster
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪloʊ ˈæstər / (AmE), / ˈwɪləʊ ˈɑːstə / (BrE)
A species of North American aster (Symphyotrichum praealtum) characterized by narrow, willow-like leaves and clusters of pale violet flowers.

Examples

The willow aster flower
In late summer, the willow aster produces delicate purple blooms.
She planted willow aster to attract butterflies to her garden.
The willow aster is a hardy perennial found in prairies and along roadsides.
