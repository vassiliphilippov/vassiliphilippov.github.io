artless

adjective
lacking skill or art; unskilled
6.7 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ërtl…ôs / (AmE), / Àà…ëÀêtl…ôs / (BrE)
(of a work or performance) lacking skill or art; not skillfully made or performed.

Examples

Artless performance
An artless translation of the novel missed many important nuances.
Her artless prose failed to capture the beauty of the original poem.
The critic described the play as artless and poorly directed.

Synonyms

  • unartful
    7.4 0.02/m
    unartful is made or shown without clever skill or style, while artless describes a performance that is simply not skillfully made.
  • talentless
    4.3 0.29/m
    talentless means a person has no natural ability or gift, while artless describes a work that is poorly made or performed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unskilled - not having or showing skill
