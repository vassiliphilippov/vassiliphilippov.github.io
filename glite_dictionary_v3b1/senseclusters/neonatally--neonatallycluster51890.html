neonatally

adverb
after birth period
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈneɪtəli / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈneɪtəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to the period just after birth, especially the first few weeks of a newborn's life

Examples

Detected neonatally
The infection was detected neonatally, allowing doctors to begin treatment right away.
Some conditions are best managed if diagnosed neonatally rather than later in childhood.
The study focused on babies who were exposed to the medication neonatally.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prenatally - in a manner relating to the period before birth
