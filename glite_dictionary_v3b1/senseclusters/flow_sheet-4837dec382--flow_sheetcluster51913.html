flow sheet

noun
diagram of steps in a process
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊ ʃiːt / (AmE), / ˈfləʊ ʃiːt / (BrE)
a diagram showing the sequence of operations or stages in a process, such as in a computer program or an accounting system

Examples

A flow sheet diagram
Before implementing changes, the team reviewed the flow sheet for potential process improvements.
Our accounting department relies on a detailed flow sheet to map out financial transactions.
The engineer created a flow sheet to illustrate the new software process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flowchart - a diagram representing a process or algorithm, typically with symbols for operations and arrows indicating flow
