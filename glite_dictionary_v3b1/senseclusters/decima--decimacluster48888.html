decima

noun
tenth part; tithe
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛsɪmə / (AmE), / ˈdɛsɪmə / (BrE)
one of ten equal parts of something, or a payment equal to one tenth, especially as a tithe

Examples

A decima payment
The villagers paid a decima of their harvest to support the local church each year.
In some traditions, a decima was collected as a religious obligation.
Each member contributed a decima of their income to the community fund.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraction - a part of a whole, especially in mathematics
  • synonym: tithe - a payment of one tenth, often to a church
