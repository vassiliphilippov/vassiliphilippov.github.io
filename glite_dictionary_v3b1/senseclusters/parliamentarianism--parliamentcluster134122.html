parliamentarianism

noun
parliamentary government system
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrləməntɛˈriːənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌpɑːlɪəˌmɛntəˈrɪənɪzəm / (BrE)
a system of government in which elected representatives in a parliament form and control the government

Examples

Parliamentarianism in Europe
Many European countries have adopted parliamentarianism as their main form of government.
Debates about parliamentarianism often compare it to presidential systems.
The stability of parliamentarianism depends on cooperation between political parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parliamentary democracy - a democracy where the government is chosen by parliament
  • antonym: presidentialism - a system where the president is the head of government and state
