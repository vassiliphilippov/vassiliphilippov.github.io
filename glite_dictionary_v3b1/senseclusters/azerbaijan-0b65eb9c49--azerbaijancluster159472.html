Azerbaijan

proper noun
country in the South Caucasus, Eurasia
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːzərbaɪˈdʒɑːn / (AmE), / ˌæzəbaɪˈdʒɑːn / (BrE)
A country in the South Caucasus region of Eurasia, located at the crossroads of Eastern Europe and Western Asia, formerly a constituent republic of the Soviet Union.

Examples

Azerbaijan country
Azerbaijan declared its independence from the Soviet Union in 1991.
Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is known for its modern architecture and historical sites.
The Caspian Sea forms the eastern border of Azerbaijan.
