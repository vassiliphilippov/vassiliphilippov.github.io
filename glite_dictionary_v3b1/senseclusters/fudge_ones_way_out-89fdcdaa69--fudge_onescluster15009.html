fudge one's way out

verb
evade responsibility by being deceitful
8.7 7.2

to avoid responsibility or escape a difficult situation by using deceitful or evasive tactics

Examples

To fudge one's way out of trouble

Whenever there's an issue at work, she tries to fudge her way out instead of taking responsibility.
