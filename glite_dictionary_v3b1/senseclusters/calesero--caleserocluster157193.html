calesero

noun
carriage driver
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑːleˈseɪroʊ / (AmE), / kæleˈseərəʊ / (BrE)
a driver of a horse-drawn carriage, especially in Spanish-speaking countries

Examples

The calesero drives horses
The calesero guided his horses through the busy streets of the old city.
Tourists enjoyed a ride with the friendly calesero who shared stories about the town.
At sunset, the calesero waited by the plaza for his last passengers of the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabman - a driver of a horse-drawn cab
  • synonym: coachman - a person who drives a horse-drawn coach
