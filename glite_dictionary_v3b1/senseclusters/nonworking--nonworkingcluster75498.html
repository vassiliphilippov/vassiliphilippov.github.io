nonworking

adjective
not functioning
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not functioning, operating, or currently in use

Examples

Nonworking machine
The nonworking printer caused delays in the office all morning.
We had to replace the nonworking lights in the hallway before the event.
A nonworking air conditioner made the summer heat unbearable in the classroom.
