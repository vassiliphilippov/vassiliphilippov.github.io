phytic

adjective
of phytic acid
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from phytic acid, a substance found in plant seeds and grains

Examples

Phytic acid content
The phytic content in whole grains can reduce the absorption of certain minerals.
Researchers measured the phytic acid levels in different types of seeds.
Some nutritionists recommend soaking beans to lower their phytic acid content.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phytate - a salt or ester of phytic acid
