seeded

adjective
with seeds removed (of food)
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːdɪd / (BrE)
(of fruit or vegetables) having the seeds removed or extracted.

Examples

Seeded grapes
I prefer seeded grapes because they are easier to eat.
She used seeded cherries to make the pie.
The bakery sells seeded bread as well as whole wheat bread.

  • synonym: deseeded - having had the seeds removed
