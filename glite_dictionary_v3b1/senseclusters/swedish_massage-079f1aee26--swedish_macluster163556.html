Swedish massage

noun
type of therapeutic massage technique
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswidɪʃ məˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / ˈswiːdɪʃ məˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
a popular type of massage therapy that uses long strokes, kneading, and movements of the muscles and joints to promote relaxation and improve circulation

Examples

A Swedish massage session
After a long week, she enjoyed a soothing Swedish massage to unwind.
I booked a Swedish massage at the spa to help relieve tension in my back.
The therapist specializes in Swedish massage, which is known for its gentle and relaxing techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: massage - the act of rubbing and manipulating the muscles and joints
