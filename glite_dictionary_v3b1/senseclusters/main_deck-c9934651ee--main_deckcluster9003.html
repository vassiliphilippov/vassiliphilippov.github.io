main deck

noun
uppermost full-length deck of a ship
4.9
Pronunciation: / meɪn dɛk / (AmE), / meɪn dɛk / (BrE)
the uppermost enclosed deck extending the full length of a large ship

Examples

The main deck of ship
During the storm, waves crashed over the main deck of the ship.
Passengers are not allowed on the main deck after midnight.
The crew assembled on the main deck for the safety drill.
