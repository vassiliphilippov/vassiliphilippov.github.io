Synonyms

  • allocation
    4.3 2/m
    allocation sets aside resources for a specific purpose or budget, while assignation describes distributing things according to a general plan.
  • apportionment
    7.8 0.1/m
    apportionment divides something among people by clear rules or shares, while assignation refers to the act of distributing under a system.
  • allotment
    4.6 0.2/m
    allotment gives set portions often by fixed rules or plans, while assignation means the process of distributing according to a system.
  • assignment
    1.8 2/m
    assignment gives something to a person or place as a specific task or duty, while assignation means general distribution by a plan.
  • distribution
    3.2 3/m
