purging

noun
removal of blame or stigma
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of clearing oneself or someone else from blame, stigma, or a charge

Examples

Purging a reputation
His public apology was a step toward purging his reputation of scandal.
She sought purging through confession and acts of contrition.
The ceremony was seen as a ritual of purging the community from past wrongs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolution - formal release from blame or guilt
  • antonym: condemnation - the act of expressing strong disapproval or pronouncing guilt
