ghostly

adjective
resembling a ghost; spectral
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstli / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstli / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a ghost; having an appearance or quality suggesting a ghost

Examples

A ghostly figure
A ghostly figure drifted silently through the abandoned hallway.
Her voice took on a ghostly whisper as she recounted the legend.
The ghostly pale moonlight bathed the cemetery in an eerie glow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: earthly - relating to the physical world rather than the supernatural
  • synonym: phantasmal - resembling or characteristic of a phantom; ghostlike
  • synonym: spectral - related to or resembling a ghost; ghostlike
