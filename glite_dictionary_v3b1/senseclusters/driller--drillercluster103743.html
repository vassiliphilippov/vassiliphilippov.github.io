driller

noun
oil rig operator
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪl.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪl.ə/ (BrE)
a person who operates and supervises the drilling rig on an oil or gas well site

Examples

Oil driller
The experienced driller made sure the drilling process ran smoothly and safely.
After years as a roughneck, he was promoted to driller on the offshore platform.
A driller must monitor equipment and coordinate with the rest of the crew during operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rig operator - a person responsible for running a drilling rig
  • synonym: roughneck - a worker on a drilling rig, usually performing manual labor
  • synonym: supervisor - a person who oversees workers or operations
