club soda

noun
carbonated water with added minerals
6.2
Pronunciation: / klʌb ˈsoʊdə / (AmE), / klʌb ˈsəʊdə / (BrE)
A type of carbonated water containing added minerals, used as a beverage or mixer.

Examples

Club soda drink
He prefers his whiskey with club soda instead of regular water.
She ordered a gin and club soda at the bar.
You can use club soda to remove some types of stains from clothing.
