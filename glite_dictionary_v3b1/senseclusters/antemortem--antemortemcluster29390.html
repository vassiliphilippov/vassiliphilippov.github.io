antemortem

adjective
before death
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈmɔr.təm / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈmɔː.təm / (BrE)
Occurring or existing before death.

Examples

An antemortem statement
An antemortem statement can be used as testimony in court if the person dies.
The coroner reviewed the patient's antemortem medical records for evidence of heart disease.
The injuries were determined to be antemortem, indicating they happened before death.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postmortem - after death
