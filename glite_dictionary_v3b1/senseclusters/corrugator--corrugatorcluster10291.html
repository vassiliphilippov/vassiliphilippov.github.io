corrugator

noun
machine for ridging
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.ə.ɡeɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈkɑːr.ə.ɡeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒr.ə.ɡeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a machine or device that shapes materials into a series of parallel ridges and grooves, especially for making corrugated cardboard or metal
