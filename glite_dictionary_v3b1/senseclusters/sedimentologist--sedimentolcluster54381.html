sedimentologist

noun
sediment scientist
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛdəməˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌsɛdɪməˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies sediments and sedimentary rocks to understand Earth's history and processes

Examples

A sedimentologist studies rocks
The sedimentologist collected samples from the riverbed to analyze their composition.
A team led by a sedimentologist discovered evidence of an ancient lake in the desert.
During the field trip, students observed how a sedimentologist studies rock layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geologist - a scientist who studies the Earth's physical structure and substances
  • synonym: paleontologist - a scientist who studies fossils and ancient life forms
