entrust

verb
give responsibility or care to someone
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtrʌst / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrʌst / (BrE)
to give someone the responsibility to take care of something or someone

Examples

To entrust a task
She felt confident enough to entrust her children to the new babysitter.
The company will entrust you with confidential information after your training.
We decided to entrust the keys to our neighbor while we were on holiday.
Hospitals often entrust important medical equipment to senior staff only.
She decided to entrust her car keys to her best friend while she was on vacation.
The company entrusted the new manager with leading the expansion project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delegate - to give a task or responsibility to another person
  • synonym: trust - to place confidence in someone or something
