sociobiologist

noun
biologist studying biological basis of social behavior
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˌoʊbaɪˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies how biological factors influence social behavior in animals and humans

Examples

A sociobiologist studies behavior
As a sociobiologist, she investigates how genes may influence group interactions in primates.
Many sociobiologists debate the extent to which human behavior is shaped by biology.
The renowned sociobiologist published a groundbreaking book on the evolutionary roots of cooperation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethologist - a scientist who studies animal behavior, particularly in natural environments
