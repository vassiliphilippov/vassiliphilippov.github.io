flammable liquid

noun
liquid that ignites easily
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflæməbəl ˈlɪkwɪd / (AmE), / ˈflæməbəl ˈlɪkwɪd / (BrE)
a liquid that catches fire easily and burns quickly

Examples

Flammable liquid burns quickly
Gasoline, paint thinner, and alcohol are all examples of flammable liquids.
Store flammable liquids away from heat sources and open flames.
The laboratory has strict rules regarding the handling of flammable liquids.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: combustible liquid - a liquid that burns but is less easily ignited than a flammable liquid
