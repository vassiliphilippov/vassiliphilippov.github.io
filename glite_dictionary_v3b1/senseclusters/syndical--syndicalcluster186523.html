syndical

adjective
relating to trade unions
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪndɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪndɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to trade unions or to the principles of syndicalism, especially in labor movements

Examples

Syndical rights
The new law was criticized for undermining syndical rights in the workplace.
Many activists supported a syndical model to give workers more control over management.
The party's platform included several syndical policies aimed at empowering labor unions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-union - opposed to trade unions
  • synonym: unionist - supporting or relating to trade unions
