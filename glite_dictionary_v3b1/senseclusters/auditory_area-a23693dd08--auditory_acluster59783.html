auditory area

noun
brain region processing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈɔ.dɪˌtɔr.i ˈɛr.i.ə / (AmE), / ˈɔː.dɪ.tər.i ˈeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
the region of the cerebral cortex that receives and processes auditory information from the medial geniculate body

Examples

The auditory area cortex
Damage to the auditory area can result in hearing difficulties or auditory processing disorder.
Scientists studied how the auditory area responds to different sound frequencies.
The surgeon carefully avoided the auditory area during the brain operation.
