partake

verb
have a share or participate
5.5 1.52/m
Pronunciation: / p…ërÀàte…™k / (AmE), / p…ëÀêÀàte…™k / (BrE)
to have, give, or receive a share of something; to participate in or consume a portion of something

Examples

To partake in profits
All employees are welcome to partake in the company's profit-sharing scheme.
Everyone was invited to partake in the celebration and enjoy the meal.
She politely declined to partake of dessert after dinner.

Synonyms

  • participate
    2.5 99/m
    participate means taking part in an activity with others, while partake can also mean eating or receiving a share.
  • take part
    1.4 13/m
    take part means joining an activity with others, while partake also covers joining and sometimes eating or receiving a share.
  • join in
    1.1 9.68/m
    join in means entering an activity that is already happening, while partake can mean joining but also eating or receiving a share.
  • share
    1.0 118/m
    share means giving or using something together with others, while partake can mean receiving a share or joining in an activity.
