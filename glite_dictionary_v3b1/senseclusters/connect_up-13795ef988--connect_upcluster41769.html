connect up

verb
establish a connection between devices or systems
Pronunciation: / kəˈnɛkt ʌp / (AmE), / kəˈnɛkt ʌp / (BrE)
to establish a link or connection between devices, systems, or components, typically using cables or other means

Examples

To connect up the wires

Did you remember to connect up the computer to the power supply?
The technician will connect up your internet tomorrow afternoon.
