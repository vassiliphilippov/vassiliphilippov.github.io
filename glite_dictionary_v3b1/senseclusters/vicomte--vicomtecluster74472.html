vicomte

noun
French noble title
Pronunciation: / viˈkɔːnt / (AmE), / viˈkɒnt / (BrE)
a French nobleman ranking below a count and above a baron

Examples

The vicomte attended
The vicomte attended the royal ball in Paris with his family.
After his father's death, he became the new vicomte of the region.
Many stories from French history mention a powerful vicomte ruling over vast lands.
