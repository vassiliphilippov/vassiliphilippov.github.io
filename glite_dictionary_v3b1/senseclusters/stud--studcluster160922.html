stud

noun
male horse kept for breeding
Pronunciation: / stʌd / (AmE), / stʌd / (BrE)

Examples

A stud horse
Breeders carefully select which mares mate with the stud each season.

The farm invested in a new stud to improve its racehorse bloodlines.
