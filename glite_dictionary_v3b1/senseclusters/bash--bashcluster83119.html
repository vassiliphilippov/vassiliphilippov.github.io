bash

noun
a forceful blow
Pronunciation: / bæʃ / (AmE), / bæʃ / (BrE)
a forceful or heavy blow

Examples

A bash on the head
He took a bash on the arm during the game, but shook it off quickly.
She gave the door a hefty bash to get it open when it was stuck.
The vase fell with a loud bash onto the floor and shattered.
