Synonyms

  • lessened
    2.1 0.00/m
    lessened means made smaller in amount or importance over time, while diminished often suggests a lasting drop in size or value.
  • reduced
    1.6 0.00/m
    reduced refers to smaller size, amount, or price in facts or sales, while diminished feels more general and formal.
  • decreased
    1.5 0.00/m
    decreased shows a drop in number, size, or degree, while diminished adds sense of less importance or lasting loss.
  • weakened
    2.7 0.00/m
    weakened describes loss of strength or power, while diminished covers smaller size, lower value, or less importance.
  • attenuated
    7.2 0.03/m
    attenuated means reduced in force or clarity, often in a technical field, while diminished is a broader everyday term.
