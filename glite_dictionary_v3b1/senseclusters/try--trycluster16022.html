try

noun
an attempt or effort
1.0 14/m
Pronunciation: / traÉª / (AmE), / traÉª / (BrE)
an attempt or effort to do or accomplish something, especially when earnest and conscientious

Examples

A good try
It was a good try, but the ball missed the goal by a few inches.
Let me have another try at solving the puzzle before you give up.
She made a brave try to finish the marathon despite her injury.

Synonyms

  • attempt
    1.0 99/m
    attempt is a single act of trying that often focuses on the result, while try is a general, earnest effort to succeed.
  • endeavor
    4.1 16/m
    endeavor is a formal, often long effort requiring determination, while try is a common word for a single earnest attempt.
  • effort
    1.0 101/m
    effort is the physical or mental energy put into doing something, while try is the act of attempting that action.
