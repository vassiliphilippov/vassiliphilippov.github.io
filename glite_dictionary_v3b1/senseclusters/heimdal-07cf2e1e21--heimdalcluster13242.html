Heimdal

proper noun
Norse god of dawn and guardian of Asgard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪmˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈheɪmˌdɑːl / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, the god of dawn and light, who serves as the guardian of Asgard.

Examples

Heimdal in Norse myths
According to legend, Heimdal watches over the rainbow bridge Bifrost, ensuring no enemy invades Asgard.
In tales of Norse mythology, Heimdal is revered as the guardian of the gods.
The ancient Norse people believed Heimdal would blow his horn at the onset of Ragnarok.
