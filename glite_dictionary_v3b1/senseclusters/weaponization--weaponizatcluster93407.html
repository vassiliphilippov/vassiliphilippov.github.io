weaponization

noun
making into a weapon
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛpənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌwɛpənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of turning something into a weapon or using it as a tool for harm or conflict

Examples

The weaponization of social media
The weaponization of social media has changed the way misinformation spreads online.
Some critics warn about the weaponization of artificial intelligence in modern warfare.
The weaponization of economic sanctions can have serious global consequences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disarmament - the process of reducing or eliminating weapons
  • synonym: militarization - the process of equipping or preparing for war
