prosocial

adjective
helping others
5.5
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / prəʊˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
describing actions or behaviors intended to help or benefit other people

Examples

Prosocial behavior
Teachers often encourage prosocial behavior among students to create a positive classroom environment.
Helping a stranger in need is considered a prosocial act in many cultures.
Research shows that children who engage in prosocial activities tend to have better relationships with their peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: altruistic - showing selfless concern for the well-being of others
  • antonym: antisocial - avoiding or harming social interaction or society
