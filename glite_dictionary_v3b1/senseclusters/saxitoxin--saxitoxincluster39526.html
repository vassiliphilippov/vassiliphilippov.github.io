saxitoxin

noun
a neurotoxin from red tide organisms
9.9
Pronunciation: /sæk.sɪ.ˈtɑːk.sɪn/ (AmE), /sæk.sɪ.ˈtɒk.sɪn/ (BrE)
a powerful neurotoxin produced by certain dinoflagellates, often accumulating in shellfish and potentially causing food poisoning in humans
