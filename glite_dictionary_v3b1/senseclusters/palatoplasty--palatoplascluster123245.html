palatoplasty

noun
palate surgery
Pronunciation: / ˈpælətəˌplæsti / (AmE), / ˈpælətəˌplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to repair or reconstruct the palate, often to correct a cleft palate

Examples

Palatoplasty surgery
The surgeon performed a palatoplasty to help the child speak and eat more easily.
After her palatoplasty, she noticed a significant improvement in her speech.
A palatoplasty is often recommended for children born with a cleft palate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cleft palate repair - surgery to fix a split or opening in the roof of the mouth
  • synonym: rhinoplasty - surgical reconstruction of the nose
