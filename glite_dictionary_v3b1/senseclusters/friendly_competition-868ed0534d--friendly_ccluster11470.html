friendly competition

noun
good-natured contest or rivalry
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛndli ˌkɑmpəˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈfrɛndli ˌkɒmpəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a contest or rivalry between participants conducted in a spirit of goodwill, without hostility

Examples

A friendly competition
A little friendly competition can make any classroom activity more exciting for students.
Our sales teams engage in friendly competition to motivate each other to perform better.
The neighbors hold a friendly competition each summer to see who can grow the biggest tomato.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rivalry - a situation in which people or groups are competing against each other
