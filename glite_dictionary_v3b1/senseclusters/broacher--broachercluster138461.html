broacher

noun
tool or introducer
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊtʃər / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊtʃə / (BrE)
a tool or person that makes an opening or introduces a subject or topic

Examples

A broacher tool
The technician used a broacher to create a precise hole in the metal plate.
As the broacher of the topic, she guided the discussion with care.
A skilled broacher can introduce sensitive issues without causing discomfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drill - a tool used to make holes in materials
  • synonym: introducer - a person who brings up a topic or subject
