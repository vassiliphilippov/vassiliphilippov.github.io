karma

noun
online reputation score
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.mə/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.mə/ (BrE)
A numerical reputation score or points total assigned to a user or content on online platforms, reflecting community approval or evaluation.

Examples

High karma score
Her karma on the forum rose quickly after she posted helpful troubleshooting steps.
I lost a lot of karma when my comment received many downvotes from other users.
The new user needed to build karma before they could create posts in the private community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reputation - a person's standing or regard within a community, often expressed as a score or level online
  • synonym: score - a numerical measure representing approval, points, or standing on a platform
