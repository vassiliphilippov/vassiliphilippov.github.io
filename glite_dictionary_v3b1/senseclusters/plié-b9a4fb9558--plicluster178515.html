plié

verb
to bend knees in ballet
8.2
Pronunciation: / pliˈeɪ / (AmE), / pliˈeɪ / (BrE)
to bend the knees outward while keeping the back straight, as in a ballet movement

Examples

To plié gracefully
The dancer began her routine with a graceful plié at the barre.
In ballet class, students are taught how to plié correctly to avoid injury.
He practiced his plié every day to improve his flexibility and strength.
