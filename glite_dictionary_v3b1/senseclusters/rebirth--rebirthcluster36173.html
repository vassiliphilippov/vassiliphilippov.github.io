rebirth

noun
renewal of learning and culture
1.0 4.9
Pronunciation: / riˈbɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈriːbɜːθ / (BrE)
a period or process in which learning and culture are revived and flourish after a decline or stagnation

Examples

Rebirth of culture
Her innovative teaching methods contributed greatly to the rebirth of interest in traditional literature.
Historians often refer to the Renaissance as a rebirth of classical learning.
The city experienced a remarkable rebirth of art and culture during the early 20th century.
