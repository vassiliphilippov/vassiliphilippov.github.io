ballonet

noun
airship airbag
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæl.ə.net/ (AmE), /ˈbæl.ə.net/ (BrE)
an internal airbag in an airship that is filled or emptied to control the shape and buoyancy of the airship

Examples

The ballonet in airship
The pilot adjusted the ballonet to maintain the airship's altitude during the flight.
Technicians inspected each ballonet before the airship took off to ensure safety.
If a ballonet fails, the airship may lose its proper shape and stability.
