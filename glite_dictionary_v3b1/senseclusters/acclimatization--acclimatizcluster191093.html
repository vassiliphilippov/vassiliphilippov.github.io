Synonyms

  • acclimation
    6.5 0.2/m
    acclimation is becoming used to a new climate or environment, while acclimatization focuses on physiological adjustments for better functioning.
  • adaptation
    3.7 14/m
    adaptation is a broader change to fit new conditions over time, while acclimatization is faster, often physiological adjustment.
  • adjustment
    3.3 11/m
    adjustment means changing behavior or settings to cope, while acclimatization usually means bodily changes to work better in a new environment.
  • accommodation
    5.3 2/m
    accommodation is adapting ideas or plans to new facts, while acclimatization describes bodily or behavioral changes to new climates.
