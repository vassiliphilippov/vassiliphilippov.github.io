buckleya

noun
genus of parasitic shrubs in eastern U.S.
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkliə / (AmE), / ˈbʌkliə / (BrE)
A genus of parasitic shrubs native to the eastern United States, characterized by opposite leaves, small greenish flowers, and dull green, olivelike fruits.
