zero

verb
adjust to zero reading
Pronunciation: / ˈziːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈzɪərəʊ / (BrE)
to adjust an instrument or device so that it shows a reading of zero, especially before making measurements

Examples

To zero the scale
Please zero the scale before weighing the ingredients.
Remember to zero the balance every time you use it to ensure accuracy.
The technician zeroed the instrument before starting the measurements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calibrate - adjust an instrument precisely, often by comparison with a standard
