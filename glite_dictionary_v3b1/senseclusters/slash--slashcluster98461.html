slash

noun
debris-strewn forest land after logging or fire
7.1
Pronunciation: / slæʃ / (AmE), / slæʃ / (BrE)
an area of forest land covered with debris and branches left from logging, fire, or wind damage

Examples

Forest slash
After the wildfire, the ground was covered in slash and charred tree stumps.
Crews spent days clearing slash left behind by the strong winds.
The logging operation left a wide slash through the center of the forest.
