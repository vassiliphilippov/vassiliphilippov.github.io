lapis

noun
blue semi-precious stone
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpɪs / (AmE), / ˈlæpɪs / (BrE)
a deep blue semi-precious stone, often used in jewelry and decoration

Examples

Lapis stone

Collectors value rare pieces of lapis for their intense color and unique patterns.
