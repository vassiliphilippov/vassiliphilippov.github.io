diavolo

noun
spicy Italian sauce
8.0
Pronunciation: / diˈɑːvəloʊ / (AmE), / diˈævələʊ / (BrE)
a spicy Italian tomato-based sauce, often made with chili and garlic, or a dish prepared with this sauce

Examples

Diavolo sauce pasta
He ordered the shrimp diavolo, which came in a fiery red sauce with plenty of chili.
The chef recommended the chicken diavolo for those who enjoy spicy food.
Pasta diavolo is popular among people who love bold, hot flavors in their meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrabbiata - a spicy Italian pasta sauce made with tomatoes and chili peppers
