contouring

noun
makeup shading technique
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒntjʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
a makeup technique that uses shading and highlighting to define and enhance the shape of facial features

Examples

Makeup contouring technique
She practiced contouring to make her nose appear slimmer for the event.
Many makeup artists recommend contouring to enhance natural bone structure.
With the right products, contouring can create the illusion of higher cheekbones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: highlighting - a makeup technique that uses lighter shades to emphasize certain facial features
