dizzying

adjective
causing confusion or vertigo
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪziɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪziɪŋ / (BrE)
causing you to feel confused or as if you might lose your balance

Examples

Dizzying heights
The dizzying heights of the skyscraper made her feel unsteady.
He tried to follow the dizzying changes in technology but quickly lost track.
The dizzying pace of the conversation left me struggling to keep up.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calming - making you feel relaxed and peaceful
  • synonym: confusing - making it hard to understand or think clearly
  • synonym: overwhelming - very intense or difficult to deal with
