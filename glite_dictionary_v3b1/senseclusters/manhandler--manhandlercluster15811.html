manhandler

noun
manipulative person
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhændlər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhændlə / (BrE)
a person who manipulates or controls others, often using charm, influence, or deceit

Examples

A skilled manhandler
The politician was known as a skilled manhandler who could sway public opinion with ease.
She warned her friend to be careful around that manhandler at work.
Many people trusted the manhandler before realizing his true intentions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest person - someone who is truthful and does not deceive others
  • synonym: manipulator - a person who controls or influences others cleverly or unfairly
  • synonym: schemer - a person who makes secret plans, often to deceive others
