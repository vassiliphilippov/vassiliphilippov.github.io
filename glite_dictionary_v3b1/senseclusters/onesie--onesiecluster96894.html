onesie

noun
one-piece garment
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌnzi / (AmE), / ˈwʌnzi / (BrE)
a one-piece garment that covers the body, usually worn by babies or for comfort by adults

Examples

A baby onesie
The baby looked adorable in a soft onesie decorated with tiny animals.
Many adults wear a onesie at home for extra comfort during winter.
She bought matching onesies for her twins as a cute holiday gift.
The newborn looked adorable in a tiny onesie decorated with animals.
For the pajama party, she wore a cozy adult onesie shaped like a bear.
He bought several cotton onesies to keep his baby comfortable at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pajamas - loose-fitting clothes worn for sleeping
