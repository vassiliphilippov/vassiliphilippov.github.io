photoetch

verb
to engrave with light
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ.e.tʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ.e.tʃ/ (BrE)
to cut or engrave fine lines or patterns on a surface, especially metal, using a photographic process and chemicals.

Examples

Specialists photoetch intricate designs onto jewelry for a unique finish.
Modern manufacturing often uses machines to photoetch precise components for electronics.
