photoetch

verb
to engrave with light
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊˌɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊˌɛtʃ / (BrE)
to cut or engrave fine lines or patterns on a surface, especially metal, using a photographic process and chemicals.

Examples

To photoetch metal plates
The company photoetched the metal plates to create detailed circuit patterns.
Specialists photoetch intricate designs onto jewelry for a unique finish.
Modern manufacturing often uses machines to photoetch precise components for electronics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engrave - to cut or carve a design onto a hard surface
  • synonym: etch - to cut or carve lines into a surface using acid or another method
