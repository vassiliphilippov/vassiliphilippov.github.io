satang

noun
Thai monetary unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈtɑŋ / (AmE), / səˈtæŋ / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Thailand, equal to one hundredth of a baht.

Examples

One satang coin
Thai prices are often listed in both baht and satang for accuracy.
The coin was worth only fifty satang, so it had little value on its own.
You will receive five satang in change after your purchase.
