sour

adjective
smelling of fermentation or staleness
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˈsaʊə / (BrE)
having a smell that suggests fermentation or staleness, often sharp or unpleasant

Examples

Sour basement smell
After a week of rain, the basement developed a sour smell that lingered for days.
His clothes had become sour after being left damp in the washing machine.
The milk in the fridge had a distinctive sour odor that warned me not to drink it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - recently made, not spoiled or stale (opposite)
  • synonym: putrid - decaying and emitting a foul smell
