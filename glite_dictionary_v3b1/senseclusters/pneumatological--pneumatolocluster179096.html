pneumatological

adjective
about the spirit
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːmətəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌnjuːmətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of spiritual beings or the spirit, especially in theology

Examples

Pneumatological study of spirit
The professor gave a lecture on pneumatological concepts in Christian doctrine.
Her research focuses on pneumatological aspects of early church writings.
The debate centered around pneumatological interpretations of the Holy Spirit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the spirit or soul
  • synonym: theological - relating to the study of religion and God
