rugulah

noun
crescent-shaped filled pastry
Pronunciation: / ˈruːɡələ / (AmE), / ˈruːɡələ / (BrE)
A small crescent-shaped pastry made with a cream cheese dough and filled with ingredients such as nuts, raisins, cinnamon, chocolate, or fruit preserves.

Examples

A rugulah pastry
My grandmother makes rugulah every holiday with cinnamon and walnuts.
The platter was arranged with rugulah alongside other traditional pastries.
We bought chocolate-filled rugulah from the bakery for dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pastry - a baked food made from dough, often with a filling
