rugulah

noun
crescent-shaped filled pastry
9.1 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈruː.ɡə.lə/ /; / /ˈruː.ɡə.lɑː/ (AmE), /ˈruː.ɡə.lə/ /; / /ˈruː.ɡə.lɑː/ (BrE)
A small crescent-shaped pastry made with a cream cheese dough and filled with ingredients such as nuts, raisins, cinnamon, chocolate, or fruit preserves.

Examples

A rugulah pastry

We bought chocolate-filled rugulah from the bakery for dessert.
