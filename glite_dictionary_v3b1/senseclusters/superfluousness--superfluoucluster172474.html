superfluousness

noun
unnecessary excess
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɜrfluəsnəs / (AmE), / suːˈpɜːfluəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being unnecessary or more than what is needed

Examples

The superfluousness of decorations
The superfluousness of decorations made the room feel cluttered rather than elegant.
He removed the superfluousness from his essay to make it clearer and more concise.
Critics pointed out the superfluousness of several scenes in the movie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: necessity - the quality of being needed or essential
  • synonym: redundancy - the state of being not needed or superfluous
