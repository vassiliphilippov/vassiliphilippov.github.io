top of the inning

noun
first half of an inning in baseball/softball
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑp əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɒp əv ði ˈɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the first half of an inning in baseball or softball, when the visiting team is at bat

Examples

The top of the inning batting
A home run was hit in the top of the inning, changing the game's momentum.
The pitcher struck out three batters in the top of the inning.
They scored two runs in the top of the inning to take the lead.
