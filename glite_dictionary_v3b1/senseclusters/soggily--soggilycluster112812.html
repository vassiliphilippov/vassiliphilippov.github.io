soggily

adverb
in a dull way
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːɡɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɒɡɪli / (BrE)
in a dull, lifeless, or weak manner; without energy, excitement, or effectiveness

Examples

Perform soggily
The team played soggily after the long break, barely making any progress.
Her speech continued soggily, failing to inspire the audience.
The project moved soggily through its final stages, lacking momentum.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetically - in a lively or vigorous way
  • synonym: listlessly - in a way that shows little energy or enthusiasm
