beating

noun
act of striking repeatedly as punishment
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
an act of striking a person repeatedly as a form of punishment or violence

Examples

A harsh beating
After admitting his mistake, he feared his father would give him a harsh beating.
Reports of beatings in the prison led to a public outcry.
The principal condemned the beating of students as an unacceptable form of discipline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrashing - a severe beating with repeated blows, often as punishment
  • synonym: whipping - the act of beating with a whip as punishment
