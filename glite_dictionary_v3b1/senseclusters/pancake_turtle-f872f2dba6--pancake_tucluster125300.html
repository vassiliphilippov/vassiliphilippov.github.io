pancake turtle

noun
aquatic turtle with flat, flexible shell
Pronunciation: / ˈpænˌkeɪk ˈtɜr.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈpæn.keɪk ˈtɜː.tl̩ / (BrE)
A species of aquatic turtle noted for its flat, flexible shell, which is covered by a leathery skin and capable of inflicting painful bites.

Examples

The pancake turtle species
Researchers observed a pancake turtle basking on a log in the afternoon sun.
The pancake turtle can flatten its body to hide in tight spaces near riverbanks.
Unlike most turtles, the pancake turtle is known for its surprising agility in the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: softshell turtle - an aquatic turtle with a soft, leathery shell
