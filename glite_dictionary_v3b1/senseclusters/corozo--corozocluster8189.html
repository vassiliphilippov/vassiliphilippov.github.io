corozo

noun
a tropical palm or its nut
9.6
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊzoʊ / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊzəʊ / (BrE)
A tropical American palm tree of the genus Attalea, or its small, hard-shelled nut, often used for making buttons or as food.
