Synonyms

  • rebuke
    6.9 2.07/m
    rebuke is to express sharp, strong disapproval, while chide is a softer spoken reproach for wrong behavior.
  • upbraid
    8.8 0.45/m
    upbraid is to scold someone strongly and often publicly, while chide is quieter and less forceful.
  • chastise
    7.6 2.94/m
    chastise is to criticize or punish someone severely, while chide is to speak mildly about their bad behavior.
  • reprimand
    6.1 1.94/m
    reprimand is an official, often formal rebuke by someone in authority, while chide is informal and personal.
  • take to task
    6.3 1.25/m
    take to task means to openly criticize someone for a fault, while chide is usually quieter, mild reproach.
  • call down
    6.8 0.01/m
    call down is to reprimand someone angrily and strongly, while chide is milder and more like a gentle warning.
