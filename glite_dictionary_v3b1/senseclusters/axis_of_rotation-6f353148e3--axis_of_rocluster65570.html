axis of rotation

noun
line an object rotates around
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæksɪs əv roʊˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈæksɪs əv rəʊˈteɪʃən / (BrE)

Examples

The axis of rotation
A spinning top remains balanced as long as its axis of rotation stays vertical.
