preservice

noun
pre-job training
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈsɜːrvɪs / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
training or preparation completed before starting a job or profession, especially in teaching or similar fields

Examples

A preservice program
All new teachers must complete a preservice program before entering the classroom.
Her preservice included both coursework and practical teaching experience.
The university offers a comprehensive preservice for future educators.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-service - training or education received while already employed
  • synonym: preparation - the process of getting ready for something
  • synonym: training - the process of learning the skills needed for a job
