nutcake

noun
informal insult
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈnʌtˌkeɪk / (BrE)
an informal and derogatory term for a person who behaves in a crazy, foolish, or eccentric way

Examples

He is a real nutcake
People thought he was a nutcake after he danced in the rain wearing pajamas.
She called her brother a nutcake for building a robot out of old kitchen appliances.
Don't listen to that nutcake shouting on the street corner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentric - a person with unusual or odd behavior
  • synonym: lunatic - a person who is crazy or wildly foolish
  • antonym: sensible - a person who shows good judgment and reason
