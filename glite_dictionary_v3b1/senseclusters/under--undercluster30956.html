under

adverb
less than a stated quantity or limit
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚ / (AmE), / ˈʌndə / (BrE)
at or to a level, amount, or number that is less than a stated quantity or limit

Examples

Under 10 dollars
Children aged twelve or under get in free.
The temperature remained under freezing throughout the night.
You can apply if your income is under $30,000 a year.
Children aged twelve and under must be accompanied by an adult.
This game is suitable for players under 18 years old.
You can get a discount if you spend under $50 at the store.

Related Terms

  • synonym: below - at a lower level or amount than something
  • antonym: over - at or to a level or quantity higher than a stated number or limit
