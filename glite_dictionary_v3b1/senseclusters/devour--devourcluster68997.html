devour

verb
eat or consume eagerly or completely
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaʊɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaʊə / (BrE)
To eat hungrily or quickly; to consume or destroy something eagerly or completely.

Examples

Flames devour a house
Flames quickly devoured the old wooden barn, leaving only ashes behind.
She devoured the novel in a single sitting, unable to put it down.
The hungry wolves devoured their prey within minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consume - to eat or use up; devour
  • synonym: destroy - to put an end to the existence of; ruin
