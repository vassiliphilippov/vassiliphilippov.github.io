pieplant

noun
rhubarb plant used in cooking
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈpaɪˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant with long, thick, pinkish, sour leafstalks that are usually cooked and sweetened, especially for use in pies; another name for rhubarb
