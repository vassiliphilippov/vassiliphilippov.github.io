bolometric

adjective
relating to use of a bolometer
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊ.ləˈmɛ.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɒl.əˈmɛ.trɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using a bolometer, especially in the measurement of radiant energy.

Examples

Bolometric corrections
Bolometric corrections are essential when comparing stars of different spectral types.
Astronomers used a bolometric method to measure the total energy output of the galaxy.
The bolometric luminosity of the star was calculated using infrared data.
