saucer

noun
small dish under a cup
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsə / (BrE)
A small, shallow dish, typically placed under a cup to catch drips or hold a spoon.

Examples

Cup and saucer
A matching cup and saucer were part of the antique dinner set.
He placed a sugar cube on the saucer next to his espresso.
She set the teacup carefully onto the saucer to avoid spilling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plate - a flat dish for serving or eating food
