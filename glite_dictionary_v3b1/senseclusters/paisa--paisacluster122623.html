paisa

noun
fractional monetary unit in South Asia
7.7 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪsə / (AmE), / ˈpaɪsə / (BrE)
A fractional currency unit used in Bangladesh, India, Nepal, and Pakistan, equal to one hundredth of the main monetary unit.

Examples

One paisa coin
After buying the fruit, she received five paisa in change.
It costs only fifty paisa to make a local phone call in that town.
The new one-rupee coin replaced one hundred paisa coins.
