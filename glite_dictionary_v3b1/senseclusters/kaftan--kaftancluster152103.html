kaftan

noun
long, loose robe with sash
5.8 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkæf.tən/ /; / /ˈkæf.tæn/ (AmE), /ˈkæf.tən/ /; / /ˈkæf.tæn/ (BrE)
a long, loose garment with wide sleeves, traditionally worn by men in the Middle East and North Africa, and now also used as a comfortable dress for women
