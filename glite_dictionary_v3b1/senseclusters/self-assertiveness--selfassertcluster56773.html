self-assertiveness

noun
bold or confident self-assurance
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf əˈsɝːtɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf əˈsɜːtɪvnəs / (BrE)
confidence in expressing one's own opinions or rights in a forceful or bold manner; aggressive self-assurance.

Examples

Self-assertiveness in speech
He developed his self-assertiveness after years of struggling to express his opinions.
Her self-assertiveness during the meeting impressed the entire team.
Some people mistake self-assertiveness for arrogance, but it can be a healthy trait.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidence - belief in oneself and one's abilities
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
