ultranationalistic

adjective
extremely patriotic; excessively nationalistic
5.5 8.1
Pronunciation: /ʌl.trə.næ.ʃə.nə.ˈlɪ.stɪk/ (AmE), /ʌl.trə.næ.ʃə.nə.ˈlɪ.stɪk/ (BrE)
having or showing extreme or fanatical support for one's own nation, often at the expense of foreign interests or international cooperation

Examples

Ultranationalistic movements
Some historians warn about the dangers of ultranationalistic movements in shaping public policy.
The leader's speech took on an ultranationalistic tone, emphasizing national purity and self-sufficiency.
The party's ultranationalistic rhetoric alienated many moderate voters.
