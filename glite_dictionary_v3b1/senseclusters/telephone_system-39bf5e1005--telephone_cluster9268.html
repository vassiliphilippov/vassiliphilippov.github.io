telephone system

noun
network for telephone communication
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛlɪˌfoʊn ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
A system that enables sound communication between people at a distance by means of telephones.

Examples

The telephone system network
After the storm, the entire telephone system in the area was down for days.
Many businesses rely on a modern telephone system for efficient communication.
The company upgraded its telephone system to handle more calls during peak hours.
