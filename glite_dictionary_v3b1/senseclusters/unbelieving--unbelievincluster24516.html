unbelieving

adjective
doubting or disbelieving, esp. in religion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈlivɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈliːvɪŋ / (BrE)
not accepting or doubting the truth of particular beliefs, especially in religion; skeptical or disbelieving.

Examples

Unbelieving look
His unbelieving expression showed he doubted the story was true.
She took an unbelieving attitude toward the existence of miracles.
The unbelieving crowd listened in silence as the preacher spoke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithful - having strong belief, especially religious belief
  • synonym: skeptical - having an attitude of doubt or questioning
