kef

noun
fermented milk drink
9.5
Pronunciation: / kɛf / (AmE), / kɛf / (BrE)
a slightly sour, fermented milk drink made by fermenting milk with bacteria and yeast, similar to yogurt

Examples

A glass of kef
She drank a tall glass of chilled kef with honey and toast for breakfast.
Many people enjoy kef for its refreshing taste and probiotic benefits.
In some cultures, kef is served as a traditional drink during family meals.
