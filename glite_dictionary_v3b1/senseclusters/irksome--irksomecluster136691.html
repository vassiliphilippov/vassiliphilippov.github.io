irksome

adjective
annoying or tedious
7.4 0.39/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ùÀêk.s…ôm / (AmE), / Àà…úÀêk.s…ôm / (BrE)
causing annoyance, irritation, or boredom; tedious or wearisome.

Examples

An irksome task
Filling out endless forms can be an irksome chore.
He found his colleague's questions increasingly irksome during the meeting.
Waiting in the long line proved to be an irksome experience.
