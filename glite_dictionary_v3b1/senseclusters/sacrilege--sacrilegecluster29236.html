sacrilege

noun
violation or misuse of something sacred
6.7 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsæ.krə.lɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈsæ.krɪ.lɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈsæ.krɪ.lɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈsæ.krə.lɪdʒ/ (BrE)
The act of treating a holy place or object with disrespect or of violating something that is considered sacred.

Examples

An act of sacrilege
Some people saw the artist's use of religious symbols as an act of sacrilege.
Stealing from the altar was punished because it was viewed as sacrilege in medieval times.
The vandalism in the ancient temple was considered a terrible sacrilege by the local community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blasphemy - profanity against the sacred, speech or actions that insult religion
  • synonym: desecration - the act of treating a holy thing with disrespect
