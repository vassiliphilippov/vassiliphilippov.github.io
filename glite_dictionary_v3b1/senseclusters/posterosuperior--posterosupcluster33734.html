posterosuperior

adjective
back and upper location
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstɪroʊsuːˈpɪriər / (AmE), / ˌpɒstərəʊsuːˈpɪəriə / (BrE)
located toward the back and upper part of a structure or body part

Examples

The posterosuperior part
The surgeon examined the posterosuperior aspect of the shoulder joint for signs of damage.
An MRI revealed a tear in the posterosuperior portion of the rotator cuff.
Pain in the posterosuperior region of the hip can indicate certain types of injuries.
