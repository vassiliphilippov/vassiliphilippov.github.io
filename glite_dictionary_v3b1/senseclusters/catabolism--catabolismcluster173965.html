catabolism

noun
biological breakdown process with energy release
6.6 6.5
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈtæ.bə.lɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kə.ˈtæ.bə.lɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the process in living organisms in which complex substances are broken down into simpler ones, releasing energy

Examples

Catabolism process
A healthy diet balances both catabolism and anabolism for optimal body function.
During intense exercise, catabolism breaks down muscle tissue to supply energy.
The doctor explained that excessive catabolism can lead to muscle wasting in certain illnesses.
