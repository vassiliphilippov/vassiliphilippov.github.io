decking

verb
to decorate festively
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɛkɪŋ / (BrE)
decorating or adorning something, especially for a festival or celebration

Examples

To deck the hall
They spent the afternoon decking the hall with colorful streamers for the party.
Every December she enjoys decking the living room with lights and evergreen garlands.
The children helped their parents by decking the tree with ornaments and tinsel.
