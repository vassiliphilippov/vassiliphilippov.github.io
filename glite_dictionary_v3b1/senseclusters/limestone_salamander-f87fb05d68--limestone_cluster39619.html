limestone salamander

noun
a rare salamander species from California
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪmstoʊn səˈlæməndɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪmstəʊn səˈlæməndə / (BrE)
a rare species of salamander found in California, living in cliff crevices and talus slopes, distinguished by its habitat preference for limestone-rich areas

Examples

The limestone salamander species
Because of habitat destruction, the limestone salamander is considered a species of special concern.
Biologists conducted surveys to assess the population of the threatened limestone salamander.
The limestone salamander is only found along a small stretch of the Merced River in California.
