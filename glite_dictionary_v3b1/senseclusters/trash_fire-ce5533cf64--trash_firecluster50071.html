trash fire

noun
fire made from burning waste materials
Pronunciation: / træʃ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / træʃ ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
A fire created by burning waste materials or trash, often in an outdoor setting.

Examples

A trash fire burns
A trash fire in the abandoned lot filled the air with smoke.
On cold nights, homeless people sometimes warm themselves by a trash fire in the city.
The firefighters quickly extinguished the trash fire behind the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bonfire - large outdoor fire, often for celebration or disposal
  • synonym: dumpster fire - situation that is disastrously out of control (informal, figurative)
