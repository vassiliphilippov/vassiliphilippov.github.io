electric fire

noun
electric space heater resembling a fireplace
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk faɪr / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛk.trɪk ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
A small space heater powered by electricity, designed to mimic the appearance and function of a fireplace.

Examples

An electric fire heater
During winter, he relies on an electric fire in his bedroom for extra warmth.
Their living room has an old-fashioned electric fire beneath the mantelpiece.
We turned on the electric fire to warm up the chilly room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space heater - A portable device used to warm a single, small area.
