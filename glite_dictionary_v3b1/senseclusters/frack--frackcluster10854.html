frack

noun
hydraulic fracturing process
6.3
Pronunciation: / fræk / (AmE), / fræk / (BrE)
the process of extracting oil or gas from underground rock by injecting liquid at high pressure, known as hydraulic fracturing

Examples

A new frack site
The company plans to begin a new frack in the northern part of the state next month.
Environmental groups are concerned about the effects of each frack on local water supplies.
After the frack, the well produced significantly more natural gas than before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drilling - the act of making a hole in the ground to extract oil or gas
  • synonym: hydraulic fracturing - the technique of extracting oil or gas by injecting fluid into rock
