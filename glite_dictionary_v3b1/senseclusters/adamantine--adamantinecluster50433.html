Synonyms

    adamant is a common word meaning firm and refusing to change, while adamantine sounds more literary and absolutely inflexible.
  • unyielding
    6.5 0.5/m
    unyielding describes someone who will not give in under pressure, while adamantine implies total, unshakeable refusal.
  • intransigent
    8.1 0.4/m
    intransigent means refusing to compromise in negotiations, while adamantine suggests an absolute refusal in any request.
  • obdurate
