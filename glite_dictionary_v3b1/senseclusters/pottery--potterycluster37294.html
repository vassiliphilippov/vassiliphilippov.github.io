pottery

noun
objects made from baked clay
2.3 4.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêt…ôri / (AmE), / Ààp…ít…ôri / (BrE)
objects made of clay and hardened by heat, such as pots, dishes, and vases.

Examples

Handmade pottery pieces
She collects handmade pottery from local artists.
The ancient village is famous for its beautiful pottery.
We visited the museum's exhibit on Greek pottery techniques.

Synonyms

  • clayware
    6.7 0.00/m
    clayware is objects made from clay and fired in a kiln, while pottery refers broadly to clay objects like pots and vases.
  • ceramic ware
    3.0 0.04/m
    ceramic ware is plates, cups, and bowls made by firing clay or similar materials, while pottery includes a wider range of clay objects.
  • ceramic
    3.7 6.27/m
    ceramic is any fired nonmetallic object like tiles or figurines, while pottery means objects made specifically from clay.
