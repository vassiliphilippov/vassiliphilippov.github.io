broadcast journalist

noun
journalist working in radio or television
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɔːd.kæst ˈdʒɜːr.nə.lɪst/ /; / /ˈbrɑːd.kæst ˈdʒɜːr.nə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈbrɔːd.kɑːst ˈdʒɜː.nəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
