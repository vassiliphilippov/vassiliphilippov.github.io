starlessness

noun
absence of visible stars
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈstɑːləsnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having no visible stars in the sky.

Examples

The starlessness of night
A heavy cloud cover brought an eerie starlessness to the landscape.
She stared at the ceiling, imagining the starlessness outside her window.
The starlessness of the night made it seem more lonely than usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darkness - the partial or total absence of light
