limited edition

noun
edition restricted to a specific number of copies
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmɪt̬ɪd ɪˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈlɪmɪtɪd ɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
An edition of a book, print, or other product of which only a fixed, usually small, number are produced for sale or distribution.

Examples

A limited edition book
Owning a limited edition book can make a collection more valuable.
She bought a limited edition print that was signed by the artist.
The company released a limited edition sneaker that sold out within hours.
