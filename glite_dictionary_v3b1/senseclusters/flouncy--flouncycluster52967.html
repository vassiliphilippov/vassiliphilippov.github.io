flouncy

adjective
with frills or ruffles
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊnsi / (AmE), / ˈflaʊnsi / (BrE)
decorated with flounces, frills, or ruffles, or having a lively, showy, or exaggerated style or movement (especially of clothing or fabric)
