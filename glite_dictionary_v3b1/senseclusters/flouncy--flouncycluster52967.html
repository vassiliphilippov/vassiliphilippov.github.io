flouncy

adjective
with frills or ruffles
7.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆflaÊŠnsi / (AmE), / ËˆflaÊŠnsi / (BrE)
decorated with flounces, frills, or ruffles, or having a lively, showy, or exaggerated style or movement (especially of clothing or fabric)

Examples

Synonyms

    4.1 1.17/m

  • ruffly
    5.3 0.04/m
    ruffly has many gathered or pleated ruffles that give texture and detail, while flouncy suggests bigger, swinging layers and showy movement.
