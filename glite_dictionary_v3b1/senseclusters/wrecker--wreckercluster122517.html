wrecker

noun
person who demolishes buildings
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈrɛkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to demolish or dismantle buildings

Examples

Building wrecker
After the earthquake, a wrecker was hired to safely dismantle damaged buildings.
The wrecker worked quickly to clear the site for new construction.
The old factory was torn down by a professional wrecker last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: builder - person who constructs buildings
  • synonym: demolisher - person who destroys buildings or structures
