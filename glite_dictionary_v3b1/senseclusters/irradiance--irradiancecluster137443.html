irradiance

noun
light energy per area
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈreɪdiəns / (AmE), / ɪˈreɪdiəns / (BrE)
the amount of light energy received on a surface per unit area

Examples

Solar irradiance level
The irradiance of the sun is highest at noon on a clear day.
Engineers calculated the irradiance to determine the efficiency of the solar panels.
Low irradiance levels can affect the growth of certain plants in shaded areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illumination - the amount of light falling on a surface
  • antonym: radiance - the amount of light emitted from a surface
