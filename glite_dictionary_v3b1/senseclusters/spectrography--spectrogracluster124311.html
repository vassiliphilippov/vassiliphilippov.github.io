Synonyms

    spectrographic analysis examines recorded spectra to find composition, while spectrography is the method of recording and measuring those spectra.
  • spectroscopic analysis
    7.2 0.04/m
    spectroscopic analysis examines how substances interact with light to identify composition, while spectrography names the recording process directly.
  • spectrometry

  • spectrum analysis
