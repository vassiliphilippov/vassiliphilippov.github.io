ladder truck

noun
fire engine with extendable ladders
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlædər ˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈlædə ˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a fire engine equipped with extendable ladders used by firefighters to access high places or rescue people
