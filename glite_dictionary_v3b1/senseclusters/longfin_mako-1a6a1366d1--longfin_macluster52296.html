longfin mako

noun
species of large mackerel shark
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːŋ.fɪn ˈmeɪ.koʊ/ /; / /ˈlɔːŋ.fɪn ˈmɑː.koʊ/ (AmE), /ˈlɒŋ.fɪn ˈmeɪ.kəʊ/ /; / /ˈlɒŋ.fɪn ˈmɑː.kəʊ/ (BrE)

Unlike its close relative, the longfin mako is rarely seen by divers.
