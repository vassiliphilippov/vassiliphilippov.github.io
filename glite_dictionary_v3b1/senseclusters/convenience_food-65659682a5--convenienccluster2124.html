convenience food

noun
pre-packaged, easy-to-prepare food
4.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈvinjəns fuːd / (AmE), / kənˈviːniəns fuːd / (BrE)
A pre-packaged food product designed for quick and easy preparation, usually by thawing, heating, or minimal cooking.

Examples

Packaged convenience food
Many busy people rely on convenience food after a long day at work.
Most supermarkets stock a wide range of convenience food options for quick meals.
She tries to avoid convenience food and cooks from scratch whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fast food - inexpensive food prepared and served quickly
  • antonym: home-cooked meal - a meal prepared and cooked at home from basic ingredients
  • synonym: ready meal - a pre-cooked meal needing only reheating
