blitz

verb
to attack intensively
4.2
Pronunciation: /blɪts/ (AmE), /blɪts/ (BrE)
to do something or carry out a campaign very quickly and with a lot of energy

We need to blitz this project if we want to finish by Friday.
He blitzed the cleaning, finishing the whole house in just an hour.
