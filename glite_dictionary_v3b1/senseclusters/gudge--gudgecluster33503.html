gudge

noun
small freshwater fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʌdʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌdʒ / (BrE)
a small freshwater fish, similar to a gudgeon, often found in rivers and streams and sometimes used as bait

Examples

A small gudge fish
He caught a tiny gudge in the river and used it as bait for larger trout.
Children often try to catch gudge in the shallow parts of the stream during summer.
The fisherman used a gudge to attract bigger fish in the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gudgeon - a small freshwater fish commonly found in rivers and streams
  • synonym: minnow - a very small freshwater fish often used as bait
