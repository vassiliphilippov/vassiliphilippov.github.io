criminalization

noun
making something illegal by law
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɪmɪnələˈzeɪʃən /; / krɪm.ə.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɪmɪnələˈzeɪʃən /; / krɪm.ɪ.nə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of making an activity illegal by law or statute
