innovative

adjective
introducing new ideas or methods
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəˌveɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈɪnəvətɪv / (BrE)
introducing or using new ideas, methods, or products; original and creative

Their innovative solutions helped solve long-standing problems in the community.
