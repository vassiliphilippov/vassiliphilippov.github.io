Bantu

noun
member of a Central/Southern African ethnolinguistic group
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæn.tu / (AmE), / ˈban.tuː / (BrE)
A member of any of a large group of peoples in Central and Southern Africa who speak Bantu languages.

Examples

Bantu people
Archaeologists study the ancient settlements of Bantu peoples.
Many Bantu traditions are still practiced in rural communities today.
The Bantu migrated across large areas of Africa over thousands of years.
