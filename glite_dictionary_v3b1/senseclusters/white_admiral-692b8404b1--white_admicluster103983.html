white admiral

noun
North American butterfly with white-banded wings
7.0
Pronunciation: / waɪt ˈædmɪrəl / (AmE), / waɪt ˈædmɪrəl / (BrE)
A North American butterfly with blue-black wings marked by a broad white band, scientifically known as Limenitis arthemis.

Examples

North American white admiral
Children chased a white admiral as it fluttered through the garden.
