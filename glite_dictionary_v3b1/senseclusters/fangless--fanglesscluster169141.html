fangless

adjective
without fangs
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæŋləs / (AmE), / ˈfæŋləs / (BrE)
lacking fangs or long, pointed teeth

Examples

Fangless snake
The fangless snake posed no threat to humans.
Scientists discovered a fangless species of bat in the remote cave.
Unlike its relatives, this spider is completely fangless and harmless.
