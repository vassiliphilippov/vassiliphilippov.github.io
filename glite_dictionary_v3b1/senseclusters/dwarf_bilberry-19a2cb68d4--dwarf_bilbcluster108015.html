dwarf bilberry

noun
a low-growing North American shrub with blue berries
7.5
Pronunciation: / dwɔrf ˈbɪlbɛri / (AmE), / dwɔːf ˈbɪlbəri / (BrE)
A low-growing deciduous shrub found in northern and alpine regions of North America, with pink to coral-red flowers and sweet blue berries.

Examples

The dwarf bilberry plant
Hikers often snack on the sweet berries produced by the dwarf bilberry.
The dwarf bilberry thrives in the cold, mountainous areas of Canada.
The pink flowers of the dwarf bilberry appear in spring before the berries develop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bilberry - a small dark blue edible berry and the plant that bears it
