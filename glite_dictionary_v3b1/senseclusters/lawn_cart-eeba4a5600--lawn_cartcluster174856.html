lawn cart

noun
small wheelbarrow for gardening loads
2.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / l…în k…ërt / (AmE), / l…îÀên k…ëÀêt / (BrE)
a small cart with handles and one or more wheels, used for carrying gardening materials and small loads.

Examples

A lawn cart for grass
After mowing, she collected the grass clippings in the lawn cart for disposal.
He loaded the bags of mulch onto the lawn cart before spreading them around the trees.
We used the lawn cart to move soil from the shed to the garden beds.

Synonyms

  • garden cart
    2.3 0.07/m
    a garden cart is a four wheeled trolley used in gardens for plants and tools, while a lawn cart is similar for general garden loads.
  • wheelbarrow
    1.0 1.47/m
    a wheelbarrow has a single front wheel and long handles for tipping loads, while a lawn cart often has more wheels and a flat bed.
  • barrow
    6.2 0.13/m
    a barrow refers to a simple hand pushed cart for small loads in many settings, while a lawn cart is mainly for garden tasks.
