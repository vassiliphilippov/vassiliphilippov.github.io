a bit of a crapshoot

noun
an uncertain or risky situation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ə ˈbɪt ʌv ə ˈkræpˌʃuːt / (AmE), / ə ˈbɪt ɒv ə ˈkræpʃuːt / (BrE)
A situation in which the outcome is highly uncertain or left to chance; a risky or unpredictable situation.

Examples

A bit of a crapshoot situation
Getting tickets to the sold-out show was a bit of a crapshoot for everyone.
Job interviews can feel like a bit of a crapshoot, no matter how well you prepare.
Launching a new product in this market is a bit of a crapshoot.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gamble - a risky action or decision
  • antonym: safe bet - something likely to be successful or secure
