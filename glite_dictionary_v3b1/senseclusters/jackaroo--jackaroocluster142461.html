jackaroo

noun
Australian ranch trainee
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒækəˈruː / (AmE), / ˌdʒækəˈruː / (BrE)
a young man working or training on a sheep or cattle station in Australia to gain experience in farming or ranching
