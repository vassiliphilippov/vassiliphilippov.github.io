curdled

adjective
turned into a semisolid or solid mass

Pronunciation: / Ààk…ùÀêd.…ôld / (AmE), / Ààk…úÀê.dld / (BrE)
Transformed from a liquid into a soft semisolid or solid mass, especially due to a chemical or physical process such as souring or coagulation.

Examples
