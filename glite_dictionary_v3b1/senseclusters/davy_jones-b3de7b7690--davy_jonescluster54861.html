Davy Jones

noun
fictional sea-demon captain
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪvi ˈdʒoʊnz / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvi ˈdʒəʊnz / (BrE)
a fictional, often monstrous sea captain or sea-demon—commonly portrayed as the captain of the ghost ship the Flying Dutchman in modern popular culture.

Examples

Davy Jones the captain
In the movie, Davy Jones is a fearsome sea captain with a tentacled face and a cursed heart.
Many fans argued that Davy Jones showed moments of regret despite his monstrous appearance and power.
The actor spent hours in makeup to transform into Davy Jones for the film's dramatic sea scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Davy Jones's locker - the figurative place at the bottom of the sea where drowned sailors go; closely associated with the name 'Davy Jones'.
  • synonym: Flying Dutchman - a legendary ghost ship whose captain is often identified as Davy Jones in modern fiction.
