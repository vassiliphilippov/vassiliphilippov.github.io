chewie

noun
chewy candy
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃuːi / (AmE), / ˈtʃuːi / (BrE)
a chewy sweet or piece of candy, often used as a nickname for such items

Examples

A strawberry chewie
He offered me a chewie from the bag of sweets after lunch.
Children often enjoy a chewie as a treat during the school break.
She unwrapped a strawberry chewie and popped it into her mouth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gum - a chewy sweet that is chewed but not swallowed
  • antonym: hard candy - a sweet that is hard rather than chewy
  • synonym: toffee - a chewy sweet made from sugar and butter
