malcontented

adjective
dissatisfied with authority
Pronunciation: / ˌmæl.kənˈtɛntɪd / (AmE), / ˌmæl.kənˈtɛntɪd / (BrE)
dissatisfied or unhappy, especially with authority or the current situation

Examples

The malcontented workers
The malcontented employees organized a protest to demand better working conditions.
She grew increasingly malcontented with the lack of progress in her career.
A malcontented group of students voiced their concerns at the school meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discontented - not satisfied; unhappy with a situation
