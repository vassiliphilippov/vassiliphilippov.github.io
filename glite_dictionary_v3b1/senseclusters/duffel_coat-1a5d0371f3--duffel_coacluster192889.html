duffel coat

noun
a hooded coat fastened with toggles
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌfəl koʊt / (AmE), / ˈdʌfəl kəʊt / (BrE)
a warm, thick, typically knee-length coat with a hood, made from duffel or other thick, coarse material, and fastened with toggle buttons (often wooden or horn)

Examples

A duffel coat with toggles
In winter, he prefers a duffel coat because it keeps him warm and comfortable.
She wore her favorite duffel coat on the chilly walk to the market.
The duffel coat is easily recognized by its distinctive toggle fastenings.
He wore a navy blue duffle coat to keep warm during the winter months.
Her father’s old duffle coat was still hanging in the hallway.
Toggle fastenings make the duffle coat easy to put on even with gloves.
