up against it

adjective
facing difficulties or pressure
3.6
Pronunciation: / ʌp əˈɡɛnst ɪt / (AmE), / ʌp əˈɡɛnst ɪt / (BrE)
facing severe difficulties or great pressure, especially with little time or resources

Examples

To be up against it
She was up against it after losing her job and dealing with mounting bills.
The team found themselves up against it as injuries piled up before the final.
With all these unexpected expenses, we're really up against it this month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in trouble - experiencing difficulties or problems
