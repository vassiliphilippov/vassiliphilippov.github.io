tourbillon

noun
rotating watch mechanism
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtʊr.bɪ.jɑːn/ /; / /ˈtʊr.bɪ.lɑːn/ (AmE), /ˈtʊə.bɪ.jɒn/ /; / /ˈtʊə.bɪ.lɒn/ (BrE)
