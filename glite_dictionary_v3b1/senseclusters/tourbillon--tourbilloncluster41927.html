tourbillon

noun
rotating watch mechanism
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrbɪˌjɒn / (AmE), / ˈtʊəbɪˌjɒn / (BrE)
a rotating mechanism in a watch that improves accuracy by counteracting the effects of gravity on the movement

Examples

Watch tourbillon mechanism
The master watchmaker carefully assembled the tourbillon to ensure the highest precision.
Collectors admire watches with a visible tourbillon for their craftsmanship and complexity.
Adding a tourbillon can significantly increase the value of a luxury timepiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balance wheel - the part of a mechanical watch that oscillates to regulate timekeeping
  • synonym: escapement - the mechanism in a watch or clock that controls the movement of the gear train
