boca

noun
water inlet or channel
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊkə / (AmE), / ˈbəʊkə / (BrE)
a narrow entrance or channel connecting a bay, lagoon, or river to the sea

Examples

River boca
The fishermen navigated the shallow boca carefully to reach the sheltered anchorage before nightfall.
A narrow boca connects the lagoon to the open sea, allowing boats to pass through.
During high tide, water rushes through the boca and fills the bay quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: channel - a narrow stretch of water joining two larger areas of water
  • synonym: inlet - a small arm of the sea, a lake, or a river
