unmenacing

adjective
not threatening
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɛnəsɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛnəsɪŋ / (BrE)
not threatening or frightening in appearance or manner

Examples

An unmenacing smile
The comedian's unmenacing style made the audience feel comfortable and relaxed.
Despite its large size, the dog had an unmenacing expression and wagged its tail happily.
Her unmenacing tone reassured the children that everything was fine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gentle - kind and not likely to hurt anyone
  • synonym: harmless - not able or likely to cause harm
  • antonym: menacing - threatening or suggesting danger
