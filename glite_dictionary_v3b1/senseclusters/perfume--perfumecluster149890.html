perfume

noun
fragrant liquid for scenting the body
1.0 5.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀê.fjuÀêm / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.fjuÀêm / (BrE)
A fragrant liquid typically made from essential oils and alcohol, used to give a pleasant scent to a person's body.

Examples

A bottle of perfume
His gift to her was a bottle of expensive perfume.
She sprayed a little perfume on her wrists before leaving the house.
The store carries an exclusive line of French perfume.

Synonyms

  • parfum
    4.8 0.07/m
    parfum is a pleasurable scent term often used for concentrated perfume, while perfume is the general fragrant liquid worn on the body.
  • essence
    4.7 0.25/m
    essence is a fragrant liquid or toiletry used for scenting clothes or skin, while perfume is a body fragrance for personal wear.
  • fragrance
    2.6 8.35/m
    fragrance is the pleasant smell you notice in air or products, while perfume is a liquid you apply to your body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cologne - A type of light perfume
  • synonym: fragrance - A pleasant, sweet smell; a product used for scent
