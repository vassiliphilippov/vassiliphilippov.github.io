pressed for time

adjective
having little time; rushed
Pronunciation: / prɛst fər taɪm / (AmE), / prɛst fə taɪm / (BrE)
in a situation where one has little time and must hurry

Examples

To be pressed for time
I'm pressed for time this morning, so I'll eat breakfast on the go.
If you're pressed for time, we can reschedule the meeting for later.
Whenever she's pressed for time, she prioritizes the most urgent tasks first.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in a hurry - rushing because of little time
  • synonym: short on time - having insufficient time available
