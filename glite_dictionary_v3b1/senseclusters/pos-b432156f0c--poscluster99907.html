POS

noun
retail checkout system
3.9
Pronunciation: / pi oʊ ɛs / (AmE), / piː əʊ ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'point of sale', meaning the place or system where a retail transaction is completed

Examples

POS terminal in store
The new POS system allows customers to pay with their phones quickly and easily.
Retailers often upgrade their POS terminals to improve security and speed.
Training staff on the POS software is essential for smooth store operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cash register - a machine used to record sales and handle cash in stores
  • synonym: checkout - the place where customers pay for goods in a store
