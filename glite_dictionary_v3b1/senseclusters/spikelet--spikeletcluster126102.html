spikelet

noun
small pointed projection on plant
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪk.lət / (AmE), / ˈspaɪk.lɪt / (BrE)
a small, sharp-pointed tip or projection resembling a spike on a stem or leaf, especially in plants

Examples

A spikelet tip
Careful handling is required, as the spikelet tips can prick the skin.
Each spikelet contains several florets arranged closely along the stem.
The grass was covered in tiny spikelets that glistened with morning dew.
