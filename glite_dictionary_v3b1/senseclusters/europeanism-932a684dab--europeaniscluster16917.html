Europeanism

noun
European language feature
8.1
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊpiˈænɪzəm / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊpiˈænɪzəm / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or linguistic feature borrowed from or characteristic of European languages, used in another language

Examples

A Europeanism in language
Linguists have observed an increase in Europeanism in the vocabulary of the local language.
The use of certain idioms is a clear example of Europeanism in modern speech.
Some critics argue that Europeanism threatens the uniqueness of regional dialects.
