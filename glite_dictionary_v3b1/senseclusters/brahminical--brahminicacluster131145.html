brahminical

adjective
relating to Brahmins or their traditions
6.0
Pronunciation: / brɑːˈmɪnɪkəl / (AmE), / brɑːˈmɪnɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Brahmins, especially in regard to orthodox Hindu tradition and values

Examples

Brahminical values
Her upbringing was deeply influenced by brahminical values and customs.
Some critics argue that the laws reflect an outdated brahminical worldview.
The temple was known for its brahminical rituals and strict observance of tradition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brahmin - a member of the highest Hindu caste, traditionally priests or scholars
