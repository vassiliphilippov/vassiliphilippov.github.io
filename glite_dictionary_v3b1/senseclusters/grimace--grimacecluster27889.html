grimace

noun
twisted facial expression
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪmɪs, ɡrɪˈmeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪməs, ɡrɪˈmeɪs / (BrE)
an expression on your face in which your features are twisted, usually showing pain, disgust, or wry amusement

Examples

A painful grimace
She tried to hide her grimace when she tasted the sour lemon.
The comedian pulled a funny grimace to make the children laugh.
With a pained grimace, he lifted the heavy box.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scowl - an angry or bad-tempered expression
  • antonym: smile - a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
