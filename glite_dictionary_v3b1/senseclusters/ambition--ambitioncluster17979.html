ambition

noun
strong desire to achieve or succeed
Pronunciation: / æmˈbɪʃ.ən /; / æmˈbɪʃən / (AmE), / æmˈbɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a strong desire to achieve something, especially success or a particular goal

Examples

Strong ambition
He finally achieved his lifelong ambition of climbing Mount Everest.
Her ambition to become a doctor kept her motivated throughout her studies.
The young artist's ambition was evident in every painting she created.
He admired her ambition and relentless pursuit of her dreams.
Her ambition to become a doctor kept her studying late into the night.
Without ambition, it is hard to achieve major goals in life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • synonym: aspiration - a hope or ambition of achieving something
  • synonym: drive - strong motivation to achieve goals
  • synonym: goal - an aim or desired result
