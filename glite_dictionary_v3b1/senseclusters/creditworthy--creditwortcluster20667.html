creditworthy

adjective
having a good credit record
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɝːði / (AmE), / ˈkrɛdɪtˌwɜːði / (BrE)
considered suitable to receive credit, especially because of having a good credit record or financial reliability

Examples

A creditworthy borrower
After reviewing his application, the bank concluded he was creditworthy and approved the loan.
Only creditworthy customers qualify for lower interest rates on mortgages and car loans.
The company’s strong financial performance makes it highly creditworthy in the eyes of potential investors.
