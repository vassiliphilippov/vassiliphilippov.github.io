12-tone music

noun
serial music using all twelve chromatic tones
5.4 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtwelv.toʊn ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (AmE), /ˈtwelv.təʊn ˈmjuː.zɪk/ (BrE)
A style of serial music using a fixed order of all twelve tones in the chromatic scale, introduced by Arnold Schoenberg.

Examples

12-tone music style
Arnold Schoenberg is credited with developing 12-tone music as a compositional method.
Many composers experimented with 12-tone music during the twentieth century.
Understanding 12-tone music requires familiarity with the chromatic scale and serial techniques.
