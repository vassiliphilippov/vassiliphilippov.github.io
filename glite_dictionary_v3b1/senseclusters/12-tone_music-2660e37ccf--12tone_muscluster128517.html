Synonyms

  • twelve-tone music
    8.6 0.00/m
    Presents a compositional system using every chromatic note in a fixed recurring order
    vs 12-tone music: A modern system of musical composition in which all twelve notes of the chromatic scale are used in a fixed, recurring order, as introduced by Arnold Schoenberg.
  • 12-tone system
    9.3 0.00/m
    Describes a technical method arranging all chromatic semitones into a fixed tone row
    vs 12-tone music: a method of musical composition using all twelve semitones of the chromatic scale in a fixed order (tone row), typically associated with serial music and developed by Arnold Schoenberg.
  • twelve-tone system
    9.9 0.00/m
    Describes a method that arranges a tone row and uses variations like inversion and retrograde
    vs 12-tone music: A method of musical composition in which all twelve semitones of the chromatic scale are arranged in a particular sequence (tone row) and used systematically, often with variations such as inversion or retrograde; introduced by Arnold Schoenberg.
