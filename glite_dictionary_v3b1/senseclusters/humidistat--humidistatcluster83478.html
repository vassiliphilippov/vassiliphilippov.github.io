humidistat

noun
humidity control device
7.1
Pronunciation: / hjuːˈmɪdɪˌstæt / (AmE), / hjuːˈmɪdɪˌstæt / (BrE)
a device that measures and controls the humidity level in the air

Examples

The humidistat device
The humidistat in the living room keeps the air comfortable during the summer.
If the humidistat detects high humidity, it will activate the ventilation system automatically.
She adjusted the humidistat to prevent mold from growing in the basement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hygrometer - an instrument for measuring humidity
  • synonym: thermostat - a device that controls temperature
