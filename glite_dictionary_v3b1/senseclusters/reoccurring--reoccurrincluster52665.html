reoccurring

adjective
happening again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈkɜːrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈkɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or appearing again, often repeatedly

Examples

A reoccurring dream
He has a reoccurring dream about flying that wakes him up every week.
The reoccurring problem with the printer delayed the project several times.
Allergy symptoms can be reoccurring during certain seasons each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: one-time - happening only once
  • synonym: recurring - happening or appearing repeatedly
