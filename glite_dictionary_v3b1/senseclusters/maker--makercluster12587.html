maker

noun
participant in the Maker Movement; DIY digital creator
3.7
Pronunciation: / c8me6ak59r / (AmE), / c8me6ak59 / (BrE)
a person who participates in the Maker Movement: a DIY creator who builds, fabrics, or prototypes projects (often using digital tools such as 3D printers or microcontrollers) and shares them with a community.

Examples

A creative maker
She is a maker who designs modular gadgets and shares plans online.
The community of makers met at the local hackerspace to test 3D-printed prototypes.
As a maker, he taught children basic woodworking and electronics in weekend workshops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: DIYer - a person who makes, modifies, or repairs things themselves rather than buying them ready-made
  • synonym: creator - a person who makes something new, especially a project or work using skill and imagination
