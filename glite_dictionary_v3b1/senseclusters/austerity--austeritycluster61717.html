austerity

noun
government spending cuts
7.4
Pronunciation: /ɔː.ˈste.rɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɔː.ˈste.rə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈste.rɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɔː.ˈste.rə.tiː/ (BrE)
an economic policy or period in which a government reduces public spending, services, or borrowing to lower budget deficits.

Examples

Austerity measures
Facing rising debt, the government announced new austerity measures to trim public services.
Many families struggled after years of austerity, with cuts to health and education budgets.
Opponents argued that prolonged austerity slowed economic growth and increased unemployment in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: budget cut - a reduction in government spending for a department or program
  • antonym: stimulus - government measures (such as increased spending or tax cuts) intended to boost economic activity
