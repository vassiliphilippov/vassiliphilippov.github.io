cataphoresis

noun
movement of charged particles in a colloid under electric field
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəfəˈriːsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkatəfəˈriːsɪs / (BrE)
the movement of charged particles in a colloid under the influence of an electric field, typically with positive particles moving to the cathode and negative to the anode
