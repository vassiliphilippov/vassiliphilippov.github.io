ultralight

adjective
very low-calorie
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
containing very few calories or fat, especially used to describe food or drink products marketed as healthier options

Examples

Ultralight yogurt
She ordered an ultralight salad dressing to go with her lunch.
Many people prefer ultralight snacks when trying to lose weight.
The new ultralight yogurt contains almost no fat or added sugar.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: light - containing fewer calories or less fat than usual
  • antonym: regular - having the standard amount of calories or fat
