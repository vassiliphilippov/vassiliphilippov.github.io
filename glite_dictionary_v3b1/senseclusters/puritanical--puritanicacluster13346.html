Synonyms

  • straight-laced
    7.1 0.12/m
    straight-laced is very proper and follows strict social rules, while puritanical focuses on harsh moral rules about pleasure and enjoyment.
  • prudish
    7.1 0.52/m
    prudish is easily shocked by sexual or nude topics and cares about modesty, while puritanical is stricter about many pleasures.
  • moralistic
    7.2 1.07/m
    moralistic judges others and talks about right and wrong, while puritanical is more rigid about avoiding pleasure.
  • Victorian
    8.2 1.22/m
    Victorian sounds old-fashioned and tied to a past era's strict manners, while puritanical means strict moral control today.
