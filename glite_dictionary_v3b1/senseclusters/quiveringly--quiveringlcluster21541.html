quiveringly

adverb
shaking slightly
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪvərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈkwɪvərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shakes or trembles slightly, often because of fear, cold, or strong emotion

Examples

To quiveringly shake
He reached out quiveringly to accept the award, his nerves clearly visible.
The candlelight flickered as she spoke quiveringly about her experience.
His voice sounded quiveringly uncertain as he confessed the truth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steadily - in a firm, stable, or unwavering manner
  • synonym: tremulously - in a trembling or shaking manner
