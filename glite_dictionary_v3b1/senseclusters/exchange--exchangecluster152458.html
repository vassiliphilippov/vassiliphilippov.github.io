Synonyms

  • swap
    2.6 7.96/m
    swap is a simple one-for-one trade often between friends, while exchange covers formal or informal giving and receiving of items.
  • interchange
    3.9 1.16/m
    interchange is mutual sharing of ideas, goods, or money between groups, while exchange is a general act of trading or replacing things.
  • quid pro quo
    8.0 3.95/m
    quid pro quo names a deliberate give-and-take in deals or favors, while exchange is any act of trading or swapping things.
  • substitution
    3.5 6.43/m
    substitution is replacing one thing with another in a set place or role, while exchange implies mutual giving and receiving.
  • commutation
    7.4 0.02/m
    commutation is a formal act of substituting one thing for another, often legal or technical, while exchange is everyday trade or swapping.
