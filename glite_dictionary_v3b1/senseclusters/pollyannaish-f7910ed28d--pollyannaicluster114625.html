Pollyannaish

adjective
unrealistically optimistic
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /pɑː.lɪ.ˈæn.ɪʃ/ /; / /pə.lɪ.ˈæn.ɪʃ/ (AmE), /pɒ.lɪ.ˈæn.ɪʃ/ /; / /pə.lɪ.ˈæn.ɪʃ/ (BrE)
pleasantly or unrealistically optimistic, especially in a way that seems naive or blindly positive

Examples

Pollyannaish optimism
Even in tough times, she maintained a Pollyannaish optimism that inspired those around her.
Her Pollyannaish outlook made her believe everything would work out for the best, no matter the obstacles.
Some critics dismissed his Pollyannaish predictions as naive and unrealistic.
