blimp out

verb
inflate or increase greatly
8.1 7.3
Pronunciation: / blɪmp aʊt / (AmE), / blɪmp aʊt / (BrE)
(informal) to become significantly larger, especially by inflating or expanding rapidly; to increase or swell considerably.

Examples

To blimp out quickly
If you keep eating like this, you'll blimp out in no time.
Once the inflatable raft was filled with air, it really blimped out.
The company's expenses blimped out after the new project started.
