nonferrous

adjective
without iron
5.8
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɛrəs / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɛrəs / (BrE)
describing metals that do not contain significant amounts of iron

Examples

Nonferrous metals
Copper and aluminum are both considered nonferrous metals in the recycling industry.
The factory specializes in producing nonferrous alloys for use in aircraft manufacturing.
Because it is nonferrous, this metal will not rust like iron or steel.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ferrous - containing or relating to iron
