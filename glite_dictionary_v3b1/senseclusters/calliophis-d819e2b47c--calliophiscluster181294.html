Calliophis

noun
genus of Asian coral snakes
Pronunciation: / ˌkæliˈɒfɪs /; / kæliˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ˌkælɪˈɒfɪs /; / kæliˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
a genus of small, highly venomous Asian coral snakes found in South and Southeast Asia, known for their bright coloration
