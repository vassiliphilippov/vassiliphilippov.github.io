hysterectomy

noun
surgical removal of the uterus
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstəˈrɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəˈrɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of a woman's uterus

Examples

A hysterectomy operation
After her doctor diagnosed uterine cancer, she had to undergo a hysterectomy.
Many women feel anxious before a hysterectomy, but support from family is helpful.
Recovery from a hysterectomy can take several weeks depending on the surgical method used.

  • synonym: mastectomy - surgical removal of a breast
