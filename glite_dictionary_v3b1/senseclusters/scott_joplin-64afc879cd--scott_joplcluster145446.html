Scott Joplin

proper noun
American composer, 'King of Ragtime'
4.9
Pronunciation: /skɑːt ˈdʒɑː.plɪn/ (AmE), /skɒt ˈdʒɒ.plɪn/ (BrE)
(1868–1917) an American composer and pianist known as the 'King of Ragtime', who was the first creator of ragtime music to systematically write down and publish his compositions.

Scott Joplin ragtime composer
