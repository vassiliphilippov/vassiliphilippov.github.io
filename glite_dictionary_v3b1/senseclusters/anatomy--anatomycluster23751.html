anatomy

noun
study of bodily structure
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnætəmi / (AmE), / əˈnætəmi / (BrE)
the scientific study of the structure of living organisms, especially their internal parts

Examples

Human anatomy class
Comparing the anatomy of different mammals helps scientists understand evolutionary relationships.
He is studying anatomy at medical school to become a surgeon.
The book includes detailed drawings to illustrate bird anatomy.
