linguistic context

noun
surrounding discourse of a language unit
4.1
Pronunciation: / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk ˈkɑːntɛkst / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪk ˈkɒntɛkst / (BrE)
the surrounding words, sentences, or discourse that help to determine the meaning or interpretation of a language unit
