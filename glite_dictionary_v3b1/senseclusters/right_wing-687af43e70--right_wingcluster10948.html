right wing

noun
conservative political group or ideology
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪt ˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈraɪt ˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
the group of people who support conservative or traditional political, social, or economic policies, often favoring minimal change.
