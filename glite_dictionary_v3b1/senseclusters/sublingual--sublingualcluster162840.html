sublingual

adjective
beneath the tongue
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈlɪŋɡwəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɪŋɡwəl / (BrE)
Situated or administered beneath the tongue.

Examples

Sublingual administration
Absorption of drugs through sublingual administration can be faster than oral ingestion.
The doctor prescribed a sublingual tablet for rapid relief of symptoms.
The patient placed the medication under his tongue for sublingual absorption.
