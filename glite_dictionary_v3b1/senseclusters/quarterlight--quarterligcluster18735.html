quarterlight

noun
small vent window in a car door
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔːrdərˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtələɪt / (BrE)
a small, typically pivoted window vent in the door or side of a car, designed to provide ventilation

Examples

Car quarterlight window
He opened the quarterlight to let in some fresh air while driving.
She noticed rain dripping through the quarterlight during the storm.
The old car's quarterlights were stuck shut and needed repairs.
