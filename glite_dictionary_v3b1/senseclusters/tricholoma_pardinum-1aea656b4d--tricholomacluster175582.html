Tricholoma pardinum

noun
a species of poisonous mushroom
9.9
Pronunciation: / trɪkəˈloʊmə pɑrˈdaɪnəm / (AmE), / trɪkəˈləʊmə pɑːˈdɪnəm / (BrE)
A species of poisonous mushroom with a pale cap covered in fine grey fibrils, found in woodlands of Europe and North America.
