make-up

noun
cosmetic products for face or body
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈmeɪk.ʌp / (BrE)
Cosmetics applied to the face or body to enhance or alter appearance.

Examples

Wear make-up daily
Do you usually buy your make-up online or in stores?
She applied her make-up carefully before the interview.
Some actors wear heavy make-up for their roles on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmetics - substances applied to the body, especially the face, to enhance or alter appearance
  • synonym: face paint - paint or colored substances applied to the face for decoration or disguise
