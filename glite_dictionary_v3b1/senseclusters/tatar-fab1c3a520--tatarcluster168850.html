Tatar

noun
a Turkic ethnic group of Eurasia
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːtɑr /; / ˈtɑːtər / (AmE), / ˈtɑːtɑː / (BrE)
a member of the Tatar people, a Turkic-speaking ethnic group from Russia and Central Asia (historically known for their influence in Eastern Europe)

Examples

A Tatar person
He learned the Tatar language while traveling through the region.
Many Tatar people live in the Republic of Tatarstan in Russia.
The Tatar community has a rich tradition of literature and music.
He was fascinated by the history of the Tatar people and their influence on Eastern Europe.
Many Tatar warriors crossed into Europe during the Mongol invasions.
The Tatar community in Russia maintains its cultural identity today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mongol - a member of an East-Central Asian ethnic group, historically known as conquerors of large parts of Eurasia
  • synonym: Turkic - relating to the family of Turkic peoples or languages
