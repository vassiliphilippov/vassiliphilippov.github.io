immortally

adverb
enduring forever
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔrtəli / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəli / (BrE)
in a way that will last forever or never die

Examples

To be immortally famous
The artist's work was immortally celebrated by future generations.
Her words were immortally inscribed on the monument for all to see.
The legend of the king lived on immortally in the hearts of his people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternally - in a way that lasts forever
  • antonym: temporarily - for a limited time only
