nonidentification

noun
refusal to identify
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn aɪˌdɛntəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or instance of not revealing or confirming one's identity or personal details

Examples

Witness's nonidentification
The witness's nonidentification made it difficult for the police to proceed with the investigation.
Strict privacy laws sometimes allow for nonidentification in sensitive legal cases.
His deliberate nonidentification at the event raised suspicions among the organizers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anonymity - the state of not being identified by name
  • antonym: identification - the act of establishing or indicating who or what someone is
