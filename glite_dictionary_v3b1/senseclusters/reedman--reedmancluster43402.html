reedman

noun
reed craftsman
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdmən / (AmE), / ˈriːdmən / (BrE)
a person who collects, works with, or crafts items from reeds

Examples

Skilled reedman
The reedman skillfully wove a new basket from freshly gathered reeds.
Villagers often relied on the reedman to repair their mats and baskets.
A local reedman demonstrated traditional weaving techniques at the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basketmaker - a person who makes baskets by hand
  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled worker who makes things by hand
