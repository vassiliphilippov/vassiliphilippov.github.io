pathbreaker

noun
pioneer or innovator
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpæθˌbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːθˌbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person who is the first to do something new or innovative, leading the way for others

Examples

A pathbreaker in science
As a pathbreaker in renewable energy, she inspired many others to join the industry.
The artist was considered a pathbreaker for introducing new techniques to the field.
Many admire him as a pathbreaker who challenged traditional ways of thinking.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who comes after or imitates others
  • synonym: pioneer - a person who is among the first to explore or develop something new
  • synonym: trailblazer - someone who makes a new track or path, especially in a new field
