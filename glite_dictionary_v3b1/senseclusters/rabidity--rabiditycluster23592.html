rabidity

noun
unrestrained excitement or enthusiasm
9.1 8.5
Pronunciation: /rə.ˈbɪd.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /reɪ.ˈbɪd.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /rə.ˈbɪd.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /reɪ.ˈbɪd.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
extreme or wild enthusiasm, intensity, or zeal (often unreasonable or fanatical)

Examples

Extreme rabidity
His rabidity for the cause inspired others to join the movement.
Some critics dismissed the fans' rabidity as mere hysteria.
The crowd's rabidity at the concert created an electric atmosphere.
Her rabidity when discussing her favorite band surprised even her closest friends.
Media coverage sometimes exaggerates the rabidity of online fan communities.
The rabidity of the political debate made it impossible for either side to compromise.

  • synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion
  • synonym: zealotry - excessive zeal or fanaticism
