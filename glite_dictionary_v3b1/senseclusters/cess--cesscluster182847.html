cess

noun
government tax or levy
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɛs / (AmE), / sɛs / (BrE)
a tax or levy, especially one imposed by a government for a specific purpose

Examples

A cess on fuel
The government introduced a new cess on fuel to fund road repairs.
Farmers protested against the increased cess imposed on agricultural produce.
A special education cess was added to support local schools.

Related Terms

  • synonym: levy - an amount of money collected by a government or authority for a specific purpose
  • synonym: tax - a compulsory financial charge imposed by a government
