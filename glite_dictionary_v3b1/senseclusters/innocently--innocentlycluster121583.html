innocently

adverb
in an innocent or harmless way
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəsəntli / (AmE), / ˈɪnəsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no guilt, malice, or knowledge of wrongdoing; naively or harmlessly

Examples

To innocently believe
He innocently believed everything he was told.
She innocently admitted her mistake, not realizing its seriousness.
The child looked up innocently when asked about the broken vase.
He innocently believed the documents were genuine and signed them without hesitation.
She protested innocently that she knew nothing about the missing money.
They claimed to have acted innocently, with no intention of breaking any rules.
