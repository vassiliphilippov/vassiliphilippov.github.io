bludgeon

verb
strike with a heavy club
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdʒən / (AmE), / ˈblʌdʒən / (BrE)
to strike someone repeatedly with a heavy object or club

Examples

To bludgeon a victim
He bludgeoned his way through the crowd with a large stick.
The criminal tried to bludgeon the guard during the escape.
The suspect was accused of bludgeoning the victim with a heavy object.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beat - strike repeatedly
  • synonym: club - to hit with a heavy stick
