Prince-of-Wales plume

noun
New Zealand fern with woolly stalks
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌprɪns əv ˈweɪlz pluːm / (AmE), / ˌprɪns əv ˈweɪlz pluːm / (BrE)
A New Zealand fern with pinnate fronds and densely woolly stalks, sometimes classified in the genus Todea.
