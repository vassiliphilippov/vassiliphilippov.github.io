climber

noun
iron spike for ice traction on shoes
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmər / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
an iron spike attached to a shoe to prevent slipping on ice when walking or climbing

Examples

Ice climber spikes
Before scaling the frozen waterfall, she secured her climbers to her boots.
The old mountaineer showed me how to attach climbers properly for safer travel on the ice.
Without climbers, hikers risk serious falls on the icy mountain paths.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crampon - a metal plate with spikes, attached to footwear for ice-climbing
