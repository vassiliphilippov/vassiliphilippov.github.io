coda

noun
final section of a musical piece
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.də / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.də / (BrE)
the concluding passage of a piece of music, typically bringing it to an end after the main part has been completed

Examples

Musical coda passage
