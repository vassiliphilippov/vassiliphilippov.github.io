lostness

noun
state of being lost
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔstnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɒstnəs / (BrE)
the state or feeling of being lost, confused, or lacking direction (emotionally, spiritually, or generally)

Examples

A feeling of lostness
After losing his job, he felt a deep sense of lostness and uncertainty about the future.
The novel explores the lostness of its main character as she searches for meaning in her life.
Many people experience lostness during major life transitions or personal crises.
After losing his job, he experienced a deep sense of lostness and uncertainty.
The novel explores the lostness that many young adults feel after graduation.
She tried to hide her lostness when she moved to a new country alone.
