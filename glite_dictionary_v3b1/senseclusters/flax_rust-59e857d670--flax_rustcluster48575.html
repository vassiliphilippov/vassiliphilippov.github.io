flax rust

noun
fungal disease affecting flax plants
Pronunciation: / flæks rʌst / (AmE), / flæks rʌst / (BrE)
a fungal disease caused by the fungus Melampsora lini that affects flax plants, producing orange-brown pustules on leaves and stems

Examples

Flax rust disease
Farmers monitor their fields closely to catch the early signs of flax rust.
Many flax crops were destroyed by flax rust this year.
Resistant varieties are being developed to combat flax rust in commercial agriculture.
