nitrogen oxide

noun
group of nitrogen-oxygen chemical compounds
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪ.tɹə.dʒən ˈɑk.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈnaɪ.tɹɪ.dʒən ˈɒk.saɪd / (BrE)
any of several chemical compounds consisting of nitrogen and oxygen, especially those found in car emissions and air pollution

Examples

Nitrogen oxide gases
Exposure to nitrogen oxide gases can cause respiratory problems.
Strict regulations have helped reduce nitrogen oxide levels in urban areas.
Vehicle engines emit large amounts of nitrogen oxide into the atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nitric oxide - a colorless gas, NO, important as a signaling molecule and pollutant
  • synonym: nitrogen dioxide - a reddish-brown gas, NO2, a significant air pollutant
