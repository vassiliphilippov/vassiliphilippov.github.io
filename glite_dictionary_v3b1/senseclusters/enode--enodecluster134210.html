enode

noun
LTE base station
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌnoʊd / (AmE), / ˈiːˌnəʊd / (BrE)
a type of LTE cellular base station (Evolved Node B) that provides radio access in mobile networks

Examples

LTE enode station
The technician visited the rural enode to perform routine maintenance on the equipment.
Upgrading the enode improved 4G coverage for thousands of mobile users in the area.
Each enode connects to the core network to manage wireless communication with mobile devices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: base station - a fixed point of communication for mobile networks
  • synonym: cell tower - a structure that transmits and receives cellular signals
