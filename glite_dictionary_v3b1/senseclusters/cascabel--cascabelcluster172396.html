cascabel

noun
round Mexican chili pepper
7.9
Pronunciation: /kæ.skə.ˈbel/ (AmE), /kæ.skə.ˈbel/ (BrE)
a small, round Mexican chili pepper (Capsicum annuum), usually sold dried; mild to medium in heat and used whole or ground in Mexican cooking.

Examples

Dried cascabel chili
She added a handful of dried cascabel to the simmering sauce for a smoky, mild heat.
The market sold whole cascabel with shiny red skin and a distinctive rattle when shaken.
For a quick chili, she ground dried cascabel into a coarse powder and stirred it in.
