supratrochlear vein

noun
a vein in the forehead draining scalp blood
9.3
Pronunciation: / suː.prəˌtroʊˈklɪr veɪn / (AmE), / suː.prəˌtrɒˈklɪə veɪn / (BrE)
a small vein that drains the front of the scalp and joins with the supraorbital vein to form the angular vein

Examples

The supratrochlear vein
