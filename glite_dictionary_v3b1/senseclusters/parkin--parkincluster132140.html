parkin

noun
spicy oat cake
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːrkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪn / (BrE)
a moist, spicy cake made with oatmeal and treacle, traditionally eaten in northern England, especially on Bonfire Night

Examples

A parkin cake
Every November, families in Yorkshire enjoy homemade parkin as part of their Bonfire Night celebrations.
The bakery sells traditional parkin with a rich, sticky texture and spicy flavor.
She brought a tray of parkin to share at the autumn festival.
