reacclimate

verb
to adjust again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈækləˌmeɪt /; / ˌriːəˈklɪmeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈæklɪmeɪt /; / ˌriːəˈklɪmeɪt / (BrE)
to become used to a climate, environment, or situation again after being away

Examples

To reacclimate to home
