balance

verb
make credits and debits equal in accounts
1.4 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbæl.əns/ (AmE), /ˈbæl.əns/ (BrE)

Examples

To balance the account

It can be stressful to balance multiple business accounts during tax season.
The accountant must balance the books at the end of every month.
