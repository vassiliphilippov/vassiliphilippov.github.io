multiplex

noun
cinema with multiple auditoriums
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌl.tiˌplɛks / (AmE), / ˈmʌl.tɪ.pleks / (BrE)
a cinema complex with several separate auditoriums within one building, allowing multiple films to be shown simultaneously

Examples

A multiplex cinema
It's convenient having a multiplex near my house, so I can always find something to watch.
The multiplex offers a wide selection of films every night of the week.
We decided to watch the new action movie at the multiplex downtown.
