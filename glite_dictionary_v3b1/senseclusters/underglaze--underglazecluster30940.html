underglaze

verb
to decorate before glazing
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
to apply colored decoration to pottery before the final glaze and firing process

Examples

To underglaze a plate
The artist chose to underglaze the vase with blue patterns before applying the final clear coat.
Students learned how to underglaze their pottery pieces in the ceramics class.
She carefully underglazed the bowl, making sure the colors would show through the glaze.
