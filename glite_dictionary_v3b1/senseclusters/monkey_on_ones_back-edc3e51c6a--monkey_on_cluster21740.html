monkey on one's back

noun
persistent burden or problem
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋki ɑn wʌnz bæk / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋki ɒn wʌnz bæk / (BrE)
a persistent burden, problem, or source of distress that is difficult to get rid of

Examples

The monkey on his back problem
After years of struggling with addiction, he finally got the monkey on his back off for good.
Student loan debt can feel like a monkey on your back long after graduation.
The constant fear of unemployment was a monkey on her back she couldn't shake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millstone around one's neck - an idiom for a heavy and inescapable burden
