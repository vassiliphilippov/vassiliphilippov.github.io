insert

noun
object placed inside something else
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɝːt / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːt / (BrE)
an object or piece that is placed inside something else, often to add, protect, or provide information

Examples

A insert in a box
Each box contained a printed insert explaining the instructions.
Please remove the plastic insert before using the oven.
The magazine included a colorful insert with discount coupons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supplement - an additional item added to enhance or complete something
