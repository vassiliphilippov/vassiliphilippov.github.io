caid

noun
local leader or judge
Pronunciation: / kaɪd / (AmE), / kaɪd / (BrE)
a local leader, chief, or judge in some North African or Middle Eastern countries

Examples

Village caid
The villagers brought their dispute before the caid for a fair decision.
As the caid, he was respected for his wisdom and leadership.
The caid presided over important ceremonies and resolved conflicts in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: judge - a person who decides legal cases
  • synonym: sheikh - a leader or elder in an Arab community
