nuttily

adverb
in a mildly eccentric way
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtəli / (AmE), / ˈnʌtɪli / (BrE)
in a mildly crazy or eccentric manner

Examples

To nuttily behave
He danced nuttily around the kitchen, singing at the top of his voice.
Her nuttily creative approach brought a fresh perspective to the team.
She laughed nuttily at his bizarre suggestion, making everyone else uncomfortable.
