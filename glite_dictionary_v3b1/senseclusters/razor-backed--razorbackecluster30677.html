razor-backed

adjective
having a sharp, narrow back
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪzərˌbækt / (AmE), / ˈreɪzəˌbækt / (BrE)
having a sharp, narrow, or prominent back or ridge, resembling the edge of a razor.

Examples

A razor-backed hog
A group of razor-backed hogs foraged in the field near the woods.
The mountain ridge appeared razor-backed against the orange glow of the setting sun.
The rider struggled to sit comfortably on the razor-backed horse as it trotted down the path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: razorback - a wild pig with a narrow back
