chatbot

noun
conversational computer program
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃæt.bɑːt/ (AmE), /ˈtʃæt.bɒt/ (BrE)
a computer program that interacts with people in online chats, either by simulating conversation or performing automated tasks like answering questions or moderating

Examples

A chatbot program
The company uses a chatbot to answer customer questions on its website 24 hours a day.
I chatted with a helpful chatbot that guided me through the registration process.
Many people prefer speaking to a real person instead of a chatbot when they need support.
The chatbot automatically removed spam messages from the group chat during the night.
Our team added a chatbot to the forum to answer common questions and keep discussions organized.
A simple chatbot can greet new users and provide helpful links in online communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bot - an automated program that performs tasks online
  • antonym: human agent - a real person who communicates and assists customers online
  • synonym: virtual assistant - a software agent that can perform tasks or services for an individual
