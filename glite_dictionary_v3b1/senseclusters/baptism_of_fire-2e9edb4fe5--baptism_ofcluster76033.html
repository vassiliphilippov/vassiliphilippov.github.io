baptism of fire

noun
difficult or challenging initiation
Pronunciation: / ˈbæp.tɪ.zəm əv ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbæp.tɪ.zəm əv ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
an initial experience of something, typically difficult or challenging, that tests someone's abilities or courage

Examples

A baptism of fire experience
Her first day as a manager was a true baptism of fire.
Moving abroad alone was his baptism of fire into independent living.
The new recruit had a baptism of fire during the busy holiday season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trial by fire - a test of one's abilities under pressure
