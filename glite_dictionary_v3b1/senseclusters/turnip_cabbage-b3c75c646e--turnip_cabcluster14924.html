turnip cabbage

noun
bulbous-rooted cruciferous vegetable
7.5 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝnɪp ˈkæbɪdʒ /; / ˈtɝː.nɪp ˈkæb.ɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnɪp ˈkæbɪdʒ /; / ˈtɜː.nɪp ˈkæb.ɪdʒ / (BrE)
