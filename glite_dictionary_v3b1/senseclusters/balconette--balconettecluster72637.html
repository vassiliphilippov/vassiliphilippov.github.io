balconette

noun
small decorative balcony
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌbælkəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌbælkəˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small decorative balcony or railing, usually outside a window or door, not meant for standing on

Examples

Iron balconette
The old building featured a charming balconette with wrought iron railings above the entrance.
She placed flower pots on the balconette outside her bedroom window to add some color.
From the street, the elegant balconette gave the apartment a touch of Parisian style.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balcony - a platform projecting from a wall, usually with space to stand on
  • synonym: parapet - a low protective wall along the edge of a roof or balcony
