brewpub

noun
restaurant brewing its own beer
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːˌpʌb / (AmE), / ˈbruːˌpʌb / (BrE)
A restaurant that brews its own beer on the premises and serves it along with food.

Examples

A brewpub restaurant
Many brewpubs feature special menus that pair their beers with food.
The new brewpub downtown offers a selection of craft beers brewed on site.
We spent Saturday evening tasting different ales at our favorite local brewpub.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brewery - a place where beer is made commercially
  • synonym: pub - an establishment licensed to sell alcoholic drinks, especially beer
