daily variation

noun
change occurring from day to day
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪli ˌvɛriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪli ˌveəriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the fluctuation or change in a quantity or condition from one day to the next

Examples

Daily variation in rainfall
Farmers need to account for daily variation in rainfall when planning irrigation schedules.
Scientists monitor the daily variation in air pollution levels to assess environmental health risks.
The daily variation in temperature can be quite dramatic in desert regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluctuation - an irregular rising and falling in number or amount; a variation
  • antonym: stability - the quality of being steady and unchanging
