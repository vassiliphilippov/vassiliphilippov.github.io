wall

noun
social‑media page for posts
3.4
Pronunciation: /wɔːl/ (AmE), /wɔːl/ (BrE)
A page or space on a social networking site where a user's posts, messages, photos, and comments appear and can be viewed or replied to by others.

Examples

Facebook wall
She posted a birthday message on her wall to celebrate online.
Fans covered his wall with supportive comments after the victory.
The organizer pinned the event details to the group's wall for all members to see.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profile - a user's personal page on a social network that often includes their posts and biographical information
  • synonym: timeline - a chronological list of a user's posts and activities on social media
