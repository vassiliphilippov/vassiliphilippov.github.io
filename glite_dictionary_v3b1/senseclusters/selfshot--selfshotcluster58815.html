selfshot

adjective
recorded by the performers themselves (video)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlfˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈsɛlfˌʃɒt / (BrE)
(of a video) recorded by the performers themselves, without the aid of an external camera operator.

Examples

Selfshot video clip
Many social media influencers prefer uploading selfshot videos to maintain authenticity with their audience.
The documentary included several selfshot interviews from participants during quarantine.
The movie's opening scene was a selfshot sequence filmed on a smartphone.

Related Terms
