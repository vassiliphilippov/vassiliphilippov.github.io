backgammon

noun
board game with dice and pieces
9.7 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌɡæm.ən /; / ˈbækˌɡæmən / (AmE), / ˈbæk.ɡæm.ən /; / ˈbakˌɡamən / (BrE)
a board game for two players in which each player moves pieces around the board according to the roll of dice, aiming to remove all their pieces from the board first
