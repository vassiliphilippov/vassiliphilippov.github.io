write-up

noun
a short article or report
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈraɪt.ʌp / (BrE)
a short article or report, especially one published in a newspaper or magazine, that gives an account of something

Examples

A write-up article
Did you see the write-up about the concert in last week's magazine?
Her performance got a brief write-up in the school newsletter.
The restaurant received a glowing write-up in the local newspaper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: report - an account or statement describing in detail an event, situation, or the like
  • synonym: review - a critical article or report, especially of a book, play, or performance
