Gidget

proper noun
fictional surfer girl
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɪdʒɪt / (BrE)
the name of a fictional teenage surfer girl in American books, films, and TV, and also used for a young female surfer, especially an enthusiastic or new one
