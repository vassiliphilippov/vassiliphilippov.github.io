crutch

noun
support or expedient relied on
9.9
Pronunciation: / krʌtʃ / (AmE), / krʌtʃ / (BrE)
something that provides support or helps someone deal with a difficult situation, often used metaphorically for a thing relied on to cope with hardship or stress.

Examples

An emotional crutch
For many people, television can become an emotional crutch during stressful times.
Relying on routine too much may serve as a comfortable crutch, but can hinder personal growth.
She used her busy schedule as a crutch to avoid dealing with her problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prop - an object that supports or steadies
  • synonym: support - something that serves as a foundation or aid
