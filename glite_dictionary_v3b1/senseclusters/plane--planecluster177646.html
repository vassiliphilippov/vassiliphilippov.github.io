plane

adjective
completely flat, level (surface)
Pronunciation: / pleɪn / (AmE), / pleɪn / (BrE)
(of a surface) having no slope, tilt, or irregularities; completely flat and level.

Examples

Plane surface
After the ground was graded, it formed a plane expanse ideal for construction.
The artist required a plane sheet of paper for her detailed drawing.
The carpenter checked that the tabletop was perfectly plane before assembling the legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flat - (of a surface) having no height or unevenness; smooth and even
  • synonym: level - having a flat or even surface without slope
