odometer

noun
instrument measuring distance travelled
Pronunciation: / oʊˈdɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / əʊˈdɒmɪtə / (BrE)
An instrument for measuring the distance travelled by a vehicle.

Examples

Car odometer
After ten years, the old truck's odometer finally reached 200,000 miles.
Resetting the odometer before a race is a common practice among rally drivers.
The car's odometer showed that we had driven 500 miles since our trip began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speedometer - an instrument measuring a vehicle's speed
