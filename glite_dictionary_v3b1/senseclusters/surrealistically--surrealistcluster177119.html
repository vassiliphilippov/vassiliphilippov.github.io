surrealistically

adverb
in a dreamlike way
7.8
Pronunciation: / səˌriːəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / səˌrɪəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is strange, dreamlike, or resembles surrealism

Examples

To act surrealistically
The story unfolds surrealistically, with talking animals and floating cities.
The artist painted the landscape surrealistically, making the trees appear to melt.
Her memories of the night seemed surrealistically distorted and dreamlike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bizarrely - in a very strange or unusual way
  • antonym: realistically - in a way that is true to life or reality
