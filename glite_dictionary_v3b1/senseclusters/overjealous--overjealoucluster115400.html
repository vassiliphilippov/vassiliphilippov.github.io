overjealous

adjective
excessively jealous or suspicious
3.4
Pronunciation: / oʊ.vɚˈdʒɛl.əs / (AmE), / əʊ.vəˈdʒɛl.əs / (BrE)
Excessively suspicious or fearful of losing someone's affection to a rival; showing extreme jealousy.

Examples

An overjealous partner
Friends advised him not to be so overjealous about minor things.
His overjealous attitude often created unnecessary tension in their relationship.
She became overjealous whenever her partner spoke to someone else.
