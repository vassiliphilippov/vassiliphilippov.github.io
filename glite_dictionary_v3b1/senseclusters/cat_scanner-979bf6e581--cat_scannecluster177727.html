A machine that creates detailed three-dimensional images of the inside of the body by combining X-ray pictures taken from different angles.

Examples

A CAT scanner machine
Modern hospitals are equipped with at least one CAT scanner for advanced diagnostics.
Technicians carefully calibrated the CAT scanner before the first patient of the day arrived.

Synonyms

  • computerized axial tomography scanner
    6.0 0.00/m
    computerized axial tomography scanner is the long, formal name used in hospitals and reports, while CAT scanner is the common short name.
  • tomograph
    8.3 0.03/m
    tomograph is a machine that images a single slice or plane, while CAT scanner builds full three-dimensional images from many X-ray angles.
