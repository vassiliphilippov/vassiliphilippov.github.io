flasher

noun
person who exposes genitals in public
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃər / (AmE), / ˈflæʃə / (BrE)
a person, usually a man, who exposes their genitals in public, often for sexual excitement

Examples

A park flasher

The police arrested the flasher after multiple complaints from people in the park.
