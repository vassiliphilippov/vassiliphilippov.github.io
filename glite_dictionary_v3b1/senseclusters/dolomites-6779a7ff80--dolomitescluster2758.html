Dolomites

proper noun
mountain range in northern Italy
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊ.ləˌmaɪts / (AmE), / ˈdɒl.əˌmaɪts / (BrE)
a mountain range in northeastern Italy that forms part of the Alps, noted for its distinctive pale-colored dolomitic limestone peaks
