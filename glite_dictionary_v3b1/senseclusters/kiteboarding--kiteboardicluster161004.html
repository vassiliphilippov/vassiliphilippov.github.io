kiteboarding

noun
sport with kite and board
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪtˌbɔrdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkaɪtbɔːdɪŋ /; / ˈkaɪtˌbɔːdɪŋ / (BrE)
