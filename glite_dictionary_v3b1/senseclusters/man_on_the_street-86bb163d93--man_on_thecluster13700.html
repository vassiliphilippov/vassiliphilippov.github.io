man on the street

noun
typical member of the general public
3.5
Pronunciation: / mæn ɑn ðə strit / (AmE), / mæn ɒn ðə striːt / (BrE)
an ordinary member of the general public; a typical or average person

Examples

An average man on the street
As a man on the street, he rarely paid attention to political debates.
Pollsters often seek the opinions of the man on the street to gauge public sentiment.
The news program interviewed the man on the street to get public reactions to the election.
