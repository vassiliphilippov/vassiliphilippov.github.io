drupelet

noun
small part of an aggregate fruit; tiny drupe
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdruːplət / (AmE), / ˈdruːplɪt / (BrE)
a small section of an aggregate fruit, such as a raspberry or blackberry, that resembles a tiny drupe

Examples

A drupelet on raspberry
A single drupelet can fall off if the fruit is handled too roughly.
Each drupelet on a raspberry develops from an individual ovary in the flower.
The blackberry's surface is made up of many shiny drupelets clustered together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drupe - a fleshy fruit with thin skin and a central stone containing the seed
