visually challenged

adjective
having reduced vision; visually impaired
6.0 4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəli ˈtʃælɪndʒd / (BrE)
Having greatly reduced or impaired vision; partially or completely lacking the ability to see well.

Examples

Visually challenged person
As a visually challenged student, she relied on braille textbooks and audio recordings for her studies.
The city installed special crosswalk signals to help visually challenged pedestrians cross safely.
The museum offers tours designed for visually challenged visitors to enhance their experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visually impaired - having partial loss of vision; not completely blind
