shraddha

noun
devout religious faith
Pronunciation: / ʃrʌˈdɑː / (AmE), / ʃrʌˈdɑː / (BrE)
deep religious faith, devotion, or reverent trust, especially in Hindu and Buddhist traditions

Examples

Strong shraddha in God
Her shraddha inspired her to visit the temple every morning before work.
The ceremony was performed with great shraddha and respect for tradition.
He approached his studies of scripture with deep shraddha and humility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: devotion - strong religious feeling or commitment
  • antonym: doubt - a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction
  • synonym: faith - complete trust or confidence in someone or something
