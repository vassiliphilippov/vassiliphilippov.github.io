spritsail

noun
a sail extended by a diagonal spar
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪt.seɪl / (AmE), / ˈsprɪt.seɪl / (BrE)
A fore-and-aft sail extended by a sprit (a spar that extends diagonally from the mast).

Examples

A spritsail sail
Historians noted that the spritsail was popular among small working vessels in the 18th century.
Sailors adjusted the spritsail to catch the changing wind.
The old fishing boat still used a spritsail for maneuvering in the shallow harbor.
