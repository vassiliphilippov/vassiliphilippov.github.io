wing nut

noun
tree (Pterocarya genus) with winged nut fruits
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /wɪŋ nʌt/ (AmE), /wɪŋ nʌt/ (BrE)
A tree of the genus Pterocarya, native to the Caucasus and southeastern Asia, bearing small winged nutlets as fruit.
