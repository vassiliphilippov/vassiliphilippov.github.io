wing nut

noun
tree (Pterocarya genus) with winged nut fruits
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ nʌt / (AmE), / wɪŋ nʌt / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Pterocarya, native to the Caucasus and southeastern Asia, bearing small winged nutlets as fruit.

Examples

Wing nut tree
A mature wing nut can provide dense shade in gardens and parks.
Botanists studied the distinctive seeds produced by the wing nut tree.
The wing nut thrives near riverbanks in southeastern Asia.
