Polish fire drill

noun
disorganized emergency practice
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.lɪʃ ˈfaɪər drɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɒ.lɪʃ ˈfaɪə drɪl / (BrE)
an emergency practice drill or situation that appears confusing or disorganized, often implying poor coordination

Examples

A Polish fire drill practice
After the alarm went off, the whole evacuation turned into a Polish fire drill.
Our team meeting yesterday felt like a Polish fire drill—no one knew what to do next.
Whenever management announces a new procedure without planning, it becomes a Polish fire drill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Chinese fire drill - an activity or situation that is confusing and chaotic
