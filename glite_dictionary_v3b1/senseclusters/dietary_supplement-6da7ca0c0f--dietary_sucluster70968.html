dietary supplement

noun
product adding nutrients to diet
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəˌtɛri ˈsʌpləmənt / (AmE), / ˈdaɪətəri ˈsʌplɪmənt / (BrE)
a product taken orally that contains nutrients intended to add to the diet or make up for a deficiency

Examples

A dietary supplement pill
Always check with your doctor before adding any dietary supplement to your regular routine.
He started taking a dietary supplement to make sure he got enough vitamins each day.
Some athletes rely on a dietary supplement to improve their performance and speed up recovery.
