Alopecurus pratensis

noun
a perennial pasture grass (meadow foxtail)
Pronunciation: / ˌæləˈpiːkjʊrəs prəˈtɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaləˈpiːkjʊrəs praˈtɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A robust perennial grass native to northern regions of Europe and Asia, with silky flower spikes; widely cultivated for pasture and hay and naturalized in North America.
