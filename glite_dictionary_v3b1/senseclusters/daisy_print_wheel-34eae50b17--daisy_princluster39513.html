daisy print wheel

noun
printing wheel with character spokes
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪzi prɪnt wiːl / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzi prɪnt wiːl / (BrE)
a circular printing component with typefaces on spokes, used in typewriters or printers to form characters on paper

Examples

A daisy print wheel component
Early word processors often used a daisy print wheel to produce typewritten text.
Replacing the daisy print wheel can significantly improve the quality of the printouts.
The printer stopped working because the daisy print wheel was out of alignment.
