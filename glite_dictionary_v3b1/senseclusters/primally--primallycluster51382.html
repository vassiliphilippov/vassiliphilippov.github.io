primally

adverb
in an instinctive way
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpraɪ.mə.liː/ /; / /ˈprɪ.mə.liː/ (AmE), /ˈpraɪ.mə.liː/ /; / /ˈprɪ.mə.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
