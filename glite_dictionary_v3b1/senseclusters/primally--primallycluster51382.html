primally

adverb
in an instinctive way
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪməli / (AmE), / ˈpraɪməli / (BrE)
in a way that is primitive, instinctive, or driven by basic natural impulses rather than reasoned thought

Examples

React primally
He reacted primally to the sudden danger, without stopping to think.
The music stirred something primally deep within the audience.
When threatened, animals often respond primally, relying on their instincts to survive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instinctively - in a way that is based on instinct rather than thought
  • synonym: primitively - in a way that is basic or not developed
  • antonym: rationally - in a way that is based on reason or logic
