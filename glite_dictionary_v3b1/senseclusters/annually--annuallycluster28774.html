Synonyms

  • yearly
    1.0 7.85/m
    yearly means happening once every year in everyday speech, while annually is the more formal adverb often used in writing.
  • each year
    1.0 28/m
    each year describes events that happen every year in a simple way, while annually sounds slightly more formal and calendar-based.
  • per year
    1.0 21/m
    per year is used for amounts or rates per twelve months, while annually is a general adverb for things happening once a year.
  • per annum
    4.2 0.95/m
    per annum is a formal, often financial phrase for each year, while annually is common and fits many general contexts.
  • p.a.
    4.1 0.00/m
    p.a. is a short abbreviation seen in finance and contracts, while annually is the clear full adverb used in regular text.
