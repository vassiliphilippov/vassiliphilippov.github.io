cramp

noun
painful involuntary muscle contraction
Pronunciation: / kræmp / (AmE), / kramp / (BrE)
a sudden, painful, and involuntary contraction of a muscle or group of muscles

Examples

Leg cramp
He stopped running because he got a terrible cramp in his leg.
She massaged her foot to relieve the cramp.
Swimming after eating can sometimes give you a cramp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spasm - a sudden involuntary muscular contraction
