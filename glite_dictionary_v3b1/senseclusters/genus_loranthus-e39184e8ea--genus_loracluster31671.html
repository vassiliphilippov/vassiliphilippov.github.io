genus Loranthus

noun
type genus of the Loranthaceae family
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs loʊˈrænθəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ləˈranθəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the Loranthaceae family, containing a single species of parasitic plants known as Loranthus.
