icing

noun
sweet topping for cakes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sweet, flavored mixture made primarily of sugar, used to coat and decorate cakes and other baked goods.

Examples

Cake icing
Add more sugar to the icing if you want it to be sweeter.
She spread a thick layer of icing over the chocolate cake.
The cookies were decorated with colorful icing and sprinkles.
