William Tecumseh Sherman

proper noun
US Civil War general, led March to the Sea
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪl.jəm tə.ˈkʌm.sə ˈʃɜːr.mən/ (AmE), /ˈwɪl.jəm tə.ˈkʌm.sə ˈʃɜː.mən/ (BrE)
(1820–1891) United States general who was commander of all Union troops in the Western Theater during the Civil War; known for capturing Atlanta and his destructive 'March to the Sea' that split the Confederacy.
