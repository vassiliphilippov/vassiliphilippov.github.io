gourmet

adjective
high-quality food
Pronunciation: / ɡʊrˈmeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡʊəmeɪ / (BrE)
of very high quality and made with special skill, especially referring to food or drink

Examples

Gourmet chocolate
The restaurant is famous for its gourmet dishes prepared by top chefs.
She enjoys shopping at stores that offer a wide selection of gourmet ingredients.
They served a gourmet breakfast with freshly baked pastries and exotic fruits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deluxe - of special elegance, sumptuousness, or fineness
  • synonym: luxurious - very comfortable and expensive; giving great pleasure
  • antonym: ordinary - not special or different; normal
