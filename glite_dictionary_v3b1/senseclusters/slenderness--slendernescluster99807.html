slenderness

noun
quality of being insufficient or slight
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛndɚnəs / (AmE), / ˈslɛndənɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being slight, insufficient, or inadequate

Examples

The slenderness of the evidence
Given the slenderness of my resources, I had to decline the trip.
The slenderness of the evidence was not enough to convict him.
We were worried about the slenderness of our chances of success.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abundance - a large quantity, more than enough
  • synonym: insufficiency - the quality of being not enough
