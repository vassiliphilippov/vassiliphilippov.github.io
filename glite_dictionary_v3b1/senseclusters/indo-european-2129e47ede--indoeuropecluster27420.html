Indo-European

noun
family of related European and Asian languages
6.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.doʊ.jʊr.ˈpiː.ən/ (AmE), /ɪn.dəʊ.jʊə.rə.ˈpiː.ən/ (BrE)
a family of languages that were spoken throughout most of Europe and parts of Asia by 1000 BC, and include most of the major languages of Europe and western Asia

Examples
