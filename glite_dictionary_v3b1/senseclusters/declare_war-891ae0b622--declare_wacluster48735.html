declare war

verb
formally announce a state of war
Pronunciation: / dɪˈklɛr wɔr / (AmE), / dɪˈkleə wɔː / (BrE)
to make a formal announcement that one country is entering into a state of war against another

Examples

To declare war officially
After months of tension, they finally declared war on their neighbor.
No country should declare war lightly, as the consequences are severe.
The government may declare war in response to a direct attack.

Related Terms

  • antonym: make peace - to formally agree to end a state of war or conflict
