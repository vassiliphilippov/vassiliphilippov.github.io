hemoperitoneum

noun
blood in abdominal cavity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm / (BrE)
the presence of blood in the abdominal cavity, often caused by injury or disease

Examples

Hemoperitoneum after injury
The doctor diagnosed a hemoperitoneum after the patient complained of severe abdominal pain.
Emergency surgery was required to stop the bleeding that caused the hemoperitoneum.
A ruptured organ can lead to a dangerous hemoperitoneum if not treated quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemorrhage - excessive or uncontrolled bleeding
  • antonym: peritonitis - inflammation of the peritoneum
