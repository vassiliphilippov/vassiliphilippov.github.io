Trasimeno

proper noun
battle between Hannibal and Romans, 217 BC
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɑːsɪˈmeɪnoʊ / (AmE), / ˌtrasɪˈmeɪnəʊ / (BrE)
a battle in central Italy in 217 BC where Hannibal defeated the Romans under Flaminius, known as the Battle of Lake Trasimeno.

Examples

Battle of Trasimeno
Many historians have studied the tactics used at Trasimeno to understand Hannibal's genius.
The Battle of Trasimeno marked one of Hannibal's most significant victories over Rome.
The defeat at Trasimeno was a severe blow to Roman morale during the Second Punic War.
