Lamnidae

noun
family of oceanic sharks
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.nɪˌdi / (AmE), / ˈlam.nɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of large, fast-swimming oceanic sharks known for their pointed snouts and robust bodies, including the great white shark and mako sharks.
