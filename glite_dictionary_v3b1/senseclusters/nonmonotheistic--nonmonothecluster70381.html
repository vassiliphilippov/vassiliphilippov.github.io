nonmonotheistic

adjective
not monotheistic
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnmɑːnoʊθiˈɪstɪk /; / ˌnɑnmɑːnəʊθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnmɒnəʊθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
not relating to or characterized by belief in only one god; involving belief in more than one god
