bastardly

adjective
illegitimate; born out of wedlock
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæstərdli / (AmE), / ˈbɑːstədli / (BrE)
(of a child) born to parents not married to each other; illegitimate.

Examples

Bastardly child
In historical records, a bastardly birth could affect inheritance rights.
Many bastardly children faced discrimination in medieval society.
The king refused to acknowledge his bastardly heirs as legitimate successors.
