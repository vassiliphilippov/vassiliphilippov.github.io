unguided

adjective
without supervision
4.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡaɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡaɪdɪd / (BrE)
not given help, direction, or supervision; done without guidance

Examples

Unguided tour
They preferred an unguided hike so they could explore the forest on their own.
The museum offers both guided and unguided tours for visitors.
He completed the project in an unguided manner, relying only on his own ideas.

Related Terms
