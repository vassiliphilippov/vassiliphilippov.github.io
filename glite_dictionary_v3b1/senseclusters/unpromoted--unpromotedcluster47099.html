unpromoted

adjective
not advanced or advertised
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈmoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈməʊtɪd / (BrE)
not having been advanced in rank, position, or status, or not having been advertised or publicized

Examples

An unpromoted employee
Despite his excellent performance, he remained unpromoted for several years.
The new product was left unpromoted and failed to attract customers.
Many unpromoted employees feel discouraged when their efforts go unnoticed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overlooked - not noticed or given attention
  • antonym: promoted - having been advanced in rank or position
