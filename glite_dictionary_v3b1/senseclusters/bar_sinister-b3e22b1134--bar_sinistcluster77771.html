bar sinister

noun
heraldic mark denoting illegitimacy
Pronunciation: / bɑːr ˈsɪnɪstər /; / bɑɹ ˈsɪnəstɚ / (AmE), / bɑː ˈsɪnɪstə / (BrE)
a diagonal band on a shield in heraldry, traditionally believed to indicate illegitimacy
