unfulfillment

noun
lack of satisfaction
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnfʊlˈfɪlmənt / (AmE), / ˌʌnfʊlˈfɪlmənt / (BrE)
the state of not achieving satisfaction, purpose, or completion in something important

Examples

Feelings of unfulfillment
After years in a job he disliked, he struggled with a deep sense of unfulfillment.
Many people experience unfulfillment when their goals remain out of reach.
She tried new hobbies to overcome the feeling of unfulfillment in her daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissatisfaction - the feeling of not being pleased or content
  • antonym: fulfillment - the achievement of satisfaction or completion
