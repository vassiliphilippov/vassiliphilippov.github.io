Pellicularia

noun
a genus of crust-forming fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛlɪkjəˈlɛriə / (AmE), / ˌpɛlɪkjʊˈlɑːrɪə / (BrE)
a genus of fungi with a crust-like hymenium; some species were previously classified as Rhizoctinia
