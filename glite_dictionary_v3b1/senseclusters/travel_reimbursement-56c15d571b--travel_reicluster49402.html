travel reimbursement

noun
repayment for travel expenses
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəl ˌriːɪmˈbɝːsmənt / (AmE), / ˈtrævəl ˌriːɪmˈbɜːsmənt / (BrE)
an amount of money paid back to cover travel expenses, typically incurred for work-related or official purposes

Examples

Travel reimbursement claim
Be sure to submit your receipts if you want to receive travel reimbursement after your business trip.
My company offers travel reimbursement for employees who attend conferences out of town.
Their policy covers full travel reimbursement for approved work-related travel.
