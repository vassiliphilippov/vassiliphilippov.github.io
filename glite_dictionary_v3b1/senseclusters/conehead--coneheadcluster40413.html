conehead

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊnˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊnˌhɛd / (BrE)
an insulting informal term for a foolish or stupid person

Examples

Call him a conehead
He called his friend a conehead after she forgot her keys for the third time.
Stop acting like a conehead and pay attention in class.
They laughed and teased him, saying he was a real conehead for making that mistake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
  • synonym: idiot - a person who is very foolish or stupid
