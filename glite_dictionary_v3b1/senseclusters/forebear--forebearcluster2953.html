forebear

noun
ancestor; person you descend from
8.1 1.67/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îrÀåb…õr / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêÀåb…õ…ô / (BrE)
a person, especially one in the past, from whom you are directly descended; an ancestor.

Examples

His forebear fought in the Civil War, passing down stories through generations.

She felt a deep connection to her Scottish forebears while visiting the old family estate.
