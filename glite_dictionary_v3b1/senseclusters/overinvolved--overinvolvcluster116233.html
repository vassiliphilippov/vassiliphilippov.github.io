overinvolved

adjective
excessively engaged
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
too engaged or participating more than is appropriate or necessary in a situation or activity

Examples

Overinvolved parents
Some teachers can become overinvolved in their students' personal lives.
His overinvolved approach to managing the project frustrated his colleagues.
Parents who are overinvolved may unintentionally limit their children's independence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detached - not involved or emotionally engaged
  • synonym: overbearing - excessively controlling or domineering
