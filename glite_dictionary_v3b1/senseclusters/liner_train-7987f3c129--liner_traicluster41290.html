liner train

noun
fast freight train for rapid cargo transit
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnər treɪn / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnə treɪn / (BrE)
a fast, long-distance freight train operating between industrial centers and seaports, designed for rapid loading and unloading of goods

Examples

A liner train service
Each liner train was scheduled to depart hourly to ensure rapid delivery of cargo.
Thanks to its advanced loading mechanisms, the liner train can handle large volumes of freight efficiently.
The new liner train reduced shipping times between the factory and the seaport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: express train - a train that travels faster and with fewer stops than regular trains
  • synonym: freight train - a train used for transporting goods rather than passengers
