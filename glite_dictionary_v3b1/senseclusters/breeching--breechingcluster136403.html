breeching

noun
harness strap for animals
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a strap or part of a harness that passes around the rear of a horse or other animal to help control or slow a vehicle

Examples

Breeching strap on a horse
The breeching kept the wagon from sliding forward when the horse stopped suddenly.
Before setting off, the farmer checked the breeching to make sure it was secure around the horse.
Without proper breeching, the cart could push dangerously against the animal on steep hills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harness - a set of straps for controlling an animal
  • synonym: reins - straps used to guide or control a horse
