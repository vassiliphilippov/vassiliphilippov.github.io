banana

noun
tropical plant yielding edible fruit
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / bəˈnænə / (AmE), / bəˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
Any of several tropical and subtropical plants of the genus Musa, with large leaves and clusters of elongated edible fruit.

Examples

Banana plant leaves
Large banana leaves are often used to wrap food for cooking in Southeast Asia.
The banana is one of the world's most important fruit crops.
The banana plant grows quickly in warm, humid climates.
