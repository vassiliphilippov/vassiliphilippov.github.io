genderqueer

adjective
non-binary gender identity
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛndərˌkwɪr / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛndəˌkwɪə / (BrE)
describing a person whose gender identity does not fit traditional categories of male or female

Examples

Genderqueer identity
Taylor describes themselves as genderqueer and prefers to use they/them pronouns.
The organization provides support for genderqueer youth seeking a safe community.
Many genderqueer people challenge traditional ideas about gender roles and expression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cisgender - identifying with the gender assigned at birth
  • synonym: nonbinary - not identifying exclusively as male or female
