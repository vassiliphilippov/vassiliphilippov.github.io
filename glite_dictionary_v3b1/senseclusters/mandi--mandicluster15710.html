mandi

noun
Middle Eastern rice dish
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːndi / (AmE), / ˈmændi / (BrE)
a traditional Middle Eastern dish made of rice, meat, and spices, often cooked in a tandoor or underground oven

Examples

Chicken mandi
We ordered mandi at the new Yemeni restaurant downtown.
The chef prepared a delicious mandi using lamb and fragrant spices for the festival.
Many people enjoy sharing a large plate of mandi with family and friends during celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biryani - a South Asian mixed rice dish with spices and meat or vegetables
  • synonym: kabsa - a spiced rice and meat dish popular in the Arabian Peninsula
