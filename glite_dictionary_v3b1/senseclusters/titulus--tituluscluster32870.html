titulus

noun
legal ownership document
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtʃʊləs / (AmE), / ˈtɪtjʊləs / (BrE)
a legal document or claim that proves ownership of property, such as a deed or title

Examples

Property titulus
The lawyer requested the titulus before proceeding with the property transfer.
Without a valid titulus, the ownership of the land could be disputed.
He carefully reviewed the titulus to ensure there were no outstanding claims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deed - a legal document showing ownership of property
  • synonym: title - a legal right to own property
