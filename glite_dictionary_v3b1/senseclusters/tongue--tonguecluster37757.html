tongue

noun
a manner or style of speaking
3.2
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
a particular manner or style of speaking, especially as characteristic of a person

Examples

A sharp tongue
He lost his job because his sharp tongue offended too many people.
Her gentle tongue calmed everyone during the stressful meeting.
She is known for her tongue, which can be both witty and biting.
