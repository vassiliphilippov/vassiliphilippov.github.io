lardoon

verb
to insert fat into meat
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / l…ërÀàduÀên / (AmE), / l…ëÀêÀàduÀên / (BrE)
to insert strips of fat or bacon into meat before cooking to keep it moist and add flavor

Examples

To lardoon meat
Before roasting, the chef carefully lardooned the beef with strips of bacon.
Some traditional recipes require you to lardoon the meat for extra juiciness.
They decided to lardoon the chicken to keep it from drying out in the oven.
