putto

noun
cherubic art figure
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpʊtəʊ / (BrE)
a chubby child figure, often nude and sometimes winged, used as a decorative element in art, especially in Renaissance paintings and sculptures
