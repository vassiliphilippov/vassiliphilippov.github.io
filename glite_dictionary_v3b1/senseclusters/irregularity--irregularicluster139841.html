irregularity

noun
lack of regular pattern or order
2.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærəti /; / ɪˌrɛɡjəˈlærɪti / (AmE), / ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarəti /; / ɪˌrɛɡjʊˈlarɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of not being regular, even, or following a pattern; unevenness or lack of symmetry
