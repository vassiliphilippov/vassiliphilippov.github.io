soy flour

noun
fine meal from ground soybeans
Pronunciation: / sɔɪ ˈflaʊɚ / (AmE), / sɔɪ ˈflaʊə / (BrE)
a fine meal made by grinding soybeans, used as an ingredient in baking and cooking

Examples

Soy flour ingredient
Breads made with soy flour have a slightly nutty flavor and soft texture.
Many gluten-free recipes call for soy flour as a substitute for wheat flour.
She bought some soy flour to add protein to her baking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soybean - a legume widely cultivated for its edible seeds
