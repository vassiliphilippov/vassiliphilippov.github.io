belt

verb
fasten with a belt
Pronunciation: / bɛlt / (AmE), / bɛlt / (BrE)
To fasten or secure something, especially an article of clothing, with a belt.

Examples

To belt the pants
He always belts his jeans before leaving the house.
Remember to belt your dress to give it some shape.
