midrange

adjective
between high and low
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
having a value, quality, or position that is between high and low

Examples

Midrange price
We chose a midrange hotel for our vacation to balance comfort and cost.
The store offers midrange smartphones that are affordable but still have good features.
He prefers midrange restaurants that are neither too expensive nor too cheap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: average - typical or normal; not extreme
  • degree higher: high-end - having the highest value or quality in a range
  • degree lower: low-end - having the lowest value or quality in a range
  • synonym: moderate - not extreme; in the middle range
