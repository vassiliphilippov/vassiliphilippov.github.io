Sylvian aqueduct

noun
canal in the brain connecting ventricles
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪl.vi.ən ˈæ.kwəˌdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈsɪl.vi.ən ˈæk.wɪˌdʌkt / (BrE)
a narrow canal in the brain connecting the third and fourth ventricles, through which cerebrospinal fluid flows

Examples

The Sylvian aqueduct canal
An obstruction in the Sylvian aqueduct can lead to hydrocephalus.
MRI scans revealed a narrowing of the Sylvian aqueduct in the patient.
The Sylvian aqueduct is crucial for the circulation of cerebrospinal fluid in the brain.
