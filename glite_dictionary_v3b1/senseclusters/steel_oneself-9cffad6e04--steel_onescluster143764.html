steel oneself

verb
mentally prepare for something difficult
Pronunciation: / stil wʌnˈsɛlf / (AmE), / stiːl wʌnˈsɛlf / (BrE)
to prepare yourself mentally or emotionally to deal with something unpleasant or difficult

Examples

To steel oneself for news
Before entering the meeting, she steeled herself to hear any criticism.
He had to steel himself before breaking the bad news to his family.
You sometimes need to steel yourself for unexpected challenges in life.
