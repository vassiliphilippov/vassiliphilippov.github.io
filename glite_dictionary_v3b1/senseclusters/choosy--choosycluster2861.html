choosy

adjective
particular; hard to please
3.9 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt ÉuÀêzi / (AmE), / Ààt ÉuÀêzi / (BrE)
very particular or difficult to please, especially when making a choice.

Examples

Very choosy person
She is very choosy about the clothes she wears.
The children became choosy eaters and refused to try new foods.
You can't afford to be choosy when on a tight budget.
