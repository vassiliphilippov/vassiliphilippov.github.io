WordNet

proper noun
machine-readable lexical database of English
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrdˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈwɜːdˌnɛt /; / ˈwɜːd.nɛt / (BrE)
a large machine-readable database of English words and their meanings, developed at Princeton University and used in language research and technology
