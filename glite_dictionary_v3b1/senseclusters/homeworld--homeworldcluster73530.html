homeworld

noun
planet of origin
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌwɜrld / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌwɜːld / (BrE)
the planet or place where someone or something originally comes from, especially in science fiction

Examples

Alien homeworld
The hero dreamed of seeing his homeworld again after years of space travel.
Each alien species in the story had a unique homeworld with its own environment.
She collected artifacts from her homeworld to remind her of her origins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homeland - the country or region where someone was born or has roots
