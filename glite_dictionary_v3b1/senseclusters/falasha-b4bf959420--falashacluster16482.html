Falasha

noun
offensive term for Ethiopian Jews
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɑːʃə / (AmE), / fəˈlɑːʃə / (BrE)
an offensive term formerly used to refer to members of the Ethiopian Jewish community

Examples

The Falasha term
The term Falasha is now widely recognized as offensive and should not be used.

Historians note that Falasha was once used in official documents, but is now avoided.
