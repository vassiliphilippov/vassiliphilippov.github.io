nontourist

noun
local resident
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtʊrɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtʊərɪst / (BrE)
a person who is not a tourist, especially a local resident or someone not visiting for leisure

Examples

A nontourist resident
The small café is a favorite spot for nontourists who live in the neighborhood.
During the off-season, the city feels quieter and mostly filled with nontourists.
Some restaurants offer special discounts to nontourists as a way to attract local customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: local - a person who lives in a particular place
  • antonym: tourist - a person who travels for pleasure
