scup

noun
flesh of a northern Atlantic fish
Pronunciation: / skʌp / (AmE), / skʌp / (BrE)
the flesh of a fish (Stenotomus chrysops) found in the colder waters of the northern Atlantic coast of the United States, often eaten as food
