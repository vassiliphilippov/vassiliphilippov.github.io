bruja

noun
female witch
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːhɑː / (AmE), / ˈbruːhɑː / (BrE)
a witch, especially a woman believed to have magical powers in Spanish-speaking cultures

Examples

A magical bruja
The villagers believed the old woman was a bruja with magical powers.
In many stories, a bruja can cast spells and brew potions.
Children were warned not to wander into the forest where the bruja was said to live.

Related Terms

  • synonym: witch - a woman thought to have magical powers
  • antonym: wizard - a man thought to have magical powers
