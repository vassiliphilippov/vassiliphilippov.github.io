assistant professor

noun
university teacher below associate professor
1.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt prə.ˈfes.ər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt prə.ˈfes.ə/ (BrE)
