ugly stick

noun
humorous term for unattractive appearance
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli stɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli stɪk / (BrE)
a humorous term for something or someone that is very unattractive or unpleasant in appearance, often used in the phrase 'hit with the ugly stick'

Examples

Hit with the ugly stick
Don't be rude—nobody deserves to be called an ugly stick behind their back.
He said his old car looked like it had been beaten with an ugly stick.
Some people claim that the artist's new sculpture was hit with the ugly stick.
