brow ptosis

noun
sagging or drooping of the eyebrows
7.4
Pronunciation: / braʊ pˈtoʊsɪs / (AmE), / braʊ pˈtəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the eyebrows sag or droop, often due to aging or nerve damage

Examples

Brow ptosis condition
Patients with brow ptosis often report obstructed vision due to sagging eyebrows.
Surgical procedures may be necessary to correct brow ptosis in elderly patients.
The plastic surgeon specializes in treating conditions such as brow ptosis and eyelid drooping.
