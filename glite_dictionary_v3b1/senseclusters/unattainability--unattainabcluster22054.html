unattainability

noun
the quality of being impossible to reach or obtain
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˌteɪ.nəˈbɪl.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˌteɪ.nəˈbɪl.ə.ti / (BrE)
The state or quality of being impossible to reach or obtain.

Examples

The unattainability of these rare artifacts made them even more desirable to collectors.
