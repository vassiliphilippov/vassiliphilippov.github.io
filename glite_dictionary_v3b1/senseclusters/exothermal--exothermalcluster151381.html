exothermal

(of a chemical reaction or process) characterized by the release of heat; occurring with the liberation of energy as heat.

Examples

Exothermal reaction
In many exothermal reactions, the temperature of the surrounding environment increases noticeably.
The exothermal nature of combustion makes it useful for generating energy.
The reaction between sodium and water is highly exothermal, producing heat and sometimes flames.

Synonyms
