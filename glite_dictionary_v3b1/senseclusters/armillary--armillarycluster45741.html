armillary

adjective
like an armillary sphere
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrməˌlɛri / (AmE), / ɑːˈmɪləri / (BrE)
relating to or resembling an armillary sphere, a model representing objects in the sky with rings and circles

Examples

Armillary sphere model
The observatory featured an armillary design in its central hall.
She admired the armillary patterns carved into the ancient sundial.
The sculpture's armillary structure symbolized the harmony of the cosmos.
