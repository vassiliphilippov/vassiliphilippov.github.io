mother

noun
term for an elderly woman
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚ / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə / (BrE)
a respectful or affectionate term of address for an elderly woman

Examples

Kind mother at the shelter
He respectfully called the matronly caregiver mother during his stay at the shelter.
In some communities, it is common to address an older woman as mother out of respect.
The villagers greeted the kindly old lady as mother whenever they saw her at the market.
