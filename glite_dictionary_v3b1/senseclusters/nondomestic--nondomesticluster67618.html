nondomestic

adjective
not domesticated
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)
not domesticated; living or existing in the wild rather than being kept or tamed by humans

Examples

Nondomestic animals
The reserve is home to many nondomestic species that are rarely seen in captivity.
Researchers studied the behavior of nondomestic cats in their natural habitat.
Some plants are considered nondomestic because they have never been cultivated by humans.
