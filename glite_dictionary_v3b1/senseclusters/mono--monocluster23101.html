mono

noun
single audio channel
7.0 0.99/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀêno ä / (AmE), / Ààm…ín…ô ä / (BrE)
a system or recording that uses a single audio channel instead of stereo sound

Examples

A mono recording
The old radio only plays music in mono.
Some classic albums were originally recorded in mono before stereo became popular.
Audiophiles sometimes prefer mono versions of songs for their unique sound quality.

Synonyms

  • monophonic
    7.0 0.02/m
    monophonic is the technical term for using a single audio channel in playback, while mono is the common short name used in recordings.
  • single-channel
    4.8 0.27/m
    single-channel describes sound sent on only one audio path, while mono is the usual short label for that single-channel recording.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stereo - a system or recording using two or more audio channels
