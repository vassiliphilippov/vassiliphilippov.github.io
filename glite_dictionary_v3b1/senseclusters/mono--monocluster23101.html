mono

noun
single audio channel
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəʊ / (BrE)
a system or recording that uses a single audio channel instead of stereo sound

Examples

A mono recording
The old radio only plays music in mono.
Some classic albums were originally recorded in mono before stereo became popular.
Audiophiles sometimes prefer mono versions of songs for their unique sound quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stereo - a system or recording using two or more audio channels
