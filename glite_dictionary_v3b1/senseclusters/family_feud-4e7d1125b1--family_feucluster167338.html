family feud

noun
prolonged conflict between families
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌfjud / (AmE), / ˈfæməli ˌfjuːd / (BrE)
a long-standing or bitter conflict or quarrel between members or branches of a family

Examples

A family feud
In the village, everyone knew about the famous family feud that divided neighbors.
The family feud has lasted for generations and shows no sign of ending.
Their wedding was almost cancelled because of an old family feud between the two households.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feud - a prolonged or bitter quarrel or conflict
  • antonym: truce - an agreement to stop fighting or arguing for a time
