lift

verb
raise or make audible a sound
5.4
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
to raise (the voice or a sound), making it audible; to utter (a cry, shout, or similar sound) aloud

Examples

To lift a cry
He lifted a cry of warning when he saw the danger ahead.
She lifted her voice so the audience could hear her clearly.
The villagers lifted a cheer as the soldiers returned home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raise - to cause (something) to move upwards or become audible, especially the voice
