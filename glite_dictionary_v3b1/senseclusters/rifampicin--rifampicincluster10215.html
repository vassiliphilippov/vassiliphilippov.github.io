rifampicin

noun
antibiotic for tuberculosis
5.5
Pronunciation: /rɪ.fæm.ˈpɪ.sɪn/ (AmE), /rɪ.fæm.ˈpɪ.sɪn/ (BrE)

Examples

Some bacteria can develop resistance to rifampicin if the medication is not taken properly.
Patients taking rifampicin should be monitored for possible side effects during treatment.
