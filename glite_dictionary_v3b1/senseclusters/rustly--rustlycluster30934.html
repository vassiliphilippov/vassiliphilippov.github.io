rustly

adjective
soft, gentle sound
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstli / (AmE), / ˈrʌstli / (BrE)
making a soft, light sound like things gently rubbing or moving together

Examples

Rustly leaves sound
The rustly leaves made the forest seem alive with quiet movement.
She loved the rustly sound of her dress as she walked across the room.
A rustly breeze moved through the dry grass on the hillside.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: crackly - making a series of small, sharp sounds
  • synonym: rustling - making a soft, light sound of things moving together
  • antonym: silent - making no sound at all
