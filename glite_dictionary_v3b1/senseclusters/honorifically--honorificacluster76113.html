honorifically

adverb
in an honorary way
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːnəˈrɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈrɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that gives an honorary or ceremonial title or position, often without real authority or duties

Examples

Honorifically named president
She was honorifically named president of the club, though she did not participate in its activities.
The title was honorifically bestowed upon him in recognition of his years of service.
He was honorifically addressed as 'Doctor' despite not holding a medical degree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremonially - in a formal or ceremonial manner
