pin

noun
flagpole marking a golf hole
5.5
Pronunciation: / pɪn / (AmE), / pɪn / (BrE)
In golf, a slender pole with a flag attached, used to mark the location of the hole on a green.

Examples

Golf pin
He aimed his shot directly at the pin to set up a short putt.
The caddie walked over to remove the pin from the hole.
With the pin at the back of the green, choosing the right club was essential.
