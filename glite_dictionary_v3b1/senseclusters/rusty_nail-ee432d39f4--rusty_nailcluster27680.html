rusty nail

noun
a nail covered with rust
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsti neɪl / (AmE), / ˈrʌsti neɪl / (BrE)
a nail that is covered with rust, typically as a result of prolonged exposure to moisture and air

Examples

A rusty nail outdoors
After spending years outdoors, the old shed was full of rusty nails.
He warned the children not to touch the exposed rusty nail in the fence.
She got a tetanus shot after stepping on a rusty nail in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nail - a small metal spike used for fastening things
  • synonym: rust - a reddish-brown coating formed on iron or steel by oxidation
