supersize

noun
extra-large portion
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌsaɪz / (BrE)
a serving, portion, or product that is larger than the standard size, especially in fast food, often for an extra charge

Examples

A supersize meal

Some people think the supersize option encourages overeating.
