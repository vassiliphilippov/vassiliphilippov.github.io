Anabaena

proper noun
cyanobacteria genus name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænəˈbiːnə / (AmE), / ˌænəˈbiːnə / (BrE)
an older scientific genus name for certain planktonic cyanobacteria, now often reclassified under other genera
