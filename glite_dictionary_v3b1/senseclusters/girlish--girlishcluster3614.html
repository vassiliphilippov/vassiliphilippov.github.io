girlish

adjective
like or characteristic of a young girl
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɜr.lɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɜː.lɪʃ / (BrE)
Having qualities or characteristics typically associated with a young girl, such as innocence or playfulness.

Examples

Girlish charm
Despite her age, his grandmother still had a certain girlish charm about her.
Her girlish laughter filled the room with a sense of fun and innocence.
She wore a girlish pink dress that reminded everyone of her childhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boyish - having qualities associated with a young boy
