Paul von Hindenburg

proper noun
German field marshal and president of the Weimar Republic
8.1
Pronunciation: /pɔːl vɔːn ˈhɪn.dən.bɜːrɡ/ (AmE), /pɔːl vɒn ˈhɪn.dən.bɜːɡ/ (BrE)
