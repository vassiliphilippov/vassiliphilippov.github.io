export

noun
goods/services sent to another country for sale
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkˌspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈɛkˌspɔːt / (BrE)
a commodity, product, or service sold and sent to another country

Examples

A main export product
Oil is one of the country's main exports.
The company increased its exports to Asia last year.
Wheat is a major export for Canada.
