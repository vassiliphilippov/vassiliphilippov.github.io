chakra

noun
divine discus weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːkrə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkrə / (BrE)
a disc-shaped weapon or spinning wheel, especially the divine discus (Sudarshana Chakra) associated with Hindu gods

Examples

Vishnu's chakra weapon
The statue of Vishnu shows him holding a chakra in one of his four hands.
Legends say the chakra can return to its owner after being thrown.
The ancient carving depicted the god holding a spinning chakra above his palm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discus - a round, flat object thrown as a weapon or in sport
  • synonym: wheel - a circular object that turns around a central point
