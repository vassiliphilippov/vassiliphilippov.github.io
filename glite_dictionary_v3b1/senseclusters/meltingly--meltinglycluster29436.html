meltingly

adverb
soft or tender manner
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛltɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmɛltɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is soft, tender, or emotionally moving

Examples

Meltingly tender smile
She smiled meltingly at the child, making everyone in the room feel warm inside.
The dessert was meltingly soft, almost dissolving on the tongue.
He gazed meltingly at his partner during their wedding dance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harshly - in a rough or unkind way
  • synonym: tenderly - in a gentle and loving way
