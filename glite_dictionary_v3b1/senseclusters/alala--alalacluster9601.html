alala

noun
loud war cry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːləˈlɑː / (AmE), / ˌæləˈlɑː / (BrE)
a loud cry or shout, especially a war cry or a shout of triumph, sometimes personified in Greek mythology

Examples

A battle alala
The warriors let out a fierce alala as they charged into battle.
Echoes of the ancient alala could be heard across the valley after their victory.
In Greek mythology, Alala was the spirit who personified the war cry.
