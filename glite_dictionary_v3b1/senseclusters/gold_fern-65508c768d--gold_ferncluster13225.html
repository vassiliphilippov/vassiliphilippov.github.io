gold fern

noun
fern with golden-yellow undersides of fronds
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld fɝːn / (AmE), / ɡəʊld fɜːn / (BrE)
A fern native to the West Indies and South America, distinguished by fronds with bright golden-yellow undersides.

Examples

The gold fern plant
Botanists studied the unique coloration of the gold fern found in South American rainforests.
She collected a specimen of gold fern during her research trip to the Caribbean.
The gold fern added a vibrant touch to the greenhouse with its luminous yellow fronds.
