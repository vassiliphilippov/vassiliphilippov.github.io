Morgellons

noun
disputed skin condition
Pronunciation: / mɔrˈɡɛlənz / (AmE), / mɔːˈɡɛlənz / (BrE)
a disputed medical condition marked by skin sensations such as crawling, itching, and the presence of fibers on or under the skin

Examples

Morgellons skin condition
Some patients diagnosed with Morgellons report persistent itching and unusual fibers on their skin.
The medical community remains divided over whether Morgellons is a distinct disease or a psychological condition.
Support groups have formed to help people who believe they suffer from Morgellons.
