gulmohar

noun
tropical flowering tree
Pronunciation: / ɡʊlˈmoʊhɑːr / (AmE), / ɡʊlˈməʊhɑː / (BrE)
a tropical tree with bright red or orange flowers, also known as the flame tree

Examples

Gulmohar tree blooms
The gulmohar in our garden is covered with fiery red blossoms every summer.
Children played under the shade of the gulmohar during the hot afternoon.
In spring, the city streets are lined with blooming gulmohar trees.
