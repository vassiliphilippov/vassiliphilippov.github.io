precompetitive

adjective
before a competition
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkəmˈpɛtətɪv / (AmE), / ˌpriːkəmˈpɛtɪtɪv / (BrE)
occurring or taking place before a competition or competitive event begins

Examples

Precompetitive warm-up
The coach designed a precompetitive routine to help the team warm up before the match.
Athletes often focus on nutrition and rest during the precompetitive phase.
A precompetitive meeting was held to discuss the rules and schedule for the tournament.
