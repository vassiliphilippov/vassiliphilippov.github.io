watch fire

noun
signal fire lit at night
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃ ˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃ ˌfaɪə / (BrE)
A fire lit and kept burning at night to serve as a signal, especially for guards or sentinels.

Examples

A watch fire signal
Every outpost maintained a watch fire to communicate with the main camp.
The soldiers lit a watch fire on the hilltop to warn the nearby villages.
Through the darkness, the watch fire was visible for miles along the coast.

  • synonym: signal fire - a fire made as a signal
