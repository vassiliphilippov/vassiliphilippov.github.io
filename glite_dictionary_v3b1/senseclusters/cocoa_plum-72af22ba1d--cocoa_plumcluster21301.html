cocoa plum

noun
tropical American fruit used in preserves
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊ.koʊ plʌm /; / ˈkoʊkoʊ plʌm / (AmE), / ˈkəʊ.kəʊ plʌm /; / ˈkəʊkəʊ plʌm / (BrE)
a tropical American plant (shrub or small tree) or its edible, plum-like fruit, often used in jams and desserts
