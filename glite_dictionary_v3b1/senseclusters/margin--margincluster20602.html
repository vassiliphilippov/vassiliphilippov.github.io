margin

noun
boundary line or area near the edge
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrdʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdʒɪn / (BrE)
the boundary line or the area immediately inside the boundary of something

Examples

Margin of the field
Flowers grew along the margin of the field.
The referee watched the ball as it approached the margin of the court.
The text should stay within the margin of the page.

Related Terms

  • antonym: center - the middle point or part of something
  • synonym: edge - the outside limit of an object or area
