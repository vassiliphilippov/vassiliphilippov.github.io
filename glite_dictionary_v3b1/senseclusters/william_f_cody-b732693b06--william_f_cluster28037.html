William F. Cody

proper noun
American showman known as Buffalo Bill
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪljəm ɛf ˈkoʊdi / (AmE), / ˈwɪljəm ɛf ˈkəʊdi / (BrE)
(1846–1917) An American showman and frontier scout celebrated for organizing and touring with the Wild West Show, where he was popularly known as Buffalo Bill.

Examples

William F. Cody Wild West Show
William F. Cody traveled across America and Europe with his famous Wild West Show.
Many people know William F. Cody better by his nickname, Buffalo Bill.
The legacy of William F. Cody continues to influence popular images of the American West.
