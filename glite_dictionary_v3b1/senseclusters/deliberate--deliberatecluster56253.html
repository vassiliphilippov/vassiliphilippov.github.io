deliberate

adjective
done carefully and unhurriedly
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪbərət / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪbərət / (BrE)
done carefully and slowly, showing thought and intention; unhurried and dignified

Examples

Deliberate movements
His movements were deliberate, each step measured and precise.
She gave a deliberate nod, signaling her agreement without haste.
The lawyer spoke in a deliberate manner, choosing her words with care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: measured - done with care and precision; unhurried
