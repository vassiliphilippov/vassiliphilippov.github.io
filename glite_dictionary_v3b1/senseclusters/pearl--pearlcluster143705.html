pearl

verb
to form beads
7.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / p…úrl / (AmE), / p…úÀêl / (BrE)
to form or cause to form small rounded drops or beads, especially of liquid, on a surface

Examples

Sweat pearls on skin
Sweat began pearling at his temples as he hurried up the stairs.
Tiny droplets were pearling on the cold glass after the rain.
Condensation started pearling along the window frame in the early morning.
After the rain, water pearled on the leaves, sparkling in the sunlight.
Sweat pearled on her brow as she finished the marathon.
Condensation pearled on the outside of the cold glass.
