sleeper nest

noun
group of dormant secret operatives
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpər nɛst / (AmE), / ˈsliːpə nɛst / (BrE)
A group or cell of secret operatives or terrorists who remain inactive until receiving instructions to act.

Examples

A sleeper nest group
Authorities uncovered a sleeper nest in the outskirts of the city after months of surveillance.
Intelligence agencies are constantly monitoring for signs of a potential sleeper nest within the country.
The sleeper nest remained undetected for years before launching a coordinated attack.
