Centaurea nigra

noun
a purple-flowered Eurasian perennial plant (black knapweed)
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛn.təˈɹiː.ə ˈnaɪ.ɡrə / (AmE), / ˌsɛn.tɔːˈriː.ə ˈnɪɡ.rə / (BrE)
a Eurasian perennial plant with tough wiry stems and purple flower heads, commonly known as black knapweed and naturalized widely outside its native range
