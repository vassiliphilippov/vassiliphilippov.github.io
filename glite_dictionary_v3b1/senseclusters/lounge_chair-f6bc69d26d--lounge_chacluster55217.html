lounge chair

noun
comfortable upholstered armchair
2.6 0.7/m
Pronunciation: / la änd í t É…õr / (AmE), / la änd í t É…õÀê / (BrE)
A comfortable upholstered armchair designed for sitting and relaxing, often with a reclining back and sometimes a footrest.

Examples

A lounge chair with footrest
He settled into the lounge chair and began to read his book.
She bought a new lounge chair that perfectly matched her living room decor.
The hotel pool area was lined with comfortable lounge chairs for guests to relax.
