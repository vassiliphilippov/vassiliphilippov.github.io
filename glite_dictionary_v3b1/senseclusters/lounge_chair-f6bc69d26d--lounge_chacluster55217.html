lounge chair

noun
comfortable upholstered armchair
2.6 0.68/m
Pronunciation: / la änd í t É…õr / (AmE), / la änd í t É…õÀê / (BrE)
A comfortable upholstered armchair designed for sitting and relaxing, often with a reclining back and sometimes a footrest.

Examples

A lounge chair with footrest
He settled into the lounge chair and began to read his book.
She bought a new lounge chair that perfectly matched her living room decor.
The hotel pool area was lined with comfortable lounge chairs for guests to relax.

Synonyms

  • recliner
    3.1 1.34/m
    a recliner has a mechanical back and lifting footrest for lying back, while a lounge chair is for general relaxed sitting.
  • reclining chair
    2.7 0.20/m
    a reclining chair has a back that tilts and often a built-in footrest, while a lounge chair is a general comfortable armchair for relaxing.
  • lounger
    4.1 0.26/m
    a lounger has an adjustable back and footrest for long reclining, while a lounge chair is a general armchair for sitting and relaxing.
  • easy chair
    2.3 1.08/m
    an easy chair is a large soft armchair mainly for sitting and reading, while a lounge chair often adds reclining and a footrest for comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recliner - an armchair that can be tilted backward
