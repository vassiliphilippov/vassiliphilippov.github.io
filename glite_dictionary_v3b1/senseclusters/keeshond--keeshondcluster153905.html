keeshond

noun
a dog breed with greyish coat from Holland
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪs.hɑnd, ˈkeɪs.hoʊnd, ˈkiːz.hɑnd / (AmE), / ˈkeɪs.hɒnd, ˈkiːz.hɒnd / (BrE)
a breed of dog of the spitz type with a thick, greyish coat and a curled tail, originally from the Netherlands

Examples

A keeshond dog
A keeshond requires regular grooming to keep its thick coat healthy.
The keeshond is known for its friendly temperament and striking appearance.
Their family recently adopted a playful keeshond puppy from a local breeder.
