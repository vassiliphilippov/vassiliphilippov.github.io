caught dead

adjective
never want to be seen doing something
Pronunciation: / kɔt dɛd / (AmE), / kɔːt dɛd / (BrE)
used to say that someone would never want to be seen doing or wearing something, typically because they find it embarrassing or unacceptable

Examples

To be caught dead in something
I wouldn't be caught dead wearing a tie like that.
Mark said he would never be caught dead eating fast food.
They refused to be caught dead at such a boring event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: keen - enthusiastic or eager to do something; similar contrasting meaning
  • antonym: willing - ready to do something; opposite in meaning in context
