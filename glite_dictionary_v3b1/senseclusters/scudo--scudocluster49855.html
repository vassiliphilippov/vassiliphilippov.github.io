scudo

noun
heraldic shield
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈskuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a shield or shield-shaped emblem, especially as used in heraldry or on coats of arms

Examples

A knight's scudo
The knight's scudo was decorated with a golden lion and red stripes.
Historians studied the scudo to learn more about the noble family's history.
A large scudo hung above the entrance, displaying the city's ancient emblem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crest - a distinctive device representing a family or organization, often on a shield
  • synonym: shield - a broad piece of armor carried for protection
