first class

noun
luxury travel accommodation category
Pronunciation: / ˌfɝst ˈklæs / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈklɑːs / (BrE)
the most luxurious and expensive category of accommodations available on a ship, train, or aircraft

Examples

First class seat
He always travels first class on international flights to ensure comfort.
She was upgraded to first class as a reward for being a frequent flyer.
The tickets for first class on the train were much more expensive than standard class.
