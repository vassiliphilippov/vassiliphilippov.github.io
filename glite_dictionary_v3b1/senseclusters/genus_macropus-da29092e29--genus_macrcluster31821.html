genus Macropus

noun
genus of kangaroos and wallabies
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmækˈroʊpəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˌmækˈrəʊpəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Macropodidae, comprising the typical kangaroos and wallabies.
