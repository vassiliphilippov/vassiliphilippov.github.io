spoken communication

noun
oral exchange of information
Pronunciation: / ˈspoʊkən kəmˌjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈspəʊkən kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the exchange of information or ideas by speaking rather than in writing

Examples

Effective spoken communication
Effective spoken communication is essential in team meetings to ensure everyone understands their roles.
Language learners often find spoken communication more challenging than reading or writing.
The interview assessed the candidate’s ability in both written and spoken communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oral communication - communication conveyed through spoken words
  • antonym: written communication - communication conveyed through written words or text
