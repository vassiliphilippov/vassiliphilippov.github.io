scamper

verb
move quickly or playfully with small steps
4.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈskæm.pər/ (AmE), /ˈskæm.pə/ (BrE)

Examples

She scampered down the hallway, her footsteps echoing behind her.
The children scampered across the playground as soon as the bell rang.
