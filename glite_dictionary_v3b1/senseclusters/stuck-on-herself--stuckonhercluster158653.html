stuck-on-herself

adjective
self-absorbed; egotistical
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌk ɑn hɚˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ˈstʌk ɒn həˈsɛlf / (BrE)
Overly self-absorbed or egotistical; having an excessively high opinion of oneself.

Examples

A stuck-on-herself person
Everyone avoids Jessica nowadays because she is so stuck-on-herself.
He accused her of being stuck-on-herself after she bragged about her promotion all night.
Lately, she's become a bit too stuck-on-herself for my taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceited - having an excessively high opinion of oneself
  • antonym: modest - having a low opinion of one's abilities; not boastful
