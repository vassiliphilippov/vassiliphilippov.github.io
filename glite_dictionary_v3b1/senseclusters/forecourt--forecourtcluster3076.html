forecourt

noun
open area in front of a building
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːkɔːt / (BrE)
an open area in front of a building or group of buildings, often serving as an entrance or approach.

Examples

The forecourt area
Please wait in the forecourt until the doors are opened.
The hotel guests parked their cars in the spacious forecourt near the main entrance.
The mansion's forecourt was beautifully tiled and lined with marble statues.
