rad

noun
unit of plane angle (radian)
Pronunciation: / ræd / (AmE), / ræd / (BrE)
a unit of plane angle equal to the angle subtended at the center of a circle by an arc equal in length to the radius; short for radian, approximately 57.295 degrees
