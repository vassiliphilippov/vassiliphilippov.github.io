uber

x
extremely; super-
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈuːbər / (AmE), / ˈuːbə / (BrE)
used as a prefix before nouns or adjectives to mean 'super', 'very', or 'extremely'.

Examples

Uber-rich people
The uber-rich often live in exclusive neighborhoods far from the city center.
She wore an uber-stylish dress to the party and caught everyone's attention.
His uber-competitive attitude made him a tough opponent in every game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ultra - extremely; to an extreme degree
