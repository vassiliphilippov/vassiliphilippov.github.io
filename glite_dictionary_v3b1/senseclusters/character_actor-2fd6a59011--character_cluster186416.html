character actor

noun
actor specializing in supporting roles
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrɪktɚ ˈæktɚ / (AmE), / ˈkærɪktə ˈæktə / (BrE)
An actor who specializes in playing distinctive, often supporting roles, rather than leading parts.

Examples

Character actor roles
As a character actor, she excelled at portraying quirky, memorable personalities.
He found lasting success as a character actor in both film and television.
The production cast a well-known character actor in the role of the eccentric neighbor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lead actor - the main actor in a play, film, or TV show
