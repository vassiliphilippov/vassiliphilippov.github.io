clupeid

noun
a fish of the herring family
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkluːpiɪd / (AmE), / ˈkluːpɪɪd / (BrE)
any fish belonging to the family Clupeidae, which includes herrings, sardines, and similar small, soft-finned schooling fishes of shallow northern seas.

Examples

A clupeid fish
Sardines and other clupeid species often travel in large schools near the surface.
Scientists studied the migration patterns of several clupeid populations in the North Atlantic.
The commercial harvest of clupeid fishes supports many coastal communities worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herring - a small, silvery fish closely related to clupeids
