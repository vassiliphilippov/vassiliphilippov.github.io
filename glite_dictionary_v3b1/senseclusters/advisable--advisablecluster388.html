advisable

adjective
recommended or wise to do
Pronunciation: / ədˈvaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ədˈvaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
sensible or prudent and likely to be recommended; appropriate to do in the circumstances.

Examples

It is advisable to wait
Delaying your decision any further would not be advisable.
It is advisable to save a backup copy of your important documents.
Wearing a helmet is advisable when riding a bicycle in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inadvisable - not recommended or sensible to do
  • synonym: prudent - acting with or showing care and thought for the future
  • synonym: sensible - chosen in accordance with wisdom or prudence
