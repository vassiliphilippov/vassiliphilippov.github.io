Examples

Synonyms

  • recommendable
    4.5 0.02/m
    recommendable is worth suggesting to others in many situations, while advisable is a cautious suggestion that an action is sensible and prudent.
  • appropriate
    3.5 109/m
    appropriate fits a situation or behavior and seems proper in context, while advisable suggests a specific cautious action to take.
  • suitable
    1.8 20/m
    suitable matches a person's needs or purpose and is about fit, while advisable focuses on whether doing something is wise now.
