defeatism

noun
acceptance of inevitable defeat
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfiːtɪzəm / (AmE), / dɪˈfiːtɪzəm / (BrE)
the attitude of accepting defeat as inevitable, often without further effort or resistance

Examples

Government defeatism
Critics accused the government of defeatism in handling the ongoing crisis.
His speech was intended to counter the spread of defeatism among the population.
The general warned that any sign of defeatism would lower the troops' morale.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pessimism - the tendency to see the worst aspect of things or believe the worst will happen
  • antonym: resistance - refusal to accept defeat or give up
