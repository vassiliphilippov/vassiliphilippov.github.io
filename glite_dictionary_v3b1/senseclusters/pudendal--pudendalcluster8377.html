pudendal

adjective
relating to the external genital organs
Pronunciation: / pjuˈdɛndəl / (AmE), / pjuˈdɛndəl / (BrE)
relating to or located near the external genital organs (pudendum), especially used in anatomical contexts

Examples

Pudendal nerve
Damage to the pudendal region can cause loss of feeling.
He suffered from pudendal neuralgia after the surgery.
The pudendal nerve is responsible for sensation in the genital area.
