variety show

noun
entertainment program with mixed performances
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /və.ˈraɪ.ə.tiː ʃoʊ/ /; / /və.ˈraɪ.ə.tɪ ʃoʊ/ (AmE), /və.ˈraɪ.ə.tiː ʃəʊ/ /; / /və.ˈraɪ.ə.tɪ ʃəʊ/ (BrE)
