salient

adjective
most noticeable or prominent
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.li.ənt / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.li.ənt / (BrE)
most noticeable or important; prominent.

Examples

Salient points of a talk
Her presentation focused on the salient points of the research.
One salient feature of the building is its glass rooftop.
The salient differences between the two candidates were clear during the debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insignificant - not important or hardly noticeable
  • synonym: prominent - easily noticeable or standing out
