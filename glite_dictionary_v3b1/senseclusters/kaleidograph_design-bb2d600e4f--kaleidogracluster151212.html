kaleidograph design

noun
artistic pattern with kaleidoscope effect
Pronunciation: / kəˈlaɪdəˌɡræf dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / kəˈlaɪdəˌɡrɑːf dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
