plesiomorphic

adjective
ancestral trait
Pronunciation: / ˌpliːziəˈmɔːrfɪk / (AmE), / ˌpliːziəˈmɔːfɪk / (BrE)
describing a trait or characteristic that is ancestral or primitive within a group in evolutionary biology

Examples

A plesiomorphic trait
The fossil record shows that this animal retained several plesiomorphic features from its distant ancestors.
Scientists identified the trait as plesiomorphic, indicating it was present in the earliest members of the group.
Unlike its relatives, the species still exhibits a plesiomorphic skeletal structure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestral - relating to ancestors or original forms
  • antonym: derived - developed or evolved from an earlier form
