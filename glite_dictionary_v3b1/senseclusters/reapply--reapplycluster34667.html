reapply

verb
to use again
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈplaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to put or use something again, especially after its previous effect has ended or worn off

Examples

To reapply sunscreen
You should reapply sunscreen every two hours when you are at the beach.
After washing your hands, remember to reapply the lotion to keep your skin moisturized.
If the paint starts to fade, you may need to reapply another coat for better coverage.
