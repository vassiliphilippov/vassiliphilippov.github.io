charge

verb
pay using a credit card
1.9 1.7
Pronunciation: /tʃɑːrdʒ/ (AmE), /tʃɑːdʒ/ (BrE)
to ask for or demand an amount of money for goods or services

Examples

To charge a customer
Can I charge this book, or do you only accept cash?
I would like to charge this dinner to my credit card, please.
She forgot her wallet and decided to charge the groceries instead.
How much do you charge for a haircut?
The restaurant charged us extra for the appetizers.
They mistakenly charged my credit card twice.
Do they charge extra for delivery?
Some app subscriptions charge a fee after a free trial period.
The hotel charged us for breakfast every morning.

  • degree higher: overcharge - to charge too high a price
  • synonym: pay - to give someone money for goods or services
  • antonym: refund - to return money paid for something
  • degree lower: undercharge - to charge too low a price
