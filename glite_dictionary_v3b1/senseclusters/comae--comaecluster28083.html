comae

noun
optical aberrations
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊmiː / (AmE), / ˈkəʊmiː / (BrE)
the plural of coma, referring to optical aberrations where point sources appear with comet-like blurring in lenses or telescopes

Examples

Lens comae
The astronomer adjusted the telescope to reduce the number of visible comae in the star field.
Photographers often encounter comae when using wide-aperture lenses at night.
Careful lens alignment can help minimize comae in astrophotography images.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astigmatism - an optical defect causing blurred or distorted images
  • synonym: coma - an optical aberration causing a comet-like blur in images
