clean room

noun
room with controlled contamination level
6.4 0.29/m
Pronunciation: / klin rum / (AmE), / kliÀên ruÀêm / (BrE)
a room maintained at a controlled level of contamination, used especially in laboratories and the manufacturing or repair of precision equipment

Examples

A clean room environment
Any dust particles entering the clean room could damage sensitive electronic components.
Technicians must wear special suits when working inside the clean room.
The new microchip facility was built with a state-of-the-art clean room for assembly.
