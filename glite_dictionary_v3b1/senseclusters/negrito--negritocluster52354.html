negrito

noun
small South American bird
9.9
Pronunciation: / neɪˈɡriːtoʊ / (AmE), / neɪˈɡriːtəʊ / (BrE)
a small South American insect-eating bird, especially of the genus Lessonia, known for its dark plumage

Examples

A negrito bird
The negrito is often seen darting along the water's edge in search of insects.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a rare Andean negrito during their trip.
A male negrito displayed its striking dark feathers as it perched on a reed.
