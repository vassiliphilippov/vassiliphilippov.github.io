drive-by event

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪv.baɪ ɪ.ˈvent/ /; / /ˈdʒraɪv.baɪ ɪ.ˈvent/ /; / /ˈdraɪv.baɪ iː.ˈvent/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪv.baɪ ɪ.ˈvent/ /; / /ˈdraɪv.baɪ iː.ˈvent/ /; / /ˈdʒraɪv.baɪ ɪ.ˈvent/ (BrE)
An organized gathering or activity that allows people to participate briefly and often without leaving their cars, typically for safety or convenience.

Examples

A drive-by event gathering
During the pandemic, our school organized a drive-by event so families could safely pick up supplies.
Many birthday celebrations became drive-by events to maintain social distancing guidelines.
The charity hosted a drive-by event for donations, allowing people to contribute without getting out of their cars.
