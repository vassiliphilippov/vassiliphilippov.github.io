iconometer

noun
measuring device in astronomy
Pronunciation: / aɪˈkɑːnəˌmiːtər / (AmE), / aɪˈkɒnəˌmiːtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the apparent size of an object, especially in astronomy

Examples

An iconometer instrument
The scientist used an iconometer to measure the apparent diameter of the distant star.
With the help of an iconometer, astronomers can compare the sizes of planets as seen from Earth.
The old observatory still has a working iconometer among its collection of antique instruments.
