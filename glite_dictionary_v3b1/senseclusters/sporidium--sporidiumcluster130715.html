sporidium

noun
small reproductive spore
Pronunciation: / spɔːˈrɪdiəm / (AmE), / spɔːˈrɪdiəm / (BrE)
a small spore or spore-like reproductive cell, especially found in certain fungi or protozoa

Examples

A sporidium cell
Under the microscope, the scientist identified several sporidia among the fungal cells.
The life cycle of this protozoan includes a stage where sporidia are released into the environment.
Researchers collected sporidia samples to study their role in disease transmission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spore - a reproductive cell capable of developing into a new individual
