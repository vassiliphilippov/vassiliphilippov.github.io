unstoned

adjective
with stone; not intoxicated
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈstəʊnd / (BrE)
not having had the stone or pit removed, or not under the influence of drugs

Examples

Unstoned cherries
The unstoned cherries made the pie difficult to eat.
He remained unstoned at the party while others used recreational drugs.
Please buy unstoned dates for the recipe, as we need the pits inside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pitted - having had the stone or pit removed
  • antonym: stoned - under the influence of drugs, especially cannabis
