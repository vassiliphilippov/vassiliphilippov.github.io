underground scene

noun
subculture outside the mainstream
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˌɡraʊnd ˈsiːn / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˌɡraʊnd ˈsiːn / (BrE)
a subculture or movement, especially in the arts, that exists outside of mainstream society and often operates in secrecy or obscurity

Examples

The underground scene music
She became a prominent figure in the underground scene of electronic music.
The underground scene often influences mainstream trends in fashion and art.
Young artists are drawn to the city's vibrant underground scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterculture - a culture with values and norms that differ from mainstream society
  • antonym: mainstream - the dominant trend in opinion, fashion, or the arts
