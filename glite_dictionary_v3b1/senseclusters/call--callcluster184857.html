call

verb
redeem before maturity (finance)

Pronunciation: / k…îl /; / k…ël / (AmE), / k…îÀêl / (BrE)
to require redemption or payment before the scheduled maturity date, or to demand repayment of a loan before its normal due date

Examples

The company decided to call the bonds early due to lower interest rates.
