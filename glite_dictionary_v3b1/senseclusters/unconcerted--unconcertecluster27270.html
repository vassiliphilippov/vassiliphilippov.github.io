unconcerted

adjective
not coordinated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈsɜːrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈsɜːtɪd / (BrE)
not planned, coordinated, or carried out together with others

Examples

Unconcerted efforts
The unconcerted actions of the team led to confusion during the project.
Several unconcerted attempts were made to solve the problem, but none succeeded.
Because their efforts were unconcerted, the campaign failed to make an impact.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concerted - planned or done together with others
  • synonym: uncoordinated - not organized or planned together
