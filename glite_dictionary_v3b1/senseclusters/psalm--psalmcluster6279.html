psalm

verb
sing or celebrate in psalms
7.5
Pronunciation: / sɑm / (AmE), / sɑːm / (BrE)
to sing or celebrate using psalms, especially in a religious context

Examples

To psalm in church
He psalmed his gratitude with a joyful heart.
Long ago, the priests would psalm in the temple during ceremonies.
The congregation began to psalm together as the service started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chant - to sing or intone repetitively, often of sacred text
