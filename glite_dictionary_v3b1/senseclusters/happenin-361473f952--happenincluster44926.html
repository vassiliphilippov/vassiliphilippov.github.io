happenin'

interjection
informal greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈhæpənɪn / (AmE), / ˈhæpənɪn / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to ask what is going on or how someone is doing

Examples

Happenin'? greeting
He walked into the room and said, 'Happenin'?' to his friends.
When she saw her cousin at the concert, she greeted him with a cheerful 'Happenin'?'
At the start of the meeting, someone joked, 'Happenin'?' to break the ice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hello - a common greeting used when meeting someone
  • synonym: what's up - an informal greeting asking how someone is or what is happening
