single crochet

noun
a basic crochet stitch
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl kroʊˈʃeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl krəʊˈʃeɪ / (BrE)
A basic crochet stitch in which a hook is inserted into a stitch, yarn is drawn through, and a single loop is completed.

Examples

A single crochet stitch
After practicing single crochet, he was able to create a simple scarf.
She learned how to single crochet before moving on to more complicated techniques.
To make this pattern, begin with a row of single crochet stitches.
