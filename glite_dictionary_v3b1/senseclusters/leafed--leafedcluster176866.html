leafed

adjective
having leaves; often in compounds
Pronunciation: / liːft / (AmE), / liːft / (BrE)
having leaves, or having leaves of a particular type (often used in combination)

Examples

Leafed plants
In the autumn, many leafed plants shed their foliage as the temperature drops.
The oak is a large, leafed tree that provides plenty of shade in summer.
We admired the lush, green leafed branches after the spring rain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leafless - having no leaves
  • synonym: leaved - having leaves, as specified, often used in combinations
