coffin dodger

noun
humorous slang for an elderly person
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑfɪn ˌdɑdʒər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfɪn ˌdɒdʒə / (BrE)
an informal, humorous, and sometimes irreverent term for an elderly person, especially one who has survived illness or old age

Examples

Old coffin dodger
He referred to himself as a coffin dodger at his 90th birthday party, making everyone laugh.
Some youngsters call their grandparents coffin dodgers, but it's meant lightheartedly.
Surviving a major operation, she proudly joked that she was a true coffin dodger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: old-timer - an elderly person, particularly one with experience or tenure
  • synonym: senior citizen - a polite term for an elderly person
