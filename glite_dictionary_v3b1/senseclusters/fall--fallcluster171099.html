fall

verb
decrease in size or amount
2.6
Pronunciation: / fɔl / (AmE), / fɔːl / (BrE)
to decrease in size, extent, or amount; to become less or lower

Examples

Prices fall quickly
After her illness, her energy levels fell to an all-time low.
Profits continued to fall during the winter months as demand slowed.
The temperature fell rapidly once the sun had set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop - to become lower in level, amount, or value
  • antonym: rise - to increase in size, amount, or level
