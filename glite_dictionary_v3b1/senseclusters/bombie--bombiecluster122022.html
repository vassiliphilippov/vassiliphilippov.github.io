bombie

noun
unexploded bomblet from a cluster bomb
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːm.bi / (AmE), / ˈbɒm.bi / (BrE)
a small, unexploded bomb or bomblet, especially one left undetonated after being dropped from a cluster bomb

Examples

A bombie found
Children are often warned not to touch any bombie they might find while playing outside.
Clearing bombies is a difficult and dangerous task for demining teams.
Many fields in Laos are still dangerous because of hidden bombies left from past conflicts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bomblet - a small bomb, especially one of many from a cluster bomb
