cover

noun
the outer part of a book
2.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.və/ (BrE)
something that protects, hides, or encloses another thing, such as a lid, blanket, book cover, or natural layer

Examples

A book cover
Most paperbacks have a flexible cover, unlike hardcovers.
She accidentally tore the cover while packing her books.
The cover of the novel features a stunning illustration.
After the storm, the ground was left with only a thin cover of snow.
The hunters waited silently, hidden behind a thick cover of bushes.
The rabbit disappeared under the cover of tall grass to escape predators.
It was so cold that he needed an extra cover on the bed.
She pulled the cover up to her chin before falling asleep.
The children hid under the cover during the thunderstorm.

  • synonym: lid - a removable or hinged cover for the top of a container

  • synonym: seal - a device or substance used to close a container tightly
