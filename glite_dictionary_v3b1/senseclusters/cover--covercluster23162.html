Synonyms

  • protective covering
    3.2 0.01/m
    a protective covering is meant to shield an object from harm, while a cover can also hide or simply sit on top.
  • casing
    3.0 1.12/m
    a casing is the hard outer housing made to hold or protect machinery, while a cover can be soft or loose and sit on top.
  • encasement
    6.8 0.12/m
    an encasement is the act of fully enclosing an item, while a cover is a simpler top or layer that may not fully protect.
