adoptively

adverb
by cell transfer
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑptɪvli / (AmE), / əˈdɒptɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner involving the transfer of cells, tissues, or immunity from one individual to another, especially in biology or medicine

Examples

Adoptively transferred cells
The scientists adoptively transferred immune cells to study the effects on disease resistance.
Immunity can be adoptively acquired through the transfer of specific lymphocytes.
The treatment was administered adoptively to boost the patient's immune response.

Related Terms

  • synonym: transplant - to move tissue or an organ from one body to another
