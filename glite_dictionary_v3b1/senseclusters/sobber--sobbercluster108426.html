sobber

noun
person who cries loudly
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːbər / (AmE), / ˈsɒbə / (BrE)
a person who cries loudly and uncontrollably, especially in response to strong emotions

Examples

A loud sobber
During the sad movie, he became the loudest sobber in the theater.
The little girl was a constant sobber whenever she lost her favorite toy.
At the funeral, a sobber could be heard from the back of the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laugher - a person who laughs, especially loudly or often
  • synonym: weeper - a person who cries, often quietly or gently
