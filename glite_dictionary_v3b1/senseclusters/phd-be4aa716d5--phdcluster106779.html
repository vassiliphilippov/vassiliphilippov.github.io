PhD

noun
doctorate degree; highest academic qualification
4.8
Pronunciation: / pi eɪtʃ ˈdi / (AmE), / piː eɪtʃ ˈdiː / (BrE)
a doctorate degree awarded for advanced academic research, typically after several years of postgraduate study and completion of a dissertation

Examples

A PhD degree
