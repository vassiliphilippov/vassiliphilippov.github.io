Gaian

adjective
of Gaia hypothesis
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪ.ən / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪ.ən / (BrE)
relating to the Gaia hypothesis or to Earth as a single living, self-regulating system

Examples

Gaian theory
The Gaian perspective emphasizes the interconnectedness of all life on Earth.
Her research is based on Gaian principles that treat the planet as a living system.
Many environmentalists adopt a Gaian approach to understanding global ecological changes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anthropocentric - viewing humans as the most important element of existence
  • synonym: ecological - relating to the relationships between living things and their environment
