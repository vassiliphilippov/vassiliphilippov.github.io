wendigo

noun
mythical flesh-eating spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwen.dɪ.goʊ/ (AmE), /ˈwen.dɪ.gəʊ/ (BrE)
a mythical creature or evil spirit from Algonquian folklore, believed to eat human flesh; also, the fear of becoming such a monster
