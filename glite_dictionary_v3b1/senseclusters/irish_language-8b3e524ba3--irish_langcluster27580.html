Irish language

noun
Celtic language of Ireland
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Celtic language of Ireland, also known as Irish or Gaeilge, spoken as a first or second language in parts of Ireland

Examples

Irish language course
He took a course to improve his Irish language skills.
She is fluent in both English and Irish language.
The Irish language has seen a revival in recent years.
