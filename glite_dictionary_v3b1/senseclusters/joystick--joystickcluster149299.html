joystick

noun
manual control device; input lever
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪ.stɪk / (BrE)
a lever-like control device that you move by hand to operate a machine, computer, or aircraft

Examples

Aircraft joystick
He used the joystick to control the robot's movements during the competition.
Modern video games often require a joystick or a gamepad for precise control.
Pilots practice with a flight simulator that features a realistic joystick for steering.
Modern airplanes often replace the traditional joystick with a side-stick controller.
Sudden turbulence caused the joystick to jerk to one side.
The pilot gripped the joystick firmly as he prepared for takeoff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: control stick - stick for controlling aircraft surfaces
  • synonym: controller - a device used to control a machine or system
  • synonym: yoke - wheel-type control for aircraft surfaces
