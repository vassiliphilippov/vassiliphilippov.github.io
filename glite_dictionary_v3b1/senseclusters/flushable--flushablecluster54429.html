flushable

adjective
can be flushed
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈflʌʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be safely disposed of by flushing down a toilet

Examples

Flushable wipes
Make sure the packaging says flushable before disposing of wipes in the toilet.
Some products labeled as flushable can still cause plumbing problems if not used properly.
The hotel provides only flushable toilet paper to prevent clogged pipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biodegradable - able to be broken down naturally by bacteria
  • synonym: disposable - intended to be thrown away after use
  • antonym: nonflushable - not able to be safely flushed down a toilet
