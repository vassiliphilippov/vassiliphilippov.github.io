capitulum

noun
dense cluster of flowers or foliage
5.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈpɪtʃələm / (AmE), / kəˈpɪtjʊləm /; / kəˈpɪtʃjʊləm / (BrE)
a dense or compact head or cluster of small flowers, leaves, or fruiting bodies, especially at the top of a plant (as in daisies or sunflowers)
