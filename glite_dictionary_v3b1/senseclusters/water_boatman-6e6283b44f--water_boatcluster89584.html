water boatman

noun
aquatic bug with paddle-like legs
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌboʊtmən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌbəʊtmən / (BrE)
A small, carnivorous aquatic insect with flattened, paddle-like hind legs used for swimming.

Examples

A water boatman insect
A water boatman darts across the pond, searching for its next meal.
The rapid paddling motion of a water boatman is fascinating to watch.
We found a water boatman swimming just below the surface of the water.
