reinforced

adjective
strengthened by added support
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.ɪnˈfɔrst /; / ˌriːɪnˈfɔrst / (AmE), / ˌriː.ɪnˈfɔːst /; / ˌriːɪnˈfɔːst / (BrE)
