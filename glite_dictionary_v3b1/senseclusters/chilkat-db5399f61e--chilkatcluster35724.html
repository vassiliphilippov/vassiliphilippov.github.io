Chilkat

noun
Alaskan woven blanket
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪlkæt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlkæt / (BrE)
a traditional handwoven blanket or shawl made by Indigenous peoples of Alaska, often featuring complex patterns

Examples

A Chilkat blanket

She learned about the cultural significance of the Chilkat during her visit to Alaska.
Artists today still create Chilkat blankets using ancient weaving techniques.
