Examples

To swell up after injury

If you leave dried beans in water overnight, they will swell considerably.
The rain caused the wood to swell and warp.

Synonyms

  • distend
    8.2 0.84/m
    distend is a formal term for expanding because of internal pressure, while swell is more general and common in speech.
  • balloon
    4.7 1.67/m
    balloon means to grow or increase quickly like an inflating object, while swell can mean slow or gradual enlargement.
  • bulge
    2.9 0.92/m
    bulge means to stick out in a rounded shape from a surface, while swell covers general growth or rounding.
  • bloat
    4.9 1.13/m
    bloat means to become enlarged often from gas or fluid, while swell can result from many causes and in many places.
  • puff
    4.1 2.05/m
    puff means to swell slightly or to puff up in small, soft ways, while swell covers larger or heavier enlargement.
