Otto the Great

proper noun
German king and Holy Roman Emperor, 912–973
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑtoʊ ðə ɡreɪt / (AmE), / ˈɒtəʊ ðə ɡreɪt / (BrE)
(912–973) King of the Germans and Holy Roman Emperor noted for consolidating the German Reich and strengthening the Holy Roman Empire.

Examples

Otto the Great emperor
Otto the Great is remembered for unifying much of central Europe under his rule.
As Holy Roman Emperor, Otto the Great played a key role in shaping medieval European politics.
