GRF

sym
growth hormone abbreviation
Pronunciation: / dʒi ɑr ɛf / (AmE), / dʒiː ɑː ɛf / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'growth hormone-releasing factor', a hormone that stimulates the release of growth hormone from the pituitary gland

Examples

GRF hormone test
The endocrinologist measured the patient's GRF levels to assess pituitary function.
Researchers are studying how GRF affects growth in children with hormone deficiencies.
A deficiency in GRF can lead to reduced growth hormone production.

Related Terms
