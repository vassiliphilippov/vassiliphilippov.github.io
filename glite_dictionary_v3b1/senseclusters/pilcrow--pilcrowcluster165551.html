pilcrow

noun
paragraph symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlˌkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl.krəʊ /; / ˈpɪlkrəʊ / (BrE)
the typographical symbol (¶) used to show the start of a new paragraph

Examples

The pilcrow symbol
The editor added a pilcrow at the start of each new paragraph in the manuscript.
In proofreading, a pilcrow is often used to show where a paragraph should begin.
The symbol pilcrow is rarely seen outside of editing and word processing software.

She drew a pilcrow in the margin to remind herself where to start a new idea.
