pilcrow

noun
paragraph symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlˌkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪl.krəʊ /; / ˈpɪlkrəʊ / (BrE)
the typographical symbol (¶) used to show the start of a new paragraph

Examples
