overuse

noun
the excessive use of something
4.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.juːs/ /; / /ˈoʊ.vər.juːz/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.juːs/ /; / /ˈəʊ.və.juːz/ (BrE)
the act or process of using something too much, especially to the point where it becomes less effective or causes harm

Examples

The overuse of muscles
Physical overuse of joints can lead to chronic injuries.
Teachers warn against the overuse of smartphones in the classroom.
The overuse of antibiotics has contributed to drug-resistant bacteria.
