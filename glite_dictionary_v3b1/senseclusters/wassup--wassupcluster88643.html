wassup

interjection
informal greeting
Pronunciation: / wəˈsʌp / (AmE), / wəˈsʌp / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to ask how someone is or what they are doing

Examples

Wassup greeting
He walked into the room and said, 'wassup?' to everyone.
When she answered the phone, her friend greeted her with a cheerful 'wassup!'.
You can often hear teenagers saying 'wassup?' to each other after school.
