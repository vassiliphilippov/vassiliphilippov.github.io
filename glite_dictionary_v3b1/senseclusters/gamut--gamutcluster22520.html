gamut

noun
entire musical note range
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæm.ət / (AmE), / ˈɡæm.ət / (BrE)
the complete scale of musical notes; the entire range of musical sounds

Examples

Gamut of musical notes
Music students must learn to play scales that encompass the full gamut of octaves.
The piano was tuned to ensure that every note in the gamut was perfectly in pitch.
The singer's voice covered the entire gamut of notes from the lowest to the highest.

  • synonym: scale - an ordered sequence of musical notes
