death adder

noun
venomous Australian snake resembling an adder
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ ˌædər / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌadə / (BrE)
a highly venomous Australian snake that resembles an adder and is known for its stout body and rapid strike

Examples

The death adder snake
Bushwalkers in Australia are advised to watch out for the death adder in forested areas.
Despite its name, the death adder is not closely related to true adders found in Europe.
The death adder is notorious for its potent venom and camouflage among leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adder - a type of venomous snake, especially of the viper family
  • synonym: cobra - a large, venomous snake found in Africa and Asia
