Synonyms

  • absorptive
    6.8 0.03/m
    absorptive appears in technical contexts and sounds formal, while absorbent is the common everyday adjective for the same property.
  • spongy
    2.2 0.5/m
    spongy is soft and porous and soaks up then releases water, while absorbent emphasizes holding liquids rather than releasing them.
  • spongelike
    4.9 0.01/m
    spongelike describes a texture that acts like a sponge with liquids, while absorbent says the material actually takes in and keeps liquid.
