shojo

noun
Japanese girls' genre
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊdʒoʊ / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊdʒəʊ / (BrE)
a genre of Japanese comics or animation aimed mainly at young girls, often featuring romance and friendship themes

Examples

Shojo manga series
Many fans of shojo appreciate the emotional depth and character development in the stories.
The bookstore has a special section dedicated to shojo manga and graphic novels.
She started watching shojo anime when she was in middle school and still enjoys it today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anime - Japanese animated films or television shows
  • synonym: manga - Japanese comic books or graphic novels
  • antonym: shonen - a genre of Japanese comics or animation aimed at young boys
