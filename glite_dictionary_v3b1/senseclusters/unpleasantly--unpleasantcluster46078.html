unpleasantly

adverb
in an unpleasant way
2.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈplɛzəntli / (AmE), / ʌnˈplɛzəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is not enjoyable, agreeable, or attractive; in an unpleasant manner.

Examples

To be unpleasantly surprised
He was unpleasantly surprised by their reaction to his idea.
She smiled unpleasantly when she heard the bad news.
The weather turned unpleasantly cold after sunset.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant manner
