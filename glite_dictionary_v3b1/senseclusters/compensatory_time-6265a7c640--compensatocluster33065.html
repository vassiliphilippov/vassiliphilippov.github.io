compensatory time

noun
time off in exchange for overtime worked
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛnsəˌtɔri taɪm / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛnsət(ə)ri taɪm / (BrE)
Paid time off granted to an employee instead of extra pay for working overtime.

Examples

Compensatory time off
After working through the weekend, she requested compensatory time to take a day off.
Many organizations use compensatory time instead of overtime pay for salaried staff.
The company offers compensatory time to employees who work beyond their regular hours.
