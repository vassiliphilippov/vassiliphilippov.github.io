angsty

adjective
full of anxiety
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋsti / (AmE), / ˈæŋsti / (BrE)
feeling or showing anxiety, worry, or emotional turmoil, especially in a restless or troubled way

Examples

An angsty teenager
The main character in the novel is an angsty teenager struggling with family issues.
His angsty lyrics resonated with many young listeners who felt misunderstood.
She wrote in her journal about her angsty feelings during her first year at college.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxious - feeling worried or uneasy
  • antonym: calm - not feeling nervous or worried
