plumbing fixture

noun
device for water distribution/use in buildings
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmɪŋ ˈfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌmɪŋ ˈfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
a device installed in water systems of a building, such as a sink, toilet, or faucet, used for distributing and using water

Examples

A plumbing fixture leak
A leaking plumbing fixture can waste a significant amount of water if not repaired promptly.
Inspectors checked every plumbing fixture before the building was approved for occupancy.
The bathroom was renovated with all new plumbing fixtures for improved efficiency.
