closed

adjective
not open (especially eyes, mouth)
1.0
Pronunciation: / kloʊzd / (AmE), / kləʊzd / (BrE)
not open; shut or blocked so that entry, passage, or view is prevented (used for doors, windows, eyes, etc.)

Examples

The museum doors remained closed during the renovation period.
The store was already closed when we arrived at nine o'clock.
Please keep the door closed while the air conditioning is on.
Several roads were closed after the heavy snowfall.
The library remained closed during the holidays due to renovations.
