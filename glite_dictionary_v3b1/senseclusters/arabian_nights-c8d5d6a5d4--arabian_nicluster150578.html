Arabian Nights

proper noun
collection of Middle Eastern folktales
5.6
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪ.bi.ən ˈnaɪts / (AmE), / əˈreɪ.bi.ən ˈnaɪts / (BrE)
A famous collection of Middle Eastern and South Asian folktales, originally written in Arabic during the 10th century, also known as 'One Thousand and One Nights'.

Examples

The Arabian Nights stories
As a child, I loved reading stories from the Arabian Nights before bed.
Many films and books have drawn inspiration from the magical tales of the Arabian Nights.
The Arabian Nights have captivated readers around the world for centuries with their adventurous plots.
