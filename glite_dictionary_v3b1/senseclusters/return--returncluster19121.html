return

verb
bring or take back to original place
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˈtɝn /; / rɪˈtɝn / (AmE), / ɹɪˈtɜːn /; / rɪˈtɜːn / (BrE)
to give, send, or bring something back to its original owner, place, or condition
