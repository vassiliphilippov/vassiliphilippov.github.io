Synonyms

  • give back
    1.0 14/m
    give back means handing something to its original owner after borrowing or use, while return is the general act of giving something back.
  • bring back
    1.7 11/m
    bring back means moving someone or something to the place they started from, while return is a broader word for giving something back.
  • send back
    1.5 3.25/m
    send back means sending an item to the place or person it came from, while return covers any act of giving something back.
  • pass-back
    3.4 0.08/m
    pass-back means handing an item back to its previous holder in a quick handover, while return is the general act of giving something back.
  • put back
    1.0 15/m
    put back means placing an object again in its original position, while return covers restoring an item to its owner or place.
  • take back
    2.1 14/m
    take back means to remove something and return it to where it came from, while return is the general word for giving something back.
