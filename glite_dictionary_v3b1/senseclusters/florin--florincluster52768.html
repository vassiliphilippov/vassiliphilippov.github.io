florin

noun
old two-shilling coin
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔrɪn / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪn / (BrE)
a former British coin worth two shillings, or a similar coin used in other countries

Examples

British florin coin

Collectors often search for a florin from the Victorian era to complete their sets.
The museum displayed a florin alongside other historical British coins.
