perfectly

adverb
in a flawless or complete way
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝː.fɪkt.li / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.fɪkt.li / (BrE)
In a perfect or faultless manner; without error or flaw.

Examples

Perfectly done
He perfectly captured the essence of the character in his performance.
She answered every question perfectly during the interview.
The cake was baked perfectly—not too dry and not too moist.

Related Terms
