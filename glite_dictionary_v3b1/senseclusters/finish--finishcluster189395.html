finish

noun
downfall or ruin of someone/something
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
the downfall or ruin of someone or something; a point at which someone is defeated or destroyed, especially in a conflict or struggle

Examples

His finish in business
His reckless decisions ultimately brought about his finish.
Many believed that his addiction would be the finish of him.
The match was a fight to the finish between the two rivals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: end - the final point of something in time or space
  • synonym: ruin - complete destruction or downfall
  • antonym: victory - success in defeating an opponent or enemy
