bunghole

noun
hole in a barrel or cask
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋˌhəʊl / (BrE)
A hole in a barrel or cask through which it can be filled or emptied.

Examples

Barrel bunghole
After cleaning the cask, she inspected the bunghole for leaks.
The brewer removed the bunghole stopper to fill the barrel with ale.
Wine is often poured into the barrel through the bunghole during production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bung - a stopper for closing the bunghole of a cask or barrel
