intercessory

adjective
praying for others
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈsɛsəri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈsɛsəri / (BrE)
relating to intervening, pleading, or praying on behalf of someone else

Examples

Intercessory prayer
The community gathered for an intercessory prayer meeting to support those in need.
She offered intercessory prayers for her friend who was facing surgery.
Many religious traditions include intercessory rituals to seek help for others.
Her intercessory actions helped resolve the dispute between the two departments.
The manager appreciated his intercessory role in calming the tense negotiations.
Without her intercessory support, the agreement might never have been reached.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocative - acting in support of someone or something
  • synonym: intervening - becoming involved to change a situation
  • synonym: mediating - acting to resolve differences between parties
  • synonym: mediatory - serving as a mediator or go-between
