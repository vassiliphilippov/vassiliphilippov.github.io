square meter

noun
unit of area (metric system)
1.0 1.08/m
Pronunciation: / skwÉ›r ËˆmiËtÉš /; / skwÉ›r ËˆmitÉš / (AmE), / skwÉ›É™ ËˆmiËtÉ™ /; / skwÉ›Ë ËˆmiËtÉ™ / (BrE)
a unit of area equal to the area of a square with sides measuring one meter; used in the metric system

Examples

One square meter
One square meter of fabric was needed to make the dress.
Prices are often listed per square meter when buying carpet.
The apartment has a total area of 85 square meters.
The garden is approximately one hundred square metres.
The room measures twelve square metres in size.
They bought a roll of carpet that covers fifty square metres.

Synonyms

  • centare
    9.9 0.01/m
