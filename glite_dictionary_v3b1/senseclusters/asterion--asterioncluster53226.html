asterion

noun
craniometric point on the skull
7.8
Pronunciation: / əˈstɪriˌɑn / (AmE), / əˈstɪəriən / (BrE)
the point on the human skull where the lambdoid, occipitomastoid, and parietomastoid sutures meet

Examples

The asterion point

The location of the asterion can vary slightly between individuals.
