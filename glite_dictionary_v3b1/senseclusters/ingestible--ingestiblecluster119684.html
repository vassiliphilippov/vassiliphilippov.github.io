ingestible

adjective
safe to eat
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɛstəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɛstɪbəl / (BrE)
able to be safely eaten or swallowed

Examples

Ingestible capsule
The doctor confirmed that the capsule is completely ingestible and will dissolve in the stomach.
Always check if a plant is ingestible before eating it.
Some packaging materials are designed to be ingestible for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edible - safe or suitable to be eaten
  • antonym: inedible - not safe or suitable to be eaten
