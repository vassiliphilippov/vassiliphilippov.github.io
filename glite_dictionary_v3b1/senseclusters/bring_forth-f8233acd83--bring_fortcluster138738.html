(formal or literary) To give birth to or produce (offspring) by reproduction.

Examples

To bring forth a child
According to the legend, the ancient queen brought forth twins destined to rule the land.
She dreams of the day she will bring forth her own children.
The mare brought forth a healthy foal in the early morning.
