tad nervous

adjective
slightly anxious; a little nervous
Pronunciation: / tæd ˈnɜrvəs / (AmE), / tæd ˈnɜːvəs / (BrE)
slightly or a little anxious; experiencing a small amount of nervousness

Examples

A tad nervous feeling
He was tad nervous about meeting his new boss for the first time.
I always get a tad nervous before giving a presentation.
She sounded just a tad nervous on the phone, but otherwise was calm.
