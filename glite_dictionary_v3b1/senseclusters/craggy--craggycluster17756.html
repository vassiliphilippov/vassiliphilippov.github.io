craggy

adjective
rough, uneven and full of crags
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkræɡi / (AmE), / ˈkræɡi / (BrE)
(of a landscape or surface) having many crags; rough and uneven.

Examples

Craggy mountain face
His craggy features gave him a rugged, distinguished appearance.
The craggy cliffs rose sharply above the crashing waves.
We hiked through craggy hills dotted with wildflowers and boulders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rugged - having a rough, uneven surface or character
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface, free from perceptible projections, lumps, or indentations
