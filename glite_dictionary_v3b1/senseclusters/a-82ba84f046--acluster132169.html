A

noun
vitamin essential for vision and health
2.8
Pronunciation: /eɪ/ (AmE), /eɪ/ (BrE)
any of several fat-soluble vitamins essential for normal vision and the maintenance of healthy skin and immune function, commonly known as vitamin A.

Examples

Vitamin A deficiency
A deficiency of A may lead to night blindness and dry eyes.
Carrots are a good source of A, especially beneficial for growing children.
Doctors recommend including foods rich in A in your diet to keep your vision healthy.

Related Terms
