terrorist cell

noun
small, covert group of terrorists
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrəˌrɪst sɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɛrərɪst sɛl / (BrE)
a small, covert group of terrorists who operate independently within a larger organization, typically consisting of a few members

Examples

A terrorist cell group
Each terrorist cell operates independently to avoid detection by authorities.
New intelligence reports revealed the existence of multiple terrorist cells across the region.
The police uncovered a terrorist cell planning an attack in the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell - a small group forming a secretive unit within a larger organization, especially for subversive or criminal purposes
