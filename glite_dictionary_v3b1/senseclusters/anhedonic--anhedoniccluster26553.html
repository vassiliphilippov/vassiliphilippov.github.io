anhedonic

adjective
unable to feel pleasure
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.hɪˈdɑː.nɪk / (AmE), / ˌæn.hɪˈdɒn.ɪk / (BrE)
unable to feel pleasure or enjoyment from activities usually found enjoyable

Examples

Anhedonic mood
After months of feeling anhedonic, she realized she needed to seek help for her depression.
The patient described his mood as anhedonic, unable to enjoy music or hobbies.
Therapists often ask if someone feels anhedonic when diagnosing mood disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depressed - feeling very sad and without hope
  • antonym: joyful - feeling or showing great happiness
