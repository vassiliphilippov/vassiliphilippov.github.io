in the wind

x
widely rumored or imminent
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə wɪnd / (AmE), / ɪn ðə wɪnd / (BrE)
if something is in the wind, it is being discussed, rumored, or is likely to happen soon.

Examples

Change in the wind
Excitement was in the wind as the team prepared for the final match.
Rumors of a new policy were already in the wind before the announcement.
There are big changes in the wind at the company this year.
