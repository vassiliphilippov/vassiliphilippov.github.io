little hand

noun
a small or childlike hand
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬l hænd / (AmE), / ˈlɪtl hand / (BrE)
a small hand, especially that of a child or someone regarded with affection or innocence

Examples

A child's little hand
His little hand barely reached the top of the kitchen counter.
She wrapped her little hand around her father's finger for comfort.
With a little hand wave, the boy said goodbye to his friends.
