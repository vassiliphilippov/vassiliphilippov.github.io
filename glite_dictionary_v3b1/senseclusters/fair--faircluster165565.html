fair

verb
join surfaces to blend smoothly
Pronunciation: / fɛr / (AmE), / feə / (BrE)
to join or shape parts so that their external surfaces blend smoothly or form a continuous line or curve

Examples

To fair a surface
Before painting, you need to fair the joint by sanding the surfaces until they blend.
The craftsman took care to fair the wooden panels so the deck looked seamless.
They used filler material to fair the hull, eliminating all unevenness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - to mix or combine together
