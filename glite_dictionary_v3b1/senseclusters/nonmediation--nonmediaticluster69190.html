nonmediation

noun
lack of mediation
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌmiːdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the absence of mediation or intervention in a process or dispute

Examples

No nonmediation involved
The conflict was settled by nonmediation, with both parties reaching an agreement on their own.
Some organizations prefer nonmediation to encourage direct communication between disputing sides.
The policy encourages nonmediation in minor workplace disagreements to promote independence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intervention - the act of becoming involved in a situation to influence it
  • antonym: mediation - the process of intervening to resolve a dispute
