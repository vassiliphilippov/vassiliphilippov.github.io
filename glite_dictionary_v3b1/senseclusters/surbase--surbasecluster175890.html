surbase

noun
molding above the base of a structure
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːˌbeɪs / (BrE)
A molding or border situated above the base of a structure such as a pedestal, podium, or wall.

Examples

A surbase molding
A marble surbase separates the wall from the ornate frieze above.
Restorers carefully cleaned the old paint from the surbase to reveal its original design.
The surbase was decorated with intricate carvings that complemented the column's base.
