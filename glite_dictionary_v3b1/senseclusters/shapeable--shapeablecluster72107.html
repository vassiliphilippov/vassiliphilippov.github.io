shapeable

adjective
easily influenced
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be influenced, changed, or formed, especially in a non-physical or figurative sense.

Examples

Shapeable minds
Children's beliefs are often highly shapeable by their environment and experiences.
The company's culture is still shapeable as it is in its early stages.
Her opinions on the subject proved to be more shapeable than expected.
