color

noun
visual property relating to hue and light
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌ.lər/ /; / /ˈkɔː.lər/ (AmE), /ˈkʌ.lə/ (BrE)
the visual quality of something as seen by the eye, such as red, blue, green, etc.

Examples
