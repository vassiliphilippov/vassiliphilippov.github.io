watch chain

noun
chain for attaching a pocket watch
Pronunciation: / wɑtʃ tʃeɪn / (AmE), / wɒtʃ tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a short chain or ribbon used to attach a pocket watch to a vest or garment

Examples

A watch chain on vest
He wore a gold watch chain across his waistcoat, giving him a distinguished look.
Her grandfather's watch chain was passed down as a cherished family heirloom.
The antique dealer found an old watch chain in the drawer.
