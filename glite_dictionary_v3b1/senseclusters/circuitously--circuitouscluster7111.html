circuitously

adverb
indirectly
Pronunciation: / sərˈkjuːɪtəsli / (AmE), / səˈkjuːɪtəsli / (BrE)
in a roundabout or indirect way, not going straight to the point

Examples

Answer circuitously
She answered the question circuitously, never giving a clear response.
The road wound circuitously through the mountains, making the journey much longer.
He circuitously brought up the topic, hoping not to offend anyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: directly - in a straight or clear way
  • synonym: indirectly - not in a direct way
