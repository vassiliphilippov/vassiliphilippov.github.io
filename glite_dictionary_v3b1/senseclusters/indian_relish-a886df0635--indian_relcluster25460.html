Indian relish

noun
spicy condiment with fruits or vegetables
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈrɛlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈrɛlɪʃ / (BrE)
A spicy condiment made from chopped fruits or vegetables, cooked in vinegar and sugar with ginger and spices.

Examples

Homemade Indian relish
He made homemade Indian relish using mangoes, vinegar, and spices last weekend.
She added a spoonful of Indian relish to her curry to enhance the flavor.
The table was set with various pickles and Indian relish for the guests to try.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chutney - a spicy condiment of Indian origin made with fruits, vegetables, and spices
