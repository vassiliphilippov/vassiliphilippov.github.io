ipsilaterally

adverb
on same side
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪpsɪˈlætərəli / (AmE), / ˌɪpsɪˈlætərəli / (BrE)
on or affecting the same side of the body as something else

Examples

Move ipsilaterally in body
The muscle contracted ipsilaterally in response to the nerve stimulation.
Symptoms appeared ipsilaterally after the patient suffered a minor stroke.
The doctor noted that the reflexes were stronger ipsilaterally than on the opposite side.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contralaterally - on or affecting the opposite side of the body
