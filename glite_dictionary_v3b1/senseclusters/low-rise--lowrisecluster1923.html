low-rise

adjective
clothing that sits low on the hips
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.raɪz/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.raɪz/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.raɪz/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.raɪz/ (BrE)
(of clothing, especially trousers or jeans) designed to sit low on the hips or below the natural waistline.

Examples

Low-rise jeans
She preferred low-rise jeans because they felt more comfortable with her cropped tops.
The store stocked both high-waisted and low-rise trousers for customers with different styles.
Designers often bring back low-rise silhouettes as part of nostalgic fashion cycles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: high-rise - (of clothing) designed to sit high at or above the natural waistline; opposite in waist position.
  • synonym: low-waisted - of clothing: having a waistline that sits low on the hips; similar in meaning.
