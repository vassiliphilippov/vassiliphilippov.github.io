proportion

noun
harmonious relationship of parts within a whole
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɔrʃən / (AmE), / prəˈpɔːʃən / (BrE)
a harmonious or pleasing balance or arrangement of parts within a whole

Examples

Perfect proportion in art
Classical sculptures are admired for the proportion and balance of their forms.
Many artists study the proportion of the human body to improve their figure drawings.
The proportion of the columns to the roof created a sense of harmony in the building's facade.
Good design often depends on the right proportion between form and function.
The architect admired the proportion of the building’s columns to its roof.
The artist studied the proportion of the model’s features before starting her portrait.

  • antonym: discord - lack of harmony among elements or parts
  • antonym: imbalance - lack of proportion or equilibrium
  • synonym: symmetry - exact similarity between two halves or parts
