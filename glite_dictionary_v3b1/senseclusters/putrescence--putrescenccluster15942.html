putrescence

noun
state of rotting or decay
9.7
Pronunciation: / pjuˈtrɛsəns / (AmE), / pjuːˈtrɛsns /; / pjuˈtrɛsəns / (BrE)
the process or state of decaying and rotting, especially with a foul smell

Examples

The biologist studied the process of putrescence in decaying plant material.
Within days, the putrescence of the meat became unbearable to those nearby.
