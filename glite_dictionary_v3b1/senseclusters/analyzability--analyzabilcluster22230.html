analyzability

noun
ability to be analyzed
Pronunciation: / əˌnæləzəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / əˌnælɪzəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be analyzed or broken down into parts for study or understanding

Examples

The analyzability of data
The analyzability of the data allowed researchers to draw clear conclusions.
Teachers appreciate the analyzability of well-structured essays when grading assignments.
The analyzability of this process makes it easier to identify areas for improvement.

  • synonym: divisibility - the quality of being able to be divided
