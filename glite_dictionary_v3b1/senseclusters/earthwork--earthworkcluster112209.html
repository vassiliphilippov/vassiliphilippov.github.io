earthwork

noun
defensive wall made of earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɝːθˌwɝːk /; / ˈɜrθˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a large wall, bank, or mound made of earth, built by people for defense, construction, or drainage

Examples

  • synonym: embankment - a raised structure of earth or stone built to hold back water or support a road
  • synonym: fortification - a defensive wall or other military structure
  • synonym: rampart - a defensive wall or bank around a fort
