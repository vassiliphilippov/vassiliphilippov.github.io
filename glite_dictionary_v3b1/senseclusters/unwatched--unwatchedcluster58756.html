unwatched

adjective
not viewed
3.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɑtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒtʃt / (BrE)
not seen or viewed by anyone or by very few people, especially referring to films, shows, or videos.

Examples

Unwatched movie
The independent film remained unwatched for months until it was featured on a popular streaming service.
Many educational videos online go unwatched despite their high quality and useful content.
The security footage was left unwatched overnight, raising concerns about safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ignored - not given attention or notice
  • antonym: popular - liked or enjoyed by many people
