call-out

noun
challenge to a fight or confrontation
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔl aʊt / (AmE), / ˈkɔːl aʊt / (BrE)
an instance of publicly challenging someone to a fight, contest, or confrontation

Examples

A call-out challenge
After weeks of tension, the call-out finally resulted in a heated confrontation.
Celebrity feuds often start with a dramatic call-out on social media.
The wrestler issued a call-out to anyone brave enough to face him in the ring.
