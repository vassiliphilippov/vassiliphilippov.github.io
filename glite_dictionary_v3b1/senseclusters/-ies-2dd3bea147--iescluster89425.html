-ies

x
verb suffix for -y
Pronunciation: / iz / (AmE), / iz / (BrE)
a suffix used to form the third-person singular present tense of verbs ending in -y, changing -y to -ies

Examples

Studies and carries
She studies every night to prepare for her exams.
He copies the notes from the board before class ends.
The machine carries heavy loads across the factory floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -s - a suffix used to form the third-person singular present tense of most verbs
