Epirus

proper noun
ancient region in Greece and Albania
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpaɪrəs / (AmE), / ɪˈpaɪərəs / (BrE)
an ancient region located on the Ionian Sea, encompassing parts of northwestern Greece and southern Albania, that flourished as a kingdom in the 3rd century BC.

Examples

Epirus region
Archaeological discoveries in Epirus have shed light on ancient Greek civilization.
Many battles between Greeks and Romans occurred in what is now known as Epirus.
The kingdom of Epirus was home to the famous leader Pyrrhus.
