remains

noun
leftover objects or material
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmeɪnz / (AmE), / rɪˈmeɪnz / (BrE)
objects or material left after the rest has been used, consumed, or removed.

Examples

Burned remains
After the storm, only the remains of the old barn could be seen.
He fed the remains of his lunch to the birds in the park.
She cleared the remains of the picnic from the grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leftovers - food remaining after a meal
  • synonym: scraps - small pieces left after something is removed
