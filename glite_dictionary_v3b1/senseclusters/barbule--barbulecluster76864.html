barbule

noun
feather filament
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrbjuːl / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbjuːl / (BrE)
a tiny filament that branches from the barbs of a feather and helps hold the feather's structure together

Examples

A barbule on feather
The scientist examined the barbule under a microscope to study its structure.
Each feather contains hundreds of barbules that interlock to keep it smooth.
Damage to a bird's barbules can affect its ability to fly efficiently.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: barb - a main branch of a feather's shaft
  • synonym: filament - a slender threadlike structure
