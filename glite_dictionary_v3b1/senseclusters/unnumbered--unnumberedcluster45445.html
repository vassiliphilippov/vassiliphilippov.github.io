unnumbered

adjective
without numbers
3.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnʌmbərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈnʌmbəd / (BrE)
not marked or identified with a number.

Examples

Unnumbered pages
The introduction was printed on several unnumbered pages at the front of the book.
Some of the tickets were unnumbered, making it difficult to keep track of them.
He noticed that the appendix section was completely unnumbered in the report.

Related Terms

  • antonym: numbered - marked or identified with a number
