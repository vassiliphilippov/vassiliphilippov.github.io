class Echinoidea

noun
class of marine animals: sea urchins, sand dollars
9.6
Pronunciation: / klæs ˌɛk.ɪˈnɔɪ.di.ə / (AmE), / klɑːs ˌɛk.ɪˈnɔɪ.di.ə / (BrE)
