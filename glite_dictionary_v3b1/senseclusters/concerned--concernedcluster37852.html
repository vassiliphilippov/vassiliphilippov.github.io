concerned

adjective
worried or showing care
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɝnd / (AmE), / kənˈsɜːnd / (BrE)
worried or anxious about something; showing care or solicitude for someone or something.

Examples

Concerned parents
Her parents looked concerned when she came home late.
Many citizens are concerned about the rising cost of living.
The teacher was concerned about her student's declining grades.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxious - worried or uneasy about something with an uncertain outcome
  • antonym: unconcerned - not worried or not interested
