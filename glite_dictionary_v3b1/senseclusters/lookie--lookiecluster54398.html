lookie

interjection
informal attention call
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊki / (AmE), / ˈlʊki / (BrE)
used informally to draw someone's attention to something, similar to 'look' or 'see'.

Examples

Lookie here
Hey, lookie, there is a rainbow outside the window!
Lookie, your favorite band is playing on TV right now.
Mom, lookie, I drew a picture of our family!

Related Terms

  • synonym: look - used to direct attention to something
  • synonym: see - used to encourage someone to notice something
