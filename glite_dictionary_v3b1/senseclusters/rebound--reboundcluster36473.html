rebound

noun
emotional recovery after a setback
9.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈbaʊnd/ /; / /rɪ.ˈbaʊnd/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈbaʊnd/ /; / /riː.ˈbaʊnd/ (BrE)
a period or process of reacting emotionally, especially recovering, after a setback, crisis, or frustration, often following the end of a relationship

Examples

After his divorce, he dated several people while still on the rebound.
Being on the rebound can make people act impulsively.
She made some major life decisions on the rebound from losing her job.
