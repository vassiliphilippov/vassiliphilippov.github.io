remote-control bomb

noun
bomb detonated by remote control
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊt kənˈtroʊl bɑm / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt kənˈtrəʊl bɒm / (BrE)
a bomb that can be detonated from a distance using a remote-control device

Examples

A remote-control bomb device
Security forces safely defused a remote-control bomb before it could be detonated.
Terrorists used a remote-control bomb to target the convoy.
The police discovered a remote-control bomb hidden under the ambassador's car.
