snowplow

verb
to hit with snowplow
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊˌplaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊˌplaʊ / (BrE)
to hit, crush, or drive over someone or something with a snowplow or a vehicle fitted with a plow

Examples

To snowplow a mailbox
The truck accidentally snowplowed a mailbox while clearing the street after the storm.
Several parked cars were snowplowed when the contractor misjudged the driveway's edge.
If you are not careful, you might snowplow the curb while driving in heavy snow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: avoid - to keep away from hitting or touching
  • synonym: plow - to move or forcefully drive through something
