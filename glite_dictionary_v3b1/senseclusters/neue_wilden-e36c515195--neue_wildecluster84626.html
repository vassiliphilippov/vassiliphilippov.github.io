Neue Wilden

noun
German neo-expressionist art movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔɪ.ə ˈvɪl.dən / (AmE), / ˈnɔɪ.ə ˈvɪl.dən / (BrE)
A group of German neo-expressionist artists active in the late 1970s and 1980s, noted for their vibrant colors and energetic brushwork.

Examples

Neue Wilden artists
Many galleries in Berlin feature works by artists associated with the Neue Wilden.
She wrote her thesis on the influence of the Neue Wilden on contemporary European art.
The Neue Wilden revitalized German painting in the 1980s with their bold use of color and emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neo-expressionism - Art movement characterized by expressive brushwork and vivid colors
