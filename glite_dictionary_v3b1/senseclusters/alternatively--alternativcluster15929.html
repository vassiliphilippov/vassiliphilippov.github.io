alternatively

adverb
another possible option or choice
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈtɜrnətɪvli / (AmE), / ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪvli / (BrE)
used to introduce another possible option or choice to consider instead.

Examples

Used alternatively to suggest
If you don't want to go to the concert, alternatively, we can see a movie instead.
The hotel restaurant is closed; alternatively, there are plenty of cafés nearby.
You can take a taxi to the station, or, alternatively, you could walk if the weather is nice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instead - in place of something else
