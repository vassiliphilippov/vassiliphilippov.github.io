unextraordinary

adjective
not remarkable
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪkˈstrɔːrdəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪkˈstrɔːdnri / (BrE)
not special, unusual, or remarkable in any way; ordinary or typical

Examples

An unextraordinary day
Despite the hype, the restaurant's food was unextraordinary and did not stand out.
Her performance was competent but ultimately unextraordinary compared to the other contestants.
The book was well-written but rather unextraordinary, offering nothing new to the genre.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extraordinary - very unusual or remarkable
  • synonym: ordinary - not special or different; normal
  • synonym: unremarkable - not worthy of attention; not unusual
