Jainist

noun
follower of Jainism
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪnɪst / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or follows Jainism, an ancient Indian religion that emphasizes non-violence and asceticism

Examples

A devoted Jainist
As a Jainist, she strictly follows the practice of non-violence in her daily life.
He became interested in the teachings of a Jainist monk while traveling in India.
Many Jainists celebrate Mahavir Jayanti as a major religious festival.
