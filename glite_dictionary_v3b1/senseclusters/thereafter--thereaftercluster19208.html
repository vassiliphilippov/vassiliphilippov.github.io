thereafter

adverb
from that time onwards
Pronunciation: / ðɛrˈæf.tɚ / (AmE), / ðeəˈrɑːf.tə / (BrE)
from that time onwards; after the time mentioned

Examples

From thereafter time
He graduated in 2002 and thereafter moved to London for work.
The law was passed in 1990; thereafter, all businesses had to comply.
They divorced in March and saw little of each other thereafter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: afterwards - at a later or subsequent time; after something else has happened
  • antonym: beforehand - before a particular event or time
