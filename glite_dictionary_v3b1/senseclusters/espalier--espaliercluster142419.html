espalier

verb
to train flat growth
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈspæl.jər/ /; / /ɪ.ˈspæl.jər/ /; / /e.ˈspæl.jər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈspæl.ɪə/ /; / /ə.ˈspæl.ɪə/ (BrE)
to train a tree or shrub to grow flat against a wall or support by tying and pruning its branches.

Examples

To espalier a tree
They had espaliered the pear trees against the brick wall to save space.
The gardener espaliers apple trees every spring to create a decorative pattern.
She learned how to espalier fruit trees to maximize sunlight and improve their yield.

Related Terms
