telangiectatic

adjective
with blood-filled spaces
Pronunciation: / tɛˌlændʒiˈɛktətɪk / (AmE), / tɛˌlændʒiˈɛktətɪk / (BrE)
describing a tumor or lesion that contains large, blood-filled spaces or cavities

Examples

Telangiectatic tumor
The doctor identified a telangiectatic lesion on the patient's skin during the examination.
A telangiectatic tumor often appears red or purple due to the presence of blood-filled spaces.
Pathologists noted the telangiectatic nature of the growth after reviewing the biopsy results.
