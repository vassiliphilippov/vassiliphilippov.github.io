miterwort

noun
herb of the genus Mitella
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪtərˌwɔrt /; / ˈmaɪtərˌwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈmaɪtəˌwɜːt /; / ˈmɪtrəwɜːt / (BrE)
Any of several perennial herbs of the genus Mitella, with capsules that resemble a bishop's miter.
