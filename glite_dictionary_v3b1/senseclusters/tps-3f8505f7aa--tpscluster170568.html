TPS

noun
business transaction system
Pronunciation: / ti pi ɛs / (AmE), / tiː piː ɛs / (BrE)
a computer system that records and processes routine business transactions such as sales, payments, or reservations

Examples

TPS software system
Many companies use a TPS to handle daily sales and inventory updates efficiently.
A reliable TPS is essential for processing thousands of transactions in large retail stores.
The bank upgraded its TPS to ensure faster and more secure payment processing.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: MIS - management information system, a system for managing and analyzing business information
  • synonym: transaction processing system - a system for recording and processing business transactions
