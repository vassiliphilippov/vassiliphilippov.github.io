unlink

verb
to remove a connection
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪŋk / (BrE)
to remove a connection or association between things, such as accounts, files, or links

Examples

If you unlink this file, it will no longer be accessible from the directory.

You should unlink your old account before creating a new one.
