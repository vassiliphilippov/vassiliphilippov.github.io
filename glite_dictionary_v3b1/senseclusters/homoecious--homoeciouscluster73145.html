homoecious

adjective
parasite or fungus: entire life cycle on one host
9.9
Pronunciation: /hoʊ.moʊ.ˈiː.ʃəs/ /; / /hə.moʊ.ˈiː.ʃəs/ (AmE), /həʊ.mə.ˈiː.ʃəs/ /; / /hɒ.mə.ˈiː.ʃəs/ (BrE)
(of parasites, especially rust fungi) completing the entire life cycle on a single host.

Examples

Homoecious rust fungi
Many rust fungi are homoecious, completing all stages of their development on a single plant species.
She studied homoecious and heteroecious fungi to compare their life cycles.
The homoecious nature of this parasite simplifies control strategies in agriculture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heteroecious - (of parasites/fungi) requiring two or more different host species to complete the life cycle
