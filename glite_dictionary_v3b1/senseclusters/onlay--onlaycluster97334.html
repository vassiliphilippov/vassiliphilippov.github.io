onlay

noun
decorative surface piece
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːn.leɪ/ /; / /ˈɔːn.leɪ/ (AmE), /ˈɒn.leɪ/ (BrE)
a decorative or protective piece applied to the surface of furniture or objects to ornament or cover part of it

Examples

Decorative onlay on furniture
The craftsman glued a wooden onlay to the front of the drawer for added detail.
Restorers used a gold leaf onlay to repair the damaged frame of the painting.
A decorative onlay can transform a plain cabinet into an elegant piece of furniture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inlay - a decorative design set into the surface of an object
  • synonym: overlay - a layer or covering placed over something else
