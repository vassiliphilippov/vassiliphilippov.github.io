nip

noun
sharp or spicy flavor
Pronunciation: / nɪp / (AmE), / nɪp / (BrE)
a small, sharp sensation, bite, or snip (such as a taste, a feeling from cold, or a quick bite)

Examples

A cold nip
The cheese left a slight nip on the tongue, followed by a creamy aftertaste.
The salad dressing has just the right nip to complement fresh greens.
There's a pleasant nip to the sauce that makes it unforgettable.
A sudden nip from the cold wind made him shiver.
He felt a slight nip when the nurse cleaned the wound.
The puppy gave her finger a playful nip as she tried to pet it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bite - an act of biting
  • antonym: blandness - lack of strong flavor
  • synonym: snip - a small or quick cut, especially with scissors
  • synonym: tang - a strong taste, flavor, or smell
