cut away

verb
remove by cutting off or away
2.2
Pronunciation: / kʌt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / kʌt əˈweɪ / (BrE)

Examples

To cut away dead wood

Rescue workers had to cut away metal to free the trapped driver.
The tailor will cut away the excess fabric before sewing the hem.
