trashy

adjective
cheap and gaudy; lacking style or taste
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtræʃi / (AmE), / ˈtræʃi / (BrE)
cheap, of poor quality, and lacking in style, value, or good taste

Examples

A trashy movie
He wore a trashy suit that caught everyone's attention for the wrong reasons.
She thought the decorations looked trashy with all that glitter and fake gold.
The movie's trashy special effects couldn't hide its lack of substance.
Many people consider those gossip magazines to be trashy and not worth reading.
She threw away the trashy souvenirs she bought on her trip because they broke so quickly.
The store sells a lot of trashy jewelry that looks nice but falls apart easily.
