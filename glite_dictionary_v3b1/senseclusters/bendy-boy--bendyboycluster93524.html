bendy-boy

noun
very flexible or acrobatic boy/person
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛndi ˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbɛndi ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy or person, especially a male, who is very flexible or acrobatic

Examples

The bendy-boy performer
As a child, he was called the bendy-boy because he could touch his toes to his head.
The bendy-boy stunned the audience with his contortionist moves on stage.
They hired a bendy-boy performer for the circus’s acrobatic show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contortionist - a person who can bend their body into unusual shapes
