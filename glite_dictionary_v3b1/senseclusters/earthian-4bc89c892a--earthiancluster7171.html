Earthian

adjective
of or relating to Earth
Pronunciation: / ˈɜːrθiən / (AmE), / ˈɜːθiən / (BrE)
relating to the planet Earth, its inhabitants, or characteristics associated with Earth

Examples

Earthian culture
The science fiction novel described an Earthian ambassador meeting with alien leaders.
Researchers studied the Earthian atmosphere to compare it with that of other planets.
The spacecraft carried messages in several Earthian languages for any intelligent life it might encounter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extraterrestrial - originating or existing outside the Earth
  • synonym: terrestrial - relating to the Earth or land
