downrange

adverb
toward firing direction
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌreɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌreɪndʒ / (BrE)
in or toward the direction away from the starting point, especially along the line of fire at a shooting range or launch site

Examples

Walk downrange safely
The safety officer reminded everyone not to walk downrange while shooting was in progress.
After the rocket launched, it was tracked as it traveled downrange over the ocean.
Always make sure the area downrange is clear before starting target practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uprange - in or toward the starting point or origin, opposite of downrange
