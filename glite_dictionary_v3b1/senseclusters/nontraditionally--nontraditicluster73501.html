nontraditionally

adverb
in an unconventional way
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑntrəˈdɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌnɒntrəˈdɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that does not follow traditional methods, customs, or expectations

Examples

Paint nontraditionally
The artist painted nontraditionally, using materials that most people would never consider.
They decided to celebrate the holiday nontraditionally by traveling instead of staying home.
The company hires employees nontraditionally, focusing on skills rather than formal education.

Related Terms

  • antonym: traditionally - in a way that follows established customs or practices
  • synonym: unconventionally - in a way that is not based on or conforming to what is generally done
