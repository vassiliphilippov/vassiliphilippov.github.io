report

verb
present oneself formally (for duty)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpɔrt / (AmE), / rɪˈpɔːt / (BrE)
to present oneself formally or officially at a place, especially for duty or work

Examples

To report for duty
All new employees must report to the main office when they arrive.
He forgot to report to his supervisor after returning from vacation.
Pilots are required to report for duty two hours before their flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: check in - to register upon arrival, especially at a hotel or airport
