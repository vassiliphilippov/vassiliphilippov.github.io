hyper

x
prefix for excess
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪpər / (AmE), / ˈhaɪpə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'over', 'excessive', or 'beyond', used in medical and technical terms

Examples

Hyper prefix word
The prefix hyper is used in words like hyperactive and hypertension.
Doctors use hyper to describe conditions involving excess, such as hyperglycemia.
In technical language, hyper often signals something beyond the normal range.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypo - a prefix meaning 'under' or 'below normal'
