cough up

verb
expel mucus by coughing
4.6
Pronunciation: / kɑf ʌp / (AmE), / kɒf ʌp / (BrE)
to expel phlegm or other substance from the lungs into the mouth by coughing

Examples

To cough up blood
If you cough up blood, see a doctor immediately.
She coughed up some phlegm and spit it into the tissue.
The patient began to cough up thick mucus during his illness.
