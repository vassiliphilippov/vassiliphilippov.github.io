punter

noun
someone who poles a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntər / (AmE), / ˈpʌntə / (BrE)
a person who propels a flat-bottomed boat with a pole, especially on rivers or shallow water

Examples

River punter
During the tour, the punter explained the history of each landmark they passed.
Some tourists tried their hand at being a punter, finding it harder than it looked.
The punter skillfully guided the boat down the narrow river channels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatman - a person who works on or operates a boat
