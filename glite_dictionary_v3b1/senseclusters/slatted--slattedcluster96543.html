slatted

adjective
with spaced strips
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈslætɪd / (AmE), / ˈslætɪd / (BrE)
made with thin, flat strips of material placed parallel and spaced apart

Examples

Slatted window blinds
The sunlight filtered through the slatted blinds, casting lines on the floor.
They built a slatted fence to allow air to pass through the garden.
The old barn had a slatted roof that let in streaks of light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: louvered - having angled slats to allow air or light through
  • antonym: solid - made of a single, continuous piece without gaps
