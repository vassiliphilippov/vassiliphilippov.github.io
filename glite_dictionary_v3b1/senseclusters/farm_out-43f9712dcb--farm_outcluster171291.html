Synonyms

  • contract out
    4.2 0.10/m
    contract out arranges for outside companies to do work under a formal agreement, while farm out sounds more informal and flexible.
  • outsource
    5.3 3/m
    outsource means hiring outside suppliers, often for cost or scale reasons, while farm out sounds less formal and everyday.
  • subcontract
    5.9 0.4/m
    subcontract means hiring another company to carry out part of a contract, while farm out usually covers whole tasks and is more general.
  • have work done
    3.8 0.1/m
    have work done means arranging a specific job by hiring a professional, while farm out often implies sending work to other firms.
