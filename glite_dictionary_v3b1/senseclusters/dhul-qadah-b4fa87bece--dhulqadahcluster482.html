Dhu'l-Qa'dah

proper noun
Islamic calendar's eleventh month
9.9
Pronunciation: / ðuːl kɑːˈdə / (AmE), / ðuːl kɑːˈdɑː / (BrE)
the eleventh month of the Islamic calendar, during which warfare is traditionally forbidden

Examples

Dhu'l-Qa'dah month
Historical records show that no wars were waged during Dhu'l-Qa'dah in early Islamic history.
Many Muslims prepare for Hajj during Dhu'l-Qa'dah, the month preceding the pilgrimage.
The festival was set to begin in Dhu'l-Qa'dah, following a period of peace and reflection.
