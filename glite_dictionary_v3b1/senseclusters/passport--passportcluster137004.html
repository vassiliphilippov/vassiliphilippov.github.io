passport

noun
official travel document
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsˌpɔrt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːs.pɔːt / (BrE)
An official document issued by a government certifying the holder's identity and citizenship, allowing them to travel abroad and re-enter their home country.

Examples

A valid passport
She renewed her passport before her trip to Europe.
The immigration officer stamped my passport at the border.
You need a valid passport to travel internationally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: visa - an endorsement allowing entry to a foreign country
