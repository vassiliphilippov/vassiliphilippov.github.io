periplus

noun
voyage around coast
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəˌplʌs / (AmE), / ˈpɛrɪˌplʌs / (BrE)
a voyage or navigation around a coastline, island, or region, especially by sea

Examples

Ancient periplus voyage
The ancient sailors recorded their periplus to help future navigators find safe harbors.
A detailed periplus of the Mediterranean coast was essential for early traders.
Historians studied the periplus to understand ancient maritime routes and exploration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circumnavigation - the act of sailing all the way around something, especially the world or an island
  • synonym: expedition - a journey undertaken for a specific purpose, often exploration
