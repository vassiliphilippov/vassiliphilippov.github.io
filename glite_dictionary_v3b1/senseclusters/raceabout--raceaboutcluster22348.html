raceabout

noun
type of small racing sloop
9.9 8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪs.ə.baʊt/ (AmE), /ˈreɪs.ə.baʊt/ (BrE)
A small sloop with the hull shape of a knockabout but more streamlined and equipped with a larger sail area, designed for racing.

Examples

A raceabout sailboat
Many enthusiasts admire the elegant lines and sail plan of a classic raceabout.

The club decided to purchase a raceabout for the upcoming sailing competitions.
