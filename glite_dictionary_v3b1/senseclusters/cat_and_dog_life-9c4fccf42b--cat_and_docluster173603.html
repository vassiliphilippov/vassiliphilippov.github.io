cat and dog life

noun
life filled with constant conflict or quarrelling
Pronunciation: / ˌkæt ənd ˌdɔɡ ˈlaɪf / (AmE), / ˌkæt ənd ˌdɒɡ ˈlaɪf / (BrE)
a life marked by continual arguing or conflict, especially between people who live together

Examples

A cat and dog life
After years of living a cat and dog life, the couple finally decided to separate.
Growing up in a cat and dog life household taught him to value peace and compromise.
The movie depicts the cat and dog life of two business partners who rarely agree on anything.
