green thumb

noun
talent for making plants grow
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrin ˈθʌm /; / ɡrin θʌm / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈθʌm /; / ɡriːn θʌm / (BrE)
a natural ability or special talent for making plants grow well
