tallboy

noun
tall chest of drawers on legs
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔlˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːlbɔɪ / (BrE)
a tall chest of drawers, usually divided into two sections and supported on four legs, used for storing clothes

Examples

A wooden tallboy
After moving, they realized the tallboy wouldn't fit through the new apartment's doorway.
He placed the antique vase on top of the tallboy in his bedroom.
The tallboy had enough drawers to store all of her clothes neatly.

Related Terms
