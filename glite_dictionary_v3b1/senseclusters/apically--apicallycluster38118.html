apically

adverb
at the tip
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪpɪkli / (AmE), / ˈapɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or at the tip or apex of something

Examples

Opens apically
The flower opens apically, revealing its pollen at the very top.
New branches develop apically as the tree continues to grow upward.
The surgeon inserted the instrument apically to reach the tip of the root.
