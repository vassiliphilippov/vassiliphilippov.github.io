horn

noun
object or part shaped like an animal horn
Pronunciation: / hɔrn / (AmE), / hɔːn / (BrE)
a part or device projecting or shaped like the horn of an animal

Examples

Chair horn
The chair's ornate arms featured a carved horn at each end.
The cyclist attached a small horn to the handlebars for safety.
The violinist carefully adjusted the horn of the instrument for better sound projection.

Related Terms
