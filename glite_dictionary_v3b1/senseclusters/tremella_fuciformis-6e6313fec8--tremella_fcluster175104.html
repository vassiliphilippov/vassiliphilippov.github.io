Tremella fuciformis

noun
a white, gelatinous mushroom used in Asian cuisine
Pronunciation: / trəˈmɛl.ə ˌfju.səˈfɔr.mɪs / (AmE), / trɪˈmɛl.ə ˌfjuː.sɪˈfɔː.mɪs / (BrE)
a species of gelatinous white mushroom, popular in East Asian cuisine, especially in China, Japan, and Taiwan; typically sold dried
