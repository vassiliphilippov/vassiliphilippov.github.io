represent

verb
describe or present as an example
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt / (BrE)
to describe or present something or someone in a particular way, or as being an example of something

Examples

To represent the city
In his speech, he represented the city as a model of sustainability.
She represented the theory as a breakthrough in modern science.
The historian represented the battle as a turning point in the war.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depict - to show or describe something in words or pictures
  • antonym: misrepresent - to give a false or misleading account
