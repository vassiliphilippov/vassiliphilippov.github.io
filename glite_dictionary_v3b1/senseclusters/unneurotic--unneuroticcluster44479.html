unneurotic

adjective
not neurotic; mentally healthy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.nʊˈrɑː.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.jʊˈrɒt.ɪk / (BrE)
not exhibiting signs of neurosis; mentally healthy and free from excessive anxiety, obsession, or emotional instability.

Examples

An unneurotic person
He seemed the most unneurotic member of the group, always calm and untroubled.
She's surprisingly unneurotic for someone in such a high-pressure job.
Their relaxed and unneurotic approach helped create a positive work environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neurotic - having or showing symptoms of neurosis
