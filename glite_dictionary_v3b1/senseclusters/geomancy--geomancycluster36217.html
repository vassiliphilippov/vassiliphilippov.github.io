geomancy

noun
divination by earth patterns
8.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.ˈɑː.mən.si/ (AmE), /dʒiː.ˈɒ.mən.si/ (BrE)
A method of divination that interprets markings on the ground or patterns formed by tossed handfuls of earth, stones, or sand.

Examples

Geomancy by earth patterns
He studied geomancy in hopes of understanding the patterns of nature and fate.
Many ancient cultures practiced geomancy to predict future events or choose favorable building sites.
The festival included a demonstration of geomancy, where earth was cast to foresee outcomes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divination - the practice of seeking knowledge of the future by supernatural means
