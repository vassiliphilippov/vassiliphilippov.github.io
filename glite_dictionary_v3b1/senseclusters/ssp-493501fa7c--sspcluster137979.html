SSP

noun
workplace safety rules
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs ɛs ˈpi / (AmE), / ˌɛs ɛs ˈpi / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'Safety Standards Procedure', a set of workplace safety rules to ensure a safe environment

Examples

Workplace SSP rules
All employees must follow the SSP to ensure a safe environment.
The company updated its SSP after the new regulations were introduced.
Before starting work, staff receive training on the SSP to prevent accidents.

  • synonym: protocol - a set of official rules or procedures
