ornithopod dinosaur

noun
bipedal herbivorous dinosaur group
9.7 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.nɪ.θə.pɑːd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.nɪ.θə.pɒd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔː/ (BrE)
a bipedal herbivorous dinosaur belonging to the group Ornithopoda, characterized by bird-like feet and often found in the Cretaceous and Jurassic periods

Examples

Many ornithopod dinosaurs
Many ornithopod dinosaurs had specialized teeth for chewing tough plants.
Paleontologists discovered fossils of an ornithopod dinosaur in the sedimentary layers of North America.
The hadrosaur is a well-known example of an ornithopod dinosaur from the Late Cretaceous period.
