Boy Scouts

noun
international youth organization for boys
1.8
Pronunciation: / bɔɪ skaʊts / (AmE), / bɔɪ skaʊts / (BrE)
an international youth organization founded in 1908 in England with the aim of teaching good citizenship and outdoor skills to boys

Examples

Boy Scouts merit badges
My son joined the Boy Scouts last year and already earned several merit badges.
The Boy Scouts organized a community clean-up as part of their service project.
When he was younger, he learned valuable survival skills through the Boy Scouts.
