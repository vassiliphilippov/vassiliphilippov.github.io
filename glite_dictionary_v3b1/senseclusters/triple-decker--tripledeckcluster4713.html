triple-decker

noun
sandwich with three slices of bread
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɪpəl ˌdɛkɚ / (AmE), / ˈtrɪpl ˌdɛkə / (BrE)

Examples

A triple-decker sandwich

The restaurant is famous for its enormous triple-decker sandwiches.
You need a big appetite to finish that triple-decker for lunch.
