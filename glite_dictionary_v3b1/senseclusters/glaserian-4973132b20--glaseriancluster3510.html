Glaserian

adjective
of the Glaserian fissure
Pronunciation: / ɡleɪˈzɪəriən / (AmE), / ɡleɪˈzɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the Glaserian fissure, a feature in the temporal bone of the skull

Examples

Glaserian fissure
The Glaserian fissure is an important landmark in ear surgery.
Damage near the Glaserian fissure can affect hearing and balance.
Anatomy students must learn the location of the Glaserian fissure in the temporal bone.
