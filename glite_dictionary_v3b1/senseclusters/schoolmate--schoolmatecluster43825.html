schoolmate

noun
person who attends the same school as you
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːlˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈskuːl.meɪt / (BrE)
a person who attends the same school as you at the same time

Examples

An old schoolmate
I ran into my old schoolmate at the coffee shop yesterday.
Many schoolmates attended the reunion last weekend.
She often studies after class with her schoolmate Emma.
