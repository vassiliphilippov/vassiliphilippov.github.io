infix

verb
insert a morpheme into a stem word
6.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪks / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪks / (BrE)
(Linguistics) To insert a morpheme into the body of a word stem.

Examples

To infix a syllable
In Tagalog, linguists often infix a syllable to indicate a completed action.
Some rare English slang forms are created when speakers infix expletives for emphasis.
The teacher showed the students how to infix certain particles into verbs to change their meaning.
