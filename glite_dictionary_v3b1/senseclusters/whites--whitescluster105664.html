whites

noun
people with light skin
3.2
Pronunciation: / waɪts / (AmE), / waɪts / (BrE)
people who are identified as belonging to the racial group described as white or having light-colored skin

Examples

Whites and minorities
The survey found that whites and other groups had different views on the new policy.
Many whites moved to the suburbs during the 20th century.
The city's population includes whites, as well as people from many other backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Caucasians - people of European descent with light skin
  • antonym: blacks - people who are identified as belonging to the racial group described as black or having dark-colored skin
