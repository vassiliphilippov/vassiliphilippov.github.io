Oomycetes

noun
fungus-like organisms that form oospores
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.oʊ.maɪˈsiːtiːz /; / ˌoʊ.oʊˈmaɪ.siˌtiːz / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.əʊ.maɪˈsiːtiːz /; / ˌəʊ.əʊˈmʌɪ.siːtiːz / (BrE)
a group or class of fungus-like, nonphotosynthetic organisms that resemble algae and reproduce by forming oospores (sometimes classified as protoctists)
