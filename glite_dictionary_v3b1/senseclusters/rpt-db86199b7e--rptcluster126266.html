RPT

noun
repeat setting
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɑr pi ti / (AmE), / ɑː piː tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'repeat', used as a playback setting or instruction to play something again automatically

Examples

RPT mode on player
