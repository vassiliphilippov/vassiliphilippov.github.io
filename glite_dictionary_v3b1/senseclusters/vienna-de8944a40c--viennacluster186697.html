Vienna

proper noun
capital city of Austria
2.5
Pronunciation: /viː.ˈe.nə/ /; / /vɪ.ˈe.nə/ (AmE), /vɪ.ˈe.nə/ /; / /viː.ˈe.nə/ (BrE)
the capital and largest city of Austria, situated on the Danube River in northeastern Austria and known for its historical association with famous composers such as Beethoven, Brahms, Haydn, Mozart, Schubert, and Strauss

Examples
