minestrone

noun
Italian vegetable soup
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnəˈstroʊni / (AmE), / ˌmɪnɪˈstrəʊni / (BrE)
A thick Italian soup made with a variety of vegetables, often including beans, pasta, and sometimes meat.

Examples

A bowl of minestrone
On cold days, nothing is better than a hot pot of minestrone.
The restaurant serves a hearty bowl of minestrone as a starter.
We had homemade minestrone with crusty bread for dinner last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soup - a liquid dish, typically savory and made by boiling meat, fish, or vegetables
