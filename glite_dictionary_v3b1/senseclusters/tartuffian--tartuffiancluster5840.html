tartuffian

noun
religious hypocrite
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / t…ërÀàtuÀêfi…ôn / (AmE), / t…ëÀêÀàtuÀêfi…ôn / (BrE)
a person who pretends to have moral or religious virtues but does not actually possess them; a hypocrite

Examples

A tartuffian leader

She warned her friends not to trust the tartuffian who preached honesty but lied constantly.
