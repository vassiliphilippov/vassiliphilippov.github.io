tartuffian

noun
religious hypocrite
9.9
Pronunciation: /tɑːr.ˈtʌf.ɪ.ən/ /; / /tɑːr.ˈtʌf.jən/ (AmE), /tɑː.ˈtʌf.ɪ.ən/ /; / /tɑː.ˈtʌf.jən/ (BrE)
