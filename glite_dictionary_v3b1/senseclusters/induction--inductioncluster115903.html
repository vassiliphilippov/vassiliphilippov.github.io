induction

noun
the act of initiating a process or state
4.1 4.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)

Examples

Rapid induction of labor was necessary for the health of the mother and child.

The induction of new policies led to a more efficient workflow across the company.
