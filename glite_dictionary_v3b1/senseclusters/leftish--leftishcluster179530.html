leftish

adjective
somewhat left-leaning politically
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛftɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈlɛftɪʃ / (BrE)
Tending to support or favor left-wing political ideas or policies, but not strongly so.

Examples

Her leftish views
Her leftish views became clear during the heated debate on healthcare reform.
Some newspapers are considered too leftish for mainstream readers.
The candidate's platform was leftish, appealing to progressive voters without being radical.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rightish - tending toward the political right
