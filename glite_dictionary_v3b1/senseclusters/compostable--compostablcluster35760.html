compostable

adjective
can decompose naturally
4.1
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpoʊstəbl̩ / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒstəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to break down naturally and safely into soil through composting processes

Examples

Compostable packaging
The restaurant uses only compostable packaging to reduce its environmental impact.
You should separate compostable materials like food scraps from regular trash.
These new bags are fully compostable and break down within a few months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biodegradable - able to be broken down by natural processes
  • antonym: noncompostable - not able to break down naturally through composting
