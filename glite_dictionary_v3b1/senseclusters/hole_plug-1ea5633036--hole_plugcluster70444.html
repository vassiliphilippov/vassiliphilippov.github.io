hole plug

noun
cap for covering holes or screws
5.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /hoʊl plʌg/ (AmE), /həʊl plʌg/ (BrE)
A small, usually flat device made of plastic, metal, or wood, used to cover holes or visible screws, especially in furniture or carpentry.

Examples

A hole plug cover
Before painting, he covered the exposed holes with a plastic hole plug for a cleaner look.
She chose a wooden hole plug to match the finish of the bookshelf.
The carpenter inserted a hole plug to conceal the screw head in the cabinet.
