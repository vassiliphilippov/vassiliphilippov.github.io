Examples

Emotional upset

The news of her sudden departure caused a great upset in the office.

Synonyms

  • disturbance
    4.1 2/m
    disturbance is mental or emotional trouble that interferes with daily life, while upset is a general state of emotional distress.
  • perturbation
    7.9 0.08/m
    perturbation sounds formal and means deep mental worry or disturbance in thought, while upset is everyday emotional distress.
  • agitation
    5.1 1/m
    agitation is restless, nervous energy often with physical signs, while upset is a broader feeling of sadness or anxiety.
  • unhappiness
    1.8 3/m
    unhappiness is a sad, low mood that makes someone gloomy, while upset can also include anxiety and sudden emotional disturbance.
