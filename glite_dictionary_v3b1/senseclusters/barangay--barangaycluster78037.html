barangay

noun
Filipino village unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑːˈrɑːŋɡaɪ /; / bɑːˈrɑːŋˌɡaɪ / (AmE), / bæˈræŋɡaɪ /; / bəˈræŋɡaɪ /; / bɑːˈrɑːŋɡaɪ / (BrE)
the smallest local community or administrative division in the Philippines, often with its own leaders and events; originally a traditional Filipino village
