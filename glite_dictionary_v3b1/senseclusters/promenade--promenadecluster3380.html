promenade

noun
leisurely walk in a public place
5.0 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Àåpr…ëÀê.m…ôÀàne…™d / (AmE), / Àåpr…ím.…ôÀàn…ëÀêd / (BrE)
a leisurely walk, especially one taken in a public place for pleasure or display

Examples

A promenade in the park
Every evening, crowds enjoyed a leisurely promenade in the city park.
The old-fashioned promenade was a highlight of the seaside town's charm.
They decided to take a promenade along the river after dinner.

Synonyms
