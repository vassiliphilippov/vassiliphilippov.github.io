platinize

verb
to coat with platinum
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplætəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈplætɪnaɪz / (BrE)
to coat (an object or material) with metallic platinum, typically to give resistance to corrosion or to provide specific chemical properties

Examples

The laboratory decided to platinize the electrodes to improve their durability.
To conduct this experiment, first platinize the surface of the metal rod carefully.
