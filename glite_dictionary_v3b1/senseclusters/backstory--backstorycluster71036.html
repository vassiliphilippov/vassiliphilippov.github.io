backstory

noun
character background
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌstɔːri / (AmE), / ˈbækˌstɔːri / (BrE)
the background or history of a character or situation, often revealed as part of a story

Examples

The backstory of a character
The novel slowly reveals the hero's backstory through a series of flashbacks.
Each character in the series has a unique backstory that explains their motivations.
Writers often create a detailed backstory to make their characters more believable.
The novel slowly reveals the hero's backstory through memories and conversations.
Understanding the villain's backstory made his actions more believable to the audience.
The director added scenes to explain the main character's backstory in greater detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: background - the circumstances or events leading up to something
  • antonym: future - the time or events that will happen after the present
  • synonym: history - the past events relating to a person or thing
  • synonym: origin - the beginning or cause of something
