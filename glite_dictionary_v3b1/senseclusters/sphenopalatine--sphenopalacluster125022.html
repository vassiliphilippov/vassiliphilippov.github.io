sphenopalatine

adjective
of sphenoid and palatine bones
Pronunciation: / ˌsfiːnoʊˈpæləˌtaɪn / (AmE), / ˌsfiːnəʊˈpælətaɪn / (BrE)
relating to both the sphenoid and palatine bones in the skull

Examples

Sphenopalatine artery
The sphenopalatine artery is often involved in severe nosebleeds.
Doctors may block the sphenopalatine ganglion to treat certain types of headaches.
The sphenopalatine foramen connects the nasal cavity with the pterygopalatine fossa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nasopalatine - relating to the nose and the palatine bone
