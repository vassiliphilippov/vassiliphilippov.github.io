rag bag

noun
a mixed, disorganized collection
Pronunciation: / ˈræɡ ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈræɡ ˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a confused mixture of different things or ideas, often lacking order or pattern

Examples

A rag bag of items
Her desk drawer was a rag bag of old receipts, letters, and forgotten trinkets.
That proposal is just a rag bag of unrelated suggestions with no clear focus.
The novel felt like a rag bag of characters and subplots stitched loosely together.

  • synonym: hodgepodge - a confused mixture
  • synonym: jumble - an untidy collection or pile of things
