intermediation

noun
the act of mediating between parties
8.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.miː.dɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.tər.mɪ.dɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.miː.dɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.tə.mɪ.dɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the action of intervening or acting as a mediator, especially to resolve a dispute or facilitate a settlement.

Examples

International intermediation
International intermediation played a crucial role in achieving peace between the two nations.
The contract was finalized after much intermediation by trusted third parties.
Without effective intermediation, the dispute could have escalated further.

  • synonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching an agreement
