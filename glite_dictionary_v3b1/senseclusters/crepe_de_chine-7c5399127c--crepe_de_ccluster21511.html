crepe de Chine

noun
thin, lightweight silk fabric
Pronunciation: / kreɪp də ʃin / (AmE), / krep də ʃiːn / (BrE)
A very thin, lightweight silk or silklike fabric with a slightly crinkled texture, often used in dresses and blouses.

Examples

A crepe de Chine scarf
A scarf of crepe de Chine adds a touch of sophistication to any outfit.
She prefers blouses crafted from crepe de Chine for their soft and airy feel.
The elegant gown was made of delicate crepe de Chine that flowed beautifully as she walked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chiffon - a light, sheer fabric, often used for dresses and scarves
