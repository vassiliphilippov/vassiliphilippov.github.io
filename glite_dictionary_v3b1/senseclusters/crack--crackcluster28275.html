crack

verb
say a joke or witty remark
Pronunciation: / kræk / (AmE), / kræk / (BrE)
to say or make (a joke or witty remark) suddenly or spontaneously

Examples

To crack a joke
He likes to crack jokes at every meeting.
It's not the right time to crack jokes, please be serious.
She suddenly cracked a witty remark that made everyone laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joke - a thing that someone says to cause amusement or laughter, especially a story with a funny punchline
  • synonym: tell - to communicate information to someone, often a story or a joke
