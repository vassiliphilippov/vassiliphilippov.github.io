pale chrysanthemum aphid

noun
aphid species harmful to chrysanthemums
5.6
Pronunciation: / peɪl krɪˌsænθəˈmiəm ˈeɪfɪd / (AmE), / peɪl krɪˌsænθəˈmiːəm ˈeɪfɪd / (BrE)
a small aphid species, Macrosiphoniella sanborni, that is a significant pest of chrysanthemums, feeding on the plant's sap and potentially damaging the flowers and foliage

Examples

The pale chrysanthemum aphid pest

The pale chrysanthemum aphid can cause yellowing leaves and stunted growth in greenhouse chrysanthemums.
