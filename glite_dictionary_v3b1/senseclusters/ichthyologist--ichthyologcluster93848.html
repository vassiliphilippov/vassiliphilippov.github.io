ichthyologist

noun
a scientist who studies fish
Pronunciation: / ˌɪkθiˈɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪkθiˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a zoologist who specializes in the scientific study of fishes

Examples

An ichthyologist studies fish
As an ichthyologist, she published several papers on deep-sea fish anatomy.
Museums often consult an ichthyologist when identifying rare fish specimens.
The ichthyologist spent years cataloguing new species discovered in the Amazon River.
