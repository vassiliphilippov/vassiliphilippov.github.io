Tennyson

proper noun
English Victorian poet (1809–1892)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnɪsən / (AmE), / ˈtɛnɪsən / (BrE)
(1809–1892) An English poet and the foremost figure of Victorian poetry, known for works such as 'In Memoriam' and 'The Charge of the Light Brigade'.
