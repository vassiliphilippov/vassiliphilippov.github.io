divider

noun
a person who separates things
Pronunciation: / dəˈvaɪdər / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdə / (BrE)
a person who separates something into parts or groups

Examples

Land divider
In ancient societies, a divider would allocate land among families.
She acted as a divider, organizing the students into different categories based on skill level.
The divider in the debate split the group into two opposing teams.
