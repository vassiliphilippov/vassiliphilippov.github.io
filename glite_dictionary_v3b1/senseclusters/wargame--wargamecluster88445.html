wargame

verb
to simulate strategies
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔːr.geɪm/ /; / /ˈwɑːr.geɪm/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.geɪm/ (BrE)
to plan, test, or rehearse strategies and responses by simulating actions or scenarios, especially in military, business, or policy contexts

Examples

To wargame a plan
The military leaders decided to wargame the possible outcomes of the new strategy.
Before launching the product, the team will wargame different market scenarios to prepare for challenges.
Experts often wargame crisis situations to improve their response plans.

  • synonym: simulate - to imitate the conditions or process of something
