huffing

noun
inhaling solvents to get high
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌf.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhʌf.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The act of deliberately inhaling volatile solvents, gases, or aerosols to achieve intoxication; a form of substance abuse.

Examples

Huffing glue
Police issued warnings after dozens of teenagers were seen huffing glue in the park.
The school nurse discussed how huffing solvents can cause sudden health emergencies and long-term damage.
After several hospital admissions, the town launched an outreach program to prevent youth huffing.

  • synonym: inhalant abuse - the misuse of volatile substances by inhalation to produce psychoactive effects
