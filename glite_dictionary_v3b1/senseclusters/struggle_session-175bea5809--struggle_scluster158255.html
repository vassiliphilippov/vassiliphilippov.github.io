struggle session

noun
meeting for group critique or problem discussion
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌɡəl ˌsɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈstrʌɡl ˌsɛʃən / (BrE)
a meeting in which participants confront, discuss, and critique problems, difficulties, or shortcomings, often with the aim of improvement or accountability

Examples

A struggle session meeting
After the new policy failed, management organized a struggle session to hear feedback from employees.
Many team members dread the weekly struggle session, but it often leads to real improvement.
The coach called a struggle session to address performance issues.
