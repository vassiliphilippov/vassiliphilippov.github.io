tomographic

adjective
imaging by slices
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌtoʊməˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌtɒməˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to imaging by sections or slices, especially as used in medical scans

Examples

Tomographic imaging technique
The hospital invested in new tomographic equipment to improve diagnostic accuracy.
A tomographic scan can reveal details that are not visible in standard X-rays.
Researchers analyzed the tomographic data to study the internal structure of the sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imaging - the process of producing images, especially for medical purposes
  • synonym: scanning - the process of examining something with a device or system
