kiss of life

noun
mouth-to-mouth resuscitation
Pronunciation: / kɪs əv laɪf / (AmE), / kɪs əv laɪf / (BrE)
an emergency procedure in which a person breathes air into the lungs of someone who has stopped breathing to try to revive them; mouth-to-mouth resuscitation

Examples

Give the kiss of life
By giving him the kiss of life, she saved his life after the accident.
He learned how to perform the kiss of life in his first aid course.
The lifeguard gave the unconscious boy the kiss of life until the ambulance arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cardiopulmonary resuscitation - an emergency procedure combining chest compressions with artificial ventilation
  • synonym: mouth-to-mouth - the method of breathing air directly into the mouth of a non-breathing person
