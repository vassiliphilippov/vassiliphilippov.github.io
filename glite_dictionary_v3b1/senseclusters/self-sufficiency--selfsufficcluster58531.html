self-sufficiency

noun
ability to provide for oneself
1.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /self.sə.ˈfɪʃ.ən.siː/ (AmE), /self.sə.ˈfɪʃ.ən.siː/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being able to provide for one's own needs without external help

Examples

Self-sufficiency in farming
Achieving self-sufficiency requires time, patience, and commitment to learning new skills.
Many people dream of self-sufficiency, growing their own food and generating their own energy.
The remote village prided itself on its self-sufficiency, rarely needing help from the outside world.

  • synonym: independence - freedom from control or influence of others
