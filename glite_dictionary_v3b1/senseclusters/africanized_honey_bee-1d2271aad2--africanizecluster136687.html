Africanized honey bee

noun
a hybrid strain of aggressive honey bee
Pronunciation: / ˌæfɹɪkənaɪzd ˈhʌni biː / (AmE), / ˌafɹɪkənaɪzd ˈhʌni biː / (BrE)
a hybrid strain of honey bee, originally bred in Brazil from European honey bees and African honey bees, known for its defensiveness and spread into the Americas

Examples

Africanized honey bee colony
After their introduction, Africanized honey bees quickly established colonies throughout South and Central America.
Many farmers in Texas have learned to identify an Africanized honey bee by its aggressive behavior.
The spread of the Africanized honey bee has caused concern among beekeepers across the Americas.
