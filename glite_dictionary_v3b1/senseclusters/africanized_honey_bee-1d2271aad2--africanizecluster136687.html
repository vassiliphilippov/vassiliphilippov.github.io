Africanized honey bee

noun
a hybrid strain of aggressive honey bee
7.1 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæfɹɪkənaɪzd ˈhʌni biː / (AmE), / ˌafɹɪkənaɪzd ˈhʌni biː / (BrE)
