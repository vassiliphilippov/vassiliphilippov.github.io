anti-vaccination

adjective
opposed to vaccines
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌvæksɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌvæksɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
opposed to the use or promotion of vaccines

Examples

Anti-vaccination group
The anti-vaccination campaign spread misinformation about the safety of childhood immunizations.
Some parents hold anti-vaccination beliefs despite scientific evidence supporting vaccines.
Public health officials are concerned about the rise in anti-vaccination attitudes in certain communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-vaccination - supporting the use or promotion of vaccines
  • synonym: vaccine-hesitant - reluctant or unsure about receiving vaccines
