matte

adjective
not shiny; dull finish
6.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /mæt/ (AmE), /mæt/ (BrE)
(of a surface or finish) not shiny; not reflecting light; having a dull or non-glossy appearance.

Examples

Matte paint
She painted her bedroom walls with a matte finish for a subtle look.
The matte black car stood out among the shiny vehicles in the lot.
The artist preferred matte paper for her photographs to avoid glare.
After printing, the photographs had a beautiful matt finish rather than a glossy shine.
She chose a matt paint for the bedroom walls to create a warm, cozy atmosphere.
The artist preferred working with matt paper because it prevented glare and reflections.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glossy - shiny and reflecting light
  • synonym: matte - variant spelling meaning dull and not shiny
