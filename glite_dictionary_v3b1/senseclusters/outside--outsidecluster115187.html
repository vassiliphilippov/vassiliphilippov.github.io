outside

adjective
far side of home plate, in baseball
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtsaɪd / (BrE)
(of a baseball pitch) on the far side of home plate from the batter.

Examples

Outside pitch
He tends to swing at outside pitches, making him vulnerable to certain pitchers.
She watched as another outside pitch sailed past for a ball.
The pitcher threw an outside fastball to avoid the batter's power zone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inside - (of a baseball pitch) on the side of home plate closest to the batter
