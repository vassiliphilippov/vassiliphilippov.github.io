spit

verb
to rain lightly or drizzly
Pronunciation: / spɪt / (AmE), / spɪt / (BrE)
(of rain) to fall lightly or as a fine drizzle.

Examples

It spits with rain
Although it had spat on and off all day, the football match continued.
It started to spit just as we left the house, so we took umbrellas.
The sky looks grey, and it's only spitting at the moment.
