on the knocker

adverb
persistent in asking or searching
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə ˈnɑkɚ / (AmE), / ɒn ðə ˈnɒkə / (BrE)
persistent or insistent in asking for something, or actively seeking or searching for it

Examples
