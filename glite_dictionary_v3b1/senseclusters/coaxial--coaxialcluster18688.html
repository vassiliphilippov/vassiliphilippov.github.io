coaxial

adjective
having a common axis
6.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈæksiəl / (AmE), / kəʊˈæksiəl / (BrE)
(of two or more items) having a common axis; sharing the same center line.

Examples

Coaxial cable
A coaxial cable is often used to transmit television signals.
The two cylinders are coaxial so they rotate smoothly around the same center.
Their design ensures that all gears remain coaxial for efficient power transfer.
