misfold

verb
to fold incorrectly
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈfoʊld / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈfəʊld / (BrE)
to fold incorrectly, especially referring to proteins or materials not taking their proper shape

Examples

To misfold a protein
If proteins misfold, they can cause serious diseases.
Scientists study how certain conditions make materials misfold during manufacturing.
When a protein misfolds, it may lose its normal function in the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fold - to bend or shape something into layers or parts
  • synonym: malform - to form incorrectly or abnormally
