otitis externa

noun
inflammation of the outer ear/canal
8.2
Pronunciation: / oʊˈtaɪtᵻs ɪkˈstɝːnə / (AmE), / əʊˈtaɪtɪs ɪkˈstɜːnə / (BrE)
inflammation of the outer ear, including the auricle and ear canal, often causing pain, redness, and possible discharge

Examples

Otitis externa infection
Doctors often prescribe antibiotic drops to treat otitis externa and reduce inflammation.
If untreated, otitis externa can lead to more severe ear problems and even temporary hearing loss.
The swimmer was diagnosed with otitis externa after complaining of pain and itching in her ear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ear infection - an infection that affects any part of the ear
