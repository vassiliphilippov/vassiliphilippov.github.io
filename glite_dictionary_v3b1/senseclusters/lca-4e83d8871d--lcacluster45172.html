LCA

noun
environmental impact analysis
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛl siː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛl siː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an analysis method for measuring the environmental impacts of a product or service throughout its entire life cycle

Examples

Product LCA report
The company performed an LCA to understand the environmental footprint of its new packaging.
Before launching the product, an LCA was required to meet sustainability standards.
An LCA can help businesses identify ways to reduce waste and energy use.
