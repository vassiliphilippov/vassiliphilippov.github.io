glasslike

adjective
resembling glass
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities similar to glass, such as being hard, smooth, clear, shiny, or brittle

Examples

Glasslike surface
The volcanic rock had a glasslike appearance and reflected the sunlight brilliantly.
Her voice took on a glasslike quality, cold and fragile in the tense silence.
The artist created a sculpture with a glasslike finish that looked delicate and smooth.
The lake was so still that its surface appeared glasslike.
The artist used a special glaze to give the vase a glasslike finish.
After the ice storm, the roads had a dangerous glasslike sheen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brittle - easily broken or cracked
  • synonym: glassy - having a smooth, shiny surface like glass
  • antonym: opaque - not able to be seen through; not transparent
