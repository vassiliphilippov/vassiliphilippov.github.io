wah

interjection
mocking complaint
Pronunciation: / wɑ / (AmE), / wɑː / (BrE)
an exclamation imitating a cry or whine, used to express distress or to mock someone's complaint

Examples

A loud wah of pain
He let out a loud wah when he realized he had lost the game.
She teased her brother by saying wah every time he complained about his chores.
Instead of sympathy, his friends responded with a mocking wah to his minor injury.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aww - an exclamation expressing sympathy or affection
  • synonym: boohoo - an exclamation imitating crying, often used to mock someone
