politique

noun
French moderate politician
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑː.lə.tɪk/ (AmE), /ˈpɒ.lɪ.tɪk/ (BrE)
a person who is pragmatic and flexible in political matters, especially one who prioritizes practical solutions over strict ideology (sometimes specifically a member of a moderate group in 16th-century France)
