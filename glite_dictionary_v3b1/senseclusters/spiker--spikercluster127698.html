spiker

noun
person who drugs drinks
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪkər / (AmE), / ˈspaɪkə / (BrE)
a person who secretly puts alcohol or drugs into someone else's drink, often to cause intoxication or harm

Examples

Drink spiker at a club
The police arrested the spiker before he could tamper with anyone's drink at the club.
After hearing rumors about a spiker at the event, guests became more cautious with their beverages.
Security cameras helped identify the spiker who had been targeting partygoers all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drugger - a person who administers drugs to someone without their knowledge
  • antonym: victim - a person harmed by the actions of a spiker
