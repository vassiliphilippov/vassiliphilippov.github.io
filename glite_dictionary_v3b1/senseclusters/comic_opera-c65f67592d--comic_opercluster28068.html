comic opera

noun
opera with a humorous tone and happy ending
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑ.mɪk ˈɑ.pɚ.ə / (AmE), / ˈkɒ.mɪk ˈɒp.ər.ə / (BrE)
An opera characterized by a light or humorous tone, spoken dialogue, and a happy ending.

Examples

A comic opera show
Many people enjoy the lively performances and witty dialogue found in comic operas.
She starred as the lead in a renowned comic opera during the summer festival.
The theater was hosting a comic opera for the first time in years.
