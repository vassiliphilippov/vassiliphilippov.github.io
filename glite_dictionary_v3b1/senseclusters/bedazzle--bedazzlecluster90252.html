bedazzle

verb
to impress greatly
5.7
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdæzəl / (AmE), / bɪˈdæzəl / (BrE)
to impress or amaze someone greatly with brilliance, glamour, or dazzling effect

Examples

To bedazzle the crowd
The fireworks display will bedazzle everyone at the festival tonight.

The new smartphone's features are designed to bedazzle tech enthusiasts.
The fireworks display bedazzled the crowd with its vibrant colors and patterns.
Her performance on stage bedazzles everyone who sees it.
He hoped his new suit would bedazzle his friends at the party.
