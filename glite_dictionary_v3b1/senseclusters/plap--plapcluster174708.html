plap

verb
to make a soft smack
9.9
Pronunciation: /plæp/ (AmE), /plæp/ (BrE)
to make a light smacking or soft splashing sound, or to strike or place something so it makes that sound

Examples

To plap a towel
She likes to plap the wet towel onto the chair after washing dishes.
Raindrops plapped softly against the window as we sat inside.
He plaps his sandals on the floor every time he walks into the room.

Related Terms
