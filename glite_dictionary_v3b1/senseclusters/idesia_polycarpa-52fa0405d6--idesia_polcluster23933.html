Idesia polycarpa

noun
ornamental Asiatic tree with colorful berries
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdiːziə ˌpɒliˈkɑːrpə / (AmE), / ɪˈdiːzɪə ˌpɒlɪˈkɑːpə / (BrE)
A deciduous, round-headed Asiatic tree grown in mild climates for its heart-shaped leaves, fragrant yellow-green flowers, and hanging clusters of orange-red berries.
