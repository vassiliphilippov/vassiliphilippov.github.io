Chichimec

adjective
of the Chichimec people
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːtʃɪˌmɛk / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtʃɪˌmɛk / (BrE)
relating to the Chichimec people, their culture, language, or historical artifacts of northern Mexico.

Examples

Chichimec pottery art
The museum displayed several Chichimec pottery pieces from the pre-Columbian era.
Researchers are interested in Chichimec traditions that influenced later Mexican cultures.
The Chichimec language is now considered extinct, but some words remain in local dialects.
