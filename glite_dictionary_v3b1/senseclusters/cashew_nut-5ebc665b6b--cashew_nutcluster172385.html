cashew nut

noun
kidney-shaped edible nut, roasted before eating
5.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈʃu nʌt / (AmE), / kəˈʃuː nʌt / (BrE)
The kidney-shaped, edible nut of the cashew tree, eaten only after roasting, used as a snack or in cooking.

Examples

Cashew nuts roasted
Cashew nuts are often used in both sweet and savory dishes around the world.
Always roast cashew nuts before eating them to remove their natural toxins.
She sprinkled chopped cashew nuts on top of the salad for extra crunch.
