crocidolite

noun
blue asbestos mineral
Pronunciation: / kroʊˈsɪdəˌlaɪt / (AmE), / krɒˈsɪdəˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a blue asbestos mineral once used in industry, now recognized as highly hazardous to health
