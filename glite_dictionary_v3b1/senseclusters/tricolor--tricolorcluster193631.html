tricolor

noun
flag with three colored stripes
3.1 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtraɪ.kə.lər/ (AmE), /ˈtraɪ.kə.lə/ (BrE)
A flag with three colored stripes, especially the national flag of France or other countries with a similar design.

Examples

French tricolor flag
Many countries use a tricolor design for their national flag, featuring three distinct bands of color.
The French tricolor was raised on Bastille Day to celebrate the nation’s independence.
The Italian tricolor consists of green, white, and red vertical stripes.
He waved the Irish tricolour proudly during the parade.
Many countries adopted a tricolour as a symbol of national unity and liberty.
The French tricolour was first raised in 1794 during the Revolution.
