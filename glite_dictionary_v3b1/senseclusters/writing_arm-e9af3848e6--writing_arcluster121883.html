writing arm

noun
chair arm broadened for writing
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪtɪŋ ɑrm / (AmE), / ˈraɪtɪŋ ɑːm / (BrE)
an arm of a chair that is broadened to serve as a surface for writing

Examples

A chair with writing arm
He found it uncomfortable to write on the small writing arm attached to the chair.
The classroom was equipped with desks instead of chairs with writing arms.
The lecture hall chairs each had a writing arm for students to take notes.
