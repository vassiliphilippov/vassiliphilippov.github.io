Lighthouse of Alexandria

proper noun
ancient lighthouse, one of the Seven Wonders
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪthaʊs əv ˌæləɡˈzændriə / (AmE), / ˈlaɪthaʊs əv ˌælɪɡˈzændriə / (BrE)
an ancient lighthouse built on the island of Pharos at Alexandria around 285 BC, regarded as one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World

Examples
