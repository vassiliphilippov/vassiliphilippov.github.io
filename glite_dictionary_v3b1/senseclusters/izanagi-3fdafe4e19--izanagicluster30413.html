Izanagi

proper noun
Naruto eye technique that rewrites reality
9.9
Pronunciation: /iː.zə.ˈnɑː.giː/ /; / /ɪ.zə.ˈnɑː.giː/ (AmE), /iː.zə.ˈnɑː.giː/ /; / /ɪ.zə.ˈnɑː.giː/ (BrE)
(in the Naruto fiction and fandom) a powerful ocular jutsu that can rewrite or alter reality for a short time, typically costing the user the sight in the activated eye.

Examples

Izanagi jutsu
In Naruto, Izanagi is a jutsu that rewrites reality but costs the user an eye.
He activated Izanagi with his Sharingan eye to undo his fatal mistake during battle.
After using Izanagi, the character lost sight in that eye, a permanent cost.
