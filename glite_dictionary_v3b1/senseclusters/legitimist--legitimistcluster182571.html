legitimist

noun
supporter of rightful ruler
9.5
Pronunciation: /lɪ.ˈdʒɪ.tɪ.mɪst/ /; / /lɪ.ˈdʒɪt.ɪ.mɪst/ (AmE), /lɪ.ˈdʒɪ.tɪ.mɪst/ /; / /lɪ.ˈdʒɪt.ɪ.mɪst/ (BrE)
