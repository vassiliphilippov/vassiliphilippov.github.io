Examples

Synonyms

    3.9 1.09/m

  • give up

    give up means stopping trying or leaving an activity because you cannot continue, while call it is choosing to stop when it seems best.
  • quit
    1.5 18/m
    quit means stopping because you accept defeat or face hopeless hardship, while call it is choosing to stop when it's the right time.
