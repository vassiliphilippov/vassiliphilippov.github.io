photosystem

noun
light-absorbing complex
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.toʊ.sɪs.təm/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.təʊ.sɪs.təm/ (BrE)
a group of molecules in plants, algae, or cyanobacteria that captures light energy to start photosynthesis

Examples

A photosystem captures light
