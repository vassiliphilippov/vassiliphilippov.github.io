training school

noun
school for vocational or technical skills
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnɪŋ skuːl / (BrE)
a school that provides practical education and instruction in vocational or technical skills.

Examples

Vocational training school
After high school, she attended a training school to become a certified electrician.
Many training schools offer programs in culinary arts, automotive repair, and information technology.
The city's new training school equips students with practical skills needed in today’s job market.
