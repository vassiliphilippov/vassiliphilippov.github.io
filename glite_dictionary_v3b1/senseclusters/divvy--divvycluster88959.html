divvy

noun
dividend from a cooperative
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪvi / (AmE), / ˈdɪvi / (BrE)
A dividend, especially one paid by a cooperative society to its members.

Examples

A yearly divvy
Each member of the co-op received their divvy at the end of the year.
My grandmother used to save her divvy for Christmas shopping.
The annual divvy was an important incentive for joining the society.
