child support

noun
court-ordered payments for a child's care after divorce
2.7 4.25/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt Éa…™ld s…ôÀåp…îrt / (AmE), / Ààt Éa…™ld s…ôÀåp…îÀêt / (BrE)
Money that a court orders one parent to pay to the parent who has custody of their children after a separation or divorce, to help cover living expenses for the children.

Examples

Child support payment
After the divorce, he was required to pay child support to help cover his children's expenses.
Many parents struggle to collect regular child support despite legal agreements.
The court increased her child support payments after she lost her job.

Synonyms

    3.5 0.32/m

  • maintenance
    4.4 1.57/m
    maintenance is a legal term for money to support a family member or spouse, while child support specifically helps a child's living expenses.
