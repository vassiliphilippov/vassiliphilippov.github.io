arribada

noun
mass turtle nesting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːriˈbɑːdə / (AmE), / ˌæriˈbɑːdə / (BrE)
a mass nesting event in which large numbers of sea turtles come ashore together to lay eggs on a beach

Examples

The arribada event
Every year, thousands of turtles participate in the arribada along the Pacific coast.
Scientists travel from around the world to study the arribada phenomenon.
Local communities protect the beaches during the arribada to ensure the turtles' safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nesting - the act of animals laying eggs in a nest
