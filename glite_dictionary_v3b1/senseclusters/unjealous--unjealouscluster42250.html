unjealous

adjective
not jealous
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛləs / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛləs / (BrE)
not feeling or showing jealousy toward others or their achievements; not possessive or protective in a jealous way

Examples

An unjealous friend
She was genuinely unjealous of her friend's success and celebrated with her.
His unjealous attitude made him easy to get along with in competitive situations.
It is rare to find someone so unjealous when others achieve great things.
She was remarkably unjealous when her partner spent time with friends.
His unjealous attitude made their relationship much more relaxed and open.
The unjealous dog happily shared its toys with the new puppy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: envious - wanting what someone else has
  • antonym: jealous - feeling or showing envy of someone or their achievements
  • synonym: trusting - showing confidence in someone without suspicion
