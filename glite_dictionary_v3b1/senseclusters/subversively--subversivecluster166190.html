subversively

adverb
in a rebellious way
7.8
Pronunciation: / səbˈvɜrsɪvli / (AmE), / səbˈvɜːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that seeks to undermine or challenge established systems, authority, or beliefs

Examples

To subversively challenge power
The artist subversively incorporated political messages into her paintings.
He subversively questioned the company's strict dress code during the meeting.
The novel subversively critiques traditional views on education and authority.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conformingly - in a way that follows established rules or norms
  • synonym: rebelliously - in a way that resists authority or control
