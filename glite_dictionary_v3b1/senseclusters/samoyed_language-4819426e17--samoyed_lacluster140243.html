Samoyed language

noun
Uralic languages of the Samoyed peoples
Pronunciation: / səˈmɔɪˌɛd ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈmɔɪɛd ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
any of several Uralic languages spoken by the Samoyed peoples of northwestern Siberia

Examples

Samoyed language family
Few people today speak a Samoyed language as their first language.
Researchers traveled to northern Siberia to document the Samoyed language spoken in remote communities.
The Samoyed languages are part of the larger Uralic language family.
