mudspringer

noun
tropical fish that moves on land
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌdˌsprɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈmʌdˌsprɪŋə / (BrE)
a tropical fish found in coastal regions of Africa and Asia that is able to move on land using its strong pectoral fins

Examples

The mudspringer fish
Scientists studied the mudspringer to better understand its unique adaptation for moving on land.
The mudspringer uses its strong fins to travel across muddy shores during low tide.
Unlike most fish, the mudspringer can breathe air and survive out of water for extended periods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mudskipper - a similar fish able to move on land, found in coastal regions
