tire tool

noun
tool for fitting or removing tires
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɚ tuːl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪə tuːl / (BrE)
a hand tool shaped as a lever, used to fit or remove the casing of a pneumatic tire onto or from a wheel rim

Examples

A tire tool lever
A bent tire tool can make changing a tire much more difficult.
He used a tire tool to pry the flat tire off the rim.
Make sure you have a tire tool and a spare in the trunk before you travel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tire iron - a similar tool used for changing tires
