dullness

noun
lack of sensibility or interest
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌlnəs / (BrE)
a lack of emotional sensitivity, liveliness, or interest; the quality of being unfeeling, unresponsive, or lacking in excitement

Examples

Emotional dullness
She tried to fight the dullness that crept into her thoughts after the long, lonely winter.
There was a sense of dullness in his voice that made the conversation difficult.
Without her friends, the dullness of his days became almost unbearable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • synonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity or awareness, especially emotionally
  • antonym: vivacity - the quality of being lively and animated
