rub one's hands together

verb
show excitement or anticipation by rubbing hands
Pronunciation: / rʌb wʌnz hændz təˈɡɛðər / (AmE), / rʌb wʌnz hændz təˈɡɛðə / (BrE)
to move one's hands rapidly across each other, typically as a sign of expectation, excitement, or satisfaction
