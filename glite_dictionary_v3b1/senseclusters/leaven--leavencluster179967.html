leaven

noun
a lightening or modifying influence
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvən / (AmE), / ˈlɛvən / (BrE)
a quality or influence that lightens, modifies, or enlivens something, especially in a subtle or indirect way

Examples

A leaven of humor
A leaven of hope spread throughout the community during difficult times.
Her witty remarks acted as a leaven in the otherwise serious meeting.
The new manager brought a leaven of innovation to the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influence - the effect one thing has on another
