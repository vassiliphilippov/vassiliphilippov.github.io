monomaniac

noun
person obsessed with a single idea
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
a person who is obsessed with a single idea or subject, especially to the exclusion of other interests or concerns

Examples

A monomaniac person
Her friends grew concerned as she turned into a monomaniac about healthy eating, neglecting other parts of her life.
Some critics described the entrepreneur as a monomaniac who pursued his vision no matter the cost.
The detective became a monomaniac, consumed by his obsession with solving the case.
