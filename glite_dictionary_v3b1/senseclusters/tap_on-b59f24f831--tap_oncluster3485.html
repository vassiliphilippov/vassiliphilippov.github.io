tap on

noun
a light touch or knock
Pronunciation: / tæp ɑn / (AmE), / tæp ɒn / (BrE)
a light touch or knock, especially made gently with the hand or a small object

Examples

A tap on the shoulder
A sudden tap on the shoulder made him turn around quickly.
She heard a soft tap on her window late at night.
There was a gentle tap on the table to get everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock - an act of striking a surface to attract attention
