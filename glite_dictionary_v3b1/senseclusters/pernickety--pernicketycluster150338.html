pernickety

adjective
fussy and overly attentive to details
7.9
Pronunciation: / pərˈnɪk.ə.ti / (AmE), / pəˈnɪk.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
Paying excessive attention to minor or trivial details; fussy or overly precise.

Examples

A pernickety person
He can be quite pernickety when it comes to organizing his desk.
Julia is so pernickety about how the table is set for dinner.
The pernickety editor insisted on correcting every tiny mistake.
