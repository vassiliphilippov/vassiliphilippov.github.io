redial

noun
telephone recall function
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌdaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈriːˌdaɪəl / (BrE)
a button or function on a telephone that automatically calls the last number dialed

Examples

Redial button
If the line is busy, you can use the redial to try again quickly.
She pressed the redial button to call her friend back without entering the number again.
Most modern phones have a redial feature for convenience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speed dial - a telephone feature that allows quick calling of stored numbers
