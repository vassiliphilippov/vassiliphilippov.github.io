border on obsession

verb
to be nearly obsessive
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdər ɑn əbˈsɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə ɒn əbˈsɛʃn̩ / (BrE)
to be so enthusiastic or interested in something that it is almost unhealthy or excessive

Examples

To border on obsession closely
His attention to detail bordered on obsession, impressing everyone in the room.
She has a passion for gardening that borders on obsession.
The artist's focus on perfection bordered on obsession, leaving little time for anything else.
