subthalamic nucleus

noun
oval mass of grey matter in the subthalamus
9.9
Pronunciation: / sʌbθəˈlæmɪk ˈnuːkliəs / (AmE), / sʌbθəˈlamɪk ˈnjuːkliəs / (BrE)
a small oval mass of grey matter located in the lower part of the subthalamus, associated with the striate body in the brain

Examples
