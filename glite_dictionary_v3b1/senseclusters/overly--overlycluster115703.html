Synonyms

  • excessively
    3.0 2/m
    excessively is stronger and more formal to say too much, while overly feels more neutral and common in speech.
  • too
    1.0 1000/m
    too is the common short word used before adjectives, while overly is slightly more formal and used in careful writing.
  • inordinately
    7.5 0.3/m
    inordinately sounds formal and rare and means an unusually large degree, while overly is more common and used often in speech.
  • unduly
    6.9 1.0/m
