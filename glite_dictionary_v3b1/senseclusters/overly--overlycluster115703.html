overly

adverb
excessively; to an undue degree
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚli / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəli / (BrE)
to an excessive degree; more than is normal, necessary, or desirable

Examples

An overly worried person
He became overly concerned about the noise next door.
She tends to be overly critical of her own work.
The food was overly salty for my taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessively - to a degree that is too much; more than is reasonable
  • antonym: insufficiently - to a degree that is not enough
