saluter

noun
person who greets or salutes
Pronunciation: / səˈluːtər / (AmE), / səˈluːtə / (BrE)
a person who greets or salutes others, typically by acknowledging them with a gesture or words

Examples

Military saluter
As a military saluter, he always stood at attention and acknowledged his superiors.
She became known as the neighborhood saluter, always saying hello to everyone she met.
The saluter at the entrance welcomed each guest with a warm smile.
