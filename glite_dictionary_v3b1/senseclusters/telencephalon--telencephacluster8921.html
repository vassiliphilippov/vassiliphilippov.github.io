telencephalon

noun
anterior part of the forebrain
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlənˈsɛfəlɒn / (AmE), / ˌtɛlənˈsɛfəlɒn / (BrE)
the anterior part of the forebrain, consisting of the cerebrum and related structures

Examples

The telencephalon part
Damage to the telencephalon can affect memory, behavior, and reasoning skills.
During embryonic development, the telencephalon differentiates into important brain regions.
The telencephalon plays a crucial role in voluntary movement and cognitive functions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cerebrum - the principal and most anterior part of the brain
