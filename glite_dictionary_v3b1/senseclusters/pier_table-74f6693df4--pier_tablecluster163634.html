pier table

noun
narrow table placed under a pier glass
5.8 7.3
Pronunciation: /pɪr ˈteɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /pɪə ˈteɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
A low, narrow table designed to be set below a pier glass against a wall, often used in hallways or entryways.
