her

determiner
female possessive determiner
Pronunciation: / hɜr / (AmE), / hɜː / (BrE)
used before a noun to indicate that something belongs to or is connected with a female person or animal previously mentioned

Examples

Her bag
She placed her keys on the kitchen table before making coffee.
The dog wagged its tail when it saw her favorite toy.
After the meeting, everyone admired her presentation skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: his - used to show something belongs to a male person or animal
  • antonym: my - used to show something belongs to the speaker
  • antonym: their - used to show something belongs to more than one person or to someone of unknown gender
