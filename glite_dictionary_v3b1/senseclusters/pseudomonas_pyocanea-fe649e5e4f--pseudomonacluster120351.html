Pseudomonas pyocanea

noun
species of aerobic bacteria
Pronunciation: / suːˌdoʊməˈnæs paɪoʊˈkeɪniə / (AmE), / sjuːˌdəʊməˈnas paɪəʊˈkeɪnɪə / (BrE)
a species of aerobic bacteria belonging to the genus Pseudomonas, known for its blue-green pigment production
