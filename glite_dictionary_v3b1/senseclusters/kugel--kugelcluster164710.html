kugel

noun
Jewish baked dish
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkuːɡəl / (BrE)
a baked dish made with noodles or potatoes, often served sweet or savory in Jewish cuisine

Examples

Sweet kugel dessert
My grandmother makes a delicious kugel with raisins and cinnamon for the holidays.
At the potluck, everyone enjoyed the savory potato kugel served alongside the roast chicken.
He brought a homemade noodle kugel to share at the community dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: casserole - a baked dish with various ingredients cooked together
  • synonym: latke - a fried potato pancake in Jewish cuisine
