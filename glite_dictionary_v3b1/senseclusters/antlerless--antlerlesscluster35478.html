antlerless

noun
animal without antlers
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈænt.lər.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈænt.lə.ləs/ (BrE)
a deer or other animal that does not have antlers, especially referred to in hunting or wildlife management contexts

Examples

An antlerless animal
The wildlife biologist recorded the number of antlerless deer observed in the area.
Hunters are often given special permits to harvest antlerless animals to help control the population.
During the survey, most of the deer spotted were antlerless and likely female.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buck - an adult male deer, typically with antlers
  • synonym: doe - an adult female deer, usually without antlers
