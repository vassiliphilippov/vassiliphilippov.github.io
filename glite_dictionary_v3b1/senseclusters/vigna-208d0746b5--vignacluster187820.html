Vigna

noun
genus of tropical vines and herbs (e.g., cowpea, mung bean)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnjə /; / ˈviːn.jə / (AmE), / ˈvɪɡnə /; / ˈvɪɡ.nə / (BrE)
