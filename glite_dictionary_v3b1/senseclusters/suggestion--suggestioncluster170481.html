suggestion

noun
a slight amount; a hint or trace
Pronunciation: / səgˈdʒɛstʃən, səˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / səgˈdʒɛstʃən, səˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
a very slight amount of something; a hint or trace

Examples

A suggestion of color
Her perfume left only a suggestion of fragrance in the room after she had gone.
The sky was cloudless, with just a suggestion of pink at the horizon.
There was a suggestion of sarcasm in his tone that was almost imperceptible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - a small or indirect amount; a slight sign
  • synonym: trace - a very small amount of something
