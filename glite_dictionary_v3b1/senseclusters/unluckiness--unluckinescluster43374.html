unluckiness

noun
state of bad luck
3.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlʌkinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌkinəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of having or experiencing bad luck or misfortune

Examples

His unluckiness continued

The team's unluckiness finally ended when they scored a last-minute goal.
She blamed her unluckiness for not getting the job despite her qualifications.
