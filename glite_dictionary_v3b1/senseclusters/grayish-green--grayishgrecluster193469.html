grayish-green

adjective
green tinged with gray
6.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgreɪ.ɪʃ griːn/ (AmE), /ˈgreɪ.ɪʃ griːn/ (BrE)
Of a green color tinged with gray; intermediate in color between green and gray.

Examples

A grayish-green color
She wore a grayish-green scarf that perfectly matched her eyes.
The grayish-green moss covered the rocks along the riverbank.
The walls were painted a soft grayish-green that felt calming and natural.
She painted her bedroom walls a calming greyish-green to create a relaxing atmosphere.
The leaves had a distinct greyish-green hue that blended with the overcast sky.
Their uniforms were a dull greyish-green, intended to provide camouflage in the forest.
