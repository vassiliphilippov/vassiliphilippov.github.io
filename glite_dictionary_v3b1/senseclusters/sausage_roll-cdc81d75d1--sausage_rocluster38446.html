sausage roll

noun
pastry-wrapped sausage meat snack
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔsɪdʒ roʊl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːsɪdʒ rəʊl / (BrE)
A baked food consisting of sausage meat rolled in pastry, typically served as a snack or party food.

Examples

A sausage roll snack
She baked a batch of sausage rolls for the party.
The bakery sells fresh sausage rolls every morning.
Would you like a sausage roll with your tea?
