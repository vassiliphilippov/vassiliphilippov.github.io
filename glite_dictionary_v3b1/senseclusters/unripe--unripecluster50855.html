unripe

adjective
not fully mature or developed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈraɪp / (AmE), / ʌnˈraɪp / (BrE)
not fully developed or mature; not ready for use or consumption (used for fruit, food, ideas, etc.)

Examples

An unripe fruit
Green tomatoes are typically unripe, but they can be cooked and eaten in some dishes.
The wood was still unripe and too wet to burn efficiently.
You shouldn't eat the unripe bananas because they can cause stomach discomfort.
Her idea seemed too unripe for implementation at this stage.
It's risky to make decisions based on unripe projections and incomplete data.
The plan was rejected because it was still unripe and lacked crucial details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immature - not fully developed or grown
  • synonym: mature - fully developed physically; full-grown
  • antonym: ripe - (of fruit or vegetables) fully matured and ready to eat
