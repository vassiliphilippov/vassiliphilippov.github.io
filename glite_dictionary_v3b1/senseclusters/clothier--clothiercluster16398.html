clothier

noun
seller of clothing; men's clothing merchant
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊðiər / (AmE), / ˈkləʊðɪə / (BrE)
a person or company that makes or sells clothing

Examples

A clothier shop
He apprenticed with a prominent clothier in the city before opening his own shop.
Many wealthy clients depended on their trusted clothier for the latest fashions.
The clothier offered a fine selection of suits and dress shirts.
The local clothier is known for high-quality suits and dresses.
After years as a clothier, she opened her own fashion store in the city.
Many clothiers attend trade shows to find the latest fabric trends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boutique - a small shop selling fashionable clothes or accessories
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes, repairs, or alters clothing professionally
