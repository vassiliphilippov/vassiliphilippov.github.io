myringotomy

noun
surgical incision into the eardrum
5.3 9.9

a surgical incision into the eardrum to relieve pressure or drain fluid from the middle ear

Examples

A myringotomy surgery

Following the myringotomy, a small tube was inserted to help with drainage.
The doctor recommended a myringotomy to relieve the buildup of pressure in the child's ear.
