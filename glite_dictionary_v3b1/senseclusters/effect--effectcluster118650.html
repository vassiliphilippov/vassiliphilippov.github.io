effect

noun
main idea or theme of a work
Pronunciation: / ɪˈfɛkt / (AmE), / ɪˈfɛkt / (BrE)
the central meaning or main idea expressed in a speech, piece of writing, or artistic work

Examples

The effect of her words
Her poems often have a subtle effect that lingers long after reading.
The effect of the novel was a powerful call for social change.
Though the speech was complex, its effect was clear to all in the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: message - the underlying idea or meaning conveyed by a work or speech
  • synonym: theme - the central topic or idea of a work
