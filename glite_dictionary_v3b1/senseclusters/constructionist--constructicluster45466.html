constructionist

adjective
related to building
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to the work of building structures or to the construction industry.

Examples

Constructionist methods in building
The company followed a strict constructionist approach to ensure the building met all safety standards.
Her constructionist background helped her manage large infrastructure projects efficiently.
A constructionist perspective is essential when planning urban development.

  • antonym: demolition - relating to the act of tearing down buildings
