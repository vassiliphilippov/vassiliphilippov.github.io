Morrigu

proper noun
Celtic war goddess
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrɪɡu / (AmE), / ˈmɒrɪɡu / (BrE)
A war goddess in Celtic mythology, often associated with fate, battle, and death.

Examples

Morrigu war goddess
In Irish mythology, Morrigu is depicted as a shape-shifting goddess of death and fate.
Legends say that Morrigu could influence the outcome of battles with her magical powers.
The ancient Celts often invoked Morrigu before going into war.
