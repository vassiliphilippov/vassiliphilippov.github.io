think twice

verb
consider carefully before acting
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /θɪŋk twaɪs/ (AmE), /θɪŋk twaɪs/ /; / /fɪŋk twaɪs/ (BrE)
to consider something carefully before acting, especially to avoid making a mistake or bad consequences

Examples

To think twice before acting
She didn't think twice about helping a stranger in need.
We need to think twice before accepting the offer.
You should always think twice before making a major purchase.
He didn't think twice about quitting his job, but later he regretted it.
Sarah always thinks twice before making big purchases.
You should think twice before sharing personal information online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: act impulsively - to act without careful consideration
  • synonym: reconsider - to consider again, especially with the possibility of change
  • antonym: rush into - to do something too quickly without proper consideration
