spool

noun
cylindrical holder for flexible material
7.1
Pronunciation: / spuːl / (AmE), / spuːl / (BrE)
a cylindrical object around which thread, tape, film, or other flexible material can be wound for storage or use

Examples

A thread spool
A film spool was jammed inside the old camera.
She wound the leftover thread onto a spool to keep it neat.
The sewing kit includes a spool of white cotton.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobbin - a cylinder or cone holding thread, yarn, or wire, used especially in sewing machines
  • synonym: reel - a cylinder on which film, wire, thread, or other flexible material can be wound
