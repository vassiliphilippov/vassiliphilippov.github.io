complexification

noun
extension to complex numbers
9.9
Pronunciation: /kəm.plek.sɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəm.plek.sɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /kɒm.plek.sɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of extending a real mathematical object, such as a vector space or manifold, to the complex numbers by allowing complex scalars or coordinates
