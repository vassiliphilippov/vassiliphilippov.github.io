introduction

noun
the act of starting something new
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən /; / ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.trəˈdʌk.ʃən /; / ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the act of bringing something into use or starting something new for the first time
