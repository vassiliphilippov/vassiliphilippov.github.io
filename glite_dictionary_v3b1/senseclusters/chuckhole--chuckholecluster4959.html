chuckhole

noun
hole in a road surface
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌkˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a hole or depression in a road surface caused by wear or weathering, especially one that is hazardous to vehicles
