practice teacher

noun
student teacher in training
Pronunciation: / ˈpræk.tɪs ˌtiː.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈpræk.tɪs ˌtiː.tʃə / (BrE)
A college student who teaches under the supervision of a certified teacher as part of their teacher training.

Examples

A practice teacher student
As a practice teacher, he learned valuable skills from his supervising mentor.
During her final semester, she worked as a practice teacher at the local elementary school.
The university requires all education majors to serve as a practice teacher before graduation.
