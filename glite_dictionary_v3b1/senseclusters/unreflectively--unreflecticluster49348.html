unreflectively

adverb
without reflecting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪvli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈflɛktɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that does not reflect light, images, or sound; without producing a reflection

Examples

Unreflectively painted walls
The new paint allowed the walls to appear unreflectively, reducing glare in the bright room.
Her sunglasses were designed to look unreflectively even in direct sunlight.
The surface of the lake shimmered unreflectively after the wind disturbed the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matte - having a dull surface that does not reflect light
  • antonym: reflectively - in a way that reflects light, images, or sound
