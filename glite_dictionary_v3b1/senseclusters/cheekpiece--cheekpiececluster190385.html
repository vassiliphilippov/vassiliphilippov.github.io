cheekpiece

noun
helmet part for cheek
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːkˌpiːs / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːkˌpiːs / (BrE)
a part of a helmet, hat, or similar headgear that covers or supports the cheek

Examples

Helmet cheekpiece
The knight adjusted the cheekpiece on his helmet for a better fit.
Modern fencing masks often include a padded cheekpiece for extra protection.
The cheekpiece of the riding helmet was designed to be both comfortable and secure.
