pulmonary tuberculosis

noun
tuberculosis affecting the lungs
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlməˌnɛri tuˌbɚkjuˈloʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˈpʌlmənəri tjuːˌbɜːkjʊˈləʊsɪs / (BrE)
A form of tuberculosis that affects the lungs, often leading to progressive wasting of the body.

Examples

Pulmonary tuberculosis infection
Early detection of pulmonary tuberculosis can significantly improve the chances of a full recovery.
The patient was admitted to the hospital with symptoms of pulmonary tuberculosis.
Vaccination programs have helped to reduce the incidence of pulmonary tuberculosis worldwide.
