starter

noun
first course of a meal
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtər / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a small dish served at the beginning of a meal to stimulate the appetite; the first course

Examples

Soup starter
The soup was served as a starter at the dinner party.
We ordered a delicious starter before our main course arrived.
Would you like a starter or go straight to the main meal?

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetizer - a small dish served before the main part of a meal
