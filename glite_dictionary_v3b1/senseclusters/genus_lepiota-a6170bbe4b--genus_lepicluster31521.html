genus Lepiota

A genus of agaric fungi with white spores, including several edible and poisonous species such as parasol mushrooms.

Examples

Genus Lepiota mushrooms
Many species in the genus Lepiota are commonly known as parasol mushrooms.
The genus Lepiota is distinguished by its white spores and scaly caps.
While some members of the genus Lepiota are edible, others can be dangerously poisonous.
