mons

noun
mound of tissue above female pubic bone
8.4
Pronunciation: / mɑnz / (AmE), / mɒnz / (BrE)
a mound of fatty tissue covering the pubic area, especially in women; often refers to the mons pubis.

Examples

The mons pubis
During the surgery, the doctor noted swelling in the mons.
Some people experience irritation of the mons after shaving.
The mons provides protection for the underlying pubic bone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mons pubis - the rounded mass of fatty tissue lying over the joint of the pubic bones, especially in women
