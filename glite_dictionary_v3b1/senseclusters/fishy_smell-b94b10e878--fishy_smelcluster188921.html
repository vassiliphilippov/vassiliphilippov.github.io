fishy smell

noun
unpleasant odor like fish
2.7 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪʃ.iː smel/ (AmE), /ˈfɪʃ.iː smel/ (BrE)
an unpleasant or strong odor that resembles the smell of fish, often considered disagreeable

Examples

A fishy smell
If you detect a fishy smell from fresh seafood, it's best not to eat it.
She noticed a fishy smell coming from the refrigerator and decided to clean it thoroughly.
The whole apartment was filled with a fishy smell after dinner last night.
