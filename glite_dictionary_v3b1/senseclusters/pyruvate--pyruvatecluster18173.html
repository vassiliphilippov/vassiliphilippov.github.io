pyruvate

noun
cellular respiration compound
8.0
Pronunciation: / paɪˈruːveɪt / (AmE), / paɪˈruːveɪt / (BrE)
a chemical compound (and its salts or esters) produced during the breakdown of glucose in cells, important in cellular respiration and metabolism
