meet

verb
experience or encounter (reaction)
2.0 20/m
Pronunciation: / mit / (AmE), / miÀêt / (BrE)
to experience or encounter something, especially an event, reaction, or something unpleasant

Examples

To meet a problem
Her speech met with strong criticism from the audience.
My application met little enthusiasm from the committee.
The new policy met widespread support across the community.
Many soldiers met their end on the battlefield during the war.
Several explorers met disaster in their search for the lost city.
She feared she would meet a similar fate if she spoke out.
