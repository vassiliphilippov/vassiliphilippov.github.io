Synonyms

  • expectably
    3.9 0.02/m
    expectably describes something that happens as expected in ordinary cases, while predictably stresses that it can be foreseen.
  • expectedly
    3.5 0.03/m
    expectedly is used when events happen as people anticipate, while predictably describes events that can be forecast.
  • unsurprisingly
    3.7 1.59/m
    unsurprisingly often shows the speaker's lack of surprise at an outcome, while predictably describes something that follows patterns.
