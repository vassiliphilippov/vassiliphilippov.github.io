predictably

adverb
in an expected or foreseeable manner
Pronunciation: / prɪˈdɪktəbli / (AmE), / prɪˈdɪktəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is expected or can be predicted.

Examples

To predictably happen
Predictably, the movie ended with the hero saving the day.
She reacted predictably to the surprise, barely showing any emotion.
The results, predictably, matched the scientists' expectations.
