Synonyms

  • option
    2.2 127/m
    an option is a single possibility presented among others, while choice is the act of picking between several possibilities.
  • alternative
    3.2 51/m
    an alternative is a different possibility offered when one option fails, while choice is the general act of selecting between options.
  • selection
    1.8 17/m
    a selection is the specific item chosen from a set, while choice refers to the act or the items you can pick between.
  • pick
    1.0 18/m
    a pick is an informal name for the thing someone chooses, while choice is the neutral word for the act or the chosen item.
  • decision
    1.0 253/m
    a decision is a firm conclusion often reached after thought, while choice can mean either the act of selecting or the thing chosen.
