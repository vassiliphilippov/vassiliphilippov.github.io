chippy

adjective
easily offended
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪpi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪpi / (BrE)
easily annoyed or quick to take offense, often in a defensive or irritable way

Examples

A chippy reply

Try not to sound chippy when you respond to their feedback.
