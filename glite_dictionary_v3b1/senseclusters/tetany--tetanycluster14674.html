tetany

noun
muscular twitching and cramps (medical)
9.9 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈte.tə.niː/ /; / /ˈte.tə.nɪ/ (AmE), /ˈte.tə.niː/ /; / /ˈte.tə.nɪ/ (BrE)
A medical condition marked by muscular twitching, cramps, and sometimes seizures, often due to calcium or vitamin D deficiency, or alkalosis.

Examples

Tetany muscle cramps
Doctors quickly recognized the involuntary muscle spasms as signs of tetany.
The patient developed tetany as a result of severe calcium deficiency.
Treatment for tetany often involves correcting underlying electrolyte imbalances.
