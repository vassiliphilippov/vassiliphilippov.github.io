ooooh

verb
to express amazement
9.3
Pronunciation: / uː / (AmE), / uː / (BrE)
to make a long exclamation of 'ooooh', usually to express surprise, admiration, or excitement

Examples

To ooooh in wonder
The children oooohed in wonder as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
Everyone in the room oooohed when the chef revealed the beautiful cake.
She couldn't help but ooooh at the sparkling necklace in the display case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aah - to make a sound expressing surprise or delight
  • antonym: boo - to make a sound expressing disapproval or surprise
