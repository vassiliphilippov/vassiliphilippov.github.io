fencing mask

noun
protective mesh mask for fencing
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛn.sɪŋ mæsk / (AmE), / ˈfɛn.sɪŋ mɑːsk / (BrE)
a protective face covering made of fine mesh, worn by fencers to shield the face during bouts

Examples

A fencing mask
All participants are required to wear a fencing mask for safety during the competition.
Before entering the piste, she carefully adjusted her fencing mask to ensure a snug fit.
His fencing mask protected him from accidentally being struck in the face by the opponent's blade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering for all or part of the face
