memorialist

noun
writer of petitions
Pronunciation: / məˈmɔːriəlɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈmɔːriəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who writes or submits a formal written statement, petition, or account to an authority

Examples

Memorialist submits petition
The memorialist carefully drafted a letter to the city council requesting a new park.
As a skilled memorialist, she often represented her community's concerns to government officials.
The memorialist gathered signatures before submitting the formal petition to the authorities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applicant - a person who formally requests something, especially in writing
  • synonym: petitioner - a person who presents a formal request to an authority
