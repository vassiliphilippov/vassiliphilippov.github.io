rubber-hose cryptanalysis

noun
coercive technique to extract cryptographic secrets
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbər hoʊz ˌkrɪptəˈnæləsɪs / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə həʊz ˌkrɪptəˈnaləsɪs / (BrE)
The use of coercion or violence, rather than technical methods, to force someone to reveal cryptographic secrets such as passwords or encryption keys.
