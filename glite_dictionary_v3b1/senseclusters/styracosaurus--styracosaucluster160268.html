styracosaurus

noun
ceratopsian dinosaur with frill and nose horn
Pronunciation: / staɪˌrækəˈsɔrəs / (AmE), / staɪˌrækəˈsɔːrəs / (BrE)
a genus of herbivorous dinosaur from the Late Cretaceous period, known for its large bony frill edged with spikes and long nose horn

Examples

The styracosaurus dinosaur
A child pointed excitedly at the picture of the styracosaurus in his dinosaur book.
Researchers believe the styracosaurus used the spikes on its frill for defense and display.
The museum displays a replica of a styracosaurus skeleton in its dinosaur exhibit.
