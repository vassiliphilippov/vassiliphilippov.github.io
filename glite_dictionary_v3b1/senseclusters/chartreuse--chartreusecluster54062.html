chartreuse

noun
yellow-green color
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrˈtruːz / (AmE), / ʃɑːˈtrɜːz / (BrE)
a pale yellowish-green color, between green and yellow

Examples

Chartreuse color
His tie added a splash of chartreuse to his otherwise conservative suit.
She chose a dress in chartreuse for the summer party.
The walls of the kitchen were painted a bright chartreuse that cheered up the space.
