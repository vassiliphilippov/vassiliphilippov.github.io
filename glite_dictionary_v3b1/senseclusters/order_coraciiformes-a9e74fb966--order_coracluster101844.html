order Coraciiformes

noun
taxonomic order of birds including kingfishers and hornbills
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər kəˌreɪsɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kəˌreɪsɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of usually colorful birds that includes rollers, kingfishers, hornbills, hoopoes, motmots, bee-eaters, and todies
