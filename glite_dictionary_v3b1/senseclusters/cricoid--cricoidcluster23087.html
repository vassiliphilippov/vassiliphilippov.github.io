cricoid

adjective
ring-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪkɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈkraɪkɔɪd / (BrE)
shaped like a ring; ring-shaped, especially used to describe anatomical structures.

Examples

Cricoid shape
The doctor pointed out the cricoid cartilage at the base of the larynx.
A cricoid structure can be seen in certain cross-sectional images of the throat.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the cricoid area during the procedure.
