lancinating

adjective
sharp, stabbing pain
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlænsɪˌneɪtɪŋ /; / ˈlæn.sɪˌneɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlansɪˌneɪtɪŋ /; / ˈlan.sɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(of pain or sensation) sharp, stabbing, and intensely piercing

Examples

Migraine sufferers often experience intense, lancinating headaches.
The patient described a lancinating pain shooting down her leg.

She was awakened by a lancinating headache that felt as if her skull was being pierced.
The patient described a lancinating pain shooting down his leg whenever he moved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piercing - (of pain or sensation) extremely sharp or intense
  • synonym: stabbing - (of pain) sharp and sudden, like a stab
