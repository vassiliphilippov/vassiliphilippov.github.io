instill

verb
introduce (a liquid) drop by drop
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪl / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪl / (BrE)
to introduce a liquid into something, especially drop by drop

Examples

To instill drops
Nurses often instill medication into patients' eyes to aid recovery.
The doctor will instill the eye drops to treat the infection.
You should instill the prescribed solution into your pet's ear carefully.
After the injury, a nurse carefully instilled saline into the wound.
Doctors often instil fluids into the ear to treat infections.
The instructions say to instil two drops into each eye every morning.
