Battle of Chapultepec

proper noun
decisive 1847 battle in the Mexican-American War
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬l əv tʃəˌpʊl.təˈpɛk / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv tʃəˌpʊl.təˈpɛk / (BrE)
a decisive battle during the Mexican-American War (1847) in which American forces defeated Mexican troops at Chapultepec, near Mexico City

Examples

The Battle of Chapultepec in 1847
Many U.S. Marines remember the Battle of Chapultepec as a significant moment in their history.
The Battle of Chapultepec marked a turning point in the Mexican-American War.
The victory at the Battle of Chapultepec allowed American forces to capture Mexico City.
