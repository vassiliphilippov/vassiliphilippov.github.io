forenoon

noun
the time between dawn and noon
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌnun / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.nuːn / (BrE)
the period of the day between dawn and noon; the morning hours.

Examples

The forenoon hours
By the forenoon, the village market was bustling with activity.
The gardener worked through the forenoon before taking a break at noon.
We planned the hike for the early forenoon to avoid the midday heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morning - the early part of the day, from sunrise to noon
