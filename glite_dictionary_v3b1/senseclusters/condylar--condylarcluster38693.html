condylar

adjective
relating to a bone's condyle
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.dɪ.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dɪ.lə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a condyle (a rounded prominence at the end of a bone, usually forming part of a joint).

Examples

The condylar joint
Abnormal condylar growth can affect jaw alignment and facial appearance.
During the examination, the doctor noted a swelling in the condylar region of the femur.
The surgeon repaired the condylar fracture using a specialized plate.
