Blue Peter

noun
blue flag signaling a ship is ready to sail
8.0
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈpiːtər / (AmE), / bluː ˈpiːtə / (BrE)
a blue flag with a white square in the center, flown by a ship to signal that it is ready to sail

Examples

The Blue Peter flag

When the Blue Peter is raised, it means the ship is about to leave port.
