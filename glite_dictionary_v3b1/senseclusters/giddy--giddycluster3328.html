giddy

adjective
feeling dizzy or unsteady
3.5 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪd.iː/ /; / /ˈgɪd.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgɪd.iː/ /; / /ˈgɪd.ɪ/ (BrE)
feeling as if you might fall because you are lightheaded or experiencing a whirling sensation; likely to make someone feel unsteady or dizzy

Examples

Giddy feeling
She felt giddy after spinning around in circles for so long.
Standing on the edge of the tall building made me feel giddy.
The giddy height of the mountain left the climbers breathless and unsteady.
