intermontane

noun
area between mountains
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈmɑːnteɪn / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈmɒnteɪn / (BrE)
a region or area of land situated between mountain ranges

Examples

An intermontane area
The scientists explored the intermontane to collect samples of unique plant species.
Many rivers flow through the intermontane separating the two mountain ranges.
Wildlife in the intermontane has adapted to the sheltered environment between the peaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
