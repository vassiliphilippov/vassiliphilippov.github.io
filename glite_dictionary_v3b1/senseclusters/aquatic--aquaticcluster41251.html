aquatic

noun
water-dwelling plant or animal
2.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɑːtɪk / (AmE), / əˈkwætɪk / (BrE)
a plant or animal that lives in or on water, especially a water-dwelling plant

Examples

Rare aquatics
Botanists collected several unusual aquatics from the Amazon river basin.
Many aquatics have adapted to life submerged beneath the surface of the pond.
The greenhouse specializes in rare aquatics for ornamental gardens.
