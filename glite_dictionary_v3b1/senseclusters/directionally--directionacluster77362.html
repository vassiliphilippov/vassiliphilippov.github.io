directionally

adverb
in a general trend
5.3
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃənəli / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows the general direction or trend, rather than exact details or precision

Examples

Directionally correct results

Analysts found the market was directionally moving upward despite some fluctuations.
Her comments were directionally accurate, even if not completely detailed.

  • antonym: precisely - in an exact and accurate way
