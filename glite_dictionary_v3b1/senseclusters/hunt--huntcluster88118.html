hunt

verb
drive or chase out by force
Pronunciation: / hʌnt / (AmE), / hʌnt / (BrE)
to force someone or something out of a place, often by pursuing them

Examples

To hunt cats away
Neighbors hunted the stray cats off their property.
The authorities hunted the protestors from the square at dawn.
The new regime hunted political opponents out of the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drive out - to force someone or something to leave a place
  • synonym: expel - to force someone to leave a place or organization officially
