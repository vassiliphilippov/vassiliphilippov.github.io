unleaded

adjective
not containing lead (esp. petrol/gasoline)
2.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛdəd / (AmE), / ʌnˈliːdɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance, especially petrol or gasoline) not containing or treated with lead.

Examples

Unleaded gasoline
He always checks to make sure his car is filled with unleaded gasoline.
Many cars now require unleaded petrol instead of the older leaded fuel.
The unleaded version of the paint is safer for indoor use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leaded - containing or treated with lead, especially in the context of fuel
