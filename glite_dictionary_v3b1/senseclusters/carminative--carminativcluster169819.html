carminative

adjective
relieving gas in the digestive tract
8.4
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈmɪnətɪv / (AmE), / kɑːˈmɪnətɪv / (BrE)
(of a medicine or substance) relieving flatulence or colic by expelling gas from the stomach or intestines.

Examples

Carminative medicine
Doctors often recommend carminative herbs to relieve excessive gas and bloating.
Peppermint oil is well known for its carminative properties, helping to soothe digestive discomfort.
The carminative effect of ginger makes it popular in traditional remedies for stomach upset.
