yerba

noun
plant for mate tea
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛrbə / (AmE), / ˈjɜːbə / (BrE)
a South American plant (or its dried leaves) used to make the traditional drink mate

Examples

Yerba mate leaves
The traditional drink is made by steeping yerba leaves in hot water.
She bought a package of yerba to prepare mate at home.

He bought a bag of yerba to prepare mate at home.
The taste of yerba can be strong and slightly bitter for first-time drinkers.

  • synonym: mate - a traditional South American drink made from yerba leaves
  • synonym: tea - a hot drink made by infusing dried leaves in water
