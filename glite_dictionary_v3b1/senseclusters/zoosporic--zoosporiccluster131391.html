zoosporic

adjective
producing zoospores
9.9
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈspɔrɪk / (AmE), / zəʊˈspɒrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or producing zoospores, which are motile asexual spores found in some fungi and algae

Examples

Zoosporic fungi phase
The zoosporic phase allows the organism to move through water to find new habitats.
Certain algae are known for their zoosporic reproduction, which helps them spread rapidly.
Researchers observed the zoosporic cells swimming toward a nutrient source under the microscope.
