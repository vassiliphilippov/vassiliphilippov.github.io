Amboina pine

noun
tropical tree in the Philippines/Moluccas; dammar resin source
Pronunciation: / æmˈboʊnə paɪn / (AmE), / æmˈbəʊnə paɪn / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to the Moluccas and Philippines, known as a source of dammar resin
