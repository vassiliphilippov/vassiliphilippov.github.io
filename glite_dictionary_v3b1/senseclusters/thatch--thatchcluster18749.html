thatch

noun
roof of straw or similar plant material
9.9 4.8
Pronunciation: /θætʃ/ (AmE), /θætʃ/ (BrE)
A roof covering made from layers of dried plant material such as straw, reeds, or grass.

Examples

A thatch roof
After the storm, part of the old thatch needed to be repaired by the skilled roofer.
The cottage had a neat thatch that blended beautifully with the countryside.
Traditional English villages often feature houses with a straw thatch instead of tiles.
