pennyweight unit
Pronunciation: / diː dʌbəl.juː tiː / (AmE), / diː dʌbəl.juː tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for pennyweight, a unit of mass used for weighing precious metals

Examples

Gold dwt value
The jeweler measured the gold chain and recorded its weight as 3 dwt.
Each silver coin was stamped with its weight in dwt for accuracy.
He bought a small nugget that weighed just under 2 dwt.
