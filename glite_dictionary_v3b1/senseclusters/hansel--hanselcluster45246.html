hansel

noun
good luck gift
Pronunciation: / ˈhænzəl / (AmE), / ˈhænzəl / (BrE)
a gift given for good luck at the beginning of a new venture, event, or home.

Examples

A hansel for luck
It is traditional to give a hansel when someone starts a new job.
They brought a small hansel to wish their neighbors luck in their new apartment.
Receiving a hansel made her feel welcomed in her new community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farewell gift - a present given when someone leaves a place or job
  • synonym: housewarming gift - a present given to celebrate moving into a new home
