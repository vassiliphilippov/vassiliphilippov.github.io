put down

verb
belittle or criticize someone
1.0 4.9
Pronunciation: / pʊt daʊn / (AmE), / pʊt daʊn / (BrE)
to criticize someone unfairly or make someone feel unimportant; to belittle someone, usually verbally

Examples

To put down a friend
He always tries to put down others to make himself feel better.
It's not right to put down your teammates when they make a mistake.
She felt discouraged after her boss put down her ideas in the meeting.

Related Terms
