angiofibroma

noun
benign skin growth
Pronunciation: / ˌændʒioʊfaɪˈbroʊmə / (AmE), / ˌændʒiəʊfaɪˈbrəʊmə / (BrE)
a small, benign skin growth made of blood vessels and fibrous tissue, often appearing as a red or flesh-colored bump

Examples

Skin angiofibroma
The doctor diagnosed the red bump on her nose as an angiofibroma after a careful examination.
Children with tuberous sclerosis may develop multiple angiofibromas on their faces.
A small angiofibroma was removed from the patient's cheek during the outpatient procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fibroma - a benign tumor made of fibrous or connective tissue
  • synonym: hemangioma - a benign tumor made up of blood vessels
