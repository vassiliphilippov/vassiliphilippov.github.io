vitamin M

noun
alternative term for folic acid (B9)
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.tə.mɪn ɛm / (AmE), / ˈvɪt.ə.mɪn ɛm / (BrE)
another name for folic acid (vitamin B9), a vitamin essential for cell growth and reproduction

Examples

Vitamin M deficiency
A deficiency in vitamin M can lead to anemia and other health problems.
Doctors may recommend taking extra vitamin M during pregnancy to support fetal development.
Leafy green vegetables are a good source of vitamin M.
