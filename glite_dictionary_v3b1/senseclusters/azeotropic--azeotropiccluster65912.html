azeotropic

adjective
constant-boiling mixture
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪziəˈtroʊpɪk / (AmE), / ˌeɪziːəˈtrəʊpɪk / (BrE)
describing a mixture whose components evaporate together at a constant ratio, making them difficult to separate by distillation

Examples

An azeotropic mixture
An azeotropic mixture cannot be separated by simple distillation.
The azeotropic properties of the solution made purification challenging for the chemists.
Some industrial solvents are designed to be azeotropic for more efficient processing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterogeneous - consisting of different or diverse components
  • synonym: homogeneous - having a uniform composition throughout
