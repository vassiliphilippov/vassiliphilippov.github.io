tagua

noun
ivory-like palm nut
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːɡwə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡwə / (BrE)
a hard white nut from a South American palm, used as a substitute for ivory in making buttons and carvings

Examples

Tagua nut carving
The craftsman carved beautiful figurines from tagua instead of elephant ivory.
Many eco-friendly buttons are now made from tagua because it is a sustainable resource.
She bought a necklace featuring polished tagua beads at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ivory - a hard, white material from elephant tusks
  • synonym: ivory nut - the hard white seed of certain palms, used as a substitute for ivory
