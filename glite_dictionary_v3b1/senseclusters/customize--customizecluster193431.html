Synonyms

  • personalize
    4.0 6.90/m
    personalize adds a person's name, interests, or style to something, while customize changes or builds it to suit specific needs.
  • tailor
    4.2 7.70/m
    tailor fits or alters an item exactly for a person's need or purpose, while customize means changing or building to suit wishes.
  • tailor-make
    4.9 0.03/m
    tailor-make creates or adapts something especially for one person's needs or a purpose, while customize can include small changes or complete builds.
  • custom-make
    5.3 0.13/m
    custom-make produces an item from scratch to a buyer's specs, while customize also covers changing ready-made goods to fit needs.
  • individualize
    6.3 0.63/m
    individualize makes something unique to one person, often for identity or difference, while customize simply adapts it to needs.
