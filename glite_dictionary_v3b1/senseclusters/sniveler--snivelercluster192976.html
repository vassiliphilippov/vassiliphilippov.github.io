sniveler

noun
person who breathes noisily due to nasal congestion
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪvələr / (AmE), / ˈsnɪvələ / (BrE)
a person who breathes audibly through a congested nose, often making sniffing or sniffling sounds

Examples

A sniveler in winter
As a child, I was a notorious sniveler every winter.
During allergy season, the office was full of snivelers struggling to breathe.
The cold was so bad that he became a constant sniveler at work.
