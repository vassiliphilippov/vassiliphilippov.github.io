Synonyms

  • mythification
    9.1 0.03/m
    mythification makes someone or something seem legendary and symbolic, while mythologization turns real people or events into myths by idealizing them.
  • mythmaking
    7.0 0.20/m
    mythmaking creates or promotes widely believed stories to shape public belief, while mythologization focuses on turning real people or events into exaggerated myths.
  • mythopoiesis
    9.9 0.00/m
    mythopoiesis invents new mythic stories for art and literature, while mythologization treats real things as myths through exaggeration or idealizing.
