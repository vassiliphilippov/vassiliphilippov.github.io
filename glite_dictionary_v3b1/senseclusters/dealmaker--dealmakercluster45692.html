dealmaker

noun
business negotiator
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːlˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈdiːlˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who arranges, negotiates, or facilitates business agreements or deals

Examples

A skilled dealmaker
As a skilled dealmaker, she brought together companies from different industries.
The dealmaker was praised for closing the contract ahead of schedule.
Every successful merger needs a talented dealmaker to guide the process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broker - a person who arranges transactions between parties
  • synonym: negotiator - a person who discusses and arranges agreements
  • antonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress or agreement
