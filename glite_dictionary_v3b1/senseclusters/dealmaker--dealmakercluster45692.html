Examples

A skilled dealmaker

Synonyms

  • broker
    4.4 3.65/m

  • negotiator
    4.6 7.68/m

  • middleman
    3.9 0.87/m
    a middleman connects parties and passes information or goods between them, while a dealmaker actively arranges and finalizes business agreements.
