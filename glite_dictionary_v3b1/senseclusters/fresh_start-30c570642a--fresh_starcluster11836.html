fresh start

noun
a new or renewed beginning
1.0
Pronunciation: / frɛʃ stɑrt / (AmE), / frɛʃ stɑːt / (BrE)
an opportunity to begin again, especially after problems or difficulties

Examples

A fresh start
After the company went bankrupt, she saw it as a fresh start in her career.
He wanted a fresh start after the end of his difficult relationship.
Moving to a new city gave them a fresh start away from their past troubles.
After losing his job, Tom looked at moving abroad as a fresh start.
Following her divorce, she was determined to make a fresh start in a different city.
Many people see the new year as a chance for a fresh start.
