dieselization

noun
conversion to diesel engines
6.1
Pronunciation: /diː.zəl.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.zəl.aɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of replacing steam or other types of engines with diesel engines, especially in transportation systems

Examples

The dieselization of trains
The dieselization of the national railways led to faster and more reliable train service.
Many factories underwent dieselization to improve energy efficiency in the mid-20th century.
After dieselization, maintenance costs for the bus fleet were significantly reduced.
