bearcat

noun
arboreal mammal of Asia (binturong)
6.2 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈber.kæt/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.kæt/ (BrE)
an arboreal mammal of Southeast Asia with shaggy black fur and a long prehensile tail, also known as the binturong
