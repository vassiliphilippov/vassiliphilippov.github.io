Lacanian

noun
follower of Lacan
Pronunciation: / ləˈkeɪniən / (AmE), / ləˈkɑːniən / (BrE)
a person who follows, supports, or practices the psychoanalytic theories of Jacques Lacan

Examples

A Lacanian scholar
The seminar attracted several prominent Lacanians from universities around the world.
As a Lacanian, he often discussed the importance of language in psychoanalysis.
Many Lacanians debate the interpretation of Lacan's later works.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Freudian - a person who follows the theories of Sigmund Freud
  • synonym: Jungian - a person who follows the theories of Carl Jung
