inferior ophthalmic vein

noun
vein draining part of the eye socket
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriər ɑfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪərɪə ɒfˈθælmɪk veɪn / (BrE)
a vein that drains blood from the floor and inner wall of the eye socket

Examples

The inferior ophthalmic vein drains
An infection in the orbital region may spread via the inferior ophthalmic vein.
During surgery, care must be taken to avoid damaging the inferior ophthalmic vein.
The inferior ophthalmic vein is important in draining blood from the lower part of the orbit.
