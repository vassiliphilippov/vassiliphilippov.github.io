valetudinarian

adjective
excessively concerned about health
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvælətjudəˈnɛriən / (AmE), / ˌvælətjuːdɪˈneəriən / (BrE)
showing excessive concern for one's own health or suffering from constant health worries; characteristic of a valetudinarian.

Examples

Valetudinarian lifestyle
His valetudinarian habits kept him from enjoying outdoor activities with friends.
She developed a valetudinarian attitude after a series of minor illnesses.
The protagonist is portrayed as a valetudinarian old man who worries endlessly about his health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypochondriac - a person abnormally anxious about their health
