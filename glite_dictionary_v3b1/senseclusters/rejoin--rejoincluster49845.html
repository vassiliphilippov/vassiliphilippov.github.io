rejoin

verb
respond quickly or sharply
Pronunciation: / riˈdʒɔɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɔɪn / (BrE)
to reply or respond quickly, especially in a sharp or witty manner

Examples

To rejoin quickly
He paused for a moment, then rejoined with a clever remark.
When challenged, the professor rejoined confidently, leaving no room for doubt.
“That isn't fair,” she rejoined with a hint of irritation in her voice.
