Pittsburgh

proper noun
city in Pennsylvania, USA
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtsbɜːrɡ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtsbɜːɡ / (BrE)
A city in southwestern Pennsylvania, USA, at the confluence of the Allegheny and Monongahela rivers to form the Ohio River, historically known for its steel industry and home to Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Pittsburgh.

Examples

Pittsburgh city in Pennsylvania
Carnegie Mellon University is a major research institution based in Pittsburgh.

The steel mills of Pittsburgh played a vital role in American industry.
