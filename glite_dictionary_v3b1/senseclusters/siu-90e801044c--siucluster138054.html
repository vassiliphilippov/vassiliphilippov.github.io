SIU

noun
investigative department
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs aɪ ˈjuː / (AmE), / ˌɛs aɪ ˈjuː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Special Investigations Unit, a department that investigates serious, complex, or suspicious incidents, especially in police or insurance contexts

Examples

SIU investigation team
The police department's SIU is responsible for investigating officer-involved incidents.
After noticing irregularities, the company called in the SIU to review the insurance claim.
The SIU handled several high-profile cases last year involving financial fraud.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fraud unit - a department that investigates fraudulent activities
  • synonym: internal affairs - a police unit that investigates officers' conduct
