witnesseth

verb
attests (archaic legal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtnəsɛθ / (AmE), / ˈwɪtnɪsɛθ /; / ˈwɪtnɪsɪθ / (BrE)
used in legal documents to mean attests, serves as evidence, or shows as proof (archaic/formal)
