rest area

noun
in-game safe zone to save and heal
Pronunciation: / c8r5bst cc5bri59 / (AmE), / c8r5bst cce59ri59 / (BrE)
(in video games) a designated safe location in a game where players can recover health, rest characters, and save or restore progress.

Examples

Game rest area
Before the boss fight they found a rest area where characters could heal and save progress.
The game's design encouraged exploration by placing rest area tents near dangerous zones to restore health.
After hours playing, she logged off from the multiplayer server and returned to the rest area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: checkpoint - a place in a game that records progress, often allowing respawn or reload from that position
  • synonym: safe zone - an area in a game where characters are protected from enemies and cannot be harmed
  • synonym: save point - a location in a game where players can save their progress
