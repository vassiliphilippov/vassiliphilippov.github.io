lacertid

noun
a lizard of the family Lacertidae
Pronunciation: / ləˈsɜrtɪd / (AmE), / ləˈsɜːtɪd / (BrE)
A lizard of the family Lacertidae, native to Europe, Asia, and Africa, typically terrestrial and active during the day.

Examples

A lacertid lizard
Scientists are studying how climate change may affect lacertid populations in Asia.
Several species of lacertid are commonly found throughout southern Europe.
The green lacertid basked in the midday sun on a rock.
