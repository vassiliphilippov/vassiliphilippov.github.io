heshima

noun
ceremony to honor
Pronunciation: / hɛˈʃimə / (AmE), / hɛˈʃiːmə / (BrE)
a formal act, tribute, or public ceremony held to honor someone

Examples

A heshima ceremony
The community gathered for a heshima to celebrate the teacher's years of service.
A special heshima was organized to honor the retiring chief.
During the heshima, many people shared stories about the leader's kindness and wisdom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: honor - to show great respect or recognition for someone
  • antonym: insult - an action or remark that is disrespectful or scornful
  • synonym: tribute - an act, statement, or gift intended to show gratitude or respect
