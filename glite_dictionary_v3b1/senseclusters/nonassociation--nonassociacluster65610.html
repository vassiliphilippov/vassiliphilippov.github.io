nonassociation

noun
prohibition of association
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəsəʊsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a legal clause or agreement that prohibits someone from associating with certain people or groups

Examples

Nonassociation clause
The contract included a nonassociation clause to prevent employees from joining rival companies.
Violating the nonassociation agreement could result in legal action against the former partner.
The court upheld the nonassociation provision as part of the settlement terms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: association - the act of joining or connecting with others
  • synonym: exclusion - the act of preventing someone from joining or being part of something
