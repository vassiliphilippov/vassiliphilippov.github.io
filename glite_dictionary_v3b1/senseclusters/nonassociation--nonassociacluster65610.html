nonassociation

noun
prohibition of association
9.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ə.soʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nə.nə.soʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /nɒn.ə.səʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /nə.nə.səʊ.sɪ.ˈeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

The court upheld the nonassociation provision as part of the settlement terms.
