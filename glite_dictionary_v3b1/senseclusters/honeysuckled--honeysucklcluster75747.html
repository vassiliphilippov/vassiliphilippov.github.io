honeysuckled

adjective
fed on honeysuckle
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌniˌsʌkəld / (AmE), / ˈhʌniˌsʌkəld / (BrE)
having fed on or been covered with nectar from honeysuckle flowers, especially referring to insects or birds.

Examples

Honeysuckled butterfly
The butterfly emerged honeysuckled after spending the morning among the fragrant blossoms.
Birds often leave the garden honeysuckled and dusted with golden pollen.
After hours in the meadow, the bee returned honeysuckled and heavy with sweet pollen.
