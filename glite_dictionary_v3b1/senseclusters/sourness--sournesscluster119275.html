sourness

noun
sullen or resentful disposition
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊrnəs / (AmE), / ˈsaʊənəs / (BrE)
a sullen, moody, or resentful attitude or disposition

Examples

Emotional sourness
Despite their success, a lingering sourness remained between the two former friends.
His sourness seemed to infect the whole office, making everyone tense.
There was a noticeable sourness in her tone when she spoke about her colleague.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitterness - resentful or cynical attitude
  • antonym: sweetness - pleasant or agreeable quality or disposition
