coeducate

verb
educate both sexes together
4.5
Pronunciation: / koʊˈedʒuˌkeɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈedʒʊkeɪt / (BrE)
to educate boys and girls together in the same classes or school

Examples

To coeducate boys and girls
Historically, some institutions refused to coeducate, maintaining separate classes for boys and girls.
Many modern schools now coeducate students to promote gender equality.
The board decided to coeducate all incoming classes starting next year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: segregate - to separate groups, often by sex or race
