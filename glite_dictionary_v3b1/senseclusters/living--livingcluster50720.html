living

adjective
still in active use
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
existing or alive; not dead, extinct, or obsolete

Examples

A living creature

These traditions have continued within living memory.
Scientists were thrilled to find a living specimen of a plant thought to be extinct.
The discovery of a living coelacanth amazed marine biologists worldwide.
This rare temple is the only living example of its architectural style.
