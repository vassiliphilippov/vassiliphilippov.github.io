castable

adjective
suitable for casting
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be shaped or formed by casting, especially in a mold

Examples

Castable metal alloy
This new alloy is highly castable and can be used for complex machine parts.
The artist chose a castable resin to create detailed sculptures.
Not all metals are castable; some require different manufacturing processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moldable - able to be shaped or formed in a mold
  • antonym: uncastable - not able to be shaped by casting
