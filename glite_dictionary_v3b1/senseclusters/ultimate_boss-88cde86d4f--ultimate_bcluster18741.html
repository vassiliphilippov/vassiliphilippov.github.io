ultimate boss

noun
final/difficult opponent in a game
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltəmət bɑs / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət bɒs / (BrE)
the final or most difficult opponent to defeat in a game, especially a video game

Examples

The ultimate boss fight
After hours of playing, he finally defeated the ultimate boss and completed the game.
Facing the ultimate boss was much harder than any of the previous challenges.
In many role-playing games, the ultimate boss has unique abilities that require special strategies.
