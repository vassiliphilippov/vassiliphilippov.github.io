ronin

noun
masterless samurai
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊniːn / (AmE), / ˈrəʊniːn / (BrE)
a samurai in historical Japan who had no master or lord

Examples

A wandering ronin
The wandering ronin searched for a new purpose after losing his master.
In many stories, a ronin is depicted as a skilled but lonely swordsman.
The village hired a ronin to protect them from bandits.
