lighten

verb
make something less severe or oppressive
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtn̩ /; / ˈlaɪtən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtn̩ /; / ˈlaɪtən / (BrE)
to make something less heavy or reduce a burden, either physically or emotionally

Examples

To lighten the load
A good conversation can often lighten your stress and improve your mood.
Her friends helped to lighten the burden after her family crisis.
Regular breaks during the workday can lighten the mental strain employees experience.
She asked her friends to lighten her suitcase before checking it in.
The workers lightened their backpacks by removing unnecessary items.
We need to lighten the load if we want the boat to go faster.

  • synonym: reduce - make smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
