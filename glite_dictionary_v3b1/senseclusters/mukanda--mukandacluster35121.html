mukanda

noun
initiation group
a group of boys or young men who have completed the mukanda initiation ritual, especially in certain Central African cultures

Examples

Mukanda group
After the ceremony, the mukanda gathered to celebrate their new status in the community.
The elders welcomed the mukanda back to the village with songs and dancing.
Each year, a new mukanda is formed as boys complete the traditional rites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: initiate - a person who has been admitted to a special group or activity through a ritual
