wingback

noun
football position on the wing
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋ.bæk / (BrE)
a player position in football, usually located on the flank, responsible for both defense and attack along the wing

Examples

Attacking wingback
Modern football formations often rely on a wingback to stretch the opposition's defense.
She played as a wingback throughout the tournament and provided several crucial assists.
The coach praised the wingback for his outstanding defensive work and attacking runs.
