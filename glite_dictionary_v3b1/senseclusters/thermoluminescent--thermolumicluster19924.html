thermoluminescent

adjective
emitting light when heated
9.9
Pronunciation: /θɜːr.moʊ.luː.mɪ.ˈnes.ənt/ (AmE), /θɜː.məʊ.luː.mɪ.ˈnes.ənt/ (BrE)
emitting light when heated after being exposed to radiation

Examples

A thermoluminescent detector
A thermoluminescent detector can measure the amount of radiation a worker has been exposed to.
Scientists use thermoluminescent materials to study the history of archaeological artifacts.
The thermoluminescent properties of certain crystals make them useful in dosimetry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fluorescent - emitting light while being exposed to radiation
  • synonym: phosphorescent - emitting light without heat after exposure to radiation
