chemoautotrophic

adjective
energy from chemicals
9.9 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåkiÀêmo äÀå…îÀêt…ôÀàtro äf…™k / (AmE), / ÀåkiÀêm…ô äÀå…îÀêt…ôÀàtr…íf…™k / (BrE)

Examples

Chemoautotrophic bacteria
Some bacteria living near hydrothermal vents are chemoautotrophic and survive without sunlight.
Scientists study chemoautotrophic organisms to understand life in extreme environments.
The chemoautotrophic process allows certain microbes to thrive deep underground.

Synonyms
