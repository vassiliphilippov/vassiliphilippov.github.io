pubococcygeal

adjective
pelvic floor muscle
Pronunciation: / ˌpjuːboʊkɑkˈsɪdʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌpjuːbəʊkɒkˈsɪdʒiəl / (BrE)
relating to the muscle or area between the pubic bone and the coccyx in the pelvic floor

Examples

Pubococcygeal muscle area
The pubococcygeal muscle plays a key role in supporting pelvic organs.
Doctors recommend exercises to strengthen the pubococcygeal area after childbirth.
Injury to the pubococcygeal muscle can lead to problems with bladder control.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pelvic - relating to the pelvis
