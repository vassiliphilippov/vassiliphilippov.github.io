reservation

noun
advance arrangement for accommodation or service
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzɚˈveɪʃən /; / ˌrɛzərˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
an arrangement made in advance to have a seat, room, or service held for you

Examples

Hotel reservation
I made a reservation for a table at the restaurant next Friday.
Please confirm your reservation before arriving at the hotel to avoid any issues.
She forgot to make a reservation, so the theater was already fully booked.
I made a reservation for two at the Italian restaurant for Friday night.
If you don't have a reservation, you may not get a room at the hotel.
The airline will confirm your reservation by email within 24 hours.
All tickets must be purchased in advance, and reservations are highly recommended during the holidays.
I called the restaurant to make a reservation for dinner at seven.
She confirmed her hotel reservation before leaving for her trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booking - an arrangement to reserve accommodations or a place in advance
  • antonym: cancellation - the act of calling off a previously made reservation
