tiger cowrie

noun
tropical sea snail with spotted shell
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡɚ ˈkaʊri / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə ˈkaʊri / (BrE)
a large tropical sea snail (Cypraea tigris) with a glossy, oval shell marked with dark spots, often used for ornamentation
