Socratism

noun
Socrates' philosophy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkrəˌtɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsɒkrəˌtɪzəm / (BrE)
the philosophical ideas, methods, or teachings associated with Socrates, especially his emphasis on dialogue and questioning
