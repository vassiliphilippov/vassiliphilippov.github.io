passing play

noun
American football play involving a forward pass
4.2 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈpæs.ɪŋ pleɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpɑː.sɪŋ pleɪ/ (BrE)
(in American football) a play in which one player throws the ball forward to a teammate.

Examples

The team executed a risky passing play in the final seconds of the game.
Their offense relies heavily on the passing play to move the ball down the field.
