counter-narrative

noun
opposing perspective
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌnærətɪv / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌnærətɪv / (BrE)
a story or perspective that opposes or challenges a dominant or mainstream narrative

Examples

A strong counter-narrative
The documentary offers a powerful counter-narrative to the official version of events.
Activists used social media to spread a counter-narrative challenging the government's claims.
Her book serves as a counter-narrative to the commonly accepted history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alternative narrative - a different story or version from the main one
  • antonym: narrative - a story or account of events
