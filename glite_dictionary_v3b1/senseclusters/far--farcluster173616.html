far

adjective
of or on the right side (animal or vehicle)
2.2
Pronunciation: / fɑr / (AmE), / fɑː / (BrE)
(in animal handling or driving) on or relating to the right side of an animal or vehicle.

Examples

Far side horse
In driving teams, the far leader is harnessed to the right.
Stand on the far side of the carriage to hold the reins.
The far horse began to move before the others.
