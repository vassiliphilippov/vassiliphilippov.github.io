grit

noun
courage and determination
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪt / (AmE), / ɡrɪt / (BrE)
courage and resolve; strength of character in facing difficulty or adversity.

Examples

Show grit under pressure
Despite many setbacks, his grit never wavered.
It takes real grit to start a business from nothing.
She showed incredible grit throughout the long and difficult journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one; bravery
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of bravery; fearfulness
