noncompliant

noun
person who refuses to comply
3.7
Pronunciation: / nɑn.kəmˈplaɪ.ənt / (AmE), / nɒn.kəmˈplaɪ.ənt / (BrE)
a person who refuses to obey rules or follow prescribed instructions, especially in medical or legal contexts.

Examples

A noncompliant patient
Schools sometimes face challenges when dealing with noncompliant students who disrupt the learning environment.
The doctor noted in the chart that the patient was noncompliant with the prescribed medication regimen.
The new policy aims to improve follow-up with noncompliant individuals who miss their appointments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defiant - openly resisting authority or rules
