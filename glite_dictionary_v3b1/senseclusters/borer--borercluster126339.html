borer

noun
wood-boring insect or larva
8.1 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…îr…ö / (AmE), / Ààb…îÀêr…ô / (BrE)
an insect, larva, or mollusk that bores into wood, often causing damage to trees and timber

Examples

Wood borer insect
An infestation of borer larvae can weaken wooden beams in old houses.
Farmers often worry about crop losses due to borers infesting their fields.
The fallen tree was riddled with holes made by a borer.

Synonyms

  • woodborer
    8.4 0.00/m
    woodborer is an insect or mollusk that bores into and damages wood, while borer often names pests attacking trees and timber.
  • woodworm
    7.2 0.02/m
    woodworm is the larva of insects that eat and bore through wood in buildings, while borer means any wood-damaging insect or mollusk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: woodworm - a larva that bores into wood, especially in furniture
