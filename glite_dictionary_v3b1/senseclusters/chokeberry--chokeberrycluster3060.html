chokeberry

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈtʃoʊk.be.riː/ /; / /ˈtʃoʊk.be.rɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃəʊk.be.riː/ /; / /ˈtʃəʊk.be.rɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

Chokeberry bush

Many people use chokeberries to make juice and jams because of their tart flavor.
In spring, the chokeberry is covered with clusters of small white flowers.
