chokeberry

noun
Aronia shrub or fruit
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃoʊkˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃəʊkˌbɛri / (BrE)
a deciduous shrub of the genus Aronia, known for its white flowers and clusters of dark, tart edible berries

Examples

Chokeberry bush
The chokeberry bush in our garden produces fruit every autumn.
Many people use chokeberries to make juice and jams because of their tart flavor.
In spring, the chokeberry is covered with clusters of small white flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aronia - a genus of shrubs producing dark, tart berries
  • degree lower: blueberry - a small, sweet blue fruit from the Vaccinium genus
