silky-leaved

adjective
having smooth, glossy leaves
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki livd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki liːvd / (BrE)
(of a plant) having leaves that are smooth, glossy, or soft like silk.

Examples

Silky-leaved plants
Gardeners often choose silky-leaved shrubs for their attractive, soft foliage.
Several species of willow are known for their silky-leaved branches.
The silky-leaved plant stood out among its rougher neighbors in the garden.
