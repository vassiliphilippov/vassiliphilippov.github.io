dazzled

adjective
temporarily blinded by intense light
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzəld / (AmE), / ˈdɑːzəld / (BrE)
(of a person) having vision temporarily impaired or overwhelmed by intense light.

Examples

Dazzled by headlights
After stepping into the spotlight, he stood dazzled for a few seconds, unable to see the audience.
She shut her dazzled eyes against the sun's brilliance.
The headlights left me dazzled as I drove down the dark road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blinded - temporarily deprived of sight
  • synonym: dazzle - to blind temporarily by a bright light; impress deeply
