squirrel away

verb
to save or store something for future use
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɝː.əl əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈskwɪr.əl əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to save or store something, especially money, for future use, often secretly or gradually

Examples

To squirrel away money
After every paycheck, she squirrels away a portion for emergencies.
He has been squirreling away extra cash for their holiday.
They squirreled away old photographs in a box under the bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoard - to accumulate and store things, often secretly
