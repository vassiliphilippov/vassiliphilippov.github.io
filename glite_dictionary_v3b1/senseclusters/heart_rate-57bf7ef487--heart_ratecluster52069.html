heart rate

noun
number of heartbeats per minute
2.5 12/m
Pronunciation: / hÉ‘rt reÉªt / (AmE), / hÉ‘Ët reÉªt / (BrE)
the number of times the heart beats in a minute, often used as an indicator of health or fitness

Examples

Heart rate monitor
During exercise, your heart rate increases to pump more blood.
She wore a smartwatch to monitor her heart rate throughout the day.
The doctor checked his heart rate after the stress test.

Synonyms

  • pulse rate
    2.8 0.50/m
    pulse rate is the number of heartbeats per minute often measured to check a person's health, while heart rate names that same measure used for fitness.
  • beats per minute
    2.7 0.49/m
    beats per minute is the unit used to report a person's pulse or heart rate in medicine and fitness, while heart rate names the actual heartbeat count.
