Erysimum cheiranthoides

noun
a yellow-flowered European weed (mustard family)
Pronunciation: / ɛˌrɪsɪˌnʊm ˌkaɪəˌrænˈθɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ɛˌrɪsɪˌnʊm ˌkaɪəˌrænˈθɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
