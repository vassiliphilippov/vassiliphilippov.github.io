stigmatist

noun
person marked by religious stigmata
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪɡmətɪst / (AmE), / ˈstɪɡmətɪst / (BrE)
a person whose body is marked by religious stigmata, especially marks resembling the wounds of the crucified Christ

Examples

A stigmatist with wounds
Many pilgrims traveled to see the renowned stigmatist who was said to bear the marks of Christ.
Skeptics questioned whether the stigmatist's wounds were of divine origin or self-inflicted.
The church conducted an investigation into the mysterious wounds that appeared on the stigmatist's hands.
