ghibli

noun
Saharan hot wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪbli / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbli / (BrE)
a hot, dry wind that blows from the Sahara Desert across North Africa

Examples

Sahara ghibli wind
The ghibli made the air thick with dust and sand for days.
Travelers waited for the ghibli to pass before continuing their journey across the desert.
Farmers prepared their crops for the arrival of the ghibli each summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sirocco - a hot, dry wind from North Africa blowing into southern Europe
