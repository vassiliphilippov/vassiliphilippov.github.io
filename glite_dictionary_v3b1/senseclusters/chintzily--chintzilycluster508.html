chintzily

adverb
in a stingy or cheap manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪnt.sɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnt.sɪ.li / (BrE)
In a stingy, cheap, or excessively frugal manner.

Examples

To tip chintzily
He tipped the waiter chintzily despite the excellent service.
The hostess decorated the party chintzily, using only paper plates and plastic cups.
Their wedding gifts were chosen chintzily, with little thought or expense.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generously - in a generous manner
  • synonym: stingily - in a stingy manner
