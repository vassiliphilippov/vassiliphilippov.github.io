stator coil

noun
stationary coil in a motor or generator
6.3 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsteɪ.tər kɔɪl/ (AmE), /ˈsteɪ.tə kɔɪl/ (BrE)
the stationary winding or coils in an electric motor or generator, around which the rotor revolves and through which current flows

Examples

The stator coil winding
Damage to the stator coil can cause the generator to fail completely.
Engineers inspected the stator coil for signs of overheating and wear.
The stator coil is responsible for generating the magnetic field in the electric motor.
