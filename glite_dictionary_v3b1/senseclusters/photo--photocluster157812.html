photo

noun
photograph; camera-made image
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
A photograph; a picture of a person or scene made using a camera, usually printed or in digital format.

Examples

A photo from party
Could you please send me a photo from the party?
I took a photo of the sunset at the beach yesterday.
She keeps a photo of her family on her desk at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photograph - an image recorded by a camera
  • synonym: picture - a visual representation such as a painting, drawing, or photograph
