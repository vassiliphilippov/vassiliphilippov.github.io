Koran basher

noun
a person who attacks the Koran
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɑːn ˌbæʃər / (AmE), / kəˈrɑːn ˌbæʃə / (BrE)
a person who harshly criticizes or attacks the Koran, often in a hostile or disrespectful manner
