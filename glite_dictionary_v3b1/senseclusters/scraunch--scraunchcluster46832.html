scraunch

verb
make a loud crushing or crunching noise
Pronunciation: / skrɔn(t)ʃ / (AmE), / skrɔːn(t)ʃ / (BrE)
to make a loud, harsh, or crushing noise, especially by treading or crushing; to crunch

Examples

Gravel scraunched loudly
Gravel scraunched beneath our tires as we pulled into the country lane.
She hated the way his boots scraunched through the frost on winter mornings.
The old carriage wheels scraunched over the pebbled driveway, breaking the morning silence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrunch - make a loud crunching sound, especially with the mouth or underfoot
