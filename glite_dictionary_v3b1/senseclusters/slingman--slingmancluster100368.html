slingman

noun
sling operator
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋmæn / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋmæn / (BrE)
a person who uses or operates a sling, especially in construction or ancient warfare

Examples

Ancient slingman
The ancient army included a skilled slingman among its ranks.
A slingman must have good aim to hit distant targets accurately.
During the demonstration, the slingman showed how to launch stones with great force.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archer - a person who shoots with a bow and arrows
  • synonym: slinger - a person who throws stones or other projectiles with a sling
