start

noun
sudden involuntary movement
8.2
Pronunciation: / stɑrt / (AmE), / stɑːt / (BrE)
a sudden involuntary movement, often caused by surprise or fear

Examples

With a start of fear
He woke from his dream with a start, heart pounding in his chest.
She gave a start when the door slammed shut unexpectedly.
The sudden noise made every child in the room jump with a start.
