pertinacity

noun
persistent determination
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝːtəˈnæsəti / (AmE), / ˌpɜːtɪˈnæsəti / (BrE)
the quality of being persistently determined or stubbornly tenacious in achieving something

Examples

Strong pertinacity
Despite many setbacks, he showed remarkable pertinacity and refused to give up.
Her pertinacity in pursuing her goals impressed everyone around her.
The team's pertinacity was key to overcoming the difficult challenges faced during the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resoluteness - firm determination or resolve
  • synonym: stubbornness - refusal to change one's mind or attitude
  • synonym: tenacity - the quality of being very determined or persistent
