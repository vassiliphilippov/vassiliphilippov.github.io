boink

verb
to bounce playfully
Pronunciation: / bɔɪŋk / (AmE), / bɔɪŋk / (BrE)
to make a light, bouncy sound or movement, often in a playful or comical way

Examples

To boink off walls
The tennis ball boinked off the net and surprised the players.
He accidentally boinked his head on the low doorframe and laughed.
The toy frog boinks across the table when you press its back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce - to spring back after hitting a surface
  • antonym: thud - to make a dull, heavy sound when hitting something
