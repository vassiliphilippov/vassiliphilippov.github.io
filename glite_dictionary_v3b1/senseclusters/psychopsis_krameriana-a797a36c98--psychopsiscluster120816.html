Psychopsis krameriana

noun
a species of South/Central American orchid
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkɑpsɪs krɑːˈmɛriːˌænə / (AmE), / saɪˈkɒpsɪs ˌkrɑːmɪəriˈɑːnə / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to South and Central America, with flowers smaller than those of Psychopsis papilio; sometimes classified under the genus Oncidium.

Examples

Psychopsis krameriana orchid
Her collection featured several orchids, including Psychopsis krameriana with its delicate, butterfly-like flowers.
Some botanists debate whether Psychopsis krameriana belongs in the genus Oncidium or Psychopsis.
