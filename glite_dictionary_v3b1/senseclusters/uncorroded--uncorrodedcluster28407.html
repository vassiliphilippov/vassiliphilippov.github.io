uncorroded

adjective
not affected by corrosion
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈroʊdɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈrəʊdɪd / (BrE)
not damaged, eaten away, or affected by corrosion

Examples

Uncorroded metal surface
The ancient coins were surprisingly uncorroded despite being buried for centuries.
After years of exposure, the steel beams remained completely uncorroded.
The laboratory stored the chemicals in uncorroded containers to ensure safety.
