piezo

noun
spark-generating device
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪzoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːɛtsəʊ / (BrE)
a device that uses the piezoelectric effect to generate a spark for igniting gas, commonly found in lighters or grills

Examples

Gas stove piezo
The piezo in my gas stove stopped working, so I have to use matches now.
He pressed the piezo button on the grill to light the burners.
Many modern lighters use a piezo instead of a traditional flint wheel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: igniter - a device used to start a fire or ignite gas
  • synonym: lighter - a small device for producing a flame
