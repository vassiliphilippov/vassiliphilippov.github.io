period of play

noun
interval of active play in sports or games
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪriəd əv pleɪ / (AmE), / ˈpɪəriəd əv pleɪ / (BrE)
A distinct interval or portion of time during which play continues in a game, match, or performance.

Examples

The second period of play
After a short break, the second period of play will begin.
Each period of play in hockey lasts twenty minutes.
The referee signaled the end of the first period of play.
