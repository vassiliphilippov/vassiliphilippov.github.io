aloft

adverb
in the upper parts of a ship
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: / əˈlɔft / (AmE), / əˈlɒft / (BrE)
at or on or toward the masthead or upper rigging of a ship; in a position high above the deck.

Examples

Climb aloft on ship
From his vantage point aloft, he could see the entire deck below.
The sailor climbed aloft to fix the rigging before the storm arrived.
While working aloft, she secured her harness tightly to the mast.
