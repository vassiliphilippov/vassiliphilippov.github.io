unroll

verb
duplicate loop body for optimization

(in programming, especially compiler optimization) to transform a loop by duplicating its body multiple times and adjusting the iterations to reduce loop overhead and improve performance.

Examples

To unroll a loop
The compiler can unroll small loops automatically during the optimization pass.
We unrolled the inner loop manually to reduce branch overhead and improve speed.
Benchmarking showed that unrolling the hot-loop doubled throughput on their test dataset.
