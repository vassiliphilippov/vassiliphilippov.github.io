heavy snow

noun
a large quantity of falling snow
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvi snoʊ / (AmE), / ˈhɛvi snəʊ / (BrE)
A large amount of snow falling within a short period, often causing disruption.

Examples

A heavy snowstorm
All flights were canceled due to heavy snow at the airport.
Driving in heavy snow can be dangerous, especially on rural roads.
The weather forecast warns of heavy snow across the region tonight.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: blizzard - a severe snowstorm with strong winds and low visibility
  • synonym: snowstorm - a storm with heavy snowfall and strong winds
