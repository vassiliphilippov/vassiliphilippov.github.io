illuminist

noun
follower of Illuminationism
Pronunciation: / ɪˈluːmənɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈluːmɪnɪst / (BrE)
a follower of Illuminationism, a medieval Islamic philosophical school that emphasizes inner light and intuitive knowledge

Examples

Medieval illuminist philosopher
The medieval illuminist believed that true knowledge comes from inner light and intuition.
Many writings by the illuminist school use symbols to express philosophical ideas.
An illuminist might focus on spiritual insight rather than logical reasoning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of philosophy
  • antonym: rationalist - a person who emphasizes reason as the main source of knowledge
