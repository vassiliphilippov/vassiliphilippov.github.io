ineffectualness

noun
lack of effectiveness or power
Pronunciation: / ɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəs / (AmE), / ɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being ineffectual; lack of ability or power to produce a desired effect.

Examples

The ineffectualness of leadership
Critics pointed out the ineffectualness of his leadership during the crisis.
She blamed her ineffectualness in the meeting on lack of preparation.
The ineffectualness of the new policy became apparent after several months without progress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: effectiveness - the degree to which something is successful in producing a desired result
  • synonym: ineffectiveness - the quality of not producing an intended result or effect
