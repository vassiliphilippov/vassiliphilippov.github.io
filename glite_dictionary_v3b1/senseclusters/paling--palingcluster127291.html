paling

noun
a fence of upright pickets
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a fence made of upright wooden or metal stakes, or one of the thin, pointed stakes used to make such a fence
