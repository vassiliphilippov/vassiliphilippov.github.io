foregone

adjective
already decided
4.6 1.09/m
Pronunciation: / f…îrÀà…°o än / (AmE), / f…îÀêÀà…°…ô än / (BrE)
already decided or certain to happen, with the outcome not in doubt

Examples

Foregone conclusion
With their strong lead, victory was a foregone conclusion.
The result of the election seemed like a foregone outcome to most observers.
Given the evidence, his resignation was almost a foregone decision.
