Scleroderma aurantium

noun
a species of earthball fungus
9.9
Pronunciation: / sklɛr.əˈdɜr.mə ɔːˈræn.ti.əm / (AmE), / sklɛr.əˈdɜː.mə ɔːˈran.ti.əm / (BrE)
A species of earthball fungus with a dingy brownish-yellow outer surface covered in small warts and a dark purplish interior.

Examples

Scleroderma aurantium fungus
Scleroderma aurantium is often mistaken for a truffle because of its round shape and tough outer skin.
Scientists collected samples of Scleroderma aurantium from a forest to study its spore patterns.
The surface of Scleroderma aurantium is usually covered with small, wart-like bumps.
