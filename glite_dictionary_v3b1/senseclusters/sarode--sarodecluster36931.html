sarode

noun
Indian string instrument
Pronunciation: / səˈroʊd / (AmE), / səˈrəʊd / (BrE)
a stringed musical instrument from India, played with a plectrum and known for its deep, resonant sound

Examples

To play the sarode
The musician captivated the audience with a beautiful performance on the sarode.
She has been practicing the sarode for several years under a master teacher.
The deep, rich tones of the sarode are central to many classical Indian concerts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: veena - a traditional Indian stringed instrument
