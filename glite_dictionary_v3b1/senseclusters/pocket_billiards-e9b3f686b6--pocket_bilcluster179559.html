pocket billiards

noun
cue games played on a table with six pockets
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑkɪt ˈbɪljɚdz / (AmE), / ˈpɒkɪt ˈbɪliədz / (BrE)
any of various games, such as pool, played on a billiard table with six pockets into which balls are struck with a cue
