disagreeably

adverb
in an unpleasant or objectionable manner
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈɡriəbli / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbli / (BrE)
In a manner that is unpleasant or causes discomfort; in an unfriendly or objectionable way.

Examples

To respond disagreeably
He responded disagreeably to my polite request, leaving me stunned by his rudeness.
She disagreeably criticized every suggestion, making the meeting uncomfortable for everyone.
The weather turned disagreeably cold as the sun went down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disagreeable - unpleasant, offensive, or unfriendly
  • antonym: pleasantly - in a pleasant or agreeable manner
