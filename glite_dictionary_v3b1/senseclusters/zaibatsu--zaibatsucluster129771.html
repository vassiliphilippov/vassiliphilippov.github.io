zaibatsu

noun
Japanese business group
9.9
Pronunciation: /zeɪ.ˈbæt.suː/ /; / /zaɪ.ˈbæt.suː/ (AmE), /zaɪ.ˈbæt.suː/ /; / /zeɪ.ˈbæt.suː/ (BrE)
