zaibatsu

noun
Japanese business group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌzaɪˈbɑːtsuː / (AmE), / ˌzaɪˈbætsuː / (BrE)
a large Japanese business conglomerate, especially one that was powerful before World War II

Examples
