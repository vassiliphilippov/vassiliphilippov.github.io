thong

verb
to bind with a strip
9.8
Pronunciation: / θɔŋ / (AmE), / θɒŋ / (BrE)
to fasten, bind, or secure something using a thong, which is a narrow strip of leather or similar material

Examples

To thong a bag
The old satchel was carefully thonged shut with a thin leather strap to protect the papers.
He thonged the bundle together before carrying it across the field.
Ancient scrolls were often thonged to keep them tightly rolled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tie - to fasten or secure with a cord or string
  • antonym: unbind - to release from being tied or fastened
