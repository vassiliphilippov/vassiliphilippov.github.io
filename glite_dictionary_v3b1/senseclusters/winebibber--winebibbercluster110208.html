winebibber

noun
heavy wine drinker
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪnˌbɪbər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌbɪbə / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a person who drinks large amounts of wine

Examples

A winebibber drinks wine
In the old village, the mayor was known as a notorious winebibber.
The poem described a winebibber who spent his evenings in the tavern.
Few people today use the word winebibber to describe someone who enjoys wine excessively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkard - a person who drinks too much alcohol
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
