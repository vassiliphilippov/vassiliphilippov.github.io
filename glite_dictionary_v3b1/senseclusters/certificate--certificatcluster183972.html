certificate

verb
formally present a certificate
Pronunciation: / sərˈtɪfɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / səˈtɪfɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to officially recognize or authorize someone by issuing a certificate

Examples

To certificate a teacher
She was certificated at the end of the course in a small formal event.
The organization certificated hundreds of volunteers last year for their training completion.
The school principal will certificate each graduate during the ceremony.
After completing all the requirements, the board will certificate you as a licensed practitioner.
Only accredited institutions are permitted to certificate professional translators in this country.
The school is authorized to certificate its own teachers after a thorough evaluation process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certify - to formally attest or confirm in writing; to attest as being true or meeting a standard
