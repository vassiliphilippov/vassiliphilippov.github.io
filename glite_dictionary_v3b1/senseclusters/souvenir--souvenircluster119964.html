souvenir

noun
object kept as a reminder
Pronunciation: / suːvəˈnɪr /; / ˌsuːvəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌsuːvəˈnɪə / (BrE)
an object kept as a reminder of a place, event, or experience, often bought while traveling

Examples

A souvenir from travel
He kept the concert ticket as a souvenir of the unforgettable night.
She bought a small souvenir from each city she visited.
The keychain was a souvenir of their trip to Paris.
She brought home a souvenir from every country she visited.
The little statue on his desk is a souvenir of his trip to Greece.
Whenever I travel, I always buy a small souvenir to remember the place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keepsake - a small item kept in memory of the person who gave it or originally owned it
  • synonym: memento - an object kept as a reminder of a person or event
