URI

noun
upper respiratory infection
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː ɑːr ˈaɪ / (AmE), / ˌjuː ɑː ˈraɪ / (BrE)
an infection of the upper respiratory tract, such as the nose, sinuses, throat, or larynx, often called a cold

Examples

URI symptoms
The doctor diagnosed her with a URI after she complained of a sore throat and congestion.
Children often miss school due to a URI during the winter months.
He stayed home to recover from a mild URI and avoid spreading it to coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - a common viral infection of the upper respiratory tract
  • antonym: flu - an infectious disease caused by the influenza virus
