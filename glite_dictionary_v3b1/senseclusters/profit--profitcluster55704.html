profit

noun
financial gain after expenses
2.0 74/m
Pronunciation: / Ààpr…ëÀêf…™t / (AmE), / Ààpr…íf…™t / (BrE)
The financial gain obtained when the revenue from business activity exceeds the expenses, costs, and taxes involved in sustaining the activity.

Examples

Company profit margin
After deducting all expenses, their net profit was surprisingly high.
She reinvested her profits from the previous year back into the business.
The company reported a record profit this quarter despite rising costs.
