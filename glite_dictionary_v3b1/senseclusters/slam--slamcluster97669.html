slam

noun
winning nearly all tricks in bridge
9.3 5.9
Pronunciation: / slæm / (AmE), / slæm / (BrE)
In bridge and some other card games, a bid or contract to win all or all but one of the tricks in a hand, or the successful accomplishment of such a contract.

Examples

A bridge slam
Bidding a slam requires confidence in your partner's hand.
Pulling off a slam can be the highlight of a competitive bridge tournament.
