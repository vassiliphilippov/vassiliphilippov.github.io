glaciate

verb
to cover with ice or a glacier
Pronunciation: / ˈɡleɪ.si.eɪt /; / ˈɡleɪʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡlæ.si.eɪt /; / ˈɡlaːsɪeɪt / (BrE)
to cover or become covered with ice or glaciers

Examples
