clammily

adverb
in a clammy manner
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmɪli / (AmE), / ˈklæmɪli / (BrE)
In a clammy manner; with a moist, sticky, and unpleasant feeling.

Examples

To feel clammily cold
His hands felt clammily cold during the interview.
She shivered as the fog settled clammily around her skin.
The humid air pressed clammily against their faces as they entered the cave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clammy - unpleasantly damp or sticky to the touch
