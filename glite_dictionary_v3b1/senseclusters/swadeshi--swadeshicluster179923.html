swadeshi

adjective
locally produced goods
9.9
Pronunciation: / swəˈdeɪʃi / (AmE), / swəˈdeɪʃi / (BrE)
relating to goods produced locally, especially as part of a movement to promote domestic products over imports

Examples

Swadeshi products
The government promoted swadeshi products to support local industries.
Many people chose swadeshi clothing as a symbol of national pride.
During the movement, swadeshi goods became more popular than imported ones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - produced within a particular country
  • antonym: imported - brought in from another country
