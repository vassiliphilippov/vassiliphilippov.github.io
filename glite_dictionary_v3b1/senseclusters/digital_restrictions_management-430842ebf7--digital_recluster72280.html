digital restrictions management

noun
controls over use of digital content
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl rɪˈstrɪkʃənz ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl rɪˈstrɪkʃənz ˌmænɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
technology or systems designed to control or limit how digital content is used, accessed, or copied

Examples

Digital restrictions management technology
Advocacy groups argue that digital restrictions management can restrict fair use rights.
Many users are frustrated by digital restrictions management that limits their ability to share purchased music files.
Some publishers implement digital restrictions management to prevent unauthorized copying of e-books.
