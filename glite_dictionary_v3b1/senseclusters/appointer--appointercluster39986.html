appointer

noun
person who appoints
6.0
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɪntər / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntə / (BrE)
a person or authority who appoints someone to a position or office

Examples

Company appointer
The appointer must follow strict guidelines when choosing candidates for the board.
As the official appointer, the mayor selected the new city manager.
The constitution defines the powers of the appointer in government appointments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appointee - a person who is appointed to a position
  • synonym: nominator - a person who formally suggests someone for a position
