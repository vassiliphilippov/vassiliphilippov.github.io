Dickless Tracy

noun
person lacking true toughness (slang)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkləs ˈtreɪsi / (AmE), / ˈdɪkləs ˈtreɪsi / (BrE)
A humorous or mocking term for someone who pretends to be tough or authoritative but lacks real courage or substance.

Examples

Dickless Tracy nickname
She laughed and dubbed her classmate Dickless Tracy after he failed to keep his boast.
They're always making jokes about Mark being a real Dickless Tracy when he tries to intimidate people.
Whenever he talks big but backs down, everyone calls him Dickless Tracy.
