opportunistic

adjective
taking unprincipled advantage of opportunities
8.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /ɑː.pər.tuː.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ /; / /ɑː.pər.tjuː.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɒp.ə.tjuː.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ /; / /ɒp.ə.tʃuː.ˈnɪs.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
