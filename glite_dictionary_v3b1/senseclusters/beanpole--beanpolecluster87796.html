beanpole

noun
tall, thin person
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːnˌpoʊl / (AmE), / ˈbiːnpəʊl / (BrE)
a very tall and thin person (informal, sometimes slightly awkward)

Examples

A tall beanpole
Everyone noticed the new student, a beanpole who towered over his classmates.
As a teenager, he was such a beanpole that he struggled to find clothes that fit.
People often called her a beanpole because of her height and slim build.
Everyone could spot Tom in a crowd because he was such a beanpole.
The gardener placed a beanpole in the soil to help the plants climb.
As a child, she was teased for being a beanpole among her shorter classmates.

  • synonym: pole - a long, slender object used for support
  • antonym: shorty - a short person (informal)
  • antonym: stocky - short and broad or sturdily built
  • synonym: string bean - a very thin person (informal)
