rice

noun
a cereal grain used as food
Pronunciation: / raɪs / (AmE), / raɪs / (BrE)
a cereal plant grown in warm climates, or its edible seeds used as a staple food

Examples

A bowl of rice
Brown rice is considered healthier than white rice because it contains more fiber.
He had rice for lunch nearly every day while living in Asia.
She cooked a pot of rice to serve with the curry.
Brown rice is often considered healthier than white rice because it retains more nutrients.
Large fields of rice stretched across the valley, shimmering green in the sunlight.
She served steamed rice alongside the spicy curry for dinner.
