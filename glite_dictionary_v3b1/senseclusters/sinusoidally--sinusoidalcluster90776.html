sinusoidally

adverb
in a sinusoidal (wave-like) manner
Pronunciation: / saɪˌnjuːˈsɔɪdəli / (AmE), / sɪˌnjuːˈsɔɪdəli / (BrE)
In a manner resembling or following a sinusoidal (wave-like) pattern.

Examples

To move sinusoidally
Sound waves often propagate sinusoidally through the air under ideal conditions.
The pendulum swings sinusoidally with respect to time, creating a smooth back-and-forth motion.
The voltage across the resistor changes sinusoidally during one complete cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sinusoidal - having the form of a sine wave; wave-like
