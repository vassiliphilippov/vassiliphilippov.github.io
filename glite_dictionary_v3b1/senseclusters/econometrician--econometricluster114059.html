econometrician

noun
economist using statistics and mathematics
Pronunciation: / ɪˌkɑːnəˌmɛˈtrɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌkɒnəˌmɛˈtrɪʃən / (BrE)
an economist who specializes in applying statistical and mathematical methods to analyze economic data and problems

Examples

An econometrician analyzes data
After earning her PhD, she began working as an econometrician at a leading university.
As an econometrician, he specializes in using data to forecast economic trends.
The government hired an econometrician to analyze the effects of tax policy changes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economist - a person who studies or works in the field of economics
