ward

noun
game item that grants vision
6.7
Pronunciation: /wɔːrd/ (AmE), /wɔːd/ (BrE)
(in video games) a small placed object that reveals an area of the map or detects hidden or approaching enemies, used to gain vision or prevent ambushes.

Examples

Place a ward
The support placed a ward in the river to reveal enemy movements before the gank.
She cleared the brush and destroyed the enemy's ward to deny vision during the fight.
Buying several wards early helped them control vision and secure objectives around the map.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reveal - to make known what was hidden; the function a ward provides by showing hidden units or areas
  • synonym: sentry - a placed device or unit that watches for or detects enemies; used similarly in some games
