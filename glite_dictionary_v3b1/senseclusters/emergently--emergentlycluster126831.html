emergently

adverb
urgently, in emergency
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɜrdʒəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈmɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or requires immediate action due to an emergency or urgent situation

Examples

Emergently treated
The patient was transferred emergently to the intensive care unit after his condition worsened.
Doctors must sometimes operate emergently to save a life.
The team responded emergently when the alarm signaled a critical failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immediately - without delay; at once
  • antonym: routinely - in a regular, non-urgent manner
  • synonym: urgently - in a way that requires immediate attention
