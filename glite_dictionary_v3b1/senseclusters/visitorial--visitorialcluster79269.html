visitorial

adjective
concerning visitors
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪzɪˈtɔːriəl / (AmE), / ˌvɪzɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to visitors or to the act of visiting, especially regarding facilities or services for guests.

Examples

Visitorial services
The hotel added new visitorial amenities to make guests feel more comfortable during their stay.
City officials discussed visitorial policies to better manage the influx of tourists each summer.
The university improved its visitorial services to help prospective students and their families.

Related Terms

  • antonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • synonym: visitor - a person who visits a place
