Adamic

adjective
of Adam; original innocence
Pronunciation: / əˈdæmɪk / (AmE), / əˈdæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to Adam, the first man in the Bible, or to a state of original innocence or origin

Examples

Adamic innocence
The poet described a world in its Adamic simplicity, untouched by civilization.
Some philosophers dream of returning to an Adamic innocence lost over time.
The painting captured an Adamic landscape, pure and unspoiled by human hands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Edenic - relating to the Garden of Eden or a paradise-like state
  • synonym: innocent - free from moral wrong or guilt
