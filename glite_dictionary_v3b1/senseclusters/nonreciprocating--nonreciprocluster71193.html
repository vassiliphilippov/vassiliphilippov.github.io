nonreciprocating

adjective
not moving back and forth
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈsɪp.rəˌkeɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɪp.rəˌkeɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a mechanism or motion) not moving alternately forward and backward.

Examples

A nonreciprocating mechanism
Modern compressors often use nonreciprocating designs to reduce vibration and noise.
The engine uses a nonreciprocating rotary motion to drive the wheels efficiently.
Unlike conventional saws, the tool features a nonreciprocating cutting blade for smoother operation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reciprocating - moving alternately backward and forward
