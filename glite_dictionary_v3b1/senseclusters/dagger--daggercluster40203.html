dagger

verb
to mark with †
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdæɡər / (AmE), / ˈdæɡə / (BrE)
to mark text or an item with a dagger symbol (†), usually to indicate a footnote or special note

Examples

To dagger a word
The editor daggered the word to indicate there was a special note at the bottom of the page.
In the manuscript, several names were daggered to show they were deceased.
Please dagger any terms that require additional explanation in the appendix.
