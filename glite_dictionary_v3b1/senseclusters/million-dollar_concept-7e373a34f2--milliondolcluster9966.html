million-dollar concept

noun
highly valuable or profitable idea
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljən ˈdɑlɚ ˈkɑnsɛpt / (AmE), / ˈmɪljən ˈdɒlə ˈkɒnsɛpt / (BrE)
an idea or plan considered to be exceptionally valuable or likely to generate significant profit, especially one worth a million dollars or more

Examples

A million-dollar concept idea
She was determined to come up with a million-dollar concept before turning thirty.
Sometimes, a seemingly simple idea turns out to be a real million-dollar concept.
Their new app was called a million-dollar concept by tech investors after its launch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goldmine - a source of great wealth or profit
  • synonym: windfall - an unexpected gain or piece of good fortune
