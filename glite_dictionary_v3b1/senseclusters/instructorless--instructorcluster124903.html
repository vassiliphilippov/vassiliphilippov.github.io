instructorless

adjective
without a teacher
7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈstrʌk.tər.ləs/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈstrʌk.tə.ləs/ (BrE)
without an instructor or teacher; designed to be used or done without a person to guide or teach

Examples

Instructorless training program
The company offers an instructorless training program that employees can complete at their own pace.
Many museums now provide instructorless tours using audio guides and interactive maps.
He preferred the instructorless course because it allowed him to study whenever he wanted.
The students managed to complete the assignment in the instructorless classroom.
After the coach left unexpectedly, the team continued their instructorless practice.
Online courses can sometimes feel instructorless if there is little guidance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guided - accompanied or directed by an instructor or guide
  • synonym: self-guided - able to be done independently without direct supervision
  • antonym: supervised - watched or overseen by someone in authority
  • synonym: unattended - not being watched or supervised
