affirmative

noun
a reply expressing agreement or 'yes'
Pronunciation: / əˈfɝːmətɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɜːmətɪv / (BrE)
A word or statement expressing agreement or consent, especially as a formal reply of 'yes'.

Examples

A clear affirmative
His answer was a clear affirmative to the question.
She responded with an affirmative when asked if she understood the rules.
The committee voted in the affirmative, approving the proposal unanimously.

Related Terms

  • antonym: negative - a word or statement expressing disagreement or 'no'
