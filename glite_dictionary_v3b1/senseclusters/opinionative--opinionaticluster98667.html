opinionative

adjective
obstinate in opinions
4.8
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪnjəˌneɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjənətɪv / (BrE)
holding stubbornly to one's own opinions; obstinate in one's views.

Examples

Opinionative attitude

It can be hard to work with someone as opinionative as Greg.
The opinionative participant refused to accept any viewpoint that contradicted his own.
