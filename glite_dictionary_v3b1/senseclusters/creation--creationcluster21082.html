creation

noun
an object or work that has been made
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / kriːˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
an object or work that has been made or invented, especially something showing artistic skill

Examples

A beautiful creation
His new recipe was a delicious creation that everyone enjoyed.
The artist proudly displayed her latest creation at the gallery opening.
The sculptor spent months perfecting his creation before unveiling it to the public.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destruction - the act of ruining or destroying something
  • synonym: invention - something that has been invented
