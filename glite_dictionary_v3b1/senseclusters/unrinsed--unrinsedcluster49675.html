unrinsed

adjective
not rinsed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪnst / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪnst / (BrE)
not having been rinsed or washed with water after use or cleaning

Examples

Unrinsed salad leaves
The salad tasted bitter because the leaves were unrinsed and still had dirt on them.
He noticed the shampoo was still in his hair after an unrinsed shower.
Please do not leave the cups unrinsed on the counter after using them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rinsed - having been washed with water to remove soap or residue
