palindromically

adverb
in palindrome form
Pronunciation: / ˌpælɪnˈdroʊmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpælɪnˈdrəʊmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that forms or relates to a palindrome, reading the same backward and forward

Examples

Spelled palindromically
The word 'racecar' is spelled palindromically.
She arranged the numbers palindromically so they read the same both ways.
The poem was cleverly written palindromically, making it a unique piece of art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palindrome - a word or phrase that reads the same backward as forward
