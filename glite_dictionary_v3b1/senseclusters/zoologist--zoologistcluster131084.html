zoologist

noun
a scientist who studies animals
1.0
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / zuːˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies or specializes in zoology, the branch of biology dealing with animals

Examples

A zoologist studies animals
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a zoologist and working with exotic animals.
The zoologist spent years researching animal behavior in the rainforest.
The documentary featured a renowned zoologist explaining the migration patterns of birds.
