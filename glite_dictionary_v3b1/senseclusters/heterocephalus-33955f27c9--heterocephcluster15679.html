Heterocephalus

noun
genus of nearly hairless African rodents
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtəroʊˈsɛfələs / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəʊˈsɛfələs / (BrE)
A genus of African rodents consisting of the naked mole rat, known for its nearly hairless body and eusocial behavior.

Examples

A Heterocephalus colony
A Heterocephalus colony can include hundreds of individuals living in an elaborate system of tunnels.
Scientists study Heterocephalus for insights into aging and cancer resistance.
The Heterocephalus genus is unique among rodents for its eusocial colony structure similar to that of some insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: naked mole rat - a nearly hairless burrowing rodent native to East Africa
