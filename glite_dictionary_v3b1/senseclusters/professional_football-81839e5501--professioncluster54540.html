professional football

noun
football played as a paid profession
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənl ˈfʊtbɔl / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ˈfʊtbɔːl / (BrE)
Football played for payment, involving athletes who are paid for their participation.

Examples

Professional football players
He dreamed of playing professional football after college, hoping to join a major league team.
In many countries, professional football attracts millions of passionate fans and media attention.
She became the first woman in her country to referee a professional football match.
