indefinite pronoun

noun
pronoun with no specific referent
3.9 3.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdef.ə.nət ˈproʊ.naʊn/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdef.ɪ.nət ˈproʊ.naʊn/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdef.ɪ.nət ˈprəʊ.naʊn/ /; / /ɪn.ˈdef.ə.nət ˈprəʊ.naʊn/ (BrE)
a pronoun that does not refer to a specific person, thing, or amount, such as 'someone', 'anybody', or 'nothing'

Examples

An indefinite pronoun example
In the sentence, 'Is there anyone at home?', 'anyone' is an indefinite pronoun.
When you say, 'Somebody left their bag,' 'somebody' functions as an indefinite pronoun.
Words like 'everything' and 'nobody' are often used as indefinite pronouns in English.
