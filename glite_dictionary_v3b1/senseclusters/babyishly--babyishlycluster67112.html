babyishly

adverb
in a childish way
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbiɪʃli / (BrE)
in a manner that resembles or behaves like a baby, especially in a childish or immature way

Examples

To speak babyishly
She spoke babyishly to get attention from her parents.
He acted babyishly when he did not win the game.
The toddler giggled babyishly at the funny faces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childishly - in a way that is typical of a child or immature
  • antonym: maturely - in a way that shows maturity or adult behavior
