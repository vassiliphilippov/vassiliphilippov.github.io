unsecure

adjective
not protected
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnsɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / ˌʌnsɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
not protected or not made safe from danger, attack, or unauthorized access

Examples

Unsecure Wi-Fi
It is risky to leave your computer unsecure when connected to public Wi-Fi.
The building was unsecure after the storm damaged the main entrance.
Many websites remain unsecure and vulnerable to cyber attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecure - not safe or protected from danger or risk
  • antonym: secure - protected from danger or unauthorized access
