Westeros

proper noun
Game of Thrones continent
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstəˌroʊs / (AmE), / ˈwɛstərəʊs / (BrE)
a fictional continent where much of the action in the Game of Thrones series takes place

Examples

Westeros continent
The Seven Kingdoms are located on the continent of Westeros in the popular fantasy series.
Many characters dream of ruling all of Westeros and sitting on the Iron Throne.
Dragons had not been seen in Westeros for centuries until Daenerys arrived.
