U.S. waters

noun
sea area under U.S. jurisdiction
Pronunciation: / juː ˈɛs ˈwɑːtərz / (AmE), / juː ˈɛs ˈwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the area of sea extending 12 nautical miles from the coasts of the United States and its territories, subject to U.S. jurisdiction

Examples

U.S. waters jurisdiction
