mouth

noun
opening for eating and speaking
1.2
Pronunciation: / maʊθ / (AmE), / maʊθ / (BrE)
the opening in the face through which food is taken in and sounds are made (including the lips and surrounding area)

Examples

Open your mouth
He covered his mouth when he yawned.
She put the spoon in her mouth and tasted the soup.
The dentist told him to open his mouth wide during the examination.
Laughter escaped from his mouth as he heard the joke.
She wiped the lipstick from her mouth before leaving the restaurant.
The doctor asked him to open his mouth wide during the examination.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lip - either of the two fleshy parts that form the upper and lower edges of the mouth opening
  • synonym: oral cavity - the cavity of the mouth, technically referred to in anatomy
