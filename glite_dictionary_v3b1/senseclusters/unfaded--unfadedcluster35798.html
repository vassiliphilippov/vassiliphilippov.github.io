unfaded

adjective
still strong or lasting
4.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪdɪd / (BrE)
not having lost strength, color, brightness, or freshness; still strong or vivid

Her memories of childhood remained unfaded even after many decades.
The artist used special paints to keep the mural unfaded over time.
