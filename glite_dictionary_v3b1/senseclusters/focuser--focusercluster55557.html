focuser

noun
device for adjusting focus
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.kə.sər/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.kə.sə/ (BrE)
a person or device that adjusts or brings something into focus, especially in optical instruments like cameras or telescopes

Examples

Camera focuser
The focuser on this camera is very sensitive and allows for sharp images.
She turned the focuser of the microscope to see the cells more clearly.
A skilled focuser can quickly adjust the lens for the best view.
The focuser on the telescope allows you to get a clear image of distant stars.
As a team focuser, she made sure everyone stayed on topic during meetings.
He bought a new focuser to improve the performance of his camera lens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjuster - a device or person that makes small changes to improve something
  • synonym: concentrator - a person or thing that concentrates something
  • antonym: defocuser - a device or person that makes something less focused
  • antonym: distractor - a person or thing that diverts attention
