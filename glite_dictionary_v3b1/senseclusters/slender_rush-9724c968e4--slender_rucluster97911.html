slender rush

noun
a wiry, tufted rush plant
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛndɚ rʌʃ / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə rʌʃ / (BrE)
a tufted, wiry rush plant found in a wide range of habitats, known for its slender stems

Examples

A slender rush plant
Botanists identified several clumps of slender rush growing in the marsh.
Gardeners sometimes plant slender rush for decorative purposes around ponds.
The slender rush thrives in wetlands and along riverbanks.
