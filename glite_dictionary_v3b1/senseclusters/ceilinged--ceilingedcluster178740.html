ceilinged

adjective
having a ceiling or type of ceiling
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪŋd / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋd / (BrE)
(of a room or building) provided with a ceiling, especially the overhead interior surface.

Examples

A ceilinged room
The house had a beautifully ceilinged dining room with ornate molding.
Their basement was finally ceilinged to keep it warmer in winter.
We admired the high, beamed-ceilinged hall as soon as we entered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ceilingless - having no ceiling
