domatium

noun
plant structure providing shelter for insects or fungi
9.1 9.9
Pronunciation: /doʊ.ˈmeɪ.ʃəm/ /; / /doʊ.ˈmeɪ.ʃɪ.əm/ (AmE), /dəʊ.ˈmeɪ.ʃəm/ /; / /dəʊ.ˈmeɪ.ʃɪ.əm/ (BrE)
A part of a plant, such as a leaf, that has been modified to provide shelter for insects, mites, or fungi.
