handheld

noun
portable electronic device
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌhɛld / (AmE), / ˈhændˌhɛld / (BrE)
a small electronic device that is designed to be used while held in the hand

Examples

A handheld game
She played games on her handheld during the flight.
Many people use a handheld to check emails while traveling.
The new handheld fits easily into your pocket and has a long battery life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: desktop - a computer designed to be used at a desk
  • synonym: mobile device - a portable electronic device such as a smartphone or tablet
