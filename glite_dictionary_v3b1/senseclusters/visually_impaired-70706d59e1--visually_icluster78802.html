visually impaired

adjective
having greatly reduced vision
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəli ɪmˈpɛrd / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒuəli ɪmˈpeəd / (BrE)
Having greatly reduced vision, either partially or completely, but not necessarily totally blind.

Examples

The visually impaired person
Buses in the city are equipped with audio announcements to assist the visually impaired.
He volunteers to read books for the visually impaired at the community center.
Technology has made life more accessible for people who are visually impaired.
