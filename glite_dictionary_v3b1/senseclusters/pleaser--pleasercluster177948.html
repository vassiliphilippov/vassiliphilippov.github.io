pleaser

noun
person who seeks approval
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːzər / (AmE), / ˈpliːzə / (BrE)
a person who tries hard to make others happy or satisfied, often by putting others' needs before their own

Examples

A people pleaser
As a natural pleaser, she always volunteered to help her friends with their problems.
Many pleasers find it difficult to say no, even when they are overwhelmed.
He realized being a pleaser sometimes made him neglect his own needs.

  • antonym: selfish - a person who only cares about their own needs
