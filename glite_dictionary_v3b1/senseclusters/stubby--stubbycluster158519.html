stubby

adjective
short and thick or blunt
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌbi / (AmE), / ˈstʌbi / (BrE)
short and thick or blunt in shape.

Examples

A stubby pencil
He replaced his stubby pencil with a new one for the exam.
Her stubby fingers made playing some musical instruments challenging.
The puppy had stubby legs, making it look even cuter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: long - having great length
  • synonym: short - of small length or not long
