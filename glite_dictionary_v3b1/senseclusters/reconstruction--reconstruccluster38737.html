reconstruction

noun
creating a 3D model from images
5.0
Pronunciation: /riː.kən.ˈstrʌk.ʃən/ /; / /rɪ.kən.ˈstrʌk.ʃən/ (AmE), /riː.kən.ˈstrʌk.ʃən/ (BrE)
(in computing and computer graphics) the process of creating a three-dimensional model or representation of a scene or object from multiple images or sensor data.

Examples

3D reconstruction
Researchers built a reconstruction of the brain's 3D structure from multiple MRI scans.
Using photos from different angles, the team produced a reconstruction of the ancient vase for study.
The software aligns point clouds and meshes to create an accurate reconstruction of the building's facade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: 3D reconstruction - the process of producing a three-dimensional model from images or sensor data
  • synonym: photogrammetry - a technique that uses multiple photographs to measure and reconstruct the shapes and positions of objects
