multisector

noun
company in many sectors
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktə / (BrE)
an organization, project, or approach that involves several different sectors or areas

Examples

A multisector project
The new multisector is investing in both renewable energy and digital services.
As a multisector, the company can adapt quickly to changes in the global market.
Many investors prefer a multisector for its diversified business approach.
The government launched a multisector to address both education and healthcare challenges in rural areas.
A successful multisector often requires cooperation between public, private, and nonprofit organizations.
The conference highlighted a new multisector focused on environmental sustainability and economic growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conglomerate - a large corporation made up of diverse companies
  • synonym: interdisciplinary - involving two or more academic or professional fields
  • antonym: monosector - a company operating in only one sector
  • antonym: sectoral - relating to a single sector or area
