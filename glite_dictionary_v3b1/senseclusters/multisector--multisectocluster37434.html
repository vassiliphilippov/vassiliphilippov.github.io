multisector

noun
company in many sectors
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktər / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈsɛktə / (BrE)
an organization, project, or approach that involves several different sectors or areas

Examples

A multisector project

As a multisector, the company can adapt quickly to changes in the global market.

A successful multisector often requires cooperation between public, private, and nonprofit organizations.
The conference highlighted a new multisector focused on environmental sustainability and economic growth.
