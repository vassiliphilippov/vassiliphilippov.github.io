Cantabrian Mountains

proper noun
mountain range in northern Spain
Pronunciation: / kænˈtæbriən ˈmaʊntənz / (AmE), / kanˈtabrɪən ˈmaʊntɪnz / (BrE)
a range of mountains in northern Spain, running parallel to the coast of the Bay of Biscay

Examples
