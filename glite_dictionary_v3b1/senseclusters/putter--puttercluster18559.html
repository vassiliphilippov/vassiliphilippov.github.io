putter

noun
golf club for putting
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈpʌtə / (BrE)
a golf club with a flat face, designed for making short, low-speed strokes on the putting green

Examples

A golf putter
Every golfer knows the importance of choosing the right putter for their game.
He selected his putter to finish the hole from just a few feet away.
She practiced with her new putter every evening to improve her putting skills.
