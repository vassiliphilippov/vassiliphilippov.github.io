texturing

noun
fiber treatment process
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈteks.tʃər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈteks.tʃə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
a mechanical or chemical process applied to fibers or yarn to create bulk, stretch, or a permanent surface pattern

Examples

Yarn texturing process
Modern sportswear often uses yarns that have undergone texturing to improve comfort and flexibility.
The texturing of synthetic fibers can make them feel more like natural materials.
Advances in texturing techniques have led to new types of stretch fabrics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finishing - the final treatment of textiles to improve appearance or performance
  • synonym: spinning - the process of making yarn from fibers
