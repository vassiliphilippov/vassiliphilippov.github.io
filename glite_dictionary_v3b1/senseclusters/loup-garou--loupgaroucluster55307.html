loup-garou

noun
mythical shapeshifter; werewolf
9.9
Pronunciation: / luː ˈɡæruː / (AmE), / luː ˈɡaruː / (BrE)
A legendary creature from folklore that can change from a human into a wolf and back again, similar to a werewolf.

Examples

The loup-garou legend
In French folklore, the loup-garou is feared as a supernatural wolf-man.
Some believed the strange tracks were evidence of a loup-garou in the area.
The villagers whispered that a loup-garou roamed the woods on moonless nights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: werewolf - a person who changes into a wolf, especially at the time of a full moon
