interstellar

adjective
between or among stars
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈstɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈstɛlə / (BrE)
existing or occurring between stars; relating to the space among the stars in a galaxy.

Examples

Interstellar space
Astronomers detected new molecules in interstellar clouds of gas.
Scientists study the properties of interstellar dust to understand the formation of galaxies.
The spacecraft was designed for interstellar travel beyond our solar system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interplanetary - existing or occurring between planets
