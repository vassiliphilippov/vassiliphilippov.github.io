guest

noun
computer connected to a network (computing)
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɡɛst / (AmE), / ɡɛst / (BrE)
(computing) A computer that is connected to a network, especially one that is not an integral part of the system or is given temporary access.

Examples

Guest network access
If your laptop is connected as a guest, you may have limited access to shared resources.
The network administrator assigned the guest computer a temporary IP address.
The new security policy restricts what a guest can do on the company network.

Related Terms

  • antonym: host - main computer that provides services to others on a network
