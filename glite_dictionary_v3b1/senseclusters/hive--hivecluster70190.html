hive

noun
busy place with many people or activities
Pronunciation: / haɪv / (AmE), / haɪv / (BrE)
A place or situation filled with a large number of people or activities, often bustling with energy.

Examples

A hive of activity
The market became a hive as vendors set up their stalls before dawn.
The office was a hive of activity during the product launch.
Throughout the holiday season, the shopping mall turned into a hive of excited shoppers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swarm - a large, dense group, often moving together
