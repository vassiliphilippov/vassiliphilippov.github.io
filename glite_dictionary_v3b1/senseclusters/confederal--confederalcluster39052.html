confederal

adjective
of a confederation
7.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛdərəl / (AmE), / kənˈfɛdərəl / (BrE)
relating to or based on a confederation, especially of states or groups joined together for a common purpose

Examples

A confederal system
The new constitution established a confederal structure to allow more autonomy for each region.
In a confederal system, the central government has limited authority over the member states.
Some political scientists debate the effectiveness of confederal arrangements in maintaining unity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confederate - joined by an agreement or treaty
  • antonym: federal - relating to a system where power is shared between a central authority and constituent units
