shibori

adjective
Japanese dyeing technique
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (AmE), / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (BrE)
dyed or patterned using the traditional Japanese shibori technique of folding, twisting, or binding fabric before dyeing

Examples

Shibori patterned dress
She wore a beautiful shibori dress with intricate blue and white patterns.
The artist displayed several shibori wall hangings at the craft fair.
They learned how to make shibori scarves in the textile workshop.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - not decorated or patterned
  • synonym: tie-dye - a method of dyeing fabric by tying sections to resist the dye
