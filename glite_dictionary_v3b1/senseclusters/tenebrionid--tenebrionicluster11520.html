tenebrionid

noun
a type of dark-bodied beetle
9.0
Pronunciation: / təˈnɛbriənɪd / (AmE), / tɛˈnɛbriənɪd / (BrE)
a member of a family of hard-bodied, usually dark-coloured beetles whose larvae feed on decaying plant matter or grain
