accept

verb
agree to pay or take responsibility for (debts, charges)
1.0
Pronunciation: / əkˈsɛpt / (AmE), / əkˈsɛpt / (BrE)
to agree to pay or take responsibility for expenses or debts of another person

Examples

To accept the bill
He offered to accept the medical bills after the accident.
If you accept the charges, you will be responsible for paying them.
She did not want to accept her brother's debts, but felt obliged to help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assume - to take on responsibility, sometimes including debts or duties
  • antonym: decline - to refuse or reject an offer or responsibility
