dive

verb
jump headfirst into water
Pronunciation: / daɪv / (AmE), / daɪv / (BrE)
to jump headfirst into water or swim below the surface

Examples

To dive into water
He hesitated for a moment before diving into the swimming pool.
She took a deep breath and dived into the lake.
The children love to dive off the rocks into the sea.
Many fish dive into the coral to hide from predators.
She took a deep breath and dove beneath the surface to explore the reef.
The children love to dive for rings at the bottom of the pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plunge - to jump or dive quickly and energetically
  • synonym: swim - to move through water by moving parts of the body
