nonsurgically

adverb
without surgery
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɜrdʒɪkli / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɜːdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not involve surgery

Examples

Treat nonsurgically
The doctor recommended treating the condition nonsurgically to avoid unnecessary risks.
Many patients prefer to be managed nonsurgically when possible.
The injury healed nonsurgically with the help of physical therapy and rest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conservatively - using non-invasive or less aggressive medical treatment
  • antonym: surgically - by means of surgery
