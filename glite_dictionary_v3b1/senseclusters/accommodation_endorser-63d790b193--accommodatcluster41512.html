accommodation endorser

noun
person who endorses a note as a favor, no benefit
5.2 8.3
Pronunciation: /ə.kɑː.mə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən ɪn.ˈdɔːr.sər/ (AmE), /ə.kɒ.mə.ˈdeɪ.ʃən ɪn.ˈdɔː.sə/ (BrE)
a person who endorses a promissory note solely as a favor to the borrower, receiving no compensation or benefit

Examples
