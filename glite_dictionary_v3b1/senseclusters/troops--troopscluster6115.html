troops

noun
soldiers as a group
3.8
Pronunciation: / truːps / (AmE), / truːps / (BrE)
soldiers, especially when considered as a group or as part of an organized military force

Examples

The troops march
After months of fighting, the troops were finally ordered to return home.
The government announced it would send additional troops to the disaster area to assist with relief efforts.
The presence of foreign troops in the region has sparked debate among local leaders.

Related Terms
