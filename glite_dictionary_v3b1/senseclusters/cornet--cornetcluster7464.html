cornet

noun
brass musical instrument with a brilliant tone
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈnɛt, ˈkɔr.nət / (AmE), / kɔːˈnɛt, ˈkɔː.nɪt / (BrE)
a brass musical instrument similar to a trumpet but with a narrower tube and a flared bell, producing a brilliant tone

Examples

A cornet player
His grandfather's old cornet was kept in a velvet-lined case.
She played the cornet in the school marching band.
The jazz ensemble featured a soloist on cornet during the performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trumpet - a brass instrument with a bright, penetrating tone, commonly used in orchestras and bands
