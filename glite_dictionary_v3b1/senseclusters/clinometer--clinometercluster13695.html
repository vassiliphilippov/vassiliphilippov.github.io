clinometer

noun
instrument for measuring angles of tilt
Pronunciation: / klɪˈnɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / klɪˈnɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring angles of elevation or inclination, used in surveying and navigation

Examples

A clinometer tool
Surveyors often carry a clinometer to measure the steepness of terrain.
The engineer used a clinometer to determine the angle of the hillside before construction began.
With a clinometer, hikers can calculate the degree of a mountain's slope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclinometer - instrument that measures angle of slope (inclination)
