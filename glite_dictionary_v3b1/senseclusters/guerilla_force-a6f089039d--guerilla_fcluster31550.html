guerilla force

noun
irregular armed group using sabotage and harassment
2.1
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɪl.ə fɔrs / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɪl.ə fɔːs / (BrE)
an irregular group of armed fighters that uses sabotage and harassment, often operating in rural areas and organized in large groups

Examples

A guerilla force group
A small guerilla force managed to evade the government troops for months.
The guerilla force launched a series of attacks on supply convoys in the mountains.
Villagers were caught in the crossfire between the army and a local guerilla force.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurgent - a person who rises in opposition to authority, often armed
