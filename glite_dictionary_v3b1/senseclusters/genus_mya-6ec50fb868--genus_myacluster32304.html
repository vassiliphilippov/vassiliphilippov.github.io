genus Mya

noun
type genus of Myacidae (marine clams)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs ˈmaɪə / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Myacidae, comprising several species of marine clams, such as the soft-shell clam.

Examples

Genus Mya clams
Marine biologists often study members of the genus Mya to understand tidal ecosystems.
One well-known species in the genus Mya is Mya arenaria, also known as the sand gaper.
The genus Mya includes common soft-shell clams found along the Atlantic coast.
