cairn terrier

noun
small rough-coated Scottish terrier breed
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrn ˌtɛriər / (AmE), / ˈkɛən ˌtɛriə / (BrE)
a small, rough-coated breed of terrier originating in Scotland, known for its alertness and spirited nature

Examples

A cairn terrier dog
Despite its small size, a cairn terrier is energetic and loves chasing after small animals.
The cairn terrier is famous for its role as Toto in 'The Wizard of Oz.'
Their rough coat makes the cairn terrier well suited to Scotland's harsh weather.
