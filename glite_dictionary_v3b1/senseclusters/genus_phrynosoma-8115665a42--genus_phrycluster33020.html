genus Phrynosoma

noun
genus of horned lizards
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌfrɪ.nəˈsoʊ.mə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs ˌfrɪ.nəʊˈsəʊ.mə / (BrE)
A genus of lizards known as horned lizards, native to North and Central America and distinguished by spiny scales on their bodies and heads.
