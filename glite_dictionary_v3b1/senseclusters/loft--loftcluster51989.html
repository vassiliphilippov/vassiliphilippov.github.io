loft

verb
draw hull lines full-scale (shipbuilding)
9.1
Pronunciation: / lɔft / (AmE), / lɒft / (BrE)
to create a full-scale drawing of the hull lines of a vessel, typically used in shipbuilding.

Examples

To loft a design
Before construction began, the team lofted the design onto the workshop floor.
It's essential to loft the hull accurately for a vessel to perform well at sea.
The shipwright spent weeks carefully lofting the lines to ensure the hull was symmetrical.
