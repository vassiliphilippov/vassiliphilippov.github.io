marathon

noun
long and demanding task or effort
Pronunciation: / ˈmærəˌθɑn / (AmE), / ˈmærəθən / (BrE)
a long and difficult task or activity that requires great effort and endurance

Examples

Marathon work session
Preparing for the annual tax season is always a marathon for accountants.
The project turned into a marathon as the team worked late every night for two weeks.
Writing her thesis was a marathon that tested her patience and determination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sprint - a short and fast race or effort
