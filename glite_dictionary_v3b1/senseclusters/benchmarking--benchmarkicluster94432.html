benchmarking

verb
to compare performance
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbentʃ.mɑːr.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbentʃ.mɑː.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
to measure or compare the performance or quality of something against a standard or best practice.

Examples

To benchmark new products
The company is benchmarking its products against industry leaders to improve quality.
Before launching the new app, the developers spent weeks benchmarking its speed and reliability.
Universities are benchmarking their programs to ensure they meet international standards.
