rosy

adjective
pinkish or blush-colored
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzi / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi / (BrE)
having a pink or reddish color, especially as a sign of good health or vitality

Examples

Rosy cheeks
Her cheeks looked especially rosy after a brisk walk in the cold morning air.
The baby's rosy complexion indicated good health.
The sky turned a rosy hue as the sun began to set over the hills.
After a brisk walk in the cold, her cheeks looked rosy and full of life.
His rosy face made everyone comment on how well he appeared.
The child’s rosy complexion was a sign of excellent health.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pale - lacking color or brightness, especially in the face
  • synonym: pink - a pale red color; the color between red and white
  • synonym: ruddy - having a healthy, red complexion or color
