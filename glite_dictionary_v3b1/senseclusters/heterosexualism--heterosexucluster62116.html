heterosexualism

noun
ideology privileging heterosexuality
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02cch\u025bt\u032c\u0259\u02c8s\u025bk\u0283u\u02d0\u0259l\u02cc\u026az\u0259m / (AmE), / \u02cch\u025bt\u0259\u02c8s\u025bk\u0283u\u02d0\u0259l\u026az(\u0259)m / (BrE)
An ideological or social system that treats heterosexuality as the normal or preferred form of sexuality and that marginalizes or discriminates against non-heterosexual orientations and relationships.

Examples

State heterosexualism policy
Scholars argued that state policy enforces heterosexualism by privileging traditional families over LGBTQ+ families.
Every textbook should explain how heterosexualism shapes institutions and everyday social expectations for non-heterosexual people.
Campaigners called the law an example of heterosexualism that systematically limited recognition of same-sex couples.
