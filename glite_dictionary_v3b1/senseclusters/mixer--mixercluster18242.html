mixer

noun
social gathering for mingling
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪksɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪksə / (BrE)
a social event where people gather to meet, mingle, and foster social relationships, often organized for newcomers or members of a group

Examples

College mixer
At the alumni mixer, former classmates reconnected over drinks and shared memories.
She met her future business partner at a networking mixer last year.
The student council hosted a mixer for all the freshmen to help them make new friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: party - a social gathering of invited guests, typically involving eating, drinking, and entertainment
  • synonym: social - involving friendly gatherings for pleasure
