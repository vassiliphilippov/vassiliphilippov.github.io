bedazzlement

noun
sparkly decoration
7.2
Pronunciation: / bɪˈdæzəlmənt / (AmE), / bɪˈdæzlmənt / (BrE)
the act or result of decorating something with many small, shiny ornaments such as sequins or rhinestones

Examples

Dress bedazzlement
The designer's use of bedazzlement made the dress sparkle under the stage lights.
Her phone case was covered in rhinestones, a true example of bedazzlement.
Some people love the bedazzlement of shiny accessories on their clothes and bags.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embellishment - the act of making something more attractive by adding decorative details
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of lacking decoration or adornment
