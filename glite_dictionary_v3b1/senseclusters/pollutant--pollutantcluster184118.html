pollutant

noun
substance that causes pollution
7.5
Pronunciation: / pəˈluːtənt / (AmE), / pəˈluːtənt / (BrE)
a substance that makes water, air, or soil dirty or harmful by contaminating it

Examples

Harmful pollutant in air
Air quality improved after strict pollutant regulations were introduced.
Factories must reduce the amount of pollutant they release into rivers.
Oil spills are a common pollutant that harm marine life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contaminant - a substance that causes contamination
