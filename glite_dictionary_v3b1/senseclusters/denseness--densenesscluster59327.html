denseness

noun
quality of being crowded or compact
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛns.nəs / (AmE), / ˈdɛns.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being closely packed or crowded together.

Examples

Denseness of the crowd
I was surprised by the denseness of the crowd at the concert.
The denseness of the forest made it difficult to walk through.
Urban planners must consider the denseness of residential areas when designing public parks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: density - the degree to which something is compacted or crowded together
  • antonym: sparsity - the quality of being thinly dispersed or scattered
