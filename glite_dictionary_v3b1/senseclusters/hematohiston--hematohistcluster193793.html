hematohiston

noun
protein from hemoglobin (heme removed)
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmətoʊˈhɪstən /; / hiˌmætəˈhɪstɑn / (AmE), / ˌhiːməˈtɒhɪstən /; / hiˌmætəˈhɪstɒn / (BrE)
A colorless protein obtained by removing heme from hemoglobin, involved in the oxygen-carrying function of red blood cells.
