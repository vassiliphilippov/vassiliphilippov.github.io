fornix

noun
arched bundle of nerve fibers in the brain
9.9 9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrnɪks / (AmE), / ˈfɔːnɪks / (BrE)
an arched bundle of nerve fibers at the base of the brain connecting the hippocampus to other brain regions, including the thalamus and mamillary bodies
