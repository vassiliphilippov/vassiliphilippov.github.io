dunk

verb
score by forcefully putting ball through basket (basketball)
Pronunciation: / dʌŋk / (AmE), / dʌŋk / (BrE)
to score by thrusting the ball down through the basket with the hand(s) above the rim, in basketball

Examples

To dunk a basketball
LeBron James can easily dunk over most defenders.
She surprised everyone when she managed to dunk the ball during her first game.
The crowd cheered loudly after he dunked near the end of the quarter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slam dunk - a forceful shot in basketball performed by jumping and pushing the ball down through the basket
