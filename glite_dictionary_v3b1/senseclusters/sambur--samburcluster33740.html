sambur

noun
large Asian deer with three-tined antlers
Pronunciation: / ˈsæmbər / (AmE), / ˈsæmbə / (BrE)
a large deer native to southern Asia, with antlers that have three tines.

Examples

The sambur deer
The sambur is often found in forested regions across India and Southeast Asia.
The male sambur has impressive antlers with three distinct tines.
We spotted a herd of samburs grazing near the river at dawn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sambar - variant spelling for the same deer species
