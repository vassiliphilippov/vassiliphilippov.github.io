burglar

noun
a thief who breaks into buildings
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːɡlər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːɡlə / (BrE)
A person who illegally enters a building, especially at night, to steal things.

Examples

A burglar breaks in
After hearing a noise upstairs, she realized a burglar was in the house.
The burglar broke in through the kitchen window while the family was away.
The police arrested the burglar as he tried to escape with stolen jewelry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robber - a person who takes property unlawfully, often by force
  • synonym: thief - a person who steals, especially by stealth
