pie-eyed

adjective
very drunk; extremely intoxicated
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ aɪd / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ aɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Very drunk; extremely intoxicated.

Examples

To be pie-eyed
After a few too many drinks, he was completely pie-eyed by midnight.
She stumbled out of the bar looking pie-eyed and confused.
We tried to get a taxi for our pie-eyed friend before he embarrassed himself further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intoxicated - under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
