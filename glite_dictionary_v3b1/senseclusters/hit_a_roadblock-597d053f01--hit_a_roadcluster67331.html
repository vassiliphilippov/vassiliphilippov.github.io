hit a roadblock

verb
encounter an obstacle or problem
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɪt ə ˈroʊdˌblɑk / (AmE), / hɪt ə ˈrəʊdblɒk / (BrE)
to experience an unexpected obstacle or problem that impedes progress

Examples

To hit a roadblock suddenly
Every time she tries to move forward, she seems to hit a roadblock.
The development team hit a roadblock that delayed the release of the app.
We hit a roadblock during negotiations and had to pause the discussion.
