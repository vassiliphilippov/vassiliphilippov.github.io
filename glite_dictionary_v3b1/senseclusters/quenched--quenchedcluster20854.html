quenched

adjective
subdued or suppressed

(of a feeling, movement, or event) subdued, suppressed, or brought to an end; overcome or extinguished.

Examples

Quenched hopes
Her curiosity was quenched when she finally found out the truth.
Rumors of a scandal were quenched as soon as the official statement was released.
The protest was quickly quenched by the authorities before it gained momentum.
