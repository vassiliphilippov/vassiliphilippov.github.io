pea-souper

noun
thick yellowish fog, esp. historic Britain
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpiː.suː.pər/ (AmE), /ˈpiː.suː.pə/ (BrE)
a very thick, yellowish fog, especially one caused by air pollution and common in British cities in the past

Examples

A thick pea-souper

We had to wait until the pea-souper lifted before continuing our journey.
