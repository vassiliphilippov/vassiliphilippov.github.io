trandolapril

noun
ACE inhibitor drug for blood pressure and heart
Pronunciation: / trændoʊˈlæprɪl / (AmE), / trændəˈlæprɪl / (BrE)
A medication in the class of ACE inhibitors, used to treat high blood pressure and to improve survival after a heart attack.

Examples

Trandolapril medication
After his heart attack, he was started on trandolapril as part of his treatment.
Some patients may experience side effects while taking trandolapril.
The doctor prescribed trandolapril to help lower his blood pressure.
