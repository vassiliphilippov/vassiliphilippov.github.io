sinkage

noun
amount of settling
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈsɪn.kɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈsɪŋ.kɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈsɪn.kɪdʒ/ (BrE)
the amount or process of sinking or settling downward, such as of soil, structures, vehicles, or objects in water
