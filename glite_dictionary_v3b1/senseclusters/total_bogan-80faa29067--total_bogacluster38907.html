total bogan

noun
unsophisticated or unrefined person (Austral., N.Z. slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬l ˈboʊ.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.tl ˈbəʊ.ɡən / (BrE)
An unsophisticated or unrefined person, especially one stereotyped as lacking in social graces or education (Austral., N.Z. informal).

Examples

A total bogan person
Some people at the party acted like a total bogan and embarrassed their friends.
When she called him a total bogan, he just laughed it off.
You don't have to dress fancy, but don't be a total bogan at the restaurant.
