lidocaine

noun
heart rhythm medication
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪɡnəˌkeɪn / (AmE), / ˈlɪɡnəˌkeɪn / (BrE)
a medication used to treat or prevent serious irregular heart rhythms, especially in emergency situations

Examples

Lidocaine for heartbeat
The doctor administered lignocaine intravenously to stabilize the patient's heartbeat.
In some countries, lignocaine is commonly used to treat dangerous arrhythmias in emergency care.
After receiving lignocaine, the patient's abnormal heart rhythm returned to normal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lidocaine - a medication used as a local anesthetic and to treat heart rhythm problems
  • synonym: procainamide - a drug used to treat abnormal heart rhythms
