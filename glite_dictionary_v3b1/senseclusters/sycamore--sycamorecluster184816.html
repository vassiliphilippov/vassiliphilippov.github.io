sycamore

noun
wood from a sycamore tree
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪk.əˌmɔr / (AmE), / ˈsɪk.ə.mɔː / (BrE)
the hard, tough, elastic wood of a sycamore tree, often variably colored or variegated, used in carpentry and fine woodworking

Examples

Sycamore wood furniture
The furniture maker selected a plank of sycamore for its beautiful grain and natural strength.
The warm hues of sycamore give the tabletop a unique, elegant appearance.
This violin's back is crafted from seasoned sycamore to enhance its rich tone.
