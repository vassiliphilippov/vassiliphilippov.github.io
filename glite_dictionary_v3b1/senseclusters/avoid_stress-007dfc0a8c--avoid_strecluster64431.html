avoid stress

verb
deliberately stay away from anxiety or tension
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈvɔɪd strɛs / (AmE), / əˈvɔɪd strɛs / (BrE)
to deliberately stay away from situations that cause anxiety or tension, especially to protect one's health or wellbeing

Examples

To avoid stress daily
Learning relaxation techniques can help people avoid stress in their daily lives.
Many doctors recommend that patients avoid stress to improve overall health.
She tries to avoid stress at work by planning her tasks carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manage stress - to deal with or control sources of stress
  • synonym: reduce stress - to lessen anxiety or tension
