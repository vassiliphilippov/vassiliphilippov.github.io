snubber

noun
person who ignores others
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌbər / (AmE), / ˈsnʌbə / (BrE)
a person who deliberately ignores, rejects, or treats others with disdain or coldness

Examples

A social snubber
The new student felt unwelcome because the class snubber refused to speak to her.
At the party, everyone avoided the notorious snubber who always ignored newcomers.
He gained a reputation as a snubber after repeatedly turning his back on colleagues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: snub - to ignore or treat someone with disdain
  • antonym: welcomer - a person who greets or receives others warmly
