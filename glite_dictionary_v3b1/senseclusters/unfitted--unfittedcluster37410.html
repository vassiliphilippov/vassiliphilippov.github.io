unfitted

adjective
not properly shaped
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having the correct size or shape to fit closely or properly, especially of clothing or parts.

Examples

Unfitted clothes
The new kitchen cabinets arrived unfitted and needed to be adjusted before installation.
She wore an unfitted dress that hung loosely from her shoulders.
The replacement part was unfitted and would not attach securely to the machine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fitted - made to fit closely or properly
  • synonym: ill-fitting - not fitting well or properly
