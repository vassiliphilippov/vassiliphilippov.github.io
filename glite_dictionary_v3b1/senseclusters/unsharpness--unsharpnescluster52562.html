unsharpness

noun
lack of sharpness
6.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈʃɑːrp.nəs/ /; / /ən.ˈʃɑːrp.nəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈʃɑːp.nəs/ /; / /ən.ˈʃɑːp.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or state of being dull or not having a fine edge, especially in a blade or tool

Examples

Unsharpness of a blade
The unsharpness of the scissors made it hard to cut the fabric cleanly.
Photographers try to avoid unsharpness in their images to keep details clear.
After months of use, the unsharpness of the kitchen knives became noticeable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dullness - the quality of being blunt or not sharp
  • antonym: sharpness - the quality of having a fine edge or point
