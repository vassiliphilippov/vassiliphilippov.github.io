curliness

noun
quality of being curly (esp. hair)
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜr.li.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.li.nɪs / (BrE)
The quality of having curls or being curled, especially in reference to hair.

Examples

Natural curliness of hair
Her natural curliness made her hair beautiful and unique.
Some people spend hours straightening the curliness from their hair.
The curliness of his hair increased in the humid weather.
