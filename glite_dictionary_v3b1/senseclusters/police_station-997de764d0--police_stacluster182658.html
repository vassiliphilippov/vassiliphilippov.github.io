police station

noun
building where police work and conduct duties
1.0 5.39/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôÀàlis Àåste…™ É…ôn / (AmE), / p…ôÀàliÀês Àåste…™ É…ôn / (BrE)
A building where police officers work and to which the public may go to report a crime or seek help; also used as a base for officers and the detention of arrested persons.

Examples

The police station building
She went to the police station to report her stolen bicycle.
The suspect was taken to the police station for questioning.
There is a police station just around the corner from the school.

Synonyms

  • station house
    3.8 0.39/m
    a station house is the officers' local headquarters where detainees are held, while a police station is the public place people go to report crimes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: station house - another term for police station, chiefly North American
