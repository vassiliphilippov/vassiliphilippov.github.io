expression

noun
group of words functioning as a unit in grammar
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃ.ən / (BrE)
a group of words that function together as a single unit within a sentence; especially in grammar, a combination of words forming a constituent

Examples

A noun expression
In linguistic analysis, an expression may refer to any string of words acting as a unit.
Some expressions can be replaced with a single word without changing the sentence's meaning.
The prepositional expression 'on the table' modifies the noun in the sentence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clause - a group of words containing a subject and predicate
  • synonym: phrase - a small group of words standing together as a conceptual unit
