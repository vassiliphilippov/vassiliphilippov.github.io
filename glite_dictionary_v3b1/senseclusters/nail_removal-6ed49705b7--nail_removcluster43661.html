nail removal

noun
forcible removal of nails as torture
Pronunciation: / neɪl rɪˈmuːvəl / (AmE), / neɪl rɪˈmuːvəl / (BrE)
a form of torture in which a person's fingernails or toenails are forcibly removed

Examples

Nail removal torture
Historical texts describe nail removal as a method of extracting confessions from prisoners.
International laws strictly prohibit nail removal as a form of torture.
The report documented cases of nail removal used during interrogations.
