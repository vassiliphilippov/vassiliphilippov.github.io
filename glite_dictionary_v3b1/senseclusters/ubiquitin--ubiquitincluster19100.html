ubiquitin

noun
protein tag for degradation
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.bɪ.kwɪ.tɪn/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.bɪ.kwɪ.tɪn/ (BrE)
a small protein found in most cells that attaches to other proteins to mark them for breakdown, recycling, or regulation
