multifunctional

adjective
having many uses
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈfʌŋkʃənəl / (BrE)
able to perform several different functions or tasks

Examples

Multifunctional kitchen tool
The multifunctional kitchen tool can chop, slice, and grate vegetables easily.
We installed a multifunctional device that acts as both a heater and a fan.
Her multifunctional bag can be used as a backpack or a shoulder bag.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-purpose - designed for only one function or use
  • synonym: versatile - able to adapt or be used for many different purposes
