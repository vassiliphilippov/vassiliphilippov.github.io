guard

noun
defensive posture in boxing or fencing
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrd / (AmE), / ɡɑːd / (BrE)
a protective posture or position adopted to defend oneself from attack, especially in sports like boxing or fencing

Examples

Boxing guard position
In fencing, maintaining a strong guard is essential for effective defense.
She quickly adjusted her guard to block the opponent's attack.
The boxer kept his guard up throughout the intense match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parry - a defensive movement to block or deflect an attack, especially in fencing
