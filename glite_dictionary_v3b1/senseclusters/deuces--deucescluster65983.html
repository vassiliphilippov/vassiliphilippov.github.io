deuces

noun
slang farewell
Pronunciation: / ˈduːsɪz / (AmE), / ˈdjuːsɪz / (BrE)
an informal slang term used to say goodbye or farewell, often accompanied by a peace sign gesture

Examples

Say deuces leaving
He waved and said 'deuces' as he left the party.
After finishing the call, she smiled and whispered 'deuces' before hanging up.
As the group split up, one friend threw up a peace sign and shouted 'deuces!'
