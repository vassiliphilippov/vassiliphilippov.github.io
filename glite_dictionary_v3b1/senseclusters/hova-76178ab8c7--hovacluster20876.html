Hova

proper noun
ethnic group in Madagascar
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊvə / (AmE), / ˈhəʊvə / (BrE)
a member of the largest historical ethnic group in Madagascar, traditionally influential in the island's central region

Examples

Hova people of Madagascar
The Hova were known for their political and social influence in Madagascar's history.
Many traditions of the Hova are still practiced in central Madagascar today.
Historians study the role of the Hova in shaping the island's culture and society.
