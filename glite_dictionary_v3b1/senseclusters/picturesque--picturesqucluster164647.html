picturesque

adjective
visually attractive or charming
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌpɪktʃərˈɛsk / (BrE)
(of a place, building, scene, etc.) visually attractive, especially in a quaint or charming way; suitable for being depicted in a picture.

Examples

Picturesque village
Her cottage was set in a picturesque valley, perfect for painting.
The picturesque village attracted many tourists each year.
We enjoyed a picnic by the river, surrounded by picturesque scenery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charming - delightfully pleasing or attractive
  • antonym: ugly - unpleasant or unattractive to look at
