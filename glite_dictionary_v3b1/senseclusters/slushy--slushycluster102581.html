slushy

adjective
overly sentimental; insincerely emotional
8.2 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈslʌ.ʃiː/ /; / /ˈslʌ.ʃɪ/ (AmE), /ˈslʌ.ʃiː/ /; / /ˈslʌ.ʃɪ/ (BrE)
Excessively sentimental or emotional in a way that seems insincere or lacking depth.

Examples

Slushy romance

He found her letter slushy and lacking genuine feeling.
I can't stand slushy love songs that try too hard to be touching.
