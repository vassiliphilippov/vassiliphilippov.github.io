Pteretis

noun
a genus of ferns (sometimes in Onoclea)
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈrɛtɪs / (AmE), / təˈrɛtɪs / (BrE)
A small genus of ferns sometimes included in the genus Onoclea; in some classifications, both are placed in the family Polypodiaceae.

Examples

The Pteretis fern
Research on North American ferns often references the classification of Pteretis.
Some botanists classify the sensitive fern under the genus Pteretis instead of Onoclea.
The placement of Pteretis in the Polypodiaceae family is still debated among taxonomists.
