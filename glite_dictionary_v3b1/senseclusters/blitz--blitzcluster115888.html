blitz

noun
a defensive rush in American football
Pronunciation: / blɪts / (AmE), / blɪts / (BrE)
In American football, a defensive play in which players rush towards the quarterback in an attempt to disrupt or tackle him.

Examples

A football blitz
A well-timed blitz can completely change the momentum of a football game.
The coach called for a blitz on third down to try to sack the quarterback.
When the defense executed a blitz, the offense struggled to protect their quarterback.

  • synonym: rush - a quick advance on the quarterback in football
