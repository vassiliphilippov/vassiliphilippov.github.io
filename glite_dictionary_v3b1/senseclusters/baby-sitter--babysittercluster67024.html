baby-sitter

noun
person who looks after children for parents
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiˌsɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbiˌsɪtə / (BrE)
a person who is paid to look after children while their parents are away from home

Examples

A baby-sitter at home
Finding a reliable baby-sitter can be a challenge for many parents.
The baby-sitter arrived at seven o'clock sharp.
We hired a baby-sitter to watch the kids while we went to dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysitter - alternative spelling of baby-sitter
  • synonym: childminder - a person who takes care of children, especially at their own home (British)
  • synonym: nanny - a person, often employed full-time, who cares for children in the employer's home
