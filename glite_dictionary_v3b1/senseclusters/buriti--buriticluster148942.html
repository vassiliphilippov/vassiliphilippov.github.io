buriti

noun
edible palm fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / buːˈriːti / (AmE), / buːˈriːti / (BrE)
the edible orange-red fruit or pulp of the buriti palm, often eaten fresh or used in food products

Examples

Fresh buriti fruit
Vendors at the market sold fresh buriti alongside other tropical fruits.
The creamy pulp of buriti is rich in vitamins and often used in desserts.
She enjoyed a juice made from buriti after her hike in the rainforest.
