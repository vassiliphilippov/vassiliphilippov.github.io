face cloth

noun
small cloth for washing face or body
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪs ˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs ˌklɒθ / (BrE)
A small piece of soft cloth used for washing the face and body.

Examples

A damp face cloth
After his workout, he refreshed himself with a damp face cloth.
Make sure to replace your face cloth regularly to maintain good hygiene.
She grabbed a clean face cloth from the cupboard before washing her face.
