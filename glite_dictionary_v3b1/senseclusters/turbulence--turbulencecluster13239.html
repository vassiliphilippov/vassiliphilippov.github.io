turbulence

noun
violent disturbance or disorder
7.7 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…úr.bj…ô.l…ôns / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀê.bj…ô.l…ôns / (BrE)
A state of violent disturbance and disorder, especially in politics or society.

Examples

Political turbulence
Periods of social turbulence often lead to major reforms.
The CEO was hired to lead the company through a time of economic turbulence.
The collapse of the government plunged the country into political turbulence.

Synonyms

  • political turmoil
    5.1 0.52/m

  • upheaval
    6.9 5.62/m

  • disorder
    4.1 6.49/m

  • tumult
    7.7 1.56/m

  • chaos
    3.1 22/m
    chaos is total confusion where order breaks down completely, while turbulence is violent disturbance but does not always mean total collapse.
