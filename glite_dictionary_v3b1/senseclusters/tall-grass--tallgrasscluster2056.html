tall-grass

noun
a type of tall-growing grass
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔl ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːl ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several species of grasses that grow tall, especially in moist, fertile environments

Examples

The tall-grass meadow
After the heavy rains, the tall-grass in the meadow grew even thicker and greener.
Ecologists studied how fires affected the growth of different tall-grass species in the region.
The bison wandered through the tall-grass that covered the prairie for miles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prairie grass - any grass species commonly found in prairies, often tall and robust
