deserted

adjective
empty and abandoned (of a place)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɝːtɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈzɜːtɪd / (BrE)
(of a place) having been left by the people who lived or used to be there; empty and abandoned.

Examples

A deserted house
A deserted house stood at the end of the road, its windows boarded up.
The deserted village felt eerie and silent in the fading light.
We wandered through the deserted streets after the festival ended.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandoned - left empty or unused, especially a place
  • antonym: populated - filled with people; inhabited
