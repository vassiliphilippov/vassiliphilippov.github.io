on your high horse

adverb
behaving in a self-righteous or arrogant way

Pronunciation: /ɑːn jɔːr haɪ hɔːrs/ /; / /ɑːn jʊr haɪ hɔːrs/ /; / /ən jʊr haɪ hɔːrs/ (AmE), /ɒn jɔː haɪ hɔːs/ /; / /ɒn jʊə haɪ hɔːs/ /; / /ən jɔː haɪ hɔːs/ (BrE)
