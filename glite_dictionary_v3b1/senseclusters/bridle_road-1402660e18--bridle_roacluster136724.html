bridle road

noun
path for riding or leading horses
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪ.dl roʊd / (AmE), / ˈbraɪ.dl rəʊd / (BrE)
A path or track intended for riding or leading horses, but not for motor vehicles.

Examples

A bridle road path
A sign at the entrance marks the start of the bridle road, warning that vehicles are not permitted.
Hikers and cyclists often share the bridle road with horses in the national park.
The old bridle road winds gently through the forest, offering riders a peaceful journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridleway - a path or track for horses, especially in the countryside
