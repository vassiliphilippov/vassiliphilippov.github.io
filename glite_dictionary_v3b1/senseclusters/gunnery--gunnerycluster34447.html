gunnery

noun
art of firing guns
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnəri / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnəri / (BrE)
the skill, science, or practice of operating and firing guns or artillery

Examples

Gunnery training
He studied gunnery at the military academy to become an artillery officer.
Modern gunnery requires both technical knowledge and practical training.
The ship's crew practiced gunnery drills every week to improve their accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artillery - large military guns used in warfare
  • synonym: marksmanship - skill in shooting accurately
