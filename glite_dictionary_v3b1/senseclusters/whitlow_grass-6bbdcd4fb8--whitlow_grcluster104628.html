whitlow grass

noun
small annual weed with white flowers and oblong capsules
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪt.loʊ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈwɪt.ləʊ ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a small annual weed of Europe and North America with a rosette of basal leaves, tiny white flowers, and oblong seed capsules

Examples

Whitlow grass weed
Whitlow grass often appears in lawns and gardens as a persistent spring weed.
Many wildflower guides mention whitlow grass because of its early blooming season.
The small white flowers of whitlow grass can blanket waste ground in April.
