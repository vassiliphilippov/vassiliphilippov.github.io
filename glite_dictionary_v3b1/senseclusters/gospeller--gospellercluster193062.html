gospeller

noun
preacher or interpreter of the Christian Gospel
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːspələr / (AmE), / ˈɡɒspələ /; / ˈɡɒspələr / (BrE)
a person, especially a preacher, who spreads or interprets the teachings of the Christian Gospel.
