camlet

noun
garment made of camlet fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmlət / (AmE), / ˈkamlɪt /; / ˈkæmlɪt / (BrE)
a luxurious woven fabric, originally made from silk and camel's hair (sometimes wool or goat hair), used for garments and upholstery
