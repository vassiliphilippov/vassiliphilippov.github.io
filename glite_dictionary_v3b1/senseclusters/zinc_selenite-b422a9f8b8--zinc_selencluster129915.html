zinc selenite

noun
a salt of zinc and selenous acid
Pronunciation: / ˌzɪŋk səˈliːnaɪt / (AmE), / ˌzɪŋk sɪˈliːnaɪt / (BrE)
a toxic white or colorless salt, ZnSeO3, formed from selenous acid and zinc, used in industry and research

Examples

Zinc selenite pigment
Zinc selenite is sometimes used as a pigment in specialty glass.
Exposure to zinc selenite can be hazardous without proper safety equipment.
The laboratory ordered more zinc selenite for the experiment.
