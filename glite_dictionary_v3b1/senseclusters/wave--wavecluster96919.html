wave

verb
move in a wavy or rising and falling motion
1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪv / (AmE), / weɪv / (BrE)
to move with a rising and falling or wavy motion, like the surface of the sea or a flag in the wind

Examples

Flags wave in wind
Clouds of smoke waved above the bonfire, slowly drifting upward.
The dancer's scarf waved in the air as she moved across the stage.
The tall grass waved gently in the summer breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flutter - to move lightly and quickly back and forth or up and down
  • synonym: undulate - to move in a smooth, wavelike motion
