haul-away

noun
removal service
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔl əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːl əˌweɪ / (BrE)

Examples

A haul-away job

After the renovation, a haul-away was needed to remove all the construction debris.
The company offers a haul-away service for unwanted furniture and large items.
