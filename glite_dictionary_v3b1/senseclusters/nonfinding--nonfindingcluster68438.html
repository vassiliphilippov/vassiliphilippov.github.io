nonfinding

noun
absence of results
8.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈfaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
a situation or report in which no result, evidence, or significant outcome is found

Examples

A nonfinding report
The experiment ended with a nonfinding, as no significant changes were detected.
Despite extensive research, the report was ultimately a nonfinding on the proposed theory.
A nonfinding can still provide valuable information for future scientific studies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: finding - a result or discovery from research or investigation
  • synonym: null result - an outcome where no effect or evidence is observed
