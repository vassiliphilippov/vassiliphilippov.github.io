sheet

noun
single piece of paper for writing or printing
1.0 19/m
Pronunciation: /  Éit / (AmE), /  ÉiÀêt / (BrE)
a single piece of paper, especially one used for writing or printing

Examples

A sheet of paper
Each student was given a sheet to complete during the exam.
She scribbled her notes on a sheet of lined paper.
The printer jammed because too many sheets loaded at once.

  • piece of paper
    1.0 8/m
