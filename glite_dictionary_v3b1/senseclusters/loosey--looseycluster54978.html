loosey

adjective
not tight
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsi / (AmE), / ˈluːsi / (BrE)
informal term meaning not tight or not firmly fixed in place

Examples

Loosey lid
The lid on the jar was a bit loosey, so I tightened it before putting it away.
After a few uses, the handle became loosey and wobbled when I tried to open the door.
Check if the bolt is loosey before you start the machine to avoid any accidents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loose - not firmly fixed in place; not tight
  • antonym: tight - firmly fixed in place; not loose
