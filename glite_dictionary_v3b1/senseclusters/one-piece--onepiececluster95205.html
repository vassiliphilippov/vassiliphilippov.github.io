one-piece

adjective
clothing made in a single piece
3.2 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw ånÀåpis / (AmE), / Ààw ånÀåpiÀês / (BrE)
(of clothing) consisting of or made in a single piece, rather than being divided into separate parts.

Examples

A one-piece swimsuit
She bought a stylish one-piece swimsuit for her vacation.
The baby's one-piece pajamas keep him warm at night.
This dress is a one-piece, so it's very easy to put on.

Synonyms
