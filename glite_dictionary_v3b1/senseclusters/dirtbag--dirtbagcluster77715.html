dirtbag

noun
outdoor lifestyle enthusiast
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrtˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːtˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a person who chooses to live simply and with few possessions, often to pursue outdoor sports or a nontraditional lifestyle

Examples

A dirtbag climber
After college she became a dirtbag, traveling the country to climb and live cheaply.
Many dirtbags spend their summers camping near national parks to save money.
He admired the dirtbag lifestyle for its freedom and connection to nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nomad - a person who moves from place to place, often for work or lifestyle
  • synonym: vagabond - a person who wanders from place to place without a permanent home
