gaspingly

adverb
with shock or amazement
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæspɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːspɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows shock, amazement, or intense surprise, often with a sharp intake of breath

Examples

Looking gaspingly
He stared gaspingly at the breathtaking view from the mountain peak.
The audience listened gaspingly as the magician performed his final trick.
She opened the letter gaspingly, unable to believe the news inside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonished - very surprised or amazed
  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed and peaceful way
