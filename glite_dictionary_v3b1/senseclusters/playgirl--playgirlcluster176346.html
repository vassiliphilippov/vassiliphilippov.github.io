playgirl

noun
sexually adventurous woman
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌɡɜrl / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɡɜːl / (BrE)
a woman who has casual sexual relationships with several partners or is known for being sexually adventurous

Examples

A playgirl with lovers
Rumors said she was a playgirl who rarely stayed with one partner for long.
The magazine featured an interview with a famous playgirl known for her glamorous lifestyle.
Some people admired her confidence, while others criticized her for being a playgirl.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playboy - a man who has many casual sexual relationships
  • antonym: prude - a person who is very proper or easily shocked by sexual matters
