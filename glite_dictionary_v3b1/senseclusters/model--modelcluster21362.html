model

adjective
worthy of imitation; exemplary
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) worthy of being copied or imitated; serving as an example.

Examples

Model behavior
His model behavior in difficult situations serves as an example to others.
She is a model student who always completes her assignments on time.
The company was praised for its model approach to environmental responsibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exemplary - serving as a desirable model; representing the best of its kind
