battered sav

noun
battered and deep-fried sausage
Pronunciation: / ˈbætərd sæv / (AmE), / ˈbætəd sav / (BrE)
A sausage dipped in batter and deep-fried, typically sold in fish and chip shops.

Examples

A battered sav snack
At the fair, kids lined up to get a hot, crunchy battered sav.
I bought a battered sav from the chip shop for lunch.
She prefers her battered sav with plenty of tomato sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saveloy - a highly seasoned red sausage, used for making battered savs
