gambeson

noun
padded armor jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmbəsən / (AmE), / ˈɡæmbɪsən / (BrE)
a padded defensive jacket worn as armor or under armor, especially in medieval times

Examples

A gambeson jacket
The knight wore a thick gambeson beneath his metal armor for extra protection.
A well-made gambeson could absorb the impact of sword blows during battle.
Before donning his chain mail, he put on a padded gambeson to cushion his body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armor - protective clothing worn to prevent injury in battle
  • synonym: hauberk - a long shirt of chain mail worn as armor
