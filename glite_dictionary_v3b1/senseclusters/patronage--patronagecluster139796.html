patronage

noun
business or support from customers
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪtrənɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpætrənɪdʒ /; / ˈpatrənɪdʒ / (BrE)
the support or business given by customers to a shop, restaurant, or similar place

Examples

Customer patronage
Since opening last year, the café has enjoyed steady patronage from local residents.
Thank you for your patronage; we appreciate your continued support of our business.
The theatre relies on the patronage of its loyal audience to survive.
The museum's exhibitions are made possible by the generous patronage of local businesses.
The restaurant relies heavily on tourist patronage during the summer months.
We appreciate your continued patronage over the years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clientele - a group of regular customers or clients
  • synonym: custom - the habitual patronage of a particular shop or business
