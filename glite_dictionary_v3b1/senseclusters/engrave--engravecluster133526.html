Synonyms

  • etch
    5.5 1.75/m
    etch cuts designs into metal or glass often with acid for prints, while engrave means cutting words or pictures into a surface.
  • carve
    2.1 19/m
    carve shapes material by cutting, often making larger forms in wood or stone, while engrave means making finer words or designs on a surface.
  • inscribe
