unhung

adjective
taken down
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌŋ / (BrE)
no longer hanging; taken down from a hook, wall, or other place where it was suspended

Examples

Unhung paintings removed
The paintings were carefully unhung before the gallery renovation began.
After the exhibition ended, each photograph was unhung and packed away.
He unhung his coat from the rack and prepared to leave the office.
