trial

noun
preliminary sports competition
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪ.əl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪ.əl / (BrE)
a preliminary competition held to select participants or determine qualifications, especially in sports

Examples

A trial match
After failing to qualify in the first trial, she trained even harder for the next one.
He performed well in the trial and was selected for the national team.
The swimmers are competing in the Olympic trials this weekend.
