deep-dish pie

noun
pie with thick fruit filling and top biscuit crust
6.3 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdiːp.dɪʃ paɪ/ (AmE), /ˈdiːp.dɪʃ paɪ/ (BrE)
A pie with a thick layer of fruit filling, typically covered with a rich biscuit dough or pastry, usually only on top.

Examples

A deep-dish pie dessert
She baked a delicious deep-dish pie filled with fresh apples and cinnamon for dessert.
The deep-dish pie was so hearty that everyone wanted a second helping.
You can use peaches or berries to make a summer deep-dish pie for your family.
