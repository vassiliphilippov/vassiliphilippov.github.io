latrine

noun
communal toilet, often in camps or military areas
5.3
Pronunciation: / ləˈtrin / (AmE), / ləˈtriːn / (BrE)
a communal toilet, especially one in a military camp or outdoor setting

Examples

A latrine in camp
During the camping trip, we were instructed to use the designated latrine to maintain cleanliness.
The base commander ordered regular cleaning of the latrine to prevent illness among the troops.
The soldiers built a latrine behind the barracks for use during the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outhouse - a small outdoor building containing a toilet
  • synonym: toilet - a fixture for defecation and urination
