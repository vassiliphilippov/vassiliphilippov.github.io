Examples

To flash synchronously
All the lights flashed synchronously to create an impressive visual effect.
The computers updated their clocks synchronously across the network.
The dancers moved synchronously to the rhythm of the music.

Synonyms

  • simultaneously
    4.2 28/m
    simultaneously means happening at the same instant without implying coordination, while synchronously suggests events are timed and happen in step together.
  • concomitantly
    8.6 0.43/m
    concomitantly is a formal way to say two things occur together, while synchronously stresses precise timing or moving in step with each other.
  • coincidently
    4.3 0.00/m
    coincidently describes things that happen at the same time by chance, while synchronously implies planned or matched timing and coordination.
