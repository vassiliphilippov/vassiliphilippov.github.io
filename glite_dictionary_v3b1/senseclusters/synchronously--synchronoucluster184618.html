synchronously

adverb
at the same time; in synchrony
3.5 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs.liː/ (AmE), /ˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs.liː/ (BrE)
In a way that occurs at the same time or in step with something else; in synchrony.

Examples

To flash synchronously
All the lights flashed synchronously to create an impressive visual effect.
The computers updated their clocks synchronously across the network.
The dancers moved synchronously to the rhythm of the music.
