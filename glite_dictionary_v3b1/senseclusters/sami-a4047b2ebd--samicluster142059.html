Sami

noun
indigenous people of northern Scandinavia
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈsɑːmi / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people inhabiting northern Scandinavia and parts of Russia, traditionally known for reindeer herding and a nomadic lifestyle
