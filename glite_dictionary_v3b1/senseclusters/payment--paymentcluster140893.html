payment

noun
an act of returning in kind or recompense
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪmənt / (BrE)
the act of repaying or returning something in kind as a response or recompense

Examples

Payment for help
As a payment for his kindness, she helped him with his work.
He saw the trouble as a payment for past mistakes.
They viewed the apology as a just payment for the harm done.

Related Terms

  • synonym: requital - the act of returning or recompensing
  • synonym: retaliation - the act of returning a like for a like
