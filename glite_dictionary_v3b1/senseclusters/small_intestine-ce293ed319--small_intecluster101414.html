small intestine

noun
part of the digestive tract between stomach and large intestine
3.6
Pronunciation: / smɔl ɪnˈtɛstɪn / (AmE), / smɔːl ɪnˈtɛstɪn / (BrE)
the long, narrow part of the digestive tract where most digestion and absorption of food occurs, between the stomach and the large intestine
