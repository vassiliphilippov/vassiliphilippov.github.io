hillfort

noun
ancient fortified settlement
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪlˌfɔrt / (AmE), / ˈhɪlˌfɔːt / (BrE)
an ancient fortified place built on a hill, usually for defense

Examples

Archaeologists discovered pottery fragments inside the old hillfort during their excavation.
From the top of the hillfort, you can see for miles across the countryside.
