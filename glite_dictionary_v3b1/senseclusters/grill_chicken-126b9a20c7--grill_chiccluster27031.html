grill chicken

verb
cook chicken on a grill over direct heat
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪl ˈtʃɪk.ən / (AmE), / ɡrɪl ˈtʃɪk.ɪn / (BrE)
to cook chicken over direct heat, often on a grill or barbecue, giving it a characteristic charred flavor

Examples

To grill chicken outdoors
Every summer, we grill chicken in the backyard for family gatherings.
He loves to grill chicken with a spicy marinade on the barbecue.
You can grill chicken breasts quickly for a healthy weeknight dinner.
