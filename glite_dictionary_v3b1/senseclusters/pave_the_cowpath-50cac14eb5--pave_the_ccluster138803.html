pave the cowpath

verb
systematize existing inefficient practice
9.2
Pronunciation: / peɪv ðə ˈkaʊˌpæθ / (AmE), / peɪv ðə ˈkaʊpɑːθ / (BrE)
to formalize or systematize an existing informal practice or method, often without improving its efficiency or questioning its usefulness

Examples

To pave the cowpath formally
Rather than paving the cowpath, the committee proposed a more innovative solution to the outdated process.
The company decided to pave the cowpath by making official what employees were already doing informally.
We shouldn't just pave the cowpath when updating our software; let's consider new workflows.
