assistor

noun
helper or supporter
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪstər / (AmE), / əˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a person or thing that helps or provides assistance or support to someone or something

Examples

Helpful assistor
The nurse acted as an assistor during the emergency procedure.
Each player had an assistor to help them during the competition.
The new software works as an assistor to make your daily tasks easier.
The coach acted as an assistor during the training session.
In the project, she was the main assistor to the team leader.
A good assistor can make a big difference in achieving success.

  • synonym: helper - a person who helps someone else
  • antonym: opponent - a person who is against or competes with another
