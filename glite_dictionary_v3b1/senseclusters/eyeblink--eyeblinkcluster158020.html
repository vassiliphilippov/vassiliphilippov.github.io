eyeblink

noun
quick eyelid closure
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈaɪˌblɪŋk / (BrE)
the act of quickly closing and opening the eyelid, or a very brief moment in time

Examples

An eyeblink of time
The entire performance was over in an eyeblink.
A sudden eyeblink can protect your eyes from dust or bright light.
He missed the winning goal in the brief eyeblink when he looked away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - the act of quickly closing and opening the eyes
  • synonym: moment - a very short period of time
