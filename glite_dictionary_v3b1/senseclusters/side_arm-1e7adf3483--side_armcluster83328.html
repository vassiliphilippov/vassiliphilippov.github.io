side arm

noun
handheld firearm (pistol or revolver)
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪd ɑrm / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd ɑːm / (BrE)
A firearm, such as a pistol or revolver, designed to be held and fired with one hand.

Examples

A side arm pistol
Military personnel are often issued a side arm in addition to a rifle.
She drew her side arm quickly when she heard the noise.
The police officer kept his side arm holstered at his waist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handgun - a firearm designed to be held and fired with one hand
  • antonym: long gun - a firearm designed to be fired from the shoulder, such as a rifle or shotgun
