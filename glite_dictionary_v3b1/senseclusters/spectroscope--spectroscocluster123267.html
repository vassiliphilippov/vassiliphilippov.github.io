spectroscope

noun
optical device for analyzing spectra
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛktɹəˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈspɛktrəˌskəʊp / (BrE)
an optical instrument used to disperse light into its spectrum for examination or analysis

Examples

A spectroscope instrument
A spectroscope can reveal the presence of different elements in a substance by their spectral lines.
The scientist used a spectroscope to analyze the composition of the distant star.
With the help of a spectroscope, she observed the unique patterns of emitted light from the gas sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spectrograph - an instrument that records spectra, especially in the context of photography or digital recording
