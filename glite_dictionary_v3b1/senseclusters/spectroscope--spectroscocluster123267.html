spectroscope

noun
optical device for analyzing spectra
3.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈspek.trə.skoʊp/ /; / /ˈspek.troʊ.skoʊp/ (AmE), /ˈspek.trə.skəʊp/ /; / /ˈspek.trəʊ.skəʊp/ (BrE)
