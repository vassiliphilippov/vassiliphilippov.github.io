discouraging

adjective
causing loss of confidence or hope
3.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈskɜːr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ /; / /dɪ.ˈskʌr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈskʌr.ɪ.dʒɪŋ/ (BrE)
making someone feel less confident, hopeful, or likely to do something; showing disapproval or lack of support

Examples

Discouraging remarks

The teacher's discouraging tone affected the students' motivation.
