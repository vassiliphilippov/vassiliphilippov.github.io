Synonyms

  • swallow
    4.8 7/m
    swallow means to take something inside so it disappears, while swallow up often stresses total enclosure and disappearance.
  • engulf
    5.1 2/m
    engulf covers or surrounds suddenly and forcefully, while swallow up often appears in everyday speech for total covering.
  • eat up
    2.8 0.2/m
    eat up is often literal or informal for using or destroying something, while swallow up means fully enclose or hide.
  • immerse
    4.9 0.1/m
    immerse means to put or cover deeply, often in liquid or work, while swallow up focuses on enclosing until hidden.
  • submerge
    4.6 0.7/m
    submerge means to put entirely under a surface, usually water, while swallow up can be used more broadly beyond liquids.
