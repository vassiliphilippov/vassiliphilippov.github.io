proprietary

noun
privately owned product
Pronunciation: / prəˈpraɪəˌtɛri / (AmE), / prəˈprʌɪətəri / (BrE)
a product or item that is owned and controlled by a private company, often with restrictions on use or modification

Examples

A proprietary product
The company sells several proprietaries that are not available from other manufacturers.
Many software proprietaries restrict how users can modify the program.
Pharmaceutical firms often protect their proprietaries with patents to prevent competition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generic - a product not protected by a brand or trademark
  • synonym: trademark - a symbol or name legally registered for use by a company
