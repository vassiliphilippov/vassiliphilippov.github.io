Gwinnett

proper noun
surname; historical figure
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɡwɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / ɡwɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a family name, especially known for Button Gwinnett, an American Founding Father and signer of the Declaration of Independence

Examples
