votable

adjective
eligible for voting
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈvoʊ.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈvəʊ.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
able or eligible to be voted for, especially as a candidate, officer, or selectable option in an election or decision process

Examples

The committee reviewed which proposals were votable at the annual meeting.
If a candidate misses the deadline, they are not votable in this election cycle.
