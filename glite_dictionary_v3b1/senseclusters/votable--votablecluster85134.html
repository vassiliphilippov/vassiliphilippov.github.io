votable

adjective
eligible for voting
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvoʊtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvəʊtəbl̩ / (BrE)
able or eligible to be voted for, especially as a candidate, officer, or selectable option in an election or decision process

Examples

Votable candidate
Only officially nominated candidates are votable in the upcoming student council election.
The committee reviewed which proposals were votable at the annual meeting.
If a candidate misses the deadline, they are not votable in this election cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electable - able to be elected to a position
  • antonym: ineligible - not qualified or allowed to be chosen or voted for
