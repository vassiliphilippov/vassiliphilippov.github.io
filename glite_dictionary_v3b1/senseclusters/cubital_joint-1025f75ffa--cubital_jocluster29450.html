cubital joint

noun
hinge joint between forearm and upper arm (elbow)
6.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkjuː.bɪ.təl dʒɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈkjuː.bɪ.təl dʒɔɪnt/ (BrE)
the hinge joint connecting the forearm and upper arm in humans, or the equivalent joint in the forelimbs of quadrupeds; commonly known as the elbow

Examples

The cubital joint hinge
In many quadrupeds, the cubital joint provides flexibility for running and digging.
Physical therapists often assess the cubital joint for signs of injury or restricted motion.
Swelling at the cubital joint can indicate underlying inflammation or trauma.
