epigoni

noun
lesser successors
Pronunciation: / ɛˈpɪɡəˌnaɪ / (AmE), / ɛˈpɪɡənaɪ / (BrE)
successors or followers, especially those regarded as less distinguished than their predecessors

Examples

The epigoni of artists
The epigoni of the famous artist tried to imitate his style but never matched his originality.
Many critics saw the new generation of writers as mere epigoni of their celebrated mentors.
The epigoni failed to achieve the same level of innovation as the original founders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - a person who comes after or supports another
  • antonym: predecessor - a person who came before another in a position or role
