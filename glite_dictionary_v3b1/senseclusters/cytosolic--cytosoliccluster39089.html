cytosolic

adjective
in the cytosol
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəˈsɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəˈsɒlɪk / (BrE)
located in or relating to the cytosol, the fluid part of a cell's cytoplasm

Examples

Cytosolic enzyme activity
The enzyme is found in the cytosolic compartment of the cell, where it helps break down sugars.
Researchers measured the cytosolic concentration of calcium ions after the cells were stimulated.
A mutation in the protein affected its cytosolic localization, preventing it from functioning properly.
The cytosolic concentration of calcium ions plays a key role in cell signaling.
Researchers measured the cytosolic levels of the protein after treatment.
Certain metabolic reactions occur only in the cytosolic compartment of the cell.

  • antonym: membranous - relating to or resembling a membrane
