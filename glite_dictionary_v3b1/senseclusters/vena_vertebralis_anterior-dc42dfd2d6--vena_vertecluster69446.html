vena vertebralis anterior

noun
a neck vein draining into the vertebral vein
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə vɜːr.tə.ˈbreɪ.lɪs æn.ˈtɪr.iː.ər/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə vɜː.tə.ˈbreɪ.lɪs æn.ˈtɪə.ri.ə/ (BrE)
a vein that runs alongside the ascending cervical artery and drains into the vertebral vein.

Examples

The vena vertebralis anterior vein
During neck surgery, care must be taken not to damage the vena vertebralis anterior.
The vena vertebralis anterior is important in draining blood from the cervical region of the spine.
The vena vertebralis anterior runs parallel to the ascending cervical artery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vertebral vein - a vein draining blood from the vertebral column
