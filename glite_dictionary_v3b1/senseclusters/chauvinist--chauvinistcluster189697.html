chauvinist

noun
fanatical supporter of own group or country
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊvənɪst /; / ˈʃoʊ.və.nɪst / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊvɪnɪst /; / ˈʃəʊ.vɪ.nɪst / (BrE)
a person who strongly and aggressively believes their own country, group, or gender is better than others, often showing prejudice or bias

Examples

A chauvinist attitude
He was labeled a chauvinist because of his unyielding support for his country's aggressive policies.
Many critics accused the politician of being a chauvinist who dismissed any foreign perspective.
Their chauvinist attitudes made it difficult to work with people from different backgrounds.
He was often called a chauvinist because of his dismissive attitude towards women in the workplace.
Her remarks labeled her as a chauvinist, unwilling to accept that others could be equally capable.
National chauvinist sentiments sometimes lead to hostility between neighboring countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bigot - a person intolerant toward those holding different opinions
  • antonym: cosmopolitan - free from national limitations or prejudices
  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly identifies with their nation and vigorously supports its interests
  • synonym: patriot - a person who loves and strongly supports their country
  • synonym: sexist - a person with prejudice based on gender
