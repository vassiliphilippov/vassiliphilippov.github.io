reflexly

adverb
in a reflexive, automatic manner
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈriːˌflɛksli / (AmE), / ˈriːˌflɛksli / (BrE)
In a reflexive manner; by means of reflexes or as a result of an automatic response to a stimulus, without conscious thought.

Examples

Muscles contract reflexly
Many muscles contract reflexly in response to sudden pain.
Swallowing can occur reflexly when food touches the back of the throat.
The doctor tested the patient’s knee, which jerked reflexly after being tapped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reflexively - in a manner related to a reflex; automatically
