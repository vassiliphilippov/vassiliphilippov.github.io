debris

noun
scattered remains or waste
5.2
Pronunciation: / dəˈbri / (AmE), / ˈdɛbri ˈdeɪbri / (BrE)
scattered pieces of waste or remains left after something has been destroyed, broken, or removed

Examples

Debris from trees
After the hurricane, the streets were littered with debris from fallen trees and damaged houses.
The explosion sent debris flying through the air, endangering everyone nearby.
Workers spent days clearing debris from the collapsed building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wreckage - the remains of something that has been badly damaged or destroyed
