Synonyms

  • detritus
    8.2 0.4/m
    detritus is loose fragments or natural waste after decay or damage, while debris means scattered pieces left after destruction.
  • wreckage
    3.6 14/m
    wreckage is the large broken parts of a vehicle or building after a crash, while debris is scattered small waste left after destruction.
  • remains
    3.6 4/m
    remains are what is left after something is used, consumed, or removed and can be broad or formal, while debris is scattered waste.
