zoophilic

adjective
fond of animals
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌzoʊəˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌzəʊəˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
having a strong affection for or attraction to animals.

Examples

Zoophilic animal lover
Her zoophilic nature was clear from the way she cared for every stray animal she found.
The organization is run by zoophilic volunteers who dedicate their time to animal welfare.
His zoophilic tendencies led him to become a veterinarian.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animal-loving - having a great affection for animals
  • antonym: zoophobic - having a fear or dislike of animals
