boy scout

noun
a naive or overly innocent man
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ skaʊt / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ skaʊt / (BrE)
A man who is regarded as naive or overly innocent, especially in matters of experience or the ways of the world.

Examples

A real boy scout
Don't be a boy scout; not everyone follows the rules in this business.
He acted like such a boy scout that he didn't see her deception coming.
His boy scout attitude made him an easy target for more cunning negotiators.
