nascency

noun
state of being born; emergence
Pronunciation: / ˈnæs.ən.si / (AmE), / ˈnas.ən.si / (BrE)
the process or state of being born or coming into existence; birth or emergence

Examples

The nascency of ideas
Scientists are fascinated by the nascency of new technologies and their impact on society.
The nascency of her artistic style was evident in her early paintings.
We witnessed the nascency of a new movement for social change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birth - the emergence of a baby or other young from the body of its mother; the start of something
  • antonym: death - the end of the life of a person or organism
  • synonym: emergence - the process of coming into existence or prominence
