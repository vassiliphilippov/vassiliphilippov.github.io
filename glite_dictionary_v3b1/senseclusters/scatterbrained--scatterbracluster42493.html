scatterbrained

adjective
easily distracted; disorganized in thought
7.5 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈskæt.ər.breɪnd/ (AmE), /ˈskæt.ə.breɪnd/ (BrE)
(of a person) disorganized, forgetful, or easily distracted; unable to concentrate or focus

Examples

Scatterbrained person
Despite her scatterbrained nature, she always manages to finish her tasks on time.
He can be quite scatterbrained in meetings, often forgetting important points.
Her scatterbrained approach to planning made group projects challenging for everyone.
Despite being brilliant, he could be a bit scatterbrained about appointments and deadlines.
Her scatterbrained approach to organizing meant she often lost important documents.
My scatterbrained friend forgot her keys at the café again yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absent-minded - frequently forgetting things or not paying attention
  • synonym: distracted - unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied
  • synonym: flighty - (of a person) fickle and irresponsible
  • antonym: focused - having clear and definite focus or purpose
  • synonym: forgetful - not able to remember things easily
