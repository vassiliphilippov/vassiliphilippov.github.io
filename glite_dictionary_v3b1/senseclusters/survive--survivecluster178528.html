survive

verb
live longer than another person
Pronunciation: / sərˈvaɪv / (AmE), / səˈvaɪv / (BrE)
to continue to live or exist after someone else has died; to outlive someone

Examples

Survived by family
He was survived by three sons and a daughter.
Of their four children, only one survived both parents.
She survived her husband by nearly two decades.
