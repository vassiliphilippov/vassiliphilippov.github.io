vaporific

adjective
capable of becoming vapor
Pronunciation: / ˌveɪpəˈrɪfɪk /; / ˌveɪpərˈɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌveɪpəˈrɪfɪk /; / ˌveɪpjʊˈrɪfɪk / (BrE)
(of a substance) capable of being converted into vapor; able to be volatilized.

Examples

Vaporific substance
Certain vaporific compounds must be stored in airtight containers to prevent loss through evaporation.
The vaporific nature of the liquid made it suitable for use in scientific experiments.
This chemical is highly vaporific, so it should only be handled in a well-ventilated area.
Engineers tested the vapourific properties of the substance under various temperatures.
The chemical was found to be highly vapourific when exposed to heat.
This solvent is vapourific and should be used in a well-ventilated area.
