verjuice

noun
sour fruit juice
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrdʒuːs / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdʒuːs / (BrE)
a sour juice made from unripe grapes or other fruit, used in cooking to add acidity

Examples

A splash of verjuice
The chef added a splash of verjuice to the salad dressing for extra tang.
In medieval recipes, verjuice was often used to balance the sweetness of dishes.
You can substitute verjuice for vinegar if you want a milder sour taste.
