truckful

noun
amount filling a truck
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌkˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈtrʌkˌfʊl / (BrE)

Examples

The farmer brought a truckful of fresh vegetables to the market every Saturday.
After the festival, there was a truckful of trash to be collected and removed.
