inducement

noun
something that motivates or encourages
7.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdusmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjuːsmənt / (BrE)
something that persuades or encourages someone to do something; a motive or incentive.

Examples

An inducement to invest
Tax breaks often serve as an inducement to attract investors to a region.
The company offered a generous bonus as an inducement for employees to meet their targets.
Without some kind of inducement, people may not be willing to participate in the study.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deterrent - something that discourages someone from doing something
  • synonym: incentive - something that encourages a person to do something
