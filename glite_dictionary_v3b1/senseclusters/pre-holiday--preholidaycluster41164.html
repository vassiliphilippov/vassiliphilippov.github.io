pre-holiday

adjective
before a holiday
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈhɑːləˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈhɒləˌdeɪ / (BrE)
happening or existing before a holiday period begins

Examples

Pre-holiday shopping rush
Many families feel stressed during the pre-holiday rush to prepare for celebrations.
The store advertised special pre-holiday discounts to attract early shoppers.
She made a pre-holiday checklist to ensure nothing was forgotten before their trip.
