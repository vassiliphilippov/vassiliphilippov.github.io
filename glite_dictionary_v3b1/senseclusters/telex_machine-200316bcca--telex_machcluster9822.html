telex machine

noun
telegraph-connected message-sending device
9.6 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…õl…õks m…ôÀà Éin / (AmE), / ÀàtiÀêl…õks m…ôÀà ÉiÀên / (BrE)
A device resembling a typewriter, used to send and receive typed messages over telegraph lines, often as part of the telex communication network.

Examples

The telex machine system
Before email, the telex machine was essential for fast business correspondence across continents.
She typed the order on the telex machine and sent it directly to the supplier overseas.
The company installed a telex machine to handle urgent international communications.

Synonyms

  • teleprinter
    8.8 0.01/m
    a teleprinter prints messages sent over telegraph or phone lines, while a telex machine is a device used on the telex network.
  • teletypewriter
    8.8 0.02/m
    a teletypewriter prints and receives typed messages over long lines for offices, while a telex machine belongs to the telex communication network.
  • teletype machine
    8.8 0.02/m
    a teletype machine prints text sent over telegraph lines in offices, while a telex machine is the device used on the telex network.
  • telex
    9.3 0.24/m
    telex often means the system or a message sent over it, while a telex machine is the physical typewriter-like device used to send messages.
