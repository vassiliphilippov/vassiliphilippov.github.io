burner

noun
device for burning fuel or refuse
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnə / (BrE)
A device or apparatus for burning fuel or refuse to produce heat or energy.

Examples

Industrial burner
The facility installed a new waste burner designed to reduce emissions.
The gas burner in the kitchen stopped working properly this morning.
The laboratory technician adjusted the Bunsen burner to get a blue flame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incinerator - a device for burning waste material at high temperatures
  • synonym: stove - a device that burns fuel for heating or cooking
