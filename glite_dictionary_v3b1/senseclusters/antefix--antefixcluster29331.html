antefix

noun
ornamental block at roof eaves
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.tɪ.fɪks / (AmE), / ˈæn.tɪ.fɪks / (BrE)
a decorative vertical block, often carved, placed at the eaves of a tiled roof to conceal the joints between tiles

Examples

A antefix on roof
An intricately carved antefix was unearthed during the excavation of the classical temple.
Many Greek temples feature a row of antefixes to hide the ends of the roof tiles.
The ancient Roman villa was adorned with terracotta antefixes along the roofline.
