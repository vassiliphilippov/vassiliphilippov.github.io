Montenegrin

noun
language of Montenegro
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːntəˈniːɡrɪn / (AmE), / ˌmɒntɪˈniːɡrɪn / (BrE)
the South Slavic language spoken mainly in Montenegro

Examples

Montenegrin language
She learned to speak Montenegrin before traveling to Montenegro for her studies.
The official documents were translated into Montenegrin for the local population.
Many people in Montenegro use Montenegrin in schools and government offices.
