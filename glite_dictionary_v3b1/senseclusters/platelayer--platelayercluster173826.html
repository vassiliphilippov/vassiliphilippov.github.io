platelayer

noun
railway track maintenance worker
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪtˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtleɪə / (BrE)
A railway worker whose job is to lay and repair railroad tracks.

Examples

A platelayer repairs tracks
During the storm, a team of platelayers was called out to repair a damaged section of rail.
The platelayer inspected the tracks early every morning to ensure safety for passing trains.
Years ago, my grandfather worked as a platelayer on the Great Western Railway.
