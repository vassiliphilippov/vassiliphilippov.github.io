media

noun
artistic materials
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
materials or tools used by artists or creators to produce works, such as paint, clay, film, or digital technology

Examples

Artistic media
The artist experimented with different media to find the best way to express her ideas.
Sculptors often use a variety of media, including metal, wood, and stone, in their work.
Digital media have opened new possibilities for creative expression in the modern art world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medium - a material or method used by an artist to create art
  • synonym: technique - a way of carrying out a particular artistic task
