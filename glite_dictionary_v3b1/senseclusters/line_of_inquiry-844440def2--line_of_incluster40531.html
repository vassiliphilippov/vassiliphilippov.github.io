line of inquiry

noun
methodical sequence of questions or investigations
2.5
Pronunciation: / laɪn əv ɪnˈkwaɪəri / (AmE), / laɪn əv ɪnˈkwaɪəri / (BrE)
a series or sequence of questions or investigations directed towards finding out something or developing a particular argument

Examples

They abandoned one line of inquiry when it proved to be a dead end.
