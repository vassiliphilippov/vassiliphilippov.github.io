incest

noun
sexual intercourse between close relatives
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.sɛst / (AmE), / ˈɪn.sɛst / (BrE)
sexual intercourse between close relatives, especially those too closely related to marry by law or custom, such as between a parent and a child or between siblings.
