feminazi

adjective
offensive for extreme feminism
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛmɪˌnɑzi / (AmE), / ˈfɛmɪˌnɑːzi / (BrE)
offensive term describing actions, statements, or policies seen as extremely or aggressively feminist

Examples

Feminazi policies
Some critics used the feminazi label to attack the new workplace equality policies.
He described her speech as a feminazi rant, which many found deeply offensive.
The article was criticized for its feminazi tone and lack of balance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderate - not extreme in views or actions
  • synonym: radical feminist - a person with extreme feminist views
