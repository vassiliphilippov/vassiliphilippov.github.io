encouragement

noun
feeling of being supported or given hope
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɜrɪdʒmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒmənt / (BrE)
The feeling of being given support, confidence, or hope.

Examples

Find encouragement in friends
He drew encouragement from his friends' support during a difficult time.
Her kind words gave me a real sense of encouragement before the exam.
The team's early success was a great source of encouragement to the new coach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouragement - the feeling of losing confidence or hope
