baseball

noun
ball used in baseball game
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsˌbɔːl / (BrE)
A hard, small ball with a leather cover, used in playing the game of baseball.

Examples

A baseball glove
He caught the baseball with his glove during practice.
Several baseballs were signed by the famous player and given to fans.
