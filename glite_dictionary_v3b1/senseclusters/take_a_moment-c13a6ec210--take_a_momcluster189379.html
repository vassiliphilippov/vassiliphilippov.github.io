take a moment

verb
pause briefly for reflection or rest
Pronunciation: / teɪk ə ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / teɪk ə ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
to pause briefly in order to think, rest, or acknowledge something

Examples

To take a moment to think
Before making a decision, please take a moment to consider all the options.
He likes to take a moment each morning to meditate before starting his day.
Let's take a moment to remember those who helped us succeed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pause - to temporarily stop an activity
  • synonym: take a break - to rest for a short time from work or activity
