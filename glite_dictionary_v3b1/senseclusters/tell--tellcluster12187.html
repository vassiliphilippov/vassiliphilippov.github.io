Synonyms

  • recount
    4.1 9.30/m
    recount gives a detailed, step-by-step account of events to someone, while tell is a more general spoken report.
  • relate
    2.9 6.99/m
    relate gives an account of events or facts, often in speech, while tell is used broadly for saying or describing things aloud.
  • narrate
    4.0 5.62/m
    narrate tells a story or describes events for readers or listeners, while tell mainly means speaking a plain account aloud.
  • tell a story
    1.1 4.29/m
    tell a story is to narrate an account or tale for listeners, while tell is a short, general verb for saying something aloud.
