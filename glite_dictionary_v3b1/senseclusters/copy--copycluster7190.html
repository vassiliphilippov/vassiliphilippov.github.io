copy

noun
reproduction of a written or printed document
1.0 64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêpi / (AmE), / Ààk…ípi / (BrE)
a reproduction or duplicate of a document, object, or work

Examples

A copy of the report

Please bring a copy of your birth certificate to the office.
The school keeps a copy of every student's record for reference.
Every student received a copy of the assignment before leaving class.
She made a copy of her passport to submit with the application.
That painting is an impressive copy of the original masterpiece.
