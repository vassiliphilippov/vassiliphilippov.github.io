undashed

adjective
not discouraged
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdæʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈdæʃt / (BrE)
not discouraged, dismayed, or having lost confidence or courage despite difficulties or setbacks

Examples

Undashed spirit
Even after several failures, his spirit remained undashed and he tried again.
The team was undashed by the early loss and played with renewed determination.
She faced criticism with an undashed attitude, refusing to give up on her goals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraged - having lost confidence or enthusiasm
  • synonym: undaunted - not intimidated or discouraged by difficulty or danger
