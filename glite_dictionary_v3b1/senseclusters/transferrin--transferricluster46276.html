transferrin

noun
blood protein that carries iron
9.9 6.7
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɛr.ɪn / (AmE), / trænsˈfɛr.ɪn / (BrE)
a globulin protein in blood plasma that binds and transports iron throughout the body

Examples

Transferrin protein

Iron binds to transferrin for safe transport in the bloodstream.
Low levels of transferrin in blood tests may indicate liver disease.
