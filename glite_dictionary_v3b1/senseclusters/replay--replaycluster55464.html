replay

verb
play again (a recording/melody)
1.0
Pronunciation: / riˈpleɪ /; / ˌriːˈpleɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈpleɪ / (BrE)
to play a recording, broadcast, or event again in order to hear or see it once more
