infeed

noun
input process
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌfiːd / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌfiːd / (BrE)
the action or process of supplying material or data into a machine, system, or process

Examples

Infeed of material
The technician adjusted the infeed to ensure the machine operated smoothly.
A steady infeed of raw materials is essential for continuous production.
The software monitors the infeed of data to detect any irregularities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: input - the act of putting something into a system or process
  • antonym: output - the amount or process of producing or delivering something from a system
