spell of enchantment

noun
magical or irresistible influence
Pronunciation: / spɛl əv ɪnˈtʃæntmənt / (AmE), / spɛl əv ɪnˈtʃɑːntmənt / (BrE)
a period or condition in which someone or something is under the influence of magical powers or irresistible charm

Examples

A spell of enchantment
He fell into a spell of enchantment as soon as he heard her sing.
The ancient forest seemed to lie under a spell of enchantment all summer long.
Tourists were drawn by the castle’s spell of enchantment and its mysterious legends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charm - a quality that makes someone or something attractive or fascinating
  • antonym: curse - a solemn utterance intended to invoke a supernatural power to inflict harm or punishment
  • synonym: enchantment - a feeling of great pleasure; magic spell
