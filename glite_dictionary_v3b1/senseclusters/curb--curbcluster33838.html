curb

noun
a horse's bit with an attached chain or strap
Pronunciation: / kɝb / (AmE), / kɜːb / (BrE)
a type of horse's bit with a chain or strap attached, used to help control a horse

Examples

A curb bit
She purchased a new curb for her horse's bridle.
Some horses respond better to a curb than to a simple snaffle.
The rider adjusted the curb before the competition began.
