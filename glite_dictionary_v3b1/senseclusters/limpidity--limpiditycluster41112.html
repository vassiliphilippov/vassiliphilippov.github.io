limpidity

noun
clarity or transparency
Pronunciation: / lɪmˈpɪdəti / (AmE), / lɪmˈpɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality of being clear, transparent, or able to transmit light without distortion or diffusion

Examples

Limpidity of water
In the sunlight, the limpidity of the crystal became even more apparent.
Scientists tested the limpidity of the new type of glass for use in camera lenses.
The limpidity of the mountain lake made it possible to see all the way to the bottom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear or easy to see through
  • antonym: opacity - the quality of being opaque, not allowing light to pass through
