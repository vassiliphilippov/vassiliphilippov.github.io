Christlikeness

noun
image of Jesus
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪstˌlaɪknəs / (AmE), / ˈkraɪstˌlaɪknɪs / (BrE)
a physical representation or likeness of Jesus Christ, such as a painting, statue, or portrait

Examples

A painted Christlikeness
The church displayed a beautiful Christlikeness above the altar for all to see.
Art historians studied the Christlikeness to determine its origin and age.
Visitors admired the detailed Christlikeness carved from marble in the cathedral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: portrait - a painting, drawing, or photograph of a person
