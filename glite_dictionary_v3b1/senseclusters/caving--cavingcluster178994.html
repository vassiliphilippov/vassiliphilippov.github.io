caving

noun
exploring caves
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkeɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of exploring caves for recreation or adventure

Examples

Go caving with friends
They spent the weekend caving in the limestone hills.
Many adventure seekers enjoy caving to discover underground rivers and rock formations.
Proper equipment is essential for safe caving in remote areas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mountaineering - the sport of climbing mountains
  • synonym: spelunking - the exploration of caves as a hobby
