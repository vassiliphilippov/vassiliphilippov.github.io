drop

noun
secret place for illicit goods
1.4
Pronunciation: / drɑp / (AmE), / drɒp / (BrE)
a secret place where illicit goods or items are left to be collected or distributed, or a place for delivery or exchange

Examples

A secret drop
Authorities managed to intercept several shipments at the drop before they reached their destination.
The package was left at the usual drop and picked up later that night.
The police set up surveillance on the drop suspected of being used by the gang.
Many companies use a central drop for employees to exchange important documents.
The key was left at the drop for the guests to pick up after hours.
You can leave your package at the drop and it will be shipped tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dead drop - a secure, secret spot used to pass items without direct contact
  • synonym: depot - a place for storage or distribution, often of goods or items
  • synonym: pick-up point - a designated location where people or items are collected
