resume

noun
a summary of qualifications and work history
Pronunciation: / rɛˈzuːmeɪ /; / rɪˈzuːm / (AmE), / ˈrɛzjʊmeɪ /; / rɪˈzjuːm / (BrE)
a short written summary of a person's education, qualifications, and experience, especially for a job application; or a brief summary of events or facts

Examples

A job resume
His resume lists several years of experience in project management.
Make sure your resume highlights your relevant skills and achievements.
She attached her resume to the job application for the company's review.
A brief resume of recent events was included in the newsletter.
At the end of the meeting, she gave a resume of the proposals discussed so far.
The report began with a resume of the key findings from the past year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CV - a written overview of someone's career, qualifications, and education, mainly used in the UK and academia
  • synonym: overview - a general review or summary of a subject
  • synonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
