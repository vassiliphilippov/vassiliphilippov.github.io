keen nose

noun
strong ability to smell or perceive
1.0
Pronunciation: / kin noʊz / (AmE), / kiːn nəʊz / (BrE)
an unusually strong ability to detect smells or to notice subtle details

Examples

A keen nose for clues
Detectives often rely on their keen nose for clues at a crime scene.
Her keen nose allowed her to detect the faint smell of smoke before anyone else noticed.
His keen nose made him the best perfume tester in the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keen sense of smell - heightened sensitivity to odors
