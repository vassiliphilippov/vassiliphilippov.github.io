time travel

noun
traveling to a different point in time
1.8
Pronunciation: / taɪm ˈtrævəl / (AmE), / taɪm ˈtrævəl / (BrE)
the idea or action of traveling from one point in time to another, especially to the past or future, typically found in science fiction.

Examples
