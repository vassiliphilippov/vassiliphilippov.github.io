common calamint

noun
a European mint-scented perennial plant
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈkæləmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈkæləmɪnt / (BrE)
a mint-scented perennial plant of central and southern Europe, belonging to the genus Clinopodium

Examples

Common calamint plant
Gardeners often plant common calamint for its attractive scent and flowers.
Herbal teas sometimes include leaves from the common calamint plant.
The common calamint grows wild along rocky hillsides in southern Europe.
