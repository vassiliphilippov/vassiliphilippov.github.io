nannie

noun
overprotective person
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnæni / (AmE), / ˈnæni / (BrE)
an overprotective or interfering person who tries to control others' behavior, often in a disapproving or informal way

Examples

A nannie friend
Her friends called her a nannie after she refused to let them drive in bad weather.
He acted like a nannie by constantly checking if everyone wore their seatbelts.
Nobody likes a nannie telling them what to do all the time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who is too interested in other people's affairs
  • synonym: meddler - someone who interferes in others' matters
