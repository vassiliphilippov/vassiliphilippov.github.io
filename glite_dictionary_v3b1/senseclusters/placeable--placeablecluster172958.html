placeable

adjective
suitable for assignment
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsəbəl / (BrE)
suitable or eligible to be assigned to a position, role, or location.

Examples

Placeable candidates
After completing the training, all candidates were considered placeable by the recruitment agency.
The organization works to make children in foster care more placeable by providing additional support.
Only those with the required skills are placeable in the new job openings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignable - able to be assigned to a role or position
  • antonym: unplaceable - not suitable or eligible to be assigned or placed
