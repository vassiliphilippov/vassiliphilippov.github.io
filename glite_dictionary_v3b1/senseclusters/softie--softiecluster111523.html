softie

noun
overly sentimental or weak person
7.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑːf.tiː/ /; / /ˈsɔːf.tiː/ (AmE), /ˈsɒf.tiː/ (BrE)
A person who is easily influenced by emotions and tends to be weak or overly sentimental.

Examples

A softie at heart
Don't let him fool you with his gruff voice—he's actually a softie at heart.
Everyone thinks Tom is tough, but deep down he's a real softie.
She tries to look stern, but she's a softie when it comes to stray animals.
