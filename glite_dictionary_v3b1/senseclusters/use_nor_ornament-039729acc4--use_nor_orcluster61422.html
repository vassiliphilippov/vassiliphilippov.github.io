use nor ornament

x
neither useful nor decorative
Pronunciation: / jus nɔr ˈɔrnəmənt / (AmE), / juːs nɔː ˈɔːnəmənt / (BrE)
of no practical purpose and not decorative; neither helpful nor pleasing to look at

Examples

Use nor ornament in meeting
His presence in the meeting was use nor ornament, so no one missed him when he left.
She criticized the gift as being use nor ornament—it served no purpose and looked unappealing.
They found the old table was use nor ornament and decided to throw it away.
