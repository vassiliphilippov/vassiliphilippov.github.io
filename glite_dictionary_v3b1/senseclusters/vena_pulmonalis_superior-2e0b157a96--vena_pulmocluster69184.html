vena pulmonalis superior

noun
pulmonary vein from the upper lung lobes
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə pʌl.mə.ˈneɪ.lɪs suː.ˈpɪr.i.ər/ /; / /ˈviː.nə pʌl.mə.ˈneɪ.lɪs sə.ˈpɪr.i.ər/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə pʌl.mə.ˈneɪ.lɪs suː.ˈpɪə.ri.ə/ /; / /ˈviː.nə pʌl.mə.ˈneɪ.lɪs sə.ˈpɪə.ri.ə/ (BrE)
either of the two pulmonary veins (left and right) that return blood from the superior lobes of the lungs to the left atrium of the heart

Examples

The vena pulmonalis superior

Surgeons carefully avoided damaging the vena pulmonalis superior during the procedure.
The vena pulmonalis superior drains blood from the upper lobe of each lung.
