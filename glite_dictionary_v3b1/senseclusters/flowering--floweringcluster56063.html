Synonyms

  • blooming
    1.8 0.04/m
    blooming describes a plant producing flowers over time, while flowering names the overall process of forming blossoms.
  • blossoming
    2.5 0.2/m
    blossoming shows buds opening and flowers developing on a plant, while flowering covers the whole act of producing blossoms.
  • florescence
    8.4 0.02/m
    florescence refers to a period when many flowers appear, while flowering is the common term for producing blossoms.
  • efflorescence
    9.3 0.00/m
    efflorescence names a flowering period often used in formal or poetic language, while flowering is everyday plant language.
  • inflorescence
    8.7 0.00/m
