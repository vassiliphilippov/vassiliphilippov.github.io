Geranium pratense

noun
a tall perennial herb with violet-blue flowers

Pronunciation: / dʒəˈreɪniəm præˈtɛnsi / (AmE), / dʒəˈreɪniəm praˈtɛnseɪ / (BrE)
A tall perennial plant with violet-blue flowers, commonly known as the meadow cranesbill, native to northern Europe and Asia and naturalized in North America.
