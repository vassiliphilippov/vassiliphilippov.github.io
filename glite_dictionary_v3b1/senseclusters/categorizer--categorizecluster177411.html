categorizer

noun
person who stereotypes
Pronunciation: / ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzər / (AmE), / ˈkætəɡəraɪzə / (BrE)
a person who habitually assigns people or things to specific groups, often in a simplified or stereotyped way

Examples

Strict categorizer
As a strict categorizer, she often overlooked the unique qualities of each student.
Some people dislike being labeled by a categorizer who ignores individual differences.
The manager was known as a categorizer because he grouped employees by their backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualist - a person who treats people as unique individuals
  • synonym: stereotyper - a person who makes generalizations about groups of people
