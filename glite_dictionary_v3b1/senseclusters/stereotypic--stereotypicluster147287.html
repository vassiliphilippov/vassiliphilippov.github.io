stereotypic

adjective
lacking originality or individuality
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛriəˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
lacking originality or individuality; typical in a way that is conventional or fits a stereotype

Examples

A stereotypic expression
Her speech was filled with stereotypic expressions rather than personal insights.
His interviews often sound stereotypic and rehearsed, lacking genuine spontaneity.
The movie relied on stereotypic characters instead of developing unique personalities.
The movie was criticized for its stereotypic portrayal of teenagers.
Teachers should avoid using stereotypic labels when discussing students' abilities.
Her comments reflected a stereotypic view of people from that region.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualistic - showing qualities unique to an individual
  • antonym: original - fresh and not copied; creative
  • synonym: stereotyped - lacking originality or individuality; not spontaneous
  • synonym: stereotypical - having the qualities of a stereotype
