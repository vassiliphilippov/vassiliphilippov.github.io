hood

noun
violent young criminal; thug
8.9 2.43/m
Pronunciation: / hÊŠd / (AmE), / hÊŠd / (BrE)
an aggressive and violent young criminal, often involved in street crime; a gangster or thug

Examples

A young hood
He grew up in a rough area and eventually became known as a hood among his peers.
Police arrested several hoods suspected of violent robberies downtown.
The neighborhood was worried about a group of hoods hanging out on the corner.
