finger bowl

noun
small bowl for rinsing fingers at the table
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡɚ boʊl / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə bəʊl / (BrE)
a small bowl of water placed on the dining table for rinsing the fingers between courses

Examples

A finger bowl of water
After eating the messy dessert, guests were offered a finger bowl to clean their hands.
In formal settings, a finger bowl is often used between courses involving shellfish or fruit.
The finger bowl was filled with warm water and a floating petal for elegance.
