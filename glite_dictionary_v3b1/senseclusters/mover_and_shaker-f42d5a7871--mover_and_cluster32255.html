mover and shaker

noun
influential and powerful person
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːvər ənd ˈʃeɪkər /; / ˈmuːvɚ ənd ˈʃeɪkɚ / (AmE), / ˈmuːvə ənd ˈʃeɪkə / (BrE)
an influential and energetic person who makes things happen or brings about change, especially in a particular field

Examples

Powerful movers and shakers
Many politicians want to be seen as movers and shakers who can implement real change.
She quickly became a mover and shaker in the tech industry, launching several successful startups.
The annual conference attracted movers and shakers from all corners of the business world.
As a mover and shaker in the finance world, she is always in demand at major events.
Many movers and shakers attended the gala to support the charity's cause.
The new director quickly established himself as a mover and shaker within the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influencer - a person who has the power to affect others' decisions, especially online
  • synonym: leader - one who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not have influence or is not a central figure in a field
  • synonym: power broker - a person who has influence over the decisions of others
