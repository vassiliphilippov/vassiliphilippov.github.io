cuckoo bread

noun
Eurasian woodland plant with trifoliate leaves and purple-veined white flowers
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkuː brɛd / (AmE), / ˈkʊkuː brɛd / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant with heart-shaped three-part leaves and white flowers with purple veins, commonly found in moist woodlands.

Examples

The cuckoo bread plant
