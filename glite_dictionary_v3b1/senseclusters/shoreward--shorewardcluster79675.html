shoreward

adverb
toward the shore
3.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…îrw…ôrd / (AmE), / Àà É…îÀêw…ôd / (BrE)
toward the shore or land from a body of water

Examples

Moving shoreward
The strong current carried the swimmers shoreward after the storm.
As the fog lifted, the fishermen rowed shoreward to end their day.
The birds flew shoreward as the sun began to set over the water.
