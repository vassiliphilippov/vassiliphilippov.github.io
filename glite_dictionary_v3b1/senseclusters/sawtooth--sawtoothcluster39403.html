sawtooth

noun
serration on a saw blade
9.9 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.tuːθ/ /; / /ˈsɔː.tuːθ/ (AmE), /ˈsɔː.tuːθ/ (BrE)
a tooth-shaped projection or notch, especially one of the serrations on a saw blade

Examples

A sawtooth edge
A missing sawtooth can cause the saw to snag during a cut.
He examined each sawtooth to ensure none were broken or dull.
The sawtooth design helps the blade cut through wood more efficiently.
