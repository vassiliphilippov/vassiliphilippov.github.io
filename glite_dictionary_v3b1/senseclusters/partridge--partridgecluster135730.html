partridge

noun
a small Old World game bird
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑr.trɪdʒ /; / ˈpɑːrtrɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɑː.trɪdʒ /; / ˈpɑːtrɪdʒ / (BrE)
a medium-sized, plump game bird with a short tail, commonly found in Europe, Asia, North America, and North Africa, often hunted for sport or food
