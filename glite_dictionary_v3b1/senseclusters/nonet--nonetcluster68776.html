nonet

noun
nine-line poem
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊnɛt / (AmE), / ˈnɒnɛt / (BrE)
a poem consisting of nine lines, often following a specific syllable or line pattern

Examples

A poem nonet
The poet challenged herself to write a nonet about the changing seasons.
In class, we learned how to compose a nonet with a descending syllable count.
He submitted his nonet to the literary magazine for publication.

Related Terms
