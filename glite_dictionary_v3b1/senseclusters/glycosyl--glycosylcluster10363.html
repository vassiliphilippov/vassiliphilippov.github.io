glycosyl

adjective
relating to sugar group
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlaɪkəˌsɪl / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌɪkəsɪl / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or involving a glycosyl group, which is a sugar residue in a molecule

Examples

Glycosyl modification
The scientist analyzed the glycosyl modifications present on the protein surface.
Certain diseases are linked to abnormal glycosyl patterns in cell membranes.
The glycosyl composition of antibodies can affect their function in the immune system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glycosidic - relating to or involving a glycoside or its formation
  • antonym: non-glycosylated - not containing or involving a glycosyl group
