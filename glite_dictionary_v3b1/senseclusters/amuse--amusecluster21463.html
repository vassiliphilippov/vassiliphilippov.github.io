amuse

verb
make someone laugh or entertain
Pronunciation: / əˈmjuz /; / əˈmjuːz / (AmE), / əˈmjuːz / (BrE)
to make someone laugh, smile, or feel entertained; to occupy someone in an enjoyable way

Examples

To amuse the crowd
Her clever jokes amuse everyone at the dinner table.
I always try to amuse my friends with funny stories.
The clown managed to amuse the children with his silly tricks.
He found ways to amuse himself while waiting for the train.
She brought a puzzle to amuse her children during the long car ride.
The comedian's quick wit amused the audience throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bore - to make someone feel uninterested or weary
  • synonym: entertain - to provide amusement or enjoyment
