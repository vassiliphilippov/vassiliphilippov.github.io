tungsten steel

noun
hard, heat-resistant steel alloy with tungsten
5.8 0.01/m

a very hard, heat-resistant steel alloy containing tungsten, used especially for cutting tools and machinery.

Examples

Tungsten steel alloy
Engineers selected tungsten steel for the new machinery due to its superior hardness.
Industrial cutting blades are often made from tungsten steel to ensure durability and strength.
The drill bits constructed from tungsten steel can withstand exceptionally high temperatures.
