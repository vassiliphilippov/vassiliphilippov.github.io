legal profession

noun
group of people qualified to practice law
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl prəˈfɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl prəˈfɛʃən / (BrE)
the group of people who are qualified and licensed to practice law in a particular country or area

Examples

The legal profession group
After graduating from law school, she joined the legal profession and began practicing as a solicitor.
Many people enter the legal profession because they want to uphold justice.
The legal profession is governed by strict ethical standards in most countries.
