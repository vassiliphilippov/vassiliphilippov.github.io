megacycle per second

noun

A frequency unit equal to one million cycles or periods per second, commonly used to measure electromagnetic wave frequencies; equivalent to one megahertz.

Examples

Megacycle per second unit
Older equipment manuals often refer to frequencies in megacycles per second instead of megahertz.
Scientists measured the electromagnetic wave as oscillating at several megacycles per second.
The radio station broadcasts at a frequency of 100 megacycles per second.
