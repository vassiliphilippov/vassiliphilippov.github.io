bronchogenic

adjective
from the bronchi
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑːŋkəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrɒŋkəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
originating in or related to the bronchi, the main air passages of the lungs

Examples

Bronchogenic carcinoma
The patient was diagnosed with a bronchogenic carcinoma after a series of tests.
Doctors are studying the causes of bronchogenic tumors in non-smokers.
A bronchogenic cyst was discovered during the routine chest scan.

  • synonym: pulmonary - relating to the lungs
