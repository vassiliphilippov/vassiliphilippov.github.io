Synonyms

  • stone dead
    5.1 0.03/m
    stone dead is completely lifeless or non-functional, while dead is the general word for no longer alive or working.
  • deceased
    3.5 0.03/m
    deceased is used politely for a person who is dead, while dead is more general and applies to people, animals, or things.
  • lifeless
    3.4 2.23/m
    lifeless looks empty or without energy, while dead means no longer alive or working in a direct, physical way.
  • nonliving
    3.9 0.09/m
    nonliving describes things that never had life or now lack life, while dead is used when life ends.
