leakage

noun
unauthorized disclosure of data
4.6 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàliÀêk…™d í / (AmE), / ÀàliÀêk…™d í / (BrE)
the unauthorized or accidental disclosure of confidential or private information, especially in digital, cybersecurity, or data contexts.

Examples

Data leakage
The latest security audit revealed a data leakage affecting several clients.
The company paid fines after a leakage exposed customers' credit card numbers.
Researchers warned of possible model leakage that could reveal sensitive training data.

Synonyms

  • data leak
    4.6 0.00/m
    a data leak exposes sensitive digital information to outsiders, while leakage is any unauthorized or accidental disclosure of private data.
  • spillage
    6.8 0.00/m
    spillage refers to accidental disclosure of sensitive or classified information in security contexts, while leakage covers any unauthorized disclosure.
  • leak
    4.8 6.76/m
    leak is the act of making secret information public, often deliberate or political, while leakage means unauthorized or accidental disclosure.

Related Terms
