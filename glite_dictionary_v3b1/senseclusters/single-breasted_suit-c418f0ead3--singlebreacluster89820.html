single-breasted suit

noun
suit with a single-breasted jacket
9.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ˈbrest.ɪd suːt/ /; / /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ˈbrest.ɪd sjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ˈbrest.ɪd sjuːt/ /; / /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ˈbrest.ɪd suːt/ (BrE)
A suit in which the jacket fastens with a single row of buttons and has no overlapping of fabric at the front.

Examples

The store offers both double-breasted and single-breasted suits to match different styles.
