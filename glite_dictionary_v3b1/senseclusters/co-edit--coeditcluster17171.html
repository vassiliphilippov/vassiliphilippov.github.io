co-edit

verb
to edit jointly
3.8
Pronunciation: /koʊ.ˈed.ət/ /; / /koʊ.ˈed.ɪt/ (AmE), /kəʊ.ˈed.ət/ /; / /kəʊ.ˈed.ɪt/ (BrE)

Examples

The two professors co-edited a textbook used in universities worldwide.
