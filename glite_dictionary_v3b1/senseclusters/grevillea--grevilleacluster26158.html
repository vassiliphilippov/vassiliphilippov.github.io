grevillea

noun
type of shrub or tree from the genus Grevillea
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈvɪliə / (AmE), / ɡrəˈvɪliə / (BrE)
Any shrub or tree of the genus Grevillea, native mainly to Australia, known for its diverse flowers and foliage.
