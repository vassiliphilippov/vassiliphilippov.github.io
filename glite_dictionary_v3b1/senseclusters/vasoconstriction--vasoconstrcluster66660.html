vasoconstriction

noun
narrowing of blood vessels
6.2 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌvæsoʊkənˈstrɪkʃən / (AmE), / ˌvæsəʊkənˈstrɪkʃən / (BrE)
the narrowing of blood vessels resulting from contraction of the muscular wall of the vessels, which reduces blood flow

Examples

Vasoconstriction of vessels
During cold weather, vasoconstriction helps reduce heat loss from the body.
Excessive vasoconstriction can contribute to medical conditions like hypertension.
The drug caused vasoconstriction, leading to an increase in blood pressure.
