oliphant

noun
medieval hunting horn
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊlɪfənt / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪfənt / (BrE)
a large medieval hunting horn made from an elephant's tusk

Examples

Blow an oliphant
The knight raised his oliphant and blew a loud call across the valley.
In medieval times, an oliphant was a prized possession among noble hunters.
The museum displayed a beautifully carved oliphant from the twelfth century.
