vocational school

noun
school for trade or occupational training
3.0 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / vo äÀàke…™ É…ôn…ôl skuÀêl / (AmE), / v…ô äÀàke…™ É…ôn…ôl skuÀêl / (BrE)
A secondary or post-secondary school where students learn the skills needed for a particular trade or occupation.

Examples

A vocational school
After graduating from vocational school, she found a job as a mechanic.
He enrolled in a vocational school to become an electrician.
Many students choose a vocational school instead of a traditional high school.

Synonyms

  • trade school
    3.0 0.64/m
    a trade school teaches specific manual or skilled jobs like plumbing or carpentry, while a vocational school covers trades and wider career training.
  • technical school
    3.2 0.34/m
    a technical school focuses on mechanical or applied sciences and technology, while a vocational school trains for particular jobs and trades.
  • training school
    3.1 0.09/m
    a training school gives short, practical instruction for skills or jobs, while a vocational school offers full programs for a specific trade or occupation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trade school - a school offering instruction in skilled trades
