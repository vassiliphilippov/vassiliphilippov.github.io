floodable

adjective
can be flooded
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌdəbəl / (AmE), / ˈflʌdəbəl / (BrE)
able to be flooded or designed to allow flooding, especially intentionally or in emergencies

Examples

A floodable area
The lower deck is floodable in case of emergency.
Some agricultural fields are intentionally made floodable to support certain crops.
The engineers designed the compartment to be floodable for safety reasons.
