business relationship

noun
formal commercial arrangement
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪz.nəs rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nɪs rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ˈbɪz.nɪs rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ /; / /ˈbɪz.nəs rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
a formal arrangement between two or more parties to provide regular professional or commercial services, often governed by a contract

Examples

They decided to formalize their business relationship with a written contract.
Trust is essential in any successful business relationship.
