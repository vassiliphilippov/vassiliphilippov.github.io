Angus Og

proper noun
Celtic god of love and beauty
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəs ɒɡ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡəs ɒɡ / (BrE)
In Celtic mythology, the god of love and beauty; patron deity of young men and women.

Examples

Angus Og in mythology
Art depicting Angus Og often shows him playing a harp surrounded by birds.
Legends say that Angus Og could make lovers invisible to all but each other.
The story of Angus Og is central to many Irish myths about love and youth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aengus - Celtic god associated with love, youth, and poetry
