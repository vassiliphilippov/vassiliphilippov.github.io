in fashion

adjective
popular or fashionable at the moment
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈfæʃ.ən / (AmE), / ɪn ˈfæʃ.ən / (BrE)
popular and considered stylish at a particular time; currently fashionable

Examples

In fashion now
Bright colors were in fashion throughout the 1980s.
Long skirts are in fashion this season.
She always tries to wear whatever is in fashion at the moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: out of fashion - no longer popular or fashionable
  • synonym: trendy - very fashionable or up to date
