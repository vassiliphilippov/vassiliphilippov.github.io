memorial

noun
formal recognition of service
5.0
Pronunciation: / məˈmɔriəl / (AmE), / mɪˈmɔːriəl / (BrE)
an official recognition or statement in honor of meritorious service, especially in a formal context

Examples

Official memorial
A special memorial was issued to honor her contributions to the company.
He received a memorial from the committee, praising his meritorious achievements.
The organization awarded him a memorial in recognition of his years of dedicated service.
