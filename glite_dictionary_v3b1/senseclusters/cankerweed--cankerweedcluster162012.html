cankerweed

noun
herb with drooping yellowish-white flowers
6.3 9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkæŋkərˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈkaŋkəˌwiːd / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant native to northeastern North America, with drooping clusters of yellowish-white flowers; sometimes classified in the genus Prenanthes.

Examples
