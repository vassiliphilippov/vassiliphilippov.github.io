cascading menu

noun
secondary menu in computer interfaces
2.2 4.2
Pronunciation: /kæ.ˈskeɪ.dɪŋ ˈmen.juː/ (AmE), /kæ.ˈskeɪ.dɪŋ ˈmen.juː/ (BrE)
