crumb

noun
a very small amount
6.3
Pronunciation: / krʌm / (AmE), / krʌm / (BrE)
a very small amount of something, especially used figuratively

Examples

A crumb of hope
She managed to hold onto a crumb of hope despite the bad news.
The employees were grateful for even a crumb of praise from their strict boss.
We don't have a crumb of evidence to support that claim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrap - a small piece or amount of something, especially left over after the greater part has been used
