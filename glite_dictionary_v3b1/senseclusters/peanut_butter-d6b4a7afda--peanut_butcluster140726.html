peanut butter

noun
spread made from ground peanuts
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːnʌt ˌbʌtər / (AmE), / ˈpiːnʌt ˌbʌtə / (BrE)
A smooth or crunchy spread made from ground roasted peanuts, commonly used on bread or in baking.

Examples

Peanut butter spread
I like to spread peanut butter on toast in the morning.
She made a sandwich with peanut butter and jelly for lunch.
The recipe calls for two tablespoons of peanut butter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almond butter - a spread made from ground almonds
  • synonym: jam - sweet fruit spread often paired with peanut butter
