Rhine River

proper noun
major river in western and central Europe
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪn ˌrɪvər / (AmE), / ˈraɪn ˌrɪvə / (BrE)
a major river in western and central Europe, rising in the Swiss Alps and flowing north through Germany and the Netherlands to the North Sea

Examples

The Rhine River banks
Many picturesque castles can be found along the banks of the Rhine River.
Shipping traffic on the Rhine River is vital to European commerce.
The Rhine River forms part of the border between France and Germany.
