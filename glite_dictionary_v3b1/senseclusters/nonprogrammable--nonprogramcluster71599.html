nonprogrammable

adjective
not able to be scheduled
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈproʊɡræməbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprəʊɡræməbəl / (BrE)
not able to be programmed or set to operate automatically or perform different functions

Examples

A nonprogrammable thermostat
This thermostat is nonprogrammable, so you have to adjust the temperature manually.
Because the coffee maker is nonprogrammable, it cannot start brewing automatically in the morning.
Older models are often nonprogrammable and lack advanced scheduling features.
The school requires students to use nonprogrammable calculators during exams.
A nonprogrammable thermostat can only be adjusted manually for temperature changes.
He bought a nonprogrammable remote that works with only one device.
