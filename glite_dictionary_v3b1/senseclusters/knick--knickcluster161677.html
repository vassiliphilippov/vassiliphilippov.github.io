knick

noun
small surface cut
Pronunciation: / nɪk / (AmE), / nɪk / (BrE)
a small cut, notch, or indentation made on a surface, often accidentally

Examples

A knick on the table
He noticed a small knick on the blade after using the knife.
The wooden desk had a knick near the corner from being bumped.
Be careful not to make a knick in the paint while moving the chair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dent - a slight hollow in a surface made by pressure or a blow
  • synonym: nick - a small cut or notch on a surface
  • synonym: scratch - a thin, shallow mark or cut on a surface
