stuporous

adjective
in a dazed, semi-conscious state
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːpərəs / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpərəs / (BrE)
in a state of partial unconsciousness or insensibility; stunned, confused, and slow to react, often due to illness, exhaustion, or intoxication

Examples

Stuporous state after accident
After the accident, he was stuporous and unable to answer simple questions.
The exhausted runner became stuporous and needed immediate medical attention.
The patient remained stuporous despite the doctor's efforts to wake him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - quick to notice things and respond
  • synonym: lethargic - sluggish and lacking energy or enthusiasm
