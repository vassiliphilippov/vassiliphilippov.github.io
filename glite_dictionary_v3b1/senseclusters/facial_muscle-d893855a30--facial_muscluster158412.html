facial muscle

noun
skeletal muscle of the face
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ˈmʌsəl / (BrE)
any of the skeletal muscles located in the face, responsible for facial expressions and movements

Examples

Facial muscle movement
Damage to a facial muscle can affect your ability to make expressions.
She felt her facial muscles tense up during the stressful meeting.
The facial muscle contracted as he smiled widely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: muscle - tissue in the body that produces movement or maintains position
