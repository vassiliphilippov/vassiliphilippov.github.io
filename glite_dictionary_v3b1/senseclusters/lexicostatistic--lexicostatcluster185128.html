lexicostatistic

adjective
related to statistical analysis of language relationships
5.8
Pronunciation: / lɛksɪkoʊstəˈstætɪk / (AmE), / lɛksɪkəʊstəˈstætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving the statistical study of the relationships between languages based on their vocabularies.

Examples

Lexicostatistic study methods
Recent studies have employed lexicostatistic methods to trace the evolution of language families.
The team presented their lexicostatistic findings at the international linguistics conference.
Using lexicostatistic techniques, researchers compared basic vocabularies across several languages.
