blush

noun
reddening of the face from emotion
1.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /blʌʃ/ (AmE), /blʌʃ/ (BrE)
a red or pink color in the cheeks, especially from emotion, embarrassment, or good health

Examples

A blush of embarrassment
A deep blush spread across his cheeks as he realized his mistake.
Her face was covered with a blush when he complimented her in front of everyone.
She tried to hide her blush by turning away.
A warm blush rose to her cheeks as she walked in the crisp morning air.
His skin had the natural blush of youth and good health.
The child’s face was lit up with a healthy blush after playing outside all day.
