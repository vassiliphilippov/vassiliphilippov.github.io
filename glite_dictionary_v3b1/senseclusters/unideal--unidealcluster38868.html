unideal

adjective
not perfect
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.aɪˈdiːəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.aɪˈdiːəl / (BrE)
not perfect or not the best possible in a situation or condition

Examples

An unideal situation
The weather was unideal for a picnic, but we went anyway.
It is an unideal solution, but it is the only one available right now.
Sharing a room with three siblings can be unideal for studying.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ideal - perfect or most suitable
  • synonym: imperfect - not perfect; having faults or errors
  • synonym: suboptimal - not the best possible; below the optimal level
