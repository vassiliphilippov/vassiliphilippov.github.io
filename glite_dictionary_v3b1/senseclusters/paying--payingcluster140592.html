paying

adjective
profitable; yielding a profit
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of a job or activity) giving financial reward, salary, or profit

Examples

A paying job

It was not a paying business, so they decided to close it down.
The new venture proved to be a highly paying enterprise.
He switched careers in search of more paying opportunities.
Many artists struggle before finding paying work in their field.
She found a paying job after months of searching.
