psychic trauma

noun
lasting emotional wound or shock
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪkɪk ˈtrɔmə / (AmE), / ˈsaɪkɪk ˈtrɔːmə / (BrE)
A deeply distressing or disturbing emotional experience that leaves a lasting psychological impact.

Examples

Psychic trauma after combat
Many soldiers experience psychic trauma after returning from combat.
The psychic trauma caused by the accident affected her for years afterwards.
The therapy sessions helped him begin to heal the psychic trauma of his childhood.
