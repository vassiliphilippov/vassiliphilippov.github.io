frijoles refritos

noun
Mexican fried mashed beans dish
Pronunciation: / friˈxoʊleɪz rɛˈfritoʊz / (AmE), / friˈxəʊleɪz rɛˈfriːtəʊz / (BrE)
A Mexican dish of cooked, mashed beans that are fried, usually with lard and seasonings.

Examples

frijoles refritos Mexican dish
