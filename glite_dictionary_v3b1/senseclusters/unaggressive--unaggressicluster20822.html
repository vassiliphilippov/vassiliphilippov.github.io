unaggressive

adjective
not aggressive or forceful
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈɡrɛs.ɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈɡrɛs.ɪv / (BrE)
not aggressive; not inclined to fight or assert oneself forcefully

Examples

An unaggressive dog
Despite his large size, the dog was surprisingly unaggressive with strangers.
Her unaggressive demeanor helped to ease tensions during the meeting.
In negotiations, being unaggressive can sometimes lead to better outcomes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aggressive - ready or likely to attack or confront; assertive
  • synonym: passive - accepting or allowing what happens without active response
