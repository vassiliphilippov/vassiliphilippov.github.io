syllable

noun
unit of sound in a word
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪləbəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪləbəl / (BrE)
A single unit of sound in a word, typically containing a vowel sound and forming part or all of a word.

Examples

A syllable sound
She divided the word into syllables to help her spell it correctly.
The word 'banana' has three syllables.
Try clapping your hands once for each syllable in the word.
