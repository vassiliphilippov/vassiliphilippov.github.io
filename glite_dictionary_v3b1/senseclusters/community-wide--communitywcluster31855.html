community-wide

adjective
involving whole community
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjunətiˈwaɪd / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪtiˈwaɪd / (BrE)
affecting or involving all the people or groups in a community

Examples

A community-wide program
The mayor announced a community-wide recycling program to reduce waste.
A community-wide survey was conducted to gather opinions on the new park design.
There was a community-wide celebration after the local team won the championship.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citywide - affecting or involving an entire city
  • synonym: nationwide - affecting or involving an entire nation
