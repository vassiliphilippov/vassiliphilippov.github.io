pledge

noun
member-in-waiting for a group or club
5.1
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
a person who has been accepted for membership in a club, fraternity, or similar group but has not yet been formally admitted

Examples

A new pledge
As a pledge, she had to complete certain tasks before becoming a full member.
He remained a pledge for several weeks before finally being initiated.
The fraternity welcomed each new pledge with a special ceremony.
