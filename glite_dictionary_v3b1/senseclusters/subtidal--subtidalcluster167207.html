subtidal

adjective
below low-tide mark
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈtaɪdəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈtaɪdəl / (BrE)
located or occurring below the low-tide mark and always underwater

Examples

Subtidal zone
Many species of fish live in the subtidal areas along the coast.
Researchers collected samples from the subtidal zone to study marine biodiversity.
The subtidal region is always underwater, even during the lowest tides.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intertidal - located between the high and low tide marks
  • synonym: submarine - existing or occurring under the sea
