nothing less than

x
at least; minimum required or expected
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ lɛs ðæn / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ lɛs ðan / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is exactly what is stated or is the very least that is expected or required.

Examples

Nothing less than total commitment
For this job, we require nothing less than total commitment.
She demanded nothing less than complete honesty from her colleagues.
Winning the championship was nothing less than a dream come true for the team.
