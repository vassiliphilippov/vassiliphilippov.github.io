culpeo

noun
South American wild canid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊlpeɪoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkʊlpeɪəʊ / (BrE)
a South American wild canid, also called the Andean fox, found in mountainous regions

Examples

The culpeo fox
The culpeo is native to the mountainous regions of South America.
Farmers sometimes spot a culpeo hunting for small animals near their fields.
Unlike true foxes, the culpeo is more closely related to wolves and jackals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fox - a small to medium-sized wild canid with a bushy tail
  • synonym: wolf - a large wild canid known for living and hunting in packs
