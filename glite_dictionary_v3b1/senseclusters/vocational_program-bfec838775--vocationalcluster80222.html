vocational program

noun
educational course for specific trades
Pronunciation: / voʊˈkeɪʃənl ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / vəʊˈkeɪʃənl ˈprəʊɡræm / (BrE)
an organized course of study that prepares participants for a specific trade, occupation, or vocation rather than academic or theoretical subjects

Examples

A vocational program course
Many high schools now offer a vocational program alongside traditional academic courses.
She enrolled in a vocational program to become a certified electrician.
The vocational program provides students with hands-on experience in culinary arts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic program - a program of study focused on academic or theoretical subjects
  • synonym: apprenticeship - a system of training a new generation of practitioners of a trade or profession
