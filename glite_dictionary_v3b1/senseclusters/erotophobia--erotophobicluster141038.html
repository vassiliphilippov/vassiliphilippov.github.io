erotophobia

noun
fear of sexuality
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.rə.tə.ˈfoʊ.bi.ə/ /; / /ə.rə.tə.ˈfoʊ.bi.ə/ (AmE), /ɪ.rə.tə.ˈfəʊ.bi.ə/ /; / /ə.rə.tə.ˈfəʊ.bi.ə/ (BrE)
a fear, strong aversion, or discomfort toward sexuality, sexual feelings, or discussion of sexual topics

Examples

Erotophobia in society
Cultural attitudes can contribute to erotophobia in some societies.
His erotophobia made it difficult for him to discuss topics related to sexuality.
Therapists sometimes help clients overcome erotophobia through counseling and education.
The country's widespread erotophobia has made comprehensive sex education difficult to implement.
Her parents' erotophobia meant that topics about relationships were never discussed at home.
Overcoming erotophobia is important for promoting healthy attitudes toward sexuality.
