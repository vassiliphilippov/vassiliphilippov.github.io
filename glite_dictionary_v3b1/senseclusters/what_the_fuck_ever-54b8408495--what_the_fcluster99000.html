what the fuck ever

interjection
vulgar expression of indifference or dismissal
Pronunciation: / wət ðə fʌk ˈɛv.ɚ / (AmE), / wɒt ðə fʌk ˈɛv.ə / (BrE)
Used as a vulgar expression of strong indifference or dismissal, indicating that one no longer cares what happens or what someone does.

Examples

What the fuck ever you say
She rolled her eyes and said, what the fuck ever, before leaving the room.
When they changed the plans without asking me, I thought, what the fuck ever.
You don't care about the rules? Fine, what the fuck ever.
