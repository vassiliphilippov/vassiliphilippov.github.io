not just you

x
others also feel the same
2.4
Pronunciation: / nɑt dʒʌst ju / (AmE), / nɒt dʒʌst juː / (BrE)
used to indicate that others also share the same feelings, experiences, or problems as the person addressed

Examples

Not just you feel tired
During the long winter, feeling tired all the time is not just you.
If you find these instructions confusing, it's not just you; many people have the same problem.
She sometimes feels overwhelmed at work, but remember, it's not just you who struggles with stress.
