turn on the heat

verb
activate a heating system
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɝn ɑn ðə hit / (AmE), / tɜːn ɒn ðə hiːt / (BrE)
to activate the heating system in a building or vehicle

Examples

To turn on the heat system

He forgot to turn on the heat before the guests arrived, so the room was chilly.
We usually turn on the heat in early November when the nights get cold.
