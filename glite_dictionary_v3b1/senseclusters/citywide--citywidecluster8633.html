citywide

adjective
throughout the whole city
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪtiˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪtiˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Happening or applying throughout the whole city.

Examples

Citywide curfew
The mayor announced a citywide curfew due to the emergency.
The new recycling program will be implemented citywide starting next month.
There was a citywide celebration after the team won the championship.
