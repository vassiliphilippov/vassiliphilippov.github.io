chauvinistic remark

noun
sexist or prejudiced comment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃoʊˈvɪnɪstɪk rɪˈmɑːrk / (AmE), / ʃəʊˈvɪnɪstɪk rɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
a comment expressing excessive or prejudiced loyalty or support for one's own gender, often showing bias or discrimination against others

Examples

Chauvinistic remark about women
After making a chauvinistic remark, he quickly realized he had crossed the line.
His chauvinistic remark made everyone uncomfortable during the meeting.
She was offended by the chauvinistic remark he made about women drivers.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: offensive remark - a comment likely to insult or upset others
  • synonym: sexist comment - a comment showing discrimination based on gender
