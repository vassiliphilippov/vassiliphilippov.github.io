booby-hatch

noun
slang for mental institution
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːbi ˌhætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbuːbi ˌhatʃ / (BrE)
slang for a mental institution or a place considered to be crazy or chaotic

Examples

The booby-hatch ward
After his bizarre outburst in the meeting, some joked he was heading straight for the booby-hatch.
Family members whispered that Uncle Joe had once spent time in the booby-hatch.
The party was so chaotic it felt like we'd wandered into a booby-hatch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insane asylum - older term for a hospital for people with mental illnesses
  • synonym: madhouse - another slang term for a mental institution
