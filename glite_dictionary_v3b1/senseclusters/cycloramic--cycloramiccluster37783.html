cycloramic

adjective
full-circle panoramic
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkləˈræmɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkləˈræmɪk / (BrE)
relating to a panoramic view or image that covers a full 360-degree circle

Examples

Cycloramic view
The artist created a cycloramic painting that allowed viewers to see the entire landscape at once.
Visitors enjoyed the cycloramic view from the observation deck, which showed the city in every direction.
A cycloramic photograph was used to capture the full circle of the mountain range.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panoramic - showing a wide, unbroken view of an area
