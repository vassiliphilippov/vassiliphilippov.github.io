day game

noun
dating strategy during daytime
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪ ˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈdeɪ ˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
An informal strategy of meeting or approaching potential romantic partners during daytime in public, casual places (for example, parks, shops, or cafés).

Examples

Day game flirting
She learned day game techniques to start friendly conversations while visiting local cafés.
After weeks of practice, he relied on day game to meet people politely during lunch breaks.
Their day games usually involved approaching strangers at bookstores and chatting about books.
