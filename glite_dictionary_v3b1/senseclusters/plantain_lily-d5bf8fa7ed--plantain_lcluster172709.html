plantain lily

noun
ornamental perennial plant (Hosta genus)
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈplæn.tɪn ˌlɪl.i / (AmE), / ˈplan.tɪn ˌlɪl.i / (BrE)
A perennial plant of the genus Hosta, known for its broad, ribbed leaves and clusters of white, blue, or lilac flowers, often grown as ground cover in gardens.
