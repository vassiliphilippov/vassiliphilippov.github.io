advance

noun
romantic or sexual proposal or approach
5.8
Pronunciation: / ədˈvæns / (AmE), / ədˈvɑːns / (BrE)
an attempt or suggestion, especially one made to express romantic or sexual interest, designed to elicit a reaction from another person

Examples

Romantic advances
Despite his repeated advances, she made it clear she was not interested.
Many employees find unwanted advances in the workplace uncomfortable and inappropriate.
She rejected his advances and asked him to leave her alone.

Related Terms
