photocatalysis

noun
light-driven reaction
8.8
Pronunciation: /foʊ.tə.kə.ˈtæl.ə.sɪs/ /; / /foʊ.toʊ.kə.ˈtæl.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /fəʊ.təʊ.kə.ˈtæl.ɪ.sɪs/ (BrE)

Examples
