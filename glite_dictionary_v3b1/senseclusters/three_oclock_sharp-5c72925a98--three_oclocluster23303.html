three o'clock sharp

adverb
exactly at 3:00
Pronunciation: / θri əˈklɑk ʃɑrp / (AmE), / θri əˈklɒk ʃɑːp / (BrE)

Examples

The train is scheduled to depart at three o'clock sharp every afternoon.
