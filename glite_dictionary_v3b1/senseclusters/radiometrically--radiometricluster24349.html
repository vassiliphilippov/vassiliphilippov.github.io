radiometrically

adverb
by measuring radiation
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪdiəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌreɪdiəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the measurement of radiation, especially for scientific analysis

Examples

To date radiometrically
The fossils were dated radiometrically to establish their age with greater accuracy.
Scientists analyzed the soil samples radiometrically to detect traces of radioactive elements.
The age of the volcanic rocks was determined radiometrically using advanced laboratory equipment.
