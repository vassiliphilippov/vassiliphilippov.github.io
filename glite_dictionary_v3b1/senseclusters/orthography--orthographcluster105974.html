orthography

noun
a language's spelling system
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the conventional spelling system or method of representing the sounds of a language with written or printed symbols

Examples

English orthography system
Reformers have debated whether English orthography should be simplified.
Students learned the orthography of Old French in their linguistics class.
The Japanese orthography is especially complex due to multiple scripts.
