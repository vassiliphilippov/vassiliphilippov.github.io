Forficulidae

noun
family of typical earwigs
Pronunciation: / fɔrˈfɪkjʊlɪˌdi /; / fɔrˌfɪkjʊˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / fɔːˈfɪkjʊlɪˌdiː /; / fɔːˌfɪkjʊˈlɪdɪˌiː / (BrE)
a family of insects known as typical earwigs, with elongated bodies and forceps-like pincers at the end of their abdomen
