labor force

noun
people available for work
2.2 6.80/m
Pronunciation: / Ààle…™.b…ö f…îrs / (AmE), / Ààle…™.b…ô f…îÀês / (BrE)
all the people in a country or area who are able and available to work, including both the employed and those seeking employment.

Examples

The labor force grows
Technological change is reshaping the global labor force.
The country's labor force is expected to grow by 2% next year.
Women now make up a significant portion of the labor force.

Synonyms

  • workforce
    2.4 23/m
    workforce is the people employed or able to work in a company or country, while labor force counts both employed and people seeking jobs.
  • manpower
    3.7 4.17/m
    manpower refers to the number of people available or employed for work in an organization, while labor force includes all who can or seek work.
