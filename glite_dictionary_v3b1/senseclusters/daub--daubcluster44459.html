daub

noun
material for coating or plastering walls
8.3
Pronunciation: / dɔb / (AmE), / dɔːb / (BrE)
a soft, sticky material, often a mixture of clay, sand, straw, and water, used for coating or plastering walls

Examples

Wall daub mixture
Over time, the daub between the wooden beams started to crack and fall away.
The builders applied a thick layer of daub to insulate the walls of the cottage.
Traditional houses were constructed using timber frames filled with daub.
