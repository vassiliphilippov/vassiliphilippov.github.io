at work

adverb
present at one's place of employment
Pronunciation: / æt ˈwɝk / (AmE), / ət ˈwɜːk / (BrE)
present at one's place of employment or engaged in one's job duties.

Examples

To be at work
He called to say he'd be at work late tonight.
Please do not disturb employees while they are at work.
She was already at work by the time her colleagues arrived.
