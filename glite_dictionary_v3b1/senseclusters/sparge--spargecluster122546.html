sparge

verb
introduce air or gas to agitate a liquid
Pronunciation: / spɑrdʒ / (AmE), / spɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to agitate or aerate a liquid by introducing air or a gas, especially in industrial, scientific, or brewing processes

Examples

To sparge the mash
During the brewing process, they sparge the mash to extract more sugars.
The technician began to sparge the solution with nitrogen to remove impurities.
To improve oxygen levels in the fish tank, simply sparge the water with air.
