titillating

adjective
pleasantly exciting or stimulating
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪtəˌleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪtɪleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
interesting or exciting in a way that is pleasant but often superficial, sometimes especially by being mildly sexual or provocative.

Examples

Titillating details
His presentation included some titillating details to keep the audience engaged.
She found the jokes more titillating than actually funny.
The magazine published a titillating story that drew a lot of attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking interest or excitement
  • synonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
