buggy

noun
baby carriage
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡi / (AmE), / ˈbʌɡi / (BrE)
a small vehicle with wheels, especially one used to carry a baby or young child

Examples

A baby buggy
She pushed the buggy along the sidewalk while her baby slept.
The family folded the buggy to fit it into the car trunk.
He bought a new buggy for his daughter before their holiday trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pram - a baby carriage, especially in British English
  • synonym: stroller - a small folding chair on wheels for pushing a baby
