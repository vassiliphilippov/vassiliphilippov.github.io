morpho

x
combining form: form
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrfoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːfəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form from Greek meaning 'form' or 'shape', used in scientific and technical terms

Examples

Morpho- words
The prefix morpho- is used in words like 'morphology' and 'morphogenesis'.
In biology, morpho- often refers to the shape or structure of organisms.
Many scientific terms use morpho- to indicate something related to form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morphology - the study of the form and structure of things
