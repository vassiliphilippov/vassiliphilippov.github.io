stuck in a stovepipe

adjective
trapped in bureaucratic process
9.5
Pronunciation: / stʌk ɪn ə ˈstoʊvpaɪp / (AmE), / stʌk ɪn ə ˈstəʊvpaɪp / (BrE)
trapped or delayed within a rigid or compartmentalized bureaucratic process, often causing progress to halt or slow significantly

Examples

Stuck in a stovepipe process
New ideas often get stuck in a stovepipe when departments refuse to share information.
Our funding proposal is currently stuck in a stovepipe somewhere in the administration.
The report became stuck in a stovepipe, holding up the approval process for weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: red tape - bureaucratic rules or procedures causing delay
  • synonym: stovepiping - the flow of information through rigid, isolated channels in an organization
