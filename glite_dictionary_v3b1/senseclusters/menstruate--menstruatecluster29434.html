menstruate

verb
to have a menstrual period
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnstruˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛnstrueɪt / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) to discharge blood and other material from the lining of the uterus as part of the menstrual cycle.

Examples

To menstruate monthly
Many women menstruate every 28 days, but cycles can vary.
She began to menstruate when she was twelve years old.
Some animals also menstruate, though it is much less common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ovulate - to produce or discharge an egg from an ovary
