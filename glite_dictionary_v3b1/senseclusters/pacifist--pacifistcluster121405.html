pacifist

adjective
opposed to war or violence
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪfɪst / (AmE), / ˈpæsɪfɪst / (BrE)
opposed to war or violence; favoring peaceful solutions.

Examples

Pacifist views
Even in the face of aggression, he maintained a pacifist stance.
Her pacifist beliefs led her to protest against military action.
The country's new leader is known for his pacifist policies and commitment to diplomacy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: militaristic - favoring war or military solutions
  • synonym: peaceful - favoring peace; not violent or warlike
