lama

noun
a Tibetan Buddhist monk or priest
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈlɑːmə / (BrE)
a Buddhist monk or priest, especially in Tibet or Mongolia, often regarded as a spiritual teacher

Examples

A lama monk
A lama performed the ceremony at the monastery.
Many travelers visit Tibet to seek guidance from a lama.
The Dalai lama is revered by Buddhists around the world.
