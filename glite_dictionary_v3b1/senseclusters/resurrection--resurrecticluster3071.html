resurrection

noun
return to life
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of coming back to life after death, especially in a religious or spiritual context

Examples

The resurrection of Jesus
Many religions teach about the resurrection of important figures after their death.
The story describes the miraculous resurrection of the hero who was thought to be lost forever.
Belief in the resurrection gives hope to many people facing difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the end of life; the state of being dead
  • synonym: rebirth - a new period of growth or activity; being born again
  • synonym: revival - a process of becoming active or popular again
