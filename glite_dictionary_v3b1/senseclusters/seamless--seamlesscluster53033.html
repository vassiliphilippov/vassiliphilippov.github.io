seamless

adjective
without seams or joints
4.2 0.43/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêml…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêml…ôs / (BrE)
(of a fabric, garment, or object) made without seams; not having or joined by a seam or seams.

Examples

Seamless metal pipe
Modern technology allows manufacturers to create seamless metal pipes for industrial use.
She bought a pair of seamless stockings for extra comfort.
The seamless design makes the shirt ideal for sports activities.

Synonyms

  • unseamed
    8.4 0.01/m
    unseamed describes a garment made without any sewn seams, while seamless describes a fabric or object joined without seams.
  • jointless
    4.7 0.01/m
    jointless describes something formed in one continuous piece without joints, while seamless refers specifically to fabrics or garments without stitched seams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seamed - having seams or joints
