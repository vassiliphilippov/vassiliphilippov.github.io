seamless

adjective
without seams or joints
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmləs / (AmE), / ˈsiːmləs / (BrE)
(of a fabric, garment, or object) made without seams; not having or joined by a seam or seams.

Examples

Seamless metal pipe
Modern technology allows manufacturers to create seamless metal pipes for industrial use.
She bought a pair of seamless stockings for extra comfort.
The seamless design makes the shirt ideal for sports activities.

  • antonym: seamed - having seams or joints
