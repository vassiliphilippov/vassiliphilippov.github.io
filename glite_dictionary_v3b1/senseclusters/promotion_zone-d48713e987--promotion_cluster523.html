The promotion zone in chess
If a pawn reaches the promotion zone, it can be exchanged for a queen, rook, bishop, or knight.
In Shogi, the rules for entering the promotion zone differ from those in Western chess.
Players must strategize to move their pawns safely into the promotion zone before promoting them.
