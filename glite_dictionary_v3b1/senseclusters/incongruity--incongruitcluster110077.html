incongruity

noun
state of being out of place or inconsistent
8.0 1.09/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…™n.k…ônÀà…°ruÀê.…ô.ti / (AmE), / Àå…™n.k…ínÀà…°ruÀê.…™.ti / (BrE)
the state of being out of place, unsuitable, or inconsistent; a lack of harmony or agreement between things

Examples

The incongruity of mood
She couldn't help but laugh at the incongruity of his cheerful mood at the funeral.
The incongruity between the modern skyscraper and the ancient temple was striking.
There is a certain incongruity in wearing a swimsuit to a formal dinner.
