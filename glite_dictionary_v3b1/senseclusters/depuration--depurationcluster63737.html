depuration

noun
ritual purification
9.9
Pronunciation: /də.pjʊ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /de.pjʊ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /də.pjʊ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /de.pjʊ.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a ritual or ceremonial act of purification, cleansing a person, place, or object for spiritual or religious reasons

Examples

Ritual depuration
Before entering the temple, the priest performed a depuration to cleanse himself spiritually.
The annual festival began with a depuration of the village square to invite good fortune.
Many cultures have a form of depuration to purify sacred objects before important ceremonies.
