profligately

adverb
in a recklessly extravagant or immoral manner
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑː.flə.ɡət.li / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.flɪ.ɡət.li / (BrE)
In a way that is recklessly extravagant or immoral; in a dissolute manner.

Examples

To spend profligately
Leaders who rule profligately often leave their nations in debt and disarray.
She lived profligately in her youth, wasting both time and money.
The company spent profligately on luxury offices and expensive dinners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extravagantly - in a manner that spends resources lavishly
  • antonym: frugally - in a thrifty or economical manner
