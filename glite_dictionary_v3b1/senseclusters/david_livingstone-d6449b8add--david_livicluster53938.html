David Livingstone

proper noun
Scottish missionary and African explorer (1813–1873)
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪvɪd ˈlɪvɪŋstən / (AmE), / ˈdeɪvɪd ˈlɪvɪŋstən / (BrE)
(1813–1873), Scottish missionary and explorer famous for his expeditions in Africa, including the discovery of Victoria Falls and exploration of the Zambezi River.

Examples

David Livingstone explorer Africa
David Livingstone is widely celebrated for being the first European to see Victoria Falls.
Many historians regard David Livingstone as a symbol of nineteenth-century European curiosity about Africa.
The legacy of David Livingstone endures in the field of African exploration and missionary work.
