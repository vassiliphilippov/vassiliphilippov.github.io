ska

adjective
Jamaican music style
6.7
Pronunciation: /skɑː/ (AmE), /skɑː/ (BrE)
relating to the Jamaican musical style ska, especially in reference to music, bands, or sounds with its characteristic rhythms and instruments

Examples

Ska band

She loves the energetic, ska rhythms that make everyone want to dance.
Their latest album features a strong ska influence, especially in the horn section.
