rectilinear

adjective
characterized by straight lines
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛktəˈlɪniər / (AmE), / ˌrɛktɪˈlɪnɪə / (BrE)
moving in or forming straight lines; characterized by straight lines instead of curves.

Examples

Rectilinear motion
Lasers travel in a rectilinear path through a vacuum.
She preferred rectilinear patterns over floral motifs for her wallpaper.
The architect designed the building with rectilinear shapes and sharp angles.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curvilinear - characterized by curved lines
