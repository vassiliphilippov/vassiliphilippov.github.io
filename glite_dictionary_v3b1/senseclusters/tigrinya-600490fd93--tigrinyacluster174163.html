Tigrinya

noun
Tigrinya-speaking person
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪˈɡrɪnjə / (AmE), / tɪˈɡrɪnjə / (BrE)
a person who speaks the Tigrinya language or belongs to the Tigrinya-speaking people of Eritrea and northern Ethiopia

Examples

A Tigrinya person
A Tigrinya joined the council to represent the needs of her neighborhood.
Many Tigrinya have settled in cities around the world as part of the diaspora.
The festival celebrated the traditions of the Tigrinya community in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eritrean - a person from Eritrea
  • synonym: Ethiopian - a person from Ethiopia
