harlequin

noun
comic character or clown
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrlɪkwɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːlɪkwɪn / (BrE)
A comic character typically dressed in multicolored, diamond-patterned costume, originating from the commedia dell'arte, or any clownish or buffoonish person.

Examples

A harlequin costume
At the festival, a harlequin in a patchwork costume handed out balloons to children.
Her colorful outfit made her look like a modern harlequin.
The harlequin danced across the stage, delighting the audience with his antics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffoon - a ridiculous but amusing person; a clown
  • synonym: clown - a comic entertainer who wears funny clothes and makeup
