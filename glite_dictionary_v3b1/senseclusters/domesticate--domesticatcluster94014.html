domesticate

verb
adapt wild plants or land for cultivation
7.5
Pronunciation: / dəˈmɛstəˌkeɪt /; / dəˈmɛstɪˌkeɪt / (AmE), / dəˈmɛstɪkeɪt / (BrE)
to make an animal, plant, or land suitable for human use by taming, training, or cultivating
