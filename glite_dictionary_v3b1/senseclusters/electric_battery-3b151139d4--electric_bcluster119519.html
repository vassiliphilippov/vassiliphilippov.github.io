electric battery

noun
device that produces electricity from electrochemical cells
2.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbætəri / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk ˈbætəri / (BrE)
A device that produces electricity, typically consisting of one or more electrochemical cells connected together.

Examples

A electric battery device
He replaced the electric battery in his remote control when it stopped working.
Many modern cars rely on a powerful electric battery for starting and running electronics.
Solar panels can charge an electric battery to provide power at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battery - a container consisting of one or more cells that produces an electric current
  • degree lower: cell - a single electrochemical unit that generates electricity
