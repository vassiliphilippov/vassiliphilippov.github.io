trumpeter

noun
person who announces news (with trumpet)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpətər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtə / (BrE)
(formal) a person who announces important news, especially with a trumpet.

Examples

Royal trumpeter
Every village celebration began with a trumpeter proclaiming the news to the people.
The trumpeter blew his horn to announce the king's arrival.
The ancient city employed a trumpeter who shared royal proclamations.
