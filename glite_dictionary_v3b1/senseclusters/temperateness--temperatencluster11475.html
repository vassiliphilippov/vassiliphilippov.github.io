temperateness

noun
moderation, especially of climate or weather
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərətnəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərətnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being moderate in degree, especially with regard to climate or weather; mildness.

Examples

Temperateness of the climate
Due to its temperateness, the valley rarely experiences extreme temperature changes.
The temperateness of the region makes it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts year-round.
Tourists appreciate the city's temperateness compared to the sweltering summers of nearby areas.

Related Terms
