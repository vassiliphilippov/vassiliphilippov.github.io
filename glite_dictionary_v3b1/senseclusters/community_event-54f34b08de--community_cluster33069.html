community event

noun
online gathering for a community
Pronunciation: / kəˈmjunəti ɪˈvɛnt / (AmE), / kəˈmjuːnəti ɪˈvɛnt / (BrE)
an organised gathering or activity for members of a community that takes place online, using digital platforms (for example webinars, livestreams, or virtual meetups).

Examples

Online community event
The open-source project organized a virtual community event to celebrate the software's major new release.
Members from five countries joined the online community event to discuss mentoring and new collaboration opportunities.
After the pandemic began, the neighborhood association moved its annual community event to a livestream format.

Related Terms

  • antonym: local event - an event held in a physical local area; typically attended in person
  • synonym: online event - an event held over the internet rather than in a physical venue
