Podocarpus amara

noun
tropical evergreen tree species
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊdəˈkɑrpəs əˈmɑrə / (AmE), / ˌpɒdɔːˈkɑːpəs əˈmɑːrə / (BrE)
A large, fast-growing tropical evergreen tree with large, glossy lance-shaped leaves, native to rainforests of Sumatra, the Philippines, and northern Queensland.
