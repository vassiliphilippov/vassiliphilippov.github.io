methylation

noun
adding methyl group
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmiːθɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the chemical process of adding a methyl group to a molecule (such as DNA, RNA, or proteins), often affecting how genes work
