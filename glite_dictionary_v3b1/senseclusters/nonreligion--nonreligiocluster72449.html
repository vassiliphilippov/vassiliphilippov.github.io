nonreligion

noun
absence of religion
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈlɪdʒ.ən / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈlɪdʒ.ən / (BrE)
the state of not having religious beliefs or not being affiliated with any religion

Examples

Discussions about nonreligion are becoming more common in modern society.
