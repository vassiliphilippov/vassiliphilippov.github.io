linguist

noun
expert in linguistics
1.0 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋɡwɪst / (BrE)
A person who studies or is an expert in linguistics; a specialist in the scientific study of language.

The conference attracted leading linguists from around the world.
The young linguist published a groundbreaking paper on language acquisition.
