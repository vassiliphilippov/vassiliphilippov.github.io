PIM

noun
product data management system
7.7
Pronunciation: / pi aɪ ɛm / (AmE), / piː aɪ em / (BrE)
Software or a system used by retailers and manufacturers to centralize, enrich, manage, and distribute product data (such as descriptions, attributes, images, and identifiers) across channels.

Examples

Product PIM system
The retailer implemented a PIM to centralize and synchronize product data across channels.
Our company uses a cloud-based PIM to manage product descriptions, prices, and supplier information.
Before launch, the team enriched every SKU in the PIM to ensure consistent listings worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: master data management - the processes and tools that ensure a single, consistent source of key business data across systems
  • synonym: product information management - the practice and systems for collecting, formatting, and distributing product data for commerce and marketing
