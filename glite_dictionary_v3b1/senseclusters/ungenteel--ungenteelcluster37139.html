ungenteel

adjective
not refined or polite
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndʒɛnˈtiːl / (AmE), / ˌʌndʒɛnˈtiːl / (BrE)
lacking refinement, good manners, or proper behavior; not polite or cultured

Examples

Ungenteel behavior
His ungenteel remarks shocked everyone at the elegant party.
Wearing jeans to the opera was considered ungenteel by the older guests.
The teacher corrected the student's ungenteel way of speaking in class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: genteel - polite, refined, or respectable in manner
  • synonym: uncouth - lacking good manners or refinement
