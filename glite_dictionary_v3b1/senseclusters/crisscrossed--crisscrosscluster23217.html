crisscrossed

adjective
marked with crossing lines
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs.krɔst / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.krɒst / (BrE)
Having a pattern of crossing lines or paths.

Examples

Crisscrossed mountain slopes
A network of trails crisscrossed the mountain slopes.
Her back was crisscrossed with scars from the accident.
The old map was crisscrossed with faded railroad tracks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crisscross - to move back and forth across something, creating a pattern of crossing lines
