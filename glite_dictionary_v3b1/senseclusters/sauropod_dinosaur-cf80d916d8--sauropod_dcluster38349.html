sauropod dinosaur

noun
large herbivorous dinosaur with long neck and tail
6.5 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑːr.ə.pɑːd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔːr/ /; / /ˈsɔːr.ə.pɑːd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈsɔː.rə.pɒd ˈdaɪ.nə.sɔː/ (BrE)
a very large herbivorous dinosaur of the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods, with a small head, long neck and tail, and five-toed limbs; among the largest known land animals
