common European dogwood

noun
European shrub with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən juˈroʊpiən ˈdɔɡˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən jʊəˈrəʊpiən ˈdɒɡˌwʊd / (BrE)
a European deciduous shrub with dull white flowers that turns red in autumn

Examples

The common European dogwood shrub
In autumn, the leaves of the common European dogwood turn a striking shade of red.
The dull white flowers of the common European dogwood appear early in the summer.
They planted a common European dogwood to brighten up the edge of the garden.
