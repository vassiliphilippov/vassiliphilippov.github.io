provocativeness

noun
sexual suggestiveness
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɑːkətɪvnəs / (AmE), / prəˈvɒkətɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being sexually suggestive or intended to arouse sexual interest

Examples

The provocativeness of an outfit
The provocativeness of the advertisement sparked debate about its appropriateness for television.
Some critics argued that the film's provocativeness overshadowed its artistic value.
Her choice of clothing was noted for its provocativeness at the event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modesty - the quality of being reserved or not intended to attract sexual attention
  • synonym: seductiveness - the quality of being sexually attractive or alluring
