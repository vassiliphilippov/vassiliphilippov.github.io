triangulator

noun
person using triangulation
9.5
Pronunciation: / traɪˈæŋɡjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / traɪˈæŋɡjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who uses triangulation, either to determine positions or to gain advantage in politics or negotiation

Examples

Political triangulator
The skilled triangulator managed to find a compromise between the two opposing parties.
As a political triangulator, she adopted opponents' popular proposals to win moderate votes.
A triangulator in surveying uses measurements from different points to determine exact locations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: negotiator - a person who discusses to reach an agreement
  • synonym: strategist - a person skilled in planning actions to achieve a goal
