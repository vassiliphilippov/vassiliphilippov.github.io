tangelo

noun
hybrid citrus fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈtændʒəˌloʊ /; / tænˈdʒɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈtan.dʒɪ.ləʊ /; / tanˈdʒɛləʊ / (BrE)
a citrus fruit that is a hybrid between a tangerine and a grapefruit or pomelo, known for being juicy and easy to peel
