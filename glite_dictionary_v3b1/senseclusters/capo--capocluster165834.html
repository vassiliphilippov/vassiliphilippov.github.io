capo

noun
Mafia branch leader; crime boss
9.7 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀêpo ä / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêp…ô ä / (BrE)
a high-ranking member, especially the head of a branch, in an organized crime syndicate, especially the Mafia

Examples

Mafia capo
During the trial, the capo denied any involvement in the syndicate's activities.
He rose quickly through the ranks to become a capo in the notorious crime family.
The police finally arrested the capo who controlled the city's drug operations.

Synonyms

  • ringleader
    6.4 1.02/m

Related Terms

  • degree lower: underboss - a subordinate to the head of a crime family; second-in-command
