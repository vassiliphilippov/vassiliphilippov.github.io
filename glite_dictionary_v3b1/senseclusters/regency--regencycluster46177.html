regency

noun
period governed by a regent
3.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.dʒən.siː/ (AmE), /ˈriː.dʒən.siː/ (BrE)
the period, office, or authority when a regent rules in place of a monarch who cannot govern

Examples

Throughout the regency, political power was exercised by the prince's uncle.

His reforms were implemented during the regency following the queen's abdication.
The regency lasted until the young prince reached adulthood.
