undrivable

adjective
not safe to drive
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdraɪvəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdraɪvəbəl / (BrE)
not possible or safe to drive on, usually because a road or area is blocked, damaged, or in poor condition

Examples

Undrivable road
The heavy snow made the mountain pass completely undrivable for several days.
After the flood, many rural roads became undrivable due to deep mud and debris.
The old bridge was declared undrivable after engineers found serious structural damage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drivable - able to be driven on safely
  • synonym: impassable - impossible to travel through or across
