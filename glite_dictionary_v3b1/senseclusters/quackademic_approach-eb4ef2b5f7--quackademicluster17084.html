quackademic approach

noun
pseudo-academic or unscientific method
Pronunciation: / kwækəˈdɛmɪk əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / kwækəˈdɛmɪk əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A method or practice that pretends to be academic or scientific but lacks real scientific rigor or legitimacy.

Examples

A quackademic approach used
He was accused of using a quackademic approach to gain public trust without presenting real evidence.
Many critics dismissed the report as a quackademic approach lacking any factual basis.
The university's decision to fund the quackademic approach sparked controversy among faculty members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: academic - relating to education or scholarship
  • synonym: pseudoscientific - based on false or unscientific claims
