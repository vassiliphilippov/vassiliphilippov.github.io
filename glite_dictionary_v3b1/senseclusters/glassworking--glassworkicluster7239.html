glassworking

verb
to shape glass
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːswɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
to shape or form objects from glass by heating, blowing, molding, or cutting

Examples

To glasswork a vase

After learning the basics of glassworking, he was able to make his first glass sculpture.
The artist demonstrated glassworking techniques to the fascinated audience at the museum.
