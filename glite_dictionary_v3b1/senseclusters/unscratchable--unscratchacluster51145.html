unscratchable

adjective
cannot be scratched
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskrætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskrætʃəbl / (BrE)
impossible to scratch or very resistant to being scratched

Examples

Unscratchable glass

After months of use, the laptop's unscratchable surface still looks brand new.
Scientists developed an unscratchable coating for smartphone screens.
