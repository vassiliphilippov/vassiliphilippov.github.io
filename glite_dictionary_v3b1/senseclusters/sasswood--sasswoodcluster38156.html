sasswood

noun
West African tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæsˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈsæsˌwʊd / (BrE)
a West African tree whose bark is used in traditional medicine and rituals

Examples

Sasswood bark medicine
The villagers collected sasswood bark for use in their ceremonies.
Traditional healers believe that sasswood has powerful medicinal properties.
During the ritual, a small piece of sasswood was placed in the water.
