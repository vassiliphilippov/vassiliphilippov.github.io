rainout

noun
canceled event due to rain
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪn.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈreɪn.aʊt/ (BrE)
an event (such as a sports game or concert) that is canceled or stopped because of rain

Examples

Baseball rainout
The baseball game was declared a rainout after hours of heavy showers.
Due to the unexpected rainout, fans were given tickets for the rescheduled match.
Several concerts this summer have ended in a rainout because of bad weather.
The concert was declared a rainout after a thunderstorm swept through the area.
Fans were disappointed when the championship match became a rainout due to heavy rain.
Because of the rainout, the baseball team had to reschedule the game for next week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cancellation - the act of calling off an event
  • synonym: postponement - the act of delaying an event to a later time
