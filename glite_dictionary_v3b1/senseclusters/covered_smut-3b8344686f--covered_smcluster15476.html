covered smut

noun
smut disease where spores are covered by grain membranes
8.2 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌv.ərd smʌt/ (AmE), /ˈkʌv.əd smʌt/ (BrE)
a fungal disease of grains in which the spore masses remain enclosed by the grain membranes, caused by a smut fungus

Examples

Covered smut disease
Early detection of covered smut can help farmers prevent significant yield loss.
Many wheat crops were destroyed by covered smut last season, reducing the overall harvest.
The presence of covered smut is indicated by dark, compact spore masses inside the grain husks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose smut - smut disease of grains where spore masses are freed early and uncovered
