perfusion

noun
pumping liquid (esp. blood) into tissue
8.0 1.01/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàfjuÀê í…ôn / (AmE), / p…ôÀàfjuÀê í…ôn / (BrE)
the process of pumping a liquid, especially blood, into an organ or tissue through blood vessels

Examples

Tissue perfusion
Adequate perfusion of the tissues is essential for organ health and function.
During surgery, doctors monitor blood perfusion closely to prevent complications.
Impaired perfusion can lead to tissue damage and delayed healing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injection - the act of forcing a liquid into something, especially with a syringe
