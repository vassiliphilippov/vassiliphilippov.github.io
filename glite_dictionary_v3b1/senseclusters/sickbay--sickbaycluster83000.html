sickbay

noun
room for medical care on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪkˌbeɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɪkˌbeɪ / (BrE)
(nautical) A room or area on a ship, or in an institution, used for the treatment of sick or injured people.

Examples

The sickbay room
After his injury, he spent several days recovering in the sickbay.
If you feel unwell during the voyage, report to the sickbay immediately.
The ship's doctor was called to the sickbay to tend to a crew member with a fever.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infirmary - a room or building where sick people are cared for, especially in a school, prison, or other institution
