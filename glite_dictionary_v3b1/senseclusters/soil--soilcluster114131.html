soil

noun
top layer of earth for plant growth
1.4
Pronunciation: / sɔɪl / (AmE), / sɔɪl / (BrE)
the top layer of the earth in which plants grow, made of organic matter and minerals

Examples

Rich soil
After years of farming, the soil became depleted of nutrients.
Rich, fertile soil is essential for growing healthy crops.
The gardener tested the soil before planting the new flowers.
After digging through layers of soil, the archaeologists found ancient artifacts.
Farmers must protect the soil from erosion caused by wind and rain.
The rich soil in the valley produces excellent crops.

  • synonym: earth - the substance forming the surface of the world; soil
