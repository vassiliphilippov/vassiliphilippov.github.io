spiegel iron

noun
pig iron with manganese for steelmaking
Pronunciation: / ˈspiɡəl ˌaɪɚn / (AmE), / ˈspiːɡl ˌaɪən / (BrE)
a form of pig iron containing a high percentage of manganese, used as a deoxidizing agent and to increase the manganese content in steelmaking

Examples

High manganese spiegel iron
Adding spiegel iron helps raise the manganese content during steel production.
The factory imported spiegel iron to improve the quality of its steel products.
The metallurgist recommended the use of spiegel iron as a deoxidizing agent.
