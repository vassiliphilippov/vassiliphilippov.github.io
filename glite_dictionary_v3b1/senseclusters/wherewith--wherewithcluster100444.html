wherewith

adverb
by means of which
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛrˌwɪð / (AmE), / ˈwɛəwɪð / (BrE)
by means of which; with what or the things needed to do something

Examples

The wherewith to pay
He lacked the wherewith to pay the rent on time.
She searched for the tools wherewith she could fix the broken chair.
The letter contained instructions wherewith the puzzle could be solved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whereby - by which; through which
