ablation

noun
surgical removal of tissue or organ
Pronunciation: / əˈbleɪʃən / (AmE), / əˈbleɪʃən / (BrE)
the surgical removal of a body part, organ, or tissue, typically to treat a disease or condition

Examples

Heart ablation surgery
Doctors recommended ablation as the best option for controlling the tumor.
Laser ablation is commonly used to treat certain eye disorders.
The patient underwent ablation to remove abnormal heart tissue.
