booster

noun
extra vaccine or medication dose
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
an extra dose of a vaccine or other medication given after the initial dose to increase or renew effectiveness

Examples

Vaccine booster
After receiving her initial vaccine, Emily scheduled her booster for six months later.
Children often receive a booster when starting school to maintain immunity.
Health officials recommended a booster to ensure continued protection against the virus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booster shot - an additional injection of a vaccine to maintain its effectiveness
