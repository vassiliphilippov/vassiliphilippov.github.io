unsarcastic

adjective
not sarcastic
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.sɑrˈkæs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.sɑːˈkæs.tɪk / (BrE)
not sarcastic; lacking or free from sarcasm.

Examples

An unsarcastic comment

Her unsarcastic response surprised everyone, as she was usually quite witty.
I appreciated her unsarcastic compliment about my presentation.
