drumstick

noun
lower leg of a cooked fowl
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈdrʌm.stɪk / (BrE)
the lower part of the leg of a cooked bird such as a chicken or turkey, especially as food

Examples

Chicken drumstick
I'd like a drumstick and some mashed potatoes, please.
She always saves the drumstick for her favorite guest at Thanksgiving dinner.
The chicken drumstick was crispy and perfectly seasoned.
