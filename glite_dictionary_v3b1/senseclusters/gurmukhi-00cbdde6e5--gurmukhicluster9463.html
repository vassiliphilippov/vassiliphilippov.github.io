Gurmukhi

adjective
of Gurmukhi script
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡʊrˈmuːki / (AmE), / ɡʊəˈmuːki / (BrE)
relating to the Gurmukhi script or to texts written in the Gurmukhi alphabet

Examples

Gurmukhi script
The ancient manuscript was written in Gurmukhi script, making it accessible to Punjabi speakers.
She published a Gurmukhi translation of the poem to reach Punjabi readers.
Many Sikh religious texts are preserved in Gurmukhi form.

Related Terms
