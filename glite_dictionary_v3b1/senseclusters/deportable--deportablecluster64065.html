deportable

adjective
able to be expelled
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpɔːrtəbl / (AmE), / dɪˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be removed or expelled from an organization, institution, or place.

Examples

Deportable members
After several violations of the code of conduct, the employee was considered deportable by the committee.
Members who break the club's rules may be deemed deportable at any time.
The university's policy states that students involved in serious misconduct are deportable from campus housing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expellable - able to be expelled or forced to leave
  • antonym: protected - not able to be removed or expelled
  • synonym: removable - able to be removed
