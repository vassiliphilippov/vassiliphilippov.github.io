unflinching

adjective
firm and fearless, not showing hesitation
4.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflɪntʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈflɪntʃɪŋ / (BrE)
remaining strong and determined, even in the face of danger or difficulty; not showing fear or hesitation

Examples

Unflinching honesty
He faced the accusations with unflinching honesty and never avoided the press.
Her unflinching courage inspired everyone in the room to keep going.
The soldier stood unflinching as the enemy advanced, refusing to back down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resolute - admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering
  • antonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence; easily frightened
