bollen

adjective
swollen or puffed up
Pronunciation: / ˈbɒlən / (AmE), / ˈbɒlən / (BrE)
swollen or puffed up, especially as a result of filling with fluid or air

Examples

The riverbanks bollen with water
After the storm, the riverbanks were bollen with water from the heavy rain.
His face looked bollen after the allergic reaction.
The sacks were bollen with freshly harvested grain.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deflated - having lost air or fullness; not swollen
  • synonym: swollen - enlarged or puffed up, often due to fluid
