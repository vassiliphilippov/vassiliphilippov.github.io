vocalize

verb
sing notes on one vowel
3.2 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈvoʊ.kə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈvəʊ.kə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to sing each note of a scale or melody using the same vowel sound, especially as a vocal exercise

Examples

To vocalize a scale
Before singing the piece, the choir members were asked to vocalize the scale on a single vowel.
She prefers to vocalize melodies on 'ah' during her warm-ups.
The instructor encouraged students to vocalize each note carefully to improve pitch accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solfège - a system for teaching pitch and sight singing using syllables
