cover-slut

noun
person who frequently remakes albums or loves tribute bands
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌvɚ slʌt / (AmE), / ˈkʌvə slʌt / (BrE)
(informal, vulgar) A person, especially an artist, who frequently remakes other musicians’ albums or is an enthusiastic supporter of tribute bands and cover versions.

Examples

A cover-slut artist
As a self-confessed cover-slut, he rarely writes original songs.
Many critics accused him of being a cover-slut for releasing another album of remakes.
She proudly called herself a cover-slut after attending three tribute band concerts in a week.
