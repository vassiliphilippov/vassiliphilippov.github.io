Allah

proper noun
the Islamic name for God
3.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlɑː / (AmE), / əˈlɑː / (BrE)
The name of God in Islam, used by Muslims and Arabic-speaking Christians and Jews.

Examples

Allah the God
Arabic-speaking Christians and Jews also use the term Allah to refer to God.
He thanked Allah for the blessing of good health.
Many Muslims pray to Allah five times a day facing Mecca.

Related Terms

  • synonym: God - the supreme being worshipped in monotheistic religions
