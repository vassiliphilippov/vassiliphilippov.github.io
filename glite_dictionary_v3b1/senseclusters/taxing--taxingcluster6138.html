taxing

adjective
requiring much effort; exhausting
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæksɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæksɪŋ / (BrE)
Demanding great effort or endurance; difficult and tiring.

Examples

A taxing task
Climbing the steep mountain trail was especially taxing for the inexperienced hikers.
Preparing for final exams can be both mentally and physically taxing.
The long and taxing negotiations lasted late into the night.
