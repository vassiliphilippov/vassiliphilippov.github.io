economic system

noun
a society's method of organizing the economy
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkɑːnəmɪk ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / iːˈkɒnəmɪk ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
the way in which the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services are organized within a society or country.

Examples

A economic system model
A capitalist economic system emphasizes private ownership and free markets.
Every country has its own unique economic system based on its history and values.
The transition to a market-based economic system was challenging for many former socialist countries.

Related Terms
