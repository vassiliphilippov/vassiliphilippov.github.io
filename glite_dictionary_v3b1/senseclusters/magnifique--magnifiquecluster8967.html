magnifique

adjective
splendid or impressive
8.5
Pronunciation: / mæɡnɪˈfik / (AmE), / maɲiˈfik / (BrE)
used to describe something as magnificent, splendid, or impressive, often for emphasis

Examples

A magnifique view
The chef prepared a magnifique meal that everyone enjoyed.
Her performance in the play was absolutely magnifique and received a standing ovation.
They described the palace as magnifique after seeing its grand architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magnificent - extremely beautiful, impressive, or grand
  • antonym: ordinary - not special or impressive
  • synonym: splendid - very impressive or beautiful
