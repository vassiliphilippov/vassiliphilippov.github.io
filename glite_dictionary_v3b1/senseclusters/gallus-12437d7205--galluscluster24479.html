Gallus

noun
genus of birds including domestic chickens
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləs / (AmE), / ˈɡal.əs / (BrE)
A genus of birds that includes the domestic chicken and its close relatives.

Examples

Gallus bird genus
Scientists have identified several species within Gallus based on morphological differences.
The junglefowl belongs to the genus Gallus, which also includes domesticated chickens.
The red junglefowl, a wild ancestor of the domestic chicken, is classified as Gallus gallus.
