spinelessly

adverb
without courage
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnlɪsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows a lack of courage or determination

Examples

He acts spinelessly
He spinelessly agreed to all their demands without protest.
The manager spinelessly avoided making any difficult decisions during the meeting.
She spinelessly accepted the unfair criticism instead of standing up for herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravely - in a way that shows courage
  • synonym: cowardly - in a way that lacks courage
