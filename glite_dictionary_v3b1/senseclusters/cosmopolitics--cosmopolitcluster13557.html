cosmopolitics

noun
global political ideology
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑzməpəˈlɪtɪks / (AmE), / ˌkɒzməpəˈlɪtɪks / (BrE)
a political ideology or movement that promotes global citizenship, cultural openness, and solidarity beyond national borders

Examples

Cosmopolitics of global citizenship
The conference on cosmopolitics attracted scholars interested in global cooperation and cultural exchange.
Many young people are drawn to cosmopolitics as a way to address worldwide challenges like climate change.
Debates about cosmopolitics often focus on balancing global solidarity with respect for local traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmopolitanism - the idea that all people belong to a single global community
  • antonym: nationalism - a political ideology focused on promoting the interests of a particular nation
