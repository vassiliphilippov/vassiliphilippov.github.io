no time to waste

x
urgency for immediate action
1.0
Pronunciation: / noʊ taɪm tə weɪst / (AmE), / nəʊ taɪm tə weɪst / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something must be done immediately, without any delay

Examples

No time to waste now
The team knew there was no time to waste and started working right away.
We have no time to waste if we're going to catch the last train.
With the deadline approaching, they realized there was no time to waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no time to lose - another phrase meaning something must be done immediately
  • synonym: urgency - the quality of being very important and needing attention immediately
