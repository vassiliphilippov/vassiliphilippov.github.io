everbearing

noun
plant with repeated harvests
Pronunciation: / ˈɛvərˌbɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɛvəˌbɛrɪŋ / (BrE)
a plant or variety that produces fruit or flowers continuously or several times during the growing season

Examples

An everbearing plant
The gardener chose an everbearing to enjoy fresh strawberries from spring to fall.
Many people prefer everbearing varieties because they provide fruit over a longer period.
An everbearing can be a great addition to a small home garden for continuous harvests.
