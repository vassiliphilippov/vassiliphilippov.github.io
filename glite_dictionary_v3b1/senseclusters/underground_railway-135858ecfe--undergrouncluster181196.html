Underground Railway

noun
secret network aiding escaped slaves (US history)
5.9 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈʌn.dər.graʊnd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ /; / /ˈʌn.dər.graʊnd ˈræl.weɪ/ (AmE), /ˈʌn.də.graʊnd ˈreɪl.weɪ/ /; / /ˈʌn.də.graʊnd ˈræl.weɪ/ (BrE)
