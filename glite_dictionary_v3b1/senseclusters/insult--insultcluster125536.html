insult

noun
offensive or hurtful remark/action
1.0 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.sʌlt/ /; / /ˈɪn.səlt/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.sʌlt/ /; / /ˈɪn.səlt/ (BrE)

Examples

The remarks were meant as an insult, not a joke.
Throwing a drink in someone's face is considered a serious insult.
