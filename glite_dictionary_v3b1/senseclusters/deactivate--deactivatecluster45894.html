Synonyms

  • disable
    3.5 3.42/m

  • turn off
    1.3 24/m
    turn off stops a device by using a switch or control, while deactivate can mean switching off a function or system more broadly.
  • switch off
    1.1 3.13/m

  • shut down
    2.9 18/m

  • de-energize
