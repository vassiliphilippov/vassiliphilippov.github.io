Cuvier

proper noun
French naturalist and anatomist (1769–1832)
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈvjeɪ / (AmE), / ˈkwiːvieɪ / (BrE)
Georges Cuvier (1769–1832), a French naturalist regarded as the founder of comparative anatomy and paleontology.

Examples

Cuvier the naturalist
Cuvier revolutionized natural history by introducing the concept of extinction.
Many species of fossils were first classified by Cuvier in the early 19th century.
The study of comparative anatomy owes much to the contributions of Cuvier.
