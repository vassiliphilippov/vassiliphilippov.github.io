Mojave Desert

proper noun
desert region in southwestern United States
Pronunciation: / moʊˈhɑːvi ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / məˈhɑːvi ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
a desert region in southern California, with parts extending into Nevada, Arizona, and Utah, known for its arid climate and unique flora and fauna
