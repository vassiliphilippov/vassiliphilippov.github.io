SOE

noun
government-owned company
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs oʊ ˈi / (AmE), / ˌɛs əʊ ˈiː / (BrE)
a company or organization that is owned and operated by a government, known as a state-owned enterprise

Examples

A government SOE
The government plans to restructure the largest SOE in the telecommunications sector.
Many countries use an SOE to manage natural resources like oil and gas.
The SOE reported record profits after expanding its international operations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: private company - a business owned by private individuals or groups
  • synonym: state-owned enterprise - a business owned and controlled by the government
