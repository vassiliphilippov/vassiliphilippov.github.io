sterilization

noun
process of making infertile
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌstɛrɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making an organism unable to reproduce, often by medical or surgical means

Examples

Animal sterilization
After the sterilization procedure, the animal was no longer capable of reproducing.
Some countries have laws regulating the sterilization of domestic animals.
The clinic offers sterilization services to help control the pet population.
Her doctor discussed different methods of sterilisation as a permanent form of contraception.
Mandatory sterilisation policies have been controversial throughout history.
The veterinary clinic performs sterilisation on stray animals to control the population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: castration - removal of the testes or ovaries to make infertile
  • antonym: fertility - ability to reproduce; opposite of being infertile
  • antonym: fertilization - the process of making fertile; the opposite of sterilization
