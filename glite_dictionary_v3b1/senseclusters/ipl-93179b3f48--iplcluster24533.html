IPL

noun
light-based skin treatment
Pronunciation: / aɪ piː ɛl / (AmE), / aɪ piː ɛl / (BrE)
a cosmetic and medical procedure that uses intense pulses of broad-spectrum light to remove hair or treat skin conditions

Examples

IPL hair removal
Many people choose IPL to reduce unwanted hair on their legs and arms.
The dermatologist recommended IPL for treating sun damage and uneven skin tone.
After several IPL sessions, her skin appeared clearer and more even.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrolysis - a method of removing hair by destroying the hair root with electricity
  • synonym: laser hair removal - a procedure using laser light to remove unwanted hair
