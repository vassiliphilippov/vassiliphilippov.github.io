Al Alamayn

proper noun
WWII battle in North Africa, 1942
Pronunciation: / ˌæl əˈlɑː.maɪn / (AmE), / ˌæl əˈlæm.aɪn / (BrE)
A decisive pitched battle in World War II (1942) in which Allied forces under Montgomery defeated German troops led by Rommel in North Africa.
