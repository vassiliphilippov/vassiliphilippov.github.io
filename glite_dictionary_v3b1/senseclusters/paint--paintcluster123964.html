paint

noun
colored coating for surfaces
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /peɪnt/ (AmE), /peɪnt/ (BrE)
a colored liquid or semi-liquid substance applied to surfaces to protect or decorate them, which dries to form a hard coating

Examples

A can of paint
Many artists mix their own paint to achieve unique colors and textures.
The fresh paint on the walls made the entire room look brighter.
We need to buy more paint to finish the fence.

  • synonym: varnish - a transparent, hard, protective finish or film used in wood finishing
