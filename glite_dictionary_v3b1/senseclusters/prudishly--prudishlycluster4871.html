prudishly

adverb
in an excessively proper or modest way
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːdɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈpruːdɪʃli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows an exaggerated or excessive propriety or modesty, especially about matters of sex or morality.

Examples

To act prudishly
He reacted prudishly to their jokes, blushing and changing the subject.
She refused to watch the film, insisting prudishly that it was inappropriate.
Some people prudishly avoid any discussion of romance in the office.
