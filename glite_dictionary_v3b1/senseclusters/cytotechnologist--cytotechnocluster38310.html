cytotechnologist

noun
cell analysis specialist
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtoʊtɛkˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəʊtɛkˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a laboratory professional who examines cells under a microscope to detect diseases such as cancer

Examples

The cytotechnologist examines cells
The cytotechnologist carefully examined the blood smear for any abnormal cells.
A skilled cytotechnologist can detect early signs of cancer in tissue samples.
Hospitals rely on a cytotechnologist to help diagnose diseases at the cellular level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laboratory technician - a person who performs technical tasks in a laboratory
  • synonym: pathologist - a doctor who studies diseases by examining tissues and body fluids
