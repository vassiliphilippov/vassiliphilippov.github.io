hornworm

noun
caterpillar with tail horn
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrnˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈhɔːnwɜːm / (BrE)
a large green caterpillar with a horn-like projection at its tail, commonly found feeding on tomato and tobacco plants

Examples

A hornworm caterpillar
The gardener found a hornworm eating the leaves of her tomatoes.
A single hornworm can quickly strip a tomato plant of its foliage.
Children were fascinated by the bright green hornworm crawling on the garden fence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: butterfly - an adult insect that develops from a caterpillar
  • synonym: caterpillar - the larval stage of a butterfly or moth
  • synonym: tomato hornworm - a species of hornworm that feeds on tomato plants
