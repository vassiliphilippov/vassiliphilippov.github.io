solecism

noun
socially awkward or tactless act
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.lɪˌsɪz.əm /; / ˈsɑːlɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈsɒl.ɪˌsɪz.əm /; / ˈsɒlɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
a mistake in grammar, language use, or a socially improper or awkward act
