by no stretch of the imagination

x
not at all; not in any way
5.5
Pronunciation: / baɪ noʊ stɹɛtʃ əv ði ɪˌmædʒəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / baɪ nəʊ stɹɛtʃ əv ði ɪˌmadʒɪˈneɪʃn̩ / (BrE)
used to emphasize that something is not true or possible in any way; not at all.

Examples

By no stretch of the imagination is true
By no stretch of the imagination could this report be considered complete.
Her performance was by no stretch of the imagination impressive.
That restaurant is by no stretch of the imagination affordable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: not by any stretch of the imagination - used for the same emphatic sense
