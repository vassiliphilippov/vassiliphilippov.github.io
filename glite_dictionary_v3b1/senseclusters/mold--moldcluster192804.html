mold

noun
a fungus growing on damp or decaying matter
4.3
Pronunciation: / moʊld / (AmE), / məʊld / (BrE)
a fungus that grows in the form of multicellular filaments, often appearing as a fuzzy or slimy surface on damp or decaying material

Examples

Green mold spots
After the flood, mold started to spread across the basement walls.
Breathing in mold can cause health problems for some people.
There was green mold growing on the bread left in the cupboard.
After the flood, mould quickly spread across the walls and ceiling.
Some people are allergic to household mould and must keep their homes dry.
The bread was covered in green and white mould after being left out for a week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mildew - a thin whitish coating of fungus growing on plants or damp organic material
  • synonym: mold - US spelling of 'mould' (fungus that grows on damp matter)
