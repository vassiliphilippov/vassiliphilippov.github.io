service

verb
(of male animal) mate with female
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrvəs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
(of a male animal) to mate with a female for breeding purposes.

Examples

To service a mare

The breeder hopes the champion stallion will service several mares this season.
The farmer arranged for the bull to service the cows in the spring.
