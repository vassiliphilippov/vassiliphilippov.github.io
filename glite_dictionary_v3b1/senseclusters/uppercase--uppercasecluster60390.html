uppercase

noun
a capital (large) letter
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpərˌkeɪs / (AmE), / ˈʌpəkeɪs / (BrE)
a capital letter; a large alphabetic character used at the beginning of proper names and sometimes for emphasis

Examples

An uppercase A
Instructions often specify entering passwords in uppercase or lowercase.
Please write your name in uppercase letters on the form.
The company changed its logo to use only uppercase characters for better visibility.
