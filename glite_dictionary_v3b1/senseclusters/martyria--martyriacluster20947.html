martyria

noun
martyr's testimony
Pronunciation: / mɑːrˈtɪriə / (AmE), / mɑːˈtɪəriə / (BrE)
the testimony or witness given by a martyr, especially through suffering or death for a cause or faith

Examples

Martyria of faith
The early church preserved the martyria of those who refused to renounce their faith.
Historians study the martyria to understand the sacrifices made by early believers.
The martyria of saints inspired many to remain steadfast in their convictions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: martyrdom - the suffering or death of a martyr
  • synonym: testimony - a formal statement or declaration, especially in court or as evidence
