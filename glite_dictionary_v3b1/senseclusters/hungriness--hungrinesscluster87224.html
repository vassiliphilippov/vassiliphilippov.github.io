hungriness

noun
intense desire or longing
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌŋɡri.nəs / (AmE), / ˈhʌŋɡri.nəs / (BrE)
a strong and intense desire for something, not necessarily food or drink

Examples

Hungriness for success
After months of setbacks, her hungriness for success only grew stronger.
The hungriness for recognition drove him to work late into the night.
There was a visible hungriness in his eyes as he spoke about his dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunger - strong desire or craving for something, especially food
  • antonym: satisfaction - fulfillment of one's wishes, expectations, or needs
  • synonym: thirst - strong desire for something, especially a nonphysical craving
