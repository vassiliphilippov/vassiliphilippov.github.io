vernonia

noun
genus of flowering plants with purple or rose heads
8.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /vɜːr.ˈnoʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /vər.ˈnoʊ.niː.ə/ (AmE), /və.ˈnəʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /vɜː.ˈnəʊ.niː.ə/ (BrE)
Any of various plants of the genus Vernonia, found in tropical and warm regions, especially in North America, with loose heads of purple to rose flowers that turn rusty as they age.
