standing

adjective
(of liquids) not moving or flowing
Pronunciation: / ˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
(of liquids) not moving or flowing; stagnant.

Examples

Standing water
After the heavy rain, pools of standing water formed all over the field.
Avoid drinking from standing ponds, as the water may be contaminated.
The city is working to remove areas of standing water to prevent mosquito breeding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stagnant - (of a liquid or air) not flowing, often stale or foul
