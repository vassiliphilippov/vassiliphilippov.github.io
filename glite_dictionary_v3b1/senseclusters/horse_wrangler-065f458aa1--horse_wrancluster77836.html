horse wrangler

noun
person who cares for saddle horses
Pronunciation: / hɔrs ˈræŋɡlər / (AmE), / hɔːs ˈræŋɡlə / (BrE)
A person, typically on a ranch or film set, who takes care of saddle horses and manages their handling.

Examples

A horse wrangler on ranch
As a horse wrangler, her job was to round up the herd each morning before sunrise.
The film hired a skilled horse wrangler to ensure all the animals were cared for during shooting.
The ranch relied on its experienced horse wrangler to train new horses for trail rides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who tends cattle and performs many of his duties on horseback
  • synonym: wrangler - a person who takes care of animals, especially cattle or horses, on a ranch
