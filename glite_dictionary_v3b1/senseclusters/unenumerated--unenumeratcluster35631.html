unenumerated

adjective
without numbers
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈnuːmɚeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not assigned numbers or sequence identifiers; lacking page or item numbers

Examples

Unenumerated pages
The report included several unenumerated tables, making it difficult to reference specific data.
Some pages in the old manuscript were unenumerated, which caused confusion during cataloging.
The list was unenumerated, so readers had to count the items themselves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enumerated - assigned numbers or listed in order
  • synonym: unnumbered - not marked with a number or numbers
