dumb cluck

noun
foolish or incompetent person (informal)
Pronunciation: / dʌm klʌk / (AmE), / dʌm klʌk / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is foolish or incompetent.

Examples

A dumb cluck person
Don't be such a dumb cluck and pay attention to what's being said.
He got lost in his own neighborhood, feeling like a real dumb cluck.
She called herself a dumb cluck after locking her keys in the car again.
