pulsatile

adjective
having regular pulses
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsəˌtaɪl / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsətaɪl / (BrE)
moving or occurring in a series of regular beats or pulses

Examples

Pulsatile swelling
The nurse observed a pulsatile swelling on the patient's wrist.
A pulsatile flow of blood can be felt in healthy arteries.
The machine produced a pulsatile vibration that could be easily detected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhythmic - having a regular repeated pattern of sounds or movements
  • antonym: steady - continuous and unchanging in movement or condition
