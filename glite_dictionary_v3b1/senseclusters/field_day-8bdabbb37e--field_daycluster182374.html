field day

noun
day for military exercises and displays
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌdeɪ / (BrE)
A day set aside for military exercises, maneuvers, and displays, often for training or demonstration purposes.

Examples

Military field day
During the annual field day, soldiers participated in a series of challenging drills.
Spectators attended the military field day to watch demonstrations of precision and discipline.
The army organized a field day to showcase their new equipment and tactics.
