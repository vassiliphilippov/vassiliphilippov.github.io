rhizomatous

adjective
having or resembling rhizomes
9.9
Pronunciation: / raɪˈzɑː.mə.təs / (AmE), / raɪˈzɒ.mə.təs / (BrE)
(of a plant) producing, having, or resembling rhizomes (horizontal underground stems).

Examples

Rhizomatous plant growth
Bamboo is a rhizomatous grass, spreading rapidly underground.
Many rhizomatous plants are valued for their ability to control soil erosion.
The gardener avoided planting rhizomatous species in small spaces due to their invasive growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rhizome - a horizontal underground plant stem
