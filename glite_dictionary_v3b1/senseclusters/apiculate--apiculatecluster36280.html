apiculate

adjective
having a short, sharp tip (esp. in botany)
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪkjʊlət / (AmE), / əˈpɪkjʊlət / (BrE)
(of a leaf or other plant part) having a short, sharply pointed tip.

Examples

Apiculate leaf tip
Botanists noted that the new variety produced apiculate petals with distinct pointed tips.
The apiculate ends of the leaves help distinguish this plant from closely related species.
You can identify the tree by examining its apiculate leaf tips under a magnifying glass.
