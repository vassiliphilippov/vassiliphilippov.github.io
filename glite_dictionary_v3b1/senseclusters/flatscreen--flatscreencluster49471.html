flatscreen

noun
thin display device
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌskriːn / (AmE), / ˈflætˌskriːn / (BrE)
a television, monitor, or display with a flat, thin screen

Examples

A flatscreen TV
They mounted the new flatscreen on the wall to save space in the living room.
A large flatscreen makes watching movies at home feel like a theater experience.
Many offices have replaced old monitors with modern flatscreens for better image quality.
The technician carefully installed a new flatscreen in the office conference room.
After dropping his tablet, the flatscreen was scratched but still worked.
Many modern laptops use a high-resolution flatscreen for better image quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: CRT - a bulky, curved-screen television or monitor using cathode ray tube technology
  • antonym: cathode ray tube - an older, bulky type of display used before flat panels
  • synonym: display - the part of an electronic device that shows images or information
  • synonym: monitor - an electronic device for displaying images or data
  • synonym: screen - the surface on which images or data are shown on a device
  • synonym: television - an electronic device for receiving and displaying broadcast images and sound
