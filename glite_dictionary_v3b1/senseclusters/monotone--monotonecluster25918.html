monotone

adjective
consistent (mathematical) increase or decrease
7.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ëÀên…ôÀåto än / (AmE), / Ààm…ín…ôt…ô än / (BrE)
(of a sequence or function) Consistently increasing or consistently decreasing in value; never reversing direction.

Examples

Monotone sequence
A monotone decreasing function will never increase at any point.
The function is said to be monotone if it never decreases throughout its domain.
This sequence is strictly monotone, as each term is larger than the one before.
