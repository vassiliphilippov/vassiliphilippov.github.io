daringness

noun
boldness; risk-taking
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrɪŋnəs / (AmE), / ˈdeərɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being bold and willing to take risks

Examples

The daringness shown
Her daringness impressed everyone during the climb.
The daringness of the rescue team saved many lives in the storm.
He admired her daringness in speaking out against injustice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boldness - the quality of being confident and courageous
  • synonym: courage - the ability to face danger or pain without fear
  • antonym: timidity - the quality of being shy or lacking courage
