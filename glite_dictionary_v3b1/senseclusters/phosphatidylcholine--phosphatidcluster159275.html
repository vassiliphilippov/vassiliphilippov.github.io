phosphatidylcholine

noun
membrane lipid
9.4
Pronunciation: /fɑːs.fə.tɪd.əl.ˈkoʊ.laɪn/ (AmE), /fɒs.fə.tɪd.əl.ˈkəʊ.laɪn/ (BrE)
