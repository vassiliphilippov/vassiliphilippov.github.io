new chapter

noun
a significant new phase in life
Pronunciation: / nuː ˈtʃæptɚ / (AmE), / njuː ˈtʃæptə / (BrE)
a period marked by a significant change or a new stage in life, often after an important event or transition

Examples

Start a new chapter
After the divorce, he moved to a new city to start a new chapter.
Graduating from college was the beginning of a new chapter in her life.
The company's merger marked a new chapter in its history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: end - the final stage or conclusion of something
  • synonym: fresh start - an opportunity to begin something again with a clean slate
  • synonym: turning point - a time at which a decisive change in a situation occurs
