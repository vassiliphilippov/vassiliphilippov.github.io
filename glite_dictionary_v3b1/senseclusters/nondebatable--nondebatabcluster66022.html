nondebatable

adjective
not open to debate
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndɪˈbeɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒndɪˈbeɪtəbəl / (BrE)
not open to discussion or argument; cannot be debated

Examples

A nondebatable fact
The facts presented in the report are nondebatable and accepted by all parties.
According to the rules, this decision is nondebatable and must be followed.
The manager made it clear that the deadline was nondebatable for everyone on the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indisputable - impossible to doubt or question
  • synonym: undebatable - not able to be debated or discussed
