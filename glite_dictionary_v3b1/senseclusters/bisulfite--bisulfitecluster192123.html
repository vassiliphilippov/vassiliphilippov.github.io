bisulfite

verb
to add bisulphite
Pronunciation: / baɪˈsʌlfaɪt / (AmE), / baɪˈsʌlfaɪt / (BrE)
to treat or combine something with bisulphite, especially in a chemical process to preserve, reduce, or modify it
