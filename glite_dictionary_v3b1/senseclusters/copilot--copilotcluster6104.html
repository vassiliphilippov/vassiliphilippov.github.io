copilot

noun
pilot assisting the main pilot
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌpaɪlət / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌpaɪlət / (BrE)
a pilot who assists or relieves the main pilot of an aircraft

Examples

Airplane copilot
In case of emergency, the copilot is trained to take over the aircraft controls.
She was promoted from flight attendant to copilot after years of training and hard work.
The copilot assisted the captain with landing the plane during heavy turbulence.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: captain - the main or chief pilot in command of an aircraft
  • synonym: pilot - the person who operates and controls an aircraft
