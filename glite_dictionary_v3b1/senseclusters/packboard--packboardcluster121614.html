packboard

noun
rigid carrying frame
Pronunciation: / ˈpækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpækˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a rigid frame designed to support and carry heavy loads on a person's back, often used in hiking or transport

Examples

Hiking packboard
The explorer secured his supplies to a sturdy packboard for the mountain trek.
Carrying a heavy load was easier with the help of a packboard.
She learned how to properly attach her tent to the packboard before the camping trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backpack - a bag carried on the back, often with shoulder straps
  • synonym: frame pack - a backpack with an external supporting frame
