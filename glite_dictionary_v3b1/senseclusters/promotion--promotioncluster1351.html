promotion

noun
publicity or advertising activity
3.9
Pronunciation: / proʊˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
a publicity message or activity designed to increase awareness or support for a product, cause, idea, or institution

Examples

A promotion campaign
Charities often rely on promotion to raise awareness for their campaigns.
The company launched a new promotion to introduce their latest smartphone model.
This promotion highlights the importance of regular exercise for a healthy lifestyle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advertisement - a public notice promoting a product, service, or event
  • synonym: publicity - notice or attention given to someone or something by the media
