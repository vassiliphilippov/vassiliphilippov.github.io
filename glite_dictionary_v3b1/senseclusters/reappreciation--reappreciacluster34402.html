reappreciation

noun
renewed value increase
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːəˌpriːsiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a renewed increase in the value of something, especially a currency or asset, after a previous decline or stability

Examples

Currency reappreciation
The sudden reappreciation of the euro surprised many investors in the financial markets.
After years of decline, the reappreciation of property values brought relief to homeowners.
Analysts are studying the causes behind the recent reappreciation of the national currency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appreciation - an increase in the value of something
  • antonym: depreciation - a decrease in the value of something
