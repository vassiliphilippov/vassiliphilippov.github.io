manchineel

noun
poisonous tropical tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmæn.tʃɪˈniːl / (AmE), / ˌman.tʃɪˈniːl / (BrE)
a tropical tree with highly poisonous fruit and sap that can cause severe harm to humans

Examples

Manchineel tree danger
The manchineel is considered one of the most dangerous trees in the world due to its toxic sap.
Local guides warn tourists to avoid standing under a manchineel during rain, as the water can carry poison.
Even the fruit of the manchineel tree can cause severe illness if touched or eaten.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apple tree - a tree that produces edible apples
  • synonym: poisonwood - a tropical tree with irritating sap
