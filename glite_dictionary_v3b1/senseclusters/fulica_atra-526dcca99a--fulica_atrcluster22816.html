Fulica atra

noun
Eurasian species of coot (bird)
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˈlaɪkə ˈɑːtrə / (AmE), / fjʊˈliːkə ˈɑːtrə / (BrE)
A species of coot found in Eurasia, identifiable by its black plumage and white bill; commonly known as the Eurasian coot.
