bodysurfing

verb
to ride waves unaided
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdiˌsɝfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɒdiˌsɜːfɪŋ / (BrE)
to ride waves in the sea using only your body, without a surfboard or other equipment.

Examples

To bodysurf a wave
We spent the afternoon bodysurfing at the beach, catching wave after wave.
He learned bodysurfing before ever trying to use a surfboard.
Many people enjoy bodysurfing because it requires no special equipment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: surf - to ride on waves using a surfboard
