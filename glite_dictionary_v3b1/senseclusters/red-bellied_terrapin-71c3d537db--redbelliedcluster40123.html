red-bellied terrapin

noun
turtle with red-marked lower shell
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdˈbɛlid ˈtɛrəˌpɪn / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈbɛlɪd ˈtɛrəpɪn / (BrE)
a freshwater turtle native to Chesapeake Bay tributaries, characterized by red markings on its lower shell

The red-bellied terrapin is easily identified by the vivid red coloring on its underside.
