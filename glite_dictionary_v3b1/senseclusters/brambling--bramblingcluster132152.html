brambling

noun
a small Eurasian finch
Pronunciation: / ˈbræm.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbræm.lɪŋ / (BrE)
A small Eurasian finch (Fringilla montifringilla) with orange breast and shoulder markings, often found in woodlands.

Examples

A brambling bird
A flock of bramblings was spotted feeding under the forest canopy.
During migration, many bramblings gather in northern Europe for the winter.
The distinctive orange breast makes the brambling easy to identify among other finches.
