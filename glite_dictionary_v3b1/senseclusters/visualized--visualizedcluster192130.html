visualized

adjective
presented as a visual representation
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪʒ.wə.laɪzd/ /; / /ˈvɪ.ʒuː.ə.laɪzd/ (AmE), /ˈvɪ.ʒuː.ə.laɪzd/ /; / /ˈvɪʒ.wə.laɪzd/ (BrE)
Rendered or presented as a visual representation (for example, charts, graphs, maps, or dashboards) rather than as text or raw data.

Examples

Visualized data chart
The complex dataset was visualized using an interactive dashboard to reveal trends.
Researchers visualized the survey results as heatmaps to highlight regional differences clearly.
Before the presentation, she had visualized the financial forecasts with clear line and bar charts.
The dataset is visualised as a heat map to show regional trends.
In the report, the company's growth was visualised through a series of bar charts.
She visualised the complex process by drawing flow diagrams on the whiteboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - to show information so that people can see it, often on a screen or chart
  • synonym: represent - to show or describe something in a particular way, often visually
  • synonym: visualize - to represent or form something in a visual way, often using charts or images
