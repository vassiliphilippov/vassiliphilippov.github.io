MBD

noun
multiple birth defects
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɛm bi di / (AmE), / ɛm biː diː / (BrE)
a medical abbreviation for 'multiple birth defect', referring to a condition involving several congenital abnormalities present at birth

Examples

MBD at birth
The pediatrician discussed the possible causes of MBD with the child's parents.
Early diagnosis of MBD can help improve treatment outcomes for affected infants.
Specialists work together to manage the complex needs of children with MBD.
