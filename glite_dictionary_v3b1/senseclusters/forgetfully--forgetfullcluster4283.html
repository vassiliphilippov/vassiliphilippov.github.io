forgetfully

adverb
in a forgetful manner
5.6
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɛtfəli / (AmE), / fəˈɡɛtfʊli / (BrE)
In a way that shows forgetfulness; in a manner that suggests forgetting or absent-mindedness.

Examples

To forgetfully leave keys
He forgetfully left his keys on the kitchen counter again this morning.
She answered forgetfully, not remembering the details of the conversation.
The professor glanced forgetfully at his notes, searching for his place in the lecture.
