tideless

adjective
unchanging; steady
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdləs / (BrE)
not changing or unaffected by shifting trends, opinions, or external influences

Examples

Tideless loyalty
Her commitment to her values was tideless, even when others changed their minds.
The artist's style remained tideless throughout decades of shifting trends in the art world.
He admired her tideless loyalty, which never wavered despite difficult circumstances.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fickle - changing frequently, especially in regards to loyalty or interests
  • synonym: steady - firm, regular, or constant in character or behavior
  • synonym: unchanging - not becoming different over time
