Larus marinus

noun
great black-backed gull species
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɹəs məˈɹaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɑːrəs məˈriːnəs / (BrE)
the great black-backed gull, a large white gull with a black back and wings, found along the coasts of the North Atlantic

Examples

The Larus marinus gull
