due east

noun
exactly east direction (90 degrees)
Pronunciation: / du ˈist / (AmE), / djuː ˈiːst / (BrE)
the direction corresponding to 90 degrees on a compass; exactly east.

Examples

Due east direction
He adjusted his course to sail due east across the ocean.
The sun rises in the due east and sets in the due west.
They marked the front entrance so it faced due east for good luck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: due west - the compass direction exactly opposite due east (270 degrees)
  • synonym: east - the cardinal compass point at 90 degrees
