calving

noun
the birth of a calf by a cow or similar animal
Pronunciation: / ˈkævɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of a cow or similar animal giving birth to a calf

Examples

Spring calving season
During calving, farmers monitor the herd closely to assist with any complications.
Proper nutrition is essential to support healthy calving in cows.
Spring is the busiest season for calving on the ranch.
