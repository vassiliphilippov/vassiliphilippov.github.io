prodigiously

adverb
extraordinarily; to a remarkable degree
5.5
Pronunciation: / prəˈdɪdʒəsli / (AmE), / prəˈdɪdʒəsli / (BrE)
To a remarkable or enormous degree; extraordinarily or impressively great in extent or amount.

Examples

To prodigiously large extent
He ate prodigiously at the banquet, sampling every dish offered.
The company has grown prodigiously since its humble beginnings ten years ago.
The young pianist played prodigiously well for her age, astonishing the entire audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enormously - to an enormous degree; extremely
  • synonym: remarkably - in a way that is worthy of attention; noticeably
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree or extent
