garniture

noun
decorative fittings
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrnɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːnɪtjʊə / (BrE)
decorative metal fittings or mountings attached to furniture, clothing, or weapons for ornament or support

Examples

Furniture garniture
The antique desk was adorned with intricate garniture along its edges.
Restorers carefully replaced the missing garniture on the old wardrobe to match the original style.
The knight's sword featured a silver garniture that made it stand out in the armory.
