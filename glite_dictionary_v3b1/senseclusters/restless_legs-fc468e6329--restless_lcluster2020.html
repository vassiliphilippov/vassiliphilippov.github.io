restless legs

noun
uncomfortable urge to move the legs, esp. in bed
3.2 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈrest.ləs legz/ (AmE), /ˈrest.ləs legz/ (BrE)
a condition marked by an uncomfortable urge to move the legs, typically occurring in bed and often associated with insomnia

Examples

Restless legs syndrome
Moving around temporarily relieved his restless legs during long flights.
She often suffers from restless legs which keep her awake at night.
There are medications available to help people who experience restless legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restless legs syndrome - a disorder causing discomfort and urge to move the legs, especially at night
