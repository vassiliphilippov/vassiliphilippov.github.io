image

noun
typical example or embodiment
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪdʒ / (BrE)
a person who closely resembles another

Examples

The image of his father

She became the very image of kindness in her community.
The company wants to project an image of professionalism and reliability.
Her daughter is the very image of her at that age.
Many fans say the actor's image can be found in the crowd.
People were amazed by how much the stand-in resembled the image of the star.
