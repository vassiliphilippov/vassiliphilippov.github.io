hypercalcinuria

noun
excess calcium in urine (medical)
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˌkælsiˈnjuːriə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəkælsiˈnjʊəriə / (BrE)
the presence of abnormally high levels of calcium in the urine, often associated with certain medical conditions such as hyperparathyroidism or osteoporosis
