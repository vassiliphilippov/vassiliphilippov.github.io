intrinsical

adjective
inherent; belonging by nature (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtrɪnsɪkəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈtrɪnsɪkəl / (BrE)
Belonging to the essential nature of something; inherent or innate. (Archaic or rare)

Examples

intrinsical nature
Many artists believe that beauty is intrinsical to nature itself.
The philosopher debated whether good and evil are intrinsical values or human inventions.
Their intrinsical differences made compromise almost impossible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intrinsic - inherent; belonging naturally; essential
