exercise

verb
make use of power, rights, etc.
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.sɚ.saɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛk.sə.saɪz / (BrE)
to make use of (a right, privilege, quality, or power) in order to achieve a result

Examples

To exercise rights
Citizens are encouraged to exercise their right to vote in every election.
The manager may exercise greater control over the project to ensure its success.
The president decided to exercise his veto power on the proposed legislation.
