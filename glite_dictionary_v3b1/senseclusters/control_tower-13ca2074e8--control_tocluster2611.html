control tower

noun
centralized operations hub
3.3
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtroʊl ˈtaʊ.ər/ /; / /kən.ˈtroʊl ˈtaʊr/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtrəʊl ˈtaʊ.ə/ /; / /kən.ˈtrəʊl ˈtəʊ.ə/ (BrE)
a centralized hub that monitors, coordinates, and controls distributed operations or systems (for example in supply-chain management, logistics, or IT), providing real-time visibility and decision support.

Examples

Supply chain control tower
The company launched a cloud-based control tower to orchestrate its global supply chain.
Our IT team uses a control tower dashboard to detect outages and reroute traffic automatically.
Retailers rely on a control tower for real-time inventory visibility across dozens of warehouses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: command center - a place or system used for centralized control and coordination of operations or activities
  • synonym: operations hub - a centralized platform that brings together data and tools to manage operational activity
