unchallenging

adjective
not difficult
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ / (BrE)
not difficult, demanding, or exciting; easy to do and not stimulating

Examples

An unchallenging task
