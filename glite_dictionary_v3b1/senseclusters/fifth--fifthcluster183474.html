fifth

noun
one part in five equal parts
6.9
Pronunciation: / fɪfθ / (AmE), / fɪfθ / (BrE)
each of the five equal parts into which a whole can be divided; one part out of five.

Examples

A fifth of students
A fifth of the students did not pass the final exam.
Only a fifth of the cake remained after the party.
She received a fifth of her inheritance from her grandmother's estate.
