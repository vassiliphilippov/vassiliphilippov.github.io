vengefully

adverb
with a desire for revenge
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛndʒfəli / (AmE), / ˈvɛndʒfʊli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong desire to punish someone or get revenge.

Examples

Look vengefully at
He glared vengefully at the man who had betrayed him.
She vengefully destroyed all traces of their former partnership.
The villain vengefully plotted to ruin his rival's reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vindictively - in a revengeful or spiteful manner
