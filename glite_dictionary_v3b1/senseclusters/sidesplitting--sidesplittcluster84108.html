sidesplitting

adjective
extremely funny
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌsplɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌsplɪtɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

A sidesplitting joke

The comedian told a sidesplitting story that had the audience in tears of laughter.
We watched a sidesplitting movie last night and couldn't stop laughing.
