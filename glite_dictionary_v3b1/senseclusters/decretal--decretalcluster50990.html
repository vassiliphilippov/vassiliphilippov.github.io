decretal

adjective
about church decrees
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkriːtəl / (AmE), / dɪˈkriːtəl / (BrE)
relating to or connected with decretals, especially papal decrees or official church rulings.

Examples

Decretal law
The scholar analyzed several decretal documents from the medieval church.
A decretal letter from the pope clarified the church's position on the issue.
The collection includes many decretal writings that influenced canon law.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canonical - relating to church law or accepted rules
