insurrectionist

noun
person involved in an armed uprising
Pronunciation: / ɪn.səˈrɛk.ʃə.nɪst / (AmE), / ɪn.səˈrɛk.ʃən.ɪst / (BrE)
a person who participates in an armed uprising against an established authority, typically aiming to improve social or political conditions

Examples

An insurrectionist leader
As an insurrectionist, she believed drastic action was needed to change the oppressive regime.
Authorities arrested several suspected insurrectionists after the violent protests.
The insurrectionist forces stormed the capital in an attempt to overthrow the government.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loyalist - a person who remains loyal to the established government, especially in the face of a revolt
  • synonym: rebel - a person who rises in opposition or armed resistance against an established government or leader
