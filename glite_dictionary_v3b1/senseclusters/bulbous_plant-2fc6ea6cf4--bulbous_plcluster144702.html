bulbous plant

noun
a plant that grows from a bulb
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌlbəs plænt / (AmE), / ˈbʌlbəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
a plant that grows from a bulb, such as a tulip, onion, or daffodil

Examples

A bulbous plant flower
Garlic, a bulbous plant, is easy to grow in most backyard gardens.
Many spring flowers, such as tulips and daffodils, are bulbous plants.
The gardener recommended planting bulbous plants in autumn for early blooms next year.
