Pronunciation: / meɪ hi rɛst ɪn pis / (AmE), / meɪ hi rɛst ɪn piːs / (BrE)
A phrase expressing a wish that a deceased man may find peace after death.

Examples

May he rest in peace
After a long and fruitful life, may he rest in peace.
His kindness touched many lives; may he rest in peace.
We said our final goodbyes at the funeral—may he rest in peace.
