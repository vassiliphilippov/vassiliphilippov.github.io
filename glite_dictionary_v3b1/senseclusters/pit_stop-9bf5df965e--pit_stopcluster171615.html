pit stop

noun
brief stop in car racing for service
1.6
Pronunciation: / pɪt stɑp / (AmE), / pɪt stɒp / (BrE)
A brief stop during a motor race, typically to refuel, change tires, or service the car.

Examples

Race car pit stop
Every race strategy must account for at least one pit stop under high-speed conditions.
The leading car made a quick pit stop to change its tires and refuel.
Unexpected issues during the pit stop cost the team several crucial seconds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pit crew - the team responsible for servicing a car during a pit stop
