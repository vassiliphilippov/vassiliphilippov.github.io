cryptographically

adverb
in a manner relating to cryptography
Pronunciation: / krɪp.təˈɡræf.ɪ.kli / (AmE), / krɪp.təˈɡræf.ɪ.kli / (BrE)
In a manner relating to or using cryptography; by means of methods for securing information.

Examples

To cryptographically protect data
Passwords are cryptographically protected to prevent unauthorized access.
Sensitive data should be cryptographically secured before transmission over the internet.
The message was cryptographically signed to ensure its authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptographic - relating to cryptography; concerning the techniques of secret writing
