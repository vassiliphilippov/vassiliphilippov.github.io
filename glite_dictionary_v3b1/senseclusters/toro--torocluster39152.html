toro

noun
bull in bullfighting
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a bull, especially one used in Spanish bullfighting

Examples

Spanish toro fight
The toro charged across the arena during the festival.
Spectators cheered as the toro faced the matador in the ring.
In Spanish culture, the toro is a symbol of strength and bravery.
