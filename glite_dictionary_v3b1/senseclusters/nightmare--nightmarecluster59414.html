nightmare

noun
very unpleasant or distressing situation
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌmɛr / (AmE), / ˈnaɪt.mɛə / (BrE)
An extremely unpleasant or difficult situation that causes anxiety, distress, or fear, resembling a terrifying dream.

Examples

A nightmare situation
For the organizers, the unexpected storm was a logistical nightmare that disrupted all their plans.
Moving to a new city without knowing anyone was a complete nightmare for her at first.
Trying to fix the leaking pipe on my own turned into a plumbing nightmare.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dream - a series of thoughts, images, and sensations during sleep
  • synonym: ordeal - a painful or horrific experience, especially a protracted one
