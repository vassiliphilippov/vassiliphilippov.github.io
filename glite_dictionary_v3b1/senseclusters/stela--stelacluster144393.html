stela

noun
ancient inscribed stone slab
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːlə / (AmE), / ˈstiːlə / (BrE)
an upright stone slab, often inscribed or carved, used as a monument or marker in ancient times

Examples

Ancient stela monument
Archaeologists discovered a Maya stela depicting a powerful ruler in the city center.
The ancient Egyptians carved intricate hieroglyphs onto the stela to honor their gods.
The museum's collection includes a Greek stela marking a soldier's grave.
