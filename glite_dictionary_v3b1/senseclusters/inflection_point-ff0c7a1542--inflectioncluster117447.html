inflection point

noun
critical turning point; significant moment of change
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛkʃən pɔɪnt / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛkʃən pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a moment or stage at which significant change occurs; a critical turning point in a process, situation, or organization

Examples

A inflection point moment
Her decision to change careers proved to be a major inflection point in her life.
Many analysts believe we are approaching an inflection point in renewable energy adoption.
The launch of their new product marked an inflection point in the company's history.
