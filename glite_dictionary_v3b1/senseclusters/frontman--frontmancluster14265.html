frontman

noun
public representative
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who acts as the main public representative or spokesperson for a group, company, or organization

Examples

Company frontman
The frontman of the band greeted the audience before the first song began.
As the company's frontman, she handled all media interviews and public appearances.
The political party chose a charismatic frontman to attract more voters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: figurehead - a person who represents an organization but has little real power
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group or organization
  • synonym: spokesperson - someone who speaks officially for a group or organization
