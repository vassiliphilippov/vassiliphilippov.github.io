spectinomycin

noun
an antibiotic drug for treating gonorrhea
8.3
Pronunciation: / spɛktɪˈnɑːmɪsɪn / (AmE), / spɛktɪˈnɒmɪsɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic drug used especially to treat gonorrhea and certain other bacterial infections

Examples

Spectinomycin antibiotic drug
Because of its effectiveness against certain bacteria, spectinomycin is used in some cases where penicillin is not suitable.
Some strains of gonorrhea are now resistant to spectinomycin, making treatment more challenging.
The doctor prescribed spectinomycin to treat the patient's gonorrhea infection.
