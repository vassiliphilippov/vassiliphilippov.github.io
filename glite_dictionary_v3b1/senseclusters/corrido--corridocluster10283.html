corrido

noun
Mexican narrative song
Pronunciation: / koˈɾiːdoʊ / (AmE), / kɒˈriːdəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Mexican narrative song or ballad that tells a story, often about historical events or folk heroes

Examples

A corrido song
The musicians performed a famous corrido about a legendary hero from northern Mexico.
At the festival, everyone sang along to the lively corrido played by the band.
He learned to play a traditional corrido on his guitar to honor his heritage.
The musician performed a corrido that recounted the bravery of a famous revolutionary.
Many families listen to a corrido during celebrations to remember their heritage.
He learned to play a traditional corrido on the guitar from his grandfather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballad - a song or poem that tells a story
  • synonym: folk song - a traditional song passed down through generations
