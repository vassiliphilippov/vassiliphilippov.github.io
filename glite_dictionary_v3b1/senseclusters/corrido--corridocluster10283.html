corrido

noun
Mexican narrative song
7.7
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈriː.doʊ/ (AmE), /kə.ˈriː.dəʊ/ (BrE)
a traditional Mexican narrative song or ballad that tells a story, often about historical events or folk heroes
