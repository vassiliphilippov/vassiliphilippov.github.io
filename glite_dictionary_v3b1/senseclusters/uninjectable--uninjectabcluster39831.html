uninjectable

adjective
not capable of being injected
Pronunciation: / ʌnˌɪnˈdʒɛktəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˌɪnˈdʒɛktəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) not capable of being injected into the body, usually due to its form or properties.

Examples

An uninjectable drug
An uninjectable antibiotic might instead be administered orally or topically.
Some medications must be reformulated because their ingredients are initially uninjectable.
The drug's thick consistency made it completely uninjectable through standard syringes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: injectable - capable of being injected
