uninjectable

adjective
not capable of being injected

Pronunciation: /ʌn.ɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.bəl/ /; / /ə.nɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.bəl/ /; / /ə.nɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
(of a drug or substance) not capable of being injected into the body, usually due to its form or properties.

Examples
