address

verb
prepare and aim (in golf)
Pronunciation: / əˈdrɛs / (AmE), / əˈdrɛs / (BrE)
(in golf) to adjust the position and aim of the club and ball in preparation for making a stroke.

Examples

To address the ball
It's important to address the ball properly to ensure a successful shot.
She paused to address her stance and grip before hitting the tee shot.
The golfer carefully addressed the ball before executing his swing.
