contractible

adjective
able to shrink
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræktəbəl / (AmE), / kənˈtræktəbəl / (BrE)
able to be made smaller or shorter by contracting

Examples

Contractible muscle
The heart is made of contractible muscle tissue that pumps blood throughout the body.
Some materials are contractible when exposed to cold temperatures.
The balloon is not contractible once it has been inflated to its maximum size.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressible - able to be pressed or squeezed into a smaller space
  • antonym: expandable - able to become larger or increase in size
