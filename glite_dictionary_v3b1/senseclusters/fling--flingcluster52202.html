fling

noun
brief attempt or try
Pronunciation: / flɪŋ / (AmE), / flɪŋ / (BrE)
a brief attempt at doing something, especially when you do not expect to succeed or are not very serious

Examples

A fling at painting
He took a fling at cooking, but quickly gave up when the first dish burned.
She decided to give painting a fling before committing to a full course.
Why not have a fling at writing your own novel this summer?

Related Terms

  • synonym: try - an attempt to do something
  • synonym: whirl - a brief attempt or try
