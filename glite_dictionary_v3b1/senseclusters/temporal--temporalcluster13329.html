temporal

noun
side skull bone
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəl / (BrE)
the part of the skull located at the sides of the head near the temples.

Examples

The temporal area
The surgeon made an incision near the temporal to access the affected area.
A fracture in the temporal can cause hearing problems and dizziness.
The anatomy class studied the structure of the temporal in detail.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parietal - a bone forming the central side and upper back part of each side of the skull
  • synonym: temple - the flat area on each side of the head above the ear
