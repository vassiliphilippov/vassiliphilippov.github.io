bespeak

verb
to be a sign or indicate
Pronunciation: / bɪˈspiːk / (AmE), / bɪˈspiːk / (BrE)
to be a sign of or indicate something; to show evidence of a quality or condition

Examples

Bespeak great confidence
His calm demeanor bespoke great confidence in the situation.
The dilapidated condition of the house bespeaks years of neglect.
The elegant handwriting bespeaks a careful and educated writer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conceal - to hide or keep from being seen or known
  • synonym: indicate - to show, point out, or be a sign of
  • synonym: signal - to be an indication or sign of something
