choke back

verb
suppress or restrain (emotion/reaction)
Pronunciation: / tʃoʊk bæk / (AmE), / tʃəʊk bæk / (BrE)
to suppress or restrain (an emotion or reaction), especially with difficulty

Examples

To choke back tears
Emma choked back a sob as she read the heartbreaking letter.
He could barely choke back his anger when he heard the news.
She tried to choke back her tears during the farewell ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suppress - to put an end to or restrain (an emotion, action, etc.)
