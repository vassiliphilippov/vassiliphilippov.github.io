divided

adjective
split or distributed into parts
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪdɪd / (BrE)
(of something) split or distributed into parts, often in equal portions and typically according to a plan or purpose.

Examples

Divided equally
After hours of negotiation, the property was divided according to the terms of the settlement.
Each group received a divided share of the supplies, ensuring fairness for everyone.
The money was divided equally among the four children after their grandmother passed away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: united - joined together or combined into a single entity
