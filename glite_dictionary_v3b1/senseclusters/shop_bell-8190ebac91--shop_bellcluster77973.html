shop bell

noun
bell on shop door that alerts proprietor
1.4 1.8
Pronunciation: /ʃɑːp bel/ (AmE), /ʃɒp bel/ (BrE)
a bell attached to the door of a shop that rings to alert the proprietor when a customer enters or leaves

Examples

The shop bell rings
Every time the shop bell rings, someone steps inside for a new purchase.
She installed a new shop bell so she could hear customers entering from the back room.
The sound of the shop bell made the owner look up from his book.
