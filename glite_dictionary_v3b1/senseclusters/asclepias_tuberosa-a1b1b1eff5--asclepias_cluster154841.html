Asclepias tuberosa

noun
a North American plant with orange flowers; butterfly weed
9.6
Pronunciation: / əˈskliːpiəs tuːˈbəroʊsə / (AmE), / əsˈkliːpiəs tjuːˈbɛrəsə / (BrE)
a perennial plant native to eastern and southern United States, known for its bright orange flowers and attraction to butterflies; commonly called butterfly weed
