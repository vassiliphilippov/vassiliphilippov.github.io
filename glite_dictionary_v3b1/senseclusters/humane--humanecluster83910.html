humane

adjective
showing compassion and consideration
5.2
Pronunciation: / hjuˈmeɪn / (AmE), / hjʊˈmeɪn / (BrE)
showing compassion, kindness, or consideration for others, especially to reduce suffering

Examples

A humane attitude
Her humane attitude inspired others to treat everyone with greater respect.
The shelter uses humane methods to care for animals in need.
We need a more humane approach to prison reform.
Many activists campaign for more humane treatment of prisoners.
Putting down injured wildlife in a humane way is sometimes necessary to prevent further suffering.
The organization works hard to ensure that animals are treated in a humane manner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • antonym: inhumane - lacking compassion or kindness
