Synonyms

  • upended
    4.4 0.00/m
    upended means tipped with the lower end now upper or standing on end, while overturned means turned fully upside down.
  • upturned
    4.5 0.01/m
    upturned describes something whose bottom now faces up, while overturned describes an object with top and bottom swapped.
  • upside down
    2.0 6/m
    upside down means the top lies where the bottom should be, while overturned implies a full flip to that position.
  • inverted
    4.8 0.00/m
    inverted is a formal term for placing something with top and bottom reversed, while overturned is more common and physical.
  • overset
    7.0 0.00/m

  • upset
    2.5 0.4/m
