drastically

adverb
in an extreme or severe way
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdræstɪkli / (AmE), / ˈdrɑːstɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that is likely to have a strong or far-reaching effect; extremely or severely.

Examples

Change drastically
After changing her diet, her health improved drastically within a few months.
The company had to drastically reduce its workforce to survive the economic crisis.
The temperature drops drastically at night in the desert.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mildly - to a moderate or slight degree; not severely
  • synonym: severely - in an intense or harsh manner
