revoltingly

adverb
in a disgusting or offensive manner
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvoʊltɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈvɒltɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes extreme disgust or aversion; in a way that is offensive or repulsive.

Examples

To act revoltingly bad
She found the movie revoltingly violent and left before it ended.
The food was revoltingly greasy, making it impossible to eat more than a few bites.
The kitchen was revoltingly dirty after the wild party last night.
