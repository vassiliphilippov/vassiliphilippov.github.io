German Luftwaffe

noun
German air force, especially WWII
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɜrmən ˈlʊftˌvɑfə / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈlʊftˌvafə / (BrE)
the air force of Germany, especially the official air service of Nazi Germany during World War II or the modern German air force

Examples

The German Luftwaffe
During the Cold War, the German Luftwaffe was reestablished as part of West Germany's defense forces.
Many historic aircraft from the German Luftwaffe are now displayed in aviation museums.
The German Luftwaffe conducted extensive bombing campaigns during World War II.
