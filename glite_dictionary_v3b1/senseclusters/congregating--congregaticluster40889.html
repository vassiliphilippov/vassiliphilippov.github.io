congregating

noun
act of gathering into a group
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋɡrəˌɡeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋɡrɪˌɡeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of gathering together as a group, usually for a particular purpose

Examples

People congregating together
Congregating in large numbers was prohibited during the outbreak to prevent the spread of disease.
Excessive congregating near the entrance caused a bottleneck during the event.
In some cultures, congregating in public squares is a long-standing tradition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dispersing - the act of scattering or breaking up a group
  • synonym: gathering - an assembly or meeting, especially a social or festive one
