telethermometer

noun
instrument indicating remote temperature
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛlɪθərˈmɒmɪtər / (AmE), / ˌtɛlɪθəˈmɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument for measuring and indicating temperature at a distance from the point of measurement

Examples

A telethermometer device
Each telethermometer in the factory sends readings back to a central control panel for safety.
The laboratory installed a telethermometer to monitor the temperature in the high-pressure chamber remotely.
Using a telethermometer, scientists tracked subtle temperature changes deep within the glacier.
