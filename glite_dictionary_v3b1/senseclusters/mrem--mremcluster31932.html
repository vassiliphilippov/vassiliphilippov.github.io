mrem

noun
millirem; unit of radiation dosage
Pronunciation: / ɛm ˌrɛm / (AmE), / ɛm ˌrɛm / (BrE)
a unit of absorbed ionizing radiation dosage equal to one thousandth of a rem

Examples

One mrem dose
Routine dental X-rays typically expose patients to less than 1 mrem each.
The radiation leak resulted in a dose of 200 mrem to the affected residents.
Workers are limited to a maximum exposure of 5,000 mrem per year.
