fordo

verb
to exhaust completely
9.9
Pronunciation: /fɑːr.ˈduː/ /; / /fɔːr.ˈduː/ (AmE), /fɔː.ˈduː/ (BrE)
to exhaust or wear out completely, often through overwork or hardship (archaic or literary)

Examples

To fordo oneself
The endless labor would fordo even the most resilient workers by nightfall.
She feared the journey would fordo her before she reached her destination.
Such heavy burdens can fordo a person both in body and spirit.
