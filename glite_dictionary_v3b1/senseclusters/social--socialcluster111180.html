social

adjective
involving companionship with others
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.ʃəl /; / ˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.ʃəl /; / ˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
involving friendly gatherings or activities where people meet and interact

Examples

Social event
He prefers a social evening with neighbors to spending time alone.

She volunteers at a social club for retirees that organizes weekly get-togethers.
The hotel offers several social spaces where guests can mingle and relax.
They renovated the old church into a social hall for community events.
