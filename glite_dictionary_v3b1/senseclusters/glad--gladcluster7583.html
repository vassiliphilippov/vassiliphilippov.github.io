glad

adjective
willing or eager to help
Pronunciation: / ɡlæd / (AmE), / ɡlæd /; / ɡlad / (BrE)
feeling or showing pleasure, happiness, or cheerfulness; pleased, delighted, or eager

Examples

Feel glad about news
He's glad to answer any questions you may have about the report.
I'm always glad to offer my assistance when someone needs help.
She was glad to stay late and finish the project with her team.
He was glad of the chance to help his friend.
I was glad of your advice when I was unsure what to do.
They were glad of the shelter during the storm.
A glad songbird greeted us as we entered the bright meadow.
The children ran through the glad sunlight of the spring morning.
The garden looked particularly glad after the long, gloomy winter.
