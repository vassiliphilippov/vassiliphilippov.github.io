Synonyms

  • happy

    happy is a steady feeling of joy or contentment, while glad often shows a quick or polite pleasure about something.
  • pleased
    1.0 41/m
    pleased expresses satisfaction about something specific or polite approval, while glad can be more general and warmer in feeling.
  • delighted
    1.0 9.32/m
    delighted shows very strong pleasure or excitement, while glad is milder and often used for simple or polite happiness.
