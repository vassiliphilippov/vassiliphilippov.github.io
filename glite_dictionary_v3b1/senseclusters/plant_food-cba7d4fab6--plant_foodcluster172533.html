plant food

noun
substance providing nutrients for plants
3.9 2.8
Pronunciation: / plænt fuːd / (AmE), / plɑːnt fuːd / (BrE)
a substance added to soil or water to provide essential nutrients to plants and promote their growth

Examples

This liquid plant food is specially formulated for flowering plants.
