wall panel

noun
wall-mounted electronic control panel
3.4
Pronunciation: / wɔl ˈpænəl / (AmE), / wɔːl ˈpænəl / (BrE)
A wall-mounted electronic control panel or user interface used to operate building or home systems such as lighting, heating, security, or automation.

Examples
