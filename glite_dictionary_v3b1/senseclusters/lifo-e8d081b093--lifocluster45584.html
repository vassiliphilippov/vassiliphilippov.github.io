LIFO

noun
inventory accounting method: last in, first out
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.foʊ/ /; / /ˈlaɪ.foʊ/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪ.fəʊ/ /; / /ˈliː.fəʊ/ (BrE)
a method where the last item added is the first to be removed or used, especially in accounting (inventory valuation) and computing (data structures)
