blood typing

noun
identifying a person's blood group
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / blʌd ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of determining a person's blood group using serological methods

Examples

Blood typing test
Blood typing is essential in emergency situations to avoid incompatible donations.
Before her surgery, the hospital performed blood typing to ensure a safe transfusion.
Modern blood typing techniques are more accurate than those used in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood group - a category of blood based on the presence or absence of antigens; also called blood type
