congruity

noun
state of being in agreement or harmony
4.1
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːəti / (AmE), / kɒŋˈɡruːɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being in agreement or harmony; appropriateness or suitability

Examples

The congruity of design
She appreciated the congruity of the artwork with the surrounding environment.
The congruity between his words and actions earned him great respect.
There is a pleasing congruity in the design of the new building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmony - agreement or concord
  • antonym: incongruity - the state of being out of place or not in harmony
