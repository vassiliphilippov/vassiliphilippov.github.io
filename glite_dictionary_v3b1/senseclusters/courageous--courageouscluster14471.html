Examples

A courageous decision
Despite her fear, she made a courageous decision to speak out against injustice.
Firefighters are often praised for their courageous actions during emergencies.
He showed a courageous spirit by volunteering for the dangerous mission.

Synonyms

  • brave
    1.0 25/m
    brave means willing to face danger or pain in everyday situations, while courageous can sound more formal or general.
  • heroic
    3.8 7.50/m
    heroic describes great, public acts of courage under pressure, while courageous covers the general ability to face danger or pain.
  • valorous
    8.8 0.14/m
    valorous sounds formal and praises great bravery in danger, while courageous is a common word for facing fear.
  • gallant
    5.6 0.50/m
    gallant often feels old-fashioned or shows bold charm, while courageous simply means willing to face danger or pain.
  • stouthearted
    8.2 0.06/m
    stouthearted stresses steady courage and strong will in long trouble, while courageous is more general everyday bravery.

  • synonym: fearless - lacking fear; brave; bold
