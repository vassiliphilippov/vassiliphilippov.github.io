Synonyms

  • join
    1.0 59/m
    join brings things or people together directly, while link names a simple connection between two or more items.
  • connect
    1.1 20/m
    connect joins parts so they form a working whole, while link means a basic tie or join between two or more things.
  • conjoin
    5.9 1.47/m
    conjoin unites things often in a formal or literary way, while link is the common, neutral term for joining objects.
  • couple
    3.1 13/m
    couple links or pairs two items together, often as a pair, while link covers general joining of several things.
  • attach
    2.5 39/m
    attach fastens one thing to another, usually physically, while link simply means joining or connecting in many ways.
  • interconnect
    4.7 0.54/m
    interconnect makes many parts work together as a system, while link names a single or simple connection between items.
  • interlink
    4.5 0.48/m
    interlink ties things so they depend on each other, while link refers to a plain connection without implying interdependence.
  • tie
    1.0 40/m
    tie fastens things with rope, string, or knot, while link means a broader joining or connection not limited to knots.
