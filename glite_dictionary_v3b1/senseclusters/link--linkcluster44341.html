link

verb
to join or connect
1.0
Pronunciation: / lɪŋk / (AmE), / lɪŋk / (BrE)
to join or connect two or more things together

Examples

To link two ideas

The two villages will be linked by a new bridge next year.
She linked her arm with his as they walked down the street.
The new bridge links the island to the mainland.
The two scientists linked their research projects to achieve better results.
