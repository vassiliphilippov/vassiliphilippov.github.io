Synonyms

  • negotiation
    3.5 44/m
    negotiation is a formal process of discussing terms to reach agreement, while bargaining often refers to making deals for better terms.
  • haggling
    3.4 0.59/m
    haggling is a direct back and forth over price in shops or markets, while bargaining can cover many kinds of deal discussions.
  • horse trading
    5.2 0.18/m
    horse trading is a tough give and take where both sides make big concessions, while bargaining can be gentler or more routine in everyday deals.
  • dealmaking
    4.1 0.15/m
    dealmaking is the activity of arranging and closing business agreements, while bargaining focuses on discussing terms to secure better prices or conditions.
  • talks
    2.3 0.19/m
    talks are formal discussions between groups or governments to reach decisions, while bargaining usually refers to direct give and take in deals or prices.
