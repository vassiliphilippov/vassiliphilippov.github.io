ultimate frisbee

noun
a team sport played with a flying disc
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltəmət frɪzˌbi / (AmE), / ˈʌltɪmət ˈfrɪzbi / (BrE)
A team sport played with a flying disc where players score by passing the disc to teammates and catching it in the opposing team's end zone. Possession changes when the disc is intercepted, hits the ground, or goes out of bounds.

Examples

Playing ultimate frisbee game
Learning the rules of ultimate frisbee was easy, but mastering the throws took time.
She plays ultimate frisbee every weekend with her friends at the local park.
The college team won the regional ultimate frisbee tournament last spring.
