confetti

noun
small pieces of colored paper thrown at celebrations
8.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛti / (AmE), / kənˈfɛti / (BrE)
Small pieces or streamers of colored paper that are thrown into the air on festive occasions, such as weddings or parties.

Examples

Throw confetti at weddings
After the ceremony, guests showered the newlyweds with confetti as they exited the church.
Children laughed and tossed confetti into the air during the birthday party.
The parade was lively, with colorful confetti falling from the rooftops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: streamer - a long, narrow strip of material used as a decoration
