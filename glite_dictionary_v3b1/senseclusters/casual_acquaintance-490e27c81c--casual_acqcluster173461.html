casual acquaintance

noun
a person one knows slightly
Pronunciation: / ˈkæʒuəl əˈkweɪntəns / (AmE), / ˈkæʒjʊəl əˈkweɪntəns / (BrE)
a person whom one knows slightly but who is not a close friend

Examples

A casual acquaintance
Although we spoke at the conference, he remained only a casual acquaintance.
He's just a casual acquaintance from my gym, not someone I see outside of workouts.
I sometimes run into a casual acquaintance at the coffee shop on my way to work.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: acquaintance - a person known to one, but not a close friend
  • degree higher: friend - a person with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
