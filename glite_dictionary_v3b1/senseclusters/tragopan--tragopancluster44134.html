tragopan

noun
an Asian pheasant with fleshy head processes
Pronunciation: / ˈtræɡəˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈtræɡəpæn / (BrE)
a brightly colored Asian pheasant of the genus Tragopan, distinguished by fleshy wattles and two horn-like processes on the head, found in forested mountain regions

The male tragopan
The male tragopan displays its colorful wattles and horns during courtship rituals.
