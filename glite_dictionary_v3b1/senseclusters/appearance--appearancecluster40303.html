appearance

noun
formal attendance in court (legal)
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪrəns / (AmE), / əˈpɪərəns / (BrE)
the act of formally attending a court or hearing as a party in a legal case

Examples

Court appearance
Failure to make a formal appearance in court can result in a default judgment.
Her appearance before the judge marked the beginning of the trial.
The lawyer entered an appearance on behalf of his client at the hearing.
