pinsetter

noun
bowling pin arranger
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌsɛtə / (BrE)
a machine or person that arranges bowling pins in their correct positions for the next frame

Examples

A pinsetter machine
The old bowling alley still uses a human pinsetter instead of a machine.
After the strike, the pinsetter quickly arranged the pins for the next player.
Modern pinsetters have made bowling games much faster and more efficient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bowling alley attendant - a person who assists with setting up pins in a bowling alley
  • synonym: mechanical pinsetter - a machine that automatically sets up bowling pins
