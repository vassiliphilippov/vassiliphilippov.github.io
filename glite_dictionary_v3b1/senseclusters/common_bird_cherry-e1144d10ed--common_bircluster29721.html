common bird cherry

noun
European cherry tree (Prunus padus) with white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən bɝd ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən bɜːd ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A small European cherry tree (Prunus padus) resembling the American chokecherry, known for its clusters of fragrant white flowers and small black fruit.
