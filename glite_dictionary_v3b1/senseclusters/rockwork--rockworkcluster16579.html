rockwork

noun
decorative rock arrangement
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːkˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈrɒkˌwɜːk / (BrE)
an arrangement of rocks, either natural or artificial, used for decoration or construction, especially in gardens or landscapes

Examples

Garden rockwork feature
The landscaper designed a beautiful rockwork feature with a small pond and waterfall.
Visitors admired the intricate rockwork along the garden path, which blended naturally with the plants.
The old estate's rockwork included arches and terraces built from local stone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masonry - the craft of building with stone, brick, or concrete
  • synonym: rock garden - a garden featuring carefully arranged rocks and plants
