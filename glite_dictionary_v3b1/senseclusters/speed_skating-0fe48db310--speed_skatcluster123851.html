speed skating

noun
competitive ice skating race
4.5
Pronunciation: / spiːd ˈskeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / spiːd ˈskeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
A competitive sport in which individuals race on ice skates, typically around an oval track, aiming for the fastest time.

Examples

Speed skating race
He won a gold medal in speed skating at the Winter Olympics.
Many athletes train year-round to improve their speed skating performance.
The city built a new oval track for speed skating competitions.
