photometrist

noun
specialist in measuring light
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊ.təˈmɛ.trɪst / (AmE), / ˌfəʊ.təˈmɛ.trɪst / (BrE)
a person who specializes in or practices photometry, the measurement of light intensity

Examples

A photometrist measures light
As a photometrist, she collaborated with astronomers to analyze the luminosity of distant stars.
Each photometrist in the laboratory maintained meticulous records of their findings.
The photometrist carefully adjusted the instruments to ensure accurate measurements of the light source.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photometry - the science of measuring light intensity
