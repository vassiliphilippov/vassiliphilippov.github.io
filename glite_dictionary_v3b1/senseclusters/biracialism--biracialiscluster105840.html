biracialism

noun
state of two races
7.5
Pronunciation: / baɪˈreɪʃəlɪzəm / (AmE), / baɪˈreɪʃəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the state or condition of involving, relating to, or identifying with two different races, especially as part of a person's identity or experience
