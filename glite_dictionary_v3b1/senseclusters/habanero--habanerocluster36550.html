habanero

noun
very hot chili pepper
Pronunciation: / ˌhɑːbəˈnɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˌhabəˈnɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a small, very hot chili pepper commonly used to add heat to foods

Examples

Habanero pepper
The chef used a single habanero to make the sauce extra spicy.
Be careful when handling habaneros, as their heat can irritate your skin.
She grew habanero plants in her garden for homemade hot sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chili pepper - a spicy fruit used to add heat to food
  • degree higher: ghost pepper - an extremely hot chili pepper
  • degree lower: jalapeño - a medium-hot chili pepper used in Mexican cuisine
