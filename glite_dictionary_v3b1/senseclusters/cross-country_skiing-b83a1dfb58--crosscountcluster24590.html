cross-country skiing

noun
skiing across country over long distances
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɔsˈkʌntri ˈskiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌkrɒsˈkʌntri ˈskiːɪŋ / (BrE)
the sport or activity of skiing over long distances across open countryside, rather than down slopes

Examples

Cross-country skiing training
He trains for biathlons by practicing cross-country skiing every winter.
I bought new equipment specially designed for cross-country skiing through forest trails.
The Olympics feature several cross-country skiing events for both men and women.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downhill skiing - the sport of skiing down slopes, typically at a ski resort
