sociologism

noun
priority of social structures
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsiˈɑːlədʒɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiˈɒlədʒɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief, doctrine, or practice of explaining things mainly or only by social or sociological factors, often ignoring individual or other influences
