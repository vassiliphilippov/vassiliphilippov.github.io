scaphoid

adjective
boat-shaped; shaped like a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈskæfɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈskæfɔɪd / (BrE)
Having a shape resembling a boat; boat-shaped, especially in reference to certain bones in the body.

Examples

A scaphoid bone
Some shells have a distinctly scaphoid form, curving gentle like the hull of a small boat.
The scaphoid bones in the wrist are named for their boat-like shape.
The doctor pointed out the scaphoid contour of the bone on the X-ray image.
