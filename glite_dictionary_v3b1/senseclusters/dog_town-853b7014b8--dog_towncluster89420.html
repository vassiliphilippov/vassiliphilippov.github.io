dog town

noun
place with many dogs or dog-friendly features
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɔɡ taʊn / (AmE), / dɒɡ taʊn / (BrE)
A place where dogs are especially numerous or where accommodations for dogs and their owners are plentiful.

Examples

A dog town park
After adopting our puppy, we searched for a dog town where he would have plenty of playmates.
Living in a dog town means you'll bump into friendly dogs and owners wherever you go.
This city is a true dog town, with parks and shops catering to every canine need.
