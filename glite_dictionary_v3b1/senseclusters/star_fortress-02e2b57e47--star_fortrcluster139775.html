star fortress

noun
fortification with triangular bastions
9.9 0.00/m

A fortification with multiple projecting triangular bastions, typically five to eight, designed so that each bastion covers the adjacent ones, originating in the mid-15th century in Italy.

Examples

A star fortress design
Historians often study the architectural features of the star fortress when exploring Renaissance military innovations.
Many star fortresses from the 16th century still exist in parts of Europe today.
The city was protected by an imposing star fortress with eight bastions facing outward.
