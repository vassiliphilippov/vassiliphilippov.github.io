intercostal

adjective
situated between the ribs
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈkɑstl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈkɒstl̩ / (BrE)
Situated or occurring between the ribs, especially referring to muscles, nerves, or spaces in human or animal anatomy.

Examples

Intercostal nerves
Intercostal nerves transmit signals between the spinal cord and the muscles of the chest.
She experienced pain in the intercostal space after her injury.
The doctor explained that the intercostal muscles play a vital role in breathing.
