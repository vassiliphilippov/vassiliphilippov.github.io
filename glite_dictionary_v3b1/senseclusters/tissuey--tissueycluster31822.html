tissuey

adjective
like biological tissue
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃuːi / (AmE), / ˈtɪʃuːi / (BrE)
having a texture or appearance similar to biological tissue, especially soft and flexible like muscle or skin.

Examples

Tissuey skin
The material felt surprisingly tissuey, almost like real skin.
Her sculpture used a tissuey fabric to mimic the look of muscle.
The sample was more tissuey than expected, making it difficult to cut cleanly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleshy - having a soft, thick, and pulpy texture like flesh
  • antonym: solid - firm and not soft or yielding in texture
