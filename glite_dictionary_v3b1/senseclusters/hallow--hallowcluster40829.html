hallow

verb
make holy by religious rites
Pronunciation: / ˈhæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈhaləʊ / (BrE)
to make holy or sacred, often by means of a religious ceremony

Examples

To hallow a place
Each year, the villagers hallow the river during their traditional festival.
The ancient priests would hallow the temple with sacred chants and rituals.
The land was hallowed by centuries of worship and pilgrimage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bless - to consecrate or make holy by a religious rite
  • antonym: desecrate - to violate or treat something sacred with disrespect
