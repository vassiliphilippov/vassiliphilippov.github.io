ritualism

noun
excessive emphasis on rituals in worship
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪz(ə)m / (AmE), / ˈrɪtjʊəlɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
excessive or undue emphasis on rituals or formal procedures, especially in religious worship

Examples

Church ritualism
Opponents argued that ritualism in the church distracted from its moral teachings.
Some churches have been criticized for their ritualism and lack of genuine spiritual engagement.
The ceremony was filled with so much ritualism that the original meaning seemed lost.
