etamin

noun
open-mesh cotton or worsted fabric
Pronunciation: / ˌɛtəˈmiːn / (AmE), / ˌɛtəˈmiːn / (BrE)
A soft cotton or worsted fabric with an open mesh, used especially for curtains or clothing.

Examples

White etamin curtains
She chose white etamin curtains to give her living room a light and airy feel.

Traditional costumes often use brightly colored etamin for their vibrant appearance.
