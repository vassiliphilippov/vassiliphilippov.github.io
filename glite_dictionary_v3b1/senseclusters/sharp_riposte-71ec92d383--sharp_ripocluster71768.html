sharp riposte

noun
quick, witty reply
Pronunciation: / ʃɑrp ɹɪˈpoʊst / (AmE), / ʃɑːp rɪˈpɒst / (BrE)
A quick and witty reply, especially one that counters a criticism or insult.

Examples

A sharp riposte
He always has a sharp riposte ready during debates.
Her sharp riposte silenced the critic instantly.
The comedian's sharp riposte drew laughter from the crowd.

Related Terms

  • synonym: retort - a quick, witty, or sharp reply
