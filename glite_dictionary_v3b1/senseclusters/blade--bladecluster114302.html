blade

noun
metal part of an ice skate
4.2
Pronunciation: / bleɪd / (AmE), / bleɪd / (BrE)
the flat, sharp part of a tool, weapon, or machine used for cutting (such as a knife, sword, razor, or ice skate)

Examples

Knife blade
After hours of use, the blade had become dull and needed replacing.
She sharpened the blade of her ice skates before the big competition.
The blade of his skate broke during practice, forcing him to stop.
Be careful not to touch the blade because it is extremely sharp.
The chef sharpened the blade before slicing the vegetables.
The knife's blade glinted in the sunlight.
During the duel, her blade clashed with her opponent's in a shower of sparks.
He admired the intricate design on the hilt of the ancient blade.
The knight drew his blade and prepared to defend the castle.
