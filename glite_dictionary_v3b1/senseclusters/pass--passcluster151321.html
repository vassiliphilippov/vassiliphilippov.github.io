pass

noun
aircraft flying over target
Pronunciation: / pæs / (AmE), / pɑːs / (BrE)
an act of flying or moving an aircraft over a target or specific area, often as part of a mission or operation

Examples

A bombing pass
After the initial bombing pass, the jets circled for another approach.
Each pass of the surveillance plane provided new photographs of the area.
The pilot made a low pass over the airfield before turning back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flyby - a flight past a point, especially in space, or over a target
  • synonym: run - a single movement or operation, such as a pass over a target
