religious belief

noun
belief in supernatural powers or religion
1.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs bɪˈliːf / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs bɪˈliːf / (BrE)
A strong conviction in the existence of a supernatural power or powers that influence human destiny.

Examples

Strong religious belief
Her religious belief guided her decisions throughout her life.
Many wars have been fought over differences in religious belief.
The school encourages respect for every student's religious belief.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atheism - lack of belief in any god or gods
  • synonym: faith - strong trust or confidence in religious teachings
