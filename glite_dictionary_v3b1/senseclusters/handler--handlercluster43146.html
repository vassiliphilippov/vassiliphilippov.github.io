handler

noun
person who manages or controls
Pronunciation: / ˈhændlər / (AmE), / ˈhændlə / (BrE)
a person who manages, directs, or is responsible for someone or something, especially in politics, entertainment, or security.

Examples

Celebrity handler
Her celebrity handler made sure the schedule ran smoothly.
Security handlers coordinated the movement of VIPs at the event.
The senator's handler arranged all of his interviews during the campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - person who acts on behalf of someone else
  • synonym: manager - person who manages people or activities
