rosewater

noun
floral-scented liquid
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzwɔːtə / (BrE)
a fragrant liquid made by steeping rose petals in water, used in cooking, cosmetics, and perfumes

Examples

A bottle of rosewater
She sprinkled a little rosewater on the cake to give it a delicate aroma.
Traditional skincare routines often include rosewater as a gentle toner.
The recipe calls for a teaspoon of rosewater to enhance the flavor of the pudding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orange flower water - a fragrant water made from orange blossoms, used in cooking and cosmetics
  • synonym: perfume - a fragrant liquid typically used to give a pleasant smell to the body or objects
