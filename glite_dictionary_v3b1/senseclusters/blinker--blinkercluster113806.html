blinker

verb
put blinders on a horse
4.2 9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkə / (BrE)
to put blinders on (a horse) to prevent it from seeing to the side and becoming distracted or frightened

Examples

To blinker a horse
Some jockeys prefer not to blinker their mounts, believing it keeps them more alert.
The trainer decided to blinker the nervous horse before the race.
When you blinker a horse, its field of vision narrows to help it focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blinder - a pair of leather flaps attached to a horse’s bridle to limit vision to the side
