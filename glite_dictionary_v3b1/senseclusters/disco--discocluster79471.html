disco

noun
1970s dance music genre
1.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪs.koʊ/ (AmE), /ˈdɪs.kəʊ/ (BrE)
A style of popular dance music, especially from the late 1970s, characterized by a regular bass beat and melodic tunes, intended mainly for dancing at discotheques.

Examples

Disco music
Classic disco tracks from the 1970s are still played at parties today.
He loves listening to disco while getting ready to go out.
The club played a mix of electronic and disco music all night.
