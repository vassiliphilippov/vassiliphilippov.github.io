European black grouse

noun

Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən blæk ɡraʊs / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən blæk ɡraʊs / (BrE)
a large northern European grouse species, black in color, with a distinctive lyre-shaped tail
