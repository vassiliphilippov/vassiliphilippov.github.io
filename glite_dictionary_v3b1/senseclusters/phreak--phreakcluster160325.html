phreak

verb
to hack phone systems
Pronunciation: / friːk / (AmE), / friːk / (BrE)
to hack or manipulate telephone or telecommunications systems, often to make free calls or explore their operation

Examples

To phreak phone lines
In the 1980s, some teenagers learned how to phreak payphones to make free long-distance calls.
He used special equipment to phreak into the telephone network and study how it worked.
Authorities warned that attempts to phreak modern digital systems are illegal and can be prosecuted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hack - to gain unauthorized access to computer or electronic systems
  • antonym: secure - to protect a system from unauthorized access
