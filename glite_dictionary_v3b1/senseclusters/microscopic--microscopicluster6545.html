microscopic

adjective
relating to microscopy
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪ.krəˈskɑː.pɪk / (AmE), / ˌmɪ.krəˈskɒ.pɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or used in microscopy or the use of a microscope.

Examples

Microscopic examination
A microscopic examination revealed the presence of bacteria invisible to the naked eye.
Scientists rely on microscopic techniques to study cells and microorganisms in detail.
The doctor performed a microscopic analysis of the tissue sample to look for abnormalities.
