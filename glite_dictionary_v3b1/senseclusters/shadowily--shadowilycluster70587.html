shadowily

adverb
in a shadowy way
Pronunciation: / ˈʃædoʊɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃædəʊɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is dark, indistinct, or filled with shadows

Examples

Shadowily lit room
The old painting was shadowily illuminated by the flickering candle.
Figures moved shadowily in the misty alley as the night grew darker.
She remembered the room shadowily, with only a sliver of moonlight coming through the window.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightly - in a way that is full of light
  • synonym: dimly - in a way that is not bright or clear
