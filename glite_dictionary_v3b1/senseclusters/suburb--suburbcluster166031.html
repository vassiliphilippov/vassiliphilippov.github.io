suburb

noun
residential area on city outskirts
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌb.ɝb / (AmE), / ˈsʌb.ɜːb / (BrE)
An area on the edge of a city or large town, typically consisting of residential houses and often forming a distinct community.

Examples

The suburbs outside city
Many people commute from the suburbs into the city for work every day.
Property prices in this suburb have risen significantly in the past five years.
They moved to a quiet suburb to raise their children away from the hustle of city life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: city - a large town or urban area
  • antonym: downtown - the central part of a city
  • degree higher: exurb - a district outside a city, beyond the suburbs, often more rural
  • synonym: neighborhood - a district, especially one forming a community within a town or city
