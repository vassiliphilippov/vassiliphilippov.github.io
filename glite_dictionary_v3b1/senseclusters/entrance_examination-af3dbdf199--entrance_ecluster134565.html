entrance examination

noun
exam for admission to studies
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntrəns ˌɛɡzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɛntrəns ˌɛɡzæmɪˈneɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
an examination set to determine if a candidate is qualified to enter a school or course of studies

Examples

Pass an entrance examination
Most medical schools require candidates to pass an entrance examination before admission.
She spent months preparing for the entrance examination to get into her preferred university.
Thousands of students will sit for the national entrance examination this weekend.

Related Terms
