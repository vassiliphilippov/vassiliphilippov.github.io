alarm clock

noun
device for waking at a set time
1.5
Pronunciation: / əˈlɑrm klɑk / (AmE), / əˈlɑːm klɒk / (BrE)
a clock that is designed to wake someone at a set time by making a sound or other signal

Examples

A alarm clock rings
My alarm clock went off at 6 a.m., waking me from a deep sleep.
She forgot to set her alarm clock and missed her meeting.
The new alarm clock has a gentle chime instead of a loud bell.
