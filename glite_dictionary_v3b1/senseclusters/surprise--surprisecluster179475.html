surprise

noun
sudden unexpected event
2.0
Pronunciation: / sərˈpraɪz / (AmE), / səˈpraɪz / (BrE)
An event or occurrence that happens suddenly and unexpectedly.

Examples

A birthday surprise
He planned a small surprise for her birthday.
The announcement came as a surprise to all employees.
Winning the competition was a complete surprise to everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expectation - a belief that something will happen
  • synonym: shock - a sudden upsetting or startling experience
