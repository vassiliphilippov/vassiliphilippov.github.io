toss

noun
act of throwing the ball to a teammate (sports)
Pronunciation: / tɑs / (AmE), / tɒs / (BrE)
In sports, an act of throwing the ball to another member of your team, especially in a casual or short pass.

Examples

A quick toss
After a skillful toss, the team scored another point.
He made a quick toss to the running back during the play.
The quarterback's toss went straight to the receiver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pass - the act of throwing, kicking, or transferring the ball to a teammate
