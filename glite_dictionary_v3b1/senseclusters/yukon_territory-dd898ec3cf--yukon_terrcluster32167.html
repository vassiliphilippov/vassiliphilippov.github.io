Yukon Territory

proper noun
territory in northwestern Canada
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːkɑn ˌtɛrɪˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈjuːkɒn ˌtɛrɪˌtəri / (BrE)
a territory in northwestern Canada, known for its vast wilderness and as the site of the Klondike gold rush in the 1890s

Examples

The Yukon Territory gold rush
In the early 2000s, the Yukon Territory officially shortened its name to simply 'Yukon'.
Many prospectors flocked to the Yukon Territory during the Klondike gold rush in search of fortune.
The Yukon Territory is renowned for its natural beauty and abundant wildlife.
