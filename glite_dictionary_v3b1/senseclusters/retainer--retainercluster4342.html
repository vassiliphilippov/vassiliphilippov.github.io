retainer

noun
household servant or attendant
5.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈteɪnər / (AmE), / rɪˈteɪnə / (BrE)
a person who is employed to work in the service of another, especially as a servant in a household

Examples

Family retainer
In medieval times, a retainer might serve a single family for generations.
The retainer prepared the guest room before the master's arrival.
The nobleman called for his most trusted retainer to bring him the message.

Related Terms

  • synonym: servant - a person employed to perform domestic duties
