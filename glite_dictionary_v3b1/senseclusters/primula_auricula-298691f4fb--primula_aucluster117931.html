Primula auricula

noun
a yellow-flowered Alpine primrose
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪmjələ ɔːˈrɪkjələ / (AmE), / ˈprɪmjʊlə ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
A species of primrose with yellow flowers, native to the Alps and commonly cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Examples

Primula auricula yellow flowers
Botanists studied wild Primula auricula populations in the Swiss Alps.

The Primula auricula is prized by gardeners for its striking yellow flowers and hardiness.
