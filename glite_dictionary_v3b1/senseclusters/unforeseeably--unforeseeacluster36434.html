unforeseeably

adverb
unexpectedly
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnfɔrˈsiːəbli / (AmE), / ˌʌnfɔːˈsiːəbli / (BrE)

Examples

Canceled unforeseeably
The event was unforeseeably canceled due to a sudden power outage.
Their plans changed unforeseeably when the main speaker fell ill.
The market shifted unforeseeably after the unexpected announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted
  • synonym: unexpectedly - in a way that is not expected
