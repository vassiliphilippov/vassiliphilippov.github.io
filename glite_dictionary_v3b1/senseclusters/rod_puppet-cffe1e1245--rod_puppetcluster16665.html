rod puppet

noun
puppet operated by rods
Pronunciation: / rɑd ˈpʌpət / (AmE), / rɒd ˈpʌpɪt / (BrE)
a type of puppet operated by rods, with a central rod supporting the head and additional rods controlling the limbs

Examples

A rod puppet show
He spent weeks building an intricate rod puppet for the school performance.
Several characters in the production were brought to life using rod puppets.
The show featured a giant rod puppet operated by three skilled puppeteers.
