fender

noun
inclined metal frame on a locomotive
6.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfen.dər/ (AmE), /ˈfen.də/ (BrE)
a part of a vehicle (such as a car or locomotive) that covers and protects the wheel area or clears obstacles from the track
