foot traffic

noun
people moving on foot in a place
3.1 2.5
Pronunciation: /fʊt ˈtræf.ɪk/ (AmE), /fʊt ˈtræf.ɪk/ (BrE)
the movement or flow of people walking in a particular area, especially in relation to businesses or public spaces
