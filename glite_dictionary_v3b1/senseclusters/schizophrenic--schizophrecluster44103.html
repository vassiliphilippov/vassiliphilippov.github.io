schizophrenic

noun
person diagnosed with schizophrenia
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəʊˈfriːnɪk / (BrE)
a person who has schizophrenia, a serious mental disorder characterized by distorted thinking, perceptions, emotions, and behavior

Examples

A schizophrenic patient

Many schizophrenics benefit from a combination of medication and therapy.
The hospital provides specialized care for schizophrenics.
