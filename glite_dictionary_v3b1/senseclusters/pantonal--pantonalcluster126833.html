pantonal

adjective
using all keys
Pronunciation: / pænˈtoʊnəl / (AmE), / pænˈtəʊnəl / (BrE)
relating to music that uses all keys or tonalities without a central key

Examples

Pantonal music style
The composer's pantonal approach allowed for greater harmonic freedom in his symphony.
Many modern pieces are described as pantonal because they avoid a single key center.
Her latest album features several pantonal works that challenge traditional listeners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atonal - not based on any key or tonality
  • antonym: tonal - based on a central key or tonality
