dramaturge

noun
theater literary advisor
Pronunciation: / ˈdræməˌtɜrdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdræmətɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a theater professional who advises on scripts, play selection, and provides background or context for a production

During the production, the dramaturge collaborated with both the director and the actors.
