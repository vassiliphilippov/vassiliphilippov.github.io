dramaturge

noun
theater literary advisor
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdræ.mə.tɜːrʒ/ (AmE), /ˈdræ.mə.tɜːʒ/ (BrE)
a theater professional who advises on scripts, play selection, and provides background or context for a production
