stock-still

adjective, adverb
completely motionless
Pronunciation: / stɑkˈstɪl /; / stɑk ˈstɪl / (AmE), / stɒkˈstɪl /; / stɒk ˈstɪl / (BrE)
completely still or motionless; not moving at all
