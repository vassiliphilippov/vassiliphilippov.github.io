Synonyms

  • motionless
    3.0 2/m
    motionless is fully without movement in body or object, while stock-still emphasizes being utterly frozen with no tiny movement.
  • unmoving
    2.2 0.05/m
    unmoving describes something that does not move, often over time, while stock-still suggests a sudden, complete lack of movement.
  • still
    1.0 60/m
    still can mean quiet, calm, or not moving in general, while stock-still means absolutely motionless with no sound or movement.
  • stationary
    2.9 2/m
    stationary often describes something fixed in one place or not moving, while stock-still suggests being completely frozen and motionless.
