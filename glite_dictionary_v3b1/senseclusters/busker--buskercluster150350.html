busker

noun
street performer
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌskər / (AmE), / ˈbʌskə / (BrE)
a person who performs music, dance, or other entertainment in public places for voluntary donations from passers-by

Examples

A busker plays music
Every Saturday, a busker juggles and entertains children at the park.
The busker played lively tunes on his guitar, attracting a small crowd in the city square.
We gave a few coins to the talented busker singing near the subway entrance.
