allusion

noun
an indirect reference
4.9
Pronunciation: / əˈluːʒən / (AmE), / əˈluːʒən / (BrE)
an indirect or passing reference to something, often without a direct mention

Examples

A allusion to history
His speech made a subtle allusion to the recent political scandal.
The poem is full of literary allusion to ancient mythology.
Without directly naming her, he made an allusion to his former boss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reference - the act of mentioning or alluding to something
