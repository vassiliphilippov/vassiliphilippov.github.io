unindexed

adjective
not index-linked
7.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈɪn.dəkst/ /; / /ən.ˈɪn.dəkst/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈɪn.dəkst/ /; / /ən.ˈɪn.dəkst/ (BrE)
not linked to or adjusted according to an economic or price index, so the value does not change automatically with the index

Examples

Unindexed salary
The company offered unindexed salaries, which did not increase with inflation.
Many retirees struggled because their unindexed pensions lost value over time.
Unlike index-linked bonds, unindexed investments can lose purchasing power during periods of high inflation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fixed - not changing in amount or value
  • antonym: index-linked - adjusted automatically according to an economic or price index
