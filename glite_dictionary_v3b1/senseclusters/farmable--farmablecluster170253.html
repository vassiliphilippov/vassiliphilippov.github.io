farmable

adjective
suitable for farming
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrməbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməbəl / (BrE)
suitable for farming or able to be used to grow crops

Examples

Farmable land
The government is investing in making more land farmable to increase food production.
Only a small portion of the island is farmable due to its rocky terrain.
They bought a plot of farmable land to start their own organic farm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barren - unable to produce crops or vegetation
  • synonym: fertile - able to produce abundant crops
