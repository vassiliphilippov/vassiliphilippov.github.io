neuroendocrine

adjective
relating to nervous and endocrine systems
6.3 0.06/m

Examples

Neuroendocrine system function
Many cancers exhibit neuroendocrine features that affect hormone secretion.

The neuroendocrine system plays a crucial role in the body's response to stress.

Synonyms

  • neurohormonal
    7.3 0.02/m
    neurohormonal describes signals where nerves release hormones or affect hormone action, while neuroendocrine covers nervous and gland hormone systems together.
  • neurohumoral
    8.3 0.01/m
    neurohumoral describes chemical messages in body fluids that link nerves and hormones, while neuroendocrine refers to the nervous system working with hormone-producing glands.
