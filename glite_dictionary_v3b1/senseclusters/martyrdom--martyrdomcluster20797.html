martyrdom

noun
death or suffering for one's beliefs
5.3 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrtɚˌdəm / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.tə.dəm / (BrE)
the suffering or death of a person because of their adherence to a religious faith or cause

Examples

Religious martyrdom

Many religious traditions honor the martyrdom of their early followers.
The story of his martyrdom inspired others to continue the struggle for justice.
