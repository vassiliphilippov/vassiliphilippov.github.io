Lingala

noun
Central African language
9.5
Pronunciation: / lɪŋˈɡɑːlə / (AmE), / lɪŋˈɡɑːlə / (BrE)
a Bantu language spoken in parts of Central Africa, especially in the Democratic Republic of the Congo and the Republic of the Congo

Examples

Speak Lingala
Many people in Kinshasa speak Lingala as their first language.
She took a course to improve her Lingala before traveling to the Congo region.
Radio stations in the area often broadcast news in Lingala.

Related Terms
