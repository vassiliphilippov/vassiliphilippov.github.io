sapota

noun
tropical fruit with brown skin and sweet pulp
Pronunciation: / səˈpoʊtə / (AmE), / səˈpəʊtə / (BrE)
A tropical fruit with a rough brown skin and very sweet brownish pulp, commonly grown in South Asia and Central America.

Examples

A sapota fruit
She bought a basket of sapotas from the local market to make smoothies.
The ripe sapota has a distinctive caramel-like flavor that is enjoyed by many.
The tree in our garden produces dozens of sapota fruits every year.
