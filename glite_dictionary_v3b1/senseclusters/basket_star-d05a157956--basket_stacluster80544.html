basket star

noun
marine animal with branched arms
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskət stɑr / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt stɑː / (BrE)
a marine animal related to brittle stars, with long, slender, complexly branched arms radiating from a central disc
