inconstancy

noun
changeability; tendency to change often
6.3 6.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈkɑːn.stən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈkɒn.stən.siː/ (BrE)
the quality of being changeable, unreliable, or unfaithful, especially in feelings, opinions, or loyalty
