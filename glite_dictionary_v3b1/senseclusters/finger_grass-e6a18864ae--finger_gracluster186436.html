finger grass

noun
grass of the genus Chloris
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡər ˌɡræs /; / ˈfɪŋɡɚ ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a type of grass (genus Chloris) that often grows in grasslands or as a weed in lawns and poor soils

Examples

Gardeners often struggle to remove finger grass from flower beds and vegetable patches.
