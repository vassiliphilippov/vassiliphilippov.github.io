interplanetary

adjective
existing or occurring between planets
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈplænəˌtɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈplænɪtəri / (BrE)
existing, occurring, or carried on between planets.

Examples

Interplanetary travel
Advancements in technology may soon make interplanetary travel a reality.
She dreamed of exploring interplanetary space as an astronaut.
The scientists are planning an interplanetary mission to Mars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interstellar - existing or occurring between stars
  • degree lower: planetary - relating to or resembling a planet or the planets
