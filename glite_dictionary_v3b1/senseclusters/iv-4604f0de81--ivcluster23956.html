IV

noun
intravenous administration of fluids or nutrients
3.8 7.75/m
Pronunciation: / a…™ Ààvi / (AmE), / a…™ ÀàviÀê / (BrE)
a procedure in which fluids or nutrients are administered directly into a vein, often through a drip; short for intravenous injection or therapy

Examples

IV drip
Nurses checked the IV bag to ensure the medication was being delivered correctly.
She felt dizzy until the doctor started her on an IV drip.
The patient had to receive an IV to stay hydrated during surgery.

Synonyms

  • intravenous drip
    4.1 0.05/m
    an intravenous drip delivers fluids drop by drop into a vein, while IV refers to any intravenous therapy or injection.
  • infusion
    5.5 1.40/m
    an infusion slowly introduces fluids or medicine into the body, while IV usually names the intravenous route or drip.
  • intravenous injection
    4.1 0.07/m
    an intravenous injection pushes medicine into a vein at once, while IV often means ongoing intravenous therapy or drip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intravenous - existing or taking place within, or administered into, a vein
