rice bowl

noun
bowl of rice (dish) or means of livelihood
4.4
Pronunciation: / raɪs boʊl / (AmE), / raɪs bəʊl / (BrE)
A bowl containing rice served with various toppings; also used metaphorically to refer to a person's source of income or livelihood.

Examples

A rice bowl of food
Many workers feared losing their rice bowl during the factory closure.
She ordered a hearty rice bowl with grilled salmon for lunch.
The new restaurant specializes in customizable rice bowls with a variety of toppings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: livelihood - a means of securing the necessities of life
