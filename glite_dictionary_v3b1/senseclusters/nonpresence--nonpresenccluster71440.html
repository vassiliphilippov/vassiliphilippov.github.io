nonpresence

noun
lack of presence
9.6
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈprɛzəns / (AmE), / nɒnˈprɛzəns / (BrE)
the lack of noticeable personal or stage presence, resulting in little effect or impression on others

Examples

Nonpresence on stage
Despite his experience, the speaker's nonpresence made the audience lose interest quickly.
Her nonpresence on stage was surprising, as she is usually very charismatic.
The manager's nonpresence during meetings led to a lack of direction for the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absence - the state of not being present
  • antonym: presence - the state of being present and making an impression
