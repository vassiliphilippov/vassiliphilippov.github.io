classman

noun
competitor in same class
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈklæsmən / (AmE), / ˈklɑːsmən / (BrE)
a person who competes in the same class or category as another, especially in sports or competitions

Examples

Rival classman
He was determined to outperform his main classman in the final round of the tournament.
During the cycling event, each classman tried to gain an early lead.
She congratulated her classman after they both finished the race in the top three.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opponent - someone who competes against another in a contest
  • synonym: rival - a person competing for the same goal as another
  • antonym: teammate - a person on the same team
