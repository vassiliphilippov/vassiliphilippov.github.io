Makalu

proper noun
mountain in the Himalayas, Nepal
Pronunciation: / məˈkɑːluː / (AmE), / məˈkɑːluː / (BrE)
A mountain in the Himalayas, located in Nepal, which is the fifth highest mountain in the world at 8,485 meters (27,838 feet).

Examples

Mount Makalu
Mountaineers from around the world dream of climbing Makalu due to its challenging terrain.
Several expeditions have been launched to reach the summit of Makalu.
The peak of Makalu offers breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Everest - the highest mountain in the world, also in the Himalayas
  • synonym: K2 - the second-highest mountain in the world, in the Karakoram range
