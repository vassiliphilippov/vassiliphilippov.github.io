annual ring

noun
yearly growth ring in a tree or plant
4.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.juː.əl rɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.jʊ.əl rɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈæn.juː.əl rɪŋ/ /; / /ˈæn.jəl rɪŋ/ (BrE)

Examples

Tree annual rings
