annual ring

noun
yearly growth ring in a tree or plant
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ju.əl rɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl rɪŋ / (BrE)
a ring of wood produced each year in the trunk or stem of a tree or woody plant, used to determine its age

Examples

Tree annual rings
A drought can be identified in the narrow annual rings of some trees.
By counting the annual rings, scientists can estimate the age of a tree.
The annual ring pattern reveals the environmental conditions each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: growth ring - a ring in the wood marking a period of growth, typically a year
