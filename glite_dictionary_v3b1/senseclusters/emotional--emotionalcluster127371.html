emotional

adjective
pertaining to emotion
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənl /; / ɪˈmoʊʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənl /; / ɪˈməʊʃənl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or influenced by emotions or feelings, rather than by logic

Examples

Emotional health
After the accident, she needed time to recover her emotional health.
He went through an emotional crisis after losing his job.
Reading that letter was a deeply emotional experience for him.
Avoid making emotional judgments when dealing with complicated situations.
Her emotional reaction surprised those who expected a logical response.
The jury made an emotional decision instead of considering the evidence carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affective - relating to moods, feelings, and attitudes
  • antonym: rational - based on or in accordance with reason or logic
