face the truth

verb
accept or confront reality
Pronunciation: / feɪs ðə truθ / (AmE), / feɪs ðə truːθ / (BrE)
to accept or confront the reality of a situation, even if it is unpleasant or difficult

Examples

To face the truth bravely

Sometimes it's painful to face the truth about our own mistakes.
We have to face the truth if we want to move forward.
