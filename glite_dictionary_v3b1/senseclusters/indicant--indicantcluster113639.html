indicant

noun
indicator value from data
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪkənt / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪkənt / (BrE)
a number or ratio derived from observed data that indicates change or trends, especially over time

Examples

Economic indicant
Researchers tracked each indicant to measure the policy’s effectiveness.
Rising prices served as a key indicant of inflation over the past year.
The unemployment rate is an important indicant of economic health.
