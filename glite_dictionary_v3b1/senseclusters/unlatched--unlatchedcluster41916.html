unlatched

adjective
not fastened with a latch
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlætʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈlætʃt / (BrE)
not fastened or secured with a latch; left open or able to be opened easily

Examples

An unlatched gate
He pushed gently on the unlatched gate, and it swung open with a creak.
She noticed the unlatched window and quickly closed it before the storm arrived.
The barn door was left unlatched, so the animals wandered out during the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fastened - secured or held firmly in place
  • antonym: latched - fastened with a latch
  • synonym: unlocked - not locked; able to be opened without a key
