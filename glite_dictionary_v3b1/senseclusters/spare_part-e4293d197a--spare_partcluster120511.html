spare part

noun
extra component for machines
Pronunciation: / spɛr pɑrt / (AmE), / spɛə pɑːt / (BrE)
An additional component kept in reserve to replace a faulty or worn-out part in a machine or device.

Examples

A spare part for repair
Always keep a few spare parts on hand for emergency repairs.
It's wise to order spare parts before you actually need them.
The technician quickly replaced the faulty wire with a spare part.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replacement part - a part intended to replace a faulty or worn one
