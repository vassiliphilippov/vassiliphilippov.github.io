caffeine addict

noun
person addicted to caffeine
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfiːn ˌædɪkt /; / kæˌfiːn əˈdɪkt / (AmE), / ˈkæfiːn ˌadɪkt /; / kæˌfiːn əˈdɪkt / (BrE)
a person who is addicted to caffeine, usually by regularly drinking coffee, tea, or energy drinks

Examples

A caffeine addict drinks coffee
As a self-confessed caffeine addict, he can't start his day without at least two cups of coffee.
She realized she had become a caffeine addict when she started getting headaches without her morning tea.
The support group was formed to help caffeine addicts reduce their dependence in a healthy way.
As a recovering caffeine addict, he now avoids energy drinks entirely.
During finals week, many students turn into full-blown caffeine addicts.
My coworker is such a caffeine addict that she can't function without her morning coffee.
