later

adverb
at a future time; afterwards
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈleɪtə / (BrE)
at a time after now or after a previously mentioned or expected time

Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: afterward - after the time in question; subsequently
  • synonym: afterwards - at a later time; subsequently
  • degree lower: late - at or near the end of a period of time; after the expected time
  • degree higher: latest - superlative of 'late'; at the most advanced or final time
  • antonym: sooner - at an earlier time
