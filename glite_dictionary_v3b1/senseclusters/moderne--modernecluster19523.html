moderne

adjective
early 20th-century style
Pronunciation: / moʊˈdɛrn / (AmE), / məʊˈdɛːn / (BrE)
relating to a style of art, architecture, or design from the early 20th century, characterized by smooth lines and modern materials

Examples

Moderne architecture style
The museum's new wing features a distinctly moderne design with curved walls and glass blocks.
Her apartment is decorated in a moderne style, with chrome fixtures and bold geometric patterns.
Many theaters built in the 1930s have a moderne appearance that sets them apart from older buildings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: art deco - a decorative style popular in the 1920s and 1930s
  • antonym: traditional - following older styles or customs
