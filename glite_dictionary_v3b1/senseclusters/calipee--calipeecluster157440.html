calipee

noun
turtle shell gelatin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkælɪpi / (AmE), / ˈkælɪpi / (BrE)
the yellowish, gelatinous substance from the lower shell of a turtle, used as an ingredient in turtle soup

Examples

The calipee substance

Authentic turtle soup often contains both calipee and calipash for flavor and texture.
Collectors sometimes value calipee for its unique culinary properties in classic recipes.
