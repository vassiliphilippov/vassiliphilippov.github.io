galloper

noun
fast horse or rider
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləpər / (AmE), / ˈɡæləpə / (BrE)
a horse that moves at a fast pace or a person who rides quickly, especially to deliver messages

Examples

A fast galloper
The galloper sped across the battlefield to deliver the general's orders.
In old stories, a galloper would risk danger to bring news between towns.
The horse was known as a swift galloper among the cavalry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courier - a person who delivers messages or packages quickly
  • synonym: messenger - someone who carries and delivers messages
  • antonym: walker - a person who moves at a slow pace on foot
