sunlight

noun
light from the sun
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.laɪt / (BrE)
the light that comes from the sun and makes things bright during the day

Examples

Bright sunlight
After a week of rain, the sunlight finally broke through the clouds.
Plants need sunlight to produce food through photosynthesis.
The room was filled with sunlight streaming through the windows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunshine - bright sunlight, often with a connotation of pleasantness
