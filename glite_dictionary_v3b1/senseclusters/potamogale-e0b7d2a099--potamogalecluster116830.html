Potamogale

noun
genus of aquatic mammals (otter shrews)
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊ.tə.moʊˈɡeɪ.li / (AmE), / ˌpɒt.ə.məˈɡɑː.li / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic mammals in the family Potamogalidae, known as otter shrews, native to Central Africa.
