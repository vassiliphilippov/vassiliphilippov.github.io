unfinished

adjective
not having a surface finish
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃt / (BrE)
not having a protective or decorative surface finish such as paint, varnish, or polish, especially referring to wood or furniture.

Examples

Unfinished wood
Many people prefer unfinished wood because it has a natural appearance.
Please keep unfinished furniture away from moisture to prevent damage.
The carpenter delivered the table in an unfinished state so we could choose our own stain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - lacking a covering or finish
