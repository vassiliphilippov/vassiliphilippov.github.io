puppyhood

noun
time as a puppy
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpiˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈpʌpiˌhʊd / (BrE)
the period in a dog's life when it is a puppy

Examples

A dog's puppyhood
During puppyhood, dogs are especially curious and eager to explore their surroundings.
Proper training in puppyhood can help prevent behavioral problems later in life.
She often looked at old photos to remember her dog's playful puppyhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adulthood - the period of being an adult
  • synonym: childhood - the period of being a child
