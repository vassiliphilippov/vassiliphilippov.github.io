splashback

noun
protective wall panel
Pronunciation: / ˈsplæʃˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsplæʃˌbæk / (BrE)
a panel or surface installed behind a sink, stove, or countertop to protect the wall from water or food splashes

Examples

Kitchen splashback
The kitchen splashback was made of colorful tiles that matched the countertops.
After cooking, he noticed grease stains on the splashback behind the stove.
Installing a glass splashback makes cleaning up after meals much easier.
