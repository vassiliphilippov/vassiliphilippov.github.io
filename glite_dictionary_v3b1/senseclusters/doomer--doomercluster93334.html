doomer

noun
pessimistic person
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈduːmər / (AmE), / ˈduːmə / (BrE)
a person who is deeply pessimistic about the future and expects disaster or collapse

Examples

A doomer person
The doomer in the group always predicts the worst possible outcome.
Online forums often attract doomers who discuss potential global catastrophes.
Despite the doomer attitude, some people remain hopeful about the future.
After reading about climate change, he started to see himself as a doomer who expected the worst.
Online communities of doomers often share dark humor and pessimistic memes about the future.
She distanced herself from doomer attitudes to focus on positive change in her life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gloom - a feeling of deep sadness or pessimism
  • antonym: optimist - a person who tends to be hopeful and confident about the future
  • synonym: pessimist - a person who tends to see the worst aspect of things
