Synonyms

  • sense of fullness
    3.8 0.01/m
    sense of fullness is the everyday feeling of not wanting more food, while a full stomach means the physical feeling in the belly.
  • satiation
    6.6 0.20/m
    satiation is the state of being fully satisfied after eating, while a full stomach describes the actual physical fullness you feel.
  • satiety
    8.0 0.51/m
    satiety is the longer, often scientific term for reduced appetite after eating, while a full stomach means the immediate physical feeling of fullness.
