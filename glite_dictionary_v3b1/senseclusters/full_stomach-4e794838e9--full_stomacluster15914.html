full stomach

noun
having eaten enough; satiated after a meal
1.0
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈstʌmək / (AmE), / fʊl ˈstʌmək / (BrE)
a feeling of having eaten enough food; the state of being satiated after a meal

Examples

A full stomach

Exercising on a full stomach can sometimes cause discomfort.
Students might find it hard to focus in class if they have a full stomach.
