khandha

noun
heap or pile
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌndə / (AmE), / ˈkʌndə / (BrE)
a heap, mass, or collection of things gathered together

Examples

A khandha of bricks
The workers created a khandha of bricks at the edge of the construction site.
After the harvest, there was a khandha of grain sacks in the barn.
Children played on a khandha of fallen leaves in the park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heap - an untidy collection of things lying one on another
  • synonym: pile - a group of things placed one on top of another
  • antonym: scatter - to throw or spread things over a wide area
