barn find

noun
unexpectedly discovered valuable collectible, typically a vehicle
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑrn faɪnd / (AmE), / bɑːn faɪnd / (BrE)
a valuable collectible, especially a classic vehicle, that is unexpectedly discovered in a barn or similar storage, often in original condition
