twit

verb
to harass with persistent criticism
5.1 7.8
Pronunciation: / twɪt / (AmE), / twɪt / (BrE)
to harass or reproach someone with persistent criticism, teasing, or carping remarks

Examples

To twit a friend
He would twit his colleagues about their mistakes in front of everyone.
Rather than offering help, he just kept twitting his friend about the error.
She was twitted endlessly for her unusual accent at school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: razz - to make fun of, mock or tease
