pit-yacker

noun
informal storyteller or gossip, often in mining
7.0
Pronunciation: / pɪtˈjækər / (AmE), / pɪtˈjakə / (BrE)
A person who tells stories or shares gossip informally, often in a humorous way, especially relating to mining circles.

Examples

A pit-yacker tells stories
Every village had at least one pit-yacker who knew all the latest happenings.
She became a well-known pit-yacker after years of sharing stories at the pub.
The old miners loved hearing the pit-yacker spin tales about their days underground.
