lapsed

adjective
no longer active or practicing
Pronunciation: / ˈlæpst / (AmE), / ˈlæpst / (BrE)
No longer participating in or practicing a religion, profession, or activity that one once did.

Examples

Lapsed club member
He described himself as a lapsed member of the club after years of inactivity.
Many lapsed subscribers returned after the magazine's redesign.
She is a lapsed Catholic who no longer attends church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: former - having previously been but no longer being; past
  • antonym: practicing - actively engaged in or adhering to a particular religion, profession, or activity
