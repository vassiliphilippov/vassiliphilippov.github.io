Wesleyism

noun
doctrines taught by John Wesley
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛzliˌɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛzliˌɪzəm / (BrE)
The evangelical religious doctrines and practices taught by John Wesley, especially as they form the basis of Methodism.

Examples

Wesleyism religious doctrines
He dedicated his life to spreading Wesleyism throughout rural communities.
Many Methodist churches still adhere to the principles of Wesleyism today.
The core tenets of Wesleyism emphasize personal faith and social holiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Methodism - a group of Protestant Christian denominations based on the teachings of John Wesley
