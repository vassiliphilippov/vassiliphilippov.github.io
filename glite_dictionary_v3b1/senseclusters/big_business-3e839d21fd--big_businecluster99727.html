big business

noun
powerful large-scale commercial enterprises
1.4
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / bɪɡ ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
large and powerful companies or corporations, especially when considered as a group that can influence society or politics

Examples

Big business groups
Environmental groups argue that big business should be held accountable for pollution.
Regulations are often challenged by big business interests.
Some politicians are known to have close ties with big business.
Big business has a significant influence on government policy in many countries.
Many people argue that big business prioritizes profits over social responsibility.
She fears that big business will drive small local shops out of the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corporation - a large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity
  • antonym: small business - an independently owned and operated company that is limited in size and revenue
