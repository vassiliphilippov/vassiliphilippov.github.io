ringneck

noun
gamebird with neck ring
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋˌnɛk / (BrE)
a common gamebird, especially the ring-necked pheasant, known for the pale ring around its neck

Examples

Ringneck pheasant
The hunter spotted a colorful ringneck hiding in the tall grass.
Many farmers see the ringneck as a symbol of the countryside.
During autumn, flocks of ringnecks can be seen in the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partridge - a small, plump gamebird related to the pheasant
  • synonym: pheasant - a large, long-tailed gamebird
