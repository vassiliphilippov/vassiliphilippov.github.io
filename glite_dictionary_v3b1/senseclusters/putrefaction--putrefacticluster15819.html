Synonyms

  • putrescence

  • rot
    1.6 2.70/m
    rot is common decay of plants or food that leaves soft, broken tissue, while putrefaction stresses foul smell and bacterial breakdown.
  • rotting
    3.4 0.33/m
    rotting is the active process of things falling apart by fungi or bacteria, while putrefaction points to smelly, foul decay.
  • putridity
    9.9 0.00/m
    putridity is the foul condition or smell of rotten matter, while putrefaction emphasizes the process that causes that bad odor.
  • putridness
    8.2 0.00/m
    putridness is the plain quality of smelling rotten, while putrefaction refers to the actual rotting process or state producing that smell.
  • decomposition
    5.3 1.47/m
    decomposition is a broad breakdown of organic matter that can be slow or chemical, while putrefaction usually means smelly bacterial rotting.
