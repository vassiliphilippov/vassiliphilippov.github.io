crash land

verb
make an emergency landing
3.1
Pronunciation: / kræʃ lænd / (AmE), / kræʃ lænd / (BrE)
to land an aircraft in an emergency, often causing damage but avoiding a total crash

Examples

To crash land a plane
Our helicopter crash landed on the beach, but everyone survived without injury.
Skilled maneuvering allowed her to crash land the plane and save the passengers.
The pilot had to crash land after both engines failed over the forest.
