stale

adjective
not fresh; deteriorated by age
2.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /steɪl/ (AmE), /steɪl/ (BrE)
No longer fresh, pleasant, or good to eat or use, usually because of age or exposure to air.

Examples

Stale bread
Nobody wanted to drink the stale coffee that had been sitting in the pot for hours.
The cookies became stale after being left out overnight.
We threw away the stale bread because it was too hard to eat.
