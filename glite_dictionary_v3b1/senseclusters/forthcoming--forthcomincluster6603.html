forthcoming

adjective
about to happen soon
Pronunciation: / fɔrθˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / fɔːθˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or appearing soon; about to take place in the near future

Examples

Forthcoming event
She is preparing for her forthcoming trip to Paris.
The school announced the schedule for the forthcoming exams yesterday.
Tickets for the forthcoming concert went on sale this morning.
