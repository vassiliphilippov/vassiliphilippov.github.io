party to the transaction

noun
participant in a legal transaction
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrti tə ðə trænˈzækʃən / (AmE), / ˈpɑːti tə ðə trænˈzækʃən / (BrE)
a person or group involved as a participant in a specific legal transaction or proceeding

Examples

Party to the transaction signs contract
Each party to the transaction must sign the final contract for it to become legally binding.
It is important that any party to the transaction understands their obligations fully.
The lawyer explained the rights of every party to the transaction before proceeding.
