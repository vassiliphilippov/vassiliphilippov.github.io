flexographic

adjective
printing with flexible plates
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌflɛksəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌflɛksəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to a printing process that uses flexible relief plates, especially for packaging and labels

Examples

Flexographic printing process
The company invested in new flexographic printing equipment for packaging.
Many food wrappers are produced using flexographic techniques for fast and efficient printing.
A flexographic press can print on a variety of materials, including plastic and paper.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gravure - relating to a printing process using engraved cylinders
  • antonym: offset - relating to a common printing process using plates and rubber blankets
