table turning

noun
spiritualist practice involving a moving table
9.3 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈteɪ.bəl ˈtɜːr.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈteɪ.bəl ˈtɜː.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
a spiritualistic practice in which a table is purportedly moved or rotated during a seance, the movements being attributed to spirits

Table turning at seances
During the nineteenth century, table turning became a popular activity at seances.

Skeptics often sought to expose fraudulent table turning by monitoring participants' movements.
