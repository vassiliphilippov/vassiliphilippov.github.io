Dactylorhiza maculata fuchsii

noun
a European spotted orchid subspecies
Pronunciation: / dæk.tɪ.lɔːˈraɪ.zə məˈkjuː.lə.tə ˈfʊk.si.aɪ / (AmE), / dæk.tɪ.lɔːˈraɪ.zə məˈkjuː.lə.tə ˈfʊk.si.aɪ / (BrE)
A subspecies of the heath spotted-orchid, native to Europe, with lance-shaped leaves spotted with purple and pink to white or mauve flowers marked with deep red or purple.
