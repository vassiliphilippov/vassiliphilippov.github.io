Kurumba

proper noun
Dravidian people or language
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːˈrʊmbə / (AmE), / kʊˈrʊmbə / (BrE)
a member of a Dravidian-speaking indigenous people of southern India, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Researchers are documenting the Kurumba language to help keep it alive for future generations.
Many Kurumba communities live in the Nilgiri hills of southern India.
