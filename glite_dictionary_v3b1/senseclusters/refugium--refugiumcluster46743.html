refugium

noun
survival area for species
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfjuːdʒiəm / (AmE), / rɪˈfjuːdʒiəm / (BrE)
an area where plants or animals survive during periods of unfavorable environmental conditions

Examples

Plant refugium
During the last ice age, the valley acted as a refugium for many rare plants.
Scientists study how species survive in a refugium when the climate changes rapidly.
The isolated island became a refugium for animals threatened on the mainland.

  • synonym: sanctuary - a safe place where something is protected
