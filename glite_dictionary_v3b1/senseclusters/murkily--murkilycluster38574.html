murkily

adverb
in a dark or dim way
8.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɜːr.kə.liː/ /; / /ˈmɜːr.kɪ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈmɜː.kə.liː/ /; / /ˈmɜː.kɪ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that is dark, unclear, or hard to see or understand

Examples

Figures move murkily
