healthfulness

noun
quality of promoting good health
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of promoting good health

Examples

The healthfulness of food
She always checks the healthfulness of the products before buying them.
Some people are skeptical about the healthfulness of low-carb diets.
The healthfulness of a Mediterranean diet has been demonstrated in many studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: healthiness - the state of being healthy
  • antonym: unhealthfulness - the quality of being unhealthy or not promoting good health
