washout

noun
someone who is unsuccessful

a person who fails to achieve success or who is unsuccessful at something, especially after initial promise or expectation

Examples

A washout in school

Nobody expected the promising athlete to become a washout after such a strong start.
She feared that one bad performance would make her seem like a washout in her new company.
