tagliarini

noun
thin Italian pasta
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːliəˈriːni / (AmE), / ˌtæliəˈriːni / (BrE)
a type of long, flat Italian pasta that is thinner than tagliatelle

Examples

Fresh tagliarini pasta
The chef made fresh tagliarini and served it with a delicate butter sauce.
She prefers tagliarini over other pastas because of its thin, light texture.
At the restaurant, I ordered tagliarini with mushrooms and truffle oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spaghetti - a long, thin, round Italian pasta
  • synonym: tagliatelle - a broad, flat Italian pasta similar to fettuccine
