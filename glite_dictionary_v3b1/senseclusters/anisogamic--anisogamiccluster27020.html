anisogamic

adjective
involving dissimilar gametes (biology)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænɪsoʊˈɡæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌanɪsəʊˈɡamɪk / (BrE)
relating to sexual reproduction involving the fusion of dissimilar gametes in size or shape

Examples

Anisogamic reproduction
Many algae species exhibit anisogamic reproduction, with large non-motile eggs and small motile sperm.
Some fungi employ anisogamic strategies, ensuring genetic diversity through gamete differentiation.
The distinction between isogamic and anisogamic reproduction is crucial in evolutionary biology.
