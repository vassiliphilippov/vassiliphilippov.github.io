template

noun
model or standard for comparison
2.6 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛm.plət / (BrE)
something that serves as a model or standard against which others may be compared or judged

Examples

A template for essays
He used her speech as a template when preparing his own remarks.
Scientists use a well-established template to compare experimental results.
The company's successful project became a template for others in the industry to follow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prototype - an original model on which something is patterned
  • synonym: standard - something established by authority, custom, or general consent as a model or example
