common daisy

noun
a low-growing plant with yellow and white flowers

Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mən ˈdeɪ.zi/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mən ˈdeɪ.ziː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒm.ən ˈdeɪ.zi/ /; / /ˈkɒm.ən ˈdeɪ.ziː/ (BrE)
