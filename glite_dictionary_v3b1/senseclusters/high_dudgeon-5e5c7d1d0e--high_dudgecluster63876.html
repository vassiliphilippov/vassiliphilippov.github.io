high dudgeon

noun
intense indignation or anger
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /haɪ ˈdʌdʒ.ən/ (AmE), /haɪ ˈdʌdʒ.ən/ (BrE)
a state of strong anger or indignation, especially shown in a proud or formal way (usually used in the phrase 'in high dudgeon')

Examples
