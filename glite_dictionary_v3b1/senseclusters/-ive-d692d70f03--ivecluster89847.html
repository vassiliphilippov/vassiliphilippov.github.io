-ive

x
noun-forming suffix
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪv / (AmE), / ɪv / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a person or thing characterized by a quality or action

Examples

-ive in 'representative'
The word 'representative' uses the suffix -ive to indicate a person who represents others.
In 'detective', the suffix -ive forms a noun for someone who investigates crimes.
Adding -ive to 'act' creates 'active', and to 'execute' creates 'executive'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -er - a suffix forming nouns indicating a person who does an action
  • synonym: -ist - a suffix forming nouns for people who practice or are concerned with something
