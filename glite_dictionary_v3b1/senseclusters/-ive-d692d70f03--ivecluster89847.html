A word ending added to adjectives to make nouns for a person or thing with that quality

Examples

-ive in 'representative'
The word 'representative' uses the suffix -ive to indicate a person who represents others.
In 'detective', the suffix -ive forms a noun for someone who investigates crimes.
Adding -ive to 'act' creates 'active', and to 'execute' creates 'executive'.
