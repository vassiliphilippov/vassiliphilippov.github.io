coevality

noun
same age or era
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ.iːˈvæl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ.iːˈvæl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the state of being the same age or existing at the same time as something else

Examples

The coevality of fossils
The coevality of the fossils indicates that these species lived during the same geological period.
Historians debated the coevality of the two ancient civilizations based on new archaeological evidence.
The coevality of the paintings suggests they were created by artists from the same era.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antiquity - the ancient past, especially before the Middle Ages
  • synonym: contemporaneity - the quality of happening or existing at the same time
