Magyarization

noun
forced cultural assimilation
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːɡjərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmæɡjərəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or policy of making people or things more Hungarian, especially by promoting the Hungarian language and culture

Examples

Magyarization policies
The government implemented Magyarization policies in schools to encourage the use of the Hungarian language.
Many minority communities resisted Magyarization during the late 19th century.
