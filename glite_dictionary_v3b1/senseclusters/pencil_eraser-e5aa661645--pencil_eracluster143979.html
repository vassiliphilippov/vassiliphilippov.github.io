pencil eraser

noun
eraser attached to a pencil
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsəl ɪˈreɪsɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəl ɪˈreɪsə / (BrE)
a small piece of rubber or similar material attached to the end of a pencil, used for removing writing or marks

Examples

A pencil eraser end
He turned his test over and used the pencil eraser to correct a mistake.
Sarah's pencil eraser wore down quickly from frequent use in math class.
The pencil eraser left a few smudges on the paper, but the answer was gone.
