microbiological

adjective
relating to microbes
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study or science of microscopic living things such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi

Examples

Microbiological study
The hospital follows strict microbiological procedures to prevent infections.
Researchers performed microbiological analysis to identify the bacteria in the sample.
A microbiological examination revealed the presence of harmful microorganisms in the food.
The hospital uses microbiological tests to detect harmful bacteria in water samples.
She is interested in microbiological research on antibiotic resistance.
Strict microbiological standards are required in food production facilities.
