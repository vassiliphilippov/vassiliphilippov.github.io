valuable insight

noun
important, useful idea or information
Pronunciation: / ˈvæljuəbəl ˈɪnˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈvaljʊəbəl ˈɪnsaɪt / (BrE)
an important and useful idea, observation, or piece of information that helps to understand a situation or solve a problem

Examples

A valuable insight
During the meeting, he contributed a valuable insight that helped us overcome the challenge.
Her research provided a valuable insight into the causes of climate change.
The consultant offered a valuable insight into how the market was changing.
