turd-eater

noun
vulgar insult for a dishonest or gossipy person
Pronunciation: / tɝd ˈitɚ / (AmE), / tɜːd ˈiːtə / (BrE)
(derogatory, vulgar slang) A highly insulting term for a person who is dishonest, unpleasant, or prone to gossip.

Examples

A turd-eater insult
He stormed out after being called a turd-eater in front of the whole office.
She refused to associate with anyone she considered a turd-eater.
Some people act like real turd-eaters when they spread rumors and lies.
