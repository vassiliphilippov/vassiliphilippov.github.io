extender

noun
one who grants extensions
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛndər / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndə / (BrE)
a person or thing that grants or provides an extension of time, credit, or similar allowance

Examples

Extender of credit
The bank acted as an extender of credit to help small businesses survive difficult times.
As an extender of deadlines, the teacher was known for her understanding and flexibility.
The committee appointed an extender to review and approve requests for additional time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: denier - a person who refuses or withholds something
  • synonym: grantor - a person who gives or allows something
