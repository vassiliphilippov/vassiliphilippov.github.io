lord it over

verb
act in a domineering or superior way
5.7
Pronunciation: / lɔrd ɪt ˈoʊvɚ /; / lɔrd ɪt oʊvər / (AmE), / lɔːd ɪt ˈəʊvə /; / lɔːd ɪt əʊvə / (BrE)
to act in a superior or domineering way toward others; to behave as if you are better or more important than someone else

Examples

To lord it over friends
Don’t lord it over your friends just because you won the competition.
He likes to lord it over his coworkers whenever he gets a promotion.
She was accused of lording it over the new employees.
After her promotion, she began to lord it over her coworkers in meetings.
He likes to lord it over his younger siblings whenever their parents are around.
They resented the way he would lord it over everyone at the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominate - to have control over; to exercise power
  • synonym: domineer - to behave in an arrogant or overbearing way
  • antonym: humble - to make someone feel less important or proud
