lofting

noun
creating 3D shapes
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːf.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈlɔːf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈlɒf.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of creating a three-dimensional shape or model from design plans, especially in architecture or engineering

Examples

Lofting a model
In shipbuilding, lofting is used to create full-size patterns from blueprints.
Modern architects often use computer software for lofting complex building shapes.
Accurate lofting ensures that the final structure matches the original design plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: modeling - the act of making a three-dimensional representation of a design
