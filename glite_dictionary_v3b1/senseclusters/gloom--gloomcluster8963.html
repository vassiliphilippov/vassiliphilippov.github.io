gloom

noun
partial or total darkness
3.6 2.89/m
Pronunciation: / …°lum / (AmE), / …°luÀêm / (BrE)
Partial or total darkness, especially when it creates an atmosphere of sadness or mystery.

Examples

Thick gloom
Fog and gloom made it hard to see the path ahead.
She turned on the lamp to chase away the evening gloom.
The old house was filled with gloom after the sun went down.
