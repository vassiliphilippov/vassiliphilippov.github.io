newsmaker

noun
person in the news
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːzˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or event that becomes the subject of news stories or attracts media attention

Examples

A newsmaker person
The young athlete became a newsmaker after winning the gold medal at the Olympics.
Major political events often turn ordinary people into unexpected newsmakers overnight.
The charity's founder was a newsmaker for her innovative approach to helping the homeless.
The politician became a newsmaker after announcing the new policy.
Every year, the magazine selects a newsmaker who has had a major impact on society.
After the discovery, the scientist was suddenly a newsmaker around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrity - a famous person who receives a lot of public attention
  • synonym: public figure - a person who is well known to the public, especially in the media
