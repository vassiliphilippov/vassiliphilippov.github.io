doo

noun
slang for feces
9.3
Pronunciation: / duː / (AmE), / duː / (BrE)
a playful or slang term for feces, often used by or with children

Examples

To do a doo
The little boy announced he needed to do a doo before leaving the house.
Parents often use the word doo when talking to young children about using the toilet.
She laughed when her niece whispered that her teddy bear had made a doo.
