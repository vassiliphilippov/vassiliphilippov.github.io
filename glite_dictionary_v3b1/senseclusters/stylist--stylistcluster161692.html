Synonyms

  • hairdresser
    1.0 2.56/m
    hairdresser is the common salon professional who cuts and styles hair, while stylist sounds more modern and general for hair work.
  • hairstylist
    2.1 0.65/m
    hairstylist focuses on creating and finishing hair looks in salons, while stylist is the broader name for a hair professional.
  • styler
    5.8 0.01/m
    styler is a less common word for someone who shapes or arranges hair, while stylist is the usual salon title.
  • coiffeur
    9.1 0.03/m
    coiffeur names a male hairdresser in an upscale salon, while stylist is a general, gender-neutral term for hair professionals.
