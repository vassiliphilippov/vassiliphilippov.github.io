handedly

adverb
using the hands
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhændɪdli / (AmE), / ˈhændɪdli / (BrE)
in a manner involving the use of a hand or hands, often used in compounds to describe how something is done.

Examples

Painted handedly
The artist painted the mural handedly, without any mechanical assistance.
Many traditional crafts are still made handedly in small workshops.
She prefers to prepare her meals handedly rather than using kitchen gadgets.
