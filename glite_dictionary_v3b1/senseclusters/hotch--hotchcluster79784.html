hotch

noun
mixture or jumble
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑtʃ / (AmE), / hɒtʃ / (BrE)
a mixture or jumble of different things, often in a confused or disorderly way

Examples

A hotch of items
The artist's studio was a hotch of paints, brushes, and unfinished canvases.
Dinner turned out to be a hotch of leftovers from the week.
Her notes were a hotch of ideas and sketches for the new project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hodgepodge - a confused mixture of different things
  • synonym: medley - a varied mixture of people or things
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged properly
