miche

noun
French country bread
Pronunciation: / miʃ / (AmE), / miʃ / (BrE)

Examples

A large miche loaf
The bakery displayed a large miche on the counter, fresh from the oven.
She bought a miche to share with her family at dinner.
A traditional miche has a thick crust and a chewy interior.
