pond apple

noun
a yellow, fragrant fruit related to custard apple
4.9
Pronunciation: / pɑnd ˈæpəl / (AmE), / pɒnd ˈapəl / (BrE)
an ovoid, yellow fruit with very fragrant, peach-colored flesh, related to custard apples and often found in swampy areas

Examples

A ripe pond apple
Many animals feed on the pond apple during the tropical wet season.
She described the taste of the pond apple as sweet and aromatic.
The swamp was filled with wild pond apples hanging from the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custard apple - a sweet, tropical fruit from the family Annonaceae
