stellate

adjective
star-shaped; radiating from a center
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛl.eɪt / (AmE), / ˈstɛl.eɪt / (BrE)
arranged like rays or having a star-like pattern; radiating from a common center.

Examples

Stellate pattern
Some types of crystal grow in a stellate formation.
The flower's petals are stellate, radiating outward from the center.
Under the microscope, the cells appeared stellate, with multiple arms extending from the center.
