putter

verb
do small, aimless tasks or pass time idly
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌtɚ /; / ˈpʌt̬ɚ /; / ˈpʌtər / (AmE), / ˈpʌtə / (BrE)
to move about or do small, unimportant tasks in a leisurely or aimless way; to spend time idly without real purpose
