garden rake

noun
tool with toothed bar for gardening
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdn ˌreɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdn ˌreɪk / (BrE)
a hand tool with a long handle and a toothed bar, used by gardeners for gathering leaves or smoothing soil

Examples

A garden rake tool
After planting seeds, she smoothed the soil with her garden rake.
He used a garden rake to collect the fallen leaves in the yard.
The shed was filled with tools, including a shovel and a garden rake.

Related Terms
