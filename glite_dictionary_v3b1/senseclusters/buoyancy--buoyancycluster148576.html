buoyancy

noun
cheerful, optimistic attitude
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪ.ən.si /; / ˈbɔɪənsi, ˈbuːjənsi / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪ.ən.si /; / ˈbɔɪənsi, ˈbɔɪənsi / (BrE)
a cheerful, optimistic, and lively attitude, especially the ability to stay positive and recover from setbacks

Examples

Emotional buoyancy
Despite many setbacks, she always maintained a sense of buoyancy that inspired others.
Even after the disappointing result, her buoyancy helped the whole team stay positive.
His natural buoyancy made him a joy to work with in difficult times.
Despite many failures, her buoyancy inspired everyone around her to stay positive.
His natural buoyancy helped him handle stressful situations with ease.
You could feel the buoyancy in the team after their victory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfulness - the state of being noticeably happy and optimistic
  • antonym: gloom - a state of depression or sadness
  • synonym: resilience - the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties
