eco

noun
environmentalist (informal)
4.9 7.40/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàiÀêko ä / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêk…ô ä / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is concerned with protecting the environment; an environmentalist

Examples

An eco at school
He became known as the local eco after organizing several tree-planting events.
Many young ecos are passionate about reducing plastic waste in their communities.
She was proud to call herself an eco after volunteering for the beach cleanup.

  • greenie
    6.7 0.03/m
