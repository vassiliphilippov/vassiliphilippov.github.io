sand shark

noun
a species of shark found in shallow Atlantic waters
7.0
Pronunciation: / sænd ʃɑrk / (AmE), / sænd ʃɑːk / (BrE)
a shallow-water shark with sharp, jagged teeth, found in the Atlantic Ocean and sometimes considered dangerous to swimmers

Examples

A sand shark in water
Swimmers are advised to watch for sand sharks near this part of the coast.
The sand shark's sharp, jagged teeth make it an impressive predator.
The aquarium features a large sand shark in its ocean exhibit.
