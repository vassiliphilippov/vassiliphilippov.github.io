lordly

adjective
showing arrogant superiority
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrd.li / (AmE), / ˈlɔːd.li / (BrE)
having or showing an attitude of arrogant superiority and disdain toward others considered unworthy

Examples

Lordly attitude
He spoke with a lordly tone that irritated everyone in the room.
Her lordly attitude made it hard for her to make friends.
The manager gave a lordly wave and ignored our questions.
