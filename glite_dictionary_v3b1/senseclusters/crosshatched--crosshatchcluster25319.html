crosshatched

adjective
shaded with intersecting lines
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑsˌhætʃt / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌhætʃt / (BrE)
shaded or marked with sets of parallel or intersecting lines, especially as a technique in drawing or engraving.

Examples

Crosshatched drawing lines
I admired the crosshatched texture on the artist’s drawing of the old barn.
The crosshatched area on the map shows the regions at risk of flooding.
The illustrator used a crosshatched pattern to create depth in the shadows.
