pendentive

noun
hanging ornament
Pronunciation: / pɛnˈdɛntɪv / (AmE), / pɛnˈdɛntɪv / (BrE)
an ornament or object that hangs down, especially as a pendant or decoration (archaic)

Examples

Necklace pendentive
The antique necklace featured a delicate pendentive shaped like a leaf.
She admired the sparkling pendentive that hung from the chandelier.
In the old portrait, the woman wore a jeweled pendentive at her throat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pendant - a piece of jewelry that hangs from a chain worn around the neck
