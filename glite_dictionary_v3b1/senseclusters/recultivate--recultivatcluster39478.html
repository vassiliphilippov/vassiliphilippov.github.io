recultivate

verb
to renew or restore

to restore, renew, or develop again a quality, skill, relationship, or cultural practice through deliberate effort

Examples

To recultivate skills
After moving back to her hometown, she worked hard to recultivate old friendships.
The organization aims to recultivate traditional crafts that were nearly forgotten.
He decided to recultivate his language skills by taking weekly classes.
