photochromatic

adjective
changes color in light
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtəkrəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəkrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
changing color or darkening in response to light, especially when exposed to sunlight

Examples

Photochromatic lenses
The photochromatic lenses in his glasses darken quickly when he steps outside.
Many modern sunglasses use photochromatic technology to adjust to changing light conditions.
Her photochromatic eyewear helps protect her eyes from bright sunlight during outdoor activities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opaque - not allowing light to pass through; not changing with light
  • synonym: photosensitive - reacting to light, especially by changing in some way
