unweighed

adjective
not yet weighed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪd / (BrE)
not measured or determined by weight; not yet weighed

Examples

Unweighed produce

Before shipping, all unweighed parcels must be checked and labeled.
He noticed several unweighed items left on the counter after the market closed.
