relume

verb
to light again
9.9
Pronunciation: / riˈluːm / (AmE), / rɪˈluːm / (BrE)
to light or illuminate again, especially after being extinguished or darkened

Examples

To relume a lamp
After the storm passed, the lighthouse relumed the dark coastline for passing ships.
The poet described how hope relumed his heart after years of sadness.
As the sun rose, it relumed the valley with golden light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extinguish - to put out a light or fire
  • synonym: rekindle - to light or start something again, especially a fire or feeling
