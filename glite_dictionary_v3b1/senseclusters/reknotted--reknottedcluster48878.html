reknotted

verb
to tie again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈnɑːtɪd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈnɒtɪd / (BrE)
to tie or fasten again into a knot after being untied or loosened.

Examples

To reknotted shoelaces
She reknotted her shoelaces before leaving the house.
After the rope came loose, he quickly reknotted it to secure the package.
The sailor reknotted the line to make sure the boat stayed in place.

  • antonym: untie - to undo a knot or fastening
