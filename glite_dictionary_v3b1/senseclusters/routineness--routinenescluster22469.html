routineness

noun
quality of being routine or repetitive
Pronunciation: / ruˈtin.nəs / (AmE), / ruːˈtiːn.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being routine, especially through repetition or adherence to usual patterns.

Examples

The routineness of life
He longed to escape the routineness of suburbia for a more adventurous life.
Some athletes thrive on the routineness of their training schedules.
The routineness of her job made each day blend seamlessly into the next.

Related Terms

  • antonym: novelty - the quality of being new, original, or unusual
  • synonym: routine - a usual or fixed way of doing things
