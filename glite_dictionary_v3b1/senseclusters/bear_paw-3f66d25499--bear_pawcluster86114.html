bear paw

noun
almond-flavored pastry resembling a bear's claw
7.1
Pronunciation: / bɛr pɑ / (AmE), / beə pɔː / (BrE)
an almond-flavored yeast pastry shaped in an irregular semicircle and resembling a bear's claw

Examples

A bear paw pastry
I ordered a fresh bear paw with my morning coffee at the bakery.
She brought a box of bear paws to the office for everyone to enjoy.
The bear paw pastry was filled with almonds and sprinkled with sugar.
