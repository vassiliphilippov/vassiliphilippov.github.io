soybean

noun
edible bean and oil source plant
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn /; / ˈsɔɪˌbin / (AmE), / ˈsɔɪˌbiːn / (BrE)
a plant native to Asia, grown for its edible, high-protein seeds (beans) used as food, animal feed, and a source of oil

The use of soybean oil has increased significantly in processed foods.
Tofu is made from soybeans, which are soaked and ground to produce soy milk.
