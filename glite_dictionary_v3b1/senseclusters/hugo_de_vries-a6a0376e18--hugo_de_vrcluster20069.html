Hugo De Vries

proper noun
Dutch botanist, rediscovered Mendel's laws
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈhjuː.goʊ də vriːz/ /; / /ˈjuː.goʊ də vriːz/ (AmE), /ˈhjuː.gəʊ də vriːs/ /; / /ˈjuː.gəʊ də vriːs/ (BrE)
(1848–1935) A Dutch botanist known for rediscovering Mendel's laws of heredity and developing the mutation theory of evolution.

Examples

Hugo De Vries mutation theory
Hugo De Vries was instrumental in advancing the study of genetics through his research on plant heredity.
Alongside Correns and von Tschermak, Hugo De Vries helped bring Mendel's laws back to scientific prominence.
Many biology textbooks credit Hugo De Vries for formulating the mutation theory of evolution.
