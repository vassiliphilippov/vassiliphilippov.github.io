rollback

noun
forcing an enemy to withdraw
1.1 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊl.bæk/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊl.bæk/ (BrE)

Examples

Military rollback

The general planned a strategic rollback to reclaim lost territory.
The military launched a massive rollback of enemy forces along the border.
