Papilio

proper noun
butterfly genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈpɪlioʊ / (AmE), / pəˈpɪliəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of large, colorful butterflies commonly known as swallowtails

Examples

Papilio butterfly genus
The Papilio genus includes some of the most beautiful butterflies in the world.
Scientists discovered a new species within the Papilio genus in Southeast Asia.
Many Papilio butterflies have striking patterns and long tails on their wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swallowtail - a butterfly of the family Papilionidae, often with tail-like extensions on the hindwings
