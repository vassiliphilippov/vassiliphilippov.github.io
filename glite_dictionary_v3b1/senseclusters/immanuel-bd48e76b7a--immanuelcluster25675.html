Immanuel

proper noun
biblical title for Jesus
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmænjuːəl / (AmE), / ɪˈmanjuːəl / (BrE)
a name or title from the Bible meaning 'God with us', used in Christian theology for the Messiah (Jesus)

Examples

The coming of Immanuel
The prophet Isaiah foretold the coming of Immanuel as a sign to the people.
Many Christmas hymns refer to Jesus as Immanuel, emphasizing his divine presence.
The name Immanuel appears in both the Old and New Testaments of the Bible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Emmanuel - alternative spelling of Immanuel, meaning 'God with us'
  • synonym: Messiah - the promised deliverer in Jewish and Christian belief
