carbuncle

noun
deep-red garnet gemstone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrˌbʌŋkəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːbʌŋkəl / (BrE)
A deep-red garnet, especially one cut in a smooth, rounded (cabochon) form without facets.

Examples

A carbuncle gem

Collectors prize the deep color and unique cut of a true carbuncle.
