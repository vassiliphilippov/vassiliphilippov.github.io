PX

noun
military store on a US Army post
8.1
Pronunciation: / pi ɛks / (AmE), / pi ɛks / (BrE)
a store on a United States Army post where members of the military and their families can buy goods tax-free

Examples

The PX store
Every Friday afternoon, soldiers would visit the PX to stock up on snacks and toiletries.
Military families depend on the PX for everyday shopping while living on base.
The PX offers a variety of goods that are often more affordable than local stores.
