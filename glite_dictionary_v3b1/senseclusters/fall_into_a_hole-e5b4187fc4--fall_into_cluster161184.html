fall into a hole

verb
to accidentally drop into a hole
Pronunciation: / fɔl ˈɪntu ə hoʊl / (AmE), / fɔːl ˈɪntuː ə həʊl / (BrE)
to accidentally descend or drop into a hole, often unexpectedly or by mistake

Examples

To fall into a hole suddenly
She fell into a hole while running across the field and injured her ankle.
The dog suddenly fell into a hole that was hidden under the leaves.
Watch out, or you might fall into a hole when walking in the dark.

Related Terms
