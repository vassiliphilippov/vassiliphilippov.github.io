soapberry family

noun
family of tropical trees and shrubs (Sapindaceae)
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊpˌbɛri ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈsəʊpˌbɛri ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical and subtropical trees and shrubs (Sapindaceae) with leathery drupes; most plants produce toxic saponins
