antileukotriene

noun
asthma or allergy drug
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌluːkoʊˈtraɪiːn / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌluːkəʊˈtriːiːn / (BrE)
a drug that blocks the action of leukotrienes to help treat asthma or allergies

Examples

An antileukotriene drug
The doctor recommended an antileukotriene to help manage her chronic asthma.
Some patients respond better to antileukotriene medications than to inhaled steroids.
He started taking an antileukotriene after experiencing frequent allergy attacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antihistamine - a drug that blocks the effects of histamine to relieve allergy symptoms
  • antonym: leukotriene - a molecule involved in inflammation and allergic reactions
