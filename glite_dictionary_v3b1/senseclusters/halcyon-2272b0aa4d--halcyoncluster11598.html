Halcyon

noun
genus of large kingfishers
Pronunciation: / ˈhæl.si.ˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈhæl.sɪ.ən / (BrE)
a genus of large, brightly colored kingfishers native to Africa, Asia, and Australasia

The Halcyon genus includes several striking species found in Africa and Asia.
