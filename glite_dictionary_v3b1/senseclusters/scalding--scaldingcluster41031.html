scalding

adjective
extremely harsh
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːldɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely harsh or sharply critical, especially in a figurative sense.

Examples

Scalding criticism
The journalist wrote a scalding review of the new movie, criticizing nearly every aspect.
After his mistake, she gave him a scalding look that made him feel even worse.
The report delivered a scalding assessment of the company's recent performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biting - harsh or sharply critical in tone
  • antonym: mild - gentle or not severe in criticism
