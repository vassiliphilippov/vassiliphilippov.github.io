azimuthally

adverb
by angle around axis
Pronunciation: / ˌæzɪˈmʌθəli / (AmE), / ˌæzɪˈmjʊθəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or measured by an angle around a reference axis, especially in a horizontal plane

Examples

Adjusted azimuthally angle
The antenna was adjusted azimuthally to improve the signal reception.
In the experiment, the sensors were placed azimuthally around the central point.
The camera rotates azimuthally to capture a panoramic view of the landscape.

Related Terms

  • antonym: radially - in a direction moving outward from a center point
