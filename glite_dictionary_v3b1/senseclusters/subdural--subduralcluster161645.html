subdural

adjective
below the dura mater (anatomy)
8.4
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈdʊrəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈdjʊərəl / (BrE)
situated or occurring below the dura mater and above the arachnoid membrane of the brain or spinal cord

Examples

Subdural space
A subdural space is created when blood accumulates beneath the dura mater.
Doctors monitored the subdural fluid collection closely for signs of increased pressure.
The patient developed a subdural hematoma after the accident.
