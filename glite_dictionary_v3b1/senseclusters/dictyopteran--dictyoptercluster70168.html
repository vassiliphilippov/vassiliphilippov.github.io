dictyopteran

adjective
relating to cockroaches and mantises (Dictyoptera)
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪktiˈɑptərən / (AmE), / ˌdɪktɪˈɒptərən / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the insect order Dictyoptera, which includes cockroaches and mantises.

Examples

Dictyopteran insect order
Dictyopteran fossils provide insight into the evolution of modern cockroaches and mantises.
Scientists compared the wing structures of several dictyopteran species.
The dictyopteran ovipositor is a distinguishing feature among related insect groups.
