attributable

adjective
able to be ascribed to a cause
4.5 4.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjə.tə.bəl/ /; / /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjuː.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjuː.tə.bəl/ /; / /ə.ˈtrɪ.bjə.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
(of something) regarded as being caused by or resulting from a particular person, event, or cause.

Examples

Attributable to cause
Many believe that the decline in sales is attributable to changing consumer habits.
The error was attributable to a miscommunication between the teams.
The work’s success is largely attributable to her leadership.
