cat and rat

noun
children's chasing circle game
Pronunciation: / kæt ənd ræt / (AmE), / kat ənd rat / (BrE)
A children's game in which players form a circle, joining hands and raising or lowering them to let one child (the 'rat') pass through while another ('cat') tries to catch them.

Examples

Cat and rat game
In cat and rat, everyone has to quickly raise or lower their hands to help or block the players inside.
The children played cat and rat in the schoolyard during recess.
We formed a big circle to start a game of cat and rat.
