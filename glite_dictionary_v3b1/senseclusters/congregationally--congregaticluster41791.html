congregationally

adverb
involving a congregation
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or relates to a congregation acting together, especially in worship or activities

Examples

Pray congregationally
The prayers were recited congregationally at the beginning of the ceremony.
During the festival, songs were performed congregationally by everyone present.
The church encourages members to participate congregationally in community service projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collectively - in a way that involves a group acting together
  • antonym: individually - in a way that involves each person acting separately
