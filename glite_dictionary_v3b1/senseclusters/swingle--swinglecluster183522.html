swingle

noun
flax processing tool
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
a wooden tool used to beat and separate fibers from flax or hemp during processing

Examples

Flax swingle tool
The farmer used a swingle to separate the fibers from the flax stalks.
After soaking the hemp, they beat it with a swingle to loosen the fibers.
Traditional linen production often involved using a swingle as part of the process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scutch - a tool used to separate fibers from plant stalks
