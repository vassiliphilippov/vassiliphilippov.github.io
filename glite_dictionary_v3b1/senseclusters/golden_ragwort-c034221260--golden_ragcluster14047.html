golden ragwort

noun
a yellow-flowered weedy herb of eastern North America
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ˈræɡwɜrt / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ˈræɡwɜːt / (BrE)
a weedy herb native to the eastern United States, with golden-yellow flowers, sometimes invasive and placed in the genus Senecio

Examples

Golden ragwort flowers
Bees are often seen collecting nectar from golden ragwort blooms in May.
Some gardeners consider golden ragwort attractive, while others see it as a weed.
The wetlands behind our house are full of golden ragwort each spring.
