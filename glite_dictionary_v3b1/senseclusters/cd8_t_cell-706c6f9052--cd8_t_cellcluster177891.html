CD8 T cell

noun
T cell with CD8 receptor that kills infected cells
4.6
Pronunciation: /siː.diː.ˈeɪt tiː sel/ /; / /ˈsiː.diː.eɪt tiː sel/ (AmE), /siː.diː.ˈeɪt tiː sel/ /; / /ˈsiː.diː.eɪt tiː sel/ (BrE)
