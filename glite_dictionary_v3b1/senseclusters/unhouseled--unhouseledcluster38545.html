unhouseled

adjective
without last sacrament
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhaʊzəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈhaʊzəld / (BrE)
not having received the sacrament of the Eucharist before death

Examples

Unhouseled before death
The soldier feared dying unhouseled on the battlefield, far from any priest.
In the old tale, the king was left unhouseled after his sudden death.
Many in medieval times dreaded being unhouseled, believing it endangered their souls.

Related Terms
