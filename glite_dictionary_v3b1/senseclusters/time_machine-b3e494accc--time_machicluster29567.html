time machine

noun
hypothetical device for time travel
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪm məˌʃin /; / taɪm məˈʃin / (AmE), / ˈtaɪm məˌʃiːn /; / taɪm məˈʃiːn / (BrE)
a fictional or imaginary device that allows someone to travel to the past or the future

Examples

A time machine story
In the novel, the scientist built a time machine to visit the distant future.
Suppose you had a time machine; where would you go first?
The idea of inventing a time machine has fascinated people for generations.
He wished he could invent a time machine to correct his mistakes.
Many science fiction novels feature a time machine as a central plot device.
The children imagined that the old wardrobe was actually a time machine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: time travel - the concept of moving between different points in time
