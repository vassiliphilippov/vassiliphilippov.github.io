beatless

adjective
without heartbeat
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈbiːtləs / (BrE)
having no heartbeat or detectable pulse, especially in a medical context

Examples

Beatless heart
The doctor checked for a pulse and found the patient was beatless.
After the accident, emergency responders discovered the victim was beatless and began CPR immediately.
A beatless state requires urgent medical intervention to try to restore heart function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lifeless - showing no signs of life
  • synonym: pulseless - having no detectable pulse
