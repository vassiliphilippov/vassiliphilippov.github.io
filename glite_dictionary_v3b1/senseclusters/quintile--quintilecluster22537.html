quintile

noun
one of five groups
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪntaɪl / (AmE), / ˈkwɪntaɪl / (BrE)
one of five equal groups into which a set of data, people, or things can be divided, especially in statistics

Examples

Top quintile
The researchers divided the participants into quintiles based on their income levels.
Students in the highest quintile scored significantly better on the test.
The data was analyzed by quintile to identify trends across different groups.
The study divided participants into each quintile based on their income levels.
Students in the lowest quintile scored significantly below the national average.
Her cholesterol level placed her in the second quintile among her age group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decile - one of ten equal groups into which data can be divided
  • synonym: quartile - one of four equal groups into which data can be divided
