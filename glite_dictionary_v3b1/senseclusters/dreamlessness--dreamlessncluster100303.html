dreamlessness

noun
absence of dreams
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdriːmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈdriːmləsnɪs / (BrE)
the state of not experiencing dreams during sleep

Examples

Nighttime dreamlessness

Some people find dreamlessness refreshing, while others miss the stories their minds create.
The medication caused a period of dreamlessness that left her feeling oddly detached.
