laryngectomized

noun
person after laryngectomy
Pronunciation: / ləˌrɪŋɡˈɛktəˌmaɪzd / (AmE), / ləˌrɪŋɡˈɛktəˌmaɪzd / (BrE)
a person who has had their larynx surgically removed, usually due to cancer or severe injury

Examples

A laryngectomized person
The hospital offers special speech therapy for every laryngectomized patient.
After becoming laryngectomized, he learned to communicate using an electronic device.
Support groups help a laryngectomized adjust to life after surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patient - a person receiving medical treatment
