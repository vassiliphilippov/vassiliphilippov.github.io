backlist

verb
to move to backlist
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.lɪst/ (BrE)
to move a book or other work to a publisher's backlist, making it available but no longer actively promoted

Examples

To backlist a book

The company will backlist older titles to focus on new releases.
Once sales dropped, the book was quietly backlisted but remained available online.
