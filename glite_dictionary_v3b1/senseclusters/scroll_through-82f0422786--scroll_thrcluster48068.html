scroll through

verb
browse digital content by moving through it on a screen
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /skroʊl θruː/ /; / /skroʊl θrʌf/ (AmE), /skrəʊl θruː/ /; / /skrəʊl θrʌf/ (BrE)
to move repeatedly up, down, or across digital content on a screen, especially to browse or view information sequentially

Examples

She likes to scroll through photos on her phone when she's bored.
