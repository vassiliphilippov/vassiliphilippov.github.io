strikeout

noun
out in baseball after three strikes
Pronunciation: / ˈstraɪkˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈstraɪkaʊt / (BrE)
(Baseball) An out that occurs when a batter accumulates three strikes during an at-bat.

Examples

Three strikeouts in baseball
After three strikeouts, the team struggled to score any runs.
He was disappointed when he ended the game with a strikeout.
The pitcher recorded his tenth strikeout of the game in the seventh inning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strike - a missed swing or a ball considered hittable as called by the umpire in baseball
