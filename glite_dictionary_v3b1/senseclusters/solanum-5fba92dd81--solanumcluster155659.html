Solanum

noun
genus of plants including potato and eggplant
8.4
Pronunciation: / səˈleɪnəm / (AmE), / səˈleɪnəm / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, including the potato, eggplant, and bittersweet.

Examples

Solanum genus plants
Eggplant belongs to the Solanum genus and is cultivated in many regions worldwide.
Many species within Solanum are studied for their medicinal and agricultural value.
The common potato, known scientifically as Solanum tuberosum, is one of the world's major food crops.
