awaken

verb
cause to wake up or become conscious
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪkən / (AmE), / əˈweɪkən / (BrE)
to stop sleeping or to cause someone to wake up

Examples

To awaken a child
It took several attempts to awaken the sleeping child.

The loud thunder awakened him in the middle of the night.

She managed to awaken just before her alarm went off.
The children awakened early on Christmas morning, full of excitement.
