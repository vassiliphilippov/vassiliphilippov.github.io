newborn

noun
baby less than four weeks old
Pronunciation: / ˈnuˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈnjuːˌbɔːn / (BrE)
a baby that has just been born, typically less than four weeks old

Examples

A newborn baby
Doctors recommend special care for a newborn in the first month of life.
The newborn slept peacefully in her mother's arms.
They dressed the newborn in a soft, warm onesie before leaving the hospital.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: infant - a very young child or baby
  • degree higher: toddler - a young child who is just beginning to walk
