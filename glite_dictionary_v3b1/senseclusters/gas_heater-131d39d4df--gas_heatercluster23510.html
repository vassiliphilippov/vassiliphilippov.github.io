gas heater

noun
a heater that runs on gas
Pronunciation: / ɡæs ˈhiːtər / (AmE), / ɡæs ˈhiːtə / (BrE)
A device that uses gas fuel to produce heat for warming indoor spaces.

Examples

A gas heater indoors
Always make sure your gas heater is properly ventilated to prevent carbon monoxide buildup.
In the freezing winter, their gas heater was essential for keeping the bedroom comfortable.
We replaced the old radiator with a new gas heater to keep our living room warm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: electric heater - a heater powered by electricity
  • synonym: space heater - a portable heater for warming a single room
