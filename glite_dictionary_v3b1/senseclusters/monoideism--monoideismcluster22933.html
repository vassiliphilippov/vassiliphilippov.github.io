monoideism

noun
prolonged absorption in a single idea
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåm…ëÀên.o äÀàa…™.diÀå…™.z…ôm / (AmE), / Àåm…ín.…ô äÀàa…™.diÀê.…™.z…ôm / (BrE)
A psychological state in which a person is intensely absorbed in a single idea or thought for a prolonged period, often seen in conditions such as depression, trance, or hypnosis.

Examples

State of monoideism
During the trance, the patient entered a state of monoideism and could not focus on anything else.
His monoideism was evident as he fixated on that one troubling thought for days.
In certain mental illnesses, monoideism may manifest as obsessive attention to a single topic.

Synonyms
