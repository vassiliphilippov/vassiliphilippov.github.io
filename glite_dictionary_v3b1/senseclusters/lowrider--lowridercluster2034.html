lowrider

noun
customized low car
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌraɪdə / (BrE)
a car that has been modified to ride very close to the ground, often with custom paint and features

Examples

Classic lowrider car
The bright red lowrider turned heads as it cruised slowly down the street.
He spent years transforming his old sedan into a show-quality lowrider.
At the festival, dozens of lowriders displayed their unique paint jobs and hydraulic systems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom car - a car that has been modified for style or performance
  • antonym: sports car - a car designed for high speed and performance
