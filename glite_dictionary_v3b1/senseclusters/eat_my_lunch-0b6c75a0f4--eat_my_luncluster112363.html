eat my lunch

verb
outperform or take advantage of someone
7.8
Pronunciation: / it maɪ lʌntʃ / (AmE), / iːt maɪ lʌntʃ / (BrE)
(informal) to defeat, outdo, or take advantage of someone, especially in a competitive context.

Examples

To eat my lunch in competition
I was worried that the new team would eat my lunch on this project, but we held our own.
If we don't innovate soon, our competitors are going to eat my lunch.
She's so smart and quick; she could easily eat my lunch in any debate.

  • synonym: outperform - perform better than someone
