bioinformatics

noun
computational biology analysis
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌɪnfərˈmætɪks / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌɪnfəˈmætɪks / (BrE)
the science of using computers and software to analyze and interpret biological data

Examples

Bioinformatics research
Advances in bioinformatics have made it possible to analyze large sets of genetic data quickly.
Many researchers use bioinformatics tools to compare DNA sequences from different species.
A career in bioinformatics often requires knowledge of both biology and computer science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computational biology - the use of computers to model and analyze biological systems
  • synonym: genomics - the study of genomes and their functions
