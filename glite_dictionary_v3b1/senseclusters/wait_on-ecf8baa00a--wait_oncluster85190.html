wait on

verb
defer action until response or result
1.4
Pronunciation: / weɪt ɑn / (AmE), / weɪt ɒn / (BrE)
to delay taking action until something happens or until someone gives a response or decision

Examples

To wait on a decision
She decided to wait on his approval before submitting the application.
We're going to wait on the official report before making a statement.
You shouldn't wait on others to chase your dreams.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wait for - to remain until something expected happens
