personality assessment

noun
test measuring personality traits
Pronunciation: / pɚˌsɑːnəˈlɪti əˌsɛsmənt / (AmE), / pɜːˌsɒnəˈlɪti əˌsɛsmənt / (BrE)
a test or questionnaire designed to evaluate and describe a person's personality traits

Examples

A personality assessment test
Clinical psychologists often use a personality assessment to better understand their patients' behavior.
She prepared for the personality assessment by answering practice questions online.
The company required each employee to complete a personality assessment as part of the hiring process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: personality test - a test designed to reveal aspects of an individual's character or psychological makeup
  • antonym: projective test - a personality test designed to let a person respond to ambiguous stimuli
