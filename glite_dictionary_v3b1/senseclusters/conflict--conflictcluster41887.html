conflict

noun
military battle or fight
2.7 33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀên.fl…™kt / (AmE), / Ààk…ín.fl…™kt / (BrE)
a serious fight or battle between opposing armed forces, especially in a war

Examples

Armed conflict
Many soldiers were injured during the conflict that lasted for days.
The sudden conflict at the border escalated into a larger war.
The two armies met in a fierce conflict on the open plain.
