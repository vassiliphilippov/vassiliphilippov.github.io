conflict

noun
military battle or fight
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.flɪkt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.flɪkt / (BrE)
a serious fight or battle between opposing armed forces, especially in a war

Examples

Armed conflict
Many soldiers were injured during the conflict that lasted for days.
The sudden conflict at the border escalated into a larger war.
The two armies met in a fierce conflict on the open plain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - a hostile meeting of opposing military forces
  • synonym: engagement - a fight or battle between armed forces
  • degree lower: skirmish - a minor or brief fight between small numbers of troops
