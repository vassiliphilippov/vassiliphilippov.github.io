chip

noun
semiconductor component for integrated circuits
5.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /tʃɪp/ (AmE), /tʃɪp/ (BrE)
a small piece of semiconductor material on which an integrated circuit is fabricated, used in electronic devices to perform various functions

Examples

A computer chip
Manufacturers are facing a shortage of chips for automotive electronics.
Modern smartphones contain several powerful chips to handle processing tasks.
The computer's speed improved after he upgraded the chip on the motherboard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microchip - an extremely small chip used especially for storing electronic data
  • synonym: semiconductor - a material used to make electronic circuits, particularly chips
