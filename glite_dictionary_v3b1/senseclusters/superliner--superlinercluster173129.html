superliner

noun
large multi-level vehicle
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a very large passenger train car or ocean liner, especially one with multiple levels for carrying people

Examples

An superliner train car
The Amtrak superliner offers comfortable overnight accommodations for travelers.
Passengers enjoyed the spacious upper deck of the superliner as it crossed the country.
The new superliner was designed to carry more people than any previous train car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ferry - a boat or ship for carrying passengers
  • synonym: liner - a large passenger ship or train car
