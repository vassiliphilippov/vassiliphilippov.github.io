viola

noun
a small flowering plant of the genus Viola
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈoʊlə / (AmE), / vaɪˈəʊlə / (BrE)
Any of a genus of small herbaceous plants with heart-shaped leaves and showy flowers, typically purple, yellow, or white.

Examples

Wild viola flowers
She planted a border of violas along the walkway for their delicate beauty.
Some species of viola are prized for their fragrance and vibrant petals.
The garden was filled with blooming violas in a variety of colors.
