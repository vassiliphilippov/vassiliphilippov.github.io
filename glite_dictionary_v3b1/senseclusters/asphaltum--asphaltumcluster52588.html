asphaltum

noun
natural bitumen
9.2
Pronunciation: / æsˈfɔltəm / (AmE), / æsˈfɔːltəm / (BrE)
a naturally occurring form of bitumen or petroleum residue found in deposits such as seeps or lakes

Examples

Natural asphaltum
Geologists collected samples of asphaltum from the exposed vein to analyze ancient organic matter.
The ancient city used asphaltum to waterproof its buildings and roads.
Large deposits of asphaltum can be found in natural seeps around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitumen - a black, sticky substance used in road surfacing and roofing
  • synonym: tar - a dark, thick, sticky liquid from burning organic material
