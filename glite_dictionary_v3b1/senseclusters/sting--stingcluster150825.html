sting

noun
sharp mental pain or distress
6.9
Pronunciation: / stɪŋ / (AmE), / stɪŋ / (BrE)
a sharp mental pain, such as distress, regret, or remorse

Examples

A sting of regret
His betrayal left a sting that was difficult to forget.
She felt the sting of his harsh words long after the argument ended.
The sting of regret kept her awake all night.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: ache - a continuous or dull pain
  • synonym: pang - a sudden sharp feeling of pain or distress
