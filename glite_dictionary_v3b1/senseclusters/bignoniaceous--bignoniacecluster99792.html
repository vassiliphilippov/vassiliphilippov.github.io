bignoniaceous

adjective
relating to the Bignoniaceae plant family
8.1
Pronunciation: / bɪɡˌnoʊniˈeɪʃəs / (AmE), / bɪɡˌnəʊniˈeɪʃəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of plants belonging to the Bignoniaceae family, which includes trumpet vines and jacarandas.

Examples

Bignoniaceous plants
Botanists collected samples of bignoniaceous vines from the rainforest for further study.
Some bignoniaceous species are popular as ornamental plants in warm climates.
The bignoniaceous trees in the park produced clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers.
