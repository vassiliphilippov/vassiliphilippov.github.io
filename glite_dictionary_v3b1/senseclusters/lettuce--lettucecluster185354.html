lettuce

noun
a green leafy salad vegetable
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛt̬ɪs /; / ˈlɛt̬.ɪs / (AmE), / ˈlɛtɪs /; / ˈlɛt.ɪs / (BrE)
a leafy green vegetable, often eaten raw in salads, from plants of the species Lactuca sativa or related types

She added fresh lettuce to her sandwich for a crunchy texture.
The chef prefers to use organic lettuce in his recipes.
