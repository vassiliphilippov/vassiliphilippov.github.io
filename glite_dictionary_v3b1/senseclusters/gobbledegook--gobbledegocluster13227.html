gobbledegook

interjection
nonsense exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑbəldiˌɡuk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒbəldiˌɡuːk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express that something is nonsense or impossible to understand

Examples

Gobbledegook again!
When he started talking about quantum physics, she just said, "gobbledegook!"
I tried to read the instructions, but it was all gobbledegook to me.
He dismissed the complicated explanation with a loud gobbledegook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are meaningless or make no sense
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is not true or makes no sense
