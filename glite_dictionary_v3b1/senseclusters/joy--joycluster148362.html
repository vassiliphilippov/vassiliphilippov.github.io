joy

verb
(archaic) make glad or happy
4.4 9.0
Pronunciation: /dʒɔɪ/ (AmE), /dʒɔɪ/ (BrE)
(archaic) to make someone happy or to feel great happiness

Examples

To joy the heart

She joyed in the success of her children, her face alight with pride.
They joyed at the arrival of spring after a long, harsh winter.
