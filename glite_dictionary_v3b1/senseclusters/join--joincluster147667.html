join

verb
become a member of a group or organization
1.0
Pronunciation: / dʒɔɪn / (AmE), / dʒɔɪn / (BrE)
to become a member of a group or to take part in an activity with others

Examples

To join a club
After graduating, she decided to join the Peace Corps and work abroad.
Anyone can join the club by filling out an application form.
He joined the local running group to meet new people and stay active.
Several friends decided to join us for the trip to the mountains.
She joined the other guests in the garden after the ceremony.
Would you like to join me for lunch this afternoon?

Related Terms

  • synonym: accompany - to go somewhere with someone
  • synonym: enroll - to officially register or sign up for membership in a group or activity
  • antonym: leave - to depart from or stop belonging to a group or organization
