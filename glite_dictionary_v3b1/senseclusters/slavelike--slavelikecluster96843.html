slavelike

adjective
resembling a slave; servile
Pronunciation: / ˈsleɪvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsleɪv.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a slave, especially in being submissive or servile.

Examples

Slavelike behavior
He performed his duties in a slavelike manner, never questioning orders from his employer.
Her slavelike devotion to perfectionism started to concern her friends.
The workers complained about the slavelike conditions in the factory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: masterful - having or showing great skill or power; domineering
  • synonym: servile - having or showing an excessive willingness to serve or please others
  • synonym: submissive - ready to conform to the authority or will of others
