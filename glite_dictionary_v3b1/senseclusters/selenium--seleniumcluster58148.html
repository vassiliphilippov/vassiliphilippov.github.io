A chemical element (symbol Se), a nonmetal related to sulfur and tellurium, toxic in large amounts, used in photocells and other electronic applications.

Examples

Selenium in electronics
Photocells often rely on selenium because it conducts electricity better in light than in darkness.
The selenium in the ore was extracted for use in electronic devices.
Too much selenium in the soil can be toxic to plants and animals.
