beastie

noun
small imaginary creature
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbisti / (AmE), / ˈbiːsti / (BrE)
a small imaginary creature or monster, often used in stories to amuse or frighten children

Examples

A scary beastie
The children giggled as they imagined a tiny beastie hiding under the bed.
In the fairy tale, a friendly beastie helped the hero find his way home.
She drew a picture of a colorful beastie with wings and three eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creature - an animal or imaginary being
  • synonym: monster - a large, frightening imaginary creature
