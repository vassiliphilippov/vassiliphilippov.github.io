Paraguay tea

noun
South American plant used for mate
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˌɡwaɪ ˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌparəˌɡwʌɪ ˈtiː / (BrE)
a South American holly (Ilex paraguariensis) whose dried leaves are used to make mate, a caffeinated herbal drink

Examples

Dried Paraguay tea leaves
Dried leaves of Paraguay tea are commonly exported to make the popular beverage mate.
He offered me a cup of Paraguay tea after dinner, explaining its cultural significance.
The indigenous people have used Paraguay tea for centuries for its stimulating effects.
