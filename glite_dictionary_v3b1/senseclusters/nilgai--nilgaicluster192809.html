nilgai

noun
large Indian antelope
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪlˌgaɪ /; / ˈnɪlˌɡaʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɪlˌgaɪ /; / ˈnɪlˌɡaʊ / (BrE)
a large antelope (Boselaphus tragocamelus) native to India, with blue-grey males bearing white markings and hornless brownish females

Examples

The nilgai antelope
Male nilgai have distinctive blue-grey coats, while the females are brown and lack horns.
The nilgai is the largest Asian antelope and is commonly found in the grasslands of northern India.
Wildlife photographers often travel to India to capture images of the elusive nilgai.
