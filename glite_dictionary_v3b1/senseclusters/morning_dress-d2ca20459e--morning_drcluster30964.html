morning dress

noun
informal house dress for women
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrnɪŋ drɛs / (AmE), / ˈmɔːnɪŋ drɛs / (BrE)
a woman's informal dress worn for housework, especially in the earlier part of the day

Examples

A floral morning dress
Floral patterns were very popular for a morning dress in the early 20th century.
Her simple cotton morning dress was both comfortable and practical for daily tasks.
She changed into a morning dress before beginning her household chores.
