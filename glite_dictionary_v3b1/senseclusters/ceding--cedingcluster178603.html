ceding

noun
the act of giving up (power, rights, etc.)
6.2 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving up power, territory, or rights to another person, group, or country; the process of ceding.

Examples

The ceding of power
His resignation was seen as the ceding of authority to his successor.
The ceding of the disputed land ended the lengthy conflict between the two countries.
The treaty involved the ceding of several important trading rights to the neighboring state.
