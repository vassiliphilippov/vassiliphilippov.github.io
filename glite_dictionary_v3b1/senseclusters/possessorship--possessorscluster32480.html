possessorship

noun
state of ownership
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛsərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsəʃɪp / (BrE)
the state or condition of having possession or ownership of something

Examples

The possessorship of land
The possessorship of the land was transferred after the sale was finalized.
Questions about possessorship often arise in disputes over inherited property.
He claimed possessorship of the artifact based on historical documents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ownership - the legal right of possessing something
  • synonym: possession - the state of having or holding something
  • degree lower: tenancy - the state of occupying or holding property as a tenant
