romance

noun
exciting and mysterious quality or atmosphere
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæns / (AmE), / rəʊˈmæns / (BrE)
an exciting and mysterious quality, especially associated with adventure or heroic times

Examples

The romance of travel
The old castle still retained the romance of ancient legends and brave knights.
There is a certain romance about the age of exploration that captivates many readers.
Travelers sought the romance of the open sea and unexplored lands.
