unaimed

adjective
not targeted
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈeɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈeɪmd / (BrE)

Examples

An unaimed shot

The criticism felt unaimed, as if it was not meant for anyone in particular.
During the chaos, several unaimed comments were shouted across the room.
