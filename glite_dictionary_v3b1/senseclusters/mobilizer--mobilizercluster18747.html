mobilizer

noun
organizer or activator
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈməʊbɪˌlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who encourages or organizes others to take action, especially for a cause or project

Examples

Community mobilizer
The mobilizer inspired hundreds of volunteers to join the environmental campaign.
As a skilled mobilizer, she brought together different groups to support the project.
A good mobilizer can turn ideas into real community action.
The community needed a strong mobilizer to coordinate the relief efforts after the storm.
As a mobilizer, he inspired hundreds of volunteers to join the environmental campaign.
Without a dedicated mobilizer, the project struggled to gain momentum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: activator - someone or something that makes others active or initiates action
  • antonym: bystander - a person who is present but does not take part in an activity
  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and follows the lead of others
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or guides others
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates activities or people
