neuter

verb
remove reproductive organs (of animal)
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtə / (BrE)
to remove the reproductive organs of an animal, especially the ovaries or testicles, to prevent it from reproducing

Examples

To neuter a cat
Many animal shelters require that all adopted cats be neutered before going to new homes.
The veterinarian will neuter your dog to prevent unwanted litters.
We decided to neuter our rabbit last month for health reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: castrate - to remove the testicles of a male animal
  • synonym: spay - to remove the ovaries of a female animal
