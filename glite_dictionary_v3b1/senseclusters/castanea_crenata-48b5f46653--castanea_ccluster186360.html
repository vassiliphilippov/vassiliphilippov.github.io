Castanea crenata

noun
Japanese chestnut tree species
Pronunciation: / kæsˌteɪniə kriˈnɑːtə / (AmE), / kæsˌtiːniə krɛˈnɑːtə / (BrE)
A species of chestnut tree native to Japan and Korea, known for its short trunk and spreading form; commonly called the Japanese chestnut.
