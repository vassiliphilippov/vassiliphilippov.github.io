sustainer

noun
person who upholds or maintains
6.9
Pronunciation: / səˈsteɪnər / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnə / (BrE)
a person who upholds, supports, or maintains something, especially over time

Examples

A sustainer of arts
As a dedicated sustainer of the arts, she donates every year to local museums.
He was known as a sustainer of community traditions, organizing festivals and parades.
Without a strong sustainer behind the movement, it may not survive for long.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maintainer - a person who keeps something in good condition or working order
  • antonym: opponent - a person who disagrees with and speaks against something
  • synonym: supporter - someone who supports or backs another person or cause
