incubator

noun
organization supporting startups
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɪŋ.kjə.beɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈɪŋ.kjuː.beɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈɪŋ.kjʊ.beɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈɪŋ.kjuː.beɪ.tə/ (BrE)
(in business) an organization or program that supports early-stage companies by providing workspace, mentoring, training, and other resources to help them grow.

Examples

Startup incubator
The city opened an incubator to help local tech startups grow.
After graduation, she joined an incubator that provided free office space and mentorship.
Several incubators in the region focus on biotechnology and health-care startups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accelerator - a program that helps startups grow quickly, often with mentoring and investment
