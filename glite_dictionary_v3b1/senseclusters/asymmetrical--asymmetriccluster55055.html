asymmetrical

adjective
not symmetrical in form or arrangement
1.0 4.9
Pronunciation: /eɪ.sɪ.ˈme.trɪ.kəl/ /; / /ə.sɪ.ˈme.trɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /eɪ.sɪ.ˈme.trɪ.kəl/ /; / /ə.sɪ.ˈme.trɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
not symmetrical; having sides or parts that are not the same in size, shape, or arrangement

Examples

Asymmetrical face
His face appeared asymmetrical because of the swelling on one side.
The branches of the tree grew in an asymmetrical pattern after the storm.
The designer chose an asymmetrical hemline to make the dress stand out.
She wore a striking asymmetrical dress with an uneven hemline to the gala.
The artist deliberately designed the sculpture to be asymmetrical, giving it a unique and dynamic appearance.
The building's asymmetrical roof makes it stand out among the traditional structures nearby.
