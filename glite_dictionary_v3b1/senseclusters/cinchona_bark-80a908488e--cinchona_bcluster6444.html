cinchona bark

noun
medicinal bark of cinchona tree
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈtʃoʊnə bɑrk / (AmE), / sɪnˈtʃəʊnə bɑːk / (BrE)
the dried bark of cinchona trees, used in medicine as a source of quinine and quinidine
