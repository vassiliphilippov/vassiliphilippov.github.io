chirpily

adverb
in a cheerful, lively way
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːpɪli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːpɪli / (BrE)
In a cheerful and lively manner; in a way that shows happiness and energy.

Examples

To speak chirpily
He answered the phone chirpily, sounding much happier than yesterday.
She greeted us chirpily despite the early hour.
The children played chirpily in the garden under the warm sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheerfully - in a happy and optimistic manner
  • antonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed manner
