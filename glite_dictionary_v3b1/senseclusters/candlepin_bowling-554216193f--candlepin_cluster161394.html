candlepin bowling

noun
form of bowling with slender pins
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌpɪn ˈboʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkandlˌpɪn ˈbəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of bowling played with slender, straight-sided pins and a small, handheld ball.

Examples

Candlepin bowling game
Candlepin bowling requires a different technique than the standard ten-pin version.
Every Friday, we get together for a game of candlepin bowling at the local alley.
She grew up in New England and is an expert at candlepin bowling.
