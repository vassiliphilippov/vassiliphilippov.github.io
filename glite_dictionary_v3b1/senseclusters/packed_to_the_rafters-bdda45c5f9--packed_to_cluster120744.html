packed to the rafters

adjective
completely full; at maximum capacity
Pronunciation: / pækt tə ðə ˈræftɚz / (AmE), / pækt tə ðə ˈrɑːftəz / (BrE)
Completely full; filled to maximum capacity with people or things.

Examples

When the sale started, the store was packed to the rafters with eager customers.
