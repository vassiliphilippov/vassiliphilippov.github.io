sea raven

noun
large Atlantic sculpin fish
Pronunciation: / si ˈreɪ.vən / (AmE), / siː ˈreɪ.vən / (BrE)
a large sculpin fish of the western Atlantic, known for inflating its body when caught

Examples

The sea raven fish
Divers often spot the distinctive sea raven resting on rocky ocean floors.
The fisherman proudly displayed the sea raven he caught off the coast of Maine.
When threatened, the sea raven inflates its body to appear larger to predators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sculpin - a type of fish with a large, spiny head
