diamond

noun
card suit with red rhombus symbols
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəmənd / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəmənd / (BrE)
one of the four suits in a standard deck of playing cards, marked with red rhombus-shaped symbols

Examples
