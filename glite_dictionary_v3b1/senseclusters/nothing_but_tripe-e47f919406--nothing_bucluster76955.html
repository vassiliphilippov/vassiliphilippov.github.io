nothing but tripe

noun
complete nonsense; worthless talk
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌθɪŋ bʌt traɪp / (AmE), / ˈnʌθɪŋ bət traɪp / (BrE)
used to describe speech or ideas regarded as complete nonsense or utterly worthless

Examples

Nothing but tripe speech
Everything he said during the meeting was nothing but tripe and a waste of time.
The politician's speech was criticized as nothing but tripe by the local newspaper.
You shouldn't trust those rumors; they're nothing but tripe spread to mislead people.
