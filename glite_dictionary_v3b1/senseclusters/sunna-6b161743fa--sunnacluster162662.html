Sunna

noun
Islamic tradition based on Muhammad's teachings
9.3 0.14/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs än…ô, ÀàsuÀên…ô / (AmE), / Ààs än…ô, ÀàsuÀên…ô / (BrE)
(in Islam) the traditional portion of Muslim law based on the words and deeds of Muhammad, accepted as authoritative by Muslims and followed as a model of behavior.

Examples

The Sunna in Islam
He strives to follow the Sunna in his daily routine, imitating the practices of Muhammad.
Many scholars debate how closely modern traditions align with the original Sunna.
Studying the Sunna is essential for understanding Islamic customs and legal principles.
