cavy

noun
South American rodent (includes guinea pig)
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪvi / (AmE), / ˈkeɪvi / (BrE)
A short-tailed, rough-haired rodent native to South America, including the guinea pig.

Examples

A cavy rodent
Scientists studied the diet of a wild cavy population in Brazil.
The cavy is commonly kept as a pet in many households worldwide.
Unlike the guinea pig, the Patagonian cavy has long legs and is adapted for running.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guinea pig - a domesticated cavy popular as a pet and laboratory animal
