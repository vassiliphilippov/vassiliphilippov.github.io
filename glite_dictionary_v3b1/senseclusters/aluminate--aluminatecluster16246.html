aluminate

noun
compound of alumina and metallic oxide
Pronunciation: / əˈluːməˌneɪt / (AmE), / əˈljuːmɪneɪt / (BrE)
a compound formed by the combination of alumina (aluminum oxide) with another metallic oxide

Examples

Barium aluminate compound
Barium aluminate is commonly used as a component in certain specialty ceramics.
Chemists synthesized a new aluminate to test its thermal stability.
The presence of aluminate in the cement affects its setting time.
