Colobus

noun
a genus of African monkeys
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊbəs / (AmE), / kəˈləʊbəs / (BrE)
A genus of African monkeys in the family Cercopithecidae, known for their long tails and striking black-and-white or red-and-white coats.

Examples

The Colobus monkey
Researchers observed that members of the Colobus display complex social behaviors.
The Colobus genus includes several species of leaf-eating monkeys native to Africa.
The conservation status of many Colobus species is threatened by habitat loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colobus monkey - any monkey of the genus Colobus
