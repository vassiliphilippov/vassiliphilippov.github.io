radial

adjective
relating to the radius bone
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.di.əl / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.di.əl / (BrE)
relating to or situated near the radius bone of the forearm.

Examples

Radial artery
A fracture of the radial head can impair elbow movement.
Swelling on the radial side of the wrist may indicate an injury to the radius.
The doctor examined the radial artery to check her pulse.
