preselection

noun
initial filtering
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsəˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːsɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the act of choosing or filtering options before making a final selection

Examples

Preselection process
All applicants must pass a preselection process before being invited for an interview.
The preselection of candidates helps narrow down the pool for the final round.
During preselection, only those who meet the basic requirements are considered further.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screening - the process of examining and selecting from a group
  • degree higher: selection - the act of choosing someone or something from a group
