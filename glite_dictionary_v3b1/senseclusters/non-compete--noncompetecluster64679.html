non-compete

adjective
restricting competition
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn kəmˈpit / (AmE), / ˌnɒn kəmˈpiːt / (BrE)
preventing a person or company from working for or starting a competing business, especially by legal agreement.

Examples

Non-compete agreement
The company required all employees to sign a non-compete agreement before starting work.
A non-compete clause can limit your job options after leaving a company.
Many professionals are cautious about accepting non-compete terms in their contracts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competitive - allowing or encouraging competition
  • synonym: restrictive - limiting what someone can do
