hold someone's hand

verb
give support or guidance
1.0
Pronunciation: / hoʊld səmˌwʌnz hænd /; / hoʊld ˈsʌmwʌnˌz hænd / (AmE), / həʊld səmˌwʌnz hænd /; / həʊld ˈsʌmwʌnz hænd / (BrE)
to support, comfort, or guide someone, especially when they are anxious, inexperienced, or facing something new or difficult
