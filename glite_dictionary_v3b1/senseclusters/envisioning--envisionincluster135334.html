envisioning

noun
the act of visualizing something mentally
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɪʒənɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɪʒənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of forming a mental image or visualization of something, especially a future event or situation

Examples

Envisioning a future
His daily meditation involves envisioning a peaceful place where he feels calm and secure.
Successful athletes often use envisioning techniques to improve their performance.
The architect's process began with envisioning the building's completed design.
