prophetic

adjective
foretelling future events
5.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛt̬ɪk / (AmE), / prəˈfɛtɪk / (BrE)
accurately describing or predicting what will happen in the future, especially by supernatural or inspired means

Examples

His prophetic words
His prophetic words about technological advances seem remarkably accurate today.
The ancient manuscript is full of prophetic visions about the fate of the kingdom.
The scientist's warnings proved to be prophetic when the predicted disaster struck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predictive - relating to or having the effect of predicting something
  • synonym: visionary - having or showing clear ideas about what should happen or be done in the future
