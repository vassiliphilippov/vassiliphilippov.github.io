patchwork

noun
quilt made of sewn-together patches
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpætʃˌwɝk /; / ˈpætʃˌwɝːk / (AmE), / ˈpætʃwɜːk /; / ˈpætʃˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a quilt or other item made by sewing together pieces of different fabrics, or the craft of making such items

Examples

A patchwork quilt
Each square in the patchwork told a different story from her family's history.
She made a beautiful patchwork for her granddaughter's birthday.
The old farmhouse was decorated with colorful patchwork quilts.
Over the years, he created a patchwork out of leftover fabric scraps.
She wrapped herself in a colorful patchwork quilt her grandmother had made.
The exhibition showcased a stunning patchwork of old denim and silk.
