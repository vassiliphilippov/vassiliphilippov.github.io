roll

noun
document stored by rolling up
2.5 2.7
Pronunciation: /roʊl/ (AmE), /rəʊl/ (BrE)
an official list or register of names, or a document stored by being rolled up

Examples

A roll of names
He carefully unrolled the roll to reveal the list of names.
Legal documents were traditionally written on a parchment roll for safekeeping.
The ancient decree was preserved on a roll hidden in the archives.
All new members must have their names added to the roll.
His name was accidentally omitted from the roll of attendees.
The teacher called out each student on the class roll to ensure everyone was present.
