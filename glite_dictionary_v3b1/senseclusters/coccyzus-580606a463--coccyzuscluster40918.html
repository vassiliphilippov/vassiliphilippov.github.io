Coccyzus

noun
genus of American cuckoos
Pronunciation: / kɑkˈsaɪzəs / (AmE), / kɒkˈsɪzəs / (BrE)
A genus of birds in the cuckoo family (Cuculidae), which includes several species of cuckoos native to the Americas.
