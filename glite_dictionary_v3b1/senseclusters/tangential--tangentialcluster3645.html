tangential

adjective
only slightly relevant
Pronunciation: / tænˈdʒɛnʃəl / (AmE), / tanˈdʒɛnʃəl / (BrE)
(of a remark, idea, etc.) only slightly or indirectly related to the main topic; not closely connected.

Examples

Tangential comment in a meeting
Her comments were interesting but ultimately tangential to the main discussion.
The professor made a tangential reference that was only loosely connected to the lecture topic.
Your question is tangential; let's focus on the core issue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digressive - departing from the main subject
  • antonym: relevant - closely connected or appropriate to the matter at hand
