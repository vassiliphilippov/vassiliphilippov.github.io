stoplight

noun
red brake signal light on a vehicle
4.1 1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈstɑːp.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈstɒp.laɪt/ (BrE)
A red light at the rear of a vehicle that illuminates when the brakes are applied to warn following drivers.

Examples

Car stoplight warning
If your stoplight is out, it can be dangerous and might get you a ticket.
She noticed the stoplights flash on the vehicle ahead as it slowed down.
The stoplight on my car isn't working, so I need to get it fixed before driving again.
