piper

noun
someone who plays the bagpipes
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪpə / (BrE)
a person who plays the bagpipes, especially as a profession or in ceremonial contexts

Examples

A piper plays bagpipes
A lone piper played a haunting melody at the wedding.
Each year, skilled pipers compete in the international bagpipe championships.
The piper led the parade through the village streets in full Highland dress.
