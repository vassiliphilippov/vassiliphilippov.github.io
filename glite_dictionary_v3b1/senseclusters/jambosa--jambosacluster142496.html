jambosa

noun
tropical fruit tree of the East Indies
8.5
Pronunciation: / dʒæmˈboʊsə / (AmE), / dʒamˈbəʊsə / (BrE)
A tropical tree of the East Indies cultivated for its edible fruit, belonging to the genus Syzygium.

Examples

The jambosa tree
Farmers in Indonesia often cultivate jambosa in orchards for commercial sale.
The jambosa is valued for both its beautiful flowers and tasty fruit.
The ripe fruit of the jambosa has a crisp texture and subtly sweet flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Syzygium - Genus including jambosa and related fruit trees
