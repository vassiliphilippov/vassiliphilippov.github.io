swallowable

adjective
can be swallowed
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːləbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈswɒləbl̩ / (BrE)

Examples

A swallowable pill

Make sure the toy parts are not swallowable to prevent choking hazards.
This vitamin comes in a small, easily swallowable capsule.
