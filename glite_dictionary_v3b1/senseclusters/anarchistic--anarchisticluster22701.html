anarchistic

adjective
relating to anarchism or absence of authority
Pronunciation: / ˌænərˈkɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌanəˈkɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting anarchism; characteristic of anarchism or tending to promote the absence of authority or government.

Examples

Anarchistic ideas
His paintings often reflect anarchistic themes, challenging authority and convention.
Some critics described the festival's atmosphere as wild and anarchistic.
The group was known for its anarchistic approach to government and social organization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anarchist - a person who advocates or believes in anarchism
  • antonym: authoritarian - favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority
