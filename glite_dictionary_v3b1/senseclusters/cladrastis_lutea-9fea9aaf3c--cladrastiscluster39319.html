Cladrastis lutea

noun
ornamental North American tree with yellow-dye wood
Pronunciation: / kləˈdræstɪs ˈluːtiə / (AmE), / kləˈdræstɪs ˈluːtiə / (BrE)
A small deciduous tree native to eastern North America, known for its rounded crown, showy clusters of white flowers, and hardwood that yields a yellow dye.
