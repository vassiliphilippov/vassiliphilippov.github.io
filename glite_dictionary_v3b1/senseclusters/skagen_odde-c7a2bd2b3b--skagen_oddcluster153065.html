Skagen Odde

proper noun
cape at Denmark's northernmost tip
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːɡən ˈɔdə / (AmE), / ˈskɑːɡən ˈɒdə / (BrE)
a sandy cape at the northernmost tip of Jutland in Denmark, between the Skagerrak and Kattegat seas

Examples

The Skagen Odde cape
From the lighthouse on Skagen Odde, you can see both the Skagerrak and the Kattegat.
The windswept dunes of Skagen Odde are home to many rare bird species.
Thousands of tourists visit Skagen Odde each year to witness where the two seas meet.
