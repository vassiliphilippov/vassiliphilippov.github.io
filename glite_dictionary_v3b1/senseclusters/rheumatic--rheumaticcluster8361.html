rheumatic

adjective
relating to rheumatism or arthritis
3.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /ruː.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /ruː.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or affected by rheumatism or arthritis, especially characterized by pain or stiffness in the joints or muscles.

Examples

Rheumatic pain
His rheumatic knees always ached more during the cold winter months.
She takes medication to help relieve her rheumatic pain.
The rheumatic symptoms made it difficult for him to walk long distances.

  • synonym: arthritic - relating to arthritis; suffering from arthritis
