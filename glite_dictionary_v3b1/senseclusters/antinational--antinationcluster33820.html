antinational

adjective
opposed to national interests
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
opposed to the interests, values, or unity of one's own nation

Examples

Antinational remarks
The politician was accused of making antinational statements during the debate.
Some people viewed the protest as antinational because it criticized government policies.
The law was introduced to prevent the spread of antinational ideas in the media.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nationalistic - strongly supporting the interests of one's own nation
  • antonym: patriotic - showing love and support for one's country
