pan

verb
criticize harshly (esp. in reviews)
6.4 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / pÃ¦n / (AmE), / pan / (BrE)
to criticize something very severely, especially in a review

Examples

To pan a movie
His latest book was panned by the critics, but it still became a bestseller.
Many reviewers panned the movie for its weak plot and poor acting.
The new play was panned so harshly that it closed after just one week.

Synonyms

  • tear apart
    5.6 1.14/m
    tear apart is used when someone criticizes in detail and shows many faults, while pan means to give a very harsh review.
  • trash
    5.8 2.82/m
    trash is a blunt, often unfair way to attack something, while pan refers to a severe critical review.
