mosquitofish

noun
small fish used for mosquito control
Pronunciation: / məˈskiːtoʊˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, silvery freshwater fish native to North America and the West Indies, noted for its effectiveness in controlling mosquito larvae

Mosquitofish are often found in warm, shallow waters near the edges of lakes and streams.
