straw vote

noun
an unofficial poll to gauge opinion
6.0
Pronunciation: / strɔ voʊt / (AmE), / strɔː vəʊt / (BrE)
an unofficial vote conducted to gauge the general opinion or support for an issue or candidate

Examples

A straw vote taken
A quick straw vote revealed that most residents opposed the new policy.
Before making a final decision, the group conducted a straw vote among all attendees.
The committee held a straw vote to see how many members supported the proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straw poll - an unofficial vote to determine public opinion
