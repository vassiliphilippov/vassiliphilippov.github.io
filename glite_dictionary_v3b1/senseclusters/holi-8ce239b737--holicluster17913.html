Holi

noun
Hindu spring festival
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli / (BrE)
a Hindu festival celebrated in spring, known for people throwing colored powders and water to mark the arrival of spring

Examples

Holi festival
During Holi, people gather in the streets to celebrate with music and bright colors.
Many families travel to India to experience the joyful atmosphere of Holi firsthand.
Children love playing with water balloons and colored powder during Holi celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Diwali - a major Hindu festival of lights
