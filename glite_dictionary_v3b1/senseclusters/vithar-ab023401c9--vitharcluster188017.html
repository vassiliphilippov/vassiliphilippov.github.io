Examples

Vithar in Norse myth
According to legend, Vithar avenged Odin by slaying the wolf Fenrir at Ragnarok.
Some scholars interpret Vithar's actions at Ragnarok as symbols of vengeance and renewal.
The god Vithar is often depicted as silent and steadfast in Norse myths.
