gondola car

noun
open railway freight car
8.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑːn.də.lə kɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈgɒn.də.lə kɑː/ (BrE)
A low, flat-bottomed railway freight car with fixed sides and no roof, used for transporting bulk materials.

Examples

A gondola car for coal
Due to its open design, a gondola car is ideal for hauling coal and gravel.
Rainwater collected in the empty gondola car after last night's storm.
The company loaded scrap metal into the gondola car at the rail yard.

  • antonym: boxcar - an enclosed freight car for carrying goods
