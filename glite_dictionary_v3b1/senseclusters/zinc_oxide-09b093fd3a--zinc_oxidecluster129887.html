zinc oxide

noun
white powder used in cosmetics and ointments
3.3
Pronunciation: / zɪŋk ˈɑksaɪd / (AmE), / zɪŋk ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a white, powdery compound of zinc and oxygen, used as a pigment and in various products such as cosmetics and ointments
