unendorsed

adjective
not officially approved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈdɔrst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈdɔːst / (BrE)
not officially approved, supported, or given formal backing by an authority or group

Examples

Unendorsed policy
The new policy remained unendorsed by the board after several meetings.
His application was unendorsed, so it could not move forward in the process.
Many proposals stay unendorsed until they receive further review and discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approved - officially accepted or supported
  • synonym: unsupported - not given help or approval
