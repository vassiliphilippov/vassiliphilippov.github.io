giant fir

noun
lofty fir tree of northwestern America
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt fɝː / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt fɜː / (BrE)
a very tall fir tree native to the Pacific coastal regions of northwestern America, known for its long curving branches and deep green leaves

Examples

A tall giant fir
A fallen giant fir blocked the path after the heavy storm.
Many hikers stopped to marvel at the height of the ancient giant fir.
The dense forests of Oregon are home to the impressive giant fir.
