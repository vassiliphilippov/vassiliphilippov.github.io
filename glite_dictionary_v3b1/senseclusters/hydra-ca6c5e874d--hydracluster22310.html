Hydra

proper noun
large constellation in the southern sky
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪdrə / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪdrə / (BrE)
a large and faint constellation in the southern sky, located near the celestial equator between Virgo and Cancer

Examples

Hydra constellation
Hydra stretches across a vast area of the night sky between Virgo and Cancer.
Astronomers often observe Hydra to study its unique arrangement of stars.
The star Alphard marks the brightest point in the constellation Hydra.
