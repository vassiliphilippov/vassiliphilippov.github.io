almond

noun
tree that produces edible nuts
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlmənd / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd / (BrE)
a small deciduous tree native to Asia and North Africa, cultivated for its pink blossoms and edible nuts enclosed in a hard green hull

Examples

Almond tree blossoms
Every spring, the almond tree outside my window bursts into pink blossoms.
Southern California has an ideal climate for growing almond orchards.
The farmer planted a row of almond trees along the fence.
