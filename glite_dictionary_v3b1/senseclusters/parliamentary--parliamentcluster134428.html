parliamentary

adjective
of a legislative assembly's rules or procedures
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrləˈmɛn.t̬ə.ri / (AmE), / ˌpɑː.lɪˈmɛn.təri / (BrE)
Relating to, or in accordance with, the rules and customs of a legislative or deliberative assembly.

Examples

Parliamentary rules
He wrote a manual on parliamentary law for organizations and clubs.
She raised a point of order under the parliamentary rules governing the debate.
The committee followed strict parliamentary procedure during the meeting.
