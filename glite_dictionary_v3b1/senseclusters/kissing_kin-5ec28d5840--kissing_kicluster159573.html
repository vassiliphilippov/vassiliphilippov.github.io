kissing kin

noun
relatives close enough for affectionate greetings
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪsɪŋ kɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɪsɪŋ kɪn / (BrE)
relatives with whom one is familiar enough to greet with a kiss, often not immediate but more distant family members

Examples

Kissing kin at reunions
At family reunions, she always makes sure to say hello to all her kissing kin.
The wedding brought out a surprising number of distant kissing kin from across the country.
Though not immediate relatives, they were still considered kissing kin in her Southern community.
