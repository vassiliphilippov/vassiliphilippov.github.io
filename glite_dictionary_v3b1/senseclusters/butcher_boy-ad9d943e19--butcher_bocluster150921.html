butcher boy

noun
young assistant in butcher's shop
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊtʃɚ bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʊtʃə bɔɪ / (BrE)
a young male who works in a butcher's shop, often as an apprentice or assistant

Examples

A butcher boy apprentice

The butcher boy wrapped up the steaks and handed them to the customer with a smile.
The restaurant hired a new butcher boy to help with the early morning deliveries.
