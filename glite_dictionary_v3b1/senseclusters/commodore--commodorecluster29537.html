commodore

noun
naval officer above captain, below rear admiral
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.məˌdɔr / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.ə.dɔː / (BrE)
a commissioned naval officer ranking above a captain and below a rear admiral; the lowest grade of admiral

Examples

A commodore rank
He was promoted to commodore after years of distinguished service in the navy.
Many retired commodores continue to serve their communities in advisory roles.
The commodore gave the order to set sail at dawn.
