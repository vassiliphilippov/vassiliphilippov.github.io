mood swing

noun
sudden change in emotional state
Pronunciation: / mud swɪŋ / (AmE), / muːd swɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden or dramatic change in a person's emotional state, often occurring unpredictably

Examples

His mood swing from joy to anger surprised everyone in the room.
She experienced a severe mood swing after hearing the unexpected news.
