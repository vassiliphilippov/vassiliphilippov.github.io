sheepskin coat

noun
coat made from sheepskin
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːpskɪn koʊt / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpskɪn kəʊt / (BrE)
a coat made from the skin of a sheep with the wool still attached, typically used for warmth

Examples

A sheepskin coat
His old sheepskin coat was incredibly soft and comfortable.
She wore a sheepskin coat to stay warm during the harsh winter.
The sheepskin coat became fashionable in the 1970s and remains popular today.
