unreduced

adjective
not reduced; not diminished
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈduːst / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈdjuːst / (BrE)
Not altered, diminished, or made less by reduction; remaining in its original or full state.

Examples

An unreduced sauce
A tablespoon of unreduced sauce was left in the pan after cooking.
The document remained unreduced despite suggestions to cut its length.
Their enthusiasm was unreduced even after the long journey.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reduced - made smaller or less in amount, degree, or size
