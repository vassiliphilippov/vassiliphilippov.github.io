Atriplex hortensis

noun
an Asiatic leafy plant used as a potherb
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈtrɪplɛks hɔrˈtɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈatrɪplɛks hɔːˈtɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a species of plant native to Asia, resembling spinach, often used as a potherb and now found in Europe and North America

Examples

Atriplex hortensis plant
Atriplex hortensis thrives in temperate climates and is sometimes grown in vegetable gardens.
Gardeners often prefer Atriplex hortensis because it is easy to cultivate and resistant to pests.
