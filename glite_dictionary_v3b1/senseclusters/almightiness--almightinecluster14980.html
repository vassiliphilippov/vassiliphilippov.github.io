almightiness

noun
unlimited power
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈmaɪtinəs / (AmE), / ɔːlˈmaɪtinəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having unlimited power or authority, especially as attributed to God or a supreme being

Examples

The almightiness of God
Many religions describe the almightiness of their gods in sacred texts.
The king believed in his own almightiness and ruled without question.

Many religions speak of the almightiness of their deity.
The story describes the almightiness that created the universe from nothing.
He prayed for guidance, trusting in the almightiness of a higher power.
