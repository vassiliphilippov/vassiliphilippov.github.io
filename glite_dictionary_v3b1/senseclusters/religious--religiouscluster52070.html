religious

adjective
extremely scrupulous or conscientious
6.5 4.5
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
(of a person or their behavior) showing extreme care, scrupulousness, or conscientiousness in following rules or procedures.

Examples

Religious attention to detail
He was religious in his daily exercise routine, never missing a single session.
She is religious about checking her work for mistakes before submitting it.
The surgeon was religious in following every step of the sterilization process.
