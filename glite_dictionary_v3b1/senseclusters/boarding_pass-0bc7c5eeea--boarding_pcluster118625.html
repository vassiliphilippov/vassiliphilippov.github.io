boarding pass

noun
document allowing passenger to board a vessel or aircraft
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdɪŋ ˌpæs / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdɪŋ ˌpɑːs / (BrE)
an official document that allows a passenger to board a ship, plane, or other form of transport.

Examples

Show boarding pass at gate
Present your boarding pass and passport at the gate before departure.
She realized she had lost her boarding pass just as the airline began boarding.
The staff scanned each passenger’s boarding pass before letting them on the plane.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticket - a slip or card giving a person a right to enter a place or use a service
