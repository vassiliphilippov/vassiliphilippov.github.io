drolly

adverb
amusingly, oddly
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdroʊli / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊli / (BrE)
in an amusing or witty way that is slightly odd or whimsical

Examples

To drolly describe stories
She drolly described her misadventures during the trip, making everyone laugh.
The teacher drolly pointed out the irony in the situation to lighten the mood.
He drolly remarked that the cat seemed to be in charge of the household.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seriously - in a solemn or earnest way
  • synonym: wittily - in a clever and humorous way
