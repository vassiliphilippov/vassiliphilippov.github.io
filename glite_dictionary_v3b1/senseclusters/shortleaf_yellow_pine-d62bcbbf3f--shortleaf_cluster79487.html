shortleaf yellow pine

noun
a pine tree species of the southern US
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtˌlif ˈjɛloʊ paɪn / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtliːf ˈjɛləʊ paɪn / (BrE)
a large pine tree of the southern United States, characterized by short needles in clusters of two or three and reddish-brown bark when mature

Examples

The shortleaf yellow pine tree
Many birds build their nests in the branches of the shortleaf yellow pine.
The shortleaf yellow pine is valued for its strong and durable timber.
We hiked through a forest dominated by shortleaf yellow pine and oaks.

Related Terms
