Red Poll

noun
breed of hornless, reddish-brown cattle
Pronunciation: / rɛd poʊl / (AmE), / rɛd pəʊl / (BrE)
a hornless breed of cattle, reddish-brown in color, developed for both beef and dairy production

Examples

A Red Poll cow
At the agricultural show, a prize-winning Red Poll drew attention from visitors.
Many dairy farmers value the Red Poll for its high milk production and docile temperament.
The farm recently introduced a herd of Red Poll cattle to improve beef quality.
