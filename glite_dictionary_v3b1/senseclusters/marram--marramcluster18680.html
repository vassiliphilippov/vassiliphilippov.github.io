marram

noun
coastal dune grass
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmærəm / (AmE), / ˈmærəm / (BrE)
a coarse grass that grows on sand dunes and helps prevent erosion by stabilizing the sand

Examples

Coarse marram grass
The marram along the dunes protects the coastline from strong winds and waves.
Conservationists planted marram to help restore the eroded sand dunes.
Children ran through the tall marram while playing on the beach.
