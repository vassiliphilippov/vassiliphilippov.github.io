bridge circuit

noun
diamond-shaped electrical circuit for measurement
1.2 5.2
Pronunciation: /brɪdʒ ˈsɜːr.kət/ /; / /brɪdʒ ˈsɜːr.kɪt/ (AmE), /brɪdʒ ˈsɜː.kɪt/ /; / /brɪdʒ ˈsɜː.kət/ (BrE)
