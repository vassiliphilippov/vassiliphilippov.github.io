Basidiomycota

noun
division of fungi including mushrooms and related species
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˌsɪdi.oʊˈmaɪkoʊtə /; / bəˌsɪdi.oʊˈmaɪkətə / (AmE), / bəˌsɪdɪə(ʊ)ˈmʌɪkətə /; / bəˌsɪdi.əʊˈmaɪkətə / (BrE)
a large group (division) of fungi that produce spores on a basidium, including mushrooms, puffballs, rusts, smuts, and bracket fungi
