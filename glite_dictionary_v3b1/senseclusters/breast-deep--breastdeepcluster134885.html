breast-deep

adverb
reaching up to the breast
Pronunciation: / brɛst diːp / (AmE), / brɛst diːp / (BrE)
(of a liquid or other substance) reaching a height level with a person's breast.

Examples

Breast-deep water
She crossed the river, the water rising breast-deep around her.
The rescuers found the children standing breast-deep in mud.
We waded breast-deep through the flooded field.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: neck-deep - reaching up to the neck
  • degree lower: waist-deep - reaching up to the waist
