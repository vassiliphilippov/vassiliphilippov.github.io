paysage

noun
scenic landscape
Pronunciation: / peɪˈsɑːʒ / (AmE), / peɪˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
the natural scenery or appearance of a region, especially as seen in a wide view of the countryside

Examples

The paysage view
The artist painted a beautiful paysage inspired by the rolling hills and distant mountains.
We stopped to admire the peaceful paysage stretching out beneath the morning mist.
Her photographs capture the unique paysage of the French countryside in every season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scenery - the natural features of a landscape
