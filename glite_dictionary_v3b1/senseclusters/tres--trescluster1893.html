tres

numeral
Spanish for three
Pronunciation: / tɾes / (AmE), / tɾes / (BrE)
the Spanish word for the number three, sometimes used in English to refer to the number three in names or phrases

Examples

Los tres band
The band was called Los tres and played Latin jazz music.
She ordered tres tacos at the Mexican restaurant for lunch.
The movie was titled 'Los tres amigos', meaning 'The Three Friends'.
