suprahyoid

noun
muscle above hyoid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈhaɪɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈhaɪɔɪd / (BrE)
a muscle located above the hyoid bone, especially one of a group that helps with swallowing and tongue movement

Examples

Suprahyoid muscle
The doctor explained that the suprahyoid muscles play a key role in swallowing.
Damage to a suprahyoid can make it difficult to move the tongue properly.
Physical therapy helped strengthen his suprahyoid after the surgery.
