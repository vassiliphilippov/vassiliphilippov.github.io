white-tailed kite

noun
American bird of prey with a white tail
Pronunciation: / waɪt teɪld kaɪt / (AmE), / waɪt teɪld kaɪt / (BrE)
a grey-and-white bird of prey found in the Americas, especially in warm and tropical regions

Examples
