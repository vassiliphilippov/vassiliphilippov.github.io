collectivist

noun
group-oriented person
7.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktɪvɪst / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktɪvɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that the needs and goals of the group are more important than those of the individual

Examples

A collectivist thinker
As a collectivist, she always put her team's needs before her own.
In some cultures, being a collectivist is seen as a sign of respect for tradition.
The debate between the collectivist and the individualist highlighted their different worldviews.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualist - a person who values individual freedom and self-reliance over group goals
