irritable

adjective
easily annoyed or angered
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɪrɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
Easily annoyed or angered; likely to become impatient or upset.

Examples

Irritable mood
After a long day at work, Mark was especially irritable and snapped at small annoyances.
Lack of sleep can make anyone irritable and short-tempered.
The irritable cat hissed whenever someone came near her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily angered or upset
  • synonym: touchy - easily upset or offended
