prepubescence

noun
stage before puberty
8.4
Pronunciation: /priː.pjuː.ˈbes.əns/ /; / /prɪ.pjuː.ˈbes.əns/ (AmE), /priː.pjuː.ˈbes.əns/ /; / /prɪ.pjuː.ˈbes.əns/ (BrE)
the stage in a child's development just before puberty, when physical and emotional changes begin but the child is not yet sexually mature
