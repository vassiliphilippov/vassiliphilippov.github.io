soak up

verb
absorb liquid fully
4.4
Pronunciation: / soʊk ʌp / (AmE), / səʊk ʌp / (BrE)
to absorb liquid completely, especially by using a material like a sponge or cloth

Examples

Soak up the water
She used a cloth to soak up the water from the floor.
The paper towels quickly soak up the spilled milk on the counter.
These sponges are designed to soak up large amounts of liquid efficiently.
