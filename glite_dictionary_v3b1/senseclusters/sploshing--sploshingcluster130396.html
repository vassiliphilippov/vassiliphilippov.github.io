sploshing

noun
splashing action or sound
Pronunciation: / ˈsplɑʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsplɒʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the action or sound of making splashes, especially in water, mud, or similar substances

Examples

The sploshing water
The sploshing of rain on the roof helped him sleep through the night.
Children love the sploshing that comes from jumping in muddy puddles after a storm.
She heard the gentle sploshing of oars as the boat moved across the lake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sloshing - the sound or movement of liquid moving around noisily
  • synonym: splashing - the act of causing liquid to scatter or make noise
