puff

verb
to swell or cause to enlarge
4.1 2.05/m
Pronunciation: / pÊŒf / (AmE), / pÊŒf / (BrE)
(of part of the body or an object) to swell or become enlarged, or to cause something to swell.

Examples

To puff up
After the bee sting, her hand began to puff noticeably.
His cheeks puffed up as he blew into the balloon.
The bread puffed as it baked in the oven.
