charcoal

noun
black carbon material from burned wood
1.0 2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrˌkoʊl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl / (BrE)
a black, carbon-rich material made from burned wood, used as fuel or for drawing

Examples

Burned wood charcoal
Artists often use charcoal for sketching because of its rich, dark lines.
