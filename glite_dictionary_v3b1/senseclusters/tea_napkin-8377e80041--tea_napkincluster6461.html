tea napkin

noun
small napkin for tea service
Pronunciation: / ˈti ˌnæpkɪn / (AmE), / ˈtiː ˌnæp.kɪn / (BrE)
a small napkin used when tea is served, often for protecting clothing or handling food

Examples

A tea napkin on table
Each guest was given a crisp tea napkin with their cup and saucer.
She embroidered her initials on the corner of every tea napkin.
The hostess replaced the soiled tea napkin with a fresh one before serving dessert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: napkin - a piece of cloth or paper used at a meal to wipe the fingers or lips
