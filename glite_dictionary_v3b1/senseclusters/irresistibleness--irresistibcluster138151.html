irresistibleness

noun
quality of being impossible to resist
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈzɪstɪblnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being so attractive, appealing, or powerful that it cannot be resisted.

Examples

The irresistibleness of cookies
He couldn't ignore the irresistibleness of the freshly baked cookies on the table.
Many advertisers count on the irresistibleness of their products to boost sales.
The irresistibleness of her charm drew everyone close to her at the party.
