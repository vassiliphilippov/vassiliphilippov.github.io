contractor

noun
the player who declares trumps in bridge
Pronunciation: / kənˈtræktər / (AmE), / kənˈtræktə / (BrE)
(in contract bridge) the player who wins the bidding and declares which suit is to be trumps.

Examples

Bridge contractor
After the auction, the contractor announced that spades would be trumps.
As the contractor, she planned her play carefully to make the contract.
The contractor must fulfil the contract bid during the play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: declarer - the player who plays the hand for their side in bridge
