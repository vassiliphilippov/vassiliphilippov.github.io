motorcoach

noun
large passenger bus
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtərˌkoʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˈməʊtəkəʊtʃ / (BrE)
A large, comfortable bus designed to carry many passengers over longer distances, typically used for public transport or tours.

Examples

A motorcoach bus
A motorcoach offers more comfort on long-distance journeys than a regular city bus.
Our group traveled across the country by motorcoach, enjoying the scenic routes.
The motorcoach departs from the city center every two hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus - a large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road
  • synonym: double-decker - a bus with two decks, often used for city tours
  • degree lower: minibus - a small bus designed to carry fewer passengers
