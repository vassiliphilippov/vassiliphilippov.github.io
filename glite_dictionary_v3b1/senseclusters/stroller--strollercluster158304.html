stroller

noun
someone who walks at a leisurely pace
Pronunciation: / ˈstroʊlɚ / (AmE), / ˈstrəʊlə / (BrE)
a person who walks at a slow, relaxed pace, typically for pleasure rather than necessity

Examples

A slow stroller
As a habitual stroller, he loved to take his time along the riverbank.
Each morning, a group of elderly strollers gathered on the main street.
The park was filled with strollers enjoying the sunny afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hiker - someone who walks long distances in the countryside
  • synonym: wanderer - someone who travels without a fixed path or destination
