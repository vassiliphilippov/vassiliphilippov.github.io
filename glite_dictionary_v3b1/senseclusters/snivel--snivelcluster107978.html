snivel

verb
to draw mucus up through the nose
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsnɪ.vəl/ (AmE), /ˈsnɪ.vəl/ (BrE)
to inhale or draw mucus up through the nose, especially quietly or repeatedly

Examples

To snivel with a cold
Children often snivel during winter when the air is cold and dry.
He tried not to snivel when he caught a cold at school.
She sniveled quietly in the back row, hoping no one would notice her runny nose.
