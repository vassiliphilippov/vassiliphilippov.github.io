prime

adjective
at the best stage of development
1.3
Pronunciation: / praɪm / (AmE), / praɪm / (BrE)
(of a person or thing) at the best, most vigorous, or most successful stage; at one's peak.

Examples

Prime years
At 25, she was in the prime of her athletic career.
Some artists create their greatest works in the prime of their lives.
The tree was in its prime before the disease struck.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - in a worse or deteriorating stage
  • synonym: peak - at the highest level or point
