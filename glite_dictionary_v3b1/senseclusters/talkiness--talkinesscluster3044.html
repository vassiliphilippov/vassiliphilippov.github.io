talkiness

noun
tendency to talk a lot
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔki.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtɔːki.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being talkative or inclined to talk a lot

Examples

Her talkiness at parties
Her talkiness made her popular at social gatherings.
Some teachers appreciate a student's talkiness because it encourages class discussion.
His talkiness sometimes made it hard for others to get a word in.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loquacity - the quality of talking a lot
  • antonym: reticence - the quality of being reserved or not talking much
  • synonym: talkativeness - the state of being talkative
