wabi

noun
simple rustic beauty
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈwɑːbi / (BrE)
a Japanese aesthetic concept that values simple, rustic beauty and quietness

Examples

The wabi of tea
The wabi of the tea room created a calming and peaceful atmosphere.
She admired the wabi in the handmade pottery, appreciating its natural imperfections.
Many designers seek wabi in their work, focusing on simplicity and authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - graceful and simple beauty
  • synonym: wabi-sabi - a Japanese worldview centered on the acceptance of transience and imperfection
