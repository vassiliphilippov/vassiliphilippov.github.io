orb-weaving spider

noun
spider that spins circular webs
6.2 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrb ˌwiːvɪŋ ˈspaɪdər / (AmE), / ˈɔːb ˌwiːvɪŋ ˈspaɪdə / (BrE)
A spider belonging to the family Araneidae, known for spinning circular or near-circular webs to catch prey.

Examples

An orb-weaving spider can usually be found in gardens, hanging on its beautifully crafted web.
Children watched as a large orb-weaving spider trapped an insect in its sticky web.
The orb-weaving spider patiently rebuilt its web each morning after the dew evaporated.
