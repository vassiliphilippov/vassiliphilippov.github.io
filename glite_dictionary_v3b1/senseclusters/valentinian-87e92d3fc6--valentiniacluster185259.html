Valentinian

proper noun
Roman emperor name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvælənˈtɪniən / (AmE), / ˌvalənˈtɪnɪən / (BrE)
the name of several Roman emperors, especially Valentinian I, who reigned from 364 to 375 CE

Examples

Emperor Valentinian
The reign of Valentinian marked a period of military reform in the Roman Empire.
Historians often debate the policies implemented by Valentinian during his time as emperor.
Coins bearing the image of Valentinian have been found throughout Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Valentinian I - Roman emperor who reigned from 364 to 375 CE
  • synonym: Valentinian II - Roman emperor who reigned from 375 to 392 CE
