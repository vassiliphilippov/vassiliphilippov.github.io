Mir

noun
Russian peasant commune
Pronunciation: / mir / (AmE), / mɪə / (BrE)
a village community or peasant commune in imperial Russia that collectively managed land and local affairs

Examples

Russian Mir commune
The Mir played a central role in organizing agricultural work in rural Russia.
Decisions about land use were made collectively by the Mir members.
Historians study the Mir to understand rural life in imperial Russia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collective - a cooperative group managing property or resources together
  • synonym: commune - a group of people living together and sharing resources
