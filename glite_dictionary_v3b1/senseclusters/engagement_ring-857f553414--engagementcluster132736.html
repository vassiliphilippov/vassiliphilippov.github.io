engagement ring

noun
ring given to signify engagement
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt rɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt rɪŋ / (BrE)
a ring, usually with a precious stone or gemstone, given to someone to show that they are engaged to be married
