binomial theorem

noun
theorem on expanding binomials in algebra
Pronunciation: / baɪˈnoʊmiəl ˈθiːərəm / (AmE), / baɪˈnəʊmiəl ˈθɪərəm / (BrE)
a theorem in algebra that gives the expanded form of a binomial expression raised to any positive integer power
