tricycle

verb
to ride a tricycle
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪsɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈtraɪsɪkl / (BrE)
to ride or travel on a tricycle.

Examples

To tricycle around
The children spent the afternoon tricycling around the playground together.
He learned to balance by tricycling before moving on to a bicycle.
On weekends, she enjoys tricycling through the park with her friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cycle - to ride a bicycle or similar vehicle
  • antonym: walk - to move on foot at a regular pace
