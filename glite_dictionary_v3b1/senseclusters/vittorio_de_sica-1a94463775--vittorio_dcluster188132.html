Vittorio De Sica

proper noun
Italian film director and actor (1901–1974)
Pronunciation: / viˈtɔrioʊ deɪ ˈsiːkə / (AmE), / viˈtɔːri.əʊ deɪ ˈsiːkə / (BrE)
(1901–1974) Italian film director and actor, a leading figure in the neorealist movement, known for films such as 'Bicycle Thieves' and 'Umberto D.'

Examples

Vittorio De Sica films
Vittorio De Sica was praised for his groundbreaking film 'Bicycle Thieves', which is considered a masterpiece of world cinema.
Many film critics cite Vittorio De Sica as one of the founders of Italian neorealism.
The influence of Vittorio De Sica can still be seen in contemporary filmmaking worldwide.
