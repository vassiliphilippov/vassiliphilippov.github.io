Vittorio De Sica

proper noun

Pronunciation: /viː.ˈtɔːr.i.oʊ de ˈsiː.kə/ /; / /vɪ.ˈtɔːr.i.oʊ də ˈsiː.kə/ (AmE), /vɪ.ˈtɔːr.i.əʊ de ˈsiː.kə/ /; / /viː.ˈtɔːr.i.əʊ də ˈsiː.kə/ (BrE)
(1901–1974) Italian film director and actor, a leading figure in the neorealist movement, known for films such as 'Bicycle Thieves' and 'Umberto D.'

Vittorio De Sica films
