Twins

noun
the third sign of the zodiac (Gemini)
Pronunciation: / twɪnz / (AmE), / twɪnz / (BrE)
(in astrology) the third sign of the zodiac, also known as Gemini, covering the period from approximately May 21 to June 20.

Examples

Twins zodiac sign
People born under the sign of the Twins are often considered versatile and sociable.
She checked her horoscope to see what the Twins had in store for her this month.
The sun enters the sign of the Twins around late May each year.
