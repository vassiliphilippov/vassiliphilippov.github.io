span

noun
section of a bridge or structure crossing an obstacle
Pronunciation: / spæn / (AmE), / spæn / (BrE)
A part of a structure, such as a bridge, that stretches across an obstacle like a river, road, or valley.

Examples

Bridge span
A new span was added to accommodate increased traffic over the highway.
Engineers measured the length of the span crossing the river.
The central span of the bridge was lifted for repairs last month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arch - a curved structure supporting the weight of a bridge or roof
  • synonym: beam - a long, sturdy piece of squared timber or metal used in construction
