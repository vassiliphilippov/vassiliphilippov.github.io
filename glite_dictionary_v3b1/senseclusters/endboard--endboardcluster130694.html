endboard

noun
book cover or game boundary
Pronunciation: / ˈɛndˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈɛndˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board attached at the end of something (such as a book, crate, or shelf) to protect, close, or support it

Examples

The endboard of a shelf
The endboard of the antique book was beautifully decorated with gold patterns.
During the match, the ball bounced off the endboard and stayed in play.
He carefully repaired the damaged endboard to preserve the old novel.
The carpenter replaced the broken endboard on the old wooden shelf.
Make sure the endboard is securely fastened to prevent anything from sliding out.
They painted the endboard to match the rest of the furniture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundary - a line or object marking a limit in a game or area
  • synonym: cover - the outer protective part of a book
  • synonym: end panel - a panel forming the end of a box or piece of furniture
  • antonym: sideboard - a board forming the side of a box or crate
