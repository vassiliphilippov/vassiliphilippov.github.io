Examples

Synonyms

    5.1 1.16/m

  • small-grained
    4.0 0.00/m
    small-grained describes something made of very fine tiny particles, while grainy covers rough or coarse textures with visible grains.
  • granulated
    4.2 0.02/m
    granulated refers to material formed into separate grains, often by processing, while grainy describes a grain-like texture or appearance.
  • gritty
    4.1 1.26/m
    gritty feels coarse and contains hard small particles like sand, while grainy simply means having a granular or textured surface.
  • granulose
    9.9 0.00/m
    granulose is a technical word for surfaces covered in tiny particles, while grainy is more common and broadly means granular or rough.
  • mealy
    7.1 0.38/m
    mealy feels dry, powdery, and soft like ground meal, while grainy means rougher texture with distinct visible grains or particles.
