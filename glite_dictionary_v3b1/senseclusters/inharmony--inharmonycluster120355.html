inharmony

noun
inner conflict
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɑːrməni / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːməni / (BrE)
a state of inner conflict or lack of harmony within a person or group

Examples

Inner inharmony
He struggled with a deep sense of inharmony after moving to a new city.
The team's inharmony made it difficult for them to achieve their goals.
Meditation helped her overcome the inharmony she felt in her daily life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discord - a lack of agreement or harmony
  • antonym: harmony - a state of agreement or peaceful coexistence
