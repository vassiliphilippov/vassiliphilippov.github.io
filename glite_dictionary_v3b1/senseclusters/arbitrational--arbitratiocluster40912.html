arbitrational

adjective
relating to arbitration
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənl / (AmE), / ˌɑːbɪˈtreɪʃənl / (BrE)
Relating to or resulting from arbitration—the process of resolving a dispute by an impartial third party.

Examples

Arbitrational decision
An arbitrational decision was made to resolve the contract dispute quickly.
His expertise in arbitrational proceedings made him a sought-after mediator.
The parties agreed to an arbitrational settlement instead of pursuing lengthy litigation.
