yellow jacaranda

noun
a South American ornamental tree with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛl.oʊ dʒəˈkɑr.əndə / (AmE), / ˈjɛl.əʊ dʒəˈkær.əndə / (BrE)
A semi-evergreen tree native to South America, with pinnate leaves and golden yellow flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.
