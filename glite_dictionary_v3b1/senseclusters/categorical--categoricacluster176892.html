categorical

adjective
unconditional; without reservations
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkæt̬.əˈɡɔːr.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌkæt.ɪˈɡɒr.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
expressed without any doubt or qualification; unconditional and explicit.

Examples

Categorical refusal
His refusal was categorical and left no room for negotiation.
She gave a categorical assurance that the contract would not be changed.
The CEO issued a categorical denial of the rumors.
