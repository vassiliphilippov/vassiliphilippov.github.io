bunk

noun
nonsense; absurd or false talk
8.9
Pronunciation: / bʌŋk / (AmE), / bʌŋk / (BrE)
foolish talk or nonsense; statements that are not true or are absurd

Examples

That is bunk
Don't give me that bunk; I know you're making it up.
Everything he said about the project was just bunk and nobody believed him.
The politician dismissed the rumors as complete bunk and refused to discuss them further.
Don’t believe all that bunk you read online.
I listened to his explanation, but it was just bunk.
The report was dismissed as political bunk by critics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baloney - foolish or deceptive talk; nonsense
  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words that have no meaning or make no sense
  • antonym: sense - reasonable or meaningful talk (as opposed to nonsense)
