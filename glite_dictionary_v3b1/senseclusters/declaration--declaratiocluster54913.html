declaration

noun
final bid in contract bridge
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛkləˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
In contract bridge, the final bid that establishes the contract and determines the number of tricks the bidder must make.

Examples

Bridge declaration
After a bold declaration, the opponents prepared their strategies for defense.
Her declaration set the contract at four spades, giving her team a challenging goal.
The team celebrated when their declaration led to a successful game.
