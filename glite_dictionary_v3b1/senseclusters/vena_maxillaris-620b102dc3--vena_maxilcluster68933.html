A vein that is the posterior continuation of the pterygoid plexus and joins the superficial temporal vein to form the retromandibular vein.

Examples

Vena maxillaris vein
During dissection, careful identification of the vena maxillaris is necessary to prevent excessive bleeding.
The vena maxillaris runs behind the jaw and is important in maxillofacial surgery.
The retromandibular vein is formed by the union of the superficial temporal vein and the vena maxillaris.
