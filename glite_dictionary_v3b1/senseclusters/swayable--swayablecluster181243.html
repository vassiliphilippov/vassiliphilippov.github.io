swayable

adjective
able to bend easily
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsweɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈsweɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈsweɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈsweɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
able to move or bend back and forth easily, especially when acted on by an external force such as wind or motion

Examples

Swayable branches
The young trees were so swayable that they bent with every gust of wind.
Because the bridge was swayable, it moved slightly when people walked across it.
The tall grass in the field looked especially swayable during the afternoon breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigid - not able to bend or be moved easily
