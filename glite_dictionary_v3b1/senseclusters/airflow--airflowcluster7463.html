Synonyms

  • flow of air
    2.6 0.13/m
    flow of air is the general movement of air caused by nature or machines, while airflow focuses on how air moves through and around things.
  • air current
    3.3 0.43/m
    air current is a fast movement of air from one place to another, while airflow describes how air moves through and around objects.
  • current of air
    3.5 0.10/m
    current of air is a flow from high to low pressure that can be strong, while airflow highlights movement through and around things.
  • airstream
    4.2 0.02/m
    airstream is a well-defined wind or steady flow often named by weather experts, while airflow is the general movement of air around things.
