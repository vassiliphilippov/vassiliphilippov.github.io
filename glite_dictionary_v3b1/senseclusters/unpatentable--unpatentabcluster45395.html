unpatentable

adjective
not eligible for patent
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈpætəntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈpætəntəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be legally protected by a patent because it does not meet the required criteria

Examples

Unpatentable design
Because the design was already in use, it was declared unpatentable by the patent office.
Simple ideas that lack originality are often considered unpatentable under current law.
The lawyer explained why the client's invention was unpatentable and suggested making improvements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: patentable - able to be legally protected by a patent
