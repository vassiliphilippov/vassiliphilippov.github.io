humanist

noun
advocate or follower of humanism
1.8 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhjuː.mə.nɪst/ /; / /ˈhuː.mə.nɪst/ (AmE), /ˈhjuː.mə.nɪst/ (BrE)
a person who supports or practices humanism, emphasizing the value and agency of human beings individually and collectively

Examples

A humanist thinker
As a lifelong humanist, she believes in the inherent dignity of every person.
He joined a humanist organization dedicated to promoting secular ethics and social justice.
Many Renaissance thinkers were prominent humanists who emphasized education and critical inquiry.
