RSVP

noun
invitation reply request
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɑr ˌɛs ˌvi ˈpi /; / ˌɑrˌɛsˌviːˈpiː / (AmE), / ˌɑː ˌɛs ˌviː ˈpiː /; / ˌɑːˌɛsˌviːˈpiː / (BrE)
a request for or a response to an invitation, asking or telling whether someone will attend an event

Examples

A wedding RSVP
Please check your calendar and send your RSVP by the end of the week.
The wedding invitation included an RSVP card for guests to confirm their attendance.
If you do not RSVP, the hosts will not know how many people to expect.
Please send your RSVP by Friday so we can finalize the guest list.
We received an RSVP from everyone except two people.
The invitation included a card for guests to write their RSVP.
