like it or lump it

verb
accept or reject without alternatives
5.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /laɪk ɪt ɔːr lʌmp ɪt/ /; / /laɪk ɪt ər lʌmp ɪt/ (AmE), /laɪk ɪt ɔː lʌmp ɪt/ /; / /laɪk ɪt ə lʌmp ɪt/ (BrE)
used to say that someone must accept a situation as it is, whether they like it or not, because it cannot be changed

Examples

To like it or lump it
It's the only option available, so like it or lump it, that's what we have to do.
The new policy will be implemented next week, like it or lump it.
You’ll have to share your room with your brother, like it or lump it.
