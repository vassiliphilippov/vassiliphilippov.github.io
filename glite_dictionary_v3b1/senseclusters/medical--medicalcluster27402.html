medical

adjective
treatable by medicine, not surgery
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to medicine, medical treatment, or the science/practice of medicine

Examples

Medical treatment

She decided to study medical science at university.
The hospital offers a wide range of medical services to the community.
She is studying to become a medical doctor at the university.
Some companies offer comprehensive medical insurance to their employees.
The hospital is known for its advanced medical equipment and skilled staff.
