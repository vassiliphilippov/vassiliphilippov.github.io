flush

noun
period of greatest prosperity/productivity
Pronunciation: / flʌʃ / (AmE), / flʌʃ / (BrE)
A period of greatest prosperity or productivity.

Examples

A flush of success
Farmers enjoyed a brief flush of abundance after the rains.
The company experienced a flush of success during the late 1990s.
This is the flush of youth, when possibilities seem endless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peak - The highest point of something, especially times of success or activity
  • antonym: trough - A period of low activity or success, opposite of peak or flush
