excursion

noun
a deviation from a set course or plan
3.9 4.0
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈskɜːr.ʒən/ /; / /ɛk.ˈskɜːr.ʒən/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈskɜː.ʒən/ /; / /ɛk.ˈskɜː.ʒən/ (BrE)
