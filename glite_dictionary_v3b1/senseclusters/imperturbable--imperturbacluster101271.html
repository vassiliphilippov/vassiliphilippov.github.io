imperturbable

adjective
calm, not easily upset
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmpərˈtɜrbəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪmpəˈtɜːbəbl / (BrE)
calm and unable to be upset or disturbed; showing extreme composure even in difficult situations

Examples

An imperturbable calm
Despite the chaos around her, Julia remained imperturbable throughout the entire ordeal.
Even when faced with harsh criticism, she responded in an imperturbable tone.
His imperturbable manner reassured everyone during the emergency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excitable - easily excited or upset
  • synonym: unflappable - not easily upset or confused, showing calmness under pressure
