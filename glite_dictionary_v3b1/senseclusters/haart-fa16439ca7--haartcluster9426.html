HAART

noun
antiretroviral therapy for HIV/AIDS
4.7 0.42/m
Pronunciation: / h…ërt / (AmE), / h…ëÀêt / (BrE)
a combination therapy using several antiretroviral drugs, especially protease inhibitors and reverse transcriptase inhibitors, to treat HIV infection and AIDS

Examples

HAART treatment

Many patients have experienced improved outcomes thanks to advances in HAART.
The doctor recommended starting HAART soon after the HIV diagnosis was confirmed.
