daylighting

noun
use of sunlight indoors
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌlaɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌlaɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of using natural sunlight to illuminate the interior of a building

Examples

Daylighting in buildings
The architects designed the building with daylighting in mind to create a brighter environment.
By maximizing daylighting, the office reduces its reliance on electric lights during the day.
Many modern schools incorporate daylighting to improve student well-being and save energy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificial lighting - illumination using electric lights or other man-made sources
  • synonym: natural lighting - illumination using sunlight instead of artificial sources
