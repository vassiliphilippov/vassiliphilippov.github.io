nitrocotton

noun
cellulose nitrate used in explosives and lacquers
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnaɪtroʊˈkɑːtən / (AmE), / ˌnaɪtrəʊˈkɒtən / (BrE)
A nitric acid ester of cellulose used in the manufacture of lacquers, explosives, and celluloid.

Examples

Clear nitrocotton lacquer
A solution of nitrocotton is often used to make clear, durable lacquers.
Improper storage of nitrocotton can lead to dangerous chemical reactions.
The factory produced nitrocotton for use in both film and explosives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guncotton - explosive form of nitrocellulose
  • synonym: nitrocellulose - cellulose nitrate used in plastics and explosives
