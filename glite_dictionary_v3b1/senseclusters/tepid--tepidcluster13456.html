Examples

Tepid response

The audience gave only tepid applause after the long speech.

Synonyms

  • unenthusiastic
    3.9 0.33/m
    unenthusiastic shows a clear lack of excitement in people or actions, while tepid sounds slightly milder and more formal.
  • halfhearted
    4.3 0.46/m

  • apathetic
    6.8 1.67/m

  • lackluster
    6.1 2.29/m
