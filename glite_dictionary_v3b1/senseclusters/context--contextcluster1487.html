context

noun
surrounding words shaping meaning
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːn.tɛkst / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.tɛkst / (BrE)
the words or passages that come before and after a particular word or phrase and help to explain its meaning

Examples

Word context
The meaning of a phrase often changes depending on its context.
Without context, her remarks could easily be misunderstood.
You need to understand the context of the sentence to interpret the word correctly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: situation - the circumstances in which something occurs
