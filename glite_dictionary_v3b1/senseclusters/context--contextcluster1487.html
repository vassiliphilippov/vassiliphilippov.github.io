context

noun
surrounding words shaping meaning
2.4 7.40/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀên.t…õkst / (AmE), / Ààk…ín.t…õkst / (BrE)
the words or passages that come before and after a particular word or phrase and help to explain its meaning

Examples

Word context
The meaning of a phrase often changes depending on its context.
Without context, her remarks could easily be misunderstood.
You need to understand the context of the sentence to interpret the word correctly.

Synonyms

  • linguistic context
    5.1 0.02/m
    linguistic context shows the surrounding words or sentences that explain meaning, while context is the nearby text before and after a word.
  • context of use
    4.6 0.06/m
    context of use refers to the situation or setting that surrounds an utterance, while context means the actual words before and after a phrase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: situation - the circumstances in which something occurs
