metallized dye

noun
dye with chelated metal ion
5.6
Pronunciation: / məˈtæləˌzaɪd daɪ / (AmE), / mɪˈtal.ɪˌzaɪd daɪ / (BrE)
an acid dye in which the negatively charged ion contains a chelated metal atom, used especially in textile dyeing

Examples

A metallized dye solution
Researchers tested the properties of a new metallized dye in acid solutions.
The textile industry often uses metallized dye to produce more vibrant and colorfast fabrics.
When exposed to sunlight, fabric treated with metallized dye maintained its color longer than conventional dyes.
