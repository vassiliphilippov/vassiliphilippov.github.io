scrubber

noun
brush for heavy cleaning
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈskrʌbə / (BrE)
a brush with short stiff bristles used for heavy cleaning tasks

Examples

A kitchen scrubber
After soaking the pot, he used a scrubber to remove the burnt residue.
She grabbed a scrubber to clean the greasy pans.
The tiles were so dirty that only a scrubber could get them clean.
