dwarf

verb
make seem small by comparison
4.9 3.87/m
Pronunciation: / dw…îrf / (AmE), / dw…îÀêf / (BrE)
to make someone or something seem small or less important by comparison, or to limit growth

Examples

This gigantic oak tree dwarfs everything else in the park.
