flaker

noun
machine making flakes
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪkər / (AmE), / ˈfleɪkə / (BrE)
a machine or person that produces thin flakes from a material, especially for making cereal products

Examples

Cereal flaker
The cereal company invested in a new flaker to improve the texture of their breakfast flakes.
A skilled operator can adjust the flaker to produce flakes of different thicknesses.
After maintenance, the flaker was able to process more grain per hour.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crusher - a machine that crushes materials into smaller pieces
  • synonym: miller - a person or machine that grinds grain into flour
