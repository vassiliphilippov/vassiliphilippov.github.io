L-plate

noun
sign for cars driven by learner drivers
6.5 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈel.pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈel.pleɪt/ (BrE)
a square sign with the letter 'L', attached to the front and rear of a vehicle to show that the driver is a learner

Examples

An L-plate sign
By law, every learner must display an L-plate on both the front and back of the car.
Seeing the L-plate, other drivers gave her more space on the road.
The L-plates fell off during her driving lesson, so she had to pull over.

Related Terms

  • synonym: learner driver - a person who is learning to drive and has not yet passed the driving test
