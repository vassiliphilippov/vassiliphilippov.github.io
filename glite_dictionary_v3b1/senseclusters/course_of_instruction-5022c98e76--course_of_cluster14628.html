course of instruction

noun
structured series of lessons or classes
Pronunciation: / kɔrs əv ɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / kɔːs əv ɪnˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
A series of lessons or classes in a particular subject, designed to teach specific skills or knowledge.

Examples

A course of instruction in French
After enrolling in the university, she signed up for a course of instruction in French literature.
Successful completion of the course of instruction leads to a recognized qualification.
The course of instruction included weekly lectures and laboratory sessions.
