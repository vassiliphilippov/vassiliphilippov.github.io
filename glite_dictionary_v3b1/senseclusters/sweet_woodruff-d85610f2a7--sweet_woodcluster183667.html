sweet woodruff

noun
fragrant perennial plant used as flavoring, ground cover
Pronunciation: / swit ˈwʊdˌrʌf / (AmE), / swiːt ˈwʊd.rʌf /; / swiːt ˈwʊdrʌf / (BrE)
a fragrant, low-growing perennial plant (Galium odoratum) with small white flowers and narrow leaves, used as ground cover and to flavor drinks
