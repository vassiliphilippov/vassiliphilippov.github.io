alchemy

noun
mysterious quality in a relationship
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.kə.mi / (AmE), / ˈæl.kə.mi / (BrE)
a mysterious or powerful quality that makes a relationship, especially between people, work well or seem extraordinary

Examples

Alchemy of friendship
Sometimes, the alchemy of a good team can't be explained—it's just there.
Their friendship is held together by a certain alchemy that neither of them fully understands.
There was a unique alchemy between the lead actors that captivated the audience.
