youth crusade

noun
a reform movement led by young people
3.9
Pronunciation: / juθ kruˈseɪd / (AmE), / juːθ kruˈseɪd / (BrE)
a political, religious, or social reform movement that is largely organized or led by young people

Examples

A youth crusade movement
During the 1960s, a powerful youth crusade swept through the nation demanding civil rights.
Her involvement in the youth crusade inspired many others to take part in social activism.
The organization launched a youth crusade to promote environmental awareness in schools.

  • synonym: crusade - a vigorous campaign for change or reform
