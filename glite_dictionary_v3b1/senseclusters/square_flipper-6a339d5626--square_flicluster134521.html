square flipper

noun
Arctic seal with bristled muzzle
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɛr ˌflɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈskwɛə ˌflɪpə / (BrE)
A medium-sized, greyish to yellow seal of the Arctic Ocean, having bristles on each side of its muzzle; also known as the bearded seal.
