neorealism

noun
realistic artistic style
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈriːəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈrɪəˌlɪzəm / (BrE)
a style in art, literature, or film that focuses on depicting everyday life and real social issues, often with a sense of authenticity

Examples

Italian neorealism films
Italian directors like Vittorio De Sica helped define neorealism in postwar cinema.
The novel's neorealism brings the struggles of ordinary people to the forefront.
Many critics praise the film's use of neorealism to portray real social problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: realism - an artistic style that represents subjects truthfully
  • antonym: romanticism - an artistic style emphasizing emotion and imagination over reality
