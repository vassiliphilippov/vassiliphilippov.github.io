transmittable

adjective
capable of being transmitted (infection)
Pronunciation: / trænˈsmɪtəbl̩ / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪtəbl̩ / (BrE)
(of a disease or condition) capable of being passed from one person, animal, or place to another; contagious or infectious.

Examples

Transmittable disease
Proper hygiene can reduce the risk of transmittable diseases spreading in a community.
Some viruses are much less transmittable through casual contact than others.
The flu is highly transmittable during the early stages of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious - (of disease) able to be transmitted from one person to another by direct or indirect contact
  • synonym: infectious - (of disease) able to be spread from one individual to another
  • antonym: nontransmissible - not capable of being transmitted
