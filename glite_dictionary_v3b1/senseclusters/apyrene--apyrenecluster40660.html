apyrene

adjective
lacking a nucleus
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈpaɪriːn / (AmE), / əˈpaɪriːn / (BrE)
describing a sperm cell that lacks a nucleus or genetic material

Examples

Apyrene sperm cell
The biologist identified several apyrene sperm among the sample collected from the insect.
Research shows that apyrene sperm do not contribute genetic material during fertilization.
Under the microscope, apyrene sperm can be distinguished by their lack of a nucleus.

Related Terms
