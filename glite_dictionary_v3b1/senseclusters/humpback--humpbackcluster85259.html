humpback

noun
a species of large whale
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmpˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpbæk / (BrE)
a large baleen whale with long flippers and a distinctive arching of its back when diving, known for its complex songs and acrobatic behavior

Examples

Humpback whale
Every summer, tourists gather to watch the humpback whales migrating along the coast.
Scientists are studying the songs of the humpback to better understand whale communication.
The humpback is easily recognized by the distinctive arch of its back before a dive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blue whale - the largest species of baleen whale
  • synonym: humpback whale - another term for the humpback whale (Megaptera novaeangliae)
