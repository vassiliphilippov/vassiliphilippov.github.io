foetid pothos

noun
a fetid swamp plant of eastern North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfiː.tɪd ˈpoʊ.θɑs / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.tɪd ˈpɒ.θɒs / (BrE)
A deciduous, perennial, low-growing swamp plant of eastern North America with a strong unpleasant odor, minute flowers, and a mottled greenish or purple cowl-shaped spathe.
