foetid pothos

noun
a fetid swamp plant of eastern North America

Pronunciation: /ˈfe.tɪd ˈpoʊ.θoʊs/ /; / /ˈfiː.tɪd ˈpoʊ.θoʊs/ (AmE), /ˈfe.tɪd ˈpəʊ.θəʊs/ /; / /ˈfiː.tɪd ˈpəʊ.θəʊs/ (BrE)
