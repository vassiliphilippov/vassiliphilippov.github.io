arthrosporous

adjective
relating to arthrospores
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrθrəˈspoʊrəs / (AmE), / ˌɑːθrəˈspɔːrəs / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by arthrospores (a type of spore formed by the breaking up of hyphae in fungi or algae)

Examples

Arthrosporous fungi
Mycologists focused on the arthrosporous organisms during their research.
Some arthrosporous bacteria are resilient in harsh environments.
The arthrosporous nature of the fungus was evident under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arthrospore - a type of spore formed by fragmentation of hyphae
