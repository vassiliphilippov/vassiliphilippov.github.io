smeller

noun
one who detects scents
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsmɛlə / (BrE)
a person or animal that detects or identifies scents using their nose

Examples

A good smeller
The bloodhound is a remarkable smeller and can track scents over long distances.
As a keen smeller, she could always tell when someone was baking cookies nearby.
