spinout

noun
new company from division
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈspɪnˌaʊt / (BrE)
a company or organization created by separating part of an existing business to form a new independent entity

Examples

A tech spinout
The university created a spinout to commercialize its latest research in biotechnology.
Several employees left to form a spinout focused on developing new software tools.
After the spinout, the new company quickly attracted investors interested in its innovative products.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinoff - a new company formed from part of an existing one
  • antonym: subsidiary - a company controlled by another company
