Se

noun
chemical symbol for selenium (element)
8.8
Pronunciation: / siː / (AmE), / siː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for selenium, a toxic nonmetallic element related to sulfur and tellurium, used in photocells and found in some ores

Examples

Se chemical element
Photoconductive cells often rely on the unique properties of Se for their function.
The chemical symbol Se is used to represent selenium on the periodic table.
Traces of Se can be found in sulfide ores such as pyrite.
