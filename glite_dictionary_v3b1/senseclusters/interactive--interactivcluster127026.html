interactive

adjective
involving active participation
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntərˈæktɪv / (BrE)
allowing people or things to communicate, respond, or work together actively, especially through technology or direct involvement

Examples

Interactive exhibits
The museum features interactive exhibits that let visitors try out scientific experiments.
Our new website has an interactive map to help users find local services easily.
The teacher used interactive activities to keep the students engaged during the lesson.

Related Terms

  • synonym: participatory - involving active participation by people
  • antonym: passive - not actively participating or responding
