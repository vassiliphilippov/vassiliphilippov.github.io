swamp honeysuckle

noun
shrub with honeysuckle-like flowers, found in eastern U.S. swamps
8.8 7.0
Pronunciation: /swɑːmp ˈhʌn.ɪ.sʌk.əl/ /; / /swɔːmp ˈhʌn.ɪ.sʌk.əl/ (AmE), /swɒmp ˈhʌn.ɪ.sʌk.əl/ (BrE)
