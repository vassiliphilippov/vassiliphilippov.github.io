homopolymer

noun
polymer of one monomer
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmoʊˈpɑlɪmər / (AmE), / ˌhəʊməʊˈpɒlɪmə / (BrE)
a polymer made by linking together only one type of monomer unit

Examples

A homopolymer chain
Polyethylene is a widely used homopolymer found in many plastic products.
Scientists compared the properties of a homopolymer and a copolymer in their experiment.
A homopolymer consists of repeating units of the same monomer throughout its structure.
