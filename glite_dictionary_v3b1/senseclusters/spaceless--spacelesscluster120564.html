spaceless

adjective
without word spaces
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsləs / (BrE)
written or printed with no spaces between words, causing the words to run together

Examples

Spaceless text
The ancient manuscript was completely spaceless, making it difficult to read.
Some early computer code was written in a spaceless format to save memory.
The artist created a spaceless poem where all the words flowed together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unspaced - written without spaces between words
