nanitic

adjective
first worker ants
9.9
Pronunciation: / nəˈnɪtɪk / (AmE), / nəˈnɪtɪk / (BrE)
describing the first, usually smaller, worker ants produced by a newly founded queen in an ant colony

Examples

Nanitic ants
The nanitic ants are responsible for caring for the queen's first eggs.
Because nanitic workers are smaller, they often perform lighter tasks in the early colony.
Scientists observed the behavior of nanitic individuals during the initial stages of colony development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: worker - a sterile member of an ant colony that performs labor
