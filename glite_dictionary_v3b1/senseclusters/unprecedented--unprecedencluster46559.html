unprecedented

adjective
never done or known before
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɛsɪˌdɛntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɛsɪdɛntɪd / (BrE)
never having happened or existed before; without previous example or precedent.

Examples

An unprecedented event
Her unprecedented achievement in the competition surprised everyone.
The discovery of the new planet was described as unprecedented in the field of astronomy.
There was unprecedented demand for the new product after its release.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ordinary - with nothing special or unusual; routine
  • synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
