go according to plan

verb
happen as intended or expected
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ əˈkɔrdɪŋ tə plæn / (AmE), / ɡəʊ əˈkɔːdɪŋ tə plæn / (BrE)
to happen or develop in the way that was intended or anticipated

Examples

To go according to plan
Despite careful preparation, nothing seemed to go according to plan that day.
Everything didn't go according to plan, but the event was still a success.
Our trip will only be enjoyable if things go according to plan.
