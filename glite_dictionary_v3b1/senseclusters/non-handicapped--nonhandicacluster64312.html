non-handicapped

adjective
not disabled
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈhændikæpt / (AmE), / nɒn ˈhændikæpt / (BrE)
not having a physical or mental disability; able-bodied or not disabled

Examples

Non-handicapped child
The new playground is designed for both non-handicapped and disabled children to play together.
Special parking spaces are reserved for those who are not non-handicapped.
The event welcomes non-handicapped participants as well as those with disabilities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disabled - having a physical or mental disability
