ashtray

noun
container for cigarette or cigar ash
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃˌtreɪ / (AmE), / ˈæʃ.treɪ / (BrE)
A small container used for holding ash and cigarette or cigar butts, typically found where people smoke.

Examples

A ashtray on table
He flicked the ashes of his cigarette into the ashtray on the table.
Please empty the ashtray before guests arrive.
The glass ashtray was full by the end of the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tray - a flat, shallow container for carrying things
