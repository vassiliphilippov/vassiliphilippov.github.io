life advice

noun
guidance on personal matters
1.8
Pronunciation: / laɪf ədˈvaɪs / (AmE), / laɪf ədˈvaɪs / (BrE)
guidance or recommendations offered to help someone with decisions and challenges in their personal life.

Examples
