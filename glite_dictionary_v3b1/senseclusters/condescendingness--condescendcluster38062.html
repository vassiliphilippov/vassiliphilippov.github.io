Examples

Condescendingness in speech
Despite his condescendingness, he believed he was being genuinely kind.
His condescendingness was evident in the way he spoke to the junior staff.

Synonyms

  • condescension
    7.9 1.39/m

  • patronization
    8.2 0.07/m
    patronization is the act of treating someone as less capable or important, while condescendingness is the general patronizing quality behind it.
  • superciliousness
    9.8 0.03/m
    superciliousness is a haughty, proud manner that shows superiority, while condescendingness is more about talking down in a polite way.
