uncropped

adjective
not trimmed (image)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrɑpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkrɒpt / (BrE)
(of a photograph or image) not cut or trimmed; remaining in its original frame, size, or composition.

Examples

Uncropped photo
The photographer released an uncropped version of the portrait to show the original framing.
Please upload only uncropped photos so the editors can choose the best composition.
The magazine demanded the uncropped image because the cropped version removed important context.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cropped - cut or trimmed to change the framing or remove parts of an image
  • synonym: original - the unedited or initial version of an image, before any trimming or alteration
