pinic

adjective
from pine trees
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnɪk / (AmE), / ˈpɪnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or derived from pine trees, especially in reference to pinic acid or substances from pine resin

Examples

Pinic acid
The chemist studied the pinic compounds extracted from the pine resin.
Researchers analyzed the pinic acid found in the forest samples.
The pinic scent of the solution indicated its origin from pine trees.
