restretch

verb
to stretch again
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈstrɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈstrɛtʃ / (BrE)
to stretch one's body or muscles again, especially to relieve stiffness or prepare for activity

Examples

To restretch your muscles
After sitting at his desk for hours, he stood up to restretch his arms and legs.
The coach told the players to restretch before starting the second half of the game.
If you feel tightness, it's helpful to restretch your muscles gently.
