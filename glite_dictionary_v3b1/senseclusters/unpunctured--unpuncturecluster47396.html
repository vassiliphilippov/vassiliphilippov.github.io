unpunctured

adjective
not pierced
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌŋktʃɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌŋktʃəd / (BrE)
not pierced or penetrated by a hole or puncture

Examples

An unpunctured balloon
The balloon remained unpunctured after the children played with it all afternoon.
Please make sure the packaging is unpunctured before you open the box.
Despite the sharp rocks, his bicycle tire was still unpunctured at the end of the ride.
