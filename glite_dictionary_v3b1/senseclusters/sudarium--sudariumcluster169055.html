sudarium

noun
face cloth relic
9.9
Pronunciation: / suˈdɛəriəm / (AmE), / suˈdɛəriəm / (BrE)
a cloth used to wipe the face, especially one regarded as a religious relic

Examples

Relic sudarium
The ancient church claims to possess a sudarium that once belonged to a saint.
Pilgrims traveled from afar to see the famous sudarium displayed in the cathedral.
Historians debate the authenticity of the sudarium said to have covered Christ's face.
