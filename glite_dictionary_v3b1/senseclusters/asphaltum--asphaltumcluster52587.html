asphaltum

noun
tar-like paving material
7.2
Pronunciation: / æsˈfɔltəm / (AmE), / æsˈfɑːltəm / (BrE)
a dark, tar-like substance used for paving roads or waterproofing surfaces

Examples

Road asphaltum
The workers spread asphaltum over the old road to create a smooth surface.
Ancient builders sometimes used asphaltum to waterproof the foundations of their structures.
A thick layer of asphaltum protected the roof from heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asphalt - a mixture of bitumen and mineral matter used for paving
  • synonym: bitumen - a black, sticky substance used in road construction
