Unknown Soldier

noun
an unidentified war memorialized soldier
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnoʊn ˈsoʊldʒɚ / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊn ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
An unidentified soldier who is commemorated as a symbol of all those who have died in war, especially those whose remains are unidentified.

Examples

Tomb of the Unknown Soldier
Every year, a wreath is laid at the tomb of the Unknown Soldier to honor fallen heroes.
Many countries have a monument dedicated to an Unknown Soldier as a tribute to unidentified war dead.
The story of the Unknown Soldier reminds us of the sacrifices made during wartime.
