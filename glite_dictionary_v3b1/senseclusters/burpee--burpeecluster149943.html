burpee

verb
to do squat-push-up-jump
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːpi / (AmE), / ˈbɜːpi / (BrE)
to perform a physical exercise that combines a squat, a push-up, and a jump in one continuous movement

Examples

To burpee quickly
He tries to burpee as many times as possible in one minute for his workout.
The coach asked everyone to burpee after each lap around the gym.
If you want to build endurance, add more burpee sets to your routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jumping jack - an exercise involving jumping to a position with legs spread and arms overhead
  • synonym: push-up - an exercise in which you raise and lower your body using your arms
