tiltmeter

noun
angle measuring device
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪlt.miː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈtɪlt.miː.tə/ (BrE)
a device or instrument used to measure the angle or small changes in the tilt or inclination of an object, ground, or structure

Examples

A tiltmeter device
The engineer used a tiltmeter to monitor ground movement near the construction site.
A tiltmeter can detect subtle changes in the slope of a volcano, warning of possible eruptions.
Technicians installed a tiltmeter on the bridge to ensure its stability during repairs.
Scientists installed a tiltmeter on the volcano to monitor ground movement.
The tiltmeter recorded a gradual shift in the building's foundation over several months.
Engineers use a tiltmeter to detect subtle changes in the angle of large structures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclinometer - an instrument for measuring angles of slope or tilt
  • synonym: level - an instrument for determining if a surface is horizontal
  • synonym: seismometer - an instrument that measures and records details of earthquakes
