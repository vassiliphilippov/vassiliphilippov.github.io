arbuscula

adjective
shrub-like (scientific)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈbʌskjʊlə / (AmE), / ɑːˈbʌskjʊlə / (BrE)
used in scientific names to describe a species with a small tree or shrub-like form

Examples

Arbuscula species name
The species was given the epithet arbuscula to highlight its shrubby appearance.
In botanical Latin, arbuscula indicates that the plant is smaller than a typical tree.
Several plants with a bushy form are named with arbuscula in their scientific classification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arboreal - relating to or resembling trees
  • antonym: herbaceous - having the characteristics of a non-woody plant
