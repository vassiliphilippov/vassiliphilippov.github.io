state of nature

noun
wild condition prior to civilization
6.0
Pronunciation: / steɪt əv ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / steɪt əv ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
a wild and primitive condition, existing before or without the influence of civilization

Examples

The state of nature
After the city was abandoned, the land slowly reverted to its original state of nature.
Some philosophers debate whether humans are happier in a state of nature or in society.
The explorers marveled at the untouched state of nature deep within the rainforest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: civilization - the society, culture, and way of life of a particular area
  • synonym: wilderness - a wild, uncultivated region; natural environment not altered by humans
