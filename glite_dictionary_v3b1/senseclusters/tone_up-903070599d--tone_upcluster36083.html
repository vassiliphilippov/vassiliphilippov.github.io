tone up

verb
make muscles firmer and healthier
1.0
Pronunciation: / toʊn ʌp / (AmE), / təʊn ʌp / (BrE)
to make the body or muscles firmer, stronger, and healthier, especially by exercise

Examples

To tone up muscles
She joined a fitness class because she wanted to tone up before the summer.
Swimming is a great way to tone up your whole body.
You can tone up your arms with regular weight training.
