bhakti

noun
Hindu path of devotion
9.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Ààb…ëÀêkti / (AmE), / Ààb åkti / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) loving devotion toward a deity, regarded as a path to salvation and spiritual liberation, open to all people regardless of caste or gender.

Examples

The bhakti movement emphasized personal devotion over ritualistic practices in Hinduism.
