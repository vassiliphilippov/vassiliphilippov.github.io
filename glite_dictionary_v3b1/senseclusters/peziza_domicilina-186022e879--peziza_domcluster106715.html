Peziza domicilina

noun
a species of cup fungus
Pronunciation: / pɛˈzaɪzə ˌdoʊmɪˈsɪlɪnə / (AmE), / pɛˈziːzə ˌdɒmɪˈsɪlɪnə / (BrE)
a species of cup fungus with fragile, ghostly white fruiting bodies that stain yellow when broken, commonly found in strongly alkaline habitats
