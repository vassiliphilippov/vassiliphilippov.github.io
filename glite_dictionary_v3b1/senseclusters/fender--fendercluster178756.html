fender

noun
fireplace guard for coals or ashes
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛn.dɚ / (AmE), / ˈfen.də / (BrE)
a low metal frame or guard placed in front of a fireplace to keep coals or ashes from falling onto the floor

Examples

Fireplace fender
In the old cottage, a battered iron fender surrounded the hearth.
Please polish the brass fender before the guests arrive for dinner.
The ornate fender kept burning coals from rolling onto the carpet.
