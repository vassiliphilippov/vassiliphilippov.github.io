southwest by south

noun
compass point between southwest and south-southwest
Pronunciation: / ˌsaʊθˌwɛst baɪ ˈsaʊθ / (AmE), / ˌsaʊθˌwɛst bʌɪ ˈsaʊθ / (BrE)
The compass point that is one point south of southwest, corresponding to an angle of 213°45′ clockwise from due north.

Examples

Southwest by south compass point
He set his course southwest by south using the compass and map.
The ship was heading southwest by south to avoid the crowded shipping lanes.
The storm approached from southwest by south, bringing heavy rain to the region.
