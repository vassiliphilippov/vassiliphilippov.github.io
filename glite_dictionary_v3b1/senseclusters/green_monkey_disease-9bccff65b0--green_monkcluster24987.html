green monkey disease

noun
serious viral disease from green monkeys; caused by Marburg virus
1.0 8.4
Pronunciation: /griːn ˈmʌŋ.ki dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /griːn ˈmʌŋ.ki də.ˈziːz/ (AmE), /griːn ˈmʌŋ.ki dɪ.ˈziːz/ /; / /griːn ˈmʌŋ.ki də.ˈziːz/ (BrE)
