reasoning by elimination

noun
elimination-based problem-solving
Pronunciation: / ˈriːzənɪŋ baɪ ɪˌlɪməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈriːzənɪŋ baɪ ɪˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of analyzing a problem by considering all possible options and systematically ruling out those that are not acceptable or plausible

Examples

Reasoning by elimination process
Physicians often rely on reasoning by elimination to diagnose rare diseases.
The detective used reasoning by elimination to narrow down the list of suspects.
Through reasoning by elimination, she identified the only solution that fit all the conditions.
