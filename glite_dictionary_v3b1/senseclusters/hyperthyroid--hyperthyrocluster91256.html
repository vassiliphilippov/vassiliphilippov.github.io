hyperthyroid

adjective
overactive thyroid
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈθaɪrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈθaɪrɔɪd / (BrE)
having an overactive thyroid gland that produces excessive amounts of hormones

Examples

Hyperthyroid symptoms
The doctor diagnosed her as hyperthyroid after several blood tests.
People who are hyperthyroid may experience weight loss and increased heart rate.
A hyperthyroid condition can be managed with medication or other treatments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hypothyroid - having an underactive thyroid gland
  • synonym: thyrotoxic - affected by excessive thyroid hormones
