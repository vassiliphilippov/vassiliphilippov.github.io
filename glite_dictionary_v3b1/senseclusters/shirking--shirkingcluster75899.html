shirking

noun
evasion of work or duty
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɝː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃɜː.kɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of avoiding or evading work, responsibility, or duty.

Examples

Shirking responsibilities
His constant shirking of responsibilities annoyed his colleagues.
Management is concerned about the shirking that has led to decreased productivity.
There will be consequences for shirking in this company.
