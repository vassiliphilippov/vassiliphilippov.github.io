distention

noun
state of being stretched or swollen
6.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈsten.ʃən/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈsten.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or state of being stretched, swollen, or enlarged beyond normal size, usually due to internal pressure
