biophysical

adjective
physical and biological
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl / (BrE)
relating to the physical environment and the interactions between living organisms and their surroundings

Examples

Biophysical environment
The biophysical properties of the soil affect which crops can be grown in the region.
Researchers analyzed the biophysical impacts of climate change on local wildlife.
Understanding biophysical processes is essential for effective environmental management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biological - relating to living organisms
  • antonym: chemical - relating to chemicals or chemistry
  • synonym: ecological - relating to the relationships between living things and their environment
