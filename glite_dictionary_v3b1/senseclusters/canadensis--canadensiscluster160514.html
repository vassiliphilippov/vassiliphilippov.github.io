canadensis

noun
species from Canada
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəˈdɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌkænəˈdɛnsɪs / (BrE)
a Latin term used in scientific names to indicate that a species originates from Canada

Examples

Branta canadensis
The scientific name for the Canada goose is Branta canadensis.
Many plants with the species name canadensis are native to Canada.
The tree Tsuga canadensis is commonly known as the eastern hemlock.
