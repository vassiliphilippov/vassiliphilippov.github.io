cattle class

noun
cheapest, most crowded class on an airplane
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬l̩ ˌklæs / (AmE), / ˈkætl̩ ˌklɑːs / (BrE)
the cheapest and most crowded class of seating on an airplane, usually with limited space and few amenities

Examples

Flying in cattle class
After a long-haul flight in cattle class, my legs were aching from the cramped space.
He upgraded to business to avoid the discomfort of cattle class.
I always fly cattle class to save money, even if it means less comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economy class - standard lowest fare class on airplanes, with minimal amenities
