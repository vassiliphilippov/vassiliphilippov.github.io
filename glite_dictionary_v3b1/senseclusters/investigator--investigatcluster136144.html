investigator

noun
police officer who investigates crimes
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvɛstəˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a police officer whose job is to examine crimes and uncover evidence or information

Examples

Police investigator
After years of experience, she became a top investigator in the homicide unit.
The investigator questioned several witnesses about the incident.
The lead investigator presented the evidence to the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detective - a person, especially a police officer, whose job is to investigate crimes
