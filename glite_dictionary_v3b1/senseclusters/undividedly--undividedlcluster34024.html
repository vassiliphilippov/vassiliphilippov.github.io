undividedly

adverb
completely, without division
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪˈvaɪdɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪˈvaɪdɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is complete and not shared, split, or distracted

Examples

Undividedly focused attention
She listened undividedly to the speaker's every word, not missing a single detail.
The team worked undividedly toward their common goal, showing great unity.
He gave his attention undividedly to his studies during the exam period.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entirely - completely, with nothing left out
  • antonym: partially - in part, not completely
  • synonym: wholly - completely or fully
