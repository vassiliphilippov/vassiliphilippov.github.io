disaster relief

noun
aid provided after a disaster
3.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzæstər rɪˈlif / (AmE), / dɪˈzɑːstə rɪˈliːf / (BrE)
organized assistance and support provided to people and communities after a disaster, such as food, shelter, and medical care

Examples

Disaster relief efforts
International organizations secured funds for disaster relief following the devastating hurricane.
The government launched a massive disaster relief operation after the earthquake struck the region.
Volunteers from around the world came together to provide disaster relief to the flood victims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humanitarian aid - assistance given for humanitarian purposes, often during crises
