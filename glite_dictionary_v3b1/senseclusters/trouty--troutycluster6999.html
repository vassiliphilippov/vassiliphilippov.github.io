trouty

adjective
like a trout
Pronunciation: / ˈtraʊti / (AmE), / ˈtraʊti / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a trout, especially in appearance or features

Examples

Trouty fish appearance
The river was full of trouty fish darting between the rocks.
Her painting captured the trouty colors and patterns of the stream's inhabitants.
He described the lake as having a distinctly trouty smell in the early morning.
