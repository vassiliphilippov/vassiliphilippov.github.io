expressed almond oil

noun
oil pressed from almonds
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛst ˈɑːlmənd ɔɪl / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛst ˈɑːlmənd ɔɪl / (BrE)
a pale yellow fatty oil obtained by pressing whole sweet or bitter almonds, used in cooking, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals

Examples

Expressed almond oil for skin
Her skincare routine includes expressed almond oil for its moisturizing properties.
Pharmacies often stock expressed almond oil for use in preparing ointments and creams.
The chef recommends using expressed almond oil for a more delicate flavor in desserts.
