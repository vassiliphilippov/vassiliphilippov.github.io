pepino

noun
South American fruit
7.4
Pronunciation: / pɛˈpinoʊ / (AmE), / pɛˈpiːnəʊ / (BrE)
a sweet, edible fruit native to South America, also known as pepino melon, with smooth skin and pale flesh

Examples

A ripe pepino
We bought a fresh pepino at the market to try for the first time.
The pepino has a mild, sweet flavor that is perfect for fruit salads.
Farmers in Peru often grow pepino because it is popular with locals and tourists.
