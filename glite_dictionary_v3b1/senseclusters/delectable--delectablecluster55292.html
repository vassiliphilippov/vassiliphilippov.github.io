delectable

adjective
delicious; extremely tasty
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈlɛktəbl / (BrE)
(especially of food or drink) extremely pleasing to the taste; delicious.

Examples

Delectable dessert
A plate of delectable pastries was set out for dessert.
She found the chocolate cake absolutely delectable and went back for seconds.
The chef prepared a delectable meal that everyone enjoyed.
