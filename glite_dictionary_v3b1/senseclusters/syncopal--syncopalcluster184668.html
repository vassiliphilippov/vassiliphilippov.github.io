syncopal

adjective
relating to fainting
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋkəpəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋkəpəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by syncope, especially fainting or temporary loss of consciousness

Examples

A syncopal episode
The doctor noted a syncopal episode after the patient stood up quickly.
A syncopal attack can be alarming but is often brief and harmless.
She was admitted to the hospital following several syncopal events in one week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conscious - aware and awake
  • synonym: fainting - temporarily losing consciousness
