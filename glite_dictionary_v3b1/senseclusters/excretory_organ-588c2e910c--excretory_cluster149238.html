excretory organ

noun
organ that removes bodily waste
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskriːtəˌri ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ɪkˈskriːtəri ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an organ in the body that removes waste products from the blood and releases them from the body

Examples

The excretory organ function
Failure of an excretory organ can lead to the buildup of harmful substances in the blood.
In many animals, different types of excretory organs have evolved to suit their environments.
The kidneys are the main excretory organs in the human body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kidney - organ that filters waste from the blood
  • synonym: liver - organ involved in detoxifying blood and breaking down waste
