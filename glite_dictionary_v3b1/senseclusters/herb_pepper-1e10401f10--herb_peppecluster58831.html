herb pepper

noun
herb and pepper seasoning blend
4.3
Pronunciation: / hɜrb ˈpepər / (AmE), / hɜːb ˈpepə / (BrE)
a seasoning blend made of ground pepper and a mixture of herbs such as basil, marjoram, oregano, sage, parsley, and thyme

Examples

Herb pepper seasoning mix
She sprinkled herb pepper over the roast chicken before putting it in the oven.
The marinade recipe calls for a pinch of herb pepper to add extra flavor.
You can make your own herb pepper by mixing dried herbs with ground black pepper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbes de Provence - a French blend of dried herbs used as seasoning
