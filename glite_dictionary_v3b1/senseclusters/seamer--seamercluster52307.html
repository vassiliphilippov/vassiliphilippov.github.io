seamer

noun
person or machine joining seams
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmər / (AmE), / ˈsiːmə / (BrE)
a person or machine that joins pieces of material by sewing or sealing seams

Examples

Industrial seamer
The factory invested in a new seamer to increase production speed.
As a skilled seamer, she could join even the toughest fabrics with precision.
The seamer ensured that every seam was strong and neatly finished.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cutter - a person or machine that cuts fabric or material
  • synonym: seamstress - a woman who sews, especially professionally
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes or alters clothing
