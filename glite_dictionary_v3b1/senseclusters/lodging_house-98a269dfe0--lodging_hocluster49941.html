lodging house

noun
house with rooms for rent
6.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈlɑːdʒ.ɪŋ haʊs/ /; / /ˈlɑːdʒ.ɪŋ haʊz/ (AmE), /ˈlɒdʒ.ɪŋ haʊs/ /; / /ˈlɒdʒ.ɪŋ haʊz/ (BrE)
