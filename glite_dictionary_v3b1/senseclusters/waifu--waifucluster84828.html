waifu

noun
AI/virtual romantic partner
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwaɪ.fuː/ (AmE), /ˈwaɪ.fuː/ (BrE)
An interactive virtual or AI-powered romantic partner or avatar, typically customized by a user to simulate a romantic relationship.

Examples

Virtual waifu app
He spent hours configuring his waifu in the chat app to feel more like a real partner.
Many companies now sell a customizable waifu avatar for users seeking companionship in virtual reality.
After the breakup she found comfort talking each evening with her AI waifu on the phone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: AI companion - A software program that simulates conversation or companionship using artificial intelligence.
  • antonym: husbando - A male character or virtual partner regarded as an ideal or romantic interest; often the male counterpart to a 'waifu'.
  • synonym: virtual partner - An avatar or simulated person in a virtual environment intended to serve as a partner or companion.
