impassivity

noun
absence of emotional response
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪm.pæˈsɪv.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˌɪm.pæˈsɪv.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality of not showing or feeling any emotion; lack of emotional response or reaction.

Examples

Calm impassivity
Despite the shocking news, his face showed only impassivity, as if nothing could disturb him.
Her impassivity during the heated argument made others wonder if she cared at all.
The judge listened to the testimony with complete impassivity, never betraying any personal feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • antonym: emotion - a strong feeling such as love or anger
  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
