unembalmed

adjective
not chemically preserved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈbɑːmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbɑːmd / (BrE)
not preserved with chemicals to prevent decay, especially referring to a dead body.

Examples

Unembalmed body
The family chose to have the body unembalmed for a natural burial.
In some cultures, it is customary to leave the deceased unembalmed before burial.
The law allows for unembalmed remains to be buried within a certain time frame.

  • antonym: embalmed - preserved with chemicals to prevent decay
