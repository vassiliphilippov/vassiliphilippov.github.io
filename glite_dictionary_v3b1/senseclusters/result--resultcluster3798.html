Synonyms

  • outcome
    2.1 118/m
    outcome describes the final picture after an event or process, while result names the general consequence of an action.
  • consequence
    4.1 72/m
    consequence is often used for a direct effect that follows an action, while result is a neutral term for any outcome.
  • effect
    2.3 300/m
    effect is the change caused by something, while result is a broader outcome that may be a change, decision, or product.
  • resultant
    6.0 0.02/m
    resultant refers to the final point reached after a process, while result is a common word for any outcome or consequence.
  • upshot
    6.3 1.30/m
    upshot is the main or often unexpected outcome after events, while result is a neutral name for any consequence of an action.
  • end product
    3.3 1.72/m
    end product is the final item made by a process, while result can mean any outcome, not only manufactured goods.
  • product
    1.0 30/m
    product is a thing made or produced by a process, while result covers outcomes more widely, including events or decisions.
