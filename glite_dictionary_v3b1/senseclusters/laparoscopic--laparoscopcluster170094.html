laparoscopic

adjective
minimally invasive surgery
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌlæpərəˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌlæpərəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to surgery performed through small incisions using a camera and specialized instruments

Examples

A laparoscopic surgery
The patient had a laparoscopic procedure to remove her gallbladder.
Recovery time is often shorter after laparoscopic surgery compared to traditional methods.
Doctors recommended a laparoscopic approach to minimize scarring and pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minimally invasive - involving the least possible damage to body tissues
  • antonym: open surgery - surgery performed through a large incision
