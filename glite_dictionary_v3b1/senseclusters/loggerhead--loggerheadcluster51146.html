loggerhead

noun
stupid person (insult)
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑɡərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈlɒɡəˌhɛd / (BrE)
an insulting term for a person regarded as stupid or lacking intelligence

Examples

Call him a loggerhead
Only a real loggerhead would forget their own birthday.
She called him a loggerhead after he missed the obvious answer.
Those loggerheads have been bickering all day over nothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockhead - a person regarded as very stupid
  • synonym: dunce - a slow-witted or stupid person
