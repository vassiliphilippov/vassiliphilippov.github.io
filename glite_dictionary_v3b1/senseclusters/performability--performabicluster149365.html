performability

noun
system performance ability
8.2
Pronunciation: / pərˌfɔrməˈbɪləti / (AmE), / pəˌfɔːməˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the ability of a system or component to perform its intended function under specified conditions, especially in engineering contexts

Examples

System performability test
The performability of the software was tested under various network conditions.
Engineers are concerned with the performability of critical infrastructure systems.
Improving the performability of the device can lead to greater customer satisfaction.
