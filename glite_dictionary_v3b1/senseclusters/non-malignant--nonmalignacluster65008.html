non-malignant

adjective
not harmful or hostile
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn məˈlɪɡnənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn məˈlɪɡnənt / (BrE)
not harmful, hostile, or intended to cause injury or ill will

Examples

Non-malignant words
His comments were completely non-malignant and meant to encourage discussion, not to upset anyone.
The teacher's feedback was non-malignant, focusing on improvement rather than criticism.
Although the debate was heated, her intentions remained non-malignant throughout the conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: malignant - harmful, hostile, or intending to cause harm
