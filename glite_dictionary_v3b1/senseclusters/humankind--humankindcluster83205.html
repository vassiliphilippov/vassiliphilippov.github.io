Synonyms

  • human race
    1.5 5.61/m
    human race refers to all people living on Earth as a single group, while humankind is a neutral collective name.
  • humanity
    1.0 28/m
    humanity means all people and often also kindness, while humankind only names people as a whole.
  • mankind
    1.0 17/m
    mankind is the older common word for all people and can sound male-focused, while humankind is gender-neutral.
  • human beings
    1.0 23/m
    human beings names people as living creatures, while humankind refers to all people together as a single group.
  • humans
    1.0 0.08/m
    humans describes individual members of our species, while humankind speaks of the whole human group or species.
  • people
    1.0 12/m
    people means a group of persons or citizens in a place, while humankind names all human beings worldwide.
