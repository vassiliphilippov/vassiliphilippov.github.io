invalidism

noun
chronic ill health; invalid state
6.7 8.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.və.ˈlɪ.dɪ.zəm/ /; / /ɪn.ˈvæ.lɪ.dɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ɪn.və.ˈlɪ.dɪ.zəm/ /; / /ɪn.ˈvæ.lɪ.dɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
The state of suffering from chronic ill health or disability, often resulting in long-term inability to work or lead a normal life.

Examples

Chronic invalidism condition
Despite years of invalidism, she remained mentally active and interested in current affairs.
His invalidism was brought on by a series of persistent and untreated illnesses.
The family had to adjust their lives around Grandpa's invalidism after his stroke.

  • synonym: invalidity - the condition of being an invalid; chronic disability
