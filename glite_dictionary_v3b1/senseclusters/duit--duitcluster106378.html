duit

noun
informal money term
Pronunciation: / duːˈiːt / (AmE), / duːˈiːt / (BrE)
an informal term for money, especially used in Southeast Asian English influenced by Malay or Indonesian

Examples

Some spare duit
She kept some spare duit in her wallet while traveling through Malaysia and Indonesia.
If you want to buy snacks at the market, make sure you have enough duit with you.
He asked his friend to lend him some duit for the bus fare.
