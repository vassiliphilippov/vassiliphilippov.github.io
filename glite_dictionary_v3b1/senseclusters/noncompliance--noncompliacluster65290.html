noncompliance

noun
failure to comply
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəmˈplaɪəns / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəmˈplaɪəns / (BrE)
Failure or refusal to follow a rule, order, or request.

Examples

Chronic noncompliance with rules
Chronic noncompliance with medication can lead to serious health risks.
His repeated noncompliance eventually resulted in disciplinary action.
The company was fined for its noncompliance with safety regulations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliance - the act of obeying a rule or request
