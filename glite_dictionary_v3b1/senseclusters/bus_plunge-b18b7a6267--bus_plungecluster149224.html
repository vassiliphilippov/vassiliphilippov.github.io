bus plunge

noun
serious accident where a bus falls from a height
Pronunciation: / bʌs plʌndʒ / (AmE), / bʌs plʌndʒ / (BrE)
a serious accident in which a bus suddenly and dangerously falls from a height, such as off a bridge or cliff

Examples
