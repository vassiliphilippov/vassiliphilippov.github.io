congruently

adverb
in modular equivalence
9.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːəntli / (AmE), / kənˈɡruːəntli / (BrE)
in a way that two numbers are equivalent under a given modulus, having the same remainder when divided by a specific integer

Examples

Congruently related numbers
In modular arithmetic, 14 and 2 are congruently related modulo 12.
The numbers 25 and 5 behave congruently when considered under modulo 10.
You can add numbers congruently to simplify calculations in number theory.
