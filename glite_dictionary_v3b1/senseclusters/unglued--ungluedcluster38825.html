unglued

adjective
disorganized or failed
5.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈgluːd/ /; / /ən.ˈgluːd/ /; / /ʌŋ.ˈgluːd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈgluːd/ /; / /ən.ˈgluːd/ /; / /ʌŋ.ˈgluːd/ (BrE)
no longer organized or functioning properly; having fallen apart or failed, especially of plans, events, or processes

Examples

Plans come unglued
After the manager left, the project quickly came unglued and missed all its deadlines.
The team's strategy fell unglued when their star player was injured.
When the computer system crashed, the entire schedule went unglued.

Related Terms
