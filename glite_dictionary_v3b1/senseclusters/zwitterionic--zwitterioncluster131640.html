zwitterionic

adjective
having dual charges
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌzwɪtərˈaɪɒnɪk / (AmE), / ˌzwɪtəˈraɪɒnɪk / (BrE)
having both positive and negative electrical charges on different parts of the same molecule

Examples

Zwitterionic molecule
At neutral pH, many amino acids exist in a zwitterionic form.
The zwitterionic nature of some molecules affects their solubility in water.
Researchers studied the zwitterionic properties of the compound to understand its behavior in solution.
