welcome

noun
the state of being gladly received
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəm / (BrE)
the act or manner of greeting or receiving someone with pleasure

Examples

A warm welcome
After three days, their welcome began to wear thin.
Please remember not to overstay your welcome as a guest.
You have truly made me feel welcome during my stay here.
The ambassador received a warm welcome when she arrived at the airport.
The new policy was met with an enthusiastic welcome from the staff.
They planned a surprise party to give him a proper welcome home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farewell - an act of saying goodbye
  • synonym: greeting - a polite word or sign of welcome or recognition
  • synonym: hospitality - friendly and generous reception of guests
