spammy

adjective
like unwanted ads
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspæmi / (AmE), / ˈspæmi / (BrE)
containing or resembling unwanted, excessive, or irrelevant advertising or messages, especially online

Examples

Spammy messages online
My inbox was full of spammy messages promoting products I never wanted.
Be careful not to make your website look too spammy or visitors might leave.
He reported the spammy comments on his social media post to the site moderators.
