tootsy

noun
affectionate nickname
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊtsi / (AmE), / ˈtʊtsi / (BrE)
an affectionate or playful term of address for a person, especially someone loved or considered attractive

Examples

Hey, tootsy
He called her tootsy as they walked hand in hand through the park.
Don't worry, tootsy, everything will be fine, she said with a reassuring smile.
Whenever he wanted to cheer her up, he would affectionately say, 'Hey, tootsy!'
