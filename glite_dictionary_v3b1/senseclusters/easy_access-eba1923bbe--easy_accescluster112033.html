easy access

noun
convenient availability or approach
Pronunciation: / ˈizi ˈæksɛs / (AmE), / ˈiːzi ˈæksɛs / (BrE)
the state of being able to approach or obtain something quickly and without difficulty

Examples

Easy access to resources
Public libraries provide easy access to a wide range of resources.
She keeps her gloves near the door for easy access during cold mornings.
The website design ensures easy access to important features for users.
