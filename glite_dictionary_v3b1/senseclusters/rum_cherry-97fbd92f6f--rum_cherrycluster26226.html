rum cherry

noun
cherry soaked or preserved in rum
4.7
Pronunciation: / rʌm ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / rʌm ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
A cherry that has been preserved, soaked, or macerated in rum and sugar, used as a cocktail garnish or flavoring ingredient.

Examples

Cocktail rum cherry
The bartender placed a bright rum cherry on the glass for a sweet finish.
She packed a jar of homemade rum cherry to give to friends at Christmas.
The recipe called for a diced rum cherry stirred into the cake batter for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cocktail cherry - a cherry preserved or candied specifically for use as a garnish in cocktails
  • synonym: maraschino cherry - a type of preserved, sweetened cherry commonly used as a garnish in drinks and desserts
