furbelow

noun
pleated decorative trim
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜr.bɪˌloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɜː.bɪ.ləʊ / (BrE)
A strip of pleated or gathered material used as a decorative trim on clothing or furnishings.

Examples

Silk furbelows trim
Antique lampshades often feature silk furbelows around the edges.
She added a delicate furbelow to the curtain for extra flair.
The dress was adorned with a bright furbelow along the hem.
