Olmec

noun
member of an early Mesoamerican civilization
Pronunciation: / ˈɑlmɛk / (AmE), / ˈɒlmɛk / (BrE)
a member of an ancient Mesoamerican civilization, centered in present-day Veracruz, Mexico, that flourished between 1300 and 400 BC

Examples

The Olmec civilization
Archaeologists have discovered many artifacts created by the Olmec in southern Mexico.
Some theories suggest that the Olmec influenced later Mesoamerican civilizations like the Maya and Aztec.
The colossal heads are a distinctive artistic feature of the Olmec culture.
