personal digital assistant

noun
handheld device for personal information management
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːsənl ˈdɪdʒɪtəl əˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənl ˈdɪdʒɪtəl əˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
a small handheld electronic device used for managing personal information such as contacts, schedules, and notes, and sometimes functioning as a phone or alarm clock

Examples

A personal digital assistant device
Before smartphones became popular, a personal digital assistant was considered essential for managing contacts and tasks.
Many professionals in the early 2000s relied on a personal digital assistant to keep track of their appointments.
She programmed her personal digital assistant to remind her of important meetings and deadlines.
