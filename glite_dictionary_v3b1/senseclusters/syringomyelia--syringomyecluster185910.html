syringomyelia

noun
spinal cord cyst
Pronunciation: / sɪˌrɪŋɡoʊmaɪˈiːliə / (AmE), / sɪˌrɪŋɡəʊmaɪˈiːlɪə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which a fluid-filled cyst forms within the spinal cord, potentially causing neurological symptoms

Examples

syringomyelia spinal cyst
The neurologist diagnosed the patient with syringomyelia after reviewing the MRI scans.
Symptoms of syringomyelia can include muscle weakness, pain, and loss of sensation.
Early treatment of syringomyelia may help prevent permanent nerve damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hydromyelia - abnormal widening of the central canal of the spinal cord
  • synonym: spinal cord injury - damage to the spinal cord causing loss of function
