reprint

noun
separately printed copy of original article
3.9 1.60/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀê.pr…™nt / (AmE), / ÀàriÀê.pr…™nt / (BrE)
a separately printed copy of an article, essay, or other material that was originally published in a larger collection or periodical

Examples

Synonyms

  • separate

    a separate is a printed reissue of an article sold alone, while reprint is a general name for any separately printed copy.
