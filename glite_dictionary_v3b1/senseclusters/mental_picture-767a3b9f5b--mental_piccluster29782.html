mental picture

noun
a vivid image formed in the mind
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.t̬l ˈpɪk.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛn.təl ˈpɪk.tʃə / (BrE)
an image or impression formed in the mind, especially one that is clear or vivid

Examples

A clear mental picture
The detective asked the witness to describe her mental picture of the suspect.
When asked to visualize the painting, she immediately formed a clear mental picture in her mind.
Writers often use descriptive language to help readers build a mental picture of the setting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mental image - an image formed in the mind rather than by the eyes
