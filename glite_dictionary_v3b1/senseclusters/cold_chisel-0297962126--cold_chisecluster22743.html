cold chisel

noun
hardened steel chisel for cutting stone or bricks
Pronunciation: / koʊld ˈtʃɪzəl / (AmE), / kəʊld ˈtʃɪzl / (BrE)
A narrow, hardened steel chisel used for cutting stone, bricks, or metal without applying heat.

Examples

A cold chisel tool
A cold chisel is essential when you need to break through bricks during renovation work.
Always wear safety goggles when using a cold chisel to prevent flying debris.
Before shaping the metal plate, he used a cold chisel to cut it to size.
