topsy-turvy

adjective
in complete disorder or confusion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑpsi ˈtɝːvi /; / ˌtɑpsi ˈtɝvi / (AmE), / ˌtɒpsi ˈtɜːvi / (BrE)
in a state of disorder, confusion, or upside down (used to describe something)
