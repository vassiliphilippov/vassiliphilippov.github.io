snowplow

verb
to slow while skiing
4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsnoʊ.plaʊ/ (AmE), /ˈsnəʊ.plaʊ/ (BrE)
to slow down or stop while skiing by turning the skis into a wedge shape with the tips together and tails apart.

Examples

To snowplow down a slope
She snowplowed down the slope, using a wedge position to slow before reaching the lift.
Beginners often snowplow to control their speed on gentle hills.
He snowplowed quickly to avoid colliding with another skier.
