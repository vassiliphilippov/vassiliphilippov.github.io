soda jerk

noun
person serving at a soda fountain
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊdə dʒɜrk /; / ˈsoʊdə dʒɝk / (AmE), / ˈsəʊdə dʒɜːk / (BrE)
a person, usually in the past, who served drinks and ice cream at a soda fountain in a drugstore or diner
