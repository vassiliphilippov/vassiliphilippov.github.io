bloodline

noun
ancestral line; hereditary lineage
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌlaɪn /; / ˈblʌd.laɪn / (BrE)
the direct line of descent from an ancestor, especially in terms of heredity or genetics (applies to people, animals, or breeds)
