tulipwood

noun
wood of a tulip tree, used in furniture-making
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlɪpˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈtjuːlɪpˌwʊd / (BrE)
the wood from a tulip tree, used in furniture and woodworking, sometimes valued for its decorative appearance
