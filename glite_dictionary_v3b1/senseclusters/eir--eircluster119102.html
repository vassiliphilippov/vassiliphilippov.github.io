eir

proper noun
Norse healing goddess
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪr / (AmE), / eɪə / (BrE)
a Norse goddess associated with healing and medical skill in Old Norse mythology

Examples

Norse goddess Eir
In Norse mythology, Eir is revered as a powerful healer among the gods.
Legends describe Eir as aiding those wounded in battle with her medical skills.
Many ancient texts mention Eir as a symbol of healing and compassion.
