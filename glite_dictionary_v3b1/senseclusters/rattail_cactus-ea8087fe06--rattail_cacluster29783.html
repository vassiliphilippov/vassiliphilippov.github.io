rattail cactus

noun
a tropical cactus with trailing stems and red flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrætˌteɪl ˈkæktəs / (AmE), / ˈrat.eɪl ˈkæktəs / (BrE)
a tropical American cactus with slender, trailing stems and large crimson flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant

Examples

The rattail cactus flower
The rattail cactus produces stunning crimson flowers that last for several days in early summer.
The slender stems of the rattail cactus can trail beautifully over the edge of a hanging basket.
You can grow a rattail cactus indoors if it gets enough sunlight and warmth.
