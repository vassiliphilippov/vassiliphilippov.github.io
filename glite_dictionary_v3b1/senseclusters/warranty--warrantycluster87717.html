warranty

noun
written guarantee for product or service
2.7 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔːr.ən.ti/ /; / /ˈwɑːr.ən.ti/ (AmE), /ˈwɒ.rən.ti/ (BrE)
A written guarantee promising that a product or service will meet certain standards or that repairs or replacements will be made if necessary within a specified period.

Examples

A warranty card
Always check the details of a warranty before purchasing any expensive electronic device.
If you lose your receipt, the warranty on the laptop will no longer be valid.
The car comes with a five-year warranty covering all major parts and repairs.
