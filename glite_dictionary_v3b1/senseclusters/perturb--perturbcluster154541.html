perturb

verb
to make anxious or unsettled
9.2 5.6
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈtɜːrb/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtɜːb/ (BrE)

Examples

The unexpected announcement seemed to perturb even the most confident investors at the meeting.
