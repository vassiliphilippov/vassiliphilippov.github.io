perturb

verb
to make anxious or unsettled
7.0 0.50/m
Pronunciation: / p…ôrÀàt…úrb / (AmE), / p…ôÀàt…úÀêb / (BrE)
to disturb or unsettle someone, making them anxious or uneasy

Examples

To perturb a person
His constant questioning began to perturb the teacher during the discussion.
She tried not to let the harsh criticism perturb her focus on the project.
The unexpected announcement seemed to perturb even the most confident investors at the meeting.
