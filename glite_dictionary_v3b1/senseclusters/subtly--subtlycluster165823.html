subtly

adverb

In a manner that is so delicate or precise as to be difficult to analyze or describe; not obvious or blatant.

Examples

To subtly change
She subtly changed the topic to avoid answering the question directly.
The artist subtly blended the colors to create a smooth transition between shades.
Their criticism was subtly expressed, so as not to offend anyone.
