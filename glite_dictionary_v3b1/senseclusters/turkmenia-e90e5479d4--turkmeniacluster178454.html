Turkmenia

proper noun
former name of Turkmenistan (Soviet era)
Pronunciation: / tɜrkˈmiːniə / (AmE), / tɜːkˈmiːnɪə / (BrE)
the former name of Turkmenistan, especially when referring to the period when it was a Soviet republic (1925–1991) in Central Asia, located east of the Caspian Sea.

Examples

Soviet Turkmenia
During the Soviet era, Turkmenia was one of the key cotton-producing republics.
His family emigrated from Turkmenia in the late 1980s before it became independent.
The landscape of Turkmenia features vast deserts and ancient Silk Road cities.
