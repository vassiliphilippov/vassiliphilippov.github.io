ply

verb
supply or provide repeatedly or generously
5.9
Pronunciation: / plaɪ / (AmE), / plaɪ / (BrE)
to provide someone with what is desired or needed, especially repeatedly or generously; to supply (such as food, drink, or support)

Examples

To ply guests with food
During the festival, vendors plied visitors with fresh fruit and snacks.
She kindly plied the hikers with warm soup after their long trek.
The bartender continued to ply the guests with drinks throughout the evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provide - to make available for use; supply
  • synonym: supply - to make (something needed or wanted) available
