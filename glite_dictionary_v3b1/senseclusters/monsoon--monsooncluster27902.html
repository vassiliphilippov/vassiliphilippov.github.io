monsoon

noun
seasonal reversing wind, often with rain
1.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /mɑːn.ˈsuːn/ /; / /mən.ˈsuːn/ (AmE), /mɒn.ˈsuːn/ /; / /mə.ˈsuːn/ (BrE)
a seasonal wind in southern Asia that brings heavy rain, or the rainy season when these rains fall
