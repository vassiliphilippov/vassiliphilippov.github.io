prattle

verb
talk at length about unimportant things
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈprætəl / (AmE), / ˈprætəl / (BrE)
to talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way, often about unimportant things

Examples

To prattle on endlessly
Children sometimes prattle on about their favorite TV shows for hours.
He would prattle endlessly, barely noticing if anyone was listening.
She prattled excitedly about her day at the amusement park.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babble - to talk quickly in a way that is difficult to understand or about unimportant things
  • synonym: chatter - to talk rapidly or incessantly about trivial matters
  • degree higher: gossip - to talk about the personal lives of other people, often in a trivial way
