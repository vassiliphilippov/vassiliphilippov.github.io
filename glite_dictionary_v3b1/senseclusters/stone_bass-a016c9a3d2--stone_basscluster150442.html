stone bass

noun
a large brown Atlantic and Mediterranean fish
Pronunciation: / stoʊn bæs / (AmE), / stəʊn bæs / (BrE)
A large brown fish (Polyprion americanus) found in the Atlantic Ocean and Mediterranean Sea, often around rocks and wrecks.

Examples

The stone bass fish
Fishermen prize the stone bass for its firm, white flesh and mild flavor.
The stone bass is often found near shipwrecks on the sea floor.
We spotted a huge stone bass lurking among the rocks while diving.
