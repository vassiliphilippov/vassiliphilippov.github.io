temper

noun
state of mind or mood
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpər / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpə / (BrE)
a characteristic or temporary state of mind or mood, especially one that affects how a person behaves

Examples

A calm temper
His temper seemed unusually short this morning.
She was in a cheerful temper after receiving the good news.
Try not to let your temper affect your decisions at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposition - a person's usual mood or temperament
  • synonym: humor - a temporary mood or frame of mind
  • synonym: mood - a temporary state of mind or feeling
