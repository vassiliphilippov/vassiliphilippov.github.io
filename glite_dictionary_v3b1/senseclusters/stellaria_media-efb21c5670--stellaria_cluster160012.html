Stellaria media

noun
common chickweed; small annual weed
9.3
Pronunciation: / stəˈlɛəriə ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / stɛˈlaːrɪə ˈmiːdɪə / (BrE)
A small, low-growing annual weed with small white flowers, commonly found worldwide and often eaten by chickens; also known as common chickweed.

Examples

Stellaria media plant
Stellaria media is often considered a troublesome weed in vegetable plots.
Many birds, especially chickens, feed on Stellaria media growing in the backyard.
The garden was overrun with Stellaria media after the warm spring rains.
