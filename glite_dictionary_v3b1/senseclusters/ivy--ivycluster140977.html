ivy

noun
evergreen climbing plant with lobed leaves
3.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.viː/ /; / /ˈaɪ.vɪ/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.vɪ/ /; / /ˈaɪ.viː/ (BrE)
A climbing or trailing plant with evergreen, lobed leaves and small black berry-like fruits, especially those of the genus Hedera native to Europe and Asia.

Examples

Ivy plant leaves
English gardens often have ivy growing along fences and trellises.
She trimmed the ivy to prevent it from blocking the windows.
The old house was covered in thick green ivy that climbed up its walls.
