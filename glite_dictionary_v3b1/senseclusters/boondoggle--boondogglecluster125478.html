boondoggle

noun
pointless or wasteful activity
6.6 8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbuːn.dɑː.gəl/ /; / /ˈbuːn.dɔː.gəl/ (AmE), /ˈbuːn.dɒg.əl/ /; / /ˈbuːn.dɔːg.əl/ (BrE)
work or activity that is wasteful or pointless but gives the appearance of having value, often done to look busy

Examples
