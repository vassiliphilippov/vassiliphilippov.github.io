use

noun
legal right to enjoy property
8.7 30/m
Pronunciation: / jus / (AmE), / juÀês / (BrE)
(law) The exercise of the legal right to enjoy the benefits of owning property or using an asset, even if it is owned by someone else.

Examples

Legal use of land
He transferred the property but retained the use of the house.
The contract granted them the use of the farmland for ten years.
Under the agreement, she has the use of the car although it's not in her name.

Synonyms

  • enjoyment
    5.0 0.68/m
    enjoyment focuses on receiving benefits or pleasure from property, while use stresses actively employing property for practical purposes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: usufruct - the right to enjoy the use and advantages of another's property
