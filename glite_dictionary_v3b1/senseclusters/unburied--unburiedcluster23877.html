unburied

adjective
not buried; above ground
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɛrid / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɛrid / (BrE)
(of a dead body or object) not buried, left above ground.

Examples

Unburied dead bodies
After the disaster, many victims lay unburied for days.
The archaeologists discovered several unburied remains at the ancient site.
The poet mourned the fate of soldiers left unburied on the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buried - placed in the ground; interred
