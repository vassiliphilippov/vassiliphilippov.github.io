tweet

verb
make a high-pitched bird sound
2.7 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / twiÀêt / (AmE), / twiÀêt / (BrE)
(of a small bird) to make a short, high-pitched, chirping sound.

Examples

Birds tweet at dawn
Every morning, the robins tweet outside my window as the sun rises.
In the quiet forest, a sparrow suddenly tweeted from a nearby branch.
We watched as the canary tweeted cheerfully in its cage.

Synonyms

  • chirp
    1.0 2.18/m
    chirp is a short high-pitched sound found in birds, insects, or devices, while tweet is the small bird's quick chirp.
  • peep
    1.5 0.17/m
    peep is a very short thin sound made by small birds or animals, often timid, while tweet is a clearer bird chirp.
  • cheep
    3.9 0.05/m
    cheep is the weak high sound of young birds calling for food or comfort, while tweet is a general short bird sound.
  • twitter
    4.4 0.11/m
    twitter is a string of light high notes a bird makes, often longer and more musical, while tweet is a single quick chirp.
