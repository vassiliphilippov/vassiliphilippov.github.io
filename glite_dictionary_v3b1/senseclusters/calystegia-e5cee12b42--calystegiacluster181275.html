Calystegia

noun
a genus of bindweed plants
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪstəˌdʒiə / (AmE), / kəˈlɪstɪdʒɪə / (BrE)
a genus of climbing or scrambling herbs commonly known as bindweed, often with trumpet-shaped flowers.
