untouchable

noun
member of the lowest caste in India
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʌtʃəbl / (BrE)
a member of the lowest social and ritual caste in the traditional Hindu system of India, often considered outside and below the four main castes

Examples

An untouchable in India
Although technically illegal, prejudice against untouchables persists in some rural areas.
Before the reforms, untouchable communities faced severe discrimination in Indian society.
The film highlights the struggles of an untouchable striving for dignity and equality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Dalit - official term for members of the lowest caste in India, previously called untouchables
