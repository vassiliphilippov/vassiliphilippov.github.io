outdraw

verb
to surpass in drawing
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈdrɔ / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈdrɔː / (BrE)

Examples

The new movie outdrew all previous releases at the box office this year.
Despite her efforts, she could not outdraw her main rival in the competition.
