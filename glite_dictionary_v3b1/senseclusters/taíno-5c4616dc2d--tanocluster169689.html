Taíno

proper noun
Caribbean indigenous language
Pronunciation: / taɪˈiːnoʊ / (AmE), / taɪˈiːnəʊ / (BrE)
the Arawakan language formerly spoken by the Taíno people of the Caribbean islands

Examples
