pan-broil

verb
cook in a pan without extra fat
5.5 5.6
Pronunciation: /pæn brɔɪl/ (AmE), /pæn brɔɪl/ (BrE)
To cook food in a pan over direct heat without added fat, allowing the fat that melts off to be removed.

Examples

To pan-broil meat
After seasoning the meat, begin pan-broiling it over medium heat for best results.
He pan-broiled the chicken breasts with just a pinch of salt and pepper.
She likes to pan-broil her steaks to keep them juicy but not greasy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broil - to cook food using direct heat, often in an oven or on a grill
  • synonym: fry - to cook food in hot fat or oil, typically in a shallow pan
