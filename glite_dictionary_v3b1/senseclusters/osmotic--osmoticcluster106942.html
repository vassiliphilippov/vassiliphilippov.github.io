osmotic

adjective
relating to osmosis
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɑzˈmɑtɪk / (AmE), / ɒzˈmɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or caused by osmosis, especially the movement of liquid through a membrane

Examples

Osmotic pressure
Researchers investigated the osmotic effects of various solutes on the semipermeable membrane.
Saline solution is often used to balance osmotic conditions in medical treatments.
The plant cells swelled due to the osmotic pressure from the surrounding water.
