Michaelmas daisy

noun
autumn-blooming perennial plant with daisy-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkəlməs ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈmɪkəlməs ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
A North American perennial plant in the aster family, bearing small purple, pink, or white flowers that bloom in autumn, often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Examples

Purple Michaelmas daisy
