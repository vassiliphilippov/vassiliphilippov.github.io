mooch off

verb
get things from others without paying or repaying
5.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /muːtʃ ɔːf/ /; / /muːtʃ ɑːf/ (AmE), /muːtʃ ɒf/ /; / /muːtʃ ɔːf/ (BrE)
to obtain something, especially food or money, from someone without paying or intending to pay, by relying on their generosity
