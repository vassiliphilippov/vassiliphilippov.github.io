Synonyms

  • selfless
    3.2 3.01/m
    selfless shows concern for others without thinking of yourself, while self-giving highlights generous personal giving to meet others' needs.
  • unselfish
    2.0 0.91/m
    unselfish puts other people first in small daily choices, while self-giving means a larger, active giving of yourself.
  • self-sacrificing
    5.5 0.30/m
    self-sacrificing gives up personal wishes or comfort for others, while self-giving focuses on freely sharing your time and care.
  • altruistic
    6.7 2.16/m
    altruistic describes a calm, principled care for others, while self-giving stresses active, personal generosity and direct help.
  • giving
    1.7 0.00/m
    giving means being generous with goods or time, while self-giving emphasizes offering your whole self to meet others' needs.
  • self-denying
    7.0 0.03/m
    self-denying rejects personal pleasure or comfort for a cause, while self-giving usually means generous sharing rather than strict self-denial.
