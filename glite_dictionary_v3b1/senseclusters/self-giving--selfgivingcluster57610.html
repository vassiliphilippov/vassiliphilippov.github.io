self-giving

adjective
selflessly generous; putting others first
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈɡɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌselfˈɡɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
willing to put the needs of others before your own; selfless and generous in giving of yourself.

Examples

Self-giving love
Acts of self-giving are essential for building trust and compassion in a group.
He showed a self-giving attitude, always ready to help others in need.
Her self-giving nature made her beloved by everyone in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: selfish - concerned chiefly with one's own advantage
  • synonym: selfless - putting others' needs before your own
