indorsement

noun
the act of endorsing or approving
4.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɔrsmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɔːsmənt / (BrE)
a signature or written statement of approval, especially on a document such as a check

Examples

Indorsement on a check
A check is invalid without the proper indorsement on the back.
Her political indorsement helped the candidate gain trust among undecided voters.
The company offered him a lucrative contract for the indorsement of their products.
Bank policies require your indorsement before they can deposit the check into your account.
The check was invalid until the payee's indorsement was added on the back.
Without an authorized indorsement, the document will not be legally recognized.
