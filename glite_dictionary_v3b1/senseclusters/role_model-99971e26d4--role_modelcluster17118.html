role model

noun
person worthy of imitation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊl ˌmɑːdəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊl ˌmɒdəl / (BrE)
a person whose behavior, example, or success is or can be emulated by others, especially by younger people

Examples

A role model for students
As a teacher, she strives to be a role model for her students.
He became a role model in his community through his volunteer work.
Many young athletes see Serena Williams as an inspiring role model.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
  • synonym: idol - a person who is greatly admired, loved, or revered
