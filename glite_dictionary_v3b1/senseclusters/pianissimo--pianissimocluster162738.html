pianissimo

adjective, adverb
very softly (music direction)
Pronunciation: / piːəˈnɪsɪˌmoʊ /; / piˌænɪˈsɪmoʊ / (AmE), / piːəˈnɪsɪməʊ /; / piˌanɪˈsɪməʊ / (BrE)
(in music) very softly; to be played or sung very softly
