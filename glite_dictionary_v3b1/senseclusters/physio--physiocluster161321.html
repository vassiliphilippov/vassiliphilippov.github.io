physio

noun
informal physiotherapy
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzioʊ / (AmE), / ˈfɪziəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for physiotherapy, referring to treatment or exercises to restore movement and reduce pain.

Examples

To do physio exercises
After his knee surgery, he attended physio twice a week to speed up his recovery.
My doctor recommended physio to help with my back pain after the accident.
She found physio sessions helpful for regaining strength in her shoulder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physiotherapy - treatment to restore movement and function after injury or illness
  • synonym: rehabilitation - the process of helping someone recover physical abilities
