shooting stick

noun
portable walking stick with fold-out seat
Pronunciation: / ˈʃuː.tɪŋ stɪk / (AmE), / ˈʃuː.tɪŋ stɪk / (BrE)
a device resembling a spiked walking stick with a handle that opens into a small folding seat, used for sitting outdoors, especially at sporting or social events

Examples

A shooting stick seat
He unfolded his shooting stick and settled down to watch the event.
She brought her shooting stick to the outdoor concert for a comfortable place to sit.
The shooting stick is popular among spectators at horse races and cricket matches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seat stick - a portable stick that can be converted into a seat
