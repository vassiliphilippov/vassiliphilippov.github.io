otoscopic

adjective
ear examination
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊtəˈskɑpɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊtəˈskɒpɪk / (BrE)
relating to the examination of the ear using an otoscope

Examples

Otosopic ear examination
The physician conducted an otoscopic inspection to look for signs of infection in the ear canal.
An otoscopic view revealed a buildup of wax that needed to be removed.
During the checkup, the nurse performed a quick otoscopic assessment of both ears.
