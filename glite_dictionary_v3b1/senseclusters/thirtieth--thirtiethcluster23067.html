thirtieth

adjective
ordinal number: 30th in a sequence
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜrtiɪθ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːtiɪθ / (BrE)
coming next after the twenty-ninth in position; being number thirty in a sequence

Examples

Thirtieth birthday
His birthday falls on the thirtieth of September.
She finished in thirtieth place out of a hundred runners.
This is the thirtieth anniversary of the school's founding.
