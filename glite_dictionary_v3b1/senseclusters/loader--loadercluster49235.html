loader

noun
construction vehicle
3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.də/ (BrE)
a large machine used for lifting and moving heavy materials, especially in construction or shipping

Examples

Construction loader
The construction crew used a loader to move gravel across the site.
A loader can quickly clear debris after a storm.
He operated the loader to fill the dump truck with sand.
The loader moved piles of dirt at the construction site.
A skilled loader can quickly transfer cargo from the truck to the warehouse.
The farm bought a new loader to help with moving hay bales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulldozer - a powerful machine with a broad blade for pushing earth or debris
  • synonym: excavator - a large machine for digging and moving earth
  • synonym: forklift - a vehicle with a pronged device for lifting and carrying heavy loads
  • antonym: unloader - a person or machine that removes loads
