higgledy-piggledy

adverb
in utter disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi /; / ˈhɪɡəldi ˈpɪɡəldi / (AmE), / ˌhɪɡldiˈpɪɡldi /; / ˈhɪɡldi ˈpɪɡldi / (BrE)
in a disorderly or chaotic way; not arranged neatly
