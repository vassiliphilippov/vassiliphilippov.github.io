asparagus fern

noun
ornamental plant with fern-like foliage
Pronunciation: / əˈspɛɹəɡəs fɝn / (AmE), / əˈspɑːɹəɡəs fɜːn / (BrE)
a plant with feathery, fern-like foliage, native to South Africa, often grown as an ornamental houseplant; not a true fern.

Examples

Delicate asparagus fern fronds
Many florists use the delicate fronds of asparagus fern in bouquets.
She placed an asparagus fern on the windowsill for some greenery.
The asparagus fern thrives indoors with moderate light and regular watering.
