Santa

proper noun
mythical Christmas gift-bringer
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsæn.tə / (AmE), / ˈsæn.tə / (BrE)
an imaginary, jolly old man who is said to bring presents to children on Christmas Eve; also known as Santa Claus

Examples

Jolly Santa Claus
As a child, I always believed that Santa came down the chimney to deliver gifts.
Every year, children write letters to Santa asking for their favorite toys.
We left cookies and milk out for Santa on Christmas Eve.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Santa Claus - another name and the full form of Santa, the mythical Christmas figure
