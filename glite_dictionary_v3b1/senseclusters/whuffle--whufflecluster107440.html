whuffle

noun
soft snuffling sound
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌfəl / (AmE), / ˈwʌfəl / (BrE)
a soft snuffling or puffing sound, especially one made by an animal or person breathing quietly

Examples

A horse's whuffle
The horse let out a gentle whuffle as it nuzzled its owner.
I heard a soft whuffle coming from the puppy curled up in its bed.
A faint whuffle in the dark told us the animal was nearby.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: snort - a sudden, forceful sound made by exhaling through the nose
  • synonym: snuffle - a soft sound made by breathing through the nose
