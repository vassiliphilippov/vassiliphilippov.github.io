broaden

verb
become wider or more extensive
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdən /; / ˈbrɔdə(n) /; / ˈbrɔd(ə)n / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd(ə)n /; / ˈbrɔːdn̩ /; / ˈbrɔːdən / (BrE)
to make or become wider, more extensive, or more inclusive
