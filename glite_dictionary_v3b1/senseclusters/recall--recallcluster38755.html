recall

noun
the act of remembering
1.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔl / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːl / (BrE)
the process of remembering or retrieving information from memory, especially by conscious effort

Examples

Strong recall of facts
Her recall of names and dates is truly impressive.
Regular review helps with rapid recall of important facts.
The witness's recall of the events proved crucial in the trial.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetfulness - the state of being unable to remember
  • synonym: memory - the faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information
