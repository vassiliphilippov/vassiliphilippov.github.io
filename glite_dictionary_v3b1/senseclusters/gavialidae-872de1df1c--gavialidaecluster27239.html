Gavialidae

noun
family of long-snouted crocodilians
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈvaɪələˌdi /; / ɡəˌvaɪəˈlɪdiː / (AmE), / ɡəˈvaɪəˌlɪdiː /; / ɡəˌvaɪəˈlɪdiː / (BrE)
a family of large, long-snouted crocodilian reptiles, including the gavials (gharials) and false gharials, native to parts of Asia
