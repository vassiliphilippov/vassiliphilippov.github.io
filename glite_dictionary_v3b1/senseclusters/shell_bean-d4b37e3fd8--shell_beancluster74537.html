shell bean

noun
bean removed from pod before cooking
2.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /ʃel biːn/ (AmE), /ʃel biːn/ (BrE)
a bean that is removed from its pod before cooking, usually while still unripe

Examples

Fresh shell beans
After removing the shell beans from their pods, rinse them thoroughly before boiling.
Farmers' markets in late summer often feature locally grown shell beans.
Many recipes call for shell beans to be cooked gently with fresh herbs and olive oil.
