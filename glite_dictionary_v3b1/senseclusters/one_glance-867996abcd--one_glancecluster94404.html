one glance

noun
a single quick look
6.2
Pronunciation: / wʌn ɡlæns / (AmE), / wʌn ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
a single quick look or observation, often giving enough information at once

Examples

At one glance

With just one glance, she knew he was trouble.
You could tell their entire story from one glance at the photograph.
