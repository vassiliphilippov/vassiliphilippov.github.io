territorially

adverb
in relation to territory
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛr.ɪˈtɔr.i.ə.li / (AmE), / ˌtɛr.ɪˈtɔː.ri.ə.li / (BrE)
In a way that relates to territory or territorial matters.

Examples

To be territorially aggressive
That species is territorially aggressive, especially during nesting season.
The province is territorially large, but much of it is sparsely populated.
The two neighboring countries remain territorially disputed over the border region.
