strong-willed

adjective
having a determined character
5.3
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ ˈwɪld / (AmE), / strɒŋ ˈwɪld / (BrE)
having a determined and resolute character; showing firmness of will

Examples

A strong-willed person
Despite facing criticism, he remained strong-willed and pursued his goals.
Her strong-willed nature helped her overcome many challenges in her career.
Raising a strong-willed child can be both rewarding and challenging for parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determined - having made a firm decision; resolute
  • synonym: headstrong - self-willed and obstinate
  • degree lower: stubborn - unwilling to change one's attitude or position
  • antonym: weak-willed - lacking willpower or determination
