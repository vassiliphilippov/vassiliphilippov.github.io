morgue

noun
place for keeping dead bodies before burial
Pronunciation: / mɔrɡ / (AmE), / mɔːɡ / (BrE)
A building or room where dead bodies are kept, especially before burial or cremation, often for identification or autopsy.

Examples

The morgue room
After the tragic accident, the police took the victims' bodies to the morgue for identification.
He visited the morgue to claim his grandfather's remains before the funeral.
The hospital morgue was cold and eerily quiet as the pathologist prepared for the autopsy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mortuary - a room or building for keeping dead bodies before burial or cremation
