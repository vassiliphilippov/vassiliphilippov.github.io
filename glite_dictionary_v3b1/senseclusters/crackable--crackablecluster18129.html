crackable

adjective
able to be broken
Pronunciation: / ˈkrækəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrækəbəl / (BrE)
able to be broken, opened, or solved, especially by force or effort

Examples

A crackable safe
The old safe was easily crackable with the right tools.
They designed a password that was not crackable by common hacking methods.
Some eggs have shells that are barely crackable without extra force.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breakable - able to be broken
  • antonym: secure - protected against being broken or accessed
  • antonym: unbreakable - impossible to break
