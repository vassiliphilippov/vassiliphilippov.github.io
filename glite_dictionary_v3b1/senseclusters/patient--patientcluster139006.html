patient

adjective
tolerant and accepting in adversity
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪʃənt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃənt / (BrE)
able to accept or tolerate delays, suffering, or difficult situations without becoming annoyed or anxious.

Examples

Patient teacher
He listened with a patient smile as the children told their stories.
It is important to be patient with yourself while learning a new skill.
She remained patient even when the meeting ran late and others were growing irritated.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impatient - easily annoyed by delays or difficulties; unable to wait calmly
