puffball

noun
a round fungus releasing spores in a cloud
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / ˈpʌfbɔːl /; / ˈpʌfˌbɔːl / (BrE)
a round fungus that releases a cloud of spores when mature, including both classic puffballs (Lycoperdaceae) and similar hard-skinned fungi (Scleroderma)

Examples

A puffball fungus
As a child, he loved to step on puffballs and watch the spores puff into the air.
We found a large puffball in the field during our mushroom hunt.
When the puffball matured, it released a cloud of brown spores.
Some puffballs are mistaken for edible mushrooms, but not all are safe to eat.
The chef compared the texture of the puffball to that of a truffle.
We discovered a rare puffball while digging in the forest soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fungus - any member of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter
  • synonym: truffle - an edible subterranean fungus prized in cooking
