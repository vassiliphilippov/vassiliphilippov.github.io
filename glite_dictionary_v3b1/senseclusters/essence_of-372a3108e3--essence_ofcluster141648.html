essence of

noun
most important quality of something
Pronunciation: / ˈɛsəns ʌv / (AmE), / ˈɛsəns ɒv / (BrE)
the most important or fundamental quality or nature of something; what makes something what it is

This book explores the essence of leadership in a modern context.
