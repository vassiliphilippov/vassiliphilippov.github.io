thuggish behavior

noun
aggressive or violent conduct
Pronunciation: / ˈθʌɡɪʃ bəˈheɪvjɚ / (AmE), / ˈθʌɡɪʃ bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
aggressive or violent conduct, especially resembling that of a thug; intimidating or brutal actions.

Examples

Thuggish behavior after match
Many people were shocked by the thuggish behavior displayed by the fans after the match.
Teachers do not tolerate any kind of thuggish behavior in the school corridors.
The company had to fire an employee for his repeated thuggish behavior towards coworkers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullying - the act of intimidating or mistreating others
  • antonym: gentle behavior - kind, calm, or nonviolent conduct
  • synonym: thuggery - violent or criminal behavior typical of a thug
