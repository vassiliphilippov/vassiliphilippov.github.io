lightstick

noun
fan wand for synchronized concert lighting
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪt.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪt.stɪk/ (BrE)
(in popular music fandoms, esp. K-pop) a battery-powered wand sold as official merchandise and used by concertgoers to display colours, flash, and synchronise lighting effects with a performance; also a symbol of fan identity.

Examples

K-pop lightstick
At the concert, every fan raised their lightstick as the arena changed to match the song's color.
The group's official lightstick connected to the stage system so it flashed in perfect unison.
During the encore, lightsticks pulsed in time with the bass, creating a sea of coordinated color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glow stick - a chemical light-emitting stick used at events; a related but non-electronic concert light source
