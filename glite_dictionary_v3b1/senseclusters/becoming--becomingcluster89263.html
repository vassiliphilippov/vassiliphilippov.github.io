becoming

adjective
flattering; making someone look attractive
Pronunciation: / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / bɪˈkʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a piece of clothing, color, etc.) enhancing someone's appearance; flattering; making someone look attractive or suitable.

Examples

Becoming dress
His becoming manner made him popular at every social gathering.
She wore a becoming shade of blue that suited her complexion perfectly.
That dress is very becoming on you and really brings out the color of your eyes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unbecoming - (of appearance or behavior) not flattering or appropriate
