bacteriologically

adverb
relating to bacteria
7.1
Pronunciation: / bækˌtɪriəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / bækˌtɪəriəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to bacteria or the study of bacteria

Examples

Tested bacteriologically safe
The laboratory confirmed that the milk was bacteriologically safe for consumption.
The river was tested bacteriologically to ensure it met health standards.
Food must be handled bacteriologically to prevent contamination and illness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chemically - in a way that relates to chemistry or chemicals
  • synonym: microbiologically - in a way that relates to microorganisms or their study
