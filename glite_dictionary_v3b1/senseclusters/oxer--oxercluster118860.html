oxer

noun
wide show jumping obstacle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksər / (AmE), / ˈɒksə / (BrE)
a wide jump in show jumping made of two or more rails set parallel to each other

Examples

A oxer jump
The rider guided her horse over the challenging oxer in the final round.
Clearing the oxer required both speed and precision from the horse and rider.
During practice, the trainer adjusted the height of the oxer to build confidence.

Related Terms
