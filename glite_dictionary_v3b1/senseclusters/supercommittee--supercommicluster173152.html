supercommittee

noun
powerful decision-making group
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərkəˌmɪti / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəkəˌmɪti / (BrE)
a committee with special authority, often formed by a government to make important decisions on major issues

Examples

Government supercommittee
Congress created a supercommittee to find solutions for the growing national debt.
The supercommittee failed to reach an agreement before the deadline.
Many citizens watched closely as the supercommittee debated new budget proposals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: committee - a group of people chosen to make decisions or plans for a larger group
  • synonym: task force - a group formed to work on a specific problem or task
