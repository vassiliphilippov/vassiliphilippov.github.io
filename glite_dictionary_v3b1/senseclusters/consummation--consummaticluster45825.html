consummation

noun
completion of marriage by sexual intercourse
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsəˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsjʊˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of making a marriage or relationship complete by having sexual intercourse, especially on the wedding night

Examples

Consummation of marriage
In some cultures, the consummation of marriage is an important legal and social milestone.
The annulment was granted on the grounds of non-consummation of the union.
They celebrated the consummation of their marriage with a traditional wedding night ceremony.
