biostratigraphy

noun
fossil-based rock dating
9.8
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.strə.ˈtɪg.rə.fi/ /; / /baɪ.ə.strə.ˈtɪg.rə.fi/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.strə.ˈtɪg.rə.fi/ /; / /baɪ.ə.strə.ˈtɪg.rə.fi/ (BrE)
the study of rock layers using fossils to determine their relative ages and to match layers across different places
