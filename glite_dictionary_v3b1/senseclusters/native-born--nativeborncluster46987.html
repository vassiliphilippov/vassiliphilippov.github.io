native-born

adjective
born in a particular country or place
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪ.t̬ɪv bɔrn / (AmE), / ˈneɪ.tɪv bɔːn / (BrE)
born in a particular country or place, rather than having moved there from elsewhere.

Examples

Native-born resident
As a native-born resident, she knows the city extremely well.
He is a native-born citizen of Australia.
The governor promised to support the rights of native-born Americans.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foreign-born - born in a different country from the one of residence
