miscommunication

noun
failure to communicate
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪskəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪskəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a failure to communicate information clearly, resulting in misunderstanding or confusion

Examples

A miscommunication problem
A simple miscommunication caused the team to arrive at different times for the event.
They missed the deadline due to a miscommunication between departments.
To avoid miscommunication, always confirm important details in writing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • synonym: misunderstanding - a situation where something is not understood correctly
