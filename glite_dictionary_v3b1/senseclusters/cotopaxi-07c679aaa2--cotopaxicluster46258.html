Cotopaxi

proper noun
active volcano in Ecuador
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊtoʊˈpæksi / (AmE), / ˌkəʊtəʊˈpæksi / (BrE)
a large active volcano in the Andes of north central Ecuador, known as one of the world's highest and most active volcanoes.

Examples

Cotopaxi volcano
Hikers often travel to Ecuador to climb Cotopaxi for its spectacular views.
Mount Cotopaxi erupted in 2015, sending ash high into the sky.
Snow can cover the summit of Cotopaxi even during the dry season.
