draw

noun
golf shot curving left (right-handed)
3.3
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
(in golf) a shot in which the ball curves gently to the left (for a right-handed golfer).

Examples

A draw shot
He perfected his draw to avoid the trees on the left side of the fairway.
Her draw helped her land the ball close to the pin on the dogleg hole.
The draw is a controlled shot many professionals use to add distance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fade - a golf shot that curves gently to the right (for a right-handed golfer)
  • degree higher: hook - a golf shot that curves sharply to the left (for a right-handed golfer)
