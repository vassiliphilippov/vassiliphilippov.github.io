explicative

adjective
giving explanations
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsplɪkətɪv / (AmE), / ɪkˈsplɪkətɪv / (BrE)
giving a clear explanation or making something easier to understand

Examples

Explicative notes
The professor's explicative approach made the complex topic much easier for students.
Her explicative notes clarified the confusing parts of the textbook.
A good teacher uses explicative language to help everyone understand difficult ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explanatory - serving to explain something
  • antonym: obscure - not clear or difficult to understand
