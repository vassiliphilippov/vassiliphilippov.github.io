propeller plane

noun
aircraft with propeller-driven engine
Pronunciation: / prəˈpɛlɚ pleɪn / (AmE), / prəˈpɛlə pleɪn / (BrE)
an aircraft powered by one or more propellers turned by an engine

Examples

A propeller plane takes off
As a child, he was fascinated by the sound of a propeller plane taking off.
The old propeller plane slowly made its way across the sky.
We took a short flight on a small propeller plane between the islands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: jet plane - an aircraft powered by jet engines
