paddleboard

verb
to ride a paddleboard
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
to travel on water by standing or kneeling on a paddleboard and propelling oneself with a paddle

Examples

To paddleboard across a lake
We decided to paddleboard across the lake early in the morning before the wind picked up.
She learned how to paddleboard during her vacation in Hawaii last year.
Many people enjoy paddleboarding as a relaxing way to explore the coastline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: row - to move a boat using oars
  • synonym: surf - to ride on ocean waves using a board
