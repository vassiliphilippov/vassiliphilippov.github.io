nonseparable

adjective
not countably dense
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛpərəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛpərəbəl / (BrE)
not having a countable dense subset; not separable in the topological sense in mathematics

Examples

Nonseparable space
The mathematician provided an example of a nonseparable space in her lecture.
Certain function spaces are nonseparable and require advanced techniques to study.
The property of being nonseparable can complicate the analysis of topological spaces.
