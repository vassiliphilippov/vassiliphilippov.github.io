upscale

verb
to increase quality
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌskeɪl / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌskeɪl / (BrE)
to increase the size, amount, or quality of something, especially by improving digital images or data

Examples

To upscale a photo
The new app can upscale old photos to a much higher resolution.
Many video streaming services use technology to upscale standard definition content to HD.
Graphic designers often need to upscale images for use in large posters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enhance - to improve the quality or value of something
  • synonym: enlarge - to make something bigger in size
  • antonym: reduce - to make something smaller or less in amount
