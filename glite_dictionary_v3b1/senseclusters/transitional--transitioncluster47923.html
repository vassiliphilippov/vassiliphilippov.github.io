transitional

adjective
relating to a process of change
3.1 3.8
Pronunciation: / trænˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / trænˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a process or period of change from one state or condition to another.

Examples

Transitional phase
Moving to a new city can be a transitional phase in someone's life.
The transitional government was formed to lead the country until elections could be held.
The company experienced a transitional period as it shifted from print to digital media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interim - temporary or provisional, occurring between events
  • antonym: permanent - lasting or intended to last indefinitely
