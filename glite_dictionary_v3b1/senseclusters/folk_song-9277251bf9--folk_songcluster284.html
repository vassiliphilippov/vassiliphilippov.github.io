folk song

noun
traditional song of a community or culture
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊk ˌsɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈfəʊk ˌsɒŋ / (BrE)
A song that is traditionally passed down and sung by the people of a region, often reflecting their culture and traditions.

Examples

Traditional folk song
Many folk songs tell stories about the history of their people.
She grew up listening to the folk songs her grandmother used to sing.
The band performed a traditional folk song from Scotland at the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballad - a narrative song or poem often with a traditional melody
