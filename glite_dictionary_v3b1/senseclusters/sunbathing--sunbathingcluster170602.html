sunbathing

noun
lying in the sun for tanning or enjoyment
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌbeɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌbeɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
The activity of sitting or lying in the sun, especially to tan the skin or for enjoyment.

Examples

Sunbathing on the beach
Long periods of sunbathing can increase your risk of skin damage.
She applied sunscreen before sunbathing at the beach.
We spent the afternoon sunbathing by the pool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tanning - deliberately making the skin darker using sunlight or ultraviolet light
