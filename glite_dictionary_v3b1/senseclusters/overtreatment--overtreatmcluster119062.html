overtreatment

noun
excessive treatment
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.triːt.mənt/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈtriːt.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.triːt.mənt/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈtriːt.mənt/ (BrE)
the act of giving too much treatment, especially medical care, often causing harm or being unnecessary

Examples

The overtreatment of crops
The overtreatment of crops with pesticides can damage the soil and harm the environment.
Frequent coloring led to overtreatment of her hair, making it dry and brittle.
Some products lose their effectiveness after overtreatment with harsh chemicals.
Doctors are concerned about the risks of overtreatment in hospitals.
Some experts warn that overtreatment can lead to unnecessary side effects and higher costs.
The patient suffered complications as a result of overtreatment for a minor illness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overuse - using something too much
  • antonym: undertreatment - not giving enough treatment or care
