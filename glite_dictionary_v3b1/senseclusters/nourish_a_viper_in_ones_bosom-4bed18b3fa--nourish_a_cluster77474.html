nourish a viper in one's bosom

verb
support someone harmful or treacherous
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrɪʃ ə ˈvaɪpər ɪn wʌnz ˈboʊsəm / (AmE), / ˈnʌrɪʃ ə ˈvaɪpə ɪn wʌnz ˈbəʊsəm / (BrE)
to support or trust someone who turns out to be dangerous, treacherous, or ungrateful
