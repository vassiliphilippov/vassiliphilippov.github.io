slam dunk

noun
forceful basketball shot
7.4 3.4
Pronunciation: / slæm dʌŋk / (AmE), / slæm dʌŋk / (BrE)
A forceful shot in basketball in which a player jumps and scores by putting the ball directly through the basket with one or both hands.

Examples

A powerful slam dunk
After executing a perfect slam dunk, she celebrated with her teammates.
LeBron James thrilled the crowd with a spectacular slam dunk in the final seconds of the game.
The highlight reel featured his powerful slam dunk over two defenders.
