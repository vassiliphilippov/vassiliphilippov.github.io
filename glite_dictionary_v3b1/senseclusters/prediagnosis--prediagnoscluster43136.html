prediagnosis

noun
initial medical assessment
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪˈɑːɡnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪˈæɡnəsɪs / (BrE)
the stage or process before a formal diagnosis is made, including early identification or assessment of a disease or condition
