boatyard

noun
place for boat building and repairs
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊtˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊtˌjɑːd / (BrE)
a place where boats are built, repaired, or stored

Examples

The boatyard repairs boats
After the storm, many boats were taken to the boatyard for maintenance.
He bought an abandoned boatyard and turned it into a thriving business.
The family spent the afternoon at the boatyard, watching workers repair old vessels.
