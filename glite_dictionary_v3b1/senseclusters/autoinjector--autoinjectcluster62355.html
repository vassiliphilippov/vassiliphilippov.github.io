autoinjector

noun
device for automatic self-injection
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtoʊɪnˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəʊɪnˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a device designed to inject a measured dose of liquid medication automatically, usually for self-administration

Examples

Modern autoinjectors
Modern autoinjectors are designed to be easy for patients to use by themselves.
She always carries an autoinjector in case she has a severe allergic reaction.
The doctor demonstrated how to use the autoinjector in an emergency situation.
