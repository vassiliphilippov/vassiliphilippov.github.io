handweaving

noun
manual textile craft
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwiːvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwiːvɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or process of making fabric or textiles by hand rather than by machine

Examples

Handweaving as a hobby
She took a class in handweaving to learn how to make her own scarves.
Traditional handweaving techniques are still practiced in many rural communities.
The museum displayed beautiful examples of handweaving from different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knitting - the craft of making fabric by interlocking loops of yarn with needles
  • synonym: weaving - the process of making fabric by interlacing threads
