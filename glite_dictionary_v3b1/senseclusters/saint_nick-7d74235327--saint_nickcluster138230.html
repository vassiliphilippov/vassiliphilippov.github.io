Saint Nick

proper noun
another name for Santa Claus
Pronunciation: / seɪnt nɪk / (AmE), / seɪnt nɪk / (BrE)
An informal name for Santa Claus, the legendary figure said to bring gifts to children at Christmas.

Examples

Saint Nick brings gifts
Every Christmas Eve, children hang stockings and hope that Saint Nick will leave them presents.
In some cultures, Saint Nick visits children with small gifts on December 6th.
My grandmother still calls Santa Claus Saint Nick when telling us holiday stories.
