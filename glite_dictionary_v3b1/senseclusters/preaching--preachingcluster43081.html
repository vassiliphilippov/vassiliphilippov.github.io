preaching

noun
religious address or sermon
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpritʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpriːtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
an address or speech on religious matters, especially one delivered during a church service

Examples

Sunday preaching
His preaching always offers comfort and practical advice to the community.
She listened attentively to the preaching during the Sunday morning service.
The pastor's preaching moved many members of the congregation to tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sermon - a talk on a religious or moral subject, especially given during a church service
