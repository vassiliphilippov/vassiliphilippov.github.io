uncared-for

adjective
not given care or attention; neglected
4.2 0.09/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàk…õrd f…îr / (AmE), /  ånÀàk…õ…ôd f…îÀê / (BrE)
not receiving the necessary care, attention, or maintenance; neglected.

Examples

An uncared-for house
After years of being uncared-for, the old house began to fall apart.
Some pets become ill if they are left uncared-for for long periods.
The uncared-for garden was overgrown with weeds and brambles.

Synonyms

    3.7 0.00/m

  • untended
    4.9 0.46/m
    untended often describes plants or places left without upkeep, while uncared-for stresses lack of needed care or maintenance.
  • unattended
    3.6 1.21/m
    unattended usually means not watched or supervised at a moment, while uncared-for means not receiving necessary ongoing care.
