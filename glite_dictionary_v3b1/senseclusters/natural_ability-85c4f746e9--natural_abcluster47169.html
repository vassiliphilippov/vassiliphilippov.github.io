natural ability

noun
inborn talent or skill
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃɚəl əˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl əˈbɪləti / (BrE)
an inherent talent or skill that someone is born with, rather than learned or acquired

Examples

Natural ability in sports
Athletes with natural ability often excel even before formal training begins.
His natural ability to solve complex puzzles impressed his teachers.
She has a natural ability for music, playing the piano effortlessly from a young age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aptitude - natural tendency or ability to do something
  • synonym: talent - a special natural ability for something
