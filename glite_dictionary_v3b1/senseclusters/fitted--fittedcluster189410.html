fitted

adjective
made to fit the shape or size closely
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪt̬ɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of clothing or an object) made or shaped to conform closely to specific dimensions or the shape of something; the right size and shape for a particular purpose.

Examples

A fitted shirt
All the parts were fitted to ensure there were no gaps or loose edges.
She bought a set of fitted sheets for her new bed.
The jacket was specially designed to be fitted at the waist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose - not fitting tightly or closely
  • synonym: tailored - made or shaped to fit closely, especially of clothing
