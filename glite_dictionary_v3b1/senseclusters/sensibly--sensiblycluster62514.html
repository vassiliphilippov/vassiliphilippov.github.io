sensibly

adverb
in a reasonable or intelligent manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnsəbli / (AmE), / ˈsɛnsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows good sense, reason, or practicality.

Examples

To speak sensibly
During the meeting, he spoke sensibly about the challenges the project faced.
It's important to dress sensibly in cold weather to stay warm.
She managed her money very sensibly after graduating from college.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foolishly - in an unwise or imprudent manner
  • synonym: reasonably - in a sensible or moderate manner
