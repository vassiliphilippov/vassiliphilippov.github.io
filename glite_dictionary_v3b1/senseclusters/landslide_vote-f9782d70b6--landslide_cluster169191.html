landslide vote

noun
vote won by a large majority
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌslaɪd voʊt / (AmE), / ˈlan(d)ˌslaɪd vəʊt / (BrE)
a vote in which one side wins by an overwhelming majority

Examples

A landslide vote
She was re-elected by a landslide vote that surprised even her opponents.
The board's decision was confirmed after a landslide vote among its members.
The measure passed with a landslide vote in parliament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landslide - an overwhelming victory in an election
