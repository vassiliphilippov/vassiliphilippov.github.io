lethal

adjective
figuratively extremely effective or impressive
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.θəl/ (AmE), /ˈliː.θəl/ (BrE)
Informal. (figurative) Extremely effective, powerful, or impressive; causing decisive defeat or damage (not literally deadly).

Examples

Lethal attack
The striker proved lethal in the final minutes, scoring twice to win the match.
Her new routine at the skate park was lethal, with flips that amazed every onlooker.
When he presents, his arguments are lethal, leaving rivals little chance to respond effectively.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effective - successful in producing a desired or intended result
  • antonym: harmless - not able to cause damage or harm; lacking power to injure or defeat
