agamogenesis

noun
asexual reproduction; reproduction without gamete fusion
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡəmoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaɡəməˈdʒɛnɪsɪs / (BrE)
reproduction that occurs without the fusion of gametes, such as asexual reproduction in certain plants and animals

Examples

Agamogenesis in plants
Many simple organisms, such as certain protozoa, reproduce through agamogenesis.
Scientists observe agamogenesis in environments where sexual reproduction may not be advantageous.
The study highlighted agamogenesis as an efficient way for some plants to propagate without seeds.
