inbox

noun
email/message folder
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌbɒks / (BrE)
a folder or location (especially digital) where incoming emails or messages are received and stored

Examples

Email inbox
I checked my inbox for new messages before starting work.
Her inbox was full of unread emails after her vacation.
Please send your questions to my inbox and I will reply soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailbox - a general term for a place where email messages are received and stored
  • antonym: outbox - a folder where outgoing emails or messages are stored before being sent
