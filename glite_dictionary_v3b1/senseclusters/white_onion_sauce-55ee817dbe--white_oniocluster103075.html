white onion sauce

noun
creamy sauce with onions and cream
1.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /waɪt ˈʌn.jən sɔːs/ /; / /waɪt ˈʌn.jən sɑːs/ (AmE), /waɪt ˈʌn.jən sɔːs/ (BrE)
A creamy sauce made by cooking chopped onions, often in butter, then mixing with a white velouté sauce and typically finished with cream.

Examples

A white onion sauce
He prefers white onion sauce over traditional gravy when accompanying his mashed potatoes.
The roast chicken was served with a delicious white onion sauce on the side.
To make white onion sauce, start by slowly sautéing onions in butter until soft.
