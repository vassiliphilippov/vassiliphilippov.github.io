huntsman's horns

noun
North American pitcher plant with trumpet-shaped yellow pitchers
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntsmənz hɔrnz / (AmE), / ˈhʌntsmənz hɔːnz / (BrE)
A pitcher plant native to the southeastern United States, known for its erect yellow trumpet-shaped pitchers with wide mouths and erect lids.
