rise

verb
come into existence; originate
6.0
Pronunciation: / raɪz / (AmE), / raɪz / (BrE)
to come into existence or begin to develop; to originate or take on form

Examples

A movement rises
A new literary movement began to rise in the early 20th century.
A sense of excitement rose among the audience as the performance started.
Tensions can rise quickly when misunderstandings occur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emerge - become apparent, important, or prominent; come into being
  • synonym: originate - to begin, arise, or come into existence
