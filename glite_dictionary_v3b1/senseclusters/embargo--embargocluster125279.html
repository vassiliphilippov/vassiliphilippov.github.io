embargo

verb
to impose a trade ban

to officially prohibit or restrict trade or other commercial activity with a particular country or organization

Examples

To embargo trade
In response to the crisis, the government decided to embargo medical supplies.
Several nations threatened to embargo goods unless the policy changed.
The U.S. announced it will embargo all oil imports from the country.
