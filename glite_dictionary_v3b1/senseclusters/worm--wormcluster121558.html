worm

verb
to treat for worms
6.7
Pronunciation: /wɜːrm/ (AmE), /wɜːm/ (BrE)
to give medicine to an animal or person to remove internal parasitic worms.

Examples

To worm a dog
The veterinarian recommended worming the kittens every three months to keep them healthy.
Farmers are careful about worming their livestock to prevent disease.
Before traveling abroad, she asked her doctor about worming treatments for her dog.
