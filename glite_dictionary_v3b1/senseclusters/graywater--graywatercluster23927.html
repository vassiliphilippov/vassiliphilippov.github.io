graywater

noun
reusable household wastewater
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
wastewater from sinks, showers, and washing machines that can be reused for purposes like irrigation

Examples

Graywater recycling system
Many eco-friendly homes collect graywater to use for watering plants and flushing toilets.
The city encourages residents to recycle graywater to conserve fresh water resources.
After installing a new system, they began using graywater from their laundry for the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blackwater - wastewater from toilets containing human waste
  • synonym: wastewater - used water from homes or industry that is no longer clean
