balloon head

noun
feeling of head pressure or fullness
5.7
Pronunciation: / bəˈlun hɛd / (AmE), / bəˈluːn hɛd / (BrE)
a sensation of the head feeling swollen, inflated, or overly full, often accompanied by pressure or discomfort

Examples

A balloon head feeling
After the long flight, I woke up with a balloon head and couldn't focus on anything.
He joked he had a balloon head after sitting through three hours of lectures.
The allergies gave her such a balloon head she felt she might float away.
