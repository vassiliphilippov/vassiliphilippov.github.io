abdominal breathing

noun
breathing using abdominal muscles
4.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈdɑːmɪnəl ˈbriðɪŋ / (AmE), / əbˈdɒmɪnəl ˈbriːðɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of breathing in which most of the respiratory effort is performed by the abdominal muscles rather than the chest.

Examples

Abdominal breathing method
Babies naturally use abdominal breathing in their early development.
Singers and actors often practice abdominal breathing to control their voices better.
Some meditation techniques emphasize abdominal breathing for relaxation and improved focus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diaphragmatic breathing - breathing technique using the diaphragm, closely related method
