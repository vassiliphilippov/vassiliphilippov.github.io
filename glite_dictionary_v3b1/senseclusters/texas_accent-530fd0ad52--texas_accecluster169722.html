Texas accent

noun
regional English accent of Texas
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛksəs ˈæk.sɛnt / (AmE), / ˈtɛksəs ˈæk.sənt / (BrE)
the distinctive way of speaking English that is characteristic of people from Texas
