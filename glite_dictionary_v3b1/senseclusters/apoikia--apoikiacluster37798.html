apoikia

noun
Greek colonial settlement
Pronunciation: / ˌæpoʊˈiːkiə / (AmE), / ˌapɔɪˈkiːə / (BrE)
a colony or community established by ancient Greeks in a foreign land, often independent from its founding city

Examples

Each apoikia maintained its own government, separate from the mother city.

Each apoikia maintained cultural ties with its original city-state in Greece.
