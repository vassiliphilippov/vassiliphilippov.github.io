tentmate

noun
person sharing tent
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛntˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɛntˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person who shares a tent with another person, especially while camping

Examples

My tentmate friend
I quickly became friends with my tentmate during the summer camp.
Each tentmate was responsible for keeping their shared space clean and organized.
My tentmate and I stayed up late telling stories by flashlight.
