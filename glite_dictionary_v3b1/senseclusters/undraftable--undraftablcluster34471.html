undraftable

adjective
not eligible for draft
7.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdræftəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrɑːftəbəl / (BrE)
not eligible or suitable to be selected in a draft, especially in sports contexts

Examples

Undraftable player
Because of his injury history, the player was considered undraftable by most teams.
Her academic record made her undraftable according to the league's strict requirements.
After the rule change, several athletes suddenly became undraftable for the upcoming season.

Related Terms

  • antonym: draftable - able or suitable to be selected in a draft
  • antonym: eligible - qualified or entitled to be chosen
