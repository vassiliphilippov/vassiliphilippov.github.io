spicy

adjective
having a strong, hot flavor (food)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspaɪsi / (BrE)
(of food) having a strong, hot flavor that produces a burning sensation in the mouth.

Examples

A spicy curry
If you find the soup too spicy, you can add some yogurt to cool it down.
She loves to cook spicy dishes using fresh chili peppers and exotic spices.
The curry was so spicy that my mouth started to burn after just one bite.
