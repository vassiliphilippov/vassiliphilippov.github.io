Synonyms

  • wording
    3.4 4.84/m
    wording is the exact words used to say something, while phrasing refers to the broader choice and order of those words.
  • choice of words
    3.8 1.20/m
    choice of words names the particular words someone picks for meaning and tone, while phrasing includes their arrangement too.
  • diction
    6.1 0.72/m
    diction is the style and clarity of a person's word use and sound, while phrasing focuses on word order and expression.
  • phraseology
    8.2 0.54/m
    phraseology shows the typical phrases and patterns a writer uses, while phrasing is the general choice and arrangement of words.
  • formulation
    5.5 0.81/m
    formulation is a formal or planned way of expressing an idea, while phrasing covers both choice and natural ordering of words.
