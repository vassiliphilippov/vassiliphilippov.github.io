phrasing

noun
the way words are chosen and arranged
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
the choice and arrangement of words in speech or writing to express something; the way something is expressed.

Examples

Clear phrasing
Good phrasing can make even a complex idea easy to understand.
He was careful with his phrasing to avoid offending anyone.
The phrasing in her letter made her intentions very clear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: formulation - the way in which something is formulated or expressed
  • synonym: wording - the words used to express something
