common person

noun
a person without a title or special rank
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈpɝsən / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who does not hold a noble title or special rank; an ordinary member of society

Examples

A common person
As a common person, he had little influence in the royal court.
Laws should protect both the common person and the wealthy elite.
The novel tells the story of a common person rising to great prominence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aristocrat - a member of the highest class in certain societies
  • antonym: noble - a person of high social rank or title
  • synonym: ordinary person - a person with no special status or title; an average member of society
