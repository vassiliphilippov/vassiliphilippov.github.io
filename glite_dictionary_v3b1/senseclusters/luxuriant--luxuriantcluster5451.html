luxuriant

adjective
rich and detailed; elaborate
1.7
Pronunciation: / lʌgˈʒɝiənt /; / lʌgˈʒʊriənt / (AmE), / lʌgˈʒʊəriənt /; / lʌgˈzjʊəriənt / (BrE)
rich, elaborate, and luxurious in appearance or detail; giving a strong impression of luxury or beauty

Examples

Luxuriant fabrics
Her wedding dress featured a luxuriant lace pattern that caught everyone's eye.
The artist's luxuriant illustrations added depth and elegance to the book.
The palace was filled with luxuriant tapestries and gilded ornaments.
She prefers luxuriant fabrics, like silk or velvet, that please both the eye and touch.
The guests were amazed at the luxuriant banquet set before them, filled with delicacies from around the world.
The royal palace was adorned with luxuriant decorations, making every room opulent and inviting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elaborate - carefully planned or detailed; complicated in design
  • synonym: luxurious - rich and comfortable; characterized by luxury
  • synonym: ornate - highly decorated with complex patterns
