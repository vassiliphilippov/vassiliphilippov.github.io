irregular

noun
guerrilla fighter; non-regular soldier
3.9 6.8
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.lər/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈre.gjʊ.lə/ /; / /ɪ.ˈre.gjə.lə/ (BrE)
a member of an armed force not belonging to a regular army, who engages in fighting using non-traditional tactics such as sabotage and harassment

Examples

An irregular fighter
As an irregular, he operated outside the conventional military chain of command.
During the conflict, irregulars used hit-and-run tactics against the invading army.
The resistance was composed mainly of irregulars who attacked supply lines at night.
