drain the cup

verb
drink all of a liquid in a cup
Pronunciation: / dreɪn ðə kʌp / (AmE), / dreɪn ðə kʌp / (BrE)
to drink all the liquid in a cup; to drink to the last drop

Examples

To drain the cup quickly
At the end of the toast, everyone was expected to drain the cup.
He hesitated for a moment before deciding to drain the cup and set it down.
She raised her glass and drained the cup in one swift motion.
