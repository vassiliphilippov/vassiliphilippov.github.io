end of an era

noun
the close of a significant period
Pronunciation: / ɛnd əv ən ˈɪrə / (AmE), / ɛnd əv ən ˈɪərə / (BrE)
the conclusion of a significant or influential period in history or someone’s life

Examples

When the historic newspaper finally shut down, many mourned the end of an era in local journalism.
