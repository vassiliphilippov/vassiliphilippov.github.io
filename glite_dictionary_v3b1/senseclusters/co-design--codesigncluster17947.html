co-design

noun
collaborative design process
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˌkəʊ dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
the process of designing something collaboratively with others, especially involving users or stakeholders in decision-making

Examples

Co-design process
The new park was created through co-design with local residents and city planners.
Effective co-design ensures that products meet the real needs of their users.
Many organizations now use co-design to develop more inclusive and successful services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others to achieve a goal
  • synonym: participatory design - a design approach involving all stakeholders in the design process
