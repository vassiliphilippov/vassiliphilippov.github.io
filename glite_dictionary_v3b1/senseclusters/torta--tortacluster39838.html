torta

noun
Mexican sandwich
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtə / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtə / (BrE)
a Mexican sandwich made with a crusty roll, such as bolillo or telera, filled with savory ingredients

Examples

Mexican torta sandwich

At the market, you can find a torta with almost any filling you like.
He enjoyed a spicy torta after his soccer game with friends.
