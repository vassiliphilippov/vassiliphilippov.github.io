Polyporaceae

noun
family of woody shelf fungi
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˌpɔːˈreɪsi.iː /; / pɑːliˌpɔːrˈeɪsi.iː / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪpɔːˈreɪsiː /; / pɒlɪˌpɔːrəˈsiːiː / (BrE)
