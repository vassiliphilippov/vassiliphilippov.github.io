allegorization

noun
making into allegory
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˌlɛɡəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌlɛɡəraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of interpreting or presenting something as an allegory

Examples

Her allegorization of the story helped readers see deeper philosophical themes.
