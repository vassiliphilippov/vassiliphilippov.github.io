klezmer

noun
Jewish folk music
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛzmər / (AmE), / ˈklɛzmə / (BrE)
a lively style of traditional Jewish folk music originating from Eastern Europe

Examples

Lively klezmer music
The musicians filled the hall with joyful klezmer during the festival.
She learned to play klezmer melodies on the clarinet from her grandfather.
Many people danced to the energetic rhythms of klezmer at the celebration.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classical music - a formal style of music with written traditions
  • synonym: folk music - traditional music passed down through generations
