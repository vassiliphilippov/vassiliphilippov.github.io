undisrupted

adjective
not interrupted
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪsˈrʌptɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈrʌptɪd / (BrE)
not interrupted, disturbed, or broken; continuing smoothly without any disruption

Examples

An undisrupted flow
The power supply remained undisrupted during the entire blackout.
Thanks to careful planning, the event proceeded undisrupted despite the rain.
Their communication was undisrupted by the technical issues that affected others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuous - happening without stopping or being interrupted
  • antonym: disrupted - interrupted or disturbed
  • synonym: uninterrupted - not stopped or broken; continuous
