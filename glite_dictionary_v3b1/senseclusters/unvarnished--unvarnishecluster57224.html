unvarnished

adjective
not coated with varnish or stain
6.0 0.05/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàv…ërn…™ Ét / (AmE), /  ånÀàv…ëÀên…™ Ét / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially wood) not having a coating of stain or varnish; not covered with a protective or decorative finish.

Examples

Unvarnished wood
Only unvarnished wood is allowed in this environmentally friendly house.
The unvarnished bench felt rough to the touch compared to one with a polished finish.
The carpenter left the table unvarnished to preserve its natural look.
