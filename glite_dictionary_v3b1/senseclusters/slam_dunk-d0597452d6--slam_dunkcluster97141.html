slam dunk

noun
something certain to succeed
8.4
Pronunciation: / slæm dʌŋk / (AmE), / slæm dʌŋk / (BrE)
an action or achievement that is certain to succeed or is impressively successful; an easy or guaranteed success

Examples

A sure slam dunk
Everyone thought her promotion was a slam dunk after the project's success.
That proposal passing the committee is a slam dunk; no one opposes it.
Winning the championship was a slam dunk for the dominant team this year.
Everyone thought the case was a slam dunk until new evidence surfaced.
The merger was expected to be a slam dunk for both companies.
Winning the championship was no slam dunk, but the team made it look easy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certainty - something certain to happen
  • antonym: long shot - something unlikely to succeed
  • synonym: sure thing - something that is certain to happen or succeed
