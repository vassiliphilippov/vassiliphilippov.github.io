electrocute

verb
kill with electricity, especially as execution
6.0 5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈlek.trə.kjuːt/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trə.kjuːt/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈlek.trə.kjuːt/ /; / /ə.ˈlek.trə.kjuːt/ (BrE)
to injure or kill someone by passing electricity through their body

Examples
