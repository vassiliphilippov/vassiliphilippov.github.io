honor

noun
digital reputation points (games/platforms)
4.8 1.64/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8\u0251\u02d0n\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8\u0252n\u0259 / (BrE)
(in online games and communities) a system of points, levels, or measurable reputation used to record a user's conduct or status and to grant privileges or rewards.

Examples

Honor points earned
Players earn honor points in the game to unlock new ranks and exclusive gear.
Her honor level rose after she reported cheaters and earned community rewards.
To join the guild, you need minimum honor accumulated from fair play and helpful actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: points - numerical scores used in games or systems to measure progress or status.
  • synonym: reputation - the beliefs or opinions generally held about someone; similar to reputation as social standing.
