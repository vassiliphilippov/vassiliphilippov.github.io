portray

verb
play or represent a character
Pronunciation: / pɔːrˈtreɪ / (AmE), / pɔːˈtreɪ / (BrE)
to represent or play a particular role or character in a performance or artistic work

Examples

To portray a character
Actors must deeply understand the person they portray to deliver an authentic performance.
In last night's play, he portrayed a famous historical figure.
She was chosen to portray the lead character in the new drama series.
