innovative idea

noun
new and creative concept
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəˌveɪtɪv aɪˈdiə / (AmE), / ˈɪnəvətɪv aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
a new and creative concept or suggestion, especially one that introduces original thinking or methods

Examples

An innovative idea
His innovative idea completely transformed the way the company approached product development.
Investors are always seeking projects that are driven by an innovative idea rather than routine solutions.
The professor encouraged students to share any innovative ideas they had during the seminar.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional idea - an idea that follows traditional or established forms
  • synonym: original idea - an idea that is new and not derived from something else
