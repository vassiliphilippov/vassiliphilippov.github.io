proficiently

adverb
in a skilled or competent way
3.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɪʃəntli / (AmE), / prəˈfɪʃəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows skill, competence, or expertise.

Examples

To proficiently perform tasks
She handled the negotiations proficiently, impressing everyone at the meeting.
Students who practice regularly are able to perform proficiently in their exams.
The technician proficiently repaired the computer despite its complex issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competently - in a competent manner
  • synonym: expertly - in an expert manner
  • antonym: ineptly - in an inept or unskilled manner
