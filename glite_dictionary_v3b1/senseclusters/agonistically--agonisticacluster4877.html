agonistically

adverb
in a combative way
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌæɡəˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that involves conflict, competition, or struggle

Examples

Play agonistically hard
The two teams played agonistically, each determined to win the championship.
During the debate, the candidates responded agonistically to each other's arguments.
Some animals behave agonistically when competing for territory or food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitively - in a way that involves competition
  • antonym: peacefully - in a calm or non-confrontational way
