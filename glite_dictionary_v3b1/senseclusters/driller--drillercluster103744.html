driller

noun
practice instructor
5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪl.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪl.ə/ (BrE)
a person who leads or instructs others by giving repeated practice exercises, especially in sports or training

Examples

Team driller

During practice, the driller introduced new drills to challenge the athletes.
