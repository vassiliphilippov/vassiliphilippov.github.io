bruh

interjection
informal exclamation
Pronunciation: / brʌ / (AmE), / brʌ / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to show disbelief, surprise, or frustration, especially in casual conversation

Examples

Bruh, really?
When he saw the price of the new phone, he just said, 'bruh'.
After missing the bus for the third time this week, she muttered, 'bruh'.
You forgot your keys again? Bruh, seriously?

Related Terms

  • synonym: bro - informal term for 'brother' or friend, often used as an exclamation
  • synonym: wow - an exclamation of surprise or admiration
