semanticist

noun
expert in semantics
Pronunciation: / səˈmæn.tɪ.sɪst / (AmE), / sɪˈmæn.tɪ.sɪst / (BrE)
a person who specializes in the study of the meaning of words and language

Examples

A semanticist studies meaning
As a semanticist, she often analyzes how language shapes our perception.
Several semanticists gathered at the conference to discuss language change.
The semanticist explained the subtle differences between the two words.
