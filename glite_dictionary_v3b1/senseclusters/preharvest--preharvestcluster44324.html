preharvest

noun
time before harvest
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈhɑrvəst / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈhɑːvɪst / (BrE)
the period or stage in crop production immediately before crops are harvested

Examples

The preharvest period
Farmers monitor the weather closely during the preharvest to protect their crops from unexpected storms.
Pest control is especially important in the preharvest stage to ensure a healthy yield.
The preharvest period often involves final irrigation and checking crop maturity.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: harvest - the process or period of gathering crops
  • antonym: postharvest - the period after crops have been harvested
