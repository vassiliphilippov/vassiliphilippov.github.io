Tofieldia pusilla

noun
a European mountain plant with white flowers
Pronunciation: / toʊˈfɪldiə pjuːˈsɪlə / (AmE), / təʊˈfɪldiə pjuːˈsɪlə / (BrE)
A small perennial plant of mountainous regions of Europe, known as false asphodel, with spikes of white flowers.
