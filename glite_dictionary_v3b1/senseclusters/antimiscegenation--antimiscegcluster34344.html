antimiscegenation

noun
opposition to interracial marriage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪmɪˌsɛdʒəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntɪmɪˌsɛdʒɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the belief, policy, or practice of opposing marriage or sexual relations between people of different races, especially by law
