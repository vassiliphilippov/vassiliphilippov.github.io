hallucinate

verb
produce ungrounded or false outputs (AI)
8.0 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / h59c8lud0s6accne6at / (AmE), / h59c8lud0s6ane6at / (BrE)
(in computing, especially AI and natural language processing) to produce information, statements, or details that are not supported by the input data or by verifiable facts; to generate false or fabricated content.

Examples

AI hallucinates facts
Researchers warned that the model might hallucinate facts when asked for obscure historical dates.
The translation system hallucinated names not present in the source text, causing serious errors.
During evaluation, the chatbot kept hallucinating citations that couldn't be verified by the reviewers.

Synonyms
