sheetless

adjective
without sheets
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːtləs / (AmE), / ˈʃiːtləs / (BrE)
without a sheet or sheets, especially referring to a bed that does not have sheets on it

Examples

Sheetless bed
After the laundry day, he had to sleep on a sheetless bed for one night.
The hotel room felt uncomfortable because the mattress was sheetless and uncovered.
She quickly made the bed, realizing it was still sheetless and needed fresh linens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bare - not covered by anything; exposed
