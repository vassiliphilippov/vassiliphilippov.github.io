simoom

noun
hot desert wind of Arabia/North Africa
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmum /; / sɪˈmun / (AmE), / sɪˈmuːm /; / sɪˈmuːn / (BrE)
a hot, dry, dust-laden wind that blows across the deserts of North Africa and Arabia, often violent and sometimes causing suffocating conditions
