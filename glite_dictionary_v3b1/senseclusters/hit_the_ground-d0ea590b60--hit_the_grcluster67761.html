hit the ground

verb
make contact with the ground
Pronunciation: / hɪt ðə ɡraʊnd / (AmE), / hɪt ðə ˈɡraʊnd / (BrE)
to make physical contact with the ground, especially after falling or being dropped

Examples

To hit the ground running
As soon as the rain started, the first drops hit the ground.
The ball hit the ground and bounced back up immediately.
The parachutist safely hit the ground after a long jump.
