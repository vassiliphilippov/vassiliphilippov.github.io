second banana

noun
supporting performer to a main comedian
7.6 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsek.ənd bə.ˈnæn.ə/ (AmE), /ˈsek.ənd bə.ˈnɑː.nə/ (BrE)
a person who plays a supporting or subordinate role, especially as an assistant or sidekick to a main leader or performer

Examples

The second banana role
After years as a second banana, he finally landed a lead role in the new series.
Despite being the second banana, her comedic timing stole the show.
He always played the second banana in comedy sketches, letting the star shine.
Although she preferred to lead, she was content being the second banana on this project.
He was always the second banana in the comedy duo, never the main star.
In most films, the second banana provides the comic relief while the hero takes center stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: straight man - the serious performer in a comedy team, setting up jokes for the comedian
  • antonym: top banana - the most important person, especially the star performer in showbusiness
