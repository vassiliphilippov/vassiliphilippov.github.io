hereunto

adverb
to this place
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪrʌnˈtuː / (AmE), / ˌhɪərʌnˈtuː / (BrE)
to this place, person, or thing; to here (now archaic or formal).

Examples

Came hereunto
The king summoned his council and they came hereunto without delay.
All documents attached hereunto shall be considered part of this agreement.
The witness testified that he had never been hereunto before that day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hereto - to this place or matter
  • antonym: thereunto - to that place or thing
