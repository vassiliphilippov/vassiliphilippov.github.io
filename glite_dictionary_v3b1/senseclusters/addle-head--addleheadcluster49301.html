addle-head

noun
a person with confused ideas
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈædəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈæd(ə)lˌhɛd / (BrE)
A person who is prone to confused thinking or incapable of serious thought.

Examples

An addle-head person
Only an addle-head would forget to bring their passport on an international flight.
Stop acting like an addle-head and focus on your work for once.
The story is full of addle-heads who stumble from one mistake to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dullard - a person who is slow to understand; a dull person
  • synonym: simpleton - a foolish or gullible person
