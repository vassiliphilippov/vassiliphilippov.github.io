red salmon

noun
fatty red flesh of Pacific sockeye salmon
3.5
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈsæmən / (AmE), / rɛd ˈsæmən / (BrE)
The fatty red flesh of a sockeye salmon, a species found along the Pacific coast and rivers, valued for its flavor.

Examples

Fresh red salmon fillet
Alaskan rivers are famous for their annual run of red salmon.
Many chefs prefer to use red salmon in sushi because of its rich flavor and color.
The can contained high-quality red salmon, perfect for making sandwiches.
