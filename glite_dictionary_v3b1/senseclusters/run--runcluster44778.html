run

noun
attempt to win elected office
Pronunciation: / rʌn / (AmE), / rʌn / (BrE)
an attempt to win an elected political office, especially as part of an election campaign

Examples

A run for office
After months of preparation, she finally announced her run for mayor.
His unsuccessful run for Congress taught him a lot about politics.
Many voters were surprised when the senator ended his run for president.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campaign - an organized course of action to achieve a particular goal, especially in politics
