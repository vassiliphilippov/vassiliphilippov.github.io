pickin'

noun
playing stringed instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɪkɪn / (BrE)
informal or dialectal term for playing a stringed instrument, especially a guitar or banjo, by plucking the strings

Examples

Pickin' a guitar
He spent the evening pickin' his guitar on the porch.
The bluegrass band entertained the crowd with some lively pickin' and singing.
She learned pickin' from her grandfather, who played banjo in a folk group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: picking - the act of plucking the strings of a musical instrument
  • antonym: strumming - playing a stringed instrument by sweeping the fingers across the strings
