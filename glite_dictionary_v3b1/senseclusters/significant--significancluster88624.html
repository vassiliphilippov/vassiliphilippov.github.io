significant

adjective
having a hidden or implied meaning
4.0
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt / (BrE)
(of a look, gesture, pause, etc.) having a special or hidden meaning; conveying a message indirectly.

Examples

Significant look
His significant silence suggested he knew more than he admitted.
She gave me a significant look as if to warn me to stay silent.
There was a significant pause before he answered the question.
