rollable

adjective
able to be rolled
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊləbəl / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləbəl / (BrE)
able to be shaped, flattened, or reduced in thickness by passing through rollers, especially in industrial processing.

Examples

Rollable steel sheet
The steel sheets are rollable and can be processed into various thicknesses for construction.
Only certain types of plastic are rollable enough to be used in this manufacturing technique.
Because the material is highly rollable, it is ideal for making thin metal foils.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brittle - hard but easily broken or cracked
  • synonym: malleable - able to be shaped or bent easily without breaking
