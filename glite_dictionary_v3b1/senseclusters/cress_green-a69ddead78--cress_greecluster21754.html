cress green

adjective
a moderate yellow-green color
7.3
Pronunciation: / krɛs ɡriːn / (AmE), / krɛs ɡriːn / (BrE)
A moderate yellow-green color that is more green and deeper than moss green, and more yellow and darker than pea green.

Examples

Cress green color
She wore a silk scarf in a shade of cress green that complemented her dress perfectly.
The artist blended yellow and blue to create the exact cress green she envisioned for her landscape painting.
The designer chose cress green walls to give the room a fresh and vibrant feel.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: pea green - a lighter, more yellowish-green color than cress green
