talks

noun
formal discussions to reach agreement
Pronunciation: / tɑks / (AmE), / tɔːks / (BrE)
formal discussions between groups, especially governments or organizations, aimed at reaching an agreement

Examples

Peace talks
Peace talks between the two countries resumed after months of tension.
The company entered into talks with the union to avoid a strike.
Trade talks have stalled over disagreements about tariffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialogue - conversation between two or more parties to resolve issues
  • synonym: negotiation - discussion aimed at reaching agreement
