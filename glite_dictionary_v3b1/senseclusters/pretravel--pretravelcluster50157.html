pretravel

noun
consultation before travel
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈtrævəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtrævəl / (BrE)
a consultation or appointment before travel, usually to receive medical advice, vaccinations, or safety information

Examples

Pretravel appointment
Before her trip to Africa, she scheduled a pretravel to discuss necessary vaccinations.
Many clinics offer pretravel services to help travelers prepare for health risks abroad.
It is wise to arrange a pretravel appointment if you are visiting a country with different health concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posttravel - relating to the period or activities after returning from a trip
  • synonym: predeparture - relating to the period or activities before leaving for a trip
