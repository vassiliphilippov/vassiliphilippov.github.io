kerchief

noun
square scarf worn on head or neck
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝː.tʃɪf / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.tʃɪf / (BrE)
A square piece of cloth, usually folded into a triangle and worn over the head or around the neck.

Examples

A kerchief around neck
He used a patterned kerchief as a stylish accessory for his outfit.
She tied a red kerchief around her neck before leaving the house.
The old woman wore a kerchief to protect her hair from the wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandana - a large colored handkerchief, often worn on the head or around the neck
  • synonym: scarf - a piece of cloth worn around the neck or head
