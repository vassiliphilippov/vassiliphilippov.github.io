Synonyms

    6.3 0.66/m

  • accusive
    8.2 0.00/m

  • accusing
    3.5 0.00/m

  • incriminatory
    8.6 0.02/m
    incriminatory suggests someone is guilty of a crime or serious wrongdoing, while accusative signals blame or suspicion more broadly.
