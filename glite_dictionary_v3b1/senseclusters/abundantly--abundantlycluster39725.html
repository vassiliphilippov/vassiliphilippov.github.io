abundantly

adverb

In large quantities; in a way that is more than enough or plentiful.

Examples

To be abundantly clear
She was abundantly clear about her expectations in the email.
The garden was abundantly filled with colorful flowers in the spring.
This region is abundantly blessed with natural resources.

Synonyms

  • copiously
    6.1 0.40/m
    copiously describes items produced or given in very large amounts, while abundantly means plentiful and more than enough in general.
  • plentifully
    2.5 0.15/m
    plentifully describes things that exist or grow in large numbers, while abundantly means more than enough in supply.
  • aplenty
    4.2 0.00/m
    aplenty often follows a noun to show a large supply, while abundantly is a general adverb meaning more than enough.
  • bountifully
    4.6 0.08/m
    bountifully suggests giving or a rich harvest with warm feeling, while abundantly describes large amounts without that generous tone.
  • plenteously
    6.3 0.00/m
    plenteously sounds more old-fashioned or formal and describes great supply, while abundantly is the common everyday adverb for plenty.
  • profusely
    4.8 1.88/m
    profusely often suggests excessive amount or intensity, especially with actions like bleeding or apologizing, while abundantly means plentiful.
