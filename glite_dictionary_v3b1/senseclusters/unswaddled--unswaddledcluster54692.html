unswaddled

adjective
not restricted
9.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈswɑːdəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈswɒdəld / (BrE)
not restricted, confined, or wrapped; free from protective or limiting restraints, often used figuratively.

Examples

Unswaddled mind
The child's imagination was unswaddled, allowing her to dream without limits.
His unswaddled creativity led to innovative solutions in the workplace.
The discussion was unswaddled by formal rules, encouraging open and honest opinions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confined - kept within limits or boundaries
  • synonym: unrestricted - not limited or controlled
