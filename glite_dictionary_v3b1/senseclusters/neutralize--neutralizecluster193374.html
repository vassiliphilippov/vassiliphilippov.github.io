Synonyms

  • eliminate
    3.4 20/m
    eliminate means to remove or get rid of a threat completely, while neutralize focuses on making it unable to act.
  • liquidate
    9.3 0.4/m
    liquidate means to kill a person who is a danger, while neutralize can also mean capture or disable without killing.
  • exterminate
    6.9 3/m
    exterminate means to destroy a pest or group completely, while neutralize aims to stop a threat without always destroying everything.
  • annihilate
    7.5 3/m
    annihilate means to destroy something totally and leave nothing, while neutralize often only makes a threat unable to act.
