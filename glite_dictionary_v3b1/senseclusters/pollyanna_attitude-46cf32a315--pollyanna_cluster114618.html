Pollyanna attitude

noun
excessively optimistic outlook
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /pɑː.liː.ˈæn.ə ˈæt.ə.tuːd/ /; / /pɑː.liː.ˈæn.ə ˈæt.ɪ.tuːd/ (AmE), /pɒ.li.ˈæn.ə ˈæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /pɒ.li.ˈæn.ə ˈæt.ə.tjuːd/ (BrE)
An excessively optimistic or cheerful outlook, often ignoring potential difficulties or negative aspects.

Examples

A Pollyanna attitude of hope
Despite the challenges ahead, her Pollyanna attitude kept the team in high spirits.
His manager appreciates his Pollyanna attitude, but sometimes wishes he were more pragmatic.
Some critics argue that a Pollyanna attitude may lead to overlooking serious problems.

Related Terms
