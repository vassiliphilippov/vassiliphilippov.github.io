Talmudist

noun
fine-detail debater
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːlmədɪst / (AmE), / ˈtælmʊdɪst / (BrE)
a person who engages in subtle, detailed argumentation or hair-splitting reasoning, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

A Talmudist in debate
During the debate, he acted like a Talmudist, focusing on every tiny detail of the argument.
Her Talmudist approach to the contract made negotiations much longer than expected.
Some critics say the committee chair is a Talmudist who complicates simple issues with excessive analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hair-splitter - someone who makes overly fine distinctions in reasoning
  • synonym: pedant - a person overly concerned with minor details or rules
