emotionally

adverb
in an emotional manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmoʊʃənəli / (AmE), / ɪˈməʊʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that involves or shows emotions or strong feelings

Examples

To speak emotionally
He spoke emotionally about his childhood experiences.
She reacted emotionally to the news of his return.
The film moved her so emotionally that she cried in the theater.
He is emotionally attached to his childhood home.
She was emotionally drained after the long and difficult conversation.
The movie left the audience emotionally moved and reflective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmly - in a calm or unemotional manner
  • synonym: mentally - in a way that relates to the mind
