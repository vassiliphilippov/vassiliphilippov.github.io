futural

adjective
relating to the future
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːtʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈfjuːtʃərəl / (BrE)
expressing or indicating future time, especially in reference to verb forms or grammatical constructions

Examples

Futural verb form
The futural aspect of the verb helps speakers talk about events that have not yet occurred.
In some languages, a special futural construction is used to express intention or prediction.
Teachers often explain the futural meaning of 'going to' when discussing English grammar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: future - relating to a time yet to come
  • antonym: past - relating to a time that has already happened
