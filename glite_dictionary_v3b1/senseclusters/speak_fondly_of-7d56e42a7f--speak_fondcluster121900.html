speak fondly of

verb
talk with affection about
Pronunciation: / spiːk ˈfɑndli əv / (AmE), / spiːk ˈfɒndli ɒv / (BrE)

Examples

To speak fondly of someone

Whenever we visit, Grandpa enjoys speaking fondly of the family farm and its traditions.
