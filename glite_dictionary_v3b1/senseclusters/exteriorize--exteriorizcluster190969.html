exteriorize

verb
bring outside the body (medicine)
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɪriəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɪəriəraɪz / (BrE)
to bring (an organ or tissue) outside the body, especially during surgery

Examples

To exteriorize an organ
During the operation, the surgeons had to exteriorize a section of the intestine to repair the damage.
If a wound becomes infected, doctors might need to exteriorize the affected tissue for effective treatment.
The procedure requires great care not to damage the organ while you exteriorize it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: externalize - to make external or bring to the outside
