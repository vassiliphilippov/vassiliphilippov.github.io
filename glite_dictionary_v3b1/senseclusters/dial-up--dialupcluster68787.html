dial-up

adjective
telephone-based internet
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪəl ʌp / (AmE), / ˈdaɪəl ʌp / (BrE)
relating to a type of internet connection that uses a telephone line to connect to the internet

Examples

Dial-up connection
In the 1990s, most people accessed the internet using a dial-up connection.
A dial-up modem is much slower than modern broadband options.
Many websites today are difficult to use with a dial-up internet service.

Related Terms
