polytene

adjective
many-stranded chromosomes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑlɪˌtin / (AmE), / ˈpɒlɪtiːn / (BrE)
having chromosomes with many parallel copies, typically found in certain insect cells

Examples

Polytene chromosomes
Researchers observed polytene chromosomes under the microscope to study gene expression in fruit flies.
The unique structure of polytene chromosomes makes them useful for genetic mapping.
In some insect larvae, the salivary gland cells contain large polytene chromosomes.
