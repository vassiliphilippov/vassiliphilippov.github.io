patient

noun
person receiving medical care
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪʃənt / (AmE), / ˈpeɪʃənt / (BrE)
a person who is receiving medical treatment or care, especially from a doctor or hospital

Examples

Hospital patient
Doctors must ensure the confidentiality of every patient's records.
The patient was admitted to the hospital after complaining of chest pain.
The nurse checked on each patient in the ward throughout the night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doctor - someone qualified to treat people's illnesses
