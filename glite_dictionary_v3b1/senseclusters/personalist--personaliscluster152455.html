personalist

adjective
focused on the person
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈpɜːsənəlɪst / (BrE)
emphasizing the importance, dignity, or primacy of the individual person in ethical, religious, or social thought

Examples

Personalist philosophy
Her personalist philosophy placed the value of each individual above all else.
The movement adopted a personalist perspective, stressing the dignity of every person.
A personalist approach to education focuses on the needs and growth of each student.

Related Terms

  • synonym: individualist - emphasizing individual freedom and self-reliance
