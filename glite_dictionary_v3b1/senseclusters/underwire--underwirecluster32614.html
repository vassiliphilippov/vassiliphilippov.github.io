underwire

noun
bra support wire
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌwaɪər / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌwaɪə / (BrE)
a thin, curved piece of metal or plastic sewn into the lower part of a bra to provide support and shape

Examples

Underwire bra
The underwire in her bra made it more supportive during exercise.
Some people find underwire bras uncomfortable after wearing them for long hours.
She bought a new bra without an underwire for extra comfort.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bra - an undergarment worn by women to support the breasts
  • degree lower: padding - soft material added to bras for comfort or shape
