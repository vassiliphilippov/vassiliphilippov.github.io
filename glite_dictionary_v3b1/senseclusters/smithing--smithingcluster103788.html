smithing

noun
metalworking craft
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsmɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
the craft or profession of shaping, making, or repairing objects from metal by heating and hammering

Examples

Traditional smithing craft
He became interested in smithing after watching a documentary about traditional metal crafts.
The village festival featured a demonstration of smithing using old-fashioned tools and techniques.
Learning smithing requires patience, skill, and a good understanding of how metals behave when heated.
He learned the art of smithing from his grandfather, who was a skilled blacksmith.
Traditional smithing requires both strength and precision to shape the hot metal.
Many museums offer demonstrations of smithing to show how tools and weapons were made in the past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blacksmithing - the craft of making and repairing things in iron by hand
  • synonym: metalworking - the process of working with metals to create parts or structures
