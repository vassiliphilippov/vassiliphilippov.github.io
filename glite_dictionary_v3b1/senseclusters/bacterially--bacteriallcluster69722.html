bacterially

adverb
in a manner involving bacteria
1.7
Pronunciation: / bækˈtɪr.i.ə.li / (AmE), / bækˈtɪə.ri.ə.li / (BrE)
In a way that involves or is caused by bacteria.

Examples

Spread bacterially
Some illnesses are spread bacterially rather than virally.
The infection can be transmitted bacterially through unsanitary water.
The laboratory confirmed the food was bacterially contaminated.
