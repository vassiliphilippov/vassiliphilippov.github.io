unremittingly

adverb
without stopping
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈmɪtɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈmɪtɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that continues without stopping or becoming less intense

Examples

Rains fall unremittingly
The rain fell unremittingly for three days, flooding the entire village.
He pursued his goals unremittingly, never allowing setbacks to slow him down.
The team worked unremittingly to meet the tight deadline for the project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occasionally - from time to time; not regularly
  • synonym: persistently - in a way that continues firmly or obstinately
  • synonym: relentlessly - in a way that does not stop or become less severe
