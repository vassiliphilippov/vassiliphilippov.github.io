downvote

noun
negative online rating
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌvoʊt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌvəʊt / (BrE)
a negative vote or rating given to content, such as a post or comment, on a website or app

Examples

A downvote on a post
The controversial post quickly received several downvotes from users who disagreed with its message.
If you think a comment is unhelpful, you can give it a downvote to lower its visibility.
After receiving a downvote, the user's answer dropped to the bottom of the discussion thread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dislike - an indication that someone does not approve of online content
  • antonym: upvote - a positive vote or rating given to online content
