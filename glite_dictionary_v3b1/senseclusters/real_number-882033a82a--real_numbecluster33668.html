(computing) a numeric data type that represents numbers with fractional parts, typically implemented as floating-point values and often an approximate representation of mathematical real numbers.

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: integer - a whole-number numeric data type without fractional parts, often contrasted with real/floating-point types
