gluteus minimus

noun
smallest gluteal muscle
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɡluˈtiəs ˈmɪnɪməs / (AmE), / ɡluːˈtiːəs ˈmɪnɪməs / (BrE)
the smallest and innermost of the three gluteal muscles, located in the buttocks

Examples

The gluteus minimus muscle
An injury to the gluteus minimus can cause pain in the hip area.
The gluteus minimus assists in stabilizing the pelvis during walking.
The physical therapist recommended exercises to strengthen the gluteus minimus.

Related Terms
