bridge

noun
type of electrical circuit (diamond-shaped)
1.0
Pronunciation: / brɪdʒ / (AmE), / brɪdʒ / (BrE)
an electrical circuit with two branches forming a diamond shape, often used for measuring resistance or other quantities

Examples

Wheatstone bridge
A bridge can be used to detect small changes in temperature by incorporating a thermistor into one of its arms.
Technicians checked the voltage across the bridge to verify the correct operation of the sensor circuit.
The Wheatstone bridge is widely used to measure unknown electrical resistances with great precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridge circuit - an electrical circuit with four arms set up in a diamond shape
