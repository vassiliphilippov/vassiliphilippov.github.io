venae sclerales

noun
veins draining the sclera of the eye
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.niː sklɪ.ˈreɪ.liːz/ /; / /ˈviː.neɪ sklɪ.ˈreɪ.liːz/ (AmE), /ˈviː.neɪ sklɪ.ˈreɪ.liːz/ /; / /ˈviː.niː sklɪ.ˈreɪ.liːz/ (BrE)
The small veins that drain the sclera of the eye and serve as tributaries to the anterior ciliary veins.

Examples
