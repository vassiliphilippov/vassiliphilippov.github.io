unidirectional

adjective
moving or operating in one direction only
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌjuːnɪdəˈrɛkʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪdɪˈrɛkʃənəl / (BrE)
Operating, moving, or allowing movement in only one direction.

Examples

A unidirectional flow
Her theory was criticized for taking a unidirectional approach to complex issues.
The river's current is strictly unidirectional in this stretch.
This microphone is unidirectional, picking up sound only from the front.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bidirectional - operating or moving in two directions
  • synonym: one-way - allowing movement or operation in only one direction
