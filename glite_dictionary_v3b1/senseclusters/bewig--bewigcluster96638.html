bewig

verb
to put a wig on
Pronunciation: / bɪˈwɪɡ / (AmE), / bɪˈwɪɡ / (BrE)
to provide someone with a wig or to place a wig on them

Examples

To bewig performers
The costume designer bewigged the performers for the historical play.
Before the photo shoot, the stylist bewigged the model to create a dramatic look.
He was bewigged and dressed in 18th-century clothing for the masquerade ball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wig - an artificial covering of hair for the head
