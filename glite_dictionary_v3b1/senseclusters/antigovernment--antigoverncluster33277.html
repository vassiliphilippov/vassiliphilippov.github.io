antigovernment

adjective
opposed to government
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / ˌæntɪˈɡʌvənmənt /; / ˌæntiˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
opposed to or against the policies, authority, or existence of a government
