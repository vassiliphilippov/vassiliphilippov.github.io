aerosol bomb

noun
bomb using a fuel-air explosive
4.2 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈer.ə.sɑːl bɑːm/ /; / /ˈer.ə.sɔːl bɑːm/ /; / /ˈer.ə.sɑːl bɔːm/ (AmE), /ˈeə.rə.sɒl bɒm/ (BrE)
