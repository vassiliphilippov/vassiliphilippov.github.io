modal logic

noun
logic dealing with necessity and possibility
8.1 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.dəl ˈlɑː.dʒɪk/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.dəl ˈlɒ.dʒɪk/ (BrE)
