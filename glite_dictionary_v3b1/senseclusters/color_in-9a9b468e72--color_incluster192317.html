color in

verb
to fill a drawing with color
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər ɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə ɪn / (BrE)
to add color to a drawing or design, especially by filling in areas with crayons, markers, or paint

Examples

To color in shapes
She asked him to color in the shapes using only red and blue pencils.
The children happily colored in the pictures in their activity books.
The teacher instructed the class to color in the map of Europe for homework.
He carefully coloured in the spaces between the lines to complete his drawing.
Many adults find colouring in to be a relaxing and enjoyable activity.
She asked the children to colour in the pictures in their books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color - to give something color; to paint or draw on
  • synonym: color in - add color to (American spelling)
  • synonym: colorize - to add color to black-and-white images or films
