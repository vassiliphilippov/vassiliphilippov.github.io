Examples

Synonyms

  • column
    1.6 11/m
    a column is an architectural upright that supports buildings or decor, while a pillar is often a non-architectural column-like formation.
  • monolith
    7.5 0.74/m
    a monolith is a single large block of stone, while a pillar is a column-shaped formation not always of one stone.
  • tower
    1.0 33/m
    a tower is usually a built, functional tall structure used by people, while a pillar is often a natural column-like formation.
