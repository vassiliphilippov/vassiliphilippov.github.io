aspersion

noun
act of sprinkling water in baptism (rare)
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˈspɝːʒən / (AmE), / əˈspɜːʃən / (BrE)
the act of sprinkling water, especially as part of a baptismal ceremony in some Christian traditions (rare)

Examples

Baptismal aspersion
During the ceremony, aspersion replaced the more common practice of immersion.
Some Christian denominations prefer aspersion to full-body baptism.
The priest performed the aspersion as part of the infant's baptism.
