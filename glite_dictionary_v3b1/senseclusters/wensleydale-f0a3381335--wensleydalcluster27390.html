Wensleydale

proper noun
British sheep breed
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛnzliˌdeɪl / (AmE), / ˈwɛnzliˌdeɪl / (BrE)
a British breed of sheep known for its long, shiny wool

Examples

Wensleydale sheep breed
The Wensleydale is prized by farmers for its exceptionally lustrous fleece.
At the agricultural show, a champion Wensleydale drew a lot of attention from visitors.
Breeders often choose the Wensleydale for its high-quality wool and gentle temperament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Merino - a breed of sheep valued for its fine wool
