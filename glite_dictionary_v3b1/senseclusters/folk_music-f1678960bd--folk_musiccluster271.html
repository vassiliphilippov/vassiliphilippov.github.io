folk music

noun
traditional music of a community or people
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊk ˌmjuzɪk / (AmE), / ˈfəʊk ˌmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
Traditional music, usually passed down orally, that reflects the customs, beliefs, and stories of a community or people.

Examples

Traditional folk music
Every summer, the small village hosts a folk music festival that attracts thousands of visitors.
Many songs in folk music are about love, struggle, and the natural landscape.
She studied the rich traditions of folk music from different parts of the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: traditional music - music passed down through generations in a culture
