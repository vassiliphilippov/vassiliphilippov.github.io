plenty

adverb
enough; as much as necessary (adverb)
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛnti / (AmE), / ˈplɛnti / (BrE)
(informal) Used as an adverb to mean 'enough' or 'as much as necessary'.

Examples

Don't worry, there's plenty to go around for everyone.
I've eaten plenty, so I really can't manage dessert.
She said she's had plenty and doesn't want any more.
