business

noun
occupation or profession for earning a living
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs / (BrE)
a person's usual occupation, profession, or trade, especially as a means of earning a living

Examples

Many people choose a business that aligns with their personal interests.
