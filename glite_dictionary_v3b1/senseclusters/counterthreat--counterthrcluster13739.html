counterthreat

noun
response to a threat
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌθrɛt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌθrɛt / (BrE)
an action or measure taken to oppose or respond to a threat

Examples

A counterthreat issued
The government issued a counterthreat in response to the hostile message from the neighboring country.
Developing a strong counterthreat can sometimes deter potential attackers.
The company’s counterthreat plan included new security protocols and staff training.
