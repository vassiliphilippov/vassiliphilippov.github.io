antiterrorism

noun
prevention of terrorism
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈtɛrərɪzəm / (BrE)
actions, policies, or measures intended to prevent or respond to acts of terrorism

Examples

Antiterrorism measures
The government increased funding for antiterrorism programs after the recent attacks.
Strict antiterrorism laws were introduced to protect public safety.
International cooperation is essential for effective antiterrorism efforts.
