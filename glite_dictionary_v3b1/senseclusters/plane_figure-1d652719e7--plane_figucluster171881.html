plane figure

noun
two-dimensional geometric shape
Pronunciation: / pleɪn ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / pleɪn ˈfɪɡjə / (BrE)
A two-dimensional geometric shape that lies entirely in one plane, such as a circle, triangle, or square.

Examples

A plane figure shape
A rectangle is a common example of a plane figure in mathematics.
Area can only be measured for a plane figure because it is two-dimensional.
The teacher drew several plane figures on the board, including a pentagon and a hexagon.
