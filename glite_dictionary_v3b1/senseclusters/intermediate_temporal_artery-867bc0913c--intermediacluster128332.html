intermediate temporal artery

noun
artery serving the middle temporal cortex
8.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈmiː.di.ət ˈtem.pər.əl ˈɑːr.tər.iː/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈmiː.di.ət ˈtem.pə.rəl ˈɑː.tə.riː/ (BrE)
an artery in the temporal region of the brain that supplies blood to the middle part of the temporal lobe's cerebral cortex

Examples

The intermediate temporal artery
