webisode

noun
online video episode
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛbɪˌsoʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɛbɪsəʊd /; / ˈwɛbɪˌsəʊd / (BrE)
a short video episode released on the internet, usually as part of a series

Examples

A webisode online
She watched the latest webisode of her favorite show online.
The comedy series released a new webisode every Friday for its fans.
Many people enjoy short webisodes during their lunch breaks at work.
The new webisode was released online before the main show aired on television.
Fans eagerly watched each webisode to learn more about their favorite characters.
The company produced a special webisode to introduce the upcoming product line.

Related Terms

  • synonym: episode - a single part of a television or radio series
  • synonym: vlog - a video blog, often posted online
  • synonym: web series - a series of scripted or non-scripted videos released on the internet
