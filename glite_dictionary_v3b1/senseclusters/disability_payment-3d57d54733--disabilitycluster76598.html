disability payment

noun
monthly benefit for disabled people unable to work
4.8 2.8
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (AmE), /dɪs.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tiː ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (BrE)
a monthly payment made to someone who has become disabled and is unable to work, usually provided by a government or insurance program

Examples

Receive a disability payment
After her accident, she began receiving a disability payment to help cover her living expenses.
He applied for a disability payment after being diagnosed with a chronic illness.
The government increased the amount of the disability payment this year.
