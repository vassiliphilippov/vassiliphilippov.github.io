Centrospermae

noun
obsolete group of flowering plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntroʊˈspɜːrmiː /; / ˌsɛn.trəʊˈspɜr.mi / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrəʊˈspɜːmiː /; / ˌsɛn.trəʊˈspɜː.miː / (BrE)
