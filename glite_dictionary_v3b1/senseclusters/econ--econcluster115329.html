econ

adjective
low-cost or basic
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkɑn / (AmE), / ˈiːkɒn / (BrE)
informal: describing a product or version that is low-cost, basic, or designed to save money

Examples

econ model car
We chose the econ package because it fit our budget perfectly.
The hotel offers an econ room for travelers who want to save money.
He bought an econ car to reduce his monthly expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economy - designed to save money or be low-cost
  • antonym: luxury - high-end, expensive, and offering extra features
