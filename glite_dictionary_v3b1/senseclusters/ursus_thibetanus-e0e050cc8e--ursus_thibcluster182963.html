Ursus thibetanus

noun
Asian black bear species
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrsəs θɪˈbeɪtənəs / (AmE), / ˈɜːsəs tɪˈbeɪtənəs / (BrE)
a species of bear with a black coat native to central and eastern Asia, also known as the Asiatic black bear or moon bear

Examples

Ursus thibetanus bear
Conservationists are concerned about the declining population of Ursus thibetanus in the wild.
The Ursus thibetanus is often called the moon bear because of the crescent-shaped marking on its chest.
Unlike the brown bear, Ursus thibetanus prefers to inhabit forested mountainous regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Asiatic black bear - common English name for Ursus thibetanus
  • degree lower: Ursus arctos - brown bear species found in Eurasia and North America
  • synonym: moon bear - another common name for Ursus thibetanus due to white patch on chest
