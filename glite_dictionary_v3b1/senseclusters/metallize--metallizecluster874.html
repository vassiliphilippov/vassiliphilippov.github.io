metallize

verb
coat with a thin layer of metal
5.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmet.əl.aɪz/ (AmE), /ˈmet.əl.aɪz/ (BrE)
to coat (an object) with a thin layer of metal, often for protection or decoration

Examples

To metallize a surface
After being metallized, the glass has a shiny, reflective surface.
Many packaging materials are metallized to improve their barrier properties.
The company can metallize plastic components for electronic devices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demetallize - to remove a metallic coating from an object
  • synonym: electroplate - to coat with a layer of metal using electrolysis
