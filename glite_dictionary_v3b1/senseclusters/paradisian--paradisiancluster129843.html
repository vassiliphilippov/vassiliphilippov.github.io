paradisian

noun
inhabitant of Paradise
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈdɪziən / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈdɪziən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Paradise, especially a mythical, religious, or ideal place of happiness

Examples

A paradisian guide
The legend tells of a paradisian who guided lost souls to safety.
In the story, every paradisian lived in eternal happiness and peace.
The poet imagined himself as a paradisian wandering through gardens of light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angel - a spiritual being often associated with Paradise
  • antonym: mortal - a human being subject to death, not living in Paradise
