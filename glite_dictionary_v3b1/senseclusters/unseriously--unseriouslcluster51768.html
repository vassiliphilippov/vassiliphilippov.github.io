unseriously

adverb
not seriously
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪriəsli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪəriəsli / (BrE)
to a minor or not serious degree; not severely or gravely.

Examples

Unseriously hurt
The players were unseriously injured and quickly returned to the game.
She was unseriously ill and recovered after just a day of rest.
Most passengers were unseriously affected by the turbulence during the flight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mildly - to a small or slight degree
  • antonym: seriously - to a severe or significant degree
