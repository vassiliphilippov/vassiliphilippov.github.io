watering can

noun
container for watering plants
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ɚɪŋ kæn / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtərɪŋ kan / (BrE)
a container with a handle and a spout with a perforated nozzle, used for sprinkling water over plants

Examples

A watering can with spout
He handed me a green watering can to help water the vegetable patch.
She filled the watering can before heading outside to tend her flowers.
The children took turns using the watering can to sprinkle water over the seedlings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sprinkler - device that sprays water, especially for watering lawns or gardens
