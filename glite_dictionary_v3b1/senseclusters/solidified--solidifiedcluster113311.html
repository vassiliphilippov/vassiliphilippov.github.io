solidified

adjective
turned into a solid
6.7
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪdəˌfaɪd / (AmE), / səˈlɪdɪfaɪd / (BrE)

Examples

Solidified metal block

The wax solidified as soon as it touched the cold water.
