non-settling

adjective
not depositing
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈsɛtlɪŋ / (AmE), / nɒnˈsɛtlɪŋ / (BrE)
not forming a deposit or not coming to rest at the bottom of a liquid or mixture

Examples

Non-settling particles
The non-settling particles made the solution appear cloudy for hours.
A non-settling mixture is important in some industrial processes where uniformity is needed.
Scientists studied the non-settling behavior of certain fine powders in water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: settling - coming to rest at the bottom of a liquid
  • synonym: suspended - remaining dispersed in a liquid without settling
