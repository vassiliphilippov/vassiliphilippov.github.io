magic trick

noun
illusion or feat performed for entertainment
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmædʒ.ɪk trɪk/ /; / /ˈmædʒ.ɪk tʃrɪk/ (AmE), /ˈmædʒ.ɪk trɪk/ /; / /ˈmædʒ.ɪk tʃrɪk/ (BrE)
