network

noun
interconnected system or group
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌwɝk / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌwɜːk / (BrE)
A group or system of interconnected people or things.

Examples

A network of friends
Researchers benefit from being part of an international network of experts.
She built a strong network of friends and colleagues in the city.
The company is expanding its network of stores to reach new customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: system - a set of things working together as parts of a mechanism or network
  • synonym: web - a complex system of interconnected elements
