till death do us part

x
expression of lifelong marital commitment
Pronunciation: / tɪl dɛθ du ʌs pɑrt / (AmE), / tɪl dɛθ dʊ ʌs pɑːt / (BrE)
used to express the intention or expectation that a marriage will last for the rest of the couple's lives, typically as part of traditional wedding vows

Examples

Till death do us part vows
Many couples still include till death do us part in their vows today.
Their grandparents' marriage was a true example of till death do us part.
They pledged to stay together till death do us part during their wedding ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: for better or (for) worse - part of traditional wedding vows expressing commitment in all circumstances
