spawner

noun
mechanism that generates game entities
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈspɑːn.ər/ /; / /ˈspɔːn.ər/ (AmE), /ˈspɔːn.ə/ (BrE)
(in computing and video games) a mechanism, component, or location that creates or releases new game entities or background processes, such as enemies, items, or tasks.

Examples

Game spawner block
The dungeon's spawner released a wave of goblins at the start of each round.
Game designers adjusted the spawner to reduce how often enemies appear near the safe zone.
On the server, a background spawner process automatically created worker tasks to handle uploads.
