define

verb
state essential qualities or nature
1.8 1.0
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfaɪn/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfaɪn/ (BrE)
to state, explain, or decide exactly what something is, what it means, or what its limits are

Examples

To define a word
It can be difficult to define what makes a work of art truly valuable.
Philosophers have long tried to define the nature of consciousness.
The law seeks to define the essential qualities of a legal contract.
Her actions define what it means to be a leader in this organization.
The committee will define the criteria for selecting the winner.
We need to define success before choosing a strategy.
Let us define the variables used in the equation before we proceed.
The agreement clearly defines the responsibilities of each party.
The law defines what constitutes acceptable behavior in this context.
