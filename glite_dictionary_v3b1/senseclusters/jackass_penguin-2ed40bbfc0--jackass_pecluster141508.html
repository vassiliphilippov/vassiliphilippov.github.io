jackass penguin

noun
small penguin with a braying call
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌæs ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌɑːs ˈpɛŋɡwɪn / (BrE)
a species of small penguin native to southern Africa and South America, known for its braying, donkey-like call

Examples

The jackass penguin
The jackass penguin is famous for the loud braying noise it makes during mating season.
Unlike other species, the jackass penguin nests in burrows to protect its eggs from the sun.
We observed a colony of jackass penguins waddling along the rocky shore in South Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: African penguin - small penguin native to southern Africa; another common name for jackass penguin
