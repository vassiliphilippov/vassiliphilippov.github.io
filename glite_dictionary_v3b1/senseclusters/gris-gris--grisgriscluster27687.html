gris-gris

noun
African/Caribbean protective amulet
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriˌɡri / (AmE), / ˈɡriːˌɡriː / (BrE)
(in African and Caribbean cultures) an amulet or charm, often worn for protection against evil or to bring good luck.

Examples

A gris-gris charm
He wore a gris-gris around his neck, believing it would protect him from harm.
In some communities, a gris-gris is prepared by a spiritual leader for special occasions.
Tourists were fascinated by the brightly colored gris-gris sold in the market.
