habitation

noun
the act of living in a place
Pronunciation: / ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of living in a particular place or occupying it as a home

Examples

Animal habitation
Beavers' lodges are a clear evidence of animal habitation along the riverbanks.
Permanent habitation on the island only began in the nineteenth century.
The cave showed signs of ancient human habitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhabitation - the act of inhabiting or living in a place
  • antonym: vacancy - the state of being unoccupied; absence of habitation
