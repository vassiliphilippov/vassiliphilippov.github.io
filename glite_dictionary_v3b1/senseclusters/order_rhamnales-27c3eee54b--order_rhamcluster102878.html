order Rhamnales

noun
order of dicotyledonous plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ræmˈneɪliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ræmˈneɪliːz / (BrE)
a former order of dicotyledonous plants that included the families Elaeagnaceae, Leeaceae, Rhamnaceae, and Vitaceae

Examples

The order Rhamnales plants
Historical botanical texts often refer to the order Rhamnales and its constituent families.
Researchers once classified grapevines in the order Rhamnales, but modern taxonomy places them in Vitales instead.
The order Rhamnales included several families of shrubs and vines before its plants were reassigned to other groups.
