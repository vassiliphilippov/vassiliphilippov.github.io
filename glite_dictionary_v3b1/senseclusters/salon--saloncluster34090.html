salon

noun
hair or beauty treatment shop
1.2
Pronunciation: / səˈlɑn / (AmE), / sæˈlɒn / (BrE)
A shop where hairdressers or beauticians provide hair and beauty treatments.

Examples

Hair salon
He opened a new salon downtown specializing in organic beauty treatments.
She visits the salon every few weeks to have her hair styled.
The salon offers a range of services, from haircuts to manicures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barbershop - a place where men have their hair cut and beards trimmed
  • synonym: parlour - another word for a shop, especially an old-fashioned one
  • synonym: spa - a place where people go for health and beauty treatments
