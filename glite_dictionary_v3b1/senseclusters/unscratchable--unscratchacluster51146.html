unscratchable

adjective
cannot be soothed by scratching
6.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskrætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈskrætʃəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be relieved or soothed by scratching, especially referring to an itch or irritation

Examples

Unscratchable itch
The mosquito bite left her with an unscratchable itch that kept her awake all night.
He complained about an unscratchable irritation deep under his cast.
Some allergies cause an unscratchable sensation that is difficult to ignore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: soothing - able to relieve pain or discomfort
  • synonym: unrelievable - not able to be relieved or eased
