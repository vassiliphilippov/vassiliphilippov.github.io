tuft

noun
small bunch of hair, feathers, grass, etc.
Pronunciation: / tʌft / (AmE), / tʌft / (BrE)
a small bunch or cluster of hair, feathers, grass, threads, or similar material held or growing together at the base

Examples

A tuft of hair
A tuft of grass grew between the cracks in the sidewalk.
She absentmindedly twisted a tuft of her hair around her finger.
The bird has a distinctive tuft of feathers on its head.
A single tuft of grass stood out in the barren field.
His hat was decorated with a bright red tuft of feathers.
The baby had a tuft of dark hair on the top of his head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clump - a small group of things growing closely together
  • synonym: cluster - a group of similar things or people positioned or occurring closely together
  • synonym: lock - a piece or strand of hair that grows or hangs together
  • degree higher: mass - a large body of matter with no definite shape
  • degree lower: strand - a single thin length of something such as thread, fiber, or hair
