appliqué

noun
decorative design sewn onto fabric
6.9 5.9
Pronunciation: /æ.plɪ.ˈkeɪ/ /; / /ə.plɪ.ˈkeɪ/ (AmE), /ə.pliː.ˈkeɪ/ /; / /æ.plɪ.ˈkeɪ/ (BrE)
a decorative design made of one material sewn onto the surface of another

Examples

A fabric appliqué
Artisans use appliqué to add color and texture to plain fabrics.
Her dress featured an intricate appliqué of lace at the neckline.
The quilt was decorated with a beautiful floral appliqué on each panel.
