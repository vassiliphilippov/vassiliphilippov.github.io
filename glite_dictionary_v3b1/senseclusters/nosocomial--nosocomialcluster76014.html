nosocomial

adjective
originating in a hospital
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnoʊsəˈkoʊmiəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒsəˈkəʊmiəl / (BrE)
(of a disease or infection) acquired or originating in a hospital.

Examples

Nosocomial infection
Doctors are concerned about the rising number of nosocomial cases involving antibiotic-resistant bacteria.
Hospitals implement strict protocols to prevent nosocomial diseases from spreading among patients.
The patient developed a nosocomial infection after two weeks in the intensive care unit.
