knot

noun
a cluster or group of people/things
7.0
Pronunciation: / nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
a small group of people or things closely clustered together

Examples

A knot of people
A knot of protesters formed near the entrance to the building.
A knot of reporters gathered outside the courthouse to await the verdict.
He found a knot of children playing under the old oak tree.
