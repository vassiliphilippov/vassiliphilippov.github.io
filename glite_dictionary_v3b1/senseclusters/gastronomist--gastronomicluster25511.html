gastronomist

noun
food expert or critic
7.4
Pronunciation: /gæs.ˈtrɑːn.ə.mɪst/ (AmE), /gæs.ˈtrɒn.ə.mɪst/ (BrE)
a person who is an expert in food, cooking, and dining
