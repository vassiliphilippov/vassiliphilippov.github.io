gastronomist

noun
food expert or critic
Pronunciation: / ɡæˈstrɑːnəmɪst /; / ɡæsˈtrɑːnəmɪst / (AmE), / ɡæˈstrɒnəmɪst /; / ɡæsˈtrɒnəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in food, cooking, and dining

Examples

The famous gastronomist visited the new restaurant and praised its creative menu.
As a gastronomist, he travels the world to discover unique culinary traditions.
Many aspiring chefs read the reviews written by the respected gastronomist.
The gastronomist wrote a detailed review of the new French restaurant in town.
As a respected gastronomist, she is often invited to judge international cooking competitions.
Many aspiring chefs seek advice from a gastronomist to improve their culinary skills.
The renowned gastronomist was invited to judge the international cooking competition.
As a passionate gastronomist, she travels the world to discover new culinary techniques.
Many aspiring chefs look up to a skilled gastronomist for inspiration and guidance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant

  • synonym: foodie - a person with a strong interest in food and cooking
  • synonym: gastronomer - a person who is knowledgeable about fine food and drink
  • synonym: gourmet - a person who enjoys and knows a lot about good food and drink
  • antonym: layperson - someone who is not an expert in a particular field
