gross

adjective
very fat in a repellent way
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs / (BrE)
(of a person) very fat in a way that is unattractive or repellent.

Examples

Gross body
He found the gross figure of the man in the movie disturbing.
That was a gross exaggeration of her size, and it was hurtful.
The villain was described as a gross old man with oily hair and a wheezing cough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obese - extremely overweight, especially to a medically significant degree; fat
  • antonym: skinny - very thin, especially unhealthily so
