badge

noun
symbol or sign of status or quality
1.0
Pronunciation: / bædʒ / (AmE), / bædʒ / (BrE)
a sign or object (such as a pin, card, or mark) that shows someone's status, membership, or achievement

Examples

A badge of honor
For him, the scar was a badge of honor earned in battle.
Her accent became a badge of her upper-class upbringing.
Wearing expensive watches is sometimes used as a badge of success.
Each employee must display their badge while on company property.
She collected every conference badge she had ever received.
The police officer wore his badge proudly on his uniform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emblem - a thing serving as a symbolic representation, especially a distinctive badge of a nation, organization, or family
  • synonym: insignia - official sign or emblem of rank, office, or membership
  • synonym: mark - a visible indication or sign used for identification or to indicate status
  • synonym: medal - a metal disc or other object awarded as a prize or honor
  • synonym: symbol - a thing that represents or stands for something else, especially a material object representing something abstract
