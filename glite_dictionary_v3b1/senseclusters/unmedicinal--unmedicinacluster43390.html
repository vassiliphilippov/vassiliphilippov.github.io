unmedicinal

adjective
not medicinal; not for medical use
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.məˈdɪs.ɪ.nəl / (AmE), / ˌʌn.məˈdɪs.ɪ.nəl / (BrE)
not having a medicinal effect; not prescribed or intended for medical use

Examples

Unmedicinal use
He used essential oils for unmedicinal purposes, enjoying their scent rather than any health benefits.
Some people consume mushrooms for unmedicinal experiences unrelated to health or healing.
The herbal tea had a pleasant flavor but was entirely unmedicinal.
