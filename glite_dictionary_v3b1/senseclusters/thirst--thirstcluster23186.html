thirst

noun
strong desire or craving (not related to drink)
Pronunciation: / θɜrst / (AmE), / θɜːst / (BrE)
a strong desire or craving for something, especially not related to food or drink

Examples

Thirst for success
Her thirst for adventure led her to travel the world.
Many writers are driven by a thirst for recognition and understanding.
Though he achieved much, his thirst for success remained unquenched.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunger - a strong desire for something (especially not food)
