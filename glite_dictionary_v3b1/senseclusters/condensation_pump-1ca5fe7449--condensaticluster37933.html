condensation pump

noun
vacuum pump using condensation
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dənˈseɪ.ʃən pʌmp / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dənˈseɪ.ʃən pʌmp / (BrE)
a type of vacuum pump that achieves a high vacuum by condensing gas or vapor onto a cold surface

Examples

A condensation pump
A condensation pump works by trapping vapor onto cooled surfaces, allowing for the creation of an extremely high vacuum.
Early particle accelerators often relied on the efficiency of the condensation pump in their vacuum systems.
Scientists used a condensation pump to evacuate air from the chamber before conducting their experiment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vacuum pump - a device that removes gas from a sealed volume to create a vacuum
