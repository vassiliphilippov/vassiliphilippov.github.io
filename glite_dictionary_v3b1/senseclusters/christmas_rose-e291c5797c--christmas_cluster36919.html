Christmas rose

noun
evergreen plant with winter-blooming rose-like flowers
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs·məs roʊz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs·məs rəʊz / (BrE)
a European evergreen plant (Helleborus niger) with white or purplish rose-like flowers that bloom in winter, often around Christmastime

Examples

The Christmas rose plant
Despite its name, the Christmas rose is not a true rose but is cherished for its winter beauty.
Many people plant the Christmas rose for its beautiful blossoms that appear just in time for the holidays.
The Christmas rose blooms in the middle of winter, brightening many gardens with its delicate flowers.
