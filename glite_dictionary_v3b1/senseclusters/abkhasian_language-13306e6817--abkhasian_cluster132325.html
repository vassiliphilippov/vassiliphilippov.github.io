Abkhasian language

noun
language of the Abkhaz people
Pronunciation: / æbˈkeɪziən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / æbˈkɑːziən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language spoken by the Abkhaz people of Abkhazia, belonging to the Northwest Caucasian language family

Examples

Abkhasian language speakers
Linguists study the Abkhasian language to understand its complex consonant system.
She is fluent in both Russian and Abkhasian language due to her Abkhazian heritage.
The Abkhasian language uses a unique script derived from Cyrillic.
