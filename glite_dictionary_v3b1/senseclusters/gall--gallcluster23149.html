gall

verb
to irritate or annoy deeply
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɡɔl / (AmE), / ɡɔːl / (BrE)
to irritate or annoy someone greatly; to make someone feel angry or resentful

Examples

To gall someone
Her constant criticism is galling to everyone around her.
It really galls me when people take credit for my work.
The unfair decision galled the whole team for weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritate - to make (someone) annoyed or angry
  • antonym: please - to make (someone) feel happy or satisfied
  • synonym: vex - to make (someone) feel annoyed, frustrated, or worried
