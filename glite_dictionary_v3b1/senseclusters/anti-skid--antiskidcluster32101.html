anti-skid

adjective
prevents slipping
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌskɪd / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌskɪd / (BrE)
designed to prevent slipping or skidding, especially on surfaces or vehicles

Examples

Anti-skid tires
The car is equipped with anti-skid brakes to improve safety on icy roads.
Workers installed anti-skid mats in the kitchen to prevent accidents.
He bought anti-skid shoes for hiking on wet and uneven trails.

Related Terms

  • synonym: non-slip - designed to prevent slipping
  • antonym: slippery - easy to slide on or lose grip
