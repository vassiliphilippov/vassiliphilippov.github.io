slumlord

noun
neglectful landlord
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈslʌmˌlɔrd / (AmE), / ˈslʌmˌlɔːd / (BrE)
a landlord who owns and rents out poorly maintained, run-down properties and neglects repairs or tenant needs

Examples

The slumlord neglects repairs
The slumlord refused to fix the broken heating system in the apartment building.
Many tenants complained that their slumlord ignored requests for basic repairs.
City officials fined the slumlord for failing to maintain safe living conditions.
The slumlord ignored repeated requests to fix the leaking roof in the apartment building.
Tenants organized a protest against the slumlord who refused to provide basic maintenance.
City officials fined the slumlord for failing to meet health and safety standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landlord - a person who owns and rents out property
  • antonym: tenant - a person who rents property from a landlord
