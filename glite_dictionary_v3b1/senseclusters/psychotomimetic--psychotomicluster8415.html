psychotomimetic

noun
drug causing psychosis-like effects
9.9
Pronunciation: /saɪ.koʊ.toʊ.mɪ.ˈmet.ɪk/ /; / /saɪ.kə.toʊ.mɪ.ˈmet.ɪk/ (AmE), /saɪ.kəʊ.təʊ.mɪ.ˈmet.ɪk/ /; / /saɪ.kəʊ.tə.mɪ.ˈmet.ɪk/ (BrE)
a drug or agent that causes effects similar to psychosis, such as hallucinations or disordered thinking

Examples

A psychotomimetic drug

Some substances classified as psychotomimetics can temporarily induce hallucinations in healthy individuals.
Doctors warn that misuse of a psychotomimetic may lead to dangerous psychological symptoms.
