quartz battery

noun
stamp mill for crushing quartz ore
Pronunciation: / kwɔrts ˈbætəɹi / (AmE), / kwɔːts ˈbætəri / (BrE)
a machine consisting of a series of heavy stamps used to crush quartz ore to extract gold or other minerals.

Examples

A quartz battery machine
A tour of the site featured a demonstration of the restored quartz battery in operation.
During the 19th century, miners relied on the quartz battery to process tons of ore.
The abandoned gold field still has a quartz battery standing near the creek.
