stopcock

noun
valve for regulating liquid flow
7.7 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆstÉ‘pËŒkÉ‘k / (AmE), / ËˆstÉ’pËŒkÉ’k / (BrE)
A valve or faucet consisting of a rotating device used to control the flow of a liquid through a pipe.

Examples

Main stopcock valve
Remember to locate the main stopcock in case of an emergency leak.
The plumber turned the stopcock to shut off the water supply.
They installed a new stopcock to better regulate the flow of water to the garden hose.

Synonyms

  • cock
    8.6 0.28/m
    a cock is a general valve for liquids or gas in pipes, while a stopcock is the valve used to shut or control water flow.
  • turncock
    9.9 0.00/m
    a turncock is a valve you operate by turning a handle, while a stopcock is a rotating valve used to control flow in a pipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faucet - device for controlling flow of liquid, especially in sinks
  • synonym: valve - device controlling the flow of liquid or gas
