scribbling block

noun
pad for quick notes or sketches
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪblɪŋ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈskrɪblɪŋ blɒk / (BrE)
A pad of paper used for preliminary or quick writing, notes, or sketches.

Examples

A scribbling block pad
