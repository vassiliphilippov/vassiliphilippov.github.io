click into gear

verb
start functioning efficiently
Pronunciation: / klɪk ˈɪntu ɡɪr / (AmE), / klɪk ˈɪntu ˈɡɪə / (BrE)
to start functioning effectively or working efficiently, especially after a period of inactivity or slow progress

Examples

To click into gear quickly
After a slow start, the business finally clicked into gear and began to grow rapidly.
As soon as the deadline approached, the whole project team clicked into gear and worked efficiently.
It took me a few minutes to click into gear on Monday morning after the weekend.
