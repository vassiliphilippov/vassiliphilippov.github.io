preside

verb
to act as leader or chairperson
2.5
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzaɪd / (AmE), / prɪˈzaɪd / (BrE)
to be in charge of a formal meeting or organization; to act as the leader or chairperson.

Examples

To preside over meetings
As club president, he regularly presides over important discussions and decisions.
She was chosen to preside at the conference due to her expertise.
The CEO will preside over the annual shareholder meeting today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chair - to act as the chairperson of a meeting or organization
