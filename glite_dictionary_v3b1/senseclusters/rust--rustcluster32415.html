rust

verb
to corrode or be destroyed (of metal)
3.8
Pronunciation: / rʌst / (AmE), / rʌst / (BrE)
to become or cause to become covered with rust (a reddish-brown coating), especially on metal

Examples

To rust a tool
Even stainless steel can rust under harsh conditions.
If you leave your tools outside, they will rust in the rain.
The old bicycle started to rust after years of neglect.
If you leave the tools outside, the rain will rust them quickly.
Saltwater can rust even stainless steel over time.
The old pipes began to rust after years of neglect.
If you leave your bike in the rain, it will eventually rust.
Old tools in the shed began to rust from years of neglect.
The car's bumper started to rust after several winters exposed to road salt.
