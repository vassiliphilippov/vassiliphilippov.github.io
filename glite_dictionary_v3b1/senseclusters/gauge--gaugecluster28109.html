gauge

noun
standard for measurement or comparison
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
a standard or accepted example of a quantity or quality, used as a point of reference for judging, measuring, or comparing others

Examples

A gauge of success
Customer satisfaction is often used as a gauge of a company's success.
The test results were compared against a gauge established by industry experts.
This report will serve as a gauge for future performance evaluations.
