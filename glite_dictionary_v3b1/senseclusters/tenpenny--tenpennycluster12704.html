tenpenny

noun
three-inch nail
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌpɛni / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌpɛni / (BrE)
a nail about three inches long, commonly used in carpentry and known as a ten-penny nail

Examples

A tenpenny nail
The carpenter reached for a tenpenny to secure the wooden beam in place.
He bought a box of tenpenny nails for his weekend construction project.
After measuring the board, she hammered a tenpenny through it to hold it steady.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nail - a small metal spike used to fasten things together
