Ophrys apifera

noun
a European orchid called the bee orchid
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊfrɪs əˈpɪfərə / (AmE), / ˈɒfrɪs apɪˈfɪərə / (BrE)
A European orchid whose flowers resemble bumble bees in shape and color, commonly known as the bee orchid.
