Eucalyptus australis

noun
oil from eucalyptus leaves
Pronunciation: / juːkəˈlɪptəs ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (AmE), / juːkəˈlɪptəs ɔːˈstreɪlɪs / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from the leaves of the Eucalyptus australis tree, used in medicine and fragrances
