midterm exam

noun
exam given halfway through a term
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌtɜrm ɪɡˈzæm / (AmE), / ˈmɪdtɜːm ɪɡˈzæm / (BrE)
an examination given in the middle of an academic term to assess students' progress

Examples

The midterm exam day

Your grade will be based on the midterm exam, final exam, and class participation.
