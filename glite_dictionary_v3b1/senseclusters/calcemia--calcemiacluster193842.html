calcemia

noun
blood calcium level
8.2
Pronunciation: /kæl.ˈsiː.miː.ə/ (AmE), /kæl.ˈsiː.miː.ə/ (BrE)

Examples

Calcemia level
Doctors monitor calcemia to assess a patient's calcium balance.
Abnormal calcemia can indicate problems with the parathyroid glands or kidneys.
