Lamium amplexicaule

noun
Eurasian plant, mint family, henbit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.mi.əm æmˌplɛk.sɪˈoʊl / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.mi.əm æmˌplɛk.sɪˈəʊl / (BrE)
A Eurasian plant of the mint family, having rounded, toothed leaves and small two-lipped white or purplish-red flowers, commonly known as henbit.

Examples

Lamium amplexicaule plant
Lamium amplexicaule is commonly found in gardens and fields throughout Europe.
