pilot burner

noun
small gas burner for igniting a main burner
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlət ˌbɝːnər / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlət ˌbɜːnə / (BrE)
a small auxiliary gas burner that provides a continuous flame to ignite a larger gas burner when needed
