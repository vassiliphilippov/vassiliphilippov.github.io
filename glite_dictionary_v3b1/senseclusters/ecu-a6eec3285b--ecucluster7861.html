ECU

noun
extreme close-up shot
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌiː siː ˈjuː / (AmE), / ˌiː siː ˈjuː / (BrE)
an extreme close-up shot in film or photography that tightly frames a subject or object, often showing only part of it

Examples

ECU of the face
