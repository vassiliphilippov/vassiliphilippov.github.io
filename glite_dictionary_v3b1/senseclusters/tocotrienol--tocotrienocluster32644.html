tocotrienol

noun
vitamin E compound
Pronunciation: / ˌtoʊkoʊˈtraɪənɒl / (AmE), / ˌtəʊkəʊˈtraɪənɒl / (BrE)
a chemical compound related to vitamin E, found in certain plant oils and known for its antioxidant properties

Examples

tocotrienol compound
Researchers are studying how tocotrienol may help protect cells from damage.
Rice bran oil contains a significant amount of tocotrienol compared to other oils.
Some supplements include tocotrienol for its potential health benefits.
