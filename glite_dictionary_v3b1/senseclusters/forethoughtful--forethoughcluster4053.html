forethoughtful

adjective
careful about future consequences
Pronunciation: / ˌfɔrˈθɔt.fəl / (AmE), / ˌfɔːˈθɔːt.fəl / (BrE)
Careful to consider or plan for the future; showing prudence or consideration of possible events.

Examples

A forethoughtful plan
Her forethoughtful approach to saving money helped her avoid financial troubles.
They implemented a forethoughtful strategy to minimize risks in the project.
We appreciated his forethoughtful questions during the planning meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prudent - acting with or showing care and thought for the future
  • antonym: rash - acting without thinking about the consequences
