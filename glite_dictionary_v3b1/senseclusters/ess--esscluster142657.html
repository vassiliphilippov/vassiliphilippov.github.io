ess

noun
S-shaped curve
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɛs / (AmE), / ɛs / (BrE)
a curve, bend, or shape that resembles the letter S, especially in a road or path

Examples

A road ess
The river made a sharp ess before flowing into the lake.
Drivers must slow down when approaching an ess in the mountain road.
The path through the garden forms an elegant ess between the flower beds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serpentine - having a winding, snake-like shape
  • antonym: straight - not curved or bent; direct
