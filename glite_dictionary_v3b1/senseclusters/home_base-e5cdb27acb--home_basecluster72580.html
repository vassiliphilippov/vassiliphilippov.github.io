home base

noun
base in baseball a runner must touch to score
Pronunciation: / hoʊm beɪs / (AmE), / həʊm beɪs / (BrE)
(Baseball) the base consisting of a flat, usually rubber, slab that the batter stands beside and must be touched by a runner to score a run.
