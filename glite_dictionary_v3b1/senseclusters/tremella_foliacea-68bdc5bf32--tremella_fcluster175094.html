Tremella foliacea

noun
a species of brown jelly fungus
9.9
Pronunciation: / trəˈmɛl.ə foʊ.liˈeɪ.ʃə / (AmE), / trəˈmɛl.ə fəʊ.lɪˈeɪ.sɪə / (BrE)
a species of jelly fungus with a broad, gelatinous, brownish fruiting body resembling a bunch of leaf lettuce, found mostly on decaying wood
