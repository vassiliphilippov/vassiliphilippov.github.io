rebelliously

adverb
in a rebellious manner
Pronunciation: / rɪˈbɛliəsli / (AmE), / rɪˈbɛliəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a desire to resist authority or control; in a rebellious manner.

Examples

Answer rebelliously
Even when asked politely, he answered rebelliously, challenging every suggestion.
She rebelliously refused to follow her parents' strict rules.
The student glared rebelliously at the teacher after being scolded.

Related Terms

  • antonym: obediently - in a manner showing willingness to comply
  • synonym: rebellious - showing a desire to resist authority or control
