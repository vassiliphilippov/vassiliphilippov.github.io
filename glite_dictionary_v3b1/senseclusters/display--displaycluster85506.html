display

noun
something shown to the public
1.3 1.6
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpleɪ /; / dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / dɪsˈpleɪ /; / dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
an object, collection, or arrangement shown to the public, such as in a museum, shop, or garden

Examples

Museum display
During the festival, there will be a display of traditional dances and costumes.
The art gallery had a stunning display of modern sculpture in its main hall.
The store arranged a colorful display of fresh fruit in the front window to attract customers.
She admired the colorful display of flowers in the garden.
The museum had a beautiful display of ancient pottery near the entrance.
There was a digital display showing the train's arrival times in the station.

  • synonym: exhibition - a public display of works of art or items of interest
  • synonym: presentation - the act of showing something to an audience
