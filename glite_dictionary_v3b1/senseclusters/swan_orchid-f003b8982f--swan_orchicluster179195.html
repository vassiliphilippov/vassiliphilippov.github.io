swan orchid

noun
orchid of genus Cycnoches
4.0
Pronunciation: / swɑn ˈɔrkɪd / (AmE), / swɒn ˈɔːkɪd / (BrE)
any of several orchids of the genus Cycnoches, noted for their slender arching columns of flowers that resemble a swan's neck.

Examples

A swan orchid flower
Botanists were excited to find a rare species of swan orchid in the rainforest.
He specializes in cultivating swan orchids due to their unique and beautiful blooms.
The swan orchid is renowned for its graceful, arching flowers that resemble a swan's neck.
