vascular bundle

noun
plant tissue strand of xylem and phloem
Pronunciation: / ˈvæskjələr ˈbʌndəl / (AmE), / ˈvæskjʊlə ˈbʌnd(ə)l / (BrE)
a strand of the vascular system in stems and leaves of higher plants, consisting mainly of xylem and phloem tissue

Examples

A vascular bundle in stems
The position of each vascular bundle is important for the plant’s growth and strength.
The stem cross-section revealed several vascular bundles arranged in a ring near the outer edge.
Transport of water and nutrients in plants occurs through the vascular bundle.
