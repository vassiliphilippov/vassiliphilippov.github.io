noncontributory

adjective
not requiring contributions
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtrɪbjətɔri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtrɪbjʊtəri / (BrE)
not requiring contributions, especially from employees, or not playing a part in causing something

Examples

A noncontributory pension plan
The company offers a noncontributory pension plan to all staff.
Her role in the project was largely noncontributory and did not affect the outcome.
Employees appreciate the benefits of a noncontributory health insurance policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contributory - requiring contributions or playing a part in causing something
