Parisienne

noun
female native or resident of Paris
Pronunciation: / ˌpærɪziˈɛn / (AmE), / ˌpaːriˈɛn / (BrE)
a woman or girl who is a native or resident of Paris, especially one regarded as typically elegant or fashionable

Examples

A Parisienne woman
At the café, a stylish Parisienne chatted with her friends over coffee.
He admired the effortless charm of every Parisienne he met during his trip.
The film portrays a glamorous Parisienne navigating the world of high fashion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Parisian - a native or resident of Paris (gender-neutral or masculine)
