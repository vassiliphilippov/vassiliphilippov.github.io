Leguminosae

noun
the legume plant family
Pronunciation: / lɪˌɡjuməˈnosi /; / lɪˌɡjuːməˈnoʊsiː / (AmE), / lɪˌɡjuːmɪˈnəʊsiː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants, including beans, peas, and related species, that have pods containing seeds
