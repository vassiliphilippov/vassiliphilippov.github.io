residue

noun
small amount left after removal
4.1 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈre.zə.duː/ /; / /ˈre.zɪ.duː/ (AmE), /ˈre.zɪ.djuː/ (BrE)
a small amount of something that remains after the main part has been removed or used

Examples

A residue of salt
After the water evaporated, a thin residue of salt was left on the surface.
He swept away the residue of dust from the floor before mopping.
The cleaning process left a sticky residue on the windows.
After the water evaporated, a thin residue of salt was left behind on the surface.
The chemist analyzed the residue remaining in the test tube.
There was a sticky residue on the table after the juice spilled.
