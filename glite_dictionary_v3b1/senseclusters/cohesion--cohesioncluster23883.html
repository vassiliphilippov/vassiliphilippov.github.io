cohesion

noun
the state of sticking together
2.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhiʒən / (AmE), / kəʊˈhiːʒən / (BrE)
the state of sticking together or forming a united whole

Examples

Social cohesion
Social cohesion is vital for the stability of any community.
The cohesion of the team was evident in their flawless performance.
Water molecules display strong cohesion, which explains surface tension.
