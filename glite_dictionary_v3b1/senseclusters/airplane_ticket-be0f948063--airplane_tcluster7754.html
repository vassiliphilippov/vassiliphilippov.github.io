airplane ticket

noun
ticket for travel by airplane
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌpleɪn ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / ˈeəˌpleɪn ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a ticket entitling the holder to travel on a specific flight or flights by airplane

Examples

An airplane ticket booking

The price of an airplane ticket can vary depending on the season and destination.
