ticker tape

noun
strip of paper for stock quotes
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkər teɪp / (AmE), / ˈtɪkə teɪp / (BrE)
a continuous narrow strip of paper on which stock prices and other financial information were printed and transmitted by a telegraphic machine

Examples

Old ticker tape machine
Before computers, investors relied on ticker tape to get the latest share prices.
In old movies, you can see stockbrokers reading the ticker tape as it rolls out of the machine.
The ticker tape scattered on the street after the parade made for an iconic scene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stock ticker - a telegraphic machine that prints stock prices on tape
  • synonym: ticker-tape parade - a festive parade during which ticker tape is thrown from buildings
