electric range

noun
kitchen stove powered by electricity
1.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪk reɪndʒ / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪk reɪndʒ / (BrE)
A kitchen stove in which heat for cooking is generated by electricity rather than gas or other fuels.

Examples

An electric range stove
She prefers using an electric range because it's easier to clean.
The electric range in her apartment heats up much faster than her previous gas stove.
We decided to replace our old stove with a new electric range for better efficiency.
