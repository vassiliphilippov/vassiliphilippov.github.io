clunker

noun
old, unreliable car
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈklʌŋkə / (BrE)
an old, worn-out, or unreliable vehicle, especially a car

Examples

An old clunker car
He drove his old clunker to work every day, hoping it would not break down.
After years of repairs, they finally decided to replace their clunker with a newer model.
The mechanic laughed when he saw the clunker I brought into the shop.
He finally traded in his old clunker for a newer, more reliable car.
The clunker broke down again on the way to the grocery store.
Despite its age, the clunker managed to get us through the snowstorm.

Related Terms

  • antonym: classic car - an old car in good condition, often valued for its age
  • synonym: jalopy - an old, dilapidated car
  • synonym: lemon - a car that is defective or constantly breaks down
