flyweight

noun
weight class for lightest fighters in sports
7.0 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈflaɪˌweɪt / (BrE)
the lightest weight division in boxing and similar sports, or a competitor in that division (usually around 112 pounds or 51 kg)
