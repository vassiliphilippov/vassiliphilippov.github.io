spoon

noun
utensil for eating or serving food
1.0 10/m
Pronunciation: / spun / (AmE), / spuÀên / (BrE)
A piece of cutlery consisting of a small shallow bowl with a handle, used for eating, stirring, or serving food.

Examples

A spoon for soup
He stirred his tea with a small spoon and added a little more sugar.
Please use a spoon to serve the soup into the bowls.
The baby tried to eat by herself but dropped her spoon on the floor.
