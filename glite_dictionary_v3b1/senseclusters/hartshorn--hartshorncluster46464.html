hartshorn

noun
deer antler; ammonia source
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtsˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtsˌhɔːn / (BrE)
an old term for the antler of a deer, or a substance made from it, especially used as a source of ammonia in baking or medicine

Examples

Hartshorn powder
In the past, bakers used hartshorn to help bread rise before modern baking powder was available.
The apothecary prepared smelling salts using hartshorn as the main ingredient.
A hunter displayed a large hartshorn above the fireplace as a trophy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baking powder - a leavening agent used in baking
