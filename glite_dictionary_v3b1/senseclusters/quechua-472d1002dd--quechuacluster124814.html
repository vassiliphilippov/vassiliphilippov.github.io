Quechua

noun
member of Peruvian Indigenous Andean people
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈke.tʃ.wə/ /; / /ˈke.tʃ.ə.wə/ (AmE), /ˈke.tʃ.wə/ /; / /ˈke.tʃ.ə.wə/ (BrE)
