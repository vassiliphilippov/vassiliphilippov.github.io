detoxifier

noun
removes toxins
Pronunciation: / diˈtɑksɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / diˈtɒksɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a substance or device that removes toxins or harmful substances from a system or body

Examples

A detoxifier supplement
Many people use a detoxifier to help cleanse their bodies after a period of unhealthy eating.
The water filter acts as a detoxifier by removing harmful chemicals from tap water.
Doctors sometimes administer a detoxifier in cases of poisoning to neutralize toxins.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contaminant - a substance that makes something impure or harmful
  • synonym: purifier - a device or substance that cleans or removes impurities
