psychiatrist

noun
doctor specializing in mental health
2.6 4.2
Pronunciation: / saɪˈkaɪəˌtrɪst / (AmE), / saɪˈkaɪətrɪst / (BrE)

The psychiatrist prescribed him medication to help manage his symptoms.
