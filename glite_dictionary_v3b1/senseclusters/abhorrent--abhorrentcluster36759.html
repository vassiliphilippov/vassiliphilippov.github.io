abhorrent

adjective
causing strong dislike or disgust
8.8
Pronunciation: / əbˈhɔːrənt / (AmE), / əbˈhɒrənt / (BrE)
causing a strong feeling of dislike or disgust; morally offensive or repugnant

Examples

Abhorrent behaviour
Animal cruelty is abhorrent to most people around the world.
She refused to participate in any abhorrent practices, no matter the cost.
The community found the politician’s remarks utterly abhorrent.
