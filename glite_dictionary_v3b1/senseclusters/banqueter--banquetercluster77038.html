banqueter

noun
banquet organizer
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkwətər / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkwɪtə / (BrE)
a person who gives, organizes, or hosts a banquet or formal meal

Examples

The banqueter hosts guests
The banqueter greeted each guest personally as they arrived at the grand hall.
As the main banqueter, he ensured the evening ran smoothly and everyone enjoyed the meal.
She became a skilled banqueter after organizing several large family celebrations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: guest - a person invited to an event or place
  • synonym: host - a person who receives or entertains guests
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates an event
