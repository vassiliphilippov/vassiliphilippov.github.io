namby-pamby

noun
weak or sentimental person
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnæmbi ˌpæmbi / (AmE), / ˈnambi ˌpambi / (BrE)
a person who is weak, feeble, or excessively sentimental

Examples

A namby-pamby person
No one respected him because they thought he was just a namby-pamby who couldn't make tough decisions.
She refused to be a namby-pamby and stood her ground during the debate.
The coach warned the players that there was no room for namby-pamby attitudes on the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sentimentalist - someone who is excessively sentimental
  • antonym: tough guy - a person who is strong or unyielding
  • synonym: weakling - a weak or feeble person
