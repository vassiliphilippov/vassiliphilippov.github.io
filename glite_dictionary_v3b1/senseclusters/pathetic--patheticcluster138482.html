Synonyms

  • pitiful
    3.9 2.27/m
    pitiful describes something so weak or poor that people feel scornful pity, while pathetic shows sadness mixed with contempt.
  • pitiable
    6.1 0.42/m
    pitiable means deserving sympathy for suffering or need, while pathetic often adds a sense of weakness and contempt.
  • piteous
    7.9 0.20/m
    piteous evokes strong sorrow or pity for clear suffering, while pathetic also implies weakness and sometimes contempt.
