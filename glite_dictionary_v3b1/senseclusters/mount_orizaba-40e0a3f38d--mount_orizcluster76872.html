Mount Orizaba

proper noun
highest mountain in Mexico
Pronunciation: / maʊnt ɔːrɪˈzɑːbə / (AmE), / maʊnt ɒrɪˈzɑːbə / (BrE)
the highest peak in Mexico, an extinct volcano located between Mexico City and Veracruz in southern Mexico
