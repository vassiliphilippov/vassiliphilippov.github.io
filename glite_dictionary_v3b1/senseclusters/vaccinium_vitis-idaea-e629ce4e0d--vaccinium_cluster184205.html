Vaccinium vitis-idaea

noun
shrub with edible red berries (lingonberry)
9.9
Pronunciation: / vækˈsɪniəm ˈvaɪtɪs ɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / vækˈsɪniəm ˈvɪtɪs ɪˈdɛə / (BrE)
A low evergreen shrub native to high northern temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North America, producing red edible berries; commonly known as lingonberry.
