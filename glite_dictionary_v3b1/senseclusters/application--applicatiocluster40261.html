application

noun
liquid medicine applied to skin
Pronunciation: / ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌaplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a liquid preparation applied to the skin for its soothing, antiseptic, or medicinal effects

Examples

Skin application
After sunburn, apply the application to the affected area for relief.
The dermatologist recommended a gentle application for treating the rash on my arm.
This soothing application should be used twice daily for best results.
