bigmouthed

adjective
talking too much; indiscreet
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌmaʊθt / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌmaʊθt / (BrE)
(of a person) talking too much or unable to keep secrets, especially in an indiscreet or tactless way.

Examples

A bigmouthed person
He's so bigmouthed that he blurted out the news before anyone else had a chance.
My bigmouthed cousin told everyone about the surprise party, so it wasn't a secret for long.
You can't trust a bigmouthed colleague with confidential information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossipy - tending to spread rumors or talk about people
  • antonym: tight-lipped - unwilling to talk or reveal information
