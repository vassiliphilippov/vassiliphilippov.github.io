Christmas graduate

noun
graduate who finishes in December
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsməs ˈɡrædʒuət / (AmE), / ˈkrɪsməs ˈɡrædʒuət / (BrE)
a student who completes the requirements for graduation and officially graduates in December, rather than during the typical spring commencement

Examples

A Christmas graduate
As a Christmas graduate, he entered the job market before many of his classmates.
She was proud to be a Christmas graduate, finishing her degree a semester earlier than most.
The university held a special ceremony for all the Christmas graduates this year.
