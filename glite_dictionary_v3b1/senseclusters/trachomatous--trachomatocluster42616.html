trachomatous

adjective
relating to trachoma
9.9
Pronunciation: / trəˈkoʊmətəs / (AmE), / trəˈkəʊmətəs / (BrE)
affected by or relating to trachoma, a contagious disease of the eye

Examples

trachomatous eye disease
The doctor noted trachomatous scarring on the patient's eyelid.
Villages with poor sanitation often have a higher rate of trachomatous infections.
Early treatment can prevent trachomatous blindness in affected communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trachoma - a contagious bacterial infection of the eye
