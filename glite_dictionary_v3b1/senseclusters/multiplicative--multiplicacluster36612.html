multiplicative

adjective
involving multiplication
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈplɪkətɪv / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈplɪkətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving multiplication or the process of multiplying numbers or quantities

Examples

Multiplicative identity
The multiplicative identity is the number one, because any number times one equals itself.
In mathematics, a multiplicative function is one that preserves multiplication.
The multiplicative property of zero states that any number times zero is zero.

Related Terms

  • antonym: additive - relating to or involving addition
