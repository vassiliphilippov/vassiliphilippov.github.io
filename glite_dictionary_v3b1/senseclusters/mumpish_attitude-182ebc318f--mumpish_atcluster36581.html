mumpish attitude

noun
sullen or moody demeanor
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌmpɪʃ ˈætɪˌtjud / (AmE), / ˈmʌmpɪʃ ˈætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a sullen or moody demeanor; behaving in a gloomy or sulky way

Examples

A mumpish attitude today
His mumpish attitude ruined the fun for everyone.
She responded to the criticism with a mumpish attitude, refusing to speak for the rest of the day.
The team's mumpish attitude was evident after their disappointing loss.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sulky - bad-tempered or resentful; refusing to be cheerful
