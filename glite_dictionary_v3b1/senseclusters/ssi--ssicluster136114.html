ssi

noun
Korean honorific title
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃi / (AmE), / ʃi / (BrE)
a Korean honorific used after a person's name to address them politely, similar to 'Mr.' or 'Ms.'

Examples

ssi after name
In Korean culture, it is respectful to add ssi after someone's name when speaking to them.
