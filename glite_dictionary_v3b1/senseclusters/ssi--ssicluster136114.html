ssi

noun
Korean honorific title
Pronunciation: / ʃi / (AmE), / ʃi / (BrE)
a Korean honorific used after a person's name to address them politely, similar to 'Mr.' or 'Ms.'

Examples

ssi after name
In Korean culture, it is respectful to add ssi after someone's name when speaking to them.
She addressed her colleague as Minho ssi during the meeting.
When meeting someone new, Koreans often use ssi to show politeness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Mr. - a polite title used before a man's surname or full name
  • synonym: Ms. - a polite title used before a woman's surname or full name
