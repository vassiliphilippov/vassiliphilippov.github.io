Vatican City

proper noun
smallest independent city-state, papal residence
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈvæt.ɪ.kən ˈsɪt.iː/ /; / /ˈvæt.ɪ.kən ˈsɪt.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈvæt.ɪ.kən ˈsɪt.ɪ/ /; / /ˈvæt.ɪ.kən ˈsɪt.iː/ (BrE)
