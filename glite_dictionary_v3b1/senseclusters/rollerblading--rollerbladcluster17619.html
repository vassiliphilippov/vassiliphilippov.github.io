rollerblading

noun
skating with inline skates
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlərˌbleɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊləˌbleɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of skating using inline skates, especially those of the Rollerblade brand

Examples

Rollerblading along beach
I love rollerblading along the beach promenade on sunny days.
Many parks now have special paths designed for rollerblading and cycling.
She went rollerblading with her friends after school yesterday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inline skating - the activity of skating with inline skates
  • degree lower: rollerskating - the activity of skating while wearing shoes with wheels (quad skates)
