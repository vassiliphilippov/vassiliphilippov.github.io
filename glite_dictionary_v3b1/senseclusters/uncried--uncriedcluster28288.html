uncried

adjective
not announced aloud
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkraɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkraɪd / (BrE)
not called out, announced, or proclaimed aloud.

Examples

Uncried winner
The winner's name remained uncried as the ceremony ended in silence.
Through the empty streets, the warning was left uncried and unheard.
His achievements went uncried despite their importance to the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: proclaimed - announced officially or publicly
  • synonym: unannounced - not made known or declared publicly
