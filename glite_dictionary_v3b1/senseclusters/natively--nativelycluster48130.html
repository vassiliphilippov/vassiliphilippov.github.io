natively

adverb
directly supported
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈneɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that is directly supported by the operating system or hardware, without the need for emulation or translation.

Examples

Runs natively
The software runs natively on both Windows and Mac without any compatibility issues.
This application is designed to work natively with the latest smartphone hardware.
You can play the video natively in your browser without installing extra plugins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: directly - without any intermediary or extra layer
  • antonym: emulated - run through imitation rather than direct support
