desexualization

noun
removal of sexual traits
Pronunciation: / diˌsɛkʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌsɛksjʊəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of removing or reducing sexual characteristics, associations, or connotations from something or someone

Examples

Desexualization of images
The desexualization of advertising images can make them more suitable for all audiences.
Some activists advocate for the desexualization of school dress codes to promote equality.
The desexualization of certain characters in media has sparked debate among fans.
