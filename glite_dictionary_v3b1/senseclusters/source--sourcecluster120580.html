source

verb
specify the origin of
7.4
Pronunciation: / sɔrs / (AmE), / sɔːs / (BrE)
to specify or state the origin of something, especially information or material

Examples

To source information
Editors demand that reporters source all their claims before publication.
Every statistic in the article was carefully sourced to reputable journals.
She sourced most of her material from historical archives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cite - to quote or refer to as proof or authority
  • synonym: reference - to mention or allude to as a source of information
