Synonyms

  • confer with
    4.5 1/m
    confer with is used to discuss something directly with another person or group before deciding, while confer is more formal.
  • talk over
    3.2 3/m
    talk over means discussing something carefully with someone before making a choice, while confer sounds more formal and official.
  • discuss
    1.6 150/m
    discuss means talking about details with others to explore ideas, while confer often implies a formal consultation to decide.
  • consult
    3.5 14/m
    consult means asking an expert or source for advice before acting, while confer means jointly discussing matters to decide.
  • confabulate
    8.8 0.00/m
    confabulate is a rare or literary word for chatting or discussing, while confer is a common formal word for consulting.
