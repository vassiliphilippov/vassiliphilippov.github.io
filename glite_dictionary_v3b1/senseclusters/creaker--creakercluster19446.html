creaker

noun
thing that creaks
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːkər / (AmE), / ˈkriːkə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes a creaking sound, especially repeatedly or noticeably

Examples

A creaker chair
The old wooden chair was a notorious creaker in the quiet library.
Every time the wind blew, the gate became a loud creaker in the backyard.
He was known as the creaker because his shoes always made noise in the hallway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creak - a harsh, high-pitched sound made when something moves or is moved
  • synonym: squeaker - something that makes a squeaking sound
