carefree

adjective
free from worry or responsibility
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛrˌfri / (AmE), / ˈkeəˌfriː / (BrE)
free from anxiety, worry, or responsibility; not troubled by problems or cares

Examples

A carefree life
Despite the looming deadline, she remained carefree and relaxed.
He spent his childhood in a carefree village, running through the fields.
Their carefree laughter echoed down the beach as the sun set.
As a child, he enjoyed a carefree existence, playing outdoors all day.
She smiled with a carefree attitude, unconcerned about tomorrow's challenges.
Their vacation in the mountains was wonderfully carefree and relaxing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxious - experiencing worry, unease, or nervousness
  • synonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • antonym: responsible - having an obligation to do something or having control over someone
