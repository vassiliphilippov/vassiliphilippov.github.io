detailer

noun
technical drawing specialist
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːteɪlər / (AmE), / ˈdiːteɪlə / (BrE)
a person who prepares detailed technical drawings or plans, especially for engineering or construction projects

Examples

Engineering detailer
The detailer spent hours ensuring every measurement on the blueprint was accurate.
As a steel detailer, he created precise plans for the construction team.
Each detailer must understand engineering standards to produce correct technical drawings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drafter - a person who makes detailed technical drawings
  • synonym: engineer - a person who designs and builds complex products or structures
