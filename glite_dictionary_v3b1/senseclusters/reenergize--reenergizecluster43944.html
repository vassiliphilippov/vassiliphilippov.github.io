reenergize

verb
to restore power
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛnərˌdʒaɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛnədʒaɪz / (BrE)
to restore electrical power or energy to a system, circuit, or device after it has been turned off or interrupted

Examples

To reenergize the system
After the maintenance was finished, the engineers reenergized the power grid to restore electricity to the area.
It is important to check all safety procedures before you reenergize the equipment.
The technician will reenergize the circuit once all repairs are complete and the area is clear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deactivate - to make something inactive or turn it off
  • synonym: reactivate - to make something active again
