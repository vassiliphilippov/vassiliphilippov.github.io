Norinyl

noun
a brand of oral contraceptive
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːrɪˌnɪl / (AmE), / ˈnɔːrɪˌnɪl / (BrE)
A trade name for an oral contraceptive containing norethindrone and mestranol, used to prevent pregnancy.
