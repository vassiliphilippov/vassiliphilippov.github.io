nameplate

noun
plate showing a name for identification
6.2 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈneɪm.pleɪt/ (AmE), /ˈneɪm.pleɪt/ (BrE)
A flat, usually rectangular piece of metal, plastic, or other material that displays a person's name or the name of a company, typically fixed to a door, desk, or machine for identification.
