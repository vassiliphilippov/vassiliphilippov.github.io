sell dearly

verb
pay a high price; suffer serious consequences
Pronunciation: / sɛl ˈdɪrli / (AmE), / sɛl ˈdɪəli / (BrE)
To pay a high price for something or suffer significant consequences, especially as a result of one's actions.

Examples

To sell dearly for mistake

She realized too late that she would sell dearly for ignoring the warnings.
They sold dearly for their complacency when the real consequences struck.
