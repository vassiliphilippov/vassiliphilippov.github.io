rallier

noun
person who unites others
Pronunciation: / ˈræliər / (AmE), / ˈræliə / (BrE)
a person who gathers or unites others for a cause, event, or purpose

Examples

Rallier of supporters
The rallier inspired hundreds of people to join the environmental movement.
As a skilled rallier, she brought together volunteers from across the city.
Every successful campaign needs at least one passionate rallier to motivate others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and follows a leader or cause
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
  • synonym: organizer - a person who arranges or coordinates an event or group
