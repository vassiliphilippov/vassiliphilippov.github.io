kerykeion

noun
herald's staff
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈrɪk.iː.ən/ /; / /e.ˈrɪk.iː.ən/ (AmE), /kə.ˈrɪk.iː.ən/ /; / /e.ˈrɪk.iː.ən/ (BrE)
a staff carried by heralds or messengers in ancient Greece, especially by Hermes, as a symbol of authority and communication (also called the caduceus, often shown with two snakes and wings)
