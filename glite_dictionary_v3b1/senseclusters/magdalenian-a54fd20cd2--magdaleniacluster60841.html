Magdalenian

adjective
Upper Paleolithic culture
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡdəˈleɪniən / (AmE), / ˌmæɡdəˈliːniən / (BrE)
relating to the late Upper Paleolithic culture in Europe known for advanced art and tools, especially cave paintings

Examples

Magdalenian cave art
The Magdalenian period is famous for its detailed cave paintings and carved bone tools.
Archaeologists discovered Magdalenian artifacts in the caves of southern France.
The Magdalenian culture marked a peak in prehistoric European art and craftsmanship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Aurignacian - relating to an earlier Upper Paleolithic culture in Europe
  • antonym: Solutrean - relating to a preceding Upper Paleolithic culture in Europe
