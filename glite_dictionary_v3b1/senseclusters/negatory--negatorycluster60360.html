negatory

noun
informal refusal
8.6
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡeɪtɔri / (AmE), / nɪˈɡeɪtəri / (BrE)
an informal way of saying no or giving a negative response or refusal

Examples

A quick negatory
When asked if he wanted dessert, he gave a quick negatory and pushed his plate away.
The team leader responded with a firm negatory to the request for extra time.
She expected permission but got a quick negatory from the manager.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affirmative - a positive answer or agreement
  • synonym: no - a negative answer or refusal
