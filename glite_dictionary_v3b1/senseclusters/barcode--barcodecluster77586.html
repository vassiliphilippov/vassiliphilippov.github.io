barcode

verb
to label with barcode
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌkoʊd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkəʊd / (BrE)
to attach a barcode label to an item so it can be identified or scanned electronically

Examples

To barcode a book
The librarian will barcode each new book before adding it to the collection.
All products in the warehouse must be barcoded for inventory tracking.
Before shipping, the staff barcodes every package to ensure accurate delivery.
