metallurgy

noun
science and technology of metals
Pronunciation: / məˈtælərdʒi / (AmE), / məˈtalədʒi / (BrE)
The science and technology concerned with the properties of metals and their production and purification.

Examples

Modern metallurgy science
Modern metallurgy has led to the development of new alloys with remarkable properties.
She decided to study metallurgy because she was fascinated by how metals are formed and processed.
The conference focused on advances in environmental aspects of metallurgy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metallurgist - a person who is an expert in metallurgy
