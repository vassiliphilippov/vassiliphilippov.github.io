trunk

noun
main part of the body, without head or limbs
Pronunciation: / trʌŋk / (AmE), / trʌŋk / (BrE)
the main part of the body of a human or animal, excluding the head, neck, and limbs.

Examples

Body trunk
In anatomy class, we studied the muscles of the trunk in detail.
Protective gear is designed to shield the trunk from injury during contact sports.
The athlete has to strengthen his trunk to improve balance and stability.
