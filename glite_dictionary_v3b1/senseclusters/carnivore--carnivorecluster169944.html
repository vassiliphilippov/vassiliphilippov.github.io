carnivore

noun
person on an all‑meat diet
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.nɪ.vɔːr/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.nɪ.vɔː/ (BrE)
A person who follows a carnivore diet, eating mainly or only animal products (meat, fish, and animal fats) and avoiding plant foods.

Examples

Carnivore diet follower
He identifies as a carnivore and follows an all‑meat diet for health reasons.
Many carnivores report higher energy after removing grains and sugar from meals.
The doctor warned that being a strict carnivore may lead to deficiencies without careful planning.
