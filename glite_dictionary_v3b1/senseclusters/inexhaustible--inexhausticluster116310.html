inexhaustible

adjective
impossible to use up completely
Pronunciation: / ɪnɪɡˈzɔstəbəl /; / ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔstəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbl /; / ˌɪnɪɡˈzɔːstəbl / (BrE)
impossible to use up completely; unlimited in supply or capacity

Examples

Inexhaustible energy
His energy for solving problems appeared inexhaustible during the long negotiation.
The ocean seemed to provide an inexhaustible source of food for the community.
There is an inexhaustible supply of creativity among the team members.
She seemed to have an inexhaustible supply of interesting stories.
The sun is an inexhaustible source of energy for our planet.
The team's inexhaustible enthusiasm inspired everyone in the company.
