scaloppine

noun
Italian meat dish
Pronunciation: / skæləˈpiːni / (AmE), / skæləˈpiːneɪ / (BrE)
thin slices of meat, usually veal or chicken, cooked in a sauce, especially in Italian cuisine

Examples

Lemon scaloppine
The chef recommended the lemon scaloppine as the house specialty.
For dinner, she prepared chicken scaloppine with a creamy mushroom sauce.
We enjoyed delicious veal scaloppine at the new Italian restaurant downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: escalope - a thin slice of meat, often breaded and fried
  • synonym: schnitzel - a thin, breaded and fried slice of meat
