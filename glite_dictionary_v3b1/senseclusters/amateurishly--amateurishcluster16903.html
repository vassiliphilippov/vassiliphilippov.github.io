amateurishly

adverb
in an unskilled or amateurish way
9.9 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæm.ə.tər.ɪʃ.liː/ /; / /ˈæm.ə.tʃər.ɪʃ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈæm.ə.tə.rɪʃ.liː/ /; / /ˈæm.ə.tʃə.rɪʃ.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that lacks professional skill, polish, or competence; like an amateur

Examples

To speak amateurishly
His speech was delivered amateurishly, with many pauses and awkward phrasing.
She painted the scenery amateurishly, but her enthusiasm was clear.
The team played amateurishly during the tournament and lost every match.
