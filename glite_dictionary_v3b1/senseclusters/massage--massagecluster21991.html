massage

noun
rubbing and kneading of the body for relaxation or therapy
Pronunciation: / məˈsɑʒ / (AmE), / məˈsɑːʒ / (BrE)
the act of rubbing and kneading parts of the body, especially to relax the muscles or increase circulation

Examples

A relaxing massage
After a long day at the office, she booked a massage to help relax her muscles.
Regular massage can help relieve stress and improve circulation.
The spa offers a variety of massage techniques, including deep tissue and hot stone.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: caress - to touch or stroke gently, sometimes used similarly but generally less therapeutic
  • synonym: rubdown - an act of briskly rubbing the body, often with massage
