sellout

noun
event with all tickets sold
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlˌaʊt /; / ˈsɛl.aʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɛl.aʊt / (BrE)
an event or occasion where all tickets or items are sold out

Examples

A concert sellout
Every show on the tour has been a sellout so far this year.
The band's performance was a complete sellout, with thousands in attendance.
Tickets went so quickly that the event became a sellout within hours.
Due to high demand, the product was a complete sellout just hours after launch.
Retailers are preparing for a possible sellout of the new smartphone on release day.
The concert was a sellout within minutes of tickets going on sale.

  • synonym: sell-out - an event for which all tickets have been sold
