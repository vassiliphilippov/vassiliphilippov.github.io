Synonyms

  • spend
    1.0 293/m
    spend means using time on a specific activity, while pass often means time goes by without focus.
  • go by
    1.0 9.91/m
    go by is the casual phrase for time moving forward, while pass is the general verb for time or things moving.
  • elapse
    5.1 2.27/m
    elapse describes time moving on in a quiet, regular way, while pass is more common and used in many contexts.
  • slip by
    3.8 0.64/m
    slip by means time moves quickly or unnoticed, while pass is neutral and does not imply speed or notice.
  • lapse
    4.0 0.11/m
    lapse often means time or chance fades or expires, while pass is a broader word for time moving or being spent.
