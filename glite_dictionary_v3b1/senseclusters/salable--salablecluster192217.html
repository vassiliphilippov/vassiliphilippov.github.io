salable

adjective
able to be sold
4.8 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈseɪ.lə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈseɪ.lə.bəl/ (BrE)
Able to be sold; suitable or fit for sale.

Examples

A salable product
Her paintings became highly salable after her first major exhibition.
Only salable items should be displayed on the shelves.
The store had to remove products that were not salable due to damage.
After extensive repairs, the old house finally became saleable.
Most of the artwork in the gallery is saleable at reasonable prices.
The farmer sorted the apples into saleable and unsaleable batches before going to market.
