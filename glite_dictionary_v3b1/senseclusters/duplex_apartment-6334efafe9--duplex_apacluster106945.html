duplex apartment

noun
apartment with rooms on two floors
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌplɛks əˈpɑːrtmənt / (AmE), / ˈdjuːˌplɛks əˈpɑːtmənt / (BrE)
An apartment consisting of rooms on two floors connected by an internal staircase.

Examples

A duplex apartment with stairs
A spiral staircase is the highlight of their stylish duplex apartment.
Her duplex apartment features a living area downstairs and bedrooms upstairs.
They decided to rent a duplex apartment to have more space for their family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maisonette - small apartment on two floors, often with its own entrance
