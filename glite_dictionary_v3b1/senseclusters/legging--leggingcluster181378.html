legging

noun
close-fitting garment for the leg
9.0 1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɛɡɪŋ / (BrE)
a tight, close-fitting garment that covers the leg, usually stretchy and worn for comfort, exercise, or fashion

Examples

A legging garment
He wore a single legging to protect his injured leg during the hike.
The knight strapped on a leather legging before riding into battle.
When the weather turned cold, she added a thick woolen legging over her stockings.
She bought a new pair of black leggings for her yoga class.
Many people wear leggings for both exercise and casual outings.
The leggings were made of a soft, stretchy fabric that was comfortable all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leggings - tight-fitting stretch pants covering the legs, usually worn by women
  • synonym: pants - an outer garment covering each leg separately, usually from the waist to the ankle
  • synonym: tights - close-fitting legwear covering the legs and sometimes the waist
