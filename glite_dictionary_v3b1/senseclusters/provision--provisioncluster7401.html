provision

noun
supplying or providing something
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒən / (BrE)
the act of supplying or providing something, especially things that are needed

Examples

Government provision of aid
Government provision of public services has increased in recent years.
The provision of clean drinking water is essential for the community's health.
They ensured the provision of enough food and blankets for the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supply - to provide something that is needed or wanted
  • antonym: withhold - to refuse to give something that is needed or requested
