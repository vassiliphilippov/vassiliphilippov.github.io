unteachable

adjective
impossible to teach
5.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈtiː.tʃə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈtiː.tʃə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈtiː.tʃə.bəl/ /; / /ən.ˈtiː.tʃə.bəl/ (BrE)
impossible to teach or unable to be taught, especially referring to a subject, skill, or method.

Examples

Unteachable skills
Some abstract techniques are simply unteachable, relying entirely on innate talent rather than classroom instruction.
He found the advanced mathematics course almost unteachable due to its complexity.
Certain life skills seem unteachable and must be learned through personal experience.
