fund-raise

verb
raise money for a cause or project
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndˌreɪz / (AmE), / ˈfʌndˌreɪz / (BrE)
to collect money for a particular purpose, cause, or project, usually by organizing events or seeking donations

Examples

To fund-raise money
Each year, the organization fund-raises to support local homeless shelters.
She has been fund-raising for cancer research since her mother was diagnosed.
They fund-raised enough money to build a new playground in the community.
