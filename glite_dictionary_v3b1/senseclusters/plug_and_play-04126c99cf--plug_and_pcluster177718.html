plug and play

adjective
easy to use without configuration
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ ən pleɪ / (AmE), / plʌɡ ənd pleɪ / (BrE)
(of hardware or software) able to function immediately after being connected or installed, without requiring manual configuration.

Examples

Plug and play device
Modern game consoles are mostly plug and play, requiring little more than connecting the power and HDMI cables.
The company advertises their software as plug and play for quick deployment in any business.
This printer is plug and play, so you don't need to install any drivers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ready-to-use - available for immediate use, requiring no setup
  • synonym: user-friendly - easy to use or understand, especially for non-experts
