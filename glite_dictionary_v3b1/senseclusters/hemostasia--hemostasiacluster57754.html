hemostasia

noun
surgical stopping of blood flow
5.5
Pronunciation: / hiːməˈsteɪziə / (AmE), / hiːməˈsteɪzɪə / (BrE)
The surgical procedure of stopping the flow of blood, typically using medical tools such as a hemostat.
