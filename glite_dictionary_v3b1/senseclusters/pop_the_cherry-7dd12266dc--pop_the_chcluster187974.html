pop the cherry

verb
do something for the first time (idiom, informal)
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑp ðə ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / pɒp ðə ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
(informal, sometimes vulgar) To do something for the first time, especially to use or experience something new; originally referring to a person's first sexual experience.

Examples

To pop the cherry first time
He finally got to pop the cherry by playing his new guitar at the concert.
She was excited to pop the cherry at her first art exhibition.
When you pop the cherry on a new project, there's always a learning curve.
