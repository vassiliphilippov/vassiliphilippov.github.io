yon

adverb
at or in a distant place (archaic)
Pronunciation: / jɑn / (AmE), / jɒn / (BrE)
At or in that (usually distant) place; over there. Now chiefly archaic or dialectal.

Examples

Over yon hill
Look yon, where the river bends out of sight.
The travelers wandered here and yon in search of shelter.
We could see the mountain rising high yon beyond the fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yonder - at or in that indicated place, over there
