blue-lilac

adjective
lavender tinged with blue
5.2
Pronunciation: / bluː ˈlaɪlæk / (AmE), / bluː ˈlaɪlæk / (BrE)
(of color) having the appearance of lavender with a tinge of blue; a shade between blue and lilac.

Examples

A blue-lilac shade
Artists often use blue-lilac tones to evoke a dreamy, serene mood in their paintings.
Her dress shimmered in the afternoon light, showcasing a delicate blue-lilac hue.
The bedroom walls were painted a soft blue-lilac that created a tranquil atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lavender - a pale bluish-purple color
  • degree lower: lilac - a pale purple color
