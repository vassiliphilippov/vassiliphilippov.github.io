market gardening

noun
growing vegetables or flowers for sale
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkɪt ˌɡɑrdn̩ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˌɡɑːdnɪŋ / (BrE)
the cultivation of vegetables, fruits, or flowers for sale in a local market rather than for personal consumption or large-scale distribution

Examples

Market gardening vegetables
He studied market gardening before starting his own vegetable farm.
Many families make a living through market gardening in rural areas.
The rise of urban market gardening helps provide fresh produce to local communities.
