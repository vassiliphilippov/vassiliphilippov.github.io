zoner

noun
zone manager
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈzəʊnə / (BrE)
a person or device that creates, manages, or divides zones, especially in urban planning or administration

Examples

City zoner
The city hired a zoner to help with the new urban development project.
A zoner can automate the process of dividing land for different uses.
After the meeting, the zoner adjusted the boundaries to include more green space.
The city hired a new zoner to oversee the district boundaries.
As a zoner, she was responsible for ensuring each area met safety regulations.
Many zoners attended the meeting to discuss changes in their neighborhood.
