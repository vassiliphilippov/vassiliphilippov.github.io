otolith

noun
ear stone in fish
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊtəˌlɪθ / (AmE), / ˈəʊtəˌlɪθ / (BrE)
a small, hard structure in the inner ear of fish or other vertebrates that helps with balance and hearing, and can be used to estimate age or identify species
