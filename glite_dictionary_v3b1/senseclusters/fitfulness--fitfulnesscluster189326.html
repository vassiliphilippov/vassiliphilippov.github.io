fitfulness

noun
irregular or spasmodic quality
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪtfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɪtfʊlnɪs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being irregular, spasmodic, or lacking steadiness.

Examples

The fitfulness of sleep
Her writing showed a fitfulness that sometimes frustrated her editor.
Periods of fitfulness in the weather made planning the picnic difficult.
The fitfulness of his sleep left him feeling exhausted in the morning.
