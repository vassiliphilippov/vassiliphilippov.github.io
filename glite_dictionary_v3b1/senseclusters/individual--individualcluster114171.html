individual

adjective
single; relating to one person or thing
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl /; / ˌɪndəˈvɪdʒuəl / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl /; / ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjʊəl / (BrE)
relating to or intended for one person or thing, not a group

Examples

She prepared individual packages for each team member.
The hotel only offers individual rooms, not shared accommodation.
