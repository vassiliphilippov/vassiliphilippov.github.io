croucher

noun
one who crouches
7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkraʊ.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈkraʊ.tʃə/ (BrE)
a person or animal that crouches or stays low to the ground, especially to move quietly or avoid being seen

Examples

A croucher in grass
The cat was a skilled croucher when stalking birds in the garden.
As a goalkeeper, he became known as a quick croucher ready to dive for the ball.
In the wild, a croucher can avoid predators by staying low and moving quietly.
