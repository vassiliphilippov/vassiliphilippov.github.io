welcomingly

adverb
in a friendly manner
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋli / (BrE)
in a friendly and inviting way that makes others feel accepted or comfortable

Examples

The staff smiled welcomingly as we entered the restaurant for dinner.
The host greeted each guest welcomingly, making everyone feel at home.
She smiled welcomingly as the new students entered the classroom.
The community responded welcomingly to the changes proposed by the council.
