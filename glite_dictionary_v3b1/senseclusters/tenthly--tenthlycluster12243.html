tenthly

adverb
in the tenth place (in a list or discussion)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnθli / (AmE), / ˈtɛnθli / (BrE)
Used to introduce the tenth item in a series of points or arguments.

Examples

Tenthly, in order
Tenthly, we must ensure that all safety protocols are strictly followed at all times.
She explained her points clearly, and tenthly addressed the need for more research.
The instructions continued, and tenthly, we were told to double-check all measurements.
