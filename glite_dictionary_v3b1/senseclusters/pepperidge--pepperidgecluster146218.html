pepperidge

noun
a North American deciduous tree with striking fall color
9.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈpɛpərɪdʒ / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to eastern North America, known for its columnar shape, horizontal limbs, small leaves that appear late in spring, and brilliant autumn foliage; also called the black gum or tupelo.
