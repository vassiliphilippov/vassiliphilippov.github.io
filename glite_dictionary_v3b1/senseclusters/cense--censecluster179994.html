cense

verb
to perfume with incense
7.4
Pronunciation: / sɛns / (AmE), / sɛns / (BrE)
to perfume or fumigate with incense, especially by using a censer, often in a religious ceremony

Examples

To cense sacred icons
As the ceremony started, the deacon censed the sacred icons, filling the church with fragrance.
Monks silently censed the chapel before the congregation entered.
The priest began to cense the altar with sweet-smelling incense during the evening service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incense - a substance burned for its fragrant smoke, or the act of burning it; to perfume with incense
