board

noun
printed circuit for computer expansion
5.4 11/m
Pronunciation: / b…îrd / (AmE), / b…îÀêd / (BrE)
an electrical or electronic panel, such as a circuit board or control board, used in computers or to control devices

Examples

Circuit board
Technicians replaced the faulty network board to restore connectivity.
The graphics board allows this computer to run high-resolution games smoothly.
You can upgrade your system by adding a new sound board.
Each section of the control room had its own board to monitor different systems.
Suddenly the board was illuminated with warning lights, indicating an emergency situation.
The engineer checked the board for any faulty connections before starting the machine.

Synonyms

  • circuit board
    3.7 1.13/m
    circuit board holds electronic parts and connects circuits inside devices, while board means an electronic panel used to control or connect systems.
  • control board
    4.1 0.45/m
    control board has switches and meters to operate machines, while board refers broadly to any electrical or electronic panel.
  • circuit card
    4.4 0.01/m
    circuit card plugs into a slot to add functions in a computer, while board is any electronic panel used in devices.
  • control panel
    3.4 1.59/m
    control panel is the user-facing surface with buttons and dials to operate equipment, while board is a general electronic panel inside devices.

  • synonym: panel - a flat board with instruments or controls
