board

noun
printed circuit for computer expansion
Pronunciation: / bɔrd / (AmE), / bɔːd / (BrE)
an electrical or electronic panel, such as a circuit board or control board, used in computers or to control devices

Examples

Circuit board
Technicians replaced the faulty network board to restore connectivity.
The graphics board allows this computer to run high-resolution games smoothly.
You can upgrade your system by adding a new sound board.
Each section of the control room had its own board to monitor different systems.
Suddenly the board was illuminated with warning lights, indicating an emergency situation.
The engineer checked the board for any faulty connections before starting the machine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expansion card - a printed circuit board inserted into a computer to provide added features
  • synonym: panel - a flat board with instruments or controls
