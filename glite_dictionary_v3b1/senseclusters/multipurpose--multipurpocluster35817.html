multipurpose

adjective
having multiple uses; adaptable
3.4 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / Àåm åltiÀàp…úÀêrp…ôs / (AmE), / Àåm ålt…™Ààp…úÀêp…ôs / (BrE)
Designed or suitable for use in several different ways or for several purposes.

Examples

A multipurpose cleaner
She bought a multipurpose cleaner that works on glass, tiles, and metal surfaces.
The community center is a multipurpose building used for meetings, sports, and local events.
This multipurpose gadget can open bottles, cut wires, and even has a mini flashlight.

Synonyms

  • multifunctional
    3.7 0.00/m
    multifunctional is a product that performs several separate functions, while multipurpose describes an item made for different uses.
  • versatile
    6.3 6.78/m
    versatile is able to adapt and be used many ways, while multipurpose implies it is designed for several specific uses.
  • general-purpose
    3.9 0.96/m
    general-purpose is meant to work in many situations with a single broad use, while multipurpose is made for several distinct functions.
  • all-purpose
    3.2 4.31/m
    all-purpose is suitable for nearly every kind of use, while multipurpose usually means having several specific functions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-purpose - designed for only one use or function
