Mother Hubbard's cupboard

noun
an empty cupboard or pantry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚ ˈhʌbɚdz ˈkʌbɚd / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə ˈhʌbədz ˈkʌbəd / (BrE)
A place, especially a cupboard or pantry, that is completely empty of food; used in reference to a situation where supplies are depleted.

Examples

Mother Hubbard's cupboard empty
After spending a week without shopping, our kitchen turned into Mother Hubbard's cupboard.
He opened the fridge, but it was a regular Mother Hubbard's cupboard situation—nothing but a bottle of milk inside.
The students joked that the snack room resembled Mother Hubbard's cupboard after a long exam week.
