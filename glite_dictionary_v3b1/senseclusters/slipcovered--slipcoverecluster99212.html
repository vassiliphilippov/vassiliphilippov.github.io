slipcovered

adjective
with removable cover
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪpˌkʌvɚd / (AmE), / ˈslɪpˌkʌvəd / (BrE)
having a removable fabric cover, especially designed to protect or decorate furniture

Examples

A slipcovered sofa
They bought a slipcovered sofa for easy cleaning.
The slipcovered armchair matched the curtains perfectly in the living room.
Many families prefer slipcovered furniture because it is easy to wash and maintain.
