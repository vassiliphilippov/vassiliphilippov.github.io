fast-paced world

noun
a world marked by rapid change
4.5
Pronunciation: / fæst peɪst wɜrld / (AmE), / fɑːst peɪst wɜːld / (BrE)
A world or environment characterized by rapid activity, development, or change.

Examples

A fast-paced world
Living in a fast-paced world can make it challenging to find time for relaxation.
Many people feel stressed by the demands of today's fast-paced world.
Technology often drives the changes in our fast-paced world, constantly altering how we live and work.
