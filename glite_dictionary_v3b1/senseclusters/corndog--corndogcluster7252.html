corndog

noun
battered sausage snack
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌdɔg / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌdɒg / (BrE)
a sausage, usually on a stick, coated in cornmeal batter and deep-fried

Examples

A corndog on stick
At the carnival, I tried my first corndog and loved the crispy coating.
He ordered a corndog and fries from the food truck for a quick lunch.
Children lined up at the fair to get a freshly made corndog on a stick.
