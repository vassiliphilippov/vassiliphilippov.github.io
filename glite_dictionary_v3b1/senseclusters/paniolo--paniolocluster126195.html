paniolo

noun
Hawaiian cowboy
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːniˈoʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˌpænɪˈəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a Hawaiian cowboy, especially one who herds cattle on ranches in Hawaii

Examples

A paniolo cowboy
The paniolo guided the cattle across the green pastures of the Big Island.
At the rodeo, a skilled paniolo demonstrated traditional Hawaiian ranching techniques.
Many families in Hawaii are proud of their paniolo heritage and cowboy traditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, especially in the western US
  • synonym: vaquero - a Spanish or Mexican cattle herder
