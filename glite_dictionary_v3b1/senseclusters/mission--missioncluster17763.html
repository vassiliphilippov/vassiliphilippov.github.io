mission

noun
religious outreach or charitable work
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃən /; / ˈmɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃən /; / ˈmɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
an activity, organization, or place where people (especially missionaries) work to spread their religion or help others

Examples

A church mission
Many volunteers joined the mission to provide food and shelter to those in need.
She devoted her life to the mission in Africa, working tirelessly for the local community.
The church established a mission in the remote village to help improve education and healthcare.
She volunteered at a Baptist mission during her travels in Africa.
The mission provides food and shelter as well as religious instruction.
The Catholic mission set up a school and a clinic in the remote village.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: church - a building for Christian worship
  • synonym: ministry - the work or vocation of a minister in a Christian church
