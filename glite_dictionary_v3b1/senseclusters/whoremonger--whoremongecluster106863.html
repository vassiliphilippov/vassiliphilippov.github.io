whoremonger

noun
a man who procures prostitutes
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːrˌmʌŋɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɔːmʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a man who procures prostitutes or has frequent dealings with prostitutes; a pimp or procurer

Examples

A whoremonger arranges clients
In old literature, a whoremonger was considered morally reprehensible and shunned by society.
She despised the corrupt officials who acted as whoremongers behind the scenes.
The notorious whoremonger was known for running several brothels in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pimp - a person, especially a man, who controls prostitutes and arranges clients for them, taking part of their earnings
  • synonym: procurer - a person who procures prostitutes for clients
