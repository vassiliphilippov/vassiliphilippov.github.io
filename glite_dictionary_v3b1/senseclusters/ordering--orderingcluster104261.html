Examples

The ordering of items
Improper ordering can make the instructions difficult to follow.
She was responsible for the ordering of the books on the shelves.
The ordering of the files was alphabetical for easy reference.
Changing the ordering of the steps can lead to confusion in the procedure.
Her systematic ordering of files saved everyone a lot of time.
The ordering of topics in the syllabus helps students build knowledge step by step.

Synonyms

  • order
    1.0 120/m
    order names the arrangement of items in a set pattern, while ordering names the act or process of arranging them.
  • arrangement
    2.7 13/m
    arrangement shows how items are placed or positioned visually, while ordering refers to putting items into a sequence or order.
  • sequencing
    4.1 0.91/m
    sequencing describes the planned process of putting events or steps in order, while ordering is the general act or result.
  • seriation
    8.2 0.06/m
    seriation arranges items by a rule like size or time for study, while ordering is a general sequence of things.
  • ordination
    7.8 0.17/m
    ordination means a careful, logical arrangement of parts in a system, while ordering is the simple act of putting things in sequence.
