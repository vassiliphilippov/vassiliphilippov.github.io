panel light

noun
light illuminating an instrument panel
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpænəl laɪt / (AmE), / ˈpænəl laɪt / (BrE)
a light or lamp that illuminates an instrument panel, typically in a vehicle or aircraft

Examples

Panel light in aircraft
