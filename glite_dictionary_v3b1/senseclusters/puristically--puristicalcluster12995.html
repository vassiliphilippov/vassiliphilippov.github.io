puristically

adverb

9.9
Pronunciation: /pjuː.ˈrɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /pjuː.ˈrɪs.tɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /pjʊə.ˈrɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /pjuː.ˈrɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
