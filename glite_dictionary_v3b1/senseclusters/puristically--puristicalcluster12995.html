puristically

adverb
in a pure, simple style
Pronunciation: / pjʊˈrɪstɪkli / (AmE), / pjʊəˈrɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that emphasizes simple geometric forms and avoids ornamentation, following the principles of Purism in art or design

Examples

Puristically designed building
The architect designed the building puristically, focusing on clean lines and basic shapes.
Her living room was decorated puristically, with no unnecessary details or decorations.
The exhibition was arranged puristically, allowing the artwork to stand out without distraction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ornately - in a highly decorated or elaborate manner
  • synonym: simply - in a plain or uncomplicated way
