Synonyms

  • barrage
    7.6 1.15/m
    barrage is a heavy, continuous artillery attack over a wide area, while bombardment is any intense, prolonged attack with bombs or artillery.
  • barrage fire
    9.3 0.00/m
    barrage fire is continuous heavy artillery fire meant to saturate an area, while bombardment is a longer, general heavy attack.
  • drumfire
    9.9 0.01/m
    drumfire is rapid, nonstop artillery firing that sounds like drums, while bombardment is any sustained heavy attack by artillery or bombs.
  • shelling
    8.3 2.32/m
    shelling is the firing of artillery shells over an area to hit it, while bombardment means a broad, intense, sustained attack.
  • bombing
    3.4 35/m
    bombing is an attack using bombs to cause damage or terror, while bombardment is continuous heavy attack with bombs or artillery.
  • onslaught
    7.6 2.68/m
    onslaught is a sudden, fierce or overwhelming attack by any force, while bombardment refers to sustained heavy fire, often artillery or bombs.
