inapparent

adjective
not obvious
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnəˈpærənt / (AmE), / ˌɪnəˈpærənt / (BrE)
not easily seen, noticed, or detected; not apparent

Examples

An inapparent symptom
The early symptoms of the disease were inapparent to most doctors.
Her disappointment was inapparent during the meeting, as she kept smiling.
The damage to the engine remained inapparent until the car broke down.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apparent - clearly visible or understood
  • synonym: hidden - kept out of sight
  • synonym: invisible - unable to be seen
