sterilize

verb
make free from bacteria
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛr.əˌlaɪz /; / ˈstɛrəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈstɛr.ɪ.laɪz /; / ˈstɛrəlaɪz / (BrE)
to make something free from bacteria or other living microorganisms, usually by using heat, chemicals, or radiation
