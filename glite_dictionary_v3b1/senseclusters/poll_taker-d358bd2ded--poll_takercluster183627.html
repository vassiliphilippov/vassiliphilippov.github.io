poll taker

noun
person who conducts opinion surveys
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊl ˌteɪkər / (AmE), / ˈpəʊl ˌteɪkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to conduct surveys to measure public opinion

Examples

Poll taker conducts survey
A poll taker must remain neutral and avoid influencing the answers of participants.
The poll taker asked me several questions about my preferences in the upcoming election.
While canvassing the neighborhood, the poll taker recorded responses on a digital tablet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollster - a person who conducts opinion polls
