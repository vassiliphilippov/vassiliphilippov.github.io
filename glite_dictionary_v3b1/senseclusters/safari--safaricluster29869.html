safari

noun
overland wildlife expedition
3.1 2.11/m
Pronunciation: / s…ôÀàf…ëri / (AmE), / s…ôÀàf…ëÀêri / (BrE)
an overland expedition, especially for hunting or observing wildlife, traditionally in Africa

Examples

A safari trip
During the 1920s, wealthy adventurers often went on safari for big-game hunting.
The safari lasted two weeks and covered hundreds of miles across the savanna.
They planned a safari through Kenya to see lions and elephants in the wild.

Synonyms

  • wildlife expedition
    3.6 0.00/m
    wildlife expedition goes to study animals in their natural habitat, while safari is an overland African trip often for sighting or hunting.
  • hunting expedition
    4.2 0.11/m
    hunting expedition seeks to hunt or track animals for sport or food, while safari is a broader overland trip for observing or sometimes hunting wildlife.

Related Terms
