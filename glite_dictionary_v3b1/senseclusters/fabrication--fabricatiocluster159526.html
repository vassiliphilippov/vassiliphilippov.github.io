fabrication

noun
a deliberate lie or falsehood
Pronunciation: / ˌfæb.rɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌfæb.rɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a false statement or the act of inventing false information to deceive
