misprise

verb
to undervalue
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈpraɪz / (AmE), / mɪsˈpraɪz / (BrE)
to undervalue or fail to appreciate the worth of something or someone

Examples

To misprise hard work
It is easy to misprise the quiet contributions of team members who do not seek attention.
She realized she had misprised the importance of his advice only after the project failed.
We should not misprise the value of small acts of kindness in our daily lives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: appreciate - to recognize the full worth of something
  • synonym: undervalue - to assign too low a value to something
