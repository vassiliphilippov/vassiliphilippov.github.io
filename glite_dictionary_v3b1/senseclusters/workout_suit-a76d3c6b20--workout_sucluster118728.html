workout suit

noun
set of clothes for exercise
1.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…úrka ät suÀêt / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀêka ät suÀêt / (BrE)
A set of loose, comfortable clothes, usually consisting of sweatpants and a sweatshirt, worn during exercise or athletic activities.

Examples

A workout suit set
He changed into his workout suit before heading to the gym.
She bought a new workout suit for her morning runs in the park.
The workout suit was made of breathable fabric to stay comfortable during exercise.
