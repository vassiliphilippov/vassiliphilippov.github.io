border patrolman

noun
man guarding national borders
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔrdər pəˈtroʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːdə pəˈtrəʊlmən / (BrE)
a man whose job is to patrol and monitor the borders of a country, preventing illegal entry or activity

Examples

Border patrolman on duty
A border patrolman spotted unusual activity near the fence late at night.
During the training exercise, the new border patrolmen practiced checking vehicles.
The border patrolman questioned the travelers about their purpose for crossing the border.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border guard - person who protects a nation's borders
  • synonym: border patrol - official agency responsible for guarding a country's borders
