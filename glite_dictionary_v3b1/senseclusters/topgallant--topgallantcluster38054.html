topgallant

noun
mast section above the topmast
6.9
Pronunciation: / tɑpˈɡæl.ənt / (AmE), / tɒpˈɡæl.ənt / (BrE)
the section of a mast fixed above the topmast on a square-rigged ship

Examples

Topgallant mast
After the mainmast, the topgallant rises further, supporting additional sails.
Damage to the topgallant required immediate repairs to maintain the ship’s speed.
The crew climbed the rigging to secure the topgallant before the approaching storm.
