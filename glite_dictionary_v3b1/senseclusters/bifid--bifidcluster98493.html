bifid

adjective
divided into two parts or lobes
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.fɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪ.fɪd / (BrE)
divided into two parts or lobes; split or cleft into two sections, especially at the tip.

Examples

A bifid tongue
A bifid tongue is sometimes seen in reptiles such as snakes.
The leaf margin was clearly bifid, splitting into two distinct lobes.
The orchid's petals were bifid, giving the flower a unique appearance.
