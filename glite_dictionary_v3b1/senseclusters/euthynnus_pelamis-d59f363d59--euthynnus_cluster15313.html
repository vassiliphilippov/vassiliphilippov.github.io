Euthynnus pelamis

noun
species of oceanic tuna (skipjack)
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˈθɪnəs pəˈleɪmɪs / (AmE), / juːˈθɪnəs pəˈleɪmɪs / (BrE)
A species of oceanic tuna, also known as the skipjack tuna, highly valued in the Pacific region; reaches up to 75 pounds in weight.
