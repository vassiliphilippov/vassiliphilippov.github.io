hadronic

adjective
relating to hadrons
Pronunciation: / hæˈdrɑnɪk / (AmE), / hæˈdrɒnɪk / (BrE)
relating to hadrons, which are subatomic particles made of quarks, such as protons and neutrons

Examples

Hadronic particles
The hadronic structure of protons is studied in high-energy physics experiments.
Scientists analyzed the hadronic collisions produced in the particle accelerator.
Understanding hadronic matter is essential for explaining the behavior of atomic nuclei.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hadron - a subatomic particle made of quarks, such as a proton or neutron
  • antonym: leptonic - relating to leptons, a different class of subatomic particles
