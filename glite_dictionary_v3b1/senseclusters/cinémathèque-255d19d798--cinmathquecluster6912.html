cinémathèque

noun
film archive or library
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪnəməˈtɛk / (AmE), / ˌsɪnəməˈtɛk / (BrE)
a film archive or library, especially one dedicated to preserving and showing classic or rare films

Examples

The cinémathèque archive
The city opened a new cinémathèque to preserve important films from the twentieth century.
She volunteers at the local cinémathèque, helping to organize film screenings for the public.
Many classic movies are restored and shown at the cinémathèque every month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: library - a place where books or other materials are kept for use
