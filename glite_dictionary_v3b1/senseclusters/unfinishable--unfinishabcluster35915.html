unfinishable

adjective
impossible to complete
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪnɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to complete or bring to an end

Examples

An unfinishable task
The novel was so complex that it felt unfinishable to many readers.
After hours of work, the massive jigsaw puzzle seemed completely unfinishable.
Some projects are so poorly planned that they become unfinishable from the start.

Related Terms

  • antonym: doable - able to be done or completed
  • synonym: impossible - not able to be done or achieved
