in-flight

adjective
transmitting in transit
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌflaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌflaɪt / (BrE)
being transmitted, processed, or updated while still in transit through a network or system.

Examples

In-flight data
The system can recover in-flight transactions if a failure occurs during processing.
Engineers analyzed in-flight messages to identify the cause of the communication delay.
It is important to secure all in-flight data to prevent unauthorized access.

Related Terms

  • antonym: completed - finished or fully processed
  • synonym: pending - awaiting completion or resolution
