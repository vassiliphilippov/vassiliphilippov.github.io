geared wheel

noun
toothed wheel for transmitting motion
1.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɪrd wil / (AmE), / ɡɪəd wiːl / (BrE)
a wheel with teeth on its rim designed to engage with another toothed mechanism to change speed or direction of motion

Examples

A geared wheel teeth
He inspected the damaged geared wheel before replacing it with a new one.
The clock's mechanism relies on a geared wheel to synchronize the hands.
The transmission system consists of several geared wheels working together to control speed.
