intergenerational

adjective
between generations
6.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.dʒen.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.dʒen.ə.ˈreɪ.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
relating to or involving relationships, obligations, or fairness between different generations, especially in social or policy contexts

Examples

Intergenerational fairness policy
The government introduced new policies to address intergenerational inequality in housing.
Climate change is often discussed as an intergenerational issue affecting both present and future citizens.
The study focused on intergenerational relationships within extended families.
