Having the shoulders and upper back rounded forward instead of standing erect.

Examples

Round-shouldered posture
The coach advised the round-shouldered player to work on standing straighter.
The elderly man became increasingly round-shouldered as he aged.
Years of poor posture left her slightly round-shouldered.

Synonyms

  • round-backed
    5.9 0.01/m
    round-backed describes an evenly rounded back and shoulders, while round-shouldered points to mainly the upper shoulders bending forward.
  • stooped
    4.7 0.00/m
    stooped means a low, forward posture often from age or tiredness, while round-shouldered describes shoulders and upper back curved forward.
  • hunched
    4.1 0.17/m
    hunched shows the shoulders pulled up and the back rounded more tightly, while round-shouldered names a longer upper shoulder curve.
