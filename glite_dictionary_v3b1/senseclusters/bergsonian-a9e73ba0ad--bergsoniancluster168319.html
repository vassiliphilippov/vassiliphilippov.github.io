Bergsonian

noun
follower of Bergson
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɜːrɡˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / bɜːɡˈsəʊniən / (BrE)

Examples

A Bergsonian thinker

Many Bergsonians gathered at the conference to discuss the influence of Bergson's philosophy.
As a dedicated Bergsonian, she often referenced Bergson's ideas in her essays.
