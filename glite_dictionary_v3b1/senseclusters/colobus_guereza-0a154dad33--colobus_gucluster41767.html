Colobus guereza

noun
species of black-and-white colobus monkey
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑləbəs ɡuˈrɛzə / (AmE), / ˈkɒləbəs ɡuːˈrɛzə / (BrE)
A large black-and-white colobus monkey of East Africa, known for its long white fringes of hair and bushy white tail.

Examples

The Colobus guereza monkey
The Colobus guereza is easily recognized by its striking black and white fur.
Unlike some other monkeys, the Colobus guereza lacks a thumb on its hands.
We spotted a group of Colobus guereza monkeys leaping through the trees in Kenya.

Related Terms
