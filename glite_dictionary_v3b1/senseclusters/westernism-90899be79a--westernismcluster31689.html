Westernism

noun
Western custom or idea
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənɪzəm / (BrE)
an idea, custom, practice, or the influence/adoption of Western cultures or societies

Examples

Westernism in fashion
The influence of Westernism can be seen in the architecture of many modern cities.
Some people worry that Westernism threatens local traditions and values.
The popularity of fast food is often cited as an example of Westernism spreading worldwide.
The spread of Westernism has influenced fashion trends in many Asian countries.
Some critics argue that Westernism threatens local traditions and cultural identities.
Debates about Westernism often arise in discussions about globalization and cultural change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Westernization - the process of adopting Western ideas or culture
  • antonym: tradition - a long-established custom or belief
  • antonym: traditionalism - the upholding of traditional customs and values
