significator

noun
representative card or symbol
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɪfɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɪfɪkeɪtə / (BrE)
a symbol or card, especially in astrology or tarot, that represents a person, thing, or situation

Examples

Tarot significator card
In a tarot reading, the significator is chosen to represent the person asking the question.
The astrologer selected a significator to focus the interpretation on a specific event.
Each significator in the spread can reveal different aspects of the situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicator - something that points out or shows something
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or represents something else
