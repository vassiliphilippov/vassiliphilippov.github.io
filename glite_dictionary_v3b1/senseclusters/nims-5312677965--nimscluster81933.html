NIMS

noun
emergency management system
Pronunciation: / nɪmz / (AmE), / nɪmz / (BrE)
a standardized system used in the United States for managing emergencies and incidents across agencies

Examples

NIMS emergency system
All emergency responders must be trained in NIMS procedures.
The city updated its disaster plan to comply with NIMS requirements.
After the hurricane, agencies coordinated their response using the NIMS framework.
