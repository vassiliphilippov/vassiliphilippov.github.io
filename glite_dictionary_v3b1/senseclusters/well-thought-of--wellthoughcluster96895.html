well-thought-of

adjective
regarded with respect or esteem
Pronunciation: / ˌwɛl ˈθɔt ʌv / (AmE), / ˌwɛl ˈθɔːt ɒv / (BrE)

The company is well-thought-of for its strong ethical values and transparency.
