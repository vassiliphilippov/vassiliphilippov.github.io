varimax

noun
factor analysis method
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛrɪˌmæks / (AmE), / ˈvɛərɪˌmæks / (BrE)
a statistical method used in factor analysis to make the interpretation of factors clearer by rotating the axes

Using varimax rotation, the team identified distinct patterns in the psychological data.
