witling

noun
person lacking wit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪtlɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who lacks real wit or intelligence (sometimes pretending to be clever)

At the party, she was labeled a witling for her shallow attempts at clever conversation.

  • synonym: poseur - a person who pretends to be something they are not
  • synonym: simpleton - a person lacking in common sense or intelligence
  • antonym: wit - a person with real cleverness and humor
