cleantech

noun
environmental technology
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkliːnˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈkliːnˌtɛk / (BrE)
technology or the industry focused on reducing environmental impact and promoting clean energy

Examples

Cleantech industry

Adopting cleantech can help industries lower their carbon emissions and save energy.
Many startups in the cleantech industry are working on innovative solar power solutions.
Government policies are encouraging investment in cleantech to address climate change.
The conference brought together leaders from the cleantech sector to discuss new environmental technologies.

  • synonym: green technology - technology that is environmentally friendly
  • synonym: greentech - technology that is environmentally friendly
  • synonym: renewable energy - energy from sources that are naturally replenished
