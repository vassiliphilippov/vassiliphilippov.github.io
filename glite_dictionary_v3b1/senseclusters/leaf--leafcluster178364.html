leaf

noun
hinged or detachable table or door section
Pronunciation: / lif / (AmE), / liːf / (BrE)
a hinged or detachable flat section of an object, such as an extension of a table or a panel of a door

Examples

A table leaf
He folded down the leaf of the drop-leaf desk to save space.
One of the door’s leaves was removed for repair.
The dining table has an extra leaf that can be added for guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panel - a flat, typically rectangular piece forming part of a surface, such as a door
