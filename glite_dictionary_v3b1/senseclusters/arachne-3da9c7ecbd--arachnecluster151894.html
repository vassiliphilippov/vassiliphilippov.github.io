Arachne

proper noun
obsolete spider genus
Pronunciation: / əˈrækni / (AmE), / əˈrækni / (BrE)
a former scientific genus name used for certain spiders, now largely reclassified in zoology

Examples

Arachne spider label
The label Arachne can still be found on some old spider specimens in natural history museums.
Taxonomists no longer use Arachne as a valid genus for classifying spiders.
Historical records refer to several species under the genus Arachne before reclassification.
