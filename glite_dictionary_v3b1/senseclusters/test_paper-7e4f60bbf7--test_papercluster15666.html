test paper

noun
a written examination
1.0
Pronunciation: / tɛst ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / tɛst ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
A written set of questions used to assess knowledge or ability, typically as part of an examination.

Examples

A math test paper

Each test paper consisted of multiple-choice and essay questions.
The students handed in their test papers at the end of the examination.
