exposure

noun
direction a building or window faces
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə / (BrE)
The direction in which a building, room, or window faces, affecting light, weather, and temperature.

Examples

South-facing exposure
Gardeners often prefer plots with an eastern exposure to catch the gentle morning sun.
The apartment's west-facing exposure means it receives lots of afternoon sunlight.
The house has a southern exposure, making it warm and bright throughout the year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orientation - the position of something in relation to its surroundings
