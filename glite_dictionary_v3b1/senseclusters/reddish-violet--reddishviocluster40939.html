reddish-violet

adjective
violet tinged with red
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈvaɪələt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdɪʃ ˈvaɪələt / (BrE)
Of a violet color with a slight tinge of red.

Examples

A reddish-violet dress
Her dress was an elegant reddish-violet that caught everyone's attention.
The artist used a reddish-violet shade to paint the evening sky.
The flowers in the garden bloomed in various reddish-violet hues.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: violet - a bluish-purple color
