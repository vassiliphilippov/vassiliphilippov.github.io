Liang

proper noun
Chinese dynasty name
9.8
Pronunciation: / liˈɑːŋ / (AmE), / liˈæŋ / (BrE)
the name of one of several historical Chinese states or dynasties, especially the Liang dynasty in ancient China

Examples

Liang dynasty
The Liang dynasty played an important role in the history of southern China.
Many ancient temples were built during the reign of the Liang.
Historians study the art and culture of the Liang to understand early Chinese civilization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Han - a major Chinese dynasty known for its long rule and cultural achievements
  • synonym: Tang - a powerful and influential Chinese dynasty
