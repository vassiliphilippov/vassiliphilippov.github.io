Jebusite

adjective
of ancient Jebusites
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛbjəˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛbjʊˌsaɪt / (BrE)
relating to the Jebusites, an ancient people who lived in Jerusalem before the Israelites.

Examples

Jebusite culture
The Jebusite walls of Jerusalem were built long before King David's conquest.
Archaeologists discovered Jebusite artifacts beneath the city's oldest ruins.
The museum features a display of Jebusite pottery and tools from ancient Jerusalem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Canaanite - relating to the ancient people of Canaan
  • antonym: Israelite - relating to the ancient people of Israel
