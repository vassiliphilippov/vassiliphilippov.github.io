steam heating

noun
heating system using steam and radiators
2.8 3.5
Pronunciation: / stim ˈhiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / stiːm ˈhiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
A heating system in which steam is generated in boilers and circulated through pipes to radiators to heat buildings.

Examples

Steam heating system
During winter, steam heating keeps the entire house warm and comfortable.
Many New York City buildings rely on steam heating for their primary source of warmth.
The old apartment building is still equipped with steam heating from the original construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: central heating - a system for heating a whole building from a single source
