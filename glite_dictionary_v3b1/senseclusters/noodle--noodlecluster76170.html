noodle

verb
to play casually
6.8 0.34/m
Pronunciation: / ÀànuÀêd…ôl / (AmE), / ÀànuÀêd…ôl / (BrE)
to play or improvise music in a casual, aimless way

Examples

To noodle on guitar
She likes to noodle on the piano when she is relaxing at home.
The guitarist spent the afternoon noodling with new melodies in the studio.
Sometimes, the best ideas come when you just noodle around on your instrument.
She noodled on the piano, letting her fingers wander over the keys without a specific tune.
The guitarist noodled quietly while the rest of the band set up their equipment.
Sometimes, I just noodle on my violin to relax and see what melodies come out.
