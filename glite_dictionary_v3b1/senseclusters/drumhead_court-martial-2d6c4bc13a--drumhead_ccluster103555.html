drumhead court-martial

noun
improvised military court for urgent offenses
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌhɛd kɔrt ˈmɑrʃəl / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌhɛd kɔːt ˈmɑːʃəl / (BrE)
A military court convened quickly to hear urgent charges of offenses committed during action, often without the usual formalities.

Examples

A drumhead court-martial ordered
During the chaos of battle, the general ordered a drumhead court-martial for the accused deserter.
Many historians criticize the lack of due process in a drumhead court-martial.
The officer was sentenced after a hasty drumhead court-martial in the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: court-martial - a court constituted to try members of the armed services
