incompleteness

noun
state of being incomplete or imperfect
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kəmˈpliːt.nəs / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəmˈpliːt.nɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being incomplete; lacking some necessary parts or aspects; imperfection or deficiency.

Examples

The incompleteness of work
He apologized for the incompleteness of his work, promising to submit the missing sections soon.
Researchers noted the incompleteness in the experimental data and suggested further tests.
The incompleteness of the report left many questions unanswered.
