tricolored

adjective
having three colors
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having three different colors, especially arranged together in a pattern or design

Examples

Tricolored uniform
The national team wore tricolored uniforms in red, white, and blue for the championship match.
A tricolored butterfly landed gently on the flower, its wings displaying orange, black, and white.
She bought a tricolored scarf to match her winter coat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monochrome - having only one color
  • synonym: multicolored - having many different colors
