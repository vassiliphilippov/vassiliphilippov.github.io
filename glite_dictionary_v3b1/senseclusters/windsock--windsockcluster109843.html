windsock

noun
device showing wind direction
8.1 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndˌsɑk / (AmE), / ˈwɪndˌsɒk / (BrE)
A truncated cloth cone mounted on a mast that shows the direction and relative strength of the wind, especially at airports.

Examples

A windsock at airport
A faded windsock fluttered at the end of the runway.
Before taking off, the pilot checked the windsock to assess the wind conditions.
The airport's windsock pointed northwest, indicating the direction of the wind.
