hypodermic needle

noun
hollow needle for injection or extraction
5.4 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpəˈdɜrmɪk ˈnidəl / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəˈdɜːmɪk ˈniːdl̩ / (BrE)
a hollow needle used for injecting substances into the body or withdrawing fluids from it, especially through the skin

Examples

A hypodermic needle injection
Always dispose of a hypodermic needle safely after use to prevent injury and infection.
Doctors often use a hypodermic needle to draw blood for laboratory tests.
The nurse used a hypodermic needle to administer the vaccine quickly and painlessly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: syringe - medical instrument used to inject fluids into or withdraw them from the body
