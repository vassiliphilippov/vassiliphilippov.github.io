gleet

noun
thin abnormal bodily discharge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡliːt / (AmE), / ɡliːt / (BrE)
a thin, abnormal discharge from a wound or the urethra, especially as a symptom of chronic gonorrhea

Examples

Thin gleet discharge
A thin, whitish gleet was observed, leading to further diagnostic tests.
In older medical texts, gleet is frequently mentioned as a complication of chronic gonorrhea.
The physician noted a persistent gleet from the patient’s wound, indicating a possible infection.
