creative

noun
person with original ideas
Pronunciation: / kriˈeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kriˈeɪtɪv / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at inventing or producing original ideas, art, or designs

Examples

A creative professional
Many creatives work in advertising, producing innovative campaigns for major brands.
The conference attracted creatives from all over the world to share their ideas and projects.
As a creative, she enjoys experimenting with new styles in her artwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially professionally
  • synonym: designer - a person who plans and creates designs
