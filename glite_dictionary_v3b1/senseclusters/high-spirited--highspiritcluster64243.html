high-spirited

adjective
exuberant and lively
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ ˌspɪrɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ ˌspɪrɪtɪd / (BrE)
full of energy, enthusiasm, and lively excitement; exuberant and lively.

Examples

A high-spirited person
His high-spirited nature made him popular at every social event.
She gave a high-spirited performance that delighted the entire audience.
The high-spirited children filled the room with laughter and excitement.
