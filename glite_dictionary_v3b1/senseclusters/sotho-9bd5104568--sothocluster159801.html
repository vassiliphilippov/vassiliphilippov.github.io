Sotho

proper noun
southern African people
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊtuː / (AmE), / ˈsəʊtuː / (BrE)
a member of a southern African ethnic group or the Bantu language spoken by this group

Examples

The Sotho of Lesotho
The Sotho are known for their colorful blankets and traditional music.
She is studying the Sotho language at university to learn more about southern African cultures.
Many Sotho live in Lesotho and parts of South Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Basotho - the people of the Sotho ethnic group
  • synonym: Sesotho - the language spoken by the Sotho people
  • antonym: Zulu - a southern African ethnic group and their language
