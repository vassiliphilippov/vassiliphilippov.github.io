weld

verb
join or unite closely
3.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /weld/ (AmE), /weld/ (BrE)
to join together or unite closely or intimately, especially in a way that makes separation difficult

Examples

To weld a team
Her deep loyalty welded her to the organization for decades.
The shared experience of hardship welded the team together.
Years of collaboration have welded them into an inseparable partnership.
