Kalenjin

noun
Nilotic language of Kenya
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɑːˈlɛndʒɪn / (AmE), / kəˈlɛndʒɪn / (BrE)
a Southern Nilotic language, or any of the closely related languages, spoken by the Kalenjin peoples of Kenya

Examples

Kalenjin language
She learned to speak Kalenjin before moving to the town.
Many children in the region grow up bilingual in Swahili and Kalenjin.
The documentary featured songs and stories in the Kalenjin language.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Kikuyu - a major Bantu language spoken in Kenya
  • synonym: Nandi - a Southern Nilotic language closely related to Kalenjin
