the Pamirs

proper noun
mountain range in Central Asia
7.6
Pronunciation: / ðə pəˈmɪrz / (AmE), / ðə paˈmɪəz / (BrE)
a mountain range in Central Asia, mainly in Tajikistan, extending into Kyrgyzstan, Afghanistan, Pakistan, and western China
