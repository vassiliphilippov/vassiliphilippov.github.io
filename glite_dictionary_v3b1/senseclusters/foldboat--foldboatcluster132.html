foldboat

noun
foldable boat
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊldˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈfəʊldˌbəʊt / (BrE)
a lightweight boat that can be folded for easy transport and storage

Examples

A foldboat for camping
He carried his foldboat to the lake in a backpack.
After unfolding the foldboat, she was ready to paddle across the river.
The foldboat fits easily in the trunk of a small car, making weekend trips convenient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a lightweight narrow boat, usually pointed at both ends and open on top
  • synonym: kayak - a small, narrow watercraft typically propelled by a double-bladed paddle
