gateman

noun
gate guard
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtmən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to guard or control access at a gate

Examples

The gateman checks passes
The gateman checked everyone's passes before letting them enter the building.
A uniformed gateman greeted visitors at the entrance to the factory.
The school hired a new gateman to improve security at the main gate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doorman - a person employed to guard the entrance of a building
  • synonym: guard - a person who protects a place or people
