excrescence

noun
abnormal outgrowth or projection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskrɛsəns / (AmE), / ɪkˈskrɛsəns / (BrE)
an abnormal outgrowth or projection, especially from the surface of a body or object

Examples
