themeless

noun
puzzle without theme
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈθiːmləs / (AmE), / ˈθiːmləs / (BrE)
a crossword puzzle or similar puzzle that does not have a central theme or set of themed answers

Examples

A themeless crossword
She prefers solving a challenging themeless on Sunday mornings with her coffee.
The latest themeless featured some of the trickiest clues I have ever seen.
Many advanced solvers enjoy the open grid of a themeless for its difficulty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crossword - a word puzzle in which words are filled into a grid based on clues
  • antonym: themed puzzle - a puzzle with a central theme or set of related answers
