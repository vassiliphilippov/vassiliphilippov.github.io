hoi

interjection
attention or surprise
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔɪ / (AmE), / hɔɪ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to call attention or express surprise, annoyance, or disapproval

Examples

Hoi! Watch out
He shouted hoi when the cyclist nearly knocked him off the pavement.
With a loud hoi, she tried to get the group's attention at the busy market.
The teacher called out hoi to quiet the noisy classroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hey - an exclamation used to attract attention or express surprise
  • synonym: oi - an exclamation used to get someone's attention, especially in British English
