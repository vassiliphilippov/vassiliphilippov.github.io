journo

noun
informal journalist
8.4 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd í…ùÀêno ä / (AmE), / Ààd í…úÀên…ô ä / (BrE)
an informal term for a journalist, especially one working in news media

Examples

A journo reports news
The journo arrived early to cover the breaking news story.
Several journos gathered outside the courthouse waiting for a statement.
As a young journo, she was eager to make a name for herself.

Synonyms

  • newsperson
