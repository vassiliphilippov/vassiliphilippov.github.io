telegram

verb
to send by telegraph
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪɡræm / (BrE)
to send a message by telegram, a written message transmitted by telegraph.

Examples

To telegram news
She telegrammed her family to announce her safe arrival.
He telegrammed the news to his business partner overseas.
Before telephones were common, people often telegrammed important information.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: email - to send a message electronically
  • synonym: wire - to send a message by telegraph
