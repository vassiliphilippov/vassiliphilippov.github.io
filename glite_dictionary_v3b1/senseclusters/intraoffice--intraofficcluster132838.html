intraoffice

adjective
within one office
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈɑːfɪs / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
happening or existing within a single office or organization

Examples

An intraoffice chat
The company implemented an intraoffice chat tool to improve communication among employees.
All intraoffice memos must be approved by the manager before distribution.
They scheduled an intraoffice meeting to discuss the new project guidelines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internal - existing or happening inside an organization
  • antonym: interoffice - existing or occurring between different offices
