probe

verb
question or examine thoroughly
5.7 5.1
Pronunciation: / proʊb / (AmE), / prəʊb / (BrE)
to ask questions or investigate something thoroughly and closely, especially to uncover information

Examples

To probe for answers
Doctors probed for details to better understand the patient's symptoms.
She probed into his reasons for leaving the company.
The journalist probed the politician with sharp questions about the controversy.
