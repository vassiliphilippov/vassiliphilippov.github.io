Slavophile

noun
admirer of Slavs
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈslæv.ə.faɪl/ /; / /ˈslɑːv.ə.faɪl/ (AmE), /ˈslæv.ə.faɪl/ /; / /ˈslɑːv.ə.faɪl/ (BrE)
a person who admires or supports Slavic peoples, their culture, or the ideas of Slavophilism (especially in 19th-century Russia)

Examples
