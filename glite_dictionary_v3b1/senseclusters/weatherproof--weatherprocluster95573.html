weatherproof

verb
make resistant to bad weather
4.2 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈweð.ər.pruːf/ (AmE), /ˈweð.ə.pruːf/ (BrE)
to make something resistant to rain, wind, or other bad weather by protecting or sealing it

Examples
