overzealously

adverb
with excessive enthusiasm
7.5 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàz…õl…ôsli / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàziÀêl…ôsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows too much enthusiasm, eagerness, or intensity

Examples

Acting overzealously at work
The new manager enforced the rules overzealously, upsetting many employees.
He overzealously cleaned the house, scrubbing every surface twice.
Some fans overzealously cheered, making it hard for others to hear the announcements.

Synonyms

  • overenthusiastically
    7.2 0.00/m
    overenthusiastically shows too much eager excitement in speech or action, while overzealously implies intense, often excessive eagerness that can irritate.
  • effusively
    7.4 0.14/m
    effusively expresses warm, unrestrained feeling in a friendly way, while overzealously stresses excess energy or strong intensity that may harm.
  • gushingly
    7.7 0.04/m
    gushingly sounds sentimental and overly friendly, overflowing with praise or feeling, while overzealously focuses on excessive eagerness or intensity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderately - in a way that is not excessive or extreme
  • degree lower: zealously - with great enthusiasm or energy
