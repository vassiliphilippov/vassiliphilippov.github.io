overzealously

adverb
with excessive enthusiasm
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈzɛləsli / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈziːləsli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows too much enthusiasm, eagerness, or intensity

Examples

Acting overzealously at work
The new manager enforced the rules overzealously, upsetting many employees.
He overzealously cleaned the house, scrubbing every surface twice.
Some fans overzealously cheered, making it hard for others to hear the announcements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderately - in a way that is not excessive or extreme
  • degree lower: zealously - with great enthusiasm or energy
