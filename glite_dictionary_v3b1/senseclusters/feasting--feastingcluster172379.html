feasting

noun

The act of eating an elaborate and abundant meal, often as part of a celebration or accompanied by entertainment.

Examples

To feast on food
After a long day of hiking, they rewarded themselves by feasting on local specialties.
During the harvest festival, families gather for feasting and celebration.
The wedding was a night of music, dancing, and endless feasting.
