swill

noun
kitchen waste used as animal feed
9.9
Pronunciation: / swɪl / (AmE), / swɪl / (BrE)
food waste mixed with water or milk, used as feed for pigs or other livestock

Examples

Pig swill
Buckets of swill were poured into the trough for the hungry animals.
Please don't throw away the leftovers; they'll go into the swill for the livestock.
The farmer collected the kitchen swill each morning to feed the pigs.
