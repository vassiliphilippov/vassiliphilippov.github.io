whereto

conjunction
to which place
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / w…õrÀàtuÀê / (AmE), / w…õ…ôÀàtuÀê / (BrE)
to which place or situation; in or to which (used as a relative adverb introducing a subordinate clause, now rare or formal).

Examples

The process whereto you apply
The document outlined the procedures whereto all applicants must adhere.
He described the destination whereto the travelers would soon arrive.
There are rules whereto every member of the club must conform.

Synonyms

  • whereunto
    9.9 0.00/m
    whereunto can mean to which place, thing, or situation in formal texts, while whereto refers mainly to place or situation and is rarer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: where - in or at what place
  • synonym: whither - to what place (archaic)
