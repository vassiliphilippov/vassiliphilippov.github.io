scoreless

adjective
with no points or goals scored
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔrləs / (AmE), / ˈskɔːləs / (BrE)
(of a game or period of play) ending or having no points or goals scored by either side.

Examples

Scoreless first half
After a tense, scoreless first half, both teams were eager to score.
The match remained scoreless until the final few minutes.
The pitcher delivered three scoreless innings to start the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goalless - (of a match or period) with no goals scored
