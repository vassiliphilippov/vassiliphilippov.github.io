Wormian bone

noun
small bones in skull sutures
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…úrmi…ôn bo än / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀêm…™…ôn b…ô än / (BrE)
any of the small, irregular bones sometimes found within the sutures of the skull, especially in the human cranium

Examples
