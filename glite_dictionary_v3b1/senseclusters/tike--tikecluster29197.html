tike

noun
uncouth or ill-bred person
Pronunciation: / taɪk / (AmE), / taɪk / (BrE)
an uncouth, ill-bred person, especially one lacking in manners or refinement

Examples

A rude tike
In the pub, the drunk tike shouted insults at everyone around him.
She refused to associate with someone she considered a tike.
The old man called the unruly boy a tike for his bad behavior.
