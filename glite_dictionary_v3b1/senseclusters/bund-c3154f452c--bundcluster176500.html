Bund

proper noun
Shanghai waterfront area
6.8
Pronunciation: / bʌnd / (AmE), / bʌnd / (BrE)
a famous waterfront area and historic promenade along the Huangpu River in Shanghai, China, known for its colonial-era buildings

Examples

The Bund Shanghai
Many visitors come to the Bund to see the impressive skyline across the river.
The Bund is especially beautiful at night when the buildings are lit up.
Walking along the Bund offers a glimpse into Shanghai's colonial history.
