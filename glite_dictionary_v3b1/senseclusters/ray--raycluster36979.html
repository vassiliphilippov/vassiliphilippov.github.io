ray

noun
bony spine in a fish fin
4.3
Pronunciation: / reɪ / (AmE), / reɪ / (BrE)
any of the stiff, bony spines that support the fin of a fish

Examples

Fin rays
Scientists counted the number of rays in the pectoral fins for identification.
Some species have soft rays, while others have hard spiny ones.
The dorsal fin of the fish is supported by sharp rays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spine - a stiff, pointed structure projecting from a surface, such as a fish fin
