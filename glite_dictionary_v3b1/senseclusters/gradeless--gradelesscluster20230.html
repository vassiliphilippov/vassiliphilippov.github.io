gradeless

adjective
completely flat
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪdləs / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪdləs / (BrE)
having no slope, gradient, or incline; completely flat or level

Examples

Gradeless road
The engineer designed a gradeless road to ensure easy access for all vehicles.
A gradeless field is ideal for playing soccer because the ball rolls evenly.
They appreciated the gradeless path, which made walking with a stroller much easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: level - having a flat or even surface
  • antonym: sloped - having an inclined or slanted surface
