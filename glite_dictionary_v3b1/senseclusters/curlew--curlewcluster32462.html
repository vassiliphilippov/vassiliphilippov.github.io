curlew

noun
large migratory shorebird with curved bill
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜr.lu / (AmE), / ˈkɜː.ljuː / (BrE)
a large migratory shorebird with a long, down-curved bill, related to the sandpiper and woodcock families

Examples

A curlew bird
A flock of curlew flew low over the salt marsh at dusk.
Birdwatchers gathered to observe the rare curlew during its migration.
The distinctive call of the curlew echoed across the estuary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whimbrel - a smaller species of curlew with a shorter bill
