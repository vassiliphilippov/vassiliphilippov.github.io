zealot

noun
fanatic or fervent supporter
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛlət / (AmE), / ˈzɛlət / (BrE)
A person who is extremely passionate and uncompromising in pursuit of their religious, political, or other ideals, often to the point of fanaticism.

Examples

A zealot for beliefs
He was labeled a zealot by his colleagues because of his uncompromising opinions.
She became a zealot for environmental protection, dedicating her life to the cause.
Social media can amplify the voices of zealots from all sides of the debate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanatic - a person filled with excessive and single-minded zeal
  • antonym: moderate - a person whose views are not extreme
