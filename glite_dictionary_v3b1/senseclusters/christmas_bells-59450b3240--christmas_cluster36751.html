Christmas bells

noun
Australian plant with bell-shaped red or orange flowers
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪs·məs bɛlz / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.məs bɛlz / (BrE)
any of several Australian plants of the genus Blandfordia, with large, bell-shaped orange or red flowers, often blooming around Christmas.
