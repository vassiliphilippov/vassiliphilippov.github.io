dawn

noun
the beginning of a period
5.0
Pronunciation: / dɔn / (AmE), / dɔːn / (BrE)
the beginning or earliest stage of a period, event, or phenomenon

Examples

The dawn of time
At the dawn of civilization, humans began to form communities and settlements.
Many historians consider the dawn of the Internet era to be the early 1990s.
The dawn of a new age in medicine brought hope to many patients.
At the dawn of the digital age, computers were rare and expensive.
Many scientists believe we are at the dawn of a new era in medicine.
The novel explores humanity at the dawn of civilization.
