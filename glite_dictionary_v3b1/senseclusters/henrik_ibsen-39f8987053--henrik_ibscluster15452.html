Henrik Ibsen

proper noun
Norwegian playwright and poet (1828–1906)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnˌrɪk ˈɪb.sən /; / ˈhɛn.rɪk ˈɪb.sən / (AmE), / ˈhɛn.rɪk ˈɪb.sən / (BrE)
(1828–1906) a Norwegian playwright and poet, regarded as a founder of modern realistic drama, known for plays about social and political issues
