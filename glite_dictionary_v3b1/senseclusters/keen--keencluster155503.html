keen

adjective
having a sharp edge or point
3.5 0.40/m
Pronunciation: / kin / (AmE), / kiÀên / (BrE)
(of an edge or point) having a sharp cutting edge or point; able to cut easily.

Examples

Keen blade
A keen blade can slice through paper effortlessly.
Always be cautious when handling tools with a keen point.
The chef prefers to use a keen knife to prepare ingredients.

Synonyms

  • razor-sharp
    4.1 0.72/m
    razor-sharp describes something extremely sharp like a razor and often used figuratively, while keen simply means sharp enough to cut.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blunt - lacking a sharp edge or point
  • synonym: sharp - having an edge or point that can cut or pierce easily
