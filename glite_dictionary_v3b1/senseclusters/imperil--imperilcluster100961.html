imperil

verb
put in danger
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɛrəl / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɛrəl / (BrE)
to put something or someone in danger; to endanger

Examples

To imperil safety
Refusing to wear a helmet can imperil your safety while biking.
Rising sea levels imperil coastal communities around the world.
Their reckless actions could imperil the entire mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endanger - to put someone or something at risk or in danger
  • antonym: protect - to keep someone or something safe from harm
