unamused

adjective
not entertained
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈmjuːzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈmjuːzd / (BrE)
not entertained, pleased, or finding something funny or enjoyable

Examples

He is unamused
He remained unamused by their attempts to cheer him up after the meeting.
The teacher was clearly unamused by the students' prank in class.
Despite the comedian's best efforts, the audience looked unamused throughout the performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: amused - finding something funny or entertaining
  • synonym: displeased - feeling unhappy or dissatisfied
