instancy

noun
insistent or urgent quality
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.stən.si / (AmE), / ˈɪn.stən.si / (BrE)
the quality of being insistent or persistent in demand or request.

Examples

Instancy of demand
He argued his case with such instancy that the committee could not ignore him.
Her instancy during negotiations impressed even the toughest opponents.
The instancy of her plea moved everyone in the room.
