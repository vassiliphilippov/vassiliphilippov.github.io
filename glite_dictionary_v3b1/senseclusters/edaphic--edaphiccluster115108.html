edaphic

adjective
relating to soil
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdæfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈdæfɪk / (BrE)
relating to soil, especially as it influences living organisms or plant growth

Examples

Edaphic soil factors
The distribution of these plants is determined by edaphic factors such as soil pH and texture.
Researchers studied the edaphic conditions that support rare wildflowers in the region.
Certain crops thrive only in specific edaphic environments due to their soil requirements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: climatic - relating to climate rather than soil
  • synonym: soil-related - connected with or caused by soil
