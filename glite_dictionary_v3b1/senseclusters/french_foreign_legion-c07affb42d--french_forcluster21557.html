French Foreign Legion

noun
unit of the French Army formed of foreign volunteers
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ ˈfɔrən liˈdʒon / (AmE), / frɛntʃ ˈfɒrən liˈdʒiən / (BrE)
a unit of the French Army historically made up of foreign volunteers, renowned for serving in military operations outside France

Examples

The French Foreign Legion unit
After joining the French Foreign Legion, he was sent to North Africa for training.
Many books and films romanticize the adventures of the French Foreign Legion.
The French Foreign Legion is famous for accepting recruits from almost any nationality.
