dichlorodifluoromethane

noun
CFC-12 refrigerant gas
9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.kloʊ.roʊ.daɪ.flʊr.oʊ.ˈmeθ.eɪn/ (AmE), /daɪ.klɔː.rə.daɪ.flʊə.rə.ˈmeθ.eɪn/ (BrE)
