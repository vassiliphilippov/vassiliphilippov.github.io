dichlorodifluoromethane

noun
CFC-12 refrigerant gas
Pronunciation: / daɪˌklɔːroʊdaɪˌflʊəroʊˈmɛθeɪn / (AmE), / daɪˌklɔːrədəˌflʊərəˈmiːθeɪn /; / daɪˌklɔːrəʊdaɪˌflʊərəˈmiːθeɪn / (BrE)
a colorless, nonflammable synthetic gas (CFC-12) used as a refrigerant and propellant, known for damaging the ozone layer
