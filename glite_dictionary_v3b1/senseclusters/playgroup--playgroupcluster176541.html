playgroup

noun
children's social group
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌɡruːp / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌɡruːp /; / ˈpleɪɡruːp / (BrE)
a group where young children meet regularly to play and socialize, often before starting school and under adult supervision

Examples
