porky

noun
small lie (slang)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːrki / (AmE), / ˈpɔːki / (BrE)
an informal word for a lie, especially a small or harmless one (mainly British slang)

Examples

Tell a porky
He told a porky to avoid getting into trouble at school.
You can always tell when she's making up a porky.
Don't believe him—he's known for telling the odd porky now and then.
