public debate

noun

a wide, ongoing discussion among the public and media about a topic or issue, often taking place in newspapers, broadcast media, and online platforms.

Examples

Public debate in media
The public debate over climate policy raged across newspapers, TV shows, and social media for several months.
During the election, the public debate on immigration shaped party platforms and influenced many voters' decisions.
Researchers examined how the public debate about vaccine safety affected people's trust in health authorities.
