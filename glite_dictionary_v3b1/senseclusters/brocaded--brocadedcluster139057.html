brocaded

adjective
decorated with a raised woven or embroidered pattern
Pronunciation: / broʊˈkeɪdɪd / (AmE), / brəʊˈkeɪdɪd / (BrE)
adorned with a raised pattern created by weaving, embroidery, or pressure, especially in fabrics.

Examples

A brocaded gown
She wore a brocaded gown adorned with golden threads.
The cushions were covered in brocaded velvet that shimmered in the light.
The palace ceiling featured brocaded fabrics that added to its luxurious feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embroidered - decorated with embroidery
  • antonym: plain - without decoration or pattern
