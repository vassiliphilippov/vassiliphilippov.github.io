trans-

x
prefix: across or through
Pronunciation: / træns / (AmE), / trænz / (BrE)
a prefix meaning across, through, or changing from one state or place to another

Examples

Trans-Atlantic flight
The trans-Pacific flight lasted over twelve hours.
A trans-continental railroad connects the east and west coasts.
The term trans-form means to change from one state to another.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cis- - prefix meaning on this side of
  • synonym: inter- - prefix meaning between or among
