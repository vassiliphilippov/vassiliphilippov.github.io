catechesis

noun
oral religious instruction in Christianity
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkætəˈkisis / (AmE), / ˌkatɪˈkiːsɪs / (BrE)
oral religious instruction given especially in preparation for Christian baptism or confirmation

Examples

Catechesis classes
During catechesis, the instructor encouraged questions about faith and doctrine.
Many churches offer weekly catechesis classes to help new members understand core beliefs.
The children received catechesis before their confirmation ceremony at the church.

Related Terms
