nymphette

noun
sexually precocious girl
Pronunciation: / nɪmˈfɛt / (AmE), / nɪmˈfɛt / (BrE)
a young girl, especially in literature, who is portrayed as sexually attractive or precociously seductive

Examples

A seductive nymphette
The author described the protagonist as a mysterious nymphette who captivated older men.
In the story, the character is often referred to as a nymphette because of her charm and youth.
Critics debated the portrayal of the nymphette in the controversial novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Lolita - a sexually precocious young girl, especially as depicted in literature
