gules

noun
heraldic red color
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡjuːlz / (AmE), / ˈɡjuːlz / (BrE)
the color red as used in heraldry, symbolizing courage or bravery

Examples

Field of gules
The family crest featured a lion on a field of gules to signify bravery.
In medieval times, knights often chose gules for their shields to represent courage.
The coat of arms displayed three golden stars on gules as a mark of honor.

  • synonym: red - the color at the end of the visible spectrum next to orange
