breacher

noun
leaping marine animal
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbriː.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈbriː.tʃə/ (BrE)
an animal, especially a whale or dolphin, that leaps out of the water and falls back with a splash

Examples

The guide explained that a breacher is often a whale showing off its strength.
Tourists cheered as a playful breacher soared above the surface before splashing down.
