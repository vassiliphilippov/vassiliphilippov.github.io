Salvadoraceae

noun
family of shrubs and trees in Gentianales
Pronunciation: / ˌsælvədɔːˈreɪsiː /; / ˌsæl.vəˌdɔːˈreɪ.siˌiː / (AmE), / ˌsalvədɔːˈreɪsiː /; / ˌsal.və.dɔːˈreɪ.siː / (BrE)
a family of Old World shrubs and trees in the order Gentianales, related to the olive family (Oleaceae), with four stamens and four petals
