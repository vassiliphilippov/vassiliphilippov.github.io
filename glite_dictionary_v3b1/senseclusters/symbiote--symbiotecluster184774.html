symbiote

noun
organism living on host
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmbioʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɪmbiəʊt / (BrE)
an organism that lives in or on another organism, typically benefiting itself while harming its host

Examples

Parasitic symbiote
The doctor explained that the symbiote was causing the patient's symptoms by weakening their immune system.
Scientists studied the symbiote to understand how it interacted with its host organism.
Removing the symbiote from the animal improved its health significantly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: host - an organism that supports another organism living on or in it
  • synonym: parasite - an organism that lives on or in a host and harms it
  • synonym: symbiont - an organism that lives in close association with another, often benefiting both
