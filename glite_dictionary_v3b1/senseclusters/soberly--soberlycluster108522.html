soberly

adverb
in a serious or calm way
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.bɚ.li / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.bə.li / (BrE)
in a serious, calm, and thoughtful manner; without excitement or exaggeration

Examples

To speak soberly
He spoke soberly about the challenges facing the company.
She addressed the committee soberly, her tone reflecting the seriousness of the issue.
The judge looked soberly at the defendant before issuing the verdict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gravely - in a serious or solemn manner
  • antonym: lightheartedly - in a cheerful or carefree manner
  • synonym: solemnly - in a formal and dignified manner
