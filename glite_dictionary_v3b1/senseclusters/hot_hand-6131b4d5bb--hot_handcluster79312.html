hot hand

noun
streak of success or good luck
Pronunciation: / hɑt hænd / (AmE), / hɒt hænd / (BrE)
A period during which someone experiences a streak of success or good luck, especially in games or sports.

Examples

A hot hand streak
After three successful projects in a row, his manager told him to take advantage of his hot hand.
She knew she was on a hot hand when she kept winning every round at the casino.
The basketball player had a hot hand and couldn't seem to miss a shot all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: winning streak - a period of repeated successes
