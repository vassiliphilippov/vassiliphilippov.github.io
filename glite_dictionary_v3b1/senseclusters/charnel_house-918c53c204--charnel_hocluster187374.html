charnel house

noun
building for storing corpses or bones
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt É…ëÀêrn…ôl ha äs / (AmE), / Ààt É…ëÀên…ôl ha äs / (BrE)
A building or vault where the bodies or bones of the dead are stored.

Examples

An old charnel house

The monastery included a charnel house where monks' bones were kept after burial.

Synonyms

    9.9 0.04/m

  • vault
    6.9 0.89/m
