burned

adjective
ruined by being overcooked (food)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /bɜːrnd/ (AmE), /bɜːnd/ (BrE)
(of food) spoiled by being overcooked or charred, often turning black or having a bitter taste.

Examples

Burned toast
He accidentally left the pizza in the oven until it was burned.
Nobody wanted to eat the burned cookies from the first batch.
The toast was completely burned and tasted bitter.
His omelette turned burnt because he forgot to flip it in time.
She apologized for the burnt cookies fresh out of the oven.
The toast was completely burnt and inedible.

  • synonym: charred - partially burned, blackened by burning
  • antonym: undercooked - (of food) not cooked enough
