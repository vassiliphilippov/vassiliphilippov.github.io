countermovement

noun
opposite movement
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌmuːvmənt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəməʊvmənt / (BrE)
a movement in the opposite direction to another movement, often to balance or counteract it

Examples

A quick countermovement
The gymnast used a quick countermovement to regain her balance on the beam.
A sudden countermovement of the gears prevented the machine from jamming.
The dancer's leap was followed by a graceful countermovement to the left.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opposing motion - a movement that goes against another movement
