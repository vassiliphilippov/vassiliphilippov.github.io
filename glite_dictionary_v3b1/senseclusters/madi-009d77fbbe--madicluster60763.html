Madi

proper noun
ethnic group or language
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdi / (AmE), / ˈmɑːdi / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group from South Sudan and Uganda, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Madi people
The Madi are known for their rich traditions and agricultural skills.
She is learning to speak Madi to communicate with her grandmother.
Many Madi people live in the northern regions of Uganda.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Acholi - an ethnic group from northern Uganda and South Sudan
  • synonym: Lugbara - an ethnic group from Uganda and the Democratic Republic of the Congo
