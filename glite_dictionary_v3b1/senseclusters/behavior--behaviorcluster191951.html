behavior

noun
the actions or responses of an organism
3.3
Pronunciation: / bɪˈheɪvjər / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjə / (BrE)
The actions, responses, or movements made by an organism, especially as observed in psychology.

Examples

Child's behavior
Her child's unusual behavior at school concerned the teacher.
Researchers measured the behavior of the monkeys after introducing a new toy.
The psychologist studied the behavior of rats in the maze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conduct - manner in which a person behaves
  • antonym: misbehavior - bad or inappropriate behavior
