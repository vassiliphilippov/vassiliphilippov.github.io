ECU

noun
vehicle control computer
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiː siː ˈjuː /; / ˈiː siː juː / (AmE), / ˌiː siː ˈjuː /; / ˈiː siː juː / (BrE)
a small electronic computer in a vehicle or machine that controls the engine or other systems
