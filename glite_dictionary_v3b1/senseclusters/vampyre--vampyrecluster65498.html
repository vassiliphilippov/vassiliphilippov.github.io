vampyre

noun
exploiter using charm
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈvæm.paɪ.ər/ /; / /ˈvæm.paɪr/ (AmE), /ˈvæm.paɪ.ə/ (BrE)
a person who preys on or exploits others, often using charm or seduction for personal or financial gain

Examples

A vampyre boss
He was known as a vampyre in the business world, charming his way to the top at others' expense.
Some novels portray the vampyre as a seductive figure who manipulates people for personal gain.
She warned her friend not to trust the vampyre who always seemed to take advantage of others.
