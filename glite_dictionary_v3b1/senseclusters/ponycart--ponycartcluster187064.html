ponycart

noun
small cart drawn by a pony
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊniˌkɑrt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊnikɑːt / (BrE)
A light, small cart with two seats, typically drawn by a pony, often having an underslung axle.

Examples

A ponycart ride
She drove the children around the farm in the old ponycart.
The antique ponycart was displayed at the county fair.
They enjoyed a leisurely ride in a ponycart along the country road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buggy - a light, horse-drawn carriage with four wheels
  • degree higher: carriage - a vehicle with wheels drawn by horses
