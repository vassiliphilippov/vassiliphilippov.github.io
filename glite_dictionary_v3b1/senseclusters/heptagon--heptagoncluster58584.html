heptagon

noun
seven-sided polygon
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛp.təˌɡɑn / (AmE), / ˈhɛp.tə.ɡən / (BrE)
A flat shape with seven straight sides and seven angles.

Examples

A heptagon shape
Each side of the heptagon must be equal for it to be considered regular.
In math class, we were asked to draw a regular heptagon using only a ruler and a compass.
The stop sign was shaped like a heptagon, making it easy to distinguish from other signs.
