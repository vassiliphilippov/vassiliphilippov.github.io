thran

noun
stubborn person
Pronunciation: / θræn / (BrE)
a person who is stubborn or obstinate, especially in Scottish or Northern Irish dialects

Examples

A thran person
No matter how much we argued, the thran would not change his mind.
She can be a real thran when it comes to following advice.
The thran in the group refused to compromise on any decision.
