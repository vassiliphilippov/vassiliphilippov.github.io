woodchuck

noun
North American marmot (groundhog)
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌt͡ʃʌk / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌt͡ʃʌk / (BrE)
a reddish-brown North American marmot, also known as a groundhog, found in wooded areas and fields

Examples

A woodchuck burrow
A woodchuck built a burrow under the old shed in our backyard.
During the spring, woodchucks can often be seen feeding near the edge of the forest.
The woodchuck is also known as a groundhog in many parts of the United States.
