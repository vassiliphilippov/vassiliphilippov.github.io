biogen

noun
life-producing agent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌdʒɛn / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌdʒɛn / (BrE)
a substance or organism that produces or originates life or biological material

Examples

A biogen substance
Researchers isolated the biogen to determine how it influences the growth of new cells.
The discovery of a new biogen could help explain the origins of certain microorganisms.
Scientists debated whether the mysterious biogen was responsible for the rapid development of life in the sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abiogen - a substance or process not derived from living organisms
  • synonym: biogenetic - relating to the production of life or living organisms
