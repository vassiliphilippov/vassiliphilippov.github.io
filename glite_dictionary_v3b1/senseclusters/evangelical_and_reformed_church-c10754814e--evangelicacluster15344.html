Evangelical and Reformed Church

proper noun
Calvinist Protestant denomination
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪkəl ənd rɪˈfɔrmd tʃɝːtʃ / (AmE), / ɪˌvan.dʒɪˈlɪk.əl ənd rɪˈfɔːmd tʃɜːtʃ / (BrE)
