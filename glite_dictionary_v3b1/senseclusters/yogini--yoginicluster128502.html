yogini

noun
female tantric deity
9.9
Pronunciation: / joʊˈgiːni / (AmE), / jəʊˈgiːni / (BrE)
a female supernatural being or goddess in Hindu and Buddhist tantric traditions, or a statue representing such a figure

Examples
