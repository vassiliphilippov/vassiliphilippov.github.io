presubmission

noun
stage before submission
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːsəbˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːsəbˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
the stage or process that takes place before something is formally submitted

Examples

Presubmission review
The team scheduled a presubmission review to ensure all documents were complete.
Attending a presubmission meeting can help clarify the application process.
We identified several issues during the presubmission phase that needed to be addressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preparation - the process of getting ready for something
  • antonym: submission - the act of formally presenting something for consideration
