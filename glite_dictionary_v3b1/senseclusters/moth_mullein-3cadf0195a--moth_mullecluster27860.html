moth mullein

noun
a species of mullein plant with yellow or purplish flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌθ ˈmʌlɪn / (AmE), / mɒθ ˈmʌlɪn / (BrE)
A European plant (Verbascum blattaria) with smooth leaves and large yellow or purplish flowers, often found as a weed in North America.

Examples

Moth mullein plant
Gardeners often remove moth mullein to prevent it from overtaking native plants.
The moth mullein has delicate yellow petals that attract pollinators in the summer.
The purple-flowered variety of moth mullein is less common but equally beautiful.
