reawakening

noun
becoming active again
4.1
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.ˈweɪ.kən.ɪŋ/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈweɪ.kən.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /riː.ə.ˈweɪ.kən.ɪŋ/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈweɪ.kən.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
