determinacy

noun
definiteness or precision
Pronunciation: / dɪˈtɜːrmɪnəsi / (AmE), / dɪˈtɜːmɪnəsi / (BrE)
the quality of being precisely determined, definite, or having a clear and fixed value

Examples

Determinacy of results
The determinacy of the experiment's results allowed scientists to draw clear conclusions.
Legal documents require a high level of determinacy to avoid misunderstandings.
Mathematicians value determinacy when proving the uniqueness of a solution.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certainty - the state of being sure or definite
  • antonym: indeterminacy - the quality of being uncertain or not precisely determined
