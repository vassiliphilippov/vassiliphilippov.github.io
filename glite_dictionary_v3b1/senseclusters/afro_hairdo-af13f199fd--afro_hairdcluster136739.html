Afro hairdo

noun
rounded, thickly curled hairstyle
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæfroʊ ˈhɛrdu / (AmE), / ˈæfrəʊ ˈheəduː / (BrE)
A hairstyle in which the hair is allowed to grow naturally outward in a rounded, thickly curled shape.

Examples

A Afro hairdo style
He decided to grow an Afro hairdo to celebrate his heritage.
Her Afro hairdo made her stand out in the crowd.
The stylist specialized in maintaining natural curls and shaping the perfect Afro hairdo.
