review

noun
critical evaluation article or essay
3.0 39/m
Pronunciation: / r…™Ààvju / (AmE), / r…™ÀàvjuÀê / (BrE)
an essay or article that evaluates and gives an opinion about a book, play, film, or other work of art

Examples

Book review
Before seeing the play, I always read at least one review.
She wrote a detailed review of the new novel for the newspaper.
The film received an excellent review from several critics.

Synonyms

  • critical review
    4.1 0.12/m
    a critical review offers a careful evaluative judgment and analysis of a work, while a review gives an opinion about that work.
  • critique
    5.6 15/m
    a critique gives a detailed, often formal analysis of an artwork's ideas and form, while a review gives a shorter opinion for general readers.
  • review article
    3.9 0.95/m
    a review article summarizes and evaluates research or works for scholars, while a review focuses on opinion and judgment about a single work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critique - a detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory
  • antonym: recommendation - an endorsement or positive opinion about something, in contrast to a neutral or negative evaluation
