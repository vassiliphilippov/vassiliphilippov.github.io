Synonyms

  • critical review
    4.1 0.1/m
    a critical review offers a careful evaluative judgment and analysis of a work, while a review gives an opinion about that work.
  • critique
    5.6 14/m
    a critique gives a detailed, often formal analysis of an artwork's ideas and form, while a review gives a shorter opinion for general readers.
  • review article
    3.9 0.5/m
    a review article summarizes and evaluates research or works for scholars, while a review focuses on opinion and judgment about a single work.
