Bimini

noun
boat sunshade
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪmɪni / (AmE), / ˈbɪmɪni / (BrE)
a collapsible canvas cover on a boat that provides shade, typically supported by a metal frame

Examples

Bimini top on a boat
The captain raised the Bimini to protect everyone from the afternoon sun.
We sat comfortably under the Bimini as the boat glided across the lake.
Before the rain started, they quickly unfolded the Bimini for shelter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awning - a sheet of material attached to a structure to provide shade
  • synonym: canopy - a covering that provides shelter or shade
