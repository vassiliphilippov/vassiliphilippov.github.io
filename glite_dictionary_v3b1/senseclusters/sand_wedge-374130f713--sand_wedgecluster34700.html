sand wedge

noun
golf club for escaping sand traps
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsænd ˌwɛdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsænd ˌwɛdʒ / (BrE)
a type of golf club with a heavy, angled head, designed for striking the ball out of sand traps

Examples

A sand wedge club
He used his sand wedge to lift the ball out of the bunker successfully.
Many golfers carry a sand wedge in their bag for tricky shots near the green.
The high loft of a sand wedge makes it ideal for escaping from sand traps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wedge - a type of golf club with a high loft used for short-range shots
