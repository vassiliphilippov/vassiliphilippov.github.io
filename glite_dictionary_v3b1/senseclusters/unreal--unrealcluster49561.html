unreal

adjective
informal: extremely impressive
5.1
Pronunciation: /ə.n.ˈriːl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈriː.əl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈriː.əl/ /; / /ə.n.ˈriːl/ (BrE)
(informal) extremely good, impressive, or surprising; used as an intensifier to say something seems extraordinary or hard to believe.

Examples

Unreal performance

Her performance in the play was unreal, earning a standing ovation from the audience.
The view from the mountain top felt unreal, like a photograph too perfect to be true.
