feel awkward

verb
experience social discomfort or embarrassment
1.0
Pronunciation: / fil ˈɔk.wɚd / (AmE), / fiːl ˈɔː.kwəd / (BrE)

Examples

To feel awkward socially

You might feel awkward asking for help, but it's completely normal.
