nose-pick

noun
picking one's nose or a person who does it
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊzˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊzˌpɪk / (BrE)
An act of picking one's nose; also, a person who engages in this behavior, often used disparagingly.
