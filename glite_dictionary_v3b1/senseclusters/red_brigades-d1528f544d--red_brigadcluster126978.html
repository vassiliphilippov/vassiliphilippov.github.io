Red Brigades

noun
Italian Marxist-Leninist militant group, 1960s–1980s
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɛd brɪˈɡeɪdz / (AmE), / rɛd brɪˈɡeɪdz / (BrE)
a Marxist-Leninist militant organization in Italy, active mainly from the late 1960s to the 1980s, known for acts of terrorism including kidnappings and assassinations, aiming to overthrow the Italian state and separate Italy from NATO.

Examples

Red Brigades terrorist group
Authorities intensified their efforts to dismantle the Red Brigades after several attacks on government officials.
Many fear the ideological legacy of the Red Brigades still influences fringe groups today.
The Red Brigades carried out a series of high-profile kidnappings during the 1970s.
