streak

noun
narrow line or band of different color
5.1 2.2
Pronunciation: /striːk/ (AmE), /striːk/ (BrE)
a narrow line, band, or flash of color or light on a surface

Examples

A bright streak
Lightning left a bright streak across the sky.
The stone had a reddish streak that made it unique.
There was a white streak running through her dark hair.
A brilliant streak lit up the night sky during the storm.
Suddenly, a streak of lightning illuminated the entire field.
We watched in awe as each streak flashed across the clouds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flash - a sudden burst of light or appearance
  • degree lower: glimmer - a faint or wavering light
  • synonym: stripe - a long narrow band or strip, typically one of several, that differ in color or texture from the surrounding surface
