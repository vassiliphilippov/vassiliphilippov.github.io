laboratory

noun
environment for testing or experimentation
3.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlæb.rə.tɔːr.ɪ/ (AmE), /lə.ˈbɒr.ə.tər.ɪ/ (BrE)
a situation or environment resembling a laboratory as it provides opportunities for observation, practice, and experimentation, especially for testing new ideas or policies

Examples

A social laboratory
His research team viewed the remote village as a unique laboratory for studying community economics.
The small country became a laboratory for innovative political reforms.
Urban schools often serve as a laboratory for new teaching methods.
