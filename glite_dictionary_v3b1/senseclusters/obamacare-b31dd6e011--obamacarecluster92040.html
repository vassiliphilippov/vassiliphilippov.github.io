Obamacare

noun
U.S. health care law
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbɑːməkɛr / (AmE), / əʊˈbɑːməkɛə / (BrE)
the informal name for the U.S. Affordable Care Act, a law that created a system to expand health insurance coverage in the United States
