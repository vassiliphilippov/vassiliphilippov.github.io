sustaining program

noun
unsponsored broadcast program
5.3 6.2
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈsteɪ.nɪŋ ˈproʊ.græm/ /; / /sə.ˈsteɪ.nɪŋ ˈproʊ.grəm/ (AmE), /sə.ˈsteɪ.nɪŋ ˈprəʊ.græm/ (BrE)
(in broadcasting) a program that is broadcast without the support of a commercial sponsor.

Examples
