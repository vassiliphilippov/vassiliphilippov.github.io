sustaining program

noun
unsponsored broadcast program
Pronunciation: / səˈsteɪnɪŋ ˈproʊɡræm / (AmE), / səˈsteɪnɪŋ ˈprəʊɡram / (BrE)
(in broadcasting) a program that is broadcast without the support of a commercial sponsor.

Examples

A sustaining program broadcast
Due to the lack of advertisers, the network decided to keep the series as a sustaining program for another season.
Many early television shows were sustaining programs before attracting commercial sponsors.
The radio station aired a sustaining program after its main sponsor withdrew support.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sponsored program - a program financed by a commercial sponsor
