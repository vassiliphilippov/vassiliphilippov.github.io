stem blight

noun
a fungal disease of plant stems
Pronunciation: / stɛm blaɪt / (AmE), / stɛm blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease of plants in which fungal infection causes decay and death of the stems

Examples

Stem blight disease
Farmers are advised to check their beans regularly for signs of stem blight.
Proper spacing between plants can help prevent the spread of stem blight.
The tomato crop was devastated by stem blight after several weeks of heavy rain.
