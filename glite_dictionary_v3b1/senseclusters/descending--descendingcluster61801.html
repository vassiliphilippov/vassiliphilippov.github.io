descending

adjective
moving downward or down
4.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
moving or directed downward; going from a higher to a lower position or level.

Examples

A descending balloon
She gazed at the descending balloon as it drifted toward the ground.
The descending sun painted the sky with brilliant colors.
The hikers followed a descending path into the valley.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ascending - moving upward; going from a lower to a higher position
