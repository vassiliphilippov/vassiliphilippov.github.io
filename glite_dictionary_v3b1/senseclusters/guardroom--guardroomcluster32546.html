guardroom

noun
military room for confining soldiers
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdˌrum / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdˌruːm / (BrE)
A room or cell in a military building where soldiers who are prisoners are held in confinement.

Examples

Prison guardroom
After breaking the rules, the officer was locked in the guardroom.
Punishments for minor offenses often included a night in the guardroom.
The guardroom was cold and sparsely furnished, with only a bench and a small window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cell - a small room in a prison or jail for confining a prisoner
