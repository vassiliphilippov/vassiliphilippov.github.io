Synonyms

  • come down
    4.2 0.2/m
    come down is used for computer systems or websites becoming unavailable, often suddenly, while go down is the general term for systems stopping.
  • crash
    3.2 5/m
    crash happens when a computer or program suddenly stops working after an error, while go down covers any system stopping.
  • fail
    2.2 19/m
    fail means a part or system stops working properly or breaks, while go down focuses on systems becoming unavailable.
  • break down
    3.2 8/m
    break down describes a machine or system that stops working due to damage or faults, while go down is broader.
  • go dead
    3.9 1/m
    go dead means a device or line suddenly becomes silent or stops, while go down is used for services failing.
  • go bad
    3.5 1/m
    go bad often means something spoils or stops working, especially parts, while go down refers to whole systems failing.
  • shut down
    2.9 20/m
    shut down means someone or a program turns a system off on purpose, while go down means it stops by fault.
