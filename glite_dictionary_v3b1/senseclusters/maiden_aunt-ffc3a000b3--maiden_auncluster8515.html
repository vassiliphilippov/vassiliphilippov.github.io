maiden aunt

noun
an unmarried aunt
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdn̩ ænt / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdn ɑːnt / (BrE)
an aunt who has never married

Examples

The maiden aunt
Her maiden aunt lived with the family for many years.
She inherited the house from her wealthy maiden aunt.
The children always visited their maiden aunt during the holidays.
