papillary

adjective
relating to a papilla or nipple-like projection
Pronunciation: / ˈpæpɪˌlɛri / (AmE), / pəˈpɪl.əri / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a papilla (a small, nipple-like projection or elevation, especially on the surface of the skin or a membrane)

Examples

Papillary renal carcinoma
Some kidney cancers are classified as papillary renal cell carcinoma.
The biopsy revealed papillary structures within the tissue sample.
The doctor noted a papillary swelling on the patient’s tongue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: papilla - a small, nipple-like projection or elevation
