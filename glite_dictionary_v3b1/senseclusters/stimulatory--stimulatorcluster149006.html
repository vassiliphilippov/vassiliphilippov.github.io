Synonyms

  • excitatory
    8.2 0.27/m
    excitatory causes strong nerve or muscle activity, while stimulatory means increasing activity across various tissues or processes.
  • facilitatory
    9.9 0.02/m
    facilitatory helps promote a process, often by easing neural signals, while stimulatory directly increases activity in tissues.
  • excitative
    9.9 0.01/m
    excitative acts like a drug to produce excitation in a body or cell, while stimulatory is a general term for increasing activity.
