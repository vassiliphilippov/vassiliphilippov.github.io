stimulatory

adjective
causing stimulation
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪmjəˌleɪˌtɔri /; / ˈstɪmjəˌleɪtɔri / (AmE), / ˈstɪmjʊlətəri / (BrE)
causing or increasing activity or stimulation in a process, organism, organ, or tissue
