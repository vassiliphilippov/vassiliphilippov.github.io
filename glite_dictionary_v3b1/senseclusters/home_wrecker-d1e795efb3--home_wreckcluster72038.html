home wrecker

noun
person causing a breakup of a marriage or relationship
5.1 7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊm ˌrɛkər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊm ˌrɛkə / (BrE)
a person who is thought to be responsible for the breakup of someone else's marriage or committed relationship, often by having an affair with one of the partners
