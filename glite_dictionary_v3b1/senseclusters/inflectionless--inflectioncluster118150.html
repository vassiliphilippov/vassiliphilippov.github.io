inflectionless

adjective
without vocal variation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛkʃənləs / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛkʃənləs / (BrE)
spoken or delivered without any variation in pitch, tone, or emphasis; monotonous.

Examples

Inflectionless speech
The actor's inflectionless delivery made the dramatic scene feel dull and lifeless.
He read the entire report in an inflectionless voice, making it hard to stay focused.
Her inflectionless speech failed to capture the audience's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressive - showing emotion or variation in voice
  • synonym: monotone - a way of speaking with no change in pitch or tone
