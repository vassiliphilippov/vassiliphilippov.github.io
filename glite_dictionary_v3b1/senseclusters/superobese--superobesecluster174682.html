superobese

adjective
extremely obese
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərəˈbiːs / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərəʊˈbiːs / (BrE)
having a body mass index (BMI) far above the standard threshold for obesity, typically indicating extreme obesity

Examples

Superobese patient
The hospital has a special program for superobese patients who need weight-loss surgery.
Doctors recommend different treatment plans for superobese individuals due to their higher health risks.
Special equipment is required to safely care for superobese people in medical settings.
