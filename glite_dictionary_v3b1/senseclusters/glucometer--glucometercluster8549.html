glucometer

noun
blood glucose device
Pronunciation: / ɡluˈkɑːmətər / (AmE), / ɡluˈkɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to measure the level of glucose in the blood, especially for people with diabetes

Examples

A glucometer device
The nurse showed him how to use a glucometer to monitor his blood sugar at home.
Many people with diabetes carry a glucometer to check their glucose levels throughout the day.
After eating, she used her glucometer to see how her blood sugar had changed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood glucose meter - a device for measuring blood sugar levels
  • antonym: sphygmomanometer - a device for measuring blood pressure
