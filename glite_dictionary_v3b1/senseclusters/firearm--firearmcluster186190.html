firearm

noun
portable gun; handheld weapon
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚˌɑːrm / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌɑːm / (BrE)
A gun that can be carried and fired by hand, such as a pistol or rifle.

Examples

A firearm licence
Carrying a firearm without a licence is illegal in many countries.
Police officers are trained to handle a firearm safely.
The suspect was found in possession of a loaded firearm.
