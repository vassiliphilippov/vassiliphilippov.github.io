fear

noun
anxious or unpleasant emotion about danger or threat
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / fɪr / (AmE), / fɪə / (BrE)
an unpleasant emotion of being afraid or anxious because you think something is dangerous or a threat

Examples

Fear of spiders
His fear of public speaking made presentations very difficult for him.
She felt a sudden surge of fear when she heard the footsteps behind her.
The child's fear of the dark disappeared as he grew older.
His fear of heights stopped him from climbing the mountain.
She could not hide her fear as she walked through the dark alley.
The sound of thunder filled the children with fear during the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • antonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one
  • degree higher: terror - intense, overpowering fear
