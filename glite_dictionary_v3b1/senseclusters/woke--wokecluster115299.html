woke

adjective
socially aware
7.5
Pronunciation: / woʊk / (AmE), / wəʊk / (BrE)
being highly aware of social injustices and issues, especially those related to race or inequality

Examples

Woke about injustice
She tries to stay woke by reading about current social movements and activism.
Some people criticize the woke culture for being too politically correct.
His woke attitude influences the way he talks about equality and justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aware - having knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • antonym: ignorant - lacking knowledge or awareness in general or about a specific topic
