sound projection

noun
penetrating quality of sound
Pronunciation: / saʊnd prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / saʊnd prəˈdʒɛkʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the capacity of a sound to carry clearly and penetrate distance, often making it noticeable in a space

Examples

Good sound projection
Good sound projection is essential for actors performing on stage.
The concert hall's design greatly enhances the sound projection of the orchestra.
This guitar is known for its remarkable sound projection and clarity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: resonance - the quality in a sound of being deep, full, and reverberating
