spat

verb
hit with a rain-like sound
3.9
Pronunciation: / spæt / (AmE), / spæt / (BrE)
to hit or fall on a surface with a sharp, repeated sound, like raindrops or bullets

Examples

Rain spatted windows
Machine-gun fire spatted across the muddy field, echoing through the dawn.
Pebbles spatted on the tin roof as the wind picked up.
Rain spatted against the windowpane all night long, lulling me to sleep.
Bullets spatted down around the soldiers hiding behind the wall.
During the hailstorm, ice pellets spatted noisily on the metal roof.
Rain suddenly spatted against the window, startling everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatter - to scatter or splash in drops or small particles
