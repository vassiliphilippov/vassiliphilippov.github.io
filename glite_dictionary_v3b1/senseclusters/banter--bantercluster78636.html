banter

verb
exchange playful teasing remarks
2.9 3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæn.tər/ (AmE), /ˈbæn.tə/ (BrE)
to speak to someone in a playful and teasing way; to exchange witty remarks with someone in a light-hearted manner

Examples

To banter with friends
She loves to banter with her colleagues during lunch breaks.
The two friends bantered back and forth, laughing at each other's jokes.
They bantered playfully about whose team would win the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insult - to speak to or treat with disrespect (opposite of playful manner)
  • synonym: joke - say something humorous or funny
  • synonym: tease - make fun of or provoke someone playfully
