lunacy

noun
foolish or senseless behavior
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnəsi / (AmE), / ˈluːnəsi / (BrE)
foolish or senseless behavior; extreme folly or madness.

Examples

His lunacy shocked everyone
Calling that dangerous stunt anything less than lunacy would be an understatement.
He couldn't believe the lunacy of driving in such bad weather.
The plan was pure lunacy and destined to fail from the start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: folly - foolishness or lack of good sense
  • antonym: sanity - the state of being sane or reasonable
