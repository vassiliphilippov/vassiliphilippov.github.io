lunacy

noun
foolish or senseless behavior
7.5 1.26/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀên…ôsi / (AmE), / ÀàluÀên…ôsi / (BrE)

Examples

He couldn't believe the lunacy of driving in such bad weather.
The plan was pure lunacy and destined to fail from the start.
