doll

verb
to dress up fancily
Pronunciation: / dɑl / (AmE), / dɒl / (BrE)
to dress or decorate someone, especially oneself, in a fancy or elaborate way

Examples

To doll herself up
She loves to doll herself up before every party.
They decided to doll the room with colorful streamers for the celebration.
Before the show, the actors would doll themselves up in elaborate costumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decorate - to make something look more attractive by adding items to it
  • synonym: dress up - to put on formal or special clothes
  • antonym: undress - to remove clothes from oneself or someone else
