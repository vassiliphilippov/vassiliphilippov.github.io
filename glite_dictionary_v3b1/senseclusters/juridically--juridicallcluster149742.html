juridically

adverb
legally, by law
Pronunciation: / dʒʊˈrɪdɪkli / (AmE), / dʒʊˈrɪdɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to law or legal principles

Examples

Examined juridically
The contract was examined juridically to ensure it complied with all regulations.
The dispute was resolved juridically rather than through informal negotiation.
All actions taken by the committee were juridically justified.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illegally - in a way that is not allowed by law
  • synonym: legally - in a way that is allowed by law
