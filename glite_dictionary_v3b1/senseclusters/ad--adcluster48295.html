ad

noun
advertisement; public promotion
Pronunciation: / æd / (AmE), / æd / (BrE)
an announcement in a newspaper, on television, on the internet, etc., that is intended to persuade people to buy a product or service or to support an idea

Examples

A ad on TV
During the show, several ads were aired on television.
She saw an ad for the new smartphone online.
The company placed a full-page ad in the newspaper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advertisement - a notice or announcement promoting a product, service, or event
