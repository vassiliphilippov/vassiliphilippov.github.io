whosever

pronoun
archaic possessive pronoun
Pronunciation: / huːzˈɛvər / (AmE), / huːzˈɛvə / (BrE)
an archaic pronoun meaning 'of whoever' or 'of whomever'; belonging to whichever person.

Examples

Whosever coat
Whosever coat is on the chair should take it before leaving.
The teacher asked whosever phone was ringing to turn it off.
Please return the keys to whosever desk they were found on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whoever's - belonging to whoever
