gemlike

adjective
resembling a gem
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of a gem, especially in beauty, brilliance, or clarity

Examples

Gemlike beauty
The necklace featured a gemlike pendant that caught the light beautifully.
Her eyes had a gemlike sparkle that made her smile unforgettable.
The lake's surface was so clear and gemlike that it reflected the mountains perfectly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking brightness or brilliance
  • synonym: sparkling - shining with bright flashes of light
