photoflash

noun
momentary light for photography
1.0 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfoʊ.tə.flæʃ/ /; / /ˈfoʊ.toʊ.flæʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfəʊ.tə.flæʃ/ /; / /ˈfəʊ.təʊ.flæʃ/ (BrE)
