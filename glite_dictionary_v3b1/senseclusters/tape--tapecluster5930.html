tape

noun
strip for binding or fastening
2.4
Pronunciation: / teɪp / (AmE), / teɪp / (BrE)
a long, thin strip of cloth, paper, or plastic used for binding, fastening, or tying things together

Examples

Sticky tape
He found some old cloth tape in the sewing box.
She used a piece of tape to tie her hair back.
The parcel was secured with a long strip of tape.
