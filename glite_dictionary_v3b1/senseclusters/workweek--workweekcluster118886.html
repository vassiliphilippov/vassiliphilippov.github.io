workweek

noun
number of working hours or days per week
4.1 1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːkˌwiːk / (AmE), / ˈwɜːkˌwiːk / (BrE)
The total number of hours or days that a person is expected or required to work in a calendar week, usually considered to be around five days from Monday to Friday.

Examples

A workweek schedule
He prefers a flexible workweek that allows him to start early and leave before rush hour.
My workweek is usually forty hours, but sometimes I stay late to finish projects.
The company is considering a shorter workweek to improve employee wellbeing.
