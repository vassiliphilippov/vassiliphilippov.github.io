dentition

noun
arrangement and type of teeth
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / dɛnˈtɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the number, kind, and arrangement of teeth in a person or animal

Examples

Human dentition pattern
Human dentition differs significantly from that of most other mammals.
The fossil's dentition suggests it belonged to a herbivorous dinosaur.
Veterinarians examine an animal's dentition to estimate its age.
