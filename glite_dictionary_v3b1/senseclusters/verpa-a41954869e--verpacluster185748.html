Verpa

noun
genus of mushrooms similar to morels
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜːrpə / (AmE), / ˈvɜːpə / (BrE)
A genus of edible mushrooms with a bell-shaped cap attached to the stalk only at the top, resembling some morels.

Examples

Verpa mushrooms
Scientists studied the habitat preferences of Verpa in early spring.
Some mushroom hunters confuse Verpa with true morels, but their attachment to the stem is different.
The forest floor was dotted with Verpa, easily identified by their bell-shaped caps.
