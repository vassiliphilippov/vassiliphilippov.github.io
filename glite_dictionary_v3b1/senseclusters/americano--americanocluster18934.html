americano

noun
diluted espresso coffee
3.5
Pronunciation: / əˌmɛrɪˈkɑːnoʊ / (AmE), / əˌmɛrɪˈkɑːnəʊ / (BrE)
a type of coffee made by adding hot water to a shot of espresso

Examples

A hot americano coffee
He prefers an americano in the morning because it is less strong than straight espresso.
The café offers a variety of drinks, but I always order an americano with breakfast.
After lunch, she enjoyed a hot americano while reading her book.

Related Terms

  • synonym: black coffee - coffee served without milk or cream
  • degree higher: espresso - strong coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans
  • synonym: latte - coffee made with espresso and steamed milk
