gynecologist

noun
doctor specializing in female reproductive system
Pronunciation: / ɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒɪst /; / ˌɡaɪnəˈkɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɡaɪnɪˈkɒlədʒɪst /; / ˌɡaɪniˈkɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
A doctor who specializes in gynecology — the medical care of the female reproductive system and women's sexual health.

Examples

She scheduled an appointment with her gynaecologist for her annual checkup.
