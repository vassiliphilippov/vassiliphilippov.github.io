cypher

verb
perform arithmetic calculation
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsʌɪfə / (BrE)

Examples

To cypher numbers

In the old schoolhouse, children would cypher sums on their slates each morning.
She cyphered out the total before handing in her account book.
