seismically

adverb
relating to earthquakes
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪzmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈsaɪzmɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to earthquakes or earth vibrations

Examples

Seismically active area
The region is seismically unstable and prone to frequent earthquakes.
Engineers designed the building to be seismically resistant to protect against tremors.
Japan is seismically active, so earthquake preparedness is very important there.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geologically - in a way that relates to the structure or history of the earth
