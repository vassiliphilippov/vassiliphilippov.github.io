the n-word

noun
euphemism for racial slur against Black people
Pronunciation: / ði ˈɛn wɝd / (AmE), / ði ˈɛn wɜːd / (BrE)
A euphemism used to refer to a highly offensive and derogatory word directed at Black individuals, without saying the actual slur.

Examples

Say the n-word carefully
Many people avoid saying the n-word out loud due to its offensive nature.

The teacher explained why it was wrong to use the n-word in any context.
