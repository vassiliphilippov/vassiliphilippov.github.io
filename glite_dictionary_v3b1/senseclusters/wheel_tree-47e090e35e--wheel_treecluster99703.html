wheel tree

noun
Australian shade tree with showy red flowers
Pronunciation: / wil triː / (AmE), / wiːl triː / (BrE)
An eastern Australian tree (Stenocarpus sinuatus) widely grown for shade, with glossy leaves and circular clusters of bright red or orange-scarlet flowers.

Examples

The wheel tree flowers
Botanists admire the wheel tree for its unique circular flower clusters.
Several wheel trees lined the avenue, brightening the street with splashes of red and orange.
The wheel tree is popular in parks for its striking flowers and broad shade.
