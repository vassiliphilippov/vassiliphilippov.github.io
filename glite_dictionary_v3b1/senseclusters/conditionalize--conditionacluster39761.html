conditionalize

verb
to train by conditioning
9.1
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /kən.ˈdɪʃ.ən.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to train or modify a behavior or response by associating it with a specific stimulus, especially in psychology

Examples

To conditionalize behavior
The psychologist tried to conditionalize the child's reaction to loud noises using positive reinforcement.
Researchers hope to conditionalize certain behaviors in animals for scientific studies.
Therapists sometimes conditionalize relaxation responses to help patients manage anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condition - to train or influence a response through repeated association
  • synonym: habituate - to make someone used to something through repeated exposure
