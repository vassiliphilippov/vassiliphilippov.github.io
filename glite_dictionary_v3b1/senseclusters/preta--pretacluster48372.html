preta

noun
hungry ghost spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpreɪtə / (AmE), / ˈpreɪtə / (BrE)
a spirit in Buddhist and Hindu belief, often depicted as a hungry ghost suffering from insatiable hunger or thirst

Examples

A preta spirit
In Buddhist folklore, a preta is doomed to wander the earth in search of food.
The monk explained that a preta suffers from endless hunger as a result of past actions.
Legends warn that a preta may appear to those who do not honor their ancestors.
