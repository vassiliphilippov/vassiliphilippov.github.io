transuranic

adjective
element with atomic number above 92
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænz.jʊˈræn.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌtranz.jʊˈran.ɪk / (BrE)
(of a chemical element) having an atomic number greater than 92 (that of uranium).

Examples

Transuranic elements
Handling transuranic waste requires stringent safety protocols due to its radioactivity.
Plutonium is the most well-known transuranic element produced in nuclear reactors.
Researchers studied the properties of several transuranic elements in the laboratory.
