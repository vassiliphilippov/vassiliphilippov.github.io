steroidogenic

adjective
producing steroids
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɛˌrɔɪdəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / stɪˌrɔɪdəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the production of steroids in the body

Examples

Steroidogenic tissue
The adrenal cortex is a highly steroidogenic tissue responsible for producing important hormones.
Researchers studied the steroidogenic effects of certain drugs on hormone levels.
A steroidogenic pathway is essential for the synthesis of cortisol and other hormones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonsteroidogenic - not producing or related to the production of steroids
  • synonym: steroid - a type of organic compound including many hormones
