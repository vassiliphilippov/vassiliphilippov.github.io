closeness

noun
extreme stinginess
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊznəs / (AmE), / ˈkləʊznəs / (BrE)
Extreme stinginess; unwillingness to spend money or share resources.

Examples

Money closeness
His closeness with money made him unpopular among his friends.
She was accused of closeness when she refused to contribute to the charity fund.
The old merchant was known throughout the town for his remarkable closeness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - willingness to give more than is necessary or expected
  • synonym: stinginess - unwillingness to spend or share; meanness
