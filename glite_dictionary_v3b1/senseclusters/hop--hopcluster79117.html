hop

noun
flowering plant used in beer making
8.4 4.7
Pronunciation: /hɑːp/ (AmE), /hɒp/ (BrE)
a climbing perennial plant with heart-shaped leaves and conelike flower clusters, whose dried flowers are used to give beer its distinctive bitter flavor

Examples

Hop plant harvest
Farmers harvest hops every summer to supply local breweries.
He grows his own hops in the garden for making homemade beer.
The bitter taste in this ale comes from the hops added during brewing.
