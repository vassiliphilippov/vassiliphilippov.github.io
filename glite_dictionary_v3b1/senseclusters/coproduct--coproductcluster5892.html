coproduct

noun
simultaneous byproduct
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌprɑːdʌkt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌprɒdʌkt / (BrE)
a product that is produced at the same time as another product during a manufacturing or chemical process

Examples

Industrial coproduct
Ethanol is a coproduct of corn processing along with animal feed.
The factory produces soap as a coproduct when refining vegetable oil.
During steel manufacturing, slag is often collected as a valuable coproduct.

Related Terms

  • synonym: byproduct - a secondary product made during the manufacture of something else
