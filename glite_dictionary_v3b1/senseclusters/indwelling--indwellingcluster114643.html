indwelling

adjective
existing as an inner force or spirit
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a quality, force, or spirit) existing or residing within something as an inner principle or presence.

Examples

An indwelling spirit
Many philosophers discuss the idea of an indwelling spirit that guides human actions.
Some scholars believe there is an indwelling sense of morality present in every individual.
The novel explores the protagonist's indwelling fears and desires through vivid imagery.
