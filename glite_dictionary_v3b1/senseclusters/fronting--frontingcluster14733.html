fronting

noun
serving as a cover
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfrʌn.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfrʌnt.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfrʌn.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈfrʌnt.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of acting as a cover or representative for someone else, especially to conceal the true owner or purpose, often for illegal or deceptive reasons

Examples

Fronting for criminals
The investigation revealed that the company was fronting for a criminal organization.
He was accused of fronting as the owner to hide the real investors.
Authorities suspect that several businesses are fronting for illegal activities in the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover - a person or thing used to hide the true identity or purpose
  • antonym: exposure - the act of revealing the true identity or purpose
  • synonym: proxy - a person authorized to act for another
