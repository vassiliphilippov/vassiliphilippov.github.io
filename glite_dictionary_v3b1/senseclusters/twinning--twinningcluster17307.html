twinning

adjective
bearing two offspring at once
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtwɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtwɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an animal, especially livestock) producing twins; bearing two offspring at a time.

Examples

Twinning sheep
Farmers prefer breeds with a tendency for twinning to maximize lamb production.
Improved nutrition has led to increased twinning in the herd over the last decade.
The ewe is known for her high rate of twinning compared to other breeds.
