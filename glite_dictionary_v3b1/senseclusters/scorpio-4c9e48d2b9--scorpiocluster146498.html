Scorpio

noun
a person born under the sign Scorpio
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔːrpioʊ / (AmE), / ˈskɔːpiəʊ / (BrE)
A person born between October 23 and November 21, when the sun is in the astrological sign Scorpio.

Examples

A Scorpio person
As a Scorpio, she values honesty in relationships.
He's a true Scorpio, passionate and determined.
Many Scorpios are known for their intense loyalty.
