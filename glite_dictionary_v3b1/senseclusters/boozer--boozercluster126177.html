boozer

noun
a habitual heavy drinker
Pronunciation: / ˈbuːzər / (AmE), / ˈbuːzə / (BrE)
a person who habitually drinks excessive amounts of alcohol

Examples

Heavy boozer
After years as a boozer, he finally sought help for his addiction.
He was known as the local boozer, often stumbling home late at night.
Lisa doesn't want to date a boozer because she's worried about his drinking habits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkard - a person who is habitually drunk
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol
