spartan

adjective
showing great self-discipline or austerity
6.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈspɑːr.tən/ (AmE), /ˈspɑː.tən/ (BrE)
showing or characterized by great self-discipline, strictness, and avoidance of comfort or luxury; austere

Examples

Spartan lifestyle
After the fire, the family lived a spartan existence while they rebuilt their home.
He led a spartan lifestyle, avoiding all unnecessary pleasures and comforts.
The athlete’s spartan training routine required early mornings and strict discipline.
He led a spartan lifestyle, refusing any unnecessary comforts.
Her apartment was spartan, with only the bare essentials for living.
The school's spartan rules demanded absolute punctuality and hard work from every student.

Related Terms

  • synonym: austere - strict, simple, and lacking luxury or comfort
  • antonym: luxurious - very comfortable or elegant, especially in a way that involves great expense
