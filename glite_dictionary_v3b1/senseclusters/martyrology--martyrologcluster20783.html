martyrology

noun
list of martyrs
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːrtiˈrɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌmɑːtɪˈrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
a written list or record of martyrs (people who died for their faith), often used in religious contexts or church liturgy
