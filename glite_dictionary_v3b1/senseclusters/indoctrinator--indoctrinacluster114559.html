indoctrinator

noun
one who promotes doctrine
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɑktrəˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɒktrɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person who teaches or imposes a set of beliefs or ideology, often without allowing questioning or critical thinking
