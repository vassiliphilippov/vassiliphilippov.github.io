secretive

adjective
tending to keep things hidden
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkriːtɪv / (AmE), / sɪˈkriːtɪv / (BrE)
tending not to reveal information or to keep things hidden from others; inclined to secrecy or reticence.

Examples

A secretive smile
She gave a secretive smile but refused to explain what she meant.
Their secretive behavior made everyone in the office suspicious.
Tom became secretive about his plans after the incident.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open - willing to share information; not secretive
