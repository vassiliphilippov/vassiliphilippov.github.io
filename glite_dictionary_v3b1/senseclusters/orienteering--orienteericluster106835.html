orienteering

noun
navigation activity
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔriənˈtɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɒriənˈtɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of finding your way across unfamiliar outdoor areas using a map and compass, often for training or recreation

Examples

Orienteering skills

The camp included a day of orienteering to teach children how to use a compass.
Many people enjoy orienteering as a way to combine exercise with outdoor adventure.
