rusty-red

adjective
reddish color resembling rust
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstiˌrɛd / (AmE), / ˈrʌstiˌrɛd / (BrE)
of a red color tinged with brownish-orange, resembling the color of rust

Examples

A rusty-red color

The fox had a beautiful rusty-red coat that shimmered in the morning light.
The old bicycle’s frame was a dull rusty-red after years of being left outside.
