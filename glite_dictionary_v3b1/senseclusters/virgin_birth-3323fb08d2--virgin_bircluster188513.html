(in biology) a form of asexual reproduction in which an offspring develops from an unfertilized egg; also called parthenogenesis.

Examples

Lizard virgin birth
Some lizards can undergo a virgin birth, producing several genetically identical offspring in a single season.
Scientists documented a rare case of virgin birth in a captive Komodo dragon last year.
Parthenogenesis, often called a virgin birth, allows some insects to reproduce without male fertilization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asexual reproduction - reproduction that does not involve the fusion of gametes or sexual fertilization
  • synonym: parthenogenesis - biological process in which an embryo develops from an unfertilized egg
