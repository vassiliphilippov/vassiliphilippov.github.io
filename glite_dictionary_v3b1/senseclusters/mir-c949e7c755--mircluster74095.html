Mir

proper noun
Russian space station
Pronunciation: / mɪr / (AmE), / mɪə / (BrE)
the name of a Russian space station that operated in orbit from 1986 to 2001

Examples

Mir space station
The Mir space station was a symbol of Russian achievements in space exploration.
Scientists from many countries conducted experiments aboard Mir during its years in orbit.
After more than a decade in space, Mir was brought down safely in 2001.

Related Terms

  • synonym: International Space Station - a large space station in low Earth orbit used by multiple countries
  • synonym: Skylab - the first United States space station, launched in 1973
