diwan

noun
collection of Persian or Arabic poems
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˈwɑn / (AmE), / diˈwɑːn / (BrE)
A collection of poems, especially in Persian or Arabic literature, usually composed by a single author.

Examples

A diwan of poems
He displayed a beautifully illustrated diwan containing centuries-old Arabic poetry.
Her doctoral research focused on the structure and themes within the diwan of Rumi.
The library houses a rare diwan by the celebrated Persian poet Hafez.

Related Terms
