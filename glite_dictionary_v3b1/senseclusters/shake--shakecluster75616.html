shake

verb
use a device gesture to trigger an app action
5.5 1.32/m

(in computing, mobile apps, and user interfaces) to move a handheld electronic device quickly from side to side or up and down so that software detects the motion and performs a specific action (for example, undoing the last change).

Examples

To shake the phone
When editing a document, you can shake the device to undo the last action.
During the game she shook her phone to roll the virtual dice and everyone laughed.
The app lets you report a bug by shaking the phone and selecting 'Send report' from the menu.
