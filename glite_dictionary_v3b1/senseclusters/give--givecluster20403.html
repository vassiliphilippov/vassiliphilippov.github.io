Synonyms

  • dedicate
    3.7 21/m
    dedicate means committing something completely to a person, activity, or cause, while give is more general and less complete.
  • devote
    3.7 40/m
    devote means using your time or energy fully for a purpose, while give is a more general act of offering.
  • commit
    3.4 40/m
    commit means promising your time, money, or effort to something long term, while give can be one simple action.
  • contribute
    2.7 102/m
    contribute means adding help, money, or ideas to a group effort, while give is a more general act of offering.
  • donate
    2.7 23/m
    donate means giving money, goods, or time to a charity or cause, while give is wider and less specific.

  • synonym: donate - to give money or goods to help a person or organization
