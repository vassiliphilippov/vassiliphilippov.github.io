Synonyms

  • plain
    1.0 16/m
    plain is simple, not decorated, and easy to see, while spare means very minimal in design or quantity, with little extra.
  • bare
    3.5 9/m
    bare shows only the minimum and often feels exposed, while spare focuses on simple, minimal design or small quantity.
  • unembellished
    5.8 0.07/m
    unembellished looks plain and lacks decoration, while spare can mean both minimal design and a low amount of something.
  • unornamented
    6.7 0.03/m
    unornamented has no decorative details and sounds formal, while spare is broader and can mean minimal quantity or simple design.
