lilting

adjective
having a light, rhythmic quality
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlɪltɪŋ / (BrE)
having a pleasantly light and rhythmic quality, often applied to music, speech, or movement.

Examples

A lilting melody
A lilting melody drifted from the open window, filling the street with music.
She recited the poem in a lilting tone that brought it to life.
The singer's lilting voice enchanted the entire audience.
