longyi

noun
traditional cloth garment (skirt/loincloth) in Myanmar and South Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋ.ji / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ.ji / (BrE)
A traditional garment consisting of a long piece of cloth, worn as a skirt or loincloth, commonly in Myanmar (Burma), India, and Pakistan.

Examples

A longyi skirt
He wore a brightly patterned longyi to the temple ceremony.
The longyi is popular for its versatility and vibrant designs among both men and women.
Tourists often purchase a longyi as a comfortable souvenir from Myanmar.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sarong - a similar garment of Southeast Asian origin, a length of fabric wrapped around the waist
