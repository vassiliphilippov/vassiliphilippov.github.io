junkie

noun
a person obsessively devoted to an activity or interest
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋki / (BrE)
An informal term for a person who is extremely enthusiastic about or addicted to a particular activity, interest, or hobby.

Examples

A news junkie
He's always been a news junkie, checking updates every hour.
My friend is a tech junkie who loves to try every new gadget.
She became a real junkie for yoga after her first class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - a person who is addicted to something, especially a substance or activity
  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
