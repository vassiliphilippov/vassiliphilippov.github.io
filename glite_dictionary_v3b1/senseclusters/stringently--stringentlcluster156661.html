stringently

adverb
in a strict or rigorous way
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪndʒəntli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪndʒəntli / (BrE)
In a strict, precise, or rigorous manner; with strictness or severity.

Examples

To stringently follow rules
Companies must stringently follow the guidelines to ensure compliance with the law.
Food safety regulations are stringently enforced in the restaurant industry.
The budget was stringently controlled to avoid any unnecessary expenses.
