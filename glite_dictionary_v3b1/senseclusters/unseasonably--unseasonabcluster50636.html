unseasonably

adverb
inappropriately for the season
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsizənəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsiːzənəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is not typical or appropriate for a particular season.

Examples

An unseasonably warm day
It was unseasonably warm during our December vacation.
The temperature dropped unseasonably low for late spring.
Unexpected rain fell unseasonably for this time of year.
