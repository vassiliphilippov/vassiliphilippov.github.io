clarinet

noun
single-reed woodwind instrument
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌklærəˈnɛt / (AmE), / ˌklærɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a musical instrument with a single reed and a straight cylindrical tube, producing sound when air is blown through it

Examples

A clarinet player
A jazz band often includes a clarinet among its instruments.
She has been playing the clarinet since she was eight years old.
The orchestra featured a beautiful clarinet solo in the second movement.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flute - a woodwind instrument without a reed, played by blowing across an opening
  • degree higher: oboe - a double-reed woodwind instrument
  • synonym: saxophone - a woodwind instrument with a single reed and a usually curved metal body
