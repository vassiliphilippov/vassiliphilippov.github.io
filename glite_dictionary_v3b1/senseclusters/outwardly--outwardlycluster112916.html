outwardly

adverb
relating to the outside or surface

in a way that relates to how something appears on the outside, not how it really is

Examples

Outwardly calm
Outwardly, the building looked unremarkable, but inside it was beautifully decorated.
She appeared calm outwardly, though she was extremely nervous.
The company is outwardly successful, but it has internal problems.
Outwardly, he seemed calm, but inside he was very nervous.
She was outwardly friendly to her colleagues, though she didn't trust them.
The building is outwardly plain, but the interior is beautifully decorated.
