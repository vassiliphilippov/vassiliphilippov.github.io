thatching

verb
to cover with straw roof
Pronunciation: / ˈθætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈθætʃɪŋ / (BrE)
covering a roof with straw, reeds, or similar plant material to provide shelter and protection from the weather

Examples

To thatch a roof
The workers spent several days thatching the cottage roof with bundles of dry reeds.
Traditional houses in the village are still thatching their roofs to maintain the historic look.
He learned the skill of thatching from his grandfather, who was a master thatcher.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roofing - the process of constructing or repairing a roof
  • antonym: tiling - covering a roof with tiles
