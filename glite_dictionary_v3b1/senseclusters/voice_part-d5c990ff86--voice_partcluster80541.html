voice part

noun
section of music for a singer
Pronunciation: / vɔɪs pɑrt / (AmE), / vɔɪs pɑːt / (BrE)
A section of a musical composition written to be sung by a particular voice or vocal range, such as soprano or tenor.

Examples

A voice part in choir
Each singer took a different voice part in the choir's performance.
She practiced her voice part until she could sing it perfectly without the music.
The composer wrote a challenging voice part for the alto section.
