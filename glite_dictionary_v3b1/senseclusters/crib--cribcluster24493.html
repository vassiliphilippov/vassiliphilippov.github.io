crib

noun
baby bed with high sides
Pronunciation: / krɪb / (AmE), / krɪb / (BrE)
A small bed with high sides, especially for a baby or young child, often made of slats to prevent the child from falling out.

Examples

A baby's crib
Before leaving the room, he made sure the side of the crib was securely locked.
She decorated the crib with a colorful mobile and soft blankets.
The baby slept peacefully in his crib through the night.
