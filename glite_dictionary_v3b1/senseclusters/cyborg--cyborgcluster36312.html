cyborg

noun
person part human, part machine
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪˌbɔrɡ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪˌbɔːɡ / (BrE)
A fictional or real person whose body is enhanced or replaced in part by mechanical or electronic devices.

Examples

A cyborg character
In many stories, a cyborg struggles to balance human emotions with technological power.
Researchers are experimenting with devices that could help create the first true cyborg.
The villain in the movie was a ruthless cyborg with superhuman strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: android - a robot with a human appearance
  • degree lower: human - a member of the species Homo sapiens
  • degree lower: robot - a machine capable of carrying out complex actions automatically
