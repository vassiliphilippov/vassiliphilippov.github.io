cattlewoman

noun
female cattle farmer or rancher
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬lˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈkætlˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who owns, raises, or tends cattle, especially as an occupation.

Examples

A cattlewoman on ranch
After inheriting the ranch, she became a respected cattlewoman in her community.
As a skilled cattlewoman, she managed the herd and oversaw the daily ranch operations.
The annual convention brought together cattlewomen from across the state to discuss best practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cattleman - a man who owns, raises, or tends cattle
