Durio zibethinus

noun
tropical Asian tree; source of durian fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʊrioʊ zɪˈbɛθɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈdjʊərɪəʊ zɪˈbɛθɪnəs / (BrE)
A tree native to southeastern Asia whose large, spiny, oval fruit is widely eaten and known as durian.

Examples

Durio zibethinus tree
Botanists studied the growth cycle of Durio zibethinus in its native rainforest habitat.
Many local farmers cultivate Durio zibethinus for its prized and pungent fruit.
The Durio zibethinus attracts tourists eager to taste the famous durian fruit.
