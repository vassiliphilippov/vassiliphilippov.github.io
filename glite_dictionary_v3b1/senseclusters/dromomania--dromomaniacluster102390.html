dromomania

noun
compulsive wandering
Pronunciation: / ˌdroʊməˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ˌdrɒməˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an uncontrollable urge or psychological condition that causes someone to travel or wander frequently and without clear purpose
