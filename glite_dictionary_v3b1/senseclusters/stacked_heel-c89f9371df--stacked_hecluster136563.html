stacked heel

noun
heel made of layered material
2.0
Pronunciation: / stækt hil / (AmE), / stækt hiːl / (BrE)
a shoe heel made from multiple layers of material, especially leather, stacked together to form a solid block

Examples

A stacked heel shoe
His new shoes featured a low stacked heel crafted from polished leather.
She preferred boots with a stacked heel for their sturdy comfort.
The designer added a stacked heel to give the shoe a vintage look.
