pennsylvanicus

noun
species name for Pennsylvania
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛn.sɪlˈveɪ.nɪ.kəs / (AmE), / ˌpɛn.sɪlˈveɪ.nɪ.kəs / (BrE)
a Latin species name used in scientific classification to indicate a species associated with Pennsylvania

Examples

Latin pennsylvanicus name
The scientific name for the American cockroach is Periplaneta pennsylvanicus.
Many species found in Pennsylvania have the Latin name pennsylvanicus to show their origin.
Researchers discovered a new beetle and named it Carabus pennsylvanicus after the state.
