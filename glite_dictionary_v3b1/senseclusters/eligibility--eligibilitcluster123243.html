eligibility

noun
state of being eligible
Pronunciation: / ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being qualified or entitled to be chosen or to participate

Examples

Financial eligibility
Financial eligibility for the benefit program depends on your total household income.
Questions about his eligibility to vote were quickly resolved by the election officials.
The eligibility requirements for the scholarship are clearly stated on the university website.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ineligibility - the state of not being eligible
  • synonym: qualification - quality that makes someone eligible or suitable
