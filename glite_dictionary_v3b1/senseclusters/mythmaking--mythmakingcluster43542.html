mythmaking

noun
creation of myths
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪθ.meɪ.kɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmɪθ.meɪ.kɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of creating or promoting myths or widely believed stories, especially in literature, art, or to shape beliefs
