Synonyms

  • optimistic
    3.2 23/m
    optimistic expects good things to happen and keeps a steady positive view, while hopeful focuses on expecting a specific good result.
  • sanguine
    7.4 1.13/m
    sanguine stays cheerfully confident even in hard situations, while hopeful expects a good outcome but may feel less steady.
  • rosy
    3.2 1.87/m
    rosy describes a bright, successful future, often as a picture, while hopeful means expecting a good result without that picture.
  • upbeat
    3.8 4.68/m

  • positive
    2.8 55/m
    positive shows confidence, certainty, or approval toward ideas or facts, while hopeful expresses a wish or expectation for a good outcome.
