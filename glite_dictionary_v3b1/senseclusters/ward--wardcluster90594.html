ward

noun
district for administration or elections
6.6
Pronunciation: / wɔrd / (AmE), / wɔːd / (BrE)
a district into which a city or town is divided for administrative or electoral purposes

Examples

City ward
Each city council member represents a ward in the municipal government.
New boundaries for the third ward were approved by the committee.
Voters in the southern ward turned out in record numbers for the election.
