cohousing

noun
shared living community
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌhaʊzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌhaʊzɪŋ / (BrE)
a type of community where people live in private homes but share common spaces and facilities

Examples

A cohousing community
Many families chose cohousing for its sense of community and shared resources.
The new cohousing development includes a common kitchen and gardens for all residents.
She enjoys living in cohousing because it combines privacy with a supportive neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: apartment complex - a group of apartments in one building or area, often with shared facilities
  • synonym: commune - a group of people living together and sharing possessions and responsibilities
