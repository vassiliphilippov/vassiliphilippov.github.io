ciderpress

noun
machine for pressing apples for juice
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdərˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdəˌprɛs / (BrE)
a machine for pressing apples to extract their juice, especially for making cider

Examples

A ciderpress machine
The old farm still has a wooden ciderpress in the barn.
They spent the afternoon feeding apples into the ciderpress to make fresh juice.
Tourists watched the traditional ciderpress in action during the autumn harvest festival.
