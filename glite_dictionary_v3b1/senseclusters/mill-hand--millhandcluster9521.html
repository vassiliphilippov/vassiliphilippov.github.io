mill-hand

noun
person who works in a mill or factory
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlˌhænd / (AmE), / ˈmɪlˌhænd / (BrE)
a person who works in a mill or factory, especially operating machinery

Examples

A mill-hand worker
During the industrial revolution, many families relied on income from a mill-hand.
My grandfather was a mill-hand in the textile factory for over two decades.
The mill-hand expertly maintained the machinery throughout his shift.

Related Terms

  • synonym: factory worker - a person employed in a factory
  • synonym: machinist - a person who operates machines, esp. in a factory
