Synonyms

  • indebtedness
    7.0 1/m
    indebtedness is the state of owing money or goods, while debt names the money or thing you owe.
  • financial obligation
    4.5 0.4/m
    financial obligation is a formal, legal amount someone must pay, while debt can mean any money or thing you owe.
  • liabilities
    4.8 0.03/m
    liabilities are sums or recorded duties a business lists in accounts, while debt is the general money or thing someone owes.
