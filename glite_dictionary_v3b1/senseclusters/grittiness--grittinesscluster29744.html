grittiness

noun
coarse texture
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪtinəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a coarse, sandy, or rough texture due to the presence of small hard particles

Examples

Grittiness of sandpaper
The grittiness of the sandpaper made it perfect for smoothing rough wood.
She disliked the grittiness in her salad caused by unwashed spinach leaves.
After the hike, he could feel the grittiness of dust on his skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roughness - the quality of being uneven or coarse to the touch
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even, flat, or gentle surface
