routinely

adverb
in a regular, usual way
Pronunciation: / ruːˈtinli / (AmE), / ruːˈtiːnli / (BrE)
In a way that is done as a usual practice; regularly and often without special thought or planning.

Examples

To routinely do tasks
He routinely eats breakfast at the same café every morning.
Supervisors routinely review employees' progress throughout the year.
The team routinely checks the equipment before each game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occasionally - not regularly or often; now and then
  • synonym: regularly - at regular intervals; frequently
