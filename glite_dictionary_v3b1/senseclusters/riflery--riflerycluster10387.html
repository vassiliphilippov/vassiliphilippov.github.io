riflery

noun
rifle shooting sport
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪfəlri / (AmE), / ˈraɪfəlri / (BrE)
the sport or skill of shooting with rifles, often as a competitive activity

Examples

Riflery competition
He practiced riflery every weekend to prepare for the national competition.
The school offers riflery as an extracurricular activity for interested students.
Her interest in riflery began after she attended a summer camp focused on shooting sports.

Related Terms
