posterior vein of the left ventricle

noun
a vein behind the left heart ventricle
Pronunciation: / poʊˈstɪriɚ veɪn əv ði lɛft vɛnˈtrɪkəl / (AmE), / pɒˈstɪəriə veɪn əv ði lɛft vɛnˈtrɪkl̩ / (BrE)
a vein that originates near the apex of the heart and drains into the coronary sinus, located on the posterior surface of the left ventricle
