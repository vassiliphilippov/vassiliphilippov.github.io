Examples

Synonyms

  • plummet
    5.8 8/m
    plummet means to fall or drop very suddenly and steeply in amount, while dive refers to prices falling quickly in value.
  • plunge
    3.8 8/m
    plunge means to move or fall quickly from a higher level, while dive is used for prices falling steeply.
  • crash
    4.8 4/m
    crash means a sudden, often severe market failure, while dive means prices fall quickly but do not always collapse.
  • slump
    4.1 2/m
    slump means a large drop that can last some time, while dive is a quick, steep fall in value.
