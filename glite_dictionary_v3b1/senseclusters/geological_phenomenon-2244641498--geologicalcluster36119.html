geological phenomenon

noun
natural event involving Earth's structure
Pronunciation: / dʒiˌɑːlədʒɪkəl fəˈnɑːməˌnɑːn / (AmE), / dʒɪˌɒlədʒɪkəl fəˈnɒmɪnən / (BrE)
A natural event or process involving the structure, composition, or physical features of the Earth.

Examples

A geological phenomenon like earthquake
An earthquake is a geological phenomenon that can cause drastic changes to the landscape.
The scientist dedicated his career to studying every geological phenomenon he could observe.
Volcanoes, as a geological phenomenon, play a vital role in shaping Earth's surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: natural phenomenon - an event in nature not produced by humans
