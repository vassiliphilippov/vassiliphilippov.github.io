psi

noun
unit of pressure (pounds per square inch)
Pronunciation: / paɪ / (AmE), / paɪ / (BrE)
a unit of pressure equal to one pound force applied per square inch of area

Examples

30 psi pressure
A pressure of 30 psi is recommended for these bicycle tires.
The hydraulic system operates at a maximum of 2,000 psi.
The tire pressure is measured in psi and should be checked regularly.
