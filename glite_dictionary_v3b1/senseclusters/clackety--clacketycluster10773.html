clackety

adjective, adverb
making clicking sounds
Pronunciation: / ˈklækəti / (AmE), / ˈklækəti / (BrE)
making or in a way that makes a series of quick, sharp, clicking or rattling sounds

Examples

We could hear the clackety sound of the sewing machine from the next room.

He walked clackety down the hallway in his hard-soled shoes.
