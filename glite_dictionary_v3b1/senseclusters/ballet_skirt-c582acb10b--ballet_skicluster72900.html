ballet skirt

noun
short skirt worn by ballerinas
Pronunciation: / ˈbæleɪ ˌskɝːt / (AmE), / ˈbæl.eɪ ˌskɜːt / (BrE)
A very short, typically lightweight skirt worn by ballerinas as part of a ballet costume.

Examples

A ballet skirt dress
She bought a new ballet skirt for her daughter's performance.
The dancer adjusted her ballet skirt before stepping onto the stage.
The shimmering ballet skirt complemented her graceful movements beautifully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tutu - a classical short ballet skirt made of many layers of tulle
