alkyd resin

noun
durable synthetic resin used in paints and adhesives
6.8 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.kɪd ˈre.zɪn/ /; / /ˈæl.kɪd ˈre.zən/ (AmE), /ˈæl.kɪd ˈre.zɪn/ /; / /ˈæl.kɪd ˈre.zən/ (BrE)
