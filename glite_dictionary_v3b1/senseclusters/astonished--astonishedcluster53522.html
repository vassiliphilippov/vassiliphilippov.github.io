Synonyms

  • astounded
    3.8 0.3/m
    astounded expresses overwhelming surprise and often feels more dramatic, while astonished means very surprised by something hard to believe.
  • amazed
    1.0 7/m
    amazed shows a sense of wonder and delight, while astonished highlights surprise at something unexpected or difficult to believe.
  • flabbergasted
    6.7 0.5/m
    flabbergasted sounds informal and extreme, showing speechless shock, while astonished is a neutral word for strong surprise.
  • dumbfounded
    4.6 0.5/m
    dumbfounded means so shocked that you cannot speak, while astonished describes strong surprise but not always speechlessness.
  • stunned
    3.0 1/m
    stunned can be physical or leave you unable to react, while astonished focuses on being very surprised or amazed.
  • staggered
    4.4 0.00/m
    staggered expresses being deeply shocked or overwhelmed, often in British use, while astonished is a more general word for surprise.
  • wonder-struck
    7.2 0.01/m
    wonder-struck suggests quiet awe and deep admiration, while astonished stresses sudden surprise at something unexpected or hard to believe.
