epauletted

adjective
with shoulder patches
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpəˌlɛtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛpəˌlɛtɪd / (BrE)
having natural shoulder patches, tufts, or markings that resemble epaulettes, especially in animals or plants

Examples

Epauletted fruit bat
The epauletted fruit bat is easily recognized by the white patches on its shoulders.
Some birds display epauletted markings during the breeding season to attract mates.
The guide pointed out an epauletted lizard with bright yellow tufts on its shoulders.

  • synonym: shouldered - having markings or features on the shoulders
