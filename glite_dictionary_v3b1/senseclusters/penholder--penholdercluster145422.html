penholder

noun
document drafter
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnhəʊldə / (BrE)
a person who is responsible for writing, drafting, or preparing an official document or text

Examples

Penholder of the report
As the penholder, she was responsible for drafting the official report for the meeting.
The committee appointed John as penholder to prepare the first version of the proposal.
Each project needs a penholder to ensure the document is completed on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: author - a person who writes a document or text
  • synonym: drafter - a person who prepares the text of documents
