tranq

noun
slang for tranquilizer
9.9
Pronunciation: / træŋk / (AmE), / træŋk / (BrE)
a slang term for a tranquilizer, a drug used to calm or sedate a person or animal

Examples

A tranq pill
The vet gave the dog a tranq before the procedure to keep it calm.
He took a tranq to help him relax before the flight.
Police sometimes use a tranq dart to safely capture wild animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sedative - a drug that induces calm or sleep
  • antonym: stimulant - a drug that increases alertness or activity
  • synonym: tranquilizer - a drug that calms or sedates
