trade edition

noun
book for general public sale
5.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /treɪd ɪ.ˈdɪʃ.ən/ /; / /treɪd ə.ˈdɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /treɪd ɪ.ˈdɪʃ.ən/ /; / /treɪd ə.ˈdɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a book published for sale to the general public, as opposed to a special edition or limited release

Examples

The trade edition book
Collectors often prefer limited runs, but most readers buy the trade edition of a book.
The trade edition of the novel was released to bookstores nationwide last spring.
Unlike the signed limited print, the trade edition is widely available to the general public.
