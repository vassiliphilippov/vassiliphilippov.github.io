tabbed

adjective
divided by tabs
Pronunciation: / tæbd / (AmE), / tæbd / (BrE)
organized or divided into separate sections using tabs, especially in software or documents

Examples

A tabbed interface
The new software features a tabbed interface for easier multitasking.
She organized her notes in a tabbed binder to quickly find each subject.
Many browsers now offer tabbed browsing to help users manage multiple websites at once.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuous - not divided into separate parts or sections
  • synonym: sectioned - divided into separate parts or sections
