physic nut

noun
tropical tree with purgative seeds used for dye
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪzɪk nʌt / (AmE), / ˈfɪzɪk nʌt / (BrE)
a small tropical American tree (Jatropha curcas) whose seeds contain a purgative oil and which is used for dye and tanning extracts

Examples

The physic nut tree
Farmers in some regions have started cultivating the physic nut for biofuel production.
Researchers extracted a purple dye from the bark of the physic nut tree.
The seeds of the physic nut must be handled carefully due to their toxicity.
