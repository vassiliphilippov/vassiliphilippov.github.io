Pinnipedia

noun
group of marine mammals (seals, sea lions, walruses)
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪnɪˈpidiə /; / ˌpɪnəˈpɪdiə / (AmE), / ˌpɪnɪˈpiːdɪə / (BrE)
a group of aquatic, fin-footed marine mammals including seals, sea lions, and walruses

Examples

Pinnipedia marine mammals

Marine biologists study Pinnipedia to understand how these mammals evolved from land ancestors.
