prog

noun
slang for program or music
Pronunciation: / prɑɡ / (AmE), / prɒɡ / (BrE)
a slang term for a computer program or for progressive rock music

Examples

A new prog
He spent the afternoon writing a new prog for his computer.
She listens to a lot of prog from the 1970s, especially British bands.
Many developers prefer to test their prog before sharing it with others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: program - a set of instructions for a computer
  • synonym: progressive rock - a style of rock music with complex structures
