pancetta

noun
Italian cured pork
Pronunciation: / pɑnˈtʃɛtə / (AmE), / panˈtʃɛtə / (BrE)
an Italian cured meat made from pork belly, usually sliced thin and used in cooking

Examples

Sliced pancetta
She added chopped pancetta to the pasta sauce for extra flavor.
The chef wrapped the chicken in pancetta before roasting it in the oven.
You can substitute pancetta for bacon in many Italian recipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prosciutto - Italian dry-cured ham, usually served thinly sliced
