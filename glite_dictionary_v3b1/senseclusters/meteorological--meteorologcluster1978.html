meteorological

adjective
relating to weather or atmospheric science
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science of weather and atmospheric conditions.

Examples

Meteorological data
She works as a meteorological analyst for a national weather service.
The meteorological data suggested a storm was approaching the coast.
The pilot checked the meteorological report before planning the flight route.
