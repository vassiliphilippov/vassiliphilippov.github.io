mid

adjective
slang for mediocre
Pronunciation: / mɪd / (AmE), / mɪd / (BrE)
slang for something that is average, mediocre, or not impressive

Examples

Mid performance review
The concert was just mid, so we left before it ended.
Some people say the new restaurant is mid, but I thought it was decent.
He called the latest episode mid because it did not meet his expectations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: average - typical or normal; not outstanding
  • synonym: mediocre - of only average quality; not very good
  • antonym: outstanding - extremely good; excellent
