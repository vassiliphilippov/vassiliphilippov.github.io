congeneric

adjective
belonging to the same genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːn.dʒəˈner.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dʒəˈner.ɪk / (BrE)
Belonging to the same genus as another organism or group of organisms.

Examples

Congeneric species comparison

Some diseases affect only congeneric hosts within the same family.
The two species are congeneric and share many morphological features.
