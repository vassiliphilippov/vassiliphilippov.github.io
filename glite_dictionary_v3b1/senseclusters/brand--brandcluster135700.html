brand

verb
mark an animal with a hot iron
1.4 2.5
Pronunciation: /brænd/ (AmE), /brænd/ (BrE)
to mark an animal, product, or item with a brand or trademark to indicate ownership or origin

Examples

To brand cattle
After the calves were branded, they were released back into the field.
It's illegal to brand horses without proper authorization in some regions.
The rancher branded all his cattle before sending them to pasture.
Farmers often brand their cattle to prove ownership.
If you brand your products well, customers can easily recognize them.
The company decided to brand all their new electronics with a distinct logo.

  • synonym: mark - to make a visible impression or stain on something
