bawk

verb
to cluck or hesitate
9.1
Pronunciation: /bɑːk/ /; / /bɔːk/ (AmE), /bɔːk/ (BrE)
to make a clucking sound like a chicken or to hesitate or show reluctance to do something

Examples

Hens bawk loudly
Every morning, the hens bawk loudly when someone enters the coop.
He started to cross the stage but bawked when he saw the large audience.
The children bawked at the idea of eating vegetables for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cluck - to make the characteristic short, sharp sound of a chicken
  • synonym: hesitate - to pause before doing something, often because of uncertainty
  • antonym: proceed - to go forward or continue with an action
