light

adjective
moving easily or nimbly
3.8
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
(of movement or a person) moving easily, quickly, and gracefully; nimble.

Examples

Light steps
He was admired for his light and agile movement on the soccer field.
She climbed the stairs with light and graceful ease.
The gymnast's light steps impressed the audience during her routine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsy - moving awkwardly or lacking grace
