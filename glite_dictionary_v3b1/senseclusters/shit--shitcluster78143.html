shit

noun
insulting term for a contemptible person
Pronunciation: / ʃɪt / (AmE), / ʃɪt / (BrE)
An insulting term for a person who is considered stupid, irritating, or contemptible.

Examples

He is a shit
Don't be such a shit when people are trying to help you.
He acted like a real shit at the meeting yesterday.
She's not a shit; she's actually pretty decent once you know her.
