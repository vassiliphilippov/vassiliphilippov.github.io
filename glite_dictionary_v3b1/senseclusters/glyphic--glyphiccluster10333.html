glyphic

adjective
like a carved symbol
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling glyphs or carved symbols, especially in art or writing

Examples

The ancient vase was decorated with glyphic patterns that told a story.
Archaeologists studied the glyphic inscriptions carved into the stone tablet.
The artist's new collection features glyphic motifs inspired by early civilizations.
The ancient temple walls were covered in glyphic carvings that told the story of the civilization.
Researchers are working to decode the glyphic symbols found on the stone tablet.
The museum displayed several glyphic artifacts from the Mayan civilization.
