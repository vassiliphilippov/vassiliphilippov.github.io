tap the screen

verb
touch a touchscreen lightly
Pronunciation: / tæp ðə skrin / (AmE), / tæp ðə skriːn / (BrE)
to touch a touchscreen device lightly with your finger, typically to select or activate something on the display

Examples

To tap the screen lightly
If the phone is unresponsive, try to tap the screen again.
She forgot to tap the screen to accept the call.
To zoom in on the photo, simply tap the screen twice.
