athymic

adjective
without thymus gland
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪˈθaɪmɪk / (AmE), / eɪˈθɪmɪk / (BrE)
lacking a thymus gland, often resulting in a weakened immune system

Examples

Athymic mice
Researchers often use athymic mice in experiments because they do not reject transplanted tissues.
An athymic condition can make an organism more susceptible to infections.
The laboratory bred athymic animals to study immune system disorders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immunodeficient - having a weakened or impaired immune system
