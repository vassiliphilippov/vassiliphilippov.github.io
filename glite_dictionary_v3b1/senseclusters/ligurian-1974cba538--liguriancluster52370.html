Ligurian

noun
Romance language of Liguria
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡjʊəriən / (AmE), / lɪˈɡjʊəriən / (BrE)
the Romance language spoken in the Liguria region of Italy and by some communities in nearby areas

Examples

Ligurian language
Many older residents of Genoa still speak Ligurian in their daily lives.
The Ligurian language has unique words and sounds not found in standard Italian.
Efforts are being made to teach Ligurian to younger generations in local schools.
