geopolitics

noun
political influence of geography and economics
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː.oʊˈpɑː.lɪ.tɪks / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əʊˈpɒl.ɪ.tɪks / (BrE)
The study of the influence of geography, economics, and demography on the politics and power relations of states or regions.

Examples

Global geopolitics study
Energy resources play a critical role in global geopolitics.
She specializes in geopolitics and often comments on international affairs.
The rise of China is reshaping the geopolitics of the Asia-Pacific region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geostrategy - the branch of geopolitics focused on strategic military planning and positioning
