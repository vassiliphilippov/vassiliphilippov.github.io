cubhood

noun
young animal stage
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌbhʊd / (AmE), / ˈkʌbhʊd / (BrE)
the state or period of being a young animal, especially a cub such as a young lion, bear, or fox

Examples

Bear's cubhood
During its cubhood, the bear learned important survival skills from its mother.
Stories of his playful cubhood were told by the older lions in the pride.
The fox's cubhood was spent exploring the forest with its siblings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adulthood - the state of being fully grown or mature
  • synonym: childhood - the period of being a child
