sella

noun
saddle-shaped structure
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlə / (AmE), / ˈsɛlə / (BrE)
a saddle-shaped anatomical structure, especially a depression in the sphenoid bone of the skull

Examples

Sella turcica
The pituitary gland is located in the sella at the base of the skull.
An MRI scan revealed an abnormality in the patient's sella region.
Doctors examined the sella to assess the size of the pituitary gland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sella turcica - the saddle-shaped depression in the sphenoid bone that holds the pituitary gland
