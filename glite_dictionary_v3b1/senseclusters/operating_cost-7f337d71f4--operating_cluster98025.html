The expense incurred in maintaining and running a property or business, such as for utilities, property taxes, and insurance, excluding depreciation and financing costs.

Examples

Reduce operating cost
Property managers work hard to minimize the operating cost of apartment buildings.

When budgeting for the year, we have to account for every operating cost to maintain profitability.
