Averrhoa carambola

noun
tree bearing star fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: /æv.ə.ˈroʊ.ə kær.əm.ˈboʊ.lə/ (AmE), /æv.ə.ˈrəʊ.ə kær.əm.ˈbəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
An East Indian tree of the wood-sorrel family that bears deeply ridged, yellow-brown, star-shaped fruit, commonly known as star fruit or carambola.

Examples

Averrhoa carambola tree
Because of its attractive fruit, Averrhoa carambola is often grown as an ornamental tree.
Scientists studied the medicinal properties of Averrhoa carambola leaves and fruit.
The Averrhoa carambola thrives in tropical climates and is cultivated for its star fruit.
