spider

noun
eight-legged arachnid that spins webs
2.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈspaɪ.dər/ (AmE), /ˈspaɪ.də/ (BrE)
A small arthropod animal with eight legs, belonging to the order Araneae, which spins silk to make webs or cocoons and often has venomous fangs.

Examples

A spider web

Some people are frightened of even the smallest spider they see indoors.
The garden was full of dewdrops clinging to spider webs after the rain.
