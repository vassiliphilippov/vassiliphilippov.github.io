haves

noun
wealthy people
5.2
Pronunciation: / hævz / (AmE), / hævz / (BrE)
people who are wealthy or have many resources, especially compared to those who do not

Examples

The haves and have-nots
The gap between the haves and the have-nots continues to widen in many countries.
Social programs aim to reduce the advantages enjoyed by the haves over others.
In times of crisis, the haves often find it easier to recover than those with fewer resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: have-nots - people who are poor or lack resources
  • synonym: privileged - having special advantages or benefits
  • synonym: rich - having a lot of money or resources
