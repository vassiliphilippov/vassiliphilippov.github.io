charcoal-gray

adjective
very dark gray color
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrkoʊl ˈɡreɪ /; / ˈtʃɑːrˌkoʊl ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːkəʊl ˈɡreɪ /; / ˈtʃɑːkɔːl ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
very dark grey in color, resembling the color of charcoal
