gimp

verb
cripple or restrict software/device
Pronunciation: / ɡɪmp / (AmE), / ɡɪmp / (BrE)
(informal) To reduce the effectiveness, features, or performance of a device, program, or system, often by removing or restricting functionality.

Examples

To gimp an app
After the update, the company gimped the app by removing offline access and several useful tools.
Many users believe manufacturers gimp older phones by limiting their processing speed through software.
The modding community warned that the patch was gimping gameplay balance and player progression.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cripple - to cause severe and usually permanent damage or to make ineffective
  • synonym: disable - to make something unable to operate or function
