unfigurable

adjective
not quantifiable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪɡjərəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪɡjʊrəbəl / (BrE)
not possible to measure, calculate, or express in numbers; not able to be quantified

Examples

Unfigurable amount
The impact of her kindness was truly unfigurable and could not be captured by statistics.
Some benefits of education are unfigurable, going far beyond what can be measured.
The value of friendship is often unfigurable in financial terms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immeasurable - too large or extreme to be measured
  • synonym: incalculable - impossible to calculate or estimate
  • antonym: quantifiable - able to be measured or expressed as a quantity
