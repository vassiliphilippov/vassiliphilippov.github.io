face time

noun
video call session
3.5
Pronunciation: / fe6as ta6am / (AmE), / fe6as ta6am / (BrE)
A scheduled session of live video communication with one or more people using a video-chat or conferencing platform.

Examples

A face time call
We scheduled face time with the client over Zoom to discuss the project.
She prefers face time for weekly catch-ups rather than long email chains.
The team set aside face time on Monday to review the latest design mock-ups together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person presence - being physically present with other people at the same location
  • synonym: video call - a telephone-like conversation in which participants see and hear each other over the internet
