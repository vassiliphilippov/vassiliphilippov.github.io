face time

noun
video call session
3.5
Pronunciation: /feɪs taɪm/ (AmE), /feɪs taɪm/ (BrE)
A scheduled session of live video communication with one or more people using a video-chat or conferencing platform.

Examples

A face time call

She prefers face time for weekly catch-ups rather than long email chains.
The team set aside face time on Monday to review the latest design mock-ups together.
