lovelock

noun
lock of hair as token
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌlɒk / (BrE)
a lock of hair grown long and worn, especially by men in the past, as a token of affection or devotion

Examples

A lovelock of hair
In old paintings, a gentleman might be seen with a single lovelock draped over his shoulder.
The poet wore a lovelock to show his devotion to his beloved.
During the 17th century, it was fashionable for men to grow a lovelock as a romantic gesture.
