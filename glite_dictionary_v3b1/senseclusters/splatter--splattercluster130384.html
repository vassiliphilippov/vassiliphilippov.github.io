splatter

noun
small amount of a liquid splashed onto something
6.9 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsplæt.ər/ (AmE), /ˈsplæt.ə/ (BrE)
a small amount of a liquid or moist substance that has been dropped or splashed onto a surface

Examples

A splatter of paint
A sudden splatter of rain hit the window as we talked.
She noticed a splatter of paint on her shoes from the art project.
There was a splatter of tomato sauce on his shirt after lunch.
