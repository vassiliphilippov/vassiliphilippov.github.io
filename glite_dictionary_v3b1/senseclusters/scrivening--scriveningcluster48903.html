scrivening

noun
written record
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪvənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪvənɪŋ / (BrE)
a document written or copied by a scrivener; a written record, especially from earlier times

Examples

An old scrivening
The historian discovered an old scrivening detailing the town's founding.
Among the artifacts was a scrivening listing the names of early settlers.
She marveled at the elegant handwriting in the ancient scrivening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manuscript - a handwritten or typed document, especially before printing
  • synonym: record - an official written account of something
