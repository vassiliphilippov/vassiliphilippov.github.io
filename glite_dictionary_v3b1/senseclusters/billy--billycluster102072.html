billy

noun
campfire kettle
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪli / (AmE), / ˈbɪli / (BrE)
a metal pot or kettle used for boiling water, especially outdoors or over a campfire

Examples

Campfire billy pot
The hikers boiled water in a billy to make coffee at sunrise.
He packed a small billy for cooking meals during the camping trip.
After fishing, they heated soup in the billy over the open fire.
