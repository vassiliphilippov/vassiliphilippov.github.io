napoleon

noun
layered custard pastry
Pronunciation: / nəˈpoʊliən / (AmE), / nəˈpəʊliən / (BrE)
A rectangular pastry made of thin, flaky layers filled with custard cream.

Examples

A napoleon pastry
For dessert, she ordered a napoleon topped with fresh raspberries.
Layer by layer, the chef assembled the perfect napoleon for the competition.
The bakery is famous for its delicate napoleons and éclairs.
