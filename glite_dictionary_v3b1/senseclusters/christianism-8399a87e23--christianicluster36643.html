Christianism

noun
Christian ideology
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃənɪzəm / (BrE)
the ideology, practices, or system of beliefs associated with Christianity, often emphasizing its political or cultural aspects

Examples

Christianism in politics
The rise of Christianism in politics has influenced many government policies.
Some scholars distinguish Christianism from the broader faith of Christianity.
Debates about Christianism often focus on its impact on cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christianity - the religion based on the teachings of Jesus Christ
  • antonym: secularism - the principle of separating religion from civic affairs
