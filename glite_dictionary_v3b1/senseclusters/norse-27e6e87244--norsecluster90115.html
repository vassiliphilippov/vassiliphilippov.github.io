Norse

noun
native or inhabitant of Norway
Pronunciation: / nɔrs / (AmE), / nɔːs / (BrE)
a person from Norway or Scandinavia, especially in ancient or medieval times (such as the Viking Age)

Examples

A Norse warrior
Artifacts from Norse settlements can still be found throughout northern Europe.
Many modern-day Norwegians take pride in their Norse ancestry and culture.
The ancient Norse were known for their seafaring skills and long voyages.
Archaeologists have uncovered remains of ancient Norse settlements in Greenland.
Many legends tell of brave Norse warriors and their adventures.
The Norse sailed across the Atlantic long before Columbus.

  • synonym: Viking - a seafaring Scandinavian warrior of the late eighth to early 11th century
