sharpshooter

noun
expert marksman, skilled shooter
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑrpˌʃuːtər / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːpˌʃuːtə / (BrE)
a person who is very skilled at shooting or hitting a target accurately, especially with firearms or in sports

Examples

Expert sharpshooter
A skilled sharpshooter can hit moving targets with remarkable accuracy.
She earned the nickname sharpshooter after winning several marksmanship competitions.
The army relies on a sharpshooter to take out targets with precision from long distances.
As a sharpshooter on the basketball court, she rarely misses a free throw.
The coach praised him for becoming the leading sharpshooter in the league.
The team's best sharpshooter scored four three-pointers in the final quarter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marksman - a person skilled in shooting at a mark; synonym of sharpshooter
  • synonym: sniper - a person who shoots from a concealed place, usually at long range; synonym of sharpshooter
