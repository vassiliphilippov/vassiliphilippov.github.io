equipotential

adjective
same potential everywhere
9.1
Pronunciation: /iː.kwɪ.pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ /; / /ɪ.kwɪ.pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ (AmE), /iː.kwɪ.pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ /; / /ɪ.kwɪ.pə.ˈten.ʃəl/ (BrE)
having the same potential or value at every point, especially in physics or mathematics

Examples

Equipotential surface
In an electric field, an equipotential surface means no work is needed to move a charge along it.
The map showed several equipotential lines representing areas of equal voltage.
Scientists studied the equipotential regions to better understand the distribution of forces.
