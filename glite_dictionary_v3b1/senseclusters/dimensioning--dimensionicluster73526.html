dimensioning

noun
specifying size and position (esp. technical)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɛnʃənɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛnʃənɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of indicating or determining the size, length, or position of objects or features in space, especially in technical drawings or measurements

Examples

Technical dimensioning
Accurate dimensioning is essential for producing reliable technical drawings.
Modern CAD software streamlines the dimensioning of complex engineering components.
The blueprint was rejected due to errors in the dimensioning of structural elements.
