First Council of Lyons

proper noun
1245 Western Church council excommunicating Frederick II
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːst ˈkaʊnsəl əv ˈlaɪənz / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˈkaʊnsəl əv ˈliːɒ̃ / (BrE)
the ecumenical council held by the Western Church in 1245 that excommunicated Holy Roman Emperor Frederick II and launched plans for a new crusade
