dressman

noun
male costume assistant
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɛsmæn / (AmE), / ˈdrɛsmæn / (BrE)
a man who helps performers put on and adjust costumes, especially backstage in theatre or film

Examples

Theatre dressman help
The dressman made sure every button was in place before the actor went on stage.
Backstage, the dressman quickly helped the performer change into a new costume.
A skilled dressman can handle last-minute wardrobe emergencies during a live show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costumer - a person responsible for costumes in theatre or film
  • synonym: dresser - a person who assists actors with costumes backstage
