counts/minute

noun
number of counts per minute
Pronunciation: / kaʊnts pər ˈmɪnət / (AmE), / kaʊnts pə ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
A unit of frequency representing the number of events, signals, or occurrences counted in one minute.

Examples

Counts/minute rate
Heart monitors often display a patient’s pulse in counts/minute.
The Geiger counter registered 150 counts/minute after the sample was placed nearby.
The technician measured the machine’s vibration at 30 counts/minute.
