automatic firearm

noun
firearm that fires automatically
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈfaɪɚˌɑːrm / (AmE), / ˌɔːtəˈmætɪk ˈfaɪəˌɑːm / (BrE)
A firearm that automatically reloads and fires repeatedly while the trigger is pressed.

Examples

An automatic firearm
Ownership of an automatic firearm is strictly regulated in many countries.
The military uses automatic firearms for rapid firepower in combat situations.
The sound of the automatic firearm echoed across the shooting range.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: semi-automatic firearm - a firearm that automatically reloads but fires only one shot per trigger pull
