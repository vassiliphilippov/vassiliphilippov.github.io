warfare

noun
competition or struggle between opponents
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrˌfɛr / (AmE), / ˈwɔːˌfeə / (BrE)
competition or active struggle between opposing forces or entities, not necessarily involving physical conflict.

Examples

Political warfare
Political warfare can be just as fierce as any battle on the field.
The companies engaged in intense warfare to dominate the smartphone market.
The rise in warfare between streaming platforms has benefited consumers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competition - the act of competing or striving for the same objective
  • antonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common goal
