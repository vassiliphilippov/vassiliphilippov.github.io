lifelong dream

noun
an aspiration held throughout one's life
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌlɔŋ driːm / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌlɒŋ driːm / (BrE)
an aspiration or goal that a person has had for most or all of their life

Examples

A lifelong dream
After years of studying, he realized his lifelong dream of becoming a scientist.
Climbing Mount Everest was her lifelong dream, and she finally achieved it at age fifty.
For many authors, publishing a bestselling novel is a lifelong dream.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambition - a strong desire to achieve something
  • antonym: fantasy - an idea that is unlikely or impossible
  • synonym: goal - an aim or desired result
