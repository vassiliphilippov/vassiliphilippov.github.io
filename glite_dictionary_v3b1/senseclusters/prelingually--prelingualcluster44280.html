prelingually

adverb
before language develops
9.5
Pronunciation: / priˈlɪŋɡwəli / (AmE), / priˈlɪŋɡwəli / (BrE)
in a way that occurs before the development of language or speech abilities

Examples

Become prelingually deaf
The child became prelingually deaf due to a genetic condition.
Researchers study how children who are prelingually blind learn to communicate.
Support services are essential for those who are prelingually affected by hearing loss.
