unsplinted

adjective
not joined or supported
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsplɪntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsplɪntɪd / (BrE)
not joined to or supported by adjacent parts, especially referring to teeth, dental prostheses, or structural elements.

Examples

Unsplinted dental implant
The dentist recommended an unsplinted implant to allow for natural movement.
In some cases, an unsplinted bridge is preferred for easier cleaning between teeth.
The engineer designed the structure with unsplinted beams to reduce material use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not connected to or relying on something else
  • antonym: splinted - joined to or supported by adjacent parts
