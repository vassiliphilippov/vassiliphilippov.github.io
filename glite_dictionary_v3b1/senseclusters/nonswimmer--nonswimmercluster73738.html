nonswimmer

noun
person who avoids swimming
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈswɪmər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈswɪmə / (BrE)
a person who does not swim, either because they cannot or because they choose not to

Examples

A nonswimmer stays shore

The camp provides special lessons for every nonswimmer to help them feel safe in the water.
As a nonswimmer, he always wears a life jacket when near the pool.
The cruise line requires all nonswimmers to attend a safety briefing before departure.
