outshoot

noun
branch from main stem
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌʃuːt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌʃuːt / (BrE)
a shoot or branch that grows out from a main stem or trunk of a plant

Examples

A plant outshoot
The gardener trimmed the outshoots to shape the bush.
New outshoots appeared on the tree after the spring rains.
If you cut back the outshoots, the plant will grow stronger.

Related Terms

  • antonym: main stem - the central stalk of a plant from which branches grow
  • synonym: offshoot - a branch or stem growing from a main one
