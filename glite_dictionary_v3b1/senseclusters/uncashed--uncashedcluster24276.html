uncashed

adjective
not yet exchanged
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæʃt / (BrE)
not yet exchanged for money, especially referring to a check or payment

Examples

Uncashed checks
She realized she had several uncashed checks sitting in her purse for months.
The company sent a reminder about the uncashed payment to the client.
Please deposit any uncashed checks before the end of the month.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cashed - already exchanged for money
  • synonym: unpaid - not yet paid or settled
