A metric unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a liter, commonly used to measure small amounts of liquid.

Examples

A milliliter of liquid

Please add 200 milliliters of water to the mixture.
The doctor prescribed 5 milliliters of medicine to be taken daily.
A standard teaspoon holds about 5 millilitres of liquid.
Each vial contains exactly 5 millilitres of serum.
The recipe called for 200 millilitres of water.

Synonyms

    1.0 0.04/m

  • cubic centimeter
    3.1 0.45/m

  • cc
