shorts

noun
short social-media videos
3.0
Pronunciation: /ʃɔːrts/ (AmE), /ʃɔːts/ (BrE)
(on social media) short vertical video clips created for quick viewing and sharing, typically lasting under a minute.

Examples

Social media shorts
Creators post shorts to reach audiences on social media feeds.
I watched three comedy shorts during my lunch break and laughed out loud.
Many channels rely on viral shorts to attract subscribers quickly and increase views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reel - a similar short vertical video format used on platforms like Instagram and YouTube
  • synonym: short-form video - very brief video content designed for quick consumption on social platforms
