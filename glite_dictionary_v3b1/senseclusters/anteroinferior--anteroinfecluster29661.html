anteroinferior

adjective
in front and below
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntəroʊɪnˈfɪriər / (AmE), / ˌæntərəʊɪnˈfɪəriə / (BrE)
situated in front and below a reference point, especially in the body

Examples

The anteroinferior region
The surgeon examined the anteroinferior region of the shoulder joint for signs of injury.
A fracture was detected in the anteroinferior portion of the vertebral body.
The ligament attaches to the anteroinferior aspect of the pelvis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anterosuperior - situated in front and above a reference point
  • antonym: posteroinferior - situated behind and below a reference point
