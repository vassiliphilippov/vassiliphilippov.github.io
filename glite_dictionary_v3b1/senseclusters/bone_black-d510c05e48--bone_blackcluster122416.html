bone black

noun
pigment made from carbonized bones
9.5 6.7
Pronunciation: /boʊn blæk/ (AmE), /bəʊn blæk/ (BrE)
a black pigment consisting of charred animal bones, used in paints, inks, and as a colouring agent

Examples

Bone black pigment
