schlieren

noun
density streaks in materials
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃlɪrən / (AmE), / ˈʃlɪərən / (BrE)
streaks or regions of varying density in transparent materials, visible when light passes through them under certain conditions

Examples

Visible schlieren patterns
The scientist observed schlieren in the heated air above the candle flame.
Imperfections in the glass created visible schlieren that affected the clarity of the lens.
Special lighting techniques revealed schlieren patterns in the transparent plastic sheet.
