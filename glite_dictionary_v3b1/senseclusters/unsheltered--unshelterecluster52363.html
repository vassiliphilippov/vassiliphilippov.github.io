unsheltered

adjective
emotionally unprotected
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɛltərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛltəd / (BrE)
emotionally or psychologically unprotected, lacking support, guidance, or experience and therefore vulnerable

Examples

Unsheltered feelings
After the loss of her mentor, he felt completely unsheltered in his new job.
Many young adults feel unsheltered when they first move out on their own.
Without friends to rely on, she became increasingly unsheltered and anxious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protected - kept safe from harm or danger
  • synonym: vulnerable - easily hurt or affected emotionally
