qual

verb
to qualify in sports
Pronunciation: / kwɑl / (AmE), / kwɒl / (BrE)
to take part in a qualifying event or achieve the required standard to advance in a competition.

Examples

To qual for finals
She hopes to qual for the Olympic trials at next month's meet.
If you don't qual in the heats, you can't compete in the finals.
He trained hard all season to qual for the national championships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance - to move forward to the next stage in a competition
  • antonym: eliminate - to remove from a competition, usually for not meeting the required standard
  • synonym: qualify - to meet the necessary standard to advance in a competition
