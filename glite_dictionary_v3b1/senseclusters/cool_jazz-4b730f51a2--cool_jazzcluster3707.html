cool jazz

noun
a relaxed, subtle jazz style
2.5
Pronunciation: / kuːl dʒæz / (AmE), / kuːl dʒæz / (BrE)
A style of jazz music characterized by relaxed tempos, fluid improvisation, and complex, subtle harmonies, often played in a restrained manner.

Examples

Cool jazz style
Many musicians in the 1950s embraced cool jazz as an alternative to the intensity of bebop.
Miles Davis is famous for his influential recordings in the cool jazz movement.
The cool jazz set last night featured smooth improvisation and subtle melodies.
