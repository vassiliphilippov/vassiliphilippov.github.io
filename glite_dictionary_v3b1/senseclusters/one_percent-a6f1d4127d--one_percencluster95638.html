one percent

noun
wealthiest 1% (socio-economic group)
3.4
Pronunciation: /wʌn pər.ˈsent/ /; / /wən pər.ˈsent/ /; / /hwʌn pər.ˈsent/ (AmE), /wʌn pə.ˈsent/ /; / /wɒn pə.ˈsent/ (BrE)
A socio-economic group consisting of the wealthiest one percent of the population, often mentioned in discussions of income inequality, wealth, and political influence.

Examples

The one percent elite
Critics argue that the one percent control disproportionate wealth and political influence.
After the tax reforms, many protesters claimed benefits favored the one percent over ordinary families.
Journalists often compare unemployment rates with how much income the one percent hold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economic elite - a small, privileged group that holds a large proportion of a society's wealth and power
  • synonym: the 1% - shorthand term for the wealthiest one percent, used in political and economic contexts
