sciaenid

noun
fish of the Sciaenidae family
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈiːnɪd / (AmE), / saɪˈiːnɪd / (BrE)
A fish belonging to the family Sciaenidae, which are carnivorous percoid fishes with a large air bladder used to produce sounds; includes drums and croakers.

Examples

A large sciaenid fish
A large sciaenid was caught near the river mouth during the annual migration.
Researchers studied the sounds produced by various sciaenid species in their natural habitats.
The Atlantic croaker is a common sciaenid found along the eastern coast of the United States.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum - any fish of the family Sciaenidae, known for making drumming sounds
