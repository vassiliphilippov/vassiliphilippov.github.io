crossbanding

noun
decorative wood veneer
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbændɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative strip of wood veneer applied across the grain of a surface, often used on furniture edges

Examples

Decorative crossbanding edge
The antique desk was restored with new crossbanding to match its original design.
Skilled craftsmen often use crossbanding to add elegance to cabinet doors.
The dining table's crossbanding contrasted beautifully with the main wood grain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inlay - a decorative technique of inserting pieces into a surface
  • synonym: marquetry - decorative work made from small pieces of wood veneer
