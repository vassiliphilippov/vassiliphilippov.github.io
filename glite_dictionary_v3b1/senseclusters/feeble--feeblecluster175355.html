feeble

adjective
lacking strength or effectiveness
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːbəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːbəl / (BrE)
Lacking strength, force, or effectiveness; weak or inadequate.

Examples

Feeble excuse
His feeble attempt at humor barely drew a smile from the audience.
She offered only a feeble excuse for her lateness, which no one believed.
The company's feeble response failed to address the main concerns of the customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weak - lacking strength or power
