blur

noun
hazy or indistinct representation
4.2 8.90/m
Pronunciation: / bl…ù / (AmE), / bl…úÀê / (BrE)
something that is hazy or indistinct in appearance, memory, or detail.

Examples

A moving blur
As the train sped by, the landscape became a colorful blur.
Her childhood memories are mostly a blur now.
The city lights at night were just a blur through the rain-soaked window.
