collectedness

noun
calm self-control
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛktɪdnəs / (AmE), / kəˈlɛktɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being calm and in control of one's emotions, especially in difficult situations

Examples

His collectedness under stress
Despite the chaos around him, his collectedness impressed everyone in the room.
She maintained her collectedness even when faced with unexpected challenges.
His collectedness helped calm the team during the emergency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composure - calmness and self-control, especially in a difficult situation
  • antonym: panic - a sudden, uncontrollable fear or anxiety
  • synonym: poise - a calm and confident manner
