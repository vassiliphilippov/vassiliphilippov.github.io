metabasis

noun
change of state
Pronunciation: / məˈtæbəsɪs / (AmE), / mɛˈtæbəsɪs / (BrE)
a change or transition from one state, condition, or form to another

Examples

A sudden metabasis
The scientist observed a rapid metabasis in the chemical reaction as the temperature increased.
After years of gradual change, the country finally underwent a political metabasis.
Literature often explores the metabasis from innocence to experience in its characters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stability - the state of being steady and unchanging
  • synonym: transformation - a thorough or dramatic change in form or appearance
  • synonym: transition - the process of changing from one state or condition to another
