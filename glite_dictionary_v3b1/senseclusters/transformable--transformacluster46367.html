transformable

adjective
capable of being transformed
1.0
Pronunciation: / trænsˈfɔrməbəl / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːməbəl / (BrE)
Capable of being changed in form, appearance, or substance, often in a significant or fundamental way.

Examples

A transformable object
Some believe the mind is more transformable than previously thought.
The material is transformable under high temperatures and pressure.
This software uses a transformable algorithm to adapt to different data types.

  • antonym: immutable - unable to be changed
