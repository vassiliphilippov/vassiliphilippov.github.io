Feldene

noun
an anti-inflammatory drug (piroxicam) brand name
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛlˌdiːn / (AmE), / ˈfel.diːn / (BrE)
A trademarked brand name for piroxicam, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory conditions.

Examples

Feldene medicine

She asked the pharmacist if Feldene was safe to take with her other medications.
The doctor prescribed Feldene to help manage her arthritis symptoms.
