purple clover

noun
a flowering forage plant; red clover
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝpəl ˈkloʊvɚ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ˈkləʊvə / (BrE)
a short-lived perennial plant with red-purple to pink flowers, widely grown as forage; also known as red clover.

Examples

Dried purple clover
Dried purple clover is often used as nutritious fodder for livestock.
Honeybees are attracted to the vibrant blooms of purple clover in the summer.
The farmer planted purple clover across the field to improve the soil's fertility.
