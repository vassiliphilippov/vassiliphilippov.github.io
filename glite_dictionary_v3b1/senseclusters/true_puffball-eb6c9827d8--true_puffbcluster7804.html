true puffball

noun
type of round spore-releasing fungus
Pronunciation: / tru ˈpʌfˌbɔl / (AmE), / truː ˈpʌf.bɔːl / (BrE)
a round fungus of the family Lycoperdaceae that emits a cloud of spores when mature
