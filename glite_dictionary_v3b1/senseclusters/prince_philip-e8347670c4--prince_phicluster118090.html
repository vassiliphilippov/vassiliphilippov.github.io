Prince Philip

proper noun
husband of Queen Elizabeth II
Pronunciation: / prɪns ˈfɪlɪp / (AmE), / prɪns ˈfɪlɪp / (BrE)
(1921–2021) The husband of Queen Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom; born Prince Philip of Greece and Denmark, later Duke of Edinburgh.
