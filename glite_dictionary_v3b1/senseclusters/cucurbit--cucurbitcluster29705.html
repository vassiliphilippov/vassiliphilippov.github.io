cucurbit

noun
plant of the gourd family (Cucurbitaceae)
Pronunciation: / kjuˈkɝː.bɪt / (AmE), / kjuːˈkɜː.bɪt / (BrE)
Any plant belonging to the gourd family (Cucurbitaceae), such as cucumbers, melons, squashes, and pumpkins.

Examples

A cucurbit plant
Many crops in the garden, including pumpkins and squash, are types of cucurbit.
Powdery mildew is a common disease affecting cucurbit plants worldwide.
Researchers are studying ways to improve resistance to viruses in cucurbit varieties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gourd - a plant of the family Cucurbitaceae, grown for its fruit
  • synonym: pumpkin - large orange fruit of a cucurbit plant
