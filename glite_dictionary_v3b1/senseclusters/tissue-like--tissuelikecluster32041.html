tissue-like

adjective
resembling tissue
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪʃuˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɪsjuˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of biological tissue, especially in texture or appearance

Examples

Tissue-like material for implants
The scientist developed a tissue-like material for use in medical implants.
Its tissue-like consistency makes the fabric comfortable to wear for long periods.
Engineers are studying tissue-like structures to create more flexible robotics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fleshy - having a soft, thick, or tissue-like texture
  • antonym: rigid - stiff and not tissue-like in texture
