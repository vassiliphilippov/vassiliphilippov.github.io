increase

noun
a rise in amount or number
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkriːs /; / ˈɪnˌkris / (AmE), / ɪnˈkriːs /; / ˈɪŋkriːs / (BrE)
the amount by which something becomes larger or greater; the added quantity

Examples

A five percent increase
A five percent increase in salary was announced for all employees.
The increase in temperature was evident throughout the week.
We noticed a significant increase in sales during the holiday season.
An increase of five percent in property taxes was announced this year.
The doctor noted a gradual increase in the patient's weight over the past month.
There was a significant increase in sales after the new product launched.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decrease - the amount by which something becomes less
  • synonym: gain - an increase in amount, especially of money or weight
  • synonym: rise - an upward change or increase
