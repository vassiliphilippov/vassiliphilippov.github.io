supervising

noun
the act of overseeing others' work
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌvaɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌvaɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of overseeing or directing the work or activities of others

Examples

Supervising a team
Her job involved supervising a team of junior researchers.
Successful supervising requires both expertise and excellent communication skills.
The company implemented new guidelines for supervising remote workers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: supervision - the act of watching and directing what someone does or how something is done
