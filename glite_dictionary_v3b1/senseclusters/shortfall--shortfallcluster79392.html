shortfall

noun
amount lacking or missing
5.5 4.03/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…îrtÀåf…îl / (AmE), / Àà É…îÀêtf…îÀêl / (BrE)
an amount by which something is less than expected, needed, or required

Examples

A shortfall in money
Farmers are concerned about a shortfall in crop yields after the drought.
The company faced a shortfall in profits due to weaker than expected sales.
There is a significant shortfall in funding for public education this year.

Synonyms

  • deficit
    4.3 42/m
    deficit usually means a money or budget lack, while shortfall means any amount missing compared with what was expected or needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deficit - the amount by which something is too small
  • antonym: surplus - an amount that is more than needed
