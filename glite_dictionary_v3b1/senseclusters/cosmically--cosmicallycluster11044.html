cosmically

adverb
extremely; intensely
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑzmɪkli / (AmE), / ˈkɒzmɪkli / (BrE)
to an extremely great or extraordinary degree; used informally as an intensifier.

Examples

Cosmically important event
He was cosmically disappointed when his favorite team lost the championship.
The movie was cosmically boring, and I struggled to stay awake until the end.
She felt cosmically lucky to have such supportive friends during a difficult time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hugely - to a very large degree
  • synonym: immensely - to a very great extent
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree
