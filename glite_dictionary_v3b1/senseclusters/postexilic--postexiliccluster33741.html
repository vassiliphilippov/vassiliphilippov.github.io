postexilic

adjective
relating to the period after the Babylonian Exile
9.9
Pronunciation: /poʊst.ɪg.ˈzɪl.ɪk/ (AmE), /pəʊst.ɪg.ˈzɪl.ɪk/ (BrE)
Relating to the period in Jewish history following the end of the Babylonian Captivity in 539 BC.

Examples

The postexilic period
Many scholars study the postexilic prophets to understand Jewish religious reforms.
Several biblical books were written in the postexilic era.
The rebuilding of the Temple occurred during the postexilic period.
