gross

adjective
coarse or indecent; vulgar
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊs / (AmE), / ɡrəʊs / (BrE)
very unpleasant, dirty, disgusting, or offensive

Examples

Gross behavior
His gross remarks during the meeting shocked everyone present.

The comedian was criticized for telling gross jokes that offended the audience.
The kitchen was so gross that no one wanted to eat there.
He found a gross stain on the carpet and quickly cleaned it up.
The smell coming from the trash can was absolutely gross.
