stupa

noun
Buddhist dome-shaped shrine
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːpə / (AmE), / ˈstjuːpə / (BrE)
a dome-shaped structure erected as a Buddhist shrine, often containing relics and used as a place of meditation

Examples

A stupa shrine
Inside the stupa are relics believed to belong to Buddhist saints.
Many pilgrims come to meditate and pay respects at the stupa every year.
The ancient stupa at Sanchi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in India.
