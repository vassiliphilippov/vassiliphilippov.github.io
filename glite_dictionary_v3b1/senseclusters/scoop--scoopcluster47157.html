scoop

noun
bucket/shovel part of excavating machinery
1.0
Pronunciation: / skuːp / (AmE), / skuːp / (BrE)
a tool or utensil with a deep, curved bowl for serving or moving things like ice cream, or the bucket part of a machine used for digging or lifting

Examples

An ice cream scoop
After each use, the scoop was rinsed to prevent material from hardening inside it.
Mud and rocks filled the scoop of the backhoe as it dug into the ground.
The operator lowered the scoop into the trench to remove the debris.
Please hand me the scoop so I can serve the punch.
She picked up the scoop and dished out the mashed potatoes onto their plates.
The server used a metal scoop to portion out three big balls of frozen yogurt.

  • synonym: ladle - a large long-handled spoon with a cup-shaped bowl, used for serving liquids
