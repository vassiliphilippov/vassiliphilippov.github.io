scanning

noun
converting photos to digital format
Pronunciation: / ˈskænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskanɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of converting photographs into a digital form that can be recognized and processed by a computer

Examples

Photo scanning
High-resolution scanning ensures that even small details from the original photo are preserved.
Modern libraries use scanning to create digital archives of historic photographs.
The old family albums required scanning so the photographs could be stored safely on a hard drive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digitization - the process of converting information into a digital format
