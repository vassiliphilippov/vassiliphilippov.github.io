pothunting

noun
illegal artifact collecting
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtˌhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɒtˌhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the illegal searching of archaeological sites or graves to remove artifacts for personal gain or sale

Examples

Illegal pothunting at ruins
The museum condemned the pothunting that destroyed valuable historical evidence at the ancient site.
Strict laws have been enacted to prevent pothunting in protected archaeological areas.
Authorities arrested three men for pothunting after they were found digging in a protected Bronze Age barrow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excavation - the careful digging and study of archaeological sites
  • synonym: looting - the act of stealing goods, especially during war or disorder
