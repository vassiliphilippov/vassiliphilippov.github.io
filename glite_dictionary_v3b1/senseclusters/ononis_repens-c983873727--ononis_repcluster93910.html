Ononis repens

noun
European woody plant; creeping restharrow
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈnoʊnɪs rɪˈpɛnz / (AmE), / əˈnəʊnɪs rɪˈpɛnz / (BrE)
A European woody plant with pink flowers, unifoliate leaves, and long tough roots, known for spreading by underground runners; commonly called creeping restharrow.
