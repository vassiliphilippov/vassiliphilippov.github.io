polysemous

adjective
having more than one meaning (of words)
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪsɪməs / (AmE), / pɒˈlɪsɪməs / (BrE)
(of a word) having multiple meanings or senses.

Polysemous word

Researchers analyzed how polysemous words are understood in different contexts.
