trade wind

noun
steady east-to-west wind near the equator
5.1 3.2
Pronunciation: / treɪd wɪnd /; / ˈtreɪd ˌwɪnd / (AmE), / treɪd wɪnd /; / ˈtreɪd ˌwɪnd / (BrE)
a steady wind that blows from east to west in the Earth's tropical regions, especially just north or south of the equator
