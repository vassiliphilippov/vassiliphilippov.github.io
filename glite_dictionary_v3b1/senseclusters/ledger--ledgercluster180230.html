ledger

noun
decentralized digital record (blockchain)

a digital record of transactions maintained across a network of computers so that entries are shared, verifiable, and tamper-resistant; often used in blockchain and distributed-ledger systems.

Examples

Blockchain ledger
The company's transactions were recorded on a public ledger maintained by thousands of computers worldwide.
Before accepting the payment, she checked the private ledger to confirm the transfer's timestamp and amount.
Researchers prefer distributed systems because a tamper-resistant ledger helps prevent fraudulent changes to recorded data.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockchain - a type of distributed ledger that stores data in linked blocks secured by cryptography.
  • synonym: distributed ledger - a database that is shared and synchronized across multiple sites or institutions; records transactions across a network.
