winter savory

noun
herb used as seasoning for meats
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈsæv.ɚ.i /; / ˈwɪntər ˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈsæv.ər.i /; / ˈwɪntə ˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
an aromatic herb (Satureja montana) with a peppery, thyme-like flavor, used as a seasoning in cooking
