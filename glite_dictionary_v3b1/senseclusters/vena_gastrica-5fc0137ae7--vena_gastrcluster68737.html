vena gastrica

noun
vein draining the stomach walls
3.0 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə ˈgæs.trɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈveɪ.nə ˈgæs.trɪ.kə/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə ˈgæs.trɪ.kə/ /; / /ˈveɪ.nə ˈgæs.trɪ.kə/ (BrE)
any of several veins that drain blood from the walls of the stomach

Examples

The vena gastrica vein
