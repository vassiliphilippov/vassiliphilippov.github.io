Synonyms

  • tread
    6.5 0.3/m
    tread names a single step or its sound in literary or formal use, while step is common everyday word for that action.
  • footstep
    1.0 4/m
    footstep is the sound of one walk movement, while step is a more general, common word for the same foot movement or mark.
  • footfall
    6.2 0.4/m
    footfall is the sound of a person's walking often heard as a series, while step refers to a single movement or its mark.
  • footprint
    1.6 6/m
    footprint is the impression left by a foot or shoe on a surface, while step can mean the sound or mark of a foot.
  • footmark
    2.4 0.05/m
    footmark is a visible mark left on ground or soft soil, while step more broadly names the foot's sound or any mark made.
