broadcast area

noun
area reached by TV or radio transmission
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæst ˈɛriə / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌkɑːst ˈeəriə / (BrE)
the geographic region over which a radio or television transmission can be received

Examples

The broadcast area map
Our village is outside the broadcast area for that radio station, so we can't tune in.
Some programs are only available within the main broadcast area due to licensing agreements.
The new transmitter expanded the broadcast area to include several neighboring counties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coverage area - the extent of territory served by a broadcast signal
