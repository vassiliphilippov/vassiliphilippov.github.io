coral bean

noun
shrub or tree with red beans and fragrant flowers
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrəl ˌbin / (AmE), / ˈkɒrəl ˌbiːn / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree with pinnate leaves, dense spikes of fragrant blue flowers, and bright red beans, some species of which are poisonous to livestock

Examples

The coral bean plant
Farmers must keep their cattle away from the coral bean because it can be poisonous to livestock.
The brightly colored seeds of the coral bean make it a popular ornamental plant.
The vibrant flowers of the coral bean attract hummingbirds to the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Erythrina - genus of flowering plants known as coral trees or coral beans
