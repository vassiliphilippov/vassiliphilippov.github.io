coral bean

noun
shrub or tree with red beans and fragrant flowers
2.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔːr.əl biːn/ (AmE), /ˈkɒr.əl biːn/ (BrE)
a shrub or small tree with pinnate leaves, dense spikes of fragrant blue flowers, and bright red beans, some species of which are poisonous to livestock

Examples

The brightly colored seeds of the coral bean make it a popular ornamental plant.
