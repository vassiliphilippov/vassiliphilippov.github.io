privileged

adjective
restricted to an exclusive group
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪv.ə.lɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˈprɪv.ɪ.lɪdʒd / (BrE)
Restricted to a particular group; available or accessible only to a select or exclusive group of people.

Examples

Privileged access
He was not privileged to attend the secret meeting.
Only a privileged few had access to the classified government documents.
The report contained privileged information not meant for public release.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confidential - meant to be kept secret or private
  • antonym: public - open or accessible to all people
