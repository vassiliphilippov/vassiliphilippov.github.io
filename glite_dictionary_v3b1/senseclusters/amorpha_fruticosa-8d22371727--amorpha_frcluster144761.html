Amorpha fruticosa

noun
a North American shrub with dark purple flowers
Pronunciation: / əˈmɔrfə fruːˈtɪkoʊsə / (AmE), / əˈmɔːfə fruːˈtɪkəʊsə / (BrE)
A dense shrub native to moist riverbanks and flood plains of the eastern United States, known for its fragrant foliage and dense clusters of dark purple flowers.
