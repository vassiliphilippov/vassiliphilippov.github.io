grind

noun
overly studious, dull student
6.9
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
a student who studies excessively and is often regarded as dull or overly serious by others

Examples

A grind at school
Although she was called a grind, she didn't mind because her grades were excellent.
Everyone knew him as the class grind who never missed an assignment.
The other students teased the grind for spending his lunch break in the library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nerd - someone who is overly intellectual or academic
  • antonym: slacker - a person who avoids work or effort, especially in studies
  • synonym: swot - a person who studies excessively; especially in a way considered boring or unsociable
