nodulation

noun
formation of nodules
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːdʒəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒdjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of forming small lumps or nodules in tissue or organs, often linked to disease or inflammation

Examples

Liver nodulation
The doctor noted significant nodulation in the patient's liver during the examination.
Chronic infections can sometimes lead to nodulation in affected tissues.
The X-ray revealed nodulation in the lungs, suggesting a possible underlying disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inflammation - a localized physical condition marked by redness, swelling, and pain
  • synonym: nodule - a small lump or swelling, especially in tissue
