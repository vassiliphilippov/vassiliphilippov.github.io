anti-corruption

noun
efforts against corruption
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌænti kəˈrʌpʃən / (AmE), / ˌænti kəˈrʌpʃən / (BrE)
actions, policies, or efforts aimed at preventing or reducing corruption, especially in government or organizations

Examples

Anti-corruption measures
The new law introduced strict anti-corruption measures for public officials.
International organizations often support anti-corruption programs in developing countries.
The company adopted an anti-corruption policy to ensure ethical business practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corruption - dishonest or illegal behavior, especially by people in power
  • synonym: transparency - the quality of being open and honest, without secrets
