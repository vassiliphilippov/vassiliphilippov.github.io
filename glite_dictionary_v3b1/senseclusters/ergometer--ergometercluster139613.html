ergometer

noun
instrument for measuring work or power
Pronunciation: / ɜrˈɡɑːmətər / (AmE), / ɜːˈɡɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device for measuring the amount of work or power performed, often used in physiology and exercise studies

Examples

A ergometer device
Modern gyms offer advanced ergometers for both research and fitness monitoring.
Rowing teams often train on a ergometer to simulate real water resistance.
The scientist used an ergometer to record the athlete’s power output during cycling tests.

  • synonym: dynamometer - an instrument for measuring mechanical force or power
