season

noun
period marked by recurring holidays
1.0 225/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàsiÀêz…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàsiÀêz…ôn / (BrE)
a period of the year marked by particular weather, events, or activities (such as spring, summer, or a holiday or sports season)

Examples

Holiday season
Every year, the city is beautifully decorated for the season of lights.
Retailers often see an increase in sales during the holiday season.
The Christmas season brings families together to celebrate.
Strawberries are sweetest during their peak season in early summer.
The basketball team performed exceptionally well this season and made it to the finals.
Tickets are hard to find at the start of the opera season.
Farmers plan their crops around the changing seasons.
The leaves change color when a new season begins.
Winter is often considered the harshest season of the year.

Synonyms

  • time of year
    1.0 8.10/m
    time of year means a general part of the yearly cycle used in everyday talk, while season often names specific weather or activities.
