overbright

adjective
excessively bright
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbraɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbraɪt / (BrE)
too bright or excessively illuminated, often causing discomfort or making it hard to see clearly

Examples

Overbright sunlight
The overbright sunlight streaming through the window made it difficult to read the book.
He squinted at the overbright display on his phone in the dark room.
The overbright headlights from the oncoming car temporarily blinded her.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bright - giving out or reflecting a lot of light
  • synonym: dazzling - extremely bright and difficult to look at
  • antonym: dim - not bright; lacking in light
