antidumping

adjective
trade protection measure
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈdʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈdʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to laws or actions that prevent the sale of goods below fair market value in international trade

Examples

Antidumping laws
The country introduced antidumping regulations to support its domestic manufacturers.
An antidumping investigation was launched after complaints from local producers.
The government announced new antidumping tariffs on imported steel products.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dumping - selling goods abroad at less than fair value
  • synonym: protective - intended to guard against unfair competition
