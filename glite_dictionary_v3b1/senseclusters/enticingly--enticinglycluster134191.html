enticingly

adverb
in a tempting way
4.4
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋli / (AmE), / ɪnˈtaɪsɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that attracts or tempts someone, making something seem appealing or desirable

Examples

To entice customers
The aroma of freshly baked bread drifted enticingly from the kitchen.
She smiled enticingly at the camera, hoping to catch everyone's attention.
The restaurant's menu was displayed enticingly at the entrance to attract customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attractively - in a way that is pleasing or appealing
  • synonym: temptingly - in a way that tempts or lures someone
  • antonym: unappealingly - in a way that is not attractive or desirable
