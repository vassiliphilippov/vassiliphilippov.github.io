horse-cart

noun
heavy cart pulled by a horse
Pronunciation: / hɔrs kɑrt / (AmE), / hɔːs kɑːt / (BrE)
A heavy cart pulled by a horse, used especially for farm work or transporting goods.

Examples

A horse-cart on farm
Before the arrival of tractors, fields were plowed with a horse-cart carrying supplies.
The farmer loaded hay onto his horse-cart for transport to the barn.
Tourists enjoyed scenic rides through the village in a traditional horse-cart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - a vehicle with wheels drawn by horses, mainly for passengers
  • synonym: wagon - a heavy vehicle for transporting goods, typically four-wheeled and pulled by animals
