pesewa

noun
currency subunit of Ghana
Pronunciation: / pɛˈseɪwə / (AmE), / pɛˈseɪwə / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Ghana, equal to one hundredth of a cedi

Examples

One pesewa coin
One Ghana cedi is made up of one hundred pesewas.
She gave the vendor fifty pesewas for a bottle of water.
The price of bread increased by twenty pesewas last month in Accra.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cedi - the basic monetary unit of Ghana
