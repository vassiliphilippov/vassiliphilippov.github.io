economic outlook

noun
predictions about the economy's future
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪk ˈaʊtlʊk / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk ˈaʊtlʊk / (BrE)
The likely future state of the economy based on current trends and forecasts.

Examples

The economic outlook today
Analysts say the global economic outlook remains uncertain due to recent policy changes.
Many investors use the economic outlook to guide their decisions on buying stocks.
The government's report suggested that the economic outlook for the coming year is stable.
