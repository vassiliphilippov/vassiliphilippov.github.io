rallying cry

noun
slogan to encourage support for a cause
5.3 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈræ.lɪŋ kraɪ/ (AmE), /ˈræ.lɪŋ kraɪ/ (BrE)
a word or phrase used to encourage people to unite and take action, especially for a cause or movement

Examples
