crocodile tears

noun
insincere or fake show of sorrow
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɑː.kəˌdaɪl tɪrz / (AmE), / ˈkrɒk.ə.daɪl tɪəz /; / ˈkrɒk.əˌdaɪl tɪəz / (BrE)
