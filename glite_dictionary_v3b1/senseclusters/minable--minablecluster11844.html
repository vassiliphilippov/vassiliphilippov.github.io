minable

adjective
suitable for mining
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnəbəl / (BrE)
suitable for being fitted with explosive mines or likely to contain mines, especially in a military context

Examples

Minable harbor area
The harbor was considered minable and required regular checks by the navy.
Engineers assessed whether the field was minable before planning the convoy route.
Maps marked certain coastal areas as minable to warn approaching ships.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: mined - already containing explosive mines
  • antonym: safe - free from explosive mines or danger
