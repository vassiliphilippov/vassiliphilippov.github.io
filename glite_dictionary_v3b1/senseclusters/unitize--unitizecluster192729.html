unitize

verb
make into a unit or organized group
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪ.taɪz / (BrE)
to make something into a single unit or group; to organize or package as a unit, especially for convenience or efficiency

Examples

To unitize car bodies
Automotive engineers may unitize car bodies to reduce production costs.
The company decided to unitize its packaging process for efficiency.
We need to unitize these shipments to maximize storage space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combine - to join or merge into a single unit or group
  • antonym: divide - to separate into parts or groups
