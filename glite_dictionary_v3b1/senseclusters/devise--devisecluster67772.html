devise

noun
bequest of real estate in a will
5.2 8.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvaɪz / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪz / (BrE)
(law) a clause or gift of real property in a will

Examples

A devise clause

He challenged the devise of the farmland on the grounds of undue influence.
The lawyer explained the devise of the estate to the beneficiary.
Under the terms of the will, the house was left as a devise to her niece.
