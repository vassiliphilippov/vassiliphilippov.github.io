Hymenomycetes

noun
former class of fungi (mushrooms, agarics, etc.)
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪmɪnoʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / haɪməˌnoʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz / (AmE), / hɪmɛnəʊmaɪˈsiːtiːz /; / hɪməˌnəʊmɪˈsiːtiːz / (BrE)
a former class of fungi that included mushrooms, toadstools, agarics, and bracket fungi
