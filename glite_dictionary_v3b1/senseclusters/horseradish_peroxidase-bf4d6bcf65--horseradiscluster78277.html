horseradish peroxidase

noun
enzyme used in immunohistochemistry
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsˌrædɪʃ pərˈɑk.sɪ.deɪs / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsˌrædɪʃ pəˈrɒk.sɪ.deɪs / (BrE)
an enzyme derived from horseradish used in immunohistochemistry and biotechnology to label or detect antigens and antibodies

Examples

Horseradish peroxidase enzyme
Researchers often conjugate horseradish peroxidase to antibodies for detection purposes.
The reaction was visualized using horseradish peroxidase as a labeling agent.
Without horseradish peroxidase, the staining procedure would not yield visible results.
