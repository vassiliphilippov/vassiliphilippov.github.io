hearty

adjective
strong or enthusiastic (especially of appetite)
3.8 0.59/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêrti / (AmE), / Ààh…ëÀêti / (BrE)
(of an appetite or eater) strong, enthusiastic, and involving the consumption of large amounts of food.

Examples

Hearty appetite
After hiking all day, she had a hearty appetite.
His hearty eating impressed everyone at the dinner table.
The children enjoyed a hearty breakfast before school.

Synonyms

  • voracious
    7.5 1.64/m
    voracious eats or wants very large amounts and rarely feels full, while hearty describes a strong, healthy appetite that enjoys big portions.
  • ravenous
    6.0 1.26/m
    ravenous is extreme, urgent hunger that makes you eat fast, while hearty is a steady, enthusiastic appetite for large, comforting meals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voracious - wanting or devouring great quantities of food
