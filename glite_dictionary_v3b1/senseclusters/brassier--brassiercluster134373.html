brassier

adjective
more brassy
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbræsiər / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːsiə / (BrE)
having a more brassy sound or appearance, especially in music or color.

Examples

Brassier sound
The new paint made the room look even brassier than before.
Her voice became brassier as she tried to get everyone's attention.
The band's sound grew brassier with the addition of more trumpets.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: brassy - having a bright, metallic sound or appearance
  • antonym: subdued - less bright or intense in sound or appearance
