yakka

noun
Sri Lankan spirit
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈjæk.ə/ (AmE), /ˈjæk.ə/ (BrE)
a spirit or demon in Sri Lankan (Sinhalese) folklore, often believed to influence people's lives or the natural world

Examples

A yakka spirit
According to legend, a powerful yakka protected the ancient village from harm.
The festival included rituals to honor the local yakka and ask for good fortune.
Some believe that a yakka lives in the forest and guards the sacred trees.
