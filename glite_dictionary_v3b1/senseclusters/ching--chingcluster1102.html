ching

noun
small metal cymbals
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a small pair of metal cymbals used in traditional Southeast Asian music to mark rhythm

Examples

A pair of ching cymbals
The musician played the ching to keep the rhythm steady during the performance.
In traditional Thai music, the ching is often used to mark the beat.
She learned how to play the ching as part of her study of Southeast Asian instruments.
