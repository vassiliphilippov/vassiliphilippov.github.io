immediate

adjective
done at once, without delay
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmiːdiət / (AmE), / ɪˈmiːdiət / (BrE)
happening or done at once; without any delay.

Examples

Immediate action needed
In case of emergency, call for immediate assistance.
She demanded an immediate answer to her question.
The company promised an immediate refund if the product was defective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delayed - happening or done later than planned or expected
  • synonym: prompt - done quickly and without delay
