Venus's curse

noun
archaic term for a sexually transmitted infection
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnəsɪz kɜrs / (AmE), / ˈviːnəsɪz kɜːs / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a sexually transmitted infection, especially syphilis

Examples

Venus's curse disease
In eighteenth-century Europe, Venus's curse was a feared and little-understood disease.
Many believed that Venus's curse was a divine punishment for immoral behavior.
Medical texts from the past often referred to syphilis as Venus's curse.
