BPD

noun
borderline personality disorder
8.0
Pronunciation: / bi pi di / (AmE), / biː piː diː / (BrE)
abbreviation for borderline personality disorder, a mental health condition marked by unstable moods, behavior, and relationships

Examples

BPD symptoms
