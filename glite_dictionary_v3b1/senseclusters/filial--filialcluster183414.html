filial

adjective
of a generation after the parents
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪliəl / (AmE), / ˈfɪliəl / (BrE)
Relating to or designating generations following the parental generation, especially in biology and genetics.

Examples

Filial generation
Each subsequent filial generation exhibited new variations from the parents.
Scientists refer to the first filial generation as F1 in Mendelian genetics.
The genetic traits were studied across several filial generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parental - relating to the parent generation; in biology, referring to the original parents in a genetic cross
