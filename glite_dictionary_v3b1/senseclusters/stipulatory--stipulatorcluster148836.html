stipulatory

adjective
based on a stipulation or agreement
7.9 7.8
Pronunciation: /stɪ.ˈpjuː.lə.tɔːr.i/ (AmE), /stɪ.ˈpjuː.lə.tɔː.riː/ (BrE)
constituted or established by stipulation or formal agreement, especially in legal or contractual contexts.

Examples

A stipulatory agreement
A stipulatory obligation is only binding if explicitly stated in the contract.
His argument relied on the stipulatory nature of the agreement.
The contract contains several stipulatory clauses that both parties must observe.
