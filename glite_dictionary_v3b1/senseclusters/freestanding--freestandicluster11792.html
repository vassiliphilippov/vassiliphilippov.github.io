freestanding

adjective
not attached; standing alone
6.7 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ / (BrE)
(of an object or structure) standing alone and not attached to or supported by anything else.

Examples

A freestanding oven
Her new oven is freestanding, so it can be moved anywhere in the kitchen.
The gallery features a freestanding sculpture in the center of the room.
They installed a freestanding bookshelf against the wall but didn't anchor it.
