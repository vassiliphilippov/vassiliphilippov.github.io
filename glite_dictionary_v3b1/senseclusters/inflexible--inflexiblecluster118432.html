inflexible

adjective
not able to be bent
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɛksəbəl / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɛksəbl / (BrE)
not able to be bent or changed in shape; not flexible.

Examples

Inflexible ruler
An inflexible ruler is ideal for drawing straight lines.
She tried to reshape the inflexible plastic, but it snapped instead.
The inflexible metal rod would not bend, even under great pressure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexible - capable of bending easily without breaking
  • synonym: rigid - unable to bend or be forced out of shape; not flexible
