Japanese cherry

noun
ornamental tree with decorative blossoms
5.3
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpænɪs ˈtʃɛri / (AmE), / dʒəˈpænɪs ˈtʃɛri / (BrE)
An ornamental tree native to Japan, widely grown for its showy white or pink blossoms and inedible fruit.

Examples

The Japanese cherry tree
Every spring, tourists flock to see the Japanese cherry trees in full bloom.
He planted a Japanese cherry in his yard to admire its blossoms each year.
The garden was transformed by the pale pink flowers of the Japanese cherry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cherry blossom - the flower of cherry trees, especially as celebrated in Japanese culture
