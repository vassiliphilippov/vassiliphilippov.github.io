metabolous

adjective
undergoing metamorphosis (biology)
Pronunciation: / məˈtæbəloʊs / (AmE), / məˈtabələs / (BrE)
(of an organism, especially an insect) undergoing metamorphosis during its development.

Examples

Metabolous insect development
Butterflies are metabolous insects that completely transform from larva to adult.
Grasshoppers exhibit metabolous changes during their life cycle.
Metabolous development is a key characteristic distinguishing certain insect orders.
