christen after

verb
to name after someone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsən ˈæftɚ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.ən ˈɑːftə / (BrE)
to give someone a name in honor of another person; to name (a child) after someone else

Examples

To christen after a person
Many people christen after a famous figure they admire.
The couple wanted to christen after a beloved friend who had recently passed away.
They decided to christen after her grandmother to keep the family tradition alive.
