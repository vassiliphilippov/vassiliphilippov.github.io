mystery story

noun
crime-focused story or novel
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪstəri ˌstɔri / (AmE), / ˈmɪstəri ˌstɔːri / (BrE)
A novel, play, or film that centers on the investigation and solution of a crime, often involving a murder.

Examples

A mystery story novel
He spent the weekend writing a new mystery story set in Victorian London.
She loves reading mystery stories about famous detectives solving impossible crimes.
The film adaptation of the mystery story kept audiences guessing until the very end.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detective story - a story featuring a detective and crime investigation
  • degree lower: thriller - a novel, play, or movie with exciting plot, often involving danger or crime
