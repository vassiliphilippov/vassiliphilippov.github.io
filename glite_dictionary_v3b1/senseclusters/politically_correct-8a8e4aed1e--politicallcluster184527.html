politically correct

adjective
avoiding potentially offensive language or behavior
7.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl.i kə.ˈrekt/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kli kə.ˈrekt/ (AmE), /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kəl.i kə.ˈrekt/ /; / /pə.ˈlɪt.ɪ.kli kə.ˈrekt/ (BrE)
avoiding language or behavior that could offend or exclude particular groups of people, especially those who have been discriminated against

Examples

Politically correct language

Some critics argue that being too politically correct can stifle honest conversation.
