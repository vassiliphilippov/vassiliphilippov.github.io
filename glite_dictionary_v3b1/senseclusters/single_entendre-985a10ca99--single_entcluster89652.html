single entendre

noun
phrase or joke with one clear meaning

Pronunciation: /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ɑːn.ˈtɑːn.drə/ /; / /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ən.ˈtɑːn.drə/ (AmE), /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ɒn.ˈtɒn.drə/ /; / /ˈsɪŋ.ɡəl ən.ˈtɒn.drə/ (BrE)
