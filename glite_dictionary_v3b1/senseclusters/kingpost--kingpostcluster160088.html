kingpost

noun
vertical support post
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌpoʊst / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋpəʊst / (BrE)
a central vertical post in a roof truss or bridge that supports the structure above it

Examples

Roof kingpost
The engineer explained how the kingpost helps distribute the weight of the roof.
During the renovation, they discovered the original kingpost was still intact after a century.
A wooden kingpost is often used in traditional barn construction for added stability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: queenpost - a pair of vertical posts in a truss, used instead of a single kingpost
  • synonym: strut - a structural support designed to resist compression
