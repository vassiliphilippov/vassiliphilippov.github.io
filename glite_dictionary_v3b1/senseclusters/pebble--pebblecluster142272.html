pebble

verb
to cover with pebbles
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛbəl / (BrE)
to cover or scatter a surface with small stones or pebbles

Examples

To pebble a path
They pebbled the garden path to improve drainage and give it a rustic appearance.
The artist pebbled the surface of the sculpture to create a unique texture.
Workers pebble the driveway every spring to keep it looking fresh and neat.
