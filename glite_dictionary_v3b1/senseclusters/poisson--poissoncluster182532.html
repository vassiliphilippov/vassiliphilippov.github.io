poisson

noun
probability distribution
9.9
Pronunciation: / pwaˈsɑn / (AmE), / pwaˈsɒ̃ / (BrE)
a probability distribution used to model the number of independent events occurring in a fixed interval at a constant average rate

Examples

Poisson distribution
The poisson is often used to predict the number of phone calls received in an hour.
Researchers applied the poisson to estimate the frequency of rare genetic mutations.
In statistics class, we learned how the poisson can model accident rates on highways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: binomial distribution - a probability distribution describing the number of successes in a fixed number of independent trials
  • synonym: normal distribution - a probability distribution that is symmetric and bell-shaped
