FPS

noun
video game genre
Pronunciation: / ɛf pi ɛs / (AmE), / ɛf piː ɛs / (BrE)
a genre of video game where the player experiences the action from the character's viewpoint, usually involving shooting and combat

Examples

FPS game
He spent the evening playing his favorite FPS with friends online.
Many popular eSports tournaments feature competitive FPS games.
The new FPS offers realistic graphics and intense action sequences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: first-person shooter - a video game genre where gameplay is from the character's perspective and involves shooting
  • antonym: third-person shooter - a video game genre where the player sees the character from an external viewpoint
