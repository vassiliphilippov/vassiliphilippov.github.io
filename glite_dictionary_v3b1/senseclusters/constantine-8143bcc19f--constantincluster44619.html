Constantine

proper noun
historical city in northeastern Algeria
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnstənˈtin / (AmE), / ˌkɒnstənˈtiːn / (BrE)
a walled city in northeastern Algeria, east of Algiers, known for its dramatic gorges and historical significance; rebuilt in the 4th century by Emperor Constantine I.
