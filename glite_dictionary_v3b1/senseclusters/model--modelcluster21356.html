model

noun
person worthy of imitation
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːdəl /; / ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒdəl / (BrE)
a person or thing that is an example to be imitated; a pattern or standard for others

Examples

A model student
Many young athletes look up to him as a model and strive to follow his example.
Parents are often reminded that their actions serve as a model for their children.
She has always been a model of honesty and integrity in the workplace.
He is the model of patience, never losing his temper even in stressful situations.
She became a model of generosity after donating so much to charity.
The teacher used his essay as a model for the rest of the class.
His actions became a model for others to follow in the community.
She served as a model of patience for her students.
They looked to the ancient philosophers as a model for living a virtuous life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: example - a person or thing regarded as representative of a group or as worthy of imitation
  • synonym: exemplar - someone or something that is a typical example
  • synonym: ideal - a person or thing regarded as perfect to be copied
  • synonym: paragon - a person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a quality
  • synonym: role model - a person whose behaviour is emulated by others, especially as an example for young people
