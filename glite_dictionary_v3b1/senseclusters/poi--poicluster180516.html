poi

noun
Hawaiian dish of pounded taro root
Pronunciation: / pɔɪ / (AmE), / pɔɪ / (BrE)
A Hawaiian food made by pounding taro root into a smooth paste, often allowed to ferment slightly.

Examples

Hawaiian poi food
Many locals eat poi every day, appreciating its nutritional value and unique flavor.
She served traditional Hawaiian poi at the luau, alongside roast pork and fresh fruit.
The texture of poi can vary from thick to runny, depending on personal preference.
