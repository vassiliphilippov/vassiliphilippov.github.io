minimal state control

noun
limited government intervention
1.3 7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnəməl steɪt kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməl steɪt kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
A situation in which the government exercises only a small degree of regulation or intervention in individuals' personal lives or societal affairs.
