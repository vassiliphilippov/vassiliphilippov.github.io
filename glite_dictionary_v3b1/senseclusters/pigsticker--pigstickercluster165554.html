pigsticker

noun
person who slaughters pigs
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪg.stɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈpɪg.stɪ.kə/ (BrE)
a person whose job is to kill or slaughter pigs, especially in a slaughterhouse or on a farm

Examples

A skilled pigsticker

Years ago, every village had a skilled pigsticker who handled the annual slaughter.
He learned the trade of a pigsticker from his father, who worked in the local abattoir.
