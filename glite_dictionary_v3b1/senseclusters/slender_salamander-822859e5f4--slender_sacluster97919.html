slender salamander

noun
small, slim salamander of Pacific US coast
Pronunciation: / ˈslɛndɚ ˈsæləˌmændɚ / (AmE), / ˈslɛndə ˈsæləˌmandə / (BrE)
Any of several small, slender salamanders found along the Pacific coast of the United States, especially of the genus Batrachoseps.

Examples

The slender salamander
The slender salamander is well adapted to living in the moist leaf litter of California forests.
Wildlife biologists are studying how the slender salamander responds to habitat changes.
You can often find a slender salamander hiding under rocks after a rain.
