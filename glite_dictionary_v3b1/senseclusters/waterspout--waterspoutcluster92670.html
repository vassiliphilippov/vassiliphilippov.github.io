waterspout

noun
tornado-like column over water
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌspaʊt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌspaʊt / (BrE)
a rotating column of air and water mist resembling a tornado, occurring over a body of water

Examples

A huge waterspout
A massive waterspout formed off the coast, drawing the attention of local fishermen.
Photographs of the rare waterspout made headlines in the morning newspapers.
The ferry captain spotted a waterspout and quickly altered course to avoid it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tornado - a violent rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground
