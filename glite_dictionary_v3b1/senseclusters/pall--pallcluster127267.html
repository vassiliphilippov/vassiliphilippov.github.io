pall

noun
a numbing feeling of dread or gloom
8.4
Pronunciation: / pɔl / (AmE), / pɔːl / (BrE)
a feeling or atmosphere of gloom, sadness, or heaviness

Examples

Pall of sadness
A pall descended on the room when the bad news was announced.
His words cast a pall over what should have been a cheerful celebration.
There was a pall of fear hanging over the city after the incident.
After the announcement of the layoffs, a pall settled over the entire office staff.
His harsh words threw a pall on what should have been a happy celebration.
The dark clouds cast a pall over the outdoor wedding, dampening everyone's spirits.
