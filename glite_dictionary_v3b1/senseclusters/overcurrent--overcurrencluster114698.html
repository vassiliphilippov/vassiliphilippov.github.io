overcurrent

adjective
excessive electrical current
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvɚˌkɝrənt / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəkʌrənt / (BrE)
relating to or caused by an electrical current that exceeds safe or normal limits.

Examples

Overcurrent protection device
The engineer checked the overcurrent protection system before starting the machine.
An overcurrent condition can damage sensitive electronic equipment if not properly managed.
Special fuses are used to prevent hazards caused by overcurrent situations in electrical circuits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overload - an excessive load or amount, especially of electricity
  • antonym: undercurrent - a current below the surface or a hidden tendency
