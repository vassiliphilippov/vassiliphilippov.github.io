Tammuz

noun
month in the Hebrew calendar (June-July)
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈmuz / (AmE), / təˈmuːz / (BrE)
The tenth month of the civil year and the fourth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Hebrew calendar, corresponding to June and July.

Examples

Tammuz month
According to the Hebrew calendar, they were married in Tammuz.
Many important historical events are commemorated during the month of Tammuz.
The fast of the seventeenth of Tammuz marks the beginning of a period of mourning in Judaism.
