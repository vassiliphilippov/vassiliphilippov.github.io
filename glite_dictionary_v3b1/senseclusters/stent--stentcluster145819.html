stent

verb
to insert a stent
Pronunciation: / stɛnt / (AmE), / stɛnt / (BrE)
to place a stent, a small tube, into a blood vessel or passage to keep it open.

Examples

To stent an artery
The cardiologist is stenting the patient's artery to improve blood flow.
After the procedure, the doctor explained why stenting was necessary for her recovery.
Advances in technology have made stenting safer and more effective for many patients.
