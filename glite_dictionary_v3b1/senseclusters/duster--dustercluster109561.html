duster

noun
long, loose protective coat
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʌstə / (BrE)
a loose, long coat or coverall, often reaching to the ankles, originally worn to protect clothing from dust

Examples

A long duster coat
He hung his duster by the door to keep the sand off his clothes.
She wore a duster over her dress when riding in the open car.
The old-fashioned duster was once common among travelers on dusty roads.
