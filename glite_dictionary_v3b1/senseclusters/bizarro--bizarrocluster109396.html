bizarro

noun
strange person
9.9
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈzɑːr.oʊ/ /; / /bə.ˈzɑːr.oʊ/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈzɑː.rəʊ/ /; / /bə.ˈzɑː.rəʊ/ (BrE)
an informal term for a person who behaves in a very strange, odd, or unconventional way

Examples

A real bizarro
Everyone at the office thought he was a bizarro after he started bringing a parrot to meetings.
The new student was quickly labeled a bizarro because of his unusual fashion choices.
She felt like a bizarro at the party since her interests were so different from everyone else's.
