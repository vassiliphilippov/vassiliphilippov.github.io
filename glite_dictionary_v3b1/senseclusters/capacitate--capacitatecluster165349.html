capacitate

verb
enable or make capable
9.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.teɪt/ (AmE), /kə.ˈpæs.ɪ.teɪt/ (BrE)
to make (someone or something) capable; to enable or equip for a particular function or activity

Examples

To capacitate a team
The new software will capacitate employees to work more efficiently from home.
Their training programs are designed to capacitate local communities in disaster response.
This course aims to capacitate students with the skills needed for modern industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enable - to make able; to give ability, power, or means to do something
  • antonym: incapacitate - to prevent from functioning in a normal way; to make incapable
