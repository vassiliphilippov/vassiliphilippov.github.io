entitlement

noun
IT access permission
Pronunciation: / \u026an\u02c8ta\u026at\u0259lm\u0259nt / (AmE), / \u026an\u02c8ta\u026at\u0259lm\u0259nt / (BrE)
(in computing and IT security) a defined permission, access right, or license that allows a user or account to use a resource, feature, or service.

Examples

User entitlement access
Before granting access, the administrator checked her entitlement to the company's payroll database.
Auditors reviewed user entitlements to ensure no one had unnecessary privileges on the network.
The cloud service displays each customer's subscription entitlement and its remaining quotas for APIs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: permission - authorization to perform an action or access a resource in a system
  • synonym: privilege - a special right or advantage granted to a user account or role
