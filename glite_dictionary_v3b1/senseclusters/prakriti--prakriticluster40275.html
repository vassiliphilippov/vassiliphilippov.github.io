prakriti

proper noun
Hindu nature goddess
Pronunciation: / prəˈkrɪti / (AmE), / prəˈkrɪti / (BrE)
the personified goddess or feminine principle of nature and creative power in Hindu religion and mythology

Examples

Prakriti goddess power
In Hindu philosophy, prakriti is seen as the source of all material existence.
The temple was dedicated to prakriti, honoring her as the creative force of the universe.
Many ancient texts describe the relationship between prakriti and purusha in the creation of the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: purusha - the cosmic spirit or masculine principle in Hindu philosophy
  • synonym: shakti - the divine feminine energy and creative power in Hinduism
