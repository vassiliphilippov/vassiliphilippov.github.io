rolled out sod

noun
grass in rolls for instant lawn installation
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊld aʊt sɑd / (AmE), / ˌrəʊld aʊt sɒd / (BrE)
grass that has been grown and cut into rolls for easy installation on the ground, typically used for creating or repairing lawns quickly

Examples

Rolled out sod lawn
After the patio was built, the landscapers installed rolled out sod to cover the bare soil.
It's important to water rolled out sod immediately to help it establish roots.
The backyard was transformed in a day thanks to fresh rolled out sod.
