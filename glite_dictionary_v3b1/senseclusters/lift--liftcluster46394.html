lift

verb
transport by air to inaccessible places
Pronunciation: / lɪft / (AmE), / lɪft / (BrE)
to transport people or goods by air to or from places that are not accessible by other means

Examples

To lift supplies by helicopter
After the flood, supplies were lifted into the remote village by helicopter.
During the crisis, the military lifted stranded tourists out of the mountains.
Medical teams are often lifted into disaster zones when roads are impassable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airlift - to transport (people or supplies) by aircraft, typically in an emergency
