textural

adjective
relating to texture
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃərəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to the way elements are combined to create the overall feel, structure, or quality, especially in music or sound.

Examples

Textural layers in music
The artist paid close attention to the textural details in her painting.
The chef described the dish as having a unique textural contrast between the crispy topping and soft filling.
The band's new album is praised for its textural complexity and layered sounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: structural - relating to the arrangement or organization of parts
  • synonym: textured - having a particular surface or quality of texture
