Phlomis fruticosa

noun
Mediterranean shrub; Jerusalem sage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfloʊmɪs fruːtɪˈkoʊsə / (AmE), / ˌfləʊmɪs fruːtɪˈkəʊsə / (BrE)
A Mediterranean subshrub with dense whorls of purple or yellow flowers, commonly cultivated for its ornamental value; also known as Jerusalem sage.
