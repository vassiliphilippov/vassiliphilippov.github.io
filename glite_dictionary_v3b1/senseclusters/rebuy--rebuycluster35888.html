rebuy

verb
to buy back in
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbaɪ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbaɪ / (BrE)
to buy more chips or pay to re-enter a gambling game or tournament in order to continue playing

Examples

To rebuy tournament chips
After losing all his chips, he decided to rebuy and continue playing in the tournament.
The casino allows players to rebuy during the first hour of the poker event.
She made a quick rebuy at the break to stay in the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buy in - to pay to participate in a game or tournament
  • synonym: reenter - to enter again, especially after being eliminated
  • antonym: withdraw - to leave or stop participating in a game or tournament
