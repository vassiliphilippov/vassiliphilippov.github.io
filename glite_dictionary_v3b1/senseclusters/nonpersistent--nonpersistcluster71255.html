nonpersistent

adjective
not lasting
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pərˈsɪstənt / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈsɪstənt / (BrE)
not lasting or continuing for a long time; not persistent

Examples

Nonpersistent chemicals
The nonpersistent pesticide breaks down quickly and does not remain in the soil.
Doctors prefer nonpersistent drugs that leave the body within a few hours.
Because the stain was nonpersistent, it washed out easily with soap and water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: persistent - continuing to exist or endure over a long period
  • synonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period of time
