Somali peninsula

noun
northeastern African peninsula (Horn of Africa)
4.6
Pronunciation: / səˈmɑːli pəˈnɪn sə lə / (AmE), / səˈmɑːli pəˈnɪn sɪ lə / (BrE)
a large peninsula in northeastern Africa, comprising Somalia, Djibouti, Eritrea, and parts of Ethiopia; the easternmost part of Africa

Examples

The Somali peninsula region
Droughts have had a severe impact on the livelihoods of people living in the Somali peninsula.
Political borders within the Somali peninsula often do not match the region's ethnic or historical boundaries.
The Somali peninsula is often referred to as the Horn of Africa due to its geographical shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Horn of Africa - the region in eastern Africa forming the shape of a horn, roughly coinciding with the Somali peninsula
