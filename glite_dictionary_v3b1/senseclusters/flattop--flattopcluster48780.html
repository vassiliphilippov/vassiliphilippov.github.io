flattop

noun
closely cropped, flat-topped haircut
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈflætˌtɒp / (BrE)
A haircut in which the hair on the top of the head is cut very short and flat, usually worn by men or boys.

Examples

A flattop haircut
His flattop was perfectly trimmed by the barber.
In high school he wore a flattop to fit in with the sports team.
The soldier's strict flattop made him look even more disciplined.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crew cut - a very short haircut for men and boys
