unpaginated

adjective
without page numbers
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpeɪdʒɪneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpeɪdʒɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having page numbers or not divided into pages

Examples

An unpaginated report
The report was unpaginated, making it difficult to cite specific information.
She struggled to find her place in the unpaginated manuscript.
Because the booklet was unpaginated, readers had trouble following the references.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paginated - having page numbers or divided into pages
