A device that detects the presence or properties of objects by measuring changes in capacitance, usually to sense objects that are conductive or have a different dielectric constant than air.

Examples

A capacitive sensor device
A capacitive sensor is often used in industrial machinery for non-contact object detection.
The smartphone screen responds to touch using a capacitive sensor that detects the electrical properties of your skin.
They installed a capacitive sensor in the faucet to enable hands-free operation.
