patron

noun
regular customer or client
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪ.trən / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.trən / (BrE)
a person who frequents and regularly supports a business, such as a shop, restaurant, or theatre

Examples

A patron of a restaurant
As a regular patron of the theatre, she knew all the staff by name.
Many patrons left positive reviews for the restaurant after their meals.
The coffee shop is always bustling with loyal patrons every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services from a business
