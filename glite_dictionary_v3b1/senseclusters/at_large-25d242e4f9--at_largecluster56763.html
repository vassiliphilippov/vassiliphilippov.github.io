at large

adjective
escaped; not captured (esp. criminal)
Pronunciation: / ət ˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ət ˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
having escaped from confinement or not yet captured, especially used of criminals or dangerous animals

Examples

Criminal at large
Authorities warned that the wild animal is still at large in the area.
Several dangerous criminals are at large following the prison break.
The suspect remains at large after escaping from police custody last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on the loose - having escaped from confinement or not under control
