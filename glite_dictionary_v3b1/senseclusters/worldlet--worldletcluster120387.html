worldlet

noun
small isolated community
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːldlət / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldlət / (BrE)
a small, self-contained community or environment with its own customs and concerns, separate from the larger world

Examples

An isolated worldlet
The remote island functioned as a worldlet, untouched by modern technology.
Within the bustling city, the artist community formed its own worldlet of creativity and support.
Each department in the company can feel like a separate worldlet with its own rules and culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enclave - a distinct area or group enclosed within a larger one
  • antonym: mainstream - the dominant or widely accepted group or way of life
  • synonym: microcosm - a small community that reflects the characteristics of something larger
