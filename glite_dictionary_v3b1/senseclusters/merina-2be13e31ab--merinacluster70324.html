Merina

proper noun
historic Malagasy kingdom
Pronunciation: / məˈriːnə / (AmE), / məˈriːnə / (BrE)
a historical kingdom and political state of the Merina people in Madagascar's central highlands, especially prominent in the 18th and 19th centuries

Examples

Merina kingdom
The Merina kingdom played a major role in unifying Madagascar in the 19th century.
Historians study the rise and fall of the Merina to understand Madagascar's past.
The capital city of Antananarivo was once the center of the Merina kingdom.
