figure

noun
figurative language or rhetorical device
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪɡjər / (AmE), / ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
an expression or use of language in a figurative or nonliteral sense, especially as a rhetorical device

Examples

A figure of speech
Metaphor is a common figure in everyday language.
Teachers often highlight when a figure of speech is used in literary analysis.
The poet's use of a bold figure enhanced the vividness of the passage.
