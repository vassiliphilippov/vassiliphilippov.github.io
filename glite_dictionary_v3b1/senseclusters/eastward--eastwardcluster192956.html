eastward

noun
direction at 90 degrees (east)
1.7 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààistw…ôrd / (AmE), / ÀàiÀêstw…ôd / (BrE)
the direction or point on the compass that is at 90 degrees, corresponding to the east

Examples

The eastward wind
A steady breeze was blowing from the eastward as we began our journey.
Many ancient temples were built facing eastward to greet the rising sun.
The ship set a course directly for eastward to reach the new continent.

Synonyms

  • due east
    1.9 0.40/m
    due east is exactly at 90 degrees on a compass, while eastward describes movement or direction toward the east.
  • east
    1.0 9.30/m
    east is the name of the compass point where the sun rises, while eastward suggests going or facing toward that direction.
  • E
    1.0 0.73/m
    E is the short compass label used on maps and charts, while eastward is a word meaning toward or in the east.
