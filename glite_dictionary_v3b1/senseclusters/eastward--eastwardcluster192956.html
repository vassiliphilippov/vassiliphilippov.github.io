eastward

noun
direction at 90 degrees (east)
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈistwərd / (AmE), / ˈiːstwəd / (BrE)
the direction or point on the compass that is at 90 degrees, corresponding to the east

Examples

The eastward wind
A steady breeze was blowing from the eastward as we began our journey.
Many ancient temples were built facing eastward to greet the rising sun.
The ship set a course directly for eastward to reach the new continent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: east - the cardinal direction at 90 degrees
  • antonym: westward - the cardinal direction opposite to eastward
