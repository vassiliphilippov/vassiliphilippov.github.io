inexistence

noun
state of nonexistence
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɪɡˈzɪstəns / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪɡˈzɪstəns / (BrE)
the state of not existing or being absent

Examples

The inexistence of data
The inexistence of reliable data made it hard to draw any conclusions.
Many people debate the inexistence of certain mythical creatures.
The lawyer argued that the inexistence of a contract meant there was no agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonexistence - the state of not existing
