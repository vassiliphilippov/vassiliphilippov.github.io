rotor shaft

noun
axis for a helicopter's main rotor
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtər ʃæft / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtə ʃɑːft / (BrE)
the central axis around which the main rotor of a helicopter or similar machine rotates

Examples

Damage to the rotor shaft can result in significant vibration and loss of control during flight.
The engineer inspected the rotor shaft for signs of metal fatigue after the test flight.
