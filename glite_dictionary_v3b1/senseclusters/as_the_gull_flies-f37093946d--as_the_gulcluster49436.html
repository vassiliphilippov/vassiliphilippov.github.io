as the gull flies

adverb
in a straight line (direct distance)
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /æz ðə gʌl flaɪz/ /; / /əz ðə gʌl flaɪz/ (AmE), /æz ðə gʌl flaɪz/ /; / /əz ðə gʌl flaɪz/ (BrE)
measured in a straight line between two places, rather than by the route taken on roads or paths

Examples

As the gull flies distance
