filterer

noun
person or device filtering
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪltərər / (AmE), / ˈfɪltərə / (BrE)
a person or device that removes unwanted substances or information by passing it through a filter

Examples

The filterer device
The filterer in the aquarium keeps the water clean for the fish.
As a data filterer, the software blocks unwanted emails from reaching your inbox.
A skilled filterer can separate useful information from irrelevant details quickly.

  • synonym: purifier - a device or substance that makes something pure
