blended whiskey

noun
whiskey made from a blend of whiskeys or neutral spirits
2.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈblen.dɪd ˈwɪs.kiː/ /; / /ˈblen.dɪd ˈhwɪs.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈblen.dɪd ˈwɪs.ki/ /; / /ˈblen.dɪd ˈhwɪs.ki/ (BrE)
whiskey made by mixing two or more different whiskeys, or by combining whiskey with neutral spirits
