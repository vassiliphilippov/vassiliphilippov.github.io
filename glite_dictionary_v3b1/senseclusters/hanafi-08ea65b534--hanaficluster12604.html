Hanafi

adjective
of Hanafi school
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːnəfi / (AmE), / ˈhænəfi / (BrE)
relating to the Hanafi school of Islamic law, its teachings, or its legal rulings

Examples

Hanafi legal opinion
The Hanafi interpretation of the law is followed by many Muslims in South Asia.
She studied Hanafi legal rulings to better understand Islamic finance.
A Hanafi scholar explained the differences between the major schools of thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Hanbali - relating to the Hanbali school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Maliki - relating to the Maliki school of Islamic law
  • antonym: Shafi'i - relating to the Shafi'i school of Islamic law
