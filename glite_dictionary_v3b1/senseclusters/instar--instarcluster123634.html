instar

noun
life stage of insects between molts
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnˌstɑr / (AmE), / ˈɪnˌstɑː / (BrE)
a stage in the life cycle of an insect or other arthropod between two periods of molting

Examples

An instar stage
After each molt, the insect enters a new instar stage.
Mosquito larvae pass through several instars during their development.
The caterpillar entered its final instar before becoming a chrysalis.
