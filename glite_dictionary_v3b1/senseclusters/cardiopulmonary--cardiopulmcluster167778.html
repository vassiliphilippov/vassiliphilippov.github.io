cardiopulmonary

adjective
of the heart and lungs
Pronunciation: / kɑːrdioʊˈpʌlməˌnɛri / (AmE), / kɑːdiəʊˈpʌlmənəri / (BrE)
Relating to or affecting both the heart and the lungs and their functions.

Examples

Cardiopulmonary resuscitation
CPR stands for cardiopulmonary resuscitation, a life-saving technique.
He specializes in cardiopulmonary diseases such as heart failure and asthma.
The patient required cardiopulmonary monitoring after surgery.
