Synonyms

  • grave
    7.7 0.05/m

  • chisel
    5.1 0.9/m
    chisel means cutting or shaping wood or stone with a chisel tool, while carve can mean shaping by hand or many tools.
  • sculpt
    4.3 3/m

  • etch
