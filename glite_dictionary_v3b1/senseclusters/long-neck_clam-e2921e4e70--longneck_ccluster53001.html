long-neck clam

noun
clam with a long siphon, eaten as seafood
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ nɛk klæm /; / ˈlɔŋ nɛk klæm / (AmE), / lɒŋ nɛk klæm /; / ˈlɒŋ nɛk klam / (BrE)
an edible clam with a long, thin neck (siphon) and a thin, oval shell, commonly found in coastal areas and eaten as seafood
