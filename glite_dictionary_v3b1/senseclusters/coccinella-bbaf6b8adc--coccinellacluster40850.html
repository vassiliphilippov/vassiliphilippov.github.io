Coccinella

proper noun
genus of ladybugs
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑksɪˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˌkɒksɪˈnɛlə / (BrE)
a genus of small beetles commonly known as ladybirds or ladybugs, recognized for their rounded bodies and bright colors
