souk

noun
Arab open-air market
8.9 0.4/m
Pronunciation: / suÀêk / (AmE), / suÀêk / (BrE)
an open-air market, especially in an Arab city or town

Examples

A souk market
On our trip to Morocco, we visited a famous souk in Marrakesh.
The souk was filled with stalls selling spices, textiles, and jewelry.
We wandered through the bustling souk, amazed by the colors and scents.

Synonyms

  • suq
    9.9 0.03/m
    suq is a direct transliteration used in formal writing and maps for an Arab marketplace, while souk is the common everyday spelling.
  • bazar
    4.9 0.08/m
    bazar is a broad market word used in South Asia and the Middle East, while souk refers to an open-air Arab market.
