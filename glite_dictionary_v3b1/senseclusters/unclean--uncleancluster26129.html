unclean

adjective
not clean; dirty
1.3 0.36/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàklin / (AmE), /  ånÀàkliÀên / (BrE)
dirty or not clean; soiled or likely to soil with dirt or grime.

Examples

Unclean clothes
He felt uncomfortable in his unclean clothes after the long hike.
Leaving unclean dishes in the sink can attract pests.
The inspector noticed the unclean floors in the restaurant kitchen.

Synonyms

  • dirty
    1.0 25/m
    dirty is the common everyday word for things covered in grime or mud, while unclean sounds slightly more formal.
  • soiled
    2.5 0.05/m
    soiled often describes clothing or surfaces stained with marks, while unclean refers to anything that is dirty.
  • unsanitized
    5.7 0.00/m
    unsanitized means not cleaned to remove germs or contaminants, while unclean mainly means simply dirty or soiled.
  • unsanitary
    3.7 0.77/m
    unsanitary describes places that risk causing illness from poor hygiene, while unclean only means dirty or soiled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dirty - covered or marked with dirt; not clean
