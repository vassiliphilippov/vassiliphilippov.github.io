substance

noun
wealth or resources
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbstəns / (AmE), / ˈsʌbstəns / (BrE)
considerable wealth or financial resources; material means or property

Examples

A person of substance
Even without a title, he was respected because he was a person of substance.
Philanthropy is often supported by those with great substance and social influence.
She married a man of substance who owned several estates across Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: means - wealth; financial resources
  • antonym: poverty - the state of having little or no money or material possessions
