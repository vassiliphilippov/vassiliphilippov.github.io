slideway

noun
sloping channel for descending objects
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪdˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈslaɪd.weɪ / (BrE)
A sloping channel or track that allows objects to move downward, typically by the force of gravity.

Examples

A slideway track
Jammed paper on the slideway caused a delay in production.
Maintenance workers checked the slideway for any debris or obstructions.
The packages moved down the slideway into the loading area.
