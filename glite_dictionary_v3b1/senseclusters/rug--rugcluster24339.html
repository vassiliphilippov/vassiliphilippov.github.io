rug

noun
thick floor covering made of fabric
3.8
Pronunciation: / rʌɡ / (AmE), / rʌɡ / (BrE)
A floor covering made of thick, heavy fabric, usually with a soft surface, used for warmth or decoration.

Examples

A rug on floor
After spilling my drink, I cleaned the rug carefully.
She placed a bright red rug in front of the fireplace.
The dog likes to sleep on the soft rug in the hallway.
