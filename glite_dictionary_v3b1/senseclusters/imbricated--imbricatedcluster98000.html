imbricated

adjective
overlapping like tiles or shingles
9.5 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɪm.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈɪm.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
(of leaves, scales, or similar objects) overlapping each other in a regular arrangement, like tiles or shingles on a roof.

Examples

The roof was made with imbricated tiles to ensure rain would easily run off.
