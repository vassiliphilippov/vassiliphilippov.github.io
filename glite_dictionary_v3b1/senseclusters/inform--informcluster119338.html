inform

verb
to give information to someone
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɔrm / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːm / (BrE)
to give someone information about something; to tell someone about a fact or situation

Examples

To inform the team
Please inform me if there are any changes to the schedule.
She informed her manager of her decision to resign.
The police informed the victim about the progress of the case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advise - to recommend or inform someone about what to do
  • antonym: conceal - to keep something secret or hidden
  • synonym: notify - to formally tell someone about something
