deselect

verb
to remove from selection
5.8
Pronunciation: /diː.sə.ˈlekt/ /; / /dɪ.sə.ˈlekt/ (AmE), /diː.sə.ˈlekt/ /; / /dɪ.sə.ˈlekt/ (BrE)
to remove someone from a list of chosen candidates or decide not to choose them for a position, especially in politics or organizations

Examples

If you do not meet the requirements, the system may automatically deselect you from the shortlist.
Party members voted to deselect their representative before the upcoming election.
