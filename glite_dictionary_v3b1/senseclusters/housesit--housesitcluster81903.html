housesit

noun
home care assignment
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌsɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌsɪt / (BrE)
a period of time spent staying in and taking care of someone's home while they are away

Examples

A two-week housesit
She found a two-week housesit in the city while the owners were on vacation.
During his summer break, he took a housesit to earn some extra money.
The housesit included caring for two cats and watering the plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babysit - to look after a child while the parents are away
  • synonym: house-sitting - the act of staying in and looking after someone's home while they are away
