tosspot

noun
habitual drunkard
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑspɑt / (AmE), / ˈtɒspɒt / (BrE)
an old-fashioned term for a habitual drunkard or a foolish person

Examples

A tosspot drunkard
He laughed and called his friend a tosspot after seeing him stumble out of the bar.
In old novels, you might find the word tosspot used to describe a foolish drunk.
The villagers whispered that the local tosspot had once been a respected teacher.
