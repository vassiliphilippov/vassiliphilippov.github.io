katakana

noun
Japanese syllabary
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑtəˈkɑnə / (AmE), / ˌkatəˈkɑːnə /; / ˌkætəˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
a Japanese syllabary mainly used for writing foreign words, names, onomatopoeia, and technical terms
