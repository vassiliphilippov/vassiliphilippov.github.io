turnout

noun
number attending an event
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrnaʊt /; / ˈtɝːnˌaʊt / (AmE), / ˈtɜːnaʊt / (BrE)
the number of people who attend or participate in an event, meeting, or election

Examples

High turnout at elections
Despite the rain, the concert had a good turnout.
There was a surprisingly large turnout at the community meeting last night.
Voter turnout in the last election was higher than expected.
Despite the rain, there was a good turnout at the outdoor concert.
Organizers were disappointed by the low turnout for the community meeting.
The charity event had an impressive turnout this year, with over 500 guests attending.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absence - the state or fact of not being present at an event
  • synonym: attendance - the act of being present at a place
