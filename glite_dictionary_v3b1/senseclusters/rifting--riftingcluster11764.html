rifting

noun
causing a split
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪftɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪftɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of causing a serious disagreement or split between people, groups, or organizations

Examples

Rifting in a team
The new regulations led to rifting within the organization, making teamwork difficult.
Years of unresolved issues resulted in rifting between the two communities.
Political disagreements have caused significant rifting among the members of the committee.

Related Terms

  • synonym: division - the act of separating into parts or groups
  • antonym: unity - the state of being joined together or in agreement
