convertor

noun
device that changes form or state
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝːtəɹ / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːtə / (BrE)
a device that changes something from one form, substance, or state into another

Examples

A convertor device
A frequency convertor allows electronic devices to operate on different power standards.
The chemical plant uses a convertor to transform raw materials into finished products.
The factory installed a new convertor to process waste into usable energy.
