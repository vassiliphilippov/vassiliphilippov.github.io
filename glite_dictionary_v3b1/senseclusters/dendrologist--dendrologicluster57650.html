dendrologist

noun
tree scientist
Pronunciation: / dɛnˈdrɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / dɛnˈdrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies trees and other woody plants

Examples

The dendrologist studies trees
The dendrologist spent years cataloging the different tree species in the national park.
A skilled dendrologist can identify trees by their leaves and bark alone.
The university hired a dendrologist to help restore the ancient woodland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants
  • synonym: forester - a person who manages or studies forests
