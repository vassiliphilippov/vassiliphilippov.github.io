test rocket

noun
rocket launched for testing purposes
Pronunciation: / tɛst ˈrɑːkɪt / (AmE), / tɛst ˈrɒkɪt / (BrE)
a rocket that is launched to test its design, performance, or components, rather than for operational or production use

Examples

A test rocket launch
Data from the test rocket helped improve the next generation of space vehicles.
Several test rockets exploded during the propulsion trials last year.
The engineers launched a test rocket to analyze the flight dynamics before building the production model.
