redecorator

noun
person who redecorates
3.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàd…õk…ôÀåre…™t…ôr / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàd…õk…ôre…™t…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to change the appearance or style of rooms or buildings by redecorating them

Examples

A redecorator at work
We hired a redecorator to give our office a fresh, modern look.
The redecorator suggested new colors and furniture for the living room.
After the renovation, the redecorator added stylish touches to every room.
