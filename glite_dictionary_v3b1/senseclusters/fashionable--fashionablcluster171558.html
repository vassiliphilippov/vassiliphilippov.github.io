Synonyms

    2.6 1.62/m
    in fashion is used to say something is currently popular and worn by many, while fashionable follows the current style.
  • in style
    2.9 2.59/m
    in style describes clothes or ideas that people like now and wear often, while fashionable follows current trends.
  • in vogue
    3.8 0.92/m
    in vogue is a formal way to say something is popular in this moment, while fashionable means following current style.
  • trendy
    3.1 3.86/m
    trendy describes something very new and popular for a short time, while fashionable can be more general and lasting.
  • on trend
    3.7 0.09/m
    on trend means matching the newest looks people wear now, while fashionable covers anything following current style.
  • stylish
    2.0 6.83/m

  • chic
    3.5 4.98/m
