barnstorming

noun
traveling performances
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrnˌstɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɑːnˌstɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving exciting public performances, especially by pilots or entertainers traveling to different places

Examples

Barnstorming airshow
The pilot amazed the audience with daring barnstorming at the airshow.
In the 1920s, barnstorming was a popular form of entertainment in rural America.
The magician's barnstorming across small towns drew large crowds every night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: performance - an act of presenting entertainment to an audience
  • synonym: touring - traveling to different places to perform or present
