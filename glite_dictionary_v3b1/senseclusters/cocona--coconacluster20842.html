cocona

noun
tropical fruit plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / koʊˈkoʊnə / (AmE), / kəˈkəʊnə / (BrE)
a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America that produces edible yellow or orange fruit

Examples

Cocona plant
The farmer harvested ripe cocona from the bushes to sell at the local market.
In the Amazon region, people often use cocona to make refreshing juices and sauces.
A young cocona grew at the edge of the garden, its glossy leaves and small yellow fruits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tomato - an edible fruit from a related plant in the nightshade family
