befuddled

adjective
confused or stupefied, often due to alcohol
Pronunciation: / bɪˈfʌdəld /; / bɪˈfʌd.əld / (AmE), / bɪˈfʌdəld /; / bɪˈfʌd.əld / (BrE)
confused and unable to think clearly

Examples

Feeling befuddled after drinks
After several drinks, he became befuddled and could barely speak coherently.
She looked befuddled when asked complicated questions, as if her mind was clouded.
The befuddled driver struggled to remember where he had parked his car.
He looked befuddled after reading the complex instructions.
She gave me a befuddled stare when I mentioned the changes to the schedule.
The befuddled manager forgot where he had put the important documents.
After staying up all night, he felt completely befuddled during the exam.
Alcohol had left him befuddled and unable to remember what happened.
The speaker's befuddled explanation left the audience even more confused.

  • antonym: clearheaded - able to think clearly; not confused
  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • synonym: perplexed - completely baffled or puzzled
