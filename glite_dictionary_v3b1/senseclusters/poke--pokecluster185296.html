poke

noun
a slow or sluggish person
Pronunciation: / poʊk / (AmE), / pəʊk / (BrE)
a person who moves slowly or takes longer than necessary to do something; someone who lags behind

Quit being such a poke—we're going to be late!
The group had to wait for the poke to catch up during the hike.
