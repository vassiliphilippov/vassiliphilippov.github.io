top up

verb
refill or add money/credit to full
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / tɑp ʌp / (AmE), / tɒp ʌp / (BrE)
to refill something so that it is full, or to add more credit or money to an account or device until it reaches the desired level.

Examples

To top up the tank

Please top up my glass with more water before dinner.
You should top up your mobile credit to avoid running out of data.
