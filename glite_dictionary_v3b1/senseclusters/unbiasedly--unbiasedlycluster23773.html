unbiasedly

adverb
fairly and impartially
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaɪəstli / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaɪəstli / (BrE)
in a fair and impartial way, without being influenced by personal feelings or prejudice

Examples

To judge unbiasedly

She tried to judge the competition unbiasedly, without letting her friendships influence her decision.
The judge listened unbiasedly to both sides before making a decision.
Scientists must analyze data unbiasedly to ensure accurate results.
She tried to evaluate the proposals unbiasedly, without letting her personal opinions interfere.

  • synonym: impartially - in a way that treats all sides equally
