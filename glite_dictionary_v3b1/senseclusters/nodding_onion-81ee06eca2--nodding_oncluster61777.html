nodding onion

noun
a wild North American onion with drooping flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑdɪŋ ˈʌnjən / (AmE), / ˈnɒdɪŋ ˈʌnjən / (BrE)
A North American wild onion (Allium cernuum) with a drooping flower head of white to rose-colored blooms.

Examples

The nodding onion flower
Many pollinators are attracted to the nodding onion because of its abundant nectar.
She planted several nodding onions in her native wildflower garden.
The nodding onion produces delicate, drooping clusters of pinkish flowers in mid-summer.
