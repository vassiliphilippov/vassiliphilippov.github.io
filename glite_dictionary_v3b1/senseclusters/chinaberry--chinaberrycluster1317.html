chinaberry

noun
tree with purple flowers and yellow berries
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪnəˌbɛri / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪnəbɛri / (BrE)
a tree called 'chinaberry', often grown for shade, with small yellow fruits; found in Asia and the Americas
