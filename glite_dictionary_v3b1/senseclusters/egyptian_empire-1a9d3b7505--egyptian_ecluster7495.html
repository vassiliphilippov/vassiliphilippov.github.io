Egyptian Empire

proper noun
ancient empire of northeastern Africa
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈɛmpaɪər / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ˈɛmpaɪə / (BrE)
the ancient empire in northeastern Africa, centered along the Nile and ruled by Pharaohs, known for its powerful dynasties and influence on early civilization

Examples

The Egyptian Empire dynasty
Artifacts from the Egyptian Empire can be found in museums around the globe.
Many historians consider the Egyptian Empire one of the world's earliest and most influential civilizations.
The Egyptian Empire was ruled by a succession of powerful Pharaohs for centuries.
