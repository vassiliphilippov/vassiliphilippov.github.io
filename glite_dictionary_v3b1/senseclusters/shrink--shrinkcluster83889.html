Synonyms

  • decrease
    2.0 42/m
    decrease is a general verb for becoming smaller in amount or size, while shrink often suggests physical or measurable reduction.
  • reduce
    1.6 181/m
    reduce is used for making something smaller, often by choice, while shrink can happen without plan or by wearing out.
  • cut down
    2.7 3.73/m
    cut down means actively reduce number or amount, often by choice, while shrink can be gradual or automatic.
  • scale down
    4.0 0.84/m
    scale down means make something smaller in size or scope intentionally, while shrink can mean becoming smaller on its own.
  • lessen
    2.8 4.32/m
    lessen means reduce degree or severity in a gentle way, while shrink usually refers to amount, size, or value.
  • diminish
    3.7 20/m
    diminish means make something have less importance or value, while shrink focuses more on size or amount.
  • go down
    1.9 13/m
    go down describes numbers or levels falling, often informally, while shrink often suggests getting smaller in size or amount.
