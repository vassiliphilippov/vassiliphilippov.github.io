outcross

verb
to breed with different line
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.krɑːs/ /; / /ˈaʊt.krɔːs/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.krɒs/ (BrE)
to breed an animal or plant with another from a different genetic line to increase genetic diversity
