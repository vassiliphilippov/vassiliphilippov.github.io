joe job

noun
forged email attack to discredit someone
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒoʊ dʒɑb / (AmE), / dʒəʊ dʒɒb / (BrE)
An email attack in which messages are forged to appear to come from a target (or to appear sent by the target) in order to discredit, harass, or frame them.
