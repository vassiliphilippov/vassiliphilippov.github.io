oyster park

noun
oyster breeding or growing area
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪstɚ pɑrk / (AmE), / ˈɔɪstə pɑːk / (BrE)
an area or facility where oysters are bred and grown for commercial purposes

Examples

An oyster park farm
During our visit, we toured an oyster park and learned about the process of oyster farming.
Local authorities regulate the water quality in every oyster park to ensure a healthy environment for the shellfish.
The company owns a large oyster park along the coast, where thousands of oysters are cultivated each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oyster bed - a place on the bottom of a body of water where oysters live and grow
