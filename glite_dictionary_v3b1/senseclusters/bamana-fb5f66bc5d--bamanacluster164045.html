Bamana

proper noun
West African kingdom
Pronunciation: / bəˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / bəˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a historic West African kingdom, also known as the Bambara or Ségou Empire, centered on Ségou in the 18th and 19th centuries

Examples

Bamana Empire history
The Bamana played a significant role in the history of West Africa during the 18th century.
Many cultural traditions in Mali today trace their roots to the Bamana kingdom.
Historians study the rise and fall of the Bamana to understand regional power shifts in West Africa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bambara Empire - another name for the Bamana kingdom in West Africa
  • synonym: Mali Empire - a major West African empire that preceded the Bamana
