wave

noun
hairstyle with curved ridges
Pronunciation: / weɪv / (AmE), / weɪv / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the hair is arranged in smooth, curved ridges or undulations

Examples

Waves in hair
He used a curling iron to create soft waves throughout her hair.
She styled her hair in elegant waves for the wedding.
The vintage photo showed women with perfectly set waves framing their faces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curl - a piece or strand of hair arranged in a spiral or curved shape
