prosumer

noun
producer-consumer
7.2
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsuːmər / (AmE), / prəˈsjuːmə / (BrE)
a person who both produces and consumes a product or service, often with advanced knowledge or involvement

Examples

Prosumer energy user
The company designed its new software for the growing prosumer market.
As a prosumer, she not only uses the product but also gives feedback to improve it.
Many prosumers share their own content online while also enjoying others' creations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: consumer - a person who buys and uses products or services
  • antonym: producer - a person or company that makes or creates goods or services
