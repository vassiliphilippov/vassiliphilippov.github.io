twofer

noun
two for one deal or coupon
Pronunciation: / ˈtuː.fɚ /; / ˈtuːfər / (AmE), / ˈtuː.fə /; / ˈtuːfə / (BrE)
a special offer or deal where you get two items for the price of one

Examples

A twofer deal
During the holiday sale, many stores featured twofer deals on select merchandise.
I picked up a twofer coupon for coffee at the café.
The theater offered a twofer promotion for Friday night's performance.
She went to buy shoes and scored a twofer at her favorite store.
The pizza place is running a twofer special every Friday night.
Today at the cinema, there's a twofer deal on all movie tickets.
