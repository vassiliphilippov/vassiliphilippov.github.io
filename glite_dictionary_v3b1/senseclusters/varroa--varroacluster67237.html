varroa

noun
bee parasite mite
Pronunciation: / vəˈroʊə / (AmE), / vəˈrəʊə / (BrE)
a parasitic mite that infests and harms honey bee colonies

Examples

Varroa mite problem
The spread of varroa has become a major threat to honey bee populations worldwide.
Beekeepers regularly inspect their hives for signs of varroa infestation.
Effective management of varroa is essential for healthy and productive bee colonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mite - a small arachnid related to ticks
  • synonym: parasite - an organism that lives on or in another and harms it
