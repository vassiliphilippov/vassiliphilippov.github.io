hubbly-bubbly

noun
water pipe for smoking flavored tobacco
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌbli ˌbʌbli / (AmE), / ˈhʌbli ˌbʌbli / (BrE)
a water pipe for smoking flavored tobacco, in which the smoke passes through water before being inhaled; also known as a hookah or shisha
