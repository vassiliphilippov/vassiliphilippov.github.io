calendaring

noun
listing documents chronologically
Pronunciation: / ˈkælənˌdɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkælɪndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of creating a chronological list or summary of documents or records, especially for archival or research purposes

Examples

Document calendaring
The historian spent weeks calendaring the letters to make them easier to study.
Accurate calendaring of old documents helps researchers track events over time.
The archive's calendaring project made it possible to quickly find relevant records.
