pallasite

noun
type of meteorite (olivine and metal)
Pronunciation: / ˈpæl.əˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈpal.ə.sʌɪt / (BrE)
A type of meteorite consisting mainly of the mineral olivine embedded in a matrix of metallic iron and nickel.

Examples

A pallasite meteorite
A pallasite can provide valuable clues about the formation of our solar system.
Geologists examined the pallasite to study its distinctive olivine crystals embedded in iron.
The museum's new display features a rare pallasite recovered from a desert in Argentina.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meteorite - a solid piece of debris from a space object that survives passage through the atmosphere
