seat cushion

noun
cushion for comfort on a seat
3.1
Pronunciation: / sit ˈkʊʃən / (AmE), / siːt ˈkʊʃən / (BrE)
a cushion designed to be placed on a seat to provide additional comfort or support

Examples

A seat cushion pad
For her aching back, he bought a special orthopedic seat cushion for her office chair.
She placed a seat cushion on the chair to make it more comfortable for long hours at her desk.
The car's seat cushion was worn out after years of use and needed to be replaced.
