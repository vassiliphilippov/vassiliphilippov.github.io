state-controlled

adjective
under government ownership or control
2.3 4.7
Pronunciation: /steɪt kən.ˈtroʊld/ /; / /steɪt.kən.ˈtroʊld/ (AmE), /steɪt kən.ˈtrəʊld/ /; / /steɪt.kən.ˈtrəʊld/ (BrE)
(of an organization, industry, or activity) under the ownership or direct control of the government rather than private individuals or companies.

Examples

State-controlled economy
In a state-controlled economy, the government makes most financial decisions.
Many industries were state-controlled following the revolution.
The state-controlled media limited coverage of the protests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: private - not under government control or ownership; owned by individuals or companies
