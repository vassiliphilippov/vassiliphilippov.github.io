lobworm

noun
marine worm used as fishing bait
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑbˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈlɒbˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a large marine worm with a row of tufted gills along each side of its back, often used as fishing bait

Examples

A lobworm for bait
Anglers often dig for lobworm to use as bait for sea fishing.
Many species of fish are especially attracted to the scent of the lobworm.
The fisherman baited his hook with a fat lobworm before casting into the surf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ragworm - another type of marine worm used as bait
