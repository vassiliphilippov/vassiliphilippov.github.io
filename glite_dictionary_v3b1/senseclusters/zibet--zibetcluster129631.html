zibet

noun
Asian mammal; musky secretion
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪbɛt / (AmE), / ˈzɪbɛt / (BrE)
a small Asian carnivorous mammal or the musky substance it produces, used in traditional perfumery

Examples

The zibet musk
The zibet is prized for its musky scent in traditional perfumery.
In some regions, the zibet is protected due to overhunting for its valuable secretion.
Perfume makers once used the zibet to create long-lasting fragrances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civet - a small, nocturnal mammal known for its musky scent
  • synonym: musk - a strong-smelling secretion used in perfumes
