HTTPS

noun
secure web protocol
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪtʃ.tiː.tiː.piː.es/ (AmE), /ˈeɪtʃ.tiː.tiː.piː.es/ (BrE)
the part at the beginning of a website address that shows the site uses a secure, encrypted connection

Examples

HTTPS address bar

Websites that use HTTPS help protect your data from being intercepted.
The browser displayed a warning because the site did not have HTTPS enabled.
