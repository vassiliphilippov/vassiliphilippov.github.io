segregationism

noun
policy supporting racial or social segregation
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /se.grə.ˈgeɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /se.grə.ˈgeɪ.ʃən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
