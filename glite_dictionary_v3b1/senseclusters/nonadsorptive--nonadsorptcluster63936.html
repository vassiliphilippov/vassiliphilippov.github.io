nonadsorptive

adjective
not capable of adsorbing
8.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.ædˈsɔr.p.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.ædˈsɔːp.tɪv / (BrE)
Not capable of adsorbing or causing the accumulation of substances on a surface.

Examples

Nonadsorptive surface
Because the coating is nonadsorptive, it does not interfere with the chemical analysis.
The filter was specifically designed to be nonadsorptive, preventing contaminants from sticking to its surface.
They chose a nonadsorptive packing material for the experiment to ensure accurate results.
