table tennis

noun
indoor game played with paddles on a table
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl ˌtɛnɪs / (AmE), / ˈteɪbəl ˌtɛnɪs / (BrE)
A game resembling tennis, played on a table with small paddles and a lightweight ball hit back and forth across a net.

Examples

Table tennis match
He won several gold medals in table tennis at the national tournament.
The school has started a new table tennis club for students interested in indoor sports.
They play table tennis every evening after dinner in the recreation room.
