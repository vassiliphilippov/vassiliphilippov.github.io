ese

noun
slang for friend
Pronunciation: / ˈɛseɪ / (AmE)
a slang term used to address a man or friend, especially among some Latino communities in the United States

Examples

Hey ese friend
He walked up to his friend and said, 'Hey ese, how have you been?'
In some neighborhoods, people greet each other with the word ese as a sign of friendship.
You might hear someone call out 'ese!' when meeting a buddy on the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homie - a slang term for a close friend from one's neighborhood
