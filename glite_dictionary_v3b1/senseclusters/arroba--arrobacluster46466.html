arroba

noun
a liquid measure (Spanish-speaking countries)
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈroʊbə / (AmE), / əˈrəʊbə / (BrE)
A liquid measure used in some Spanish-speaking countries, with varying values depending on the region.

Examples

A arroba of wine
Farmers in Spain once measured their grape harvests in arrobas.
In colonial times, an arroba of wine was a common unit of purchase.
The recipe called for an arroba of olive oil, which is much more than a liter.
