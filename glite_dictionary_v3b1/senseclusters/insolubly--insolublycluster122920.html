insolubly

adverb
impossibly to solve
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɑːljəbli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɒljʊbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be solved or separated

Examples

Insolubly connected
The two issues are insolubly connected and cannot be addressed separately.
Some mysteries remain insolubly complex despite years of research.
Their fates were insolubly intertwined by the events of that day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inextricably - in a way that cannot be separated or untangled
  • antonym: solubly - in a way that can be solved or dissolved
