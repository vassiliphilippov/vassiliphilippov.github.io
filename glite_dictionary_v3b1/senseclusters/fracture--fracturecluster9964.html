Synonyms

  • break
    1.0 2.91/m
    break is a sudden separation of something into parts, while fracture usually names a break in bone or hard material.
  • crack
    1.0 16/m
    crack is a narrow, often visible opening on a surface, while fracture suggests a deeper break in bone or rock.
  • split
    1.1 1.11/m
    split is a separation that divides something into parts, while fracture usually refers to a single break in bone or hard material.
