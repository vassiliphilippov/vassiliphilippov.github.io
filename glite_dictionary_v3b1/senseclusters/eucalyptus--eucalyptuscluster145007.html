eucalyptus

noun
an aromatic Australian evergreen tree
5.3 5.3
Pronunciation: / juːˌkəˈlɪptəs / (AmE), / juːˌkəˈlɪptəs / (BrE)
A fast-growing evergreen tree native to Australia, belonging to the genus Eucalyptus, with aromatic leaves often used medicinally and as food for koalas.

Examples

Eucalyptus tree leaves
Koalas feed almost exclusively on eucalyptus leaves, which are toxic to most other animals.
Several eucalyptus trees were planted in the park to provide shade and improve the air quality.
The distinctive scent of eucalyptus leaves filled the air during our walk through the forest.
