overbuild

verb
to build above
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈbɪld / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈbɪld / (BrE)
to construct additional buildings or structures on top of or above an existing one

Examples

To overbuild a roof
The city decided to overbuild the old library by adding two new floors.
Developers want to overbuild the shopping center with luxury apartments.
It is common in urban areas to overbuild existing structures to maximize space.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demolish - to completely destroy a building
  • synonym: extend - to make something larger or longer
