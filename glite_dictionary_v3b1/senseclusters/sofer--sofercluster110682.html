sofer

noun
Jewish religious scribe
Pronunciation: / soʊˈfɛr / (AmE), / ˈsəʊfə / (BrE)
a Jewish scribe who writes Torah scrolls and other religious texts by hand according to traditional rules

Examples

The sofer writes Torah
The sofer spent months preparing a new Torah scroll for the synagogue.
Each letter written by the sofer must follow strict religious guidelines.
A skilled sofer is highly respected in Jewish communities for preserving sacred texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who copies out documents, especially in the past
