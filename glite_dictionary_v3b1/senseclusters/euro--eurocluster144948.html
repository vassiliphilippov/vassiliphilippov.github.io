euro

noun
currency of most European Union countries
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊəroʊ / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
the official currency of most members of the European Union, used as their basic unit of money

Examples

The euro currency
She exchanged her dollars for euros before flying to Rome.
Since joining the European Union, Slovakia switched its currency to the euro.
The price of the book was fifteen euros at the Paris bookstore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dollar - the basic monetary unit in the United States and several other countries
  • antonym: pound - the basic monetary unit of the United Kingdom
  • antonym: yen - the basic monetary unit of Japan
