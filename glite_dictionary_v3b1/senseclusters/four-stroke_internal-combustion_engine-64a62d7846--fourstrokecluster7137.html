four-stroke internal-combustion engine

noun
internal combustion engine with four piston strokes
8.3 6.3
Pronunciation: / fɔr stroʊk ɪnˌtɜrnəl kəmˈbʌs.tʃən ˈɛn.dʒɪn / (AmE), / fɔː strəʊk ɪnˌtɜː.nəl kəmˈbʌs.tʃən ˈen.dʒɪn / (BrE)
an internal combustion engine that completes a power cycle in four strokes of the piston: intake, compression, power, and exhaust

Examples

A four-stroke internal-combustion engine
