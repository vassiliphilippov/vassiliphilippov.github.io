endochondral

adjective
within cartilage
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈkɑːndrəl / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈkɒndrəl / (BrE)
located within cartilage or relating to the formation of bone inside cartilage, especially during development

Examples

Researchers studied the endochondral process to better understand bone diseases.
Cartilage is gradually replaced by bone during endochondral development.
