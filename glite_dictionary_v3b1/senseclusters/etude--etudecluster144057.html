etude

noun
musical exercise piece for solo instrument
7.2
Pronunciation: / eɪˈtuːd / (AmE), / eɪˈtjuːd / (BrE)
a short musical composition for a solo instrument, written as an exercise or to showcase technical skill

Examples

A etude piece
Many famous composers have written at least one etude to challenge musicians.
She performed a Chopin etude at the piano recital.
The violinist practiced the difficult passages in the new etude.

Related Terms

  • synonym: study - a piece composed for practice of a particular musical skill
