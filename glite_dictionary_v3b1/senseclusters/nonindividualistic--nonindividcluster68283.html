nonindividualistic

adjective
not individualistic
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɪndɪˌvɪdjuˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
not emphasizing or based on individualism; focused on the group rather than the individual

Examples

Nonindividualistic approach
The company has a nonindividualistic approach, encouraging teamwork and shared responsibility.
In many nonindividualistic societies, group harmony is valued above personal achievement.
Her nonindividualistic attitude made her a great fit for the collaborative project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collectivist - emphasizing the group over the individual
  • antonym: individualistic - emphasizing the importance of the individual
