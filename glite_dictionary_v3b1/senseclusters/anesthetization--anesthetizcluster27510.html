anesthetization

noun
emotional numbing
8.6
Pronunciation: / əˌniːsθətɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌniːsθətaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a figurative numbing or loss of emotional responsiveness, awareness, or sensitivity, often due to repeated exposure.

Examples

Emotional anesthetization
Years of constant bad news produced a social anesthetization toward distant suffering.
The repeated exposure to violence in media led to an anesthetization of viewers' emotional responses.
Some people experience anesthetization after dealing with stressful situations for a long time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern
  • synonym: desensitization - the process of becoming less sensitive to something
  • antonym: sensitivity - the quality of being sensitive or responsive
