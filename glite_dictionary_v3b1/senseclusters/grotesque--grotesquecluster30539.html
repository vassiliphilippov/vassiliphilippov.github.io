grotesque

adjective
comically or repulsively strange
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɡroʊˈtɛsk / (AmE), / ɡrəʊˈtɛsk / (BrE)
strange, unnatural, or extremely distorted in a way that is comically or repulsively odd
