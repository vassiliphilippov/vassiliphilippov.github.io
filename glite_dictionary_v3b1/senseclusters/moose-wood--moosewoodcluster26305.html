moose-wood

noun
a North American maple tree with striped bark
6.7 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmuːs.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈmuːs.wʊd/ (BrE)
a maple tree of eastern North America (Acer pensylvanicum) with striped bark and large, two-lobed leaves that turn clear yellow in autumn

Examples

Moose-wood maple tree

The leaves of the moose-wood turn bright yellow in the fall, creating a striking display in the forest.
Wildlife such as moose and deer sometimes feed on the twigs of moose-wood during the winter.
