communication theory

noun
study of information transmission methods
5.8
Pronunciation: / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ˈθɪəri / (AmE), / kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən ˈθɪəri / (BrE)
the academic discipline that examines the principles, processes, and methods by which information is transmitted and received, especially through media such as print, radio, or television

Examples

Communication theory helps professionals design messages more effectively for television and radio.
