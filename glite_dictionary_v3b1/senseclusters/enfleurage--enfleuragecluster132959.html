enfleurage

noun
fragrance extraction method
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˈflɜːrɑːʒ /; / ɑ̃.flɚˈɑːʒ / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈflɜːrɑːʒ /; / ɑ̃.flœˈʁaʒ / (BrE)
the process or product of extracting fragrance from flowers by absorbing their scent into fat
