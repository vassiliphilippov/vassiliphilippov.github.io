self-conceitedly

adverb
in a self-conceited manner
9.1 7.3
Pronunciation: /self.kən.ˈsiː.tɪd.liː/ /; / /səlf.kən.ˈsiː.tɪd.liː/ (AmE), /self.kən.ˈsiː.tɪd.lɪ/ /; / /self.kən.ˈsiː.tɪd.liː/ (BrE)
In a self-conceited or overly proud manner; behaving as if one thinks highly of oneself.

Examples

To act self-conceitedly
He strutted self-conceitedly into the room, sure that everyone admired him.
She spoke self-conceitedly about her achievements, ignoring the contributions of her colleagues.
The student answered self-conceitedly, giving the impression he considered himself smarter than the rest.

  • synonym: conceitedly - in a conceited or vain manner
