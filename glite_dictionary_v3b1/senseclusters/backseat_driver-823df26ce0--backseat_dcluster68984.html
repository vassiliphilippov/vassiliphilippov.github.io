backseat driver

noun
person giving unwanted advice
Pronunciation: / ˌbækˌsiːt ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˌbakˌsiːt ˈdrʌɪvə / (BrE)
a person who gives unwanted advice or tries to control situations they are not directly responsible for, especially while someone else is driving

Examples

A backseat driver complains
Even on vacation, he couldn't resist being a backseat driver about the itinerary.
My sister is a notorious backseat driver whenever I'm at the wheel.
Stop being such a backseat driver—let me make my own decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: busybody - a person who is too interested in other people's business
  • synonym: meddler - a person who interferes in others' affairs
