Synonyms

  • pour out
    2.9 1/m
    pour out means to let liquid flow out in large, continuous amounts, while pour means making liquid flow in a steady stream.
  • stream
    1.7 3/m
    stream means to flow continuously and can describe liquids, people, or things, while pour focuses on liquid moving in large amounts.
  • pour forth
    7.1 0.08/m
    pour forth is a slightly formal phrase for liquid or things coming out in small or poetic bursts, while pour implies heavy steady flow.
