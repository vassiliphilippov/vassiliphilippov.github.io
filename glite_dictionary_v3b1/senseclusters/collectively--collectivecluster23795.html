  • as a group
    2.3 6.09/m
    as a group emphasizes members acting as a unit in everyday speech, while collectively is more neutral and formal.
  • jointly
    3.5 5.50/m
    jointly is common in legal or formal contexts for shared action, while collectively has a broader, less legal sense.
  • conjointly
    6.8 0.09/m
    conjointly is a rare, formal word for combined action, while collectively is common and used in many contexts.
  • in concert
    4.1 2.13/m
    in concert implies people act together with agreement or plan, while collectively simply means as a whole group.
  • concertedly
    6.2 0.02/m

  • en masse
    5.6 2.08/m

  • en bloc
