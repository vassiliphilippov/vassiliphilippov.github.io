quarterly

adjective, adverb
every three months
3.0 4.85/m
Pronunciation: / Ààkw…îrt…öli /; / Ààkw…îÀêrd…öli / (AmE), / Ààkw…îÀêt…ôli / (BrE)
happening, done, or produced every three months (four times a year)

Examples

Quarterly report
Dividends are paid quarterly to shareholders according to the schedule.
The company reviews its financial statements quarterly to track progress and set new goals.
The maintenance checks are performed quarterly to ensure safety compliance.
Many magazines are published on a quarterly basis rather than monthly.
Our team holds quarterly meetings to review progress and set new goals.
The company releases a quarterly report to keep investors informed.
