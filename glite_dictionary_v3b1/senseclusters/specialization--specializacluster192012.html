Examples

Medical specialization
After finishing medical school, she chose oncology as her specialization.
His specialization in environmental law made him a sought-after consultant.
The university offers a wide range of specialization options for graduate students.
Her specialization in cardiology helped her provide expert care to her patients.

The company underwent specialization to focus exclusively on renewable energy sources.

Synonyms

  • specialism
    7.4 0.12/m
    specialism is a focused area of study or work often used in British English, while specialization includes both the field and the process of gaining expertise.
  • specialty
    4.3 11/m
    specialty is an area of work, study, or a product someone is known for, while specialization emphasizes becoming an expert or adapting something for a purpose.
