parental quality

noun
characteristic appropriate to a parent
5.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛntl ˈkwɑːləti / (AmE), / pəˈrɛntl ˈkwɒləti / (BrE)
A characteristic or attribute regarded as suitable or expected in a parent.

Examples

A parental quality
Patience is often considered an important parental quality.

Trustworthiness is a key parental quality that children value in their caregivers.
