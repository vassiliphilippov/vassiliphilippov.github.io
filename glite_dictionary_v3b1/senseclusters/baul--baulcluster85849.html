baul

noun
Bengali spiritual music
Pronunciation: / baʊl / (AmE), / baʊl / (BrE)
a style of traditional spiritual music and songs from the Baul tradition of Bengal, often expressing mystical themes

Examples

Baul music
She listened to a baul performance that filled the hall with soulful melodies.
The festival featured several baul singers from rural Bengal.
Many people find peace in the spiritual messages of baul music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bhajan - a Hindu devotional song
  • synonym: folk music - traditional music passed down through generations
