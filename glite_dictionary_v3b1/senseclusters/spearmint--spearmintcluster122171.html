spearmint

noun
aromatic herb used as flavoring
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrˌmɪnt / (AmE), / ˈspɪə.mɪnt / (BrE)
A common garden herb with aromatic leaves and clusters of small purplish flowers, widely used for its minty flavor and essential oil.

Examples

Fresh spearmint leaves
Fresh spearmint leaves are often used to garnish desserts and beverages.
The chewing gum was flavored with natural spearmint oil.
You can smell the scent of spearmint when you walk through the herb garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peppermint - a hybrid mint with a stronger, more pungent flavor than spearmint
