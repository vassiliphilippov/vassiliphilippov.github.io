kitchen sink

noun
everything possible; a wide variety
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪtʃən sɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈkɪtʃɪn sɪŋk / (BrE)
a large or exhaustive variety of things, covering every possible option or element, often to excess

Examples

Everything but the kitchen sink
His essay tried to cover every argument, throwing in the kitchen sink for good measure.
The recipe includes spices, herbs, and even fruit—she really put the kitchen sink into it.
They packed the car with clothes, snacks, and electronics—almost the whole kitchen sink.
