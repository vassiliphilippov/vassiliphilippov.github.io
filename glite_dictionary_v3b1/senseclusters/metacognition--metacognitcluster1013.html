metacognition

noun
thinking about thinking
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˌkɑːɡˈnɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˌkɒɡˈnɪʃən / (BrE)
awareness and understanding of your own thought processes, including monitoring and controlling how you think and learn
