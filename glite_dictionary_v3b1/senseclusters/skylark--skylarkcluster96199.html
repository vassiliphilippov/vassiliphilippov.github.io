skylark

verb
frolic or play boisterously
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌlɑrk / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌlɑːk / (BrE)
to frolic or play boisterously, generally in a lively and carefree way

Examples

To skylark on deck
Instead of working, the sailors spent the afternoon skylarking on deck.
The children skylarked around the playground, laughing and shouting with joy.
We watched the puppies skylarking in the grassy field under the summer sun.
