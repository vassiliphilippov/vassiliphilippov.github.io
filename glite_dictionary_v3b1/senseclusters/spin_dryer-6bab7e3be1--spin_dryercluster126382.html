spin dryer

noun
machine for drying clothes by spinning
4.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /spɪn ˈdraɪ.ər/ /; / /spɪn ˈdʒraɪ.ər/ (AmE), /spɪn ˈdraɪ.ə/ (BrE)
A machine that dries clothes by spinning them at high speed to remove water through centrifugal force.

Examples

A spin dryer machine
After washing her shirts, she put them in the spin dryer for a quick dry.
He removed the damp towels from the washing machine and placed them into the spin dryer.
The spin dryer is ideal for small apartments where space is limited.
