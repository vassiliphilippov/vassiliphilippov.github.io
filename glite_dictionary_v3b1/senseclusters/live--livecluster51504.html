live

adjective
charged with explosive; able to explode
3.0 5.9
Pronunciation: / laɪv / (AmE), / laɪv / (BrE)
containing energy, able to act, or potentially dangerous (such as ammunition, explosives, wires, objects, or volcanoes)

Examples

It is illegal to carry live ammunition in certain public areas.

Scientists are monitoring the live volcano for signs of increased activity.

After the power outage, the crew made sure that no live equipment remained connected.
Do not touch that live wire, as it could give you a shock.
Electricians always check if a cable is live before working on it.
