bidi

noun
Indian hand-rolled cigarette
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːdi / (AmE), / ˈbiːdi / (BrE)
a thin, hand-rolled cigarette made from tobacco wrapped in a tendu leaf, commonly smoked in India

Examples

A bidi cigarette
He bought a pack of bidi from the corner shop.
Many workers in rural India smoke a bidi during their lunch break.
The distinctive aroma of a bidi filled the air at the festival.
