trust corporation

noun
company acting as trustee or fiduciary
Pronunciation: / trʌst ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / trʌst ˌkɒːpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a company, often a bank, authorized to act as a trustee or manage trust funds, estates, or related financial services

Examples

A trust corporation manages funds
Many clients rely on a trust corporation to administer their charitable foundations.
The bank set up a trust corporation to oversee the management of large estates.
When her father passed away, a trust corporation was appointed to manage the inheritance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trust company - a company that acts as trustee, fiduciary, or agent for individuals or businesses
