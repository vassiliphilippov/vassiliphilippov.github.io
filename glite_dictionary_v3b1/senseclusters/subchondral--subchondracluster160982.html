subchondral

adjective
beneath joint cartilage
9.4
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈkɑndrəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒndrəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath the cartilage of a joint

Examples

Subchondral bone fracture
The MRI revealed a subchondral fracture beneath the knee joint.
Osteoarthritis often leads to changes in the subchondral bone structure.
Doctors examined the subchondral area for signs of bone damage after the injury.
