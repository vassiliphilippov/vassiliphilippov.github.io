unlived

adjective
not experienced
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪvd / (BrE)
not experienced or not lived through; never having happened in one's life

Examples

Unlived dreams
He regretted the many unlived dreams of his youth.
The novel explores the pain of unlived possibilities and missed chances.
She felt a sense of loss for the unlived adventures she once imagined.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unfulfilled - not achieved or realized
