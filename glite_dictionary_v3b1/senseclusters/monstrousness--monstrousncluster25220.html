monstrousness

noun
extreme cruelty or ugliness
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnstrəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈmɒnstrəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being extremely ugly, cruel, or shocking

Examples

The monstrousness of his actions
The monstrousness of the dictator's actions horrified the world.
She could not believe the monstrousness of the lies told to the public.
The film depicted the monstrousness of war in a very realistic way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atrocity - an extremely cruel or violent act
  • synonym: brutality - cruel and violent behavior
  • antonym: kindness - the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate
