Marlovian

noun
Marlowe authorship supporter
9.9
Pronunciation: /mɑːr.ˈloʊ.viː.ən/ (AmE), /mɑː.ˈləʊ.viː.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports the theory that Christopher Marlowe, not William Shakespeare, wrote certain plays attributed to Shakespeare
