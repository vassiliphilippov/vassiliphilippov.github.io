act

verb
have an effect; produce a result
6.3
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / ækt / (BrE)
to have an effect, perform a function, or serve temporarily in a role

Examples

To act as a filter
The new cleaning solution can act very quickly on tough stains.
The new security system failed to act when the alarm was triggered.
This drug will act almost immediately once it enters your bloodstream.
He agreed to act in a temporary capacity until a replacement was found.
In his absence, I will act as the chair of the meeting.
She was asked to act as manager while her supervisor was away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: function - to perform an official function or duty
  • synonym: serve - to fulfill a role or function, especially officially
  • synonym: work - to be effective or successful in producing a result
