johnnie

noun
hospital gown
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑni / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒni / (BrE)
a slang term for a hospital gown worn by patients during medical care

Examples

Hospital johnnie
The nurse handed him a johnnie to wear during his stay.
After changing into a johnnie, she waited for the doctor to arrive.
He felt a bit exposed walking down the hallway in just a johnnie.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robe - a loose-fitting outer garment
