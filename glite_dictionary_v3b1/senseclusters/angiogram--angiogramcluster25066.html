angiogram

noun
X-ray image of blood vessels with dye
7.6 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈæn.dʒiː.oʊ.græm/ (AmE), /ˈæn.dʒiː.əʊ.græm/ (BrE)
an X-ray image of blood vessels that is produced after injecting a radiopaque dye to make them visible

Examples

An angiogram image
An angiogram revealed several narrowed blood vessels in her heart.
He was nervous before his angiogram, but the procedure went smoothly.
The doctor reviewed the angiogram to locate the blocked artery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiography - the medical imaging technique used to produce angiograms
