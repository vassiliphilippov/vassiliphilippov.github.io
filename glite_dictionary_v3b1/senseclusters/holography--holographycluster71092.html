holography

noun
technique for making 3D holograms
Pronunciation: / həˈlɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / hɒˈlɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the branch of optics that involves using coherent light, usually from a laser, to create holograms that can produce three-dimensional images

Examples

Holography with lasers
Researchers in holography have developed new methods to improve the quality of three-dimensional images.
Thanks to advancements in holography, realistic 3D displays are becoming more common.
The museum featured an exhibit on the history and future of holography.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hologram - a three-dimensional image created using holography
