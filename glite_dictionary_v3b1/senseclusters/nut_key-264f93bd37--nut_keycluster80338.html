nut key

noun
mountaineering tool for extracting nuts/chocks
7.8
Pronunciation: / nʌt ki / (AmE), / nʌt kiː / (BrE)
A tool used in mountaineering for removing a nut or chock from a crack in a rock after use.

Examples

A nut key tool
Every climber should carry a nut key when tackling traditional routes.
She used her nut key to remove the gear from the crack quickly.
Without a nut key, retrieving stuck chocks can be very difficult.
