monumentality

noun
impressive grandeur
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːnjəmenˈtæləti / (AmE), / ˌmɒnjʊmenˈtælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being grand, impressive, or massive, especially in size, importance, or lasting significance
