additively

adverb
in addition
5.7
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪt̬ɪvli / (AmE), / əˈdɪtɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that introduces an additional point or idea; in addition or moreover.

Examples

Additively mention a point
The new policy is effective; additively, it encourages more community involvement.
She explained the main points and, additively, provided several useful examples.
Additively, the report suggests that further research is needed in this area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: furthermore - used to add information or emphasize a point
  • antonym: however - used to introduce a contrast or exception
  • synonym: moreover - used to introduce an additional point
