kiwi

noun
fuzzy brown fruit with green flesh
1.0 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀêwi / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀêwi / (BrE)
A small, oval, fuzzy brown fruit with bright green flesh and tiny black seeds, known for its sweet and slightly tart flavor.

Examples

A kiwi fruit
I love the tangy flavor of a ripe kiwi for breakfast.
She sliced a fresh kiwi to add to the fruit salad.
The smoothie tasted delicious with banana and kiwi blended together.
