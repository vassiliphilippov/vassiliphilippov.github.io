kiwi

noun
fuzzy brown fruit with green flesh
1.0 0.19/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkiÀêwi / (AmE), / ÀàkiÀêwi / (BrE)
A small, oval, fuzzy brown fruit with bright green flesh and tiny black seeds, known for its sweet and slightly tart flavor.

Examples

A kiwi fruit
I love the tangy flavor of a ripe kiwi for breakfast.
She sliced a fresh kiwi to add to the fruit salad.
The smoothie tasted delicious with banana and kiwi blended together.

Synonyms

  • kiwifruit
    2.0 0.25/m
    kiwifruit is the standard modern name for the small fuzzy brown fruit eaten raw and in desserts, while kiwi is its short everyday name.
  • kiwi fruit
    1.6 0.05/m
    kiwi fruit is the two-word form often seen in recipes and labels, while kiwi is the shorter everyday name people use.
  • Chinese gooseberry
    3.5 0.00/m
    Chinese gooseberry is an older historical name once used in trade, while kiwi is the modern common name people say.
