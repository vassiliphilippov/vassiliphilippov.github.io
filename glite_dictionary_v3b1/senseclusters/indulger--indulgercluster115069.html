indulger

noun
one who pampers others
7.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒər/ /; / /ən.ˈdʌl.dʒər/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdʌl.dʒə/ /; / /ən.ˈdʌl.dʒə/ (BrE)
a person who allows others, especially children, to have or do whatever they want, often by spoiling or pampering them

Examples

A generous indulger
As a grandmother, she was known as an indulger, always giving her grandchildren extra treats.
The teacher warned that being an indulger could lead to spoiled children in the classroom.
He was an indulger when it came to his pets, letting them do whatever they wanted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disciplinarian - a person who enforces strict discipline
  • synonym: spoiler - a person who spoils or pampers others excessively
