attribution

noun
credit to creator
4.2
Pronunciation: /æ.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ /; / /ə.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ (AmE), /ə.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ /; / /æ.trɪ.ˈbjuː.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of giving credit to the creator or source of an idea, work, or information

Examples

Attribution of authorship
The article included proper attribution for all the photographs used.
Teachers emphasize the importance of attribution when students use online sources in their essays.
Without clear attribution, it is difficult to know who originally wrote the report.
