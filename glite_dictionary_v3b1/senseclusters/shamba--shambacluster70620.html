shamba

noun
small farm in East Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːmbə / (AmE), / ˈʃæmbə / (BrE)
a small farm or cultivated plot of land, especially in East Africa

Examples

A shamba farm
The family grows maize and beans on their shamba near the village.
During the rainy season, everyone helps to plant crops in the shamba.
They built a small house next to their shamba to watch over the crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farm - an area of land used for growing crops or raising animals
  • synonym: garden - a piece of ground for growing vegetables or flowers
