sweet talker

noun
one who flatters or charms with words
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswit ˌtɔkər / (AmE), / ˈswiːt ˌtɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who flatters or charms others, often with insincere or persuasive talk

Examples

A sweet talker charms
Don't let that sweet talker convince you to do something you'll regret.
He's such a sweet talker that he can sell anything to anyone.
She realized too late that he was just a sweet talker with no real intentions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charmer - a person who attracts and influences others through personal charm
  • synonym: flatterer - a person who praises others insincerely to gain advantage
