flatwise

adjective, adverb
with flat side down
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈflætˌwaɪz / (BrE)
in a flat position, with the broad surface facing down or horizontal

Examples

Lay flatwise on table
Lay the board flatwise on the table to prevent it from falling.
The tiles should be stacked flatwise to avoid chipping the edges.
He placed the book flatwise on the shelf instead of standing it up.
Place the glass flatwise on the shelf to prevent it from rolling.
The carpenter set the plank flatwise before measuring and cutting it.
For best results, lay the dough flatwise on the baking tray.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flat - having a level or even surface
  • antonym: upright - in a vertical position or standing on an edge
