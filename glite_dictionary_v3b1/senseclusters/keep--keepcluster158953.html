keep

noun
main tower of a medieval castle
5.1
Pronunciation: / kip / (AmE), / kiːp / (BrE)
the main tower within the walls of a medieval castle or fortress, serving as the last line of defense and residence for the lord

Examples

Castle keep
Many medieval keeps still stand today, towering over the countryside.
The lord and his family lived in the keep for added security.
The soldiers retreated to the keep when the outer walls were breached.

Related Terms
