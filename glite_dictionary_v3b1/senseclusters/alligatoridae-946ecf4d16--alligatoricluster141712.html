Alligatoridae

noun
family of reptiles: alligators and caimans
Pronunciation: / æˌlɪɡəˈtɛrɪˌdiː /; / əˌlɪɡəˈtɔrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ælɪˌɡeɪtəˈraɪdiː /; / əˌlɪɡəˈtɔːrɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of large aquatic reptiles, including alligators and caimans, found mainly in the Americas and China
