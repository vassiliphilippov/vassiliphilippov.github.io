statistical

adjective
relating to statistics
1.0
Pronunciation: / stəˈtɪstɪkəl / (AmE), / stəˈtɪstɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to, or based on statistics or the use of statistics.

Examples

Statistical summaries
She is responsible for preparing the statistical summaries each month.
The report contained several statistical analyses of the data.
There was a statistical increase in sales compared to last year.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: descriptive - involving description, especially in statistics (as in descriptive statistics)
  • synonym: statistic - a single numerical fact derived from a set of data
