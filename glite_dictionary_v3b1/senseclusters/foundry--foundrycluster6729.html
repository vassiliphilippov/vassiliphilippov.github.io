foundry

noun
factory where metal is cast
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndɹi / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndɹi / (BrE)
A factory where metal is melted and cast into shapes using molds.

Examples

A foundry factory
The city became prosperous due to its thriving steel foundry industry.
The old foundry was converted into an art gallery after it closed.
Workers in the foundry must wear protective equipment at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forge - workshop where metal is shaped by heating and hammering
