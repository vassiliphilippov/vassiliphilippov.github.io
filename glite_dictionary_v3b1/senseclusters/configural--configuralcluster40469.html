configural

adjective
relating to arrangement
8.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɪɡjərəl / (AmE), / kənˈfɪɡjʊrəl / (BrE)
relating to the arrangement or relationships of parts within a whole, especially as an integrated structure

Examples

Configural processing
The experiment measured configural processing in how people recognize faces.
Researchers analyzed the configural properties of the new architectural design.
Learning a language involves both individual words and their configural relationships in sentences.
Researchers studied the configural processing of faces in both children and adults.
The experiment tested whether participants used configural cues to recognize different objects.
Infants are sensitive to configural changes in the arrangement of features in visual stimuli.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elemental - relating to individual parts rather than the whole
  • synonym: holistic - relating to the whole rather than just the parts
  • synonym: structural - relating to the arrangement or organization of parts
