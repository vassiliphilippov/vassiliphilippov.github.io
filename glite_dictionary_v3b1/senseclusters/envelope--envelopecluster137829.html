envelope

noun
maximum operating limits (esp. of a system or aircraft)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnvəˌloʊp / (AmE), / ˈɛnvələʊp / (BrE)
the maximum performance limits or capabilities within which a system or device, especially an aircraft, can safely operate

Examples

Expand the envelope
Innovative companies constantly try to expand the envelope of what's technologically possible.
Test pilots are always looking for new ways to push the envelope of their aircraft.
The engineers warned that operating the system beyond its envelope could be dangerous.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capacity - the maximum amount that something can contain or produce
  • synonym: limit - a point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass
