sweet birch

noun
North American birch tree with spicy bark
2.6
Pronunciation: / swit bɜrtʃ / (AmE), / swiːt bɜːtʃ / (BrE)
A North American birch tree (Betula lenta) with dark, spicy-scented bark used for oil and hard wood for furniture.

Examples

The sweet birch tree
Furniture crafted from sweet birch is known for its strength and elegant finish.
Many hikers recognize sweet birch by the spicy aroma released when the bark is scratched.
The sweet birch is prized for its distinctive wintergreen scent and attractive dark bark.
