coyly

adverb
in a shy or flirtatious manner
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈkɔɪ.li / (BrE)
in a way that shows shyness or modesty intended to be alluring or playful

Examples

To smile coyly
He glanced coyly at the camera, uncertain whether to reveal his excitement.
She smiled coyly at her admirer from across the room.
The actress answered the question coyly, refusing to give too much away.
