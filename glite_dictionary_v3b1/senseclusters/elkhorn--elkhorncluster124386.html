elkhorn

noun
elk antler material
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlkˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈɛlkˌhɔːn / (BrE)
the antler of an elk, or the material from such an antler used for handles or decoration

Examples

Carved elkhorn handle
The craftsman carved a knife handle from polished elkhorn for a unique look.
Each spring, hikers sometimes find a shed elkhorn lying on the forest floor.
Collectors value elkhorn pieces for their natural beauty and strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antler - the branched horn of a deer or similar animal
  • antonym: ivory - hard, white material from the tusks of animals, used for carving
