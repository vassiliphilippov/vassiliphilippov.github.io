Straussian

adjective
of Strauss's music
Pronunciation: / straʊsiən / (AmE), / straʊsiən / (BrE)
relating to the music, style, or characteristics of composers named Strauss, especially Richard Strauss or Johann Strauss.

Examples

Straussian waltz concert
The conductor's Straussian interpretation brought out the lush harmonies of the orchestra.
Her waltz had a distinctly Straussian flair, reminiscent of Vienna's golden age.
Many critics praised the Straussian qualities in the new symphonic work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Romantic - relating to the Romantic period in music
  • antonym: classical - relating to classical music or its style
