peaceful

adjective
free from war or disturbance
Pronunciation: / ˈpisfəl / (AmE), / ˈpiːsf(ə)l / (BrE)
Free from disturbance, strife, or war; calm and tranquil.

Examples

A peaceful morning
She enjoyed a peaceful walk in the quiet countryside.
The citizens were grateful for the peaceful period after years of conflict.
The leaders signed an agreement to ensure a peaceful future for both nations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - free from noise or agitation
  • synonym: tranquil - free from disturbance or turmoil
  • antonym: violent - involving physical force to harm or damage
