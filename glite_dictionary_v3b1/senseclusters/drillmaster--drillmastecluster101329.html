drillmaster

noun
training instructor
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪlˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈdrɪlˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who instructs or leads others in military or physical training drills

Examples

Military drillmaster
The drillmaster demanded perfect coordination from every member of the squad.
During boot camp, the drillmaster led the recruits through a series of challenging exercises.
Our new drillmaster is known for his strict discipline and attention to detail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - a person who trains people in sports or skills
  • synonym: instructor - a person who teaches or trains others
  • antonym: trainee - a person who is being trained
