rashly

adverb
in a hasty or reckless manner
Pronunciation: / ˈræʃli / (AmE), / ˈræʃli / (BrE)
In a hasty and reckless manner, without considering the consequences.

Examples

To rashly act
After hearing the rumor, he rashly confronted his friend without finding out the truth.
Investing rashly in unfamiliar stocks can lead to significant financial losses.
She rashly quit her job, only to regret it a week later.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautiously - in a careful and thoughtful manner
  • synonym: recklessly - without thinking about the possible danger or harm
