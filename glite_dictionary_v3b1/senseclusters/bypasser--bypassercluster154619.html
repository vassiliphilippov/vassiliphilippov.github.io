bypasser

noun
person who circumvents rules
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌpæsər / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌpɑːsə / (BrE)
a person who avoids or circumvents a rule, system, or official control

Examples

Rule bypasser
The security team caught a bypasser who entered the building through an unauthorized door.
A clever bypasser found a loophole in the new regulations.
The system was designed to detect any bypasser attempting to avoid official procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complier - a person who follows rules or instructions
  • synonym: evader - a person who avoids something, especially by cleverness
