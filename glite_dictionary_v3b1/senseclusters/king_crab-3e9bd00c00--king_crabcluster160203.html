king crab

noun
large Atlantic marine arthropod, domed carapace
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / kɪŋ kræb / (AmE), / kɪŋ kræb / (BrE)
a large, spiny, edible crab found in cold northern waters, especially in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans
