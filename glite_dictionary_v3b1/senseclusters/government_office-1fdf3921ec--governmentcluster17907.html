government office

noun
an office used by government workers
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌvərnmənt ˈɑfɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡʌvənmənt ˈɒfɪs / (BrE)
A building or room where employees of a government department work and carry out official business.

Examples

The government office building
Many people visited the government office to renew their passports.
She works at a government office in the city center.
The government office was closed for a public holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: government agency - an organization that is part of the government
  • antonym: private office - an office not run by the government
