pyrogenetic

adjective
producing or caused by fever
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪ.rə.dʒəˈnɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌpaɪ.rə.dʒəˈnet.ɪk / (BrE)
Produced by or producing fever, especially as a result of infection or inflammation.

Examples

A pyrogenetic reaction
A pyrogenetic reaction can be dangerous in patients with compromised immune systems.
Researchers studied the pyrogenetic effects of certain viral pathogens.
The pyrogenetic response in the body was triggered by a bacterial infection.
