pegged-down

adjective
fastened with pegs
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛɡdˈdaʊn / (AmE), / ˌpɛɡdˈdaʊn / (BrE)
fastened or secured using pegs, especially to keep something in place on the ground

Examples

Pegged-down netting
The pegged-down netting kept the young plants safe from birds.
The gardener showed us how to keep the branches pegged-down until new roots form.
We used pegged-down tarps to protect the tents against the wind.
