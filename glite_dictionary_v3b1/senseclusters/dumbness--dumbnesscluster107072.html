Examples

Sudden dumbness after injury

In some cases, emotional shock can cause sudden dumbness.

Synonyms

  • muteness
    6.8 0.20/m
    muteness is a general state of not speaking used in everyday speech, while dumbness sounds more informal or old-fashioned.
  • mutism
    9.2 0.15/m
    mutism is a clinical condition often linked to medical or psychological causes, while dumbness can be physical, chosen, or colloquial.
  • speechlessness
    5.5 0.10/m
    speechlessness is the sudden silence from shock or strong feeling, while dumbness refers to being unable or refusing to speak.
