dumbness

noun
inability to speak
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʌmnəs / (BrE)
the state of being unable to speak, either due to a physical condition or by choice

Examples

Sudden dumbness after injury
The accident left him with a temporary dumbness that made communication difficult.
Her dumbness during the interview was mistaken for nervousness.
In some cases, emotional shock can cause sudden dumbness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mutism - the inability or refusal to speak
  • synonym: speechlessness - the state of being unable to speak
