time of day

noun
the hour or moment during the day
Pronunciation: / taɪm əv deɪ / (AmE), / taɪm əv deɪ / (BrE)
the specific point or period during the day as indicated by a clock; the hour or moment when something happens

Examples

Morning time of day
Depending on the time of day, the streets can be either busy or deserted.
It's not a good time of day to call since he is probably sleeping.
She glanced at her watch to check the time of day before leaving for her appointment.
