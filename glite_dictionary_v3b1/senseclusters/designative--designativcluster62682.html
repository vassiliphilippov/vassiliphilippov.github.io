designative

adjective
serving to designate or indicate
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzaɪnətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈzɪɡnətɪv / (BrE)
serving to designate or indicate something; having the function of assigning or specifying.

Examples

Designative function
Color can have a designative function in distinguishing different product categories.
The committee adopted a designative system for assigning tasks to its members.
The officer held a designative role, responsible for identifying potential threats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indicative - serving as a sign or indication of something
