BEM

noun
numerical boundary method
9.9
Pronunciation: /biː iː em/ /; / /bem/ (AmE), /biː iː em/ /; / /bem/ (BrE)
a numerical method for solving physical problems by discretizing only the surfaces or boundaries of a domain

Examples

BEM calculation method
The engineer applied BEM to analyze the stress distribution on the bridge's surface.
Using BEM reduced the computational cost compared to traditional methods for this acoustic problem.
Researchers often choose BEM when only the boundaries of a structure are of interest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundary element method - a numerical method that models only the boundaries of a domain
  • antonym: finite element method - a numerical technique that discretizes the entire domain to solve physical problems
