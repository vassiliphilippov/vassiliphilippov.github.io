midheaven

noun
peak of success
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌhɛvən / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌhɛvən / (BrE)
the highest point of success, power, or fame in someone's career or reputation

Examples

Career midheaven
After years of setbacks the band finally reached its midheaven with a bestselling album.
Winning the award marked the midheaven of her acting career.
Many athletes dream of reaching their midheaven before retirement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nadir - the lowest point or most unsuccessful moment
  • synonym: zenith - the highest point or peak of something
