Synonyms

  • Romani
    7.3 0.04/m
    Romani is the standard name for this ethnic group with a distinctive culture, while Gypsy is an older term often seen as offensive.
  • Romany
    8.5 0.01/m
    Romany refers to people known for a distinct language and culture, while Gypsy is an informal name many find offensive.
  • Roma
    7.0 0.03/m
    Roma names the people who speak the Romany language in many countries, while Gypsy is an informal term often judged offensive.
