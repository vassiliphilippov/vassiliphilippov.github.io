dump truck

noun
truck with tilting bed for unloading
2.3 0.83/m
Pronunciation: / dÊŒmp trÊŒk / (AmE), / dÊŒmp trÊŒk / (BrE)
A large truck designed to transport and unload materials by raising its open-box bed from the front, allowing the contents to slide out.

Examples

A dump truck unloads sand
A dump truck backed up to the edge and emptied its load of sand onto the ground.
The construction crew loaded gravel into the dump truck before driving to the building site.
We watched as the driver raised the bed of the dump truck, dumping the rocks out easily.
