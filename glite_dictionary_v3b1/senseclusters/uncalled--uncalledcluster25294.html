uncalled

adjective
not contacted
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɔld / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɔːld / (BrE)
not having received a telephone call or direct contact from someone expected.

Examples

Uncalled by midnight
By midnight she was still uncalled, despite leaving multiple messages for him.
After the interview, he remained uncalled for several days and started to worry.
The applicants who were uncalled assumed they had not been selected for the next round.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ignored - deliberately not given attention or response
  • synonym: uncontacted - not having been contacted or reached out to
