black lung

noun
lung disease from coal dust
6.3
Pronunciation: / blæk lʌŋ / (AmE), / blæk lʌŋ / (BrE)
A lung disease caused by prolonged inhalation of coal dust, typically affecting coal miners.

Examples

The black lung disease
He was diagnosed with black lung after decades in the coal mines.
Many coal miners developed black lung after years of working underground.
The new safety regulations aim to reduce cases of black lung among workers.
