picogram

noun
trillionth of a gram
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkoʊˌɡræm / (AmE), / ˈpɪkəʊˌɡræm / (BrE)
a unit of mass equal to one trillionth (10⁻¹²) of a gram

Examples

Modern instruments can measure substances as small as a picogram with high accuracy.
Each cell contained less than one picogram of DNA after the experiment.
The laboratory equipment can detect substances as small as one picogram.
Scientists measured the DNA sample and found it weighed only a few picograms.
A picogram is such a tiny amount that it is used in advanced scientific research.
