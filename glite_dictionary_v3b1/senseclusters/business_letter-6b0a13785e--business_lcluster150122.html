business letter

noun
formal letter about business matters
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs ˌlɛtɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɪznɪs ˌlɛtə / (BrE)
a formal letter sent from one company or professional individual to another, dealing with business matters

Examples

A business letter format
All employees must learn how to write a proper business letter.
She drafted a business letter to request a meeting with her client.
The company received several business letters in response to its inquiry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cover letter - a letter sent with a job application
  • antonym: personal letter - a letter written for personal or private matters
