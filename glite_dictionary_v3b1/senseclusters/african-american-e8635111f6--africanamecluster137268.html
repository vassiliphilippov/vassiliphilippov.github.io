Synonyms

  • African American
    1.3 38/m
    African American is the common form written without a hyphen used today, while African-American appears with a hyphen and sounds more formal.
  • Black American
    2.0 1.95/m
    Black American often refers to any American of African descent, including recent immigrants, while African-American stresses family ancestry and ties to slavery.
