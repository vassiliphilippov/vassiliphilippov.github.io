grr

noun
angry growl sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡɜr / (AmE), / ɡɜː / (BrE)
a written representation of a low, angry growl or a sound expressing annoyance or frustration

Examples

A dog's grr
The little dog let out a loud grr when the mail carrier walked by.
She typed grr in the chat to show her frustration with the slow computer.
With a playful grr, the child pretended to be a tiger chasing her friend.
