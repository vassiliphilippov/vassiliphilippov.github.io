antihuman

noun
person hostile to humans
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhjuːmən / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhjuːmən / (BrE)
a person who hates, opposes, or seeks harm against human beings

Examples

An antihuman leader
The dictator's antihuman policies led to widespread suffering and fear.
Some critics described the villain in the novel as an antihuman with no empathy for others.
His antihuman attitude made it difficult for him to form close relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misanthrope - a person who dislikes or distrusts other people
  • antonym: philanthropist - a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others
