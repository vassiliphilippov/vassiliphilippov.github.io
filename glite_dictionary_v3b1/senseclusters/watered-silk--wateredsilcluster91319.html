watered-silk

noun
silk fabric with a wavy pattern
9.3 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tərd.sɪlk/ /; / /ˈwɑː.tərd.sɪlk/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.təd.sɪlk/ (BrE)
