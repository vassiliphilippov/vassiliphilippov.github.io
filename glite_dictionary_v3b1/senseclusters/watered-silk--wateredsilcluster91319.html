watered-silk

noun
silk fabric with a wavy pattern
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚd sɪlk / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəd sɪlk / (BrE)
A silk fabric that has a wavy, watered appearance on its surface, often used in fine clothing and upholstery.

Examples

Rich watered-silk fabric
Antique furniture was sometimes upholstered with richly colored watered-silk.
She wore a pale blue watered-silk ribbon in her hair for the ceremony.
The dress was made from exquisite watered-silk and shimmered in the light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moiré - fabric with a rippled or watered appearance
