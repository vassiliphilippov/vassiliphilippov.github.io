offtake

noun
pipeline outlet point
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌteɪk / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌteɪk / (BrE)
a physical outlet or connection on a pipeline or network where materials, fuel, or power are drawn off

Examples

Pipeline offtake point
The engineers checked the offtake to ensure there were no leaks in the gas pipeline.
A new offtake was added to supply water to the nearby industrial park.
Regular maintenance of each offtake is essential for the safe operation of the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outlet - a point where something is released or taken out
