watchmate

noun
viewing companion
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌmeɪt / (BrE)
a person with whom one regularly watches television, films, or live broadcasts

Examples

Movie watchmate
Every Friday night, he meets his watchmate to enjoy the latest movie releases together.
During the football season, my watchmate and I never miss a single game.
She found a new watchmate through an online fan group for her favorite series.

Related Terms

  • synonym: companion - a person who accompanies another
  • synonym: partner - a person who takes part in an activity with another
