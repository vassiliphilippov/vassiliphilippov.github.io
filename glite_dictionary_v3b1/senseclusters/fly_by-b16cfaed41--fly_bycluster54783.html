fly by

verb
to pass very quickly (of time/events)
Pronunciation: / flaɪ baɪ / (AmE), / flaɪ baɪ / (BrE)
(of time or events) to seem to pass very quickly.

Examples

The days fly by
The summer seemed to fly by and soon it was time for school again.
Those two hours really flew by while we were watching the movie.
When you're having fun, the afternoon often just flies by.
