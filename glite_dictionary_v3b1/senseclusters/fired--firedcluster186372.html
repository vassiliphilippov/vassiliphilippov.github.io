fired

adjective
dismissed from a job
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəd / (BrE)
Having lost your job, especially as a result of being dismissed by your employer.

Examples

To be fired from work
After being fired, he found it difficult to pay his bills.
Many fired employees were offered outplacement services.
She was fired for repeatedly arriving late to work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hired - given a job, especially after applying for one
