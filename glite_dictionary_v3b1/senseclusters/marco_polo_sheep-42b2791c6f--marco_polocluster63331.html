Marco Polo sheep

noun
Central Asian wild sheep with long spiral horns
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːr.koʊ ˈpoʊ.loʊ ʃiːp/ (AmE), /ˈmɑː.kəʊ ˈpəʊ.ləʊ ʃiːp/ (BrE)
