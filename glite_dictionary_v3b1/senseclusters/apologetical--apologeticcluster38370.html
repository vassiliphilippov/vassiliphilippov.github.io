apologetical

adjective
defending beliefs
8.9
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑlədʒɛtɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌpɒlədʒɛtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the formal defense or justification of a religious or philosophical belief, especially through argument or writing

Examples

Apologetical writing
Her apologetical essay aimed to justify the core principles of her faith.
The book takes an apologetical approach to explain the origins of the movement.
He delivered an apologetical lecture defending the philosophy against its critics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critical - expressing adverse or disapproving comments or judgments
  • synonym: defensive - intended to defend or protect
