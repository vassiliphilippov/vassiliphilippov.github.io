wise elder

noun
respected, knowledgeable older person
1.9
Pronunciation: / waɪz ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / waɪz ˈɛldə / (BrE)
an older person who is respected for their knowledge, experience, and good judgment

Examples

The wise elder speaks
At the ceremony, the wise elder shared stories to teach the younger generation.
Many communities consult a wise elder when faced with difficult decisions.
She became known as the wise elder who offered guidance to all who sought it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sage - a wise person, especially one venerated for experience and judgment
