tea towel

noun
cloth for drying dishes
Pronunciation: / ti ˈtaʊəl / (AmE), / tiː ˈtaʊəl / (BrE)
a cloth used for drying washed dishes, cutlery, and kitchen utensils

Examples

A tea towel for dishes
After washing the plates, she reached for a tea towel to dry them.
Every kitchen drawer seems to have at least one tea towel.
He folded the clean tea towel before putting it away.
