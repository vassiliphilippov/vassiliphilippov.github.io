hunting and gathering tribe

noun
tribe relying on hunting and gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌntɪŋ ənd ˈɡæðəɹɪŋ traɪb / (AmE), / ˈhʌntɪŋ ənd ˈɡæð(ə)ɹɪŋ traɪb / (BrE)
a group of people who sustain themselves primarily by hunting, fishing, and gathering wild plants, typically living a nomadic lifestyle

Examples

A hunting and gathering tribe lifestyle
A hunting and gathering tribe traditionally depended on the natural environment for all their needs.
Anthropologists spent several years living among a hunting and gathering tribe in Africa.
The hunting and gathering tribe moved with the seasons to follow migrating herds and wild fruits.
