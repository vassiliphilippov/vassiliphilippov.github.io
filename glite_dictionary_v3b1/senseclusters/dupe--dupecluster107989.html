dupe

verb
to deceive or trick someone
6.4 2.79/m
Pronunciation: / duÀêp / (AmE), / djuÀêp / (BrE)
to deceive or trick someone, usually by making them believe something that is not true

Examples

To dupe a friend
He realized too late that he had been duped by his supposed friend.
Many customers were duped by the fake advertisement.
The scammers tried to dupe her into revealing her bank details.
