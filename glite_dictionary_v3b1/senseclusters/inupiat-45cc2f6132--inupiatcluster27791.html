Inupiat

adjective
of the Inupiat people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnuːpiˌæt / (AmE), / ɪˈnuːpiˌæt / (BrE)
relating to the Inupiat people, their language, culture, or traditions of northern Alaska.

Examples

Inupiat culture
The museum features Inupiat artifacts and traditional clothing from northern Alaska.
She is proud of her Inupiat heritage and often shares stories from her culture.
Researchers studied the impact of climate change on Inupiat communities in the Arctic.
