lagophthalmos

noun
inability to fully close the eyelids
Pronunciation: / ˌlæɡ.əfˈθæl.moʊs / (AmE), / ˌlaɡ.ɒfˈθal.mɒs / (BrE)
an abnormal condition in which a person is unable to close their eyelids completely

Examples

Lagophthalmos eye condition
Sleeping with lagophthalmos can lead to dryness and irritation of the eye.
