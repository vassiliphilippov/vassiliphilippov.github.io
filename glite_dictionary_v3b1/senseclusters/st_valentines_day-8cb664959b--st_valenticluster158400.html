St Valentine's Day

noun
day for expressing love (Feb 14)
1.8
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈvælənˌtaɪnz deɪ / (AmE), / seɪnt ˈvæləntaɪnz deɪ / (BrE)
A day celebrated on February 14th, traditionally observed by the exchange of cards, flowers, and tokens of affection between loved ones.

Examples

St Valentine's Day cards
Every year on St Valentine's Day, people exchange cards and gifts with their loved ones.
He received a secret note on St Valentine's Day and wondered who had sent it.
The restaurant was fully booked for St Valentine's Day celebrations.
