fletching

noun
arrow feathers
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfletʃ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfletʃ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the feathers or vanes attached to the shaft of an arrow to stabilize its flight
