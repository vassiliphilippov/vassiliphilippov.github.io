fletching

noun
arrow feathers
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflɛtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the feathers or vanes attached to the shaft of an arrow to stabilize its flight

Examples

Arrow fletching color
The fletching on his arrows was bright red, making them easy to spot after shooting.
Proper fletching helps the arrow fly straight and accurately toward the target.
She repaired the damaged fletching before entering the archery competition.
The fletching on the arrow was bright red, making it easy to spot after shooting.
He carefully glued new fletching onto his arrows before the archery contest.
Damaged fletching can cause an arrow to fly off course.
