speech disorder

noun
disorder affecting the ability to speak
2.0
Pronunciation: / spiːtʃ dɪˈsɔrdər / (AmE), / spiːtʃ dɪˈsɔːdə / (BrE)
A medical condition that affects a person's ability to produce speech sounds correctly or fluently, or to use voice appropriately.

Examples

A speech disorder diagnosis
A speech disorder may make it difficult to pronounce certain sounds correctly.
Stuttering is one type of speech disorder that can affect people of all ages.
Therapy can help children with a speech disorder improve their communication skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: language disorder - a disorder that affects understanding or using language
