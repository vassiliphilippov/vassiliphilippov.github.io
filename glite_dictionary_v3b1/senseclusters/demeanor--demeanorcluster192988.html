demeanor

noun
outward behavior or manner

A person's outward behavior or manner, especially as it reflects their attitude toward others.

Examples

Professional demeanor
Despite her nervousness, she maintained a professional demeanor throughout the meeting.
He impressed the interviewers with his friendly demeanor and polite answers.
Her calm demeanor helped reassure everyone during the crisis.
Despite his angry words, his demeanour remained polite and composed.
Her calm demeanour impressed the interview panel.
The teacher's friendly demeanour put the students at ease.

  • manner

  • conduct
