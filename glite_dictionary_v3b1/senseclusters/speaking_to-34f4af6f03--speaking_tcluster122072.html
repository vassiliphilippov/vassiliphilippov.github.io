speaking to

noun
a reprimand or scolding
Pronunciation: / ˈspiːkɪŋ tu / (AmE), / ˈspiːkɪŋ tə / (BrE)
an instance of being reproved or scolded; a reprimand

Examples

A speaking to from manager
After missing the deadline, Mark got a speaking to from his manager.
If you don't finish your chores, you'll be in for a speaking to later.
The coach gave the team a stern speaking to after their poor performance.
