pend

verb
to hang suspended
8.4
Pronunciation: / pɛnd / (AmE), / pɛnd / (BrE)
to hang or be suspended from above, often so as to dangle freely

Examples

To pend from the ceiling
A single lantern pend from the rafters, casting light across the barn's wooden floor.
Several ornaments pended from the branches of the old pine tree.
The sign pends above the shop entrance, swaying gently in the breeze.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dangle - to hang loosely with a swinging motion
  • synonym: suspend - to hang something from above so it moves freely
