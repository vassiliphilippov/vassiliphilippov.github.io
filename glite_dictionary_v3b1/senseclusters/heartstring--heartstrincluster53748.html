heartstring

noun
deepest emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtˌstrɪŋ / (BrE)
a person's deepest feelings or emotions, often referred to in the plural to mean the source of strong emotional response

Examples

Pull at the heartstrings
The story pulled at my heartstrings and made me cry.
Charity advertisements often try to touch your heartstrings to encourage donations.
Hearing the old song tugged at his heartstrings and brought back many memories.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emotion - a strong feeling such as love, anger, or joy
  • synonym: feeling - an emotional state or reaction
