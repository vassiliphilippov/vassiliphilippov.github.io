atomist

noun
believer in atomism
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈætəmɪst / (AmE), / ˈætəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that complex systems can be understood by analyzing their simplest, individual parts

Examples

Social atomist thinker
The philosopher was known as an atomist who analyzed society by studying individuals.
As an atomist, she believed that understanding each part would explain the whole system.
Many scientists in history were atomists, focusing on the smallest elements of matter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: holist - a person who believes that systems should be viewed as wholes, not just as a collection of parts
  • synonym: reductionist - a person who explains complex things by reducing them to simpler parts
