helicopter

verb
to travel by aircraft
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlɪˌkɑptər / (AmE), / ˈhɛlɪˌkɒptə / (BrE)
to travel by helicopter or to transport someone or something by helicopter

Examples

To helicopter supplies
The rescue team helicoptered supplies to the remote village after the flood.
She helicoptered into the city for an important business meeting.
The president was helicoptered to the event to avoid traffic delays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airlift - to transport people or goods by aircraft, especially in emergencies
  • antonym: drive - to travel by car
