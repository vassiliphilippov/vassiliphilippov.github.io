vulnerably

adverb
in a vulnerable manner
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlnərəbli / (AmE), / ˈvʌlnərəbli / (BrE)
In a way that shows vulnerability or susceptibility to harm or attack.

Examples

Wait vulnerably outside
He waited vulnerably outside, uncertain if he would be welcomed.
She vulnerably admitted her fears to the group, hoping for understanding.
The baby animal looked vulnerably at its surroundings, clearly needing protection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vulnerable - susceptible to physical or emotional harm
