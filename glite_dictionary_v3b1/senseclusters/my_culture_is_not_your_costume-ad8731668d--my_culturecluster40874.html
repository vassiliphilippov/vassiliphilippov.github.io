my culture is not your costume

x
disapproval of cultural appropriation
5.2
Pronunciation: / maɪ ˈkʌltʃɚ ɪz nɑt jʊr ˈkɑstum / (AmE), / maɪ ˈkʌltʃə ɪz nɒt jɔː ˈkɒstjuːm / (BrE)
used to express disapproval of cultural appropriation, especially when aspects of one culture are worn or used disrespectfully by others as fashion or entertainment

Examples

My culture is not your costume message
He apologized after realizing that my culture is not your costume means respecting others' traditions.
Many activists have used the phrase my culture is not your costume to criticize insensitive Halloween outfits.
Wearing a traditional headdress for a party, she was told, 'my culture is not your costume.'
