originalist

adjective
favoring original meaning
9.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ /; / /ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ɪ.nəl.ɪst/ (AmE), /ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ɪ.nəl.ɪst/ /; / /ə.ˈrɪdʒ.ə.nəl.ɪst/ (BrE)
supporting the interpretation of laws or constitutions based on their original meaning at the time they were written

Examples

Originalist legal theory
The judge is known for her originalist views on constitutional interpretation.
Many legal scholars debate whether an originalist approach is suitable for modern society.
His originalist philosophy often leads him to oppose changes in established law.

Related Terms

  • antonym: living constitutionalist - believing the constitution's meaning can evolve over time
  • synonym: textualist - interpreting legal texts strictly by their wording
