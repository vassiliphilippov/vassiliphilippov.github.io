regeneratively

adverb
in a restorative way
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtɪvli /; / rɪˈdʒɛnərətɪvli / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛnərətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that restores, renews, or recovers something, especially resources, energy, or health
