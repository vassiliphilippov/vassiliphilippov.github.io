childcare

noun
care of children by others as a service
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪldˌkɛr / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldˌkɛə / (BrE)
the care of children, especially by someone other than their parents, as a service or profession

Examples

Childcare services

Some companies offer on-site childcare to support their employees.
The cost of childcare has increased significantly over the past decade.
