rancho

noun
military mess hall
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːntʃoʊ / (AmE), / ˈræn.tʃəʊ / (BrE)
a place where meals are served, or the food provided, especially in a military or camp setting

Examples

Military rancho meal
The soldiers gathered at the rancho every evening for a hot meal.
During the expedition, the team relied on the rancho for their daily rations.
After a long day of training, everyone looked forward to dinner at the rancho.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canteen - a place in a factory, office, or military base where people eat
  • synonym: mess hall - a place where soldiers or workers eat together
