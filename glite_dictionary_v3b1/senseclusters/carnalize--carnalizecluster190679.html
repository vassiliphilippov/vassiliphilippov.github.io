carnalize

verb
ascribe to sensual or bodily origin
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkɑːnəlaɪz / (BrE)
to attribute something to sensual or bodily origin

Examples

To carnalize inspiration
Critics often carnalize artistic inspiration, reducing it to mere physical urges.
It is unfair to carnalize her motives simply because she showed compassion.
Some philosophies tend to carnalize all forms of human love, ignoring their spiritual aspects.
