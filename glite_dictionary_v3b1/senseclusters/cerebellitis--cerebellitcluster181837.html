cerebellitis

noun
cerebellum inflammation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛrəˌbɛˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌsɛrəˌbɛˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the cerebellum, the part of the brain that controls movement and coordination

Examples

cerebellitis inflammation
The doctor suspected cerebellitis when the child developed sudden problems with balance and coordination.
MRI scans confirmed that the patient was suffering from cerebellitis after a viral infection.
Treatment for cerebellitis often involves managing symptoms and addressing the underlying cause.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encephalitis - inflammation of the brain
  • synonym: meningitis - inflammation of the membranes covering the brain and spinal cord
