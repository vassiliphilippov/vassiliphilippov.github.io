spiritual bouquet

noun
card listing promised prayers or devotions
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtʃuəl boʊˈkeɪ / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtjʊəl bʊˈkeɪ / (BrE)
A card or message indicating that the sender will offer prayers or devotional acts for the recipient, especially in Roman Catholic tradition.

Examples

A spiritual bouquet card
For her confirmation, Maria received a spiritual bouquet from her godmother.
He thanked his friend for the spiritual bouquet and their prayers during his illness.
The parish sent a spiritual bouquet to comfort the grieving family.
