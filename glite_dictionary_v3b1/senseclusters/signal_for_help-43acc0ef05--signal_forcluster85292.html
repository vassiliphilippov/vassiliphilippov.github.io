signal for help

noun
sign indicating a need for assistance
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪg.nəl fər help/ /; / /ˈsɪg.nəl fɔːr help/ (AmE), /ˈsɪg.nəl fə help/ /; / /ˈsɪg.nəl fɔː help/ (BrE)
a sign or action used to indicate a need for assistance, especially in an emergency

Examples

A signal for help
He fired a flare as a signal for help when his boat began to sink.
In an emergency, waving both arms overhead is often used as a signal for help.
The hikers created a large X with rocks as a signal for help to passing aircraft.
