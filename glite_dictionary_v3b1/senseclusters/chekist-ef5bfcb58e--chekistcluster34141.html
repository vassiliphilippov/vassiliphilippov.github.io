Chekist

noun
Soviet secret police member
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkɪst / (BrE)
a member of the Soviet secret police (Cheka or its successors), or someone who acts as a secret police agent, especially to suppress opposition
