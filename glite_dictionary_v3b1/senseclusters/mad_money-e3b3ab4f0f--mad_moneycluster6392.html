mad money

noun
money set aside for fun spending
Pronunciation: / ˈmæd ˌmʌni / (AmE), / ˈmad ˌmʌni / (BrE)
A small amount of money set aside for discretionary spending or unexpected enjoyment.

Examples

A little mad money
After paying her bills, she put aside a little mad money to go to the movies with friends.

She always keeps some mad money in her wallet for unexpected treats.
