Delairea odorata

noun
a South African evergreen climbing plant
Pronunciation: / dəˈlɛəriə oʊˈdɔrətə / (AmE), / dəˈlɛəriə əʊˈdɔːrətə / (BrE)
A South African evergreen climbing plant with yellow flowers, grown mainly as a houseplant for its attractive foliage; sometimes classified within the genus Senecio.
