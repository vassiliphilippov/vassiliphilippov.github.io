preadoption

adjective
before adoption
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː.əˈdɑːp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriː.əˈdɒp.ʃən / (BrE)
existing, occurring, or done before the process of adoption is completed

Examples

Preadoption visit
The agency requires a thorough preadoption evaluation before placing a child with a family.
They attended several preadoption counseling sessions to prepare for the responsibilities ahead.
A preadoption visit helped the child become familiar with their future home.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postadoption - relating to the period after adoption
