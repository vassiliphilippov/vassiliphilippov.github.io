keen

adjective
eager or enthusiastic
Pronunciation: / kiːn /; / kin / (AmE), / kiːn / (BrE)
very eager, enthusiastic, or interested in something

Examples

Keen interest
She is keen to start her new job and meet her colleagues.
He has always been keen on learning new languages.
The students were keen to participate in the science competition.
She is a keen student who always asks questions in class.
He has always been keen to learn new languages and travel abroad.
Their keen interest in science led them to join the robotics club.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eager - wanting to do or have something very much
  • synonym: enthusiastic - showing intense and eager enjoyment or interest
  • antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy
  • antonym: reluctant - unwilling or hesitant to do something
