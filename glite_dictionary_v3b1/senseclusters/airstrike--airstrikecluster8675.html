airstrike

noun
attack from aircraft
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌstraɪk / (AmE), / ˈeəˌstraɪk / (BrE)
a military attack in which aircraft drop bombs or fire missiles at targets on the ground

Examples

An airstrike attack
The military launched an airstrike on the enemy base early in the morning.
Civilians were evacuated from the area before the airstrike began.
News reports confirmed that several buildings were destroyed in the airstrike yesterday.
The city was heavily damaged after a series of airstrikes last night.
International news reported that an airstrike targeted the rebel base early this morning.
Civilians were urged to evacuate the area before the planned airstrike took place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bombing - an attack in which bombs are used to destroy a target
  • antonym: ground assault - a military attack carried out by land forces
  • synonym: missile attack - an attack using missiles, often launched from a distance
  • synonym: missile strike - an attack using missiles to hit a target
