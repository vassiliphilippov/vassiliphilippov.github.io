all one's might

noun
with maximum effort or strength
3.3
Pronunciation: / ɔl wʌnz maɪt / (AmE), / ɔːl wʌnz maɪt / (BrE)
using the greatest possible effort or strength; as hard as one possibly can.

Examples

With all his might
He pushed the heavy door with all his might, but it wouldn’t budge.
She shouted at the top of her lungs, calling for help with all her might.
The little boy kicked the ball with all his might across the field.
