bribable

adjective
susceptible to bribery or corruption
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪ.bə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈbraɪ.bə.bəl / (BrE)
easily influenced by bribery; capable of being corrupted or persuaded by a bribe.

Examples

A bribable judge
A bribable judge can undermine faith in the entire justice system.
She refused to deal with anyone who seemed even remotely bribable.
The investigation exposed several bribable officials in the organization.
