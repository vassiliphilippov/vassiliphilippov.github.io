upward spiral

noun
a process of continual improvement
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpwɚd ˈspaɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈʌpwəd ˈspaɪrəl / (BrE)
a process or situation that consistently improves or intensifies in a positive direction over time

Examples

An upward spiral of success
After years of hard work, his career entered an upward spiral that brought him international recognition.
Focusing on healthy habits can set you on an upward spiral of physical and mental well-being.
The company's innovation led to an upward spiral in sales and investor confidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downward spiral - a situation that continually gets worse
