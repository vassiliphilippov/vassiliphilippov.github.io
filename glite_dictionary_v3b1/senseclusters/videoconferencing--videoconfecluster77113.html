videoconferencing

adjective
for video meetings
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪdioʊˌkɑːn.fɚ.ənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɪdiəʊˌkɒn.fər.ənsɪŋ / (BrE)
relating to or designed for the use of video communication between people in different locations.

Examples

Videoconferencing software
The company upgraded to a videoconferencing platform to connect employees in different countries.
Many schools now use videoconferencing technology for remote learning and virtual classrooms.
A videoconferencing system was installed in the meeting room to facilitate international discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-person - taking place with people physically present together
  • synonym: virtual - existing or occurring on computers or the internet
