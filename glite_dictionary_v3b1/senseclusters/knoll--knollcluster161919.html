knoll

noun
small rounded hill or mound
Pronunciation: / noʊl / (AmE), / nəʊl / (BrE)
a small, rounded natural hill or mound

Examples

A small knoll
A solitary oak tree stood on top of the grassy knoll.
From the knoll we had a clear view of the entire valley.
The children rolled down the knoll laughing and shouting.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hill - a naturally raised area of land, larger than a knoll
  • synonym: mound - a general term for a raised area of earth or stone
