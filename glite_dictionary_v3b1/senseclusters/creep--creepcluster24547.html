creep

verb
grow or spread slowly over a surface
4.2 4.17/m
Pronunciation: / krip / (AmE), / kriÀêp / (BrE)
(of a plant or substance) to grow or spread slowly and gradually, often so as to cover a surface.

Examples

Vines creep over walls
A thin layer of rust started to creep across the old metal gate after the rain.
Colorful moss would creep quietly over the rocks, giving the forest a vibrant green look.
Poison ivy began to creep along the garden fence, threatening to overtake the other plants.

Synonyms

  • ramp
    8.9 0.02/m
    ramp grows or spreads over a surface by climbing, while creep grows slowly and spreads to cover an area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spread - to extend or grow so as to cover a larger area
