genus Fagopyrum

noun
genus of plants including buckwheat
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs fəˈɡoʊ.pɪˌrʌm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs fəˈɡəʊ.pɪˌrʌm / (BrE)
