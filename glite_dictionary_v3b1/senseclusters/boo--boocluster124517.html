boo

interjection
surprise exclamation
Pronunciation: / buː / (AmE), / buː / (BrE)
an exclamation used to surprise, scare, or playfully startle someone

Examples

Boo to scare

The children took turns sneaking up and shouting boo at each other during the game.
She whispered boo in his ear to playfully startle him.
