these

pronoun
plural demonstrative pronoun
Pronunciation: / ðiz / (AmE), / ðiːz / (BrE)
the plural form of 'this', used to refer to people or things that are close in place or time

Examples

These are mine
These are the books I was telling you about yesterday.
Could you hand me these papers on the desk, please?
These are the shoes that fit me best out of all the options.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: this - singular demonstrative pronoun for something near
  • antonym: those - plural demonstrative pronoun for things farther away
