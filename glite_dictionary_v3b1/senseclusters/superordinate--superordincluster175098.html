superordinate

noun
a more generic word; general class term
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈɔːr.dən.ət / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pərˈɔː.dɪ.nət / (BrE)
a word whose meaning includes that of another, more specific word; a more generic term.

Examples

Superordinate word
In the relationship between 'animal' and 'dog', 'animal' is the superordinate.
Teachers often use a superordinate to help students categorize new vocabulary.
The word 'fruit' is a superordinate to 'apple', 'banana', and 'pear'.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hyponym - a word of more specific meaning than a general or superordinate term
