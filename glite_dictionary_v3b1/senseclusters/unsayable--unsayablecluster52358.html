unsayable

adjective
inexpressible in words
8.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈseɪəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈseɪəbl / (BrE)
impossible or too difficult to express in words

Examples

Unsayable feelings
There are moments of grief that feel completely unsayable to those around us.
The beauty of the sunset was almost unsayable, leaving everyone in silent awe.
Some experiences are so personal and deep that they remain unsayable forever.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expressible - able to be put into words
  • synonym: indescribable - too unusual or extreme to be described
  • synonym: inexpressible - impossible to put into words
