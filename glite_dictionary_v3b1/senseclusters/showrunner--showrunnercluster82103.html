showrunner

noun
entertainment organizer
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊˌrʌnə / (BrE)
the person with overall creative authority and management responsibility for a television series (sometimes also used for someone who organizes or directs a live performance or entertainment production)

Examples

The showrunner manages production
The showrunner made sure every detail of the concert was carefully planned.
As the showrunner, she coordinated the entire festival from start to finish.
A successful showrunner balances creative vision with practical management.
The showrunner announced a new cast member for the upcoming season.
As showrunner, she made all the key creative decisions for the series.
Many fans praised the showrunner for the show's unique storytelling style.
The showrunner decided to change the direction of the story for season two.
As showrunner, she oversees both the writing and production teams for the series.
Many fans credit the showrunner for the show's unique style and consistent quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - a person who supervises the creative aspects of a production
  • synonym: producer - a person responsible for the financial and managerial aspects of a production
