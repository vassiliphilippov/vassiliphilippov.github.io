nonaccidental

adjective
intentional or deliberate
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnækˈsɪdəntəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnækˈsɪdəntəl / (BrE)
not happening by chance; done on purpose or caused by specific factors

Examples

Nonaccidental injury
The doctor determined that the child's injuries were nonaccidental and required further investigation.
Researchers analyzed the data to identify nonaccidental patterns in the results.
The report concluded that the fire was nonaccidental and likely caused by arson.

Related Terms

  • antonym: accidental - happening by chance, not planned
  • synonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • synonym: intentional - done on purpose
