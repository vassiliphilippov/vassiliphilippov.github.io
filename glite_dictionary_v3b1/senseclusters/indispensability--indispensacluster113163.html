indispensability

noun
the state of being absolutely necessary
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.dɪˌspɛn.səˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌɪn.dɪˌspen.səˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being absolutely necessary or essential; the condition of being indispensable

Examples

The indispensability of skills
As technology evolves, the indispensability of basic computer skills becomes increasingly evident.
Her role in the project quickly demonstrated her indispensability to the team.
The indispensability of fresh water makes it a priority in disaster relief efforts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dispensability - the quality of being able to be dispensed with or done without
  • synonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary, essential
