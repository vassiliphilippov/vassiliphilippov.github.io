malting

noun
grain preparation process
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of soaking and germinating grains, especially barley, to prepare them for brewing or distilling

Examples

Malting of barley
The malting of barley is a crucial step in producing high-quality beer.
Modern malting facilities use advanced technology to control temperature and moisture.
During malting, grains are soaked in water and allowed to sprout before drying.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brewing - the process of making beer or other alcoholic drinks from grains
  • synonym: germination - the process by which a seed develops into a new plant
