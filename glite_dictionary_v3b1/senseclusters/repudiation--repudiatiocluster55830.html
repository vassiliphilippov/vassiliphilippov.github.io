repudiation

noun
exposure of falseness or pretension
9.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˌpjuːdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌpjuːdɪˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of exposing the falseness or pretensions of something

Examples

Repudiation of myths
Her article was a thorough repudiation of the prevailing myths.
The repudiation of the fraud exposed the company's dishonest practices.
The scientist was known for his repudiation of pseudoscientific claims.
