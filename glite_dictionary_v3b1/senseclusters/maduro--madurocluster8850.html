maduro

noun
dark-wrapped cigar
Pronunciation: / məˈdʊəroʊ / (AmE), / məˈdjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
a cigar with a dark, well-fermented wrapper leaf, known for its rich flavor and color

Examples

A maduro cigar
He selected a maduro from the humidor, appreciating its deep brown color and smooth texture.
The rich, sweet aroma of the maduro filled the lounge as he took his first puff.
Collectors often seek out a rare maduro for its unique flavor profile and appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cigar - a rolled bundle of dried and fermented tobacco leaves for smoking
  • antonym: claro - a cigar with a light-colored wrapper
