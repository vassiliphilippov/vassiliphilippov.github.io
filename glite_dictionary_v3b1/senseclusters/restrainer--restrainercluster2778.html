restrainer

noun
person who holds back or controls others
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstreɪnər / (AmE), / rɪˈstreɪnə / (BrE)
a person who directs or holds back others, preventing them from acting freely or excessively

Examples

A strict restrainer
Sometimes a teacher needs to be a restrainer in a classroom full of energetic children.
The security guard acted as a restrainer during the chaotic protest.
Without a restrainer, the crowd could have become unruly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: controller - a person who directs or regulates something
  • antonym: enabler - a person who encourages or allows a behavior
