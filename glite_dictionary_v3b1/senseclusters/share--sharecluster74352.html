share

verb
have in common with others
Pronunciation: / ʃɛr / (AmE), / ʃeə / (BrE)
to have a quality, interest, or experience in common with another person or thing

Examples

Share an interest
All members of the team share responsibility for the result.
Although we come from different countries, we share similar values.
The two authors share a passion for environmental issues.
