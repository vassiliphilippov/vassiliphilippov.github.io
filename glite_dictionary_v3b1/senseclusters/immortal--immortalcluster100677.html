immortal

noun
person of enduring fame
2.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)
a person, especially a writer or artist, whose fame endures for generations

Examples

An immortal writer
After his remarkable achievements, many consider him an immortal in the world of science.
The museum dedicated an entire exhibition to the immortals of nineteenth-century art.
Throughout history, only a handful of writers have been called immortal for their lasting influence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icon - a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol or as worthy of veneration
  • synonym: legend - a person who is very famous and admired, often from the past
