sanctimonious

adjective
hypocritically moral or pious
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsæŋk.təˈmoʊ.ni.əs / (AmE), / ˌsaŋk.tɪˈməʊ.ni.əs / (BrE)
Making a show of being morally superior to others; hypocritically pious or righteous.

Examples

A sanctimonious attitude
His sanctimonious attitude made him unpopular with his colleagues.
She gave a sanctimonious speech about the importance of honesty, but everyone knew she often lied.
The politician's sanctimonious claims about integrity were met with widespread skepticism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - modest or not arrogant
  • synonym: hypocritical - pretending to have virtues one does not possess
  • synonym: pious - devoutly religious
