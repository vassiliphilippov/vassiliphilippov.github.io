draw

verb
pull something to make it move
3.5
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
to pull, move, or take something out from a place or container, or to cause something to move by pulling

To draw a sword

She always draws the blinds in the morning to let the light in.

She drew a knife from her bag in self-defense.
The officer quickly drew his pistol when he heard the noise.
