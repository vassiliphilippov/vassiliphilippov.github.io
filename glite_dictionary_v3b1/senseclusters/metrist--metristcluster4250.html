metrist

noun
utility meter worker
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːtrɪst / (AmE), / ˈmiːtrɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to read, record, or service utility meters such as those for water, gas, or electricity

Examples

Utility metrist
The metrist visited our house to check the electricity meter this morning.
A metrist is responsible for ensuring that all water meters are working correctly.
If you notice a problem with your gas meter, contact your local metrist for assistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: meter reader - a person who reads utility meters
  • synonym: technician - a person skilled in a technical field, often maintaining equipment
