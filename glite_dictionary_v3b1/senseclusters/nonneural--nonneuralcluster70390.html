nonneural

adjective
not using neural networks
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈnʊrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈnjʊərəl / (BrE)
not using or based on artificial neural networks, especially in computational or machine learning methods

Examples

Nonneural algorithm design

Comparing nonneural and neural models helps researchers understand the strengths of each method.
