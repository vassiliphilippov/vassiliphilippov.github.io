nanocephaly

noun
abnormally small head condition
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnænoʊˈsɛfəli / (AmE), / ˌnanəʊˈsɛfəli / (BrE)
A condition characterized by an abnormally small head and an underdeveloped brain.

Examples

Severe nanocephaly condition
Because of her nanocephaly, she required specialized medical care throughout her childhood.
Scientists are studying the genetic causes of nanocephaly and related developmental disorders.
The infant was diagnosed with nanocephaly shortly after birth.

  • synonym: microcephaly - a condition involving an abnormally small head, often with brain underdevelopment
