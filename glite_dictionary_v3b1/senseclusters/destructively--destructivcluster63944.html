destructively

adverb
in a destructive manner
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrʌktɪvli / (AmE), / dɪˈstrʌktɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that causes harm, damage, or destruction.

Examples

To act destructively
She criticized the project destructively instead of offering helpful suggestions.
Some children behave destructively when they feel ignored.
They acted destructively towards the abandoned building, smashing windows and doors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: constructively - in a way that serves to improve or build up, rather than destroy
