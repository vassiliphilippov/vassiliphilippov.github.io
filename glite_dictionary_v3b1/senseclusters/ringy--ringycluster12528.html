ringy

adjective
having a ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋi / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋi / (BrE)
having a ringing sound or quality, or characterized by the presence of rings.

Examples

A ringy sound
The bell produced a ringy echo that lingered in the hallway.
She noticed the ringy quality of the old guitar's sound.
The forest was quiet except for the ringy call of a distant bird.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - lacking a clear or ringing sound
  • synonym: resonant - having a deep, clear, and continuing sound
