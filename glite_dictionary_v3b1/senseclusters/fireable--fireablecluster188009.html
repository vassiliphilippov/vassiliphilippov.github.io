fireable

adjective
suitable for firing
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪərəbl / (BrE)
suitable for being fired in a kiln or oven to harden, especially referring to clay or pottery

Examples

Fireable clay
The potter checked if the bowl was fireable before placing it in the kiln.
Only fireable clay should be used for making durable ceramic dishes.
After drying for several days, the sculpture became fireable and ready for the next step.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusible - able to be melted or fused by heat
  • antonym: unfireable - not suitable for being fired in a kiln or oven
