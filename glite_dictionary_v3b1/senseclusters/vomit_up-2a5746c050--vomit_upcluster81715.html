vomit up

verb
eject stomach contents through the mouth
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːmɪt ʌp / (AmE), / ˈvɒmɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to force the contents of the stomach out through the mouth; to throw up.

Examples

To vomit up food
After eating spoiled food, she began vomiting up everything in her stomach.
He felt so ill that he had to vomit up his lunch.
The cat vomited up a hairball on the carpet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regurgitate - to bring swallowed food back up into the mouth
  • antonym: retain - to keep something in the stomach; not to vomit
  • synonym: throw up - to vomit; to eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth
