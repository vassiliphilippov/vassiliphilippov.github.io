firetruck

noun
emergency firefighting vehicle
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌtrʌk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌtrʌk / (BrE)
a large vehicle equipped with firefighting equipment and used to transport firefighters to the scene of a fire

Examples

A firetruck siren
The loud siren signaled that a firetruck was racing down the street.
Children waved excitedly as the bright red firetruck passed by during the parade.
A firetruck arrived within minutes to help put out the blaze.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambulance - a vehicle for transporting sick or injured people
  • synonym: fire engine - a vehicle for carrying firefighters and equipment to a fire
