team up

verb
join with others to form a team
2.5 5.25/m
Pronunciation: / tim  åp / (AmE), / tiÀêm  åp / (BrE)
to join with one or more people or groups to work together towards a shared goal

Examples

To team up with others
Several companies will team up to create the new product.
She asked her friend to team up with her for the competition.
The two scientists decided to team up to conduct the research.
If you team up with others, the task will be easier.
The two companies decided to team up to develop a new product.
We teamed up for the science fair and won first prize.
