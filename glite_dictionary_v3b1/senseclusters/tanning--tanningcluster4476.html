tanning

noun
process of making leather from hides
Pronunciation: / ˈtænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtanɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of converting raw animal hides into leather by treating them with tannins or other chemicals

Examples

Leather tanning
He learned the ancient art of tanning from his grandfather.
Modern tanning methods often use synthetic chemicals instead of traditional plant materials.
The tanning of hides was a vital part of the town's economy.

  • synonym: leatherworking - craft or industry of making things from leather
