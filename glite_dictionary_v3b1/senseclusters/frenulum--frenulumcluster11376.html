frenulum

noun
tissue fold in body
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛn.jə.ləm / (AmE), / ˈfriː.njʊ.ləm / (BrE)
a small fold of tissue that restricts the movement of an organ or part in the body

Examples

Tongue frenulum
The dentist checked the frenulum under my tongue during the exam.
A short frenulum can sometimes cause speech difficulties in children.
The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the frenulum during the procedure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ligament - a band of tissue connecting bones or supporting organs
