Synonyms

  • feasible
    4.9 12/m
    feasible is possible and commonly used to mean doable under current conditions, while viable stresses long-term success or survival.
  • practicable
    5.8 1.53/m
    practicable is a formal word for something you can put into practice in real situations, while viable focuses on overall success.
  • workable
    3.9 3.91/m
    workable describes a plan that functions in practice with available means, while viable adds whether it will truly succeed long-term.
  • executable
    5.8 0.39/m
    executable means something can be carried out now, often legally or technically, while viable asks if it can succeed overall.
