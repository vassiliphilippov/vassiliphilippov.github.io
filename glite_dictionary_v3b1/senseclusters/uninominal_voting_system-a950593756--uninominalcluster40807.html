uninominal voting system

noun
system with one representative per district
Pronunciation: / ˌjuː.nɪˈnɑː.mɪn.əl ˈvoʊ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm /; / ˌjuːnɪˈnɑːmɪnəl ˈvoʊtɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˌjuː.nɪˈnɒ.mɪn.əl ˈvəʊ.tɪŋ ˌsɪs.təm /; / ˌjuːnɪˈnɒmɪnəl ˈvəʊtɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a voting or electoral system in which only one representative is elected from each district or constituency
