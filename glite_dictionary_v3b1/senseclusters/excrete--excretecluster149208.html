excrete

verb
eliminate waste from the body
3.5 5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈskrit / (AmE), / ɪkˈskriːt / (BrE)
to get rid of waste material from the body through the skin, lungs, or excretory organs

Examples

To excrete waste
Most animals excrete some form of waste to maintain healthy body functions.
Sweat glands excrete excess salts and water during hot weather.
The kidneys excrete waste products from the bloodstream into the urine.
