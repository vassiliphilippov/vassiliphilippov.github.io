dero

noun
Australian slang for vagrant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal and often derogatory Australian slang term for a homeless or disreputable person

Examples

Many deros gather
The old station was a place where many deros gathered at night.
He was unfairly called a dero by people who did not know his story.
Some locals complained about the number of deros sleeping in the park.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentleman - a man of good social position and manners
  • synonym: hobo - a homeless person, often one who travels for work
  • synonym: vagrant - a person without a settled home or regular work
