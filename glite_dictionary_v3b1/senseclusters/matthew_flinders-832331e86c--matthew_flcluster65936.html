Matthew Flinders

proper noun
British explorer who mapped Australian coast

Pronunciation: / ˈmæθju ˈflɪndərz / (AmE), / ˈmæθju ˈflɪndəz / (BrE)
(1774–1814) British explorer who was the first to map much of the Australian coastline and popularized the name 'Australia'.

Examples
