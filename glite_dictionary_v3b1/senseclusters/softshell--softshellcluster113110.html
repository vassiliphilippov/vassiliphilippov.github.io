softshell

adjective
flexible outer layer
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌʃɛl / (BrE)
having a soft, flexible outer layer instead of a hard shell, often providing wind or water resistance

Examples

Softshell jacket
A softshell jacket is ideal for outdoor activities in changing weather conditions.
The softshell turtle is known for its smooth, flexible shell compared to other turtles.
He wore a lightweight softshell layer to protect himself from the wind on the mountain trail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: hardshell - having a rigid, hard outer layer
