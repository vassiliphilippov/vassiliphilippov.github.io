pari passu

adverb
at an equal rate; on equal terms (legal/finance)
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːri ˈpɑːsuː / (AmE), / ˌpɑːri ˈpɑːsuː / (BrE)
(law, finance) With equal step; at the same rate or on an equal footing, especially regarding legal claims or the ranking of creditors.

Examples

Distributed pari passu
Profits were distributed pari passu among the partners according to their investment.
The debts must be treated pari passu, ensuring equal rights for all claimants.
Under the agreement, all unsecured creditors will be paid pari passu with each other.
