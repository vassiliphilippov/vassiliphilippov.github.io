generation

noun
people of the same age group
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛn.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛn.əˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
all the people or organisms born and living at about the same time; a group in a family or species

Examples

Young generation
Each generation faces its own unique set of challenges.
The younger generation is much more comfortable with technology than their parents.
There is often a gap in values between one generation and the next.
A mutation in a single plant can affect all future generations of its species.
Many biologists study how traits are passed from one generation to the next.
This family has lived in the same house for five generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cohort - a group of people with a shared characteristic, especially birth year
