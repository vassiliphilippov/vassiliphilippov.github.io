clerically

adverb
in an office manner
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛrɪkli / (AmE), / ˈklɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to office work, clerks, or administrative tasks

Examples

Clerically processed documents
The reports were filed clerically before the new software was introduced.
She handled the registration process clerically, ensuring all paperwork was complete.
Many tasks in the office are still performed clerically despite advances in technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administratively - in a way relating to management or administration
  • antonym: manually - by hand rather than by machine or electronically
