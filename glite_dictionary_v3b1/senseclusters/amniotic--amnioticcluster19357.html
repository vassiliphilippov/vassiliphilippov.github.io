amniotic

adjective
relating to the amnion membrane
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæmniˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌamnɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the amnion, the innermost membrane enclosing the embryo in higher vertebrates.

Examples

Amniotic membrane
Doctors performed an amniotic membrane transplant to aid in the patient's recovery.
Scientists study amniotic cells to understand early human development.
The baby is protected by the amniotic fluid inside the womb.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amnion - the innermost membrane that encloses the embryo of a higher vertebrate
