Synonyms

  • hit
    1.0 142/m
    hit means making forceful contact that may cause damage, while strike means delivering a strong blow or forceful contact.
  • knock
    1.0 32/m
    knock is a sudden hit often by accident or a light tap, while strike is a broader, stronger blow used intentionally.
  • collide with
    3.7 2.82/m
    collide with usually describes two moving things hitting each other, while strike means to hit or deliver a blow.
