jukebox

noun
music-playing machine operated by coins
4.7 2.60/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íuÀêkÀåb…ëks / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀêkÀåb…íks / (BrE)
A machine, typically found in public places, that plays music selected from a list by inserting a coin or token.

Examples

A jukebox machine
As a teenager, he spent hours playing tunes from the local jukebox.
She selected her favorite song on the jukebox and everyone started dancing.
The diner had a classic jukebox filled with old rock and roll records.

Synonyms

  • nickelodeon
    9.8 0.34/m
