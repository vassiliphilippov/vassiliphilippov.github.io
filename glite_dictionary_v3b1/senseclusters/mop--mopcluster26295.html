mop

verb
clean or wipe with a mop
1.0 1.15/m
Pronunciation: / mÉ‘p / (AmE), / mÉ’p / (BrE)
to clean or wipe a surface using a mop or a similar implement

Examples

To mop the floor
He had to mop the kitchen floor after the party.
I watched as the janitor mopped up the spilled water in the hallway.
She gently mopped his brow as he lay in bed, exhausted.
