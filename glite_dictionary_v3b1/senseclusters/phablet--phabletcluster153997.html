phablet

noun
large mobile device
Pronunciation: / ˈfæblət / (AmE), / ˈfæblət / (BrE)
a mobile device with a screen size larger than a smartphone but smaller than a tablet

Examples

A phablet device
She prefers reading on her phablet because of its large display.
A phablet is easier to use for watching videos than a regular smartphone.
He bought a new phablet to combine the features of a phone and a tablet.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: smartphone - a mobile phone with advanced features and internet access
  • degree higher: tablet - a portable touchscreen computer larger than a smartphone
