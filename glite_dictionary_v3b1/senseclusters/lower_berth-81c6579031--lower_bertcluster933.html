lower berth

noun
the bottom bed of two stacked berths
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊ.ər bɜrθ / (AmE), / ˈləʊ.ə bɜːθ / (BrE)
the bottom bed or sleeping place of two stacked one above the other, especially in a train or ship compartment
