de-escalate

verb
reduce the intensity or seriousness of something
Pronunciation: / ˌdiːˈɛskəˌleɪt /; / ˌdiˈɛskəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌdiːˈɛskəleɪt / (BrE)
to make a conflict, crisis, or tense situation less intense, serious, or dangerous
