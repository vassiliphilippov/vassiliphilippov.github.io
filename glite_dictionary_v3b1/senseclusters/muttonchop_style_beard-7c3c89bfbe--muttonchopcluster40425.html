muttonchop style beard

noun
beard with large sideburns, no mustache
9.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌt.ən.tʃɑːp staɪl bɪrd/ (AmE), /ˈmʌt.ən.tʃɒp staɪl ˈbɪ.əd/ (BrE)
A type of beard featuring thick sideburns that extend down the cheeks and are typically joined by hair along the jawline, with no mustache.

Examples

A thick muttonchop style beard
Actors in period dramas often wear a muttonchop style beard for authenticity.
After years of being clean-shaven, he decided to grow a muttonchop style beard.
His muttonchop style beard made him stand out in the crowd.
