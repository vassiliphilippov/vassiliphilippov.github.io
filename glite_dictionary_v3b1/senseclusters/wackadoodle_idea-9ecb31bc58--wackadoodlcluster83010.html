wackadoodle idea

noun
strange or unconventional idea
Pronunciation: / ˈwækəˌduːdəl aɪˈdiːə / (AmE), / ˈwækədʊdəl aɪˈdɪə / (BrE)
an idea that is strange, eccentric, or highly unconventional

Examples

Some dismissed her proposal as just another wackadoodle idea, but it actually worked.
