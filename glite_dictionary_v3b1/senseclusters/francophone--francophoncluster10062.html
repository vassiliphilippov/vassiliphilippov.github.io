francophone

adjective
French-speaking
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfræŋkəˌfoʊn / (AmE), / ˈfræŋkəfəʊn / (BrE)
relating to people, countries, or regions where French is spoken as a main language

Examples

Francophone countries
Several francophone countries participated in the international summit.
The school offers special programs for francophone students from Africa and Europe.
He grew up in a francophone community in Canada, speaking French at home and at school.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anglophone - relating to English-speaking people or regions
