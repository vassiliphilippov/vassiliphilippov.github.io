lace bug

noun
insect with lacy-patterned wings
1.8
Pronunciation: / leɪs bʌɡ / (AmE), / leɪs bʌɡ / (BrE)
a small insect with body and wings covered by a lacy network of raised lines, commonly found on plants

Examples

A lace bug insect
A single lace bug can cause noticeable stippling on plant leaves.
Gardeners often watch for lace bug infestations on azaleas and other shrubs.
The leaves were damaged by lace bugs feeding on their undersides.
