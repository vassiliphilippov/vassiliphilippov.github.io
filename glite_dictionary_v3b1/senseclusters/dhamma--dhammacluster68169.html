dhamma

noun
Buddhist teachings
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːmə / (AmE), / ˈdʌmə / (BrE)
the teachings or doctrine of the Buddha, especially referring to universal truth or law in Buddhism

Examples

Study the dhamma
She studies dhamma daily to deepen her understanding of Buddhism.
The monk gave a talk on the importance of dhamma in everyday life.
Many people travel to learn about dhamma at Buddhist temples around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dharma - the principle of cosmic order or law in Indian religions
  • synonym: teaching - a principle or lesson taught by a religion or philosophy
