lazily

adverb
in a slow or unenergetic manner
3.7 1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪzəli /; / ˈleɪzɪli / (AmE), / ˈleɪzɪli / (BrE)
in a slow, relaxed, or unenergetic way; without much effort or energy

Examples

Clouds move lazily
Clouds drifted lazily across the summer sky, undisturbed by the gentle breeze.
She stretched lazily on the sofa, enjoying her afternoon nap.
The cat lazily batted at the toy without much interest.
He lazily stretched out on the couch and watched the clouds drift by.
She lazily flipped through the magazine, barely glancing at the articles.
The cat lazily batted at the string but soon lost interest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actively - in an energetic or active manner
  • antonym: energetically - in a way that shows a lot of energy and enthusiasm
  • synonym: idly - in a lazy or inactive manner
