sockpuppet

noun
puppet made from sock
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑkˌpʌpɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkˌpʌpɪt / (BrE)
a simple puppet made by putting a sock over the hand and moving it to perform or entertain

Examples

A sockpuppet show
The children made a colorful sockpuppet for their school play.
She entertained her little brother with a talking sockpuppet during the car ride.
At the party, each guest created a unique sockpuppet to join the puppet show.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hand puppet - a puppet controlled by the hand inside it
  • antonym: marionette - a puppet moved by strings or wires
