noun

Synonyms

  • eulogist
    8.1 0.04/m
    eulogist is the common, neutral word for someone who gives a tribute speech, while eulogizer is a rarer variant.
  • panegyrist
    9.9 0.01/m
    panegyrist is a formal speaker who gives praise at public events, while eulogizer usually refers to someone praising at a funeral.
