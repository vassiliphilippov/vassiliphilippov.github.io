Tarpeian

adjective
sudden ruinous downfall
Pronunciation: / tɑːrˈpiːən / (AmE), / tɑːˈpiːən / (BrE)
causing or relating to a sudden, severe, or ruinous downfall or punishment, often used figuratively

Examples

Tarpeian downfall
The politician's Tarpeian fall shocked the entire nation and ended his long career.
After years of success, the company faced a Tarpeian collapse due to a sudden scandal.
Her Tarpeian punishment was swift and left her reputation in ruins.
