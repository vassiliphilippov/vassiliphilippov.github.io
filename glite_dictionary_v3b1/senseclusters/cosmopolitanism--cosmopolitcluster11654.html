cosmopolitanism

noun
worldwide distribution
7.3
Pronunciation: /kɑːz.mə.ˈpɑːl.ɪ.tə.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kɒz.mə.ˈpɒl.ɪ.tə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the condition of being found or distributed across many regions or continents, especially in reference to species or populations

Examples

Cosmopolitanism of species
The cosmopolitanism of the bird species means it can be found on every continent except Antarctica.
Scientists were surprised by the cosmopolitanism of the plant, which thrives in both tropical and temperate zones.
Marine biologists study the cosmopolitanism of certain fish that inhabit oceans worldwide.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endemism - the state of being native to a particular region only
  • synonym: ubiquity - the state of being present everywhere
