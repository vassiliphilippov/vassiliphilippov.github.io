whap

verb
hit forcefully with a loud sound
Pronunciation: / wæp / (AmE), / wæp / (BrE)
to hit something or someone hard, usually making a loud sound

Examples

To whap the ball
He whapped the ball with his bat and sent it flying over the fence.
She whaps the dust out of the rug every Saturday.
The child whapped the table with his spoon, making everyone laugh.
