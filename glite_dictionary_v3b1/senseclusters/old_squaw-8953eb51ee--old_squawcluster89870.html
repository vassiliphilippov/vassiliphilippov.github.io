old squaw

noun
long-tailed sea duck of North America
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: / oʊld skwɑ / (AmE), / əʊld skwɔː / (BrE)
a long-tailed sea duck (Clangula hyemalis) found in the northern parts of North America, now more commonly called the long-tailed duck

Examples

Although now referred to as the long-tailed duck, old squaw was the traditional name for the species.

The distinctive tail feathers of the old squaw make it easy to identify during migration.
