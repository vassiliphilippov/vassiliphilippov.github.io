production

noun
overly elaborate display or fuss
Pronunciation: / pɹəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / pɹəˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
an exaggerated or unnecessarily elaborate display or fuss, often made over something trivial

Examples

Make a production
He created a whole production just to announce his new haircut.
She always makes a big production out of planning a simple dinner party.
There's no need to turn this into such a production; it's just a phone call.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuss - an excessive display of unnecessary or overly elaborate concern, especially about something unimportant
