Synonyms

  • adoration
    3.4 1/m
    adoration is strong loving praise shown openly and warmly in speech or acts, while worship is more formal devotion.
  • reverence
    6.3 4/m
    reverence is a quiet feeling of deep respect and awe often felt in sacred places, while worship is more active praise.
  • veneration
    7.8 0.9/m
    veneration is respectful honor shown especially to saints or elders in formal rituals, while worship is broader loving praise.
  • devotion
    4.6 2/m
    devotion is steady love and loyal service shown by regular religious acts and care, while worship is often individual praise.
  • admiration
    1.8 9/m
    admiration is warm respect and approval for skill or quality, while worship is deeper, often religious love and praise.
