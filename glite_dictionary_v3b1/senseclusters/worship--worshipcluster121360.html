worship

noun
deep love and admiration
4.2 2.51/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw…ùÀê É…™p / (AmE), / Ààw…úÀê É…™p / (BrE)
a feeling of deep love, admiration, and respect, often towards a person or deity

Examples

Worship of a leader
Her worship of her mentor was evident in the way she spoke about him.
His worship of the artist bordered on obsession.
The fans expressed their worship for the actor by cheering loudly at every appearance.

Synonyms

  • adoration
    3.4 1.25/m
    adoration is strong loving praise shown openly and warmly in speech or acts, while worship is more formal devotion.
  • reverence
    6.3 4.03/m
    reverence is a quiet feeling of deep respect and awe often felt in sacred places, while worship is more active praise.
  • veneration
    7.8 0.92/m
    veneration is respectful honor shown especially to saints or elders in formal rituals, while worship is broader loving praise.
  • devotion
    4.6 2.65/m
    devotion is steady love and loyal service shown by regular religious acts and care, while worship is often individual praise.
  • admiration
    1.8 9.30/m
    admiration is warm respect and approval for skill or quality, while worship is deeper, often religious love and praise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoration - deep love and respect
  • antonym: disdain - the feeling of not respecting someone or something
  • synonym: reverence - profound respect and awe
