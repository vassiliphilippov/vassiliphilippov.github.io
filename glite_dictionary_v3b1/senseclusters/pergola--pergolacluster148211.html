pergola

noun
framework supporting climbing plants
5.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.gə.lə/ /; / /pə.ˈgoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.gə.lə/ /; / /pə.ˈgəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
an outdoor structure consisting of vertical posts supporting an open framework of beams and rafters, often used to support climbing plants or provide shaded walkways or sitting areas.

Examples

A pergola in garden
She built a pergola to provide shade on the patio.
The roses climbed up the wooden pergola at the garden entrance.
We had lunch under the pergola covered in wisteria.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbor - a garden shelter of branches or climbing plants
