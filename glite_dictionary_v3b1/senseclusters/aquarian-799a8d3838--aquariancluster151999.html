Aquarian

adjective
of Aquarius sign
7.3
Pronunciation: / əˈkwɛriən / (AmE), / əˈkwɛːrɪən / (BrE)
relating to the zodiac sign Aquarius or to qualities associated with Aquarius, such as innovation and progressiveness

Examples

Aquarian personality
Her Aquarian outlook made her open to new ideas and unconventional solutions.
The group embraced Aquarian values of equality and humanitarianism.
He wore an Aquarian symbol on his necklace to show his astrological sign.
