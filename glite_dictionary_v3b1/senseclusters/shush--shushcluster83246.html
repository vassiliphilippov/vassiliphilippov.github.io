shush

interjection
quiet command
4.5
Pronunciation: / ʃʊʃ / (AmE), / ʃʊʃ / (BrE)
used to tell someone to be quiet or stop talking

Examples

Shush in class
The librarian raised her finger and said shush to the noisy students.
During the movie, someone in the audience whispered shush when people started talking.
He gave a quick shush as the baby was finally falling asleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hush - used to ask for silence
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound
