election

noun
status of having been elected
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛkʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛkʃən / (BrE)
the fact of being chosen for a position, especially by vote; the state of having been elected

Examples

Her election as leader
After her election, she thanked everyone who had supported her campaign.
The election of the new chairman was announced to the board members.
They held a party to celebrate his recent election to the city council.
