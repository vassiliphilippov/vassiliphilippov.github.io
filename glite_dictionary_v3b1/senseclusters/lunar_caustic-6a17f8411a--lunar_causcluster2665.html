lunar caustic

noun
fused silver nitrate used as a caustic
Pronunciation: / ˈluːnər ˈkɔːstɪk / (AmE), / ˈluːnə ˈkɔːstɪk / (BrE)
silver nitrate fused into sticks, formerly used in medicine as a cauterizing agent to destroy tissue or remove warts, etc.

Examples

lunar caustic sticks
Although lunar caustic is rarely used today, it once played a key role in minor surgical procedures.
Before antibiotics, doctors would sometimes use lunar caustic to treat wounds or remove growths.
The pharmacist prepared a stick of lunar caustic for the physician's bag.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cautery - instrument or substance used to burn or destroy tissue
  • synonym: silver nitrate - a colorless crystalline compound used in chemistry and medicine
