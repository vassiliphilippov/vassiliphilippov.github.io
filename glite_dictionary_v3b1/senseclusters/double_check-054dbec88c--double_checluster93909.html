double check

noun
a secondary verification or review
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl tʃɛk / (BrE)
an additional check made to ensure the accuracy or correctness of something after it has already been checked once

Examples

A double check of data
Her double check of the data caught several errors the first reviewer missed.
The accountant performed a double check of the numbers before submitting the report.
We should do a double check to make sure all the doors are locked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: review - an assessment or examination of something
  • synonym: verification - the process of checking that something is true or correct
