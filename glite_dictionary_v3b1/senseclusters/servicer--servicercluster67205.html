servicer

noun
company that manages loans
6.4
Pronunciation: / \u02c8s\u025d\u02d0v\u026as\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8s\u025c\u02d0v\u026as\u0259 / (BrE)
a company or agent that manages loans on behalf of a lender, handling payment collection, account records, escrow, and customer service for borrowers.

Examples

Loan servicer
The mortgage servicer sent a statement showing the remaining balance and escrow changes.
During the refinance, their loan servicer provided the documents needed to complete closing.
If you fall behind, contact your servicer immediately to discuss payment assistance options.

Related Terms
