parentless

adjective
having no parents or parental care
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛɹəntlɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛə.rəntləs / (BrE)
Having no living parents or being without the care of parents or parent surrogates.

Examples

A parentless child
She felt parentless and alone in the big city.
The movie tells the story of a parentless boy searching for a family.
The rescue center provided shelter for parentless children after the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orphan - a child whose parents are dead
