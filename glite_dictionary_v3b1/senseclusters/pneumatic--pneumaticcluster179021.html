pneumatic

adjective
relating to or using air or gas
Pronunciation: / nuˈmætɪk / (AmE), / njuːˈmætɪk / (BrE)
operated by or containing air or a similar gas; relating to air or gases

Examples

pneumatic doors
Many buses now have pneumatic doors that open and close automatically.
Modern dentists often use pneumatic drills for precision and speed.
The engineer repaired the pneumatic tube system in the office building.
