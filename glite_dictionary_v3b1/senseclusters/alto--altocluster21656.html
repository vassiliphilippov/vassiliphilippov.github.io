alto

adjective
of the lowest female singing voice
6.7 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.toʊ/ (AmE), /ˈæl.təʊ/ (BrE)
having the lowest range for a female voice, or relating to the part sung by such a voice; or having a range above that of a tenor and below that of a soprano, typically the highest male adult voice

Examples

Alto part
As an alto singer, her range impressively covers the lower female register.
She sang the alto part in the school choir with confidence.
The role requires an alto voice to reach those deep, resonant notes.
Although a man, he sang in the alto range with remarkable ease.
His alto voice made him stand out in the choir.
The composer wrote this role for an alto singer rather than a tenor.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: soprano - the highest female singing voice
  • degree lower: tenor - the male singing voice just below alto in pitch
