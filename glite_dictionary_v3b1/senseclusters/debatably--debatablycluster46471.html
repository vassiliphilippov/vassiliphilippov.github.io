debatably

adverb
open to argument
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbeɪtəbli / (AmE), / dɪˈbeɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is open to argument, doubt, or discussion

Examples

Is debatably true
The movie is debatably the director's finest work, though some critics disagree.
It is debatably the most important discovery of the decade.
His actions were debatably justified, depending on your point of view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arguably - in a way that can be supported by argument
  • antonym: indisputably - in a way that cannot be doubted or argued
