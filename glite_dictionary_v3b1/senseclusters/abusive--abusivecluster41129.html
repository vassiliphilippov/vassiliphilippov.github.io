abusive

adjective
using insulting or hurtful language
Pronunciation: / əˈbjusɪv / (AmE), / əˈbjuːsɪv / (BrE)
using or expressing offensive, insulting, or hurtful language or behavior toward someone

Examples

Abusive language
After years of abusive language, she decided to quit the job.
His abusive remarks deeply hurt everyone in the meeting.
The manager was fired for sending abusive emails to his staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insulting - rude or offensive in a way that hurts someone
  • antonym: respectful - showing or expressing politeness or honor
