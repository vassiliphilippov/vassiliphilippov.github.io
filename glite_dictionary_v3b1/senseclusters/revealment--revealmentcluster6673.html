revealment

noun
divine disclosure
Pronunciation: / rɪˈviːlmənt / (AmE), / rɪˈviːlmənt / (BrE)
a divine or supernatural disclosure believed to come from a deity or spiritual source

Examples

Divine revealment
The ancient text describes a revealment that guided the people through difficult times.
Many followers believed the prophet's revealment was a message from a higher power.
During meditation, she experienced a sudden revealment that brought her peace.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concealment - the action of hiding or keeping something secret
  • synonym: revelation - a surprising and previously unknown fact, especially one made known in a dramatic way
