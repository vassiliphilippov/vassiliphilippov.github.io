Meibomian cyst

noun
swelling of eyelid due to blocked Meibomian gland
Pronunciation: / maɪˈboʊmiən sɪst / (AmE), / maɪˈbəʊmiən sɪst / (BrE)
A small, painless swelling on the eyelid caused by blockage of a Meibomian gland.

Examples

A Meibomian cyst on eyelid
If you notice a painless lump on your eyelid, it could be a Meibomian cyst.
She developed a Meibomian cyst on her upper eyelid, which gradually disappeared on its own.
The doctor diagnosed the swelling as a Meibomian cyst and recommended warm compresses.
