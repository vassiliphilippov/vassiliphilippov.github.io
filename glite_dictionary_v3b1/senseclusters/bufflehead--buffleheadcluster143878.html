bufflehead

noun
small North American diving duck
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfəlˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌfəlˌhɛd / (BrE)
A small North American diving duck, the male of which has a large, bushy head of white plumage.

Examples

A bufflehead duck
A flock of buffleheads landed on the lake, their striking plumage catching the sunlight.
Many birdwatchers come here in winter hoping to spot the elusive male bufflehead.
The bufflehead dives underwater with remarkable speed to catch its prey.
