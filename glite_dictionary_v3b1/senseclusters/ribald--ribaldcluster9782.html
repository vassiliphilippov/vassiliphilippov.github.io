ribald

noun
person making coarse, vulgar jokes
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪb.əld, rɪˈbɔld / (AmE), / ˈrɪb.əld, rɪˈbɔːld / (BrE)
a person who makes coarse jokes or uses vulgar and offensive language, typically in a humorous way

Examples

A ribald at parties
His reputation as a ribald made him unwelcome at formal gatherings.
Medieval plays often featured a ribald for comic relief.
The king's court always included at least one ribald to keep the nobles laughing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buffoon - a person who amuses others by ridiculous behavior
  • antonym: prude - a person easily shocked by rude things
