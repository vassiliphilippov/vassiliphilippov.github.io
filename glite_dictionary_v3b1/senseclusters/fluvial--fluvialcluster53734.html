fluvial

adjective
relating to rivers
Pronunciation: / ˈfluː.vi.əl / (AmE), / ˈfluː.vi.əl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in a river; produced by the action of a river.

Examples

Fluvial processes
Many valleys are shaped by fluvial processes such as erosion and deposition.
The fluvial landscape was dotted with meandering streams and oxbow lakes.
The scientists studied fluvial sediments to learn about the ancient river's path.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riverine - relating to or situated on a river or riverbank
