maximalist

noun
person refusing compromise
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmæk.sɪ.mə.lɪst/ /; / /ˈmæk.sə.mə.lɪst/ (AmE), /ˈmæk.sɪ.mə.lɪst/ /; / /ˈmæk.sə.mə.lɪst/ (BrE)
a person who insists on the most extreme or complete demands, often refusing to compromise in politics or negotiations

Examples

A maximalist politician
The maximalist in the group refused to accept any compromise during the negotiations.
As a known maximalist, he always pushed for the most ambitious goals in every project.
Critics argued that her maximalist approach made it difficult to reach an agreement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremist - a person who holds extreme views or takes extreme actions
  • antonym: minimalist - a person who favors the smallest possible amount or least extreme approach
