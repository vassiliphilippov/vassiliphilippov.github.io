lumine

verb
to decorate with gold or color
to decorate a manuscript or text with gold, silver, or bright colors, especially in the style of illuminated manuscripts

Examples

To lumine a manuscript
The artist lumined the ancient manuscript with intricate gold patterns and vivid blue ink.
During the workshop, children lumined their own pages using bright paints and metallic foil.
In medieval times, monks often lumined religious texts to make them more beautiful and valuable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embellish - to make something more attractive by adding decorative details
  • synonym: illuminate - to decorate a manuscript with elaborate designs and bright colors
