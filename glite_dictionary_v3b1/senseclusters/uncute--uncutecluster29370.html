uncute

adjective
not attractive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkjuːt / (AmE), / ʌnˈkjuːt / (BrE)
not cute; lacking in attractiveness or charm, especially in a way that is noticeably unappealing

Examples

Uncute doll
Some people found the handmade doll a little uncute but still charming in its own way.
He worried that his drawing looked too uncute to win the art contest.
The kitten was surprisingly uncute compared to its siblings, but it was very friendly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cute - attractive in a pretty or endearing way
  • synonym: unattractive - not pleasing to look at; lacking beauty or charm
