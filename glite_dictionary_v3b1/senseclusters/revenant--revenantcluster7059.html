revenant

adjective
returning, especially from the dead
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛvənənt /; / ˈrɛv.nənt / (AmE), / ˈrɛvənənt /; / ˈrɛv.nənt / (BrE)
returning, especially from the dead or after a long absence; relating to or resembling a revenant
