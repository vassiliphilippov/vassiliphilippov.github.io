skene arch

noun
arch with height less than half its width
Pronunciation: / skiːn ɑrtʃ / (AmE), / skiːn ɑːtʃ / (BrE)
An architectural arch whose height is less than half its width.

Examples

A skene arch design
A skene arch is particularly effective for allowing light and open space in a building's design.
The architect recommended a skene arch to achieve both stability and aesthetic appeal in the facade.
The old bridge was constructed using several skene arches to span the wide river.
