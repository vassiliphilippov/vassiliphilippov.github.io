Synonyms

  • extinction
    4.6 0.27/m
    extinction is the process of a fire, light, or activity stopping, while extinguishment focuses on actively putting something out or ending it.
  • cessation
    7.2 5.05/m
    cessation is a general stopping of an action or state, while extinguishment implies actively ending or removing a right, debt, or fire.
  • termination
    4.7 6.71/m
    termination usually ends contracts, jobs, or agreed periods, while extinguishment often removes an obligation, right, debt, or a fire completely.
