unpropitiously

adverb
in an inauspicious manner
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.proʊˈpɪʃ.əs.li / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈpɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a manner that suggests a lack of good fortune; in an inauspicious or unfavorable way.

Examples

Begins unpropitiously
The athlete began his season unpropitiously, suffering an injury in the very first game.
The ceremony started unpropitiously with technical difficulties and a late arrival of the guest of honor.
They launched the new product unpropitiously during an economic downturn, leading to disappointing sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inauspiciously - in an inauspicious or unlucky manner
  • antonym: propitiously - in a favorable or auspicious manner
