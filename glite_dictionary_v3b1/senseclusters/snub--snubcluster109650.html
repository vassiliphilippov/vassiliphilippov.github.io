snub

noun
an act of ignoring someone
Pronunciation: / snʌb / (AmE), / snʌb / (BrE)
an act of ignoring or refusing to acknowledge someone, especially as a social slight

Examples

A cold snub
After years of friendship, his snub at the reunion was deeply hurtful.
Everyone noticed the obvious snub when she walked past her old colleague without saying hello.
The actress took the public snub from the committee with surprising grace.
