tappable

adjective
able to be accessed
Pronunciation: / ˈtæpəbəl / (AmE), / ˈtæpəbəl / (BrE)
able to be accessed or used to draw off liquid or other resources.

Examples

Tappable maple trees
The maple trees are tappable in early spring when the sap begins to flow.
Engineers confirmed that the reservoir remained tappable despite the long dry season.
After repairs, the keg was once again tappable for the party guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accessible - able to be reached or used
  • antonym: untappable - not able to be accessed or used to draw off resources
