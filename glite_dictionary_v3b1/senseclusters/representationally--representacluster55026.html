representationally

adverb
in a depicting way
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves showing, depicting, or representing something, especially in art or theory

Examples

Paint representationally
The artist worked representationally to capture the likeness of her subject.
In this essay, the author writes representationally about social issues, using vivid imagery.
The sculpture is not abstract but was created representationally to resemble a real animal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstractly - in a way that does not attempt to represent reality accurately
  • synonym: figuratively - in a way that uses figures or representation
