plush

adjective
soft and luxurious
3.5
Pronunciation: / plʌʃ / (AmE), / plʌʃ / (BrE)
very soft, thick, and comfortable to touch, often giving a feeling of luxury

Examples

Plush carpet
The hotel room featured a plush carpet that felt wonderful underfoot.
He sank into the plush armchair and immediately felt relaxed.
They chose the plush seats for extra comfort during the long movie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
  • synonym: velvety - having a soft, smooth texture like velvet
