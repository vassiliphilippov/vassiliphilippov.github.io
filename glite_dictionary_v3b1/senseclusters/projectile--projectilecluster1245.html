projectile

adjective
impelling or impelled forward
5.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛktʃaɪl / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛktʃaɪl / (BrE)
(of a force or object) impelling or impelled forward; propelled onward.

Examples

Projectile motion
He described the comet's projectile movement through space.
The invention used a projectile mechanism to accelerate particles.
The missile followed a projectile path toward its target.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propulsive - tending to propel or push forward
  • antonym: stationary - not moving, not intended to be moved
