Labrador

noun
dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈlæbrədɔr / (AmE), / ˈlæbrədɔː / (BrE)
a large, friendly breed of dog known for its intelligence, loyalty, and suitability as a family pet or working dog

Examples

Labrador retriever dog
The Labrador quickly became friends with everyone at the park.
Many families choose a Labrador because of its gentle nature.
Guide dogs are often trained from the Labrador breed due to their intelligence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: golden retriever - a friendly, intelligent breed of retriever dog
  • synonym: poodle - a breed of dog known for its intelligence and curly coat
