collect

verb
obtain payment or money owed
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɛkt / (AmE), / kəˈlɛkt / (BrE)

Examples

To collect money
She was responsible for collecting membership fees from all the members.
The agency has successfully collected thousands of dollars in unpaid taxes.
The landlord hired a company to collect the overdue rent from tenants.
