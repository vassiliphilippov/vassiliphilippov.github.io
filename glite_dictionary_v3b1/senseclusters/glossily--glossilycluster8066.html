glossily

adverb
in a glossy or shiny way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑːsɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is smooth and shiny; in a glossy manner.

Examples

To smile glossily
He smiled glossily, as if trying to impress everyone in the room.
The actress appeared glossily on the red carpet, her dress sparkling under the lights.
The brochure was glossily finished, making it visually appealing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glossy - smooth and shiny in appearance
