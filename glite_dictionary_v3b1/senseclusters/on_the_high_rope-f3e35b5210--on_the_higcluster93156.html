on the high rope

adverb
in a risky or precarious situation
5.1
Pronunciation: / ɑn ðə haɪ roʊp / (AmE), / ɒn ðə haɪ rəʊp / (BrE)
in a risky or precarious situation where failure is possible or consequences are severe

Examples

On the high rope situation
Handling two jobs at once left her on the high rope for months.
Negotiating the deal placed him on the high rope professionally.
She felt on the high rope when making critical decisions without support.
