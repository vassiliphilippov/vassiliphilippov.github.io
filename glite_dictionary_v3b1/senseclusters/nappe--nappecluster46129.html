nappe

noun
sheet of water
9.9
Pronunciation: / næp / (AmE), / nap / (BrE)
a sheet or curtain of water, especially the layer that flows over a weir or waterfall

Examples

Waterfall nappe
The thin nappe fell like a transparent curtain over the old dam in autumn.
A wide nappe formed as the river spilled over the edge of the waterfall.
Engineers studied the nappe to understand how water flowed over the weir.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cascade - a small waterfall or series of waterfalls
  • synonym: curtain - a hanging layer or sheet, often of fabric or water
