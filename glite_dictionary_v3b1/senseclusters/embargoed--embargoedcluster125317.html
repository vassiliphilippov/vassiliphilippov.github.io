embargoed

adjective
blocked from release
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbɑrɡoʊd / (AmE), / ɛmˈbɑːɡəʊd / (BrE)
legally blocked from being traded, published, or released until a specific time or condition is met

Examples

Embargoed report release
The new research findings remain embargoed until the journal publishes them next week.
Certain goods are embargoed and cannot be imported due to international sanctions.
Journalists received the embargoed press release ahead of the official announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: permitted - allowed or authorized
  • synonym: restricted - limited or controlled in access or availability
