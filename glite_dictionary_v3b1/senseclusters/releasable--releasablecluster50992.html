releasable

adjective
able to be detached
4.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlisəbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈliːsəbəl / (BrE)
designed or made so that it can be detached, opened, or released easily from something else

Examples

Releasable strap
The backpack has a releasable strap for added convenience.
This device comes with a releasable cover that makes cleaning easier.
The releasable panel allows quick access to the battery compartment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to be removed
  • synonym: removable - able to be taken off or separated from something
