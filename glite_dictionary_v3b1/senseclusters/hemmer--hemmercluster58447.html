hemmer

noun
sewing edge tool
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmər / (AmE), / ˈhɛmə / (BrE)
a tool or sewing machine attachment used to fold and sew the edge of fabric neatly

Examples

Hemmer foot attachment
The tailor used a hemmer to create a clean edge on the skirt.
If you want a professional finish, try attaching a hemmer to your sewing machine.
She bought a new hemmer to help with her quilting projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: presser foot - a sewing machine part that holds fabric flat as it is sewn
  • antonym: seam ripper - a tool used to remove stitches from fabric
