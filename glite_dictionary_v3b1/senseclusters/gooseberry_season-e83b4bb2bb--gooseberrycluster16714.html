gooseberry season

noun
period for harvesting gooseberries
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːsbɛri ˈsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈɡʊzbəri ˈsiːzən / (BrE)
the time of year when gooseberries are ripe and harvested

Examples

Gooseberry season harvest
During gooseberry season, the market stalls are filled with green and purple berries.
Local farmers hold a festival to celebrate the start of gooseberry season.
We always make jam during gooseberry season when the fruit is at its best.
