common corn salad

noun
a salad green; Valerianella locusta
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən kɔrn səˈlæd / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən kɔːn ˈsæləd / (BrE)
A small leafy plant (Valerianella locusta) cultivated for use as a salad green and sometimes grown as a pot herb; also found as a weed.
