bong

noun
water pipe for smoking
Pronunciation: / bɑŋ / (AmE), / bɒŋ / (BrE)
a device, often using water, for smoking substances such as tobacco or cannabis

Examples

A glass bong
He bought a new bong from the shop to replace his old one.
After cleaning the bong, the smoke tasted much smoother.
Some people prefer using a bong because the water cools the smoke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cigarette - a thin cylinder of tobacco for smoking
  • synonym: hookah - a device for smoking, typically with a hose and water chamber
  • synonym: water pipe - a pipe that uses water to cool smoke before inhalation
