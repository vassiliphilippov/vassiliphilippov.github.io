gloppy

adjective
overly sentimental
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑpi / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒpi / (BrE)
excessively sentimental, cloying, or overly sweet in style or emotion, often to the point of being unpleasant.

Examples

Gloppy love story
The movie's ending was so gloppy that it made some viewers cringe.
I prefer stories with genuine emotion, not ones that feel gloppy and forced.
Her speech became gloppy when she started talking about true love and destiny.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry - lacking emotion or sentimentality
  • synonym: maudlin - self-pityingly or tearfully sentimental
  • synonym: sappy - excessively sentimental or emotional
