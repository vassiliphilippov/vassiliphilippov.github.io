gloppy

adjective
overly sentimental
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈglɑː.piː/ (AmE), /ˈglɒp.iː/ (BrE)
excessively sentimental, cloying, or overly sweet in style or emotion, often to the point of being unpleasant.

Examples
