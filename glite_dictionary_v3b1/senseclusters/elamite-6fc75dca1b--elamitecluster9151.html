Elamite

noun
member of ancient Elamite people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈiː.ləˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈiː.lə.maɪt / (BrE)
a member of an ancient people who lived in Elam, east of Babylonia, as early as 3000 BC

Examples

An Elamite ruler
Ancient Elamite rulers built ziggurats that still fascinate historians today.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts believed to belong to early Elamite settlers in the region.
The Elamite army often clashed with their Babylonian neighbors.
