mouthbreeder

noun
fish that carries eggs/young in its mouth
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊθˌbriːdər / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθˌbriːdə / (BrE)
A type of fish that keeps its eggs and young in its mouth for protection until they are ready to survive on their own.

Examples

A mouthbreeder fish
During spawning, the female mouthbreeder collects her eggs in her mouth for safekeeping.
Many aquarists are fascinated by the unique parenting behavior of mouthbreeders.
The cichlid is a well-known mouthbreeder that protects its young by carrying them in its mouth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouthbrooder - fish that broods its eggs and young in its mouth
