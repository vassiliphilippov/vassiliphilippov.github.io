pork loin

noun
cut of meat from a pig's loin
7.6
Pronunciation: / pɔrk lɔɪn / (AmE), / pɔːk lɔɪn / (BrE)
meat cut from the loin of a pig, often used for roasting or grilling.

Examples

A juicy pork loin
A juicy pork loin makes an impressive centerpiece for a holiday meal.
She bought a two-pound pork loin to prepare for Sunday lunch.
The chef roasted a pork loin with fresh herbs and garlic for dinner.
