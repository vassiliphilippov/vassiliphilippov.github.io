wounded

adjective
having a physical injury, often from battle or violence
2.8 0.30/m
Pronunciation: / Ààwund…™d / (AmE), / ÀàwuÀênd…™d / (BrE)
Suffering from a physical injury, especially one received in battle or as the result of violence or an accident.

Examples

Wounded soldier
Several wounded passengers were taken to the hospital after the accident.
She tended to the wounded animal until it was safe to move.
The soldier was wounded in the leg but managed to stay calm.

Synonyms

  • injured
    1.0 1.66/m
    injured describes being physically harmed by an accident or attack, while wounded often implies a serious or battle-related injury.
  • hurt
    1.0 2.79/m
    hurt is more informal and can mean feeling pain from a minor injury, while wounded suggests a more serious physical injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injured - hurt or physically damaged
  • antonym: unharmed - not physically injured or hurt
