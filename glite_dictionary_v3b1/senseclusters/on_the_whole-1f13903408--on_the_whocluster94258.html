Synonyms

  • all in all
    3.2 5/m
    all in all sounds casual and sums up good and bad points, while on the whole is slightly more formal in tone.
  • in general
    1.6 18/m
    in general states what is true in most cases, while on the whole often adds a final summary after considering details.
  • generally
    1.0 120/m
    generally means something happens most of the time or as a rule, while on the whole gives a rounded summary.
  • broadly speaking
    3.7 0.2/m

  • altogether
    3.0 13/m
    altogether often means in total or completely, while on the whole normally means after weighing things, not absolute completeness.
