hand and glove

adverb
in close cooperation or partnership
8.3
Pronunciation: / hænd ənd ɡlʌv / (AmE), / hænd ənd ɡlʌv / (BrE)
In close cooperation; in a very close or intimate relationship, especially when working together.

Examples

Hand and glove work
The manager and the team leader worked hand and glove to resolve the issue.
The two companies operate hand and glove to bring new products to market.
Those two departments function hand and glove on every major project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: at odds - in disagreement or conflict
  • synonym: in cahoots - in partnership or conspiracy with someone
