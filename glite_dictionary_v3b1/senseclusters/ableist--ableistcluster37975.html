ableist

adjective
discriminatory toward disabled
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪbəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪbəlɪst / (BrE)
showing discrimination or prejudice against people with disabilities

Examples

Ableist comments
Many activists work to challenge ableist attitudes in society.
The building's lack of ramps was seen as an ableist oversight.
Using ableist language can make people with disabilities feel unwelcome.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discriminatory - treating people unfairly based on group or category
  • antonym: inclusive - including all people, especially those who might be excluded
