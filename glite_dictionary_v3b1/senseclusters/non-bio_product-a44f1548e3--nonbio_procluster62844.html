non-bio product

noun
product without biological enzymes or chemical additives
3.9 4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˈbaɪ.oʊ ˈprɑ.dʌkt / (AmE), / nɒn ˈbʌɪ.əʊ ˈprɒd.ʌkt / (BrE)
A product, especially a detergent or soap, that does not contain biological enzymes or chemical additives, often marketed as suitable for sensitive skin.

Examples

Non-bio products for skin
Doctors often recommend non-bio products for people with sensitive skin.
Many parents choose non-bio products for washing their baby's clothes.
Supermarkets stock a wide variety of non-bio products alongside regular detergents.
