comforter

noun
quilted bed covering with filling
4.9 2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌm.fər.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkʌm.fət.ə/ (BrE)
a thick bedding quilt made of two layers of cloth with insulating filling stitched between them, used as a bed covering

Examples

A warm comforter
He pulled the comforter up to his chin and quickly fell asleep.
She replaced her old blanket with a new down-filled comforter for extra warmth.
The floral comforter added a splash of color to the bedroom.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: blanket - a large piece of soft cloth used to cover oneself for warmth
  • synonym: duvet - a soft quilt filled with down or synthetic fiber, used instead of an upper sheet and blankets
