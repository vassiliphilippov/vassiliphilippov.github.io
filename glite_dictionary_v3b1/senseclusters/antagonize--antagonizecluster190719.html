Examples

Synonyms

  • provoke
    3.6 9.13/m
    provoke means annoying someone to cause a reaction, while antagonize means making someone unfriendly and angry toward you.
  • anger
    1.0 6.41/m
    anger makes someone feel angry, while antagonize makes them unfriendly and likely to act against you.
  • stir up
    4.8 2.53/m
    stir up causes strong emotions or trouble in people or groups, while antagonize targets a person to make them unfriendly.
