greenly

adverb
in a green color or manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinli / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnli / (BrE)
In a way that is green in color or appearance; in a green manner.

Examples

Shine greenly after rain
After the rain, the hills shone greenly under the morning sun.
The countryside rolled greenly down into the valley.
Vines crept greenly along the old stone wall.
