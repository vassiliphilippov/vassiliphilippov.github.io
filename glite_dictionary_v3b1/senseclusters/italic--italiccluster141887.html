italic

noun
handwriting with right-slanting letters
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtælɪk / (AmE), / ɪˈtælɪk / (BrE)
a style of handwriting in which the letters slant to the right, often used for emphasis or distinction

Examples

Italic handwriting style
Learning italic can improve the legibility of your handwriting.
She practiced her italic until her slanted letters looked elegant.
The invitation was written entirely in italic, making it look formal.
