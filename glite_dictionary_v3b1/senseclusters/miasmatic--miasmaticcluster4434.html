miasmatic

adjective
foul-smelling vapor
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪəzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəzˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or having the qualities of something unhealthy, foul-smelling, or oppressive, either literally (like bad air) or figuratively (like a corrupt atmosphere)
