Synonyms

  • pulsate
    4.1 1/m
    pulsate is a steady, regular rise and fall like a heartbeat, while throb often suggests pain or strong emotion.
  • pulse
    4.2 2/m
    pulse is a regular beat or wave you can measure, while throb usually feels stronger and often links to pain or emotion.
  • thump
    3.5 1.0/m
    thump is a heavy, blunt sound or beat you hear, while throb is a steadier, aching rhythm felt inside.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: flutter - to move with a light, quick, irregular motion (weaker than throb)
  • synonym: pound - to beat repeatedly or with force, often with a regular rhythm
  • synonym: pulsate - to expand and contract rhythmically; to throb
  • synonym: quiver - to shake with slight, rapid motion
  • synonym: tremble - to shake involuntarily, typically as a result of anxiety, excitement, or frailty
