fern

noun
a flowerless, seedless vascular plant
3.5
Pronunciation: / fɝn / (AmE), / fɜːn / (BrE)
A flowerless, seedless vascular plant with feathery fronds, reproducing by spores, typically having true roots arising from a rhizome.

Examples

Green fern leaves
Moist, forested areas provide an ideal environment for ferns to thrive.
She identified the plant as a rare species of fern native to the region.
The shaded garden was filled with ferns growing between the rocks.
