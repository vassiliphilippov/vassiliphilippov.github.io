abstinent

adjective
refraining from indulgence, especially food or drink
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæbstɪnənt / (AmE), / ˈæbstɪnənt / (BrE)
Deliberately avoiding or refraining from indulging in certain foods, drinks, or other pleasurable activities, especially for self-discipline or religious reasons.

Examples

Abstinent lifestyle
After many years of excess, he adopted an abstinent lifestyle for his health.
People who are abstinent from sugary foods often report increased energy and focus.
She remained abstinent during the month, choosing not to drink any alcohol at events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abstemious - not allowing oneself much in the way of food or drink; moderate
