milkily

adverb
in a milky way
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪkɪli / (AmE), / ˈmɪkɪli / (BrE)
in a way that looks or appears like milk, especially being white, cloudy, or opaque

Examples

Milkily cloudy water
The glass of water looked milkily cloudy after the powder was mixed in.
Moonlight shone milkily through the thin curtains, lighting up the room softly.
The pond reflected the sky milkily on a foggy morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or transparent
  • synonym: opaquely - in a way that is not clear or transparent
