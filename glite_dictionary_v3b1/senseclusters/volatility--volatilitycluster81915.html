volatility

noun
tendency to change rapidly and unpredictably
6.5 5.7
Pronunciation: /vɑː.lə.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /vɒ.lə.ˈtɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
