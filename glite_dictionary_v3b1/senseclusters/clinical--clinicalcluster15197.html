clinical

adjective
relating to clinics or medical observation
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪnɪkl̩ / (AmE), / ˈklɪnɪkl / (BrE)
relating to a medical clinic, or based on direct observation and treatment of patients rather than theoretical or laboratory studies

Examples

Clinical trial results
His clinical experience helps him provide excellent care to his patients.
She is conducting a clinical trial to test the effectiveness of the new drug.
The doctor made a clinical diagnosis based on the patient's symptoms and appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laboratory - relating to work done in a laboratory or through experimental study
