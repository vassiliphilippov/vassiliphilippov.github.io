footworn

adjective
tired from walking
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtwɔrn / (AmE), / ˈfʊtwɔːn / (BrE)
tired or exhausted from walking, especially with sore or aching feet

Examples

Footworn traveler
After hours of sightseeing, she was completely footworn and needed to rest.
The footworn travelers finally reached the village as the sun was setting.
He looked footworn after walking all day through the crowded city streets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: refreshed - feeling rested and energetic
  • synonym: weary - feeling or showing tiredness
