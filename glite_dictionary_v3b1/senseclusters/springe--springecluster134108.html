springe

verb
to catch with a snare
9.9
Pronunciation: / sprɪndʒ / (AmE), / sprɪndʒ / (BrE)
to catch or entrap a person or animal, especially by using a snare or trap

Examples

To springe a rabbit
The hunters hoped to springe several rabbits before nightfall.
In old tales, villagers would springe wolves to protect their livestock.
He tried to springe the thief with a cleverly hidden rope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trap - to catch an animal or person using a device or trick
