Cape lobster

noun
small lobster found in southern Africa
2.8
Pronunciation: / keɪp ˈlɑb.stɚ / (AmE), / keɪp ˈlɒb.stə / (BrE)
a small species of lobster native to southern Africa, especially the coastal waters near the Cape of Good Hope

Examples

The Cape lobster species
Local fishermen occasionally catch a Cape lobster among the more common species.
The waters around South Africa are home to the rare Cape lobster.
Unlike its larger relatives, the Cape lobster is quite small and elusive.
