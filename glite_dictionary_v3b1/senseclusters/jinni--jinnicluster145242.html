jinni

noun
supernatural spirit in Islamic belief
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪni / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪni / (BrE)
(in Islamic belief) a supernatural spirit capable of appearing in human or animal form and influencing people, mentioned in the Quran.

Examples

A jinni in stories
According to legend, a powerful jinni once inhabited the ancient ruins.
Many stories in Arabian folklore feature a clever jinni who interacts with humans.
She read about a jinni who could shape-shift into any animal.
