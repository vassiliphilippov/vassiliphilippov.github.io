flashing

noun
brief vivid experience or sensation
Pronunciation: / ˈflæʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈflæʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a sudden and brief experience or feeling, especially a vivid emotion or sensation

Examples

A flashing of fear
He felt a sudden flashing of anxiety before the interview began.
The novel captures flashings of joy amid the character's struggles.
There were occasional flashings of inspiration during the long meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flash - a sudden, brief burst of light or emotion
