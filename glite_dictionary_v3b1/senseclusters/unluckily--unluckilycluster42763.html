unluckily

adverb
by bad luck; unfortunately
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlʌkɪli / (AmE), / ʌnˈlʌkɪli / (BrE)
By bad luck; in an unfortunate or unfavorable manner.

Examples

Sadly unluckily
Unluckily, we missed the last train by just five minutes.
He had prepared well for the test, but unluckily fell ill on the exam day.
The weather, unluckily, turned bad just as the picnic began.
