wonder

noun
something that inspires awe or admiration
3.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈwʌn.dər/ (AmE), /ˈwʌn.də/ (BrE)
something or someone that is very surprising, beautiful, or amazing and causes admiration or awe

Examples

A natural wonder
She gazed in awe at the natural wonder before her.
The ancient pyramids are a wonder of engineering and design.
The rapid progress in technology is one of the many wonders of our time.
At just sixteen, he quickly became the scientific wonder of his generation.
People called her a mathematical wonder due to her extraordinary problem-solving skills.
The young chess wonder astounded audiences with her brilliant strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marvel - something that causes great surprise or admiration
  • synonym: miracle - a remarkable event that inspires wonder, often believed to be caused by a supernatural force
  • synonym: prodigy - a young person endowed with exceptional qualities or abilities
