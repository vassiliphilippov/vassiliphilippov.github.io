lace

verb
add alcohol or drugs to drink/food
Pronunciation: / leɪs / (AmE), / leɪs / (BrE)
to add alcohol or a strong substance to a drink or food, often secretly or to enhance its effect

Examples

To lace a drink
He always laces his eggnog with extra brandy during the holidays.
She decided to lace his coffee with a dash of whiskey before the meeting.
The drink was laced with rum, making it much stronger than expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spike - to add alcohol or a drug to a drink, usually secretly
