micromanagement

noun
precise game control
5.6
Pronunciation: /maɪ.krə.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /maɪ.kroʊ.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ (AmE), /maɪ.krə.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /maɪ.krəʊ.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ (BrE)
the practice of controlling individual units or actions in strategy or simulation games with great precision and speed to gain an advantage

Examples

Game micromanagement skills

Learning micromanagement skills can make a big difference in competitive gaming.
