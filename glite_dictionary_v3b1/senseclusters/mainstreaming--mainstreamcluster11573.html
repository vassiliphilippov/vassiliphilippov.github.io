mainstreaming

noun
inclusion in regular groups
4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪn.striː.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪn.striː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of including people, especially those with disabilities, in regular activities or groups rather than separating them

Examples

School mainstreaming
Mainstreaming has become a common approach in many schools to support students with disabilities.
Advocates believe that mainstreaming helps promote understanding and acceptance among all students.
The policy of mainstreaming aims to provide equal opportunities for everyone in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclusion - the act of including all people, especially those who might be excluded
  • antonym: segregation - the act of separating people, often by group or ability
