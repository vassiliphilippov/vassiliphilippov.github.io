pastoral

adjective
relating to raising livestock or herding
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpæstərəl / (AmE), / ˈpɑːstərəl / (BrE)
relating to the countryside, rural life, or the raising of livestock, often in an idealized or peaceful way

Examples

Pastoral countryside scene
Her research focuses on the pastoral traditions of Central Asian communities.
Many nomadic groups lead a pastoral lifestyle, tending flocks across vast grasslands.
The region is known for its pastoral economy based on sheep and cattle raising.
Artists of the Romantic era often painted pastoral scenes filled with grazing sheep and shepherds.
The novel depicts a pastoral landscape untouched by modern civilization.
We spent our vacation in a pastoral village surrounded by rolling hills and meadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agricultural - relating to farming or agriculture
  • synonym: bucolic - Relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and country life
  • synonym: rustic - Having a simplicity and charm considered typical of the countryside
  • antonym: urban - Relating to a city or town, as opposed to the countryside
