midrange

noun
middle audio frequencies
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪd.reɪndʒ/ /; / /mɪd.ˈreɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ˈmɪd.reɪndʒ/ /; / /mɪd.ˈreɪndʒ/ (BrE)
