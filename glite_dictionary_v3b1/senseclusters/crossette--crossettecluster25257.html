crossette

noun
corner decoration
Pronunciation: / krəˈsɛt / (AmE), / krəˈsɛt / (BrE)
a small projecting or decorative feature at the corner of a structure or in fireworks

Examples

A crossette corner
The architect added a crossette to each corner of the window frame for extra detail.
During the fireworks show, a bright crossette burst at the edge of the display.
The old building's stone arches were each finished with a carved crossette.
