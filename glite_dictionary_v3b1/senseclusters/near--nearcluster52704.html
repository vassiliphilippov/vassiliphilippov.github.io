near

adverb
close in space, time, or relationship
Pronunciation: / nɪr / (AmE), / nɪə / (BrE)
at or to a short distance in space, time, or relationship; close by.

Examples

Come near quickly
As the train moved, the station quickly came near.
Her birthday is drawing near, so she's making plans for a party.
Please don't stand too near the edge of the platform.
