slinker

noun
person who sneaks
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋkər / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋkə / (BrE)
a person or animal who moves quietly or acts in a sneaky way to avoid being noticed or to avoid responsibility

Examples

A slinker moves quietly
The slinker crept along the hallway, hoping no one would notice him.
At night, the alley was often visited by a slinker searching for food.
She moved like a true slinker, silent and almost invisible in the shadows.
No one trusted the slinker who always disappeared when work needed to be done.
Instead of helping his friends, he acted like a slinker and hid in the background.
They accused her of being a slinker for avoiding the difficult conversation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and willingness to face danger
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • synonym: lurker - someone who stays hidden or moves quietly to avoid attention
  • synonym: sneak - a person who moves quietly to avoid being seen
