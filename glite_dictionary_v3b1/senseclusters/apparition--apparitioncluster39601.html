apparition

noun
appearance of a ghost or spirit
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈɹɪʃən /; / ˌæpəˈrɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌapəˈɾɪʃən /; / ˌæpəˈrɪʃən / (BrE)
a ghost or supernatural figure that appears suddenly or unexpectedly

Examples

Ghostly apparition
Late at night, he claimed to see an apparition in the hallway.
Some people believe that an apparition foretells important events.
The sudden apparition of the old woman startled everyone in the room.
For centuries, villagers have spoken of an apparition haunting the churchyard.
She was startled by an apparition that seemed to vanish as quickly as it appeared.
The children claimed to see an apparition drifting through the old house at night.
He awoke in the night and thought he saw an apparition standing at the foot of his bed.
The children claimed to see an apparition in the old, abandoned house.
The sudden apparition of a robed figure startled everyone in the church.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ghost - the spirit of a dead person that is believed to appear to the living
  • synonym: hallucination - an experience involving the perception of something not present
  • synonym: phantom - something that has appearance but no reality; a ghost
  • antonym: reality - the state of things as they actually exist
  • synonym: specter - a ghost; something widely feared as a possible unpleasant occurrence
