crawly

adjective
infested with insects
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔːli / (AmE), / ˈkrɔːli / (BrE)
full of or covered with small animals or insects that crawl, often causing discomfort or disgust

Examples

A crawly log
The old log was so crawly that I didn't want to touch it.
After lifting the stone, we found the ground underneath was crawly with ants and worms.
She felt a crawly sensation on her skin after walking through the tall grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infested - overrun by unwanted animals or insects
  • synonym: swarming - filled with large numbers of moving creatures
