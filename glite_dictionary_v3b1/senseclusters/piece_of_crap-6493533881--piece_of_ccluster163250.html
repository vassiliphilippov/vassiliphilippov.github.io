piece of crap

noun
something of very poor quality
2.8
Pronunciation: / pis əv kræp / (AmE), / piːs əv kræp / (BrE)
an object or thing regarded as being of very poor quality or worthless

Examples

A piece of crap car
He refused to buy that used car, saying it was just a piece of crap.
My old laptop is a piece of crap that crashes all the time.
She threw away the blender because it turned out to be a piece of crap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piece of junk - an item considered worthless or of very poor quality
  • synonym: piece of trash - an item regarded as useless or of very poor quality
