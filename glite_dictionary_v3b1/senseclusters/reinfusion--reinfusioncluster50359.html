reinfusion

noun
restoring energy or resources
7.0
Pronunciation: /riː.ɪn.ˈfjuː.ʒən/ /; / /rɪn.ˈfjuː.ʒən/ (AmE), /riː.ɪn.ˈfjuː.ʒən/ /; / /rɪn.ˈfjuː.ʒən/ (BrE)
