reinfusion

noun
restoring energy or resources
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈfjuːʒən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈfjuːʒən / (BrE)
the act of adding something again, such as energy, funds, or enthusiasm, to revive or strengthen a person, group, or organization

Examples

Reinfusion of energy
The manager's speech provided a much-needed reinfusion of motivation for the entire team.
After the grant, the organization experienced a reinfusion of funds that allowed it to expand its programs.
A reinfusion of enthusiasm helped the club attract new members and plan exciting events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depletion - the reduction or exhaustion of resources or energy
  • synonym: replenishment - the act of filling something up again
  • synonym: revitalization - the process of giving new life or energy to something
