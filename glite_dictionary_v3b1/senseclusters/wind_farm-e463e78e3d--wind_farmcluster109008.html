wind farm

noun
area with wind turbines for electricity
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɪnd fɑrm / (AmE), / wɪnd fɑːm / (BrE)
An area of land or sea with a group of wind turbines installed to generate electricity.

Examples

A wind farm site
A large company invested in building a wind farm to supply renewable energy.
Photographs showed dozens of turbines stretching across the wind farm on the hillside.
The new wind farm off the coast can power thousands of homes.
