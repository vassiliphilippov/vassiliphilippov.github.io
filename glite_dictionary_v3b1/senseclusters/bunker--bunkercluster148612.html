bunker

noun
sand hazard on a golf course
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋ.kər / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋ.kə / (BrE)
an obstacle on a golf course, typically a hollow filled with sand, designed to challenge players

Examples

Sand bunker
A well-placed bunker can dramatically increase the difficulty of a golf hole.
She practiced getting out of the bunker before her big golf tournament.
The ball landed in the bunker, making his next shot much more challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hazard - danger or obstacle in a golf course
