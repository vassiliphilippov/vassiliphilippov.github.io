Dumuzi

proper noun
Sumerian-Babylonian god of pastures and vegetation
9.9 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / duËËˆmuËzi / (AmE), / duËËˆmuËzi / (BrE)
A Sumerian and Babylonian god of pastures and vegetation, regarded as the consort of the goddess Inanna.

Examples

Dumuzi the god
Ancient texts often depict Dumuzi as a vital figure in the yearly agricultural cycle.
Priests performed rituals to honor Dumuzi during the spring festival.
The myth of Inanna and Dumuzi represents death and rebirth in Sumerian religion.

Synonyms

  • Tammuz
