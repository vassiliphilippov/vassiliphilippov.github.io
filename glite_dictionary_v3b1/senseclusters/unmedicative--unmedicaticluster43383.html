unmedicative

adjective
not having a medicinal effect
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈmɛdɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmɛdɪkətɪv / (BrE)
Not having a medicinal effect or not intended for medical purposes.

Examples

An unmedicative treatment
It’s important to distinguish between unmedicative and prescribed treatments in clinical studies.
Many people use dietary supplements for unmedicative reasons, such as taste or habit, rather than health.
The herbal tea turned out to be entirely unmedicative, offering no real relief for his cold.
