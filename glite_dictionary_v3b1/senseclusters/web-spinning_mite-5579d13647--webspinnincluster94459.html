web-spinning mite

noun
mite that produces webs
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛb ˌspɪnɪŋ ˈmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛb ˌspɪnɪŋ ˈmaɪt / (BrE)
a type of mite that produces webs, often found on plants and known to cause damage by feeding on them
