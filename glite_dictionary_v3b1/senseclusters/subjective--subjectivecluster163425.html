subjective

adjective
based on personal feelings or opinions
Pronunciation: / səbˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / səbˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
based on or existing in a person's mind, feelings, or opinions, not on external facts

Examples

A subjective opinion
Her evaluation of the painting was highly subjective, influenced by her personal tastes.
It is difficult to eliminate subjective bias when making decisions.
Pain is a subjective experience that can vary greatly between individuals.
Dreams are often described as subjective events that take place entirely within one's mind.
Her response was based on a purely subjective interpretation rather than observable facts.
The perception of beauty can be considered a subjective experience, unique to each individual.

Related Terms

  • antonym: objective - not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; unbiased
