Synonyms

  • instinctive
    4.1 3/m
    instinctive is prompted by natural feelings and urges without thought, while automatic covers actions done without thinking or by habit.
  • reflexive
    6.8 0.8/m
    reflexive is an immediate bodily response without thought, while automatic describes learned or instinctive actions done without thinking.
  • involuntary
    5.4 5/m
    involuntary is done without control and sometimes against your will, while automatic often means a habit or action done without thinking.
