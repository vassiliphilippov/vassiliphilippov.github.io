rutabaga plant

noun
plant yielding the rutabaga root
Pronunciation: / ˌruːtəˈbeɪɡə plænt / (AmE), / ˌruːtəˈbɑːɡə plɑːnt / (BrE)
a cruciferous plant (Brassica napus Napobrassica group) with a thick, bulbous, edible yellow root, commonly grown as a root vegetable
