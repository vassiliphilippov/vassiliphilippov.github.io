postmenopausal

adjective
after menopause (in women)
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstmɛnoʊˈpɔzəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstmɛnəʊˈpɔːzəl / (BrE)
relating to the period of a woman's life following menopause

Examples

Postmenopausal women
Most postmenopausal women experience a decrease in bone density.
She began hormone therapy after becoming postmenopausal.
The study focused on the health risks for postmenopausal patients.
