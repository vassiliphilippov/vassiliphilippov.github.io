Panax quinquefolius

A North American woodland herb, commonly known as American ginseng, used especially as a substitute for Chinese ginseng.

Examples

Panax quinquefolius herb
Due to its similar properties, Panax quinquefolius is sometimes marketed as an alternative to Asian ginseng.
Many herbal supplements contain extracts of Panax quinquefolius for their potential health benefits.
Traditional healers in North America often use Panax quinquefolius as a natural remedy for fatigue.
