half-and-half dressing

noun
dressing of mayonnaise and vinaigrette with seasonings
8.0 4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhæf.ən.hæf ˈdres.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhæf.ænd.hæf ˈdres.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːf.ən.hɑːf ˈdres.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhɑːf.ænd.hɑːf ˈdres.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
A salad dressing made from equal parts mayonnaise and vinaigrette, seasoned with minced garlic, mashed anchovies, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Examples

A half-and-half dressing recipe
Many people enjoy a half-and-half dressing when they want a creamy yet tangy salad topping.
She prepared a half-and-half dressing to accompany the grilled vegetables.
The chef recommended the half-and-half dressing for the mixed green salad.
