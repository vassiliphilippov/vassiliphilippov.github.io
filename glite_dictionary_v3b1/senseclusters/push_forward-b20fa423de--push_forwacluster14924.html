push forward

verb
make progress despite difficulties
3.1 0.64/m
Pronunciation: / p ä É Ààf…îrw…ôrd / (AmE), / p ä É Ààf…îÀêw…ôd / (BrE)
to continue to make progress or move ahead, especially despite obstacles or difficulties

Examples

To push forward with plans
Despite the setbacks, our team kept pushing forward until we achieved our goals.
Even when things get tough, it's important to keep pushing forward.
He faced many challenges in his career, but he never stopped pushing forward.
