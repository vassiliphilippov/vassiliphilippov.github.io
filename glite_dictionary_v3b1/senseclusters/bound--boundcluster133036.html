bound

verb
to spring or bounce back after impact
6.7
Pronunciation: / baʊnd / (AmE), / baʊnd / (BrE)
(of an object) to spring back or bounce away after striking a surface or object.

Examples

Coins bound off tiles
Coins bound across the tiles when they fell from his pocket.
The glass marble bounded several times before coming to rest.
The tennis ball bounded off the wall and landed in the grass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bounce - to spring back from a surface after hitting it
  • synonym: rebound - to bounce back after hitting a surface
