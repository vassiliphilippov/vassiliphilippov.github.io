corded

adjective
with a cord attached
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdɪd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɪd / (BrE)

Examples

He bought a corded drill because it provides continuous power for long projects.
Many people still use corded headphones for better sound quality.
The old office had only corded telephones on every desk.
