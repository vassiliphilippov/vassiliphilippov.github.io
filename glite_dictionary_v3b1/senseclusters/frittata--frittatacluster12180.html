frittata

noun
Italian omelet with vegetables and meats
Pronunciation: / friˈtɑtə / (AmE), / frɪˈtɑːtə / (BrE)
An Italian omelet made with beaten eggs, diced vegetables, and meats, cooked on one side and then inverted or finished to cook the top.

Examples

A frittata with vegetables
I ordered a spinach and mushroom frittata for breakfast at the café.
She prepared a vegetable frittata for the family brunch.
The recipe suggests inverting the frittata to brown the top evenly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: omelet - A dish of beaten eggs cooked in a frying pan and usually folded around a filling
  • degree higher: quiche - A baked dish of eggs and cream in a pastry shell with various ingredients
