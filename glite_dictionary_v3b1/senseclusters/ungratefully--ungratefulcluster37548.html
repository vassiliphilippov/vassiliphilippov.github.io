ungratefully

adverb
in an ungrateful manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡreɪtfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪtfəli / (BrE)
In an ungrateful manner; without showing thanks or appreciation.

Examples

To act ungratefully
He accepted her help ungratefully, barely acknowledging her efforts.
She noticed that her efforts were often received ungratefully by her colleagues.
The children left the table ungratefully, without a word of thanks for their meal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gratefully - in a grateful manner; showing thanks
