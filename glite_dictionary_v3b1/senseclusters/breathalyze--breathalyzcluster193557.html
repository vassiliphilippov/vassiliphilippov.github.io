breathalyze

verb
test with a breathalyzer
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to test someone's alcohol level in their blood using a breathalyzer device

Examples

To breathalyze a driver
Officers routinely breathalyze motorists during random checks for impaired driving.
She was breathalyzed at the roadside following a traffic stop.
The police decided to breathalyze the driver after noticing erratic behavior.
All motorists involved in the accident were breathalysed by the officers at the scene.
She was relieved when she passed the test after being breathalysed late at night.
The police decided to breathalyse the driver after he was seen swerving on the road.
