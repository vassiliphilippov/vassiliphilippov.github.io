vexatiously

adverb
in an annoying or irritating way
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɛkˈseɪʃəsli / (AmE), / vɛkˈseɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that causes annoyance, irritation, or worry, often deliberately or persistently.

Examples

To act vexatiously
He acted vexatiously to disrupt the meeting and irritate everyone involved.
She kept interrupting vexatiously whenever anyone tried to speak.
The defendant filed multiple complaints vexatiously, delaying the legal proceedings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annoyingly - in a manner that causes annoyance
  • antonym: calmly - in a calm and untroubled manner
