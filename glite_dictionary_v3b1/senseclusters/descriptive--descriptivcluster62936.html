descriptive

adjective
giving details; serving to describe
1.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskrɪptɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈskrɪptɪv / (BrE)
Giving details about someone or something; serving to describe or to provide information.

Examples

Descriptive essay
A descriptive essay paints a picture using sensory details.
The author used descriptive language to create vivid images in the reader's mind.
The report included several descriptive statistics about the survey results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: explanatory - intended to explain or make clear
  • synonym: narrative - telling a story or relating events
  • antonym: prescriptive - giving rules or directions
