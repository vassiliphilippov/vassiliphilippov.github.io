backsplash

noun
protective kitchen panel
3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.splæʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.splæʃ/ (BrE)
a panel or surface installed on a wall behind a sink or stove to protect it from splashes and stains

Examples

Kitchen backsplash
They chose a glass backsplash to add a modern touch to their kitchen.
Cleaning the backsplash is easy because it is made of smooth ceramic tiles.
A stainless steel backsplash protects the wall behind the stove from oil and food splatters.
