braker

noun
person or device using brake
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkə / (BrE)
a person or device that operates or applies a brake to slow down or stop a vehicle or machine

Examples

The braker on train
The braker on the old train was responsible for stopping each car safely.
A skilled braker can prevent accidents by reacting quickly in emergencies.
Modern vehicles often use an automatic braker to assist with sudden stops.

  • synonym: operator - a person who controls a machine or vehicle
