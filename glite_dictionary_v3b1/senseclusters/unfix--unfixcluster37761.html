unfix

verb
to remove fixative
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪks / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪks / (BrE)
to remove a fixative or undo the process of fixation, especially in scientific or photographic contexts

Examples

To unfix a sample
The scientist needed to unfix the tissue sample before applying the next chemical.
After developing the photograph, the technician will unfix the image to complete the process.
It is important to unfix the specimen carefully to avoid damaging the cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remove - to take something away or off
