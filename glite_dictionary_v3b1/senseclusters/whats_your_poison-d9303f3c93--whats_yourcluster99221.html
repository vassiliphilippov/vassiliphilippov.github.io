what's your poison

x
ask what alcoholic drink someone wants
Pronunciation: / wʌts jɚ ˈpɔɪzən / (AmE), / wɒts jə ˈpɔɪzən / (BrE)
(informal) Used to ask someone what kind of alcoholic drink they would like.

Examples

What's your poison?
At the wedding reception, my uncle asked, "what's your poison?" before heading to the bar.
During happy hour, the waiter approached our table and asked, "what's your poison tonight?"
He signaled the bartender and said, "what's your poison?" with a friendly smile.
