Sophonias

proper noun
biblical book; Book of Zephaniah
Pronunciation: / soʊfəˈnaɪ.əs / (AmE), / sɒfəˈnaɪ.əs / (BrE)
another name for the biblical Book of Zephaniah in the Old Testament, recording prophecies of judgment against Judah.

Examples

The Book of Sophonias
In some versions of the Bible, Sophonias is used instead of Zephaniah.
Many biblical scholars have studied the prophecies found in Sophonias.
The book of Sophonias contains warnings about the coming Day of the Lord.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Zephaniah - another name for the Book of Sophonias or its author, a prophet in the Old Testament
