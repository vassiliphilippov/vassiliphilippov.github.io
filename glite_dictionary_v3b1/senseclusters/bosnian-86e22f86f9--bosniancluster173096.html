Bosnian

proper noun
person from Bosnia
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːzniən / (AmE), / ˈbɒzniən / (BrE)
a person from Bosnia and Herzegovina or relating to its people, language, or culture

Examples

A Bosnian person
Many Bosnian refugees settled in different countries during the 1990s.
The Bosnian language is one of the official languages of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
She learned to cook traditional Bosnian dishes from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Herzegovinian - a person from Herzegovina, a region of Bosnia and Herzegovina
