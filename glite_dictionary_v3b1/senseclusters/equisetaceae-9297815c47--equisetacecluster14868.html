Equisetaceae

noun
family of horsetail plants
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkwɪˈsɛtəˌsiː /; / ˌekwɪsɪˈteɪsiːˌaɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛkwɪˌsiːtəˈsiː /; / ˌɛkwɪsɪˈtɛɪsɪˌiː / (BrE)
a family of primitive, non-flowering plants called horsetails; the only surviving family of the order Equisetales
