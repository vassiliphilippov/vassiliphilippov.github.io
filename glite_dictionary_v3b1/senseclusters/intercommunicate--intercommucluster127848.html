intercommunicate

verb
be interconnected; allow passage
7.2 0.00/m

(of rooms, spaces, or systems) to be directly connected so as to allow passage or exchange between them.

Examples

Intercommunicate rooms
All the rooms on this floor intercommunicate for easy access during emergencies.
In this design, the library and lounge intercommunicate by means of a sliding partition.
The two offices intercommunicate through a hidden doorway.
