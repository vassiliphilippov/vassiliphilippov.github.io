sparkling

adjective
shining with flashes of light
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrk.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋ / (BrE)
shining with flashes of light; bright and lively in appearance.

Examples

Sparkling eyes
Champagne is known for its sparkling appearance and lively bubbles.
She greeted us with sparkling eyes and a warm smile.
The sparkling snow covered the entire landscape, making it look magical.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - not bright or shiny; lacking luster
  • synonym: glittering - shining with a bright, shimmering reflected light
