astrally

adverb
in a star-like or spiritual way
Pronunciation: / ˈæstrəli / (AmE), / ˈæstrəli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to stars or the spirit world, especially in a mystical or supernatural sense

Examples

To astrally travel
Some people believe they can travel astrally while meditating or dreaming.
The ceremony was said to connect participants astrally with distant realms.
She described seeing herself astrally floating above her sleeping body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: physically - in a way relating to the body or material world
  • synonym: spiritually - in a way relating to the spirit or soul
