unlettered person

noun
illiterate or uneducated person
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɛtɚd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / ʌnˈletɪd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is illiterate or lacks formal education

Examples

An unlettered person
As an unlettered person, he found it difficult to fill out the required paperwork.
Employers sometimes offer special training programs for the unlettered person seeking work.
The charity focuses on teaching reading skills to every unlettered person in the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: educated person - a person with formal education
  • synonym: illiterate - unable to read or write
