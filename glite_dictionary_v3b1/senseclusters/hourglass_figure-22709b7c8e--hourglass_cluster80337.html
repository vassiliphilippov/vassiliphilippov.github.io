hourglass figure

noun
body shape with narrow waist and wide hips and bust
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊɚˌɡlæs ˈfɪɡjɚ / (AmE), / ˈaʊəˌɡlɑːs ˈfɪɡə / (BrE)
a body shape characterized by a narrow waist with proportionally wider hips and bust, resembling an hourglass

Examples

A hourglass figure shape
Many models in the 1950s were praised for their hourglass figure.
