humeral

adjective
of insect shoulder region
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuːmərəl / (AmE), / ˈhjuːmərəl / (BrE)
relating to the humeral region, especially the area near the base of a wing or the shoulder-like part of the thorax in insects or arthropods

Examples

Humeral patch on wing
The butterfly displayed a distinctive humeral patch near the base of its forewing.
Researchers observed a humeral marking that helped identify the beetle species.
The humeral area of the insect's thorax was covered in fine yellow hairs.
