tidal wave of emotions

noun
sudden surge of emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪdəl weɪv əv ɪˈmoʊʃənz / (AmE), / ˈtaɪdəl weɪv əv ɪˈməʊʃənz / (BrE)
a sudden and overwhelming surge of strong feelings

Examples

A tidal wave of emotions hits
Standing on stage, he was hit by a tidal wave of emotions that nearly brought him to tears.
The old photographs triggered a tidal wave of emotions that she found hard to control.
When she heard the news, a tidal wave of emotions crashed over her, leaving her speechless.
