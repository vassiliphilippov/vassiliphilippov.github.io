temerity

noun
excessive boldness; audacity
Pronunciation: / təˈmɛrəti / (AmE), / tɪˈmɛrɪti / (BrE)
excessive confidence or boldness; audacity that disregards danger or consequences

Examples

His temerity shocked
His temerity stunned even those accustomed to bold behavior.
No one else had the temerity to climb the mountain in such harsh weather.
She had the temerity to question the manager's decision during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audacity - boldness or daring, often with disregard for safety or rules
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence; shyness
