flat-leaf parsley

noun
parsley with flat leaves, used in cooking
Pronunciation: / ˈflæt liːf ˈpɑrsli / (AmE), / ˈflæt liːf ˈpɑːsli / (BrE)
A variety of parsley with flat, broad leaves, commonly used in cooking for its strong flavor.

Examples

Flat-leaf parsley leaves
She prefers using flat-leaf parsley over curly parsley in her recipes.
The chef sprinkled freshly chopped flat-leaf parsley over the pasta dish before serving.
You can substitute flat-leaf parsley for cilantro if you want a milder flavor.
