screamer

noun
sensational newspaper headline
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːmər / (AmE), / ˈskriːmə / (BrE)
a sensational or shocking newspaper headline intended to attract attention

Examples

Newspaper screamer
Editors know that a good screamer can draw in curious readers.
Every tabloid seems to have a screamer about scandals or shocking events.
The newspaper ran a screamer on the front page to boost sales.
