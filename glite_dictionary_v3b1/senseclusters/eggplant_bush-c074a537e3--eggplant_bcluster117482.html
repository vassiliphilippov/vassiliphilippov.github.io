eggplant bush

noun
plant producing eggplant fruit
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌplænt bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌplɑːnt bʊʃ / (BrE)
A hairy, upright plant native to southeastern Asia, widely cultivated for its large, glossy edible fruit (eggplant), commonly used as a vegetable.

Examples

The eggplant bush grows
After a few months, the eggplant bush produced several shiny, purple fruits ready for harvest.
Gardeners often start eggplant bush seedlings indoors before transferring them outside.
The eggplant bush thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eggplant - the large, glossy, edible fruit of the eggplant bush, often used as a vegetable
