eggplant bush

noun
plant producing eggplant fruit
7.4 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɛɡˌplænt bʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈɛɡˌplɑːnt bʊʃ / (BrE)
A hairy, upright plant native to southeastern Asia, widely cultivated for its large, glossy edible fruit (eggplant), commonly used as a vegetable.

The eggplant bush thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil.
