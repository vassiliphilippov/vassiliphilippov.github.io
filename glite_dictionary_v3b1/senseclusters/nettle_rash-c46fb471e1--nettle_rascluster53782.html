nettle rash

noun
itchy skin condition with pale weals and red edges
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛt̬l ˌræʃ / (AmE), / ˈnɛtl ˌræʃ / (BrE)
An itchy skin condition with raised, pale patches surrounded by red margins, caused by an allergic reaction.

Examples

A nettle rash reaction
After eating strawberries, she developed a nettle rash on her arms and legs.
He was prescribed antihistamines to relieve the itching caused by the nettle rash.
The doctor explained that a nettle rash can result from contact with certain plants or allergens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hives - skin condition with itchy, raised welts (urticaria)
