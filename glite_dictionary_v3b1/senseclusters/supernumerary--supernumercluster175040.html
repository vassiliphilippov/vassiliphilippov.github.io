supernumerary

noun
person serving no apparent function
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈnuː.məˌrɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnjuː.mə.rər.i / (BrE)
a person who is present but has no essential or assigned role; an extra or surplus person

Examples

Supernumerary staff member
During the reorganization, several employees were classified as supernumerary staff.
In the film industry, a supernumerary often appears in crowd scenes without a specific role.
The company found it hard to dismiss any supernumerary without disrupting team morale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: essential - a person or thing absolutely necessary
  • synonym: extra - a person engaged temporarily, especially in a performance, with no speaking part
