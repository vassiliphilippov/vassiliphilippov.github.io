craptastic

adjective
very poor quality
Pronunciation: / kræpˈtæstɪk / (AmE), / kræpˈtæstɪk / (BrE)
extremely bad or poor in quality, often in a way that is so ridiculous or incompetent it is almost funny (usually used humorously or sarcastically)
