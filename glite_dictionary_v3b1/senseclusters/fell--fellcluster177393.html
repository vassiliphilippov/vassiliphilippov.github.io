fell

adjective
cruel or fierce; merciless
Pronunciation: / fɛl / (AmE), / fɛl / (BrE)
fierce, cruel, or merciless, especially in intent to harm; able or disposed to inflict pain or suffering.

Examples

Fell enemy attack
Few survived the fell wrath of the invaders who showed no mercy to anyone.
Legends spoke of a fell beast that haunted the dark forest, preying on the unwary.
The fell tyrant ordered the execution of hundreds without remorse.

  • antonym: merciful - showing compassion or forgiveness
