hammerman

noun
worker using hammer
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈhæməˌmæn / (BrE)
a construction worker, especially a carpenter, who uses a hammer to drive nails and assemble wooden structures

Examples

Hammerman builds houses
The experienced hammerman quickly assembled the wooden frame for the new house.
Each hammerman on the team was responsible for a different section of the roof.
As a skilled hammerman, he could drive nails with just a few strong swings.
