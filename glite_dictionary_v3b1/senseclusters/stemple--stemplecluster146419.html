stemple

noun
tool for imprinting
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛmpəl / (AmE), / ˈstɛmpəl / (BrE)
a metal tool used to mark or imprint designs onto materials such as metal, leather, or paper

Examples

Metal stemple tool
The craftsman pressed the stemple into the metal to create a decorative pattern.
Each leather wallet was marked with a logo using a heated stemple.
She carefully aligned the stemple before imprinting the design onto the paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: die - a specialized tool used to cut or shape material
  • synonym: punch - a tool for making holes or indentations in material
  • synonym: stamp - a tool or device for impressing a mark or design
