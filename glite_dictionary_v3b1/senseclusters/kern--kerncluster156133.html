kern

noun
overhanging part of metal type
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɝn / (AmE), / kɜːn / (BrE)
the part of a piece of metal type that projects beyond its body, allowing the letter to overhang adjacent type

Examples

A metal kern
During the digital transition, the concept of the kern was adapted for software-based typography.
In traditional printing, a damaged kern could cause unsightly gaps between letters.
The kern on the letter 'f' allows it to overlap slightly with adjacent letters.
