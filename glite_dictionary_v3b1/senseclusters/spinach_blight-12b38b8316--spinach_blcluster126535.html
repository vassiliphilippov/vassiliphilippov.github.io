spinach blight

noun
disease of spinach plants
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnɪtʃ blaɪt / (AmE), / ˈspɪnɪtʃ blaɪt / (BrE)
a disease that affects spinach plants, causing yellowing and wilting of the leaves

Examples

Spinach blight disease
Careful crop rotation can help prevent spinach blight in the garden.
The farmers lost much of their crop to spinach blight this year.
Yellowed leaves on the plants were an early sign of spinach blight.
