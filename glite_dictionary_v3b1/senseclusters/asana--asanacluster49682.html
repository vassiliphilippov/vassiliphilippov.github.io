asana

noun
yoga or meditation posture
Pronunciation: / əˈsɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈæsənə / (BrE)
A posture or manner of sitting in yoga or meditation, especially as part of Hindu practice.

Examples

A asana posture
Each morning, she begins her day with a series of asanas to improve flexibility and focus.
He practiced the lotus asana to help with meditation and breathing exercises.
The instructor guided the class through a difficult asana that required balance and concentration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pose - a physical position of the body, often in exercise or yoga
