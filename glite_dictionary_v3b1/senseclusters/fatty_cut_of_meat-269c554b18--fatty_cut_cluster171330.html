fatty cut of meat

noun
meat portion with high fat content
Pronunciation: / ˈfæti kʌt əv mit / (AmE), / ˈfæti kʌt əv miːt / (BrE)
a piece of meat that contains a high proportion of fat

Examples

A fatty cut of meat
Butchers often display fatty cuts of meat alongside leaner options in the shop window.
Nutritionists recommend limiting your intake of fatty cuts of meat to maintain a healthy diet.
She trimmed the excess fat off the fatty cut of meat before cooking it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lean cut of meat - a piece of meat with little fat
