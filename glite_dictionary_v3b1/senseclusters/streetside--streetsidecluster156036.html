streetside

adverb
beside the street
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈstriːtsaɪd / (BrE)
at or on the side of a street; beside the street.

Examples

Vendors wait streetside
Vendors set up their stalls streetside to attract passing customers.
We found a small café with tables placed streetside for people-watching.
The delivery truck was parked streetside while the driver unloaded packages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curbside - at or near the edge of a street, especially by the curb
