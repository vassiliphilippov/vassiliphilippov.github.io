lower-ranking

adjective
having inferior rank or status
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊər ˈræŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊə ˈræŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a person or position) having a lower status or rank in an organization or hierarchy.

Examples

Lower-ranking employees
After the restructuring, many lower-ranking employees were transferred to other departments.
Complaints from lower-ranking staff members were often overlooked by upper management.
The general delegated minor tasks to his lower-ranking officers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junior - person or position with less experience or lower rank
  • antonym: senior - person or position of higher status or authority
