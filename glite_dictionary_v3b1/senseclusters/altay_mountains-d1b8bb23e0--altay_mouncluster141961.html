Altay Mountains

proper noun
mountain range in central Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.taɪ ˈmaʊn.tənz / (AmE), / ˈæl.taɪ ˈmaʊn.tɪnz / (BrE)
a mountain range in central Asia, stretching about a thousand miles from Kazakhstan east into western Mongolia and northern China
