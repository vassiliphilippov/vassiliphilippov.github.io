multipath

noun

Examples

Multipath interference problem
Engineers designed the antenna to reduce multipath effects in urban environments.
The accuracy of the navigation system was affected by severe multipath near tall buildings.
Wireless communication can suffer from multipath when signals reflect off various surfaces.
