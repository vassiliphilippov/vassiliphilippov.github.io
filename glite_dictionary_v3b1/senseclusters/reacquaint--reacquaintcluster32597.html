reacquaint

verb

5.3
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.ˈkweɪnt/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈkweɪnt/ (AmE), /riː.ə.ˈkweɪnt/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈkweɪnt/ (BrE)
