centricity

noun
state of being centered
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtrɪsəti / (AmE), / sɛnˈtrɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being centered around a main point, focus, or principle

Examples

Customer centricity
The centricity of the customer experience is a key factor in modern marketing strategies.
Her research emphasized the centricity of community involvement in successful projects.
Many companies are shifting toward greater centricity around environmental sustainability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centrality - the quality of being central or most important
  • antonym: peripherality - the quality of being on the edge or periphery
