sociometric

noun
social relationship test
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsi.əˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
a measurement, test, or survey used to assess relationships, preferences, or social status within a group

Examples

A sociometric test
The psychologist administered a sociometric to understand the group dynamics in the classroom.
A sociometric can reveal which team members are most influential among their peers.
After analyzing the sociometric results, the manager reorganized the project groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sociogram - a visual representation of social relationships within a group
  • synonym: survey - a method of collecting information from people
