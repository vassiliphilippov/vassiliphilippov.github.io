banding

noun
a decorative strip of different color or material
6.4 4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbændɪŋ / (BrE)
a strip or pattern of stripes of a different color or material, used as decoration or as a natural marking

Examples

Decorative banding
Architects added black banding to the building's facade for contrast.
The dress features silver banding along the sleeves and hem.
The wallpaper has gold banding that catches the light beautifully.
Some species of fish are easily identified by the banding on their fins.
The artist used bold banding to create a dramatic effect in the painting.
The caterpillar displays vivid banding along its entire length.
