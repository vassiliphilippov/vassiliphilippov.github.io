naturale

noun
natural card or note
Pronunciation: / ˌnætʃuˈrɑːleɪ / (AmE), / ˌnatʃuˈrɑːleɪ / (BrE)
a card or note that is natural, especially in games or music, often referring to an unaltered or standard form

Examples

A naturale card
He drew a naturale in the first round of the game.
The musician played a naturale instead of a sharp in the melody.
Winning with a naturale is considered lucky in some card games.
