pedologist

noun
child development researcher
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪˈdɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / pɪˈdɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a researcher who studies the development and behavior of children (archaic or rare usage)

Examples

Pedologist studies children
In the early 20th century, a pedologist would often visit schools to observe classroom behavior.
The pedologist published a study on the social interactions of young children.
Few people today use the term pedologist for experts in child development.

Related Terms

  • synonym: child psychologist - a specialist in the mental development of children
  • synonym: pediatrician - a doctor specializing in children's health
