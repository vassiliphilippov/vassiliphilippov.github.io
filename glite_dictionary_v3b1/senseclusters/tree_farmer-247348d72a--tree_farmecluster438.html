tree farmer

noun
person who cultivates and manages trees
Pronunciation: / tri ˈfɑrmər / (AmE), / tri ˈfɑːmə / (BrE)
a person who cultivates and manages trees for commercial, environmental, or conservation purposes

Examples

A tree farmer plants trees
As a tree farmer, she is committed to sustainable forestry practices.
Many tree farmers work closely with environmental organizations to protect wildlife habitats.
The tree farmer checked the young saplings every morning for signs of disease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forester - a person in charge of managing, planting, and caring for forests
