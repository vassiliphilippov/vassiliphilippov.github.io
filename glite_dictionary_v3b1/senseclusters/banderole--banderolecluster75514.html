banderole

noun
decorative ribbon or flag
Pronunciation: / ˈbændəˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈbændərəʊl / (BrE)
a narrow flag, ribbon, or streamer, often with an inscription, used for decoration or as an emblem

Examples

Decorative banderole
The ancient manuscript was decorated with a painted banderole containing a Latin phrase.
A colorful banderole fluttered above the entrance to the festival.
The artist added a golden banderole to the coat of arms for emphasis.
Colorful banderoles hung across the street during the festival.
Each float in the parade was decorated with bright banderoles fluttering in the wind.
The castle entrance was adorned with a banderole bearing the family crest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banner - a long strip of cloth bearing a slogan or design
  • synonym: pennant - a long, narrow flag, often used for signaling or decoration
