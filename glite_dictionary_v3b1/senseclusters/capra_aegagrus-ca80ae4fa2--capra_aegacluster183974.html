Capra aegagrus

noun
wild goat species native to Iran
Pronunciation: / ˈkæprə iːˈɡæɡrəs / (AmE), / ˈkæprə iːˈɡæɡrəs / (BrE)
A species of wild goat native to Iran and adjacent regions, also considered to be the ancestor of the domestic goat.
