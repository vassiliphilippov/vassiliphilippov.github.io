dancing shoe

noun
shoe designed for dancing
Pronunciation: / ˈdænsɪŋ ʃu / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnsɪŋ ʃuː / (BrE)
a shoe specifically designed or suitable for dancing, often providing flexibility, support, and appropriate sole for dance movements

Examples

Dancing shoes for salsa
He forgot his dancing shoes and had to sit out during the salsa lesson.
Leather-soled dancing shoes help dancers move smoothly across the floor.
She picked out her best dancing shoes for the big performance tonight.
