fair

noun
traveling entertainment event with rides and games
3.7
Pronunciation: / fɛr / (AmE), / feə / (BrE)
a public event with entertainment, rides, games, exhibitions, or sales

Examples

A local fair
After sunset, the lights of the fair made the whole field sparkle with color.
The traveling fair comes to this town every summer, attracting families from all over.
We spent the afternoon at the fair, trying different games and riding the Ferris wheel.
At the church fair, you could buy homemade cakes and second-hand books.
Many people volunteered to set up stalls for the annual charity fair.
The community organized a school fair to raise money for new playground equipment.
Local farmers bring their produce to the spring fair to sell and promote their businesses.
The annual book fair attracts publishers and readers from across the country.
We visited a science fair where students presented their inventions.
