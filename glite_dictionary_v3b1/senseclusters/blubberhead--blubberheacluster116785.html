blubberhead

noun
slang insult
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌbərˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈblʌbəˌhɛd / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who is considered foolish or silly

Examples

Silly blubberhead
Stop acting like a blubberhead and pay attention to the instructions.
He called his friend a blubberhead after she forgot her keys again.
Only a real blubberhead would try to fix the computer by hitting it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blockhead - a stupid or foolish person
  • antonym: genius - a person with exceptional intelligence
