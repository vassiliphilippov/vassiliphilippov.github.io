sunup

noun
time when sun rises
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈsʌnʌp / (BrE)
the time in the morning when the sun first appears above the horizon; sunrise

Examples

At sunup time
By sunup, the field was already bustling with activity.
They began their hike at sunup to avoid the afternoon heat.
We planned to set out as soon as sunup broke the darkness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sunrise - the time in the morning when the sun appears above the horizon
