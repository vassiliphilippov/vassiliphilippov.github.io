sunup

noun
time when sun rises
1.0 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆsÊŒnËŒÊŒp / (AmE), / ËˆsÊŒnÊŒp / (BrE)
the time in the morning when the sun first appears above the horizon; sunrise

Examples

At sunup time
By sunup, the field was already bustling with activity.
They began their hike at sunup to avoid the afternoon heat.
We planned to set out as soon as sunup broke the darkness.

Synonyms

  • sunrise
    1.0 8.28/m
    sunrise is the moment the sun appears and the day begins, while sunup is the everyday name for that first morning light.
  • crack of dawn
    3.2 0.64/m
    crack of dawn describes an especially early time when first light comes, while sunup simply names the moment the sun appears.
