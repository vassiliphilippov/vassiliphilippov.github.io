Birkenstock

noun
casual sandal style
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrkənˌstɑk / (AmE), / ˈbɜːkənstɒk / (BrE)
a sandal or pair of sandals with a contoured cork footbed and thick straps, originally made by the Birkenstock brand

Examples

A pair of Birkenstocks
She slipped on her favorite Birkenstocks before heading out for a walk in the park.
Many people choose Birkenstock sandals for their comfort and support during long days.
He wore a pair of old Birkenstocks with jeans and a T-shirt to the outdoor concert.
