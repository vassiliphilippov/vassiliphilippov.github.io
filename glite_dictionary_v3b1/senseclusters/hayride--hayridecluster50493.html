hayride

noun
wagon ride with hay
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌraɪd / (BrE)
an informal ride in a wagon or truck filled with hay, often as a group activity for fun or celebration

Examples

A hayride with friends
Every fall, the farm hosts a hayride for families to enjoy the changing leaves.
The children laughed and sang songs during the hayride at the harvest festival.
We bundled up in warm clothes for the chilly evening hayride through the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wagon ride - a ride in a wagon, often for recreation
