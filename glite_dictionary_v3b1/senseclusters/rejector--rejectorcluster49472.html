rejector

noun
person or device that rejects
Pronunciation: / rɪˈdʒɛktər / (AmE), / rɪˈdʒɛktə / (BrE)
a person or thing that rejects or refuses something

Examples

As a known rejector of new technology, she prefers traditional methods.
The committee's rejector argued that the proposal lacked sufficient evidence.
