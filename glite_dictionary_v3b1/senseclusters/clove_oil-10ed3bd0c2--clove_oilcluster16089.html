clove oil

noun
essential oil from cloves
Pronunciation: / kloʊv ɔɪl / (AmE), / kləʊv ɔɪl / (BrE)
an essential oil extracted from cloves, used as a flavoring, fragrance, and in medicinal preparations

Examples

Clove oil for toothache
Dentists often use clove oil to relieve toothache due to its soothing properties.
She added a drop of clove oil to the cake batter for extra flavor.
The strong aroma of clove oil makes it popular in perfumes and cosmetics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clove - a spice made from the dried flower bud of a tropical tree
