pcm

sym
monthly rate abbreviation
Pronunciation: / pi si ɛm / (AmE), / piː siː ɛm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'per calendar month', used to show a monthly rate, especially for rent or payments

Examples

$800 pcm
The apartment is available for £800 pcm, not including utilities.
Most rental listings in the city show prices as pcm to make comparisons easier.
The landlord requires a deposit equal to one month's pcm rent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pm - an abbreviation for 'per month', indicating a monthly rate
