ascendence

noun
state of having power or influence
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛndəns / (AmE), / əˈsɛndəns / (BrE)
the state of having power or influence over other people or groups

Examples

Political ascendence
After years of conflict, the political party achieved clear ascendence in the parliament.
She opposed any form of ascendence by one social class over another.
The company’s ascendence in the tech industry was marked by rapid innovation and expansion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ascendancy - the state of being in a position of power or dominance
  • synonym: dominance - power and influence over others
  • antonym: subordination - the state of being under the authority or control of another
