sacramentary

noun
priest's liturgical book
Pronunciation: / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəri / (AmE), / ˌsækrəˈmɛntəri / (BrE)
a liturgical book containing the prayers and rites used by a priest during Christian ceremonies such as the Mass or sacraments
