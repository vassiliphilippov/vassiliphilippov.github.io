color scheme

noun
planned combination of colors
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlər skiːm / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə skiːm / (BrE)
a planned or coordinated combination of colors, especially used for the decoration of a space or an object

Examples

A color scheme plan
Changing the color scheme of your website can make it feel completely new.
The designer created a color scheme that made the office look warm and inviting.
We chose a color scheme of blue and white for the wedding decorations.
Her wardrobe follows a neutral colour scheme that makes mixing and matching easy.
The designer chose a blue and green colour scheme for the living room walls.
We need to decide on a colour scheme before painting the nursery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: color scheme - American spelling of 'colour scheme', planned combination of colors
  • synonym: palette - the range of colors used in a particular context or by a particular artist
