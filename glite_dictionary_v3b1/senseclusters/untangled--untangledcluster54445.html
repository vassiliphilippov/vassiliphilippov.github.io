untangled

adjective
not twisted or tangled
3.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaŋɡəld / (BrE)
not twisted together or caught in a confused mass; free from tangles.

Examples

An untangled mess
His explanation made the complex issue seem untangled and easy to understand.
She brushed her hair until it was completely untangled.
The cables were spread out on the table, finally untangled and organized.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tangled - twisted together in a messy way
