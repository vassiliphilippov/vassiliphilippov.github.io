suckage

noun
removal by suction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsʌkɪdʒ / (BrE)
the action or process of drawing in or removing something by suction, especially by the mouth or a machine.

Examples

Strong suckage
The vacuum cleaner's powerful suckage made it easy to clean the carpet quickly.
Engineers measured the suckage produced by the new pump during the test.
He was surprised by the strong suckage when he tried to drink the thick milkshake through a straw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suction - the act of drawing something in by creating a partial vacuum
