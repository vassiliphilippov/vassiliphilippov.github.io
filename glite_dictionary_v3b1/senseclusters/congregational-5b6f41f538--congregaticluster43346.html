Congregational

adjective
relating to Congregational churches
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑnɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənl̩ / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Congregational churches, which are Protestant Christian churches practicing congregationalist church governance

Examples

Congregational church community
She grew up in a Congregational community known for its democratic spirit.
The Congregational tradition emphasizes the independence of each local church.
The church adopted a Congregational form of governance, giving more power to its members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Episcopal - relating to the Episcopal system of church governance
  • antonym: Presbyterian - relating to Presbyterian churches or their forms of governance
