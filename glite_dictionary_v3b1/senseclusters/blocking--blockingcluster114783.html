blocking

noun
preventing access or contact on social media
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑːk.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈblɒk.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
The act of preventing a user or account on an online platform from contacting, viewing, or interacting with another user's content or profile.

Examples

Account blocking
After the argument, she activated blocking to stop him from sending messages to her account.
The platform's new safety features include easier blocking of abusive users and management of blocked lists.
Many users rely on blocking as a quick way to prevent unknown accounts viewing their posts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ban - to prohibit or forbid a user from using a service or accessing content, often more formal or platform-enforced than blocking
