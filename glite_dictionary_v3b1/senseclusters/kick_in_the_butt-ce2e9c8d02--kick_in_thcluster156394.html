kick in the butt

noun
punishment by kicking the behind
Pronunciation: / kɪk ɪn ðə bʌt / (AmE), / kɪk ɪn ðə bʌt / (BrE)
A punishment or reprimand inflicted by kicking someone in the behind.

Examples

A kick in the butt punishment
After breaking the rules, she received a kick in the butt as punishment.
He joked that what he needed was a good old-fashioned kick in the butt to get moving.
The unruly student got a kick in the butt for disobeying the teacher.
