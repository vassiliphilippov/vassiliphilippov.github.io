Firth of Forth

proper noun
large estuary in eastern Scotland
6.9
Pronunciation: / fɜrθ əv fɔrθ / (AmE), / fɜːθ əv fɔːθ / (BrE)
a large estuary on the east coast of Scotland, forming the mouth of the River Forth and the location of Edinburgh

Examples

The Firth of Forth estuary
Edinburgh is situated on the southern shore of the Firth of Forth.
Many birds migrate to the Firth of Forth each year, making it an important wildlife habitat.
The Firth of Forth is spanned by several iconic bridges, including the famous Forth Bridge.
