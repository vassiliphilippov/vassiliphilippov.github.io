Synonyms

  • obligate
    6.7 4/m
    obligate makes someone legally or morally required to act, while bind often stresses a promise, contract, or duty.
  • oblige
    4.5 3/m
    oblige makes someone do something from duty or circumstances, while bind implies a legal, contractual, or promised requirement.
  • indenture
    8.9 0.4/m
    indenture formally binds someone by contract, often for apprenticeship or service, while bind is a general legal or moral obligation.
