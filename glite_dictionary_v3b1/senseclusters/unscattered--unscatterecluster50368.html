unscattered

adjective
not dispersed
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskætərd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskætəd / (BrE)
not spread out or dispersed; remaining together in one place or group

Examples

The unscattered papers
After the meeting, the documents remained unscattered on the table.
Despite the strong wind, the petals stayed unscattered on the ground.
The children left their toys unscattered in the playroom after cleaning up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gathered - brought together in one place
  • antonym: scattered - spread out or dispersed in different directions
