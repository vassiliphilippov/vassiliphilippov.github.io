rugmaker

noun
person who makes rugs
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌɡˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈrʌɡˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person who makes rugs by hand or with a machine

Examples

The rugmaker creates rugs
The skilled rugmaker spent months creating a beautiful design for the new hotel lobby.
At the market, a local rugmaker explained how she dyes the wool before weaving.
Many families have passed down the art of rugmaker from generation to generation.

  • synonym: weaver - a person who makes fabric by weaving
