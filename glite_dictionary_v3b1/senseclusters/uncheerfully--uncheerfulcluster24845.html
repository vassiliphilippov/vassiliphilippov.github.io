uncheerfully

adverb
without cheerfulness
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃɪrfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃɪəfəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not cheerful or happy

Examples

To uncheerfully do chores
She uncheerfully completed her chores, wishing she could be outside with her friends.
The students uncheerfully listened to the long and boring lecture.
He uncheerfully accepted the news that his vacation had been canceled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy or positive way
  • synonym: gloomily - in a sad or depressed way
