lapidarist

noun
expert in cutting precious stones
8.1
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɪdərɪst / (AmE), / ləˈpɪdərɪst / (BrE)
An expert in precious stones and the art of cutting and engraving them.

Examples

A lapidarist cuts gems
As a skilled lapidarist, she is highly sought after by jewelry designers.
The lapidarist carefully examined the uncut ruby before beginning his work.
The museum invited a renowned lapidarist to teach a workshop on gem cutting techniques.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lapidary - a person who cuts, polishes, or engraves gems
