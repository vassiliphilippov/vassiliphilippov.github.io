sleaziness

noun
shabbiness or lack of elegance
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːzinɪs / (AmE), / ˈsliːzinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being shabby, dirty, or lacking in elegance, especially due to worn or untidy clothing

Examples

Sleaziness of the place
Despite the sleaziness of the venue, the party was lively and fun.
She was determined to overcome the sleaziness of her surroundings and make a better life.
The sleaziness of his appearance put off many potential employers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegance - the quality of being stylish and graceful
  • synonym: shabbiness - the condition of being untidy or worn out
