Neurospora

noun
genus of fungi used in genetics research
Pronunciation: / nʊˈroʊspərə /; / ˌnʊroʊˈspɔrə / (AmE), / njʊəˈrɒspərə /; / ˌnjʊərəˈspɔːrə / (BrE)
