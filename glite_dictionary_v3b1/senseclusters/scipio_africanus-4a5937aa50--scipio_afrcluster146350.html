Scipio Africanus

proper noun
famed Roman general of Second Punic War
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪpi.oʊ ˌæfrɪˈkeɪnəs /; / ˈsɪpioʊ ˌæf.rɪˈkeɪ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈskɪpɪəʊ ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnəs /; / ˈsɪpɪəʊ ˌæf.rɪˈkɑː.nəs / (BrE)
a prominent Roman general (circa 237–183 BC) who led the invasion of Carthage and defeated Hannibal at the Battle of Zama in the Second Punic War
