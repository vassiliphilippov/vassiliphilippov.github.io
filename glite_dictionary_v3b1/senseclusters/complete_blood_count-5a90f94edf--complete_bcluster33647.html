complete blood count

noun
medical test measuring blood cell counts
6.7
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplit blʌd kaʊnt / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːt blʌd kaʊnt / (BrE)
a medical test that measures the numbers of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets in a sample of blood

Examples

A complete blood count test

The doctor ordered a complete blood count to check for signs of infection.
