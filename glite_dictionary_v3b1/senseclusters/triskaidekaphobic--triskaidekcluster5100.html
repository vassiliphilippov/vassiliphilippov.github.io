triskaidekaphobic

adjective
abnormally afraid of the number 13
7.1 9.9
Pronunciation: /trɪ.skaɪ.deɪ.kə.ˈfoʊ.bɪk/ (AmE), /trɪ.skaɪ.deɪ.kə.ˈfəʊ.bɪk/ (BrE)
Having or showing an abnormal fear of the number 13.

Examples

Triskaidekaphobic fear
As a triskaidekaphobic, he refused to live in an apartment on the 13th floor.
She laughed nervously, admitting that her triskaidekaphobic tendencies made Friday the 13th especially stressful.
The hotel catered to triskaidekaphobic guests by omitting a 13th room from every floor.
