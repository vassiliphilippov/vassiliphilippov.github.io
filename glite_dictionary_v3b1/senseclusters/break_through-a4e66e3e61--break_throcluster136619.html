break through

verb
to penetrate an obstacle
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /breɪk θruː/ /; / /breɪk θrʌf/ (AmE), /breɪk θruː/ /; / /breɪk θrʌf/ (BrE)
to force a way through or emerge from a barrier or obstacle, often suddenly

Examples
