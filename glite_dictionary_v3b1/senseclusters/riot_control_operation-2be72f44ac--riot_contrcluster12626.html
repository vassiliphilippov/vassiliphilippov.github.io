riot control operation

noun
action to manage or suppress a riot
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪət kənˈtroʊl ˌɑpəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈraɪət kənˈtrəʊl ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
an organized set of actions taken by authorities to manage and suppress a riot, usually involving law enforcement or military intervention

Examples

A riot control operation plan
Authorities planned a large-scale riot control operation to ensure public safety during the protest.
Many civilians were injured during the riot control operation last night.
The police launched a riot control operation after violence erupted in the city center.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riot control - measures or equipment used to contain or disperse riots
