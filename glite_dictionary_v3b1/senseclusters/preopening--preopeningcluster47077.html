preopening

adjective
before official opening
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈoʊpənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈəʊpənɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or arranged before an official opening or start of something, especially a business or event

Examples

Preopening event
The restaurant held a preopening event for friends and family before welcoming the public.
A preopening inspection ensured that all safety standards were met before the store launched.
They offered special discounts during the preopening sale to attract early customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postopening - happening after an official opening or start
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before the main event or activity
