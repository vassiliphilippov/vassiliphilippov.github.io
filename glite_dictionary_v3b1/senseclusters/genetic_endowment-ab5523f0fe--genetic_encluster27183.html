genetic endowment

noun
total inherited genetic traits
3.8
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛtɪk ɪnˈdaʊmənt / (AmE), / dʒɪˈnɛtɪk ɪnˈdaʊmənt / (BrE)
the total of inherited genetic traits or attributes that an individual receives from their parents

Examples

The genetic endowment of traits
Advances in science allow us to study the genetic endowment of various species.
Her athletic ability is partly due to her strong genetic endowment.
The twins share a similar genetic endowment but have different personalities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heredity - the passing of traits from parents to offspring
