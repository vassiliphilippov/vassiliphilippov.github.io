cracksman

noun
safe-breaking thief (usually male)
Pronunciation: / ˈkræksmən / (AmE), / ˈkraksmən / (BrE)
A thief, especially a man, who breaks open safes or strongboxes to steal their contents.

Examples

A cracksman opens safes
He boasted of being the best cracksman in the city, able to open any safe without a sound.
In Victorian novels, a cracksman was often portrayed as a cunning and skilled criminal.
The notorious cracksman eluded police for years before finally being caught during a heist.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burglar - a person who illegally enters buildings to steal
  • synonym: safecracker - someone who breaks open safes
