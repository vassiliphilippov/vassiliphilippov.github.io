crimson-yellow

adjective
yellow tinged with crimson
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmzən ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmzn ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
A shade of yellow that is tinged with or overlaid by crimson, giving it a reddish-yellow appearance.

Examples

Crimson-yellow color
As the sun set, the sky glowed with a beautiful crimson-yellow hue.
She chose a crimson-yellow fabric for the curtains to bring warmth into the living room.
The walls were painted a soft crimson-yellow that brightened the entire room.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: crimson - a strong, bright, deep red color
  • degree lower: yellow - the color between green and orange in the spectrum; the color of ripe lemons
