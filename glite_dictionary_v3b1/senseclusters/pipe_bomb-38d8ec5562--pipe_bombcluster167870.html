pipe bomb

noun
homemade bomb inside a pipe
Pronunciation: / paɪp bɑm / (AmE), / paɪp bɒm / (BrE)
A homemade explosive device contained within a metal pipe, often constructed to cause damage or injury.

Examples

A pipe bomb device
He was arrested for attempting to construct a pipe bomb in his garage.
Security officials issued a warning after the discovery of a pipe bomb near the station.
The police discovered a pipe bomb hidden under the vehicle.

  • synonym: improvised explosive device - homemade bomb constructed and deployed in unconventional ways
