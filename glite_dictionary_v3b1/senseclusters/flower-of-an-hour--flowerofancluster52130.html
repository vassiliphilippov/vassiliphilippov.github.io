flower-of-an-hour

noun
a tropical annual with short-lived flowers
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌflaʊɚ əv ən ˈaʊɚ / (AmE), / ˌflaʊə əv ən ˈaʊə / (BrE)
A small annual herb native to the Old World tropics, known for its yellow, purple-centered flowers that last for a very short time; often found as a weed in North America.
