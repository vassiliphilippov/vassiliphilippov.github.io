curette

noun
surgical instrument for removing tissue
6.6 7.4
Pronunciation: / kjʊˈrɛt / (AmE), / kjʊəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a surgical instrument with a scoop-shaped end, used for scraping or removing tissue from a body cavity

Examples

A curette tool
Dentists sometimes employ a curette to clean the surfaces of the teeth or gums.

The surgeon used a curette to remove tissue for a biopsy.
