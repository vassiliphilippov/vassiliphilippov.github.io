ridgetop

noun
crest of a ridge
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒˌtɒp / (BrE)
the highest part or crest of a ridge, hill, or mountain

Examples

A ridgetop view
We set up our tent on the ridgetop to get the best view of the valley.
Hiking along the ridgetop offered a cool breeze and stunning scenery.
From the ridgetop, you could see for miles in every direction.
We watched the sunset from the ridgetop after a long day of hiking.
Trees on the ridgetop were bent by strong winds throughout the year.
The trail follows the ridgetop for several miles, offering stunning views of the valley below.

Related Terms

  • synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
  • antonym: valley - a low area between hills or mountains
