leatherworker

noun
person who works leather
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛðərˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈlɛðəˌwɜːkə / (BrE)
a person who makes, repairs, or crafts items from leather

Examples

A leatherworker crafts goods
The leatherworker crafted a beautiful handmade belt for the customer.
A skilled leatherworker can create wallets, bags, and shoes from raw materials.
The museum displayed tools once used by a famous leatherworker in the 1800s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled manual worker who makes things by hand
