transparentness

noun
the quality of being transparent or clear
Pronunciation: / trænˈspɛr.ənt.nəs / (AmE), / trænˈspær.ənt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being transparent; clarity that allows light to pass through freely

Examples

The transparentness of glass
Scientists measured the transparentness of the newly developed material in various lighting conditions.
She admired the transparentness of the crystal vase on the dining table.
The transparentness of the water made it easy to see the fish beneath the surface.
