tacamahac

noun
North American poplar tree
Pronunciation: / ˌtækəməˈhæk / (AmE), / ˌtækəməˈhæk / (BrE)
A poplar tree of northeastern North America with broad heart-shaped leaves, especially the balsam poplar.
