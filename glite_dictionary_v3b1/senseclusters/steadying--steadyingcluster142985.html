steadying

adjective
making steadier or more stable
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛdiɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɛdiɪŋ / (BrE)
making someone or something become steadier or more stable; having a calming, stabilizing effect.

Examples

A steadying hand
He placed a steadying hand on her shoulder as she stood up.
Her calm voice had a steadying influence during the crisis.
The steadying presence of their coach helped the team stay focused.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destabilizing - making something less stable or more unsteady
  • synonym: stabilizing - making something more stable or secure
