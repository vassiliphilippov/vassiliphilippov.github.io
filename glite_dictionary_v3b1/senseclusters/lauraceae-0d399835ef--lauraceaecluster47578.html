Lauraceae

noun
the laurel family of flowering plants
Pronunciation: / lɔːˈreɪsi.iː / (AmE), / lɔːˈreɪsi.iː / (BrE)
a large family of flowering plants, commonly known as the laurel family, which includes trees and shrubs such as the bay tree and avocado

Examples

The Lauraceae family
Avocado is a member of the Lauraceae, which also contains the cinnamon tree.
Botanists study the characteristics of the Lauraceae to classify and identify its species.
The Lauraceae family includes many aromatic trees and shrubs found worldwide.
