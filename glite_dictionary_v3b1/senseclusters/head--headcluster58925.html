head

noun
one domestic animal (livestock)
Pronunciation: / hɛd / (AmE), / hɛd / (BrE)
an individual person or animal, especially when counted as part of a group

Examples

Fifty head of cattle
The farmer sold fifty head of sheep at the market.
The rancher owns over two hundred head of cattle.
They counted every head of livestock before the auction.
The restaurant charges $30 per head for the dinner buffet.
Tickets to the concert are $10 per head, children under five are free.
We counted nearly two hundred heads at the event last night.
