weight

noun
a feeling of mental or emotional burden
Pronunciation: / weɪt / (AmE), / weɪt / (BrE)
a feeling of heavy pressure or burden, especially in a mental or emotional sense

Examples

Weight of worry
After losing his job, he felt a tremendous weight on his shoulders.
Many leaders feel the weight of public expectation every day.
She carried the weight of guilt long after the incident.
