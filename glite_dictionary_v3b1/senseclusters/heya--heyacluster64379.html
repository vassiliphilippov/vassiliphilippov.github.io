heya

interjection
informal greeting
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪə / (AmE), / ˈheɪə / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to say hello to someone in a friendly way

Examples

Heya everyone
When she walked into the room, she smiled and said heya to everyone.
Heya, are you coming to the party tonight?
He waved and greeted his friend with a cheerful heya.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodbye - a word used to express farewell
  • synonym: hello - a common greeting
  • synonym: hi - an informal way to say hello
