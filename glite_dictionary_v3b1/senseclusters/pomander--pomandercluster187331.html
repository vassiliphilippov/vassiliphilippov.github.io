pomander

verb
to scent with aromatics
Pronunciation: / poʊˈmændər / (AmE), / ˈpɒməndə / (BrE)
to perfume or scent something with aromatic substances, especially by using a pomander

Examples

To pomander clothes
They asked her to pomander the room before the guests arrived.
He would pomander his clothes to keep them smelling fresh during the winter.
The hostess pomandered the hallway with orange and clove before the party started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deodorize - to remove or mask unpleasant odors
  • synonym: perfume - to add a pleasant scent to something
