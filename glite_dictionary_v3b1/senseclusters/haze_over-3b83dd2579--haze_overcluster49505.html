haze over

verb
become less clear or visible
Pronunciation: / heɪz ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / heɪz ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
to become or make less clear or less visible, as if covered by a thin mist or cloud

Examples

Windows haze over
As the sun set, the windows began to haze over with condensation.
In the early morning, the mountains haze over under a blanket of mist.
Tears caused her vision to haze over, making it hard to read the letter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear up - to become clear or restore clarity
  • synonym: obscure - to make difficult to see or understand
