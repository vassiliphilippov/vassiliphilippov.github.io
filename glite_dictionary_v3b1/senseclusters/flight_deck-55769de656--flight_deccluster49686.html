flight deck

noun
upper deck runway on aircraft carrier
7.2
Pronunciation: / flaɪt dɛk / (AmE), / flaɪt dɛk / (BrE)
the upper deck of an aircraft carrier, used as a runway for launching and landing aircraft

Examples

The flight deck of carrier
A sudden gust of wind swept across the flight deck, making it dangerous for the crew.
During the storm, aircraft had to be carefully secured to the flight deck to prevent them from rolling.
The jets lined up on the flight deck as the carrier prepared for takeoff operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: runway - a strip of level, usually paved ground on which airplanes take off and land
