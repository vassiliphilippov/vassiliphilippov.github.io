peppermint patty

noun
chocolate-covered peppermint confection
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərˌmɪnt ˈpæti / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəmənt ˈpæti / (BrE)
A sweet confection consisting of a soft, peppermint-flavored filling coated in chocolate, typically in a round, flat shape.
