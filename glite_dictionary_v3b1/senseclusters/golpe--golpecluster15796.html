golpe

noun
illegal government takeover
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊlpeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒlpeɪ / (BrE)
a sudden, illegal seizure of government power, especially by a small group or the military; a coup d'état

Examples

A military golpe
The military launched a golpe that changed the country's leadership overnight.
After the golpe, international organizations condemned the new regime's actions.
Rumors of a possible golpe spread quickly through the capital city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coup - a sudden, violent, and illegal seizure of power from a government
  • antonym: election - a formal and legal process of choosing government leaders
  • degree higher: revolution - a forcible overthrow of a government or social order in favor of a new system
