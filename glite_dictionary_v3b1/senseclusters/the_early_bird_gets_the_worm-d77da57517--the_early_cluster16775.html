the early bird gets the worm

x
acting early brings success or advantage
Pronunciation: / ði ˈɜrli bɝd ɡɛts ðə wɜrm / (AmE), / ði ˈɜːli bɜːd ɡɛts ðə wɜːm / (BrE)
people who act or arrive early are more likely to succeed or gain an advantage

Examples

The early bird gets the worm proverb
