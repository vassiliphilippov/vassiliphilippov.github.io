precipitin

noun
antibody causing precipitation with antigen
Pronunciation: / prɪˈsɪpɪtɪn / (AmE), / prɪˈsɪpɪtɪn / (BrE)
an antibody that forms an insoluble complex and causes its antigen to precipitate out of solution
