argan

noun
plant oil for food or beauty
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrɡən / (AmE), / ˈɑːɡæn / (BrE)
a plant oil obtained from the kernels of the argan tree, used in cooking and cosmetics

Examples

Argan oil benefits
Many people use argan to moisturize their skin and hair.
Chefs in Morocco often cook with argan because of its unique flavor.
The bottle of argan oil lasted her several months despite daily use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coconut oil - oil extracted from coconuts, used in food and beauty products
  • synonym: olive oil - oil made from olives, used in cooking and cosmetics
