rabbit skin

noun
rabbit pelt or hide
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt skɪn / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt skɪn / (BrE)
the pelt or hide of a rabbit, often used in crafts, clothing, or art materials

Examples

A rabbit skin coat
The craftsman used rabbit skin to make a soft lining for the gloves.
Traditional artists sometimes prepare canvases with glue made from rabbit skin.
She bought a piece of rabbit skin for her next sewing project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fur - the thick hair covering the skin of many animals
  • synonym: leather - animal skin that has been treated for use in clothing or crafts
