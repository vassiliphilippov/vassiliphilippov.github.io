dupioni

adjective
silk-like, textured
Pronunciation: / duːˈpjoʊni / (AmE), / djuːˈpɪəni / (BrE)
made of or resembling dupioni silk, characterized by a slightly rough, uneven texture with visible slubs

Examples

Dupioni dress material
The designer selected a dupioni fabric for the dress to add texture and elegance.
Curtains made from dupioni silk bring a subtle shimmer and unique feel to the room.
He admired the dupioni jacket, noting its distinctive slubs and rich color.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shantung - a type of silk fabric with a similar slubbed texture
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, regular surface without slubs
