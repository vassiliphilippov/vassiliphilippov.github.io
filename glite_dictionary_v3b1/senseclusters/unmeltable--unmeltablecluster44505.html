unmeltable

adjective
emotionally unresponsive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmɛltəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɛltəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be softened, moved, or made sympathetic; emotionally unresponsive or unmoved.

Examples

Unmeltable heart
Despite her apology, his anger remained unmeltable, and he refused to speak to her.
The judge's unmeltable expression showed no sign of pity during the sentencing.
Her unmeltable attitude made it difficult for others to connect with her emotionally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - lacking warmth or emotion
  • antonym: sympathetic - showing understanding or compassion
  • synonym: unfeeling - not showing emotion or sympathy
