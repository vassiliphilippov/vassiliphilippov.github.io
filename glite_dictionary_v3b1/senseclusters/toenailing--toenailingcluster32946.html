toenailing

noun
angled nailing method
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌneɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌneɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of joining two pieces of wood by driving a nail at an angle through one into the other

Examples

Toenailing wood pieces
The builder used toenailing to attach the floor joists securely to the frame.
Proper toenailing ensures that the wooden beams stay in place during construction.
He demonstrated toenailing as an effective way to join two boards at a right angle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nailing - the act of fastening with nails
  • antonym: screwing - the act of fastening with screws
