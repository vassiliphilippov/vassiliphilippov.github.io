overlayer

noun
protective covering layer in construction
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌleɪər / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəˌleɪə / (BrE)
a layer added as a protective covering, especially one applied over structural elements such as studs and joists to strengthen a building and provide a base for further finishing

Examples

A overlayer of plywood
Before installing the weatherproofing, a plywood overlayer is often added for extra support.
Removing the old overlayer revealed damage to the original framework.
The new siding was attached directly to the overlayer on the exterior walls.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overlay - a covering laid over something else, often decorative or protective
