repeated

adjective
occurring again and again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpitɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈpiːtɪd / (BrE)
Happening or done again and again.

Examples

A repeated action
Noise complaints were filed after repeated parties at the house.
She ignored his repeated requests for an explanation.
The team made repeated attempts to contact the missing hiker.

Related Terms

  • antonym: occasional - happening from time to time, not frequently
  • synonym: recurring - occurring repeatedly or periodically
