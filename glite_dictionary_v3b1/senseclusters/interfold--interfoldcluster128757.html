interfold

noun
folded sheet for dispensing
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.tər.foʊld/ (AmE), /ˈɪn.tə.fəʊld/ (BrE)
a single sheet, such as a tissue or paper towel, folded and interleaved with others for easy one-at-a-time dispensing

Examples

An interfold tissue
She pulled an interfold from the dispenser, which released the next sheet automatically.
The restroom was stocked with interfolds to make it easy for people to take just one at a time.
Using an interfold helps prevent waste because only one sheet comes out with each pull.
