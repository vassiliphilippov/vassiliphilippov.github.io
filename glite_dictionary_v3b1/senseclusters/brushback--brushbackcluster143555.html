brushback

noun
hair brushed backward
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃˌbæk / (BrE)
a hairstyle in which the hair is brushed backward away from the face and forehead

Examples

Brushback hairstyle
He styled his hair in a classic brushback before the interview.
The brushback look became popular among young men in the 1950s.
After washing her hair, she used a comb to create a neat brushback.

Related Terms
