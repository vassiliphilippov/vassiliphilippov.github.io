unfearful

adjective
not afraid
5.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪrfl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪəfl̩ / (BrE)
not feeling or showing fear; fearless or unafraid

Examples

The unfearful child
The unfearful child approached the barking dog without hesitation.
She remained unfearful even when facing difficult challenges at work.
His unfearful attitude inspired others to try new things without worry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courageous - showing bravery; not easily frightened
  • antonym: fearful - feeling or showing fear
  • synonym: fearless - not afraid of anything; brave
