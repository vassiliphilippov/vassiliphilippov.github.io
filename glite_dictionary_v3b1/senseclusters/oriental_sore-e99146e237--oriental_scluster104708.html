oriental sore

noun
ulcerative skin disease (cutaneous leishmaniasis)
8.3 9.2
Pronunciation: / ɔːr.iˈɛn.təl sɔːr / (AmE), / ˌɔː.riˈen.təl sɔː / (BrE)
A tropical infectious disease caused by certain protozoa, characterized by ulcerative skin lesions; a form of cutaneous leishmaniasis.

Examples

An oriental sore rash
After months of no improvement, the persistent rash was finally identified as an oriental sore.
The doctor diagnosed the ulcer as an oriental sore contracted during travel in the Middle East.
Treatment for oriental sore often depends on the severity and location of the lesions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leishmaniasis - A disease caused by Leishmania parasites, transmitted by sandflies.
