campmaster

noun
military camp organizer
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈkæmpˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person in charge of organizing, managing, or supervising a camp or campsite

Examples

The campmaster manages camp
The campmaster ensured that every tent was set up before nightfall.
As campmaster, she coordinated the distribution of food and equipment to the troops.
During the training exercise, the campmaster managed all camp logistics efficiently.
The campmaster greeted each camper with a smile on the first day of camp.
If you have any questions about the schedule, ask the campmaster for help.
The campmaster organized a talent show for everyone to enjoy on Friday night.
The campmaster greeted new campers and explained the rules of the site.
After a storm, the campmaster checked all the cabins for damage and organized repairs.
Each summer, the campmaster trains staff to keep the campsite clean and safe for visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camp director - the person responsible for managing a camp
  • synonym: caretaker - a person who looks after a building or property
  • degree lower: counselor - a person who supervises and guides campers
  • synonym: groundskeeper - a person who maintains the grounds of a property
  • synonym: quartermaster - an officer responsible for supplies and logistics in the military
