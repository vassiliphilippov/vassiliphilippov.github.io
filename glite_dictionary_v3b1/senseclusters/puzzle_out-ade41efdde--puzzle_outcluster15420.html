puzzle out

verb
find the answer by thinking carefully
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌzəl aʊt / (AmE), / ˈpʌzl aʊt / (BrE)
to find the answer to something by thinking hard about it; to figure out or make sense of something difficult or confusing

Examples

To puzzle out the problem
After thinking for a while, he finally puzzled out how the machine worked.
It took her hours to puzzle out the meaning of the ancient manuscript.
They sat together trying to puzzle out the solution to the difficult riddle.
