account

noun
reason or grounds for something
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt / (BrE)
a reason or consideration that explains or justifies something; grounds

Examples

On no account
On no account should you share your password with others.
She refused to go to the party on his account.
The club was closed on account of the severe weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grounds - basis or justification for something
  • synonym: reason - cause or explanation for something
