shopping bag

noun
virtual container for online purchases
2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈʃɑːp.ɪŋ bæg/ (AmE), /ˈʃɒp.ɪŋ bæg/ (BrE)
(in e-commerce) a virtual container on a retailer's website or app where a shopper places selected items before completing a purchase.

Examples

Online shopping bag
I added the sneakers to my shopping bag before proceeding to checkout on the retailer's website.
She checked the discount code then reviewed the items in her shopping bag and removed one jacket.
The site saved my shopping bag so I could return later and finish the purchase without searching again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basket - chiefly British term for an online container of items chosen for purchase.
  • synonym: shopping cart - a virtual list or container of selected items for purchase on a website; often used interchangeably with 'shopping bag'.
