Thursday

noun
day of the week between Wednesday and Friday
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθɜrzˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈθɜːzdeɪ / (BrE)
The day of the week between Wednesday and Friday, typically considered the fifth day of the week.

Examples

Thursday morning meeting
She plans to start her new job on Thursday next week.
The art class is held every Thursday evening.
We have our weekly meeting every Thursday at noon.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: Friday - the day of the week after Thursday
  • degree lower: Wednesday - the day of the week before Thursday
