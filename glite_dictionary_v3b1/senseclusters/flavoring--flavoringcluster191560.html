flavoring

noun
substance added to give flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈfleɪvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfleɪvərɪŋ / (BrE)
A substance added to food or drink to enhance or change its taste.

Examples

Artificial flavoring
Some candies contain artificial flavoring to achieve their fruity taste.
The chef used a natural flavoring to make the sauce taste better.
Vanilla is a popular flavoring in ice cream and desserts.
Artificial flavouring is often used in processed foods to mimic real fruit tastes.
She prefers to use vanilla extract as a flavouring in her cakes.
The chef added a natural flavouring to enhance the taste of the dessert.

  • synonym: seasoning - a substance added to food to enhance its taste
  • synonym: spice - an aromatic substance used to flavor food
