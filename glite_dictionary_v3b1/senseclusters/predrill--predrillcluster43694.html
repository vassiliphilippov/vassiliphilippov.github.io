predrill

verb
to make a hole first
Pronunciation: / priːˈdrɪl / (AmE), / priːˈdrɪl / (BrE)
to make a hole in a material before performing another operation, such as inserting a screw or bolt

Examples

To predrill a hole
It is important to predrill holes in metal to avoid damaging the material.
The instructions say to predrill before inserting the screws into the wood.
She always predrills holes to make assembly easier and prevent splitting.

Related Terms
