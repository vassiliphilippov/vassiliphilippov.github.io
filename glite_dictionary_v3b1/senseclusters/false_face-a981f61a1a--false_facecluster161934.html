false face

noun
mask worn as a disguise
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔls feɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɔːls feɪs / (BrE)
a mask worn to disguise the face, especially as part of a masquerade costume

Examples

A false face mask
He removed his false face and revealed his true identity.
She wore a beautifully decorated false face to the masquerade ball.
The children made their own false faces for Halloween.
