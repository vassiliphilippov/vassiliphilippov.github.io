family Pseudomonodaceae

noun
bacterial family with significant pathogens
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ˌsjuːdəʊmɒnoʊˈdeɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ˌsjuːdəʊmɒnəˈdiːsaɪ / (BrE)
A family of rod-shaped, Gram-negative bacteria, including species that are important pathogens of plants and animals.
