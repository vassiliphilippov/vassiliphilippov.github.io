deist

noun
follower of deism; believes God created universe but does not intervene
9.1 0.29/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdiËÉªst / (AmE), / ËˆdiËÉªst / (BrE)
A person who believes that God created the universe but does not intervene in its workings, having allowed it to run according to natural laws.

Examples

A deist thinker
A deist argues that the universe operates according to natural laws set by a creator.
Thomas Jefferson is often described as a deist rather than a traditional Christian.
Unlike theists, a deist does not believe in divine intervention in daily affairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of God
  • antonym: theist - a person who believes in the existence of a God who intervenes in the universe
