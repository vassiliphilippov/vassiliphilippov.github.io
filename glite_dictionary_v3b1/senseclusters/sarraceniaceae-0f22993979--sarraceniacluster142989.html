Sarraceniaceae

noun
family of insectivorous pitcher plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / sæɹəˌsiːniˈeɪsiː /; / sæɹəsəˌniːˈeɪsiː / (AmE), / sarəˌsiːnɪˈeɪsiː /; / sarəˌsiːnɪˈiːsiː / (BrE)
a family of insect-eating (carnivorous) plants, known as pitcher plants, native to the Americas and having tubular leaves that trap insects
