perchloric acid

noun
a powerful, corrosive acid (HClO₄)
Pronunciation: / pərˈklɔrɪk ˈæsɪd / (AmE), / pəˈklɒrɪk ˈæsɪd / (BrE)
a strong, highly corrosive acid (HClO₄) and powerful oxidizing agent, used in chemical analysis and manufacturing perchlorates

Examples

Concentrated perchloric acid
Concentrated perchloric acid must be handled with extreme care due to its highly reactive nature.
Spills of perchloric acid can cause dangerous reactions if not cleaned properly.
The laboratory uses perchloric acid to prepare perchlorate salts.

Related Terms
