attired

adjective
dressed in a particular way
Pronunciation: / əˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / əˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
dressed in clothes, especially in a particular or fine way; often used after adverbs or in compound forms.

Examples

Well attired guests
At the gala, guests were attired in elegant evening gowns and tuxedos.
He arrived at the interview smartly attired in a navy suit and tie.
The children were attired in costumes for the school play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clad - wearing or provided with clothing
  • synonym: dressed - wearing clothes; clothed
