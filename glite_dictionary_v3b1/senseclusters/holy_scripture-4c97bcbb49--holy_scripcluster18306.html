Holy Scripture

noun
the Christian Bible; sacred Christian texts
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊli ˈskrɪptʃər / (AmE), / ˈhəʊli ˈskrɪptʃə / (BrE)
the sacred writings of Christianity, comprising the Old and New Testaments of the Bible

Examples

The Holy Scripture
Many churches encourage their members to study the Holy Scripture regularly.
She spent every morning reading passages from the Holy Scripture before breakfast.
The translation of the Holy Scripture into common languages was a turning point in history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bible - the holy book of the Christian religion, consisting of the Old and New Testaments
  • degree lower: scripture - sacred writings of any religion regarded as authoritative
