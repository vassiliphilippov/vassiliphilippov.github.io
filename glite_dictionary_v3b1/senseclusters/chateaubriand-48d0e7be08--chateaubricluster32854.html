Chateaubriand

noun
thick center cut of beef tenderloin
Pronunciation: / ʃætoʊˈbriː.ɑːnd / (AmE), / ˌʃætəʊˈbriː.ɒ̃ / (BrE)
a very thick, center cut of beef tenderloin, often served as a steak for two people

Examples

A Chateaubriand steak
The chef recommended the Chateaubriand for our anniversary dinner, carving it at the table.
The restaurant is famous for its perfectly cooked Chateaubriand.
We decided to share a Chateaubriand because it was large enough for two people.

Related Terms
