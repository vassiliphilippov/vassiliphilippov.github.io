debtor

noun
person who owes money
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛtər / (AmE), / ˈdɛtə / (BrE)
a person or organization that owes money to another, especially as a result of borrowing or credit

Examples

A debtor owes money
If a debtor fails to pay, legal action may be taken by the creditor.
The bank contacted the debtor to remind him of the overdue payment.
The company negotiated new terms with every major debtor on its books.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creditor - a person or organization to whom money is owed
