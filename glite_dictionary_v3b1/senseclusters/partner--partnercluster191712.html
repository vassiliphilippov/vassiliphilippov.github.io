partner

noun
husband, wife, or romantic companion
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtnər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtnə / (BrE)
a person's husband, wife, or long-term companion in a romantic relationship

Examples

Life partner
His partner supported him throughout his illness.
Many people introduce their partner rather than specifying 'husband' or 'wife'.
She arrived at the dinner with her partner of ten years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spouse - a husband or wife
