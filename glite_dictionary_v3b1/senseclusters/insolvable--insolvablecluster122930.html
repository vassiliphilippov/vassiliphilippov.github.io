Examples

Synonyms

  • unsolvable
    5.3 0.45/m

  • unresolvable
    8.2 0.35/m
    unresolvable stresses that an issue cannot be settled or fixed, while insolvable simply says it has no solution.
  • irresolvable
    7.9 0.10/m
    irresolvable sounds more formal and suits legal or long disputes, while insolvable is a general word for no solution.
  • insoluble
    7.5 0.55/m
    insoluble often names problems that seem impossible to deal with, while insolvable focuses on having no possible solution.
  • unsoluble
    9.4 0.00/m
    unsoluble suggests something very hard or nearly impossible to resolve, while insolvable states it cannot be solved at all.
