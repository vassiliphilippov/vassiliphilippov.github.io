primeur

noun
young wine for sale
9.9
Pronunciation: / priːˈmɜr / (AmE), / priːˈmɜː / (BrE)
a wine sold while still young, especially before its official release date

Examples

Young primeur wine
The vineyard released its primeur to buyers before the official launch date.
Wine enthusiasts eagerly tasted the new primeur at the annual festival.
Collectors often purchase primeur wines to secure rare bottles early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: en primeur - the practice of selling wine before it is bottled and released
  • antonym: vintage - wine made from grapes harvested in a particular year
