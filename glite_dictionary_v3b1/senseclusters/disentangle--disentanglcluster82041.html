disentangle

verb
free from entanglement; untangle
8.4 4.3
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ən.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ /; / /dɪs.ɪn.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ (AmE), /dɪs.ən.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ /; / /dɪs.ɪn.ˈtæŋ.gəl/ (BrE)
to free or untangle something that is twisted, knotted, or caught with something else

The rescue team worked quickly to disentangle the whale from the fishing nets.
