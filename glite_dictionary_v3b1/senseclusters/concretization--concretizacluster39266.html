concretization

noun
making with concrete
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːŋkrɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒŋkrɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of applying or forming concrete, especially by pouring or laying it in construction work

Examples

After the concretization of the floor, workers must wait for it to cure before continuing.
