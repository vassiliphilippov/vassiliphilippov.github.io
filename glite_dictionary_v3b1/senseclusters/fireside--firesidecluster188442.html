fireside

noun
home and family life (figurative)
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪər.saɪd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə.saɪd / (BrE)
the home, especially as a place of warmth, comfort, and family life, often symbolized by the side of a fireplace

Examples

Fireside comfort
Defending our firesides became a rallying cry during the invasion.
She longed for the peace and security of her childhood fireside.
The family gathered by the fireside to share stories on winter evenings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hearth - home as symbolized by the area in front of a fireplace
