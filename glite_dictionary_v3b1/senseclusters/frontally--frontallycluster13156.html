frontally

adverb
in, at, or toward the front
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌntəli / (AmE), / ˈfrʌntəli / (BrE)
In, at, or toward the front or a frontal position.

Examples

Approach frontally
The animal approached its prey frontally, without attempting to hide.
The building was damaged frontally when the car crashed into its entrance.
The statue is displayed frontally to highlight its intricate design.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laterally - in, at, or toward the side
  • antonym: posteriorly - toward the rear or back end
