nontarget

noun
unintended recipient
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑnˌtɑrɡɪt / (AmE), / ˈnɒnˌtɑːɡɪt / (BrE)
a person, organism, or thing that is not the intended target of an action, treatment, or attack but may be affected unintentionally

Examples

A nontarget species
The pesticide affected several nontarget species in the ecosystem.
Researchers must ensure that their methods do not harm any nontarget organisms.
The medication was designed for cancer cells but also impacted some nontarget tissues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present but not directly involved
  • antonym: target - the intended recipient of an action or treatment
