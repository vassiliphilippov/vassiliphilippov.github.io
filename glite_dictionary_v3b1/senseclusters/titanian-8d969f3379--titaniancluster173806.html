Titanian

noun
inhabitant of Titan
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈteɪniən / (AmE), / taɪˈteɪniən / (BrE)
a person, creature, or inhabitant of the moon Titan, especially in astronomy or science fiction

Examples

A Titanian explorer
In the novel, a brave Titanian sets out to explore the methane lakes of Saturn's largest moon.
Scientists imagined what life forms a Titanian might develop in such a cold, alien environment.
The movie depicted a Titanian civilization with advanced technology and unique customs.
