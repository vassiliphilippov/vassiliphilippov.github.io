cot

noun
finger-protecting sheath
8.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / kÉ‘t / (AmE), / kÉ’t / (BrE)
A small sheath, usually made of rubber or similar material, worn over a finger to protect it from contamination, injury, or contact.

Examples

Finger cot
A cot can help keep wounds on your finger clean and dry during healing.
He wore a cot while slicing spicy peppers to avoid skin irritation.
The nurse put a cot over her finger before changing the patient's bandage.

Synonyms

  • fingerstall
    9.1 0.00/m
