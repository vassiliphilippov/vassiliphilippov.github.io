injury

noun
violation of rights; legal wrong
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndʒəri / (AmE), / ˈɪndʒəri / (BrE)
A violation of another person's rights or legal interests, typically regarded as a wrong inflicted unjustly.

Examples

Legal injury
Any injury to his reputation through defamation may result in legal action.
The court found that the company had caused an injury to the plaintiff's rights.
They sought compensation for the injury done to their property by the unlawful decision.
