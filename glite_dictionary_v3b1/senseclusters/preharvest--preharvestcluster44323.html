preharvest

adjective
before harvesting
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈhɑrvəst / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈhɑːvɪst / (BrE)
occurring or applied before the harvesting of crops

Examples

Preharvest treatment
Farmers monitor preharvest conditions to ensure a good yield.
Applying preharvest treatments can help protect crops from disease.
Weather forecasts are especially important during the preharvest period.
