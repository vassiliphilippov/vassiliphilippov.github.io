opal glass

noun
milky white translucent or opaque glass
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.pəl ɡlæs / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.pəl ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a milky white glass that is translucent or opaque, often used for decorative objects or lighting

Examples

Opal glass vase
Collectors prize vintage vases crafted from opal glass for their unique appearance.
He chose opal glass panels for the bathroom window to ensure both privacy and light.
The lampshade was made of opal glass, giving the room a soft glow.
