hypopharynx

noun
lower pharynx region
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈfærɪŋks / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊˈfærɪŋks / (BrE)
the lower part of the pharynx, located behind and beside the larynx, leading to the esophagus

Examples

The hypopharynx area
The doctor examined the patient's hypopharynx for signs of infection.
Cancer can sometimes develop in the hypopharynx, making early detection important.
A sore throat may be caused by inflammation in the hypopharynx.

Related Terms

  • antonym: larynx - the voice box, located above the hypopharynx
  • degree higher: pharynx - the muscular tube connecting the mouth and nasal passages to the esophagus
