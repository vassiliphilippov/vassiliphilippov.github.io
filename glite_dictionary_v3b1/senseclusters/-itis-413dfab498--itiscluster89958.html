-itis

x
humorous suffix for obsession
9.3
Pronunciation: / aɪˈtaɪs / (AmE), / aɪˈtaɪtɪs / (BrE)
an informal or humorous suffix added to words to suggest an obsession, craze, or persistent problem, not an actual medical condition

Examples

Travel-itis joke
He claimed to have a serious case of travel -itis after booking three vacations in a month.
Whenever a new trend appears, she jokes about catching the latest -itis.
My constant urge to organize my desk is just my neatness -itis acting up again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: -mania - a suffix indicating an excessive enthusiasm or obsession
