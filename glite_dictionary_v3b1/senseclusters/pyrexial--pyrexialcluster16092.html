pyrexial

adjective
having a fever
Pronunciation: / paɪˈrɛksiəl / (AmE), / paɪˈrɛksiəl / (BrE)
having or relating to fever or an abnormally high body temperature

Examples

A pyrexial patient
The doctor noted that the child was pyrexial and needed further tests.
After the surgery, the patient became pyrexial and was monitored closely.
A pyrexial response can indicate an underlying infection in the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: afebrile - not having a fever
  • synonym: febrile - having or showing symptoms of a fever
