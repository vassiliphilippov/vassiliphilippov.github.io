Examples

Synonyms

  • dawn
    1.0 19/m
    dawn is the first pale light in the sky before sunrise, while first light is the very first morning light you see.
  • daybreak
    1.0 1.53/m
    daybreak is the moment when morning light begins across the land, while first light names that exact first glow.
  • dawning
    3.7 0.63/m
    dawning is the gradual arrival of morning light and color, while first light refers to the single first flash of daylight.
  • break of day
    1.0 0.16/m
    break of day is an older, poetic phrase for the early morning light, while first light is the common, neutral term.
  • sunrise
    1.0 8.28/m
    sunrise is when the sun itself appears above the horizon, while first light is the earlier glow before the sun shows.
  • sunup
    1.0 0.51/m
    sunup is an informal way to say the sun's rise in casual speech, while first light names the earliest morning glow.
