frag

noun
video game kill
Pronunciation: / fræɡ / (AmE), / fræɡ / (BrE)
a kill or elimination of an opponent, especially in a video game or competitive match

Examples

A frag in a game
He scored five frags in the first round of the match.
Getting the final frag secured his team the victory.
She celebrated her first frag after hours of practice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: death - the state of being killed or eliminated in a game
  • synonym: kill - an act of defeating or eliminating an opponent
