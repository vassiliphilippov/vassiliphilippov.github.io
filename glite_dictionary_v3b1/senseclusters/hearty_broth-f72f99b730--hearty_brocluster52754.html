hearty broth

noun
a rich, nourishing soup
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrti brɔθ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːti brɒθ / (BrE)
a rich, nourishing, and substantial soup, often made with meat, vegetables, or grains

Examples

A hearty broth bowl
On a cold winter evening, nothing is more comforting than a bowl of hearty broth.
She simmered vegetables and beef bones for hours to create a flavorful hearty broth.
The restaurant is famous for its hearty broth served with freshly baked bread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broth - a clear soup made by boiling meat, fish, or vegetables
  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
