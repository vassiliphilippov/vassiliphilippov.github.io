supersonic wave

noun
wave moving faster than sound
1.0 5.1
Pronunciation: /suː.pər.ˈsɑː.nɪk weɪv/ /; / /suː.pər.ˈsɔː.nɪk weɪv/ (AmE), /suː.pə.ˈsɒn.ɪk weɪv/ (BrE)
A wave that travels at a speed faster than the speed of sound in a given medium.
