cut-up

noun
someone who plays practical jokes
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌtˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈkʌtˌʌp / (BrE)
a person who enjoys playing practical jokes or being playful and mischievous, especially to make others laugh

Examples

A cut-up person
At the office party, Jake proved he was a real cut-up with his hilarious jokes.
Even as an adult, Sue remained a cut-up, forever pulling pranks on her friends.
He was always known as a cut-up in high school, constantly making his classmates laugh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joker - a person who jokes or makes fun
  • synonym: prankster - someone who plays tricks or practical jokes
  • antonym: spoilsport - a person who spoils the fun of others
