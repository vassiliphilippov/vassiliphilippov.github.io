tache

noun
informal for moustache
Pronunciation: / tæʃ / (AmE), / tæʃ / (BrE)
a moustache, especially used in British English as an informal term

Examples

A thin tache
He grew a thin tache to look more mature for the role.
In the photo, her uncle sports a large, bushy tache that covers his upper lip.
Some people think a tache adds character to a person's face.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean-shaven - having no facial hair
  • synonym: moustache - hair grown on the upper lip
