luthier

noun
maker of stringed instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈluːtiər / (AmE), / ˈluːtɪə / (BrE)
A maker or repairer of stringed instruments such as lutes, guitars, or violins.

Examples

A luthier repairs guitars
Many musicians consult a trusted luthier to maintain their instruments.
She apprenticed with a luthier to learn the art of guitar making.
The famous luthier spent months crafting each violin by hand.
