unnecessarily

adverb
in an unnecessary manner
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.nəˈsɛs.əˌrɪli /; / ˌʌnˈnɛsəˌsɛrɪli / (AmE), / ˌʌn.nɪˈsɛs.ər.ɪli /; / ˌʌnˈnɛsəsərəli / (BrE)
in a way that is not needed or required; without good reason

Examples

Act unnecessarily slowly
Don't make the process more difficult unnecessarily.
She became upset when he unnecessarily criticized her efforts.
The road was unnecessarily blocked for hours after the event.
Please don't worry unnecessarily; everything is going to be fine.
She apologized unnecessarily because no one was offended by her comment.
The company spent unnecessarily on luxury furniture for the office.

Related Terms

  • antonym: necessarily - in an essential or unavoidable way
  • synonym: needlessly - without necessity
