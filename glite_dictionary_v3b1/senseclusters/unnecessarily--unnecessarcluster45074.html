Examples

Act unnecessarily slowly
Don't make the process more difficult unnecessarily.

Synonyms

  • needlessly
    3.9 2.22/m
    needlessly describes actions done without any need or purpose, while unnecessarily highlights lack of good reason or requirement.
  • unduly
    6.9 2.35/m
    unduly suggests something happens to an excessive or unfair degree, while unnecessarily simply means it is not needed.
  • superfluously
    8.1 0.03/m
    superfluously means more than is needed and adds excess detail or amount, while unnecessarily means it is not required.
  • unjustifiably
    7.0 0.12/m
    unjustifiably means done without any good defence or excuse, while unnecessarily focuses on lack of need rather than argument.
  • unwarrantably
    9.9 0.00/m
    unwarrantably is used for actions lacking reasonable grounds or permission, while unnecessarily points to something not needed.
  • gratuitously
    7.9 0.72/m
    gratuitously often means given or done without good reason, often harming or adding offence, while unnecessarily only means not needed.
