Übermensch

noun
exceptional person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈuːbɚˌmɛnʃ / (AmE), / ˈyːbɐˌmɛnʃ / (BrE)
an exceptionally talented, powerful, or impressive person, often seen as surpassing ordinary human limits

Examples

A true Übermensch
After the concert, everyone called him an Übermensch for his flawless piano performance.
The scientist was regarded as an Übermensch because of her groundbreaking discoveries.
Some people view great leaders in history as a kind of Übermensch who changed the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: genius - a person with exceptional intellectual or creative power
  • antonym: ordinary person - a person without exceptional abilities
  • synonym: superman - a person with extraordinary powers or abilities
