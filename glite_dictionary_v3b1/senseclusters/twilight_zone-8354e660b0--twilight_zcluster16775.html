twilight zone

noun
ocean layer with faint light
6.9 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈtwaɪ.laɪt zoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈtwaɪ.laɪt zəʊn/ (BrE)
the layer of the ocean between the surface and the deep ocean, at depths where only faint light reaches
