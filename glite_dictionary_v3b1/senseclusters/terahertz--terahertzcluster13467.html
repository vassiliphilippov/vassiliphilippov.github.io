terahertz

adjective
of or relating to the THz frequency range
8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈter.ə.hɜːrts/ (AmE), /ˈter.ə.hɜːts/ (BrE)
Relating to, using, or operating in the terahertz region of the electromagnetic spectrum (about 0.1–10 THz).

Examples

Terahertz imaging device
Researchers developed a terahertz camera that can reveal skin tissue differences without radiation exposure.
Car manufacturers are testing terahertz sensors to detect tiny cracks in composite panels during assembly.
Universities now offer courses on terahertz spectroscopy for students interested in ultrafast materials research.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: gigahertz - a unit of frequency equal to one billion hertz; a lower frequency band than terahertz
  • degree lower: hertz - the base unit of frequency, one cycle per second
