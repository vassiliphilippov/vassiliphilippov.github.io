freakiness

noun
unusual behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːkinəs / (AmE), / ˈfriːkinəs / (BrE)
unusual or unconventional behavior, especially relating to sexuality or personal expression, that is considered strange or out of the ordinary

Examples

His freakiness at parties
His freakiness made him stand out at every party he attended.
Some people embrace their freakiness as a way to express their true selves.
They joked about the freakiness that kept their relationship exciting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eccentricity - unusual or odd behavior
  • antonym: normality - the state of being usual or expected
