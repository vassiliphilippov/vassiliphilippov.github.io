homage

noun
respectful tribute or honor
2.5 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑː.mɪdʒ/ /; / /ˈɑː.mɪdʒ/ /; / /oʊ.ˈmɑːʒ/ (AmE), /ˈhɒ.mɪdʒ/ /; / /ə.ˈmɑːʒ/ (BrE)
