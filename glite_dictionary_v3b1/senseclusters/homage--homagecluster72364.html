homage

noun
respectful tribute or honor
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.mɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhɒ.mɪdʒ / (BrE)
respect or honor shown publicly to someone or something, especially in a formal or ceremonial way.

Examples

Pay homage to a leader
Guests offered homage to the retiring professor during the farewell ceremony.
Many films today pay homage to classics from decades past.
The artist's new painting is a clear homage to the Impressionist masters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disrespect - lack of respect or courtesy
  • synonym: tribute - an act, statement, or gift intended to show gratitude, respect, or admiration
