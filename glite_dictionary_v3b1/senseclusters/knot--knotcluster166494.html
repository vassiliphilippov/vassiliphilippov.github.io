knot

verb
to tangle or complicate
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɑt / (AmE), / nɒt / (BrE)
to tangle or complicate, making something more involved or difficult to unravel

Examples

To knot your thoughts
Fears and doubts can knot the mind, making decisions seem impossible.
She tried to explain, but her words only knotted the situation further.
The report's logic became so knotted that even the experts struggled to follow it.
