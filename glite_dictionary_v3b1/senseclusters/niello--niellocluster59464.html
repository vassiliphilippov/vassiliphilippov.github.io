niello

noun
black inlay alloy
9.9
Pronunciation: / niˈɛloʊ / (AmE), / niˈɛləʊ / (BrE)
a black metallic alloy used to fill engraved designs on metal, or the decorative inlay created by this process

Examples

The jeweler used niello to highlight the fine lines engraved on the silver pendant.
Many antique Russian icons feature niello work to create striking black-and-silver patterns.
Collectors admire the craftsmanship of boxes decorated with niello inlays from the 18th century.
The ancient ring was decorated with niello that contrasted beautifully with the silver.
Artisans used niello to highlight the intricate patterns engraved on the sword's hilt.
A collection of medieval chalices with niello inlay was displayed at the exhibition.
