niello

noun
black inlay alloy
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.e.loʊ/ /; / /ˈnɪ.e.loʊ/ (AmE), /ˈniː.e.ləʊ/ /; / /ˈnɪ.e.ləʊ/ (BrE)
