paned

adjective
with glass panels
4.5
Pronunciation: / peɪnd / (AmE), / peɪnd / (BrE)
having sections or panels of glass, especially in windows or doors

Examples

Paned windows
The living room featured large paned windows that let in plenty of sunlight.
They admired the old paned door with its intricate wooden frame.
Many historic buildings have paned glass to preserve their original appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: multi-pane - having more than one panel of glass
  • antonym: solid - not having any glass panels or sections
