mid-Atlantic

adjective
of the US region from New York to Virginia
1.0
Pronunciation: / mɪd ətˈlæntɪk / (AmE), / mɪd ətˈlæntɪk / (BrE)
relating to the region of the United States that includes (variously) New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Maryland, and Virginia
