lookie

noun
informal onlooker
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊki / (AmE), / ˈlʊki / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who looks at or watches something, especially as a bystander or onlooker

Examples

A curious lookie
A group of curious lookies gathered around the accident scene, trying to see what had happened.
The security guard asked the lookie to step back from the construction site for safety reasons.
At the parade, every lookie tried to get the best view of the colorful floats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event but does not take part
  • antonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
  • synonym: spectator - a person who watches an event or activity
