that

pronoun
relative pronoun
1.0
Pronunciation: /ðæt/ /; / /ðət/ (AmE), /ðæt/ /; / /ðət/ (BrE)
a relative pronoun used to introduce a clause giving essential information about a noun or noun phrase mentioned earlier.

Examples

The book that won
The book that you lent me was fascinating and full of surprises.
She wore the dress that her grandmother had made many years ago.
This is the restaurant that everyone has been talking about lately.
