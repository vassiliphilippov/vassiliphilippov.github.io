exoskeletal

adjective
external structural framework
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksoʊˈskɛlətl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɛksəʊˈskɛlɪtəl / (BrE)
having or relating to an external skeleton or structural framework that supports or protects something (such as an animal, device, or building)

Examples

Exoskeletal structure
The robot's exoskeletal design allows it to carry heavy loads with minimal internal structure.

The crab's exoskeletal shell provides protection from predators and harsh environments.
