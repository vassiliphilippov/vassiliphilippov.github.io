cisgender

noun
person whose gender matches birth sex
6.8
Pronunciation: /sɪs.ˈdʒen.dər/ (AmE), /sɪs.ˈdʒen.də/ (BrE)
a person whose gender identity matches the sex they were assigned at birth

Examples

A cisgender person
The survey compared the experiences of cisgender and transgender participants in the workplace.
As a cisgender person, he recognized his experiences were different from those of his transgender friends.
Many policies are designed with cisgender individuals in mind, sometimes overlooking the needs of others.
