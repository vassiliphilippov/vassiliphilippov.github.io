frigid

adjective
showing little sexual desire
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (AmE), / ˈfrɪdʒɪd / (BrE)
(of a person, especially a woman) showing or feeling little or no sexual desire or response.

Examples

Frigid partner
Calling someone frigid is now seen as outdated and insensitive.
Despite his affection, she remained frigid and distant during their relationship.
The novel depicts a frigid character who seems incapable of intimacy.
