fish house

noun
building for fish processing
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃ haʊs / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃ haʊs / (BrE)
a building where fish are cleaned, processed, stored, or sold

Examples

A busy fish house
The fishermen delivered their catch to the fish house early in the morning.
Workers at the fish house prepared the seafood for local restaurants.
The old fish house by the docks is now a popular tourist attraction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish market - a place where fish and seafood are sold
  • synonym: warehouse - a large building for storing goods
