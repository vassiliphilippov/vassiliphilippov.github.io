irised

adjective
rainbow-like colors
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪst / (AmE), / ˈaɪrɪst / (BrE)
having colors that shimmer or change like those of a rainbow

Examples

Irised colors
The soap bubble appeared irised in the sunlight, reflecting many shifting colors.
She admired the irised surface of the beetle's shell as it glimmered in the garden.
The artist painted an irised sky to capture the magical effect of the morning light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iridescent - showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles
