blockout

noun
opaque mask or cover
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑːk.aʊt/ /; / /ˈblɔːk.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈblɒk.aʊt/ (BrE)
an opaque mask, cover, or painted area used to prevent exposure or printing in a specific area in photography, printing, or design

Examples

Ink blockout
The designer applied a blockout to prevent ink from reaching certain areas of the poster.
A black blockout was used in the darkroom to shield the film from unwanted light.
In screen printing, a blockout helps create sharp edges by covering parts of the screen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mask - a covering used to protect or conceal part of an image
  • synonym: stencil - a template used to block out parts of a surface when applying paint or ink
