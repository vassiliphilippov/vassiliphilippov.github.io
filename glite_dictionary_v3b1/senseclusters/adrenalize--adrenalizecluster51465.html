adrenalize

verb
to excite intensely
8.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈdren.ə.laɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈdren.ə.laɪz/ (BrE)
to cause someone or something to experience a sudden rush of adrenaline, making them highly excited or energized

Examples

To adrenalize the crowd
The thrilling music adrenalized the crowd, making everyone jump and dance with excitement.
A sudden announcement adrenalized the employees, who quickly prepared for the big event.
The action movie adrenalizes viewers with its fast-paced scenes and dramatic stunts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - to make someone less excited or agitated
  • synonym: energize - to give energy or enthusiasm to someone
  • synonym: stimulate - to encourage activity or increased action
