redbrick

adjective
made of red bricks
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌbrɪk / (BrE)
made of or relating to red-colored bricks, especially in reference to buildings constructed with them

Examples

Redbrick building

The city center features many redbrick warehouses that have been converted into offices.
