atherogenic

adjective
promoting artery plaque
9.6
Pronunciation: /ə.θə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /æ.θə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (AmE), /ə.θə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /æ.θə.rə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (BrE)
causing or promoting the formation of fatty deposits in the arteries, increasing the risk of atherosclerosis

Examples

Atherogenic fats
A diet high in trans fats is considered highly atherogenic by many health experts.
Doctors warn that smoking has an atherogenic effect on blood vessels.
Reducing atherogenic foods can help lower your risk of heart disease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antiatherogenic - preventing the formation of fatty deposits in arteries
  • synonym: arteriosclerotic - relating to or causing hardening of the arteries
