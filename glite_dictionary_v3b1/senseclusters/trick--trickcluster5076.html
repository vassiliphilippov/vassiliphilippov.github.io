trick

noun
a magic or deceptive act
Pronunciation: / trɪk / (AmE), / trɪk / (BrE)
a clever or deceptive act or feat, especially one performed as entertainment to create the illusion of magic

Examples

Magic trick show
No matter how closely I watched, the trick remained a mystery to me.
She tried to figure out how the card trick was done.
The magician amazed the crowd with his latest trick.

Related Terms
