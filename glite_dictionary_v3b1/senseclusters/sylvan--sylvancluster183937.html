sylvan

noun
woodland spirit or deity
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvən / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvən / (BrE)
A spirit or deity that inhabits or frequents the woods, especially in mythology or folklore.

Examples

A sylvan spirit
In the story, a sylvan lured travelers deeper into the enchanted forest.
Legends spoke of a sylvan who guarded the ancient grove from intruders.
The poet imagined a sylvan watching silently amid the mossy trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dryad - a tree or forest nymph in Greek mythology
  • synonym: nymph - a mythological spirit of nature imagined as a beautiful maiden
