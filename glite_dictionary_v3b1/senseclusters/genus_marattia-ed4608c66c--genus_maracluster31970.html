genus Marattia

noun
genus of ferns (Marattiaceae) with fused sporangia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs məˈrætiə / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs məˈrætiə / (BrE)
A genus of ferns in the family Marattiaceae, characterized by sporangia fused together in two rows.

Botanists studied the unique sporangial structure of the genus Marattia.
