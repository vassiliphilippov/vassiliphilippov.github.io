overweening

adjective
excessively arrogant or presumptuous
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈwiniŋ /; / ˌoʊvərˈwinɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwiːnɪŋ / (BrE)
having or showing too much pride, ambition, or self-importance; excessively confident or unrestrained
