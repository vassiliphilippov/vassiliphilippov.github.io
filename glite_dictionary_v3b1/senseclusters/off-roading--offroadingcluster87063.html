off-roading

noun
driving vehicles on rough or unpaved terrain
9.3 4.1
Pronunciation: /ɔːf.ˈroʊ.dɪŋ/ /; / /ɑːf.ˈroʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɒf.ˈrəʊ.dɪŋ/ /; / /ɔːf.ˈrəʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity of driving vehicles over rough, unpaved, or natural terrain rather than on standard roads

Examples

Off-roading adventure
Off-roading can be a thrilling adventure, but it requires skill and proper equipment.

Many enthusiasts love off-roading because it lets them explore areas that normal vehicles can't reach.
