arborization

noun
branching pattern
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːrbərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɑːbərəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a branching, tree-like pattern, especially seen in nerve cells or blood vessels

Examples

Neural arborization
The arborization of neurons allows for complex connections in the brain.
Microscopic images revealed the arborization of blood vessels in the tissue sample.
The scientist studied the arborization patterns to understand how nerve cells communicate.
