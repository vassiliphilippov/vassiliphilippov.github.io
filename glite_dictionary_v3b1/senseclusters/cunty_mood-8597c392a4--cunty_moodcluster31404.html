cunty mood

noun
rude or abrasive demeanor (vulgar, offensive)
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌnti muːd / (AmE), / ˈkʌnti muːd / (BrE)
A state of being rude, abrasive, or unpleasant, often in a particularly offensive or spiteful way.

Examples

In a cunty mood
Don't talk to him now; he's in one of his cunty moods again.
My boss showed up in a cunty mood and made everyone's morning miserable.
She was clearly in a cunty mood and snapped at everyone who tried to help.
