pignon

noun
triangular wall section
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / piÀênÀàjo än / (AmE), / piÀênÀàj…ín / (BrE)
the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof, also known as a gable

Examples
