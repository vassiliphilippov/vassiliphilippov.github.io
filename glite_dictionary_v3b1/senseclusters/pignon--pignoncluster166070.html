pignon

noun
triangular wall section
Pronunciation: / piːnˈjoʊn / (AmE), / piːnˈjɒn / (BrE)
the triangular upper part of a wall at the end of a ridged roof, also known as a gable

Examples

Stone pignon on house
The old farmhouse displayed a decorative pignon at the end of its steeply pitched roof.
Architects admired the elegant pignon that crowned the historic building.
The stone pignon was carefully restored to preserve the house's original character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gable - the triangular part of a wall between the edges of a sloping roof
