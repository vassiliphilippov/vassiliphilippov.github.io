unvoted

adjective
not voted on
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvoʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈvəʊtɪd / (BrE)
not having received any votes or not having been subjected to a vote

Examples

An unvoted motion
The motion remained unvoted after the meeting ended early.
Several proposals were still unvoted by the close of the session.
The committee left the issue unvoted until more information was available.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approved - officially accepted or agreed upon
  • synonym: pending - awaiting decision or settlement
