channelize

verb
cause to form a channel
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænəˌlaɪz /; / ˈtʃæn.ə.laɪz /; / ˈtʃænəlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈtʃænəlaɪz /; / ˈtʃæn.ə.laɪz / (BrE)
to direct, guide, or confine something (such as water, traffic, or information) into a channel or along a particular route
