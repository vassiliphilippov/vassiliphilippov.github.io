tangerine

noun
a small, sweet orange citrus fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌtæn.dʒəˈrin / (AmE), / ˌtæn.dʒəˈriːn / (BrE)
a small, sweet citrus fruit with a deep orange, easy-to-peel skin, similar to a mandarin orange
