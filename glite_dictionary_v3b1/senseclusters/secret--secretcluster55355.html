secret

noun
something kept hidden from others
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːkrət /; / ˈsiː.krət / (AmE), / ˈsiːkrɪt /; / ˈsiː.krɪt / (BrE)
a piece of information or fact that is kept hidden from others and not meant to be shared

Examples

A secret
He promised to keep my secret and not tell anyone.
She told her best friend a secret she had never shared with anyone else.
The project is top secret, and only a few people know about it.
He swore to never reveal the secret to anyone outside the group.
The secret to his success is dedication and hard work.
Their grandmother shared her chocolate cake secret with the whole family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mystery - something that is difficult or impossible to understand or explain
  • antonym: revelation - the act of making something known that was secret
