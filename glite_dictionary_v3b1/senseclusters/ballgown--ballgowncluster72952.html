ballgown

noun
formal evening dress
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlˌɡaʊn / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlˌɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, elegant dress worn by women for formal dances or special occasions

Examples

A ballgown dress
She wore a sparkling ballgown to the charity gala and received many compliments.
The designer created a custom ballgown for the actress to wear at the awards ceremony.
Many young women dream of wearing a beautiful ballgown to their first formal dance.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cocktail dress - a semi-formal dress for evening parties
  • synonym: evening gown - a formal dress worn for evening events
