blacklight

noun
ultraviolet lamp
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈblækˌlaɪt / (BrE)
a lamp or light that emits ultraviolet (UV) light, making certain materials glow in the dark

Examples

Blacklight lamp
The club used a blacklight to make the white decorations glow during the party.
Artists sometimes use blacklight to create glowing effects in their paintings.
