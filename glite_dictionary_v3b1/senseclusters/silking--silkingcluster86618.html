silking

noun
corn reproductive stage
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkɪŋ / (BrE)
the stage in the growth of certain plants, especially corn, when silk-like threads appear as part of reproduction

Examples

Silking stage of corn
Farmers monitor the silking of corn to determine the best time for pollination.
Heavy rain during silking can affect the quality of the corn crop.
The field reached the silking stage earlier than usual this season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pollination - the transfer of pollen to enable fertilization in plants
