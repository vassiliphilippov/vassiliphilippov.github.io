Synonyms

  • hearing impairment
    3.9 0.4/m
    hearing impairment is the formal medical term for reduced hearing, while hearing loss is the common everyday name for that problem.
  • deafness
    2.7 1/m
    deafness is often used for complete or long-term inability to hear, while hearing loss covers small or partial problems too.
  • hearing disorder
    3.3 0.02/m
    hearing disorder is a medical phrase for a diagnosed ear condition, while hearing loss describes how much hearing is reduced.
