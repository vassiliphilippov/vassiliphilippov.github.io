animate

adjective
(of a noun) referring to living beings
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ə.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈæn.ɪ.meɪt / (BrE)
(of a noun) denoting a living being or referring to living things, as opposed to nonliving or inanimate objects.

Examples

Animate noun
In Russian, whether a noun is animate or inanimate affects grammar.
Some languages treat collective nouns as animate when they refer to groups of people.
The word 'cat' is considered animate in linguistic classification.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inanimate - (of a noun) denoting something that is not alive
