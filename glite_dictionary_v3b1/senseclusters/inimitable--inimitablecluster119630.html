inimitable

adjective
impossible to imitate or match
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪmɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪmɪtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to imitate; so good or unique as to be unmatched.

Examples

An inimitable style
He cooked with an inimitable flair that no one else could replicate.
Her inimitable sense of humor made every gathering memorable.
The artist's inimitable voice captivated audiences around the world.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imitable - able to be copied or imitated
  • synonym: matchless - so good as to be impossible to match; incomparable
  • synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
