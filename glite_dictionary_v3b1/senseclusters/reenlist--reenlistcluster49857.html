reenlist

verb
to join again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˈlɪst / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˈlɪst / (BrE)
to sign up again as a member of an organization or service after a period away.

Examples

To reenlist with a group
After a year away, he decided to reenlist with the local community theater group.
Many volunteers choose to reenlist for another term after their initial service ends.
She was welcomed back warmly when she chose to reenlist with the environmental organization.
