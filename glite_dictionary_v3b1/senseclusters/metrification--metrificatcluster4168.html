metrification

noun
composition in metrical form
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtrəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of composing text in metrical form, or the arrangement of a composition's metrical structure

Examples

Metrification of poetry
Her study focused on the metrification of classical epics across different cultures.
Some scholars argue that metrification can transform even simple prose into a compelling work of art.
The poet devoted hours to the metrification of his latest piece, ensuring each line followed a precise rhythm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: versification - the art or practice of composing verses
