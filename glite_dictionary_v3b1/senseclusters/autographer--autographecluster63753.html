autographer

noun
automatic recorder
Pronunciation: / ɔˈtɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / ɔˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a device or person that automatically records or writes signatures, data, or information

Examples

Automatic autographer device
The laboratory installed a new autographer to monitor temperature changes throughout the day.
An autographer can save time by automatically logging important data during experiments.
The engineer checked the autographer to ensure it was recording the correct information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scribe - a person who writes or copies documents
