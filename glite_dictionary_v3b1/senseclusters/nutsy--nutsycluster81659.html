nutsy

adjective
mentally unstable
9.4 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆnÊŒtsi / (AmE), / ËˆnÊŒtsi / (BrE)
mentally unstable or behaving in a wildly irrational or uncontrolled way

Examples

Nutsy behavior
After the shocking news, he started acting completely nutsy and wouldn't listen to anyone.
Her nutsy behavior at the party surprised all of her friends.
People thought the inventor was nutsy until his ideas changed the world.
