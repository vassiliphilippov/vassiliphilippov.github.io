pandal

noun
temporary event platform
Pronunciation: / ˈpændɑl / (AmE), / ˈpændɑːl / (BrE)
a temporary decorated structure or tent used for public, religious, or festive events in South Asia

Examples

A decorated pandal
During the festival, a beautifully decorated pandal was set up in the town square.
The chief guest delivered his speech from the main pandal in front of a large audience.
Volunteers worked all night to build the pandal for the upcoming cultural event.
During the festival, a large pandal was set up in the center of the village.
