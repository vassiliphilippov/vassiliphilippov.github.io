stickwater

noun
fish oil byproduct
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˈstɪkˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
the watery byproduct remaining after fish are cooked for oil extraction, containing dissolved nutrients and solids
