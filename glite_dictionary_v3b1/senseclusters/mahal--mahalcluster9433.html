mahal

noun
palace in South Asia
Pronunciation: / məˈhɑl / (AmE), / məˈhɑːl / (BrE)
a palace or large, impressive building, especially in South Asia

Examples

Grand mahal
The royal family lived in a magnificent mahal overlooking the river.
Tourists from around the world visit the famous mahal every year.
The city is known for its historic mahals and beautiful gardens.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hut - a small, simple shelter or dwelling
  • synonym: palace - a large, grand residence, especially for royalty
