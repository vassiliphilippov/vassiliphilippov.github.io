smart

adjective
painfully severe (of physical impact)
Pronunciation: / smɑrt / (AmE), / smɑːt / (BrE)
(of a blow, slap, etc.) painfully severe; causing a sharp physical pain.

Examples

A smart slap
He delivered a smart blow to the punching bag during practice.
The argument ended with a smart kick to the shin.
The child cried after receiving a smart slap on the hand.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle - mild; not harsh or severe
  • synonym: painful - causing pain or suffering
