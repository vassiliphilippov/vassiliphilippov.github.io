Herodian

proper noun
Roman historian
9.9
Pronunciation: / həˈroʊdiən / (AmE), / hɪˈrəʊdiən / (BrE)
a 3rd-century Roman historian who wrote a history of the Roman Empire covering the period after Marcus Aurelius

Examples

Herodian wrote histories
The works of Herodian provide valuable insight into the Roman Empire after Marcus Aurelius.
Many historians reference Herodian when studying the political changes of the 3rd century.
In his writings, Herodian described the challenges faced by Roman emperors during his lifetime.
