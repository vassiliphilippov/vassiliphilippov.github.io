zillionaire

noun
person with countless things
7.4
Pronunciation: /zɪl.jə.ˈner/ (AmE), /zɪl.jə.ˈneə/ (BrE)
an informal or humorous term for a person or organization that has an extremely large number of something, such as followers or possessions, not necessarily money

Examples

Zillionaire of fans
After the viral video, she became a zillionaire of followers overnight.
He owns so many books that he is practically a zillionaire in literature.
The company became a zillionaire in online subscribers within a year.

Related Terms
