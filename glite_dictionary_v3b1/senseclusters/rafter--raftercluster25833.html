rafter

noun
person who travels by raft
Pronunciation: / ˈræf.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈrɑːf.tə / (BrE)
a person who travels on or navigates a raft, especially as a sport or as a means of transport.

Examples

Expert rafter on rapids
As an experienced rafter, she navigated the whitewater rapids with ease.
Several rafters set out early in the morning to enjoy the river's strong currents.
The rafter tied all of his gear securely before launching into the water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boater - someone who travels by boat
