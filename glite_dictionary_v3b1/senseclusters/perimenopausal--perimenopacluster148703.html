perimenopausal

adjective

8.8
Pronunciation: /pə.rɪ.mə.nə.ˈpoʊ.zəl/ /; / /pɪ.rɪ.mə.nə.ˈpoʊ.zəl/ (AmE), /pə.rɪ.mə.nə.ˈpɔː.zəl/ /; / /pɪ.rɪ.mə.nə.ˈpɔː.zəl/ (BrE)
relating to the period just before menopause when symptoms of hormonal changes begin to appear

Examples

Perimenopausal symptoms
Some perimenopausal women notice changes in their menstrual cycles and mood.
Doctors often discuss hormone therapy options with perimenopausal patients experiencing severe symptoms.
Hot flashes and night sweats are common complaints among perimenopausal individuals.
