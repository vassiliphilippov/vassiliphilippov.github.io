peppery rub

noun
spice mix for meat seasoning
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpəri rʌb / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəri rʌb / (BrE)
a mixture of spices, typically featuring black pepper, used to season meat before cooking

Examples

A peppery rub mix

This recipe calls for a peppery rub made from black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
