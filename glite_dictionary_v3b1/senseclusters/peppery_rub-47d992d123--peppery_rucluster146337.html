peppery rub

noun
spice mix for meat seasoning
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpəri rʌb / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəri rʌb / (BrE)
a mixture of spices, typically featuring black pepper, used to season meat before cooking

Examples

A peppery rub mix
Before grilling, he coated the chicken with a peppery rub to enhance the flavor.
The chef recommended applying a generous peppery rub to the ribs before roasting them.
This recipe calls for a peppery rub made from black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dry rub - a blend of spices and herbs applied to food before cooking
