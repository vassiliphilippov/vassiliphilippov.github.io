hoary alison

noun
a tall European annual plant with small white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔri ˈælɪsən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːri ˈælɪsən / (BrE)
a tall European annual plant with grey-green, downy leaves and clusters of small white flowers, sometimes considered a weed and naturalized in North America

Examples

Hoary alison plant
Hoary alison is valued by some for its beauty but disliked by others for being a persistent weed.
Gardeners often struggle to remove hoary alison when it invades their flowerbeds.
The field was overrun with hoary alison, its white blossoms bright against the dull green leaves.
