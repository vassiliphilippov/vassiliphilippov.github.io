quarterly dividend

noun
dividend paid every three months
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɔrtərli dɪˈvɪdənd / (AmE), / ˈkwɔːtəli dɪˈvɪdənd / (BrE)
a dividend paid to shareholders every three months by a corporation

Examples

A quarterly dividend payment
Many companies reward their investors with a quarterly dividend if profits have been strong.
Retirees often prefer stocks that provide a steady quarterly dividend for regular income.
The quarterly dividend was announced at the last board meeting and will be distributed in March.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dividend - a sum of money paid regularly (typically quarterly) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits
