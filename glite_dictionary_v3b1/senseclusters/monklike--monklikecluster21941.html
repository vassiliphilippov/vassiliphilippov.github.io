monklike

adjective
resembling a monk
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌŋkˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmʌŋkˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a monk, especially in lifestyle, appearance, or behavior

Examples

Monklike lifestyle
He adopted a monklike routine, waking early and spending hours in quiet reflection.
Her monklike dedication to her studies impressed all her professors.
The artist led a monklike life, rarely socializing and focusing entirely on his work.
