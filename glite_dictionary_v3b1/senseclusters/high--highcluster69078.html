high

adjective
of considerable height or elevation
Pronunciation: / haɪ / (AmE), / haɪ / (BrE)
at or having a considerable distance from the ground or base to the top; having great elevation or rising far above the ground or level surface

Examples

A high mountain
The high mountain was covered in snow even during the summer months.
The room felt spacious thanks to its high ceilings and large windows.
Their apartment is on a high floor, offering an impressive view of the city.
