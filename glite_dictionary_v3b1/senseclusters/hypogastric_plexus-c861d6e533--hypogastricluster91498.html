hypogastric plexus

noun
nerve network in pelvic region
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈɡæstrɪk ˈplɛksəs / (AmE), / ˌhɪpəˈɡæstrɪk ˈplɛksəs / (BrE)
a network of nerves in the pelvic region, supplying the pelvic organs

Examples

Hypogastric plexus nerves
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the hypogastric plexus.
Pain in the pelvic region may sometimes be caused by irritation of the hypogastric plexus.
The hypogastric plexus transmits nerve signals to the lower digestive tract and reproductive organs.
