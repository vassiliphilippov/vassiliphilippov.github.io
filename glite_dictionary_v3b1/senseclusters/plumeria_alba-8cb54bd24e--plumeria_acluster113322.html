Plumeria alba

noun
a tropical tree with fragrant yellow and white flowers
8.3
Pronunciation: / pluˈmɪəriə ˈælbə / (AmE), / pluːˈmɪərɪə ˈalbə / (BrE)
a tall, sparsely branched tree with large, fragrant yellow flowers with white centers, commonly grown in tropical regions

A Plumeria alba tree
