parallel

adjective
(of lines) equally distant and never meeting
3.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpæ.rə.lel/ /; / /ˈpe.rə.lel/ (AmE), /ˈpæ.rə.lel/ (BrE)
(of lines, planes, or objects) being everywhere the same distance apart and never meeting or intersecting, even if extended indefinitely.

Examples

Parallel lines
In the diagram, all vertical lines are parallel to the base.
The architect designed the buildings so their walls were perfectly parallel.
