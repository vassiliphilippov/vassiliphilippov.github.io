goofily

adverb
in a silly way
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuːfɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡuːfɪli / (BrE)
in a silly, playful, or awkward manner that may seem funny

Examples

To smile goofily
He smiled goofily at the camera, making everyone laugh.
The puppy ran goofily around the yard, chasing its own tail.
She waved goofily to her friends from across the street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable way
  • antonym: seriously - in a serious or earnest manner
  • synonym: sillily - in a silly or foolish manner
