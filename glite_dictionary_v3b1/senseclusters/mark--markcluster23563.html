mark

verb
be a distinctive feature of
3.8
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to be a distinctive feature, attribute, or characteristic of someone or something, often in a notable or positive way

Examples

Talent marks her work
Curiosity marked the early years of her scientific research.
Her attention to detail marks her as an exceptional manager.
Integrity and dedication marked his entire career in public service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: characterize - to be a typical feature or quality of someone or something
  • synonym: distinguish - to recognize or treat as different; to be a distinguishing characteristic
