apothecary

noun
shop selling medicines and herbal remedies
7.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpɑː.θə.kə.riː/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpɒθ.ə.kə.riː/ (BrE)
a shop that sells medicines, herbal remedies, essential oils, and other health or wellness products; often used for small boutiques with a traditional or vintage style.

Examples

A modern apothecary
I stopped by the local apothecary to pick up lavender oil.
The town's old apothecary sells traditional herbs alongside modern over-the-counter medicines.
She works part-time at an apothecary that also offers aromatherapy and natural skincare.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbal shop - a retail shop specialising in herbal remedies, tinctures, and natural health products
  • synonym: pharmacy - a shop or department where medicinal drugs are dispensed and sold
