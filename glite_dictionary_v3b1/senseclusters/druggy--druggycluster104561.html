druggy

adjective
drowsy or stupefied
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdrʌ.gɪ/ (AmE), /ˈdrʌ.gɪ/ (BrE)
feeling or appearing as if affected by drugs, especially drowsy, sluggish, or stupefied.

Examples

Feeling druggy
After the operation she felt unusually druggy and slept for most of the afternoon.
The cold medicine made him feel a bit druggy during the meeting.
She looked druggy after staying up all night and taking her painkillers.
