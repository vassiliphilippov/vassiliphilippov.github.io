enterer

noun
person who enters
Pronunciation: / ˈɛntərər / (AmE), / ˈɛntərə / (BrE)
a person or thing that enters a place, event, or data into a system

Examples

The enterer of data
The enterer of the building must sign in at the front desk.
Each data enterer is responsible for checking the accuracy of their entries.
Only the authorized enterer can access the secure area after hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrant - a person who enters a competition or place
  • antonym: leaver - a person who leaves a place or situation
