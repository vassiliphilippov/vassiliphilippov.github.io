domiciliary

adjective
provided or occurring at home
7.6 6.9
Pronunciation: /doʊ.mɪ.ˈsɪl.ə.riː/ /; / /dɑː.mɪ.ˈsɪl.ə.riː/ (AmE), /dɒ.mɪ.ˈsɪl.ə.riː/ /; / /dɒ.mɪ.ˈsɪl.jə.riː/ (BrE)
