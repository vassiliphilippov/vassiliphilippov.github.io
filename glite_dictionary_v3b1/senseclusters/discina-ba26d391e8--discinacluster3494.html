Discina

noun
a genus of cup fungi
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪnə / (AmE), / dɪˈsiːnə / (BrE)
a genus of cup-shaped fungi, some called false morels, found mainly in temperate regions and belonging to the family Helvellaceae
