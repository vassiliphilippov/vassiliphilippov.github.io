Tamus

noun
genus of tuberous vines in Dioscoreaceae
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪ.məs /; / ˈteɪməs / (AmE), / ˈteɪ.məs /; / ˈteɪməs / (BrE)
a genus of tuberous climbing plants (vines) in the family Dioscoreaceae, with twining stems, heart-shaped leaves, and clusters of small flowers
