equitably

adverb
in a fair and impartial way
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛkwɪtəbli / (AmE), / ˈɛkwɪtəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is fair and impartial; in an equitable manner.

Examples

To treat equitably
Management strives to treat all employees equitably regardless of their background.
The judge ruled that the property must be equitably divided.
The resources should be equitably distributed to ensure everyone has a fair share.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairly - in a fair or just manner
  • antonym: unfairly - in a manner that is not fair or just
