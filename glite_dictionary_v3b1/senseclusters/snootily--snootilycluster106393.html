snootily

adverb
in a snobbish or superior manner

Pronunciation: / ÀàsnuÀêt…™li / (AmE), / ÀàsnuÀêt…™li / (BrE)
In a snobbish or superior manner; showing disdain or aloofness due to a sense of superiority.

Examples

To snootily refuse
He snootily declined the offer, thinking it beneath him to participate.
She looked snootily at the other guests, clearly unimpressed by their attire.
The critics spoke snootily about the new play, dismissing it as unoriginal.
