plate

noun
flat dish for eating or serving food
1.9
Pronunciation: / pleɪt / (AmE), / pleɪt / (BrE)
A flat, usually round dish from which food is eaten or on which food is served.

Examples

Dinner plate
Please put your dirty plate in the sink when you finish eating.
She placed the steaming vegetables on the plate before serving dinner.
They bought a set of matching plates for their new kitchen.
