crash

verb
(of computers) stop working suddenly
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / kræʃ / (AmE), / kræʃ / (BrE)
to suddenly stop working or fail, especially for a computer or system

Examples

The app crashes
After the update, the app began to crash every time I tried to log in.
My laptop tends to crash if I open too many programs at once.
The software will crash unless you install the latest update.
My laptop crashed before I had a chance to save my document.
She was frustrated when her video call suddenly crashed during an important meeting.
The application will crash if you try to open too many files at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - (of a computer) to become unresponsive or stop working properly
  • synonym: hang - (of a computer or software) to stop responding, often without closing
  • synonym: malfunction - (of a device/system) to fail to work properly
  • antonym: restart - to start again after stopping or crashing
