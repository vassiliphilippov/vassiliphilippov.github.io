Synonyms

  • go down
    3.4 6.18/m
    go down is used for services that stop working temporarily, while crash describes a sudden failure often needing a restart.
  • come down
    4.2 0.22/m
    come down often means a website or network becomes unavailable suddenly, while crash highlights an internal software error causing failure.
  • break down
    3.2 8.68/m
    break down describes a machine or system stopping or failing, while crash implies a sudden software or system failure.
  • fail
    2.2 19/m
    fail means a part or system does not work, while crash suggests a sudden complete stoppage often with error messages.
  • collapse
    4.1 28/m
    collapse often describes a physical or business breakdown, while crash is used especially for sudden computer or system failure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeze - (of a computer) to become unresponsive or stop working properly
  • synonym: hang - (of a computer or software) to stop responding, often without closing
  • synonym: malfunction - (of a device/system) to fail to work properly
  • antonym: restart - to start again after stopping or crashing
