whirly

adjective
spinning rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrli / (AmE), / ˈwɜːli / (BrE)
spinning or moving quickly in circles, or causing something to spin rapidly

Examples

Whirly ride at the fair
The children loved the whirly ride that spun them around at the fair.
She watched the whirly leaves dance across the playground in the autumn wind.
The artist created a whirly pattern that looked like a spinning top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinning - turning around quickly in a circle
  • antonym: still - not moving or spinning
  • synonym: twirling - spinning or turning around repeatedly
