Herero language

noun
Bantu language of the Herero people
Pronunciation: / həˈrɛroʊ ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / həˈrɛərəʊ ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A Bantu language spoken by the Herero people in Namibia, Botswana, and Angola.
