Butea monosperma

noun
South Asian tree with bright flowers
Pronunciation: / buːˈtiːə ˌmɒnəˈspɝːmə / (AmE), / bjuːˈtiːə ˌmɒnəˈspɜːmə / (BrE)
A deciduous tree native to South Asia, known for its bright vermilion flowers and its use in producing yellow dye.
