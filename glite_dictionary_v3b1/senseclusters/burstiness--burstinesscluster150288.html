burstiness

noun
sudden irregular bursts
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrstinəs / (AmE), / ˈbɜːstɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of happening in sudden, irregular bursts instead of at a steady or continuous rate

Examples

Burstiness of data
The burstiness of internet traffic can make it difficult to predict network performance.
Scientists studied the burstiness of rainfall patterns during the stormy season.
High burstiness in customer orders can challenge supply chain management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intermittency - the state of occurring at irregular intervals
  • antonym: steadiness - the quality of being regular and continuous
