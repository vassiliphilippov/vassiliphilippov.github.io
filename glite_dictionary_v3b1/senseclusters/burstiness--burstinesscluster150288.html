burstiness

noun
sudden irregular bursts
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɜːr.stɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbɜː.stɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of happening in sudden, irregular bursts instead of at a steady or continuous rate

Examples

Burstiness of data

High burstiness in customer orders can challenge supply chain management.
