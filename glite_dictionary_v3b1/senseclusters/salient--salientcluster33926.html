salient

adjective
(heraldry) leaping or springing figure
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.li.ənt / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.li.ənt / (BrE)
(Heraldry) Depicted as leaping or springing forward, with the front legs raised together and the body tilted forward.

Examples

Salient stag on a shield
On the ancient shield, a stag salient represented swiftness and agility.
The herald described the animal as salient, distinguishing it from rampant or couchant poses.
The lion was depicted salient on the family's crest, symbolizing courage in action.
