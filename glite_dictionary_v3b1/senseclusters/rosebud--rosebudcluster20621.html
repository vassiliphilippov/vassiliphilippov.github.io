rosebud

noun
the unopened flower of a rose
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzˌbʌd / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzˌbʌd / (BrE)
the unopened flower of a rose; the bud stage before the rose opens

Examples

A fresh rosebud
Each rosebud in the garden promised to bloom into a beautiful flower.
She placed a single rosebud in the small glass vase on her desk.
The rosebud slowly opened over the next few days, revealing delicate pink petals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bud - a young, undeveloped or unopened flower or leaf
