contractable

adjective
able to be transmitted by infection
6.4 5.7
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtræk.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtræk.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
(of a disease) capable of being transmitted to others through infection.

Examples

Contractable disease
Hepatitis B is contractable by sharing contaminated needles or exposure to infected blood.
Some diseases are not contractable through casual contact.
The flu is a highly contractable illness, especially during the winter months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contagious - (of disease) able to be spread from one person to another
  • synonym: infectious - (of disease) likely to be transmitted to people, organisms, etc.
