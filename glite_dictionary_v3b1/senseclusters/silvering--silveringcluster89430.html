silvering

noun
mirror-coating process
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of coating a surface, especially glass, with a thin layer of silver to create a reflective finish

Examples

Glass silvering process
The silvering on the antique mirror had faded over the years.
Modern mirrors are made using a chemical silvering process on glass.
He studied the art of silvering to restore old picture frames.
