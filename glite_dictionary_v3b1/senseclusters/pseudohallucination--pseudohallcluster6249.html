pseudohallucination

noun
vivid but recognized-as-unreal perception
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˌhæluːsɪˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˌhæljuːsɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
An image or perception that is as vivid as a hallucination but recognized by the person as unreal.

Examples

Visual pseudohallucination
Some medication side effects may include episodes of pseudohallucination.
The patient reported experiencing pseudohallucinations during periods of intense stress.
Unlike a true hallucination, a pseudohallucination is recognized by the individual as not being real.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hallucination - a perception with no external stimulus believed to be real
