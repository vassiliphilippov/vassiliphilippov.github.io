ladies' man

noun
man popular with or pursuing women
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiz mæn / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz mæn / (BrE)
a man who is very fond of women and is popular with them or actively seeks their company, often implying flirtatiousness or romantic pursuit

Examples

A ladies' man flirts
Despite his reputation as a ladies' man, he was looking for true love.
Everyone knew Tom was a ladies' man who loved the thrill of the chase.
She warned her friend that Mark had always been a ladies' man.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philanderer - a man who frequently enters into romantic or sexual relationships with women, often deceitfully
  • synonym: womanizer - a man who engages in numerous casual sexual affairs with women
