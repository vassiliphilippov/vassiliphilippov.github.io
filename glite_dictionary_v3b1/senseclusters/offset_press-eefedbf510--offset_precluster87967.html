offset press

noun
printing press for offset printing
8.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːf.set pres/ /; / /ɔːf.ˈset pres/ (AmE), /ˈɒf.set pres/ /; / /ɒf.ˈset pres/ (BrE)
