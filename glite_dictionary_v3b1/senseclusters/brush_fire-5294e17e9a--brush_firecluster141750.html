brush fire

noun
uncontrolled fire burning brush or shrubs
6.7
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ ˈfaɪɚ / (AmE), / brʌʃ ˈfaɪə / (BrE)
An uncontrolled fire that burns brush, shrubs, and bushes, typically over a relatively small area compared to forest fires.

Examples

A brush fire spreads
A small spark was enough to ignite a brush fire in the parched landscape.
Firefighters battled the brush fire for hours before finally bringing it under control.
The dry weather increased the risk of a brush fire spreading across the hillside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest fire - an uncontrolled fire in a forest area
  • synonym: wildfire - a large, uncontrolled fire burning in the countryside or wilderness
