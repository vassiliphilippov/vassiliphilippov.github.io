jump cut

noun
abrupt transition between scenes in film/video
9.3
Pronunciation: / dʒʌmp kʌt / (AmE), / dʒʌmp kʌt / (BrE)
an abrupt transition from one scene to another in film or video, creating a discontinuity in the flow of action or time

Examples

A jump cut scene
A jump cut can be jarring if not used intentionally for artistic effect.
The director used a jump cut to create a sense of urgency in the chase scene.
You can notice a jump cut when the actor suddenly changes position between frames.
