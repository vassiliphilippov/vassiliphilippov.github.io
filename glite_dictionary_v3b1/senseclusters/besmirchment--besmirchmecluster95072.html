besmirchment

noun
damage to reputation
9.0
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈsmɜːr.tʃ.mənt/ /; / /bə.ˈsmɜːr.tʃ.mənt/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈsmɜː.tʃ.mənt/ /; / /bə.ˈsmɜː.tʃ.mənt/ (BrE)
the act of damaging someone's reputation or making something dirty or stained

Examples

The besmirchment of reputation
The scandal resulted in the besmirchment of the politician's reputation.
She was upset by the besmirchment of her family's good name in the press.
Years of hard work were undone by the sudden besmirchment caused by false rumors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defamation - the act of harming someone's reputation by making false statements
  • antonym: praise - the act of expressing approval or admiration
  • synonym: slander - the action of making false spoken statements damaging to a person's reputation
