Sierran

adjective
of the Sierra mountains
Pronunciation: / siˈɛrən / (AmE), / siˈɛərən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the Sierra mountain range or its surrounding region

Examples

Sierran landscape
The Sierran climate supports a wide variety of plant and animal life.
Many hikers are drawn to the Sierran trails for their breathtaking views.
The Sierran region is famous for its granite peaks and clear lakes.
