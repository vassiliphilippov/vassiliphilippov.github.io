pairwise

adverb
involving two at a time
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpeəˌwaɪz / (BrE)
involving or relating to two things considered together at a time

Examples

Compared pairwise
The scientists compared the results pairwise to identify any significant differences.
Each item was matched pairwise with every other item in the set.
The algorithm checks the data pairwise to find duplicate entries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collectively - as a group, all together
  • antonym: individually - one at a time, separately
