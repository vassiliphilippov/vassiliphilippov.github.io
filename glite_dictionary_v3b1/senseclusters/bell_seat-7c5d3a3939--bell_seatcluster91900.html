bell seat

noun
seat shaped like a bell (18th-century chairs)
Pronunciation: / bɛl siːt / (AmE), / bɛl siːt / (BrE)
A seat on certain 18th-century chairs that is shaped like a bell.

Examples

An 18th-century bell seat
