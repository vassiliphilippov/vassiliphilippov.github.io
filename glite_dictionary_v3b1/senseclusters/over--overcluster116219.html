Synonyms

  • throughout
    1.0 134/m
    throughout is used for every part of a place or whole period, while over is a more general preposition.
  • all over
    1.0 68/m
    all over shows things spread across every part of a place, while over is shorter and used more generally.
  • around
    1.0 18/m
    around can mean near in space or time and sounds informal, while over means throughout a whole area or period.
