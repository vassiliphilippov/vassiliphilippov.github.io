miffy

adjective
slightly annoyed
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪfi / (AmE), / ˈmɪfi / (BrE)
feeling slightly annoyed or offended, especially in a childish or petty way

Examples

A miffy child
He became miffy when his friends started the game without him.
Don't get miffy just because you lost the last round.
She sounded a bit miffy after being left out of the conversation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritated - feeling annoyed or bothered
  • synonym: petulant - easily annoyed or upset, especially in a childish way
  • antonym: pleased - feeling happy or satisfied
