ring-stalked fungus

noun
genus of brown-spored gill fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪŋ stɔːkt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ˈrɪŋ stɔːkt ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
A genus of gill fungi producing brown spores, closely related to Agaricus, and placed in its own family, Strophariaceae.
