minah

noun
tropical Asian starling bird
7.1 7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.nə/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.nə/ (BrE)
A tropical Asian starling of the genus Acridotheres, known for its loud calls and ability to mimic sounds.

Examples

A minah bird
A minah perched on the telephone wire, repeating the farmer’s laughter with uncanny accuracy.
Many tourists are fascinated by the minah for its remarkable ability to mimic human speech.
The forest was alive with the chattering calls of the local minahs at dawn.
