exert

verb
make a strong effort
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɜrt / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɜːt / (BrE)

Examples

To exert yourself
After running the marathon, she was exhausted from having exerted herself so much.
He exerted all his strength to lift the heavy box.
It is important to exert yourself if you want to achieve your goals.

Related Terms
