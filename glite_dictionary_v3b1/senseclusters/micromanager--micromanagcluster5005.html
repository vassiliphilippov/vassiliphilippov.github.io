micromanager

noun
overcontrolling manager
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.mæn.ə.dʒər/ /; / /maɪ.kroʊ.ˈmæn.ə.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krə.mæn.ə.dʒə/ /; / /maɪ.krə.ˈmæn.ə.dʒə/ (BrE)
a manager who closely observes or controls every small detail of employees' work, often excessively

Examples

A micromanager boss
Working under a micromanager can be stressful because every decision is questioned.
The team felt frustrated by their new micromanager who insisted on approving every minor task.
As a micromanager, he rarely trusted his employees to work independently.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delegator - a manager who assigns tasks and responsibility to others
  • synonym: overseer - a person who supervises others, especially closely
