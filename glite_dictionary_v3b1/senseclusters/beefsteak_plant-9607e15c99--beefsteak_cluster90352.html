beefsteak plant

noun
South American ornamental plant
Pronunciation: / ˈbifˌsteɪk plænt / (AmE), / ˈbiːfsteɪk plɑːnt / (BrE)
A South American ornamental herb with green to purple or red branches, colorful foliage, and spikes of insignificant woolly flowers.

Examples

Colorful beefsteak plant
Gardeners prize the beefsteak plant for its unique color and decorative value.
She planted a beefsteak plant beside the walkway to add a tropical touch.
The beefsteak plant is often grown for its striking purple foliage in garden borders.
