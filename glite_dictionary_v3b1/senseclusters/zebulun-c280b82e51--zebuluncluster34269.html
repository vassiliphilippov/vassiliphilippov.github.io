Zebulun

proper noun
male biblical name
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛbjələn / (AmE), / ˈzɛbjʊlən / (BrE)
a male given name of Hebrew origin, derived from a biblical figure who was one of the twelve sons of Jacob

Examples

The name Zebulun
They named their son Zebulun after the biblical character.
In the story, Zebulun was one of the twelve tribes of Israel.
The name Zebulun is rarely used in modern times but has historical significance.
