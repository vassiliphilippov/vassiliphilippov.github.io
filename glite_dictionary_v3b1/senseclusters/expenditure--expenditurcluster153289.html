Synonyms

  • spending
    1.7 77/m
    spending means the act of using money for goods or services, while expenditure covers both that act and total amounts.
  • outlay
    6.9 2.53/m
    outlay is the money paid for a specific project or purchase, while expenditure means total spending or money used over time.
  • expense
    2.7 36/m
    expense is a cost for a particular purpose like work or travel, while expenditure refers to overall spending or amounts used.
  • outgo
    7.0 0.05/m
    outgo is an older word for money paid out in a period, while expenditure is the general act or total of spending.
