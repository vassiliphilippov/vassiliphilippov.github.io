expenditure

noun
money spent; amount spent
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛndɪtʃɚ /; / ɪkˈspɛndɪtʃər / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛndɪtjə /; / ɪkˈspɛndɪtʃə / (BrE)
the act of spending or the amount of money, time, or energy that is used, especially money
