jihad

noun
Islamic struggle for a holy or moral goal
3.2
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhɑd / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɑːd / (BrE)
(in Islam) a spiritual struggle within oneself against sin, or a holy war undertaken for a religious cause.

Examples

A jihad of faith
He believes that jihad should be focused on self-discipline and charity, not violence.
Many Muslims interpret jihad primarily as an internal struggle to live a good life.
The concept of jihad has played a major role in Islamic history and thought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holy war - a war declared or fought for a religious cause
