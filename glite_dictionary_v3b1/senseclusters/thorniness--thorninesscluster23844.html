thorniness

noun
difficulty or complexity; controversial nature
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈθɔrniˌnɪs / (AmE), / ˈθɔːniˌnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being complicated, difficult, or full of controversial points.

Examples

Thorniness of the issue
He underestimated the thorniness of the negotiations with the labor union.
Her book addresses the thorniness of modern political discourse.
The thorniness of the debate made it difficult to find a consensus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complexity - the state of being complex or hard to analyze
  • synonym: difficulty - state or quality of being hard to do, understand, or deal with
  • antonym: simplicity - the quality of being easy to understand or do
