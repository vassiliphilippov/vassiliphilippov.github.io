wooo

verb
to cheer loudly
9.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / wuÀê / (AmE), / wuÀê / (BrE)
to shout or make a prolonged 'wooo' sound to express excitement, approval, or encouragement, especially at events or performances

Examples

To wooo at a show
The crowd began to wooo as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
Fans woooed their favorite player when he scored the winning goal.
She couldn't help but wooo during the exciting roller coaster ride.

Synonyms

  • woot
    9.9 0.07/m
    woot is a short, loud shout used by groups to show excitement, while wooo is a long drawn cheer at events.
  • ooh
    9.1 0.45/m
    ooh is a soft sound of surprise or pleasure in close moments, while wooo is a loud sustained cheer at events.
