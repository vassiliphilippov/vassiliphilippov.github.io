wooo

verb
to cheer loudly
9.2
Pronunciation: /wuː/ /; / /woʊ/ (AmE), /wuː/ /; / /wɔː/ (BrE)
to shout or make a prolonged 'wooo' sound to express excitement, approval, or encouragement, especially at events or performances

Examples

The crowd began to wooo as the fireworks lit up the night sky.
Fans woooed their favorite player when he scored the winning goal.
She couldn't help but wooo during the exciting roller coaster ride.
