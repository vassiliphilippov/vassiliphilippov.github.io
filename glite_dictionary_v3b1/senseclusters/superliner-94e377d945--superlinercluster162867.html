Superliner

proper noun
Amtrak railcar class
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
the name of a specific class of bi-level long-distance passenger railcar used by Amtrak in the United States

Examples

The Superliner train
We boarded the Superliner for our cross-country trip from Chicago to Los Angeles.
The Superliner offers both sleeping and seating accommodations for long-distance travelers.
Many passengers enjoy the scenic views from the upper level of the Superliner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coach - a passenger car of a train
  • synonym: railcar - a vehicle used for carrying passengers or goods on a railway
