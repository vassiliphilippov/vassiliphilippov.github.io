first-time user

noun
person using something for the first time
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst taɪm ˈjuːzɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst taɪm ˈjuːzə / (BrE)
a person who is using something for the first time

Examples

A first-time user
As a first-time user, she needed detailed instructions to set up the device.
Many first-time users find the registration process confusing at first.
The software includes a tutorial specifically designed for first-time users.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner - a person who is starting to learn something or do something new
  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
