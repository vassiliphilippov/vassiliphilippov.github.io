Sistine

adjective
of Pope Sixtus
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstiːn / (AmE), / ˈsɪstiːn / (BrE)
relating to the Sistine Chapel in the Vatican, especially its art or decoration, or to Pope Sixtus IV, after whom it is named

Examples

The Sistine Chapel
The Sistine Chapel in Vatican City is famous for its ceiling painted by Michelangelo.
Raphael's Sistine Madonna is considered one of the masterpieces of Renaissance art.
Many tourists visit Rome to admire the Sistine artworks commissioned by Pope Sixtus IV.
The Sistine ceiling is one of the most famous works of art in the world.
Many tourists visit the Vatican to admire the Sistine frescoes painted by Michelangelo.
The restoration of the Sistine Chapel revealed vibrant colors hidden for centuries.
