contain

verb
include as part or component
1.0
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪn / (AmE), / kənˈteɪn / (BrE)
to have or hold something within; to include something as a part or be able to hold it inside

Examples

Contain many items
The box contains all the necessary tools for the job.
The salad contained nuts, so she couldn't eat it.
This book contains several chapters on ancient history.
A stadium is designed to contain thousands of spectators during an event.
The bottle does not contain enough liquid for everyone at the table.
This suitcase can contain up to 20 kilograms of clothes.
Does this document contain all the necessary information for the application?
The bottle contained a mysterious blue liquid when they found it.
This box can contain up to 50 books if you stack them carefully.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accommodate - to have enough space for; to be able to contain
  • antonym: exclude - to not include something as a part or component
  • synonym: hold - to be able to have or keep within; to accommodate
  • synonym: include - to have something as a part of a whole
