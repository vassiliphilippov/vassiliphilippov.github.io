shellcracker

noun
person who opens shells
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlˌkrækər / (AmE), / ˈʃɛlˌkrækə / (BrE)
a person who cracks or opens the shells of nuts or shellfish, often as a job or task

Examples

An expert shellcracker
The shellcracker worked quickly to prepare the oysters for the guests at the seafood festival.
As a skilled shellcracker, she could open dozens of pecans in just a few minutes.
At the restaurant, the shellcracker demonstrated the best way to open crab shells without making a mess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shucker - a person who removes shells from shellfish
