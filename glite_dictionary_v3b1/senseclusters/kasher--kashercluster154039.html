kasher

adjective
fit under Jewish law
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑʃər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːʃə / (BrE)
conforming to Jewish dietary law; fit for consumption or ritual use according to Jewish law

Examples

Kasher food
The family ensured all the food served at the event was kasher.
He asked if the restaurant offered kasher options for observant guests.
Only kasher wine was used during the religious ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kosher - prepared or permitted by Jewish dietary law
  • antonym: treif - not fit or permitted under Jewish dietary law
