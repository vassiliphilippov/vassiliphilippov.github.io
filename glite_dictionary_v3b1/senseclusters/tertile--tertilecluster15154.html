tertile

adjective
divided into thirds
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrtaɪl / (AmE), / ˈtɜːtaɪl / (BrE)
divided into or relating to one of three equal parts, especially in statistical analysis

Examples

Tertile range
The researchers divided the data into tertile groups to compare health outcomes.
Income levels were analyzed by placing participants in the lowest tertile of the distribution.
Students in the highest tertile scored significantly better on the exam.

Related Terms
