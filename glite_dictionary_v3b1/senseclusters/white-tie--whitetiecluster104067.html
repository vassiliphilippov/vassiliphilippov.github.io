white-tie

adjective
requiring the most formal evening dress
4.4 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪt taɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪt taɪ / (BrE)
(of an event or dress code) requiring men to wear white ties and tailcoats; denoting the most formal Western evening dress code.

Examples

White-tie event
He rented a tailcoat for his first-ever white-tie event.
Only ambassadors and dignitaries were invited to the exclusive white-tie dinner at the palace.
The charity gala was a white-tie affair with guests dressed in their most elegant evening wear.
