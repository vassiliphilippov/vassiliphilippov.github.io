inexperienced

adjective
lacking experience or training
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnɪkˈspɪriənst / (AmE), / ˌɪnɪkˈspɪəriənst / (BrE)
lacking knowledge or skill gained through practical experience or training; not experienced.

Examples

An inexperienced worker
Because she was inexperienced, she was given simple tasks at her new job.
Many inexperienced hikers underestimate how challenging the trail can be.
The inexperienced driver struggled with parallel parking on a busy street.

Related Terms

  • antonym: experienced - having knowledge or skill through practice or training
  • synonym: novice - a person new to and lacking experience in a job or situation
