biosphere

noun
regions of Earth where life exists
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.oʊ.sfɪr / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪ.ə(ʊ).sfɪə / (BrE)
The regions of the Earth's surface, atmosphere, and waters where living organisms exist.

Examples

The biosphere of Earth
Astronomers speculate about the possibility of a biosphere existing on other planets.
Pollution and deforestation are major threats to the health of the entire biosphere.
Scientists study the biosphere to understand how different forms of life interact with the environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecosystem - a community of interacting organisms and their environment
