centerless

adjective
without a center
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɚləs / (AmE), / ˈsɛntələs / (BrE)
lacking a central axis or point, especially in mechanical or engineering contexts

Examples

Centerless grinding machine
The factory uses a centerless grinding machine to produce smooth metal rods.
A centerless design allows for faster processing of cylindrical parts.
Some bearings are made using centerless techniques for greater efficiency.

  • antonym: central - having or relating to a center
