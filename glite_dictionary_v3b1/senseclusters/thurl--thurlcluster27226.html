thurl

verb
to pierce or bore
9.9
Pronunciation: / θɜːrl / (AmE), / θɜːl / (BrE)
to make a hole through something by piercing or boring (archaic or dialect)

Examples

To thurl a hole
The craftsman would thurl the wood to create holes for the pegs.
In olden times, people would thurl leather with simple hand tools.
He managed to thurl a small opening in the thick hide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bore - to make a hole in something using a tool
  • synonym: pierce - to make a hole in something with a sharp object
