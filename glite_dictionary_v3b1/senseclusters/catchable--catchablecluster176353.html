catchable

adjective
able to be caught
Pronunciation: / ˈkætʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkætʃəbəl / (BrE)
able to be caught, captured, or seized, especially by hand or with effort

Examples

A catchable ball
The coach praised the player for making a difficult but catchable ball look easy.
Not every fish in the lake is catchable with ordinary bait.
The pass was perfectly thrown and clearly catchable by the receiver.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reachable - able to be reached or attained
  • antonym: unattainable - impossible to achieve or reach
