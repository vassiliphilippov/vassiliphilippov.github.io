osteoid

adjective
like unmineralized bone
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑstiˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒstiˌɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to or resembling bone tissue, especially in its early, unmineralized form

Examples

Osteoid tissue formation
The pathologist noted an osteoid structure forming in the tissue sample.
In certain bone diseases, the amount of osteoid increases due to impaired mineralization.
The tumor was characterized by the presence of abundant osteoid material.
