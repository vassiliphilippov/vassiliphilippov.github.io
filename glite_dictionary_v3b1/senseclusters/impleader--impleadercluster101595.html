impleader

noun
third-party legal action
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpliːdər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpliːdə / (BrE)
a legal procedure in which a third party is brought into a lawsuit by a defendant

Examples

Impleader in court
The defendant used impleader to bring a subcontractor into the ongoing lawsuit.
Without impleader, the court could not consider the third party's responsibility.
The judge explained the process of impleader to the parties involved in the case.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpleader - a legal action to resolve claims by multiple parties to the same property
  • synonym: joinder - the joining of additional parties in a legal action
