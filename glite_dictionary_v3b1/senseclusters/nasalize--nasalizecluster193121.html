nasalize

verb
pronounce a sound through the nose
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪzəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈneɪzəlaɪz /; / ˈneɪzəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
to pronounce a sound or speak with a nasal quality, letting air escape through the nose
