black

noun
total absence of light
8.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /blæk/ (AmE), /blæk/ (BrE)
the darkest color or a state of complete darkness; the quality or state of being black

Examples

In the black of night
A lone candle flickered in the black that filled the old church.
In the black of the unlit basement, it was impossible to see anything.
The hikers lost their way in the black after sunset.
Artists often mix colors with black to create darker shades.
She wore a dress of pure black to the formal event.
The night was so dark, it seemed painted in black.
