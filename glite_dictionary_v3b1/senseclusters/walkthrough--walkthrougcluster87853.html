walkthrough

noun
guided inspection
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔkˌθru / (AmE), / ˈwɔːkˌθruː / (BrE)
an inspection or guided tour of a place or facility to check its features or condition

Examples

House walkthrough
The real estate agent scheduled a walkthrough of the house before the final sale.
We did a walkthrough of the new office to check for any unfinished work.
Before the event, the organizers conducted a walkthrough to ensure everything was ready.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspection - a careful examination of something
  • synonym: tour - a visit to look around a place
