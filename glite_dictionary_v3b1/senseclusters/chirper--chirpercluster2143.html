chirper

noun
one who makes chirps
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɜːrpər / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːpə / (BrE)
a person or animal that makes a short, high-pitched sound, especially like a bird's call

Examples

A morning chirper
The little chirper outside my window greeted the sunrise every morning.
Among the group of birds, one chirper stood out with its cheerful melody.
My cat is quite the chirper when she sees birds through the glass.

Related Terms

  • synonym: singer - a person or animal that produces musical sounds with the voice
  • antonym: squawker - a person or animal that makes a loud, harsh sound
