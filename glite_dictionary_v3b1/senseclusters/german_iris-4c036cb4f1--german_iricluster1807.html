German iris

noun
a species of iris with blue-purple flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝːmən ˈaɪrɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜːmən ˈaɪrɪs / (BrE)
a species of large iris plant (Iris germanica) with showy flowers, commonly grown in gardens
