beachside

noun
area next to beach
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃsaɪd / (BrE)
the area or land next to a beach, often used for homes, businesses, or recreation

Examples

A beachside shop
They bought a small cottage on the beachside, enjoying sea breezes all year round.
Many tourists prefer to stay at a beachside hotel for easy access to the ocean.
Shops and restaurants line the beachside, making it a lively place in summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beachfront - land directly facing or next to a beach
  • antonym: inland - away from the coast or beach
  • synonym: seaside - the area by the sea, especially as a holiday destination
