beachside

noun
area next to beach
2.3 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêt Ésa…™d / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêt Ésa…™d / (BrE)

Examples

Shops and restaurants line the beachside, making it a lively place in summer.
