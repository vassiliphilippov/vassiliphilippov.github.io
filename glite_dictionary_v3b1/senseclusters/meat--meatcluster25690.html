meat

noun
animal flesh used as food
Pronunciation: / miːt / (AmE), / miːt / (BrE)
the flesh of animals, including fish and birds, used as food

Examples

Cooked meat dish
Many people choose to avoid meat for health or ethical reasons.

The restaurant offers a wide selection of meat dishes, including chicken, beef, and lamb.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: fish - cold-blooded vertebrate used as food
  • degree higher: lamb - meat from young sheep
  • degree higher: pork - meat from pigs
  • degree lower: poultry - domestic fowl, such as chickens and turkeys, used as food
