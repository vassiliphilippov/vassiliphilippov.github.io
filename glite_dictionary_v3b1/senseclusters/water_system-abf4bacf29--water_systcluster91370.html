water system

noun
facility supplying water to an area
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔdɚ ˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
an arrangement or facility for collecting, treating, and distributing water to a community or area

Examples

City water system
A break in the water system left the entire neighborhood without running water for hours.
Many rural areas still lack a reliable water system for their communities.
The city upgraded its water system to provide cleaner drinking water to residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: water supply - the available amount or system of water for a region or building
