uxoriously

adverb
in a way showing fondness for one's wife
Pronunciation: / ʌgˈzɔriəsli / (AmE), / ʌkˈsɔːrɪəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that shows excessive or devoted fondness for one's wife.

Examples

To speak uxoriously
He always spoke uxoriously about his wife's talents and achievements.
He deferred uxoriously to his wife's opinion on every household matter.
The politician acted uxoriously in public, often praising his spouse above all else.
