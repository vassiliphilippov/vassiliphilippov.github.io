rapeseed oil

noun
edible oil from rapeseed
9.1 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪp.siːd ɔɪl/ /; / /ˈreɪp.siːd ɔɪ.əl/ (AmE), /ˈreɪp.siːd ɔɪl/ /; / /ˈreɪp.siːd ɔɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a light yellow to brown edible oil obtained from rapeseed, used in cooking and as a lubricant or fuel

Examples

Rapeseed oil cooking
Industrial lubricants sometimes include rapeseed oil due to its natural viscosity.
Many chefs prefer to use rapeseed oil for its mild flavor and high smoke point.
The lamp was filled with rapeseed oil to provide light during the night.
