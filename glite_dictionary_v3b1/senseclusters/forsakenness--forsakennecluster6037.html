forsakenness

noun
state of abandonment
Pronunciation: / fərˈseɪkənnəs / (AmE), / fəˈseɪkənnəs / (BrE)
the state of being abandoned, deserted, or left completely alone

Examples

Lonely forsakenness
The old house radiated a sense of forsakenness after years of neglect.
She wrote about her feelings of forsakenness during the long winter months.
The abandoned puppy's eyes reflected its forsakenness and need for care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of leaving someone or something completely
  • antonym: companionship - the state of having company or friends
  • synonym: loneliness - the state of being alone and unhappy about it
