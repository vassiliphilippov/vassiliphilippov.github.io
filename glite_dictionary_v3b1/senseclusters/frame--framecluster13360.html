frame

noun
structure supporting a picture or mirror
Pronunciation: / freɪm / (AmE), / freɪm / (BrE)
a rigid border or structure that surrounds and supports something, such as a picture, mirror, or door

Examples

Picture frame
She accidentally dropped the frame, shattering the glass but leaving the picture untouched.
The artist chose a golden frame to highlight the painting.
The ornate frame around the mirror matched the room’s elegant decor.
She placed the painting in a gold frame and hung it on the wall.
The glasses had a thin metal frame that suited his face.
The wooden frame of the house was visible before the walls were built.

Related Terms

  • synonym: border - an edge or boundary around something, especially decorative
  • synonym: framework - a basic supporting structure underlying a system or concept
  • synonym: skeleton - the supporting framework of an object, often used metaphorically or literally
