detinue

noun
property recovery lawsuit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛtɪˌnuː / (AmE), / ˈdɛtɪnjuː / (BrE)
a legal situation or action where someone wrongfully keeps another person's property and the owner seeks to recover it
