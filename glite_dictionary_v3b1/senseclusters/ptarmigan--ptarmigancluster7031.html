ptarmigan

noun
large Arctic or subarctic grouse
9.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːr.mɪ.gən/ (AmE), /ˈtɑː.mɪ.gən/ (BrE)
A large Arctic or subarctic grouse with feathered feet and mostly white plumage in winter.

Examples

The ptarmigan bird
During winter, the ptarmigan is almost invisible against the snow due to its white plumage.
The ptarmigan changes color with the seasons to blend into its surroundings.
We spotted a flock of ptarmigan while hiking through the tundra.
