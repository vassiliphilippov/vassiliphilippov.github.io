allocentric

adjective
other-focused
Pronunciation: / ˌæloʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌaləʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
focusing on the needs, interests, or perspectives of others rather than oneself

Examples

Allocentric attitude
Her allocentric approach to teamwork helped everyone feel included and valued.
The organization encourages an allocentric mindset among its volunteers.
He admired her allocentric nature, always putting others before herself.

Related Terms

  • antonym: egocentric - focused on oneself and one's own interests
  • synonym: selfless - concerned more with the needs of others than with one's own
