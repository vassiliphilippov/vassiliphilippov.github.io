sign off

verb
end a broadcast; go off the air
3.8 3.8
Pronunciation: / saɪn ˈɔf /; / saɪn ɑf / (AmE), / saɪn ˈɒf /; / saɪn ɒf / (BrE)
to end a broadcast, communication, or work session, often with a closing remark or farewell

Examples

Before leaving the video call, she remembered to sign off and thank everyone.
