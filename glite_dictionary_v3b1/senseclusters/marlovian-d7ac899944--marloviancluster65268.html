Marlovian

noun
admirer of Marlowe
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɑrˈloʊviən / (AmE), / mɑːˈləʊviən / (BrE)
a person who admires, imitates, or is strongly influenced by Christopher Marlowe or his works

Examples

A true Marlovian
The literary critic described the playwright as a true Marlovian in both style and ambition.
Many Marlovians gather annually to celebrate Marlowe's influence on English drama.
Her poetry, praised by fellow Marlovians, often echoes the themes found in Marlowe's works.
