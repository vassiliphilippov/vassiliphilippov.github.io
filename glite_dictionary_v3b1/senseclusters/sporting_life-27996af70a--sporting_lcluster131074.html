sporting life

noun
life focused on gambling on sports
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔrtɪŋ laɪf / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪŋ laɪf / (BrE)
a way of life characterized by active participation in or interest in gambling on sporting events

Examples

The sporting life of gambling
After leaving his job, he became drawn to the sporting life and its many temptations.
He spent most of his youth immersed in the sporting life of racetracks and betting parlors.
Many novels from the 1920s depict characters who are captivated by the thrills of the sporting life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gambling - the activity of betting money on games or events
