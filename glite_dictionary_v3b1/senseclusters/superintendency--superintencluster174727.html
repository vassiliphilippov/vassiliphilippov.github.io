superintendency

noun
term as superintendent
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛndənsi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛndənsi /; / ˌsjuːpərɪnˈtɛndənsi / (BrE)
the office, position, or period of being a superintendent (a person in charge of an organization, department, or system)
