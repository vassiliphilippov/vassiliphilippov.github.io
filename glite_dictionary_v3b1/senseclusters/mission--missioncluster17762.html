Examples

A mission to Mars

An economic mission will visit Japan next week to discuss trade.

Synonyms

  • operation
    3.2 44/m
    an operation is a planned, often large coordinated action by groups like the military, while a mission is a specific important task or assignment.
  • task
    1.0 156/m
    a task is a single piece of work that can be ordinary or small, while a mission is an important official assignment.
  • task force
    3.3 11/m
    a task force is a temporary group formed to solve a specific problem, while a mission is the important job or assignment they carry out.
  • detail
    6.6 9.89/m
    a detail is a small team, often soldiers, sent to do a particular duty, while a mission is a more important official task.
  • deputation
    6.8 0.22/m
    a deputation is a group sent to represent others or deliver a message, while a mission is a broader important assignment.
  • delegation
    3.8 15/m
    a delegation is a group chosen to represent people at meetings or events, while a mission is an assigned important task or operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment - a task given to someone as part of their job or studies
  • synonym: delegation - a group of people chosen to represent others
  • synonym: duty - something that one is required to do as part of a job or position
  • synonym: operation - a planned activity involving many people, especially in the military
  • degree lower: routine - a regular or usual task (less important than mission)
