resource

verb
to provide support
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɔrs / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːs / (BrE)
to provide a person, group, or project with the necessary materials, people, or support to accomplish a task

Examples

To resource a project
The school was finally resourced with enough computers for every student.
It is important to resource your team properly before starting a big project.
Many charities struggle to stay resourced during difficult economic times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deprive - to take away needed resources or support
  • synonym: equip - to provide with necessary items for a purpose
  • synonym: supply - to provide what is needed
