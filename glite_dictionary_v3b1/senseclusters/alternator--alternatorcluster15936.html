alternator

noun
generator producing alternating current
4.4 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːl.tə.neɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈɔːl.tə.neɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device that generates alternating current, especially as used in cars to charge the battery and power the electrical system

Examples

Car alternator
If your headlights dim while driving at night, your alternator might need to be replaced.
Modern vehicles rely on the alternator to recharge the battery while the engine is running.
The faulty alternator was the reason her car kept losing power on long drives.
