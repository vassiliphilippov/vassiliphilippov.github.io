riverfront

noun
area beside river
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪvərˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈrɪvəˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
the land or area directly beside a river, often developed for recreation, business, or housing

Examples

A riverfront park
The city built a beautiful park along the riverfront for residents to enjoy.
Many restaurants and shops have opened on the riverfront in recent years.
They bought an apartment with a stunning riverfront view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: riverside - the area or land next to a river
  • synonym: waterfront - the land along the edge of a body of water, especially in a city
