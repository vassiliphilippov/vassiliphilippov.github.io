pebble

verb
to pelt with stones
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɛbəl / (BrE)
to throw or pelt with small stones, especially repeatedly or in large numbers

Examples

To pebble the water
The children pebbled the pond, watching the ripples spread across the water.
During the protest, some people pebbled the building's windows, causing minor damage.
After the game, fans pebbled the field in celebration, tossing handfuls of small stones.
