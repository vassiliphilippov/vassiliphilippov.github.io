ccm

noun
flow unit
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.siː.em/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.siː.em/ (BrE)
a unit of volumetric flow equal to one cubic centimetre per minute, used to measure small flow rates

Examples

50 ccm per minute
The technician set the gas flow to 50 ccm for the experiment.
Precise control of ccm is important in chemical reactions requiring small amounts of liquid.
The device can measure flow rates as low as 1 ccm accurately.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: l/min - liters per minute, a larger unit of flow rate
  • synonym: ml/min - milliliters per minute, a unit of flow rate equal to ccm
