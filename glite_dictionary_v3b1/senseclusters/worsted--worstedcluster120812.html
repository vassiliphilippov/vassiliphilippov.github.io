worsted

noun
strong, smooth woolen yarn
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrstɪd / (AmE), / ˈwʊstɪd / (BrE)
a tightly twisted yarn made from long-staple wool fibers, used for weaving and knitting

Examples

Worsted yarn
Fine worsted fabrics are often used to make suits and uniforms.
She bought a skein of worsted to knit a warm winter scarf.
The tailor recommended using worsted for a smooth, professional finish.
