curatorship

noun
position of a curator
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊr.eɪ.tərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / kjʊəˈreɪ.təʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or responsibilities of a curator, especially in a museum, gallery, or similar institution

Examples

Museum curatorship
His curatorship brought fresh attention to overlooked artists and modernized the gallery's collection.
She was offered the curatorship of the city's new art museum after years of dedicated service.
The university announced a new curatorship dedicated to digital media and technology in art.
