gantlet

noun
protective glove with a long cuff
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæntlət /; / ˈɡænt.lət / (AmE), / ˈɡæntlɪt /; / ˈɡɔːnt.lɪt / (BrE)
a protective glove with a long cuff, often made of armor or leather, used for safety or as part of a suit of armor

Examples

The museum displayed a medieval gantlet alongside other pieces of armor.
