damnedest

adverb
extremely; with emphasis
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdæmdɪst / (AmE), / ˈdæmdɪst / (BrE)
used informally to add strong emphasis, meaning extremely or to the highest degree

Examples

It was the damnedest thing I had ever seen in my life.
He ran his damnedest during the race, determined to win.
