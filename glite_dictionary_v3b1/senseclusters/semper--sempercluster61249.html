Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmpər / (AmE), / ˈsɛmpə / (BrE)
a Latin word meaning always or forever, used in English mottos and phrases

Examples

The motto semper fidelis
The Marine Corps motto is 'semper fidelis', where semper means 'always'.
Many schools use the word semper in their Latin mottos to express loyalty.
The phrase 'semper paratus' includes semper, meaning 'always ready'.

Related Terms

  • antonym: never - at no time; not ever
