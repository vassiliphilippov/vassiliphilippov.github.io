swabber

noun
person who swabs
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑbər / (AmE), / ˈswɒbə / (BrE)
a person who collects biological samples by swabbing, especially for medical tests or laboratory analysis

Examples

Medical swabber
The swabber carefully collected samples from each patient for the laboratory.
During the pandemic, the role of a swabber became essential in testing centers.
A trained swabber ensures that samples are taken safely and correctly.

  • synonym: tester - a person who performs tests or examinations
