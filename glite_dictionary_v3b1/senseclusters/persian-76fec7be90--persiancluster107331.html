Persian

noun
long-haired cat breed
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrʒən / (AmE), / ˈpɜːʒən / (BrE)
a breed of domestic cat known for its long, thick fur and flat face

Examples

Persian cat
The Persian is popular for its gentle temperament and beautiful appearance.
She brushed her Persian every day to keep its fur from matting.
Many cat shows feature the elegant Persian as a favorite among judges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Maine Coon - a large breed of domestic cat with a long, thick coat
  • antonym: Siamese - a breed of cat with a slender body and short coat
