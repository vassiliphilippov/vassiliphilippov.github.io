somnambulant

adjective
like sleepwalking
Pronunciation: / sɑmˈnæmbjələnt / (AmE), / sɒmˈnæmbjʊlənt / (BrE)
moving or behaving as if sleepwalking, in a drowsy or unresponsive manner

Examples

Somnambulant state
After staying up all night, she moved through her morning routine in a somnambulant state.
The students listened in a somnambulant haze during the early lecture.
His somnambulant walk across the stage made everyone wonder if he was fully awake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alert - fully awake and attentive
  • synonym: sleepwalking - walking or performing actions while asleep
