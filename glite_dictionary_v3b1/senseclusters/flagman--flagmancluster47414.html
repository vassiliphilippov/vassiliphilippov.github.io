flagman

noun
motorsport race official
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈflæɡ.mən/ /; / /ˈflæɡ.mæn/ (AmE), /ˈflæɡ.mən/ /; / /ˈflæɡ.mæn/ (BrE)
an official at a motorsport event who uses colored flags to signal track conditions and instructions to drivers

Examples

Race flagman
The flagman signaled a red flag, bringing the race to a temporary stop.
After the accident, the flagman quickly waved the yellow flag to warn the drivers.
Every driver must pay close attention to the flagman during the race for safety instructions.
