vivandière

noun
female army supplier
9.9
Pronunciation: /vɪv.ən.ˈdjɪr/ /; / /vɪv.æn.ˈdjɪr/ (AmE), /vɪv.ən.ˈdjɪə/ /; / /vɪv.æn.ˈdjɪə/ (BrE)
a woman who accompanied soldiers (especially in the 19th century) to provide food, drink, and supplies during military campaigns
