molded

adjective
shaped by forming in a mold
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊldɪd / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪd / (BrE)
shaped to fit by being formed in a mold or by altering the contours of a pliable material, especially through work or effort.

Examples

Molded to fit
Each helmet was carefully molded to fit the athlete’s head.
The artist created a molded sculpture from the soft clay.
The furniture featured beautifully molded wooden legs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shaped - having a particular shape or form
  • synonym: wrought - formed or manufactured, especially by hammering or pressure
