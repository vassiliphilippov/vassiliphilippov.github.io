organismal

adjective
of or relating to an organism as a whole
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrɡəˈnɪzməl / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡəˈnɪzməl / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to an organism considered as a whole, rather than to its individual parts

Examples

Organismal adaptation
Organismal adaptation often involves complex changes at various biological levels.
She is studying organismal biology, which focuses on the whole animal rather than just its cells.
The decline in organismal health was apparent in the altered behavior of the population.
