zero-tolerance policy

noun
strict policy with no exceptions
Pronunciation: / ˌzɪəroʊ ˈtɑlɚəns ˈpɑlɪsi / (AmE), / ˌzɪərəʊ ˈtɒl(ə)rəns ˈpɒlɪsi / (BrE)
a strict policy that allows no exceptions and prescribes a fixed punishment for every infraction, regardless of circumstances

Examples

A zero-tolerance policy
Many workplaces enforce a zero-tolerance policy regarding substance abuse.
The company stated that its zero-tolerance policy applied to all forms of harassment.
The school adopted a zero-tolerance policy on bullying to protect its students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leniency - the practice of showing mercy or being less strict
  • synonym: zero tolerance - the principle of not allowing any exception to a rule or law
