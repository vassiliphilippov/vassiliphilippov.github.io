look

verb
be oriented in a certain direction
Pronunciation: / lʊk / (AmE), / lʊk / (BrE)
(of a building, window, or similar) to face or be oriented in a particular direction.

Examples

The window looks east
Our office looks south, so we get sunlight all day long.
The master bedroom looks onto the garden, giving a great view every morning.
The restaurant looks out over the harbor and offers stunning sunset views.

Related Terms

  • synonym: face - to be positioned so as to look toward something
  • synonym: overlook - to look out over or have a view of
