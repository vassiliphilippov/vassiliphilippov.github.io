handmaid

noun
female personal attendant; maid
Pronunciation: / ˈhænˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈhanˌmeɪd / (BrE)
a female servant or personal attendant who helps with domestic duties or accompanies a lady.

Examples

A handmaid serves a lady
Even as a handmaid, she earned the respect of everyone in the household.
In the historical novel, the wealthy lady was always accompanied by her trusted handmaid.
The handmaid carried fresh linens and prepared the lady's chambers for the evening.
