noncontrollable

adjective
unable to be controlled
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈtroʊləbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊləbəl / (BrE)
not able to be controlled, managed, or influenced (by a person or system)

Examples

Several noncontrollable factors
The experiment was affected by several noncontrollable factors that the researchers could not predict.
Some risks in business are noncontrollable and must be managed differently from those you can influence.
Weather is often a noncontrollable variable in outdoor event planning.
He struggled with noncontrollable urges to laugh during the serious ceremony.
The disease can cause noncontrollable muscle movements in some patients.
Her reaction was so strong it seemed completely noncontrollable in that moment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: controllable - able to be controlled or managed
  • synonym: uncontrollable - impossible to control or restrain
