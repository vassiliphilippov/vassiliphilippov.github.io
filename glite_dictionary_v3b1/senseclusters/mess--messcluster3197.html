mess

noun
state of confusion or disorder
4.5 1.0
Pronunciation: /mes/ (AmE), /mes/ (BrE)
a state of untidiness, confusion, or a situation full of problems

Examples

A big mess

He found himself in a legal mess after signing that contract.
Her finances became a mess when she lost her job.
