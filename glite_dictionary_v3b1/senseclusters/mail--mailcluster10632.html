Synonyms

  • chain armor
    8.2 0.01/m
    chain armor refers to flexible protective rings worn in battle, while mail is the historic garment of interlinked metal rings.
  • ring mail
    9.9 0.00/m
    ring mail is a Middle Ages style named for visible circular rings, while mail is the general historical term for that armor.
  • ring armor
    9.9 0.00/m
    ring armor describes protection built from round linked rings used in battle, while mail is the older, broader historical name.
  • coat of mail
    9.9 0.04/m
    coat of mail is a full garment that may include rings or plates for added cover, while mail simply names ring armor.
