unranked

adjective
not arranged by rank or order
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈræŋkt / (AmE), / ʌnˈræŋkt / (BrE)
not arranged in order according to rank or level; lacking an assigned position in a hierarchy.

Examples

Unranked species list
Many species of insects remain unranked due to insufficient classification.
The teams in this tournament are currently unranked and will be seeded later.
Unlike the top seeds, unranked players must compete in the qualification rounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ranked - arranged according to order, level, or position
