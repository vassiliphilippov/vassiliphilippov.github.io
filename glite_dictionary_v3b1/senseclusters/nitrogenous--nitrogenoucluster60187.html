nitrogenous

adjective
containing or relating to nitrogen
Pronunciation: / naɪˈtroʊdʒənəs / (AmE), / naɪˈtrɒdʒənəs / (BrE)
containing, relating to, or derived from nitrogen.

Examples

Nitrogenous wastes
Fish excrete nitrogenous wastes such as ammonia into the water.
Some bacteria are capable of decomposing nitrogenous compounds in the soil.
The fertilizer used on the crops is highly nitrogenous to promote strong plant growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nitrogen-free - not containing nitrogen
