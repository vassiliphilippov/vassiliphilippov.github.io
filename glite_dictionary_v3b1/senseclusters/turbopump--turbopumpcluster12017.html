turbopump

noun
turbine-driven pump
7.2 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ùÀêbo äÀåp åmp / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀêb…ô äÀåp åmp / (BrE)

Examples

A malfunction in the turbopump can lead to a dangerous drop in fuel pressure.
