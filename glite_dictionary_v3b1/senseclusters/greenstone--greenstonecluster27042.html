greenstone

noun
New Zealand jade
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a type of jade, especially nephrite, valued as a gemstone and sacred material, particularly by the Maori people of New Zealand

Examples

Maori greenstone
The museum displayed a beautiful greenstone pendant carved by a Maori artist.
He treasured the greenstone necklace he received as a family heirloom.
Many traditional Maori tools and ornaments were made from greenstone found in New Zealand rivers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jade - a hard, typically green stone used as a gemstone and for carving
  • synonym: nephrite - a variety of jade, usually green, used for ornaments and jewelry
