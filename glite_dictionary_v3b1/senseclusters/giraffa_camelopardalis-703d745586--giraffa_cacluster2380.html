Giraffa camelopardalis

noun
scientific name for the giraffe
8.8
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈræfə ˌkæməloʊˈpɑːrdəlɪs / (AmE), / dʒɪˈræfə ˌkæmələʊˈpɑːdəlɪs / (BrE)
The scientific name for the giraffe, the tallest living land animal, native to the savannas of tropical Africa, characterized by its long neck, long legs, and spotted coat.
