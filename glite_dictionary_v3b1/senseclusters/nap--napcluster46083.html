nap

verb
sleep lightly or briefly during the day
1.0 1.43/m
Pronunciation: / nÃ¦p / (AmE), / nÃ¦p / (BrE)
to sleep lightly or for a short period, especially during the day

Examples

To nap after lunch
I like to nap for twenty minutes in the afternoon when I get the chance.
She usually naps on the couch after returning from work.
The baby was napping peacefully in her crib while the house was quiet.
