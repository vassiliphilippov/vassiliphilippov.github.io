predigested

adjective
partially digested before consumption
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌpriːdaɪˈdʒɛstɪd / (BrE)
(of food or nutrients) partially digested through artificial or enzymatic processes before being consumed, to aid easier absorption or digestion.

Examples

Predigested protein supplements
Doctors often recommend predigested protein supplements for patients with difficulty absorbing nutrients.
Infant formulas containing predigested milk are easier on sensitive stomachs.
Many health food products now offer predigested ingredients to promote better nutrient uptake.
