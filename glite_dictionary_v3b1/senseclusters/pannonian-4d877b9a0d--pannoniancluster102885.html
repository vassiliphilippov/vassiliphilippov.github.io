Pannonian

noun
person from Pannonia
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈnoʊniən / (AmE), / pəˈnəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Pannonia or the Pannonian Basin region of Central Europe

Examples

A Pannonian person
The Pannonian proudly displayed artifacts from their region at the cultural festival.
As a Pannonian, she often spoke about the unique traditions of her homeland.
Many Pannonians settled along the Danube River during ancient times.
