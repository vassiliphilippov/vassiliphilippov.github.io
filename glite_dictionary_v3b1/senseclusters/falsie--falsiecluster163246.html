falsie

noun
artificial eyelash
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔl.si / (AmE), / ˈfɔːl.si / (BrE)
an artificial eyelash used to enhance the appearance of natural lashes

Examples

Long falsie lashes
After losing one falsie, she quickly fixed her makeup before going on stage.
Many people use falsies for special occasions when they want extra-long lashes.
She wore a pair of falsies to make her eyes look more dramatic for the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eyelash extension - a semi-permanent enhancement added to eyelashes
  • degree lower: mascara - cosmetic used to darken and thicken eyelashes
