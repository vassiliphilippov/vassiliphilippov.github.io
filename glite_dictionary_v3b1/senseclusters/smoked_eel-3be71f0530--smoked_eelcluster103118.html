smoked eel

noun
eel preserved and flavored by smoking
3.9
Pronunciation: / smoʊkt il / (AmE), / sməʊkt iːl / (BrE)
Eel that has been cured and flavored by exposure to smoke, often eaten as a delicacy.

Examples

A smoked eel dish
He bought a package of smoked eel from the local fish market.
The restaurant's specialty is smoked eel served on rye bread with horseradish cream.
Traditional Dutch breakfasts sometimes include slices of smoked eel.
