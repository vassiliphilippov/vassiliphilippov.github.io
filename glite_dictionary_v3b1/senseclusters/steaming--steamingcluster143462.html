steaming

adjective
filled with steam or emitting vapor
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstiːmɪŋ / (BrE)
(of something) filled with steam or emitting moisture in the form of vapor or mist.

Examples

Fresh steaming towels
Fresh steaming towels were brought to the table after the meal.
He handed her a steaming mug of hot chocolate as they sat by the fire.
The kitchen was steaming after she boiled several pots of water simultaneously.

Related Terms

  • synonym: steamy - full of steam or appearing moist with vapor
