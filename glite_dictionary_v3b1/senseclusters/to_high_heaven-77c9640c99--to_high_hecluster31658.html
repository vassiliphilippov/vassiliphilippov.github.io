to high heaven

adverb
to an extreme or excessive degree
Pronunciation: / tə haɪ ˈhɛvən / (AmE), / tə haɪ ˈhɛvən / (BrE)
to an extreme or excessive degree; extremely or very much; very loudly or intensely

Examples

Praise to high heaven
He praised his team to high heaven for their efforts.
The children were screaming to high heaven during the rollercoaster ride.
The kitchen smelled to high heaven after she burnt the toast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessively - to a greater degree than is usual or appropriate
  • synonym: to the nines - to the highest degree
