Punic War

proper noun
wars between Rome and Carthage (264–146 BC)
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpjuː.nɪk wɔːr/ /; / /ˈpjuː.nɪk wɑːr/ (AmE), /ˈpjuː.nɪk wɔː/ (BrE)
