Mastigomycota

noun
group of fungi with motile spores and gametes
Pronunciation: / ˌmæs.tɪɡ.oʊ.maɪˈkoʊ.tə /; / ˌmæs.tɪ.ɡoʊˈmaɪ.kə.tə / (AmE), / ˌmas.tɪ.ɡəʊ.maɪˈkəʊ.tə /; / ˌmæs.tɪ.ɡəʊˈmʌɪ.kə.tə / (BrE)
a group of fungi with motile spores and gametes, sometimes formerly classified with Zygomycota in Phycomycetes
