Mytilidae

noun
family of marine mussels
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪˈtɪləˌdiː / (AmE), / mɪˈtɪlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine bivalve mollusks known as marine mussels, found in oceans and estuaries worldwide.

Examples

The Mytilidae family
Many edible mussels are classified under the Mytilidae family.
Scientists collected several species belonging to the Mytilidae family from the rocky shoreline.
The Mytilidae play an important role in filtering water in marine ecosystems.
