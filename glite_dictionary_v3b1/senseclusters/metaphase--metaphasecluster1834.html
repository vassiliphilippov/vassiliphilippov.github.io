metaphase

noun
stage in cell division when chromosomes align
2.8 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌfeɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌfeɪz / (BrE)
the stage in cell division (mitosis or meiosis) when chromosomes line up in the center of the cell before being separated
