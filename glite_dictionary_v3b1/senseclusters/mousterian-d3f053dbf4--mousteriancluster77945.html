Mousterian

adjective
Neanderthal stone tools
9.9
Pronunciation: / muˈstɪriən / (AmE), / muˈstɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to a Middle Paleolithic stone tool culture associated with Neanderthals in Europe and western Asia

Examples

Mousterian culture
Archaeologists discovered Mousterian artifacts in the cave, indicating Neanderthal occupation.
The Mousterian culture is known for its distinctive flint tools and scrapers.
Researchers studied Mousterian remains to learn more about prehistoric human behavior.

  • synonym: Paleolithic - relating to the early phase of the Stone Age
