eye black

noun
anti-glare face paint
3.1
Pronunciation: / aɪ blæk / (AmE), / aɪ blæk / (BrE)
a dark substance applied under athletes' eyes to reduce glare from sunlight or bright lights during sports

Examples

Athlete applies eye black
The baseball player applied eye black before stepping onto the sunny field.
Many athletes believe that eye black helps them see better during day games.
She wore eye black for the championship match to reduce the glare from the stadium lights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: face paint - colored makeup applied to the face for decoration or protection
