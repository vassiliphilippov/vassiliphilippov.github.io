lantern slide

noun
framed photographic slide for projection
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlæntɚn slaɪd / (AmE), / ˈlæntən slaɪd / (BrE)
a transparent photographic image mounted in a frame, designed to be projected onto a screen using a lantern or slide projector

Examples

A lantern slide image
He used a lantern slide to illustrate his lecture on ancient ruins.
Old family photographs were sometimes preserved as lantern slides for projection.
The museum displayed several rare lantern slides from the 19th century.
