hemianopia

noun
blindness in half of the visual field
4.1 8.6
Pronunciation: /he.mɪ.ə.ˈnoʊ.piː.ə/ /; / /hə.mɪ.ə.ˈnoʊ.piː.ə/ (AmE), /he.mɪ.ə.ˈnəʊ.piː.ə/ /; / /hə.mɪ.ə.ˈnəʊ.piː.ə/ (BrE)
blindness in one half of the visual field in one or both eyes, often caused by damage to the brain or optic pathways
