California sycamore

noun
tree native to Baja California with lobed leaves
1.0
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɪfɔːrniə ˈsɪkəmɔr / (AmE), / kəˌlɪfəniə ˈsɪkəmɔː / (BrE)
a tall tree native to Baja California, with deciduous bark, large, palmately lobed leaves, and round clusters of flowers
