by hand

adverb
manually, without a machine
Pronunciation: / baɪ hænd / (AmE), / baɪ hænd / (BrE)
using your hands to do something, not using machines or tools; manually

Examples

Written by hand
He wrote the entire letter by hand because he couldn't find his laptop.
Many traditional rugs are still made by hand in rural areas.
The pottery was crafted by hand, giving each piece a unique feel.
He repaired the old clock by hand, refusing to use any power tools.
She decorates every cake by hand to ensure each one is unique.
The invitations were written by hand rather than printed.
