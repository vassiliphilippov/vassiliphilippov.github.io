Oppression Olympics

noun
competition over who has suffered more oppression
8.6
Pronunciation: / əˈprɛʃən əˈlɪmpɪks / (AmE), / əˈprɛʃ(ə)n əˈlɪmpɪks / (BrE)
an informal term for competition among individuals or groups to establish who has experienced greater levels of oppression or victimization
