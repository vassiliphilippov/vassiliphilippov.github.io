Yankees

proper noun
New York baseball team
Pronunciation: / ˈjæŋkiz / (AmE), / ˈjæŋkiːz / (BrE)
the professional baseball team based in New York City, officially known as the New York Yankees

Examples

New York Yankees
The Yankees are one of the most successful teams in Major League Baseball history.
Many fans travel from all over the country to watch the Yankees play at their home stadium.
After a close game, the Yankees celebrated their victory with their supporters.
