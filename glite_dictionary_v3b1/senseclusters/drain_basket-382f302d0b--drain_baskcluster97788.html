drain basket

noun
perforated strainer for sink drains
3.7 2.4
Pronunciation: /dreɪn ˈbæs.kət/ /; / /dreɪn ˈbæs.kɪt/ (AmE), /dreɪn ˈbɑː.skɪt/ /; / /dreɪn ˈbæs.kɪt/ (BrE)
