Dromaeosauridae

noun
family of bipedal predatory dinosaurs
Pronunciation: / droʊˌmiːoʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / droʊˌmiː.oʊˈsɔːrɪˌdiː / (AmE), / drəʊˌmɪəʊˈsɔːrɪdiː /; / drəˌmiː.əʊˈsɔːrɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of swift-running, bipedal, carnivorous dinosaurs (often called 'raptors') that lived during the Cretaceous period, including species like Velociraptor
