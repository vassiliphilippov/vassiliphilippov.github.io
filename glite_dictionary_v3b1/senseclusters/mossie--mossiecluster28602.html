mossie

noun
South African sparrow
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɒsi / (AmE), / ˈmɒsi / (BrE)
a small brown sparrow or similar small bird, especially used in South African English to mean 'sparrow'

Examples

A mossie on the fence
A little mossie hopped along the garden fence looking for seeds this morning.
Children watched as a mossie built its nest under the roof.
The early morning was filled with the chirping of mossies in the trees.
