biopharmaceutical

adjective
relating to biological drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌfɑrməˈsuːtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌfɑːməˈsjuːtɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to drugs that are produced using living organisms or biological processes

Examples

Biopharmaceutical industry
The biopharmaceutical sector is rapidly growing due to advances in biotechnology.
Many biopharmaceutical products are used to treat complex diseases like cancer and autoimmune disorders.
A biopharmaceutical company developed a new vaccine using living cells.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biologic - relating to medicines derived from living organisms
  • synonym: pharmaceutical - relating to drugs or their preparation
  • antonym: synthetic - made by chemical synthesis, not from living organisms
