uncaptioned

adjective
without a caption
5.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæpʃənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæpʃənd / (BrE)
not having a caption or explanatory text accompanying it

Examples

Uncaptioned images
The article featured several uncaptioned images that left readers guessing about their meaning.
He submitted an uncaptioned photo to the contest, letting the picture speak for itself.
Many of the paintings in the gallery were uncaptioned, encouraging visitors to interpret them freely.
