grind

noun
monotonous routine work
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd / (BrE)
Hard, monotonous, and often tedious routine work.

Examples

The daily grind
After months of the same grind, she finally took a vacation.
College can feel like a never-ending grind during exam season.
He grew tired of the daily grind at the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drudgery - tedious, menial, or unpleasant work
  • antonym: pleasure - a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment; opposite of routine hard work
  • synonym: slog - a period of difficult or tiring work
