lookout woman

noun
female person who keeps watch
7.1 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊk.aʊt ˈwʊ.mən / (AmE), / ˈlʊk.aʊt ˈwʊ.mən / (BrE)
A woman whose job or role is to keep watch for a particular event or to warn others of approaching danger, especially in criminal contexts.
