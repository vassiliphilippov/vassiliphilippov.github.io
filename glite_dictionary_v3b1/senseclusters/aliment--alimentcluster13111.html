aliment

noun
food; nourishment
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈæl.ə.mənt / (AmE), / ˈæl.ɪ.mənt / (BrE)
food; nourishment; something that provides sustenance to the body.

Examples

Nutritious aliment
Many ancient texts refer to honey as both an aliment and a medicine.
Proper aliment is essential for the growth and health of young children.
The botanist studied plants used as aliment by indigenous communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: food - any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink to maintain life and growth
  • synonym: nourishment - the substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition
