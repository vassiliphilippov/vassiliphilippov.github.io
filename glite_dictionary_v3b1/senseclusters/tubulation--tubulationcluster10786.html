tubulation

noun
formation of tubes
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌtjuːbjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtjuːbjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming or arranging something into tubes

Examples

Tubulation of pipes
The tubulation of the laboratory equipment improved the flow of chemicals.
Engineers focused on the tubulation of the cooling system to enhance efficiency.
Proper tubulation is essential for the safe transport of fluids in industrial plants.
