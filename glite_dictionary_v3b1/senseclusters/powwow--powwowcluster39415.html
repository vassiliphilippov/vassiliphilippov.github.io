powwow

noun
informal private meeting or discussion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊˌwaʊ / (AmE), / ˈpaʊˌwaʊ / (BrE)
an informal meeting or discussion, especially one held to make plans or decisions quickly and privately

Examples

Quick powwow at work
After a brief powwow in the hallway, they returned with a decision.
Let's have a quick powwow before the presentation to make sure we're all on the same page.
The managers called a powwow to settle the dispute quietly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion
  • synonym: discussion - the action or process of talking about something
  • synonym: meeting - an assembly of people for a particular purpose, especially for formal discussion
