blackmailer

noun
person who extorts money by threats
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌmeɪlər / (AmE), / ˈblakˌmeɪlə / (BrE)
a person who obtains money from someone by threatening to reveal embarrassing, damaging, or incriminating information about them

Examples

The blackmailer demands money
Authorities finally caught the blackmailer after a lengthy investigation into the extortion scheme.
She contacted the police when she realized she was dealing with a blackmailer.
The blackmailer threatened to release private photos if he wasn't paid immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extortionist - someone who obtains money by force or threats
  • antonym: victim - the person targeted or threatened by a blackmailer
