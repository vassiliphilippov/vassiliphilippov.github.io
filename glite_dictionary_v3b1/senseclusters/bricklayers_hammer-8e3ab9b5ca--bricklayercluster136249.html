bricklayer's hammer

noun
hammer used for bricklaying
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌleɪərz ˈhæmər / (AmE), / ˈbrɪkleɪəz ˈhamə / (BrE)
a type of hammer with a flat face and a chisel-like blade, used by bricklayers for cutting and setting bricks

Examples

A bricklayer's hammer tool
He reached for his bricklayer's hammer before starting the new wall.
The bricklayer's hammer is perfect for splitting bricks and chiseling away mortar.
With a swift motion, she used the bricklayer's hammer to trim the edges of the brick.
