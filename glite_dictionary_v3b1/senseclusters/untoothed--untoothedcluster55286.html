untoothed

adjective
having no notches or teeth
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtuːθt / (AmE), / ʌnˈtuːθt / (BrE)
lacking notches or teeth; smooth-edged.

Examples

Untoothed smooth edge
The craftsman preferred untoothed blades for making precise cuts in soft wood.
The leaf margins are untoothed and appear smooth to the touch.
This species is recognized by its untoothed jawline, unlike its close relatives.
