whisperingly

adverb
in a whispering way
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that resembles or is characterized by whispering

Examples

To speak whisperingly
He answered whisperingly, afraid that someone might overhear their conversation.
The wind moved whisperingly through the tall grass at dusk.
She read the poem whisperingly to create a mysterious atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loudly - in a way that produces a lot of noise
  • synonym: softly - in a quiet or gentle manner
