whisperingly

adverb
in a whispering way
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspərɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈwɪspərɪŋli / (BrE)

Examples

To speak whisperingly

The wind moved whisperingly through the tall grass at dusk.
She read the poem whisperingly to create a mysterious atmosphere.
