steelwork

noun
making steel structures
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈstiːlwɜːk / (BrE)
the activity or process of making, assembling, or installing structures or components from steel

Examples

Steelwork installation
The new bridge required complex steelwork to support its weight and span.
He found a job in steelwork, helping to assemble large metal frames for buildings.
Modern steelwork techniques allow for taller and more durable skyscrapers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carpentry - the activity of making or repairing wooden structures
  • synonym: metalwork - the activity of making things out of metal
