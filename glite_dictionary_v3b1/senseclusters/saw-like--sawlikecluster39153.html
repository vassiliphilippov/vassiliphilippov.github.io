saw-like

adjective
having rough or jagged, saw-resembling edges
Pronunciation: / sɔːˌlaɪk / (AmE), / sɔːˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having rough or jagged edges resembling those of a saw, especially able to cut or tear

Examples

Saw-like teeth
Some fish have saw-like teeth to help them tear through prey.
The saw-like blade easily cut through the thick branch.
The leaf's saw-like margin protects it from insects and animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serrated - having a notched or saw-toothed edge
