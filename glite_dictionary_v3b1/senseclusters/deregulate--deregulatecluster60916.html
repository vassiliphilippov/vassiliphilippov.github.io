to remove regulations or restrictions from an industry, market, or activity, typically by government action

Examples

To deregulate the market
After the energy sector was deregulated, prices became more volatile.
Many economists argue that it's risky to deregulate financial markets without proper safeguards.
The government decided to deregulate the telecommunications industry to encourage competition.
