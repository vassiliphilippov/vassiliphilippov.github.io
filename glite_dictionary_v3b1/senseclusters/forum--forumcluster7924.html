forum

noun
public meeting for open discussion
3.2 38/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…îr…ôm / (AmE), / Ààf…îÀêr…ôm / (BrE)
a meeting, place, or online space where people discuss ideas and exchange views

Examples

Public forum meeting
Citizens attended the public forum to share their views on local government policies.
The annual forum attracted experts from around the world to debate environmental issues.
The school organized a forum to discuss improvements to campus safety.
Online forums provide a space for users to share advice and exchange ideas.
She raised her question during the public forum held at the community center.
The city council organized a forum for residents to voice their concerns about housing.

Synonyms

  • meeting place
    1.3 1.09/m
    a meeting place is a simple physical spot where people gather to talk, while a forum can be online or formal for wider idea exchange.
  • assembly
    3.5 13/m
    an assembly is a public building or gathering for events and formal meetings, while a forum often hosts open discussion and may be online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conference - a formal meeting for discussion
  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
  • synonym: panel - a group of people gathered to discuss a topic publicly
  • synonym: symposium - a formal meeting for discussion of a subject
