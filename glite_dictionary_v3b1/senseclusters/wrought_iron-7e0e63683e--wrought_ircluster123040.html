wrought iron

noun
finish or colour imitating wrought iron
4.8
Pronunciation: /rɔːt ˈaɪ.ərn/ /; / /rɔːt aɪrn/ (AmE), /rɔːt ˈaɪ.ən/ (BrE)
A paint, coating, or surface finish that imitates the dark gray–black metallic appearance of wrought iron, used on furniture, fixtures, or decorative objects.

Examples

Wrought iron finish
The patio set is available in a wrought iron finish that resists rust and hides scratches.
She chose a lamp with a wrought iron look to match the room's industrial decor.
The new door handle comes in a wrought iron coating that gives it an aged, matte look.
