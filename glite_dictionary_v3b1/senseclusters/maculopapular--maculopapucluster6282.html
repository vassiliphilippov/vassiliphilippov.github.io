maculopapular

adjective
rash with flat and raised spots
Pronunciation: / ˌmækjəloʊˈpæpjələr / (AmE), / ˌmakjʊləʊˈpapjʊlə / (BrE)
describing a rash that has both flat and raised red spots on the skin

Examples

Maculopapular rash
The child developed a maculopapular rash after receiving the vaccine.
Doctors often look for a maculopapular eruption when diagnosing certain viral infections.
A maculopapular skin reaction can be a sign of an allergic response to medication.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: macular - describing a rash with only flat spots
  • degree lower: papular - describing a rash with raised bumps
