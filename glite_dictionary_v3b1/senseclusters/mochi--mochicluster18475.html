mochi

noun
Japanese rice cake
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.tʃi / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.tʃi / (BrE)
a soft, sticky rice cake made from glutinous rice, traditionally eaten in Japan

Examples

Sweet mochi dessert
For the festival, they prepared sweet red bean mochi for everyone to enjoy.
She bought fresh mochi from the Japanese bakery near her house.
Children love eating mochi during the New Year celebrations in Japan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rice cake - a cake made from compressed rice
