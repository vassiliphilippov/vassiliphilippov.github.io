jus

noun
natural liquid extract
Pronunciation: / ʒu / (AmE), / ʒuː / (BrE)
a thin sauce or natural liquid made from the juices released by meat (or sometimes other foods) during cooking, often served with the meat

Examples

Lamb with jus
The chef poured a rich jus over the roasted lamb to enhance its flavor.
She prefers to drink fresh orange jus with her breakfast every morning.
The recipe calls for a small amount of beef jus to add depth to the sauce.
The chef poured a rich jus over the roasted lamb before serving.
For extra flavor, the steak was accompanied by a red wine jus.
He asked for more jus to go with his prime rib.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broth - liquid in which meat or vegetables have been cooked
  • synonym: gravy - a sauce made from meat juices, often thickened
  • synonym: juice - the liquid part of fruit or vegetables
  • synonym: sauce - a liquid or semi-liquid served with food to add flavor
