John Wilkes

proper noun
English reformer, 18th century politician
8.1
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn wɪlks / (AmE), / dʒɒn wɪlks / (BrE)
(1727–1797) An English reformer known for publishing attacks on King George III and supporting the rights of American colonists.

Examples

John Wilkes the reformer
