brown butter

noun
butter cooked until brown and nutty
6.6
Pronunciation: / braʊn ˈbʌtər / (AmE), / braʊn ˈbʌtə / (BrE)
Butter that has been melted and cooked until it turns brown, giving it a nutty flavor, often used as a sauce or ingredient in cooking.

Examples

Brown butter sauce
Many classic French sauces start with brown butter as a base for their rich taste.
She made cookies with brown butter to give them a deep, nutty aroma.
The chef drizzled brown butter over the roasted vegetables for extra flavor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarified butter - butter with milk solids and water removed
