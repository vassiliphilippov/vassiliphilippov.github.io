protestingly

adverb
showing objection
Pronunciation: / prəˈtɛstɪŋli / (AmE), / prəˈtɛstɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows objection, disagreement, or protest

Examples

To speak protestingly
He raised his hand protestingly when the teacher announced the surprise test.
She looked protestingly at her friend when asked to keep a secret.
The child shook his head protestingly when told to eat his vegetables.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreeably - in a way that shows agreement or acceptance
  • synonym: objectingly - in a manner that expresses objection
