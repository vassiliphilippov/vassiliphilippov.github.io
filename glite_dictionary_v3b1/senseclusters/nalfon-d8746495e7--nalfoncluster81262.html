Nalfon

noun
anti-inflammatory drug (brand name)
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnæl.fɑn / (AmE), / ˈnal.fɒn / (BrE)
A brand name for fenoprofen, a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat arthritis and other inflammatory disorders.
