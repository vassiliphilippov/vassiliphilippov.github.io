panda

noun
small, raccoon-like mammal of Asia (red panda)
8.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpæn.də/ /; / /ˈpɑːn.də/ (AmE), /ˈpæn.də/ /; / /ˈpʌn.də/ (BrE)
a small, reddish-brown mammal native to the Himalayas and China, resembling a raccoon and distinct from the giant panda; also known as the red panda.

Examples

Red panda
The panda spends most of its time in trees searching for bamboo and berries.
The zookeeper explained that the panda is more closely related to raccoons than to bears.
Unlike the giant panda, the panda has a long, bushy tail and reddish fur.
