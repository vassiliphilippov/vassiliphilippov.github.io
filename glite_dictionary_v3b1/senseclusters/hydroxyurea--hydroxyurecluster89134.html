hydroxyurea

noun
DNA synthesis inhibitor
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdrɑksijuˈriə /; / haɪˌdrɑksi juˈriːə / (AmE), / haɪˌdrɒksiˈjʊəriə /; / haɪˌdrɒksi juˈriːə / (BrE)
a drug or chemical used in medicine and research to slow cell growth, especially for treating some cancers and blood disorders
