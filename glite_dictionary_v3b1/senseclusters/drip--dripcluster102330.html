drip

noun
sound of liquid falling in drops
3.5
Pronunciation: / drɪp / (AmE), / drɪp / (BrE)
the sound made by liquid falling in drops, especially at regular intervals

Examples

The drip sound
She could hear the drip of rainwater echoing in the empty hallway.
The only sound in the room was the drip of water into a metal bucket.
The steady drip from the faucet kept him awake all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drip - a small drop of liquid that falls from something
