Dipladenia boliviensis

noun
a species of shrubby climbing plant
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪpləˈdiːniə ˌbɒlɪˈviːənsɪs / (AmE), / ˌdɪpləˈdiːniə ˌbɒlɪˈviːənsɪs / (BrE)
A species of shrubby climbing plant with glossy leaves and white funnel-shaped flowers with yellow throats, native to Bolivia and neighboring regions.
