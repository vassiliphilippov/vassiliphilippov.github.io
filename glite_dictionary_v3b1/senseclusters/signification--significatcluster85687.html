signification

noun
meaning expressed by word or symbol
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪɡnəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the meaning or sense that is conveyed or expressed by a word, sign, or symbol

Examples

The signification of words

Some Chinese characters have more than one signification, depending on context.
The signification of the symbol changed over time due to cultural shifts.
