oarsman

noun
man who rows a boat
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrzmən / (AmE), / ˈɔːzmən / (BrE)
A man who rows a boat, especially as a member of a crew.

Examples

An oarsman rows
As an experienced oarsman, he competed in many regattas around the world.
The oarsman maintained a strong and steady rhythm throughout the race.
The team's best oarsman trained for hours each morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oarswoman - a female who rows a boat
  • synonym: rower - person who rows a boat
