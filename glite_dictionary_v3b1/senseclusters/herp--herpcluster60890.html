herp

noun
slang for reptile or amphibian
Pronunciation: / hɜrp / (AmE), / hɜːp / (BrE)
a slang term for a reptile or amphibian, or for an animal studied in herpetology

He is a herp enthusiast who keeps several snakes and frogs.
The club meets monthly to discuss herps and share care tips for their pets.
Many herp keepers enjoy observing reptiles and amphibians in their natural habitats.
