longest

adverb
for the greatest duration
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋɡəst / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋɡɪst / (BrE)
for the most time; for the greatest duration compared to others.

Examples

Held breath longest

She managed to stay awake the longest during the overnight study session.
This train takes the longest to reach its destination.
