order Isospondyli

noun
former order of primitive teleost fishes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔr dər ˌaɪ soʊˈspɒn dɪlaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɔː də ˌaɪ səʊˈspɒn dɪlaɪ / (BrE)
