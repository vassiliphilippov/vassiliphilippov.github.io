pattern

noun
regular way of behaving or operating
Pronunciation: / ˈpætərn / (AmE), / ˈpætən / (BrE)
a regular or characteristic way in which something is done or occurs, especially as a repeated or habitual behavior

Examples

A sleep pattern
Many animals have a migration pattern that repeats each year.
She noticed a distinct pattern in his behavior over the past few weeks.
The study tracks changes in the pattern of sleep among teenagers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: habit - a regular tendency or practice, especially one that is hard to give up
  • synonym: routine - a sequence of actions regularly followed
