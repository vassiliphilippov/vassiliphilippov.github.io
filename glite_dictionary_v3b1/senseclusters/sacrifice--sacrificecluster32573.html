sacrifice

noun
ritual killing as religious offering
Pronunciation: / ˈsækrəˌfaɪs / (AmE), / ˈsækrɪfʌɪs / (BrE)
the act of killing an animal or person as an offering to a deity, often as part of a religious ceremony

Examples

A sacrifice ceremony
Historical records describe a sacrifice involving a white bull during the festival.
In some cultures, a sacrifice was believed to secure a good harvest.
The ancient temple was the site of an annual sacrifice to honor the gods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offering - something presented to a deity as a religious act
