Hibiscus rosa-sinensis

noun
showy Asiatic shrub with red flowers
Pronunciation: / haɪˈbɪskəs ˌroʊsə sɪˈnɛnsɪs / (AmE), / hɪˈbɪskəs ˌrəʊsə sɪˈnɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A tropical Asian shrub or small tree with large, showy red to deep-red flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental plant and known as the China rose.

Examples

The Hibiscus rosa-sinensis flower
Bright red blossoms of the Hibiscus rosa-sinensis attract butterflies and hummingbirds.
She potted a Hibiscus rosa-sinensis on her balcony to add a touch of color.
The Hibiscus rosa-sinensis is often grown as a decorative hedge in tropical gardens.
