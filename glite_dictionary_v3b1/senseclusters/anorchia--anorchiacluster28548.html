anorchia

noun
absence of one or both testes
Pronunciation: / əˈnɔrkiə / (AmE), / əˈnɔːkiə / (BrE)
a medical condition in which one or both testes are absent, either congenitally or as a result of loss

Examples

Anorchia medical condition
Advances in hormone therapy help individuals affected by anorchia lead healthy lives.
Congenital anorchia is a rare condition detected soon after birth.
The patient was diagnosed with anorchia after medical imaging showed an absence of testes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorchism - the condition of having no testes
