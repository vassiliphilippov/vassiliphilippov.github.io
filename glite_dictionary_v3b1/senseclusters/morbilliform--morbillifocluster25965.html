morbilliform

adjective
measles-like rash (medical)
8.7 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmɔːr.bɪl.ɪ.fɔːrm/ /; / /ˈmɔːr.bɪl.ə.fɔːrm/ (AmE), /ˈmɔː.bɪl.ɪ.fɔːm/ /; / /ˈmɔː.bɪl.ə.fɔːm/ (BrE)
(of a rash) resembling that seen in measles, typically consisting of red macules and papules.

Examples

Morbilliform rash
A morbilliform eruption can be caused by viral infections as well as allergic reactions to drugs.
Doctors noted the morbilliform appearance of the skin during examination.
The patient developed a morbilliform rash on her chest and back.
