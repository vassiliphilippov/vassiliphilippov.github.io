chorus

noun
simultaneous group utterance
3.2 4.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îÀêr…ôs / (AmE), / Ààk…îÀêr…ôs / (BrE)
a simultaneous utterance by a group of people, especially as a response or expression of feeling

Examples

A chorus of voices
A chorus of voices rose up in protest against the decision.
The announcement was met with a chorus of cheers from the crowd.
There was a chorus of laughter when the comedian told the joke.

Synonyms

  • unison
    2.8 10/m
    unison means people speak or act exactly at the same time, while chorus is a group utterance that expresses feeling together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shout - a loud cry or call
