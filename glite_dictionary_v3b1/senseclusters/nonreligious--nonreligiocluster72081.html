nonreligious

adjective
not related to religion
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
not connected to, influenced by, or practicing any religion

Examples

Nonreligious ceremony
They wanted a nonreligious wedding ceremony that focused on their personal values.
The school offers both religious and nonreligious holiday celebrations for students.
Her views are completely nonreligious, and she prefers to keep discussions secular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: religious - connected with or believing in a religion
  • synonym: secular - not connected with religious matters
