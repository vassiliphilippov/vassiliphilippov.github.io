inspissation

noun
thickening by dehydration
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌspɪˈseɪʃən /; / ˌɪnspəˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪnˌspɪˈseɪʃən /; / ˌɪnspɪˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming thick or more viscous, especially by removing water

Examples

The inspissation of mucus in the airways can complicate respiratory diseases.

In certain diseases, mucus inspissation can block airways and complicate breathing.
The inspissation of the solution was achieved by gentle heating.
