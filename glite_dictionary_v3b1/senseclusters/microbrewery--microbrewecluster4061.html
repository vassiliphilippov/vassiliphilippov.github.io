microbrewery

noun
a small-scale brewery
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruəri / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌbrʊəri / (BrE)
a small brewery that produces limited quantities of beer, typically for distribution outside the brewery rather than for on-site consumption

Examples

A microbrewery business
Many microbreweries prioritize quality and experimentation over mass production.
She loves visiting every microbrewery she can find while traveling through different cities.
The new microbrewery in town has quickly gained a loyal following for its unique craft beers.
