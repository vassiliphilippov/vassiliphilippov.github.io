rescreening

noun
replacing mesh screen
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈskriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈskriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of fitting or replacing the mesh screen on a window, door, or porch

Examples

Porch rescreening
After the storm, the family scheduled a rescreening of their porch to replace the damaged mesh.
The handyman offered rescreening services for windows that had holes or tears.
Spring is a popular time for rescreening doors to keep insects out of the house.

  • synonym: repair - the act of fixing or restoring something
