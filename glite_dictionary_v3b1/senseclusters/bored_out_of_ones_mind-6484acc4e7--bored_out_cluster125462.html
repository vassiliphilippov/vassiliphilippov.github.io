bored out of one's mind

adjective
extremely bored
1.7
Pronunciation: / bɔrd aʊt ʌv wʌnz maɪnd / (AmE), / bɔːd aʊt ɒv wʌnz maɪnd / (BrE)
extremely bored; having nothing to interest or engage oneself to the point of frustration or restlessness.

Examples

To be bored out of my mind
If I have to watch that movie again, I'll be bored out of my mind.
She was bored out of her mind during the three-hour meeting.
The rain kept us inside all day, and the kids were bored out of their minds.
