lightman

noun
street lamp lighter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtmən / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtmən / (BrE)
a person whose job was to light and maintain public street lamps before the use of electric lighting

Examples

Street lightman
In the 19th century, a lightman would walk the streets at dusk to illuminate the city.
The lightman carried a long pole to reach the tops of the gas lamps.
With the arrival of electric lights, the job of the lightman gradually disappeared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lamplighter - a person employed to light and maintain street lamps
