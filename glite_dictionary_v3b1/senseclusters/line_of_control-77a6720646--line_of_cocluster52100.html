Line of Control

proper noun
boundary between Indian- and Pakistani-controlled Kashmir
8.1
Pronunciation: / laɪn əv kənˈtroʊl / (AmE), / laɪn əv kənˈtrəʊl / (BrE)
the military control line that separates the Indian- and Pakistani-controlled parts of the former princely state of Jammu and Kashmir.
