Av

noun
a month in the Jewish calendar (July–August)
Pronunciation: / ɑːv / (AmE), / ɑːv / (BrE)
The eleventh month of the civil year and the fifth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, occurring in July and August.

Examples

The month Av
Many Jewish communities commemorate historical tragedies during Av.
The month of Av usually falls in midsummer on the Gregorian calendar.
Tisha B'Av is observed on the ninth day of Av each year.
