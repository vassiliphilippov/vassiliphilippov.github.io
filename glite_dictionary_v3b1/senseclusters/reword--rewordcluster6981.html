Synonyms

  • rephrase
    3.7 1/m
    rephrase uses different words to make an idea clearer, while reword simply means changing the wording of the same message.
  • paraphrase
    4.1 5/m
    paraphrase puts spoken or written ideas into new words to explain them, while reword means changing wording without changing meaning.
  • word it differently
    4.4 0.01/m
    word it differently uses different phrasing to say the same idea, while reword is a general verb for changing wording.
  • restate
    3.4 2/m
    restate says something again, often using similar words for emphasis, while reword focuses on changing the words.
