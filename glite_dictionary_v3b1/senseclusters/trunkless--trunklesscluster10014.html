trunkless

adjective
without a rear trunk
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌŋkləs / (AmE), / ˈtrʌŋkləs / (BrE)
lacking a trunk or storage compartment at the rear, especially in reference to an automobile.

Examples

Trunkless car design
They opted for a trunkless hatchback for easier loading in the narrow city streets.
The new electric model is trunkless, offering more interior space for passengers.
Some drivers prefer a trunkless design because it allows for flexible cargo arrangements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatchback - a car with a door across the full width at the back end that opens upward
  • antonym: sedan - a car with a separate trunk at the rear
