lacteal

noun
a lymphatic vessel in the small intestine
Pronunciation: / ˈlæk.ti.əl / (AmE), / ˈlæk.ti.əl / (BrE)
any of the lymphatic vessels in the small intestine that absorb and convey chyle to the thoracic duct

Examples

Intestinal lacteal vessel
Chylomicrons enter the lacteal of each intestinal villus during digestion.
Damage to the lacteals can impair the body's ability to absorb nutrients properly.
The lacteals play a crucial role in the absorption of dietary fats in the small intestine.

  • synonym: lymphatic vessel - a vessel of the lymphatic system
