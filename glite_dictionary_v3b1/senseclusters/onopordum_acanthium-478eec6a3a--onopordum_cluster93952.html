Onopordum acanthium

noun
a Eurasian thistle with pale purple flowers (Scotch thistle)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊnəˈpɔrdəm əˈkænθiəm / (AmE), / ˌɒnəˈpɔːdəm əˈkanθɪəm / (BrE)
A tall biennial thistle native to Eurasia with white-hairy stems and leaves, and pale purple flowers; commonly called Scotch thistle and naturalized in North America.

Examples

Onopordum acanthium plant
Onopordum acanthium often grows over two meters tall in favorable conditions.
