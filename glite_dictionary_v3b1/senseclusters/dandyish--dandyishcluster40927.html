dandyish

adjective
affectedly elegant or showy in appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈdændiɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈdændɪɪʃ / (BrE)
Having or affecting extreme elegance and style in dress and manners, in a way that may seem self-conscious or showy.

Examples

Dandyish style
Even in casual settings, his dandyish attention to detail set him apart.
He arrived at the party looking particularly dandyish in his tailored suit and silk cravat.
Her dandyish airs amused everyone at the otherwise informal gathering.
