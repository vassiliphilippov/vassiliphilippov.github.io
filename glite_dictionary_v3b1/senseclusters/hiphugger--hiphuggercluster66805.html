hiphugger

noun
low-rise clothing
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpˌhʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈhɪpˌhʌɡə / (BrE)
a style of pants or skirt designed to sit low on the hips rather than at the waist

Examples

A pair of hiphuggers
She bought a new pair of hiphuggers for the summer festival.
In the 1970s, hiphuggers became a popular fashion trend among young people.
The store displays several styles of hiphuggers in different colors and fabrics.
