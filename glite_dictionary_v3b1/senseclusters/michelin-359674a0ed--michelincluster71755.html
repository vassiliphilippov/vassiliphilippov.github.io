Michelin

adjective
restaurant rating
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪʃəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈmɪʃəlɪn / (BrE)
relating to the Michelin Guide or indicating that a restaurant has been awarded a Michelin star for excellence.

Examples

Michelin star restaurant
They celebrated at a Michelin restaurant known for its exceptional cuisine.
The chef was proud to receive a Michelin star for his innovative dishes.
Many food critics consider a Michelin rating the highest honor in the culinary world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starred - awarded a star for quality, especially in a guide or review
