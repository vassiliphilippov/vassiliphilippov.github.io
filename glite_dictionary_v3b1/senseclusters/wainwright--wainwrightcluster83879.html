wainwright

noun
wagon or cart maker
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪnˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈweɪnˌraɪt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or repair wagons and carts

Examples

A wainwright makes wagons
His grandfather was a skilled wainwright who crafted beautiful wooden wagons by hand.
In the 19th century, every town needed a good wainwright to keep its transportation running.
The village wainwright repaired the farmer’s broken cart quickly and efficiently.
