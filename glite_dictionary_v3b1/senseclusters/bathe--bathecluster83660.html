bathe

noun
act of swimming or bathing for pleasure
Pronunciation: / beɪð / (AmE), / beɪð / (BrE)
An act of swimming or bathing for pleasure or recreation, especially in the sea or a pool.

Examples

A bathe in the sea
After a long walk, they enjoyed a refreshing bathe on the beach.
He went for a quick bathe in the chilly lake before breakfast.
You mustn't swim after eating, but a gentle bathe is fine, said the instructor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swim - the act of swimming, especially as recreation
