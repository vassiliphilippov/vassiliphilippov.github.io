pocket money

noun
money for small personal expenses, esp. for children
1.0 1.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɑːk.ət ˈmʌn.ɪ/ /; / /ˈpɑːk.ɪt ˈmʌn.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpɒk.ɪt ˈmʌn.ɪ/ /; / /ˈpɒk.ət ˈmʌn.ɪ/ (BrE)
