luck out

verb
to succeed or have good fortune by chance
5.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /lʌk aʊt/ (AmE), /lʌk aʊt/ (BrE)
to have good luck or succeed by chance, especially unexpectedly

Examples

To luck out big

She lucked out and found her lost keys at the park.
