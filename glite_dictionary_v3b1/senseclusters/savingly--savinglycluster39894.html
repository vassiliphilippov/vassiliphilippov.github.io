savingly

adverb
in a sparing way
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that saves resources or avoids waste; sparingly or economically

Examples

Use savingly with water
She used the expensive perfume savingly to make it last longer.
During the drought, water was distributed savingly to ensure everyone had enough.
He spent his money savingly, always looking for the best deals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lavishly - in a generous or extravagant manner
  • synonym: sparingly - in a restricted or moderate manner
