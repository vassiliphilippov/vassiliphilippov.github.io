apocalyptic

adjective
relating to the end of the world
5.4 6.9
Pronunciation: /ə.pɑː.kə.ˈlɪp.tɪk/ (AmE), /ə.pɒ.kə.ˈlɪp.tɪk/ (BrE)

Examples
