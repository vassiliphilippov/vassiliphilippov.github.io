apocalyptic

adjective
relating to the end of the world
5.4
Pronunciation: / əˌpɑːkəˈlɪptɪk /; / əˌpɑːkəˈlɪktɪk / (AmE), / əˌpɒkəˈlɪptɪk /; / əˌpɒkəˈlɪktɪk / (BrE)
relating to or describing the end of the world or great destruction; resembling an apocalypse

Examples

An apocalyptic event
News of the disaster spread quickly, with some calling it an apocalyptic event.
The movie depicts an apocalyptic future where humanity struggles to survive.
The sky turned a strange red during the storm, creating an almost apocalyptic atmosphere.
Many films depict the future in an apocalyptic manner, showing the collapse of civilization.
She described the potential consequences of climate change in apocalyptic terms.
The sky turned an apocalyptic shade of red as the wildfire spread across the hills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catastrophic - involving or causing sudden great damage or suffering
  • antonym: hopeful - giving hope for the future; optimistic
