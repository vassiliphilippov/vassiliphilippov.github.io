tela

noun
layer of tissue
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlə / (AmE), / ˈtiːlə / (BrE)
a thin layer or sheet of tissue in the body, especially used in anatomical descriptions

Examples

Tela of tissue
The surgeon carefully separated the tela during the procedure.
In anatomy class, we learned that the tela forms a barrier between different organs.
Damage to the tela can affect the function of the underlying tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: layer - a sheet or thickness of material, typically one of several
  • synonym: membrane - a thin layer of tissue covering a surface or dividing a space
