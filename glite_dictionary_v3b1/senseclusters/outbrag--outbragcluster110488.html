outbrag

verb
to boast more
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈbræɡ / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈbræɡ / (BrE)
to boast or brag more than someone else

Examples

To outbrag a friend
He always tries to outbrag his colleagues by telling bigger stories.
During the reunion, everyone attempted to outbrag each other with tales of success.
She did not want to outbrag her friends, so she kept her achievements quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brag - to talk with too much pride about achievements
  • synonym: outdo - to do better than someone else
  • antonym: understate - to describe something as less important than it is
