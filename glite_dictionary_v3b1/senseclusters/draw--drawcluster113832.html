draw

verb
finish a game with an equal score
2.4
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
(of two teams or competitors) to finish a game or contest with an equal score; to tie.

Examples

Teams draw 1-1
England and Germany drew 1-1 in the group stage.
If both players draw, the trophy will be shared.
The two teams drew after a hard-fought match in the finals.
