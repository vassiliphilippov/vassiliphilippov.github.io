bis

noun
encore performance
Pronunciation: / bɪs / (AmE), / bɪs / (BrE)
an additional performance or encore, especially one requested by the audience at a concert or show

Examples

A bis performance
After the final song, the crowd cheered for a bis from the band.
The pianist graciously returned to the stage for a bis at the audience's request.
It is common in classical concerts for listeners to call out bis after an outstanding performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encore - an extra performance given in response to audience demand
