jackbooted

adjective
brutally authoritarian
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒækˌbuːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdʒækˌbuːtɪd / (BrE)
using harsh, authoritarian, or brutally oppressive methods, especially by a government or authority.

Examples

Jackbooted government actions
The new laws were criticized as jackbooted attempts to silence dissent.
Many feared the jackbooted tactics used by the regime to control the population.
Activists spoke out against the jackbooted response to peaceful demonstrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarian - favoring strict obedience to authority
  • antonym: lenient - not strict or harsh
  • synonym: oppressive - unjustly harsh or tyrannical
