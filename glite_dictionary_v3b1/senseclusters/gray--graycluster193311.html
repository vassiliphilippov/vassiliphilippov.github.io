gray

noun
SI unit of absorbed radiation dose (Gy)
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ / (BrE)
The SI unit of absorbed radiation dose, equivalent to the absorption of one joule of radiation energy per kilogram of matter; symbol Gy.
