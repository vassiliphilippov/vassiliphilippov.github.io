forgivingness

noun
quality of being forgiving
Pronunciation: / fərˈɡɪvɪŋnəs / (AmE), / fəˈɡɪvɪŋnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to forgive others or overlook faults

Examples

The forgivingness of people
A society built on forgivingness is often more peaceful and harmonious.
Her forgivingness allowed her to mend broken relationships quickly.
Many admire his forgivingness even after repeated betrayals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forgiveness - the act of forgiving or state of being forgiven
  • synonym: mercifulness - the quality of being merciful
  • antonym: vindictiveness - the quality of seeking revenge or holding grudges
