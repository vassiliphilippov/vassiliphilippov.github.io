Antilope cervicapra

noun
Indian antelope with spiral horns
Pronunciation: / ænˈtaɪloʊp sərˌvɪkɑːprə / (AmE), / ænˈtɪləʊp səːˌvɪkɑːprə / (BrE)
a species of antelope native to India, known for the male's dark back and long spiral horns; commonly called the blackbuck

Examples
