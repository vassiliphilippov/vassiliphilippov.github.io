slave driver

noun
very harsh, demanding employer
3.3
Pronunciation: / sleɪv ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / sleɪv ˈdrʌɪvə /; / sleɪv ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who makes others work very hard, often in a harsh or oppressive way

Examples

A slave driver boss
He quit his job because he couldn't stand working for a slave driver any longer.
My friends warned me that their coach was a slave driver during training sessions.
The new manager is a real slave driver, expecting us to work late every night.
He acted like a slave driver, giving his workers no rest.
History books describe the cruelty of the slave driver on the plantation.
The slave driver demanded that everyone work from sunrise to sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overseer - a person who supervises others, especially workers
  • synonym: taskmaster - a person who imposes hard or burdensome work
  • synonym: tyrant - a cruel and oppressive ruler or employer
