trend line

noun
line showing pattern in data
1.0 3.0
Pronunciation: /trend laɪn/ (AmE), /trend laɪn/ (BrE)
A line drawn on a graph to indicate the general direction or pattern of a set of statistical data points.

Examples

A trend line on graph
Adding a trend line to the chart helped highlight the overall direction of the market.
The graph shows a clear upward trend line in sales over the past five years.
To analyze the pattern, she drew a trend line through the fluctuating data points.

Related Terms

  • synonym: line of best fit - a straight line drawn through points to show their trend
