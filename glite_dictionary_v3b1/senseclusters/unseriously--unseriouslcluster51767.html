unseriously

adverb
not seriously
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪriəsli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪəriəsli / (BrE)
in a way that is not serious or earnest

Examples

Talk unseriously
She spoke unseriously about her plans, so no one believed she would actually do it.
The students answered the teacher's question unseriously, making the whole class laugh.
He often responds unseriously to important topics, which can be frustrating in meetings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jokingly - in a humorous or playful way
  • synonym: lightly - in a way that is not serious or intense
  • antonym: seriously - in a serious or earnest way
