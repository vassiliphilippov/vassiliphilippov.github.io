gunslinging

adjective
using guns boldly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌslɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
involving or characteristic of using or carrying guns in a bold, daring, or dangerous manner, like a gunslinger

Examples

Gunslinging hero
The movie features a gunslinging hero who never backs down from a fight.
She became famous for her gunslinging style in the action-packed television series.
The gunslinging outlaws were feared throughout the Wild West.

Related Terms

  • synonym: armed - carrying weapons, especially guns
  • antonym: unarmed - not carrying weapons
