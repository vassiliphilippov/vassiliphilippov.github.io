telework

noun
remote work practice
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌwɜːk / (BrE)
the practice of working from a location outside the traditional office, usually using computers and telecommunications

Examples

Remote telework
Many employees prefer telework because it allows for a better work-life balance.
The company expanded its telework options after the success of remote projects.
Advances in technology have made telework possible for a wide range of professions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: office work - work performed at a company's physical office
  • synonym: telecommuting - working from home using electronic communication
