cokehead

noun
Coca-Cola enthusiast
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊkˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊkˌhɛd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who drinks a lot of Coca-Cola or is very enthusiastic about it

Examples

A cokehead with soda
My brother is such a cokehead that he always has a can of soda in his hand.
At every party, the cokehead in our group brings a cooler full of Coca-Cola.
She joked that being a cokehead was her only bad habit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cola fan - a person who likes cola drinks
