Kota

noun
member of Dravidian people of southern India
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊtə / (AmE), / ˈkəʊtə / (BrE)
A member of a Dravidian-speaking ethnic group indigenous to the Nilgiri Hills in southern India.

Examples

Kota people
A celebration was organized by the Kota community to preserve their traditional crafts.
Anthropologists have studied the social structure of the Kota for over a century.
The Kota have a distinctive culture and language within the Nilgiri region.
