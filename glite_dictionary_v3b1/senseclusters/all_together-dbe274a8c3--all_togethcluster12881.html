Synonyms

  • collectively

    collectively is used when people act as one unit, while all together often means everyone is in the same place or time.
  • together
    1.0 300/m
    together means being with other people in one place, while all together stresses that everyone is present or happening at the same time.
  • as a group
    2.3 3/m
    as a group shows people acting or being seen as one unit, while all together often means everyone is in the same place or time.
  • en masse
    5.6 1/m
    en masse means a large group acting together at once, while all together is a neutral phrase for everyone at the same time or place.
  • en bloc
    8.3 0.01/m

  • all at once
