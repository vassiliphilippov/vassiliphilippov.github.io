Synonyms

  • together
    1.0 215/m

  • as a group
    2.3 6.09/m
    as a group shows people acting or being seen as one unit, while all together often means everyone is in the same place or time.
  • en masse
    5.6 2.08/m

  • en bloc
    8.3 0.03/m

  • all at once
