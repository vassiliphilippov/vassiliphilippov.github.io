Louis XVI

proper noun
king of France, executed in French Revolution
Pronunciation: / luˈi sɪksˈtinθ / (AmE), / ˈluːi sɪksˈtiːnθ / (BrE)
the king of France from 1774 to 1792, whose reign ended with the French Revolution and who was executed by guillotine

Examples

Louis XVI king of France
Louis XVI was overthrown during the French Revolution in 1792.
Many history books detail the trial and execution of Louis XVI.
The reign of Louis XVI marked a turning point in European history.
