strand

noun
a twisted fiber or thread in rope or cable
Pronunciation: / strænd / (AmE), / strænd / (BrE)
a single thin length of fiber, thread, hair, or similar material

Examples

A strand of hair
She carefully separated a single strand of hair from the bundle.
The cable's strength depends on the number of strands inside it.
The rope was made from several thick strands twisted together.
A strand of silk glistened in the sunlight.
She found a single strand of hair on her jacket.
The cable is made up of several metal strands twisted together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fiber - a thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed
  • synonym: filament - a slender threadlike object or fiber, especially found in animals or plants
  • synonym: thread - a long, thin strand of cotton, nylon, or other fibers used in sewing or weaving
