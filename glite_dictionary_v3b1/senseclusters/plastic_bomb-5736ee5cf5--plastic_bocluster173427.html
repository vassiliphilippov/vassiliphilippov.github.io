plastic bomb

noun
bomb made of plastic explosive
Pronunciation: / ˈplæstɪk bɑm / (AmE), / ˈplæstɪk bɒm / (BrE)
A bomb constructed using plastic explosives.

Examples

A plastic bomb
A plastic bomb can be shaped to fit into small or irregular spaces.
Police discovered a stash of plastic bombs during the raid.
The terrorists planted a plastic bomb under the politician's car.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plastic explosive - an explosive material that is malleable like putty
