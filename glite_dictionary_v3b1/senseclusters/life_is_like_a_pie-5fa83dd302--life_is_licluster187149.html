life is like a pie

x
life has many enjoyable sides
Pronunciation: / laɪf ɪz laɪk ə paɪ / (AmE), / laɪf ɪz laɪk ə paɪ / (BrE)
used to say that life has many enjoyable or diverse aspects, each to be savored separately as one would enjoy slices of a pie

Examples

Life is like a pie to enjoy
He truly believes that life is like a pie and tries to make the most of every moment.
Her grandmother often told her, 'life is like a pie; every slice has its own flavor.'
When she retired, she realized that life is like a pie, with each stage offering its own delights.
