unretired

adjective
returned from retirement
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈtaɪrd / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
no longer retired; having returned to work or active status after retirement

Examples

Unretired teacher returns
After two years of travel, he became unretired and accepted a part-time teaching job.
Many athletes have become unretired to compete in one more season.
She was happily unretired when her old company asked her to consult on a project.

  • antonym: retired - having stopped working after reaching a certain age or years of service
