apposition

noun
the grammatical relationship of two adjacent nouns
8.8 6.5
Pronunciation: /æ.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /æ.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ə.pə.ˈzɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
