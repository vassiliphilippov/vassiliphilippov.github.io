kidling

noun
young goat
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪdlɪŋ / (BrE)
a very young kid, especially a small or newborn goat

Examples

Newborn kidling
The farmer watched over the newborn kidling in the barn.
Each spring, several kidlings are born on the hillside farm.
The children were delighted to feed the tiny kidling with a bottle.
