covert meeting

noun
a secret or hidden gathering
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊvərt ˈmitɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌvət ˈmiːtɪŋ / (BrE)
a secret or hidden gathering, often for confidential or illicit purposes

Examples

A covert meeting held
A covert meeting between whistleblowers and investigators was organized in a secure location.
Reports emerged that executives held a covert meeting late at night to avoid detection.
The two diplomats arranged a covert meeting to discuss the peace proposal.
