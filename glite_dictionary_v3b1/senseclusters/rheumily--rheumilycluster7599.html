rheumily

adverb
with watery discharge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmɪli / (AmE), / ˈruːmɪli / (BrE)
in a manner related to or affected by rheum, especially with watery discharge from the eyes or nose

Examples

To blink rheumily
After the long walk in the cold, his eyes blinked rheumily as tears formed.
She sniffled rheumily during allergy season, reaching for a tissue.
The old dog looked up rheumily, its eyes moist from age.

Related Terms

  • synonym: watery - containing or resembling water, especially in discharge
