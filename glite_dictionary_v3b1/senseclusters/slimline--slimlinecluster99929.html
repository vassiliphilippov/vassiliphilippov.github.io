slimline

noun
thin product version
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪmˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈslɪmˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a version of a product that is thinner or smaller than the standard model, often with fewer features.

Examples

A slimline laptop
The new slimline is perfect for people who want a lighter laptop to carry around.
Many customers prefer the slimline because it takes up less space in the kitchen.
She bought a slimline model that fits easily into her small apartment.
