system of measurement

noun
set of units for measuring quantities
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəm əv ˈmɛʒərmənt / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm əv ˈmɛʒəmənt / (BrE)
A set of related units and standards used to quantify particular characteristics, such as length, mass, or volume.

Examples

Standard system of measurement
Scientists rely on a standard system of measurement to compare their results accurately.
Switching to a new system of measurement requires updating all technical documentation.
The metric system of measurement is used in most countries around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperial system - British-derived system of measurement using feet, pounds, etc.
  • synonym: metric system - International decimalized system of measurement based on meters, liters, and grams
