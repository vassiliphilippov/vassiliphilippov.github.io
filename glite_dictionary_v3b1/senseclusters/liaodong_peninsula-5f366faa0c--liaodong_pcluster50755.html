Liaodong Peninsula

proper noun
peninsula in northeastern China
8.3
Pronunciation: / liˌaʊˈdʊŋ pəˈnɪnsələ / (AmE), / liˌaʊˈdɒŋ pəˈnɪnsjələ / (BrE)
a large peninsula in northeastern China that extends into the Yellow Sea, between the Bohai Sea and Korea Bay

Examples

The Liaodong Peninsula
