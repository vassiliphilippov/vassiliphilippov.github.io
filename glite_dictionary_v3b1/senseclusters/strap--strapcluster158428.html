strap

verb
sharpen a blade with a strap
6.2
Pronunciation: / stræp / (AmE), / stræp / (BrE)
to sharpen a blade, especially a razor, by rubbing it on a strap of leather or similar material

Examples

To strap a razor
Barbers used to strap their razors on leather to maintain a sharp edge.
He carefully strapped his straight razor before shaving.
She watched her grandfather strap the knife, fascinated by the ritual.
