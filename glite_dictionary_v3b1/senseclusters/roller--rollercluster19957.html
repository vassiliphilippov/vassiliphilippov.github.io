roller

noun
revolving cylinder
2.0 2.17/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆroÊŠlÉ™r / (AmE), / ËˆrÉ™ÊŠlÉ™ / (BrE)
a cylinder used to move, flatten, or shape something by rolling, such as a kitchen tool or a device for curling hair

Examples

A dough roller
He used a roller to flatten the dough on the kitchen counter.
She applied a fresh coat of paint to the wall with a paint roller.
The conveyor belt was supported by several metal rollers underneath.
After removing the last roller, her hair was full of tight waves.
My grandmother used to sleep with rollers to get perfect curls for the morning.
She put a roller in each section of her hair before going to bed.

Synonyms

  • rolling pin
    1.5 0.82/m
    a rolling pin is a long kitchen cylinder used to flatten dough, while a roller is a more general cylinder for many rolling jobs.
  • curler
    2.8 0.76/m
    a curler is a small cylinder that hair wraps around to make curls, while a roller is any cylinder used to shape or move things.
