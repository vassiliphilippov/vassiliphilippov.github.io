industrialized

adjective
having developed industries
1.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈdʌ.strɪ.ə.laɪzd/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈdʌ.strɪ.ə.laɪzd/ (BrE)
(of a country or area) having developed a wide range of industries or having transitioned to an economy based largely on manufacturing and production.

Examples

Heavily industrialized region
Air quality issues are common in heavily industrialized regions.
Many industrialized nations have stricter environmental regulations than developing countries.
The city became rapidly industrialized during the early twentieth century.
Many industrialised nations have strict environmental regulations.
The gap between industrialised and developing countries has widened over time.
Urbanization is a common feature of industrialised societies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrialized - (of a country or area) made industrial; American spelling
  • antonym: undeveloped - not yet developed or industrialized
