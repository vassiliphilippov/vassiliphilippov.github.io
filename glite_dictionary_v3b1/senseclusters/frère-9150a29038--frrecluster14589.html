frère

noun
French word for brother
Pronunciation: / fʁɛʁ / (AmE), / fʁɛʁ / (BrE)
a French word for 'brother', sometimes used in English to refer to a male member of a religious order or as a stylistic term for brother.

Examples

A frère in religion
The monk greeted his companion as frère during the ceremony.
In the novel, the character was known as frère Jacques among the villagers.
She addressed the visiting priest as frère out of respect for his role.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sister - a female sibling
