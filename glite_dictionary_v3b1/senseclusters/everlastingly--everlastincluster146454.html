everlastingly

adverb
for an infinitely long time
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌɛvəˈlɑːstɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that continues for an infinitely long time; forever.

Examples

To love everlastingly
He claimed he would love her everlastingly, through all the years ahead.
She believed their friendship would last everlastingly, no matter what happened.
The river flows everlastingly to the sea, never ceasing its journey.
