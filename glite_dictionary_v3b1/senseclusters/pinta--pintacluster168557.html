pinta

noun
tropical skin disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpintə / (AmE), / ˈpintə / (BrE)
a tropical infectious skin disease that causes colored patches on the skin, mainly found in Central and South America

Examples

Pinta skin disease
The doctor diagnosed the patient with pinta after observing the skin lesions.
Public health campaigns in rural areas aim to reduce cases of pinta among children.
Treatment for pinta usually involves antibiotics to clear the infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leprosy - a chronic infectious disease causing skin lesions and nerve damage
  • synonym: yaws - a tropical infection of the skin, bones, and joints
