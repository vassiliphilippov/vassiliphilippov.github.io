palankeen

noun
covered litter carried by bearers
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpælənˈkin / (AmE), / ˌpalənˈkiːn / (BrE)
a covered litter, typically for one passenger, carried on poles by bearers, especially in South and Southeast Asia.

Examples

A palankeen carried
During the procession, the prince reclined comfortably inside his palankeen.
Historians described how noble families used palankeens when traveling long distances.
The bride was carried to the ceremony in a beautifully decorated palankeen.
