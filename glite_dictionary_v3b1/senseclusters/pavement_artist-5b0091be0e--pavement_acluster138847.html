pavement artist

noun
street artist drawing on pavements
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪvmənt ˈɑrtɪst / (AmE), / ˈpeɪvmənt ˈɑːtɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates pictures on public pavements using colored chalks, often hoping to receive money from passers-by

Examples

Pavement artist draws pictures
As a pavement artist, he relied on the generosity of strangers for his income.
Many tourists stopped to admire the work of the talented pavement artist near Covent Garden.
The pavement artist spent hours turning the grey sidewalk into a colorful masterpiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: street artist - an artist who performs or creates art in public places
