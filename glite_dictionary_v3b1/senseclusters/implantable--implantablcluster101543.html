implantable

adjective
able to be inserted
5.3
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈplæntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ɪmˈplɑːntəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be placed inside the body, especially by surgery, for medical purposes

Examples

An implantable device
The doctor recommended an implantable device to help regulate her heartbeat.
Pacemakers are a common type of implantable medical technology.
Researchers are developing new implantable sensors to monitor blood sugar levels.

Related Terms

  • antonym: removable - able to be taken out or detached
  • synonym: surgically inserted - placed into the body by surgery
