finickiness

noun
demanding precision
8.0
Pronunciation: / fɪˈnɪkinəs / (AmE), / fɪˈnɪkinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being fussy, difficult to please, or needing special care and attention

Examples

The finickiness of care
The finickiness of the old typewriter meant it often jammed if not handled gently.
Her finickiness about food made dining out a challenge for her friends.
Due to the finickiness of the process, only experienced technicians were allowed to operate the machine.
His finickiness about his clothes made shopping with him a long process.
The chef admired her finickiness when it came to selecting the freshest ingredients.
Because of his finickiness, he often sent his food back at restaurants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or precision
  • synonym: fastidiousness - the quality of being very attentive to detail or hard to please
  • synonym: fussiness - the quality of being hard to satisfy or overly particular
  • antonym: indifference - lack of concern or interest
  • synonym: meticulousness - the quality of showing great attention to detail
