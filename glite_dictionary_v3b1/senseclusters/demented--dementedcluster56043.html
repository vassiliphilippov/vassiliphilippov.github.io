demented

adjective
suffering from mental illness
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɛntɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈmɛntɪd / (BrE)
suffering from mental illness; behaving in a wild or irrational way often due to madness.

Examples

A demented laugh
His demented laughter echoed through the empty halls, alarming everyone nearby.
She dismissed his wild story as the ramblings of a demented mind.
The patient had become increasingly demented over the past few months.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insane - being seriously mentally ill
  • antonym: sane - having a healthy mind, not mentally ill
