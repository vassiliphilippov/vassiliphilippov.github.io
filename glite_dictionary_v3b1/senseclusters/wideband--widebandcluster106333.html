wideband

adjective
operating over a wide range of frequencies
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪdˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪdbænd / (BrE)
relating to or able to operate over a wide range of frequencies, especially in telecommunications and electronics.

Examples

A wideband amplifier
Engineers used a wideband amplifier to test the equipment.
Our lab is equipped with several wideband antennas for research purposes.
The new radio supports wideband communication for improved audio quality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadband - having or relating to a wide bandwidth
  • antonym: narrowband - operating over a narrow range of frequencies
