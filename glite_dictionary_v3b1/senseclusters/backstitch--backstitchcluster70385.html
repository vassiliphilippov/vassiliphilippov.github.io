backstitch

verb
to sew with a strong, overlapping stitch
6.6 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.stɪtʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.stɪtʃ/ (BrE)
to sew using a stitch in which the needle is always brought up behind the previous stitch, creating a strong, continuous line

To backstitch a seam
