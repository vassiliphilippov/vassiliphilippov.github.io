ballsy decision

noun
a bold or risky choice (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔlzi dɪˈsɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈbɔːlzi dɪˈsɪʒən / (BrE)
an informal term for a choice that is notably bold, daring, or risky

Examples

A ballsy decision
Her ballsy decision to invest everything in the startup surprised everyone.
Making a ballsy decision can set you apart from your competitors.
The team's ballsy decision to go for it on fourth down paid off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bold decision - a choice requiring bravery or confidence
  • antonym: cautious decision - a careful or risk-averse choice
