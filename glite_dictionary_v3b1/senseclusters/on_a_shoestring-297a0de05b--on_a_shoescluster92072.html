on a shoestring

adverb
with very little money
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə ˈʃuˌstrɪŋ / (AmE), / ɒn ə ˈʃuːstrɪŋ / (BrE)
using very little money or with only a very small budget.

Examples

On a shoestring budget
Despite launching the company on a shoestring, they quickly found loyal customers.
She traveled across Europe on a shoestring by staying in hostels and eating street food.
The independent film was made on a shoestring but received critical acclaim.
