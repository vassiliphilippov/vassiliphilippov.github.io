nonresponsive

adjective
not reacting
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.rɪˈspɑnsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒnsɪv / (BrE)
not reacting or replying to a stimulus, request, or question

Examples

Nonresponsive patient
The patient remained nonresponsive despite repeated attempts to wake him.
Her email was nonresponsive to the specific questions I asked.
The device became nonresponsive after the software update and had to be restarted.

Related Terms

  • antonym: responsive - reacting quickly and positively
  • synonym: unresponsive - not reacting to something or someone
