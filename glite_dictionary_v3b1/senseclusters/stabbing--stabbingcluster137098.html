stabbing

noun
knife attack
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstæbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstæbɪŋ / (BrE)
an attack in which someone is wounded or killed with a sharp object such as a knife

Examples

A stabbing attack
The city has seen a rise in stabbings over the past year.
Police responded quickly to reports of a stabbing outside the train station.
After the stabbing, the victim was rushed to the hospital for emergency treatment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assault - a physical attack on someone
  • antonym: shooting - an attack in which someone is wounded or killed with a gun
