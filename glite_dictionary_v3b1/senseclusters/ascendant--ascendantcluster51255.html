ascendant

noun
state of dominance or control
3.4
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛndənt / (AmE), / əˈsɛndənt / (BrE)
a position of dominance, influence, or control; the state of being dominant over others

Examples

With the rise of technology, digital media has become the ascendant force in communication.
