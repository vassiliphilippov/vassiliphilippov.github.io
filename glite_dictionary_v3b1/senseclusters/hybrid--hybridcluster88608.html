hybrid

noun
thing made from mixed origins
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.brɪd / (AmE), / ˈhʌɪ.brɪd / (BrE)
Something made by combining two or more different elements or sources of origin.

Examples

A hybrid style
Her apartment's design is a hybrid of modern and traditional styles.
The committee's decision was a hybrid of several proposals, combining their strongest features.
The new festival is a hybrid of music, art, and technology exhibitions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blend - a mixture of different things or qualities
  • synonym: composite - something made up of various parts or elements
