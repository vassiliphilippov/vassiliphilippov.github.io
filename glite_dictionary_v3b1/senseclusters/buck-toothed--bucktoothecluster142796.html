buck-toothed

adjective
having prominent upper front teeth
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌtuːθt / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌtuːθt / (BrE)
Having upper front teeth that project noticeably forward.

Examples

Buck-toothed smile
Despite being buck-toothed, she had a charming and confident personality.
He was a buck-toothed child who often felt self-conscious about his smile.
The cartoon character is famous for its buck-toothed grin.
