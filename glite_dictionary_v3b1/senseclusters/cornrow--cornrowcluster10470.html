cornrow

verb
to braid in rows
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnˌrəʊ / (BrE)
to braid hair into narrow, tight rows that lie flat against the scalp, typically in straight lines.

Examples

To cornrow hair
She decided to cornrow her hair before going on vacation to make it easier to manage.
The stylist can cornrow your hair in different patterns depending on your preference.
He learned how to cornrow his sister's hair by watching online tutorials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: braid - to weave three or more strands of hair together
  • antonym: unbraid - to undo or loosen braided hair
