evening

noun
polite greeting or farewell at night
Pronunciation: / ˈiv.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːv.nɪŋ / (BrE)
used as a polite expression for greeting or taking leave in the evening, often as 'Good evening'.

Examples

Polite 'evening'
He waved goodbye with a cheerful 'evening' before heading home.
I nodded a quick 'evening' to my neighbors on the porch.
She smiled and said 'evening' as she entered the room.
