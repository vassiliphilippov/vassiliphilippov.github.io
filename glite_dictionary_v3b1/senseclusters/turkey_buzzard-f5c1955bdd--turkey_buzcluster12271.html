turkey buzzard

noun
a large vulture native to the Americas
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːki ˌbʌzɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːki ˌbʌzəd / (BrE)
a large New World vulture (Cathartes aura) found in the Americas, known for its bald red head and habit of feeding on carrion

Examples

The turkey buzzard flies
Turkey buzzards are often seen cleaning up roadkill along rural highways.
A turkey buzzard soared over the fields in wide, lazy circles.
The farmer recognized the turkey buzzard by its bald red head and broad wings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turkey vulture - another common name for Cathartes aura
