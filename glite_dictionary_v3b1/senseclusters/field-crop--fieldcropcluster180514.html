field-crop

adjective
relating to field-grown crops
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌkrɑp / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌkrɒp / (BrE)
Relating to crops that are grown on fields, especially as opposed to those grown in gardens or greenhouses.

Examples

Field-crop farming
He specializes in field-crop management and consults with farms across the region.
The research focused on improving field-crop yields under changing climate conditions.
Their agricultural supply center stocks a variety of field-crop seeds for local farmers.
