bogman

noun
peat cutter
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɒɡˌmæn / (BrE)
a person who cuts or collects peat from a bog, especially for use as fuel

Examples

Bogman cutting peat
The bogman spent long hours in the wet fields gathering peat for the village.
Every spring, the local bogman would begin preparing his tools for the harvest.
Stories about the old bogman were often told around the fireplace on cold nights.

Related Terms
