unwoke policy

noun
insensitive or outdated social policy
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwoʊk ˈpɑːləsi / (AmE), / ʌnˈwəʊk ˈpɒləsi / (BrE)
a rule, practice, or approach considered outdated or insensitive to social and cultural issues, especially regarding equality or inclusion

Examples

The unwoke policy
Many activists campaigned against the company's unwoke policy on employee rights.
The school board was accused of maintaining an unwoke policy that ignored harassment complaints.
Voters were divided over the candidate's promise to revise former unwoke policy stances.
