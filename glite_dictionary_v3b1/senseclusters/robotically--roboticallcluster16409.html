robotically

adverb
mechanically, without emotion
Pronunciation: / roʊˈbɑːtɪkli / (AmE), / rəʊˈbɒtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is mechanical, automatic, or lacking emotion, like a robot

Examples

To speak robotically
She repeated the instructions robotically, without looking up from her notes.
The workers moved robotically through their daily routines, barely speaking to each other.
He greeted everyone robotically, as if he were programmed to do so.
She answered the interview questions robotically, without showing any enthusiasm.
Every morning, he robotically followed the same routine before leaving for work.
The students repeated the pledge robotically, barely paying attention to the words.
She answered the questions robotically, without showing any interest or emotion.
The workers moved robotically through their daily routines, barely speaking to each other.
He typed robotically at his computer, lost in thought and unaware of his surroundings.

  • synonym: mechanically - in a way that is automatic or without emotion
