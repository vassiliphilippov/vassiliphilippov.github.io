roofing paper

noun
tar-treated paper for roof waterproofing
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈruːfɪŋ ˌpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈruːfɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a heavy paper material impregnated with tar, used in roofing to provide waterproofing protection

Examples

Layer of roofing paper
The builders laid a layer of roofing paper before installing the shingles.
This type of roofing paper is designed to withstand harsh weather conditions.
You should always check the condition of the roofing paper during roof repairs.
