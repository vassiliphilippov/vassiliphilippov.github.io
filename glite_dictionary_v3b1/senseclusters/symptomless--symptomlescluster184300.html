symptomless

adjective
without symptoms of illness
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmptəm.ləs / (AmE), / ˈsɪmptəm.ləs / (BrE)
Having no symptoms of illness or disease.

Examples

Completely symptomless carrier
Many carriers of the virus are completely symptomless and unaware they have it.
Routine check-ups are important, as some conditions may be symptomless in the early stages.
The patient remained symptomless throughout the course of the infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asymptomatic - showing no symptoms of disease
  • antonym: symptomatic - showing symptoms of a disease
