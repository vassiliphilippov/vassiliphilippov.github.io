gating

noun
controlling access
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈgeɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.təŋ/ (AmE), /ˈgeɪ.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.təŋ/ (BrE)
the process of controlling or limiting access to something, often by using a physical or electronic barrier

Examples

Gating access
The security system uses gating to prevent unauthorized entry after hours.
Effective gating ensures that only employees can access sensitive areas of the facility.
Many online services use gating to limit content to registered users.
