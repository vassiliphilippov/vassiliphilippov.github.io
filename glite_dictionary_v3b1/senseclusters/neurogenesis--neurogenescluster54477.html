neurogenesis

noun
formation of new nerve cells
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs / (BrE)
the process of growth and development of nerve tissue, especially the formation of new neurons in the brain

Examples

Neurogenesis in brain
Exercise has been linked to increased neurogenesis in the hippocampus, improving memory and learning.
Recent research shows that neurogenesis can occur even in the adult brain.
Scientists study neurogenesis to understand how the nervous system repairs itself after injury.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurogenetic - relating to the origin and development of nervous tissue
