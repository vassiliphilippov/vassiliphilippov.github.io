sanctionable

adjective
liable to penalty
Pronunciation: / ˈsæŋkʃənəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈsaŋkʃənəbl̩ / (BrE)
subject to penalty or punishment according to rules or law

Examples

Sanctionable offense punished
The committee determined that the employee's conduct was sanctionable under company policy.
Failing to disclose this information is a sanctionable offense in many jurisdictions.
The lawyer argued that his client's actions were not sanctionable by the court.
