Perseus

proper noun
Greek mythological hero, slayer of Medusa
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜr.si.əs / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.sɪ.əs / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the son of Zeus who beheaded Medusa and rescued Andromeda from a sea monster.
