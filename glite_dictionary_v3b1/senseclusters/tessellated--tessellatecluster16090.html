tessellated

adjective
decorated in a mosaic pattern
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛsəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɛsɪleɪtɪd / (BrE)
decorated or covered with a pattern of small shapes (like tiles or squares) that fit together with no gaps, often forming a mosaic or checkered appearance
