bear down

verb
contract abdominal muscles during childbirth
Pronunciation: / bɛr daʊn / (AmE), / beə daʊn / (BrE)
(of a woman in labor) to contract the abdominal muscles and push, especially during childbirth, to aid delivery.

Examples

To bear down in labor
During childbirth, many women are told when and how to bear down to help deliver the baby.
She took a deep breath and began to bear down as instructed by her doctor.
The nurse encouraged her to bear down during the final stage of labor.
