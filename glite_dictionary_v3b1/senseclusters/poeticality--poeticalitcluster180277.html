poeticality

noun
poetic quality
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊɪtɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌpəʊɪtɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)

Examples

Critics praised the poeticality in his descriptions of everyday life.
