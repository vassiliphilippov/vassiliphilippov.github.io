scribbler

noun
an informal term for a journalist or writer
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪblɚ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪblə / (BrE)
an informal, often mildly derogatory term for a journalist or writer, especially one considered to lack skill or importance

Examples

A newspaper scribbler
As a seasoned scribbler, she knew how to craft a catchy headline.
He started his career as a young scribbler in a small-town newspaper.
The famous novelist dismissed critics as mere scribblers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journalist - a person who writes for newspapers, magazines, or news websites
