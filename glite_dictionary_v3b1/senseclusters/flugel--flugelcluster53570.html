flugel

noun
brass instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɡəl / (AmE), / ˈfluːɡəl / (BrE)
a brass musical instrument, short for flugelhorn, similar to a trumpet but with a wider bore and a mellower sound

Examples

Jazz flugel solo
He played a beautiful solo on his flugel during the jazz concert.
The mellow sound of the flugel added warmth to the brass ensemble.
She prefers the flugel over the trumpet for its softer tone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flugelhorn - a brass instrument with a wide, conical bore and mellow tone
  • synonym: trombone - a brass instrument with a telescoping slide
  • synonym: trumpet - a brass instrument with a bright, piercing sound
