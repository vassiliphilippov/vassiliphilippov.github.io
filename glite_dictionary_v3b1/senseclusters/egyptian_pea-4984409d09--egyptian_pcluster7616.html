Egyptian pea

noun
edible legume, also called chickpea
5.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən piː / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən piː / (BrE)
An annual Asiatic herb (Cicer arietinum) cultivated for its short pods containing one or two edible seeds, also known as the chickpea.

Examples

Cooked Egyptian pea
Dishes made with Egyptian pea are popular in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine.
She planted several rows of Egyptian pea in her garden for the summer harvest.
The Egyptian pea is valued both for its taste and nutritional benefits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chickpea - Edible round seed of Cicer arietinum, also called garbanzo bean
