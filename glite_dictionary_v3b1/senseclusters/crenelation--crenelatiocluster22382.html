crenelation

noun
the construction of battlements with gaps
7.9 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkrɛnəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkrɛnɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a defensive wall or battlement at the top of a castle or fort, with regular gaps (crenels) for shooting or observation
