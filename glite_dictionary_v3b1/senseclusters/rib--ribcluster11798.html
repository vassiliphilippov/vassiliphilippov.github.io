rib

noun
teasing or joking remark
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪb / (AmE), / rɪb / (BrE)
a teasing or joking remark, often made in a friendly way

Examples

A funny rib
He couldn't help making a little rib about my haircut at dinner.
Her ribs during the meeting kept everyone laughing.
It was just a friendly rib, nothing to get upset about.
