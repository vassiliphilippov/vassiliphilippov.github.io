numbskull

noun
foolish person
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈnʌm.skʌl/ (AmE), /ˈnʌm.skʌl/ (BrE)
a person who is foolish or not very intelligent

Examples

A numbskull person
He called his friends numbskulls after they forgot the directions.
Only a numbskull would try to fix the computer by hitting it.
The teacher was frustrated by the numbskull who never paid attention in class.
Don't be such a numbskull—read the instructions before you start.
He called his friend a numbskull after she forgot her keys again.
Only a numbskull would try to fix the computer without turning it off first.
