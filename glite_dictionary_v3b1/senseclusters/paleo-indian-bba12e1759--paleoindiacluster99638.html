Paleo-Indian

noun
early inhabitant of the Americas
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.li.oʊ ˈɪn.diː.ən/ /; / /ˈpeɪ.li.oʊ ˈɪn.dɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.li.əʊ ˈɪn.dɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈpeɪ.li.əʊ ˈɪn.diː.ən/ (BrE)

Examples
