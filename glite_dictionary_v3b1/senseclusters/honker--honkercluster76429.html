honker

noun
person who honks a car horn
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑŋkər / (AmE), / ˈhɒŋkə / (BrE)
a person who honks a car horn, especially loudly or repeatedly

Examples

Noisy honker
An impatient honker startled the pedestrians at the busy crossroads.
Nobody wanted to be stuck behind the honker in rush hour traffic.
The honker was reprimanded by the police for excessive noise.

Related Terms
