nav

noun
website navigation menu
6.2
Pronunciation: / næv / (AmE), / næv / (BrE)
the navigation menu or set of links on a website or app that allows users to move between pages or sections

Examples

Website nav menu
The new website design features a simple nav at the top of every page.
If you cannot find the contact page, check the nav for a direct link.
She customized the nav to include quick access to the most popular sections.
