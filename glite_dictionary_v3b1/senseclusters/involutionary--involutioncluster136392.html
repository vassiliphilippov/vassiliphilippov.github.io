involutionary

adjective
self-inverse in math
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnvəˈluːʃəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˌɪnvəˈluːʃənəri / (BrE)
relating to an involution in mathematics, especially a function or operation that is its own inverse.

Examples

Involutionary function
An involutionary function returns the original value when applied twice.
The concept of an involutionary mapping is important in abstract algebra.
He explained that the operation was involutionary, meaning it undoes itself.
