herbalist

noun
person using herbs for healing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɝː.bəl.ɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɜː.bəl.ɪst / (BrE)

Examples

A herbalist uses plants
Centuries ago, people would visit the local herbalist for remedies to common ailments.
She studied to become an herbalist after discovering her passion for medicinal plants.
The herbalist recommended a blend of chamomile and mint for better sleep.
