Indian rattlebox

noun
Indian plant with rattling seed pod
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈræt.əlˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈræt.əlˌbɒks / (BrE)
an erect subshrub native to India, having purple-tinged flowers and an inflated seed pod that rattles when shaken

Examples

The Indian rattlebox plant
Children played with the dried Indian rattlebox pods, shaking them to make a rattling sound.
The Indian rattlebox is often grown for its decorative, noisy seed pods.
The Indian rattlebox thrives in warm climates across the Indian subcontinent.
