jitney

noun
small public transport vehicle
9.9 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdÊ’Éªtni / (AmE), / ËˆdÊ’Éªtni / (BrE)
a small bus or car that carries passengers for a low fare, often on a flexible route, used for public transport

Examples

A jitney bus
In some cities, the jitney is a popular way for locals to travel short distances affordably.
The old jitney rattled along the crowded streets, stopping wherever passengers needed to get on or off.
We flagged down a jitney to take us to the market when the regular buses were full.

Synonyms

  • minibus
    2.7 0.27/m
    A minibus is a small passenger bus for about four to ten people, while a jitney is an informal, low-fare vehicle running flexible routes.
  • microbus
    4.1 0.08/m
    A microbus is a very small bus for a few passengers, while a jitney is an often informal, low-fare vehicle with flexible stops.
