screwy

adjective
strange or not normal; odd
Pronunciation: / ˈskru.i / (AmE), / ˈskruː.i / (BrE)
strange, odd, or not behaving in a normal way; eccentric or peculiar.

Examples

A screwy idea
Ever since he quit his job, his behavior has been really screwy.
That is the most screwy theory I've ever heard.
The lights keep flickering in a screwy way.
