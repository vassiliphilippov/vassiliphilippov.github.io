doggery

noun
cheap bar (slang)
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡəri / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡəri / (BrE)
an old slang term for a cheap, disreputable bar or drinking place

Examples

A doggery bar
He spent his evenings in a noisy doggery on the edge of town.
The old doggery was filled with smoke and laughter every night.
Locals warned travelers to avoid the doggery after dark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dive - a shabby or disreputable bar
  • synonym: saloon - a public bar or tavern, especially in the past
