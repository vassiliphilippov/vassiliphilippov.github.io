hair

noun
a very small distance or space
Pronunciation: / hɛr / (AmE), / heə / (BrE)
a very small distance or space, usually used to emphasize how close something was to happening or not happening

Examples

Missed by a hair
He missed the target by a hair, barely grazing the bullseye.
The boat avoided the rocks by a hair, thanks to the captain's quick reaction.
They won the race by a hair, with only a fraction of a second to spare.

  • synonym: whisker - a very small amount or distance
