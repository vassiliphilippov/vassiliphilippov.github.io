Coleus blumei

noun
ornamental herb with colorful leaves

Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊli.əs ˈbluːmi.aɪ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊlɪəs ˈbluːmi.aɪ / (BrE)
A perennial aromatic herb native to southeastern Asia, known for its large, brightly colored or blotched leaves and spikes of blue-violet flowers, sometimes classified in the genus Solenostemon.
