Celsius

proper noun
Swedish astronomer
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛlsiəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛlsiəs / (BrE)
the surname of Anders Celsius, a Swedish astronomer who created the Celsius temperature scale

Examples

Anders Celsius
Anders Celsius was a Swedish astronomer who introduced a new temperature scale in the 18th century.
The name Celsius is now used worldwide for the metric temperature scale.
Many scientific instruments are named after Celsius in recognition of his contributions.
