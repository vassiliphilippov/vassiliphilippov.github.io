storehouse of knowledge

noun
a place with much information
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrˌhaʊs əv ˈnɑlɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈstɔːˌhaʊs əv ˈnɒlɪdʒ / (BrE)
a place or source containing a vast amount of information or learning

Examples

A storehouse of knowledge
Many scholars regard the internet as a storehouse of knowledge available to everyone.
My grandmother was a living storehouse of knowledge about our family history.
The museum serves as a storehouse of knowledge about ancient civilizations.
