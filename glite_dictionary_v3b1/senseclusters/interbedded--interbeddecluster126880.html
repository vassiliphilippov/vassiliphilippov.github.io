interbedded

adjective
alternating layers
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈbɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈbɛdɪd / (BrE)
having layers of different materials or things arranged alternately among each other

Examples

Interbedded rock layers
The geologist examined the interbedded layers of limestone and clay in the rock formation.
Fossils were found within the interbedded sediments along the riverbank.
The mountain range features interbedded volcanic ash and sedimentary rocks.
The artist created a sculpture with interbedded pieces of wood and metal.
The report included interbedded data tables to support the main arguments.
Her speech was interbedded with personal anecdotes that made it more engaging.
