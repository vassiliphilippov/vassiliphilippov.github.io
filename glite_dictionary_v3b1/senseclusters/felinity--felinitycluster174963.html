felinity

noun
cat-like quality
8.6
Pronunciation: / fəˈlɪnəti / (AmE), / fəˈlɪnɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being like a cat, especially in grace or manner

Examples

The felinity of movement
Her movements had a certain felinity that was both graceful and silent.
The dancer's felinity was evident in her smooth, agile steps across the stage.
He admired the felinity of the animal as it leapt effortlessly onto the wall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catlike - having the characteristics of a cat
  • synonym: feline - relating to or resembling a cat
