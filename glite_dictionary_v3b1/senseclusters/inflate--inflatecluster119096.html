inflate

verb
fill with air or gas
3.6 2.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈfleɪt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈfleɪt/ (BrE)
to fill something with air or gas so that it becomes larger or rounder, or to become larger in this way

Examples

To inflate a balloon
Please inflate the air mattress before our guests arrive.
She watched as the clown began to inflate a bright red balloon.
You need a pump to easily inflate your bicycle tires.
After a few moments, the parachute began to inflate and slow the descent.
The party balloons will inflate quickly once you start blowing air into them.
When placed in water, the self-inflating raft will inflate automatically.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expand - to increase in size or volume, become larger
