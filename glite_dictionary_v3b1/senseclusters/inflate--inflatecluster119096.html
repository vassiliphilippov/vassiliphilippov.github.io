Synonyms

  • blow up
    2.7 0.58/m
    blow up means filling a balloon or tyre with air, while inflate is the general verb for making something larger with gas.
  • balloon
    4.7 1.67/m
    balloon means to grow quickly or swell, often in a figurative way, while inflate is the physical act of adding air or gas.
  • puff up
    4.4 0.26/m
    puff up means to swell slightly, often from air, liquid, or pride, while inflate means to fill with gas until it becomes larger.
