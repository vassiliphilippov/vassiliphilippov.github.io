affiliative

adjective
showing association
Pronunciation: / əˈfɪliˌeɪtɪv / (AmE), / əˈfɪliətɪv / (BrE)
relating to forming or showing an official connection, association, or alliance between organizations or groups

Examples

Affiliative agreement
The university's affiliative programs allow students to study at partner institutions abroad.
An affiliative relationship between the two companies led to new business opportunities.
The club's affiliative status with the national organization provides access to more resources.

Related Terms

  • synonym: associative - relating to association or connection
  • antonym: independent - not connected or affiliated with others
