Ethiopian banana

noun
large Ethiopian herbaceous plant with banana-like fruit
8.1
Pronunciation: / iˌθi.oʊˈpiː.ən bəˈnæn.ə / (AmE), / iˌθi.əʊˈpiː.ən bəˈnæn.ə / (BrE)
a large evergreen plant with huge paddle-shaped leaves, bearing inedible banana-like fruit, but whose young flower shoots are edible; native to Ethiopia and sometimes classified in the genus Musa

Examples

The Ethiopian banana plant
Although the fruit of the Ethiopian banana is not eaten, its leaves are used for wrapping food.
Gardeners grow the Ethiopian banana for its impressive foliage and ornamental value.
The Ethiopian banana is valued for its edible flower shoots, which are a staple food in some regions.
