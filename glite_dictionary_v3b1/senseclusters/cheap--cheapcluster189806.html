Synonyms

  • inexpensive
    1.0 4/m
    inexpensive describes a low price in a neutral way, while cheap often carries a negative idea of poor quality.
  • low-priced
    1.0 0.5/m
    low-priced describes items sold at relatively small amounts, while cheap can mean low quality as well as low price.
  • low-cost
    2.0 2/m
    low-cost describes something that needs little money to buy or run, while cheap focuses mainly on low price.
  • affordable
    2.1 14/m
    affordable means within most people's budget and is about ability to pay, while cheap only means low in price and may imply poor quality.
