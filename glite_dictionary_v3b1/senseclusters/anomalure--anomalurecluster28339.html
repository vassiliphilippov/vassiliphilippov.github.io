anomalure

noun
African gliding rodent
Pronunciation: / əˈnɑːməˌlʊr / (AmE), / əˈnɒməlʊə / (BrE)
a type of African rodent with a scaly tail, also known as a scaly-tailed squirrel, that can glide between trees

Examples

The anomalure glides
The anomalure glided from tree to tree in the rainforest.
Researchers observed a group of anomalures feeding on fruit high in the canopy.
Unlike true flying squirrels, the anomalure has a distinctive scaly tail.
