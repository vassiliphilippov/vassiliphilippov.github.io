abandon

verb
give up an idea or claim
1.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈbæn.dən/ (AmE), /ə.ˈbæn.dən/ (BrE)
to stop supporting, maintaining, or insisting on (an idea, claim, or plan)

Examples

To abandon a plan
After much debate, they finally abandoned the proposal for a new highway.
She decided to abandon her initial theory in light of the new evidence.
The company abandoned its claim to the disputed land after the court ruling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: give up - to stop trying or abandon an effort or claim
  • antonym: maintain - to continue to support or assert
  • synonym: relinquish - to voluntarily give up or cease to claim
