flax rust fungus

noun
fungus causing disease in flax plants
Pronunciation: / flæks rʌst ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / flæks rʌst ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a fungus of the species Melampsora lini that causes flax rust, a disease affecting flax plants

Examples

Flax rust fungus disease
Scientists are studying the lifecycle of the flax rust fungus to develop resistant flax varieties.
Signs of flax rust fungus include orange pustules on the leaves of infected plants.
The flax rust fungus can devastate crops if not managed properly.

Related Terms
