saddhu

noun
Hindu ascetic holy man
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːduː / (AmE), / ˈsɑːduː / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) a holy man who renounces worldly life to pursue spiritual goals, often living as an ascetic.

Examples

A saddhu man
During our visit to the temple, we saw a saddhu performing rituals by the riverbank.
Many saddhus travel across India, relying on alms for sustenance.
The saddhu sat silently beneath the banyan tree, lost in meditation.

  • synonym: ascetic - a person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention, typically for religious reasons
