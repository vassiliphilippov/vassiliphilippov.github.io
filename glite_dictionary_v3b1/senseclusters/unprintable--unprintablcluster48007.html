unprintable

adjective
not suitable to be printed, offensive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈprɪntəbl̩ /; / ʌnˈprɪntəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈprɪntəbl̩ /; / ʌnˈprɪntəbl / (BrE)
not suitable or able to be printed, especially because it is offensive, in bad taste, or for technical reasons

He made an unprintable comment that shocked everyone in the room.
