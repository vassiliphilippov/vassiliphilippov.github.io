cutaneous leishmaniasis

noun
skin disease causing ulcerative lesions
Pronunciation: / kuːˈteɪniəs liːʃˌmænɪˈaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / kjʊˈteɪnɪəs liːʃˌmænɪˈaɪəsɪs / (BrE)
A form of leishmaniasis that affects the skin, characterized by ulcerative skin lesions.

Examples

Cutaneous leishmaniasis skin disease
Effective treatment of cutaneous leishmaniasis requires prompt medical attention to prevent scarring.
The patient was diagnosed with cutaneous leishmaniasis following a trip to South America.
This region has seen an increase in cases of cutaneous leishmaniasis over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leishmaniasis - disease caused by protozoan parasites of the genus Leishmania
