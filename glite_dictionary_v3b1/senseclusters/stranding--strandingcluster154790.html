stranding

noun
twisting fibers or wires
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈstrændɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrændɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of twisting or combining fibers or wires together to form a strand or cable

Examples

Stranding of wires
The stranding of wires is essential for making flexible electrical cables.
Modern rope production relies on efficient stranding techniques to ensure strength and durability.
The engineer inspected the stranding process to guarantee the quality of the finished cable.
