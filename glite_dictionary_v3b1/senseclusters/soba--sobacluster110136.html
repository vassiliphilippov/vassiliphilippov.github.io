soba

noun
Japanese buckwheat noodle
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊbə / (AmE), / ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
a thin Japanese noodle made from buckwheat flour, often served in hot broth or chilled with dipping sauce

Examples

A bowl of soba
For dinner, she prepared a bowl of hot soba with vegetables and tofu.
Many people enjoy eating chilled soba with a dipping sauce during the summer.
He learned how to make homemade soba noodles in a Japanese cooking class.
