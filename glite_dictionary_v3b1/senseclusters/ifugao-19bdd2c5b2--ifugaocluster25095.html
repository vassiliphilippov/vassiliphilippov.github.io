Ifugao

proper noun
Philippine ethnic group
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌiːfuˈɡaʊ / (AmE), / ˌiːfuˈɡaʊ / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people of northern Luzon in the Philippines, or the region they inhabit

Examples

The Ifugao people
The Ifugao are famous for building the Banaue Rice Terraces in the mountains of Luzon.
Many Ifugao traditions and rituals are still practiced in their communities today.
She traveled to the Ifugao region to learn about its unique culture and history.
