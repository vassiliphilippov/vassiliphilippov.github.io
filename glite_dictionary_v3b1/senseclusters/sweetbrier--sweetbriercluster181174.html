sweetbrier

noun
fragrant wild rose with prickly stems
3.8 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈswitˌbraɪər / (AmE), / ˈswiːtbraɪə / (BrE)
A Eurasian wild rose with prickly stems, fragrant leaves, and bright pink flowers that are followed by scarlet hips.

Examples

The sweetbrier bush
Children picked the bright red hips from the sweetbrier bushes that dotted the hillside.
Clusters of pink sweetbrier blossoms attract bees and butterflies to this corner of the park.
The garden's sweetbrier filled the air with a lovely, subtle fragrance each spring.
