naturopathic

adjective
using natural remedies
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪtʃəroʊˈpæθɪk / (AmE), / ˌnætʃərəˈpæθɪk / (BrE)
relating to a system of medicine that uses natural remedies and therapies to promote healing

Examples

Naturopathic medicine
Many people seek naturopathic care for chronic health conditions.
The clinic offers a range of naturopathic therapies, including herbal medicine and acupuncture.
She became interested in naturopathic approaches after reading about natural healing methods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conventional - based on standard or mainstream medicine
  • synonym: holistic - considering the whole person in treatment
