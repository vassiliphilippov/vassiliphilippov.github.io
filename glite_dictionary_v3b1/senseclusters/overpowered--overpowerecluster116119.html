overpowered

adjective
too strong or effective
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈpaʊəd / (BrE)
much stronger or more effective than is fair, balanced, or expected, especially in games or competitions

Examples

An overpowered character
Many players complained that the new character was overpowered and ruined the balance of the game.
The boss in the final level felt overpowered compared to the rest of the enemies.
Some weapons in the update are so overpowered that everyone wants to use them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unbalanced - not fair or equal in strength or effectiveness
  • antonym: weak - not strong or effective
