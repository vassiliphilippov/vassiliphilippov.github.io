hop

verb
travel a short distance by plane
1.4 3.78/m
Pronunciation: / hÉ‘p / (AmE), / hÉ’p / (BrE)
to travel a short distance quickly, especially by vehicle or for a brief trip

Examples

To hop on a bus
She hopped across the border on a quick business trip.
They decided to hop from one island to another during their vacation.
We're going to hop a flight to Chicago for the weekend.
After missing the last train, he hopped a ride with a friend.
She often hops flights between New York and Boston for work.
We decided to hop a bus downtown instead of walking.

Synonyms

    5.1 0.03/m

Related Terms

  • synonym: catch - to board or get on a bus, train, etc.
  • synonym: commute - to travel regularly over some distance, such as to work
  • synonym: fly - to travel by aircraft
  • synonym: ride - to be carried or travel in a vehicle
