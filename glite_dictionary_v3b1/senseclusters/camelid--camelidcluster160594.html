camelid

adjective
of the camel family
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməlɪd / (AmE), / ˈkæməlɪd / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of animals of the family Camelidae, such as camels, llamas, or alpacas

Examples

Camelid antibody
The researchers studied a unique camelid antibody for its potential in medicine.
Wool from camelid animals like alpacas is prized for its softness and warmth.
The museum displayed several camelid artifacts from ancient South American cultures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bovine - relating to cattle or cows
  • synonym: camel - a large, humped mammal of the desert
