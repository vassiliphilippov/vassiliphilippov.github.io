florist's gloxinia

noun
houseplant with large, colored flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrɪsts ˌɡlɒkˈsɪniə / (AmE), / ˈflɒrɪsts ˌɡlɒkˈsɪniə / (BrE)
A South American herb (Sinningia speciosa) cultivated as a houseplant for its large leaves and colorful, bell-shaped flowers.
