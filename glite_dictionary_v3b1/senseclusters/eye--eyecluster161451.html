eye

noun
central part of an area (metaphorical)
Pronunciation: / aɪ / (AmE), / aɪ / (BrE)
the central part of a particular area, especially when surrounded by disturbance or activity

Examples

Eye of the storm
The climbers found themselves in the eye of the storm, where the winds suddenly died down.
The protesters gathered in the eye of the city, blocking the main square.
The rescue team reached the eye of the disaster area just as help was most needed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heart - the central or most important part of something
