leechlike

adjective
having a parasitic or exploitative nature
Pronunciation: / ˈlitʃˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈliːtʃˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Tending to behave like a leech by exploiting others or living off them; parasitic or clingy.

Examples

Leechlike behavior
He led a leechlike existence, always borrowing money from his friends and never paying it back.
She grew weary of her leechlike relatives asking for favors and money.
The leechlike attachment of the vines slowly killed the old tree.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasitic - having the characteristics of a parasite
  • antonym: self-sufficient - relying on oneself; not dependent on others
