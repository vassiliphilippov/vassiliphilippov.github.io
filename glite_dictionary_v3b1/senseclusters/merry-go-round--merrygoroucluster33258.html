merry-go-round

noun
cycle of repetitive or pointless events
7.2 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmeri ɡoʊ ˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈmɛri ɡəʊ ˌraʊnd / (BrE)
a situation or process that keeps repeating itself in a continuous, often pointless or confusing cycle

Examples

Corporate merry-go-round
After years on the corporate merry-go-round, he decided to take a break and change careers.
She grew frustrated with the merry-go-round of daily meetings that accomplished nothing.
The legal dispute became a merry-go-round of appeals and counterclaims with no end in sight.
