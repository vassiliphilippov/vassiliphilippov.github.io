let's have it

verb
ask someone to express their opinion
3.2
Pronunciation: / lɛts hæv ɪt / (AmE), / lɛts hav ɪt / (BrE)
used to ask someone to say what they are thinking or to express their opinion or statement

Examples

Let's have it now
If you know a better way to solve this problem, let's have it.
We're all curious about your thoughts—let's have it.
You've been quiet for a while—come on, let's have it!
