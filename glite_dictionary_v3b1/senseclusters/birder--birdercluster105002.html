birder

noun
person who observes or studies birds
Pronunciation: / ˈbɝːdɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɜːdə / (BrE)
a person who observes, identifies, and studies birds in their natural habitats, often as a hobby or for scientific purposes

Examples

A birder watches birds
As a passionate birder, she travels to different countries for birdwatching tours.
Many birders keep detailed lists of all the species they have seen.
The experienced birder spotted a rare warbler in the marshlands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birdwatcher - a person who observes birds for pleasure
