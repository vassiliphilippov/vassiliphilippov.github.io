checked

adjective
patterned with squares of color
Pronunciation: / tʃɛkt / (AmE), / tʃɛkt / (BrE)
(of fabric or material) having a pattern of alternating squares of color, resembling a checkerboard.

Examples

Checked shirt
He prefers checked shirts to plain ones.
She wore a stylish checked skirt to the party.
The tablecloth had a red and white checked pattern.
