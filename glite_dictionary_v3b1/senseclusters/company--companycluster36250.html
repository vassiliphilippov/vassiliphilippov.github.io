company

noun
group of performers (theater, dance, etc.)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (AmE), / ˈkʌm.pə.ni / (BrE)
a group of actors, dancers, or other performers who work together, especially in theater or dance; also includes associated personnel

Examples

A theater company
She joined a touring company after finishing drama school.
The ballet company will perform 'Swan Lake' next month.
The theater company rehearsed every day for the new play.
