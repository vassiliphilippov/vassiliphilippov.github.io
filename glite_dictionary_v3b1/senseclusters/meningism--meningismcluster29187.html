meningism

noun
symptoms mimicking meningitis, without actual inflammation
9.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmen.ɪn.dʒɪ.zəm/ /; / /mə.ˈnɪn.dʒɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈmen.ɪn.dʒɪ.zəm/ /; / /mə.ˈnɪn.dʒɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
A set of symptoms resembling those of meningitis, such as neck stiffness and headache, but occurring without inflammation of the meninges.

Examples
