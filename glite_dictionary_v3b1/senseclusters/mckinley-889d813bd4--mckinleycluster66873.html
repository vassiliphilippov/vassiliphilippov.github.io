McKinley

proper noun
mountain in Alaska; highest in North America
Pronunciation: / məˈkɪnli / (AmE), / məˈkɪnli / (BrE)
a mountain in south central Alaska, also known as Denali; the highest peak in North America, standing at 20,310 feet (6,190 meters).

Examples

McKinley mountain peak
Many still refer to Denali as McKinley, despite the official change in name.
Mount McKinley attracts climbers from around the world due to its challenging conditions.
The snow-capped peak of McKinley dominates the Alaskan landscape.
