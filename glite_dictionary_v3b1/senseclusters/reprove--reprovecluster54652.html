to express disapproval of someone's actions or behavior, often in a gentle or constructive way

Examples

To reprove gently
Instead of yelling, he chose to reprove his son with a calm explanation.
She gently reproved her colleague for arriving late to the meeting.
The teacher had to reprove the students for talking during the exam.

Synonyms

  • admonish
    6.8 3.29/m
    admonish warns someone firmly to change behavior, while reprove is milder and focuses on gentle, constructive correction.
