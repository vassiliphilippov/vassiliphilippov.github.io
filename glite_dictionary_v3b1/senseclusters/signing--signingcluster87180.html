signing

noun
communicating by hand gestures (sign language)
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the use of hand gestures to communicate, especially as part of a sign language for the deaf or hard of hearing

Examples

Basic signing skills
Children in the program learn basic signing to communicate with their peers.
During the meeting, signing was used to ensure everyone could participate.
She is fluent in American signing and often interprets for the deaf community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sign language - a language communicated using hand gestures and other visual signs
