concur

verb
happen at the same time
5.2
Pronunciation: / kənˈkɜr / (AmE), / kənˈkɜː / (BrE)
(of events) to happen or occur at the same time; coincide.

Examples

Events concur in time
Her vacation plans concurred perfectly with the scheduled maintenance at work, so she didn't miss anything important.
The parade and the festival concurred this year, making the city especially lively.
Three important deadlines concurred on the same day, causing a lot of stress for the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coincide - to occur at the same time
