fetal monitor

noun
device to monitor fetal heartbeat and contractions
5.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfiː.təl ˈmɑːn.ə.tər/ /; / /ˈfiː.təl ˈmɑːn.ɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈfiː.təl ˈmɒn.ɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈfiː.təl ˈmɒn.ə.tə/ (BrE)
An electronic device used to monitor a baby's heartbeat and the mother's uterine contractions during childbirth.

Examples

A portable fetal monitor
A portable fetal monitor allowed her to walk around the room while being observed.
Doctors rely on a fetal monitor to detect signs of fetal distress.
The nurse adjusted the fetal monitor to track the baby's heartbeat during labor.
Continuous data from the foetal monitor can help detect early signs of distress.
The hospital equipped each delivery room with a state-of-the-art foetal monitor.
The nurse checked the foetal monitor to ensure the baby's heart rate was stable.
