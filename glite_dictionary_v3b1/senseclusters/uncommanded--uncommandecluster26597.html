uncommanded

adjective
involuntary; spontaneous
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmændɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɑːndɪd / (BrE)
happening without deliberate control or intention, especially of movements, sounds, or reactions

Examples

Uncommanded movement
An uncommanded laugh escaped her during the serious meeting, surprising everyone.
The patient experienced uncommanded muscle twitches while resting in bed.
He was embarrassed by his uncommanded tears during the emotional speech.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally
  • synonym: involuntary - done without conscious control or intention
  • synonym: spontaneous - happening naturally, without planning or external cause
