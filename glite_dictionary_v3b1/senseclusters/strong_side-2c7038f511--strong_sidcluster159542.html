strong side

noun
side with more players
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɔŋ saɪd / (AmE), / ˈstrɒŋ saɪd / (BrE)
the side of a sports formation that has more players, strength, or emphasis, often used in football or basketball

Examples

Pass to strong side
The quarterback threw the ball to the strong side where the receivers were lined up.
Defenders shifted toward the strong side to cover the extra players.
Coaches often design plays that take advantage of the strong side in basketball.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weak side - the side of a sports formation with fewer players or less strength
