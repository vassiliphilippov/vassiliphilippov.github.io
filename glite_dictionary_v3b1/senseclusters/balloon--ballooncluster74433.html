balloon

noun
inflatable rubber toy or decoration
3.8
Pronunciation: / bəˈlun / (AmE), / bəˈluːn / (BrE)
a small, thin, inflatable bag, usually made of rubber, with a narrow neck, typically filled with air or gas and used as a toy or decoration

Examples

A party balloon
He accidentally popped the balloon with a pin.
She tied a red balloon to each chair for the birthday party.
The children laughed as the balloon floated away into the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airship - a powered aircraft that is kept buoyant by a body of gas, contrasting with a balloon
  • synonym: blimp - a non-rigid airship, similar in shape to a balloon
  • synonym: parachute - a device used to slow a person or object when falling, which resembles a balloon when inflated
