unearth

verb
discover by digging up
4.2 1.41/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀà…úrŒ∏ / (AmE), /  ånÀà…úÀêŒ∏ / (BrE)
to discover something by digging it up from the ground; to bring to light something buried or hidden

Examples

To unearth old bones
Archaeologists hope to unearth new artifacts at the ancient site.
Construction workers unearthed a centuries-old foundation while digging for the new building.
They plan to unearth fossils believed to be millions of years old.
