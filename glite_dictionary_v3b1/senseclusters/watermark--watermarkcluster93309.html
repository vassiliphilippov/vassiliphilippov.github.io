watermark

noun
identifying mark in digital media
4.2 0.3/m
Pronunciation: / c8w54t59rccm51rk / (AmE), / c8w54d0t59m51d0k / (BrE)
(in digital media) a visible or invisible identifying mark, logo, or encoded data embedded in an image, audio, video, or document to indicate ownership, deter copying, or track usage.

Examples

Digital watermark
The photographer embedded a digital watermark in the photo to deter copying.
Many stock-photo sites display a visible watermark until users purchase a licence to download.
Researchers use invisible watermark techniques to track unauthorised distribution of audio and video files.
