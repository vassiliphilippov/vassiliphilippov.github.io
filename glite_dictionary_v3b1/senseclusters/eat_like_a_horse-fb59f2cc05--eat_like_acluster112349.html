eat like a horse

verb
eat a large amount of food
3.6
Pronunciation: / it laɪk ə hɔrs / (AmE), / iːt laɪk ə hɔːs / (BrE)
to eat a very large quantity of food, especially habitually or at a particular time

Examples

To eat like a horse all day
After hiking all day, I could eat like a horse.
He always eats like a horse during the holidays.
Their teenage son started eating like a horse since he joined the football team.
