synaptically

adverb
at synapses
9.3
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnæptɪkli / (AmE), / sɪˈnaptɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner relating to or occurring at synapses between nerve cells

Examples

Transmitted synaptically
Information is transmitted synaptically from one neuron to another in the brain.
Some drugs act synaptically to enhance or inhibit nerve signal transmission.
The researchers measured how quickly signals were passed synaptically in the spinal cord.
