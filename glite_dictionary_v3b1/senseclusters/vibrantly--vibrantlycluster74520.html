vibrantly

adverb
brightly or energetically
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrəntli / (AmE), / ˈvaɪbrəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is lively, full of energy, or very bright and colorful

Examples

Paint vibrantly with colors
The artist painted the mural vibrantly, using bold colors to catch everyone's attention.
She spoke vibrantly about her travels, making the stories come alive for her audience.
The city is decorated vibrantly during the festival, with lights and banners everywhere.
The artist used colors so vibrantly that the painting seemed to come alive.
She spoke vibrantly, capturing the attention of everyone in the room.
The orchestra played vibrantly, filling the concert hall with rich, resonant music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: energetically - in a way that shows energy or enthusiasm
