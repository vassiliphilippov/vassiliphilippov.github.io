slide

verb
move smoothly or sideways along a surface
Pronunciation: / slaɪd / (AmE), / slaɪd / (BrE)
to move smoothly along a surface

Examples

To slide across ice
She accidentally slid across the wet kitchen floor and caught herself on the counter.
The car began to slide on the icy road as it rounded the curve.
The soccer player slid sideways to intercept the ball from his opponent.
Be careful not to slide on the wet floor in the kitchen.
He slid the book across the table toward her.
The children love to slide down the icy hill in winter.
