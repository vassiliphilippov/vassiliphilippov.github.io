esthetic

noun
philosophical theory of beauty
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɛsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / iːsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
a set of principles or theory regarding what is considered beautiful, especially in art or philosophy

Examples

Modern esthetic
Many debates in architecture center around the dominant esthetic of a period.
Minimalism has its own esthetic that emphasizes simplicity and functionality.
She appreciated the esthetic of the modern art gallery.
He studied the aesthetic of minimalism during his time at art school.
Many artists develop a unique aesthetic that sets their work apart.
The aesthetic of the new building was inspired by classic Greek architecture.
