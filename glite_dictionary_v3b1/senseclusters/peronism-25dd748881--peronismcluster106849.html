Peronism

noun
Argentine political movement
Pronunciation: / pəˈroʊnɪzəm / (AmE), / pəˈrəʊnɪzəm / (BrE)
a political movement in Argentina based on the ideas and policies of Juan Perón, emphasizing social justice, labor rights, and state involvement in the economy
