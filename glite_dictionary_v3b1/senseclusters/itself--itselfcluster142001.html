itself

pronoun
reflexive pronoun
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪtˈsɛlf / (AmE), / ɪtˈsɛlf / (BrE)
the reflexive or emphatic form of 'it', used to refer back to or emphasize a thing, animal, or concept already mentioned

Examples

Itself alone
The company prides itself on excellent customer service.
After the rain, the garden seemed to refresh itself.
The machine will shut off by itself if it overheats.
The company itself was surprised by the sudden increase in sales.
The cat cleaned itself after eating its meal.
The solution itself is not complicated, but it requires careful attention.
The house itself was not damaged, but the garden was destroyed by the flood.
The book itself is quite short, but the ideas inside are complex.
The machine itself is easy to use, though the instructions are confusing.
