martinet

noun
strict disciplinarian
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːr.tənˈɛt / (AmE), / ˌmɑː.tɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a person who demands strict adherence to rules or discipline

Examples

A strict martinet
His reputation as a martinet made employees nervous about breaking any rules.
She became known as a martinet for enforcing the dress code without exception.
The new manager was a real martinet, insisting that everyone arrive exactly on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disciplinarian - a person who believes in enforcing strict discipline
