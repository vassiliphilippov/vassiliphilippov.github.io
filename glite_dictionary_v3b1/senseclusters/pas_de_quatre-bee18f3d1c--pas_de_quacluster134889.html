pas de quatre

noun
ballet dance for four performers
6.6
Pronunciation: / pɑ də ˈkɑt̬r̩ / (AmE), / pɑː də ˈkatʁ / (BrE)
A dance in ballet choreographed for four dancers.

Examples

A pas de quatre dance
Choreographing a pas de quatre requires balancing the movements of all four dancers.
The company performed a beautiful pas de quatre in the third act of the ballet.
The famous pas de quatre from 'Swan Lake' was met with thunderous applause.
