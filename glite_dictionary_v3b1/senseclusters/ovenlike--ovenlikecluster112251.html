ovenlike

adjective
extremely hot
Pronunciation: / ˈʌvənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈʌvənˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling an oven, especially in being very hot or stuffy

Examples

An ovenlike room
The small room became ovenlike in the afternoon sun, making it hard to concentrate.
After hours of baking, the kitchen felt ovenlike and everyone wanted to open a window.
Wearing a heavy coat made the crowded subway car seem almost ovenlike during rush hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cool - moderately cold; not hot
  • synonym: stifling - very hot and difficult to breathe in
