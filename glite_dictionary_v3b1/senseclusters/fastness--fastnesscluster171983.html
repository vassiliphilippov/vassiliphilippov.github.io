fastness

noun
strongly fortified place; stronghold
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfæst.nəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːst.nəs / (BrE)
a strongly fortified place, especially a secure refuge or stronghold against attack

Examples

Mountain fastness
Legends tell of a hidden fastness deep within the forest.
The castle served as an impregnable fastness during the long siege.
The rebels retreated to their mountain fastness to escape the advancing army.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortress - a large strong building used for defending a place
  • synonym: stronghold - a place that has been fortified to defend against attack
