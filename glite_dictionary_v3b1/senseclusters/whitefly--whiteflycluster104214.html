whitefly

noun
small insect pest of plants
7.3 6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌflʌɪ / (BrE)
A small, white-winged insect that feeds on plant sap, often considered a pest of greenhouse and garden plants.

Examples

A whitefly insect
A severe whitefly outbreak can stunt plant growth and reduce crop yields.
Gardeners often use yellow sticky traps to monitor whitefly populations.
The greenhouse was infested with whitefly, damaging many of the tomato plants.
