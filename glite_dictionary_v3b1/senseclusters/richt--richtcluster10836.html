richt

verb
to set upright
9.9 0.00/m
to set something upright again or return it to its correct position, especially after it has been overturned

Examples

To richt a cart
The villagers worked together to richt the cart that had fallen in the mud.
He tried to richt the chair after it was knocked over by the wind.
With some effort, they managed to richt the barrel and continue their work.

Synonyms

  • straighten

  • upright
