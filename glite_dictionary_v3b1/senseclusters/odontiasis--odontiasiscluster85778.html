odontiasis

noun
eruption of baby teeth through gums
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊdɒnˈtaɪəsɪs / (AmE), / ˌəʊdɒnˈtʌɪəsɪs / (BrE)
the medical process by which a baby's first teeth emerge through the gums
