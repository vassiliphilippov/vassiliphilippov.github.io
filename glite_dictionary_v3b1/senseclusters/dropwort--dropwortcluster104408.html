dropwort

noun
wetland poisonous plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɑːpwɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈdrɒpwɜːt / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Oenanthe, often found in wetlands, with umbrella-shaped clusters of small flowers and usually poisonous parts

Examples

Wetland dropwort
The botanist identified the plant as a dropwort growing near the edge of the pond.
Many species of dropwort are considered dangerous due to their toxic properties.
Children are warned not to touch the dropwort that thrives in the marshy fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemlock - a highly poisonous plant found in damp areas
  • synonym: water-dropwort - a common name for several species of Oenanthe, often poisonous wetland plants
