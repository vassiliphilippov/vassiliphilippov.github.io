luminously

adverb
brightly, glowing
5.3 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀêm…™n…ôsli / (AmE), / ÀàluÀêm…™n…ôsli / (BrE)
in a way that shines brightly or gives off light

Examples

Stars shine luminously
The stars sparkled luminously in the clear night sky.
Her eyes shone luminously with excitement as she opened the gift.
The city skyline was luminously reflected on the surface of the river.
