tiddlywink

verb
to flick a disc
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪdliˌwɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈtɪdliˌwɪŋk / (BrE)
to play the game of tiddlywinks or to flick a small disc as part of that game

Examples

To tiddlywink a disc
She tried to tiddlywink the disc into the cup but missed by an inch.
During the tournament, players tiddlywinked their way to the final round.
He learned how to tiddlywink from his older brother, who loved the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flick - to make a quick, light movement with a part of the body or an object
