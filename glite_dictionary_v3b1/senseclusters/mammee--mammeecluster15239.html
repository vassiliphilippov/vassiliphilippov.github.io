mammee

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / mæˈmi /; / mæˈmiː / (AmE), / mæˈmi /; / mamˈiː / (BrE)
a tropical American tree (Mammea americana) with large, sweet, edible fruit and wood similar to mahogany
