solicitor

noun
person who requests contributions or support
6.6
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪsɪtər / (AmE), / səˈlɪsɪtə / (BrE)
a person who asks for contributions, business, or support, especially by going from door to door or making persistent requests.

Examples

Door-to-door solicitor
A solicitor came to my door asking me to support the local animal shelter.
Many residents find the frequent visits from solicitors to be intrusive.
The company hired a solicitor to gather signatures for their petition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canvasser - person who seeks support or votes, often by direct contact
  • antonym: donor - person who gives or donates, opposite role of solicitor
  • synonym: fundraiser - person who seeks donations or contributions for a cause
