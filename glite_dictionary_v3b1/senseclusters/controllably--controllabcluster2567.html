controllably

adverb
with self-control
Pronunciation: / kənˈtroʊləbli / (AmE), / kənˈtrəʊləbli / (BrE)
in a way that shows control, restraint, or deliberate self-management.

Examples

Breathe controllably
He breathed controllably to stay calm during the stressful interview.
The athlete moved controllably through the difficult routine, impressing the judges.
She laughed controllably, making sure not to disturb the quiet library.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliberately - in a careful and intentional way
  • antonym: recklessly - in a careless or uncontrolled way
