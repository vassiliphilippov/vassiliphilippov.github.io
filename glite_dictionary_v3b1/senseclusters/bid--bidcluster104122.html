bid

noun
an offer to buy at a stated price
6.0
Pronunciation: / bɪd / (AmE), / bɪd / (BrE)
an offer of a particular amount of money to buy something, especially at an auction or as part of a competition

Examples

Bid at auction
He made a bid to buy the company last month.
Her bid of $300 was the highest at the auction.
Several bids were submitted before the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offer - an amount of money that someone is willing to pay for something
