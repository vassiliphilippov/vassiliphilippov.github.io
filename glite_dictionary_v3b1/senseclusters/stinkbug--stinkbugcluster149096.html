stinkbug

noun
odor-releasing insect
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌbʌɡ / (BrE)
a small insect that emits a strong, unpleasant odor as a defense mechanism

Examples

A stinkbug smells
A stinkbug crawled across the windowsill, leaving a faint smell.
When threatened, the stinkbug released a strong odor to scare away predators.
Farmers often find stinkbugs among their crops during the late summer months.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: beetle - an insect with a hard shell-like back
  • synonym: shield bug - a type of insect with a shield-shaped body, often called stinkbugs
