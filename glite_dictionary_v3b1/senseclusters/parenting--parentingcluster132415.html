Synonyms

  • raising
    2.5 0.5/m
    raising focuses on the action of nurturing and caring for children, while parenting refers to the wider ongoing role and responsibility.
  • bringing up
    3.5 0.8/m
    bringing up emphasizes teaching children manners and behaviour, while parenting covers day-to-day care and long-term guidance.
  • rearing
    3.2 0.8/m
    rearing often refers to methods of caring, sometimes for animals too, while parenting focuses on raising human children.
