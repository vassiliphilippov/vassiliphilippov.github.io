parenting

noun
raising children
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɛərəntɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of raising and caring for children

Examples

Good parenting skills
Effective parenting requires patience, understanding, and good communication skills.
Many books offer advice on parenting for new mothers and fathers.
Balancing work and parenting can be difficult for many families.

Related Terms

  • synonym: child-rearing - the process of bringing up and caring for children
  • synonym: motherhood - the state or experience of being a mother
  • antonym: neglect - failure to care for someone properly
