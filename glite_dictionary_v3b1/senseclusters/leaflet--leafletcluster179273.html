leaflet

verb
to distribute pamphlets
4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.flət/ /; / /ˈliː.flɪt/ (AmE), /ˈliː.flət/ /; / /ˈliː.flɪt/ (BrE)
to distribute printed sheets or pamphlets to people in public places, usually for advertising or information.

Examples

To leaflet the street
Activists plan to leaflet the city center to inform people about the new recycling program.
During the campaign, volunteers leafleted outside the train station every morning.
It is common for organizations to leaflet at public events to reach a wider audience.
