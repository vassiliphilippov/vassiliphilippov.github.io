spader

noun
person who digs soil
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪdər / (AmE), / ˈspeɪdə / (BrE)
a person who digs or turns soil, especially using a spade, often as a laborer preparing ground

Examples

A skilled spader
The spader worked all morning to prepare the field for planting.
Each spader was given a section of the garden to turn over.
After the heavy rain, the spader made sure the soil was ready for new seeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digger - a person or machine that digs
  • synonym: gardener - a person who tends and cultivates a garden
