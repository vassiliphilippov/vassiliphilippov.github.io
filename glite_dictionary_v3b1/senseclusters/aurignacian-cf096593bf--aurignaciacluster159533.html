Aurignacian

noun
prehistoric human group
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɪɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːrɪɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a member of a prehistoric human group associated with the Aurignacian culture of the Upper Paleolithic period in Europe

Examples

An Aurignacian person
Archaeologists discovered tools made by an Aurignacian in the cave.
The Aurignacian left behind remarkable cave paintings and carvings.
Scientists study the remains of Aurignacians to learn about early human culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cro-Magnon - an early modern human of the European Upper Paleolithic
