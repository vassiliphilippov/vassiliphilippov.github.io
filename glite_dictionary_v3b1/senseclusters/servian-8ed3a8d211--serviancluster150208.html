Servian

adjective
of Servius Tullius
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrviən / (AmE), / ˈsɜːviən / (BrE)
relating to Servius Tullius, the sixth king of Rome, or to the ancient Servian Wall and related Roman institutions.

Examples

Servian Wall
The Servian Wall once protected the early city of Rome from invaders.
Historians debate the exact date when the Servian reforms were introduced.
A Servian inscription was discovered near the ancient Roman forum.
