acousmatic

adjective
sound without visible source
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.kuːz.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /ə.kuːs.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (AmE), /ə.kuːz.ˈmæt.ɪk/ /; / /ə.kuːs.ˈmæt.ɪk/ (BrE)
describing sound or music that is heard without seeing the source that produces it
