stewpot

noun
deep cooking pot
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːˌpɑt / (AmE), / ˈstjuːˌpɒt / (BrE)
a large, deep pot used for cooking stews, soups, or similar dishes

Examples

A large stewpot
She placed the vegetables into the stewpot to simmer.
The chef used a heavy stewpot to prepare the traditional beef stew.
After hours on the stove, the aroma from the stewpot filled the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • synonym: saucepan - a deep pan with a handle, used for cooking
  • synonym: stockpot - a large pot for making stock or soup
