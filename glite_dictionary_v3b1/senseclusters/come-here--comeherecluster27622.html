come-here

noun
gesture inviting someone to approach
4.0
Pronunciation: / kʌmˈhɪr / (AmE), / kʌmˈhɪə / (BrE)
a gesture or expression used to invite someone to approach or attract their attention

Examples

A come-here gesture
He gave his dog a come-here and waited for it to run over.
She motioned to her son with a gentle come-here when it was time to leave.
With a quick come-here, the manager signaled the waiter across the room.
