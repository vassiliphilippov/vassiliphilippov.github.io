bucko

noun
bullying authority figure
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbʌkəʊ / (BrE)
a person in authority who is aggressive, bullying, or gives harsh orders, especially in a domineering way

Examples

A bucko boss
The new manager quickly gained a reputation as a bucko who barked orders at everyone.
Nobody wanted to work under the bucko supervisor because of his harsh attitude.
She stood up to the bucko in charge and demanded fair treatment for the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate others
  • antonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
  • synonym: tyrant - a cruel and oppressive ruler or boss
