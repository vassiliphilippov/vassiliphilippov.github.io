trifold

adjective
three-part fold
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtraɪfəʊld / (BrE)
folded into three sections or having three parts that fold together

Examples

Trifold brochure
The company designed a trifold brochure to showcase its new products.
She handed out trifold pamphlets at the conference entrance.
A trifold menu fits easily into a pocket or small bag.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bifold - folded into two sections or having two parts that fold together
  • synonym: tripartite - divided into or composed of three parts
