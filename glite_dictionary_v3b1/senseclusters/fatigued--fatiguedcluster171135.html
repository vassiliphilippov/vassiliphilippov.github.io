Synonyms

  • exhausted
    2.0 4/m
    exhausted means completely without energy and unable to continue activity, while fatigued describes extreme tiredness that slows you down.
  • drained
    3.4 0.00/m
    drained feels empty of energy after effort or stress, while fatigued is extreme tiredness that reduces your strength and focus.
  • spent
    3.5 0.00/m
    spent means you have no energy left and feel used up, while fatigued stresses being extremely tired and less effective.
  • worn-out
    2.4 0.01/m
    worn-out describes being so tired you stop working well or break down, while fatigued focuses on deep loss of energy.
  • dog-tired
    6.4 0.1/m
    dog-tired is a strong, informal way to say very tired after hard work, while fatigued sounds more general and formal.
  • weary
    3.2 10/m
    weary means tired in a quiet, long-lasting way after effort, while fatigued suggests stronger, often sudden, extreme tiredness.
