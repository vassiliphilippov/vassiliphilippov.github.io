sayable

adjective
easy to pronounce
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈseɪəbəl / (BrE)
able to be spoken or pronounced easily and clearly.

Examples

A sayable word
The word was simple and sayable for children learning to read.
Some scientific terms are not easily sayable for most people.
They chose a brand name that was short and sayable in many languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pronounceable - able to be pronounced easily
  • antonym: unsayable - impossible or very difficult to say or pronounce
