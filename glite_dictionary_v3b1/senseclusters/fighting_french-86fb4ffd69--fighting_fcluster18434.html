Fighting French

noun
French resistance movement in WWII
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪ.tɪŋ frentʃ/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.tɪŋ frentʃ/ (BrE)
the section of the French forces, led by Charles de Gaulle during World War II, who fought for the liberation of France from German occupation and for the restoration of the republic
