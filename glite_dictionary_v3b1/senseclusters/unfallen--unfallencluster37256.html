unfallen

adjective
morally innocent
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔlən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːlən / (BrE)
not corrupted by sin or moral wrongdoing; remaining pure or innocent.

Examples

Unfallen world
The story describes an unfallen world untouched by evil or suffering.
He admired her unfallen character, believing she had never been tempted by wrongdoing.
Some poets write about an unfallen innocence that humanity has lost.

Related Terms

  • antonym: corrupt - morally depraved or wicked
  • synonym: innocent - not guilty of a crime or sin; pure
  • synonym: pure - free from moral fault or sin
