dorbeetle

noun
a type of Old World dung beetle
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔrˌbiːtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːˌbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
An Old World dung beetle, known for flying with a droning sound.

Examples

The dorbeetle flies
The dorbeetle plays an important role in recycling nutrients in many European forests.
The loud humming flight of the dorbeetle can often be heard on warm summer evenings.
We spotted a dorbeetle buzzing around the edge of the meadow at dusk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dung beetle - a beetle that feeds on or buries dung
