dictyopterous insect

noun
insect of the order Dictyoptera
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪktiˈɑptərəs ˈɪnˌsɛkt / (AmE), / ˌdɪktɪˈɒptərəs ˈɪnˌsɛkt / (BrE)
an insect belonging to the order Dictyoptera, such as cockroaches or mantids

Examples

A dictyopterous insect
A dictyopterous insect can be identified by the distinctive veined patterns on its wings.
Researchers studied the predatory behavior of dictyopterous insects in tropical forests.
The museum's exhibit showcased a wide variety of dictyopterous insects, including rare species of cockroaches and mantids.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cockroach - a nocturnal, fast-moving insect of the order Dictyoptera
  • synonym: mantid - a predatory insect of the order Dictyoptera, also known as praying mantis
