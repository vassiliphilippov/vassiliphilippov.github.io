decide

verb
influence or determine a result
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsaɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈsaɪd / (BrE)
to influence or determine the result of something

Examples

To decide a match
Sometimes a single point can decide an entire match.
The committee's choice will decide who wins the grant.
The judge's ruling may decide the fate of the new law.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affect - to have an effect on; to make a difference to
  • synonym: determine - to decide or establish exactly, especially as a result of research or calculation
