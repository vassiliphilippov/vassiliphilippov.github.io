nasogastric

adjective
nose and stomach
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˈɡæstrɪk / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˈɡæstrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving both the nose and the stomach, especially a tube passing from the nose to the stomach

Examples

A nasogastric tube
The nurse carefully inserted a nasogastric tube to provide medication directly to the patient's stomach.
A nasogastric feeding tube is often used when patients cannot eat by mouth.
Doctors may recommend a nasogastric tube for patients with severe swallowing difficulties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orogastric - relating to the mouth and the stomach
