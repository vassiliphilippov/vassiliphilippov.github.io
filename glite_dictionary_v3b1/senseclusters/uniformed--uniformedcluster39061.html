uniformed

adjective
wearing a uniform
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nəˌfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪˌfɔːmd / (BrE)
(of a person) wearing a uniform, especially as part of their official duties.

Examples

Uniformed soldiers
A group of uniformed soldiers marched down the street in perfect formation.
Several uniformed guards stood at the entrance to the embassy.
The school requires all students to be uniformed during class hours.
