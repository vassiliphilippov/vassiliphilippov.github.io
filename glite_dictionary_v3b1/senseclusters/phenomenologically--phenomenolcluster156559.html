Examples

Described phenomenologically
The symptoms were described phenomenologically before any diagnosis was made.
Scientists often analyze data phenomenologically to avoid making assumptions about underlying causes.
The event was explained phenomenologically, focusing only on what was directly observed.
The therapist listened phenomenologically to understand the client's unique perspective.
In philosophy, some arguments are analyzed phenomenologically to focus on conscious experience.
The study approached the data phenomenologically, emphasizing how participants perceived their reality.
