Synonyms

  • worried
    1.0 63/m
    worried feels focused on a specific problem or danger, while anxious is broader, more about uncertain future outcomes.
  • apprehensive
    5.0 2.58/m
    apprehensive is quietly fearful and expects something bad might happen, while anxious shows general worry about uncertain results.
  • nervous
    1.3 49/m
    nervous feels jumpy or shaky, often with physical signs, while anxious is more mental worry about what may happen.
  • uneasy
    3.2 9.04/m
    uneasy gives a quiet feeling of discomfort or doubt, while anxious means a stronger worried feeling about unknown outcomes.
  • on edge
    4.3 2.12/m
    on edge is tense and easily upset, often from short-term stress, while anxious is a more lasting worry about uncertain events.
