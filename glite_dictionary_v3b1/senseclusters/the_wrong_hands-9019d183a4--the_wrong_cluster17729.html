the wrong hands

noun
untrustworthy or harmful person's possession
Pronunciation: / ðə rɔŋ hændz / (AmE), / ðə rɒŋ hændz / (BrE)
used to refer to people who are untrustworthy or may misuse something, especially if they come into possession of it
