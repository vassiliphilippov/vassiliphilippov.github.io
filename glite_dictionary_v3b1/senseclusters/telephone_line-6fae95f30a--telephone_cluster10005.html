Synonyms

  • phone line
    1.6 3.02/m
    phone line is the common, everyday name people use for a domestic connection, while telephone line sounds more formal and technical in writing.
  • telephone wire
    2.4 0.42/m
    telephone wire is a single physical conductor you can see on poles or in walls, while telephone line can mean the whole circuit.
  • telephone circuit
    4.0 0.01/m
    telephone circuit is the technical path used inside networks to carry calls, while telephone line often means the physical wire to a house.
