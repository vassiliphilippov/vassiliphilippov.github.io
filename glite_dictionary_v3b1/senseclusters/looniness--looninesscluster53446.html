looniness

noun
silliness or craziness
6.8 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàluÀênin…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàluÀênin…ôs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being very silly, foolish, or crazy

Examples

The looniness of jokes
The comedian's looniness made the whole audience burst out laughing.
Sometimes a little looniness can make a boring day much more fun.
Her looniness was obvious when she danced around the office in a funny costume.
