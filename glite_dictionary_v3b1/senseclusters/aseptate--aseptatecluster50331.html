aseptate

adjective
without partitions
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsep.teɪt/ /; / /eɪ.ˈsep.teɪt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsep.teɪt/ /; / /eɪ.ˈsep.teɪt/ (BrE)
lacking dividing walls or partitions, especially in biological cells or fungal hyphae

Examples

aseptate hyphae
Certain molds have aseptate hyphae, which means their filaments are not divided by cross-walls.
The biologist observed that the sample contained aseptate cells under the microscope.
Fungi with aseptate structures can grow rapidly because there are no partitions to slow cytoplasmic flow.
