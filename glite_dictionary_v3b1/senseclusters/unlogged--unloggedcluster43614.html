unlogged

adjective
not cut for timber
6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɔgd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɒgd / (BrE)
not having been cut down or affected by logging; untouched by timber removal.

Examples

Unlogged forest
The national park protects several unlogged areas of old-growth forest.
Researchers studied the biodiversity in unlogged woodland compared to areas that had been harvested.
Many rare species thrive in unlogged tracts where the ecosystem remains undisturbed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: logged - having been cut down for timber
  • synonym: untouched - not changed or affected by people
