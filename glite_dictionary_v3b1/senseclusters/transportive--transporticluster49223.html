transportive

adjective
causing transfer or movement
7.5
Pronunciation: / trænˈspɔrtɪv / (AmE), / trænˈspɔːtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or capable of causing the movement or transfer of substances, energy, or particles, especially in scientific contexts

Examples

The transportive properties of the filter help remove impurities from the water efficiently.
