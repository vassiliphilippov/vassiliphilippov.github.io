cannulize

verb
insert a cannula or tube
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.juˌlaɪz /; / ˈkæn.jə.laɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæn.juˌlaɪz /; / ˈkæn.ju.laɪz / (BrE)
to insert a cannula or tube into a body cavity, duct, or vessel, typically for medical procedures
