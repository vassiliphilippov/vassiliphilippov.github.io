tracer

noun
instrument for making tracings
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪsər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪsə / (BrE)
an instrument used to make copies or outlines by following the lines of an original drawing or pattern.

Examples

Drawing tracer
Artists often rely on a tracer when working with intricate designs.
She carefully followed the lines of the old map using a tracer.
The architect used a tracer to reproduce the complex pattern onto a new sheet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stylus - instrument for drawing or tracing lines
