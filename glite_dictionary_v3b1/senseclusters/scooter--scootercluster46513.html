scooter

noun
child's two-wheeled foot-propelled vehicle
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈskuː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈskuː.tə/ (BrE)
A child's two-wheeled vehicle with a narrow footboard mounted between the wheels, propelled by pushing one foot against the ground.

Examples

A child's scooter
Children raced their scooters along the sidewalk after school.
He received a new scooter for his birthday and was excited to try it out.
Her daughter rode her scooter to school every morning.
