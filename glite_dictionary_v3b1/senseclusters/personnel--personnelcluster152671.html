personnel

noun
people employed or involved in an organization
Pronunciation: / ˌpɝː.səˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˌpɜː.səˈnel / (BrE)
the people who are employed in an organization or engaged in an activity, especially those who are subject to orders or directives

Examples

Company personnel
All personnel must attend the safety training session this Friday.
Military personnel were deployed to the region to assist with disaster relief efforts.
The company is hiring new personnel to meet the demands of its growing business.

Related Terms

  • antonym: management - the people in charge of an organization, contrasted with personnel
  • synonym: staff - the employees of an organization
  • synonym: workforce - all the people working for a company or country
