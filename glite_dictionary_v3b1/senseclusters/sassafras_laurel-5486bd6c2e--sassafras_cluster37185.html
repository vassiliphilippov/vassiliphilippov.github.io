sassafras laurel

noun
Pacific tree with aromatic leaves and hard wood
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæsəˌfræs ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / ˈsasəˌfras ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
A Pacific coast tree with aromatic leaves, small umbel-shaped flowers, and olive-like fruit, providing hard, durable wood.

Examples

The sassafras laurel tree
Carpenters value the sassafras laurel for its hard, tough wood used in furniture making.
In spring, the sassafras laurel is covered in small clusters of yellow-green flowers.
The sassafras laurel produces a distinctive aroma when its leaves are crushed.
