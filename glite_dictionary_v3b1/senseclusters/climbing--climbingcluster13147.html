climbing

noun
act of rising to a higher point or level
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of rising to a higher point or level, especially in altitude, temperature, or intensity

Examples

The climbing mountain
He watched the steady climbing of the mountain’s shadow as the sun set.
The stock market’s climbing last quarter gave investors hope.
The sudden climbing of temperatures in July surprised the residents.

Related Terms

  • antonym: falling - the act of coming down or dropping to a lower level
  • synonym: increase - the process of becoming greater in size, amount, or degree
