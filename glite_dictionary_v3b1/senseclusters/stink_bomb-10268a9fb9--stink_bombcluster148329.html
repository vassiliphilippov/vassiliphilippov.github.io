stink bomb

noun
device releasing foul odor when activated
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɪŋk bɑm / (AmE), / stɪŋk bɒm / (BrE)
a small device that releases an unpleasant or foul smell when it bursts or is activated

Examples

A stink bomb explodes
After the stink bomb went off, everyone quickly evacuated the room.
Someone set off a stink bomb in the hallway before class started.
The prank involved hiding a stink bomb at the birthday party.
