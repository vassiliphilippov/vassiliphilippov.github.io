endodontically

adverb
in a dental pulp manner
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈdɑntɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈdɒntɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

To be endodontically treated

Modern dentists often repair damaged teeth endodontically using advanced root canal techniques.
