hyperexcitable

adjective
easily overstimulated
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtəbəl / (BrE)
easily or excessively excited or stimulated, often reacting strongly to stimuli

Examples

Hyperexcitable children
Some children with ADHD may seem hyperexcitable in busy classrooms.
The puppy was so hyperexcitable that it jumped at every little sound.
After drinking too much soda, he became unusually hyperexcitable and restless.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not easily excited or disturbed
  • degree lower: excitable - easily excited or stimulated
