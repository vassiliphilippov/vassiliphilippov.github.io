Lipo-Lutin

noun
trade name for a steroid hormone
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪpoʊˈluːtɪn / (AmE), / ˌlɪpəʊˈluːtɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for a steroid hormone (progesterone) produced in the ovary, which prepares and maintains the uterus for pregnancy.

Examples

Lipo-Lutin hormone
Lipo-Lutin is often used in treatments that support pregnancy and fertility.
Some patients may experience side effects when administered Lipo-Lutin as part of their therapy.
The doctor prescribed Lipo-Lutin to help regulate her hormonal cycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progesterone - a natural steroid hormone involved in the menstrual cycle and pregnancy
