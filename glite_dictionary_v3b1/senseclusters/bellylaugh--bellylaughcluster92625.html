bellylaugh

verb
laugh loudly and heartily
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛliˌlæf / (AmE), / ˈbɛliˌlɑːf / (BrE)
to laugh in a loud, deep, and hearty way, often with physical expression from the abdomen

Examples

To bellylaugh loudly
Even the usually reserved boss bellylaughed at the story during the meeting.
She couldn't help but bellylaugh when she saw the look on his face.
The comedian's joke was so funny, the entire audience bellylaughed for minutes.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: chuckle - to laugh quietly or softly
  • degree lower: giggle - to laugh in a light, silly way
