north

adjective
facing or coming from the north
Pronunciation: / nɔrθ / (AmE), / nɔːθ / (BrE)
Situated in, facing, moving toward, or coming from the north direction.

Examples

North wind blows
The north wind brings colder temperatures each winter.
The hotel has a north entrance that is rarely used.
We enjoyed the view from the north terrace.

  • antonym: south - situated in or facing or moving toward or coming from the south
