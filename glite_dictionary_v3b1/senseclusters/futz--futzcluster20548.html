futz

verb
to tinker aimlessly
9.6
Pronunciation: /fʌts/ (AmE), /fʌts/ (BrE)
to waste time or mess around with something in an unproductive or trivial way

Examples

To futz around
He wasted the afternoon futzing with his old computer instead of finishing his work.
Stop futzing around and help me set the table for dinner.
She spent an hour futzing with the settings but nothing improved.
He spent the whole morning futzing with his computer instead of finishing his work.
Stop futzing around and help me clean the kitchen before our guests arrive.
She was futzing with her phone, not really paying attention to the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: focus - to concentrate attention or effort on something
  • synonym: procrastinate - to delay or postpone doing something
  • synonym: tinker - to make small changes to something, often to improve it
