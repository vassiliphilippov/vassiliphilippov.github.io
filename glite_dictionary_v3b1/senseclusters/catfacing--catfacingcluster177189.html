catfacing

adjective
misshapen from disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌfeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkætˌfeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
having scars, deformities, or a misshapen surface caused by a disorder affecting fruit, especially tomatoes

Examples

Catfacing tomatoes
The farmer sorted out the catfacing tomatoes before sending the best ones to market.
Fruit with catfacing is often safe to eat but looks unusual.
Gardeners try to prevent catfacing by protecting plants from cold weather during flowering.

  • synonym: deformed - having an abnormal or misshapen form
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
