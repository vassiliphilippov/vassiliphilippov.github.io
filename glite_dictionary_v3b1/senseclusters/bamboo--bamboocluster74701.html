bamboo

noun
woody stem of the bamboo plant, used as material
1.0
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbu / (AmE), / bæmˈbuː / (BrE)
the hard woody stem of the bamboo plant, used as a material for construction, crafts, and various products

Examples

Bamboo stick
Fishermen along the river still use traditional bamboo rods to catch fish.
She crafted a beautiful basket out of woven bamboo.
The roof was supported by thick bamboo poles tied together with rope.
