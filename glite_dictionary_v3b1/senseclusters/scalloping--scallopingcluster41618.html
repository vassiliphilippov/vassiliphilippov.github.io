scalloping

noun
wavy decorative edge
Pronunciation: / ˈskæləpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskæləpɪŋ / (BrE)
a series of wavy or curved edges, or the process of creating such a decorative pattern

Examples

Decorative scalloping
The scalloping on the tablecloth added a delicate touch to the dining room.
She admired the scalloping along the edge of the handmade invitation cards.
The craftsman used special scissors to create scalloping on the paper decorations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: serration - a notched or saw-like edge
  • antonym: straight edge - an edge without curves or waves
