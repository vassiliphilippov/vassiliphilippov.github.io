mark

verb
to leave a scar or permanent trace
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to leave a scar or a permanent trace on the skin or surface of something

Examples

To mark the skin

The illness marked her face, leaving faint lines across her cheeks.
Years of hard labor marked his body with scars.
