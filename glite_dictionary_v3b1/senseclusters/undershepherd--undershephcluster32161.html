undershepherd

noun
assistant shepherd
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌʃɛpərd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌʃɛpəd / (BrE)
a person who assists in tending sheep, working under the supervision of a head shepherd

Examples

Sheep undershepherd
The undershepherd rose early each morning to help guide the flock across the fields.
During lambing season, the undershepherd worked closely with the head shepherd to care for the newborns.
He was promoted from undershepherd to head shepherd after several years of dedicated service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistant - a person who helps someone in a job
  • degree higher: shepherd - a person who takes care of sheep
