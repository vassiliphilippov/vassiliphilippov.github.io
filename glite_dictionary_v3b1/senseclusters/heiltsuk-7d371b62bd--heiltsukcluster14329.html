Heiltsuk

proper noun
Indigenous group in Canada
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪltsək / (AmE), / ˈhaɪltsək / (BrE)
an Indigenous people of the Central Coast of British Columbia, Canada, or the language they speak

Examples

The Heiltsuk people
The Heiltsuk have lived on the Central Coast of British Columbia for thousands of years.
Many Heiltsuk people are working to preserve their language and cultural traditions.
A collection of Heiltsuk art was displayed at the local museum.
