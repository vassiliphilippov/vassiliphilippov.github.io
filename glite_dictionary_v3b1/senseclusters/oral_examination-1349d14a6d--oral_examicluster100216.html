oral examination

noun
spoken test; exam by conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrəl ˌɛɡzæməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəl ˌɛɡzæmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
an examination in which a person's knowledge or ability is tested by spoken questions and answers rather than written ones

Examples

The oral examination test
During the oral examination, the candidate must defend their thesis verbally.
She felt nervous before her French oral examination but managed to answer all the questions fluently.
The final oral examination will be conducted by a panel of professors.
