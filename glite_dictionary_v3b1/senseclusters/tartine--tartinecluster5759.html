tartine

noun
open-faced sandwich
Pronunciation: / tɑrˈtin / (AmE), / tɑːˈtiːn / (BrE)
a slice of bread with a spread or topping, especially as served in French cuisine

Examples

A tartine with jam
For breakfast, she enjoyed a tartine topped with fresh strawberries and cream cheese.
The café specializes in serving a variety of tartines with seasonal ingredients.
He prepared a simple tartine with butter and jam for his afternoon snack.

Related Terms

  • synonym: open sandwich - a sandwich with only one slice of bread and toppings
  • antonym: sandwich - two slices of bread with filling between them
