defibrillate

verb
restore normal heartbeat with electric shock
9.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfɪ.brə.leɪt/ /; / /diː.ˈfɪ.brə.leɪt/ (AmE), /diː.ˈfɪ.brɪ.leɪt/ /; / /dɪ.ˈfɪ.brɪ.leɪt/ (BrE)
to stop fibrillation of the heart and restore a normal heartbeat, typically using electric shocks

Examples

To defibrillate the heart
It is crucial to defibrillate within minutes to increase a patient's chance of survival.
Paramedics quickly defibrillated her and restored a regular heartbeat.
The medical team had to defibrillate the patient after his heart went into arrhythmia.
