tumultuous disturbance

noun
sudden violent disruption
6.3
Pronunciation: / tuˈmʌltʃuəs dɪsˈtɝbəns / (AmE), / tjuːˈmʌltjʊəs dɪsˈtɜːb(ə)ns / (BrE)
a sudden, violent, and confused disruption or commotion

Examples

A tumultuous disturbance breaks out
After the announcement, a tumultuous disturbance broke out in the usually quiet meeting hall.
The courtroom fell into a tumultuous disturbance when the verdict was read.
The protest erupted into a tumultuous disturbance that quickly drew police intervention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of peace and tranquility
  • synonym: commotion - a state of confused and noisy disturbance
  • synonym: uproar - a loud or impassioned noise or disturbance
