expensive drunk

noun
person who spends a lot drinking alcohol
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛnsɪv drʌŋk / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛnsɪv drʌŋk / (BrE)
A person who drinks alcohol excessively and tends to spend a large amount of money on it.

Examples

An expensive drunk at bar
After the promotion, he became notorious at the bar as an expensive drunk who never ordered the house wine.
Being an expensive drunk can quickly drain your savings if you're not careful.
Her friends were concerned because she had become an expensive drunk who always bought the most expensive cocktails.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: alcoholic - a person addicted to alcohol
  • synonym: heavy drinker - a person who often drinks large quantities of alcohol
