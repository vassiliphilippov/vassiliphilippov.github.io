kingfisher daisy

noun
South African plant with blue daisy-like flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃər ˈdeɪzi / (AmE), / ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃə ˈdeɪzi / (BrE)
a South African flowering plant with softly hairy leaves and bright blue daisy-like flowers, commonly grown as an ornamental.
