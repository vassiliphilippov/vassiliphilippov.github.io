convex lens

noun
a lens that focuses light rays to a point
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈveks lenz/ /; / /kɑːn.ˈveks lenz/ (AmE), /kɒn.ˈveks lenz/ /; / /kən.ˈveks lenz/ (BrE)
a lens that is thicker at the center than at the edges and focuses light rays to a point

Examples

A convex lens focuses light
A convex lens can be used to focus sunlight to start a fire.
I looked at the distant tree through a convex lens and saw it appear larger and inverted.
The microscope uses a convex lens to magnify small objects.
