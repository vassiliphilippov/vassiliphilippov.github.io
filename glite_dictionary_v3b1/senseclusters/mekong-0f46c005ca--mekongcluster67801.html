Mekong

proper noun
major river in Southeast Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.kɔŋ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.kɒŋ / (BrE)
A major river in Southeast Asia that flows through several countries before forming a large delta in southern Vietnam and emptying into the South China Sea.

Examples

The Mekong River
Environmentalists are concerned about the impact of dams on the Mekong's ecosystem.
The Mekong River supports millions of people who live along its banks.
The delta formed by the Mekong is one of the world's most productive agricultural regions.
