sacque

noun
loose hip-length women's jacket
9.5
Pronunciation: / sæk / (AmE), / sak / (BrE)
a woman's full, loose, hip-length jacket, often worn as outerwear in the 18th and 19th centuries

Examples

A sacque jacket
