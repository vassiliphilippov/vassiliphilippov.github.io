carburete

verb
to mix fuel and air
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bjə.ret/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bjə.ret/ (BrE)
to supply an engine with a mixture of fuel and air using a carburetor

Examples

To carburete an engine
Older cars often carbureted their engines before fuel injection became common.
The mechanic carbureted the motorcycle to improve its performance.
They carbureted the old tractor's engine yesterday to improve its starting and overall performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fuel - to supply with fuel
  • antonym: inject - to introduce fuel into an engine, often by fuel injection
