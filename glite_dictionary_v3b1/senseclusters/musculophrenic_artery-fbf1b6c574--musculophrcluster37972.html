musculophrenic artery

noun
artery supplying intercostal muscles and diaphragm
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌs.kjə.loʊˈfrɛn.ɪk ˈɑr.t̬ə.ri / (AmE), / ˌmʌs.kjʊ.ləʊˈfrɛn.ɪk ˈɑː.tər.i / (BrE)
an artery branching from the internal thoracic artery that supplies blood to the lower anterior intercostal spaces and the diaphragm's muscular part

Examples

The musculophrenic artery branch
A blockage in the musculophrenic artery can affect diaphragm function and breathing.
During surgery, care was taken not to damage the musculophrenic artery near the diaphragm.
The musculophrenic artery supplies blood to several intercostal muscles on each side.
