non-greasy

adjective
not oily
3.2
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɡrisi / (AmE), / nɒnˈɡriːsi / (BrE)
not oily and not leaving an oily or sticky residue

Examples

Non-greasy sunscreen
I prefer using non-greasy sunscreen because it does not make my skin feel sticky.
The chef recommended a non-greasy salad dressing for a lighter meal.
This moisturizer is non-greasy and absorbs quickly, leaving no residue.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: dry - free from moisture or liquid; not wet or oily
  • antonym: greasy - covered with or containing oil or grease
