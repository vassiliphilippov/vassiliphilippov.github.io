yellowhammer

noun
European bunting with bright yellow male
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌhæmər / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌhamə / (BrE)
A small European bunting, the male of which has bright yellow plumage.

Examples

European yellowhammer
A bright male yellowhammer perched on the fence, singing its distinctive song.
Birdwatchers were excited to spot a flock of yellowhammers in the hedgerow.
The yellowhammer can be recognized by the streaks on its brown back and its vibrant yellow head.
