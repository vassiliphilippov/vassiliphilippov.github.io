mintiness

noun
having the flavor or smell of mint
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪn.ti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɪn.ti.nəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of having the flavor or aroma characteristic of mint.

Examples

Strong mintiness
I prefer toothpaste with a strong mintiness to keep my mouth feeling clean.
Some people find the mintiness of certain teas too overpowering.
The mintiness of the dessert was refreshing on a hot summer day.
