musk sheep

noun
large bovid mammal of Arctic regions
9.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / m åsk  Éip / (AmE), / m åsk  ÉiÀêp / (BrE)
a large, shaggy-coated bovid mammal native to Canada and Greenland, intermediate in size and anatomy between an ox and a sheep, also known as the musk ox

Examples

The musk sheep animal
In the wild, the musk sheep defends itself against predators by forming a defensive circle.
Researchers observed a musk sheep grazing alongside caribou in northern Canada.
The musk sheep is well adapted to surviving the harsh winters of the Arctic tundra.
