adda

noun
bus station (South Asia)
Pronunciation: / ˈʌdə / (AmE), / ˈʌdə / (BrE)
a bus or transport station where vehicles are based, start, or stop, especially in South Asian English

Examples

Bus adda
The morning rush at the adda made it difficult to find a seat on the bus.
He waited for his friend at the local adda before their journey began.
Many vendors set up stalls near the adda to sell snacks to travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: station - a place where buses or trains regularly stop
