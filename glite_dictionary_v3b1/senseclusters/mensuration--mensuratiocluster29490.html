mensuration

noun
the process or science of measuring
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛn.səˈreɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛn.sjʊˈreɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act, process, or science of measuring; especially, the measurement of geometric magnitudes such as length, area, or volume

Examples

Mensuration in geometry
In mathematics class, we learned about mensuration and how to calculate the area of different shapes.
Precise mensuration is necessary when constructing a building to ensure all components fit correctly.
The scientist specialized in the mensuration of plant growth in varying environmental conditions.
