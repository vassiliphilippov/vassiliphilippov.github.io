Jivaro

proper noun
Amazonian indigenous group
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːvəˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈhiːvərəʊ / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people living in the Amazon region of Ecuador and Peru

Examples

A Jivaro person
The Jivaro have a rich tradition of storytelling and music.
Anthropologists have studied the customs of the Jivaro for many years.
Many Jivaro communities continue to live in the Amazon rainforest today.
