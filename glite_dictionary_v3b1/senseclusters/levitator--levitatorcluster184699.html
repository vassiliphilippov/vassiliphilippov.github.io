levitator

noun
device for floating objects
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvɪˌteɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlɛvɪˌteɪtə / (BrE)
a device or person that causes something to float or rise in the air, often using magnetic or other forces

Examples

A levitator device
The engineer built a levitator that could make small objects float above the table.
A skilled magician used a hidden levitator to amaze the audience with floating cards.
Researchers tested a new magnetic levitator for transporting delicate materials without contact.

Related Terms

  • synonym: levitation - the act of rising or floating in the air
  • synonym: magnet - an object that produces a magnetic field
