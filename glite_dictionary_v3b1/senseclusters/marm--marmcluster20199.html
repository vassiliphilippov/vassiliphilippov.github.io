marm

noun
polite address for woman
Pronunciation: / mɑrm / (AmE), / mɑːm / (BrE)
a respectful or polite form of address for a woman, similar to 'madam', used regionally or in dialects

Examples

Excuse me, marm
Excuse me, marm, could you tell me where the post office is?
The young boy politely addressed the teacher as marm during class.
In some regions, people still use marm when speaking to older women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: madam - a polite form of address for a woman
  • antonym: sir - a polite form of address for a man
