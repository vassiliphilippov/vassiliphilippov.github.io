phial

noun
small bottle for medicines or samples
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪəl / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəl / (BrE)
a small glass or plastic bottle, especially one used for storing medicines, chemicals, or samples

Examples

A phial of medicine
After use, the nurse disposed of the empty phial in a special container.
She handed the doctor a phial containing the patient's blood sample.
The laboratory technician carefully filled the phial with the vaccine.
