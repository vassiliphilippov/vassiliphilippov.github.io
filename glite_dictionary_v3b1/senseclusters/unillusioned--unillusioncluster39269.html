unillusioned

adjective
realistic; not deceived
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪˈluːʒənd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪˈluːʒənd / (BrE)
not having illusions or false beliefs; seeing things as they really are

Examples

Remain unillusioned about life
After years in politics, she remained unillusioned about the system.
His unillusioned view of the world helped him make practical decisions.
The documentary presents an unillusioned look at the challenges facing the community.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: cynical - believing people are motivated by self-interest; distrustful
  • antonym: idealistic - believing in or aiming for perfection or high ideals
  • synonym: realistic - accepting things as they are; practical
