Examples

Synonyms

  • undeluded
    8.5 0.02/m
    undeluded is not deceived and sees facts clearly, while unillusioned means lacking false beliefs and knowing things as they really are.
  • undeceived
    8.1 0.02/m
    undeceived is freed from a former false belief after learning the truth, while unillusioned simply lacks false beliefs.
  • unfooled
    7.4 0.01/m
    unfooled is not tricked and sounds informal, while unillusioned is a neutral word for seeing reality without false ideas.
