negatively

adverb
in a negative manner
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡ.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡ.ətɪv.li / (BrE)
In a way that expresses or shows disapproval, denial, or the absence of something; in a negative manner.

Examples

Negatively respond
If you think about yourself negatively, it can affect your self-esteem.
She reacted negatively to the feedback, refusing to make any changes.
The proposal was received negatively by the board members, who raised several concerns.

Related Terms

  • antonym: positively - in a positive way; expressing approval or affirmation
