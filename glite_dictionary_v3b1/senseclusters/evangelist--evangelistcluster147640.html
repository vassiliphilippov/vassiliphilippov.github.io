evangelist

noun
preacher of the Christian gospel
5.8
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvæn.dʒə.lɪst / (AmE), / ɪˈvan.dʒə.lɪst / (BrE)
a person who seeks to convert others to the Christian faith, especially by public preaching of the gospel

Examples

TV evangelist
As a television evangelist, she reached millions of viewers around the world.
He became an evangelist after studying theology in college.
The famous evangelist spoke to a crowd of thousands at the stadium.

Related Terms

  • synonym: missionary - a person sent on a religious mission
  • synonym: preacher - a person who gives religious sermons
