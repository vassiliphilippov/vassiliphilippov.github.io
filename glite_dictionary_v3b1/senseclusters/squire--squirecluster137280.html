squire

noun
man who escorts a woman
Pronunciation: / ˈskwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈskwaɪə / (BrE)
a man who accompanies or acts as an escort to a woman, especially at a social event

Examples

A lady's squire
At the dance, every lady seemed to have a squire eager to escort her across the floor.
He played the squire at the banquet, ensuring she was well attended all evening.
She arrived at the ball with a handsome squire by her side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: escort - a person who goes with someone to provide protection or as a companion
