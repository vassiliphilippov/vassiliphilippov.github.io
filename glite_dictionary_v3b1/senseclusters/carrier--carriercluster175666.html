carrier

noun
warship for launching and landing planes
Pronunciation: / ˈker.i.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈkeə.ri.ə / (BrE)
a large warship equipped with a long, flat deck for carrying, launching, and landing military aircraft

Examples

Aircraft carrier
Fighter jets took off from the carrier during the training exercise.
The carrier sailed into the harbor after a six-month deployment.
The navy commissioned a new carrier to enhance its air power capability.
