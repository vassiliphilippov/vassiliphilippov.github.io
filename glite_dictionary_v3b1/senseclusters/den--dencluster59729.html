den

noun
secret hideout or lair
Pronunciation: / dɛn / (AmE), / dɛn / (BrE)
a secret or hidden place used as a hideout, especially by criminals or for illegal activities

Examples

Thieves' den
Legends say the forest was once filled with the dens of notorious outlaws.
Police discovered the drug lord's secret den hidden beneath the warehouse.
The bandits retreated to their den deep in the mountains.
The police discovered a den where the gang planned their illegal activities.
Authorities raided several dens suspected of hosting underground gambling operations.
The old warehouse was used as a secret den for stolen goods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: headquarters - the main office or center of operations for an organization
  • synonym: hideout - a place where someone goes to hide, especially from the law
  • synonym: lair - a wild animal's resting place, especially one that is hidden
