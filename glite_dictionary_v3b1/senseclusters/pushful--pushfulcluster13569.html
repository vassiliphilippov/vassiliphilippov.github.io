pushful

adjective
aggressively ambitious; energetic
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃ.fəl / (BrE)
having aggressive ambition and energy; showing a strong desire to achieve or succeed, often forcefully

Examples

A pushful person
Her pushful attitude helped her quickly advance in a competitive field.
Some people might find a pushful salesperson overwhelming, while others admire their drive.
The new manager is extremely pushful, always looking for ways to climb the corporate ladder.

  • synonym: pushy - excessively assertive or forward in behavior
