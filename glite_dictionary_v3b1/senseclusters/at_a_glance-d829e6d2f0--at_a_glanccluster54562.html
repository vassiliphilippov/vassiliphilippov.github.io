at a glance

adverb
immediately, with a quick look
Pronunciation: / æt ə ɡlæns / (AmE), / ət ə ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
immediately, after a quick look or with very little consideration

Examples

See at a glance
She could see at a glance that something was wrong with the machine.
The results are clear at a glance from the summary table.
You can tell the quality of the fabric at a glance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: on inspection - after examining or looking closely
  • synonym: right away - immediately, without delay
