bushel basket

noun
basket holding one bushel
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃəl ˌbæskɪt / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃl ˌbɑːskɪt / (BrE)
a basket with the capacity to hold one bushel, typically used for carrying produce or other goods

Examples

A bushel basket of apples
A bushel basket is often used to measure produce during harvest season.
She filled a bushel basket with fresh apples from the orchard.
The farmer carried a bushel basket of corn to the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bushel - a unit of dry capacity equal to 8 gallons (about 36 liters)
