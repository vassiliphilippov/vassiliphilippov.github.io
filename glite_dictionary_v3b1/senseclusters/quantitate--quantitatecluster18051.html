quantitate

verb
to measure quantity
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːntɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈkwɒntɪteɪt / (BrE)
to measure or determine the quantity of something, especially in a scientific context

Examples

To quantitate chemical concentration
Scientists use special equipment to quantitate the concentration of chemicals in a solution.
The lab technician will quantitate the amount of DNA present in the sample.
Accurate results depend on the ability to quantitate small differences between test groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estimate - to roughly calculate or judge the value or amount of something
  • synonym: measure - to find the size, amount, or degree of something
  • synonym: quantify - to express or measure the quantity of something
