overturn

verb
change radically or completely
5.0 0.71/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàt…úrn / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàt…úÀên / (BrE)
to change or alter something completely so that it is very different from before

Examples

To overturn traditions
A sudden policy change may completely overturn established social norms.
Innovative technologies have the power to overturn traditional business models.
The new evidence could overturn everything we thought we knew about the case.
