star anise

noun
star-shaped spice from East Asia
Pronunciation: / stɑr ˈænɪs /; / stɑr əˈnis / (AmE), / stɑː ˈænɪs /; / stɑː əˈniːs / (BrE)
a star-shaped spice with a flavor similar to anise, used in cooking and traditional medicine

Examples
