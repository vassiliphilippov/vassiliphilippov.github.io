strictly

adverb
restricted to what is specified
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪktli / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli / (BrE)
only involving or limited to what is specified, with no other elements included; exclusively.

Examples

Strictly by invitation
Admission to the club is strictly by invitation only.
During the meeting, we discussed strictly
The policy applies strictly to employees; visitors are not affected.

  • synonym: solely - not involving anyone or anything else, only
