parastatal

noun
government-owned company
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsteɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈsteɪtəl / (BrE)
a company or agency that is owned or controlled wholly or partly by the government

Examples

A parastatal company
The government announced reforms to improve the efficiency of several parastatals.
Many parastatals provide essential services such as electricity and water.
After years of losses, the parastatal was finally privatized.

Related Terms

  • antonym: private company - a business owned by private individuals or groups
  • synonym: state enterprise - a business owned by the government
