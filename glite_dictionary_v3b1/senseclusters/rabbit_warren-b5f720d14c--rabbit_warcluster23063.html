rabbit warren

noun
overcrowded, maze-like residential area
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt ˌwɔɹən / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt ˌwɒrən / (BrE)
An overcrowded and often confusing or maze-like residential area.

Examples

Crowded rabbit warren
Many migrants found themselves living in a rabbit warren where privacy was impossible to find.
The city's old quarter had become a rabbit warren of tightly packed homes and narrow alleyways.
The volunteers struggled to navigate through the rabbit warren to reach those in need.
