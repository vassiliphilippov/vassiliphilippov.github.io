industrial bank

noun
financial institution offering small loans
1.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʌstriəl bæŋk / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʌstriəl baŋk / (BrE)

Examples

An industrial bank loan
An industrial bank often serves those who cannot access traditional commercial banks.
