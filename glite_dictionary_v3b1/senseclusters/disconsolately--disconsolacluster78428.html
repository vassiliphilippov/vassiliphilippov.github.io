disconsolately

adverb
in a deeply unhappy or hopeless way
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkɑːnsələtli / (AmE), / dɪsˈkɒnsələtli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or expresses deep unhappiness or hopelessness; without comfort or consolation

Examples

Walk disconsolately alone
He wandered disconsolately through the empty streets after hearing the bad news.
She sat disconsolately by the window, watching the rain fall all afternoon.
The puppy whimpered disconsolately when it was left alone in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfully - in a happy and positive way
  • synonym: disconsolate - deeply unhappy or without hope (adjective form)
