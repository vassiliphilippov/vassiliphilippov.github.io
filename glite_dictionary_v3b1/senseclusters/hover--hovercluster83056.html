hover

verb
place cursor over element to reveal info
4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈhʌ.vər/ (AmE), /ˈhʌ.və/ /; / /ˈhɒ.və/ (BrE)
(in computing) to place a pointer or cursor over an on-screen element without clicking so that additional information, a tooltip, or a preview appears.

Examples

Hover the mouse pointer
To see the full caption, hover the cursor over the thumbnail image on the webpage.
She hovered over the download button, waiting for the file size preview to appear.
When you hover on a calendar event, a small popup shows the event details and location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouse-over - to move the mouse pointer over an element (often used interchangeably with 'hover' in UI contexts).
