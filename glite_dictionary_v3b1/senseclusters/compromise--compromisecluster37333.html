compromise

noun
security breach; data exposure
6.5 0.85/m
Pronunciation: / c8k51d0mpr59ccma6az / (AmE), / c8k52m.pr59.ma6az / (BrE)
(in information security) an incident in which systems, accounts, or data are accessed, exposed, or altered without authorization, reducing confidentiality or integrity.

Examples

A data compromise
The company announced a compromise that exposed millions of customer records to hackers.
After detecting unusual activity, the team investigated the compromise to identify affected accounts.
Regulatory fines followed when auditors confirmed a compromise of the database's encryption keys.

Synonyms

  • breach
    5.8 2.75/m
    a breach is when protected data is exposed or taken, while a compromise is any unauthorized access that harms confidentiality or integrity.
  • break-in
    4.5 0.06/m
    a break-in is an unauthorized intrusion into a system or network, while a compromise includes access plus possible data exposure or alteration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breach - an incident in which security is broken and unauthorized access occurs
  • synonym: data breach - an event in which sensitive or confidential information is exposed or stolen
