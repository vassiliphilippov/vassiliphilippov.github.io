virus infection

noun
infection by a harmful virus
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪrəs ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˈvaɪərəs ɪnˈfɛkʃən / (BrE)
An infection caused by a virus that can cause disease in humans.

Examples

Virus infection symptoms
During the winter months, cases of virus infection such as influenza tend to increase significantly.
The doctor diagnosed her with a virus infection after she developed a high fever and severe fatigue.
You can reduce your risk of virus infection by washing your hands regularly and avoiding close contact with sick people.

  • antonym: bacterial infection - an infection caused by bacteria
