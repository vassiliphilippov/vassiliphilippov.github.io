nonplussed

adjective
bewildered or perplexed
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈplʌst / (AmE), / nɒnˈplʌst / (BrE)
so surprised and confused that you are unsure how to react; bewildered

Examples

Look nonplussed
He looked nonplussed when he saw the mess in the kitchen.
She was completely nonplussed by the unexpected question during the interview.
The magician's trick left the entire audience nonplussed and speechless.
His sudden question left me completely nonplussed.
She stood nonplussed when she was announced as the unexpected winner.
The audience was nonplussed by the performer’s bewildering ending.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewildered - confused and puzzled
  • antonym: clear - able to think, understand, or react easily
  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly; bewildered
  • antonym: unfazed - not perturbed or concerned
