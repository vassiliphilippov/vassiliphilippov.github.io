year

noun
group of students graduating together
Pronunciation: / jɪr / (AmE), / jɪə / (BrE)
a group of students who begin or graduate at the same time, especially within the same school or university.

Examples

Graduating year
Our year organized a reunion ten years after graduation.
She was in my year at university, and we shared many classes together.
The year of 2005 produced some of the school's most successful alumni.
