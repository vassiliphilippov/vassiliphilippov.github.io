homebuilding

noun
house construction industry
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌbɪldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌbɪldɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or activity of constructing houses and residential buildings

Examples

The homebuilding industry
The homebuilding sector has seen rapid growth in suburban areas over the past decade.
Many companies in homebuilding are adopting new technologies to improve efficiency.
After the recession, the homebuilding industry struggled to find investors and skilled labor.
The city experienced rapid homebuilding to accommodate the growing population.
New technologies have made homebuilding faster and more energy-efficient.
After the hurricane, there was a surge in homebuilding across the affected region.

  • antonym: demolition - the act of tearing down buildings or structures
  • synonym: real estate development - the business of buying land and building on it or improving existing buildings
