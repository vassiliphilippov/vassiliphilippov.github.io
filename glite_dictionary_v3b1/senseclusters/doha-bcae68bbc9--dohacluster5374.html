Doha

proper noun
WTO trade negotiations (Doha Round)
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdoʊ.hɑː/ /; / /ˈdəʊ.hɑː/ (AmE), /ˈdəʊ.hɑː/ /; / /ˈdoʊ.hɑː/ (BrE)
(in international trade) the World Trade Organization's multilateral trade negotiation round launched in Doha in 2001; also called the Doha Development Agenda.

Examples

Doha Round talks
The Doha Round collapsed after members failed to reach agreement on agricultural subsidies.
Negotiators at Doha faced intense pressure from developing countries for clearer trade concessions.
Many analysts cite Doha as a turning point in talks about global trade liberalization.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Doha Round - the WTO negotiation round (Doha Development Agenda) begun in 2001 focusing on development and agricultural issues
