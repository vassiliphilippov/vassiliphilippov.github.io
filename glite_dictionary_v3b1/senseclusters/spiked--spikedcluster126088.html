spiked

adjective
having a sharp point or points
Pronunciation: / spaɪkt / (AmE), / spaɪkt / (BrE)
having a long, sharp point or points

Examples

Spiked heels
She wore spiked heels that made her appear taller and more imposing.
Some species of cactus have spiked leaves to deter herbivores.
The wall was topped with spiked metal bars to prevent intruders.
