banking game

noun
gambling game against the house
6.0 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbæŋ.kɪŋ geɪm/ (AmE), /ˈbæŋ.kɪŋ geɪm/ (BrE)
A gambling game in which players place bets against the gambling house or dealer rather than against other players.

Examples

A banking game in casinos
Baccarat is a popular banking game found in many casinos around the world.
In a typical banking game like roulette, players bet against the casino rather than each other.
The rules of a banking game usually give a mathematical advantage to the house.
