antipoverty

adjective
against poverty
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈpɑːvərti / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈpɒvəti / (BrE)
intended to reduce or eliminate poverty

Examples

Antipoverty program
The city launched several antipoverty programs to support struggling families.
Many organizations focus on antipoverty initiatives to improve living conditions.
Effective antipoverty policies can help close the gap between rich and poor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poverty-reduction - aimed at decreasing poverty
  • antonym: propoverty - supporting or increasing poverty
