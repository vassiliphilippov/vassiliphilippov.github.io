intercommunity

adjective
between communities
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərkəˈmjunɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪntəkəˈmjuːnɪti / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between different communities

Examples

Intercommunity cooperation
The mayor encouraged intercommunity cooperation to address the city's challenges.
An intercommunity sports event brought together teams from several neighborhoods.
Efforts to improve intercommunity relations have led to greater understanding among residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercommunal - existing or happening between communities
  • antonym: intracommunity - existing or occurring within a single community
