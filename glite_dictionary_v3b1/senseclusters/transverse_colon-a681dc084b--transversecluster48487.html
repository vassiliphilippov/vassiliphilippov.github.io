transverse colon

noun
part of large intestine crossing the abdomen
7.5 4.5
Pronunciation: /trænz.ˈvɜːrs ˈkoʊ.lən/ /; / /træns.ˈvɜːrs ˈkoʊ.lən/ (AmE), /trænz.ˈvɜːs ˈkəʊ.lən/ /; / /træns.ˈvɜːs ˈkəʊ.lən/ (BrE)
the part of the large intestine that passes horizontally across the abdomen, connecting the ascending colon to the descending colon

Examples

The transverse colon section
After the ascending colon, food waste passes through the transverse colon before reaching the descending colon.

The transverse colon runs across the upper part of the abdomen from right to left.
