woodnote

noun
birdsong in woods
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌnoʊt / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.nəʊt / (BrE)
a natural musical sound or song, especially one made by a bird in the woods

Examples

The woodnote song
The poet was inspired by the sweet woodnote drifting through the morning forest.
As we walked in silence, a single woodnote echoed above the rustling leaves.
She paused to listen to the clear woodnote of a thrush hidden among the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: birdsong - the musical sounds made by birds
  • synonym: melody - a sequence of musical notes that are pleasant to listen to
