dioon

noun
a genus of palmlike cycads
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˈoʊ.ɑn / (AmE), / dɪˈəʊ.ɒn / (BrE)
Any cycad of the genus Dioon, a group of palmlike, robust cycads with crowns of thick leaves and rugged trunks, native to Mexico and Central America.

Examples

A dioon plant
Some species of dioon can live for hundreds of years, developing impressively rugged trunks.
The rare dioon thrives in the arid regions of Mexico, making it a prized plant for collectors.
We visited a botanical garden that featured an ancient dioon in its tropical conservatory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cycad - a type of seed plant with a stout trunk and large, compound leaves
