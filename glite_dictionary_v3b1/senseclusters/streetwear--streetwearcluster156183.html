streetwear

noun
urban casual fashion
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈstriːtˌwɛə / (BrE)
a style of casual clothing influenced by urban culture, often featuring comfortable and trendy items like hoodies, sneakers, and graphic T-shirts

Examples

Streetwear brands
Many young people express their individuality through streetwear brands and unique accessories.
The new collection features bold colors and oversized fits typical of streetwear style.
He started a business selling limited-edition streetwear online to fashion enthusiasts.
