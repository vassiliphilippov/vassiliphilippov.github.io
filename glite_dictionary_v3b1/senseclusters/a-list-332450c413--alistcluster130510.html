A-list

noun
the most important or prominent people in a group
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ ˌlɪst / (AmE), / ˈeɪ ˌlɪst / (BrE)
a group of people who are considered to be the most important, popular, or admired, especially in a particular field such as entertainment or business

Examples

The A-list celebrities
Many actors dream of making it onto the A-list in Hollywood.
Only members of the A-list were invited to the charity gala.
She is definitely on the A-list of fashion designers this season.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: B-list - people who are somewhat less important or popular
  • synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment
