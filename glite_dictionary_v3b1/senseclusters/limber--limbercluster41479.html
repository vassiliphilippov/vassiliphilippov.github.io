limber

verb
make or become flexible or supple
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmbɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪmbə / (BrE)
to make or become flexible and supple, especially by physical exercise or warm-up movements

Examples

To limber up
He limbered his fingers with a quick series of exercises before playing the piano.
It's important to limber your muscles before and after heavy exercise.
The dancers limbered up backstage before their performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loosen up - to make or become more relaxed or flexible
