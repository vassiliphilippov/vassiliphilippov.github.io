maltreater

noun
a person who abuses others
4.1
Pronunciation: / mælˈtritər / (AmE), / mælˈtriːtə / (BrE)
a person who treats someone or something cruelly or abusively

Examples

Maltreaters of animals
Animal shelters often care for pets rescued from their maltreaters.
Society must take action against any maltreater who inflicts suffering on others.
The child was removed from the care of the maltreater for his own safety.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protector - a person who protects others from harm or abuse
