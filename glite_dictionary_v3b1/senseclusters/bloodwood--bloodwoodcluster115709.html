bloodwood

noun
tree with red wood
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈblʌdˌwʊd / (BrE)
a type of tree known for its reddish wood or sap, found especially in Australia and Africa

Examples

A bloodwood tree
The craftsman carved a beautiful bowl from bloodwood because of its rich color.
Many Australian forests are home to the distinctive bloodwood tree.
The reddish sap of the bloodwood is sometimes used as a natural dye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mahogany - a tropical hardwood tree with reddish-brown wood
  • synonym: oak - a common hardwood tree used for timber
