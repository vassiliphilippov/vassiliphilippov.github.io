piggyback

noun
act of carrying on the back or shoulders
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡiˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡibæk / (BrE)
the act of carrying someone or something on your back or shoulders, especially with their arms around your neck and legs around your waist

Examples

A piggyback ride
After our hike, I offered my friend a piggyback since he twisted his ankle.
She gave her little brother a piggyback to the playground when he got tired.
The children took turns giving each other piggyback rides in the backyard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ride - an act of being carried on the back of someone
