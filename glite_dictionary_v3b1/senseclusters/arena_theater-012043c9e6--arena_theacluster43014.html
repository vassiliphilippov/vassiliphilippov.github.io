arena theater

noun
theater with audience around stage
2.1 3.6
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈriː.nə ˈθiː.ə.tər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈriː.nə ˈθiː.ə.tə/ (BrE)
A theater in which the stage is surrounded by seats on at least three sides.

Examples

An arena theater stage
Many directors enjoy the intimacy of an arena theater because it brings the audience closer to the actors.
The festival was held in an open-air arena theater to create a unique staging experience.
The new community center features an arena theater that can seat over 500 people.
