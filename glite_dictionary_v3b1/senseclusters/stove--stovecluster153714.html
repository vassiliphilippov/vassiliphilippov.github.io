stove

noun
kitchen appliance for cooking food

Examples

Synonyms

    1.0 0.16/m
    kitchen stove has burners and a flat cooktop for home cooking, while stove is any appliance with burners used to cook food.
  • cooking stove
    1.0 0.05/m
    cooking stove runs on gas or electricity and is built for regular meal cooking, while stove is a general cooktop appliance with burners.
  • kitchen range
    2.4 0.06/m
    kitchen range combines a cooktop and oven in one large unit for many dishes, while stove is a smaller appliance with burners.
  • range
    3.5 8.03/m
