stove

noun
kitchen appliance for cooking food
Pronunciation: / stoʊv / (AmE), / stəʊv / (BrE)
a kitchen appliance with one or more burners or heating elements used for cooking food

Examples

Gas stove
After the storm, we had to use a gas stove to cook our meals.
She placed the saucepan on the stove and turned on the heat.
The kitchen was warm because the stove had been on all day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oven - enclosed compartment for baking or roasting food
