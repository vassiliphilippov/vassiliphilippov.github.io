diving board

noun
springboard for diving into water
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪvɪŋ bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdaɪvɪŋ bɔːd / (BrE)
A board, usually made of wood or fiberglass and mounted over water, from which swimmers jump or dive into a pool.

Examples

A diving board over water
After slipping off the diving board, he splashed into the water with a big grin.
She stood nervously on the diving board before attempting her first somersault.
The children lined up to take turns on the diving board at the public pool.

  • synonym: springboard - a flexible board for jumping or diving
