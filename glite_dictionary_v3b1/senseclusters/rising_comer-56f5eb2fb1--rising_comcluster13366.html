rising comer

noun
an emerging and promising newcomer
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈkʌmər / (AmE), / ˈraɪzɪŋ ˈkʌmə / (BrE)
A person who is new to a field and quickly gaining recognition or showing great potential.

Examples

A rising comer in sports
Despite being a rising comer, he has already made a significant impact in the tech industry.
Many see her as a rising comer in the world of contemporary art.
The marketing team is excited to welcome another rising comer to the department this quarter.
