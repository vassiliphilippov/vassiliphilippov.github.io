monocular

noun
single-eye telescope
Pronunciation: / məˈnɑːkjələr / (AmE), / məˈnɒkjʊlə / (BrE)
a small optical instrument with a single eyepiece used for viewing distant objects with one eye

Examples

A monocular device
He used a monocular to watch birds from across the lake.
A compact monocular fits easily in your pocket for hiking trips.
The sailor scanned the horizon with his trusty monocular.

Related Terms

  • antonym: binoculars - an optical instrument with two eyepieces for both eyes
  • synonym: telescope - an instrument for viewing distant objects, usually with one or two eyepieces
