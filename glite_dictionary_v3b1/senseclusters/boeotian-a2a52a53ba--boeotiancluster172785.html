Boeotian

adjective
relating to ancient Boeotia or its dialect
9.9
Pronunciation: / boʊˈoʊʃən / (AmE), / bəʊˈiːʃən / (BrE)
of or relating to ancient Boeotia, its people, or the dialect spoken there in classical times

Examples

Boeotian pottery
He studied ancient Boeotian pottery during his travels.
The Boeotian army played a significant role in the history of Greece.
The Boeotian dialect was distinct from Attic Greek.
