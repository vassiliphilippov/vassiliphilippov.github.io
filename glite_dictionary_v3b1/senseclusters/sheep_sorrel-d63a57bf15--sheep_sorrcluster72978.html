sheep sorrel

noun
a small wild plant with acid-tasting leaves
Pronunciation: / ʃip ˈsɔr.əl / (AmE), / ʃiːp ˈsɒr.əl / (BrE)
a small plant with arrow-shaped, pleasantly acid-tasting leaves, commonly found in dry places

Examples

Sheep sorrel plant
After the rain, patches of sheep sorrel appeared across the dry hills.
The tangy leaves of sheep sorrel are sometimes added to salads or soups.
You can often find sheep sorrel growing along the edge of country roads.
