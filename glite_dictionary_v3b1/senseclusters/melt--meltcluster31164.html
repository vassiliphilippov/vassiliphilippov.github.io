melt

verb
to become less intense and fade away
2.8 4.62/m
Pronunciation: / mÉ›lt / (AmE), / mÉ›lt / (BrE)
(of feelings, qualities, etc.) to become less intense and disappear gradually.

Examples

Feelings melt away
As the negotiations progressed, his stubbornness seemed to melt little by little.
Their fears melted as they realized they were safe.
When she saw the puppy's sad eyes, her anger began to melt away.
