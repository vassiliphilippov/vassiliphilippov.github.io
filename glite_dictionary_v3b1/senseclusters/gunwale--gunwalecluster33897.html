gunwale

noun
upper edge of a boat's side
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnəl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnəl / (BrE)
The upper edge or topmost plank of the side of a boat or ship.

Examples

The gunwale of a boat
He grabbed the gunwale to steady himself as the boat rocked in the waves.
Paint was peeling from the gunwale, exposing the wood beneath.
Water splashed over the gunwale during the stormy crossing.
