godliness

noun
devout religious quality; piety
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdlinəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdlɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being devoutly religious or showing reverence for God; piety.

Examples

Godliness and kindness
Her godliness was evident in the way she treated everyone with kindness and respect.
The community admired his godliness and looked to him for spiritual guidance.
They valued humility and godliness above all other virtues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impiety - lack of piety or reverence for God
  • synonym: piety - the quality of being religious or reverent
