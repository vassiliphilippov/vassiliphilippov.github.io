chelate

adjective
relating to or involving chelation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkiːleɪt / (AmE), / ˈkiːleɪt / (BrE)
(of a molecule or compound) relating to or characterized by chelation, especially by forming a ring structure in which an atom is bound to two or more other atoms.

Examples

Chelate molecule
Certain antibiotics have chelate properties that can remove heavy metals from the body.
In coordination chemistry, chelate ligands play a crucial role in stabilizing metal complexes.
The chelate structure enables the molecule to bind tightly to metal ions.
