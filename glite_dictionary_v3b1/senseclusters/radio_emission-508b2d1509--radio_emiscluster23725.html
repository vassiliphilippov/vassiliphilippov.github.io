Examples

Strong radio emission
Astronomers detected a strong radio emission coming from the distant galaxy.
Background radio emission can interfere with sensitive radio telescopes.
The radio emission from the planet helps scientists study its atmosphere.

Synonyms

  • radio wave
    3.2 1.69/m
    a radio wave is a single electromagnetic signal used for communication, while radio emission names the emitted radio-frequency energy from a source.
  • radio radiation
    4.0 0.01/m
    radio radiation is a general flow of electromagnetic energy across radio frequencies, while radio emission often names specific waves sent from a source.
