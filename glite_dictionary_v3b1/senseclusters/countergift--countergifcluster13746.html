countergift

noun
reciprocal gift
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntərˌɡɪft / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntəˌɡɪft / (BrE)
a gift given in return for another gift, usually as a gesture of reciprocity or gratitude

Examples

A countergift of thanks
After receiving a beautiful painting, she sent a countergift to express her thanks.
It is common in some cultures to offer a countergift after being given something valuable.
He chose a thoughtful countergift to show his appreciation for their hospitality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: present - something given to someone on a special occasion or as a sign of appreciation
  • synonym: return gift - a gift given in response to another gift
