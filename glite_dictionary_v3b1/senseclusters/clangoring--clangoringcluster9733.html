clangoring

noun
loud, repeated metallic noise
Pronunciation: / ˈklæŋəɹɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklæŋərɪŋ / (BrE)
a loud, resonant, and repeating metallic noise

Examples

The clangoring noise
As the factory doors opened, the clangoring of heavy machinery filled the air.
She found it hard to concentrate with the constant clangoring of construction equipment outside.
The clangoring of the church bells echoed across the valley at noon.
