air mail

noun
mail sent by air transport
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌmeɪl / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌmeɪl / (BrE)
mail that is transported and delivered by aircraft, especially over long distances or internationally.

Examples

Air mail letters
International letters are often delivered faster if sent via air mail.
She sent the package by air mail to ensure it arrived quickly.
The company used air mail to ship documents overseas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: surface mail - mail transported by land or sea rather than by air
