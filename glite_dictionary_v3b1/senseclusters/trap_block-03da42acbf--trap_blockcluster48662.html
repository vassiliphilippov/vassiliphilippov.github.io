trap block

noun
illegal block in American football
Pronunciation: / træp blɑk / (AmE), / træp blɒk / (BrE)
In American football, an illegal block made by a player, typically involving blocking an opponent from the side or behind in a manner prohibited by the rules.

Examples

An illegal trap block
A trap block is considered illegal and can lead to player disqualification.
Coaches warn players to avoid the trap block because it can cost the team valuable yards.
The team received a penalty for a trap block during the crucial fourth quarter.
