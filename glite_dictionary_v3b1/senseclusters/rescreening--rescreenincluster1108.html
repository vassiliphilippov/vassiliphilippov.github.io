rescreening

noun
checking again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈskriːnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈskriːnɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of checking, examining, or testing something again, especially to ensure accuracy or catch errors missed before

Examples

Medical rescreening
After the initial test, a rescreening was scheduled to confirm the results.
The airport implemented rescreening procedures for passengers who left the secure area.
A rescreening of the data revealed several mistakes that had been overlooked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reexamination - the act of examining something again
  • degree lower: screening - the process of checking or examining something
