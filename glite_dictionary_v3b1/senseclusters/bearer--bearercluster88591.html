bearer

noun
person carrying a coffin at a funeral
3.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈber.ər/ /; / /ˈbeə.rər/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.rə/ (BrE)
a person who carries or presents something, such as an object, message, or in a ceremonial role

The bearer of this letter is authorized to collect the parcel on my behalf.
