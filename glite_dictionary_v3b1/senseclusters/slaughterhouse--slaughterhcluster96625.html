slaughterhouse

noun
place where animals are butchered
3.2 2.07/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsl…ît…öÀåha äs / (AmE), / Ààsl…îÀêt…ôÀåha äs / (BrE)
a building where animals are killed and prepared for meat

Examples

A slaughterhouse building
Many people choose not to eat meat after visiting a slaughterhouse.
She campaigned to improve conditions in the local slaughterhouse.
The cattle were sent to the slaughterhouse early in the morning.

Synonyms

  • abattoir
    8.8 0.32/m
    abattoir is the formal, often technical word for a building where animals are killed for meat, while slaughterhouse is the common term.
  • packing plant
    3.2 0.25/m
    packing plant is a factory that slaughters, processes and packs meat for sale, while slaughterhouse mainly refers to the place animals are killed.
  • butchery
    4.7 0.06/m
    butchery is often a shop where meat is cut and sold to customers, while slaughterhouse is where animals are killed and initially prepared.
  • packinghouse
    7.3 0.29/m
    packinghouse is a building where food like fruit or meat is processed and packed for shipment, while slaughterhouse is where animals are killed.
