in stages

If something is done in stages, it is done gradually, with each part completed before moving to the next.

Examples

Done in stages
Introducing the new system in stages allowed employees to adjust gradually.
The medication must be discontinued in stages to prevent withdrawal symptoms.
The renovations will be completed in stages over the next year.
