impassability

noun
state of being blocked
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpæsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpæsəˈbɪlɪti /; / ɪmˌpɑːsəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to pass through, over, or overcome

Examples
