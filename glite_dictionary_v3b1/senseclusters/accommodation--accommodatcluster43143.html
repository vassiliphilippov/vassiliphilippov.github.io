accommodation

noun
settlement of differences
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑməˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
An agreement or arrangement reached to resolve a disagreement or dispute.

Examples

Reach an accommodation
After months of negotiations, both parties finally reached an accommodation that satisfied everyone.
International relations often require skillful accommodation to avoid conflict.
The union and management sought an accommodation to prevent a strike.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disagreement - a lack of consensus or harmony between parties
