villanelle

noun
rustic song or dance
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪləˈnɛl / (AmE), / ˌvɪləˈnɛl / (BrE)
a short, simple rustic song or dance, originally from Italy

Examples

A villanelle song
The musicians played a cheerful villanelle as the villagers danced in the square.
She learned to sing a traditional villanelle from her grandmother.
At the festival, everyone joined in the lively villanelle that marked the end of the harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ballad - a narrative song or poem, often traditional
  • synonym: folk song - a traditional song passed down orally among people
