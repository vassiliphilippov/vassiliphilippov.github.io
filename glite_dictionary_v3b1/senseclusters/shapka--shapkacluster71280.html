shapka

noun
Russian fur hat
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːpkə / (AmE), / ˈʃæpkə / (BrE)
a Russian-style fur hat with ear flaps, typically worn in cold weather

Examples

A shapka hat
He pulled his shapka down over his ears before stepping into the snow.
Tourists often buy a shapka as a souvenir when visiting Russia in winter.
The thick fur of her shapka kept her warm during the icy wind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: beanie - a small, close-fitting hat, usually made of wool
  • synonym: ushanka - a Russian fur hat with ear flaps that can be tied up or down
