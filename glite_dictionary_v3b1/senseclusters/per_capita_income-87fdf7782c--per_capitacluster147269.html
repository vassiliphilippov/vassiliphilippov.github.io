per capita income

noun
average income per person in a country
Pronunciation: / pər ˈkæpətə ˈɪnˌkʌm /; / pɚ ˈkæpɪtə ˈɪn.kʌm / (AmE), / pə ˈkapɪtə ˈɪnˌkʌm /; / pə ˈkæpɪtə ˈɪn.kʌm / (BrE)
the average income earned per person in a specific area and time period, calculated by dividing the total income by the population

Examples

Average per capita income
Economists use per capita income to compare the prosperity of different nations.
Low per capita income often indicates limited economic development in a region.
The country's per capita income rose significantly over the past decade.
Many economists use per capita income as an indicator of a nation's standard of living.
Regional differences in per capita income highlight economic disparities across the nation.
The country's per capita income increased steadily over the last decade.

  • synonym: gross domestic product - total value of goods and services produced within a country
  • synonym: per capita - for each person; per person
