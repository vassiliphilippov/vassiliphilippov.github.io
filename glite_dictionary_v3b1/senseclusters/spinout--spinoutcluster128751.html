spinout

noun
derived project or work
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈspɪ.naʊt/ (AmE), /ˈspɪ.naʊt/ (BrE)
a new work, product, or project that is developed from an existing one, often featuring related ideas or characters

Examples

A movie spinout
The popular video game is actually a spinout of a successful movie franchise.
After the main series ended, the author wrote a spinout focusing on a supporting character.
The company launched a spinout to develop new technology based on its original research.
