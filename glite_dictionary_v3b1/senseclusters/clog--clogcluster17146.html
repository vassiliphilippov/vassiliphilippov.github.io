clog

noun
traditional dance in wooden-soled shoes
Pronunciation: / klɑɡ / (AmE), / klɒɡ / (BrE)
a lively traditional dance performed while wearing shoes with thick wooden soles, characterized by heavy stamping steps

Examples

Clog dance
She learned the traditional clog from her grandmother, who grew up in the North of England.
The festival featured a lively clog performed by dancers in colorful costumes.
The group performed a fast-paced clog that impressed the audience with its rhythmic footwork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clog dancing - dancing while wearing clogs; style of dance
  • degree higher: tap dance - dance style involving rhythmic tapping of the feet
