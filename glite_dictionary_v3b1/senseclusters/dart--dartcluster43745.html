dart

noun
sudden quick movement
Pronunciation: / dɑrt / (AmE), / dɑːt / (BrE)
a sudden quick movement

Examples

A quick dart

There was a sudden dart of movement in the corner of her eye.
With a dart, the rabbit disappeared into the bushes.
