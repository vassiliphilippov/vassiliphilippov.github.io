adaptively

adverb
in an adjusting way
Pronunciation: / əˈdæptɪvli / (AmE), / əˈdæptɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that involves adapting or adjusting to new conditions or needs

Examples

To adaptively adjust settings
The new thermostat adaptively adjusts the temperature based on the time of day.
Students who learn adaptively often perform better in changing classroom environments.
The robot moved adaptively to avoid obstacles in its path.
The species responded adaptively to changes in climate by developing thicker fur.
Students who think adaptively can solve problems even when conditions change unexpectedly.
Some businesses act adaptively to market trends to stay competitive.
