colonus

noun
historical farmer
Pronunciation: / kəˈloʊnəs / (AmE), / kəˈləʊnəs / (BrE)
a farmer or settler, especially in ancient or historical contexts (often in ancient Rome)

Examples

In ancient times, a colonus would often receive land in exchange for farming it.
