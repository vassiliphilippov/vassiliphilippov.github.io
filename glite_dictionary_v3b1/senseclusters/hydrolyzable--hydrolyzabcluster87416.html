hydrolyzable

adjective
able to be broken down by hydrolysis
7.7 7.1
Pronunciation: /haɪ.ˈdrɑː.lɪ.zə.bəl/ /; / /haɪ.ˈdrɑː.lə.zaɪ.bəl/ (AmE), /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lɪ.zə.bəl/ /; / /haɪ.ˈdrɒ.lə.zaɪ.bəl/ (BrE)
Capable of undergoing hydrolysis; able to be chemically broken down by reaction with water.

Examples

Hydrolyzable chemical bonds
Not all polymers are hydrolyzable; some resist breakdown in the presence of water.
Starch is a hydrolyzable carbohydrate, which means it can be digested by enzymes.
The experiment focused on separating hydrolyzable substances from those that could not be decomposed.
