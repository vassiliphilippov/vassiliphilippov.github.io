pediatrist

noun
doctor for children and infants
Pronunciation: / piˈdiːətrɪst / (AmE), / piˈdiːətrɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the medical care and treatment of children and infants

Examples

Pediatrist for children
After the baby was born, they made regular visits to their pediatrist for checkups.
The pediatrist advised us on the best vaccination schedule for our daughter.
When his son developed a rash, he called the pediatrist immediately.

Related Terms

  • antonym: general practitioner - a doctor who does not specialize in one branch of medicine; a family doctor
  • synonym: pediatrician - a doctor specializing in children's medicine
