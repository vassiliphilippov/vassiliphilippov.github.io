in a pig's eye

adverb
expressing strong disbelief; absolutely not
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə pɪɡz aɪ / (AmE), / ɪn ə pɪɡz aɪ / (BrE)
used to express strong disbelief or refusal; absolutely not.

Examples

In a pig's eye no way
In a pig's eye will I lend you my car after last time.
She said she'd be home by midnight, but in a pig's eye she was!
You think he'll actually admit he was wrong? In a pig's eye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: no way - used to strongly refuse or deny something
  • synonym: not on your life - used to emphasize that there is no chance of something happening
