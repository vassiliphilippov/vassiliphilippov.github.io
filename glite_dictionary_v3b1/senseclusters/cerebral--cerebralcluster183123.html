cerebral

adjective
intellectual or brainy
Pronunciation: / səˈriː.brəl / (AmE), / səˈriː.brəl / (BrE)
Involving or requiring the use of the intellect rather than emotions or instinct.

Examples

Cerebral movies
He prefers cerebral films that challenge the mind instead of providing simple entertainment.
Her cerebral style of writing appeals most to readers who enjoy thoughtful analysis.
The debate became more cerebral as they shifted from personal stories to abstract theory.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - involving emotion rather than intellect
  • synonym: intellectual - relating to the intellect
