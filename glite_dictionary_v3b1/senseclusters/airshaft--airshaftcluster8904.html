airshaft

noun
ventilation duct
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌʃæft / (AmE), / ˈeəˌʃɑːft / (BrE)
a duct or pipe designed to carry air for ventilation, heating, or cooling in machinery, vehicles, or buildings

Examples

Building airshaft
The engineer checked the airshaft to ensure proper airflow throughout the building.
Dust had collected inside the airshaft, reducing the efficiency of the ventilation system.
A bird became trapped in the airshaft and had to be carefully rescued.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duct - a tube or passage for air, liquid, or cables
  • synonym: ventilation shaft - a vertical passage for air to circulate in a building or mine
