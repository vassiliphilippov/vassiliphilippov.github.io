tree hugger

noun
(slang) derogatory term for environmentalist
4.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /triː ˈhʌg.ər/ /; / /tʃriː ˈhʌg.ər/ (AmE), /triː ˈhʌg.ə/ /; / /tʃriː ˈhʌg.ə/ (BrE)
