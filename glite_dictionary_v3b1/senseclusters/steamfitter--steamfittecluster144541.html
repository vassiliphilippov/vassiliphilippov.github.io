steamfitter

noun
worker installing pipes for heating/cooling systems
4.5 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈstiːm.fɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈstiːm.fɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a skilled worker who installs, maintains, and repairs pipes and equipment for heating, ventilation, refrigeration, or steam systems

Examples

A steamfitter installs pipes
A steamfitter must be familiar with blueprints and complex piping systems.
After years of apprenticeship, he finally qualified as a steamfitter in the construction industry.
The hospital hired a steamfitter to upgrade their heating and cooling equipment.
The steamfitter arrived early to inspect the heating system in the hospital.
After years of training, she became a certified steamfitter working on large construction projects.
A skilled steamfitter is essential for maintaining safe and efficient steam systems in factories.
