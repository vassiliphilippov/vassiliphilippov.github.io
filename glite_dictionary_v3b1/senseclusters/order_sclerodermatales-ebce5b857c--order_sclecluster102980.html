order Sclerodermatales

noun
order of fungi with peridium and gleba
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər skleˌroʊdərˈmeɪtəliːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə skleˌrəʊdəmɑːˈteɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi characterized by a peridium enclosing a gleba, sometimes classified under the subclass Homobasidiomycetes.
