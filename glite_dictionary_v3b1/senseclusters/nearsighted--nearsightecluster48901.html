nearsighted

adjective
unable to see distant objects clearly
8.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈniːr.saɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ˈnɪr.saɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /ˈnɪə.saɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
Unable to see distant objects clearly, seeing nearby objects better than those farther away.

Examples

Nearsighted person
Because he was nearsighted, road signs were hard to read from a distance.
If you are nearsighted, you may struggle to recognize people across the street.
She is nearsighted and needs glasses to see the whiteboard at school.
