dory

noun
small, shallow-draft rowing boat
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔːri / (AmE), / ˈdɔːri / (BrE)
a small, shallow-draft boat with flat bottom, high sides, and cross seats, designed to be rowed

Examples

Wooden dory boat
He repaired the old dory in his backyard each spring.
Several dories were tied to the dock, bobbing gently in the tide.
The fisherman rowed his dory out to sea before sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: canoe - a light, narrow boat pointed at both ends, paddled rather than rowed
  • synonym: skiff - a small, light, shallow boat for rowing or sailing
