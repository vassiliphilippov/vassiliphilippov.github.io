hairsbreadth

noun
a very small distance
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrzˌbrɛdθ / (AmE), / ˈheəzˌbrɛdθ / (BrE)
A very small distance or space; the width of a hair.

Examples

A hairsbreadth escape
He escaped punishment by a hairsbreadth thanks to new evidence.
She missed the bus by a hairsbreadth and had to wait for the next one.
The car stopped within a hairsbreadth of the edge of the cliff.
