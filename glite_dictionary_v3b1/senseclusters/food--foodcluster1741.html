food

noun
solid substance for nourishment
1.0 352/m
Pronunciation: / fuÀêd / (AmE), / fuÀêd / (BrE)
something that people or animals eat or drink to live and grow; nourishment

Examples

Synonyms

  • sustenance
    5.7 1.95/m
    sustenance means the food and drink that keep people alive and healthy, while food is the everyday, general term.
  • nourishment
    2.0 2.68/m
    nourishment refers to substances that help growth and health, while food is the common word for what people eat.
  • foodstuff
    3.2 1.63/m
    foodstuff describes specific food products or ingredients sold in shops, while food is the broad everyday name for what people eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drink - a liquid that can be swallowed as refreshment or nourishment
  • synonym: meal - an eating occasion made up of food
  • synonym: nourishment - food or other substances that provide energy and nutrients
  • degree lower: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
  • antonym: starvation - suffering or death caused by lack of food
