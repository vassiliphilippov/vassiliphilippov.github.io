John T. Scopes

proper noun
American teacher tried for teaching evolution
8.0
Pronunciation: / dʒɑn ti skoʊps / (AmE), / dʒɒn tiː skəʊps / (BrE)
(1900–1970) An American high school teacher in Tennessee, best known for being tried in 1925 for teaching evolution in violation of state law.
