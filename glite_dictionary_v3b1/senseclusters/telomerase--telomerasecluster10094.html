telomerase

noun
enzyme that adds telomeres to chromosomes
8.3 7.7
Pronunciation: / təˈlɑmɚˌeɪs / (AmE), / tɪˈlɒmərˌeɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme found in eukaryotic cells that adds telomeres to the ends of chromosomes after cell division

Examples

Telomerase enzyme activity
