multivoiced

adjective
multiple perspectives
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈvɔɪst / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈvɔɪst / (BrE)
having or involving more than one voice, perspective, or point of view

Examples

A multivoiced story
The novel is multivoiced, with several characters narrating the story.
A multivoiced discussion allows for a richer understanding of the topic.
The documentary takes a multivoiced approach, featuring interviews from people with different backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monologic - involving only one voice or point of view
  • synonym: polyphonic - having many voices or sounds, especially in music or literature
