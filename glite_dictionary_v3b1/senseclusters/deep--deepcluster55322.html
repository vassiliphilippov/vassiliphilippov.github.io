deep

adjective
bent low (of bow or gesture)
3.0
Pronunciation: / dip / (AmE), / diːp / (BrE)
(of a bow or gesture) with the head or back bent low, indicating respect or submission.

Examples

Deep bow
He greeted the queen with a deep bow of respect.
She made a deep curtsy before stepping onto the stage.
The samurai performed a deep bow to honor his opponent.

  • synonym: profound - of a bow or gesture, very intense or deep
