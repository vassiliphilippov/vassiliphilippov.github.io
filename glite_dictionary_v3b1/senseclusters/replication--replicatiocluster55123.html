replication

noun
repetition of an experiment
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛpləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛplɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the repetition of an experiment or procedure, especially to test the validity or reliability of its result

Examples

Replication of results
Many studies fail to succeed at replication, raising concerns about their initial findings.
The scientists insisted on replication before accepting the new discovery.
Without proper replication, an experiment's results may be unreliable.

  • synonym: repetition - the act of doing or saying something again
