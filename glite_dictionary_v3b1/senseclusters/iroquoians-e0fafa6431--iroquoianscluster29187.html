Iroquoians

proper noun
Indigenous North American group
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈkwɔɪənz / (AmE), / ˌɪrəˈkwɔɪənz / (BrE)
a group of Indigenous peoples of North America who speak Iroquoian languages and are known for their culture and history
