discredited

adjective
having lost credibility or respect
3.3 4.8
Pronunciation: /dɪs.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /dɪs.ˈkred.ɪ.təd/ (AmE), /dɪs.ˈkred.ɪ.tɪd/ /; / /dɪs.ˈkred.ɪ.təd/ (BrE)
having lost respect, trust, or credibility, often because of disapproval or being proven wrong
