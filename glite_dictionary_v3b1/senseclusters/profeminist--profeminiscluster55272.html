profeminist

adjective
supporting feminism
Pronunciation: / proʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (AmE), / prəʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
supporting or advocating feminist ideas, principles, or movements

Examples

Profeminist views
He has always been a profeminist voice in his community, advocating for gender equality.
Many men in the organization identify as profeminist and support women's rights.
The conference welcomed profeminist speakers from around the world to share their perspectives.

Related Terms
