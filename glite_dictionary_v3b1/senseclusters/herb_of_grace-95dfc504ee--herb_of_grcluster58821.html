herb of grace

noun
a strong-scented European perennial herb
8.7
Pronunciation: / hɜrb əv ɡreɪs / (AmE), / hɜːb əv ɡreɪs / (BrE)
A European perennial herb (Ruta graveolens) with strong-scented, grey-green, bitter-tasting leaves, sometimes causing skin irritation.

Examples

A sprig of herb of grace
She wore a sprig of herb of grace as a symbol of remembrance.
