antelope squirrel

noun
small ground squirrel of the western US
Pronunciation: / ˈæntɪˌloʊp ˌskwɝːrəl / (AmE), / ˈæntɪˌləʊp ˌskwɪrəl / (BrE)
a small ground squirrel native to the western United States, known for its white side stripes and quick movements

Examples

The antelope squirrel runs
The antelope squirrel is often seen darting between rocks in the desert.
Unlike other rodents, the antelope squirrel has a distinctive white stripe along its sides.
We spotted an antelope squirrel gathering seeds near our campsite.
