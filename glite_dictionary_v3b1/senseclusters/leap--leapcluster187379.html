leap

noun
sudden and decisive increase
3.2 7.78/m
Pronunciation: / lip / (AmE), / liÀêp / (BrE)
a sudden or significant change, increase, or transition from one state or situation to another

Examples

A leap in progress
Advances in technology led to a huge leap in efficiency.
The company reported a leap in profits this quarter.
There has been a significant leap in sales since the new product launched.
Her career took a significant leap when she became the company's youngest manager.
Switching from teaching to medicine was a bold leap in his professional life.
The new technology represents a major leap forward in communication.
