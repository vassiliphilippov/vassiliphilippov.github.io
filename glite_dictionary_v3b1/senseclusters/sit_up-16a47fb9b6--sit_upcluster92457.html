sit up

verb
move to an upright sitting position
2.6
Pronunciation: / sɪt ʌp / (AmE), / sɪt ʌp / (BrE)
to move from a lying or leaning position to an upright sitting position

Examples

Sit up in bed
He woke up suddenly and sat up in bed.
The toddler giggled as she sat up for the first time.
When the nurse entered, the patient tried to sit up unaided.
