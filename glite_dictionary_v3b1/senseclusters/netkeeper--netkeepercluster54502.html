netkeeper

noun
goal-defending player in team sports
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛtˌkiːpər / (AmE), / ˈnɛtˌkiːpə / (BrE)
a player in sports like soccer, hockey, or lacrosse whose job is to protect the goal and stop the other team from scoring

The team's netkeeper made several impressive saves in the first half.
When the regular netkeeper was injured, the coach had to find a substitute quickly.
