direction

noun
course or tendency of development
1.3
Pronunciation: / dəˈrɛkʃən / (AmE), / dɪˈrɛkʃən / (BrE)
the general way in which something develops or changes, or the course that is followed by something such as an activity, idea, or career

Examples

New direction
After much reflection, she realized her career needed a different direction.
The artist's latest work marks a significant shift in the direction of his style.
The company's new CEO took the business in a bold new direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: course - a path or direction followed by something
  • synonym: trend - general direction in which something is developing or changing
