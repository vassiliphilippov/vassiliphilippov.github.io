gastric digestion

noun
breakdown of proteins in the stomach
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæstrɪk daɪˈdʒɛs.tʃən / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrɪk daɪˈdʒɛs.tʃən / (BrE)
the breakdown of proteins in the stomach by gastric juice during the digestive process

Examples

Gastric digestion of proteins
Gastric digestion is the initial stage in processing proteins as food enters the stomach.
Pepsin and hydrochloric acid both play crucial roles in gastric digestion.
Problems with gastric digestion can lead to nutritional deficiencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digestion - the process of breaking down food in the body
