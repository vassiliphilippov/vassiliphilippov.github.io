PPD

noun
hair dye chemical
9.9
Pronunciation: / pi pi di / (AmE), / piː piː diː / (BrE)
an organic chemical, p-phenylenediamine, used in some hair dyes and industrial products that can cause allergic skin reactions

Examples

PPD in hair dye
Some people experience allergic reactions to PPD in hair dye products.
Manufacturers are required to list PPD as an ingredient on hair dye packaging.
If you are sensitive to PPD, consult your doctor before using certain hair dyes.
