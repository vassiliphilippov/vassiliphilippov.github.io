sylphlike

adjective
slender and graceful (like a sylph)
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlfˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsɪlf.laɪk / (BrE)
(of a woman or girl) slender and graceful, resembling a sylph.

Examples

A sylphlike woman
In the dim light, her sylphlike figure appeared delicate and almost ethereal.
She moved through the garden with a sylphlike grace that seemed almost otherworldly.
The sylphlike model glided across the runway, captivating everyone with her elegance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graceful - having or showing grace or elegance
  • synonym: slender - thin in an attractive or elegant way
