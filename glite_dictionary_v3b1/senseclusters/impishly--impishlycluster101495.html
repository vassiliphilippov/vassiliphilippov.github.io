In a manner that is playfully mischievous or cheekily bold, often in an appealing way.

Examples

To smile impishly
He winked impishly at her after making the clever remark.
She smiled impishly as she handed him the note.
The child peeked out from behind the curtain impishly before running away giggling.

Synonyms

  • roguishly
    7.3 0.07/m
    roguishly is playfully naughty and charming in tone, while impishly is cheekily bold and playfully mischievous.
  • puckishly
    9.6 0.03/m
    puckishly is playful and boldly mischievous with a fairy-tale feel, while impishly is common playful cheekiness in everyday speech.
  • mischievously
    4.6 0.45/m
    mischievously shows a wish to tease or cause small trouble for fun, while impishly adds a cheeky, appealing boldness.
  • archly
    7.9 0.21/m
    archly is sly and teasing with a slight formal tone, while impishly is more playful, charming, and cheekily bold.
  • cheekily
    6.2 0.09/m
    cheekily is boldly rude or impudent and may annoy people, while impishly is playfully bold and often seen as charming.
  • waggishly
    7.7 0.01/m
    waggishly is joking and comical in a friendly way, while impishly is mischievous and cheekily bold rather than merely funny.
