impishly

adverb
in a mischievous or cheeky manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.pɪʃ.li / (AmE), / ˈɪm.pɪʃ.li / (BrE)
In a manner that is playfully mischievous or cheekily bold, often in an appealing way.

Examples

To smile impishly
He winked impishly at her after making the clever remark.
She smiled impishly as she handed him the note.
The child peeked out from behind the curtain impishly before running away giggling.
