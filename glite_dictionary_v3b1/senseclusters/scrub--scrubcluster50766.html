scrub

noun
cleaning by rubbing with a brush and water
Pronunciation: / skrʌb / (AmE), / skrʌb / (BrE)
the act of cleaning something by rubbing it hard, often with a brush and soap and water

Examples

A good scrub
After a long hike, I needed a thorough scrub in the shower.
Give the floor a good scrub before you start painting.
She gave the kitchen sink a quick scrub to make it shine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wash - to clean something using water (verb)
