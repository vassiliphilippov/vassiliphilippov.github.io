pendular

adjective
like a pendulum
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛndjələr / (AmE), / ˈpɛndjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or resembling the motion of a pendulum, especially swinging back and forth in a regular way

Examples

A pendular motion
The scientist observed the pendular motion of the suspended weight during the experiment.
Her mood seemed to follow a pendular pattern, swinging between happiness and sadness.
The bridge swayed in a slow, pendular movement during the strong winds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oscillating - moving back and forth in a regular rhythm
  • antonym: steady - not moving or changing; stable
