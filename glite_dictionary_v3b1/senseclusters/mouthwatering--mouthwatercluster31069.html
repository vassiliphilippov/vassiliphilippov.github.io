mouthwatering

adjective
very appetizing
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊθˌwɔtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊθˌwɔːtərɪŋ / (BrE)
very delicious or appealing, especially in appearance or smell, making you want to eat it

Examples

A mouthwatering dish
The chef prepared a mouthwatering dish that everyone wanted to try.
We could smell the mouthwatering aroma of fresh bread from outside the bakery.
The photos in the cookbook look absolutely mouthwatering and inspired me to cook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appetizing - appealing to the appetite or senses
  • synonym: delicious - having a very pleasant taste or smell
  • antonym: unappetizing - not appealing or attractive to eat
