huk

noun
Filipino guerrilla fighter
9.9
Pronunciation: / huk / (AmE), / huk / (BrE)
a member of the Hukbalahap, a communist guerrilla group active in the Philippines during and after World War II

Examples

A huk fighter
The villagers were cautious whenever a huk was rumored to be nearby.
After the war, many former huks tried to reintegrate into society.
The government launched a campaign to suppress the activities of the huks in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guerrilla - a member of an irregular armed force fighting by sabotage and harassment
  • degree lower: soldier - a person who serves in an army
