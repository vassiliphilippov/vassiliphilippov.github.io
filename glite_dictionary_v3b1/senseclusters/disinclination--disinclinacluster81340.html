disinclination

noun
unwillingness; reluctance
3.2 8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪs.ɪn.klɪˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌdɪsɪnkləˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪs.ɪn.klɪˈneɪ.ʃən /; / ˌdɪsɪŋklɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
a lack of willingness or desire to do something; reluctance or aversion
