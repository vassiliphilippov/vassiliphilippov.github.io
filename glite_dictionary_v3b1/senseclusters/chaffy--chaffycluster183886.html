chaffy

adjective
covered with or resembling chaff
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæfi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːfi / (BrE)
abounding in, covered with, or resembling chaff; having the texture or appearance of chaff.

Examples

The wheat had a chaffy outer layer that needed to be removed.
