misallocation

noun
incorrect assignment
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˌæləˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˌæləˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of assigning resources, money, or effort to the wrong place or purpose

Examples

The misallocation of funds
The company suffered losses due to the misallocation of funds across several projects.
An audit revealed a significant misallocation of expenses in the marketing department.
Proper training can help prevent the misallocation of resources in large organizations.
A misallocation of memory caused the program to crash after several hours of running.
The project failed due to a misallocation of resources among the team members.
Careful planning can help prevent the misallocation of processor time in complex systems.
The misallocation of medical supplies led to shortages in several hospitals.
Government officials investigated the misallocation of public funds in the education sector.
A misallocation of time and effort can prevent a project from succeeding.

  • synonym: distribution - the act of giving out or sharing resources among recipients
  • synonym: misappropriation - the wrongful or dishonest use of funds or resources
  • synonym: mismanagement - the process of handling something badly or wrongly
