loot rail

noun
fair item distribution system in games
5.2
Pronunciation: / lut reɪl / (AmE), / luːt reɪl / (BrE)
A designated system or area in a game where items or rewards are fairly distributed among players.

Examples

The loot rail system
After a tough boss fight, everyone gathered at the loot rail to claim their share.
Some online games implement a loot rail to prevent unfair grabbing of rewards.
The loot rail system ensures all party members receive items equally.
