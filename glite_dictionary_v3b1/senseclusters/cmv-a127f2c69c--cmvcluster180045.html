CMV

noun
online 'Change My View' tag
9.6
Pronunciation: / siː ɛm viː / (AmE), / siː ɛm viː / (BrE)
An internet initialism (Change My View) used to label a post in which the author invites others to present arguments that might persuade them to change their opinion.

Examples

A CMV post
She posted a CMV asking why remote work isn't better for productivity in software teams.
The subreddit requires posters to include a clear argument when they tag a post with CMV.
He started his thread with CMV to invite thoughtful replies challenging his view on remote learning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Change My View - the full phrase used online to request others to challenge the author's opinion
