Cydonian

noun
person from Cydonia
Pronunciation: / saɪˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / saɪˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
a person from Cydonia, an ancient city on Crete, or from the region called Cydonia on Mars in fiction or astronomy

Examples

An ancient Cydonian
The archaeologists believed the pot belonged to a Cydonian who lived on Crete centuries ago.
In science fiction novels, a Cydonian might be imagined as an inhabitant of Mars.
The ancient ruins were said to have been built by a skilled Cydonian craftsman.
