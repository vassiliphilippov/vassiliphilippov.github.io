orgeat

noun
almond syrup
Pronunciation: / ɔrˈʒæt / (AmE), / ˈɔːʒɑː / (BrE)
a sweet syrup made from almonds, sugar, and rose or orange flower water, used in drinks and desserts

Examples

Orgeat syrup in cocktails
The bartender added orgeat to the cocktail for a nutty flavor.
Traditional recipes for Mai Tai often include orgeat as a key ingredient.
She drizzled some orgeat over the fruit salad to give it a sweet, floral taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almond milk - a drink made from ground almonds and water
  • synonym: syrup - a thick, sweet liquid used in food and drinks
