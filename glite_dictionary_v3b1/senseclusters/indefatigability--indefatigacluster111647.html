indefatigability

noun
tireless determination or persistence
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˌfætɪɡəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˌfætɪɡəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
The quality of being incapable of becoming tired; tireless persistence or determination.

Examples

His indefatigability in work
He was known for his indefatigability on the campaign trail, never seeming to need rest.
Her indefatigability in pursuing justice inspired everyone in the community.
The scientist's indefatigability kept the research project moving forward, even during difficult times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fatigue - extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
  • synonym: perseverance - continued effort to do or achieve something despite difficulties
