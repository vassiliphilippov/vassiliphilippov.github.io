muzzle

noun
animal's projecting jaws and nose
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəl / (AmE), / ˈmʌzl / (BrE)
the projecting part of the head of an animal, including the jaws and nose

Examples

A dog's muzzle
A wolf's keen sense of smell comes from its highly sensitive muzzle.
The dog pressed its muzzle against my hand, sniffing curiously.
The horse's muzzle was soft and velvety to the touch.
