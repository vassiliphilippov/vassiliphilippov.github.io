emanant

noun
source of emission
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmənənt / (AmE), / ˈɛmənənt / (BrE)
a person or thing that emits or radiates a quality, substance, or influence.

Examples

A healing emanant
The ancient spring was considered an emanant of healing energy by the local people.
In the story, the mysterious light was an emanant of hope in dark times.
Some philosophers describe the sun as the primary emanant of life on Earth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emitter - something that sends out or gives off something
  • synonym: source - the origin or starting point of something
