paper

noun
scholarly article
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpə / (BrE)
a written work for school or academic purposes, such as an essay or a scholarly article

Examples

A school paper
His paper was cited by several other studies in the field.
She published her first paper on molecular biology last year.
The conference received over 200 papers from researchers around the world.
His paper received high praise from the professor.
I have to finish my paper on Shakespeare before Monday.
She spent the weekend revising her paper for history class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: article - a piece of writing included in a publication, especially a newspaper or magazine
  • synonym: essay - a short piece of writing on a particular subject
  • degree higher: journal - a regular publication containing scholarly articles and research
  • degree lower: report - an official or formal account of findings, often less scholarly than a paper
