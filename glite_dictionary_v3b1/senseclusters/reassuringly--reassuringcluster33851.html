reassuringly

adverb
in a way that calms or comforts
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈʃʊrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈʃʊərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that makes someone feel less worried or anxious.

Examples

Smiled reassuringly
He smiled reassuringly as he told her everything would be okay.
She patted his hand reassuringly before the interview began.
The doctor spoke reassuringly to calm the nervous patient.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiously - in a way that shows worry or nervousness
  • synonym: comfortingly - in a way that gives comfort or solace
