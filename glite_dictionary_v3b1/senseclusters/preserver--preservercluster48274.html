preserver

noun
agent that prevents spoilage
4.0
Pronunciation: / prɪˈzɜːrvər / (AmE), / prɪˈzɜːvə / (BrE)
a substance or agent that keeps something, especially food, from spoiling or being damaged

Examples

A food preserver
Salt is a common preserver used in curing meat.
Refrigeration acts as a powerful preserver for perishable foods.
Vinegar can serve as a natural preserver in pickling vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preservative - a substance used to prevent decay in food or other materials
  • synonym: protector - someone or something that keeps another safe from harm
  • antonym: spoiler - something that causes decay or ruin
