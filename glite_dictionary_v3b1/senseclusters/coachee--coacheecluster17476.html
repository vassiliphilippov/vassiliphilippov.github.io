coachee

noun
person being coached
Pronunciation: / koʊˈtʃiː / (AmE), / kəʊˈtʃiː / (BrE)
a person who is being coached or trained by a coach, especially in a professional or personal development context

Examples

The coachee sets goals
The coachee set clear goals for personal growth during the first session.
A good relationship between the coach and coachee is essential for success.
Each coachee receives personalized feedback to help them improve their skills.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coach - a person who trains or instructs others
  • synonym: trainee - a person who is being trained for a job or skill
