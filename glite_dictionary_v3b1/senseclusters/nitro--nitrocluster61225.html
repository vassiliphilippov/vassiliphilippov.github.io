nitro

adjective
containing nitro group
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtroʊ / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtrəʊ / (BrE)
containing or relating to the nitro group (—NO2), especially in chemical compounds

Examples

Nitro compound
The laboratory handled several nitro compounds with extreme caution due to their reactivity.
A nitro group was added to the molecule to change its chemical properties.
Explosives often contain nitro groups that make them highly energetic.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: nitroso - containing the nitroso group (—NO)
