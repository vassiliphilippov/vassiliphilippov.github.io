body plethysmograph

noun
device for measuring lung function
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑdi plɛˈθɪzməˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈbɒdi plɪˈθɪzməˌɡrɑːf / (BrE)
A medical device consisting of a chamber that surrounds the entire body, used to measure lung volumes and respiratory function.

Examples

A body plethysmograph device
A body plethysmograph provides accurate measurements for various respiratory studies.
Researchers used a body plethysmograph to determine the subject’s total lung capacity.
The patient was placed inside the body plethysmograph to assess his lung function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plethysmograph - an instrument for measuring changes in volume within an organ or whole body
