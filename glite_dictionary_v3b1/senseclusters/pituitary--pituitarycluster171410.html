pituitary

adjective
relating to the pituitary gland
5.8
Pronunciation: / pɪˈtuːɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / pɪˈtjuːɪˌtəri / (BrE)
relating to or associated with the pituitary gland, a small organ at the base of the brain that regulates various body functions

Examples

Pituitary disorder
Doctors examined her for signs of pituitary abnormalities.
He was diagnosed with a pituitary disorder that affected his growth.
The pituitary hormones play a crucial role in regulating metabolism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pituitary gland - a small gland at the base of the brain responsible for hormone production
