flushable

adjective
level with surface
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌʃ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈflʌʃ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)

Examples

The architect recommended flushable panels to create a smooth, continuous surface.
