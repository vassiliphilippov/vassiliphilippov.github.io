flushable

adjective
level with surface
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌʃəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈflʌʃəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be installed or positioned so that it is level or even with a surrounding surface.

Examples

Flushable wall panel
The new light switch is flushable, making it blend seamlessly with the wall.
For a modern look, choose flushable floor vents that do not stick out.
The architect recommended flushable panels to create a smooth, continuous surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protruding - sticking out from a surface
  • synonym: recessed - set into a surface so as to be level or below it
