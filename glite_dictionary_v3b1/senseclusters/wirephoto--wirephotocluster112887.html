wirephoto

verb
to send photo electronically
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌfoʊtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌfəʊtəʊ / (BrE)
to send a photograph electronically, especially for publication in newspapers or news agencies

Examples

To wirephoto a picture
The reporter will wirephoto the pictures from the scene to the newsroom.
They wirephotoed the winning team's celebration to newspapers across the country.
Before digital cameras, journalists often had to wirephoto images for urgent news stories.
