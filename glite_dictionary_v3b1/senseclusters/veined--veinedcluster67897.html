veined

adjective
marked with lines like veins
7.1
Pronunciation: / veɪnd / (AmE), / veɪnd / (BrE)
having lines, streaks, or markings that resemble veins, often used to describe leaves, marble, or similar surfaces

Examples

Veined marble surface
His hands were thin and veined after years of hard work.
She admired the veined leaves, their patterns glowing in the sunlight.
The marble countertop was beautifully veined with grey and gold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: veinless - lacking veins or vein-like markings
