phonics

noun
teaching reading by letter-sound association
4.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑː.nɪks / (AmE), / ˈfəʊ.nɪks / (BrE)
a method of teaching people to read by training them to associate letters or groups of letters with the sounds they represent
