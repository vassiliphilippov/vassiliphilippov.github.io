bratwurst with kraut

noun
bratwurst sausage with pickled cabbage
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːt.wɜrst wɪð kraʊt / (AmE), / ˈbrat.wɜːst wɪð kraʊt / (BrE)
