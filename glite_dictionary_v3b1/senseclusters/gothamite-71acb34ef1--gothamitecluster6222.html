Gothamite

noun
resident of Gotham City
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθəˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθəˌmaɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Gotham City, the fictional city in DC Comics associated with Batman

Examples

Gothamite in Batman's city
Every Gothamite knows to be cautious when the city gets dark.
The mayor promised to improve safety for every Gothamite after the recent events.
As a lifelong Gothamite, she had seen the city change many times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: New Yorker - a person from New York City
