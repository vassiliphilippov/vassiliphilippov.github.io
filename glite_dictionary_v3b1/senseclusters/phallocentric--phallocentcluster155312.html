phallocentric

adjective
male-centered perspective
Pronunciation: / ˌfæloʊˈsɛntrɪk /; / ˌfæl.oʊˈsɛn.trɪk / (AmE), / ˌfæləʊˈsɛntrɪk /; / ˌfæl.əʊˈsɛn.trɪk / (BrE)
focused on or centered around male perspectives, values, or the male sexual organ, especially in culture or theory, often ignoring other viewpoints

Examples

A phallocentric view
Critics argued that the film's narrative was overly phallocentric and ignored female perspectives.
The academic debate challenged the phallocentric assumptions present in traditional theories.
Some literature from the era is now seen as phallocentric in its themes and character portrayals.
The novel was criticized for its phallocentric portrayal of power and authority.
Some theories in early psychoanalysis were strongly phallocentric in their assumptions about gender.
Feminist scholars often challenge phallocentric interpretations in literature and art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: androcentric - centered on men or the male point of view
  • antonym: feminist - supporting women's rights and equality
  • antonym: gynocentric - focused on women or the female perspective
