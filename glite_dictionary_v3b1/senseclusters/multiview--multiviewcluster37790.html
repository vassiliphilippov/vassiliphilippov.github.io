multiview

noun
technical drawing views
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (BrE)
a technical drawing that shows multiple orthographic views, such as the front, side, and top, of an object

Examples

Multiview technical drawing
The engineer prepared a multiview of the machine part to show every important detail.
Students learned how to create a multiview to accurately represent the object from different angles.
A clear multiview helps manufacturers understand the exact dimensions of a product.

Related Terms

  • antonym: isometric drawing - a drawing showing a three-dimensional object in two dimensions with all axes at equal angles
  • synonym: orthographic projection - a method of representing three-dimensional objects in two dimensions
