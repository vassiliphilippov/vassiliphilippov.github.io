harebell

noun
small plant with blue bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌbɛl / (AmE), / ˈheə.bɛl /; / ˈheəˌbɛl / (BrE)
a small, slender wild plant with delicate, blue, bell-shaped flowers, found in grasslands and rocky places

Examples

The harebell flower

Hikers admired the delicate harebell growing between the rocks along the trail.
