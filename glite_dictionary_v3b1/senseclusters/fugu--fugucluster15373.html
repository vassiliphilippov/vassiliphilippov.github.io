fugu

noun
a poisonous Japanese blowfish delicacy
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːɡu / (AmE), / ˈfuːɡu / (BrE)
a Japanese delicacy prepared from certain species of blowfish, which can be highly poisonous if not properly prepared

Examples

Japanese fugu dish
Many tourists visit Japan hoping to try authentic fugu at a reputable restaurant.
Preparing fugu requires special training because its organs contain lethal toxins.
The chef skillfully sliced the raw fugu and served it as sashimi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowfish - a type of fish known for inflating its body, some species are poisonous
  • synonym: pufferfish - a fish that can inflate itself and may be toxic
