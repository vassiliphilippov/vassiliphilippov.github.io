harmlessness

noun
lack of harm
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈhɑːmləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not causing harm or danger

Examples

The harmlessness of medicine
The harmlessness of the new medication was confirmed after several clinical trials.
Parents appreciated the harmlessness of the educational games provided to their children.
Despite its size, the animal's harmlessness made it a popular pet.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dangerousness - the quality of being likely to cause harm
  • synonym: innocuousness - the quality of being unlikely to cause harm
