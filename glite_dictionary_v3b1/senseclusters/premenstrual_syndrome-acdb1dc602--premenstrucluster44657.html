premenstrual syndrome

noun
symptoms before menstruation
3.6 0.17/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåpriÀêÀàm…õnstru…ôl Ààs…™nÀådro äm / (AmE), / ÀåpriÀêÀàm…õnstru…ôl Ààs…™nÀådr…ô äm / (BrE)
A group of physical and emotional symptoms that occur in many women during the days leading up to menstruation.

Examples

Premenstrual syndrome symptoms
Many women experience premenstrual syndrome with symptoms such as irritability and bloating.
Regular exercise may help relieve the effects of premenstrual syndrome.
She visited her doctor to discuss treatment options for premenstrual syndrome.
