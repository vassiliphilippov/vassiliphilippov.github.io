Rhynchospora

noun
beak-rush plant
Pronunciation: / rɪŋˈkɑspərə / (AmE), / rɪŋˈkɒspərə / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Rhynchospora, commonly known as beak-rush, found in wet or marshy areas

The distinctive beak-shaped fruit helps identify a Rhynchospora in the field.
