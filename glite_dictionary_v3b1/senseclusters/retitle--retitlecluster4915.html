retitle

verb
to change ownership
6.6
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈtaɪ.təl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtaɪ.təl/ /; / /rə.ˈtaɪ.təl/ (AmE), /riː.ˈtaɪ.təl/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtaɪ.təl/ /; / /rə.ˈtaɪ.təl/ (BrE)
to change the legal title or registered ownership of property, such as a vehicle or real estate, to another person or entity

Examples

To retitle a car
After purchasing the house, they had to retitle it in both of their names.
You must retitle your car at the DMV when you sell it to someone else.
The lawyer explained how to retitle the property after the inheritance was finalized.
