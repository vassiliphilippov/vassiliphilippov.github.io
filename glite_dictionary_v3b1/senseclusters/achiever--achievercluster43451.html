achiever

noun
person who attains success
2.4
Pronunciation: / əˈtʃivər / (AmE), / əˈtʃiːvə / (BrE)
a person who achieves success or attains goals, especially through effort and ability

Examples

A top achiever
As a top achiever, she was often recognized at school assemblies for her accomplishments.
Despite many obstacles, he became an achiever in his chosen profession.
The company values every achiever who helps drive our progress forward.

Related Terms

  • antonym: failure - a person who does not succeed
  • synonym: success - a person who is successful
