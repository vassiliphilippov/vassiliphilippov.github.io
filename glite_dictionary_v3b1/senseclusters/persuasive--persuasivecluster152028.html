Examples

Persuasive argument

Synonyms

  • convincing
    3.4 11/m
    convincing gives clear, logical reasons that make people accept a claim, while persuasive often uses forceful argument to change beliefs or actions.
  • compelling
    5.1 23/m
    compelling feels strong, urgent, and hard to ignore, while persuasive focuses on reasoned argument to convince someone to believe or act.
