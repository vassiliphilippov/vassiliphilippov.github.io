extemporize

verb
make do with what is available; improvise
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛmpəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪz /; / ɪkˈstɛmpərəʌɪz /; / ɪkˈstɛmpərʌɪz / (BrE)
to do or create something without preparation; to improvise
