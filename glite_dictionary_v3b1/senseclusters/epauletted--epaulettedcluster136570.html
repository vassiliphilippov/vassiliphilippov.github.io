epauletted

adjective
with epaulettes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛpəˌlɛtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɛpəlɛtɪd / (BrE)
wearing or decorated with epaulettes, which are ornamental shoulder pieces on clothing

Examples

Epauletted uniform jacket
The officer's epauletted jacket made him stand out during the parade.
She admired the epauletted uniforms worn by the marching band.
A row of epauletted soldiers stood at attention in the grand hall.
