blanket

noun
a covering layer or something that covers
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
a thick covering or layer that envelops something, especially used metaphorically to refer to anything that covers a surface completely

Examples

A blanket of fog
A thick blanket of fog descended over the city in the early morning.
Smoke created a dark blanket that hid the buildings from view.
The ground was covered by a blanket of leaves in the autumn.
