positive feedback

noun
affirming or praising comments
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːzətɪv ˈfiːdbæk / (AmE), / ˈpɒzətɪv ˈfiːdbak / (BrE)
comments or responses that affirm, praise, or encourage someone's performance, work, or behavior

Examples

Receive positive feedback
Her manager gave positive feedback on her recent presentation, which boosted her confidence.
Students are more motivated when they regularly receive positive feedback on their work.
The team meeting ended with plenty of positive feedback for everyone's efforts during the project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval of someone or something based on perceived faults or mistakes
