mincy

adjective
affectedly dainty
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnsi / (AmE), / ˈmɪnsi / (BrE)
behaving or moving in an affectedly dainty, delicate, or overly careful manner

Examples

A mincy walk
She took a mincy walk down the hallway, careful not to step on her dress.
His mincy gestures made the performance seem overly dramatic to the audience.
The cat moved in a mincy way, barely making a sound on the wooden floor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkward - lacking grace or ease in movement
  • synonym: dainty - delicate and graceful in movement or manner
