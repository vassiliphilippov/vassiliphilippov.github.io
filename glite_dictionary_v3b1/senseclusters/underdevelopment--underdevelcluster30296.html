underdevelopment

noun
insufficient or incomplete development
1.5 4.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dər.dɪ.ˈvel.əp.mənt/ (AmE), /ʌn.də.dɪ.ˈvel.əp.mənt/ (BrE)
The condition of not having developed sufficiently, especially in economic, social, or physical aspects.

Examples

Underdevelopment of the brain
Health issues can result from the underdevelopment of vital organs during childhood.
Many experts focus on the causes of underdevelopment in sub-Saharan Africa.
The persistent underdevelopment of rural regions hampers national economic progress.
