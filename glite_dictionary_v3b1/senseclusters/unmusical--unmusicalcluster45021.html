unmusical

adjective
lacking musical talent or interest
5.3 0.03/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàmjuÀêz…™k…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàmjuÀêz…™k…ôl / (BrE)
not having interest in or talent for music; not musical.

Examples

Unmusical person
Although her family played several instruments, she remained completely unmusical.
His unmusical background made learning the piano especially challenging.
It was obvious from his singing that he was rather unmusical.
