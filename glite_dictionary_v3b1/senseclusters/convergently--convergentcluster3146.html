convergently

adverb
in a focusing manner
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrdʒəntli / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒəntli / (BrE)
in a way that causes rays, lines, or paths to come together at a single point.

Examples

Light focuses convergently
The microscope lens focuses light convergently to create a sharp image.
In geometry, lines meeting convergently form an angle at their intersection.
The scientist explained how the waves moved convergently toward the center of the dish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divergently - in a way that causes things to move apart from a point
