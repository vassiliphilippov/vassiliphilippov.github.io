salamander

noun
legendary fire-dwelling reptile
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləˌmændɚ / (AmE), / ˈsæləˌmændə / (BrE)
A mythical reptilian creature reputed to live in and withstand fire.

Examples

Legendary salamander
According to medieval legends, a salamander could walk through flames unscathed.
Stories of the salamander fascinated scholars who believed in creatures impervious to fire.
The alchemist claimed he had seen a salamander emerge from the hearth.
