stray cat

noun
abandoned or homeless domestic cat
1.7 1.2
Pronunciation: / streɪ kæt / (AmE), / streɪ kæt / (BrE)
A domestic cat that has become homeless or has been abandoned and lives without an owner, often wandering freely.

Examples

A stray cat
She began feeding the stray cat that appeared in her backyard every morning.
The city runs a program to help stray cats get adopted or returned to their owners.
We found a stray cat sheltering under our porch during the storm.
