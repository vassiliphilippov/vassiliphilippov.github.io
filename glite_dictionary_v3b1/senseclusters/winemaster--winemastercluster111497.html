winemaster

noun
wine production expert
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪnˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌmɑːstə / (BrE)

Examples

Winemaster selects grapes

During the tour, the winemaster shared secrets about the aging process of red wines.
A skilled winemaster can influence the flavor and quality of every bottle produced.
