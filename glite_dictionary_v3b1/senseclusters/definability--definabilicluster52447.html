definability

noun
the quality of being definable
Pronunciation: / dɪˌfaɪnəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / dɪˌfaɪnəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being capable of being clearly defined, limited, or explained

Examples

The definability of terms
In logic, researchers often discuss the definability of certain properties within a system.
The definability of mathematical concepts is essential for formal proofs.
The limits of definability can impact how laws or terms are interpreted.

  • synonym: definable - able to be defined or described clearly
