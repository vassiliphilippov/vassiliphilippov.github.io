Henry Louis Gehrig

proper noun
American baseball player (1903–1941)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnri ˈluːɪs ˈɡɛrɪɡ / (AmE), / ˈhɛnri ˈluːɪs ˈɡɛrɪɡ / (BrE)
(1903–1941) American baseball player famed for his record-breaking career and for being diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, now often called Lou Gehrig's disease.

Examples

Henry Louis Gehrig baseball player
