to arrange for or reserve something in advance, such as accommodation, tickets, or the services of someone

Examples

To book a hotel

Synonyms

  • reserve
    3.2 10/m
    reserve sets something aside for your future use, while book arranges and confirms that choice with a time and place.
  • book in
    3.3 0.04/m
    book in registers or records you as arriving for a stay or event, while book means to arrange or reserve something beforehand.
  • hold
    1.3 110/m
    hold keeps something set aside for someone, or pauses action, while book specifically arranges and confirms a reservation ahead of time.
