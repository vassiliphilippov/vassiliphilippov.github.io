antidogmatic

adjective
opposed to dogma
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪdɔgˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntɪdɒgˈmætɪk / (BrE)
opposed to dogma or rigid, unquestioned beliefs

Examples

Antidogmatic teaching style
The professor's antidogmatic teaching style encouraged students to question traditional theories.
An antidogmatic attitude is important in scientific research to avoid bias.
Her antidogmatic views made her popular among those seeking open discussion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dogmatic - insisting on strict adherence to beliefs
  • synonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas
