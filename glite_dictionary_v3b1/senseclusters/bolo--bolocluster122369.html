bolo

noun
sweet round cake
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊloʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊləʊ / (BrE)
a round, often sweet bread or cake, especially found in Portuguese or Latin American cuisine

Examples

A bolo cake
She served a homemade bolo with coffee after dinner.
At the bakery, I bought a delicious bolo filled with coconut cream.
For his birthday, they baked a traditional Portuguese bolo decorated with fruit.
