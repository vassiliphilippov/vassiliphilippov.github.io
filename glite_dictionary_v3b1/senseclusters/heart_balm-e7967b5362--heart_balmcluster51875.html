heart balm

noun
something that comforts emotionally
Pronunciation: / hɑrt bɑm / (AmE), / hɑːt bɑːm / (BrE)
something that provides emotional comfort or soothes emotional pain

Examples

A heart balm remedy
After her breakup, spending time with friends was a true heart balm for her wounded spirit.
Memories of their childhood together were a heart balm in his moments of sadness.
That gentle letter acted as a heart balm amid the chaos of the day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consolation - the comfort received after a loss or disappointment
  • antonym: grief - intense sorrow, especially caused by someone's death
  • synonym: solace - comfort or consolation in a time of distress
