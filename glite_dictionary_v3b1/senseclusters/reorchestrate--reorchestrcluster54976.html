reorchestrate

verb
to arrange music again
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɔːrkɪstreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɔːkɪstreɪt / (BrE)

They will reorchestrate the musical to give it a more modern sound for the new production.
