portico

noun
covered entrance with columns
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːr.tɪ.koʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔː.tɪ.kəʊ / (BrE)
a covered entrance to a building, usually supported by columns and forming a porch

Examples

A portico entrance
Several columns supported the marble portico facing the public square.
The grand portico at the museum's entrance impressed every visitor.
They took shelter under the church's portico when it started raining.
