cecropia

noun
a large North American silkworm moth
Pronunciation: / səˈkroʊpiə / (AmE), / sɪˈkrəʊpiə / (BrE)
A large North American silkworm moth (Hyalophora cecropia) with brown and red markings; its larvae feed on the leaves of forest trees.
