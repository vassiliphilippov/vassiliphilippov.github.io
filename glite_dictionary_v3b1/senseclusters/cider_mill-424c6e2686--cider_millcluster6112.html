cider mill

noun
mill for pressing apples for cider
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdər mɪl / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdə mɪl / (BrE)
a mill where apples are pressed to extract juice, typically for making apple cider

Examples

An old cider mill
Every autumn, our family visits the old cider mill to watch apples being pressed.
He learned to operate the cider mill from his grandfather, who ran the business for decades.
The cider mill down the road sells fresh apple cider and donuts all season.
