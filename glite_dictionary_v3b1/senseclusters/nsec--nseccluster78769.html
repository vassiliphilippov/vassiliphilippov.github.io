nsec

noun
nanosecond abbreviation
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnˌsɛk / (AmE), / ˈɛnˌsɛk / (BrE)
an abbreviation for nanosecond, a unit of time equal to one billionth of a second

Examples

One nsec delay
The processor can execute billions of instructions in just one nsec.
Signal delays in the circuit are measured in nsec for accuracy.
It only took a few nsec for the data to transfer between devices.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: microsecond - a unit of time equal to one millionth of a second
  • synonym: nanosecond - a unit of time equal to one billionth of a second
  • degree lower: picosecond - a unit of time equal to one trillionth of a second
