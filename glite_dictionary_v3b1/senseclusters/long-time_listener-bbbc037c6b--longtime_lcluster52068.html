long-time listener

noun
person listening for a long period
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔŋ.taɪm ˈlɪs.nɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɒŋ.taɪm ˈlɪs.nə / (BrE)
a person who has regularly listened to a broadcast, program, or show for an extended period of time

Examples

A long-time listener
As a long-time listener, he felt personally connected to the host.
Many long-time listeners appreciated the anniversary special episode.
She has been a long-time listener of the morning talk show.
