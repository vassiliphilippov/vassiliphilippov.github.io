replicative

adjective
able to reproduce
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛplɪˌkeɪtɪv / (AmE), / ˈrɛplɪkətɪv / (BrE)
relating to or capable of making a copy or reproducing, especially at the cellular or molecular level

Examples

Replicative cycle
The replicative cycle of the virus determines how quickly it can infect new cells.
Scientists studied the replicative ability of the bacteria under different conditions.
Certain drugs can inhibit the replicative process of harmful microorganisms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: destructive - causing damage or destruction rather than reproduction
  • synonym: reproductive - relating to the process of producing offspring or copies
