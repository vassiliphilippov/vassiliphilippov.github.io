quadratic

noun
a second-degree polynomial
3.4
Pronunciation: / kwɑˈdrætɪk /; / kwəˈdrætɪk / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrætɪk / (BrE)
a mathematical expression or equation in which the highest power of the variable is two (degree two)

The graph of a quadratic is always a parabola.
