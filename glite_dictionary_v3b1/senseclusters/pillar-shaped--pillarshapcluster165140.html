pillar-shaped

adjective
shaped like a pillar
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlər ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈpɪlə ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape of a cylindrical pillar or column.

Examples

Pillar-shaped rock formations
The pillar-shaped rock formations attract tourists from all over the world.
The architect designed a pillar-shaped sculpture for the entrance of the building.
The strange fungus had a pillar-shaped stem rising above the forest floor.
