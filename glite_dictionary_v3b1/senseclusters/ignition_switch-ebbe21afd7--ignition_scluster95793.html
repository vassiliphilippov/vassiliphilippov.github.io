ignition switch

noun
switch for starting a vehicle engine
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɪʃən swɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɪʃən swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a switch in a motor vehicle that activates the electrical circuit needed to start the engine

Examples

A ignition switch in car
A faulty ignition switch can prevent your car from starting.
After losing her keys, she had the ignition switch replaced by a mechanic.
He turned the ignition switch and waited for the engine to start.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starter - a device that starts the engine of a vehicle
