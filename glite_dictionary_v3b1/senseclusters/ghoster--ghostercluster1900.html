ghoster

noun
person who cuts contact
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊstər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊstə / (BrE)
a person who suddenly ends all communication with someone, especially in social or dating situations, without explanation

Examples

Online ghoster
After their third date, he became a ghoster and never replied to her messages.
Being a ghoster can leave the other person confused and hurt.
She realized she had been talking to a ghoster when all communication suddenly stopped.

Related Terms

  • antonym: friend - a person with whom one has a bond of mutual affection
  • synonym: ghost - to suddenly stop communicating with someone
