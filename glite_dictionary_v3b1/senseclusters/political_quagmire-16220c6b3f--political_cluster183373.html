political quagmire

noun
complicated, difficult political situation
5.3
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdɪkəl ˈkwæɡˌmaɪɚ / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈkwæɡˌmaɪə / (BrE)
a complicated and difficult political situation that is hard to resolve or escape

Examples

A political quagmire
Debate about the legislation quickly turned into a political quagmire for both parties.
New reforms are needed to help the government escape its current political quagmire.
The country found itself in a political quagmire after months of disputed elections.
