Examples

Synonyms

  • wildfire
    3.0 3.99/m
    wildfire is a large, fast-spreading uncontrolled fire over natural land, while bushfire is the Australian name for these fires.
  • brush fire
    4.1 0.20/m
    brush fire burns shrubs and low plants in small areas, while bushfire often means larger, more dangerous fires in wild vegetation.
