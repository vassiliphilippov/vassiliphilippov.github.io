bushfire

noun
wild vegetation fire
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a fire that burns out of control in areas of wild vegetation, especially in Australia

Examples

A bushfire spreads
The bushfire destroyed thousands of hectares of land before firefighters could contain it.
During the summer, many communities prepare for the risk of bushfires by clearing dry vegetation.
Smoke from the distant bushfire could be seen for miles across the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forest fire - a large fire that spreads quickly through a forest
  • synonym: wildfire - an uncontrolled fire in a forest or wild area
