speak

verb
produce a characteristic or expressive sound
Pronunciation: / spiːk / (AmE), / spiːk / (BrE)
(of an object or thing) to produce a characteristic or natural sound, often in a way that seems expressive or meaningful.

Examples

Thunder speaks loudly
The thunder spoke across the midnight sky.
When the old piano finally spoke, its notes filled the room with warmth.
With every beat, the drums spoke to the crowd in their own language.
