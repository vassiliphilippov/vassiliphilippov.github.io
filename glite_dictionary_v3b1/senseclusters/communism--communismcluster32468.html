communism

noun
political system of collective ownership
5.0 11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀê.mjuÀån…™.z…ôm /; / Ààk…ëÀê.mj…ôÀån…™z…ôm / (AmE), / Ààk…ím.juÀêÀån…™.z…ôm /; / Ààk…í.mj äÀån…™z…ôm / (BrE)
a political and economic system in which all property is owned collectively or publicly, and people are paid according to their abilities and needs, aiming for a classless society
