communism

noun
political system of collective ownership
1.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑː.mjə.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈkɑː.mjʊ.nɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.mjʊ.nɪ.zəm/ /; / /ˈkɒ.mjə.nɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a political and economic system in which all property is owned collectively or publicly, and people are paid according to their abilities and needs, aiming for a classless society

Examples

The history of communism
He studied the history of communism in Eastern Europe for his thesis.
Many countries in the 20th century experimented with communism as an alternative to capitalism.
The ideals of communism include the abolition of private property and a classless society.
Critics of communism argue that it limits individual freedom and economic growth.
He spent years studying the theories and practice of communism.
The rise of communism in Eastern Europe had a major impact on global politics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalism - an economic system based on private ownership and free markets
  • synonym: socialism - a political and economic system advocating collective or government ownership of the means of production
