shibori

noun
Japanese tie-dyed fabric
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (AmE), / ʃɪˈbɔːri / (BrE)
a piece of fabric that has been dyed or patterned using a traditional Japanese tie-dye technique

Examples

A shibori scarf
She bought a beautiful shibori scarf at the craft market.
The artist displayed several shibori wall hangings at the exhibition.
He learned how to make shibori patterns during his trip to Japan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: batik - a fabric-dyeing method using wax to create patterns
  • synonym: tie-dye - a method of dyeing fabric by tying it to produce patterns
