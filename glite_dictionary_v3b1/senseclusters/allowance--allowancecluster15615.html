allowance

noun
permissible difference or margin
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊəns / (AmE), / əˈlaʊəns / (BrE)
an amount or margin allowed, especially as a permissible difference or degree of flexibility within limits

Examples

A design allowance
The engineer built an allowance for thermal expansion into the bridge design.
The measurement is accurate to within the allowance specified by regulations.
You need to make an allowance for human error when planning the schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: margin - an amount allowed beyond what is needed
  • synonym: tolerance - the allowable deviation from a standard
