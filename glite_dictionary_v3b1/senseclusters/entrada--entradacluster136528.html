entrada

noun
passage or entrance
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (AmE), / ɛnˈtrɑːdə / (BrE)

Examples

Stone entrada
The old church has a beautiful stone entrada leading to its main hall.

We waited by the entrada until our friends arrived for the tour.
