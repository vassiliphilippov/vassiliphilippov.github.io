bath plug

noun

A stopper used to seal the drain of a bath, washbasin, or similar water container, preventing water from escaping.

Examples

A bath plug stopper
After washing, please remember to replace the bath plug so no water leaks out.
He reached down to pull out the bath plug and let the water drain away.
The hotel bathroom was missing its bath plug, so I couldn't fill the tub.

Synonyms

  • basin plug
    3.0 0.00/m
    a basin plug fits small washbasins and tubs to hold water, while a bath plug names the stopper used for bath drains.
  • tub stopper
    2.8 0.00/m
    a tub stopper seals a bathtub drain during a bath, while a bath plug is the general stopper for any bath drain.
  • sink plug
    2.5 0.00/m
    a sink plug blocks a basin or kitchen sink drain for washing, while a bath plug specifically seals a bath or bathtub drain.
  • drainplug
    5.2 0.00/m
