to form or establish a group, organization, or union

Examples

To organize a union
Efforts to organize the warehouse workers were met with resistance from management.
She helped her colleagues organize so they could demand better working conditions.

Activists worked together to organize the student group.

Synonyms

  • form
    1.0 48/m
    form means to bring people together to create a group, while organize means to set up and arrange its work.
  • found
    3.9 30/m
    found is to start an institution or company officially, while organize means form a group and arrange how it works.
  • constitute
    4.8 15/m
    constitute is to set up something in a formal way, often by law or rule, while organize means form and arrange a group.
