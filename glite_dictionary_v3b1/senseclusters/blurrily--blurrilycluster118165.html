blurrily

adverb
unclearly; indistinctly
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈblɜːrɪli / (AmE), / ˈblɜːrɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is not clear or is difficult to see or understand

Examples

He remembered the events of that night only blurrily, as if in a dream.
The words on the chalkboard appeared blurrily to students sitting at the back of the classroom.
