incher

noun
object measured in inches
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈɪntʃə / (BrE)
an object or unit that measures or is measured in inches, often used to specify the size of something

Examples

A twelve-incher
He bought a twelve-incher to use as a ruler.
The new television is a fifty-five incher, perfect for watching movies.
She used a six-incher to measure the length of the fabric.

Related Terms
