assay

noun
written report of analytical test results
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæseɪ / (AmE), / ˈæseɪ / (BrE)
a written report containing the results of an analysis, especially of the composition or quality of a substance

Examples

Assay report
A written assay confirmed the purity of the newly discovered chemical compound.
According to the assay, the medication contained the correct amount of active ingredient.
The laboratory issued an assay detailing the mineral content of the ore sample.
