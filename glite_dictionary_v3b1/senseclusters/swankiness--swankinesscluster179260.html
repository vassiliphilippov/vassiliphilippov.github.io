swankiness

noun
stylish luxury
Pronunciation: / ˈswæŋkinəs / (AmE), / ˈswæŋkɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being stylish, luxurious, or impressively showy

Examples

The swankiness of restaurant
The swankiness of the restaurant made it a popular spot for celebrations.
He admired the swankiness of her new apartment, with its modern furniture and large windows.
Despite its swankiness, the club welcomed everyone with a friendly atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elegance - the quality of being graceful and stylish
  • synonym: luxury - a state of great comfort and extravagant living
  • antonym: plainness - the quality of being simple or lacking decoration
