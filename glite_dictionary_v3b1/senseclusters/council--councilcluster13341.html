council

noun
formal meeting for consultation
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl /; / ˈkaʊnsəl / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl /; / ˈkaʊnsəl / (BrE)
a group of people who meet to discuss, manage, or advise on important matters; or the meeting itself

Examples

City council meeting
An international council was convened to discuss environmental policy.
Delegates from each department attended the monthly council.
The city held a special council to address the emergency.
A local council has been set up to oversee the festival planning.
She was elected president of the student council this year.
The city council voted unanimously to approve the new housing project.

  • synonym: committee - A group of people appointed for a specific function
