bathroom break

noun
pause to use the bathroom
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθ.ruːm breɪk / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθ.ruːm breɪk / (BrE)
a short or temporary pause in an activity to use the bathroom

Examples

Take a bathroom break
She asked her teacher if she could take a bathroom break during the exam.
The meeting was paused for a quick bathroom break after an hour.
We usually schedule a bathroom break halfway through the road trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restroom break - a brief pause to use the restroom
  • synonym: toilet break - brief pause to use the toilet (British English)
