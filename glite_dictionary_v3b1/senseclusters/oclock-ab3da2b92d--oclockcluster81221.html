o'clock

adverb
used to state the hour (time)
Pronunciation: / əˈklɑk / (AmE), / əˈklɒk / (BrE)
Used to indicate the exact hour in telling the time of day.

Examples

At midnight o'clock
By midnight o'clock, the party had ended and most guests had gone.
She usually arrives home from work around five o'clock.
The train is scheduled to depart at six o'clock exactly.
