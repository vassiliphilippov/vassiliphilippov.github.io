punctuation

noun
marks used in writing for clarity and separation
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌŋktʃuˈeɪʃən /; / ˌpʌŋk.tʃuˈeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpʌŋktjʊˈeɪʃən /; / ˌpʌŋk.tjʊˈeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the marks (such as periods, commas, and question marks) used in writing, and the system or rules for using them to make meaning clear

Related Terms

  • synonym: comma - a punctuation mark (,) used to indicate a pause or separation in sentences
  • synonym: period - a punctuation mark (.) used to indicate the end of a sentence
