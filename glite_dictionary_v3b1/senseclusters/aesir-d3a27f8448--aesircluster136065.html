Aesir

noun
principal gods in Norse mythology
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪ.sɪr / (AmE), / ˈeɪ.sɪə / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the principal group of gods who live in Asgard and are associated with power and war.

Examples

The Aesir gods
According to the mythology, the Aesir waged war against the Vanir.
Odin is considered the chief of the Aesir in Norse legend.
The Aesir resided in Asgard and interacted with both humans and giants.
