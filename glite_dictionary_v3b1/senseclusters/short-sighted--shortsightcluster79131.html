Lacking foresight or consideration for the future; making decisions without regard for long-term consequences.

Examples

A short-sighted decision
Critics argue that funding cuts are a short-sighted response to economic pressure.
Her short-sighted approach failed to account for future market changes.
The board's short-sighted policy ignored the environmental impact of the project.

Synonyms

  • myopic
    8.4 0.91/m
    myopic is used for a narrow, inward view or formal critique, while short-sighted is common for everyday poor long-term decisions.
  • improvident
    9.1 0.11/m
    improvident often describes failing to save money or plan financially, while short-sighted covers general lack of future thinking.
  • visionless
    7.2 0.03/m
    visionless describes someone with no clear plan or imagination, while short-sighted focuses on not thinking about future effects.
