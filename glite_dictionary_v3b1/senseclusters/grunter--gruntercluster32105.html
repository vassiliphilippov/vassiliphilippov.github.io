grunter

noun
person who grunts
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌntər / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌntə / (BrE)
a person who makes low, guttural vocal sounds, typically to express effort or discomfort

Examples

A gym grunter
During the heavy lifting session, the gym was full of grunters.
He was known as a grunter on the tennis court, making noise with every serve.
Some people find it distracting when a runner is a loud grunter on the track.
