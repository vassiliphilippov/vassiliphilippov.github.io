nonsuccess

noun
unsuccessful person
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.səkˈsɛs / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.səkˈsɛs / (BrE)
a person who does not achieve success or is regarded as unsuccessful

Examples

A nonsuccess in school
Despite his hard work, some labeled him a nonsuccess because he never won any awards.
She refused to let one setback define her as a nonsuccess in her career.
The media often focuses on winners, ignoring the stories of every nonsuccess who tried their best.
