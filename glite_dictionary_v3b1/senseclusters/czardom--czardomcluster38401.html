czardom

noun
rule of a czar
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààz…ëÀêrÀåd…ôm / (AmE), / Ààz…ëÀêd…ôm / (BrE)
the domain, rule, or period during which a czar governs a country, especially in Russia

Examples

The Russian czardom
The Russian czardom was known for its powerful rulers and vast territories.
During the czardom, many reforms were introduced to modernize the country.
Historians often debate the legacy of the czardom in shaping Russian society.
