bettong

noun
a small short-nosed Australian marsupial
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛt.ɑŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɛt.ɒŋ / (BrE)
a small Australian marsupial with a short nose, related to the kangaroo and sometimes called a rat kangaroo

Examples
