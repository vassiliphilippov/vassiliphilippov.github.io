tetanilla

noun
syndrome of muscle twitching and cramps
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛtəˈnɪlə / (AmE), / ˌtɛtəˈnɪlə / (BrE)
A clinical neurological syndrome marked by muscular twitching, cramps, and sometimes seizures, usually linked to calcium deficiency or related conditions.

Examples

Muscle tetanilla cramps
Hypoparathyroidism can sometimes lead to tetanilla if left untreated.
Symptoms of tetanilla may include not only cramps but also sporadic seizures in severe cases.
The doctor suspected tetanilla because of the patient's persistent muscle cramps and twitching.
