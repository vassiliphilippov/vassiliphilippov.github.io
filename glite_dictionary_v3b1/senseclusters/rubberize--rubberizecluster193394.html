rubberize

verb
to coat with rubber
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌb.əˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.ə.raɪz / (BrE)
to coat or impregnate (a material) with rubber, especially to make it waterproof or more durable

Examples

To rubberize a surface
Factories often rubberize containers to prevent leaks and corrosion.
The company will rubberize the soles of the shoes to improve their grip.
They decided to rubberize the fabric, making it perfect for outdoor use.
Before shipping, the tools are rubberised to protect them from corrosion.
It is common to rubberise fabric intended for raincoats to make them waterproof.
The company decided to rubberise all its cables to improve safety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubberize - to coat or impregnate with rubber
  • synonym: waterproof - to make something resistant to water
