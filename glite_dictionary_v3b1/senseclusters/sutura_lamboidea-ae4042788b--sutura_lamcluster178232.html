sutura lamboidea

noun
skull suture between occipital and parietal bones
9.9
Pronunciation: / suːˈtʊr ə læmˈbɔɪdiə / (AmE), / suːˈtjʊərə læmˈbɔɪdiə / (BrE)
the suture in the human skull between the occipital and parietal bones

Examples

The sutura lamboidea bone
A fracture was visible along the sutura lamboidea in the X-ray image.
During the anatomy exam, students were asked to identify the sutura lamboidea.
The sutura lamboidea connects the occipital bone with the parietal bones at the back of the skull.
