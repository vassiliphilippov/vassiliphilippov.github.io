too arty

adjective
excessively artistic or pretentious
6.3 6.3
Pronunciation: /tuː ˈɑːr.tiː/ /; / /tuː ˈɑːr.tɪ/ (AmE), /tuː ˈɑː.tiː/ /; / /tuː ˈɑː.tɪ/ (BrE)
Excessively artistic in style or character, often to the point of being pretentious or not appealing to mainstream tastes.

Examples

A too arty style
His living room decor is too arty for most people, filled with unusual sculptures and abstract paintings.
Some critics found the novel too arty for their tastes and preferred simpler storytelling.
We enjoyed the show, but my brother thought it was too arty and left early.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artsy - pretentiously or excessively artistic; affectedly creative
  • synonym: pretentious - attempting to impress by affecting greater importance or artistic value than is actually possessed
