cytological

adjective
relating to cytology (study of cells)
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪtəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌsaɪtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the study of cells, especially their structure, function, and chemistry

Examples

Cytological study
Her research focused on cytological changes observed under certain conditions.
Recent advancements have improved cytological techniques for identifying abnormal cells.
The doctor requested a cytological analysis of the tissue sample.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cytology - the study of the structure and function of animal and plant cells
