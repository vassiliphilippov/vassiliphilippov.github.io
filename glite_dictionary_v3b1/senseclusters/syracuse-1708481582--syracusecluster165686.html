Syracuse

proper noun
historic city in southeastern Sicily, Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪr.ə.kjus /; / ˈsɪr.əˌkjuːz /; / ˈsɪrəˌkjus / (AmE), / ˈsɪr.ə.kjuːs /; / ˈsɪərəkjuːs / (BrE)
a city in Sicily, Italy, famous for its ancient Greek and Roman history and archaeological sites
