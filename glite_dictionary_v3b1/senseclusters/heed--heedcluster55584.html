heed

noun
careful attention or consideration
5.3 2/m
Pronunciation: / hid / (AmE), / hiÀêd / (BrE)
careful attention or consideration to something, especially to advice or warnings.

Examples

Take heed of advice
She gave little heed to the doctor's advice and continued her normal routine.
The company failed to pay heed to the complaints of its customers.
Without proper heed, important details can easily be overlooked.
