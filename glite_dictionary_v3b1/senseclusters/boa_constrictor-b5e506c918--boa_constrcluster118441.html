boa constrictor

noun
large tropical American snake
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊ.ə kənˈstrɪktɚ / (AmE), / ˌbəʊ.ə kənˈstrɪktə / (BrE)
a very large, non-venomous snake of tropical America and the West Indies, known for killing its prey by constriction

Examples

A boa constrictor snake
A boa constrictor can grow to more than three meters in length.
He was amazed at how the boa constrictor swallowed its food whole.
The boa constrictor wrapped itself around the tree branch, waiting for its prey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anaconda - a very large South American snake that constricts its prey
