propane

noun
a flammable gas fuel from petroleum
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊˌpeɪn / (AmE), / ˈprəʊˌpeɪn / (BrE)
a colorless, flammable gas found in natural gas and petroleum, used as a fuel for heating, cooking, and engines

Examples

A propane heater
A propane heater can quickly warm up a garage or workshop.
The campers carried a small propane tank for cooking in the wilderness.
We use propane to fuel our outdoor grill during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butane - a similar hydrocarbon gas used as a fuel
