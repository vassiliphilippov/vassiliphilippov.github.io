swadeshi

noun
domestic goods movement
9.9
Pronunciation: /swə.ˈdeɪ.ʃiː/ /; / /swə.ˈde.ʃiː/ (AmE), /swə.ˈdeɪ.ʃiː/ /; / /swə.ˈde.ʃiː/ (BrE)
a political or economic movement promoting the use of domestic goods and the boycott of foreign products, especially in India

Examples

Swadeshi movement
The swadeshi movement encouraged people to buy locally made cloth instead of imported textiles.
Many leaders promoted swadeshi as a way to strengthen the national economy.
During the early 1900s, swadeshi became a symbol of resistance against colonial rule.
