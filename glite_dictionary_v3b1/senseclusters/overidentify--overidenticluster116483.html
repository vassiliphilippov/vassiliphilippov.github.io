overidentify

verb
to relate too strongly
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərʌɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ /; / ˌoʊvərˌaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərʌɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ /; / ˌəʊvəraɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to relate to or associate yourself too strongly with someone, a group, or an idea, often losing objectivity or personal boundaries

Examples
