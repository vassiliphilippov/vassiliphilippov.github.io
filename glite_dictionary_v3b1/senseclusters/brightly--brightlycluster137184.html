brightly

adverb
in a bright manner; with brightness

In a bright manner; with a lot of light, color, or cheerfulness.

Examples

To shine brightly
She smiled brightly at the good news, lighting up the entire room.
The Christmas tree lights sparkled brightly in the dark living room.
The morning sun shone brightly through the window, waking him up early.
