remontant

adjective
reblooming plant
9.7
Pronunciation: / riˈmɑntənt / (AmE), / rɪˈmɒntənt / (BrE)
describing a plant, especially a rose, that blooms more than once in a single season

Examples

Remontant rose bush
The remontant rose delighted gardeners by flowering again in late summer.
Many prefer remontant varieties because they provide color throughout the growing season.
Unlike traditional roses, remontant types can bloom several times before autumn arrives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ever-blooming - producing flowers repeatedly throughout the season
  • antonym: once-blooming - producing flowers only once in a season
