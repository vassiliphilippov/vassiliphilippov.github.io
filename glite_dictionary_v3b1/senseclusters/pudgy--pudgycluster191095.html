pudgy

adjective
slightly fat and short; plump
4.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpʌdʒ.iː/ /; / /ˈpʌdʒ.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈpʌdʒ.iː/ /; / /ˈpʌdʒ.ɪ/ (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) slightly fat and short; sometimes seen as unattractive but often considered cute or not unattractive; short and plump.

Examples

The little boy was podgy but full of energy as he played in the yard.
