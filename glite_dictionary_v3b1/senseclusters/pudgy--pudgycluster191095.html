pudgy

adjective
slightly fat and short; plump
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌdʒi /; / ˈpɑdʒi / (AmE), / ˈpʌdʒi /; / ˈpɒdʒi / (BrE)
(of a person or part of the body) slightly fat and short; sometimes seen as unattractive but often considered cute or not unattractive; short and plump.

Examples

His pudgy fingers
He has pudgy fingers that make playing the piano a challenge.
The toddler's pudgy cheeks turned rosy when she smiled.
Their new puppy is adorable and a bit pudgy from all the treats.
His podgy fingers struggled to reach all the piano keys at once.
She smiled as she squeezed her cat’s podgy belly.
The little boy was podgy but full of energy as he played in the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chubby - slightly fat in a pleasant way
  • synonym: plump - having a full, rounded shape; slightly fat
  • antonym: slender - gracefully thin
  • antonym: slim - slender, thin (especially in an attractive way)
