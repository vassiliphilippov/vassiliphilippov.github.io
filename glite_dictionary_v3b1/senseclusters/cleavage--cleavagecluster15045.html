cleavage

noun
the space between a woman's breasts
Pronunciation: / ˈklivɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkliːvɪdʒ / (BrE)
the narrow space or line formed between a woman's breasts, especially as revealed by a low-cut garment

Examples

Deep cleavage
Some people feel uncomfortable with too much cleavage showing in the workplace.
The actress wore a dress that emphasized her cleavage on the red carpet.
The magazine cover featured a model with visible cleavage in a low-cut gown.
