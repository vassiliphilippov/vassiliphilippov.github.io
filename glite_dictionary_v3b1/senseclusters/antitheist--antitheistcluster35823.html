antitheist

noun
opponent of theism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈθiːɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈθiːɪst / (BrE)
a person who actively opposes or rejects belief in the existence of any god or gods

Examples

An outspoken antitheist
As an outspoken antitheist, she often debated religious leaders in public forums.
The book explores the arguments of a prominent antitheist against organized religion.
He described himself as an antitheist rather than simply an atheist.
The philosopher identified himself as an antitheist during the debate on religion.
Some argue that an antitheist takes a more active stance than an atheist.
Her essay explored the arguments of prominent antitheists in modern society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheist - a person who does not believe in the existence of any god
  • antonym: theist - a person who believes in the existence of a god or gods
