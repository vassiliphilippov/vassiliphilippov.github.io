necro

x
prefix: death or corpses
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛkroʊ / (AmE), / ˈnɛkrəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning related to death or corpses

Examples

Necrotizing tissue
The medical term necrotizing refers to the death of body tissue.
The prefix necro appears in words like necrophobia and necrology, both relating to death.
In biology, necrosis describes the process of cell or tissue death.
