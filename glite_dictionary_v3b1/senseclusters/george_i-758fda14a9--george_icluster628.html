George I

proper noun
first Hanoverian King of Great Britain
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔrdʒ ðə ˈfɝst / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔːdʒ ðə ˈfɜːst / (BrE)
(1660–1727) Elector of Hanover who became the first Hanoverian King of Great Britain and Ireland, reigning from 1714 to 1727.

Examples

George I of Hanover
George I ascended to the throne in 1714 following the death of Queen Anne.
Many political changes occurred during the rule of George I.
The reign of George I marked the beginning of the Hanoverian dynasty in Britain.
