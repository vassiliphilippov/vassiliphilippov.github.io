hola

interjection
greeting exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊlɑ / (AmE), / ˈəʊlɑ / (BrE)
an exclamation used to greet someone or call attention, similar to 'hello' or 'hey'

Examples

Say hola to friends
She waved and said hola as she entered the room.
When he answered the phone, he greeted me with a cheerful hola.
The children shouted hola to their friends across the playground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodbye - an expression used when parting
  • synonym: hello - a greeting or expression of hello
  • synonym: hey - an informal greeting or call for attention
