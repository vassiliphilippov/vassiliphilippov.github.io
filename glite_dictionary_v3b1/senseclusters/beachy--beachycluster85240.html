beachy

adjective
resembling the beach
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃi / (BrE)
having qualities, style, or atmosphere that are relaxed, sunny, or reminiscent of the beach

Examples

Beachy style
She decorated her apartment in a beachy style with light blue and sandy colors.
His shirt had a beachy pattern of palm trees and surfboards.
The restaurant's beachy atmosphere made us feel like we were on vacation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tropical - having characteristics of the tropics, especially warm and sunny
