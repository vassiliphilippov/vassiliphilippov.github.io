projection

noun
prediction or estimate based on data
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʒɛkʃən / (BrE)
an estimate or forecast of a future situation based on analysis of past and present data

Examples

Sales projection
Based on current trends, the population projection shows a steady increase over the next decade.
His projection was that the market would recover by the end of the year.
The company revised its projection for next year's profits after the latest sales report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forecast - an estimate beforehand of future events, especially weather or business trends
  • synonym: prediction - a statement about what will happen in the future
