bend someone's ear

verb
talk to someone at length, often tediously
6.0
Pronunciation: / bɛnd sʌmwʌnz ɪr / (AmE), / bɛnd sʌmwʌnz ɪə / (BrE)
to talk to someone for a long time, especially about personal matters or complaints, often to the point of annoyance

Examples

My coworker always tries to bend someone's ear about his weekend plans during lunch breaks.
Whenever I visit my grandmother, she loves to bend my ear with family stories.
