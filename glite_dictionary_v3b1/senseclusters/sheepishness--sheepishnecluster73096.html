sheepishness

noun
embarrassed or shy feeling
Pronunciation: / ˈʃiːpɪʃnəs / (AmE), / ˈʃiːpɪʃnɪs / (BrE)
a feeling or quality of being embarrassed or shy, especially because one has done something wrong or foolish

Examples

His sheepishness showed
His sheepishness was evident as he admitted forgetting the meeting.
She tried to hide her sheepishness after tripping in front of the class.
There was an air of sheepishness about him when he apologized for his mistake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidence - the feeling or belief that one can rely on someone or something; self-assurance
  • synonym: embarrassment - a feeling of awkwardness or self-consciousness
