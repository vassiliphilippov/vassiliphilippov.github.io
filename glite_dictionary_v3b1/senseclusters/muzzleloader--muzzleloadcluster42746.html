muzzleloader

noun
front-loading firearm
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌzəlˌloʊdər / (AmE), / ˈmʌzəlˌləʊdə / (BrE)
a firearm that is loaded from the open end of the barrel (the muzzle) rather than from the rear
