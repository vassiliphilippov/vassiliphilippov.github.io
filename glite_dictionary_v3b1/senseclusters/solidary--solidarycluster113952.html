solidary

adjective
mutually supportive
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːlɪˌdɛri / (AmE), / ˈsɒlɪˌdɛri / (BrE)
showing or characterized by mutual support, unity, or shared responsibility among members of a group

Examples

Solidary team
The team remained solidary in the face of difficult challenges.
A solidary community can overcome even the toughest obstacles together.
During the crisis, neighbors showed a solidary spirit by helping each other.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divided - not united; separated or in disagreement
  • synonym: supportive - giving help or encouragement to others
  • synonym: united - joined together for a common purpose
