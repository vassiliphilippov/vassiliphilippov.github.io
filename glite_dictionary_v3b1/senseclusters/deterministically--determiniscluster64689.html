deterministically

adverb
without randomness
Pronunciation: / dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪkli / (AmE), / dɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is completely determined by previous conditions, without any randomness or chance

Examples

To act deterministically
The computer program operates deterministically, always producing the same output for the same input.
In classical physics, particles move deterministically according to the laws of motion.
The results can be reproduced deterministically if all variables are controlled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: predictably - in a way that can be predicted or expected
  • antonym: randomly - in a way that happens by chance, not determined
