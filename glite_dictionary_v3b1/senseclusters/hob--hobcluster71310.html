hob

noun
shelf beside fireplace for warming
1.0 8.0
Pronunciation: /hɑːb/ (AmE), /hɒb/ (BrE)
A shelf or projection at the side of a fireplace, used for keeping food or utensils warm.

Examples

Fireplace hob
He set the stew to simmer on the hob while the bread finished baking.
Old cottages often have a stone hob beside the fireplace for resting pots and pans.
She placed the teapot on the hob to keep it warm throughout the evening.
