semigovernmental

adjective
partly government-run
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌɡʌvərnˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌɡʌvənˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
partly controlled, funded, or operated by a government but not fully governmental

Examples

A semigovernmental organization
The new committee is a semigovernmental organization responsible for regulating the energy sector.
Many semigovernmental agencies receive both public and private funding for their projects.
A semigovernmental board was established to oversee the development of public housing.
