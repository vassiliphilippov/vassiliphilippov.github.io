blossom

noun
peak period of prosperity or productivity
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈblɑːsəm / (AmE), / ˈblɒsəm / (BrE)
a period of greatest prosperity or productivity; the peak or flourishing stage of something

Examples

Career blossom
Her career reached its blossom in her thirties, when she published her best-known work.
The city experienced its cultural blossom during the Renaissance.
The company is enjoying a blossom of innovation under its new leadership.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decline - a period of decreasing prosperity or productivity
  • synonym: peak - the highest point or greatest level
  • synonym: prime - the time of greatest strength or success
