forerun

noun
precursor or harbinger
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfɔːr.rʌn/ (AmE), /ˈfɔː.rʌn/ (BrE)
something or someone that comes before another and indicates or signals what is to follow; a precursor or harbinger

Examples

A forerun to change
The invention served as a forerun to modern communication devices.
Many consider the artist's early work a forerun of the abstract movement.
The small tremors were a forerun to the major earthquake that followed.

  • synonym: precursor - something that comes before and leads to another
  • antonym: successor - a person or thing that comes after another
