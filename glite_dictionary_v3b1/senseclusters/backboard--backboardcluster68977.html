backboard

noun
vertical board behind basketball hoop
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbækˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a raised, vertical board with a basket attached, used as part of a basketball hoop to bounce the ball off when shooting

Examples

Basketball backboard
He banked the ball off the backboard to score.
Players practice layups by aiming at the corner of the backboard.
The glass backboard shattered after a powerful dunk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: basketball hoop - circular rim and net used in basketball
