glance at

verb
look quickly at
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæns æt / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːns æt / (BrE)
to look quickly or briefly at someone or something

Examples

To glance at notes
During the test, he glanced at his notes for a quick reminder.
I glanced at the headline but didn't have time to read the full article.
She glanced at the clock and realized she was late for her appointment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peek at - to look quickly or secretly at something
