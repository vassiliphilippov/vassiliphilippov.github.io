Phoenix dactylifera

noun
date palm tree species
8.3
Pronunciation: / fəˈniːks dækˈtɪlɪfərə / (AmE), / fəˈniːks dækˈtɪlɪfərə / (BrE)
a tall tropical palm tree native to the Middle East and North Africa, cultivated for its sweet edible fruit, the date
