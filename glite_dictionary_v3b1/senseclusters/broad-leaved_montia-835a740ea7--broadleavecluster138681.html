broad-leaved montia

noun
North American succulent plant with broad leaves and white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔd liːvd ˈmɑntiə / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːd liːvd ˈmɒntiə / (BrE)
A succulent plant of western North America with mostly basal leaves, a stem with a pair of broad ovate or heart-shaped leaves, and a loose cluster of white flowers.

Examples

The broad-leaved montia plant
Botanists often identify the broad-leaved montia by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves.
In spring, the delicate white flowers of the broad-leaved montia can be seen along shaded trails.
The broad-leaved montia thrives in moist woodlands of western North America.
