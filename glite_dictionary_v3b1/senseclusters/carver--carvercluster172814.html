carver

noun
person who decorates wood by carving
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvə / (BrE)
a person who uses tools to shape and decorate wood, especially to create decorative wooden panels and sculptures

Examples

Wood carver
Each chair in the collection was made by a master carver known for his detailed designs.

The museum featured an exhibit on the history of carver guilds in medieval Europe.
