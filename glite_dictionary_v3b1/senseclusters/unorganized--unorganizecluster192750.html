unorganized

adjective
not belonging to a trade union
2.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈɔːr.gə.naɪzd/ (AmE), /ən.ˈɔː.gə.naɪzd/ (BrE)
(of workers or employees) not belonging to or represented by a trade union.

Examples

Unorganized workers
A campaign was launched to unionize the unorganized employees at the factory.
Many unorganized workers do not have access to collective bargaining.
Most unorganized staff members felt isolated from the negotiations.
He advocates unionization in industries where workers are largely unorganised.
Most of the staff are unorganised and have little protection in the workplace.
The government is seeking to improve conditions for unorganised laborers across the country.
