glossopharyngeal

adjective
relating to tongue and throat
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlɑːsoʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (AmE), / ˌɡlɒsəʊfəˈrɪndʒiəl / (BrE)
Relating to both the tongue and the pharynx (throat), especially as in the glossopharyngeal nerve.

Examples

Glossopharyngeal nerve
Anatomy students studied the path of the glossopharyngeal artery during dissection.
Damage to the glossopharyngeal nerve can lead to difficulty in swallowing.
The doctor explained the role of glossopharyngeal fibers in taste sensation.
