common symptom

noun
frequently occurring sign of a condition
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmən ˈsɪmptəm / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈsɪmptəm / (BrE)
a sign or indication of a medical condition that occurs frequently among affected individuals

Examples

A common symptom of flu
A headache is a common symptom of the flu.
Fatigue is a common symptom reported by many patients with this illness.
Sneezing is a common symptom of allergies during spring.
