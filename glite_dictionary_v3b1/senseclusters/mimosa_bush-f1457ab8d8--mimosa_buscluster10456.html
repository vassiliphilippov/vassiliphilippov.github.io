mimosa bush

noun
tropical shrub with fragrant yellow flowers
7.9
Pronunciation: / mɪˈmoʊsə bʊʃ / (AmE), / mɪˈməʊsə bʊʃ / (BrE)
a tropical American thorny shrub or small tree with fragrant yellow flowers, often used in perfumery

Examples

Fragrant mimosa bush
Its mimosa bush was covered in bright yellow, fragrant blossoms all spring.
Perfume makers value the mimosa bush for its pleasant-smelling flowers.
The mimosa bush thrived in the warm, humid climate of the botanical garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mimosa - a genus of shrubs and trees with sensitive leaves
