ignis fatuus

noun
pale light over marshy ground
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfæt.ʃu.əs/ /; / /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfæt.ju.əs/ /; / /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfɑː.tu.əs/ (AmE), /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfæt.ju.əs/ /; / /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfæt.ʃu.əs/ /; / /ˈɪg.nɪs ˈfɑː.tu.əs/ (BrE)
a mysterious light seen at night over marshy ground (will-o'-the-wisp), or something that misleads people like an illusion or false hope
