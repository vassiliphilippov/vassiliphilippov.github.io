submucosal

adjective
beneath mucous membrane
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbmjuˈkoʊzəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbmjuːˈkəʊzəl / (BrE)
located or occurring beneath a mucous membrane

Examples

Submucosal growth
The surgeon carefully removed the submucosal growth from the patient's nasal cavity.
A submucosal injection is often used to deliver medication directly beneath the mucous membrane.
Doctors observed a submucosal mass during the endoscopic examination of the stomach.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mucosal - relating to a mucous membrane
  • synonym: subcutaneous - located or applied under the skin
