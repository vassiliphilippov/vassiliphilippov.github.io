prebendal

adjective
relating to church stipend
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈbɛndəl / (AmE), / prɪˈbɛndəl / (BrE)
relating to or receiving a prebend, a stipend or income from church revenues given to a member of the clergy

Examples

Prebendal house
The bishop lived in a prebendal house provided by the cathedral.
Several prebendal stalls were reserved for senior clergy members.
He was appointed to a prebendal position within the church chapter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ecclesiastical - relating to the Christian Church or its clergy
