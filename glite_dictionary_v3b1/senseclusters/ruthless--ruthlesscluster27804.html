ruthless

adjective
without pity or mercy
Pronunciation: / ˈruθ.ləs / (AmE), / ˈruːθ.ləs / (BrE)
Having or showing no pity or compassion for others; cruel and without mercy.

Examples

Ruthless ambition
His ruthless ambition drove him to betray even his closest friends.
She made a ruthless decision to fire half the staff.
The dictator was known for his ruthless disregard for human life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compassionate - showing sympathy and concern for others
  • synonym: merciless - showing no mercy; cruel
  • synonym: pitiless - lacking pity; very cruel
