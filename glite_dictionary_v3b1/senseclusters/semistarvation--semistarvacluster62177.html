semistarvation

noun
partial food deprivation
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˌstɑrˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˌstɑːˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
a state of having less food than needed, but not complete starvation (can be intentional or unintentional)
