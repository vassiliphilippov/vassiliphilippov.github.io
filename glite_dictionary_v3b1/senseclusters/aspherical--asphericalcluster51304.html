aspherical

adjective
not quite spherical in shape
Pronunciation: / əˈsfɛrɪkəl / (AmE), / æˈsfɛrɪkəl / (BrE)
Not quite spherical; deviating slightly from a perfectly round shape, especially in reference to lenses or mirrors in optics.

Examples

An aspherical lens
Astronomers designed a telescope with an aspherical mirror for clearer images.
Manufacturers choose aspherical elements to make thinner and lighter eyeglass lenses.
The new camera uses an aspherical lens to reduce optical distortions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: spherical - having the shape of a sphere
