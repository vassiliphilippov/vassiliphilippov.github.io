vervet monkey

noun
South African monkey with black face and hands
Pronunciation: / ˈvɜrvət ˈmʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈvɜːvɪt ˈmʌŋki / (BrE)
A small South African monkey with a black face and hands, found in wooded and savanna regions.

Examples

The vervet monkey species
Researchers studied the social behavior of the vervet monkey in its natural habitat.
The vervet monkey is known for its distinctive black face and agile movements.
Tourists were delighted to spot a group of vervet monkeys playing near the riverbank.
