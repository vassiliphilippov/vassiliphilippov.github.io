pseudoscorpion

noun
small tailless arachnid, resembles scorpion
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːdoʊˈskɔːrpiən / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəˈskɔːpiən / (BrE)
a small, nonvenomous arachnid that resembles a scorpion but lacks a tail and stinger

Examples

A pseudoscorpion arachnid
Although it looks dangerous, a pseudoscorpion does not have a venomous sting.
Researchers discovered several species of pseudoscorpion living in the old library's archives.
The pseudoscorpion was hiding beneath the bark, barely noticed because of its tiny size.
