moon shot

noun
extremely ambitious project or goal
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmun ʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈmuːn ʃɒt / (BrE)
an extremely ambitious or innovative project or goal, often one that is difficult or unlikely to succeed

Examples

A bold moon shot
His proposal was dismissed as a moon shot, but it inspired creative thinking among the team.
Opening a school on Mars might seem like a moon shot, but some scientists are seriously considering it.
The company's plan to develop a flying car is a true moon shot in the tech industry.
