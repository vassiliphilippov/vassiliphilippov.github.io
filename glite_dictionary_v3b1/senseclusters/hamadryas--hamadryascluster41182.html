hamadryas

noun
African baboon species
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhæməˈdraɪəs / (AmE), / ˌhæməˈdraɪəs / (BrE)
a species of baboon native to northeastern Africa, especially noted for its complex social structure and the male's distinctive mane

Examples

Hamadryas baboon
The hamadryas lives in large groups on the rocky hills of Ethiopia.
Male hamadryas are known for their impressive silver manes.
Researchers observed the social interactions of a hamadryas troop in the wild.
