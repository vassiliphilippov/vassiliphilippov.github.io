phobe

noun
person with strong fear
Pronunciation: / foʊb / (AmE), / fəʊb / (BrE)
a person who has a strong fear or dislike of a particular thing or group

Examples

A crowd phobe
As a phobe, she avoids all situations involving large crowds.
Some people call him a technology phobe because he refuses to use smartphones.
The discussion included both supporters and phobes of the new policy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in something
  • synonym: phobic - having or showing a strong fear or dislike
