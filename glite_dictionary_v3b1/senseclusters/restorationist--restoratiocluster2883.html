restorationist

noun
person who restores
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who restores old objects, buildings, or artworks to their original condition

Examples

Art restorationist

A skilled restorationist can make damaged antiques look as good as new.
Museums often hire a restorationist to repair valuable historical artifacts.
