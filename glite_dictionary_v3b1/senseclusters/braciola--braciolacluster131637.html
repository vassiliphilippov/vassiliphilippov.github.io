braciola

noun
Italian meat cut
8.6
Pronunciation: /brə.ˈtʃoʊ.lə/ /; / /brɑː.ˈtʃoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /brə.ˈtʃəʊ.lə/ /; / /brɑː.ˈtʃəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
a small steak or chop, especially a thin slice of meat that is grilled or fried, often in Italian cuisine

Examples

A grilled braciola
He grilled a juicy braciola for dinner and served it with lemon and herbs.
The restaurant's special included a tender braciola cooked with fresh rosemary.
For the barbecue, she prepared several braciola and marinated them overnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: roast - to cook food, especially meat, in an oven or over a fire
  • synonym: steak - a thick slice of meat, usually beef, cooked by grilling or frying
