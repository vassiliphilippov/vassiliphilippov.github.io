untimed

adjective
without set tempo
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtaɪmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtaɪmd / (BrE)
performed or played without a strict tempo or set timing; free in rhythm or duration

Examples

Untimed music
The pianist played an untimed introduction, allowing the music to flow naturally.
Some exercises in the exam were untimed to reduce pressure on the students.
The conductor encouraged an untimed passage to highlight the soloist's interpretation.
