corrections

noun
government body managing convicted offenders
8.6 4.6
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈrek.ʃənz/ (AmE), /kə.ˈrek.ʃənz/ (BrE)
the government system or branch responsible for punishing, supervising, and rehabilitating people convicted of crimes

Examples
