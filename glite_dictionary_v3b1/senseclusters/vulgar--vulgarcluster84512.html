vulgar

adjective
lacking refinement or taste
Pronunciation: / ˈvʌlɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈvʌlɡə / (BrE)
lacking refinement, sophistication, or good taste; coarse or crude in manners or style.

Examples

Vulgar manners
His jokes were not just rude, but rather vulgar and in poor taste.
Some celebrities are known for their vulgar displays of wealth and taste.
The lavish decorations were criticized as vulgar by many art critics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarse - lacking delicacy or refinement; rough or crude
  • antonym: refined - having or showing well-bred feeling, taste, etc.
