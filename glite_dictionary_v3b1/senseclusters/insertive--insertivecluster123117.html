insertive

adjective
involving insertion
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɜrtɪv / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɜːtɪv / (BrE)
relating to or involving the act of inserting, especially in medical or biological contexts

Examples

Insertive technique
The insertive technique must be performed with precision to avoid tissue damage.
During the insertive stage, the catheter is carefully guided into the vein.
Proper training is essential for anyone performing insertive medical procedures.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extractive - relating to or involving removal or extraction
  • synonym: invasive - involving entry into the body, especially by cutting or inserting instruments
