anterior pituitary

noun
front lobe of the pituitary gland
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚ pɪˈtuːɪˌtɛri / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə pɪˈtjuːɪtəri / (BrE)
the front lobe of the pituitary gland, responsible for producing and releasing several important hormones

Examples

The anterior pituitary gland
Damage to the anterior pituitary can lead to hormonal imbalances.
Doctors monitored her anterior pituitary function after the surgery.
The anterior pituitary secretes hormones that regulate growth and reproduction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: posterior pituitary - the rear lobe of the pituitary gland
