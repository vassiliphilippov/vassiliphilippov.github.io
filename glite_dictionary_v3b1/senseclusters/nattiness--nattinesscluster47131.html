nattiness

noun
stylish and neat appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈnæti.nəs / (AmE), / ˈnæti.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being smart, stylish, and neat in appearance

Examples

Smart nattiness
He took pride in his nattiness, never leaving the house without looking sharp.
Her nattiness made her stand out at every social event.
The nattiness of his suit impressed the interviewers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: natty - smart and fashionable in appearance
  • antonym: sloppiness - the quality of being untidy or careless in appearance
