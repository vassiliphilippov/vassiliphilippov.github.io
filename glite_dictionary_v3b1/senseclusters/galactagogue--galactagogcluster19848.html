galactagogue

noun
substance inducing milk secretion
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlæktəkˌɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡəˈlaktəkˌɒɡ / (BrE)
a substance that promotes or increases the secretion of milk

Examples

Natural galactagogues
Oats and fenugreek are sometimes used as natural galactagogues to support breastfeeding mothers.
Research is ongoing to determine the safety and effectiveness of herbal galactagogues.
The doctor recommended a galactagogue to help stimulate milk production after childbirth.
