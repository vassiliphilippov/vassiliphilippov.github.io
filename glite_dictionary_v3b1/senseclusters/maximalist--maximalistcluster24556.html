maximalist

noun
person favoring excess
Pronunciation: / ˈmæksɪməlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmæksɪməlɪst / (BrE)
a person who favors using many elements, bold ideas, or elaborate styles, especially in art, design, or politics

Examples

A maximalist designer
As a proud maximalist, she decorated her apartment with vibrant colors and eclectic furniture.
The artist is known as a maximalist who fills every canvas with intricate details.
In politics, a maximalist often pushes for the most ambitious goals rather than compromise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extravagant - showing a lack of restraint in using resources or style
  • antonym: minimalist - a person who prefers simplicity and minimal elements
