potrero

noun
pasture or meadow
Pronunciation: / poʊˈtreɪroʊ / (AmE), / pəˈtrɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a pasture or enclosed field, especially in the southwestern United States, used for grazing or keeping livestock, especially horses
