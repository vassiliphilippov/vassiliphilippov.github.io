procuratorate

noun
public prosecutor's office
Pronunciation: / proʊˈkjʊrətərət / (AmE), / prəˈkjʊərətərət / (BrE)
an official office or body responsible for public prosecution, especially in certain legal systems

Examples

Procuratorate investigation
The procuratorate launched an investigation into the corruption allegations.
After reviewing the evidence, the procuratorate decided to press charges against the suspect.
In some countries, the procuratorate plays a key role in the criminal justice system.

Related Terms

  • antonym: court - a place where legal cases are heard and decided
  • synonym: prosecutor - a legal official who brings charges in a court of law
