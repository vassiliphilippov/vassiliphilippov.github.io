specialize in

verb
focus expertise on a subject or field
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛʃəˌlaɪz ɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɛʃəlaɪz ɪn / (BrE)
to concentrate on and become an expert in a particular subject, field, or activity

Examples

To specialize in medicine
After completing medical school, she decided to specialize in pediatric medicine.
He is specializing in renewable energy engineering at his university.
Many law firms specialize in intellectual property cases to serve specific client needs.
