quencher

noun
metal cooling medium
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkwen.tʃər/ (AmE), /ˈkwen.tʃə/ (BrE)
a liquid or medium used to cool hot metal quickly during heat treatment to harden it

Examples

Metal quencher
The blacksmith prepared the quencher before heating the iron rod in the forge.
Different metals require different types of quencher to achieve the desired hardness.
After shaping the blade, she dipped it into the quencher to rapidly cool and strengthen it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coolant - a substance used to reduce or control temperature
  • synonym: quenching oil - a type of oil used as a quencher in metalworking
