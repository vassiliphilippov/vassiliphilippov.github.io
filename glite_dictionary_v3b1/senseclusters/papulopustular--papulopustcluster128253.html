papulopustular

adjective
with papules and pustules
Pronunciation: / ˌpæpjəloʊˈpʌstjələr / (AmE), / ˌpapjʊləʊˈpʌstjʊlə / (BrE)
describing a skin condition characterized by the presence of both papules and pustules

Examples

Papulopustular skin rash
The dermatologist noted a papulopustular rash on the patient's cheeks and forehead.
Treatment for papulopustular acne often includes topical antibiotics and retinoids.
A papulopustular eruption can be a sign of certain skin infections or inflammatory conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acneiform - resembling or related to acne
