unfallen

adjective
not dropped
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɔlən / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɔːlən / (BrE)
not having fallen or dropped down; still in its original position or place

Examples

Unfallen branch
After the storm, only a few unfallen branches remained on the old tree.
The hikers admired the unfallen leaves that still clung to the branches in late autumn.
Despite the strong winds, the flag was unfallen and continued to wave proudly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fallen - having dropped down from a higher place
  • synonym: upright - standing straight or not fallen over
