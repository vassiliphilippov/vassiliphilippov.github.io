Jain

noun
follower of Jainism
7.2
Pronunciation: / dʒeɪn / (AmE), / dʒeɪn / (BrE)

Examples

A Jain monk

The festival was attended by thousands of Jains from around the world.
Non-violence is a central value for every practicing Jain.
