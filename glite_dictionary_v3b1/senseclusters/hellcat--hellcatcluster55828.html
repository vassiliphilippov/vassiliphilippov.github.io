hellcat

noun
malicious or fiercely tempered woman
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈhɛl.kæt / (BrE)
a woman who is very malicious or bad-tempered and fiercely aggressive

Examples

A hellcat woman
Despite her reputation as a hellcat, she showed kindness to those in need.
Everyone in the office avoids that hellcat because of her constant angry outbursts.
The main character is portrayed as a real hellcat when provoked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrew - an ill-tempered or scolding woman
  • synonym: virago - a domineering, violent, or bad-tempered woman
