auction

verb
sell at a public sale to the highest bidder
3.6 2.43/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëk. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àà…îÀêk. É…ôn / (BrE)
to sell something at a public sale in which goods or property are sold to the highest bidder

Examples

To auction a painting
She auctioned her car online instead of selling it privately.
The company will auction off several rare artifacts next month.
They decided to auction the old paintings to raise money for charity.

Synonyms

  • auction off
    3.5 1.02/m
    auction off is a phrasal verb used to sell goods at public bids quickly, while auction is the act of selling to the highest bidder.
  • auctioneer
    6.7 0.00/m
    auctioneer is a professional who calls and manages bids at a public sale, while auction is the process of selling to the highest bidders.
