dead mail

noun
undeliverable and unreturnable mail
3.2
Pronunciation: / dɛd meɪl / (AmE), / dɛd meɪl / (BrE)
Mail that cannot be delivered to the recipient or returned to the sender.

Examples

Dead mail delivery
After checking every address, they finally classified the package as dead mail.
Sometimes, valuable items are discovered among the stacks of dead mail.
The post office sends all dead mail to a special facility for further inspection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undeliverable mail - mail that cannot be delivered to its intended recipient
