meltable

adjective
able to be melted
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛltəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛltəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being melted; able to change from solid to liquid when heated

Examples

meltable plastic
Some plastics are meltable and can be reshaped under heat.
The scientist needed a meltable metal for the experiment.
This chocolate is meltable and perfect for making fondue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fusible - able to be melted or fused; similar meaning
  • antonym: infusible - incapable of being melted; opposite meaning
