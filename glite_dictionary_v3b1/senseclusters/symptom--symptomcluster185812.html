symptom

noun
an indication of disease or disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmptəm / (AmE), / ˈsɪmptəm / (BrE)
A physical or mental feature that is regarded as indicating a condition of disease, particularly one experienced by the patient.

Examples

A symptom of illness
If you develop a cough or fever, these could be a symptom of infection.
She reported experiencing severe headaches as her primary symptom.
Unusual fatigue can be an early symptom of many illnesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sign - an objective indication of a medical condition observed by others
