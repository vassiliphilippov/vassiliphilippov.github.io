linen

noun
high-quality paper made from linen or with linen finish
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪnən / (AmE), / ˈlɪnɪn / (BrE)
A high-quality paper made from linen fibers or with a linen-like texture or finish, often used for fine stationery and certificates.

Examples

Linen paper
He chose linen for his résumé to make a strong impression.
The invitation was printed on elegant linen to give it a sophisticated feel.
This certificate is issued on official linen for added prestige.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rag paper - Paper made from cotton or linen rags
