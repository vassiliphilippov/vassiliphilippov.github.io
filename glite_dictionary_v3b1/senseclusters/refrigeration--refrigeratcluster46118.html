refrigeration

noun
lowering body temperature for therapy
Pronunciation: / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of deliberately lowering the body's temperature for therapeutic purposes, especially in medical treatment

Examples

Medical refrigeration
After the accident, the doctors used refrigeration to reduce swelling and limit brain damage.
Rapid refrigeration of the body can help slow down certain metabolic processes during emergency care.
Therapeutic refrigeration is sometimes applied to patients following cardiac arrest.
