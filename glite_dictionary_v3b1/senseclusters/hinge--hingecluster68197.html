hinge

noun
joint allowing objects to swing open or closed
Pronunciation: / hɪndʒ / (AmE), / hɪndʒ / (BrE)
A joint or mechanism that connects two solid objects, allowing one to move relative to the other, typically used on doors, lids, or gates.

Examples

Door hinge
A well-made hinge allows the cabinet door to open smoothly.
She repaired the old chest by replacing a broken hinge on the lid.
The door creaked loudly because its hinge needed oiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pivot - a central point on which something turns
