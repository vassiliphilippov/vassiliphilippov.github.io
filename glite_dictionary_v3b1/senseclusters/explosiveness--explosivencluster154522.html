explosiveness

noun
sudden intense emotion
6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈsploʊ.sɪv.nəs/ /; / /ɛk.ˈsploʊ.sɪv.nəs/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈspləʊ.sɪv.nəs/ /; / /ɛk.ˈspləʊ.sɪv.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of reacting suddenly and intensely, especially with anger or strong emotion.

Examples

Explosiveness of his anger
The explosiveness of his temper made it difficult for others to work with him.
Her explosiveness in meetings often surprised her colleagues.
The coach admired the player's explosiveness on the field, both physically and emotionally.
