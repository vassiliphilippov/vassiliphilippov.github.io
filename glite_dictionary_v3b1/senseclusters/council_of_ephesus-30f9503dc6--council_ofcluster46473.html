Council of Ephesus

proper noun
third ecumenical council held in 431
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊn.səl əv ˈɛf.ə.səs / (AmE), / ˈkaʊn.səl ɒv ˈɛf.ɪ.səs / (BrE)
the third ecumenical council of the Christian Church, held in 431, which affirmed Mary as the mother of God and condemned the teachings of Pelagius
