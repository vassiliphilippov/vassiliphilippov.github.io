Yamato

proper noun
Japanese WWII battleship
9.9
Pronunciation: / jɑːˈmɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / jəˈmɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
the name of a Japanese battleship from World War II, famous for being one of the largest battleships ever built

Examples

Yamato battleship
The Yamato was renowned for its massive size and powerful armament during World War II.
Many historians consider the sinking of the Yamato a turning point in naval warfare.
Visitors to the museum can see a detailed model of the Yamato on display.
