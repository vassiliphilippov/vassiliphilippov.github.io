electrically

adverb
by electricity; using electricity
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrɪkli / (BrE)
by means of electricity; using or involving electricity.

Examples

Powered electrically
Many devices in the home are electrically powered rather than running on batteries.
The gate can be opened electrically from inside the building.
The laboratory door is electrically operated for added security.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mechanically - by means of machinery or mechanical action
