talar

noun
ankle bone
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlər / (AmE), / ˈteɪlə / (BrE)
the bone in the ankle that connects the leg to the foot, also called the talus

Examples

Talar bone fracture
The X-ray revealed a fracture in the talar bone after the accident.
Injuries to the talar region can affect ankle movement and stability.
The doctor explained that the talar bone plays a key role in walking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: talus - the large bone in the ankle that connects the leg and foot
