coal chute

noun
sloping channel for delivering coal
Pronunciation: / koʊl tʃuːt / (AmE), / kəʊl tʃuːt / (BrE)
A sloping channel or passage used for delivering coal, typically from a truck or storage area into a building or cellar.

Examples
