stanch

verb
stop the flow of liquid
9.1
Pronunciation: / stæntʃ / (AmE), / stɑːntʃ / (BrE)
to stop the flow of a liquid, especially blood

Examples

To stanch the flow
Crews worked to stanch the flow of oil from the leak.
He pressed his hand to the cut, hoping to stanch the blood.
The nurse tried to stanch the bleeding with a clean cloth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staunch - alternate spelling, sometimes used interchangeably with 'stanch'
  • synonym: stem - to stop or restrict the flow of something, especially a liquid
