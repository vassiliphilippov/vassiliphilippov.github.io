brownish-green

adjective
green with a brownish tint
Pronunciation: / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (AmE), / ˈbraʊnɪʃ ˈɡriːn / (BrE)
Of a color that is mainly green with a noticeable tinge of brown.

Examples

brownish-green moss
A brownish-green moss covered the stones on the riverbank.
Her eyes appeared brownish-green in the dappled sunlight.
The old sofa was upholstered in a brownish-green fabric that had faded over time.
