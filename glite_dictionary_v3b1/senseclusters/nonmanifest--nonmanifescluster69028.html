nonmanifest

adjective
not apparent
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈmænɪˌfɛst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈmænɪˌfɛst / (BrE)
not obvious, apparent, or made clear; not easily seen or detected

Examples

A nonmanifest disease
The disease remained nonmanifest until more advanced testing was performed.
Some cultural influences are nonmanifest and require careful study to identify.
Her anxiety was nonmanifest, making it difficult for others to notice her struggles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latent - existing but not yet developed or obvious
  • antonym: manifest - clear or obvious to the eye or mind
