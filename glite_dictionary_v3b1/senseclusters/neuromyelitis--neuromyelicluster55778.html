neuromyelitis

noun
spinal cord inflammation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnuːroʊˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which the spinal cord and often the optic nerves become inflamed, usually causing weakness and sometimes vision problems
