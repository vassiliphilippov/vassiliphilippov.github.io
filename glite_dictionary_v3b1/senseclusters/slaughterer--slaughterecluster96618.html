slaughterer

noun
person who kills animals for meat
6.5 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsl…ît…ö…ö / (AmE), / Ààsl…îÀêt…ôr…ô / (BrE)
a person whose job is to kill animals and prepare their meat for sale or consumption

Examples

The slaughterer kills animals
In rural areas, the local slaughterer often knows every farmer by name.
Regulations require every slaughterer to maintain strict hygiene standards.
The slaughterer worked carefully to ensure humane treatment of the animals.

Synonyms

    7.4 0.00/m

  • butcher
    1.0 4.41/m
    a butcher prepares, cuts, and sells meat in shops, while a slaughterer mainly kills animals and readies meat for sale.
