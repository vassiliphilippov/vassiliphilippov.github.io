disguiser

noun
costume maker
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɡaɪzər / (AmE), / dɪsˈɡaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who creates, provides, or fits disguises, costumes, or theatrical masks for others

Examples

Theater disguiser
The theater company relied on a talented disguiser to transform actors into historical figures.
As a professional disguiser, she crafted elaborate costumes for the annual festival.
The film's disguiser was praised for creating realistic masks for the main characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: costumer - a person who designs or supplies costumes
  • synonym: mask maker - a person who creates masks for performances or events
