sheave

verb
to thread through pulley
9.8
Pronunciation: / ʃiːv / (AmE), / ʃiːv / (BrE)
to pass a rope, cable, or chain through a sheave or pulley for lifting or moving objects

Examples

Before raising the flag, they sheaved the line through the top pulley.
