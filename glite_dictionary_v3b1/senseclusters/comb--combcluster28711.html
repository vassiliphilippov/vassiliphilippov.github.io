comb

noun
tool for straightening fibers
5.5
Pronunciation: / koʊm / (AmE), / kəʊm / (BrE)
a flat tool with a row of teeth, used for untangling or arranging hair or fibers

Examples

A hair comb
A metal comb is often used in textile manufacturing to align fibers.
She used a fine-toothed comb to straighten the wool before spinning.
The artisan picked up a wooden comb to prepare the fibers for weaving.
After his shower, Jake used a comb to style his hair neatly.
He found a comb tucked into his jacket pocket.
She ran a comb through her tangled hair before leaving the house.
