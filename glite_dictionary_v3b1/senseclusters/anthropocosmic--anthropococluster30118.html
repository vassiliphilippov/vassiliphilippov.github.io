anthropocosmic

adjective
human-universe connection
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəpoʊˈkɑzmɪk / (AmE), / ˌænθrəpəʊˈkɒzmɪk / (BrE)
relating to the connection or relationship between humans and the universe as a whole

Examples

Anthropocosmic view
The ancient text presents an anthropocosmic view, emphasizing the unity of people and the cosmos.
Her research explores anthropocosmic themes in Eastern philosophy and their influence on modern thought.
Many myths reflect an anthropocosmic understanding, linking human actions to cosmic events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anthropocentric - regarding humans as the central element of the universe
  • synonym: cosmic - relating to the universe or cosmos
