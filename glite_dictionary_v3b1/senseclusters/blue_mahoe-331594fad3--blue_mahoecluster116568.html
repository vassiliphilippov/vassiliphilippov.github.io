blue mahoe

noun
Caribbean tree valued for timber
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / bluÀê m…ôÀàho ä / (AmE), / bluÀê m…ôÀàh…ô ä / (BrE)
an evergreen tree native to Cuba and Jamaica, noted for its hairy leaves, orange-yellow to orange-red flowers, and valuable timber used in cabinetwork and gunstocks

Examples

Blue mahoe tree
In Jamaica, blue mahoe is frequently used to make decorative wooden bowls and sculptures.
The craftsman selected blue mahoe for the elegant table because of its unique coloration.
The durable wood from the blue mahoe is prized for making high-quality furniture and gunstocks.

Synonyms
