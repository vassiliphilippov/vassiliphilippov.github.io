fish wrap

noun
dish of fish wrapped in flatbread
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ ræp / (AmE), / fɪʃ ræp / (BrE)
A dish consisting of cooked fish rolled or wrapped in a tortilla or flatbread, often with vegetables and sauces.

Examples

A grilled fish wrap
For lunch, she ordered a grilled fish wrap with a side of salad.
He prefers a fish wrap over a traditional fish sandwich for a lighter meal.
The new café down the street serves an excellent spicy fish wrap.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wrap - a sandwich in which the filling is rolled up in a soft flatbread
