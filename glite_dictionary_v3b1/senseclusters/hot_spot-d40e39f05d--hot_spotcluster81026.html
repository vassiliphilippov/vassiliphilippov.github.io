hot spot

noun
place or device offering Wi‑Fi
Pronunciation: / c8ht ccspt / (AmE), / c8ht ccspt / (BrE)
a place or device that provides wireless internet access (Wi‑Fi) to nearby devices.

Examples

Wi-Fi hot spot
The café offered a free hot spot, so I could finish emails while drinking coffee.
She turned on her phone's hot spot to share internet with her laptop during the meeting.
The train station now includes several public hot spots for passengers who need fast Wi‑Fi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Wi‑Fi hotspot - a (often public) location that provides wireless internet access to users
  • synonym: wireless access point - a networking device that allows wireless devices to connect to a wired network
