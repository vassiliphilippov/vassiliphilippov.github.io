ogle

verb
look at with sexual interest
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊɡəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊɡl̩ /; / ˈəʊɡl / (BrE)
to look at someone or something with strong, often inappropriate, desire or interest, especially in a sexual way
