kindling

noun
the act of starting a fire
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪndlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪndlɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Kindling of a fire

The kindling of the campfire took only a few minutes with dry twigs.
The rapid kindling of the flames alarmed the firefighters.
