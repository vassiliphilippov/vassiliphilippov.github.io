upness

noun
state of wakefulness
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpnəs / (AmE), / ˈʌpnəs / (BrE)
the state of being awake, alert, or not asleep, especially when one might be expected to sleep

Examples

Late-night upness
Her upness after the long flight made it hard for her to sleep.
Despite the late hour, his upness kept him working on the project.
The excitement of the event contributed to everyone's upness well past midnight.
