antipestilential

adjective
disease-preventing
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌpɛstəˈlɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌæntaɪˌpɛstəˈlɛnʃəl / (BrE)
preventing or counteracting the effects of pestilence or infectious disease

Examples

Antipestilential measures
The city implemented antipestilential measures to protect its citizens during the epidemic.
Wearing masks and disinfecting surfaces are considered antipestilential practices.
The herbal remedy was believed to have antipestilential properties in ancient times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiseptic - preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms
  • antonym: pestilential - relating to or causing infectious disease
