chanky old car

noun
unreliable, worn-out car
1.9 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæŋki oʊld kɑr / (AmE), / ˈtʃæŋki əʊld kɑː / (BrE)
A car that is unreliable, poorly maintained, and prone to mechanical problems.

Examples

The chanky old car rattled noisily down the street, leaving a trail of smoke behind.
