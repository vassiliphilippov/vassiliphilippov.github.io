breezeless

adjective
without wind
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbriːzləs / (AmE), / ˈbriːzləs / (BrE)
without any wind or breeze; completely still and calm

Examples

A breezeless day
The air was so breezeless that not a single leaf moved on the trees.
We sat by the breezeless lake, enjoying the quiet and stillness of the evening.
On a hot, breezeless afternoon, the city felt even more stifling than usual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not windy or rough; peaceful
  • antonym: windy - with a lot of wind
