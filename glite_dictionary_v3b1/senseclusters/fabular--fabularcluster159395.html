fabular

adjective
like a fable
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfæbjələr / (AmE), / ˈfæbjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of fables or stories, often with imaginative or mythical qualities

Examples

Fabular style stories
The film's fabular structure gives it a timeless, almost magical feeling.
Her writing style is distinctly fabular, weaving moral lessons into imaginative tales.
Critics praised the fabular elements that blurred the line between reality and myth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fabulous - resembling or relating to a fable; extraordinary or mythical
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they are; true to life
