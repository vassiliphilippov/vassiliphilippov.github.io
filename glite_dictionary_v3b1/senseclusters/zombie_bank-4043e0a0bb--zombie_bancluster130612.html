zombie bank

noun
insolvent but operating bank
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈzɑːm.biː bæŋk/ /; / /ˈzɑːm.bɪ bæŋk/ (AmE), /ˈzɒm.bɪ bæŋk/ /; / /ˈzɒm.biː bæŋk/ (BrE)
A bank that is insolvent but continues to operate, often relying on government support or other external aid.

Examples

A zombie bank
During the financial crisis, several institutions became zombie banks that survived only through government bailouts.
Experts warn that allowing a zombie bank to continue operations can drag down the broader economy.
Investors are wary of putting their money into a zombie bank due to the risk of collapse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insolvent bank - a bank unable to pay its debts
