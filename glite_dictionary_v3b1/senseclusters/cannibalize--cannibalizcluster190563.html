cannibalize

verb
eat human flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈkæn.ɪ.bəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ˈkæn.ɪ.bə.laɪz /; / ˈkan.ɪ.bəˌlaɪz / (BrE)
