headband

noun
band worn around the head
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌbænd / (AmE), / ˈhɛdbænd / (BrE)
A band of material worn around or over the head, typically to keep hair in place or for decoration.

Examples

A headband for hair
He adjusted his headband before putting on the headphones.
She wore a colorful headband to keep her hair out of her eyes during yoga.
The headband matched perfectly with her outfit for the festival.
