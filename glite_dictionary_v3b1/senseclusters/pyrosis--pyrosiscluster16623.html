pyrosis

noun
medical term for heartburn
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /paɪ.ˈroʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /paɪ.ˈrəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
A painful burning sensation in the chest caused by backflow of stomach acid irritating the esophagus; commonly known as heartburn.
