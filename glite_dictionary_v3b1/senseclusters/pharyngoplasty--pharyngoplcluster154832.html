pharyngoplasty

noun
surgery on the pharynx
9.9
Pronunciation: / fəˌrɪŋɡoʊˈplæsti / (AmE), / fəˌrɪŋɡəʊˈplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to repair or reconstruct the pharynx

Examples

Pharyngoplasty surgery
After the pharyngoplasty, the patient noticed significant improvement in swallowing.
Doctors recommended a pharyngoplasty to help correct the child's speech difficulties.
Recovery from pharyngoplasty usually involves a short hospital stay and careful monitoring.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pharyngectomy - surgical removal of part or all of the pharynx
  • synonym: uvulopalatoplasty - surgical procedure to reshape the uvula and soft palate
