certainly

adverb
without doubt; definitely
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.tən.lɪ/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.tə.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.tən.lɪ/ /; / /ˈsɜː.tə.lɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
