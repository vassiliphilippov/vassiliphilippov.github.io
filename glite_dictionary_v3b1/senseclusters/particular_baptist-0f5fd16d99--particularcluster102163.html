Particular Baptist

noun
Calvinistic Baptist denomination member
8.8
Pronunciation: / pərˈtɪkjələr ˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / pəˈtɪkjʊlə ˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
a member of a branch of Baptists who adhere to Calvinistic doctrines, especially the belief in predestination and particular atonement
