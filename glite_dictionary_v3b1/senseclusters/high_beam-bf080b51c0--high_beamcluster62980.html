high beam

noun
bright, long-distance headlight setting

the setting of a vehicle's headlights that provides a bright, far-reaching beam of light for distant illumination

Examples

Turn on high beams
He accidentally left his high beams on, blinding the driver in front of him.
Newer cars automatically adjust the high beam depending on road conditions.
Remember to switch off your high beam when there is oncoming traffic.
