straying

adjective
lost; unable to find one’s way
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstreɪɪŋ / (BrE)
unable to find one’s way; lost or wandering from the correct path or place

Examples

A straying hiker
Late at night, we encountered a straying hiker who had wandered off the main trail.
The shepherd searched for the straying lamb until he found it in the woods.
Volunteers reunited the straying dog with its owners after days of searching.
