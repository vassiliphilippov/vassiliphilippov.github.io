Newtonian

adjective
relating to Isaac Newton's science
5.1
Pronunciation: / nuːˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / njuːˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to or derived from the scientific principles and theories of Sir Isaac Newton, especially in physics

Examples

Newtonian physics
In a Newtonian universe, time and space are considered absolute.
Modern physics has challenged some traditional Newtonian ideas about motion and gravity.
The experiment demonstrated behavior consistent with Newtonian mechanics.
