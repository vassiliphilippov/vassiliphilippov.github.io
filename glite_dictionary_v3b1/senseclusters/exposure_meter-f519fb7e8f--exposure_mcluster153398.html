exposure meter

noun
device measuring light for correct photographic exposure
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspoʊʒɚ ˈmiːtɚ / (AmE), / ɪkˈspəʊʒə ˈmiːtə / (BrE)
a device used in photography to measure the intensity of light to determine the correct exposure setting for a photograph

Examples

A exposure meter device
He used his exposure meter to set the correct aperture before taking the photo.
Modern cameras often have a built-in exposure meter for automatic adjustments.
The old film photographer always carried an external exposure meter for accurate readings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: light meter - device for measuring light intensity, especially in photography
