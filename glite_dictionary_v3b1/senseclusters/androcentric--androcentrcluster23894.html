androcentric

adjective
male-centered perspective
Pronunciation: / ˌændroʊˈsɛntrɪk / (AmE), / ˌandrəʊˈsɛntrɪk / (BrE)
focused on or centered around men or the male perspective, often excluding women

Examples

Androcentric view
The novel was criticized for its androcentric portrayal of society, ignoring female experiences.
Many early scientific studies were androcentric, focusing only on male subjects.
She argued that the curriculum was too androcentric and needed more diverse perspectives.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gynocentric - focused on or centered around women or the female perspective
  • synonym: patriarchal - relating to a system controlled by men
