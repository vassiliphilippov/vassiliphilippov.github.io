olive-drab

adjective
light brownish-green color
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːlɪv ˈdræb / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪv ˈdræb / (BrE)
of a light brownish-green color, especially associated with military uniforms and equipment

Examples

Olive-drab color
She painted her old bicycle an olive-drab to match her camping gear.
The soldiers wore olive-drab uniforms during field exercises.
The walls were a dull olive-drab that gave the room a somber feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: olive-green - a green color similar to that of the olive fruit
