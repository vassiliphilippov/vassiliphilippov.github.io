Nureyev

proper noun
famous Russian ballet dancer (1938–1993)
Pronunciation: / nʊˈreɪɛv / (AmE), / njʊˈreɪɛv / (BrE)
Rudolf Nureyev (1938–1993), a renowned Russian ballet dancer and choreographer who became internationally famous after defecting to the West.

Examples

Nureyev ballet dancer
Nureyev was widely celebrated for his charisma and technical brilliance on stage.
Many young dancers cite Nureyev as a major influence on their careers.
The partnership between Margot Fonteyn and Nureyev is legendary in the world of ballet.
