letter box

noun
box for receiving delivered mail
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtər bɑks / (AmE), / ˈletə bɒks / (BrE)
A box or slot at a house or building where letters and other mail are delivered.

Examples

Home letter box
I found a postcard from my friend waiting in the letter box this morning.
She placed the package inside the letter box by the front door.
Their new house has a bright red letter box for all their mail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mailbox - a box for receiving incoming mail, especially used in American English
