muleta

noun
walking support
Pronunciation: / muˈleɪtə / (AmE), / mjuˈleɪtə / (BrE)
a support, such as a crutch, used by an injured person to help walk

Examples

A muleta for walking
After his surgery, he relied on a muleta to move around the house.
The doctor recommended using a muleta until her ankle healed completely.
He walked slowly down the hallway, steadying himself with a muleta.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crutch - a stick or support used to help someone walk
