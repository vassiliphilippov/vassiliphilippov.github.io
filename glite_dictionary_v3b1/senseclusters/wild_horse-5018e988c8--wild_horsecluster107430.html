wild horse

noun
undomesticated or feral horse
1.0
Pronunciation: / waɪld hɔrs / (AmE), / waɪld hɔːs / (BrE)
A horse that lives in the wild and has not been domesticated, or a domestic horse that has become feral.

Examples

A wild horse
A wild horse can be difficult to approach, as they are not used to human contact.
Conservationists are working to protect the habitat of the wild horse in Australia.
The wild horse ran freely across the open plains, its mane flying in the wind.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domesticated horse - horse tamed and bred for human use
  • synonym: mustang - free-roaming horse of the western United States
