yep

interjection
informal yes
Pronunciation: / jɛp / (AmE), / jɛp / (BrE)

Examples

Yep, I agree

When asked if he wanted dessert, he simply said, "Yep."
Are you coming to the meeting later? Yep, I'll be there on time.
