heterocyclic

noun
compound with a heterocyclic ring
8.0 8.1
Pronunciation: /het.ə.rə.ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /het.rə.ˈsaɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /het.ə.rə.ˈsaɪ.kəl/ /; / /het.rə.ˈsaɪ.kəl/ (BrE)
A chemical compound that contains a ring structure composed of atoms of at least two different elements (typically carbon and another element, such as nitrogen, oxygen, or sulfur).

Examples
