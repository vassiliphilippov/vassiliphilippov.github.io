occupied

adjective
busy or engaged

busy or actively involved in an activity, so as not to be free to do something else

Examples

Keep kids occupied
He managed to keep the children occupied during the long trip with games and puzzles.
My mind has been occupied with thoughts of the upcoming exam all week.
She was occupied with paperwork and couldn't answer the phone right away.
