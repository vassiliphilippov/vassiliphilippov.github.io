sweepstakes

noun
lottery where pooled money is the prize
Pronunciation: / ˈswipˌsteɪks / (AmE), / ˈswiːpsteɪks / (BrE)
a competition or contest where winners are chosen by chance, often with a prize collected from participants

Examples

She entered the sweepstakes hoping to win a new car.
The company announced the winner of its annual sweepstakes during a live event.
Many people dream of winning big prizes in a sweepstakes.

  • synonym: lottery - a contest in which winners are chosen by chance
