firmness

noun
steadfastness; determination
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːmnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːmnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being steadfast and determined in character or action

Examples

Firmness in decisions
He faced the criticism with firmness and did not waver from his beliefs.
Her firmness in difficult situations inspired confidence in everyone around her.
The teacher's firmness helped maintain discipline in the classroom.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flexibility - the ability to change easily or adapt; the opposite of firmness
  • synonym: resolve - strong determination to do something
