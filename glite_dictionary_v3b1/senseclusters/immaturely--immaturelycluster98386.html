immaturely

adverb
in an immature manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪməˈtʃʊrli / (AmE), / ˌɪməˈtjʊəli / (BrE)
In a way that shows a lack of emotional or intellectual maturity.

Examples

React immaturely to criticism
Despite being an adult, he often reacts immaturely to criticism.
She admitted she had behaved immaturely during the meeting.
The children handled the situation immaturely, refusing to share their toys.
