catch of the day

noun
freshest fish/seafood available that day
4.2
Pronunciation: / kætʃ əv ðə deɪ / (AmE), / kætʃ ɒv ðə deɪ / (BrE)
the freshest fish (or seafood) available on a particular day, often featured on a restaurant menu

Examples

The catch of the day
If you want something local and fresh, try the catch of the day at this seafood restaurant.
She always asks about the catch of the day when dining by the coast.
The waiter recommended the catch of the day, which was freshly caught sea bass.
