scouring

noun
vigorous cleaning by rubbing with soap and water

Pronunciation: /ˈskaʊr.ɪŋ/ /; / /ˈskaʊ.ər.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskaʊ.ə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
