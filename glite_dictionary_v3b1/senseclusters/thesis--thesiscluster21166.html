thesis

noun
long research paper for a degree
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθiː.sɪs / (AmE), / ˈθiː.sɪs / (BrE)
a long piece of writing based on original research, submitted as part of the requirements for a university degree, especially a doctorate or master's degree

Examples

A thesis paper
His thesis was praised for its originality and depth of analysis.
Many students feel anxious about defending their thesis in front of a committee.
She spent three years researching and writing her thesis on early modern philosophy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissertation - a long essay, especially one written for a doctoral degree
