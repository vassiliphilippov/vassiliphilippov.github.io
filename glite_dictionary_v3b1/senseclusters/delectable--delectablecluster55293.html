delectable

adjective
sexually attractive; alluring (of a person)
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈlɛktəbl / (BrE)
(of a person) highly attractive in a way that arouses admiration or sexual desire.

Examples

Delectable model
Rumors spread about the delectable new model featured in the ad campaign.
She became the focus of attention thanks to her delectable looks.
The actor was praised for his delectable charm in the romantic comedy.

  • antonym: repellent - unattractive or causing aversion
