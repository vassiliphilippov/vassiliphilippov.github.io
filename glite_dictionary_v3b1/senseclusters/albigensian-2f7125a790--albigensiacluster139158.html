Albigensian

adjective
relating to the Albigenses or their beliefs
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.bəˈdʒɛn.si.ən / (AmE), / ˌal.bɪˈdʒɛn.si.ən / (BrE)
of or relating to the Albigenses or their doctrines, especially Albigensianism, a medieval Christian religious movement regarded as heretical by the Catholic Church

Examples

The Albigensian faith
Some scholars study the Albigensian faith as part of medieval European history.
The Albigensian Crusade led to significant political changes in southern France.
The Albigensian heresy was condemned by the Catholic Church in the thirteenth century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cathar - a member of a related medieval Christian sect
