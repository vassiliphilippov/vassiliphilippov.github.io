unblinded

adjective
able to see again
6.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈblaɪndɪd / (BrE)
having had a blindfold or eye covering removed and able to see again

Examples

Unblinded children
After the game, the children were unblinded and laughed at the surprise waiting for them.
The patient was unblinded once the bandages were removed from his eyes.
She felt relieved to be unblinded after the experiment ended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blindfolded - having the eyes covered so as not to see
  • synonym: sighted - able to see; not blind
