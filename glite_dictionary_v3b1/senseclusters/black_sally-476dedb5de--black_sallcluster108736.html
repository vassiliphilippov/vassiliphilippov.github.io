black sally

noun
Australian eucalyptus tree; yields red kino
9.7
Pronunciation: /blæk ˈsæl.iː/ /; / /blæk ˈsæl.ɪ/ (AmE), /blæk ˈsæl.ɪ/ /; / /blæk ˈsæl.iː/ (BrE)
A small eucalyptus tree (Eucalyptus stellulata) with rough dark-colored bark at the base, found in Australia and known for producing a red gum (kino).

Examples

The black sally tree
Bushwalkers admired the dark bark of the black sally along the forest trail.
Some Aboriginal communities harvest kino from the black sally for traditional uses.
The old homestead was shaded by a towering black sally near the creek.
