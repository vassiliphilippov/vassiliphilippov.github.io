nonrural

adjective
not rural
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈrʊrəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈrʊərəl / (BrE)
not relating to or characteristic of the countryside; not rural

Examples

Most nonrural areas
Most nonrural areas have better access to public transportation.
The study compared health services in rural and nonrural communities.
Internet speeds are generally faster in nonrural regions than in remote villages.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rural - relating to the countryside
  • synonym: urban - relating to a city or town
