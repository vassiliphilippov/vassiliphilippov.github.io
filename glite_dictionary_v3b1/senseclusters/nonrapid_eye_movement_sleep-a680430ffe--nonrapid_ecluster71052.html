nonrapid eye movement sleep

noun
stage of sleep without rapid eye movements
8.8 3.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈræp.ɪd aɪ ˈmuːv.mənt sliːp/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈræp.ɪd aɪ ˈmuːv.mənt sliːp/ (BrE)
A recurring stage of sleep characterized by the absence of rapid eye movements and dreaming, accounting for about 75% of normal sleep time.
