signorial

adjective
lordly or stately
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪɡˈnɔːriəl / (AmE), / sɪɡˈnɔːriəl / (BrE)
having the manner, dignity, or appearance of a lord; lordly or stately in behavior or bearing

Examples

Signorial presence
The ambassador entered the hall with a signorial presence that commanded respect.
Her signorial manner made her stand out among the other guests at the ceremony.
He decorated his office in a signorial style, with grand furniture and rich fabrics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
  • synonym: lordly - having the qualities or appearance of a lord
  • synonym: regal - suitable for a king or queen; royal
