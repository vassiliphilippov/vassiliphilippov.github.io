copper oxide

noun
a compound of copper and oxygen
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌpər ˈɑksaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkʌpə ˈɒksaɪd / (BrE)
a chemical compound consisting of copper and oxygen, found in two forms: cupric oxide (CuO, black) and cuprous oxide (Cu2O, red).

Examples

Copper oxide powder
Heating copper in air results in the production of copper oxide as a black powder.
Scientists analyzed the copper oxide layer using advanced microscopy techniques.
The statue developed a green patina due to the formation of copper oxide on its surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupric oxide - a black oxide of copper (CuO)
  • synonym: cuprous oxide - a red oxide of copper (Cu2O)
