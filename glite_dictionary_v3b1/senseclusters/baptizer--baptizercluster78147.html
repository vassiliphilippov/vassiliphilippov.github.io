baptizer

noun
person performing baptism
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæptaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈbæptaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who performs the religious ceremony of baptism

Examples

A baptizer at church
The baptizer gently lowered the child into the water during the ceremony.
Each baptizer in the church follows a special tradition when performing the ritual.
The congregation watched as the baptizer welcomed new members into the faith.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baptism - the religious ceremony of admitting someone into a faith, often using water
  • synonym: officiant - a person who leads a religious ceremony
