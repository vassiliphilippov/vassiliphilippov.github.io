breakfast staple

noun
essential food for breakfast
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛkˌfəst ˈsteɪpəl / (AmE), / ˈbrɛkfəst ˈsteɪpəl / (BrE)
a food item commonly eaten or regarded as essential for breakfast

Examples

A breakfast staple food
In Australia, Vegemite on toast is considered a breakfast staple.
Oatmeal is a breakfast staple in many households around the world.
Rising food prices have made it harder for some families to afford their favorite breakfast staples.
