Cape dagga

noun
South African herb smoked like tobacco
9.9
Pronunciation: / keɪp ˈdɑɡə / (AmE), / keɪp ˈdæɡə / (BrE)
A South African herb (Leonotis leonurus), relatively nontoxic, sometimes smoked like tobacco for its mild effects.

Examples

Cape dagga herb
Researchers are studying the effects of Cape dagga on anxiety and mood.
Some people use Cape dagga as a herbal alternative to tobacco.
The leaves of Cape dagga are often dried and smoked in traditional ceremonies.
