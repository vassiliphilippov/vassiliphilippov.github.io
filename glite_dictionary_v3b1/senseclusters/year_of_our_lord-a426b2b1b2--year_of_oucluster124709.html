year of our Lord

noun
expression for year in the Christian calendar
9.4
Pronunciation: / jɪr əv aʊər lɔrd / (AmE), / jɪə əv aʊə ˈlɔːd / (BrE)
(in formal use) used to refer to a year in the Christian calendar (Anno Domini, AD) when stating a date, especially in legal or historical contexts.

Examples

The year of our Lord 2024
Records were meticulously kept, each labeled with the year of our Lord followed by the number.
The document states the property was transferred in the year of our Lord 1865.
The letter was dated in the year of our Lord 1798.

Related Terms

  • synonym: AD - abbreviation for Anno Domini, indicating years in the Christian era
