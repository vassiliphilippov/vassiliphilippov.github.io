stage director

noun
person who directs a stage show
1.0
Pronunciation: / steɪdʒ dəˈrɛktər / (AmE), / steɪdʒ dɪˈrɛktə / (BrE)
a person who supervises the actors and directs the action in a theatrical production

Examples

A stage director leads actors
A talented stage director can bring a script to life in unexpected ways.
After years as an actor, she became a successful stage director for Broadway productions.
The stage director coordinated every movement on set to ensure a seamless performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - person who supervises and controls artistic aspects of a production
  • antonym: producer - person responsible for the business and managerial aspects of a stage show
