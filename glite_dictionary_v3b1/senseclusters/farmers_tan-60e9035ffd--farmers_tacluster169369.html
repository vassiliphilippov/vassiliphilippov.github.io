farmer's tan

noun
tan pattern caused by sun exposure while clothed
8.6 6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑrmərz tæn / (AmE), / ˈfɑːməz tæn / (BrE)
A tan pattern on the skin where areas exposed to the sun are darker than those covered by clothing, typically seen after wearing a T-shirt or short sleeves.

Examples

Farmer's tan pattern
His farmer's tan was obvious after months of working outdoors in short sleeves.
She laughed when she noticed her farmer's tan in the mirror after removing her watch.
Wearing a swimsuit at the beach, his farmer's tan stood out in sharp contrast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tan lines - the distinct lines or patterns on skin where areas have been tanned or not tanned due to clothing
