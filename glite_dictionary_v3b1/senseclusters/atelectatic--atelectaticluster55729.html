atelectatic

adjective
lung collapse
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌætəlɛkˈtætɪk / (AmE), / ˌætəlɛkˈtætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or affected by the collapse of part or all of a lung

Examples

Atelectatic lung area
The doctor noted atelectatic areas on the patient's chest X-ray.
After surgery, some patients may develop atelectatic changes in their lungs.
The radiologist described the lower lobe as partially atelectatic due to mucus blockage.
