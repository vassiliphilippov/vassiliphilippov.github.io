nonstatistically

adverb
without statistics
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnstəˈtɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnɒnstəˈtɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not involve or relate to statistics or statistical methods

Examples

Analyzed nonstatistically
The data was analyzed nonstatistically because there were too few participants for meaningful results.
She explained the findings nonstatistically, focusing on general trends rather than numbers.
Due to limited information, the report was written nonstatistically and relied on qualitative observations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: statistically - in a way that involves statistics or statistical methods
