nonperforming

adjective
not generating income
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.pərˈfɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.pəˈfɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
not generating income or failing to meet expected standards of performance, especially in finance or business

Examples

Nonperforming asset
The company had to sell several nonperforming assets to improve its financial health.
Banks are required to report the amount of nonperforming loans on their balance sheets.
Investors are concerned about the rising number of nonperforming accounts in the portfolio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defaulted - having failed to meet financial obligations
  • antonym: profitable - generating income or profit
