stinking smut

noun
wheat disease marked by foul-smelling spore masses
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˌsmʌt /; / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ smʌt / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ ˌsmʌt /; / ˈstɪŋkɪŋ smʌt / (BrE)
a disease of wheat and related cereals where the grains are replaced by greasy, foul-smelling dark masses caused by certain fungi
