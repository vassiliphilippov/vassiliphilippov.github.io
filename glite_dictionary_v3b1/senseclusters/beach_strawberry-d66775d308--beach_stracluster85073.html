beach strawberry

noun
wild strawberry plant, strawberry ancestor
6.6
Pronunciation: / biːtʃ ˈstrɔːbəri / (AmE), / biːtʃ ˈstrɒbəri / (BrE)
a wild strawberry plant (Fragaria chiloensis) native to the western United States and South America, used as a source for many cultivated strawberry varieties
