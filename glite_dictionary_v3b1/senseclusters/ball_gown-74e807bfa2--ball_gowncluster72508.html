ball gown

noun
formal long dress for balls
Pronunciation: / bɔl ɡaʊn / (AmE), / bɔːl ɡaʊn / (BrE)
a long, formal dress that is typically worn by women to balls or very formal social occasions

Examples

A ball gown dress
Finding the perfect ball gown for the event took her several weeks.
She looked stunning in a shimmering ball gown at the charity gala.
The museum displayed a Victorian-era ball gown made of silk and lace.
