ad-free

adjective
without advertisements
Pronunciation: / æd friː / (AmE), / æd friː / (BrE)
Without advertisements; not containing any advertising content.

Examples

An ad-free experience
All subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience on this platform.
Many users are willing to pay more for ad-free streaming services.
The company launched an ad-free version of its popular app.
