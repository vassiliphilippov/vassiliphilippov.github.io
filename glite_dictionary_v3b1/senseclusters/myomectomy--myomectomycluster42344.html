myomectomy

noun
fibroid removal surgery
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪəˈmɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəˈmɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove fibroids from the uterus while preserving the uterus itself
