unstress

verb
to relieve stress
4.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstrɛs / (AmE), / ʌnˈstrɛs / (BrE)
to make someone feel less anxious or tense; to relieve psychological or emotional stress

Examples

To unstress after work
After a busy week, she likes to unstress by listening to music in her room.
Taking deep breaths can help you unstress before an important meeting.
He found that a short walk outside helped him unstress after work.
