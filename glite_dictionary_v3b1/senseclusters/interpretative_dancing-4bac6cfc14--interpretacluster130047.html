interpretative dancing

noun
a modern dance style expressing emotions or stories
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˌtɜrprəˌteɪtɪv ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˌtɜːprɪˌteɪtɪv ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
a form of modern dance in which movements are used to express emotions or narrate a story

Examples

Interpretative dancing style
Many artists use interpretative dancing to bring abstract ideas to life through movement.
She took classes in interpretative dancing to better express her emotions on stage.
The performance featured breathtaking interpretative dancing that conveyed the struggle of the characters.
