Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae

noun
a rust fungus affecting apples and pears
Pronunciation: / d͡ʒɪm.nə.spəˈræn.dʒi.əm d͡ʒuːˌnɪ.pə.ri vɜːr.d͡ʒɪ.niˈeɪ.iː / (AmE), / d͡ʒɪm.nə.spəˈræn.dʒi.əm d͡ʒuːˌnɪ.pə.ri vɜː.d͡ʒɪ.niˈɑː.iː / (BrE)
a species of rust fungus that causes cedar-apple rust, resulting in orange-brown spots on apples, pears, and related plants
