unreferenced

adjective
lacking citations
6.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛfərənst / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛfərənst / (BrE)
not provided with references or citations to sources of information

Examples

Unreferenced report
The report was criticized for being largely unreferenced and lacking supporting evidence.
Many online articles remain unreferenced, making it hard to verify their claims.
Academic journals rarely accept unreferenced submissions from researchers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cited - provided with references to sources
  • antonym: referenced - containing references or citations
