workboat

noun
utility boat
3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrk.boʊt/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːk.bəʊt/ (BrE)
a boat used for practical work such as transporting goods, equipment, or people, or for construction or maintenance, rather than for leisure

Examples

A workboat delivers goods
The harbor was crowded with workboats delivering supplies to the island.
A sturdy workboat is essential for transporting equipment to offshore platforms.
Unlike pleasure craft, a workboat is built for heavy-duty tasks and reliability.
The harbor relies on a workboat to transport supplies to the ships anchored offshore.
A sturdy workboat is essential for maintaining the floating docks throughout the year.
Engineers boarded the workboat early in the morning to inspect the bridge supports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tugboat - a small, powerful boat used to tow or push ships
  • antonym: yacht - a boat used for pleasure or sport
