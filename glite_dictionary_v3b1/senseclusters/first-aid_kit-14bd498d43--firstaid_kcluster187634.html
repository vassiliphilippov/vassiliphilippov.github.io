first-aid kit

noun
box of emergency medical supplies
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrst ˌeɪd kɪt / (AmE), / ˌfɜːst ˈeɪd kɪt / (BrE)
A box or bag containing bandages, medicines, and other supplies for giving emergency medical treatment.

Examples

A first-aid kit
Every car should have a first-aid kit in case of an emergency.
She opened the first-aid kit to treat his cut finger.
The organizer checked that a first-aid kit was available at the event.
