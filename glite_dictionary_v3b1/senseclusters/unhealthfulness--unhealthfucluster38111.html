unhealthfulness

noun
the quality of being unhealthful
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlθfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθfəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of promoting poor health; the condition of being unhealthful

Examples

The unhealthfulness of air
Many residents complained about the unhealthfulness of the polluted air in the city.
Regularly consuming sugary drinks adds to the unhealthfulness of a poor diet.
The unhealthfulness of fast food has become a growing concern among nutritionists.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthfulness - the quality of promoting good health
  • synonym: unhealthiness - the condition of being unhealthy
