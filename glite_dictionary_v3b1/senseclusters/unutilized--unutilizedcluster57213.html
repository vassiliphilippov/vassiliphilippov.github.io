Synonyms

  • unused
    1.0 2.63/m
    unused describes something not in use at all, while unutilized sounds more formal and can also suggest underuse.
  • underused
    3.4 0.35/m
    underused means used less than it should be, while unutilized often means not used at all or left idle.
  • unexploited
    6.3 0.24/m
    unexploited refers to resources or opportunities not developed, while unutilized is a general word for things not used.
  • untapped
    4.4 1.50/m
    untapped describes unused potential or resources waiting to be used, while unutilized is a plain, more formal term.
