Palmetto State

proper noun
nickname for South Carolina (US state)
Pronunciation: / pælˈmɛtoʊ steɪt / (AmE), / pælˈmɛtəʊ steɪt / (BrE)
A nickname for the US state of South Carolina, referring to its symbol, the palmetto tree.

Examples

Palmetto State nickname
South Carolina is often called the Palmetto State due to the abundance of palmetto trees.
The flag of the Palmetto State features a distinctive palmetto tree and a crescent moon.
Tourism is a major industry in the Palmetto State, drawing visitors to its beaches and historic sites.
