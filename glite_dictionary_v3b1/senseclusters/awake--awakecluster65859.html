awake

verb
to stop sleeping
5.1
Pronunciation: / əˈweɪk / (AmE), / əˈweɪk / (BrE)
to stop sleeping or to make someone stop sleeping

Examples

To awake from sleep
Every morning, I awake to the chirping of birds outside my window.
He awoke suddenly in the night, unsure of what had disturbed him.
She often awakes before sunrise to meditate and exercise.
He tried not to awake his roommate when he left early in the morning.
The loud thunder awoke everyone in the house last night.
She has awoken before sunrise every day this week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sleep - to be in a state of rest with eyes closed and mind inactive
  • synonym: wake - to stop sleeping or to make someone stop sleeping
  • synonym: wake up - to stop sleeping; to become awake
