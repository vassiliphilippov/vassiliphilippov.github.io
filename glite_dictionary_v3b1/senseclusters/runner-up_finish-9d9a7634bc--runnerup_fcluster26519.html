runner-up finish

noun
second place finish
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌrʌnər ʌp ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌrʌnə ʌp ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
an achievement of second place in a competition, race, or contest

Examples

A runner-up finish in a race
After a strong performance, the team celebrated their runner-up finish in the national championship.
Despite her disappointment, a runner-up finish at the Olympics brought her international recognition.
He was proud of his runner-up finish, knowing he had beaten hundreds of other competitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: second place - the position immediately after the winner in a competition
  • antonym: victory - winning first place in a competition
