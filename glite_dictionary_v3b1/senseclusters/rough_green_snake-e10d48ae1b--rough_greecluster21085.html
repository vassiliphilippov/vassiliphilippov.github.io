rough green snake

noun
a green non-venomous snake of the US
Pronunciation: / rʌf ɡrin sneɪk / (AmE), / rʌf ɡriːn sneɪk / (BrE)
a slender, bright green, non-venomous snake (Opheodrys aestivus) native to the southern and eastern United States, known for its rough-textured scales
