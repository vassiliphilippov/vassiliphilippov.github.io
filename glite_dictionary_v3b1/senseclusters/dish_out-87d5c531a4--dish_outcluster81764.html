dish out

verb
give or distribute in portions
Pronunciation: / dɪʃ aʊt / (AmE), / dɪʃ aʊt / (BrE)
to give or distribute something, especially food, comments, or criticism, often in portions or freely

  • synonym: hand out - to give something to each person in a group
