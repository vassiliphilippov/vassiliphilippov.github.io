Lepilemur

proper noun
genus of lemurs
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈliːmər / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈliːmər / (BrE)
a genus of nocturnal lemurs, known as sportive lemurs, native to Madagascar

Examples

Lepilemur genus
The Lepilemur is recognized for its large eyes and nocturnal habits in Madagascar's forests.
Researchers observed a group of Lepilemur feeding quietly in the treetops at night.
Conservation efforts are underway to protect the endangered Lepilemur from habitat loss.
