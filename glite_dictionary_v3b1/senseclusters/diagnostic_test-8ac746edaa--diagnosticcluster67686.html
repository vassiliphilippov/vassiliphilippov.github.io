diagnostic test

noun
a test to determine a disease or condition
Pronunciation: / ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑstɪk tɛst / (AmE), / ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk tɛst / (BrE)
a medical or scientific procedure performed to identify the nature or cause of a condition, disease, or problem

Examples

A diagnostic test result
Before surgery, a diagnostic test confirmed the presence of the tumor.
Blood samples are often used in a diagnostic test to check for infections.
The doctor ordered a diagnostic test to determine the cause of her symptoms.
