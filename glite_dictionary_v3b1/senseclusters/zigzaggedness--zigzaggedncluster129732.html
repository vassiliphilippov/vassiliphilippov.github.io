zigzaggedness

noun
the quality of being zigzagged
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈzɪɡˌzæɡɪdnəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of having a zigzag form or pattern, with sharp alternations in direction.

Examples
