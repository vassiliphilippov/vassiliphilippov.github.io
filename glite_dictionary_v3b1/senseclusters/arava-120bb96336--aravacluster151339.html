Arava

noun
trade name for a rheumatoid arthritis drug
7.4
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈrɑː.və/ /; / /ə.ˈræ.və/ (AmE), /ə.ˈrɑː.və/ /; / /ə.ˈreɪ.və/ (BrE)
A trademarked drug (leflunomide) taken orally to treat rheumatoid arthritis by inhibiting the proliferation of white blood cells and reducing joint inflammation.
