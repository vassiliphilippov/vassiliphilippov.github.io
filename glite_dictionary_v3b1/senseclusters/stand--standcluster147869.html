stand

noun
defensive effort or resistance, often final
Pronunciation: / stænd / (AmE), / stænd / (BrE)
a determined effort to resist attack or opposition, especially in a final or critical situation

Examples

A last stand
Her last stand in the debate won her unexpected support.
The soldiers made a desperate stand against the advancing enemy.
They will take a stand at the boundary to defend their land.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defense - the act of defending against attack
  • antonym: surrender - the act of giving up to the enemy or opposition
