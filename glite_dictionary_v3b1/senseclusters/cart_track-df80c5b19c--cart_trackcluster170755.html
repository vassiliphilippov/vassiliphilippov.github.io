cart track

noun
rough path or road for carts
Pronunciation: / kɑrt træk / (AmE), / kɑːt træk / (BrE)
a rough road or path, especially one made by the repeated passage of carts

Examples

A narrow cart track
A narrow cart track wound up the hillside between the rows of olive trees.
The rain turned the cart track into a muddy and impassable route.
We followed the cart track through the fields until we reached the old farmhouse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cart path - path made for carts, especially on golf courses or farms
  • degree higher: road - a wide way leading from one place to another, especially one with a prepared surface
  • synonym: trail - a path or track, especially one used for walking or riding
