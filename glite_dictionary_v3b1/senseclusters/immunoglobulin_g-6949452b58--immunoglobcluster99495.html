immunoglobulin G

noun
the main antibody in the blood
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmjunoʊɡləˌbjuːlɪn ˈdʒi / (AmE), / ˌɪmjuːnəʊˌɡlɒbjʊlɪn ˈdʒi / (BrE)
the most common type of antibody in the blood, important in fighting bacterial and viral infections

Examples

Immunoglobulin G antibody
