scryer

noun
person who sees visions
Pronunciation: / ˈskraɪər / (AmE), / ˈskraɪə / (BrE)
a person who claims to see visions or gain information by gazing into a crystal or similar object

Examples

The scryer gazes crystal
The scryer claimed to see distant lands in the swirling mist of her crystal ball.
Many people visited the scryer in hopes of learning about their future.
During the festival, a famous scryer offered to reveal secrets hidden from ordinary eyes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fortune-teller - a person who predicts the future
  • synonym: seer - a person who claims to see the future or hidden things
