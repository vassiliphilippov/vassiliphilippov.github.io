dispositionally

adverb
by temperament
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌdɪspəˈzɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to a person's usual mood, character, or tendency

Examples

She is dispositionally cheerful
She is dispositionally cheerful, always bringing a positive attitude to the team.
Some people are dispositionally cautious and prefer to avoid taking risks.
He remained dispositionally calm even in stressful situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temperamentally - in a way that relates to a person's temperament
