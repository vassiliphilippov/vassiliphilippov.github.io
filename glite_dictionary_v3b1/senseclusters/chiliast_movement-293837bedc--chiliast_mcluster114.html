chiliast movement

noun
religious movement expecting a coming millennium
9.9 9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪl.ɪ.əst ˈmuːv.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈkɪl.ɪ.əst ˈmuːv.mənt/ (BrE)
