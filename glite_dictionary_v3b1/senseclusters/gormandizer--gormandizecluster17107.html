gormandizer

noun
greedy eater
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔrmənˌdaɪzər / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːmənˌdaɪzə / (BrE)
a person who eats food in a greedy or gluttonous way

Examples

A gormandizer at buffet
At every buffet, he quickly gained a reputation as a gormandizer among his friends.
The gormandizer finished three plates before anyone else had finished their first.
She tried not to appear like a gormandizer at the formal dinner party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glutton - a person who eats or drinks excessively
  • synonym: gourmand - a person who enjoys eating and often eats too much
  • antonym: moderate eater - a person who eats in moderation
