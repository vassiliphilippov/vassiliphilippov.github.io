skin disorder

noun
disease affecting the skin
4.1
Pronunciation: / skɪn dɪsˈɔrdɚ / (AmE), / skɪn dɪsˈɔːdə / (BrE)

Examples

Common skin disorder
Eczema is a common skin disorder that causes inflammation and itching.
Many people experience a skin disorder at some point in their lives.
The dermatologist specializes in treating various skin disorders.
