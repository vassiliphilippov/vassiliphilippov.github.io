Ithaca

proper noun
Greek island; kingdom of Odysseus in legend
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɪθəkə / (AmE), / ˈɪθəkə / (BrE)
A Greek island in the Ionian Sea, west of mainland Greece, known in Homeric legend as the kingdom of Odysseus.

Examples

Ithaca island Greece
In literature, Ithaca symbolizes homecoming and longing.
Odysseus yearned to return to Ithaca after the Trojan War.
Tourists often visit Ithaca to explore its ancient sites and natural beauty.
