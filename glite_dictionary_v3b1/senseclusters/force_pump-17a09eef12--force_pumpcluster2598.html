force pump

noun
pump expelling liquid under pressure
Pronunciation: / fɔrs pʌmp / (AmE), / fɔːs pʌmp / (BrE)
a type of pump designed to raise liquid to a height and expel it under pressure

Examples

A force pump delivers water
A force pump can deliver water at much higher pressures than a simple lift pump.
Engineers installed a force pump to transfer water from the well to the overhead tank.
The old fire engine was equipped with a force pump to project water onto the flames.

  • synonym: pump - device for moving liquids or gases
