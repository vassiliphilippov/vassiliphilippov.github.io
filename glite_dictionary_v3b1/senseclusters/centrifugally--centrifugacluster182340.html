centrifugally

adverb
by centrifugal force
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛntrəˈfjuːɡəli / (AmE), / ˌsɛntrɪˈfjuːɡəli / (BrE)
in a way that moves outward from the center, especially by centrifugal force

Examples

Move centrifugally outward
The liquid was separated centrifugally to isolate its different components in the laboratory.
Particles in the spinning drum moved centrifugally away from the center.
The machine operates centrifugally to remove excess water from the clothes.
The children watched as the paint spread centrifugally from the spinning wheel.
Particles in the machine are thrown centrifugally during the rapid rotation.
The seeds were scattered centrifugally by the force of the spinning device.
