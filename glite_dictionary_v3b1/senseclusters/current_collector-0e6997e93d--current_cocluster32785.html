current collector

noun
device for transferring power to electric vehicles
6.8 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜr.ənt kəˈlɛk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈkʌr.ənt kəˈlɛk.tə / (BrE)
a device mounted on electric vehicles, such as trolleybuses, trams, or locomotives, that transfers electrical power from overhead wires or a third rail to the vehicle's equipment

Examples

Electric current collector
Technicians inspected the current collector to ensure safe operation along the entire railway route.
The tram's current collector needed maintenance after striking a branch on the overhead wire.
Without a properly functioning current collector, an electric locomotive cannot draw power.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pantograph - an apparatus mounted on the roof of trains or trams to collect electrical power from overhead lines
  • synonym: trolley pole - a pole mounted on electric vehicles for drawing current from overhead wires
