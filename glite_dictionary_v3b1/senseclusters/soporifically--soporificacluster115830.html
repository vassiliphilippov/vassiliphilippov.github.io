soporifically

adverb
in a sleep-inducing way
Pronunciation: / ˌsɑːpəˈrɪfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that causes sleep or extreme drowsiness

Examples

To soporifically cause sleep
The movie progressed so soporifically that several people in the audience began to doze off.
He spoke soporifically, and soon the entire class was struggling to stay awake.
The medication worked soporifically, helping her fall asleep within minutes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energetically - in a lively or active way
  • synonym: sleepily - in a way that shows tiredness or a desire to sleep
