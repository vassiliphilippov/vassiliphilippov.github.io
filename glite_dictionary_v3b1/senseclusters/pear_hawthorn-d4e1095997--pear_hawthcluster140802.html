pear hawthorn

noun
American hawthorn tree with pear-shaped berries
Pronunciation: / pɛr ˈhɔːθɔrn / (AmE), / pɛə ˈhɔːθɔːn / (BrE)
an American hawthorn tree or shrub (Crataegus calpodendron) that is erect, mostly thornless, and bears somewhat pear-shaped berries

Examples

The pear hawthorn tree
The pear hawthorn is known for its distinctive pear-shaped fruit.
Unlike other species, the pear hawthorn has very few thorns on its branches.
We planted a pear hawthorn in the garden to attract birds in autumn.
