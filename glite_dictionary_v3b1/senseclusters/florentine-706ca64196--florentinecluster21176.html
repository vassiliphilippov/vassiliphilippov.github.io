Florentine

noun
nut and fruit biscuit
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːrəntiːn / (AmE), / ˈflɒrəntiːn / (BrE)
a sweet biscuit made with nuts and dried fruit, often coated with chocolate on one side

Examples

A Florentine biscuit
She offered a delicious Florentine with her afternoon tea.
The bakery sells a variety of treats, including the popular Florentine.
He enjoyed a Florentine with his coffee during the break.
