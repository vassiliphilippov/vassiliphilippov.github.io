BLS

noun
emergency medical care
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbiː.el.es/ (AmE), /ˈbiː.el.es/ (BrE)
emergency medical care that supports vital body functions such as breathing and circulation until full treatment is available

Examples

BLS certification
All paramedics must be certified in BLS before working in the field.
The ambulance crew provided BLS to the patient until advanced help arrived.
Learning BLS can help you save lives in emergency situations.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ALS - advanced life support, a higher level of emergency medical care
  • synonym: CPR - a lifesaving technique used in emergencies when breathing or heartbeat has stopped
