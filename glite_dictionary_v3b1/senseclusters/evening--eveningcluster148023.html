evening

noun
final or concluding period
Pronunciation: / ˈivnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ / (BrE)
the final stage or concluding period of a process, era, or entity, often used metaphorically

Examples

Evening of his life
His latest works were written in the evening of his career.
Many historians regard the Renaissance as the evening of the Middle Ages.
The reforms came during the evening of the empire's power.
