steamboat

verb
to travel by steamboat
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstiːmˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈstiːmbəʊt / (BrE)
to travel by steamboat or to transport people or goods using a steamboat

Examples

To steamboat downriver
They spent the summer steamboating down the river, stopping at small riverside towns.
In the 19th century, many traders made a living by steamboating goods along the Mississippi.
Tourists enjoy steamboating on the old paddlewheelers for a taste of history.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: row - to move a boat using oars
  • synonym: sail - to travel in a boat or ship on water
