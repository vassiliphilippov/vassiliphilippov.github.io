actinomycetous

adjective
of or pertaining to actinomycetes (bacteria)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ækˌtaɪnəˌmaɪˈsitəs / (AmE), / ækˌtɪnəʊmaɪˈsiːtəs / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of actinomycetes, a group of filamentous bacteria.

Examples

Actinomycetous bacteria
Researchers studied the actinomycetous flora present in marine sediments.
Some antibiotics are produced by actinomycetous bacteria found in decaying organic matter.
The actinomycetous colonies formed a dense, branching network in the soil sample.
