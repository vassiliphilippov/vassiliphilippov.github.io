hypersexual

noun
person with excessive libido
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈsɛkʃuəl / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈsɛkʃuəl / (BrE)
a person who has an unusually high or excessive level of sexual desire or activity

Examples

A hypersexual person
The psychologist explained that a hypersexual may struggle to control their impulses.
Some people unfairly judge a hypersexual without understanding the underlying causes.
Being labeled as a hypersexual can affect a person's relationships and self-esteem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: asexual - a person who does not experience sexual attraction
  • synonym: nymphomaniac - a person, especially a woman, with excessive sexual desire
  • synonym: sex addict - a person who is addicted to sexual activity
