associate

noun
thing or event that accompanies another
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈsəʊsiˌeɪt / (BrE)
an event, thing, or phenomenon that typically accompanies or is closely connected with another

Examples

An associate of stress
Anxiety can sometimes be the associate of major life changes.
In that climate, heavy rainfall is often the associate of severe winds.
The mayor's speech was the associate to the unveiling of the statue.
