Norfolk terrier

noun
small English terrier breed
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔr.fək ˈtɛr.i.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɔː.fək ˈter.i.ə / (BrE)
A small English breed of terrier with a straight, wiry, grizzled coat and dropped ears, known for its lively and good-natured temperament.

Examples

A Norfolk terrier dog
At the dog show, her Norfolk terrier won first prize for best coat.
Many city dwellers choose the Norfolk terrier because of its compact size and energetic nature.
The Norfolk terrier is known for its friendly personality and adaptability to family life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Norwich terrier - a similar small English terrier breed with erect ears
