mottling

noun
spotting with different shades or colors
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑtlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɒtlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or result of coloring or marking with spots or blotches of different shades or colors

Examples

Stone mottling
The mottling of the stone gives it a unique and attractive appearance.
The leaves showed mottling as a result of the virus infection.
The veterinarian noted mottling on the horse's skin during the examination.

  • synonym: spotting - the act of marking with spots
