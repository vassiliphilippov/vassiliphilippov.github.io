battlesight

noun
sight arrangement for rapid short-range aiming
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬lˌsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈbætlˌsaɪt / (BrE)
a type of sight arrangement on a firearm that allows quick aiming at short distances without adjusting for range

Examples

The battlesight setting
He adjusted the battlesight before entering the urban training course.
The rifle was equipped with a battlesight for quick target acquisition during combat.
Using a battlesight, soldiers can aim more rapidly in close-quarters situations.
