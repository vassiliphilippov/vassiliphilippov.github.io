extendable

adjective
able to be extended or lengthened
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɛndəbl / (BrE)
able to be made longer or larger; capable of being extended.

Examples

Extendable ladder
Some ladders are extendable, allowing you to reach higher places safely.
The table has an extendable leaf for extra guests.
This contract includes an extendable option for another year of service.

Related Terms

  • antonym: collapsible - able to be folded into a smaller form
  • synonym: expandable - able to be made larger or to increase in size
