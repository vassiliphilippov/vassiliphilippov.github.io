Rhiptoglossa

noun
group of Old World chameleons
Pronunciation: / ˌrɪptəˈɡlɒsə / (AmE), / ˌrɪptəˈɡlɒsə / (BrE)
A group of Old World chameleons; in some classifications treated as a superfamily within the order Sauria.
