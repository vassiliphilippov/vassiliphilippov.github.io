thrower

noun
a person twisting silk or rayon into yarn
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊər / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to twist silk or rayon filaments into thread or yarn

Examples

Silk thread thrower
Becoming a skilled thrower required both patience and dexterity.
In the textile factory, each thrower was responsible for several spools of rayon.
The experienced thrower carefully twisted the silk filaments into a strong, even thread.
