makuta

noun
Afro-Cuban hand drum
Pronunciation: / maˈkuːtə / (AmE), / maˈkuːtə / (BrE)
a large hand drum used in Afro-Cuban music and religious ceremonies, especially in traditional rhythms and dances

Examples

Makuta drum performance
The musicians played a lively rhythm on the makuta during the festival.
She learned to perform traditional songs using the makuta in her music class.
The deep sound of the makuta filled the room during the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bata - a double-headed drum used in Yoruba and Afro-Cuban music
  • synonym: conga - a tall, narrow drum used in Latin American music
