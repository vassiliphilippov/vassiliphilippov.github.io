homemade vanilla extract

noun
vanilla extract made at home
Pronunciation: / ˌhoʊmˌmeɪd vəˈnɪlə ɪkˈstrækt / (AmE), / ˌhəʊmˌmeɪd vəˈnɪlə ɪkˈstrakt / (BrE)
vanilla extract prepared at home rather than bought commercially, typically by infusing alcohol with vanilla beans

Examples

Homemade vanilla extract recipe
It takes several weeks for homemade vanilla extract to develop its full taste.
Making homemade vanilla extract requires only vanilla beans and a bottle of vodka.
Many bakers prefer to use homemade vanilla extract for its rich and authentic flavor.

Related Terms
