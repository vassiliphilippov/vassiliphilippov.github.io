varnished

adjective
covered with a layer of varnish
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑrnɪʃt / (AmE), / ˈvɑːnɪʃt / (BrE)
covered with a layer of varnish or a similar protective coating, usually to provide a shiny finish or protect the surface.

Examples

Varnished wooden floors
She admired the varnished floors throughout the old mansion.
The antique table had a beautifully varnished surface that glowed in the sunlight.
You can tell if wood is varnished by its glossy sheen and smooth texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unvarnished - not coated with varnish or left in a natural state
