nonenterprising

adjective
lacking initiative or adventurousness
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnˈɛn.tɚˌpraɪ.zɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈɛn.təˌpraɪ.zɪŋ / (BrE)
not willing to take risks or show initiative; lacking in enterprise or adventurousness

Examples

A nonenterprising person
He was criticized for being nonenterprising when it came to making business decisions.
Her nonenterprising attitude keeps her from seizing new opportunities at work.
The team remained nonenterprising, preferring to stick to familiar routines rather than try innovative approaches.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: adventurous - willing to take risks or try out new methods
  • antonym: enterprising - having or showing initiative and resourcefulness
