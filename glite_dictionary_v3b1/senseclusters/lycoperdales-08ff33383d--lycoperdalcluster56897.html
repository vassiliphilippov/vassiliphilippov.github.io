Lycoperdales

noun
order of fungi including puffballs
9.9
Pronunciation: / laɪkoʊˈpɜːrdeɪliːz / (AmE), / laɪkəʊˈpɜːdeɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of fungi characterized by fleshy, often globose fruiting bodies, including puffballs and earthstars
