Malpighian

adjective
of Malpighi or his structures
9.9
Pronunciation: / mælˈpɪgiən / (AmE), / mælˈpɪgiən / (BrE)
relating to Marcello Malpighi or anatomical structures named after him, such as Malpighian tubules or bodies

Examples

Malpighian tubules
The Malpighian tubules in insects function as excretory organs.
A Malpighian layer can be found in the epidermis of the skin.
Researchers studied the Malpighian bodies in the kidney to understand filtration.
