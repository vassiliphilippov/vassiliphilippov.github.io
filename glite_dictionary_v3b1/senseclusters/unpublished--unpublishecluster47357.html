unpublished

adjective
not published; not made public
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpʌblɪʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈpʌblɪʃt / (BrE)
not made available to the public through print or other media; not published.

Examples

An unpublished work
Many artists have works that are still unpublished and only known to close friends.
Researchers studied letters that remained unpublished for over a century.
The author donated several unpublished poems to the university archive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: published - made available publicly; issued in print or digital form
