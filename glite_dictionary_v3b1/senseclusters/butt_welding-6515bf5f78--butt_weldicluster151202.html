butt welding

noun
joining parts end-to-end by welding
9.4
Pronunciation: / bʌt ˈwɛldɪŋ / (AmE), / bʌt ˈwɛldɪŋ / (BrE)
A welding process in which two parts are joined end-to-end in a single plane to form a butt joint.

Examples

Butt welding process
Many modern pipelines are constructed with butt welding techniques for maximum durability.
Proper alignment is crucial when performing butt welding on metal sheets.
The pipes were joined using butt welding to ensure a strong, seamless connection.
