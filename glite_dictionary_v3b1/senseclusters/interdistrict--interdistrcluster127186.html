interdistrict

adjective
between districts
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈdɪstrɪkt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈdɪstrɪkt / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between different districts

Examples

Interdistrict transfers
The new policy allows for interdistrict transfers of students between neighboring school districts.
An interdistrict agreement was signed to improve cooperation on public transportation.
The competition is an annual interdistrict event involving teams from across the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intradistrict - within the same district
