Kublai Khan

proper noun
Mongolian emperor who founded the Yuan dynasty
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːblaɪ ˈkɑn /; / ˈkuːblaɪ ˈkɑːn / (AmE), / ˈkuːblaɪ ˈkɑːn / (BrE)
the Mongolian emperor of China (1215–1294), grandson of Genghis Khan, who completed the conquest of China, founded the Yuan dynasty, and established his capital at Beijing
