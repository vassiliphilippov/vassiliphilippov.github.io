hypoproteinemia

noun
low blood protein condition
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪproʊˌproʊtiˈnimiə / (AmE), / ˌhaɪprəʊˌprəʊtiːˈniːmiə / (BrE)
a medical condition characterized by an abnormally low level of protein in the blood, often indicating dietary deficiency or underlying health disorders

Examples
