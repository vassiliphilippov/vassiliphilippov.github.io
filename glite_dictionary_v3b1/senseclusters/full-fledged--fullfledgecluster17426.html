full-fledged

adjective
(of a bird) fully grown, ready to fly
Pronunciation: / fʊl ˈflɛdʒd / (AmE), / fʊl ˈflɛdʒd / (BrE)
(of a bird) having reached full development with fully grown adult feathers and ready to fly.

Examples

Full-fledged bird
After weeks of care, the young robin is now a full-fledged bird ready to leave the nest.
The sanctuary releases only full-fledged owls back into the wild to ensure their survival.
When the chicks become full-fledged, their first flight is a remarkable sight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fledged - (of a bird) having the feathers needed to fly
