nonreligion

noun
opposition to religion
8.2
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.rɪ.ˈlɪdʒ.ən/ (AmE), /nɒn.rɪ.ˈlɪdʒ.ən/ (BrE)
the belief or attitude that religion should be rejected, excluded, or is harmful, often involving active opposition to religion in public life

Examples

Nonreligion in schools
The organization advocates for nonreligion in government policies and public education.
Her views on nonreligion have sparked debates in the local community.
Many people who support nonreligion believe in keeping religious influence out of politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheism - the belief that there is no god or gods
  • antonym: religion - the belief in and worship of a superhuman controlling power, especially a personal God or gods
  • synonym: secularism - the principle of separating religion from government and public affairs
