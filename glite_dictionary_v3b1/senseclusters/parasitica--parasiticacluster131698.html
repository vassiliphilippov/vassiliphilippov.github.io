parasitica

adjective
parasitic species
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkə / (AmE), / ˌpærəˈsɪtɪkə / (BrE)
used in scientific names to indicate that a species is parasitic, living on or attached to another organism

Examples

Parasitica species
The fungus named Ustilago parasitica infects certain types of grass.
Researchers discovered a new plant species called Loranthus parasitica in the rainforest.
The label on the specimen included the term parasitica to show its parasitic nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasitic - living on or in another organism and benefiting at its expense
