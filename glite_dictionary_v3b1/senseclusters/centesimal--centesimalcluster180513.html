centesimal

adjective
relating to hundredths
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtɛsəməl / (AmE), / sɛnˈtɛsɪməl / (BrE)
relating to or divided into hundredths; based on the number one hundred.

Examples

centesimal system
Interest rates are often calculated on a centesimal basis.
The centesimal system is fundamental in decimal notation and currency.
The solution was measured to the centesimal degree for accuracy.
