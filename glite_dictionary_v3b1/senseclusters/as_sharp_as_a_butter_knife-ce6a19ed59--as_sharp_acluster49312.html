as sharp as a butter knife

adjective
ineffective or not incisive (idiom)
Pronunciation: / əz ʃɑrp æz ə ˈbʌtər naɪf / (AmE), / əz ʃɑːp æz ə ˈbʌtə naɪf / (BrE)
used to describe something or someone as ineffective, blunt, or lacking incisiveness or precision, often in a sarcastic manner
