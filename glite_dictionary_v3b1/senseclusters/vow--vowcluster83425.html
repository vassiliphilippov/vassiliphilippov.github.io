vow

verb
dedicate by a solemn promise to a deity
6.9
Pronunciation: / vaʊ / (AmE), / vaʊ / (BrE)
to dedicate someone or something to a deity or sacred purpose by making a solemn promise or declaration

Examples

To vow a life
He decided to vow his life to the service of his god.
In a moving ceremony, the family vowed their child to the temple's care.
The ancient tribe would vow their first harvest to the goddess each year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dedicate - to devote for a special purpose, often religious
