Swiss cheese

noun
cheese with holes, from Switzerland
Pronunciation: / swɪs tʃiz / (AmE), / swɪs tʃiːz / (BrE)
A hard, pale yellow cheese with characteristic holes, originally made in Switzerland.

Examples

A slice of Swiss cheese
He offered me a slice of Swiss cheese with large holes in it.
Many sandwiches are made with Swiss cheese for its mild, nutty flavor.
The recipe called for grated Swiss cheese to be sprinkled on top.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Emmental - a type of Swiss cheese with large holes, also called Emmentaler
