logicality

noun
quality of being logical
Pronunciation: / ˌlɑːdʒɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌlɒdʒɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being logical or characterized by correct and valid reasoning

Examples

The logicality of reasoning
His explanation lacked the logicality expected in a scientific discussion.
The logicality of her argument convinced everyone in the meeting.
We must consider the logicality of each solution before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: illogicality - the quality of being illogical or lacking valid reasoning
  • synonym: logic - reasoning conducted according to strict principles of validity
