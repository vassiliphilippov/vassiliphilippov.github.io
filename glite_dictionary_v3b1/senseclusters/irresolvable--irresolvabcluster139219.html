irresolvable

adjective
impossible to solve
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈzɑːlvəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈzɒlvəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to solve, settle, or bring to a conclusion

Examples

Irresolvable conflict
The committee encountered an irresolvable conflict that prevented any agreement from being reached.
Some philosophical questions are considered irresolvable and have been debated for centuries.
Their differences proved irresolvable, so they decided to part ways.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intractable - very difficult or impossible to manage or solve
  • antonym: resolvable - able to be solved or settled
  • synonym: unsolvable - impossible to solve or find an answer to
