succussion

noun
shaking to mix
Pronunciation: / səˈkʌʃən / (AmE), / səˈkʌʃən / (BrE)
the act of shaking something, especially a liquid, to mix or energize it

Examples

Lab succussion of liquid
The laboratory technician performed succussion to ensure the chemicals were thoroughly blended.
Before taking the medicine, the instructions advised a brief succussion of the bottle.
Proper succussion is important in some scientific experiments to achieve accurate results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitation - the act of stirring or shaking a liquid
  • synonym: mixing - the process of combining or blending substances
