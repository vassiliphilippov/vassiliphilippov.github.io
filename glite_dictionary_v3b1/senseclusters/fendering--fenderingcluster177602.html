fendering

noun
installation of fenders
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɛndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the system or process of installing fenders to protect a ship or dock from impact or damage

Examples

Dock fendering
Proper fendering is essential to prevent damage when ships dock.
The port authority upgraded the fendering system to handle larger vessels safely.
Without adequate fendering, the dock suffered repeated damage from incoming boats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fender - a device used to prevent damage from impact, especially on ships or docks
  • synonym: protection - the act of keeping something safe from harm or damage
