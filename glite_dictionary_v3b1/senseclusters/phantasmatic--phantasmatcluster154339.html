phantasmatic

adjective
unreal or dreamlike
Pronunciation: / ˌfænˌtæzˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌfanˌtazˈmatɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a phantasm; unreal or dreamlike in appearance or quality

Examples

A phantasmatic image

The film's phantasmatic atmosphere made it difficult to distinguish reality from fantasy.
