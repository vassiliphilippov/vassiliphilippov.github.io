electrographic

adjective
using electrography
8.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to the production of images or copies using electrography, a process involving electrical or electrostatic methods.

Examples

Electrographic printing process
The laboratory used an electrographic process to duplicate the technical drawings efficiently.
Advances in electrographic technology have made high-speed printing more affordable.
The report was reproduced using an electrographic copier for distribution to all members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrostatic - relating to stationary electric charges or fields
  • antonym: photographic - relating to the process of producing images using light
