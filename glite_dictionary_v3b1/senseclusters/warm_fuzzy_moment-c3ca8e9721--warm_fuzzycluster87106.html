warm fuzzy moment

noun
emotionally touching or pleasant experience
Pronunciation: / wɔrm ˈfʌzi ˈmoʊmənt / (AmE), / wɔːm ˈfʌzi ˈməʊmənt / (BrE)
a brief experience that is pleasant and emotionally touching, evoking feelings of warmth or affection

Examples

A warm fuzzy moment
A warm fuzzy moment filled the room when the family reunited after years apart.
The old friends shared a warm fuzzy moment as they reminisced about their childhood.
Watching his daughter graduate was a warm fuzzy moment he would never forget.
