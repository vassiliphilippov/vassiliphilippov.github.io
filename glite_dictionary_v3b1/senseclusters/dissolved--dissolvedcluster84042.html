dissolved

adjective
(of solids) mixed into a liquid
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzɑːlvd / (AmE), / dɪˈzɒlvd / (BrE)
(of a solid substance) having been mixed with a liquid so as to become part of the liquid

Examples

Dissolved sugar
After stirring for several minutes, the dissolved sugar made the tea taste sweet.
Only add the dissolved powder to the solution once it is fully clear.
The instructions say to use dissolved yeast for best results when baking bread.

Related Terms

  • antonym: undissolved - (of a substance) not having gone into solution with a liquid
