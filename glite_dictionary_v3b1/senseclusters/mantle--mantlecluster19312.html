mantle

noun
a covering or something that envelops
2.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.təl/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.təl/ (BrE)
a covering, often one that conceals or envelops something, especially used in a figurative or poetic sense

Examples

Mantle of fog
A thick mantle of fog settled over the valley, hiding everything from view.
At night, the city is hidden beneath the mantle of darkness.
The forest was covered by a green mantle of moss and vines.
