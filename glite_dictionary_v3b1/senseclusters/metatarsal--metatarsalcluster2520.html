metatarsal

noun
bone between ankle and toes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛtəˈtɑrsəl / (AmE), / ˌmɛtəˈtɑːsəl / (BrE)
Any of the long bones in the foot located between the ankle and the toes.

Examples

A broken metatarsal
A broken metatarsal can take several weeks to heal properly.
He fractured his metatarsal during the football match and had to leave the field.
The doctor examined the X-ray and confirmed a crack in the second metatarsal bone.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: phalanx - any of the bones in the toes or fingers
  • degree lower: tarsal - any of the bones in the ankle region of the foot
