sniveling

noun
tearful, whining complaint
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪvəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsnɪvəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of complaining or whining in a tearful, self-pitying way

Examples

Constant sniveling
His constant sniveling began to annoy everyone in the office.
No one wanted to listen to the sniveling of someone unwilling to help themselves.
She dismissed his sniveling with a wave of her hand, insisting he be brave.

Related Terms
