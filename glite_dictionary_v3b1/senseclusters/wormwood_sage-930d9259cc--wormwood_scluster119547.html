wormwood sage

noun
silky-leaved aromatic perennial plant
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrmˌwʊd seɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈwɜːmwʊd seɪdʒ / (BrE)
A silky-leaved aromatic perennial plant (Artemisia wormwood) found in dry, northern regions of the Northern Hemisphere, known for its tawny flowers.

Examples

wormwood sage plant
In traditional herbal medicine, wormwood sage has been valued for its potential therapeutic properties.
The wormwood sage thrived in the well-drained soil of the northern hillside.
The gardener planted wormwood sage along the dry, sunny border for its aromatic foliage.
