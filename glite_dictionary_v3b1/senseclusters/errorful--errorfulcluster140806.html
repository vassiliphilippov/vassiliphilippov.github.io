errorful

adjective
full of mistakes
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrərfəl / (AmE), / ˈɛrərfʊl / (BrE)
containing many mistakes or errors

Examples

An errorful text
The first draft of his essay was so errorful that it was hard to understand.
An errorful calculation led to the wrong results in the experiment.
She was embarrassed by her errorful presentation at the meeting.
The report was so errorful that it had to be rewritten from scratch.
Her errorful calculations led to a major mistake in the final results.
If your work is errorful, it may not be accepted by the editor.
