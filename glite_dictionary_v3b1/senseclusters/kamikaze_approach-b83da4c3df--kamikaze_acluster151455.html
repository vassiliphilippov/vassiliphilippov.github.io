kamikaze approach

noun
reckless or self-destructive strategy
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑməˈkɑzi əˈproʊtʃ / (AmE), / ˌkamɪˈkɑːzi əˈprəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a reckless or self-destructive strategy or way of acting, often involving great risk or disregard for consequences

Examples

A kamikaze approach to climbing
Her kamikaze approach to rock climbing worried her friends.
Taking a kamikaze approach to negotiations can often lead to disastrous results.
The team's kamikaze approach paid off, but it could have ended very badly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cautious approach - an approach that avoids unnecessary risk
  • synonym: reckless - without regard for danger or consequences
