fuddy

adjective
old-fashioned; dull
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌdi / (AmE), / ˈfʌdi / (BrE)
old-fashioned or dull in attitude, style, or behavior

Examples

Fuddy clothes
Her fuddy taste in music made her seem much older than her friends.
The teacher's fuddy approach to technology frustrated the students.
He was called fuddy for refusing to try new styles of clothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times
  • synonym: outdated - no longer modern or fashionable
