glossy-furred

adjective
having shiny, smooth fur
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑsi fɝrd / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsi fəːd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having fur that is shiny and smooth in appearance.

Examples

A glossy-furred rabbit
A glossy-furred rabbit won first prize at the animal show.
Everyone admired the cat's glossy-furred back as it stretched in the sunlight.
The glossy-furred otter slipped silently into the river, water beading off its coat.
