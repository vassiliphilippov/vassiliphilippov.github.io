basket rummy

noun
rummy card game using two decks and wild cards
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskət ˈrʌmi / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪt ˈrʌmi / (BrE)
A form of rummy played with two decks and four jokers, where jokers and deuces are wild cards and the aim is to form groups of the same rank.

Examples

Basket rummy game
He taught his children how to play basket rummy during summer vacation.
The rules of basket rummy allow both jokers and twos to be used as wild cards.
We played basket rummy all evening with our friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rummy - a group of matching-card games based on forming sets and runs
