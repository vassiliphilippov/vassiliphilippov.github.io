reupholstery

noun
furniture fabric replacement
Pronunciation: / ˌriːʌpˈhoʊlstəri / (AmE), / ˌriːʌpˈhɒlstəri / (BrE)
the process of replacing or repairing the fabric, padding, or covering on furniture to make it look new or different
