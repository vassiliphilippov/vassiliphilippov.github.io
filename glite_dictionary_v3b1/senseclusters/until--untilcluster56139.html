until

conjunction
up to a time
Pronunciation: / ənˈtɪl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɪl / (BrE)
up to the time or event that is mentioned, marking when something ends or changes

Examples

Wait until it ends
We cannot leave until the meeting is finished.
She waited until her friend arrived before starting dinner.
The store is open until 9 p.m. on weekdays.

Related Terms

  • antonym: after - following the time of an event or action
  • synonym: till - up to the time of an event or action
