memorialize

verb
honor or commemorate a person or event
5.8 5.8
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈmɔː.rɪ.əl.aɪz/ (AmE), /mə.ˈmɔː.rɪ.əl.aɪz/ (BrE)
to honor or remember someone or something in a lasting or formal way, especially by creating a memorial or holding a ceremony

Examples

Historically significant events are often memorialised through annual public ceremonies.
