Examples

Synonyms

  • commemorate
    5.8 5.21/m
    commemorate is to publicly honor someone with ceremonies, events, or moments of remembrance, while memorialize means to honor in a lasting, formal way.
  • monumentalize
    9.5 0.07/m
    monumentalize means making a physical monument or plaque to remember someone, while memorialize covers any lasting or formal act of honor.
