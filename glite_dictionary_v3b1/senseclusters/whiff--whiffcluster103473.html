whiff

verb
briefly smell by inhaling
Pronunciation: / wɪf / (AmE), / wɪf / (BrE)
to briefly smell something by inhaling through the nose

Examples

To whiff a candle
Before buying, I always whiff the scented candles to choose my favorite.
He paused to whiff the aroma of the freshly brewed coffee as he entered the café.
She whiffed the bouquet of wildflowers, smiling at the delicate fragrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniff - to draw air audibly through the nose to detect a smell
