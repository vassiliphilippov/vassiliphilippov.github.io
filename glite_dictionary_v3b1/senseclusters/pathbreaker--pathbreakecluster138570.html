pathbreaker

noun
trailblazer or pioneer
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpæθ.breɪ.kər/ (AmE), /ˈpɑːθ.breɪ.kə/ (BrE)

Examples

As the pathbreaker, she cleared the way for the rest of the hiking group.
In deep snow, a skilled pathbreaker is essential for making progress up the mountain.
