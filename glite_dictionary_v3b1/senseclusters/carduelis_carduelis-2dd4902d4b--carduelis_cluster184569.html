Carduelis carduelis

noun
European goldfinch species
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈdjuːəlɪs kɑrˈdjuːəlɪs / (AmE), / kɑːˈdjuːəlɪs kɑːˈdjuːəlɪs / (BrE)
A small European finch with a crimson face and distinctive yellow-and-black wings, also known as the European goldfinch.
