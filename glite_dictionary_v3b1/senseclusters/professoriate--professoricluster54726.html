professoriate

noun
body of professors
Pronunciation: / prəˌfɛsəriˈeɪt / (AmE), / prəˌfɛsəriˈeɪt / (BrE)
the collective body of professors at a college or university

Examples

The professoriate group
The university's professoriate plays a crucial role in shaping academic policies.
Changes in funding affected the morale of the professoriate across several departments.
The annual meeting brought together the entire professoriate to discuss curriculum updates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faculty - the teaching staff at a university or college
  • antonym: student body - all the students at a school or university
