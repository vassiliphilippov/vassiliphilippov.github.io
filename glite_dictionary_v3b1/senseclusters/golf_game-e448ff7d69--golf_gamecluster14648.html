golf game

noun
the sport of playing golf
2.6
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf ɡeɪm / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ɡeɪm / (BrE)
A game played on a large open course in which players use clubs to hit a small ball into each of a series of holes in as few strokes as possible.

Examples

A golf game round
He enjoys playing a golf game with friends every weekend.
She improved her golf game after taking lessons from a professional coach.
The tournament features a challenging golf game with eighteen holes.
