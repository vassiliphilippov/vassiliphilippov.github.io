wayment

noun
sorrow; lamentation
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈweɪmənt / (BrE)
an archaic term for sorrow or lamentation

Examples

Old wayment song
The old ballad was filled with wayment for the fallen hero.
She spoke of her wayment after the long and bitter winter.
In ancient tales, wayment often followed times of great loss.
