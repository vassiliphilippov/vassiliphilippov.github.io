polychromatic

adjective
having multiple wavelengths or colors
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊliˈkroʊmætɪk /; / ˌpɑːlikroʊˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪkrəˈmatɪk / (BrE)
having or showing many different colors; multicolored (including light made of several colors or wavelengths)
