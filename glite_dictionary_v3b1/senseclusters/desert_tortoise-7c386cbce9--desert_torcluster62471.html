desert tortoise

noun
burrowing tortoise of western US and northern Mexico
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛz.ɚt ˈtɔr.təs / (AmE), / ˈdez.ət ˈtɔː.təs / (BrE)
a burrowing tortoise found in the arid regions of the western United States and northern Mexico, known for its domed shell and slow lifestyle

Examples
