in prohibitum

adjective
prohibited by law
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪn proʊˈhɪbɪtəm / (AmE), / ɪn prəʊˈhɪbɪtəm / (BrE)
forbidden or prohibited by law or statute

Examples

In prohibitum goods
The import of certain goods is considered in prohibitum under international trade agreements.
Actions taken in prohibitum may result in severe legal penalties for the offender.
Operating a business without the required license is clearly in prohibitum in this jurisdiction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illegal - not allowed by law
  • antonym: permitted - allowed by law or rules
