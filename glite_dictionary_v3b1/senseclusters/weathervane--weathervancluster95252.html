weathervane

noun
rotating device showing wind direction
9.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈweð.ər.veɪn/ (AmE), /ˈweð.ə.veɪn/ (BrE)
A device, often mounted on a roof, that rotates to show the direction from which the wind is blowing.

Examples

Roof weathervane
A rusty weathervane perched on top of the old barn, spinning gently in the breeze.
He admired the intricate design of the copper weathervane shaped like a sailing ship.
The weathervane always pointed east during storms in the region.

  • synonym: weathercock - weathervane often shaped like a rooster
