teletype

noun
message-sending machine
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.taɪp/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.taɪp/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.taɪp/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.taɪp/ (BrE)
