measurably

adverb
to a measurable degree
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚəbli / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒərəbli / (BrE)
To a degree that can be measured or detected; noticeably or significantly.

Examples

To measurably decrease
Prices have measurably decreased in the last quarter due to increased competition.
The patient's condition has measurably improved since starting the new medication.
The water level dropped measurably during the drought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imperceptibly - to a degree that cannot be perceived or measured
  • synonym: significantly - to a notable or important degree
