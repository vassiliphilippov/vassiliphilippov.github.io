Examples

To measurably decrease

The patient's condition has measurably improved since starting the new medication.
The water level dropped measurably during the drought.

Synonyms

    6.3 0.04/m
    quantifiably expresses change in exact amounts or numbers, while measurably means change that can be measured or detected.
  • appreciably
    4.9 1.03/m
    appreciably means enough change to be noticed or measured, while measurably focuses on being actually detected by measurement.
  • observably
    3.8 0.02/m

  • markedly
    4.6 4.88/m

  • significantly
