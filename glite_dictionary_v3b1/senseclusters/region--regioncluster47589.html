region

noun
large area of land or territory
1.0 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.dʒən/ (AmE), /ˈriː.dʒən/ (BrE)
a large area of land, space, or a virtual environment that is distinct from other areas because of particular characteristics or boundaries

Examples

Wildfires often occur in the western region of the country during dry summers.
Astronomers study different regions of the universe to understand its origins.
The Amazon basin is an important region for global biodiversity.
