Synonyms

  • highway
    1.0 36/m
    highway is a long fast road for travel between towns and cities, while main road serves as the principal route through an area.
  • thoroughfare
    5.1 1.86/m
    thoroughfare is a formal word for a public road people use to go from place to place, while main road is the common term for the principal route.
  • arterial road
    3.2 0.02/m
    arterial road is a technical urban highway designed for heavy traffic between key areas, while main road simply means the principal route through an area.
