aljamiado

noun
Romance text in Arabic script
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.hɑː.miˈɑː.doʊ /; / ˌælhɑːmiˈɑːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˌæl.hæ.miˈɑː.dəʊ /; / ˌaljamiˈaːdəʊ / (BrE)
a text or the practice of writing Romance languages, especially Spanish, using the Arabic script
