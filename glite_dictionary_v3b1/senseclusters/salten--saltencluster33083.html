salten

verb
to add salt
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːltən / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltən / (BrE)
to add salt to something, especially as a method of preserving or flavoring; archaic usage

Examples

To salten fish
In medieval kitchens, cooks would salten fish to preserve it for winter months.
Old instructions advised people to salten their butter to keep it fresh longer.
Before refrigeration, it was common practice to salten meat after slaughter.
