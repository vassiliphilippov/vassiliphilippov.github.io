wasting

noun
decrease in size of body tissue or organ
1.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈweɪ.stɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈweɪ.stɪŋ/ (BrE)
a gradual loss of body tissue, strength, or vitality, especially due to illness
