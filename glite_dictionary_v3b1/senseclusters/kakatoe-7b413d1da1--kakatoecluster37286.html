Kakatoe

noun
genus of cockatoos (parrots)
9.9
Pronunciation: /kæ.kə.ˈtoʊ/ /; / /kɑː.kə.ˈtoʊ/ (AmE), /kæ.kə.ˈtəʊ/ /; / /kɑː.kə.ˈtəʊ/ (BrE)
A genus of parrots in the family Psittacidae, commonly known as cockatoos, found from the Philippines and Wallacea to the Solomon Islands and south to Australia.

Examples

Kakatoe parrots
Members of the Kakatoe genus are known for their strong beaks and playful behavior.
Researchers discovered a new subspecies within the Kakatoe genus in northern Australia.
The Kakatoe genus includes several species of strikingly crested cockatoos.
