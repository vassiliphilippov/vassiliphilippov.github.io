war zone

noun
area where military conflict occurs
3.8 1.59/m
Pronunciation: / w…îr zo än / (AmE), / w…îÀê z…ô än / (BrE)
an area where fighting between armed forces is taking place, especially a region designated for military operations during a conflict

Examples

Military war zone
Many civilians have been displaced from the war zone in search of safety.
Reporters risked their lives to enter the war zone and cover the ongoing conflict.
The city was declared a war zone after heavy fighting erupted between rival factions.

Synonyms

  • combat zone
    5.5 0.38/m
    combat zone is where forces are actively fighting at the front, while war zone is any area designated for military operations.
  • combat area
    6.1 0.07/m
    combat area is a specific place where troops fight nearby, while war zone covers any region used for military action.
  • conflict zone
    4.3 0.16/m
    conflict zone is where armed clashes or unrest occur, while war zone refers to areas devoted to organized military operations.
  • field of operations
    7.2 0.01/m
    field of operations is a formal military area for active missions, while war zone is any region where fighting takes place.
  • theater of operations
    8.4 0.10/m
    theater of operations is a broad military region covering many campaigns, while war zone often means a smaller area of active fighting.
  • theater of war
    7.5 0.05/m
    theater of war is the whole land, sea, or air area for a war, while war zone usually names a more local fighting area.
  • theater
    7.7 3.97/m
    theater is a large strategic region of military operations, while war zone is a more local area where fighting happens.
