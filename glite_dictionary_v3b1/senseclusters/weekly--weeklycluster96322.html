weekly

noun
periodical published weekly
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkli / (AmE), / ˈwiːkli / (BrE)
a magazine or newspaper published once a week

Examples

A local weekly
He wrote a column for the local weekly for over ten years.
Our town has a free weekly delivered to every household.
She subscribes to a popular weekly focused on world affairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: daily - a newspaper published every day
  • antonym: monthly - a periodical published every month
