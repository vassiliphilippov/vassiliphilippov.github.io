pier glass

noun
a tall mirror between windows
Pronunciation: / pɪr ɡlæs / (AmE), / pɪə ɡlɑːs / (BrE)
a tall, narrow mirror designed to be placed between two windows or on a pier wall

Examples

A pier glass mirror
She hung a gilded pier glass between the windows to make the room look larger.
The antique pier glass in the hallway reflected the light beautifully.
Victorian homes often feature a pier glass as a decorative piece in the sitting room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mirror - a reflective surface, typically of glass, that forms an image of an object placed in front of it
