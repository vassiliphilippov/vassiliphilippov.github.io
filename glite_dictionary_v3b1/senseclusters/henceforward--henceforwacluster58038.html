henceforward

adverb
from this time on
7.6
Pronunciation: / hɛnsˈfɔrwɚd / (AmE), / hɛnsˈfɔːwəd / (BrE)
From this time on; starting now and continuing into the future.

Examples

Henceforward from now
Henceforward, the company will only accept digital submissions.
He vowed that henceforward he would never repeat his past mistakes.
The new policy will be effective henceforward, replacing the previous guidelines.
