spectrum

noun
a wide range or series
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛk.trəm / (AmE), / ˈspɛk.trəm / (BrE)
A broad range of related objects, values, qualities, ideas, or activities, often arranged in order or sequence.

Examples

A wide spectrum
Mental health exists on a spectrum, not as separate categories.
The artist's work covers a broad spectrum of styles and influences.
There is a wide spectrum of opinions among the group members.
