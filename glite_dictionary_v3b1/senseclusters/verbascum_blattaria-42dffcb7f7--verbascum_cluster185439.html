Verbascum blattaria

proper noun
species of mullein with yellow or purplish flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / vərˈbæskəm bləˈtɛəriə / (AmE), / vəˈbaskəm bləˈtɛːrɪə / (BrE)
A species of mullein native to Europe with smooth leaves and large yellow or purplish flowers, now naturalized as a weed in North America.

Examples

Verbascum blattaria plant
Verbascum blattaria can often be found growing along roadsides and waste places.
Gardeners sometimes remove Verbascum blattaria because it spreads aggressively.
The tall, flowering spikes of Verbascum blattaria are easy to recognize in the summer.
