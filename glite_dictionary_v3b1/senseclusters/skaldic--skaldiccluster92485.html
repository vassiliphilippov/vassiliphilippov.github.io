skaldic

adjective
Norse poetic tradition
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔldɪk / (AmE), / ˈskɔːldɪk / (BrE)
relating to the poetry or poets of medieval Scandinavia, especially those known as skalds

Examples

Skaldic poetry
The museum displayed ancient manuscripts featuring skaldic poetry from the Viking Age.
He studied the complex structure of skaldic verses for his thesis on Norse literature.
Many skaldic poems were composed to honor kings and warriors in medieval Scandinavia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Norse - relating to medieval Scandinavia or its people
  • synonym: epic - relating to long narrative poems about heroic deeds
