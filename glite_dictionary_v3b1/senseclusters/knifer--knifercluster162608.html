knifer

noun
person who carves
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪfər / (AmE), / ˈnaɪfə / (BrE)
a person who uses a knife to cut or carve food or other materials, especially as a job or craft

Examples

A food knifer
The skilled knifer prepared intricate vegetable garnishes for the banquet.
As a professional knifer, she specialized in carving ice sculptures for events.
During the demonstration, the knifer showed how to safely cut different types of meat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carver - a person who cuts or shapes something, especially food or wood
