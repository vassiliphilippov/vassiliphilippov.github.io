mana

noun
spiritual authority
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈmɑːnə / (BrE)
a person's or group's social authority, prestige, or influence, often regarded as a source of power

Examples

Chief's mana
The chief's mana was recognized by everyone in the community.
She gained mana through years of dedicated service to her people.
Losing the competition caused his mana to diminish among his peers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: influence - the power to affect people or events
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unknown or unimportant
  • synonym: power - the ability to control or direct others
  • synonym: prestige - respect and admiration felt for someone based on their achievements or quality
