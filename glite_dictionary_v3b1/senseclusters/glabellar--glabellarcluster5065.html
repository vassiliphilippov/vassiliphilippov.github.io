glabellar

adjective
relating to the glabella (forehead area)
Pronunciation: / ɡləˈbɛlɚ / (AmE), / ɡləˈbɛlə / (BrE)
relating to the glabella, the smooth area of the forehead above and between the eyebrows

Examples

The glabellar region
A visible scar was present in the glabellar region after the accident.
The doctor checked the patient’s glabellar reflex during the neurological exam.
Wrinkles can sometimes appear in the glabellar area as a person ages.
