mold

noun
dish or dessert shaped in a mold
3.8 3.8
Pronunciation: / moʊld / (AmE), / məʊld / (BrE)
a container, object, or pattern used to give shape to something, or the result of shaping; also, a food that has been shaped in a mold

Examples

Mold for jelly
A traditional holiday mold graced the center of the table, filled with aspic and vegetables.
Her favorite dessert is a fruit gelatin mold made with strawberries and cream.
The chef prepared a stunning salmon mold for the party buffet.
A sculptor uses a mold to reproduce statues in various materials.
She carefully removed the ice from the mold and placed it in the glass.
The chocolate was poured into a mold to create fun shapes for the party.
A plaster mold of her hand was made as a keepsake.
Many ancient molds have been discovered during archaeological excavations.
The artist created a bronze mold of a horse for the exhibition.

  • synonym: gelatin - a jelly-like substance used especially in desserts
