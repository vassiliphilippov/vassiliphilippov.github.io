bloating

noun
abdominal swelling
3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbloʊ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbləʊ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
an uncomfortable swelling or feeling of fullness in the stomach area, often caused by gas or overeating

Examples

Stomach bloating
After eating a large meal, he experienced uncomfortable bloating for several hours.
Certain foods can cause bloating and make your stomach feel swollen.
She tried drinking herbal tea to relieve her bloating after dinner.
After eating too quickly, he complained of severe bloating and discomfort.
Certain foods can cause bloating in people with sensitive stomachs.
She tried herbal tea to relieve her bloating after the heavy meal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfort - a state of physical ease and freedom from pain or discomfort
  • synonym: discomfort - a feeling of slight pain or unease
  • synonym: distension - the state of being stretched or swollen, especially in the abdomen
  • synonym: flatulence - the accumulation of gas in the digestive system
