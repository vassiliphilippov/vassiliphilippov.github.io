basmati

adjective
of basmati rice
4.1
Pronunciation: / bɑːsˈmɑːti / (AmE), / bɑːsˈmɑːti / (BrE)
describing rice or dishes made from or flavored like basmati rice, known for its fragrance and long grains

Examples

Basmati rice dish
The chef recommended a basmati rice side to complement the spicy curry.
She prefers basmati dishes because of their unique aroma and texture.
A bowl of basmati pilaf was served with roasted vegetables at the dinner party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aromatic - having a noticeable and pleasant smell
  • antonym: plain - lacking distinctive or strong flavor or aroma
