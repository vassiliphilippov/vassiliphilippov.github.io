positiveness

noun
quality of being definite or unquestionable

the quality of being definite, certain, or assertively confident

Examples

Firm positiveness
Her statement was marked by a positiveness that silenced all objections.
The positiveness of scientific laws makes them widely accepted in the academic community.
The positiveness of the evidence left no room for doubt at the trial.
He spoke with such positiveness that no one questioned his decision.
Her positiveness in discussions often persuaded others to follow her ideas.
The positiveness of her response reassured everyone in the room.

Synonyms

  • positivity
    4.2 0.02/m
    positivity is confidence in a view or statement and often feels upbeat, while positiveness stresses clear, assertive certainty in what is said.
  • sureness
    3.6 0.22/m
    sureness means being free from doubt and quietly certain, while positiveness adds an outward, assertive tone that shows definite belief.
  • definiteness
    5.6 0.09/m
    definiteness is clear and precisely defined so ideas leave little doubt, while positiveness focuses on firm, assertive confidence.
  • assuredness
    6.1 0.07/m
    assuredness is calm, composed confidence shown under pressure, while positiveness is a more forceful, definite form of confidence.
