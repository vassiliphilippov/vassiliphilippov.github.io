Synonyms

  • caution
    3.0 10/m
    caution is used to advise someone carefully about a risk or danger, while warn is a more general act of telling about danger.
  • alert
    2.3 13/m
    alert signals or warns people quickly about an immediate danger or problem, while warn is a general notice to be careful.
  • forewarn
    4.8 1/m
    forewarn tells someone in advance about possible trouble, while warn may be used at any time to tell about danger.
  • prewarn
    4.2 0.01/m
    prewarn means to warn someone ahead of time before an event, while warn is the basic verb for telling about danger.

  • antonym: reassure - to say or do something to remove someone's doubts or fears
