evader

noun
person who avoids
5.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈveɪdər / (AmE), / ɪˈveɪdə / (BrE)
a person who avoids or escapes something, especially by using clever or indirect methods

Examples

A clever evader
The police finally caught the evader after a long search.
As a skilled evader, she managed to avoid paying taxes for years.
The border guards set up extra patrols to catch any evader trying to slip through.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dodger - a person who avoids something, especially a responsibility
  • synonym: escapee - a person who has escaped from a place or situation
  • antonym: pursuer - a person who chases or follows someone
