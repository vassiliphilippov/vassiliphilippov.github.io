keep it juicy

verb
maintain moisture in food during cooking
4.2 6.3
Pronunciation: /kiːp ɪt ˈdʒuː.siː/ /; / /kiːp ət ˈdʒuː.siː/ (AmE), /kiːp ɪt ˈdʒuː.siː/ /; / /kiːp ət ˈdʒuː.siː/ (BrE)

Examples
