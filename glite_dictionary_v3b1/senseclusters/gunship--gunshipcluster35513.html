gunship

noun
armed military aircraft
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnʃɪp / (BrE)
a military aircraft equipped with heavy weapons, designed to attack ground targets and support troops

Examples

Attack gunship
The gunship circled above, firing at enemy positions to support the ground troops.
A heavily armed gunship can change the outcome of a battle by providing powerful air support.
During the night mission, a gunship illuminated the area with flares before launching its attack.
The gunship circled above, providing support to the soldiers on the ground.
A heavily armed gunship launched missiles at enemy positions during the battle.
The convoy was protected by a gunship flying overhead throughout the mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attack helicopter - a helicopter armed with weapons for attacking ground targets
  • antonym: fighter jet - a fast military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat
