attaboy

noun
expression of praise
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈætəˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈætəˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal expression used to praise or encourage a boy or man for doing something well

Examples

A proud attaboy
After he fixed the car by himself, his father said, 'attaboy!'
The teacher gave an enthusiastic 'attaboy' when the student solved the difficult problem.
You scored the winning point—attaboy!

Related Terms

  • synonym: bravo - an exclamation used to express approval or praise
  • synonym: well done - an expression used to praise someone for an achievement
