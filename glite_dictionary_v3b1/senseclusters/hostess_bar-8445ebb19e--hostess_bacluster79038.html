hostess bar

noun
bar with female hostesses entertaining guests
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊstɪs bɑr / (AmE), / ˈhəʊstɪs bɑː / (BrE)
A bar where female hostesses provide entertainment and company to customers, often engaging in conversation and serving drinks.

Examples

A hostess bar with drinks
