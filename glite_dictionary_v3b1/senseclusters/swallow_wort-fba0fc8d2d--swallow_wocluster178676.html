swallow wort

noun
herbaceous plant with yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːloʊ wɜːrt / (AmE), / ˈswɒləʊ wɜːt / (BrE)
A perennial herb with branched woody base and bright yellow flowers, commonly found in Eurasia, especially in dry, rocky places.

Examples

Bright swallow wort flowers
Botanists often study swallow wort for its unique adaptations to dry climates.
