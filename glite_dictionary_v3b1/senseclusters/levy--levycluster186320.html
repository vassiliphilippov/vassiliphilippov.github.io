levy

noun
act of drafting into military service
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvi / (AmE), / ˈlɛvi / (BrE)
the act of enlisting people for compulsory military service

Examples

A military levy
During the 19th century, a levy could force young men into service.
The government issued a levy to build up its army during wartime.
The new levy caused widespread protests among the populace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conscription - the compulsory enlistment of people in military service
  • degree lower: recruitment - the act of enlisting people for service or work
