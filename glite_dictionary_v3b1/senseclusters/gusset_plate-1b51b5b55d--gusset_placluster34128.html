gusset plate

noun
metal plate for reinforcing joints
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌsɪt pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌsɪt pleɪt / (BrE)
a metal plate used to reinforce or connect structural elements, such as joists or beams, to provide strength and stability

Examples

Gusset plate metal
After inspection, they found a crack in the gusset plate supporting the roof truss.
Gusset plates are often used in steel structures to join beams and columns securely.
The engineer recommended installing a gusset plate at each joint to improve the bridge’s stability.
