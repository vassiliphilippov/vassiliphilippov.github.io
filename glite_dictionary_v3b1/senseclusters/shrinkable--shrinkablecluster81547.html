shrinkable

adjective
able to be shrunk
2.4 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈʃrɪŋ.kə.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈʃrɪŋ.kə.bəl/ (BrE)
Able to become smaller in size, especially when exposed to heat, moisture, or washing.

Examples

Shrinkable fabric
Always check the label to see if the fabric is shrinkable before putting it in the dryer.
The manufacturer recommends washing shrinkable garments in cold water to prevent size reduction.
This dress is made from a shrinkable cotton blend, so it may get smaller after washing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: shrinkproof - resistant to shrinking
  • antonym: stretchable - able to be stretched
