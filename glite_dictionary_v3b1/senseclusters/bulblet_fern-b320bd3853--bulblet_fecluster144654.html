bulblet fern

noun
a North American fern with bulbils
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌlb.lət fɝn / (AmE), / ˈbʌlb.lət fɜːn / (BrE)
A North American fern, especially Cystopteris bulbifera, often producing small bulbils on its leaflets.
