guttural

adjective
throat-produced sound
6.0 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌt̬.ɚ.əl / (AmE), / ˈɡʌt.ər.əl / (BrE)
(of a sound) produced in the throat; relating to or articulated in the throat.

Examples

Guttural sound
Certain languages feature guttural sounds that are unfamiliar to English speakers.
She spoke in a low, guttural tone that carried across the room.
The singer's voice had a distinctive guttural quality that captivated the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: throaty - having a deep, harsh sound as if produced in the throat
