flood of applications

noun
large number of applications at once
Pronunciation: / flʌd əv ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz / (AmE), / flʌd əv ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃənz / (BrE)
a very large number of applications submitted or received at the same time, often more than can be easily handled

Examples

A flood of applications
After posting the job online, the company was overwhelmed by a flood of applications.
The sudden popularity of the event led to a flood of applications from people eager to participate.
The university received a flood of applications after it announced new scholarship programs.
