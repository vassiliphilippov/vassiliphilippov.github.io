manifestness

noun
obviousness
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌfɛstnəs / (AmE), / ˈmænɪˌfɛstnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being clear, obvious, or easily perceived

Examples

The manifestness of intentions
The manifestness of his intentions left no room for doubt among the team.
She admired the manifestness of the solution, which made the process easy to follow.
The manifestness of the evidence convinced the jury quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being unclear or difficult to understand
