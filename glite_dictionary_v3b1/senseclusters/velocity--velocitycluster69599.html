velocity

noun
rate of incoming data
4.8
Pronunciation: /və.ˈlɑː.sə.tiː/ (AmE), /və.ˈlɒ.sə.tiː/ (BrE)
(in data engineering and big-data contexts) the speed or rate at which data arrives and must be processed, often considered when designing streaming systems.

Examples

Data velocity in streaming
Real-time dashboards depend on data velocity to reflect events as they happen across the network.
The streaming platform struggled to keep up with the high velocity of incoming sensor readings overnight.
Architects must account for data velocity when designing pipelines for rapid ingestion and processing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: data rate - the amount of data transmitted or received per unit of time; closely related to velocity in streaming contexts
  • synonym: throughput - the quantity of data processed by a system in a given time; often considered alongside velocity when sizing systems
