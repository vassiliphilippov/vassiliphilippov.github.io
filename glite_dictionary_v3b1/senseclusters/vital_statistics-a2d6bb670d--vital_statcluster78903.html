vital statistics

noun
official records of births, deaths, marriages, and health
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪt̮l stəˈtɪstɪks / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.təl stəˈtɪs.tɪks / (BrE)
official data relating to births, deaths, marriages, and health, typically collected by government agencies.
