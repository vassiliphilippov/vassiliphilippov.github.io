Indian blanket

noun
a North American wildflower with yellow and red flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
an annual wildflower of the central United States, with showy yellow flower heads marked with scarlet or purple in the center (Gaillardia pulchella)
