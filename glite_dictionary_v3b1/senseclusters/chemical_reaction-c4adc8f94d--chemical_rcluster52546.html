chemical reaction

noun
process of chemical change
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛmɪkəl riˈækʃən / (AmE), / ˈkɛmɪkəl riˈækʃən / (BrE)
a process in which substances are transformed into different substances through chemical change

Examples

A chemical reaction occurs
A chemical reaction occurs when baking soda mixes with vinegar and produces carbon dioxide gas.
Photosynthesis is a complex chemical reaction that plants use to make food.
Rust on metal is the result of a slow chemical reaction with oxygen and moisture.
