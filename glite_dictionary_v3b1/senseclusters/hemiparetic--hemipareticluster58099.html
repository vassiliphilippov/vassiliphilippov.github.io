hemiparetic

adjective
one-sided weakness
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmiˌpəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛmiˌpəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)
having weakness or partial paralysis affecting one side of the body

Examples

Hemiparetic patient
The patient was hemiparetic after suffering a stroke on the left side of his brain.
Physical therapy helped the hemiparetic child regain some movement in her right arm and leg.
Doctors noted that the hemiparetic symptoms improved gradually with rehabilitation.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hemiplegic - having complete paralysis on one side of the body
  • antonym: paraparetic - having weakness in both legs
