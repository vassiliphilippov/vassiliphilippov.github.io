PM

noun
medical examination of a dead body
8.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / piÀê Àà…õm / (AmE), / piÀê Àà…õm / (BrE)
An examination and dissection of a dead body to determine the cause of death or changes produced by disease; a postmortem examination.

Examples

A PM report

The family requested a second PM for further investigation.
The pathologist performed a PM to establish the cause of death.
