pre-enlistment

adjective
before joining
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpriː ɪnˈlɪstmənt / (AmE), / ˌpriː ɪnˈlɪstmənt / (BrE)
happening or required before someone officially joins the military or another organization

Examples

Pre-enlistment medical exam
All recruits must complete a pre-enlistment physical exam before starting basic training.
The pre-enlistment interview helps determine if candidates are suitable for military service.
She attended a pre-enlistment seminar to learn more about what to expect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: post-enlistment - happening after someone has joined the military or organization
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before something more important
