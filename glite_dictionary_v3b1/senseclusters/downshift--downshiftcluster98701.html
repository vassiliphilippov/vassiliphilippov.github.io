downshift

noun
change to a lower gear
7.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈdaʊn.ʃɪft/ (AmE), /ˈdaʊn.ʃɪft/ (BrE)
a change to a lower gear in a car, bicycle, or similar vehicle, typically to gain more power or control

Examples

A downshift in gear
After the downshift, her bike was much easier to pedal up the steep road.
He heard the engine rev louder after the downshift on the highway.
The sudden downshift helped the car accelerate as it climbed the hill.
