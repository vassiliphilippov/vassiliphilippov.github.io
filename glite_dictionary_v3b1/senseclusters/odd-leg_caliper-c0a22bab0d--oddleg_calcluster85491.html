odd-leg caliper

noun
type of caliper with curved legs
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɑd lɛɡ ˈkælɪpɚ / (AmE), / ɒd lɛɡ ˈkælɪpə / (BrE)
a caliper with both legs curved in the same direction, used for marking or measuring distances, especially in metalworking

Examples

An odd-leg caliper tool
An odd-leg caliper is especially useful when marking out work on flat metal surfaces.
Before drilling, she measured the distance with her odd-leg caliper to ensure precision.
The machinist used an odd-leg caliper to scribe a parallel line on the metal plate.
