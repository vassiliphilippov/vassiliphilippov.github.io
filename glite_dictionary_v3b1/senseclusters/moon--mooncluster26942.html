moon

noun
object resembling the moon
Pronunciation: / mun / (AmE), / muːn / (BrE)

Examples

Moon-shaped lamp
Her collection included a glass paperweight in the shape of a moon.
The antique clock features a rotating moon displaying different lunar phases.
The new lamp was designed to look exactly like a full moon on his nightstand.
