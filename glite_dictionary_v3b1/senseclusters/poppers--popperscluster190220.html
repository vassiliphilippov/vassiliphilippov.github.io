poppers

noun
party noise makers
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑpərz / (AmE), / ˈpɒpəz / (BrE)
small handheld devices that make a loud popping sound and release confetti or streamers when activated, often used at celebrations

Examples

Party poppers
The children laughed as they pulled the poppers and colorful streamers flew everywhere.
At the end of the party, everyone set off their poppers to celebrate the new year.
She bought a box of poppers to add excitement to her birthday party.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: firecracker - a small explosive device that makes a loud noise
  • synonym: party popper - a small device that pops and releases confetti at parties
