fretless

adjective
without frets
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfret.ləs/ (AmE), /ˈfret.ləs/ (BrE)
describing a stringed instrument that does not have raised strips (frets) on its neck

Examples

A fretless violin
She prefers playing a fretless violin for its smooth gliding notes.
The musician switched to a fretless bass to achieve a warmer tone.
Learning to play a fretless guitar requires a good ear for pitch.
