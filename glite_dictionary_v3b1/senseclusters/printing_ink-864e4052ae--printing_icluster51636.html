printing ink

noun
ink used for printing
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪntɪŋ ɪŋk / (AmE), / ˈprɪntɪŋ ɪŋk / (BrE)
a semisolid ink that dries quickly, used especially in the process of printing

Examples

Printing ink batch
He ordered a new batch of printing ink for the presses at the publishing house.
Some artists use printing ink to create bold, vibrant prints in their work.
The printing ink on the newspaper dried quickly, preventing any smudges.
