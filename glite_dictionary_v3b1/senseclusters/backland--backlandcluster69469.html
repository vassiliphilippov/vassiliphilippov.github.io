backland

noun
remote rural area
Pronunciation: / ˈbæklænd / (AmE), / ˈbæklænd / (BrE)
a remote or rural area far from towns and cities, often undeveloped or sparsely populated

Examples

A remote backland
They built a small cabin in the backland to escape the noise of the city.
Farmers in the backland rely on traditional methods to grow their crops.
Wild animals are more commonly seen in the backland than near urban areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backcountry - a sparsely inhabited rural area; wilderness far from cities
  • antonym: city - a large and densely populated urban area
  • synonym: hinterland - an area lying beyond what is visible or known, especially remote from urban centers
