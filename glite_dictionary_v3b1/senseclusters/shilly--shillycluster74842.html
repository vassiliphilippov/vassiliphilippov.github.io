shilly

adjective
shy or hesitant
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪli / (BrE)
old-fashioned term meaning shy, hesitant, or lacking confidence

Examples

A shilly answer
The child gave a shilly reply when asked to speak in front of the class.
Her shilly manner made it difficult for her to make new friends.
He looked shilly as he hesitated at the door, unsure whether to enter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - sure of oneself; not shy or hesitant
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
