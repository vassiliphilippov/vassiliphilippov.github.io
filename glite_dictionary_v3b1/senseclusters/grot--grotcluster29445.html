grot

noun
small decorative cave
Pronunciation: / ɡrɑt / (AmE), / ɡrɒt / (BrE)
A small picturesque cave, especially one used as a decorative feature in gardens.

Examples

Garden grot
During their walk through the estate, they discovered a hidden grot beside the ornamental lake.
The children played in a shady grot at the back of the garden.
Victorian gardens often featured a decorative grot as a place to rest and enjoy the scenery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grotto - a small picturesque cave, often artificial
