trichotillomania

noun
urge to pull out one's own hair
Pronunciation: / ˌtrɪk.əˌtɪl.oʊˈmeɪ.ni.ə / (AmE), / ˌtrɪk.əˌtɪl.əˈmeɪ.ni.ə / (BrE)
a psychological disorder characterized by an irresistible urge to pull out one's own hair

Examples

Trichotillomania hair pulling
After years of struggling with trichotillomania, she finally sought help from a therapist.
Children and adults with trichotillomania often feel ashamed of their behavior.
Support groups can provide valuable encouragement to those living with trichotillomania.
