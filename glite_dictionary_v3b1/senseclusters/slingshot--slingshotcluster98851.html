slingshot

noun
Y-shaped tool for shooting small objects
6.8 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋˌʃɑt / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋˌʃɒt / (BrE)
A plaything or tool consisting of a Y-shaped stick with elastic fastened between the arms, used for shooting small stones or other projectiles.

Examples

A slingshot toy
Carrying a slingshot was common among children in the countryside.
He used his slingshot to knock a can off the fence.
The boy built a slingshot from a tree branch and a rubber band.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catapult - a device for launching projectiles, especially larger ones
