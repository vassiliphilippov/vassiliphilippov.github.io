nonintoxicating

adjective
not exciting
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˌtɑksɪˈkeɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˌtɒksɪˈkeɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing excitement, strong pleasure, or exhilaration; not thrilling or highly stimulating

Examples

Nonintoxicating experience
The documentary was informative but deliberately nonintoxicating in its presentation.
He preferred a nonintoxicating atmosphere for studying, free from distractions.
Their conversation was pleasant but rather nonintoxicating, lacking any real excitement.
