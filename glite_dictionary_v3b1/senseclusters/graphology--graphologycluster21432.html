graphology

noun
study of handwriting for character analysis
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈfɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ɡrəˈfɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of handwriting, especially as a means of analyzing the writer's character or personality
