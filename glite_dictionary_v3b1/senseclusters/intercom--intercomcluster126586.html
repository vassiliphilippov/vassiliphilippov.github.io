A communication system that allows people in different parts of a building, ship, or other large area to speak to each other.

Examples

The intercom system
A voice crackled over the intercom announcing that the meeting would start in five minutes.
Schools often use an intercom system to make announcements to students and staff.
The receptionist called me on the intercom to let me know my guest had arrived.

Synonyms

  • intercommunication system
    4.8 0.01/m
    an intercommunication system is a formal building-wide setup for voice links between rooms, while an intercom is the common device people use.
  • interphone
    8.4 0.12/m
    an interphone is a telephone-style system for talking between rooms, while an intercom is the general name for room-to-room voice systems.
