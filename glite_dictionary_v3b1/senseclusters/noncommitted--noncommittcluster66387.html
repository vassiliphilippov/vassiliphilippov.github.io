noncommitted

adjective
not allocated
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəˈmɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtɪd / (BrE)
not allocated, assigned, or pledged to a particular purpose or use; available for other uses.

Examples

Noncommitted funds available
The organization kept some noncommitted funds in case of unexpected expenses.
Several employees remain noncommitted so they can be assigned to urgent projects as needed.
The manager explained that the resources were still noncommitted and could be used elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reserved - set aside or kept for a particular purpose
  • synonym: unallocated - not assigned or set aside for a specific purpose
