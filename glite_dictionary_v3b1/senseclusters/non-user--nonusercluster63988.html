non-user

adjective
not using something
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈjuːzər / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈjuːzə / (BrE)
describing a person or group that does not use a particular product, service, or system

Examples

Non-user group
The program offers special incentives to attract non-user groups to try the new service.
Researchers compared the habits of non-user households with those who regularly use the internet.
A non-user of social media may have different privacy concerns than frequent users.
