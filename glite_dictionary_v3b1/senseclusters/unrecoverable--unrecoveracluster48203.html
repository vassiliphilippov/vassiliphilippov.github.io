unrecoverable

adjective
impossible to recover
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈkʌvərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈkʌvərəbl / (BrE)
impossible to recover, restore, or regain; not able to be retrieved or recovered.

Examples

Unrecoverable losses
His mistake led to unrecoverable financial losses for the company.
Some memories lost to amnesia are unfortunately unrecoverable.
The damage to the computer's hard drive was unrecoverable after the fire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recoverable - able to be recovered or regained
