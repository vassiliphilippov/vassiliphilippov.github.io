autobus

noun
large public transportation vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈɔt̬oʊˌbʌs / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌbʌs / (BrE)
A large motor vehicle designed to carry many passengers, typically along a fixed route as part of public transportation.

Examples

A autobus stop
The city introduced a new fleet of autobuses to improve public transport.
The old autobus was painted bright yellow for greater visibility.
Tourists found it easy to travel between sites by autobus.
