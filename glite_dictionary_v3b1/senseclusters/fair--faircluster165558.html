fair

adjective
clear, pleasant weather
1.0
Pronunciation: / fɛr / (AmE), / feə / (BrE)
(of weather) free from clouds, rain, or storms; clear and pleasant.

Examples

Fair weather
After a week of rain, it was finally a fair and sunny day.
It remained fair throughout the afternoon, perfect for a picnic.
The forecast predicts fair weather for the entire weekend.
