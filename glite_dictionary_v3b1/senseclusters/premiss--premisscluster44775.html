premiss

noun
proposition assumed as basis for argument
6.0 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈpre.mɪs/ /; / /ˈpre.məs/ (AmE), /ˈpre.mɪs/ /; / /ˈpre.məs/ (BrE)
A statement or proposition that is assumed to be true and from which a conclusion is drawn, especially in reasoning or argument.
