julienne vegetable

noun
vegetable cut into thin strips
Pronunciation: / dʒuˈliːən ˈvɛdʒtəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʒuː.liˈɛn ˈvɛdʒ.tə.bəl / (BrE)
