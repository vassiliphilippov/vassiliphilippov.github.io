creep

noun
video-game AI unit (minion)
7.7 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / krip / (AmE), / kriÀêp / (BrE)
(in video games, especially MOBAs and RTS) a computer-controlled unit that regularly spawns and moves along a set path or lane; players defeat creeps to earn gold, experience, or map advantage.

Examples

Defeat a creep
In the early game, waves of creeps push down the center lane toward enemy towers.
You should last-hit the final blow on a creep to earn gold for your hero.
Developers adjusted the spawn rate so neutral creeps appear less frequently in the jungle.
