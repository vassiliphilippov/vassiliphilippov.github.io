sour gourd

noun
Australian tree with acid fruit
6.3
Pronunciation: / saʊɚ ɡɔrd / (AmE), / saʊə ɡɔːd / (BrE)
an Australian tree bearing an agreeable, acid-tasting fruit that resembles a gourd

Examples

Sour gourd tree in Australia
Botanists studied the sour gourd to better understand its adaptation to the Australian climate.
Many birds are attracted to the sour gourd tree because of its acidic fruit.
The fruit of the sour gourd is often used in traditional Australian recipes.
