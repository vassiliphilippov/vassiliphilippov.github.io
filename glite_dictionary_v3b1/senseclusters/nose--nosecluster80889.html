nose

verb
search or inquire in a meddlesome way
5.4
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
to search or inquire about something, especially in a meddlesome or prying way

Examples

To nose around
He nosed around the store, hoping to overhear something interesting.
Journalists were nosing around the politician’s private life, looking for a scandal.
Please stop nosing into my business—it's personal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pry - to inquire too closely into a person's private affairs
  • synonym: snoop - to investigate or look around furtively
