cowpat

noun
cow dung on ground
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌpæt / (AmE), / ˈkaʊˌpæt / (BrE)
a flat, round mass of cow dung found on the ground, especially in fields or pastures

Examples

A cowpat on grass
Be careful not to step in a cowpat while walking through the field.
The children laughed when they saw a large cowpat near the barn.
Farmers sometimes use a cowpat as natural fertilizer for their crops.
The children laughed as they jumped over each cowpat in the field.
Farmers sometimes collect cowpats to use as natural fertilizer for their crops.
We set up our picnic away from the cowpats scattered across the meadow.

  • synonym: dung - the solid waste of animals, especially farm animals
