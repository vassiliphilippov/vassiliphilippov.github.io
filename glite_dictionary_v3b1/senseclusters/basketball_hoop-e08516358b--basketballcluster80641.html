basketball hoop

noun
metal ring with net used in basketball
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbæskətˌbɔl hup / (AmE), / ˈbɑːskɪtbɔːl huːp / (BrE)
A horizontal circular metal ring with a net attached, through which players attempt to throw the basketball in the game of basketball.

Examples

A basketball hoop with net
After school, kids gathered at the park to shoot hoops at the old basketball hoop.
He installed a basketball hoop in his driveway so he could practice at home.
The player leaped high and dunked the ball through the basketball hoop.
