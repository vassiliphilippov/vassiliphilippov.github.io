rebait

verb
to bait again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbeɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbeɪt / (BrE)
to put bait on something again, such as a hook or trap

Examples

To rebait a hook
After each catch, the fisherman would rebait his hook to attract more fish.
She paused to rebait the trap before setting it back in the garden.
If the bait is gone, you should rebait your line before casting again.
