contextual definition

noun
definition by use in context
5.7 5.3
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtekst.tʃuː.əl def.ə.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtekst.juː.əl def.ɪ.ˈnɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
a definition of a word or phrase by illustrating how it is used in a specific context, often by embedding the term within a broader explanatory statement

Examples

A contextual definition example
A contextual definition often explains a word by showing it within the sentence or scenario where it typically appears.
Students were asked to provide a contextual definition of 'democracy' in their essays.
The philosopher used a contextual definition to clarify how the term was understood in everyday conversation.
