touch typing

noun
typing without looking at the keyboard
5.1 3.6
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / tʌtʃ ˈtaɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
a method of typing in which the fingers are trained to hit specific keys without the need to look at the keyboard, allowing reading and typing at the same time

Examples

Touch typing method
After several weeks of practice, she became proficient in touch typing.
Learning touch typing can significantly increase your productivity on the computer.
Many office jobs require applicants to have touch typing skills.
