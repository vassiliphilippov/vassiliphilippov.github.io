set one's watch by

verb
be extremely punctual (of a person)
1.5
Pronunciation: / sɛt wʌnz wɑtʃ baɪ / (AmE), / sɛt wʌnz wɒtʃ baɪ / (BrE)
(of a person) to be so consistently punctual that one can confidently predict the time by their arrival or departure.

Examples

To set my watch by her
If she says she’ll be there at six, I’d set my watch by it.
People often joke that they set their watch by his daily arrival on the train platform.
You can set your watch by her because she's never late to a meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: punctual - arriving or doing something at the expected time
