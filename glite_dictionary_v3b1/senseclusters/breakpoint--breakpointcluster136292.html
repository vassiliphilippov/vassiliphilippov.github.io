breakpoint

noun
DNA break location
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbreɪk.pɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈbreɪk.pɔɪnt/ (BrE)
a specific location on a chromosome or DNA where a break or structural change occurs, often related to genetic disorders

Examples

DNA breakpoint
Scientists discovered a breakpoint in the patient's DNA that explained the genetic disorder.

Identifying the exact breakpoint is crucial for understanding certain inherited diseases.
