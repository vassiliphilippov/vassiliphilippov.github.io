engineer

noun
person using science to solve problems
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.dʒəˈnɪr / (AmE), / ˌɛn.dʒɪˈnɪə / (BrE)
A person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or structures, using scientific knowledge to solve practical problems.

Examples

A civil engineer
As an engineer, he specializes in designing safer bridges.
She trained as an engineer to work in the renewable energy sector.
The engineer fixed the problem with impressive speed and precision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: architect - a person who designs buildings
  • degree lower: technician - a person skilled in technical work or equipment
