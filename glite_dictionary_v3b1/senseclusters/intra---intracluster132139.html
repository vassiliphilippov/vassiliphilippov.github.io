intra-

x
prefix: within
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrə / (AmE), / ˈɪntrə / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'within' or 'inside', used to form words indicating something occurring inside or within something else

Examples

Intra- venous line
The doctor inserted an intra-venous line to deliver medication directly into the patient's vein.
Company policies often differ for intra-office and inter-office communications.
The research focused on intra-cellular processes occurring within a single cell.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inter- - a prefix meaning 'between' or 'among'
