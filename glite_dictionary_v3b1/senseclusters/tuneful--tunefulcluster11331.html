tuneful

adjective
having a pleasant, melodic sound
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːn.fəl / (AmE), / ˈtjuːn.fəl / (BrE)
having a pleasant and melodic sound; full of tune or melody

Examples

A tuneful song
She always hums tuneful tunes while working in the kitchen.
That was a particularly tuneful melody played on the violin.
The choir's tuneful voices echoed through the cathedral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melodious - having a pleasant melody
  • antonym: tuneless - lacking musical quality or melody
