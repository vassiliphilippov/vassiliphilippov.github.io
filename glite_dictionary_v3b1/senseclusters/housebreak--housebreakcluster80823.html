housebreak

verb
train a pet to be clean indoors
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsˌbreɪk / (BrE)
to train a pet to behave cleanly indoors, especially by teaching it not to urinate or defecate inside the house

Examples

To housebreak a puppy
Are you trying to housebreak your new kitten?
It can be challenging to housebreak older dogs compared to young puppies.
We managed to housebreak our puppy in just a few weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: house-train - to train a pet to urinate or defecate outside or in a designated place indoors
  • synonym: housetrain - to potty-train a pet; teach to be clean indoors
