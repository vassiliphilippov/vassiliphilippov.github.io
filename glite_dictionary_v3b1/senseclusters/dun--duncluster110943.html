dun

noun
horse with dull brownish grey color
4.4
Pronunciation: / dʌn / (AmE), / dʌn / (BrE)
A horse of a dull brownish grey color, often with a darker mane and tail.

Examples

Dun pony
Her favorite pony was a spirited dun with a black mane.
Several duns were among the new foals born this spring.
The rancher pointed to a sturdy dun grazing near the fence.
