polo-neck collar

noun
folded, high-neck collar
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊloʊ nɛk ˈkɑlɚ / (AmE), / ˈpəʊləʊ nɛk ˈkɒlə / (BrE)
A high, close-fitting collar that folds over and covers the neck, typically on sweaters or shirts.

Examples

A polo-neck collar sweater
A polo-neck collar is ideal for keeping your neck warm in winter.
Designer sweaters with a polo-neck collar became very popular in the 1960s.
She wore a black polo-neck collar under her jacket for a sleek look.
