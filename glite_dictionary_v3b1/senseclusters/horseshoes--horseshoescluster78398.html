horseshoes

noun
game of throwing rings at a stake
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔr.shoʊz / (AmE), / ˈhɔː.sʃuːz / (BrE)
a game in which players take turns throwing U-shaped iron rings at a stake in the ground, aiming to encircle it or come as close as possible

Examples

Playing horseshoes game
Every summer, our family plays horseshoes in the backyard during the barbecue.
He spent the afternoon demonstrating his skill at horseshoes to his friends.
Tournament rules require that official horseshoes are used in each match.
