sprite

noun
mythical small magical being
8.9
Pronunciation: / spraɪt / (AmE), / spraɪt / (BrE)
(in folklore) a small, playful being with magical powers, usually depicted as human in form.

Examples

A forest sprite
Children dressed as sprites for the village festival, with wings and sparkling costumes.
In the story, a mischievous sprite led the lost travelers out of the forest.
Legends say that sprites live among the flowers and help them bloom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - a small imaginary being of human form with magical powers
