Synonyms

    3.7 1/m
    frame of mind is an informal phrase for someone's temporary mental attitude, while state of mind names that general mental condition.
  • mood
    1.0 20/m
    mood is a short-lasting feeling shown in behavior, while state of mind covers both mood and a wider mental condition.
  • emotional state
    3.6 1/m
    emotional state describes a person's current feelings more strongly, while state of mind also includes thoughts and overall mental condition.
  • mental state
    4.0 2/m
    mental state refers to a person's thoughts and psychological health, often in a clinical or longer-term sense, while state of mind is broader.
  • psychological state
    6.3 0.07/m
    psychological state is a more technical term about mental processes and stability, while state of mind is a common, everyday phrase.
