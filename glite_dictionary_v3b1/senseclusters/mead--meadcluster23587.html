mead

noun
honey-based alcoholic drink
2.3
Pronunciation: / mid / (AmE), / miːd / (BrE)
An alcoholic drink made by fermenting honey with water, sometimes with added spices or fruit.

Examples

A glass of mead
Artisanal mead has become popular among craft beverage enthusiasts in recent years.
He poured himself a glass of mead to celebrate the festival.
The banquet featured roast meats and mead served in wooden cups.
