blindfold

noun
cloth that covers the eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈblaɪnˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈblaɪnˌfəʊld / (BrE)
A piece of cloth used to cover a person's eyes to prevent them from seeing.

Examples

A blindfold cloth
He removed the blindfold and opened his eyes to see the gift.
The child wore a blindfold during the party game.
They gently tied a blindfold around his eyes before leading him to the surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eye mask - a mask covering the eyes to block light or vision
