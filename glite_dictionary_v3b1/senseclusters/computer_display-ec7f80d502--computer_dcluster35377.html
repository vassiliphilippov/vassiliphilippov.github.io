computer display

noun
screen for viewing a computer's output
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjutər dɪˈspleɪ / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə dɪˈspleɪ / (BrE)
A screen that shows the visual output from a computer to the user.
