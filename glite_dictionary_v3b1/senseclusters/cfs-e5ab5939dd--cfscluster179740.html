CFS

noun
chronic fatigue illness
Pronunciation: / si ɛf ɛs / (AmE), / siː ɛf ɛs / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, a medical condition causing long-term, severe tiredness and other symptoms

Examples

CFS diagnosis
After years of unexplained exhaustion, she was finally diagnosed with CFS by her physician.
Many people with CFS struggle to maintain their daily routines due to constant fatigue.
Researchers are working to better understand the causes and treatment of CFS.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronic fatigue syndrome - a disorder marked by extreme, long-lasting tiredness
  • synonym: fibromyalgia - a medical condition causing widespread pain and fatigue
