transmedial

adjective
across media types
8.5
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmiːdiəl / (AmE), / trænzˈmiːdiəl / (BrE)
relating to or spanning across multiple forms or types of media

Examples

A transmedial story

The franchise became transmedial by expanding its universe into movies, comics, and podcasts.
