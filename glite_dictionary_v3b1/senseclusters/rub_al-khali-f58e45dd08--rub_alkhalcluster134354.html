Rub al-Khali

proper noun
desert in southern Saudi Arabia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌruːb æl ˈkɑːli / (AmE), / ˌruːb æl ˈkɑːli / (BrE)
a vast desert region in southern Saudi Arabia, known as the Empty Quarter, and one of the largest sand deserts in the world

Examples

The Rub al-Khali covers parts of Saudi Arabia, Oman, the UAE, and Yemen.
The Rub al-Khali is known for its towering dunes and harsh climate.
