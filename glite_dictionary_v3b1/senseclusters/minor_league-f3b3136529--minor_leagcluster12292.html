minor league

noun
sports league below the major league
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnɚ liɡ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnə liːɡ / (BrE)
a sports league made up of teams that are not part of the major league, especially in baseball

Examples

The minor league baseball

Many talented players spend several years in the minor league developing their skills.
