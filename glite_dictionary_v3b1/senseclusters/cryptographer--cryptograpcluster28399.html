cryptographer

noun
expert in codes and ciphers
Pronunciation: / krɪpˈtɑːɡrəfər / (AmE), / krɪpˈtɒɡrəfə / (BrE)
a person skilled in the analysis or creation of codes and ciphers

Examples

A cryptographer creates codes
Modern technology relies on the expertise of a cryptographer to secure online transactions.
She became a renowned cryptographer after designing an unbreakable code.
The cryptographer quickly deciphered the encoded message sent by the spies.
