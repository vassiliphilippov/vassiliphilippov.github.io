horsehair

noun
fabric from horse mane or tail
5.8 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…îrsÀåh…õr / (AmE), / Ààh…îÀêsh…õ…ô / (BrE)
A stiff fabric made from fibers from the mane or tail of horses, used especially in upholstery and clothing.

Examples

Horsehair fabric
During the 19th century, horsehair was commonly used in the making of mattresses and sofas.
She appreciated the durability of horsehair fabric in traditional furniture.
The antique chair was upholstered with horsehair, which gave it a distinctive texture.

Synonyms

  • haircloth
