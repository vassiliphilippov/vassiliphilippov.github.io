gone

adjective
stupefied by alcohol or drugs (informal)
1.4
Pronunciation: / ɡɑn / (AmE), / ɡɒn / (BrE)
(informal) deeply affected, stupefied, or uninhibited due to the effects of alcohol or drugs.

Examples

He was gone after drinks
After three drinks, he was completely gone and could barely keep his balance.
By midnight, most of the party guests were gone and singing loudly off-key.
She realized her friend was gone when he started declaring his love for everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intoxicated - affected by alcohol or drugs
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol or drugs
