manifolding

noun
multiplicity or variety
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪˌfoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmænɪˌfəʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
the state or quality of being many or having great variety; multiplicity or diversity
