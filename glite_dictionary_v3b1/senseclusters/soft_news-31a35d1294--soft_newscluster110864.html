soft news

noun
news focusing on entertainment or human-interest
5.7
Pronunciation: / sɔft nuːz / (AmE), / sɒft njuːz / (BrE)
News that focuses on entertaining or human-interest stories, rather than on serious topics or important events.

Examples

Soft news stories
Magazines often focus on soft news like celebrity interviews and lifestyle tips.
Many television programs today include segments of soft news to attract a wider audience.
The article was criticized for being soft news rather than covering important developments.

Related Terms
