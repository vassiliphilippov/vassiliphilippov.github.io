Contras

noun
Nicaraguan anti-Sandinista guerrilla group (1979–1990)
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.trəz/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.trəz/ (BrE)
The Nicaraguan counterrevolutionary guerrilla group active from 1979 to 1990, which opposed the Sandinista government and received support from the United States.

Examples

The Contras group
Debates about the legitimacy of the Contras remain a sensitive political issue in Nicaragua.
Several former Contras attempted to reintegrate into Nicaraguan society after the end of the war.
The United States secretly funded the Contras during the 1980s, leading to controversy at home and abroad.
