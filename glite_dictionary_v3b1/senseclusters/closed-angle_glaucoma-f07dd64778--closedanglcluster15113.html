a type of glaucoma in which the iris blocks the outflow of aqueous humor, causing a rapid increase in intraocular pressure

Examples

Closed-angle glaucoma attack
Closed-angle glaucoma usually requires urgent medical attention to prevent permanent vision loss.
If untreated, closed-angle glaucoma may lead to irreversible blindness in the affected eye.
Symptoms of closed-angle glaucoma can develop suddenly and include severe eye pain and blurred vision.

Synonyms

  • acute glaucoma
