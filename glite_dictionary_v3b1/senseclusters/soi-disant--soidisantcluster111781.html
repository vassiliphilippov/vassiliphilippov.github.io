soi-disant

Describing someone who claims or styles themselves as having a particular title, rank, or quality, often without justification or recognition by others.

Examples

His soi-disant credentials

Many doubted the soi-disant prince's claim to royalty.
The soi-disant expert offered bizarre advice that no reputable doctor would endorse.

Synonyms

    7.3 1/m

  • self-proclaimed
    5.8 2/m
    self-proclaimed means someone loudly declares a title without approval, while soi-disant often carries a critical or ironic tone.
