sidestick

noun
aircraft side control
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪd.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪd.stɪk/ (BrE)
a control stick or joystick positioned at the side of a seat, especially in an aircraft, used to operate or control movement

Examples

The sidestick control
The pilot used the sidestick to make smooth turns during the flight.
Modern airliners often feature a sidestick instead of a traditional yoke.

Modern gaming chairs sometimes include a sidestick for added control during simulations.
