tie

verb
make something by fastening pieces together
Pronunciation: / taɪ / (AmE), / taɪ / (BrE)
to fasten, join, or secure things together by making a knot or using string, rope, or similar material

Examples

To tie a knot
She carefully ties fishing flies by joining feathers and thread.

She learned how to tie a proper bow for her dress.
Before leaving, she tied her shoelaces tightly.
He quickly tied the packages together with a piece of string.
The boat was tied to the dock with a thick rope.
