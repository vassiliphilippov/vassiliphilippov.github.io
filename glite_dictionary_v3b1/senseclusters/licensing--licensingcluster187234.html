licensing

noun
granting permission
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪsənsɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of granting official permission or a license to do something

Examples

Driver licensing
The government is responsible for licensing new drivers before they can operate a vehicle.
Many businesses require licensing to legally sell certain products or services.
The process of licensing can take several months, depending on the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorization - the act of giving official permission
  • antonym: prohibition - the act of forbidding something by law or authority
