swingle

verb
to process flax fibers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɡəl / (BrE)
to beat or strike flax or hemp with a tool to separate and loosen the fibers for processing

Examples

To swingle flax fibers

The process to swingle flax is essential for making high-quality linen.
