Examples

Public disgrace

He was dismissed from the company in utter disgrace.

Synonyms

    9.3 0.31/m

  • opprobrium
    9.9 0.48/m

  • discredit
    6.3 0.21/m

  • disrepute
    7.6 0.46/m
