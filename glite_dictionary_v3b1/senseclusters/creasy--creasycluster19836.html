creasy

adjective
full of wrinkles
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːsi / (AmE), / ˈkriːsi / (BrE)
having many small wrinkles or folds, especially in fabric or paper

Examples

A creasy shirt
The letter was so creasy that it looked like it had been folded many times.
After a long flight, his shirt became creasy and needed to be ironed.
She tried to smooth out the creasy pages of the old book.
