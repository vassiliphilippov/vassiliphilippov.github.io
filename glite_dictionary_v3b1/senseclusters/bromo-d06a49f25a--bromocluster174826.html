Bromo

proper noun
Indonesian volcano
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊməʊ / (BrE)
an active volcano in East Java, Indonesia, famous for its crater and scenic sunrises

Examples

Bromo volcano
Many travelers visit Bromo to witness the spectacular sunrise over the volcanic landscape.
The view from the rim of Bromo is considered one of the most beautiful in Indonesia.
Local guides offer tours to Bromo for those wanting to explore the active volcano.
