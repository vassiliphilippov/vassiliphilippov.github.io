Gulf of Aqaba

proper noun
northeastern arm of the Red Sea
Pronunciation: / ˌɡʌlf əˈɑːkəbə / (AmE), / ˌɡʌlf əˈækəbə / (BrE)
a northeastern extension of the Red Sea located between the Sinai Peninsula and Saudi Arabia
