pallu

noun
sari end piece
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːluː /; / ˈpʌlu / (AmE), / ˈpʌluː /; / ˈpʌlu / (BrE)
the loose, decorative end of a sari, dupatta, or similar garment that is draped over the shoulder or head
