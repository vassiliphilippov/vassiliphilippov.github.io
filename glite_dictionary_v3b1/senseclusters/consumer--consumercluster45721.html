consumer

noun
person who uses goods or services
1.8 100/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàsuÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / k…ônÀàsjuÀêm…ô / (BrE)
a person who buys and uses goods or services, rather than producing them

Examples

Consumer rights
As a consumer, you should always read the fine print before making a purchase.
New government regulations are designed to protect the rights of the consumer.
The consumer demanded higher quality products from the company.

Synonyms

  • buyer
    1.0 28/m
    a buyer pays for goods or services in a transaction, while a consumer buys and then uses them rather than producing.
  • purchaser
    2.8 3.38/m
    a purchaser sounds formal and often appears in legal or business text, while a consumer is the everyday person who uses products.
  • customer
    1.0 133/m
    a customer buys from a specific shop or company and may be a repeat buyer, while a consumer emphasizes end use.
  • shopper
    1.0 8.94/m
    a shopper visits shops to look for and buy items, while a consumer is someone who actually uses goods or services.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services from a shop or business
  • antonym: producer - a person or company that makes or grows goods to be sold
