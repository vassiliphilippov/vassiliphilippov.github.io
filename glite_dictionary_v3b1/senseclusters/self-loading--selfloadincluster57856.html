self-loading

adjective
firearm that loads itself automatically
6.2 5.9
Pronunciation: /self ˈloʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /self ˈləʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
(of a firearm) capable of automatically loading the next round after firing, allowing for continuous fire until the magazine is empty.

Examples

Self-loading firearm
Collectors often seek self-loading firearms due to their historical significance.
Many modern pistols are self-loading, offering faster firing rates than older models.
The soldier carried a self-loading rifle into the field.
