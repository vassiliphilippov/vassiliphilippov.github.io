nurse

noun
person trained to care for sick or children
Pronunciation: / nɜrs / (AmE), / nɜːs / (BrE)
a person trained to care for the sick or children, especially under the supervision of a doctor

Examples

Hospital nurse
After his surgery, the nurse helped him with his medication and recovery exercises.
She trained as a nurse because she wanted to work with children in pediatric care.
The nurse checked on each patient every hour throughout the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caregiver - someone who provides care, especially for the sick or elderly
  • antonym: doctor - a person qualified to practice medicine; a physician
