Cervus elaphus canadensis

noun
North American elk subspecies
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrvəs ɪˈlæfəs kəˈnædɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvəs ɪˈlæfəs kəˈnædɛnsɪs / (BrE)
A large subspecies of elk native to North America, with males distinguished by large, branched antlers.
