water chute

noun
slide with water leading into a pool
7.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔː.tər ʃuːt/ /; / /ˈwɔː.tər ʃjuːt/ (AmE), /ˈwɔː.tə ʃuːt/ /; / /ˈwɔː.tə ʃjuːt/ (BrE)
a sloping slide or chute with flowing water, designed for people to slide down into a pool—especially as part of an amusement ride or water park attraction

Examples

A water chute slide
Every summer, the amusement park opens a new water chute for visitors to enjoy.
He lost his sunglasses at the top of the water chute but found them in the pool below.
The children squealed with excitement as they raced down the water chute into the pool.

Related Terms
