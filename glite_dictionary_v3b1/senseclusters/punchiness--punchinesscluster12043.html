punchiness

noun
forceful quality
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being forceful, lively, or having a strong impact

Examples

Punchiness in writing
The punchiness of the advertisement made it stand out from the competition.
Her writing style is known for its punchiness and directness.
The band's performance lacked the punchiness that fans expected from their live shows.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - lack of liveliness or impact
  • synonym: vigor - energy and enthusiasm
