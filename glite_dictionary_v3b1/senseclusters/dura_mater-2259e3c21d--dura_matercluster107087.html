dura mater

noun
outer tough membrane of brain and spinal cord
Pronunciation: / ˌdʊr.ə ˈmeɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌdjʊə.rə ˈmeɪ.tə / (BrE)
the tough, outermost membrane covering the brain and spinal cord, forming one of the three meninges

Examples

The dura mater membrane
An injury to the dura mater can result in serious complications such as infection or bleeding.
During the surgery, the doctor carefully exposed the dura mater to reach the brain.
The dura mater acts as a protective barrier for the central nervous system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arachnoid mater - the middle membrane of the three meninges
  • synonym: pia mater - the delicate innermost membrane of the meninges
