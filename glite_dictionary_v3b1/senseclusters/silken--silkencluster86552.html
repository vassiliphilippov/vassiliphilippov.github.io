silken

adjective
smooth and lustrous like silk
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlkən / (AmE), / ˈsɪlkən / (BrE)
Having the smooth, soft, and lustrous quality or appearance of silk.

Examples

Silken smooth texture

She wore a silken dress that shimmered under the lights.
The silken petals of the rose felt delicate to the touch.
