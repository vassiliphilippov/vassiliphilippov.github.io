clog up

verb
become blocked or obstructed
7.1 3.1
Pronunciation: /klɑːg ʌp/ /; / /klɔːg ʌp/ (AmE), /klɒg ʌp/ (BrE)
to become or cause to become blocked so that movement or flow is difficult or impossible
