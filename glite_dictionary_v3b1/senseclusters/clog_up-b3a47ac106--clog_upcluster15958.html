clog up

verb
become blocked or obstructed
Pronunciation: / klɑg ʌp / (AmE), / klɒg ʌp / (BrE)
to become or cause to become blocked so that movement or flow is difficult or impossible

Examples

To clog up a drain
Hair can clog up the shower drain if not removed regularly.
Leaves in the gutter will eventually clog up the drainage system.
The traffic tends to clog up the main street during rush hour.
Hair and soap can easily clog up the shower drain.
Leaves in the gutter may clog up your downspouts if not removed.
The printer tends to clog up if it isn't cleaned regularly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - to cause something to become obstructed
  • antonym: unblock - to remove an obstruction and allow movement or flow
  • antonym: unclog - to remove an obstruction or blockage
