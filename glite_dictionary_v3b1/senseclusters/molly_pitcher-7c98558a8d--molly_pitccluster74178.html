Molly Pitcher

proper noun
American Revolutionary War heroine
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːli ˈpɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈmɒli ˈpɪtʃə / (BrE)
a heroine of the American Revolution, noted for bringing water to soldiers and assisting artillerymen during the Battle of Monmouth.
