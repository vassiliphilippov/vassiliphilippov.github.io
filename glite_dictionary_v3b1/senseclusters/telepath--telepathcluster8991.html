telepath

noun
mind reader
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌpæθ / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪˌpæθ / (BrE)
a person who is able to read minds or communicate mentally without using the normal senses

Examples

A telepath person
The main character in the novel is a powerful telepath who can sense danger before it happens.
In science fiction, a telepath might communicate silently with others across great distances.
The secret organization recruited a telepath to help them uncover hidden information.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clairvoyant - a person who claims to see things beyond normal perception
  • synonym: psychic - a person who claims to have supernatural mental abilities
