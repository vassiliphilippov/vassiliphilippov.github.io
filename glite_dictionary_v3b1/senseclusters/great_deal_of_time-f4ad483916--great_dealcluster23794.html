great deal of time

noun
a large amount of time
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪt diːl əv taɪm / (AmE), / ɡreɪt diːl əv taɪm / (BrE)
a large or significant amount of time; a long period spent on something

Examples

A great deal of time spent
He devoted a great deal of time to training for the marathon.
Restoring the old house required a great deal of time and effort.
She spent a great deal of time preparing for the exam.

Related Terms

  • antonym: short time - a brief or limited amount of time
