small-town charm

noun
attractive qualities of a small community
Pronunciation: / smɔl taʊn tʃɑrm / (AmE), / smɔːl taʊn tʃɑːm / (BrE)
the attractive and pleasant qualities that are characteristic of a small community, such as friendliness, simplicity, and a close-knit atmosphere

Examples

Small-town charm and friendliness
Despite moving to the city, she missed the small-town charm she grew up with.
Many visitors are attracted to this village because of its small-town charm and welcoming residents.
The restaurant's cozy decor perfectly captures the small-town charm that makes the area so inviting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hospitality - friendly and generous reception of guests, often associated with small towns
  • antonym: urban anonymity - lack of personal connection typical in large cities, opposite of small-town charm
