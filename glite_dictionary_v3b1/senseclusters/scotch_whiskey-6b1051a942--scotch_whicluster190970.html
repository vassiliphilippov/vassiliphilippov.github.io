Scotch whiskey

noun
whiskey distilled in Scotland
Pronunciation: / skɑtʃ ˈwɪski / (AmE), / skɒtʃ ˈwɪski / (BrE)
Whiskey distilled in Scotland, especially that made from malted barley and often aged in oak barrels.
