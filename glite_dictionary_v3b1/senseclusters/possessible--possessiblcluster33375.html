possessible

adjective
able to be possessed
Pronunciation: / pəˈzɛsəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈzɛsəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being taken over, controlled, or inhabited, especially by a spirit or supernatural force

Examples

Possessible by spirits
The villagers believed the old mansion was possessible by ghosts after midnight.
Some cultures consider certain objects possessible by spirits if rituals are not performed.
The story described a possessible body that could be controlled by supernatural beings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inhabitable - able to be lived in or occupied
  • antonym: unpossessable - not able to be possessed or taken over
