inaugural

noun
opening ceremony
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɔɡjərəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəl / (BrE)
a ceremony or event that marks the official beginning of something, especially a new position or period in office

Examples

Presidential inaugural
Thousands of people gathered to witness the president's inaugural in the capital city.
The university held its first inaugural to celebrate the opening of the new campus.
She was honored to speak at the inaugural of the international conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commencement - a ceremony to mark the beginning of something
  • synonym: opening - the beginning or start of an event or activity
