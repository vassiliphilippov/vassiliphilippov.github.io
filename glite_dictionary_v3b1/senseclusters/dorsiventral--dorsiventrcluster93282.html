dorsiventral

adjective
distinct top and bottom
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdɔrsɪˈvɛntrəl / (AmE), / ˌdɔːsɪˈvɛntrəl / (BrE)
having distinct upper and lower surfaces, especially referring to leaves or animal bodies

Examples

Dorsiventral leaf surface
The dorsiventral structure of the leaf allows it to capture sunlight efficiently.
Many animals have a dorsiventral body plan, with a clear back and belly.
Botanists often describe leaves as dorsiventral when their upper and lower surfaces differ.
