minx

noun
flirtatious, seductive woman
6.8
Pronunciation: / mɪŋks / (AmE), / mɪŋks / (BrE)
a woman who is attractive and playfully flirtatious, often using her charm to manipulate or tease men

Examples

A minx woman
Don't let her innocent look fool you; she's quite the minx.
She played the minx at the party, charming everyone with her mischievous smile.
The actress often portrayed a minx on screen, delighting audiences with her wit and allure.
