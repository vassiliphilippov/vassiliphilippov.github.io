fellow

noun
informal form of address for a man
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛl.oʊ /; / ˈfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈfel.əʊ /; / ˈfɛləʊ / (BrE)
an informal or friendly way to refer to or address a man or boy

Examples

A nice fellow
Fellow, could you help me carry these boxes?
Excuse me, fellow, but I think you dropped your wallet.
Listen here, fellow, that's not the right way to do it.
He's a nice fellow and always helps his neighbors.
Several young fellows volunteered to carry the heavy boxes.
There was a fellow standing at the bus stop, waiting in the rain.
