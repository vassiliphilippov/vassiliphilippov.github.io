bent

adjective
curved or stooped (back or knees)
6.3
Pronunciation: / bɛnt / (AmE), / bɛnt / (BrE)
curved or not straight in shape, often due to pressure, damage, or bending

Examples

Bent metal bar
He knelt on the ground with bent knees, tying his shoelace.
She walked with a bent back after years of hard labor.
The elderly man rose slowly, straightening his bent posture with effort.
After the accident, the bicycle's front wheel was badly bent.
She tried to fix the bent picture frame, but it would not straighten.
The metal rod became bent when too much pressure was applied.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curved - having a smoothly bending shape without angles
  • antonym: straight - not bent, having no curve or angle, upright
  • degree higher: twisted - forced out of shape by being turned or spun around
