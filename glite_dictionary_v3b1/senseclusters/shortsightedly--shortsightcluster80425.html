shortsightedly

adverb
without long-term thinking
6.9
Pronunciation: /ʃɔːr.ˈsaɪ.tɪd.liː/ /; / /ʃɑːr.ˈsaɪ.tɪd.liː/ (AmE), /ʃɔːt.ˈsaɪ.tɪd.liː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not consider the future or long-term consequences; lacking foresight

Examples

Act shortsightedly now
The company shortsightedly cut research funding, which hurt its innovation in the long run.
He shortsightedly ignored the warnings about climate change, focusing only on immediate profits.
Many people shortsightedly spend all their money without saving for emergencies.
He invested shortsightedly, ignoring the risks that could appear in the next few years.
The company acted shortsightedly by cutting research funding to boost short-term profits.
She shortsightedly refused to save money for her retirement, focusing only on immediate needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: farsightedly - in a way that shows the ability to anticipate future needs or problems
  • synonym: myopically - in a way that lacks foresight or long-term planning
  • antonym: prudently - in a way that shows careful and wise judgment for the future
  • synonym: recklessly - in a way that ignores possible dangers or consequences
