clammer

noun
noisy protester
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmər / (AmE), / ˈklæmə / (BrE)
a person who makes loud noises or demands something insistently, especially in protest

Examples

A noisy clammer
The clammer at the meeting refused to let the speaker continue without addressing his concerns.
Several clammers gathered outside the building, demanding immediate action from the officials.
During the debate, a clammer shouted questions from the back of the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heckler - a person who interrupts or shouts at a speaker
  • synonym: protester - a person who publicly expresses strong objection
  • antonym: supporter - a person who supports or defends a cause
