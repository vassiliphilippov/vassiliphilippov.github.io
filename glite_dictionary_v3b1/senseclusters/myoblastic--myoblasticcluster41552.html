myoblastic

adjective
of muscle precursor cells
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪoʊˈblæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌmaɪəʊˈblæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of myoblasts, the cells that develop into muscle tissue

Examples

Myoblastic muscle cells
The myoblastic stage is crucial for proper muscle development in embryos.
Researchers studied the myoblastic response to injury in laboratory mice.
Certain diseases can disrupt normal myoblastic activity, affecting muscle growth.
