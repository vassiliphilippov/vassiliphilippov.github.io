waveguide

noun
hollow conductor guiding microwaves
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪvˌɡaɪd / (AmE), / ˈweɪvˌɡaɪd /; / ˈweɪvɡʌɪd / (BrE)
a hollow tube or structure that directs and confines electromagnetic waves, such as microwaves or radio waves
