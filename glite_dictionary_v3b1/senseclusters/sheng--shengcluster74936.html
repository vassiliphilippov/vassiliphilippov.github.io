sheng

noun
Chinese reed instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃʌŋ / (AmE), / ʃɛŋ / (BrE)
a Chinese mouth-blown free reed musical instrument with vertical pipes, used in traditional and modern music

Examples

Play the sheng

She learned to play the sheng to connect with her cultural heritage.
The unique sound of the sheng added depth to the ensemble's performance.
