wakey

adjective
awake and alert
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪki / (AmE), / ˈweɪki / (BrE)
awake, alert, and ready to be active, especially after sleeping or resting

Examples

Feel wakey after coffee
I need a strong coffee to feel wakey in the morning.
After a quick shower, she was much more wakey and ready for work.
He never feels wakey until he gets some fresh air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awake - not asleep; conscious and alert
  • antonym: sleepy - feeling ready to sleep; drowsy
