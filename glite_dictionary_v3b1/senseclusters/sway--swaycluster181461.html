Synonyms

  • swing
    1.0 30/m
    swing moves back and forth while hanging or attached, while sway moves slowly side to side without needing a fixed point.
  • oscillate
    6.5 1/m
    oscillate moves back and forth in a regular rhythm, while sway is a slower, more natural side-to-side motion.
  • rock
    1.0 15/m
    rock moves gently back and forth and often comforts someone, while sway describes a slow, rhythmic side-to-side movement.
  • rocking
    1.9 0.00/m
    rocking is a gentle repeated motion used to calm, while sway is a slow side-to-side movement of whole objects.
  • waver
    4.8 2/m
    waver moves or shakes in an unsteady, trembling way, while sway is a smoother, rhythmic side-to-side motion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rock - to move (something or someone) gently back and forth or from side to side
  • antonym: steady - to make or become stable and not move back and forth
  • synonym: swing - to move back and forth or from side to side while suspended or as if suspended
