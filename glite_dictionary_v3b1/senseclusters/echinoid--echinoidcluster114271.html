echinoid

noun
spiny marine animal
9.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkaɪnɔɪd / (AmE), / ɪˈkiːnɔɪd / (BrE)
a marine animal with a spiny shell, belonging to the class Echinoidea, such as a sea urchin or sand dollar

Examples

A living echinoid
The rocky tide pools were full of colorful echinoids clinging to the stones.
Scientists studied the fossilized echinoids to learn about ancient marine environments.
A diver carefully observed a group of echinoids moving slowly across the ocean floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sand dollar - a flat, burrowing echinoid with a hard shell
  • synonym: sea urchin - a small, round marine animal with spines
