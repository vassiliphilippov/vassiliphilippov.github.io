onanistic

adjective
relating to coitus interruptus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊnəˈnɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌəʊnəˈnɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to coitus interruptus or the act of deliberately spilling or wasting semen, especially in reference to the biblical Onan.

Examples

Onanistic practices
The old medical text used the term onanistic to describe certain contraceptive practices.
Some religious discussions refer to onanistic acts as morally questionable.
The physician's notes included an onanistic reference that is rarely seen in modern writing.

Related Terms
