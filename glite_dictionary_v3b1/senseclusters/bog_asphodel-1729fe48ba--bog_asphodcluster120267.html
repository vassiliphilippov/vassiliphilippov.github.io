bog asphodel

noun
herbaceous bog plant with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / bɑɡ ˈæs.foʊ.dəl / (AmE), / bɒɡ ˈæs.fə.dəl / (BrE)
A herbaceous plant found in boggy areas of northern temperate regions, with yellow flowers and grasslike leaves.

Botanists monitored the growth of bog asphodel to track changes in the bog’s ecosystem.
