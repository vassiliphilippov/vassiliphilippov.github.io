deep-fat-fry

verb
cook by immersing in fat or oil
Pronunciation: / ˈdip ˌfæt ˈfraɪ / (AmE), / ˈdiːp ˌfæt ˈfraɪ / (BrE)
to cook food by immersing it completely in hot fat or oil

Examples

To deep-fat-fry chicken
Modern kitchens often deep-fat-fry chicken to achieve a crisp coating.
Professional chefs know exactly how long to deep-fat-fry potatoes for perfect fries.
She had never deep-fat-fried anything before trying to make tempura at home.
