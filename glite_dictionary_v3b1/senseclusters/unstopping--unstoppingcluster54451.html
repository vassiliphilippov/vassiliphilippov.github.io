unstopping

adjective
unceasing; constant
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɑpɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɒpɪŋ / (BrE)
continuing without stopping; unceasing or constant

Examples

Unstopping rain
The unstopping noise from the construction site made it hard to concentrate.
She admired the unstopping flow of the river as it wound through the valley.
Despite the unstopping rain, the festival continued as planned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceaseless - never ending or stopping
  • synonym: continuous - happening without interruption
  • antonym: intermittent - stopping and starting at intervals
