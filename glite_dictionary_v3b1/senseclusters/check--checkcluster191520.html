check

noun
inspection; appraisal
Pronunciation: / tʃɛk / (AmE), / tʃɛk / (BrE)
an act of examining or verifying something to ensure accuracy, safety, or quality

Examples

A safety check
The mechanic performed a routine check of the car's brakes before the race.
There will be a security check before entering the building.
You should do a quick check of your facts before presenting.
All emergency exits underwent a routine check before the concert began.
She did a final check of her assignment to make sure there were no mistakes.
The inspector performed a check on the restaurant’s cleanliness and hygiene standards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examination - a detailed inspection or investigation
  • synonym: inspection - the act of examining something closely
  • antonym: neglect - the lack of care or attention
  • synonym: review - a formal assessment or examination of something
