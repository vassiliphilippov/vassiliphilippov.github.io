baby buggy

noun
four-wheeled baby or child carriage
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌbʌɡ.i / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌbʌɡ.i / (BrE)
a small vehicle with four wheels designed to carry a baby or young child while being pushed by an adult

Examples

Push a baby buggy
He struggled to push the baby buggy up the steep hill.
She took her newborn out for a walk in the baby buggy every morning.
The store had a wide selection of baby buggies in different colors and designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pram - a British term for a baby carriage
  • synonym: stroller - a pushchair for transporting a baby or young child
