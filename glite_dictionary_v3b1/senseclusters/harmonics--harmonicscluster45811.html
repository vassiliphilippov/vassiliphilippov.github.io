harmonics

noun
the study of musical sound and overtones
2.0
Pronunciation: / hɑrˈmɑnɪks / (AmE), / hɑːˈmɒnɪks / (BrE)
the branch of music or physics concerned with the study of musical sounds, especially their overtones and structure
