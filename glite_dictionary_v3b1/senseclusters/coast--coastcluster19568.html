Synonyms

  • seacoast
    1.0 0.50/m
    seacoast is the land along a sea or ocean, while coast is the common word for the same shoreline area.
  • seashore
    1.0 1.94/m
    seashore is the sandy edge people walk on by the sea, while coast is a more general shore area.
  • shore
    1.0 33/m
    shore is the land beside any large water like a lake or sea, while coast refers specifically to the sea or ocean edge.
  • coastland
    3.4 0.04/m
    coastland is a larger area of land near the sea, often named on maps, while coast names the shoreline itself.
  • seaside
    1.1 2.67/m
    seaside is the area by the sea where people go for holidays, while coast is the simple geographical shore.
  • seaboard
    4.8 1.94/m
    seaboard is the coastal part of a country or region, often used in formal talk, while coast is the local shoreline.
