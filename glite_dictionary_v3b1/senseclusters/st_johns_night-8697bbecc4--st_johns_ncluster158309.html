St John's Night

noun
night before Midsummer Day; festival night
Pronunciation: / seɪnt ˈdʒɑnz ˌnaɪt / (AmE), / sənt ˈdʒɒnz ˌnaɪt / (BrE)
The night before Midsummer Day, traditionally celebrated with various customs and festivities in some countries.

Examples

St John's Night celebration
In many countries, St John's Night is associated with ancient rituals and the celebration of midsummer.
On St John's Night, the whole village gathered to light bonfires and celebrate together.
Traditional songs and dances marked the festivities on St John's Night.
