zona fasciculata

noun
adrenal cortex layer
Pronunciation: / ˈzoʊnə fəˌsɪkjəˈleɪtə / (AmE), / ˈzəʊnə fəˌsɪkjʊˈlɑːtə / (BrE)
the middle layer of the adrenal cortex that produces hormones such as cortisol

Examples

The zona fasciculata layer
The zona fasciculata is responsible for producing cortisol in the body.
Damage to the zona fasciculata can affect hormone levels and stress response.
Doctors studied the zona fasciculata to understand its role in adrenal disorders.

Related Terms

  • antonym: zona glomerulosa - the outer layer of the adrenal cortex
  • antonym: zona reticularis - the inner layer of the adrenal cortex
