rhizomatic

adjective
non-hierarchical structure
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪzəˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌraɪzəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a rhizome, especially describing a structure with many connections and no clear hierarchy

Examples

Rhizomatic structure
The organization adopted a rhizomatic approach, allowing ideas to spread in all directions.
Her research explores rhizomatic learning, where knowledge grows through many interconnected paths.
Social media platforms often develop in a rhizomatic way, without a single central authority.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hierarchical - arranged in a ranked or ordered structure
  • synonym: networked - connected in a network with many links
