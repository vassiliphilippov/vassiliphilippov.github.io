hunting lodge

noun
small rustic shelter used by hunters
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌn.tɪŋ lɑːdʒ / (AmE), / ˈhʌn.tɪŋ lɒdʒ / (BrE)
a small, rustic house or cabin used as a temporary shelter by people hunting in the countryside

Examples

A hunting lodge cabin
The king entertained his guests in a lavish hunting lodge during the autumn season.
The old hunting lodge was nestled deep in the forest, far from the nearest village.
They spent the weekend at a hunting lodge by the lake, enjoying the peace and quiet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabin - a small simple house or shelter, usually in a wild or remote area
