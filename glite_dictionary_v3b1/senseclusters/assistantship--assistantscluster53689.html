assistantship

noun
student paid position
5.9
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt.ʃɪp/ (AmE), /ə.ˈsɪs.tənt.ʃɪp/ (BrE)
a paid position for a student, usually at a university, to assist with teaching or research, often to gain experience or training
