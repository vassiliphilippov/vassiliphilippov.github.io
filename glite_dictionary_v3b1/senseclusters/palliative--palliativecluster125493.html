palliative

adjective
relieving pain or symptoms
6.8 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈpæl.ɪ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈpæl.iː.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪ.eɪ.tɪv/ (AmE), /ˈpæl.ɪ.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈpæl.iː.ə.tɪv/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪ.eɪ.tɪv/ (BrE)
Relieving pain or alleviating a problem without dealing with the underlying cause.

Examples

Palliative care
He found the medication to be only palliative, offering some relief but not a cure.
Hospice care often focuses on providing palliative support for terminally ill patients.
The doctor recommended a palliative treatment to help manage her chronic pain.
