Relieving pain or alleviating a problem without dealing with the underlying cause.

Examples

Palliative care
He found the medication to be only palliative, offering some relief but not a cure.
Hospice care often focuses on providing palliative support for terminally ill patients.
The doctor recommended a palliative treatment to help manage her chronic pain.

Synonyms

  • alleviative
    7.7 0.00/m

  • lenitive
    9.9 0.00/m
    lenitive soothes pain or distress in a gentle, often old-fashioned way, while palliative refers to symptom relief without cure.
  • mitigative
    8.0 0.01/m
    mitigative reduces the severity or harmful effects of a problem, while palliative focuses on easing symptoms rather than fixing the cause.
