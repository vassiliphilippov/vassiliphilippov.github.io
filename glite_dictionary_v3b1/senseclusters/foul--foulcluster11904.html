foul

adjective
manuscript marked by many corrections
6.1
Pronunciation: / faʊl / (AmE), / faʊl / (BrE)
(of a manuscript or copy) defaced or marked with numerous changes, corrections, or erasures; not in a clean or final state.

Examples

Foul draft
Mistakes and scribbles covered the entire foul draft of the manuscript.
She spent hours revising her foul copy before producing a final version.
The editor asked the author to submit a foul copy alongside the clean one.
