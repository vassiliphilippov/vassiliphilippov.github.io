foul

adjective
manuscript marked by many corrections
8.1 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / faÊŠl / (AmE), / faÊŠl / (BrE)
(of a manuscript or copy) defaced or marked with numerous changes, corrections, or erasures; not in a clean or final state.

Examples
