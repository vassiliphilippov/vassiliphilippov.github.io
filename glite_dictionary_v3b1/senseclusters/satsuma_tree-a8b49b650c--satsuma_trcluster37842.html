satsuma tree

noun
tree bearing satsuma mandarin oranges
9.9
Pronunciation: / sætˈsuːmə triː / (AmE), / sætˈsuːmə triː / (BrE)
A small citrus tree that produces satsuma oranges, a seedless and easy-to-peel variety of mandarin.

Examples

A satsuma tree fruit
After years of care, their satsuma tree reached maturity and began yielding fruit in time for Christmas.
He planted a satsuma tree because it thrives in colder climates than most citrus.
The satsuma tree in her garden produced dozens of sweet, seedless oranges each winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mandarin - a small, sweet citrus fruit; the general type including satsumas
  • synonym: orange tree - the tree that produces oranges, a related citrus species
