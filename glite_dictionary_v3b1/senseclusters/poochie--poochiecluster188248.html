poochie

noun
toy dog for children
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpuːtʃi / (BrE)
a child's toy dog or stuffed animal that looks like a small dog

Examples

A plush poochie
Every night, the little girl cuddled her favorite poochie before going to sleep.
He brought his old poochie to school for show-and-tell.
The store had a shelf full of colorful poochies in different sizes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plush toy - a soft toy made of fabric and stuffing
  • synonym: stuffed animal - a soft toy filled with material, often shaped like an animal
