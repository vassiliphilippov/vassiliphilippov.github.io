improvise

verb
perform or create without preparation
Pronunciation: / ˈɪm.prəˌvaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɪm.prəˌvaɪz /; / ˈɪm.prə.vaɪz / (BrE)
to do, make, or perform something without preparation, often using whatever is available
