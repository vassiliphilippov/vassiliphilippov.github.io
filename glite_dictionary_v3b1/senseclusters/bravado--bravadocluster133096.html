bravado

noun
swaggering display of boldness
4.4 6.2
Pronunciation: /brə.ˈvɑː.doʊ/ /; / /brɑː.ˈvɑː.doʊ/ (AmE), /brə.ˈvɑː.dəʊ/ /; / /brɑː.ˈvɑː.dəʊ/ (BrE)
a confident or showy manner that is intended to impress or intimidate, often masking fear or insecurity

Examples

False bravado
His speech was full of bravado, but it failed to inspire real confidence.
The young man's bravado masked his nervousness before the competition.
Their bravado vanished as soon as the danger became real.
