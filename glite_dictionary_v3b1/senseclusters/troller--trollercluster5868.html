troller

noun
fisherman using hook and line for trolling
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊlə / (BrE)
a fisherman who catches fish with a moving hook and line, typically by trolling

Examples

A troller fisherman
As a seasoned troller, she knew the best spots to find trout in the lake.
Each troller aboard the boat had a specific line to monitor throughout the trip.
The experienced troller set out before dawn to catch salmon in the open sea.
