tank truck

noun
vehicle carrying liquids or gases on roads
1.9
Pronunciation: / tæŋk trʌk / (AmE), / tæŋk trʌk / (BrE)
a large motor vehicle with a tank for carrying liquids, gases, or bulk materials by road

Examples

A tank truck carrying fuel
A chemical spill occurred when a tank truck overturned on the highway.
Drinking water was distributed to the affected area using a tank truck after the storm.
The tank truck delivered thousands of gallons of fuel to the service station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lorry - a large motor vehicle for transporting goods, especially in Britain
  • synonym: tanker - a ship, airplane, or vehicle for carrying liquids in bulk
  • synonym: truck - a motor vehicle for carrying goods; especially common in American English
