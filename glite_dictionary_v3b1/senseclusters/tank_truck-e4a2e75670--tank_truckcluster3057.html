tank truck

noun
vehicle carrying liquids or gases on roads
1.9 2.8
Pronunciation: /tæŋk trʌk/ /; / /teɪŋk trʌk/ (AmE), /tæŋk trʌk/ /; / /teɪŋk trʌk/ (BrE)
