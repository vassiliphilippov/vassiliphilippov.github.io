couch

verb
to express in a particular way
Pronunciation: / kaʊtʃ / (AmE), / kaʊtʃ / (BrE)
to express something in a particular way; to phrase or word (especially carefully or deliberately)

Examples

To couch a message
His offer was couched in terms that suggested he was doing us a favor.
She tried to couch her criticism as diplomatically as possible.
The report was couched in very formal language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phrase - to express in particular words or language
  • synonym: word - to choose or express using specific words
