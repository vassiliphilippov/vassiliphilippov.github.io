tree-shaped

adjective
resembling the shape or branching of a tree
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtriˌʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈtriːˌʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a form or structure resembling that of a tree, especially with a main trunk and branches spreading out.

Examples

A tree-shaped chandelier
A tree-shaped chandelier hung in the center of the hall, illuminating the entire room.
Holiday cookies in a tree-shaped design are popular during the festive season.
The scientist marveled at the tree-shaped coral formations on the ocean floor.
