ethnic

noun
member of an ethnic group
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθ.nɪk / (AmE), / ˈɛθ.nɪk / (BrE)
a person who belongs to a particular ethnic group, especially as a minority within a larger society

Examples

An ethnic minority
In the city's census, the number of ethnics from diverse backgrounds has steadily increased.
Some policies are designed to promote the inclusion of ethnic minorities in education and employment.
The club aimed to provide support for young ethnics adjusting to life in a new country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minority - a group that is smaller in number within a larger society
