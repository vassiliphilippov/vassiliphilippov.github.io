composite

adjective
made up of interconnected parts
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɑːzɪt / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒzɪt / (BrE)
made up of separate interconnected parts or elements.

Examples

Composite image
The artist created a composite image using elements from various photographs.
The bridge was constructed from composite materials to provide extra strength and durability.
Their proposal was actually a composite of several different plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compound - something composed of two or more separate elements
  • antonym: simple - not composed of separate parts; single or basic
