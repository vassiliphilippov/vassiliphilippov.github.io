Shi'ite

noun
follower of Shia branch of Islam
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʃiˈaɪt / (AmE), / ʃiˈaɪt / (BrE)
a member of the branch of Islam that regards Ali as the legitimate successor to Muhammad and rejects the first three caliphs

Examples

Shi'ite leader
Shi'ite leaders often play an influential role in the country's politics.
As a Shi'ite, he commemorates Ashura with a deep sense of devotion.
The city has a significant Shi'ite population that observes distinct religious practices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Shia - the branch of Islam that Shi'ites follow
  • antonym: Sunni - a member of the largest branch of Islam, which accepts the first four caliphs
