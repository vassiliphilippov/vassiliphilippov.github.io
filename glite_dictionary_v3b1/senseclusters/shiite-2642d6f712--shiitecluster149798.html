Shi'ite

noun
follower of Shia branch of Islam
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈʃiː.aɪt/ /; / /ʃiː.ˈaɪt/ (AmE), /ˈʃiː.aɪt/ /; / /ʃiː.ˈaɪt/ (BrE)
