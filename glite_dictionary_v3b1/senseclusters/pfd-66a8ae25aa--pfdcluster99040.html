PFD

noun
process flow diagram
8.6 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / pi …õf di / (AmE), / piÀê …õf diÀê / (BrE)

Examples

PFD diagram

Before starting the project, the team carefully analyzed the PFD for potential safety issues.
A clear PFD helps everyone understand how materials move through the system.
