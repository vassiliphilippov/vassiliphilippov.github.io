PFD

noun
process flow diagram
8.6
Pronunciation: / pi ɛf di / (AmE), / piː ɛf diː / (BrE)
a diagram that shows the sequence and flow of processes, equipment, or materials in an industrial or chemical system

Examples

PFD diagram
The engineers used a PFD to plan the layout of the new chemical plant.
Before starting the project, the team carefully analyzed the PFD for potential safety issues.
A clear PFD helps everyone understand how materials move through the system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: P&ID - a piping and instrumentation diagram used in engineering
  • synonym: flowchart - a diagram that shows the steps in a process
