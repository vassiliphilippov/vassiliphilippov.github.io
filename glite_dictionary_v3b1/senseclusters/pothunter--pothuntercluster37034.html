pothunter

noun
someone who enters contests to win trophies
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːthʌntər / (AmE), / ˈpɒthʌntə / (BrE)
A person who enters competitions or contests primarily to win trophies or prizes, rather than for enjoyment or genuine competition.

Examples

Prize-winning pothunter
As a well-known pothunter, he joins every major cooking contest in the city.
Some people accuse her of being a pothunter, but she insists she truly loves competing.
The club discourages pothunters who care more about the prize than the spirit of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trophy hunter - a person who participates to collect trophies or prizes
