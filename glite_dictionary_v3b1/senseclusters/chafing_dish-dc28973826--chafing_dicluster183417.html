chafing dish

noun
pan for keeping food warm at table
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪfɪŋ dɪʃ / (BrE)
A metal pan mounted over a small heater, used for cooking or keeping food warm at the table.

Examples

A chafing dish heater
At the buffet, several chafing dishes held a variety of hot entrees.
Eggs Benedict is often prepared tableside using a chafing dish.
