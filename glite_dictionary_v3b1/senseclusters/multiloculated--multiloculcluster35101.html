multiloculated

adjective
having many compartments
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈlɑːkjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈlɒkjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
divided into many small compartments or cavities, especially in reference to cysts or other structures in the body

Examples

A multiloculated cyst
The doctor explained that the tumor was multiloculated, making it more complex to treat.
A multiloculated abscess may require different management than a simple one.
Imaging showed a multiloculated mass with several distinct chambers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unilocular - having a single compartment or cavity
