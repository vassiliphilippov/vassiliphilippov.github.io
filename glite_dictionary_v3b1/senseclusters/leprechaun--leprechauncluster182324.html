leprechaun

noun
Irish folklore elf
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛprəˌkɔn / (AmE), / ˈlɛprəkɔːn / (BrE)
(in Irish folklore) a small, mischievous elf believed to have a hidden pot of gold.

Examples

A leprechaun hides gold
As the story goes, a leprechaun hides his pot of gold at the end of the rainbow.
Many believe that catching a leprechaun can bring you good luck.
The children dressed up as a leprechaun for St. Patrick’s Day.
