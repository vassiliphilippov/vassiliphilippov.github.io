euphoric

adjective
feeling intense happiness or excitement
Pronunciation: / juˈfɔrɪk / (AmE), / juˈfɒrɪk / (BrE)
feeling or expressing intense excitement and happiness; marked by a strong sense of well-being or elation

Examples

Feeling euphoric joy
She was euphoric when she received the news of her promotion.
The patient's euphoric mood was noted by the psychiatrist.
The team felt euphoric after their unexpected victory in the final.

Related Terms

  • antonym: depressed - feeling very sad; opposite of euphoric
  • synonym: elated - very happy and excited
