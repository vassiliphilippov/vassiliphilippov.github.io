valveless

adjective
without valves
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvælv.ləs / (AmE), / ˈvælv.ləs / (BrE)
lacking a valve or valves, especially in a biological or mechanical context

Examples

A valveless heart
A valveless heart cannot control blood flow properly.
The engineer designed a valveless pump for the new irrigation system.
Some veins in the body are naturally valveless, allowing blood to flow freely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: valved - having one or more valves
