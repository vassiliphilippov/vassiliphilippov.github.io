receptiveness

noun
willingness to receive ideas or impressions
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛptɪvnəs / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛptɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing or ready to receive new ideas, impressions, or suggestions

Examples

Open receptiveness to ideas
A classroom environment that encourages receptiveness can inspire creative thinking.
His receptiveness to new experiences made him an excellent traveler.
The manager praised her receptiveness to feedback from the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: openness - the quality of being open to new ideas or experiences
  • synonym: receptivity - the quality of being receptive; responsiveness to ideas or impressions
  • antonym: resistance - refusal to accept or be affected by ideas or changes
