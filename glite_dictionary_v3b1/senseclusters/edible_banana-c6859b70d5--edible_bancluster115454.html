edible banana

noun
banana grown for food
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdəbl bəˈnænə / (AmE), / ˈɛdɪbl bəˈnɑːnə / (BrE)
A type of banana cultivated for its sweet, yellow fruit, commonly eaten as food.

Examples

A bunch of edible bananas
She bought a bunch of edible bananas at the market to make a fruit salad.
The edible banana is a staple fruit in many tropical countries.
Unlike the wild varieties, the edible banana has a soft, sweet flesh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plantain - starchy, less sweet banana often cooked before eating
