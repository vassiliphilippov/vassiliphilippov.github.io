grindstone

noun
revolving stone for sharpening tools
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndstəʊn / (BrE)
a thick, circular stone that revolves to grind, sharpen, or polish tools by friction

Examples

A grindstone wheel
She pressed the blade carefully against the grindstone until it was razor-sharp.
The blacksmith used a grindstone to sharpen his knives and axes.
Years of turning the grindstone had worn it smooth in the middle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whetstone - a flat stone used for sharpening cutting tools by hand
