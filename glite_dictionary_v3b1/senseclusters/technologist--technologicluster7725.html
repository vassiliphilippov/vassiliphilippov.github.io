technologist

noun
a person skilled in applied science or technology
2.2 6.0
Pronunciation: / tɛkˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / tɛkˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who applies scientific knowledge to solve practical or technical problems, often in industry or applied science

Examples

Medical technologist
A medical laboratory technologist analyzes patient samples to assist doctors in making a diagnosis.
After graduating from university, she became a technologist specializing in renewable energy systems.
The company hired a technologist to streamline their manufacturing process and improve efficiency.
