bundler

noun
political fundraiser
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌn.də.lər/ (AmE), /ˈbʌn.də.lə/ (BrE)
a person who collects political donations from multiple individuals and delivers them together to a campaign or candidate

Examples

Political bundler
The campaign relied on a skilled bundler to gather contributions from local business leaders.
As a prominent bundler, he helped raise significant funds for the candidate's election effort.
Many political parties seek out a bundler to maximize their fundraising potential.
