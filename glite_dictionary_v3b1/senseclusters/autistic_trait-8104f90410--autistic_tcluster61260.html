autistic trait

noun
characteristic associated with autism
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɔˈtɪstɪk treɪt / (AmE), / ɔːˈtɪstɪk treɪt / (BrE)
a characteristic or behavior often associated with autism spectrum disorder, such as social communication differences or repetitive actions

Examples

Autistic traits in behavior
His autistic traits became more noticeable during group activities at school.
People with autistic traits often excel in tasks that require attention to detail.
She displays several autistic traits, such as sensitivity to loud noises and a preference for routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autism spectrum disorder - a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication
