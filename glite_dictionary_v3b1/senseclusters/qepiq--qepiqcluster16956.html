qepiq

noun
Azerbaijani monetary subunit
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛpɪk / (AmE), / ˈkɛpɪk / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Azerbaijan, equal to one hundredth of a manat.

Examples

One qepiq
One qepiq is worth one hundredth of an Azerbaijani manat.
She had several qepiq coins left after her trip to Baku.
The price of the newspaper was fifty qepiq in the capital city.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: manat - the basic unit of currency in Azerbaijan
