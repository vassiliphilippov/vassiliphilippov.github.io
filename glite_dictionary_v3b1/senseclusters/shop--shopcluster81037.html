shop

noun
small workplace for making or repairing things
3.6
Pronunciation: / ʃɑp / (AmE), / ʃɒp / (BrE)
a small building or room where things are manufactured or repaired, especially by hand

Examples

You could smell the scent of fresh paint drifting from the artist's shop.
