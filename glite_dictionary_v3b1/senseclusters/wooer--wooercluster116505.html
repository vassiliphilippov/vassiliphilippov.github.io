wooer

noun
man who courts a woman
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːər / (AmE), / ˈwuːə / (BrE)
a man who courts or seeks the affection of a woman, typically with the intention of marriage

Examples

A wooer seeks love
In the old folktale, the wooer must solve three riddles to win the princess's heart.
She was courted by many a hopeful wooer at the royal ball.
The story told of a wooer who serenaded his beloved each evening beneath her window.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lover - a person in a romantic relationship
  • synonym: suitor - a man who pursues a woman for marriage
