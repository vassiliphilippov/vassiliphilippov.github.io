fogproof

adjective
prevents fogging
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent the formation of fog on a surface, especially on optical devices

Examples

Fogproof glasses
The fogproof coating on these glasses keeps your vision clear in humid weather.
He bought a fogproof camera to use during his mountain hikes.
These fogproof goggles are perfect for swimming in cold water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: foggy - full of or covered with fog
  • synonym: waterproof - designed to prevent water from entering or affecting something
