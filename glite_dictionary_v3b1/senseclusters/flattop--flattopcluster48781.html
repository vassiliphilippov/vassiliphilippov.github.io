flattop

noun
aircraft carrier warship
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈflætˌtɒp / (BrE)
a large warship with a long flat deck for planes to take off and land on; an aircraft carrier

Examples

A navy flattop
During the ceremony, several fighter jets took off from the flattop.
The aging flattop was finally retired after decades of service.
The navy deployed a flattop to the region as a show of force.
