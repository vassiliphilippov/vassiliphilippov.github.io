notional

adjective
imaginary; not based in fact
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊʃənl / (AmE), / ˈnəʊʃənl /; / ˈnəʊʃənəl / (BrE)
existing only in the mind, imagination, or as an idea; not real, theoretical, or speculative
