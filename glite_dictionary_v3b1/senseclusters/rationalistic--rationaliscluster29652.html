rationalistic

adjective
relating to rationalism or reason-based thinking
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˌræʃ.ə.nəˈlɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌræʃ.ə.nəˈlɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or based on the philosophical doctrine of rationalism, which emphasizes reason as the chief source of knowledge.

Examples

A rationalistic approach
His arguments were rooted in a rationalistic approach to philosophy.
Some critics find modern architecture too rationalistic and lacking emotional appeal.
The scientist preferred a rationalistic explanation over supernatural ones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable; lacking reason
  • synonym: rationalist - a person who believes in or supports rationalism
