Synonyms

  • armed forces
    2.3 14/m
    armed forces names a nation's combined military branches in common use, while armed services is a slightly more formal official term for the same groups.
  • military
    2.7 75/m
    military refers to the armed forces as an organized institution, while armed services names the country's service branches more formally.
  • defense force
    3.8 0.50/m
    defense force is usually a specific group focused on protection, while armed services means the country's full military branches together.
