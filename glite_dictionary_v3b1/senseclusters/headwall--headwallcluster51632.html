headwall

noun
steep valley cliff
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌwɔːl / (BrE)
a steep slope or cliff at the upper end of a valley or glacial cirque

Examples

Steep headwall climb
The hikers reached the base of the headwall and paused to admire the view.
Snow collected at the top of the headwall before tumbling down into the valley below.
Climbers prepared their gear before attempting to scale the icy headwall of the cirque.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cliff - a steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea
  • synonym: slope - a surface of which one end or side is at a higher level than another
