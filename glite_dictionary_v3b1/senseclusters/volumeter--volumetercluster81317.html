volumeter

noun
device for measuring volume
8.0 9.3
Pronunciation: / vəˈluːmətɚ / (AmE), / vəˈluːmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring the volume of gases, liquids, or solids, either directly or by displacement

Examples

A volumeter device
A volumeter can be calibrated for liquids, solids, or gases, depending on the experiment's requirements.
Accurate measurements from the volumeter ensured the results were reliable.
The laboratory technician used a volumeter to determine the amount of gas produced in the reaction.
