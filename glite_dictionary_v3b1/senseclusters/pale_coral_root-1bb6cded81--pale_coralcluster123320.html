pale coral root

noun
a nearly leafless, pale-stemmed North American orchid
9.9
Pronunciation: / peɪl ˈkɔrəl ˌrʊt / (AmE), / peɪl ˈkɒrəl ˌruːt / (BrE)
a plant with nearly leafless pale yellow to greenish stems and similarly colored flowers with white lower lips, found from northern New Mexico to Alaska
