enlarge

verb
add detail; expand (on an idea)
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈlɑrdʒ / (AmE), / ɪnˈlɑːdʒ / (BrE)
to add detail or information to something, especially to explain or clarify an idea in greater depth

Examples

To enlarge on a point
During the presentation, she enlarged on her initial proposal with several case studies.
If you want to enlarge upon your opinion, please submit a written explanation.
The professor encouraged students to enlarge their responses with more historical context.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elaborate - to add more detail or information; to expand upon
  • synonym: expand - to give more details about something or make it larger in scope
