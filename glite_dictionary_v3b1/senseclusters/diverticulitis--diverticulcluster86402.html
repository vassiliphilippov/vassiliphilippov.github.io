diverticulitis

noun
inflammation of a colon diverticulum
Pronunciation: / dəˌvɝː.tɪ.kjəˈlaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / daɪˌvɜː.tɪ.kjʊˈlaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A medical condition involving inflammation of a diverticulum in the digestive tract, especially in the colon, often causing abdominal pain, fever, and constipation.

Examples

Diverticulitis pain
A high-fiber diet is often recommended for those who have experienced diverticulitis.
After several days of abdominal pain, the doctor diagnosed her with diverticulitis.
Severe cases of diverticulitis may require hospitalization and intravenous antibiotics.
