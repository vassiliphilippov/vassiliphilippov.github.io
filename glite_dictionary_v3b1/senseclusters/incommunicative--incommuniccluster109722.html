incommunicative

adjective
unwilling to talk or share information
2.7 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪn.kə.ˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.tɪv/ (AmE), /ɪn.kə.ˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.tɪv/ (BrE)
unwilling or reluctant to talk, give information, or express opinions

Examples

An incommunicative person
Despite our efforts to involve him, Mark remained incommunicative throughout the meeting.
His incommunicative manner made it difficult to understand what he was thinking.
She became very incommunicative after the argument and avoided answering our questions.
