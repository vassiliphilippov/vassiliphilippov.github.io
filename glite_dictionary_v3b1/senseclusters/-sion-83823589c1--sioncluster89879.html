noun-forming suffix
Pronunciation: / ʃən / (AmE), / ʒən /; / ʃən / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns that refer to an action, process, state, or the result of an action (as in 'decision', 'expansion')

Examples

The -sion suffix
The word 'expansion' uses the -sion suffix to show the process of expanding.
Many English nouns, such as 'decision' and 'confusion', end with -sion.
Adding -sion to 'expand' creates a noun meaning the act of expanding.
