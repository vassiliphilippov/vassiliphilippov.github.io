bass viol

noun
largest and lowest violin family instrument
8.3 7.5
Pronunciation: /beɪs ˈvaɪ.əl/ (AmE), /beɪs ˈvaɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a large, low-pitched string instrument from the viol family, similar to the double bass or cello
