runaround

noun
evasive delay
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrʌnəˌraʊnd / (BrE)
evasive or delaying action that prevents someone from getting a direct answer or help

Examples

Get the runaround
After calling three departments, she realized she was just getting the runaround from the company.
He was tired of the runaround and demanded a clear answer from the manager.
Whenever I ask about my refund, they give me the runaround instead of a solution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assistance - help or support given directly
  • synonym: stalling - delaying action to avoid giving an answer
  • synonym: stonewalling - refusing to answer or cooperate
