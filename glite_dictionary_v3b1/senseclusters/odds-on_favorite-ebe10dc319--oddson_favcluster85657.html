odds-on favorite

noun
most likely winner (betting/competition)
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɑdz ɑn ˈfeɪvərɪt / (AmE), / ɒdz ɒn ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
The person, team, or competitor considered most likely to win, especially in a competition or contest.

Examples

The odds-on favorite team
Despite being the odds-on favorite, the team lost in a shocking upset.
In the final election polls, the incumbent became the clear odds-on favorite.
She was considered the odds-on favorite to win the championship before the race even started.

Related Terms

  • synonym: favorite - the competitor considered most likely to win
  • antonym: underdog - a competitor thought to have little chance of winning
