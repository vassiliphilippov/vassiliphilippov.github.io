mimsy

noun
timid or weak person
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪmzi / (AmE), / ˈmɪmzi / (BrE)
an informal term for a timid, ineffectual, or weak person, often used humorously or critically

Examples

A mimsy at work
Stop being such a mimsy and try speaking up for yourself.
He was called a mimsy by his friends for refusing to join the game.
No one wants to be seen as a mimsy in a competitive environment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not being afraid
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage or is easily frightened
  • synonym: wimp - a person who is weak or lacks courage
