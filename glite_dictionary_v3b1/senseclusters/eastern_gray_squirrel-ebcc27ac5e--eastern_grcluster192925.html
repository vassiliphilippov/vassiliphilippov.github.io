eastern gray squirrel

noun
North American gray squirrel species
5.8 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.stərn greɪ ˈskwɜːr.əl/ /; / /ˈiː.stərn greɪ ˈskwɪr.əl/ (AmE), /ˈiː.stən greɪ ˈskwɪr.əl/ (BrE)
a common medium-sized squirrel native to eastern North America, distinguished by its gray fur and bushy tail, and introduced to other regions such as England
