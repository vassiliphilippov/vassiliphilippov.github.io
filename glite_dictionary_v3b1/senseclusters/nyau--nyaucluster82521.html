nyau

noun
Chewa ritual dance
Pronunciation: / ˈnjaʊ / (AmE), / ˈnjaʊ / (BrE)
a traditional masked ritual dance and the associated secret society of the Chewa people in Malawi, Zambia, and Mozambique

Examples

A nyau dance
The nyau is performed during important ceremonies in Chewa communities.
Children watched in awe as the nyau dancers appeared in colorful masks and costumes.
Learning the nyau is considered a rite of passage for some Chewa boys.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masquerade - a festive gathering featuring masked dancers
  • synonym: ritual - a ceremony with a set order of actions, often religious or cultural
