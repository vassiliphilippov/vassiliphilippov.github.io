parson

noun
Protestant minister or parish priest
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrsən / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsən / (BrE)
a member of the clergy, especially a Protestant minister with responsibility for a parish

Examples

A parson in church
Many old English novels feature a wise parson as a central character.
She sought advice from the local parson during a difficult time.
The village parson delivered a thoughtful sermon on Sunday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: minister - a member of the clergy, especially in Protestant churches
  • synonym: priest - an ordained minister in the Catholic, Orthodox, or Anglican Church
