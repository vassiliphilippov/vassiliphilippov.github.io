Gnetales

noun
order of tropical or dry-region woody plants
Pronunciation: / nɛˈteɪliːz / (AmE), / nɛˈteɪliːz / (BrE)
an order of chiefly tropical or xerophytic woody plants, sometimes considered close to the ancestral line of flowering plants
