over the transom

adverb
unsolicited or unexpected submission
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvər ðə ˈtræn.səm / (AmE), / ˈəʊvə ðə ˈtræn.səm / (BrE)
(of something, especially a manuscript or idea) submitted or received unexpectedly and without prior arrangement, typically unsolicited.

Examples

Sent over the transom
New artists sometimes get their first break when their work lands over the transom.
The magazine often discovers excellent short stories that come in over the transom.
We weren't expecting the proposal; it just arrived over the transom one morning.
