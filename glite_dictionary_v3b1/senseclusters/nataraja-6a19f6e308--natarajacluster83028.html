Nataraja

proper noun
Indian male name
9.9
Pronunciation: / nəˈtɑːrədʒə / (AmE), / nəˈtɑːrədʒə / (BrE)
a male given name in India, derived from the Hindu god Shiva in his form as the cosmic dancer

Examples

Nataraja as a name
They named their son Nataraja to honor the family's devotion to Lord Shiva.
The statue of Nataraja stood at the entrance of the temple, symbolizing cosmic dance.
Many families in South India choose Nataraja as a traditional name for boys.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Shiva - a principal Hindu deity, known as the destroyer and transformer
