Brahminism

noun
orthodox Hindu religious and social system
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbrɑː.mɪ.nɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈbræ.mɪ.nɪz.əm/ (AmE), /ˈbrɑː.mɪ.nɪz.əm/ /; / /ˈbræ.mɪ.nɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the religious and social system of orthodox Hinduism, based on ancient Indian texts and emphasizing the authority of the Brahmin priestly caste
