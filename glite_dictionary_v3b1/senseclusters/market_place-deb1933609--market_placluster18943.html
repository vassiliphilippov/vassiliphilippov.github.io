market place

noun
an area where goods are bought and sold
1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkət ˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt ˌpleɪs / (BrE)
an open area in a town or city where people gather to buy and sell goods, especially regularly or on a designated day

Examples

Busy market place
Street performers often entertain crowds in the central market place.
The town's market place is bustling with vendors every Saturday morning.
They opened a new café right on the edge of the historic market place.
