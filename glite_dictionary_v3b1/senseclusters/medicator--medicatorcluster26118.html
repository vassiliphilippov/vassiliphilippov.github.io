medicator

noun
medicine dispenser
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdɪˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈmɛdɪˌkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or device that administers medicine or medication

Examples

Automatic medicator
The hospital uses an automatic medicator to ensure patients receive the correct dosage.
A trained medicator visited the home to administer the daily injections.
Farmers rely on a medicator to add medicine to the animals' water supply.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick and administer medicine
