god-fearing

adjective
devoutly religious; reverent toward God
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌfɪrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌfɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a deep respect and reverence for God; devoutly religious.

Examples

A god-fearing person
Being god-fearing was considered an important virtue in that community.
She came from a god-fearing family that attended church every Sunday.
The town was known for its honest and god-fearing citizens.
