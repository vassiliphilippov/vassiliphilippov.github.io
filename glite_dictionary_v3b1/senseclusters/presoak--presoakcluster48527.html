presoak

noun
pre-wash soaking
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌsoʊk / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌsəʊk / (BrE)
a liquid or process used to soak items before the main washing or cleaning step

Examples

A presoak cycle
The instructions recommend a presoak for heavily stained clothes before putting them in the washing machine.
She mixed a special presoak solution to help remove the tough dirt from the tablecloth.
A quick presoak can make it much easier to clean greasy pans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prewash - a treatment or process before the main wash
  • antonym: rinse - to wash lightly with water
