gun deck

noun
deck on a warship with cannons
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn dɛk / (AmE), / ɡʌn dɛk / (BrE)
a deck on a warship below the main deck, formerly fitted entirely with cannons along its length

Examples

The gun deck on ship
During the battle, smoke filled the gun deck as cannons fired from both sides.
Restoration of the old frigate included repairing the original gun deck.
The sailors slept in hammocks slung above the gun deck after their shifts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: weather deck - the uppermost deck exposed to the weather
