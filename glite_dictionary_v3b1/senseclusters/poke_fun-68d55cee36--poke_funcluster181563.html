poke fun

verb
mock or ridicule playfully
4.4 3.2
Pronunciation: /poʊk fʌn/ (AmE), /pəʊk fʌn/ (BrE)
to make jokes about someone or something in a way that makes them seem silly or ridicules them, often in a playful or mocking manner

Examples

To poke fun at friends
It's not nice to poke fun at people just because they're different.
She gently poked fun at her brother for his messy room, making everyone laugh.
The comedian loved to poke fun at celebrities during his act.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mock - to laugh at or make jokes about someone, often unkindly
  • antonym: praise - to express strong approval or admiration
  • synonym: ridicule - to make fun of someone or something in a cruel or harsh way
