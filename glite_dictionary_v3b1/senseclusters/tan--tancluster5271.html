tan

noun
brown skin from sun exposure
4.3
Pronunciation: / tæn / (AmE), / tæn / (BrE)
a light brown coloration of the skin resulting from exposure to sunlight

Examples

A summer tan
After a week at the beach, she had a noticeable tan on her arms and face.
He wore sunscreen to avoid getting a tan during the summer hike.
Her tan faded quickly once autumn arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sunburn - reddening and inflammation of the skin from excessive sun exposure
