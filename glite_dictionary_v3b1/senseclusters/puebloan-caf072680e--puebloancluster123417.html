Puebloan

noun
member of Pueblo peoples
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpwɛbloʊən / (AmE), / ˈpwɛbləʊən / (BrE)
a member of the Pueblo peoples, Indigenous groups of the southwestern United States known for their unique culture and adobe dwellings

Examples

A Puebloan artist
A Puebloan artist displayed her pottery at the cultural festival in Santa Fe.
Many Puebloans continue to practice traditional farming methods passed down for generations.
The museum features exhibits about Puebloan history and architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Native American - a member of the Indigenous peoples of the Americas
  • antonym: Navajo - a member of a Native American people of the southwestern United States
