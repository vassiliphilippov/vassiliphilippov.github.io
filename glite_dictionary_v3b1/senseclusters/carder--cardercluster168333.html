carder

noun
fiber-combing worker
8.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.dər/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.də/ (BrE)

Examples

In the textile mill, a large carder processed raw cotton into smooth fibers.
Before spinning, the carder ensured all the fibers were clean and untangled.
