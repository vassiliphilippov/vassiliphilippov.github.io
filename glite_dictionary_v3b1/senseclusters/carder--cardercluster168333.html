carder

noun
fiber-combing worker
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrdər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdə / (BrE)
a person or machine that combs and cleans fibers such as wool before spinning

Examples

Wool carder machine
The carder carefully prepared the wool before it was spun into yarn.
In the textile mill, a large carder processed raw cotton into smooth fibers.
Before spinning, the carder ensured all the fibers were clean and untangled.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinner - a person or machine that spins fibers into yarn or thread
  • synonym: weaver - a person who weaves fabric from yarn or thread
