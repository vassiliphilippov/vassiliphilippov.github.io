demoiselle

noun
small crane or damselfly
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛm.wɑːˈzɛl / (AmE), / ˌdɛm.wɑːˈzɛl / (BrE)
a small, graceful crane or a slender damselfly, especially found in Europe and Asia

Examples

Demoiselle crane
We watched a demoiselle crane gracefully walk along the edge of the lake.
The bright blue wings of the demoiselle caught the sunlight as it flew over the stream.
In the nature reserve, several demoiselles could be seen among the reeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crane - a large, long-legged bird found in wetlands
  • synonym: damselfly - a slender insect related to the dragonfly
