cycler

noun
spacecraft on regular route
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪklər / (AmE), / ˈsaɪklə / (BrE)
a spacecraft or orbital path that repeatedly travels between two celestial bodies to transport people or cargo

Examples

Mars cycler spacecraft
The new cycler will make regular trips between Earth and Mars, reducing travel costs.
Scientists are designing a cycler to provide continuous transport for future space missions.
A permanent cycler could revolutionize how we move people and supplies in the solar system.
