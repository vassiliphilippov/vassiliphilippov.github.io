cherub

noun
a sweet, innocent baby or child
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrəb / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛrəb / (BrE)
A sweet, innocent-looking baby or young child, often depicted as beautiful and angelic.

Examples

A smiling cherub
At the family picnic, her daughter ran around laughing, a little cherub with golden curls.
Everyone admired the newborn, calling him a perfect cherub.
The baby slept peacefully, looking like a cherub with rosy cheeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angel - a spiritual being regarded as a messenger of God
