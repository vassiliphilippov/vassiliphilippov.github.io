labrocyte

noun
a connective tissue cell involved in allergic responses
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlæb.rə.saɪt/ /; / /ˈlæ.brə.saɪt/ (AmE), /ˈlæb.rə.saɪt/ /; / /ˈlæ.brə.saɪt/ (BrE)
A large connective tissue cell that contains histamine, heparin, and serotonin, which are released during allergic reactions or in response to injury or inflammation.
