oophorosalpingectomy

noun
removal of ovaries and Fallopian tubes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊ.əˌfɔroʊ.sælˌpɪnˈdʒɛk.tə.mi / (AmE), / ˌəʊ.əˌfɒr.əʊ.sælˌpɪŋˈdʒɛk.tə.mi / (BrE)
A surgical procedure involving the removal of one or both ovaries and the corresponding Fallopian tubes.

Examples

An oophorosalpingectomy surgery
After her oophorosalpingectomy, she was monitored closely for any hormonal changes.
Doctors may perform an oophorosalpingectomy in cases of ovarian cancer.
The patient underwent an oophorosalpingectomy to treat severe endometriosis.
