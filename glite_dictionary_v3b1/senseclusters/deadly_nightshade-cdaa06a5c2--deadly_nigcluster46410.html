deadly nightshade

noun
poisonous plant; source of atropine
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdli ˈnaɪtˌʃeɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdli ˈnaɪtʃeɪd / (BrE)
a highly poisonous plant (Atropa belladonna) with bell-shaped flowers and shiny black or red berries, also known as belladonna
