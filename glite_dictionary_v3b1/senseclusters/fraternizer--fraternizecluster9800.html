fraternizer

noun
one who befriends others
Pronunciation: / frəˈtɜːrnaɪzər / (AmE), / frəˈtɜːnɪzaː / (BrE)
a person who forms friendly or close relationships, especially when such behavior is discouraged or forbidden

Examples

Enemy fraternizer
During wartime, a fraternizer might be viewed with suspicion.
The company discouraged employees from being a fraternizer with competitors.
As a known fraternizer, he often made friends even in difficult situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: associate - a person who joins or connects with others
  • antonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a group or avoids close relationships
