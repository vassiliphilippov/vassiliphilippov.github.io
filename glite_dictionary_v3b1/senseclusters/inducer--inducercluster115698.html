inducer

noun
someone who persuades or leads others
4.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdusər / (AmE), / ɪnˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person who tries to persuade or lead someone to take a particular action or adopt a particular opinion

Examples

A skilled inducer of change
As the chief inducer of the plan, she convinced the others to participate.
He acted as an inducer when he urged his friends to take up the challenge.
The inducer presented compelling arguments to change public opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: persuader - a person who convinces others to do or believe something
