whenever

conjunction
at any time that
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɛnˈɛvər / (AmE), / wɛnˈɛvə / (BrE)
at any time or every time that a particular event or situation happens

Examples

Visit us whenever possible
You can visit us whenever you are in town.
Please call me whenever you have any questions about the project.
He feels happy whenever he listens to his favorite music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: when - at the time that something happens
