modern dance

noun
expressive theatrical dance style
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑdɚn dæns / (AmE), / ˈmɒdən dɑːns / (BrE)
a style of theatrical dance characterized by expressive, often improvisational movements and less rigidity than classical ballet

Examples

Modern dance style
Many choreographers are inspired by modern dance when creating new performances.
She has been taking modern dance classes for three years and loves their expressive style.
The festival included workshops in both ballet and modern dance for dancers of all levels.
