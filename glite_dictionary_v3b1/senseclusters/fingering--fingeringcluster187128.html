fingering

noun
method of placing fingers on instruments
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɪŋɡərɪŋ / (BrE)
the arrangement or technique of placing the fingers on the keys, strings, or holes of a musical instrument to produce particular notes or passages
