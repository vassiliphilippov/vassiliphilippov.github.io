juice up

verb
give performance-enhancing drugs
Pronunciation: / dʒus ʌp / (AmE), / dʒuːs ʌp / (BrE)
to give someone performance-enhancing drugs or stimulants, especially an athlete, in order to improve their performance.

Examples

To juice up athletes
The coach was accused of trying to juice up several players before the championship.
Several reporters claimed those athletes had been juiced up by an unlicensed trainer.
Investigators found evidence that the team doctor had been juicing up competitors for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dope (verb) - to give an athlete drugs that enhance performance
  • synonym: doping - the use of banned drugs or methods to improve athletic performance
