home-brewed

adjective
brewed at home
6.7
Pronunciation: / hoʊm ˈbruːd / (AmE), / həʊm ˈbruːd / (BrE)
Brewed at home, rather than in a commercial facility.

Examples

Home-brewed cider
He served us some of his home-brewed cider, which was surprisingly good.
She prefers the taste of home-brewed beer to the commercial varieties.
The pub offers a selection of home-brewed ales alongside imported brands.
