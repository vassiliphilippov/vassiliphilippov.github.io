ready-made

noun
pre-manufactured item; not custom-made
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdiˌmeɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɛdiˌmeɪd / (BrE)
an item, such as a garment or piece of furniture, that is manufactured in advance rather than made to order

Examples

A ready-made shirt
Many people prefer to buy ready-mades rather than waiting for custom orders.
She decorated her first apartment with inexpensive ready-mades from a local department store.
The boutique sells both handmade dresses and ready-mades for customers in a hurry.

Related Terms

  • antonym: custom-made - an item made to order according to specific requirements
