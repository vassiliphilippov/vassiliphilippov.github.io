R&D

noun
research and development
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɑr ən ˈdi / (AmE), / ɑːr ən ˈdiː / (BrE)
the department or activities in a company focused on research and development of new products or technologies

Examples

R&D department
The company increased its R&D budget to stay ahead of competitors in the tech industry.
Many breakthroughs in medicine come from dedicated R&D teams working behind the scenes.
Startups often rely on strong R&D to develop innovative products quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: innovation - the process of creating new ideas, products, or methods
  • antonym: production - the process of making goods for sale
