cogitate

verb
think deeply; reflect
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːdʒɪˌteɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt / (BrE)
to think deeply or carefully about something; to reflect or consider thoroughly

Examples

To cogitate deeply
After hearing the proposal, they cogitated for hours before reaching an agreement.
During the meeting, he sat in silence, cogitating on the alternatives.
She needed a quiet place to cogitate before making such an important decision.
Before answering, the professor cogitated for a moment to consider all perspectives.
He sat quietly by the window, cogitating on the meaning of life.
She needed time to cogitate before making her final decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contemplate - think deeply about something
  • antonym: forget - to fail to remember or give consideration to something
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
  • synonym: ponder - to think about something carefully, especially before making a decision
  • synonym: think - to use one's mind to consider or reason about something
