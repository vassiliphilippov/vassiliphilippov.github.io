etrog

noun
citrus fruit or tree
Pronunciation: / ˈɛtrɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈɛtrɒɡ / (BrE)
a large, fragrant yellow citrus fruit (Citrus medica) or the tree that produces it, used in cooking and as a botanical specimen

Examples

Fresh etrog slices
The chef used slices of etrog to add a unique flavor to the dessert.
An etrog tree can be grown in warm climates with plenty of sunlight.
She admired the bright yellow etrog displayed at the market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citron - a large, yellow citrus fruit with thick rind
  • degree lower: lemon - a yellow citrus fruit with sour juice
