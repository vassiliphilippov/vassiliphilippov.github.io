fifth

noun
musical interval of five scale degrees
9.8
Pronunciation: / fɪfθ / (AmE), / fɪfθ / (BrE)
(Music) The interval between one note and another five scale degrees above it, including both notes.

Examples

A perfect fifth
A perfect fifth is one of the most consonant intervals in music.
She practiced singing in fifths with her partner.
The melody jumped a fifth for dramatic effect.
