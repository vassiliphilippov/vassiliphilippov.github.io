overconcentration

noun
excessive gathering
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
a situation in which too much of something is gathered or focused in one place, group, or area

Examples

Overconcentration of power
The overconcentration of wealth in a few hands can lead to social inequality.
Environmentalists warn about the overconcentration of pollutants in certain lakes.
An overconcentration of students in one classroom can affect the quality of education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concentration - the act of gathering or focusing things in one place
  • antonym: dispersion - the process of spreading things over a wide area
