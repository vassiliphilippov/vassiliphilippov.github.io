exponential

adjective
relating to exponents or rapid growth
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.spəˈnɛn.tʃəl / (AmE), / ˌɛk.spəˈnɛn.tʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving exponents; increasing at a rate that becomes more rapid in proportion to the growing total number or size.

Examples

Exponential growth
The population of the city experienced exponential growth over the past decade.
The scientist described the trend as exponential, not linear.
This process follows an exponential curve, doubling in value each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: logarithmic - relating to logarithms
