pokey

noun
slang: jail or prison
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊki / (AmE), / ˈpəʊki / (BrE)
(slang) a jail or prison, especially a small one for people who are awaiting trial or serving a short sentence.

Examples

A night in the pokey
He spent the night in the pokey after a minor traffic violation.
She joked that if she kept parking illegally, she'd end up in the pokey.
The old Western movies often show outlaws being thrown into the pokey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - a building where people are kept as a punishment for a crime
