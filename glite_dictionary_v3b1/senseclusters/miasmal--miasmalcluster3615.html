miasmal

adjective
filled with vapor or unhealthy mist
9.9
Pronunciation: / miˈæzməl / (AmE), / miˈæzməl / (BrE)
filled with vapor or a noxious, unhealthy atmosphere, especially a dense or fetid mist.

Examples

A miasmal swamp
An eerie silence hung over the miasmal marsh at dawn, broken only by the occasional croak of a frog.
He described the cave as damp and miasmal, with an oppressive air that made it hard to stay long.
The explorers struggled to breathe in the miasmal swamps, where a thick fog clung to everything.
