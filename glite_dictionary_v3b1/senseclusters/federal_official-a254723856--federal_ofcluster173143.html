federal official

noun
law-enforcement officer working for the federal government
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛdərəl əˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˈfɛdərəl əˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
a law-enforcement officer employed by the federal government, especially in the United States
