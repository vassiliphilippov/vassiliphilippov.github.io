broken

adjective
irregular, uneven ground or terrain
Pronunciation: / ˈbroʊkən / (AmE), / ˈbrəʊkən / (BrE)
(of terrain or ground) having an uneven surface, characterized by irregular features such as hills, rocks, or abrupt changes in elevation.

Examples

Broken ground
Farmers found it difficult to cultivate the broken ground.
The area is marked by broken hills and sharp ravines.
The hikers struggled to cross the broken landscape after the storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rugged - having an uneven or jagged surface
