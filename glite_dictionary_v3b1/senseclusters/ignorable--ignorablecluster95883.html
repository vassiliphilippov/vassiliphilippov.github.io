ignorable

adjective
able to be disregarded
5.6
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈnɔːrəbəl / (AmE), / ɪɡˈnɔːrəbəl / (BrE)

Examples

An ignorable mistake

Most of the background noise was ignorable during the important phone call.
He found the minor details in the report to be ignorable compared to the main findings.
