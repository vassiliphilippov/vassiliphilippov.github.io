summer snowflake

noun
a European plant with star-shaped white flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚ ˈsnoʊˌfleɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌmə ˈsnəʊfleɪk / (BrE)
a European perennial plant with narrow, grasslike leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers, often cultivated for ornament and naturalized in parts of North America

Examples

Summer snowflakes flowers
She planted several summer snowflakes along the walkway for their ornamental appeal.
The summer snowflake blooms in late spring, filling the garden with clusters of delicate white flowers.
Unlike its relative the snowdrop, the summer snowflake flowers later and grows taller.
