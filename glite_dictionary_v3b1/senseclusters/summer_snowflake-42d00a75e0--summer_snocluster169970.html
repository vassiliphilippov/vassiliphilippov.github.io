summer snowflake

noun
a European plant with star-shaped white flowers
1.0 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsʌm.ər ˈsnoʊ.fleɪk/ (AmE), /ˈsʌm.ə ˈsnəʊ.fleɪk/ (BrE)
a European perennial plant with narrow, grasslike leaves and clusters of star-shaped white flowers, often cultivated for ornament and naturalized in parts of North America
