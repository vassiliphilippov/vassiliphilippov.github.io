Neisseria meningitidis

noun
bacterium causing meningitis
Pronunciation: / naɪˈsɪəriə məˌnɪnˌdʒɪˈtaɪdɪs / (AmE), / naɪˈsɪəriə mɛnɪnˌdʒɪˈtɪdɪs /; / naɪˈsɪəriə mɛnɪnˈdʒɪtɪdɪs / (BrE)
a species of bacterium that can cause meningitis and other serious infections, but may also live harmlessly in the human nose or throat
