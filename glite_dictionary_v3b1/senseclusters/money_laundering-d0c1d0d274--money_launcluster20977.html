money laundering

noun
concealing illegally obtained money's origins
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌni ˌlɔndərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmʌni ˌlɔːndərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of concealing the origins of money obtained illegally, typically by passing it through complex transfers or transactions

Examples

Money laundering process
She was convicted of money laundering after helping to process illegal funds through foreign banks.
The company was used as a front for money laundering by several criminal organizations.
The investigation resulted in charges of money laundering and tax evasion against the suspects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embezzlement - theft or misappropriation of funds placed in one's trust
  • synonym: fraud - wrongful or criminal deception intended to result in financial or personal gain
  • degree lower: money - medium of exchange in the form of coins and banknotes
