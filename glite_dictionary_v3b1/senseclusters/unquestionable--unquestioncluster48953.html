unquestionable

adjective
impossible to doubt or dispute
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənəbl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkwɛstʃənəbl / (BrE)
impossible to doubt or dispute; certain or definite

Examples

He has unquestionable authority in this field and is respected by all his peers.
Her honesty is unquestionable, and no one has ever doubted her integrity.
The evidence presented in court was unquestionable and convinced the jury.
