tv

noun
device for watching television
1.0 16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåtiÀê ÀàviÀê / (AmE), / ÀåtiÀê ÀàviÀê / (BrE)
An electronic device that receives television signals and displays them on a screen.

Examples

A tv screen
He likes to watch movies on a big tv screen at home.
She turned off the tv before going to bed.
We bought a new tv for the living room last weekend.

Synonyms

  • tv set
    1.0 2.57/m
    tv set is the common household screen and tuner you place in a room, while tv is the shorter everyday name.
  • television set
    1.4 3.35/m
    television set names the full device used to watch broadcasts at home, whereas tv is its short, informal form in speech.
  • television
    1.0 34/m
    television refers to the device or broadcast medium in formal contexts, but tv is the casual spoken or written short form.
  • television receiver
    2.4 0.03/m
    television receiver is a technical term for the electronic unit that takes in signals, while tv is the everyday name.
