coolness

noun
moderate coldness; cool temperature
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlnəs / (AmE), / ˈkuːlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being moderately cold

Examples

Coolness of the air
I enjoy the coolness of the shade on a summer day.
She wrapped herself in a sweater to guard against the morning coolness.
The coolness in the air hinted at the coming of autumn.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chilliness - the quality of being unpleasantly cold
  • antonym: warmth - the quality or state of being warm
