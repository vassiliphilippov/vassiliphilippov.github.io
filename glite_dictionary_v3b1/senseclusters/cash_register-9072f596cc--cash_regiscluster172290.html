cash register

noun
machine for recording and totaling sales
3.0 1.3
Pronunciation: /kæʃ ˈre.dʒɪ.stər/ /; / /kæʃ ˈre.dʒə.stər/ (AmE), /kæʃ ˈre.dʒɪ.stə/ /; / /kæʃ ˈre.dʒə.stə/ (BrE)
A machine used in shops for adding up the cost of purchases and recording transactions, often with a cash drawer.

Examples

The cash register machine
Every sale must be entered into the cash register for accurate accounting.
She counted the money in the cash register at the end of her shift.
The cashier opened the cash register and handed me my change.

Related Terms
