way

noun
category or type (in the way of)
4.1
Pronunciation: / weɪ / (AmE), / weɪ / (BrE)
(used in the expression 'in the way of') a general category or type of thing; regarding or in respect to.

Examples

In the way of help
He has little in the way of practical experience, but he's very eager to learn.
In the way of dessert, we only have fruit and cheese.
They didn't have much in the way of furniture in their new apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kind - a group of things with similar characteristics
  • synonym: regard - with respect to or concerning
