literalism

noun
interpretation by literal meaning
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.əˌrəl.ɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.ər.əl.ɪz.əm / (BrE)
the tendency to interpret words, statements, or texts in their most basic and straightforward sense, rather than recognizing figurative or metaphorical meanings.

Examples

Strict literalism in reading
A strict literalism can overlook the richness of metaphorical language.
Academic debates sometimes stall because of too much literalism in interpreting texts.
Her literalism made it difficult for her to understand jokes that relied on wordplay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: figurative - involving a metaphorical or symbolic interpretation
  • synonym: literalist - a person who interprets texts or statements literally
