western narrow-mouthed toad

noun
a small toad native to central and western North America
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstɚn ˈnæroʊ maʊθt toʊd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ˈnærəʊ maʊθt təʊd / (BrE)
