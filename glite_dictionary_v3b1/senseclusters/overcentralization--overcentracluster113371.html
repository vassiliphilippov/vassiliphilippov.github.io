overcentralization

noun
excessive central control
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˌsɛntrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the excessive concentration of control or authority in a single central group or location

Examples

Excessive overcentralization of power
Many experts warn that overcentralization can make organizations less flexible and responsive.
The government faced criticism for the overcentralization of power in the capital city.
Efforts to reduce overcentralization led to more decision-making at the local level.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centralization - the concentration of control in a central authority
  • antonym: decentralization - the distribution of authority away from a central location
