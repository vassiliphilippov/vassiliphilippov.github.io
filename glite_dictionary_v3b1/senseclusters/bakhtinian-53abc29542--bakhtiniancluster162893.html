Bakhtinian

noun
follower of Bakhtin
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɑːkˈtiːniən / (AmE), / bakˈtiːniən / (BrE)
a person who follows, supports, or applies the theories and ideas of Mikhail Bakhtin, a Russian philosopher and literary critic

Examples

A Bakhtinian scholar
The Bakhtinian presented a compelling analysis of dialogism in the novel.
Many literary scholars identify as Bakhtinians due to their interest in heteroglossia.
At the conference, a Bakhtinian perspective was used to interpret the text's multiple voices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Marxist - a person who follows the theories of Karl Marx
  • antonym: structuralist - a person who supports structuralism in philosophy or literary theory
