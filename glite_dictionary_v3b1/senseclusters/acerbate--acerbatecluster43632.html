acerbate

verb
make bitter or resentful
Pronunciation: / ˈæsɚˌbeɪt / (AmE), / ˈasəˌbeɪt / (BrE)
to make someone feel bitter or resentful

Examples

To acerbate her feelings
Her bitter disappointment was acerbated by their lack of understanding.
The constant criticism only acerbated his feelings toward his classmates.
Years of neglect acerbated the community's resentment against the authorities.
