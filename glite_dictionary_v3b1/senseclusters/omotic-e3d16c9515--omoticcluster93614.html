Omotic

noun
language group of southern Ethiopia
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /oʊ.ˈmɑː.tɪk/ /; / /ə.ˈmoʊ.tɪk/ (AmE), /ɒ.ˈmɒt.ɪk/ /; / /əʊ.ˈmɒt.ɪk/ (BrE)
A group of closely related languages spoken in the Omo Valley of southern Ethiopia, considered part of the Afro-Asiatic language family.

Examples

Omotic languages
Linguists have classified several tongues spoken in southern Ethiopia as Omotic languages.
Many Omotic languages are spoken by small communities along the Omo River.
The Omotic language group is considered part of the larger Afro-Asiatic family.
