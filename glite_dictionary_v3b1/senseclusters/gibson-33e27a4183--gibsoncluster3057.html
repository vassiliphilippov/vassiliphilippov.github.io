Gibson

noun
gin cocktail with onion
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪbsən / (AmE), / ˈɡɪbsən / (BrE)
a cocktail made with gin and dry vermouth, typically garnished with a pickled onion

Examples

A classic Gibson cocktail
He ordered a Gibson at the bar, enjoying the unique flavor of the pickled onion garnish.
The bartender prepared a classic Gibson with extra dry vermouth for her.
If you prefer a savory twist, try a Gibson instead of a traditional martini.
