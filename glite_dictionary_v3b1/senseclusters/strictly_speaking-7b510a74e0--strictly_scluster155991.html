strictly speaking

adverb
according to the most exact meaning
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstrɪktli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstrɪktli ˈspiːkɪŋ / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is true if you consider the exact meaning or the most precise details; in the strictest sense.

Examples

Strictly speaking exactly
Strictly speaking, the treatment began last year, not this year.
Strictly speaking, whales are not fish, but mammals.
The rule, strictly speaking, applies only to members of the club.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loosely speaking - not in a strict or precise way
  • synonym: technically - according to the precise meaning or strictest rules
