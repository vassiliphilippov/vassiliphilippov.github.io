luv

noun
informal 'love'
Pronunciation: / lʌv / (AmE), / lʌv / (BrE)
an informal or playful way of writing or saying 'love', often used in text messages or casual speech

Examples

Much luv
He ended his message with 'Much luv to you all!' before signing off.
She sent a quick text saying 'luv you lots!' to her best friend.
On her birthday card, he wrote 'luv from your favorite cousin.'

Related Terms

  • synonym: love - a strong feeling of affection
