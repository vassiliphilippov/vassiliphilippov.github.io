superheavy

adjective
very high atomic mass
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈhɛvi / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈhɛvi / (BrE)
relating to chemical elements with extremely high atomic numbers and masses, especially those created artificially in laboratories

Examples
