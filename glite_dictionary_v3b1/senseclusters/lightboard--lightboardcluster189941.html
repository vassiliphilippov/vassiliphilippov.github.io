lightboard

noun
stage lighting console
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtbɔːd / (BrE)
a control panel used to operate, program, and adjust stage or theatrical lighting during performances

Examples

Theater lightboard
The stage manager checked the lightboard before the show to ensure all cues were programmed correctly.
During the performance, the technician operated the lightboard to create dramatic lighting effects.
Learning to use the lightboard is essential for anyone interested in theater production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dimmer board - a panel for adjusting the brightness of stage lights
  • synonym: lighting console - a device for controlling stage or studio lights
