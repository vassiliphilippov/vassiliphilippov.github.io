grissino

noun
long, thin Italian breadstick
Pronunciation: / ɡriˈsiːnoʊ / (AmE), / ɡrɪˈsiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A long, thin, crisp breadstick, especially of Italian origin, often served as an appetizer or snack.

Examples

Fresh grissino breadstick
Fresh grissino pairs perfectly with antipasto platters.
I love dipping a grissino into olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
The waiter placed a basket of grissino on our table before the main course.
