homophonous

adjective
having the same pronunciation as another word
5.7
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmɑːfəˌnoʊs / (AmE), / həʊˈmɒfənəs / (BrE)
(of words) having the same pronunciation but different meanings or origins; relating to or characteristic of homophones.

Examples

Homophonous words
In the test, students had to identify ten homophonous words and explain their meanings.
Spelling errors often occur because of homophonous words that sound identical but have different meanings.
The English language contains many homophonous pairs, such as ‘there’ and ‘their’.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homophone - a word pronounced the same as another but differing in meaning or origin
