hand-wave

verb
to evade details
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌweɪv / (AmE), / ˈhændˌweɪv / (BrE)
to avoid giving a detailed or satisfactory explanation, often by being vague or using gestures instead of facts

Examples

To hand-wave an answer
When asked about the missing data, the scientist started to hand-wave instead of providing real answers.
Politicians sometimes hand-wave difficult questions during debates to avoid controversy.
She tried to hand-wave the problem, but her manager insisted on a thorough explanation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evade - to avoid answering directly or giving details
  • antonym: explain - to make something clear by giving details
