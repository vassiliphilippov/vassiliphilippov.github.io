wolfsbane

noun
poisonous Eurasian herb (Aconitum)
7.7 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfsˌbeɪn / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfsˌbeɪn / (BrE)
a poisonous Eurasian perennial herb (Aconitum species), with broad rounded leaves, yellow or blue flowers, and a fibrous rootstock
