adscription

noun
assignment or attribution
Pronunciation: / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ædˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)
the act of assigning or attributing something to a particular person, group, or source

Examples

Adscription of a poem
The adscription of the poem to the famous author was later proven incorrect.
Legal documents require the adscription of property rights to the rightful owner.
The adscription of responsibility for the project was discussed at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment - the act of giving someone a particular task or responsibility
  • synonym: attribution - the act of regarding something as being caused by a person or thing
