act

noun
pretended or insincere behavior
Pronunciation: / ækt / (AmE), / akt / (BrE)
A display of behavior that is not genuine; an insincere or pretended performance.

Examples

Put on an act
After the show, his friendly act quickly disappeared.
He always puts on an act when his boss is around.
Nobody was fooled by her cheerful act at the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pretense - an attempt to make something that is not the case appear true
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being free from pretense or deceit
