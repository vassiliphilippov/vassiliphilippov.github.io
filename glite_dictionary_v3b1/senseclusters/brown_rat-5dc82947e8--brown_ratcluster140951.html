brown rat

noun
common global rat species; household pest
1.0
Pronunciation: / braʊn ræt / (AmE), / braʊn ræt / (BrE)
a common species of rat (Rattus norvegicus) found worldwide, often living near human settlements and considered a serious pest

Examples

The brown rat species
Infestations of the brown rat can cause significant damage to property and food supplies.
Scientists have studied the brown rat to learn more about disease transmission.
The brown rat is frequently found near garbage dumps in urban areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Norway rat - another common name for the brown rat (Rattus norvegicus)
  • antonym: black rat - a different species of rat, Rattus rattus
