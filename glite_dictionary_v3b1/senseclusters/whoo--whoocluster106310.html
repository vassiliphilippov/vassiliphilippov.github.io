whoo

verb
to hoot like an owl
9.9
Pronunciation: / wuː / (AmE), / wuː / (BrE)

Examples

Owls whoo at night
At night, you can hear owls whoo in the quiet forest.
The children tried to whoo like owls during the nature walk.
A distant whoo echoed through the trees as the sun set.
