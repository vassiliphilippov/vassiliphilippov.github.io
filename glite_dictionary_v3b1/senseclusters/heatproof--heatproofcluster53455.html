heatproof

adjective
resistant to heat
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhiːtˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈhiːtˌpruːf / (BrE)
able to resist damage or change when exposed to high temperatures

Examples

Heatproof bowl
Make sure to use a heatproof bowl when melting chocolate in the microwave.
The laboratory is equipped with heatproof gloves for handling hot equipment.
Always place hot pans on a heatproof surface to avoid burns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fireproof - able to resist burning or damage by fire
  • antonym: heat-sensitive - easily affected or damaged by heat
