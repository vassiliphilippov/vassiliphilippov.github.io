ungentle

adjective
not of noble birth (archaic)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdʒɛntl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈdʒɛntl̩ / (BrE)
(archaic) Not belonging to the nobility; of common or humble origin.

Examples

Ungentle birth
He was rejected from the order because of his ungentle birth.
In feudal times, ungentle origins could limit one's opportunities at court.
Some viewed the rise of an ungentle man to such a position with suspicion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gentle - of noble birth
  • synonym: ignoble - of low birth or common origin
