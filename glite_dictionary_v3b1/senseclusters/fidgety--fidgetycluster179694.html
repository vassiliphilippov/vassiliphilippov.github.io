fidgety

adjective

unable to stay still or relax because of nervousness or restlessness.

Examples

A fidgety child
He became fidgety when asked to wait in silence.
I felt fidgety all day before the big presentation.
The children grew fidgety after sitting in the waiting room for an hour.

Synonyms

  • antsy
    7.0 1.02/m
    antsy feels like small, restless impatience often before something happens, while fidgety is a nervous inability to stay still.
  • restless
    3.0 8.27/m
    restless means unable to relax or stay still over time, while fidgety suggests quick, nervous small movements.
  • jittery
    4.5 1.36/m
    jittery describes shaky, nervous feelings that show in the body, while fidgety is more about small restless movement.
  • twitchy
    4.6 0.40/m
    twitchy describes sudden small twitches or being easily startled, while fidgety means general restless movement.
  • squirmy
    3.5 0.08/m
    squirmy describes twisting, uncomfortable movements like when someone feels itchy, while fidgety is broader nervous restlessness.
  • squirrelly
    9.1 0.30/m
    squirrelly is informal and can mean oddly restless or unpredictable in action, while fidgety specifically shows nervous small movements.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restless - unable to rest or relax, constantly in motion
