nippleless

adjective
without threaded connector
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪpəlləs / (AmE), / ˈnɪpəlləs / (BrE)
lacking a short threaded connector (nipple), especially in reference to pipes, fittings, or valves in hardware contexts

Examples

Nippleless fitting
The technician chose a nippleless fitting to ensure a smooth connection between the pipes.
A nippleless valve was required for the new plumbing installation in the kitchen.
Some modern systems use nippleless couplings to reduce the risk of leaks.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: nipple - a short piece of pipe with threads on both ends
  • antonym: threaded - having a helical ridge for screwing parts together
