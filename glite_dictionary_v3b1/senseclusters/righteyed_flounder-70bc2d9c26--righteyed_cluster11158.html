righteyed flounder

noun
flounder with both eyes on right side of head
9.7 8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈraɪt.aɪd ˈflaʊn.dər/ (AmE), /ˈraɪt.aɪd ˈflaʊn.də/ (BrE)
Any of several flounder species with both eyes located on the right side of the head, commonly found in the families Pleuronectidae and related groups.

Examples

The righteyed flounder is prized by fishermen for its delicate flavor and firm texture.
You can often distinguish a righteyed flounder from a lefteyed flounder by the position of its eyes.
