fell

noun
type of seam in sewing
Pronunciation: / fɛl / (AmE), / fɛl / (BrE)
A seam made by folding or turning under the edge of fabric and stitching it down, so the raw edge is enclosed and not exposed.

Examples

Fell seam finish
If you want a clean finish, a fell seam is an excellent choice.
She examined the fell on her jeans to check the quality of the stitching.
The tailor used a fell to ensure the shirt seams were strong and neat.
