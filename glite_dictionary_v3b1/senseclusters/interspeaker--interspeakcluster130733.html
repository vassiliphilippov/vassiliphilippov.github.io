interspeaker

adjective
between speakers
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈspiːkə / (BrE)
relating to or occurring between different speakers, especially in linguistic studies

Examples

Interspeaker differences
The research focused on interspeaker differences in the use of regional accents.
Linguists often study interspeaker variation to understand language change.
The experiment measured interspeaker consistency in pronouncing certain words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraspeaker - relating to variation within a single speaker
