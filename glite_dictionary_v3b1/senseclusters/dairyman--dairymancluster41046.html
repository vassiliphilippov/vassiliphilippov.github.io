dairyman

noun
owner or manager of a dairy
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛriˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈdeəriˌman / (BrE)
a man who owns, manages, or works in a dairy, especially producing or selling milk and milk products

Examples

A dairyman on farm
Her grandfather had been a respected dairyman in the community for over thirty years.
The dairyman delivered fresh milk to local stores each day.
The dairyman rose before dawn every morning to tend to his cows.
My grandfather was a dairyman in the English countryside.
The dairyman delivered fresh milk to our doorstep every day.
The dairyman rises early every morning to tend to the cows.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dairymaid - a woman who works in a dairy
  • synonym: dairywoman - a woman who owns or manages a dairy
