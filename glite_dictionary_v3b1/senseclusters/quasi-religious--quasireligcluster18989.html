quasi-religious

adjective
resembling something religious
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkwɑzi rɪˈlɪdʒəs / (AmE), / ˌkwɑːzi rɪˈlɪdʒəs / (BrE)
Resembling or having some, but not all, characteristics of something religious.

Examples

A quasi-religious feeling
His devotion to his work was almost quasi-religious in its intensity.
Some political movements take on a quasi-religious fervor among their followers.
The festival has a quasi-religious significance for the local community.
