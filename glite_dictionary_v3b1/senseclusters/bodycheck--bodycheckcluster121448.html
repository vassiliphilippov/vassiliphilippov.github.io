bodycheck

noun
collision in sports
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑː.dɪ.tʃek/ (AmE), /ˈbɒd.ɪ.tʃek/ (BrE)
a physical move in a contact sport where a player uses their body to block or stop an opponent's movement

Examples

A strong bodycheck
The hockey player delivered a powerful bodycheck to stop his opponent from reaching the puck.
After a hard bodycheck, the referee blew the whistle for a penalty.
Players are trained to perform a legal bodycheck without injuring others.

Related Terms

  • synonym: block - an action to stop or hinder an opponent's movement
  • synonym: tackle - an act of stopping an opponent by grabbing or knocking them down
