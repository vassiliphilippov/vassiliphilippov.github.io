blowlamp

noun
portable burner for producing a hot flame
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊˌlæmp / (AmE), / ˈbləʊˌlamp / (BrE)
A portable burner that mixes air and fuel gas to produce a very hot flame, used especially in metalworking and laboratory settings.

Examples

A blowlamp burner
He carefully adjusted the blowlamp to achieve the right temperature for soldering.
Old-fashioned blowlamps are often found in antique tool collections.
The plumber used a blowlamp to heat the pipe before bending it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blowtorch - a device for producing a hot flame using pressurized fuel, chiefly American term
