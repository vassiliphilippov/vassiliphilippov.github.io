weather radar

noun
radar for detecting weather conditions
1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðɚ ˌreɪ.dɑɹ / (AmE), / ˈwɛð.ə ˌreɪ.dɑː / (BrE)
a type of radar used to detect clouds and precipitation, primarily for monitoring and forecasting weather conditions

Examples

Doppler weather radar
Doppler weather radar helps forecast rainfall and severe weather events.
The airport relies on weather radar to ensure the safety of landing and departing flights.
The meteorologists used the weather radar to track the approaching storm.
