barbless

adjective
not hurtful or cutting
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrbləs / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbləs / (BrE)
not containing sharp, hurtful, or cutting remarks; free from sarcasm or cruelty in speech or criticism

Examples

Barbless criticism
His comments were refreshingly barbless and focused on helping the team improve.
The teacher's barbless advice encouraged the students without making them feel bad.
She appreciated the barbless nature of the review, which offered suggestions without any sarcasm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gentle - kind and not harsh or severe
  • antonym: harsh - cruel or unkind in speech or criticism
