equine encephalitis

noun
viral brain inflammation transmitted from horses
Pronunciation: / ˈiː.kwain ɛnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈɛk.wain ɪnˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
An infectious disease causing inflammation of the brain, transmitted by mosquitoes from horses and caused by certain viruses.

Examples

Equine encephalitis virus
The latest research focuses on vaccines to prevent equine encephalitis in horses and humans.
Veterinarians are on alert for equine encephalitis outbreaks in the summer.
Widespread mosquito activity can increase the risk of equine encephalitis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: encephalitis - inflammation of the brain, often caused by infection
