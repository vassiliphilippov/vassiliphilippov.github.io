unfit

adjective
not in good physical or mental condition
3.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfɪt/ /; / /ə.ˈfɪt/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfɪt/ /; / /ə.ˈfɪt/ (BrE)
not healthy or not in good physical or mental condition

Examples

Physically unfit
After months of inactivity, he felt unfit and sluggish.
Many soldiers were found physically unfit during the medical examination.
The doctor declared her unfit for work after the accident.
After the accident, his leg was unfit for running long distances.
The doctor declared him unfit for strenuous activity due to his heart condition.
The neglected horse was found to be unfit because of severe malnutrition.
