avert

verb
turn away or aside
5.5 4.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈvɜːrt/ (AmE), /ə.ˈvɜːt/ (BrE)
to turn away or aside (one's eyes, thoughts, etc.), especially to avoid something unpleasant or unwanted

Examples

To avert your eyes
He averted his eyes from the gruesome scene on the screen.
She quickly averted her gaze when he caught her staring.
They averted their faces as the sun rose over the hills.
