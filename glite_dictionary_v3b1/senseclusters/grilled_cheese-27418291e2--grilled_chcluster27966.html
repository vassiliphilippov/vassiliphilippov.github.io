grilled cheese

noun
hot sandwich with melted cheese filling
2.1
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪld tʃiz / (AmE), / ɡrɪld tʃiːz / (BrE)
a hot sandwich made with bread and melted cheese, grilled or fried until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted

Examples

At the café, their grilled cheese comes with a cup of homemade soup.
I made a grilled cheese for lunch with a side of pickles.
Nothing beats a classic grilled cheese on a cold, rainy day.
