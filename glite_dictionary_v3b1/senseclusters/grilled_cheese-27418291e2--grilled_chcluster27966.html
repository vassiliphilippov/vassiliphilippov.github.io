grilled cheese

noun
hot sandwich with melted cheese filling
2.1 1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪld tʃiz / (AmE), / ɡrɪld tʃiːz / (BrE)
a hot sandwich made with bread and melted cheese, grilled or fried until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted
