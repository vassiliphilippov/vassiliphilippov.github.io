pochismo

noun
mixing English into Spanish
9.9
Pronunciation: / poˈtʃizmoʊ / (AmE), / pəˈtʃɪzməʊ / (BrE)
the practice of mixing English words, phrases, or constructions into Spanish, especially in speech or writing

Examples

Pochismo in conversation
Her writing showed obvious pochismo, mixing English words into otherwise Spanish sentences.
Some people criticize pochismo as a loss of traditional language, while others see it as creative expression.
The radio host often uses pochismo to connect with bilingual listeners in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Spanglish - a blend of Spanish and English, especially as spoken by bilingual people
  • synonym: code-switching - the practice of alternating between two or more languages in conversation
