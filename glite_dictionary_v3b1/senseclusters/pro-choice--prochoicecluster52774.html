pro-choice

adjective
supporting abortion rights
Pronunciation: / proʊ ˈtʃɔɪs / (AmE), / prəʊ ˈtʃɔɪs / (BrE)
supporting or advocating a woman's right to choose whether or not to have an abortion

Examples

Pro-choice activists
Many pro-choice activists attended the rally at the capitol.
She has always identified as pro-choice in debates about reproductive rights.
The senator clarified her pro-choice stance during the televised interview.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-life - opposing abortion and supporting the rights of the fetus
