nerf

verb
to weaken in games
Pronunciation: / nɜrf / (AmE), / nɜːf / (BrE)
to make something weaker or less effective, especially in a video game or software update

Examples

To nerf a character
After the last update, the developers decided to nerf several overpowered characters.
Players complained when their favorite weapon was nerfed in the latest patch.
If a feature is too strong, the company might nerf it to balance the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buff - to make stronger or more effective, especially in a game
  • synonym: weaken - to make less strong or powerful
