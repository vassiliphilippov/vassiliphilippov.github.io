atheromatous

adjective
relating to atheroma or fatty arterial deposits
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæθəˈroʊmətəs / (AmE), / ˌaθəˈrəʊmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of atheroma, especially concerning thickened or diseased arteries

Examples

Atheromatous artery disease
Atheromatous degeneration can lead to narrowing and hardening of blood vessels.
Calcification is often found in atheromatous plaques within the arteries.
The doctor observed atheromatous changes during the vascular examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheroma - a fatty deposit in the inner lining of an artery
