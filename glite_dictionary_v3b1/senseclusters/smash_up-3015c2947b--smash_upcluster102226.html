smash up

verb
damage or destroy violently
Pronunciation: / smæʃ ʌp / (AmE), / smæʃ ʌp / (BrE)
to damage or destroy something violently, especially by breaking it into pieces

Examples

To smash up a vase
She accidentally smashed up her favorite vase while cleaning.
The crash smashed up the front of the car badly.
The vandals smashed up the old phone booth beyond repair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: destroy - to damage something so badly that it no longer exists or cannot be repaired
