compound morphology

noun
study of how compound words are formed
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpaʊnd mɔːrˈfɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌkɒmpaʊnd mɔːˈfɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of linguistics that studies the formation of compound words from simpler words
