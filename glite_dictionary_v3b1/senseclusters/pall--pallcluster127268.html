pall

verb
cover with a funeral cloth
5.1
Pronunciation: / pɔl / (AmE), / pɔːl / (BrE)
to cover something, especially a coffin, with a pall (a cloth used at funerals)

Examples

To pall the coffin
At the funeral, they quietly palled the remains with a deep blue fabric.
The velvet cloth was used to pall the coffin before the ceremony.
Traditionally, mourners would pall the casket as a sign of respect.
