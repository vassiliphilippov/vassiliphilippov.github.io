fringed gentian

noun
blue wildflower with fringed petals
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (AmE), / frɪndʒd ˈdʒɛnʃən / (BrE)
a North American wildflower of the genus Gentianopsis, with blue, lacy-margined petals
