railside

adjective
beside railway tracks
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪlˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈreɪlˌsaɪd / (BrE)
located beside or next to a railway track

Examples

Railside shop
The new café is located on the railside of the station, offering a view of passing trains.
Workers painted the railside fence to improve the appearance of the area.
A row of railside warehouses lines the edge of the freight yard.
