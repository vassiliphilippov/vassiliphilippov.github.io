dispersity

noun
degree of spread
7.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɜːrsəti / (AmE), / dɪˈspɜːsɪti / (BrE)
the extent to which things are spread out or scattered over an area or among a group

Examples

Dispersity of particles
Scientists measured the dispersity of pollen grains in the air during spring.
The dispersity of the population made it difficult to provide services to everyone.
High dispersity in the data set indicated a wide range of results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentration - the state of being closely gathered together
  • synonym: dispersion - the process of spreading things over a wide area
