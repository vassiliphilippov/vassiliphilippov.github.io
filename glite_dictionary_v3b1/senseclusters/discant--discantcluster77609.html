discant

noun
musical accompaniment above a melody
9.9 9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪs.kənt/ (AmE), /ˈdɪs.kənt/ (BrE)
a decorative musical accompaniment, often improvised, added above a basic melody in music; especially in medieval and Renaissance polyphony.
