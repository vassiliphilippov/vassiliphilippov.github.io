mukanda

noun
African initiation ceremony
9.9
a traditional initiation ceremony for boys marking the transition to adulthood in some African cultures

Examples

Mukanda ceremony
The community gathered to witness the boys' mukanda at the end of the dry season.
After completing the mukanda, the boys were welcomed back as young men.
He spoke proudly about his experiences during the mukanda ceremony.
