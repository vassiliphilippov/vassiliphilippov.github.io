Chicana

noun
Mexican-American woman
8.3
Pronunciation: / tʃɪˈkɑːnə / (AmE), / tʃɪˈkɑːnə / (BrE)
a woman or girl of Mexican descent in the United States, often identifying with Mexican-American culture or activism
