tribally

adverb
in a tribal manner
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪbəli / (AmE), / ˈtraɪbəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to a tribe or tribal group

Examples

Tribally organized society
The community is tribally organized, with each group following its own traditions.
They are tribally linked by a common language and shared ancestry.
Political decisions were often made tribally rather than by the entire population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnically - in a way that relates to an ethnic group
