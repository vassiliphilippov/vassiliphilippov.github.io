debt of nature

noun
the inevitability of death
5.3
Pronunciation: / dɛt ʌv ˈneɪtʃɚ / (AmE), / dɛt əv ˈneɪtʃə / (BrE)
the inevitable obligation of death; the unavoidable fate that every living being must die

Examples

Pay the debt of nature
After a long illness, he finally paid the debt of nature.
It is said that kings and beggars alike must pay the debt of nature.
Writers throughout history have reflected on the debt of nature that all humans owe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: die - to stop living; to cease to exist as a living being
