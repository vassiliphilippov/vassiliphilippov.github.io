snow goose

noun
a North American white goose species
Pronunciation: / snoʊ ɡus / (AmE), / snəʊ ɡuːs / (BrE)
A North American goose with white plumage and black wingtips, scientifically known as Anser caerulescens.

Examples

A snow goose flock
Large flocks of snow geese gather in the wetlands during migration season.
The white feathers of a snow goose make it easy to recognize in flight.
We spotted a single snow goose among a group of Canada geese at the lake.
