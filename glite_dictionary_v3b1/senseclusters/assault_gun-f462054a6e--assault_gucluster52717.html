assault gun

noun
military automatic or semiautomatic rifle
Pronunciation: / əˈsɔlt ɡʌn / (AmE), / əˈsɔːlt ɡʌn / (BrE)
a type of automatic or semiautomatic rifle with a large magazine, designed primarily for military use
