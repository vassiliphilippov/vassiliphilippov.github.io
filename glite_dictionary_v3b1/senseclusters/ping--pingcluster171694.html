ping

verb
send a test message to check computer/device status
3.5
Pronunciation: / pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
to send a special signal or message from one computer or device to another, typically to check if it is reachable or active

Examples

To ping a server
I tried to ping the server, but it didn't respond.
She pinged his laptop before sending the important files.
You can ping the network printer to make sure it's online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: probe - to investigate or examine something closely, often to check response
  • synonym: test - to check the quality, performance, or reliability of something
