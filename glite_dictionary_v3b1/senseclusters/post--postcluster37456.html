post

noun
message on a social media site or forum
Pronunciation: / poʊst / (AmE), / pəʊst / (BrE)
a message, comment, photo, or other content published on a social media website or online forum

Examples

A social media post
Before making a post, always check your facts to avoid spreading misinformation.
She wrote a post about her vacation, and it quickly went viral.
The company shared an official post on its Facebook page announcing the new product.
