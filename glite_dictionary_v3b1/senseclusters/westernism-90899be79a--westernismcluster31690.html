Westernism

noun
belief in Western superiority
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstərnɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈwɛstənɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or ideology that Western political, economic, or cultural values are superior or should be adopted by others

Examples

Strong Westernism beliefs
The new government was criticized for its strong Westernism and disregard for local traditions.
Debates about Westernism often arise in countries balancing modernization with cultural heritage.
Some scholars argue that Westernism influences global education systems and media.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eurocentrism - the belief that European values and culture are central or superior
  • antonym: anti-Westernism - opposition to Western values or influence
