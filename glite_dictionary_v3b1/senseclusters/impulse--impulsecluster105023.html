impulse

noun
sudden electrical surge or wave

(electronics) A sharp, brief surge or wave of electrical energy, or a series of such transients, marking a sudden change in a circuit's electrical state.

Examples

An impulse signal
A sharp impulse was detected in the signal during the test.
The circuit board is designed to respond quickly to an impulse.
The radio receiver filtered out every unwanted impulse to improve sound clarity.
