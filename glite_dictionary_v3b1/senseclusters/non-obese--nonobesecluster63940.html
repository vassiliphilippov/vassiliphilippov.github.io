non-obese

adjective
not obese
Pronunciation: / nɑn oʊˈbis / (AmE), / nɒn əʊˈbiːs / (BrE)
not obese; having a body weight that is not considered obese, typically within the normal or overweight range

Examples

Non-obese adults
The doctor explained that non-obese patients may still be at risk for certain health conditions.
Researchers found differences in metabolism between non-obese and obese individuals.
A non-obese lifestyle can help reduce the risk of diabetes and heart disease.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: normal weight - having a body weight within the healthy range
  • antonym: obese - having excessive body fat
  • degree lower: overweight - weighing more than is considered healthy but not obese
