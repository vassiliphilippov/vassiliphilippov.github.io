non-obese

adjective
not obese
Pronunciation: / nɑn oʊˈbis / (AmE), / nɒn əʊˈbiːs / (BrE)
not obese; having a body weight that is not considered obese, typically within the normal or overweight range

Examples

A non-obese lifestyle can help reduce the risk of diabetes and heart disease.
