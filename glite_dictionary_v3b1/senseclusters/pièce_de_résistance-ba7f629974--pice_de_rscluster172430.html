pièce de résistance

noun
the main or featured dish of a meal
7.4
Pronunciation: / pjɛs də rɪˈzistɑ̃s /; / pjɛs də ɹeɪ.zɪˈstɑns / (AmE), / pjɛs də reˈziːstɑ̃s /; / pjɛs də ɹe.zɪˈstɑ̃ːs / (BrE)
the most important or outstanding part of something, especially the main dish at a meal or the main highlight in a group or event

Examples

The pièce de résistance dish
Everyone complimented the pièce de résistance at the banquet, which was an impressive seafood paella.
For the holiday meal, the pièce de résistance was a perfectly glazed ham in the center of the table.
The chef prepared a pièce de résistance of roast duck with cherry sauce for the dinner guests.
At the dinner, the chef unveiled his pièce de résistance, a beautifully roasted duck.
Every magician has a pièce de résistance that leaves the audience speechless.
The art exhibit's pièce de résistance was a stunning sculpture by a local artist.
