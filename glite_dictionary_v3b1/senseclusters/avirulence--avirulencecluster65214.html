avirulence

noun
inability to cause disease
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪvəˈrɪljəns / (AmE), / ˌeɪvɪˈrɪljəns /; / ˌeɪvəˈrɪljəns / (BrE)
the inability or property of a microorganism or pathogen that prevents it from causing disease in a host
