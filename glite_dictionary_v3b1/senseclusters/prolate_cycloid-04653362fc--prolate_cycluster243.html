prolate cycloid

noun
curve traced by a point outside a rolling circle
Pronunciation: / proʊˌleɪt ˈsaɪ.k.lɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.leɪt ˈsaɪ.klɔɪd / (BrE)
A type of cycloid generated by a point outside the circumference of a rolling circle.

Examples
