Florentine

adjective
from or related to Florence, Italy
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːr.ənˌtin /; / ˈflɔːrəntiːn / (AmE), / ˈflɒr.ənˌtiːn /; / ˈflɒrəntiːn / (BrE)
relating to Florence, Italy, its people, culture, or art; or describing a dish prepared with spinach as a main ingredient

Examples

Florentine art style

The architecture in the city center is distinctly Florentine in style.
