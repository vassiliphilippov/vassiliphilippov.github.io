overrated

adjective
valued too highly
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈreɪtɪd / (BrE)
considered to be better, more important, or more valuable than it really is

Examples

An overrated movie
Many critics believe the movie is overrated and does not deserve its high praise.
Some people think that expensive designer brands are often overrated.
The restaurant was nice, but I found it a bit overrated compared to the reviews.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overvalued - considered to be worth more than its real value
  • antonym: underrated - not given enough value or recognition
