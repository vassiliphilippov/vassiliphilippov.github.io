overrated

adjective
valued too highly
4.4
Pronunciation: /oʊ.vər.ˈreɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /əʊ.və.ˈreɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)

The restaurant was nice, but I found it a bit overrated compared to the reviews.
