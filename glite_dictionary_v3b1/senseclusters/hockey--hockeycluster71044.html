hockey

noun
ice-based team sport with puck and sticks
1.0 5.58/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêki / (AmE), / Ààh…íki / (BrE)
a fast-paced game played on an ice rink between two teams of six players, who use sticks to direct a puck into the opponent's goal

Examples
