preadult

adjective
before adulthood
6.0
Pronunciation: / priːˈædʌlt / (AmE), / priːˈædʌlt / (BrE)
describing a stage or individual that has not yet reached adulthood

Examples

Preadult frogs
The preadult frogs still have tails and live in the water.
Researchers studied the behavior of preadult birds before they learned to fly.
Many insects go through a preadult stage before reaching maturity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: adult - fully grown or mature
  • synonym: juvenile - not yet fully grown or developed
