hippogriff

noun
mythical hybrid creature
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpəˌɡrɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɪpəˌɡrɪf / (BrE)
a mythical creature with the front half of an eagle and the back half of a horse

Examples

A hippogriff creature
In the legend, only the bravest knights could tame a hippogriff.
The wizard summoned a majestic hippogriff to carry him over the mountains.
Children listened in awe as the storyteller described the flight of the hippogriff.
