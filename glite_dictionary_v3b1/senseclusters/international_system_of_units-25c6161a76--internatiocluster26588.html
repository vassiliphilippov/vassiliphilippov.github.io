International System of Units

noun
the metric system of scientific measurement
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnt̬ɚˈnæʃənəl ˈsɪstəm əv ˈjuːnɪts / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl ˈsɪstəm əv ˈjuːnɪts / (BrE)
the modern, globally adopted system of measurement based on seven fundamental units, including meter, kilogram, second, ampere, kelvin, mole, and candela

Examples

Many scientific journals require data to be presented using the International System of Units.
