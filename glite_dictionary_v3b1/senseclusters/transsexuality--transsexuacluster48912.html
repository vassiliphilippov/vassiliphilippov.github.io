transsexuality

noun
gender identity condition
7.9
Pronunciation: / trænˌsɛkʃuˈæləti / (AmE), / trænˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti / (BrE)
the condition or experience of identifying with a gender different from one's birth sex, often involving a desire to transition physically or medically
