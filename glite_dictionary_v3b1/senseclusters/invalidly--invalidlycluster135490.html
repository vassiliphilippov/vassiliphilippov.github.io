invalidly

adverb
in an illogical way
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvælɪdli / (AmE), / ɪnˈvælɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not logically valid or is based on incorrect reasoning or false premises

Examples

Invalidly drawn conclusion
The conclusion was reached invalidly because the initial data was misinterpreted.
If you reason invalidly, your argument will not be convincing to others.
The results were dismissed as they were obtained invalidly through flawed methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illogically - in a way that lacks logical reasoning
  • antonym: validly - in a way that is logically valid or correct
