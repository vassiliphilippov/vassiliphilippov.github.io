phlomis

noun
a genus of flowering plants
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊmɪs / (AmE), / ˈfləʊmɪs / (BrE)
a genus of plants in the mint family, grown mainly for their dense whorls of lipped flowers and attractive foliage
