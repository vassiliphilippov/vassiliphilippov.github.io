flair

noun
distinctive and stylish elegance
Pronunciation: / flɛr / (AmE), / fleə / (BrE)
a natural ability or talent for doing something, especially in a stylish or original way

Examples

A flair for style
His writing has a flair that sets him apart from other journalists.
She decorated her apartment with a flair that impressed all her friends.
The chef prepared every dish with a flair for presentation.
His unique flair for design makes his work stand out in the industry.
She has a flair for languages and picks them up quickly.
You could see her artistic flair in the way she decorated her apartment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aptitude - natural ability to do something
  • synonym: panache - distinctive and stylish flair or confidence
  • synonym: talent - natural aptitude or skill
