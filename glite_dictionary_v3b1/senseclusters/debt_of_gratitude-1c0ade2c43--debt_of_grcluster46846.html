debt of gratitude

noun
feeling of grateful obligation
Pronunciation: / dɛt əv ˈɡræt̬ɪˌtjud / (AmE), / dɛt əv ˈɡrætɪˌtjuːd / (BrE)
a feeling of being grateful to someone who has helped you, often accompanied by a sense of obligation

Examples

A debt of gratitude
After the surgery, she felt a deep debt of gratitude to her doctors and nurses.
He acknowledged his debt of gratitude during his acceptance speech.
We all owe a huge debt of gratitude to those who volunteered their time for the project.
