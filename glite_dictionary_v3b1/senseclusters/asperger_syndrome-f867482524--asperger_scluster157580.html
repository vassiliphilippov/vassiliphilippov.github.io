Asperger syndrome

noun
autism spectrum disorder
Pronunciation: / ˈæspɚɡɚ ˌsɪnˌdroʊm / (AmE), / ˈæspɜːɡə ˌsɪndrəʊm / (BrE)
a developmental disorder (now considered part of autism spectrum disorder) characterized by difficulties in social interaction and communication, often with restricted interests and repetitive behaviors

Examples

Asperger syndrome diagnosis
Children with Asperger syndrome may have trouble understanding social cues at school.
Many adults with Asperger syndrome develop strong interests in specific topics.
A diagnosis of Asperger syndrome can help families find the right support and resources.
Many people previously diagnosed with Asperger syndrome are now considered to have autism spectrum disorder.
The term Asperger syndrome is no longer used in official medical guidelines.
He was diagnosed with Asperger syndrome as a child, but now identifies as autistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autism - a developmental disorder affecting communication and behavior
  • synonym: autism spectrum disorder - a range of developmental disorders affecting communication and behavior
