relativistically

adverb
according to relativity theory
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˌleɪtəˈvɪstɪkli / (AmE), / rɪˌlætɪˈvɪstɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner consistent with the theory of relativity or according to the principles of relativity.

Examples

Move relativistically fast
At velocities close to the speed of light, objects behave relativistically rather than classically.
Black holes are best understood when analyzed relativistically rather than using Newtonian physics.
The particles were accelerated to energies where their masses increased relativistically.
