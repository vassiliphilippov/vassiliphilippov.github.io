fist magnet

noun
person who attracts physical fights
Pronunciation: / fɪst ˈmæɡnət / (AmE), / fɪst ˈmaɡnət / (BrE)
a person who attracts physical confrontations or is frequently the target of aggression

Examples

A fist magnet person
Ever since the incident, he's been something of a fist magnet.
His rude remarks quickly made him a fist magnet at the party.
You don't want to act like a fist magnet in that neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: troublemaker - a person who causes trouble or attracts it
