size stick

noun
tool for measuring feet for shoes
Pronunciation: / saɪz stɪk / (AmE), / saɪz stɪk / (BrE)
a mechanical measuring stick used by shoe fitters to determine the length and width of a person's foot

Examples

A size stick tool
Every footwear store should have a size stick to assist customers.
The shoe salesman measured my feet with a size stick before recommending a pair.
Using the size stick, she confirmed that my left foot was slightly larger than my right.
