malted

adjective
converted into malt
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔltɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɔːltɪd / (BrE)
(of grain) having been converted into malt by soaking, germinating, and drying, often used in brewing and distilling.

Examples

Malted barley
Chocolate made with malted milk powder has a distinctive flavor.
Many whiskies are distilled from malted barley for a richer taste.
The brewery uses only malted barley in its traditional recipes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malt - grain, especially barley, that has been germinated and dried for use in brewing or distilling
