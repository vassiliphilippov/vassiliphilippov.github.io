tarsometatarsus

noun
bird leg bone
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːrsoʊˌmɛtəˈtɑːrsəs / (AmE), / ˌtɑːsəʊˌmɛtəˈtɑːsəs / (BrE)
a bone in the lower leg of birds formed by the fusion of the tarsal and metatarsal bones
