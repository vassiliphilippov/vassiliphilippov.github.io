Synonyms

  • watchfulness
    4.2 0.31/m
    watchfulness means keeping careful eyes on surroundings for danger or change, while vigilance is the steady, ongoing readiness to notice problems.
  • alertness
    2.9 2.47/m
    alertness shows quick noticing and fast response to small signs of trouble, while vigilance is sustained watch for danger over time.
  • wariness
    5.4 0.65/m
    wariness means cautious suspicion that makes one avoid risks, while vigilance is active watching and readiness without panic.
  • wakefulness
    5.4 0.05/m
    wakefulness is simply being awake and not asleep, while vigilance is active, alert watching for danger or problems.
