avariciously

adverb
in a greedy manner
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈrɪʃəsli / (AmE), / ˌæv.əˈrɪʃ.əs.li / (BrE)
In a greedy or grasping manner, showing an intense desire to acquire wealth or possessions.

Examples

To avariciously seek wealth
He avariciously accumulated wealth throughout his lifetime, showing little concern for others.
She eyed the jewelry avariciously, unable to hide her desire for it.
The king avariciously demanded more tribute from his subjects every year.
