endways

adverb
in an upright or end-up position
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn(d)ˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈɛn(d)weɪz / (BrE)
In a position with the end or ends up; on end or upright.

Examples

Stand planks endways
She placed the planks endways against the wall for painting.
The books were stacked endways on the shelf to save space.
The logs stood endways in the corner of the shed.
