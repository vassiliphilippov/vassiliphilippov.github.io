temple of immensity

noun
grand, awe-inspiring space
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpəl əv ɪˈmɛnsəti / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpəl əv ɪˈmɛnsɪti / (BrE)
a grand and awe-inspiring space, especially one that inspires reverence, as if it were a vast sacred place

Examples

A grand temple of immensity
Standing under the endless desert sky, he felt surrounded by a temple of immensity.
The ancient redwood forest was often called a temple of immensity by awed visitors.
The cathedral's soaring arches made it feel like a temple of immensity.
