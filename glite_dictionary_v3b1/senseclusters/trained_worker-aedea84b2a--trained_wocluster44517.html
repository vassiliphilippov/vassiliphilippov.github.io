trained worker

noun
worker with specialized skills or training
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪnd ˈwɝːkər / (AmE), / ˈtreɪnd ˈwɜːkə / (BrE)
a worker who has learned special skills or received specific training for a job or occupation

Examples

A trained worker completes tasks
A trained worker can complete the task more efficiently than someone without training.
It is difficult to find a trained worker in this highly specialized field.
The company is seeking a trained worker to operate the new machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skilled worker - a worker who has special skills, training, and experience
