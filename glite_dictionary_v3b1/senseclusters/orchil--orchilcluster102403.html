orchil

noun
lichen that yields the dye archil
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrtʃɪl / (AmE), / ˈɔːtʃɪl / (BrE)
Any of various lichens, especially of the genus Roccella, that yield the purple-red dye archil.

Examples

Orchil lichen on rocks
Certain species of lichen are valued for producing orchil, a natural purple dye.
Textiles colored with orchil have a distinctive violet hue.
The extraction of orchil was once an important industry in Mediterranean regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: archil - a purple-red dye obtained from certain lichens
