flash in the pan

noun
someone with brief, fleeting success
6.0 5.7
Pronunciation: /flæʃ ɪn ðə pæn/ /; / /flæʃ ɪn ðiː pæn/ (AmE), /flæʃ ɪn ðə pæn/ /; / /flæʃ ɪn ðiː pæn/ (BrE)
a person, thing, or event that has a brief period of success or attention but does not last
