door

noun
means of access or opportunity
Pronunciation: / dɔɹ / (AmE), / dɔː / (BrE)
A means or opportunity of access or escape.

Examples

Door to success
Education is often described as the door to a better life.
For many artists, social media has opened a door to new audiences.
Winning the competition was the door that led to her professional success.

Related Terms
