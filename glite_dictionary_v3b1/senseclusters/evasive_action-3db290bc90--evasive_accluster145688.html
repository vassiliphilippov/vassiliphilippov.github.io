evasive action

noun
action to avoid harm or defeat
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈveɪsɪv ˈækʃən / (AmE), / ɪˈveɪsɪv ˈækʃən / (BrE)
an action taken to avoid or escape someone or something, especially to prevent harm or defeat

Examples

Evasive action in driving
Drivers are trained to perform evasive action in emergency situations.
She executed a quick evasive action to dodge the charging player.
The pilot took evasive action to avoid the incoming missile.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confrontation - direct engagement with an opponent
  • synonym: dodge - quick movement to avoid something
