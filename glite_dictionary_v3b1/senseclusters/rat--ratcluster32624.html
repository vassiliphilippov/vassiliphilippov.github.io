rat

noun
large, long-tailed rodent
Pronunciation: / ræt / (AmE), / ræt / (BrE)
a rodent with a long tail, similar to but larger than a mouse, found worldwide and often living close to human settlements

Examples

A rat in the house
Farmers use traps to keep rats away from their crops.
The city has a problem with rats nesting in old buildings.
We saw a large rat scurrying across the alley last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mouse - a small rodent with a long tail, related to the rat
