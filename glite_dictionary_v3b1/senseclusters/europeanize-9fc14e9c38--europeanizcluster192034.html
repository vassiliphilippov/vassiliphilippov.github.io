Europeanize

verb
make more European in character or customs
Pronunciation: / jʊˌroʊˈpiːəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / jʊəˌrəʊˈpiːənaɪz / (BrE)
to make something more European in character, customs, culture, ideas, or under the influence of European institutions

Examples

After the treaty, the administration began to Europeanise the management of its borders.

Some fear that globalization might Europeanise traditional cultural festivals.
