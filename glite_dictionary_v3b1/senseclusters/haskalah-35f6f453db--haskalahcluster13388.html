Haskalah

noun
Jewish Enlightenment movement
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːˈskɑːlə / (AmE), / hɑːˈskɑːlə / (BrE)
the Jewish Enlightenment movement in 18th and 19th century Europe that promoted education, secular knowledge, and integration into wider society
