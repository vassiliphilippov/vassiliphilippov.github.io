instrument

verb
write an instrumental score for
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (AmE), / ˈɪnstrəmənt / (BrE)
to write an instrumental arrangement or score for a musical work

Examples

To instrument a song
It can be challenging to instrument a melody originally written for piano.
She skillfully instrumented the song, giving each section a clear voice.
The composer was asked to instrument the piece for a full orchestra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrange - adapt a musical composition for performance by particular voices or instruments
  • synonym: orchestrate - arrange or score music for performance by an orchestra
