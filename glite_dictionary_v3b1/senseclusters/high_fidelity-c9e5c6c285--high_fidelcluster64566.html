high fidelity

noun
accurate sound reproduction
Pronunciation: / haɪ fəˈdɛlɪti / (AmE), / haɪ fɪˈdɛlɪti / (BrE)
the reproduction of sound that is very accurate and has little or no distortion

Examples

High fidelity sound system
Audiophiles often invest in expensive systems to achieve high fidelity in sound reproduction.
Many music lovers appreciate the warmth and clarity of high fidelity recordings.
The new speakers are engineered for high fidelity performance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hi-fi - equipment or reproduction with high fidelity sound
