Bouyei

noun
Tai language of southern China
Pronunciation: / boʊˈjeɪ / (AmE), / bəʊˈjeɪ / (BrE)
A Tai language spoken mainly in southern and southwestern Guizhou Province in China, particularly in Buyi-Miao and Miao-Dong autonomous prefectures, as well as parts of Yunnan and Sichuan provinces.

Examples

Bouyei language
He traveled to Yunnan to document oral narratives in Bouyei.
Linguists have published several studies on the grammar of Bouyei.
The Bouyei language is an important means of communication among ethnic groups in Guizhou.
