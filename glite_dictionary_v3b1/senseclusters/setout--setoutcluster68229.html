setout

noun
outline or statement
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈset.aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈset.aʊt/ (BrE)
a clear statement or outline of aims, methods, or the structure of a report, argument, or project

Examples

Project setout
The architect provided a detailed setout of the building's design for the construction team.
Before starting the project, the manager distributed a setout of the objectives and deadlines.
A clear setout in the introduction helped readers follow the report's structure.
