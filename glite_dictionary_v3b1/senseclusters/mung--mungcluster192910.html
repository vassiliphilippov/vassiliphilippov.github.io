mung

noun
a bean plant used for edible seeds/sprouts
5.4
Pronunciation: / mʌŋ / (AmE), / mʌŋ / (BrE)
an erect, bushy annual plant (Vigna radiata), widely grown in warm regions for its edible seeds, especially used for bean sprouts in Asian cooking

Examples

Mung bean plant
Mung is often used to make nutritious bean sprouts in Asian cuisine.
Farmers in the region plant mung as a valuable summer crop.
She soaked the mung beans overnight before cooking them with spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mung bean - the edible seed of the mung plant
  • degree lower: soybean - another major bean cultivated worldwide for its seeds
