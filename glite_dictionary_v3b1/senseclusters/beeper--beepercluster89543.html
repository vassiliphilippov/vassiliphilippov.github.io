beeper

noun
electronic paging device; pager
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːpər / (AmE), / ˈbiːpə / (BrE)
an electronic device that emits a beeping sound to signal that the person carrying it is being paged, especially used before the prevalence of mobile phones

Examples

A beeper device
Before cell phones became common, a beeper was an essential tool for many professionals.
Doctors used to carry a beeper so they could be reached in case of emergencies.
Her beeper went off during the meeting, alerting her to call the hospital back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pager - an electronic device for receiving messages and signals from a central source
