inspiration

noun
creative idea or work
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
a creative idea, work, or product that is the result of imaginative thought

Examples

Inspiration for success
Her new app was the inspiration behind the company's recent success.
The artist considered his latest painting to be his greatest inspiration.
The novel was an inspiration that took years of thought and effort to complete.
