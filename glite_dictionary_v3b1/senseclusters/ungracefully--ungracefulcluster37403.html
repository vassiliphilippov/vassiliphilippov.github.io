ungracefully

adverb
in an awkward or clumsy manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡreɪsfəli / (BrE)
In a manner lacking grace, elegance, or smoothness; awkwardly or clumsily.

Examples

To move ungracefully
He exited the meeting ungracefully, bumping into a chair on his way out.
She stumbled ungracefully across the dance floor, her shoes too tight for her feet.
The cat landed ungracefully after missing the jump to the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in an awkward manner; lacking grace or skill
  • antonym: gracefully - in an elegant or smooth manner
