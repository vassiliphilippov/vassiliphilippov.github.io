knight of the shears

noun
exceptionally talented hairdresser or stylist
8.8
Pronunciation: / naɪt əv ðə ʃɪrz / (AmE), / naɪt əv ðə ʃɪəz / (BrE)
A highly skilled hairdresser or stylist, especially one renowned for their talent and artistry in hairdressing.

Examples

The knight of the shears
Friends raved about the knight of the shears who gave them the best haircut they've ever had.
The salon owner is known as the knight of the shears for her incredible precision.
Whenever there's a special event, everyone seeks out the knight of the shears for the perfect look.
