irretrievably

adverb
in a way that cannot be reversed
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrəˈtrivəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbli / (BrE)
In a way that cannot be recovered, undone, or rectified.

Examples

To be irretrievably lost
After the fire, the documents were irretrievably destroyed.
He realized too late that the opportunity was irretrievably lost.
Their trust had been irretrievably broken by the betrayal.
