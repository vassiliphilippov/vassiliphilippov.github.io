English springer spaniel

noun
a breed of spaniel dog with a black-and-white coat
Pronunciation: / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsprɪŋər spæniəl / (AmE), / ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ˈsprɪŋə spanɪəl / (BrE)
a breed of dog known for its typically black-and-white or liver-and-white coat, used especially as a hunting and retrieving dog

Examples

The English springer spaniel dog
During the competition, the English springer spaniel performed exceptionally in the agility trial.
She adopted an English springer spaniel with a beautiful black-and-white coat.
The English springer spaniel is a popular choice for families due to its friendly temperament.
