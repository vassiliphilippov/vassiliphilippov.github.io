heterotypic

adjective
different type specimen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛtərəˈtaɪpɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɛtərəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
in biological classification, describing a synonym that is based on a different type specimen than another name

Examples

Heterotypic synonym name
The two plant species were considered heterotypic synonyms because they were described from different specimens.
Taxonomists often debate whether certain names are heterotypic or homotypic in botanical literature.
A heterotypic synonym can clarify the classification of organisms with multiple names.

Related Terms

  • antonym: homotypic - based on the same type specimen
