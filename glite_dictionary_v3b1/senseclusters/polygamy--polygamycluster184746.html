polygamy

noun
practice of having multiple spouses
3.8
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪɡəmi / (AmE), / pəˈlɪɡəmi / (BrE)
the practice or custom of having more than one spouse at the same time

Examples

Practice of polygamy
Debates about polygamy often involve questions of gender equality and human rights.
In countries where polygamy is illegal, only one marriage is recognized by law.
Some societies have long practiced polygamy as part of their cultural traditions.

Related Terms
