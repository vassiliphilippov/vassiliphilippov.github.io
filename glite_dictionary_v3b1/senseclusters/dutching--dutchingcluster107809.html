dutching

noun
betting strategy for profit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌtʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʌtʃɪŋ / (BrE)
a betting strategy in which stakes are divided among several outcomes to ensure a guaranteed profit regardless of the result

Examples

dutching multiple bets
He used dutching to cover multiple horses in the race.
Many experienced gamblers rely on dutching to minimize their risk and secure a profit.
Online betting platforms often provide calculators to help users with dutching strategies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbitrage - the practice of taking advantage of price differences in different markets
  • synonym: hedging - reducing risk by making offsetting bets or investments
