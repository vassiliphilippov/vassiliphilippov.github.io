geez

interjection
expression of surprise
8.8 4.13/m
Pronunciation: / d íiÀêz / (AmE), / d íiÀêz / (BrE)
used to express surprise, annoyance, sympathy, or strong emotion in a mild and informal way

Examples

Geez, that's surprising
Geez, I didn't expect the test to be so difficult!
When he saw the mess in the kitchen, he exclaimed, 'Geez, what happened here?'
Geez, you scared me when you came in so quietly.
Geez, I can't believe how late it is already!
Geez, do you have to slam the door every time you leave?
He looked at the bill and said, 'Geez, that's more expensive than I expected.'
He looked at the long line and said, 'geez, this is going to take forever.'
Geez, I can't believe how much homework we have tonight.
When the car wouldn't start again, she muttered 'geez' in frustration.
