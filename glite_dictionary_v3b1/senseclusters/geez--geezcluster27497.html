geez

interjection
expression of surprise
8.8
Pronunciation: / dʒiːz / (AmE), / dʒiːz / (BrE)
used to express surprise, annoyance, sympathy, or strong emotion in a mild and informal way

Geez, I can't believe how late it is already!

Geez, I can't believe how much homework we have tonight.
