crossed

adjective
(of a cheque) marked for deposit only
Pronunciation: / krɔst / (AmE), / krɒst / (BrE)
(of a cheque) marked with two parallel lines across it to indicate that it may only be deposited directly into a bank account and not cashed.

Examples

Crossed cheque
Always write your recipient’s name on a crossed cheque to prevent misuse.
She deposited the crossed cheque directly into her bank account.
The company issued a crossed cheque to ensure security for the payment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uncrossed - (of a cheque) not marked with lines and thus payable over the counter
