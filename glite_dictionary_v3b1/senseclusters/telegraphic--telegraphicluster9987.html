telegraphic

adjective
relating to telegraph or telegraphy
4.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /tel.ɪ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /tel.ə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (AmE), /tel.ɪ.ˈgræf.ɪk/ /; / /tel.ə.ˈgræf.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to telegrams or using a very short, concise style like a telegram

Examples

A telegraphic style

She spoke in a telegraphic manner, wasting no words during the meeting.
