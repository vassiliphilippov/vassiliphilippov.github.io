scissor grip

noun
wrestling hold using the legs to squeeze
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪzɚ ɡrɪp / (AmE), / ˈsɪzə ɡrɪp / (BrE)
a wrestling hold in which a person wraps their legs around the opponent's body or head and squeezes by locking their feet together

Examples

A scissor grip hold
Applying a scissor grip around the waist can quickly exhaust the other fighter.
He managed to escape the scissor grip just in time to avoid defeat.
The wrestler used a powerful scissor grip to immobilize his opponent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: body scissors - a wrestling hold where the legs wrap around the opponent's body
