unfiltered

adjective
not censored or edited
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪltɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪltəd / (BrE)
not censored, edited, or toned down; expressed openly and without omission or modification

Examples

Unfiltered opinion
She gave an unfiltered opinion about the movie, not worrying about what others might think.
The documentary offers an unfiltered look at life in the city, showing both good and bad sides.
His unfiltered comments sometimes surprise people who are used to more careful language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: candid - expressed honestly and without holding back
  • antonym: filtered - censored, edited, or modified before being presented
  • synonym: raw - presented in a natural, unrefined, or unedited way
