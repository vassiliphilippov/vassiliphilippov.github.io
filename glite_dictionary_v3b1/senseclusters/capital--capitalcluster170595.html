capital

noun
official seat of government (city/town)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / ˈkæpɪtəl / (BrE)
The city or town that functions as the official seat of government of a country, state, or region.

Examples

The capital city
Every state has its own capital where official business is conducted.
Paris is the capital of France and a center of art, fashion, and culture.
The government decided to move the capital to a more central location.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capital city - city where a country's government is based
  • synonym: metropolis - a large, important city, especially the chief city of a country or region
