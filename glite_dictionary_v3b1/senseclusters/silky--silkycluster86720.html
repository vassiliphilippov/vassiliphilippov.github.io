silky

Examples

A silky smooth scarf

Synonyms

    4.3 1.10/m
    silken feels soft and glossy like real silk in clothing, while silky emphasizes a smooth, touchable texture.
  • satiny
    4.1 0.41/m
    satiny looks smooth and shiny like satin fabric on dresses, while silky is more about soft, fine texture you can feel.
  • sleek
    3.5 1.75/m
    sleek is smooth and glossy often used for hair or cars, while silky refers to soft, fabric-like smoothness you can touch.
