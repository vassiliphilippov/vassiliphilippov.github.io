silky

adjective
smooth and soft like silk
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki / (BrE)
Having a smooth, soft, and lustrous surface or texture, like that of silk.

Examples

A silky smooth scarf
A silky scarf draped across the chair caught the sunlight.
She loves how her hair feels silky after using that conditioner.
The silky petals of the rose felt delicate against her fingers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or coarse surface
  • synonym: satiny - having a smooth, glossy surface like satin
  • synonym: silken - made of or resembling silk
