get off

verb
leave a vehicle; stop bothering someone
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ɔf / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɒf / (BrE)
to leave a bus, train, vehicle, or aircraft; or to stop bothering or irritating someone

Examples

She watched the children get off the train, laughing and chatting.
