toasting

noun
browning food with heat or a grill
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊstɪŋ / (BrE)
The act of cooking something, especially bread, until it becomes brown and crisp, typically by exposure to heat or a grill.

Examples

Toasting bread
He monitored the toasting closely to ensure it wouldn't burn.
Proper toasting requires attention to heat and timing.
The toasting of the bread gives it a delicious crunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baking - using dry heat in an oven to cook food
