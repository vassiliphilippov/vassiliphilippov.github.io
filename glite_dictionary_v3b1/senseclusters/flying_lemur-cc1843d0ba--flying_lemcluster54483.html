flying lemur

noun
arboreal gliding mammal of Southeast Asia
7.6 7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈliː.mər/ (AmE), /ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ ˈliː.mə/ (BrE)
A nocturnal, tree-dwelling mammal of Southeast Asia and the Philippines, resembling a lemur and able to glide long distances due to flaps of skin extending from neck to tail.

Examples

The flying lemur glides
Although called a lemur, the flying lemur is not closely related to primates.
Researchers observed the nocturnal habits of the flying lemur during their trip to Borneo.
The flying lemur glided gracefully from one tree to another in search of food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colugo - another name for the flying lemur, mammal capable of gliding
