Berber Coast

proper noun
historic North African coastal region
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrbər koʊst /; / ˈbɜrbər ˈkoʊst / (AmE), / ˈbɜːbə kəʊst /; / ˈbɜːbə ˈkəʊst / (BrE)
