soft spot

noun
membranous gap in infant's skull
6.1 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ”ft spÉ‘t / (AmE), / sÉ’ft spÉ’t / (BrE)
a membranous gap between the bones of an infant's or fetus's skull, also known as a fontanelle

Examples

Baby's soft spot
The doctor gently examined the baby's soft spot to check for normal development.
The pediatrician explained that the soft spot will gradually close as the child grows.
You should be careful not to press too hard on the soft spot on a newborn's head.

Synonyms

  • fontanelle
    9.6 0.04/m
    fontanelle is the medical term for the membranous gap on a baby's skull, while soft spot is the common everyday name.
  • fontanel
    9.2 0.05/m
    fontanel is an alternative, shorter form used in some texts, while soft spot is the familiar everyday term used by parents.
