unwell

adjective
feeling ill; not well
3.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛl / (BrE)
ill or not feeling well; suffering from a slight illness or discomfort.

Examples

Feeling unwell
I'm feeling unwell and think I should rest today.
If you are unwell, please let your supervisor know before taking time off.
She looked slightly unwell during the meeting and left early.
