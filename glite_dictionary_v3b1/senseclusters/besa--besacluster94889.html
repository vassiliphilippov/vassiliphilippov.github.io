besa

noun
Albanian pledge of honor
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪsə / (AmE), / ˈbeɪsə / (BrE)
a solemn promise or pledge of honor, especially in Albanian culture, to keep one's word or protect someone

Examples

A besa promise
In Albanian tradition, a besa is considered sacred and must never be broken.
He gave his besa to protect the guest at all costs.
The villagers trusted her because she always kept her besa.

Related Terms

  • antonym: betrayal - the act of breaking a promise or trust
  • synonym: oath - a formal promise to do something or tell the truth
  • synonym: promise - a declaration that one will do or not do something
