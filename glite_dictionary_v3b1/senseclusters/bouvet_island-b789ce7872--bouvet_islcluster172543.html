Bouvet Island

proper noun
uninhabited Norwegian island in the South Atlantic
Pronunciation: / buːˈveɪ ˈaɪlənd / (AmE), / buːˈveɪ ˈaɪlənd / (BrE)
A remote uninhabited island in the South Atlantic Ocean, belonging to Norway, located near the Antarctic Circle.

Examples

Bouvet Island location
Few people have ever set foot on Bouvet Island due to its harsh climate and remoteness.
Norway has administered Bouvet Island as a nature reserve since 1971.
