saddleback

noun
animal with saddle-shaped back
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsædəlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈsædəlˌbæk / (BrE)
an animal or object with a back shaped like a saddle, often marked by a distinct band or patch

Examples

Saddleback pig
The saddleback pig is known for its dark band across the back.
We spotted a rare saddleback bird with a striking white patch on its back.
The old chair had a saddleback design that made it comfortable to sit in for hours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: piebald - an animal, especially a horse, with irregular patches of two colors
