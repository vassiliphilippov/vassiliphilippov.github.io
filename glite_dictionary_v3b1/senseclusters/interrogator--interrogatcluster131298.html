interrogator

noun
a harsh or aggressive questioner
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɹɑːɡəˌtɔɹ /; / ɪnˈtɛrəˌɡeɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɹɒɡəˌteɪtə /; / ɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who formally questions someone, especially to get information, often in an official or investigative situation
