pawnbroking

noun
lending against property
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔnˌbroʊkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɔːnˌbrəʊkɪŋ / (BrE)
the business or practice of lending money to people in exchange for personal property as security (collateral), which can be sold if the loan is not repaid
