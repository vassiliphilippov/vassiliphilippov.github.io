control stick

noun
aircraft lever for steering
2.8 4.1
Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtroʊl stɪk/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtrəʊl stɪk/ (BrE)
a lever in an aircraft used by the pilot to control the ailerons and elevators, thereby steering the plane

Examples

The control stick in aircraft
A gentle push on the control stick causes the aircraft to climb smoothly.
She learned how to operate the control stick during her first flight lesson.
The pilot gripped the control stick tightly as the plane encountered turbulence.

  • synonym: joystick - a lever that can be moved in several directions to control a machine or computer
