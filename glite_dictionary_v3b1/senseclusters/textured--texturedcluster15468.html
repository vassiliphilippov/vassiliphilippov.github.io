textured

adjective
having a rough or patterned surface
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛkstʃɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɛkstʃəd / (BrE)
having a surface that is not smooth, often with a noticeable pattern or roughness

Examples

Textured fabric
I love the feel of textured fabrics like corduroy and tweed.
The artist created a textured effect on the canvas using thick layers of paint.
The newly renovated hallway features a textured stone wall that adds character to the space.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; free from roughness or projections
