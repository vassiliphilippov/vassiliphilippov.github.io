Advent Sunday

noun
first Sunday of Advent
Pronunciation: / ˈædˌvɛnt ˈsʌn.deɪ / (AmE), / ˈæd.vənt ˈsʌn.deɪ / (BrE)
the first Sunday of Advent, marking the beginning of the Christian liturgical season leading up to Christmas

Examples

Advent Sunday celebration
Families often start using Advent calendars on Advent Sunday to count down the days to Christmas.
Many churches hold a special service on Advent Sunday to begin the spiritual preparation for Christmas.
The lighting of the first candle on the Advent wreath takes place on Advent Sunday.
