cobblestoned

adjective
paved with cobblestones
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑbəlstoʊnd / (AmE), / ˈkɒbəlstəʊnd / (BrE)
having a surface paved with rounded stones set closely together

Examples

Cobblestoned street
The tourists enjoyed walking along the cobblestoned streets of the historic district.
Many European cities have beautiful cobblestoned squares in their old towns.
The cobblestoned alley was slippery after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paved - covered with a hard surface such as concrete or stones
  • antonym: unpaved - not covered with a hard surface; made of dirt or gravel
