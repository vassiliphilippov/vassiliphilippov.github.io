excoriation

noun
an area of scraped or worn skin
7.8
Pronunciation: / ɛkˌskɔriˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛkˌskɔːriˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
a place where the skin has been scraped or worn away, often as a result of injury or irritation

Examples

Skin excoriation
He went to the doctor to treat the excoriation on his knee after the fall.
Repeated scratching can lead to excoriation of the affected areas.
The nurse cleaned the excoriation carefully to prevent infection.

Related Terms
