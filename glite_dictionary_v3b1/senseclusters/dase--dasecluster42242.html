dase

x
enzyme suffix
Pronunciation: / eɪs / (AmE), / eɪz / (BrE)
a suffix used in the names of enzymes, indicating that the substance is an enzyme

Examples

The suffix dase
The suffix dase is found at the end of many enzyme names, such as 'lactase' and 'amylase'.
In biology class, we learned that dase indicates an enzyme in scientific terminology.
When you see dase at the end of a word, it usually means the substance is an enzyme.
