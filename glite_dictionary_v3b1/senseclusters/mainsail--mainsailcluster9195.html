mainsail

noun
principal sail on the mainmast of a ship
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪnˌseɪl / (AmE), / ˈmeɪnseɪl / (BrE)
the chief and lowest sail set on the mainmast of a sailing vessel

Examples

The mainsail flaps
After a sudden gust, the mainsail tore along its seam and flapped loudly.
The crew hurried to hoist the mainsail as the wind picked up.
With the sun setting, they lowered the mainsail and prepared to anchor for the night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foresail - the lowest sail set on the foremast of a ship
  • synonym: mizzen - the sail set on the mizzenmast, behind the mainmast
