preprimary

noun
early childhood school
3.9
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈpraɪ.mə.ri/ /; / /prɪ.ˈpraɪ.mə.ri/ (AmE), /priː.ˈpraɪ.mə.ri/ /; / /prɪ.ˈpraɪ.mə.ri/ (BrE)
a school, class, or educational program for children before they begin primary school

Examples

A preprimary class
Many parents enroll their children in a preprimary to help them develop social skills before starting school.
The new preprimary offers a creative curriculum for children aged three to five.
Attending a preprimary can make the transition to primary school much easier for young children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kindergarten - a class or school for young children, usually before primary school
  • synonym: preschool - a school for children too young for primary school
  • degree higher: primary school - a school for children aged about 5 to 11, after preprimary
