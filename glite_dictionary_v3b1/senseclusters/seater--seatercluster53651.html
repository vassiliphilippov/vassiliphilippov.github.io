seater

noun
person who seats guests
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtər / (AmE), / ˈsiːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to show people to their seats, especially in a theater or at an event

Examples

Theater seater
The seater greeted us at the entrance and guided us to our row.
After checking our tickets, the seater led us to the correct section of the auditorium.
At large events, a seater helps guests find their assigned places quickly.
