grubber

noun
cricket ball or digger
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌbə / (BrE)
a cricket ball that stays low after bouncing, or a person or thing that digs or searches for something

Examples

A grubber ball
The batsman was bowled out by a grubber that barely bounced.
He worked as a grubber, searching through the soil for old coins.
In cricket, a grubber can be very difficult for the batter to play.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bouncer - a cricket ball that bounces high after pitching
  • synonym: digger - a person or machine that digs
  • synonym: searcher - someone who looks for something
