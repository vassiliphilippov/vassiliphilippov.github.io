overindex on

verb
emphasize excessively or focus too much
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɪndɛks ɑn / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɪndɛks ɒn / (BrE)
to give too much emphasis or priority to a single factor, area, or variable, often to the detriment of others

Examples

To overindex on technology
Startups often overindex on technology and neglect customer support.
The team overindexed on marketing, causing product development to lag behind.
We shouldn't overindex on one data point when making important decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overemphasize - to give something too much importance or attention
  • antonym: underemphasize - to give something too little importance or attention
