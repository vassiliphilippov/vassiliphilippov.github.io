tamarau

noun
small wild buffalo of Mindoro
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːməˈraʊ / (AmE), / ˌtaməˈraʊ / (BrE)
A small, rare wild buffalo native to the island of Mindoro in the Philippines.

Examples

Wild tamarau buffalo
Researchers traveled to Mindoro to study the elusive tamarau in its natural environment.
The tamarau is critically endangered due to habitat loss and hunting.
Unlike the larger water buffalo, the tamarau is much smaller and has distinct horns.
