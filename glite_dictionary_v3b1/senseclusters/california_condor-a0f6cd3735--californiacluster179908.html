California condor

noun
large endangered North American vulture
Pronunciation: / kæləˌfɔrniə ˈkɑndɔr / (AmE), / kælɪˌfɔːniə ˈkɒndɔː / (BrE)
A large endangered vulture (Gymnogyps californianus) found in western North America, mostly black with white underwing patches.

Examples

The California condor bird
Conservationists have worked tirelessly to save the California condor from extinction.
The California condor is the largest flying land bird in North America.
We spotted a California condor soaring above the canyon.
