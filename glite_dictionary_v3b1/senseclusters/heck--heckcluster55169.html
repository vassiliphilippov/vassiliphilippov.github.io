heck

noun
mild exclamation
Pronunciation: / hɛk / (AmE), / hɛk / (BrE)
a mild exclamation used to express surprise, annoyance, or emphasis, often as a substitute for 'hell'

Examples

What the heck
What the heck are you doing in my room at this hour?
He said he didn't give a heck about the rules anymore.
For heck's sake, can you please stop making so much noise?

Related Terms

  • synonym: darn - a mild exclamation used to express annoyance or surprise
  • degree higher: hell - a strong exclamation used to express anger or surprise
