cart away

verb
remove by vehicle
Pronunciation: / kɑrt əˈweɪ / (AmE), / kɑːt əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to take or remove something by transporting it in a vehicle, especially when dealing with something bulky or unwanted

Examples

To cart away old furniture
She hired a truck to cart away all her old appliances.
The city will cart away your yard waste if you leave it by the curb.
The workers carted away the broken bricks after the demolition.
