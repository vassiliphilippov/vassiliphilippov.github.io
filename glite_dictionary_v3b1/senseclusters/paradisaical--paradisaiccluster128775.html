paradisaical

adjective
relating to Paradise; heavenly
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəˈdaɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌparəˈdʌɪsɪəkəl / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Paradise; heavenly or idyllic.

Examples

The garden was a paradisaical retreat, filled with rare flowers and the soothing sound of water.
