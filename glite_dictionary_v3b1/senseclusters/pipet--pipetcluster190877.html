pipet

noun
precise liquid-measuring laboratory tube
5.7 5.6
Pronunciation: / paɪˈpɛt / (AmE), / pɪˈpɛt /; / paɪˈpɛt / (BrE)
A slender, graduated tube used in laboratories to measure and transfer precise volumes of liquid.

Examples

A pipet of water
Accurate measurement of liquids is essential, and a pipet helps ensure precision.
Be sure to clean the pipet after each use to avoid contamination.

Modern laboratories often use an automatic pipette for greater precision.
