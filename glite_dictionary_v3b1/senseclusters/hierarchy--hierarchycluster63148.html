hierarchy

noun
system of ranked authority or status
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪəˌrɑːrki /; / ˈhaɪ.əˌrɑr.ki / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərɑːki /; / ˈhaɪəˌrɑː.ki / (BrE)
a system in which people or things are ranked according to their status, authority, or importance

Examples
