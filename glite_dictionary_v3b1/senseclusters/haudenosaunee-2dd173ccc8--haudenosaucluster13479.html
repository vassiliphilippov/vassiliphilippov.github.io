Haudenosaunee

proper noun
Iroquois Confederacy
Pronunciation: / hoʊˌdiːnoʊˈʃoʊni / (AmE), / həʊˌdiːnəʊˈʃəʊni / (BrE)
the confederacy of indigenous peoples in northeastern North America, also known as the Iroquois Confederacy

Examples

The Haudenosaunee Confederacy
The Haudenosaunee have a rich history of diplomacy and governance.
Many traditions of the Haudenosaunee are still practiced today among their descendants.
The Haudenosaunee played a significant role in the early history of North America.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Six Nations - the six member nations of the Haudenosaunee confederacy
