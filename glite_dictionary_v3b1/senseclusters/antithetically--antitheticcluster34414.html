antithetically

adverb
in a contrasting or opposing manner
9.1 6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tɪˈθɛt.ɪk.li / (AmE), / ˌæn.tɪˈθet.ɪk.əl.i / (BrE)
In a way that shows or expresses direct contrast or opposition.

Examples

To antithetically oppose
Her beliefs are antithetically opposed to those of her parents.
The artist’s new work stands antithetically to traditional forms of expression.
They approached the problem antithetically, leading to very different solutions.
