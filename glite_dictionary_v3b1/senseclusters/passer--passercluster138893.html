passer

noun
person passing by casually
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsər / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsə / (BrE)
a person who passes by, especially casually or by chance

Examples

Street passer
A sudden shout startled the passer walking along the quiet street.
The artist sketched a passer who paused briefly to admire his work.
The old man sat on the bench, greeting each passer with a gentle smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bystander - a person who is present at an event but does not take part
  • synonym: onlooker - a person who watches an event without participating
