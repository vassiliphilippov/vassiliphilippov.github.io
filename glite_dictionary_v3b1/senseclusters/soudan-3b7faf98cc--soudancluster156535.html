Soudan

proper noun
alternative spelling of Sudan (country in Africa)
9.9
Pronunciation: / suˈdɑn / (AmE), / suˈdɑːn / (BrE)
an alternative or archaic spelling of Sudan, a country in northeastern Africa bordering the Red Sea, which became independent in 1956.
