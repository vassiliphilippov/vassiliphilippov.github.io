taiko

proper noun
retired Japanese regent
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪ.koʊ/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪ.kəʊ/ (BrE)
a historical Japanese title for a retired regent, especially referring to Toyotomi Hideyoshi

Examples

Taiko title holder

The term taiko is closely associated with powerful leaders in Japanese history.
