godless

adjective
lacking belief in God or piety
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.ləs / (BrE)
not believing in or revering God; lacking religious faith or piety.

Examples

Godless society
In the novel, the town was plagued by a godless spirit of lawlessness and fear.
Many considered the regime godless because of its hostility to religion.
She was accused of leading a godless life, much to her family's disappointment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atheistic - not believing in the existence of God or gods
  • antonym: pious - showing reverence for God or religion
