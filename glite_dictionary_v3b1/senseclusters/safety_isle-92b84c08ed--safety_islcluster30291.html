safety isle

noun
curbed pedestrian safety zone in roadway
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti aɪl / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti aɪl / (BrE)
a curbed area in a roadway, excluded from vehicular traffic, providing a safe space for pedestrians
