swab

verb
clean with a swab or mop
3.9 3.3
Pronunciation: /swɑːb/ (AmE), /swɒb/ (BrE)
to clean or apply liquid to a surface using a swab or similar absorbent tool

Examples

To swab a surface
After the surgery, the nurse carefully swabbed the operating table.
Please swab the floors before the guests arrive.
The sailor was ordered to swab the deck until it shone.
He swabbed the counter with a damp cloth to remove the spill.
The nurse swabbed his arm before administering the injection.
To disinfect the wound, she carefully swabbed it with an antiseptic solution.
