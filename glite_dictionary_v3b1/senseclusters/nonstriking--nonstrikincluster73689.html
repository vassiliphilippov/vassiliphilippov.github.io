nonstriking

adjective
without strikes
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈstraɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈstraɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
not involving or using strikes, hits, or blows, especially in martial arts or combat sports

Examples

Nonstriking martial art
Judo is a nonstriking martial art that emphasizes throws and holds.
He prefers nonstriking techniques because they focus on control rather than force.
Many self-defense classes teach nonstriking methods for safely restraining an opponent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grappling - using holds, throws, or joint locks instead of strikes
  • antonym: striking - involving hitting or delivering blows
