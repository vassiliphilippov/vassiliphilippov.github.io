billeting

noun
housing soldiers by order
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪlɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbɪlɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of assigning soldiers to live in private homes or other accommodations, usually by official order

Examples

The billeting of troops
The villagers were uneasy about the sudden billeting of troops in their homes.
During the occupation, billeting became a daily reality for many families.
The government issued new rules regarding the billeting of soldiers in civilian areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quartering - the act of providing lodging, especially for soldiers
