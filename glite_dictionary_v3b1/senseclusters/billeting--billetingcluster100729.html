billeting

noun
housing soldiers by order
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪl.ə.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbɪl.ə.tɪŋ/ /; / /ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
