outstretch

verb
to extend farther
Pronunciation: / ˌaʊtˈstrɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˌaʊtˈstrɛtʃ / (BrE)
to extend or stretch out farther than something else

Examples

Outstretch the competition
The eagle's wings outstretch those of any other bird in the region.
During the ceremony, the dancers outstretched their arms gracefully above their heads.
The new bridge will outstretch the old one by several meters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extend - to make something longer or reach further
  • antonym: retract - to draw back or shorten
