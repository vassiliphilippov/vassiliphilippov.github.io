palpebration

noun
repeated or uncontrolled blinking
Pronunciation: / pælˌpɪˈbreɪʃən / (AmE), / palˌpɪˈbreɪʃən / (BrE)
repeated blinking or winking of the eyes, especially when uncontrolled or persistent, often as a medical symptom

Examples

Frequent palpebration
Frequent palpebration can sometimes be mistaken for habitual winking.
Her palpebration became more noticeable under stress, prompting a visit to the specialist.
The doctor noted excessive palpebration as a possible sign of neurological issues.
