universal

adjective
adaptable for different purposes or sizes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌjunəˈvɝsəl / (AmE), / ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəl / (BrE)
(of a tool or device) adaptable for use with various sizes, forms, or purposes.

Examples

Universal remote
He bought a universal wrench to handle bolts of different sizes.
The universal screwdriver comes with several interchangeable heads.
This universal adapter allows you to connect devices from various countries.
