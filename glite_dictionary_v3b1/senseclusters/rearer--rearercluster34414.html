rearer

noun
animal rising up
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪrər / (AmE), / ˈrɪərə / (BrE)
an animal, especially a horse, that rises up on its hind legs, often suddenly or unexpectedly

Examples

A horse rearer
The startled horse became a rearer when it saw the snake on the trail.
Experienced riders know how to handle a sudden rearer during a competition.
The trainer warned that the young stallion was a frequent rearer and needed careful handling.
