smudger

noun
blending tool
Pronunciation: / ˈsmʌdʒər / (AmE), / ˈsmʌdʒə / (BrE)
a tool used to blend or soften lines or colors, especially in drawing or makeup application

Examples

Makeup smudger
The artist used a smudger to create soft shadows in her charcoal drawing.
She applied eyeshadow with a smudger for a smooth, blended look.
A smudger can help you achieve a more natural effect when sketching portraits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blender - a tool for mixing or blending colors or lines in art or makeup
  • antonym: eraser - a tool used to remove pencil or other marks from paper
