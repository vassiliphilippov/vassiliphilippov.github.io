X-ray

noun
image produced using X rays
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈeks.reɪ/ (AmE), /ˈeks.reɪ/ (BrE)
a photograph or image produced using X-rays, typically to view the inside of the body for medical or security purposes

Examples

A chest X-ray
A dental X-ray can reveal cavities that are not visible during a routine exam.
After the accident, she had an X-ray taken of her wrist.
The doctor reviewed the X-ray to check for any broken bones.
After my fall, I had an X-ray of my ankle at the hospital.
The X-ray revealed a small fracture that was not visible externally.
