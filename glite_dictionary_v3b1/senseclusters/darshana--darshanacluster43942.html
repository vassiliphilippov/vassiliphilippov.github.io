darshana

noun
Indian philosophical school
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːrʃənə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːʃənə / (BrE)
a viewpoint or school of philosophy in Indian tradition, especially one of the six classical Hindu philosophies

Examples

Hindu darshana
The six classical darshana are central to Hindu philosophy.
Each darshana offers a unique perspective on the nature of reality and existence.
Students of Indian philosophy often study every darshana to understand different viewpoints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: philosophy - the study of fundamental nature of knowledge, reality, and existence
  • synonym: school of thought - a particular philosophy or way of thinking about a subject
