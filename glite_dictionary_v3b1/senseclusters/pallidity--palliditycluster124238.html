pallidity

noun
paleness; lack of color
8.7
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪdəti / (AmE), / pəˈlɪdəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being pale or lacking normal color

Examples

The pallidity of skin
After weeks without sunlight, a certain pallidity settled over the town's residents.
The pallidity of his face made everyone worry about his health.
The artist captured the pallidity of dawn in her delicate watercolor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pallor - unnatural lack of color in the skin
