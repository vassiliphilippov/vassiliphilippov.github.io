rubber boot

noun
a high waterproof boot made of rubber
1.0 0.89/m
Pronunciation: / Ààr åb.…ö buÀêt / (AmE), / Ààr åb.…ô buÀêt / (BrE)
A high waterproof boot made of rubber, typically worn to protect the feet and lower legs from water or mud.

Examples

A pair of rubber boots
Make sure to pack a pair of rubber boots for the camping trip in case it rains.
She wore her rubber boots to walk through the muddy field.
The children love jumping in puddles with their new rubber boots.
