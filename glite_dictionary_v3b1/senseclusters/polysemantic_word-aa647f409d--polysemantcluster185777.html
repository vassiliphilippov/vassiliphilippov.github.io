polysemantic word

noun
word with multiple meanings
6.3 6.1
Pronunciation: /wɜːrd/ (AmE), /wɜːd/ (BrE)
A word that has more than one meaning; a word with multiple senses.

Examples

A polysemantic word entry
A dictionary entry usually lists all the meanings of a polysemantic word.
The word 'bank' is a polysemantic word, as it can refer to a financial institution or the side of a river.
When learning English, students are often challenged by polysemantic words with several distinct definitions.
