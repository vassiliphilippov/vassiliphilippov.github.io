mutual dorkdom

noun
shared nerdy interests or traits
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːtʃuəl ˈdɔrk.dəm / (AmE), / ˈmjuː.tju.əl ˈdɔːk.dəm / (BrE)
A state or experience of shared nerdy interests or socially awkward characteristics between two or more people.

Examples

Shared mutual dorkdom
Bonding over science fiction movies revealed their long-standing mutual dorkdom.

They laughed about their mutual dorkdom over classic video games.
