pog

noun
game disc
Pronunciation: / pɑɡ / (AmE), / pɒɡ / (BrE)
a small, circular cardboard or plastic disc used as a playing piece in the children's game called pogs

Examples

A rare pog
He traded his rare pog with a friend during lunch at school.
The children collected dozens of pogs to play with on the playground.
She kept her favorite pog in a special case to avoid losing it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slammer - a heavier disc used to hit and flip pogs in the game
  • synonym: token - a small object used as a marker or playing piece in games
