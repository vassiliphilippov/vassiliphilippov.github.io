Piegan

proper noun
Blackfoot Confederacy group
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪɡən / (AmE), / ˈpaɪɡən / (BrE)
a member of a Native American people of the northern Plains, part of the Blackfoot Confederacy

Examples

A Piegan person
The Piegan are known for their traditional beadwork and storytelling.
Many Piegan live on reservations in Montana and Alberta today.
Historians have studied the migration patterns of the Piegan across the northern Plains.

Related Terms
