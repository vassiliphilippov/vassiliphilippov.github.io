queen post

noun
vertical support in a roof truss
9.9
Pronunciation: / kwiːn poʊst / (AmE), / kwiːn pəʊst / (BrE)
a vertical supporting post in a roof truss, situated between the principal rafters and providing additional support

Examples

A queen post support
The engineer specified a queen post to support the central section of the old barn's roof.
The restoration involved repairing the damaged queen post along with the rest of the wooden frame.
Without a queen post, the timbers would not have provided enough strength for the wide span.
