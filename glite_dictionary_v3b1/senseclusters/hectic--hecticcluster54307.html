Synonyms

    7.6 1.51/m

  • frantic
    3.9 7.38/m
    frantic shows extreme panic and urgent, wild action, while hectic describes busy, confused activity with strong excitement or disorder.
  • feverish
    3.8 1.17/m
    feverish suggests intense, almost sick excitement and quick action, while hectic is busy and confused with strong agitation.
  • bustling
    4.1 0.81/m
    bustling means lively, noisy activity in a place like a market, while hectic focuses more on agitation and emotional confusion.
  • busy
    1.0 79/m
    busy describes having a lot to do or many tasks, while hectic adds confusion, excitement, and strong, often chaotic, activity.
  • fast-paced
    3.9 2.16/m
    fast-paced means moving or happening quickly over time, while hectic adds strong agitation, confusion, and intense busy activity.
