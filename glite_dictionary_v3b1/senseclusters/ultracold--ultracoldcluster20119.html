ultracold

adjective
extremely low temperature
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌkoʊld / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəkəʊld / (BrE)
at a temperature extremely close to absolute zero, much colder than normal

Examples

Ultracold atoms
Scientists study ultracold gases to understand the behavior of atoms at near absolute zero.
The experiment required an ultracold environment to observe quantum effects.
Special lasers are used to create ultracold conditions for advanced physics research.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cold - having a low temperature
  • synonym: cryogenic - relating to very low temperatures
  • antonym: hot - having a high temperature
