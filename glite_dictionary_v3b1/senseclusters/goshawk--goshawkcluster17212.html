goshawk

noun
large hawk used in falconry
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːsˌhɔk / (AmE), / ˈɡɒsˌhɔːk / (BrE)
A large hawk found in Eurasia and North America, often used in falconry, with broad wings and a long tail.

Examples

A goshawk bird
Centuries ago, the goshawk was highly prized by falconers for its hunting skill.
The goshawk soared above the trees in search of prey.
We spotted a rare goshawk while hiking in the northern forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hawk - a bird of prey of the family Accipitridae
