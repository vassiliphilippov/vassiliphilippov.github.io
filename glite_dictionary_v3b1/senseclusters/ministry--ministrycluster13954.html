ministry

noun
work of a minister of religion
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪstri / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪstri / (BrE)
the work or duties of a minister of religion, especially as a profession

Examples

Her ministry work
After years of preparation, she finally entered the ministry and began serving her congregation.
He felt a calling to devote his life to the ministry and spiritual guidance of others.
Many students at the seminary are studying for the ministry and hope to become ordained ministers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clergy - people ordained for religious duties
  • antonym: laity - ordinary people, not clergy
