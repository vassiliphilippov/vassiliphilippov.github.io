batting

noun
soft filling material for quilts or upholstery
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbætɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbætɪŋ / (BrE)
soft material made of layers of cotton, wool, or synthetic fiber, used as filling in quilts, upholstery, and similar items

Examples

Cotton batting material
Cotton batting gives pillows a soft and fluffy feel.
She bought a roll of polyester batting for her craft project.
The quilt was filled with thick batting to keep it warm during winter.

  • synonym: wadding - soft, thick material used for padding or stuffing
