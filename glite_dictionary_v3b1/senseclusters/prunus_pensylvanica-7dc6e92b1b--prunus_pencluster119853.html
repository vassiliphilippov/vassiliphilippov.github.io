Prunus pensylvanica

noun
a North American wild cherry tree
Pronunciation: / ˈpruːnəs ˌpɛn.sɪlˈvæn.ɪ.kə / (AmE), / ˈpruːnəs ˌpɛn.sɪlˈveɪ.nɪ.kə / (BrE)
a small shrubby North American wild cherry tree with small, bright red, acidic fruit, commonly known as pin cherry.
