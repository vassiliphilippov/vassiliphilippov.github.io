green-winged teal

noun
a small duck with a green wing patch
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrin ˈwɪŋd ˈtil / (AmE), / ˌɡriːn ˈwɪŋd ˈtiːl / (BrE)
a small species of duck, found in Eurasia and North America, notable for the iridescent green patch on its wings
