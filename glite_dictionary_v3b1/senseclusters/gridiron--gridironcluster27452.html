gridiron

noun
cooking utensil for grilling food
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪdˌaɪrn / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪdˌaɪən / (BrE)
A frame of parallel metal bars used for grilling fish or meat over a fire.

Examples

A gridiron for grilling
After cleaning the gridiron, she used it to grill fresh fish for dinner.
He placed the steaks on the gridiron and began to cook them over the open flame.
The campfire meal required a sturdy gridiron to hold the heavy pieces of meat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grill - a device or utensil for cooking food over direct heat
