waggishly

adverb
in a humorous, playful way
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwæɡɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈwæɡɪʃli / (BrE)
in a humorous or playful manner; in the style of someone who likes to joke or jest

Examples

To waggishly joke
He waggishly suggested that the dog might be the family's true genius.
She smiled waggishly at him before delivering her clever retort.
The comedian waggishly commented on the day's news, entertaining the entire audience.
