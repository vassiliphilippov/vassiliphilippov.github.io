unfamiliar

adjective
not known or recognized
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.fəˈmɪl.jɚ / (AmE), / ˌʌn.fəˈmɪl.i.ə / (BrE)
not known, experienced, or recognized by someone; not familiar

Examples

An unfamiliar place
Many children are hesitant to try unfamiliar foods for the first time.
She felt anxious in the unfamiliar city, unsure of where to go next.
The manual was written in terms that were completely unfamiliar to me.
