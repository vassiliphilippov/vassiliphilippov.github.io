epigastric vein

noun
vein draining anterior abdominal wall
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk veɪn / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪˈɡæstrɪk veɪn / (BrE)
any of several veins that drain the anterior (front) walls of the abdomen, located in the epigastric region.

Examples

Epigastric vein damage
Damage to an epigastric vein can result in internal bleeding.
The epigastric vein runs parallel to the inguinal ligament.
The surgeon identified the epigastric vein during the abdominal procedure.
