phalarope

noun
small swimming shorebird with lobed toes
8.4 9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈfæl.ə.roʊp/ (AmE), /ˈfæl.ə.rəʊp/ (BrE)
A small shorebird of the genus Phalaropus, known for its lobed toes and strong swimming ability, which breeds in the Arctic and winters in the tropics.
