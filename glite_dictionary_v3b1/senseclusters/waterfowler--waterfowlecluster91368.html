waterfowler

noun
duck or waterfowl hunter
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtərˌfaʊlər / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌfaʊlə / (BrE)
a person who hunts ducks or other waterfowl, especially as a sport

Examples

An experienced waterfowler
The experienced waterfowler set out before sunrise to find the best spot.
As a skilled waterfowler, she knew how to attract ducks with realistic decoys.
Many waterfowlers travel long distances each season to find the best hunting grounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hunter - a person who hunts wild animals
  • antonym: poacher - a person who hunts animals illegally
