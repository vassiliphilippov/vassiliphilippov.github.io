legal transfer

noun
voluntary transfer of ownership by law
Pronunciation: / ˈliɡəl trænsˈfɝː / (AmE), / ˈliːɡəl trænsˈfəː / (BrE)
the voluntary transfer of ownership or possession of property from one party to another according to law

Examples

A legal transfer of property
A legal transfer of property requires all parties to sign the necessary documents.
The lawyer facilitated the legal transfer of the estate to its new owner.
Without a legal transfer, the change of ownership is not recognized by the court.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conveyance - the legal process of transferring property from one owner to another
