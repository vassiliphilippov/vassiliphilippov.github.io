sourdine

noun
violin mute; sound-dampening device
9.4
Pronunciation: / suˈrdin / (AmE), / suəˈdiːn / (BrE)
A device used to muffle or soften the sound of a violin (a mute).

Examples

Violin sourdine
Please bring your sourdine to rehearsal for the quiet passages.
The violinist placed a sourdine on her instrument to achieve a gentler tone.
You can reduce your practicing volume at night by using a sourdine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mute - a device that muffles or softens the sound of a musical instrument
