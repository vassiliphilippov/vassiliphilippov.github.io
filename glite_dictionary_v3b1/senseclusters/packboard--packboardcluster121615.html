packboard

noun
protective packing board
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpæk.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈpæk.bɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈpæk.bɔːd/ (BrE)
a stiff board made of cardboard, wood, or similar material used to protect or separate items during packing or shipping

Examples

Shipping packboard
The fragile glasses were separated by a packboard to prevent them from breaking in transit.
He inserted a packboard between the books to keep them from shifting inside the box.
A sturdy packboard was used to reinforce the bottom of the package for extra support.
