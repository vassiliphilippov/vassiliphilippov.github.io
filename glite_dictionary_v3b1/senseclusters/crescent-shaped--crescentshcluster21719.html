Examples

Synonyms

  • crescentic
    6.6 0.01/m
    crescentic is a direct adjective used in descriptions, while crescent-shaped is the common everyday adjective for the same curved new-moon form.
  • semilunar
    8.3 0.01/m
    semilunar is a formal scientific term for a half-moon shape, while crescent-shaped is more general and used in everyday speech.
  • lunate
    8.6 0.00/m
    lunate often appears in anatomy or formal description to mean crescent-like, while crescent-shaped is the plain, widely used word for the same curve.
  • sickle-shaped
    5.3 0.08/m
    sickle-shaped suggests the sharp hooked curve of a tool, while crescent-shaped describes the smooth new-moon curve more generally.
