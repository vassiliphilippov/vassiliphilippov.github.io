crescent-shaped

adjective
having a crescent or curved shape
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɛsənt ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈkrɛsənt ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
Having the shape of a crescent; resembling the new moon in its curved form.

Examples

Crescent-shaped leaf
Some leaves have a distinctly crescent-shaped outline, making them easy to identify.
The croissant has a crescent-shaped form that is instantly recognizable.
We admired the crescent-shaped bay as the sun set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crescent - curved shape that is wide in the middle and tapers to points at both ends
  • synonym: curved - having a smooth, continuous bend
