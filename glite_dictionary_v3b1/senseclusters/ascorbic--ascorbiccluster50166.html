ascorbic

adjective
vitamin C related
Pronunciation: / əˈskɔrbɪk / (AmE), / əˈskɔːbɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing vitamin C, especially as found in foods or supplements

Examples

Ascorbic acid
Oranges and strawberries are rich in ascorbic acid, which helps support the immune system.
The nutrition label lists the ascorbic content of the juice as a source of vitamin C.
Doctors recommend foods high in ascorbic acid to help prevent vitamin deficiencies.
