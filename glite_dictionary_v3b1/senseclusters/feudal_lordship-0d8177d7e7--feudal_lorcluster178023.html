feudal lordship

noun
position or domain of a feudal lord
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfjudl ˈlɔrdʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈfjuːd(ə)l ˈlɔːdʃɪp / (BrE)
the position, authority, or domain of a feudal lord, especially during the medieval period
