stalagmitic

adjective
like a stalagmite
9.1
Pronunciation: / stəˌlæɡˈmɪtɪk / (AmE), / stəˌlæɡˈmɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or resembling a stalagmite, especially in shape or formation

Examples

Stalagmitic columns
The stalagmitic columns in the cave took thousands of years to form.
Researchers studied the stalagmitic deposits to learn about ancient climate conditions.
The sculpture had a stalagmitic appearance, rising sharply from the base.

  • antonym: stalactitic - relating to or resembling a stalactite
