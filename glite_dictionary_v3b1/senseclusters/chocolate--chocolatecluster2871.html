chocolate

noun
sweet food from cacao beans
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑklət / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət / (BrE)
A sweet, brown food made from roasted and ground cacao beans, typically eaten as a confection or used for flavoring.

Examples

A bar of chocolate
He prefers dark chocolate over milk chocolate for its intense flavor.
She bought a box of chocolate as a gift for her friend.
The rich aroma of chocolate filled the kitchen as the cake baked.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: carob - a sweet, chocolate substitute made from carob tree pods
  • synonym: cocoa - a powder made from roasted cacao beans and used in cooking or as a drink
