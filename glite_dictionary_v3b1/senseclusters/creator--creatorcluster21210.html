creator

noun
online content producer
3.6
Pronunciation: / krice6at5br /; / krice6at59r / (AmE), / krice6at5a /; / krice6at59 / (BrE)
a person who produces and shares digital content on online platforms (such as videos, blogs, or social media posts), often as a profession or to build an audience

Examples

Online creator
The platform rewards a creator who produces daily online tutorials.
Independent creators often sell merchandise and memberships to support their work.
After the video went viral, the creator gained thousands of new subscribers overnight.
The creator of the popular channel earns revenue from ads and sponsorships.
Independent creators often diversify their income with merchandise, memberships, and brand collaborations.
She became a full-time creator after one of her videos went viral and attracted sponsors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: content creator - someone who produces videos, articles, or social media posts for an online audience
  • synonym: influencer - a person who builds an audience on social media and can affect followers' opinions or purchases
