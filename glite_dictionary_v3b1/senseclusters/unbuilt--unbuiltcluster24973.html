unbuilt

adjective
not constructed
4.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪlt / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪlt / (BrE)
not yet constructed or built; existing only as a plan or idea

Examples

Unbuilt project
The city has many unbuilt roads that appear only on planning maps.
Her favorite project remains an unbuilt museum designed for the waterfront.

The new bridge remains unbuilt because the city has not approved the plans.
Many unbuilt houses are still just empty lots in the new development.
The museum's expansion project is still unbuilt after several years of planning.
