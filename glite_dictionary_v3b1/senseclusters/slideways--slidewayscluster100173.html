slideways

noun
machine tool guides
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈslaɪd.weɪz/ (AmE), /ˈslaɪd.weɪz/ (BrE)
the precisely machined tracks or guides on a machine tool that allow parts to move smoothly in a straight line

Examples

Machine slideways
The engineer lubricated the slideways to ensure the machine operated smoothly.
Regular maintenance of the slideways prevents wear and extends the life of the equipment.
Dust and debris can cause damage if the slideways are not kept clean.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guideway - a track or groove that guides moving parts
  • synonym: rail - a bar or track that supports and guides movement
