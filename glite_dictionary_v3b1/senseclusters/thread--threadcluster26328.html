thread

noun
helical ridge around a screw or bolt
5.5
Pronunciation: / θrɛd / (AmE), / θrɛd / (BrE)
a raised helical ridge that spirals around the shaft of a screw or bolt, allowing it to fasten securely

Examples

Screw thread
He examined the thread to make sure it matched the one in the socket.
The thread on this screw is damaged, so it won't hold properly.
To tighten the bolt, align its thread with the nut and turn clockwise.
