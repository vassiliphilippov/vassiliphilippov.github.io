vena circumflexa

noun
a group of curved veins accompanying similarly named arteries
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈviːnə ˌsɜrkəmˈflɛksə / (AmE), / ˈviːnə ˌsɜːkəmˈflɛksə / (BrE)
Any of several curved veins that run alongside arteries of the same name in the body.

Examples

The vena circumflexa vein
Damage to the vena circumflexa can cause complications during certain orthopedic procedures.
Several branches of the vena circumflexa are visible near the femoral artery.
The surgeon carefully examined the vena circumflexa to ensure proper blood flow.
