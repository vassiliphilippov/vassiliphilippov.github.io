end of the world

noun
catastrophic event or outcome (idiomatic)
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈwɝld /; / ˌɛnd əv ðə ˈwɝːld / (AmE), / ˈɛnd əv ðə ˈwɜːld /; / ˌɛnd əv ðə ˈwɜːld / (BrE)
