structural genomics

noun
study of protein structures in genomics
Pronunciation: / ˈstrʌktʃərəl dʒəˈnoʊmɪks / (AmE), / ˈstrʌktʃərəl dʒɪˈnəʊmɪks / (BrE)
the branch of genomics that focuses on determining the three-dimensional structures of proteins on a large scale

Examples

Structural genomics research
Advances in structural genomics accelerate the discovery of new drug targets.
Many universities have established centers dedicated to structural genomics projects.
Research in structural genomics has revealed new details about protein folding and function.
