downheartedness

noun
feeling of sadness, low spirits
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈhɑrtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈhɑːtɪdnəs / (BrE)

Examples

A feeling of downheartedness

Even in moments of downheartedness, he tried to remain hopeful.
She battled her downheartedness by staying busy and connecting with friends.
