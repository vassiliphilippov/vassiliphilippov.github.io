petrol station

noun
place for purchasing petrol for vehicles
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpe.trəl ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈpe.trəl ˈsteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
A place where petrol and often other vehicle fuels are sold and where vehicles can be refueled.

Examples

They built a new petrol station just outside the village.
