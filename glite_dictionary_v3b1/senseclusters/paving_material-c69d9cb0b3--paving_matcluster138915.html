paving material

noun
material used to surface roads or paths
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪvɪŋ məˌtɪriəl / (AmE), / ˈpeɪvɪŋ məˈtɪəriəl / (BrE)
a substance used for surfacing roads, paths, or other outdoor areas to make them firm and level

Examples

Eco-friendly paving material
Engineers are testing a new eco-friendly paving material for urban sidewalks.
Selecting the right paving material can affect both the appearance and durability of your patio.
The driveway was constructed using high-quality paving material that should last for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asphalt - a black, sticky substance used for paving roads
  • synonym: concrete - a building material used for pavements and roads
  • synonym: gravel - small stones used as a surface for roads and paths
