comedogenic ingredient

noun
ingredient that clogs pores

Pronunciation: /kə.mə.ˈdʒen.ɪk ɪn.ˈgriː.di.ənt/ /; / /koʊ.mə.ˈdʒen.ɪk ɪn.ˈgriː.di.ənt/ (AmE), /kɒm.ə.ˈdʒen.ɪk ɪn.ˈgriː.di.ənt/ /; / /kə.mə.ˈdʒen.ɪk ɪn.ˈgriː.di.ənt/ (BrE)
an ingredient in cosmetics or skincare products that tends to clog pores, potentially leading to acne or blackheads

Examples
