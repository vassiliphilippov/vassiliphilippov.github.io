nor'-nor'-west

noun
compass point between north and northwest
9.9
Pronunciation: / nɔr nɔr wɛst / (AmE), / nɔː nɔː west / (BrE)
the compass point halfway between north and northwest, corresponding to north-northwest (NNW) on a compass

Examples

Nor'-nor'-west on compass
The ship altered course to nor'-nor'-west to avoid the island.
The wind was blowing steadily from nor'-nor'-west throughout the morning.
They sighted the coastline at nor'-nor'-west, just as the captain had predicted.
