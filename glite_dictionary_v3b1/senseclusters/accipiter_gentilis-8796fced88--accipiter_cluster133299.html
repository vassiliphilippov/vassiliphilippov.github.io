Accipiter gentilis

noun
a species of large hawk (northern goshawk)
Pronunciation: / ækˈsɪpɪtər ˈdʒɛntɪlɪs / (AmE), / ækˈsɪpɪtə ˈdʒɛntɪlɪs / (BrE)
a large hawk native to Eurasia and North America, commonly known as the northern goshawk, and often used in falconry
