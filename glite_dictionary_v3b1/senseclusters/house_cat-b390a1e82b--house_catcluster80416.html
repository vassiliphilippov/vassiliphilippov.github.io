house cat

noun
domesticated pet cat
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊs ˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs ˌkat / (BrE)
a domesticated cat, especially one kept as a pet in the home; a member of the species Felis catus.

Examples

A playful house cat
She adopted a playful house cat from the local animal shelter.
The house cat curled up on the windowsill, soaking up the afternoon sun.
Unlike lions or tigers, a house cat is content living indoors with humans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic cat - a cat that is kept as a household pet
  • antonym: stray - a domestic animal that has no home or owner
