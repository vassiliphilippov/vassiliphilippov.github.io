quinta

noun
one-fifth share
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪn.tə / (AmE), / ˈkwɪn.tə / (BrE)
a fifth part or one-fifth share, especially as a division or payment

Examples

A quinta share
Each heir received a quinta of the estate, which they divided equally among themselves.
The tax was set at a quinta of the harvest, collected by the local authorities.
Under the agreement, each partner would contribute a quinta of the total investment.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: quarter - a fourth part or one-fourth share
  • degree lower: tenth - a tenth part or one-tenth share
