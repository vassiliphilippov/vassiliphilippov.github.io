insignia

noun
badge or mark of official position
9.8
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪɡniə / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪɡniə / (BrE)
a badge or distinguishing mark worn to show official position, rank, or membership

Examples

Police insignia
Each police officer wore a unique insignia on their shoulder.
The general's uniform was decorated with several insignia indicating his rank.
The new recruits proudly received their unit insignia during the ceremony.
