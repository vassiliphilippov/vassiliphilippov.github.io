thirst

verb
to strongly desire or yearn for
5.2 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / Œ∏…úrst / (AmE), / Œ∏…úÀêst / (BrE)
to strongly desire or yearn for something, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

To thirst for change
After years of neglect, the community thirsted for change.
He found himself thirsting for knowledge that books alone could not provide.
She thirsts for adventure and new experiences.

Synonyms

  • hanker
    7.4 0.37/m

  • hunger
