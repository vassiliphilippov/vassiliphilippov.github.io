greenstick

noun
partial bone fracture
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnˌstɪk / (BrE)
an incomplete fracture of a bone, where the bone bends and cracks but does not break all the way through

Examples

A greenstick in the arm
The X-ray showed a greenstick in the child's forearm after he fell from the playground.
Doctors explained that a greenstick is more common in young children because their bones are softer.
After the accident, the nurse identified the injury as a greenstick and called for a cast.
