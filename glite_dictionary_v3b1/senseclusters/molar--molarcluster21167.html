molar

adjective
relating to the grinding teeth
3.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.lər/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.lə/ (BrE)
relating to the grinding teeth at the back of a mammal's mouth

Examples

Molar surface
Elephants have large molar surfaces to help them grind tough plant material.
In mammals, the molar teeth are crucial for processing food before swallowing.
The dentist examined her molar area for any signs of decay.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incisor - a tooth adapted for cutting
  • antonym: premolar - a tooth situated between the canine and molar teeth
