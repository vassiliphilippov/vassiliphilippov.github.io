stroke

noun
punctuation mark / used as a separator
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
a punctuation mark (/) used to separate related items of information, such as alternatives or parts of a date

Examples

A stroke in a date
In the file path, each folder is divided by a stroke.
Use a stroke to separate the day and month, like 12/03.
When writing alternatives, such as 'and/or', place a stroke between the words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slash - punctuation mark (/) used as a separator
  • synonym: solidus - another name for the slash or stroke punctuation mark
