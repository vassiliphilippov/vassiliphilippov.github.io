mirthfulness

noun
great merriment; joyful laughter

Pronunciation: / ˈmɝθ.fəl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːθ.fəl.nəs / (BrE)

Examples

Full mirthfulness

His mirthfulness was contagious, spreading laughter throughout the room.
The children’s mirthfulness made the entire playground come alive.
