huh

interjection
expression of disbelief
8.2
Pronunciation: / hʌ / (AmE), / hʌ / (BrE)
used to express disbelief, doubt, or mild contempt in response to something said.

Examples

Huh, really?
He told me he finished the project in one hour, and I just said huh.
When she heard the unbelievable news, she replied with a skeptical huh.
If that's really your plan, then huh, good luck with that.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eh - used to request repetition or express indifference
  • antonym: wow - used to express surprise or admiration
