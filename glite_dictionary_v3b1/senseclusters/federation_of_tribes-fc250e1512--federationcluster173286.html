federation of tribes

noun
an alliance or union of tribes
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən əv traɪbz / (AmE), / ˌfɛdəˈreɪʃən ɒv traɪbz / (BrE)
a formal alliance or union of multiple tribes, especially in historical or cultural contexts such as among Native American peoples
