champa

noun
tropical flowering plant
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌmpə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌmpə / (BrE)
a tropical flowering plant, often referring to the frangipani or plumeria, known for its fragrant blossoms

Examples

A champa tree
The sweet fragrance of champa filled the air during the summer evening.
She planted a champa tree in her backyard to enjoy its beautiful flowers.
Many temples decorate their altars with fresh champa blossoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frangipani - a tropical tree with fragrant flowers, also called plumeria
  • synonym: plumeria - a genus of tropical flowering plants with fragrant blossoms
