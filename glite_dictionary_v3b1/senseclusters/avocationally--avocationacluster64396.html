avocationally

adverb
as a hobby
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəli / (AmE), / ˌævəˈkeɪʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to a hobby or activity done for pleasure rather than as a profession

Examples

To work avocationally
He writes poetry avocationally, finding inspiration in his daily life.
Many people pursue photography avocationally alongside their regular jobs.
She learned to play the piano avocationally rather than through formal training.

Related Terms

  • antonym: professionally - in a way that relates to paid work or a career
