deafly

adverb
as if not hearing
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛfli / (AmE), / ˈdɛfli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows an inability or unwillingness to hear or respond.

Examples

To deafly ignore cries
She deafly walked past the cries for help, pretending not to notice.
The manager deafly dismissed the team's concerns during the meeting.
He deafly continued his work, ignoring the loud construction noise outside.
