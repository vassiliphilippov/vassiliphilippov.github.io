Dactylopterus

noun
genus of flying gurnard fish
Pronunciation: / ˌdæk.tɪˈlɑːp.tər.əs /; / ˌdæk.tɪˈlɑːp.tɚ.əs / (AmE), / ˌdaktɪˈlɒptərəs /; / ˌdæk.tɪˈlɒp.tər.əs / (BrE)
a genus of marine fish (flying gurnards) known for their large, wing-like pectoral fins
