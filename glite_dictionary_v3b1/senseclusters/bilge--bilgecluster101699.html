bilge

noun
enclosed lowest area inside a ship's hull
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /bɪldʒ/ (AmE), /bɪldʒ/ (BrE)
the lowest part of a ship's hull, where the sides meet the bottom

Examples

After the storm, the crew inspected the bilges for any signs of leakage or damage.

Water often accumulates in the bilges and must be pumped out regularly for safety.
Inspecting the bilge is a routine part of ship maintenance.
