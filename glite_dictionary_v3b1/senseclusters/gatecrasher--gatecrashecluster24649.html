gatecrasher

noun
uninvited person at a party or event
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌkræʃər / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkraʃə / (BrE)
a person who attends a party or event without being invited or without paying

Examples

A gatecrasher at party
Security caught a gatecrasher trying to sneak into the exclusive event.
The gatecrasher managed to slip into the wedding unnoticed.
We asked the gatecrasher to leave the party immediately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intruder - a person who enters a place without permission
