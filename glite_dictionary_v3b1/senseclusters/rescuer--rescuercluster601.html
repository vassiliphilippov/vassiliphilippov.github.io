rescuer

noun
person who saves from danger
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛskjuɚ /; / ˈrɛskjuːər / (AmE), / ˈrɛskjuːə / (BrE)
a person who saves someone or something from danger or harm

Examples

Mountain rescuers
Firefighters acted as rescuers during the dramatic mountain rescue.
She always dreamed of becoming a rescuer and helping people in emergencies.
The rescuer pulled the dog out of the freezing river just in time.
The firefighter was hailed as a rescuer after pulling the child from the burning house.
The stranded sailor waited anxiously for a rescuer to spot his signal flare.
Without a rescuer arriving in time, the hikers could have been lost in the mountains.
