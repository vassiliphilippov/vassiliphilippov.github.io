obnoxiously

adverb
in an extremely unpleasant manner
Pronunciation: / əbˈnɑk.ʃəs.li / (AmE), / əbˈnɒk.ʃəs.li / (BrE)
In a way that is extremely unpleasant or annoying.

Examples

To obnoxiously boast
He obnoxiously boasted about his achievements at every opportunity.
She obnoxiously interrupted every speaker during the meeting.
The comedian obnoxiously insulted the audience, making everyone uncomfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obnoxious - extremely unpleasant or offensive
