giant silkworm

noun
large saturniid moth larva that spins strong silk
5.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒaɪ.ənt ˈsɪl.kwɜːrm/ /; / /dʒaɪnt ˈsɪl.kwɜːrm/ (AmE), /ˈdʒaɪ.ənt ˈsɪl.kwɜːm/ /; / /dʒaɪnt ˈsɪl.kwɜːm/ (BrE)
