storax family

noun
family of shrubs and trees in order Ebenales
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔˌræks ˈfæməli / (AmE), / ˈstɔːraks ˈfæməli / (BrE)
a family of widely distributed shrubs and trees, belonging to the order Ebenales, known for species such as the snowbell and storax trees

Examples

Storax family shrubs
Botanists recently discovered a new shrub belonging to the storax family.

The storax family includes many ornamental trees popular for their fragrant flowers.
