wimpled

adjective
with folded fabric
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪmpəld / (AmE), / ˈwɪmpəld / (BrE)
having folds, pleats, or gathered fabric resembling the folds of a wimple

Examples

Wimpled curtains
The wimpled curtains framed the window in soft, elegant folds.
She wore a wimpled veil that draped gracefully over her shoulders.
The artist painted a wimpled cloth to show the delicate texture of the fabric.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pleated - having fabric folded into pleats
  • antonym: smooth - having an even, flat surface without folds
