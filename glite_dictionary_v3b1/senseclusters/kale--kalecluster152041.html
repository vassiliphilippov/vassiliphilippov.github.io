kale

noun
a leafy green vegetable (curly cabbage)
Pronunciation: / keɪl / (AmE), / keɪl / (BrE)
a hardy, curly-leafed green vegetable, a type of cabbage, often eaten as food

Examples

He added chopped kale to the soup for extra flavor and nutrition.
