stumping

noun
campaigning with political speeches
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstʌmpɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of campaigning by making political speeches, especially during an election

Examples

Political stumping tour
During election season, stumping becomes a major part of every politician’s schedule.
His stumping in small towns was credited with boosting his popularity.
The candidate spent weeks stumping across rural communities to connect with voters.
