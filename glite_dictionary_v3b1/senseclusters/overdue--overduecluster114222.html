overdue

adjective
past due; late
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈdu / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈdjuː / (BrE)
not done, paid, or returned by the expected or scheduled time; past due

Examples

An overdue report
His report is overdue, and the manager has already asked about it.
The library book is overdue and needs to be returned immediately.
You will be charged a fee for any overdue payments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: late - after the expected or usual time; tardy
  • antonym: punctual - happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time
