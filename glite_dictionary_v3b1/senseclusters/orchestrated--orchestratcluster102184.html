orchestrated

adjective
arranged for orchestra
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrkəˌstreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈɔːkɪstreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of music) arranged for performance by an orchestra.

Examples

Orchestrated music arrangement
Many of Gershwin's songs have been orchestrated by other composers for larger ensembles.
The film score was originally written for piano but later orchestrated for a big band.
The piece was beautifully orchestrated for a full symphony orchestra.
