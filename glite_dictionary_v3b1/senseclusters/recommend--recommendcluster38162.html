recommend

verb
advise or suggest as best course
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛk.əˈmɛnd / (AmE), / ˌrɛk.əˈmɛnd / (BrE)
to say that someone or something is good or suitable, or to suggest something as a good choice or course of action

Examples

Doctors recommend exercise
Doctors often recommend regular exercise for a healthy lifestyle.
I recommend that you try the new Italian restaurant downtown.
She recommended taking an umbrella, as it looked like it might rain.
I would recommend this book to anyone interested in history.
Many doctors recommend daily exercise to improve overall health.
She recommended a new restaurant that recently opened downtown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advise - to give advice to someone
  • antonym: discourage - to advise against something
  • antonym: disparage - to speak about someone or something in a derogatory or negative way
  • synonym: endorse - to publicly express support or approval for someone or something
  • synonym: suggest - to mention as a possible option or idea
