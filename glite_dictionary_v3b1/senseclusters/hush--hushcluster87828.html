hush

noun
tranquil silence; calm quiet
4.5
Pronunciation: / hʌʃ / (AmE), / hʌʃ / (BrE)
a state of calm and peaceful silence, especially as described in literary or poetic language

Examples

A gentle hush
As the sun set behind the hills, a gentle hush fell over the village.
She was moved by the hush of the snowy landscape stretching out before her.
There was a profound hush in the old church before the ceremony began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noise - unwanted or unpleasant sound
  • synonym: silence - the absence of sound
