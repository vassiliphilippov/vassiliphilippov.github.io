Dadaist

noun
Dada movement artist
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːdəˌɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɑːdəˌɪst / (BrE)
a person who creates, supports, or is associated with the Dada art movement, which rejected traditional artistic values
