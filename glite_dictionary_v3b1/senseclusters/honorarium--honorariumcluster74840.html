honorarium

noun
payment for voluntary or free service
6.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /ɑː.nər.ˈer.iː.əm/ (AmE), /ɒ.nə.ˈreə.ri.əm/ (BrE)
a payment given for services for which no official fee is required, typically as a gesture of appreciation

Examples

A small honorarium
Although the work was voluntary, the charity offered a small honorarium to its speakers.
An honorarium was included to recognize the expert's contribution to the panel discussion.
The guest lecturer received an honorarium for her talk at the university.
