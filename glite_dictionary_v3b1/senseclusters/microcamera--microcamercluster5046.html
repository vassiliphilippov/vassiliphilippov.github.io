microcamera

noun
tiny camera device
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌkæmərə / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌkæmərə / (BrE)
a very small camera used for detailed recording, inspection, or built into devices for discreet or specialized purposes

Examples

A microcamera device
The scientist attached a microcamera to the insect to study its movement patterns.
Security experts often use a microcamera to monitor sensitive areas without being noticed.
She hid a microcamera inside her bag to record the meeting discreetly.
The surgeon used a microcamera to see inside the patient's knee during the operation.
A microcamera can help engineers inspect the inside of machinery without taking it apart.
Thanks to the microcamera, the technician quickly located the blockage in the pipe.
The security system uses a hidden microcamera to monitor the entrance discreetly.
Engineers installed a microcamera inside the robot to help it navigate tight spaces.
Her new glasses feature a built-in microcamera for hands-free photography.
