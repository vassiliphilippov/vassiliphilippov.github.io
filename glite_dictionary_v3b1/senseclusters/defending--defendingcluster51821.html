defending

adjective
attempting to prevent an opponent from scoring
2.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a player, team, or strategy) attempting to prevent an opponent from winning or scoring, especially in sports or games.

Examples

The defending champions
As the defending champions, they were determined not to give up their title easily.
Our defending players worked tirelessly to block every shot on goal.
The team's defending tactics kept their opponents from scoring for most of the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attacking - attempting to win or score; offensive play in sports or games
