natural

adjective
not artificial; as found in nature
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃ.ɚ.əl /; / ˈnætʃərəl /; / ˈnætʃ(ə)rəl /; / ˈnætʃɚəl / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ.ər.əl /; / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl /; / ˈnatʃrəl / (BrE)
existing or produced by nature; not artificial, processed, or supernatural

Examples

Natural beauty
His response was completely natural, showing no sign of pretense.
She prefers a natural look instead of wearing heavy makeup.
The room was filled with natural light coming from the large windows.
Granite is a natural stone used in construction and home décor.
Scientists study natural phenomena such as earthquakes and volcanic eruptions to understand the Earth’s processes.
The reserve is home to a wide variety of natural habitats, including forests, wetlands, and grasslands.
She prefers to use natural ingredients instead of artificial chemicals in her recipes.
The scientists were searching for a natural explanation rather than a supernatural one.
There was nothing magical about the event; everything had a natural cause.
