May beetle

noun
North American leaf-eating beetle found in late spring
7.9 5.8
Pronunciation: /meɪ ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /meɪ ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
a large beetle that appears in late spring, whose larvae feed on plant roots and can damage vegetation
