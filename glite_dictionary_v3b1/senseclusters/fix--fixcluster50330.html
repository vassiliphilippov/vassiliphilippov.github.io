fix

verb
to fasten or direct firmly
Pronunciation: / fɪks / (AmE), / fɪks / (BrE)
to direct or fasten (something, such as your eyes or attention) firmly in a particular position or on an object

Examples

To fix your eyes
Before speaking, he fixed his attention on the document in front of him.
She tried to fix her eyes on the distant lighthouse through the fog.
The teacher asked the students to fix their gaze on the diagram on the board.
