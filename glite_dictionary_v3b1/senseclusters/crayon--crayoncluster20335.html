crayon

noun
colored wax stick for drawing or writing
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪˌɑn / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.ɒn / (BrE)
a stick of colored wax, chalk, or other material used for writing or drawing, especially by children

Examples

A red crayon
He bought a box of twenty-four crayons for his art class.
She used a red crayon to draw a heart on the paper.
The children scattered crayons across the table as they colored their pictures.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: marker - a pen with a thick, colored tip used for drawing or writing
  • synonym: pencil - an implement for writing or drawing, consisting of a thin stick of graphite or a similar substance inside a long thin piece of wood or a mechanical holder
