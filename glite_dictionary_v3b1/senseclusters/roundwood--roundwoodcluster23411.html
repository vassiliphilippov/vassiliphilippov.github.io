roundwood

noun
cylindrical wood pieces
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈraʊn.dwʊd/ /; / /ˈraʊnd.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈraʊn.dwʊd/ /; / /ˈraʊnd.wʊd/ (BrE)
wood in the form of whole, unprocessed logs, stems, or branches, used as raw material or for construction (such as poles or posts)
