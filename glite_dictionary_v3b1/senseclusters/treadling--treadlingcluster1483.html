treadling

verb
to use a foot pedal
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtred.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtred.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
to operate a machine or device by pressing a treadle (foot pedal) repeatedly with the foot.

Examples

To treadle a machine
She spent the afternoon treadling the sewing machine to finish her dress.
The old loom requires constant treadling to keep the weaving process smooth.
He became tired after hours of treadling the spinning wheel.
