reenter

verb
to return to atmosphere
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɛntər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛntə / (BrE)
to move back into a planet's atmosphere from space, especially referring to spacecraft or objects returning from orbit

Examples

To reenter Earth's atmosphere
The spacecraft will reenter Earth's atmosphere after completing its mission in orbit.
Engineers monitored the capsule as it prepared to reenter at a precise angle.
Debris from the satellite is expected to reenter the atmosphere and burn up tonight.
