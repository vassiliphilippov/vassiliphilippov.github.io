noncirculating

adjective
not for borrowing
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsɜrˈkjuːleɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsɜːˈkjʊleɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not allowed to be borrowed or taken out, especially referring to library materials that must stay on the premises

Examples

Noncirculating library books
The rare manuscripts are noncirculating and can only be viewed in the special collections room.
All items marked as noncirculating must remain inside the library at all times.
Because the atlas is noncirculating, you will need to use it here rather than check it out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: circulating - allowed to be borrowed or taken out
  • synonym: reference - library materials used only within the library
