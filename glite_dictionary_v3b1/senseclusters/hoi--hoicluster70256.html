hoi

interjection
informal greeting
Pronunciation: / hɔɪ / (AmE), / hɔɪ / (BrE)
an informal greeting used to say hello, similar to 'hi' or 'hey'

Examples

Hoi everyone
When she entered the room, she waved and said hoi to everyone.
He greeted his friends with a cheerful hoi as he joined the group.
You can simply say hoi when you meet someone in an informal setting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goodbye - a farewell or parting phrase
  • synonym: hey - an informal greeting or way to attract attention
  • synonym: hi - an informal greeting or expression of hello
