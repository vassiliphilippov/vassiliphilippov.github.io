space agency

noun
organization for space exploration
Pronunciation: / speɪs ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (AmE), / speɪs ˈeɪ.dʒən.si / (BrE)
an organization responsible for the planning and execution of activities related to the exploration and use of outer space.

Examples

The space agency NASA
NASA is the best-known space agency in the United States.
Several countries have their own space agency to conduct research and launch satellites.
The space agency announced its plans for a new Mars mission next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space program - a planned series of activities relating to space travel or exploration
