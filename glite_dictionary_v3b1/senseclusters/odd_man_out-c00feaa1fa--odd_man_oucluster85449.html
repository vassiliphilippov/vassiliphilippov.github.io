odd man out

noun
person who stands out as different
5.2
Pronunciation: / ɑd mæn aʊt / (AmE), / ɒd mæn aʊt / (BrE)
a person who is noticeably different from those around them, especially regarded as eccentric or unusual

Examples

The odd man out
During family gatherings, Uncle James is always the odd man out because of his unusual hobbies.
Ever since she dyed her hair bright green, she has been the odd man out at the office.
In a group of athletes, the artist felt like the odd man out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: misfit - a person whose behavior or attitude sets them apart from others
  • synonym: outsider - a person who does not belong to a group or organization
  • antonym: team player - a person who works well with others in a group
