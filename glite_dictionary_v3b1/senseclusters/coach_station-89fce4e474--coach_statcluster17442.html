coach station

noun
terminal for long-distance buses
Pronunciation: / koʊtʃ ˈsteɪʃən / (AmE), / kəʊtʃ ˈsteɪʃən / (BrE)
A terminal where long-distance buses (coaches) pick up and drop off passengers.

Examples

The coach station
Please meet me outside the coach station when you get into town.
The city’s main coach station is located near the central railway station.
We arrived at the coach station just in time to catch our bus to London.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bus station - a terminal where urban or regional buses pick up and drop off passengers
