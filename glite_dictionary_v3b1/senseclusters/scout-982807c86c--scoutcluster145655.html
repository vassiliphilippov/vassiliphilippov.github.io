Scout

noun
member of the Scouting movement
2.5
Pronunciation: / skaʊt / (AmE), / skaʊt / (BrE)
a member of the Scouting movement, an international organization for young people fostering outdoor skills, teamwork, and leadership

Examples

A Scout troop
As a Scout, he learned valuable survival skills and the importance of teamwork.
She became a Scout at the age of ten and loved camping trips with her friends.
The Scouts volunteered to help clean up the local park last weekend.
