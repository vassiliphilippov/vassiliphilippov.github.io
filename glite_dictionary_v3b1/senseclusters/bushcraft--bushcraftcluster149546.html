bushcraft

noun
wilderness survival skills
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃkræft / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃkrɑːft / (BrE)
the skills and knowledge required to survive and live comfortably in the wild using natural resources

Examples

bushcraft skills
He spent years mastering bushcraft before leading expeditions into remote forests.
Learning bushcraft can help you build shelters and find food in the wild.
Many outdoor enthusiasts practice bushcraft to connect more deeply with nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: campcraft - skills for living and camping outdoors
  • synonym: survival - the act of staying alive in difficult conditions
