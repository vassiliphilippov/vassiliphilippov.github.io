microtia

noun
underdeveloped external ear
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈkroʊʃə /; / maɪˈkroʊʃiə / (AmE), / maɪˈkrəʊtiə /; / maɪˈkrəʊʃiə / (BrE)
a medical condition present at birth in which the external ear is small and underdeveloped
