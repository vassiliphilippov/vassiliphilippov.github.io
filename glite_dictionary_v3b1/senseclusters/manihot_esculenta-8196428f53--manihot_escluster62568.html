Manihot esculenta

noun
tropical plant; source of cassava root
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmæ.nɪ.hɑːt es.kjʊ.ˈlen.tə/ (AmE), /ˈmæ.nɪ.hɒt es.kjʊ.ˈlen.tə/ (BrE)
A tropical plant also known as cassava, cultivated for its starchy, edible root, used as food and for making products like tapioca.
