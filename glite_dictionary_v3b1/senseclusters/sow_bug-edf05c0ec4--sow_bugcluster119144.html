sow bug

noun
a terrestrial isopod crustacean
Pronunciation: / soʊ bʌɡ / (AmE), / səʊ bʌɡ / (BrE)
A small terrestrial isopod crustacean with an oval, segmented body, commonly found under stones or logs.

Examples

A sow bug under stones
A sow bug curls up when it feels threatened.
The children collected sow bugs for their science project.
We found a sow bug hiding under a piece of damp bark in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pill bug - a terrestrial isopod that can roll into a ball
  • synonym: woodlouse - a terrestrial isopod crustacean, also known as pill bug or sow bug
