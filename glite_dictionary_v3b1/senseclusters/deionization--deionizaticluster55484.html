deionization

noun
removal of ions
7.1
Pronunciation: /diː.aɪ.ə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.aɪ.ə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.aɪ.ə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /dɪ.aɪ.ə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)

Examples

Deionization of water

Industrial plants often use deionization to ensure their water supply is free of impurities.
After deionization, the water can be safely used in sensitive electronic manufacturing.
