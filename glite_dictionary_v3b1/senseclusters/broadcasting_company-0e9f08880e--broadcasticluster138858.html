broadcasting company

noun
company managing TV or radio stations
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔdˌkæstɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdkɑːstɪŋ ˈkʌmpəni / (BrE)
