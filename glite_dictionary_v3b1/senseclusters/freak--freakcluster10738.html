freak

noun
person obsessively devoted to something
1.5
Pronunciation: / frik / (AmE), / friːk / (BrE)
A person who is extremely enthusiastic or obsessed with a particular activity, subject, or interest.

Examples

A sports freak
As a comic book freak, she owns thousands of rare issues from around the world.
He’s a real freak when it comes to mountain biking—he rides almost every day.
My brother’s always been a fitness freak, spending hours in the gym every week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: addict - A person who is unable to stop doing or using something, especially something harmful.
  • synonym: fanatic - A person filled with excessive zeal, especially for an extreme cause or activity.
  • synonym: junkie - A person with a compulsive habit or obsession, often used informally.
