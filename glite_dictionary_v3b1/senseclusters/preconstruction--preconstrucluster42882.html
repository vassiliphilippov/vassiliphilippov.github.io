preconstruction

noun
planning before building
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkənˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːkənˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
the planning and preparation phase that takes place before actual building work begins on a construction project

Examples

Preconstruction phase
The architect presented the final designs during the preconstruction meeting.
Accurate cost estimates are crucial in the preconstruction stage of any project.
They identified potential risks early in the preconstruction process.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: construction - the process of building something, especially large structures
  • synonym: planning - the process of making plans for something
