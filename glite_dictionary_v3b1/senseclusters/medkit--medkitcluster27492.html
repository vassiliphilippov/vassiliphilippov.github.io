medkit

noun
emergency medical pack
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛdˌkɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛdˌkɪt / (BrE)
a small pack containing basic medical supplies used for treating minor injuries or emergencies

Examples

A medkit for cuts
Before hiking, she made sure to pack a medkit in her backpack.
The video game character used a medkit to restore his health after the battle.
Every car should have a medkit in case of minor accidents on the road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: first aid kit - a box or bag containing basic medical supplies for emergencies
