Polypodium polypodioides

noun
a species of fern with greyish, scurfy fronds
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑː.lɪˈpoʊ.di.əm ˌpɑː.lɪˌpoʊ.diˈoʊ.diz / (AmE), / ˌpɒ.lɪˈpəʊ.di.əm ˌpɒ.lɪˌpəʊ.dɪˈiː.dɪz / (BrE)
A fern species found in the Americas and South Africa, growing on rocks or tree trunks, with fronds that are greyish and scurfy underneath.
