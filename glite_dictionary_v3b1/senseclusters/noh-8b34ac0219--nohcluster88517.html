Noh

noun
Japanese masked drama
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊ / (AmE), / nəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese musical drama featuring masked actors and highly stylized movement

Examples

Noh theater performance
During their visit to Kyoto, they watched a famous Noh play at a historic theater.
The actors in Noh performances wear beautiful masks to express different emotions.
Many students study Noh to learn about traditional Japanese culture and performing arts.
