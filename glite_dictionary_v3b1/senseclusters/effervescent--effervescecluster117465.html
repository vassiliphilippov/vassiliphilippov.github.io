effervescent

adjective
giving off bubbles (liquid)
Pronunciation: / ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt / (AmE), / ˌɛfəˈvɛsənt / (BrE)
(of a liquid) fizzy or sparkling; giving off bubbles

Examples

Effervescent drink
Champagne is known for its effervescent quality and lively bubbles.
The effervescent tablet fizzed rapidly as it dissolved in water.
When she poured the soda into the glass, it became immediately effervescent.
The effervescent water left a pleasant tingling sensation on my tongue.
They celebrated with an effervescent champagne toast at midnight.
This winery produces an effervescent rosé that is perfect for summer picnics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flat - (of a beverage) lacking bubbles or fizz
  • synonym: sparkling - (of a liquid) containing bubbles of gas; fizzy
