mouse deer

noun
small, hornless, deer-like ruminant
7.6
Pronunciation: / maʊs dɪr / (AmE), / maʊs dɪə / (BrE)
a very small, hornless, deer-like ruminant found in tropical Asia and West Africa, belonging to the family Tragulidae

Examples

The mouse deer animal
In folklore, the mouse deer is often portrayed as clever and quick-witted.
Researchers tracked a mouse deer through the dense rainforests of Borneo.
The mouse deer is one of the smallest hoofed mammals in the world.
