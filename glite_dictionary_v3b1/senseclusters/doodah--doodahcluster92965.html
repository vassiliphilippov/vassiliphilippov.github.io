doodah

interjection
nonsense exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈduːˌdɑː / (AmE), / ˈduːˌdɑː / (BrE)
a cheerful, meaningless exclamation or refrain often used in songs or playful speech

Examples

A doodah cheer
The children sang the chorus, adding a loud doodah at the end of each line.
Whenever he was in a good mood, he would walk around humming and saying doodah to himself.
During the parade, the crowd joined in with a cheerful doodah as the band passed by.
