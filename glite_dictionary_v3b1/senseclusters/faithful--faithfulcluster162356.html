faithful

adjective
loyal and steadfast; showing allegiance
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪθ.fəl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪθ.fəl / (BrE)
remaining loyal and steadfast in affection or allegiance to a person, cause, or duty

Examples

A faithful friend
After decades together, he remained faithful to his wife.
Her dog was always faithful and never left her side.
The company rewarded its faithful employees with long-service awards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loyal - showing firm and constant support or allegiance
  • antonym: unfaithful - not remaining loyal or steadfast in affection or allegiance
