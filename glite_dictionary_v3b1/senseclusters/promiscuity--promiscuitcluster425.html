promiscuity

noun
casual sexual relationships
Pronunciation: / prəˌmɪskjuˈɪɾi / (AmE), / prəˌmɪskjuˈɪəti / (BrE)
the practice or state of having many casual sexual relationships or partners, often without commitment

Examples

Promiscuity in relationships
He was criticized for his promiscuity during his college years.
Some people believe that promiscuity leads to emotional detachment in relationships.
The novel explores themes of promiscuity and its effects on modern society.

Related Terms

  • antonym: celibacy - the state of abstaining from marriage and sexual relations
