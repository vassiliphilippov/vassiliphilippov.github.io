cone-nosed bug

noun
large bloodsucking insect (kissing bug)
4.4
Pronunciation: / koʊn noʊzd bʌɡ / (AmE), / kəʊn nəʊzd bʌɡ / (BrE)
A large bloodsucking insect of the family Reduviidae, particularly of the genus Triatoma, known for its conical nose and sometimes called a kissing bug.

Examples

A cone-nosed bug insect
A scientist captured a cone-nosed bug specimen for further study in the laboratory.
Farmers are advised to check for cone-nosed bugs in barns and animal shelters.
The cone-nosed bug is notorious for transmitting Chagas disease in some regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kissing bug - another name for the cone-nosed bug, known for biting humans near the mouth
