cladistic

adjective
classification by ancestry
Pronunciation: / kləˈdɪstɪk / (AmE), / kləˈdɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the classification of organisms based on shared ancestry and evolutionary relationships

Examples

Cladistic classification method
The cladistic method helps scientists understand how different species are related through evolution.
A cladistic analysis can reveal surprising connections between seemingly unrelated animals.
Modern biology often uses cladistic principles to organize the tree of life.
