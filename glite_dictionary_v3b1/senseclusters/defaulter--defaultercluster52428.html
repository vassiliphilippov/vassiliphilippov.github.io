defaulter

noun
person who fails to pay a debt
1.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɔltər / (AmE), / dɪˈfɔːltə / (BrE)
a person who fails to meet a financial obligation, such as paying a debt or fulfilling a contract

Examples

Loan defaulter
As a defaulter, he faced heavy penalties and restrictions on future loans.
If you are a defaulter on your mortgage, the lender may seize your property.
The bank sent several reminders before finally taking legal action against the defaulter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creditor - a person or organization to whom money is owed
  • synonym: debtor - a person who owes money
