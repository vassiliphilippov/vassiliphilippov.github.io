stegosaurian

noun
armored herbivorous dinosaur
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛɡəˈsɔːriən / (AmE), / ˌstiːɡəˈsɔːriən / (BrE)
a dinosaur of the group Stegosauria, known for its bony plates or spikes and herbivorous diet during the Jurassic and early Cretaceous periods

Examples

A stegosaurian dinosaur
The paleontologist discovered a nearly complete stegosaurian skeleton in the rocky desert.
Children were fascinated by the life-sized model of a stegosaurian at the natural history museum.
Scientists believe that the plates on a stegosaurian may have helped regulate its body temperature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ankylosaur - a different group of armored herbivorous dinosaurs
  • synonym: stegosaurus - a well-known genus of stegosaurian dinosaurs with large plates along its back
