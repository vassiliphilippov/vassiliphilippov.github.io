pin-tailed grouse

noun
a sandgrouse with long tail feathers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɪn teɪld ˈɡraʊs / (AmE), / ˌpɪn teɪld ˈɡraʊs / (BrE)
a sandgrouse found in Europe and Africa, distinguished by its elongated middle tail feathers

Ornithologists study the habits and habitats of the pin-tailed grouse in Africa's arid regions.
The pin-tailed grouse is easily recognized by the striking length of its tail feathers.
