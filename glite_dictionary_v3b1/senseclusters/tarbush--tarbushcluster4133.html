tarbush

noun
Middle Eastern hat
Pronunciation: / tɑrˈbuːʃ / (AmE), / tɑːˈbuːʃ / (BrE)
a traditional Middle Eastern hat, usually red and shaped like a cylinder, also known as a fez

Examples

A red tarbush
He placed the red tarbush carefully on his head before leaving for the festival.
The museum displayed a collection of traditional clothing, including a rare tarbush from the 19th century.
During the ceremony, each participant wore a tarbush as a symbol of cultural pride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fez - a flat-topped, red felt hat with a tassel, worn especially in the Middle East and North Africa
  • antonym: turban - a long piece of cloth wound around the head, worn especially in South Asia and the Middle East
