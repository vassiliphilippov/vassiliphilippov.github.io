meenie

noun
childish insult
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːni / (AmE), / ˈmiːni / (BrE)
a childish or playful term for a person who is mean or unkind

Examples

A meenie child
Don't be a meenie and let your little brother play with you.
The kids called her a meenie when she refused to share her snacks.
He didn't want to be seen as a meenie by his classmates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - someone who habitually seeks to harm or intimidate others
  • antonym: friend - a person whom one knows and likes
  • synonym: meanie - a person who is mean or unkind
