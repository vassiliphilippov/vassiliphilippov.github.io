mump

verb
to beg for money
Pronunciation: / mʌmp / (AmE), / mʌmp / (BrE)
to beg or go about asking for money or food, especially in an old-fashioned or dialectal context

Examples

To mump for bread
In the old village, he would mump at the church door every Sunday morning.
Travelers sometimes mumped their way from town to town in search of food.
It was common for the poor to mump during hard winters in the past.
