sealing

noun
making something watertight or water-repellent
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of applying a substance to something in order to make it watertight or to repel water

Examples

Regular sealing of deck
Regular sealing of your deck can prevent water damage and extend its life.
The contractor recommended sealing the basement walls to keep out moisture.
Without proper sealing, rain can seep into the roof and cause leaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waterproofing - the process of making something resistant to water
