travelog

noun
film or lecture about travel experiences
Pronunciation: / ˈtrævəˌlɔg /; / ˈtrævəˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈtrævəlɒg /; / ˈtrævəlɒɡ / (BrE)
A film, lecture, or written work that describes someone's experiences and observations while traveling, often illustrated or featuring footage from the journey.

Examples

A travelog film
He spent months editing his footage for a travelog to show at the library.
She attended a travelog featuring stunning footage of South American wildlife.
The documentary channel aired an inspiring travelog about the ancient sites of Egypt.
Last night's travelogue showcased breathtaking footage from the Amazon rainforest.
She decided to create a travelogue documenting her European adventure.
The filmmaker presented his travelogue about trekking through the Himalayas to a captivated audience.
