unsmirched

adjective
untainted or pure
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmɜːrtʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmɜːtʃt / (BrE)
not stained, tarnished, or damaged; completely clean or pure in reputation or condition

Examples

An unsmirched record
Despite the accusations, his record remained unsmirched after the investigation.
The white tablecloth was still unsmirched at the end of the dinner party.
She valued her unsmirched reputation above all else in her career.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spotless - completely clean or pure
  • antonym: tarnished - damaged or stained, especially in reputation
  • synonym: unblemished - not damaged or marked; perfect
