religieuse

noun
French cream puff pastry
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʁə.li.ʒjøz / (AmE), / ʁə.li.ʒjøz / (BrE)
a French pastry made of two stacked cream puffs filled with cream and covered with icing

Examples

A religieuse pastry

The bakery's display case was filled with colorful religieuses and other French desserts.
He learned to make a classic religieuse during his pastry class in Paris.
