supramental

adjective
beyond the mind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈmɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈmɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to a level of consciousness or awareness considered to be above or beyond the ordinary mind

Examples

Supramental awareness
The spiritual teacher described a supramental state that transcends ordinary thought.
Some philosophies propose a supramental consciousness as the next step in human evolution.
Achieving a supramental awareness is considered rare in many mystical traditions.
