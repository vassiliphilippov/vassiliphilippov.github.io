unbusy

adjective
not occupied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɪzi / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɪzi / (BrE)
not occupied, engaged, or in use; idle or available for use.

Examples

Unbusy server
The technician waited until the server was unbusy before starting the update.
You can call now because the phone line is unbusy.
During the afternoon, the help desk is usually unbusy and can answer questions quickly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: available - free to be used or accessed
  • antonym: busy - actively engaged or in use
  • synonym: idle - not active or in use
