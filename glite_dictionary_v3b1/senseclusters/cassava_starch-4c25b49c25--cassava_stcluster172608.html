cassava starch

noun
starch from the cassava plant root
5.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / k…ôÀàs…ëÀêv…ô st…ërt É / (AmE), / k…ôÀàs…ëÀêv…ô st…ëÀêt É / (BrE)
