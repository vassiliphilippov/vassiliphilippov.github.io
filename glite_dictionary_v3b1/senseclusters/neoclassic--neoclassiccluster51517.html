neoclassic

adjective
of a revival of classical style
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌniː.oʊˈklæs.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌniː.əʊˈklæs.ɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of a revival of an earlier classical style, especially in art, architecture, or literature.

Examples

Neoclassic art style
Her paintings show a clear neoclassic influence through their focus on balance and harmony.
Many buildings in the city center are noted for their neoclassic architectural details.
The gallery features several neoclassic sculptures inspired by ancient Greece and Rome.
