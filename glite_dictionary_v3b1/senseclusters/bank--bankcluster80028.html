bank

noun
dealer's or house's money in gambling
9.1
Pronunciation: / bæŋk / (AmE), / baŋk / (BrE)
the money held by the gambling house or dealer for use in a gambling game

Examples

The bank at roulette
He dreamed of winning enough to break the bank at the casino.
Players placed their bets against the bank in the high-stakes game.
The bank was quickly depleted after an extraordinary winning streak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: house - the gambling establishment acting as the banker in a game
