practice

verb
perform or do regularly
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpræk.tɪs/ /; / /ˈpræk.təs/ (AmE), /ˈpræk.tɪs/ /; / /ˈpræk.təs/ (BrE)
to carry out or apply something regularly as a custom, habit, or principle

Examples

To practice good habits
Doctors urge people to practice safe food handling at home.
It is important to practice healthy habits every day.
She believes everyone should practice kindness whenever possible.
It is important to practice kindness and patience in your everyday interactions.
Many people practice their faith through daily rituals and community service.
She decided to practice non-violent resistance in response to the injustice.

  • synonym: perform - to carry out or do a particular action
