political unit

noun
entity with political responsibilities
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈjuːnɪt / (AmE), / pəˈlɪtɪkəl ˈjuːnɪt / (BrE)
an area or group that forms a single entity with recognized political and administrative responsibilities, such as a country, state, or municipality

Examples

A political unit territory
A state is a political unit with its own government and defined territory.
European countries are each considered a separate political unit.
The city council governs the smallest political unit in our area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrative division - a portion of a country or region delineated for official government purposes
