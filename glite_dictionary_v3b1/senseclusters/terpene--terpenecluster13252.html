terpene

noun
a class of plant hydrocarbons
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.pin / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.piːn / (BrE)
a class of unsaturated hydrocarbons found mainly in plants, often responsible for their scent and used in essential oils and resins

Examples

Terpene compounds
Many essential oils contain high concentrations of terpene compounds that give them their characteristic aroma.
Pine trees produce a sticky resin rich in terpenes to defend against insects.
Scientists are studying the potential medical uses of terpene extracts from various plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isoprene - a basic structural unit found in terpenes
