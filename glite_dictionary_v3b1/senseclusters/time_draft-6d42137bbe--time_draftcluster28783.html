time draft

noun
draft payable at a future date
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: /taɪm dræft/ (AmE), /taɪm drɑːft/ /; / /taɪm dræft/ (BrE)
a draft, such as a bill of exchange, that is payable on a specified future date.

Examples

A time draft payment
A time draft gives the buyer additional time to make payment after receiving goods.
Banks often charge a fee for processing a time draft.
The exporter received a time draft that would mature in 60 days.
