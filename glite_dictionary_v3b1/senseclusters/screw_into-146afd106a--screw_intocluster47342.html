screw into

verb
to attach using a screw
2.8
Pronunciation: / skru ˈɪntu / (AmE), / skru ˈɪntu / (BrE)
to attach or fasten something to a surface by twisting a screw through it

Examples

To screw into a wall
Before hanging the picture, be sure to screw into a wall stud for extra support.
He used a drill to screw into the wooden beam and attach the bracket.
The technician will screw into the metal plate to secure it firmly in place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screw on - to attach something by twisting a screw or cap onto another object
  • antonym: unscrew - to remove something by twisting it in the opposite direction of screwing
