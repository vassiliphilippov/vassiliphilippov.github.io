mid-fifties

noun
ages 50 to 60
Pronunciation: / mɪd ˈfɪftiz / (AmE), / mɪd ˈfɪftɪz / (BrE)
the period of life between the ages of 50 and 60 years

Examples

In the mid-fifties age
He started running marathons in his mid-fifties and feels healthier than ever.
Many people begin to think about retirement as they reach their mid-fifties.
She retired in her mid-fifties to travel the world with her husband.
