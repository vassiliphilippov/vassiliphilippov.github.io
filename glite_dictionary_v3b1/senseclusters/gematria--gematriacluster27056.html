gematria

noun
numerical letter system
9.9
Pronunciation: / gəˈmeɪtriə / (AmE), / gəˈmeɪtriə / (BrE)
a system of assigning numerical values to letters, words, or phrases (especially in Hebrew) to find hidden meanings
