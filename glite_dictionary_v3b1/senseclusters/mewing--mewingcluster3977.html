mewing

noun
soft animal cry
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmjuːɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmjuːɪŋ / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched cry or sound made by a cat or a young bird

Examples

Kitten mewing
The constant mewing of the kittens let us know they were hungry.
Early in the morning, I heard the mewing of young birds outside my window.
She followed the faint mewing until she found the lost cat in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirping - a short, sharp sound made by small birds or insects
  • synonym: meowing - the crying sound made by a cat
