annual lek

noun
yearly bird mating gathering
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ju.əl lɛk / (AmE), / ˈæn.ju.əl lɛk / (BrE)
a seasonal gathering of certain bird species for the purpose of mating, occurring once each year

Examples

The annual lek gathering
Male grouse display their feathers at the annual lek to impress potential mates.
Scientists study the annual lek to better understand avian mating behaviors.
The annual lek attracts birdwatchers from around the world each spring.
