chiromance

verb
tell fortunes by reading palms
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkaɪrəˌmæns / (AmE), / ˈkaɪərəˌmans / (BrE)
to tell someone's fortune by reading the lines on their palm

Examples

To chiromance a palm
He claimed he could chiromance and reveal secrets hidden in your hands.
Many novels describe mysterious characters who chiromance to predict destinies.
The old woman offered to chiromance for the curious travelers at the fair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: palmistry - the art of interpreting the lines on the palm to foresee the future
