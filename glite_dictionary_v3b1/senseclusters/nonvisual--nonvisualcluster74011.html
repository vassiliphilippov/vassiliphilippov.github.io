nonvisual

adjective
not related to vision
1.4
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvɪʒuəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈvɪʒuəl / (BrE)
not related to or resulting in vision; not involving the sense of sight.

Examples

Nonvisual cues
Scientists studied the effects of nonvisual cues on the participants' behavior.
Some animals rely heavily on nonvisual senses, such as hearing and smell, to find food.
The device was designed to provide nonvisual feedback through sound and vibration.
