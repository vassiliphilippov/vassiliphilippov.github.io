rat

noun
remote-access malware
9.3
Pronunciation: /ræt/ (AmE), /ræt/ (BrE)
(computing) a type of malicious software that allows an attacker to access and control a computer remotely, often secretly.

Examples

A computer rat
Security researchers detected a new rat enabling attackers to control dozens of compromised computers remotely.
The company's IT team found multiple rats communicating with an unknown server after the breach.
After the attack, investigators discovered a rat that allowed the hacker to take screenshots and record audio.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Trojan (Trojan horse) - a type of malware that deceives users by appearing legitimate while performing harmful actions.
  • synonym: malware - software designed to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to a computer system.
