scleral

adjective
of the sclera
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsklɪrəl / (AmE), / ˈsklɪərəl / (BrE)
relating to the sclera, the white outer layer of the eyeball

Examples

Scleral discoloration
The doctor noted some scleral discoloration during the eye examination.
A scleral lens is designed to rest on the white part of the eye.
Inflammation of scleral tissue can cause significant discomfort and vision problems.
