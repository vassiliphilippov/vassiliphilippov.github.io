Muong

proper noun
Vietnamese ethnic group
Pronunciation: / muːˈɒŋ / (AmE), / muːˈɒŋ / (BrE)
an ethnic group native to northern Vietnam, also referring to their language

Examples

Muong people
The Muong are known for their unique traditional costumes and vibrant festivals.
She is studying the Muong language as part of her research on ethnic minorities in Vietnam.
Many Muong villages are located in the mountainous regions of northern Vietnam.

Related Terms
