matrilocal

adjective
living with wife's family
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmætrɪˈloʊkəl / (AmE), / ˌmætrɪˈləʊkəl / (BrE)
describing a social system or custom in which a married couple lives with or near the wife's family after marriage

Examples

Matrilocal family system
In some cultures, it is common for newlyweds to follow a matrilocal tradition and live with the wife's parents.

In some cultures, it is common for families to follow a matrilocal tradition after marriage.
