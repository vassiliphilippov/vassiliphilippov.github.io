uninvolved

adjective
not taking part or connected
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
not participating or connected with a situation, activity, or relationship; not involved.

Examples

Uninvolved in the project
Despite the heated debate, Maria remained uninvolved and impartial in her comments.
Many parents prefer to stay uninvolved in their teenagers' disagreements unless absolutely necessary.
The judge must be completely uninvolved to guarantee a fair trial.
