domed stadium

noun
sports arena with a domed roof
Pronunciation: / doʊmd ˈsteɪdiəm / (AmE), / dəʊmd ˈsteɪdiəm / (BrE)
a large sports arena with a roof, typically shaped like a dome, that covers the playing field and seating areas
