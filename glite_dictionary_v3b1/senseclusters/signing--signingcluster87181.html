signing

noun
official agreement event
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
an event or act where someone officially signs a document or formally joins an organization, especially in sports or business

Examples

Book signing event
The author attended a signing at the local bookstore to meet her fans.
After months of negotiation, the company celebrated the signing of the new partnership agreement.
The football club announced several new signings before the start of the season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement
  • synonym: contract - a formal agreement between two or more parties
  • antonym: termination - the act of ending an agreement or contract
