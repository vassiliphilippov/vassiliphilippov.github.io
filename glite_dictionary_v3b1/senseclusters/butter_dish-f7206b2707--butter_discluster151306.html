butter dish

noun
container for serving butter
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚ dɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtə dɪʃ / (BrE)
A small dish, often with a cover, used to hold and serve butter at the dining table.

Examples

A butter dish with cover

She placed the butter dish in the center of the table for breakfast.
