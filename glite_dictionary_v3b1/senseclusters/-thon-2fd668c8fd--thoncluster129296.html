-thon

x
event suffix
6.5
Pronunciation: / θɑn / (AmE), / θɒn / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns for long or continuous events or competitions, often for a special goal such as raising money for charity

Examples

A -thon event
The school organized a read-thon to encourage students to read as many books as possible in one week.
A local charity held a dance-thon to raise funds for the community center.
During the hack-thon, programmers worked nonstop to develop innovative software solutions.
The radio station held a dancethon to collect donations for the local hospital.
Students participated in a readthon to encourage reading and raise funds for new books.
Our community organized a bikethon to support environmental awareness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marathon - a long-distance running race or any long-lasting activity
