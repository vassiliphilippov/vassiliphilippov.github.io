Japanese spaniel

noun
small breed of toy dog from Japan
2.4
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈpæniz ˈspænl̩ / (AmE), / dʒəˈpæniːz ˈspænɪl / (BrE)
a small breed of toy dog originating in Japan, with a silky black-and-white or red-and-white coat and a short, broad muzzle
