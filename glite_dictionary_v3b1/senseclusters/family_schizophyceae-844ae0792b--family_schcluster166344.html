An obsolete scientific name for a group of photosynthetic bacteria, more commonly known as Cyanophyceae or blue-green algae.

Examples

family Schizophyceae blue-green algae
Many older textbooks refer to blue-green algae as family Schizophyceae.
Modern taxonomy has replaced the term family Schizophyceae with Cyanophyceae or cyanobacteria.
The family Schizophyceae was once thought to be distinct from other forms of algae.
