Bermudan rig

noun
yacht rig with triangular sails
9.7 7.1
Pronunciation: /bər.ˈmjuː.dən rɪg/ (AmE), /bə.ˈmjuː.dən rɪg/ (BrE)
a type of sailing rig for yachts that uses triangular sails, typically with a mainsail set behind the mast and no gaff.

Examples

Bermudan rig yacht
He learned to sail using a Bermudan rig rather than the traditional gaff rig.
Sailing enthusiasts often prefer the Bermudan rig due to its efficiency and simplicity.
The yacht was equipped with a modern Bermudan rig for easy handling.
