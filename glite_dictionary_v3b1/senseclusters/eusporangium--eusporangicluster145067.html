eusporangium

noun
sporangium arising from multiple cells
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːspɔːˈrændʒiəm / (AmE), / juːspəˈrandʒɪəm / (BrE)
A type of sporangium that develops from a group of epidermal cells, typical of certain vascular plants.

Examples

A eusporangium type
A eusporangium arises from several epidermal cells, not just one.
Botanists distinguish between eusporangium and leptosporangium spore development.
The eusporangium of certain ferns can be observed under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leptosporangium - a sporangium developed from a single cell
