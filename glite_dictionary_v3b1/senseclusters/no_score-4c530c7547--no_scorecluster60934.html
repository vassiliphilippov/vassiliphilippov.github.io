no score

noun
when no points or goals have been scored
2.4
Pronunciation: / noʊ skɔr / (AmE), / nəʊ skɔː / (BrE)
a situation in a game or competition where neither side has scored any points or goals

Examples

A no score game
After a tense first half, the teams went into the break with no score.
Despite several chances, there was still no score by the end of the match.
Monday's baseball game ended in no score after extra innings.
