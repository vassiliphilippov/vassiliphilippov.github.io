Georgia pine

noun
a timber pine tree from the southeastern US
6.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒɔːr.dʒə paɪn/ /; / /ˈdʒoʊr.dʒə paɪn/ (AmE), /ˈdʒɔː.dʒə paɪn/ (BrE)
