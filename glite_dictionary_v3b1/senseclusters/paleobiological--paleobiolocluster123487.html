paleobiological

adjective
ancient life biology
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪliˌoʊˌbaɪəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌpeɪlɪəʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the biology of ancient or fossil organisms

Examples

Paleobiological record
The paleobiological record helps scientists understand how life evolved on Earth.
She specializes in paleobiological research, focusing on ancient marine ecosystems.
New paleobiological discoveries can change our understanding of prehistoric environments.
