lysergic

adjective
surreal or hallucinatory
Pronunciation: / laɪˈsɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / laɪˈsɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
intensely surreal, hallucinatory, or dreamlike, especially in a way that resembles the effects of lysergic drugs such as LSD

Examples

Lysergic art style
The artist's new collection features lysergic landscapes that seem to shift and melt before your eyes.
Viewers described the music video as a lysergic journey through a world of swirling colors and shapes.
Her writing style is often called lysergic for its dreamlike and surreal imagery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychedelic - having vivid colors, patterns, or effects like those produced by hallucinogenic drugs
  • antonym: realistic - representing things in a way that is true to life
