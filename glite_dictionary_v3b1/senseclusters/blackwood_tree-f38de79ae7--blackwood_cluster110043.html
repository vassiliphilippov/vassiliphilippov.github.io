blackwood tree

noun
tree yielding dark hardwood
Pronunciation: / ˈblæk.wʊd ˌtri / (AmE), / ˈblæk.wʊd ˌtriː / (BrE)
any of several hardwood trees that produce very dark-colored wood, often used for furniture or musical instruments

Examples

Blackwood trees wood
Furniture made from blackwood trees is prized for its durability and rich color.
Several species are considered a blackwood tree because of their distinctive dark timber.
The blackwood tree is highly valued for making musical instruments like clarinets and oboes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ebony - a dense black hardwood from several tree species
