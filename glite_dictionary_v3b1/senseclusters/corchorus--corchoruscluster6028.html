corchorus

noun
genus of plants used for jute fiber
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔr.kə.rəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔː.kə.rəs / (BrE)
A genus of tropical plants with large leaves and yellow flowers, several species of which are cultivated as a source of jute fiber.
