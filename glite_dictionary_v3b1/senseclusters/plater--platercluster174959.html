plater

noun
food arranger
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪtər / (AmE), / ˈpleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who arranges or serves food on plates, especially in a professional setting

Examples

Professional plater arranges food
The plater made sure each dish looked beautiful before it left the kitchen.
At the banquet, the plater worked quickly to prepare hundreds of plates for the guests.
She was hired as a plater because of her attention to detail and artistic skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chef - a professional cook, especially the chief cook in a restaurant
  • synonym: server - a person who serves food in a restaurant
