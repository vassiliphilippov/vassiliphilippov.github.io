barad

noun
clay water vessel
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɑːd / (AmE), / bəˈrɑːd / (BrE)
a traditional clay vessel used in the Middle East for cooling and storing water

Examples

A barad of water
The family kept their drinking water cool in a barad during the hot summer months.
She poured a glass of water from the barad before serving lunch.
Artisans in the village still make each barad by hand using traditional methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matka - an earthen pot used for storing water in South Asia
