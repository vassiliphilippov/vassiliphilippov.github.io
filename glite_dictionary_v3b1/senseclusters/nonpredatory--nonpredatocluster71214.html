nonpredatory

adjective
not hunting animals
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈprɛdəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈprɛdətri / (BrE)
not hunting, killing, or eating other animals for food

Examples

Nonpredatory fish species
The nonpredatory fish feed mainly on plants and small insects.
Many birds are nonpredatory and rely on seeds and fruit for their diet.
Unlike sharks, dolphins are generally considered nonpredatory toward other marine animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: herbivorous - feeding only on plants
  • antonym: predatory - hunting and eating other animals for food
