saffron

verb
to add saffron
Pronunciation: / ˈsæfrən / (AmE), / ˈsæfrən / (BrE)
to flavor or color something, especially food or liquid, with saffron.

Examples

To saffron the rice
She saffroned the rice to give it a beautiful golden color.
The chef saffrons the soup for a unique flavor and aroma.
They had never saffroned a dish before trying the new recipe.
