ugli fruit

noun
hybrid citrus fruit (tangerine × grapefruit)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʌɡli frut / (AmE), / ˈʌɡli fruːt / (BrE)
a large, sweet citrus fruit that is a hybrid between a tangerine (or mandarin) and a grapefruit, with thick, wrinkled skin
