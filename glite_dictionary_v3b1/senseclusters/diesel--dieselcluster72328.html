diesel

noun
petroleum-based fuel
2.4 4/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêz…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêz…ôl / (BrE)
a type of fuel made from petroleum, commonly used in engines of trucks, buses, and some cars

Examples

Diesel fuel
The bus runs on diesel instead of gasoline to save on fuel costs.
Many large trucks use diesel because it provides more power for heavy loads.
She filled the tank with diesel before starting her long journey.
