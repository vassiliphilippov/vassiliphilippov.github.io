masculinely

adverb
in a manly way
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskjəlɪnli / (AmE), / ˈmæskjʊlɪnli / (BrE)
in a manner that is typical of or associated with men or masculinity

Examples

To act masculinely
He walked masculinely, with broad shoulders and a confident stride.
The actor delivered his lines masculinely, adding strength to the character.
She dressed masculinely for the role, wearing a suit and tie.

Related Terms

  • antonym: femininely - in a way that is typical of or associated with women
  • synonym: manfully - in a brave or strong way typical of a man
