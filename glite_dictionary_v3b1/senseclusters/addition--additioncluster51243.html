addition

noun
planned suburban residential area
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
A suburban area that has been planned, with streets and lots, for future residential development.

Examples

A housing addition
Many families moved into the newly constructed addition south of the city center.
The city council approved the plans for the Oak Ridge addition to accommodate future growth.
The developer purchased land to create a new addition on the outskirts of town.
