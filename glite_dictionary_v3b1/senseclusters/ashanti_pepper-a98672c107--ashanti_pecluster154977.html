Ashanti pepper

noun
West African spice from Piper guineense
1.0 7.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈʃæn.tiː ˈpɛp.ər/ /; / /ə.ˈʃɑːn.tiː ˈpɛp.ər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈʃæn.tiː ˈpɛp.ə/ /; / /ə.ˈʃɑːn.tiː ˈpɛp.ə/ (BrE)
