Transfiguration Day

noun
Christian festival commemorating the Transfiguration of Jesus
8.8
Pronunciation: /træns.fɪg.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən deɪ/ (AmE), /træns.fɪg.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən deɪ/ (BrE)
(in the Christian Church) a festival celebrating the Transfiguration of Jesus Christ, observed on August 6 in many traditions.

Examples

Transfiguration Day festival
August 6 is recognized as Transfiguration Day in the liturgical calendar of several Christian denominations.
Many churches hold special services on Transfiguration Day to commemorate the event.
The icon of the Transfiguration is prominently displayed during Transfiguration Day celebrations.
