liner

noun
fabric sewn inside a garment
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnə / (BrE)
a piece of fabric sewn into the inside surface of a garment to provide a finished look and added comfort

Examples

A jacket liner
Many luxury handbags have a leather exterior and a fabric liner for extra durability.
She replaced the worn liner in her jacket with a new one.
The coat's silk liner makes it comfortable to wear even in cold weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lining - material used to line the inside surface of something, especially clothing
