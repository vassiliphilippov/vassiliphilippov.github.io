selenium cell

noun
photoelectric cell using selenium
Pronunciation: / səˈliniəm sɛl / (AmE), / sɪˈliːniəm sɛl / (BrE)
a photoelectric cell in which a strip of selenium is used to convert light into electrical energy

Examples

A selenium cell device
A selenium cell changes the intensity of light into a corresponding electrical signal.
Engineers replaced the faulty selenium cell in the exposure meter to restore its sensitivity.
The museum displayed an old selenium cell used in early light meters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: photocell - an electrical device that generates a current when exposed to light
