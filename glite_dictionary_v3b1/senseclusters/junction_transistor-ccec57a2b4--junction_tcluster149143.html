junction transistor

noun
semiconductor device with two p-n junctions
7.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən træn.ˈzɪs.tər/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌŋk.ʃən træn.ˈzɪs.tə/ (BrE)
A semiconductor device with two p-n junctions, used to amplify or switch electronic signals.
