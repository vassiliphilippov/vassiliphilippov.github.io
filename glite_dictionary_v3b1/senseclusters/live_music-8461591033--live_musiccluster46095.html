live music

noun
music performed to an audience
Pronunciation: / laɪv ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / laɪv ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music that is performed directly in front of an audience, not pre-recorded or played from a recording

Examples

Live music performance
Many restaurants now offer live music to create a lively atmosphere for diners.
The bar features live music every Friday night, attracting crowds from all over the city.
We enjoyed an evening of live music at the open-air festival downtown.
