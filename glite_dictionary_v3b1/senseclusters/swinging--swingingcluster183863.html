swinging

noun
act of moving back and forth
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving back and forth with a regular rhythm, especially as on a swing or suspended object

Examples

Swinging on a swing
She loves the feeling of swinging under the old oak tree in her backyard.
The children spent hours swinging at the playground before lunchtime.
There’s a sense of freedom in the simple joy of swinging back and forth.
