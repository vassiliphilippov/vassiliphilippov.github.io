outboard

noun
external engine for boats
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɔːd / (BrE)
a motor for small boats that is mounted externally at the stern and used to provide propulsion

Examples

Outboard motor
He repaired the outboard before their trip across the lake.
Many small rescue boats use a portable outboard for easy maneuverability.
The fishing boat was powered by a 40-horsepower outboard.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inboard - an engine enclosed within the hull of a boat
