titanite

noun
gemstone mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪtəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈtɪtənaɪt / (BrE)
a yellow, green, or brown mineral, also called sphene, used as a gemstone and source of titanium

Examples

Geologists often study titanite to learn about the formation of igneous rocks.
