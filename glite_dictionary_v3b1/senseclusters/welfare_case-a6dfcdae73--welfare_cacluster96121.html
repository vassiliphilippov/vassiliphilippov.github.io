welfare case

noun
recipient of welfare assistance or case handled by welfare services
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlˌfɛr keɪs / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌfeə keɪs / (BrE)
a situation or person being handled by a welfare agency, typically as a recipient of public assistance

Examples

A welfare case
Each welfare case is carefully reviewed before assistance is granted.
She worked tirelessly, managing more than fifty welfare cases at the agency.
The social worker visited the family's home after their situation was classified as a welfare case.

Related Terms
