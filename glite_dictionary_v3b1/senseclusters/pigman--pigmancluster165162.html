pigman

noun
male pig herder
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡmən / (BrE)
a man who tends or herds pigs; a male swineherd

Examples

A pigman on the farm
During the fair, the pigman proudly showed his prize-winning piglets.
In the old village, the pigman was known for his knowledge of swine breeds.
The pigman rose at dawn each morning to feed the animals in the sty.
