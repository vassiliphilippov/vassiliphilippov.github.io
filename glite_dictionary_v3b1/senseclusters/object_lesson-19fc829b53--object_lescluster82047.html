object lesson

noun
Example serving as a warning
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbdʒɛkt ˌlɛsən / (AmE), / ˈɒbdʒɪkt ˌlɛsən / (BrE)
a practical example, especially a punishment or incident, that serves as a warning to others

Examples

An object lesson in punishment
The court case became an object lesson in the consequences of financial fraud.
The severe penalty was meant to provide an object lesson for anyone thinking of committing a similar offense.
They made his dismissal an object lesson to discourage other employees from breaking the rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: example - a person or thing regarded as typical of a group or class, often used in 'make an example of' meaning to punish as a warning
