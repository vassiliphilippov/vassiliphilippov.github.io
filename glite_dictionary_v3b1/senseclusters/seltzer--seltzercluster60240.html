seltzer

noun
alcoholic sparkling water (hard seltzer)
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsel.t.sər/ (AmE), /ˈsel.t.sə/ (BrE)
An alcoholic, lightly flavoured carbonated drink made from fermented sugar or malt, usually sold in cans; also called 'hard seltzer'.

Examples

A can of seltzer
At the trendy bar she ordered a seltzer instead of wine and liked its light taste.
Many breweries now sell canned seltzer in multiple fruit flavors for summer drinkers.
She chose a low-calorie seltzer to bring to the picnic instead of beer or cider.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard seltzer - a pre-mixed alcoholic sparkling water beverage, often lightly flavored
  • synonym: spiked seltzer - an informal name for an alcoholic seltzer; usually a canned alcoholic sparkling water
