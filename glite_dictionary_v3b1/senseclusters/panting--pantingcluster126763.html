panting

noun
heavy, rapid breathing
6.3 2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpæn.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpæn.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of breathing quickly and heavily, typically after physical exertion or due to excitement or distress

Examples

Heavy panting
After running up the stairs, his panting filled the quiet hallway.
Her panting slowed as she recovered from the intense workout.
The dog's panting was a clear sign of how excited it was.
