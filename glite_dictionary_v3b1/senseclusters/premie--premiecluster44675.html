premie

noun
a premature infant
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːmi / (AmE), / ˈpriːmi / (BrE)
an infant that is born before 37 weeks of gestation; a premature baby

Examples

A premie baby
Despite being born a premie, he grew up healthy and strong.
Support groups for parents of premies can be incredibly helpful after leaving the NICU.
The doctor explained that her premie would need to stay in the hospital a few extra weeks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preemie - alternate informal spelling meaning a premature infant
