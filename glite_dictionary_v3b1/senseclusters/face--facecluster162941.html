face

noun
front part of the head
2.7 1.0
Pronunciation: /feɪs/ (AmE), /feɪs/ (BrE)
the front part of a person's or animal's head, including the eyes, nose, and mouth

Examples

A smiling face
A smile lit up his face when he saw the surprise.
Please keep your face covered in the sun to avoid sunburn.
She splashed cold water on her face to wake up.
Some animals show expressions through movements of their face.
The cat washed its face with its paw after eating.
The horse had a white mark running down the center of its face.
A smile spread across her face when she saw the gift.
He covered his face with his hands in embarrassment.
She gently touched his face, feeling the warmth of his skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: countenance - a person's facial expression; another word for face
  • synonym: muzzle - the projecting part of the face of some animals, including the nose and mouth
