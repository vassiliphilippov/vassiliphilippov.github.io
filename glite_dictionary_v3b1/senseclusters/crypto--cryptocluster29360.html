crypto

noun
digital currency slang
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪptoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkrɪptəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for digital currencies that use cryptography, such as Bitcoin or Ethereum

Examples

Invest in crypto
He decided to invest a portion of his savings in crypto after reading about Bitcoin.
Many young entrepreneurs are launching new projects in the crypto space.
Some people believe that crypto will change the future of finance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital currency secured by cryptography
  • antonym: fiat money - government-issued currency not backed by a physical commodity
