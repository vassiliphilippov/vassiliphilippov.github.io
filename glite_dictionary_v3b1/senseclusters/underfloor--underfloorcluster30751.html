underfloor

adjective
beneath the floor
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌflɔr / (AmE), / ˈʌndəflɔː / (BrE)
located or installed beneath the surface of a floor

Examples

Underfloor heating system
The new apartment features underfloor heating to keep the rooms warm in winter.
Cables were hidden using an underfloor system to maintain a clean look in the office.
They installed underfloor insulation to improve the house's energy efficiency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overhead - located above head height or above something
  • synonym: subfloor - the structural layer beneath the finished floor
