joint

noun
place where two bones connect
1.3 2.4
Pronunciation: /dʒɔɪnt/ (AmE), /dʒɔɪnt/ (BrE)
a point at which two bones are connected in the body, especially one that permits movement

Examples

Knee joint
Arthritis often affects the joints, making it difficult to move comfortably.
Repeated bending can sometimes cause a joint to become painful or swollen.
The knee is the largest joint in the human body.
