sniveller

noun
person who habitually complains or whines
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪvələr / (AmE), / ˈsnɪvələ / (BrE)
a person who habitually complains or whines in an annoying or excessive way

Examples

A sniveller complains
He dismissed their objections, calling them snivellers who always found something to complain about.
Stop acting like a sniveller and face your responsibilities.
The coach had no patience for snivellers on the team.

Related Terms
