inertial

adjective
relating to inertia
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɜrʃəl / (AmE), / ɪˈnɜːʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or caused by inertia, especially in physics or engineering

Examples

Inertial navigation system
An inertial navigation system is used in aircraft and spacecraft for guidance.
Due to its inertial mass, the vehicle takes longer to accelerate.
The inertial properties of the object were measured in the laboratory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inertia - the tendency of objects to resist changes in motion
