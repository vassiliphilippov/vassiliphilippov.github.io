estrogenic

adjective
relating to or caused by estrogen
Pronunciation: / ˌɛstrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌiːstrəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or caused by estrogen, especially producing effects similar to those of the hormone estrogen.

Examples

Estrogenic effects
Researchers studied the estrogenic activity of certain plant compounds in laboratory mice.
Some chemicals in plastics are known to have estrogenic effects on the body.
The patient was prescribed a drug with estrogenic properties to help with hormonal balance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estrogen - a female sex hormone produced mainly in the ovaries
