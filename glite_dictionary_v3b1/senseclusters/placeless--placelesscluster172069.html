placeless

adjective
without local identity
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈpleɪsləs / (BrE)
not connected to any particular place or lacking a distinctive local character or identity

Examples

A placeless place
The hotel lobby felt strangely placeless, with no hint of the local culture or history.
Many modern airports have a placeless design that looks the same in every country.
She missed the unique charm of her hometown and disliked the placeless atmosphere of the new city.
After years of traveling, she felt completely placeless and longed for a home.
The digital world can sometimes seem placeless, with no real sense of location.
His memories were placeless, drifting between cities and countries without a clear origin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distinctive - having a quality that makes something clearly different and easy to recognize
  • synonym: generic - not specific or unique; lacking distinctive features
  • antonym: grounded - having a strong connection to a place or situation
  • synonym: homeless - without a home or permanent place to live
  • synonym: rootless - having no permanent home or connection to a place
