gerrymandering

noun
manipulation of voting districts
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛriˌmændərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛrɪˌmændərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or practice of changing the boundaries of voting districts to give an unfair advantage to one group or political party
