coumarone-indene resin

noun
thermoplastic resin used in coatings and paints
Pronunciation: / ˌkuːməˌroʊn ɪnˈdiːn ˈrɛz.ɪn / (AmE), / ˌkuːməˌrəʊn ɪnˈdiːn ˈrɛz.ɪn / (BrE)
a thermoplastic resin produced by polymerizing indene and coumarone, used in coatings, paints, and asphalt tiles
