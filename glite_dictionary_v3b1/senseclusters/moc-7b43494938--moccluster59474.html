MOC

noun
online course
Pronunciation: / ɛm oʊ si / (AmE), / ɛm əʊ si / (BrE)
an online course designed to be accessed by a large number of people over the internet

Examples

Online MOC course
She completed a MOC in digital marketing to improve her skills.
Many universities now offer at least one MOC each semester.
He found the MOC format convenient for balancing study with work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: webinar - an online seminar or presentation
