pin-up

noun
glamorous model in pictures for display
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌʌp / (BrE)
a person (especially a woman) who appears in glamorous or sexually attractive photos, or the photo/poster of such a person, intended for public display
