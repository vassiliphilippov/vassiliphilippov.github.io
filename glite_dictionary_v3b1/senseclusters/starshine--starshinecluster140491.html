starshine

noun
light from stars
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːʃaɪn / (BrE)
the light that is emitted by stars and visible at night

Examples

Soft starshine light
The quiet lake reflected the soft starshine on its surface.
They walked under the clear sky, guided only by starshine.
The mountain was beautiful at night, glowing faintly in the starshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: starlight - the light that comes from stars
  • antonym: sunshine - the light from the sun
