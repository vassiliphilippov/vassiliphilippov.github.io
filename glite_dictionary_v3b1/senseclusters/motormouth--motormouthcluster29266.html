motormouth

noun
person who talks incessantly
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊ.t̬ɚˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈməʊ.təˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
A person who talks very quickly or incessantly, especially in an annoying way.

Examples

A motormouth person
Jenny can be a real motormouth when she's excited about something.
My colleague is such a motormouth that meetings always run over time.
Sometimes being a motormouth gets him into trouble with his friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, especially a child
