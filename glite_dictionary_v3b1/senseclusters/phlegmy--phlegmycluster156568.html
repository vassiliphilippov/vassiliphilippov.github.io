phlegmy

adjective
full of or relating to phlegm
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛg.mi / (AmE), / ˈflɛg.mi / (BrE)
full of or producing phlegm; characterized by the presence of phlegm, especially in the throat or respiratory passages.

Examples

Phlegmy throat sound

The doctor examined the patient's phlegmy cough for signs of infection.
