blankness

noun
state of being blank or empty
Pronunciation: / ˈblæŋknəs / (AmE), / ˈblaŋknɪs / (BrE)
the state or quality of being blank, empty, or void

Examples

A blankness of mind
A sense of blankness filled the room after the announcement was made.
Her mind was overtaken by a sudden blankness as she faced the difficult question.
The letter's blankness made it seem cold and impersonal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emptiness - the state of containing nothing; emptiness
  • antonym: fullness - the state of being full; abundance
