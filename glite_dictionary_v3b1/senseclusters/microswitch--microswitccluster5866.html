microswitch

noun
small sensitive switch
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌswɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌswɪtʃ / (BrE)
a very small, sensitive electrical switch that is activated by a slight physical force

Examples

A microswitch device
The safety mechanism relies on a microswitch to stop the machine if the cover is opened.
A microswitch inside the mouse detects every click with high precision.
Technicians replaced the faulty microswitch to restore the automatic door's function.
