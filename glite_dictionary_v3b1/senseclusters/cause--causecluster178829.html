cause

noun
legal proceeding in court
8.2 10/m
Pronunciation: / k…îz / (AmE), / k…îÀêz / (BrE)
(Legal) A proceeding in a court of law in which an individual or group seeks a legal remedy.

Examples

A legal cause
Several parties were named in the cause pending before the court.
She hired a lawyer to present her cause in civil court.
The judge dismissed the cause due to lack of evidence.
