noncharitable

adjective
not for charity
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtʃærətəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtʃærətəbəl / (BrE)
not relating to, established for, or qualifying as a charity; not eligible for charitable status or tax relief
