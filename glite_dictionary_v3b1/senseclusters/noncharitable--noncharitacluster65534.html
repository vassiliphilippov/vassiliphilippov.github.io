noncharitable

adjective
not for charity
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈtʃærətəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈtʃærətəbəl / (BrE)
not relating to, established for, or qualifying as a charity; not eligible for charitable status or tax relief

Examples

Noncharitable organization status
The organization was classified as noncharitable and could not receive tax-deductible donations.
Only charitable donations are tax-exempt; noncharitable contributions do not qualify.
Their noncharitable activities included commercial ventures unrelated to their original mission.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charitable - relating to or intended for charity
