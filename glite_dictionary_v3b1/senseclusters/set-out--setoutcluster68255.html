set-out

noun
layout in construction
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛt aʊt / (AmE), / ˈsɛt aʊt / (BrE)
the process or result of marking or laying out the position of structures or features, especially in construction

Examples

Construction set-out
The architect reviewed the set-out before construction began to ensure accuracy.
Accurate set-out is essential for building foundations to be placed correctly.
Mistakes in the set-out can lead to costly errors during the construction process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blueprint - a detailed plan or drawing for construction
  • synonym: layout - the arrangement or plan of something
