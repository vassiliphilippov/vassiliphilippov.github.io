unwatchable

adjective
not worth watching
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɑtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɒtʃəbəl / (BrE)
so bad, boring, or unpleasant that it is not worth watching

Examples

Unwatchable movie
The movie was so poorly made that it was almost unwatchable.
Many viewers found the television series unwatchable after the first season.
He turned off the documentary because he thought it was completely unwatchable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boring - not interesting or exciting
  • antonym: entertaining - enjoyable and interesting to watch
