winepress

noun
machine for extracting grape juice
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪnˌprɛs / (AmE), / ˈwaɪnˌprɛs / (BrE)
A machine or device used to squeeze juice from grapes in wine-making.

Examples

A winepress machine
After crushing the grapes, they transferred them to the winepress to extract the juice.
Modern wineries often use a mechanical winepress to improve efficiency.
The old vineyard still has a traditional winepress used by generations of winemakers.
