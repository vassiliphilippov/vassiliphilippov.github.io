search for prey

verb
hunt for animals to eat
6.8
Pronunciation: / sɝtʃ fər preɪ / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ fə preɪ / (BrE)
to look for animals to hunt and eat, especially used of predators in the wild

Examples

To search for prey at dawn

Owls typically search for prey at night when their sharp vision gives them an advantage.
Several sharks can be seen searching for prey near the coral reef.
