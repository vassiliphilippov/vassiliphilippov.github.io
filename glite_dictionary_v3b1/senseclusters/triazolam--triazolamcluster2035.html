triazolam

noun
benzodiazepine sedative for insomnia
Pronunciation: / traɪˈæzəˌlæm / (AmE), / traɪˈazəlæm / (BrE)
A benzodiazepine drug used as a short-term treatment for insomnia, commonly known by the trade name Halcion.

Examples

Triazolam drug
Her doctor prescribed triazolam to help manage her severe insomnia.
Patients taking triazolam are advised not to drive or operate machinery.
The pharmacist explained the possible side effects of triazolam to the patient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Halcion - trade name for triazolam
  • synonym: diazepam - another benzodiazepine drug, used for anxiety and muscle spasms
  • antonym: insomnia - condition of being unable to sleep
