rat-licker

noun
sycophant; self-serving flatterer
9.9 9.3
Pronunciation: /ræt ˈlɪk.ər/ /; / /ˈræt.lɪk.ər/ (AmE), /ræt ˈlɪk.ə/ /; / /ˈræt.lɪk.ə/ (BrE)
A person who flatters or ingratiates themselves with others, especially for personal advantage; a sycophant.

His colleagues dismissed him as a rat-licker because he always agreed with management, no matter what.
