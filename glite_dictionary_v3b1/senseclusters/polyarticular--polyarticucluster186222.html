polyarticular

adjective
having many joints
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliɑrˈtɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌpɒliɑːˈtɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
having many joints or articulated segments, especially in limbs, antennae, or mechanical parts

Examples

Insects with polyarticular antennae can sense their environment more precisely.
