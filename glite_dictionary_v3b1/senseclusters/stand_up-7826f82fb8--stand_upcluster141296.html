stand up

verb
rise or be upright on the feet
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /stænd ʌp/ /; / /stænd əp/ (AmE), /stænd ʌp/ /; / /stænd əp/ (BrE)
to rise from sitting or lying to a standing position; to get onto your feet

Examples

To stand up from a chair
After the long movie, he could barely stand up from his seat.
Please stand up when the judge enters the courtroom.
She told everyone to stand up and stretch during the meeting.
Everyone was asked to stand up when the teacher entered the classroom.
He struggled to stand up after sitting for so long.
Please stand up if you know the answer.
He quickly stood up to greet his guests.
Please stand up when your name is called.
The entire audience was asked to stand up for the national anthem.
