Synonyms

  • get up
    1.0 32/m
    get up is an everyday verb for rising from bed or a chair, while stand up focuses on the physical act of getting onto your feet.
  • get to one's feet
    2.9 3.24/m
    get to one's feet is a longer phrase often used to show reaction or formality, while stand up is a simple, common verb for rising.
  • rise
    1.0 27/m
    rise is more formal and often used for getting out of bed or in writing, while stand up is more everyday and physical.
  • stand
    1.0 376/m
    stand often means being upright or placing something upright, while stand up means the movement of rising to your feet.
