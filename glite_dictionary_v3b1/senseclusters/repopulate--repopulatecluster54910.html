repopulate

verb
to fill again
4.1
Pronunciation: /riː.pə.ˈpɑːp.jə.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.pə.ˈpɑːp.jə.leɪt/ (AmE), /riː.pə.ˈpɒp.jʊ.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.pə.ˈpɒp.jʊ.leɪt/ (BrE)
to fill a place again with people, animals, or plants after they have disappeared, declined, or been removed
