faggy

adjective
smoky with cigarettes
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæɡi / (BrE)
smelling of or filled with cigarette smoke, especially in a room or place

Examples

Faggy air
The small bar was faggy and full of lingering cigarette smoke after midnight.
After the party, the living room felt faggy and needed to be aired out.
He disliked staying in faggy hotel rooms where previous guests had smoked.
