Amsonia

noun
genus of flowering plants (Amsonia)
Pronunciation: / æmˈsoʊniə / (AmE), / æmˈsəʊniə /; / amˈsəʊnɪə / (BrE)
a genus of perennial herbaceous plants and subshrubs with milky sap and bluish flowers, native to Europe, Asia Minor, Japan, and North America
