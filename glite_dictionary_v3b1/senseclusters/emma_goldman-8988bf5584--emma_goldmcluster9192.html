Emma Goldman

proper noun
Russian-American anarchist and activist (1869–1940)
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmə ˈɡoʊldmən / (AmE), / ˈɛmə ˈɡəʊldmən / (BrE)
(1869–1940) An influential Russian-born American anarchist, writer, and activist known for her advocacy of free speech, birth control, and workers' rights, and for her opposition to conscription during World War I.

Examples

Emma Goldman activist
Emma Goldman was a prominent figure in early 20th-century anarchist movements.
