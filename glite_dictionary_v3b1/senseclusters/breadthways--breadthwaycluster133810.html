breadthways

adverb
across the breadth (adverb)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛdθˌweɪz / (AmE), / ˈbrɛdθˌweɪz / (BrE)
In the direction of the breadth; across the shorter side of something.

Examples

Lay planks breadthways
Lay the planks breadthways to cover the entire width of the floor.
The fabric should be folded breadthways before cutting out the pattern.
The tablecloth was placed breadthways across the table for a different look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breadthwise - in the direction of the breadth
  • antonym: lengthways - in the direction of the length
