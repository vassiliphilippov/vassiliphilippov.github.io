dictator

noun
a person who acts in a domineering way
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkˌteɪtər / (AmE), / dɪkˈteɪtə / (BrE)
A person who behaves in an autocratic or tyrannical way, often imposing their will on others without regard for their opinions.

Examples

Family dictator
At home, her brother can be a real dictator about what to watch on TV.
Everyone avoided arguing with the new manager, who acted like a dictator during meetings.
The committee grew frustrated by the chairman’s dictator-like decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autocrat - a person with absolute power and authority
  • antonym: democrat - a person who supports democracy and equal participation
  • synonym: tyrant - a person who uses power cruelly or unjustly
