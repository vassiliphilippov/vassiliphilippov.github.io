mountaintop

noun
summit of a mountain
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊntənˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈmaʊntɪnˌtɒp / (BrE)
the highest point or summit of a mountain

Examples

The mountaintop view
After hours of climbing, we finally stood on the mountaintop and admired the breathtaking view.
Snow covered the mountaintop even in the middle of summer.
The hikers set up camp just below the mountaintop to watch the sunrise.
After hours of climbing, they finally reached the mountaintop and enjoyed the breathtaking view.
Snow often remains on the mountaintop long after it has melted in the valleys below.
From the mountaintop, you can see for miles in every direction on a clear day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: base - the bottom or lowest part of something
  • synonym: peak - the pointed top of a mountain
  • synonym: summit - the highest point of a hill or mountain
