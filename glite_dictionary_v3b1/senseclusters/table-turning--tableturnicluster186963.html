table-turning

noun
a decisive reversal of circumstances
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪbəl ˌtɜrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪbl̩ ˌtɜːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a decisive change in circumstances or the dynamics of a situation, especially one that shifts the advantage from one party to another

Examples

A table-turning moment
Her brilliant strategy resulted in a table-turning that surprised everyone.
It was a night of table-turning as the underdogs defeated the champions.
The team managed a dramatic table-turning in the final quarter of the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turn the tables - to reverse a situation in one's favor
