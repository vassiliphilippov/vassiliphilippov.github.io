saver

noun
a person who saves money
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɚ /; / ˈseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈseɪvə / (BrE)
a person who saves money, energy, or resources instead of spending or wasting them

Examples

A careful saver
As a lifelong saver, he always plans for unexpected expenses.
Banks often offer higher interest rates to encourage savers.
She is a disciplined saver who puts aside part of her salary every month.
As a careful saver, she always puts aside a portion of her salary each month.
Many savers prefer to invest their money in secure accounts with steady interest.
Being a saver helped him afford a new car without taking out a loan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: economizer - a person who uses resources carefully to avoid waste
  • antonym: spender - a person who spends money freely
