forme

noun
shape or appearance
Pronunciation: / fɔːrm / (AmE), / fɔːm / (BrE)
the shape, figure, or outward appearance of something, used in archaic or literary contexts

Examples

The forme of a statue
The ancient manuscript described the forme of the mythical creature in great detail.
In the dim light, the forme of the castle appeared mysterious and grand.
The poet admired the delicate forme of the statue against the evening sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: structure - the arrangement or organization of parts in an object
