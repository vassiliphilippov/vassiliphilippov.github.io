paranoid

adjective
suffering from paranoia
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛrəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈpærənɔɪd / (BrE)
Suffering from paranoia or showing unreasonable fear or suspicion of others.

Examples

Paranoid feelings
His paranoid thoughts made it difficult for him to trust even his closest friends.
She became increasingly paranoid after the break-in, always suspecting someone was watching her.
Some people may act paranoid when facing high levels of stress or uncertainty.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suspicious - having or showing a cautious distrust of someone or something
  • antonym: trusting - willing to rely on the honesty or reliability of others
