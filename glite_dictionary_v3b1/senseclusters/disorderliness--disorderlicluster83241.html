disorderliness

noun
rowdy or unruly behavior
3.3
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈɔrdɚli.nəs / (AmE), / dɪsˈɔːdəli.nɪs / (BrE)

Examples

Classroom disorderliness

Teachers were concerned about the disorderliness during school assemblies.
The disorderliness of the crowd forced the police to intervene.
