reception

noun
quality of a received broadcast signal
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the quality or clarity of the signal received by a radio, television, or other broadcast device

Examples

Radio reception
Clear weather usually improves radio reception in this area.
The hotel's remote location meant the reception on my phone was very poor.
We couldn't watch the match because the television reception kept breaking up.
