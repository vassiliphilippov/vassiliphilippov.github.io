tutting

noun
expression of disapproval
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtʌt.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈtʌt.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or sound of expressing disapproval by making a 'tut' noise with the tongue or mouth

Examples

Annoyed tutting
There was a lot of tutting when the children ran through the museum.
She responded to the bad news with a loud tutting that everyone could hear.
The teacher's tutting made the students realize they were being too noisy.
