undrunk

adjective
not consumed
8.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdrʌŋk / (AmE), / ʌnˈdrʌŋk / (BrE)
not consumed as a drink, or not affected by drinking alcohol

Examples

An undrunk cup
The cup of tea remained undrunk on the table after the meeting ended.
She poured out the undrunk wine before cleaning the kitchen.
Despite the celebration, he left the party completely undrunk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drunk - consumed as a drink or intoxicated by alcohol
  • synonym: unconsumed - not eaten or drunk
