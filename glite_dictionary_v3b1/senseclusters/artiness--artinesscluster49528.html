artiness

noun
artistic skill
6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈɑːr.tiː.nəs/ /; / /ˈɑːr.tɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈɑː.tiː.nəs/ /; / /ˈɑː.tɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of showing skill, cleverness, or inventiveness in creating or doing something, often in an artistic way

Examples

Subtle artiness
The gallery was filled with paintings that radiated a quiet artiness appreciated by visitors.

The film's artiness was evident in its creative camera angles and lighting.
