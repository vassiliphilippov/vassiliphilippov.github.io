estate agent

noun
person who arranges property sales or rentals
1.9 2.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˈsteɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪˈsteɪt ˌeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange the sale, renting, or management of homes, land, and buildings for clients

Examples

The estate agent showed us several houses before we made our decision.
