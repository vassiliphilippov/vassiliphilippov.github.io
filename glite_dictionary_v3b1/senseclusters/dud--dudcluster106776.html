dud

noun
a thing or event that is a failure
Pronunciation: / dʌd / (AmE), / dʌd / (BrE)
an event or thing that is a complete failure or is ineffective

Examples

A dud show
Everyone was disappointed when the fireworks show ended up being a dud.
His presentation was a dud and failed to impress the audience.
The highly anticipated new product turned out to be a complete dud in the marketplace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: failure - lack of success in achieving or doing something
  • antonym: success - the achievement of a desired aim or result
