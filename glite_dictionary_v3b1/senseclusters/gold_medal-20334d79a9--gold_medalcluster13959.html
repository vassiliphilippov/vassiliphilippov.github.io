gold medal

noun
medal awarded for first place
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊld ˈmɛdəl /; / goʊld ˈmɛdəl / (AmE), / ɡəʊld ˈmɛdəl / (BrE)
a medal made of or colored like gold, given as the highest prize to the winner in a competition, especially in sports

Examples

A gold medal
He has trained for years to earn a gold medal at the Olympics.
She proudly displayed her gold medal after winning the race.
The team celebrated their victory when they were awarded the gold medal.
After years of hard work, she finally won a gold medal at the championship.
He dreams of bringing home a gold medal for his country one day.
The gymnast celebrated her gold medal victory with her teammates.
