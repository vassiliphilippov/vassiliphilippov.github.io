blotter

noun
LSD‑impregnated paper (slang)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈblɑː.tər/ (AmE), /ˈblɒt.ə/ (BrE)
a small square or sheet of blotting paper that has been soaked with LSD (acid) and is used as a single dose for ingestion.

Examples

LSD blotter
Police found a colorful blotter believed to contain LSD during the raid.
He carefully placed a blotter on his tongue and waited for the effects to begin.
The festival's first-aid tent removed several blotters from people feeling dizzy and disoriented.
