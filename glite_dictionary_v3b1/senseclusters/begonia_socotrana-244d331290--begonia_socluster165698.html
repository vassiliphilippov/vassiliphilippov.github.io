Begonia socotrana

noun
a species of begonia native to Yemen
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡoʊniəˌ soʊkoʊˈtrɑːnə / (AmE), / bɪˈɡəʊniə ˌsɒkəˈtrɑːnə / (BrE)
A species of semi-tuberous begonia native to Yemen, known for its peltate leaves and rose-pink flowers.
