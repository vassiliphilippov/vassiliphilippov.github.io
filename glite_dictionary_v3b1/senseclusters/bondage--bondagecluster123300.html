bondage

noun
state of being controlled by a force or influence
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːn.dɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈbɒn.dɪdʒ / (BrE)
the state of being under the control of a force or influence, such as fear or addiction, especially when difficult to escape

Examples

Bondage of fear
He struggled for years to break free from the bondage of addiction.
Her beliefs kept her in mental bondage, unable to accept new ideas.
The story is about a journey from the bondage of fear to personal empowerment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freedom - the power or right to act, speak, or think as one wants
  • synonym: servitude - the state of being a slave or completely subject to someone more powerful
