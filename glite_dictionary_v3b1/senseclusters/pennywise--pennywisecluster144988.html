pennywise

adjective
careful with small sums
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛniˌwaɪz / (AmE), / ˈpɛniˌwaɪz / (BrE)
careful or thrifty in managing small amounts of money, especially minor expenses

Examples

Pennywise spending habits
She is pennywise when it comes to shopping for groceries, always looking for the best deals.
Being pennywise helped him save money on everyday expenses, but he ignored bigger financial issues.
The manager was pennywise about office supplies but spent too much on unnecessary renovations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extravagant - spending money freely or wastefully
  • synonym: frugal - careful in spending money or resources
