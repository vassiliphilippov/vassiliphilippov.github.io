myocardial infarct

noun
destruction of heart muscle due to blocked blood supply

Pronunciation: /maɪ.ə.ˈkɑːr.diː.əl ɪn.ˈfɑːrkt/ /; / /maɪ.oʊ.ˈkɑːr.diː.əl ɪn.ˈfɑːrkt/ (AmE), /maɪ.əʊ.ˈkɑː.di.əl ɪn.ˈfɑːkt/ (BrE)
