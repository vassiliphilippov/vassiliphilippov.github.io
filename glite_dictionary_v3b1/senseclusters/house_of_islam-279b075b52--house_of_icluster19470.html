House of Islam

noun
areas where Muslims are the majority
Pronunciation: / ˌhaʊs əv ɪsˈlɑm / (AmE), / ˌhaʊs əv ɪsˈlɑːm / (BrE)
The parts of the world where Muslims are in the majority, especially considered collectively in Islamic tradition.

Examples

The House of Islam countries
A number of countries in the Middle East and North Africa are considered part of the House of Islam.
Historically, scholars debated the boundaries of the House of Islam and its relation to neighboring regions.
The concept of the House of Islam has influenced geopolitical strategies and interfaith relations throughout history.

Related Terms

  • antonym: House of War - areas not under Muslim rule in Islamic tradition
