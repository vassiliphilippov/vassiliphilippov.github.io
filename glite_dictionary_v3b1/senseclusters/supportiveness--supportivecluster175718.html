supportiveness

noun
degree of support
3.8
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrtɪvnəs / (AmE), / səˈpɔːtɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of providing physical, structural, or mechanical support, holding, or stabilization to something or someone

Examples

Supportiveness of a chair
The supportiveness of the mattress helped him sleep comfortably through the night.
She appreciated the supportiveness of her new office chair during long work hours.
Engineers tested the supportiveness of the bridge under heavy loads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firmness - the quality of being solid or stable
  • antonym: softness - the quality of being soft or yielding easily
