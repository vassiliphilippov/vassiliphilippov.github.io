untoned

adjective
not altered in tone
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtoʊnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈtəʊnd / (BrE)
not having been chemically or digitally toned, especially in photography or printing; left in its original tone

Examples

Untoned photo
The photographer preferred to display the untoned prints to highlight their natural contrast.
After processing, the image remained untoned, preserving its original black-and-white appearance.
Collectors sometimes value untoned photographs for their authenticity and historical accuracy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: original - in its initial or unaltered state
  • antonym: toned - chemically or digitally altered in tone
