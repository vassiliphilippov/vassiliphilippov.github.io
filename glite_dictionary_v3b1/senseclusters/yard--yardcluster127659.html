yard

noun
unit of length (3 feet; 91.44 cm)
Pronunciation: / jɑrd / (AmE), / jɑːd / (BrE)
a unit of length equal to three feet or 36 inches, standardized as 91.44 centimeters

Examples

Fifty yards long
He stood about ten yards away from the goal.
She measured the fabric and bought two yards for her project.
The football field is one hundred yards long.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: foot - a unit of length equal to 12 inches or one third of a yard
  • synonym: meter - the SI base unit of length (approximately 1.094 yards)
