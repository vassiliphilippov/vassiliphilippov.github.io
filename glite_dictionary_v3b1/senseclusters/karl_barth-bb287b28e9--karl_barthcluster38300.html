Karl Barth

proper noun
Swiss Protestant theologian (1886–1968)
Pronunciation: / kɑrl bɑrth / (AmE), / kɑːl bɑːt / (BrE)
(1886–1968) A Swiss Protestant theologian regarded as one of the most influential Christian thinkers of the 20th century, known for founding neo-orthodoxy.

Examples

Karl Barth theology
Karl Barth is often cited as a key figure in modern Protestant theology.
