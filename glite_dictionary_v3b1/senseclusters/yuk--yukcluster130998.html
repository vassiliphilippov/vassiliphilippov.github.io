yuk

interjection
expression of disgust
Pronunciation: / jʌk / (AmE), / jʌk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express disgust, distaste, or strong dislike

Examples

Yuk! That smell
When he opened the lunchbox and saw the moldy sandwich, he shouted yuk.
She wrinkled her nose and said yuk after tasting the bitter medicine.
The children all cried yuk when they found worms in the garden soil.

  • synonym: ugh - an exclamation showing strong dislike or disgust
