distancing

noun
emotional withdrawal
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪs.təns.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdɪs.təns.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of creating emotional or social distance from someone or something, often by withdrawing or avoiding closeness

Examples

Emotional distancing after conflict
After the argument, she practiced distancing to protect herself from further emotional harm.
Therapists sometimes notice distancing behaviors in clients who feel overwhelmed by their emotions.
His sudden distancing from the group made his friends worry about his well-being.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avoidance - the act of keeping away from something or someone
  • antonym: engagement - the act of being involved or connected with others
  • synonym: withdrawal - the act of pulling away from social or emotional involvement
