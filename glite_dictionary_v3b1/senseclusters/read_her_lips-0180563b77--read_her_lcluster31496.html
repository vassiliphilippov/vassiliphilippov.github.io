read her lips

verb
interpret speech by lip movements (female subject)
Pronunciation: / riːd hɚ lɪps / (AmE), / riːd hə lɪps / (BrE)
to interpret what a woman is saying by observing the movement of her lips, especially when unable to hear her voice

Examples

To read her lips carefully
Even though the audio was off, he managed to read her lips during the televised interview.
She was whispering across the room, and I tried to read her lips to understand the message.
Standing outside the window, she attempted to read her lips, hoping to catch what was being said.

  • synonym: read his lips - interpret what a man is saying from his lip movements
