stop over

verb
temporarily interrupt journey, often overnight
2.2 3.6
Pronunciation: / stɑp ˈoʊvər /; / ˈstɑp ˌoʊvər / (AmE), / stɒp ˈəʊvə /; / ˈstɒp ˌəʊvə / (BrE)
to interrupt a journey by staying somewhere temporarily (often overnight) before continuing to your destination
