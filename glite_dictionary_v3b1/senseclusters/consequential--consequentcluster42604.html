consequential

adjective
having important results; significant
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsəˈkwɛnʃəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl / (BrE)
having important consequences or results; significant in effect or outcome.

Examples

A consequential impact
Her research had a consequential impact on public health policy.
Only the most consequential cases will be heard by the Supreme Court.
This was a consequential decision for the future of the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: important - of great significance or value
  • synonym: significant - having meaning or importance
  • antonym: trivial - of little value or importance
