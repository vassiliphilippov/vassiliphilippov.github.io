medieval mode

noun
musical scale in early Western music
Pronunciation: / mɪˈdiːvəl moʊd / (AmE), / mɪˈdiːvəl məʊd / (BrE)
any of the musical modes used in Gregorian chant and Western music until about 1600, originating from Greek modes

Examples

Medieval mode music
