gem

noun
small, sweet quick bread; muffin-like cake
Pronunciation: / dʒɛm / (AmE), / dʒɛm / (BrE)
A type of sweet quick bread baked in a small, cup-shaped pan, similar to a muffin or cupcake.

Examples

Fresh gems for breakfast
For breakfast, she served warm gems with butter and homemade jam.
Freshly baked gems are best enjoyed with a hot cup of tea.
My grandmother taught me how to bake gems in her old cast-iron pan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cupcake - a small cake baked in a cup-shaped foil or paper container and typically iced
  • synonym: muffin - a small domed spongy cake made from batter and baked in a cup-shaped mold
