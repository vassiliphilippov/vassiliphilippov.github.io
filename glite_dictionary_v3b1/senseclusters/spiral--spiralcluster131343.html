spiral

noun
downward helical flight path
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪ.rəl / (AmE), / ˈspaɪ.rəl / (BrE)
a downward movement or flight in a helical path with a large radius, especially in the air

Examples

Spiral of smoke
Smoke rose in a slow spiral as the wind began to swirl.
The hawk went into a spiral as it dived toward its prey.
The pilot executed a controlled spiral to lose altitude safely.
