menstruation

noun
monthly discharge of uterine blood
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛnstruˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛnstruˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the monthly flow of blood from the uterus that occurs in women and other female mammals from puberty to menopause, except during pregnancy

Examples

Monthly menstruation flow
Education about menstruation helps reduce stigma and promote good hygiene.
Irregular menstruation can be a sign of underlying health problems.
She kept track of her menstruation dates in a journal.

  • synonym: period - the recurring cycle of menstruation in women
