menstruation

noun
monthly discharge of uterine blood
5.0 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛnstruˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛnstruˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
the monthly flow of blood from the uterus that occurs in women and other female mammals from puberty to menopause, except during pregnancy
