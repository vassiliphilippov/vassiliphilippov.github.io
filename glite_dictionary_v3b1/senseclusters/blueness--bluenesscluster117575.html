blueness

noun
the quality of being blue
2.7 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbluÀên…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàbluÀên…™s / (BrE)
the quality or state of being blue; the characteristic of resembling the color of the clear sky in the daytime

Examples

The blueness of the sky was striking against the white clouds above the valley.
