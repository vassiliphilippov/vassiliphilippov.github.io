edutainment

noun
entertainment with educational content
Pronunciation: / ˌɛdʒuˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / ˌɛdjʊˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
Entertainment designed to educate as well as amuse, especially in television, film, or digital media.

Examples

Edutainment video games
Educational video games provide a blend of edutainment that keeps kids engaged while they learn.
Many TV shows now incorporate edutainment elements to teach viewers about history and culture.
The new science museum uses edutainment to make learning fun for children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infotainment - broadcast material that is intended both to entertain and to inform
