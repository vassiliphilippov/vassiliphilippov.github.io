Cardinal Richelieu

proper noun
French cardinal and statesman (1585–1642)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrdɪnəl rɪʃəˈlu / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdɪnəl ˈrɪʃəljuː / (BrE)
