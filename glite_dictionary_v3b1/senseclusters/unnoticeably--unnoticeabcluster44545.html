unnoticeably

adverb
in an imperceptible way
6.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnoʊtɪsəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊtɪsəbli / (BrE)
In a way that is difficult or impossible to notice; imperceptibly.

Examples

To leave unnoticeably
She slipped out of the meeting unnoticeably so as not to disturb anyone.
The stain faded unnoticeably over several months.
The temperature in the room dropped unnoticeably as the night went on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imperceptibly - in a way that is impossible or difficult to perceive
