sheet

noun
newspaper with half-size pages
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ʃit / (AmE), / ʃiːt / (BrE)

Examples

A daily sheet

The latest gossip was splashed across the front page of the evening sheet.
Unlike the broadsheets, the local sheet focused on sensational stories and photographs.
