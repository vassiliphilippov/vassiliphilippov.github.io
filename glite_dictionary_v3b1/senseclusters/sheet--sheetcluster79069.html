sheet

noun
newspaper with half-size pages
4.4 3.19/m
Pronunciation: /  Éit / (AmE), /  ÉiÀêt / (BrE)
A newspaper with pages that are half the size of a standard broadsheet.

Examples

A daily sheet
She bought a sheet every morning to catch up on the neighborhood news.
The latest gossip was splashed across the front page of the evening sheet.
Unlike the broadsheets, the local sheet focused on sensational stories and photographs.

Synonyms

  • tabloid
    3.7 8.19/m
