Braillewriter

noun
Braille writing machine
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪlˌraɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbreɪlˌraɪtə / (BrE)
a machine used to produce written text in Braille for people who are blind or visually impaired

Examples

A Braillewriter machine
The teacher demonstrated how to use a Braillewriter to create documents for blind students.
After her vision loss, he bought her a Braillewriter so she could continue writing letters.
Modern technology has made the Braillewriter more portable and easier to use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Braille - a system of writing for the blind using raised dots
  • synonym: typewriter - a machine for writing characters similar to those produced by a printer's type
