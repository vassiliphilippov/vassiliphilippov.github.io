blockader

noun
obstructing device
7.4
Pronunciation: / blɑˈkeɪdər / (AmE), / blɒˈkeɪdə / (BrE)
a device or structure used to block or obstruct passage through a road, entrance, or channel

Examples

Street blockader
The city installed a blockader at the end of the street to stop unauthorized vehicles.
During the protest, a makeshift blockader prevented people from entering the building.
Workers placed a large metal blockader across the entrance for safety reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - a structure that blocks or restricts movement
  • antonym: gateway - an opening or passage that allows entry
  • synonym: obstacle - something that blocks progress or movement
