creative

adjective
involving creation or original ideas
1.5
Pronunciation: / kɹiˈeɪtɪv /; / kriˈeɪtɪv / (AmE), / kɹiːˈeɪtɪv /; / kriˈeɪtɪv / (BrE)
using imagination or original ideas to create something; able to invent or produce new things

Examples

Creative thinking
She decorated her apartment with creative artwork that she made herself.
The company encourages employees to find creative solutions to everyday problems.
The novel was praised for its creative approach to storytelling and unique narrative style.
He has a very creative mind and always comes up with original solutions.
Her creative energy inspired the whole team.
The company is looking for someone with a creative approach to design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inventive - having the ability to create or design new things
  • antonym: unimaginative - lacking originality or inventiveness
