pretty boy

noun
attractive young man
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪti bɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈprɪti bɔɪ / (BrE)

Examples

The pretty boy image

The new actor is more than just a pretty boy; he’s talented too.
