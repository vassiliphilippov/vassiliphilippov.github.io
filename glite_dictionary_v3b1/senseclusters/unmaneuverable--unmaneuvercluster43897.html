unmaneuverable

adjective
hard to control
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnməˈnuːvərəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnməˈnjuːvərəbəl / (BrE)
difficult or impossible to control, change, or direct, especially in reference to systems, organizations, or situations

Examples

Unmaneuverable system
The outdated bureaucracy was unmaneuverable, making any attempt at reform slow and frustrating.
Because the plan was so complex, it quickly became unmaneuverable and hard to adjust.
Some large organizations are unmaneuverable when it comes to responding to sudden changes.

  • synonym: unmanageable - difficult or impossible to manage or control
