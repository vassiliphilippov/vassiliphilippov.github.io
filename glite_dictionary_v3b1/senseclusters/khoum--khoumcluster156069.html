khoum

noun
Mauritanian monetary unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / kuːm / (AmE), / kuːm / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Mauritania, equal to one-fifth of an ouguiya

Examples

Five khoums
Five khoums are needed to make one ouguiya.
She collected rare khoum coins from her trip to Mauritania.
The smallest coin available in Mauritania was the khoum.
