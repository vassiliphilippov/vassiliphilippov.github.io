oscillometric

adjective
measuring oscillations
Pronunciation: / ˌɑsələˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒsɪləˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of oscillations, especially in medical devices for monitoring blood pressure

Examples

The oscillometric method
The doctor preferred the oscillometric method for its accuracy in measuring blood pressure.
Modern automatic blood pressure monitors often use oscillometric technology.
An oscillometric reading can be less affected by patient movement than traditional methods.
