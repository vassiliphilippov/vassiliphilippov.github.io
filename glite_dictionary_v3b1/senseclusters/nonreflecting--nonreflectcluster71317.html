nonreflecting

adjective
not capable of reflecting light or energy
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɪˈflɛktɪŋ / (BrE)
Not capable of reflecting light, sound, or other energy; not producing a reflected image or signal.

Examples

Nonreflecting surface
Astronomers use nonreflecting surfaces inside telescopes to improve image clarity.
Nonreflecting coatings are commonly used on camera lenses to minimize unwanted reflections.
The glass was treated to become nonreflecting, reducing glare in bright sunlight.
