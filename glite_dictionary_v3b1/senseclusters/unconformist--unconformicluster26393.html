unconformist

adjective
not conforming to social norms
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkənˈfɔrmɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkənˈfɔːmɪst / (BrE)
not conforming to established customs, attitudes, or norms; behaving or thinking differently from what is generally accepted

Examples

An unconformist attitude
He has always been an unconformist in his approach to art and creativity.
Her unconformist outlook often set her apart from her peers.
The company's unconformist policies attracted both admiration and criticism.
