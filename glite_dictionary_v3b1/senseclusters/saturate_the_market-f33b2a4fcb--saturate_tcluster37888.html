to supply so much of a product or service that the demand is fully met and no further sales can be made without reducing prices or displacing other providers

Examples

To saturate the market quickly
After several companies entered the industry, they began to saturate the market and profits declined.
If you saturate the market too quickly, some products may remain unsold.
The smartphone manufacturer tried to saturate the market by launching devices in every price range.
