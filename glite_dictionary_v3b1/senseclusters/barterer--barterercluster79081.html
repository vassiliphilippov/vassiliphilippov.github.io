barterer

noun
person who trades by barter
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrtərər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːtərə / (BrE)
a person who trades goods or services by exchanging them directly for other goods or services, rather than using money

Examples

A barterer trades goods
In ancient marketplaces, a skilled barterer could secure valuable items without spending a coin.
The barterer exchanged a sack of grain for a set of handmade tools.
The barterer thrived in communities where currency was scarce or unavailable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buyer - a person who purchases goods or services
  • synonym: trader - a person who buys and sells goods or assets
