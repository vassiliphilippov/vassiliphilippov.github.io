chayote

noun
edible plant shoots
7.5
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪˈoʊteɪ / (AmE), / tʃaɪˈəʊti / (BrE)
the tender shoots or leaves of the chayote plant, eaten as a vegetable in various cuisines

Examples

Chayote shoots salad
She added fresh chayote shoots to the soup for extra flavor and nutrition.
Farmers harvest chayote leaves early in the season to sell at local markets.
In some regions, people stir-fry chayote with garlic and serve it as a side dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinach - an edible leafy green vegetable
