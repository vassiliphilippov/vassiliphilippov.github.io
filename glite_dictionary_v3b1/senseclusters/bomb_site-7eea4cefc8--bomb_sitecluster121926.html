bomb site

noun
area devastated by bombs
5.5
Pronunciation: / bɑːm saɪt / (AmE), / bɒm saɪt / (BrE)
an area, especially in a town or city, that has been severely damaged or destroyed by bombs

Examples

A bomb site area
After the air raid, the city was left with a vast bomb site in the heart of downtown.
Developers plan to build new housing where the old bomb site was cleared.
The children played among the rubble of the bomb site years after the war ended.
