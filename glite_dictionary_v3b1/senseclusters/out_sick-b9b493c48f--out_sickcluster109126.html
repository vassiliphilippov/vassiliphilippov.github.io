out sick

adjective
absent due to illness
Pronunciation: / aʊt sɪk / (AmE), / aʊt sɪk / (BrE)
absent from work, school, or usual activities because of illness

Examples

Out sick from work
He had to cancel the meeting because he was out sick with a bad cold.
Several students were out sick during the peak of the flu season.
She emailed her manager to let him know she was out sick today.

Related Terms

  • synonym: off sick - absent from work or duties due to illness (used especially in British English)
