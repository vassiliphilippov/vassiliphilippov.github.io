crema

noun
espresso foam layer
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪmə / (AmE), / ˈkrɛmə / (BrE)
a layer of creamy, golden foam that forms on top of a freshly made espresso or certain desserts

Examples

Espresso crema
A perfect espresso should have a thick layer of crema on top.
The rich crema added a smooth texture to the coffee.
Baristas often judge the quality of espresso by the color and consistency of its crema.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foam - a mass of small bubbles formed on or in liquid
  • synonym: froth - a mass of small bubbles on the surface of a liquid
