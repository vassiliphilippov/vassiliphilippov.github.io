warm smile

noun
a friendly, cheerful smile
Pronunciation: / wɔrm smaɪl / (AmE), / wɔːm smaɪl / (BrE)
a smile that expresses friendliness and genuine cheerfulness

Examples

A warm smile
His warm smile made everyone in the room feel welcome.
She greeted the children with a warm smile that instantly put them at ease.
The manager offered a warm smile as she introduced herself to the new employees.
