wintery

adjective
characteristic of winter
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.təri / (BrE)
Characteristic of or typical of winter; relating to or resembling winter.

Examples

A wintery mix
A wintery mix of sleet and rain made the roads slippery.
She wore a coat and scarf to brave the wintery wind outside.
The wintery landscape was covered with a fresh layer of snow.
