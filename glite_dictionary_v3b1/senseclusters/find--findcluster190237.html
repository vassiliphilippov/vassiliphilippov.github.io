find

noun
thing or fact discovered
Pronunciation: / faɪnd / (AmE), / faɪnd / (BrE)
An object or fact that is discovered, especially one of interest or value.

Examples

A rare find
Her last find was a vintage watch worth thousands of dollars.
That rare book was a real find at the flea market.
The archaeologists called the ancient vase an extraordinary find.
