nail-biting finish

noun
exciting, suspenseful ending
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋ ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋ ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
a very exciting or suspenseful ending to a situation or event, especially a competition

Examples

A nail-biting finish moment
Fans cheered wildly during the nail-biting finish of the basketball tournament.
Nobody expected such a nail-biting finish to what seemed like an ordinary race.
The match ended in a nail-biting finish that kept everyone on the edge of their seats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cliffhanger - an ending that leaves the audience in suspense
  • synonym: photo finish - a finish in a race so close that results are determined by examining a photograph
