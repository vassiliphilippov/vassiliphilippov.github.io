fogging

noun
mist spraying for control
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡɪŋ / (BrE)
the spraying of a fine mist, especially of insecticide or disinfectant, to control pests or disease

Examples

Mosquito fogging
The city scheduled fogging in residential areas to reduce the mosquito population.
After the outbreak, regular fogging was carried out in public buildings for safety.
Residents were advised to stay indoors during the fogging of the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disinfection - the process of cleaning to destroy harmful microorganisms
  • synonym: fumigation - the use of smoke or gas to disinfect or destroy pests
