since

preposition
from past until now
Pronunciation: / sɪns / (AmE), / sɪns / (BrE)
from a specific time in the past up to the present or another point in time

Examples

Since last year
He has worked at this company since 2015 and has gained a lot of experience.
We have not seen each other since the school reunion last year.
The museum has been closed since the renovation started in March.

Related Terms

  • synonym: after - following a particular event or time
  • antonym: until - up to a particular time or event
