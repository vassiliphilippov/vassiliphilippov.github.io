bonum

noun
philosophical good
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnəm / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnəm / (BrE)
a good thing or the concept of good, especially as discussed in philosophy or ethics

Examples

The highest bonum
In ancient philosophy, the highest bonum was often considered the ultimate goal of life.
The debate centered on whether happiness or virtue should be regarded as the true bonum.
He wrote an essay exploring the nature of the bonum in ethical theory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: good - that which is morally right or beneficial
