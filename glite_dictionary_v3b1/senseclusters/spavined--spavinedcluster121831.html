spavined

adjective
afflicted by spavin (horse's hock-joint swelling)
Pronunciation: / ˈspævɪnd / (AmE), / ˈspævɪnd / (BrE)
(of a horse) afflicted with or affected by spavin, a disease causing swelling and lameness in the hock-joint.

Examples

A spavined horse
A spavined horse may show signs of lameness during exercise.
Owners should provide special care for spavined animals to ease their discomfort.
The veterinarian diagnosed the old mare as spavined after noticing swelling in her hock.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spavin - a swelling of the hock-joint in horses
