Tunga

noun
genus of fleas (including chigoe flea)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌŋɡə / (AmE), / ˈtʌŋɡə / (BrE)
a genus of fleas (including the chigoe flea) known for burrowing into the skin of mammals

Examples

The chigoe flea, classified as Tunga penetrans, is known for burrowing into human skin.
