Brule

noun
a group of Teton Sioux people
Pronunciation: / bruːˈleɪ / (AmE), / bruːˈleɪ / (BrE)
A member of a division of the Teton Sioux people, now living mainly on the Rosebud and Lower Brule Indian Reservations in South Dakota.

Examples

Brule Sioux people
A delegation of Brule leaders met with government officials to discuss land rights.
Many Brule still maintain traditional customs and ceremonies on the Rosebud Reservation.
The Brule played an important role in the history of the northern Great Plains.
