irreplaceably

adverb
cannot be replaced
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈpleɪsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be replaced or substituted by anything else

Examples

An irreplaceably lost item
The handwritten letters were irreplaceably lost in the fire.
Some childhood memories are irreplaceably precious to her.
The museum's rare painting was irreplaceably damaged during the move.

  • synonym: uniquely - in a way that is one of a kind
