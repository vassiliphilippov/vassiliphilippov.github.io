Examples

Coal power station

Synonyms

  • power plant
    2.9 11/m
    power plant is the common term for a large facility that generates electricity for homes and businesses, while power station names the same site.
  • electrical plant
    3.8 0.05/m
    electrical plant is a more technical term for a utility facility that generates and supplies electricity, while power station is the everyday name.
  • powerhouse
    4.7 0.28/m
    powerhouse often means a very strong person or team and sometimes a power-producing building, while power station is the usual term for a generating site.
