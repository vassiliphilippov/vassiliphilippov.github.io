propjet

noun
plane with turbojet-driven propeller
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑpˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈprɒpˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
an airplane powered by a turbojet engine that drives an external propeller

Examples

A propjet airplane
A propjet combines the efficiency of a propeller with the speed of a jet engine.
Many modern commuter aircraft are designed as propjets for improved fuel economy.
The airline recently added several new propjets to its regional fleet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turboprop - an aircraft powered by a turbine engine which drives a propeller
