against

preposition
contrary to expectation
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɛnst / (AmE), / əˈɡɛnst / (BrE)

Examples

Against the rules

The team played against their biggest rivals in the final match.
She leaned against the wall while waiting for her friend.
Many people spoke out against the proposed changes to the policy.
