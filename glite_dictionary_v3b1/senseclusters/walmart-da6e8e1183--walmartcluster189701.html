Walmart

proper noun
US retail chain
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑlˌmɑrt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːlmɑːt / (BrE)
a large American chain of discount department and grocery stores known for low prices

Examples

Walmart store
She bought all her school supplies at Walmart because the prices were lower than other stores.
Many small towns rely on Walmart as their main source for groceries and household goods.
During the holiday season, Walmart is often crowded with shoppers looking for deals.

  • synonym: Target - a major American retail chain offering a wide range of products
