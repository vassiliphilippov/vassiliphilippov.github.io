fire company

noun
group organized to fight fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ˌkʌmpəni / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌkʌmpəni / (BrE)
a group of people, especially in a local area, organized and equipped to fight fires, often within a fire department

Examples

Volunteer fire company
Donations were collected to help the volunteer fire company purchase new equipment.
Each member of the fire company undergoes rigorous training to ensure public safety.
The local fire company responded quickly to the house fire on Maple Street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fire brigade - a group of firefighters organized to combat fires, especially in British English
