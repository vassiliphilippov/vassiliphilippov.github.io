one-half

noun
one of two equal parts (half)
3.5
Pronunciation: / wʌn hæf / (AmE), / wʌn hɑːf / (BrE)
Either of two equal parts into which something can be divided; half.

Examples

A one-half share
He divided the pizza into one-half for each of the two children.
If you cut the rope in one-half, each piece will be equal in length.
Only one-half of the tickets were sold by the end of the week.

  • synonym: half - one of two equal parts; 50%
