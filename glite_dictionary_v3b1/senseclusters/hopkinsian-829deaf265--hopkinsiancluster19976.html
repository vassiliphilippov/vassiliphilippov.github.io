Hopkinsian

adjective
of Gerard Manley Hopkins
Pronunciation: / hɑpˈkɪnziən / (AmE), / hɒpˈkɪnziən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Gerard Manley Hopkins or his works

Examples

Hopkinsian poetry style
The poet's use of Hopkinsian language made the verses feel innovative and fresh.
Her analysis focused on the Hopkinsian qualities of rhythm and imagery in the collection.
Many critics admire the Hopkinsian style for its musicality and complexity.
