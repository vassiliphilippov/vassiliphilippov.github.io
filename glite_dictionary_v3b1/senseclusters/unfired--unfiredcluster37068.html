unfired

adjective
not baked in kiln
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfaɪɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfaɪəd / (BrE)
not heated or baked in a kiln, especially referring to clay, pottery, or bricks before hardening

Examples

Unfired clay
The artist shaped the unfired clay into a delicate vase before letting it dry.
These unfired bricks are still soft and must be handled carefully.
You should not paint the unfired pottery until it has dried completely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fired - heated or baked in a kiln to harden
  • synonym: raw - in a natural or unprocessed state
