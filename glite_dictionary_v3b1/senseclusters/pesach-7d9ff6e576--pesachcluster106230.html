Pesach

noun
Jewish festival of Passover
9.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpeɪ.sɑːk/ /; / /ˈpeɪ.sæk/ (AmE), /ˈpeɪ.sɑːk/ /; / /ˈpeɪ.sæk/ (BrE)
The Jewish festival of Passover, commemorating the exodus of the Israelites from Egypt, traditionally celebrated for eight days beginning on the 15th of Nissan.

Examples

Pesach festival
During Pesach, it is customary to avoid bread and eat matzah instead.
Many Jewish families gather together to celebrate Pesach with a special meal called the Seder.
The story of the exodus is retold each year at Pesach in Jewish households around the world.
