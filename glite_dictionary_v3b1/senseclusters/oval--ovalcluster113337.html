oval

noun
an elongated round shape; ellipse-like curve
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊ.vəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊ.vəl / (BrE)
a closed curve on a plane, shaped like an elongated circle or ellipse, having no sharp corners and a single continuous edge

Examples

Draw an oval shape
Please draw an oval around your answer on the test sheet.
The artist painted a large oval on the canvas to represent the face.
The stadium was built in the shape of an oval to accommodate the track.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ellipse - a regular oval shape, traced by a point moving so that the sum of its distances from two other points is constant
