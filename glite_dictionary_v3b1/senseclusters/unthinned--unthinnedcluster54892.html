unthinned

adjective
not made thinner
6.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɪnd / (BrE)
not made thinner, less dense, or reduced in number or amount

Examples

The unthinned paint
The unthinned paint was too thick to apply smoothly to the wall.
Walking through the unthinned forest took much longer than expected.
The farmer left several rows of carrots unthinned to see how they would grow.
