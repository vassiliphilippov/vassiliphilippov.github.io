sportily

adverb
in a lively manner
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈspɔːrtɪli / (AmE), / ˈspɔːtɪli / (BrE)
in a lively, energetic, or stylish manner, often resembling sportswear or an active attitude

Examples

To dress sportily
He walked sportily into the room, full of confidence and energy.
The children were dressed sportily for their day at the park.
She decorated her apartment sportily, with bright colors and athletic posters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dully - in a boring or unenergetic way
  • synonym: energetically - in a way that shows a lot of energy
  • synonym: stylishly - in a fashionable or elegant way
