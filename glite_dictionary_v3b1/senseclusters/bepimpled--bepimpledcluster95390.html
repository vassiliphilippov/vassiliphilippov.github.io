bepimpled

verb
to cover with pimples
Pronunciation: / bɪˈpɪmpəld / (AmE), / bɪˈpɪmpəld / (BrE)

After eating too much chocolate, her cheeks became bepimpled overnight.
The old painting was bepimpled with tiny spots from years of neglect.
