nonconclusive

adjective
not decisive
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈkluːsɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈkluːsɪv / (BrE)
not leading to a definite or final conclusion; not decisive

Examples

A nonconclusive result
The test results were nonconclusive, so the doctor ordered additional examinations.
Because the data was nonconclusive, the researchers could not publish their findings yet.
The committee's investigation ended with a nonconclusive report, leaving many questions unanswered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: decisive - producing a definite result or conclusion
  • synonym: inconclusive - not producing a clear or definite result
