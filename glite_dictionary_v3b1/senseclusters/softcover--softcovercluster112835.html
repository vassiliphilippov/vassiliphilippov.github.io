softcover

adjective
with a flexible cover
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔftˌkʌvər / (AmE), / ˈsɒftˌkʌvə / (BrE)
having a soft or flexible cover, especially referring to books published as paperbacks rather than hardbacks

Examples

Softcover edition
The softcover version of the novel was much cheaper than the hardcover edition.
Students often prefer softcover textbooks because they are lighter to carry.
She found a rare softcover copy of the book at the used bookstore.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardcover - a book with a rigid, hard cover
  • synonym: paperback - a book with a soft, flexible cover
