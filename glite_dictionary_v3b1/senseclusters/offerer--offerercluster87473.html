offerer

noun
person who makes an offer
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːfəɹɚ / (AmE), / ˈɒfəɹə / (BrE)
a person who presents something to another for acceptance or rejection; someone who makes an offer

Examples

The offerer of a contract
As the offerer of a job contract, the company outlined clear expectations for the role.
In negotiations, the offerer may withdraw the proposal before it is accepted.
The offerer patiently waited for a response after extending his proposal.

Related Terms

  • antonym: offeree - person to whom an offer is made
  • synonym: offeror - person making an offer, especially in law
