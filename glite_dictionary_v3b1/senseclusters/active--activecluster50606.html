active

adjective
grammatical term; subject performs action
Pronunciation: / ˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˈæktɪv / (BrE)
expressing an action performed by the subject (grammar: active voice)

Examples

Active voice
'She wrote the letter' is an example of an active construction.
English teachers often ask students to rewrite passive sentences in the active form.
In the sentence 'The cat chased the mouse,' the verb is in the active voice.
Grammarians often distinguish between active and passive constructions when analyzing sentence structure.
In the sentence 'She runs every morning', the verb is in the active voice.
The word 'flowing' in 'flowing river' is an active participial adjective.

Related Terms

  • antonym: passive - (of a verb or sentence) expressing an action received by the subject rather than performed by it
