anticonscription

adjective
opposed to the draft
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntikaˈnskrɪpʃən / (AmE), / ˌæntikənˈskrɪpʃən / (BrE)

Examples

His anticonscription views were shaped by his family's experiences in previous wars.
