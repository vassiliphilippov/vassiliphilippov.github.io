order Anostraca

noun
taxonomic order of fairy and brine shrimps
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈɔːr.dər ə.ˈnɑː.strə.kə/ (AmE), /ˈɔː.də ə.ˈnɒs.trə.kə/ (BrE)
A taxonomic order of small aquatic crustaceans, including fairy shrimps and brine shrimps, characterized by the absence of a carapace.
