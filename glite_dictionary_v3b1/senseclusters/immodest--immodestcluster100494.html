immodest

adjective
lacking modesty; boastful
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɑdəst / (AmE), / ɪˈmɒdɪst / (BrE)
having or showing an exaggerated opinion of your own importance, ability, etc.; lacking humility or modesty

Examples

Immodest boast
His immodest claims of success made him unpopular with colleagues.
She gave an immodest account of her achievements during the interview.
The athlete's immodest boasting irritated fans and opponents alike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceited - having an excessively high opinion of oneself
  • antonym: modest - having a humble opinion of one's own importance
