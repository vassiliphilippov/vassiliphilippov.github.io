listless

adjective
lacking energy or vitality
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪstləs / (AmE), / ˈlɪstləs / (BrE)
lacking energy, enthusiasm, or spirit; without liveliness

Examples

Feeling listless and tired
After the long meeting, she felt listless and drained of all energy.
He gave a listless performance, lacking his usual passion on stage.
The hot weather made the usually playful children seem listless and tired.
A listless crowd watched the team lose for the third time in a row.
After the long flight, I felt so listless that I could barely keep my eyes open.
The patient's listless appearance worried the doctor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathetic - showing or feeling no interest or concern
  • antonym: energetic - having a lot of energy and enthusiasm
  • synonym: lethargic - affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
  • antonym: lively - full of life and energy
