Ragusa

proper noun
former name for Dubrovnik, Croatia
Pronunciation: / rəˈɡuːsə / (AmE), / rəˈɡuːzə / (BrE)
the former name for Dubrovnik, a port city in southwestern Croatia on the Adriatic Sea, known for its historic old town and as a tourist destination

Examples

Ragusa old town
Many medieval travelers wrote about Ragusa as a thriving center of trade and culture.
The Republic of Ragusa was once a major maritime power in the Adriatic.
Today, the ancient walls of Ragusa attract tourists from all over the world.
