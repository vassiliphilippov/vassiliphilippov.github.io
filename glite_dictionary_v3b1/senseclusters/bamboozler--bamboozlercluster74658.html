bamboozler

noun
trickster or deceiver
7.8
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbuːzlər / (AmE), / bæmˈbuːzlə / (BrE)
a person who tricks, deceives, or cheats others, often in a clever or playful way

Examples

A bamboozler tricks people
The bamboozler managed to sell fake tickets to dozens of people before being caught.
Everyone laughed when the playful bamboozler revealed his harmless prank.
She warned her friends not to trust the bamboozler who was always up to some trick.
The clever bamboozler managed to sell fake tickets to dozens of people at the concert.
Everyone warned me that he was a notorious bamboozler who could not be trusted.
She realized too late that the charming bamboozler had tricked her out of her savings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swindler - a person who cheats others out of money or assets
  • synonym: trickster - a person who deceives or cheats people
