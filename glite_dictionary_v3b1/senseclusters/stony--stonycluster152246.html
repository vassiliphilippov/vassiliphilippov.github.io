stony

adjective
full of stones or rocks
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊni / (AmE), / ˈstəʊni / (BrE)
(of ground or an area) full of stones or rocks.

Examples

Stony ground
Farmers struggled to plant crops in the stony soil left after the flood.
The path became almost impassable over the stony hillside, slowing our progress.
We found a quiet spot to sit among the stony outcrops overlooking the valley.
