switchgear

noun
electrical control equipment
6.3
Pronunciation: /ˈswɪtʃ.gɪər/ /; / /ˈswɪtʃ.gɪr/ (AmE), /ˈswɪtʃ.gɪə/ (BrE)
equipment used to control, protect, and isolate electrical circuits and devices in a power system

Examples

Electrical switchgear equipment
The engineer checked the switchgear to ensure the power supply was safe before repairs began.
Modern switchgear is designed to protect both equipment and personnel from electrical faults.
Routine maintenance of the switchgear helps prevent unexpected power outages in the facility.

  • synonym: panelboard - a component that houses electrical circuit breakers and controls
