last rites

noun
religious ceremony at death
2.7 5.7
Pronunciation: /læst raɪts/ (AmE), /lɑːst raɪts/ (BrE)
the religious ceremony or acts performed for a person who is dying or has just died, especially in the Christian or Catholic tradition
