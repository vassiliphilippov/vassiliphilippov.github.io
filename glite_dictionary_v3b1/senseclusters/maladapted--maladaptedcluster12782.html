maladapted

adjective
poorly adjusted
7.6
Pronunciation: /mæ.lə.ˈdæp.tɪd/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈdæp.tɪd/ (AmE), /mæ.lə.ˈdæp.tɪd/ /; / /mə.lə.ˈdæp.tɪd/ (BrE)
having behaviors or coping strategies that are ineffective or harmful, making it difficult to adjust to social or emotional demands

Examples

Maladapted children
Children who are maladapted may struggle to form healthy relationships at school.
The therapy program helps maladapted teens develop better coping skills.
Her maladapted response to stress made it hard for her to succeed at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: well-adapted - able to adjust successfully to new conditions
