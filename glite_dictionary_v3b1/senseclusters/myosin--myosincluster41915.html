myosin

noun
muscle protein involved in contraction
5.8 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.ə.sən/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.oʊ.sɪn/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.əʊ.sɪn/ /; / /ˈmaɪ.ə.sən/ (BrE)

Examples
