twank

verb
to pluck with a twang
9.9
Pronunciation: / twæŋk / (AmE), / twæŋk / (BrE)
to make a sharp, resonant plucking or twanging sound, especially by plucking a string or similar object

Examples

To twank a string

The child liked to twank the rubber band and listen to its sound.
She twanked the wire, producing a quick, metallic note.
