battery charger

noun
device for charging rechargeable batteries
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈbæt.ər.iː ˈtʃɑːr.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈbæt.ə.ri ˈtʃɑː.dʒə/ (BrE)
