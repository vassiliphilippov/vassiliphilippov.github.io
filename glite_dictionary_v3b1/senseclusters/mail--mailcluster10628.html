mail

noun
letters and packages transported by post
Pronunciation: / meɪl / (AmE), / meɪl / (BrE)
letters, packages, or other items sent or delivered by the postal service (including the bags or vehicles used to carry them)

Examples

A pile of mail
He sorted through the mail to find the package he was expecting.
The mail was delivered early this morning.
The company receives a large amount of mail every day.
All of the mail was stacked neatly on the kitchen counter.
She sorted through her mail and found a letter from an old friend.
The mail arrived earlier than usual this morning.
Every morning, the mail leaves the post office at 8 AM.
The mail arrived late today because the delivery truck broke down.
The old mail rattled down the country road, delivering letters to remote homes.

  • synonym: post - letters and parcels sent or delivered by the postal service (British English)
