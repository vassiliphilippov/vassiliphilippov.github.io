colostomy

noun
surgical opening from colon to abdomen
7.3 6.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɑstəmi / (AmE), / kəˈlɒstəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation in which an opening is made from the colon to the surface of the abdomen to allow the removal of waste

Examples

A colostomy operation
After his surgery, a colostomy bag was used to collect waste from his abdomen.
Doctors explained the reasons a colostomy might be necessary after treating certain bowel diseases.
The patient adjusted to life with a colostomy more easily than she expected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anastomosis - a surgical connection between two structures
  • synonym: ileostomy - surgical opening from the ileum to the surface of the abdomen
