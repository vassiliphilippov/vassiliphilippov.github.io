throw one's weight around

verb
use power or authority aggressively
5.8
Pronunciation: / θɹoʊ wʌnz weɪt əˈɹaʊnd / (AmE), / θrəʊ wʌnz weɪt əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to use one's position, power, or influence aggressively or in a domineering manner

Examples

To throw one's weight around at work
She dislikes colleagues who throw their weight around and intimidate others with their authority.
The new manager immediately began to throw his weight around by making sweeping changes without consulting anyone.
You shouldn't throw your weight around just because you received a promotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bossy - domineering or authoritative in a way considered overbearing
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low view of one's importance
