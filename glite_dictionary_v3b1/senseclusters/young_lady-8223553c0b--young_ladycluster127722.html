young lady

noun
girl or young woman
Pronunciation: / jʌŋ ˈleɪdi / (AmE), / jʌŋ ˈleɪdi / (BrE)
a girl or young woman, typically in her teens or early twenties

Examples

A young lady reading
As a young lady, she loved to read books in the garden.
He bought a bouquet of flowers for the young lady he was courting.
The young lady at the front desk greeted us with a warm smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: girl - a female child or young woman
  • antonym: old lady - an elderly woman; sometimes used informally for 'wife' or 'mother'
