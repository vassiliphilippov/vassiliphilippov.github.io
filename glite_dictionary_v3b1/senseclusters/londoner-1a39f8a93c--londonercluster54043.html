Londoner

noun
person from or living in London
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌndənər / (AmE), / ˈlʌndənə / (BrE)
a person who was born in or lives in London, England

Examples

A Londoner in England
After living abroad for years, he still misses the energy of being a Londoner.
As a proud Londoner, she knows every corner of the city.
Many Londoners depend on public transport to get to work.
