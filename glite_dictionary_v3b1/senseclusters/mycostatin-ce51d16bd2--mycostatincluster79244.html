Mycostatin

noun
antifungal/antibiotic drug (trade name)
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkoʊˈstætɪn / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkəʊˈstætɪn / (BrE)
A trade name for nystatin, an antifungal and antibiotic drug used to treat infections caused by fungi.

Examples

Mycostatin antifungal medicine
Mycostatin is commonly used in hospitals for patients with weakened immune systems.
