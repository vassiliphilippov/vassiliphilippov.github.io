snappiness

noun
irritability; abruptness
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈsnæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being easily annoyed or speaking in a short, sharp way

Examples

Snappiness in his voice
His snappiness during meetings made it difficult for colleagues to ask questions.
After a long day, her snappiness was understandable but still hurtful to her friends.
The manager apologized for his snappiness after realizing he had upset the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being relaxed and not easily annoyed
  • synonym: irritability - the tendency to become easily annoyed or angered
