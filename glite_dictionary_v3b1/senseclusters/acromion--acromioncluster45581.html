acromion

noun
bony process on the shoulder blade
Pronunciation: / əˈkroʊ.mi.ən / (AmE), / əˈkrəʊ.mi.ən / (BrE)
the bony process on the scapula (shoulder blade) that forms the highest point of the shoulder

Examples

The acromion process
An X-ray revealed a spur on the acromion that was causing discomfort.
Pain near the acromion is a common symptom of shoulder injuries.
The surgeon examined the patient's acromion for any signs of fracture.
