myrmecophile

noun
organism living with ants
7.9 9.9
Pronunciation: /mɜːr.ˈmek.ə.faɪl/ (AmE), /mɜː.ˈmek.ə.faɪl/ (BrE)
An organism, often an insect, that habitually lives in association with ants, especially within their nests.
