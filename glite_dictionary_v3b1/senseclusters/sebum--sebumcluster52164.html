sebum

noun
oily skin secretion
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːbəm / (AmE), / ˈsiːbəm / (BrE)
an oily substance secreted by the sebaceous glands that helps to moisturize and protect the skin and hair

Examples

Sebum secretion
Babies sometimes produce more sebum, making their scalps appear greasy.
Excess sebum can clog pores and contribute to the development of acne.
The dermatologist explained how sebum plays an important role in skin health.

Related Terms
