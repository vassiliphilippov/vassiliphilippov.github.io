sebum

noun
oily skin secretion
4.7 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.bəm/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.bəm/ (BrE)
an oily substance secreted by the sebaceous glands that helps to moisturize and protect the skin and hair

Examples
