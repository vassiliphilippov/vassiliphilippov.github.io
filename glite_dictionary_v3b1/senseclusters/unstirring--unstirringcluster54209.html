unstirring

adjective
steadfast; unmoved
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɜrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɜːrɪŋ / (BrE)
remaining steady and unchanging in feeling, opinion, or behavior; steadfast and not easily moved

Examples

Unstirring support
Her unstirring support for her friend never wavered, even in difficult times.
The leader's unstirring resolve inspired confidence among the team members.
Despite the heated debate, his opinion remained unstirring throughout the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fickle - changing frequently, especially in loyalty or interests
  • synonym: steadfast - firm and unwavering in purpose or loyalty
  • synonym: unwavering - not changing or becoming weaker in determination
