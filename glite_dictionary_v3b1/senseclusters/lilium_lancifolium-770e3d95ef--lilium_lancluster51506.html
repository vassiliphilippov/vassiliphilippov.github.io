Lilium lancifolium

noun
species of lily; tiger lily
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪliəm ˌlæn.sɪˈfoʊ.li.əm / (AmE), / ˈlɪliəm ˌlan.sɪˈfəʊ.li.əm / (BrE)
An East Asian perennial plant with large, reddish-orange, black-spotted flowers with recurved petals, commonly called the tiger lily.

Examples

Lilium lancifolium tiger lily
Lilium lancifolium is often called the tiger lily due to its spotted petals.
Many gardeners grow Lilium lancifolium for its vibrant summer blooms.
The distinctive flowers of Lilium lancifolium can add color to any landscape.
