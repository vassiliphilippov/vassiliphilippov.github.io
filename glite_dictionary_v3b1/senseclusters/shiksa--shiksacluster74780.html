shiksa

noun
non-Jewish woman (derogatory)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪk.sə / (AmE), / ˈʃɪk.sə / (BrE)
(offensive) A term, often derogatory, used by some Jewish people to refer to a non-Jewish woman.

Examples

A shiksa woman
He upset his parents by dating a shiksa, which went against family tradition.
Some people find the term shiksa offensive or outdated and choose not to use it.
The novel explores the stereotypes associated with the word shiksa in modern society.
