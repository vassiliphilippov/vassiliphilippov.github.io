completely indefensible

adjective
impossible to justify or defend
Pronunciation: / kəmˈplitli ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈpliːtli ˌɪndɪˈfɛnsəbl̩ / (BrE)
impossible to justify or defend in any way; utterly without excuse

Examples

Completely indefensible behavior
His comments about the incident were completely indefensible in a professional setting.
Ignoring safety regulations is completely indefensible when lives are at risk.
The company’s actions were completely indefensible and caused outrage among the public.
