square

noun
tool for measuring right angles
Pronunciation: / skwɛr / (AmE), / skweə / (BrE)
a hand tool with two straight arms joined at a right angle, used for constructing or checking right angles, especially in carpentry and metalwork

Examples

A carpenter's square
Before cutting the wood, she used a square to mark a right angle.
The builder couldn't find his square, so he borrowed one from a coworker.
The carpenter checked the corners with a square to ensure they were perfectly aligned.
