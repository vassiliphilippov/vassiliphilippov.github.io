unstacked

adjective
not in a stack
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstækt / (AmE), / ʌnˈstækt / (BrE)
not arranged in a stack or pile; separated from a previously stacked position

Examples

Unstacked boxes
After the delivery, the boxes were unstacked and placed on the shelves.
The chairs remained unstacked after the meeting ended.
He found the files unstacked and scattered across the desk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stacked - arranged in a pile or stack
