seahawk

noun
fish-eating coastal bird
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌhɑk / (AmE), / ˈsiːˌhɔːk / (BrE)
a large bird of prey, such as an osprey, that hunts fish near the sea or coastal areas

Examples

The seahawk hunts fish
The seahawk circled above the waves before diving to catch its prey.
We watched a seahawk build its nest on the tall tree near the shore.
A seahawk soared gracefully over the coastline, searching for fish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eagle - a large bird of prey with a strong beak and keen eyesight
  • synonym: osprey - a large bird of prey that catches fish, often called a seahawk
