ocker

noun
uncultured Australian man
Pronunciation: / ˈɒk.ə / (BrE)
an Australian slang term for a boorish, uncultured, or unsophisticated Australian man, often with a strong accent

Examples

A loud ocker
He was considered a typical ocker because of his loud voice and love of beer.
The movie features an ocker character who always cracks jokes in a thick accent.
Some Australians embrace the ocker stereotype as part of their national identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bogan - an unsophisticated or unrefined person in Australia
