pit bull terrier

noun
breed of muscular American terrier
7.4 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪt bʊl təˈrɪr.i / (AmE), / ˈpɪt bʊl tɛˈrɪə / (BrE)
An American breed of muscular terriers with a short, stiff, close-lying coat, known for their strength and agility.
