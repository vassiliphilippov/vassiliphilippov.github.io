fix

noun
determination of position or location
3.9 5.3
Pronunciation: /fɪks/ (AmE), /fɪks/ (BrE)
an exact determination of the position or location of something, especially in navigation or targeting

Examples

A fix on position
After consulting the stars, the captain got a fix on the ship's location.
It's hard to get a fix on his exact whereabouts at the moment.
The radar technician obtained a fix on the incoming aircraft.
