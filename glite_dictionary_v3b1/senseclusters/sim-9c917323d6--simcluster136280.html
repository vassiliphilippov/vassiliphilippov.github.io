SIM

noun
mobile phone card
Pronunciation: / sɪm / (AmE), / sɪm / (BrE)
a small removable card or chip in a mobile phone that stores user identity and enables connection to a cellular network

Examples

A SIM card
If you lose your phone, you should contact your provider to block your SIM immediately.
She inserted a new SIM to activate her phone on the local network while traveling.
Some phones now support more than one SIM for using multiple numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: SIM card - a card that stores information for mobile phone users
  • degree higher: eSIM - an embedded digital SIM used in some mobile devices
