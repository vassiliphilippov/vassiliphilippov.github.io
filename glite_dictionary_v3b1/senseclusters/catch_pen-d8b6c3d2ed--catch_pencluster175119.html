catch pen

noun
animal enclosure for holding livestock
7.3 5.1
Pronunciation: /kætʃ pen/ (AmE), /kætʃ pen/ (BrE)
a small or medium enclosure used to temporarily hold animals, especially livestock, for handling or sorting

Examples

The catch pen
After sorting, the sheep remained in the catch pen before being returned to the pasture.
The rancher led the cattle into the catch pen for their vaccinations.
Workers cleaned the catch pen thoroughly between each group of animals.
