reflectivity

noun
capacity for deep thought or contemplation
Pronunciation: / rɪˌflɛkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / rɪˌflɛkˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being capable of deep thought or contemplation; thoughtfulness or introspection

Examples

Personal reflectivity
Her reflectivity set her apart from her peers, who tended to act on impulse.
Periods of reflectivity can help people gain a clearer sense of themselves.
Writers often value reflectivity when developing complex characters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: introspection - the examination or observation of one's own mental and emotional processes
  • synonym: thoughtfulness - the state of being considerate or attentive, often involving deep or careful thinking
