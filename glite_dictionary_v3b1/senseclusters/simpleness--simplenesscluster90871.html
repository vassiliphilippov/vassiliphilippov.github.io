simpleness

noun
lack of ornamentation; plainness
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɪmplnəs / (BrE)
the quality of having little or no decoration; the state of being plain or unadorned

Examples

Simpleness of design
Many admire the simpleness of traditional Japanese interiors.
She preferred the simpleness of a white dress to ornate patterns.
The simpleness of the design made the space feel open and inviting.
