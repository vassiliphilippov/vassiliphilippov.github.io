Irish gorse

noun
spiny shrub with yellow flowers
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ɡɔrs / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ɡɔːs / (BrE)
a very spiny and dense evergreen shrub with fragrant golden-yellow flowers, common throughout western Europe

Examples

Irish gorse shrub
Gardeners often plant Irish gorse as a natural barrier due to its dense, spiny branches.
The hills were covered with Irish gorse, creating a brilliant yellow landscape in the spring.
You can smell the fragrance of Irish gorse as you walk through the countryside.
