Cordylidae

noun
family of African spiny lizards
Pronunciation: / kɔrˈdɪlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / kɔːˈdɪlɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of small, spiny, ovoviviparous lizards native to Africa.

Examples

Cordylidae lizards
Many Cordylidae give birth to live young, a trait unusual among reptiles.
Researchers observed a variety of defensive behaviors among several Cordylidae species.
The Cordylidae are known for their distinctive spiny scales and robust bodies.
