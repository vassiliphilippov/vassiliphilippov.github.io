drypoint

noun
printmaking technique
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪ.pɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪ.pɔɪnt/ (BrE)
a printmaking technique where an image is scratched into a plate with a hard-pointed needle
