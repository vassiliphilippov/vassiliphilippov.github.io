bup

soft landing sound
9.9
Pronunciation: / bʌp / (AmE), / bʌp / (BrE)
a playful sound used to represent a soft jump or gentle landing, often in stories or cartoons

Examples

A soft bup
The kitten landed on the pillow with a soft 'bup'.
As the cartoon character hopped across the screen, each jump made a cheerful 'bup'.
She described the toy's gentle bounce with a quick 'bup' sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boing - a sound representing a springy or bouncy movement
  • antonym: thud - a dull sound made by a heavy object hitting something
