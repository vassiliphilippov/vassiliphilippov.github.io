toy

noun
object used for amusement
Pronunciation: / tɔɪ / (AmE), / tɔɪ / (BrE)
an object or device, especially one not considered essential, that is used for amusement or enjoyment

Examples

A high-tech toy
He treats his latest gadget as a high-tech toy, showing it off to everyone.
Some collectors buy rare watches as a status toy rather than for practical use.
The new sports car is just another expensive toy for him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plaything - an object for children to play with
