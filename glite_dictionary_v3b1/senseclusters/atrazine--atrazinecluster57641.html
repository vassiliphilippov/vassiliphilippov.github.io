atrazine

noun
synthetic herbicide for weed control
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈætrəziːn / (AmE), / ˈætrəziːn / (BrE)
a synthetic herbicide derived from triazine, commonly used to control broadleaf and grassy weeds in crops such as corn and sugarcane

Examples

Atrazine herbicide use
Atrazine is one of the most widely used herbicides in the United States.
Some studies link atrazine exposure to environmental and health concerns.
The government introduced new regulations on the use of atrazine in agriculture.
