shake

noun
handshake; act of shaking hands
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
an act of clasping and moving someone's hand up and down, especially as a greeting or to finalize an agreement

Examples

A friendly shake
He offered me a friendly shake at the start of our meeting.
They sealed the deal with a firm shake and a smile.
With a quick shake, they introduced themselves to each other.

  • synonym: handshake - the act of shaking hands with someone
