yarding

noun
gathering livestock
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈjɑːr.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈjɑː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of bringing animals into a yard or holding pen, or the group of animals gathered in this way

Examples

Yarding cattle
The yarding of cattle was completed before the storm arrived.
After a long day, the workers finished the yarding of the sheep into the holding pen.
This year's yarding included more animals than usual due to favorable weather.
