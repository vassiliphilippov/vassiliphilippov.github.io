Synonyms

  • mujahadeen
    9.9 0.01/m
    mujahadeen is a spelling variant for Muslim guerrilla fighters in a religious or military struggle, while mujahideen is the more common modern form.
  • mujahedeen
    9.9 0.00/m
    mujahedeen describes Muslim fighters who oppose non-Muslim or secular rulers, while mujahideen has a broader historic and general use.
  • mujahedin
    9.9 0.24/m
    mujahedin often refers to fighters noted in historic conflicts like the Soviet-Afghan War, while mujahideen names fighters generally in jihad contexts.
