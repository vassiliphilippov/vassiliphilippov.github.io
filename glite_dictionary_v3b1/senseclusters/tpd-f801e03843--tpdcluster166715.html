TPD

sym
EU tobacco law
Pronunciation: / ti pi di / (AmE), / tiː piː diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Tobacco Products Directive, a European Union law regulating the manufacture, packaging, and sale of tobacco products

Examples

TPD rules
Manufacturers must follow the TPD when designing cigarette packaging for the European market.
The new vaping products were reviewed to ensure they met TPD requirements.
Retailers updated their stock to comply with the latest TPD regulations.
