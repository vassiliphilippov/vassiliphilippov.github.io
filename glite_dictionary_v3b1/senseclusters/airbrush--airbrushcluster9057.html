to retouch, edit, or alter a photograph or image to improve its appearance or hide flaws, either digitally or with an airbrush

Examples

To airbrush a photo
They airbrush photos in post-processing to remove blemishes and soften the subject's skin tone.
The magazine editor airbrushed the cover image before sending it to print.
He learned quick techniques for airbrushing portraits during the online photography workshop.
The magazine airbrushed the model's skin to remove all blemishes before publishing the cover.
Some historical photos have been airbrushed to erase people who fell out of favor.
The company's report airbrushed over the financial problems to make the results look better.
The magazine cover was airbrushed to remove any imperfections from the model's skin.
He spent hours airbrushing the old photograph to restore its original colors.
Some celebrities deny that their photos have been airbrushed before publication.

Synonyms

  • retouch
    4.2 0.51/m
    retouch fixes small flaws and makes light improvements to a photo, while airbrush alters images more heavily to hide defects.
  • photoshop
    4.8 0.15/m
    photoshop digitally changes a photo using software to remove or add things, while airbrush can mean editing by tool or digitally.
