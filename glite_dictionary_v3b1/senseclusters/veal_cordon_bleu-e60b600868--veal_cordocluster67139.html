veal cordon bleu

noun
veal dish stuffed with cheese and ham
7.1
Pronunciation: / vil ˌkɔrdɑn ˈblu / (AmE), / viːl ˌkɔːdɒ̃ ˈbluː / (BrE)
A dish consisting of thin slices of veal stuffed with cheese and ham, then coated and sautéed.

Examples

Veal cordon bleu dish
For a special dinner, she prepared homemade veal cordon bleu for her guests.
He ordered the veal cordon bleu at the fancy French restaurant.
The veal cordon bleu was perfectly cooked and oozing with melted cheese.
