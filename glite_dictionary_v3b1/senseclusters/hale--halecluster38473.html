hale

adjective
in good health, strong and healthy (esp. elderly)
Pronunciation: / heɪl / (AmE), / heɪl / (BrE)
(especially of an old person) strong and healthy; exhibiting vigorous good health.

Examples

A hale old person
After her long illness, she returned looking hale and cheerful once more.
Despite his age, my grandfather remains hale and active.
The retired teacher was surprisingly hale, walking miles each morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frail - physically weak or delicate, especially due to age
  • synonym: robust - strong and healthy; vigorous
