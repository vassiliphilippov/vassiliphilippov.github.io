surfman

noun
coastal rescue worker
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜrfmən /; / ˈsɝːfmən /; / ˈsɝːfmæn / (AmE), / ˈsɜːfmən / (BrE)
a person, especially in the past, whose job was to rescue people from shipwrecks or danger near the shore, often as part of a lifesaving crew
