rehire

noun
employment again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈhaɪər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈhaɪə / (BrE)
the act or process of employing someone again after they have previously left or been dismissed from a job

Examples

The rehire of staff
The sudden rehire of the coach surprised fans and sparked debate about club management.
After a year away, her rehire brought new energy to the department.
The company announced the rehire of several former employees after the expansion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dismissal - the act of removing someone from their job
  • synonym: reinstatement - the act of giving someone their job back after dismissal
