stirred up

adjective
emotionally agitated or excited
4.8
Pronunciation: / stɝd ʌp / (AmE), / stɜːd ʌp / (BrE)
Emotionally aroused or agitated; experiencing strong feelings or excitement.

Examples

Feeling stirred up emotions
After the argument, he felt completely stirred up and restless.
She was so stirred up by the news that she could hardly speak.
The movie left many people stirred up and deeply moved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger
  • synonym: excited - feeling or showing happiness and enthusiasm
