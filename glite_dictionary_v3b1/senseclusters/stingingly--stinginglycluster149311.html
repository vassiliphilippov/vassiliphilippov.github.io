stingingly

adverb
in a scathing way
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋɪŋli / (BrE)
in a sharply critical, scathing, or hurtful manner that causes emotional pain or offense

Examples

Stingingly harsh words
The teacher stingingly criticized the student's careless mistakes in front of the whole class.
She replied stingingly to his comment, making everyone in the room uncomfortable.
His review was stingingly negative, leaving the author feeling deeply hurt.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gently - in a kind or mild manner
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or unkind way
  • synonym: scathingly - in a harshly critical or severe manner
