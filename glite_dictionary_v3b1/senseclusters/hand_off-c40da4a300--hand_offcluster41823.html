Synonyms

  • hand over
    2.8 9.21/m
    hand over is used when you must give control or possession to someone by obligation, while hand off is a more casual or planned transfer.
  • pass on
    2.7 6.49/m
    pass on often means giving information or an item to another person, while hand off focuses on transferring control or responsibility.
  • turn over
    3.2 7.88/m
    turn over usually implies giving someone or something to another's care or authority, while hand off is a general transfer of responsibility or an object.
