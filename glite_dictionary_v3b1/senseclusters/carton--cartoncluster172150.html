carton

noun
a cardboard box for packaging
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑrtən / (AmE), / ˈkɑːtən / (BrE)
a box made from cardboard or similar material, typically with flaps that open at the top, used for packaging and storing goods

Examples

A carton box
A milk carton was left open on the kitchen counter.
He packed the books into a large carton for the move.
We recycle every carton after unpacking the groceries.
