flitter

verb
move back and forth rapidly and lightly
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˈflɪtə / (BrE)
to move back and forth or flutter rapidly and lightly

Examples

To flitter between branches
A small bird began to flitter between the branches.
Her fingers would often flitter nervously as she spoke.
Tiny moths flitter around the lamp in the dark.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flicker - to move quickly and irregularly; to shine unsteadily
  • synonym: flutter - to move with quick, light movements
