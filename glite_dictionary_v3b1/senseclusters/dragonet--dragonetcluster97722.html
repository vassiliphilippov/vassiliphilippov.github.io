dragonet

noun
small, brightly colored marine bottom-dwelling fish
Pronunciation: / ˈdræɡənɛt / (AmE), / ˈdræɡənɪt / (BrE)
a small, often brightly colored, scaleless marine fish found on the sea bottom in tropical and warm temperate waters, especially of Europe and America
