unglue

verb
to separate glued items
3.5 0.01/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀà…°luÀê / (AmE), /  ånÀà…°luÀê / (BrE)
to separate, detach, or remove something that was stuck with glue, or to come apart after being glued

Examples

To unglue a photo
She managed to unglue the old photograph from the album page.
It took some effort to unglue the two pieces of wood without damaging them.
After soaking the label in water, he was able to unglue it from the jar.
After years of use, the wallpaper started to unglue from the wall.
The label will unglue if you soak the jar in warm water.
The crafts project fell apart when the decorations began to unglue.
He unglued the label from the jar carefully before returning it to the shelf.
After soaking the envelope in water, she easily unglued the flap.
The children tried to unglue the pieces of the broken model airplane.
