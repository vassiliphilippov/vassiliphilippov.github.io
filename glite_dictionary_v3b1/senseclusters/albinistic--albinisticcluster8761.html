albinistic

adjective
relating to albinism
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌæl.bɪˈnɪs.tɪk / (AmE), / ˌal.bɪˈnɪs.tɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by albinism; having the characteristics of an albino.

Examples

Albinistic features
Her brother was born with albinistic features, including pale skin and light hair.
Researchers studied the albinistic traits in several animal species.
The albinistic rabbit had pure white fur and pink eyes.
