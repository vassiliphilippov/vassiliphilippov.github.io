peephole

noun
small hole for looking through
Pronunciation: / ˈpipˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈpiːpˌhəʊl / (BrE)
a small hole in a door, wall, or other surface that allows a person to look through, especially without being seen

Examples

A peephole in door
Bakers sometimes use a small peephole to check the bread in the oven.
She checked through the peephole before opening the door to the visitor.
The security guard monitored the activity in the room using a peephole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spyhole - a small hole for looking through secretly, especially in a door
