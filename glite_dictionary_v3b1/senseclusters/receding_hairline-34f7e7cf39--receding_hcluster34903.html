receding hairline

noun
hair loss at the front of the scalp
2.6
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsidɪŋ ˈhɛrˌlaɪn / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːdɪŋ ˈheə.laɪn / (BrE)
a gradual loss of hair at the front of the scalp, resulting in the hairline moving backward

Examples

A receding hairline pattern
A receding hairline can be an early sign of male pattern baldness.
He tried different hairstyles to make his receding hairline less noticeable.
Many people seek treatments to slow down their receding hairline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baldness - the condition of having no hair on the scalp
  • antonym: full head of hair - a scalp completely covered with hair
