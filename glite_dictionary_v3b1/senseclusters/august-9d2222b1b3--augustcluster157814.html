August

noun
eighth month of the year
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɡəst / (AmE), / ˈɔːɡəst / (BrE)
The eighth month of the year, following July and preceding September.

Examples

August month
My birthday falls in August every year.
The new school year usually starts at the end of August.
