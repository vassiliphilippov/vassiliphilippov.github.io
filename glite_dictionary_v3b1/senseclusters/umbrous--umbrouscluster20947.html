umbrous

adjective
mysterious or shadowy
Pronunciation: / ˈʌmbrəs / (AmE), / ˈʌmbrəs / (BrE)
mysterious, obscure, or shadowy in meaning or origin; difficult to understand or interpret

Examples

Umbrous poetry
The poet's language was so umbrous that readers struggled to grasp the true meaning.
Legends about the ancient ruins remain umbrous, shrouded in mystery and speculation.
Her explanation was umbrous, leaving the audience more confused than before.
