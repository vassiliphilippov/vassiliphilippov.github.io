sundew

noun
a sticky-leaved carnivorous plant
9.9 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / Ààs ånÀådu / (AmE), / Ààs ån.djuÀê / (BrE)
a small carnivorous plant of the genus Drosera, with leaves covered in sticky hairs that trap and digest insects, typically found in boggy areas.

Examples

A sundew plant
Many people are fascinated by how a sundew digests insects to supplement its nutrition.
The sundew uses its sticky, glistening leaves to catch unsuspecting insects.
We spotted a rare sundew growing beside the peat bog during our hike.

Synonyms

  • Drosera
    9.9 0.01/m
    Drosera is the scientific name for the whole genus of sticky insect-eating plants, while sundew is the common name for each plant.
