Caryophyllidae

noun
subclass of flowering plants (includes carnations, cacti, etc.)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɛriəˌfaɪəˈlɪdi.i /; / ker.i.oʊˌfɪl.ɪˈdiː / (AmE), / ˌkarɪəˌfɪlɪˈdiː /; / kar.ɪ.əʊˌfɪl.ɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a botanical subclass of flowering plants, including families like Caryophyllaceae and Cactaceae, characterized by basal or central placentation and trinucleate pollen
