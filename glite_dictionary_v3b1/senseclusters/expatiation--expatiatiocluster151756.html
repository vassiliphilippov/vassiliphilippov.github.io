expatiation

noun
lengthy, detailed discussion
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɛkˌspeɪʃiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛkˌspeɪʃiˈeɪʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
the act of discussing or writing about something at length and in detail

Examples

Long expatiation on topic
His expatiation on the subject lasted for nearly an hour and covered every possible detail.
She appreciated the speaker's expatiation on the history of the monument.
The essay was criticized for excessive expatiation and lack of focus.
