biocycle

noun
biological process cycle
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
a recurring sequence of biological processes or events, such as the carbon or nitrogen cycle in nature

Examples

The biocycle of nature
The nitrogen biocycle plays a crucial role in supporting plant growth.
Scientists study the biocycle of carbon to understand climate change.
Each biocycle in an ecosystem helps maintain balance among living organisms.
