Highlands

noun
mountainous region of northern Scotland
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪ.ləndz / (AmE), / ˈhaɪ.ləndz / (BrE)
A mountainous region in northern Scotland, known for its rugged beauty, traditional dress such as the kilt and tartan, and its historic clan system.

Examples

The Highlands of Scotland
She spent her summer hiking through the Highlands and learning about Scottish history.
The Scottish Highlands attract tourists from all over the world for their stunning landscapes.
Traditional music and dance are still alive in many villages in the Highlands.
