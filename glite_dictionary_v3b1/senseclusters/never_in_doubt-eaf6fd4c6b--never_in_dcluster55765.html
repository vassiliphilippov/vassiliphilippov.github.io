never in doubt

adjective
confident regardless of correctness
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛvɚ ɪn ˈdaʊt / (AmE), / ˈnɛvə ɪn ˈdaʊt / (BrE)
always completely confident or certain, even when incorrect or lacking good reason for that certainty

Examples

Always never in doubt
Even when she guesses, she is never in doubt about her answers.
He's often wrong, but never in doubt when he speaks about history.
The manager was never in doubt, even when his strategy didn't work out.
