antiretroviral

adjective
against retroviruses
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˌrɛtroʊˈvaɪrəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌrɛtrəʊˈvaɪərəl / (BrE)
describing drugs or treatments that prevent or inhibit the growth of retroviruses such as HIV

Examples

Antiretroviral treatment
Doctors prescribe antiretroviral drugs to help control HIV infection.
Access to antiretroviral therapy has improved the lives of many patients worldwide.
The new antiretroviral treatment showed promising results in clinical trials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiviral - describing drugs that act against viruses in general
