terra firma

noun
solid ground; earth's firm surface
9.6 4.5
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrə ˈfɜrmə /; / ˌtɛrə ˈfɜːrmə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrə ˈfɜːmə / (BrE)
dry, solid ground; land as opposed to the sea or air

  • antonym: sea - the expanse of salt water that covers most of the earth's surface
