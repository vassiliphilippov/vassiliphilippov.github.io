goldbeater

noun
person who makes gold leaf
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbiːtər / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbiːtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to hammer gold into thin sheets called gold leaf

Examples

A goldbeater hammers gold
For centuries, a goldbeater provided materials for artisans and gilders.
My grandfather worked as a goldbeater in the city workshop before the process became industrialized.
The goldbeater skillfully transformed a lump of gold into delicate, shimmering leaf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gold leaf - very thin sheet of gold, produced by goldbeaters
