Goliath

proper noun
giant warrior defeated by David (Bible)
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlaɪəθ / (AmE), / ɡəˈlaɪəθ / (BrE)
(in the Bible) a giant Philistine warrior defeated by the young David with a slingshot; by extension, a person or thing of great size or power.

Examples

Goliath giant warrior
In the biblical story, David defeats Goliath with a single stone.
Many see the small startup as a David facing a corporate Goliath.
The legend of Goliath has inspired countless stories of underdogs overcoming great odds.
