hairgrip

noun
flat wire hairpin for holding hair
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌɡrɪp / (AmE), / ˈheəɡrɪp / (BrE)
A small, flat wire hairpin with prongs that press tightly together, used to hold hair in place.

Examples

A hairgrip holds hair
A gold hairgrip lay forgotten on the bathroom sink.
After losing her favorite hairgrip, Julia had to borrow one from her sister.
She pinned her bangs back with a hairgrip before starting to cook dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobby pin - a small, U-shaped hairpin used for holding hair in place (American English)
