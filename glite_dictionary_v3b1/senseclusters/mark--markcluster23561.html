mark

verb
notice or pay attention to
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to notice or pay attention to something; to perceive or observe carefully

Examples

To mark a warning
He didn't mark the warning signs and got into trouble.
Please mark what I say—it's important for your safety.
She always marks any mistakes in her students' work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to intentionally not notice or pay attention to something
  • synonym: notice - to become aware of something or someone through observation
