construction

noun
group of words forming a grammatical unit
Pronunciation: / kənˈstrʌkʃən / (AmE), / kənˈstrʌkʃən / (BrE)
a group of words arranged together in a particular grammatical pattern, forming a unit within a sentence

Examples

A sentence construction
His sentence contained an unusual grammatical construction that confused the readers.
Some English learners find passive constructions difficult to understand.
The teacher explained that this construction is commonly used in formal writing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clause - a group of words containing a subject and predicate
  • synonym: phrase - a small group of words that forms a unit within a sentence
