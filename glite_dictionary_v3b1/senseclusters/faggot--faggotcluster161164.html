faggot

verb
fasten iron rods to heat/weld
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfæ.gət/ (AmE), /ˈfæ.gət/ (BrE)
(historical, metallurgy) To fasten together rods of iron so they can be heated or welded into a single piece.

Examples

To faggot iron rods
The blacksmith faggoted several iron rods before placing them in the forge.
To prepare for welding, the apprentices faggoted the metal strips tightly together.
Traditional ironworkers often faggot bundles of rods to form strong bars.
