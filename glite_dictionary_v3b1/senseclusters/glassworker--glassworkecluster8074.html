glassworker

noun
glass maker
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsˌwɜrkər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːswɜːkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or shape objects from glass

Examples

Artistic glassworker makes vases
The glassworker demonstrated how to create a delicate glass sculpture for the visitors.
Many glassworkers spend years perfecting their craft in specialized workshops.
A skilled glassworker can turn molten glass into beautiful vases and ornaments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artisan - a skilled craftsperson who makes things by hand
  • synonym: glassblower - a person who shapes glass by blowing air into it
