precontact

noun
time before contact
8.6
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈkɑːn.tækt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈkɑːn.tækt/ (AmE), /priː.ˈkɒn.tækt/ /; / /prɪ.ˈkɒn.tækt/ (BrE)
the period before the first contact between different peoples or cultures, especially in archaeology or anthropology

Examples

The precontact era
Archaeologists study artifacts from the precontact era to learn about indigenous cultures.
The language spoken in the precontact period was very different from what is spoken today.
Many traditions from the precontact times have been preserved by local communities.
