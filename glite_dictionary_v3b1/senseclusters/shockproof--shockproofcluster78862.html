shockproof

adjective
resistant to impact
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑkˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈʃɒkˌpruːf / (BrE)
designed to resist damage or malfunction when subjected to sudden impacts or shocks

Examples

Shockproof phone case
He bought a shockproof case to protect his phone from accidental drops.
The new watch is shockproof, making it ideal for outdoor activities.
Engineers tested the shockproof equipment by dropping it from several heights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fragile - easily broken or damaged
  • synonym: waterproof - designed to prevent water from entering or damaging
