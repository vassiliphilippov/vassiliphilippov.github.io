jet-propelled

adjective
moved by a jet engine
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɛt.pɹəˈpɛld / (AmE), / ˌdʒɛt.prəˈpɛld / (BrE)
Moved or driven forward by a jet engine or in a way resembling the thrust of a jet engine.

Examples

Jet-propelled engine
Her imagination raced ahead like a jet-propelled train, unstoppable and swift.
The jet-propelled aircraft reached supersonic speeds during its test flight.
The company unveiled a new jet-propelled boat designed for rapid water rescues.
