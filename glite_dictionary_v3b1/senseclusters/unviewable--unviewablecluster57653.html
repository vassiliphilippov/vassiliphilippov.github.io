unviewable

adjective
not able to be seen
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvjuːəbl̩ / (AmE), / ʌnˈvjuːəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be seen or watched, usually because of technical problems, damage, or access restrictions

Examples

Unviewable video online
The video was unviewable in some countries due to copyright restrictions.
After the website update, several images became unviewable to users.
Because of a technical error, the document was temporarily unviewable online.
The document was damaged and became unviewable on all devices.
Some websites are unviewable in certain countries due to restrictions.
After the update, the video file was suddenly unviewable in my media player.
