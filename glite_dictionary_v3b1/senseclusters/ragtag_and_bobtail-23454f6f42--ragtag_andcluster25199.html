ragtag and bobtail

noun
disparaging term for an undistinguished group
8.4 8.0
Pronunciation: /ˈræg.tæɡ ənd ˈbɑːb.teɪl/ /; / /ˈræg.tæɡ ænd ˈbɑːb.teɪl/ (AmE), /ˈræg.tæɡ ənd ˈbɒb.teɪl/ /; / /ˈræg.tæɡ ænd ˈbɒb.teɪl/ (BrE)
used to refer disparagingly to a collection of ordinary, disreputable, or undistinguished people or things, especially as a motley group.

Examples

The ragtag and bobtail group
Critics dismissed the protestors as just the usual ragtag and bobtail causing trouble.
The invitation list included everyone from the elite to the ragtag and bobtail of the town.
When the parade reached the square, a ragtag and bobtail of supporters followed.
