kolinsky

noun
fine weasel hair brush
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪnski / (AmE), / kəˈlɪnski / (BrE)
the fine hair from a Siberian weasel, used to make high-quality paintbrushes, or a paintbrush made from this hair

Examples

Kolinsky paintbrush
Artists often prefer a kolinsky for its ability to hold a fine point and retain water.
She invested in a set of kolinsky brushes for her watercolor paintings.
A genuine kolinsky is prized for its softness and durability among professional painters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: synthetic brush - a paintbrush made from artificial fibers
