ungraspable

adjective
cannot be held
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡræspəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrɑːspəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to be physically held, seized, or gripped

Examples

Ungraspable fish
The tiny fish was so quick and ungraspable that it escaped every attempt to catch it.
For the children, the soap in the bath was almost ungraspable as it slipped from their hands.
The magician's trick used an ungraspable object that seemed to vanish whenever someone tried to hold it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: graspable - able to be held or gripped
  • synonym: slippery - difficult to hold or keep a grip on
  • synonym: unattainable - impossible to reach or achieve
