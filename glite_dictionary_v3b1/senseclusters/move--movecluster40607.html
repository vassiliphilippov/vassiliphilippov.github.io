move

verb
associate or operate within a social group or environment
Pronunciation: / muːv / (AmE), / muːv / (BrE)
to live, operate, or associate within a particular social environment or group

Examples

To move in circles
During college, he moved among the intellectual elite of the city.
Many aspiring actors hope to move in celebrity circles someday.
She moves in wealthy circles that most people never experience.

Related Terms
