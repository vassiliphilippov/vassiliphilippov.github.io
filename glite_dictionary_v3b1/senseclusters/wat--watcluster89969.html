wat

noun
Buddhist temple
Pronunciation: / wɑt / (AmE), / wɒt / (BrE)
a Buddhist temple or monastery, especially found in Thailand or Cambodia

Examples

A Thai wat
The golden spires of the wat shone brightly in the morning sun.
During their travels in Cambodia, they visited a famous wat near the river.
Monks gathered at the wat every evening for prayers and meditation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pagoda - a tiered tower with multiple eaves, common in East Asian Buddhist temples
