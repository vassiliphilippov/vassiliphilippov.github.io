charrette

noun
design planning session
Pronunciation: / ʃəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ʃəˈrɛt / (BrE)
an intense, collaborative meeting or workshop to develop ideas or solutions, especially in design or planning

Examples

Design charrette workshop
The architecture students participated in a week-long charrette to develop new ideas for the city park.
During the charrette, team members from different departments collaborated to solve the design challenge.
A successful charrette can generate creative solutions under tight deadlines.
The city planners held a charrette to involve residents in designing the new park.
During the charrette, teams worked late into the night to finalize their proposals.
A charrette can help gather creative ideas from people with different backgrounds.
