inharmony

noun
lack of harmony
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɑːrməni / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɑːməni / (BrE)
a lack of agreement, harmony, or compatibility between people or things

Examples

Inharmony between teams
There was clear inharmony between the two departments after the merger.
The project failed due to inharmony among the team members.
Cultural differences sometimes lead to inharmony in international partnerships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conflict - serious disagreement or argument
  • synonym: discord - disagreement or lack of harmony
  • antonym: harmony - agreement or concord
