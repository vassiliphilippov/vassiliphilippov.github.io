electromagnetically

adverb
using electromagnetic fields
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊmæɡˈnɛtɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses or involves electromagnetism (the interaction of electricity and magnetism), especially for transmitting energy or information

Examples

Powered electromagnetically
The devices are powered electromagnetically without any physical connection.
Information can be transmitted electromagnetically over long distances using radio waves.
The lock opens electromagnetically when the correct code is entered.
The door locks can be opened electromagnetically using a remote control.
Signals are transmitted electromagnetically between the two devices for faster communication.
The train is powered electromagnetically rather than by traditional engines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electrically - in a way that relates to electricity
  • synonym: magnetically - in a way that relates to magnetism
  • synonym: wirelessly - without using wires, typically by radio waves
