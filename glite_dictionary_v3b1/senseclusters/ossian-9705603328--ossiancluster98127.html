Ossian

proper noun
legendary Gaelic bard
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑsiən / (AmE), / ˈɒsiən / (BrE)
a legendary Gaelic bard whose epic poems were popularized in the 18th century, especially through works attributed to him by James Macpherson

Examples

Legendary Ossian bard
The poems of Ossian inspired many artists and writers during the Romantic period.
Scholars have debated whether Ossian was a real historical figure or a literary creation.
James Macpherson claimed to have translated ancient Gaelic works by Ossian.
