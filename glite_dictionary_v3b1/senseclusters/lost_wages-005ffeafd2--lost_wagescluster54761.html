lost wages

noun
income not earned due to absence from work
Pronunciation: / lɔst ˈweɪdʒɪz / (AmE), / lɒst ˈweɪdʒɪz / (BrE)
money that a person does not earn because they are unable to work, typically due to injury, illness, or other incidents

Examples

Lost wages claim
Employees may be entitled to claim lost wages if they are unable to work due to a workplace injury.
She filed a lawsuit to recover her lost wages after the accident kept her from working for two months.
The insurance company reimbursed him for his lost wages while he was on medical leave.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compensation - payment given to make up for loss or injury
  • synonym: damages - money claimed in compensation for a loss or injury
