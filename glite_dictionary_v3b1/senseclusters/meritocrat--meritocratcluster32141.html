meritocrat

noun
person with earned status
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈme.rɪ.tə.kræt/ /; / /ˈme.rə.tə.kræt/ (AmE), /ˈme.rɪ.tə.kræt/ /; / /ˈme.rə.tə.kræt/ (BrE)
a person who achieves power or status because of their ability and talent rather than social class or wealth

Examples

Successful meritocrat
As a meritocrat, she believed only the most capable should lead the organization.
The new manager was a true meritocrat who promoted employees based on performance.
In a meritocrat society, people advance because of their skills and achievements.
