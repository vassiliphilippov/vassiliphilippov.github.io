detainment

noun
the act of being kept in custody
7.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈteɪnmənt / (AmE), / dɪˈteɪnmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of being kept in custody or confined, usually for a short period

Examples

Police detainment
Due to a case of mistaken identity, her detainment was resolved quickly, and she was released by evening.
Reporters questioned whether the detainment of the activist was justified under international law.
The suspect's detainment lasted only a few hours while police checked his alibi.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detention - the act of detaining or the state of being detained, especially as a form of punishment or for questioning
