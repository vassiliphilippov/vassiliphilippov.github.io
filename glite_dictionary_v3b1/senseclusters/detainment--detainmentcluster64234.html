Synonyms

  • detention
    3.9 10/m
    detention is keeping someone confined as punishment or while waiting a decision, while detainment usually means short-term custody.
  • custody
    3.9 9.03/m

  • confinement
    5.9 3.97/m
    confinement is being kept in a small or limited space, often long or like imprisonment, while detainment is usually short-term.
