gastroparesis

noun
delayed stomach emptying
8.9
Pronunciation: /gæs.trə.pə.ˈriː.sɪs/ /; / /gæs.troʊ.pə.ˈriː.sɪs/ (AmE), /gæs.trə.pə.ˈriː.sɪs/ /; / /gæs.trəʊ.pə.ˈriː.sɪs/ (BrE)
a medical condition in which the stomach muscles do not function properly, causing delayed emptying of food

Examples

Gastroparesis symptoms
After being diagnosed with gastroparesis, she had to change her diet to manage her symptoms.
Doctors explained that gastroparesis can cause bloating and discomfort after meals.
People with gastroparesis may feel full after eating only a small amount.
