nonmetrically

adverb
without regular rhythm
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / nɒnˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that does not follow a regular meter or beat, especially in music or poetry

Examples

Sung nonmetrically
The choir sang the introduction nonmetrically, creating a sense of freedom and unpredictability.
Her piano solo moved nonmetrically, ignoring the usual rhythmic structure of the piece.
The lines of the poem flowed nonmetrically, imitating the patterns of natural speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freely - in a way that is not restricted or controlled
  • antonym: metrically - in a way that follows a regular meter or beat
