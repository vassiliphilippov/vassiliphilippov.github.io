nonmetrically

adverb
without regular rhythm
9.1
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈmet.rɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈmet.rɪ.kliː/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈmet.rɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /nɒn.ˈmet.rɪ.kliː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not follow a regular meter or beat, especially in music or poetry

Examples
