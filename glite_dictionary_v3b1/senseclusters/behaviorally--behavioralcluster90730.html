behaviorally

adverb
in terms of behavior
Pronunciation: / bɪˈheɪvjɚəli / (AmE), / bɪˈheɪvjərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to actions, conduct, or behavior

Examples

Improved behaviorally
The students improved behaviorally after the new discipline policy was introduced.
Therapists often measure progress both emotionally and behaviorally in their clients.
Animals can adapt behaviorally to changes in their environment to survive.

Related Terms

  • antonym: psychologically - in a way that relates to the mind or mental processes
