garter stitch

noun
a basic knitting stitch with horizontal ridges
9.7 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrtər stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːtə stɪtʃ / (BrE)
a knitting stitch that produces a pattern of horizontal ridges by knitting every row instead of alternating with purl stitches

Examples

Garter stitch pattern
Beginners often start with garter stitch because it is simple and easy to learn.
By using only garter stitch, the scarf has a wonderfully squishy texture.
She alternated between stockinette and garter stitch for a striped effect.
