Tajiki

noun
Iranian language of the Tajiks
8.9
Pronunciation: / tɑːˈdʒiːki / (AmE), / tɑːˈdʒiːki / (BrE)
the Iranian language spoken primarily by the Tajik people in Tajikistan and parts of Uzbekistan, closely related to Farsi

Examples

Tajiki language
Many classical Persian poems are easily understood by speakers of Tajiki.
She is fluent in Russian, Uzbek, and Tajiki.
The majority of people in Tajikistan speak Tajiki as their native language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Farsi - the modern Persian language spoken in Iran
