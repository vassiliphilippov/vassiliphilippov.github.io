public servant

noun
government employee or official
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɝːvənt / (AmE), / ˈpʌblɪk ˈsɜːvənt / (BrE)
a person who works for the government, either through election or appointment, especially in administrative or civil service roles

Examples

Public servant role
As a public servant, she felt a strong responsibility to serve her community with integrity.
Many public servants work behind the scenes to keep essential services running smoothly.
The mayor is not only a politician but also a dedicated public servant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: civil servant - a member of the civil service, especially in a non-political governmental role
