Lhasa

noun
Tibetan terrier breed
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑː.sə / (AmE), / ˈlɑː.sə / (BrE)
a breed of small terrier with a long, heavy coat, originally raised in Tibet as a watchdog

Examples

Lhasa dog
Her Lhasa always barks when someone approaches the door.
Many families choose a Lhasa because it makes an excellent apartment dog.
The Lhasa is known for its long, flowing coat and keen sense of hearing.
