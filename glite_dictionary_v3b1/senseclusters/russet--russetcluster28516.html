russet

noun
brown-skinned potato or apple
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌsɪt / (AmE), / ˈrʌsɪt / (BrE)
a type of potato with rough brown skin, or a variety of apple with a brownish skin

Examples

Russet potato
She bought a bag of russet potatoes for baking.
The farmer grew several varieties of russet apples in his orchard.
For the recipe, choose a firm russet to get the best texture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apple - a round fruit with red, green, or yellow skin
  • synonym: potato - an edible tuber, commonly used as a vegetable
