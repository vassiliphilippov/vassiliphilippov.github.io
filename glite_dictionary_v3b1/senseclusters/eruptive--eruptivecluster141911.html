eruptive

adjective
actively spewing out lava or ash
4.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (AmE), / ɪˈrʌptɪv / (BrE)
actively spewing out or capable of spewing out lava, ash, or other material (especially of a volcano or geological feature)

Examples

Eruptive volcano
An eruptive volcano can pose significant risks to nearby communities.
Mount Etna is currently eruptive, sending columns of ash into the sky.
The researchers studied patterns in eruptive geysers across the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: active - currently active, not dormant or extinct (as of a volcano)
  • antonym: dormant - temporarily inactive (as of a volcano)
