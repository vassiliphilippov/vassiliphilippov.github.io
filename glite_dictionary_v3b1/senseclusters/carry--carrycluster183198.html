carry

noun
the act of carrying something
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkæri / (AmE), / ˈkæri / (BrE)
an act or period of carrying something from one place to another

Examples

A carry trip
After the flood, the volunteers made several carry trips with supplies to the shelter.
The hunters had a long carry through the woods before reaching the river.
The player made a spectacular carry down the field, gaining twenty yards.
