unhedged

adjective
without reservation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛdʒd / (BrE)
expressed directly and without any qualification, reservation, or attempt to soften the statement

Examples

Unhedged statement
She gave an unhedged response, making her opinion completely clear to everyone in the room.
His unhedged criticism surprised the team, as he usually chose his words carefully.
The article offered unhedged support for the new policy, without any reservations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: direct - expressed in a straightforward and honest way
  • antonym: hedged - expressed with qualifications or reservations
  • synonym: unqualified - not limited or restricted in any way
