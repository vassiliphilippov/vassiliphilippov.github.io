circulate

verb
make information widely known
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjəˌleɪt /; / ˈsɝː.kjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt /; / ˈsɜː.kjə.leɪt / (BrE)
to spread or pass information, news, or documents among people or places

Examples

False stories can circulate rapidly on social media.
Rumors began to circulate after the announcement was made.

The news of their engagement circulated quickly through the small town.
