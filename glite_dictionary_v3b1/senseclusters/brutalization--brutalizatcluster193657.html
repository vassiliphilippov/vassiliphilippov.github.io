brutalization

noun
act of treating with extreme cruelty
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbruːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbrudl̩ɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌbruːtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌbruːtəlʌɪˈzeɪʃ(ə)n /; / ˌbruːtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of treating someone with extreme cruelty or violence, or making someone cruel or insensitive through exposure to violence
