purine

noun
nitrogenous base in biological molecules
Pronunciation: / ˈpjʊrˌin /; / ˈpjʊˌrin / (AmE), / ˈpjʊəriːn / (BrE)
a colorless crystalline compound that forms the basic structure of important biological molecules, such as the DNA and RNA bases adenine and guanine
