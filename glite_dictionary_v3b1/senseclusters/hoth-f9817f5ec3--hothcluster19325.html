Hoth

proper noun
blind Norse god who killed Balder
Pronunciation: / hoʊθ / (AmE), / hɒθ / (BrE)
In Norse mythology, a blind god who, being misled by Loki, killed his brother Balder by throwing a shaft of mistletoe.

Examples

Hoth in mythology
In the myth, Hoth was manipulated by Loki into causing his brother's death.
Many stories in Norse mythology revolve around the tragic actions of Hoth.
Scholars debate the symbolic meaning of Hoth's blindness in ancient legends.
