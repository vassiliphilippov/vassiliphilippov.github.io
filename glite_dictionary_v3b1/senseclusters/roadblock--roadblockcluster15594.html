roadblock

noun
an obstacle to progress or achievement
3.9 2.25/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆroÊŠdËŒblÉ‘k / (AmE), / ËˆrÉ™ÊŠdËŒblÉ’k / (BrE)
an obstacle or condition that prevents progress or makes achieving an objective difficult

Examples

A roadblock to success
Cultural misunderstandings can often act as a roadblock to international cooperation.
Financial difficulties became a serious roadblock in launching the project on time.
He refused to let failure become a roadblock to his future success.
