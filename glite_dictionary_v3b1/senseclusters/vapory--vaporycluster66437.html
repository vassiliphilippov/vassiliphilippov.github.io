vapory

adjective
misty or steamy
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpəri / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəri / (BrE)
full of, emitting, or resembling vapor, mist, or fog; misty or hazy in appearance

Examples

Vapory morning air
The early morning air was vapory, making the distant hills look mysterious.
A vapory haze drifted across the lake after the rain stopped.
The vapory breath of the horses was visible in the cold winter air.
The vapory air made it difficult to see the distant mountains.
A vapory mist hung over the lake at dawn, softening the landscape.
She painted the scene with vapory colors to capture the morning haze.
