seitan

noun
wheat gluten meat substitute
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪtɑn / (AmE), / ˈseɪtæn / (BrE)
a food made from wheat gluten, commonly used as a high-protein meat substitute in vegetarian and vegan diets

Examples

A seitan dish
Many vegetarians use seitan as a protein source in their meals.
The chef prepared a delicious stir-fry using marinated seitan instead of chicken.
You can grill, fry, or bake seitan to create a variety of meatless dishes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: meat - the flesh of animals used as food
  • synonym: tempeh - a firm, fermented soy product used as a meat alternative
  • synonym: tofu - a soft food made from soybeans, used as a meat substitute
