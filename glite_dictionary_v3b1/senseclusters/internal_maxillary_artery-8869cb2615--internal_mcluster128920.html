internal maxillary artery

noun
artery supplying deep facial structures

Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtɜːr.nəl ˈmæk.sɪ.lə.riː ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtɜː.nəl ˈmæk.sɪ.lə.riː ˈɑː.tə.riː/ (BrE)
