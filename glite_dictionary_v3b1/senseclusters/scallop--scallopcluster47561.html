scallop

noun
thin fried or broiled slice of meat
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskæləp / (AmE), / ˈskæləp / (BrE)
a thin slice of meat, especially veal, usually fried or broiled

Examples

Veal scallop
He ordered the veal scallop with a lemon butter sauce.
She learned how to fry a scallop to a perfect golden brown.
The chef prepared several chicken scallops for the guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: escalope - a thin slice of meat without bone, typically in French cuisine
