chanted

adjective
sung or spoken rhythmically in monotone
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃæn.tɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːn.tɪd / (BrE)
spoken or sung rhythmically, often in a monotone, as in chanting lyrics or prayers.

Examples

The poem was chanted by the children during the ceremony.
Their chanted slogans filled the street with rhythmic energy.
