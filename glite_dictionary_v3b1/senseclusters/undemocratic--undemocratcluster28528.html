undemocratic

adjective
not in agreement with democratic principles
1.8 4.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.de.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ /; / /ʌn.dɪ.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ (AmE), /ʌn.de.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ /; / /ʌn.dɪ.mə.ˈkræt.ɪk/ (BrE)
