undemocratic

adjective
not in agreement with democratic principles
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˌdɛməˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.dɪˌmɒˈkræt.ɪk / (BrE)
not in agreement with or according to democratic principles, practice, or ideals; lacking characteristics of democracy.

Examples

Undemocratic decision
Many considered the government's decision to be undemocratic and protested in the streets.
Such undemocratic practices are often criticized by advocates of political reform.
The undemocratic system limited people's choices in the election.

Related Terms

  • antonym: democratic - following the principles of democracy
