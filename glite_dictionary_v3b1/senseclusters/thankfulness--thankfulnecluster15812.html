thankfulness

noun
state of being grateful
Pronunciation: / ˈθæŋkf(ə)lnəs / (AmE), / ˈθæŋkf(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being grateful; a feeling or expression of gratitude.

Examples

Express thankfulness
He expressed his thankfulness with a heartfelt letter to his coworkers.
Moments of quiet thankfulness helped him appreciate the small joys in life.
She felt a deep sense of thankfulness for the kindness shown to her family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gratitude - the quality of being thankful; readiness to show appreciation
  • antonym: ingratitude - lack of gratitude; ungratefulness
