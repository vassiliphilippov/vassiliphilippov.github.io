caterwaul

noun
loud, wailing cry of a cat
Pronunciation: / ˈkætərˌwɔl / (AmE), / ˈkatəˌwɔːl / (BrE)
a shrill, wailing noise, especially the loud, yowling cry of a cat in heat

Examples

A cat's caterwaul
At midnight, the caterwaul of stray cats echoed through the neighborhood.
The loud caterwaul startled everyone who was sitting nearby.
We couldn't sleep because of the persistent caterwaul coming from the alley behind our house.
