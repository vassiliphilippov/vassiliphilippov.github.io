Slavic language

noun
branch of the Indo-European languages
Pronunciation: / ˈslævɪk ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈslævɪk ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A group of closely related languages that form a branch of the Indo-European language family, including Russian, Polish, Czech, and others.
