Bundt cake

noun
ring-shaped cake
3.2
Pronunciation: /bʌnt keɪk/ (AmE), /bʌnt keɪk/ (BrE)
a cake baked in a round pan with a hole in the center and fluted sides, often served at gatherings

Examples

A chocolate Bundt cake
She baked a chocolate Bundt cake for her friend's birthday celebration.
The distinctive shape of a Bundt cake makes it a centerpiece at many family gatherings.
After dinner, everyone enjoyed a slice of homemade Bundt cake with coffee.
