unmannered

adjective
natural; unaffected by artificiality
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænəd / (BrE)
not affected by artificiality or pretense; natural and straightforward in behavior or appearance.

Examples

Unmannered style
Despite his status, his speech was warm and unmannered.
She admired his unmannered way of interacting with both patients and staff.
The actor's unmannered performance brought a sense of realism to the film.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affected - artificial or pretentious in manner or behavior
  • synonym: natural - innate, genuine; not artificial in manner
