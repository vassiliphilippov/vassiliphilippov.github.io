anglaise

noun
custard dessert sauce
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɑŋˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ɑːŋˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
a light, sweet pouring custard sauce made from milk, eggs, and sugar, often served with desserts

Examples

Vanilla anglaise sauce
The chef poured a delicate anglaise over the fresh berries for dessert.
A classic apple tart is often served with warm anglaise on the side.
He learned to make anglaise in his French cooking class last semester.

Related Terms
