frontal

noun
forehead adornment
Pronunciation: / ˈfrʌn.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈfrʌn.təl / (BrE)
an ornamental band or decoration worn on the forehead, often as part of ceremonial attire

Examples

Ceremonial frontal
A silver frontal adorned the dancer's forehead, catching the light as she moved.
Ancient priests often included a golden frontal as part of their sacred vestments.
The bride wore a jeweled frontal that sparkled beneath her veil.
