heat rash

noun
skin irritation from blocked sweat ducts
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /hiːt ræʃ/ (AmE), /hiːt ræʃ/ (BrE)
A skin irritation caused by blocked sweat ducts, typically appearing as small red bumps during hot and humid conditions.

Examples

A heat rash on skin
After exercising in the hot sun, she developed a heat rash on her neck and back.
Babies are especially prone to heat rash in warm, humid weather.
Wearing tight clothing can sometimes lead to heat rash in the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prickly heat - a synonym for heat rash, especially when describing the rash as itchy
