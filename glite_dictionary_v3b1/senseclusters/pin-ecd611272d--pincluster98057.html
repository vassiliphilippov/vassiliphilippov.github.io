PIN

noun
a secret personal identification number
Pronunciation: / pɪn / (AmE), / pɪn / (BrE)
a secret number, usually four or more digits, that you choose and use to gain access to bank accounts, credit cards, or other secure systems

Examples

Enter PIN code
For security reasons, never share your PIN with anyone.
If you forget your PIN, you may have to reset your card.
You need to enter your PIN to withdraw money from the ATM.

Related Terms

  • synonym: password - a string of characters used to gain access to a system
