quai

noun
riverside walkway
9.4
Pronunciation: / keɪ / (AmE), / keɪ / (BrE)
a platform or walkway along the edge of a river or harbor, especially used in France

Examples

The quai by river
Tourists enjoyed a peaceful walk along the quai beside the river.
Artists often gather on the quai to paint scenes of the bustling harbor.
Cafes line the quai, offering beautiful views of the boats passing by.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pier - a platform extending from the shore into the water
  • synonym: wharf - a structure built on the shore for ships to load and unload
