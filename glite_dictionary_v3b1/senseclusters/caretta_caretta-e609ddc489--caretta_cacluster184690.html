Caretta caretta

noun
species of large sea turtle (loggerhead)
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɛtə kəˈrɛtə / (AmE), / kəˈrɛtə kəˈrɛtə / (BrE)
a large species of carnivorous sea turtle, known as the loggerhead, inhabiting warm and temperate oceans worldwide
