drabble

noun
100-word story
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdræbəl / (AmE), / ˈdræbəl / (BrE)
a very short story, typically exactly 100 words long

Examples

A 100-word drabble
The writing group challenged each member to create a drabble on the theme of hope.
Her drabble captured a powerful moment in just 100 words.
He enjoys reading a drabble every morning with his coffee.
She wrote a drabble every day to improve her writing skills.
The magazine published a collection of drabbles from new authors.
It can be challenging to tell a complete story in a single drabble.

Related Terms
