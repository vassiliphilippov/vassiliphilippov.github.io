peep of day

noun
first light of dawn
8.0
Pronunciation: / pip əv deɪ / (AmE), / piːp əv deɪ / (BrE)
the very early morning or the first light of dawn

Examples

At the peep of day
At the peep of day, fishermen set out to sea to catch the best fish.
She finished reading her novel before the peep of day broke over the city.
The birds began to sing at the peep of day, ushering in the morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawn - the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise
  • synonym: daybreak - the time in the morning when daylight first appears
