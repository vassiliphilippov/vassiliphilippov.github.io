insupportable

adjective
not able to be justified or explained
Pronunciation: / ɪn.səˈpɔɹ.tə.bəl / (AmE), / ɪn.səˈpɔː.tə.bəl / (BrE)
(of a statement, argument, or situation) incapable of being justified or explained; not able to be defended or accepted.

Examples

Insupportable claim
It is insupportable to suggest that the data was manipulated with no proof.
The lawyer found the charges against his client insupportable after reviewing the facts.
Your claim that everyone agrees with you is insupportable without any evidence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defensible - able to be defended or justified
  • synonym: untenable - impossible to defend or maintain, especially in argument
