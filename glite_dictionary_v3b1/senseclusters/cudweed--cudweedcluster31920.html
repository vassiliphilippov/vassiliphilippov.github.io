cudweed

noun
a plant of the genus Filago with woolly flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌdˌwid / (AmE), / ˈkʌdwiːd /; / ˈkʌdˌwiːd /; / ˈkʌd.wiːd / (BrE)
a plant with woolly or cottony flowers or leaves, often found in dry, sandy places (such as those of the genera Filago or Gnaphalium)
