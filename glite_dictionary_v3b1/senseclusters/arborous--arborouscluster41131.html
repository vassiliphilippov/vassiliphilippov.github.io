arborous

adjective
relating to or formed by trees
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbərəs / (AmE), / ˈɑːbərəs / (BrE)
relating to or resembling trees; formed by or covered with trees.

Examples

Arborous landscape
An arborous landscape surrounded the estate, making it a haven for wildlife.
The park was known for its arborous avenues that provided cooling shade in summer.
We enjoyed a picnic beneath the arborous canopy formed by ancient oaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arborescent - resembling a tree in growth or appearance
  • antonym: treeless - lacking trees; without trees
