masklike

adjective
expressionless appearance
Pronunciation: / ˈmæskˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɑːskˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a fixed, expressionless, or emotionless appearance, resembling a mask

Examples

Masklike face
His face remained masklike even when he received the surprising news.
The actor adopted a masklike expression to portray the emotionless character.
She kept her features masklike so no one could guess her true feelings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressionless - not showing any emotion or feeling in the face
  • synonym: impassive - showing no emotion or feeling
