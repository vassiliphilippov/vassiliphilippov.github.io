indefatigableness

noun
tireless determination
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəblnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being tireless and persistent; persistent determination that does not weaken or give up

Examples

The indefatigableness of effort
He attacked every new challenge with remarkable indefatigableness and never seemed to lose energy.
Her indefatigableness inspired everyone on the team to keep working even when they felt exhausted.
The indefatigableness of the activists eventually brought about significant social change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fatigue - extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
  • synonym: indefatigability - the quality or state of being indefatigable
  • antonym: weariness - the state of being tired or exhausted
