hansom cab

noun
two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage
Pronunciation: / ˈhæn.səm kæb / (AmE), / ˈhan.səm kab / (BrE)
a two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage with a covered passenger compartment and a driver's seat above and behind the passengers

Examples

A hansom cab carriage
Seeing a hansom cab in the city museum brought Victorian history to life for the children.
The detective hailed a hansom cab to quickly reach the scene of the crime.
Tourists enjoyed a hansom cab ride through the cobbled streets of old London.
