kaf

noun
inside of the hand
Pronunciation: / kæf / (AmE), / kæf / (BrE)
the inside surface of the human hand between the wrist and the fingers; the palm

Examples

Kaf of the hand
He placed the coin gently in the kaf of her hand.
The healer examined the lines on the kaf to predict the future.
Raindrops collected in the kaf as she reached out to the sky.

Related Terms

  • antonym: back of the hand - the outer surface of the hand opposite the palm
  • synonym: palm - the inner surface of the hand between the wrist and fingers
