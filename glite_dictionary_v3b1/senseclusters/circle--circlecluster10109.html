circle

noun
a round plane figure, all points equidistant from the center
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.kəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.kəl / (BrE)
a round shape or figure where every point on the edge is equally distant from the center; anything with this shape

Examples

A perfect circle
A ring is shaped like a circle.
Draw a circle with a compass to get a perfect shape.
The children sat in a circle and played a game together.
She drew a perfect circle on the paper with a compass.
The cabins are built in a circle around the central fire pit.
The children stood in a circle and held hands during the game.
