wolfish

adjective
greedy or ravenous, like a wolf
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊlfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈwʊlfɪʃ / (BrE)
having a strong or greedy appetite, especially for food; ravenous or voracious.

Examples

Wolfish appetite
After not eating all day, he had a wolfish appetite at dinner.
He looked at the buffet table with a wolfish expression, eager to fill his plate.
The children gave their mother a wolfish grin before diving into the cookies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ravenous - extremely hungry; starving; greedy for food
  • synonym: voracious - wanting or devouring great quantities of food
