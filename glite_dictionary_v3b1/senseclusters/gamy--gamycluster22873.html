gamy

adjective
smelling unpleasantly spoiled (meat)
8.6 7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈgeɪ.miː/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.mɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgeɪ.miː/ /; / /ˈgeɪ.mɪ/ (BrE)
