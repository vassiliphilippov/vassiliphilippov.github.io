gamy

adjective
smelling unpleasantly spoiled (meat)
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪmi / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪmi / (BrE)
(of meat) having a strong, unpleasant smell indicating that it is spoiled or tainted.

Examples

Gamy meat smell
A gamy odor wafted from the kitchen, warning us something was off.
He wrinkled his nose at the gamy smell of the stew.
The venison was left out too long and had become quite gamy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - not stale or spoiled (opposite of gamy for meat)
