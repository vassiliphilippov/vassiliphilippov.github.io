fluidly

adverb
smoothly and easily
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈfluːɪdli / (AmE), / ˈfluːɪdli / (BrE)
in a smooth, easy, and uninterrupted way, often showing flexibility or grace

Examples

To move fluidly
The pianist's fingers moved fluidly over the keys, creating a beautiful melody.
He spoke fluidly in three different languages during the conference.
The conversation flowed fluidly from one topic to another without any pauses.
The dancers moved fluidly across the stage, impressing the audience with their grace.
She adapted fluidly to the new software, learning each feature quickly.
The conversation shifted fluidly from one topic to another without any awkward pauses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a way that is clumsy or not smooth
  • antonym: rigidly - in a strict or inflexible way
  • synonym: smoothly - in a way that is even and without difficulty
