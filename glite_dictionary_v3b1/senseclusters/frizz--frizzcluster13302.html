frizz

verb
to form tight curls, especially in hair
1.4
Pronunciation: / frɪz / (AmE), / frɪz / (BrE)
to form (hair or fiber) into small, tight curls or to become tightly curled

Examples

To frizz hair
Humidity can frizz even the straightest hair.
She likes to frizz her hair before going out for parties.
The stylist will frizz the ends to give your hairstyle extra volume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crimp - to make small regular folds or waves in something, especially hair
