arise

verb
to get up from sitting or lying
1.1 2.6
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈraɪz/ (AmE), /ə.ˈraɪz/ (BrE)
to get up from bed or from a sitting or lying position

Examples

To arise from bed

Many farmers must arise before the sun to tend to their animals.
