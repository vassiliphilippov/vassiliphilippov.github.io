arise

verb
to get up from sitting or lying
Pronunciation: / əˈraɪz / (AmE), / əˈraɪz / (BrE)
to get up from bed or from a sitting or lying position

Examples

To arise from bed
After the meeting, the participants arose and left the room quietly.
She arose from her chair to greet her guests.
When the judge entered the courtroom, everyone arose as a sign of respect.
After a long illness, she was finally able to arise from her bed.
He likes to arise at dawn to enjoy the quiet mornings.
Many farmers must arise before the sun to tend to their animals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: get up - to get out of bed after sleeping
  • synonym: rise - to get up from lying, sitting, or kneeling position
  • antonym: sit down - to move from standing to a sitting position
  • synonym: stand up - to rise to a standing position
