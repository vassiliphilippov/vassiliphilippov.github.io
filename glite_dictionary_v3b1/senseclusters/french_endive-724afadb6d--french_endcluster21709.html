French endive

noun
type of pale, narrow endive
Pronunciation: / frɛntʃ ˈɛn.daɪv / (AmE), / frɛnʃ ˈɛnd.aɪv / (BrE)
A variety of endive plant grown in darkness to produce a narrow, pale head, often used in salads or as a vegetable dish.

Examples

Fresh French endive
French endive has a mild, slightly bitter taste that pairs well with walnuts and blue cheese.
I bought a head of French endive for tonight's salad recipe.
The chef garnished the plate with crisp French endive and a drizzle of vinaigrette.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicory - another name for certain endive varieties; a related salad plant
  • synonym: endive - a leafy salad vegetable of the species Cichorium endivia
