perspirable

adjective
allows sweating
Pronunciation: / pərˈspaɪrəbəl / (AmE), / pəˈspaɪərəbl / (BrE)
allowing perspiration to pass through or capable of sweating

Examples

Perspirable material
This new sports shirt is made from a perspirable material that keeps you cool during exercise.
The tent's perspirable fabric helps reduce condensation inside overnight.
Doctors recommend wearing perspirable clothing in hot weather to prevent overheating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathable - allowing air and moisture to pass through
  • antonym: impermeable - not allowing liquid or gas to pass through
