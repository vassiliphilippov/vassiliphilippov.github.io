yellow chamomile

noun
a yellow-flowered Eurasian perennial herb
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊ kəˈmoʊmiːl / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊ kəˈmæmɪl / (BrE)
A Eurasian perennial herb with hairy divided leaves and yellow flowers, naturalized in North America.

Examples

Yellow chamomile plant
Researchers are studying the medicinal properties of yellow chamomile.
She brewed a calming tea from dried yellow chamomile flowers.
The garden was filled with yellow chamomile blooming in the summer sun.
