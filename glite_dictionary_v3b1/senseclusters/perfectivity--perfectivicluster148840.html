perfectivity

noun
state of perfection
9.9
Pronunciation: / pərˌfɛkˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / pəˌfɛkˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being perfect or complete, especially in a formal or philosophical sense

Examples

Philosophical perfectivity
The philosopher discussed the concept of perfectivity in relation to human nature.
Some believe that technological progress brings us closer to perfectivity in society.
The artist's work strives for perfectivity, aiming to eliminate every possible flaw.
