transom window

noun
window above a door
8.1 7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtræn.səm ˈwɪn.doʊ / (AmE), / ˈtræn.səm ˈwɪn.dəʊ / (BrE)
A window set above a door, typically hinged to a horizontal crosspiece over the door.

Examples

A transom window above door
Light from the hallway filtered through the transom window into the room.
The old house featured a decorative stained-glass transom window above the main entrance.
When the weather was warm, they opened the transom window to let in a breeze.
