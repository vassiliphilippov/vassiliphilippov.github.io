cube

noun
tropical plant (genus Lonchocarpus) used as fish poison
Pronunciation: / kjuːb / (AmE), / kjuːb / (BrE)
any of several tropical American woody plants of the genus Lonchocarpus, whose roots are used as fish poison and as a source of rotenone

Examples

Cube plant roots
Scientists extract rotenone from the roots of certain cube species for use as a pesticide.
Some species of cube are valuable for their natural insecticidal properties.
The villagers crushed the roots of the cube plant to stun fish in the river.
