girandola

noun
ornate candle holder, often with a mirror
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈrændələ / (AmE), / dʒɪˈrandələ / (BrE)
an ornate candle holder, often with branches and sometimes backed by a mirror, used as a decorative lighting piece

Examples

A silver girandola
She placed a silver girandola in the foyer to impress her guests.
The antique girandola on the mantelpiece caught the candlelight beautifully.
The room was illuminated by an ornate girandola adorned with crystal pendants.
