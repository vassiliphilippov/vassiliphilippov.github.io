flying lizard

noun
gliding tropical Asian lizard
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈflaɪɪŋ ˈlɪzəd / (BrE)
A small tropical Asian lizard capable of gliding by spreading winglike membranes along its body.

Examples

A flying lizard gliding
In some Asian forests, spotting a flying lizard gliding between branches is a rare and impressive sight.
Scientists have studied the flying lizard to better understand how gliding evolved in reptiles.
The flying lizard soared from tree to tree, using its winglike flaps to glide through the rainforest canopy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draco - a genus of flying lizards, also known as Draco lizards
