reform

noun
organized campaign for improvement
1.6 3.2
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfɔrm / (AmE), / rɪˈfɔːm / (BrE)
an attempt or process to improve a system, law, or institution by correcting faults or abuses

Examples

A reform movement
Many activists joined the reform movement to fight against corruption in public offices.
Sweeping reforms were introduced after years of public discontent with the healthcare sector.
The government promised a series of reforms to address the failing education system.
Educational reform can be a long and challenging process.
Many citizens demanded reform after years of political corruption were uncovered.
The government announced a major reform of the healthcare system.

  • synonym: improvement - the act of making something better
