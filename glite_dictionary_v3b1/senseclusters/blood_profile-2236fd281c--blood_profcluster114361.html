blood profile

noun
medical test analysing blood cells and components
5.2
Pronunciation: / blʌd ˈproʊ.faɪl / (AmE), / blʌd ˈprəʊ.faɪl / (BrE)
a medical test that measures and analyses the numbers of different blood cells and other components in a sample of blood

Examples

Routine blood profile
A routine blood profile can help detect early signs of disease.
He was anxious while waiting for his blood profile results.
The doctor ordered a blood profile to check for signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blood count - the determination of the number of blood cells in a given volume of blood
