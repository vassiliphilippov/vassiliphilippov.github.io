water-washed

adjective
washed by water or waves
1.2 1.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ˌwɑʃt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ˌwɒʃt / (BrE)
washed or cleaned by water, either naturally (by rain, waves, etc.) or by being washed
