Italian rye

noun
a European grass used for hay and turf
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæljən raɪ / (AmE), / ɪˈtælɪən raɪ / (BrE)
A European grass (Lolium multiflorum) widely used for hay, turf, and as green manure, especially in agriculture.
