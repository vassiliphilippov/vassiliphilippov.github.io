hardscrabble

adjective
involving strenuous, barely sufficient effort
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌskræb.əl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌskræb.əl / (BrE)
involving hard work and struggle, especially for minimal or barely sufficient results

Examples

Hardscrabble farming
After years of hardscrabble farming, he finally saved enough to buy new equipment.
Many immigrants faced hardscrabble beginnings when they first arrived in the city.
The family endured a hardscrabble existence in the remote mountain village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impoverished - reduced to poverty, very poor
  • antonym: prosperous - characterized by wealth and success
