mujahadeen

noun
Muslim guerrilla fighters in jihad
4.5 8.9
Pronunciation: /muː.dʒə.hiː.ˈdiːn/ /; / /mʊ.dʒə.hiː.ˈdiːn/ (AmE), /mʊ.dʒə.hiː.ˈdiːn/ /; / /muː.dʒə.hiː.ˈdiːn/ (BrE)
