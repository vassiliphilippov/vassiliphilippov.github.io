search for a needle in a haystack

verb
an extremely difficult search
Pronunciation: / sɜrtʃ fər ə ˈniːdəl ɪn ə ˈheɪˌstæk / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ fə ə ˈniːdəl ɪn ə ˈheɪˌstak / (BrE)
to try to find something that is extremely difficult to locate, especially because it is small or hidden among many other things

Examples

To search for a needle in a haystack
After hours of looking for the missing file, it felt like I was searching for a needle in a haystack.
Finding the right part in this massive warehouse is truly a case of searching for a needle in a haystack.
Tracking down that rare book in the old library was like trying to search for a needle in a haystack.
