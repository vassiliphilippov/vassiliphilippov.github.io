agitational

adjective
nervous excitement
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to a state of nervous excitement, restlessness, or disturbed behavior in a person.

Examples

Agitational behavior
The doctor noted the patient's agitational state during the examination.
Her agitational behavior made it difficult for her to sit still in class.
Nurses were trained to handle agitational symptoms in patients with dementia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitated - feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
  • antonym: calm - not showing nervousness or agitation
