futuristic

adjective
relating to futurism or the future
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of futurism or of ideas, styles, or designs imagined as belonging to the future.

Examples

A futuristic design
Her futuristic novel envisions a society driven entirely by artificial intelligence.
The building's futuristic design attracted architecture enthusiasts from around the world.
The stage was decorated with futuristic shapes and bright neon lights for the play.
