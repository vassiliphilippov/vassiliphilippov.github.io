averting

noun
the act of turning away
Pronunciation: / əˈvɜrtɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈvɜːtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of turning away, especially one's eyes, face, or attention from something

Examples

Averting his eyes
His averting of attention whenever the subject came up was noticeable.
The averting of her eyes made it clear she did not want to answer the question.
There was a moment of averting their gazes when the embarrassing truth was revealed.
