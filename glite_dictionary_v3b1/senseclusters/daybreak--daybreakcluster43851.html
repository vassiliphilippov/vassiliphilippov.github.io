daybreak

noun
the first light of morning
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪˌbreɪk / (AmE), / ˈdeɪˌbreɪk / (BrE)
the time in the early morning when light first appears in the sky

Examples

At daybreak
He worked all night and collapsed into bed just as daybreak approached.
The sky turned a soft pink at daybreak, promising a sunny day.
We set out on our hike at daybreak to avoid the midday heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dawn - the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise
  • antonym: nightfall - the time when it becomes dark in the evening
  • degree higher: sunrise - the time when the sun first appears above the horizon in the morning
