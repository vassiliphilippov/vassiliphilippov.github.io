Vallisneria spiralis

noun
submerged aquatic plant with ribbonlike leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌvælɪsˈnɛriə spaɪˈrælɪs / (AmE), / ˌvalɪsˈnɪərɪə spɪˈreɪlɪs / (BrE)
a submerged aquatic plant with long, ribbonlike leaves, native to the Old World and Australia, commonly known as eelgrass and used in aquariums
