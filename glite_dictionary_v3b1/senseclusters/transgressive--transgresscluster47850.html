transgressive

adjective
breaking rules
Pronunciation: / trænzˈɡrɛsɪv / (AmE), / trænzˈɡrɛsɪv / (BrE)
going beyond accepted limits or breaking social or moral rules

Examples

Transgressive art
The director's transgressive films often challenge traditional ideas about society.
Some people admire transgressive artists for pushing boundaries and questioning norms.
His transgressive behavior at the event surprised everyone who knew him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebellious - resisting authority or control
