Republic of Guinea

proper noun
country in western Africa
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɡɪni / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˈɡɪni / (BrE)
a country in western Africa on the Atlantic Ocean, formerly a French colony, which gained independence in 1958
