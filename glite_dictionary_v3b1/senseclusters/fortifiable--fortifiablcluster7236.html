fortifiable

adjective
able to be enriched
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔːrtɪˌfaɪəbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪˌfaɪəbəl / (BrE)
capable of being enriched or strengthened by adding substances such as vitamins, minerals, or alcohol

Examples

Fortifiable juice
This juice is fortifiable with extra vitamin C to boost its health benefits.
Some foods are specifically made to be fortifiable during processing for better nutrition.
The wine is fortifiable, allowing producers to increase its alcohol content if desired.
