surreal humor

noun
humour using bizarre, illogical elements
1.0
Pronunciation: / səˈriəl ˈhjumər /; / səˈriəl ˈhjuːmɚ / (AmE), / səˈriːəl ˈhjuːmə /; / səˈrɪəl ˈhjuːmə / (BrE)
A form of humour characterized by bizarre, illogical events and situations that violate normal expectations of reasoning.

Classic examples of surreal humor include sketches by Monty Python that involve talking animals and nonsensical scenarios.

The film relied heavily on surreal humour to create a sense of dreamlike absurdity.
