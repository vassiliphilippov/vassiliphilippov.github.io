gyroscopically

adverb
using gyroscope principles
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɑpɪkli / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɒpɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves or uses the principles of a gyroscope, especially to maintain balance or stability

Examples

To be gyroscopically stable
The drone remained gyroscopically stable even in strong winds.
The spinning top balanced gyroscopically on its tip for several minutes.
Modern smartphones use sensors to adjust their screens gyroscopically as you move them.
