blanch the vegetable

verb
briefly boil and cool vegetables
6.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /blæntʃ ðə ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ /; / /blæntʃ ðiː ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ /; / /blɑːntʃ ðə ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ (AmE), /blɑːntʃ ðə ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ /; / /blæntʃ ðə ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ /; / /blɑːntʃ ðiː ˈvedʒ.tə.bəl/ (BrE)
to briefly boil vegetables and then immerse them in cold water, often to preserve color, texture, or prepare for freezing

Examples

To blanch the vegetable briefly
It is best to blanch the vegetable briefly to retain its bright color.
Many cooks blanch the vegetable to make it easier to peel.
Remember to blanch the vegetable before adding it to the stir-fry.
