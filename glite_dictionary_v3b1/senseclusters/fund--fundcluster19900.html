fund

noun
reserve of money for a purpose
3.9
Pronunciation: / fʌnd / (AmE), / fʌnd / (BrE)
an amount of money that has been saved or made available for a particular purpose

Examples

An emergency fund
Contributions to the emergency fund made a significant difference during the crisis.
She set up a college fund for her children to ensure they could afford tuition.
The charity established a fund to help rebuild the local school after the disaster.
