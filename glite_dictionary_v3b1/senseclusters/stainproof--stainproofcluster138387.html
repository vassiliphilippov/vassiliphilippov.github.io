stainproof

adjective
resistant to stains
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪnˌpruːf / (AmE), / ˈsteɪnˌpruːf / (BrE)
not easily marked, damaged, or affected by stains

Examples

Stainproof carpet
The new carpet is stainproof, so spills are much less of a problem.
Parents often choose stainproof clothing for their young children.
This tablecloth is made from a stainproof material that is easy to wipe clean.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absorbent - able to soak up liquids easily
  • synonym: stain-resistant - able to resist being stained
