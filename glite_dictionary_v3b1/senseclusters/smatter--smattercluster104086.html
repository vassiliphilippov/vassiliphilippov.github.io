smatter

verb
to scatter small spots
Pronunciation: / ˈsmætər / (AmE), / ˈsmætə / (BrE)
to cover or scatter with small spots or amounts of something

Examples

To smatter with mud
After the rain, mud smattered the bottom of his trousers.
The chef accidentally smattered sauce across the edge of the plate.
Tiny drops of paint smattered the floor during the renovation.

  • synonym: speckle - to mark with small spots or patches
