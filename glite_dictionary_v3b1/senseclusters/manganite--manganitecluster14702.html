manganite

noun
a mineral; manganese oxide ore
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡəˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡənʌɪt / (BrE)
a black or brownish-black mineral consisting of manganese oxide and hydroxide, used as a source of manganese

Examples

Black manganite mineral
Collectors prize manganite crystals for their luster and unique structure.
Geologists discovered a rich vein of manganite in the hillside.
The mining company processes manganite to extract manganese for industrial use.
