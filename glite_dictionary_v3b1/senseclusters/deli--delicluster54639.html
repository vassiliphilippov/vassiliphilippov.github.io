deli

noun
shop selling ready-to-eat foods
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛli / (AmE), / ˈdɛli / (BrE)
a shop or section of a store that sells ready-to-eat foods such as cooked meats, cheeses, salads, and sandwiches

Examples

A deli shop
She works at a small neighborhood deli that is famous for its fresh bagels.
The deli on the corner closes early on Sundays.
We stopped by the deli to pick up some sandwiches for lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicatessen - a store selling prepared foods such as cooked meats and cheeses
