non-skier

adjective
not skiing
4.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈskiːər / (AmE), / nɒnˈskiːə / (BrE)
describing a person who does not ski or something intended for people who do not ski

Examples

Non-skier activities
The hotel offers many non-skier activities for guests who prefer not to hit the slopes.
A special lounge was created for non-skier visitors to relax and enjoy the view.
Many non-skier tourists come to the mountain resort for the scenery and local cuisine.

Related Terms

  • antonym: skier - a person who skis
