wannabee

noun
person who wants to be like someone else
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːnəˌbi / (AmE), / ˈwɒnəˌbi / (BrE)
a person who aspires to be or imitate someone else, typically a famous or successful person

Examples

A wannabee artist
Aspiring writers and other wannabees flocked to the city's art scene.
He dismissed his critics as mere wannabees who had never achieved anything.
The party was crowded with wannabees hoping to catch the producer's attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aspirant - a person with ambitions to achieve something
  • synonym: hopeful - a person who is hoping or trying to achieve something
