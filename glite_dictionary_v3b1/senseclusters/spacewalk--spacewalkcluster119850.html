spacewalk

verb
move outside a spacecraft in space
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsˌwɔk / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsˌwɔːk / (BrE)
to move in space outside a spacecraft, usually while wearing a spacesuit.

Examples

To spacewalk outside
During the mission, the astronauts spacewalked to repair the satellite's solar panels.
She trained for months before she could spacewalk safely outside the International Space Station.
Two cosmonauts will spacewalk tomorrow to install new equipment on the station’s exterior.
