mamey

noun
tropical American tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / məˈmeɪ / (AmE), / məˈmeɪ / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Pouteria sapota or Mammea americana) bearing edible fruit with a leathery rind.

Examples

Mamey tree
Farmers in Central America often plant mamey trees for their delicious harvest.
She made a smoothie using ripe mamey blended with milk and honey.
The mamey is prized in the Caribbean for its sweet and nutritious fruit.
