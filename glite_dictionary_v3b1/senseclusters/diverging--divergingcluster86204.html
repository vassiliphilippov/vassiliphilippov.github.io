diverging

adjective
moving apart; separating
4.1 5.4
Pronunciation: / daɪˈvɝdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / dɪˈvɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
Tending to move apart in different directions; becoming different from each other.

Examples

Diverging opinions

The river splits into diverging channels as it enters the delta.
The two roads are diverging, each leading to a different village.
