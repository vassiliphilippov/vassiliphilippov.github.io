occupational group

noun
group in the same occupation
2.8 2.6
Pronunciation: /ɑː.kjə.ˈpeɪ.ʃən.əl gruːp/ (AmE), /ɒ.kjə.ˈpeɪ.ʃən.əl gruːp/ (BrE)
a group of people who are engaged in the same type of occupation or profession

Examples
