occupational group

noun
group in the same occupation
3.1 0.27/m
Pronunciation: / Àå…ëÀê.kj…ôÀàpe…™. É…ôn.…ôl …°ruÀêp / (AmE), / Àå…ík.j äÀàpe…™. É…ôn.…ôl …°ruÀêp / (BrE)
a group of people who are engaged in the same type of occupation or profession

Examples

An occupational group of teachers
Teachers are often considered part of a distinct occupational group within the education sector.
The survey compared the health risks faced by different occupational groups.
This benefit package is available to every occupational group in the company.

Synonyms

  • vocation
    3.8 1.01/m
    vocation is a community of people with the same calling or trade, while occupational group is a neutral term for workers in one occupation.
  • profession
    2.9 27/m
    profession refers to trained people who share a learned occupation, while occupational group describes people engaged in the same type of occupation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: profession - a paid occupation, especially one requiring formal training
  • synonym: trade - a job, especially one requiring manual skills and special training
