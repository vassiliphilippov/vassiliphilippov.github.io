toucher

noun
person who touches
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃər / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃə / (BrE)
a person or thing that touches something, especially as part of a game or activity

Examples

Game toucher
The first toucher in the game sets the pace for the others.
In some sports, the toucher is responsible for starting the play.
Each toucher must follow the rules to avoid penalties during the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: observer - a person who watches but does not take part
  • synonym: participant - a person who takes part in an activity
