balbriggan

noun
fine cotton knit fabric
Pronunciation: / bælˈbrɪɡən / (AmE), / bælˈbrɪɡən / (BrE)
A soft, finely ribbed cotton knit fabric, originally used for making underwear and nightwear.

Examples

Soft balbriggan fabric
My grandfather used to wear balbriggan undershirts during the winter.
The store sells pajamas made from soft balbriggan fabric.
Traditional balbriggan is prized for its comfort and breathability.
