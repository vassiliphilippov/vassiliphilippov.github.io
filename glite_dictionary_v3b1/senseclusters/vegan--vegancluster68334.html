vegan

noun
someone who avoids all animal products
2.4 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈviː.ɡən / (AmE), / ˈviː.ɡən / (BrE)
A person who does not eat or use any animal products, including meat, dairy, and often leather or other animal-derived materials.

Examples

A strict vegan
As a vegan, she avoids wearing leather or wool clothing.
He became a vegan after learning about animal welfare issues.
The restaurant offers several delicious vegan options on its menu.
