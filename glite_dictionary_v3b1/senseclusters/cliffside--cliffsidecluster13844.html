cliffside

noun
side of a cliff
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪfˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈklɪfˌsaɪd / (BrE)
the steep side or edge of a cliff, often overlooking a drop or body of water

Examples

The cliffside drops
The small village was perched on the cliffside with breathtaking views of the sea.
Hikers carefully made their way along the narrow cliffside path.
A lighthouse stood alone on the rugged cliffside, warning ships of danger.
