whip-scorpion

noun
nonvenomous tailed arachnid resembling a scorpion
Pronunciation: / wɪp ˈskɔːrpiən / (AmE), / wɪp ˈskɔːpiən / (BrE)
a nonvenomous arachnid that resembles a scorpion, with a long, thin tail and no stinger

Examples

A whip-scorpion arachnid
Many people confuse the harmless whip-scorpion with dangerous arachnids due to its appearance.
The whip-scorpion captured in the garden startled the children because of its long tail.
Unlike true scorpions, a whip-scorpion does not possess venom or a stinger.
