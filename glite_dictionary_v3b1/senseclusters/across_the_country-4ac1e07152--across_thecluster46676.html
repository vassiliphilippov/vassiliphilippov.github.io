across the country

adverb
throughout the entire nation
3.2
Pronunciation: / əˈkrɔs ðə ˈkʌntri / (AmE), / əˈkrɒs ðə ˈkʌntri / (BrE)
in or to every part of a nation; throughout the whole country

Examples

News across the country
After the announcement, people across the country shared their reactions on social media.
New laws were implemented across the country to improve public safety.
The music festival was celebrated across the country with events in every major city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationwide - covering or extending throughout an entire nation
