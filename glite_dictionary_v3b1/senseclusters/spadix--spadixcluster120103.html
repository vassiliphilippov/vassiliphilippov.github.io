spadix

noun
fleshy spike-like plant inflorescence
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪ.dɪks / (AmE), / ˈspeɪ.dɪks / (BrE)
A fleshy, spike-like inflorescence typically surrounded by a spathe, found in certain plants such as arums and palms.

Examples

A spadix flower spike
A banana flower develops along a thick spadix hidden by purple bracts.
The calla lily's spadix is surrounded by a large, white spathe.
The plant's spadix was covered in small, clustered flowers.
