bagworm

noun
moth larva in case
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɡˌwɜrm / (AmE), / ˈbæɡˌwɜːm / (BrE)
a type of moth or its larva that builds a protective case from silk and plant material, often feeding on trees or shrubs
