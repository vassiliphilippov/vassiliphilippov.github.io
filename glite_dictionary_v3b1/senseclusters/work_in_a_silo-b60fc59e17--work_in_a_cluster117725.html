work in a silo

verb
operate independently without collaboration
7.0
Pronunciation: / wɝk ɪn ə ˈsaɪloʊ / (AmE), / wɜːk ɪn ə ˈsaɪləʊ / (BrE)
to operate independently without sharing information or collaborating with others, especially within an organization

Examples

To work in a silo alone
If departments work in a silo, they may duplicate efforts instead of sharing resources.
Leaders encourage employees not to work in a silo but to exchange ideas across divisions.
Many large firms struggle when teams work in a silo, stifling innovation and communication.
