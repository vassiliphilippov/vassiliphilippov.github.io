dialysis machine

noun
device for filtering blood in kidney treatment
2.2 2.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.sɪs mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /daɪ.ˈæl.ə.səs mə.ˈʃiːn/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈæl.ɪ.sɪs mə.ˈʃiːn/ /; / /daɪ.ˈæl.ə.səs mə.ˈʃiːn/ (BrE)
a medical device that removes waste products and excess fluid from the blood by passing it through a semipermeable membrane, typically used for patients with kidney failure

Examples

A dialysis machine treatment
After being diagnosed with kidney failure, she needed treatment using a dialysis machine three times a week.
Technicians must regularly maintain the dialysis machine to ensure it functions properly.
The hospital invested in a new dialysis machine to improve patient care.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dialysis - the process of removing waste from the blood when the kidneys are not working
