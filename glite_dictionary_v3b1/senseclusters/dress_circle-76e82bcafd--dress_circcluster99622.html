dress circle

noun
first balcony tier in a theater
5.4 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / dr…õs Ààs…ùk…ôl / (AmE), / dr…õs Ààs…úÀêkl / (BrE)
the section of seats in a theater or opera house, usually the first tier above the orchestra, often curved in shape

Examples

The dress circle seats
Seats in the dress circle provide a great view of the stage.
The theater was almost full, especially in the dress circle.
We managed to get tickets in the dress circle for the opening night.

Synonyms

    6.3 2.70/m
    circle is a curved tier of seats usually above the stalls, while dress circle names the first tier above the orchestra.
  • first balcony
    3.9 0.01/m
    first balcony is the lower projecting tier of seats above the main floor, while dress circle is the curved first tier above the orchestra.
