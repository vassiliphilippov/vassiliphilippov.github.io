PTT

noun
communication or medical abbreviation
7.7
Pronunciation: / pi ti ti / (AmE), / piː tiː tiː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'push-to-talk' in communication systems or 'partial thromboplastin time' in medical testing

Examples

PTT button pressed
The technician instructed him to press the PTT button to speak on the radio.
The doctor ordered a PTT test to check the patient's blood clotting ability.
Many walkie-talkies have a dedicated PTT switch for quick communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partial thromboplastin time - a blood test measuring clotting time
  • synonym: push-to-talk - a method of communication where a button is pressed to speak
