multipoint

noun
multi-location connection
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipoɪnt / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipoɪnt / (BrE)
a communication system or connection that links three or more locations or participants for sharing information

Examples

Multipoint video call
The company set up a multipoint video conference to connect teams in different countries.
A multipoint network allows several offices to share data in real time.
Using a multipoint connection, the teacher could interact with students in three cities at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: network - a system of interconnected computers or devices
  • antonym: point-to-point - a direct connection between two locations or devices
