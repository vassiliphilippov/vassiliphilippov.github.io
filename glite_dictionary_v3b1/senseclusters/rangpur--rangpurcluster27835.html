rangpur

noun
acidic citrus fruit, mandarin-lemon hybrid
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈræŋ.pʊr / (AmE), / ˈræŋ.pʊə / (BrE)
A citrus fruit that is a hybrid between a mandarin orange and a lemon, known for its very acidic fruit and orange-colored peel.

Examples

The rangpur fruit
Rangpur trees are cultivated in tropical regions for their distinctive rangpur fruit.
She added slices of rangpur to her salad to give it a bright citrusy kick.
The bartender squeezed a fresh rangpur into the cocktail for a sharp, tangy flavor.
