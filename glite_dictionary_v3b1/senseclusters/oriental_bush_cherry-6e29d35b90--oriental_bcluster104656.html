oriental bush cherry

noun
East Asian shrub with red fruit and pale blossoms
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈriː.ən.tl bʊʃ ˈtʃɛr.i / (AmE), / ɒˈriː.ən.tl bʊʃ ˈtʃɛr.i / (BrE)
a woody plant native to East Asia, cultivated for its smooth red fruit and especially for its white or pale pink blossoms

Examples

The oriental bush cherry blossoms
Each spring, the oriental bush cherry in our garden bursts into pale pink blossoms.
Gardeners often grow the oriental bush cherry for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
The oriental bush cherry thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
