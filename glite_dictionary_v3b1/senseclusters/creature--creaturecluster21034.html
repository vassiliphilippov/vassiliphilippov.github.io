creature

noun
person controlled and used by others
Pronunciation: / ˈkriː.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈkriː.tʃə / (BrE)
a person who is controlled by others and used to perform unpleasant or dishonest tasks for someone else

Examples

Creature of habit
He was merely a creature of the corrupt politician, carrying out orders without question.
Many regarded the new manager as a creature of the board, lacking any original ideas of his own.
She refused to become a creature of her wealthy employer and insisted on her own independence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puppet - person who is controlled by someone else
  • synonym: tool - person used by others to accomplish something, often derogatory
