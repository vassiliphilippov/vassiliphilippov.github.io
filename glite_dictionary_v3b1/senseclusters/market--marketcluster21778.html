market

noun
system of buying and selling goods/services
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkɪt / (BrE)
the activity or system by which goods and services are bought and sold; the world of commercial activity.

Examples

The market system
After the scandal, the company's reputation in the market suffered a serious blow.
Many companies are trying to gain a foothold in the Asian market.
The global market for renewable energy is expanding rapidly each year.

Related Terms
