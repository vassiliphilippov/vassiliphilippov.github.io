wink-a-peep

noun
playful or secretive wink or glance
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪŋk ə ˌpip / (AmE), / ˈwɪŋk ə ˌpiːp / (BrE)
A playful or flirtatious wink, or a quick glance, often indicating complicity or secret understanding.

Examples

A wink-a-peep glance
He caught her eye with a subtle wink-a-peep as the conversation turned serious.
She flashed a mischievous wink-a-peep across the crowded room.
Their secret plans began with a quick wink-a-peep during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glance - a brief or hurried look
  • synonym: wink - a quick closing and opening of one eye, often as a signal
