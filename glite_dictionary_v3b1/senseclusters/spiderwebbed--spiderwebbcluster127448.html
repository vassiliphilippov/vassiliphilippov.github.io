spiderwebbed

adjective
covered in webs
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪdərˌwɛbd / (AmE), / ˈspaɪdəˌwɛbd / (BrE)
covered with spider webs, often as a sign of neglect or being unused

Examples

Spiderwebbed attic window

She brushed aside the spiderwebbed branches as she walked through the abandoned garden.
The attic was spiderwebbed and filled with forgotten boxes from decades ago.
She found an old, spiderwebbed bicycle leaning against the shed wall.
After years of neglect, the library shelves stood spiderwebbed and silent.
