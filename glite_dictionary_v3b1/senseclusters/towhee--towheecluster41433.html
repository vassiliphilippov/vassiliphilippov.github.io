towhee

noun
North American songbird with a long tail
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌhi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌhiː / (BrE)
A North American songbird of the finch family, typically with a long tail and often distinctive coloring.

Examples

A towhee bird

The distinctive call of the towhee can be heard in wooded areas.
