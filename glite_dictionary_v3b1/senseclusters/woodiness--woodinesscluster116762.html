woodiness

noun
quality of being rich in trees
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdinəs / (AmE), / ˈwʊdɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being abundant in trees or woody plants

Examples

Woodiness of a forest
Researchers studied the woodiness of different habitats to understand biodiversity.
Some regions have lost much of their original woodiness due to deforestation.
The woodiness of the landscape made it a perfect place for nature walks.
