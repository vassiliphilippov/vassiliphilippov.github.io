glitter

noun
shiny decorative pieces
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪtər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪtə / (BrE)
very small, shiny pieces used for decoration, often sprinkled on objects to make them sparkle

Examples

Colorful glitter decorations
The children covered their cards with colorful glitter.
After the party, glitter was scattered all over the floor and furniture.
She added some gold glitter to her costume to make it stand out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confetti - small pieces of colored paper thrown at celebrations
  • synonym: sparkle - to shine with bright, flashing points of light
