tune-up

noun
preparatory exercise session
1.6 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtuːn.ʌp/ /; / /ˈtjuːn.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈtjuːn.ʌp/ /; / /ˈtʃuːn.ʌp/ (BrE)
a session of exercise or practice designed to prepare for more strenuous activity or competition

Examples

Pre-game tune-up
A quick tune-up helped the team shake off their nerves before the competition.
She scheduled a tune-up at the gym to prepare for the marathon.
The athletes did a short tune-up before the big game to get their muscles ready.

Related Terms
