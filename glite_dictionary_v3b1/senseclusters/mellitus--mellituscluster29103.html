mellitus

adjective
relating to sweetness
Pronunciation: / məˈlaɪtəs / (AmE), / mɛˈlɪtəs / (BrE)
used in medical terms to indicate the presence of sweetness, especially relating to sugar in bodily fluids

Examples

Mellitus diabetes
The term mellitus in 'diabetes mellitus' refers to the sweet nature of the urine.
Doctors use the word mellitus to distinguish this type of diabetes from other forms.
Historically, the diagnosis of diabetes mellitus involved tasting the patient's urine for sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sweet - having the taste or quality of sugar or honey
