breathily

adverb
soft and airy
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛθɪli / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθɪli / (BrE)
in a soft, airy, or out-of-breath way, as if mixed with breath

Examples

To speak breathily
He spoke breathily into the microphone, creating a mysterious atmosphere.
The actor delivered his lines breathily, adding emotion to the scene.
She laughed breathily after hearing the surprising news.
She spoke breathily after running to catch the bus.
His voice sounded breathily with excitement as he shared the news.
The singer sang the opening lines breathily to create a soft, intimate mood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to hear or understand
  • antonym: hoarsely - in a rough or harsh-sounding way
  • synonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet way
