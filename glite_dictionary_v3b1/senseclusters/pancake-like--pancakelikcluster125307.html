pancake-like

adjective
resembling a pancake in shape
Pronunciation: / ˈpænkeɪk ˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈpænkeɪk ˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling a pancake in shape, typically flat and round

Examples

Pancake-like flat shape
She cooked a pancake-like bread made from rice flour.
The frog's body is pancake-like to help it hide under leaves.
The rock had a pancake-like appearance, completely flat and round.
