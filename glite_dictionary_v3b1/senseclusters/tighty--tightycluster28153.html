tighty

adjective
tight-fitting
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪti / (AmE), / ˈtaɪti / (BrE)
fitting closely to the body; tight-fitting

Examples

Tighty shirts
She prefers wearing tighty shirts when she goes jogging in the park.
His tighty pants made it difficult for him to sit comfortably during the meeting.
The dancer chose a tighty outfit to show off her movements on stage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loose - not fitting closely; not tight
  • synonym: tight - fitting closely or firmly
