breakfront

noun
projecting façade section
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪkˌfrʌnt / (AmE), / ˈbreɪkˌfrʌnt / (BrE)
a part of a building's façade that projects forward from the main line, often for decorative effect

The museum's new wing includes a glass breakfront that faces the main street.
