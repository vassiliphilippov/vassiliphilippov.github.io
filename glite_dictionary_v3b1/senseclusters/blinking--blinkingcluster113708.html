blinking

noun
rapid closing and opening of eyelids
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkɪŋ / (BrE)
the rapid closing and opening of the eyelids, usually as a reflex

Examples

Frequent blinking
Blinking helps to keep your eyes moist and free from dust.
Frequent blinking can sometimes indicate eye irritation or discomfort.
The doctor observed her blinking during the eye examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blink - to close and open the eyes quickly
