blueberry root

noun
plant with blue fruit and medicinal rootstock
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌbɛri ruːt / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɛri ruːt / (BrE)
A tall plant native to eastern North America and Asia, producing blue, berrylike fruit and possessing a thick, knotty rootstock formerly used in medicine.
