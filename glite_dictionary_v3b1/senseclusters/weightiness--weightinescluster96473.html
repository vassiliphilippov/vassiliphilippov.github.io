weightiness

noun
importance or significance
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtinəs / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being important, serious, or significant

Examples

Weightiness of the topic
He tried to add some weightiness to the discussion by referencing historical events.
Many critics admired the weightiness of the film's themes and message.
The weightiness of her remarks made everyone pause and consider their actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: importance - the state of being important
  • synonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious or grave
  • antonym: triviality - the quality of being trivial or unimportant
