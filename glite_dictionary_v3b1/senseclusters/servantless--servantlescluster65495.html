servantless

adjective
without servants
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrvəntləs / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvəntləs / (BrE)
not having any servants employed in a household

Examples

Servantless household
After moving to the city, they adjusted to a servantless household.
The novel describes the challenges of managing a large, servantless home.
Many modern families prefer a servantless lifestyle for privacy and independence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-sufficient - able to provide for oneself without help from others
  • antonym: staffed - having employees or servants present
