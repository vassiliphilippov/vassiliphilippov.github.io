Anglo

noun
white English-speaking person
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡləʊ / (BrE)
a white, English-speaking person of non-Hispanic European background, especially in the United States

Examples

Anglo community
The new neighborhood was mostly Anglo, with only a few families from other backgrounds.
Many Anglo residents attended the town meeting while Latino neighbors met separately.
The festival celebrated both Anglo and Hispanic traditions in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Anglo-American - a person of English descent in America
  • antonym: Latino - a person of Latin American origin or descent
