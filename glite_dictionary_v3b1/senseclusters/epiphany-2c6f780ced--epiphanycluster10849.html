Epiphany

noun
Christian festival on January 6th

A Christian festival on January 6th, commemorating the visit of the three wise men to the infant Jesus.

Examples

Epiphany festival
Many Christians attend special church services to celebrate Epiphany every January 6th.
Some families in Europe exchange gifts on Epiphany instead of Christmas.
The tradition of the Three Kings Day parade marks the arrival of Epiphany in Spain.

Synonyms

  • Epiphany of Our Lord
    8.4 0.00/m
    Epiphany of Our Lord is the formal church name for the feast, while Epiphany is the common name for the same festival.
  • Three Kings' Day
    4.9 0.00/m
    Three Kings' Day is the popular, family name used in many countries, while Epiphany is the religious feast term used in churches.
  • Twelfth day
    8.1 0.00/m
    Twelfth day refers to the twelfth day after Christmas and can mean the date itself, while Epiphany names the festival on that date.
