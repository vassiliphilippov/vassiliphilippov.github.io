abatable nuisance

noun
nuisance that can be remedied by action
9.3
Pronunciation: / əˈbeɪ.tə.bəl ˈnuː.səns / (AmE), / əˈbeɪ.tə.bəl ˈnjuː.səns / (BrE)
A nuisance that can be stopped, removed, or made harmless, especially as recognized by law.

Examples

An abatable nuisance
An abatable nuisance like excess noise can be legally addressed by local authorities.
Legal action may be taken if an abatable nuisance is not corrected in a timely manner.
