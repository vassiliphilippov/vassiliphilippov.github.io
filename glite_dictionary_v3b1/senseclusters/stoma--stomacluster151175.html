stoma

noun
surgically created body opening
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈstoʊ mə / (AmE), / ˈstəʊ mə / (BrE)
an artificial opening created by surgery on the surface of the body to allow passage to or from an internal organ

Examples

Surgical stoma
After the surgery, a stoma was created to allow waste to exit the body.
Different types of clothing can be worn comfortably over a stoma.
The nurse taught the patient how to care for his stoma at home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ostomy - a surgical procedure to create an opening (stoma) in the body
