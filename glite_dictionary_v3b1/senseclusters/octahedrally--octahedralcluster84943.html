octahedrally

adverb
in an octahedral way
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɑktəˈhiːdrəli / (AmE), / ˌɒktəˈhiːdrəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or has the shape of an octahedron, especially in geometry or chemistry

The chemist explained how the ligands bond octahedrally to the central atom.
