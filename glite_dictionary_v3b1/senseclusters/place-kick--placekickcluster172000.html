place-kick

verb
kick a stationary ball in football or rugby
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪsˌkɪk / (AmE), / ˈpleɪs.kɪk / (BrE)
to kick a ball from a stationary position on the ground, especially in football or rugby, often to score a goal or point
