neuroendocrinology

noun
study of nervous and endocrine systems
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˌɛndəkrəˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˌɛndəkrɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how the nervous system and the endocrine system interact and influence each other

Examples

Study of neuroendocrinology
Advances in neuroendocrinology have helped explain how stress affects hormone levels in the body.
She decided to pursue a career in neuroendocrinology after learning about brain-hormone interactions.
Research in neuroendocrinology explores how the brain regulates growth, metabolism, and reproduction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endocrinology - the study of hormones and the endocrine system
  • synonym: neuroscience - the scientific study of the nervous system
