fricassee

verb
cook by stewing or frying in sauce
9.9 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfrɪkəˈsi / (AmE), / ˌfrɪkəˈsiː / (BrE)
to cook meat or vegetables by stewing or frying them lightly and then serving them in a sauce

Examples

The chef expertly fricasseed the mushrooms to bring out their flavor.
