storyboard

verb
to plan with drawings
4.7
Pronunciation: /ˈstɔːr.bɔːrd/ /; / /ˈstoʊr.bɔːrd/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊ.bɔːd/ /; / /ˈstɔː.bɔːd/ (BrE)
to create a sequence of drawings or images to plan the scenes of a story, film, or project

Examples

To storyboard a film
Before shooting the movie, the director storyboarded every important scene to visualize the action.
The advertising team storyboards their ideas to present them clearly to the client.
We storyboarded the animation to make sure the story flowed smoothly from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outline - to give a general description or plan
  • synonym: sketch - to make a quick drawing
