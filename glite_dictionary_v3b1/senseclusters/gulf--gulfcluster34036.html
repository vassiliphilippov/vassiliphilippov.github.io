gulf

noun
large difference or separation between groups/ideas
Pronunciation: / ɡʌlf / (AmE), / ɡʌlf / (BrE)
a large difference or separation between people, groups, ideas, or things; a wide gap or chasm (literal or figurative)

Examples

A gulf between people
After years of conflict, there was a growing gulf between the two communities.
Her strict upbringing created a gulf of misunderstanding with her more liberal friends.
The gulf between the rich and the poor has widened in recent decades.
After years of conflict, a gulf remained between the two communities.
The earthquake created a massive gulf in the landscape.
There was a gulf of misunderstanding between the generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chasm - a profound gap or division between two people, groups, or viewpoints
  • synonym: divide - a wide separation or difference between people or groups
  • synonym: gap - an open or empty space in or between things
