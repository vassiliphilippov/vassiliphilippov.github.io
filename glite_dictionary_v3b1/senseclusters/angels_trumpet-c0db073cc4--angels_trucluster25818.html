angel's trumpet

noun
ornamental plant with trumpet-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˌtrʌmpɪt /; / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈtrʌmpɪt / (AmE), / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˌtrʌmpɪt /; / ˈeɪndʒəlz ˈtrʌmpɪt / (BrE)
a South American plant (genus Brugmansia) with large, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers, often grown as an ornamental; all parts are highly toxic if eaten
