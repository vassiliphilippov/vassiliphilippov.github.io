enduro

adjective
for endurance racing
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdʊroʊ / (AmE), / ɛnˈdjʊərəʊ / (BrE)
designed for or relating to long-distance off-road endurance racing, especially for bicycles or motorcycles

Examples

Enduro bike gear
He bought an enduro helmet and protective gear for the rough mountain trails.
The new enduro bike is built to handle long, challenging rides over rocky terrain.
She prefers enduro motorcycles because they are durable and suitable for endurance events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: off-road - designed for use on rough or unpaved surfaces
  • synonym: racing - competing in speed contests
