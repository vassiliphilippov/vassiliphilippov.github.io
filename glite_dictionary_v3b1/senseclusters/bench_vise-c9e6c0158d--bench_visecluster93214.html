bench vise

noun
workbench clamping device with jaws
4.1 5.3
Pronunciation: /bɛntʃ vaɪs/ /; / /bɛntʃ viː.ˈzeɪ/ (AmE), /bɛntʃ vaɪs/ /; / /bɛntʃ viː.ˈzeɪ/ (BrE)
