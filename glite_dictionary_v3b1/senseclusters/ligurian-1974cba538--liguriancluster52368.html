Ligurian

adjective
of Liguria or its people
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡjʊriən / (AmE), / lɪˈɡjʊəriən / (BrE)
relating to Liguria, a region in northwestern Italy, its people, or their language

Examples

Ligurian coast
The Ligurian coast is famous for its beautiful scenery and colorful villages.

Many Ligurian dishes feature fresh seafood and aromatic herbs.
