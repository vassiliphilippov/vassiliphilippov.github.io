busted

adjective
broken, not working (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌstɪd / (AmE), / ˈbʌstɪd / (BrE)
(informal) Not working; broken or out of order.

Examples

A busted phone
After dropping his phone, he discovered the screen was totally busted.
Can you call maintenance? The elevator is busted again.
My old radio is completely busted and won’t even turn on.
