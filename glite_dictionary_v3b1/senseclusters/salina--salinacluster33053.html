salina

noun
salt flat or basin
Pronunciation: / səˈliːnə / (AmE), / səˈliːnə / (BrE)
a natural area or basin where salt is deposited, often forming a salt flat or salt lake

Examples

A natural salina
The salina stretched for miles, its surface sparkling under the midday sun.
After the rainy season, the water evaporated and left a thick layer of salt in the salina.
Flamingos gathered at the edge of the salina to feed on tiny crustaceans.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: lake - a large body of water surrounded by land
  • synonym: salt flat - a flat area covered with salt left by evaporated water
  • synonym: salt pan - a natural basin where salt collects after water evaporates
