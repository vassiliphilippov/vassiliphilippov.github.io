trepan

noun
surgical tool for cutting bone
9.9 9.3
Pronunciation: / trɪˈpæn /; / ˈtrɛpæn / (AmE), / trɪˈpan /; / ˈtrɛpan / (BrE)
a tool or surgical instrument with a circular blade used for cutting holes, especially in bone (such as the skull) or in materials like stone
