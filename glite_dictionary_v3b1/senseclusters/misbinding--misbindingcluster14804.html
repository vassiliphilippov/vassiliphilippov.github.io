misbinding

noun
incorrect binding
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈbaɪndɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈbaɪndɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or result of binding something incorrectly, especially in book production or assembly

Examples

Book misbinding
The publisher had to recall the books because of a misbinding that mixed up several chapters.
A simple misbinding can ruin the appearance and usability of a finished book.
After discovering the misbinding, the company offered replacements to all affected customers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: binding - the process of fastening pages together to form a book
  • synonym: binding error - a mistake made during the process of binding
