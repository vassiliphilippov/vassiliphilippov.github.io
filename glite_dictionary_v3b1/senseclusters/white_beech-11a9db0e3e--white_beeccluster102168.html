white beech

noun
North American tree with edible nuts
5.6
Pronunciation: / waɪt bitʃ / (AmE), / waɪt biːtʃ / (BrE)
A North American forest tree with light green leaves and edible nuts.

Examples

The white beech tree
The white beech is commonly found in eastern North American forests.
The lumber from the white beech is prized for its strength and smooth grain.
We collected the edible nuts under the white beech during our hike.
