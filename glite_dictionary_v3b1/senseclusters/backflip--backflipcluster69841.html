backflip

verb
to rotate backward in air
3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.flɪp/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.flɪp/ (BrE)
to perform a jump in which the body rotates backward in the air and lands on the feet

Examples

To backflip on stage
The gymnast managed to backflip perfectly during her floor routine.
He tried to backflip off the diving board but lost his balance.
Crowds cheered as the skateboarder did a backflip over the ramp.
