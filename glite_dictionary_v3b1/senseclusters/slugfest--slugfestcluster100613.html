slugfest

noun
intense fight or fierce contest
8.3 6.4
Pronunciation: /ˈslʌg.fest/ (AmE), /ˈslʌg.fest/ (BrE)
An intense fight or contest, especially a bare-knuckle brawl or a fierce, prolonged struggle.

Examples

A slugfest fight
Last night's hockey game was a real slugfest with players brawling on the ice several times.
The boxing match quickly turned into a slugfest as both fighters refused to back down.
The debate became a verbal slugfest as the candidates exchanged fierce accusations.
