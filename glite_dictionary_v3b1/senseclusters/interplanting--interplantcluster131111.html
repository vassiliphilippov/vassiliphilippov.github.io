interplanting

adjective
mixed-crop farming
6.3
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈplæn.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈplɑːn.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
relating to or used for the practice of planting different crops together in the same area.

Examples

Interplanting method
The farm used an interplanting strategy to improve soil health and reduce pests.
Many gardeners prefer interplanting methods to maximize space and crop diversity.
An interplanting system can help prevent the spread of disease among crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: companion planting - growing different plants together for mutual benefit
  • antonym: monoculture - the cultivation of a single crop in a given area
