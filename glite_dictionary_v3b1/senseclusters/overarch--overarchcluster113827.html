overarch

verb
to be central or dominant
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɑrtʃ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɑːtʃ / (BrE)
to be central, dominant, or overarching, especially in relation to a subject or theme

Examples

Overarch the story
A sense of mystery overarches the novel from start to finish.
The new policy overarches all departmental guidelines to ensure consistency.
The theme of forgiveness overarches every story in the collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dominate - to have control or power over; to be the most important feature
