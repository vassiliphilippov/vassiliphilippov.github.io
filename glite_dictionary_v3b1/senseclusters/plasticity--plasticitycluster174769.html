Synonyms

  • malleability
    7.4 0.29/m
    malleability is the property of a metal to be shaped by hammering or pressure, while plasticity means being easily molded without breaking.
  • moldability
    5.8 0.01/m
    moldability describes how well a material fits a mold and keeps shape, while plasticity means general ease of shaping without breaking.
  • formability
    6.0 0.01/m
    formability is used for making parts by shaping or forming in industry, while plasticity means general malleability without breaking.
  • pliability
    6.0 0.03/m
    pliability means being easy to bend or fold in use, while plasticity refers to being shaped or molded without breaking.
  • deformability
    7.3 0.00/m
    deformability means a material can change shape under force, while plasticity means it can be shaped without cracking or breaking.
