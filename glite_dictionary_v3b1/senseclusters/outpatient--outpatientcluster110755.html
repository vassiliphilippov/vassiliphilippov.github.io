outpatient

noun
patient not staying overnight in hospital
4.6 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊt.peɪ.ʃənt/ (AmE), /ˈaʊt.peɪ.ʃənt/ (BrE)
a patient who receives medical treatment at a hospital or clinic but does not stay overnight

Examples

An outpatient visit
After her surgery, Jane was treated as an outpatient and went home the same day.
He visited the doctor as an outpatient, avoiding the need for an overnight stay.
The hospital has a new wing dedicated to outpatient services.
