exclusion

noun
the state of being excommunicated
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɪks.ˈkluː.ʒən/ /; / /ɪk.ˈskluː.ʒən/ (AmE), /ɪks.ˈkluː.ʒən/ /; / /ɪk.ˈskluː.ʒən/ (BrE)
the act or state of not allowing someone or something to be included or to take part; being left out
