Noahic

adjective
of Noah or descendants
Pronunciation: / noʊˈeɪɪk / (AmE), / nəʊˈeɪɪk / (BrE)
relating to the biblical figure Noah or his descendants

Examples

Noahic covenant
The Noahic covenant is an important concept in many religious traditions.
Scholars debate the influence of Noahic laws on later legal systems.
The story describes a Noahic lineage that survived the great flood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Abrahamic - relating to Abraham or his descendants
  • synonym: Noachian - relating to Noah or the time of Noah
