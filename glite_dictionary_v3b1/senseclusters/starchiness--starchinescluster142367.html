starchiness

noun
quality of being starchy
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of containing a lot of starch or resembling starch in texture or taste

Examples

Starchiness of rice
The starchiness of the rice made it ideal for sushi rolls.
Some people prefer the starchiness of russet potatoes when making baked dishes.
The soup was thickened by the natural starchiness of the vegetables.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dryness - the quality of being dry, lacking moisture
  • synonym: starch - a white, tasteless carbohydrate found in foods like potatoes and rice
