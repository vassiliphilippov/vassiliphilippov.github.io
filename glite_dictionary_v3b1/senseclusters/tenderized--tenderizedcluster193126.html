tenderized

adjective
made more tender (meat)
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dɚ.aɪzd /; / ˈtɛndəˌraɪzd / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.də.raɪzd /; / ˈtɛndəraɪzd / (BrE)
(of meat) made more tender by marinating, pounding, or other methods.
