cheeseparing

adjective
extremely reluctant to spend money
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃizˌpɛrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌpeərɪŋ / (BrE)
very reluctant to give or spend money; excessively frugal or miserly

Examples

A cheeseparing attitude
A cheeseparing attitude can sometimes harm long-term growth despite short-term savings.
He is known for his cheeseparing habits, rarely treating himself to anything unnecessary.
The company's cheeseparing approach left employees frustrated with outdated equipment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give and spend freely
  • synonym: miserly - unwilling to spend money; mean
