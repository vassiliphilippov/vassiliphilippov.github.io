atmospheric condition

noun
state of the atmosphere (weather)
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˌætməsˈfɛrɪk kənˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌatməsˈfɛrɪk kəndɪʃən / (BrE)
the state of the atmosphere at a particular place and time, especially regarding temperature, wind, clouds, and precipitation
