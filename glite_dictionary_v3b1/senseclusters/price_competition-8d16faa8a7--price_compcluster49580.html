price competition

noun
competition by lowering prices
3.0 2.5
Pronunciation: /praɪs kɑːm.pə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ /; / /praɪs kəm.pə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (AmE), /praɪs kɒm.pə.ˈtɪʃ.ən/ (BrE)
