plum

noun
tree producing edible fruit (plums)
1.0 0.69/m
Pronunciation: / plÊŒm / (AmE), / plÊŒm / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Prunus that produces edible, oval fruit with smooth skin and a single hard stone.

Examples

Farmers graft different varieties of plum onto strong rootstocks to improve resilience.
The plum in our backyard bears fruit every summer.
