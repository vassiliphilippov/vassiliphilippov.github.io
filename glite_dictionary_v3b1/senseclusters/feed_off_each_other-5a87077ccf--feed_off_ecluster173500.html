feed off each other

verb
draw energy or motivation from each other
5.3 4.2
Pronunciation: /fiːd ɔːf iːtʃ ˈʌ.ðər/ /; / /fiːd ɑːf iːtʃ ˈʌ.ðər/ (AmE), /fiːd ɒf iːtʃ ˈʌ.ðə/ /; / /fiːd ɔːf iːtʃ ˈʌ.ðə/ (BrE)
(of two or more people, groups, etc.) to gain energy, motivation, or inspiration from the actions or emotions of one another, often intensifying each other's feelings or behavior.
