factoid

noun
brief or trivial piece of information

Pronunciation: / ˈfæk.tɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈfæk.tɔɪd / (BrE)

Examples

A fun factoid
Did you know this factoid: the inventor of the Frisbee was turned into a Frisbee?

The magazine included a factoid about how honey never spoils.
