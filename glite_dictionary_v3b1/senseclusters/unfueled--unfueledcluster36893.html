unfueled

adjective
not provided with fuel
5.0 6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfjuld / (AmE), / ʌnˈfjʊəld / (BrE)
Not provided with fuel; operating or existing without being supplied with fuel.

Examples

Unfueled rocket launch
Many satellites are launched unfueled and receive power from solar panels instead.
The generator sat unused and unfueled in the shed during the summer months.
The rocket launch had to be delayed because the vehicle remained unfueled due to safety checks.
