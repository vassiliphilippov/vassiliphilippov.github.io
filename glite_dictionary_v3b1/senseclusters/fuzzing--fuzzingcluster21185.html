fuzzing

noun
guitar distortion effect
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfʌ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfʌ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
a musical distortion effect that creates a coarse, warm, or 'fuzzy' tone, especially on electric guitars

Examples

Guitar fuzzing effect
The band's signature sound relies heavily on fuzzing to create a gritty atmosphere.
He adjusted the pedal settings to increase the fuzzing during the chorus.
Classic rock songs often feature fuzzing to give the guitar a unique texture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - a guitar sound without distortion or effects
  • synonym: distortion - an audio effect that alters the sound to make it rougher or more intense
  • synonym: overdrive - a guitar effect that produces a warm, driven sound
