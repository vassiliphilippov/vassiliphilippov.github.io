barycentric

adjective
coordinate system method
9.9
Pronunciation: /bæ.rɪ.sen.ˈtrɪk/ /; / /bə.rɪ.sen.ˈtrɪk/ (AmE), /bæ.rɪ.sen.ˈtrɪk/ /; / /bə.rɪ.sen.ˈtrɪk/ (BrE)
relating to a coordinate system or method that expresses a point as a combination of weights at the vertices of a triangle or simplex.

Examples

Barycentric coordinates method

The mathematician explained the concept of barycentric coordinates in relation to triangle geometry.
Using barycentric methods, the location of a point inside a simplex can be easily described.
