hinge upon

verb
depend on; be contingent on
Pronunciation: / hɪndʒ əˈpɑn / (AmE), / hɪndʒ əˈpɒn / (BrE)
to depend entirely on or be determined by something

Examples

To hinge upon success
Her promotion may hinge upon the successful completion of this project.
The entire decision will hinge upon the outcome of the negotiations.
Whether we go on vacation this year will hinge upon our financial situation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depend on - to be determined by or rely on something
  • synonym: rest on - to depend on or be based on something
