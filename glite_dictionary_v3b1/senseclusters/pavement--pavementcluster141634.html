A paved surface or path, especially one at the side of a road for people to walk on.

Examples

Walk on the pavement

The rain made the pavement slippery and dangerous to walk on.
We walked along the pavement to avoid the busy road.

Synonyms

  • sidewalk
    1.0 35/m
    sidewalk is a paved path beside a street used by pedestrians, while pavement is the general term for such roadside walkways.
  • footway
    2.5 0.02/m
    footway is a public path often next to a road for people to walk on, while pavement refers to the paved surface beside a road.
  • walkway
    2.0 5.06/m
    walkway is a passage often built or raised for walking in many places, while pavement specifically means the paved path beside a road.
