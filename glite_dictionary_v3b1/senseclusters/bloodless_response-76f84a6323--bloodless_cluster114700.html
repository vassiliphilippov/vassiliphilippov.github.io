bloodless response

noun
emotionless or cold reply
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌd.ləs rɪˈspɑns / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs rɪˈspɒns / (BrE)
a reply or reaction that shows no emotion, enthusiasm, or warmth

Examples

A cold bloodless response
Despite the emotional topic, she gave only a bloodless response during the interview.
His apology was met with a bloodless response that lacked any hint of forgiveness.
The manager's bloodless response to the complaint left the customer feeling ignored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold response - a reply lacking warmth or emotion
  • antonym: warm response - a reply showing warmth or enthusiasm
