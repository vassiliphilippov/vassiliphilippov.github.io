bounce-back

noun
undelivered email notice
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊns ˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbaʊns ˌbæk / (BrE)
an automatic message sent to inform the sender that an email or electronic message could not be delivered

Examples

Email bounce-back
After sending the email, she received a bounce-back saying the recipient's address did not exist.
If you get a bounce-back, double-check that you typed the email address correctly.
The bounce-back included details about why the message could not be delivered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: delivery receipt - a message confirming successful delivery of an email
  • synonym: non-delivery report - a notification that an email was not delivered
