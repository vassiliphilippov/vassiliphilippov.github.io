haecceity

noun
essence making something unique

the property or quality of being a unique or individual thing; the essence that makes something distinct from others

Examples

The haecceity of things
In medieval philosophy, haecceity was contrasted with quiddity to distinguish 'thisness' from 'whatness'.
Modern metaphysicians debate whether haecceity is necessary to account for individuality in objects.
The artist sought the haecceity of each subject, capturing their unique individuality.
