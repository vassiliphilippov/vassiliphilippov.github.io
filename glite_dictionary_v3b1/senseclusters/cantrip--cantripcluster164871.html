cantrip

noun
minor magic spell
Pronunciation: / ˈkæntrɪp / (AmE), / ˈkæntrɪp / (BrE)
a minor or simple magic spell, especially in fantasy stories or games

In the game, every player can use at least one cantrip without using up their magical energy.
