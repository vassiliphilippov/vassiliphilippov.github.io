flag

noun
flat stone slab used for paving
Pronunciation: / flæɡ / (AmE), / flæɡ / (BrE)
A flat stone slab that splits into layers and is used for paving surfaces such as paths, patios, or floors.

Examples

A stone flag
The garden path was laid with large flag stones to create a rustic look.
The workers set the flag securely in place to form the patio.
We replaced the broken flag near the front steps with a new one.
