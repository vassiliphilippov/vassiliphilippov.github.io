sporty

adjective
athletic or stylish in appearance
3.3 1.64/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsp…îrti / (AmE), / Ààsp…îÀêti / (BrE)
having a style, appearance, or quality that is energetic, attractive, and related to sports or sportswear

Examples

Sporty style
His sporty attire made him stand out among the formal crowd.
She wore a sporty jacket with bold stripes to the party.
The new model has a more sporty look compared to last year's design.
He wore a sporty jacket to the party, giving him a relaxed but stylish look.
Her new car has a sporty design that makes it stand out on the road.
The team chose a sporty logo that emphasized energy and movement.

Synonyms

  • athletic
    2.8 23/m
    athletic describes someone physically fit and active, while sporty describes clothing or style that looks energetic and sport-inspired.

Related Terms

  • synonym: athletic - physically strong, fit, and active
  • degree lower: casual - relaxed in style; not formal (sometimes overlapping with 'sporty' style)
  • antonym: plain - simple in style, not elaborate or showy
