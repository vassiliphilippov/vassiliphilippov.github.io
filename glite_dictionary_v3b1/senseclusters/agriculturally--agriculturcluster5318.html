agriculturally

adverb
in a farming manner
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəli / (AmE), / ˌæɡrɪˈkʌltʃərəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to farming or agriculture

Examples

To be agriculturally rich
The country is agriculturally rich, producing large amounts of wheat and corn each year.
Many areas in the valley are agriculturally developed thanks to advanced irrigation systems.
The land was not agriculturally suitable due to its rocky soil and lack of water.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agricultural - relating to farming or agriculture
  • antonym: industrially - in a way that relates to industry or manufacturing
