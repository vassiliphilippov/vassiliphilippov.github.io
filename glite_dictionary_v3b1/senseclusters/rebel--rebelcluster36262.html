Examples

To rebel against rules

Synonyms

  • resist
    2.2 19/m
    resist means to act against something firmly, while rebel often implies refusing authority openly or joining wider opposition.
  • defy
    3.7 8.02/m
    defy is to openly challenge or refuse orders in front of others, while rebel is broader opposition to authority.
  • rise up
    5.8 3.45/m
    rise up is to take part in an uprising or revolt, while rebel can mean oppose authority in many ways.
  • revolt
    4.9 1.38/m
    revolt means to rise against a ruler or government on a large scale, while rebel can be individual or group opposition.
  • rise
    8.0 3.31/m
    rise means to take up arms in violent opposition, while rebel covers any act of opposing authority or tradition.
