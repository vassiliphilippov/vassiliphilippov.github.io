Nez Perce

noun
member of a Native American tribe
Pronunciation: / nɛz ˈpɜrs / (AmE), / nɛz ˈpɜːs / (BrE)
A member of a Native American tribe of the Shahaptian people indigenous to the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.

Examples

Nez Perce tribe
A noted Nez Perce leader was Chief Joseph, known for his eloquence and courage.
She is proud of her heritage as a Nez Perce and works to preserve tribal traditions.
The Nez Perce resisted forced removal from their ancestral lands in the 19th century.
