crossbred

noun
hybrid animal or plant
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌbrɛd / (BrE)
an animal or plant produced by mating individuals of different breeds or varieties

Examples

A crossbred cow
The farmer introduced several crossbred cows to improve milk production on his farm.
Many gardeners prefer crossbred tomatoes for their resistance to disease and better flavor.
The show featured a prize-winning crossbred sheep with a unique wool pattern.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hybrid - an organism produced by combining different varieties or species
  • antonym: purebred - an animal or plant of unmixed lineage
