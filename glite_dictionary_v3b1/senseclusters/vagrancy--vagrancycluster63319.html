vagrancy

noun
the state of being homeless and wandering
8.5 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈveɪ.grən.siː/ /; / /ˈveɪ.grən.sɪ/ (AmE), /ˈveɪ.grən.sɪ/ /; / /ˈveɪ.grən.siː/ (BrE)
the state or condition of wandering from place to place without a permanent home or regular means of support, often resulting in homelessness

Examples

Life of vagrancy
After losing his job, he fell into a life of vagrancy for several years.
Laws against vagrancy were enforced strictly in Victorian England.
Many cities have struggled to address the complex social issues linked to vagrancy.
