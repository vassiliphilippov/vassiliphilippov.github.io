duckie

noun
bath toy duck
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌki / (AmE), / ˈdʌki / (BrE)
a small rubber or plastic toy shaped like a duck, often used by children during bath time

Examples

Bath duckie
The baby laughed as she squeezed her yellow duckie in the bathtub.
He collected every color of duckie he could find for his bath time.
A floating duckie made the water games even more fun for the children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rubber duck - a toy duck made of rubber or plastic for use in water
