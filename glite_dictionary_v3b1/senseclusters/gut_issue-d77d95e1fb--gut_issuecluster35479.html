gut issue

noun
emotionally charged issue
6.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /gʌt ˈɪ.ʃuː/ /; / /gʌt ˈɪʃ.juː/ /; / /gʌt ˈɪʃ.jə/ (AmE), /gʌt ˈɪ.ʃuː/ /; / /gʌt ˈɪʃ.jə/ (BrE)
an issue or topic that provokes strong instinctive or emotional reactions, rather than rational or purely intellectual responses

Examples

Climate change became a gut issue after several devastating hurricanes hit the coast.
