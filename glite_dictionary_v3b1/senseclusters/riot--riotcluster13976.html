riot

noun
wild, rowdy gathering (drinking/sex)
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪət / (AmE), / ˈraɪət / (BrE)
A wild or rowdy gathering, especially one involving excessive drinking and sexual activity.

Examples

A wild riot party
The party quickly turned into a riot, with guests drinking and dancing wildly.
The weekend retreat became a full-blown riot by midnight.
They were notorious for turning even quiet events into a riot whenever they showed up.
