metabolizable

adjective
can be metabolized
6.1
Pronunciation: / məˈtæbəˌlaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / mɪˈtæbələˌlaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be broken down or used by the metabolism of a living organism

Examples

Metabolizable energy source
Glucose is a metabolizable source of energy for most animals.
The nutrition label lists only the metabolizable carbohydrates in the product.
Some artificial sweeteners are not metabolizable and pass through the body unchanged.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digestible - able to be digested by the body
  • antonym: nonmetabolizable - not able to be broken down or used by metabolism
