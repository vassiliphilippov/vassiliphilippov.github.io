supertitle

noun
translation projected above stage
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsuː.pɚˌtaɪ.təl / (AmE), / ˈsuː.pəˌtaɪ.təl / (BrE)
A translation of the words of an opera or choral work, projected onto a screen above the stage for the audience.

Examples

Opera supertitles
At the concert, the supertitles ensured everyone understood the choral work.
I found the supertitle very helpful during the French performance, even though I don't speak the language.
The orchestra projected supertitles so that the audience could follow the Italian opera.

Related Terms

  • synonym: subtitle - a translation of dialogue displayed at the bottom of a screen
