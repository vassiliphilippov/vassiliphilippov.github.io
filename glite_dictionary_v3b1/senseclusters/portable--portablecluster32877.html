portable

adjective
easily carried or moved; detachable equipment
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔːrtəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɔːtəbəl / (BrE)
able to be easily carried or moved, especially because it is lighter and smaller than usual

Examples

A portable speaker
Modern boats often rely on portable motors for greater convenience and safety.
The portable engine can be attached to the hull when needed and removed for storage.
We bought a portable outboard motor for our small fishing boat.
She prefers using a portable computer so she can work from different locations.
The portable speaker lasted for hours on a single charge during our picnic.
They bought a portable air conditioner to keep the bedroom cool during the summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detachable - able to be removed from something else
  • synonym: mobile - able to move or be moved easily and freely
  • antonym: stationary - not moving or not intended to be moved
