polyarchic

adjective
ruled by many
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈɑrkɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɑːkɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by rule or government by many people

Examples

Polyarchic government system
The council adopted a polyarchic system to ensure that power was shared among many citizens.
Historians debate whether the ancient republic was truly polyarchic or just a form of oligarchy.
A polyarchic structure can help prevent the concentration of authority in a single leader.

Related Terms

  • synonym: democratic - relating to government by the people
  • antonym: oligarchic - relating to rule by a small group
