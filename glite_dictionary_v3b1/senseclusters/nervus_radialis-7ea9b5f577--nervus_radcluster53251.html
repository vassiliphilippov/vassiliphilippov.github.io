nervus radialis

noun
major nerve branch; arm and hand innervation

Examples

The nervus radialis nerve
Damage to the nervus radialis may cause loss of sensation on the back of the hand.
Surgeons must take care to avoid the nervus radialis during certain arm procedures.
The nervus radialis can be injured during fractures of the humerus, leading to weakness in the wrist and fingers.
