imprecisely

adverb
inexactly; not precisely
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpriːsli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpriːsli / (BrE)
In a way that is not exact, clear, or accurate.

Examples

To speak imprecisely
If you measure imprecisely, your results may not be reliable.
She imprecisely estimated the number of guests attending the event.
The instructions were written imprecisely, making them difficult to follow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: precisely - in a precise or exact manner
