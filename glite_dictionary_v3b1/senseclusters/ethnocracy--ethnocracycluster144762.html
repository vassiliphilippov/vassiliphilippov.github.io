ethnocracy

noun
ethnic group rule
Pronunciation: / ɛθˈnɑkrəsi /; / ɛθˈnɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ɛθˈnɒkrəsi / (BrE)
a system of government or society in which one ethnic group holds most or all political power, often excluding others

Examples

Critics argue that the country is moving toward an ethnocracy rather than a true democracy.
