Fresnel

adjective
optical or physics-related
Pronunciation: / freɪˈnɛl / (AmE), / freɪˈnɛl / (BrE)
relating to the optical theories, effects, or devices developed by or named after Augustin-Jean Fresnel, especially in physics or engineering

Examples

Fresnel effect in physics

A Fresnel zone is important in understanding how radio waves travel around obstacles.
The experiment demonstrated a classic Fresnel diffraction pattern on the screen.
