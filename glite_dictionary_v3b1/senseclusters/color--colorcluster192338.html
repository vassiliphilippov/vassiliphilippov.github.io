color

noun
substance used to give color
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌlɚ /; / ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a substance such as paint, dye, or pigment used to impart color to something

Examples

You will need to mix the colours thoroughly before applying them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - substance used to change color of materials
  • synonym: paint - coloured substance spread over a surface to leave a thin decorative or protective coating
  • synonym: pigment - substance providing color to other materials
