make a dash for

verb
run quickly towards something
6.7 3.4
Pronunciation: / meɪk ə dæʃ fər / (AmE), / meɪk ə daʃ fə / (BrE)
to run quickly towards a particular place or object, usually in order to reach it before something happens or before someone else

Examples

She made a dash for her umbrella when it started to rain.
When the bell rang, the students made a dash for the cafeteria.
