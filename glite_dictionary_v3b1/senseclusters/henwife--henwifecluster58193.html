henwife

noun
woman tending hens
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛnˌwaɪf / (AmE), / ˈhɛnˌwaɪf / (BrE)
an old term for a woman who keeps or takes care of hens

Examples

The henwife cares hens
The village henwife was known for her delicious fresh eggs.
Every morning, the henwife would feed the hens and collect their eggs.
Stories about the wise old henwife were told to children in the countryside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poultry keeper - a person who looks after domestic birds such as chickens
