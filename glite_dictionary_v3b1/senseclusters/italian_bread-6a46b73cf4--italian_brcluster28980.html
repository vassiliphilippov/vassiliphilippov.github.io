Italian bread

noun
unsweetened, thick, long loaf of bread
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæliən brɛd / (AmE), / ɪˈtalɪən brɛd / (BrE)
A type of unsweetened yeast bread made without shortening, baked in long, thick loaves with tapered ends.

Examples

A loaf of Italian bread
He sliced the Italian bread and arranged it on a platter for the guests.
The bakery is famous for its crusty Italian bread baked every morning.
We bought a fresh loaf of Italian bread to serve with dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: French bread - long, thin unsweetened white bread, often with a crisp crust
