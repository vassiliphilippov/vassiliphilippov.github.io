maneuverable

adjective
easy to move or steer
6.4
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːvərəbəl /; / məˈnuːvərəbl̩ / (AmE), / məˈnjuːvərəbl /; / məˈnjuːvərəbl̩ / (BrE)
Able to be easily moved or steered in different directions or positions.

Examples

A maneuverable wheelchair
A maneuverable wheelchair is essential for navigating crowded areas.
Thanks to its compact design, the drone is highly maneuverable indoors.
The new car is much more maneuverable in tight city streets than the old model.
A smaller car tends to be more manoeuvrable on narrow city streets.
Despite its size, the yacht remained remarkably manoeuvrable in the crowded harbor.
The new drone is highly manoeuvrable, allowing it to avoid obstacles easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agile - able to move quickly and easily
  • antonym: clumsy - awkward in movement, not easily controlled
  • antonym: unwieldy - difficult to move or control because of size or shape
