profeminist

noun
feminism supporter
7.5
Pronunciation: / proʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (AmE), / prəʊˈfɛmɪnɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates feminist ideas or principles, especially someone who is not a woman

Examples

A profeminist man
He identified as a profeminist and worked to promote gender equality in his community.
Many men consider themselves profeminist allies and support women's rights movements.
The organization welcomed anyone who was a profeminist regardless of their gender.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antifeminist - a person who opposes feminist ideas or gender equality
  • synonym: feminist - a person who supports women's rights and gender equality
