blackfly

noun
a dark aphid that infests crops
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblækˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈblækˌflʌɪ / (BrE)
a small, dark aphid that infests plants such as beans and sugar beets, often causing damage to crops

Examples

Bean blackfly
A severe outbreak of blackfly can significantly reduce crop yields.
Gardeners tried various methods to control the blackfly infestation on their sugar beets.
The bean plants were covered with blackfly by mid-summer, stunting their growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphid - a small sap-sucking insect
