mining

noun
obtaining cryptocurrency by computational validation
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.nɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of using computer hardware to perform calculations that validate transactions and add new blocks to a blockchain, often in order to earn cryptocurrency rewards.

Examples

Cryptocurrency mining
The startup's data center is dedicated to mining cryptocurrency for steady revenue.
She spent evenings mining Bitcoin on her laptop until the fans began to overheat.
Police seized several high-powered rigs used for illegal mining after complaints about a power outage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptocurrency mining - the process of earning digital currency by validating blockchain transactions and creating new blocks
  • synonym: proof-of-work - a consensus method in which miners perform computational work to validate transactions and secure a blockchain
