vacation package

noun
bundled travel offer
1.4
Pronunciation: / vəˈkeɪʃən ˌpækɪdʒ / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃn ˌpakɪdʒ / (BrE)
a set of travel services, such as transportation, accommodation, and sometimes meals or activities, sold together at a single price

Examples

A vacation package deal
Her parents surprised her with a luxury vacation package to the Caribbean.
Many travel agencies offer discounted vacation packages to popular destinations.
We booked a vacation package that included flights, hotel, and guided tours.

Related Terms

  • synonym: package tour - a tour in which travel, accommodation, and sometimes meals and sightseeing are arranged for one price
