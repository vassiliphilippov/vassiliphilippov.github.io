Synonyms

  • intertwine
    5.4 5/m
    intertwine means to wind two things together in a linked, tangled way, while interlace means to cross them so they overlap.
  • entwine
    5.4 2/m
    entwine means to twist things closely together, often in a natural or poetic image, while interlace focuses on crossing parts.
  • interweave
    6.3 0.6/m
    interweave means to blend different elements by weaving them together, often as a pattern, while interlace means their parts cross.
  • twine
    4.1 0.1/m
    twine means to twist strands or wrap one thing around another tightly, while interlace means making crossing patterns between parts.
  • weave
    3.5 6/m
    weave means to make fabric or join things into a whole by thread-like actions, while interlace means to cross parts tightly.
