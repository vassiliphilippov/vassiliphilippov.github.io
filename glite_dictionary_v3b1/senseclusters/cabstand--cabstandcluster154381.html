cabstand

noun
place where taxis wait for passengers
Pronunciation: / ˈkæb.stænd / (AmE), / ˈkab.stand / (BrE)
a place where taxis wait to be hired by passengers

Examples

Taxi cabstand
A group of drivers chatted at the cabstand while waiting for their next fare.
He owns three cabs that operate from the main cabstand in town.
We waited for a taxi at the cabstand outside the train station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taxi rank - place where taxis queue for customers (chiefly British)
