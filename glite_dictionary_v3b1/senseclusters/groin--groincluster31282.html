groin

verb
to hit in groin
8.4
Pronunciation: /grɔɪn/ (AmE), /grɔɪn/ (BrE)
to strike or injure someone in the groin area, usually accidentally or in sports (informal)

Examples

To groin a player

The referee stopped the fight after one boxer groined his opponent.
She apologized after she groined her friend while playing basketball.
