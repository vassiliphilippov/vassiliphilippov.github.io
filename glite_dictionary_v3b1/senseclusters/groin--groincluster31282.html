groin

verb
to hit in groin
8.4
Pronunciation: / ɡrɔɪn / (AmE), / ɡrɔɪn / (BrE)
to strike or injure someone in the groin area, usually accidentally or in sports (informal)

Examples

To groin a player
He was accidentally groined during the soccer match and had to leave the field.
The referee stopped the fight after one boxer groined his opponent.
She apologized after she groined her friend while playing basketball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kick - to strike with the foot
  • synonym: punch - to hit with a closed fist
