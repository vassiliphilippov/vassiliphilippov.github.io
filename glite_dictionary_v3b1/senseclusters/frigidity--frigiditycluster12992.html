frigidity

noun
lack of sexual responsiveness
Pronunciation: / frɪˈdʒɪd.ə.t̬i / (AmE), / frɪˈdʒɪd.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
(medical) Lack of sexual responsiveness, especially in women, often involving inability to experience orgasm during intercourse.

Examples

Sexual frigidity
Doctors now recognize that frigidity can have psychological as well as physical causes.
Some couples seek counseling to address frigidity and improve intimacy in their relationship.
The patient's sense of frigidity caused significant distress in her marriage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anorgasmia - inability to achieve orgasm
  • antonym: libido - sexual drive or desire
