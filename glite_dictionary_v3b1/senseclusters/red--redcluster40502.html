red

adjective
reddened (face or skin) by emotion or exertion
3.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /red/ (AmE), /red/ (BrE)
(especially of the face or skin) appearing reddened due to emotion, stress, or physical exertion.

Examples

Her cheeks turned red with embarrassment after she realized her mistake.
She was so angry that her face became visibly red.
