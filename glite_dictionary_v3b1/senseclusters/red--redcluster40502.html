red

adjective
reddened (face or skin) by emotion or exertion
Pronunciation: / rɛd / (AmE), / rɛd / (BrE)
(especially of the face or skin) appearing reddened due to emotion, stress, or physical exertion.

Examples

Red cheeks
He came in from the cold, his nose and ears red from the wind.
Her cheeks turned red with embarrassment after she realized her mistake.
She was so angry that her face became visibly red.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flushed - having a red or warm face, especially from emotion or exertion
  • antonym: pale - lacking color in the face, often from fear or illness
