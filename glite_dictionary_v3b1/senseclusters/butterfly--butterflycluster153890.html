butterfly

noun
diurnal insect with colorful wings
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌt̬ɚˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ / (BrE)
An insect typically active during the day, with a slender body, knobbed antennae, and broad, colorful wings.

Examples

A colorful butterfly
A butterfly flapped its wings gently before taking off toward the trees.
Children chased butterflies across the meadow on a sunny afternoon.
We watched a beautiful butterfly land on the sunflower in the garden.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moth - nocturnal insect related to butterflies, often dull-colored
