street corner

noun
intersection of two streets
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɹit ˈkɔɹnɚ /; / strit ˈkɔrnər / (AmE), / stɹiːt ˈkɔːnə /; / striːt ˈkɔːnə / (BrE)
the place where two streets meet or intersect, forming a corner

Examples

A musician was playing guitar at the street corner last night.
Children often gather near the street corner to wait for the school bus.
The bakery is right on the street corner, making it easy to find.
