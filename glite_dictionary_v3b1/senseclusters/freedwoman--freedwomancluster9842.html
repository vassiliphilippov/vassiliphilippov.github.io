freedwoman

noun
a woman released from slavery
Pronunciation: / ˈfridˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈfriːdˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who has been released from slavery.

Examples

A freedwoman after slavery
After the abolition of slavery, she became a respected freedwoman in her community.
Many freedwomen dedicated themselves to helping others escape bondage.
The life of a freedwoman in ancient Rome could still be challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freedman - a man who has been freed from slavery
  • antonym: slave - a person who is the legal property of another and is forced to obey them
