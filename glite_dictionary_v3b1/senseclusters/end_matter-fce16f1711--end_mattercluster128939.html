end matter

noun
material following main text in a book
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnd ˌmæt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛnd ˌmæt.ə / (BrE)
the written material found after the main text of a book, such as appendices, indexes, or bibliographies

Examples

The end matter section
Authors sometimes use the end matter to provide additional resources for readers.
Please check the end matter for helpful appendices and a glossary.
The end matter includes an index and a list of references.
