monsoon forest

noun
woodland in tropical monsoon climates
Pronunciation: / mɑnˈsun ˈfɔrɪst / (AmE), / mɒnˈsuːn ˈfɒrɪst / (BrE)
An open woodland found in tropical regions with a monsoon climate, characterized by distinct wet and dry seasons.

Examples

A monsoon forest area
Conservation efforts focus on protecting endangered trees in monsoon forests throughout Southeast Asia.
Many species in the monsoon forest lose their leaves during the dry season.
The explorers camped overnight in a monsoon forest near the river.
