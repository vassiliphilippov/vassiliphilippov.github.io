starward

adjective
toward the stars
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrwərd / (AmE), / ˈstɑːwəd / (BrE)
pointing or directed toward the stars or outer space

Examples

Starward path
The rocket launched on its starward journey, leaving a trail of fire behind.
He gazed with starward eyes, dreaming of exploring distant galaxies.
The observatory's starward telescope captured images of a newly discovered planet.
