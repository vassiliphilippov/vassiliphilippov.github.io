Hieracium

noun
genus of perennial herbs (hawkweeds)
Pronunciation: / haɪˈræsiəm / (AmE), / hɪəˈræsɪəm / (BrE)
a large genus of perennial hairy herbs, commonly known as hawkweeds, found in Europe, Asia, Africa, and North America
