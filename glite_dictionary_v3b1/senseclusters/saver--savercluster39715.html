saver

noun
person who rescues from danger
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈseɪvə / (BrE)
a person who rescues someone or something from danger or harm

Examples

A life saver
Firefighters are often seen as heroes and savers of lives in their communities.
She will always remember her dog as the saver who barked and warned her of the fire.
The lifeguard acted as a true saver when he pulled the child from the pool.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endangerer - a person who puts someone or something in danger
  • synonym: rescuer - a person who saves someone or something from danger or difficulty
  • synonym: savior - a person who saves someone or something, especially from danger
