non-automated

adjective
not automatic
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn ˈɔtəˌmeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌnɒn ˈɔːtəmeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not operated or controlled by machines or automatic systems; requiring manual operation or supervision

Examples

A non-automated system

Many small businesses prefer non-automated bookkeeping methods for greater control.
A non-automated process can be slower but allows for more flexibility and customization.
