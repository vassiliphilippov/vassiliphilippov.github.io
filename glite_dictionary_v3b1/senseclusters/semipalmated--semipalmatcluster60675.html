semipalmated

adjective
partly webbed toes
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈpælmətɪd / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈpɑːlmətɪd / (BrE)
having toes that are partially joined by a web, as in certain birds or animals

Examples

Semipalmated sandpiper
The semipalmated sandpiper migrates thousands of miles each year.
Some ducks have semipalmated feet, which help them swim but also walk on land.
Scientists observed that the chick's semipalmated toes made it easier to move through muddy areas.
