racketeer

verb
engage in illegal criminal business
Pronunciation: / ˌrækəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌrækəˈtɪə / (BrE)
to engage in illegal business activities, especially by extorting money or running fraudulent enterprises

Examples

To racketeer money
Authorities arrested several men suspected to racketeer in the area for years.
If you racketeer, you risk severe legal consequences and prison time.
The mob used threats and violence to racketeer money from local businesses.
