measured

adjective
deliberate and unhurried; careful
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛʒɚd / (AmE), / ˈmɛʒəd / (BrE)
(of actions, speech, or movement) done carefully, deliberately, and with restraint

Examples

Measured response
His measured response to the criticism earned him respect.
She spoke in a measured tone, choosing her words carefully.
The guards marched with a slow, measured pace down the hall.
He gave a measured speech, choosing his words with great care.
Her measured response helped to calm the heated debate.
The judge spoke in a measured tone, emphasizing fairness and reason.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deliberate - done consciously and intentionally; purposeful
  • antonym: hurried - done quickly or with haste, lacking care
  • antonym: reckless - without care or thought for consequences
