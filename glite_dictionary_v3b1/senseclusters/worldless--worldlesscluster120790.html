worldless

adjective
without a world
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɜrldləs / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldləs / (BrE)
lacking a world or not belonging to any world; existing outside of any known world or reality

Examples

Worldless wanderer
The novel explores the feelings of a worldless wanderer searching for meaning.
After the disaster, the survivors felt worldless and disconnected from everything they knew.
In her dreams, she drifted through a worldless space, free from time and place.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isolated - separated from others or alone
  • antonym: rooted - firmly established or connected to a place
