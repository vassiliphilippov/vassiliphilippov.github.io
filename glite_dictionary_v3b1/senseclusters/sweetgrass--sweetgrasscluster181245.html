sweetgrass

noun
fragrant ceremonial grass
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈswiːtˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈswiːtˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a fragrant grass often braided or burned in ceremonies, especially for its pleasant scent

Examples

She carefully braided strands of sweetgrass to give as gifts to her friends.
The scent of sweetgrass filled the room, creating a calm and peaceful atmosphere.
