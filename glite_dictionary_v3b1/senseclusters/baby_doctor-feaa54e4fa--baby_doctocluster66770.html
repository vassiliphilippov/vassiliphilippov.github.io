baby doctor

noun
doctor for babies; pediatrician (informal)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌdɑk.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈbeɪ.bi ˌdɒk.tə / (BrE)
an informal term for a doctor who specializes in the medical care of babies and young children; a pediatrician

Examples

Baby doctor check-up
After their son was born, they took him to the baby doctor for a check-up.
My sister wants to become a baby doctor when she grows up.
The baby doctor reassured the new parents about their child's health.
