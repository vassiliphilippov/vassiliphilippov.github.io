constituent

noun
person represented by an elected official
3.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈstɪtʃuənt / (AmE), / kənˈstɪtjʊənt / (BrE)
a person who lives in a constituency and is represented by an elected official, especially one who votes for that official

Examples

Local constituent votes
Every constituent has the right to voice opinions on government policies.
He regularly meets with constituents to discuss legislative priorities.
The senator listened to concerns raised by her constituents during the town hall meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: voter - a person who votes, especially in an election
