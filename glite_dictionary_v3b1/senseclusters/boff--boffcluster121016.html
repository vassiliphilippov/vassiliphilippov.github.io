boff

verb
to hit forcefully
Pronunciation: / bɑf / (AmE), / bɒf / (BrE)
to hit or strike someone or something forcefully, usually in a sudden or informal way

Examples

To boff his head
He accidentally boffed his thumb with the hammer while fixing the fence posts.
During the game, one player boffed the ball so hard it flew over the fence.
She boffed her brother on the arm in a playful way after he told a joke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: caress - to touch or stroke gently
  • synonym: punch - to hit someone or something with a closed fist
  • synonym: smack - to hit someone or something with a flat object or hand
