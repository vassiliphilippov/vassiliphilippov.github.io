property owner

noun
owner of virtual or digital property
3.3 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8pr\u0251\u02d0p\u025ati \u02cco\u028an\u025a / (AmE), / \u02c8pr\u0252p\u0259ti \u02cc\u0259\u028an\u0259 / (BrE)
a person who owns digital or virtual property (for example virtual land, in-game parcels, or NFT-based assets) and may have rights to use, sell, or rent those assets within a platform.

Examples

A property owner sells NFTs
In many metaverses, a property owner can monetize virtual land by renting parcels to other users.
When the platform changed its rules, several property owners lost access to their purchased NFTs.
A dispute arose after one property owner claimed the virtual building had been sold without consent.
