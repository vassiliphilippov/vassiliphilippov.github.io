lap

noun
an overlapping flap or layer
Pronunciation: / læp / (AmE), / læp / (BrE)
a part of a material or object that overlaps or lies over another part, especially in construction or sewing.

Examples

Shingle lap
Always make sure each shingle's lap is at least six inches for proper coverage.
She measured the lap of the metal sheets for a secure fit.
The lap of the roof tiles prevents water from seeping through the gaps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overlap - the extent to which one thing covers another
