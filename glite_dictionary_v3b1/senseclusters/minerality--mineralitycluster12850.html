minerality

noun
mineral-like taste
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪnəˈræləti / (AmE), / ˌmɪnəˈrælɪti / (BrE)
the quality in food or drink, especially wine, of tasting or smelling of minerals such as stone or earth

Examples

Minerality in wine
The minerality of this white wine gives it a crisp and refreshing finish.
Wine critics often praise the subtle minerality found in bottles from this region.
Some people prefer wines with more minerality and less sweetness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthiness - a taste or smell reminiscent of soil or earth
  • antonym: fruitiness - a taste or smell reminiscent of fruit
