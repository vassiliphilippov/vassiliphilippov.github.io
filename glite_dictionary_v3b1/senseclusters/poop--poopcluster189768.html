poop

verb
to be swamped by wave
9.9
Pronunciation: /puːp/ (AmE), /puːp/ (BrE)
to be overwhelmed or swamped at the stern of a ship by a wave, especially in nautical contexts

Examples

The old sailing ship nearly pooped when a sudden swell struck its stern.
If a vessel is pooped, it can quickly become dangerous for the crew.
