blessing

noun
formal approval or support
Pronunciation: / ˈblɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈblɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
formal approval or support for something, often given by a person in authority

Examples

With her blessing
She cannot proceed without her manager's blessing.
The government gave its blessing to the new initiative.
We received our parents' blessing before getting married.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approval - acceptance, agreement, or official permission
  • antonym: disapproval - an expression or feeling that something is wrong or unacceptable
  • synonym: permission - the action of allowing something to happen
