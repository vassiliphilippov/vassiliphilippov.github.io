army ant

noun
a nomadic predatory tropical ant
Pronunciation: / ˈɑr.mi ænt / (AmE), / ˈɑː.mi ænt / (BrE)
a tropical, nomadic ant that moves in large groups and preys mainly on other insects

Examples

Army ants in groups
Army ants are known for their aggressive behavior and nomadic lifestyle.
A swarm of army ants marched relentlessly across the forest floor.
Some birds follow army ants to feed on insects driven out by the ants.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ant - a small insect, usually living in colonies
  • synonym: driver ant - a type of army ant found in Africa
