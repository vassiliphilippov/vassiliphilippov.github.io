package tour

noun
holiday with transport and accommodation included
5.2 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpæk.ɪdʒ tʊr/ /; / /ˈpæk.ədʒ tʊr/ /; / /ˈpæk.ɪdʒ tuːr/ (AmE), /ˈpæk.ɪdʒ tʊə/ /; / /ˈpæk.ədʒ tʊə/ (BrE)
