data processor

noun
legal role processing personal data
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdeɪ.tə ˈprɑː.ses.ər/ /; / /ˈdæ.tə ˈprɑː.ses.ər/ (AmE), /ˈdeɪ.tə ˈprəʊ.ses.ə/ /; / /ˈdɑː.tə ˈprəʊ.ses.ə/ (BrE)
A person, company, or service that processes personal data on behalf of a data controller, under data-protection law (for example, the GDPR).

Examples

GDPR data processor
Under the GDPR, a data processor must follow the controller's instructions and protect personal data.
The vendor acted as a data processor when it stored customer records on behalf of the company.
Contracts with a data processor should specify security measures, permitted uses, and retention periods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: data controller - an entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data, distinct from a processor.
  • synonym: processor - shorthand for a data processor; an entity that processes personal or other data on behalf of another.
