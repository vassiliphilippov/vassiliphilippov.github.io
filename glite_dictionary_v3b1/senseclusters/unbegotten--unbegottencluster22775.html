unbegotten

adjective
not born or created
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnbɪˈɡɑːtən / (AmE), / ˌʌnbɪˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
not generated or born; existing without having been created or born by another.

Examples

An unbegotten being
The philosopher wrote about an unbegotten being that existed before all things.
In some religious texts, the supreme deity is described as unbegotten and without origin.
Legends speak of an unbegotten force that shaped the universe.

Related Terms

  • antonym: created - brought into existence by someone or something
  • synonym: eternal - existing forever without beginning or end
