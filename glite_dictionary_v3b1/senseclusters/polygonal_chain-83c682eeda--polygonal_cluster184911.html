polygonal chain

noun
connected sequence of line segments (geometry)
9.0
Pronunciation: / pəˈlɪgənl tʃeɪn / (AmE), / pəˈlɪɡənəl tʃeɪn / (BrE)
a connected sequence of straight line segments in geometry, where each segment joins two points and consecutive segments share an endpoint
