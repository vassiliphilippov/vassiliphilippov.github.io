ureteric

adjective
of the ureter
7.2
Pronunciation: / jʊˈriːtərɪk / (AmE), / jʊˈriːtərɪk / (BrE)
relating to the ureter, the tube that carries urine from the kidney to the bladder

Examples

Ureteric stone
The patient was admitted with a ureteric stone causing severe pain.
A ureteric injury can occur during abdominal surgery if care is not taken.
Doctors used imaging to locate the ureteric obstruction before treatment.
