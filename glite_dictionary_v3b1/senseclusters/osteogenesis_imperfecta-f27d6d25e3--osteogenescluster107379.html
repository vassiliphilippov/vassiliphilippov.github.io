osteogenesis imperfecta

noun
genetic disorder causing brittle bones
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˌdʒɛnəsɪs ɪmˈpɜrfɪktə / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəʊˌdʒɛnɪsɪs ɪmˈpɜːfɪktə / (BrE)
a genetic disorder of connective tissue causing brittle bones that fracture easily

Examples

Osteogenesis imperfecta disease
Children with osteogenesis imperfecta require special care to prevent bone fractures.
Her diagnosis of osteogenesis imperfecta explained the frequent breaks she suffered throughout her childhood.
Treatment options for osteogenesis imperfecta focus on managing symptoms and improving bone strength.
