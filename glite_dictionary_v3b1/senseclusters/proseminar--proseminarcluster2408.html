proseminar

noun
an advanced, discussion-based university course
Pronunciation: / proʊˈsɛm.ɪˌnɑr / (AmE), / prəʊˈsɛm.ɪ.nɑː / (BrE)
A university course similar to a seminar, usually involving advanced discussion and research, but often open to advanced undergraduates as well as graduate students.

Examples

A proseminar course
He enrolled in a proseminar on modern European history to prepare for graduate research.
Participation in the proseminar helped her develop her thesis proposal.
The department requires all students to complete a proseminar in their second year.
