downregulate

verb
to decrease activity
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌrɛɡjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌrɛɡjʊleɪt / (BrE)
to reduce or decrease the activity, amount, or expression of something, especially a gene or protein in biology

Examples

To downregulate gene expression
Certain drugs can downregulate the expression of specific genes.
The body may downregulate hormone production in response to high levels.
Researchers found that stress can downregulate immune system activity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suppress - to stop or reduce the activity of something
  • antonym: upregulate - to increase the activity or amount of something, especially in biology
