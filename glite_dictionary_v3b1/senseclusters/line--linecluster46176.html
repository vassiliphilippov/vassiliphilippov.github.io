line

noun
fold or crease on a surface, esp. skin
3.5 8.82/m
Pronunciation: / laÉªn / (AmE), / laÉªn / (BrE)
a slight depression or fold in the smoothness of a surface, typically one of the marks on the skin caused by age, expression, or movement

Examples

Smile lines
After years of smiling, he developed laughter lines around his eyes.
The old book's cover had deep lines and creases from use.
Using moisturizer daily can help reduce the appearance of fine lines on your face.
