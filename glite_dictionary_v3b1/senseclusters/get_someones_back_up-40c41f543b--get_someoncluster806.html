get someone's back up

verb
to annoy or irritate someone
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ˈsʌmwʌnz bæk ʌp /; / ɡɛt sʌmwʌnz bæk ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɛt ˈsʌmwʌnz bæk ʌp /; / ɡɛt sʌmwʌnz bæk ʌp / (BrE)
to make someone annoyed, angry, or defensive, especially because of criticism or provocation
