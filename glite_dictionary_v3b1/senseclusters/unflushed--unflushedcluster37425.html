unflushed

adjective
not flushed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈflʌʃt / (AmE), / ʌnˈflʌʃt / (BrE)
not having been flushed, especially referring to a toilet or similar fixture after use

Examples

Unflushed toilet
He was annoyed to find the toilet unflushed after someone had used it.
Please do not leave the restroom with the toilet unflushed.
The janitor checked each stall to make sure none were unflushed before closing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flushed - having been cleaned by flushing, especially a toilet
