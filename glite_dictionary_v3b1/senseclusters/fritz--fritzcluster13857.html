fritz

noun
not working
7.7
Pronunciation: /frɪts/ (AmE), /frɪts/ (BrE)
a state in which something is not working properly or is broken, especially in informal contexts

Examples

On the fritz
My laptop went on the fritz right before my big presentation.
The elevator is on the fritz again, so we have to use the stairs.
Whenever the air conditioner is on the fritz, the office gets unbearably hot.
