love handle

noun
excess fat at the waist
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌv ˌhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈlʌv ˌhændl / (BrE)
a small amount of excess fat at the sides of the waist, often visible over the top of trousers or skirts

Examples

Many people feel self-conscious about their love handles when wearing tight clothes.
She wore a loose shirt to hide her love handles at the beach.
