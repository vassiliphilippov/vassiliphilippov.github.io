transverse flute

noun
side-blown woodwind instrument (concert flute)
4.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /trænz.ˈvɜːr fluːt/ /; / /træns.ˈvɜːr fluːt/ (AmE), /træns.ˈvɜːs fluːt/ /; / /trænz.ˈvɜːs fluːt/ (BrE)
a side-blown woodwind instrument, held horizontally and played by blowing across a hole; commonly known as the Western concert flute
