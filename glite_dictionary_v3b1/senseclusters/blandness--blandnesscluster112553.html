blandness

noun
lack of strong taste or character
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈblænd.nəs / (AmE), / ˈblænd.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of lacking strong or interesting flavor, taste, or character

Examples

Blandness of food
Many critics noted the blandness of the restaurant's signature dish.
She complained about the blandness of the food served at the hospital.
The blandness of the soup left everyone unimpressed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flavor - distinctive taste of food or drink
  • synonym: insipidity - lack of flavor or interest
