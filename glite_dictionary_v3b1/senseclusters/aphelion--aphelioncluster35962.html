aphelion

noun
the farthest point from the sun in an orbit
Pronunciation: / əˈfiːliən / (AmE), / əˈfiːlɪən / (BrE)
the point in the orbit of a planet or comet where it is farthest from the sun

Examples

The aphelion point
A comet is often closest to the sun at perihelion and farthest away at aphelion.
Because of its aphelion, the planet receives less sunlight at that point in its orbit.
The Earth reaches its aphelion in early July each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perihelion - the point in the orbit nearest to the sun
