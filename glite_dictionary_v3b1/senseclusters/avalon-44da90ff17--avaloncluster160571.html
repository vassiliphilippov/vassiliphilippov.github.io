Avalon

proper noun
mythical island
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈævəˌlɑn / (AmE), / ˈævəlɒn / (BrE)
a mythical island from Arthurian legend, often described as a paradise or place of healing

Examples

Mythical Avalon
According to legend, King Arthur was taken to Avalon to recover from his wounds.
Many stories describe Avalon as a magical place hidden from the world.
Some believe that the sword Excalibur was forged on the island of Avalon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paradise - an ideal or perfect place of happiness
