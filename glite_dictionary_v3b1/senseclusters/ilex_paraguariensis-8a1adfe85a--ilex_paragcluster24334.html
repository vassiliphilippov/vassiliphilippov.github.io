Ilex paraguariensis

noun
South American holly used for mate
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪlɛks ˌpærəˌɡwɛriˈɛnsɪs / (AmE), / ˌaɪlɛks ˌpærəˌɡwɛəriˈɛnsɪs / (BrE)
