albeit

conjunction
although; even though
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɔlˈbiɪt / (AmE), / ɔːlˈbiːɪt / (BrE)
although; even though something is true, another fact or situation also exists.

Examples

Although albeit true
The meal was delicious, albeit a bit expensive for my budget.
He finished the race, albeit much later than he had hoped.
The new policy was accepted, albeit with some reservations from the staff.
