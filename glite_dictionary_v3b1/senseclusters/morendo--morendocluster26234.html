morendo

adverb
fading away musically
Pronunciation: / mɔˈrɛndoʊ / (AmE), / mɔˈrɛndəʊ / (BrE)
in music, gradually fading away in both sound and speed

Examples

To play morendo
The orchestra ended the symphony morendo, leaving the audience in a moment of silence.
She played the melody morendo, letting each note slowly fade into the air.
The choir sang the final phrase morendo, creating a haunting and delicate finish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: crescendo - gradually becoming louder in music
  • synonym: diminuendo - gradually becoming softer in music
