black and tan

noun
beer cocktail of stout and pale ale
Pronunciation: / blæk ənd tæn / (AmE), / blæk ənd tæn / (BrE)
a drink consisting of a mixture of dark stout and pale ale, typically poured so the two layers remain separate in the glass

Examples
