laryngectomize

verb
to remove larynx surgically
9.9
Pronunciation: /læ.rɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.maɪz/ /; / /lə.rɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.maɪz/ (AmE), /læ.rɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.maɪz/ /; / /lə.rɪn.ˈdʒek.tə.maɪz/ (BrE)
to surgically remove the larynx of a person, usually as a treatment for cancer.

Examples

To laryngectomize a patient
The doctors decided to laryngectomize the patient to prevent the spread of cancer.
After being laryngectomized, he learned to communicate using an electrolarynx.
Surgeons may need to laryngectomize when other treatments for throat cancer are not effective.
