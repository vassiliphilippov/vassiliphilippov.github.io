kleptocratic

adjective
corrupt government
Pronunciation: / ˌklɛptəˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌklɛptəˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
relating to a government or leader that uses power to steal money and resources from the people

Examples

kleptocratic government
The kleptocratic regime enriched itself while the citizens remained in poverty.
International organizations condemned the kleptocratic practices of the country's leaders.
A kleptocratic government often leads to widespread distrust among the population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrupt - dishonest or using power for personal gain
  • antonym: honest - acting with integrity and fairness
