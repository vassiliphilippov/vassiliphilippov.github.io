molding

noun
decorative strip for ornamentation or finishing
3.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊl.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈməʊl.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
a decorative strip or trim, usually of wood, plaster, or similar material, used for ornamentation or to cover transitions on walls, ceilings, or furniture
