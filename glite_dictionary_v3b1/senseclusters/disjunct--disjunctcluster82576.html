disjunct

adjective
separate; disconnected
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈdʒʌŋkt / (AmE), / dɪsˈdʒʌŋkt / (BrE)
separated or disconnected from something with which it is usually joined or associated.

Examples

Disjunct ideas
Her disjunct ideas made the essay seem fragmented and hard to follow.
In the painting, the shapes appear disjunct rather than forming a continuous pattern.
The two companies operated as disjunct entities with no shared resources.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connected - joined or linked together
  • synonym: distinct - recognizably different from something else
