dime bag

noun
packet of drugs sold for $10
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪm ˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪm ˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a packet of illegal drugs, especially marijuana, sold for ten dollars

Examples

A dime bag of drugs
He was arrested for carrying a dime bag in his backpack.
The teenager asked his friend if he could buy a dime bag from him.
They found a dime bag of marijuana hidden in the car.
