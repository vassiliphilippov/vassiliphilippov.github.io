unprotested

adjective
not opposed by protest
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnprəˈtɛstɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnprəˈtɛstɪd / (BrE)
not objected to or opposed by protest or complaint

Examples

Unprotested decision
The new regulations were implemented unprotested by the community.
His decision to leave the company went unprotested by his colleagues.
The changes to the schedule remained unprotested throughout the semester.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protested - objected to or opposed by protest
  • synonym: uncontested - not disputed or challenged
