unsmilingly

adverb
in an unsmiling manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈsmaɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no smile; without smiling or showing amusement.

Examples

He unsmilingly accepted
He unsmilingly accepted the award, showing little emotion.
She listened unsmilingly to his long explanation.
The teacher looked at the noisy class unsmilingly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seriously - in a serious manner, without humor or lightness
