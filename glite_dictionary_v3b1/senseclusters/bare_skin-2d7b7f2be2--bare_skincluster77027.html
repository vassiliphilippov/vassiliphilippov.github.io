bare skin

noun
uncovered, exposed skin
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɛr skɪn / (AmE), / beə skɪn / (BrE)
skin that is not covered by clothing or anything else; exposed skin.

Examples

Bare skin exposed
After swimming, she wrapped a towel around her bare skin to keep warm.
Direct sunlight on bare skin can lead to sunburn very quickly.
The artist carefully painted the details of the model’s bare skin.

Related Terms

  • antonym: covered - having a covering; not exposed
  • synonym: naked - having no covering; exposed, especially of the body
