intrapsychically

adverb
within the mind
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈsaɪkɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈsaɪkɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that takes place within an individual's own mind or psyche

Examples

Processed intrapsychically alone
The patient processed her anxiety intrapsychically before discussing it with anyone.
Some conflicts are managed intrapsychically and never become visible to others.
He dealt with the disappointment intrapsychically, showing no outward reaction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: internally - in a way that happens inside oneself
