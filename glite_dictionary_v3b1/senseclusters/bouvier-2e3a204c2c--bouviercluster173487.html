Bouvier

proper noun
dog breed for herding
Pronunciation: / buːˈvjeɪ / (AmE), / buːˈvjeɪ / (BrE)
a breed of large, strong dog originally developed for cattle herding and known for its loyalty and protective nature

Examples

Bouvier dog breed
The Bouvier is a loyal companion and an excellent guard dog for families.
Farmers often rely on their Bouvier to help herd cattle across the fields.
At the dog show, the judge praised the Bouvier for its strength and calm temperament.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collie - a breed of herding dog known for intelligence and agility
  • synonym: sheepdog - a dog bred or trained to herd sheep or cattle
