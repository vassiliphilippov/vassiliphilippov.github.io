misanthrope

noun
person who dislikes other people
7.5 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪs.ənˌθroʊp / (AmE), / ˈmɪs.ənˌθrəʊp / (BrE)
A person who dislikes or distrusts other people in general.

Examples

A misanthrope person
After years of betrayal, she became a true misanthrope, trusting no one.
Some critics declared the character a misanthrope because of his cynical views on humanity.
The misanthrope avoided all social gatherings, preferring the company of books to people.
