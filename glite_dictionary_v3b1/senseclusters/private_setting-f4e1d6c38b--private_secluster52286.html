private setting

noun
a place with privacy
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪvət ˈsɛtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪvət ˈsɛtɪŋ / (BrE)
a place or environment where one is not observed by others and has privacy

Examples

A private setting
Counseling sessions are often held in a private setting to protect confidentiality.
He prefers studying in a private setting where there are no distractions.
We discussed the matter in a private setting to avoid being overheard.
