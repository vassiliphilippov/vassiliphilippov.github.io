Tombigbee River

proper noun
river in Mississippi and Alabama, USA
Pronunciation: / tɒmˈbɪɡbi ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / tɒmˈbɪɡbi ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in the southeastern United States that rises in northeastern Mississippi, flows southward through western Alabama, and joins the Alabama River to form the Mobile River

Examples

The Tombigbee River
Heavy rainfall caused the Tombigbee River to overflow, flooding several nearby communities.
