Bible Belt

noun
region in the US known for religious conservatism
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪbəl bɛlt / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbəl bɛlt / (BrE)
an area of the southern and midwestern United States where Protestant evangelical and fundamentalist Christianity is especially strong and influential

Examples

The Bible Belt region
Many politicians modify their campaigns when speaking in the Bible Belt to appeal to local values.
She grew up in the Bible Belt, where religion played a central role in daily life.
The Bible Belt is known for its high concentration of church attendance and religious events.
