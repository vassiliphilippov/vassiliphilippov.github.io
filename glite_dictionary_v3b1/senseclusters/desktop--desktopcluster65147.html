desktop

noun
personal computer
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛskˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈdɛsktɒp / (BrE)
a personal computer designed to be used on a desk, typically larger and less portable than a laptop

Examples

A new desktop computer
He bought a new desktop for his graphic design projects at home.
Many offices still use desktops because they are easy to upgrade and repair.
Her old desktop finally stopped working after ten years of daily use.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laptop - a portable personal computer small enough to use on your lap
  • synonym: workstation - a powerful computer used for technical or scientific work
