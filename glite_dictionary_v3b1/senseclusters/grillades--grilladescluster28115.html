grillades

noun
Creole meat dish
Pronunciation: / ɡriˈjɑːdz / (AmE), / ɡriˈjɑːdz / (BrE)
a Creole dish of thinly sliced beef or pork, browned and simmered in a savory sauce, often served with grits

Examples

Creole grillades with grits
For Sunday brunch, we ordered grillades and grits at a famous New Orleans restaurant.
The chef prepared traditional grillades using thin slices of pork simmered in a spicy tomato sauce.
Many families in Louisiana enjoy grillades as a comforting breakfast or dinner dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gumbo - a thick Creole stew with meat or seafood and vegetables
  • synonym: étouffée - a Cajun and Creole dish of shellfish or chicken in a seasoned sauce
