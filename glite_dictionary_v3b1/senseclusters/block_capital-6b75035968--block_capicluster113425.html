block capital

noun
plain, unjoined capital letter
Pronunciation: / blɑk ˈkæpɪtəl / (AmE), / blɒk ˈkapɪtəl / (BrE)
a capital letter written in a plain, distinct, and unjoined style, typically used for clarity in forms or headings.

Examples

Block capital letters
He printed the important words in block capital so they would stand out.
Please write your name in block capital letters on the form.
The instructions say to fill out the address using block capital letters only.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lowercase letter - a small letter, as opposed to a capital letter
  • synonym: uppercase letter - a capital letter, as opposed to a lowercase letter
