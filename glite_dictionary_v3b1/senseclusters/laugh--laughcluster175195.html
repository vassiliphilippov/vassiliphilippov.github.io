laugh

noun
humorous remark or joke
Pronunciation: / læf / (AmE), / lɑːf / (BrE)
a humorous remark, story, or event that is intended to make people laugh; a joke or witty anecdote

Examples

A good laugh

Thanks for the laugh; I really needed that.
That story about his trip was an absolute laugh.
