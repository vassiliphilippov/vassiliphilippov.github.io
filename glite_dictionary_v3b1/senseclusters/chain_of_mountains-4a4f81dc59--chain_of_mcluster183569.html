chain of mountains

noun
a series of connected mountains
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪn əv ˈmaʊn.tənz / (AmE), / tʃeɪn əv ˈmaʊn.tɪnz / (BrE)
a series of mountains or hills connected in a line

Examples

A chain of mountains
A dense forest covered the entire chain of mountains along the horizon.
The Andes are the longest chain of mountains in the world.
The explorers crossed a rugged chain of mountains before reaching the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hill chain - a series of connected hills
  • synonym: mountain range - a line of mountains connected by high ground
