mistranslation

noun
an incorrect translation
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪstrænzˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪstrænzˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
An incorrect or inaccurate translation from one language into another.

Examples

A mistranslation error
Her speech was misunderstood due to a subtle mistranslation in the transcript.
One famous mistranslation led to a diplomatic misunderstanding.
The document contained a mistranslation that changed the original meaning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpretation - the oral rendering of spoken language from one language to another
  • antonym: translation - the process of rendering text from one language to another
