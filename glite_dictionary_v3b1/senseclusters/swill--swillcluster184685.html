swill

verb
to feed pigs with food waste
8.9
Pronunciation: / swɪl / (AmE), / swɪl / (BrE)
to feed pigs with liquid or semi-liquid food waste, often called swill

Examples

To swill the pigs
After collecting all the restaurant waste, he went out to swill the livestock.
Every morning, the farmer would swill the pigs in the trough behind the barn.
They used leftover kitchen scraps to swill the hungry sows.
