Rolls-Royce

noun
highest quality standard
Pronunciation: / ˌroʊlz ˈrɔɪs / (AmE), / ˌrəʊlz ˈrɔɪs / (BrE)
a person or thing considered to be the best or of the highest quality in its category

Examples

The Rolls-Royce of hotels
This hospital is considered the Rolls-Royce of medical centers in the region.
Their new smartphone is the Rolls-Royce of mobile technology, offering unmatched performance.
He wanted his company to be the Rolls-Royce of customer service in the industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benchmark - a standard by which others are measured
  • synonym: gold standard - the best or most reliable example of something
  • antonym: mediocrity - the quality of being average or not very good
