failure

noun
loss of normal functioning
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪljɚ / (AmE), / ˈfeɪliə / (BrE)
A loss of the ability to function normally, especially referring to organs, systems, or devices.

Examples

Failure of the engine
Engine failure forced the pilot to make an emergency landing.
He underwent dialysis due to chronic kidney failure.
The patient was diagnosed with acute failure of the liver.
