Phanariot

adjective
of the Phanariots
Pronunciation: / fəˈnæriət / (AmE), / fəˈnæriət / (BrE)
relating to the Phanariots, a prominent Greek community in the Ottoman Empire, or their culture and period of influence

Examples

Phanariot culture
The Phanariot families played a significant role in the administration of the Danubian Principalities.
Many Phanariot customs influenced the local traditions of Moldavia and Wallachia.
Historians debate the impact of Phanariot rule on regional reforms and education.
