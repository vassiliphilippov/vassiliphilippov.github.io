extremism

noun
advocacy of extreme views or actions
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstriːmɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪkˈstriːmɪzəm / (BrE)
the holding of extreme political or religious views; advocacy of immoderate, uncompromising policies or actions

Examples

Political extremism
His views were criticized for bordering on extremism rather than reasonable debate.
Schools are working to prevent the spread of extremism among young people.
The government's crackdown was a response to the rise in political extremism.

Related Terms

  • antonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes, especially in behavior or political views
  • synonym: radicalism - the beliefs or actions of people who advocate thorough or complete social or political reform
