verboten

adjective
prohibited; forbidden by rule or custom
Pronunciation: / vərˈboʊtən / (AmE), / vɜːˈbəʊtən / (BrE)
prohibited or forbidden by an authority or by social custom

Examples

Verboten during exams
Cell phones are verboten during examinations.
In some cultures, discussing money is considered verboten.
Smoking is strictly verboten in all areas of the building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forbidden - not allowed by law or custom
  • antonym: permitted - allowed or authorized
