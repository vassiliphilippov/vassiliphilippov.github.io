secretaire

noun
writing desk with drawers or compartments
5.0
Pronunciation: / sɛk.rəˈtɛr / (AmE), / ˌsɛkrɪˈtɛə / (BrE)
a desk, often with a hinged writing surface and drawers, used especially for writing and storing correspondence

Examples

A secretaire desk
A compact secretaire is ideal for small spaces where a traditional desk would be too large.
She kept her important letters and documents neatly organized in the drawers of her secretaire.
The antique shop displayed a beautiful secretaire with intricate woodwork and brass fittings.
