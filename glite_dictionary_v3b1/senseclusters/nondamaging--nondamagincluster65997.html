nondamaging

adjective
not causing harm
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdæmɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdæmɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
not causing harm, injury, or damage to something or someone

Examples

A nondamaging technique
The researchers developed a nondamaging technique to analyze the ancient artifact.
This cleaning product is completely nondamaging to delicate fabrics.
Doctors prefer nondamaging procedures when treating sensitive tissues.

Related Terms
