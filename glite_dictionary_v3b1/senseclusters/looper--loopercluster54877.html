looper

noun
music recording device
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈluːpər / (AmE), / ˈluːpə / (BrE)
an electronic device or software that records and plays back repeated sections of sound or music in real time

Examples

Guitar looper
The guitarist used a looper to layer different melodies during the performance.
With a looper, musicians can create complex tracks by recording multiple parts live.
She experimented with her new looper to build rhythmic backgrounds for her songs.

  • synonym: sequencer - a device or software that arranges and plays musical patterns
