Chinchilla laniger

noun
South American rodent bred for soft fur
Pronunciation: / tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə ləˈnɪdʒər / (AmE), / tʃɪnˈtʃɪlə ləˈnɪdʒə / (BrE)
A small South American rodent with extremely soft pearly grey fur, native to the Andes and widely bred in captivity for its fur.
