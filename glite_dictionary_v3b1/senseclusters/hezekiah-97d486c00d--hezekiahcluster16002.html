Hezekiah

proper noun
king of Judah in the Old Testament
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛzɪˈkaɪə / (AmE), / ˌhɛzɪˈkaɪə / (BrE)
(in the Bible) a king of Judah who ruled from 715 to 687 BC and is noted for abolishing idolatry.
