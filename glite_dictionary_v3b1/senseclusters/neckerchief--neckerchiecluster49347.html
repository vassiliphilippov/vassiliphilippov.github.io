neckerchief

noun
cloth worn around the neck
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛkɚˌtʃɪf / (AmE), / ˈnɛkəˌtʃɪf / (BrE)
A square of cloth worn around the neck, typically folded into a triangle, often used as part of a uniform or for decoration.

Examples

A neckerchief scarf
A blue neckerchief is standard in many scout uniforms.
He tied a bright red neckerchief around his neck as part of the costume.
She wore a silk neckerchief to add a splash of color to her outfit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scarf - a long piece of cloth worn around the neck for warmth or decoration
