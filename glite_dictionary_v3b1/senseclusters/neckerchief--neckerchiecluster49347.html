neckerchief

noun
cloth worn around the neck
2.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈnek.ər.tʃiːf/ /; / /ˈnek.ər.tʃɪf/ (AmE), /ˈnek.ə.tʃiːf/ /; / /ˈnek.ə.tʃɪf/ (BrE)
A square of cloth worn around the neck, typically folded into a triangle, often used as part of a uniform or for decoration.

Examples
