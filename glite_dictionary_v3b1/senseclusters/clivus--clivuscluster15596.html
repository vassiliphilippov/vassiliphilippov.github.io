clivus

noun
skull base slope
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪvəs / (AmE), / ˈklaɪvəs / (BrE)
a sloping part of the base of the skull, located between the sphenoid and occipital bones

Examples

Clivus of the skull
The neurosurgeon explained that the tumor was pressing against the clivus.
Damage to the clivus can affect important nerves at the base of the skull.
An MRI scan revealed an abnormality near the patient's clivus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skull base - the bottom part of the skull supporting the brain
