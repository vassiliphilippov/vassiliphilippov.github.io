Calochortus nuttallii

noun
a North American flowering plant, also known as sego lily
Pronunciation: / ˌkæləˈkɔrtəs nʌˈtælaɪ / (AmE), / ˌkæləˈkɔːtəs nʌˈtalɪaɪ / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the lily family with clusters of one to four showy white, bell-shaped flowers, native to western North America; its edible bulbs were historically used as food during scarcity
