family Mytilidae

noun
family of marine mussels
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli mɪˈtɪlɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli mɪˈtɪlɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of marine bivalve mollusks commonly known as mussels, found attached to rocks in seawater.

Many species within the family Mytilidae play an important role in marine ecosystems as filter feeders.
