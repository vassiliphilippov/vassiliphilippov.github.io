seed cleaner

noun
machine for cleaning seeds or grain
Pronunciation: / sid ˈklinər / (AmE), / siːd ˈkliːnə / (BrE)
a machine used to remove impurities and unwanted materials from seeds or grain before storage or sale

Examples

A seed cleaner machine
A modern seed cleaner can process large quantities of grain quickly and efficiently.
After running the wheat through the seed cleaner, most debris and broken seeds were removed.
The farmer invested in a new seed cleaner to improve the quality of his grain harvest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seed separator - a device for separating seeds from other plant material
