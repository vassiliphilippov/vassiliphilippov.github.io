pulsatory

adjective
rhythmic pulsing
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌlsəˌtɔri / (AmE), / ˈpʌlsətəri / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by rhythmic pulsing or beating

Examples

A pulsatory feeling
The nurse noticed a pulsatory sensation in the patient's wrist.
A pulsatory flow of blood could be felt beneath the skin.
The device measured the pulsatory movement of the artery during the examination.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steady - not changing or fluctuating; constant
