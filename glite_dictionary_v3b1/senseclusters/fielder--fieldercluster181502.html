fielder

noun
player who fields in baseball
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːldər / (AmE), / ˈfiːldə / (BrE)
a player in baseball or cricket whose job is to catch or stop the ball when the other team is batting

Examples

A fielder catches balls
During the inning, every fielder must stay alert for fly balls.
She is one of the best fielders on the team, always making incredible catches.
The coach positioned the fielder closer to second base for this batter.
Every fielder was alert as the batsman prepared to hit the ball.
The fielder made a brilliant catch near the boundary.
The captain positioned each fielder carefully before the next delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: batsman - a player who is batting in cricket
  • antonym: batter - a player hitting the ball in baseball
  • synonym: bowler - the player who delivers the ball in cricket
  • synonym: fielder - a player in cricket who fields the ball
