paleoclimatology

noun
study of past climates
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪlioʊˌklaɪməˈtɑlədʒi /; / ˌpeɪl.i.oʊˌklaɪ.məˈtɑː.lə.dʒi / (AmE), / ˌpeɪlɪəʊˌklaɪməˈtɒlədʒi /; / ˌpal.i.əʊˌklaɪ.məˈtɒl.ə.dʒi / (BrE)
The scientific study of climates in the geological past, as determined from geological, biological, and chemical evidence.
