sippy cup

noun
childish insult
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪp.i kʌp/ /; / /ˈsɪp.iː kʌp/ (AmE), /ˈsɪp.i kʌp/ /; / /ˈsɪp.iː kʌp/ (BrE)
an object given to or used by someone considered childish or unable to cope independently, often as a joke or insult.

Examples

Adult's sippy cup joke
When he started whining at work, his colleague jokingly handed him a sippy cup.
They teased her about her complaints by offering her a pink sippy cup during the meeting.
Calling him a child, they placed a sippy cup on his desk as a prank.
