discountable

adjective
able to be reduced
4.4
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈkaʊntəbl̩ / (AmE), / dɪsˈkaʊntəbl̩ / (BrE)
able to be reduced in price or regarded as less important or significant

Examples

Discountable items
Only discountable items are included in the special promotion this week.
The manager explained that clearance products are not discountable during the sale.
Some fees are discountable if you meet certain conditions or ask in advance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonnegotiable - not open to reduction or change
  • synonym: reducible - able to be made smaller or less in amount
