tonka

noun
aromatic seed
9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑːŋ.kə/ (AmE), /ˈtɒŋ.kə/ (BrE)
the seed of a South American tree used for its sweet, vanilla-like flavor or fragrance in cooking and perfumery

Examples

Grated tonka seed
The chef added a pinch of grated tonka to the dessert for a unique aroma.
Perfume makers often use tonka to create warm, sweet scents.
Some countries restrict the use of tonka in food due to its coumarin content.
