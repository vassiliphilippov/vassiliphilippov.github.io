Qatar Peninsula

proper noun
peninsula in Western Asia forming most of Qatar
Pronunciation: / kəˈtɑr pəˈnɪnsələ / (AmE), / ˈkætɑː pəˈnɪnsjʊlə / (BrE)
a peninsula in Western Asia extending northward from the Arabian mainland into the Persian Gulf, forming most of the territory of the State of Qatar

Examples

The Qatar Peninsula region
Archaeologists have discovered ancient settlements on the Qatar Peninsula.
The Qatar Peninsula projects into the Persian Gulf, providing the country with a long coastline.
The climate of the Qatar Peninsula is typically arid with high temperatures and little rainfall.
