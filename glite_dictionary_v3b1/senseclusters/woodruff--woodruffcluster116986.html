woodruff

noun
fragrant perennial plant used in flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌrʌf / (AmE), / ˈwʊd.rʌf / (BrE)
a fragrant woodland plant with small white flowers, especially sweet woodruff (Galium) or related species, often used in flavoring or as ground cover
