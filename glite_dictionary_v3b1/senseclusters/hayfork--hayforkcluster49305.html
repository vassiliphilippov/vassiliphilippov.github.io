hayfork

noun
long-handled fork for hay
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪ.fɔrk / (AmE), / ˈheɪ.fɔːk / (BrE)
A long-handled fork with two or more prongs, used for turning or lifting hay.

Examples

A hayfork tool
He handed me a hayfork and showed me how to turn the drying hay.
She leaned her hayfork against the barn after a morning of work.
The farmer lifted a pile of straw using his hayfork.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pitchfork - a long-handled fork for lifting and pitching hay
