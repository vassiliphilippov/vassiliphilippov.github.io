Araneae

noun
the order of arachnids including all true spiders
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈreɪni.i / (AmE), / əˈreɪnɪ.iː / (BrE)
The order of arachnids that comprises all true spiders, characterized by having eight legs and the ability to spin silk.

Studies of Araneae help researchers understand the evolution of web-building behavior among spiders.
