pandit

noun
expert or authority
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌndɪt / (AmE), / ˈpʌndɪt / (BrE)
an expert or authority on a particular subject, especially one who gives opinions publicly

Examples

Political pandit
The political pandit offered his analysis of the election results on television.
Many people rely on a technology pandit to explain the latest gadgets and trends.
She became known as a financial pandit after years of writing insightful articles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authority - a person whose opinions or knowledge are respected in a particular area
  • synonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a field
  • antonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field
