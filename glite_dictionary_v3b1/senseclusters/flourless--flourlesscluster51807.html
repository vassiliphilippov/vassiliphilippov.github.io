flourless

adjective
without flour
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊərləs / (AmE), / ˈflaʊələs / (BrE)
made without any flour as an ingredient

Examples

Flourless chocolate cake
She baked a delicious flourless chocolate cake for dessert.
Many people with gluten intolerance prefer flourless recipes for their meals.
The restaurant offers a popular flourless almond torte on its dessert menu.

Related Terms

  • antonym: floury - containing or covered with flour
  • synonym: gluten-free - not containing gluten, often made without wheat flour
