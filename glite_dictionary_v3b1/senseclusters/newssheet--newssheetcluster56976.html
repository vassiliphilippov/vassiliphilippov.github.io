newssheet

noun
printed news publication for a group
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnuʃit / (AmE), / ˈnjuːʃiːt / (BrE)
A printed sheet or publication, often produced for a specific group, providing informal or confidential news of interest to its readers.

Examples

Company newssheet
Employees eagerly awaited the newssheet for updates on company developments.
He contributed a regular column to the community newssheet focused on local history.
The club distributes a newssheet every month to keep members informed of upcoming events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: newsletter - a printed or electronic report with news or information, sent regularly to people with interest in a subject
