aye aye, sir

interjection
expression of acknowledgment and readiness (naval)
6.4 7.2
Pronunciation: / aɪ aɪ sɝ / (AmE), / aɪ aɪ sə / (BrE)
a phrase used, especially by sailors, to indicate that an order has been received and will be carried out promptly

Examples

When the captain gave his instructions, the sailor responded with a crisp aye aye, sir.
Whenever orders were issued on deck, the crew would promptly shout aye aye, sir.
