chap

noun
crack in skin, especially from cold weather
Pronunciation: / tʃæp / (AmE), / tʃap / (BrE)
A crack or split in the skin, especially on the lips or hands, typically caused by cold or dry weather.

Examples

A painful chap
After a week of hiking in the mountains, he noticed a chap forming on his hands.
She touched the chap on her lip, wincing as it stung from the cold air.
Wearing lip balm can help prevent your lips from developing a painful chap in winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fissure - a long, narrow crack or opening
