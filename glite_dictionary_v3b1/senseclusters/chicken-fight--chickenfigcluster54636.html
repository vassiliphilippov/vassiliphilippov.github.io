chicken-fight

verb
to fight while sitting on someone's shoulders
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkən faɪt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪkɪn faɪt / (BrE)
to engage in a game or contest in which two people sit on others' shoulders and try to knock each other off

Examples

To chicken-fight in pool
The kids love to chicken-fight in the swimming pool every summer.
They began chicken-fighting in the shallow end, laughing and splashing everywhere.
We used to chicken-fight at recess until the teacher stopped us.
