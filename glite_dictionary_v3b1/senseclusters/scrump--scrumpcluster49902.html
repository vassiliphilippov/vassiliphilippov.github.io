scrump

noun
stealing fruit
9.9
Pronunciation: / skrʌmp / (AmE), / skrʌmp / (BrE)
the act of stealing fruit, especially from an orchard or garden, usually as a prank or for fun

Examples

A midnight scrump
The children planned a midnight scrump in the neighbor's apple orchard.
After their daring scrump, they shared the apples behind the old barn.
He was caught during a scrump and had to apologize to the farmer.

  • synonym: theft - the act of stealing something
