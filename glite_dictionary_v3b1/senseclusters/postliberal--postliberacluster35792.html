postliberal

adjective
beyond liberal principles
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
describing ideas, policies, or attitudes that move beyond or reject traditional liberal principles

Examples

Postliberal ideas
The postliberal approach seeks to balance individual rights with a stronger sense of community.
Some critics argue that postliberal policies may limit personal freedoms in favor of social cohesion.
The party's new platform reflects a distinctly postliberal vision for the country's future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liberal - favoring individual rights, democracy, and free markets
