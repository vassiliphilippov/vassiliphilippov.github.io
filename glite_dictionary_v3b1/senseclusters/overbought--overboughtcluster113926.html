overbought

adjective
excessively purchased
6.7 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Àåo äv…ôrÀàb…ît / (AmE), / Àå…ô äv…ôÀàb…îÀêt / (BrE)
purchased in quantities greater than needed, resulting in a surplus of goods or stock.

Examples

Overbought inventory
The store was left with overbought inventory after the holiday season ended.
Because they had overbought supplies, many items had to be discounted.
The manager regretted the overbought stock that filled the warehouse.
