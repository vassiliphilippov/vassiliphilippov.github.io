flower bud

noun
bud that develops into a flower
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ ˌbʌd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ˌbʌd / (BrE)
a bud on a plant from which only a flower or flowers will develop, not leaves or shoots

Examples

A flower bud on a tree
In early spring, the magnolia tree is covered with large, fuzzy flower buds.
She carefully examined each flower bud for signs of disease.
The rose flower bud opened slowly, revealing its vibrant petals.
