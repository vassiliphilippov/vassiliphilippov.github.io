flower bud

noun
bud that develops into a flower
Pronunciation: / ˈflaʊ.ɚ ˌbʌd / (AmE), / ˈflaʊ.ə ˌbʌd / (BrE)
a bud on a plant from which only a flower or flowers will develop, not leaves or shoots

Examples
