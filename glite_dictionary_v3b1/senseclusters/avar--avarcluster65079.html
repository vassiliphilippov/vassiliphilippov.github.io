avar

noun
Eurasian nomadic people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːvɑr / (AmE), / ˈɑːvɑː / (BrE)
a member of the Eurasian Avars, a nomadic people who established a state in Central and Eastern Europe during the early Middle Ages

Examples

Legends about the avar horsemen have been passed down for generations in the region.
