wincey

noun
a warm wool-cotton fabric
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnsi / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsi / (BrE)
a plain or twilled fabric made of wool and cotton, used especially for making warm shirts, skirts, and pajamas

Examples

Soft wincey fabric
Her pajamas were made of soft, comfortable wincey.
Historically, wincey was a popular choice for school uniforms in colder regions.
The tailor recommended wincey for making a durable winter dress.
