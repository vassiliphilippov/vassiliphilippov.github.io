Rocky Mountain goat

noun
mountain-dwelling goat of North America
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑki ˈmaʊntən ɡoʊt / (AmE), / ˌrɒki ˈmaʊntən ɡəʊt / (BrE)
a large, white, sure-footed mammal native to the mountains of northwestern North America, known for its agility on rocky slopes

Examples

The Rocky Mountain goat climbs
The Rocky Mountain goat is well adapted to life on steep, rugged cliffs.
Tourists are always thrilled to see a Rocky Mountain goat climbing effortlessly among the rocks.
We spotted a Rocky Mountain goat grazing near the alpine meadow.
