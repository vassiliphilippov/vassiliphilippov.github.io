dead person

noun
someone who has died
Pronunciation: / dɛd ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / dɛd ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
a person who is no longer alive; a human who has died

Examples

The police needed to identify the dead person before contacting the family.
The will of the dead person was read aloud to the relatives.
