larid

noun
bird of the gull family
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛrɪd / (AmE), / ˈlærɪd / (BrE)
a long-winged, web-footed aquatic bird belonging to the gull family (Laridae)

Examples

A larid colony

The coastline is home to several species of larid that can be seen soaring overhead.
Unlike some other seabirds, larids are known for their adaptability to urban environments.
