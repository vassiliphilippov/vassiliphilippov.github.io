limewood

noun
wood from linden tree
5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈlaɪm.wʊd/ (AmE), /ˈlaɪm.wʊd/ (BrE)
the pale, soft wood from a lime (linden) tree, often used for carving and fine woodworking or sculpture
