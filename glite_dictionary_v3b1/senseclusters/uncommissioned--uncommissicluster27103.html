uncommissioned

adjective
not officially operational
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈmɪʃənd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈmɪʃənd / (BrE)
not yet officially put into service or operation, especially referring to equipment, buildings, or systems

Examples

Uncommissioned building
The hospital's new wing remained uncommissioned due to delays in equipment delivery.
Several uncommissioned ships were docked at the harbor, waiting for their first assignments.
The power plant was still uncommissioned after months of construction and testing.

  • synonym: inactive - not currently in use or operation
