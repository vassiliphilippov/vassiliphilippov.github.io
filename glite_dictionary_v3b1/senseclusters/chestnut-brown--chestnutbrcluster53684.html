chestnut-brown

adjective
brown tinged with reddish (chestnut)
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛst.nət ˈbraʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛs.nʌt ˈbraʊn / (BrE)
of a brown color tinged with a reddish hue, like that of a chestnut; especially used to describe hair or feathers.

Examples

Chestnut-brown hair
His chestnut-brown coat made the horse stand out among the others.
She dyed her hair a beautiful chestnut-brown before the wedding.
The bird's feathers shimmered with a chestnut-brown hue in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auburn - reddish-brown in color, especially of hair
