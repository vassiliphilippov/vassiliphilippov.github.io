Malcolmia

noun
genus of coastal flowering plants
Pronunciation: / mælˈkoʊlmiə / (AmE), / mælˈkɒlmiə / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants typically found in coastal habitats, native to regions from the Mediterranean to Afghanistan.
