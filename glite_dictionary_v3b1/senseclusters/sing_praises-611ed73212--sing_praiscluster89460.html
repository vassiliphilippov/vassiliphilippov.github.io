sing praises

verb
express strong admiration or approval
6.2
Pronunciation: / sɪŋ ˈpreɪzɪz / (AmE), / sɪŋ ˈpreɪzɪz / (BrE)
to express enthusiastic admiration or approval, often in a public or celebratory manner

Examples

To sing praises loudly
After the project succeeded, the manager could not stop singing praises of her dedicated team.
She always sings praises of her children when speaking to friends.
The audience sang praises of the performer's incredible talent after the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: praise - to express approval or admiration for someone or something
