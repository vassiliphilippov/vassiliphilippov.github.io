Synonyms

  • sanctify
    7.4 2.25/m
    sanctify is to make something holy by a blessing or rite, while consecrate is a formal ceremony to declare it sacred.
  • hallow
    8.9 1.95/m
    hallow is used to make a place or person holy in religious speech, while consecrate is a formal act by clergy.
  • dedicate
    5.3 3.57/m
    dedicate means to set something apart often at a public ceremony, while consecrate emphasizes making it sacred by religious rites.
  • sacralize
    9.9 0.14/m
    sacralize describes treating something as sacred or giving it religious value, while consecrate refers to an official sacred ceremony.
