assay

noun
analysis to determine content or quality
Pronunciation: / ˈæˌseɪ / (AmE), / ˈæseɪ / (BrE)
a test or analysis to determine the content or quality of a substance, especially in scientific or technical contexts

Examples

Chemical assay
Before proceeding, they requested an assay of the newly synthesized compound.
The laboratory performed an assay to detect the presence of toxins in the sample.
The quality control department conducts an assay on every product batch.
A gold assay is required to determine the purity of the sample.
She published results from an assay that measures the presence of specific antibodies.
The laboratory performed an assay to detect traces of lead in the water.
