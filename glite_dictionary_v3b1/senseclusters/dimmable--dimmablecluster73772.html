dimmable

adjective
adjustable brightness
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪməbəl / (AmE), / ˈdɪməbəl / (BrE)
able to have its brightness adjusted, especially by a dimmer switch or control

Examples

Dimmable light bulb
This new LED bulb is dimmable, so you can set the perfect light level for any occasion.
Make sure the light fixture is dimmable before connecting it to a dimmer switch.
The dimmable lamps in the living room create a cozy atmosphere in the evenings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjustable - able to be changed or modified to fit different needs
  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or adjusted
