adenosine deaminase

noun
enzyme that deaminates adenosine
8.4
Pronunciation: / əˌdiːnoʊˌsiːn diːˈæmɪˌneɪs / (AmE), / əˌdiːnəʊˌsiːn diːˈæmɪˌneɪs / (BrE)
an enzyme found in mammals that catalyzes the removal of an amino group from adenosine, producing inosine and ammonia
