shibuichi

noun
grey metal patina
Pronunciation: / ʃiːˈbuːiːtʃi / (AmE), / ʃɪˈbuːɪtʃi / (BrE)
a dull grey or greenish-grey color or patina on metal, resembling the finish of the Japanese alloy shibuichi

Examples

Shibuichi patina
The antique sword displayed a beautiful shibuichi that highlighted its age and craftsmanship.
Collectors admire the subtle shibuichi that develops on traditional Japanese metalwork.
Over time, the bronze statue acquired a soft shibuichi that enhanced its appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tarnish - a dullness or discoloration on metal surfaces
