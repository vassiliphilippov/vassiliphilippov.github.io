viability

noun
ability to work successfully; feasibility
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvaɪ.əˈbɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌvaɪ.əˈbɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
the ability to work successfully or to be done in a practical and useful way; feasibility

Examples

Viability of the plan
Due to technical limitations, the viability of the plan was questioned.
The viability of the project depends on securing adequate funding.
They conducted a study to determine the viability of the proposed method.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feasibility - the state or degree of being easily or conveniently done
  • antonym: impracticality - the quality of not being workable or possible
