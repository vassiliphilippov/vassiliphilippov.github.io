winter squash plant

noun
plant producing hard-rinded winter squashes
9.9 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.tər skwɑːʃ plænt/ /; / /ˈwɪn.ər skwɑːʃ plænt/ /; / /ˈwɪn.tər skwɔːʃ plænt/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.tə skwɒʃ plɑːnt/ (BrE)
any of various plants of the species Cucurbita maxima and Cucurbita moschata that produce squashes with hard rinds, harvested in autumn
