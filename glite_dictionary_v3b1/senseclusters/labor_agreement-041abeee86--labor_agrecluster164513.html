labor agreement

noun
contract on wages and work conditions

Examples

Synonyms

  • labor contract
    2.8 0.28/m
    labor contract is a formal agreement between workers and employers that sets wages and conditions, while labor agreement is a broader term for such contracts.
  • collective agreement
    3.5 0.11/m
    collective agreement is a formal contract made with a group of employees represented by a union, while labor agreement is a general contract between workers and management.
