muskox

noun
Arctic mammal
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌskˌɑks / (AmE), / ˈmʌskˌɒks / (BrE)
a large, shaggy Arctic mammal with curved horns, known for its thick coat and ability to survive cold climates

Examples

A muskox herd
A herd of muskoxen moved slowly across the snowy tundra in search of food.
The thick fur of the muskox helps it survive the freezing Arctic temperatures.
Scientists observed several muskoxen grazing together near the icy river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bison - a large, shaggy-haired wild ox found in North America and Europe
  • synonym: reindeer - a large deer found in Arctic and subarctic regions
