snow-white

adjective
pure white in color
3.9
Pronunciation: / snoʊ waɪt / (AmE), / snəʊ waɪt / (BrE)
pure white in color, like freshly fallen snow

Examples

Her dress was snow-white and sparkled in the sunlight.
The snow-white sheets smelled fresh and clean.
