Synonyms

    ravening means extremely hungry and often fierce or predatory, while ravenous simply means very hungry for food.
  • wolfish
    7.2 0.01/m
    wolfish means greedy and animal-like in appetite, while ravenous focuses on strong hunger without animal image.
  • voracious
    7.5 1.64/m
    voracious means eager and sometimes unending in appetite for food or ideas, while ravenous refers mainly to strong physical hunger.
  • edacious
    9.9 0.01/m
    edacious means eating large amounts and is a rare word, while ravenous is common and describes urgent hunger.
  • starved
    2.1 0.01/m
    starved means suffering from long lack of food and weak body, while ravenous means intensely hungry in the moment.
  • rapacious
    8.9 0.04/m

  • esurient
