supercell

noun
rotating thunderstorm
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsɛl / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəsɛl / (BrE)
a large, powerful thunderstorm with a rotating updraft that can produce severe weather such as tornadoes, hail, and heavy rain

Examples

A supercell storm
A powerful supercell developed over the plains, bringing the threat of tornadoes and large hail.
Meteorologists warned that the approaching supercell could cause flash flooding in the region.
The storm chasers followed the supercell as it moved across the countryside, hoping to observe a tornado.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: tornado - a violent, rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground
