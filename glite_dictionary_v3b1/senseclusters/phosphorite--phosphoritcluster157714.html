phosphorite

noun
phosphate-rich rock
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑsfəˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɒsfəˌraɪt / (BrE)
a sedimentary rock containing a high concentration of phosphate minerals, used as a source of phosphorus

Examples

A phosphorite rock
Farmers rely on phosphorite as a key ingredient in many fertilizers.
The geologist collected samples of phosphorite from the ancient seabed.
Large deposits of phosphorite are found in several countries around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phosphate rock - rock containing significant amounts of phosphate minerals
