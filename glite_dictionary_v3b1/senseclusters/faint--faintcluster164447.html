faint

adjective
close to losing consciousness
Pronunciation: / feɪnt / (AmE), / feɪnt / (BrE)
(of a person) feeling weak and close to losing consciousness.

Examples

Feeling faint
If you feel faint, try to put your head between your knees.
She suddenly felt faint after standing up too quickly.
The heat made him so faint that he had to sit down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dizzy - feeling as if everything is spinning or moving around you
  • synonym: light-headed - feeling dizzy and slightly faint
