Coriandrum sativum

noun
herb; coriander; cilantro
5.2
Pronunciation: / kɔːriˈændrəm səˈtɪvəm / (AmE), / ˌkɒr.iˈæn.drəm səˈtɪv.əm / (BrE)
An Old World herb with aromatic leaves and seeds, widely used in cooking; also known as coriander or cilantro.

The chef sprinkled fresh leaves of Coriandrum sativum over the spicy curry just before serving.
