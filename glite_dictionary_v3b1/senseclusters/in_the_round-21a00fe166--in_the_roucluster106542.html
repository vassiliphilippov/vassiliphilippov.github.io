in the round

adverb
arranged or performed in a circle; all-sided participation
7.4
Pronunciation: / ɪn ðə raʊnd / (AmE), / ɪn ðə raʊnd / (BrE)
in a format in which participants are arranged in a circle, or in which everyone is able to participate or observe equally from all sides

Examples

Seated in the round
By arranging the chairs in the round, the workshop fostered open and lively debate.
The council held their discussions in the round to ensure everyone's voice was heard.
The play was performed in the round, with the audience seated on all sides of the stage.
