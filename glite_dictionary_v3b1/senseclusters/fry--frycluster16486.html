fry

verb
cook in hot fat or oil
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /fraɪ/ (AmE), /fraɪ/ (BrE)
to cook food in hot oil or fat, usually in a pan

Examples

She likes to fry eggs for breakfast every morning.
When you fry onions, they become sweet and golden brown.
