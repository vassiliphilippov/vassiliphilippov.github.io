coolly

adverb
in a calm or unconcerned manner
6.2 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkuː.liː/ /; / /ˈkuːl.iː/ (AmE), /ˈkuː.liː/ /; / /ˈkuːl.iː/ (BrE)
In a way that shows calmness or a lack of concern, especially in situations that might cause agitation or emotion.

Examples

To answer coolly
Despite the chaos around him, he answered the reporter’s questions coolly.
He handled the unexpected criticism coolly and did not show any emotion.
She walked into the meeting coolly, showing no sign of nervousness.
