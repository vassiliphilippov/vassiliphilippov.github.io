paraxial

adjective
near main axis
9.9
Pronunciation: / pæˈræksiəl / (AmE), / pæˈræksiəl / (BrE)
situated close to or alongside the main axis of an object, especially in scientific contexts

Examples

Paraxial rays
In optical systems, only paraxial rays are considered for simple calculations.
The scientist measured the angle of the paraxial beam relative to the main axis.
The paraxial approximation simplifies the analysis of light passing through a lens.
