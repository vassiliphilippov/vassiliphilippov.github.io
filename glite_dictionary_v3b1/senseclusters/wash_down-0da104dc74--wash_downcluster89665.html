wash down

verb
swallow food with drink; soothe with drink
4.4 0.75/m
Pronunciation: / wÉ‘Êƒ daÊŠn / (AmE), / wÉ’Êƒ daÊŠn / (BrE)
To eat food and immediately swallow it with a drink; also, to metaphorically soothe or lessen something with a drink.

Examples

To wash down food with water
After delivering the bad news, she washed down her worries with a cup of hot tea.
He needed a drink to wash down his dry sandwich at lunch.
She always tries to wash down spicy food with a cold glass of milk.

Synonyms

  • get down
    4.4 0.34/m

  • drown
    6.5 0.81/m
    drown is using something, often alcohol, to overwhelm a feeling, while wash down suggests easing or lessening feelings with a drink.
