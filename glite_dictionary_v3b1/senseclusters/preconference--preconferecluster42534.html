preconference

adjective
before a conference
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkɑːnfərəns / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkɒnfərəns / (BrE)
happening or arranged before a conference, especially to prepare for the main event

Examples

Preconference planning session
The organizers held a preconference meeting to finalize the schedule.
All participants received preconference materials to help them prepare for the sessions.
A preconference workshop introduced the main topics to new attendees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postconference - happening after a conference
  • synonym: preliminary - coming before the main part or event
