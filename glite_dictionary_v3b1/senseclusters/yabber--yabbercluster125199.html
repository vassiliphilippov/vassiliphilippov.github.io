yabber

noun
idle chatter
9.5
Pronunciation: /ˈjæb.ər/ (AmE), /ˈjæb.ə/ (BrE)
informal talk or chatter, especially when it is idle or meaningless

Examples

Loud yabber in the room
The room was filled with the sound of yabber as everyone waited for the meeting to start.
He ignored the constant yabber from the next table and focused on his book.
After hours of yabber, they finally got down to business.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatter - quick, informal, and often meaningless talk
  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound or talk
