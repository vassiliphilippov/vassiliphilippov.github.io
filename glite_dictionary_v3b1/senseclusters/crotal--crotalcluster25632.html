crotal

noun
lichen used for dyes
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkroʊ.təl / (AmE), / ˈkrəʊ.təl / (BrE)
any of several lichens of the genus Parmelia, used especially for making reddish-brown or purple dyes

Examples

A crotal lichen
Dye made from crotal was prized in Scottish textiles for its rich color.
She collected crotal from the rocks to use in natural dyeing.
The traditional weavers used crotal to create deep purple hues in their fabrics.
