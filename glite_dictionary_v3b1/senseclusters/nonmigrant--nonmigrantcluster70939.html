nonmigrant

noun
person who stays
6.5
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈmaɪ.grənt/ /; / /nə.ˈmaɪ.grənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈmaɪ.grənt/ /; / /nə.ˈmaɪ.grənt/ (BrE)
a person or animal that does not migrate and remains in the same place rather than moving to another region or country

Examples

A nonmigrant resident
Many nonmigrants in the region have lived in the same town their entire lives.
Unlike migratory birds, nonmigrants stay in their habitat year-round.
The government offered support to nonmigrants who chose not to relocate after the disaster.

Related Terms

  • antonym: migrant - a person or animal that moves from one place to another
  • synonym: resident - a person who lives in a particular place
