snappily

adverb
in a curt manner
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsnæpɪli / (AmE), / ˈsnæpɪli / (BrE)
in a sharp, curt, or irritable manner; brusquely

Examples

Answered snappily
He replied snappily when asked about his plans for the weekend.
The manager spoke snappily to the staff after the mistake was discovered.
She snappily dismissed the question and continued with her work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brusquely - in a blunt or abrupt manner
  • antonym: politely - in a courteous or respectful manner
