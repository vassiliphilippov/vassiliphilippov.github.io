spongelike

adjective
resembling a sponge in texture or compressibility
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈspʌndʒ.laɪk /; / ˈspʌndʒˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈspʌn.dʒlaɪk /; / ˈspʌndʒˌlaɪk / (BrE)
having a soft, porous texture or able to absorb and release liquids, like a sponge
