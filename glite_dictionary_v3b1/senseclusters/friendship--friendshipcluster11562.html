friendship

noun
close relationship between friends
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈfrɛn(d)ʃɪp / (BrE)
A close and supportive relationship between two or more people who enjoy each other's company and care for one another.

Examples

A strong friendship
A good friendship is built on trust and understanding.
She treasures the friendship she shares with her childhood neighbor.
Their friendship grew stronger after they traveled together.

Related Terms

  • synonym: companionship - the state of spending time with someone in a friendly way
  • antonym: enmity - the state or feeling of being actively opposed or hostile to someone
