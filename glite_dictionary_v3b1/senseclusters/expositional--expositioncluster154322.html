expositional

adjective
relating to explanation
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the explanation or presentation of information or ideas

Examples

Expositional writing
The teacher used an expositional style to make the lesson clear to everyone.
Many documentaries rely on expositional narration to provide background information.
The film's expositional scenes helped the audience understand the main character's past.

Related Terms

  • synonym: descriptive - giving details about how something looks, feels, or works
  • synonym: explanatory - serving to explain something
