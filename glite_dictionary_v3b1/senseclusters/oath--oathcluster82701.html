oath

noun
profane or obscene expression
9.8
Pronunciation: / oʊθ / (AmE), / əʊθ / (BrE)
a profane or obscene word or expression, typically used to express strong emotion such as surprise or anger

Examples

Shout an oath
His angry oath echoed through the empty hallway.
She muttered an oath under her breath as the door slammed shut.
Unable to control himself, he let out a loud oath when he hit his thumb with the hammer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curse - a profane or obscene word, especially as an expression of anger
  • synonym: expletive - an oath or swear word expressing strong emotion
