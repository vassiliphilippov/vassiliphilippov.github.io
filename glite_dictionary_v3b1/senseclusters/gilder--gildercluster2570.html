gilder

noun
person who applies gold overlay
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪldɚ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪldə / (BrE)
a person whose occupation is to apply a thin layer of gold or a gold-like substance to a surface

Examples

A gilder applies gold
In the 18th century, a gilder was a highly respected craftsman.
She apprenticed with a master gilder to learn the art of decorative gilding.
The gilder carefully applied gold leaf to the picture frame.
