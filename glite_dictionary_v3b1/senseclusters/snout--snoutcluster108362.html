snout

verb
to root with snout
8.1
Pronunciation: /snaʊt/ (AmE), /snaʊt/ (BrE)
to search for food or objects by nosing or rooting with the snout, like a pig.

Examples

To snout for food
The pig snouted through the garden until it found the fallen apples.
Wild boars often snout in the forest soil looking for roots and insects.
The animal snouted around the base of the tree, searching for hidden food.
