headwaiter

noun
chief waiter in a restaurant
5.2 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhed.weɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈhed.weɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a person who supervises the other waiters in a restaurant and is responsible for seating customers

Examples

The headwaiter supervises staff

The headwaiter greeted us at the door and showed us to our table.
When the restaurant grew busy, the headwaiter coordinated the staff efficiently.
