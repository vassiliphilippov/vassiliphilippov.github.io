headwaiter

noun
chief waiter in a restaurant
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛdˈweɪtɚ / (AmE), / ˌhedˈweɪtə / (BrE)
a person who supervises the other waiters in a restaurant and is responsible for seating customers

Examples

The headwaiter supervises staff
She asked the headwaiter if they could accommodate an additional guest.
The headwaiter greeted us at the door and showed us to our table.
When the restaurant grew busy, the headwaiter coordinated the staff efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maître d'hôtel - the person in charge of a dining-room; synonym of headwaiter
  • degree lower: waiter - a person who serves food and drink in a restaurant
