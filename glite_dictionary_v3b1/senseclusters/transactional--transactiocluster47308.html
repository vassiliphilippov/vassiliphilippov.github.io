transactional

adjective
relating to transactions
5.4
Pronunciation: /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən.əl/ (AmE), /træn.ˈzæk.ʃən.əl/ (BrE)
relating to a process, system, or operation that is carried out as a transaction, especially in computing or databases.

Examples

Transactional database system
The new software provides transactional support to ensure data integrity during updates.
A transactional system can roll back changes if an error occurs during processing.
Many banking applications rely on transactional databases to handle large numbers of secure operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atomic - relating to operations that are indivisible and complete fully or not at all
  • antonym: nontransactional - not involving or supporting transactions
