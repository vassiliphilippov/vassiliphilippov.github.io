hotbed

noun
place where rapid development occurs (often negative)
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈhɒtbɛd / (BrE)
a place or environment where something, especially something undesirable, develops quickly and strongly

Examples

Hotbed of crime
That nightclub was once a hotbed of criminal activity.
The internet quickly became a hotbed for rumors and conspiracy theories.
This area is considered a hotbed of innovation in the tech industry.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeding ground - a place where something, especially something bad, develops or is fostered
