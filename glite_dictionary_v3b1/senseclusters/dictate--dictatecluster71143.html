Examples

To dictate the rules
For decades, the king dictated every aspect of daily life in his nation.
She fears her new boss will try to dictate the team's every move.
The general attempted to dictate the country's laws without consulting anyone.

  • instruct
    2.8 14/m

  • enjoin
    8.6 1.67/m

  • decree
    5.3 3.14/m
    decree is an official public order from a ruler or court, while dictate is the general act of ordering or controlling.
  • prescribe
    4.2 8.98/m
    prescribe is to state official rules or medical directions for action, while dictate is to give orders or to control people.
  • mandate
    4.9 16/m
    mandate is to officially require or give authority for an action, while dictate means to order directly or control people.
  • rule
    1.0 26/m
    rule is to govern or hold power over a country or group, while dictate stresses giving orders or commanding others.
  • rule over
    4.2 1.44/m

  • govern
