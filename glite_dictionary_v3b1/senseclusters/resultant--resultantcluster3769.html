Synonyms

  • consequent
    6.1 0.3/m
    consequent is a direct effect that follows from an action, while resultant is a general outcome that comes after something.
  • attendant
    6.2 0.3/m
    attendant often describes things that happen in connection with an event, while resultant focuses on what happens as a direct effect.
  • accompanying
    3.4 1/m
    accompanying means occurring together or alongside an event, while resultant highlights what happens because of that event.
