resultant

adjective

occurring as a result or consequence of something; following as an effect.

Examples

Resultant effect
Costs were reduced, and the resultant savings helped the company survive.
Pollution was an unfortunate resultant effect of rapid industrial expansion.
The resultant damage from the storm was much worse than expected.

Synonyms

  • consequent
    6.1 2.61/m
    consequent is a direct effect that follows from an action, while resultant is a general outcome that comes after something.
  • attendant
    6.2 1.38/m
    attendant often describes things that happen in connection with an event, while resultant focuses on what happens as a direct effect.
  • accompanying
    3.4 4.27/m
    accompanying means occurring together or alongside an event, while resultant highlights what happens because of that event.
