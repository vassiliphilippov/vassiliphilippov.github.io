audibleness

noun
quality of being audible
Pronunciation: / ˈɔdəbəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪb(ə)lnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being able to be heard; audibility.

Examples

The audibleness of sound
During the storm, the audibleness of the siren was crucial for public safety.
Engineers tested the audibleness of the alarm across different rooms in the building.
The audibleness of her whisper was barely enough to catch his attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audibility - the quality of being heard or perceivable by the ear
  • antonym: inaudibility - the quality of being impossible to hear
