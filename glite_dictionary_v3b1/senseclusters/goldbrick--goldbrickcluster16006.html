goldbrick

noun
a fake brick-shaped gold bar
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbrɪk / (BrE)
a brick-shaped object that resembles a gold bar but is not made of gold; a fake gold bar

Examples

Fake goldbrick
Collectors must be careful not to mistake a goldbrick for the real thing.
He bought a goldbrick as a prop for his amateur film about bank robbers.
The supposedly valuable goldbrick turned out to be worthless when it was examined closely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterfeit - something made in exact imitation of something valuable with the intention to deceive
  • synonym: fake - something made to look real in order to deceive
