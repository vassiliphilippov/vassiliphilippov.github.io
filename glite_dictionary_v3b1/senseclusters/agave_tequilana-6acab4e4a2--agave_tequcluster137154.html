Agave tequilana

noun
Mexican plant used to make tequila
Pronunciation: / əˈɡɑːveɪ tɛˌkiːˈlɑːnə / (AmE), / əˈɡɑːvi tɛˌkiːˈlɑːnə / (BrE)
A species of agave native to Mexico, cultivated primarily for the production of tequila.
