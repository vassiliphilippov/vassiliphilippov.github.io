arrester hook

noun
hook to slow landing aircraft on carrier
7.9
Pronunciation: / əˈrɛstɚ hʊk / (AmE), / əˈrɛstə hʊk / (BrE)
a hinged device on an aircraft that catches a wire on an aircraft carrier deck to rapidly slow and stop the plane during landing

Examples

The arrester hook on aircraft
Engineers inspected the arrester hook after each landing to ensure it remained in good condition.
The fighter jet deployed its arrester hook as it touched down on the carrier deck.
Without a functioning arrester hook, landing on an aircraft carrier would be extremely dangerous.
