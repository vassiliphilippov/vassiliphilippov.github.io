treadling

noun
operating a treadle
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtrɛdlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of operating a treadle, especially on a spinning wheel or sewing machine, by moving it with the foot

Examples

Smooth treadling
Her smooth treadling kept the sewing machine running at a steady pace.
Learning proper treadling is important for anyone using a traditional spinning wheel.
He demonstrated treadling techniques to the class during the workshop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pedaling - the act of operating a pedal with the foot
