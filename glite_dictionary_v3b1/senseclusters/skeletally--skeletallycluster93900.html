skeletally

adverb
in a skeleton-like way
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛlətəli / (AmE), / ˈskɛlɪtəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to or resembles a skeleton, especially being extremely thin or bony

Examples

Skeletally thin person
After months of poor nutrition, he looked skeletally thin and frail.
The stray dog wandered the streets skeletally gaunt, searching for food.
She moved skeletally across the stage, her bones visible beneath her skin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emaciatedly - in an extremely thin or wasted manner
