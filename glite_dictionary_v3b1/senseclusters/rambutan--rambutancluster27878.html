rambutan

noun
a tropical tree or its hairy red fruit
6.0
Pronunciation: / ræmˈbuːtən / (AmE), / ræmˈbuːtən / (BrE)
A tropical tree native to Southeast Asia that produces a bright red, hairy fruit with sweet, juicy flesh.

Examples

Rambutan tree
Peeling a rambutan reveals a translucent, sweet interior similar to lychee.
The rambutan tree thrives in warm, humid climates and produces fruit twice a year.
We bought fresh rambutan at the market during our trip to Malaysia.
