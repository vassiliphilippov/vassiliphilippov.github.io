stinginess

noun
unwillingness to be generous
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪndʒi.nəs / (AmE), / ˈstɪndʒɪnɪs / (BrE)
an unwillingness to give or spend; lack of generosity

Examples

His stinginess with money
Despite his wealth, his stinginess prevented him from donating to charity.
Her stinginess was well known among her friends, who never expected her to pick up the bill.
The company's stinginess with employee benefits led to low morale.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - the quality of being generous; willingness to give or share
  • synonym: miserliness - extreme stinginess or reluctance to spend money
