unnavigated

adjective
lacking direction
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnævɪˌɡeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈnævɪɡeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not guided, directed, or previously traveled or explored; lacking navigation or clear paths

Examples

An unnavigated forest

The explorers ventured into unnavigated territory, hoping to discover new resources.
Much of the deep ocean remains unnavigated and mysterious to scientists.
They faced many dangers as they crossed unnavigated rivers and dense forests.
