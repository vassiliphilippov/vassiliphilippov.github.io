weigh the costs against the benefits

verb
evaluate disadvantages and advantages
1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪ ðə kɑsts əˈgɛnst ðə ˈbɛnəfɪts / (AmE), / weɪ ðə kɒsts əˈɡɛnst ðə ˈbɛnɪfɪts / (BrE)
