Synonyms

  • maize
    1.9 3.38/m
    maize is the standard term for the plant and grain in many countries, while corn is the everyday English name.
  • Zea mays
    5.6 0.01/m
    Zea mays is the scientific Latin name used by biologists and on seed packets, while corn is the common food and feed name.
