corn

noun
a major cereal crop; maize
Pronunciation: / kɔrn / (AmE), / kɔːn / (BrE)
a tall cereal plant (maize) and its seeds or products, especially as food or animal feed

Examples

Sweet corn on the cob
Farmers planted corn across hundreds of acres this spring.
Many traditional Mexican dishes use corn as a main ingredient.
The corn stood tall and golden in the late summer sun.
Farmers harvested the corn and sold it at the local market.
She added butter and salt to her bowl of steaming corn.
We served fresh corn on the cob at the barbecue.
Bags of corn are shipped to the mill every week.
The farmer stored the corn in silos to use as animal feed during the winter.
They ground the corn into meal to make traditional bread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maize - the cereal plant known in American English as 'corn'
