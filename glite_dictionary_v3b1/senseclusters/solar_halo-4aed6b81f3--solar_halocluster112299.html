solar halo

noun
bright ring around the sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊlər ˈheɪloʊ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊlə ˈheɪləʊ / (BrE)
A luminous ring or halo appearing in the sky parallel to the horizon at the altitude of the sun, caused by the refraction of sunlight through ice crystals in the atmosphere.

Examples

A solar halo ring
After the storm, a beautiful solar halo could be seen encircling the midday sun.
Photographers gathered early in the morning to capture the appearance of a solar halo.
The rare solar halo created a stunning circle of light above the snowy landscape.
