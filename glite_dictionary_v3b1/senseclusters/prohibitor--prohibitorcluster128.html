prohibitor

noun
one who forbids
Pronunciation: / proʊˈhɪbɪtər / (AmE), / prəˈhɪbɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that prevents or forbids something from happening

Examples

A prohibitor of smoking
The new law acts as a prohibitor of smoking in public places.
A strong sense of ethics can be a powerful prohibitor against dishonest behavior.
The fence serves as a prohibitor to keep animals out of the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: preventer - a person or thing that stops something from happening
