breve

noun
U-shaped diacritical mark for a short vowel
9.1 8.5
Pronunciation: / bɹiv /; / briːv / (AmE), / ˈbriːv /; / briːv / (BrE)
a mark (˘) placed over a vowel to show it is pronounced with a short sound
