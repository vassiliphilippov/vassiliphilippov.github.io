breve

noun
U-shaped diacritical mark for a short vowel
Pronunciation: / bɹiv /; / briːv / (AmE), / ˈbriːv /; / briːv / (BrE)
a mark (˘) placed over a vowel to show it is pronounced with a short sound

Examples

Breve over a vowel
A breve over the letter helps learners distinguish between long and short vowel sounds.
The word 'cup' contains a short 'u', shown with a breve in phonetic notation.
When teaching phonics, the teacher wrote a breve over each short vowel in the words.
In the word 'cup', the breve above the 'u' shows it is a short vowel.
The choir held the breve for its full value during the performance.
A breve is often used in dictionaries to help with pronunciation.
