coonskin

noun
raccoon fur cap with tail hanging down
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkunˌskɪn / (AmE), / ˈkuːnskɪn / (BrE)
a cap made from raccoon fur, typically with the animal's tail hanging down the back, traditionally worn in North America

Examples

A coonskin cap
He wore a coonskin cap just like the pioneers in old movies.
The museum displayed an authentic coonskin from the early 19th century.
Tourists often buy a coonskin cap as a souvenir from their trip to Tennessee.
