zingerone

noun
ginger flavor compound
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈzɪŋɡəˌroʊn / (AmE), / ˈzɪŋɡərəʊn / (BrE)
a chemical compound found in ginger that gives it a spicy aroma and flavor, and is sometimes used as a flavoring or fragrance in foods, perfumes, and cosmetics
