testimonial

noun
a tribute or statement of esteem
1.7 3.7
Pronunciation: /tɛs.tɪ.ˈmoʊ.niː.əl/ /; / /tɛs.tɪ.ˈmoʊ.nɪ.əl/ (AmE), /tɛs.tɪ.ˈməʊ.nɪ.əl/ (BrE)
a statement, event, or object that expresses appreciation, praise, or serves as evidence of someone's character, ability, or achievement
