northward

noun
direction toward the North (0 or 360° compass point)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔrθ.wɚd / (AmE), / ˈnɔːθ.wəd / (BrE)
the direction or point on the compass corresponding to 0 or 360 degrees; the direction towards the North

Examples

A cold northward
A cold wind was blowing from the northward as the sun began to set.
Navigators used the northward as their primary bearing during the early voyage.
The expedition set a course due northward from the base camp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: north - the direction corresponding to 0 or 360 degrees on a compass
  • antonym: southward - direction toward the South (180° compass point)
