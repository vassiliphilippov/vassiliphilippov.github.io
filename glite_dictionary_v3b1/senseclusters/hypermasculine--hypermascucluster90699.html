hypermasculine

adjective
exaggerated male traits
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈmæskjəlɪn / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈmæskjʊlɪn / (BrE)

Examples

Hypermasculine style

Critics argue that hypermasculine behavior can discourage emotional expression in boys.
Some sports teams promote a hypermasculine culture that values aggression over teamwork.
