premeasure

noun
advance measurement
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːˌmɛʒər / (AmE), / ˈpriːˌmɛʒə / (BrE)
a measurement or specific amount that is taken or prepared in advance before something is needed or used

Examples

The recipe calls for a premeasure of sugar to be added at the start.
