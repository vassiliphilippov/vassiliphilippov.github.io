calorically

adverb
relating to heat
8.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɔrɪkli / (AmE), / kəˈlɒrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to heat or thermal energy, especially in physics or engineering contexts

Examples

Calorically active metal
The metal responded calorically to the rapid temperature changes during the test.
Some materials behave calorically like insulators, resisting the flow of heat.
Engineers analyzed how the device performed calorically under different operating conditions.
