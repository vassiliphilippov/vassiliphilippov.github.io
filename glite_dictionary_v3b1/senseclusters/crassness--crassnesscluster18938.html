crassness

noun
lack of refinement or sensitivity
Pronunciation: / ˈkræs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkrɑːs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of being insensitive, lacking refinement, or showing no understanding of what is proper or appropriate.

Examples

The crassness of words
Her crassness at the dinner table shocked the guests.
People were offended by the crassness of his remarks.
The movie's crassness made it difficult to enjoy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insensitivity - lack of sensitivity to the feelings of others
  • antonym: refinement - elegance, sophistication, or cultured taste
