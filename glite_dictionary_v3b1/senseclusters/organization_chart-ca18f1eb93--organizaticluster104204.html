organization chart

noun
diagram showing a company's structure
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən tʃɑːrt / (AmE), / ˌɔːɡənaɪˈzeɪʃən tʃɑːt / (BrE)
a diagram showing the structure of an organization and the relationships and ranks of its parts and positions

Examples

An organization chart diagram
Before the merger, both firms compared their organization charts to identify overlapping roles.
Each department head appears on the organization chart just below the CEO.
The company's organization chart was updated to reflect recent changes in management.
