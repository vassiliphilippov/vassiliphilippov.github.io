biplane flying fish

noun
flying fish with enlarged pectoral and pelvic fins
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbaɪ.pleɪn ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ fɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈbaɪ.pleɪn ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ fɪʃ/ (BrE)
A flying fish with both pectoral and pelvic fins enlarged, allowing it to glide long distances above water.
