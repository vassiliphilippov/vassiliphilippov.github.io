miliary

adjective
small seed-like spots
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪliˌɛri / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪəri / (BrE)
having many small spots or nodules, especially resembling millet seeds, often used in medical contexts

Examples

Miliary pattern on X-ray
The doctor identified a miliary pattern on the patient's chest X-ray.
A miliary rash appeared, covering the skin with tiny red spots.
Miliary tuberculosis is named for its miliary appearance in lung tissue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granular - consisting of small grains or particles
