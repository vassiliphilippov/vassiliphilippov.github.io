trio

noun
musical work for three performers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtri.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈtriː.əʊ / (BrE)
A musical composition written for three performers or instruments.

Examples

A piano trio
Each member of the jazz trio brought a unique style to the performance.
She composed a piano trio for her graduation recital.
The string trio performed an enchanting piece by Beethoven at the concert.
