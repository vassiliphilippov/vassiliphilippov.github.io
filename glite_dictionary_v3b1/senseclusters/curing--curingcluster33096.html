curing

noun
hardening of a material by drying or cooling
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkjʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process by which a substance becomes hard or solid through cooling, drying, or crystallization

Examples

Curing of glue
After curing, the adhesive bond was strong enough to hold the parts together.
Proper ventilation is important during the curing of epoxy resin.
The curing of the concrete took several days before it achieved full strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardening - the process of becoming hard or harder
