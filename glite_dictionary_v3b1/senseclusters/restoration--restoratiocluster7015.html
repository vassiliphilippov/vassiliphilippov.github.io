restoration

noun
returning something to its former state or owner
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: /res.tə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /res.tə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act or process of returning something to its original or former state

Examples

After years of legal battles, the restoration of property to its original owner was finally completed.
