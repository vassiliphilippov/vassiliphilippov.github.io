lookee

interjection
attention exclamation
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊki / (AmE), / ˈlʊki / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to draw attention to something or someone, similar to 'look' or 'see'

Examples

Lookee here
Well, lookee — the missing keys were under the sofa all along.
Lookee here, I found the document you were searching for.
If you check the results, lookee, your name is at the top of the list.

Related Terms

  • synonym: see - an exclamation to encourage someone to notice something
