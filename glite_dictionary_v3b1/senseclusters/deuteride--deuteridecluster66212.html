deuteride

noun
compound with deuterium
Pronunciation: / ˈduːtɚˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˈdjuːtəˌraɪd / (BrE)
a chemical compound in which deuterium atoms replace hydrogen atoms

Examples

Lithium deuteride compound
Lithium deuteride is used as a fusion fuel in certain nuclear reactions.
Researchers compared the properties of sodium hydride and sodium deuteride in their experiments.
The laboratory synthesized a new deuteride compound for testing its stability.
