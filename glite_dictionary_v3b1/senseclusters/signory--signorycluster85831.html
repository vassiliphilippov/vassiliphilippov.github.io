signory

noun
territory of a feudal lord
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪnəri / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡnəri / (BrE)
An estate or domain owned by a seigneur; the territory or authority of a feudal lord.

Examples

The signory estate
After the king's decree, the family's signory was divided among his three sons.
Many disputes arose over the boundaries of each signory during the medieval era.
The powerful duke ruled over a vast signory in the northern provinces.
