swaraj

noun
Indian self-rule
9.9
Pronunciation: / swəˈrɑːdʒ / (AmE), / swəˈrɑːdʒ / (BrE)
political self-rule or self-government, especially as advocated in India during the independence movement

Examples

Swaraj movement
The call for swaraj united people from different regions of India.
Leaders like Gandhi promoted swaraj as a path to national freedom.
Many believed that true swaraj meant not just political, but also economic independence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colonialism - control or governing influence of a nation over a dependent country
  • synonym: independence - freedom from control by another country or authority
