Titoist

noun
supporter of Tito
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtoʊɪst / (AmE), / ˈtiːtəʊɪst / (BrE)
a supporter of the political policies or ideology of Josip Broz Tito, especially in opposition to Soviet influence

Examples

A Titoist leader
As a dedicated Titoist, he promoted Yugoslav independence from Soviet control.
The conference brought together many former Titoists to discuss the legacy of Yugoslavia.
During the Cold War, being a Titoist often meant rejecting Moscow's authority.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Stalinist - a supporter of the policies of Joseph Stalin
