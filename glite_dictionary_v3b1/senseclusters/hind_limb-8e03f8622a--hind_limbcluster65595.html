hind limb

noun
back leg or appendage of animal
3.7
Pronunciation: / haɪnd lɪm / (AmE), / haɪnd lɪm / (BrE)
Either of the back legs or corresponding appendages of an animal.

Examples

Strong hind limbs
A frog uses its powerful hind limbs to leap long distances.
Some lizards can run on their hind limbs for short periods.
The skeleton showed that the dinosaur's hind limbs were much larger than its forelimbs.
