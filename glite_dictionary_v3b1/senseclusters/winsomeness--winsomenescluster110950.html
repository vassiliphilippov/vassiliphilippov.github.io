winsomeness

noun
charming or appealing quality
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnsəmnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪnsəmnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or charming in a fresh, innocent, or endearing way

Examples

Her winsomeness captivated everyone at the party immediately.
Many people are drawn to the winsomeness of young children.
