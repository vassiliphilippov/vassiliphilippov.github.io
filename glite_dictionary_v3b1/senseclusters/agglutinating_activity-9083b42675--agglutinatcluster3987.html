agglutinating activity

noun
process of clumping suspended particles
9.0 8.9
Pronunciation: / əˌɡluːtəˌneɪtɪŋ ækˈtɪvəti / (AmE), / əˌɡluːtəˌneɪtɪŋ ækˈtɪvɪti / (BrE)
the process by which small suspended particles join together to form larger clumps, often leading to precipitation from solution
