punctuation mark

noun
symbol for separating and clarifying written text
Pronunciation: / ˌpʌŋktʃuˈeɪʃən ˌmɑrk / (AmE), / ˌpʌŋktjʊˈeɪʃən ˌmɑːk / (BrE)
a written symbol, such as a period, comma, or question mark, used in text to separate sentences and clarify meaning

Examples

A punctuation mark
A question mark is the punctuation mark used to indicate a direct question.
Be sure to use the correct punctuation mark at the end of each sentence.
The teacher circled every missing punctuation mark in my essay.
