hoarsen

verb
to make rough-sounding
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrsən / (AmE), / ˈhɔːsən / (BrE)
to make or become rough or harsh in sound, especially referring to a voice

Examples

To hoarsen voice
After cheering at the game, his voice began to hoarsen and sound strained.
Cold weather can hoarsen your voice if you do not stay hydrated.
Her voice hoarsened from speaking loudly all day at the conference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: roughen - to make something rougher in texture or sound
  • antonym: soften - to make something softer or less harsh
