sun heater

noun
solar-powered heater
3.9
Pronunciation: /sʌn ˈhiː.tər/ /; / /sʌn ˈhɪ.tər/ (AmE), /sʌn ˈhiː.tə/ (BrE)
A heater that uses solar energy (sunlight) to produce heat, often as part of a solar-thermal system for space or water heating.

Examples

Solar sun heater
They installed a sun heater on the roof to provide hot water for the family.
Rural schools saved energy and cut bills by using a sun heater for classroom heating.
The report compared solar panel types and recommended a larger sun heater for colder climates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: solar heater - a device that uses solar energy to heat water or air
  • synonym: solar water heater - a system that collects sunlight to heat water for domestic use
