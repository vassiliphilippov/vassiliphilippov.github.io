half-consciousness

noun
partial state of awareness
1.0
Pronunciation: / hæfˈkɑnʃəsnəs / (AmE), / hɑːfˈkɒnʃəsnəs / (BrE)
a state of being only partially aware of or sensitive to one's surroundings or sensations

Examples

In half-consciousness state
After the accident, he drifted in and out of half-consciousness for hours at the hospital.
He recalled the event only dimly, wrapped in a blurry half-consciousness that made everything seem unreal.
The medication left her in a state of half-consciousness, unable to respond clearly to questions.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: consciousness - the state of being aware of and able to think and perceive
  • degree lower: unconsciousness - the state of not being aware of the surroundings or oneself
