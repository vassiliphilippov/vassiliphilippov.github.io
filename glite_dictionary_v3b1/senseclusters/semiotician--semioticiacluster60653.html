semiotician

noun
specialist in semiotics
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmi.oʊˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛmɪəˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
an expert or specialist in semiotics, the study of signs and symbols and their use or interpretation

Examples

Semiotician studies signs
As a semiotician, she analyzes language, images, and cultural symbols in media.
Many semioticians believe that understanding signs helps explain how societies create and share knowledge.
The renowned semiotician gave a lecture on how advertisements use visual cues to convey meaning.
