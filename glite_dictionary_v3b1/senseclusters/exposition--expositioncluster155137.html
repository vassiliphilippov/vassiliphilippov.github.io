exposition

noun
detailed explanation or account
Pronunciation: / ˌɛk.spəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌɛk.spəˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a clear and detailed explanation or account of an idea, theory, or subject, especially in writing or speech

Examples

Clear exposition
Her clear exposition of the theory impressed everyone at the conference.
The professor's exposition of quantum mechanics made the complex subject much easier to understand.
The textbook begins with an exposition of basic economic principles.
Her clear exposition of the issue impressed the entire audience.
The novel begins with a lengthy exposition of the main characters and setting.
The professor's exposition of the theory made it much easier to understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interpretation - an explanation of the meaning of something
  • antonym: summary - a brief statement of the main points
