poor little rich boy

noun
wealthy but emotionally troubled young man
6.6
Pronunciation: / pɔr ˈlɪt̬l̩ rɪtʃ bɔɪ / (AmE), / pɔː ˈlɪtl rɪtʃ bɔɪ / (BrE)
A wealthy young man who is regarded as suffering from emotional problems or loneliness despite material wealth.

Examples

A poor little rich boy
Despite his expensive education, he remained a poor little rich boy at heart.
People were unsympathetic to the problems of the poor little rich boy.
The film tells the story of a poor little rich boy searching for meaning beyond his fortune.
