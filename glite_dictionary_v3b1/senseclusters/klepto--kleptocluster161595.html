klepto

noun
slang thief
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛptoʊ / (AmE), / ˈklɛptəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who habitually steals things, especially small items

Examples

Tom is a klepto
Everyone knows Tom is a bit of a klepto when it comes to office supplies.
My friend joked that I was a klepto after I accidentally took her pen home.
The group suspected a klepto was responsible for the missing snacks in the break room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: honest person - someone who does not steal
  • synonym: kleptomaniac - a person with a compulsive desire to steal
  • synonym: thief - a person who steals
