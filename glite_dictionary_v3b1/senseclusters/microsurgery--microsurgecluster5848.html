microsurgery

noun
delicate surgery using microscopes and fine instruments
1.0 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈsɝːdʒəri / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈsɜːdʒəri / (BrE)
surgery performed using operating microscopes and delicate instruments to carry out intricate procedures on very small structures, such as blood vessels or nerves.

Examples

Microsurgery techniques
Advances in microsurgery have made it possible to reattach severed fingers with great success.
After her injury, the patient underwent microsurgery to repair the nerves in her hand.
Specialized training is required for surgeons who wish to perform microsurgery on delicate structures.
