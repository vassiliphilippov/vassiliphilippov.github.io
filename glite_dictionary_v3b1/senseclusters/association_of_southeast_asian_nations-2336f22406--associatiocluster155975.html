Association of Southeast Asian Nations

proper noun
Southeast Asian regional organization (ASEAN)
Pronunciation: / əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən əv ˌsaʊθˌist ˈeɪʒən ˈneɪʃənz / (AmE), / əˌsəʊsiˈeɪʃən əv ˌsaʊθˌiːst ˈeɪʒ(ə)n ˈneɪʃ(ə)nz / (BrE)
an intergovernmental organization of Southeast Asian countries established to promote economic, political, and security cooperation among its member states

Examples

Association of Southeast Asian Nations members
Indonesia is a founding member of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations.
The Association of Southeast Asian Nations aims to foster regional stability and economic growth among its members.
The Association of Southeast Asian Nations often holds summits to discuss issues affecting the region.
