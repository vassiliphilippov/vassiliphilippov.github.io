nonpartisan

adjective
not biased toward any political party
7.2 4.9
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈpɑːr.tɪ.zən/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈpɑːr.tə.zən/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈpɑː.tɪ.zən/ /; / /nɒn.ˈpɑː.tə.zən/ (BrE)
Not supporting or controlled by any political group; free from party affiliation or bias.

Examples

Nonpartisan decision
She was praised for her nonpartisan approach to policymaking.
The commission is a nonpartisan organization dedicated to fair elections.
We need a nonpartisan analysis to understand the facts objectively.
