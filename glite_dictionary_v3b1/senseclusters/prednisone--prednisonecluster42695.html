prednisone

noun
synthetic corticosteroid drug
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpred.nɪ.soʊn/ /; / /ˈpred.nɪ.zoʊn/ (AmE), /ˈpred.nɪ.zəʊn/ /; / /ˈpred.nɪ.səʊn/ (BrE)
