handicraft

noun
object made by hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændiˌkræft / (AmE), / ˈhændɪkrɑːft / (BrE)
an object made by hand, or the activity of making things skillfully by hand

Examples

A handicraft basket

Learning a new handicraft can be both relaxing and rewarding.
She sells her beautiful handicraft items at the market every Saturday.
These festivals often showcase traditional handicraft from local artisans.
