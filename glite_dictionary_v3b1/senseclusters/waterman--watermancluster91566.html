waterman

noun
man who works driving boats
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔt̬ər.mən / (AmE), / ˈwɔː.tə.mən / (BrE)
a man whose occupation is to drive or work a boat, especially one who ferries passengers on rivers or canals

Examples

Watermen on rivers
Many watermen in London once ferried people across the Thames for a living.
The waterman offered us a ride on his small wooden craft.
The old waterman skillfully guided his boat across the busy river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boatman - a person who works or travels in a boat
