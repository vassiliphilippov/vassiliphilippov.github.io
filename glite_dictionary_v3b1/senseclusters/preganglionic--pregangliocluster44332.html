preganglionic

adjective
before a ganglion
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɡæŋˈɡliːɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːɡæŋˈɡliːɒnɪk / (BrE)
describing nerve fibers or neurons located before reaching a ganglion in the nervous system

Examples

Preganglionic nerve fibers
The preganglionic fibers carry signals from the spinal cord to the autonomic ganglia.
Damage to preganglionic neurons can disrupt normal nervous system function.
Researchers studied the effects of drugs on preganglionic nerve transmission in laboratory animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postganglionic - describing nerve fibers or neurons located after a ganglion
