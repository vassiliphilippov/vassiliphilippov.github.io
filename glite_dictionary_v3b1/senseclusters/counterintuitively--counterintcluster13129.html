counterintuitively

adverb
in a contrary-to-expectation manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtuɪɾɪvli / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtjuːɪtɪvli / (BrE)
In a way that is contrary to what one would intuitively expect.

Examples

Counterintuitively surprising result
Counterintuitively, adding more users to the project delayed its completion instead of speeding it up.
Counterintuitively, exercising late at night helped him fall asleep faster.
Counterintuitively, lower interest rates sometimes cause people to save more rather than spend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterintuitive - contrary to what intuition expects
  • antonym: intuitively - in a manner based on intuition
