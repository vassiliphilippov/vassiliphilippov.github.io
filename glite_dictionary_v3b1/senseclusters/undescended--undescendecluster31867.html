undescended

adjective
not having moved to its normal position (esp. of a testis)
3.9 7.7
Pronunciation: /ʌn.dɪ.ˈsen.dɪd/ /; / /ə.n.dɪ.ˈsen.dɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.dɪ.ˈsen.dɪd/ /; / /ə.n.dɪ.ˈsen.dɪd/ (BrE)
(of a testis or organ) remaining in the abdomen instead of moving to its normal position, especially into the scrotum.

Examples

Undescended testicle
Boys with an undescended testicle are at higher risk of fertility problems later in life.
Surgery may be recommended to correct an undescended organ in infants.
The doctor diagnosed the child with an undescended testis after the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptorchid - having undescended testes
  • antonym: descended - (of a testis) having moved into the scrotum
