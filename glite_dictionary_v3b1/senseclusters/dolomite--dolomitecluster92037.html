dolomite

noun
a mineral; calcium magnesium carbonate
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊ.ləˌmaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɒl.əˌmaɪt / (BrE)
a light-colored mineral composed of calcium magnesium carbonate, used as a source of magnesium and in ceramics and fertilizers

Examples

Crushed dolomite fertilizer
Farmers often use crushed dolomite to improve soil quality and pH balance.
The mountain was rich in dolomite, making it valuable for mining operations.
The pottery factory sources dolomite for its ceramic production processes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calcite - a carbonate mineral and the most stable polymorph of calcium carbonate
  • synonym: limestone - a sedimentary rock mainly composed of calcite or aragonite
