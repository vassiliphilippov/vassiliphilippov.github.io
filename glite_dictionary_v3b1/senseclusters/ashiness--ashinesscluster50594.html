ashiness

noun
grayish quality
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃi nəs / (AmE), / ˈæʃɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being ashy, especially having a pale, grayish, or powdery appearance or texture

Examples

The ashiness of soil
The drought left the soil with a noticeable ashiness.
After the fire, a layer of ashiness covered the furniture in the room.
Her skin showed some ashiness during the cold winter months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightness - the quality of being bright or vivid
  • synonym: dullness - the quality of lacking brightness or vividness
  • synonym: paleness - the quality of being pale in color
