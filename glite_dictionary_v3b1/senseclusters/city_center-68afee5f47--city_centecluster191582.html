city center

noun
the central area of a city
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti ˈsɛntər /; / ˈsɪti ˌsentər / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˈsɛntə /; / ˈsɪti ˌsentə / (BrE)
the central and most important part of a city, often containing the main shops, offices, and public buildings

Examples

The city center shops
Shops and restaurants in the city center usually stay open late.

Many historic buildings are located in the city centre.
Roadworks in the city centre caused major traffic delays.
The city centre was crowded with shoppers on Saturday afternoon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: suburb - an outlying residential district of a city
