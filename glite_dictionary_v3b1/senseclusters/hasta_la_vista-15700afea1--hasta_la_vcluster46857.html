hasta la vista

x
goodbye; until we meet again
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstə lə ˈvistə / (AmE), / ˌhæstə lə ˈviːstə / (BrE)
used to say goodbye, especially until we meet again; originally from Spanish, commonly used as a farewell in English.

Examples

Hasta la vista goodbye
After their vacation, he told his friends, 'hasta la vista—we'll meet again soon.'
At the end of the meeting, he smiled and said, hasta la vista.
She waved and shouted, hasta la vista, as the train pulled away.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - an expression used to say farewell
