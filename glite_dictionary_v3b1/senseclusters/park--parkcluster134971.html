park

noun
gear position for immobilizing a vehicle
4.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /pɑːrk/ (AmE), /pɑːk/ (BrE)
the gear position in an automatic vehicle that locks the transmission, preventing the vehicle from moving when stationary

Examples

Shift to park
He accidentally tried to start the car while it was still in park.
The instructor reminded the student to shift into park before getting out.
When you stop the car, make sure it is in park before turning off the engine.
