rowen

noun
second hay crop
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊ.ən / (AmE), / ˈrəʊ.ən / (BrE)
a second crop of grass or hay that grows after the first has been harvested in the same season

Examples

The rowen grass

Cows enjoyed the fresh rowen that was collected in late summer.
The quality of the rowen depends on the weather after the initial hay harvest.
