hieroglyphic

noun
indecipherable or obscure writing
3.9 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk / (BrE)
writing that is difficult to read because it resembles or imitates hieroglyphics; illegible or obscure writing.

Examples

Unreadable hieroglyphic
Her signature looked like a hieroglyphic to everyone except herself.
I couldn’t read his notes; they were just a hieroglyphic on the page.
The teacher returned the homework because it was written in near-hieroglyphic script.
