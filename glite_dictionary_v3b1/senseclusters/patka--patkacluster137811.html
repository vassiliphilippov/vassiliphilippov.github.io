patka

noun
scarf worn by Sikh men to cover hair
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑtkə / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtkə / (BrE)
A square or rectangular scarf, typically tied over the head, worn especially by Sikh men and boys to cover their hair.

Examples

A patka scarf
He selected a blue patka to match his kurta for the festival.
Many Sikh boys wear a patka to school as part of their daily attire.
The coach allowed players to wear a patka instead of a helmet during practice for comfort.
