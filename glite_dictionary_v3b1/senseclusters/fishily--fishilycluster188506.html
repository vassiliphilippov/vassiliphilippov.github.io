fishily

adverb
in a suspicious or questionable way
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪli / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪli / (BrE)
In a suspicious or questionable manner; suggesting something is not quite right.

Examples

To laugh fishily
He laughed fishily when asked about his whereabouts last night.
She glanced fishily at the stranger who entered the room.
The accountant explained the figures fishily, making everyone uneasy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocently - in a way that shows no suspicion or guilt
  • synonym: suspiciously - in a way that shows suspicion or distrust
