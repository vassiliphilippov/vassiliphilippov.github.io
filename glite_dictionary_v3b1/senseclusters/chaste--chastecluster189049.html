chaste

adjective
abstaining from immoral sexual activity
Pronunciation: / tʃeɪst / (AmE), / tʃeɪst / (BrE)
not having sex, or avoiding sexual activity considered immoral; sexually pure or modest

Examples

Chaste until marriage
Many ancient cultures praised those who remained chaste until marriage.
She lived a chaste life, devoted to spiritual pursuits instead of romance.
The monks vowed to remain chaste for the rest of their lives.
Despite many temptations, he chose to stay chaste and true to his values.
She lived a chaste life dedicated to her faith and service to others.
The knight took a vow to remain chaste until marriage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: impure - not morally pure or chaste
  • synonym: virginal - not having had sexual experience; pure
  • synonym: virtuous - having or showing high moral standards
