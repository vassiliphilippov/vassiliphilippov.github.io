nikkeijin

noun
returnee of Japanese descent
9.9
Pronunciation: / niːˈkeɪdʒɪn / (AmE), / niːˈkeɪdʒɪn / (BrE)
a person of Japanese descent who has returned or moved to live in Japan from another country, often for work or residency
