enabling

adjective
granting legal power or authority
2.0
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈeɪblɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈeɪblɪŋ / (BrE)
giving someone or something the authority or power, especially by legal means; granting the legal right or sanction to do something.

Examples

An enabling provision
An enabling provision in the law allowed the board to expand its authority.
The parliament passed an enabling act to allow the new regulations.
They adopted an enabling resolution to proceed with the merger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authorizing - giving official permission for something
