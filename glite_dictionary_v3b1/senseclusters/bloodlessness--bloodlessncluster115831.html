bloodlessness

noun
absence of violence
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌdləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈblʌdləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being without violence, bloodshed, or injury; peacefulness in which no one is harmed

Examples

Bloodlessness of the protest
The bloodlessness of the revolution surprised many observers who expected violent clashes.
Negotiators worked hard to ensure the bloodlessness of the transition of power.
Historians often highlight the bloodlessness of the agreement as a rare achievement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: peacefulness - the state of being calm and free from violence
  • antonym: violence - the use of physical force to harm someone or something
