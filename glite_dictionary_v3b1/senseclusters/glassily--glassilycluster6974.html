glassily

adverb
cold, blank manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlæsɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡlɑːsɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that appears cold, expressionless, or like glass, often lacking emotion or warmth

Examples

Glassily stare
He looked glassily at the interviewer, revealing nothing of his true feelings.
The child stared glassily at the television, barely blinking as the show played.
After hearing the bad news, she responded glassily, her face showing no emotion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blankly - in a way that shows no emotion or understanding
  • antonym: warmly - in a friendly or affectionate manner
