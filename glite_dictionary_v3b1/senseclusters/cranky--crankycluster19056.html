Synonyms

  • tender
    8.9 0.06/m
    tender is a sailing term for a boat that heels easily in wind because it lacks stability, while cranky is used the same way.
  • crank
    9.1 0.06/m
    crank is used for a boat that tips or heels easily under sail, while cranky emphasizes being unstable in water.
  • tippy
    7.5 0.23/m
    tippy is informal and describes any unstable object likely to tip over, while cranky refers specifically to a boat's balance under sail.
