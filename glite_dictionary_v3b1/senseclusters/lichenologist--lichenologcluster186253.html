lichenologist

noun
lichen scientist
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlaɪkəˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌlɪkəˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies lichens, which are symbiotic organisms made of fungi and algae

Examples

A lichenologist studies lichens
The lichenologist spent months in the mountains collecting rare specimens for her research.
As a lichenologist, he often examines the relationship between fungi and algae in different environments.
The museum invited a lichenologist to give a talk about the importance of lichens in ecosystems.
