tubocurarine

noun
toxic plant alkaloid; muscle relaxant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtuːboʊkjʊˈrɑːriːn / (AmE), / ˌtjuːbəʊkjʊˈrɑːriːn / (BrE)
a toxic alkaloid obtained from certain South American plants, used as a muscle relaxant, especially during surgery
