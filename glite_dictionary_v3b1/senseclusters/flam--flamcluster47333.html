flam

noun
drumming grace note
9.5
Pronunciation: /flæm/ (AmE), /flæm/ (BrE)
a drumming technique where a quick grace note is played just before the main stroke to create a fuller sound

Examples

A flam stroke
The drummer used a flam to add emphasis to the beginning of the song.
Practicing flams can help improve your timing and control on the snare drum.
She played a series of flams to make the drum solo more dynamic.
