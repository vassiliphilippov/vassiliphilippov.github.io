bit

noun
short act within a longer show
Pronunciation: / bɪt / (AmE), / bɪt / (BrE)
a short performance or piece of entertainment that is part of a longer show, such as a skit or routine

Examples

Comedy bit
She performed a clever magic bit as part of the gala.
The comedian's opening bit had the audience laughing within seconds.
They rehearsed the dance bit before the main act began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: number - a short self-contained performance, especially in a variety show
  • synonym: routine - a regularly performed sequence in a show, especially comedy or dance
  • synonym: skit - a short, humorous performance or piece of acting
