microdrive

noun
miniature drive device
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌdraɪv / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdraɪv / (BrE)
a very small mechanical or electromechanical device used to produce controlled movement in miniature machines or instruments

Examples

Instrument microdrive
The technician installed a microdrive to enable precise adjustments in the medical instrument.
Advances in robotics have made the microdrive an essential component for miniature devices.
A faulty microdrive can cause the entire system to malfunction in delicate machinery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actuator - a device that causes a machine or mechanism to operate
  • synonym: motor - a machine that supplies power for movement
