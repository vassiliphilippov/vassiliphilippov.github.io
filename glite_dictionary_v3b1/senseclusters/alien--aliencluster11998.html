alien

noun
foreigner; non-citizen
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪliən / (AmE), / ˈeɪliən / (BrE)
a person from another country who is not a citizen or subject of the country where they are living

Examples

An alien worker
As an alien, he had to apply for a visa to work legally.
The government issued new regulations for all aliens residing in the country.
The law grants aliens certain rights and protections under the constitution.

Related Terms

  • antonym: citizen - a legally recognized subject or national of a state
  • synonym: foreigner - a person from another country or place
