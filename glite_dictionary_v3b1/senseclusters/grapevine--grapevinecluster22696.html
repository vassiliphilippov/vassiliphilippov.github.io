grapevine

verb
learn through rumors or informal sources
7.1 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈgreɪp.vaɪn/ (AmE), /ˈgreɪp.vaɪn/ (BrE)
to hear or learn information informally through rumors or unofficial sources.

Examples

To grapevine the news
I grapevined the news about the company's merger before it was officially announced.
She usually finds out what's happening in the office by grapevining.
We grapevine most of our information before HR confirms anything to us.
