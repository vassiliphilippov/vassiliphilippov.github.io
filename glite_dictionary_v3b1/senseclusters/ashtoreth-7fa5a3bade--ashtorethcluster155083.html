Ashtoreth

proper noun
Phoenician goddess of love and fertility
Pronunciation: / ˈæʃtəˌrɛθ / (AmE), / ˈæʃtərɛθ / (BrE)
an ancient Phoenician goddess of love and fertility, regarded as the counterpart of Ishtar.

Examples

Ashtoreth goddess worship
Ancient worshippers built temples to honor Ashtoreth in many Phoenician cities.
In some biblical texts, Ashtoreth is mentioned alongside other deities.
The rituals dedicated to Ashtoreth often emphasized themes of fertility and love.
