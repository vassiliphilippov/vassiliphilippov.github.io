Synonyms

    manage means being in charge of people or projects in many settings, while administer often implies formal responsibility.
  • administrate
    5.6 0.06/m
    administrate sounds formal and official in organizations, while administer is used more widely for running programs daily.
  • run
    2.5 149/m
    run often means leading a business or service day-to-day, while administer focuses on official management or execution.
  • operate
    3.2 39/m
    operate usually means control how a system or machine works, while administer means manage or direct an organization.
  • superintend
    7.0 0.14/m
    superintend is a formal word for being in charge of workers and work, while administer covers managing programs or operations.
  • supervise
    3.5 14/m
    supervise means watch and direct people closely at work, while administer means take overall responsibility for running an organization.
  • steward
    3.9 0.17/m
    steward means care for resources or events carefully or responsibly, while administer means manage and run larger programs or organizations.
  • handle
    2.1 96/m
    handle means deal with a task or problem directly, often informally, while administer implies formal duty and overall management.
