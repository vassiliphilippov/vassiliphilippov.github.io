recorder

noun
device that records audio, video, etc.
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔrdər / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːdə / (BrE)
a device used to capture and store audio, video, or other data for later playback or analysis

Examples

Voice recorder
She always keeps a small recorder in her bag to save her song ideas.
The meeting was documented using a digital recorder placed in the center of the table.
The police used a recorder to capture the suspect's confession during the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tape recorder - device that records and plays audio on magnetic tape
