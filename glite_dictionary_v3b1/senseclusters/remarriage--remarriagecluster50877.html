remarriage

noun
the act of marrying again
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
the act or process of marrying again after a previous marriage has ended

Examples

To remarry after divorce
After her divorce, she considered remarriage but wanted to wait a few years.
Legal issues can sometimes complicate remarriage after a lengthy separation.
The children struggled to adjust to their father's remarriage.
