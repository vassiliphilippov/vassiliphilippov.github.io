curate's egg

noun
something partly good, partly bad
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkjʊrˌeɪts ɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈkjʊərɪts ɛɡ / (BrE)
something that is partly good and partly bad; having both positive and negative qualities.

Examples

A curate's egg review
Critics called the play a curate's egg, noting both its brilliant dialogue and awkward pacing.
Her new novel is a bit of a curate's egg, offering some sharp insights alongside confusing passages.
The team's performance was a curate's egg, with some impressive moments but clear mistakes too.
