hemicycle

noun
figure with the shape of half a circle
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛmɪˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈhɛmɪˌsaɪkl / (BrE)
A plane figure or structure that has the shape of half a circle.

Examples

A hemicycle shape
Architects designed the forum in the shape of a hemicycle to improve acoustics.
The ancient theater featured a massive hemicycle where the audience would sit.
The classroom's seating arrangement formed a hemicycle around the lecturer.
