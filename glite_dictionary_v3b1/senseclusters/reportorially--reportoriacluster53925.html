reportorially

adverb
like a reporter
8.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˌpɔːrˈtɔːriəli / (AmE), / rɪˌpɔːˈtɔːriəli / (BrE)
in a manner characteristic of a reporter, focusing on facts and objectivity

Examples

Written reportorially
The article was written reportorially, presenting only the verified facts without personal opinions.
He described the events reportorially, avoiding any emotional language or speculation.
During the interview, she responded reportorially, sticking to the details and remaining neutral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: objectively - in a way that is not influenced by personal feelings
  • antonym: subjectively - in a way that is influenced by personal feelings or opinions
