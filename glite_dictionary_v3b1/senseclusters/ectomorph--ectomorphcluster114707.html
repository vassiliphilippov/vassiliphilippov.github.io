ectomorph

noun
person with a thin and lean body type
Pronunciation: / ˈɛk.təˌmɔrf / (AmE), / ˈɛk.təˌmɔːf / (BrE)
a person who has a naturally thin and lean body type with little body fat or muscle mass

Examples

An ectomorph body type
Although he eats a lot, Tom remains an ectomorph and finds it challenging to gain weight.
As an ectomorph, she focuses on strength training to build muscle.
Many long-distance runners are natural ectomorphs due to their slender physiques.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endomorph - a person with a rounder, softer body type
  • antonym: mesomorph - a person with a naturally muscular or athletic body type
