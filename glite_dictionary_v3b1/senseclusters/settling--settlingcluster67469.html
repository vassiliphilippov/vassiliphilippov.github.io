Synonyms

  • sinkage
    7.4 0.02/m
    sinkage is the measured sinking of soil, structures, or objects, while settling is the general gradual drop caused by natural forces.
  • subsidence
    8.5 0.4/m
    subsidence is land or ground sinking, often sudden or caused by human activity, while settling is the slow natural sinking of materials.
  • sedimentation
    6.0 0.6/m
    sedimentation is particles dropping and building up on a liquid bottom, while settling means the broader process of things sinking slowly.
