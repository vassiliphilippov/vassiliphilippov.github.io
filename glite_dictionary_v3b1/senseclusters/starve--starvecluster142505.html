starve

verb
deprive of food
1.2
Pronunciation: / stɑːrv / (AmE), / stɑːv / (BrE)
to deprive someone or something of food, causing extreme hunger or possible death

Examples

To starve animals
It is cruel to starve animals as a form of punishment.
The army was accused of trying to starve the city into surrender.
They were deliberately starved by their captors for several days.
