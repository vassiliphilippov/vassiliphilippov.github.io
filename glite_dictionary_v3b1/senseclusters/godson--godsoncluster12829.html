godson

noun
male godchild
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑd.sʌn / (AmE), / ˈɡɒd.sʌn / (BrE)
A male child whose baptism is sponsored by a godparent.

Examples

The godson at baptism
After the christening, everyone welcomed their new godson into the family.
My nephew became my godson when I agreed to be his godparent.
She bought a special gift for her godson on his birthday.
