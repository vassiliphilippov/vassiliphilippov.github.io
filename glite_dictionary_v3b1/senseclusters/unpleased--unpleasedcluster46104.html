unpleased

adjective
not satisfied
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpliːzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpliːzd / (BrE)
not feeling happy or satisfied about something; dissatisfied or displeased

Examples

He was unpleased
He was clearly unpleased with the outcome of the discussion.
The manager seemed unpleased by the team's performance this quarter.
She left the restaurant unpleased after waiting an hour for her food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: displeased - feeling annoyed or dissatisfied
  • antonym: satisfied - pleased or content with what has happened
