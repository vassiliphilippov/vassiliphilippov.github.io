aching

noun
dull, persistent pain
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈeɪkɪŋ / (BrE)
a dull, persistent physical pain that is usually not severe but lingers over time

Examples

A dull aching
After running the marathon, he felt an aching in his legs that lasted for days.
She complained of a dull aching behind her eyes whenever she stared at the screen too long.
The constant aching in her back made it hard for her to sleep.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pain - physical suffering or discomfort caused by illness or injury
  • antonym: sharp pain - sudden, intense pain
