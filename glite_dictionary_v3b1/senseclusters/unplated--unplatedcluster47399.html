unplated

adjective
without metal coating
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈpleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈpleɪtɪd / (BrE)
not covered with a layer of metal or protective plating

Examples

Unplated metal parts
The unplated parts of the machine rusted quickly after being exposed to moisture.
It is important to avoid using unplated wires in outdoor electrical installations.
The antique spoon was unplated, showing its original metal surface.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plated - covered with a layer of metal
