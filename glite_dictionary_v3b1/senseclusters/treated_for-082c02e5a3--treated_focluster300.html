treated for

verb
received care to cure a problem or disease
Pronunciation: / ˈtritɪd fər / (AmE), / ˈtriːtɪd fə / (BrE)
received action or medical care to remove or cure a harmful condition or disease.

Examples

To treat for injuries
After the accident, she was treated for minor injuries at the clinic.
The garden was treated for pests to prevent them from destroying the vegetables.
The water supply is regularly treated for bacteria to keep it safe.
