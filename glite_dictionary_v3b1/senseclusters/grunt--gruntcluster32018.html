grunt

noun
a tropical marine food fish that grunts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌnt / (AmE), / ɡrʌnt / (BrE)
a medium-sized tropical marine fish, noted for making grunting sounds when caught, and often used as food

Examples

A grunt fish
Fishermen prize the grunt for its tasty, white flesh and distinctive flavor.
The boat's catch included several snapper and a large grunt.
When pulled from the water, the grunt emits a curious, croaking sound.
