unstaged

adjective
not performed on stage
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsteɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪdʒd / (BrE)
(of a play, performance, etc.) not performed or presented on a stage.

Examples

Unstaged play
