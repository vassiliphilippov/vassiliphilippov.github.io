Frasera speciosa

proper noun
tall herb with white-green flowers, NW US

Pronunciation: / frəˈsɪərə spəˈsioʊsə / (AmE), / frəˈsɪərə spɛˈsiːəʊsə / (BrE)
A tall herb of the gentian family with panicles of white flowers tinged with green, native to the northwestern United States; sometimes classified in the genus Swertia.
