trackless trolley

noun
electric bus powered by overhead wires
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækləs ˈtrɑli / (AmE), / ˈtrækləs ˈtrɒli / (BrE)
a passenger bus powered by electricity from overhead wires, not running on tracks

Examples

A trackless trolley bus
A trackless trolley glided silently down the avenue, powered by the overhead wires.
Residents prefer the trackless trolley for its quiet and environmentally friendly operation.
The city plans to replace its old trackless trolleys with newer, more efficient models next year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trolleybus - a bus powered electrically from overhead wires, not on rails
