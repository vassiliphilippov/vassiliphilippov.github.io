equalist

noun
advocate of equality
Pronunciation: / ˈiːkwəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈiːkwəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or advocates for equality among all people

Examples

An equalist leader
As an equalist, he campaigns for equal pay regardless of gender or background.
The organization was founded by a group of passionate equalists seeking social justice.
Many young people today identify as equalists and support movements for equality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discriminator - a person who treats people unfairly based on differences
  • synonym: egalitarian - a person who supports equal rights and opportunities for all
