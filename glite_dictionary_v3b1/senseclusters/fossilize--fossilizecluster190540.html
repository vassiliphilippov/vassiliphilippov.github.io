fossilize

verb
become or make into a fossil

Pronunciation: /ˈfɑː.sə.laɪz/ /; / /ˈfɑː.sɪ.laɪz/ (AmE), /ˈfɒ.sɪ.laɪz/ /; / /ˈfɒ.səl.aɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

Bones fossilize underground
