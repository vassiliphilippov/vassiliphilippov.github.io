canopying

noun
parachute gliding activity
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkæn.ə.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkæn.ə.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity of gliding or moving through the air using a parachute-like canopy, often for recreation or sport

Examples

To go canopying with a parachute
We tried canopying over the rainforest during our vacation.
Many adventure seekers enjoy canopying to experience the thrill of flying above the trees.
Safety equipment is essential when participating in canopying in mountainous regions.
