fruitlessness

noun
lack of creative imagination; lack of results
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfruːt.ləs.nəs /; / ˈfruːtləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfruːtlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
the state of not producing results, success, or productivity

Examples

The fruitlessness of efforts
She worried that fruitlessness was creeping into her work as a writer.
The fruitlessness of his approach became clear when no innovative ideas emerged from the meeting.
Years of fruitlessness discouraged the team from proposing new concepts.
After years of trying, he finally accepted the fruitlessness of his efforts.
She was frustrated by the fruitlessness of searching for answers that never came.
The fruitlessness of their negotiations became clear after months without progress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrenness - the state of being unproductive or sterile
  • antonym: creativity - the use of imagination or original ideas to create something
  • antonym: fruitful - producing good or helpful results; productive
  • synonym: futility - the quality of being pointless or unable to produce a useful result
  • antonym: imagination - the faculty or action of forming new ideas or images
