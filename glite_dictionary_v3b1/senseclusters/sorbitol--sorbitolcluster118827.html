sorbitol

noun
sugar alcohol sweetener
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrbɪˌtɔl / (AmE), / ˈsɔːbɪtɒl / (BrE)
a sweet, sugar alcohol used as a sweetener, sugar substitute, humectant, or stabilizer in foods, medicines, cosmetics, and other products
