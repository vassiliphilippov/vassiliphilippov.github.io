unspin the misinformation

verb
correct or clarify misinformation
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɪn ði ˌmɪs.ɪn.fɔˈmeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɪn ði ˌmɪs.ɪn.fəˈmeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
to correct or clarify false or misleading information by removing distortions or bias

Examples

To unspin the misinformation
It's crucial for educators to unspin the misinformation students encounter online.
Journalists must work hard to unspin the misinformation that circulates after major events.
The scientist made a point to unspin the misinformation spread by social media influencers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarify - make a statement or situation less confused and more comprehensible
  • synonym: debunk - expose the falseness of a myth or belief
  • antonym: spread misinformation - to disseminate false or misleading information
