adhesive plaster

noun
adhesive material for covering wounds
4.4 2.7
Pronunciation: /əd.ˈhiː.sɪv ˈplæs.tər/ /; / /æd.ˈhiː.sɪv ˈplæs.tər/ (AmE), /əd.ˈhiː.sɪv ˈplɑː.stə/ /; / /æd.ˈhiː.sɪv ˈplɑː.stə/ (BrE)
A strip of material coated with an adhesive, used to cover and protect minor wounds or hold dressings in place.

Examples

An adhesive plaster strip
After the injection, the nurse covered the spot with an adhesive plaster.
It's a good idea to keep some adhesive plasters in your first aid kit.
She applied an adhesive plaster to the small cut on her finger.
