A policy, protocol, or technical control used to protect digital information, computer systems, or networks from unauthorized access, damage, or other cyber threats.

Examples

A security measure for passwords
The company introduced a new security measure requiring two-factor authentication for all employee logins.
After the breach, IT rolled out an encryption-based security measure to protect customer databases.
Banks often test each security measure thoroughly before deploying it on their public systems.
