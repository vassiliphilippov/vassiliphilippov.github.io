cementum

noun
hard tissue covering tooth root
9.7 9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈmɛntəm / (AmE), / sɪˈmɛntəm / (BrE)
A hard, bony tissue covering the root of a tooth, helping to anchor it within the jaw.

Examples

Tooth cementum layer
After the injury, an X-ray showed damage to the cementum of his molar.
In healthy teeth, the cementum covers the entire root, supporting overall stability.
The dentist explained that the cementum helps protect the roots of teeth from decay.
