habitus

noun
socially shaped habits
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhæbɪtəs / (AmE), / ˈhæbɪtəs / (BrE)
the set of habits, attitudes, and ways of thinking shaped by a person's background and experiences

Examples

Social habitus
Her habitus influenced how she approached new challenges in her career.
Sociologists study how a person's habitus affects their choices and opportunities.
Growing up in a rural area shaped his habitus and daily routines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: custom - a traditional and widely accepted way of behaving
  • synonym: disposition - a person's usual attitude or way of behaving
