undercard

noun
preliminary sports matches
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkɑrd /; / ˈʌndərˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈʌndəkɑːd / (BrE)
a set of preliminary or less important matches or performances that take place before the main event, especially in sports or entertainment
