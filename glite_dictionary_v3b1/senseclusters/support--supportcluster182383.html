support

noun
evidence or documentation for a claim
Pronunciation: / səˈpɔrt / (AmE), / səˈpɔːt / (BrE)
evidence or documentation that provides proof or validation for a claim, argument, or idea.

Examples

Documentary support
Historical support for the theory was found in newly discovered documents.
The researcher gathered substantial support for her hypothesis from previous studies.
Without proper support, the court could not accept his arguments as valid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contradiction - assertion of the opposite or denial of a statement
  • synonym: evidence - information that gives proof or support to an argument or claim
  • synonym: proof - evidence or argument establishing a fact or the truth of a statement
