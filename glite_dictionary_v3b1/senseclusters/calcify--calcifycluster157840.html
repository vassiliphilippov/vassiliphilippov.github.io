calcify

verb
harden by calcium deposits
Pronunciation: / ˈkælsɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈkælsəˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈkælsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to become or cause to become hard or stony by the deposition of calcium salts (literal, physical process)
