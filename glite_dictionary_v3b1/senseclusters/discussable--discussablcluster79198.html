discussable

adjective
open for discussion
3.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈskʌsəbəl / (AmE), / dɪˈskʌsəbəl / (BrE)

Examples

A discussable topic
The new policy is fully discussable at the upcoming board meeting.
Some topics are not discussable in this forum due to company rules.
Is the budget proposal discussable before the final decision is made?
