insister

noun
persistent demander
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪstər / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a person who insists on something, either by demanding it repeatedly or by strongly maintaining a belief or opinion

Examples

The insister kept asking
The insister kept asking for a meeting until the manager finally agreed.
Even after being told no several times, the insister would not give up.
As an insister, she always made sure her opinions were heard in every discussion.
The insister would not back down, even when everyone else disagreed with her.
As an insister on honesty, he always spoke his mind regardless of the situation.
Despite repeated explanations, the insister continued to argue his original point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy
  • synonym: demanding person - someone who asks for things forcefully or persistently
  • antonym: doubter - a person who questions or lacks belief in something
  • degree lower: requester - a person who asks for something
