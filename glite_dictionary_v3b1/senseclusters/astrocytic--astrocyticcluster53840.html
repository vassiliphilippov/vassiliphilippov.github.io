astrocytic

adjective
relating to astrocytes (star-shaped brain cells)
Pronunciation: / ˌæstrəˈsɪtɪk / (AmE), / ˌastrəʊˈsɪtɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of astrocytes, the large star-shaped cells found in the neuroglia of the brain and spinal cord

Examples

Astrocytic brain cells
Certain neurological diseases are marked by abnormal astrocytic activity.
The astrocytic network plays a key role in maintaining the blood-brain barrier.
The researchers observed an astrocytic response following the injury to the brain tissue.
