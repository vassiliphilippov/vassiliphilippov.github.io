call

noun
demand or request
9.9
Pronunciation: / kɔl / (AmE), / kɔːl / (BrE)
a demand, request, or appeal for something to be done or for someone to be present

Examples

A call for help
Many people responded to the call for volunteers after the disaster.
The union issued a call to strike if their demands were not met.
There was a call for reform in the education system.
The mayor issued a call for volunteers to help clean up the park.
There have been renewed calls for better workplace safety regulations.
There is little call for typewriters these days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demand - an insistent and peremptory request
  • synonym: request - an act of asking for something
