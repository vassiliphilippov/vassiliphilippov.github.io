come up

verb
start operating or become active
6.3
Pronunciation: / kʌm ʌp / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp / (BrE)
(of equipment, a computer system, or lights) to start operating, become active, or begin running.

Examples

The lights come up
It took a few minutes for the lights to come up after the blackout.
The heating system will come up automatically at 6 a.m. every day.
When the power was restored, the server finally came up and users could log in again.
