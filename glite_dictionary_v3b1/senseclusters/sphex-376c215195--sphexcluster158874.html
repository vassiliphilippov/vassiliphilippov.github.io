Sphex

noun
solitary digger wasp
9.9
Pronunciation: / sfɛks / (AmE), / sfɛks / (BrE)
a wasp of the genus Sphex, known as a solitary digger wasp that nests in the ground

Examples

A Sphex wasp
A Sphex wasp dug a tunnel in the sandy soil to lay its eggs.
Scientists observed a Sphex carrying a paralyzed cricket back to its nest.
Unlike social wasps, the Sphex lives and hunts alone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wasp - an insect with a slender body and a sting, related to bees and ants
