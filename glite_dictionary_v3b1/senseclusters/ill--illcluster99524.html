ill

noun
illness; bodily disorder (noun)
2.5
Pronunciation: / ɪl / (AmE), / ɪl / (BrE)
An illness, disease, or bodily disorder, often causing discomfort or giving reason to complain.

Examples

A serious ill
Decades of hard living brought on a variety of ills in old age.
Many social ills can be traced back to poverty and inequality.
The patient suffered from a chronic ill that made daily life difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disease - a disorder or malfunction in an organism
  • synonym: malady - a disease or ailment
