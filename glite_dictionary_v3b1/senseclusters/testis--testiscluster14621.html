testis

noun
male reproductive gland
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛstɪs / (AmE), / ˈtɛstɪs / (BrE)
either of the two male reproductive glands that produce sperm and secrete hormones such as testosterone

Examples

The testes gland
Injury to the testes can affect fertility in men.
The doctor explained the function of the testis to the students.
The hormone testosterone is mainly produced in the testis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ovary - female reproductive gland producing eggs
  • synonym: testicle - another term for testis, less formal
