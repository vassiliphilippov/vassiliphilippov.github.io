page turner

noun
device for turning book pages automatically
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪdʒ ˌtɝːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪdʒ ˌtɜːnə / (BrE)
A mechanical device that automatically turns the pages of books or magazines.

Examples

A page turner device
She demonstrated how the page turner works at the accessibility exhibition.
The library installed a page turner to help patrons with limited mobility enjoy reading.
This page turner can be operated with a remote control for convenience.
