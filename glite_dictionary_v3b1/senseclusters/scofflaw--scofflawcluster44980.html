scofflaw

noun
habitual lawbreaker
9.9 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / Ààsk…îfÀål…î / (AmE), / Ààsk…ífÀål…îÀê / (BrE)
a person who habitually disregards or flouts the law, especially by failing to answer court summonses or comply with regulations

Examples

A scofflaw ignores rules
Authorities launched a crackdown on scofflaws who ignore court-ordered fines.
Despite repeated warnings, the scofflaw continued parking illegally throughout the city.
The mayor called for stricter enforcement to deal with persistent scofflaw behavior.

Synonyms

  • violator
    5.1 1.64/m
    a violator breaks laws, rules, or agreements regularly, while a scofflaw habitually ignores court orders and official regulations with open contempt.
  • flouter
    8.9 0.02/m
    a flouter mocks or shows contempt for rules and authority, while a scofflaw specifically refuses to obey laws or legal orders.
  • lawbreaker
    3.8 0.54/m
    a lawbreaker commits crimes that may be serious, while a scofflaw often ignores minor regulations and may avoid court summonses.

Related Terms
