scofflaw

noun
habitual lawbreaker
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔfˌlɔ / (AmE), / ˈskɒfˌlɔː / (BrE)
a person who habitually disregards or flouts the law, especially by failing to answer court summonses or comply with regulations

Examples

A scofflaw ignores rules
Authorities launched a crackdown on scofflaws who ignore court-ordered fines.
Despite repeated warnings, the scofflaw continued parking illegally throughout the city.
The mayor called for stricter enforcement to deal with persistent scofflaw behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: outlaw - someone who has broken the law and is not protected by it
