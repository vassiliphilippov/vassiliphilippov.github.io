blotchiness

noun
irregular spots
6.2 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààbl…ëÀêt Éin…ôs / (AmE), / Ààbl…ít Éin…ôs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having irregular spots or patches, especially on a surface or skin

Examples

Skin blotchiness
The blotchiness on her cheeks faded after she applied the cream.
Some fabrics show blotchiness if the dye is not evenly applied.
Doctors examined the patient's skin for signs of blotchiness caused by the medication.

Synonyms

  • spottiness
    5.8 0.01/m
    spottiness means being covered in many small spots on a surface, while blotchiness means irregular larger patches or stains.
  • mottling
    6.9 0.13/m
    mottling is a pattern of mixed shades or spots on skin or surface, while blotchiness means uneven irregular patches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mottling - the appearance of spots or blotches of different color or shade
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of having an even and regular surface
