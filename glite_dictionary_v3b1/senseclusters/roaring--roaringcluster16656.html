roaring

adverb
used to intensify adjectives, e.g., 'roaring drunk'
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɔːrɪŋ / (BrE)
(informal, before another adjective) extremely; used as an intensifier in certain expressions.

Examples

Roaring drunk
He got roaring drunk at the party last night.
She was roaring hungry after her hike in the mountains.
The fans were roaring mad when their team lost the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremely - to a very great degree; used as an intensifier
