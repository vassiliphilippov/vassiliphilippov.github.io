title bar

noun
horizontal label at top of window
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪt̬l̩ bɑr / (AmE), / ˈtaɪtl̩ bɑː / (BrE)
A horizontal label at the top of a computer window displaying the name of the current document or application.

Examples

The title bar name
If you double-click the title bar, the window will maximize.
The title bar of the document showed 'Report2022.docx' as the active file.
You can move the window by clicking and dragging its title bar.
