unsterile

adjective
able to reproduce
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɛrəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɛraɪl / (BrE)
able to produce offspring; not sterile in the reproductive sense.

Examples

Unsterile animal breeds
The veterinarian confirmed that the cat was unsterile and could have kittens.
Farmers prefer unsterile animals to increase their livestock numbers.
Because the rabbit was unsterile, it was important to keep it separated from the males.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fertile - able to produce offspring or new growth
  • antonym: sterile - unable to produce offspring
