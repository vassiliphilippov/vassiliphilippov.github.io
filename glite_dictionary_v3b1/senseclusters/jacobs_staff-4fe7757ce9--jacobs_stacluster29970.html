Jacob's staff

noun
desert shrub with spiny branches and red flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪkəbz stæf / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪkəbz stɑːf / (BrE)
a desert shrub of the southwestern United States and Mexico with slender, naked spiny branches that produce foliage and clusters of red flowers after the rainy season
