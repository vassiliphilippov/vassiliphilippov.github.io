incuriosity

noun
lack of curiosity
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkjʊriˈɑːsəti / (AmE), / ˌɪnkjʊəriˈɒsəti / (BrE)
a lack of interest in learning, discovering, or seeking new information

Examples

Her incuriosity about cultures
Her incuriosity about other cultures made it difficult for her to enjoy traveling.
The manager was frustrated by the team's incuriosity toward new technologies.
Despite the exciting news, his incuriosity was obvious to everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indifference - lack of interest or concern
