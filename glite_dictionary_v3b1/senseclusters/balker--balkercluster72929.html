balker

noun
person who refuses to comply
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːkər / (AmE), / ˈbɔːkə / (BrE)
a person or animal who refuses to proceed, comply, or continue with something

Examples

A balker refuses
As a known balker, he often disrupted the team's progress with his stubbornness.
No matter how much they negotiated, the balker refused to yield to their demands.
The committee struggled to persuade the lone balker to support the new policy.
The horse was a known balker at water crossings.
As a balker, he often hesitated to make important decisions quickly.
The stubborn mule became a balker whenever it reached the narrow bridge.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complier - a person who complies with rules or requests
  • synonym: refuser - a person who refuses something
