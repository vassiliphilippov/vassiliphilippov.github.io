saccharine

overly sweet or sentimental

Excessively sweet, sentimental, or ingratiating, to the point of being unpleasant or insincere.

Examples

A saccharine smile
He spoke with such saccharine sincerity that nobody believed a word he said.
Her saccharine smile made it clear she was just trying to win favor.
The movie's ending was criticized as saccharine and unrealistic by most reviewers.
