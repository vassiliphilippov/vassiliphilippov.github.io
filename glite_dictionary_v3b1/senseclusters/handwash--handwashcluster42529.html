handwash

verb
wash by hand, not machine
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌwɒʃ / (BrE)
to wash or launder something by hand, rather than using a machine

Examples

To handwash clothes
I always handwash my delicate scarves to keep them looking new.
Please handwash all wool sweaters to prevent shrinking.
The care label says to handwash this blouse in cold water.

Related Terms

  • antonym: machine-wash - to wash with a washing machine
