linker histone

noun
protein that binds nucleosomes (H1/H5)
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪŋkər ˈhɪstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈlɪŋkə ˈhɪstəʊn / (BrE)
a simple protein (such as histone H1 or H5) that binds to nucleosomes, helping to package DNA into chromatin
