(of land, resources, or something else) not advanced, improved, or used for a practical purpose; lacking development.

Examples

Undeveloped land
Many countries have vast areas of undeveloped farmland waiting to be cultivated.
Property values are often lower for undeveloped land compared to areas with buildings and infrastructure.
Some regions remain largely undeveloped due to environmental protection laws.

Synonyms

  • unimproved
    6.5 0.37/m
    unimproved describes land kept in its original, natural state, while undeveloped is broader and means lacking growth or practical use.
  • unbuilt
    4.9 0.00/m
    unbuilt refers to land without buildings, while undeveloped means the area is not improved or put to practical use.
  • untapped
    4.4 1.50/m
    untapped describes unused resources or potential waiting to be used, while undeveloped covers a more general lack of improvement or use.
  • unexploited
    6.3 0.24/m
    unexploited means resources or chances that people have not used, while undeveloped is a broader term for lacking development or use.
  • underdeveloped
    5.0 1.00/m
    underdeveloped describes countries or regions with low industry and infrastructure, while undeveloped simply means not advanced or improved.
