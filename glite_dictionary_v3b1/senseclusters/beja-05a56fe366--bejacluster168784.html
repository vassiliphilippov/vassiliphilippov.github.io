Beja

proper noun
ethnic group or language
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪdʒə / (AmE), / ˈbiːdʒə / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group or the Cushitic language spoken in northeast Africa, mainly in Sudan, Eritrea, and Egypt

Examples

Beja people
The Beja have a rich cultural heritage in northeastern Africa.
She is studying the Beja language as part of her research on Cushitic peoples.
Many Beja communities live in the eastern regions of Sudan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cushitic - a branch of the Afroasiatic language family spoken in the Horn of Africa
