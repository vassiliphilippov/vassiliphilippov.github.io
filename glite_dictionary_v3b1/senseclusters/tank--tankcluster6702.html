tank

noun
the amount a tank holds
1.5
Pronunciation: / tæŋk / (AmE), / tæŋk / (BrE)
a large container for storing liquids or gases, or the amount it holds (including special vehicles for transporting such substances)

Examples

A tank of water
He drank nearly a whole tank of water during the hike.
The car needs a full tank of gas before our trip.
We emptied another tank of propane while grilling.
Be sure to keep the fuel tank full before starting a long trip.
Oxygen is supplied through a tank during surgery.
The farm had a large tank to store water for irrigation.
A tank filled with crude oil derailed near the city last night.
Inspectors regularly check each tank for leaks or safety hazards.
The railway company used a tank to transport chemicals across the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cylinder - a container with a circular section used for storing gases under pressure
  • synonym: reservoir - a large natural or artificial lake for storing water
  • synonym: tank car - a railroad car specifically built to transport liquids or gases
