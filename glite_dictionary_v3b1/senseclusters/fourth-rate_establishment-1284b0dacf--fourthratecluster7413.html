fourth-rate establishment

noun
very low-quality business or place
5.6 6.2
Pronunciation: /fɔːrθ reɪt ɪ.ˈstæb.lɪʃ.mənt/ (AmE), /fɔːθ reɪt ɪ.ˈstæb.lɪʃ.mənt/ (BrE)
A very low-quality business, place, or institution; one that is considered far below average in standard or reputation.

Examples

A fourth-rate establishment
Avoiding a fourth-rate establishment is wise if you value service and cleanliness.
Despite its promising location, the shop became known as a fourth-rate establishment and quickly lost customers.
He complained that the hotel was just a fourth-rate establishment with grimy rooms and rude staff.
