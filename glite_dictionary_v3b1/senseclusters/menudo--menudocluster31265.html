menudo

noun
Mexican tripe soup
8.5
Pronunciation: / məˈnuːdoʊ / (AmE), / məˈnuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Mexican soup made with beef tripe, chili peppers, and seasonings

Examples

A bowl of menudo
On Sundays, many families gather to enjoy homemade menudo together.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy menudo served with fresh tortillas.
After a long night, he ordered a bowl of menudo to help him recover.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pozole - a Mexican soup made with hominy and meat
  • synonym: tripe - the edible stomach lining of a cow, used in cooking
