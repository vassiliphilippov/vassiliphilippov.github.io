Examples

He therefore decided
He forgot his umbrella and therefore got soaked in the rain.

Synonyms

  • consequently
    3.4 17/m
    consequently shows a clear result after a fact, while therefore is a common connector used for the same reason.
  • thus
    3.2 142/m
    thus introduces a result or summary in formal writing, while therefore is more common in everyday statements.
  • hence
    3.2 30/m
    hence sounds formal and appears in logical statements, while therefore fits both speech and informal writing.
  • accordingly
    3.3 18/m
    accordingly often links an action taken to earlier facts, while therefore just shows the result or reason.
  • consequentially
    7.0 0.05/m
    consequentially describes something happening as a consequence, but therefore simply marks a result without added emphasis.
