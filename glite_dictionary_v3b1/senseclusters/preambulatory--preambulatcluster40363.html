preambulatory

adjective
serving as a preamble
9.9
Pronunciation: / priˈæmbjələtɔri / (AmE), / priˈæmbjʊlətəri / (BrE)
relating to or serving as a preamble, especially in formal documents or statements

Examples

A preambulatory statement
The committee drafted several preambulatory statements to clarify the intent of the agreement.
In legal documents, preambulatory clauses often explain the background and purpose.
The speech began with a preambulatory section outlining the main issues to be discussed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concluding - serving as an ending or conclusion
  • synonym: introductory - serving as an introduction
