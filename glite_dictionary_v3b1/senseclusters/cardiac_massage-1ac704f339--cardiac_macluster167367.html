cardiac massage

noun
emergency heart compression procedure
4.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /kɑːr.ˈdiː.æk mə.ˈsɑːʒ/ (AmE), /kɑː.ˈdɪ.æk mə.ˈsɑːʒ/ (BrE)
an emergency procedure involving rhythmic compression of the heart to maintain blood circulation during cardiac arrest

Examples

Direct cardiac massage
During the operation, the surgeon initiated direct cardiac massage to stimulate the heart.
The doctor performed cardiac massage when the patient suffered sudden cardiac arrest.
Training in cardiac massage can be lifesaving in emergency situations.

  • synonym: cardiopulmonary resuscitation - an emergency procedure combining chest compressions with artificial ventilation
