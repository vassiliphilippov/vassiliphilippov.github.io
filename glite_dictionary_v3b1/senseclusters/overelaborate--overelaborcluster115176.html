overelaborate

adjective
too complicated
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvərɪˈlæbəˌreɪt / (BrE)
made too complicated or detailed, often more than necessary

Examples

Overelaborate instructions
The instructions were so overelaborate that nobody could follow them easily.
Her overelaborate speech made a simple idea seem confusing.
They decorated the room in an overelaborate style that felt overwhelming.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excessive - more than is necessary or reasonable
  • antonym: simple - easy to understand or not complicated
