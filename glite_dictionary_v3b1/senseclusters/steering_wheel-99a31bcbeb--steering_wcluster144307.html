steering wheel

noun
wheel for steering a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪrɪŋ wiːl / (AmE), / ˈstɪə.rɪŋ wiːl / (BrE)
A wheel in a vehicle that the driver turns to control the direction in which it moves.

Examples

The steering wheel turns
He gripped the steering wheel tightly as he navigated the icy road.
She decorated her steering wheel with a colorful cover.
The car's steering wheel was adjusted to fit the driver's height.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wheel - circular object that rotates on an axle and is used for movement or steering
