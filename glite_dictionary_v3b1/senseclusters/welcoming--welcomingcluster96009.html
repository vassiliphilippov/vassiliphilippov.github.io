welcoming

adjective
friendly and inviting; making others feel welcome
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (BrE)
Friendly or inviting; making someone feel welcome and comfortable.

Examples

A welcoming smile
Her welcoming smile instantly made the new students feel comfortable.
The host's welcoming attitude put everyone at ease during the party.
We were greeted by a welcoming fire in the cozy living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hospitable - friendly and generous to guests or visitors
  • antonym: unwelcoming - not friendly or making one feel not welcome
