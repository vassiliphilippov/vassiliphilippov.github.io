ball

noun
solid projectile for muskets
5.8
Pronunciation: / bɑl / (AmE), / bɔːl / (BrE)
a solid spherical projectile used as ammunition for firearms such as muskets and early rifles.

Examples

A musket ball
Each pouch contained several balls for quick reloading during battle.
The ramrod was used to push the ball firmly down the barrel of the gun.
The soldier loaded powder and a ball into his musket before taking aim.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bullet - a small metal projectile for modern firearms
