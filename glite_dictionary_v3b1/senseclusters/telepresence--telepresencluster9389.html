telepresence

noun
remote presence technology
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈtel.ə.prez.əns/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.prez.əns/ (AmE), /ˈtel.ə.pres.əns/ /; / /ˈtel.ɪ.pres.əns/ (BrE)
technology that enables a person to appear or interact as if present in a distant location, often using video or virtual reality

Examples

Telepresence technology
The new telepresence system made it feel like the manager was in the same room with us.
Doctors used telepresence to consult with patients in remote villages.
With telepresence, students can attend classes from anywhere in the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: videoconferencing - a meeting between people in different places using video technology
  • synonym: virtual reality - a computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional environment
