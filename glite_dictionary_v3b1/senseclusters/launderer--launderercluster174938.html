launderer

noun
person who washes clothes
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔndərər / (AmE), / ˈlɔːndərə / (BrE)
a person or business that washes and irons clothes for other people

Examples

Professional launderer
The launderer pressed my dress and returned it the next day.
We hired a launderer to take care of our uniforms every week.
A professional launderer can remove even the toughest stains from clothing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laundry - a place where clothes are washed and ironed
  • synonym: washerwoman - a woman whose job is washing clothes
