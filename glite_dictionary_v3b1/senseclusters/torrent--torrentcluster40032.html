torrent

noun
violent, fast-flowing stream of liquid
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːrənt / (AmE), / ˈtɒrənt / (BrE)
a strong and fast-moving stream of water or other liquid, especially one flowing suddenly or violently

Examples

A torrent of water
After the dam broke, a torrent swept away the bridge in seconds.
During the storm, a torrent of water rushed down the hillside and flooded the village.
He struggled to cross the torrent without being carried off by the powerful current.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flood - an overflowing of water beyond its normal confines
  • degree lower: trickle - a small, slow flow of liquid
