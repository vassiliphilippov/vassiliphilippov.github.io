Examples

Synonyms

  • mime
    5.0 0.23/m
    mime is a silent performance that copies gestures to entertain, while mimic can mean copying a person's manner to mock or amuse.
  • imitate
    2.7 12/m
    imitate is a neutral verb used to copy actions for learning or style, while mimic often implies copying to tease or entertain.
  • impersonate
    7.2 1.89/m
    impersonate is to act as another person, copying voice or look to trick or entertain, while mimic usually means copying a person's manner quickly.
  • ape
    5.8 0.74/m

  • copy
