still

adverb
continuing without change; up to now
1.0
Pronunciation: / stɪl / (AmE), / stɪl / (BrE)
continuing to happen or be the case up to and including the present or a particular time; without interruption or change

Examples

Are you still here
Are you still interested in going to the concert tomorrow night?
Despite the rain, the parade is still scheduled to proceed as planned.
It was midnight, but the lights in her office were still on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yet - up to now or a particular time; still, even now
