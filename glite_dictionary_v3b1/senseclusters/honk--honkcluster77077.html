honk

verb
make a loud, harsh sound like a horn
7.4
Pronunciation: / hɑŋk / (AmE), / hɒŋk / (BrE)
to make a loud, harsh sound with a horn (especially a car horn); to sound a horn

Examples

To honk the horn
Geese often honk loudly as they migrate across the sky.
Please don’t honk outside the hospital late at night.
The impatient driver honked his horn several times in the traffic jam.
Please don't honk your horn late at night; people are sleeping.
She honked several times when the car in front wouldn't move.
The driver honked as he turned the corner to warn pedestrians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beep - to make a short, high-pitched sound (often of a horn or electronic device)
  • synonym: blare - to make a loud, harsh sound, especially loudly announced or played
