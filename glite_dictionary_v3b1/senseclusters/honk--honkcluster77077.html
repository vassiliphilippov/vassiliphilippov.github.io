honk

verb
make a loud, harsh sound like a horn
7.4 1.4
Pronunciation: /hɔːŋk/ /; / /hɑːŋk/ (AmE), /hɒŋk/ (BrE)
to make a loud, harsh sound with a horn (especially a car horn); to sound a horn

Examples

Geese often honk loudly as they migrate across the sky.

She honked several times when the car in front wouldn't move.
The driver honked as he turned the corner to warn pedestrians.
