turnip plant

noun
a plant grown for its edible root
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.nɪp plænt / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.nɪp plɑːnt / (BrE)
any of several widely cultivated plants with edible swollen roots, especially the turnip (Brassica rapa) and related species.
