stirrer

noun
tool for stirring
Pronunciation: / ˈstɜrər / (AmE), / ˈstɜːrə / (BrE)
a utensil or tool used for stirring liquids or mixtures

Examples

A coffee stirrer
Plastic stirrers are often provided with takeaway drinks.
She used a stirrer to mix sugar into her coffee.
The bartender grabbed a metal stirrer to blend the cocktail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoon - a utensil consisting of a small shallow bowl on a handle, used for mixing or eating
