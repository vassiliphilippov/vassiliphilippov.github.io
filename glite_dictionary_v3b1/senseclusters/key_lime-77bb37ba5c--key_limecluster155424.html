key lime

noun
variety of small lime from Florida
Pronunciation: / kiː laɪm / (AmE), / kiː laɪm / (BrE)
a small, round yellow-green lime native to southern Florida, known for its tart flavor and commonly used in desserts

Examples

Fresh key lime juice
She used fresh key lime juice to make a traditional pie.
The tartness of key lime makes it perfect for summer drinks.
We found key lime trees growing in the Florida Keys.
