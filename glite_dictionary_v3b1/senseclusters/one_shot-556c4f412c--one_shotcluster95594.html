one shot

adjective
learn from a single example (ML)
Pronunciation: / w\u028cn \u0283\u0251t / (AmE), / w\u028cn \u0283\u0252t / (BrE)
(in machine learning) requiring or able to learn to recognise a new class or concept from a single example.

Examples

One shot learning
Researchers developed a one shot model that recognizes new faces from a single photograph.
In robotics, engineers prefer a one shot training method to reduce data-collection time.
Her thesis compared one shot learning with few-shot techniques on image-classification tasks.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: few-shot - learning methods that use a small number (more than one) of examples per class.
  • degree lower: zero-shot - methods that generalize to classes with no training examples.
