Australian chestnut

noun
Australian tree with chestnut-like seeds
1.3
Pronunciation: / əˈstreɪliən ˈtʃɛsnət / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪliən ˈtʃɛsnət / (BrE)
an Australian tree with pinnate leaves, orange-yellow flowers, and large woody pods containing edible seeds resembling chestnuts; valued for its dark, strong wood

Examples

An Australian chestnut tree
She planted an Australian chestnut in her garden to enjoy its shade and vibrant flowers.
The large pods of the Australian chestnut contain seeds that look similar to true chestnuts.
The timber from the Australian chestnut is prized for its durability in construction projects.
