transnational

adjective
involving multiple countries or nationalities
5.7
Pronunciation: / trænzˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / tranzˈnæʃnəl / (BrE)
involving or operating across several nations or among multiple nationalities

Examples

Transnational cooperation
Climate change requires transnational cooperation among governments and organizations worldwide.
The company expanded its operations to become a truly transnational enterprise.
They studied the growth of transnational crime networks over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic - relating to a single country; internal
  • synonym: international - existing, occurring, or carried on between nations
