melomaniac

noun
music enthusiast
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛləˈmeɪniæk / (AmE), / ˌmiːləˈmeɪniæk / (BrE)
a person who has an excessive enthusiasm or obsession for music

Examples

A melomaniac music lover
As a melomaniac, he attended every concert in the city.
Her friends called her a melomaniac because she collected thousands of records.
The melomaniac spent hours discussing his favorite composers with anyone who would listen.
