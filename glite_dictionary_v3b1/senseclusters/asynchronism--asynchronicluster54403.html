asynchronism

noun
lack of synchronization in timing
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪŋkrəˌnɪzəm / (AmE), / əˈsɪŋkrənɪzəm / (BrE)
the state or condition of not occurring at the same time or rate; lack of synchronization

Examples

Biological asynchronism
Biological clocks in different organisms can display asynchronism under varying light conditions.
Researchers noted significant asynchronism between the heartbeats and the patient’s breathing patterns.
The asynchronism of signals caused data transfer errors in the network.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asynchrony - the state of being asynchronous; lack of synchronization
  • antonym: synchronism - occurrence at the same time; synchronization
