wood block

noun
wooden printing block
4.8 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / wÊŠd blÉ‘k / (AmE), / wÊŠd blÉ’k / (BrE)
a block of wood with a design cut into its surface, used for printing images or patterns by pressing it onto a surface

Examples

A wood block print
Many traditional Japanese prints were made using wood blocks.
The artist carefully carved a design into the wood block for her new print.
The museum displayed an original wood block alongside the printed artwork.

Synonyms

  • woodblock
    4.0 0.29/m
    woodblock is the single-word name for a carved printing block used in art and craft, while wood block is the spaced variant.
  • woodcut
    6.5 1.13/m
    woodcut is the print made from a carved block or sometimes the carved block itself, while wood block names the carved printing block.
