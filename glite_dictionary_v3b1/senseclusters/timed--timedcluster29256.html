timed

adjective
occurring at regular time intervals
Pronunciation: / taɪmd / (AmE), / taɪmd / (BrE)
occurring at regular intervals; having actions or events arranged so that they happen at specific, evenly spaced times

Examples

A timed event
The timed release of fertilizer ensures the plants receive nutrients steadily.
The medication is administered in timed doses throughout the day.
They practiced shooting at timed intervals to improve their performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irregular - not occurring at regular intervals
  • synonym: periodic - happening at regular intervals
