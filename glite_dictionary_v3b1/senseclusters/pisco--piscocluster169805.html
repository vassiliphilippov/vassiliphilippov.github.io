pisco

proper noun
river in Peru
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpiskoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpiːskəʊ / (BrE)
a river in the Ica Region of Peru that flows into the Pacific Ocean near the city of Pisco

Examples

Pisco river valley
The Pisco provides water for agriculture in the surrounding valleys.
Many migratory birds stop along the Pisco during their journey along the coast.
Local communities celebrate festivals near the banks of the Pisco every year.
