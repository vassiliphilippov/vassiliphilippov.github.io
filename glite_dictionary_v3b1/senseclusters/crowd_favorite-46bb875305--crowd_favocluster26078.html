crowd favorite

noun
person or thing widely liked by many
Pronunciation: / kraʊd ˈfeɪvərət / (AmE), / kraʊd ˈfeɪvərɪt / (BrE)
A person or thing that is very popular with a large group of people.

Examples

A crowd favorite performer
During the talent show, her performance was an obvious crowd favorite.
The new restaurant quickly became a crowd favorite because of its delicious menu.
The veteran player remains a crowd favorite despite the arrival of younger stars.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan favorite - a person or thing much loved by fans
