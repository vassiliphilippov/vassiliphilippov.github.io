residence

noun
large and imposing house
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzɪdəns / (AmE), / ˈrɛzɪdəns / (BrE)
a large or official house, especially for an important person such as a president or governor

The ambassador hosted the dinner at his residence in the capital.
