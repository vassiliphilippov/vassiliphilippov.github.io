commonplace

adjective
ordinary; unremarkable
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmənˌpleɪs /; / ˈkɑː.mənˌpleɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɒmənˌpleɪs /; / ˈkɒm.ənˌpleɪs / (BrE)
ordinary, not special or original; usual or unremarkable

Examples

Commonplace event
For many people, remote work has become commonplace in recent years.

The speech was full of commonplace expressions that failed to inspire the audience.
