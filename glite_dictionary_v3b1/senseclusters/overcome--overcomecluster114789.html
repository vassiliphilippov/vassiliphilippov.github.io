overcome

verb
be overwhelmed by emotions or sensations
2.5 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈkʌm /; / ˌoʊvərˈkʌm / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkʌm / (BrE)
to be strongly affected or overwhelmed by emotions, sensations, or forces, so that you cannot resist or control them

Examples

Overcome with emotion
He overcame with grief when he heard the sad news.
I was completely overcome by the beauty of the sunset.
She was overcome with emotion as she accepted the award.
Even the strongest athletes can be overcome by exhaustion in extreme conditions.
He tried to stay calm, but was overcome with emotion during the ceremony.
She was overcome by grief after hearing the sad news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overwhelm - to overpower emotionally or physically
  • antonym: resist - to withstand the action or effect of something
  • synonym: succumb - to fail to resist pressure or temptation
