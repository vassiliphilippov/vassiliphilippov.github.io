frontal

adjective
located at or relating to the front
3.9 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈfrʌn.təl/ (AmE), /ˈfrʌn.təl/ (BrE)
relating to the front part of something, the forehead, or involving direct contact from the front

Examples

Frontal bone
Several animals have frontal appendages used for sensing their environment.
The frontal lobe of the brain is responsible for decision making and problem solving.
The museum displayed a statue with a frontal view that emphasized the face.
Doctors observed swelling in the frontal region near the patient’s forehead.
She suffered a cut on her frontal bone after the accident.
The MRI scan showed an abnormality in the frontal lobe of the brain.
Doctors warned that a frontal impact at high speed is extremely dangerous.
The army launched a frontal assault on the enemy's main entrance.
The two cars were involved in a frontal collision on the highway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: head-on - directly involving front-to-front impact or confrontation
  • antonym: parietal - relating to the parietal bone or the upper sides of the skull
  • antonym: posterior - situated at the back part
