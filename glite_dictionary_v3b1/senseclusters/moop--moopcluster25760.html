moop

verb
to clean up trash
Pronunciation: / muːp / (AmE), / muːp / (BrE)
to pick up and remove trash or debris left in a public place, especially after an event.

Examples

To moop the park
After the parade, volunteers gathered to moop the park and leave it spotless.
Everyone was asked to moop their campsite before leaving the festival grounds.
The organizers encouraged attendees to moop so the beach would stay clean for everyone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: litter - to leave trash or waste in a public place
