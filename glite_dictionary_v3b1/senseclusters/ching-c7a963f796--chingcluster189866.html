Ch'ing

proper noun
last imperial Chinese dynasty (1644–1912)
Pronunciation: / tʃɪŋ / (AmE), / tʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the last imperial dynasty of China, ruling from 1644 to 1912, established by the Manchu people.

Examples

The Ch'ing dynasty
Artists during the Ch'ing period developed distinctive styles that influenced Chinese culture.
The Ch'ing dynasty was marked by both territorial expansion and internal strife.
The collapse of the Ch'ing dynasty ushered in a new era for China.
