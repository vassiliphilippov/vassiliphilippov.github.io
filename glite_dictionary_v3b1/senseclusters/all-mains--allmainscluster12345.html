all-mains

adjective
adaptable to all mains voltages (electronics)
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔl ˌmeɪnz / (AmE), / ˈɔːl ˌmeɪnz / (BrE)
(of electrical devices, especially radio receivers) designed to operate on any standard mains voltage specification.

Examples

All-mains electrical device
Collectors often look for all-mains radios because of their versatility with power sources.
Many British radios from the mid-20th century were marketed as all-mains sets for convenience.
The old radio in my grandfather's study is an all-mains model that works wherever you plug it in.
