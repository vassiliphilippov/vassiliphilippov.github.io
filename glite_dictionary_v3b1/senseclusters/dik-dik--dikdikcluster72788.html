dik-dik

noun
a small African antelope (genus Madoqua)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪkˌdɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɪkˌdɪk / (BrE)
a small antelope of eastern Africa, of the genus Madoqua, noted for its diminutive size and elongated snout

Examples

A dik-dik antelope
A dik-dik darted across the dry savanna, blending perfectly with the tall grasses.
During their safari, they spotted several dik-diks near the waterhole.
Unlike larger antelopes, the dik-dik relies on its agility and camouflage to avoid predators.
