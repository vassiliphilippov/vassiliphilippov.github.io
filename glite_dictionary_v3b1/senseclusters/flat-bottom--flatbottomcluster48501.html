flat-bottom

adjective
level base
Pronunciation: / ˈflætˌbɑtəm / (AmE), / ˈflætˌbɒtəm / (BrE)
having a base or bottom that is level, even, and not curved or rounded

Examples

Flat-bottom pan
The flat-bottom pan is perfect for making pancakes on the stove.
A flat-bottom boat is ideal for navigating shallow waters.
He bought a flat-bottom container to store his tools safely.

Related Terms

  • antonym: curved - having a rounded or bent shape
  • synonym: flat - having a smooth, even surface
