eval

verb
to assess quickly
Pronunciation: / ˈiːvæl / (AmE), / ˈiːvæl / (BrE)
to judge, assess, or analyze something, especially in a quick or informal way

Examples

To eval a problem
Can you eval this math problem before class ends?
The coach asked me to eval the new player's performance during the game.
Before we proceed, let's eval the risks involved in this project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: analyze - to examine something in detail to understand it better
  • synonym: evaluate - to judge or assess the value or quality of something
  • antonym: ignore - to pay no attention to something
