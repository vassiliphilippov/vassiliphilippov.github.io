Arabize

verb
to make Arabic
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈærəˌbaɪz / (AmE), / ˈærəˌbaɪz / (BrE)
to make something or someone adopt the Arabic language, customs, or culture

Examples

To Arabize a country
The new policy aimed to Arabize the education system by introducing Arabic as the main language of instruction.
Many local names were Arabized after the country gained independence.
Some communities resisted efforts to Arabize their traditional customs and language.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assimilate - to cause to become similar to a different culture or group
  • antonym: westernize - to make something adopt Western customs or culture
