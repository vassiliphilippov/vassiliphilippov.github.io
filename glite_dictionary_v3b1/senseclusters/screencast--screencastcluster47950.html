screencast

noun
video of screen activity
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈskriːnkæst / (AmE), / ˈskriːnkɑːst / (BrE)
a digital video recording of computer screen activity, often with spoken explanation or narration

Examples

A screencast tutorial
The instructor created a screencast to demonstrate how to use the new software.
You can follow the screencast step by step to complete the installation process.
Many online courses include at least one screencast to help students understand complex tasks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tutorial - a lesson or guide that teaches how to do something
  • synonym: webinar - an online seminar or presentation
