breathiness

noun
airy vocal quality
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛθinəs / (AmE), / ˈbrɛθinəs / (BrE)
a soft, airy quality in a person's voice or in a sound, often caused by audible breath

Examples

Soft breathiness in voice
The breathiness in his singing gave the performance a gentle, intimate feeling.
Some microphones can enhance the breathiness of a singer's voice, making it sound softer.
She noticed a slight breathiness in her speech after recovering from a cold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airiness - a light, open, or airy quality
  • antonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to hear
  • antonym: hoarseness - a rough or harsh quality in the voice
