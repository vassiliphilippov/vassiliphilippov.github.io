halfling

noun
small fantasy creature
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhæf.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːf.lɪŋ / (BrE)
a small, human-like creature in fantasy stories, often resembling a hobbit and known for agility and stealth

Examples

A halfling adventurer
The halfling snuck quietly through the forest, careful not to make a sound.

Legends say that a clever halfling once outwitted a powerful dragon.
The halfling used her quick reflexes to escape the trap in the dungeon.
In the game, a halfling character is known for being light on their feet.
Legends say that a clever halfling once outwitted a powerful wizard.
