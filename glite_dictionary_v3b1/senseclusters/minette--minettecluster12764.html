minette

noun
lime-rich iron ore
Pronunciation: / mɪˈnɛt / (AmE), / mɪˈnɛt / (BrE)
a type of iron ore rich in lime, used in steelmaking

Examples

Minette iron ore
The steel plant imported minette to improve the quality of its products.
Geologists discovered a large deposit of minette in the region.
Because of its lime content, minette is especially valued in certain steelmaking processes.
