tenfold

adverb
by ten times as much
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈtɛnˌfəʊld / (BrE)
By ten times as much; increased or multiplied by ten.

Examples

Grew tenfold
Her investment returned tenfold after only five years.
Profits have increased tenfold since we launched the new product.
The number of tourists grew tenfold over the last decade.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hundredfold - by one hundred times as much
  • synonym: ten times - multiple of ten; by ten times as much
  • degree lower: twofold - by two times as much
