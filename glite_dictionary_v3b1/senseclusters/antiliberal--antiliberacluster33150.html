antiliberal

adjective
opposed to liberalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈlɪbərəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈlɪbərəl / (BrE)
opposed to liberal principles, policies, or attitudes

Examples

Antiliberal policies
The new law was criticized as antiliberal by human rights organizations.
Many voters were concerned about the party's increasingly antiliberal rhetoric.
His antiliberal views put him at odds with much of the academic community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: liberal - supporting individual freedoms and progressive policies
