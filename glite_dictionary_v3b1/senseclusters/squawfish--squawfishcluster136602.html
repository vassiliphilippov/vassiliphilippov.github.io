squawfish

noun
North American pikeminnow
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑːˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈskwɔːˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a large freshwater fish of the genus Ptychocheilus, native to rivers of western North America, now usually called pikeminnow; the term 'squawfish' is considered offensive and outdated
