gracioso

noun
comic servant character
Pronunciation: / ɡrɑːˈsjoʊsoʊ / (AmE), / ɡraˈθjəʊsəʊ / (BrE)
a comic servant or jester character in Spanish Golden Age theatre who provides humor, commentary, and satire

Examples

Theatre gracioso
The gracioso delighted the audience with clever jokes and witty observations throughout the play.
In many Spanish comedies, the gracioso serves as both a servant and a source of satire.
The actor was praised for his energetic portrayal of the gracioso in the classic production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clown - a comic entertainer who amuses others by jokes and antics
  • synonym: jester - a professional joker or fool in a royal court
  • antonym: tragedian - an actor who specializes in tragic roles
