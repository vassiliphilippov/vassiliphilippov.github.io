long distance

noun
telephone call outside local area
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ ˈdɪstəns / (AmE), / lɒŋ ˈdɪstəns / (BrE)
a telephone call made to a place outside the local calling area, usually requiring an extra charge

Examples

A long distance call
I had to pay extra for the long distance to New York.
Making a long distance was expensive before cell phones and internet calls.
She received a long distance from her cousin overseas last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: local call - a telephone call within a local calling area
