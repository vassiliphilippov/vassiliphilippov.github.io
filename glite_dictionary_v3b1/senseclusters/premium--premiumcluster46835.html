premium

noun
insurance payment
4.6 13/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆpriËmiÉ™m / (AmE), / ËˆpriËmiÉ™m / (BrE)

Examples

Insurance premium
If you fail to pay your car insurance premium, your coverage may lapse.

The company raised the premium on its home insurance policies after the recent floods.
