premium

noun
insurance payment
4.6 15/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpriÀêmi…ôm / (AmE), / ÀàpriÀêmi…ôm / (BrE)
a regular payment made to an insurance company for coverage against loss, damage, illness, etc.

Examples

Insurance premium
If you fail to pay your car insurance premium, your coverage may lapse.
She pays a high premium every month to keep her health insurance active.
The company raised the premium on its home insurance policies after the recent floods.
