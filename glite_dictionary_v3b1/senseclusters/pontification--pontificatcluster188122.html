pontification

noun
church ceremony
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the performance of official duties or ceremonies by a high-ranking church official, especially a bishop or pope

Examples

Ceremonial pontification
The pontification was attended by clergy from across the country.
During the annual pontification, the bishop blessed the congregation.
Many people traveled to witness the grand pontification at the historic cathedral.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consecration - the act of making something sacred, especially in a religious ceremony
  • synonym: ordination - the ceremony of conferring holy orders on someone
