short

adverb
curtly; abruptly or brusquely
Pronunciation: / ʃɔrt / (AmE), / ʃɔːt / (BrE)
In a curt, abrupt, or discourteous manner; without politeness or ceremony.

Examples

Speak short to people
He replied short to the reporter's question and walked away.
If you speak short to your coworkers, they may think you are upset with them.
She answered short when asked about her plans, making it clear she didn't want to discuss it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abruptly - suddenly and unexpectedly, often with rudeness
  • synonym: curtly - in a rudely brief or abrupt manner
  • antonym: politely - in a respectful and considerate manner
