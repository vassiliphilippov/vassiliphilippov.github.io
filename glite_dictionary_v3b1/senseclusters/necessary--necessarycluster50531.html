Synonyms

  • indispensable
    4.6 6.29/m
    indispensable means absolutely essential and cannot be done without, while necessary describes something required or unavoidable.
  • vital
    3.5 34/m
    vital means crucial for life or success, while necessary is a more general word for what is needed.
  • requisite
    6.2 4.24/m
    requisite is a formal word for what is needed for a particular purpose, while necessary is more general.
  • required
    1.4 0.00/m
    required is often set by a rule or authority, while necessary simply means needed or unavoidable.
  • needed
    1.0 0.00/m
    needed is the everyday word for something wanted to achieve a result, while necessary sounds more formal and broad.
