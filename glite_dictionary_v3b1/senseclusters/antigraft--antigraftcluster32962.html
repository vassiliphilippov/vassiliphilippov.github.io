antigraft

adjective
opposed to graft
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈɡræft / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈɡrɑːft / (BrE)
opposed to graft, especially corruption or illegal practices, or against tissue transplantation in medicine

Examples

Antigraft laws
The government launched an antigraft initiative to tackle bribery in public offices.
Strict antigraft measures were introduced to ensure fair business practices.
The hospital adopted new antigraft protocols to prevent tissue rejection during transplants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticorruption - opposed to corruption or dishonest practices
  • antonym: pro-graft - supporting or allowing graft or corruption
