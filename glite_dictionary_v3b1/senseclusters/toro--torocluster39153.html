toro

noun
fatty tuna cut
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊroʊ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːrəʊ / (BrE)
a cut of fatty tuna, prized in Japanese cuisine and often served as sushi or sashimi

Examples

Sushi toro slice
He ordered toro at the sushi bar because he loves the rich, buttery flavor.
The chef recommended trying toro sashimi for its delicate texture and taste.
Fresh toro is considered a delicacy and is often the most expensive item on the menu.

Related Terms

  • antonym: akami - the leaner, red part of tuna used in sushi
  • synonym: maguro - the Japanese term for tuna, especially used in sushi
