immigrant

noun
person who moves to another country to live
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪmɪˌɡrənt / (AmE), / ˈɪmɪɡrənt / (BrE)
a person who comes to live permanently in a country other than where they were born

Examples

An immigrant to Canada
After years of paperwork, she finally became an immigrant to Canada.
Many immigrants face challenges adapting to a new culture and language.
The city is known for its diverse immigrant communities.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emigrant - a person who leaves their own country to settle in another
  • synonym: migrant - a person who moves from one region or country to another, especially for work
