withdrawer

noun
student who leaves school or university early
Pronunciation: / wɪðˈdrɔɚ / (AmE), / wɪðˈdrɔːə / (BrE)
a student who leaves an educational institution before completing their studies

Examples

Student withdrawer
After facing financial difficulties, the withdrawer decided to return home and work full-time.
Being a withdrawer does not mean you cannot return to education later in life.
The university tracks the number of withdrawers each semester to improve student retention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dropout - a person who leaves school or college before completing a course of study
