witch's broomstick

noun
mythical broom ridden by witches
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtʃɪz ˈbruːmstɪk / (AmE), / ˈwɪtʃɪz ˈbruːmstɪk / (BrE)
a broom, especially in folklore, said to be ridden by witches to fly through the air

Examples

A witch's broomstick
At Halloween, many decorations show a witch's broomstick silhouetted against the moon.
She dressed as a witch, complete with a pointed hat and a witch's broomstick.
The legend says that a witch's broomstick can carry its rider across the night sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: besom - a type of broom, often associated with witchcraft in folklore
