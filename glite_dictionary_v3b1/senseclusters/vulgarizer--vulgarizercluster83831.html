vulgarizer

noun
someone who simplifies or popularizes
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈvʌl.gə.raɪ.zər/ (AmE), /ˈvʌl.gə.raɪ.zə/ (BrE)
a person who makes something more accessible or popular, often by simplifying or adapting it for the general public

Examples

Science vulgarizer
As a skilled vulgarizer, she made scientific concepts understandable to children and adults alike.
Some critics accused the author of being a vulgarizer who reduced complex ideas to simple slogans.
The documentary's success was due largely to its host, a renowned vulgarizer of history.
