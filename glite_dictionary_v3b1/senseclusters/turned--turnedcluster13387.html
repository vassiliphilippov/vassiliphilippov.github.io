turned

adjective
of food: become sour or spoiled
Pronunciation: / tɝːnd / (AmE), / tɜːnd / (BrE)
(of food or drink) having become sour, spoiled, or unpalatable.

Examples

The milk turned sour
Don’t drink that juice; it has already turned.
I tasted the milk and realized it had turned overnight.
The cheese smelled strange because it was turned.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fresh - not spoiled, recently made or obtained; opposite of 'turned' (when referring to food)
  • synonym: spoiled - having become unfit for eating, especially due to decay or souring
