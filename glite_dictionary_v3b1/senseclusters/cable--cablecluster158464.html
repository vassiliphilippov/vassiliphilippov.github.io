cable

noun
television service transmitted via cable
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.bəl /; / ˈkeɪbəl / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.bəl /; / ˈkeɪbəl / (BrE)
a television system in which programs are sent through cables directly to subscribers' televisions (cable TV)

Examples

Cable TV channels
Many sports channels are only available on cable rather than regular TV.
She spends her evenings watching old movies on cable.
We decided to cancel our cable service to save money.
Many people subscribe to cable for a wider variety of channels.
They decided to cancel their cable because it was too expensive.
With the rise of streaming services, fewer households rely on cable today.
