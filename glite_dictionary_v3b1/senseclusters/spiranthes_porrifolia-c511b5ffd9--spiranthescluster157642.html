Spiranthes porrifolia

noun
a species of orchid native to western North America
Pronunciation: / spaɪˈrænθiz ˌpɔrɪˈfoʊliə / (AmE), / spaɪˈrænθiːz ˌpɔːrɪˈfəʊlɪə / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to western North America, known for its spiral arrangement of small white flowers.
