Identikit

noun
composite face image for police identification
9.7 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.den.tɪ.kɪt/ /; / /aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kɪt/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.den.tɪ.kɪt/ /; / /aɪ.ˈden.tɪ.kɪt/ (BrE)
