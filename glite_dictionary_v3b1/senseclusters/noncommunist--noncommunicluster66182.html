noncommunist

adjective
not communist
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈkɑːmjənɪst / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈkɒmjʊnɪst / (BrE)
not supporting, practicing, or relating to communism or communist principles

Examples

Noncommunist government
The government formed a noncommunist coalition to oppose the left-wing parties.
Many countries in the region adopted noncommunist policies after the war.
He joined a noncommunist organization that promoted free-market reforms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticommunist - opposed to communism
  • antonym: communist - supporting or relating to communism
