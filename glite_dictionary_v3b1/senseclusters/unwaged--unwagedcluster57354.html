unwaged

noun
people without paid work
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪdʒd / (BrE)
people who do not receive wages or are not in paid employment, considered as a group

Examples

The unwaged group
After the factory closed, many of the unwaged struggled to find new jobs.
Government programs aim to support the unwaged during periods of high unemployment.
The charity provides food assistance to the unwaged in the local community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: employed - people who have paid jobs
  • synonym: unemployed - people without jobs or paid work
