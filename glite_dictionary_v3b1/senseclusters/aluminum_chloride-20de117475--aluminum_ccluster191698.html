aluminum chloride

noun
chemical compound used as a catalyst
4.5
Pronunciation: / əˈluːmɪnəm klaɪˈraɪd /; / əˌluːmɪnəm ˈklɔrˌaɪd / (AmE), / ˌaljʊˈmɪnɪəm klaɪˈraɪd /; / ˌaljʊˈmɪniəm ˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
a white or yellowish crystalline chemical compound (AlCl₃) used as a catalyst in chemical reactions and as a wood preservative
