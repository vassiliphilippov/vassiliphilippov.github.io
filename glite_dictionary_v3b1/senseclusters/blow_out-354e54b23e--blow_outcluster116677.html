blow out

verb
(of well) erupt forcefully and uncontrollably
5.2 6.5
Pronunciation: /bloʊ aʊt/ /; / /bləʊ aʊt/ (AmE), /bləʊ aʊt/ /; / /bloʊ aʊt/ (BrE)
(of a well, especially an oil or gas well) to erupt forcefully and uncontrollably, typically releasing gas, oil, or water.

Examples

The oil well blows out
Experts rushed to contain the gas well after it blew out during drilling operations.
If an oil well blows out, it can cause significant environmental damage.
The old oil rig suddenly blew out, spilling crude into the ocean.
