Parsi

noun
member of Indian Zoroastrian community
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrsi / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsi / (BrE)
A member of a Zoroastrian religious community originally from Persia, now primarily found in western India.

Examples

A Parsi family
Many Parsi families settled in Mumbai after fleeing religious persecution in Persia.
The Parsi community is known for its contributions to business and philanthropy in India.
Traditional Parsi cuisine features dishes like dhansak and patra ni machhi.
