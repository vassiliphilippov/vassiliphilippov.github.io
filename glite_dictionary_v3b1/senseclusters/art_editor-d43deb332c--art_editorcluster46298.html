art editor

noun
editor for illustrations and layouts
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrt ˈɛdɪtər / (AmE), / ɑːt ˈɛdɪtə / (BrE)
an editor responsible for the selection and arrangement of illustrations and layouts in printed publications

Examples

The art editor role
As the art editor, she coordinated the magazine's new visual style.
He was promoted to art editor after years of designing book layouts.
The art editor worked closely with photographers and graphic designers to create a unified look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: editor - a person who is in charge of and determines the final content of a publication
