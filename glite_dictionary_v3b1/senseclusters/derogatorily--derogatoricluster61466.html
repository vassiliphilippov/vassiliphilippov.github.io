derogatorily

adverb
disrespectfully
7.2
Pronunciation: / dɪˈrɑːɡəˌtɔrəli / (AmE), / dɪˈrɒɡəˌtɔːrɪli / (BrE)
in a manner that expresses disrespect or criticism toward someone or something

Examples

To speak derogatorily
The article referred derogatorily to the politician's past mistakes.
She spoke derogatorily about the new policy during the meeting.
Comments made derogatorily about others can damage relationships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disparagingly - in a way that belittles or criticizes
  • antonym: respectfully - in a way that shows respect
