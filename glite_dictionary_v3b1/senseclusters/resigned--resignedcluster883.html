resigned

adjective
no longer in position
4.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaɪnd / (AmE), / rɪˈzaɪnd / (BrE)
having left a job or official position by formally giving notice; no longer holding that role or employment

Examples

Resigned employee
After the scandal, the resigned minister avoided public appearances for several months.
The company quickly appointed a replacement for the resigned CEO.
A resigned employee must return all company property before leaving.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dismissed - having been removed from a job or position
  • synonym: retired - having stopped working, usually due to age
