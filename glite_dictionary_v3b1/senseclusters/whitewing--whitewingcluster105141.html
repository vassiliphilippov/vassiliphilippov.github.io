whitewing

noun
bird or insect with white wings
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪtˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwaɪtˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
a bird or insect that has noticeable white markings on its wings

Examples

A whitewing bird
A whitewing fluttered past the window in the early morning.
The children watched as a whitewing landed gently on the garden fence.
Many people enjoy spotting the rare whitewing during their nature walks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mourning dove - a type of dove sometimes called whitewing for its pale wing markings
  • synonym: white butterfly - a butterfly with mostly white wings
