crisscross

adverb
crossing in opposite directions
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪsˌkrɔs / (AmE), / ˈkrɪs.krɒs / (BrE)
in a way that crosses or moves in different directions over one another, especially forming a pattern of intersecting lines or paths

Examples

Paths crisscross easily
The narrow paths crisscross through the park, making it easy to explore every corner.
Their journeys crisscross across Europe, never staying in one place for long.
Wires crisscross overhead, creating a tangled web above the street.

  • synonym: intersect - to cross or pass through each other
