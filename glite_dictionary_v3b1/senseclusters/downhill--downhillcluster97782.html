downhill

noun
high-speed ski race down a slope
7.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈdaʊn.hɪl/ (AmE), /ˈdaʊn.hɪl/ (BrE)
A ski race in which competitors race down a slope, usually at high speed, following a set course.

Examples

Olympic downhill race
He won the gold medal in the Olympic downhill last year.
She trained for months to improve her speed in the downhill.
The downhill is considered one of the most challenging events in alpine skiing.
