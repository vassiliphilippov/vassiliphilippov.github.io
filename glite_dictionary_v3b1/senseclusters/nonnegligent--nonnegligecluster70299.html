nonnegligent

adjective
not legally at fault
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt / (AmE), / nɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt / (BrE)
not negligent; showing proper care or attention

The inspector confirmed that the staff had been nonnegligent in following all safety protocols.
A nonnegligent attitude is essential when handling sensitive information at work.
The court ruled that the company was nonnegligent in maintaining its equipment.
