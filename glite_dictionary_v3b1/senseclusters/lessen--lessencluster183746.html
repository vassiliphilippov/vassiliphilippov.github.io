lessen

verb
make or become smaller
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛs.ən / (AmE), / ˈlɛs.ən / (BrE)
to make or become less in amount, degree, intensity, or severity

Examples

To lessen the impact
New regulations are intended to lessen the environmental impact of factories.
Nothing seemed to lessen his anxiety before the exam.
The pain began to lessen after she took the medication.
After the rain stopped, the floodwaters gradually lessened and the streets became passable.
Taking deep breaths helped to lessen her anxiety before the exam.
The government introduced new measures to lessen air pollution in the city.
Her fear did not lessen after hearing the news.
Over time, the swelling will lessen as your body heals.
The pain finally began to lessen after I took some medicine.
