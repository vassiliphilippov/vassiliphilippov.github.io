Republic of Kazakhstan

proper noun
the official name of Kazakhstan (Central Asian country)
Pronunciation: / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæzəkˈstæn / (AmE), / rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ˌkæzækˈstɑːn / (BrE)
The official name for Kazakhstan, a landlocked country in Central Asia, south of Russia and northeast of the Caspian Sea.
