sweltering

adjective
extremely hot and humid
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛl.tɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɛl.tər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
Uncomfortably hot and humid; oppressively warm, often causing sweating and discomfort.

Examples

A sweltering day
After a few minutes in the sweltering kitchen, she was dripping with sweat.
The stadium was packed with fans despite the sweltering heat.
We tried to find some shade on that sweltering summer afternoon.
