go on one's merry way

verb
continue cheerfully without concern
8.1 5.6
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ ɑn wʌnz ˈmɛri weɪ / (AmE), / ɡəʊ ɒn wʌnz ˈmɛri weɪ / (BrE)
to continue on one's path or activity happily and without concern for possible problems or consequences.
