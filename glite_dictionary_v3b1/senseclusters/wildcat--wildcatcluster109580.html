wildcat

verb
to strike without approval
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪldˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈwaɪldˌkæt / (BrE)
to take part in an unauthorized strike or stop work without official union approval

Examples

To wildcat at work
The workers decided to wildcat after their demands for safer conditions were ignored.
Management was surprised when employees began wildcatting without any warning from the union.
If you wildcat, you risk disciplinary action because the strike is not officially approved.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boycott - to refuse to take part in something as a protest
  • antonym: comply - to act in accordance with rules or requests
  • synonym: strike - to stop working as a form of protest
