multicoil

noun
device with multiple coils
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌkɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌkɔɪl / (BrE)
a device or component containing more than one coil, used in electrical or magnetic systems

Examples

A multicoil device
The engineer designed a multicoil to improve the efficiency of the transformer.
A faulty multicoil can cause problems in the magnetic field of the device.
The new guitar pickup uses a multicoil for a richer sound.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coil - a loop or series of loops of wire, often used to produce a magnetic field
  • synonym: solenoid - a coil of wire that acts as a magnet when carrying electric current
