embolic

adjective
pertaining to an embolism or embolus
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbɑːlɪk / (AmE), / ɛmˈbɒlɪk / (BrE)
relating to, caused by, or characteristic of an embolism or embolus.

Examples

Embolic stroke
Doctors observed embolic fragments blocking several arteries during the procedure.
He was treated with anticoagulants to reduce the risk of embolic events.
The patient suffered an embolic stroke due to a blood clot traveling to the brain.
