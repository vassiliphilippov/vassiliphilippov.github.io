disappointed

adjective
feeling unhappy due to unmet hopes or expectations
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
unhappy because something you hoped for or expected did not happen or was unsuccessful

Examples

Feel disappointed with results
His parents were clearly disappointed with his poor exam results.
Many fans left the stadium feeling disappointed after the team's unexpected loss.
She was disappointed when she didn't get the promotion she had hoped for.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frustrated - feeling annoyed and disappointed because you cannot achieve what you want
  • antonym: satisfied - pleased because you have achieved what you wanted
