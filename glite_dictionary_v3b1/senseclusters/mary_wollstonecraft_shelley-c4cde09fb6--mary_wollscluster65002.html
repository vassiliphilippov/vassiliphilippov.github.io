Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley

proper noun
English author of 'Frankenstein'
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛri ˈwʊlstənkræft ˈʃɛli / (AmE), / ˈmeəri ˈwʌlstənkrɑːft ˈʃɛli / (BrE)
(1797–1851) English writer best known as the author of 'Frankenstein,' and wife of the poet Percy Bysshe Shelley.
