lower jaw

noun
bottom part of the jaw
2.2 0.51/m
Pronunciation: / Ààlo ä.…ôr d í…î / (AmE), / Ààl…ô ä.…ô d í…îÀê / (BrE)
the bottom part of the jaw in vertebrates, hinged to open and close the mouth

Examples

The lower jaw
He pressed his lower jaw forward in determination.
Some animals can dislocate their lower jaw to swallow large prey.
The dentist noticed a crack in my lower jaw during the examination.
