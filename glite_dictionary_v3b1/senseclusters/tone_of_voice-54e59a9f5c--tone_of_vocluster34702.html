tone of voice

noun
quality or character of a person's voice
Pronunciation: / toʊn əv vɔɪs / (AmE), / təʊn əv vɔɪs / (BrE)
the quality or character of a person's voice as expressed in speech, which can convey emotion, attitude, or mood

Examples

Angry tone of voice
Her angry tone of voice made it clear she was upset.
Try to keep your tone of voice calm during the discussion.
You can tell a lot about someone's mood from their tone of voice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tone - the general character or attitude of a sound or piece of music
