jupaty

noun
tall Brazilian feather palm
Pronunciation: / huːˈpɑːti / (AmE), / huːˈpæti / (BrE)
a tall Brazilian feather palm with a terminal crown of very large, leathery, pinnately divided leaves, whose long, strong stems are used for structural purposes

Examples

The jupaty palm
Large leaves from the jupaty provide shade and are sometimes woven into roofing thatch.
Researchers studied the jupaty to learn more about sustainable construction materials in tropical regions.
The jupaty is highly valued in rural Brazil for building shelters and fences.
