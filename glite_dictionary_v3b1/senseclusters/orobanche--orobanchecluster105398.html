orobanche

noun
parasitic plant genus
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːrəˈbæŋki / (AmE), / ˌɒrəˈbæŋki / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic plants, commonly called broomrapes, that attach to the roots of other plants to obtain nutrients

Examples

The orobanche plant
The orobanche can severely damage crops by feeding on their roots.
Scientists study orobanche to find ways to protect agricultural plants from parasitic threats.
Farmers are concerned about the spread of orobanche in their fields.
