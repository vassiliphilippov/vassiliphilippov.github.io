novillada

noun
bullfight with young bulls
Pronunciation: / noʊ.viˈjɑː.də / (AmE), / nɒ.vɪˈjɑː.də / (BrE)
a bullfight in which the bulls are less than four years old, often featuring less experienced bullfighters

Examples

A novillada bullfight
During the novillada, the young bull charged unexpectedly, catching the matador off guard.
Many Spanish towns host a novillada as part of their cultural celebrations.
The annual festival included a novillada for aspiring bullfighters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrida - a traditional Spanish bullfight
