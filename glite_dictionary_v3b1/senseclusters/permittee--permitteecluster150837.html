permittee

noun
holder of a permit
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜːrmɪˈtiː / (AmE), / ˌpɜːmɪˈtiː / (BrE)
a person or organization that has been officially given a permit to do something

Examples

The permittee follows rules
Each permittee must follow the conditions of the license.
The permittee is responsible for ensuring all regulations are met during construction.
Only the permittee may access the restricted area under the terms of the permit.
The permittee is responsible for ensuring all construction follows local safety codes.
If the permittee violates the terms, the permit may be revoked by the authorities.
Each permittee must display their permit at the work site at all times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: licensee - a person or company that has been given official permission to do something
