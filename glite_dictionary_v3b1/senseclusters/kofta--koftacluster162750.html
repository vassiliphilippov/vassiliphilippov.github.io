kofta

noun
spiced meat balls
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊftə / (AmE), / ˈkɒftə / (BrE)
a dish of spiced ground meat shaped into balls or patties, popular in South Asian and Middle Eastern cuisine

Examples

Spicy kofta balls
We enjoyed delicious kofta served with a spicy tomato sauce at the new restaurant.
My grandmother taught me how to make lamb kofta using her traditional recipe.
Vegetarian kofta made with paneer is a popular option in Indian cuisine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kebab - pieces of meat or vegetables grilled on a skewer
  • synonym: meatball - a ball of ground meat, often mixed with other ingredients
