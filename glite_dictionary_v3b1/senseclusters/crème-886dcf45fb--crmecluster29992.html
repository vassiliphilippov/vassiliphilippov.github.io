crème

noun
rich, thick substance
3.9
Pronunciation: / krɛm / (AmE), / krɛm / (BrE)
a smooth, thick substance used in desserts or as a cosmetic, often rich and creamy in texture

Examples

Vanilla crème dessert
The chef filled the pastry with a vanilla crème that was light and delicious.
She applied a moisturizing crème to her face every morning.
For dessert, we enjoyed strawberries topped with a sweet crème.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cream - the thick, fatty part of milk or a similar substance
  • synonym: frosting - a sweet, creamy topping for cakes and desserts
