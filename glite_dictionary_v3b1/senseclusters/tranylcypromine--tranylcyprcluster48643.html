tranylcypromine

noun
antidepressant monoamine oxidase inhibitor
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtræn.ɪl.saɪˈprəʊ.miːn / (AmE), / ˌtran.ɪl.saɪˈprəʊ.miːn / (BrE)
