bounty

noun
generosity; willingness to give freely
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊnti / (AmE), / ˈbaʊnti / (BrE)
(literary) Generosity, especially as shown by a readiness to give freely or to bestow gifts.

Examples

His bounty to others
He was touched by the bounty of his neighbors after the storm.
Nature's bounty is evident in the abundance of crops this year.
The philanthropist was admired for her bounty and kindness to the poor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generosity - willingness to give or share freely
  • antonym: stinginess - unwillingness to give or share; lack of generosity
