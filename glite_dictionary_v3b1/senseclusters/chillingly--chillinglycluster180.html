chillingly

adverb
in a frightening way
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that causes fear, discomfort, or a sense of coldness

Examples

To chillingly reveal truth
The documentary chillingly revealed the dangers of ignoring climate change.
She chillingly described the events of that night to the police.
The silence in the abandoned house was chillingly complete.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comfortingly - in a way that makes someone feel less worried or afraid
  • synonym: eerily - in a strange and frightening way
