incipiently

adverb
at an early stage
7.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɪpiəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɪpiəntli / (BrE)

Examples

To incipiently begin
The project was incipiently successful, showing promise in its first phase.
Clouds gathered incipiently on the horizon, hinting at an approaching storm.
Her interest in art developed incipiently during her childhood.
