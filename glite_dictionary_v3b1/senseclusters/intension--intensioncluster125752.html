intension

noun
the meaning or content of a term
3.9 8.6
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈten.ʃən/ (BrE)
the internal content or meaning of a concept or term, specifying the qualities or properties something must have to be included under it

Examples

The intension of a term
Linguists often analyze both the intension and extension of a concept.
The intension of the term 'bachelor' is an unmarried man.
Understanding the intension of a word helps to clarify its use in logic.
