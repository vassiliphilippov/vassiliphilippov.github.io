headcloth

noun
cloth for the head
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌklɔθ / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌklɒθ / (BrE)
a piece of cloth worn on the head, often for cultural, religious, or protective reasons

Examples

A white headcloth
She wore a white headcloth as part of her traditional dress.
The monk covered his head with a simple headcloth during the ceremony.
In some cultures, a headcloth is worn daily for modesty or protection from the sun.
She wore a white headcloth to shield herself from the sun.
In some cultures, a headcloth is worn during religious ceremonies.
The traveler wrapped a headcloth around his head to keep out the dust.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hat - a shaped covering for the head
  • synonym: headscarf - a scarf worn around the head or neck
  • synonym: veil - a piece of fine material worn by women to protect or conceal the face
