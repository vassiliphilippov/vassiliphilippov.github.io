venography

noun
X-ray examination of veins with contrast dye
6.0
Pronunciation: / vəˈnɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / vɪˈnɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a medical imaging test in which an X-ray is used to examine veins after injecting a contrast dye

Examples

Venography test
After the venography, the radiologist discussed the images with the vascular surgeon.
Complications from venography are rare but may include allergic reactions to the contrast dye.
The doctor ordered a venography to get a clear view of the patient's leg veins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiography - imaging of blood vessels using X-rays and contrast dye
