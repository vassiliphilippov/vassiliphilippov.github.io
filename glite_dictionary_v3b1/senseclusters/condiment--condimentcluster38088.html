condiment

noun
substance added to enhance food flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.dɪ.mənt / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.dɪ.mənt / (BrE)
A substance such as a sauce, relish, or spice added to food to enhance its flavor.

Examples
