percent sign

noun
the symbol % showing a number out of 100
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɛnt saɪn / (AmE), / pəˈsɛnt saɪn / (BrE)
the symbol (%) used to show that a number is a proportion out of 100; used after a number to indicate 'per hundred'
