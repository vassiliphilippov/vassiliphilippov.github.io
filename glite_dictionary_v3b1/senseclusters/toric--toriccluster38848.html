toric

adjective
relating to a torus shape
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɒrɪk / (BrE)
having the shape of a torus or relating to a lens with different curvatures in different planes, often used to correct astigmatism

Examples

Toric contact lenses
The optometrist recommended toric lenses to help correct her astigmatism.
A toric surface is used in mathematics to describe certain curved shapes.
He learned that toric contact lenses are specially designed for people with irregular corneas.
