torturing

noun
infliction of severe suffering
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtʃərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtʃərɪŋ / (BrE)
the deliberate infliction of physical or mental suffering in order to force someone to provide information, confess, or for other reasons

Examples

The torturing of prisoners
Historical accounts documented the torturing of suspects to obtain confessions during the inquisition.
Psychological torturing can be just as damaging as physical abuse.
The torturing of prisoners was condemned by international organizations as a violation of human rights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: torture - the act of inflicting severe pain as a punishment or to force information
