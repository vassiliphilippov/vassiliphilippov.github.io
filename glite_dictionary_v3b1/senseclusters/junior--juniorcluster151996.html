junior

adjective
student in third year (US education)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒu.njɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒuː.ni.ə / (BrE)
(chiefly US) Relating to the third or next-to-final year of high school or college.

Examples

Junior year classes
As a junior, she started to take more advanced courses in her major.
He transferred to the university as a junior after finishing his associate's degree.
The junior class organized the school dance this year.
