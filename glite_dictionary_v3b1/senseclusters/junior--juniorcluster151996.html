junior

adjective
student in third year (US education)
3.2 12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààd íu.nj…ö / (AmE), / Ààd íuÀê.ni.…ô / (BrE)
(chiefly US) Relating to the third or next-to-final year of high school or college.

Examples

Junior year classes
As a junior, she started to take more advanced courses in her major.
He transferred to the university as a junior after finishing his associate's degree.
The junior class organized the school dance this year.

Synonyms

  • third-year
    3.0 2.18/m
    third-year means being in the third year of study, while junior is the US term for that third, next-to-final school year.
  • next-to-last
    5.1 0.35/m
    next-to-last describes something immediately before the end in any series, while junior specifically names the US third year of school.
