Islamize

verb
convert to Islam; make Islamic
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsləˌmaɪz /; / ˈɪzlɑːˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɪsləˌmaɪz /; / ˈɪzlɑːmaɪz /; / ˈɪsləmaɪz / (BrE)
to make a person, group, or institution Islamic, either by converting them to Islam or by making them follow Islamic laws or customs

Examples

Policies designed to Islamize the country have been a topic of debate.

In the 1980s, some leaders wanted to Islamize local banking practices.
The government sought to Islamize the education system by introducing more religious content.

During the 16th century, the empire sought to Islamise vast regions of its new territory.
Some rulers attempted to Islamise their populations through law and education reforms.
The policy was gradually put in place to Islamise public life and institutions.
