gimpy

adjective
having difficulty walking; lame
1.5 7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈgɪm.piː/ /; / /ˈgɪm.pɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgɪm.piː/ /; / /ˈgɪm.pɪ/ (BrE)
Having difficulty walking due to injury or disability in the feet or legs; lame or crippled.

Examples

A gimpy leg
After the accident, Mark became a bit gimpy and needed a cane to walk.
Some kids teased him for his gimpy walk, but he never let it bother him.
The old dog is gimpy in his hind leg but still loves to play fetch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: able-bodied - not disabled; having full use of one's body
  • synonym: lame - unable to walk easily because of an injured leg or foot
