wey

noun
slang for man or friend
Pronunciation: / weɪ / (AmE), / weɪ / (BrE)
an informal slang term for a man or friend, especially used in Mexican Spanish-influenced English

Examples

Hey wey!
He called out to his friend, saying, 'What's up, wey?' as they met at the park.
In some groups, people use wey to greet each other in a friendly way.
You might hear wey in conversations among young people influenced by Mexican culture.
