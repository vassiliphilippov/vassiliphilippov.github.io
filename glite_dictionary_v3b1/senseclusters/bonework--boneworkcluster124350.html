bonework

noun
bone-crafted objects
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnwɜːk / (BrE)
objects or decorative items crafted from bone, often used for art or ornamentation

Examples

Decorative bonework
The museum displayed ancient bonework from early human settlements.
She admired the delicate bonework on the handle of the old knife.
Collectors often seek out rare pieces of bonework from different cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ivory carving - art or objects made by carving ivory
  • synonym: woodwork - objects or decorative work made from wood
