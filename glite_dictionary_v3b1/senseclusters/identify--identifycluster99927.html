identify

verb
consider as connected or associated
2.6
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdɛntɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to consider or describe something as being closely connected or associated with something else

Examples

To identify success with money
Many people identify success with financial wealth.
Patriotism is sometimes identified with loyalty to the government.
The company is often identified with its famous founder.

Related Terms

  • synonym: associate - to connect something with something else in one’s mind
  • antonym: dissociate - to separate or distinguish between things
