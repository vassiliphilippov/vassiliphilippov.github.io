meteoritics

noun
study of meteorites
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmiːtiəˈraɪtɪks / (AmE), / ˌmiːtiəˈraɪtɪks / (BrE)
the scientific study of meteorites and related phenomena such as their origin, composition, and impact on Earth

Examples

Meteoritics study
He published several papers on meteoritics after analyzing samples from a recent meteorite fall.
The museum's new exhibit highlights advances in meteoritics over the past century.
Students interested in space science often take courses in meteoritics to learn about meteorite impacts.
