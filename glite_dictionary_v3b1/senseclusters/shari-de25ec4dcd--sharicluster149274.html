Shari

proper noun
African river flowing into Lake Chad
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑːri / (AmE), / ˈʃɑːri / (BrE)
a river in central Africa that flows northwest into Lake Chad, forming part of the border between Chad and the Central African Republic.

Examples

Shari River
Hippos and crocodiles are often seen in the Shari River.
Many fishermen depend on the Shari for their daily catch.
The Shari is a vital water source for the communities living along its banks.
