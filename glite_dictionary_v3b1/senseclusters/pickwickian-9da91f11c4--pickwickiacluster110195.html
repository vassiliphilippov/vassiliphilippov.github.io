Pickwickian

adjective
obesity-related syndrome
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɪkˈwɪkiən / (AmE), / pɪkˈwɪkiən / (BrE)
relating to Pickwickian syndrome, a medical condition marked by obesity, shallow breathing, and excessive daytime sleepiness.

Examples

Pickwickian syndrome diagnosis
The patient was diagnosed with Pickwickian syndrome after showing signs of chronic sleepiness and shallow breathing.
Doctors observed a Pickwickian pattern in the patient's breathing during the sleep study.
Treatment for Pickwickian conditions often includes weight loss and respiratory support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obesity hypoventilation syndrome - a disorder involving obesity and poor breathing leading to low oxygen levels
