westward

noun
direction on the compass at 270 degrees
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstwərd / (AmE), / ˈwɛstwəd / (BrE)
the direction or point on the compass that is directly opposite east, at 270 degrees

Examples

From the westward
The storm was approaching from the westward as predicted.
The sun sets in the westward every evening.
They sailed toward the westward, hoping to discover new lands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: east - the cardinal compass point at 90 degrees; opposite of west
  • synonym: west - the cardinal direction at 270 degrees; the opposite of east
