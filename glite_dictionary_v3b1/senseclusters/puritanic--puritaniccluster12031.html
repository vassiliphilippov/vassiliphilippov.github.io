puritanic

adjective
morally strict or rigorous
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpjʊrəˈtænɪk / (AmE), / ˌpjʊə.rɪˈtæn.ɪk / (BrE)
Having or showing very strict moral or religious standards; morally rigorous and strict.

Examples

Strict puritanic views
His puritanic views made him unpopular among his more liberal friends.
She imposed a puritanic set of rules on her household.
The school's puritanic policies forbade even minor displays of affection.
