method

noun
systematic way of doing something
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛθ.əd / (AmE), / ˈmɛθ.əd / (BrE)
a particular way of doing something, especially in a systematic or organized manner involving logical steps

Examples

A method of teaching
Different teachers often have their own methods for managing classrooms.
She developed a new method for analyzing the chemical samples.
The scientific method requires careful observation and experimentation.
