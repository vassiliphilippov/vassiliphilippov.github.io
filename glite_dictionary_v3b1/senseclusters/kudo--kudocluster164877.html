kudo

noun
praise or compliment
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a praise or compliment given to someone for an achievement

Examples

A kudo for effort
He received a kudo from his teacher for his creative project.
Each kudo she earned motivated her to work even harder.
The manager gave a special kudo to the team for meeting the deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compliment - a polite expression of praise or admiration
  • antonym: criticism - the expression of disapproval or finding fault
  • synonym: praise - expression of approval or admiration
