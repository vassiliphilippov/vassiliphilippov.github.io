kudo

noun
praise or compliment
8.3 1.98/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàkuÀêdo ä / (AmE), / ÀàkjuÀêd…ô ä / (BrE)

Examples

A kudo for effort

Each kudo she earned motivated her to work even harder.
The manager gave a special kudo to the team for meeting the deadline.
