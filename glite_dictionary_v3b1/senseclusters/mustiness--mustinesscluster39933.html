mustiness

noun
state of being musty or stale
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈmʌstɪnɪs / (BrE)
the quality or state of smelling or tasting old, stale, or moldy

Examples

The mustiness of air
Despite the room's mustiness, the hotel staff did their best to make our stay comfortable.
He noticed a slight mustiness in the bread and decided not to eat it.
The mustiness of the old library was overwhelming when we first entered.

Related Terms

  • antonym: freshness - the quality of being fresh, not stale
