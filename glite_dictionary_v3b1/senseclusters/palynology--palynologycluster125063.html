palynology

noun
study of pollen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpælɪˈnɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌpælɪˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of pollen and spores, especially to understand plants and environmental history

Examples

Palynology study
Researchers use palynology to reconstruct past climates by analyzing ancient pollen samples.
Her expertise in palynology helped identify the types of plants that once grew in the region.
The museum's new exhibit explains how palynology reveals information about prehistoric environments.
