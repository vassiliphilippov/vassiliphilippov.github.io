Synonyms

  • coronary artery bypass graft
    4.6 0.03/m
    coronary artery bypass graft is the full formal name for the operation, while CABG is the common abbreviation clinicians use.
  • coronary bypass surgery
    4.2 0.04/m
    coronary bypass surgery is the phrase that emphasizes the surgical act of bypassing blocked arteries, while CABG names that specific operation.
  • coronary bypass
    4.4 0.1/m
    coronary bypass is a shorter, less precise term for bypass surgery, while CABG refers to the specific grafting operation on coronary arteries.
