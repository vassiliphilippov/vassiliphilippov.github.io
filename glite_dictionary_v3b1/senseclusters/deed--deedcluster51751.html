deed

noun
an intentional or notable action
3.0
Pronunciation: / did / (AmE), / diːd / (BrE)
an action, especially one that is significant, intentional, or notable

Examples

A brave deed
His good deeds did not go unnoticed by the community.
She was praised for her brave deed during the rescue mission.
The story celebrates the heroic deeds of its main character.

Related Terms

  • synonym: act - a thing done; an action
  • synonym: action - something done or performed
  • antonym: word - a promise or statement as opposed to actions
