fishmouth

noun
notch in pipe or beam
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌmaʊθ / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌmaʊθ / (BrE)
a notch or cut shaped like an open mouth, typically made in a pipe or beam to fit another part

Examples

Fishmouth cut in pipe
The welder created a fishmouth at the end of the pipe for a better joint.
To ensure a snug fit, the carpenter cut a fishmouth into the wooden beam.
A precise fishmouth is essential when joining two metal tubes at an angle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groove - a long, narrow cut or depression in a surface
  • synonym: notch - a small cut or indentation on an edge or surface
