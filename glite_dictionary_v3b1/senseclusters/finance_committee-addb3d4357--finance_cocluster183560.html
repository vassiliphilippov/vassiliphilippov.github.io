finance committee

noun
committee for overseeing finances
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪnæns kəˌmɪti / (AmE), / ˈfʌɪnæns kəˌmɪti / (BrE)
a group of people appointed to oversee and make decisions about financial matters within an organization or institution

Examples

The finance committee
All major purchases must be approved by the finance committee.
She was recently appointed to chair the university's finance committee.
The finance committee will meet on Thursday to review this quarter's spending.

Related Terms

  • synonym: budget committee - a committee responsible for preparing and overseeing a budget
