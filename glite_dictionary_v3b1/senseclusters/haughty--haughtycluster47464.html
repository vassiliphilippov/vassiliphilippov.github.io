haughty

adjective
arrogantly superior; disdainful
8.1 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…îti / (AmE), / Ààh…îÀêti / (BrE)
having or showing an attitude of arrogant superiority and disdain for others considered unworthy

Examples

A haughty look
Despite his haughty attitude, he knew little about the subject.
She gave her servants a haughty look before leaving the hall.
The haughty tone of her response offended everyone in the room.
