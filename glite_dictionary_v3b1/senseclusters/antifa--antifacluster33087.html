antifa

noun
anti-fascist movement
7.7
Pronunciation: / ænˈtiːfə / (AmE), / ænˈtiːfə / (BrE)
a political movement or group that actively opposes fascism, racism, and far-right ideologies, often through direct action

Examples

The antifa movement
The antifa group organized a counter-protest in response to the far-right rally.
Many members of antifa believe in using direct action to fight against racism and fascism.
Debates about the tactics of antifa have appeared frequently in the news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anti-fascist - a person or group opposed to fascism
  • antonym: fascist - a person who supports fascism
