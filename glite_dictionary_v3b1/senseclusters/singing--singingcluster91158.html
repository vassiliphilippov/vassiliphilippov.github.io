singing

noun
the act of producing musical sounds with the voice
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of producing musical sounds with the voice, usually to a tune or melody

Examples

Beautiful singing
His singing was so beautiful that the audience gave him a standing ovation.
She has always loved singing in the school choir since she was a child.
We could hear the joyful singing of birds outside our window every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: song - a short piece of music with words that are sung
