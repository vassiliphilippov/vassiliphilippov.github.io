erroneously

adverb
in a mistaken or incorrect manner
Pronunciation: / ɪˈroʊniəsli / (AmE), / ɪˈrəʊniəsli / (BrE)
In a mistaken or incorrect way.

Examples

To erroneously assume
He erroneously assumed the meeting was scheduled for Tuesday.
Many people erroneously believe that lightning never strikes the same place twice.
The article erroneously reported that the company had declared bankruptcy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: correctly - in a correct manner
  • synonym: mistakenly - in a mistaken manner
