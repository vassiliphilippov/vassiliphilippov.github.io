PPD

noun
postpartum depression
7.0
Pronunciation: / pi pi di / (AmE), / piː piː diː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for postpartum depression, a form of clinical depression that occurs after childbirth, characterized by persistent sadness and difficulty bonding with the baby

Examples

Post-birth PPD
After her son was born, she struggled with PPD and needed support from her family.
Doctors are trained to recognize the signs of PPD in new mothers.
Many women do not realize they are experiencing PPD until they seek help.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: baby blues - mild mood changes after childbirth
  • synonym: postpartum depression - depression occurring after childbirth
