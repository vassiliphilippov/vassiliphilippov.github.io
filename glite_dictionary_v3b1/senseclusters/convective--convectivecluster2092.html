convective

adjective
heat transfer by fluid
6.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɛktɪv / (AmE), / kənˈvɛktɪv / (BrE)
relating to the transfer of heat by the movement of a fluid such as air or water

Examples

The convective currents
The convective currents in the ocean help distribute heat around the planet.
Meteorologists study convective storms to better predict severe weather events.
A convective oven cooks food more evenly by circulating hot air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conductive - relating to the transfer of heat or electricity through a material
  • antonym: radiative - relating to the transfer of energy by radiation
