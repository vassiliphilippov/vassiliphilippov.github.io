unlaid

adjective
not yet deposited
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈleɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈleɪd / (BrE)
not yet laid or deposited, especially referring to eggs still inside a bird or animal

Examples

Unlaid egg
The veterinarian explained that the bird was suffering from an unlaid egg.
Some reptiles can retain unlaid eggs for several days before depositing them.
The study focused on the development of unlaid eggs within the female fish.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laid - already deposited or produced, especially of eggs
