get off

verb
leave work or be temporarily relieved from duties
1.0 2.1
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt ɔf / (AmE), / ɡɛt ɒf / (BrE)
to leave work at the end of one's scheduled time or to be temporarily relieved of duty

Examples

To get off work

I usually get off work at five o'clock.
Will you get off early today because of the holiday?

Related Terms
