shower of shit

noun
intense criticism; backlash (vulgar)
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊɚ əv ʃɪt / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊə əv ʃɪt / (BrE)
(vulgar, informal) a sudden and intense outpouring of harsh criticism or backlash.

Examples

A shower of shit
After the error was discovered, the manager unleashed a shower of shit on the whole team.
The politician received a shower of shit from the public after his controversial statement.
Whenever mistakes happen in that office, there's always a shower of shit from upper management.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backlash - a strong negative reaction or criticism
  • synonym: flak - strong criticism or opposition
