rate of respiration

noun
number of breaths per minute
1.0
Pronunciation: / reɪt əv ˌrɛspəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / reɪt əv ˌrɛspɪˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the number of breaths a person takes per minute, used to assess their health status

Examples

Increased rate of respiration
An increased rate of respiration can indicate fever, anxiety, or lung problems.
Doctors closely monitor the rate of respiration in patients after surgery.
The nurse measured the patient's rate of respiration to check for any signs of distress.
