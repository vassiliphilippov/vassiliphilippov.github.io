fish slice

noun
broad bladed kitchen utensil for lifting food
7.7
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ slaɪs / (AmE), / fɪʃ slaɪs / (BrE)
a kitchen utensil with a broad, flat blade used for lifting, turning, or serving fish or other foods from a frying pan

Examples

A fish slice tool
In British kitchens, a fish slice is a common tool for frying breakfast items.
She used a fish slice to turn the delicate fillets without breaking them.
The chef reached for his fish slice to serve the crispy fried salmon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spatula - a kitchen utensil with a broad, flat, blunt blade for mixing, spreading, or lifting food
