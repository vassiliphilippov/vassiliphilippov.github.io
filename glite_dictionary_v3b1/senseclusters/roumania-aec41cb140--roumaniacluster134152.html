Roumania

proper noun
variant spelling of Romania (country)
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmeɪniə / (AmE), / ruːˈmeɪniə / (BrE)
an alternative spelling of Romania, a country in southeastern Europe bordering the Black Sea

Examples

Roumania country
In older British texts, Roumania was commonly used instead of 'Romania'.
She traveled to Roumania to study its folk traditions.
The map from 1920 labels the country as Roumania.
