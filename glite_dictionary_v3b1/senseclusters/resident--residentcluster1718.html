resident

noun
physician in hospital-based training

a physician in training who lives at and works in a hospital, caring for patients under the supervision of senior staff

Examples

Hospital resident
After graduating from medical school, she became a resident at a large urban hospital.
He completed his training as a surgical resident before moving on to a fellowship.
The resident was called in the middle of the night to assess a new patient.

Synonyms

  • resident physician
    4.1 0.09/m
    resident physician trains in a hospital for a medical specialty and treats patients under senior doctors, while resident names a doctor in hospital training.
  • house physician
    6.1 0.02/m
    house physician is often an intern living and working in a hospital under staff supervision, while resident is any doctor in hospital training.
