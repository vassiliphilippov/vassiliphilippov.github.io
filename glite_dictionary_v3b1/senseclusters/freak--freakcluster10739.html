freak

noun
unusually abnormal or deformed person or animal
Pronunciation: / friːk / (AmE), / friːk / (BrE)
a person or animal that is extremely unusual in appearance, behavior, or physical form, sometimes as a result of deformity

Examples

A circus freak
In the 19th century, people with rare medical conditions were sometimes displayed as freaks in traveling shows.
She felt like a freak in high school because she was so much taller than everyone else.
The child was cruelly called a freak because of his unusual birthmark.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - conforming to a standard; usual, typical, or expected
  • synonym: oddity - an unusual or peculiar person or thing
