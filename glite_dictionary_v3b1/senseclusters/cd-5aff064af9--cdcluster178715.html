CD

noun
compact disc for digital data or audio
Pronunciation: / siː ˈdiː / (AmE), / siː ˈdiː / (BrE)
a small, round optical disc on which digital audio, video, or other data is stored and that is played or read using a laser

Examples

A music CD
Many artists now release their albums both as digital downloads and on CD.
My collection includes over a hundred CDs from various genres.
She put the new CD into the player and listened to her favorite songs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compact disc - synonym for 'CD'; an optical disc for digital storage
  • antonym: vinyl record - an older analog audio storage medium
