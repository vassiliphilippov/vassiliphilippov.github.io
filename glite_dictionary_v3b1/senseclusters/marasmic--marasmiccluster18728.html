marasmic

adjective
lacking vitality
9.9
Pronunciation: / məˈræzmɪk / (AmE), / məˈræzmɪk / (BrE)
lacking energy, growth, or vitality; stagnant or feeble, especially when describing organizations or activities.

Examples

Marasmic company
After years of poor leadership, the company became marasmic and failed to innovate.
The once lively festival now feels marasmic, attracting fewer visitors each year.
Critics described the government's response as marasmic and lacking any real initiative.
