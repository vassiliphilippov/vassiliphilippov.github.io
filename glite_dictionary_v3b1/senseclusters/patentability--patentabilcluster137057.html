patentability

noun
eligibility for patent
Pronunciation: / ˌpætəntəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌpeɪtəntəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality or condition of being eligible for legal protection as a patent

Examples

The patentability of invention
Before filing, it is important to assess the patentability of your invention.
The lawyer explained the criteria for patentability to the research team.
Questions about patentability often arise during the development of new technologies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ineligibility - the state of not meeting the requirements for something
  • synonym: patent - an official right to make or sell an invention
