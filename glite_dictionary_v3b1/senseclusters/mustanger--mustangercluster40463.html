mustanger

noun
wild horse catcher
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstæŋər / (AmE), / ˈmʌstæŋə / (BrE)
a person who captures, tames, or manages wild mustang horses, especially in the American West

Examples

Wild horse mustanger
The experienced mustanger tracked a herd of wild horses across the open plains.
As a young man, he dreamed of becoming a mustanger and living a life of adventure.
Stories of the legendary mustanger spread throughout the frontier towns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowboy - a person who herds and tends cattle, often on horseback
  • synonym: wrangler - a person who takes care of livestock, especially horses
