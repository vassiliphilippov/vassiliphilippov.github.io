karyotypic

adjective
chromosome characteristics
Pronunciation: / ˌkæriəˈtɪpɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæriəˈtɪpɪk / (BrE)
relating to the number, size, and shape of chromosomes in the cells of an organism

Examples

Karyotypic features
The karyotypic features of the species help scientists identify genetic differences.
Doctors examined the karyotypic abnormalities to diagnose the genetic disorder.
A karyotypic analysis is often performed during prenatal testing.
