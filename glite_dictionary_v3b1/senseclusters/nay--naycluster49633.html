nay

noun
a vote or word meaning 'no'
8.4 1.02/m
Pronunciation: / neÉª / (AmE), / neÉª / (BrE)
a word or vote expressing a negative response or refusal, especially in formal or official procedures such as meetings or parliamentary sessions

Examples

A nay vote

The final count recorded more nays than yeas, so the proposal was rejected.
The motion passed despite several loud nay votes from the opposition.
