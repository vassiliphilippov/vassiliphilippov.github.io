Strelitziaceae

noun
a family of tropical woody plants
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɹɛˌlɪtsiˈeɪsiˌaɪ /; / strɛˌlɪtsiˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / stɹɛˌlɪtsiˈeɪsiˌaɪ /; / strɛˌlɪtsiˈeɪsiˌi / (BrE)
a family of woody, usually tropical plants with erect stems, native to South America, South Africa, and Madagascar, sometimes included in the family Musaceae
