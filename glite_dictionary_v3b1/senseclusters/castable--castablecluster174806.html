castable

adjective
able to be thrown
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstəbəl / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstəbəl / (BrE)
able to be thrown, sent through the air, or shaped by casting

Examples

Castable fishing line
The lightweight material made the ball easily castable for children and adults alike.
Only castable metals can be used in this type of mold for sculpture.
The fishing rod is designed for use with highly castable lures.
