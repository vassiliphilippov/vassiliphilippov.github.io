nautilus

noun
nuclear-powered submarine
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔdələs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːtɪləs / (BrE)
a submarine that is powered by nuclear energy, especially referring to the USS Nautilus, the world's first operational nuclear-powered submarine

Examples

The USS nautilus submarine
Many advances in naval engineering were tested on the nautilus before being adopted by other submarines.
The USS nautilus made history as the first submarine to complete a submerged transit beneath the North Pole.
The development of a nuclear-powered nautilus marked a new era in maritime technology.
