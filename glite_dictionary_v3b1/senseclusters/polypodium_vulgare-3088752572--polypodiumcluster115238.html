Polypodium vulgare

noun
species of mat-forming fern
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑː.lɪˈpoʊ.di.əm vəlˈɡɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌpɒ.lɪˈpəʊ.di.əm vʌlˈɡɛə.ri / (BrE)
A species of fern with creeping rootstocks and divided fronds, found across North America, Europe, Africa, and East Asia.
