bialystoker

noun
onion-topped flat bread roll
Pronunciation: / biˌɑːlɪsˈtoʊkər / (AmE), / biˌælɪsˈtəʊkə / (BrE)
A flat, round bread roll with a thick crust and an onion filling, traditionally baked with an indented center topped with chopped onions and sometimes poppy seeds.

Examples

A bialystoker roll
Many New York delis serve the classic bialystoker alongside bagels.
She ordered a fresh bialystoker from the bakery to have with her coffee.
The bialystoker was still warm, packed with savory onions in the center.
