Examples

To enroll in a course

International students must enroll before the start of the term.

Synonyms

  • register
    2.8 32/m
    register means to record official details with an authority, while enroll focuses on joining a course or activity.
  • sign up
    1.3 20/m
    sign up is an informal phrase people use to agree to join activities, while enroll sounds more formal and official.
  • enter
    1.1 41/m
    enter often means joining a competition or list or going into a place, while enroll means officially join a course or group.
  • inscribe
    5.9 0.44/m
    inscribe is a formal or old-fashioned term for writing a name on a list, while enroll is the common modern word for joining.
