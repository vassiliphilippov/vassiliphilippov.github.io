iconize

verb
to minimize window
6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.kə.naɪz/ /; / /ˈaɪ.kɑː.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.kə.naɪz/ /; / /ˈaɪ.kɒ.naɪz/ (BrE)
to minimize a window or application so that it appears as an icon on the screen

Examples

To iconize a window
If you iconize the program, it will stay open but out of the way.
She quickly iconized all her windows before the meeting started.
You can iconize the chat window to keep your desktop organized.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maximize - to enlarge a window to fill the screen
  • synonym: minimize - to reduce a window to an icon or taskbar button
