dog

noun
fireplace metal log support
9.1
Pronunciation: / dɔg / (AmE), / dɒg / (BrE)
a metal support, often in pairs, used to hold logs in a fireplace; also called an andiron

Examples

Fireplace dogs
Carefully place the logs on the dogs to improve air circulation and burning.
The old mansion still had the original dogs in its grand fireplace.
The ornate bronze dogs were both functional and decorative in the library's hearth.
