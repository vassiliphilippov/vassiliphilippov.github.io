Examples

Synonyms

  • admission fee
    2.4 0.20/m
    admission fee is the common phrase on tickets and signs, while admission charge sounds more formal and is used in notices.
  • entrance fee
    1.6 0.49/m
    entrance fee is widely used in everyday speech for entry payments, while admission charge feels more formal and official.
  • admission
    2.7 8.83/m
    admission can mean the act of entering or the payment, while admission charge clearly refers to the fee to get in.
  • admission price
    1.6 0.05/m
    admission price names the amount asked for entry, while admission charge is a slightly more formal way to say the same fee.
  • price of admission
    2.5 0.45/m
    price of admission is a conversational phrase for how much entry costs, while admission charge reads more formal or official.
  • entrance money
    3.3 0.01/m
    entrance money sounds old or informal and refers to cash paid at the door, while admission charge is a neutral formal term.
