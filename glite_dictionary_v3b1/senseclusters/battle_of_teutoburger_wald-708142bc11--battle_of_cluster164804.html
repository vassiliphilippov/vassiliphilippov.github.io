Battle of Teutoburger Wald

proper noun
famous AD 9 battle between Romans and Germanic tribes
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt̬əl əv ˌtɔɪtoʊˈbʊrɡər ˈvalt / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ˌtɔɪtəbʊˈɡɜː wald / (BrE)
a battle in 9 AD in which Germanic tribes led by Arminius destroyed three Roman legions, halting Roman expansion into Germania

Examples

The Battle of Teutoburger Wald in 9 AD
Historians still debate the exact location of the Battle of Teutoburger Wald.
