facial expression

noun
look on a person's face showing feeling or mood
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪʃl ɪkˈsprɛʃən /; / ˈfeɪʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / ˈfeɪʃəl ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a look or movement of the face that shows a person's feelings, thoughts, or mood

Examples

A facial expression change
Her facial expression changed from happy to worried in an instant.
His calm facial expression gave no hint of his excitement.
The actor is known for his ability to convey emotion through subtle facial expressions.
He tried to hide his disappointment, but his facial expression gave him away.
Her facial expression showed exactly how surprised she felt.
The comedian changed his facial expression remarkably for each character.
