To put something down forcefully and noisily; to consume food or drink quickly and energetically.

Examples

To slam down a glass
He slammed down a glass of water before heading back into the sun.
Please don't slam down your laptop on the desk; it might break.

  • plank down
    8.0 0.00/m
    plank down means to put something down carelessly and loudly, while slam down suggests a stronger, angry or violent motion.
  • ram down
    7.1 0.01/m
    ram down is to force or strike something down with great power, while slam down can be strong but less brutal.
  • jam
    3.4 0.71/m
    jam means to press or push something down quickly and tightly, while slam down focuses on noisy, forceful placing.
  • bolt down
    3.7 0.01/m
    bolt down means to eat or drink very quickly without pausing, while slam down also covers forceful putting as well as fast eating.
  • down
    5.4 1.92/m

  • drink down
