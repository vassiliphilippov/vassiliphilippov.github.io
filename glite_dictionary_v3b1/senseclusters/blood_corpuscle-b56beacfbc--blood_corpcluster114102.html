blood corpuscle

noun
cell found in blood
5.5 3.5
Pronunciation: /blʌd ˈkɑːr.pə.səl/ /; / /blʌd ˈkɔːr.pə.səl/ (AmE), /blʌd ˈkɔː.pə.səl/ (BrE)
