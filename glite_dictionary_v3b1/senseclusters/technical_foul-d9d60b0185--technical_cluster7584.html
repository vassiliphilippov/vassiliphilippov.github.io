technical foul

noun
basketball penalty for unsportsmanlike conduct
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛknɪkəl faʊl / (AmE), / ˈtɛknɪkəl faʊl / (BrE)
an infraction in basketball, committed by a player, coach, or team for unsportsmanlike conduct or rule violations not involving physical contact during play; often results in a free throw for the opposing team

Examples

A technical foul penalty
Each technical foul gives the other team a free throw opportunity.
He received a technical foul for unsportsmanlike behavior on the court.
The referee called a technical foul after the coach argued too aggressively.

Related Terms

  • antonym: personal foul - a foul involving physical contact between players
