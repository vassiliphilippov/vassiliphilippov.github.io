babi

noun
Indonesian pork dish
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːbi / (AmE), / ˈbɑːbi / (BrE)
an Indonesian or Malay dish made with pork, especially roasted, often served with spices and rice

Examples

Babi guling dish
We ordered babi guling at the Balinese restaurant for a special treat.
The chef prepared traditional babi with a blend of local spices and herbs.
Many tourists enjoy tasting babi when visiting Indonesia for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: babi guling - a Balinese dish of spit-roasted pork
  • synonym: roast pork - pork cooked by roasting, often with seasonings
