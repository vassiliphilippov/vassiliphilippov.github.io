buckthorn

noun
a spiny shrub or small tree with berries
6.3 8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌθɔːn / (BrE)
a shrub or small tree, often with spiny branches and berries; some types are used to make dyes

Examples

The buckthorn shrub
