indispensably

adverb
essentially, necessarily
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbli / (BrE)
in a manner that is absolutely necessary or essential

Examples

Indispensably helpful advice
Her advice was indispensably helpful during the most difficult stages of the project.
The new software is indispensably integrated into our daily workflow.
He played an indispensably important role in organizing the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: essentially - in a way that is absolutely necessary
  • synonym: necessarily - in a way that is required or needed
  • antonym: optionally - in a way that is not required or necessary
