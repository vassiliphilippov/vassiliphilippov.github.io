stench bomb

noun
device emitting a foul odor
Pronunciation: / stɛntʃ bɑm / (AmE), / stɛntʃ bɒm / (BrE)
A small device that releases a very unpleasant smell when triggered, often used as a prank or for crowd control.

Examples

A stench bomb prank
Police used a stench bomb to disperse the crowd during the protest.
The prankster ordered a stench bomb online to play a joke on his classmates.
The students set off a stench bomb in the hallway and the entire floor had to be evacuated.
