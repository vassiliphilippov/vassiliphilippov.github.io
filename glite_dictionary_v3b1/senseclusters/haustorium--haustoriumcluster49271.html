haustorium

noun
root-like organ in parasitic plants
Pronunciation: / hɔːˈstɔːriəm / (AmE), / hɔːˈstɔːriəm / (BrE)
a specialized structure in parasitic plants or fungi that penetrates a host to absorb nutrients
