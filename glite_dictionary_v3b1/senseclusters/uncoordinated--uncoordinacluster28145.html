uncoordinated

adjective
lacking organization or cooperative planning
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkoʊˈɔrdəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəʊˈɔːdɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of plans, actions, or groups) lacking organization or cooperative planning, resulting in poor efficiency or conflicting outcomes.

Examples

Uncoordinated efforts
Uncoordinated responses from emergency teams led to confusion at the scene.
Because of uncoordinated efforts between departments, the project suffered multiple delays.
The festival was marred by uncoordinated planning, causing schedules to overlap and attendees to miss events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: coordinated - well organized and planned working effectively together
