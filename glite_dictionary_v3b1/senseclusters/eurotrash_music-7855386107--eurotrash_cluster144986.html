eurotrash music

noun
derogatory term for cheap European pop music
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊroʊˌtræʃ ˈmjuːzɪk / (AmE), / ˈjʊərəʊˌtræʃ ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
(derogatory) European pop music regarded as cheap, tasteless, or of low quality.
