laryngoplasty

noun
surgery on larynx
Pronunciation: / ləˈrɪŋɡəˌplæsti / (AmE), / ləˈrɪŋɡəˌplæsti / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to repair or reconstruct the larynx

Examples

Laryngoplasty surgery
After the accident, the doctor recommended laryngoplasty to restore the patient's ability to speak.
Advances in laryngoplasty have improved outcomes for people with damaged vocal cords.
The singer underwent laryngoplasty to repair an injury and regain her voice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laryngectomy - surgical removal of the larynx
  • synonym: tracheoplasty - surgical repair of the trachea
