CRO

noun
conversion rate optimization (marketing)
7.4
Pronunciation: /kroʊ/ /; / /krəʊ/ (AmE), /krəʊ/ /; / /kroʊ/ (BrE)
(in digital marketing) the practice or set of techniques for increasing the percentage of website visitors who complete a desired action, such as making a purchase or signing up.

Examples

CRO expert advice
The startup hired a CRO expert to boost its website's conversion rates.
Our marketing team runs weekly A/B tests as part of their CRO program to increase sales.
Effective CRO depends on clear calls to action and fast, mobile-friendly page design.
