tobramycin

noun
an antibiotic drug
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˌtoʊbrəˈmaɪsən / (AmE), / ˌtəʊbrəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic used to treat infections caused by Gram-negative bacteria, often administered for severe bacterial infections

Examples
