voiding

noun
bodily discharge of waste, esp. urine
Pronunciation: / ˈvɔɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈvɔɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of discharging waste matter from the body, especially urine

Examples

Frequent voiding of urine
Frequent voiding can be a symptom of a urinary tract infection.
Proper hydration is important for regular voiding and kidney health.
The patient reported difficulty with voiding after surgery.
