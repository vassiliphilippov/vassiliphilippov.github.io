laborer

noun
person doing manual physical work
6.4 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.bər.ər/ /; / /ˈleɪ.bə.rər/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.bə.rə/ /; / /ˈleɪ.bər.ə/ (BrE)
a person who does hard unskilled physical work that does not require special training or skill, often working outdoors on building sites or in factories

Examples

A laborer on site
He started his career as a laborer before becoming a building supervisor.
Many laborers work long hours under challenging conditions to support their families.
The construction site hired several laborers for the building project.
He found work as a labourer in a local factory.
Many farms hire seasonal labourers during harvest time.
The construction labourer worked long hours in the hot sun.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artisan - a skilled manual worker, especially one in a trade
  • synonym: laborer - an alternative spelling of 'labourer', mainly used in American English
  • synonym: worker - a person who works, especially one employed in a particular field
