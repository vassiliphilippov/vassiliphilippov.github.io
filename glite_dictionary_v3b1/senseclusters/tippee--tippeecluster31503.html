tippee

noun
recipient of inside information
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈtɪ.piː/ /; / /ˈtɪp.iː/ (AmE), /ˈtɪ.piː/ /; / /ˈtɪp.iː/ (BrE)
a person who receives confidential or inside information, especially in the context of insider trading or legal matters

Examples

The tippee gets secrets
The tippee was investigated for trading stocks based on confidential company news.
In court, the lawyer argued that the tippee did not know the information was secret.
Authorities monitor both the tipper and the tippee in cases of insider trading.
