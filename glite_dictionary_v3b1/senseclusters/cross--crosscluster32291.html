cross

verb
to draw a line through or across
2.0 5.79/m
Pronunciation: / krÉ‘s / (AmE), / krÉ’s / (BrE)
to draw a line through or across something, especially to form or complete a letter or symbol

Examples

To cross a letter
He accidentally forgot to cross the 'f' in his signature.
Make sure you cross your t's clearly so the words are easy to read.
She told the students to always cross their t's and dot their i's.

Synonyms

  • cross out
    2.3 0.74/m
    cross out means drawing a line through words to show they are wrong or removed, while cross mainly means drawing a line across objects.
  • cross off
    1.8 0.93/m
    cross off marks list items as done by drawing a line through them, while cross means drawing a line across in writing.
