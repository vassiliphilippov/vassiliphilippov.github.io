Synonyms

  • illness
    1.1 58/m
    illness describes a common state of being unwell with noticeable symptoms, while malady sounds more formal and literary.
  • disease
    2.3 194/m
    disease is a medically defined condition that affects organs or specific functions, while malady is a general, more old-fashioned word for illness.
  • disorder
    4.3 46/m
    disorder refers to a disturbance of normal physical or mental function, often used in medical contexts, while malady is more general.
  • sickness
    1.0 5.38/m
    sickness describes feeling unwell or being ill, often used in everyday speech, while malady sounds more formal.
