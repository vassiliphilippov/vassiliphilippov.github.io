jack salmon

noun
pike-like freshwater fish; young salmon or pike-perch
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæk ˌsæmən /; / dʒæk ˈsæm.ən / (AmE), / ˈdʒæk ˌsamən /; / dʒæk ˈsæm.ən / (BrE)
a common or regional name for certain young or small salmon, especially male Pacific salmon that return early, and sometimes for other similar freshwater fish like walleye or pike-perch
