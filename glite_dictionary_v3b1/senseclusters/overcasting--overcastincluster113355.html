overcasting

noun
sewing stitch to prevent raveling
7.5 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
A long whipstitch or overhand stitch sewn over the edge of fabric to prevent it from unraveling.

Examples

Overcasting stitch
Learning overcasting is essential for anyone interested in hand sewing.

The edges of the fabric were protected by carefully applied overcasting.
