mythification

noun
making mythic
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪθɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɪθɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making someone or something appear mythic, legendary, or symbolic

Examples

The mythification of heroes
The mythification of historical figures can make it hard to separate fact from legend.
In her paintings, there is a clear mythification of everyday life, turning ordinary moments into epic scenes.
The film's mythification of the city gives it a timeless, almost magical atmosphere.
The mythification of ancient leaders often exaggerates their achievements.
Some movies contribute to the mythification of real historical events.
The mythification of celebrities can make them seem larger than life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: demystification - the act of making something less mysterious or mythical
  • antonym: demythologization - the process of removing mythic or legendary qualities
  • synonym: mythologization - the act of turning something into a myth
