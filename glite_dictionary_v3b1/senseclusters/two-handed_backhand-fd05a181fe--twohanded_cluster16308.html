two-handed backhand

noun
backhand shot using both hands
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌtuː ˈhændɪd ˈbækˌhænd / (AmE), / ˌtuː ˈhændɪd ˈbækhænd / (BrE)
A backhand shot in tennis or similar sports, made by holding the racquet with both hands.

Examples

A two-handed backhand shot
He learned to switch from a one-handed to a two-handed backhand during his training.
Her two-handed backhand is one of the strongest shots in her game.
Many modern players prefer the two-handed backhand for added control and power.
