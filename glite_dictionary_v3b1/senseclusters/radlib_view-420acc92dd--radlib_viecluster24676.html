radlib view

noun
a radical liberal viewpoint
Pronunciation: / ˈræd.lɪb vjuː / (AmE), / ˈræd.lɪb vjuː / (BrE)
A viewpoint or attitude characterized by radical liberalism, often emphasizing progressive social reforms beyond mainstream liberal positions.

Examples

A radlib view on politics
He was criticized by moderates for expressing a radlib view on police funding.
Her radlib view on healthcare calls for universal coverage with no private insurers.
Many activists hold a radlib view on prison abolition, advocating for sweeping systemic change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: radical - favoring thorough or complete political or social reform
