shopman

noun
workshop employee
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpmən / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpmən / (BrE)
a worker, craftsman, or mechanic employed in a workshop or industrial shop

Examples

Workshop shopman
The experienced shopman quickly fixed the broken engine in the workshop.
Each shopman in the factory had a specific set of tools for their tasks.
As a skilled shopman, he trained new employees in machine maintenance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled worker who makes or repairs things by hand
  • synonym: mechanic - a person who repairs and maintains machinery
