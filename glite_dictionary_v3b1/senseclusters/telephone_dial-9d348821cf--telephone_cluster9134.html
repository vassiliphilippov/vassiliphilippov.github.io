telephone dial

noun
rotary disc for dialing numbers on a phone
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləˌfoʊn daɪəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛlɪfəʊn daɪəl / (BrE)
a circular disc on a telephone that is rotated to select each number when making a call, especially on older rotary phones

Examples

Old telephone dial
Restoring the vintage phone included cleaning its original telephone dial.
She placed her finger in the hole and turned the telephone dial to call her friend.
The old telephone dial made a distinctive clicking sound with each number chosen.
