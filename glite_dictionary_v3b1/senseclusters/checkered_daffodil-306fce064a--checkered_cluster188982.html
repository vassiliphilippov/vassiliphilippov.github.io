checkered daffodil

noun
ornamental plant with checkered bell-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛkɚd ˈdæfədɪl / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛkəd ˈdafədɪl / (BrE)
A Eurasian ornamental plant (Fritillaria meleagris) with bell-shaped, pendent flowers typically veined and checkered with shades of purple or maroon on a pale background.

Examples

A checkered daffodil flower
A single checkered daffodil stood out among a sea of yellow tulips.
The checkered daffodil is prized in gardens for its striking patterned petals.
We planted several checkered daffodils along the edge of the pond last spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fritillary - plant of the genus Fritillaria, including the checkered daffodil
