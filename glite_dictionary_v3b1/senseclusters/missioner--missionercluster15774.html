missioner

noun
person sent on a religious or charitable mission
5.6 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪʃ.ən.ər/ (AmE), /ˈmɪʃ.ən.ə/ (BrE)
A person sent on a mission, especially one sent by a church to teach or carry out charitable work in a foreign country.

Examples

A missioner abroad
As a young missioner, she traveled to remote villages to offer medical care and education.
The missioner devoted years to building schools and clinics in rural Africa.
The church honored the retiring missioner for decades of service overseas.
