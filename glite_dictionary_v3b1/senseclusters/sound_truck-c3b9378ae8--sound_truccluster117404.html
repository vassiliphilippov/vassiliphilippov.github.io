sound truck

noun
truck with loudspeaker for ads
Pronunciation: / saʊnd trʌk / (AmE), / saʊnd trʌk / (BrE)
A truck fitted with a loudspeaker, used to broadcast advertisements or public announcements, especially while driving through areas.

Examples

A sound truck broadcasts
A sound truck drove through the neighborhood, announcing the start of the festival.
Local businesses often hire a sound truck to advertise new store openings.
The politician's speech was broadcast to the crowd by a sound truck circling the area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loudspeaker van - a van equipped with loudspeakers for announcements or advertising
