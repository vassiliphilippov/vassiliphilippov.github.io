tellable

adjective
able to be told
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛləbəl / (AmE), / ˈtɛləbəl / (BrE)
able to be told or suitable for telling to others

Examples

A tellable secret
The secret was finally tellable after so many years of silence.
Some experiences are not easily tellable to friends or family.
He wondered if his story was tellable in a way people would understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narratable - able to be narrated or told as a story
  • antonym: untellable - not able or suitable to be told
