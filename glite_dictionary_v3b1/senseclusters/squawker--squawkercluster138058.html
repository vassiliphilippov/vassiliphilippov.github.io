squawker

noun
noisy person or animal
Pronunciation: / ˈskwɑkər / (AmE), / ˈskwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person or animal that makes a loud, harsh, and unpleasant noise

Examples

A noisy squawker
The old radio was such a squawker that we could barely hear the music clearly.
At dawn, the squawker in the tree woke everyone with its harsh calls.
During the meeting, one squawker kept interrupting with loud complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chatterbox - a person who talks a lot, often noisily
  • synonym: screamer - a person or animal that screams loudly
  • antonym: whisperer - a person who speaks very quietly
