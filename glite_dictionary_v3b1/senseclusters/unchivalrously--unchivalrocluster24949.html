unchivalrously

adverb
in an unchivalrous manner
2.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɪvəlrəsli / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɪvəlrəsli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks chivalry; without courtesy, bravery, or gallantry, especially toward women.

Examples

To act unchivalrously
He unchivalrously refused to hold the door open for her despite seeing her arms full of packages.
Some critics argue that the character behaves unchivalrously throughout the novel.
The knight acted unchivalrously when he mocked his defeated opponent.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chivalrously - in a chivalrous and gallant manner
  • synonym: ungallantly - in an ungallant or discourteous manner
