aguish

adjective
suffering from or relating to ague
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪɡwɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈeɪɡwɪʃ / (BrE)
Relating to or affected by ague; suffering from fever and chills.

Examples

Feeling aguish and weak
After walking through the marshes, he felt aguish and weak from the lingering fever.
Her aguish appearance concerned the village doctor who suspected another case of malaria.
The patient's aguish chills lasted throughout the long winter night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: febrile - having or showing symptoms of fever
  • antonym: healthy - not suffering from illness or disease
