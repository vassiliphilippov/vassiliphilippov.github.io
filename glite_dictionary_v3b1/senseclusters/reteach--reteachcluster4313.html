reteach

verb
to teach again
3.7
Pronunciation: /riː.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /rə.ˈtiːtʃ/ (AmE), /riː.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /rɪ.ˈtiːtʃ/ /; / /rə.ˈtiːtʃ/ (BrE)
to teach something again, usually to help someone understand better, correct mistakes, or reinforce learning
