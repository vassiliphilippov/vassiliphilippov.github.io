custard

noun
sweet dessert of milk and eggs
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstərd / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd / (BrE)
A sweetened mixture of milk and eggs, often thickened by heating, and served as a dessert or a filling.

Examples

Creamy custard dessert
For dessert, we had warm apple pie topped with creamy custard.
Many recipes for ice cream start with a basic egg custard.
She poured a thick layer of vanilla custard over the fresh berries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pudding - soft sweet dessert, sometimes similar to custard
