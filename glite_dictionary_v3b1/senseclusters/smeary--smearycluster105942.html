smeary

adjective
defamatory or slanderous
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɪri / (AmE), / ˈsmɪəri / (BrE)
containing or intended to harm someone's reputation by making unfair or unproven allegations; slanderous or defamatory.

Examples

Smeary news story
The newspaper issued a smeary report that damaged the actor's reputation.
She was upset by the smeary comments circulating online about her work.
During the campaign, several smeary stories were spread to influence public opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defamatory - intended to damage someone's reputation
  • antonym: praiseworthy - deserving approval or admiration
  • synonym: slanderous - containing false and damaging statements about someone
