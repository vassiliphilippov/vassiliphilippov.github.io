whooping

noun
severe defeat
Pronunciation: / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwuːpɪŋ / (BrE)
an informal term for a severe defeat or heavy beating, especially in sports or competition

Examples

A whooping defeat
The home team gave their rivals a real whooping in last night's game.
After the debate, the losing side admitted the whooping was well deserved.
No one expected such a whooping in the championship finals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thrashing - a decisive or overwhelming defeat
  • antonym: victory - the act of winning or defeating an opponent
