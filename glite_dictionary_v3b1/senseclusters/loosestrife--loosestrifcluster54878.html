loosestrife

noun
herbaceous plant of the genus Lysimachia
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsˌstraɪf / (AmE), / ˈluːsˌstraɪf / (BrE)
any of several wetland plants with spikes of colorful flowers, especially of the genera Lysimachia or Lythrum
