Synonyms

  • retirement pension
    2.7 0.13/m
    retirement pension is a regular payment from government or employer after leaving work, while retirement benefit is a broader term for such payments.
  • pension
    3.2 39/m
    pension is a regular income paid to retired people for support, while retirement benefit is a general term for post-work payments.
  • old-age pension
    3.2 0.25/m
    old-age pension is a payment given because of reaching retirement age, while retirement benefit covers any payment after stopping work.
  • superannuation
    9.7 0.02/m
    superannuation is a fund-based retirement payment usually from workplace savings, while retirement benefit is the general name for such income.
  • retirement check
    3.1 0.10/m
    retirement check is a physical monthly payment often sent by post, while retirement benefit is the overall term for any retirement payment.
