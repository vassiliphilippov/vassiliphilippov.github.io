retirement benefit

noun
regular payment after retirement
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪɚmənt ˈbɛnəfɪt / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪəmənt ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
a regular payment made to someone after they have retired from work, usually from a pension plan or government scheme

Examples

A retirement benefit payment
After thirty-five years at the company, she was finally eligible to receive her retirement benefit.
He reviewed the terms of his retirement benefit carefully before signing the paperwork.
Many people depend on their retirement benefits to cover living expenses after leaving the workforce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pension - a regular payment made during retirement from an investment fund
