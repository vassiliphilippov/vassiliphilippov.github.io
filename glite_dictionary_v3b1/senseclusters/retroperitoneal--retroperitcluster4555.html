retroperitoneal

adjective
behind the peritoneum
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛtroʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəl / (AmE), / ˌrɛtrəʊˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəl / (BrE)
located behind the peritoneum, the membrane lining the abdominal cavity

Examples

Retroperitoneal organs
The kidneys are retroperitoneal organs in the human body.
A retroperitoneal tumor can be difficult to detect because of its location.
Surgeons must take special care when operating in the retroperitoneal space.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraperitoneal - located within the peritoneal cavity
