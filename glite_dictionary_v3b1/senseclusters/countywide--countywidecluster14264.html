countywide

adjective
throughout an entire county
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊntiˌwaɪd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊntiˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Occurring or extending throughout the whole of a county.

Examples

Countywide road repairs
Officials called for countywide road repairs after the harsh winter.
The new health initiative will be implemented countywide next year.
Voters showed strong support for the countywide recycling program.
