fin

noun
swimming or diving shoe aiding movement
7.4
Pronunciation: / fɪn / (AmE), / fɪn / (BrE)
a shoe or device worn on the foot to aid movement in water, shaped like a paddle, used especially for swimming or diving

Examples

Swim fins
Before snorkeling, she made sure her fins were securely fastened to her feet.
He accidentally left one fin on the beach after his swim.
Many divers prefer using long fins for underwater propulsion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flipper - a paddle-like shoe worn on the foot for swimming or diving
