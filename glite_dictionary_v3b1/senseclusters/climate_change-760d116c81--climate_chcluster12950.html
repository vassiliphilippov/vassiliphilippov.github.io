climate change

noun
long-term change in Earth's climate
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmət tʃeɪndʒ / (BrE)
A long-term change in the Earth's climate, especially a change due to an increase in average atmospheric temperature.

Examples

Climate change effects
Extreme weather events are increasingly linked to climate change.
Many governments are developing policies to address climate change.
Scientists around the world are researching the causes and effects of climate change.

Related Terms

  • synonym: global warming - a gradual increase in the overall temperature of the Earth's atmosphere
