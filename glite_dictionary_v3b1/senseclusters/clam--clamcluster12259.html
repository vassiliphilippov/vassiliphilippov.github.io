clam

noun
edible flesh of a clam
Pronunciation: / klæm / (AmE), / klam / (BrE)
The edible flesh of a hard-shell or soft-shell clam, often used as food.

Examples

Clam chowder
He added chopped clam to the pasta for extra flavor.
She ordered a bowl of steamed clam with garlic butter.
The chowder was packed with fresh clam and potatoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clam - an aquatic mollusk with two hinged shells
