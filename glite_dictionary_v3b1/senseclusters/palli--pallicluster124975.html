palli

noun
place of worship
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌli / (AmE), / ˈpʌli / (BrE)
a place of worship, such as a church, mosque, or chapel, especially in South India and Sri Lanka

Examples

Palli prayers
The villagers gathered at the palli every evening for prayers.
A new palli was built in the center of the town to serve the growing community.
During the festival, the palli was decorated with colorful lights and flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: church - a building for Christian worship
  • synonym: mosque - a building for Muslim worship
  • synonym: temple - a building for religious worship
