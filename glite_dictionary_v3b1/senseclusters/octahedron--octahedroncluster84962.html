octahedron

noun
polyhedron with eight faces
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːktəˈhiːdrən / (AmE), / ˌɒktəˈhiːdrən / (BrE)
A solid figure with eight plane faces, each usually an equilateral triangle.

Examples

A octahedron shape
Each octahedron in the sculpture was painted a different color.
Students built a model of an octahedron for their geometry project.
The diamond crystal is shaped like an octahedron with eight triangular faces.
