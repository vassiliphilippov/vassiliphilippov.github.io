new edition

noun
updated published version
Pronunciation: / nu ɪˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / njuː ɪˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a version of a book or publication that has been revised or updated and reissued

Examples

A new edition of a book
A new edition of the textbook is coming out next semester.
She collected every new edition of her favorite mystery series.
The publisher announced a new edition of the novel with added commentary.
