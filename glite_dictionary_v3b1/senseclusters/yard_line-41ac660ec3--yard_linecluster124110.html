yard line

noun
line marking yardage on a football field
3.4 1.68/m

(in American football) a line marked parallel to the goal lines on the field, indicating distance in yards from the nearest goal line.

Examples

10-yard line
He ran all the way from his own 10-yard line to score a touchdown.
The ball was placed on the 20-yard line after the kickoff.
The referee signaled that the play stopped at the 35-yard line.
