secure

adjective
financially safe or guaranteed
1.1
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkjʊr / (AmE), / sɪˈkjʊə / (BrE)
(of investments or finances) certain to remain safe and not likely to lose value or be risky.

Examples

Secure investment
In uncertain times, it is wise to choose a secure place to keep your money.
Many people prefer to put their savings into secure investments such as government bonds.
The company offered a secure financial package that guaranteed returns for five years.
