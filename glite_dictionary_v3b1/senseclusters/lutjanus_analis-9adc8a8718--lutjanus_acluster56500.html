Lutjanus analis

noun
a species of Atlantic snapper fish
Pronunciation: / luˈtʃeɪnəs əˈnælɪs / (AmE), / luˈtʃeɪnəs əˈnælɪs / (BrE)
A species of snapper fish found in the Atlantic Ocean, also known as the mutton snapper, similar to the red snapper.

Examples

Lutjanus analis fish
Fishermen sometimes mislabel Lutjanus analis as red snapper due to their similar appearance.
Seafood markets may sell Lutjanus analis under various names, including mutton snapper.
The Lutjanus analis inhabits reefs and coastal waters in the western Atlantic Ocean.
