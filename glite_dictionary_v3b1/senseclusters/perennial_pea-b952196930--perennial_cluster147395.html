perennial pea

noun
a perennial climbing plant with colored flowers
Pronunciation: / pəˈrɛn.i.əl pi / (AmE), / pəˈrɛn.i.əl piː / (BrE)
a perennial climbing plant of central and southern Europe, having purple, pink, or white flowers; naturalized in North America

Examples

The perennial pea plant
Gardeners often choose the perennial pea for its ability to return year after year.
The perennial pea adds vibrant color to the summer garden with its pink blossoms.
Wild populations of the perennial pea have become established throughout parts of North America.
