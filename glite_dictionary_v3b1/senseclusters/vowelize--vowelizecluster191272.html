vowelize

verb
add vowel marks to text
9.1 0.00/m

(in orthography and linguistics) to add vowel marks or diacritic signs to a consonant-based writing system (for example, Hebrew or Arabic) to show vowel sounds and indicate pronunciation.

Examples

To vowelize a text
Scholars often vowelize medieval Hebrew manuscripts to show pronunciation for students.
The editor vowelized the Arabic text with diacritics to aid non-native readers.
Before publication, they were vowelizing poetry lines to preserve intended vocal rhythms.
