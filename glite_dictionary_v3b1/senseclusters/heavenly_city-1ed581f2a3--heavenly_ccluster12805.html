Heavenly City

noun
literary term for Heaven
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənli ˈsɪti / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli ˈsɪti / (BrE)
a poetic or literary expression for Heaven, often depicting it as a perfect city in religious or allegorical texts

Examples

The Heavenly City
In the final chapter, the pilgrims finally entered the Heavenly City after a long and arduous journey.
Many hymns describe a longing to reach the Heavenly City and dwell in eternal peace.
The author imagined the Heavenly City as a place of perfect harmony and light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Celestial City - another literary phrase for Heaven, famously used in Bunyan's 'Pilgrim's Progress'
  • synonym: Heaven - the abode of God and the angels, and of the good after death
