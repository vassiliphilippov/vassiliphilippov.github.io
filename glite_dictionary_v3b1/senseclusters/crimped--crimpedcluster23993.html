crimped

adjective
pressed and fastened
Pronunciation: / krɪmpt / (AmE), / krɪmpt / (BrE)
compressed or fastened by being pressed into shape, especially to join two parts securely in electrical or mechanical work

Examples

Crimped wire ends
The ends of the cable were crimped to ensure a secure electrical connection.
She checked that each metal sleeve was properly crimped before finishing the assembly.
A crimped connector prevents the wire from slipping out during use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clamped - held tightly together with a clamp
  • antonym: loose - not fastened or held tightly
