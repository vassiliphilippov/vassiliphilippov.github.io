elance

verb
to throw forcefully
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlæns / (AmE), / ɪˈlɑːns / (BrE)
to throw or hurl something forward, especially a weapon

Examples

To elance a spear
The knight prepared to elance his spear at the target.
With a swift motion, she elanced the javelin across the field.
He elances the stick into the river, watching it float away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: catch - to seize or take hold of something thrown
  • synonym: hurl - to throw something with great force
  • degree lower: toss - to throw something lightly or casually
