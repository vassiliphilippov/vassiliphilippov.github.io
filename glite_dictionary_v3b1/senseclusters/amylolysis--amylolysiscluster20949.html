amylolysis

noun
conversion of starch to sugar
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæm.ɪˈlɑː.lɪ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌæm.ɪˈlɒ.lɪ.sɪs / (BrE)
the process by which starch is converted into sugar, especially by the action of enzymes.

Examples
