unkillable

adjective
impossible to stop
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɪləbəl / (BrE)
impossible to stop, terminate, or destroy, especially referring to a computer process or program that cannot be ended by normal means

Examples

Unkillable computer process
The virus created an unkillable process that kept reappearing after every attempt to remove it.
Some players believe the boss character is practically unkillable due to its high defense.
After the update, the background service became unkillable and would not shut down.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
  • synonym: unstoppable - impossible to prevent from continuing
  • antonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or affected
