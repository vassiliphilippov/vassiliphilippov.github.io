recaller

noun
person who remembers
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɔlər / (AmE), / rɪˈkɔːlə / (BrE)
a person or thing that remembers or brings something back to mind

Examples

A skilled recaller
As a skilled recaller, she could easily list every detail from the meeting.
The device acted as a recaller for important dates and appointments.
Among the group, he was known as the best recaller of childhood stories.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetter - a person who forgets
  • synonym: rememberer - a person who remembers
