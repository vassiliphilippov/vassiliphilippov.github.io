In a manner that uses sarcasm, often saying the opposite of what one means to mock or convey contempt.

Examples

To speak sarcastically
'Nice job cleaning up,' he said sarcastically, pointing to the messy room.
She replied sarcastically when asked if she enjoyed waiting in the long line.
The student sarcastically thanked the teacher for the surprise quiz.
