over-earnest

adjective
excessively serious
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈɜrnɪst / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈɜːnɪst / (BrE)
too serious or sincere in a way that seems excessive or awkward

Examples

An over-earnest speech
His over-earnest speech made the audience feel uncomfortable.
She gave an over-earnest response to a simple question, making it seem awkward.
People sometimes find his over-earnest attitude difficult to relate to in casual settings.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: earnest - showing sincere and serious intention
  • antonym: insincere - not expressing genuine feelings or intentions
