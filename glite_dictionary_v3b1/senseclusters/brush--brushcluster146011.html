brush

noun
an act of brushing hair or a surface
Pronunciation: / brʌʃ / (AmE), / brʌʃ / (BrE)
the act of cleaning, polishing, or arranging something by rubbing it with a brush

Examples

A quick brush

He gave his hair a quick brush before leaving the house.
She found a mirror and started to brush her hair.
A quick brush before bed helps prevent cavities.
He gave his teeth a thorough brush after eating sweets.
The child had a brush before going to school.
