Binghi ceremony

noun
Rastafarian ceremonial gathering
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪŋɡi ˈsɛrəˌmoʊni / (AmE), / ˈbɪŋɡi ˈsɛrɪməni / (BrE)
A ceremonial gathering in Rastafarian culture, often involving drumming, chanting, and celebration, used to mark significant occasions and promote unity.

Examples

Binghi ceremony drumming
During the Binghi ceremony, participants played drums and sang traditional songs.
