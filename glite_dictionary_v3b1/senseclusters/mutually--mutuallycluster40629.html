mutually

adverb
in a shared or reciprocal way
3.3 8.87/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôli / (AmE), / ÀàmjuÀêt Éu…ôli / (BrE)
in a way that involves two or more people or groups equally; shared by two or more parties

Examples

To mutually agree
The two companies agreed to end the partnership mutually.
Their decision was mutually beneficial and satisfied both parties involved.
We must find a solution that is mutually acceptable to everyone.

Synonyms

  • reciprocally
    6.8 0.24/m
    reciprocally shows a back-and-forth exchange between two parties, while mutually means something shared equally by both sides.
  • jointly
    3.5 5.50/m
    jointly means doing something together as one group or team, while mutually means it is shared equally between the people involved.
