alder-leaved serviceberry

noun
shrub or small tree native to NW North America
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːldər liːvd ˈsɜːrvɪsˌber.i / (AmE), / ˈɔːldə liːvd ˈsɜːvɪsbɛri / (BrE)
