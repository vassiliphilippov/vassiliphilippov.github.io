pipefish

noun
a slender, tubular-snouted fish
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪpˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪp.fɪʃ / (BrE)
A slender fish with a long tubular snout and a body covered with bony plates, related to seahorses.
