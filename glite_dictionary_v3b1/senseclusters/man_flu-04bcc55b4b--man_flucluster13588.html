man flu

noun
humorous exaggeration of men's colds
9.9
Pronunciation: / mæn fluː / (AmE), / mæn fluː / (BrE)
a mild infection such as a cold that men are humorously said to exaggerate or complain excessively about

Examples

The man flu joke
He insisted his man flu was far worse than anyone else's cold.
Some people think man flu is just an excuse to stay in bed all day.
Whenever he catches a sniffle, he jokes about having man flu.
