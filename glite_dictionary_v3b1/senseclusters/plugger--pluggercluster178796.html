plugger

noun
active supporter or promoter
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈplʌɡə / (BrE)
a person who actively supports or promotes something or someone, especially with enthusiasm and persistence

Examples

A plugger for a cause
As a plugger for environmental causes, he attends every rally and meeting he can.
She has always been a passionate plugger of local music and new artists.
The team's coach is a tireless plugger for community engagement programs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends something
  • synonym: promoter - a person who supports or encourages something
