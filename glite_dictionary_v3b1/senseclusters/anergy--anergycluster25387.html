anergy

noun
lack of energy; physiological inactivity
Pronunciation: / ˈænɚdʒi / (AmE), / ˈænəːdʒi / (BrE)
A state of inactivity or lack of energy, especially in a physiological or medical context.

Examples

Physical anergy
After weeks of stress, he felt a deep sense of anergy and struggled to focus.
Scientists studied the effects of chronic illness on immune anergy in the test subjects.
The patient's anergy made it difficult for her to participate in everyday activities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apathy - lack of interest or concern
  • antonym: energy - the capacity for vigorous activity
  • synonym: lethargy - a state of sluggishness or laziness
