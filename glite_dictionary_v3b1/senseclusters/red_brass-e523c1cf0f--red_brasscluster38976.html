red brass

noun
copper-rich reddish brass alloy
9.7 3.3
Pronunciation: /red bræs/ (AmE), /red brɑːs/ /; / /red bræs/ (BrE)
A type of brass alloy containing a high proportion of copper, giving it a reddish color; commonly used in plumbing, hardware, and inexpensive jewelry.

Examples

Red brass alloy
Cheap trinkets are often fashioned from red brass due to its affordability and attractive color.
Plumbers sometimes prefer red brass pipes because of their resistance to corrosion.
The antique vase appeared to be made of red brass, distinguished by its warm hue.
