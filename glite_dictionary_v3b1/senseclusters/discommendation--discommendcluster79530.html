discommendation

noun
reason not to recommend
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪskəˌmɛnˈdeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪskəˌmɛnˈdeɪʃən / (BrE)
a fact, quality, or circumstance that gives a reason not to recommend someone or something

Examples

A discommendation for a job
The lack of experience was a significant discommendation for the job candidate.
His poor attendance record served as a discommendation during the selection process.
Despite her skills, one discommendation was her limited teamwork experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disadvantage - an unfavorable condition or circumstance
  • antonym: recommendation - a reason to suggest someone or something as suitable
