vena palatina

noun
palate-draining vein
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈviː.nə pə.ˈlæt.ɪ.nə/ /; / /ˈviː.nə pæl.ə.ˈtiː.nə/ (AmE), /ˈviː.nə pə.ˈlæt.ɪ.nə/ /; / /ˈviː.nə pæl.ə.ˈtiː.nə/ (BrE)
A vein that drains the region of the palate and empties into the facial vein.

Examples
