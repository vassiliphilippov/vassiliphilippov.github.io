sweet calabash

noun
a fruit-bearing tropical vine of the West Indies
Pronunciation: / swit ˈkæl.əˌbæʃ /; / swit ˈkæləbæʃ / (AmE), / swiːt ˈkæl.əˌbæʃ /; / swiːt ˈkæləbæʃ / (BrE)
a tropical West Indian plant (Passiflora maliformis) that produces small, apple-sized fruits with sweet, edible pulp
