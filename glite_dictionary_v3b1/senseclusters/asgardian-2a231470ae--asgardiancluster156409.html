Asgardian

adjective
majestic or godlike
9.9
Pronunciation: / æsˈɡɑːrdiən / (AmE), / æsˈɡɑːdiən / (BrE)
relating to Asgard, its gods, people, or culture, or having qualities associated with them (such as being powerful or majestic), especially in Norse mythology or fiction
