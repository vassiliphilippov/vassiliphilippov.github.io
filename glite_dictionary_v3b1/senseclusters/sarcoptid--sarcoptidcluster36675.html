sarcoptid

noun
mite of Sarcoptidae family
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɑrˈkɑp.tɪd / (AmE), / sɑːˈkɒp.tɪd / (BrE)
A mite belonging to the family Sarcoptidae, known for infesting the skin of humans and animals.

Examples
