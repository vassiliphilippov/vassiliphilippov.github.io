barchan

adjective
crescent-shaped dune
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrkən / (AmE), / ˈbɑːkæn / (BrE)
having the shape of a crescent, especially describing dunes or ridges formed by wind.

Examples

Barchan shaped ridge
The barchan dunes in the desert are easily recognized by their curved shape.
Scientists studied the movement of barchan ridges to understand wind patterns.
A barchan formation can indicate the direction of prevailing winds in an area.
