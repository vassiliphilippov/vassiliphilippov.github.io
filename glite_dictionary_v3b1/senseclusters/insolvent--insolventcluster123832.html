insolvent

adjective
unable to pay debts
2.8 6.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈsɑːl.vənt/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsɒl.vənt/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsɑːl.vənt/ (BrE)
Unable to pay debts owed; having insufficient assets to cover financial obligations.

Examples

Insolvent company
After years of financial trouble, the company was declared insolvent by the court.
If you are insolvent, you may need to seek advice from a financial counselor.
The charity became insolvent after losing its major source of funding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bankrupt - legally declared unable to pay debts
  • antonym: solvent - having enough money to pay all debts
