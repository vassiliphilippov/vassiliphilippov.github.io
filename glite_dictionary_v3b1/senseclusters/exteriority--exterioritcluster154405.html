exteriority

noun
state of being external
Pronunciation: / ɪkˌstɪriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ɪkˌstɪəriˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of being outside or external to something

Examples

The exteriority of a building
The exteriority of the sculpture made it stand out in the open plaza.
Philosophers often discuss the relationship between exteriority and interiority in human experience.
The building's exteriority was designed to blend with the surrounding landscape.
