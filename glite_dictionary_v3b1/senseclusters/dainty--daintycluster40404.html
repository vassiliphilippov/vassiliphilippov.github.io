dainty

adjective
overly fastidious or easily disgusted (of a person)
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪnti / (AmE), / ˈdeɪnti / (BrE)
(of a person) excessively fastidious or easily disgusted, especially about cleanliness or eating.

Examples

Dainty eater
He was too dainty to eat the food served at camp.
She grew up so dainty that even the smallest mess bothered her.
Their dainty habits made family dinners a challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: squeamish - easily made to feel sick or disgusted
