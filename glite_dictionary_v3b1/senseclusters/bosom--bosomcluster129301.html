bosom

noun
the chest as the seat of emotions
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊzəm / (AmE), / ˈbʊzəm /; / ˈbʊz.əm / (BrE)
the chest as the place of deep emotions or feelings, often used in a literary or poetic way

Examples

A secret in his bosom
He finally shared the terrible secret that had weighed on his bosom for years.
Love and hope blossomed in her bosom as she read the letter.
She kept her worries hidden deep within her bosom.
His words touched my bosom and brought tears to my eyes.
In the quiet of night, sorrow weighed heavily on her bosom.
The letter stirred something deep within her bosom that she could not explain.
