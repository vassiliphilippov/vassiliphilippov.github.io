arbuscule

noun
small tree or shrub
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːrbəˌskjuːl / (AmE), / ˈɑːbjuːskjuːl / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub, used in archaic or poetic language

Examples

Lonely arbuscule
A single arbuscule stood at the edge of the quiet pond, its leaves trembling in the breeze.
In the poet's verse, the arbuscule symbolized hope growing in barren land.
They rested in the shade of a delicate arbuscule during their afternoon walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shrub - a woody plant smaller than a tree with several stems
  • degree higher: tree - a perennial plant with an elongated stem or trunk
