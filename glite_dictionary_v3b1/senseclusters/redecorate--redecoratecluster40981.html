redecorate

verb
change or update decorating style
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈdɛkəˌreɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːˈdɛkəreɪt / (BrE)
to change the appearance of the interior of a place by applying new paint, wallpaper, etc., or by updating its decorative style

Examples

To redecorate the house
After moving in, they decided to redecorate the entire house.
She spent the weekend redecorating her bedroom with new paint and curtains.
We plan to redecorate the living room next month.
