redecorate

verb
change or update decorating style
3.2 1.16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåriÀêÀàd…õk…ôÀåre…™t / (AmE), / ÀåriÀêÀàd…õk…ôre…™t / (BrE)
to change the appearance of the interior of a place by applying new paint, wallpaper, etc., or by updating its decorative style

Examples

To redecorate the house
After moving in, they decided to redecorate the entire house.
She spent the weekend redecorating her bedroom with new paint and curtains.
We plan to redecorate the living room next month.

Synonyms

  • restyle
    3.9 0.14/m
    restyle changes the style or look to give a fresh appearance, while redecorate focuses on updating interior decor like paint or wallpaper.
  • refurbish
    4.6 2.73/m
    refurbish makes a building or furniture like new by repairing and decorating it, while redecorate focuses mainly on interior decorative updates.
  • redo
    3.4 0.58/m
    redo means renovate or remodel parts of a room, often including structural work, while redecorate mainly changes cosmetic interior finishes.
  • revamp
    4.9 3.16/m
    revamp improves or modernizes something with major changes or repairs, while redecorate focuses on cosmetic interior updates like paint.
  • make over
    4.1 0.32/m
    make over means to change or remake appearance or function often in dramatic ways, while redecorate is updating interior decoration.
  • freshen up
    3.0 0.17/m
    freshen up makes a room look cleaner or brighter with small fixes, while redecorate changes interior style or finishes more thoroughly.
