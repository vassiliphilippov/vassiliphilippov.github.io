unsanitariness

noun
state of being unsanitary
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsænɪˌtɛrinəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsænɪtərɪnɪs / (BrE)
The state of being unsanitary; a condition not conducive to health or cleanliness.

Examples

The unsanitariness of conditions
He was alarmed by the unsanitariness of the living conditions in the refugee camp.
Inspectors reported on the unsanitariness of the restaurant’s kitchen, leading to its temporary closure.
The unsanitariness of the water supply was a major public health concern in the city.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sanitariness - the state of being sanitary; promoting health or cleanliness
  • synonym: unsanitary - not clean or hygienic; likely to cause disease
