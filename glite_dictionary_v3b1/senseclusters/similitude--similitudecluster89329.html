similitude

noun
a duplicate or close copy
5.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈmɪlɪˌtjud / (AmE), / sɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd / (BrE)
a person or thing that closely resembles another; a duplicate or counterpart.

Examples

A similitude of the original
Each coin bore a remarkable similitude to its predecessor, making them hard to distinguish.
In the museum, a similitude of the ancient vase was displayed alongside the authentic piece.
The artist created a similitude of the original painting for the exhibition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duplicate - an exact copy of something
  • antonym: original - the first or initial form from which copies are made
