drop hammer

noun
machine for shaping metal by dropping a heavy weight
2.3
Pronunciation: / drɑp ˈhæmər / (AmE), / drɒp ˈhamə / (BrE)
a large machine that forges metal by dropping a heavy weight onto it, used in metalworking to shape or stamp parts

Examples

A drop hammer machine
A drop hammer delivers powerful blows, making it essential in heavy forging operations.
Modern factories rely on a drop hammer to produce large, uniform metal parts.
The blacksmith operated the drop hammer to shape the red-hot steel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forge - a fireplace or furnace for heating metal, or the workshop using it
  • synonym: power hammer - machine that shapes metal using mechanical or electrical power
