hag-ridden

adjective
tormented by nightmares or fears
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhæɡˌrɪdən / (AmE), / ˈhæɡˌrɪd.ən / (BrE)
Tormented or harassed by nightmares, anxieties, or unreasonable fears.

Examples

Hag-ridden by nightmares
After weeks of stress, she felt completely hag-ridden by irrational fears and bad dreams.
He was hag-ridden by memories of his time at war, unable to find peace at night.
The novel describes a hag-ridden protagonist, obsessed with dark visions and imaginary terrors.
