agrochemical

adjective
relating to farm chemicals
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡroʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌæɡrəʊˈkɛmɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to chemicals used in agriculture, especially for improving crop production or controlling pests.

Examples

Agrochemical industry
The agrochemical sector is developing new products to increase crop yields.
Many farmers rely on agrochemical solutions to protect their fields from pests.
Government regulations now limit certain agrochemical substances to protect the environment.
