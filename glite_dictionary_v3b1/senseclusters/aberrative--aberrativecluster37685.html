aberrative

adjective
causing distortion
9.8
Pronunciation: / əˈbɛrətɪv / (AmE), / əˈbɛrətɪv / (BrE)
causing or relating to an aberration, especially a distortion or deviation from the normal, often in optics

Examples

Aberrative lens effect
The aberrative effect of the old telescope made the stars appear blurry.
Engineers worked to minimize the aberrative qualities in the new camera lens.
If a lens is highly aberrative, it can distort the image significantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distorting - causing something to be twisted or altered from its original form
