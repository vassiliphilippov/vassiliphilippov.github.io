potter

noun
a person who makes pottery
3.9 12/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ëÀêt…ôr / (AmE), / Ààp…ít…ô / (BrE)
a person who makes pottery by shaping clay and firing it in a kiln

Examples

A skilled potter
A master potter always knows the right temperature to fire the clay in the kiln.
She has been a skilled potter for over twenty years, selling her work at local markets.
The potter shaped the wet clay into a beautiful vase on his wheel.

Synonyms

  • potmaker
    5.1 0.01/m
    potmaker is someone whose job is to make pots from clay, while potter shapes clay into pottery and fires it in a kiln.
  • ceramicist
    8.2 0.04/m
    ceramicist makes a wide range of fired clay items, often including art pieces, while potter focuses on making pottery vessels.
  • ceramist
    6.9 0.51/m
    ceramist creates objects from ceramic materials for art or industry, whereas potter mainly makes pottery by hand and fires it in a kiln.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceramist - an artist who makes objects from ceramic materials
  • synonym: sculptor - an artist who makes sculptures
