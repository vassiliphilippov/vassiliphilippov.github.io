okey-doke

interjection
informal way to agree or acknowledge
6.1 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.kiː doʊk/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.kiː dəʊk/ (BrE)
An informal way of saying 'okay', used to indicate agreement or acknowledgment.

Examples

Okey-doke okay
'We should meet at 3 p.m.' – 'Okey-doke, I'll see you then.'
He gave me a thumbs up and said, 'okey-doke, let's do it.'
If you need any help, just let me know. Okey-doke!

Related Terms

  • synonym: okay - used to express agreement or acceptance
  • synonym: okey-dokey - an informal variant of 'okay'; same meaning as 'okey-doke'
