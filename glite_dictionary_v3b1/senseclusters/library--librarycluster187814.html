library

noun
building or room for books and study
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪˌbrɛri / (AmE), / ˈlaɪ.brəri /; / ˈlaɪbrəri / (BrE)
a building or room where books and other materials are kept for people to read, borrow, or study
