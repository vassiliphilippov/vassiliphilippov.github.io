stone cold sober

adjective
completely sober; not under influence
Pronunciation: / stoʊn koʊld ˈsoʊbɚ / (AmE), / stəʊn kəʊld ˈsəʊbə / (BrE)
completely sober; not affected by alcohol or drugs at all

Examples

Stone cold sober all night

Even though everyone else was drinking, she remained stone cold sober throughout the night.
He was stone cold sober when he gave his statement to the police.
