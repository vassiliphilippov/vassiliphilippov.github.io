scorekeeping

noun
tracking wins or debts
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈskɔːr.kiː.pɪŋ/ /; / /ˈskoʊr.kiː.pɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈskɔː.kiː.pɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of keeping track of perceived wins, losses, or debts between people, especially in arguments or relationships

Examples

Relationship scorekeeping
Constant scorekeeping in a relationship can lead to resentment and misunderstandings.
They agreed to stop scorekeeping and focus on supporting each other instead.
Healthy friendships rarely involve any kind of scorekeeping about favors or past mistakes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the act of letting go of resentment or debts
  • synonym: tit for tat - an equivalent retaliation or response to someone's action
