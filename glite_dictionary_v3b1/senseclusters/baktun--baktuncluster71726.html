baktun

noun
Mayan time period
Pronunciation: / bɑkˈtuːn / (AmE), / bækˈtuːn / (BrE)
a period of approximately 394 years in the ancient Mayan calendar system

Examples

A baktun period
The ancient Maya marked the end of each baktun with important ceremonies and rituals.
Historians study the Mayan calendar to understand when a new baktun would begin.
The thirteenth baktun ended in 2012, leading to many modern interpretations and myths.
