addressable

adjective
targetable for personalized ads
6.8
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈdres.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈæd.res.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈdres.ə.bəl/ /; / /ˈæd.res.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
(in advertising and marketing) capable of being uniquely identified and targeted, especially at the household or individual level, allowing delivery of personalized ads or content.

Examples

Addressable TV ads
Advertisers now buy addressable inventory that lets them show different ads to each household.
The streaming service introduced addressable ad breaks to improve relevance for individual viewers.
Brands prefer addressable audiences because they can tailor creatives to household interests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: targetable - able to be aimed at or directed toward a particular person, group, or market segment
  • synonym: targeted advertising - advertising that is directed at specific individuals or groups based on data about them
