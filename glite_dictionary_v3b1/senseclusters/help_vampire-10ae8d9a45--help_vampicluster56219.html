help vampire

noun
person who constantly asks for help without reciprocating
8.8
Pronunciation: / hɛlp ˈvæmpaɪɚ / (AmE), / hɛlp ˈvæmpaɪə / (BrE)
a person who persistently seeks help from others without attempting to solve problems independently or reciprocate, often draining the time and energy of those they approach

Examples

A help vampire online
Every online forum has at least one help vampire who never tries to find answers on their own.
Instead of being a help vampire, try to solve problems independently before seeking assistance.
It can be exhausting when a help vampire repeatedly interrupts your workday with the same questions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freeloader - a person who habitually relies on others for things such as food or support without reciprocating
