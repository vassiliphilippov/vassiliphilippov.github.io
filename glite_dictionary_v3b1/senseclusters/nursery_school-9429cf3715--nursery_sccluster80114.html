nursery school

noun
school for young children before primary school
1.6 1/m
Pronunciation: / Ààn…ùÀês…ôri skuÀêl / (AmE), / Ààn…úÀês…ôri skuÀêl / (BrE)
A school or institution for very young children, typically between the ages of three and five, that prepares them for primary education.

Examples

A nursery school
Many parents send their children to a nursery school before they start primary education.
She works as a teacher at the local nursery school.
The nursery school held a spring concert to showcase the children's progress.
