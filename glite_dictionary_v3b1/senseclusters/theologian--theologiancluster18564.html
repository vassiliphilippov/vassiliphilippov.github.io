theologian

noun
expert in theology; religious scholar
Pronunciation: / θiˈoʊlədʒən / (AmE), / θiˈɒlədʒən / (BrE)
a person who studies theology or is an expert in religious theory and doctrine

Examples

A theologian studies religion
As a theologian, she wrote several influential books on Christian doctrine.
The conference was attended by every leading theologian in the country.
The young student aspired to become a theologian after college.

Related Terms
