critic

noun
a person who judges or reviews something
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪtɪk /; / ˈkrɪt̬ɪk / (AmE), / ˈkrɪtɪk / (BrE)
a person who gives opinions or judgments about art, literature, music, or similar subjects, often as a job

Examples

A film critic
As a respected critic, her opinions were highly valued by the theater community.
He became a fierce critic of government policies after leaving office.
The film received poor reviews from almost every critic in the country.
As a respected art critic, she frequently writes essays for major magazines.
Some critics argue that the novel fails to capture the complexity of modern life.
The film critic gave the movie a glowing review in the newspaper.
