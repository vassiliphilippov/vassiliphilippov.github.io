conversational

adjective
typical of informal spoken language
3.9 2.61/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên.v…ôrÀàse…™. É…ô.n…ôl / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.v…ôÀàse…™. É…ô.n…ôl / (BrE)
(of language or style) characteristic of informal spoken conversation rather than formal speech or writing.

Examples

Conversational style
Her tone during the meeting was surprisingly conversational and relaxed.
His lectures are engaging and conversational, rather than rigid or formal.
The book is written in a conversational style, making it easy for everyone to understand.

Synonyms
