ruler

noun
person who governs or leads
Pronunciation: / ˈruːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈruːlə / (BrE)
a person who governs or exercises authority over a country, region, or group of people

Examples

A powerful ruler
Many people lived in fear of the harsh ruler who controlled the land.
The ancient pharaoh was the absolute ruler of Egypt.
The country has been without a clear ruler since the king's death.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country
  • antonym: subject - a person under the authority of a ruler
