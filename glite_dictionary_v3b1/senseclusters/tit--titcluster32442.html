tit

noun
nipple or small projection of a mammary gland
Pronunciation: / tɪt / (AmE), / tɪt / (BrE)
a nipple; the small projecting part of a mammary gland, especially in mammals

Examples

A baby's tit
She breastfed her baby using only one tit at a time.
Some mammals have more than one tit on each side.
The sculpture featured the realistic depiction of a mother’s tit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nipple - the small protuberance on the breast through which milk is drawn
  • synonym: teat - the fleshy part of a mammal's mammary gland through which milk is sucked
