rhythm method

noun
natural family planning method using menstrual cycle
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪðəm ˌmɛθəd /; / ˈrɪðəm ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / ˈrɪðəm ˌmɛθəd /; / ˈrɪð.əm ˌmeθ.əd /; / ˈrɪðəm ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)
a method of natural family planning where a woman avoids sex during her most fertile days, estimated by tracking her menstrual cycle, to prevent pregnancy
