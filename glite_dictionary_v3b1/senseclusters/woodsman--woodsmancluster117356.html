woodsman

noun
man living in the woods
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdz.mən / (AmE), / ˈwʊdz.mən / (BrE)
a man who lives in the woods and is skilled in woodcraft, forestry, or making things from wood

Examples

A skilled woodsman
As a skilled woodsman, he could track animals and build shelters with ease.
Many legends feature a mysterious woodsman who appears in times of trouble.
The old woodsman lived alone in a cabin deep in the forest.
Every woodsman in the village contributed to the construction of the new barn.
His grandfather was a skilled woodsman who could make anything from cabinets to toys.
The woodsman carved a beautiful chair from a single block of timber.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpenter - a person whose job is making or repairing wooden structures or their parts
  • synonym: forester - a person who works in or is an expert in forestry
  • synonym: hermit - a person living in solitude, often in the wild
  • synonym: woodworker - a person who makes or repairs wooden objects
