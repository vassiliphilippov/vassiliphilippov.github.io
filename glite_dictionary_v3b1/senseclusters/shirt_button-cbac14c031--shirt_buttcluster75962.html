shirt button

noun
button used on a shirt
2.7
Pronunciation: / ʃɝːt ˈbʌtən / (AmE), / ʃɜːt ˈbʌt(ə)n / (BrE)
a small, usually round fastener sewn onto a shirt, used to join its two parts together by fitting through a buttonhole

Examples

A shirt button fastened
He carefully fastened every shirt button before leaving for the meeting.
I need to sew a new shirt button onto my favorite blouse.
She lost a shirt button while getting dressed this morning.

Related Terms
