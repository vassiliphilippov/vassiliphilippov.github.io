come up with snake eyes

verb
to get a very bad or unlucky result
Pronunciation: / kʌm ʌp wɪð sneɪk aɪz / (AmE), / kʌm ʌp wɪð sneɪk aɪz / (BrE)
to experience a very poor or unlucky result; to fail or have bad luck, especially in a crucial situation
