imperial decree

noun
edict from an emperor or sovereign
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpɪriəl dɪˈkri / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɪəriəl dɪˈkriː / (BrE)
an official order or proclamation issued by an emperor or sovereign ruler

Examples

An imperial decree
According to the imperial decree, all citizens were required to pay a new tax.
Historians discovered an imperial decree preserved in the archives of the ancient capital.
The emperor issued an imperial decree banning the use of foreign currency within his domain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edict - an official order or proclamation issued by a person in authority
