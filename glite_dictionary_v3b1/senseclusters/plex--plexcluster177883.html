plex

noun
multi-unit building
Pronunciation: / plɛks / (AmE), / plɛks / (BrE)
a building divided into several separate units, such as apartments or offices, within the same structure

Examples

An apartment plex
They moved into a new plex near the city center.
The plex has both residential and office units in the same building.
She bought a small plex to rent out the extra units for income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apartment building - a building containing several separate apartments
  • degree higher: complex - a group of buildings or units forming a single development
  • degree lower: duplex - a house divided into two separate units
