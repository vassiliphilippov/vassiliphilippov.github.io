prohibitionism

noun
support for legal bans
Pronunciation: / proʊˌhɪbɪˈʃɪnɪzəm / (AmE), / prəʊˌhɪbɪˈʃɪnɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in, support for, or movement to ban certain substances or activities by law, especially alcohol or drugs
