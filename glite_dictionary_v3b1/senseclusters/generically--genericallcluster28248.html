generically

adverb
in a general way
4.2
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛrɪkli / (AmE), / dʒəˈnɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a general way, without referring to anything specific or detailed

Examples

Spoke generically about ideas
The teacher spoke generically about the topic, avoiding any particular details.
When asked about his plans, he answered generically without giving specifics.
The instructions were written generically, so users from any country could understand them.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generally - in most cases; usually
  • antonym: specifically - in a detailed and exact way
