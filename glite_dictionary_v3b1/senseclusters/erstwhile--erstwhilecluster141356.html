erstwhile

adjective
former; previous
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrstˌwaɪl / (AmE), / ˈɜːstˌwaɪl / (BrE)
former; belonging to an earlier time; previous.

Examples

Erstwhile friend
His erstwhile colleagues barely recognized him at the reunion.
Several erstwhile rivals have now joined forces for a common cause.
The castle was the erstwhile residence of the royal family.

Related Terms

  • antonym: current - belonging to the present time
  • synonym: former - previous or earlier in time
