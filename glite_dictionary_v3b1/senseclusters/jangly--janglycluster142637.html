jangly

adjective
having a harsh metallic ringing sound
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒæŋ.li / (AmE), / ˈdʒæŋ.li / (BrE)
having a harsh, metallic, and discordant ringing sound, like metal objects striking together

Examples

A jangly guitar riff
A jangly guitar riff gave the song a raw, energetic feel.
She loved the jangly sound of her stacked silver bracelets as she walked.
The old piano produced a jangly tune that echoed through the empty house.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jangle - to make a discordant ringing sound
