unworthy

adjective
morally reprehensible; despicable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɝði / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːði / (BrE)
morally reprehensible; deserving of condemnation or contempt.

Examples

Unworthy behavior
He apologized for his unworthy attempt to deceive his friends.
She considered his betrayal an unworthy act and severed all ties with him.
The unworthy behavior of the officials shocked the entire community.
