Synonyms

  • speak out against
    4.1 3/m
    speak out against is to publicly say strong opposition to something, while protest involves expressing strong disagreement by words or actions.
  • speak against
    4.2 1.0/m
    speak against is to openly criticise something in speech, while protest covers both public words and formal actions.
  • dissent
    6.8 4/m
    dissent means disagreeing with an official or majority view, while protest is the act of publicly or formally showing disagreement.
  • demonstrate
    3.2 5/m
    demonstrate is to join a public march to show opposition, while protest can be done by words or a range of public actions.
  • remonstrate
    8.5 0.3/m
    remonstrate is to strongly object, often in formal speech to authority, while protest is more general public or formal disagreement.
