gunpoint

noun
end of a gun
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌpɔɪnt / (BrE)
the muzzle or pointed end of a gun, especially as a position from which force or threat is applied

Examples

Gunpoint inspection
The soldier inspected the gunpoint for any obstruction before they began the patrol.
He was forced to hand over his wallet at gunpoint during the robbery.
The guard kept the intruder at gunpoint until the police arrived.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrel - the long metal tube of a gun through which the bullet travels
  • synonym: muzzle - the open end of a gun's barrel from which the bullet exits
