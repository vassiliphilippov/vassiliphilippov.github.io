Aesopian

adjective
hidden or allegorical
Pronunciation: / iˈsoʊpiən / (AmE), / iːˈsəʊpiən / (BrE)
describing language or stories that use hidden or allegorical meanings, especially to avoid censorship or criticism, often in the style of Aesop's fables
