when they go low, we go high

x
responding to negativity with integrity
Pronunciation: / wɛn ðeɪ ɡoʊ loʊ wi ɡoʊ haɪ / (AmE), / wɛn ðeɪ ɡəʊ ləʊ wiː ɡəʊ haɪ / (BrE)
