grounds

noun
land set aside for a special purpose
2.7
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊndz / (AmE), / ɡraʊndz / (BrE)
an area of land that is used or set aside for a specific purpose, such as recreation, a building site, or burial.

Examples

Burial grounds
The family gathered at the burial grounds for the memorial service.
The university grounds are beautifully landscaped with gardens and old trees.
Visitors strolled through the museum grounds enjoying the outdoor sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cemetery - a place where the dead are buried
  • synonym: estate - a large area of land, especially with a grand house
