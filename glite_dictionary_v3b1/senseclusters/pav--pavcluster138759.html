pav

noun
Indian bread roll
8.9
Pronunciation: / pɑːv / (AmE), / pɑːv / (BrE)
a soft, white bread roll commonly served with Indian dishes such as curry or street food

Examples

A pav bread roll
He ordered a plate of pav with spicy curry at the restaurant.
Vendors on the street were selling hot pav with butter and chutney.
The recipe calls for fresh pav to be served alongside the vegetable dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bun - a small, round bread roll
  • antonym: naan - a type of leavened, oven-baked flatbread from India
