kicker

noun
player who kicks the football
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkɪkə / (BrE)
a player whose main role is to kick the football, especially in American football or soccer

Examples

Football kicker
As a kicker, he practices his technique every day to improve his accuracy.
The coach relied on the kicker for long-range attempts during close matches.
The team's kicker made the winning field goal in the final seconds of the game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: goalkeeper - a player whose role is to defend the goal in football
  • synonym: punter - a football player who punts the ball
