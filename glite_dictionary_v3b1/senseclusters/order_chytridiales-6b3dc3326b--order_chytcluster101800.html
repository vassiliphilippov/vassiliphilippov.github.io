order Chytridiales

noun
an order of aquatic fungi
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər kaɪˌtrɪdiˈeɪliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə kaɪˌtrɪdiˈeɪliːz / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of simple aquatic fungi, mostly saprophytic but some parasitic, sometimes classified in Oomycetes
