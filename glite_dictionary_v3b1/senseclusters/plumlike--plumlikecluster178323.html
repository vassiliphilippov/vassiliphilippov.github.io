plumlike

adjective
resembling a plum (fruit)
Pronunciation: / ˈplʌmˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈplʌmˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of a plum, especially in appearance, taste, or texture

Examples

Plumlike fruit
Several trees in the garden bore small, plumlike fruits.
She described the color as a deep, plumlike purple.
The dessert had a plumlike flavor that reminded me of fresh fruit.
