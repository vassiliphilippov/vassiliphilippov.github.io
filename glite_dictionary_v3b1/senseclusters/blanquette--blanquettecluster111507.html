blanquette

noun
French meat stew
Pronunciation: / blæŋˈkɛt / (AmE), / blæŋˈkɛt / (BrE)
a French dish of meat, typically veal, cooked in a white sauce made with stock and cream

Examples

Classic blanquette dish
The chef recommended the classic blanquette as the perfect comfort food for a cold evening.
She learned to prepare a traditional veal blanquette during her cooking class in Paris.
At the bistro, he enjoyed a delicious blanquette served with rice and fresh herbs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ragout - a stew of meat and vegetables, typically seasoned and cooked slowly
  • synonym: stew - a dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid
