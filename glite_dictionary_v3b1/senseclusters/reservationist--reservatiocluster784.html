reservationist

noun
person making reservations
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzərˈveɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈveɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make or manage reservations for services such as hotels, restaurants, or events

Examples

The reservationist confirms bookings
The reservationist confirmed our hotel booking and sent us a confirmation email.
If you have any questions about your table, please speak to the reservationist at the front desk.
As a reservationist, she handles dozens of calls each day for dinner bookings.
The reservationist confirmed our table for Saturday evening at the restaurant.
When I called the hotel, the reservationist quickly found a room for me.
A skilled reservationist can handle multiple bookings and customer requests at once.

Related Terms

  • synonym: booking agent - a person who arranges reservations or tickets for clients
  • synonym: concierge - a hotel employee who assists guests with services and information
  • synonym: receptionist - a person who greets visitors and answers calls at a business or hotel
