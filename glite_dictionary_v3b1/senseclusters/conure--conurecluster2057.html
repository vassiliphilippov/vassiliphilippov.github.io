conure

noun
small parrot species
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːnjər / (AmE), / ˈkɒnjə / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored parrot native to Central and South America, often kept as a pet

Examples

A conure parrot
The colorful conure perched on her shoulder and chirped happily.
Many bird enthusiasts enjoy training their conure to perform simple tricks.
A conure can be a lively and affectionate companion for families.
