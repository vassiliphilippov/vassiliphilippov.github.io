Dapsang

proper noun
mountain peak in the Karakoram Range, also called K2
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɑpˈsɑŋ / (AmE), / dæpˈsæŋ / (BrE)
a mountain peak in the Karakoram Range in northern Kashmir, also known as K2; the second highest peak in the world at 28,250 feet (8,611 meters) high.

Examples

Dapsang mountain peak
Dapsang, or K2, stands as a remote sentinel in the Karakoram Range.
Many climbers dream of reaching the summit of Dapsang, which is known for its extreme challenges.
The treacherous weather on Dapsang has claimed the lives of experienced mountaineers.
