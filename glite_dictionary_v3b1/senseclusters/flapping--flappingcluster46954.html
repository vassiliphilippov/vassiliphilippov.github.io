flapping

noun
the motion of moving up and down repeatedly
5.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈflæp.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈflæp.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or motion of moving up and down, especially repeatedly or with a loose, waving movement

Examples

The flapping wings

The flapping of the flag could be heard throughout the quiet night.
The steady flapping of sails signaled an increase in wind.
