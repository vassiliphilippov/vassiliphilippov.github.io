schizophrenic psychosis

noun
type of psychosis in schizophrenia
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk saɪˈkoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk saɪˈkəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a psychotic disorder characterized by distortions of reality, disorganized thought and language, and withdrawal from social interaction, occurring in schizophrenia
