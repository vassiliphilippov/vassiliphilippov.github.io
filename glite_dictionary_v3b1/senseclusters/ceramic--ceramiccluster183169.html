ceramic

noun
object made from fired clay or similar material
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈræm.ɪk/ (AmE), /sə.ˈræm.ɪk/ (BrE)
an object made from a nonmetallic mineral that has been fired at a high temperature, such as pottery, tiles, or figurines

Examples

A ceramic vase
She collects ceramics from around the world, including plates, vases, and tiles.
The artist shaped the wet clay before firing it to create a unique ceramic sculpture.
The museum's exhibit featured an ancient ceramic bowl decorated with intricate patterns.

  • synonym: pottery - vessels or objects made of baked clay
