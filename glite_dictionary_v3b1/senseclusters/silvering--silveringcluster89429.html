silvering

noun
hair turning grey
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvərɪŋ / (BrE)
the gradual process of hair or fur turning grey or silver as a result of aging

Examples

Silvering of hair
The silvering of his beard gave him a distinguished appearance at the conference.
She noticed the first signs of silvering in her hair after her fortieth birthday.
The old dog's silvering muzzle showed his many years of loyal companionship.

Related Terms

  • antonym: darkening - the process of hair becoming darker in color
  • synonym: graying - the process of hair becoming grey with age
