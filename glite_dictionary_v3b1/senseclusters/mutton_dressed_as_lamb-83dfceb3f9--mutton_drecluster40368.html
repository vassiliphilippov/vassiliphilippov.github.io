mutton dressed as lamb

noun
older person trying to appear younger
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌtən drɛst æz læm / (AmE), / ˈmʌtən drɛst əz læm / (BrE)
a term describing an older person, especially a woman, who tries to dress or appear much younger than they are, often in a way considered unsuitable or inauthentic

Examples

Older mutton dressed as lamb
He tried to recapture his youth, but the result was just mutton dressed as lamb.
The term mutton dressed as lamb is often used unkindly about people ignoring their age.
Wearing that sparkly mini dress, she was accused of being mutton dressed as lamb.
