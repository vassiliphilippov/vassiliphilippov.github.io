balaclava

noun
close-fitting head and face covering
Pronunciation: / ˌbæləˈklɑvə / (AmE), / ˌbæləˈklɑːvə / (BrE)
A close-fitting woolen cap that covers the head, neck, and usually all of the face except the eyes.

Examples

A balaclava mask
During the bank robbery, the suspects were seen with balaclavas covering their faces.
He wore a balaclava to keep warm while skiing in the mountains.
Soldiers were issued a balaclava as part of their winter gear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ski mask - a mask that covers the head and face, typically worn by skiers
