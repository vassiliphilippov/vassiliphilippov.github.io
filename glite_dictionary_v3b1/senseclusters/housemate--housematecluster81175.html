housemate

noun
person sharing a house
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsˌmeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhaʊs.meɪt / (BrE)
a person who lives in the same house as someone else, especially when not a relative or partner

Examples

A housemate shares rent
After college, she found a housemate through an online listing service.
My housemate and I split the rent evenly each month.
Sometimes it can be tricky to adjust to a new housemate's habits.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flatmate - person sharing a flat (apartment), chiefly British
  • synonym: roommate - person sharing the same room, especially in a dormitory or apartment
