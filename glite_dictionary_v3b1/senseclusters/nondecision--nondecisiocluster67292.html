nondecision

noun
no official result
7.7
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.dɪ.ˈsɪ.ʒən/ /; / /nən.dɪ.ˈsɪ.ʒən/ (AmE), /nɒn.dɪ.ˈsɪ.ʒən/ /; / /nən.dɪ.ˈsɪ.ʒən/ (BrE)
an outcome in a sporting contest where no winner is declared and no official result is recorded

Examples

Boxing nondecision
The boxing match ended in a nondecision after an accidental injury stopped the fight.
Because of the weather, the game was ruled a nondecision and will not count in the standings.
Fans were disappointed when the highly anticipated bout resulted in a nondecision.
