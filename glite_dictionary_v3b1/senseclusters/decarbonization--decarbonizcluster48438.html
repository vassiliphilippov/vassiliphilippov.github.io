decarbonization

noun
removal of carbon
6.8
Pronunciation: /diː.kɑːr.bə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /də.kɑːr.bə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.kɑː.bə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /də.kɑː.bə.naɪ.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the process of removing carbon or reducing carbon content from a material, especially in industrial or chemical processes

Examples

Industrial decarbonization
The steel plant invested in new technology to improve the decarbonization of its products.
Environmental policies now encourage the decarbonization of industrial processes to reduce emissions.
Effective decarbonization is essential for producing high-quality metals in modern manufacturing.
