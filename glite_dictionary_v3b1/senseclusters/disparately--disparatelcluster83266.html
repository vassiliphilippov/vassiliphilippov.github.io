disparately

adverb
unequally or unevenly
7.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspærətli / (AmE), / dɪˈspærətli / (BrE)
in a way that treats people or things unequally or affects them to different degrees

Examples

Disparately given resources
Resources were distributed disparately across the regions, leaving some communities with much less support.
The new policy has disparately impacted small businesses compared to large corporations.
Technological advances have disparately benefited urban and rural areas.

Related Terms
