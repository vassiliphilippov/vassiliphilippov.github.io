broodingly

adverb
like incubating eggs
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbruːdɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner of sitting on or protecting eggs, as an incubating bird does.

Examples

Sit broodingly on eggs
The hen sat broodingly on her eggs, barely moving for hours.
He watched the nest broodingly, making sure no predators came near.
The bird returned broodingly to the nest after gathering food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protectively - in a way that protects or guards
