refry

verb
to rework old material
7.6
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈfraɪ/ /; / /riː.ˈfraɪ/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈfraɪ/ /; / /riː.ˈfraɪ/ (BrE)

Examples

To refry old ideas

Instead of creating something new, the article just refried old arguments.
