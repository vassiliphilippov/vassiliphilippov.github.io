Victorian

adjective
relating to Queen Victoria or her era
Pronunciation: / vɪkˈtɔriən / (AmE), / vɪkˈtɔːrɪən / (BrE)
of or relating to Queen Victoria of Great Britain, her reign (1837–1901), or the culture, morals, and styles of that period.

Examples

Victorian architecture
Her grandmother’s house was decorated in a distinctly Victorian style.
Some people view Victorian values as overly strict and conservative today.
The museum has an extensive collection of Victorian paintings and furniture.
