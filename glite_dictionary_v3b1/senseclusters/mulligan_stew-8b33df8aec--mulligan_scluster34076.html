mulligan stew

noun
thick, improvised meat and vegetable stew
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌlɪɡən stu / (AmE), / ˈmʌlɪɡən stjuː / (BrE)
A thick stew made of whatever meat and vegetables are available, traditionally associated with hobos or improvised meals.
