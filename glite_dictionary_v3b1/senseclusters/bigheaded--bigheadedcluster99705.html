bigheaded

adjective
overly conceited or arrogant
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
having an excessively high opinion of oneself; conceited or arrogant.

Examples

A bigheaded person
After winning the award, she became a bit bigheaded and started bragging.
He's so bigheaded that he can't accept any criticism.
Try not to get bigheaded just because everyone praised your presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrogant - having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities
  • antonym: humble - having or showing a modest or low estimate of one's importance
