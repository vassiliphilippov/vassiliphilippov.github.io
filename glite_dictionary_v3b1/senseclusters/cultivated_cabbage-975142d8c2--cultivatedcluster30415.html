cultivated cabbage

noun
edible Brassica oleracea varieties
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌltɪˌveɪtɪd ˈkæbɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd ˈkæbɪdʒ / (BrE)
Any of various cultivated forms of Brassica oleracea grown for their edible leaves or flowers, such as cabbage, broccoli, or cauliflower.
