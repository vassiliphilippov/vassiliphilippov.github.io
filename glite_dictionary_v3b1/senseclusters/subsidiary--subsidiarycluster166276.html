subsidiary

adjective
supporting or supplementary
Pronunciation: / səbˈsɪdiˌɛri / (AmE), / səbˈsɪdiəri / (BrE)
serving to assist or support; functioning in a subordinate or supplementary role to something more important

Examples

Subsidiary role
Most universities have subsidiary organizations that support student life.
The company established a subsidiary branch to handle customer service operations.
The research project received support from several subsidiary institutions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auxiliary - providing additional help or support
  • antonym: primary - of chief importance; principal
