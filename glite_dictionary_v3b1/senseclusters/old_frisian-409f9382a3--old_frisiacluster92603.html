Old Frisian

noun
historical West Germanic language of ancient Frisia
Pronunciation: / oʊld frɪˈziːən / (AmE), / əʊld frɪˈziːən / (BrE)
the West Germanic language spoken in Friesland until the 16th century, ancestral to modern Frisian

Old Frisian language
