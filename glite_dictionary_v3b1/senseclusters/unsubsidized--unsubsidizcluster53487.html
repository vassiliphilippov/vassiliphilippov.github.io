unsubsidized

adjective
without financial support
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsʌbsɪˌdaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd / (BrE)
not receiving financial support or assistance from an outside source, especially from the government

Examples

Unsubsidized loans
Many students rely on unsubsidized loans to cover their college expenses.
The project was entirely unsubsidized, so the team had to raise all the funds themselves.
Farmers with unsubsidized crops often face higher financial risks during bad weather.
