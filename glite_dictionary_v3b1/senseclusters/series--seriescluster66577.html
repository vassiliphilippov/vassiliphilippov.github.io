series

noun
set of stamps or coins with a common theme
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪriːz / (AmE), / ˈsɪəriːz / (BrE)
a set of postage stamps, coins, or currency notes with a common design or theme, often collected or issued together

Examples

Series of stamps
He collected every series of commemorative stamps released that year.
My grandfather's collection includes a rare series of Victorian era coins.
The bank introduced a new series of currency notes featuring national landmarks.
