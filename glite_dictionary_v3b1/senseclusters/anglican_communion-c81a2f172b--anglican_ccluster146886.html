Anglican Communion

proper noun
worldwide association of Anglican churches
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡlɪkən kəˈmjunjən / (AmE), / ˈæŋɡlɪkən kəˈmjuːnjən / (BrE)
The worldwide association of national and regional churches in full communion with the Church of England, with the Archbishop of Canterbury as its symbolic head.

Examples

Anglican Communion churches worldwide
Discussions within the Anglican Communion often focus on issues of doctrine and unity.
She attended a conference that gathered bishops from across the Anglican Communion.
The Anglican Communion includes churches in over 165 countries worldwide.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Church of England - the national church of England
