sallowness

noun
sickly yellowish skin complexion
Pronunciation: / ˈsæloʊnəs / (AmE), / ˈsaləʊnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of having a sickly yellow or pale complexion, especially of the skin.

Examples

Pale sallowness skin
After weeks indoors, there was a distinct sallowness to her complexion.
She noticed the sallowness of his cheeks and asked if he was feeling ill.
The patient's sallowness made the doctor suspect liver problems.
