vivipara

noun
live-bearing organism
Pronunciation: / vɪˈvɪpərə / (AmE), / vɪˈvɪpərə / (BrE)
an organism that gives birth to live young rather than laying eggs

Examples

vivipara fish species
Some species of fish are classified as vivipara because they give birth to live young.
Unlike most reptiles, these lizards are vivipara and do not lay eggs.
The study focused on the reproductive habits of vivipara in different environments.
