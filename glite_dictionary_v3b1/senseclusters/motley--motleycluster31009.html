motley

adjective
composed of varied, mismatched elements
5.0 6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑːt.liː/ (AmE), /ˈmɒt.liː/ (BrE)
consisting of a mixture of people or things that are very different from each other and do not seem to belong together

Examples

A motley crowd
She assembled a motley assortment of old chairs for the dinner party.
The exhibition attracted a motley crowd of artists, students, and tourists.
The pirates on the island were a motley band with wildly different backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assorted - of various different kinds; mixed
  • synonym: miscellaneous - consisting of many different kinds of things that are not connected
