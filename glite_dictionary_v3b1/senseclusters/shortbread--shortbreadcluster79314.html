shortbread

noun
rich, buttery biscuit or cookie
3.1 1.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɔrtˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈʃɔːtbɹɛd / (BrE)
a rich, crumbly biscuit made with a large amount of butter, flour, and sugar, typically associated with Scotland

Examples

A shortbread biscuit
I love the rich, buttery taste of homemade shortbread during the holidays.
She offered me a piece of shortbread with my afternoon tea.
Traditional Scottish shortbread is often baked in round shapes and cut into wedges.
