bottle-nosed dolphin

noun
species of dolphin with rounded forehead
7.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːt.əl.noʊzd ˈdɑːl.fɪn/ (AmE), /ˈbɒt.əl.nəʊzd ˈdɒl.fɪn/ (BrE)
