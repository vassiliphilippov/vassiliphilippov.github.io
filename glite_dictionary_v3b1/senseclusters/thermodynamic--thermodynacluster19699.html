thermodynamic

adjective
relating to thermodynamics
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌθɜːrmoʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (AmE), / ˌθɜːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with the science of thermodynamics, which deals with heat and its relation to energy and work.
