moonily

adverb
in a dreamy or absent-minded manner
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnɪli / (AmE), / ˈmuːnɪli / (BrE)
In a dreamy, absent-minded, or romantic manner.

Examples

To walk moonily
After hearing the good news, he walked moonily down the street, barely noticing his surroundings.
He stared moonily out of the window, lost in his thoughts.
She smiled moonily at the painting, imagining a world of her own.

Related Terms
