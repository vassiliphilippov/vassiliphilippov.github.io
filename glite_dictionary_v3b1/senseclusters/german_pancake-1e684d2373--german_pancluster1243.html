German pancake

noun
oven-baked puffy pancake, often lemon-flavored
2.4 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɝː.mən ˈpænˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɜː.mən ˈpænˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A thick, puffy, mildly sweet oven-baked pancake, usually flavored with lemon and served with confectioners' sugar, jam, or fruit sauce.

Examples

She served a delicious German pancake topped with fresh strawberries and powdered sugar for brunch.
