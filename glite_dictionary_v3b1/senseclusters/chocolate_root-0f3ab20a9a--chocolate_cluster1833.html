chocolate root

noun
a perennial plant with nodding, chocolate-colored flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑklət ruːt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒklət ruːt / (BrE)
a perennial herbaceous plant of the north temperate zone, with pinnate leaves and nodding flowers with a brown-purple calyx and orange-pink petals

The chocolate root plant
