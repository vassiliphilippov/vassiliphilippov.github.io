pristinely

adverb
perfectly cleanly
Pronunciation: / prɪˈstiːnli / (AmE), / prɪˈstiːnli / (BrE)
in a perfectly clean, fresh, or unspoiled manner

Examples

Pristinely clean floors
The museum's floors were pristinely polished, reflecting the light from the chandeliers above.
She kept her desk pristinely organized, with every item in its proper place.
The mountain air was pristinely fresh, untouched by pollution or noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immaculately - in a perfectly clean or neat way
  • antonym: messily - in a dirty or untidy way
  • synonym: spotlessly - in a way that is completely clean
