unneighborly

adjective
not friendly or kind as a neighbor
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈneɪbɚli /; / ʌnˈneɪbɚˌli / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪbəli /; / ʌnˈneɪb(ə)li / (BrE)
not friendly or considerate as a neighbor; lacking the helpfulness or goodwill expected from neighbors

Examples

Unneighborly attitude
Her unneighborly attitude discouraged others from reaching out.
His unneighborly remarks made everyone at the meeting uncomfortable.
Leaving trash in the hallway is a very unneighborly thing to do.
Her unneighbourly attitude made it difficult for others to approach her.
It was unneighbourly of him to complain about the children playing in the garden.
Leaving rubbish outside the building is considered very unneighbourly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neighborly - friendly and helpful as a neighbor
  • antonym: neighbourly - friendly, helpful, and considerate as a neighbor
  • synonym: unfriendly - not friendly or kind
