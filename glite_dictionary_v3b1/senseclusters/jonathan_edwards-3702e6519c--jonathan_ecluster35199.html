Jonathan Edwards

proper noun
American theologian and preacher (1703–1758)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɑːnəθən ˈɛdwərdz / (AmE), / ˌdʒɒnəθən ˈɛdwədz / (BrE)
(1703–1758) American theologian and preacher whose sermons and writings played a key role in the First Great Awakening, a period of religious revival in colonial America.

Examples

Jonathan Edwards sermons
Jonathan Edwards emphasized the importance of personal religious experience in his preaching.
Jonathan Edwards is considered one of the most influential theologians in American history.
Many students read the sermons of Jonathan Edwards to understand the First Great Awakening.
