Deinonychus

noun
a wolf-sized bipedal dinosaur with a large claw
9.9
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈnɑː.nɪ.kəs/ /; / /daɪ.ˈnɔː.nɪ.kəs/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈnɒ.nɪ.kəs/ (BrE)
a swift, agile, bipedal carnivorous dinosaur from the Cretaceous period, known for its large curved claw on each hind foot
