ling

noun
a water chestnut with two-pronged fruit
Pronunciation: / lɪŋ / (AmE), / lɪŋ / (BrE)
A type of water chestnut whose spiny fruit has two rather than four prongs, especially Trapa bicornis.

Examples

Ling water chestnut
Botanists collected samples of ling from the muddy pond.
The lings are harvested for their edible seeds in the late summer.
The spiny fruit of the ling is often used in Asian cuisine.
