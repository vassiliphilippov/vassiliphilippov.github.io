wing

noun
side of a military formation
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ / (BrE)
the side of a military or naval formation, especially a flank used for attack or defense

Examples

The army's left wing
Reinforcements were sent to strengthen the Western wing of the army.
The cavalry charged the enemy's left wing at dawn.
Their commander ordered troops to advance along the northern wing of the formation.
