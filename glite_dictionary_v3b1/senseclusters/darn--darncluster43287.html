darn

interjection
mild exclamation
6.7
Pronunciation: / dɑrn / (AmE), / dɑːn / (BrE)
a mild exclamation used to express annoyance, frustration, or surprise

Examples

Darn, that hurts
Darn, I forgot to bring my umbrella and now it's raining.
Oh darn, the printer is out of paper again.
Darn, I missed the last bus by just a minute.

Related Terms

  • synonym: damn - an exclamation expressing anger or annoyance, considered stronger than 'darn'
  • synonym: drat - a mild exclamation of annoyance or disappointment
