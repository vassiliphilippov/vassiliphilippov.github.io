gleaming

adjective
brightly shining or polished
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlimɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡliːmɪŋ / (BrE)
shining brightly with a steady, often subdued, light; polished or glossy in appearance.

Examples

Gleaming white teeth
Her gleaming white teeth shone as she smiled.
The antique car's gleaming chrome caught everyone's eye at the show.
The sun reflected off the gleaming surfaces of the skyscrapers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dull - not shiny; lacking brightness or gloss
  • synonym: glossy - shiny and smooth; having a surface that reflects light
