hour hand

noun
shorter clock hand showing hours
6.1 1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊɚ hænd / (AmE), / ˈaʊə hænd / (BrE)

Examples

The hour hand points

The hour hand moved slowly as the morning passed.
