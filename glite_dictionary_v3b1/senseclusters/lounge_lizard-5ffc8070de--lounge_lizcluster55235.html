lounge lizard

noun
idle man seeking women in lounges
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊndʒ ˌlɪzərd / (AmE), / ˈlaʊndʒ ˌlɪzəd / (BrE)
a man who frequents hotel or bar lounges, often seeking the company or financial support of women

Examples

lounge lizard at hotel
He gained a reputation as a lounge lizard after spending every evening at the hotel bar.
Nobody took the lounge lizard seriously, but he always seemed to find a new admirer.
The story features a slick lounge lizard who tries to charm wealthy women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gold digger - a person who forms relationships with others purely to obtain money or gifts
