slip

noun
a mooring place for a boat
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a place at a marina or dock where a boat or craft can be securely moored

Examples

A boat slip
Every slip at the harbor was occupied during the festival weekend.
The boat returned to its slip after a long day on the water.
We reserved a slip at the marina for our sailboat this summer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: berth - a place where a ship can be moored, usually larger than a slip
