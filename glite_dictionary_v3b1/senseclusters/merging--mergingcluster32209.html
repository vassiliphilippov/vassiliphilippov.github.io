  • fusion
    4.1 4.05/m
    fusion is joining two things into a single unit often in art or science, while merging means coming together as one.
  • unification
    4.3 4.51/m
    unification is bringing separate groups into one unit, often by law or policy, while merging is the general act of joining.
  • confluence
    7.0 1.29/m

  • coming together

  • integration
    4.0 14/m
