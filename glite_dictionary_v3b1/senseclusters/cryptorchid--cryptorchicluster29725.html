cryptorchid

noun
undescended testicles
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪpˈtɔːrkɪd / (AmE), / krɪpˈtɔːkɪd / (BrE)
a male animal or person whose testicles have not descended into the scrotum

Examples

A cryptorchid horse
The veterinarian diagnosed the colt as a cryptorchid after a thorough examination.
A cryptorchid may require surgery to correct the condition and prevent complications.
In some cases, a cryptorchid can still reproduce, but fertility may be reduced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: undescended testicle - a testicle that has not moved into the scrotum
