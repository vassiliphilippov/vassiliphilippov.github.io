ditalini

noun
small tube pasta
Pronunciation: / ˌditəˈlini / (AmE), / ˌdiːtəˈliːni / (BrE)
a type of small, short tube-shaped pasta often used in soups and salads

Examples

Ditalini pasta soup
The chef sprinkled cooked ditalini into the vegetable soup before serving.
For a quick lunch, she tossed ditalini with olive oil, tomatoes, and cheese.
Many Italian salads include ditalini because of its small, easy-to-eat shape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: macaroni - a type of short, curved tube-shaped pasta
  • degree higher: penne - a type of pasta in the shape of large tubes
