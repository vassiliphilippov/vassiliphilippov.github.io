lac

noun
resin from lac insects, used in varnish etc.
Pronunciation: / læk / (AmE), / læk / (BrE)
A resinous substance secreted by certain insects, used in making varnishes, dyes, and sealing wax.

Examples

A lac resin
The craftsmen coated the ornaments with lac to give them a glossy finish.
The extraction of lac from tree branches is an important industry in some regions.
Traditional sealing wax often contains lac as a main ingredient.
