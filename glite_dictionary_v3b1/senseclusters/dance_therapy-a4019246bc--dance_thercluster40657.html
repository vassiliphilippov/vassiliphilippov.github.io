dance therapy

noun
therapeutic use of dance
Pronunciation: / dæns ˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / dɑːns ˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a form of therapy that uses dance and movement to promote emotional, mental, and physical well-being

Examples

Dance therapy session
Many hospitals now offer dance therapy as a complementary treatment for patients recovering from trauma.
Participating in dance therapy can help individuals express emotions they may find difficult to verbalize.
She chose dance therapy to cope with stress and improve her mental health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: art therapy - use of artistic methods for therapeutic purposes
  • synonym: music therapy - use of music in therapeutic treatment
