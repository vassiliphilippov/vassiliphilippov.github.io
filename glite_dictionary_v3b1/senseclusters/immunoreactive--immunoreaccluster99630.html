immunoreactive

adjective
detectable by immune tests
Pronunciation: / ɪˌmjunəʊriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ɪˌmjuːnəʊriˈæktɪv / (BrE)
reacting to or detectable by the immune system or by immune tests

Examples

Highly immunoreactive tissue
The laboratory found the tissue to be highly immunoreactive when tested for specific antibodies.
Certain cells are immunoreactive and can be identified using special staining techniques.
The doctor explained that the tumor was immunoreactive to the marker used in the analysis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: immunonegative - showing no reaction in an immune test
  • synonym: immunopositive - showing a positive reaction in an immune test
