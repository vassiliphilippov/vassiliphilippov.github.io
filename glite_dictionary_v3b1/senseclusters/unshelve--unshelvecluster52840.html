unshelve

verb
to remove from shelf
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈʃɛlv / (AmE), / ʌnˈʃɛlv / (BrE)
to remove something, especially a book or product, from a shelf

Examples

To unshelve a book
The librarian needed to unshelve several books for the upcoming event.
Before restocking, employees must unshelve expired products from the display.
She decided to unshelve her favorite novel and read it again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remove - to take something away from a place
  • antonym: shelve - to place something on a shelf
