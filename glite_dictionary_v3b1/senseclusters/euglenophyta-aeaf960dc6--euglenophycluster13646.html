Euglenophyta

noun
a division of single-celled flagellate algae
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˌɡliːnəˈfaɪtə / (AmE), / juːˌɡliːnəˈfʌɪtə / (BrE)
A division of mostly single-celled aquatic algae that move by means of flagella, commonly known as euglenoids.
