resonating

adjective
producing resonance; echoing
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛzəˌneɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛzəneɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
producing or characterized by resonance; having a deep, clear, and continuing sound or effect

Examples

A resonating sound
His message had a resonating impact on those who heard it, lingering long after the speech ended.
She was drawn to the resonating tones of the antique bell in the courtyard.
The singer’s resonating voice filled the entire auditorium with rich, beautiful sound.
