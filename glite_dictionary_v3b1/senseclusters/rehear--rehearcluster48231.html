rehear

verb
to practice again
3.7
Pronunciation: / riˈhɪr / (AmE), / riˈhɪə / (BrE)
to practice or perform something again, especially to improve it or prepare for a final presentation

Examples

Choir rehears a song
The choir decided to rehear the song to perfect their harmonies before the concert.
Before the competition, the dancers will rehear their routine several more times.
The director asked the cast to rehear the final scene to ensure everyone was confident.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practice - to do something repeatedly to improve skill
  • synonym: rehearse - to practice a performance or speech before presenting it
