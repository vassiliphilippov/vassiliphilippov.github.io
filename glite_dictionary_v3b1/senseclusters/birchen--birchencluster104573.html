birchen

adjective
made of birch wood
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrtʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɜːtʃən / (BrE)
consisting of or made from the wood of the birch tree

Examples

A birchen rod
A birchen rod was once used as a form of punishment in schools.
The chair was crafted from birchen branches, giving it a rustic appearance.
We admired the delicate carvings on the birchen walking stick.
