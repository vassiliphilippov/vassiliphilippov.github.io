weather satellite

noun
satellite for observing weather
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðɚ ˈsæt̬əˌlaɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˈsætəlaɪt / (BrE)
an artificial satellite equipped to observe and relay information about the Earth's weather patterns and atmospheric conditions

Examples

A weather satellite orbiting Earth
A new weather satellite was launched last week to improve climate monitoring.
Data from a weather satellite helped meteorologists predict the storm's path.
The weather satellite captured images of the hurricane approaching the coast.
