passive

adjective
lacking energy or initiative
3.1 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpæs.ɪv/ (AmE), /ˈpæs.ɪv/ (BrE)
accepting things without taking action or resisting; not active or energetic

Examples

Passive attitude
John remained passive during the heated discussion, refusing to voice his opinion.
Laura's passive attitude made it difficult for her to stand up for herself.
The patient's passive behavior frustrated the nurses who wanted him to engage in therapy.
Many historical movements succeeded through passive means, such as boycotting and peaceful marches.
She chose a passive approach, refusing to fight back but standing her ground firmly against injustice.
The protesters adopted a strategy of passive resistance to avoid violent confrontation with the police.
