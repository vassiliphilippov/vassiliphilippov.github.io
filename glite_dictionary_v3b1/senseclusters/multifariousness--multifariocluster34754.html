multifariousness

noun
variety; diversity; manifoldness
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌl.tɪˈfer.i.əs.nəs / (AmE), / ˌmʌl.tɪˈfeə.ri.əs.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of having many different kinds, parts, or aspects; variety or diversity.

Examples

The multifariousness of styles
Critics praised the multifariousness in the artist's exhibition, noting the wide range of styles.
Society benefits from the multifariousness of cultures and traditions that enrich daily life.
The multifariousness of her talents makes her stand out in any team.

  • synonym: variety - the quality of being different or diverse; the absence of uniformity
