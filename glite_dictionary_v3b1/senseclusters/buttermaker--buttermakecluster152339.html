buttermaker

noun
butter-making machine
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a machine or device used to make butter, especially by churning cream or milk

Examples

Electric buttermaker
The old dairy still uses a traditional buttermaker to produce fresh butter every morning.
After cleaning the buttermaker, the workers prepared it for the next batch of cream.
Modern farms often rely on an electric buttermaker to speed up the butter-making process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: churn - a container or machine for making butter by shaking cream
