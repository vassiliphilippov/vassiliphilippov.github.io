Ipomoea tricolor

noun
species of morning glory plant
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪ.pəˈmiː.ə ˈtraɪ.kəl.ɔɹ / (AmE), / ˌɪ.pəˈmiː.ə ˈtrɪ.kə.lɔː / (BrE)
