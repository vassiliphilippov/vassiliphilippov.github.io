Eruca sativa

noun
Mediterranean plant; salad green (arugula, rocket)
Pronunciation: / əˈruːkə səˈtiːvə / (AmE), / ɪˈruːkə səˈtiːvə / (BrE)
An annual Mediterranean plant whose young leaves are harvested for use in salads, commonly known as arugula or rocket.
