unversed

adjective
inexperienced or unskilled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈvɜrst / (AmE), / ʌnˈvɜːst / (BrE)
having little or no experience, training, or knowledge in a particular subject or activity

Examples

To be unversed in law
Because he is unversed in legal matters, he hired an attorney to help him.
Many people feel unversed in the art world and hesitate to discuss paintings.
She was completely unversed in computer programming when she started the job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexperienced - having little knowledge or experience
  • antonym: versed - experienced or knowledgeable
