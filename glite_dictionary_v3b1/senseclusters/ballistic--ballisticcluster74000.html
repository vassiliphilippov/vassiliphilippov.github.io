ballistic

adjective
extremely fast or angry
6.5
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
moving extremely fast or becoming extremely angry or excited

Examples

Go ballistic with anger
When he heard the news, he went ballistic and started shouting at everyone.
The car went ballistic down the highway, easily passing all the other vehicles.
She became ballistic after discovering her laptop was missing from her desk.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing anger or excitement
  • synonym: furious - extremely angry
