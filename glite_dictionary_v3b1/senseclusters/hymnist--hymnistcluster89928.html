hymnist

noun
hymn singer or leader
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪmnɪst / (AmE), / ˈhɪmnɪst / (BrE)
a person who sings hymns or leads the singing of hymns, especially in a religious service

Examples

Church hymnist sings
The hymnist stood at the front, guiding the congregation through each verse.
As a skilled hymnist, she inspired everyone to sing with enthusiasm.
During the ceremony, the hymnist chose traditional songs that everyone knew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cantor - a person who leads singing in a religious service
  • synonym: choir - a group of people who sing together, especially in church
