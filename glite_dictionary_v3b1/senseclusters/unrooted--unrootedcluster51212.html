unrooted

adjective
without roots
5.8
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈruː.tɪd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈruː.tɪd/ (BrE)
not having developed or produced roots, especially referring to plants or cuttings lacking a root system

Examples

Unrooted plant cutting
The gardener planted several unrooted cuttings in moist soil to encourage growth.
It is important to keep unrooted stems moist until they develop roots.
Some species can survive for weeks as unrooted plants before establishing themselves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rooted - having developed roots or a root system
  • synonym: rootless - having no roots or not fixed in one place
