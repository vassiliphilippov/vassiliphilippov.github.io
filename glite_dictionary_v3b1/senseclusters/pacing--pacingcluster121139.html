pacing

noun
speed of music performance
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪsɪŋ / (BrE)
(music) The speed or rate at which a musical composition is played or performed.

Examples

Song pacing
Good pacing can dramatically affect the listener's emotional response to the music.
She adjusted the pacing to create more excitement in the middle of the composition.
The pacing of the piece was too slow for the energetic dance routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tempo - The rate of speed of a musical piece
