target organ

noun
organ targeted in radiation therapy
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑrɡɪt ˈɔrɡən /; / ˈtɑrɡət ˈɔrɡən / (AmE), / ˈtɑːɡɪt ˈɔːɡən / (BrE)
an organ in the body that is specifically affected by a treatment, drug, hormone, or radioactive substance
