backpack

noun
bag carried on the back
Pronunciation: / ˈbækˌpæk / (AmE), / ˈbæk.pæk / (BrE)
a bag with shoulder straps that you carry on your back to hold your belongings

Examples

A school backpack
Hikers should always carry a first aid kit in their backpack.
My backpack was so heavy that my shoulders started to ache during the trip.
She packed her books and lunch into her backpack before leaving for school.
After the hike, my shoulders ached from wearing a heavy backpack all day.
He bought a new backpack for his trip to Europe.
She carried her textbooks and lunch in a sturdy backpack.
