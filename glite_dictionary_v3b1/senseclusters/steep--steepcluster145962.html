steep

verb
soak in liquid to extract flavor or cleanse
3.5 0.96/m
Pronunciation: / stiÀêp / (AmE), / stiÀêp / (BrE)
to soak something in a liquid, especially to extract flavor or to cleanse

Examples

To steep tea
Before dyeing, they steep the fabric in warm water overnight.
It's traditional to steep dried herbs in hot oil to make an infusion.
You should steep the tea leaves for five minutes to get a rich flavor.
