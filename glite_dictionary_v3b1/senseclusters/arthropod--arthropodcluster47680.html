arthropod

noun
invertebrate with jointed legs and exoskeleton
Pronunciation: / ˈɑr θrəˌpɑd / (AmE), / ˈɑː θrəˌpɒd / (BrE)
an invertebrate animal with jointed limbs, a segmented body, and an exoskeleton made of chitin, belonging to the phylum Arthropoda

Examples

A arthropod animal
A characteristic feature of every arthropod is its segmented body and jointed legs.
Scientists discovered a new species of arthropod in the rainforest.
The arthropod family includes insects, spiders, and crustaceans like crabs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crustacean - an aquatic arthropod of the class Crustacea, such as a crab, lobster, or shrimp
  • synonym: insect - a small arthropod animal with six legs and generally one or two pairs of wings
