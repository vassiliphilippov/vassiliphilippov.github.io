reassimilate

verb
to integrate again
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌriːəˈsɪməˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˌriːəˈsɪmɪleɪt / (BrE)
to become part of a group, community, or society again, or to help someone do this after being away or separated

Examples

To reassimilate into society
After living overseas for a decade, he found it challenging to reassimilate into his home culture.
The program helps refugees reassimilate into their new communities after resettlement.
She needed time to reassimilate with her old friends after returning from university.
After years abroad, it took time for her to reassimilate into her hometown community.
The program helps refugees reassimilate by offering language classes and job training.
It can be challenging for former prisoners to reassimilate into society after their release.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to prevent someone from entering or being part of a group
  • synonym: integrate - to become part of a group or society
  • antonym: isolate - to separate from others or become alone
  • synonym: readapt - to adjust again to a new or changed environment
  • synonym: readjust - to adapt again to new or changed conditions
