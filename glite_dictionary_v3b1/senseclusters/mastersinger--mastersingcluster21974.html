mastersinger

noun
medieval German poet-musician
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmæstərˌsɪŋər / (AmE), / ˈmɑːstəˌsɪŋə / (BrE)
a member of a guild of poets and musicians in medieval Germany, known for composing and performing songs

Examples

Medieval mastersinger
The mastersinger was highly respected for his skill in both poetry and music.
During the festival, a mastersinger performed traditional songs for the townspeople.
Many legends tell of a mastersinger who won contests with his beautiful verses.
