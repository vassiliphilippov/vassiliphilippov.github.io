taiko

noun
Japanese drum
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪkəʊ / (BrE)
a large Japanese drum played with sticks, often used in traditional and modern musical ensembles

Examples

A taiko drum
The energetic taiko performance captivated everyone at the cultural festival.
She learned to play the taiko as part of a traditional Japanese music group.
The deep sound of the taiko echoed through the auditorium during the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drum - a percussion instrument sounded by being struck with sticks or hands
