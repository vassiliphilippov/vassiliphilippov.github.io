velocity

noun
Agile team output per sprint
5.3
Pronunciation: /və.ˈlɑː.sə.tiː/ (AmE), /və.ˈlɒ.sə.tiː/ (BrE)
(in Agile software development) a metric of a team's completed work in a sprint, usually measured in story points and used for planning and forecasting.

Examples

Team velocity in Agile
The project manager tracked the team's velocity to plan how much work to accept next sprint.
During the retrospective we discussed why our velocity fell after the holiday break last month.
Estimating future work is easier when the squad's average velocity is stable across sprints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: speed - the rate at which work or activity is done
  • synonym: throughput - the amount of work or number of items processed in a given period; often used similarly when planning teams
