shade

noun
covering for a lamp to soften or block light
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪd / (AmE), / ʃeɪd / (BrE)
a protective covering or device that blocks or reduces light, such as for a lamp or window

Examples

A window shade
A decorative shade can completely change the look of a table lamp.
She replaced the old shade on the lamp with a new blue one.
The shade helped reduce the brightness of the bulb in the living room.
He pulled down the window shade to block the bright sunlight.
The patio shade kept the living room cool throughout the afternoon.
We sat under the shade of a large umbrella to escape the scorching sun.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awning - a sheet of fabric or other material stretched on a frame and used to keep the sun or rain off a storefront, window, doorway, or deck
  • synonym: canopy - an overhead covering that provides shelter or shade
  • synonym: lampshade - a cover for a lamp, usually made of cloth, paper, or glass
