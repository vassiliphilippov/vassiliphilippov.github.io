darner

noun
person who repairs fabric by darning
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrnər / (AmE), / ˈdɑːnə / (BrE)
a person who repairs holes in fabric by darning

Examples

A skilled darner
In the old days, every household had a darner to keep clothing in good repair.
My grandmother was an expert darner who fixed socks for the whole family.
With fewer people learning to darn, finding a skilled darner has become difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darn - to repair a hole in fabric by weaving thread across it
