revert

verb
return to a previous state or condition
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɝt / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːt / (BrE)
to return to a previous state, condition, method, or activity

Examples

To revert to old habits
After months of healthy eating, he reverted to his old habits.
If the negotiations fail, the company may revert to its earlier strategy.
To fix the bug, the team reverted to the previous software version.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relapse - to fall back into a former state, especially a bad one
  • synonym: return - to come or go back to a place, situation, or condition
