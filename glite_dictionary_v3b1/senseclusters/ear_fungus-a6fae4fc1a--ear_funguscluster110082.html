ear fungus

noun
edible fungus shaped like a human ear
Pronunciation: / ɪr ˈfʌŋɡəs / (AmE), / ɪə ˈfʌŋɡəs / (BrE)
a widely distributed edible fungus, shaped like a human ear, that grows on decaying wood, especially in temperate and subtropical regions

Examples

Cooked ear fungus
Many Asian dishes include ear fungus for its unique texture and appearance.
She found several ear fungi growing on the old log in the forest.
The dried ear fungus must be soaked in water before cooking.
