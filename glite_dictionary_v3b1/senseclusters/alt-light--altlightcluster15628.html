alt-light

noun
right-wing subculture, distinct from 'alt-right'
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɑltˈlaɪt / (AmE), / ɒltˈlaɪt / (BrE)
A loosely defined political grouping or subculture characterized by right-leaning views that reject overtly extremist or white nationalist positions associated with the 'alt-right'.

Examples

The alt-light movement
Many journalists have written about the alt-light and its influence on online politics.
Several prominent speakers from the alt-light attended the event.
The alt-light distances itself from the more extreme elements of the alt-right.
