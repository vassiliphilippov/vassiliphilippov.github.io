filler

noun
pause word in speech
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪlər / (AmE), / ˈfɪlə / (BrE)
a word or sound used in speech to pause, think, or fill a gap without adding meaning

Examples

Speech filler words
People often use fillers like 'um' or 'uh' when they are thinking about what to say next.
In public speaking, reducing fillers can make your speech sound more confident and clear.
Children learning a new language may rely on fillers while searching for the right words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disfluency - an interruption or break in the flow of speech
  • synonym: pause - a temporary stop or break in speech
