obligately

adverb
necessarily, unavoidably
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːblɪɡətli / (AmE), / ˈɒblɪɡətli / (BrE)
in a way that is required or necessary, especially due to biological or environmental conditions

Examples

Obligately live inside
Some bacteria live obligately inside the cells of their hosts.
The species is obligately dependent on water for reproduction.
These animals feed obligately on a single type of plant throughout their lives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: necessarily - in a way that cannot be avoided
  • antonym: optionally - in a way that is not required; by choice
