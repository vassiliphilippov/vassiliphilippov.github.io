droning

noun
continuous, monotonous sound
Pronunciation: / ˈdroʊnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdrəʊnɪŋ / (BrE)
a continuous low, monotonous sound or tone; an unchanging intonation

Examples

A droning sound
Her speech was little more than a droning that made the audience lose interest.
The droning of the insects was the only sound in the empty field.
The droning of the refrigerator kept him awake at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buzz - a continuous, low, vibrating sound
  • synonym: hum - a continuous low sound
  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound
