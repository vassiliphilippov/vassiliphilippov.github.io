hutia

noun
Caribbean rodent
Pronunciation: / huːˈtiːə / (AmE), / huːˈtiːə / (BrE)
a large rodent native to the Caribbean islands, especially Cuba and nearby regions

Examples

The hutia rodent
The hutia is an endangered animal found in Cuba and nearby islands.
Scientists are studying the habitat of the hutia to help protect the species.
Local farmers sometimes spot a hutia searching for food near their crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: capybara - the largest rodent in the world, native to South America
  • synonym: rat - a medium-sized rodent found worldwide
