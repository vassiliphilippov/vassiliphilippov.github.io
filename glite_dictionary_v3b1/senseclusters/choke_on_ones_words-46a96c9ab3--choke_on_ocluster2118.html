choke on one's words

verb
be unable to speak due to nerves or emotion
7.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / t Éo äk …ën w ånz w…úrdz / (AmE), / t É…ô äk …ín w ånz w…úÀêdz / (BrE)

Examples
