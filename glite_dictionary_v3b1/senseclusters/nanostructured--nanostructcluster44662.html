nanostructured

adjective
having nanoscale structure
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnænoʊˈstrʌktʃərd / (AmE), / ˌnanəʊˈstrʌktʃəd / (BrE)
made with or having a structure on the scale of nanometers, often to achieve special properties

Examples

Nanostructured surface
The nanostructured surface of the device allows for improved sensitivity in detecting chemicals.
Researchers developed a nanostructured material that is both lightweight and extremely strong.
A nanostructured coating can make glass surfaces resistant to water and dirt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nanomaterial - a material with structure at the nanometer scale
