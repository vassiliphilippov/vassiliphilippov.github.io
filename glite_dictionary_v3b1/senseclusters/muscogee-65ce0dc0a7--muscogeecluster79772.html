Muscogee

proper noun
Native American tribe
Pronunciation: / mʌsˈkoʊgi / (AmE), / mʌsˈkəʊgi / (BrE)
the Native American people originally from the southeastern United States, also known as the Creek Nation

Examples

The Muscogee people
The Muscogee have a rich cultural heritage that is celebrated throughout the southeastern United States.
Many Muscogee gathered to celebrate the opening of the cultural center in their town.
The language of the Muscogee people is still taught in some schools today.

  • synonym: Creek - another name for the Muscogee people
