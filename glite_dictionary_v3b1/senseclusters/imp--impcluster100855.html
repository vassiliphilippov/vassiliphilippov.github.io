imp

noun
playfully mischievous person or child
Pronunciation: / ɪmp / (AmE), / ɪmp / (BrE)
A child or person who behaves in a playfully mischievous or naughty way.

Examples

A playful imp
At recess, the imp in the red hat led the others into harmless mischief.
He smiled like an imp after sneaking a cookie from the jar.
Their daughter is a little imp, always playing tricks on her brother.

Related Terms

  • antonym: angel - someone who is very good and well-behaved
  • synonym: rascal - a mischievous or cheeky person, especially a child
