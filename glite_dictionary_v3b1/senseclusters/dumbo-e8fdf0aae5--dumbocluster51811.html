DUMBO

proper noun
Brooklyn neighborhood
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
a neighborhood in Brooklyn, New York City, located between the Manhattan and Brooklyn Bridges, known for its converted warehouses, art spaces, and waterfront views

Examples

DUMBO neighborhood Brooklyn
They spent the afternoon exploring art galleries in DUMBO before walking along the waterfront.
The view of Manhattan from DUMBO is especially beautiful at sunset.
Many tech startups have opened offices in DUMBO because of its creative atmosphere.
