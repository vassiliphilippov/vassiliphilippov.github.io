vis-à-vis

noun
counterpart; person or thing with same function
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌviːz‿əˈviː / (AmE), / ˌviːz‿əˈviː / (BrE)
a person or thing having the same function or characteristics as another; a counterpart

Examples

A vis-à-vis in the team
Each department in the company has a vis-à-vis at headquarters to coordinate with.
The finance minister met with his German vis-à-vis to discuss the new agreement.
The senator arranged a meeting with her diplomatic vis-à-vis from France.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterpart - a person or thing corresponding to another in function or characteristics
