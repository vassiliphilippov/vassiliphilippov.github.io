offseason

adjective
during inactive period
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔfˌsiːzən / (AmE), / ˈɒfˌsiːzən / (BrE)
happening, produced, or used during the period when regular activity, such as a sports season, is reduced or paused

Examples

Offseason training camp
The team held an offseason camp to help players stay in shape.
Many athletes focus on recovery and skill development during the offseason months.
He found a part-time job to keep busy in the offseason.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in-season - relating to the main period of regular activity or competition
  • antonym: preseason - relating to the period before the main season begins
