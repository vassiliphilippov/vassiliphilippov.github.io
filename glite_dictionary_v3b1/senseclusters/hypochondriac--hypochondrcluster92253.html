hypochondriac

noun
person excessively worried about illness
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪ.poʊˈkɑn.dri.æk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.pəˈkɒn.dri.æk / (BrE)
a person who is excessively worried about having a serious illness, often without medical evidence

Examples

A hypochondriac person
Being a hypochondriac can make it difficult to enjoy life without constant anxiety.
Ever since his last checkup, Tom has been a hypochondriac who visits the doctor every week.
Her friends dismissed her constant complaints as those of a hypochondriac.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypochondria - excessive worry about one's health
