fuzzily

adverb
unclearly or blurrily
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌzɪli / (AmE), / ˈfʌzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is unclear, blurry, or not sharply defined

Examples

Fuzzily printed photo
The photograph was printed so fuzzily that I could not recognize anyone in it.
He remembered the details of the story only fuzzily after so many years.
Through the foggy window, the city lights shone fuzzily in the distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blurrily - in a way that is not clear or sharp
  • antonym: clearly - in a way that is easy to see or understand
