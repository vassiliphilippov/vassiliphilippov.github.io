router

noun
worker who directs shipments for delivery
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊtər / (AmE), / ˈraʊtə / (BrE)
a worker responsible for directing shipments or materials for distribution and delivery

Examples

Warehouse router
A skilled router can improve the entire warehouse's productivity.
After the morning rush, the router reviewed the day's delivery schedules.
The router makes sure all packages reach the correct destinations efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dispatcher - a person who sends out vehicles according to a schedule
