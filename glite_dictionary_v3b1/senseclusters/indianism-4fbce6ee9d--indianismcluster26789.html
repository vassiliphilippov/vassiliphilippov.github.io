Indianism

noun
Indian English feature
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiənɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɪndiənɪzəm / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or custom that is characteristic of Indian English or Indian culture

Examples

English Indianism word
The word 'prepone' is an Indianism that is widely used in India.
Many Indianisms have become part of everyday speech in Indian English.
He explained that the phrase was an Indianism not commonly understood outside India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Americanism - a word or phrase characteristic of American English
  • synonym: anglicism - a word or phrase characteristic of English used in another language
