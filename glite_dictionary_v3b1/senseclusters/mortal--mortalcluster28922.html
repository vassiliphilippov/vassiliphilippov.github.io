mortal

adjective
deadly; showing absolute enmity
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔrtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɔːtəl / (BrE)
(of hostility or conflict) persistent and deadly; showing absolute enmity or opposition likely to end in death.

Examples

Mortal enemy
He considered his rival a mortal enemy and would never forgive him.
She vowed mortal vengeance after the betrayal.
Their feud had become a mortal conflict, with neither side willing to compromise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deadly - causing or able to cause death
  • synonym: lethal - sufficient to cause death
  • synonym: venomous - showing strong hostility
