osteoclastic

adjective
bone tissue breakdown
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiəˈklæstɪk / (AmE), / ˌɒstiəˈklæstɪk / (BrE)
relating to the breaking down or resorption of bone tissue by osteoclasts

Examples

Osteoclastic activity
The doctor noted increased osteoclastic activity in the patient's bone scan.
Certain medications can inhibit osteoclastic processes to prevent bone loss.
Researchers study osteoclastic cells to better understand osteoporosis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: osteoblastic - relating to the formation of bone tissue
