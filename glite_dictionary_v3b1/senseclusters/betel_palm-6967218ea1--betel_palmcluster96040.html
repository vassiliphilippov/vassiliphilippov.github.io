betel palm

noun
tropical palm producing betel nuts
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêt…ôl p…ëÀêm / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêt…ôl p…ëÀêm / (BrE)
a tropical Southeast Asian palm tree (Areca catechu) that produces betel nuts, used especially for chewing in some cultures

Examples

Betel palm tree
Chewing the nut of the betel palm is a common practice in many Southeast Asian communities.
Farmers in the region traditionally grow betel palm along with coconut and banana.
