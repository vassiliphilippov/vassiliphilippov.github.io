incompatible

noun
things that do not match
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪnkəmˈpætəbəl / (BrE)
a person or thing that cannot exist or work together with another due to differences or conflict

Examples

Drug incompatibles
Some medicines are incompatibles and should never be taken together.
The new software and the old printer are incompatibles that cannot be connected.
Their personalities proved to be incompatibles, making teamwork difficult.

Related Terms

  • antonym: match - a person or thing that goes well with another
  • synonym: opposite - something completely different or contrary
