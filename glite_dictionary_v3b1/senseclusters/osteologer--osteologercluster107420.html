osteologer

noun
expert in the study of bones
Pronunciation: / ˌɑstiˈɑlədʒər / (AmE), / ˌɒstɪˈɒlədʒə / (BrE)
an anatomist who specializes in the study of bones (osteology)

Examples

An osteologer studies bones
An osteologer can provide crucial insights into the health of past populations through bone analysis.
As an osteologer, she identified species from skeletal fragments unearthed at the dig site.
The famous museum curator was an experienced osteologer specializing in fossil remains.
