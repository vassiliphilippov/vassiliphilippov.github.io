mime

noun
actor communicating by gesture without speech
3.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /maɪm/ (AmE), /maɪm/ (BrE)
an actor who performs and conveys stories or emotions entirely through gestures and facial expressions, without spoken words

Examples

A skilled mime
As a skilled mime, she could convey a wide range of emotions with just a look and a gesture.
Children watched the mime pretend to be trapped inside an invisible box.
The mime entertained the crowd with silent comedy in the town square.
