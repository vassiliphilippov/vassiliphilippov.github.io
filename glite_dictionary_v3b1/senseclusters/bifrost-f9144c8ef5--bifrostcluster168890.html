Bifrost

proper noun
mythical rainbow bridge
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌfrɔst / (AmE), / ˈbiːˌfrɒst / (BrE)
in Norse mythology, the rainbow bridge that connects Earth (Midgard) to the realm of the gods (Asgard)

Examples

The Bifrost bridge
According to legend, the gods traveled across the Bifrost to reach their home in Asgard.
The shimmering colors of the Bifrost were said to be visible to humans during storms.
Thor was often depicted crossing the Bifrost on his way to Earth.
