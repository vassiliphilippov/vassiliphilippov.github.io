muleta

noun
figurative support or excuse
9.9
Pronunciation: / muːˈleɪtə / (AmE), / mjuːˈleɪtə / (BrE)
something used as a support, aid, or excuse to hide a weakness or true purpose, especially in a figurative sense

Examples

A muleta excuse
The politician's apology was just a muleta to avoid answering difficult questions.
He used his busy schedule as a muleta for not attending the meeting.
The flashy event served as a muleta to distract the public from the company's financial troubles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crutch - something used for support or as an excuse
  • synonym: pretext - a reason given to hide the real purpose
