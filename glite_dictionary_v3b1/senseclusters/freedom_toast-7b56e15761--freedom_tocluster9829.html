freedom toast

noun
toast for liberty or new beginnings
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfriːdəm toʊst / (AmE), / ˈfriːdəm təʊst / (BrE)
A spoken or ceremonial toast made to celebrate liberty, independence, or the start of something new.

Examples

A freedom toast speech
At midnight, everyone stood and raised their glasses for a freedom toast to the nation's independence.
She proposed a heartfelt freedom toast to mark the beginning of their new venture.
The dinner ended with a freedom toast in honor of those who fought for their rights.
