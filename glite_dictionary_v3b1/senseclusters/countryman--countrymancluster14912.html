countryman

noun
man from the same country
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntrimæn / (AmE), / ˈkʌntrɪmən / (BrE)
a man from the same country as oneself

Examples

Fellow countryman
At the event, I recognized a countryman among the crowd.
During the interview, she was asked how she supported her countryman abroad.
He was delighted to meet a fellow countryman so far from home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compatriot - a fellow countryman; someone from the same country
