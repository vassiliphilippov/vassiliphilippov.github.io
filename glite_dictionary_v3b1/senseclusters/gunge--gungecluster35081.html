gunge

verb
to cover with slime
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɡʌndʒ / (AmE), / ɡʌndʒ / (BrE)
to cover someone or something with a thick, sticky substance, often as a joke or punishment, especially on television shows

Examples

To gunge a contestant
The losing team was gunged with bright pink slime at the end of the game show.
If you answer incorrectly, you might get gunged in front of the whole audience.
She laughed as her friend was gunged during the messy television challenge.
