casemaker

noun
case or box manufacturer
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪsˌmeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈkeɪsˌmeɪkə / (BrE)
a person or company that manufactures cases or boxes to protect, carry, or store goods

Examples

Professional casemaker
The casemaker designed a custom foam interior for the fragile camera before shipping.
Many musicians rely on a trusted casemaker to protect their valuable instruments during travel.
The electronics company partnered with a local casemaker to create durable packaging for their new devices.
