shouted

adjective
spoken forcefully in a shout
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊtɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtɪd / (BrE)
(of words or speech) spoken loudly with force or emotion, especially in a shout.

Examples

A shouted answer
Everyone was startled by her shouted response during the meeting.
His shouted warnings saved the hikers from danger.
The coach's shouted instructions could be heard across the entire field.
