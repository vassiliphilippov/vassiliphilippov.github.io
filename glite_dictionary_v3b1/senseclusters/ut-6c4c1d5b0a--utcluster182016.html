UT

noun
Universal Time standard (Greenwich meridian)
5.1
Pronunciation: /juː tiː/ /; / /ʊt/ /; / /ʌt/ (AmE), /juː tiː/ /; / /ʊt/ /; / /ʌt/ (BrE)
abbreviation for Universal Time, the local time at the prime meridian (0° longitude) in Greenwich, England, used as a standard worldwide.

Examples

12:00 UT
Astronomers record observation times in UT to avoid confusion across different time zones.
Meeting times are sometimes set in UT so participants can coordinate internationally.
The rocket launch was scheduled for 13:00 UT regardless of local time.

Related Terms
