unphysical

adjective
not material
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfɪzɪkəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfɪzɪkəl / (BrE)
not related to the physical body or material world; immaterial, spiritual, or mental

Examples

Unphysical mind
Some philosophers believe that consciousness is an unphysical phenomenon that cannot be explained by science alone.
The story explores unphysical realms where thoughts and emotions shape reality.
He argued that love is an unphysical force, not bound by the laws of nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: immaterial - not consisting of matter; spiritual or mental
  • antonym: physical - relating to the body or material things
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the soul or spirit, not physical things
