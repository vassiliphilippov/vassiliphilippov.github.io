unscripted

adjective
not following a script; improvised
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈskrɪptɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈskrɪptɪd / (BrE)
not prepared from or following a written script; spontaneous or improvised, especially in broadcasting or performances.

Examples

An unscripted show
Many interviews on the podcast are entirely unscripted and flow naturally.
The actors delivered an unscripted performance that thrilled the audience.
The debate quickly turned unscripted as the speakers abandoned their prepared remarks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: improvised - created and performed spontaneously or without preparation
  • antonym: scripted - prepared using a written script
