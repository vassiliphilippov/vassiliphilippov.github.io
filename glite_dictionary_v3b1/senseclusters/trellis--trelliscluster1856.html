trellis

noun
framework for supporting climbing plants
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛlɪs / (AmE), / ˈtrɛlɪs / (BrE)
a framework of light wooden or metal bars, chiefly used as a support for climbing plants

Examples

Garden trellis
I painted the trellis green to match the rest of the backyard décor.
The roses climbed up the trellis along the garden wall.
We installed a wooden trellis to support the growing grapes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lattice - an interlaced structure or pattern resembling a grid
