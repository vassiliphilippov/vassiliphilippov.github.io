unsoftened

adjective
not palatalized
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɑftənd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɒfənd / (BrE)
in phonetics, pronounced without palatalization or without being made softer by a palatal or similar articulation

Examples

Unsoftened consonant
In Russian, the consonant unsoftened 't' sounds harder than its palatalized counterpart.
The linguist explained how unsoftened consonants differ from their softened forms in pronunciation.
Some languages make a clear distinction between unsoftened and palatalized sounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hard - pronounced with a hard or unsoftened articulation
  • antonym: palatalized - pronounced with the tongue near the hard palate
