fall off

verb
become detached or come off
2.3 3.96/m
Pronunciation: / f…îl …îf / (AmE), / f…îÀêl …íf / (BrE)
to become detached or separated from something by dropping or breaking away

Examples

Fall off the chair
Be careful with that chair leg; it might fall off if you sit down too hard.

The leaves fall off the trees every autumn.
