healthy

adjective
sensible or showing good judgment
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlθi / (AmE), / ˈhɛlθi / (BrE)
(of an attitude, fear, relationship, etc.) showing good judgment, prudence, or being sensible, rather than excessive or lacking.

Examples

Healthy attitude
Her healthy skepticism helped her make more informed decisions.
It's wise to maintain a healthy fear of wild animals when hiking in remote areas.
The company encourages healthy competition among its employees to promote innovation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: imprudent - not showing care for the consequences of an action
  • synonym: sensible - practical and showing good judgment
  • synonym: sound - sensible; based on reason or good judgment
