cheek

noun
side of the face below the eye
1.0
Pronunciation: / tʃik / (AmE), / tʃiːk / (BrE)
either side of the face below the eye, forming part of the face between the nose and the ear

Examples

Red cheek
After running, his cheeks were bright red from the cold air.
She gently touched her daughter's cheek and smiled warmly.
The baby has the softest cheeks I've ever felt.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: chin - the protruding part of the face below the mouth
  • synonym: jaw - the lower part of the face used for chewing
