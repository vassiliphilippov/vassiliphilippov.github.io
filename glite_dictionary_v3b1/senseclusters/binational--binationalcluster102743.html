binational

adjective
of two nationalities
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
having legal citizenship or official ties to two different nations at the same time

Examples

Binational citizenship
She is binational, holding both French and Canadian citizenship.
The binational agreement improved cooperation between the two countries.
Growing up in a binational family gave him a unique cultural perspective.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dual citizenship - the status of being a citizen of two countries
  • degree higher: multinational - involving or relating to several countries or nationalities
