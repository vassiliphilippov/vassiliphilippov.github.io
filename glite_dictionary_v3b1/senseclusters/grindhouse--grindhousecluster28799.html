grindhouse

adjective
gritty film style
Pronunciation: / ˈɡraɪndˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈɡraɪndˌhaʊs / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of grindhouse films, especially being gritty, low-budget, and sensational in style

Examples

Grindhouse style movie
The movie's grindhouse aesthetic included over-the-top violence and a deliberately rough look.
Fans of grindhouse cinema appreciate its raw, unpolished storytelling and shocking visuals.
The director's latest film is a tribute to classic grindhouse features from the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploitation - relating to films that sensationalize controversial or shocking content
  • antonym: mainstream - typical or conventional, not unusual or sensational
