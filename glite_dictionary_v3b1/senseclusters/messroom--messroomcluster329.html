messroom

noun
military dining room
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsˌruːm / (AmE), / ˈmɛsˌruːm / (BrE)
a room where members of a group, especially in the military, eat meals together

Examples

The messroom for officers
The officers met in the messroom every evening to discuss the day's events.
After a long shift, the crew relaxed and ate together in the messroom.
The messroom was filled with laughter as the team shared stories over dinner.
