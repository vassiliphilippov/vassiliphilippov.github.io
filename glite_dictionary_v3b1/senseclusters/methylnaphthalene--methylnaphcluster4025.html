methylnaphthalene

noun
naphthalene derivative
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛθəlˈnæfθəˌliːn /; / ˌmɛθəlˈnæfθəˌlin /; / ˌmɛθilˌnæfθəˈliːn / (AmE), / ˌmiːθɪlˈnæfθəliːn /; / ˌmiːθəlˈnæfθəliːn /; / ˌmiːθɪlˌnæfθəˈliːn / (BrE)
a chemical compound derived from naphthalene by replacing one hydrogen atom with a methyl group; a colorless aromatic hydrocarbon used in industry
