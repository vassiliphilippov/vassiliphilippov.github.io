handloader

noun
person reloading ammunition
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌloʊdər / (AmE), / ˈhændˌləʊdə / (BrE)
a person who makes or reloads ammunition cartridges by hand rather than buying factory-made ones

Examples

Ammunition handloader
The experienced handloader spent hours preparing custom ammunition for his rifle.
As a dedicated handloader, she enjoys experimenting with different powder charges.
Many handloaders believe their homemade cartridges are more accurate than store-bought ones.
