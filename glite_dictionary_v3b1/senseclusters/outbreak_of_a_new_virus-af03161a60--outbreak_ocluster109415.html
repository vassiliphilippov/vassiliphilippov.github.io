outbreak of a new virus

noun
sudden emergence of a new viral disease
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbreɪk əv ə nu ˈvaɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌbreɪk ɒv ə njuː ˈvaɪərəs / (BrE)
a sudden occurrence of a previously unknown viral disease within a population

Examples

The outbreak of a new virus
Health authorities mobilized quickly to contain the outbreak of a new virus.
Hospitals struggled to cope during the outbreak of a new virus last winter.
Scientists are monitoring the outbreak of a new virus in several regions across the globe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: epidemic - a widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community
  • synonym: outbreak - a sudden start of something unwelcome, like disease or violence
  • degree higher: pandemic - an epidemic that spreads over a whole country or the world
