courser

noun
a swift-running bird of Asia and Africa
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrsər / (AmE), / ˈkɔːsə / (BrE)
a swift-running bird of southern Asia and Africa, resembling plovers and related to pratincoles

Examples

Indian courser
Researchers tracked the movements of the Indian courser during the breeding season.
Several species of courser can be found in the grasslands of Africa.
The courser is well adapted to running swiftly across open ground.
