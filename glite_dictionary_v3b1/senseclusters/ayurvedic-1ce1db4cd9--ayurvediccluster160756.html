Ayurvedic

adjective
traditional Indian medicine
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪjərˈveɪdɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪjʊəˈveːdɪk / (BrE)
relating to Ayurveda, the traditional system of medicine from India based on natural remedies and holistic health practices

Examples

Ayurvedic medicine
Many people seek Ayurvedic treatments for chronic health conditions.
The spa offers a range of Ayurvedic therapies, including herbal massages and detox programs.
She follows an Ayurvedic diet to improve her overall well-being.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allopathic - relating to conventional Western medicine
  • synonym: holistic - considering the whole person, not just symptoms
