encircle

verb
to bind or surround in a circle
2.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /en.ˈsɜːr.kəl/ /; / /ɪn.ˈsɜːr.kəl/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈsɜː.kəl/ /; / /en.ˈsɜː.kəl/ (BrE)
to form a circle around someone or something; to surround completely in a circular way

Examples

To encircle a group
A ring of children encircled the magician, eager for his next trick.
She used a silk scarf to encircle her waist and tie the dress.
The castle was encircled by a deep, wide moat.
His arms encircled her shoulders in a warm embrace.
Police vehicles began to encircle the building after spotting suspicious activity.
She used a red pen to encircle all the correct answers on the test.
