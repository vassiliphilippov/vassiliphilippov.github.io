hick

adjective
unsophisticated and provincial
6.3
Pronunciation: / hɪk / (AmE), / hɪk / (BrE)
(of a person or place) awkwardly simple, unsophisticated, and characteristic of the countryside; provincial.

Examples

Hick style clothes
His clothes looked a bit hick compared to the city's fashionable crowd.
She thought the little diner was too hick for her tastes.
They dismissed the town's traditions as old-fashioned and hick.
