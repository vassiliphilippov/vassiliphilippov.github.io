Mexican Spanish

noun
Spanish dialect spoken in Mexico
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈspænɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌmɛksɪkən ˈspænɪʃ / (BrE)
the variety of the Spanish language as spoken in Mexico, characterized by distinctive pronunciation, vocabulary, and expressions

Examples

Mexican Spanish dialect
Her grandmother speaks only Mexican Spanish at home.
Many people in Los Angeles are fluent in Mexican Spanish as well as English.
Some vocabulary in Mexican Spanish differs from what you hear in Spain.
