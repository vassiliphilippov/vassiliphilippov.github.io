bezique

noun
a two-player card game with a special deck
Pronunciation: / bəˈzik / (AmE), / bəˈziːk / (BrE)
A card game for two players, played with a pack of forty-eight cards (two of each high card in each suit), in which the play resembles whist.

Examples

A game of bezique
A set of special cards is required for a proper game of bezique.
She taught her grandson how to play bezique during the holidays.
They spent the evening playing bezique by the fire.
