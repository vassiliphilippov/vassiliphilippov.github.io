magnetospheric

adjective
of a planet's magnetic field
Pronunciation: / ˌmæɡnɪtoʊˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌmæɡnɪtəʊˈsfɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to the region around a planet that is controlled by its magnetic field

Examples
