timeful

adjective
relating to time
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪmfəl / (AmE), / ˈtaɪmfəl / (BrE)
relating to or taking time into account; concerned with the passage or measurement of time.

Examples

Timeful analysis
The scientist proposed a timeful approach to understanding climate change over centuries.

A timeful analysis can reveal patterns that are not obvious in a single moment.
