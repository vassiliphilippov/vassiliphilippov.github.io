rallying

adjective
inspiring unity and renewed effort
Pronunciation: / ˈræliɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈræliɪŋ / (BrE)
bringing people together to inspire unity and renewed effort, especially in support of a cause or action.

Examples

Rallying speech
Her words had a rallying effect on the divided community.
The leader delivered a rallying speech to boost the troops' morale.
They issued a rallying call to supporters ahead of the decisive vote.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inspiring - arousing enthusiasm or motivation
