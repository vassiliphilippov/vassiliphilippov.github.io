Synonyms

    4.8 0.01/m
    Bahasa Indonesia is the national standard used across Indonesia in schools and government, while Bahasa is the general name for those national Malay varieties.
  • Indonesian
    2.9 0.07/m
    Indonesian is the English name for the national Malay used in Indonesia, while Bahasa is the local general term for those varieties.
  • Bahasa Malaysia
    6.7 0.01/m
    Bahasa Malaysia is the formal national Malay used in schools and media in Malaysia, while Bahasa is a broader name for such standardized Malay.
  • Bahasa Melayu
    5.8 0.00/m
    Bahasa Melayu is the Malay spoken across Malaysia and nearby regions, while Bahasa refers to the specific national standardized Malay varieties.
