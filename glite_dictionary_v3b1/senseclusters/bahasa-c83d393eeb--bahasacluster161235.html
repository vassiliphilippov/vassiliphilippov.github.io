Bahasa

noun
national language of Indonesia or Malaysia
Pronunciation: / bɑːˈhɑːsə / (AmE), / bɑːˈhɑːsə / (BrE)
The official national language of Indonesia (Bahasa Indonesia) or Malaysia (Bahasa Malaysia); the standardized dialect of Malay used in these countries.

Examples

Bahasa Indonesia language
Her fluency in Bahasa helped her make friends while living in Jakarta.
I am taking classes to learn Bahasa before visiting Indonesia.
Many official documents in Malaysia are written in Bahasa.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Malay - A major Austronesian language spoken in Southeast Asia
