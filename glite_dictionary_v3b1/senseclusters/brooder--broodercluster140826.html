brooder

noun
person who worries
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdər / (AmE), / ˈbruːdə / (BrE)
a person who thinks deeply or worries often, especially in a silent or withdrawn way

Examples

A quiet brooder
He is a natural brooder, always lost in his own thoughts.
As a brooder, she often spends hours reflecting on her worries.
People sometimes mistake a brooder for being unfriendly, but he is just deep in thought.

  • synonym: worrier - a person who worries a lot
