ABO group

noun
human blood type classification system
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈɑː.bəʊ gruːp/ /; / /ˈæ.boʊ gruːp/ (AmE), /ˈæ.bəʊ gruːp/ /; / /ˈeɪ.biː.əʊ gruːp/ (BrE)
a system for classifying human blood based on the presence or absence of A and B antigens, dividing blood into four groups: A, B, AB, and O

Examples
