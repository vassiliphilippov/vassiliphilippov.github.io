egocentrically

adverb
self-centeredly
8.2
Pronunciation: /iː.ɡoʊ.ˈsen.trɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /iː.ɡəʊ.ˈsen.trɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)
in a self-centered way, focusing only on yourself and not considering others

Examples

To act egocentrically
He answered the question egocentrically, ignoring how his decision would affect the rest of the team.
Children often view the world egocentrically before they learn to understand other perspectives.
She described the event egocentrically, focusing only on her own experience.
She egocentrically dominated the conversation, rarely letting anyone else speak.
The manager made decisions egocentrically, without considering the team's opinions.
He egocentrically assumed his needs were more important than everyone else's.
