staurikosaur

noun
primitive dinosaur from Brazil
9.9
Pronunciation: / stɔːˈrɪkəˌsɔr / (AmE), / stɔːˈrɪkəsɔː / (BrE)
a primitive, small, bipedal dinosaur from the Late Triassic period, fossils of which have been found in Brazil

Examples

A staurikosaur dinosaur
A staurikosaur could run quickly on its two hind legs, hunting small prey.
Paleontologists study the staurikosaur to learn about early dinosaur evolution.
The fossilized bones of a staurikosaur were discovered in southern Brazil.
