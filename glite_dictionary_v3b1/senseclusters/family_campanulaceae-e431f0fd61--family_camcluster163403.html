family Campanulaceae

noun
a family of flowering plants (bellflowers)
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli kæmˌpænjuˈleɪsiː / (AmE), / ˈfæməli kamˌpanjuːˈleɪsiː / (BrE)
a family of flowering plants, including bellflowers, of the order Campanulales; sometimes including Lobeliaceae in some classifications

Examples

family Campanulaceae plants
Botanists often debate whether Lobelia should be part of the family Campanulaceae.
Several ornamental plants belong to the family Campanulaceae, popular in gardens worldwide.
The family Campanulaceae includes many species known for their bell-shaped flowers.
