squash vine

noun
trailing plant of the genus Cucurbita
Pronunciation: / skwɑʃ vaɪn / (AmE), / skwɒʃ vaɪn / (BrE)
any of various annual trailing plants of the genus Cucurbita grown for their fleshy edible fruits, such as pumpkins and squashes

Examples

After harvesting, the withered squash vine was removed to prepare the soil for next year.
Farmers often protect the squash vine from pests to ensure a healthy crop.
The squash vine rapidly spread across the garden, producing large yellow blossoms.
