wild plum tree

noun
uncultivated plum tree or shrub
Pronunciation: / waɪld plʌm triː / (AmE), / waɪld plʌm triː / (BrE)
A plum tree or shrub that grows naturally without cultivation and produces wild fruit.

Examples

A wild plum tree blossom
In spring, the wild plum tree bursts into beautiful white blossoms.
The park ranger explained that the wild plum tree is native to this region.
We found a wild plum tree in the woods, covered in tiny purple fruits.
