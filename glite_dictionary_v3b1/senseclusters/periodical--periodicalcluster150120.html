Synonyms

  • periodic
    3.9 2/m
    periodic is the common adjective for events that repeat regularly, while periodical also appears as a noun for magazines and sounds less common.
  • recurring
    3.3 0.00/m
    recurring means happening again at intervals, while periodical stresses a regular, repeated schedule for events or publications.
  • recurrent
    4.7 0.5/m
    recurrent describes something that returns often and sometimes unexpectedly, while periodical implies a planned, regular timing.
  • cyclical
    5.1 1/m
    cyclical describes things that move through a repeating cycle, while periodical focuses on regular timing rather than cycle shape.
  • cyclic
    4.8 0.2/m
    cyclic is a technical word for repeating cycles in science, while periodical is a general term for regular recurrence.
