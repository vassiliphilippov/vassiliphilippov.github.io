terminally online

adjective
constantly engaged with the internet
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝː.mə.nəl.i ɑnˈlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɜː.mɪ.nəl.i ɒnˈlaɪn / (BrE)
Spending so much time on the internet, especially on social media, that it noticeably affects one's behavior, views, or well-being.

Examples

Terminally online person
My friend is so terminally online that he knows every internet meme before I do.
Sometimes being terminally online makes it hard to relate to people offline.
Their terminally online perspective can make minor online debates seem extremely important.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chronically online - spending an excessive and habitual amount of time on the internet
