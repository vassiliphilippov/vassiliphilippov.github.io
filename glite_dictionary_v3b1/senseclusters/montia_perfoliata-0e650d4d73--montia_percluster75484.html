Montia perfoliata

noun
succulent herb, also called miner's lettuce
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑntiə pɜrˌfoʊliˈeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈmɒntiə pɜːˌfəʊliˈɑːtə / (BrE)
A succulent herb native to western North America, sometimes cultivated for use as a salad green or pot herb; also known as miner's lettuce.
