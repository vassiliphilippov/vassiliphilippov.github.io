wanted list

noun
list of desired or sought items/people
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːntɪd lɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɒntɪd lɪst / (BrE)
a list of items or people that are desired or being actively sought after

Examples

A wanted list of items
Before going shopping, he made a wanted list of all the new electronic gadgets he wanted to buy.
Maria updates her wanted list every month to keep track of books she hopes to read.
The police added the fugitive's name to the wanted list after his escape from prison.
