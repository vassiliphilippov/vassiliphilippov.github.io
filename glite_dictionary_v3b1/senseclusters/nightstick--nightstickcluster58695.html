nightstick

noun
police officer's club
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪtˌstɪk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪtˌstɪk / (BrE)
a short, heavy club carried by police officers as a weapon or for crowd control

Examples

A police nightstick
She saw a nightstick hanging from the officer's belt.
The police officer gripped his nightstick as he monitored the crowd.
The suspect was struck on the arm with a nightstick during the scuffle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: truncheon - a short thick stick carried as a weapon by a police officer
