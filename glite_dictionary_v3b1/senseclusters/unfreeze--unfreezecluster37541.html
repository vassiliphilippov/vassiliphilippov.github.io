unfreeze

verb
make or become liquid after freezing
5.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfriːz / (AmE), / ʌnˈfriːz / (BrE)
to make or become soft or liquid after being frozen; to thaw

Examples

To unfreeze the meat
She left the meat out to unfreeze overnight.
The lake will unfreeze in the spring when the weather gets warmer.
You need to unfreeze the chicken before cooking it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: defrost - to remove frost or ice; to thaw
  • antonym: freeze - to become hard or solid as a result of extremely cold temperatures
  • synonym: thaw - to become liquid or soft after being frozen
