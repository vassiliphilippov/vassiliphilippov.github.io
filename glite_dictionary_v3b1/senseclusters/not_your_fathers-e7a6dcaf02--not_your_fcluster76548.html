markedly different from the past; modern or advanced
1.8
Pronunciation: / nɑt jʊr ˈfɑðɚz / (AmE), / nɒt jɔː ˈfɑːðəz / (BrE)
(informal) Used to indicate that someone or something is markedly different from what existed or was typical in the past, usually due to being more advanced, modern, or unconventional.

Examples

Not your father's television
Streaming services are not your father's television.
This electric car is not your father's automobile.
Today's video games are not your father's entertainment.
