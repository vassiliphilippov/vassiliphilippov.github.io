totalitarianism

noun
absolute dictatorial government system
3.3 7.4
Pronunciation: /toʊ.tæl.ə.ˈter.i.ən.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /toʊ.tæl.ɪ.ˈter.i.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /təʊ.tæl.ɪ.ˈteə.ri.ən.ɪ.zəm/ /; / /təʊ.tæl.ə.ˈteə.ri.ən.ɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a political system or belief in which the government has absolute power and controls all aspects of public and private life
