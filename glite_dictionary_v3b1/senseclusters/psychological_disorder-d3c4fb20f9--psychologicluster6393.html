A condition characterized by abnormal thoughts, feelings, or behaviors that impair functioning or distress an individual; a more neutral term than mental illness.

Examples

A psychological disorder diagnosis
Many people mistakenly believe that anyone with a psychological disorder is dangerous, which is not true.
The doctor diagnosed him with a psychological disorder after several weeks of evaluation.
Therapy and medication can both be effective treatments for various psychological disorders.

Synonyms

  • mental disorder
    3.3 2/m
    mental disorder describes a clinical condition of disrupted thinking or behavior used in medicine, while psychological disorder is a neutral, general term.
  • mental disturbance
    6.1 0.07/m
    mental disturbance names milder or temporary psychiatric changes often seen in clinics, while psychological disorder covers any condition that impairs functioning.
  • mental illness
    3.1 9/m
    mental illness refers to conditions often serious enough to need medical treatment, while psychological disorder is a broader, more neutral label.
