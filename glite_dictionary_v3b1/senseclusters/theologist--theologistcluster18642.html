theologist

noun
person learned in theology
Pronunciation: / θiˈɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / θɪˈɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who studies theology or speculates about religious questions

Examples

A theologist studies religion
As a theologist, he often contributed to debates on the nature of faith.
Several theologists attended the international conference on world religions.
She aspired to become a renowned theologist after years of studying religious texts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: theologian - an expert in theology
