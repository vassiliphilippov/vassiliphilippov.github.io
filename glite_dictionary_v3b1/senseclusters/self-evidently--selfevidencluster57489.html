self-evidently

adverb
in an obvious or clear manner
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlfˈɛvɪdəntli / (AmE), / ˌsɛlfˈɛvɪdəntli / (BrE)
In a way that is obvious or does not need to be demonstrated or explained.

Examples

Is self-evidently true
His argument was self-evidently flawed, as no evidence supported his claims.
It is self-evidently true that water boils at 100 degrees Celsius under normal pressure.
The answer to the problem was self-evidently correct to everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clearly - in a clear manner; without doubt
  • synonym: obviously - in a way that is easily perceived or understood
