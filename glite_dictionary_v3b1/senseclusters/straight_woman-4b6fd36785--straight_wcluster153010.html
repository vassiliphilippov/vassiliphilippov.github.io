straight woman

noun
female comedy partner who sets up jokes
1.0
Pronunciation: / streɪt ˈwʊmən / (AmE), / streɪt ˈwʊmən / (BrE)
A female performer in a comedy duo or act whose role is to feed lines to the comedian, serving as the serious partner or stooge.

Examples

The straight woman role
As the straight woman, her calm reactions only made the jokes funnier.
In the duo, she played the straight woman to his wild antics.
Many famous comedy pairs featured a straight woman who delivered clever setups.
