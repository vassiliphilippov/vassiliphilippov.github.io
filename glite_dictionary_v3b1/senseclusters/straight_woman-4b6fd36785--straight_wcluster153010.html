straight woman

noun
female comedy partner who sets up jokes
1.0 6.4
Pronunciation: /streɪt ˈwʊm.ən/ /; / /streɪt ˈwoʊ.mən/ /; / /streɪt ˈwʊm.ɪn/ (AmE), /streɪt ˈwʊm.ən/ (BrE)
