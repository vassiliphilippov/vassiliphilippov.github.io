beet

noun
edible root vegetable plant
1.0 5.85/m
Pronunciation: / bit / (AmE), / biÀêt / (BrE)
a plant with a round, usually red or purple, edible root that is grown as a food crop or vegetable

Examples

Red beet root
Farmers harvest beet in autumn when the roots are at their largest.
She added chopped beet to the salad for extra color and flavor.
The field was filled with rows of red beet ready for picking.
Farmers harvest the beet crop in the late summer months.
I added roasted beet to the salad for extra color and flavor.
She prefers her beet sliced thinly and served with goat cheese.

Synonyms

  • beetroot
    2.1 0.10/m
    beetroot is the red edible root often eaten in salads or juice, while beet names the whole plant and vegetable.
  • common beet
    3.0 0.00/m
    common beet is the species name for the cultivated plant grown for food, whereas beet is the common everyday vegetable name.

Related Terms
