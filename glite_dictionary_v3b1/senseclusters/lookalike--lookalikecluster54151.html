lookalike

adjective
resembling another
Pronunciation: / ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈlʊkəˌlaɪk / (BrE)
resembling another person or thing in appearance, often as an imitation

Examples

Lookalike actor
The actor hired a lookalike to attend the event in his place.
She wore a lookalike jacket that was almost identical to the designer brand.
Many people buy lookalike products because they are more affordable than the originals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: different - not the same as another
  • degree higher: identical - exactly the same in every detail
  • synonym: similar - having a resemblance in appearance or character
