silver chloride

noun
a white chemical compound (AgCl) used in photography
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər klaɪˌraɪd / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə ˈklɔːraɪd / (BrE)
A white, insoluble chemical compound (AgCl) used mainly in photographic materials, electrochemistry, and some medicines.
