gateway

verb
to route between systems
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌweɪɪŋ / (BrE)
to act as a gateway by routing, translating, or managing access between different systems, networks, or services

Examples

To gateway data
The new software is gatewaying data between the company's internal network and the cloud.
By gatewaying messages, the device allows different communication protocols to work together.
Our IT team is gatewaying access to ensure only authorized users can reach sensitive systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: block - to prevent access or connection between systems
  • synonym: bridge - to connect or link different systems or networks
