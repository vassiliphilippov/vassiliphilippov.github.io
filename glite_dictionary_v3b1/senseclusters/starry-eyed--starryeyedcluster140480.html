Examples

Starry-eyed dreamer

Many starry-eyed graduates think they'll change the world overnight.
The starry-eyed proposal ignored the practical limitations of the plan.

Synonyms

  • Pollyannaish
    9.9 0.00/m
    Pollyannaish is cheerfully naive and ignores problems, while starry-eyed is dreamy, idealistic, and often impractical in real situations.
  • rose-colored
    5.6 0.08/m
    rose-colored means an unduly hopeful view that sees only good, while starry-eyed is romantic and naively optimistic about reality.
  • Panglossian
    9.9 0.02/m
    Panglossian describes extreme optimism that ignores real problems, while starry-eyed is more dreamy, romantic, and impractically hopeful.
  • overoptimistic
    5.1 0.06/m
    overoptimistic expects only the best and may be unrealistic, while starry-eyed is more dreamy and idealistically impractical.
