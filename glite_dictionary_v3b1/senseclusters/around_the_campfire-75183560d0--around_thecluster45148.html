around the campfire

preposition
gathered near an outdoor fire for conversation
Pronunciation: / əˈraʊnd ðə ˈkæmpˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / əˈraʊnd ðə ˈkæmpˌfaɪə / (BrE)
in a group gathered near a fire outdoors, typically to talk, relax, or share stories in a friendly, informal setting

Examples

Talk around the campfire
As children, we used to roast marshmallows around the campfire every summer.
The hikers sat around the campfire, sharing stories and laughter.
We sang folk songs around the campfire late into the evening.
