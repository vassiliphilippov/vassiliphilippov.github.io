flexibleness

noun
quality of being adaptable or pliable
Pronunciation: / ˈflɛksəblnəs / (AmE), / ˈflɛksɪblnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being able to adapt or change easily; adaptability or pliability

Examples

Negotiation flexibleness
Her flexibleness in negotiations often led to successful compromises.
The flexibleness of this material makes it ideal for a range of uses.
We value the flexibleness of our new schedule, which allows us more personal time.
