drawers

noun
software slide-out panel
7.2
Pronunciation: /drɔːrz/ (AmE), /drɔːz/ (BrE)
(in computing) a panel or container in a software interface that slides out from the edge of the screen to reveal navigation, tools, settings, or content.

Examples

App drawers
The new messaging app hides many settings in left-hand drawers that slide out when tapped.
Designers debated whether the mobile site's drawers should open from the left or right edge.
She used drawers to store extra tools and avoid cluttering the main toolbar for novices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: navigation drawer - a UI pattern that presents app navigation in a slide-out panel, commonly used on mobile devices
  • synonym: sidebar - a vertical panel in a user interface that provides navigation or additional content; often similar in function to a drawer
