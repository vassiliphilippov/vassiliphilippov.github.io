debaucher

noun
person who corrupts others morally
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈbɔtʃər / (AmE), / dɪˈbɔːtʃə / (BrE)
a person who leads others into immoral or corrupt behavior, typically by encouraging excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures

Examples

A debaucher at parties
He warned his daughter to stay away from that debaucher who was notorious for his wild parties.
In the novel, the main villain is a wealthy debaucher who preys on the innocence of others.
The infamous debaucher was finally brought to justice after years of corrupting young minds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrupter - a person who corrupts others
  • antonym: moralist - a person concerned with right and wrong, often a critic of immoral behavior
  • synonym: seducer - a person who entices someone into sexual activity
