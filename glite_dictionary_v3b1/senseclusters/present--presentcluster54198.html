present

adjective
existing or happening now
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛzənt / (AmE), / ˈprɛzənt / (BrE)
existing or occurring at this time; happening or being considered now, as opposed to in the past or future

Examples

Present problems
She prefers to focus on present concerns rather than worry about the future.
The present government is facing several major challenges.
The present situation requires immediate action.

Related Terms

  • antonym: future - yet to happen; occurring after the present time
  • antonym: past - gone by in time and no longer existing
