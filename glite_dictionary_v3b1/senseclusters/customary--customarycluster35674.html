Examples

A customary practice
It is customary to shake hands when you meet someone for the first time.
She gave her customary polite smile before starting the meeting.
The restaurant will close on Sundays, as is customary in this region.
A customary silence fell over the room before the big announcement.
He made his customary morning visit to the coffee shop.
It is customary for guests to bring a gift to the party.

Synonyms

  • usual
    1.0 71/m
    usual is what happens most of the time in everyday life, while customary describes practices tied to tradition or custom.
  • traditional
    1.5 119/m
    traditional belongs to long-established beliefs and old practices, while customary describes what people commonly do according to local custom.
  • conventional
    2.8 24/m
    conventional follows accepted rules or social norms and sounds formal, while customary focuses on practices done because of tradition.
  • habitual
    4.0 3.06/m
    habitual is done regularly by habit or routine, while customary refers to actions done because they are part of local or social custom.
