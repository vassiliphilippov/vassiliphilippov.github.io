isoelectric

adjective
no electrical activity
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsoʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəʊɪˈlɛktrɪk / (BrE)
showing no detectable electrical activity, especially as a flat line on an ECG or EEG recording

Examples

Isoelectric line on ECG
The EEG showed an isoelectric line, indicating a lack of brain activity.
Doctors became concerned when the patient's ECG became isoelectric during the procedure.
An isoelectric segment on the monitor suggested the heart was not generating electrical impulses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: active - showing electrical activity
  • synonym: flatline - to show a flat, unchanging line on a medical monitor
