Kanchanjanga

proper noun
mountain on Nepal–India border in the Himalayas
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.tʃənˈdʒʌŋ.gə / (AmE), / ˌkæn.tʃənˈdʒʌŋ.gə / (BrE)
the third highest mountain in the world, located in the Himalayas on the border between Nepal and the Indian state of Sikkim, rising to 28,208 feet (8,586 meters)
