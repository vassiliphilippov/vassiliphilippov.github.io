sentience

noun
capacity for feeling or consciousness
9.9 8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛn.ʃəns /; / ˈsɛnʃəns /; / ˈsɛn.ti.əns / (AmE), / ˈsɛn.tɪ.əns /; / ˈsɛnʃiəns /; / ˈsɛn.tɪəns / (BrE)
