paddleboard

noun
board for paddling
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpædəlˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈpædəlˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a long, flat board designed for standing or kneeling while paddling on water

Examples

A paddleboard on water
She learned to balance on a paddleboard at the lake.
He rented a paddleboard to explore the calm waters near the beach.
Many people enjoy exercising on a paddleboard because it improves balance and strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kayak - a small, narrow watercraft propelled by a double-bladed paddle
  • synonym: surfboard - a board used for riding waves in surfing
