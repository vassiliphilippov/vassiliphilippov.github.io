switchable

adjective
able to be changed
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪtʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˈswɪtʃəbl / (BrE)
able to be exchanged, replaced, or changed for another similar item or function

Examples

Switchable parts
The device has switchable covers so you can change its appearance easily.
Many modern cars have switchable driving modes for different road conditions.
This lamp features a switchable color setting to suit your mood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to be changed or replaced
  • synonym: interchangeable - able to be exchanged with another without making a difference
