Synonyms

  • interchangeable
    4.8 2.96/m
    interchangeable is used when two parts do the same job without loss of function, while switchable means it can be changed for a similar function.
  • substitutable
    5.9 0.09/m
    substitutable is used for something you can put in place of another without changing how it works, while switchable stresses being able to be changed.
  • replaceable
    3.3 0.97/m
    replaceable means it can be replaced with another item when needed, while switchable highlights being able to change function or item.
  • exchangeable
    4.0 0.18/m
    exchangeable suggests items can be traded or swapped for equal value, while switchable refers to changing or replacing similar items or functions.
  • swappable
    3.9 0.04/m
    swappable is informal tech-talk for parts that can be swapped quickly, while switchable is more general about being able to be changed.
