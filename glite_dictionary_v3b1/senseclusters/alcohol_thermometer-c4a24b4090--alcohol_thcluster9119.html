alcohol thermometer

noun
thermometer using alcohol as liquid
3.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈæl.kə.hɑːl θər.ˈmɑː.mə.tər/ /; / /ˈæl.kə.hɔːl θər.ˈmɑː.mə.tər/ (AmE), /ˈæl.kə.hɒl θə.ˈmɒm.ɪ.tə/ (BrE)
A thermometer in which alcohol is used as the liquid, typically consisting of a glass tube marked with a temperature scale.

Examples

An alcohol thermometer reading
He checked the freezer's temperature with an alcohol thermometer designed for very low readings.
Since mercury is toxic, an alcohol thermometer is often preferred in schools and homes.
The laboratory used an alcohol thermometer to measure the temperature of the solution.
