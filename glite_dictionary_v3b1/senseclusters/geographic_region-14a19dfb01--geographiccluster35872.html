geographic region

noun
area defined by physical or human features
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk ˈriː.dʒən / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əˈɡræf.ɪk ˈriː.dʒən / (BrE)
An area of the Earth's surface that is defined by natural, physical, or human-made boundaries.

Examples
