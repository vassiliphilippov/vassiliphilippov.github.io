dreamer

noun
person who escapes into fantasy
3.8 3.59/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdriÀêm…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàdriÀêm…ô / (BrE)
a person who lives in an imaginary world, escapes reality, or is guided by ideals and imagination rather than practical realities

Examples

A hopeful dreamer
As a dreamer, she often lost herself in thoughts of magical worlds far away.
He is a dreamer who sees possibilities where others see obstacles.
Many inventors begin as dreamers, imagining the impossible before making it real.
She has always been a dreamer, imagining a world without conflict or pain.
Some call him a dreamer, but his bold ideas inspire those around him.
While his friends pursued steady jobs, he remained a dreamer chasing artistic visions.
