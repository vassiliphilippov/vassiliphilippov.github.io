stockinette stitch

noun
knitting stitch with alternating knit and purl rows
5.6 5.8
Pronunciation: /stɑː.kə.ˈnet stɪtʃ/ /; / /stɑː.kɪ.ˈnet stɪtʃ/ (AmE), /stɒk.ɪ.ˈnet stɪtʃ/ /; / /stɒk.ə.ˈnet stɪtʃ/ (BrE)
A knitting stitch pattern consisting of alternating rows of knit and purl stitches, resulting in a smooth, even fabric commonly used for stockings and garments.

Examples

Stockinette stitch pattern
After knitting a few rows of stockinette stitch, the fabric began to curl at the edges.
She practiced both the garter stitch and the stockinette stitch to learn the basics of knitting.
The sweater is made using the stockinette stitch for a smooth and classic finish.
