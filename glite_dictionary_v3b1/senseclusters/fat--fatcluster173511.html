fat

adjective
containing or composed of fat
3.0
Pronunciation: / fæt / (AmE), / fæt / (BrE)
(of food or tissue) containing or composed of a large amount of fat.

Examples

Fat food
Avocados are considered a healthy but fat food.
Nutritionists warn against eating too much fat meat.
The surgeon removed some fat tissue during the operation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fatty - containing or composed of fat; greasy
  • antonym: lean - (of meat) containing little or no fat
