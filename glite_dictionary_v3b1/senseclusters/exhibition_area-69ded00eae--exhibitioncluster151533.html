exhibition area

noun
virtual space for online exhibitions
3.7
Pronunciation: /eks.ɪ.ˈbɪʃ.ən ˈe.riː.ə/ (AmE), /eks.ɪ.ˈbɪʃ.ən ˈeə.riə/ (BrE)
a virtual or online space, often part of a website or digital platform, used for displaying works, products, or booths in online exhibitions or trade shows.

Examples

Virtual exhibition area
The conference offered a 3D exhibition area where attendees browsed virtual booths.
Artists uploaded videos and descriptions to the museum's new exhibition area for global visitors.
After registration, participants explored the exhibition area and chatted with exhibitors at virtual stands.

  • synonym: virtual exhibition - an exhibition presented online rather than in a physical venue
