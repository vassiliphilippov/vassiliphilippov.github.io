share

noun
steel wedge on a plough for cutting soil
Pronunciation: / ʃɛr / (AmE), / ʃeə / (BrE)
a sharp steel wedge forming part of a plough, which cuts the top layer of soil to prepare it for turning

Examples

Plough share cuts soil
Careful sharpening of the share ensures the plough can easily penetrate the earth.

Years of use had worn down the share, making it less effective at cutting through the soil.
