congenialness

noun
pleasant compatibility; congenial quality
Pronunciation: / kənˈdʒiːniəlnəs / (AmE), / kənˈdʒiːniəlnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being pleasant and compatible with others; the state of being congenial.

Examples

A congenialness of people
A sense of congenialness quickly developed in the group, making collaboration easier.
Her congenialness made her popular among her colleagues.
The congenialness between the two business partners led to a successful venture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congeniality - the quality of being friendly, pleasant, and compatible
