ravehook

noun
boatbuilding tool for seam preparation
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪvˌhʊk / (AmE), / ˈreɪvˌhʊk / (BrE)
a hooked hand tool used in boatbuilding to prepare the seams of a boat for oakum caulking

Examples

A ravehook tool
A good ravehook makes the caulking process much easier for traditional wooden boats.
The shipwright selected a ravehook from his toolbox to start working on the boat's seams.
Using a ravehook, she carefully cleared out old material before packing new oakum.
