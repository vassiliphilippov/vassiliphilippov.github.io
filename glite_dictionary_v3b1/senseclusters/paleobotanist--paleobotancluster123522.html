paleobotanist

noun
fossil plant scientist
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪli.oʊˈbɑːtənɪst / (AmE), / ˌpeɪli.əʊˈbɒtənɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies fossilized plants and ancient plant life

Examples

The paleobotanist studies fossils
The paleobotanist carefully examined the ancient leaf impressions found in the rock.
As a paleobotanist, she travels to remote sites to collect fossilized plant samples.
A team led by a renowned paleobotanist published new findings about prehistoric forests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: botanist - a scientist who studies plants
  • synonym: paleontologist - a scientist who studies fossils and ancient life forms
