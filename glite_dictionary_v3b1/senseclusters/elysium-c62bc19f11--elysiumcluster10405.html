Elysium

noun
a place/state of perfect happiness
Pronunciation: / ɪˈliːziəm / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪzɪəm / (BrE)
a place or state of perfect happiness; paradise

Examples

A personal Elysium
After years of hardship, she felt as if she had finally entered Elysium.
He spoke of his childhood home as his personal Elysium, a place of pure joy.
Poets often describe their vision of Elysium as a land of eternal spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paradise - a place or condition of extreme happiness
  • synonym: utopia - an imagined place where everything is perfect
