legation

noun
the office of a diplomatic representative or legate
9.0
Pronunciation: / lɪˈɡeɪʃən / (AmE), / lɪˈɡeɪʃən / (BrE)
the official position or office of a legate, especially a diplomatic representative of the Pope or, in historical contexts, a lesser diplomatic mission than an embassy.

Examples

Papal legation office
During the 19th century, many European countries maintained a legation rather than an embassy in smaller capitals.
He was appointed to the legation in Rome as the Pope's representative.
The legation was responsible for negotiating treaties and maintaining diplomatic relations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: legate - an official representative, especially a diplomatic messenger or envoy
