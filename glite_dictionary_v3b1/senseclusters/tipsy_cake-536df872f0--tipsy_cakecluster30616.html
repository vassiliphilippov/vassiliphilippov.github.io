tipsy cake

noun
dessert of cake soaked in wine with almonds and fruit
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪpsi keɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɪpsi keɪk / (BrE)
A traditional dessert consisting of sponge cake or trifle soaked in wine or sherry and decorated with almonds and candied fruit.

Examples

A tipsy cake dessert
For dessert, she served a delicious tipsy cake soaked in sherry and topped with candied cherries.
Guests praised the chef’s unique take on tipsy cake with toasted almonds and fresh cream.
The traditional English tipsy cake is often enjoyed during festive occasions.
