anatomopathological

adjective
of anatomy and pathology
Pronunciation: / əˌnæt̬əmoʊˌpæθəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / əˌnætəməʊˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to both anatomy and pathology, especially in the study of disease in tissues

Examples

Anatomopathological examination
The anatomopathological examination revealed significant tissue changes caused by the infection.
Doctors rely on anatomopathological analysis to confirm the presence of certain diseases.
A detailed anatomopathological report was included in the patient's medical file.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anatomical - relating to the structure of the body
  • synonym: pathological - relating to the study of disease
