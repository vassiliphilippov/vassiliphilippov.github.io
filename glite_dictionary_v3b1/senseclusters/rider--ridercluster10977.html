rider

noun
person who rides a vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪ.dɚ /; / ˈraɪdər / (AmE), / ˈraɪ.də /; / ˈraɪdə / (BrE)
a person who travels by riding on or in something, such as a vehicle or an animal

Examples

A rider on a horse
A rider waved to us as he sped past on his motorcycle.
As an experienced rider, she always wears a helmet when cycling.
The club welcomes every rider, whether on a mountain bike or a road bike.
A young rider fell off his pony during the show but was not hurt.
Each rider in the caravan wore protective gear to cross the desert on camels.
The experienced rider guided her horse skillfully through the narrow path.
Carpooling apps match drivers with riders heading in the same direction.
Each rider must pay the fare before boarding the train.
The bus was crowded, and every rider had to hold on tightly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: driver - person who drives a vehicle
  • synonym: equestrian - a person who rides horses, especially as a sport
  • synonym: passenger - a person who is traveling in a vehicle but not operating it
