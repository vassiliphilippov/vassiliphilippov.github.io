family Guttiferae

noun
tropical plant family producing oils and resins
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfæməli ˌɡʌtəˈfɪriː / (AmE), / ˌfæmɪli ˌɡʌtəˈfɪəriː / (BrE)
a family of chiefly tropical trees, shrubs, and vines that produce oils, resins, and some timber, widely distributed in the tropics
