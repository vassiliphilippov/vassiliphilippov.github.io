indecisively

adverb
in an undecided or inconclusive manner
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you cannot make a clear or firm decision

Examples

Answer indecisively
He answered the question indecisively, unsure of what he truly believed.
She glanced indecisively at the menu, unable to choose what to order.
The meeting ended indecisively, with no decision reached on the proposal.
He indecisively moved between the two options, unable to make up his mind.
She answered indecisively, unsure of what she truly wanted.
They discussed the matter indecisively, never reaching a firm conclusion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conclusively - in a manner that leaves no doubt; decisively
  • antonym: decisively - in a way that shows the ability to make decisions quickly and confidently
