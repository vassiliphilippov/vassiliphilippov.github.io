indecisively

adverb
in an undecided or inconclusive manner
4.6 3.5
Pronunciation: /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv.lɪ/ /; / /ɪn.də.ˈsaɪ.sɪv.lɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.dɪ.ˈsaɪ.sɪv.lɪ/ /; / /ɪn.də.ˈsaɪ.sɪv.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that shows you cannot make a clear or firm decision
