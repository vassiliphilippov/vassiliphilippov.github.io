nonpurulent

adjective
not containing pus
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈpjʊrələnt / (AmE), / nɒnˈpjʊərʊlənt / (BrE)
(of a wound or discharge) not containing pus.

Examples

Nonpurulent wound
A nonpurulent sore is generally less concerning than one with pus.
Her cough was nonpurulent, producing clear rather than pus-filled mucus.
The doctor described the wound as nonpurulent, indicating it showed no signs of infection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: purulent - containing or producing pus
