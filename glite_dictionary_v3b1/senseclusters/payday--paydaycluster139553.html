payday

noun
day when wages are paid
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪ.deɪ / (BrE)
The day on which employees receive their wages or salary.

Examples

Waiting for payday
Everyone in the office eagerly waits for payday each month.
He budgeted carefully to make sure his money lasted until the next payday.
Since payday fell on a Friday, the staff celebrated after work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salary - a fixed regular payment, typically paid monthly or biweekly
  • synonym: wage - money earned for work or services, typically paid daily or weekly
