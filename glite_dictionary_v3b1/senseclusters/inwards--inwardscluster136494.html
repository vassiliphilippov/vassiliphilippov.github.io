inwards

adverb
toward the inside
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnwɚdz /; / ˈɪnwərdz / (AmE), / ˈɪnwədz / (BrE)
towards or into the inside or center of something

Examples

Flow inwards slowly
As the tide comes in, the water flows inwards towards the shore.

She felt a strange sensation pulling her focus inwards rather than outwards.
The petals of the flower curl inwards at night.
