Arabidopsis thaliana

noun
model organism in plant genetics
Pronunciation: / əˌræb.ɪˈdɑp.sɪs θəˈliː.ə.nə / (AmE), / əˌræb.ɪˈdɒp.sɪs θəˈlɪə.nə / (BrE)
a small flowering plant of the mustard family, widely used as a model organism in plant genetics and biology
