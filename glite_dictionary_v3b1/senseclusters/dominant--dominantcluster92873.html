dominant

noun
genetic allele always determining trait

An allele that produces the same observable effect whether paired with an identical or a different allele.

Examples

Dominant allele
If one parent has a dominant for curly hair, the child may inherit curly hair as well.
In a Punnett square, a dominant masks the effect of a recessive allele.
The brown eye color gene acts as a dominant in humans.
