horn-shaped

adjective
shaped like a horn
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrn ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈhɔːn ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having the shape or form of a horn; curved and tapering to a point.

Examples

Horn-shaped seashell
Certain seashells are naturally horn-shaped, curving gracefully like an animal's horn.
She admired the elegant, horn-shaped vase on the mantelpiece.
The flower has a distinctive horn-shaped petal that makes it easy to identify.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horn - a hard, pointed projection from the head of certain animals
