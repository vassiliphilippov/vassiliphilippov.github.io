masseter

noun
muscle used in chewing
Pronunciation: / mæˈsitɚ / (AmE), / məˈsiːtə / (BrE)
a large muscle at the side of the jaw that raises the lower jaw and is used in chewing

Examples

The masseter muscle
During the dental exam, the dentist checked the patient's masseter for tenderness.
Pain in the masseter can cause difficulties with chewing and jaw movement.
The masseter is one of the strongest muscles in the human body by weight.
