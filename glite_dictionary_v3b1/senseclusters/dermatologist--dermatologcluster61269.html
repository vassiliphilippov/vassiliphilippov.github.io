dermatologist

noun
doctor specializing in skin conditions
3.9 3.88/m
Pronunciation: / Àåd…ùÀê.m…ôÀàt…ëÀê.l…ô.d í…™st / (AmE), / Àåd…úÀê.m…ôÀàt…íl.…ô.d í…™st / (BrE)

Examples

A dermatologist doctor
