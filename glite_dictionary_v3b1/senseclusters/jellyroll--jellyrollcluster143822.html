jellyroll

noun
sponge cake rolled with jelly
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛl.iˌroʊl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛl.iˌrəʊl / (BrE)
A thin sheet of sponge cake spread with jelly and then rolled up into a cylindrical shape.

Examples

A jellyroll cake
After dinner, they enjoyed a slice of jellyroll with their tea.
She brought a homemade jellyroll to the picnic, and everyone loved it.
The bakery displays a fresh jellyroll every morning in the front window.
