Synonyms

  • disperse
    4.4 10/m
    disperse is used when things or people move apart across an area, while scatter suggests a more random, thrown spread.
  • strew
    6.3 0.35/m
    strew means to scatter things untidily over a surface, while scatter means to throw or spread things more widely.
  • spread
    1.6 48/m
    spread covers or extends something smoothly over an area, while scatter means to throw or distribute items in a random, irregular way.
