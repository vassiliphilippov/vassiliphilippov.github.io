push-button

adjective
automatic or effortless
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (BrE)
designed to be simple, automatic, or requiring little effort or skill to operate or use, often with a critical tone.

Examples

Push-button convenience
The new coffee machine is completely push-button, so anyone can use it without training.
Critics argue that push-button solutions often fail to address the root causes of complex problems.
He prefers traditional methods over push-button technology that removes all the challenge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automatic - operating by itself with little or no human control
  • antonym: manual - requiring human effort or skill to operate
