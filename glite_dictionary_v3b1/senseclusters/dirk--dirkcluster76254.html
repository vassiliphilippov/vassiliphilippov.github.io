dirk

noun
a long, straight dagger
Pronunciation: / dɜrk / (AmE), / dɜːk / (BrE)
a long, straight-bladed dagger, especially one formerly carried by Scottish Highlanders

Examples

A dirk dagger
He wore a traditional kilt and a dirk at his side during the ceremony.
She recognized the ornate hilt of a Scottish dirk among the antique weapons.
The old glass case displayed a dirk believed to have belonged to a Highland warrior.
