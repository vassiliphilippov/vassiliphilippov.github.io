Synonyms

  • rectification
    7.4 0.03/m
    rectification is a formal act of setting things right after an error, while correction is the general fixing of a mistake.
  • remediation
    6.3 0.4/m
    remediation is the process of correcting a specific fault or problem, while correction is a general act of fixing errors.
  • remedy
    3.8 4/m
    remedy is a means or solution used to fix a problem, while correction is the act or result of making something right.
  • revision
    2.9 4/m
    revision is changing or improving something, often making a new version, while correction focuses on fixing a specific mistake.
