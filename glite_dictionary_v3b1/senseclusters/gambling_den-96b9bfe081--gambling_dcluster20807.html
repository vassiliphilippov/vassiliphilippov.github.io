gambling den

noun
place for playing games of chance
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæmblɪŋ dɛn / (AmE), / ˈɡamblɪŋ dɛn / (BrE)
A public place where various games of chance are played for money, usually operated as a business.

Examples

An illegal gambling den
After dark, the old warehouse turned into an illegal gambling den bustling with activity.
Many people lost their savings in the secret gambling den that operated in the back room.
The police raided the gambling den after receiving multiple complaints from residents.

  • synonym: casino - a facility for certain types of gambling
