semimatte

adjective
partly matte finish
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈmæt / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈmat / (BrE)
having a surface finish that is partly matte and partly shiny, with a low level of gloss

Examples

Semimatte finish
The photographer preferred a semimatte paper for printing portraits because it reduced glare.
Her lipstick had a semimatte finish, giving her lips a soft, natural look.
The new smartphone comes with a semimatte back that resists fingerprints.
