mute

noun
device for softening musical instrument's tone
7.2 0.58/m
Pronunciation: / mjuÀêt / (AmE), / mjuÀêt / (BrE)
a device that reduces the volume or alters the timbre of a musical instrument, especially a brass or string instrument

Examples

Mute for trumpet
Different types of mutes can dramatically change the color of an instrument's tone.
He inserted the mute into his trumpet before the soft passage.
The violinist used a mute to create a more mellow sound for the piece.

Synonyms
