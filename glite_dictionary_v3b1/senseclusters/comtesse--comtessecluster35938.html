comtesse

noun
French noblewoman
Pronunciation: / kɔmˈtɛs / (AmE), / kɒ̃ˈtɛs / (BrE)
a French noblewoman who holds the rank equivalent to a countess

Examples

The comtesse greeted guests
The comtesse greeted her guests with elegance and grace at the evening reception.
Many admired the comtesse for her charitable work throughout the region.
The portrait depicted a distinguished comtesse in a lavish gown from the 18th century.

Related Terms

  • antonym: comte - a French nobleman equivalent in rank to a count
  • synonym: countess - a woman holding the rank of count or earl
