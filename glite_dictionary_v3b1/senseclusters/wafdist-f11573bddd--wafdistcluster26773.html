Wafdist

noun
Egyptian nationalist party member
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːfdɪst / (AmE), / ˈwɒfdɪst / (BrE)
a member or supporter of the Egyptian nationalist Wafd party, especially active in the early 20th century

Examples

A Wafdist leader
The Wafdist leaders played a key role in Egypt's independence movement.
As a proud Wafdist, he attended every rally supporting the party's goals.
Many historians study the influence of Wafdist politicians in shaping modern Egypt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their country's independence
  • antonym: royalist - a supporter of a monarchy or royal family
