long haul

noun
journey over a long distance
5.9
Pronunciation: / lɔŋ hɔl / (AmE), / lɒŋ hɔːl / (BrE)
a journey that covers a great distance, especially by road, rail, or air

Examples

A long haul flight
After such a long haul, the team was glad to finally reach their destination.
The flight from London to Sydney is definitely a long haul for any traveler.
Truck drivers often have to prepare for a long haul across several states.
