independista

adjective
supporting independence
Pronunciation: / ˌɪndɪpɛnˈdistə / (AmE), / ˌɪndɪpɛnˈdiːstə / (BrE)
favoring or supporting political independence or separation from a larger state or authority

Examples

Independista movement leader
The independista movement gained momentum after the controversial election results.
Many young activists identify as independista in their political beliefs.
The party's independista stance attracted supporters seeking greater autonomy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: separatist - supporting separation from a larger group or country
  • antonym: unionist - supporting political union with a larger state
