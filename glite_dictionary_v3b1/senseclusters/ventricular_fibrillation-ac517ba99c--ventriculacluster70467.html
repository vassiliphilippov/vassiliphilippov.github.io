A serious cardiac condition characterized by rapid, irregular contractions of the ventricles, disrupting normal heart rhythm and potentially leading to cardiac arrest.

Examples

Ventricular fibrillation episode
Doctors quickly identified the signs of ventricular fibrillation on the electrocardiogram.
If left untreated, ventricular fibrillation can quickly lead to cardiac arrest and death.
The patient suffered from ventricular fibrillation and required immediate defibrillation.
