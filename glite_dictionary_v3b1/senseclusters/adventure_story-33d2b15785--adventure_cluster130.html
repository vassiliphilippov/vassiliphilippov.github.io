adventure story

noun
story involving exciting adventures
Pronunciation: / ədˈvɛntʃər ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ədˈvɛntʃə ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
a story that involves exciting and often risky experiences, typically featuring action and exploration

Examples

An adventure story book
My first adventure story was about a group of friends searching for lost treasure in the jungle.
She prefers reading adventure stories that are filled with daring heroes and exotic locations.
The film was based on a classic adventure story by a well-known author.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adventure novel - a novel featuring exciting and risky experiences
  • degree lower: detective story - a story involving solving a mystery or crime
  • degree higher: thriller - a story designed to excite and surprise, often with danger
