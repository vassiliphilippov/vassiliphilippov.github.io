billy buttons

noun
Australian/New Zealand plant with round yellow flower heads
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪli ˌbʌtnz / (AmE), / ˈbɪli ˌbʌtnz / (BrE)
any of various plants of the genus Craspedia, native to Australia and New Zealand, grown for their spherical heads of golden-yellow flowers and downy leaves

Examples

Billy buttons flowers
Billy buttons thrive in sunny, well-drained locations and are popular in native Australian gardens.
Australian florists often use billy buttons in bouquets for their unique, cheerful appearance.
The billy buttons in her garden added a bright splash of color throughout spring.
