brace

noun
elastic straps to hold up trousers
9.9 2.5
Pronunciation: /breɪs/ (AmE), /breɪs/ (BrE)
an elastic strap or pair of straps fastened to trousers and worn over the shoulders to hold them up

Examples

Her grandfather still uses braces to keep his trousers from slipping down.
In Britain, braces are a common alternative to belts.
