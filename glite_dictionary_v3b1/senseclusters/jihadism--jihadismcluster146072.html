jihadism

noun
militant Islamist ideology
Pronunciation: / dʒɪˈhɑːdɪzəm / (AmE), / dʒɪˈhɑːdɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief in or support for violent Islamist movements or armed struggle to achieve Islamic religious or political goals
