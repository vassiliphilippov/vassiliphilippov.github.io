pulmonary stenosis

noun
abnormal narrowing of pulmonary artery opening
2.6 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpʊl.mə.ne.r.iː stə.ˈnoʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈpʌl.mə.ne.r.iː stə.ˈnoʊ.sɪs/ (AmE), /ˈpʌl.mə.nə.riː stɪ.ˈnəʊ.sɪs/ /; / /ˈpʊl.mə.nə.riː stɪ.ˈnəʊ.sɪs/ (BrE)
