exorbitance

noun
the state of being excessive
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɔrbətəns / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɔːbɪtəns / (BrE)
the quality or state of exceeding reasonable limits; excessive amount or degree

Examples

The exorbitance of prices
Critics pointed out the exorbitance of the new tax proposals.
Her exorbitance in spending led to mounting credit card debts.
The exorbitance of the prices shocked many customers at the restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excess - an amount more than is necessary
  • antonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes
