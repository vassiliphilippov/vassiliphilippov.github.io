content

noun
the substance of what is included or conveyed
2.9
Pronunciation: / kənˈtɛnt /; / ˈkɑn.tɛnt /; / ˈkɑnˌtɛnt / (AmE), / kənˈtɛnt /; / ˈkɒn.tɛnt / (BrE)
the things, information, or subject matter that are inside or included in something (such as a book, website, or container)
