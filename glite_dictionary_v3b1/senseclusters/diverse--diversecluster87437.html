Synonyms

  • varied
    3.3 9.57/m
    varied is used to show many different types or forms in a group, while diverse emphasizes strong difference across that group.
  • heterogeneous
    7.9 3.43/m
    heterogeneous sounds formal and describes a mix of unlike parts, while diverse is common and used in everyday speech.
  • various
    1.0 151/m
    various lists different kinds or several items, while diverse stresses how different those items are from each other.
  • assorted
    3.7 2.69/m
    assorted often describes a mixed collection of items together, while diverse points to strong variety across people or things.
  • dissimilar
    3.8 2.13/m
    dissimilar means not alike between two things, while diverse refers to a group showing many different kinds at once.
