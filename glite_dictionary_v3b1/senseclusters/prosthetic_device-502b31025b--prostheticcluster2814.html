prosthetic device

noun
artificial replacement for a body part
6.5 3.3
Pronunciation: /prɑːs.ˈθet.ɪk dɪv.ˈaɪs/ /; / /prɑːs.ˈθet.ɪk də.ˈaɪs/ (AmE), /prɒs.ˈθet.ɪk dɪv.ˈaɪs/ /; / /prɒs.ˈθet.ɪk də.ˈaɪs/ (BrE)
