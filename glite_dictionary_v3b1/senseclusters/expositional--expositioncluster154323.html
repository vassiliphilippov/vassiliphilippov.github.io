expositional

adjective
about musical exposition
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to the exposition section in music, especially the part that introduces the main themes of a composition

Examples

Expositional section
The expositional section of the sonata clearly states the main musical themes.
Her analysis focused on the expositional passages in Beethoven's symphonies.
Composers often use the expositional part to introduce motifs that will be developed later.

  • synonym: expository - intended to explain or describe something
