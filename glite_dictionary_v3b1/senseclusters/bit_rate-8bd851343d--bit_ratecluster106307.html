bit rate

noun
number of bits per time unit (digital transmission)
Pronunciation: / bɪt reɪt / (AmE), / bɪt reɪt / (BrE)
The number of bits transmitted or processed per unit of time, typically used in reference to digital audio or video files.

Examples

The bit rate speed
Some devices can automatically adjust the bit rate based on network speed.
The audio bit rate was reduced to decrease the file size without losing much quality.
To ensure high-quality streaming, you need a higher bit rate for your video files.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandwidth - the maximum data transfer rate of a network or system
