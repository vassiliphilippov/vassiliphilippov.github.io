tang

noun
sharp or strong taste
Pronunciation: / tæŋ / (AmE), / tæŋ / (BrE)
a strong, sharp, and distinctive taste or smell, especially in food

Examples

Tang of cheese
The cheese has a distinctive tang that lingers on your tongue.
The sauce added a spicy tang to the otherwise simple dish.
There's a citrus tang in this salad dressing that makes it refreshing.
The cheese has a sharp tang that lingers on the palate.
The lemonade had a delightful tang that made it refreshing on a hot day.
There was a salty tang in the air near the ocean cliffs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blandness - lack of strong taste or flavor
  • synonym: zest - a pleasantly sharp taste or flavor
