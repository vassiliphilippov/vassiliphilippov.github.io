midlatitude

noun
temperate region
5.2
Pronunciation: /mɪd.ˈlæt.ɪ.tuːd/ /; / /mɪd.ˈlæt.ə.tuːd/ (AmE), /mɪd.ˈlæt.ɪ.tjuːd/ /; / /mɪd.ˈlæt.ə.tjuːd/ (BrE)
a region of the Earth located between the tropics and the polar circles, typically characterized by moderate climates
