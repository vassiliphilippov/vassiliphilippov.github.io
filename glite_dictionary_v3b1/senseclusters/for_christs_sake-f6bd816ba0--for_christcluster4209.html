for Christ's sake

adverb
expressing annoyance
9.2
Pronunciation: / fər kraɪsts seɪk / (AmE), / fə kraɪsts seɪk / (BrE)
used to express annoyance, frustration, or urgency, often to emphasize a request or command

Examples

For Christ's sake, listen

Will you hurry up, for Christ's sake, or we'll miss the train!
Oh, for Christ's sake, just tell me what happened already.
