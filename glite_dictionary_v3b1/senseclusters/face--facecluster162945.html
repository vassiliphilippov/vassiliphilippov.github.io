face

noun
impudent boldness, cheek
7.2
Pronunciation: / feɪs / (AmE), / feɪs / (BrE)
shameless or impudent boldness; insolent attitude or behaviour

Examples

Have the face
He had the face to ask for a raise after arriving late every day.
I can't believe she had the face to stand up and argue with the boss.
You need some face to question the teacher in front of everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nerve - bold and disrespectful audacity
  • antonym: timidity - lack of boldness or confidence
