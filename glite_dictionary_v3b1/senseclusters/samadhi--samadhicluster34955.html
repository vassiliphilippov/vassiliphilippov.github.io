samadhi

noun
memorial shrine
9.9
Pronunciation: / səˈmɑːdi / (AmE), / səˈmɑːdi / (BrE)
a tomb or memorial shrine built over the burial place of a saint, guru, or revered religious teacher, especially in South Asia

Examples

A saint's samadhi
Devotees gathered at the samadhi to pay their respects on the anniversary of the saint's passing.
The samadhi was beautifully decorated with garlands and candles during the festival.
Many pilgrims visit the samadhi of the guru to seek blessings and meditate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mausoleum - a large, stately tomb or building housing tombs
  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy because of its associations with a sacred person or event
