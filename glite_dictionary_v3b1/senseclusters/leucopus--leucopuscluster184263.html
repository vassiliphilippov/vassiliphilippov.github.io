leucopus

noun
species with white feet
9.9
Pronunciation: / luːˈkoʊpəs / (AmE), / luːˈkəʊpəs / (BrE)
a species name used in scientific classification, meaning 'white foot', often referring to animals with pale feet

Examples

Peromyscus leucopus
The scientific name for the white-footed mouse is Peromyscus leucopus.
Researchers studied the population of leucopus in the forest ecosystem.
The term leucopus is used in zoology to describe animals with pale or white feet.
