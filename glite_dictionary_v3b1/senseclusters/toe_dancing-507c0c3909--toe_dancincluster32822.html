toe dancing

noun
dance performed on tiptoe
3.3
Pronunciation: / toʊ ˈdænsɪŋ / (AmE), / təʊ ˈdɑːnsɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of dance, especially ballet, performed on the tips of the toes, typically with special shoes

Examples

Toe dancing on tips

He began training in toe dancing at the age of ten.
