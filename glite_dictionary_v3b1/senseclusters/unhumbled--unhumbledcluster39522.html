unhumbled

adjective
not made humble
8.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhʌmbəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈhʌmbəld / (BrE)
not made humble or not feeling ashamed, embarrassed, or defeated after criticism, failure, or mistakes

Examples

Unhumbled after defeat
He walked away from the defeat unhumbled, refusing to admit any mistakes.
The leader remained unhumbled by the public's criticism and continued with her plans.
Even after losing the competition, she was unhumbled and kept her confidence.
Despite the criticism, she remained unhumbled and continued to defend her decisions.
He walked out of the meeting unhumbled, showing no sign of regret for his actions.
Even after losing the match, the team was unhumbled and kept their spirits high.

  • synonym: proud - feeling deep pleasure or satisfaction in one's achievements
