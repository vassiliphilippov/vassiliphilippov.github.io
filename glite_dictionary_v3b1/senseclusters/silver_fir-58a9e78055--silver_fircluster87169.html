silver fir

noun
a fir tree with silvery-white undersides to its leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvər fɜr / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvə fɜː / (BrE)
a species of fir tree (genus Abies) with flat needles that are white or silvery-white on the underside, often found in Europe and Asia

Examples

The silver fir tree
The silver fir is prized for its attractive foliage and tall, straight trunk.
The wood of the silver fir is often used in the construction industry for its durability.
We spotted a majestic silver fir during our hike in the mountains.
