make a stink

verb
complain or protest loudly
8.2 5.5
Pronunciation: /meɪk ə stɪŋk/ (AmE), /meɪk ə stɪŋk/ (BrE)
to complain or protest loudly and publicly, especially in a way that draws attention

Examples

To make a stink loudly

The parents threatened to make a stink if the school didn't address the issue.
