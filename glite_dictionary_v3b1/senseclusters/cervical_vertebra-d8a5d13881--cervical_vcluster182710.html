cervical vertebra

noun
one of the neck vertebrae in the spine
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɜːrvɪkəl ˈvɜːrtɪbrə / (AmE), / ˈsɜːvɪkəl ˈvɜːtɪbrə / (BrE)
any one of the seven bones that form the neck region of the human spine
