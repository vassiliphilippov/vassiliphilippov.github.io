intimate

adjective
innermost or essential part
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.t̬ə.mət / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tɪ.mət / (BrE)
forming the innermost, most essential, or most personal part of something; deeply intrinsic or fundamental.

Examples

Intimate structure

She delved into the intimate aspects of the relationship between mind and body.
The novel reveals the intimate details of the protagonist’s thoughts and fears.
