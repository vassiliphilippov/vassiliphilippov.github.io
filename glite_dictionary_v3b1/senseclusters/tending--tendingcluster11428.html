tending

adjective
having a natural inclination toward
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛn.dɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.dɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a natural inclination or disposition toward something, especially when followed by 'to'.

Examples

Children tending to ask
Children are tending to ask more questions than adults in this class.
She is tending to agree with the majority on this issue.
The manager is tending to be strict about deadlines lately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclined - having a tendency toward something
