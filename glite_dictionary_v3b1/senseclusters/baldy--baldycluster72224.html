baldy

noun
a bald or balding person (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔl.di / (AmE), / ˈbɔːl.di / (BrE)
a person, especially a man, who has little or no hair on their head, typically used humorously or informally

Examples

A baldy man
As he grew older, he embraced being a baldy and often joked about it.
Some kids at school used to call him baldy, but he never seemed to mind.
The barber laughed, "Another baldy for my collection of satisfied customers!"

  • synonym: bald - having little or no hair on the scalp
