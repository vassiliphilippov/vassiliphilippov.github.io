ejaculatory duct

noun
duct in male reproductive system
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /ɪ.dʒæk.jə.ˈleɪ.tər.iː dʌkt/ (AmE), /ɪ.dʒæk.jə.ˈleɪ.tə.riː dʌkt/ (BrE)
either of two ducts formed by the union of the vas deferens and the duct from the seminal vesicle, carrying semen through the prostate gland to the urethra

Examples

The ejaculatory duct blockage
A blockage in the ejaculatory duct can lead to male infertility.
During a vasectomy, the ejaculatory duct remains intact, but the vas deferens is severed.
The ejaculatory duct plays a crucial role in transporting semen during ejaculation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urethra - the duct through which urine and semen are discharged
  • synonym: vas deferens - the duct that conveys sperm from the testicle to the urethra
