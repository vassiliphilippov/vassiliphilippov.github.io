lighter

noun
device for creating a flame
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtə / (BrE)
a small device or substance used to produce a flame for lighting cigarettes, candles, or fires

Examples

A cigarette lighter
He borrowed my lighter to light his cigarette.
Please keep the lighter out of reach of children.
She bought a new lighter because she lost her old one.
Always keep a lighter handy when hiking in case you need to make a fire.
He reached into his pocket for a lighter to ignite the campfire.
The barbecue wouldn't start until someone found a lighter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: match - a small stick that produces a flame when struck
