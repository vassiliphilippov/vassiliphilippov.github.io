guilefully

adverb
with cunning or deceit
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaɪlfəli / (AmE), / ˈɡaɪlfəli / (BrE)
in a way that shows cleverness or deceit, often to achieve a goal

Examples

To guilefully trick
She guilefully convinced her classmates to support her idea without revealing her true intentions.
The fox guilefully escaped the trap set by the farmer.
He guilefully negotiated a better deal by hiding some important details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deceitfully - in a dishonest or misleading way
  • antonym: honestly - in a truthful or sincere way
