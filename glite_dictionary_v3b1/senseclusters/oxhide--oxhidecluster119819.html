oxhide

noun
skin of an ox
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɑksˌhaɪd / (AmE), / ˈɒksˌhaɪd / (BrE)
the hide or skin of an ox, especially when used to make leather

Examples

A belt of oxhide
The craftsman selected a piece of oxhide to make a sturdy belt.
Ancient warriors often used shields covered with oxhide for extra protection.
The boots were made from high-quality oxhide that lasted for years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cowhide - the hide or skin of a cow, used for leather
  • synonym: leather - material made from animal skin, often used for goods
