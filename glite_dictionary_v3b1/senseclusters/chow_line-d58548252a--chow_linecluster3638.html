chow line

noun
queue for food, esp. military
4.9
Pronunciation: / tʃaʊ laɪn / (AmE), / tʃaʊ laɪn / (BrE)
a line of people waiting to be served food, especially in a military camp or institution

Examples

The chow line
After the long hike, the soldiers eagerly joined the chow line for dinner.
There was a spirited conversation among friends as they waited in the chow line.
When the lunch bell rang, everyone quickly got in the chow line at the cafeteria.
