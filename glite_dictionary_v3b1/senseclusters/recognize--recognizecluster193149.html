recognize

verb
be aware or realize the existence of
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈrɛkəɡnʌɪz /; / ˈrɛkəɡˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to be fully aware of or realize the existence or truth of something

Examples

To recognize the problem
After reading the report, she finally recognized the seriousness of the problem.
He did not recognize how much stress he was under until he became ill.
It took time for the scientists to recognize the significance of their findings.
He did not recognise the seriousness of the situation until it was too late.
Only later did they recognise how important that decision was.
She finally recognised the need to take a break from work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignore - to refuse to acknowledge or notice
  • synonym: realize - to become fully aware of something as a fact
