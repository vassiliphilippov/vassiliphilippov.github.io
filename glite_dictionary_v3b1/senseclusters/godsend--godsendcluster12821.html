godsend

noun
unexpected and fortunate event
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdˌsɛnd / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdˌsɛnd / (BrE)
An unexpected event, opportunity, or piece of luck that comes at just the right time and proves to be extremely helpful or beneficial.

Examples

A godsend moment
Her arrival was a true godsend during the busiest week of our year.
The extra funding was a godsend for the struggling community center.
The mild winter proved to be a godsend for local farmers worried about their crops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boon - a thing that is helpful or beneficial
  • antonym: curse - a cause of harm or trouble
  • synonym: windfall - an unexpected gain or piece of good fortune
