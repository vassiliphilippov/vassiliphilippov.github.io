claro

noun
light-colored cigar wrapper
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈklɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
A cigar made with a light-colored wrapper, especially one that is pale yellowish-brown.

Examples

A claro cigar
A claro is distinguished by its pale, golden-brown wrapper.
He prefers a claro because he likes the milder taste of the wrapper.
The cigar shop had a selection of claro and maduro cigars on display.

Related Terms

  • antonym: maduro - a cigar with a dark, mature wrapper
