capstone

noun
final, crowning achievement
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkæpˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkæpstəʊn / (BrE)
The final and most important part of something, serving as a crowning achievement or culmination.

Examples

The novel serves as the capstone to his decades-long writing journey.
