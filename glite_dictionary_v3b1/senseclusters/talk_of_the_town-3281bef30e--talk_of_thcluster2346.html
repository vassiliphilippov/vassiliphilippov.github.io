talk of the town

noun
something widely talked about; subject of gossip
3.1 3.3
Pronunciation: /tɔːk əv ðə taʊn/ /; / /tɑːk əv ðə taʊn/ (AmE), /tɔːk əv ðə taʊn/ (BrE)
a person, event, or thing that everyone in a place is talking about; something or someone that is very popular or widely discussed

Examples

The talk of the town
After her stunning performance, she became the talk of the town.
Rumors about the mayor's resignation were the talk of the town for weeks.
The new cafe's unique decor has made it the talk of the town.
Her performance was so impressive that she was the talk of the town for months.
The mysterious disappearance of the statue is still the talk of the town.
The new bakery quickly became the talk of the town after opening last week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossip - casual or unconstrained conversation about other people, typically involving details that are not confirmed as being true
  • synonym: hot topic - subject that is currently being widely discussed
  • degree higher: scandal - action or event that causes public outrage or interest
