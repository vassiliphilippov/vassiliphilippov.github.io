bone-deep tired

adjective
extremely fatigued
Pronunciation: / boʊn diːp ˈtaɪɚd / (AmE), / bəʊn diːp ˈtaɪəd / (BrE)
Extremely fatigued; experiencing a profound level of exhaustion that feels as if it reaches deep into one's bones.

Examples

Bone-deep tired after work
After the night shift, she felt bone-deep tired and could barely stay awake.
By the end of exam week, most students are bone-deep tired and desperate for rest.
He collapsed onto the couch, bone-deep tired from working two jobs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhausted - very tired; completely worn out
  • synonym: weary - very tired, especially from hard work or effort
