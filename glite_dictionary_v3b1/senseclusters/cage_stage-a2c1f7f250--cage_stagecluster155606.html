cage stage

noun
period of intense zeal after change
6.0
Pronunciation: / keɪdʒ steɪdʒ / (AmE), / keɪdʒ steɪdʒ / (BrE)
A period of intense enthusiasm or extreme behavior, especially following a significant personal change or conversion, often leading to overzealous actions or attitudes.

Examples

The cage stage period
After joining the group, he went through a cage stage where he talked about it constantly.
Many people remember their own cage stage and laugh about how intense they were.
The cage stage can sometimes strain relationships with friends who do not share the same enthusiasm.
