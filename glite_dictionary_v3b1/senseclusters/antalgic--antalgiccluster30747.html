antalgic

adjective
reducing pain
9.3
Pronunciation: / ænˈtældʒɪk / (AmE), / ænˈtældʒɪk / (BrE)
adopted to reduce or avoid pain, especially describing a movement or posture

Examples

Antalgic gait
The doctor noted the patient's antalgic posture during the examination.
He developed an antalgic gait to minimize the pain in his left knee.
Physical therapists often observe antalgic movements in patients with chronic injuries.
