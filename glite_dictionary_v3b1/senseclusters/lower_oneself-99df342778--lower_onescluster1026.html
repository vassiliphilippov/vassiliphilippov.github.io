lower oneself

verb
behave in a less dignified or honorable way
3.1 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.ər wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ər wʌn.ˈself/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.ə wʌn.ˈself/ /; / /ˈlaʊ.ə wʌn.ˈself/ (BrE)

Examples
