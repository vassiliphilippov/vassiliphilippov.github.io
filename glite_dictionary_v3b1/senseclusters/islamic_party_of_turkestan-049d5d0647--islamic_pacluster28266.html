Islamic Party of Turkestan

proper noun
militant Islamist group in Central Asia
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪzˈlɑːmɪk ˈpɑːrti əv tʊrkɛˈstɑːn / (AmE), / ɪzˈlɑːmɪk ˈpɑːti ɒv tɜːkɪˈstɑːn / (BrE)
a militant Islamist group founded in 1996 that opposes Uzbekistan's secular government and seeks to establish an Islamic state in Central Asia; known for militant activities and links to drug trafficking.

Examples

Islamic Party of Turkestan militant group
