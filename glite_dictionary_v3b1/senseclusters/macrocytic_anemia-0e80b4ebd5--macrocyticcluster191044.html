macrocytic anemia

noun
anemia with large red blood cells
2.5 4.7
Pronunciation: /mæk.rə.ˈsɪt.ɪk ə.ˈniː.mi.ə/ (AmE), /mæk.rə.ˈsɪt.ɪk ə.ˈniː.mi.ə/ (BrE)
Anemia characterized by unusually large red blood cells (erythrocytes).

The doctor diagnosed her with macrocytic anemia after reviewing her blood tests.
