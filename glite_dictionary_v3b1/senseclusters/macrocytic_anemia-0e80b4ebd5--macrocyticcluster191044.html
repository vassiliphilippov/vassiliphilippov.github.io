macrocytic anemia

noun
anemia with large red blood cells
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊˈsɪtɪk əˈniːmiə /; / ˌmæk.roʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (AmE), / ˌmak.rəʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə /; / ˌmæ.krəʊˈsɪt.ɪk əˈniː.mi.ə / (BrE)
Anemia characterized by unusually large red blood cells (erythrocytes).
