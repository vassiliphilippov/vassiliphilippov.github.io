toenail

noun
nail at the tip of a toe
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊˌneɪl / (AmE), / ˈtəʊˌneɪl / (BrE)
the hard, flat covering at the tip of a toe in humans and some animals

Examples

A broken toenail
After a long hike, her toenail turned black and eventually fell off.
He accidentally stubbed his toenail against the edge of the bed.
You should cut your toenail straight across to avoid ingrown nails.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fingernail - the nail at the end of a finger
