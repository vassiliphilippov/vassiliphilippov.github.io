certify

verb
attest or provide evidence for
1.6 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.təˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.tɪ.faɪ / (BrE)
to provide evidence for or formally attest to something; to show or prove through one's behavior, attitude, or external attributes

Examples

To certify leadership skills
Her composure in the crisis seemed to certify her leadership abilities.
The accountant will certify these financial statements as accurate and complete.
The diploma will certify that you have successfully completed the course.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attest - to provide or serve as clear evidence of
  • synonym: demonstrate - to clearly show the existence or truth of something by giving proof or evidence
  • antonym: disprove - to prove that something is false
