rheumatology

noun
medical study of joints and related disorders
Pronunciation: / ˌruːməˈtɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌruːməˈtɒlədʒi / (BrE)
The branch of medicine concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases affecting the joints, muscles, and connective tissues.

Examples

Rheumatology specialist
After years of joint pain, she finally decided to consult a rheumatology specialist.
He published several papers in respected rheumatology journals about novel treatments for arthritis.
The conference focused on the latest advances in rheumatology research and patient care.

  • synonym: rheumatologist - a doctor specializing in rheumatology
