propyl alcohol

noun
volatile liquid alcohol, solvent and antiseptic
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊpəl ˈælkəhɑl / (AmE), / ˈprəʊpɪl ˈælkəhɒl / (BrE)
a clear, colorless volatile liquid alcohol used as a solvent and antiseptic

Examples

Propyl alcohol solvent use
Propyl alcohol is commonly used as a disinfectant in hospitals and laboratories.
For cleaning delicate equipment, technicians often prefer propyl alcohol over stronger solvents.
The safety data sheet lists propyl alcohol as flammable and recommends proper ventilation during use.
