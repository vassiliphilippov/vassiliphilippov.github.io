betrust

verb
to entrust
Pronunciation: / bɪˈtrʌst / (AmE), / bɪˈtrʌst / (BrE)
to entrust or commit something to the care or responsibility of someone

Examples

To betrust a friend
He did betrust his secrets to his closest friend.
In ancient times, people would betrust their valuables to the care of trusted guardians.
The king chose to betrust the safety of the realm to his most loyal knight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entrust - to give someone responsibility for something
  • antonym: withhold - to refuse to give something to someone
