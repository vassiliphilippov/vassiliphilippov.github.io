lethal agent

noun
something that can cause death
Pronunciation: / ˈliːθəl ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ˈliːθl ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)

Examples

A lethal agent substance

Some nerve gases are classified as lethal agents due to their extreme toxicity.
