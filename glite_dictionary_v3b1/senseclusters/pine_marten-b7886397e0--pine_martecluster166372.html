pine marten

noun
small Eurasian forest mammal
3.3
Pronunciation: / paɪn ˈmɑːrtn̩ / (AmE), / paɪn ˈmɑːtən / (BrE)
A small, dark brown carnivorous mammal (Martes martes) of northern Eurasian coniferous forests, related to the weasel.

Examples

The pine marten animal
Conservation efforts have helped increase the pine marten population in Scotland.
Forests with plenty of old trees provide ideal habitats for the pine marten.
The pine marten is a shy animal rarely seen during the day.
