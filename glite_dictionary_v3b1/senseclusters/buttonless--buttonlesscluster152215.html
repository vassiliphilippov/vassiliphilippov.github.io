buttonless

adjective
without buttons
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtnləs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtnləs / (BrE)
without buttons, especially as fasteners or decoration on clothing

Examples

A buttonless shirt
He wore a buttonless shirt to the party for a more relaxed look.
The new jacket design is completely buttonless and uses a zipper instead.
Many summer dresses are buttonless for comfort and simplicity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buttoned - having buttons as fasteners or decoration
