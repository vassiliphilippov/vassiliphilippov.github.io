Tetraonidae

noun
family of birds including grouse
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɛˌtreɪˈɑːnɪˌdiː / (AmE), / tɛˌtreɪˈɒnɪdiː / (BrE)
A family of birds in the order Galliformes that includes grouse and related species, known for their robust bodies and feathered legs.
