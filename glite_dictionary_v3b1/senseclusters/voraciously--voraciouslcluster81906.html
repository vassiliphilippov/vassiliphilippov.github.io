voraciously

adverb
eagerly and hungrily
7.3
Pronunciation: / vɔˈreɪʃəsli / (AmE), / vɔːˈreɪʃəsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows great eagerness or hunger for something, especially food or knowledge

Examples

To eat voraciously
After the long hike, he ate his dinner voraciously, as if he hadn't eaten in days.
She devoured the novel voraciously, unable to put it down until the last page.
The students listened voraciously as the professor revealed new research findings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: greedily - in an excessively eager way, especially for food
  • antonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant manner
