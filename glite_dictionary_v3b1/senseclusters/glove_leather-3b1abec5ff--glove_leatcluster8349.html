glove leather

noun
leather for making gloves
Pronunciation: / ɡlʌv ˈlɛðɚ / (AmE), / ɡlʌv ˈlɛðə / (BrE)
Soft, flexible leather that is specially processed to be suitable for making gloves.

Examples

Glove leather material
Glove leather is valued for its softness and pliability in fashion accessories.
The fine pair of driving gloves was crafted from high-quality glove leather.
You should condition glove leather regularly to keep it from drying out.
