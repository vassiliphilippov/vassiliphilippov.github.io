tippee

noun
person receiving tip
6.7
Pronunciation: / tɪˈpiː / (AmE), / tɪˈpiː / (BrE)

Examples

As a regular tippee, she always appreciated the kindness of her patrons.
After delivering the groceries, the driver became a happy tippee thanks to a small cash reward.
