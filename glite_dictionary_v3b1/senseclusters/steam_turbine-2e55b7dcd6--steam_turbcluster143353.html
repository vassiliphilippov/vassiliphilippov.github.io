steam turbine

noun
machine using steam to generate rotary motion
5.1
Pronunciation: / stiːm ˈtɝːˌbaɪn / (AmE), / stiːm ˈtɜːˌbaɪn / (BrE)
A machine in which high-pressure steam is used to spin blades and produce rotary motion, commonly for electrical generation or mechanical work.

Examples

A steam turbine machine
After maintenance, the engineers restarted the steam turbine safely.
The invention of the steam turbine revolutionized the shipping industry in the early 20th century.
The power plant uses a steam turbine to convert heat into electricity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turbine - machine that generates rotary motion, often from fluid flow
