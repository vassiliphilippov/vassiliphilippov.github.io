emanator

noun
originator or source
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tər / (AmE), / ˈɛm.əˌneɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person or thing that originates or gives rise to something

Examples

Art emanator
The artist became the emanator of a new style that influenced many others.
As the emanator of the idea, she received much recognition from her peers.
The sun is the emanator of light and warmth for our planet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: originator - a person who creates or initiates something
  • synonym: source - the place, person, or thing from which something comes
