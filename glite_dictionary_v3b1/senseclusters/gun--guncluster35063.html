gun

noun
grease pump shaped like a pistol
Pronunciation: / ɡʌn / (AmE), / ɡʌn / (BrE)
a hand-operated device resembling a pistol, used to force grease into parts of a machine

Examples

A grease gun
A good gun can make the process of greasing joints much easier.
Make sure to refill the gun with grease before starting maintenance.
The mechanic reached for the gun to lubricate the gears.
