Synonyms

  • penchant
    8.2 3.41/m
    penchant is a strong, habitual liking for something, while predilection is a formal word for a lasting personal preference.
  • preference
    2.1 24/m
    preference is a greater liking for one thing over others, while predilection implies a stronger, often personal liking.
  • inclination
    4.2 6.09/m
    inclination is a natural tendency or urge to do something, while predilection is a steady liking for a thing.
  • proclivity
    8.5 1.54/m
    proclivity is a natural tendency, often for a bad habit or action, while predilection means a liking or preference.
