guesstimate

noun
an estimate made by guessing
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛs.tɪ.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɛs.tɪ.meɪt / (BrE)
An estimate made without sufficient information, combining elements of guessing and estimating.

Examples

A quick guesstimate
My guesstimate is that there were around fifty people at the party.
The contractor gave a guesstimate of how long the repairs would take.
Without all the data, any figure we provide will be just a guesstimate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estimate - a rough judgment or calculation
  • synonym: guess - an opinion or answer based on little or no evidence
