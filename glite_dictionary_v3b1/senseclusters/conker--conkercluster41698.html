conker

noun
inedible horse chestnut seed
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːŋ.kər / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋ.kə / (BrE)
the hard, shiny, brown, inedible nutlike seed of the horse chestnut tree

Examples

A shiny conker
A large, shiny conker fell from the horse chestnut tree onto the sidewalk.
As children, we would collect conkers every autumn and prepare them for the playground game.
Some people drill holes in their conkers to thread string through for the traditional game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: horse chestnut - a tree producing conkers; species Aesculus hippocastanum
