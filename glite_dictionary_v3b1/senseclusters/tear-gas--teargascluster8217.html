tear-gas

adjective
relating to tear gas
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪr ˌɡæs / (AmE), / ˈtɪə ˌɡæs / (BrE)
containing, relating to, or caused by tear gas, especially describing weapons, canisters, or effects involving tear gas

Examples

A tear-gas attack left many people coughing and unable to see clearly.
