research grant program

Pronunciation: / dʒi ɑr ɛf / (AmE), / dʒiː ɑː ɛf / (BrE)
an abbreviation for General Research Fund, a competitive grant program supporting academic research projects

Examples

GRF research grant
The university encourages faculty to apply for a GRF to support innovative research projects.
Her team secured a GRF last year to study climate change impacts on agriculture.
Winning a GRF can significantly boost a researcher's academic career.
