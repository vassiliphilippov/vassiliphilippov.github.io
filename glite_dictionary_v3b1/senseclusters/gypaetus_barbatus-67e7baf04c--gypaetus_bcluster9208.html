Gypaetus barbatus

noun
species of large Eurasian vulture
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒaɪˈpiːətəs bɑrˈbeɪtəs / (AmE), / dʒɪˈpiːətəs bɑːˈbeɪtəs / (BrE)
A large Eurasian vulture, also known as the bearded vulture, distinguished by black bristles beneath its beak.

Examples

The Gypaetus barbatus vulture
Researchers have observed the nesting habits of Gypaetus barbatus in the Himalayas.
The Gypaetus barbatus is often spotted soaring over mountain ranges in Europe and Asia.
Unlike many other vultures, the Gypaetus barbatus primarily feeds on bone marrow.
