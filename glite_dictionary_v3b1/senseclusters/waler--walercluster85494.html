waler

noun
horizontal support beam
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪlər / (AmE), / ˈweɪlə / (BrE)
a horizontal timber or metal beam used to support or reinforce formwork, retaining walls, or sheet piling in construction

Examples

Steel waler beam
The engineers installed a steel waler to reinforce the retaining wall during excavation.
Each wooden waler was carefully measured to fit the length of the concrete formwork.
Without a proper waler, the sheet piling could have collapsed under the pressure of the soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brace - a device that supports or strengthens a structure
  • synonym: strut - a structural support designed to resist compression
