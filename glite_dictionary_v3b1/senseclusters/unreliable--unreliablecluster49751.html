unreliable

adjective
not able to be trusted or depended on
3.5 3.4
Pronunciation: /ʌn.rɪ.ˈlaɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.riː.ˈlaɪ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.rɪ.ˈlaɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.riː.ˈlaɪ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be trusted or depended on; likely to be wrong, misleading, or not do what is expected
