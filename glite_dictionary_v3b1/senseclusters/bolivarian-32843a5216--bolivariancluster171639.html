Bolivarian

adjective
of Simón Bolívar
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊlɪˈvɛriən / (AmE), / ˌbɒlɪˈvɑːriən / (BrE)
relating to Simón Bolívar or to his political and social ideals, especially in Latin America

Examples

Bolivarian ideals
The new constitution was inspired by Bolivarian principles of social justice.
Many leaders in South America have adopted a Bolivarian approach to governance.
The Bolivarian movement seeks to unite Latin American countries under shared ideals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bolívarian - pertaining to Simón Bolívar or his ideas
