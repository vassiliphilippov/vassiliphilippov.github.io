blanquette

noun
French sparkling wine
Pronunciation: / blɑ̃ˈkɛt / (AmE), / blɑ̃ˈkɛt / (BrE)
a sparkling white wine from the Limoux region of southwestern France, traditionally made from the Mauzac grape
