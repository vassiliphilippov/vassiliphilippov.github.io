black-barred

adjective
marked with black bars or stripes
Pronunciation: / ˌblækˈbɑrd / (AmE), / ˌblækˈbɑːd / (BrE)
having a pattern marked with black stripes or bars, especially on animals or objects.

Examples

Black-barred wings
He admired the black-barred wings of the butterfly resting on his hand.
Several black-barred fish swam past the coral reef, blending into the shadows.
The black-barred owl is known for its distinctive dark stripes across its feathers.

  • synonym: barred - having bars or stripes as a pattern
