grapey

adjective
tasting or smelling like grapes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪpi / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪpi / (BrE)
Having a taste or aroma characteristic of grapes.

Examples

Grapey taste
She found the juice too sweet and grapey for her liking.
The wine was unusually grapey, with a fresh, fruity aroma.
This jelly has a delightfully grapey flavor that reminds me of homemade preserves.
