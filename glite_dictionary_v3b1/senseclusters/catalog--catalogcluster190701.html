catalog

verb
make an itemized list; classify
Pronunciation: / ˈkæt̬.ə.lɔɡ /; / ˈkæt.əl.ɔɡ /; / ˈkæt̬.ə.lɑːɡ /; / ˈkæt.əˌlɔɡ / (AmE), / ˈkæt.əl.ɒɡ /; / ˈkæt.əlɒɡ / (BrE)
to make or organize a systematic list of items; to classify or group items methodically
