mesh

verb
entangle or catch in a mesh
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɛʃ / (AmE), / mɛʃ / (BrE)
to entangle or catch in a net or web-like structure

Examples

To mesh insects
He watched as the sticky tape meshed the insects on the window.
Her hair became meshed in the brambles as she walked through the dense forest.
The fishing net quickly meshed the small fish as it was dragged through the water.
