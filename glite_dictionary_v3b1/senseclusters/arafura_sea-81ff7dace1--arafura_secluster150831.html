Arafura Sea

proper noun
sea between Australia and New Guinea
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌærəˈfjʊrə si / (AmE), / ˌarafˈjʊərə siː / (BrE)
a marginal sea of the western Pacific Ocean, located between northern Australia and southern New Guinea
