smack

verb
make a loud noise with the lips, often while eating
Pronunciation: / smæk / (AmE), / smæk / (BrE)
to press the lips together and open them noisily, especially while eating or in anticipation of food

Examples

To smack his lips
He always smacks his lips when something smells good to him.
She smacked her lips after tasting the delicious dessert.
The child smacked his lips noisily as he ate his candy.
