blackish-gray

adjective
a dark shade of gray
Pronunciation: / ˈblækɪʃ ˌɡreɪ /; / ˈblækɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (AmE), / ˈblækɪʃ ˌɡreɪ /; / ˈblakɪʃ ˈɡreɪ / (BrE)
