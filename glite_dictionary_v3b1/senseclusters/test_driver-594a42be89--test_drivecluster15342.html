test driver

noun
person who evaluates vehicle performance by driving
Pronunciation: / tɛst ˈdraɪvər / (AmE), / tɛst ˈdraɪvə / (BrE)
A person who drives a vehicle to assess its performance, typically as part of the vehicle's development or evaluation process.

Examples

Professional test driver
Each test driver must carefully record their impressions after driving the prototype vehicles.
She always dreamed of becoming a test driver and traveling the world to evaluate the latest cars.
The car company hired a skilled test driver to assess the new model's handling and safety features.

  • synonym: test pilot - a person who flies aircraft to evaluate performance and handling
