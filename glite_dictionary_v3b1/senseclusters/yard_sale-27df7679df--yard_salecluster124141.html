yard sale

noun
outdoor sale of used items
Pronunciation: / jɑrd seɪl / (AmE), / jɑːd seɪl / (BrE)
An outdoor sale of used personal or household items, typically held on the seller's property.

Examples

A yard sale event
My neighbors set up a yard sale every summer weekend.
She found a vintage lamp at a yard sale for just five dollars.
We decided to host a yard sale to get rid of old clothes and books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garage sale - an informal sale of used items, often in a garage
