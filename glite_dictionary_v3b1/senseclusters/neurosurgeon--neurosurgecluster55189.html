neurosurgeon

noun
surgeon specializing in nervous system operations
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnʊroʊˌsɝdʒən / (AmE), / ˈnjʊərəʊˌsɜːdʒən / (BrE)
a medical doctor who specializes in performing surgery on the nervous system, especially the brain and spinal cord

Examples

A neurosurgeon operates
After the accident, the neurosurgeon performed a delicate operation to relieve pressure on the brain.
Many neurosurgeons spend years training before they can operate independently.
She consulted a neurosurgeon about her chronic back pain related to her spinal cord.
