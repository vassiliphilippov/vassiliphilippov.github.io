labrum

noun
cartilage ring in joint
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbrəm / (AmE), / ˈleɪbrəm / (BrE)
a ring of cartilage in a joint, such as the shoulder or hip, that helps stabilize and cushion the joint

Examples

Shoulder labrum
The athlete suffered a torn labrum in his shoulder during the game.
Doctors explained that the labrum helps keep the hip joint stable and functioning smoothly.
After surgery to repair her labrum, she needed several months of physical therapy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cartilage - a firm, flexible connective tissue found in joints
