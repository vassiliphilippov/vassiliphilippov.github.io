pounder

noun
tool for grinding or mixing materials
4.2 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊn.dər/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊn.də/ (BrE)
a heavy tool, usually with a flat base and a handle, used for grinding and mixing materials such as grain, drugs, or pigments against a slab of stone

Examples

Stone pounder tool
A heavy iron pounder was found among the archaeological relics.
Ancient artists would prepare pigments with a pounder and a flat slab.
The apothecary used a stone pounder to crush medicinal herbs.
