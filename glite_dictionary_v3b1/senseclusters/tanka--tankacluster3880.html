tanka

noun
Tibetan religious painting on fabric
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑŋkə / (AmE), / ˈtæŋkə / (BrE)
A Tibetan religious painting made on fabric, often depicting Buddhist deities or scenes, and used for meditation or ceremonial purposes.

Examples

A Buddhist tanka painting
Conservators carefully restored the damaged tanka to preserve its intricate details.
