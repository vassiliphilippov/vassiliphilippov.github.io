incorruption

noun
integrity; freedom from corruption
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.kəˈrʌp.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.kəˈrʌp.ʃən / (BrE)
The quality of being free from corruption; integrity or honesty.

Examples

The incorruption of leaders
Her reputation for incorruption was well known throughout the organization.
Public officials must strive for incorruption in all aspects of their work.
The judge was admired for his incorruption and sense of justice.

Related Terms
