androcratic

adjective
male-dominated society
Pronunciation: / ˌændroʊˈkrætɪk / (AmE), / ˌandrəʊˈkrætɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by male dominance in social or political systems

Examples

Androcratic system
The government was criticized for its androcratic structure, which excluded women from leadership roles.
Historians often describe ancient Athens as an androcratic society.
Efforts to reform the androcratic traditions faced strong resistance from conservative groups.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patriarchal - relating to a system controlled by men
