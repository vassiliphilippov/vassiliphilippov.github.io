equipped

adjective
having what is needed for a purpose
Pronunciation: / ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
having the necessary tools, equipment, items, features, or abilities for a particular purpose

Examples

Equipped with tools
Each classroom is equipped with a smart board and sound system.
She felt equipped to handle any challenge after months of training.
The laboratory was fully equipped for advanced research.
Only equipped vehicles are allowed on the icy mountain roads in winter.
She felt well equipped to handle the challenges of her new job.
The expedition was equipped with advanced navigation tools and plenty of food supplies.
Every mountain climber needs to be properly equipped before starting the expedition.
The laboratory is fully equipped for advanced scientific research.
The modern kitchen was equipped with the latest appliances.
