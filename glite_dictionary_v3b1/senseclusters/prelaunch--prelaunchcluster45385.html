prelaunch

noun
before official launch
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːlɔːntʃ / (AmE), / ˈpriːlɔːntʃ / (BrE)
the period or set of activities that take place before something is officially launched or released

Examples

Prelaunch phase
The marketing team finalized their strategy during the prelaunch phase of the product.
Many important tests are conducted in the prelaunch period to avoid any issues later.
Excitement built up among fans throughout the prelaunch of the new video game.

Related Terms

  • antonym: launch - the official start or release of something
  • synonym: preparation - the process of getting ready for something
