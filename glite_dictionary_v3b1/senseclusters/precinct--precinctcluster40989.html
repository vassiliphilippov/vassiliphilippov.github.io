precinct

noun
a city area for administrative purposes
6.2 8.18/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàpriÀê.s…™≈ãkt / (AmE), / ÀàpriÀê.s…™≈ãkt / (BrE)
an area within a city or town that is defined for administrative or official purposes, especially for police or electoral functions

Examples

Police precinct
Each police precinct has its own headquarters and officers.
The city is divided into several precincts for local elections.
The new stadium falls within the university precinct.

Synonyms

  • voting precinct
    5.2 0.16/m
    a voting precinct serves one polling place and local voters, while a precinct can mean any administrative area also used by police.
  • election district
    3.5 0.09/m
    an election district is a voting area with its own polling place, while a precinct can also mean a broader administrative or police area.
  • ward
    3.4 3.64/m
    a ward is a city district often used for local government, whereas a precinct is usually a smaller administrative area used for police or voting.
