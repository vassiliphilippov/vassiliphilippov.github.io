moper

noun
gloomy person
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊpər / (AmE), / ˈməʊpə / (BrE)
a person who is often gloomy, sulks, or appears sad and withdrawn

Examples

A gloomy moper
After losing the game, he became a real moper and refused to talk to anyone.
Nobody wants to spend time with a moper who always brings down the mood.
She tried to cheer up her friend, but he remained a moper all afternoon.
Whenever he loses a game, he turns into a moper for the rest of the day.
Nobody wants to work with a moper who brings down the team's mood.
She was called a moper because she always looked sad and rarely smiled.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful person - someone who is happy and optimistic
  • synonym: complainer - a person who often expresses dissatisfaction
  • synonym: sulker - a person who sulks or pouts
  • synonym: whiner - someone who complains in an annoying way
