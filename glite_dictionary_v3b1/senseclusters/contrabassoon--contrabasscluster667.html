contrabassoon

noun
large, deep woodwind instrument lower than the bassoon
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːntrə bəˈsuːn / (AmE), / ˌkɒntrə bəˈsuːn / (BrE)
a large, deep-pitched woodwind instrument in the oboe family, an octave lower than the standard bassoon

Examples

A contrabassoon instrument
A contrabassoon is rarely used in chamber music but adds depth to symphonic works.
She learned to play the contrabassoon, fascinated by its deep, rumbling sound.
The orchestra featured a solo by the contrabassoon during the dramatic finale.
