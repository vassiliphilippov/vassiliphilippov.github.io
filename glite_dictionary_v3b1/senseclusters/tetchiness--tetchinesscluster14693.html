tetchiness

noun
state of being easily irritated
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛtʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈtɛtʃɪnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being easily irritated or prone to annoyance.

Examples

Increased tetchiness
A lack of sleep often leads to increased tetchiness in children.
He apologized for his tetchiness during the meeting, blaming it on a headache.
Her tetchiness was apparent after a long, stressful day at work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the quality of being free from agitation or irritation
  • synonym: irritability - the quality of being easily irritated
