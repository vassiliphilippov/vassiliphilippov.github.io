chubby

adjective
slightly plump or rounded
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌbi / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌbi / (BrE)
slightly fat in a way that is attractive or endearing

Examples

A chubby baby
Although he was a bit chubby as a child, he grew taller and slimmer later.
She gave her chubby cat an extra treat.
The baby's chubby cheeks made everyone smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plump - having a full, rounded shape; slightly fat
  • antonym: slim - thin in an attractive way; not fat
