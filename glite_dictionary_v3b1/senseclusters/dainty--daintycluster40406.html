dainty

noun
delicate, tasty piece of food
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪnti / (AmE), / ˈdeɪnti / (BrE)
a small, delicate, and tasty piece of food

Examples

Sweet dainty
Children filled their plates with sweet dainties at the holiday party.
She could not resist sampling the dainties laid out at the reception.
The baker arranged the dainties on a silver platter for the guests.
