gingerbread

noun
warm brown colour
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪndʒərbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒəbrɛd / (BrE)
A warm, medium brown colour resembling the shade of gingerbread cookies, used for paints, fabrics, and interior or fashion design.

Examples

Gingerbread color paint
The kitchen walls were painted a warm gingerbread to create a cozy vibe.
She chose a gingerbread dress that matched her autumn-themed makeup perfectly.
The interior designer recommended a gingerbread palette to complement the reclaimed wood floors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brown - a general colour produced by mixing red, yellow, and blue; darker yellowish-brown shades.
  • synonym: tan - a light brown colour; often used for leather or fabric shades.
