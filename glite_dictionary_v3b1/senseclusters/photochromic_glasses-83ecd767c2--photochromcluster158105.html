photochromic glasses

noun
eyeglasses with sunlight-darkening lenses
7.5 4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊˈkroʊmɪk ˈɡlæsɪz / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊˈkrəʊmɪk ˈɡlɑːsɪz / (BrE)
Eyeglasses fitted with lenses that automatically darken in response to sunlight and become clear again in low light.

Examples

Photochromic glasses darken outside
He always wears his photochromic glasses so he doesn't have to switch to sunglasses outside.
My photochromic glasses automatically darkened when I stepped into the bright afternoon sun.
The optician recommended photochromic glasses for people sensitive to changing light conditions.
