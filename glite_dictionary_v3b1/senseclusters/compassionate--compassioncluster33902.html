Synonyms

  • kindhearted
    2.5 0.30/m
    kindhearted describes a naturally warm, generous person who often helps others, while compassionate focuses on concern and sympathy for suffering.
  • tenderhearted
    3.1 0.12/m
    tenderhearted describes a gentle, easily moved person who feels deep pity, while compassionate stresses active concern for others' suffering.
  • sympathetic
    3.4 10/m
    sympathetic means showing understanding and pity for someone's trouble, while compassionate implies deeper concern and a wish to help.
  • humane
    3.4 6.30/m
    humane describes actions or rules that reduce suffering, while compassionate names a feeling or person who cares about others' pain.
