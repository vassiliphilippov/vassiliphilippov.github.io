mere trumpery

noun
worthless or showy items
Pronunciation: / mɪr ˈtrʌmpəri / (AmE), / mɪə ˈtrʌmpəri / (BrE)
Worthless or showy objects; things of little real value, often with a pretentious appearance.

Examples

Mere trumpery objects
Don't be fooled by mere trumpery—focus on what truly matters.
His office was filled with mere trumpery to impress visitors.
The jewelry she wore was mere trumpery, lacking any real value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trumpery - showy but worthless finery or nonsense
