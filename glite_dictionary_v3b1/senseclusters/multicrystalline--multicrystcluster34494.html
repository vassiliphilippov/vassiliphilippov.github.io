multicrystalline

adjective
having many crystals
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈkrɪstəlaɪn / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈkrɪstəlaɪn / (BrE)
consisting of or relating to multiple crystals or grains within a material

Examples

Multicrystalline silicon
Many solar panels use multicrystalline silicon because it is less expensive to produce.
The multicrystalline structure of the metal affects its strength and durability.
Researchers compared the efficiency of multicrystalline and monocrystalline solar cells in different conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monocrystalline - consisting of a single crystal
  • synonym: polycrystalline - made up of many small crystals or grains
