wheeling

noun
propelling on wheels
4.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwiː.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhwiː.lɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈwiː.lɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhwiː.lɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act of moving or turning something on wheels or in a circular motion

Examples
