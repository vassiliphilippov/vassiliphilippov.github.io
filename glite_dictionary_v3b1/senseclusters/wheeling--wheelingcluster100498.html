wheeling

noun
propelling on wheels
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving or turning something on wheels or in a circular motion

Examples

The wheeling of trolleys
Her job involved the wheeling of heavy trolleys from the storeroom to the kitchen.
The noise from the wheeling of luggage across the tiles echoed through the hall.
There was a steady wheeling of carts along the village lanes each morning.
The workers made quick wheeling trips with the carts across the warehouse floor.
Her wheeling of the suitcase through the airport was smooth and effortless.
The parade featured impressive wheeling formations by the marching band.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carrying - the act of holding and moving something from one place to another
  • synonym: rolling - the act of moving something by turning it over and over on a surface
