Tiresias

proper noun
blind prophet in Greek mythology
9.9
Pronunciation: / taɪˈriːsiəs / (AmE), / taɪˈriːsiəs / (BrE)
(in Greek mythology) a blind prophet of Thebes, renowned for his wisdom, who revealed to Oedipus that he had killed his father and married his mother.

Examples
