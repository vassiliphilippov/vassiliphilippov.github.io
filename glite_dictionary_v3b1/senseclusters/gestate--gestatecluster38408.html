gestate

verb
develop an idea or plan over time
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛsteɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛsteɪt / (BrE)
to develop an idea or plan in the mind over a period of time before it is fully formed or realized

Examples

To gestate an idea
Many great inventions are gestated quietly before they revolutionize the world.
She spent months gestating the novel's intricate plot before starting to write.
The director began to gestate the concept for his new film over several years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conceive - to form or develop an idea or plan in the mind
