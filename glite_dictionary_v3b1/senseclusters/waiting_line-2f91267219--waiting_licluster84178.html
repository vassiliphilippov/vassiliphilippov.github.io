waiting line

noun
a line of people or vehicles waiting
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪŋ laɪn / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ laɪn / (BrE)
A line of people or vehicles that are waiting their turn for something.

Examples

A waiting line of people
After arriving at the airport, she joined the waiting line for check-in.
The waiting line for the new restaurant stretched down the block.
We stood in a waiting line at the ticket booth for over an hour.
