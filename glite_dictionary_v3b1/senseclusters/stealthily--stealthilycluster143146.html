stealthily

adverb
in a secret or sneaky way
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛlθɪli / (AmE), / ˈstɛlθɪli / (BrE)
In a way that is quiet and secret, in order to avoid being noticed.

Examples

To stealthily move
Moving stealthily through the shadows, the spy avoided the security cameras.
She stealthily slipped the letter into his bag while he was distracted.
The cat moved stealthily across the garden, avoiding the dog's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in a manner that is not secret or hidden
  • synonym: secretly - in a secret manner
