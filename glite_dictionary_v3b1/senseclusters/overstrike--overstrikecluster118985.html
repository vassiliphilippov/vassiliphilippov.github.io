overstrike

noun
mark over original
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.straɪk/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.straɪk/ (BrE)
a mark, stamp, or printed character made by striking or printing over an existing design, especially on coins or tokens

Examples
