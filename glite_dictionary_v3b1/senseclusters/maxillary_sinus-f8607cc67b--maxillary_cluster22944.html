maxillary sinus

noun
air cavity in the upper jaw bone
7.1 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmæk.sɪ.lər.iː ˈsaɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmæks.ɪ.lər.iː ˈsaɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmæk.sɪ.lə.riː ˈsaɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈmæks.ɪ.lə.riː ˈsaɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
either of the pair of air-filled cavities located in the maxilla (upper jaw bone) on each side of the nose.
