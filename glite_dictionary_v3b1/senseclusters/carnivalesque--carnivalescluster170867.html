carnivalesque

adjective
like a carnival
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːrnɪvəˈlɛsk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːnɪvəˈlɛsk / (BrE)

Examples

Carnivalesque parade

Bright colors and masks gave the party a truly carnivalesque feel.
The novel's carnivalesque scenes were filled with laughter and chaos.
