revitalize

verb
give new life or energy to
6.5
Pronunciation: / riˈvaɪtəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / rɪˈvʌɪtəlʌɪz /; / rɪˈvɪtəlaɪz /; / riˈvaɪtəlaɪz / (BrE)
to give new life, energy, or strength to something; to make something active, effective, or healthy again
