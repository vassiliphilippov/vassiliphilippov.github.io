iconographer

noun
creator of religious icons
9.1
Pronunciation: /aɪ.kə.ˈnɑːg.rə.fər/ (AmE), /aɪ.kə.ˈnɒg.rə.fə/ (BrE)
a person who creates or studies religious images, especially icons, often in the context of Christian art

Examples

Religious iconographer
The iconographer carefully applied gold leaf to the surface of the wooden panel.
Many churches commission a skilled iconographer to create images for their altars.
As an iconographer, she studied ancient techniques to preserve traditional styles.
