temporality

noun
state of being temporary
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛmpəˈrælɪti / (AmE), / ˌtɛmpəˈrælɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being related to time or lasting for a limited period

Related Terms

  • antonym: eternity - infinite or unending time
  • antonym: permanence - the state of lasting forever or for a long time
  • synonym: timeliness - the quality of being done at the right time
  • synonym: transience - the state of lasting only for a short time
