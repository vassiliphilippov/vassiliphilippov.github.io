sand hopper

noun
small hopping beach crustacean
Pronunciation: / ˈsænd ˌhɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈsænd ˌhɒpə / (BrE)
a small amphipod crustacean found on ocean beaches that moves by making hopping motions similar to fleas

Examples

Small sand hopper
Birds often feed on sand hoppers along the shore.
Children were fascinated by the way the sand hopper leaped away when touched.
We found several sand hoppers scurrying across the wet sand at low tide.
