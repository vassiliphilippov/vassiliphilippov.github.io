intersubjective

adjective
shared understanding
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərsəbˈdʒɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌɪntəsʌbˈdʒɛktɪv / (BrE)
shared, agreed upon, or understood by two or more people (not just personal or individual)
