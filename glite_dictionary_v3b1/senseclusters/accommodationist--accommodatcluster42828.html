accommodationist

noun
person who compromises
Pronunciation: / əˌkɑːməˈdeɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˌkɒməˈdeɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who adapts to different groups or situations, or seeks compromise to avoid conflict or confrontation

Examples

The committee valued the input of the accommodationist who helped resolve disagreements.
Some criticized him for being an accommodationist rather than standing firm on his beliefs.
As an accommodationist, she often mediates between opposing sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compromiser - a person who is willing to make concessions
  • antonym: hardliner - a person who sticks firmly to a set of beliefs or policies
  • synonym: mediator - a person who helps others resolve disputes or reach agreement
  • antonym: opportunist - a person who takes advantage of situations for personal gain
