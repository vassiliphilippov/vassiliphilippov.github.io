beetle-headed

adjective
stupid or slow-witted
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtəlˌhɛdɪd / (AmE), / ˈbiːtlˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
old-fashioned term meaning stupid or slow-witted

Examples

Beetle-headed fool
In the old play, the villain was described as beetle-headed by the clever heroine.
He dismissed the suggestion as the idea of a beetle-headed fool.
The teacher scolded the student for his beetle-headed mistake on the exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dull - not intelligent or quick to understand
  • synonym: foolish - lacking good sense or judgment
