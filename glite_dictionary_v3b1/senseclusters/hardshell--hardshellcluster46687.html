hardshell

noun
protective outer jacket
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌʃɛl / (BrE)
a protective outer garment or case with a rigid or tough outer surface, especially a waterproof jacket for outdoor activities

Examples

A hardshell jacket
Before hiking in the rain, she put on her hardshell to stay dry.
A good hardshell is essential for mountaineers facing harsh weather conditions.
He packed his hardshell in case the weather turned stormy during the trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raincoat - a waterproof coat worn to protect against rain
  • antonym: softshell - a flexible, breathable outer garment for outdoor activities
