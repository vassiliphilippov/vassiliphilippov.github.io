Marlowesque

adjective
like Marlowe's style
9.9
Pronunciation: /mɑːr.loʊ.ˈesk/ (AmE), /mɑː.ləʊ.ˈesk/ (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of the style of Christopher Marlowe, especially in literature or drama

Examples

Marlowesque poetry
The director gave the production a distinctly Marlowesque atmosphere with poetic language and bold themes.
Her writing style is often described as Marlowesque because of its dramatic intensity.
Critics praised the Marlowesque elements in the new historical play.
