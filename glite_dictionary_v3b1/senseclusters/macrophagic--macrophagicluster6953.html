macrophagic

adjective
involving macrophages
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊˈfædʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌmakrəˈfædʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving macrophages, which are large immune cells that engulf and digest foreign substances

Examples

Macrophagic activity
The macrophagic activity in the tissue increased during the infection.
Researchers studied the macrophagic response to the new vaccine.
A strong macrophagic reaction can help clear bacteria from the body.

Related Terms

  • synonym: phagocytic - relating to the process of engulfing and digesting particles by cells
