hog plum

noun
small tree or shrub with edible fruit
4.3
Pronunciation: / hɑɡ plʌm / (AmE), / hɒɡ plʌm / (BrE)
a shrub or tree (especially of the genus Spondias) that produces small, edible, yellow fruit called hog plums
