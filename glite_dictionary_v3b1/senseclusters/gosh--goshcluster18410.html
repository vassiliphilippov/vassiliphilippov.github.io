gosh

interjection
mild exclamation
Pronunciation: / ɡɑʃ / (AmE), / ɡɒʃ / (BrE)
a mild exclamation used to express surprise, wonder, emphasis, or mild annoyance (used as a polite substitute for 'God')

Examples

Gosh, that's surprising
Gosh, I didn't realize the test would be so difficult!
When she saw the view from the mountain, she exclaimed, 'Gosh, this is beautiful!'
Gosh, I almost forgot your birthday was today!
Oh gosh, I completely forgot about your birthday!
Gosh, that was a close call on the highway this morning.
She said, 'gosh, this cake is delicious!' and asked for another slice.
