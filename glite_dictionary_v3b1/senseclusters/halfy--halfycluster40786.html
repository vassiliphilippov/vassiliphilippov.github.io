halfy

noun
slang for half portion
Pronunciation: / ˈhæfi / (AmE), / ˈhɑːfi / (BrE)
a slang term for a half portion of something, or in some regions, a person of mixed heritage

Examples

A halfy sandwich
He ordered a halfy instead of a full sandwich because he wasn't very hungry.
In some places, the word halfy is used to describe a person of mixed heritage.
She asked for a halfy of soup to go with her salad.

Related Terms

  • synonym: half - one of two equal parts of something
