moline

adjective
heraldic cross shape
Pronunciation: / moʊˈliːn / (AmE), / məʊˈliːn / (BrE)
in heraldry, having arms that split and curve outward at the ends, resembling a millrind

Examples

Moline cross
The knight's shield displayed a moline cross as its central emblem.
A moline design was chosen for the new family coat of arms.
The ancient banner was decorated with a large, gold moline at the top.
