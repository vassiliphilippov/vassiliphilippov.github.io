gage

verb
to pledge or measure
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪdʒ / (AmE), / ɡeɪdʒ / (BrE)
to offer something as security for a loan or to measure or assess something.

Examples

To gage property
He gaged his property to secure the loan from the bank.
The scientist gaged the pressure inside the chamber before starting the experiment.
They gaged the risks before making a final decision on the investment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: measure - to determine the size, amount, or degree of something
  • synonym: pledge - to offer something as a guarantee for repayment
