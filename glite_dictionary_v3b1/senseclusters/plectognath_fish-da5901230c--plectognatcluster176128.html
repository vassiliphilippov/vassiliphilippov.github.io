plectognath fish

noun
tropical fish with beak-like teeth and bony skin plates
6.5 9.2
Pronunciation: / plɛkˈtɑɡ.næθ fɪʃ / (AmE), / plɛkˈtɒɡ.næθ fɪʃ / (BrE)
