parchment

noun
superior paper resembling sheepskin
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrtʃmənt / (AmE), / ˈpɑːtʃmənt / (BrE)
a high-quality, stiff paper made to resemble sheepskin, often used for diplomas, certificates, or special documents

Examples

Parchment paper certificate
Many wedding invitations are still made using high-quality parchment paper.
She wrote her letter on parchment to make it look more distinguished.
The certificate was printed on elegant parchment to give it a traditional feel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: vellum - fine parchment made originally from calfskin
