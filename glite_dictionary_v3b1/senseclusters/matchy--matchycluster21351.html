matchy

adjective
closely coordinated
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmætʃi / (AmE), / ˈmætʃi / (BrE)
having a style or color that closely matches or coordinates with something else, often in a noticeable or deliberate way

Examples

Matchy outfit
She wore a matchy outfit with her shoes and purse in the same shade of blue.
Some people think being too matchy with accessories can look old-fashioned.
His matchy tie and pocket square made his suit look very put-together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clashing - not matching in style or color
  • synonym: coordinated - arranged to look good together
