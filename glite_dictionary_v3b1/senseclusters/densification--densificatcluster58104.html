densification

noun
process of increasing density
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɛn.sə.fəˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɛn.sɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of increasing the density of something, especially in materials, urban development, or scientific contexts

Examples

Urban densification
As the population grew, the densification of housing became increasingly necessary.
The densification of ceramic materials improves their strength and durability.
Urban planners often promote densification to make cities more sustainable and efficient.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compression - the act of pressing together to make something more compact
  • antonym: expansion - the process of increasing in size or volume, making less dense
