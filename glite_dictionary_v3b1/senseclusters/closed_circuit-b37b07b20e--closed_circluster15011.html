closed circuit

noun
complete electrical circuit
4.3 0.07/m
Pronunciation: / klo äzd Ààs…úrk…™t / (AmE), / kl…ô äzd Ààs…úÀêk…™t / (BrE)
an electrical circuit in which the path around which current flows is complete, allowing electricity or a signal to circulate

Examples

A closed circuit system
A light switch can turn a closed circuit into an open one, turning off the light.
Security cameras are monitored through a closed circuit system.
The technician tested the closed circuit to ensure electricity was flowing properly.

Synonyms

  • electrical circuit
    3.3 0.16/m
    an electrical circuit is a technical network of wires and parts, while a closed circuit specifically has a complete path so current can circulate.
  • loop
    4.9 7.20/m
    a loop is a simple closed path often used for signals or wiring, while a closed circuit is an electrical path that must be complete to let current flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: open circuit - an electrical circuit in which the path is incomplete, preventing current from flowing
