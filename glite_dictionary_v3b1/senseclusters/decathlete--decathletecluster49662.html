decathlete

noun
multi-skilled person
Pronunciation: / dɪˈkæθliːt / (AmE), / dɪˈkæθliːt / (BrE)
a person who is skilled or competent in many different fields or activities, especially outside of sports

Examples

Office decathlete
As a manager who handles finance, marketing, and design, he was a true decathlete of the company.
In today's fast-changing world, being a decathlete can help you adapt to new challenges.
She became known as the office decathlete because she could solve problems in any department.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polymath - a person with knowledge in many different subjects
  • antonym: specialist - a person who is an expert in a particular area
