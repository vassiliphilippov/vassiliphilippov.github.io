wash

verb
be accepted as valid or credible
Pronunciation: / wɑʃ / (AmE), / wɒʃ / (BrE)
(of an idea, argument, excuse, etc.) to be accepted as valid, credible, or satisfactory, especially after scrutiny or criticism.

Examples

That won't wash
His explanation simply won't wash with the committee.
I doubt her story will wash in a court of law.
That excuse might wash with your parents, but not with me.

Related Terms
