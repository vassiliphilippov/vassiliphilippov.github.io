Ibn Talal Hussein

proper noun
King of Jordan (1935–1999)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪbˈn tæˈlɑːl huːˈseɪn / (AmE), / ɪbˈn tæˈlɑːl hʊˈseɪn / (BrE)
(1935–1999) King of Jordan from 1952 to 1999, credited with maintaining stability in Jordan and pursuing peace with Israel.
