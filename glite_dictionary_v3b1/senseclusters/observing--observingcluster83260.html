observing

adjective
quick to notice; perceptive
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝːvɪŋ / (AmE), / əbˈzɜːvɪŋ / (BrE)
quick to notice things and perceptive; having or showing keen observation skills

Examples

An observing detective
An observing detective quickly spotted the hidden clue.
She is an observing child who always notices the smallest details.
The teacher valued students who were observing and attentive during experiments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perceptive - quick to notice or understand things
  • antonym: unobservant - not quick to notice things
