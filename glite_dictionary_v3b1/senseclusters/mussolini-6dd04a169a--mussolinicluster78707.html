Mussolini

proper noun
Italian fascist dictator (1883–1945)
Pronunciation: / ˌmusəˈlini / (AmE), / ˌmʌsəˈliːni / (BrE)
(1883–1945) Italian fascist dictator who ruled Italy as Prime Minister from 1922 to 1943.

Examples

Mussolini fascist leader
Mussolini came to power in Italy in 1922, establishing a fascist regime.
Many historians analyze the rise and fall of Mussolini when studying twentieth-century Europe.
The alliance between Mussolini and Hitler contributed to the outbreak of World War II.
