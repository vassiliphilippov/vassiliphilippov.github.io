indisputability

noun
quality of being undisputable
6.8 7.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.dɪs.pjuː.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /ɪn.dɪs.puː.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /ɪn.dɪs.pjuː.tə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)

Examples
