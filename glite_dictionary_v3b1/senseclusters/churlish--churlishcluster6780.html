churlish

adjective
rude or surly; bad-tempered
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːlɪʃ / (BrE)
rude, mean-spirited, and bad-tempered; having an ungracious or surly attitude or manner

Examples

A churlish reply
His churlish behavior made everyone feel uncomfortable at dinner.
It would be churlish to refuse such a generous invitation.
She gave a churlish reply that ended the conversation abruptly.
Despite her churlish tone, he remained calm and polite throughout the conversation.
It would be churlish to refuse their offer of help after everything they've done.
The critic's churlish remarks offended many in the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: gracious - courteous, kind, and pleasant
  • antonym: polite - having or showing good manners and respect
  • synonym: surly - bad-tempered and unfriendly
