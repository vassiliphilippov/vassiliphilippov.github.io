forthright

adjective, adverb
in a direct or straightforward way
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrθˌraɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɔːθˌraɪt / (BrE)
direct and honest; saying or doing something in a straightforward way, without hiding the truth

Examples

A forthright answer
She answered the question forthright, leaving no room for doubt.
The manager spoke forthright about the changes coming to the company.
When criticized, he always responds forthright, never avoiding tough topics.
He gave a forthright response, leaving no room for misunderstanding.
Her forthright comments during the meeting impressed everyone with their honesty.
It's refreshing to meet someone so forthright about their opinions.
