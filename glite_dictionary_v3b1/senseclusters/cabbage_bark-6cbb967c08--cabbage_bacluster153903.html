cabbage bark

noun
a toxic tropical tree with medicinal bark and seeds
4.2 9.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkæb.ɪdʒ bɑːrk/ /; / /ˈkæb.ə.dʒ bɑːrk/ (AmE), /ˈkæb.ɪdʒ bɑːk/ /; / /ˈkæb.ə.dʒ bɑːk/ (BrE)
