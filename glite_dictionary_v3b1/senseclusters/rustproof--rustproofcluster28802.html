rustproof

adjective
resistant to rust
4.2 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / Ààr åstÀåpruf / (AmE), / Ààr åstÀåpruÀêf / (BrE)
made so as to be resistant to rust; treated or manufactured to prevent rust formation

Examples

Rustproof steel frame
Many modern cars have rustproof bodies to withstand harsh winter conditions.
The manufacturer offers a ten-year warranty on all rustproof parts.
These garden tools are rustproof, so they can be left outside without worry.

Synonyms

    3.9 0.08/m
    rust-resistant is not easily damaged by rust and used on goods, while rustproof is made or treated to prevent rust forming.
  • antirust
    3.5 0.00/m
    antirust is designed to stop rust or corrosion on surfaces, while rustproof means a product is made to resist rust.
  • corrosion-resistant
    4.7 0.34/m
    corrosion-resistant can resist many kinds of corrosion on metals and in wet places, while rustproof focuses on preventing rust formation.
  • anticorrosion
    5.6 0.00/m
    anticorrosion measures protect surfaces and reduce rust or other damage over time, while rustproof describes something made specifically to resist rust.
