sick pay

noun
wages paid during sick leave
1.0 2.0
Pronunciation: /sɪk peɪ/ (AmE), /sɪk peɪ/ (BrE)
wages or salary paid to an employee while they are absent from work due to illness

Examples

Sick pay during absence
He was off work for a week and received sick pay during his absence.
If you don't get sick pay, taking time off due to illness can hurt your income.
The company offers sick pay to all full-time employees.
