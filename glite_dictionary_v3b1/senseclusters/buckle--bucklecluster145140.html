Synonyms

  • warp
    4.9 2/m
    warp is to bend or twist slowly from heat or damp, while buckle is to bend or collapse under heavy force.
  • give
    3.5 90/m
    give is to yield or fail under pressure in many situations, while buckle usually means bend or collapse from strong force.
  • crumple
    3.9 3/m
    crumple is to fold or fall in suddenly as if crushed, while buckle describes bending or deforming under pressure.
  • heave
    5.9 0.2/m
    heave is to bulge or lift a surface from heat or pressure, while buckle means bend or give way under load.
