afterparty

noun
party after event
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌpɑːrti / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌpɑːti / (BrE)
a social gathering that takes place after a main event or party, often for continued celebration

Examples

The afterparty event
After the awards ceremony, everyone headed to the exclusive afterparty downtown.
The afterparty lasted until the early hours of the morning, with music and dancing.
She met several celebrities at the afterparty following the film premiere.

Related Terms

  • antonym: preparty - a social gathering held before a main event or party
  • synonym: reception - a formal social gathering after a ceremony or event
