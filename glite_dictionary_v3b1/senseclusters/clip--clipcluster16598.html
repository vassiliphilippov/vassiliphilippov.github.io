clip

noun
short video or audio extract
1.9
Pronunciation: /klɪp/ (AmE), /klɪp/ (BrE)
a short extract of recorded video or audio taken from a longer work, often shared or played independently.

Examples

A video clip
A clip from the latest trailer circulated widely on fans' social feeds.
During the lecture, she showed a short clip to illustrate the director's editing style.
He saved the funny clip to his phone to send to friends later that evening.
