spiritist

noun
Spiritism believer
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪrɪtɪst / (AmE), / ˈspɪrɪtɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes in or practices Spiritism, especially communicating with spirits of the dead

Examples

A spiritist leader
The spiritist led a session where participants tried to contact their ancestors.
As a devoted spiritist, she attended weekly meetings to discuss messages from the spirit world.
Many people were curious about the beliefs of a spiritist in their community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medium - a person who claims to communicate with spirits
  • antonym: skeptic - a person who doubts or questions beliefs such as Spiritism
