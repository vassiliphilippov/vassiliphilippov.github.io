demand

noun
need or requirement
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmænd / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːnd / (BrE)
a need or requirement for something that must be satisfied or that requires effort, time, or resources

Examples

High demand on time
Hospitals are struggling to meet the demand for ventilators during the crisis.
The demand for skilled workers has increased in the past decade.
There is a growing demand for clean energy in modern cities.
Caring for young children makes huge demands on parents' patience and time.
The demands of the new job left her feeling exhausted by the end of the week.
There are increasing demands on students to perform well in school.

Related Terms

  • synonym: need - a necessity or requirement
  • degree lower: request - an act of asking for something politely or formally
  • synonym: requirement - something that is needed or demanded
  • antonym: supply - the amount of something available
