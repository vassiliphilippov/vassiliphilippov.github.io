incubate

verb
support development of startups or projects
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈɪn.kjə.beɪt/ /; / /ˈɪŋ.kjʊ.beɪt/ (AmE), /ˈɪŋ.kjʊ.beɪt/ /; / /ˈɪn.kjʊ.beɪt/ (BrE)
to support and help develop a new business, project, or idea within a specialised programme or environment that provides resources such as mentoring, funding, or workspace.

Examples

To incubate a startup
The accelerator aims to incubate several AI startups within its twelve-week mentorship program.
They incubated the social enterprise for six months, providing workspace and expert advice.
Local universities are incubating student projects to help turn ideas into marketable products.
