bentonitic

adjective
containing or relating to bentonite clay
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˌbɛn.təˈnɪt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɛn.təˈnɪt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to, containing, or characteristic of bentonite, a type of absorbent clay.

Examples

Bentonitic clay
