seethingly

adverb
in an agitated manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːðɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsiːðɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is full of intense agitation or activity, either physically or emotionally

Examples

The crowd moves seethingly
The crowd moved seethingly through the narrow streets during the parade.
Water bubbled seethingly in the pot as it reached a rolling boil.
Rumors spread seethingly among the students after the unexpected announcement.
He spoke seethingly about the injustice he had witnessed at work.
The crowd reacted seethingly to the controversial decision made by the referee.
She listened seethingly as her colleague took credit for her idea.
