Arabism

noun
Arabic-derived word
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈærəbɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈærəbɪzəm / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or feature borrowed from Arabic and used in another language

Examples

An Arabism in English
The English word 'safari' is an Arabism that originally came from Arabic.
Many scientific terms in Spanish are actually Arabisms introduced during the Middle Ages.
Linguists study Arabisms to understand the influence of Arabic on other languages.
