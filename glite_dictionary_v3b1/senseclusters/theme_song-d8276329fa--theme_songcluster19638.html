theme song

noun
melody identifying a performer or TV/radio program
Pronunciation: / θim sɔŋ / (AmE), / θiːm sɒŋ / (BrE)
a piece of music or melody that is closely associated with and identifies a particular show, series, or performer

Examples

The theme song plays
Every time I hear that theme song, I think of my childhood.
The band played their theme song at every concert to open the show.
The show's theme song became an instant classic and is still recognized today.
Every time the theme song played, the audience cheered in recognition.
She hummed the movie's theme song all the way home after watching it.
The composer won an award for creating the memorable theme song of the series.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soundtrack - the recorded music accompanying a film or show
