unfussed

adjective
not concerned
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfʌst / (AmE), / ʌnˈfʌst / (BrE)
not worried, bothered, or concerned about something

Examples

Unfussed by problems
He seemed completely unfussed by the loud noise outside the window.
Despite the tight deadline, she remained unfussed and worked steadily.
The children were unfussed about the rain and continued playing outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or anger
  • synonym: unconcerned - not worried or interested
  • antonym: worried - anxious or troubled about problems
