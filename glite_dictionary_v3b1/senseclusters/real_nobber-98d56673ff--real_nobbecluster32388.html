real nobber

noun
informal insult for a foolish or annoying person
Pronunciation: / ɹiəl ˈnɑbər / (AmE), / ɹɪəl ˈnɒbə / (BrE)
An informal insult for someone who behaves foolishly or in an irritating manner.

Examples

A real nobber person

He made a scene at the restaurant and looked like a real nobber.
She called him a real nobber after his silly prank.
