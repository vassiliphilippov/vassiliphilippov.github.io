gedanken

noun
thought experiment
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈdɑŋkən / (AmE), / ɡəˈdaŋkən / (BrE)
a thought experiment, especially one used in philosophy or physics to explore ideas theoretically

Examples

A gedanken experiment
The scientist proposed a gedanken to illustrate the paradox in quantum mechanics.
In his lecture, he used a famous gedanken to explain the concept of relativity.
Many philosophical arguments rely on a gedanken to test the limits of logic.

Related Terms
