Uria

noun
a genus of seabirds (murres)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊriə / (AmE), / ˈjʊəriə / (BrE)
A genus of seabirds in the auk family, comprising the murres, which are found in northern seas.

Examples

Uria seabirds
Researchers have observed a decline in the population of Uria due to changes in sea temperature.
The diet of Uria includes mainly fish and invertebrates caught by diving.
The thick-billed Uria can be found breeding on steep coastal cliffs.
