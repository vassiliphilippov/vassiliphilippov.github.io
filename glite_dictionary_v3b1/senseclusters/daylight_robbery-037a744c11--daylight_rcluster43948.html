daylight robbery

noun

Examples

Charging ten dollars for a cup of coffee is daylight robbery.

Synonyms

  • highway robbery
    5.8 0.01/m
    highway robbery means selling something at an unreasonably high price that feels unfair, while daylight robbery names that same shocking overcharge.
  • price gouging
    6.6 0.66/m
    price gouging means charging very high prices in emergencies to exploit buyers, while daylight robbery is a general phrase for unfairly high cost.
