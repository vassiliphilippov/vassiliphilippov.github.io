scallop

verb
bake food in sauce with breadcrumbs
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːl·əp / (AmE), / ˈskæləp / (BrE)
to bake food in a sauce, often with milk and breadcrumbs on top

Examples

To scallop potatoes
My grandmother used to scallop potatoes for Sunday dinner every week.
She scalloped the vegetables before serving them at the party.
You can scallop fish by baking it with cream and breadcrumbs on top.
