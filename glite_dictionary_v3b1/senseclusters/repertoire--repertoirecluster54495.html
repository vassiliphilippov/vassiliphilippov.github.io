repertoire

noun
entire range of skills or techniques
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛpərˌtwɑr / (AmE), / ˈrɛp.ə.twɑː / (BrE)
the entire range of skills, techniques, or devices available to a person or used in a particular field or activity

Examples

A repertoire of skills
As a musician, she has an impressive repertoire of classical and modern pieces.
Every chef brings a unique repertoire of techniques to their kitchen.
Negotiation is just one of the many skills in his repertoire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arsenal - a collection of resources or skills available for use
  • synonym: inventory - a complete list of items, such as skills or possessions
