goitrous

adjective
relating to goiter
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔɪtrəs / (AmE), / ˈɡɔɪtrəs / (BrE)
having or relating to a goiter, which is an enlarged thyroid gland in the neck

Examples

A goitrous neck
The patient presented with a goitrous neck that was visibly swollen.
A goitrous condition can result from iodine deficiency in the diet.
Doctors examined the goitrous swelling to determine the best treatment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal - not affected by goiter or swelling
  • synonym: thyroid - a gland in the neck that produces hormones
