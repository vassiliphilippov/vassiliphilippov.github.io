layer

noun
an abstract level or stratum
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪər / (AmE), / ˈleɪə / (BrE)
an abstract level or stratum in a structure, system, or meaning, often used to express complexity or depth

Examples

A layer of meaning
Every relationship has its own unique layer of trust and understanding.
Sometimes you need to peel back each layer to find the true meaning of the poem.
The novel's plot has a surprising layer of political symbolism beneath the surface story.
