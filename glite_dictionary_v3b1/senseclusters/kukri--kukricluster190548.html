kukri

noun
Asian snake species
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːkri / (AmE), / ˈkuːkri / (BrE)
a small Asian snake of the genus Oligodon, known for its curved rear teeth resembling a kukri knife

Examples

Kukri snake
The researcher photographed a small kukri slithering between the rice stalks at dawn.
Farmers in Southeast Asia sometimes encounter a kukri while working in the fields.
The kukri is named for its teeth, which are shaped like the traditional Nepali knife.

Related Terms
