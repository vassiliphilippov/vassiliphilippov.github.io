sawfish

noun
large ray with a saw-like snout
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːfɪʃ / (BrE)
A large, primitive ray with a long, flattened snout edged with sharp teeth, resembling a saw, found in warm coastal waters.

Examples

A sawfish with snout
A sawfish can be easily identified by its long, saw-like snout.
