santy

noun
sailor's work song
Pronunciation: / ˈsænti / (AmE), / ˈsænti / (BrE)
a sailor's work song, especially one sung to coordinate group labor on a ship

Examples

A santy song
The sailors began a santy to keep their movements in sync as they raised the anchor.
Each santy had a unique rhythm that matched the task the crew was performing.
He learned an old santy from his grandfather, who had worked on ships as a young man.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sea song - a song about the sea or sailors
  • synonym: shanty - a traditional sailor's work song
