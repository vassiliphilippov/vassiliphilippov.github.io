natural resin

noun
substance exuded by plants, used in products
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnætʃrəl ˈrɛzən / (AmE), / ˈnatʃrəl ˈrɛzɪn / (BrE)
a sticky substance exuded by certain plants, used in varnishes, adhesives, and other products

Examples

Natural resin from trees
Amber is a fossilized form of natural resin from ancient trees.
Some varnishes are made with natural resin instead of synthetic alternatives.
The artist used natural resin to create a glossy finish on the painting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: synthetic resin - an artificial resin produced by chemical synthesis
