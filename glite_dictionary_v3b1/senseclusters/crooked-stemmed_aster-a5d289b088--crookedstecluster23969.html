crooked-stemmed aster

noun
North American aster species with crooked stems

Pronunciation: /ˈkrʊk.ɪd.stemd ˈæs.tər/ /; / /ˈkrʊkt.stemd ˈæs.tər/ (AmE), /ˈkrʊk.ɪd.stemd ˈæs.tə/ /; / /ˈkrʊkt.stemd ˈæs.tə/ (BrE)
