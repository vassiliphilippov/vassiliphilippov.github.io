document

verb
to support with references or evidence
3.6 2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑː.kjə.mənt /; / ˈdɑːkjəˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈdɒk.jʊ.mənt /; / ˈdɒkjʊmənt / (BrE)
to record or provide evidence of something, especially in writing or with supporting materials

Examples

To document events
