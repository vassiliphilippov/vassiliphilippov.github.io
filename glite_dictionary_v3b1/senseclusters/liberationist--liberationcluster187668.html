liberationist

adjective
supporting social freedom
Pronunciation: / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
supporting or relating to movements that seek political or social freedom and equality

Examples

Liberationist ideas
The liberationist movement gained momentum as more people demanded equal rights.
Her liberationist views influenced many of her friends to join the protest.
A liberationist approach to education encourages critical thinking and social change.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - opposed to rapid change or reform
  • synonym: progressive - favoring social reform or new ideas
