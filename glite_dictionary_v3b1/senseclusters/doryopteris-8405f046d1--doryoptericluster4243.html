Doryopteris

noun
genus of tropical tufted ferns
Pronunciation: / dɔriˈɑptərɪs / (AmE), / dɒrɪˈɒptərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of small to medium tropical tufted ferns, sometimes placed in the family Adiantaceae.
