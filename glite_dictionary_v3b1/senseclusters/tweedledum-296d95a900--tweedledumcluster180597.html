Tweedledum

proper noun
fictional twin character
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌtwiːdəlˈdʌm / (AmE), / ˌtwiːdəlˈdʌm / (BrE)
a fictional character, usually paired with Tweedledee, representing one of two people who are very similar or indistinguishable

Examples

Tweedledum and Tweedledee
In the story, Tweedledum and Tweedledee are always arguing but never truly different.
The two politicians were compared to Tweedledum and Tweedledee because their views were almost identical.
For the school play, he dressed up as Tweedledum with his friend as Tweedledee.
