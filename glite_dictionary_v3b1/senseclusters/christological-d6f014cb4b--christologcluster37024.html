Christological

adjective
relating to Christology (study of Christ)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkraɪstəˈlɑdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌkrɪstəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or concerned with Christology, the branch of theology dealing with the nature and work of Jesus Christ.

Examples

Christological study
Her research focused on the Christological themes in early Christian writings.
Many theological disputes in the early church were fundamentally Christological in nature.
The council debated several Christological issues concerning the divine and human nature of Jesus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: theological - relating to the study of the nature of God and religious belief
