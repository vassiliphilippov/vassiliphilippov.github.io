praemunire

noun
historical legal offense
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpriː.mjuː.nər/ /; / /ˈpriː.mjʊ.nər/ (AmE), /ˈpriː.mjuː.nɪə/ /; / /ˈpriː.mjʊ.nɪə/ (BrE)
