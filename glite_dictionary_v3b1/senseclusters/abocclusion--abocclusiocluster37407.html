abocclusion

noun
upper and lower teeth do not touch when biting
Pronunciation: / ˌæbəˈkluʒən / (AmE), / ˌabəˈkluːʒ(ə)n / (BrE)
a dental condition in which the upper teeth do not make contact with the lower teeth when the jaws are closed or biting

Examples

Abocclusion problem
Because of his abocclusion, chewing certain foods was difficult and uncomfortable.
Correcting abocclusion often requires orthodontic treatment to realign the bite.
The orthodontist diagnosed her with abocclusion, explaining why her teeth did not come together properly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: malocclusion - improper alignment of the teeth and jaws
