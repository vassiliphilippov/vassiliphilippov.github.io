psyched up

adjective
mentally/emotionally motivated and ready
Pronunciation: / saɪkt ʌp / (AmE), / saɪkt ʌp / (BrE)
mentally or emotionally prepared and motivated for an activity, event, or challenge

Examples

To be psyched up
Before going on stage, she always gets psyched up with a quick pep talk.
Everyone in the office was psyched up for the big product launch.
The athletes were psyched up and ready to give their best performance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: discouraged - lacking motivation or enthusiasm
  • synonym: motivated - feeling eager to do something or achieve a goal
