stinkhorn

noun
a foul-smelling brownish fungus
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪŋkˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈstɪŋkˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a foul-smelling fungus of the order Phallales, usually with a brownish cap, known for attracting insects with its odor

Examples

A stinkhorn fungus
During the hike, we spotted a cluster of stinkhorns near a decaying log.
Gardeners often complain about the strong odor given off by a stinkhorn in bloom.
The biologist carefully collected samples of the stinkhorn from the forest floor.
