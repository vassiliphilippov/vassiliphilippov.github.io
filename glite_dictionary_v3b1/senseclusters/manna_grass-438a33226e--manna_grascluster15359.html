manna grass

noun
sweet-flavored grass of the Glyceria genus
Pronunciation: / ˈmænə ˌɡræs / (AmE), / ˈmænə ˌɡrɑːs / (BrE)
any of several moisture-loving grasses of the genus Glyceria, known for their sweet flavor or fragrance

Examples

Manna grass plant
Manna grass often grows in marshy meadows and is prized for its sweet aroma.
Cattle are known to graze on manna grass during the spring when it is most plentiful.
The botanist identified several species of manna grass along the riverbank.
