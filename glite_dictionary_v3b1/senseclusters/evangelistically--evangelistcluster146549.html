evangelistically

adverb
with great zeal
8.4
Pronunciation: /ɪ.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (AmE), /ɪ.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ /; / /iː.væn.dʒə.ˈlɪs.tɪ.kəl.iː/ (BrE)

She spoke evangelistically about the benefits of healthy eating to her friends.
