evangelistically

adverb
with great zeal
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ɪˌvæn.dʒəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is enthusiastic or zealous about promoting a cause or belief

Examples

To argue evangelistically
He argued evangelistically for the new policy, hoping to convince everyone in the room.
The volunteers worked evangelistically to spread awareness about the environmental campaign.
She spoke evangelistically about the benefits of healthy eating to her friends.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifferently - without interest or enthusiasm
  • synonym: passionately - with strong feelings or enthusiasm
  • synonym: zealously - with great energy or enthusiasm
