reclamation

noun
making land usable again
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making land that was previously unusable suitable for habitation or cultivation

Examples

Land reclamation project
Coastal reclamation has provided new space for city development.
Efforts at reclamation have dramatically increased the region's arable land.
The government invested heavily in reclamation projects to turn marshes into farmland.

Related Terms

  • synonym: restoration - the act of returning something to a former condition
