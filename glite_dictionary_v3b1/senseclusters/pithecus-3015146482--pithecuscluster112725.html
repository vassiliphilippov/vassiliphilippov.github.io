Pithecus

noun
ape-related combining form
Pronunciation: / pɪˈθiːkəs / (AmE), / pɪˈθiːkəs / (BrE)
a combining form used in scientific names to indicate an ape or ape-like characteristics

Examples

Pithecus in species names
The genus name Dryopithecus uses the combining form Pithecus to indicate its ape-like features.
In taxonomy, Pithecus is often found in the names of extinct primate genera.
Scientists coined several genus names with Pithecus to classify ancient apes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: simian - relating to or resembling apes or monkeys
