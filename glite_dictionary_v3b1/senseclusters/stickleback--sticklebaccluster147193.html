stickleback

noun
small spiny-backed fish
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkəlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈstɪkəlbak / (BrE)
a small, spiny-backed fish of northern fresh and coastal waters, commonly studied for its behavior and biology

Examples

Male stickleback fish
