ferruginous

adjective
iron-containing; rust-colored
Pronunciation: / fəˈruːdʒənəs / (AmE), / fəˈruːdʒɪnəs / (BrE)
containing iron or having a reddish-brown color similar to rust

Examples

Ferruginous red color
The ferruginous rocks gave the landscape a striking reddish hue.
Birds with ferruginous feathers are often found in dry, open habitats.
The river ran through ferruginous soil, turning the water a rusty color after heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: iron-rich - containing a high amount of iron
  • synonym: rusty - having the color or characteristics of rust
