nonconservative

adjective
favoring progressive change
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkənˈsɜːrvətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkənˈsɜːvətɪv / (BrE)
not supporting or characteristic of conservative political views; favoring change, reform, or progressive policies

Examples

Nonconservative views
The candidate's nonconservative views attracted younger voters seeking social reform.
Many nonconservative policies focus on expanding access to education and healthcare.
Her nonconservative stance on environmental issues set her apart from other politicians in her party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conservative - holding to traditional attitudes and values; resistant to change
  • synonym: liberal - open to new behavior or opinions and willing to discard traditional values
  • synonym: progressive - favoring or implementing social reform or new ideas
