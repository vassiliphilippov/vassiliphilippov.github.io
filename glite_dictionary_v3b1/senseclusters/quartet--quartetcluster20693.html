quartet

noun
group of four people or things
5.6
Pronunciation: / kwɔrˈtɛt / (AmE), / kwɔːˈtɛt / (BrE)
A group or set of four people or things, especially considered as a unit.

Examples

A quartet of friends
A quartet of scientists contributed to the groundbreaking research project.
He arranged his paintings into a quartet to be displayed together on one wall.
We finished the dinner with a delicious quartet of desserts to share.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: trio - group of three people or things
