Revolutionary War

proper noun
American War of Independence (1775–1783)
3.7
Pronunciation: / ɹɪˌvoʊluˈʃəˌnɛɹi wɔɹ / (AmE), / ɹɪˌvɒljuˈʃənəri wɔː / (BrE)
the war of 1775–1783 in which the thirteen American colonies fought for independence from Britain

Examples

The Revolutionary War marked a turning point in world history and the rise of the United States as a nation.
