humpback

noun
abnormal backward curve of the spine
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmpˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈhʌmpˌbak / (BrE)
an abnormal backward curvature of the spine, causing a humped or rounded back

Examples

Severe humpback condition
A severe humpback can affect breathing and movement in some cases.
Medical treatment can help manage the pain associated with a humpback.
The old man developed a noticeable humpback due to years of poor posture.
