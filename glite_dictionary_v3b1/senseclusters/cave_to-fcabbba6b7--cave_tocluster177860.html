cave to

verb
yield under pressure or demands
9.9
Pronunciation: / keɪv tu / (AmE), / keɪv tʊ / (BrE)
to surrender or give in to pressure or demands

Examples

To cave to pressure
Despite the criticism, she refused to cave to public pressure and maintained her decision.
Politicians often cave to popular opinion during election campaigns.
The company finally caved to employee demands after weeks of protests.
