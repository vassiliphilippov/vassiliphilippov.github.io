laminal

adjective
arranged in thin layers
Pronunciation: / ˈlæmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈlæmɪnəl / (BrE)
composed of or arranged in thin layers (laminae)

Examples

A laminal layer
Geologists study the laminal patterns found in sedimentary formations.
Some types of tissue exhibit a distinctly laminal organization when viewed under a microscope.
The rock has a laminal structure, making it easy to split along the layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: laminar - in the form of thin plates or layers
  • synonym: laminate - to cover with or form into thin layers
