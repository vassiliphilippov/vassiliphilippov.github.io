appealingly

adverb
in a pleasing or attractive way
3.4
Pronunciation: / əˈpiːlɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpiːlɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that attracts interest, pleasure, or admiration; pleasantly or attractively.

Examples

Smiles appealingly
She smiled appealingly at the audience, winning their hearts instantly.
The food was presented appealingly on the plate, making it look delicious.
The room was decorated appealingly for the special occasion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attractively - in a way that is pleasing to look at or attracts interest
  • antonym: unappealingly - in a way that is not attractive or pleasant
