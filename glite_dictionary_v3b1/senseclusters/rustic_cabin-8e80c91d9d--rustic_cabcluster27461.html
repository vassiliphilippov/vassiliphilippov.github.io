rustic cabin

noun
simple rural wooden dwelling
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌstɪk ˈkæbɪn / (AmE), / ˈrʌstɪk ˈkabɪn / (BrE)
A simple, rural dwelling, typically made of wood and characterized by a natural, unrefined appearance.

Examples

A rustic cabin in woods
The rustic cabin had a stone fireplace and handmade furniture, creating a cozy atmosphere.
They dreamed of retiring to a rustic cabin surrounded by nature.
We spent the weekend relaxing in a rustic cabin deep in the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cabin - a small simple house, usually made of wood, in a rural or wild area
