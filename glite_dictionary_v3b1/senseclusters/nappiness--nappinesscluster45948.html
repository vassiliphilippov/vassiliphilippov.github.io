nappiness

noun
tightly curled hair
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnæpinəs / (AmE), / ˈnæpinəs / (BrE)
the quality or degree of having tightly curled, coarse, or woolly hair texture

Examples

Nappiness of hair
The nappiness of her hair made it difficult to comb without special products.
Some people embrace the natural nappiness of their hair as a sign of pride.
He learned techniques to manage the nappiness and keep his hair healthy.
