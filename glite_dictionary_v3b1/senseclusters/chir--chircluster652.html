chir

verb
to make trilling sound
9.3
Pronunciation: / tʃɜr / (AmE), / tʃɜː / (BrE)
to make a trilling or vibrating sound, especially as some insects or birds do

Examples

To chir loudly
The crickets chirred loudly in the summer night.
As dusk fell, cicadas chirred in the trees, filling the air with their song.
A grasshopper chirred softly from the tall grass near the fence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chirp - to make a short, sharp sound like a small bird or insect
  • antonym: silence - the absence of sound
  • synonym: trill - to produce a repeated, high-pitched sound
