swarmy

adjective
full of swarms
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːrmi / (AmE), / ˈswɔːmi / (BrE)
full of swarms of insects or other small creatures; teeming or swarming with life.

Examples

Swarmy pond
The pond was so swarmy with gnats that we had to cover our faces.
After the rain, the garden became swarmy with tiny flying insects.
We avoided the swarmy field at dusk because of all the mosquitoes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled
  • synonym: swarming - filled with large numbers of moving creatures
  • synonym: teeming - full of people, animals, or things; overflowing
