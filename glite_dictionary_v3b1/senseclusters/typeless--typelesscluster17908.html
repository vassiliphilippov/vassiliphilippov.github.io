typeless

adjective
without distinctive style
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪpləs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪpləs / (BrE)
having no distinctive or characteristic style, features, or qualities; bland or generic

Examples

Typeless building
The office building was so typeless that it could have been anywhere in the world.
She found the restaurant's decor typeless and uninspiring, with nothing to set it apart.
Many modern products are criticized for being typeless and lacking originality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: characterless - lacking distinctive or interesting qualities
  • antonym: distinctive - having a quality or characteristic that makes something different and easily noticed
  • synonym: generic - lacking distinctive features or characteristics
