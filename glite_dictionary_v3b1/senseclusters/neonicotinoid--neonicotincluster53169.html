neonicotinoid

adjective
nicotine-like insecticide
Pronunciation: / ˌniːoʊˈnɪkətɪnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈnɪkətɪnɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to insecticides that are chemically similar to nicotine and used to control pests.

Examples

Neonicotinoid pesticide use
Farmers often use neonicotinoid sprays to protect crops from insect damage.
The neonicotinoid chemicals have raised concerns about their impact on pollinators.
Some countries have restricted neonicotinoid products to help preserve bee populations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecticidal - having the quality of killing insects
  • antonym: organic - produced without synthetic chemicals or pesticides
