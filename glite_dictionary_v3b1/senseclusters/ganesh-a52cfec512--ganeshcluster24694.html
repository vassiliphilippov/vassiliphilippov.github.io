Ganesh

proper noun
Hindu god of wisdom and remover of obstacles
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈneɪʃ / (AmE), / ɡəˈneɪʃ / (BrE)
a Hindu god of wisdom and remover of obstacles, depicted with the head of an elephant

Examples

Ganesh festival
During the festival, people celebrate Ganesh with music, dancing, and elaborate decorations.
Many devotees pray to Ganesh before starting a new venture, hoping for success and the removal of obstacles.
The statue of Ganesh at the entrance is adorned with fresh flowers every morning.
