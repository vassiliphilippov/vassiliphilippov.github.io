pimple

verb
to form small bumps
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪmpəl / (BrE)
to develop or cause to develop small raised bumps or spots, especially on a surface or skin

Examples

To pimple the surface
The humid weather started pimpling the paint on the old wall.
After the reaction, the metal surface was badly pimpled and needed to be replaced.
If you leave the dough uncovered, it may begin pimpling as it dries out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blemish - to mark or spoil the surface of something
  • antonym: smooth - to make or become free from bumps or roughness
