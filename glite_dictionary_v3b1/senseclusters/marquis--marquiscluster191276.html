marquis

noun
nobleman above a count, below a duke
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːrk(w)ɪs /; / ˈmɑr.kwəs /; / ˈmɑːrkwəs / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkwɪs /; / ˈmɑː.kwɪs / (BrE)
A nobleman ranking below a duke and above an earl or count; in certain European countries, especially France.
