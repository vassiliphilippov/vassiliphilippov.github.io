nut pine

noun
pine tree with edible seeds
Pronunciation: / nʌt paɪn / (AmE), / nʌt paɪn / (BrE)
A pine tree that produces edible nutlike seeds, especially certain species of pinon.

Examples

A nut pine tree
A grove of nut pine provides food for both wildlife and humans.
Many people harvest seeds from the nut pine during late summer.
The nut pine is valued for its delicious, nutritious seeds.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: pine - a coniferous tree of the genus Pinus
  • synonym: pinon - a type of pine tree with edible seeds
