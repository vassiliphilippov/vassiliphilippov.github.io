ill repute

noun
bad reputation
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (AmE), / ɪl rɪˈpjuːt / (BrE)
The state of having a bad reputation; the condition of being regarded unfavorably by others.

Examples

Ill repute in town
His association with criminals earned him a reputation for ill repute.
Many people avoid businesses of ill repute in the city center.
The scandal brought ill repute to the politician’s name.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good repute - the state of having a good reputation
  • synonym: notoriety - the state of being famous for something bad
