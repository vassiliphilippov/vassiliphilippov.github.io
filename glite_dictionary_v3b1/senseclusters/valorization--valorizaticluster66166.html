valorization

noun
praising or honoring
9.1
Pronunciation: /væl.ə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /væl.ə.rə.ˈzeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the act of praising, glorifying, or giving increased value or honor to someone or something

Examples

Valorization of heroes
The documentary contributed to the valorization of local artists who had been ignored for decades.
Some critics argue that the valorization of historical leaders can overlook their flaws.
Educational reforms led to the valorization of vocational training alongside academic studies.

  • synonym: glorification - the act of praising or honoring greatly
