stall

noun
market booth for displaying goods
5.2
Pronunciation: / stɑl / (AmE), / stɔːl / (BrE)
a small booth or stand in a market or fair where goods are displayed for sale

Examples

Market stall
Each stall at the craft fair sold something unique and handmade.
She bought fresh fruit from the stall at the farmer's market.
The food stall was busy all afternoon serving snacks to hungry customers.
