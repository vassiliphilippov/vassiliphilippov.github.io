furnace

noun
enclosed chamber that produces heat
Pronunciation: / ˈfɜrnɪs / (AmE), / ˈfɜːnɪs / (BrE)
an enclosed structure or chamber in which heat is produced for heating buildings, destroying waste, or processing materials such as metals

Examples

A furnace chamber
The metal was melted inside a blast furnace at the factory.
The old mansion was heated by a coal-fired furnace in the basement.
They installed a new energy-efficient furnace to reduce their heating bills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for cooking or heating
