gnu

noun
large African antelope
Pronunciation: / nuː / (AmE), / ɡnjuː / (BrE)
a large African antelope with a head resembling an ox, backward-curving horns, and a long tufted tail

Examples

A gnu antelope
A herd of gnus crossed the savanna at dusk, heading for water.
The gnu is often called a wildebeest in southern Africa.
The long, shaggy mane of the gnu helps it stand out among other antelopes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wildebeest - another name for the gnu
