sublet

verb
lease out rented property to another
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈlɛt / (AmE), / sʌbˈlɛt / (BrE)
to lease or rent all or part of a leased or rented property to another person

Examples

To sublet an apartment
After losing his job, he decided to sublet part of his office space.
She sublet the extra bedroom to a college student for the summer.
We plan to sublet our apartment while we travel abroad this year.
