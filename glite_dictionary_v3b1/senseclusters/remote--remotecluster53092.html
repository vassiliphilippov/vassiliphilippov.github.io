remote

adjective
unlikely to happen
Pronunciation: / rɪˈmoʊt / (AmE), / rɪˈməʊt / (BrE)
(of a chance or possibility) very unlikely to happen or occur.

Examples

Remote chance
His chances of winning the lottery are extremely remote.
It’s a remote risk, but we should be prepared nonetheless.
There is only a remote possibility of snow this weekend.
