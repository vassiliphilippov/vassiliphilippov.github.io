galloper

noun
carousel horse ride
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæləpər / (AmE), / ˈɡæləpə / (BrE)
a fairground or amusement-park ride with horse-shaped seats that move up and down as the platform rotates, or one of the horses on that ride

Examples

A galloper ride
The children queued eagerly to ride the shiny new galloper at the summer fair.
Each galloper on the ride was painted in bright colors and decorated with gold trim.
She chose her favorite galloper and waved to her parents as the ride began to spin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carousel - a rotating amusement ride with seats, often shaped like animals
  • synonym: merry-go-round - a rotating amusement ride with seats, usually for children
