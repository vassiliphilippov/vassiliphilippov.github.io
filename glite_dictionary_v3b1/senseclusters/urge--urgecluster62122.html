urge

noun
strong impulse or desire
Pronunciation: / ɝdʒ / (AmE), / ɜːdʒ / (BrE)
a strong desire or impulse to do something

Examples

A sudden urge
Despite her urge to laugh, she managed to keep a straight face.
He felt a sudden urge to call his old friend after so many years.
The urge to travel became stronger after months of staying at home.
He felt an urge to check his phone even though there were no notifications.
Resisting the urge to eat the last cookie was harder than he expected.
She suddenly had the urge to travel the world after graduation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aversion - a strong dislike or disinclination
  • synonym: craving - a powerful desire for something
  • synonym: impulse - a sudden strong desire to act
  • antonym: resistance - an effort to oppose or withstand a desire
