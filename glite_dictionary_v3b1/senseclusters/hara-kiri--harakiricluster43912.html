hara-kiri

noun
ritual suicide by samurai in Japan
4.4 9.6
Pronunciation: /hɑː.rə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ /; / /hær.ə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ /; / /hə.rə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ (AmE), /hɑː.rə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ /; / /hær.ə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ /; / /hə.rə.ˈkɪ.r.iː/ (BrE)
A ritual suicide by self-disembowelment with a sword, traditionally practiced by samurai in Japan to restore or protect honor.

Examples

Japanese hara-kiri ritual
He referred to his resignation as political hara-kiri, suggesting the end of his career.
Some historians have written extensively about the tradition of hara-kiri in feudal Japan.
The disgraced samurai committed hara-kiri to preserve his family's honor.
