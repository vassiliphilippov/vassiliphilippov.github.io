nun's habit

noun
the traditional outer garment worn by nuns
Pronunciation: / nʌnz ˈhæbɪt / (AmE), / nʌnz ˈhæbɪt / (BrE)
the distinctive long, loose outer garment worn by nuns, often as part of their religious uniform

Examples

A nun's habit
Several nun's habits were hanging neatly in the laundry room of the convent.
Sister Maria wore her nun's habit with quiet dignity every day.
The young woman admired the simplicity of the nun's habit as she entered the chapel.
