accoutrement

noun
additional item of clothing or equipment

An additional item of dress or equipment, typically worn or carried for a particular activity or purpose.

Examples

Fashion accoutrement
She added every possible accoutrement to her costume, including jewelry and scarves.
The knight's armor was impressive, but his feathered accoutrement drew the most attention.
The travelers checked their suitcases for any forgotten accoutrement before heading to the airport.

Synonyms

  • accouterment
    9.9 0.23/m
    accouterment is a less common variant spelling seen in some texts, while accoutrement is the standard form learners meet today.
  • accessory
    3.9 5.63/m
    accessory is a fashion item like a scarf or bag that makes an outfit look better, while accoutrement includes extra gear for a particular activity.
  • appurtenance
    9.9 0.17/m
    appurtenance is a formal word for an added item that helps a place or tool, while accoutrement mainly names worn or carried gear.
