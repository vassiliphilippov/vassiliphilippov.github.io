smeller

noun
odor-detecting device
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˈsmɛlə / (BrE)
a device or instrument used to detect or analyze odors or gases

Examples

Gas smeller
The laboratory installed a new smeller to monitor for dangerous gas leaks.
Technicians rely on a smeller to identify the presence of specific chemicals in the air.
A portable smeller can help firefighters detect toxic fumes during emergencies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detector - a device that discovers or identifies the presence of something
  • synonym: sensor - a device that senses and responds to a physical stimulus
