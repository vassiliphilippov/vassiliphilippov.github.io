forced-birther

noun
person strongly opposed to abortion (slang, derogatory)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrst ˌbɜrðər / (AmE), / ˈfɔːst ˌbɜːðə / (BrE)
A derogatory term for someone who opposes abortion, especially someone who seeks to prevent others from accessing abortion services.

Examples

A forced-birther activist
Many activists objected to being called a forced-birther during the heated discussion.
She was labeled a forced-birther by her opponents after expressing her views on abortion.
The term forced-birther is often used in political debates about reproductive rights.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pro-choice - supporter of a woman's right to choose abortion
  • synonym: pro-life - someone who opposes abortion; anti-abortion advocate
