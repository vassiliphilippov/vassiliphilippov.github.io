merchant vessel

noun
commercial ship; non-naval vessel
5.1 0.15/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…ùÀê.t É…ônt Ààv…õs.…ôl / (AmE), / Ààm…úÀê.t É…ônt Ààv…õs.…ôl / (BrE)
A ship that is used for commercial transport of goods or passengers, and is not part of a country's navy.

Examples

A merchant vessel ship
During the war, several merchant vessels were sunk by submarines.
International law protects merchant vessels sailing in neutral waters.
The port was crowded with merchant vessels waiting to unload their cargo.

Synonyms

  • merchantman
