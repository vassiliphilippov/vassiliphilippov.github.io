family Ardeidae

noun
bird family: herons, egrets, bitterns
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ɑːrˈdiːɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ɑːˈdiːɪdiː / (BrE)
