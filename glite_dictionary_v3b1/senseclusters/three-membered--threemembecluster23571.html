three-membered

adjective
having a three-atom ring (chemistry)
4.4 6.6
Pronunciation: /θriː ˈmem.bərd/ (AmE), /θriː ˈmem.bəd/ (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure composed of three atoms or groups.

Examples

Three-membered ring
