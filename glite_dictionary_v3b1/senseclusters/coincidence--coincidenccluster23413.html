coincidence

noun
occupying the same position in space
Pronunciation: / koʊˈɪnsɪdəns / (AmE), / kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns / (BrE)
the state or fact of occupying exactly the same position or area in space

Examples

Coincidence of lines
A perfect coincidence between the grid lines and the mark confirmed the alignment.
In optics, the coincidence of two images allows for precise measurements.
The experiment depends on the exact coincidence of the beams at the detector.
