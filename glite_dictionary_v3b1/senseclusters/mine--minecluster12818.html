mine

verb
place explosive mines in an area
2.9
Pronunciation: / maɪn / (AmE), / maɪn / (BrE)
to place explosive mines, such as landmines or naval mines, in an area for military purposes

Examples

To mine a field
After discovering the new border area was mined, the convoy had to change its route.
During the war, the army mined the main roads leading to the city.
Engineers were sent to mine the coastline before the enemy fleet approached.
