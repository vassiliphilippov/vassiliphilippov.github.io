Papio ursinus

noun
species of baboon in southern and eastern Africa
Pronunciation: / ˌpeɪpi.oʊ ɜːrˈsaɪnəs / (AmE), / ˌpæp.i.əʊ ɜːˈsaɪ.nəs / (BrE)
a greyish species of baboon found in southern and eastern Africa, also known as the chacma baboon

Examples

The Papio ursinus is the largest species of baboon in Africa.
