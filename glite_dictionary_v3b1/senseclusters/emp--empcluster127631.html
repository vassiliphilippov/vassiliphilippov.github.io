emp

verb
to remove temporarily
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.em.piː/ /; / /emp/ (AmE), /ˈiː.em.piː/ /; / /emp/ (BrE)
to temporarily and forcefully remove someone from a group or activity, especially in online contexts.

Examples

To emp a user
If you keep spamming, the admin will emp you from the server for a while.
The moderator decided to emp several users who were breaking the rules.
After a heated argument, she was emped from the online group for a day.
