wand

noun
slender rod used by a magician or diviner
Pronunciation: / wɑnd / (AmE), / wɒnd / (BrE)
a slender stick or rod, especially one used by a magician or a water diviner to produce magical effects or locate water

Examples

Magic wand
She bought a crystal wand for her collection of mystical objects.
The magician waved his wand and made the rabbit disappear.
The water diviner used a special wand to search for underground springs.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: scepter - an ornamented staff carried by rulers as a symbol of sovereignty
  • synonym: staff - a long stick or pole used as a support or symbol of authority
