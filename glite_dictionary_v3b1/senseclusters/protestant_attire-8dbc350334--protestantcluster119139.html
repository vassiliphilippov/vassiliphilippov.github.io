Protestant attire

noun
clothing associated with Protestantism
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊ.t̬ə.stənt əˈtaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈprɒ.tɪ.stənt əˈtaɪə / (BrE)
clothing typically worn by members of Protestant churches, often characterized by simplicity or features reflecting Protestant beliefs or culture.

Examples

Protestant attire style
Her simple black dress was considered appropriate Protestant attire for the occasion.
Historical paintings often depict Puritans in distinctive Protestant attire.
The missionaries wore traditional Protestant attire during the church service.
