Lallans

noun
Scots dialect of the Scottish Lowlands
Pronunciation: / ˈlælənz / (AmE), / ˈlalənz / (BrE)
the variety of Scots spoken in the Lowlands of Scotland; also used to refer to standard written Scots

Examples

Lallans language
He wrote his poetry in Lallans to authentically capture the voice of the Scottish Lowlands.
Many words in Lallans differ from both Standard English and Highland Scots.
The novel's dialogue is written entirely in Lallans, adding to its regional charm.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: English - the language from which Lallans is partly derived
  • synonym: Scots - the Germanic language variety spoken in Lowland Scotland
