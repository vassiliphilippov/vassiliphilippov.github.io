scum

noun
film of impurities on liquid surface
2.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /skʌm/ (AmE), /skʌm/ (BrE)
a layer of dirt, impurities, or floating matter, such as foam or algae, that forms on the surface of a liquid

Examples

Scum on water
After the storm, a thick layer of scum covered the surface of the pond.
Algae can quickly create green scum that floats on stagnant water.
Be sure to remove the scum from the top of the boiling soup.
