malefic

adjective
having a harmful influence
7.9
Pronunciation: / məˈlɛfɪk / (AmE), / məˈlɛfɪk / (BrE)
Having or exerting a harmful or malignant influence.

Examples

A malefic force
A malefic force seemed to linger over the abandoned house, discouraging visitors.
Ancient astrologers warned that certain planets brought a malefic influence on people's lives.
The villain seemed surrounded by a malefic aura that unsettled everyone around him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: benevolent - well meaning and kindly
  • synonym: malevolent - having or showing a wish to do evil to others
