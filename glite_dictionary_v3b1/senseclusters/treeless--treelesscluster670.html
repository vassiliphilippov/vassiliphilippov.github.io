treeless

adjective
having no trees
2.7 0.65/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàtriÀêl…ôs / (AmE), / ÀàtriÀêl…ôs / (BrE)
(of an area or landscape) having no trees; not covered with trees.

Examples

A treeless landscape
A treeless landscape can be the result of harsh climatic conditions.
The vast treeless plains stretched out to the horizon.
They built their cabin on a treeless hilltop for a panoramic view.

Synonyms

  • unwooded
    4.3 0.01/m
    unwooded is land without trees and used as a neutral description, while treeless often describes wide open areas lacking trees.
  • untimbered
    8.2 0.00/m
    untimbered sounds older or more formal and is rare in speech, while treeless is simple and common in everyday talk.
  • nonforested
    5.0 0.01/m
    nonforested is a formal, technical term used in reports or maps, while treeless is general everyday language about landscapes.

Related Terms
