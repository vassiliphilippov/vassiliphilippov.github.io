efflorescence

noun
red skin eruption (medical)
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɛfloʊˈrɛsəns / (AmE), / ˌɛfləˈrɛsəns / (BrE)
A red eruption or rash on the skin, especially as a symptom of a disease.

Examples

Skin efflorescence
A measles infection is often accompanied by a characteristic efflorescence on the skin.
The doctor examined the efflorescence on her arm and asked about other symptoms.
The sudden appearance of a efflorescence can indicate an allergic reaction.
