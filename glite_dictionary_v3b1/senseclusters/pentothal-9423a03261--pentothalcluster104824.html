Pentothal

noun
brand of general anesthetic (thiopental)
8.7
Pronunciation: /pen.ˈtoʊ.θæl/ /; / /pen.ˈtoʊ.θəl/ (AmE), /pen.ˈtəʊ.θæl/ /; / /pen.ˈtəʊ.θəl/ (BrE)
A brand name for thiopental sodium, a barbiturate used as a rapid-acting general anesthetic, especially in surgical anesthesia.

Examples

Pentothal injection
Pentothal is no longer commonly used in many hospitals due to newer, safer alternatives.

The patient was given Pentothal to induce anesthesia before the surgery.
