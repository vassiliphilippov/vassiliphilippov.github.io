inhospitableness

noun
quality of being unfriendly or unwelcoming
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈhɑːspɪtəblnɪs / (AmE), / ɪnˈhɒspɪtəblnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being unfriendly or unwelcoming to guests or strangers.

Examples

Inhospitableness to guests
His inhospitableness made visitors feel unwelcome in his home.
Some regions are known for the inhospitableness of their climate as well as their people.
The inhospitableness of the mountain villagers surprised the exhausted travelers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hospitality - friendly and welcoming behavior toward guests
  • synonym: inhospitality - the quality of being inhospitable or unwelcoming
