spondylitis

noun
inflammation of a spinal joint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌspɑːndəˈlaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌspɒndɪˈlaɪtɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a spinal joint, causing pain and stiffness.

Examples

Ankylosing spondylitis
Ankylosing spondylitis is a type of arthritis that affects the spine.
He was diagnosed with spondylitis after experiencing persistent back pain and stiffness.
Physical therapy can help manage the symptoms of spondylitis.
