operator gene

noun
gene regulating adjacent gene expression
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pəˌreɪ.tɚ ˌdʒin / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ər.eɪ.tə ˌdʒiːn / (BrE)
a segment of DNA that regulates the activity of adjacent structural genes by controlling the production of messenger RNA
