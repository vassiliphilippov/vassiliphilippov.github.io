mimeograph

verb
copy using a mimeograph machine
4.7 8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪmiəˌɡræf / (AmE), / ˈmʌɪmɪəɡrɑːf / (BrE)
to make copies of (a document) using a mimeograph machine, by pressing ink through a prepared stencil.

Examples

To mimeograph documents
Before the meeting, she mimeographed the agenda for everyone.
He mimeographs notices for the community center using the old machine.
In the 1960s, many schools mimeographed class newsletters for parents.
