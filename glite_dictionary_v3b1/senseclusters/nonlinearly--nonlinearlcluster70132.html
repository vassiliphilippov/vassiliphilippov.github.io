nonlinearly

adverb
not in a straight line
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɪnɪrli / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɪniəli / (BrE)
in a way that does not follow a straight line, regular pattern, or direct sequence

Examples

Story develops nonlinearly
The plot develops nonlinearly, revealing key events out of chronological order.
In mathematics, some equations behave nonlinearly under certain conditions.
The artist arranged the images nonlinearly to create a sense of surprise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregularly - in a way that is not regular or even
  • antonym: linearly - in a straight line or regular sequence
