kangaroo-foot plant

noun
Australian plant with kangaroo-foot-shaped flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌkæŋɡəˌru fʊt plænt / (AmE), / ˌkaŋɡəˌruː fʊt plɑːnt / (BrE)
an Australian herbaceous plant, chiefly of the genus Anigozanthos, with spring flowers covered in woolly hairs and shaped like a kangaroo's foot
