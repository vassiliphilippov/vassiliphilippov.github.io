fraidy

adjective
easily scared
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfreɪdi / (AmE), / ˈfreɪdi / (BrE)
informal or playful term for someone who is easily frightened or scared

Examples

A fraidy cat
Don't be such a fraidy—the dog is friendly and won't bite you.
She laughed and called him a fraidy when he jumped at the thunder.
Even the bravest people can feel like a fraidy in the dark sometimes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not easily frightened
  • synonym: timid - showing a lack of courage or confidence
