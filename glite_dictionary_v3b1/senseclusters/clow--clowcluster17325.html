clow

verb
to hit forcefully
to hit or strike someone or something, especially as a blow; chiefly used in regional or dialect speech

Examples

To clow someone
If anyone steals from her, she will not hesitate to clow them.
He threatened to clow the ball out of the park with one swing.
The boy was warned not to clow his brother during their argument.
