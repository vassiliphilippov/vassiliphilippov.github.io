far and wide

adverb
over a large area or everywhere
1.0
Pronunciation: / fɑr ənd waɪd / (AmE), / fɑː ənd waɪd / (BrE)
In or to many places over a large area; everywhere or across a great distance.

Examples

Far and wide spread
Rumors about the discovery spread far and wide within days.
She searched far and wide for the perfect wedding dress.
The charity's influence extends far and wide across the country.

  • synonym: far and near - from all distances; from all places
