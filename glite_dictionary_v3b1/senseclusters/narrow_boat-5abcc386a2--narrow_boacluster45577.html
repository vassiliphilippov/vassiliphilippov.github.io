narrow boat

noun
long, slender canal boat for freight
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛroʊ ˌboʊt / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊ bəʊt / (BrE)
a long, slender boat designed to carry freight on canals, especially those with narrow locks

Examples

A narrow boat on canals
A traditional narrow boat is often decorated with bright colors and ornate designs.
Many narrow boats have been converted into comfortable homes for people who love life on the water.
The family decided to rent a narrow boat for a week-long journey along the Grand Union Canal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barge - a flat-bottomed boat for carrying freight, especially on canals and rivers
