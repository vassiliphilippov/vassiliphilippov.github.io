saiga

noun
goat-like Eurasian antelope
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪɡə / (AmE), / ˈsaɪɡə / (BrE)
A goat-like antelope of central Eurasia with a distinctive swollen, proboscis-like nose.

Examples

A saiga antelope
Researchers observed a herd of saiga grazing on the steppes of Kazakhstan.
The saiga population has been rapidly declining due to habitat loss and poaching.
The unique nose of the saiga helps filter dust and regulate air temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antelope - a fast-running hoofed mammal of the family Bovidae
