fatherlessness

noun
not having children
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːðərləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɑːðələsnəs / (BrE)
the state or condition of a man not having children or not being a father

Examples

His fatherlessness
His fatherlessness was a result of his decision not to start a family.
Some men embrace fatherlessness as a personal or lifestyle choice.
The topic of fatherlessness is sometimes discussed in conversations about changing family structures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childlessness - the state of not having any children
  • antonym: fatherhood - the state of being a father
