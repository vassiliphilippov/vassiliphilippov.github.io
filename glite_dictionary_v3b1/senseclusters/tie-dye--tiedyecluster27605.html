tie-dye

verb
to dye fabric by tying and coloring for patterns
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪˌdaɪ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪˌdaɪ / (BrE)
to color fabric by tying it in knots or folds and dyeing it, producing irregular patterns

Examples

To tie-dye shirts
I want to tie-dye some old shirts this weekend for a fun new look.
She is tie-dyeing a scarf to wear at the music festival.
They tie-dyed their pillowcases with bright, swirling colors at summer camp.
