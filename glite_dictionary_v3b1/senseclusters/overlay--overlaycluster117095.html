Synonyms

  • coating
    3.4 5.07/m
    coating is a thin applied film used mainly for protection or finish, while overlay can be material or information added on top.
  • layer
    2.0 41/m
    layer is a general sheet or thickness of material that lies over something, while overlay implies an added top layer for features or decoration.
  • covering
    1.1 1.73/m
    covering is something placed over for protection or hiding, while overlay often means a designed layer added for function or decoration.
