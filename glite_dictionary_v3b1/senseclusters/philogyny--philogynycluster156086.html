philogyny

noun
admiration for women
Pronunciation: / fɪˈlɑdʒəni / (AmE), / fɪˈlɒdʒɪni / (BrE)
Admiration or love for women.

Examples

Philogyny for women
His lifelong philogyny was reflected in his support for women's education.
Many viewed his respectful attitude towards women as a sign of philogyny.
The writer's philogyny is evident throughout his work, celebrating women's achievements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: misogyny - dislike of or prejudice against women
  • synonym: philanthropy - love of humanity
