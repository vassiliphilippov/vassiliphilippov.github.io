obelus

noun
division or reference symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑbələs / (AmE), / ˈɒbɪləs / (BrE)
a symbol (÷ or †) used in mathematics to indicate division or to mark a doubtful passage in a text

Examples

Math obelus symbol
In older math textbooks, the obelus is often used to show division between numbers.
The editor placed an obelus next to the line to indicate it was questionable.
Many students first encounter the obelus when learning basic arithmetic operations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: asterisk - the symbol (*) used to mark a footnote or annotation
  • synonym: division sign - the symbol (÷) used to indicate division in mathematics
