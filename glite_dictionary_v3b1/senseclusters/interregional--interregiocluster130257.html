interregional

adjective
between regions
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənl̩ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈriːdʒənl / (BrE)
happening or existing between two or more regions

Examples

Interregional transportation
The new highway will improve interregional transportation across the country.
An interregional conference was held to discuss environmental policies affecting several areas.
They signed an interregional agreement to promote cultural exchange between the two provinces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: international - involving two or more countries
  • antonym: intraregional - happening within a single region
