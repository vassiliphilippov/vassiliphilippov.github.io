auricular artery

noun
artery supplying blood to the ear
7.6
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈrɪkjələr ˈɑːrˌtɛri / (AmE), / ɔːˈrɪkjʊlə ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
An artery that supplies blood to the ear.

Examples

The auricular artery
Injuries to the auricular artery can lead to significant bleeding around the ear.
Surgeons must be cautious of the auricular artery during otologic procedures.
The posterior auricular artery is a branch of the external carotid artery.

Related Terms
