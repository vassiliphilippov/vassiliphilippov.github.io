circumstellar

adjective
around a star
Pronunciation: / ˌsɜrkəmˈstɛlɚ / (AmE), / ˌsɜːkəmˈstelə / (BrE)

Examples

The presence of circumstellar material can influence the evolution of planetary systems.
Astronomers study circumstellar environments to understand how planets are born.
