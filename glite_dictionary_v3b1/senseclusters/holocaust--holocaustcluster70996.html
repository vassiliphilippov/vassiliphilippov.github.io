holocaust

noun
mass destruction, especially by fire or war
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊ.ləˌkɑst / (AmE), / ˈhɒl.əˌkɑːst / (BrE)
an event involving widespread destruction and loss of life, especially caused by fire or war

Examples

A global holocaust
Many fear that another world war could bring about a global holocaust.
The fire quickly turned the building into a holocaust, leaving nothing behind.
The threat of a nuclear holocaust has haunted international relations for decades.

  • synonym: disaster - an event causing great damage or loss of life
