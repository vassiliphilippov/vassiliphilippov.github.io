intransparent

adjective
not clear or transparent
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrænˈspɛrənt / (AmE), / ˌɪntrænˈspærənt / (BrE)
not allowing light to pass through, or not clear and easy to understand

Examples

Intransparent explanation
The report was written in such an intransparent way that few people could follow it.
The glass was so intransparent that we could not see anything on the other side.
Critics argued that the process was intransparent and lacked proper explanation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opaque - not letting light through; hard to understand
  • antonym: transparent - allowing light to pass through; easy to understand
