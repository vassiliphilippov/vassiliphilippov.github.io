olive

noun
small, oval fruit used as relish or for oil
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv /; / ˈɑː.lɪv / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv /; / ˈɒl.ɪv / (BrE)
a small, oval fruit (green or black) from the olive tree, eaten as food or used to make olive oil

Examples

Have you ever tasted a freshly picked olive from the tree?
She added chopped olives to the salad for extra flavor.
The Mediterranean diet includes plenty of olive oil, pressed from the ripe fruit.
