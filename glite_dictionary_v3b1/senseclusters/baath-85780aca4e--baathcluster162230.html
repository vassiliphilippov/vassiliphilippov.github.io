Baath

proper noun
Arab nationalist party
8.7
Pronunciation: / bɑːθ / (AmE), / bɑːθ / (BrE)
a political movement or party in the Arab world advocating Arab unity, socialism, and national revival

Examples

Baath Party
The Baath Party was founded to promote Arab unity and socialism in the Middle East.
Syria and Iraq both had governments led by the Baath Party at different times.
Many historians study the influence of the Baath movement on modern Arab politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Arab nationalism - a political ideology advocating the unity of Arab peoples and countries
  • synonym: pan-Arabism - the idea or advocacy of political union of all Arab states
