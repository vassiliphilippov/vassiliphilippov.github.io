Medicago

noun
genus of clover-like flowering plants
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛdɪˈkɑːɡoʊ /; / ˌmɛdɪˈkɑgoʊ / (AmE), / ˌmɛdɪˈkɑːɡəʊ /; / ˌmɛdɪˈkɑːgəʊ / (BrE)
a genus of flowering plants in the pea family (Fabaceae), commonly known as medicks, including important forage crops such as alfalfa
