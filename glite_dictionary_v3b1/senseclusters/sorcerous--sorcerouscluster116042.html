sorcerous

adjective
relating to magic or supernatural powers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔːrsərəs / (AmE), / ˈsɔː.sər.əs / (BrE)
possessing or relating to supernatural or magical powers; characteristic of a sorcerer.

Examples

Sorcerous powers
Her sorcerous abilities were feared throughout the kingdom.
Legends told of a sorcerous amulet that protected its wearer from harm.
The sorcerous atmosphere in the old castle sent chills down their spines.
