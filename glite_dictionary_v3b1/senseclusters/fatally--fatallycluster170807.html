fatally

adverb
in a way that causes death or disaster
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪtəli / (AmE), / ˈfeɪtəli / (BrE)
In a way that causes death or disaster; with fatal consequences or results.

Examples

To fatally injure
Mistaking those berries for edible ones proved fatally wrong.
She was fatally wounded during the battle, despite immediate medical attention.
The driver was fatally injured in the accident last night.

Related Terms

  • antonym: harmlessly - in a way that does not cause harm or death
  • synonym: lethally - in a way that is capable of causing death
