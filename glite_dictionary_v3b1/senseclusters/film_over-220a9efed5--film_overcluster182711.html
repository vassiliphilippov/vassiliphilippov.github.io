film over

verb
become covered with a film; lose clarity
Pronunciation: / fɪlm ˈoʊvɚ / (AmE), / fɪlm ˈəʊvə / (BrE)
(of a surface, especially the eyes) to become covered with a thin layer that makes it less clear or glassy; to lose clear vision or brightness.

Examples

Eyes film over
As she listened to the dull lecture, her eyes began to film over with boredom.
Fatigue made everything blurry, and his vision started to film over.
The glass of water had filmed over after sitting out all night.
