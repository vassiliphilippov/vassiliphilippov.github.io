littleness

noun
lack of strength or vigor
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt.əl.nəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.əl.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality or condition of having little strength, force, or vigor; weakness or feebleness

Examples

Littleness of strength
Despite the littleness of his voice, his words were confident and clear.
She was aware of the littleness in her handshake, a sign of her exhaustion.
The author's writing captured the littleness of human power against nature.
