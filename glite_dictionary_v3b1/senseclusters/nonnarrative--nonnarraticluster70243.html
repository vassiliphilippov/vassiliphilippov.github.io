nonnarrative

noun
work without story
8.3
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈnær.ə.tɪv/ /; / /nə.ˈnær.ə.tɪv/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈnær.ə.tɪv/ /; / /nə.ˈnær.ə.tɪv/ (BrE)
a work, such as a film, poem, or artwork, that does not follow a story or narrative structure

Examples

A nonnarrative film

Some modern art installations are intentionally nonnarrative to encourage personal interpretation.
The poetry collection was praised for its nonnarrative approach, breaking away from traditional storytelling.
