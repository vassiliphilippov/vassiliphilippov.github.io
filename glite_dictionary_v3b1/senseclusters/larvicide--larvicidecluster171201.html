larvicide

noun
chemical for killing insect larvae
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑrvɪˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈlɑːvɪˌsaɪd / (BrE)
a chemical substance used to kill the larval stage of insects, especially pests such as mosquitoes

Examples

A larvicide chemical
Proper use of a larvicide can significantly reduce the transmission of mosquito-borne diseases.
Public health workers applied a larvicide to standing water to prevent mosquito breeding.
This new larvicide is particularly effective against the larvae of agricultural pests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insecticide - a chemical used to kill insects
  • degree lower: larva - the immature form of an insect before metamorphosis
