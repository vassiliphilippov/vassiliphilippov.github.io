pharmacological

adjective
relating to pharmacology or drugs
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑːrməkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌfɑːməkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to the science or study of drugs and their effects on the body

Examples

Pharmacological research
Her expertise in pharmacological research led to significant medical breakthroughs.
Researchers are investigating the pharmacological effects of the new compound.
The drug has several pharmacological properties that make it effective for treating pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pharmacology - the branch of medicine concerned with drugs and their effects
