unhittable

adjective
impossible to hit
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɪtəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɪtəbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to hit, especially in sports such as baseball or cricket

Examples

Unhittable fastball
The pitcher's fastball was completely unhittable last night.
During the match, her serve was nearly unhittable for her opponent.
The new video game boss is so strong that some players think it's unhittable.

  • synonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
