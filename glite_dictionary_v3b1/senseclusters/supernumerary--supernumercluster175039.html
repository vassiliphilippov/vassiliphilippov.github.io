supernumerary

noun
minor actor in crowd scenes
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsuː.pɚˈnuː.məˌrɛr.i / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈnjuː.mə.rə.ri / (BrE)
a minor actor in a play, opera, or film who appears in crowd scenes and does not have speaking parts

Examples

Supernumerary in a play
As a supernumerary, she enjoyed being part of the theatre world without the pressure of speaking lines.
He worked as a supernumerary in several opera productions before landing larger roles.
The casting director is looking for supernumeraries to fill the crowd scenes in the film.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extra - a minor actor who appears in a non-speaking role, usually part of a crowd scene
