Synonyms

  • immovable
    4.4 0.86/m
    immovable describes things fixed so they cannot be moved, while immobile can describe people or objects unable to move.
  • motionless
    3.0 6.24/m
    motionless looks still and shows no movement, while immobile often means unable to move or be moved.
  • stationary
    2.9 6.00/m
    stationary stays in one place, often by design or choice, while immobile means it cannot be moved.
  • unmoving
    2.2 0.18/m
    unmoving is quietly without motion or action, while immobile suggests it cannot be moved or start moving.
  • nonmoving
    3.0 0.03/m
    nonmoving simply means not in motion or position, while immobile often adds the idea that it cannot be moved.
