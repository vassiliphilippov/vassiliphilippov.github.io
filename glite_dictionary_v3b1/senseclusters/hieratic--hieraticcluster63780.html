hieratic

adjective
cursive form of Egyptian writing
Pronunciation: / haɪ.əˈræt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌhaɪ.əˈræt.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to or written in a cursive form of ancient Egyptian writing used by priests.

Examples

Hieratic text
Scholars deciphered the hieratic writing to reveal details of temple rituals.
She specialized in reading ancient hieratic inscriptions found in Egyptian tombs.
The museum displayed a rare papyrus covered in hieratic script.
