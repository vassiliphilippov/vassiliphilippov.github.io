shake

verb
get rid of or escape from
Pronunciation: / ʃeɪk / (AmE), / ʃeɪk / (BrE)
to get rid of or escape from someone or something, especially by skillful or evasive action

Examples

To shake a follower

Despite my best efforts, I couldn't shake the taxi tailing me through traffic.
She tried to shake the persistent salesperson but he followed her down the street.
