silkie

noun
chicken breed with fluffy feathers
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlki / (AmE), / ˈsɪlki / (BrE)
a breed of chicken known for its soft, fluffy plumage that feels like silk and its gentle temperament

Examples

A silkie chicken
The children loved watching the silkie wander around the backyard with its fluffy feathers.
Many farmers keep a silkie because of its friendly nature and unique appearance.
At the poultry show, her prize-winning silkie attracted a lot of attention from visitors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bantam - a small breed of chicken
  • antonym: leghorn - a breed of chicken known for egg production
