overman

noun
supervisor in mining
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəmæn / (BrE)
a person in charge of supervising workers, especially in a mine or industrial setting

Examples

Mine overman
The overman checked the safety equipment before the miners started their shift.
As an overman, he was responsible for ensuring all work was done according to regulations.
The new overman introduced stricter safety rules in the mining operation.
