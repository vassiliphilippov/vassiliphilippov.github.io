evaporated milk

noun
milk concentrated by evaporation
9.2 2.0
Pronunciation: /ɪ.ˈvæp.ə.reɪ.tɪd mɪlk/ /; / /ɪ.ˈvæp.ə.reɪ.təd mɪlk/ /; / /iː.ˈvæp.ə.reɪ.tɪd mɪlk/ (AmE), /ɪ.ˈvæp.ə.reɪ.tɪd mɪlk/ /; / /ɪ.ˈvæp.ə.reɪ.təd mɪlk/ (BrE)
milk that has been thickened by removing about 60% of its water content through evaporation, usually then canned and used in cooking or as a milk substitute
