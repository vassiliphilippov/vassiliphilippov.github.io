professional chef

noun
trained and skilled chef
Pronunciation: / prəˈfɛʃənl ʃɛf / (AmE), / prəˈfɛʃənəl ʃɛf / (BrE)
A chef who has received formal training and possesses advanced culinary skills, typically working in a restaurant or similar setting.

Examples

A professional chef cooks
After attending culinary school, she became a professional chef at a renowned restaurant.
As a professional chef, he understands the importance of using fresh ingredients.
The dinner was exquisite, thanks to the professional chef they hired for the event.
