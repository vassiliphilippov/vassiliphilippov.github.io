hospital chaplain

noun
chaplain serving in a hospital
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːspɪtl ˈtʃæplɪn / (AmE), / ˈhɒspɪtl ˈtʃæplɪn / (BrE)
a clergy member who provides spiritual care and support to patients, families, and staff in a hospital

Examples

Hospital chaplain provides support
After the accident, a hospital chaplain provided emotional support to those in the waiting area.
Many staff members confide in the hospital chaplain during difficult times at work.
The hospital chaplain visited every room to offer comfort to patients and their families.
