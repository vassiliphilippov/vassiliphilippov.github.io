overregulated

adjective
too many rules
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈrɛɡjəˌleɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈrɛɡjʊleɪtɪd / (BrE)
subject to too many rules or excessive government control or restrictions

Examples

The overregulated market
Critics argue that the financial sector is now overregulated, making it hard for small businesses to compete.
Some believe the healthcare industry has become overregulated due to constant new policies.
The new manager promised to simplify procedures in the overregulated department.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: regulated - controlled or governed by rules or laws
  • antonym: unregulated - not controlled or governed by rules or laws
