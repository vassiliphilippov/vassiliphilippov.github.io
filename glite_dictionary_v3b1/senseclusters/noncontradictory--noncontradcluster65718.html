noncontradictory

adjective
free from contradiction
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnkəntrəˈdɪktəri / (AmE), / ˌnɒnkəntrəˈdɪktəri / (BrE)
not containing or involving any contradiction; consistent and free from logical conflict

Examples

A noncontradictory statement
The witness's statement was noncontradictory and matched the evidence presented in court.
A noncontradictory argument is essential for a strong scientific theory.
Her explanation was clear and noncontradictory, making it easy to understand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consistent - not containing any contradictions; in agreement
  • antonym: contradictory - containing or involving contradictions
