dawdling

noun
wasting time by delaying or being slow
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔdlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɔːdlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of wasting time by moving slowly or being deliberately delayed instead of working or being productive

Examples

Constant dawdling
He was scolded for his constant dawdling when chores needed to be done.
Her dawdling in the mornings made them late for school every day.
There was no time for dawdling if they wanted to catch the train.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dallying - the act of delaying or idling instead of hurrying or working
  • synonym: dawdle - to waste time; to move slowly and idly
