round

noun
outburst or series of applause, cheers, etc.

an instance of applause, cheering, or other activity that occurs as a single, distinct event within a series

Examples

A round of applause
After every joke, there was a loud round of laughter from the audience.
The band was greeted with an enthusiastic round of cheers as they took the stage.
The speaker received a round of applause after her inspiring presentation.
