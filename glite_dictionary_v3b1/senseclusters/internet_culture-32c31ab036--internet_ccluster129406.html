internet culture

noun
online customs and social norms
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.t̬ɚ.nɛt ˈkʌl.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈɪn.tə.nɛt ˈkʌl.tʃə / (BrE)
the customs, practices, memes, and norms that develop from people interacting online, especially in social media and virtual communities

Examples
