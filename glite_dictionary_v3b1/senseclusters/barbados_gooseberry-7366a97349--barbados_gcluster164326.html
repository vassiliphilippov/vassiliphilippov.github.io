Barbados gooseberry

noun
edible fruit of a cactus
Pronunciation: / bɑrˈbeɪdoʊs ˈɡuːsbɛri / (AmE), / bɑːˈbeɪdəʊs ˈɡuːsbəri / (BrE)
A small yellow to orange berry produced by a species of cactus (Pereskia aculeata), used in desserts, preserves, and jellies.

Examples

Fresh Barbados gooseberry
Local markets often sell Barbados gooseberry for use in tropical desserts.

The Barbados gooseberry makes a delicious addition to homemade jams and jellies.
