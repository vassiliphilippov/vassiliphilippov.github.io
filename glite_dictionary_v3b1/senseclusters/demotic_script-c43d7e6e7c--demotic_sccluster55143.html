Demotic script

noun
simplified ancient Egyptian writing system
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːtɪk skrɪpt / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒtɪk skrɪpt / (BrE)
an ancient Egyptian script derived from hieratic and used for everyday writing, characterized by its simplified, cursive form

Examples

Demotic script writing
Learning to read Demotic script is essential for studying late Egyptian texts.
Scholars have deciphered part of the Rosetta Stone by understanding the Demotic script.
The Demotic script was commonly used for everyday administrative documents in ancient Egypt.
