governor's plum

noun
tropical shrub with plum-like red fruits
8.5 8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgʌv.ər.nərz plʌm/ /; / /ˈgʌv.ən.ərz plʌm/ (AmE), /ˈgʌv.ən.əz plʌm/ (BrE)
