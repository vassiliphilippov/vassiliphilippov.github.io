living wage

noun
wage sufficient for decent living
3.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈlɪv.ɪŋ weɪdʒ/ (AmE), /ˈlɪv.ɪŋ weɪdʒ/ (BrE)
a wage that is high enough to maintain a normal standard of living for a worker and their family

Examples

A living wage standard
Earning less than a living wage often forces families to make difficult choices.
Many activists campaign for a living wage to ensure workers can afford basic necessities.
The company pledged to pay all its employees a living wage by the end of the year.
