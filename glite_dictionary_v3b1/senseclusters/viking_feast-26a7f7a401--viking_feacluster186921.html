Viking feast

noun
elaborate meal in Viking style
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪkɪŋ fiːst / (AmE), / ˈvaɪkɪŋ fiːst / (BrE)
a large, elaborate meal inspired by Viking traditions, typically featuring hearty foods and communal dining

Examples

A Viking feast meal
At the museum, families enjoyed a reenactment of a Viking feast featuring ancient recipes.
Guests arrived in costume for the Viking feast, eager to sample mead and hearty stews.
The village hosted a Viking feast to celebrate the summer solstice, complete with roasted meats and traditional songs.
