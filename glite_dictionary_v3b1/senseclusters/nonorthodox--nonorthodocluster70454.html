nonorthodox

noun
unconventional person
8.2
Pronunciation: /nən.ˈɑːr.θə.dɑːks/ /; / /nən.ˈɔːr.θə.dɑːks/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈɔː.θə.dɒks/ (BrE)
a person who holds beliefs or practices that differ from what is generally accepted or traditional

Examples

A nonorthodox thinker
As a nonorthodox, he often challenged the usual ways of thinking in his field.
The committee included at least one nonorthodox who questioned every traditional approach.
Being a nonorthodox in a conservative community can be both difficult and rewarding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heretic - a person who holds beliefs that are strongly at odds with established doctrine
  • synonym: iconoclast - a person who attacks or rejects traditional beliefs or institutions
