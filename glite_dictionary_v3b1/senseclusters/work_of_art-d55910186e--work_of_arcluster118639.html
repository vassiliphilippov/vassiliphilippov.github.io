work of art

noun
an object of artistic value, especially painting or sculpture
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɝːk əv ɑrt /; / wɝːk əv ɑːrt / (AmE), / wɜːk əv ɑːt / (BrE)
an object or creation, such as a painting, sculpture, performance, or other work, that is considered to have artistic value or merit
