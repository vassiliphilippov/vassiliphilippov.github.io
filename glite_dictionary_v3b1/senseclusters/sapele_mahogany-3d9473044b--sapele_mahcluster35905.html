sapele mahogany

noun
African tree with cedar-scented wood
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / səˈpiːli məˈhɑːɡəni / (AmE), / səˈpiːli məˈhɒɡəni / (BrE)
An African tree (Entandrophragma cylindricum) valued for its lightweight, cedar-scented wood, which ranges in color from pink to reddish brown and is often used in furniture and musical instruments.
