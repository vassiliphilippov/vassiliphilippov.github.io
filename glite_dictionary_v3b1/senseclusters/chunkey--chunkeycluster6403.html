chunkey

noun
Native American game
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌŋki / (BrE)
a traditional Native American game in which players roll a stone disc and throw spears or sticks at it to score points

Examples

Play chunkey game
The ancient game of chunkey was played by many Native American tribes across North America.
At the festival, children learned how to play chunkey using replica stone discs and wooden sticks.
Artifacts related to chunkey have been discovered at several archaeological sites.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lacrosse - a team sport played with a ball and stick, originating with Native Americans
