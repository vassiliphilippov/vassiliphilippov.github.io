Lhasa apso

noun
small long-haired Tibetan dog breed
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑː.sə ˈæp.soʊ / (AmE), / ˈlɑː.sə ˈæp.səʊ / (BrE)
a small breed of dog with a long, heavy coat, originally from Tibet, often kept as a companion or watchdog

Examples

A Lhasa apso dog
Grooming a Lhasa apso regularly is important to keep its coat in good condition.
Many families choose the Lhasa apso for its friendly nature and suitability as an apartment dog.
The Lhasa apso is known for its luxurious, long coat and alert personality.
