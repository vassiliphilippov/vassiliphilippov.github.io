mete

noun
boundary line or marker
9.9
Pronunciation: / miːt / (AmE), / miːt / (BrE)
a line or marker that indicates a boundary, especially of land; a limit or border

Examples

Stone mete marker
During the survey, they discovered that the stone mete had shifted over the years.
In colonial times, disputes often arose when settlers disagreed about the exact location of their metes.
The property was clearly marked by an old wooden mete at the edge of the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boundary - a line marking the limits of an area
  • synonym: limit - a point or line beyond which something may not pass
