saprozoic

adjective
feeding on dead or decaying animal matter
7.1 9.8
Pronunciation: /sæp.rə.ˈzɔɪ.ɪk/ (AmE), /sæp.rə.ˈzɔɪ.ɪk/ (BrE)
(of certain animals) feeding on dead or decaying animal matter.

Examples
