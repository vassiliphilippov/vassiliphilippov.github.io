historic house

noun
house of significant historical importance
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɪˈstɔrɪk haʊs / (AmE), / hɪˈstɒrɪk haʊs / (BrE)
a house that has significant historical importance or interest, often preserved due to its age, architecture, or connection to notable events or people

Examples

A historic house museum
Preserving each historic house helps keep the town's heritage alive.
The museum is located in a beautifully restored historic house downtown.
They took a guided tour through the historic house that once belonged to the poet.
