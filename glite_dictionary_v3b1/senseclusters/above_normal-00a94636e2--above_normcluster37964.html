above normal

adjective
higher than usual or expected
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbʌv ˈnɔrməl / (AmE), / əˈbʌv ˈnɔːməl / (BrE)
Higher than the usual or expected standard, level, or amount.

Examples

Above normal levels
Air pollution levels are expected to remain above normal through the weekend.
Her blood pressure readings were consistently above normal during the examination.
This year, rainfall in the region has been above normal for three consecutive months.

Related Terms

  • antonym: below normal - lower than the usual or expected standard
  • antonym: normal - the usual or expected standard, level, or condition
