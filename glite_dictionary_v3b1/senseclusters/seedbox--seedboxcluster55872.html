seedbox

noun
remote file-sharing server
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːdbɑks / (AmE), / ˈsiːdbɒks / (BrE)
a remote server with high-speed internet used to upload, download, and share files on peer-to-peer networks, especially BitTorrent

Examples

Many users prefer using a seedbox to keep their internet connection fast while sharing files.
After uploading the movie to his seedbox, he could share it with friends easily.
