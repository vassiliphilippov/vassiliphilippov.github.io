ghat

noun
steps leading to water in India
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɡɑt / (AmE), / ɡɑːt / (BrE)
In India, a series of steps leading down to a river or other body of water, used especially for bathing or ceremonial purposes.

Examples

A ghat by river
Smoke rose from the riverbank ghat where a family was holding a ritual.
The pilgrims gathered at the ghat to participate in the sunrise ceremony.
Tourists admired the view from the ancient stone ghat overlooking the Ganges.
