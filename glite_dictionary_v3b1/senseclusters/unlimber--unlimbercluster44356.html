unlimber

verb
to prepare for use
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlɪmbər / (AmE), / ʌnˈlɪmbə / (BrE)
to prepare equipment or weapons, especially artillery, for use by removing restraints or making them ready for action

Examples

The crew quickly unlimbered the field gun as the enemy approached.
Before the demonstration, the team began unlimbering the equipment for setup.
It took only minutes for the soldiers to unlimber the heavy artillery and prepare to fire.
The soldiers quickly unlimbered the cannons before the battle began.
Before the demonstration, the team unlimbered their equipment and checked everything carefully.
It took only a few minutes to unlimber the heavy machinery for use on the site.
