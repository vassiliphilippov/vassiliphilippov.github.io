touch-typist

noun
a person who types without looking at the keyboard
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃ ˌtaɪpɪst / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ ˌtaɪpɪst / (BrE)
a person who is skilled at typing without looking at the keyboard

Examples

A touch-typist types fast
As a touch-typist, she can easily type over eighty words per minute.
Learning to become a touch-typist can greatly improve your productivity.
Many employers prefer hiring a touch-typist for their administrative tasks.
