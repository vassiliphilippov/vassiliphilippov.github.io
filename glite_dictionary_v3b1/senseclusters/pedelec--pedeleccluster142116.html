pedelec

noun
bicycle with pedal-assist electric motor
Pronunciation: / ˈpiːdəˌlɛk / (AmE), / ˈpiːdəlɛk / (BrE)
a bicycle equipped with an electric motor that assists the rider's pedaling effort

Examples

An electric pedelec
He decided to commute to work on his new pedelec to save energy and avoid traffic.
Many tourists rent a pedelec to explore the city without getting too tired.
The pedelec made cycling uphill much easier for the group.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electric bicycle - a bicycle powered by an electric motor, can include pedal-assist or throttle modes
