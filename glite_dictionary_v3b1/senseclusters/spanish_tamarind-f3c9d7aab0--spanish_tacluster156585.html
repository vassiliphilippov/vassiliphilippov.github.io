Spanish tamarind

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ təˈmærɪnd / (AmE), / ˈspanɪʃ təˈmarɪnd / (BrE)
A shrubby tree native to Madagascar, occasionally cultivated for its edible apple-shaped fruit.

Examples

Spanish tamarind tree
Researchers studied the medicinal properties of the Spanish tamarind tree in Madagascar.
She planted a Spanish tamarind in her garden for its unique fruit.
The Spanish tamarind produces small, apple-shaped fruits popular in local markets.
