gyromagnetic

adjective
magnetic from rotation
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒaɪroʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒaɪərəʊmæɡˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the magnetic properties produced by the rotation of a body or particle

Examples

The gyromagnetic effect
The gyromagnetic effect is crucial in understanding how certain particles behave in a magnetic field.
Scientists measured the gyromagnetic ratio to analyze the properties of the atom.
A gyromagnetic material responds differently to external magnetic forces due to its rotation.
