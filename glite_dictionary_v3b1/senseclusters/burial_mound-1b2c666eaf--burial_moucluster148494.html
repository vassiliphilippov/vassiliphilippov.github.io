burial mound

noun
prehistoric earthwork over a tomb
6.6 7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛriəl maʊnd / (AmE), / ˈbɛriəl maʊnd / (BrE)
an artificial mound of earth or stones built over a grave or graves, especially in ancient or prehistoric times
