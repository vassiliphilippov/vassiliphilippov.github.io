Carpathian

adjective
of the Carpathians
Pronunciation: / kɑrˈpeɪθiən / (AmE), / kɑːˈpeɪθiən / (BrE)
relating to the Carpathian Mountains or the region surrounding them in Central and Eastern Europe

Carpathian mountains
