antitheistic

adjective
opposed to theism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiθiˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌæntiθiˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
actively opposed or hostile to religion, religious beliefs, or the existence of any deity

Her antitheistic views often sparked debates with her religious colleagues at work.
