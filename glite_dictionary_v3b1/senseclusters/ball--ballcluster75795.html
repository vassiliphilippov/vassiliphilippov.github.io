ball

noun
a compact mass, usually rounded
Pronunciation: / bɔl /; / bɑl / (AmE), / bɔːl / (BrE)
a solid or compact mass or object that is spherical or nearly spherical in shape

Examples

A ball of yarn
A ball of yarn rolled under the sofa.
He threw a ball of crumpled paper into the bin.
She shaped the clay into a ball before placing it on the table.
Lightning struck the tree, creating a glowing ball of fire.
The children played catch with a ball in the backyard.
The kitten chased a ball of yarn across the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lump - a compact mass of a substance
  • synonym: sphere - a perfectly round geometrical object in three-dimensional space
