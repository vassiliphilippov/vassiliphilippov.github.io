unthreaded

adjective
without thread
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɛdɪd / (BrE)
having had a thread removed or not containing a thread.

Examples

Unthreaded needle
She carefully placed the unthreaded needle back in the box.
The unthreaded screw would not hold the pieces together properly.
After removing the yarn, the unthreaded button was ready to be replaced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: threaded - containing or having a thread inserted
