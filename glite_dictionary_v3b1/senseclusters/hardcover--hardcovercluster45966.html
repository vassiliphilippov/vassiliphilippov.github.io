hardcover

adjective
book with a rigid protective cover
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrdˌkʌvɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɑːdˌkʌv.ər / (BrE)
(of a book) having a rigid protective cover, as opposed to a paperback.

Examples

Hardcover edition
Most collectors prefer to buy hardcover books for their durability.
She gave him a beautiful hardcover biography for his birthday.
The library just received a hardcover edition of the novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hardback - a book with a hard cover
  • antonym: paperback - a book bound in flexible paper covers
