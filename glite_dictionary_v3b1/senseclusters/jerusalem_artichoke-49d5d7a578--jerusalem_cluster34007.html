Jerusalem artichoke

noun
edible tuber-producing plant
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈruːsələm ˈɑːrtɪˌtʃoʊk /; / dʒəˈruːsələm ˈɑːrtʃɪˌkoʊk / (AmE), / dʒəˈruːsələm ˈɑːtɪˌtʃəʊk / (BrE)
a North American sunflower (Helianthus tuberosus) grown for its edible, knobby tubers, which are eaten as a vegetable
