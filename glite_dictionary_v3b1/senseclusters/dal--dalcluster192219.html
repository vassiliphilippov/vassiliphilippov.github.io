dal

noun
metric unit (10 liters)
Pronunciation: / dæl / (AmE), / dæl / (BrE)
a metric unit of volume or capacity equal to 10 liters

Examples

A dal of water
The recipe called for one dal of milk, which is equal to 10 liters.
The tank can hold up to 5 dal of water.
Wine production is often measured in dals for larger quantities.
