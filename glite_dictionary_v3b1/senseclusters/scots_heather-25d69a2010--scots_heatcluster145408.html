Scots heather

noun
evergreen shrub with purple or pink flowers
4.7
Pronunciation: / skɑts ˈhɛð.ɚ / (AmE), / skɒts ˈhɛð.ə / (BrE)
A low, evergreen shrub (Calluna vulgaris) with small purple or pink flowers, widely found in the Northern Hemisphere, especially in Scotland.

Scots heather flowers
