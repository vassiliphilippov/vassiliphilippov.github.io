gutter

noun
tool for removing fish entrails
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌtər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌtə / (BrE)
A tool used for removing the internal organs of fish.

Examples

Fish gutter
He keeps a sharp gutter in his tackle box for filleting and cleaning fish.
Make sure to clean the gutter thoroughly after gutting several fish.
The fisherman swiftly used a gutter to prepare the day's catch.
