Daubentonia

noun
genus of lemurs (aye-aye)
9.9
Pronunciation: /doʊ.bən.ˈtoʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /doʊ.bɛn.ˈtoʊ.niː.ə/ (AmE), /dəʊ.bən.ˈtəʊ.niː.ə/ /; / /dəʊ.bɛn.ˈtəʊ.niː.ə/ (BrE)
A genus of lemurs comprising the aye-aye, native to Madagascar, and serving as the type genus of the family Daubentoniidae.

Examples

Daubentonia of Madagascar
Evidence suggests that members of the Daubentonia genus are unique to Madagascar.
Scientists have classified the aye-aye within the genus Daubentonia.
The Daubentonia is best known for its only extant species, the aye-aye.
