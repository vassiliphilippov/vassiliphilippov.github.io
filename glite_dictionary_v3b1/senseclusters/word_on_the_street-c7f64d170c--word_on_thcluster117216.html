word on the street

noun
circulating rumor or informal information
5.9
Pronunciation: / wɝd ɑn ðə strit / (AmE), / wɜːd ɒn ðə striːt / (BrE)
informal information or rumors circulating among the public; what people are saying unofficially

Examples

The word on the street
If you believe the word on the street, the mayor might resign soon.
She always knows the latest word on the street before anyone else.
The word on the street is that the company will launch a new product next month.
