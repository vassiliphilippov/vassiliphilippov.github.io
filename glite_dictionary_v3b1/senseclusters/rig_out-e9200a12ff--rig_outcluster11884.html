rig out

verb
equip or prepare with necessary items
Pronunciation: / rɪɡ aʊt / (AmE), / rɪɡ aʊt / (BrE)
to equip, furnish, or set up with the necessary accessories or equipment; to prepare (something or someone) for use or a specific purpose

Examples

To rig out a car
He rigged out his car with the latest sound system and speakers.
They spent the afternoon rigging out their campsite before dark.
We need to rig out the gym with chairs and tables for the event.
