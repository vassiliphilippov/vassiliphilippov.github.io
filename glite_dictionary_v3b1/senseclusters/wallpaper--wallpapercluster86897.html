wallpaper

noun
digital background image
2.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɔːl.peɪ.pər/ /; / /ˈwɑːl.peɪ.pər/ (AmE), /ˈwɔːl.peɪ.pə/ (BrE)

Examples

Phone wallpaper image
