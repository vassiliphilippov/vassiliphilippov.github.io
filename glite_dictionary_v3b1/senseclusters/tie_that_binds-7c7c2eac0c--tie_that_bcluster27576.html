tie that binds

noun
unifying force or connection
Pronunciation: / taɪ ðæt baɪndz / (AmE), / taɪ ðæt baɪndz / (BrE)
a factor or force that unites people or groups; something that keeps people together

Examples

The tie that binds people
For them, music serves as the tie that binds the generations together.
Shared values are the tie that binds our organization through difficult times.
Trust is often the tie that binds partnerships that last a lifetime.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a thing used to tie something or to fasten things together; a connection between people
  • synonym: connection - a relationship in which a person, thing, or idea is linked or associated with something else
