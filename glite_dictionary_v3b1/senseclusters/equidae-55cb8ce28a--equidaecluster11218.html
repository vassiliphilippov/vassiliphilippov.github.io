Equidae

noun
family of horses, asses, and zebras
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈekwɪˌdeɪ / (AmE), / ˈɛkwɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of mammals that includes horses, asses, zebras, and their extinct relatives.

Examples

The Equidae family
All living members of Equidae are characterized by a single toe on each foot.
The Equidae family once included many species that are now extinct.
Zebras, horses, and donkeys all belong to the Equidae family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: donkey - a domesticated animal related to the horse, with long ears
  • synonym: horse - a large domesticated animal with solid hooves and a flowing mane and tail
  • synonym: zebra - an African wild animal with black and white stripes
