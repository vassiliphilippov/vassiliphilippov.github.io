visitorship

noun
number of visitors
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪtərʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtəʃɪp / (BrE)
the state of being a visitor or the total number of visitors to a place or event

Examples

Museum visitorship
The art gallery saw a significant rise in visitorship after the famous painting was displayed.
Annual reports showed that visitorship to the national park doubled over the past decade.
Efforts to improve visitorship included new marketing campaigns and special events.

Related Terms

  • antonym: absence - the state of not being present
  • synonym: attendance - the number of people present at an event or place
