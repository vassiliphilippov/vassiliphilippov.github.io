approver

noun
person with power to approve
4.0 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàpruv…ö / (AmE), / …ôÀàpruÀêv…ô / (BrE)
a person or authority who has the official power to approve something; someone who gives consent or authorization

Examples

An approver gives permission
Each request must be reviewed by an approver before being processed.
Only the designated approver can authorize this transaction.
The document was delayed because the approver was on vacation.

Synonyms

  • authorizer
    6.8 0.02/m
    an authorizer gives official permission or approval, while an approver is the authority who formally consents or authorizes actions.
  • sanctioner
    7.7 0.00/m
    a sanctioner gives formal official permission in legal or policy settings, while an approver is a general authority who consents.
  • assenter
    7.7 0.00/m
    an assenter agrees or gives assent, often as a person expressing support, while an approver holds official power to authorize actions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approver - person who approves (alternative term: approver of proposals, documents, etc.)
  • antonym: rejector - person who rejects or declines something
