approver

noun
person with power to approve
5.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈpruː.vər/ (AmE), /ə.ˈpruː.və/ (BrE)
a person or authority who has the official power to approve something; someone who gives consent or authorization

Examples

Only the designated approver can authorize this transaction.
The document was delayed because the approver was on vacation.
