helplessly

adverb
in a powerless or unable manner
4.1 2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛlpləsli / (AmE), / ˈhɛlpləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows an inability to act or help oneself; without the power to do anything.

Examples

To wave helplessly
He waved helplessly as the train disappeared into the distance.
She looked helplessly at the broken engine, unsure of what to do next.
The dog whined helplessly as it waited for someone to rescue it.
