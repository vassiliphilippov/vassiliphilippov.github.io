tit

noun
small insectivorous songbird
Pronunciation: / tɪt / (AmE), / tɪt / (BrE)
A small songbird of the family Paridae, typically with a stout bill and a lively manner, found especially in Eurasia and Africa.

Examples

A blue tit
A great tit sang loudly near the woodland path.
Many garden birdwatchers enjoy seeing blue tits at the feeder.
The tit flitted from branch to branch searching for insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chickadee - a North American bird of the same family as the tit
