enjoyer

noun
person who enjoys something
3.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒɔɪɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒɔɪə / (BrE)
a person who takes pleasure in having, using, or experiencing something

Examples

An enjoyer of food
As an enjoyer of fine cuisine, she visited many top restaurants around the world.
Every enjoyer of outdoor sports should try hiking in the Alps at least once.
He was a passionate enjoyer of classical music throughout his life.

  • antonym: hater - a person who dislikes something
