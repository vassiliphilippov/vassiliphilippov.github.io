conservatism

noun
preference for traditional values and gradual change
4.9 8.11/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàs…ùÀê.v…ôÀåt…™z.…ôm / (AmE), / k…ônÀàs…úÀê.v…ôÀåt…™z.…ôm / (BrE)
