sick benefit

noun
government payment to the sick unable to work
1.9
Pronunciation: / sɪk ˈbɛnəfɪt / (AmE), / sɪk ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
money paid by the government to someone who is unable to work due to illness

Examples

Sick benefit payment
After her surgery, she had to apply for sick benefit to cover her living expenses.
He relied on sick benefit while he recovered from his illness.
The government increased sick benefit payments during the flu epidemic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sick pay - regular payment to an employee who is away from work because of illness
