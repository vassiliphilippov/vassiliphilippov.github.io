egalitarian

adjective
promoting social equality
8.1 6.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ɪˌɡælɪˈteəriən / (BrE)
believing in or promoting the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities

Examples

Egalitarian society
She supports egalitarian policies that aim to reduce income inequality.
The company's egalitarian approach ensures that all employees have a voice in decision-making.
Their club operates on an egalitarian basis, with no special privileges for any members.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elitist - favoring an elite as superior
  • synonym: equalitarian - believing in and promoting equality
