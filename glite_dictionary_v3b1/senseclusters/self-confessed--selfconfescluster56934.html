self-confessed

adjective
having admitted doing something bad
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf.kənˈfɛst / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf.kənˈfɛst / (BrE)
having admitted, especially publicly, to having done something bad or illegal; having confessed about oneself

Examples

Self-confessed workaholic
As a self-confessed workaholic, she rarely takes vacations.
He is a self-confessed fan of science fiction novels.
The self-confessed thief offered a detailed account of how he broke into the house.

  • synonym: confessed - having openly admitted to something
