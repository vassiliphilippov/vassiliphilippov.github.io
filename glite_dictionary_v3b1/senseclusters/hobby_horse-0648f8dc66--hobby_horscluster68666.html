hobby horse

noun
favorite or obsessive topic
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːbi hɔːrs / (AmE), / ˈhɒbi hɔːs / (BrE)
a subject or topic about which someone is excessively enthusiastic or talks about frequently, often at length or insistently

Examples

Her hobby horse topic
During conversations, she often steers discussion back to her environmental hobby horse.
I've heard enough about his hobby horse; it's time to change the subject.
The professor returned to his hobby horse about the importance of critical thinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: obsession - an idea or topic that preoccupies someone's mind continually
  • synonym: pet topic - a subject someone especially likes discussing
