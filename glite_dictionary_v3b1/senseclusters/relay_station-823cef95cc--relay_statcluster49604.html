relay station

noun
signal amplifier and retransmitter
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈriː.leɪ ˌsteɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˈrɪ.leɪ ˌsteɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
a device that amplifies and retransmits a signal to extend its range or strength, especially in telecommunications

Examples

A relay station device
Cell phone coverage improved after the company installed a new relay station nearby.
Radio waves are picked up and retransmitted by a relay station to ensure coverage in remote areas.
The TV signal is boosted by a relay station halfway up the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repeater - a device that receives and retransmits a signal to extend its range
