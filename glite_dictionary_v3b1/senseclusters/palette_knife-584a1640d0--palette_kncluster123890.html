palette knife

noun
artist's tool for mixing and applying paint
6.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpæl.ət naɪf/ /; / /ˈpæl.ɪt naɪf/ (AmE), /ˈpæl.ɪt naɪf/ /; / /ˈpæl.ət naɪf/ (BrE)
A thin, flexible knife with a rounded tip, used by artists for mixing, applying, or scraping oil paints on a palette or canvas.

Examples

A palette knife tool
A palette knife is essential for creating texture in oil paintings.
After squeezing out the paint, she mixed it with a palette knife on her wooden palette.
He used a palette knife to blend the colors on the canvas.
