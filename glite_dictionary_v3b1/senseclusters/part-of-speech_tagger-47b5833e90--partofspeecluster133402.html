part-of-speech tagger

noun
software for labeling words by part of speech
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑrt əv ˈspiːtʃ ˈtægər / (AmE), / ˌpɑːt əv ˈspiːtʃ ˈtæɡə / (BrE)
A computer program that assigns part-of-speech labels to each word in a text, identifying whether words are nouns, verbs, adjectives, etc.

Examples

A part-of-speech tagger program
After training the part-of-speech tagger on more data, its performance improved significantly.
Researchers use a part-of-speech tagger to analyze grammatical patterns in large text corpora.
The part-of-speech tagger accurately identified all the nouns and verbs in the sentence.
