sourdough bread

noun
bread made with fermented starter
5.8 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsaʊr.doʊ bred/ /; / /ˈsaʊ.ər.doʊ bred/ (AmE), /ˈsaʊ.ə.dəʊ bred/ /; / /ˈsaʊ.dəʊ bred/ (BrE)
