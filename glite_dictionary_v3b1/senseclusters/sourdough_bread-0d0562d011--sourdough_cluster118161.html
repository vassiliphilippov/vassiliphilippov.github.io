sourdough bread

noun
bread made with fermented starter
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊrˌdoʊ ˌbrɛd / (AmE), / ˈsaʊəˌdəʊ ˌbrɛd / (BrE)
Bread made with a fermented dough starter, which gives it a distinctive tangy flavor and chewy texture.

Examples

A sourdough bread loaf
Many people prefer sourdough bread because of its tangy flavor and chewy crust.
She bought a fresh loaf of sourdough bread from the bakery for breakfast.
The café serves sandwiches made with homemade sourdough bread.
