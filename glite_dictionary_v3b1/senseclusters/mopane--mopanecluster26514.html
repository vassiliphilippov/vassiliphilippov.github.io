mopane

noun
African hardwood tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / moʊˈpɑːni / (AmE), / məʊˈpɑːni / (BrE)
a tree native to southern Africa, known for its butterfly-shaped leaves and durable hardwood

Examples

Mopane tree leaves
The mopane is easily recognized by its unique butterfly-shaped leaves.
Many animals in southern Africa feed on the leaves of the mopane tree.
Furniture made from mopane wood is valued for its strength and durability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baobab - a large African tree with a thick trunk and edible fruit
