Hypochaeris

proper noun
genus of flowering herbs (cat's ear)
Pronunciation: / haɪpoʊˈkɪərɪs / (AmE), / hɪpəʊˈkɪərɪs / (BrE)
A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, commonly known as cat's ear, consisting of herbs with milky sap, some of which are widespread weeds.

Examples

Hypochaeris plant species
Hypochaeris species are often found in grasslands and lawns throughout temperate regions.
Gardeners sometimes have difficulty removing Hypochaeris because of its deep taproot.
The flower heads of Hypochaeris resemble those of dandelions but belong to a different genus.
