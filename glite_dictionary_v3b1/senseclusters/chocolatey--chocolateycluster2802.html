chocolatey

adjective
tasting of chocolate
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːkləti / (AmE), / ˈtʃɒkləti / (BrE)
having a strong taste, smell, or quality of chocolate

Examples

Chocolatey flavor
The cake was so chocolatey that everyone wanted a second slice.
I love the rich, chocolatey flavor of this ice cream.
Her brownies are famous for being extra chocolatey and moist.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vanilla - having the taste or quality of vanilla
