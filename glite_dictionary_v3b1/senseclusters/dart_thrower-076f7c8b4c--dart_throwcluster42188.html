dart thrower

noun
tool for throwing darts or spears
Pronunciation: / dɑrt ˈθroʊɚ / (AmE), / dɑːt ˈθrəʊə / (BrE)
a tool, often resembling a sling, used by various indigenous societies to propel darts or spears with greater force and distance

Examples

A dart thrower tool
A skilled hunter can launch a spear much farther using a dart thrower than by hand alone.
Researchers found a dart thrower among the artifacts at the archeological site.
The ancient Aztecs used a dart thrower called an atlatl to hunt animals.
