unwarped

adjective
of sound judgment
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɔrpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɔːpt / (BrE)
having a sound, normal judgment or character; not mentally or morally distorted

Examples

Unwarped judgment
Her unwarped sense of fairness made her a respected mediator in community disputes.
He is known for his unwarped perspective, always seeing situations clearly and honestly.
The judge's unwarped character earned the trust of everyone in the courtroom.
