climbing frame

noun
playground structure for climbing
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmɪŋ freɪm / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmɪŋ freɪm / (BrE)
A structure made of bars or logs, typically found in playgrounds, for children to climb on.

Examples

A climbing frame in playground
After school, they raced to the climbing frame to see who could reach the top first.
The children spent hours playing on the climbing frame at the park.
The new playground features a colorful climbing frame with slides and ropes.
