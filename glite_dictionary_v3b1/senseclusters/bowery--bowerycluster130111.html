bowery

noun
leafy shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈbaʊəri / (AmE), / ˈbaʊəri / (BrE)
a shady, leafy shelter or area, often formed by trees, bushes, or vines

Examples

A cool bowery
They enjoyed a picnic in the cool bowery behind the old house.
The path led to a quiet bowery where birds sang in the shade.
Children played in a natural bowery formed by overhanging branches.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arbor - a shelter of branches or vines in a garden
  • synonym: grove - a small group of trees
