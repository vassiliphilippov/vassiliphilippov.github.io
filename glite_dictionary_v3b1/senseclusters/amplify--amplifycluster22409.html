amplify

verb
exaggerate or make something bigger
2.1 4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈæm.plə.faɪ/ /; / /ˈæm.plɪ.faɪ/ (AmE), /ˈæm.plɪ.faɪ/ /; / /ˈæm.plə.faɪ/ (BrE)
to exaggerate or make something seem larger, greater, or more important than it really is

Examples
