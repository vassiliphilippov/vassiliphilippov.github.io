genus Aramus

noun
genus of wading birds, includes the limpkin
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈʤiːnəs əˈræməs / (AmE), / ˈʤiːnəs əˈræməs / (BrE)
A genus of large brown long-billed wading birds found in warm swampy regions of the Western Hemisphere, including the limpkin.
