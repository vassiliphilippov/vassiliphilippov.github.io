Scolopendrium

noun
a genus of ferns
Pronunciation: / skoʊləˈpɛndrɪəm / (AmE), / skɒləˈpɛndrɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of ferns in the family Polypodiaceae, sometimes included in the genus Asplenium, known for their undivided, strap-shaped fronds.

Examples

Scolopendrium fern leaves
Scolopendrium species are prized for their simple, tongue-shaped leaves.
Many botanists now classify Scolopendrium within the broader genus Asplenium.
The hart's-tongue fern belongs to the genus Scolopendrium.
