stairs

noun
set of steps between floors
1.0 0.51/m

A set of steps that allows people to go from one level of a building to another.

Examples

Wooden stairs
Please be careful going down the stairs when carrying heavy boxes.
She ran quickly up the stairs to answer the phone.
The old house has a creaky wooden stairs leading to the attic.

Synonyms

  • staircase
    1.0 8.89/m
    staircase is a built, often indoor structure with a banister and landing, while stairs is the general word for steps.
  • stairway
    1.0 4.84/m
    stairway is another neutral term often used in buildings or signs, while stairs is the common everyday name for steps.
  • flight of stairs
    1.6 2.23/m
    flight of stairs is one continuous set between two floors, while stairs can mean any steps connecting different levels.
  • steps
    1.0 0.00/m
    steps are individual or an outside set used to reach a door, while stairs refers to the full indoor set between floors.
