mail order

noun
buying or selling by mail
3.4
Pronunciation: / meɪl ˈɔrdər / (AmE), / meɪl ˈɔːdə / (BrE)
the activity of buying or selling goods by mail, especially when goods are ordered from a catalog and delivered to the customer

Examples

Mail order business
Many people used to buy clothes through mail order before the rise of online shopping.
She received the gardening tools she purchased via mail order last week.
The company specializes in selling rare books by mail order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: catalog shopping - shopping by selecting items from a printed or online catalog
