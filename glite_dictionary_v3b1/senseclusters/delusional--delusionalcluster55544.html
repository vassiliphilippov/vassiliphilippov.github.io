delusional

adjective
suffering from delusions
Pronunciation: / dɪˈluːʒənl̩ / (AmE), / dɪˈluːʒənəl / (BrE)
Suffering from or characterized by delusions; having false or unrealistic beliefs or opinions.

Examples

Delusional belief
His delusional belief that everyone was plotting against him worried his friends.
It's delusional to think that success requires no effort.
The patient was described as delusional by the psychiatrist after claiming he could fly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paranoid - characterized by excessive suspicion or distrust
  • antonym: rational - based on or in accordance with reason or logic
