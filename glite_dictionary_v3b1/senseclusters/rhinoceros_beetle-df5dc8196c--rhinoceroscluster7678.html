rhinoceros beetle

noun
large tropical beetle with horn-like projections
7.6 6.3
Pronunciation: /raɪ.ˈnɑː.sə.rəs ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /raɪ.ˈnɒs.ə.rəs ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
any of several large tropical beetles of the subfamily Dynastinae, known for their horn-like projections on the head, some of which are pests on crops such as coconuts.

Examples

The rhinoceros beetle horn
Children were fascinated by the giant rhinoceros beetle crawling across the forest floor.
Farmers often consider the rhinoceros beetle a major pest because it damages coconut palms.
The rhinoceros beetle is famous for its impressive strength and distinctive horn.
