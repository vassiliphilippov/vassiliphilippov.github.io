Synonyms

  • amalgamation
    7.5 0.3/m
    amalgamation is the process of joining two or more organizations into one, while merger is the formal combining of companies.
  • consolidation
    5.5 2/m
    consolidation often means combining parts to make a stronger, simpler whole, while merger is the act of companies joining.
  • unification
    4.3 1/m
    unification is bringing separate groups or states together to form one body, while merger usually refers to companies combining.
  • fusion
    4.1 2/m
    fusion describes blending two things into one style or form, often outside business, while merger is a legal joining of companies.
  • uniting
    3.5 0.1/m
    uniting means joining people or things together for any purpose, while merger specifically means companies legally joining into one.
