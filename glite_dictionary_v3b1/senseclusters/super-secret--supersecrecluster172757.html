super-secret

noun
extremely confidential item
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpər ˈsiːkrət / (AmE), / ˈsuːpə ˈsiːkrɪt / (BrE)
a piece of information, document, or plan that is kept extremely confidential and hidden from most people

Examples

A super-secret file
The government stored the super-secret in a secure vault deep underground.
Only a few trusted employees had access to the super-secret during the project.
Rumors spread quickly after the super-secret was accidentally leaked to the press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classified - officially designated as confidential or restricted
  • antonym: public - openly known or accessible to everyone
  • degree lower: secret - something kept hidden or unknown from others
