firefight

noun
gun battle
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈfaɪr.faɪt/ /; / /ˈfaɪ.ər.faɪt/ (AmE), /ˈfaɪ.ə.faɪt/ (BrE)
a battle or exchange of gunfire between opposing armed groups

Examples

A fierce firefight
The soldiers were caught in a fierce firefight that lasted for over an hour.
After the ambush, a sudden firefight erupted between the patrol and the attackers.
Civilians took cover as a firefight broke out in the city streets.
The soldiers were caught in a fierce firefight near the border.
After a long firefight, the police managed to arrest the suspects.
Several buildings were damaged during the firefight in the city center.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ceasefire - an agreement to stop fighting
  • synonym: shootout - a fight in which people shoot at each other with guns
  • synonym: skirmish - a brief and unplanned fight between small groups
