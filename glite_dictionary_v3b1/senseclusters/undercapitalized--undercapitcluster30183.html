undercapitalized

adjective
lacking funds
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈkæpɪtəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌndəkˈæpɪtəlaɪzd / (BrE)
lacking sufficient financial resources to operate effectively or grow

Examples

Undercapitalized business
The undercapitalized company struggled to pay its employees on time.
Many small businesses remain undercapitalized during their first few years.
An undercapitalized restaurant may not survive unexpected expenses or slow seasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insolvent - unable to pay debts owed
  • antonym: well-funded - having plenty of financial resources
