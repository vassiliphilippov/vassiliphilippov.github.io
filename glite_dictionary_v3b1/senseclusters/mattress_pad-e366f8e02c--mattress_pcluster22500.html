mattress pad

noun
protective cover for a mattress
Pronunciation: / ˈmætrəs pæd / (AmE), / ˈmætrəs pad / (BrE)
a thin protective covering placed on top of a mattress, usually for added comfort or to protect the mattress

Examples

A mattress pad protector
Spilling coffee on your mattress pad is much better than ruining the mattress itself.
The hotel replaces every mattress pad between guests to ensure cleanliness.
We bought a soft mattress pad to make the guest bed more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mattress topper - a layer added for extra comfort or support on a mattress
