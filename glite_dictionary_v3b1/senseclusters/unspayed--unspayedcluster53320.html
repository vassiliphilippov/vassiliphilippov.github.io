unspayed

adjective
not sterilized (female animal)
7.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspeɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspeɪd / (BrE)
describes a female animal that has not been sterilized by surgical removal of the ovaries

Examples

Unspayed female dog
The veterinarian recommended that all unspayed dogs be brought in for surgery.
Shelters often separate unspayed female cats from males to prevent unwanted litters.
It is important to keep unspayed pets indoors to avoid accidental breeding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intact - an animal that has not been sterilized
  • antonym: spayed - a female animal that has been sterilized by removing the ovaries
