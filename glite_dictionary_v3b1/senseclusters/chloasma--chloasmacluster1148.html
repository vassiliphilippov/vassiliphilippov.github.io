chloasma

noun
tan facial discoloration in women
Pronunciation: / kloʊˈæzmə / (AmE), / kləʊˈazmə / (BrE)
A tan or brown discoloration of a woman's face, often associated with pregnancy or the use of oral contraceptives.

Examples

Facial chloasma spots
Many pregnant women develop chloasma due to hormonal changes.
The dermatologist diagnosed her facial spots as chloasma.
Treatment for chloasma usually involves topical creams and sun protection.
