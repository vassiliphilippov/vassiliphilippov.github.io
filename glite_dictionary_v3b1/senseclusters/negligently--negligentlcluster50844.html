negligently

adverb
in a careless manner
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡ.lɪ.dʒənt.li / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡ.lɪ.dʒənt.li / (BrE)
In a careless or irresponsible manner, failing to take proper care in doing something.

Examples

To negligently leave things
She negligently left the front door unlocked all night.
The company acted negligently in failing to address the safety hazard.
The contractor was sued for completing the repairs negligently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carelessly - in a way that shows no care or attention
  • antonym: meticulously - in a way that shows great attention to detail
