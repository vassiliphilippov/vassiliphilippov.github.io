California stop

noun
failing to completely stop at a stop sign
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪfɔrnjə stɑp / (AmE), / kəˈlɪfəˌniːə stɒp / (BrE)
an instance of slowing down but not coming to a complete stop at a stop sign or traffic signal, typically in violation of traffic laws

Examples

A California stop at sign
Drivers sometimes make a California stop late at night when there's little traffic.
He didn't notice the camera catching his California stop at the four-way stop sign.
The police officer pulled her over for a California stop at the intersection.
