whistle buoy

noun
buoy that emits a whistling sound for navigation
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsəl ˌbuːi / (AmE), / ˈwɪsl ˌbɔɪ / (BrE)
a buoy equipped with a device that makes a whistling sound, used as a navigational aid at sea

Examples

A whistle buoy sound
In heavy fog, the sound of the whistle buoy alerted sailors to nearby hazards.
Maintenance crews checked the whistle buoy to ensure its signal was loud and clear.
The captain spotted a whistle buoy marking the entrance to the harbor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bell buoy - a buoy with a bell that rings as a navigational aid
