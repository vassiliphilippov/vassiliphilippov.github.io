notebook

noun
book for writing notes
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊtˌbʊk / (AmE), / ˈnəʊtˌbʊk / (BrE)
A book with blank or lined pages used for writing notes, memoranda, or other information.

Examples

A paper notebook
He found an old notebook from college in his desk drawer.
Please bring a notebook and a pen to the meeting.
She filled her notebook with ideas for her new novel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pad - a stack of blank or lined sheets for writing or drawing
