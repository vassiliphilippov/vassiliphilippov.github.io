microsystem

noun
small ecological community
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɪstəm / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌsɪstəm / (BrE)
a small, distinct ecological community or habitat within a larger ecosystem, supporting specific plants, animals, or interactions

Examples

Pond water microsystem
A single drop of pond water can contain a complex microsystem of bacteria and algae.
Scientists studied the microsystem created by mosses and insects on the forest floor.
Each tide pool is a unique microsystem supporting its own community of marine life.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: ecosystem - a community of living organisms and their environment functioning as a system
  • synonym: habitat - the natural home or environment of an organism
