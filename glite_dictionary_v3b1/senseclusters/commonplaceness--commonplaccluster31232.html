commonplaceness

noun
quality of being ordinary or commonplace
5.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ëÀê.m…ônÀåple…™s.n…ôs / (AmE), / Ààk…í.m…ônÀåple…™s.n…ôs / (BrE)
The quality of being ordinary or unremarkable due to being frequently encountered or lacking originality.

Examples

The commonplaceness of life
He was frustrated by the commonplaceness of daily routines that offered no challenge or excitement.
Her poetry deliberately embraced the commonplaceness of urban life to find beauty in the ordinary.
The commonplaceness of the design made it easy to overlook among more creative entries.
