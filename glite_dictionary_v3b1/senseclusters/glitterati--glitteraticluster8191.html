glitterati

noun
famous fashionable people
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlɪtəˈrɑːti / (AmE), / ˌɡlɪtəˈrɑːti / (BrE)
famous and fashionable people who are part of an elite social group, often seen at glamorous events

Examples

The glitterati attend parties
The glitterati filled the ballroom, dressed in designer gowns and tuxedos.
Paparazzi waited outside the club, hoping to photograph the arriving glitterati.
At fashion week, the glitterati are always in the front row.
The film festival was attended by the glitterati of the fashion and entertainment industries.
At the exclusive party, the city's glitterati mingled with international guests.
Magazines often feature photos of the glitterati at glamorous events around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrity - a famous person, especially in entertainment or sport
  • antonym: commoner - an ordinary person without special status or fame
  • synonym: jet set - wealthy and fashionable people who travel frequently for pleasure
