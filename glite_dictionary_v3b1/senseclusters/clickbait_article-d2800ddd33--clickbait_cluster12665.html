clickbait article

noun
article with sensational headline meant to attract clicks
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪkˌbeɪt ˈɑrtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈklɪkbeɪt ˈɑːtɪkl̩ / (BrE)
an online article designed to attract attention and encourage people to click on a link, often using sensational or misleading headlines

Examples

A clickbait article
She refuses to share clickbait articles because they often mislead readers.
That website is filled with clickbait articles that rarely deliver on their promises.
The rise of clickbait articles has made it harder to find credible news online.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clickbait - content designed to attract attention and clicks
