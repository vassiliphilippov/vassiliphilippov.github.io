antiwear

adjective
reducing wear
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈwɛr / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈwɛə / (BrE)
designed to reduce or prevent damage or deterioration caused by friction or use

Examples

Antiwear coating
The manufacturer recommends using antiwear oil to extend the life of your car's engine.
Special antiwear coatings are applied to machine parts to prevent early failure.
This lubricant contains antiwear additives that help protect moving components from friction.
