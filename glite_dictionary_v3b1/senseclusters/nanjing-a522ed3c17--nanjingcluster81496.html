Nanjing

proper noun
city in eastern China, former Chinese capital
Pronunciation: / nænˈdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / nanˈdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
A city in eastern China on the Yangtze River, formerly the capital of China and the site of a major historical massacre during the 1930s.

Examples

Nanjing city in China
During the 1930s, Nanjing endured a tragic period known as the Nanjing Massacre.

The ancient walls of Nanjing attract tourists from all over the world.
