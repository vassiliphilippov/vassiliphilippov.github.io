program

noun
schedule of events or participants at an event
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊɡræm /; / ˈproʊ.ɡræm / (AmE), / ˈprəʊɡræm /; / ˈprəʊ.ɡræm / (BrE)
a printed or written list or schedule of events, performances, or items at an event
