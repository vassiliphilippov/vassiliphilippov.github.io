devoted follower

noun
loyal supporter or adherent
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvoʊtɪd ˈfɑloʊər / (AmE), / dɪˈvəʊtɪd ˈfɒləʊə / (BrE)
A person who is extremely loyal and enthusiastic in supporting someone or something, such as a leader, cause, or religion.

Examples

A devoted follower of a band
As a devoted follower of the band, she attended every concert on their tour.
He became a devoted follower of the new religious movement, dedicating much of his time to its cause.
The politician's devoted followers cheered loudly during his speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adherent - someone who supports a particular person, party, or set of ideas
  • synonym: disciple - a follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosophy
  • degree higher: fanatic - a person filled with excessive zeal, especially for an extreme religious or political cause
