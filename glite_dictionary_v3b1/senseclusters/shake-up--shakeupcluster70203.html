shake-up

noun
major reorganization or restructuring
Pronunciation: / ˈʃeɪkˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈʃeɪkˌʌp / (BrE)
a major reorganization or restructuring, often involving extensive and sometimes drastic changes

Examples

A shake-up in government
After the scandal, there was a major shake-up in the government’s top ranks.
The company announced a shake-up of its entire management structure to boost performance.
The school is undergoing a shake-up to improve academic standards and efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overhaul - a thorough examination and repair or improvement of a system
  • synonym: reorganization - the act or process of changing the way in which something is organized
