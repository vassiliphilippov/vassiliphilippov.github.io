tinkling

adjective
having a high, bell-like ringing sound
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
making or having a series of short, high, ringing sounds like those of a small bell

Examples

A tinkling stream
A tinkling stream ran past the old cottage, creating a peaceful atmosphere.
The tinkling of wind chimes could be heard in the gentle breeze.
There was a tinkling laughter that filled the quiet room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinking - making a series of short, sharp, ringing sounds (like glass or metal)
