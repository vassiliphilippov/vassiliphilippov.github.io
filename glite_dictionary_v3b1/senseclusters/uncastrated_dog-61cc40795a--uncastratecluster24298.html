uncastrated dog

noun
male dog not neutered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæstreɪtɪd dɔg / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæstrətɪd dɒg / (BrE)
a male dog that has not been neutered

Examples

An uncastrated dog shows behavior
An uncastrated dog may display more territorial behavior than a neutered one.
Local regulations require that all pets, except uncastrated dogs used for breeding, must be sterilized.
The shelter prefers to adopt out only uncastrated dogs to experienced owners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: neutered dog - a dog that has been castrated or spayed
