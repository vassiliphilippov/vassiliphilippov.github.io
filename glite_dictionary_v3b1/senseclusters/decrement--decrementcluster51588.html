decrement

noun
amount of decrease or reduction
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛkrəmənt /; / ˈdɛkrɪˌmɛnt / (AmE), / ˈdɛkrɪmənt / (BrE)
an amount by which something is reduced, or the process of becoming smaller or less

Examples

A decrement in value
A steady decrement in resources could threaten the project.
If there is a decrement in sales, we must investigate the cause.
The decrement in temperature overnight was noticeable.
Regular exercise can slow the decrement in muscle mass that comes with aging.
The company noticed a significant decrement in sales over the last quarter.
There was a steady decrement in the amount of available water during the drought.

Related Terms

  • synonym: decrease - a process of becoming smaller or less
  • antonym: increase - the amount by which something increases
  • synonym: reduction - the act or process of making something smaller or less
