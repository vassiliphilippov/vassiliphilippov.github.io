Synonyms

  • decrease
    2.2 4/m
    a decrease is a general reduction or amount lost, while a decrement refers especially to a measured or counted drop.
  • reduction
    3.2 20/m
    reduction means making something smaller or less in amount, while a decrement names a specific measured amount of that change.
  • diminution
    7.9 0.2/m
    diminution means a formal reduction in size, amount, or importance, while a decrement focuses on the single amount reduced.
  • diminishment
    6.7 0.2/m
    diminishment is the process of becoming smaller or weaker, while a decrement is the amount or drop produced by that process.
  • shrinkage
    5.1 0.2/m
    shrinkage is the loss in size, amount, or value often of physical things, while a decrement is any measured reduction.
  • lessening
    3.8 0.2/m
    lessening is a general decrease in amount, degree, or intensity, while a decrement is the specific amount of that decrease.
  • lowering
    2.8 0.00/m
    lowering is an action that makes something go down in level or amount, while a decrement names the resulting amount.
  • abatement
    6.8 0.5/m
    abatement is a formal reduction, often of harm or cost, while a decrement is a neutral amount by which something gets smaller.
