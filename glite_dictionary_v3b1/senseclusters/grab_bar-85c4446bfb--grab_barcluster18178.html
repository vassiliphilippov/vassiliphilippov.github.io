grab bar

noun
support bar fixed to a wall
6.5
Pronunciation: / ɡræb bɑr / (AmE), / ɡræb bɑː / (BrE)
a strong bar attached to a wall, especially in bathrooms, to provide support and help steady yourself

Examples

Bathroom grab bar
He slipped on the wet floor and reached for the grab bar to prevent a fall.
Installing a grab bar near the toilet is recommended for improved bathroom safety.
The elderly woman used the grab bar in the shower to steady herself while bathing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handrail - a rail fixed to posts or a wall for people to hold on to, especially on stairs
