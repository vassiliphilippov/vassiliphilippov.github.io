okoume

noun
African hardwood
Pronunciation: / oʊˈkuːmeɪ / (AmE), / əʊˈkuːmeɪ /; / əˈkuːmeɪ / (BrE)
a tall African tree and its lightweight wood, often used in making plywood, boats, and furniture
