peach

noun
a soft, sweet stone fruit
Pronunciation: / pitʃ / (AmE), / piːtʃ / (BrE)
a round, soft fruit with sweet yellow or white flesh, a slightly furry skin, and a stone in the center

Examples

A juicy peach
He added slices of peach to the fruit salad for extra sweetness.
She bit into a ripe peach and let the juice run down her chin.
The orchard behind our house is famous for its peach trees.
A warm peach cobbler is one of my favorite desserts.
She sliced a ripe peach and added it to her fruit salad.
The tree in our backyard produces delicious peaches every summer.
