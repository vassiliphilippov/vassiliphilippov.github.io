worst

adjective
poorest in quality, value, or condition
Pronunciation: / wɝst / (AmE), / wɜːst / (BrE)
of the poorest quality, value, or condition; superlative form of 'bad.'

Examples

The worst storm
During the storm, conditions reached their worst just after midnight.
Out of all the candidates, he was considered the worst choice for the job.
That was the worst movie I have seen all year.
