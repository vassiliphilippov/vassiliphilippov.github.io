deep discussion

noun
serious or thorough conversation
Pronunciation: / dip dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / diːp dɪˈskʌʃən / (BrE)
a conversation that is serious, thorough, and often focused on important or complex topics

Examples

A deep discussion about problems
A deep discussion sometimes helps people resolve difficult personal issues.
During dinner, they engaged in a deep discussion about philosophy and life's purpose.
The team had a deep discussion about the company's future direction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conversation - an informal talk between two or more people
