hairy

adjective
covered with hair
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛri / (AmE), / ˈheəri / (BrE)
having a lot of hair on the body or surface; covered with hair.

Examples

Hairy arms
Despite the summer heat, he wore a sweater to hide his hairy arms.
Some plants have hairy leaves to help protect them from insects.
The hairy caterpillar crawled slowly along the branch.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hairless - lacking hair; without any hair
