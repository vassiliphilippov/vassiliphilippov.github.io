dun

noun
debt collector
9.3
Pronunciation: / dʌn / (AmE), / dʌn / (BrE)
a person who repeatedly demands payment of a debt

Examples

A dun at the door
The dun visited the office every week to remind them about the unpaid bill.
After months of calls from the dun, he finally settled his debt.
She was tired of hearing from the persistent dun about her overdue account.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collector - a person who collects debts or payments
  • antonym: debtor - a person who owes money
