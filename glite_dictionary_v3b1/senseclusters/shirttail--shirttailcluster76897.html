shirttail

noun
lower part of a shirt below the waist
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɝːtˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌteɪl / (BrE)
The lower, usually curved or pointed, part of a shirt that hangs below the waist and is often tucked into trousers or a skirt.

Examples

Tuck in your shirttail
He tucked his shirttail neatly into his pants before leaving the house.
His shirttail kept coming untucked during the meeting.
She noticed a stain near the bottom of her shirttail.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hem - the edge of a piece of cloth that is folded over and sewn
