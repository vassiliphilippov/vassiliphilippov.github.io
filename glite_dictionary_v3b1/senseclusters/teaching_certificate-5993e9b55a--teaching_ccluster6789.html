teaching certificate

noun
certificate qualifying a person to teach
Pronunciation: / ˈtitʃɪŋ sərˌtɪfɪkət / (AmE), / ˈtiːtʃɪŋ səˈtɪfɪkət / (BrE)
an official document showing that someone is qualified to teach in public schools

Examples

A teaching certificate application
After graduating, she applied for a teaching certificate to qualify for public school positions.
His teaching certificate expired, so he had to complete additional coursework to renew it.
You must have a valid teaching certificate to work as a teacher in most states.
