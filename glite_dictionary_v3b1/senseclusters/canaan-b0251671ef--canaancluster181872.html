Canaan

proper noun
ancient region in the eastern Mediterranean
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪnən / (AmE), / ˈkeɪnən / (BrE)
an ancient region in the eastern Mediterranean, located roughly in present-day Israel and surrounding areas, with significant importance in biblical history.

Examples

Ancient Canaan region
Archaeological findings in Canaan shed light on the early civilizations of the region.
In the Bible, the land of Canaan is described as the Promised Land for the Israelites.
Pilgrims from around the world have visited sites that once belonged to Canaan.
