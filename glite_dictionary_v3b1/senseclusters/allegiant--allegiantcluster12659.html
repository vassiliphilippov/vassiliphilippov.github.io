allegiant

adjective
loyal and devoted
Pronunciation: / əˈlidʒənt / (AmE), / əˈliːdʒənt / (BrE)
loyal and steadfast in devotion, especially to a ruler, government, or cause.

Examples

Allegiant to the king
As a knight, he remained allegiant to his king even in difficult times.
Despite the political turmoil, she stayed allegiant to her beliefs.
Many citizens felt allegiant to their country's traditions and values.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disloyal - not loyal or devoted
  • synonym: faithful - remaining true and devoted
  • synonym: loyal - showing strong support or allegiance
