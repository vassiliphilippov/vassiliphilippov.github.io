handload

verb
to assemble ammunition
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌloʊd / (AmE), / ˈhændˌləʊd / (BrE)
to assemble ammunition by hand, rather than using factory-made cartridges

Examples

To handload ammunition
Many shooting enthusiasts choose to handload their own ammunition for better control over performance.
He learned how to handload cartridges to save money and customize his loads.
Some hunters prefer to handload because it allows them to tailor ammunition to their specific needs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manufacture - to make something, especially on a large scale
  • synonym: reload - to refill spent ammunition cases with new components
