ribboning

noun
formation of streaks
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪb.ən.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrɪb.ən.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the formation of long, narrow bands or streaks on a surface or material, often as an unwanted effect in manufacturing or printing

Examples

Metal ribboning
The technician noticed ribboning on the metal sheet after it passed through the rollers.
Poor ink distribution can cause ribboning in printed photographs, resulting in visible lines.
To avoid ribboning, the machine must be properly calibrated before starting the production run.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banding - the appearance of stripes or bands on a surface
  • synonym: streaking - the presence of lines or marks differing in color or texture
