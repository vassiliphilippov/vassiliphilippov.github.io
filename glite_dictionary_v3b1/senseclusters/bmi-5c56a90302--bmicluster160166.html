BMI

noun
body mass index (weight-to-height ratio)
3.5 2.47/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåbiÀê Àå…õm Ààa…™ / (AmE), / ÀåbiÀê Àå…õm Ààa…™ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for body mass index, a numerical value of a person's weight in relation to their height, used to assess whether they are underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese.

Examples

BMI value
A BMI above 25 is considered overweight by health professionals.
Many fitness apps can help you track your BMI over time.
The doctor calculated my BMI to determine if I was at a healthy weight.

Synonyms

  • body mass index
    3.2 0.56/m
