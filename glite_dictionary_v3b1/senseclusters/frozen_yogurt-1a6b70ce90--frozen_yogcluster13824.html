frozen yogurt

noun
soft frozen yogurt dessert
Pronunciation: / ˈfroʊzən ˈjoʊɡɚt / (AmE), / ˈfrəʊzən ˈjəʊɡət / (BrE)
A soft, frozen dessert made from sweetened and flavored yogurt, often served as an alternative to ice cream.

Examples

A cup of frozen yogurt
She prefers frozen yogurt over traditional ice cream because it tastes lighter.
The shop offers a variety of frozen yogurt flavors with unlimited toppings.
We stopped at a café to get some frozen yogurt on a hot afternoon.
