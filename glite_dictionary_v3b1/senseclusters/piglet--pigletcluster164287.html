piglet

noun
young pig
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪɡ.lət / (AmE), / ˈpɪɡ.lɪt / (BrE)
a young pig, especially one that is not yet weaned

Examples

A piglet playing
Children gathered around to watch the piglets play in the straw.
Raising a piglet requires special care and attention.
The farmer carefully carried the newborn piglet back to its mother.
