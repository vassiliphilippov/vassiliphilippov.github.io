papism

noun
doctrines and practices of the Roman Catholic Church
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪˌpɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpɪzəm / (BrE)
(historical, often offensive) an old, offensive term for the beliefs and practices of the Roman Catholic Church, especially as used by its opponents
