Tigrinya

noun
language of Eritrea, Ethiopia
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɪˈɡrɪnjə / (AmE), / tɪˈɡrɪnjə / (BrE)

Examples

Tigrinya language

She took a course to improve her Tigrinya writing skills.
The book was translated from Tigrinya into English for a wider audience.
