hurtful

adjective
causing pain or distress
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜrtfəl / (AmE), / ˈhɜːtf(ə)l / (BrE)
causing pain, harm, or distress; likely to hurt someone, especially emotionally

Examples

Hurtful words

Sometimes, even small jokes can be unintentionally hurtful.
The teacher warned the students not to use hurtful language in class.
