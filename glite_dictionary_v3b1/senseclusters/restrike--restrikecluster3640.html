restrike

verb
to mint again
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈstraɪk / (AmE), / ˌriːˈstraɪk / (BrE)
to strike a coin, medal, or similar item again after the original minting, often for collectors or special issues

Examples

To restrike a coin
The mint decided to restrike the commemorative coins for collectors.
Due to high demand, the museum authorized a limited restrike of the historic medal.
Some collectors prefer original coins, while others are happy with a modern restrike.

Related Terms

  • synonym: strike - to produce a coin or medal by stamping metal
