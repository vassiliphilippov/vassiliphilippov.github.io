protegee

noun
female protege; supported woman
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ.təˈʒeɪ / (AmE), / ˈprəʊ.tə.ʒeɪ / (BrE)
A woman who is guided and supported by an older and more experienced person.

Examples

A protegee of mentor
As his protegee, she received personalized guidance and opportunities he rarely offered others.
The conference featured a keynote by Dr. Day, once a protegee of pioneering neuroscientist Marie Curie.
The young artist was a protegee of the famous sculptor, learning valuable skills from her mentor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protege - a person who is guided and supported by another, especially in career or personal development
