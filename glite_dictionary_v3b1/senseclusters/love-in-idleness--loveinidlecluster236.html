love-in-idleness

noun
a European wildflower; ancestor of garden pansies
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌv ɪn ˈaɪdlnɪs / (AmE), / ˌlʌv ɪn ˈaɪdlnɪs / (BrE)
a common European wildflower (Viola tricolor) with purple, yellow, and white flowers, from which most common garden pansies are derived
