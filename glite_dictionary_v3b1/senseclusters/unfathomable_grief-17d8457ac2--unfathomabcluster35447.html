unfathomable grief

noun
profound, incomprehensible sorrow
7.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfæðəməbəl ɡriːf / (AmE), / ʌnˈfaðəməbəl ɡriːf / (BrE)
a sorrow that is so deep and intense it cannot be fully understood or measured

Examples

Unfathomable grief after loss
He was consumed by unfathomable grief after the tragedy struck his family.
The novel explores a mother's journey through unfathomable grief after losing her child.
The sight of the destroyed home filled her with unfathomable grief.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anguish - severe mental or physical pain or suffering
  • synonym: grief - intense sorrow, especially caused by someone's death
  • antonym: unfathomable joy - joy that is impossible to measure or fully comprehend
