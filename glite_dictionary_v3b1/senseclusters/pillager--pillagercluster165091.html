pillager

noun
someone who loots or plunders, usually in war
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪlədʒər / (AmE), / ˈpɪlɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person who steals goods, especially during war or by force; a looter or plunderer

Examples

pillagers after battle
After the battle, pillagers roamed the countryside, searching for valuables.
History books describe the ruthless pillager who burned the village to the ground.
The ancient city fell to the pillager, its treasures scattered and lost.

Related Terms

  • synonym: looter - a person who takes goods illegally, especially in times of chaos
  • synonym: plunderer - a person who steals goods, especially by force during war
