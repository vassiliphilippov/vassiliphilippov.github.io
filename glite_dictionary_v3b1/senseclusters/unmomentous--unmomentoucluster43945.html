unmomentous

adjective
not significant
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnmoʊˈmɛntəs / (AmE), / ˌʌnməʊˈmɛntəs / (BrE)
not important or significant; lacking great consequence or impact

Examples

An unmomentous event
The announcement turned out to be unmomentous and did not affect the company's plans.
Most of the changes in the document were unmomentous and went unnoticed by the team.
Despite the excitement, the event was rather unmomentous and quickly forgotten.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insignificant - not important or worthy of attention
  • antonym: momentous - very important or significant
  • synonym: trivial - of little value or importance
