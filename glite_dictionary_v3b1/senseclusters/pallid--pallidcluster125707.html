pallid

adjective
pale or sickly in appearance
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpælɪd / (AmE), / ˈpælɪd / (BrE)
(of a person's face) pale, typically because of poor health, shock, or emotional distress.

Examples

Pallid face
After the fright, her cheeks turned pallid with shock.
She noticed his pallid complexion as soon as he entered the room.
The patient's pallid face worried the attending nurse.

  • synonym: wan - pale and giving the impression of illness or exhaustion
