upsetment

noun
state of being upset
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛtmənt / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛtmənt / (BrE)
the act or state of being upset, disturbed, or emotionally unsettled

Examples

Widespread upsetment
The sudden cancellation of the event caused widespread upsetment among the participants.
She tried to hide her upsetment after hearing the disappointing news.
There was a sense of upsetment in the office after the announcement of layoffs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of being peaceful and free from disturbance
  • synonym: distress - a state of extreme worry, pain, or sadness
