pelletization

noun
making pellets
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛlɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɛlɪtəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of forming or compressing materials into small, rounded pellets

Examples

The pelletization process
The pelletization of animal feed makes it easier to store and transport.
Modern factories use automated systems for the pelletization of plastic materials.
Efficient pelletization helps reduce waste in the production process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compaction - the process of compressing material to reduce its volume
  • synonym: granulation - the process of forming materials into grains or granules
