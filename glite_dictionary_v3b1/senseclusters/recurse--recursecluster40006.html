recurse

verb
to happen again
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɜrs / (AmE), / rɪˈkɜːs / (BrE)
to occur again or happen repeatedly, especially in a rare or archaic sense.

Examples

Problems recurse often
If the same error should recurse, please notify the system administrator immediately.
In rare cases, the symptoms may recurse after initial treatment.
The legend says that the haunting will recurse every hundred years.
