rosiness

noun
pinkish glow, especially in cheeks
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊzi.nɪs /; / ˈroʊzinəs / (AmE), / ˈrəʊzi.nɪs /; / ˈrəʊzinəs /; / ˈrəʊzinɪs / (BrE)
a pinkish or reddish color or glow, especially in the cheeks, often showing health or cheerfulness
