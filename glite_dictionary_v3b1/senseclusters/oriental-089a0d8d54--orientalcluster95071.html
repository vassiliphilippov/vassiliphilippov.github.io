Oriental

noun
offensive slur for East Asian
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔriˈɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːriˈɛntəl / (BrE)
an offensive term formerly used to refer to a person from East Asia; now considered inappropriate and disrespectful

Examples
