prospectless

adjective
without prospects
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːspɛktləs / (AmE), / ˈprɒspɛktləs / (BrE)
having no prospects, hope, or chance for improvement or success

Examples

A prospectless future
After years of drought, the farmland looked prospectless and abandoned.
He felt trapped in a prospectless job with no chance of promotion.
The team's situation seemed prospectless after losing their best player.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopeless - having no hope or chance of success
  • antonym: promising - showing signs of future success
