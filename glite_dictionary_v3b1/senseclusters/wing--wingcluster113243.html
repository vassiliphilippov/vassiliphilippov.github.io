wing

noun
aircraft position in formation
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ / (BrE)
A position to the side and just behind another aircraft in a flight formation.

Examples

Wing position in formation
He kept his wing position during the entire training exercise.
Military pilots are trained to hold a tight formation on the wing of their leader.
The lead fighter took position, while the second jet flew on its wing.
