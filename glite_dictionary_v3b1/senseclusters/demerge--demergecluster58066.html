demerge

verb
to reverse a merge
Pronunciation: / diˈmɜrdʒ / (AmE), / diˈmɜːdʒ / (BrE)
to undo or reverse a merge operation, especially by separating previously combined branches, records, or datasets in computing or data management.

Examples

To demerge data
After the faulty update, the team had to demerge the commit that combined two feature branches.
The software allows users to demerge datasets if they were merged by mistake.
It took several hours to demerge the records and restore the original data structure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: merge - to combine two or more things into one
  • synonym: separate - to divide or split into different parts
