pillow slip

noun
cloth cover for a pillow
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪloʊ slɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪləʊ slɪp / (BrE)
a removable cloth cover for a pillow, used to protect it and provide a clean surface for sleeping

Examples

A white pillow slip

She changed the pillow slip on her bed every week for freshness.
The guest house provided clean pillow slips and towels for all visitors.
