mousy

adjective
dull pale brown color (like a mouse)
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsi / (AmE), / ˈmaʊzi / (BrE)
having a dull, pale brown or brownish-grey color, especially used to describe hair or fabric

Examples

Mousy brown hair
He chose a mousy brown paint for the living room walls, giving it a muted look.
Her mousy hair made her seem almost invisible in the crowd.
The coat was a mousy color that faded even more with age.
He wore a mousy brown jacket that blended into the background.
She always wished her mousy hair could be a more striking color.
The walls were painted a dull, mousy shade that made the room feel gloomy.
