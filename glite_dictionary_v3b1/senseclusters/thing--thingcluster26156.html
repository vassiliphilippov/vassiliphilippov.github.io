thing

noun
irrational desire or aversion
Pronunciation: / θɪŋ / (AmE), / θɪŋ / (BrE)
an often irrational or persistent feeling of desire or aversion about someone or something

Examples

Have a thing for cats
He can't stand dogs; it's just a thing he has.
I've always had a thing for classic cars.
She has a thing about keeping her desk perfectly tidy.
