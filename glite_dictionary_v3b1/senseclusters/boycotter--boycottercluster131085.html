boycotter

noun
protest participant
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔɪˌkɑtər / (AmE), / ˈbɔɪˌkɒtə / (BrE)
a person who refuses to buy, use, or take part in something as a protest or to show disapproval

Several boycotters stood outside the store handing out flyers to raise awareness.
