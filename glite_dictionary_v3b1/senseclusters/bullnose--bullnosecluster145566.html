bullnose

noun
small carpenter's plane with front-edge blade
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌnoʊz / (AmE), / ˈbʊl.nəʊz / (BrE)
a small carpenter's plane with the cutting edge positioned very close to the front, used for trimming and finishing work in tight spaces

Examples

A bullnose plane
A bullnose is ideal for getting into tight spaces where a regular plane won't reach.
After switching to his bullnose, he was able to smooth the edge with precision.
The craftsman used a bullnose to fit the pieces perfectly into the corner.
