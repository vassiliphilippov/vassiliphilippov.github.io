resolution

noun
firm decision to act in a certain way
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən /; / ˌrɛzəˈluʃən / (AmE), / ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən / (BrE)
a firm decision or determination to do or not do something; strong will or purpose

Examples

New Year resolution
After much thought, he announced his resolution to quit smoking.
Despite many setbacks, Maria stuck to her resolution to finish writing her thesis.
She made a resolution to exercise more regularly in the new year.
Despite the criticism, his resolution to continue never faltered.
Her resolution helped her overcome all the obstacles she faced.
It took great resolution to stand up for his beliefs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determination - the quality of being determined; firmness of purpose
  • antonym: indecision - inability to make a decision or commitment
