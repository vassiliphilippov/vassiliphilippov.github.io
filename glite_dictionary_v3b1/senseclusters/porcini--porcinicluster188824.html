porcini

noun
edible wild mushroom
Pronunciation: / pɔːrˈtʃiːni / (AmE), / pɔːˈtʃiːni / (BrE)
a type of edible wild mushroom with a thick stem and rich, nutty flavor, often used in cooking

Examples

Porcini mushrooms
The risotto was full of flavor thanks to the addition of porcini mushrooms.
She bought dried porcini at the market to use in her soup recipe.
Many Italian dishes feature porcini for their distinctive taste and aroma.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boletus - a genus of mushrooms that includes porcini
  • synonym: shiitake - an edible mushroom popular in Asian cuisine
