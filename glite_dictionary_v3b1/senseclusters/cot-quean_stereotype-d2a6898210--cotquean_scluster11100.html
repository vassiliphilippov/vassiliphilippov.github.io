cot-quean stereotype

noun
stereotype of a man doing household work
Pronunciation: / kɑtˌkwin ˈstɛriəˌtaɪp / (AmE), / kɒtˌkwiːn ˈstɛriətaɪp / (BrE)
A stereotype depicting a man who does household work, traditionally considered as women's work.

Examples

The cot-quean stereotype
He fought against the cot-quean stereotype by openly discussing his role as a homemaker.
Many reject the outdated cot-quean stereotype and embrace shared household responsibilities.
The cot-quean stereotype has often been used to mock men who stay at home to care for children.

Related Terms

  • synonym: househusband - a man who manages the household while his partner works
  • synonym: stereotype - a widely held but fixed and oversimplified idea of a particular type of person
