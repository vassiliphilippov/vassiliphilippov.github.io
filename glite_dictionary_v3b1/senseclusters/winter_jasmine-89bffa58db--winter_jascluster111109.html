winter jasmine

noun
shrub with yellow winter flowers
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˈdʒæz.mɪn / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˈdʒæz.mɪn / (BrE)
A deciduous shrub, Jasminum nudiflorum, widely cultivated for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in winter.

Examples

Bright winter jasmine flowers
She planted winter jasmine along the stone wall to enjoy color during the cold months.
The winter jasmine brightened the garden with its cheerful yellow blooms in January.
Unlike other jasmines, winter jasmine flowers appear before the leaves emerge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jasmine - a flowered shrub or vine (genus Jasminum) with fragrant white or yellow blossoms
