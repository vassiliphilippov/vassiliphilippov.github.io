lepidopteron

noun
an insect of the order Lepidoptera
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈdɑptəˌrɑn / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərɒn / (BrE)
an insect of the order Lepidoptera, which in the adult stage has four wings usually covered with tiny scales; includes butterflies and moths
