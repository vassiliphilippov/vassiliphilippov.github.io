violet

noun
small plant with purple or blue flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪ.əl.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈvaɪ.əl.ɪt / (BrE)
A small low-growing plant of the genus Viola, typically having purple, blue, or white flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

Examples

Wild violet flowers
In the spring, violet flowers carpeted the woodland floor.
Many perfumes are scented with the delicate fragrance of violet.
She picked a violet from the garden and put it in her hair.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pansy - a cultivated plant of the genus Viola with large, rounded flowers
