boatkeeper

noun
person caring for boat
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈboʊt.kiː.pər/ (AmE), /ˈbəʊt.kiː.pə/ (BrE)
a person who looks after, manages, or maintains a boat, often as their job (may also rent out or operate the boat)

Examples

The boatkeeper checks boats
The boatkeeper checked the vessel every morning for safety.
A skilled boatkeeper ensures the boat is always clean and ready for use.
They hired a boatkeeper to maintain the yacht during the off-season.
The boatkeeper checked each boat for safety before allowing customers to use them.
During the summer, the boatkeeper worked long hours to meet the demand from tourists.
After a busy day, the boatkeeper carefully locked up all the boats for the night.
The boatkeeper checked the engine before every trip to ensure safety.
As a boatkeeper, she was responsible for maintaining the ferry and assisting passengers.
The old boatkeeper knew every current and hidden rock along the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caretaker - a person who takes care of something or someone
  • synonym: landlord - a person who rents out property to others
  • antonym: owner - a person who possesses something
  • synonym: skipper - the captain or person in charge of a boat
