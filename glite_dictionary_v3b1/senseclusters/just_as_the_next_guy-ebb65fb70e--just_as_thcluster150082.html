just as the next guy

x
like anyone else
Pronunciation: / dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡaɪ / (AmE), / dʒʌst æz ðə nɛkst ɡaɪ / (BrE)
used to indicate that someone is similar to others in a particular respect, usually in terms of a common interest or feeling

Examples

Feel nervous just as the next guy
I get nervous about public speaking just as the next guy does.
I'm concerned about the environment just as the next guy.
She loves a good cup of coffee just as the next guy.
