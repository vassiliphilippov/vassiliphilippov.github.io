microjump

verb
to make a tiny jump
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.dʒʌmp/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.dʒʌmp/ (BrE)

Examples

Gamers use the term microjump when describing tiny, precise movements in platform games.
