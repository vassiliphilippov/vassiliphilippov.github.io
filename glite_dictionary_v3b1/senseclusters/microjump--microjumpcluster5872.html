microjump

verb
to make a tiny jump
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪkroʊˌdʒʌmp / (AmE), / ˈmaɪkrəʊˌdʒʌmp / (BrE)
to make a very small jump or sudden short movement, especially over a short distance

Examples

To microjump forward
The robot can microjump to adjust its position on the assembly line.
To avoid the obstacle, the drone performed a quick microjump to the left.
Gamers use the term microjump when describing tiny, precise movements in platform games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hop - to make a short, quick jump
  • degree higher: leap - to jump a long distance or with force
