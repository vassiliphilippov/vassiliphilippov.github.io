jadeite

noun
a hard green mineral; source of jade
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒeɪˌdaɪt / (AmE), / ˈdʒeɪdaɪt / (BrE)
A hard, usually green mineral made of sodium aluminum silicate in monoclinic crystals, used as a source of jade.

Examples

Green jadeite mineral
Collectors often value jadeite for its vibrant green color and rarity.
She displayed a stunning necklace made entirely of polished jadeite beads.
The ancient artifacts were carved from rare jadeite found in Burma.
