indigo

noun
deep blue-violet color
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɪndɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
A deep blue-violet color, typically positioned between blue and violet in the visible spectrum.

Examples

Indigo color
He wore a shirt of deep indigo to the party.
The artist chose indigo for the night sky in her painting.
The flowers bloomed in brilliant shades of indigo and purple.
