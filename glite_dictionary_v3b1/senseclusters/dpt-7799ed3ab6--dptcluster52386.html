DPT

noun
physical therapy doctorate
Pronunciation: / di pi ti / (AmE), / diː piː tiː / (BrE)
a professional doctoral degree in physical therapy qualifying someone to practice as a physical therapist

Examples

DPT degree holder
After graduating, he proudly added DPT to his nameplate at the clinic.
To become a licensed physical therapist in the US, you usually need a DPT degree.
Her DPT program included both classroom learning and hands-on clinical experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: MD - a professional doctoral degree in medicine
  • antonym: PhD - a research-based doctoral degree
  • synonym: physical therapist - a healthcare professional specializing in physical therapy
