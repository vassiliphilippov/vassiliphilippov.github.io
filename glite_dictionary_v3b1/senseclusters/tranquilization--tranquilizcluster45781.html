tranquilization

noun
calming with medication
Pronunciation: / ˌtræŋkwəlɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtræŋkwɪlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or state of making someone or something calm or sedated, especially by using medication or tranquilizers
