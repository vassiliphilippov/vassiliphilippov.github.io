bulbar

adjective
relating to the medulla oblongata
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌl.bɑr / (AmE), / ˈbʌl.bɑː / (BrE)
relating to or involving the medulla oblongata, the lower part of the brainstem

Examples

Bulbar palsy
Bulbar palsy results from damage to motor neurons in the medulla oblongata.
Some patients with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis experience bulbar symptoms early in the disease.
The doctor noted signs of bulbar weakness in the neurological examination.
