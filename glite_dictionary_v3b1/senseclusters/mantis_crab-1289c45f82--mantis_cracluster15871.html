mantis crab

noun
tropical marine burrowing crustacean
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntɪs kræb / (AmE), / ˈmæntɪs kræb / (BrE)
A tropical marine crustacean that lives in burrows and has large, grasping appendages used for hunting.

Examples

A mantis crab hunting
A mantis crab uses its grasping appendages to catch unsuspecting prey.
Divers sometimes spot mantis crabs hiding in crevices on the reef.
The mantis crab is famous for its incredibly fast and powerful claw strikes.

  • synonym: mantis shrimp - another name for mantis crab, a burrowing marine crustacean
