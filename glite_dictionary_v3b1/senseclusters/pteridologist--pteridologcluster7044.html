pteridologist

noun
expert in the study of ferns
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrɪˈdɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌtɛrɪˈdɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who is an expert in or who studies ferns

Examples

A pteridologist studies ferns
As a renowned pteridologist, she has published many articles about fern ecology.
He became interested in botany as a child and eventually specialized as a pteridologist.
The museum hired a pteridologist to organize its collection of rare ferns.
