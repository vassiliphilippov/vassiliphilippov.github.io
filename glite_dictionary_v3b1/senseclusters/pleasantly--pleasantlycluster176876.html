pleasantly

adverb
in a friendly or good-humoured manner
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛzəntli / (AmE), / ˈplɛzəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is enjoyable, friendly, or agreeable

Examples

Respond pleasantly

She greeted her guests pleasantly and made everyone feel at home.
He was pleasantly surprised by the thoughtful gift.
She smiled pleasantly at the waiter as he brought their food.
The weather was pleasantly warm for an afternoon walk.
