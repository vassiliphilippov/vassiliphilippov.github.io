guajillo

noun
Mexican dried chili
Pronunciation: / ɡwaˈhiːjoʊ / (AmE), / ɡwaˈhiːjəʊ / (BrE)
a mild to medium dried chili pepper commonly used in Mexican cooking for its flavor and color

Examples

A guajillo pepper
The chef soaked a guajillo pepper in hot water before blending it into the sauce.
You can find dried guajillo chilies in most Latin American grocery stores.
Adding a guajillo gives the stew a rich red color and a mild heat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancho - a mild, dried poblano chili pepper used in Mexican cuisine
  • synonym: pasilla - a dried, dark chili pepper used in Mexican cooking
