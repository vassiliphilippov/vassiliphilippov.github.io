pressureless

adjective
without stress or urgency
Pronunciation: / ˈprɛʃərləs / (AmE), / ˈprɛʃələs / (BrE)
not involving stress, coercion, or urgency; relaxed and free from external pressure or demands

Examples

Pressureless interview
The interview was surprisingly pressureless, making it easy for candidates to express themselves.
She prefers a pressureless environment where creativity can flourish without deadlines.
Their conversation was open and pressureless, allowing everyone to share their honest opinions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
  • antonym: stressful - causing mental or emotional stress
