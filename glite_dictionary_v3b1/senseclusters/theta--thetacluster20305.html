theta

noun
Greek letter; used in maths and science
Pronunciation: / ˈθeɪtə / (AmE), / ˈθiːtə / (BrE)
the eighth letter of the Greek alphabet (Θ, θ), used in mathematics, science, and linguistics.

Examples
