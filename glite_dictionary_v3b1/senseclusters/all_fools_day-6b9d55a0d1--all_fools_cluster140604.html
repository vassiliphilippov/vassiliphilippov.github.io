All Fools' day

noun
April 1st, day for practical jokes
5.9
Pronunciation: / ɔl ˈfulz deɪ / (AmE), / ɔːl ˈfuːlz deɪ / (BrE)
the first day of April, celebrated by playing practical jokes and tricks on people

Examples

All Fools' day pranks
Many people enjoy pulling harmless pranks on their friends on All Fools' day.
Teachers warned students not to believe every rumor they heard on All Fools' day.
The origins of All Fools' day are uncertain, but the tradition is celebrated in many countries.
