clique

noun
small exclusive group
6.2
Pronunciation: / klik / (AmE), / kliːk / (BrE)
a small, exclusive group of people who share common interests and do not readily allow others to join

Examples

A school clique
He found it difficult to make friends because every group seemed to form its own clique.
Rumors often circulate quickly within a clique, but rarely spread to the whole office.
The high school cafeteria was divided into different cliques that rarely mixed with each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circle - a group of people with shared interests or pursuits
  • synonym: coterie - a small close-knit group with shared interests
  • antonym: outsider - someone who is not part of a group or organization
