uterus

noun
muscular female organ where fetus develops
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.tə.rəs / (AmE), / ˈjuː.tər.əs / (BrE)
the hollow muscular organ in the female pelvic cavity where the fetus develops during pregnancy

Examples

The uterus during pregnancy
Doctors examined her uterus using an ultrasound scan to check for any abnormalities.
Endometrial tissue normally lines the inside of the uterus.
The uterus expands significantly during pregnancy to accommodate the growing fetus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: womb - another term for the uterus, especially in non-technical contexts
