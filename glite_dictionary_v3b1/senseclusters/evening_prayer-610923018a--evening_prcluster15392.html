Evening Prayer

noun
Anglican daily evening church service
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈivnɪŋ ˈprɛr / (AmE), / ˈiːvnɪŋ ˈpreə / (BrE)
In the Anglican Church, a daily evening service of prayers and readings, as prescribed in the Book of Common Prayer.

Examples

Evening Prayer service
Many people attend Evening Prayer at the local parish every Sunday.
The choir sang beautifully during Evening Prayer last night.
The order of service for Evening Prayer is set out in the Book of Common Prayer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Vespers - a service of evening worship in the Christian Church, especially in the Catholic and Orthodox traditions
