order Rosales

noun
a plant order including roses and elms
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər roʊˈseɪliz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə rəʊˈseɪliːz / (BrE)
a large order of flowering plants that includes families such as Rosaceae (roses) and Ulmaceae (elms), but in some classifications excludes Leguminosae (legumes).

Examples

The order Rosales plants
Many economically important fruit trees belong to the order Rosales.
Scientists debate whether legumes belong in the order Rosales or a separate group.
The order Rosales includes well-known families such as the rose and elm families.
