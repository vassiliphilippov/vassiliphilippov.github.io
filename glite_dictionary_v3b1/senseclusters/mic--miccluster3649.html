mic

noun
device converting sound to electrical signals
Pronunciation: / maɪk / (AmE), / maɪk / (BrE)
a device for converting sound waves into electrical energy, typically to record or amplify sound

Examples

Hold the mic close
Please hold the mic close to your mouth for better sound quality.
She adjusted the mic before starting her speech.
The band set up several mics around the stage for the concert.
