obviate

verb
to prevent or remove (especially a problem)

to prevent a problem or need by taking action so it does not happen or is not necessary

Examples

To obviate problems
Careful planning can obviate the need for last-minute changes.
Installing smoke detectors obviates many fire-related risks in the home.
The new medical treatment obviates the need for surgery in most cases.
Regular maintenance can obviate many mechanical problems before they start.
The new security system obviates the need for a night guard.
The use of technology has obviated several manual processes in the office.
