mismatch

verb
to match things or people badly
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /mɪ.ˈsmætʃ/ /; / /ˈmɪ.smætʃ/ (AmE), /mɪ.ˈsmætʃ/ /; / /ˈmɪ.smætʃ/ (BrE)
to pair or combine things or people in a way that does not fit or match well together
