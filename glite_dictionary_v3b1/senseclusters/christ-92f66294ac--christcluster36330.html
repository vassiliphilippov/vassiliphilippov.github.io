Christ

proper noun
title for Jesus, central figure in Christianity
3.5
Pronunciation: / kraɪst / (AmE), / kraɪst / (BrE)
The title for Jesus, regarded by Christians as the promised Messiah and the Son of God whose life and teachings form the basis of Christianity.

Examples

Christ the Messiah

Many great works of art have depicted scenes from the life of Christ.
The teachings of Christ emphasize love, forgiveness, and compassion.
