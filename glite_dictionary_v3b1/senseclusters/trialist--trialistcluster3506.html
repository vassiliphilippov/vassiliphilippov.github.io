trialist

noun
person being tested
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈtraɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is being tested or evaluated for a place on a team, job, or position

Examples

Football trialist
The football club invited several trialists to train with the team this week.
Each trialist was given a chance to show their skills during the selection process.
After a week as a trialist, she was offered a permanent spot on the team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: applicant - a person who formally requests something, especially a job
  • synonym: candidate - a person who applies for a job or is nominated for election
  • antonym: member - a person who belongs to a group or team
