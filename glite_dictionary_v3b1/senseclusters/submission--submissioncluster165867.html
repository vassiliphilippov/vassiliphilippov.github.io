Synonyms

  • obedience
    2.6 8.88/m
    obedience is doing what an authority orders or demands, while submission is broader acceptance of another's control or will.
  • compliance
    5.0 22/m
    compliance is following rules or requests, often for practical reasons, while submission is a deeper giving up of control.
  • deference
    7.8 3.60/m
    deference is polite respect shown to someone's wishes or opinion, while submission is yielding control or authority to them.
