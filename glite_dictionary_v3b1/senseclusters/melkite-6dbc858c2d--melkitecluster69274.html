Melkite

noun
member of an Eastern Christian church
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛlˌkaɪt /; / ˈmɛlkaɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛlkaɪt / (BrE)
a member of an Eastern Christian church, especially in the Middle East, who accepted the authority of the Byzantine emperor and followed the Orthodox faith
