commissural

adjective
relating to a commissure
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɪʃ.ə.rəl/ /; / /kə.ˈmɪs.ə.rəl/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɪʃ.ə.rəl/ /; / /kə.ˈmɪs.ə.rəl/ (BrE)
relating to a commissure, especially a band of nerve fibers or tissue connecting corresponding parts on each side of the brain or heart

Examples

Commissural fibers
The commissural fibers in the brain allow communication between the left and right hemispheres.
Damage to commissural pathways can affect coordination between both sides of the body.
Doctors studied the commissural structures of the heart to understand the patient's condition.
