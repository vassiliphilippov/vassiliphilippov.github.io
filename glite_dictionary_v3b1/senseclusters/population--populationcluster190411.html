population

noun
total number of people in an area
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
all the people who live in a particular area, city, or country

Examples

City population
A large proportion of the senior population relies on public transportation.
Scientists are studying the effects of pollution on the local population.
The population of the city grew rapidly over the past decade.
Much of the population relies on agriculture for their livelihood.
The population of the city has doubled in the past twenty years.
The local population was surveyed about their views on public transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citizenry - all the citizens of a particular place
  • synonym: demography - the study of populations and their characteristics
  • synonym: inhabitants - people who live in a particular place
  • degree higher: population density - the number of people living per unit area
