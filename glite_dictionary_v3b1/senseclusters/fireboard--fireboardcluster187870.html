fireboard

noun
fire-resistant board
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəbɔːd / (BrE)
a board made from fire-resistant material, used in construction to prevent the spread of fire

Examples

Fireboard wall panel
The builders used fireboard to line the walls for extra protection.
Installing fireboard can help slow the spread of flames in case of a fire.
The safety inspector checked that fireboard was properly fitted in the stairwell.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drywall - a panel made of gypsum used in interior construction
  • degree lower: plywood - a board made from thin layers of wood glued together
