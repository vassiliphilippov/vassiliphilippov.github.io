dummy

noun
cartridge with charge but no bullet
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmi / (AmE), / ˈdʌmi / (BrE)
a cartridge containing an explosive charge but no bullet, used for training or testing firearms

Examples

Dummy cartridge
Before using live ammunition, always check that you are loading a dummy cartridge for safety drills.
He demonstrated the weapon's operation using a dummy to avoid any risk of accidents.
The soldiers practiced loading their rifles with dummy rounds during the training exercise.
