do well for oneself

verb
prosper or achieve personal success
3.7 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / duÀê w…õl f…ôr w ånÀàs…õlf / (AmE), / duÀê w…õl f…ô w ånÀàs…õlf / (BrE)

Examples
