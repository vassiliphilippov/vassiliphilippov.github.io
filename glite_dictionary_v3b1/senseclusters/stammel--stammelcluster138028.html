stammel

noun
coarse red woolen cloth
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstæməl / (AmE), / ˈstæməl / (BrE)
a coarse woolen cloth, typically dyed bright red, that was formerly used for undergarments or outerwear

Examples

A stammel cloth
Historical records describe children clothed in stammel, especially during cold months.
In medieval times, peasants often wore coarse stammel garments dyed a distinctive red.
The merchant’s cart was piled high with rolls of stammel ready for market.
