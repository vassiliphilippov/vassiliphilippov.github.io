quantal

adjective
relating to quanta or discrete states
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːn.təl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒn.təl / (BrE)
relating to or involving quanta, or occurring only in distinct, separate states rather than continuously

Examples

A quantal state
Certain chemical reactions exhibit quantal behavior, occurring only at specific energy levels.
In neuroscience, synaptic transmission can be described as a quantal process.
The quantal nature of light was a revolutionary discovery in modern physics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: continuous - happening without interruptions, not discrete
  • synonym: quantum - a discrete quantity of energy or matter
