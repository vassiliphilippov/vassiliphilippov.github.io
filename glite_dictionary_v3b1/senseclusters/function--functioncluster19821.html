function

noun
expected role or responsibility
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌŋkʃən / (AmE), / ˈfʌŋkʃən / (BrE)
the purpose, role, or responsibility of a person or thing

Examples

The function of a teacher
As a parent, your function extends beyond simply providing for your children.
One of the main functions of a manager is to motivate their team.
The committee's function is to oversee financial matters within the organization.
As a parent, one important function is teaching children right from wrong.
In biology, each organ has its own function in the body.
The main function of this device is to filter water for drinking.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duty - a task or action that someone is required to perform
  • antonym: form - the visible shape or structure of something (contrasted with function)
  • synonym: purpose - the reason for which something is done or created
  • synonym: role - the function assumed or part played by a person or thing
  • synonym: use - the act of using something or the purpose it serves
