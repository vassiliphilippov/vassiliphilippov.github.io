impassably

adverb
in an uncrossable way
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæsəbli / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that cannot be crossed, traveled through, or overcome

Examples

Road impassably blocked
After the heavy snowfall, the mountain road was impassably blocked for several days.
The river had flooded its banks, making the fields impassably muddy for vehicles.
Thick fog and fallen branches rendered the hiking trail impassably dangerous.
The mountain road was impassably blocked by snow after the storm.
Negotiations broke down because the two sides were impassably opposed on key issues.
The river swelled impassably after days of heavy rain, cutting off the village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inaccessibly - in a way that cannot be reached or entered
  • synonym: insurmountably - in a way that cannot be overcome or dealt with
  • antonym: passably - in a way that can be crossed or traveled through
