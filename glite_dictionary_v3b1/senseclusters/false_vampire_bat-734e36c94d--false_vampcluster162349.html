A carnivorous bat, especially of the families Megadermatidae or Phyllostomidae, that does not suck blood but feeds mainly on insects, despite its misleading name.

Examples

The false vampire bat
Scientists confused the false vampire bat with true vampires due to its large size and fearsome appearance.
The false vampire bat primarily eats insects rather than drinking blood.
Unlike its name suggests, the false vampire bat poses no threat to livestock or people.
