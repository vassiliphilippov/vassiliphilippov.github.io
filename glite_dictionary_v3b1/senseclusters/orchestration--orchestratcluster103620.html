orchestration

noun
automated coordination of software services
6.3 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / \u02cc\u0254rk\u0259\u02c8stre\u026a\u0283\u0259n / (AmE), / \u02cc\u0254\u02d0k\u026a\u02c8stre\u026a\u0283\u0259n / (BrE)
(in computing) the automated coordination, scheduling, and management of distributed services, workflows, or cloud resources to ensure they run together correctly.

Examples

Cloud orchestration tools
The platform uses orchestration to deploy and scale microservices across clouds.
Kubernetes provides container orchestration, automating deployment, networking, and scaling for clustered applications.
Their DevOps team adopted orchestration to coordinate CI/CD pipelines and reduce manual steps.
