subrational

adjective
below rational thought
Pronunciation: / sʌbˈræʃənəl / (AmE), / sʌbˈræʃənəl / (BrE)
existing or occurring below the level of rational thought or conscious reasoning

Examples

A subrational response
Some instincts are considered subrational responses to stimuli.
Fear can trigger a subrational reaction before you have time to think.
The artist tried to express subrational emotions through abstract shapes and colors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irrational - not based on reason or logic
  • antonym: rational - based on reason or logic
