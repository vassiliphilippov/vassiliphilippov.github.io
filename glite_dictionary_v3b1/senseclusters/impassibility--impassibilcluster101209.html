impassibility

noun
emotional calmness
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌpæsəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ɪmˌpæsɪˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the state of not showing or feeling emotion, especially calmness or lack of response to events

Examples

Emotional impassibility
Despite the chaos around him, his impassibility impressed everyone in the room.
The judge's impassibility made it difficult to guess her decision before the verdict.
She maintained complete impassibility even when faced with unexpected news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionality - the quality of showing strong emotions
  • synonym: impassiveness - lack of visible emotion or reaction
  • synonym: stoicism - endurance of pain or hardship without showing feelings
