hexavalent

adjective
having valence of six
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛksəˈveɪlənt / (AmE), / ˌhɛksəˈveɪlənt / (BrE)
having a valence of six, especially referring to a chemical element or compound with six available bonds

Examples

Hexavalent chromium
Chromium in its hexavalent form is known to be highly toxic to humans.
The chemist explained that a hexavalent compound can form six chemical bonds.
Some industrial processes produce hexavalent chromium, which must be carefully managed.
