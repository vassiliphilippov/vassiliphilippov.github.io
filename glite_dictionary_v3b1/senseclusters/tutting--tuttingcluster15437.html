tutting

noun
street dance style
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʌtɪŋ / (BrE)
a style of street dance characterized by sharp, angular movements of the arms, hands, and fingers

Examples

Tutting dance moves

She learned tutting by watching online tutorials and practicing in front of a mirror.
Many hip-hop routines now include elements of tutting to showcase creativity and control.
