banned

adjective
officially forbidden or prohibited
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
officially forbidden by law or authority; prohibited.

Examples

Officially banned goods
Importing ivory has been banned to protect endangered species.
Smoking is banned in all public buildings in this city.
The government released a list of banned substances for athletes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allowed - permitted by law or authority
  • synonym: prohibited - forbidden by law or authority
