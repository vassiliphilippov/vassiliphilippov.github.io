total depressoid

noun
person who is extremely gloomy or depressed
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl dɪˈprɛsˌɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl dɪˈprɛsˌɔɪd / (BrE)
an informal term for a person who is extremely depressed or persistently gloomy

Examples

A total depressoid person
After the breakup, Tom was a total depressoid and barely left his room for days.
Don't be such a total depressoid—things will get better soon.
Her posts on social media make her sound like a total depressoid sometimes.
