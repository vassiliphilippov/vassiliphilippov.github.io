koodoo

noun
a spiral-horned African antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːduː / (AmE), / ˈkuːduː / (BrE)
Either of two large African antelopes with long, twisted horns, also known as kudu.

Examples

The koodoo antelope
Local guides pointed out the difference between the greater and lesser koodoo.
The twisting horns of a male koodoo can reach impressive lengths.
We saw a magnificent koodoo grazing by the riverbank during our safari.
