wooden spoon

noun
prize given to last-place finisher
4.0 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààw äd…ôn Àåspun / (AmE), / Ààw äd.…ôn ÀåspuÀên / (BrE)
an informal prize, often a literal wooden spoon, awarded to the person or team coming last in a competition

Examples

Wooden spoon for last place
He joked about proudly displaying his wooden spoon from the quiz night.
No one wants to go home with the wooden spoon at the end of the tournament.
The team received the wooden spoon after finishing at the bottom of the league.

Synonyms
