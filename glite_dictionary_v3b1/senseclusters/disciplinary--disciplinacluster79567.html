disciplinary

adjective
relating to discipline or enforcement of rules
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsəpləˌnɛri /; / ˈdɪs.əˌplɪ.ner.i / (AmE), / ˈdɪsɪplɪnəri /; / ˈdɪs.ɪ.plɪ.nər.i / (BrE)
connected with discipline, order, or the enforcement of rules, especially by correcting or punishing misbehavior

Examples

Disciplinary action
Her disciplinary record was reviewed before she was considered for promotion.
Management took disciplinary action against several employees who broke company rules.
The school has strict disciplinary procedures for handling student misconduct.
He was given a disciplinary warning after violating company policies.
Strict disciplinary rules were enforced in the military academy.
The school implemented disciplinary actions to address repeated absenteeism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrective - intended to correct or remedy faults or errors
  • synonym: punitive - intended as punishment
