wild woman

noun
an unsocialized or uncivilized woman
1.0 6.2
Pronunciation: /waɪld ˈwʊm.ən/ (AmE), /waɪld ˈwʊm.ən/ (BrE)
A woman who behaves in an uncivilized or unsocialized manner, often disregarding conventional social norms.

Examples

Legends often depict a wild woman as someone shunned by society for her unconventional ways.

The villagers were frightened by the stories of a wild woman living alone in the forest.
