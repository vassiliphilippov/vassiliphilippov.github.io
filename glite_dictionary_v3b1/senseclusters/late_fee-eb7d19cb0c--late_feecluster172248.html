late fee

noun
extra charge for overdue payment
Pronunciation: / leɪt fiː / (AmE), / leɪt fiː / (BrE)
an extra charge added when payment is made after the due date

Examples

Pay a late fee
If you don't return your library books on time, you'll have to pay a late fee.
Some landlords impose a late fee if rent is not received by the fifth of the month.
The credit card company charged her a late fee for missing the payment deadline.

  • synonym: penalty - a punishment or disadvantage resulting from breaking a rule or contract
