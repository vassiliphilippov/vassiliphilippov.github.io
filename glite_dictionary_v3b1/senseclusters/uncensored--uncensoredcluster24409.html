uncensored

adjective
not censored; unaltered by censorship
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛnsɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsensəd / (BrE)
(of information, speech, media, etc.) not having had parts removed or suppressed by censorship; presented in its complete or original form.

Examples

Uncensored news sources
Many people prefer reading uncensored news sources to avoid state propaganda.
The author published an uncensored account of his experiences during the war.
The film was released in its uncensored version, which included several controversial scenes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: censored - having had content removed or suppressed by censorship
