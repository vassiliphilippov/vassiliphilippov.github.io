slave to

noun
completely controlled by something
Pronunciation: / sleɪv tu / (AmE), / sleɪv tə / (BrE)
to be completely controlled or dominated by something, such as a habit, emotion, or obligation, often in a way that limits freedom or choice.

Examples

Slave to routine
After years in the corporate world, he became a slave to routine.
Many people feel they are slaves to their smartphones.
She refused to be a slave to fashion trends and wore what she liked.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: servant to - someone who works for or is devoted to something or someone
