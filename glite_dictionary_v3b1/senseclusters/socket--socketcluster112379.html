socket

noun
electrical receptacle for a plug or device
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkɪt / (BrE)
a device or opening designed to receive and hold an electric plug or component, enabling a connection to a power supply or circuit

Examples

Electric socket
Be sure the lamp is switched off before inserting the bulb into the socket.
European sockets often require adapters for American appliances.
He plugged his cellphone charger into the wall socket next to the bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plug - a device that fits into a socket to make an electrical connection
