socket

noun
electrical receptacle for a plug or device
3.0 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːkɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒkɪt / (BrE)
a device or opening designed to receive and hold an electric plug or component, enabling a connection to a power supply or circuit

Examples
