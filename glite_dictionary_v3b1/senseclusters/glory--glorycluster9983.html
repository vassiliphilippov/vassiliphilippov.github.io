glory

noun
radiant beauty; splendor
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɔri / (AmE), / ˈɡlɔːri / (BrE)
Brilliant, radiant beauty or splendor, especially that which inspires admiration or awe.

Examples

Full glory
The cathedral's stained-glass windows shimmered in all their glory in the afternoon light.
The garden was at the height of its glory in spring, bursting with color and life.
We stood in awe as the mountain revealed its snow-covered glory at sunrise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beauty - a combination of qualities that pleases the senses
  • antonym: drabness - lacking brightness or interest; drearily dull
  • synonym: splendor - magnificent and splendid appearance
