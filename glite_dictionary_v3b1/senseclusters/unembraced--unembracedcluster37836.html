unembraced

adjective
not surrounded or clasped
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈbreɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbreɪst / (BrE)
not surrounded, clasped, or attached to another part; not embraced, especially in a botanical context

Examples

Unembraced petals
The delicate petals remained unembraced by the sepals as the flower bloomed.
In some plants, the stem is left unembraced by the leaves, giving it a bare appearance.
The sculpture stood unembraced in the center of the gallery, drawing attention to its isolation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: embraced - surrounded or clasped closely
  • synonym: unattached - not joined or connected to something
