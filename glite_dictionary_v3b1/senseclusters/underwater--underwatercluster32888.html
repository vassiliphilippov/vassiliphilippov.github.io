underwater

adjective
beneath the surface of water
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈwɔtər /; / ˈʌndərˌwɔtər / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈwɔːtə /; / ˈʌndəˌwɔːtə / (BrE)
located, happening, or existing beneath the surface of water

Examples

Underwater world
Many species thrive in underwater environments that are difficult for humans to access.

The photographer captured stunning images of underwater life on the coral reef.
Many aquatic plants have underwater leaves that help them absorb carbon dioxide.
Submarines are capable of prolonged underwater travel.
The divers explored an underwater cave filled with unusual rock formations.
