adonis

noun
exceptionally handsome young man
Pronunciation: / əˈdoʊnɪs / (AmE), / əˈdəʊnɪs / (BrE)
A handsome young man, especially one who is exceptionally attractive or admired for his looks.

Examples

A handsome adonis
He strutted into the room like an adonis, drawing everyone's attention.
In his youth, people compared him to an adonis for his striking looks.
The actor was often described as an adonis by entertainment magazines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heartthrob - a man who is very attractive and admired, especially by women
