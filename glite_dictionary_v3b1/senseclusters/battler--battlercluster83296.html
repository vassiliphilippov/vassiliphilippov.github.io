battler

noun
a person who fights or perseveres
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbæt.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈbæt.lə / (BrE)
a person who fights in a battle or, figuratively, someone who struggles persistently against adversity or difficulty

Examples

A battler in life
After years of hardship, she earned the reputation of a true battler.
In the tournament, each battler showed remarkable courage and determination.
The seasoned battler never gave up, even when the odds were against him.
