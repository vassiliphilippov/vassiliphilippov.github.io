glute muscle

noun
muscle in the buttocks area
3.1
Pronunciation: / ɡluːt ˈmʌsəl / (AmE), / ɡluːt ˈmʌsl̩ / (BrE)
any of the muscles in the buttocks, especially the gluteus maximus, gluteus medius, and gluteus minimus, responsible for movement of the hip and thigh

Examples

The glute muscle injury
He injured a glute muscle while sprinting during practice.
Regular lunges help strengthen your glute muscles for better athletic performance.
The physical therapist recommended exercises targeting the glute muscles to improve posture.
