royale

noun
savory custard dish
Pronunciation: / roʊˈjɑːl / (AmE), / rɔɪˈal / (BrE)
a rich, savory custard or pudding, often molded and served as a small dish or garnish in cooking

Examples

Royale custard
The chef prepared a delicate royale with fresh herbs as an appetizer.
A salmon royale was served as a garnish alongside the main course.
For the special menu, they offered a mushroom royale in small ramekins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: quiche - a savory pie with a custard filling
