mantlet

noun
portable protective shelter
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmæntlɪt / (AmE), / ˈmæntlɪt / (BrE)
a portable shield or shelter, often bulletproof, usually used to protect soldiers from enemy fire

Examples

A mantlet shield
During the siege, they built a wooden mantlet to approach the enemy walls safely.
Engineers rolled the mantlet forward as cover during the assault.
The soldiers advanced behind a heavy mantlet to protect themselves from arrows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shield - a broad piece of armor carried for protection against weapons
