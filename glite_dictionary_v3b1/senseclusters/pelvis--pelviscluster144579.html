pelvis

noun
funnel-shaped kidney structure for urine collection
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛlvɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɛlvɪs / (BrE)
the funnel-shaped structure at the outlet of the kidney that collects urine before it passes into the ureter

Examples

Renal pelvis blockage
An obstruction in the renal pelvis can lead to a buildup of urine and possible infection.
Doctors examined the patient's pelvis for signs of kidney disease.
The pelvis of the kidney collects urine before it is sent to the bladder.
