clearish

adjective
somewhat clear
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈklɪrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈklɪərɪʃ / (BrE)
somewhat clear or almost clear, but not completely transparent or pure

Examples

Somewhat clearish water
The lake water was clearish, but you could still see some particles floating in it.
She wore a clearish lip gloss that gave her lips a subtle shine.
After cleaning the window, it looked clearish but still had a few smudges.
