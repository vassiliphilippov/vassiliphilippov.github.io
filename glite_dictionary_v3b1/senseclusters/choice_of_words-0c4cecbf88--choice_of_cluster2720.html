choice of words

noun
the particular words used in expression
1.5
Pronunciation: / tʃɔɪs əv wɝdz / (AmE), / tʃɔɪs əv wɜːdz / (BrE)
the specific words or phrases someone uses to express something

Examples

Choice of words in writing
A writer's choice of words can greatly affect the tone and impact of a story.
Her choice of words made the explanation clear and easy to understand.
The politician's choice of words during the speech was criticized by the media.
She apologized for her poor choice of words during the interview.
Sometimes a simple choice of words can completely change the message's tone.
The teacher praised his careful choice of words in the essay.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diction - the choice and use of words in speech or writing
  • synonym: phrasing - the way in which something is put into words
