watershed

noun
image-segmentation algorithm
Pronunciation: / \u02c8w\u0251\u02d0t\u0259r\u02cc\u0283\u025bd / (AmE), / \u02c8w\u0254\u02d0t\u0259\u02cc\u0283\u025bd / (BrE)
(in computer vision and image processing) an image‑segmentation algorithm or transform that treats image intensities or gradients as a topographic surface and 'floods' basins from markers or minima to separate adjacent regions.

Examples

Watershed algorithm
Scientists used a watershed transform to segment touching cells in the microscope image.
The research team applied a watershed algorithm to separate overlapping regions in the aerial photograph.
When gradients are noisy, the basic watershed method often produces too many small regions and requires markers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: watershed algorithm - alternate name for the watershed-based segmentation technique
  • synonym: watershed transform - the same image‑segmentation method often called the watershed transform
