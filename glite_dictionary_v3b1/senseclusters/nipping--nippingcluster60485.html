nipping

adjective
pleasantly cold and invigorating (of weather)
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnɪpɪŋ / (BrE)
(of weather or air) pleasantly cold and invigorating; bracing.

Examples

We enjoyed a long walk outside despite the nipping air.
