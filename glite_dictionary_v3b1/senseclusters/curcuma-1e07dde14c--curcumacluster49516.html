Curcuma

noun
genus of tropical Asian herbs
9.3
Pronunciation: / kɜrˈkuːmə / (AmE), / kɜːˈkjuːmə / (BrE)
A genus of tropical perennial herbs in the ginger family, including turmeric, native to Asia.

Examples

Curcuma plant
Many gardeners appreciate Curcuma for its attractive foliage and flowers.
The roots of Curcuma are widely used in traditional Asian medicines.
Turmeric, a species of Curcuma, is a staple in Indian cuisine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: turmeric - a yellow spice from the rhizome of Curcuma longa
