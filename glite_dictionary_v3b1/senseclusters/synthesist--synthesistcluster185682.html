synthesist

noun
person who synthesizes ideas or elements
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnθəsɪst / (AmE), / ˈsɪnθəsɪst / (BrE)
a person who combines separate elements or ideas to form a coherent whole, especially in intellectual or creative fields.

Examples

A synthesist creates ideas
As a synthesist, she excels at creating new theories from a wide range of sources.
In group discussions, a synthesist often turns diverse viewpoints into actionable strategies.
The research team needed a synthesist to bring together ideas from multiple disciplines.

Related Terms

  • antonym: analyst - a person who examines or breaks down elements to understand them
