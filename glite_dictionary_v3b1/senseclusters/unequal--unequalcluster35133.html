unequal

adjective
not the same in quantity or value
3.0 4.55/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàiÀêkw…ôl / (AmE), /  ånÀàiÀêkw…ôl / (BrE)
not the same in quality, amount, or value; not evenly matched or balanced.

Examples

Unequal pay
An unequal division of the profits caused tension among the partners.
The distribution of resources in the region is unequal, leading to significant disparities.
They faced an unequal contest, as one team was much stronger than the other.

Synonyms

  • inequivalent
    6.8 0.04/m
    inequivalent means different in value, function, or meaning, while unequal covers not being the same in amount, quality, or balance.
  • uneven
    2.7 0.47/m
    uneven describes things or contests that are not level or fairly matched, while unequal means not the same in amount, value, or quality.
