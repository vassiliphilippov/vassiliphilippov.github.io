re-enter

verb
to enter again
Pronunciation: / ˌriˈɛntər / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɛntə / (BrE)
to enter a place, organization, or activity again after having left it

Examples

To re-enter the password
After fixing the typo, please re-enter your password to continue.
She had to re-enter the building to retrieve her forgotten umbrella.
Tourists must obtain permission before they can re-enter the restricted area.
