fallaciousness

noun
the state of being fallacious
9.5 7.7
Pronunciation: /fə.ˈleɪ.ʃəs.nəs/ (AmE), /fə.ˈleɪ.ʃəs.nəs/ (BrE)

The fallaciousness of his argument became clear when the evidence was examined.
