overprivileged

adjective
excessively advantaged
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈprɪvəlɪdʒd / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈprɪvɪlɪdʒd / (BrE)
having more advantages, money, or opportunities than is typical or considered fair

Examples

An overprivileged child
Many people feel that the overprivileged should contribute more to society.
The documentary explored the lives of overprivileged teenagers in wealthy neighborhoods.
Critics argue that overprivileged individuals often take their opportunities for granted.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: privileged - having special advantages or rights
  • antonym: underprivileged - lacking basic advantages or opportunities
