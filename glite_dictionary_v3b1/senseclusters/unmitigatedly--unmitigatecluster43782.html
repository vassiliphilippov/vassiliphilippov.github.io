unmitigatedly

adverb
completely, absolutely
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈmɪt̬ɪɡeɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈmɪtɪɡeɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is complete, absolute, or without any reduction or moderation

Examples

To be unmitigatedly successful
The project was unmitigatedly successful, exceeding all expectations.
She was unmitigatedly honest about her feelings, never hiding the truth.
The movie was unmitigatedly boring, with no exciting scenes at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absolutely - completely; totally
  • antonym: partially - in part; not completely
