watchwoman

noun
female guard or night watch
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːtʃˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman whose job is to guard a building or area, especially at night

Examples

The watchwoman patrols
As a watchwoman, she checked every entrance twice during her shift.
The museum hired a watchwoman to patrol the galleries after hours.
Two watchwomen were on duty at the construction site all night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: watchman - a man whose job is to guard a building or area, especially at night
