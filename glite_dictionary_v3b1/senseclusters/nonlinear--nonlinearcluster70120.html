nonlinear

adjective
not in a straight line
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈlɪniɚ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈlɪniə / (BrE)
not arranged in a straight line or regular sequence; not following a direct or predictable pattern

Examples

Nonlinear story
The story was told in a nonlinear fashion, jumping back and forth in time.
Some mathematical equations are nonlinear and cannot be solved easily.
Her career path was nonlinear, with many unexpected changes along the way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irregular - not following a regular pattern
  • antonym: linear - arranged in a straight line or regular sequence
