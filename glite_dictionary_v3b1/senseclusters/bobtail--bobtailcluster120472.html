bobtail

noun
a sheepdog with a short tail and shaggy coat
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːb.teɪl / (AmE), / ˈbɒb.teɪl / (BrE)
a large sheepdog, especially the Old English Sheepdog, with a shaggy bluish-grey and white coat and a short tail

Examples

An bobtail sheepdog
Her bobtail won first prize at the dog show for its beautiful coat.
The farm was guarded by a friendly bobtail that loved to play with children.
They adopted a bobtail because of its gentle nature and intelligence.
