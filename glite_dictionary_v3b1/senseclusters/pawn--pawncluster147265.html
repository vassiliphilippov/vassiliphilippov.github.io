pawn

noun
chess piece with limited power, moves forward
3.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /pɔːn/ /; / /pɑːn/ (AmE), /pɔːn/ (BrE)
in chess, the least powerful piece, which moves only forward and captures diagonally; it can be promoted if it reaches the opponent's end of the board

Examples

Chess pawn
If a pawn reaches the eighth rank, it can be promoted to a queen or another piece.
Many beginners underestimate the strategic importance of a pawn in the opening moves.
She sacrificed a pawn early in the game to gain a strong attacking position.
