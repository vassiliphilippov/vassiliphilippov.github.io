military position

noun
occupied spot for military forces
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri pəˈzɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri pəˈzɪʃən / (BrE)
a place or area occupied by military forces for tactical or strategic purposes

Examples

Enemy military position
After several days of fighting, the enemy finally abandoned their military position.
The commander ordered his troops to defend the military position at all costs.
We advanced quietly to avoid alerting the enemy to our military position.
