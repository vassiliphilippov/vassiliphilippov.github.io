lithography

noun
microfabrication patterning process
8.1 0.26/m

(in microfabrication) the process of using light or other radiation and a photosensitive resist to transfer a pattern onto a substrate, enabling the creation of integrated circuits and microdevices.

Examples

Chip lithography process
Modern chip fabrication relies on lithography to pattern nanoscale circuits on silicon wafers.
Researchers developed a new EUV technique to improve lithography resolution for next-generation processors.
The foundry increased investment in advanced lithography equipment to manufacture smaller transistors at scale.

Synonyms

  • photolithography
    9.9 0.06/m
    photolithography is a process that uses light and masks to make fine patterns, while lithography covers many pattern transfer methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microfabrication - the set of processes used to fabricate miniature structures and devices, especially integrated circuits
  • synonym: photolithography - the specific lithography process using ultraviolet light and photoresist to pattern semiconductor wafers
