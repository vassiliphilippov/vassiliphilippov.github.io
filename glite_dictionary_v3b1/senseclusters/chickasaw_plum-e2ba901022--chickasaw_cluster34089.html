Chickasaw plum

noun
North American shrubby tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.ə.sɑː ˈplʌm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ə.sɔː ˈplʌm / (BrE)
a small North American shrubby tree (Prunus angustifolia) that bears small, edible yellow to reddish fruit

Examples

The Chickasaw plum tree
Jams made from Chickasaw plum are popular in some Southern cuisines.
