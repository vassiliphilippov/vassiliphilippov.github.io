wokeist agenda

noun
set of beliefs on social justice activism
Pronunciation: / ˈwoʊkɪst əˈdʒɛndə / (AmE), / ˈwəʊkɪst əˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
A set of beliefs and goals associated with social justice activism, especially those considered progressive or radical by critics.

Examples

The wokeist agenda movement
Many critics argue that the new educational policies reflect a wokeist agenda.
Some politicians deny supporting a wokeist agenda but advocate for certain social reforms.
The commentator accused the corporation of pushing a wokeist agenda through its advertising.
