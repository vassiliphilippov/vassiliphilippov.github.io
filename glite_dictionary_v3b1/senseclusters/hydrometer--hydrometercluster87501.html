hydrometer

noun
instrument for measuring liquid density
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒmɪtə / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the specific gravity (relative density) of liquids

Examples

A hydrometer reading
A hydrometer reading indicated that the battery fluid was too weak.
The technician used a hydrometer to check the sugar content in the wine.
You can determine a solution's density accurately using a hydrometer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: densitometer - instrument for measuring density, especially in liquids or solids
