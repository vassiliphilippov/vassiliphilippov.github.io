suction curettage

noun
a method of abortion using suction
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌkʃən kjʊˈrɛtɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsʌkʃən kjʊəˈrɛtɪdʒ / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the embryo and placenta are removed from the uterus by applying suction through the dilated cervix, typically performed during the first trimester of pregnancy

Examples

Suction curettage procedure
Suction curettage is commonly used for removing tissue after a miscarriage.
After undergoing suction curettage, she was monitored for any complications.
The doctor recommended suction curettage as the safest option for an early abortion.
