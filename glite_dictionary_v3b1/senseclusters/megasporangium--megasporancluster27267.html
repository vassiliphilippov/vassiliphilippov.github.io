megasporangium

noun
plant structure producing megaspores
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛɡ.ə.spɔːˈræn.dʒi.əm / (AmE), / ˌmɛɡ.ə.spɔːˈran.dʒi.əm / (BrE)
A plant structure that produces megaspores, typically found in certain groups such as ferns and seed plants.

Examples

The megasporangium structure
Botanists examined the megasporangium under a microscope to study the process of spore formation.
In ferns, the megasporangium typically produces only a few large spores.
The ovule of a seed plant contains a megasporangium where the megaspore develops.
