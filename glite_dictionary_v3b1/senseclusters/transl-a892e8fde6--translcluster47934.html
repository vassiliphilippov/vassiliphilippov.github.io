transl.

noun
translator abbreviation

an abbreviation for 'translator', used to indicate the person who translated a work in bibliographies or book credits

Examples

Transl. John Smith
The book listed transl. John Smith on the copyright page.

She was acknowledged as transl. in several academic publications.
