evergreen

noun
long-lasting online content
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.griːn/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.griːn/ (BrE)
(in publishing, marketing, and online media) a piece of content (for example, an article, video, or guide) that remains useful, relevant, or attractive to audiences for a long period after publication.

Examples

Evergreen content
The blog post became an evergreen that continues to attract readers years after publication.
Our content strategy focuses on producing evergreen guides that bring steady traffic regardless of news cycles.
She updated several evergreen tutorials so new users could learn without relying on outdated posts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: timeless - continuing to be of lasting interest or appeal
  • antonym: trending - content that is currently popular but often short-lived
