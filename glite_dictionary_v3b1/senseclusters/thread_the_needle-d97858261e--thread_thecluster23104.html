thread the needle

verb
accomplish a delicate or difficult task
4.8 7.6
Pronunciation: / θrɛd ðə ˈniːdəl / (AmE), / θrɛd ðə ˈniːdl̩ / (BrE)
to successfully pass a thread through the eye of a needle, or figuratively, to accomplish a very delicate or difficult task requiring precision or care
