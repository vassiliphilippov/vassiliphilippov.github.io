freewheel

verb
coast in a vehicle without pedaling or power
5.9 5.1
Pronunciation: /friː.ˈwiːl/ /; / /ˈfriː.wiːl/ (AmE), /friː.ˈwiːl/ /; / /ˈfriː.wiːl/ (BrE)
to coast in a vehicle, especially a bicycle, without using power from the pedals or engine, often relying on a freewheel mechanism
