sling

verb
move or lift with a sling
1.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /slɪŋ/ (AmE), /slɪŋ/ (BrE)
to hang, move, or lift something using a sling or strap

Examples

To sling a box
The workers sling the heavy equipment onto the truck using sturdy straps.
They carefully slung the piano into the moving van with the help of a crane.
To load the supplies, you need to sling each box onto the platform with the rig.
He slung his bag over his shoulder and walked out the door.
The hammock was slung between two trees, swaying gently in the breeze.
They sling the lantern from a hook on the porch each night.
