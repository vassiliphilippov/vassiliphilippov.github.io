blast

noun
a sudden loud noise
1.6 2.3
Pronunciation: /blæst/ (AmE), /blɑːst/ (BrE)
a sudden, very loud noise, often caused by an explosion or strong impact

Examples

A blast of noise

There was a deafening blast as the fireworks exploded overhead.
We covered our ears just before the blast from the cannon.
