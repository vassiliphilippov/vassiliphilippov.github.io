blast

noun
a sudden loud noise
Pronunciation: / blæst / (AmE), / blɑːst / (BrE)
a sudden, very loud noise, often caused by an explosion or strong impact

Examples

A blast of noise
The sudden blast woke everyone in the building.
There was a deafening blast as the fireworks exploded overhead.
We covered our ears just before the blast from the cannon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bang - a sudden loud noise, such as from a gun or explosion
  • synonym: boom - a deep, resonant sound, often sudden and loud
  • antonym: whisper - a very soft, quiet sound
