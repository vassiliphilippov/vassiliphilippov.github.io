workspace

noun
virtual collaborative environment
Pronunciation: / \u02c8w\u025c\u02d0rk.spe\u026as / (AmE), / \u02c8w\u025c\u02d0k.spe\u026as / (BrE)
(in computing and business) a virtual environment within software or a cloud platform where people or teams store files, share resources, and organize tasks and projects to collaborate.

Examples

Shared workspace online
The company migrated to a shared workspace to collaborate on documents and track tasks in real time.
She opened the project workspace and assigned new tasks to the design team.
They created two new workspaces to separate marketing and product materials.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collaboration platform - software that allows people to work together, share files, and communicate online
  • synonym: project management tool - an application used to plan, track, and manage tasks and projects
