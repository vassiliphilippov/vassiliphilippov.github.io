Synonyms

    3.3 21/m
    clarify removes confusion and makes a point simple and clear, while explain gives information to make something easier to understand.
  • elucidate
    6.8 2.15/m
    elucidate makes a difficult idea clear in a formal, careful way, while explain is more general and used in everyday speech.
  • explicate
    7.4 1.48/m
    explicate analyzes texts or ideas in detail to show meaning clearly, while explain is broader and used in plain description.
  • expound
    7.3 2.16/m
    expound explains ideas at length and often teaches them step by step, while explain means make something clear more generally.
  • spell out
    3.5 5.25/m
    spell out states something clearly and in detail for listeners, while explain can be shorter and give less full information.
  • clear up
    2.3 5.07/m
    clear up removes confusion and fixes misunderstandings directly, while explain gives information to make something clear in general.
  • shed light on
    3.9 2.90/m
    shed light on gives new facts or details that make a topic clearer, while explain covers any way of making something easy to understand.
  • illuminate
    4.8 7.56/m
    illuminate makes an idea clear often by showing meaning or new view, while explain gives information to make things easy to understand.
  • provide insight into
    3.9 3.43/m
    provide insight into offers deeper understanding and perspective about a topic, while explain focuses on making facts or ideas clear and simple.
  • delineate
    6.8 1.54/m
    delineate describes something precisely and shows its parts or limits, while explain gives general information to make it easy to understand.
  • detail
    3.1 8.41/m
    detail lists all the facts or parts carefully to show specifics, while explain gives a general account to make the main idea clear.
  • illustrate
    3.5 49/m

  • interpret
    3.3 37/m
