search mission

noun
operation to find something or someone
Pronunciation: / sɝtʃ ˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / sɜːtʃ ˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
an organized activity or operation to locate or find someone or something missing or lost

Examples

A search mission launched
After the plane disappeared, authorities launched an extensive search mission in the mountains.
The team was deployed on a search mission to locate the missing hikers.
Volunteers joined the search mission to look for survivors after the earthquake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rescue mission - an operation to save someone in danger or distress
