talkathon

noun
long talking event
7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtɑː.kə.θən/ /; / /ˈtɔː.kə.θən/ (AmE), /ˈtɔː.kə.θɒn/ /; / /ˈtɔː.kə.θən/ (BrE)
a long event where people talk continuously, often to raise money or attention, either as one long speech or many short talks

Examples

A talkathon event
The charity organized a talkathon to collect donations for disaster relief.
Listeners tuned in to the overnight talkathon hosted by their favorite radio personalities.
During the talkathon, guests shared inspiring stories to encourage people to give.
The senator's talkathon lasted for over eight hours and drew national attention.
Organizers held a talkathon to raise awareness about climate change.
The students participated in a talkathon to protest the new university policy.
The charity held a talkathon to raise funds, inviting dozens of speakers to share their stories.
During the science talkathon, each researcher had only five minutes to present their findings.
Our school organized a talkathon where students gave short talks on topics they were passionate about.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
  • synonym: filibuster - a prolonged speech to delay legislative action
  • synonym: marathon - an event or activity that lasts a long time
  • synonym: symposium - a meeting or conference for discussion of a topic, often with several speakers
  • synonym: telethon - a long television broadcast to raise money for charity
