poplin

noun
ribbed fabric for clothing/upholstery
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːplɪn / (AmE), / ˈpɒplɪn / (BrE)
A strong, plain-woven fabric with a fine ribbed surface, used especially for clothing and upholstery.

Examples

A poplin fabric
She chose a dress made of poplin for its comfort and durability.
The tailor recommended poplin for the lightweight summer jacket.
Upholstery in poplin adds a touch of elegance to the furniture.
