specimen

noun
sample of tissue or fluid for testing
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛsəmɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɛsɪmɪn / (BrE)
a small sample of tissue, blood, urine, or other material taken from a person or animal for medical examination or testing

Examples

A blood specimen
Please bring a urine specimen to your appointment for testing.
The doctor asked for a tissue specimen to help diagnose the illness.
The nurse labeled the blood specimen before sending it to the laboratory for analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sample - a small part or quantity intended to show what the whole is like
