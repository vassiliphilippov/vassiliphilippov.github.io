camper

noun
person staying in tent or cabin for recreation
Pronunciation: / ˈkæmpər / (AmE), / ˈkæmpə / (BrE)
a person who stays temporarily in a tent, cabin, or similar shelter for recreation, especially as part of a vacation or outdoor activity

Examples

A young camper
As a young camper, she learned useful survival skills during summer camp.
Every camper is required to follow the safety rules in the park.
The camper set up his tent by the lake and enjoyed the peaceful scenery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hiker - a person who goes for long walks, especially in the countryside
