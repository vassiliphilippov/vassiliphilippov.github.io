taur

noun
mythical hybrid creature
9.9
Pronunciation: / tɔr / (AmE), / tɔː / (BrE)
a mythical creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of an animal

Examples

A taur warrior
The fantasy novel described a powerful taur leading the ancient army into battle.
In the game, you can choose to play as a taur with special abilities.
The museum displayed a statue of a taur among other legendary creatures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: centaur - a creature with the upper body of a human and the lower body of a horse
  • synonym: minotaur - a mythical creature with the head of a bull and the body of a man
