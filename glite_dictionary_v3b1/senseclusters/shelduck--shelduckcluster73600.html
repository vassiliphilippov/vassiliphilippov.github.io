shelduck

noun
a large, colorful duck of the genus Tadorna
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛlˌdʌk / (AmE), / ˈʃɛl.dʌk / (BrE)
A large, brightly colored duck, especially of the genus Tadorna, found in Eurasia and Africa.

Examples

A shelduck bird
Several shelducks nested near the estuary last spring.
The shelduck is known for its bold plumage and loud calls.
We spotted a striking shelduck gliding across the pond during our morning walk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sheldrake - an alternative name for a male shelduck or sometimes any shelduck
