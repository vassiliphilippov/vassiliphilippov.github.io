parking zone

noun
designated area for parking vehicles
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑrkɪŋ zoʊn / (AmE), / ˈpɑːkɪŋ zəʊn / (BrE)
an area or space designated for parking vehicles, especially automobiles

Examples

A parking zone area
Make sure your car is within the parking zone to avoid getting a ticket.
Residents are allowed to park in the designated parking zone with a permit.
The city recently expanded the parking zone near the train station to accommodate more commuters.

Related Terms

  • antonym: no-parking zone - an area where parking is not permitted
  • synonym: parking lot - an area, often outdoors, where vehicles may be parked
