biochemical

noun
substance in living things
4.1
Pronunciation: /baɪ.oʊ.ˈkem.ɪ.kəl/ (AmE), /baɪ.əʊ.ˈkem.ɪ.kəl/ (BrE)

Examples

A biochemical marker
Researchers analyzed the biochemical found in the plant to understand its medicinal properties.
The doctor ordered tests to measure certain biochemicals in the patient's blood.
Many diseases are caused by imbalances in important biochemicals within the body.
