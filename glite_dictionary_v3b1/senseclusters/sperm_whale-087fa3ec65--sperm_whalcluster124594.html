sperm whale

noun
large marine whale with a big head
5.7
Pronunciation: / spɝm weɪl / (AmE), / spɜːm weɪl / (BrE)
a large marine mammal with a massive head containing spermaceti, known for producing ambergris and found in deep oceans worldwide

Examples

The sperm whale swims
Ambergris, used in perfume, is produced by the sperm whale.
Scientists study sperm whales to understand their complex social structures and communication.
The sperm whale is the largest toothed predator on Earth.
