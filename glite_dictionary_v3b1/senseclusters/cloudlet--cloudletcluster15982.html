cloudlet

noun
small cloud; local server
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdlət / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdlət / (BrE)
a small cloud or, in computing, a small-scale cloud computing resource located near users

Examples

A small cloudlet
A single cloudlet drifted across the otherwise clear blue sky.
The mobile app connects to a nearby cloudlet for faster data processing.
Researchers deployed a cloudlet at the stadium to support real-time video streaming.
