color wash

noun
tinted wash of water-based paint
9.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.ər wɑːʃ/ /; / /ˈkɔː.lər wɑːʃ/ /; / /ˈkʌl.ər wɔːrʃ/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.ə wɒʃ/ (BrE)
A semi-transparent layer of water-based paint, usually tinted with color, applied to a surface to create a subtle or blended effect.

Examples

A color wash effect
She learned how to use a color wash technique to enhance her watercolor paintings.
The artist applied a soft color wash to the canvas, creating a dreamy effect.
The living room walls were finished with a pale blue color wash for a serene atmosphere.
A blue colour wash can give a calming effect to any room.
The artist applied a gentle colour wash over the canvas to create a soft background.
Victorian homes often feature walls decorated with a pastel colour wash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wash - a thin, transparent layer of diluted paint
  • synonym: whitewash - a solution of lime and water, used for whitening walls
