run on fumes

verb
continue with little energy or resources
7.5 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / r ån …ën fjumz / (AmE), / r ån …ín fjuÀêmz / (BrE)
to continue to operate or function with very little energy, resources, or motivation left

Examples

To run on fumes all day
After working for twelve hours straight, he was running on fumes by the time he got home.
She finished her final exam week by running on fumes and lots of coffee.
The small business kept running on fumes until they finally secured a loan.

Synonyms

  • running on empty
    7.0 0.10/m
    running on empty describes having almost no energy, while run on fumes means continuing to work using only a tiny reserve.

Related Terms

  • antonym: energized - full of energy or vitality
