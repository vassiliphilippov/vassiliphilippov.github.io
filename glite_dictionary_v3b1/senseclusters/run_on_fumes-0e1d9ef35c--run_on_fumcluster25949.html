run on fumes

verb
continue with little energy or resources
8.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /rʌn ɑːn fjuːmz/ /; / /rʌn ɑːn fuːmz/ (AmE), /rʌn ɒn fjuːmz/ /; / /rʌn ɒn fuːmz/ (BrE)
