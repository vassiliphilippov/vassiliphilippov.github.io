unwilted

adjective
energetic; undiscouraged
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɪltɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɪltɪd / (BrE)
not showing signs of tiredness, discouragement, or loss of energy; remaining strong or spirited.

Examples

Unwilted spirit
After hours of hiking, he appeared remarkably unwilted and ready for more adventure.
The team remained unwilted despite the setbacks they faced during the competition.
Her unwilted spirit inspired everyone around her to keep going.

Related Terms

  • synonym: energetic - having or showing a lot of energy
  • synonym: resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties
  • antonym: wilted - showing signs of tiredness or loss of spirit
