par excellence

adverb
the very best of its kind
7.4
Pronunciation: / pɑr ˌɛksəˈlɑns, ˌpɑr ˈɛksələns / (AmE), / ˌpɑːr ɛk.sɛˈlɑ̃ːs / (BrE)
better or more outstanding than all others of the same kind; to the highest degree of excellence.

Examples

Par excellence example
He became the statesman par excellence, admired by both friends and opponents.
She is the chef par excellence, known for her exquisite desserts.
This novel is the detective story par excellence of the twentieth century.
