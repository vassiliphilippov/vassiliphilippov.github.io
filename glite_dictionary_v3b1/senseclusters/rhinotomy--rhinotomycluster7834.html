rhinotomy

noun
surgical incision of the nose
4.0 9.9
Pronunciation: /raɪ.ˈnɑː.tə.mi/ (AmE), /raɪ.ˈnɒt.ə.mi/ (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which an incision is made in the nose to drain accumulated pus

Examples
