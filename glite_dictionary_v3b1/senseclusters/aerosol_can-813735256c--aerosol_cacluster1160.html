aerosol can

noun
pressurized spray dispenser
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrəˌsɑl kæn / (AmE), / ˈɛərəˌsɒl kæn / (BrE)
A container that stores a substance under pressure and releases it as a fine spray, typically using a propellant gas.

Examples

An aerosol can spray
Always use the aerosol can in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling fumes.
He shook the aerosol can before spraying paint onto the wall.
The hairspray comes in an aerosol can for easy application.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spray can - a container that dispenses liquids in spray form
