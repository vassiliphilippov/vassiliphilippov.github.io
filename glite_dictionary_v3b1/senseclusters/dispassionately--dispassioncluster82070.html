dispassionately

adverb
objectively, without emotion or bias
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpæʃənətli / (AmE), / dɪsˈpæʃənətli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no strong emotion or bias; objectively and calmly

Examples

To speak dispassionately
He reported the incident dispassionately, without letting his feelings interfere.
She evaluated the evidence dispassionately before making her decision.
The judge listened dispassionately to both sides of the argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotionally - in a manner showing strong emotion or feeling
  • synonym: objectively - in an unbiased and impartial manner
