gone

adjective
no longer present or available
Pronunciation: / ɡɑn / (AmE), / ɡɒn / (BrE)
no longer present, used up, or belonging to the past

Examples

All hope is gone

The cake was gone before I even got a slice.

Historians study documents from gone eras to understand society's evolution.
Photographs remind us of gone summers spent at the lake.
