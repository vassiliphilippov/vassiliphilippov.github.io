official

noun
person holding an office or position of authority
2.3 3.1
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈfɪ.ʃəl/ (AmE), /ə.ˈfɪ.ʃəl/ (BrE)
a person who has authority in an organization, government, or in enforcing rules (such as in sports)

Examples

Government official
An immigration official checked our documents at the border.
Local officials are responsible for organizing the election.
The official spoke to the press after the meeting.
A heated argument broke out, so the official stepped in to mediate between the players.
Tennis matches require at least one official to ensure fair play and resolve disputes.
The official signaled that the goal was offside and disallowed the point.

  • synonym: functionary - a person who has a specific official position in an organization, especially in government
  • synonym: referee - a person appointed to oversee and enforce the rules in a sporting event
  • synonym: umpire - an official who rules on the play in certain sports
