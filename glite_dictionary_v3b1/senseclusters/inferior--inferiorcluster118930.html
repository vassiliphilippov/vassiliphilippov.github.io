inferior

adjective
of low rank or importance
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɪriɚ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɪəriə / (BrE)
of low rank, status, or importance; less important or lower in position.

Examples

Inferior job position
Despite her qualifications, she was given an inferior position in the company.
Many felt that local products were considered inferior to imported goods.
The officer treated the enlisted men as inferior staff members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: junior - lower in rank or position
  • antonym: superior - of higher rank or importance
