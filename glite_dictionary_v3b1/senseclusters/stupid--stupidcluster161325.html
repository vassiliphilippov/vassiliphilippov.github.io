stupid

adjective
lacking intelligence; senseless
Pronunciation: / ˈstuː.pɪd / (AmE), / ˈstjuː.pɪd / (BrE)
lacking intelligence or common sense; showing poor judgment

Examples

Stupid mistake
Don't ask stupid questions when you know the answer already.
It was a stupid mistake to leave his keys in the car.
Some people thought the movie was hilarious, while others found it simply stupid.
I felt stupid for forgetting her birthday again this year.
It was a stupid mistake to leave the door unlocked overnight.
The movie has a stupid plot, but it was still entertaining.
