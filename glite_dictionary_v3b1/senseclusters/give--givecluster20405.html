give

verb
to yield or collapse under pressure
9.9 3.0
Pronunciation: /gɪv/ (AmE), /gɪv/ (BrE)
to yield, bend, or collapse under pressure (physically or metaphorically)

Examples

The rope gives under weight

The bookshelf gave under the weight of the books and collapsed to the floor.
The old bridge finally gave during the storm and crashed into the river.
The rope began to give as more weight was added.
The shelf won't give under the weight of these books.
This fabric will give a little if you stretch it.
