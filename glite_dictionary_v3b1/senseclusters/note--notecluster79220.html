note

noun
short informal written message
Pronunciation: / noʊt / (AmE), / nəʊt / (BrE)
a short written message, record, or comment, often used to communicate or remember information

Examples

A note to a friend
Could you write your phone number on a note for me?

Please add a note explaining any changes you've made.
She wrote a helpful note in the margin of the report.
There was a short note attached to the end of the letter.
He passed her a note during class, hoping the teacher wouldn't notice.
Please make a note of the meeting time in your calendar.
She left a note on the kitchen table to remind him about dinner.
