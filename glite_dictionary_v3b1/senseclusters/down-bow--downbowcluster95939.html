down-bow

noun
downward stroke of a stringed instrument's bow
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊnˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊnˌbəʊ / (BrE)
a downward stroke of the bow, moving from the frog (heel) to the tip when playing a stringed instrument

Examples
