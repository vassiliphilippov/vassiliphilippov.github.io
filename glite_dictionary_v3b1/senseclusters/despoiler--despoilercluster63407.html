despoiler

noun
one who plunders or takes spoils
8.5
Pronunciation: / dɪˈspɔɪlər / (AmE), / dɪˈspɔɪlə / (BrE)
a person who steals valuables, especially by force in war; one who plunders or pillages

Examples

The despoiler of cities
Historians wrote about the despoiler who looted the city’s treasures.
The country was left in ruins by the invading despoilers who took everything of value.
The villagers tried to hide their valuables from the approaching despoilers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pillager - a person who pillages or loots, especially during war
  • synonym: plunderer - a person who steals goods, especially during war or chaos
