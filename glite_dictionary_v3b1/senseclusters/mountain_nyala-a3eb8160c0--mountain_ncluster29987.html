mountain nyala

noun
Ethiopian mountain antelope
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tən naɪˈɑː.lə / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪn naɪˈæ.lə / (BrE)
a large, shaggy antelope native to the mountains of Ethiopia, known for its long horns and striking coat
