scream

noun
loud, sharp piercing cry
1.7
Pronunciation: / skrim /; / skɹim / (AmE), / skriːm /; / skɹiːm / (BrE)
a loud, sharp, high-pitched cry or sound, especially from a person, animal, or thing

Examples

A loud scream
A sudden scream from the next room startled everyone at the party.
The child's scream echoed across the playground when she fell.
We heard a scream in the night and called the police.
A scream from the alarm echoed through the empty warehouse late at night.
The eagle's scream could be heard across the valley.
The tires let out a piercing scream as the car came to a sudden stop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: screech - a loud, harsh, piercing cry or noise
  • synonym: shriek - a high-pitched, piercing cry or sound
  • antonym: whisper - a soft or low speech sound
