bluebill

noun
North American diving duck with a bluish bill
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbluːˌbɪl / (AmE), / ˈbluːˌbɪl / (BrE)
A North American diving duck with a bluish-grey bill, commonly referring to species such as the lesser and greater scaup.

Examples

A bluebill duck
A bluebill is easily recognized by its distinctive bluish-grey beak.
