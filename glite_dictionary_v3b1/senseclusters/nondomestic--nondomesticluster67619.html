nondomestic

adjective
not domestic
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.dəˈmɛstɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.dəˈmɛstɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Her research focuses on nondomestic animals found in the wild.
The company expanded its nondomestic operations to several countries in Asia.
