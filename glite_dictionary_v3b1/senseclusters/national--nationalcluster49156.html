national

adjective
relating to nationality
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃnəl / (AmE), / ˈnaʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to a particular nationality or the status of being a member of a specific nation.

Examples

National identity
Certain scholarships are restricted to students of a particular national background.
Her national heritage played a big role in shaping her identity.
The company was accused of discriminating based on employees' national origin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnic - relating to a population subgroup with a common national or cultural tradition
  • antonym: international - involving two or more nations or nationalities
