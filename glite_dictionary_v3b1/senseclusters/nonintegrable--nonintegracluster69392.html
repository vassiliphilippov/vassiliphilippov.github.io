nonintegrable

adjective
not solvable by integration
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɪntəɡrəbl̩ / (AmE), / nɒnˈɪntɪɡrəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be solved or expressed by mathematical integration

Examples

Nonintegrable function
The function was determined to be nonintegrable over the given interval.
Physicists encountered a nonintegrable system that could not be solved analytically.
Some equations in advanced mathematics are classified as nonintegrable due to their complexity.
