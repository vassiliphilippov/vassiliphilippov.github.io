cenacle

noun
intellectual discussion group
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnəkəl / (AmE), / ˈsɛnəkəl / (BrE)
a small group of people, especially writers or artists, who meet regularly for discussion or creative exchange

Examples

A cenacle of writers
The writers' cenacle met every Friday to share their latest poems and stories.
She was invited to join a cenacle of artists who discussed new trends in painting.
The cenacle became famous for its lively debates and creative collaborations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circle - a group of people with shared interests who meet regularly
  • synonym: club - an organization or group of people with a common interest
  • synonym: salon - a gathering of people, often intellectuals or artists, for conversation
