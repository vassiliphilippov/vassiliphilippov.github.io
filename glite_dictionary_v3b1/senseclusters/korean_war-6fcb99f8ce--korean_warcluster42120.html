Korean War

proper noun
war between North and South Korea, 1950–1953
3.7
Pronunciation: / kəˈriː.ən wɔr / (AmE), / kəˈriː.ən wɔː / (BrE)
the war fought between North Korea and South Korea from 1950 to 1953, in which South Korea was supported by United Nations forces, principally the United States, and North Korea was supported by China and the Soviet Union.

Examples

The Korean War
He studied the causes and effects of the Korean War in his history class.
Many families were separated during the Korean War and still have not been reunited.
The Korean War broke out in 1950 and lasted until 1953.
