Sierra plum

noun
a shrub with small, bland red fruit
Pronunciation: / siˈɛrə plʌm / (AmE), / siˈeərə plʌm / (BrE)
a shrub native to the Pacific coast of the United States, bearing small red fruit that is typically bland in flavor

Examples

The Sierra plum shrub
Birds in the region often feed on the Sierra plum during late summer.
Despite its bright color, the fruit of the Sierra plum is usually too bland to eat fresh.
The Sierra plum thrives in coastal California, often found near streams and woodlands.
