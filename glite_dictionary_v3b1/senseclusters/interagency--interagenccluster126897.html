interagency

noun
joint agency group
3.8
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.ˈeɪ.dʒən.siː/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.ˈeɪ.dʒən.siː/ (BrE)
a group or activity involving two or more government agencies working together on a common task or policy

Examples

An interagency group
The interagency was established to improve communication between the police and fire departments.
An interagency met to discuss strategies for disaster response and recovery.
Funding for the project was provided by an interagency focused on environmental protection.
