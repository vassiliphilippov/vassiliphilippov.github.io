soaking syrup

noun
sweet liquid for moistening cakes
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊ.kɪŋ ˈsɪr.əp / (AmE), / ˈsəʊ.kɪŋ ˈsɪr.əp / (BrE)
a sweet liquid made from water, sugar, and sometimes other flavorings or alcohol, used to moisten and infuse cakes and pastries

Examples

A soaking syrup for cakes

The pastry chef brushed the cake layers with soaking syrup to ensure they stayed moist.
