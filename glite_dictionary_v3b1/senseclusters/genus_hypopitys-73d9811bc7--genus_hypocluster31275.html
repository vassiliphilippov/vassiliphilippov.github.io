genus Hypopitys

noun
former genus of pinesaps (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs haɪˈpɑː.pɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs hɪˈpɒ.pɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A former botanical genus name used for pinesaps, now usually included in the genus Monotropa.
