secundum

adverb
next in sequence
9.9
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈkʌn.dəm/ /; / /sɪ.ˈkʌn.dəm/ (AmE), /sə.ˈkʌn.dəm/ /; / /sɪ.ˈkʌn.dəm/ (BrE)
used to indicate the next point or item in a sequence, similar to 'secondly' or 'next'.

Examples

Secundum in sequence
The philosopher began with his main argument and then wrote secundum to introduce the next point.
In medieval manuscripts, scribes often used secundum to mark the second section.
He listed his reasons as 'primum' for the first and secundum for the second.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firstly - used to introduce the first point or reason
  • synonym: secondly - used to introduce the second point or reason
