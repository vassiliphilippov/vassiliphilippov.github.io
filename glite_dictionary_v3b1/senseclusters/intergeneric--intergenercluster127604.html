intergeneric

adjective
between genera
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərdʒəˈnɛrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntədʒəˈnɛrɪk / (BrE)
occurring or existing between different genera, especially in biological classification

Examples

Intergeneric hybrid
Scientists observed an intergeneric hybrid resulting from the cross of two unrelated plant genera.
The creation of intergeneric organisms is rare and often requires advanced genetic techniques.
Botanists discussed the challenges of producing intergeneric hybrids in their research paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interspecific - occurring between different species
  • antonym: intrageneric - occurring within the same genus
