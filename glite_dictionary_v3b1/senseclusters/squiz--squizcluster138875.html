squiz

verb
to glance quickly
Pronunciation: / skwɪz / (AmE), / skwɪz / (BrE)
to look quickly or take a brief glance at something, especially in an informal way

Examples

To squiz the menu
He took a quick squiz at the newspaper headlines before leaving for work.
Can I squiz your notes to see what I missed in class?
She gave the menu a squiz before deciding what to order.

Related Terms

  • synonym: glance - to look quickly at something
  • synonym: peek - to look quickly and secretly at something
  • antonym: stare - to look at something for a long time
