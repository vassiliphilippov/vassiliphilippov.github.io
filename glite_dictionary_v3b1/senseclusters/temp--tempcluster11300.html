temp

noun
temporary office worker
7.9
Pronunciation: / tɛmp / (AmE), / tɛmp / (BrE)
a person employed to work for a short period, especially in an office, usually to cover for another employee or to meet a temporary increase in workload

Examples

An office temp
After their assistant went on leave, they hired a temp to keep the office running smoothly.
Many recent graduates work as a temp before finding a full-time job.
The company relies on temps during the busy holiday season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: temporary - lasting for only a limited period; not permanent
