double-bass

adjective
octave below standard bass (musical)
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʌbəlˈbeɪs / (AmE), / ˌdʌbəlˈbeɪs / (BrE)
(of a musical instrument or vocal range) pitched an octave below the standard bass.

Examples

A double-bass flute
A double-bass flute is extremely rare due to its deep, resonant range.
He specializes in double-bass voices, singing parts an octave lower than most basses.
The double-bass clarinet produces notes much lower than the regular bass clarinet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contrabass - an instrument or range an octave below the standard bass
