closing curtain

noun
the final part of a performance
Pronunciation: / ˈkloʊzɪŋ ˈkɝːtn̩ / (AmE), / ˈkləʊzɪŋ ˈkɜːtn̩ / (BrE)
the final part of a performance, especially when the stage curtain is lowered to mark the end

Examples

The closing curtain
After the closing curtain, the actors gathered onstage to thank the director.
As the closing curtain fell, the audience gave the cast a standing ovation.
The surprise twist was revealed just before the closing curtain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finale - the last part of a performance or event
