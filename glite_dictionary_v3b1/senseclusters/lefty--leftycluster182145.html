lefty

noun
left-handed person or pitcher
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛfti / (AmE), / ˈlɛfti / (BrE)
an informal term for a left-handed person, especially in sports

Examples

As a lefty, he has a natural curveball that confuses most batters.

As a lefty, he found it difficult to find a pair of scissors that worked comfortably.
