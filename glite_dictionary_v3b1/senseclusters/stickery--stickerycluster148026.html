stickery

adjective
prickly or covered in stickers
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪkəri / (AmE), / ˈstɪkəri / (BrE)
having a surface covered with stickers or small sharp points that can prick or scratch

Examples

Stickery plants
Be careful when walking through the stickery field, as the plants can scratch your legs.
The dog's fur was full of stickery seeds after our hike in the woods.
She avoided the stickery bushes along the path to prevent getting pricked.

  • antonym: smooth - having an even and flat surface without roughness
