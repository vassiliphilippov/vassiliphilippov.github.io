proleptic

adjective
applied before official use
Pronunciation: / proʊˈlɛptɪk / (AmE), / prəˈlɛptɪk / (BrE)
relating to the application of a calendar or system to a time before its official introduction

Examples

Proleptic calendar date
The proleptic Julian calendar is used to assign dates to events before its official adoption.
Researchers often use proleptic dating to standardize historical timelines.
A proleptic approach helps historians compare events across different calendar systems.
