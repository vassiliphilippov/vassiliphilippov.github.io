shipfitter

noun
ship metalworker
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪpˌfɪtər / (AmE), / ˈʃɪpˌfɪtə / (BrE)
a skilled worker who assembles, shapes, fits, and repairs metal parts or structures on ships

Examples

A shipfitter repairs ships
The navy hired a skilled shipfitter to help with the repairs on the destroyer.
As a shipfitter, she spent her days welding and shaping metal plates for new vessels.
During the overhaul, the shipfitter inspected the hull for any signs of damage.
The experienced shipfitter worked long hours to finish the new vessel on time.
A shipfitter must be skilled in reading blueprints and handling heavy metal plates.
During the repair, the shipfitter replaced several damaged sections of the hull.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carpenter - a person who builds or repairs wooden structures
  • synonym: welder - a person who joins metal parts together by welding
