lime tree

noun
deciduous tree of the genus Tilia
3.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààla…™m tri / (AmE), / Ààla…™m triÀê / (BrE)
A deciduous tree of the genus Tilia, with heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish, often fragrant flowers, several species of which provide valuable timber.

Examples

Tall lime tree
Carpenters prized the timber from the lime tree for its fine grain and workability.
In June, the sweet scent of lime tree blossoms fills the park.
The avenue was lined with ancient lime trees whose branches formed a green canopy overhead.
