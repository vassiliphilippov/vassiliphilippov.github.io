usually

adverb
in most cases; ordinarily
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːʒuəli / (AmE), / ˈjuːʒuəli / (BrE)
Under normal conditions; in most cases or on most occasions.

Examples

He usually has cereal
He usually has cereal for breakfast, but sometimes he makes eggs instead.
I usually take the bus to work, but today I decided to walk.
The weather is usually warm in July, though there are occasional cold days.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: always - at all times; on all occasions
  • synonym: normally - under normal circumstances; in the usual way
  • degree lower: occasionally - now and then; from time to time
  • antonym: rarely - not occurring often; infrequently
