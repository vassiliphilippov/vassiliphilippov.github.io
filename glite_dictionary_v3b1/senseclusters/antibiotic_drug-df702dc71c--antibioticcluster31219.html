antibiotic drug

noun
medicine that kills bacteria
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.ti.baɪˈɑ.tɪk ˌdrʌɡ / (AmE), / ˌæntɪ.baɪˈɒ.tɪk ˌdrʌɡ / (BrE)
A chemical substance, typically derived from microorganisms, that kills or inhibits the growth of bacteria and is used to treat bacterial infections.

Examples

An antibiotic drug treatment
Many common illnesses are now easily managed with the right antibiotic drug.
Overuse of antibiotic drugs can lead to antibiotic resistance.
The doctor prescribed an antibiotic drug to treat the bacterial infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antibiotic - a substance used to kill or inhibit bacteria
  • synonym: antimicrobial - an agent that kills microorganisms or stops their growth
