sheltered workshop

noun
workplace for people with disabilities
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɛltɚd ˈwɝkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈʃɛltəd ˈwɜːkʃɒp / (BrE)
a workplace that provides employment for people with physical or developmental disabilities in a supportive environment

Examples

A sheltered workshop
After her accident, she found employment at a local sheltered workshop.
Many sheltered workshops focus on skills development for their employees.
The organization provides training and meaningful work in a sheltered workshop.
