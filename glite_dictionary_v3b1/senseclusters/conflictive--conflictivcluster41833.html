conflictive

adjective
showing disagreement
7.4
Pronunciation: / kənˈflɪktɪv / (AmE), / kənˈflɪktɪv / (BrE)
showing or involving disagreement, incompatibility, or mutual conflict between facts, statements, or people

Examples

Conflictive opinions
The committee faced conflictive opinions that made it difficult to reach a decision.
Their conflictive statements confused everyone at the meeting.
The evidence presented by the two experts was highly conflictive and led to further debate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compatible - able to exist or work together without conflict
  • synonym: contradictory - containing or involving elements that oppose or disagree with each other
