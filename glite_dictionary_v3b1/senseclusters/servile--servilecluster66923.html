servile

adjective
excessively submissive; fawning
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝː.vaɪl, ˈsɝː.vəl / (AmE), / ˈsɜː.vaɪl, ˈsɜː.vɪl / (BrE)
having or showing an excessive willingness to serve or please others; overly submissive or fawning.

Examples

Servile attitude
His servile flattery made him unpopular with his colleagues.
She refused to adopt a servile attitude, even when faced with authority.
The manager demanded servile obedience from all her employees.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assertive - having a confident and forceful personality
  • synonym: obsequious - obedient or attentive to an excessive degree
  • synonym: submissive - willing to submit to others; meek
