agent provocateur

noun
person who incites others to break law secretly
8.3 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈeɪ.dʒənt prə.və.kə.ˈtɜːr/ /; / /ˈeɪ.dʒənt prə.vɑː.kə.ˈtɜːr/ (AmE), /ˈeɪ.dʒənt prə.və.kə.ˈtɜː/ /; / /ˈeɪ.dʒənt prə.vɒ.kə.ˈtɜː/ (BrE)
A person who secretly encourages others to commit illegal acts in order to incriminate or discredit them, often on behalf of a government or police force.

Examples

An agent provocateur infiltrates groups
Authorities denied using an agent provocateur to infiltrate the activist movement.
Several arrests were made after an agent provocateur encouraged the group to break into the building.
The protesters feared that an agent provocateur was trying to start violence to justify a police crackdown.
