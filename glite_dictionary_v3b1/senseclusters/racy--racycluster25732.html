racy

adjective
sexually suggestive or mildly indecent
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪ.si / (AmE), / ˈreɪ.si / (BrE)
(of language, stories, etc.) slightly shocking, often because of referring to sex in a slightly rude or humorous way.

Examples

Racy jokes
She blushed at the racy gossip being shared at the dinner table.
Some people found the comedian's jokes a bit too racy for a family audience.
The author was known for his racy anecdotes that amused and shocked in equal measure.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocent - not intended to be shocking or indecent; not suggestive
  • synonym: naughty - (of language or humor) mildly indecent; referring to sex in a cheeky way
  • synonym: risqué - slightly indecent or liable to shock, especially by being sexually suggestive
  • synonym: spicy - (of language or stories) exciting or slightly indecent, sexually suggestive
