pencil sharpener

noun
device for sharpening pencils
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnsəl ˌʃɑːrpnər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnsəl ˌʃɑːpənə / (BrE)
A device used to make the tip of a pencil sharp by shaving its wood and graphite.
