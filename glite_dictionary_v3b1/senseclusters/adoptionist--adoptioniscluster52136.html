adoptionist

adjective
relating to adoptionism
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈdɑpʃənɪst / (AmE), / əˈdɒpʃənɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the doctrine that Jesus was adopted as the Son of God rather than being divine by nature.

Examples

Adoptionist belief
The scholar presented an adoptionist interpretation of early Christian texts.
Some theologians criticized the adoptionist view as inconsistent with traditional doctrine.
Her research explores the influence of adoptionist beliefs in medieval Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adoptionism - the doctrine that Jesus was adopted as the Son of God
  • antonym: orthodox - conforming to traditional or established beliefs, especially in religion
