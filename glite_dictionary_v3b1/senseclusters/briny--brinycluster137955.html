briny

adjective
slightly salty, like seawater
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪni / (AmE), / ˈbraɪni / (BrE)
(of water or taste) containing or resembling salt; somewhat salty, especially from mixing with seawater.

Examples

The briny sea
A sharp breeze carried the briny scent of the ocean across the harbor.
The oysters were plump and had a distinctly briny flavor.
They explored a briny marsh where the river met the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brackish - slightly salty, especially from a mix of sea and fresh water
  • antonym: fresh - not salty, as in fresh water
