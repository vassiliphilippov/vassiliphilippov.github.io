nog

noun
sweet holiday drink
7.2
Pronunciation: / nɑɡ / (AmE), / nɒɡ / (BrE)
a sweet, creamy drink made with eggs and milk, often served during holidays such as Christmas

Examples

A glass of nog
We served homemade nog at the Christmas party.
He poured himself a glass of nog and sat by the fire.
Many families have their own special recipe for nog during the holidays.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eggnog - a traditional holiday drink made with eggs, milk, sugar, and often alcohol
