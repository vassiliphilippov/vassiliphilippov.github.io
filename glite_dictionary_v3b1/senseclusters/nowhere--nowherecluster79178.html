nowhere

noun
an imaginary or unimportant place
Pronunciation: / ˈnoʊˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈnəʊˌwɛə / (BrE)
an imaginary or insignificant place that is considered to be remote or unimportant

Examples

A town called nowhere
After years living in nowhere, he moved to the city to pursue his dreams.
Suddenly, a new competitor appeared out of nowhere and surprised everyone.
The town was so small, it felt like nowhere to most visitors.
