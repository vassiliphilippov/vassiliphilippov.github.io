Hollywoke agenda

noun
perceived promotion of progressive values in entertainment
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːliˌwoʊk əˈdʒɛndə / (AmE), / ˈhɒliˌwəʊk əˈdʒɛndə / (BrE)
A derogatory term for the perceived promotion of progressive, politically correct, or socially aware values in the entertainment industry, especially in film and television.

Examples

Hollywoke agenda in films
Online forums debated whether new TV shows were pushing a Hollywoke agenda to attract younger audiences.
Some critics argue that the latest superhero movie has a Hollywoke agenda overshadowing its story.
The director denied claims that there was a deliberate Hollywoke agenda in the casting choices.
