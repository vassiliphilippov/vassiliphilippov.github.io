with eyes closed

adverb
without hesitation or with full trust
1.6
Pronunciation: / wɪð aɪz kloʊzd / (AmE), / wɪð aɪz kləʊzd / (BrE)
doing something without hesitation, careful consideration, or with complete trust, often relying on instinct or confidence

Examples

To do with eyes closed
After years of practice, she could bake the cake with eyes closed.
He accepted the job offer with eyes closed, trusting his friend's advice.
They would follow their leader with eyes closed, believing in her completely.
