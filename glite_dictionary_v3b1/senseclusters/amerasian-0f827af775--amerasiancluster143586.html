Amerasian

adjective
of mixed American-Asian
Pronunciation: / ˌæməˈreɪʒən / (AmE), / ˌæməˈreɪʒən / (BrE)
relating to a person of mixed American and Asian parentage, especially with one American and one Asian parent.

Examples

Amerasian children
The organization provides support for Amerasian children and their families.
Many Amerasian students face unique challenges in adapting to new cultures.
The documentary explores the lives of Amerasian communities in Southeast Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biracial - having parents of two different races
  • synonym: multiracial - relating to people of more than one racial background
