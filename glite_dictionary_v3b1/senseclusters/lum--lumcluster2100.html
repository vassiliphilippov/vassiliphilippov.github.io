lum

noun
chimney (dialect)
Pronunciation: / lʌm / (AmE), / lʌm / (BrE)
a chimney, especially as used in Scottish or northern English dialects

Examples

A lum chimney
The old cottage had a single lum that puffed smoke into the frosty air.
After lighting the fire, they watched the smoke curl up the lum and disappear.
In the village, every house had a stone lum rising above its roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chimney - a vertical structure that allows smoke to escape from a building
