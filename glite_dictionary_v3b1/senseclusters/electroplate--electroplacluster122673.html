electroplate

noun
object plated with metal by electrolysis
7.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɛktroʊˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈlɛktrəʊˌpleɪt / (BrE)
an object that has been coated with a thin layer of metal by the process of electrolysis

Examples

A silver electroplate
Many cutlery items were made from electroplate rather than solid silver to reduce costs.
She displayed an antique electroplate tea set inherited from her grandmother.
The silver electroplate on the candlestick had worn thin over the years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electroplating - the process of coating objects with metal using electrolysis
