Vedantist

noun
follower of Vedanta
Pronunciation: / veɪˈdɑːntɪst / (AmE), / vɪˈdæntɪst / (BrE)
a person who follows or studies the Vedanta school of Hindu philosophy

Examples

A Vedantist teacher
The Vedantist shared insights from ancient texts during the seminar.
As a dedicated Vedantist, she meditates daily and studies spiritual philosophy.
Many Vedantists gather annually to discuss interpretations of the Upanishads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hindu - a follower of Hinduism
  • synonym: philosopher - a person engaged in the study of philosophy
