micropayment

noun
very small online payment
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ ˈpeɪ.mənt/ (BrE)
a very small payment, usually made online for digital content or services

Examples

A micropayment system
The website uses a micropayment system so users can pay a few cents for each article they read.
Many mobile games rely on micropayments for players to unlock extra features or content.
With the rise of digital media, micropayment models have become more popular for online services.
Many online newspapers charge a small micropayment to access individual articles.
The app uses micropayments so users can unlock extra features for just a few cents.
Artists can earn revenue through micropayments when fans stream their music online.
