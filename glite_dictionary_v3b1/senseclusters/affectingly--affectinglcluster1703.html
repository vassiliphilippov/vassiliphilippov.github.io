affectingly

adverb
in a moving or touching manner
6.5
Pronunciation: / əˈfɛktɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈfɛktɪŋli / (BrE)
In a manner that evokes strong emotions, especially sympathy or sadness; touchingly or movingly.

Examples

To speak affectingly
He described his loss affectingly, capturing the attention of everyone in the room.
She spoke affectingly about her childhood experiences during the ceremony.
The letter was written so affectingly that it moved many readers to tears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: poignantly - in a way that causes a strong feeling of sadness or regret
  • synonym: touchingly - in a way that arouses feelings of tenderness or sympathy
