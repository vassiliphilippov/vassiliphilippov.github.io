integrationist

noun
supporter of unification
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈɡreɪʃənɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports the unification of different groups, especially racial or social groups, into one society

Examples

An integrationist leader
As an integrationist, she worked tirelessly to bring communities together.
The integrationist movement gained momentum during the civil rights era.
Many integrationists believe that diversity strengthens society as a whole.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integration - the process of combining different groups into one society
  • antonym: segregationist - a person who supports keeping different groups separate
