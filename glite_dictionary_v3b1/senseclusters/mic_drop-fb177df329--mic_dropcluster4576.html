mic drop

verb
end with an impressive closing
8.1 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / maÉªk drÉ‘p / (AmE), / maÉªk drÉ’p / (BrE)
(informal) to end a speech, performance, remark, or online exchange with a decisive, impressive closing or gesture, implying that nothing more needs to be said.

Examples

To mic drop after speaking
She decided to mic drop after her last line to show she had no more arguments.
After his brilliant reply, he mic-dropped and left the stage to thunderous applause.
In the thread, users often mic drop with one-line comebacks to end the discussion.
