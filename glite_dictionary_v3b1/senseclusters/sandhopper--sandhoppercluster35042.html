sandhopper

noun
small jumping beach crustacean
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌhɑːpər / (AmE), / ˈsændˌhɒpə / (BrE)
a small amphipod crustacean that hops like a flea and is commonly found on ocean beaches.

Examples

A sandhopper on beach
A sandhopper can often be found hiding beneath seaweed on the beach.
As we walked along the shore, we watched sandhoppers leap away from our footsteps.
Children love to chase the sandhopper as it jumps quickly across the sand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beach flea - another name for a sandhopper, small hopping crustacean of beaches
