rostrocaudal

adjective
front-to-back direction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɑstroʊˈkɔdəl / (AmE), / ˌrɒstrəʊˈkɔːdəl / (BrE)
extending or relating to the direction from the front (rostral) to the back (caudal) of an organism

Examples

Rostrocaudal direction

Damage in the rostrocaudal pathway can affect both movement and sensation.
