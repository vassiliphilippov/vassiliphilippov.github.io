petite

adjective
small and dainty in build (woman)
6.6 3.3
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈtiːt/ (AmE), /pə.ˈtiːt/ (BrE)
describing a woman or a clothing size as small and slender in build

Examples

Petite woman
Despite her petite stature, she has a commanding presence on stage.
In the modelling world, being petite is considered unique and appealing.

She always chooses petite dresses because they fit her frame perfectly.
The store has a special section for petite clothing to suit shorter women.
As a petite woman, she sometimes finds it hard to buy jeans that fit well.
