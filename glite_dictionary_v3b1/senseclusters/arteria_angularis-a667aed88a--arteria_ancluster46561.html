arteria angularis

noun
terminal branch of the facial artery
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪriə æŋˈɡjularɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriə æŋˈɡjuːlɑːrɪs / (BrE)
the terminal branch of the facial artery, supplying blood to the area around the inner corner of the eye

Examples

The arteria angularis branch
In surgical procedures around the eye, care must be taken to avoid damaging the arteria angularis.
The arteria angularis is important in the vascularization of the upper cheek and nose area.
The arteria angularis supplies blood to structures near the medial canthus of the eye.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angular artery - the terminal branch of the facial artery near the nose and eye
