double rainbow

noun
two rainbows visible together
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈreɪnˌboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈreɪnbəʊ / (BrE)
a phenomenon in which two rainbows appear in the sky at the same time, with the second one usually fainter and outside the primary arc

Examples

A double rainbow in sky
Many people stopped to take photos of the rare double rainbow spanning the sky.
Seeing a double rainbow is considered by some cultures to be a sign of good luck.
The kids were thrilled when a double rainbow appeared after the summer storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rainbow - an arc of colors visible in the sky caused by refraction of sunlight
