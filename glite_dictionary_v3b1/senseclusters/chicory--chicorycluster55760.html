chicory

noun
inulin extract used as a prebiotic
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪ.kə.riː/ /; / /ˈtʃɪ.krɪ/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪ.kə.riː/ /; / /ˈtʃɪ.krɪ/ (BrE)
a soluble dietary fiber (inulin) extracted from the root of the chicory plant, used as a prebiotic and as a food ingredient or supplement additive.
