Synonyms

  • demolish
    3.7 3/m
    demolish is a formal word for destroying a building completely, while pull down is more casual and physical.
  • raze
    7.1 0.8/m
    raze means to destroy a building so nothing remains, while pull down suggests bringing a building down to ground level.
  • tear down
    3.7 6/m
    tear down is a common phrase for removing a building piece by piece or force, while pull down feels more physical and casual.
  • knock down
    3.2 3/m
    knock down suggests quick violent collapse from impact or blast, while pull down often means to bring a structure down deliberately.
  • take down
    3.4 2/m
    take down often means to remove or dismantle parts carefully, while pull down suggests demolishing to make a building level.
  • dismantle
    4.6 3/m
    dismantle implies taking a structure apart piece by piece for reuse, while pull down implies knocking it flat to ground level.
  • level
    3.7 4/m
    level means make something flat or even in any setting, while pull down specifically means demolish a building to ground level.
