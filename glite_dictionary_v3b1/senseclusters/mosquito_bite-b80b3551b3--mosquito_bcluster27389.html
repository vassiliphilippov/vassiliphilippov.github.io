mosquito bite

noun
itchy swelling from a mosquito's sting
1.0
Pronunciation: / məˈskitoʊ baɪt / (AmE), / məˈskiːtəʊ baɪt / (BrE)
A small, itchy swelling on the skin caused by a mosquito's sting when it feeds on blood.
