popular music genre

noun
widely popular type of music
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑːpjələr ˈmjuːzɪk ˈʒɑːnrə / (AmE), / ˈpɒpjʊlə ˈmjuːzɪk ˈʒɒnrə / (BrE)
