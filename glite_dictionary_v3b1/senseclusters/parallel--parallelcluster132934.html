Examples

The 30th parallel
Explorers used the 30th parallel as a reference point for their journey.
Many cities are located along the same parallel, experiencing similar climates.
The 49th parallel marks part of the border between the United States and Canada.

Synonyms

    3.4 0.03/m
    line of latitude is an imaginary circle around Earth that marks position north or south, while parallel names that circle.
  • parallel of latitude
    5.1 0.02/m
    parallel of latitude is the more formal map term for an imaginary circle, while parallel is the shorter common name.
  • latitude
    3.9 5/m
    latitude usually means the distance north or south in degrees, while parallel names the imaginary line that marks that distance.
