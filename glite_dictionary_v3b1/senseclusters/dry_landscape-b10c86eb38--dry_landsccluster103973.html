dry landscape

noun
area with little moisture or vegetation
2.4
Pronunciation: / draɪ ˈlænd.skeɪp / (AmE), / draɪ ˈlan(d).skeɪp / (BrE)
an area of land with little vegetation or moisture, often characterized by arid conditions

Examples

A dry landscape area
After months without rain, the valley became a dry landscape dotted with rocks and sparse shrubs.
Artists are often inspired by the colors and textures of a dry landscape under the harsh midday sun.
The road wound its way through the dry landscape for miles without a single tree in sight.
