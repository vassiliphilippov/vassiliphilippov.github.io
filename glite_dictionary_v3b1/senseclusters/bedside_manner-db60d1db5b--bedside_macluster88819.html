bedside manner

noun
way a doctor treats and talks to patients
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛd.saɪd ˈmæn.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɛd.saɪd ˈmæn.ə / (BrE)
the way in which a doctor or other medical professional behaves towards and communicates with patients, especially in showing empathy, care, and professionalism

Examples

Bedside manner of doctor
Despite his skill, his poor bedside manner made some patients uncomfortable.
Patients appreciate nurses who have a calm and gentle bedside manner.
The new doctor has an excellent bedside manner that puts patients at ease.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bedside - the area beside a person's bed (especially in hospital)
