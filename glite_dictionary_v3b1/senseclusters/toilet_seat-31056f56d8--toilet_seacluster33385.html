toilet seat

noun
hinged seat for a toilet
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔɪlət siːt / (AmE), / ˈtɔɪlɪt siːt / (BrE)
a hinged, often oval seat attached to the top of a toilet bowl

Examples

The toilet seat is up
He forgot to put the toilet seat down after using the bathroom.
She wiped the toilet seat before sitting down.
The toilet seat was broken and needed to be replaced.
