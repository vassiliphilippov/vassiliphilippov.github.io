wise

noun
archaic: manner, way
5.0
Pronunciation: / waɪz / (AmE), / waɪz / (BrE)
(archaic) A way or manner of doing something; method or fashion.

Examples

In no wise
He claimed, in no wise, to be involved in the affair.
The instructions were followed in the same wise as before.
The treaty was concluded in this wise, bringing peace to the region.
