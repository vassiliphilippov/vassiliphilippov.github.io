shrub

noun
sweetened fruit-and-vinegar drink
8.2
Pronunciation: /ʃrʌb/ (AmE), /ʃrʌb/ (BrE)
a sweetened, vinegar-based beverage made by infusing fruit (or fruit juice) with sugar and vinegar; used as a refreshing drink or a cocktail mixer.

Examples

Raspberry shrub drink
The bartender served a bright raspberry shrub to brighten the cocktail.
She made a jar of apple shrub by steeping fruit with sugar and vinegar.
Cocktail recipes often call for a tablespoon of shrub to add sweet-tart flavor.
