proponent

noun
supporter or advocate
9.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊnənt / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊnənt / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates a particular idea, plan, or cause

Examples

A proponent of change
Many proponents of organic food believe it is healthier than conventionally grown food.
She has long been a strong proponent of environmental protection policies.
The proponents of the new law argued that it would benefit small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advocate - a person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy
  • antonym: opponent - a person who is against or competes with another in a contest, conflict or debate
