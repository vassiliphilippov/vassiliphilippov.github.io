conferment

noun
the act of bestowing an honor or gift
7.1 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / k…ônÀàf…ùÀêm…ônt / (AmE), / k…ônÀàf…úÀêm…ônt / (BrE)
The act of officially giving someone an honor, degree, or gift.

Examples

The conferment of honors
At the banquet, the conferment of the prize was met with enthusiastic applause.
She felt deeply honored by the conferment of the national award.
The conferment of the honorary degree took place during the university's commencement ceremony.

Synonyms
