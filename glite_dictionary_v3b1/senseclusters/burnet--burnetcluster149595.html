burnet

noun
meadow herb
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜrnɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɜːnɪt / (BrE)
a herbaceous plant with small clustered flowers, commonly found in meadows and grasslands

Examples

Wild burnet plant
The burnet in her garden attracted many bees.
Wild burnet grows abundantly in the meadows near the river.
