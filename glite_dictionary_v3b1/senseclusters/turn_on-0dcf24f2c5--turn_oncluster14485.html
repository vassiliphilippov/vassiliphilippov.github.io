Synonyms

  • depend on
    1.7 40/m
    depend on is the neutral, common verb used in speech and writing, while turn on is a phrasal verb meaning the same in many contexts.
  • hinge on
    4.2 2/m
    hinge on is a stronger phrase that suggests a result depends completely on one factor, while turn on is more general.
  • ride
    5.3 11/m
    ride often sounds informal and suggests an outcome hangs on luck or chance, while turn on stays neutral and means depend on.
