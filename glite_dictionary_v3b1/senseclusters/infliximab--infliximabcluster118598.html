infliximab

noun
monoclonal antibody medication for autoimmune diseases
9.6
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈflɪksɪmæb / (AmE), / ɪnˈflɪksɪmæb / (BrE)
a drug (monoclonal antibody) used to treat autoimmune diseases such as Crohn's disease and rheumatoid arthritis
