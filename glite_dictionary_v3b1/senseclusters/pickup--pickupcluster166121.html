pickup

noun
device on a record player holding the cartridge
7.6 5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.ʌp/ (BrE)
a device on a record player that holds the needle and converts vibrations from the record into sound

Examples

Record player pickup
A high-quality pickup can significantly improve the sound of your record player.

The technician replaced the faulty pickup on the vintage turntable.
Be careful when cleaning the pickup to avoid damaging the needle.
He upgraded his old pickup to get better audio from his vinyl records.
The turntable's pickup needed to be replaced to improve sound quality.
