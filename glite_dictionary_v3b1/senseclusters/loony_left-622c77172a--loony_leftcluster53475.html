loony left

noun
derogatory term for extreme left-wing politics
9.8 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààluni l…õft / (AmE), / ÀàluÀêni l…õft / (BrE)
A derogatory term for individuals or groups who hold extremely left-wing political views, often implying that their positions are irrational or unreasonable.

Examples

The loony left group

The new candidate was quickly labeled part of the loony left by her opponents.
