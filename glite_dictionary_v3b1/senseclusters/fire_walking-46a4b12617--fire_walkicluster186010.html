fire walking

noun
walking barefoot over hot embers as a ritual
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪər ˌwɔkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ˌwɔːkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or ceremony of walking barefoot over hot stones or a bed of embers, often as part of a ritual or demonstration

Examples

Fire walking ceremony
Participants in the festival bravely performed fire walking to honor their ancestors.
Safety precautions are essential when organizing a public fire walking event.
Some cultures believe fire walking can bring spiritual purification and strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: firewalker - a person who participates in fire walking
