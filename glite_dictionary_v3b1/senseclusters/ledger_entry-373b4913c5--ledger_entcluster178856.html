ledger entry

noun
record of a financial transaction in a ledger
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒɚ ˈɛntri / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒə ˈɛntri / (BrE)
a record in a ledger showing details of a single commercial transaction; an account entry in bookkeeping

Examples

A ledger entry record
Auditors carefully review every ledger entry to ensure financial accuracy.
Each ledger entry must include the date, amount, and a description of the transaction.
The accountant recorded the purchase as a ledger entry in the company's books.

Related Terms

  • synonym: journal entry - an initial accounting record of a transaction before posting to the ledger
