closed corporation

A corporation whose shares are held by a small group of people and are not available for public trading.

Examples

A closed corporation group
Many small tech startups begin as a closed corporation with only a few shareholders.
The family business was converted into a closed corporation to limit outside ownership.
Unlike a public company, a closed corporation does not trade its shares on the stock market.
