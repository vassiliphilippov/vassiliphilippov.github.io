temperance

noun
moderation; avoidance of excess
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpərəns /; / ˈtɛmp(ə)rəns / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpərəns /; / ˈtɛmp(ə)rəns / (BrE)
moderation or self-restraint, especially in eating, drinking, or behavior

Examples
