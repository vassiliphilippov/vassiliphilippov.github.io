channeling

noun
spirit communication
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃænəlɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of receiving and communicating messages from a spirit, deity, or other non-physical entity, often through a medium

Examples

Channeling a spirit
She believes her channeling allows her to receive guidance from ancient spirits.
During the channeling session, the medium spoke in a voice that was not her own.
Some people attend workshops to learn the art of channeling messages from the spirit world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mediumship - the practice of mediating communication between spirits and living people
  • synonym: seance - a meeting at which people attempt to make contact with the dead
