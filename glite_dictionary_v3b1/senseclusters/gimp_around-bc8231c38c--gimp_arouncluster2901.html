gimp around

verb
move awkwardly or slowly
Pronunciation: / ɡɪmp əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡɪmp əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move awkwardly or slowly, often due to injury or stiffness

Examples

To gimp around slowly
