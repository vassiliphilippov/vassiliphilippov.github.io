reverse

noun
a running play in American football
2.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɝːs / (AmE), / rɪˈvɜːs / (BrE)
(in American football) a running play in which a player running in one direction hands the ball to a teammate running in the opposite direction.

Examples

A football reverse
Fans cheered as the wide receiver ran a reverse for a big gain.
The coach called a reverse late in the fourth quarter to catch the defense off guard.
Their offense scored a touchdown on a perfectly executed reverse during the playoff game.
