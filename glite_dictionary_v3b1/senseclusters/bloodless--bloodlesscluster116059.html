bloodless

adjective
lacking blood or appearing so
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈblʌd.ləs / (AmE), / ˈblʌd.ləs / (BrE)
pale or drained of blood; lacking color, especially from illness or shock

Examples

Bloodless face
Her bloodless lips hinted at her illness.
The battlefield was strewn with the bloodless remains of fallen soldiers.
The vampire left behind a bloodless body, drained of all life.
After the accident, his face became suddenly bloodless and drawn.
She tried to speak, but her bloodless lips trembled with fear.
The doctor's bloodless appearance worried his colleagues.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flushed - red or rosy in the face, especially from emotion or exertion
  • synonym: pale - having little color; light in color
  • synonym: pallid - pale, typically because of poor health or shock
