quick breather

noun
short rest or pause
9.2
Pronunciation: / kwɪk ˈbriðɚ / (AmE), / kwɪk ˈbriːðə / (BrE)
A short break or pause to rest and recover, especially during work or physical activity.

Examples

Take a quick breather
After jogging for ten minutes, she stopped for a quick breather under the tree.
He paused during the exam to have a quick breather and refocus.
Let's take a quick breather before continuing with the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break - a period of rest from work or activity
  • synonym: rest - a period of relaxing or ceasing activity
