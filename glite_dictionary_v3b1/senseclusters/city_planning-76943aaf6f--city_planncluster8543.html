city planning

noun
designing and managing urban development
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti ˌplænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪti ˌplænɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of designing and managing the physical development of urban areas, including the layout of buildings, roads, and public spaces

Examples

Effective city planning
Effective city planning can improve quality of life for urban residents.
She decided to study city planning because she wanted to help design better cities.
The conference will address recent innovations in city planning across different regions.
