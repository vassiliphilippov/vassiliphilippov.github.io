rawhide

noun
untanned animal hide, esp. from cattle
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔˌhaɪd / (AmE), / ˈrɔːhaɪd / (BrE)
untanned animal hide, especially from cattle, used for making whips, ropes, and similar items

Examples

Rawhide rope
