sonorant

adjective
phonetic sound type
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑː.nə.rənt/ /; / /sə.ˈnɔːr.ənt/ (AmE), /ˈsɒ.nə.rənt/ /; / /sə.ˈnɔː.rənt/ (BrE)
relating to speech sounds produced with continuous, non-turbulent airflow in the vocal tract, such as vowels and some consonants

Examples

Sonorant speech sound
In linguistics, a sonorant sound is produced with a smooth, uninterrupted airflow.
Vowels and certain consonants like 'l' and 'm' are classified as sonorant in phonetics.
The teacher explained that sonorant sounds are different from those that block the airflow.
