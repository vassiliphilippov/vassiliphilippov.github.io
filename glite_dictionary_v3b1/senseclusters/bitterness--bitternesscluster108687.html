bitterness

noun
deep anger and resentment
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪtərnəs / (AmE), / ˈbɪtənəs / (BrE)
a feeling of deep anger, resentment, or a harsh attitude

Examples

Feel bitterness inside
He struggled to let go of the bitterness that had built up over time.
Her bitterness toward her former employer was obvious in every conversation.
Years of injustice fueled his bitterness and mistrust of authority.
Despite his bitterness, he managed to congratulate the winner with a forced smile.
Her bitterness was evident in the way she spoke to her former colleagues.
There was a hint of bitterness in his response, making the conversation tense.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the act of ceasing to feel resentment or anger against someone
  • synonym: resentment - a feeling of persistent ill will or anger at being treated unfairly
  • antonym: sweetness - a gentle, pleasant, or kind manner or attitude
