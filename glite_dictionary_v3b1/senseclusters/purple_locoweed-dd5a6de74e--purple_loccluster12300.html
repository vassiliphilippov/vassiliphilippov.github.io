purple locoweed

noun
a plant with purple, pink, or white flowers found in the southwestern US
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːpəl ˈloʊ.kəˌwid / (AmE), / ˈpɜː.pəl ˈləʊ.kəˌwiːd / (BrE)
a tufted plant of the southwestern United States, known for its purple, pink, or white flowers, belonging to the genus Oxytropis and sometimes causing locoism in livestock
