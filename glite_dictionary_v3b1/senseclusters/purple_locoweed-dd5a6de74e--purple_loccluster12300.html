purple locoweed

noun
a plant with purple, pink, or white flowers found in the southwestern US
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀêp…ôl Ààlo ä.k…ôÀåwid / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀê.p…ôl Ààl…ô ä.k…ôÀåwiÀêd / (BrE)
