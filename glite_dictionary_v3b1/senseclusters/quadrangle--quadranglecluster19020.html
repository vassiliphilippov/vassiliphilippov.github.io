quadrangle

noun
rectangular courtyard enclosed by buildings
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːˌdræŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒˌdræŋɡl̩ / (BrE)
a rectangular courtyard or open space surrounded on all four sides by buildings, especially in a school or college

Examples

School quadrangle
A statue stands in the center of the ancient quadrangle, surrounded by ivy-covered walls.
Our dormitory rooms overlook the college quadrangle, giving us a beautiful view of the lawns.
The students gathered in the quadrangle between classes to socialize and enjoy the sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courtyard - an open space surrounded by walls or buildings
