compañero

noun
romantic partner
9.6
Pronunciation: / kəmˌpɑːnˈjɛɾoʊ / (AmE), / kəmˌpænˈjɛərəʊ / (BrE)
a romantic partner or close companion in a relationship, especially in Spanish-speaking contexts

Examples

Many people seek a compañero who supports their dreams and ambitions.
