scattergun

adjective
indiscriminate approach
Pronunciation: / ˈskætərˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈskætəɡʌn / (BrE)
involving a wide, unfocused, or indiscriminate approach rather than a targeted one

Examples

Scattergun promises
The politician's scattergun promises failed to convince the voters of any clear plan.
Their scattergun strategy wasted resources on too many unrelated projects.
Instead of a scattergun approach, the team decided to focus on one key market.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broad - covering a wide area or range
  • antonym: targeted - focused on a specific aim or group
