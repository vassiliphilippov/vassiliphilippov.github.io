melilot

noun
fodder plant; genus Melilotus
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛlɪˌlɑt / (AmE), / ˈmɛlɪlɒt / (BrE)
An erect annual or biennial plant of the genus Melilotus, grown extensively as fodder and for improving soil.

Examples

The melilot plant
Farmers often plant melilot to enrich the soil with nitrogen before sowing crops.
