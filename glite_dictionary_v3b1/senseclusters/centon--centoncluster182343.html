centon

noun
patchwork garment
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛntɒn / (AmE), / ˈsɛntɒn / (BrE)
an old-fashioned garment or piece of fabric made from patches sewn together; a patchwork

Examples

A patchwork centon
The museum displayed a rare centon crafted from colorful fabric scraps.
In ancient times, a centon was often worn by those who could not afford new clothes.
She admired the intricate stitching on the old centon found in her grandmother's trunk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patchwork - a piece of fabric made by sewing together smaller pieces
