shin bone

noun
the larger lower leg bone; tibia
7.0 2.1
Pronunciation: /ʃɪn boʊn/ /; / /ʃɪn bəʊn/ (AmE), /ʃɪn bəʊn/ /; / /ʃɪn boʊn/ (BrE)
the inner and larger of the two bones in the lower human leg, extending from the knee to the ankle; also called the tibia

Examples

Broken shin bone
She rubbed her shin bone after bumping it against the coffee table.
The soccer player suffered a fracture to his shin bone during the match.
X-rays revealed a small crack in his shin bone that would take weeks to heal.
