booker

noun
person arranging bookings
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊkər / (AmE), / ˈbʊkə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to arrange reservations, appointments, or bookings for others

Examples

Hotel booker
The booker arranged hotel rooms for the entire band before their tour.
As a booker, she handles all the appointments for the busy salon.
Our travel booker found us the best deals on flights and hotels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agent - a person who arranges services or bookings for clients
  • synonym: manager - a person who organizes and coordinates activities or people
