spoked

adjective
having spokes
Pronunciation: / spoʊkt / (AmE), / spəʊkt / (BrE)
having spokes, especially referring to wheels or similar structures with rods radiating from a center.

Examples

Spoked wheel
The bicycle had a classic spoked wheel design that made it lightweight.
Many old wagons featured large spoked wheels for better support on rough roads.
He admired the intricate pattern of the spoked metal gate at the entrance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solid - having no openings or spokes; continuous throughout
