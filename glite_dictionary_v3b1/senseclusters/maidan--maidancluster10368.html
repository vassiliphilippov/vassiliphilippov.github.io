maidan

noun
open field in South Asia
8.2
Pronunciation: / maɪˈdɑːn / (AmE), / maɪˈdɑːn / (BrE)
a large open area or field, especially used for gatherings, sports, or public events in South Asia

Examples

A maidan for sports
Every weekend, families gather at the maidan to enjoy picnics and games.
The political rally attracted thousands of people to the central maidan in the city.
Children played cricket on the maidan every evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: field - an open area of land, often used for sports or farming
  • synonym: square - an open public space in a city or town
  • degree higher: stadium - a large structure for sports events and gatherings
