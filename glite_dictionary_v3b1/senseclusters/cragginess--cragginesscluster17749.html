cragginess

noun
rough, rugged quality
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkræɡinəs / (AmE), / ˈkræɡinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being rough, uneven, or rugged, especially like a rocky cliff or crag

Examples

The cragginess of cliffs
The cragginess of the old castle walls gave it a dramatic appearance.
His face had a certain cragginess that made him look distinguished.
Hikers admired the cragginess of the coastline as they walked along the cliffs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ruggedness - the quality of being rough and uneven
  • antonym: smoothness - the quality of being even and free from roughness
