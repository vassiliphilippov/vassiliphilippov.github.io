overlooked

adjective
not noticed or considered
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈlʊkt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈlʊkt / (BrE)
Not noticed, considered, or taken into account; missed or ignored.

Examples

An overlooked detail
Her contributions to the project were sadly overlooked by the committee.
Some important details were overlooked during the initial investigation.
The report contained several overlooked errors that should have been corrected before publication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ignored - not given attention to; disregarded
  • antonym: noticed - observed or paid attention to
