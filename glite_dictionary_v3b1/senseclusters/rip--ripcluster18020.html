rip

verb
criticize or attack strongly
4.0 6.3
Pronunciation: /rɪp/ (AmE), /rɪp/ (BrE)
(informal) To criticize or attack someone or something strongly and violently, often in a verbal or written form.

Examples

To rip into someone
During the meeting, she ripped her colleague for missing several important deadlines.
In his speech, the CEO ripped into the media for spreading false rumors about the company.
The movie critics ripped the latest blockbuster for its poor script and lackluster acting.
