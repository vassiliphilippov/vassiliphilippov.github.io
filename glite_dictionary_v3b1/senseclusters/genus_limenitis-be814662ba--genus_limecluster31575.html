genus Limenitis

noun
genus of dark butterflies with white wing bars
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs lɪˈmɛn.ɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs lɪˈmɛn.ɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
A genus of mainly dark butterflies found in northern regions, characterized by white wing bars.
