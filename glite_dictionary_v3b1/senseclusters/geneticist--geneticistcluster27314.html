geneticist

noun
scientist specializing in genetics
1.7
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈnɛtəsɪst / (AmE), / dʒəˈnɛtɪsɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies or specializes in genetics, the science of genes, heredity, and variation in living organisms

Examples

A geneticist studies genes
As a geneticist, he analyzes DNA samples to trace hereditary diseases.
Many geneticists are researching ways to edit genes to cure rare illnesses.
The geneticist presented her groundbreaking research at the international conference.

  • synonym: biologist - a scientist who studies living organisms
