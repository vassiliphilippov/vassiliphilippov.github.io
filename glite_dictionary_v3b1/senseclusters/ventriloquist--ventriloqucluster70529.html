ventriloquist

noun
performer who projects voice through a dummy
5.4 7.1
Pronunciation: / vɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst / (AmE), / vɛnˈtrɪlɪkwɪst / (BrE)
a performer who can speak or utter sounds so that they seem to come from somewhere else, especially from a puppet or dummy

Examples

A ventriloquist with a puppet
As a child, she dreamed of becoming a ventriloquist after seeing a puppet show at the fair.
The ventriloquist amazed the audience by making his puppet appear to speak in different voices.
The ventriloquist skillfully threw his voice so it sounded like the dummy was arguing back.
