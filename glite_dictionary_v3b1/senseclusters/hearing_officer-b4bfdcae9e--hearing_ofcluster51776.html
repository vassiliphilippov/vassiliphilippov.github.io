hearing officer

noun
official who conducts administrative hearings
3.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhiː.rɪŋ ˈɔː.fɪ.sər/ /; / /ˈhɪ.rɪŋ ˈɔː.fə.sər/ (AmE), /ˈhɪə.rɪŋ ˈɒf.ɪ.sə/ /; / /ˈhɪə.rɪŋ ˈɒf.ə.sə/ (BrE)
an official appointed by a government agency to conduct investigations or administrative hearings, enabling the agency to exercise its authority
