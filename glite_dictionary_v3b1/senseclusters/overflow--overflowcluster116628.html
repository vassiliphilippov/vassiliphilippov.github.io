overflow

noun
liquid spilling over due to excess
1.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈoʊ.vər.floʊ/ /; / /oʊ.vər.ˈfloʊ/ (AmE), /ˈəʊ.və.fləʊ/ /; / /əʊ.və.ˈfləʊ/ (BrE)
the event or result of liquid or things spilling or flowing beyond the limits of a container or place
