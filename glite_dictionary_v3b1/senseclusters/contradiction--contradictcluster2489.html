contradiction

noun
opposition between ideas or statements
4.3 12/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên.tr…ôÀàd…™k. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.tr…ôÀàd…™k. É…ôn / (BrE)

Examples
