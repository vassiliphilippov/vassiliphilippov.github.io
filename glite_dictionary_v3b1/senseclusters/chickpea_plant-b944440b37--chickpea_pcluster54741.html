chickpea plant

noun
plant producing edible chickpeas
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪkˌpiː plænt / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.piː plɑːnt / (BrE)
An annual Asiatic plant (Cicer arietinum) cultivated for its short pods containing one or two edible seeds known as chickpeas.

Examples

The chickpea plant seeds
Farmers in India cultivate the chickpea plant for its nutritious seeds.

The chickpea plant thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.
