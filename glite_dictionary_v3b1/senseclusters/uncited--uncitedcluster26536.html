uncited

adjective
not given a citation
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈsaɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having been issued a formal legal citation, ticket, or summons for an offense.

Examples

Uncited driver
The driver was relieved to leave the scene uncited after the officer gave only a warning.
Despite the minor infraction, she remained uncited and was allowed to continue on her way.
Most of the motorists stopped for speeding that day were uncited and received verbal warnings instead.
