vaporize

verb
disappear or decrease rapidly
Pronunciation: / ˈveɪpəˌraɪz / (AmE), / ˈveɪpəraɪz / (BrE)
(informal) To disappear or decrease rapidly, especially in value or quantity.

Examples

Savings vaporize overnight
During the crisis, investors saw their life savings vaporize almost overnight.
My travel plans vaporized after I lost my passport.
When the scandal broke, public trust seemed to vaporize instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evaporate - to disappear, especially gradually
  • synonym: vanish - to disappear suddenly and completely
