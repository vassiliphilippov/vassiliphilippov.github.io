transmigrate

verb
be reborn in another body
6.0
Pronunciation: / trænzˈmaɪɡreɪt / (AmE), / trænzˈmɪɡreɪt / (BrE)
(of the soul) to pass into a new body after death; to be born anew in another body, especially as believed in some religious and philosophical systems.

Examples

Souls transmigrate after death
Many Eastern religions teach that souls transmigrate after death.
Some people hope they will transmigrate into a better life next time.
The belief that souls transmigrate shapes burial rituals in several cultures.
