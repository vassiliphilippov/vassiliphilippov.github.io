unwieldiness

noun
difficulty in being carried or managed due to bulk or shape
9.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈwiːl.dɪ.nəs/ /; / /ʌn.ˈwiːl.dɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈwiːl.dɪ.nəs/ /; / /ən.ˈwiːl.dɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being difficult to carry, move, or manage because of size, shape, or weight

Examples

Unwieldiness of the box
