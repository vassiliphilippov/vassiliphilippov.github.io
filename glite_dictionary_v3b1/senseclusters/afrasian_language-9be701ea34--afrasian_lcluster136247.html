Afrasian language

noun
language family of North Africa and Western Asia
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæfəˈreɪʒən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌafrəˈreɪʒɪən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A language belonging to a large family spoken in North Africa and parts of Western Asia, also known as Afro-Asiatic.

Examples

Afrasian languages family
Linguists study the characteristics shared across Afrasian languages.
Many modern Ethiopians speak an Afrasian language such as Amharic.
The ancient Egyptian script was an early form of an Afrasian language.
