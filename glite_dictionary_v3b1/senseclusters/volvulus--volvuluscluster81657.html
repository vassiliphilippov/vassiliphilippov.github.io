volvulus

noun
intestinal twisting causing obstruction
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːlvjələs / (AmE), / ˈvɒlvjʊləs / (BrE)
A medical condition in which a part of the intestine twists around itself, causing an obstruction.

Examples

A volvulus twist
A volvulus can cause severe abdominal pain and vomiting in affected patients.
Prompt diagnosis of volvulus is essential to prevent intestinal damage.
The X-ray revealed a volvulus that required immediate surgical intervention.
