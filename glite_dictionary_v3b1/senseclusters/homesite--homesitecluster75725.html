homesite

noun
land for a house
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmsaɪt / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmsaɪt / (BrE)
a piece of land for building a house or where a house already stands

Examples

A homesite for building
They chose a homesite with a beautiful view of the mountains.
The developer is selling each homesite with access to utilities and roads.
Before construction began, the homesite was cleared of trees and debris.
They bought a homesite in the countryside to build their dream house.
The developer is selling several homesites near the new school.
Each homesite in the community comes with access to a private park.

  • synonym: parcel - a piece of land, especially one considered as part of an estate
  • synonym: property - land or real estate owned by someone
