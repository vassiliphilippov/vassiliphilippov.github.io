roach clip

noun
device for holding a marijuana cigarette end
7.4
Pronunciation: / roʊtʃ klɪp / (AmE), / rəʊtʃ klɪp / (BrE)
A small metal device, typically resembling tweezers, used to hold the remains of a marijuana cigarette so it can be smoked without burning the fingers.

Examples

A roach clip tool
A roach clip is often made from metal and looks like a small pair of tweezers.
He used a roach clip to finish smoking the joint without burning his fingers.
Some head shops sell colorful roach clips as accessories for marijuana smokers.
