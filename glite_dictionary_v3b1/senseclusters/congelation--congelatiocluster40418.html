congelation

noun
process of becoming solid by freezing
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑn.dʒəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒn.dʒɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of becoming solid, especially through freezing or congealing.

Examples

Rapid congelation
Rapid congelation can crack certain materials due to the stress of freezing.
The congelation of water forms ice in cold temperatures.
The experiment involved observing the congelation of various solutions at different temperatures.
