gladden

verb
make happy or joyful
3.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /ˈglæ.dən/ (AmE), /ˈglæ.dən/ (BrE)
to make someone feel happy or joyful; to bring pleasure or gladness

Examples

To gladden the heart
Her unexpected visit truly gladdened his heart after a long, hard week.
Nothing gladdens me more than seeing my children laugh together.
The news of their victory gladdened the entire village.
His cheerful presence always seems to gladden the hearts of those around him.
It would gladden me to see you succeed in your career.
The news of her recovery gladdened everyone in the family.
