grutch

noun
resentful grudge
9.9
a feeling of resentment or a grudge, especially an unwillingness to forgive or let go of past wrongs

Examples

Hold a grutch
She could not let go of her grutch even after many years had passed.
Holding a grutch can make it difficult to move forward in life.
Despite their apology, he still felt a deep grutch toward them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgiveness - the act of letting go of resentment or anger
  • synonym: grudge - a persistent feeling of resentment from a past insult or injury
