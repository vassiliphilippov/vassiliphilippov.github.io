pia

noun
plant yielding Otaheite arrowroot
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpiː.ə / (AmE), / ˈpiː.ə / (BrE)
A perennial herb native to the East Indies, Polynesia, and Australia, cultivated for its large edible root, which yields Otaheite arrowroot starch.

Examples

The pia root
Farmers in the region continue to cultivate pia for both food and export.
The roots of the pia are harvested to produce a fine arrowroot starch.
Traditional Polynesian cuisine often uses pia as a staple ingredient.
