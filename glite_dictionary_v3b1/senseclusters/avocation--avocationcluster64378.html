avocation

noun
secondary occupation or hobby
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌævəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌavəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
an activity taken up in addition to one's main occupation, especially for enjoyment; a hobby or minor occupation

Examples

An avocation hobby
Gardening became more than an avocation for her during retirement.
He regards painting as an avocation, not as a way to earn a living.
Many successful people pursue a creative avocation alongside their professional work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hobby - an activity done regularly in one's leisure time for pleasure
  • antonym: vocation - a person's main job or profession
