pinchpenny

adjective
stingy with money
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪntʃˌpɛni / (AmE), / ˈpɪntʃˌpɛni / (BrE)
unwilling to spend money; very stingy or miserly

Examples

Pinchpenny attitude
The pinchpenny manager refused to approve any extra spending for the project.
Her pinchpenny attitude made it difficult for her friends to plan group outings.
Despite his wealth, he remained a pinchpenny when it came to charitable donations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give or share freely
  • synonym: miserly - unwilling to spend money; very stingy
