overweary

verb
to tire excessively
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚˈwɪri / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈwɪəri / (BrE)
to tire someone or oneself excessively; to exhaust through overexertion

Examples

To overweary the body
Continuous worrying will only overweary your mind and body.
She warned not to overweary oneself by working without rest.
The long journey did nothing but overweary the already exhausted travelers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exhaust - to make someone extremely tired
  • antonym: invigorate - to give energy or strength to someone
