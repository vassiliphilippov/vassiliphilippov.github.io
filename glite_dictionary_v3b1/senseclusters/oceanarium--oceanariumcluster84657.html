oceanarium

noun
large marine aquarium
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊʃəˈnɛriəm / (AmE), / ˌəʊʃəˈnɛːrɪəm / (BrE)
a large public aquarium where marine animals and plants are kept and displayed for visitors

Examples

A large oceanarium
The city opened a new oceanarium that attracts thousands of visitors each month.
Children were amazed by the huge tanks at the oceanarium filled with colorful fish and sea turtles.
We spent the afternoon exploring the oceanarium and learning about marine life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquarium - a tank or building for keeping aquatic animals and plants
  • synonym: zoo - a place where animals are kept for public display
