tour

verb
travel around a place for pleasure
Pronunciation: / tʊr / (AmE), / tʊə / (BrE)
to travel to and around different places, especially for pleasure, business, or to perform

Examples

To tour the country
Next summer, she plans to tour the national parks in the United States.
The band toured across Europe, playing in major cities every night.
We toured Italy for two weeks and visited many historic sites.
The famous chef will tour several countries to promote his new cookbook.
After graduation, she decided to tour Europe with her friends for a month.
The theater group toured the country, performing in different cities each week.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stay - to remain in one place
  • synonym: travel - to make a journey, especially over a long distance
  • synonym: visit - to go to see and spend time in a place for a short period
