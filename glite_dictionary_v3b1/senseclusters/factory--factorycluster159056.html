Synonyms

    3.2 0.80/m
    a manufacturing plant is a large facility using heavy machinery for mass production, while a factory is any building where goods are made.
  • industrial plant
    3.5 0.32/m
    an industrial plant makes goods or runs heavy processes for industry, while a factory focuses mainly on making products.
  • plant
    4.8 61/m
    a plant refers to any industrial site with equipment and machines, while a factory is specifically where goods are manufactured.
  • manufactory
    9.2 0.22/m

  • mill
    2.3 11/m
