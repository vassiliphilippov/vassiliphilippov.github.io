tailor-make

verb
make specially to fit an individual
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪlər ˌmeɪk /; / ˈteɪlərˌmeɪk / (AmE), / ˈteɪlə ˌmeɪk /; / ˈteɪləˌmeɪk / (BrE)
to make or adapt something especially for a particular person, purpose, or requirement

Examples

To tailor-make clothes
She had her wedding gown tailor-made by a famous couturier.
The designer will tailor-make a dress to match your exact measurements.
We can tailor-make a training program that suits your company's needs.
The wedding dress was tailor-made for her and fit perfectly.
They can tailor-make a training program to fit your company's unique needs.
We offer services to tailor-make furniture according to your space and style preferences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adapt - to change something to suit a new use or situation
  • synonym: custom-make - to make something specifically for a person or requirement
  • synonym: customize - to modify something according to individual requirements
  • antonym: mass-produce - to manufacture goods in large quantities, not tailored to individuals
