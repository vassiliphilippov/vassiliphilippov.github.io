tailor-make

verb
make specially to fit an individual
8.6 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈteɪ.lər meɪk/ (AmE), /ˈteɪ.lə meɪk/ (BrE)
to make or adapt something especially for a particular person, purpose, or requirement

Examples

To tailor-make clothes
She had her wedding gown tailor-made by a famous couturier.
The designer will tailor-make a dress to match your exact measurements.
We can tailor-make a training program that suits your company's needs.
