gehlenite

noun
grayish mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɛləˌnaɪt / (AmE), / ˈɡɛlɪnaɪt / (BrE)
a grayish mineral composed of calcium, aluminum, and silicon, found in some igneous rocks
