hypothyroid

adjective
underactive thyroid
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpoʊˈθaɪrɔɪd / (AmE), / ˌhʌɪpəʊˈθʌɪrɔɪd / (BrE)
having an underactive thyroid gland that produces too little hormone

Examples

Hypothyroid symptoms
A person who is hypothyroid may experience fatigue and weight gain.
Doctors often prescribe medication to help hypothyroid patients regulate their hormone levels.
If you feel unusually tired, your doctor might check if you are hypothyroid.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hyperthyroid - having an overactive thyroid gland
  • synonym: hypothyroidism - a condition of having an underactive thyroid gland
