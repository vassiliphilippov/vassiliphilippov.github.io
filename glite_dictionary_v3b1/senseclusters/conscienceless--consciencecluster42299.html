conscienceless

adjective
lacking a conscience; unscrupulous
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.ʃən.sləs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃən.slɪs / (BrE)
Without a conscience; lacking any sense of right or wrong.

Examples

A conscienceless person
A conscienceless government can be dangerous to its citizens.
She described her boss as utterly conscienceless in his pursuit of profit.
The conscienceless fraudster deceived hundreds of people without remorse.

Related Terms

  • synonym: unprincipled - not acting in accordance with moral principles
  • synonym: unscrupulous - having or showing no moral principles; not honest or fair
