blind man

noun
person with covered eyes
2.8
Pronunciation: /blaɪnd mæn/ (AmE), /blaɪnd mæn/ (BrE)
a person whose eyes are covered or who cannot see, especially during a game or experiment.

Examples

Blind man game
During the game, the blind man tried to catch the other children by listening for their voices.
In the experiment, a blind man was asked to identify objects by touch alone.
They tied a scarf over his eyes, making him the blind man for the next round.
