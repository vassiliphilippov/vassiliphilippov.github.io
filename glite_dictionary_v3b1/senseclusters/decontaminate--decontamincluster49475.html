decontaminate

verb
remove dangerous substances
7.0 0.37/m
Pronunciation: / diÀêÀàk…ëÀên.t…ôÀåme…™t / (AmE), / diÀêÀàk…ín.t…ô.me…™t / (BrE)
to remove dangerous substances or pollutants from something, especially a place or object, to make it safe

Examples

To decontaminate equipment
All equipment must be decontaminated before leaving the laboratory.
Special teams worked overnight to decontaminate the affected area after the spill.
The hospital staff carefully decontaminated each uniform to prevent the spread of infection.
