heathlike

adjective
resembling heath or heathland
Pronunciation: / ˈhiθ.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈhiːθ.laɪk / (BrE)
resembling or characteristic of heath, especially in appearance or vegetation

Examples

A heathlike landscape
A heathlike scent filled the air as the wind swept over the wildflowers.
Her garden has a heathlike quality, with heathers and gorse predominating.
The landscape was heathlike, dotted with low shrubs and patches of sandy soil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heathy - covered with or resembling heath
