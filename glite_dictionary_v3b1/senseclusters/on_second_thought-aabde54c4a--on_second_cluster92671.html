on second thought

adverb
after reconsidering a decision
1.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈsɛkənd θɔt / (AmE), / ɒn ˈsɛkənd θɔːt / (BrE)
used to indicate that one has reconsidered a decision or opinion after further reflection

Examples

On second thought maybe wait
On second thought, maybe we should wait until tomorrow to make a decision.
He initially agreed to help, but on second thought he changed his mind.
I was going to order dessert, but on second thought, I decided to skip it.
