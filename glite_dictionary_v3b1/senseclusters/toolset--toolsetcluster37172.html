toolset

noun
set of skills or tools
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlˌsɛt / (AmE), / ˈtuːlˌsɛt / (BrE)
a collection of skills, methods, or tools used to accomplish a particular task or goal

Examples

A programmer's toolset

The workshop will help you expand your toolset for managing difficult conversations at work.
She updated her toolset to include the latest digital marketing techniques.
