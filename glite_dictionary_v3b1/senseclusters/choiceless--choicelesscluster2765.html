choiceless

adjective
without options
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɔɪsləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃɔɪsləs / (BrE)
having no choice or alternative available in a situation

Examples

A choiceless decision
He felt completely choiceless when his only option was to accept the offer.
Many people in the region are choiceless due to economic hardship.
She described her situation as choiceless, with no alternatives left to consider.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free - able to choose or act without restriction
