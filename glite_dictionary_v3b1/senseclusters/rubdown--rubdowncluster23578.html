rubdown

noun
act of massaging or rubbing the body
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈrʌb.daʊn / (BrE)
an act of vigorously rubbing or massaging a person's body, usually to relax them or improve circulation

Examples

A professional rubdown
A professional rubdown can help reduce tension after a stressful day.
After his workout, he enjoyed a relaxing rubdown at the spa.
The physiotherapist gave her a quick rubdown to ease her sore muscles.
