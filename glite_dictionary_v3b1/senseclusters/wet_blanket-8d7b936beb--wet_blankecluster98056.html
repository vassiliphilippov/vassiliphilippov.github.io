wet blanket

noun
person who spoils others' fun
Pronunciation: / wɛt ˈblæŋkət / (AmE), / wɛt ˈblaŋkɪt / (BrE)
a person who discourages enjoyment or dampens the enthusiasm of others

Examples

A wet blanket person
I don’t mean to be a wet blanket, but this plan sounds risky to me.
Nobody wants to invite Greg, because he's always such a wet blanket at parties.
She tried not to act like a wet blanket when everyone else was excited.

Related Terms

  • synonym: killjoy - a person who deliberately spoils the enjoyment of others
  • antonym: life of the party - a very lively person who is fun at social events
  • synonym: party pooper - someone who spoils the fun at social events
