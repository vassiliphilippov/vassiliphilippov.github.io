so

adverb
in the same way or condition
Pronunciation: / soʊ / (AmE), / səʊ / (BrE)
in the same way or manner; like this or that; also, in the same way as someone or something else

Examples

Act so
If you want to be successful, you must act so.
She was angry, and she remained so for several hours.
They said they were ready, and I believe that is so.
He arranged the flowers so, just as his grandmother had taught him.
If you hold your hands so, you can see the illusion.
Please fold the paper so before cutting it.
He forgot his umbrella, and so did I.
She loves classical music, and so does her brother.
They decided to stay late, and so did we.

Related Terms

  • synonym: either - used after a negative statement to mean 'also not'
  • synonym: like this - in this manner; in the way shown or referred to
  • synonym: thus - in this way, as indicated
  • synonym: too - in addition; also
