tubing

noun
recreational tube riding
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtjuːbɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of riding on water or snow using a large inflatable tube for fun

Examples

River tubing
We spent the afternoon tubing down the river with friends.
Many ski resorts offer tubing as a fun winter activity for families.
On hot summer days, tubing on the lake is a popular way to cool off.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rafting - the activity of traveling on water in an inflatable raft
  • synonym: sledding - the activity of sliding down snow-covered hills on a sled
