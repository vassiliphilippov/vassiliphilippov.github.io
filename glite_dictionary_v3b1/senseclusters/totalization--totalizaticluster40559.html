totalization

noun
combining amounts
Pronunciation: / ˌtoʊtələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌtəʊtələˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of combining or aggregating amounts, especially for social security or accounting purposes

Examples

Totalization of earnings
The totalization of different income sources is necessary for accurate tax reporting.
International agreements may allow the totalization of work periods for pension eligibility.
The accountant explained the totalization process to ensure all figures were included.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aggregation - the act of collecting or gathering things together
  • antonym: separation - the act of keeping things apart or dividing them
