purr

verb
make a soft, continuous, vibrating sound
8.4 2.9
Pronunciation: /pɜːr/ (AmE), /pɜː/ (BrE)
(of a machine or vehicle) to make a soft, continuous, vibrating sound, similar to a cat's purr.

Examples

Engines purr smoothly
As we drove through the night, the motorcycle purred beneath us, steady and smooth.
She sat listening to the old refrigerator purring in the kitchen corner.
The new electric car's engine purrs quietly as it accelerates down the road.

  • synonym: whir - to make a low, continuous, regular sound
