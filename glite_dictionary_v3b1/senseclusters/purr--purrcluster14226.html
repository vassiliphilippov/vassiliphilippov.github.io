purr

verb
make a soft, continuous, vibrating sound
3.5 0.49/m
Pronunciation: / p…úr / (AmE), / p…úÀê / (BrE)
(of a machine or vehicle) to make a soft, continuous, vibrating sound, similar to a cat's purr.

Examples

Engines purr smoothly
As we drove through the night, the motorcycle purred beneath us, steady and smooth.
She sat listening to the old refrigerator purring in the kitchen corner.
The new electric car's engine purrs quietly as it accelerates down the road.

Synonyms

  • whirr
    6.7 1.11/m
    whirr is a continuous low vibrating or humming sound from fast moving parts, while purr is a softer, cat like engine vibration.
  • whir
    4.6 0.19/m
    whir is a rapid soft buzzing or swishing noise from moving parts, while purr is a gentler continuous, cat like vibration.
  • birr
    9.9 0.00/m
    birr is a soft whirring or swishing sound often used for objects, while purr suggests a warm, cat like machine vibration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whir - to make a low, continuous, regular sound
