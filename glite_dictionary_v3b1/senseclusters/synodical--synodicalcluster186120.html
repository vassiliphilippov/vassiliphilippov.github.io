synodical

adjective
of synodic period
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɑːdɪkəl / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒdɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or measured by a synodic period, especially the time between similar alignments of celestial bodies.

Examples

Synodical month
The synodical month is the time it takes the Moon to return to the same phase.
Astronomers use synodical periods to predict planetary alignments.
The synodical cycle of Venus helps determine when it will appear brightest in the sky.

Related Terms

  • synonym: synodic - relating to the period between similar configurations of celestial bodies
