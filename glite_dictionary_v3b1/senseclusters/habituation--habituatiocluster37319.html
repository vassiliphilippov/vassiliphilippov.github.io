Examples

Habituation to alcohol
After years of alcohol abuse, his habituation made it nearly impossible for him to quit.
Long-term habituation to narcotics can have severe health consequences.
Medical professionals monitor patients for signs of habituation when prescribing strong painkillers.

Synonyms

  • dependence
    5.5 2.36/m
    dependence is a state of physical need for a drug, while habituation is the process of becoming tolerant and dependent.
  • addiction
    4.1 25/m
    addiction is a strong, harmful need to keep using a substance, while habituation is the gradual process of tolerance and dependence.

Related Terms
