Eurocentric

adjective
focused on Europe and Europeans
4.1 6.5
Pronunciation: /juːr.ə.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /jʊr.oʊ.ˈsen.trɪk/ (AmE), /jʊə.rə.ˈsen.trɪk/ /; / /juː.rə.ˈsen.trɪk/ (BrE)
Focusing on or centered around Europe and Europeans, often to the exclusion or marginalization of other cultures or perspectives.

Examples

Eurocentric view
Many critics argue that art history has traditionally been taught in a Eurocentric manner.
The company's Eurocentric policies failed to account for the needs of their Asian customers.
The textbook's Eurocentric perspective leaves out important contributions from non-European civilizations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ethnocentric - evaluating other cultures according to the standards of one's own culture
