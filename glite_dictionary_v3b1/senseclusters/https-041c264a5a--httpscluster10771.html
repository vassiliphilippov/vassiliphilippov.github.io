HTTPS

sym
secure web protocol
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪtʃ tiː tiː piː ˈɛs / (AmE), / ˌeɪtʃ tiː tiː piː ˈɛs / (BrE)
a secure protocol for transferring data over the internet, commonly used to protect information on websites

Examples

HTTPS protocol
When you shop online, always check for HTTPS in the website address to ensure your data is secure.
Many browsers display a padlock icon when a site uses HTTPS for encrypted communication.
Banks and email providers rely on HTTPS to protect sensitive user information from hackers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: HTTP - a standard protocol for transferring data over the internet without encryption
