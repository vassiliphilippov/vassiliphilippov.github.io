unlighted

adjective
not illuminated; without light
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪtɪd / (BrE)
not provided with light; not illuminated.

Examples

Unlighted street
He was uneasy about leaving his car on an unlighted street.
The parking lot was unlighted after the power outage last night.
We walked down the unlighted path, feeling our way in the darkness.
