chintzy

adjective
embarrassingly stingy or cheap
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪntsi / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪntzi / (BrE)
Embarrassingly stingy or cheap in a way that is noticeable or unpleasant.

Examples

Chintzy behavior
His chintzy attitude meant he never contributed to group gifts.
She refused to buy the chintzy knockoff, opting for something of better quality.
The decorations looked chintzy and poorly made, disappointing the guests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give money, help, or kindness freely
  • synonym: stingy - unwilling to spend money; mean; close-fisted
