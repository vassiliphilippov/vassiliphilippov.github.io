summerhouse

noun
small garden building for shade or rest
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɚˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈsʌməˌhaʊs / (BrE)
a small building with a roof, typically in a garden, used for relaxation or enjoying shade during warm weather

Examples

A summerhouse in garden
Guests can relax in the summerhouse after a walk through the gardens.
The old summerhouse in the park is surrounded by roses.
They spent the afternoon reading in the summerhouse by the pond.
