floater

noun
person voting illegally in multiple places
Pronunciation: / ˈfloʊtər / (AmE), / ˈfləʊtə / (BrE)
a person who votes illegally at multiple polling places during the same election

Examples

Election floater
Authorities arrested a floater who attempted to cast ballots in three different precincts.
Efforts to prevent floaters include stricter ID checks at polling stations.
The election commission is investigating allegations of floaters affecting last year's vote.
