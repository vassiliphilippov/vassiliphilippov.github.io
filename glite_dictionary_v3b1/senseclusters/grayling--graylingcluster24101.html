grayling

noun
brownish butterfly
Pronunciation: / ˈɡreɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡreɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
a brownish butterfly, especially Hipparchia semele and related species, with camouflaged wings found in Europe and parts of Asia

Examples

A grayling butterfly
We spotted a grayling resting quietly on a sun-warmed rock during our hike.
The grayling blends in so well with dry grass that it is hard to see.
Photographers often search meadows in late summer to capture images of the elusive grayling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butterfly - an insect with large, often colorful wings
  • antonym: moth - an insect similar to a butterfly, usually active at night
