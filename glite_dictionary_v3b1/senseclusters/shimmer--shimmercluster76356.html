shimmer

noun
a faint, flickering light
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈʃɪmə / (BrE)
a soft, wavering or flickering light; a faint or tremulous gleam

Examples

A shimmer of light
A faint shimmer in the distance caught her eye as the sun set.
He admired the shimmer of dew on the spider web in the morning.
The shimmer of moonlight on the lake made the night feel magical.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gleam - a brief or faint light or shine
  • synonym: glimmer - a faint or wavering light
  • degree higher: glow - a steady light, often softer than a shine
