ironmaster

noun
owner of ironworks
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪərnˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈaɪənˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who owns or manages an ironworks or iron foundry

Examples

The ironmaster manages foundry
The local ironmaster employed hundreds of workers at the foundry.
In the nineteenth century, an ironmaster played a key role in industrial development.
The ironmaster invested in new machinery to increase production at his plant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrialist - a person involved in the ownership or management of industry
