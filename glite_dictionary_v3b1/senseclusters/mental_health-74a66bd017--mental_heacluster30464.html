mental health

noun
psychological and emotional well-being
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛn.t̬əl hɛlθ /; / ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ / (AmE), / ˈmen.təl hɛlθ /; / ˈmɛntəl hɛlθ / (BrE)
a person's condition with regard to their psychological and emotional well-being

Examples

Good mental health
Good sleep and exercise can significantly improve your mental health.
It's important to talk openly about mental health to reduce stigma.
The pandemic has made mental health a global concern.
Access to support services is important for maintaining good mental health.
Discussions about mental health have become more common in the workplace.
She takes time each day to care for her mental health through meditation and exercise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mental illness - a condition which negatively affects mental health
  • synonym: well-being - state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy
