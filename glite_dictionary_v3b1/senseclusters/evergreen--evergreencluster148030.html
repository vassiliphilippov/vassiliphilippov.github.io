evergreen

noun
plant that stays green all year
6.7 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈev.ər.griːn/ (AmE), /ˈev.ə.griːn/ (BrE)
a plant that retains green leaves throughout the year, rather than losing them seasonally

Examples

An evergreen tree
Many people choose evergreen shrubs for their gardens to enjoy greenery during cold months.
The forest was filled with towering evergreen trees that provided shade even in winter.
We planted an evergreen in the yard so it would stay lush all year round.
