red algae

noun
marine algae with red or purplish pigment
4.3 0.08/m

A large group of mostly marine algae in which the green chlorophyll is masked by red or purplish pigments; used as a source of agar and carrageenan.

Examples

Red algae source agar
Agar, commonly used in microbiology, is typically extracted from certain types of red algae.
Many sushi rolls use nori, which is made from red algae.
The laboratory is studying the health benefits of red algae found in tropical oceans.

Synonyms

    9.8 0.00/m
