allower

noun
person giving permission
6.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlaʊər / (AmE), / əˈlaʊə / (BrE)
a person or thing that allows, permits, or makes something possible

Examples

The allower of time
The teacher is the main allower of extra time for assignments in this class.
In some companies, the HR department acts as the allower for remote work requests.
The system administrator is the only allower of access to the secure files.
The new policy acted as an allower of greater flexibility for employees.
She was the main allower of change within the organization.
Technology can be a powerful allower of access for people with disabilities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approver - a person who officially accepts or agrees to something
  • antonym: denier - a person who refuses permission or does not allow something
  • synonym: enabler - a person or thing that makes something possible
  • antonym: preventer - a person or thing that stops something from happening
