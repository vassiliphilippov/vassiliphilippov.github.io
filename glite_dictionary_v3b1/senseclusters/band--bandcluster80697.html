band

noun
cord-like tissue connecting body parts
Pronunciation: / bænd / (AmE), / bænd / (BrE)
a cord-like bundle or strip of tissue connecting or supporting parts of the body in anatomy

Examples

A tissue band
A congenital band can sometimes block the intestine in newborns.
Scar tissue formed a thick band around the injured muscle.
The surgeon identified a fibrous band attaching the two bones.
