opinionist

noun
public commentator
Pronunciation: / əˈpɪnjənɪst / (AmE), / əˈpɪnjənɪst / (BrE)
a person who regularly expresses or promotes opinions, especially in public or through the media

Examples

TV opinionist
The popular opinionist often appears on television to discuss current events.
As an opinionist, she writes weekly columns about social issues.
Many people follow the opinionist on social media for his views on politics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commentator - a person who discusses or analyzes events or topics publicly
  • synonym: pundit - an expert who gives opinions publicly, especially in the media
