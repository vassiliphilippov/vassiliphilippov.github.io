milk vetch

noun
plant of the genus Astragalus
9.9
Pronunciation: / mɪlk vɛtʃ / (AmE), / mɪlk vɛtʃ / (BrE)
Any of various plants belonging to the genus Astragalus, which often have clusters of pea-like flowers and are commonly found in temperate regions.

Examples

Milk vetch plants
Farmers sometimes plant milk vetch as a cover crop to enrich the soil.
The hillside was covered in blooming milk vetch during the early summer months.
We identified several species of milk vetch on our botanical survey.
