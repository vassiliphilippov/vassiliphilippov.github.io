premillennialist

noun
believer in Christ's return before millennium
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌpriːmɪˈlɛniəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who believes that Christ will return to earth before a thousand-year period of peace and righteousness

Examples

A premillennialist thinker
The church's doctrine was shaped by several prominent premillennialists in the early twentieth century.
As a premillennialist, he believes the Second Coming will happen before the millennium.
Debates between premillennialist and postmillennialist theologians have influenced Christian eschatology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: millenarian - a person who believes in a coming major transformation of society, often involving a thousand-year period
  • antonym: postmillennialist - a person who believes Christ will return after a thousand-year reign
