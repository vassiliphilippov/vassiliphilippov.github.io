tortoiseshell

noun
mottled brown pattern
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtəsˌʃɛl / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtəsʃɛl / (BrE)
a mottled pattern of brown, yellow, and orange, resembling the shell of a tortoise, often used in design or to describe certain cats

Examples

Tortoiseshell glasses
She wore stylish tortoiseshell glasses to the meeting.
The cat's tortoiseshell fur made her stand out among the others at the shelter.
Many vintage combs were made from real tortoiseshell before plastic became common.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calico - a cat with a coat of three colors, often including orange and brown
  • antonym: plain - having a single color or lacking a pattern
