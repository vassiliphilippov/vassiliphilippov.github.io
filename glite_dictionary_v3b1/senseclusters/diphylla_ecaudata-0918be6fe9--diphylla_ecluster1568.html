Diphylla ecaudata

noun
a species of tropical American vampire bat
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɪlə iːˈkaʊdə də / (AmE), / dɪˈfɪlə iːˈkaʊdə tə / (BrE)
a species of vampire bat found in tropical America, similar in size and habits to Desmodus rotundus

Examples

Diphylla ecaudata bat
Researchers studied the feeding behavior of Diphylla ecaudata in Central America.
The Diphylla ecaudata is distinguished by its tailless appearance among vampire bats.
Unlike some other bats, Diphylla ecaudata primarily feeds on bird blood.
