nasopharyngitis

noun
nasopharynx inflammation
Pronunciation: / ˌneɪzoʊˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (AmE), / ˌneɪzəʊˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs / (BrE)
inflammation of the nasopharynx (the area behind the nose and above the throat), often called the common cold

Examples

Nasopharyngitis symptoms
The doctor explained that her persistent cough was due to nasopharyngitis.
Children are especially prone to developing nasopharyngitis during the winter months.
Treatment for nasopharyngitis usually involves rest and plenty of fluids.
The doctor diagnosed her with nasopharyngitis after she complained of a sore throat and runny nose.
Children often miss school because of nasopharyngitis, especially during the winter months.
Simple home remedies can help relieve the symptoms of nasopharyngitis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: common cold - a mild viral infection causing nasal congestion and sore throat
  • synonym: pharyngitis - inflammation of the pharynx, causing sore throat
