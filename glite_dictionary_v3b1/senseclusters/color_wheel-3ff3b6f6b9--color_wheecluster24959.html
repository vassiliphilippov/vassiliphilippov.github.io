color wheel

noun
diagram showing color relationships
3.2 0.24/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk ål…ôr ÀåwiÀêl / (AmE), / Ààk ål…ô ÀåwiÀêl / (BrE)
a circular chart showing the relationships between different colors, especially complementary colors arranged opposite each other

Examples

A color wheel chart
If you look at the color wheel, you'll see that blue and orange are complementary colors.
She used the color wheel to choose a harmonious palette for the living room.
The color wheel is an essential tool for painters and designers alike.

Synonyms

  • degree higher: complementary color - color opposite another on the color wheel, creating high contrast when paired
