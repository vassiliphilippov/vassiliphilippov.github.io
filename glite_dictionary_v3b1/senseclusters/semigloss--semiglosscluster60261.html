semigloss

noun
paint with a partly glossy finish
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌɡlɑs / (AmE), / ˈsɛmɪˌɡlɒs / (BrE)
a type of paint that dries to a finish with a moderate sheen, between glossy and flat

Examples

A semigloss finish
Many people prefer semigloss for bathrooms because it resists moisture and wipes clean easily.
The trim was finished with a semigloss to provide a subtle sheen without being too reflective.
They decided to use a semigloss paint for the kitchen walls to make it easy to clean.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: gloss - paint or finish that is highly shiny
  • degree lower: matte - paint or finish with no shine
