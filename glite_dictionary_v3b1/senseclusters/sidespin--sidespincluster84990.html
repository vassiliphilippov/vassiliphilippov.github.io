sidespin

verb
to spin ball sideways
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌspɪn / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌspɪn / (BrE)
to make a ball spin sideways so that it curves in the air or moves sideways after bouncing

Examples

To sidespin a ball
The tennis player tried to sidespin the ball to make it curve away from his opponent.
If you sidespin the cue ball in pool, it will move sideways after hitting the cushion.
She learned how to sidespin her serves to make them harder to return.

Related Terms

  • antonym: backspin - to make a ball spin backward
  • synonym: curve - to make a ball move in a curved path
  • antonym: topspin - to make a ball spin forward
