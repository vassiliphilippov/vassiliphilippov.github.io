run around

verb
play boisterously, move about energetically
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌn əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈrʌn əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to play in a lively and noisy way, especially by moving quickly in different directions

Examples

Children run around outside
After school, the kids love to run around in the backyard until dinner time.
The puppies started to run around the living room, knocking over toys as they played.
We watched the children run around at the playground, laughing and chasing each other.
