paedobaptist

adjective
infant baptism supporter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdoʊˈbæptɪst / (AmE), / ˌpiːdəʊˈbæptɪst / (BrE)
relating to or supporting the practice of baptizing infants in a Christian context

Examples

Paedobaptist tradition
The church follows a paedobaptist tradition, baptizing infants as part of their faith.
Her paedobaptist beliefs influenced her views on family and religious upbringing.
A paedobaptist perspective is common in many historic Christian denominations.

Related Terms
