scribing block

noun
marking gauge for testing surfaces
Pronunciation: / ˈskraɪbɪŋ blɑk / (AmE), / ˈskraɪbɪŋ blɒk / (BrE)
A gauge consisting of a pointed marking tool mounted on an adjustable stand, used to test the accuracy of plane surfaces or for marking lines on metal in engineering.

Examples

A scribing block gauge
A scribing block is essential for checking the accuracy of a machined surface.
Before marking the pattern, he adjusted the scribing block for precise measurement.
The machinist used a scribing block to ensure the metal plate was perfectly level.
