gown

noun
a loose dress for sleeping (nightgown)
Pronunciation: / ɡaʊn / (AmE), / ɡaʊn / (BrE)
A loose dress intended to be worn in bed by women; a nightgown or nightdress.

Examples

A night gown
After her shower, she changed into a soft cotton gown for sleep.
She slipped into her favorite gown before going to bed.
The silk gown felt cool against her skin on summer nights.
