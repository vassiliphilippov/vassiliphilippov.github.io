metric

adjective
based on the metric system
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or using the meter as a standard of measurement; based on the metric system.

Examples

Metric ruler
A metric ruler is divided into centimeters and millimeters.
Most countries have adopted the metric system for standardizing measurements.
The laboratory records all data in metric units such as liters and kilograms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: SI - the International System of Units, the modern form of the metric system
  • antonym: imperial - relating to the system of measurement formerly used in the UK and US, not based on the metric system
