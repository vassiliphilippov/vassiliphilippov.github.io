Pistia

noun
genus of aquatic plants (water lettuce)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪsti.ə / (AmE), / ˈpɪsti.ə / (BrE)
a genus of aquatic plants in the arum family, consisting of a single species, commonly known as water lettuce

Examples

Pistia water lettuce
Gardeners often remove excess Pistia to prevent it from choking out other aquatic plants.
Scientists discovered a new population of Pistia thriving in the freshwater canal.
The pond was covered with floating Pistia, making it difficult for fish to surface.
