unstaple

verb
remove staples from (paper, etc.)
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsteɪpəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈsteɪpəl / (BrE)
to remove staples from something, especially paper or documents

Examples

To unstaple papers
After you unstaple the pages, put the staples in the recycling bin.
He carefully unstapled the documents to make photocopies.
Please unstaple all the forms before handing them in.
