ordinarily

adverb
usually; under normal conditions
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdənˌɛrəli / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪn(ə)rəli / (BrE)
Under normal circumstances; usually or generally.

Examples

Ordinarily under normal conditions
Ordinarily, the store closes at 8 p.m., but today it's open late for a special event.
I ordinarily have coffee in the morning, but today I tried tea instead.
The weather is ordinarily mild in May, though this year it has been unusually cold.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exceptionally - in an unusual or rare way; not typical
  • synonym: usually - in most cases; generally
