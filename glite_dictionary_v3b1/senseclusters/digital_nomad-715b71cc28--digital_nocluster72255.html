digital nomad

noun
person working remotely while traveling
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪdʒɪt̬əl noʊˌmæd / (AmE), / ˈdɪdʒɪtəl ˈnəʊ.mæd / (BrE)
a person who earns a living working online in various locations, often while traveling to different places

Examples

A digital nomad lifestyle
As a digital nomad, he relies on internet cafes to stay connected with clients.
More people are choosing the digital nomad lifestyle in search of flexibility and adventure.
The freedom of being a digital nomad allowed her to explore new countries while staying employed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telecommuter - person who works remotely, usually from home
