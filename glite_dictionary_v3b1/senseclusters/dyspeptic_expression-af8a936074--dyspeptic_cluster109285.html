dyspeptic expression

noun
facial look showing discomfort or displeasure
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈpɛptɪk ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (AmE), / dɪsˈpɛptɪk ɪkˈsprɛʃən / (BrE)
a facial expression showing discomfort or displeasure, often resembling the look of someone suffering from indigestion

Examples

A dyspeptic expression of discomfort
After tasting the bitter medicine, she wore a dyspeptic expression for several minutes.
He greeted the guests with a dyspeptic expression, clearly displeased by their arrival.
The manager noticed his employee's dyspeptic expression during the entire meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scowl - an angry or bad-tempered facial expression
  • antonym: smile - a pleased, kind, or amused facial expression
