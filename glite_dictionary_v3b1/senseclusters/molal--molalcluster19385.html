molal

adjective
containing one mole per kilogram of solvent
8.9 8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.ləl/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.ləl/ (BrE)
(of a solution) containing one mole of solute per kilogram of solvent.

Examples

Molal concentration
Calculations involving molal concentration are common in thermodynamics.
The chemist prepared a molal solution by dissolving one mole of salt per kilogram of water.
Unlike molar solutions, molal concentrations are not affected by temperature changes.
