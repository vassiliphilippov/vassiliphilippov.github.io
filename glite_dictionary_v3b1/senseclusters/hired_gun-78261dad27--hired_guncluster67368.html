hired gun

noun
professional killer for hire
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪərd ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈhaɪəd ɡʌn / (BrE)
a professional killer who is employed to kill someone, usually for money

Examples

She discovered her business partner had secretly arranged for a hired gun to scare her.
The mafia boss sent a hired gun to eliminate his rival.
