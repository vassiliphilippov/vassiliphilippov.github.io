oubliette

noun
dungeon with ceiling trapdoor entry
9.4
Pronunciation: / uːbliˈɛt / (AmE), / uːbliˈɛt / (BrE)
A secret dungeon with an opening only in the ceiling, used historically to imprison people.

Examples

A hidden oubliette
Medieval castles sometimes contained a hidden oubliette beneath a trapdoor.
The prisoner was thrown into the oubliette and left in darkness.
They discovered an ancient oubliette while renovating the old fortress.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dungeon - a strong underground prison
