Polychaeta

noun
class of marine bristle worms
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈkiːtə /; / ˌpɑːliˈkiːtə / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈkiːtə / (BrE)
a class of mostly marine annelid worms with segmented bodies and paired, bristled appendages on most segments
