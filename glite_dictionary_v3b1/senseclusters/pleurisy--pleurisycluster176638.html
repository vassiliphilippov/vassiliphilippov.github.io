pleurisy

noun
inflammation of the lung lining
Pronunciation: / ˈplʊrəsi / (AmE), / ˈplʊərəsi / (BrE)
Inflammation of the membrane (pleura) lining the lungs and chest cavity, often causing chest pain and difficulty breathing.

Examples

Pleurisy chest pain
Doctors explained that pleurisy may result from a viral or bacterial infection.
Persistent coughing and fever can sometimes be symptoms of pleurisy.
The patient was diagnosed with pleurisy after complaining of sharp chest pains when breathing deeply.
