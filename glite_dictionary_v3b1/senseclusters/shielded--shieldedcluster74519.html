shielded

adjective
protected by a shield; covered
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʃildɪd / (AmE), / ˈʃiːl.dɪd / (BrE)
(especially of machinery) protected by a shield or covering to prevent harm or injury.

Examples

Shielded machinery parts
All moving parts in the factory are shielded to ensure worker safety.
The shielded machinery reduced the risk of accidental contact.
This cable is shielded to protect it from electromagnetic interference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unshielded - not protected by a shield or covering
