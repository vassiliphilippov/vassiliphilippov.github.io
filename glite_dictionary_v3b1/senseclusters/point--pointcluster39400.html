point

noun
unit for scoring in games and contests
1.1 2.1
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt / (AmE), / pɔɪnt / (BrE)
a unit for measuring or recording (such as a score in a game, size of type, compass direction, or a small amount in finance or mechanics)
