Synonyms

  • flexible
    3.4 3.92/m
    flexible bends without breaking and returns to shape, while bendy is an informal word for something easy to bend.
  • pliant
    5.6 0.27/m
    pliant is easily shaped or molded and sounds slightly formal, while bendy is more casual and used for objects.
  • pliable
    4.2 0.73/m
    pliable bends or adapts easily and can mean flexible in tough materials, while bendy is a casual, everyday term.
  • supple
    3.7 1.65/m
    supple bends and moves smoothly and often looks graceful, while bendy simply means able to bend without that smooth quality.
  • limber
    4.1 0.33/m
    limber can move or be bent easily, often for bodies or athletes, while bendy mainly describes inanimate objects' shape.
