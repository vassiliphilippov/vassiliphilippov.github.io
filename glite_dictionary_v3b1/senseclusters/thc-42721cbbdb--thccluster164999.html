THC

noun
psychoactive compound in marijuana
Pronunciation: / ti eɪtʃ si / (AmE), / tiː eɪtʃ siː / (BrE)
the main psychoactive compound found in marijuana, responsible for most of its psychological effects

Examples

THC in marijuana
Driving under the influence of THC can impair your ability to operate a vehicle safely.
Medical marijuana is often regulated by its THC content.
The concentration of THC in cannabis products has increased over the past decade.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: cannabidiol - non-psychoactive compound in cannabis
  • synonym: marijuana - dried leaves and flowers of cannabis plant used as a drug
