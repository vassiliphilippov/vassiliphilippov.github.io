sistren

noun
group of women
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstrən / (AmE), / ˈsɪstrən / (BrE)
women regarded as a group, especially expressing solidarity, friendship, or shared identity in certain communities or movements

Examples

Supportive sistren group
The activist spoke to her sistren about the importance of unity and support.
At the festival, the sistren gathered to celebrate their shared heritage and achievements.
She found comfort among her sistren during challenging times in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brethren - people, especially men, regarded as a group or community
  • synonym: sisters - female siblings or women with a close bond
