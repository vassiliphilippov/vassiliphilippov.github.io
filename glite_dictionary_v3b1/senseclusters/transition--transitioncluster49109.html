Synonyms

  • switch over
    3.8 0.70/m
    switch over is what people say when they change from one system or activity to another, while transition names the whole process.
  • switch
    2.0 39/m
    switch means change, exchange, or shift between items or activities quickly, while transition refers to the whole movement from one state to another.
  • transform
    3.4 48/m
    transform means change something deeply in form or character, while transition means moving from one state or activity to another.
  • turn into
    2.2 39/m
    turn into means become something different or cause that change in form or identity, while transition covers change between states or activities.
  • morph
    5.8 4.39/m
    morph describes a smooth gradual change in shape or character, while transition is the general process of moving between states.
  • turn
    1.0 258/m
