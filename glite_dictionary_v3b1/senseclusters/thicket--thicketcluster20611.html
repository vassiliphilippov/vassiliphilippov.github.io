thicket

noun
dense group of bushes or small trees
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈθɪk.ɪt / (BrE)
A dense group of bushes or small trees growing close together.

Examples

A dense thicket
A deer darted into the thicket to hide from view.
Birds chirped from deep within the thicket, unseen but not unheard.
They hacked a path through the dense thicket by the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: copse - a small group of trees or bushes
