universal

adjective
applicable to all; common to all
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /juː.nə.ˈvɜːr.səl/ /; / /juː.nɪ.ˈvɜːr.səl/ (AmE), /juː.nɪ.ˈvɜː.səl/ /; / /juː.nə.ˈvɜː.səl/ (BrE)
existing or true everywhere or for everyone; applicable in all cases or to all people or things

Examples

Universal truth
Her speech received universal praise from critics and fans alike.

A universal language would make international communication much easier.

The concept of human rights has a universal appeal that transcends borders and cultures.
