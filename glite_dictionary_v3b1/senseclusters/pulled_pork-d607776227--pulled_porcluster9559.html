pulled pork

noun
slow-cooked shredded pork dish
Pronunciation: / pʊld pɔrk / (AmE), / pʊld pɔːk / (BrE)
Pork that has been cooked slowly until tender and then shredded into pieces, typically served with barbecue sauce.

Examples

Pulled pork sandwich
At the picnic, they served pulled pork alongside cornbread and baked beans.
I tried making pulled pork in my slow cooker, and it turned out delicious.
The restaurant is famous for its pulled pork sandwiches served with coleslaw.
