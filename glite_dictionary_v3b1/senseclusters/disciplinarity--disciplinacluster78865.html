disciplinarity

noun
academic field characteristics
9.2
Pronunciation: /dɪ.sə.plɪ.ˈnær.ə.tɪ/ /; / /dɪ.sɪ.plɪ.ˈnær.ə.tɪ/ (AmE), /dɪ.sɪ.plɪ.ˈnær.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /dɪ.sə.plɪ.ˈnær.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
the qualities, organization, or state of being related to a particular academic discipline or field of study

Examples
