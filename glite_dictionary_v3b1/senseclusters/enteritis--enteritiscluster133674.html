enteritis

noun
inflammation of the intestine
8.8 7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛn.təˈraɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / ˌɛn.təˈraɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of the intestine, especially the small intestine, often resulting in diarrhea and abdominal discomfort.

Examples

Chronic enteritis
Chronic enteritis can lead to nutritional deficiencies if left untreated.
Some cases of enteritis are caused by bacterial infections from contaminated food or water.
The doctor diagnosed him with enteritis after he developed severe abdominal pain and diarrhea.
