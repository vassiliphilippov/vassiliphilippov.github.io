double-furrow plow

noun
plow with two moldboards
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌbəl ˈfɝːroʊ plaʊ /; / ˈdʌb.əl ˈfɜr.oʊ plaʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʌbəl ˈfʌrə plau /; / ˈdʌb.əl ˈfʌr.əʊ plaʊ / (BrE)
A plow equipped with two moldboards, allowing it to turn over two furrows of soil at once.
