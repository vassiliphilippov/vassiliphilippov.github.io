petroleum future

noun
contract for future petroleum delivery
7.2
Pronunciation: / pəˈtroʊliəm ˈfjuːtʃər / (AmE), / pəˈtrəʊliəm ˈfjʊːtʃə / (BrE)
A financial contract to buy or sell petroleum at a predetermined price for delivery at a specific future date.

Examples

Petroleum futures contract
Many investors use petroleum futures to hedge against fluctuations in oil prices.
The price of petroleum futures rose sharply due to concerns over supply disruptions.
Trading in petroleum futures can be risky because the market is highly volatile.
