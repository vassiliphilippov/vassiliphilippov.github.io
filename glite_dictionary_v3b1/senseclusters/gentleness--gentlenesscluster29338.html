gentleness

noun
quality of being gradual or not steep
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛntlnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛntlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being gradual or not steep, especially when referring to a slope or incline.

Examples

The gentleness of a slope
Because of its gentleness, the ramp was accessible to everyone.
Hikers appreciated the gentleness of the trail as it wound through the valley.
The gentleness of the hillside made it easy for children to climb.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steepness - the quality of being steep or rising sharply
