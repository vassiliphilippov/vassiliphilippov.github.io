sheet

noun
rope or chain adjusting a sail (nautical)
Pronunciation: / ʃit / (AmE), / ʃiːt / (BrE)
(Nautical) A rope or chain used to control the angle of a sail in relation to the wind.

Examples

A rope sheet

If the sheet is too tight, the sail will not work properly.
