system of weights and measures

noun
set of measurement units and standards
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstəm əv ˈweɪts ənd ˈmɛʒərz / (AmE), / ˈsɪstəm əv ˈweɪts ənd ˈmɛʒəz / (BrE)
an organized set of units and standards used to measure length, weight, and time
