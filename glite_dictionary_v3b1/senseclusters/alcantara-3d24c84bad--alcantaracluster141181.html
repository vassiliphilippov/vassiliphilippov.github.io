Alcantara

proper noun
Lisbon district
8.1
Pronunciation: / alˈkɑːntərə / (AmE), / alˈkæntərə / (BrE)
a district of Lisbon, Portugal, located on the north bank of the Tagus River, known for its nightlife and waterfront attractions

Examples

Alcantara nightlife district
They spent the evening wandering the bars and galleries of Alcantara by the river.
Many tourists visit Alcantara to enjoy its lively restaurants and scenic waterfront.
The bridge connecting Alcantara to other parts of Lisbon offers stunning views of the city.
