batwoman

noun
female military orderly (British, historical)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbætˌwʊm.ən / (AmE), / ˈbætwʊm.ən / (BrE)
(historical, British) A female orderly assigned to serve a British military officer, responsible for personal and domestic duties.
