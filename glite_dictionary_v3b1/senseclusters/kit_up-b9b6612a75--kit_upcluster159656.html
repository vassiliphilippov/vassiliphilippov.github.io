kit up

verb
provide with necessary equipment or gear
5.4
Pronunciation: / kɪt ʌp / (AmE), / kɪt ʌp / (BrE)

Examples

To kit up with gear

She kitted up the children with new raincoats and boots for the trip.
The climbers kitted up before starting their ascent.
