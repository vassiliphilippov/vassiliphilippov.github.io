renourishment

noun
restoring nutrition
Pronunciation: / riˈnɜrɪʃmənt / (AmE), / riˈnʌrɪʃmənt / (BrE)
the process of restoring nutritional health to a person or animal by providing food or nutrients after a period of deficiency

Examples

Patient renourishment plan
The patient required careful renourishment after suffering from severe malnutrition.
Veterinarians focused on renourishment to help the rescued animals recover their strength.
Proper renourishment is essential for children recovering from illness or neglect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: malnutrition - a condition caused by lack of proper nutrition
  • synonym: rehabilitation - the process of restoring health or normal life through therapy or training
