Stuart

proper noun
royal family of Scotland and England (1371–1714)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːərt / (AmE), / ˈstjuːət / (BrE)
the royal family that ruled Scotland from 1371 to 1714 and England from 1603 to 1649 and again from 1660 to 1714

Examples

Stuart royal family
Mary, Queen of Scots, was a prominent member of the Stuart family.
The Stuart dynasty faced numerous challenges during its reign over England and Scotland.
The Stuart monarchy was restored in 1660 with the return of Charles II.
