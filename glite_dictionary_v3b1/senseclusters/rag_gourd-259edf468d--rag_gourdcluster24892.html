rag gourd

noun
tropical vine; fruit used as a sponge
6.7 7.4
Pronunciation: /ræg gɔːrd/ /; / /ræg gʊərd/ (AmE), /ræg gɔːd/ /; / /ræg gʊəd/ (BrE)
A tropical climbing plant with large yellow flowers and edible young fruits, cultivated for its mature fruit, whose dried fibrous interior is used as a sponge.

Examples

The rag gourd plant
Farmers cultivate rag gourds both for food and for the fibrous sponges inside their mature fruit.
She uses a dried rag gourd to scrub her dishes instead of a synthetic sponge.
The young fruits of the rag gourd are often eaten as vegetables in tropical countries.
