lapacho

noun
hardwood for construction
9.7
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɑːtʃoʊ / (AmE), / ləˈpætʃəʊ / (BrE)
a very hard, durable wood from the lapacho tree, often used in construction, decking, or furniture

Examples

Lapacho wood
The builder recommended lapacho because it can withstand harsh weather conditions.
They installed a beautiful lapacho deck that will last for decades.
Furniture made from lapacho is known for its strength and resistance to insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ipe - a dense, durable tropical hardwood used for decking and outdoor furniture
  • synonym: teak - a tropical hardwood valued for its durability and water resistance
