metacone

noun
molar tooth cusp
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌkoʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəkəʊn / (BrE)
a cusp on the upper molar teeth of some mammals, located on the outer back part of the tooth

Examples

The metacone cusp
The paleontologist identified the metacone on the fossilized molar to determine the animal's diet.
In some mammals, the metacone is larger than the other cusps on the upper molar.
The absence of a metacone can help distinguish between different species in fossil records.
