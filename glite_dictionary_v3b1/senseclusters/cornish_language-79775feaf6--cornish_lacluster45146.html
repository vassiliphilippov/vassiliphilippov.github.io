Cornish language

noun
Celtic language of Cornwall
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪʃ ˈlæŋgwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪʃ ˈlaŋgwɪdʒ / (BrE)
