blasphemously

adverb
in a disrespectful or irreverent manner (towards sacred things)
6.1 7.8
Pronunciation: / blæsˈfɛməsli / (AmE), / blɑːsˈfɛməsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows disrespect or irreverence toward something considered sacred or holy.

Examples

To speak blasphemously
Critics accused the artist of treating sacred symbols blasphemously in her controversial exhibit.
He spoke blasphemously about religious traditions, shocking the congregation.
The novel was banned for portraying historical figures blasphemously in fictional scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irreverently - in a manner showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously
  • antonym: reverently - in a manner showing deep respect for someone or something
