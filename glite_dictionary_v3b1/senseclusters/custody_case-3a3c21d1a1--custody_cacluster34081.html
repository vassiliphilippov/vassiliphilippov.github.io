custody case

noun
legal case deciding child custody
3.3 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstədi keɪs / (AmE), / ˈkʌstədi keɪs / (BrE)

Examples

She hired an attorney experienced in family law to represent her during the custody case.
The judge presiding over the custody case considered the best interests of the child.
