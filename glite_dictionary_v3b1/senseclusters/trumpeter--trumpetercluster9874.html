trumpeter

noun
large North American white swan
Pronunciation: / ˈtrʌmpətər / (AmE), / ˈtrʌmpɪtə / (BrE)
a large white swan (Cygnus buccinator) native to western North America, known for its resonant, trumpet-like call.

Examples

Trumpeter swan
A flock of trumpeters glided gracefully across the lake at dawn.
Conservationists have worked to reintroduce the trumpeter to its former range.
The wetlands are a protected habitat for the endangered trumpeter.
