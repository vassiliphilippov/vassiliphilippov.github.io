varimax

adjective
factor analysis method
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛrɪˌmæks / (AmE), / ˈvɑːrɪˌmæks / (BrE)
relating to or produced by the varimax rotation method used in factor analysis to simplify factor loadings.

Examples

Varimax rotation method
The varimax rotation made the factor structure much easier to interpret in the results.
Researchers often use a varimax solution to clarify the relationships between variables in factor analysis.
Applying a varimax rotation can help separate the underlying factors more distinctly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oblique - relating to statistical methods with correlated factors
  • synonym: orthogonal - relating to statistical methods with uncorrelated factors
