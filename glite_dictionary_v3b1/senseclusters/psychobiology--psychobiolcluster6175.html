psychobiology

noun
mind-body science
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how mental processes and behavior are influenced by biological factors

Examples

Study of psychobiology
She decided to study psychobiology because she was interested in how the brain affects behavior.
The field of psychobiology explores the connection between emotions and physical health.
Researchers in psychobiology examine how hormones influence mood and decision-making.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biology - the scientific study of living organisms
  • synonym: biopsychology - the study of how biology influences behavior
  • synonym: psychology - the scientific study of the mind and behavior
