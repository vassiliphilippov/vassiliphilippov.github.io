side-whiskers

noun
facial hair on the sides of the face
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪd ˌwɪskɚz / (AmE), / ˈsaɪd ˌwɪskəz / (BrE)
Facial hair grown down the sides of a man's face in front of the ears, typically with the rest of the beard shaved off.

Examples

Long side-whiskers
He wore impressive side-whiskers that gave him a distinguished, old-fashioned appearance.
His side-whiskers contrasted sharply with his clean-shaven chin and upper lip.
In Victorian times, side-whiskers were a popular style among men of certain classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sideburns - patches of facial hair grown in front of the ears
