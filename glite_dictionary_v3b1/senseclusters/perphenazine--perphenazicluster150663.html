perphenazine

noun
a tranquilizer and antidepressant drug
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛnəˌzin / (AmE), / pəːˈfɛnəziːn / (BrE)
a drug used as a tranquilizer and antidepressant, sometimes prescribed as an antiemetic for adults

Examples

Perphenazine medication
Doctors sometimes use perphenazine in combination with other medications to stabilize mood disorders.
Due to severe nausea, the patient was treated with perphenazine as an antiemetic.
The psychiatrist prescribed perphenazine to help manage her symptoms of psychosis.
