whiskey neat

noun
whiskey served without mixer or ice
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪski nit / (AmE), / ˈwɪski niːt / (BrE)
Whiskey or whisky served plain, without ice, water, or any mixer, typically served at room temperature.
