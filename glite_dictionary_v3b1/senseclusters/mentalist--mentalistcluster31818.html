mentalist

noun
informal insult
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəlɪst / (BrE)
an informal and sometimes offensive term for a person who behaves in a crazy, foolish, or unpredictable way

Examples

He is a mentalist
He called his friend a mentalist after she danced on the table at the party.
You would have to be a mentalist to try swimming in that freezing lake.
The coach shouted, 'Only a mentalist would attempt that risky move!'

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunatic - an offensive term for a person who is crazy or behaves wildly
  • synonym: madman - a man who behaves in a crazy or reckless way
  • antonym: sensible person - someone who acts in a reasonable and calm way
