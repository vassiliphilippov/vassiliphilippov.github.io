sugar beet

noun
beet grown for sugar production
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʊɡɚ ˌbit /; / ˈsuːɡɚ biːt / (AmE), / ˈʃʊɡə ˌbiːt /; / ˈsuːɡə biːt / (BrE)
a type of beet with a large, sweet, white root grown for making sugar
