lateen sail

noun
triangular Mediterranean sail
5.8 7.2
Pronunciation: /lə.ˈtiːn seɪl/ /; / /læ.ˈtiːn seɪl/ (AmE), /lə.ˈtiːn seɪl/ (BrE)
A triangular sail set on a long yard mounted at an angle on the mast, used especially on boats in the Mediterranean region.

Examples

A lateen sail
