mixologist

noun
professional bartender who creates cocktails
Pronunciation: / mɪkˈsɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / mɪkˈsɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a person who mixes and serves alcoholic drinks, especially as a skilled professional at a bar

Examples

She trained as a mixologist to elevate her skills beyond standard bartending.
The mixologist crafted a unique cocktail for every customer at the event.
