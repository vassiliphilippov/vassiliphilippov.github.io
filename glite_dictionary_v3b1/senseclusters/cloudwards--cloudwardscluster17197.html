cloudwards

adverb
upward toward sky
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdwɚdz / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdwədz / (BrE)
in the direction of the clouds or upward toward the sky

Examples

Balloon rises cloudwards
The balloon floated cloudwards until it disappeared from sight.
As the rocket launched, it soared cloudwards with a trail of fire.
The flock of birds spiraled cloudwards in the early morning light.

Related Terms

  • antonym: downwards - toward a lower place or position
  • synonym: upwards - toward a higher place or position
