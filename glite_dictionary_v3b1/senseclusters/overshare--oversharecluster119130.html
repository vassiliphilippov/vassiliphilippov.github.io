overshare

noun
excessive disclosure
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊvərˌʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈəʊvəʃeə / (BrE)
an act of revealing more personal or private information than is appropriate or necessary

Examples

A social media overshare
His long post about his breakup was a clear overshare on social media.
During the meeting, her overshare made everyone feel a bit uncomfortable.
Sometimes an overshare can damage your professional reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confession - the act of admitting something personal or private
  • synonym: disclosure - the act of making something known or public
  • antonym: withhold - to keep information back or not reveal it
