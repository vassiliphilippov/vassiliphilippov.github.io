essay

verb
attempt; try (verb)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɛˈseɪ / (AmE), / ɛˈseɪ / (BrE)
to attempt or try to do something, especially in a tentative or experimental way

Examples

To essay a task
He essays to explain the theory in simple terms.
She essayed a smile, despite her nerves.
