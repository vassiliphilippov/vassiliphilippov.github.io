disseminator

noun
a person who spreads information
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsɛmɪˌneɪtər / (AmE), / dɪˈsɛmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person who spreads information, news, or ideas to others

Examples

A disseminator of news
As a trusted disseminator of news, she takes her responsibility very seriously.
Effective disseminators can help public health information reach vulnerable communities.
Social media has turned many users into disseminators of both facts and rumors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a person or thing that distributes something
  • antonym: receiver - a person who receives information, not spreads it
