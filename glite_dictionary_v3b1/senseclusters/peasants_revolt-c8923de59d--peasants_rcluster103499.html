Peasants' Revolt

noun
1381 English peasants' uprising
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛzənts rɪˈvoʊlt / (AmE), / ˈpɛzənts rɪˈvəʊlt / (BrE)
a widespread uprising in England in 1381 by peasants and lower classes against taxation and social injustice, ultimately suppressed by King Richard II
