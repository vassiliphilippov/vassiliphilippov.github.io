Examples

Synonyms

  • conversation
    1.0 132/m
    conversation is an informal talk between people about everyday topics, while interlocution means a more neutral or formal exchange of words.
  • dialog
    4.3 0.00/m
    dialog is a back-and-forth talk often to solve a problem, while interlocution is a general exchange of words.
  • discussion
    1.4 144/m
    discussion is a focused talk where people exchange views to decide or understand, while interlocution simply means any exchange of words.
  • colloquy
    9.7 0.30/m
    colloquy is a formal, serious conversation on a set topic, while interlocution can refer to any spoken exchange.
  • discourse
    6.4 28/m
    discourse is long or formal written or spoken discussion in public or study, while interlocution is a simple exchange of words.
