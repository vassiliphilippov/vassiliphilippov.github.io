aseptically

adverb
without contamination
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛptɪkli / (AmE), / əˈsɛptɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is free from contamination by harmful microorganisms

Examples

Aseptically handle samples
The laboratory samples were handled aseptically to prevent any risk of infection.
Food must be packaged aseptically to ensure it remains safe for consumption.
During surgery, all instruments are prepared aseptically to protect the patient.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contaminatedly - in a way that is affected by harmful substances or microorganisms
  • synonym: sterilely - in a way that is completely clean and free from germs
