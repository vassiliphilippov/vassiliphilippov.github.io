purfling

verb
to add decorative edging
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrflɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpɜːflɪŋ / (BrE)
to insert a narrow decorative strip into the edge of a musical instrument, especially stringed instruments, for protection and ornamentation

Examples

To purfle a guitar
The craftsman carefully purfled the violin to enhance both its beauty and durability.
Many high-quality guitars feature intricate purfling along their edges.
She learned how to purfle the instrument as part of her luthier training.
