purfling

verb
to add decorative edging
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.flɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.flɪŋ/ (BrE)
to insert a narrow decorative strip into the edge of a musical instrument, especially stringed instruments, for protection and ornamentation
