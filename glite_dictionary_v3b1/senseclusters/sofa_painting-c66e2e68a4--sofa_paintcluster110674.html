sofa painting

noun
decorative painting for display near a sofa
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊfə ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsəʊfə ˌpeɪntɪŋ / (BrE)
a decorative painting designed to hang above or near a sofa, often chosen to complement living room décor

Examples

A sofa painting above
Many interior designers recommend adding a sofa painting to create a focal point in the room.
She spent hours searching for the perfect sofa painting to match her furniture.
The living room felt empty until we hung a beautiful sofa painting above the couch.
