scrap

verb
to quarrel or fight over something minor
1.0
Pronunciation: / skræp / (AmE), / skræp / (BrE)
to quarrel or have a minor fight, especially about something unimportant

Examples

To scrap with friends
My brothers always scrap over who gets the front seat in the car.
The neighbors scrapped about the property line for years.
They were scrapping again, this time over which movie to watch.
