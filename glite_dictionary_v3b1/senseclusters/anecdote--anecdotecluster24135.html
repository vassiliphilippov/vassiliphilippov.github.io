anecdote

noun
short, amusing story about an incident
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈænɪkˌdoʊt / (AmE), / ˈænɪkdəʊt / (BrE)
A short, interesting or amusing story about a real incident or person.

Examples

A funny anecdote
He told an anecdote about his travels in Asia that made everyone laugh.
She began her speech with a humorous anecdote from her childhood.
The book is filled with anecdotes about famous scientists and their discoveries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: story - narrative of real or fictional events
