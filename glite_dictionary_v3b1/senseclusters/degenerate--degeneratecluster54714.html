degenerate

noun
morally corrupt person
4.3
Pronunciation: / dɪˈdʒɛnəˌreɪt / (AmE), / dɪˈdʒɛnərət / (BrE)
a person regarded as morally corrupt or having declined from a former or normal state, especially with reference to sexual behavior

Examples

A degenerate gambler
He was labeled a degenerate because of his scandalous lifestyle.
Some critics argued that the novel's hero was a degenerate unworthy of admiration.
The neighborhood became known as a refuge for degenerates and criminals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reprobate - a morally unprincipled person
  • antonym: upstanding citizen - a person regarded as honest and virtuous
