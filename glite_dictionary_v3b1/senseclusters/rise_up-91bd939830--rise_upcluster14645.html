rise up

verb
rebel against authority
1.0
Pronunciation: / raɪz ʌp / (AmE), / raɪz ʌp / (BrE)
to take part in a rebellion or revolt; to rebel against authority or a previous allegiance

Examples

To rise up against rulers
The people decided to rise up against the oppressive regime.
Throughout history, many have risen up to fight for their freedom.
Workers threatened to rise up if their demands were ignored.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebel - to refuse allegiance or oppose authority
  • antonym: submit - to accept authority or yield to control
