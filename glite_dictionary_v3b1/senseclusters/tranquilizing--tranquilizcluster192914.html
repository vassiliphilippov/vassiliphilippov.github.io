tranquilizing

adjective
having a calming or soothing effect
Pronunciation: / ˈtræŋkwəˌlaɪzɪŋ /; / træŋˈkwɪlaɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtræŋkwɪˌlaɪzɪŋ /; / træŋˈkwɪlʌɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
having a calming or soothing effect; tending to make someone less anxious or more relaxed.

The medicine had a tranquillizing effect on the anxious patient.
