strip-mine

verb
harvest large amounts of data
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstrɪp.maɪn/ /; / /strɪp maɪn/ (AmE), /ˈstrɪp.maɪn/ /; / /strɪp maɪn/ (BrE)
