growing issue

noun
an increasingly significant problem
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊɪŋ ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊɪŋ ˈɪʃuː / (BrE)
a problem or concern that is becoming increasingly significant or widespread over time

Examples

A growing issue worldwide
Climate change has become a growing issue for governments around the world.
Many parents are concerned about social media addiction as a growing issue among teenagers.
Online privacy is increasingly seen as a growing issue by technology experts.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: minor issue - a problem of less importance or urgency
  • synonym: pressing issue - a problem requiring urgent attention
