saving

noun
the act of protection from harm or loss
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈseɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of protecting, rescuing, or preserving someone or something from loss, harm, or danger

Examples

Saving of lives
Firefighters are dedicated to the saving of lives during emergencies.
The saving of historical artifacts is important for cultural preservation.
The charity focuses on the saving of endangered species.
Her dedication led to the saving of many endangered species in the region.
Thanks to early warning systems, there was a substantial saving of lives during the disaster.
The firefighter's quick saving of the child was praised by everyone in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: endangerment - the act of putting someone or something at risk
  • antonym: loss - the state of being deprived of something valuable or necessary
  • synonym: rescue - the act of saving someone from danger or harm
