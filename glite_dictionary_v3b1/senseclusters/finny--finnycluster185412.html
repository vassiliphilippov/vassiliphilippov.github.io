finny

adjective
fish-like; poetic
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪni / (AmE), / ˈfɪni / (BrE)
relating to or resembling fish, especially in a poetic or literary context

Examples

The finny creatures
The old tale spoke of finny creatures that danced beneath the waves.
In the painting, a school of finny animals swam through the blue-green sea.
Writers often use finny to describe the mysterious life found in deep waters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aquatic - relating to water or living in water
  • synonym: fishy - resembling or characteristic of fish
