demimonde

noun
group on the fringes of respectable society
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛm.iˌmɑnd / (AmE), / ˈdɛ.mɪ.mɒnd / (BrE)
A group of people, especially women, considered to be on the fringes of respectable society because of their indiscreet or promiscuous behavior, often associated with social or sexual unconventionality.

Examples

The demimonde women
In 19th-century Paris, the demimonde was both glamourous and scandalous.
She wrote a novel about a demimonde of artists and courtesans in London.
The protagonist falls for a woman from the demimonde, risking his reputation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: underworld - criminal or morally dubious social sphere
