rooming house

noun
house renting rooms to tenants
4.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈruː.mɪŋ haʊs/ /; / /ˈruː.mɪŋ haʊz/ (AmE), /ˈruː.mɪŋ haʊs/ /; / /ˈruː.mɪŋ haʊz/ (BrE)

Examples

The city is passing new regulations on rooming houses to improve tenant safety.
