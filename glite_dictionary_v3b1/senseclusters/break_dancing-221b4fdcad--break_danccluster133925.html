break dancing

noun
acrobatic street dance style
3.3 2.7
Pronunciation: /breɪk ˈdæn.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /breɪk ˈdɑːn.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
A style of solo street dance featuring fast, acrobatic movements and ground work, typically performed to hip-hop or rap music.
