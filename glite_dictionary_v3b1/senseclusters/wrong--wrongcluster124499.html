wrong

adjective
the inward side of cloth; not meant to show
1.1
Pronunciation: / rɔŋ / (AmE), / rɒŋ / (BrE)
(of cloth or clothing) referring to the side intended to face inward and not be seen when worn or used.

Examples

Wrong side of fabric
If you sew the fabric wrong side up, the pattern won't show correctly.
Please check that your sweater isn't on the wrong side out before we take photos.
She realized she was wearing her shirt wrong side out after leaving the house.
