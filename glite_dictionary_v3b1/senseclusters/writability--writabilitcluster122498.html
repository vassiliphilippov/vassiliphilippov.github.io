writability

noun
ability to be written on
Pronunciation: / ˌraɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌraɪtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of a surface or material that allows it to be written on clearly and legibly

Examples

Writability of paper
The writability of the notebook paper made it ideal for taking clear notes in class.
Artists often test the writability of different surfaces before starting a new project.
Low writability can make it difficult to use certain pens on glossy cards.

Related Terms

  • antonym: erasability - the quality of being able to be erased
  • synonym: writeable - able to be written on
