unhealthful

adjective
harmful to health; not sanitary
6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈhɛlθfʊl /; / ʌnˈhɛlθfəl / (BrE)
harmful or bad for your health; likely to cause illness or injury

The doctor advised her to avoid unhealthful foods high in artificial additives.
