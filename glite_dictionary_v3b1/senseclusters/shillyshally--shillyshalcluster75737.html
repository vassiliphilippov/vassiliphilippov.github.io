shillyshally

verb
hesitate; delay making a decision
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪliˌʃæli / (AmE), / ˈʃɪliˌʃæli / (BrE)
to hesitate or be indecisive, especially by delaying taking action or making a decision

Examples

To shillyshally over plans
Don't shillyshally; we need an answer by tomorrow.
She tends to shillyshally when important decisions must be made.
The meeting was delayed because the committee kept shillyshallying over minor details.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procrastinate - to delay or postpone action; to put off doing something
