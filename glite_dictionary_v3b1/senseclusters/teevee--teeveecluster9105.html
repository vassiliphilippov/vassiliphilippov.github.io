teevee

noun
informal for television
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːˌviː / (AmE), / ˈtiːˌviː / (BrE)
an informal or playful way of referring to a television

Examples

Cartoons on the teevee
We watched cartoons on the teevee all morning.
He turned on the teevee to catch the evening news.
The kids argued over which show to watch on the teevee after school.
