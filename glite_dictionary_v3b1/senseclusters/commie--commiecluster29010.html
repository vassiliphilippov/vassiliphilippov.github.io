commie

noun
communist (informal, often derogatory)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmi / (BrE)
(informal, often derogatory) A communist or a person who advocates communism.

Examples

A commie supporter
He was called a commie because of his radical views.

The book tells the story of a family labeled as commies in the 1950s.
