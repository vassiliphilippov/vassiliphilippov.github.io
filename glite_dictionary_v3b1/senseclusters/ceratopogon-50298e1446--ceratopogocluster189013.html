Ceratopogon

noun
genus of biting midges (insects)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛrətoʊˈpoʊɡən / (AmE), / ˌsɛrətəˈpəʊɡɒn / (BrE)
A genus of small biting midges in the family Ceratopogonidae, known for their biting habits and wide distribution.
