ex libris

noun
owner's book label
Pronunciation: / ɛks ˈliːbrɪs / (AmE), / ɛks ˈliːbrɪs / (BrE)
a label bearing the owner's name or design, pasted inside a book to show ownership

Examples

A ex libris label
Collectors often value books more highly when they include a rare ex libris.
He pasted an elegant ex libris inside the front cover of every book he owned.
The antique ex libris revealed that the novel once belonged to a famous collector.
