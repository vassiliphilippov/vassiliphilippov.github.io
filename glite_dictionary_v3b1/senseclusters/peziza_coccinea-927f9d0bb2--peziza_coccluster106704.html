Peziza coccinea

noun
scarlet European cup fungus
Pronunciation: / pəˈzaɪzə kɒkˈsɪniə / (AmE), / pɛˈziːzə kɒkˈsɪniːə / (BrE)
a European fungus with a bright scarlet, cup-shaped fruiting body, commonly found on woodland ground in spring
