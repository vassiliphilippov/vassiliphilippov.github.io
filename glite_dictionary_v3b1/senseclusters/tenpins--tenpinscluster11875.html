tenpins

noun
a bowling game with ten pins
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛnˌpɪnz / (AmE), / ˈtɛn.pɪnz / (BrE)
A North American form of bowling in which a large ball is rolled down an alley to knock down a triangular arrangement of ten wooden pins.

Examples

Tenpins bowling game
Many people enjoy watching tenpins tournaments on television.
Unlike in duckpins, tenpins uses a larger ball and taller pins.
We played tenpins at the bowling alley on Saturday night.
