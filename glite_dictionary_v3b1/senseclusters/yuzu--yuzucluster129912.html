yuzu

noun
East Asian citrus fruit
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈjuː.zuː/ (AmE), /ˈjuː.zuː/ (BrE)
a small, yellowish citrus fruit from East Asia with a tart flavor, or its juice, oil, or extract, used in cooking, drinks, or as a flavoring

Examples

Fresh yuzu juice
The chef squeezed fresh yuzu over the grilled fish for extra flavor.
You can find bottled yuzu juice in many Asian grocery stores.

You can find yuzu extract in specialty stores for use in desserts and drinks.
The fragrance of yuzu is popular in Japanese bath products and perfumes.
