floribunda

noun
rose with clustered blooms
Pronunciation: / ˌflɔːrəˈbʌndə / (AmE), / ˌflɒrɪˈbʌndə / (BrE)
a type of rose bush that produces large clusters of flowers, often used in gardens for its abundant blooms

Examples

A floribunda rose bush
The garden was filled with colorful floribunda roses that bloomed all summer long.
She chose a pink floribunda for the centerpiece of her flower bed.
Many gardeners prefer floribunda roses because of their hardiness and abundant flowers.
