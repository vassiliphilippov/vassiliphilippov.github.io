ibidem

adverb
used in citations to mean 'in the same place'
8.1 9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈbɪdɛm / (AmE), / ɪˈbɪdɛm / (BrE)
used in citations to indicate that a source has already been cited directly above; in the same place.
