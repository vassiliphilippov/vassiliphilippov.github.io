Synonyms

  • rot
    1.6 3/m
    rot is the common word for decay of organic matter, while rotting emphasizes the ongoing process of breakdown.
  • decomposition
    5.3 1/m
    decomposition is a scientific term for breaking down organic matter, while rotting is more everyday and often used in speech.
  • putrefaction
    8.9 0.2/m
    putrefaction is decay that gives a strong foul smell, while rotting describes decay more generally and not only the smell.
  • putrescence
    9.9 0.04/m
    putrescence is the state of decaying matter with a foul smell used in formal contexts, while rotting is common everyday language.
