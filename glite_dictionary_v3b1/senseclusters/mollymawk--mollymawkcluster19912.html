mollymawk

noun
a large Southern Hemisphere seabird
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑliˌmɑk / (AmE), / ˈmɒlimɔːk / (BrE)
A large seabird of the albatross family, found in the Southern Hemisphere, with long narrow wings and known for powerful gliding flight.

Examples

A mollymawk bird
A mollymawk was spotted near the cliffs by the birdwatchers.
Researchers studied the migration patterns of the mollymawks in the Southern Ocean.
The mollymawk soared effortlessly above the waves, barely flapping its wings.
