extravasation

noun
discharge of lava or fumes from a volcano
Pronunciation: / ɛkˌstrævəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɛkˌstrævəˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of pouring out fumes or lava from a volcano, or the material deposited in this way

Examples

Volcanic extravasation
Scientists measured the extravasation of fumes to assess the activity of the volcano.
The extravasation of lava covered the entire valley after the volcanic eruption.
This region is characterized by frequent extravasation events that have shaped its unique landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eruption - the act or process of erupting (especially of a volcano)
