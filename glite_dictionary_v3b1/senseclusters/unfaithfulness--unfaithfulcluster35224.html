unfaithfulness

noun
the state of being unfaithful
1.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪθfəlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪθfʊlnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of not being loyal or faithful, especially in a relationship or commitment.

Examples

His unfaithfulness in marriage
He confessed to his unfaithfulness and apologized sincerely.
Rumors of unfaithfulness can destroy even the strongest of friendships.
The unfaithfulness of her partner deeply hurt her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: faithfulness - the quality of being faithful
  • synonym: infidelity - the action or state of being unfaithful to a spouse or partner
