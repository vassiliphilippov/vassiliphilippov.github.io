misgovernment

noun
bad or dishonest government
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnmənt / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈɡʌvənmənt / (BrE)
The act or instance of governing badly, especially through inefficiency or dishonesty.

Examples

Bad misgovernment
Many blamed the economic crisis on prolonged misgovernment and neglect.
Reports have highlighted widespread misgovernment at every level of the local administration.
The nation suffered years of misgovernment under the corrupt regime.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good government - efficient and honest government
  • synonym: maladministration - bad or corrupt management of public affairs
