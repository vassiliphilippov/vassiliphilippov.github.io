supercity

noun
very large metropolis
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌsɪti / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌsɪti /; / ˈsuːpəsɪti / (BrE)
a very large city or urban area formed by merging several cities or towns, often imagined as highly advanced or futuristic

Examples

A supercity plan
The government plans to connect several cities into a single supercity by 2050.
In the movie, the supercity is filled with flying cars and towering skyscrapers.
Living in a supercity can be both exciting and overwhelming due to its size and pace.
The government decided to create a supercity by combining several neighboring towns.
Living in a supercity offers more services but can also mean longer commutes.
After the merger, the new supercity became the largest urban area in the country.
The government is planning new transportation systems to connect the entire supercity.
Living in a supercity offers many job opportunities but also comes with high living costs.
The supercity has grown so much that it now includes several former suburbs and towns.

Related Terms

  • synonym: megacity - a city with a very large population, typically over ten million
  • synonym: megalopolis - a very large urban area made up of several cities and towns
  • synonym: metropolis - a large and important city, often the main city in a region
  • antonym: town - a smaller urban area than a city
  • antonym: village - a small settlement, usually in a rural area
