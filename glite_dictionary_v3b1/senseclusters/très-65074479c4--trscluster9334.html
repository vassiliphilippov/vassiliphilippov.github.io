très

adverb
very (French, stylish use)
7.1
Pronunciation: / treɪ / (AmE), / treɪ / (BrE)
Used in English to mean 'very', often for stylistic effect and to evoke French sophistication or emphasis.

Examples

A tres chic style
Her style is always très chic, especially at parties.
The atmosphere at the gala was très sophisticated and elegant.
The food was très delicious at that restaurant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: very - to a high degree or extremely
