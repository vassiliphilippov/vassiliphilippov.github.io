crumber

noun
restaurant table cleaner
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmbər / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmbə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to remove crumbs from tables in a restaurant or dining setting

Examples

Restaurant crumber
The crumber quickly cleared the crumbs from the table before the next guests arrived.
In fine dining restaurants, a crumber ensures that every table stays spotless during the meal.
After the main course, the crumber discreetly swept the table clean.
