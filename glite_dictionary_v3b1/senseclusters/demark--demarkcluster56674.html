demark

verb
to set boundaries
8.0
Pronunciation: / diˈmɑrk /; / dɪˈmɑrk / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːk / (BrE)
to set or mark the boundaries or limits of something

Examples

To demark property lines
The property lines were demarked with a row of stones along the edge.
Officials demark the construction zone to keep pedestrians safe.
The new law will demark the responsibilities of each department more clearly.
The river was used to demark the border between the two countries.
A line of trees helped demark the edge of the property.
Surveyors were hired to demark the new construction site.

  • synonym: define - to mark out the limits or edges of something
  • synonym: demarcate - to set the limits or boundaries of something
