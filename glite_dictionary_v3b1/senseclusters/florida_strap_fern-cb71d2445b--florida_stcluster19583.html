Florida strap fern

noun
a tropical fern with strap-shaped leaves
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈflɔːr.ə.də stræp fɜːrn/ /; / /ˈflɔːr.ɪ.də stræp fɜːrn/ (AmE), /ˈflɒ.rə.də stræp fɜːn/ /; / /ˈflɒ.rɪ.də stræp fɜːn/ (BrE)
a fern native to Florida, the West Indies, Mexico, and south to Uruguay, with pale yellow-green, strap-shaped leaves; often growing as an epiphyte or on the ground
