mayo

noun
mayonnaise; creamy condiment
4.1 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪ.oʊ/ /; / /ˈmeɪ.joʊ/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪ.əʊ/ /; / /ˈmeɪ.jəʊ/ (BrE)
