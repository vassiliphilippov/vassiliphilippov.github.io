mayo

noun
mayonnaise; creamy condiment
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪ.oʊ / (AmE), / ˈmeɪ.əʊ / (BrE)
A thick, creamy sauce made from egg yolks, oil, and vinegar or lemon juice, usually used as a condiment; short for mayonnaise.

Examples

A jar of mayo
Can you pass the mayo, please?
Most people like their tuna salad mixed with mayo and celery.
She added a generous spoonful of mayo to her sandwich.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mayonnaise - A thick, creamy dressing made of eggs, oil, and acid
