interchangeably

adverb
so as to be freely exchanged or substituted
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɚˈtʃeɪndʒəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈtʃeɪndʒəbli / (BrE)
In a way that allows things to be exchanged or substituted for one another without difference or effect.

Examples

Used interchangeably
In this recipe, butter and margarine can be used interchangeably without affecting the flavor.
The printer's parts can be replaced interchangeably thanks to their standard design.
The words 'big' and 'large' are often used interchangeably in everyday conversation.
