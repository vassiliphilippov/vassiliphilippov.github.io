Matthean

noun
follower of Matthew
Pronunciation: / məˈθiːən / (AmE), / məˈθiːən / (BrE)
a follower of the teachings of the apostle Matthew or a scholar who studies the Gospel of Matthew

Examples

Matthean scholar
The Matthean argued that the teachings in the Gospel of Matthew were unique among the Gospels.
At the conference, several Mattheans presented research on early Christian communities.
A respected Matthean contributed a chapter to the book on New Testament studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Markan - a follower or scholar of the Gospel of Mark
  • synonym: disciple - a follower or student of a teacher or leader
