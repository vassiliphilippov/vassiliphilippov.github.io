watchless

adjective
not on duty
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑtʃləs / (AmE), / ˈwɒtʃləs / (BrE)
not assigned to a watch or guard duty, especially in nautical or military contexts

Examples

Watchless sailor
After his shift ended, he was finally watchless and could relax in his cabin.
The watchless crew members gathered in the mess hall to enjoy their free time.
Being watchless for the evening, she took the opportunity to write a letter home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: off-duty - not currently working or on duty
  • antonym: on-duty - currently working or assigned to duty
