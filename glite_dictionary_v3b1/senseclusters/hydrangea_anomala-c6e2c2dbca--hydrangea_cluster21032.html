Hydrangea anomala

noun
species of climbing hydrangea plant
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdreɪndʒə əˈnɑːmələ / (AmE), / hɪˈdrændʒɪə əˈnɒmələ / (BrE)
A species of deciduous climbing plant with aerial roots, bearing white to creamy flowers in relatively flat clusters.
