rounder

noun
tool for rounding edges or corners
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndər / (AmE), / ˈraʊndə / (BrE)
a tool used to round off corners or edges, typically in woodworking or metalworking

Examples

Metal rounder tool
A metal rounder is ideal for softening the corners of sheet metal pieces.
The craftsman used a rounder to smooth the sharp edges of the table.
You will need a rounder to get that perfect curved finish on the wood.
