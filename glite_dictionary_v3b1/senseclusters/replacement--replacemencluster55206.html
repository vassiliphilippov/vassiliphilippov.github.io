Synonyms

    3.5 6/m
    substitution means taking one thing or person and putting another in its place, while replacement can also mean refilling.
  • refilling
    1.6 0.08/m
    refilling is putting more of something into a container or supply when it runs low, while replacement can also mean swapping items.
  • replenishment
    4.9 0.5/m
    replenishment is supplying what is used up to fill stocks again, while replacement often means changing one thing for another.
  • switch
    3.2 9/m
    switch is a change from one state or person to another, often temporary, while replacement means a permanent or direct substitute.
