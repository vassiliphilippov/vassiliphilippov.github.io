fair-haired

adjective
favorite or especially favored person
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈfeəˌheəd / (BrE)
Regarded with special favor or treated as a favorite, especially by those in authority.

Examples

The fair-haired favourite
Despite her mistakes, she remained the fair-haired employee in the company.
He was the fair-haired boy of the coaching staff, receiving every opportunity to succeed.
The press treated the young politician as their fair-haired candidate, overlooking his inexperience.
