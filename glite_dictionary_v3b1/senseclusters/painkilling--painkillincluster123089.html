painkilling

adjective
relieving emotional pain
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪnˌkɪlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpeɪnˌkɪlɪŋ / (BrE)
relieving or reducing emotional or psychological pain, often by providing temporary comfort or numbness from distress.

Examples

Painkilling comfort
Listening to music had a painkilling effect on her after the breakup.
He sought painkilling distractions to help him cope with his loss.
The support of friends was a painkilling comfort during her difficult time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: comforting - making someone feel less sad or worried
  • antonym: distressing - causing emotional pain or anxiety
  • synonym: soothing - making someone feel calmer or less upset
