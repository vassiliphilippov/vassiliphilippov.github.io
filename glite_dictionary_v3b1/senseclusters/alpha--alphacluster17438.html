alpha

noun
dominant group member
Pronunciation: / ˈælfə / (AmE), / ˈælfə / (BrE)
the dominant individual in a group, especially among animals or people

Examples

Alpha wolf
In the wolf pack, the alpha makes all the important decisions for the group.
He acted like the alpha during the meeting, taking charge of every discussion.
Among her friends, she is clearly the alpha who everyone looks up to.

Related Terms

  • antonym: follower - a person who follows the lead of others
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
