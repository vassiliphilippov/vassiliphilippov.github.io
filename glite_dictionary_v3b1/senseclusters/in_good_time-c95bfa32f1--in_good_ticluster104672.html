in good time

adverb
at the appropriate time
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɪn ɡʊd taɪm / (AmE), / ɪn ɡʊd taɪm / (BrE)
at the appropriate or proper time; not too late or too early

Examples

Arrive in good time
