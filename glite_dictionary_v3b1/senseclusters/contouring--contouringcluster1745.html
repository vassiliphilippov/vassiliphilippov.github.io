contouring

noun
reshaping body or face

Pronunciation: /kən.ˈtʊr.ɪŋ/ /; / /kən.ˈtuːr.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /kən.ˈtʊə.rɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkɒn.tʊə.rɪŋ/ (BrE)
