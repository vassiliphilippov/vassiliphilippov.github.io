contouring

noun
reshaping body or face
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːntʊrɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɒntjʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of reshaping the body or face, often through surgical or cosmetic methods, to improve appearance

Examples

Facial contouring surgery
After her surgery, she underwent contouring to achieve a more defined jawline.
Many people choose contouring procedures to enhance their body shape after major weight loss.
Advances in non-surgical contouring have made it easier to refine facial features.

Related Terms

  • synonym: augmentation - a procedure to increase or enhance a part of the body
  • synonym: sculpting - reshaping or modeling the body or face for cosmetic purposes
