distribute

verb
to release or make available
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪbjut / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪbjuːt / (BrE)
to make something available to the public or to a particular group; to release for sale or use

Examples

To distribute a movie
Many companies distribute free samples to promote their products.
The organization will distribute food to those affected by the hurricane.
The publisher plans to distribute the new novel internationally next month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: release - to make something available for use or sale
  • antonym: withdraw - to remove something from availability
