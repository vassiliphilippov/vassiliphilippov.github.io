ramp production

verb
increase production levels
5.9 4.1
Pronunciation: /ræmp prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (AmE), /ræmp prə.ˈdʌk.ʃən/ (BrE)
to increase the level or rate of production, typically in response to higher demand or new project phases.

Examples

To ramp production quickly
After solving the supply chain issues, the factory was able to ramp production quickly.
The automaker announced plans to ramp production of electric vehicles next year.
We need to ramp production to meet the holiday demand.
