Marxian

adjective
of Karl Marx's theories
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrksiən / (AmE), / ˈmɑːksiən / (BrE)
relating to the theories, ideas, or works of Karl Marx, especially in economics or social science

Examples

Marxian theory
The university offers a course on Marxian approaches to social change.
Her research is grounded in Marxian economic theory and class analysis.
Many debates in sociology involve Marxian perspectives on power and inequality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Marxist - relating to the political and economic theories of Karl Marx
  • antonym: capitalist - relating to the economic system based on private ownership and free markets
