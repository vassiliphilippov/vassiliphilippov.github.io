mail out

verb
send by mail to many recipients
2.5
Pronunciation: / meɪl aʊt / (AmE), / meɪl aʊt / (BrE)
to send something to someone by mail, especially to a large group of recipients

Examples

To mail out letters
After updating the brochure, they mailed out over 1,000 copies to customers.
The organization mails out monthly newsletters to its members.
We mailed out invitations to all of our friends for the wedding.
