Cineraria maritima

noun
a Mediterranean perennial with white woolly leaves
Pronunciation: / sɪnəˈrɛəriə mərɪˈtiːmə / (AmE), / sɪnəˈrɛːrɪə mæˈrɪtɪmə / (BrE)
a perennial plant of the Mediterranean region with stiff, much-branched stems and very white, woolly leaves, often grown for its ornamental foliage

Examples

Cineraria maritima plant
Cineraria maritima thrives in full sun and well-drained soils near the coast.
Gardeners often choose Cineraria maritima for its striking silver foliage in landscape designs.
The white woolly leaves of Cineraria maritima contrast beautifully with brightly colored flowers.
