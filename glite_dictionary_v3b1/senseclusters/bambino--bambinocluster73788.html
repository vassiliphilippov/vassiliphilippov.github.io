bambino

noun
a young child or toddler
5.6
Pronunciation: / bæmˈbiːnoʊ / (AmE), / bæmˈbiːnəʊ / (BrE)
A young child, especially one who is just beginning to walk; a toddler.

Examples

A bambino toddler
In the old photograph, a smiling bambino clings to his mother's hand.
She watched as the bambino took his first steps across the living room.
The artist painted a beautiful portrait of a sleeping bambino.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toddler - a young child who is just learning to walk
