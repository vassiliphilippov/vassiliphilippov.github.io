Synonyms

  • rhythm
    3.9 3.13/m
    rhythm is the repeating pattern of stressed and unstressed sounds in speech or music, while cadence is the flowing pattern.
  • meter
    6.0 3.08/m
    meter is the set pattern of beats in poetry or music used to build lines, while cadence is a more natural flow of sound.
  • beat
    1.0 20/m
    beat is the basic pulse you tap with your foot in music, while cadence is the way phrases flow and end.
  • cadency
    9.9 0.00/m
    cadency is a rare term that names the quality of rhythmic flow in technical use, while cadence means the pattern itself.
