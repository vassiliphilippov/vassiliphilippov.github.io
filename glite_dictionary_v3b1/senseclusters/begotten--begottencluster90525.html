begotten

adjective
generated by biological procreation
Pronunciation: / bɪˈɡɑtən / (AmE), / bɪˈɡɒtən / (BrE)
(of a child) generated by procreation; born as the result of biological reproduction, especially as used in formal or religious contexts.

Examples

The begotten son
He claimed to be the begotten son of the former king.
In ancient times, a naturally begotten child had a special status in the family.
The doctrine refers to Jesus as the only begotten son of God.

Related Terms

  • synonym: born - having come into existence as a result of birth
