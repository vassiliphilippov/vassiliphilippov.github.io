royal court

noun
monarch's governing body and advisers
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrɔɪ.əl kɔrt / (AmE), / ˈrɔɪ.əl kɔːt / (BrE)
the group of people who surround and serve a monarch, including advisers, family, attendants, and nobility
