itch

verb
to cause an itching sensation
2.9
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪtʃ / (BrE)
to feel or cause an irritating sensation on the skin that makes you want to scratch, or to scratch in response to this feeling

Examples

To itch badly
If your skin continues to itch, you should see a doctor.
My arm began to itch after I touched the plant.
Wool sweaters always itch me in the winter.
If you itch your skin too much, it might become irritated.
She tried not to itch her arm after getting stung by the nettles.
Whenever he had a mosquito bite, he would itch it until it turned red.
Every time I wear this sweater, my arms itch like crazy.
My scalp started to itch after I used the new shampoo.
Sometimes mosquito bites itch so much that it's hard not to scratch them.
