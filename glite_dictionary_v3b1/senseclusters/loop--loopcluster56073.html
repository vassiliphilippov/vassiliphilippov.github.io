loop

noun
contraceptive intrauterine device
5.3 2.1
Pronunciation: /luːp/ (AmE), /luːp/ (BrE)
a shape or object that bends around and closes to form a ring, curve, or oval (such as a piece of string, a metal ring, a fingerprint pattern, or a contraceptive device)

A loop of string

  • synonym: arch - a fingerprint pattern with ridges that enter from one side and exit the other
