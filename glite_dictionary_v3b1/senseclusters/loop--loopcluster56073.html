loop

noun
contraceptive intrauterine device
5.3
Pronunciation: / luːp / (AmE), / luːp / (BrE)
a shape or object that bends around and closes to form a ring, curve, or oval (such as a piece of string, a metal ring, a fingerprint pattern, or a contraceptive device)

Examples

A loop of string

Related Terms

  • synonym: IUD - a contraceptive device inserted into the uterus to prevent pregnancy
  • synonym: arch - a fingerprint pattern with ridges that enter from one side and exit the other
  • synonym: circle - a round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from the center
  • degree higher: knot - a fastening made by tying a piece of string, rope, etc.
  • synonym: whorl - spiral-shaped pattern of fingerprint ridges
