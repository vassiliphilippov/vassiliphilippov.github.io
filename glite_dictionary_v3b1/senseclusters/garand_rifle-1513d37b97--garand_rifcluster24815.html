Garand rifle

noun
semiautomatic U.S. military rifle (M1 Garand)
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrænd ˈraɪfəl / (AmE), / ɡəˈrænd ˈraɪfəl / (BrE)
A semiautomatic rifle, officially known as the M1 Garand, used by the U.S. military during World War II and the Korean War.

Examples

The Garand rifle
Collectors often seek out a pristine Garand rifle for their historical value.
He learned to fire the Garand rifle during basic training.
The Garand rifle was standard issue for American infantry during World War II.
