pitch

noun
degree of slope from the horizontal
7.8
Pronunciation: / pɪtʃ / (AmE), / pɪtʃ / (BrE)
The degree of slope or angle from a horizontal plane, especially in reference to roofs or surfaces.

Examples

Roof pitch
A shallow pitch on the driveway allows for easier walking.
Builders must carefully measure the pitch of the stairs for safety.
The roof had a steep pitch to prevent snow buildup.
