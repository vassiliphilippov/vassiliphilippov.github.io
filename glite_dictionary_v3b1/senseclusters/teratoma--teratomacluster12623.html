teratoma

noun
tumor with mixed abnormal tissues
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛrəˈtoʊmə / (AmE), / ˌtɛrəˈtəʊmə / (BrE)
a type of tumor composed of a mixture of different tissues, such as hair, muscle, or bone, that are not normally present at that location

Examples

A teratoma tumor
A teratoma can contain several types of tissues, including teeth and hair.
Pediatric surgeons frequently treat cases where a teratoma develops in newborns.
The doctors discovered a teratoma in her ovary during surgery.
