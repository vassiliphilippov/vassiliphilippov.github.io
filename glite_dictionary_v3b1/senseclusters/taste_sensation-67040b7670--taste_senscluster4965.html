taste sensation

noun
the experience of perceiving flavor
4.3
Pronunciation: / teɪst sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / teɪst sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
the sensation experienced when taste buds detect the chemical composition of a substance, resulting in the perception of flavor

Examples

Strong taste sensation
A spicy dish can trigger a strong taste sensation on the tongue.
Each fruit offers a unique taste sensation that delights the palate.
The medicine's bitter taste sensation lingered in her mouth.
