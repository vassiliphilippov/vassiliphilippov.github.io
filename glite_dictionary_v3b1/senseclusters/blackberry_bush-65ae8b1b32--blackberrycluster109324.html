blackberry bush

noun
shrub that bears blackberries
3.5 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈblæk.ber.ɪ bʊʃ/ /; / /ˈblæk.bər.ɪ bʊʃ/ (AmE), /ˈblæk.bə.rɪ bʊʃ/ /; / /ˈblæk.briː bʊʃ/ (BrE)
A bushy plant with thorny stems that produces sweet, edible black or dark purple berries called blackberries.

Examples

A blackberry bush plant
After getting scratched by a blackberry bush, she wore gloves the next time she went picking.
The blackberry bush sprawled across the garden fence, covered with ripening fruit.
We picked fresh berries from the old blackberry bush behind the shed.
