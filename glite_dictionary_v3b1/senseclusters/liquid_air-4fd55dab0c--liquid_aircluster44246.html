liquid air

noun
air in a liquid state; cooled, pressurized air
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪkwɪd ˌɛr / (AmE), / ˈlɪkwɪd ˌɛə / (BrE)
air that has been cooled and pressurized until it becomes a liquid, typically used in scientific and industrial processes.

Examples

Store liquid air in tanks
The laboratory stored liquid air to conduct low-temperature experiments.
Transportation of liquid air requires specialized insulated containers due to its extremely low temperature.
When liquid air evaporates, it expands rapidly and can be used to power engines.

Related Terms

  • synonym: liquid nitrogen - nitrogen in liquid form at very low temperature
  • synonym: liquid oxygen - oxygen in a liquid state used in industry
