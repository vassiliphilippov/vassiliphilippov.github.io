Italianate

adjective
influenced by Italian style
Pronunciation: / ɪˈtæliəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈtæliəneɪt / (BrE)
having characteristics or style influenced by Italian art, architecture, or design

Examples

Italianate architecture
The museum's Italianate facade features arched windows and decorative cornices.
Many 19th-century homes in the city were built in the Italianate style.
The Italianate garden was filled with statues and symmetrical pathways.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Gothic - relating to a style of architecture from medieval Europe
  • synonym: Italian - relating to Italy or its people, language, or culture
