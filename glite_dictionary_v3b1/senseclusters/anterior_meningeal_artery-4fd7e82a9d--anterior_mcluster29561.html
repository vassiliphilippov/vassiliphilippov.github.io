anterior meningeal artery

noun
artery supplying anterior cranial meninges
Pronunciation: / ænˈtɪriɚ məˈnɪndʒiəl ˈɑrtəri / (AmE), / ænˈtɪəriə məˈnɪndʒiəl ˈɑːtəri / (BrE)
a branch of the anterior ethmoidal artery that supplies blood to the meninges in the anterior cranial fossa

Examples

The anterior meningeal artery branch
An aneurysm in the anterior meningeal artery is a rare but serious condition.
Damage to the anterior meningeal artery can cause bleeding in the anterior cranial fossa.
The neurosurgeon located the anterior meningeal artery during the procedure to avoid accidental injury.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: middle meningeal artery - a major artery supplying the meninges in the middle cranial fossa
  • antonym: posterior meningeal artery - artery supplying the meninges at the back (posterior) of the cranial cavity
