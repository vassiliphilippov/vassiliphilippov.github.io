Australian sword lily

noun
Australian flowering herb with woolly flowers
9.6
Pronunciation: / əˌstreɪljən ˈsɔrd ˌlɪli / (AmE), / ɒˈstreɪlɪən ˈsɔːd ˌlɪli / (BrE)
