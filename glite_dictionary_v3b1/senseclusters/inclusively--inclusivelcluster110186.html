inclusively

adverb
including all items
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkluːsɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkluːsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that includes everything or everyone within stated limits, including the endpoints in a range or list

Examples

Price covers inclusively all
The price covers all meals and activities inclusively during your stay.
The rule applies inclusively to students from every department.
The dates are counted inclusively, so both the start and end days are included.
The museum is open from Monday to Friday, inclusively, during the summer months.
Children aged five to twelve, inclusively, are eligible for the camp program.
The offer is valid from June 1 to June 10, inclusively.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclusively - in a way that leaves out others or other things
