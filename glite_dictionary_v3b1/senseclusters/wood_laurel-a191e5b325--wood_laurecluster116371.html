wood laurel

noun
Eurasian shrub with glossy leaves
7.1
Pronunciation: / wʊd ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / wʊd ˈlɒrəl / (BrE)
A bushy Eurasian shrub with glossy, leathery oblong leaves and yellow-green flowers.

Examples

Wood laurel with yellow flowers
Clusters of yellow-green flowers make the wood laurel stand out in the spring.
Gardeners often admire the glossy leaves of the wood laurel.
The wood laurel thrives in well-drained woodland soil.
