non-

x
negative prefix
Pronunciation: / nɑn / (AmE), / nɒn / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'not' or 'without', used to form words with the opposite or negative meaning

Examples

Non-smoking policy
The company introduced a non-smoking policy in all its offices.
He prefers to use non-toxic cleaning products at home.
Many people choose non-dairy milk for dietary reasons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: in- - a prefix meaning 'not' or 'without'
  • synonym: un- - a prefix meaning 'not' or 'the opposite of'
