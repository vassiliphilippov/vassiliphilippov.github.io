strip away

verb
remove or eliminate something completely
Pronunciation: / strɪp əˈweɪ / (AmE), / strɪp əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to gradually or completely remove or eliminate something, especially something unnecessary or unwanted

Examples

To strip away layers
The investigation aims to strip away the lies and reveal the truth.
Years of exposure to the elements have stripped away the building’s paint.
You need to strip away the old wallpaper before painting the walls.

Related Terms

  • antonym: add - put something together with something else, opposite of remove
  • synonym: remove - take something away or get rid of it
