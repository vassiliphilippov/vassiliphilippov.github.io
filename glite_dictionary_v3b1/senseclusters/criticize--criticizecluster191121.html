Synonyms

  • pick apart
    5.2 0.65/m
    pick apart means examine and criticize something in detail, while criticize is a general act of pointing out faults.
  • reproach
    6.5 1.20/m
    reproach means telling someone you are disappointed in their actions, while criticize focuses on pointing out faults.
  • reprove
    7.3 0.63/m
    reprove means gently telling someone their behavior is wrong in private, while criticize can be harsher or public.
  • fault
    4.3 2.99/m
    fault means blaming someone for a mistake, while criticize covers pointing out faults about people or things.
  • disparage
    7.7 2.20/m
    disparage means speaking of someone to show no respect and belittle them, while criticize simply points out faults.
  • deprecate
    7.6 0.16/m
    deprecate means saying you disapprove, often in a mild or formal way, while criticize is any direct pointing out of faults.
  • decry
    7.7 3.43/m
    decry means publicly state strong disapproval of something, while criticize can be milder or used in private situations.
  • denounce
    6.8 9.42/m
    denounce means openly and strongly condemn someone or something, often publicly, while criticize is a more general expression of disapproval.
  • disapprove
    2.9 6.14/m
    disapprove means to have or show a negative opinion, often quietly, while criticize is actively pointing out faults.
