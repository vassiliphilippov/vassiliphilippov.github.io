hot on

adjective
keenly interested or enthusiastic about
7.0
Pronunciation: / hɑt ɑn / (AmE), / hɒt ɒn / (BrE)
very enthusiastic about or keen on pursuing or achieving something; strongly interested or focused on a particular subject

Examples

To be hot on learning
He’s really hot on learning new languages these days.
Journalists are hot on the trail of the story.
The company is hot on developing innovative technologies for its products.
