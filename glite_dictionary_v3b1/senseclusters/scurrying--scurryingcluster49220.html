scurrying

adjective
moving quickly in a hurried way
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈskɜriɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskʌriɪŋ / (BrE)
moving quickly and with short, hurried movements, especially because of fear or urgency

Examples

The scurrying mice
A group of scurrying mice disappeared into the shadows when the lights came on.
The children watched the scurrying ants as they carried crumbs back to their nest.
We had to step carefully to avoid the scurrying pigeons in the city square.

Related Terms

  • synonym: darting - moving or running suddenly and quickly
  • synonym: scurry - to move quickly with short, hurried movements
