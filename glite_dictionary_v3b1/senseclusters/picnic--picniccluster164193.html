picnic

noun
outdoor social meal or gathering
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈpɪk.nɪk/ (AmE), /ˈpɪk.nɪk/ (BrE)
an occasion or informal meal where people eat outdoors, usually for pleasure or as a social event

Examples

Family picnic
She packed sandwiches and fruit for their afternoon picnic.
The weather was perfect for a picnic by the riverbank.
We're planning a family picnic at the park this Saturday.
Isabella spread a blanket on the grass for their picnic as the sun began to shine.
The family packed sandwiches and drinks for their afternoon picnic in the park.
We decided to have a picnic by the lake on Sunday afternoon.
