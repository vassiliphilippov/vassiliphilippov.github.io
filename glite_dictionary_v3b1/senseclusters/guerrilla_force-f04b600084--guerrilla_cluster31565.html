guerrilla force

noun
irregular armed group using sabotage and harassment
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɪl.ə fɔrs / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɪl.ə fɔːs / (BrE)
a group of irregular armed fighters, often operating in rural areas, that uses sabotage and harassment tactics against a larger or conventional military

Examples

A guerrilla force attacks
Journalists risked their lives to report from areas held by the guerrilla force.
The army struggled to defeat the elusive guerrilla force using conventional tactics.
The mountainous region was controlled by a guerrilla force opposed to the central government.

Related Terms

  • synonym: insurgent - a person who rises in revolt against authority, especially in armed forces
  • synonym: militia - a military force that is raised from the civil population to supplement a regular army
  • antonym: regular army - an official, government-maintained standing army
