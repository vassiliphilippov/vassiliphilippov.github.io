mouse-ear chickweed

noun
a plant related to common chickweed
9.9 8.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmaʊs.ɪr ˈtʃɪk.wiːd/ /; / /ˈmaʊs.iːr ˈtʃɪk.wiːd/ (AmE), /ˈmaʊs.ɪə ˈtʃɪk.wiːd/ /; / /ˈmaʊs.iːə ˈtʃɪk.wiːd/ (BrE)
any of several low-growing plants related to the common chickweed, especially those of the genera Cerastium and Stellaria, with small white flowers and hairy leaves resembling a mouse's ear.

The garden was quickly overrun by mouse-ear chickweed after the warm spring rains.
