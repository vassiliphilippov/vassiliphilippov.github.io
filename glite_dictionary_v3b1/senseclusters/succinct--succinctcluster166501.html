Synonyms

  • concise
    4.2 3/m
    concise gives a lot of information clearly in few words and is common in formal writing, while succinct means short and without extra words.
  • crisp
    4.1 1/m
    crisp sounds sharp and lively in speech or writing, while succinct simply points to being short and free of unnecessary words.
  • compact
    4.2 1/m
    compact describes an idea or text packed into a small space, while succinct focuses on clear, brief phrasing without extra words.
  • brief
    2.0 50/m
    brief means short in time or words and may lack detail, while succinct stresses being clear and not wasting words.
