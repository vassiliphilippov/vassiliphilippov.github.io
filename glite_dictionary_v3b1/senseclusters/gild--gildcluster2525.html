gild

verb
cover or decorate with gold
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɡɪld / (AmE), / ɡɪld / (BrE)
to cover or decorate with a thin layer of gold or a substance that looks like gold

Examples

To gild with gold
Artisans spent months carefully gilding the ornate ceilings of the palace.
The domes of the cathedral were gilded and sparkled in the sunlight.
They decided to gild the picture frame to make it look more elegant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gold-plate - to coat (a metal object) with a thin layer of gold
