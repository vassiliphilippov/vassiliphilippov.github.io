PPA

noun
online ad payment model
8.6
Pronunciation: / piː piː eɪ / (AmE), / piː piː eɪ / (BrE)
an online advertising payment model in which the advertiser pays only when a specific user action, such as a sale or signup, occurs

Examples

PPA ad model
The marketing team decided to use a PPA model to maximize their return on investment.
With PPA advertising, companies only pay when a user completes a desired action.
Many online businesses prefer PPA because it directly links spending to results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CPA - cost per action; an advertising model where payment is made only when a specific action occurs
  • antonym: CPC - cost per click; an advertising model where payment is made for each click
  • antonym: CPM - cost per mille; an advertising model where payment is made per thousand impressions
