out

adjective
unconscious (after a blow)
Pronunciation: / aʊt / (AmE), / aʊt / (BrE)
Unconscious, especially as a result of a blow to the head or body.

Examples

Knocked out cold
After falling and hitting her head, she was out for several minutes.
He was knocked out in the first round of the match.
The boxer was completely out before the referee even started counting.

  • antonym: conscious - awake and aware of one's surroundings
