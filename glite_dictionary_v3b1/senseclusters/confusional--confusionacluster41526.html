confusional

adjective
related to confusion
8.8
Pronunciation: / kənˈfjuːʒənəl / (AmE), / kənˈfjuːʒənəl / (BrE)

Examples

Confusional state

A confusional episode can sometimes occur in elderly patients during hospitalization.
The nurse monitored the man's confusional symptoms throughout the night.
