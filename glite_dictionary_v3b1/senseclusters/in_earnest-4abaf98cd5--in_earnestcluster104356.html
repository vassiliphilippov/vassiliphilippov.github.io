in earnest

adverb
seriously; with sincere intention
Pronunciation: / ɪn ˈɜrnəst / (AmE), / ɪn ˈɜːnɪst / (BrE)
with serious intention, effort, or sincerity; not joking or superficial.

Examples

Start in earnest
She started training in earnest when she decided to run the marathon.
The negotiations began in earnest after both parties signed the agreement.
The search for missing hikers continued in earnest despite the bad weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seriously - in a serious manner; sincerely or earnestly
