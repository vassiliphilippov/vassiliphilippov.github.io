swirly

noun
bullying toilet prank
Pronunciation: / ˈswɜːrli / (AmE), / ˈswɜːli / (BrE)
a bullying act in which someone's head is forced into a toilet and the toilet is flushed or the water is swirled around

Examples

Give a swirly at school
The older students threatened to give the new kid a swirly if he didn't hand over his lunch money.
He was embarrassed after getting a swirly in the school restroom by the bullies.
Stories about the dreaded swirly circulated among the students before the first day of high school.

Related Terms
