swingarm

noun
hinged support arm
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋˌɑrm / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋˌɑːm / (BrE)
a hinged support arm that allows equipment such as lights, monitors, or cameras to be positioned or moved easily

Examples

Camera swingarm
The technician adjusted the swingarm to position the camera for the perfect shot.
A flexible swingarm makes it easy to move the monitor closer or farther away.
She installed a new swingarm to support the studio lights above her desk.
