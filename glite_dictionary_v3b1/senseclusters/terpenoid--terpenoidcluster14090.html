terpenoid

adjective
from terpenes
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrpəˌnɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈtɜːpənɔɪd / (BrE)
relating to or derived from terpenes, especially describing chemical compounds with structures similar to terpenes

Examples

Terpenoid structure

A terpenoid structure can be found in many natural products used in medicine.
