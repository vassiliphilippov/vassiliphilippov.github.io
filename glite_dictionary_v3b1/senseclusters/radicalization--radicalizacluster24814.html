radicalization

noun
adopting extreme beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˌrædɪkələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrædɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adopting extreme political, social, or religious beliefs, often leading to support for drastic change or action

Examples

Education programs can help counter the radicalization of vulnerable individuals.
