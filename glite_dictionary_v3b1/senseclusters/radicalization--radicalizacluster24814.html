radicalization

noun
adopting extreme beliefs
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌrædɪkələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌrædɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adopting extreme political, social, or religious beliefs, often leading to support for drastic change or action

Examples

Online radicalization
The government is working to prevent the radicalization of young people through online platforms.
Experts study the causes and effects of radicalization in different communities.
Education programs can help counter the radicalization of vulnerable individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremism - the holding of extreme political or religious views
  • antonym: moderation - the avoidance of extremes or excesses
