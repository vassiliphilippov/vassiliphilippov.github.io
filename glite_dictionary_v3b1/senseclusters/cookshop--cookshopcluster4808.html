cookshop

noun
small food shop
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌʃɒp / (BrE)
an old-fashioned small restaurant or shop where cooked food is sold to customers

Examples

Lunch at a cookshop
They stopped at a local cookshop for a quick lunch after their walk.
In the past, a cookshop was a common place for workers to buy hot meals.
The old cookshop on the corner is now a modern café.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eatery - a place where people can buy and eat food
  • synonym: restaurant - a place where meals are prepared and served to customers
