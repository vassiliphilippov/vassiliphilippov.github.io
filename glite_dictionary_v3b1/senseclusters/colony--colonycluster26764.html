colony

noun
community with shared interest or occupation
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːləni / (AmE), / ˈkɒləni / (BrE)
A community of people with a shared interest or occupation living together in a particular place.

Examples

An artist colony
The secluded mountain colony attracts people interested in sustainable living.
The village was once a famous colony for poets and writers seeking inspiration.
They moved to a colony of artists by the lake to pursue a quieter life.
