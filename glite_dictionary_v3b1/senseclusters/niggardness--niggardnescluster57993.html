niggardness

noun
extreme stinginess
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɪɡərdnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɪɡədnəs / (BrE)
Extreme unwillingness to spend money or share resources; excessive stinginess.

Examples

Extreme niggardness
He was criticized for his niggardness in contributing to the community fund.
Her niggardness stood in sharp contrast to her neighbor's generosity.
The niggardness of the landlord made repairs to the building a rare event.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generosity - willingness to give more than necessary or expected
  • synonym: stinginess - unwillingness to spend money or share; lack of generosity
