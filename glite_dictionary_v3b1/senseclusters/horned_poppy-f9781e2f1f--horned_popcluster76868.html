horned poppy

noun
yellow-flowered Eurasian coastal plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɔrn̩d ˈpɑpi / (AmE), / hɔːnd ˈpɒpi / (BrE)
A yellow-flowered Eurasian plant with bluish-green leaves, often found along sandy shores and naturalized in eastern North America.
