doubting

adjective
showing doubt or uncertainty
Pronunciation: / ˈdaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
feeling or showing uncertainty or lack of conviction; marked by doubt

Examples

A doubting look
His doubting nature made it difficult for him to accept new ideas easily.
She gave him a doubting look, unsure if he was telling the truth.
The student's doubting questions challenged the assumptions in the lecture.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confident - certain about something; having no doubt
  • synonym: skeptical - having or expressing doubt or disbelief
