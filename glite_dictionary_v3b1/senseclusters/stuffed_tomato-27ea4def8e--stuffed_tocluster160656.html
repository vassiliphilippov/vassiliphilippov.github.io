stuffed tomato

noun
tomato filled with mixture and baked
2.1 2.8
Pronunciation: /stʌft tə.ˈmeɪ.toʊ/ /; / /stʌft tə.ˈɑː.toʊ/ (AmE), /stʌft tə.ˈmɑː.təʊ/ (BrE)
