apostolicity

noun
connection to apostles
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˌstɑːˈlɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌæpəˌstɒˈlɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being connected to the apostles or their teachings, especially in a church context

Examples

The apostolicity of church
The apostolicity of the church is a key part of its doctrine.
Historians debate the apostolicity of certain early Christian communities.
Many denominations claim apostolicity as a sign of their authenticity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apostolic - relating to the apostles or their teachings
  • synonym: catholicity - the quality of being universal, especially in a church context
