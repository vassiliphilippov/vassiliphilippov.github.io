carrier

noun
vehicle for transporting goods or people
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛriɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a self-propelled vehicle designed to transport goods, people, or other cargo

Examples

Armored carrier
Military convoys often include an armored personnel carrier for troop movement.
The company invested in a new carrier to transport fresh produce across the country.
The refrigerated carrier kept the seafood cold during the long journey.
