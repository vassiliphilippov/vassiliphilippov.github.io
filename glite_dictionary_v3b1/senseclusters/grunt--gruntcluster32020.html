grunt

verb
make a low animal-like sound
Pronunciation: / ɡrʌnt / (AmE), / ɡrʌnt / (BrE)
to make a short, low, guttural sound, typically of effort, discomfort, or disapproval

Examples

To grunt with effort
He grunted a response and turned back to his book.
She grunted in frustration when her computer froze again.
The wrestler grunted as he lifted the heavy weights above his head.

Related Terms

  • synonym: groan - make a long, low sound expressing pain or discomfort
