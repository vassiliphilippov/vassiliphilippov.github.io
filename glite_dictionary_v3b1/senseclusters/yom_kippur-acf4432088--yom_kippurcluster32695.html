Yom Kippur

proper noun
Jewish holy day
Pronunciation: / joʊm kɪˈpʊr / (AmE), / jɒm kɪˈpʊə / (BrE)
the holiest day in Judaism, observed with fasting, prayer, and reflection to seek forgiveness and atonement

Examples

Yom Kippur prayers
Many families gather at the synagogue to observe Yom Kippur together.
On Yom Kippur, people fast and reflect on their actions from the past year.
The city was quiet as most businesses closed for Yom Kippur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Passover - a major Jewish festival commemorating the Exodus from Egypt
  • synonym: Rosh Hashanah - the Jewish New Year festival
