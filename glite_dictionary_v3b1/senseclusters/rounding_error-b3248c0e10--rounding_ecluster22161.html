rounding error

noun
error due to rounding numbers
5.6 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈraʊndɪŋ ˌɛrɚ / (AmE), / ˈraʊndɪŋ ˌɛrə / (BrE)
an inaccuracy in a calculation that occurs when numbers are rounded to a limited number of decimal places

Examples

A rounding error in math
Careful accounting reduces the chance of a significant rounding error in financial reports.
Even small rounding errors can accumulate and affect the results over time.
The calculation was slightly off because of a rounding error in the final step.
