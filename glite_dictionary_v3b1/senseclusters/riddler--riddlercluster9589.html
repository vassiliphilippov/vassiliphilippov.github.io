riddler

noun
person who asks riddles
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdlər / (AmE), / ˈrɪdlə / (BrE)
a person who asks riddles or puzzles, often to challenge or entertain others

Examples

The riddler asks puzzles
The riddler stumped the children with a clever question about animals.
At the party, a skilled riddler kept everyone entertained with brain teasers.
She became known as the office riddler for her daily lunchtime puzzles.
