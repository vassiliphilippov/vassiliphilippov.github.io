PPH

noun
pounds per hour
Pronunciation: / pi pi eɪtʃ / (AmE), / piː piː eɪtʃ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for pounds per hour, a unit measuring the rate of mass flow or consumption

Examples

Fuel PPH
The fuel pump delivers 50 PPH to the engine during normal operation.
To increase efficiency, the system must reduce its water usage below 100 PPH.
The technician recorded the air flow as 200 PPH in the test results.
