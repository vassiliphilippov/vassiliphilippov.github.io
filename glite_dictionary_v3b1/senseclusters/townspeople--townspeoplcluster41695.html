townspeople

noun
people living in a town
2.7 2.34/m
Pronunciation: / Ààta änzÀåpip…ôl / (AmE), / Ààta änzÀåpiÀêp…ôl / (BrE)
the people who live in a town, especially as distinct from those living in the countryside

Examples

Local townspeople
She quickly became friends with many of the local townspeople.
The townspeople gathered in the square for the annual festival.
The mayor thanked the townspeople for their support during the difficult year.

Synonyms
