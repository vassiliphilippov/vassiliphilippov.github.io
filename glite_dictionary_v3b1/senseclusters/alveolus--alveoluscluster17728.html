alveolus

noun
bony tooth socket in the jaw
8.3
Pronunciation: / ælˈvioʊləs / (AmE), / alˈviːələs / (BrE)
a small bony cavity in the jaw in which the root of a tooth is held

Examples

Tooth alveolus
A fractured alveolus can complicate the healing process after dental surgery.
Bone loss in the alveolus may lead to loosening of the surrounding teeth.
The dentist examined the alveolus to check for signs of infection after the tooth was removed.
