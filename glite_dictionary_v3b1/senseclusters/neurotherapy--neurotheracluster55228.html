neurotherapy

noun
brain function treatment
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnʊroʊˈθɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌnjʊərəʊˈθɛrəpi / (BrE)
a treatment method that uses techniques to improve or regulate brain function

Examples

Neurotherapy treatment
Many clinics now offer neurotherapy to help patients with attention disorders.
After several sessions of neurotherapy, he noticed improvements in his memory and focus.
Researchers are studying how neurotherapy can support recovery after brain injuries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neurofeedback - a technique that trains brain activity using real-time monitoring
  • antonym: psychotherapy - treatment of mental disorders by psychological means
