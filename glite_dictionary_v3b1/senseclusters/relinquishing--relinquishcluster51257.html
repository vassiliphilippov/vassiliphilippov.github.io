relinquishing

noun
act of giving up or surrendering
9.0
Pronunciation: / rəˈlɪŋkwɪʃɪŋ /; / rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving up, surrendering, or renouncing something such as a right, claim, position, or possession

Examples

The relinquishing of rights
After much thought, she accepted the relinquishing of her claim to the inheritance.
His relinquishing of the position surprised everyone at the company.
The relinquishing of control was a difficult but necessary decision for the board.
By relinquishing control, the organization allowed for greater innovation.
Her relinquishing of the post surprised many of her colleagues.
The relinquishing of his claim cleared the way for new ownership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abandonment - the act of giving up or leaving something permanently
  • synonym: renunciation - the act of formally rejecting or giving up something
  • antonym: retaining - the act of keeping or holding on to something
  • synonym: surrender - the act of giving up something or yielding
