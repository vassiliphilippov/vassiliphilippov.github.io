scuffle

noun
a hoe operated by pushing
5.5 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈskʌf.əl/ (AmE), /ˈskʌf.əl/ (BrE)
a kind of hoe designed to be pushed to loosen or weed soil, rather than pulled

Examples

Garden scuffle
The gardener used a scuffle to remove the weeds between the rows of vegetables.
Unlike traditional hoes, the scuffle is meant to be pushed through the soil.
When the soil is loose, a scuffle makes weeding quick and easy.
