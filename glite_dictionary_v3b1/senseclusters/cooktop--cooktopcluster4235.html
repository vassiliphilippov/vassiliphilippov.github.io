cooktop

noun
kitchen cooking surface
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊkˌtɑp / (AmE), / ˈkʊkˌtɒp / (BrE)
a flat surface with burners or heating elements for cooking food, usually built into a kitchen counter

Examples

Kitchen cooktop

The new apartment features a modern induction cooktop built into the counter.
