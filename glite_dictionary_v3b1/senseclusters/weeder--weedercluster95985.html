weeder

noun
gardening tool for removing weeds
Pronunciation: / ˈwidər / (AmE), / ˈwiːdə / (BrE)
a hand tool used for removing weeds from soil or lawns

Examples

Garden weeder
Every spring, I buy a new weeder to keep my vegetable patch tidy.
She used a weeder to pull out the dandelions from the flower bed.
The new weeder has an ergonomic handle for easier gardening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoe - a garden tool with a long handle and flat blade used for weeding and breaking up soil
