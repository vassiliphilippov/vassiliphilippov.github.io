dazzler

noun
impressive person or thing
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdæz.lər/ (AmE), /ˈdæz.lə/ (BrE)
a person or thing that impresses or attracts attention by being extremely bright, beautiful, skillful, or outstanding

Examples

The new sports car is a dazzler with its sleek design and bright red color.
He wore a suit that made him the dazzler of the evening.
The new smartphone is a real dazzler with its sleek design and advanced features.
She wore a dazzler of a dress that caught everyone's eye at the party.
His performance on stage was a dazzler that left the audience speechless.
