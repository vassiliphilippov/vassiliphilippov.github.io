cannelloni

noun
large stuffed pasta tubes
Pronunciation: / ˌkænəˈloʊni / (AmE), / ˌkanɛˈlɒni / (BrE)
Large tubular pasta stuffed with a filling, typically of meat or cheese, and often baked in sauce.

Examples

Baked cannelloni pasta
For tonight's dinner, I prepared homemade cannelloni stuffed with spinach and ricotta.
She learned to make traditional Italian cannelloni from her grandmother.
The restaurant serves delicious cannelloni baked in a rich tomato sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manicotti - similar tubular pasta, often stuffed and baked, especially in American cuisine
