kludge

noun
awkward, makeshift solution
9.9 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / kl åd í / (AmE), / kluÀêd í / (BrE)
A poorly assembled or inelegant solution, especially one created hastily from available parts to solve a particular problem.

Examples

A messy kludge
Instead of rewiring the whole system, he put together a kludge from spare parts.
That homemade repair is more of a kludge than a permanent solution.
The software update was just a kludge to fix the immediate problem.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elegant solution - a simple, efficient, and effective solution to a problem
  • synonym: hack - an improvised, usually inelegant solution to a technical problem
  • synonym: jury rig - to make or assemble something quickly or temporarily
