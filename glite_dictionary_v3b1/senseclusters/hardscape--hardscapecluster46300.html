hardscape

verb
to build outdoor features
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrd.skeɪp/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːd.skeɪp/ (BrE)
to design, build, or install the non-living features of an outdoor area, such as paths, patios, or walls

Examples

To hardscape a yard
They hired a contractor to hardscape their backyard with a new stone patio and walkway.
Many homeowners choose to hardscape areas of their garden to reduce maintenance.
We plan to hardscape the front yard by adding a brick path and retaining wall.
