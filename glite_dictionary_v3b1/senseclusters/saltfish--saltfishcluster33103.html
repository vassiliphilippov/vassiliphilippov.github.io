saltfish

noun
dried, salted fish
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
fish that has been preserved by drying and salting, often used in traditional dishes

Examples

Dried saltfish pieces
The chef prepared a traditional dish using saltfish and vegetables.
In many Caribbean countries, saltfish is a key ingredient in national recipes.
Before cooking, you should soak saltfish in water to remove excess salt.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bacalao - salted and dried cod, especially in Spanish and Portuguese cuisine
  • antonym: fresh fish - fish that has not been preserved by salting or drying
