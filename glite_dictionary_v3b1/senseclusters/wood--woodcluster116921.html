wood

noun
type of golf club for long shots
4.2
Pronunciation: / wʊd / (AmE), / wʊd / (BrE)
a golf club with a long shaft designed for hitting long-distance shots, originally made with a wooden head but now often made of metal or other materials

Examples

A golf wood
He carried three woods and eight irons in his golf bag.
Many professional golfers prefer to use a wood for their tee shots on long holes.
With the introduction of metal woods, golfers can hit the ball farther than ever before.

Related Terms

  • antonym: iron - a type of golf club with a metal head, used for shorter or more controlled shots
