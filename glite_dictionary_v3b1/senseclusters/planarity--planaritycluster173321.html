planarity

noun
state of being flat
7.9
Pronunciation: / pləˈnærəti / (AmE), / pləˈnærəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being flat or existing in a single plane

Examples

Planarity of the surface
The planarity of the glass is crucial for making high-quality mirrors.
Engineers tested the planarity of the metal sheet before using it in construction.
In mathematics, planarity is important when studying the properties of graphs.
