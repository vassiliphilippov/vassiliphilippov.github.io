bullcrap

noun
slang for nonsense
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlˌkræp / (AmE), / ˈbʊlˌkræp / (BrE)
rude slang for something that is not true, nonsense, or a lie

Examples

A story of bullcrap
He said the whole story was just bullcrap and refused to believe it.
Don't give me that bullcrap; I know what really happened.
She called his excuse bullcrap because it made no sense at all.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are not true or make no sense
  • antonym: truth - something that is true or factual
