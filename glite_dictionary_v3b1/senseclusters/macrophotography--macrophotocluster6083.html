macrophotography

noun
close-up photography
Pronunciation: / ˌmækroʊfəˈtɑːɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌmakrəʊfəˈtɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the practice of taking close-up photographs of small subjects at high magnification

Examples

Macrophotography of insects
She used macrophotography to reveal the intricate patterns on a butterfly's wings.
With macrophotography, even the smallest details of a flower become visible and striking.
Many nature magazines feature stunning macrophotography of insects and plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: microphotography - photography of very small objects, often using a microscope
  • degree lower: photography - the art or practice of taking and processing photographs
