unopposable

adjective
not able to be opposed
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈpoʊzəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈpəʊzəbəl / (BrE)

Examples

The legal case was built on unopposable evidence that could not be disputed.
