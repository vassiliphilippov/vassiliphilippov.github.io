neuroscience

noun
scientific study of the nervous system
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnʊroʊˌsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈnjʊərəʊˌsaɪəns / (BrE)
The scientific study of the nervous system, including its structure, function, development, and diseases.

Examples

Modern neuroscience research
Advances in neuroscience have provided new insights into memory and learning.
She studied neuroscience at university, focusing on brain development in children.
The field of neuroscience combines biology, psychology, and chemistry to understand how the nervous system works.
