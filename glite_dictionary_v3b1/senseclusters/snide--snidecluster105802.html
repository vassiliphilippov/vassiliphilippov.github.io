snide

adjective
contemptuously mocking or disparaging
Pronunciation: / snaɪd / (AmE), / snaɪd / (BrE)
expressing contempt or mocking in an indirect or insincere way; disparaging or slyly insulting

Examples

Snide remark
He spoke in a snide tone that made everyone uncomfortable.
Ignore the snide remarks and focus on your own progress.
She couldn't help making a snide comment about his new haircut.

Related Terms

  • antonym: complimentary - expressing praise or approval
  • synonym: sarcastic - using irony to mock or convey contempt
