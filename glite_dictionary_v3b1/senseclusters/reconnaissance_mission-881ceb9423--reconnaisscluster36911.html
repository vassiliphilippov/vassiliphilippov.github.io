reconnaissance mission

noun
military operation to gather enemy information
2.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɑn.əˌsɑns ˈmɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / rɪˈkɒn.ɪ.səns ˈmɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a military operation undertaken to obtain information about an enemy or potential enemy's position, movements, or operations

Examples

A reconnaissance mission planned
An unexpected storm forced the reconnaissance mission to be postponed until the following day.
During the reconnaissance mission, they mapped the area and took photographs of enemy installations.
The team set out on a reconnaissance mission to gather intelligence behind enemy lines.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: patrol - an expedition to observe or guard an area
  • synonym: reconnoitre - to make a military observation of a region
