sistren

noun
archaic plural of sister
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪstrən / (AmE), / ˈsɪstrən / (BrE)
an archaic plural form of 'sister', now rarely used except in religious or poetic contexts

Examples

The sistren of the convent
The convent was home to many devoted sistren.
In ancient texts, the word sistren was used to refer to groups of women in religious orders.
The sistren gathered in the chapel each morning for prayer.
