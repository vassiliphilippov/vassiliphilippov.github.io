reshare

verb
to share again
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈʃɛr / (AmE), / ˌriːˈʃeə / (BrE)
to share something again, especially by posting or forwarding it on social media or online platforms

Examples

To reshare a post

He chose to reshare the video after it went viral.

Many users reshare important news updates to spread information quickly.
If you like this photo, feel free to reshare it with your followers.
He decided to reshare the funny video with all his friends on social media.
Many users reshared the announcement to help spread the news quickly.
If you like this post, feel free to reshare it with your followers.

  • degree lower: share - to make something available to others, especially online
