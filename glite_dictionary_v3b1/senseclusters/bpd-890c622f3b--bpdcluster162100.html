BPD

noun
business process diagram
7.7 0.32/m
Pronunciation: / bi pi di / (AmE), / biÀê piÀê diÀê / (BrE)
an abbreviation for a business process description or diagram that outlines the steps, sequence, and responsibilities in a company's process

Examples

Company BPD diagram
The manager asked the team to update the BPD after the new software was installed.
A clear BPD helps everyone understand their roles in the project.
Before starting the process, they reviewed the BPD to ensure all steps were included.
