extraterritorially

adverb
beyond national borders
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrətɛˈrɪtɔːriəli / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrətɛˈrɪtəriəli / (BrE)
in a manner that applies or operates beyond the borders of a particular country.

Examples

Applied extraterritorially
The new regulations will be enforced extraterritorially to ensure compliance by foreign companies.
Some countries claim the right to act extraterritorially in cases involving their citizens abroad.
The court ruled that the law could not be applied extraterritorially without clear authorization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestically - within a particular country
  • synonym: internationally - involving or relating to two or more countries
