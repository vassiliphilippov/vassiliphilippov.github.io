sinus ethmoidales

noun
paranasal sinus in the ethmoid bone
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪnəs ɛθˌmɔɪˈdeɪliz / (AmE), / ˌsaɪnəs ɛθˌmɔɪˈdɑːliːz / (BrE)
One of the paranasal sinuses located within the ethmoid bone, behind the bridge of the nose.

Examples

Infected sinus ethmoidales
A CT scan revealed infection in both the left and right sinus ethmoidales.
Inflammation of the sinus ethmoidales can cause pain between the eyes and nasal congestion.
The ethmoid bone contains several small air cells forming the sinus ethmoidales.
