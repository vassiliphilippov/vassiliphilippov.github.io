multichannel

adjective
using multiple audio channels
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəl / (AmE), / ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəl / (BrE)
relating to recording, transmitting, or reproducing sound using more than two separate audio channels, such as in surround sound systems

Examples

Multichannel audio system
The new home theater system supports multichannel audio for a more immersive experience.
Engineers tested the multichannel recording to ensure each speaker produced the correct sound.
Many movies now offer multichannel soundtracks to enhance the viewing experience.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: stereo - using two audio channels for sound reproduction
  • synonym: surround sound - a system using multiple audio channels to create immersive sound
