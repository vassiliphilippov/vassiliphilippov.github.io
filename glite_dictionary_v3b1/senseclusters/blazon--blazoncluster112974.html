blazon

verb
to adorn with heraldic arms or display prominently
Pronunciation: / ˈbleɪ.zən / (AmE), / ˈbleɪ.zən / (BrE)
to decorate or display (a surface) with heraldic arms, symbols, or designs; to depict or exhibit prominently

Examples

To blazon a shield
Ancient banners were blazoned with family coats of arms and symbols.
The artist spent days carefully blazoning the emblems onto the knight’s armor.
The king ordered his shield to be blazoned with the royal crest.
