ambrosian

adjective
extremely pleasing; sweet and fragrant
9.6
Pronunciation: / æmˈbroʊʒən /; / æmˈbroʊziən / (AmE), / æmˈbrəʊziən /; / amˈbrəʊziən / (BrE)
extremely pleasing to the taste or smell; delicious and fragrant, like the food of the gods

The chef prepared an ambrosian feast that delighted every guest.
