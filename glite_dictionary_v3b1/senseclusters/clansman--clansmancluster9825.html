clansman

noun
male member of a clan
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˈklænzmən / (AmE), / ˈklænzmən / (BrE)
A male member of a clan, especially in Scottish or Irish contexts.

Examples

A clansman in tartan
As a proud clansman, he wore the traditional tartan to the gathering.
Every clansman was expected to defend the territory fiercely if threatened.
The chief called his most loyal clansman to discuss their family legacy.
