obesity

noun
condition of excessive body fat
3.9 15/m
Pronunciation: / o äÀàbis…ôti / (AmE), / …ô äÀàbiÀês…™ti / (BrE)
The condition of having an excessive amount of body fat, often considered unhealthy and associated with various health risks.

Examples

Childhood obesity
Childhood obesity is becoming a serious public health concern worldwide.
He was advised to lose weight to reduce his risk of obesity-related illnesses.
The study measured the correlation between diet and obesity in adults.

Synonyms

  • corpulence
    8.7 0.04/m
    corpulence is a somewhat old-fashioned word for being very overweight, while obesity is the medical condition tied to health risks.
  • adiposity
    9.1 0.09/m
    adiposity refers to the amount of body fat measured clinically, while obesity is the unhealthy excess of fat with health risks.
  • fatness
    1.0 0.60/m
    fatness simply means having extra body fat in everyday speech, while obesity is a diagnosed, often unhealthy, medical condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leanness - the quality of being lean or having little body fat
  • degree lower: overweight - having more body weight than is considered normal or healthy
