preauricular

adjective
in front of ear
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːɔːˈrɪkjələr / (AmE), / ˌpriːɔːˈrɪkjʊlə / (BrE)
located in front of the external part of the ear

Examples

The preauricular area
The doctor examined the preauricular area for signs of swelling.
A small lump was found in the preauricular region during the checkup.
Pain in the preauricular area can indicate an infection or inflammation.

Related Terms
