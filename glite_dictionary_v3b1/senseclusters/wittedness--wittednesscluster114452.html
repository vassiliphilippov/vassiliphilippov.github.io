wittedness

noun
state of alertness
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪtɪdnəs / (BrE)
the state of being conscious, alert, or of sound mind

Examples

Full wittedness
After the accident he slowly returned to full wittedness and answered questions coherently.
The medication helped restore her wittedness after a long period of confusion.
He prided himself on his sharp wittedness even in stressful situations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: consciousness - the state of being awake and aware
  • antonym: insanity - the state of being seriously mentally ill
  • synonym: sanity - the condition of having a healthy mind
