inkjet

noun
type of printer
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnkˌdʒɛt /; / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
a type of printer that creates images or text by spraying tiny drops of ink onto paper

Examples

A color inkjet
The office purchased a new inkjet to print high-quality color photos.
Many students use an inkjet at home for their school assignments.
Replacing the cartridges in an inkjet can be more expensive than the printer itself.
The office bought a new inkjet to replace the old printer.
Photos printed on an inkjet often have vibrant colors and sharp details.
She prefers using an inkjet for her art projects because of the color quality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: laser printer - a printer that uses a laser to produce images on paper
  • synonym: printer - a machine that produces text or images on paper
