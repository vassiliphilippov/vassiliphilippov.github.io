promotability

noun
potential for promotion
6.4
Pronunciation: / prəˌmoʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / prəˌməʊtəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the likelihood or potential that someone will be promoted to a higher position at work

Examples

Employee promotability
Her excellent teamwork skills improved her promotability in the organization.
Managers often consider promotability when deciding who should take on new responsibilities.
Regular training can increase an employee's promotability over time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advancement - progress to a higher position or level
  • synonym: promotion - the act of being raised to a higher position
  • antonym: stagnation - lack of progress or advancement
