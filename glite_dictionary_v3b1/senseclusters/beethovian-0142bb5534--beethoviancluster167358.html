Beethovian

noun
Beethoven admirer
8.8
Pronunciation: / biːˈtoʊviən / (AmE), / biːˈtəʊviən / (BrE)
a person who admires, studies, or strongly favors Beethoven or his musical style

Examples

A true Beethovian

The music club attracted many Beethovians who discussed Beethoven's influence on classical music.
He considered himself a true Beethovian and could recite facts about the composer's life from memory.
