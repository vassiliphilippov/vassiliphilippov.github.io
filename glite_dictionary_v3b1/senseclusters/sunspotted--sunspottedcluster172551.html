sunspotted

adjective
dappled with sunlight
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌnˌspɑtɪd / (AmE), / ˈsʌnˌspɒtɪd / (BrE)
marked with patches of sunlight, especially where light filters through openings and creates a dappled effect

Examples

Sunspotted garden bench
The old bench sat in a sunspotted corner of the garden, surrounded by blooming flowers.
Children played on the sunspotted grass beneath the tall oak trees.
She loved reading in the sunspotted room where light streamed through the window blinds.
