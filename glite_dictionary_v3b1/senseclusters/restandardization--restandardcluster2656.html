restandardization

noun
adjusting to standard again
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˌstændərdaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːˌstændədaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adjusting, recalibrating, or rescaling something again so that it meets an established or new standard

Examples

Restandardization of equipment
The laboratory performed a complete restandardization of its equipment after the new guidelines were released.
Annual restandardization ensures that all measuring tools provide accurate and reliable results.
Following the software update, a restandardization of procedures was necessary to maintain quality control.
The team performed restandardization of the test scores to ensure fair comparisons between groups.
After changes in the curriculum, restandardization of assessment tools became necessary.
The data required restandardization before it could be analyzed across different years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calibration - the process of adjusting the accuracy of a device or instrument
  • synonym: normalization - the process of adjusting values to a common scale
  • synonym: standardization - the process of making things conform to a standard
