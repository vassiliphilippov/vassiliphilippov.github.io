genus Tribolium

noun
a genus of flour beetles
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs traɪˈboʊliəm / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs trɪˈbəʊlɪəm / (BrE)
A genus of small beetles, commonly known as flour beetles, that infest and feed on stored grain and flour products.

Examples

Genus Tribolium beetles
Researchers study the genus Tribolium to better understand the evolution of insecticide resistance.
The genus Tribolium includes several species that are notorious pests in grain storage facilities.
The sawtoothed grain beetle is often mistaken for a member of the genus Tribolium.
