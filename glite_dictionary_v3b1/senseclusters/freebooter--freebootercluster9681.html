freebooter

noun
someone who plunders or pirates
6.1 8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfriˌbutər / (AmE), / ˈfriːˌbuːtə / (BrE)
a person who engages in plundering or taking spoils, especially during war; a pirate or adventurer

Examples

Freebooters plunder villages
During the chaos of the war, freebooters roamed the countryside in search of valuables.
He was regarded as a daring freebooter rather than a traditional soldier.
The ship was attacked and looted by a notorious freebooter off the coast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buccaneer - a pirate, especially one in the Caribbean in the 17th century
  • synonym: pirate - a person who attacks and robs ships at sea
  • synonym: privateer - a privately owned armed ship authorized to engage in warfare
