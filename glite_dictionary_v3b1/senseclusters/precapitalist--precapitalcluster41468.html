precapitalist

noun
society before capitalism
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈkæpɪtəlɪst / (BrE)
a society, system, or period that existed before the development of capitalism

Examples

Precapitalist period
Historians often compare precapitalist societies to those that developed after the rise of capitalism.
The village maintained many precapitalist traditions, including communal farming and shared resources.
Anthropologists study precapitalist economies to understand how people lived before modern markets.

Related Terms

  • antonym: capitalist - a person or system based on private ownership and profit
  • synonym: feudalism - a social system before capitalism based on land ownership and service
