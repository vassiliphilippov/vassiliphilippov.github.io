sultan

noun
Muslim sovereign ruler
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌl.tən / (AmE), / ˈsʌl.tən / (BrE)
The sovereign ruler of a Muslim country, especially the former Ottoman Empire.

Examples

The sultan of Ottoman Empire
Many historians study the art and culture from the time of the Ottoman sultans.
The sultan ruled over vast territories for decades.
The sultan welcomed foreign diplomats with elaborate ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: king - male sovereign of a nation or territory
  • degree lower: sultana - a female sultan or wife/widow of a sultan
