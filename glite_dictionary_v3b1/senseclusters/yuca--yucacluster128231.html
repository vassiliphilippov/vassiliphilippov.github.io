yuca

noun
tropical starchy root
3.5 0.13/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàjuÀêk…ô / (AmE), / ÀàjuÀêk…ô / (BrE)
a tropical plant with starchy roots, also known as cassava, used as a food source in many countries

Examples

Fried yuca roots
She made fries from yuca instead of potatoes for a unique flavor.
In many Latin American countries, yuca is a staple food prepared in various ways.
The market sells fresh yuca roots alongside other tropical vegetables.

Synonyms

  • cassava
    6.3 0.83/m
    cassava is the common English name for the tropical plant with starchy roots used as food, while yuca is used as another regional name.
  • manioc
    7.5 0.40/m
    manioc is another common name for the same tropical plant whose starchy root feeds many people, while yuca is a Spanish name.
  • Manihot esculenta
    7.5 0.00/m
