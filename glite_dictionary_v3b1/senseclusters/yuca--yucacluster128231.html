yuca

noun
tropical starchy root
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːkə / (AmE), / ˈjuːkə / (BrE)
a tropical plant with starchy roots, also known as cassava, used as a food source in many countries

Examples

Fried yuca roots
She made fries from yuca instead of potatoes for a unique flavor.
In many Latin American countries, yuca is a staple food prepared in various ways.
The market sells fresh yuca roots alongside other tropical vegetables.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cassava - a tropical plant with edible starchy roots
  • degree lower: potato - a common starchy root vegetable
  • degree lower: yam - an edible starchy tuber from a tropical plant
