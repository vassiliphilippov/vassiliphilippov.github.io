production

noun
creation of goods or services for value
Pronunciation: / prəˈdʌkʃən /; / prəˈdʌk.ʃən / (AmE), / prəˈdʌkʃən /; / prəˈdʌk.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of making or creating goods, materials, or services, especially on a large scale
