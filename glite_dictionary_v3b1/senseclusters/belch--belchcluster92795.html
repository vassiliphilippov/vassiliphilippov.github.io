belch

verb
send out lava or rocks forcefully (volcano)

(of a volcano or chimney) to send out large amounts of something, especially smoke, fire, lava, or rocks, suddenly and forcefully.

Examples

Chimneys belch smoke
Clouds of steam belched from the crater after the seismic activity increased.
During the eruption, the mountain belched rocks and ash for several hours.
The old volcano began to belch lava and smoke into the night sky.
