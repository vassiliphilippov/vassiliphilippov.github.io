misfire

noun
failure of an explosion as expected
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪsˌfaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪsˌfaɪə / (BrE)
an instance of an explosion—especially in a gun or engine—that fails to occur as intended

Examples

A gun misfire
After the misfire, the technician carefully checked the rocket's ignition system.
Fortunately, the grenade was a misfire and did not explode.
The old rifle had a misfire during the demonstration, alarming the onlookers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dud - an explosive device that fails to detonate
