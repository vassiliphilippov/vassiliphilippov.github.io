saddle

verb
assign a burdensome responsibility
6.1 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈsæ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈsæ.dəl/ (BrE)

Examples

She felt saddled by expectations she could never meet.
The new manager didn't want to saddle his team with unnecessary paperwork.
