soy milk

noun
milk made from soybeans
Pronunciation: / sɔɪ mɪlk / (AmE), / sɔɪ mɪlk / (BrE)
A plant-based milk made from soybeans, commonly used as a substitute for dairy milk.

Examples

A glass of soy milk
I usually drink soy milk with my cereal every morning.
Many people choose soy milk because they are lactose intolerant.
The café offers both soy milk and almond milk as alternatives to regular milk.

Related Terms

  • synonym: almond milk - plant-based milk made from almonds
  • antonym: dairy milk - milk produced by mammals, usually cows
