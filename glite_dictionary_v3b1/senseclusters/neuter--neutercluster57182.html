neuter

adjective
lacking functional sex organs (biology)
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtər / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtə / (BrE)
(of an organism or animal) having no, nonfunctional, or imperfectly developed sex organs.

Examples

Neuter flowers
Certain flowers are classified as neuter because they lack both male and female organs.
In some ant species, the worker caste is neuter and cannot reproduce.
The neuter animals in the colony serve different functions than the reproductively active individuals.
