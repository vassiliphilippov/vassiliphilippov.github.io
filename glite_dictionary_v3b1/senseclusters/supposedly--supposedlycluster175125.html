supposedly

adverb
as is commonly thought or believed
3.3 2.9
Pronunciation: /sə.ˈpoʊ.zɪd.liː/ /; / /sə.ˈpoʊ.zəd.liː/ (AmE), /sə.ˈpəʊ.zɪd.liː/ /; / /sə.ˈpəʊ.zəd.liː/ (BrE)

Examples
