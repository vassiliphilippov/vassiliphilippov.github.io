supposedly

adverb
as is commonly thought or believed
Pronunciation: / səˈpoʊzɪdli / (AmE), / səˈpəʊzɪdli / (BrE)
according to what is generally believed or claimed, although not certain or proven

Examples

He is supposedly working
He is supposedly working late tonight, but I haven't seen him all day.
The treasure was supposedly buried under the old oak tree.
This restaurant is supposedly the best in town, though I've had better meals elsewhere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allegedly - according to what has been asserted but not proved
  • antonym: certainly - without doubt; definitely
