clamminess

noun
unpleasant dampness or moisture
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈklæmi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈklæmɪnɪs / (BrE)
an unpleasant, damp and sticky feeling, often referring to the skin

Examples

The clamminess of his hands made her uneasy during the handshake.
