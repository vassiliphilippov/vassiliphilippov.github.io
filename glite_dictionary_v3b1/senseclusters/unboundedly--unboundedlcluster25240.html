unboundedly

adverb
without limits
7.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbaʊndɪdli / (AmE), / ʌnˈbaʊndɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that has no limits or boundaries; without any upper limit

Examples

To be unboundedly grateful
He was unboundedly grateful for the support he received from his friends.
The children were unboundedly excited about the upcoming holiday trip.
Her creativity seemed to flow unboundedly when she worked on her art.
The function increases unboundedly as the input approaches infinity.
In theory, the value can grow unboundedly if no restrictions are applied.
The population would expand unboundedly in the absence of natural limits.
