garni

adjective
with a garnish
Pronunciation: / ɡɑrˈni / (AmE), / ɡɑːˈniː / (BrE)
served or decorated with a garnish, especially in the style of French cuisine

Examples

Salmon garni
The restaurant served salmon garni with lemon slices and fresh dill.
Her plate arrived beautifully garni with colorful vegetables and herbs.
He ordered steak garni to enjoy the extra flavors from the garnish.

  • antonym: plain - without any decoration or garnish
