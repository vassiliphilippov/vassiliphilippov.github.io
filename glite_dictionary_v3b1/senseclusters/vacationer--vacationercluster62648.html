vacationer

noun
person on vacation
Pronunciation: / vəˈkeɪʃənər / (AmE), / vəˈkeɪʃənə / (BrE)
a person who is spending time away from home for pleasure or relaxation, especially during holidays

Examples

A vacationer on holiday
As a vacationer, she enjoyed exploring new cities at her own pace.
Many vacationers flock to the beach during the summer months.
The hotel offers special packages for vacationers seeking a relaxing getaway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: traveler - a person who is traveling or who travels often
  • antonym: worker - a person who works, especially as part of their occupation
