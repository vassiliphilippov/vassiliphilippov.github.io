Ionian

adjective
of the Ionian region
Pronunciation: / aɪˈoʊniən / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to the Ionian Sea, the surrounding region, or its people in ancient Greece

Examples

Ionian islands
The Ionian islands are famous for their clear blue waters and scenic landscapes.
Many ancient philosophers came from Ionian cities along the coast.
The Ionian culture played a significant role in the development of Greek art and science.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aegean - relating to the Aegean Sea or region in Greece
