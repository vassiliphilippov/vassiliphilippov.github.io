sidebar

noun
supplementary news story
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdˌbɑr / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdˌbɑː / (BrE)
A short news story that provides additional or supplementary information related to a major story.

Examples

A news sidebar
A sidebar on the front page explained the origins of the ongoing conflict.
Reporters sometimes write a sidebar to highlight personal stories related to the main article.
The newspaper included a sidebar describing the background of the main event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feature - a special or prominent article in a newspaper or magazine
