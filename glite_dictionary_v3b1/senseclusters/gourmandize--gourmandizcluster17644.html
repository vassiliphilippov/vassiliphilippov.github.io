gourmandize

verb
to eat excessively or greedily
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʊrməndaɪz / (AmE), / ˈɡʊəməndʌɪz / (BrE)
to eat excessively or with great greed; to overindulge in food

Examples

To gourmandize food greedily
After a week of strict dieting, she couldn't resist gourmandizing at the buffet.
He would often gourmandize at holiday feasts, sampling every dish several times.
They gourmandized on desserts until they could barely move.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fast - to abstain from all or some kinds of food or drink
  • synonym: gluttonize - to eat to excess; to be gluttonous
  • synonym: overeat - to eat more food than necessary
