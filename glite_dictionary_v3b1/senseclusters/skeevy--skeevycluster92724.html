skeevy

adjective
unpleasant or sleazy
Pronunciation: / ˈskiːvi / (AmE), / ˈskiːvi / (BrE)
unpleasant, sleazy, or making you feel uncomfortable, often in a way that seems dirty or suspicious

Examples

A skeevy motel
The old motel looked so skeevy that we decided to find another place to stay.
He avoided the skeevy alleyway on his way home at night.
There was something skeevy about the way the salesman kept glancing around.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clean - free from dirt or unpleasantness
  • synonym: creepy - causing an unpleasant feeling of fear or unease
  • synonym: sleazy - morally low or dirty in appearance
