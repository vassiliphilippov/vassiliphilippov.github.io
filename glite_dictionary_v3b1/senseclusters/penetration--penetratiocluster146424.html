penetration

noun
attack entering enemy territory
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌpɛnəˈtreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɛnɪˈtreɪʃən / (BrE)
a military attack that breaks through an enemy's defenses and enters their territory

Examples

Enemy penetration of defenses
Reports described a deep penetration into hostile areas by the special forces unit.
Successful penetration of enemy territory requires careful planning and coordination.
The army's unexpected penetration of the frontline changed the course of the battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: incursion - a sudden attack or invasion into another's territory
