persona non grata

noun
an unwelcome or unacceptable person
8.2 7.5
Pronunciation: /pər.ˈsoʊ.nə nɑːn ˈgrɑː.tə/ (AmE), /pə.ˈsəʊ.nə nɒn ˈgrɑː.tə/ /; / /pɜː.ˈsəʊ.nə nɒn ˈgrɑː.tə/ (BrE)
a person who is not welcome or is unacceptable in a particular place or group, especially for official or diplomatic reasons
