iodophor

noun
iodine disinfectant
Pronunciation: / aɪˈɑdəˌfɔr / (AmE), / aɪˈəʊdəˌfɔː / (BrE)
a disinfectant solution in which iodine is combined with a solubilizing agent, used to sterilize surfaces or equipment

Examples

An iodophor solution
The nurse applied an iodophor to the patient's skin before the injection.
All surgical instruments were soaked in an iodophor solution for sterilization.
Using an iodophor helps reduce the risk of infection in medical settings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antiseptic - a substance that prevents infection by killing microorganisms
  • synonym: disinfectant - a chemical used to destroy bacteria and viruses on surfaces
