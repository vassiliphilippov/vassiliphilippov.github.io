overcompliant

adjective
excessively obedient
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkəmˈplaɪənt / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkəmˈplaɪənt / (BrE)
too willing to obey rules or follow others' wishes, often excessively so

Examples

Overcompliant employee behavior
The manager found the overcompliant employee rarely offered new ideas or suggestions.
Parents sometimes worry if their child is overcompliant and never speaks up for themselves.
Her overcompliant attitude made her easy to take advantage of in group projects.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiant - openly resisting authority or rules
  • synonym: submissive - willing to obey or yield to others
