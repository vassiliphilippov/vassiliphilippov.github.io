barnacle

verb
to become encrusted
8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbɑːr.nə.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.nə.kəl/ (BrE)
to become covered with barnacles or to develop a hard crust of barnacles on a surface, especially in water.

Examples

Ship barnacled over time
The old ship had barnacled after years of sitting unused in the harbor.
If left in the water too long, the boat's hull will quickly barnacle and slow down.
Divers found that the underwater pipes had completely barnacled over the winter.
