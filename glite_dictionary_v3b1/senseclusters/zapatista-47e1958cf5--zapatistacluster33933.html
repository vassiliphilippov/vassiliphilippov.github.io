Zapatista

adjective
of the Zapatistas
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˌzɑːpəˈtistə / (AmE), / ˌzapəˈtiːstə / (BrE)
relating to the Zapatistas, their movement, or their political ideas and actions, especially in Mexico.

Examples

Zapatista movement
The Zapatista uprising in Chiapas drew international attention to indigenous rights.
She wore a Zapatista scarf to show her support for the movement.
Many activists are inspired by Zapatista principles of autonomy and social justice.

Related Terms

  • synonym: revolutionary - relating to or supporting political revolution
