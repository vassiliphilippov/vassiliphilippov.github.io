scholarliness

noun
academic quality
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑːlərlinəs / (AmE), / ˈskɒləlɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being scholarly, showing academic knowledge, learning, or research ability

Examples

Academic scholarliness
Her scholarliness was evident in the detailed research she presented at the seminar.
The committee admired his scholarliness and dedication to academic excellence.
A high level of scholarliness is required to publish in that scientific journal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erudition - deep, extensive learning or knowledge
  • antonym: ignorance - lack of knowledge or education
