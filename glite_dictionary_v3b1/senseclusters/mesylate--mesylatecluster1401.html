mesylate

verb
to add mesyl group
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛsɪˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈmɛsɪˌleɪt / (BrE)
to convert a compound, usually an alcohol, into its mesylate derivative by introducing a methanesulfonyl group.

Examples

To mesylate an alcohol
The chemist decided to mesylate the alcohol to improve its leaving group for substitution.
Before the next reaction step, you should mesylate the compound to increase its reactivity.
They mesylated the intermediate to facilitate the nucleophilic substitution process.
