Chemnitzer concertina

noun
a hand-held European concertina instrument
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkem.nɪt.sər kɑːn.sər.ˈtiː.nə/ (AmE), /ˈkem.nɪt.sə kɒn.sə.ˈtiː.nə/ (BrE)
a type of hand-held, bellows-driven free-reed musical instrument similar to an accordion, originating from Chemnitz, Germany, and characterized by its square shape and distinct button layout.
