Dolichos lablab

noun
tropical vine grown for edible seeds and ornament
9.9
Pronunciation: / doʊˈlaɪkəs læˈblæb / (AmE), / dəˈlɪkəs ˈlæblæb / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant of the Old World tropics, with trifoliate leaves and racemes of fragrant purple flowers, followed by maroon pods containing edible seeds, cultivated as an ornamental and a vegetable, especially in the Indian subcontinent; also known as hyacinth bean.
