monogenean

adjective
of Monogenea flatworms
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑnəˈdʒiːniən / (AmE), / ˌmɒnəˈdʒiːniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of Monogenea, a class of parasitic flatworms that typically infect fish.

Examples

Monogenean parasite species
The scientist identified several monogenean species attached to the gills of the fish.
A monogenean infection can cause significant health problems in aquaculture environments.
Researchers are developing treatments to control monogenean parasites in commercial fisheries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: free-living - not living as a parasite; independent
  • synonym: parasitic - living as a parasite on another organism
