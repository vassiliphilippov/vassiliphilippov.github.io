stage manager

noun
person in charge of a theatre stage during a show
1.0 3.7
Pronunciation: /steɪdʒ ˈmæn.ə.dʒər/ /; / /steɪdʒ ˈmæn.ɪ.dʒər/ (AmE), /steɪdʒ ˈmæn.ɪ.dʒə/ (BrE)
a person who supervises the physical aspects of a theatrical production and is responsible for managing the stage during performances

Examples

Stage manager at theatre
As stage manager, she kept everyone on schedule and solved problems quickly.
During the performance, the stage manager communicated cues to the lighting and sound teams.
The stage manager coordinated every scene change and ensured the props were ready.
