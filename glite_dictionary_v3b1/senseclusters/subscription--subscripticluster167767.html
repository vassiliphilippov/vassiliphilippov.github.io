subscription

noun
recurring paid access to digital services
2.9
Pronunciation: /səb.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (AmE), /səb.ˈskrɪp.ʃən/ (BrE)
a recurring payment or arrangement that gives a person access to digital content or an online service (for example, streaming media, software, or cloud storage).

Examples

Subscription to a service
She renewed her subscription to the streaming platform for another year.
The company's new app offers a weekly trial before users choose a subscription plan.
Many people cancel a subscription when the monthly fee becomes too expensive.

Related Terms

  • synonym: membership - the state of belonging to a group or organization, often involving periodic payment or access rights
