rookie

noun
inexperienced newcomer; beginner
3.7 12/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆrÊŠki / (AmE), / ËˆrÊŠki / (BrE)
a person who is new to an activity, job, or organization and lacks experience; a beginner

Examples

A rookie player
As a rookie reporter, she still had a lot to learn about interviewing sources.
He made the typical mistakes of a rookie in his first month on the job.
The team relies on seasoned players to mentor the rookie during his first season.
