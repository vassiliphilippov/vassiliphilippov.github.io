ostentatiousness

noun
quality of being showy or pretentious
8.7 9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌɒstənˈteɪʃəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being showy or pretentious, often in an attempt to impress others; excessive display or vanity

Examples

The ostentatiousness of style
Despite his wealth, there was little ostentatiousness in the way he lived his life.
He was criticized for the ostentatiousness of his expensive jewelry and flashy cars.
The ostentatiousness of the wedding decor impressed some guests but embarrassed others.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modesty - lack of vanity or boastfulness; simplicity in behavior or appearance
  • synonym: pretentiousness - the quality of trying to appear more important or clever than one is
