lapillus

noun
small stone
Pronunciation: / ləˈpɪləs / (AmE), / ləˈpɪləs / (BrE)
a small stone or pebble, especially a tiny naturally occurring rock fragment

Examples

A river lapillus
She picked up a shiny lapillus from the riverbank and slipped it into her pocket.
The geologist examined each lapillus to learn more about the area's history.
Children often collect a lapillus or two while playing near the lake.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boulder - a large rock, much bigger than a pebble
  • synonym: pebble - a small, rounded stone
