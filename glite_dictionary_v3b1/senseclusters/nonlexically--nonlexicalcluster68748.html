nonlexically

adverb
without using words
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɛksɪkli / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɛksɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that does not involve the use of words; without verbal expression.

Examples

To communicate nonlexically
Some messages are best conveyed nonlexically, relying on body language and tone of voice.
The anger was expressed nonlexically, without uttering a single word.
The speaker communicated her feelings nonlexically, through gestures and facial expressions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lexically - using or relating to words or vocabulary
