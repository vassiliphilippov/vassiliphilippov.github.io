bowlful

noun
amount that fills a bowl
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊlˌfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbəʊl.fʊl / (BrE)
An amount of something that fills a bowl.

Examples

A bowlful of soup
He ate a bowlful of soup before leaving for work.
She measured out a bowlful of rice for the recipe.
There was only one bowlful of berries left after the picnic.
