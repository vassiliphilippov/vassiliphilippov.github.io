Sidonian

noun
person from Sidon
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈdoʊniən / (AmE), / saɪˈdəʊniən / (BrE)
a person who lives in or comes from Sidon, especially a member of the ancient Phoenician people of Sidon

Examples

An ancient Sidonian
The ancient Sidonian merchants were famous for their purple dye.
A skilled Sidonian craftsman created the intricate glassware displayed in the museum.
Legends tell of a brave Sidonian sailor who explored distant lands.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Phoenician - a member of the ancient Semitic people of Phoenicia
