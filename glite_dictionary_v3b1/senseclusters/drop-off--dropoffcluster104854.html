drop-off

noun
designated unloading place
2.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɑːp.ɔːf/ /; / /ˈdrɔːp.ɔːf/ (AmE), /ˈdrɒp.ɒf/ /; / /ˈdrɒp.ɔːf/ (BrE)
a place or area where people or things are left, delivered, or unloaded

Examples

Drop-off point
The mall's drop-off zone is clearly marked near the bus stop.
Please leave the fresh donations at the community center's drop-off desk before noon.
Parents should use the school drop-off lane to avoid blocking the main entrance during pickup.
Taxis wait at the airport drop-off area while passengers collect their luggage.
Local residents collected parcels left at the building's drop-off point during the holiday rush.
The school organizes a morning drop-off lane to keep traffic moving and children safe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drop off (phrasal verb) - to deliver someone or something to a place and leave them there
  • synonym: drop-off point - a specific location where passengers or items are dropped off for pickup.
  • synonym: drop-off zone - a marked area (often at airports, schools, or stores) for leaving passengers or deliveries.
  • antonym: pick up - to collect someone or something from a place (opposite action)
