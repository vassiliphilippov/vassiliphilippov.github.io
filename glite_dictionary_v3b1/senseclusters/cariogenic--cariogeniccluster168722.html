cariogenic

adjective
causing tooth decay
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkæriəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkæriəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
causing or promoting the development of dental cavities

Examples

Cariogenic foods
Dentists warn that frequent consumption of cariogenic foods can increase the risk of cavities.
A diet low in cariogenic substances helps maintain healthy teeth.
Researchers study which bacteria are most cariogenic in the human mouth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anticariogenic - preventing the formation of dental cavities
  • synonym: decay-causing - leading to the breakdown or destruction of teeth
