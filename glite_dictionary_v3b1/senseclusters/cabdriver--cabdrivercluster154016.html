cabdriver

noun
taxi driver
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkæbˌdraɪvɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæbˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to drive a taxi and carry passengers to their destinations

Examples

The cabdriver drives passengers
During rush hour, the cabdriver skillfully navigated through heavy traffic.
I tipped the cabdriver extra for helping me with my luggage.
The cabdriver knew the city streets like the back of his hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taxi driver - a person who drives a taxi for hire
