sum

noun
the essential or most important part
5.6
Pronunciation: / sʌm / (AmE), / sʌm / (BrE)
the most important or essential part of an idea, argument, or experience; the essence.

Examples

The sum of the idea
After hours of discussion, she finally got to the sum of her concerns.
The sum of the matter is that we must act now before it's too late.
What he said was interesting, but the sum of his argument was quite simple.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detail - an individual feature, fact, or item
  • synonym: essence - the intrinsic nature or indispensable quality
  • synonym: gist - the essential or main part of a matter
