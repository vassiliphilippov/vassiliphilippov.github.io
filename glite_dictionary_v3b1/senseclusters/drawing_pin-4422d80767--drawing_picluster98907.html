drawing pin

noun
short pin for fastening papers
2.5 2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɔɪ.ɪŋ pɪn / (AmE), / ˈdrɔːɪŋ pɪn / (BrE)
A short pin with a sharp point and a flat head, used for fastening papers to a wall, board, or similar surface.

Examples
