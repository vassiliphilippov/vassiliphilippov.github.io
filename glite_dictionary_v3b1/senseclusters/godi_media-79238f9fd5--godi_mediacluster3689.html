Godi media

noun
Indian term for pro-government media
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊdi ˈmiːdiə / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊdi ˈmiːdiə / (BrE)
news organizations that are perceived as uncritically supportive of the government, especially in India

Examples

Godi media support government
Activists argue that Godi media damages journalistic integrity by not holding authorities accountable.
Many critics accuse Godi media of failing to question the government's policies.
Social media users often use the term Godi media to describe biased news coverage.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independent media - media outlets not influenced by government or corporate interests
  • synonym: mainstream media - traditional and widely distributed news outlets
