foreverness

noun
state of being eternal
8.3
Pronunciation: / fəˈrɛvərnəs / (AmE), / fəˈrɛvənɪs /; / fəˈrɛvənəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of lasting forever or seeming to last forever

Related Terms

  • synonym: eternity - endless or infinite time; the state of being eternal
  • antonym: momentariness - the quality of lasting for only a very short time
  • synonym: permanence - the state of lasting or remaining unchanged indefinitely
  • synonym: perpetuity - the state of lasting forever or for an indefinite period
  • antonym: temporariness - the state of lasting for only a limited period
