nebulosity

noun
vagueness or obscurity
9.9
Pronunciation: /nə.bjuː.ˈlɑː.sə.tiː/ /; / /ne.bjʊ.ˈlɑː.sɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /nə.bjʊ.ˈlɒ.sɪ.tiː/ /; / /ne.bjʊ.ˈlɒ.sɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples

Nebulosity in ideas

Her argument was weakened by the nebulosity of her main points.
