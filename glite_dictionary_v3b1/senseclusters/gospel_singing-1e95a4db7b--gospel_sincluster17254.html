gospel singing

noun
African-American style of spiritual singing
5.1 2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑspəl ˌsɪŋɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡɒspəl ˌsɪŋɪŋ / (BrE)
A style of vocal music rooted in African-American spirituals, often performed a cappella with call and response, influential in the development of soul and other genres.

Examples

Gospel singing style
Many contemporary artists credit gospel singing with shaping their musical style.
Sam grew up listening to gospel singing at family gatherings every Sunday.
The choir's spirited gospel singing moved everyone in the packed church.
