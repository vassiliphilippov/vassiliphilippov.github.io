annulet

noun
heraldic symbol shaped like a small ring
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.jə.lət / (AmE), / ˈan.jʊ.lɪt / (BrE)
(Heraldry) A charge or emblem in the shape of a small ring, used as a symbol on a coat of arms.

Examples

Heraldic annulet
An annulet is often used to distinguish cadet branches in heraldic traditions.
The crest was adorned with a golden annulet symbolizing fidelity.
The family's coat of arms displays an annulet in the center shield.
