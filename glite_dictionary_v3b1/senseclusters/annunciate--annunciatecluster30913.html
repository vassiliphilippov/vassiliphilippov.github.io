annunciate

verb
foreshadow or presage
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈnʌnsiˌeɪt / (AmE), / əˈnʌn.sɪ.eɪt / (BrE)
To indicate, foreshadow, or presage a future event.

Examples

To annunciate a change
In her novel, subtle hints annunciate the tragic ending.
The change in tone annunciated the beginning of a new era.
The darkening clouds annunciated the coming storm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: foreshadow - to indicate or suggest beforehand
  • synonym: presage - to be a sign or warning of a future event
