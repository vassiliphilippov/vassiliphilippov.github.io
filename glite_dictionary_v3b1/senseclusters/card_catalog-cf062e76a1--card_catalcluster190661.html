card catalog

noun
library index with cards for items
Pronunciation: / kɑrd ˈkæt̬.əl.ɔg /; / kɑrd ˈkætəlˌɔg /; / kɑrd ˈkætəlɔg / (AmE), / kɑːd ˈkæt.əl.ɒg /; / kɑːd ˈkatəlɒg / (BrE)
a set of cards, usually in drawers, used in libraries to list and organize books or other items, with one card for each item

Related Terms

  • synonym: card index - a system of recording information on index cards, often used as a synonym for card catalogue
  • synonym: catalogue - a complete list of items, typically in alphabetical or systematic order
  • synonym: library catalog - a list of all the books and materials in a library
  • antonym: online catalog - a digital system for listing items, especially in a library
  • antonym: online catalogue - a computerized listing of library holdings, often replacing the card catalogue
