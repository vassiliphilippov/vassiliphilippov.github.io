meretriciousness

noun
showy but insincere or superficial attractiveness
9.9 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛr.əˈtrɪʃ.əs.nəs /; / ˌmɛrəˈtrɪʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˌmɛr.ɪˈtrɪʃ.əs.nəs /; / ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being attractive or impressive in a superficial or flashy way, but lacking real value or genuineness
