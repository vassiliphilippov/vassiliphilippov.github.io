uncentralized

adjective
not concentrated
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈsɛntrəˌlaɪzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈsɛntrəlaɪzd / (BrE)
not centralized; spread out or distributed among several places or groups

Examples

An uncentralized system
The new system is uncentralized, allowing each department to manage its own resources.
An uncentralized network can be more resilient to failures than a single central hub.
The country's healthcare services remain largely uncentralized, with clinics spread across rural areas.
The organization became more uncentralized to allow local branches greater independence.
An uncentralized system can respond more quickly to regional needs and changes.
Many modern companies prefer an uncentralized approach to encourage creativity and flexibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: centralized - concentrated in one central place or under one authority
  • synonym: decentralized - distributed away from a central authority or location
