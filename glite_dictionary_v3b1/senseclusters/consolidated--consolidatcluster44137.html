consolidated

adjective
joined or unified into a whole
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɑlɪˌdeɪtɪd / (AmE), / kənˈsɒlɪdeɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of things) joined together into a single whole; unified or made stronger by combining separate parts.

Examples

Consolidated company
After several mergers, the company became a consolidated industry leader.
The schools were consolidated to improve efficiency and save resources.
This report presents the consolidated results from all regional offices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amalgamated - joined together to form one organization or structure
  • antonym: fragmented - broken into parts or not unified
  • synonym: unified - made into a single unit
