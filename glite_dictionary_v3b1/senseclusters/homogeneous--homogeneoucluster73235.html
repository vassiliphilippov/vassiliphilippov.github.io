homogeneous

adjective
of the same kind; uniform
2.5
Pronunciation: / hoʊˈmoʊdʒəˌniəs / (AmE), / həˈmɒdʒɪniəs / (BrE)
consisting of people or things that are all of the same kind or that are similar in nature

Examples

A homogeneous group
Many businesses seek to create a homogeneous brand experience across all locations.
The club was known for its homogeneous membership, attracting people with similar interests and backgrounds.
The scientist explained that the mixture was not homogeneous because the substances had not fully blended.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heterogeneous - consisting of different or diverse kinds
  • synonym: uniform - not varying or changing; staying the same at all times
