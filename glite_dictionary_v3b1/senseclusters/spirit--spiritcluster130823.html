spirit

noun
vital animating force or essence
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈspɪr.ɪt/ /; / /ˈspɪr.ət/ (AmE), /ˈspɪr.ɪt/ /; / /ˈspɪr.ət/ (BrE)
The vital or animating force believed to exist within living beings, often seen as the essence of life or consciousness.

Examples

A living spirit
Many cultures believe that a person's spirit lives on after death.
She felt a renewed spirit within her as she recovered from her illness.
The ceremony was meant to honor the spirit of their ancestors.

  • synonym: soul - the spiritual or immaterial part of a living being
