greenfly

noun
a small green aphid pest
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrinˌflaɪ / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnflaɪ / (BrE)
A small green aphid that is a common pest on garden and crop plants.

Examples

The greenfly pest
Aphid infestations, especially of greenfly, can damage vegetable crops.
Gardeners often use ladybirds to control outbreaks of greenfly.
The rose bushes were covered in greenfly after the warm spring.
