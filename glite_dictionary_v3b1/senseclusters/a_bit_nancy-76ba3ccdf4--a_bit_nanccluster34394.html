a bit nancy

adjective
somewhat effeminate or delicate
Pronunciation: / ə bɪt ˈnænsi / (AmE), / ə bɪt ˈnænsi / (BrE)
Somewhat effeminate or overly delicate, often used disparagingly to describe a person or thing perceived as not masculine enough.

Examples

A bit nancy style
He said the curtains looked a bit nancy for his living room.
That movie was a bit nancy for my taste, but it was still entertaining.
Wearing pink used to be considered a bit nancy, but attitudes are changing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: effeminate - having characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly
