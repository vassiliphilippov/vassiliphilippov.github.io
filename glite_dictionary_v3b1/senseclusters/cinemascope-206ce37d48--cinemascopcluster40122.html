CinemaScope

adjective
widescreen cinematic style
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəməˌskoʊp / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəməˌskəʊp / (BrE)
in the style of or using the CinemaScope widescreen film format, especially very wide and cinematic in appearance

Examples

CinemaScope visuals
The film's CinemaScope visuals made the mountain scenery look even more impressive.
She admired the CinemaScope proportions of the new television in the store window.
The documentary was shot in a CinemaScope format to capture the vastness of the savannah.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrow - having a small width or limited view
  • synonym: panoramic - showing a wide, unbroken view
  • synonym: widescreen - having a wider aspect ratio than standard film formats
