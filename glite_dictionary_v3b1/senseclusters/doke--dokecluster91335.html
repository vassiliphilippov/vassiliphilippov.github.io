doke

noun
small dent or bump
9.9
Pronunciation: /doʊk/ (AmE), /dəʊk/ (BrE)
a small dent or bump, especially on the surface of a car or other object

Examples

A small doke on the car
After the hailstorm, he found a small doke on the hood of his car.
She was upset to see a doke on her new refrigerator door.
The mechanic said the doke was only cosmetic and easy to fix.
