hardscape

noun
non-living garden features
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːrd.skeɪp/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːd.skeɪp/ (BrE)
the non-living elements of a garden or landscape, such as paths, walls, patios, or rocks

Examples

Stone hardscape
The designer added a stone hardscape to create a modern look in the backyard.
Maintaining the hardscape is easier than caring for large areas of grass.
They installed a new patio as part of the home's hardscape renovation.
