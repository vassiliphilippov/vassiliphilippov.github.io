Synonyms

  • close its doors
    4.0 0.8/m
    close its doors means a business stops for good and leaves the market, while close down focuses on ending all operations permanently.
  • fold up
    4.4 0.05/m
    fold up often describes a business that fails and closes for good, while close down is neutral for permanent closure.
  • close up shop
    3.9 0.5/m
    close up shop is an informal phrase for stopping trade, while close down stresses a permanent end of business operations.
  • close up
    3.5 2/m
    close up can mean shutting a place temporarily after hours, while close down implies a permanent stop to the business.
  • shut down
    2.9 30/m
    shut down can refer to machines or short closures in factories, while close down specifically means ending a business permanently.
