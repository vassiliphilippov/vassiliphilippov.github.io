upset

noun
mild disturbance of normal physical condition
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈsɛt / (AmE), / ʌpˈsɛt / (BrE)
a mild disturbance or disorder in a person's normal physical condition, especially of the stomach or digestive system

Examples

A stomach upset
After eating too quickly, he suffered an upset that lasted all afternoon.
She missed school because of a severe upset of her stomach.
Travelers often experience digestive upset in foreign countries due to unfamiliar food.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disorder - a disturbance in physical or mental health or functions
