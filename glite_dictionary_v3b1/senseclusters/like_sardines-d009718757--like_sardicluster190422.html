like sardines

adverb
in a very crowded or cramped way
5.8
Pronunciation: / laɪk ˈsɑrˌdinz / (AmE), / laɪk ˈsɑːˌdiːnz / (BrE)
in a very crowded or cramped way; with people or things packed closely together, as in a confined space

Examples

Packed like sardines
The passengers stood like sardines on the subway during rush hour.
They squeezed into the tiny elevator like sardines.
We were seated like sardines at the small family dinner table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crammed - filled completely so that there is hardly any space
  • synonym: packed - full of people or things; very crowded
