synchronize

verb
make film sound match the action
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋ.krəˌnaɪz /; / ˈsɪŋkrəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋ.krə.naɪz / (BrE)
to make the sound in a motion picture precisely match the action on screen

Examples
