nonnuclear

adjective
not nuclear
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈnuːkliɚ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈnjuːkliə / (BrE)
not using, involving, or related to nuclear energy or nuclear weapons

Examples

Nonnuclear energy sources
The government is promoting nonnuclear energy sources to reduce environmental risks.
Several countries signed a treaty to remain strictly nonnuclear in their military policies.
The new power plant will use nonnuclear technology to generate electricity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nuclear - using or relating to nuclear energy or weapons
