organic compound

noun
chemical compound with carbon; often in living things
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ɔːr.ˈgæn.ɪk ˈkɑːm.paʊnd/ /; / /ɔːr.ˈgæn.ɪk kəm.ˈpaʊnd/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈgæn.ɪk ˈkɒm.paʊnd/ /; / /ɔː.ˈgæn.ɪk kəm.ˈpaʊnd/ (BrE)
