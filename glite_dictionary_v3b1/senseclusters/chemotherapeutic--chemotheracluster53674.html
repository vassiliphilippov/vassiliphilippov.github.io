chemotherapeutic

noun
cancer treatment drug
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkiːmoʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌkiːməʊˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk / (BrE)
a drug or substance used in the treatment of cancer with chemicals

Examples

A chemotherapeutic drug
The doctor recommended a new chemotherapeutic to help shrink the tumor.
Researchers are developing more effective chemotherapeutic agents with fewer side effects.
Some patients respond better to one chemotherapeutic than to another.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antineoplastic - a drug that prevents the growth of tumors
  • synonym: chemotherapy - the treatment of disease, especially cancer, using chemical substances
