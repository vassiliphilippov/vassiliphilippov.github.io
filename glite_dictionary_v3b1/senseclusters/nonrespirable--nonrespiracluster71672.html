nonrespirable

adjective
not suitable for inhalation
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈrɛspərəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈrɛspɪrəbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be breathed in or not suitable for inhalation

Examples

Nonrespirable dust particles
The laboratory uses special filters to remove nonrespirable dust from the air.
Workers must wear masks to avoid inhaling nonrespirable particles on the construction site.
The report identified several nonrespirable substances present in the factory environment.
