neurotoxicity

noun
damage to nerve tissue
8.2
Pronunciation: /nʊr.oʊ.tɑːk.ˈsɪs.ə.tiː/ /; / /njuː.rə.tɑːk.ˈsɪs.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /njʊə.tɒk.ˈsɪs.ɪ.tiː/ /; / /njuː.rə.tɒk.ˈsɪs.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)

Examples

Researchers are developing drugs to reduce neurotoxicity in neurological diseases.
