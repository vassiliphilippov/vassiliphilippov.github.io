sticky

adjective
fixed in viewport (web design)
5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈstɪ.kiː/ (AmE), /ˈstɪ.kiː/ (BrE)
(in web design and user interfaces) remaining fixed to the viewport or a containing element while the rest of the page scrolls; typically implemented with CSS (e.g. position: sticky) or JavaScript.

Examples

Sticky header
Web designers use sticky headers that stay visible while users scroll through a page.
She made the navigation bar sticky so visitors can access it from any scroll position.
On mobile, a small sticky footer with actions improved conversion during long article reading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fixed - not moving; remaining in place relative to the viewport or screen
  • synonym: pinned - kept visible or attached in a specific location on the screen
