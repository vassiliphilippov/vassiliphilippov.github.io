wellness

noun
state of good health
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɛlnəs / (BrE)
a state of good health or wellbeing, especially as an actively pursued goal or lifestyle

Examples

A wellness program
Many companies now offer wellness programs to promote a healthy lifestyle among employees.
Regular exercise is a key component of overall wellness.
She is passionate about nutrition and wellness, often sharing tips on how to stay healthy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disease - an abnormal condition affecting the body of an organism, often with symptoms
  • synonym: health - the general condition of the body or mind with reference to soundness and vigor
