clubbing

noun
nightlife dancing
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌbɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈklʌbɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity of going to nightclubs to dance, listen to music, and socialize, usually at night

Examples

Go clubbing at night
They love clubbing on weekends and often stay out until sunrise.
After finishing their exams, the group went clubbing to celebrate.
Many young people enjoy clubbing in big cities for the lively atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nightclubbing - going out to nightclubs for entertainment
  • synonym: partying - attending social gatherings for fun and celebration
