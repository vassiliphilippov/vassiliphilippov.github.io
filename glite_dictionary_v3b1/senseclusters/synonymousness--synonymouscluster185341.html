synonymousness

noun
the quality of being synonymous
8.0
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɑnɪməsnəs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɒnɪməsnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being synonymous; the condition in which two or more words have the same or nearly the same meaning in a particular context.
