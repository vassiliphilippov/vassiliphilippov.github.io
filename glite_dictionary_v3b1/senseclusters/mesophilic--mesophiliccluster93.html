mesophilic

adjective
growing at moderate temperatures
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛsəˈfɪlɪk / (AmE), / ˌmiːsəˈfɪlɪk / (BrE)
describing organisms that grow best at moderate temperatures, typically between 20°C and 45°C

Examples

Mesophilic bacteria
Many types of cheese are produced using mesophilic bacteria that prefer moderate temperatures.
The laboratory studied mesophilic organisms to understand their adaptation to room temperature environments.
Composting is often most efficient when mesophilic microbes are active in the pile.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: psychrophilic - describing organisms that grow best at low temperatures
  • degree higher: thermophilic - describing organisms that grow best at high temperatures
