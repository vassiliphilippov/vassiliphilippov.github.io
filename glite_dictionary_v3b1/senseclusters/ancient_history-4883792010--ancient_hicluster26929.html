ancient history

noun
the history of the ancient world
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈhɪstəri / (AmE), / ˈeɪnʃənt ˈhɪstəri / (BrE)
the history of the ancient world, typically from the earliest recorded times until the early Middle Ages

Examples
