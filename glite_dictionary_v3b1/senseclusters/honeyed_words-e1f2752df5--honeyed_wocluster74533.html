honeyed words

noun
flattering or insincere expressions
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌnid wɝdz / (AmE), / ˈhʌnɪd wɜːdz / (BrE)
Words or phrases that are pleasing and flattering, but often insincere or intended to deceive.

Examples

Honeyed words of praise
Despite his honeyed words, his actions betrayed his true intentions.
Politicians often use honeyed words to win over the public.
She saw through his honeyed words and refused to trust him.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flattery - Excessive and insincere praise
  • antonym: sincere words - Genuine and honest speech
  • synonym: sweet talk - Flattering speech intended to persuade or manipulate
