yew

noun
wood from the yew tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / juː / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
The durable, fine-grained wood of the yew tree, especially valued for making furniture and archery bows.

Examples

Yew wood
Furniture made of yew is known for its rich color and durability.
She admired the antique chest carved from yew in the corner of the room.
The longbow was traditionally made from yew because of its strength and flexibility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ash - wood from the ash tree; also used for bows and tools
  • synonym: oak - wood from the oak tree; commonly used hardwood
