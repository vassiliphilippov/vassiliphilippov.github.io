socio-economic class

noun
group by economic and social status
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊsi.oʊ ɪˈkɑnəmɪk klæs / (AmE), / ˌsəʊsiəʊ ɪˈkɒnəmɪk klɑːs / (BrE)
