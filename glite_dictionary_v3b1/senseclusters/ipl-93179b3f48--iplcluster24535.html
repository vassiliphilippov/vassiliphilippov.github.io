IPL

noun
mainframe startup process
7.9
Pronunciation: /ˈaɪ.piː.el/ (AmE), /ˈaɪ.piː.el/ (BrE)
the process of starting or restarting a computer's operating system, especially on a mainframe; also called a boot or reboot

Examples

IPL system restart
The engineer initiated an IPL to load the new system software on the mainframe.
A scheduled IPL is required after major hardware changes to ensure proper operation.
If the system becomes unresponsive, performing an IPL can often resolve the issue.
