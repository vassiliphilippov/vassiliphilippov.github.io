pop

verb
drink quickly and completely (informal)
Pronunciation: / pɑp / (AmE), / pɒp / (BrE)
(informal) To drink down (especially an alcoholic drink) quickly and completely.

Examples

To pop a beer
After work, they like to pop a couple of beers with friends.
He popped the shot of whiskey in one go.
We decided to pop some champagne to celebrate the news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: down - to drink something quickly or in one swallow
  • antonym: sip - to drink in small amounts
