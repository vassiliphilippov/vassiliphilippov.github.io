papist

adjective
supporting or relating to Roman Catholicism
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpeɪpɪst / (AmE), / ˈpeɪpɪst / (BrE)
of or relating to the Roman Catholic Church, especially in a manner that supports its doctrines or practices (often used disparagingly or in historical contexts)

Examples

Papist beliefs
He was accused of holding papist sympathies during the Reformation.
Her family was considered dangerously papist by their neighbors.
The old laws discriminated against those with papist beliefs in England.
