Eurasianism

noun
Europe-Asia unity movement
Pronunciation: / jʊˈreɪʒənɪzəm / (AmE), / jʊəˈreɪʒənɪzəm / (BrE)
a political and cultural movement that emphasizes the unity and shared identity of Europe and Asia, especially in Russian thought

Examples

Eurasianism political movement
The idea of Eurasianism has shaped some modern Russian foreign policies.
Many intellectuals debated the principles of Eurasianism in the early twentieth century.
Some political parties promote Eurasianism as a way to strengthen ties between Europe and Asia.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nationalism - a political ideology focused on promoting the interests of a particular nation
  • synonym: pan-Slavism - a movement advocating the unity of Slavic peoples
