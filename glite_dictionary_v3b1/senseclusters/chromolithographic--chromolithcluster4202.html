chromolithographic

adjective
multi-color printing
Pronunciation: / ˌkroʊmoʊˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌkrəʊməʊˌlɪθəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
relating to or produced by chromolithography, a method of making multi-color prints using lithography

Examples

Chromolithographic color prints
The gallery featured several chromolithographic prints from the Victorian era.
She admired the vivid colors of the chromolithographic illustrations in the antique book.
Collectors value chromolithographic posters for their historical and artistic significance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lithographic - relating to lithography, a printing process using stone or metal plates
  • antonym: monochrome - produced in a single color
