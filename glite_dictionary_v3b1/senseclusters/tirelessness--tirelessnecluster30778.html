tirelessness

noun
persistent energy; not becoming tired
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɚləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪəlɪsnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of showing great energy and persistence, without becoming tired.

Examples

Her tirelessness in work
Her tirelessness in volunteering impressed everyone at the shelter.
His tirelessness during the campaign set a new standard for political candidates.
The team's tirelessness helped them achieve success despite many setbacks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endurance - the ability to withstand hardship or stress for a long time
  • antonym: fatigue - extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion
  • synonym: tireless - never seeming to become tired
