eye-catching

adjective
attracting attention; visually striking
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪ ˌkætʃɪŋ /; / ˈaɪˌkætʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈaɪ ˌkatʃɪŋ /; / ˈaɪˌkatʃɪŋ / (BrE)
immediately attracting attention because of being visually striking, attractive, or unusual

Examples

An eye-catching dress
She wore an eye-catching red dress to the party, drawing everyone's attention.
The designer created an eye-catching logo to help the brand stand out.
The museum's new exhibit features several eye-catching sculptures.
She wore an eye-catching necklace that drew everyone's attention at the party.
The architecture of the building is modern and eye-catching, attracting many tourists.
The bold colors of the new packaging made it truly eye-catching on the shelves.
