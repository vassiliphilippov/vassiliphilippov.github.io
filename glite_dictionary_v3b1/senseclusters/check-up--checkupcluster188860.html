check-up

noun
routine medical examination
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛk.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈtʃek.ʌp / (BrE)
a medical examination to test your general health, often carried out routinely

Examples

A check-up appointment
My doctor scheduled a check-up for next Monday to review my blood pressure.
Regular check-ups can help detect health problems at an early stage.
She took her son to the pediatrician for his annual check-up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: examination - thorough inspection of someone's health by a doctor
