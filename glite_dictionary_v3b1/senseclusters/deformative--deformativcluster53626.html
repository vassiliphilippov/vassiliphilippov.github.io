deformative

adjective
causing deformation
7.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɔrmətɪv / (AmE), / dɪˈfɔːmətɪv / (BrE)
causing or relating to a change in shape, often in a damaging or harmful way

Examples

Deformative effect
The rare condition has a deformative impact on the patient's joints.
Prolonged pressure can have deformative effects on soft materials.
The artist used a deformative technique to create abstract sculptures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distortive - causing something to be twisted or deformed
  • antonym: restorative - having the ability to restore or return to original form
