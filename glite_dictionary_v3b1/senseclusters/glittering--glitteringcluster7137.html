Synonyms

  • sparkling
    1.3 0.2/m
    sparkling gives bright, lively flashes and a fresh shine, while glittering means many small, bright flashing points across a surface.
  • scintillating
    8.2 0.01/m
    scintillating means brilliantly shining or impressively clever in speech, while glittering focuses on visible many bright flashing points.
  • glinting
    3.6 0.00/m
