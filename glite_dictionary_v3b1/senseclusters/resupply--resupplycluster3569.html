resupply

noun
delivery of supplies
3.9
Pronunciation: /riː.sə.ˈplaɪ/ (AmE), /riː.sə.ˈplaɪ/ (BrE)
an act of providing supplies again, especially to replace what has been used or depleted

Examples

A resupply mission
The mountain climbers awaited a resupply of food and equipment at the base camp.
After several days at sea, the ship required a resupply of fresh water and fuel.
The research station depends on a regular resupply to continue its operations in the remote region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: replenishment - the act of filling something up again
  • synonym: supply - the act of providing something needed
