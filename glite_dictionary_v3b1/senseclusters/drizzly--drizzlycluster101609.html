drizzly

adjective
lightly rainy or damp
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdrɪz.li / (AmE), / ˈdrɪz.li / (BrE)
characterized by light and persistent rain; slightly wet due to drizzle

Examples

A drizzly day
I prefer sunny weather to the drizzly days we often get in April.
It was a drizzly morning, so we decided to take our umbrellas.
The park looked empty and forlorn on the drizzly afternoon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drizzle - light rain falling in fine drops
  • degree higher: rainy - characterized by rain
