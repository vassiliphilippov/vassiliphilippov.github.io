blech

interjection
disgust exclamation
Pronunciation: / blɛk / (AmE), / blɛk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express dislike, disgust, or distaste

Examples

Blech at bad food
She took a bite of the overcooked broccoli and immediately said, 'blech!'
When he smelled the spoiled eggs, he wrinkled his nose and exclaimed, 'blech!'
The medicine tasted so bitter that all she could say was 'blech'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yuck - an exclamation expressing disgust or distaste
