buttress

verb
support or strengthen
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtrəs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtrəs / (BrE)
to support, strengthen, or reinforce something, especially an argument or position

Examples

To buttress an argument
She buttressed her argument by citing several scientific studies.
The lawyer sought to buttress his case with additional evidence.
The professor encouraged students to buttress their essays with solid facts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinforce - to strengthen or make more forceful
  • antonym: undermine - to weaken or damage, especially gradually or insidiously
