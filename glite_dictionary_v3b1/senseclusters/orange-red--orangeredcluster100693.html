orange-red

adjective
red tinged with orange
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrɪndʒ ˈrɛd / (AmE), / ˈɒrɪndʒ ˈrɛd / (BrE)
of a color that is primarily red with a tinged or mixed presence of orange; situated between red and orange on the color spectrum.

Examples

Orange-red color
She chose an orange-red lipstick to complement her evening gown.
The autumn leaves turned a brilliant orange-red before falling from the trees.
The sunset painted the sky an intense orange-red above the horizon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scarlet - a bright red color with a slightly orange tinge
  • synonym: vermillion - a vivid red to reddish-orange color
