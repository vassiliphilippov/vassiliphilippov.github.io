law book

noun
book of legal texts
2.5
Pronunciation: / lɔ bʊk / (AmE), / lɔː bʊk / (BrE)
a book that contains the text of laws or provides legal information and references

Examples

A law book text
The lawyer reached for a law book to check the latest regulations.
Students often use a law book to study important legal principles for their exams.
Every judge keeps a law book handy in the courtroom for quick reference.

Related Terms

  • synonym: statute book - a book containing the written laws enacted by a legislature
