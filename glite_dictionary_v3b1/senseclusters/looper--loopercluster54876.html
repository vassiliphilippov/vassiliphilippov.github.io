looper

noun
one who moves in loops
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈluːpər / (AmE), / ˈluːpə / (BrE)
a person or thing that moves in loops or circles, or something that forms loops.

Examples

Playground looper
The children acted as loopers in the game, running in circles around the playground.
A skilled looper can create intricate patterns by moving the ribbon in loops.
In the parade, each looper wore a bright costume and spun around joyfully.
