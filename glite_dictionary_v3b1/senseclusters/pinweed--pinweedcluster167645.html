pinweed

noun
slender-stemmed plant
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌwiːd / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌwiːd / (BrE)
a small, slender-stemmed plant, especially any of several species in the genus Lechea

Examples

Small pinweed plant
The botanist identified the small plant as pinweed growing along the roadside.
We found patches of pinweed scattered throughout the sandy field.
Some gardeners appreciate pinweed for its delicate appearance and resilience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Lechea - a genus of small, slender-stemmed plants known as pinweeds
  • synonym: herb - a plant with a soft, non-woody stem
