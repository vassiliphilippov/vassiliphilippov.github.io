Orcinus

proper noun
killer whale genus
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrsɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈɔːsɪnəs / (BrE)
a genus of marine mammals that includes the killer whale (orca)

Examples

Genus Orcinus
The genus Orcinus contains only one living species, the orca.
Scientists study Orcinus fossils to learn about the evolution of killer whales.
The classification of Orcinus places it within the dolphin family.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Delphinidae - the family of marine mammals that includes dolphins and orcas
  • synonym: orca - a large black-and-white predatory whale, also known as the killer whale
