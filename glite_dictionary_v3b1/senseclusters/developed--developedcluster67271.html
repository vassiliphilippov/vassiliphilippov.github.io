developed

adjective
having advanced or matured through change
Pronunciation: / dɪˈvɛləpt / (AmE), / dɪˈvɛləpt / (BrE)
having advanced or grown to a more complete, mature, or useful state through a process of change or improvement.

Examples

Developed skills
His musical skills are highly developed after years of practice.
Many countries are striving to become more developed in terms of infrastructure and technology.
Only birds with fully developed wings are able to migrate long distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mature - having reached full development or growth
  • antonym: undeveloped - not having grown, advanced, or matured; lacking development
