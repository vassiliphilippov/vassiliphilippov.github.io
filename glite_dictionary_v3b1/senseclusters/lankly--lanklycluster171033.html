lankly

adverb
awkwardly, thinly
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈlæŋkli / (AmE), / ˈlæŋkli / (BrE)
in a manner that is tall, thin, and awkward, especially in movement or posture

Examples

Walks lankly
The teenager walked lankly down the hallway, his arms swinging at his sides.
She stood lankly in the doorway, her tall frame making her look even more awkward.
The model moved lankly across the runway, her long legs drawing attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in an ungainly or clumsy way
  • antonym: gracefully - in an elegant and smooth way
