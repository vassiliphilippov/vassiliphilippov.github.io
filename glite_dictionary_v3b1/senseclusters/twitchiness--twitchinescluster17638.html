a state or quality of having small, involuntary muscle movements or spasms in a part of the body

Examples

Twitchiness in muscles
The twitchiness in his fingers made it difficult for him to write steadily.
After too much coffee, she noticed a slight twitchiness in her eyelid.
Doctors often ask about twitchiness as a symptom of certain neurological conditions.

Synonyms

  • myokymia
    9.9 0.03/m
    myokymia is a fine, often long-lasting quiver in small muscles like the eyelid, while twitchiness is the general state of twitching.
  • vellication
    9.9 0.00/m
    vellication is a sudden, small muscle spasm usually linked to nerves, while twitchiness is a broader, ongoing tendency to twitch.
  • myoclonus
    9.9 0.15/m
    myoclonus is a sudden, often strong muscle jerk that may move a limb, while twitchiness means small, mild involuntary twitches.
