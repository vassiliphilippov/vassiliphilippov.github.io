mux

verb
to combine signals
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌks / (AmE), / mʌks / (BrE)
to combine multiple signals or data streams into one, or to select and forward one input to an output, especially in electronics and computing

Examples

To mux audio streams
The engineer will mux the video and audio feeds before sending them to the server.
You need to mux the data streams so they can be transmitted over a single cable.
After editing, they muxed all the tracks into one file for the final output.

Related Terms

  • synonym: combine - to join or merge two or more things together
  • antonym: demux - to separate a combined signal or data stream into its original components
