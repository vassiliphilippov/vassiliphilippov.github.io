order Sphenisciformes

noun
scientific order including all penguins
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdɚ sfɛnˌɪsɪˈfɔrmiːz / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə sfɛnˌɪsɪˈfɔːmiːz / (BrE)
The scientific order of aquatic, flightless birds that comprises all species of penguins.

Examples

The order Sphenisciformes
All living species of penguins belong to the order Sphenisciformes.
Scientists study the order Sphenisciformes to understand the adaptations of penguins to cold environments.
The fossil record provides insight into the evolution of the order Sphenisciformes.
