corkboard

noun
board for pinning notes
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈkɔːkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board made of cork used for pinning notes, photos, or reminders with pushpins or thumbtacks

Examples

A corkboard for notes
She pinned her schedule to the corkboard above her desk.
The classroom corkboard was covered with colorful drawings and announcements.
He used a corkboard to organize his important reminders and photos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: noticeboard - a board for displaying notices or information
  • antonym: whiteboard - a smooth board for writing on with markers
