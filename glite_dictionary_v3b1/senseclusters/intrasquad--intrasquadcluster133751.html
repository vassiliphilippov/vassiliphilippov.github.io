intrasquad

adjective
within one team
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntrəˌskwɑd / (AmE), / ˈɪntrəˌskwɒd / (BrE)
taking place within a single team, especially referring to practice or competition among members of the same team

Examples

Intrasquad match
The coach scheduled an intrasquad game to help players practice their skills.
During the intrasquad match, teammates competed against each other to earn starting positions.
An intrasquad scrimmage is a common way to evaluate new strategies before the season begins.
