disidentify

verb
to stop relating
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (AmE), / ˌdɪsaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to stop seeing oneself as connected with or belonging to a particular group, role, or idea

Examples

To disidentify with a group
After moving to a new city, she began to disidentify with her old school friends.
Some employees disidentify from their company after a major restructuring.
He tried to disidentify with the group when their values changed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissociate - to separate oneself from a group or idea
  • antonym: identify - to see oneself as connected with a group or idea
