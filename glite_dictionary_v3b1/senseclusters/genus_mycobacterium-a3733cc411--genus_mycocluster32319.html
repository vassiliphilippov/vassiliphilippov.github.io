genus Mycobacterium

noun
bacterial genus including tuberculosis agents
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒiː.nəs maɪ.kə.bæk.ˈtɪr.i.əm/ (AmE), /ˈdʒiː.nəs maɪ.kə.bæk.ˈtɪə.ri.əm/ /; / /ˈdʒɛn.əs maɪ.kə.bæk.ˈtɪə.ri.əm/ (BrE)
