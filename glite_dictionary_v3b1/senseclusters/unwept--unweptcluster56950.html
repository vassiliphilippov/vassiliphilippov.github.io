unwept

adjective
not mourned
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɛpt / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɛpt / (BrE)
not mourned or cried over; lacking tears or expressions of sorrow.

Examples

An unwept death
The hero died unwept, with no one to remember his sacrifice.
She left the town unwept and unnoticed by her former friends.
The old house was demolished, its history unwept and forgotten by the community.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mourned - grieved for or lamented
  • synonym: unmourned - not grieved for or lamented
