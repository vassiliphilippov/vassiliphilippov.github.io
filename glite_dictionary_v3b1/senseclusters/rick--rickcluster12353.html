rick

noun
stack of hay or straw
Pronunciation: / rɪk / (AmE), / rɪk / (BrE)
A stack of hay, corn, or straw, especially one built outdoors.

Examples

A hay rick
Rain can quickly ruin a rick if it isn't properly covered.
The farmer built a rick of hay in the corner of the field.
They loaded pitchforks full of straw onto the growing rick by the barn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stack - a pile of objects, typically one that is neatly arranged
