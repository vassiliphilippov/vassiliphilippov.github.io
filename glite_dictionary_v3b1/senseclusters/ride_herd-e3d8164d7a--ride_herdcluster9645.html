ride herd

verb
drive and manage animals on horseback
8.5
Pronunciation: / raɪd hɜrd / (AmE), / raɪd hɜːd / (BrE)
to drive and manage a group of animals such as cattle or horses by riding alongside them, typically on horseback.

Examples

To ride herd cattle
During the roundup, she often rides herd with her father in the early morning.
For weeks, the cowboys rode herd over the cattle as they moved north.
He was riding herd on the mustangs to keep them from straying.
