forward motion

noun
the act of moving ahead
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwɚd ˈmoʊʃən / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwəd ˈməʊʃən / (BrE)
the act of moving ahead, especially toward a goal or objective

Examples

Constant forward motion
He admired her constant forward motion in the face of obstacles.
The forward motion of the train was suddenly halted by an emergency brake.
Without forward motion, the team’s chances of reaching the finals diminished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progress - movement towards a place or goal
  • antonym: regression - a return to a previous, less advanced state
