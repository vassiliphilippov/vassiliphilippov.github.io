treat

verb
pay for or give a special gift/entertainment
Pronunciation: / trit / (AmE), / triːt / (BrE)
to pay for or provide someone with something special, such as a meal or entertainment, as a gift or show of kindness

Examples

To treat a friend
Every year, he treats his friends to tickets for the big game.
Let me treat you to dinner to celebrate your promotion.
When I finished the project, I treated myself to a massage.
Let me treat you to dinner tonight to celebrate your promotion.
She always treats her friends to coffee when they visit.
We were treated to a delicious meal at their house last weekend.

Related Terms

  • synonym: entertain - to provide someone with amusement or hospitality
  • synonym: host - to provide hospitality or entertainment to guests
  • synonym: invite - to ask someone to go somewhere or do something, often paying for them
