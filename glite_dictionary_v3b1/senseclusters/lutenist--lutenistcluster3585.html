lutenist

noun
a person who plays the lute
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈluːtənɪst / (AmE), / ˈluːtənɪst / (BrE)
a person who plays the lute, a stringed musical instrument popular in the Renaissance and Baroque periods

Examples

A lutenist plays lute
During the recital, a young lutenist played pieces from the Baroque era.
She studied with a master lutenist to perfect her technique on the instrument.
The renowned lutenist captivated the audience with his delicate performance.
