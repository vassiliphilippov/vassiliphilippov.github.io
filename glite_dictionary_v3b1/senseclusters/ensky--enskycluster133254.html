ensky

verb
to exalt or praise highly
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈskaɪ / (AmE), / ɛnˈskaɪ / (BrE)
to praise or exalt someone or something highly, as if raising them to the sky or heaven

Examples

To ensky a hero
Historians sometimes ensky figures from the past, overlooking their flaws.
Some critics ensky the artist beyond all reason, treating her as a genius.
The poet sought to ensky his beloved in his writing, elevating her virtues.
