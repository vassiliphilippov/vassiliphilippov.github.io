agitato

adjective
agitated or excited
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒɪˈtɑːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˌadʒɪˈtɑːtəʊ / (BrE)
showing agitation or nervous excitement, especially in manner, voice, or behavior.

Examples

Agitato voice
Her agitato reply made it clear she was upset by the news.
The conductor asked the orchestra to play the passage in an agitato style.
He spoke in an agitato voice, unable to hide his excitement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agitated - feeling or appearing troubled or nervous
  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness or agitation
