gizmo

noun
small device or gadget
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɪzmoʊ /; / ˈɡɪzˌmoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡɪzməʊ / (BrE)
an informal word for a small device or gadget, especially when you do not know or remember its name

Examples

A small gizmo
Can you pass me that gizmo on the table?
He invented a clever little gizmo to open bottles easily.
I forgot the name of the gizmo that came with this set.
I can't figure out how this gizmo works; do you have the instructions?
She bought a new kitchen gizmo that chops vegetables in seconds.
There's a gizmo under the sink that's supposed to stop leaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doohickey - an informal term for a small object or gadget, name unknown
  • synonym: gadget - a small mechanical or electronic device with a particular purpose
  • synonym: thingamajig - an informal term for an object whose name is unknown
