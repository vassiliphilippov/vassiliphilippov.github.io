fitness

noun
suitability; appropriateness
3.8 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪt.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈfɪt.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being suitable or qualified for a particular purpose, role, or activity

Examples

Fitness for the job
Questions were raised about his fitness to hold public office after the investigation.
The board assessed the candidate's fitness for leading the new department.
We need to evaluate the fitness of each material for use in construction.
Her fitness for the role was never in doubt.
The board questioned his fitness to serve as the new director.
We must assess the fitness of all candidates before making a final decision.
