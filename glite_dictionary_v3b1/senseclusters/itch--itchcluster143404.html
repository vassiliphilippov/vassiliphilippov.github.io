itch

noun
uncomfortable skin sensation
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪtʃ / (BrE)
an uncomfortable sensation on the skin that causes a desire to scratch

Examples

An itch on my arm
I've had an itch on my arm all morning and it's driving me crazy.
Mosquito bites can cause a persistent itch that is hard to ignore.
Scratching an itch sometimes only makes it worse.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: tickle - a light sensation on the skin often causing laughter or discomfort
