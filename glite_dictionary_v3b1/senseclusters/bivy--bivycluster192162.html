bivy

noun
outdoor sleeping shelter
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪvi / (AmE), / ˈbɪvi / (BrE)
a small, simple tent or shelter used outdoors for sleeping, often temporary or improvised, commonly used for camping, fishing, or overnight stays
