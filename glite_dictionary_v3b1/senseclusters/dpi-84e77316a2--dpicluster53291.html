education agency abbreviation
Pronunciation: / diː piː aɪ / (AmE), / diː piː aɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for Department of Public Instruction, a government agency overseeing public education and school policy in some U.S. states

Examples

State DPI report
The DPI released a report on student performance across the state this year.
Teachers attended a workshop organized by the DPI to learn about new teaching methods.
Funding for special education programs was increased by the DPI last month.
