flippy

adjective
not serious; flippant
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈflɪpi / (AmE), / ˈflɪpi / (BrE)
showing a lack of seriousness or respect; flippant or impertinent in tone or manner

Examples

Flippy comment
His flippy attitude during the meeting annoyed his more serious colleagues.
The comedian's flippy remarks kept the audience laughing, but some found them disrespectful.
She gave a flippy answer when asked about the important project deadline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flippant - not showing a serious or respectful attitude
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and respect
