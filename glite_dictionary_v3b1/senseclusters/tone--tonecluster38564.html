Synonyms

  • muscle tone
    3.4 0.58/m
    muscle tone is the natural slight firmness in resting muscles that keeps them ready for movement, while tone refers to muscle or tissue tension.
  • tonus
    9.8 0.05/m
    tonus is a medical term for steady slight tension in muscles or vessels that keeps them responsive, while tone is the everyday name.
  • tonicity
    9.9 0.01/m
