tone

noun
muscle or tissue tension; readiness to respond
2.6 6.8
Pronunciation: / toʊn / (AmE), / təʊn / (BrE)
the normal level of tension or firmness in muscles or tissue, enabling them to respond to stimuli

Examples

Muscle tone

Regular exercise helps maintain healthy muscle tone throughout the body.
