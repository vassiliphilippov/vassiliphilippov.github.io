ship

verb
endorse a romantic pairing (online fandom)
Pronunciation: / ʃɪp / (AmE), / ʃɪp / (BrE)
(informal, internet slang) to support, endorse, or imagine a romantic relationship between two real or fictional people, especially characters in fan communities.

Examples

To ship a couple
Many fans ship the two detectives despite the show's lack of romantic scenes.
After the finale, she admitted she had shipped the characters since season one.
The online community is shipping unexpected pairings and creating dozens of fanfics weekly.
