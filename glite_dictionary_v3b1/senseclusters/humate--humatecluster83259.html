humate

noun
material high in humic acids
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhjuː.meɪt / (AmE), / ˈhjuː.meɪt / (BrE)
A substance containing a high concentration of humic acids, often used in agriculture to enrich soil.

Examples

Soil humate
Farmers often add humate to the soil to improve its fertility and structure.
High-quality compost may contain a significant amount of natural humate.
The laboratory tested the sample for its humate content.
