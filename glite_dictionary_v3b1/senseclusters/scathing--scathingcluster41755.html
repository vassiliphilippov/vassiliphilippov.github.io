scathing

adjective
harshly critical; biting
Pronunciation: / ˈskeɪðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskeɪðɪŋ / (BrE)
severely critical in a harsh or biting way; expressing strong disapproval or criticism.

Examples

A scathing review
He responded with a scathing email that made his frustrations unmistakably clear.
Her scathing comments left the team feeling demoralized and resentful.
The critic wrote a scathing review of the author's latest book, highlighting every flaw.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biting - sharp or cutting in tone or effect
  • antonym: mild - gentle or not severe in criticism
