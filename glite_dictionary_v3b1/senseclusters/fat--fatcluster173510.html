fat

adjective
having excess body flesh
1.0 50/m
Pronunciation: / fÃ¦t / (AmE), / fÃ¦t / (BrE)
(of a person or animal) having a large amount of excess flesh or body fat.

Examples

A fat person
After months of indulging in desserts, he became noticeably fat.
Despite being fat, the cat was surprisingly agile.
The doctor advised her to lose weight because she was getting too fat.
