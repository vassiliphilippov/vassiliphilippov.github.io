visiting nurse

noun
nurse who cares for patients at home
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ nɝs / (AmE), / ˈvɪzɪtɪŋ nɜːs / (BrE)
a nurse who provides medical care to patients in their homes rather than in a hospital or clinic

Examples

Visiting nurse at home
As a visiting nurse, she travels across the city to help patients who cannot leave their homes.
Many families appreciate the support provided by a visiting nurse when caring for a loved one at home.
The visiting nurse checked on the elderly patient every morning after her surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: district nurse - nurse who visits patients at home (chiefly British)
