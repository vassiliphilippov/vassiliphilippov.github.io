observant

adjective
strictly following rules or customs
4.0
Pronunciation: / əbˈzɝː.vənt / (AmE), / əbˈzɜː.vənt / (BrE)
(of a person) strictly following laws, rules, or customs.

Examples

Observant Jew
He is known for being observant of religious dietary laws.
She is very observant of all the traditions during the festival.
The school expects students to be observant of its code of conduct.

Related Terms

  • synonym: law-abiding - obeying the laws, not breaking rules or laws
  • antonym: rebellious - resisting authority or control, not following rules
