Muskhogean

noun
member of southeastern US Indigenous peoples
9.9
Pronunciation: / mʌsˈkoʊɡiən / (AmE), / mʌsˈkəʊɡɪən / (BrE)
a member of any of the Indigenous peoples formerly living in the southeastern United States who spoke Muskhogean languages

Examples

Muskhogean people

Many anthropologists have studied the burial practices of the Muskhogean peoples.
The Muskhogean were known for their agricultural skills and complex societies.
