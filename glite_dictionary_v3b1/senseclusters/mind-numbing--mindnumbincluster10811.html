mind-numbing

adjective
extremely boring or dull
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaɪnd ˌnʌmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaɪnd ˌnʌmɪŋ / (BrE)
extremely boring or dull; so tedious or monotonous as to cause a loss of interest or mental engagement.

Examples

Mind-numbing boredom
She struggled to stay awake during the mind-numbing presentation.
The data entry job quickly became mind-numbing after several hours.
We escaped the mind-numbing routine by taking a spontaneous trip.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exciting - causing great enthusiasm and eagerness
  • synonym: tedious - too long, slow, or dull; tiresome or monotonous
