unbanked

adjective
not sloped (road/track)
7.0
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈbæŋkt/ /; / /ə.ˈbæŋkt/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈbæŋkt/ /; / /ə.ˈbæŋkt/ (BrE)
not sloped or angled to one side; flat rather than raised at the edges, especially of roads, tracks, or turns

Examples

Unbanked road corner

Pilots prefer not to land on unbanked runways during strong crosswinds.
