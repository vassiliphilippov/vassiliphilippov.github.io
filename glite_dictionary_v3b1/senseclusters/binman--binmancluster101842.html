binman

noun
rubbish collector
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪnmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɪnmæn / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect household rubbish or trash from outside homes

Examples

The binman collects rubbish
The binman comes every Tuesday to collect the garbage.
Children waved at the binman as he emptied the bins on their street.
Our local binman always greets everyone with a friendly smile.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dustman - a person who collects household rubbish, especially in British English
  • synonym: garbage collector - a person who collects household waste
