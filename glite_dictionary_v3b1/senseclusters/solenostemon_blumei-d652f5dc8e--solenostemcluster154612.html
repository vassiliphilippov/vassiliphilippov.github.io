Solenostemon blumei

noun
ornamental aromatic herb; also called coleus
Pronunciation: / ˌsoʊlɪˈnɑstəˌmɒn ˈbluːmiː / (AmE), / ˌsɒlɪˈnɒstɪˌmɒn ˈbluːmiː / (BrE)
A perennial aromatic herb native to southeastern Asia, known for its large, brightly colored or blotched leaves and spikes of blue-violet flowers; also called coleus.
