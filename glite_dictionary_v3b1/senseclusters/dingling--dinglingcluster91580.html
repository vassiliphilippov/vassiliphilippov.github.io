dingling

noun
light ringing sound
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪŋlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈdɪŋlɪŋ / (BrE)
a light, high ringing sound or a small bell that makes this sound

Examples

The dingling of bells
The dingling of the wind chimes could be heard from the garden.
A soft dingling echoed through the hallway as the elevator arrived.
She smiled at the cheerful dingling of the tiny bell on her cat's collar.
She attached a dingling to her keychain so she could always hear it.
The gentle sound of a dingling greeted them as they opened the shop door.
A tiny dingling hung from the kitten's collar, ringing with every step.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chime - a bell or set of bells that produce musical sounds
  • antonym: clang - a loud, resonant metallic sound
  • synonym: jingle bell - a small bell that makes a jingling sound
  • synonym: tinkle - a light, clear ringing sound
