purge

verb
to vomit; to eject stomach contents
5.4 6.3
Pronunciation: /pɜːrdʒ/ (AmE), /pɜːdʒ/ (BrE)
to make someone vomit or empty their bowels, or to cleanse the body of impurities, especially as a medical treatment

Examples
