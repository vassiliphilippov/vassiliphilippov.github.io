bed check

noun
inspection to confirm people are in bed
2.8
Pronunciation: / bɛd tʃɛk / (AmE), / bɛd tʃɛk / (BrE)
an inspection to ensure that everyone is in bed at the required time, especially in institutions such as the military, dormitories, or camps

Examples

Bed check at night
After the unexpected bed check, no one dared sneak out of the dormitory.
Every night, the sergeant performed a bed check to verify that all soldiers were present.
The counselor made a bed check at 10 p.m. to ensure all the campers were tucked in.
