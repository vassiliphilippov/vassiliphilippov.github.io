biped

adjective
having two feet; walking on two legs
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪ.pɛd / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪ.pɛd / (BrE)
(of an animal) having two feet; walking on two legs.

Examples

Biped movement
Humans are biped creatures, unlike most mammals that move on four legs.
Some robots are designed to be biped so they can walk up stairs easily.
The fossil showed a biped dinosaur that once roamed the ancient forests.

Related Terms

  • antonym: quadruped - an animal having four feet
