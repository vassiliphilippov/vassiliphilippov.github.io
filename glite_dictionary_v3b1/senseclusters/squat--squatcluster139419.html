squat

adjective
short and thickset; built low to ground
Pronunciation: / skwɑt / (AmE), / skwɒt / (BrE)
short and thickset in appearance; low to the ground

Examples

The squat little dog waddled happily after its owner.
The squat old building stood out among the taller apartment blocks.
