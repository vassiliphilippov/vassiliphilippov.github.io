citrus fruit

noun
fruit of the citrus genus, e.g., orange
5.8 1.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪ.trəs fruːt/ (AmE), /ˈsɪ.trəs fruːt/ (BrE)
Any of various fruits of the genus Citrus, such as oranges, lemons, and limes, characterized by a thick rind and juicy pulp, typically grown in warm climates.

Examples

A citrus fruit tree
Eating a citrus fruit every day provides a good source of vitamin C.
She bought a bag filled with different citrus fruits, including grapefruits and oranges.
The refreshing scent of citrus fruit filled the kitchen as he peeled a lemon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lemon - a yellow, oval citrus fruit with acidic juice
  • synonym: lime - a small, green, acidic citrus fruit
  • synonym: orange - a round juicy citrus fruit with a tough bright reddish-yellow rind
