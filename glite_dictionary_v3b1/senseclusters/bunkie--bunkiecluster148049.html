bunkie

noun
mattress support board
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋki / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋki / (BrE)
a thin, flat board or low platform placed under a mattress to provide support, especially on a bed frame without slats

Examples

Bunkie board
We bought a bunkie to make the mattress firmer on our son's bed.
The old bed frame needed a bunkie to support the new mattress properly.
If your bed feels too soft, adding a bunkie can help improve support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bunkie board - a thin board used under a mattress for support
