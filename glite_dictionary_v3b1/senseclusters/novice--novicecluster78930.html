novice

noun
a beginner; new learner
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑvɪs / (AmE), / ˈnɒvɪs / (BrE)
a person who is new to and inexperienced in a particular activity or field

Examples

A novice driver
As a novice in photography, she still has much to learn about using her camera.
It's normal for a novice to make mistakes in the early stages of training.
The club welcomes both novice and experienced climbers to its weekly sessions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner - a person just starting to learn a skill or activity
  • antonym: expert - a person with in-depth knowledge or skill in a particular area
