unloader

noun
pressure-relief device
6.7
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈloʊ.dər/ /; / /ʌn.ˈloʊ.dər/ (AmE), /ən.ˈləʊ.də/ /; / /ʌn.ˈləʊ.də/ (BrE)
a valve or device that relieves pressure or reduces load on a pump or compressor, especially during start-up or shutdown

Examples

Compressor unloader
The technician adjusted the unloader so the air compressor would start without overloading the motor.
If the unloader fails, the pump may not be able to release pressure safely.
Regular maintenance of the unloader ensures the compressor operates efficiently and safely.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compressor - a machine that increases the pressure of a gas
  • synonym: relief valve - a device that releases excess pressure from a system
