death camas

noun
a poisonous plant of the genus Zigadenus
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛθ ˌkæməs / (AmE), / ˈdɛθ ˌkaməs / (BrE)
any of several poisonous plants of the genus Zigadenus, characterized by grass-like leaves and clusters of small white flowers
