ether

noun
cryptocurrency of Ethereum
Pronunciation: / \u02c8i\u02d0\u03b8\u0259r / (AmE), / \u02c8i\u02d0\u03b8\u0259 / (BrE)
The native cryptocurrency (token) of the Ethereum blockchain; a digital asset used to pay transaction fees and to execute smart contracts.

Examples

Buy ether cryptocurrency
She bought ether to deploy a smart contract on the Ethereum network.
Developers pay transaction fees in ether when they interact with decentralized applications.
After the transfer, his wallet showed an increased balance of ether and several new tokens.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cryptocurrency - a digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security
  • synonym: token - a unit of value created on a blockchain, often used for payments or access
