polycyclic

adjective
math group property
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈsaɪklɪk / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
relating to a mathematical group that has a finite subnormal series with cyclic factor groups.

Examples

Polycyclic group
A polycyclic group can be described by a subnormal series whose factors are all cyclic groups.
Researchers studied the properties of polycyclic groups in advanced algebra courses.
The theorem applies only to polycyclic groups, not to all infinite groups.
