extramurally

adverb
outside a hospital
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstrəˈmjʊrəli / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəˈmjʊərəli / (BrE)
in a medical context, occurring or provided outside a hospital, especially for outpatients.

Examples

Extramurally treated patients
Some cancer therapies are now administered extramurally so patients can spend more time at home.
The clinic offers extramurally delivered services for those unable to travel to the hospital.
Doctors coordinated extramurally with local health providers to ensure continuous patient care.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inpatient - a patient who stays in a hospital while receiving treatment
  • synonym: outpatient - a patient who receives treatment without staying in a hospital
