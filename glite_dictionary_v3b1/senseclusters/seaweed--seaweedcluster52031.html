seaweed

noun
marine plant; especially algae
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌwid / (AmE), / ˈsiːwiːd / (BrE)
A plant that grows in the sea, especially large marine algae.

Examples

A pile of seaweed
At low tide, piles of seaweed lined the shore.
Children collected colorful seaweed during their walk along the beach.
Many sushi dishes are wrapped in seaweed for added flavor and nutrition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: algae - simple non-flowering plants that include the seaweeds
