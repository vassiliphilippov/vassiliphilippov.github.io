transorbital

adjective
crossing orbits
Pronunciation: / trænzɔrˈbɪtəl / (AmE), / trænzɔːˈbɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving movement or transfer that crosses from one orbit to another, especially in spaceflight or orbital mechanics

Examples

Transorbital flight
The spacecraft followed a transorbital path to reach its destination beyond Earth's orbit.
Mission planners calculated a transorbital maneuver to transfer the probe to Mars.
A transorbital injection is required to move the satellite into a higher orbit.

  • synonym: orbital - relating to an orbit or orbits
