tearlessly

adverb
without tearing
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛrləsli / (AmE), / ˈtɪələsli / (BrE)
in a way that does not cause tearing or ripping; without being torn.

Examples

Tearlessly remove a sticker
He removed the old photograph tearlessly from the album to scan it.
The label came off tearlessly, leaving no residue on the glass jar.
She managed to unwrap the delicate gift tearlessly, saving the beautiful paper for later use.
