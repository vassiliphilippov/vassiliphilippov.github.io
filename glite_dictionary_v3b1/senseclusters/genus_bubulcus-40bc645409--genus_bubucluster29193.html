genus Bubulcus

noun
genus of small white egrets
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiː.nəs bjuːˈbʌl.kəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiː.nəs bjuːˈbʌl.kəs / (BrE)
A genus of small white egrets, commonly known as cattle egrets, found in warm regions worldwide.

Examples

Unlike other egrets, the genus Bubulcus often feeds alongside cattle and horses.
