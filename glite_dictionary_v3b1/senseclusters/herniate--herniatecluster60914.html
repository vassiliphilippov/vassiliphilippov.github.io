herniate

verb
to bulge through tissue
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɜːrniˌeɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɜːniˌeɪt / (BrE)
to push or bulge through surrounding muscle or tissue, especially when an organ or tissue moves out of its normal place in the body (medical)
