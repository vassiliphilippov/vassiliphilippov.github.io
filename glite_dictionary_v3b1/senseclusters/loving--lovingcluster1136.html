loving

verb
to feel strong affection
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

Loving her new job
She is loving her new job and the friendly team she works with.
They were loving every minute of their vacation by the sea.
He is really loving the sunshine this afternoon.
