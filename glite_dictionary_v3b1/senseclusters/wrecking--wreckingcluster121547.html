wrecking

noun
demolition of a structure
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛkɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of demolishing or destroying a building or structure

Examples

Environmentalists protested the wrecking of the historic bridge.
The wrecking of the old theater made way for a new shopping mall.
