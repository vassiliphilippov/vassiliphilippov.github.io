southeast

noun
compass direction between south and east
1.0 2.06/m
Pronunciation: / Àåsa äŒ∏Ààist /; / Àåsa äŒ∏ÀàiÀêst / (AmE), / Àåsa äŒ∏ÀàiÀêst / (BrE)
the direction on the compass halfway between south and east (at 135 degrees)

Examples

Southeast on the compass
A strong wind blew from the southeast all afternoon.
The sun rises in the southeast during the winter months.
They traveled in the direction of the southeast to reach the next village.
Birds often migrate toward the southeast in autumn.
She checked the map and turned the car to face southeast.
The storm was moving from the southeast toward the coast.

Synonyms

  • SE
    3.1 1.11/m
    SE is the short map label used on signs and charts, while southeast is the full word used in speech and text.
  • southeastward
    3.4 0.02/m
    southeastward is a directional form often showing movement or a path, while southeast names the compass point itself.
