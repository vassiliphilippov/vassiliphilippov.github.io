a hot minute

noun
(idiom) a brief or indefinite time period
Pronunciation: / ə hɑt ˈmɪnɪt / (AmE), / ə hɒt ˈmɪnɪt / (BrE)
an informal expression for a brief or, sometimes ironically, an unexpectedly long period of time

Examples

A hot minute
I haven't seen you in a hot minute; how have you been?
It took a hot minute for my computer to start up this morning.
She returned after a hot minute away from the office, surprising everyone.
