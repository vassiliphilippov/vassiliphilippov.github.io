unrepentance

noun
lack of remorse
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛntəns / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈpɛntəns / (BrE)
the state of not feeling regret or remorse for one's actions or wrongdoing

Examples

His unrepentance shocked
Despite the evidence, his unrepentance shocked the entire community.
The judge noted her unrepentance when deciding the sentence.
His unrepentance made it difficult for others to forgive him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contrition - the state of feeling sorry for bad behavior
  • synonym: defiance - open resistance or bold disobedience
  • antonym: remorse - a deep feeling of regret for wrongdoing
