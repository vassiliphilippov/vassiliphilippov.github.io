olive tree

noun
tree cultivated for olives and oil
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlɪv ˌtriː / (AmE), / ˈɒlɪv ˌtriː / (BrE)
A small evergreen tree of the genus Olea, especially Olea europaea, cultivated for its edible fruit (the olive) and oil.

Examples

An ancient olive tree
An ancient olive tree stood in the center of the village square, providing shade.
The fruit of the olive tree has been harvested for thousands of years in the Mediterranean region.
They planted a young olive tree in the garden to celebrate their anniversary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: olive - the small oval fruit of the olive tree, used for food and oil
