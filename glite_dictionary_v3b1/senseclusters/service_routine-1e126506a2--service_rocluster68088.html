service routine

noun
standard maintenance procedure
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈsɜːr.vɪs ruː.ˈtiːn/ /; / /ˈsɜːr.vəs ruː.ˈtiːn/ (AmE), /ˈsɜː.vɪs ruː.ˈtiːn/ /; / /ˈsɜː.vəs ruː.ˈtiːn/ (BrE)
a standard, scheduled set of maintenance tasks carried out on machinery, vehicles, or equipment to ensure proper operation and prevent failure.

Examples

Vehicle service routine
Every six months the technician follows the service routine to check filters, oil, and safety systems.
The manufacturer’s manual lists a detailed service routine that must be completed before the warranty expires.
To keep the fleet reliable, mechanics perform the scheduled service routine on every vehicle each month.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maintenance procedure - a planned series of tasks to keep equipment in good working order
  • synonym: preventive maintenance - regular maintenance intended to prevent equipment failure before it occurs
