2 Maccabees

proper noun
Apocryphal book recounting the Maccabean revolt
9.9
Pronunciation: / tu ˈmækəˌbiz / (AmE), / tuː ˈmækəˌbiːz / (BrE)
an Apocryphal book of the Bible that describes the life of Judas Maccabeus and the Jews' revolt against Antiochus IV Epiphanes, ending with the defeat of Nicanor in 161 BC.
