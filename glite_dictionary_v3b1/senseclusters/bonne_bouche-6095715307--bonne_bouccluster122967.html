bonne bouche

noun
small delicious morsel of food
Pronunciation: / bɔn ˈbuʃ / (AmE), / bɔn ˈbuːʃ / (BrE)
a small, delicious morsel of food, especially served as a delicacy or appetizer

Examples

A bonne bouche appetizer
Each bonne bouche at the event was more delectable than the last.
The chef presented a bonne bouche before the main meal to excite the guests' palates.
The critic praised the restaurant's inventive bonne bouche that greeted every diner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicacy - a rare or luxurious food item
