lovestruck

adjective
overwhelmed by love
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvˌstrʌk / (AmE), / ˈlʌvˌstrʌk / (BrE)
deeply affected or overwhelmed by romantic feelings for someone

Examples

A lovestruck person
After meeting her, he walked around all day looking completely lovestruck.
The lovestruck teenager wrote poems for his secret crush every night.
She was so lovestruck that she could hardly concentrate on her work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy
  • synonym: infatuated - experiencing an intense but short-lived passion for someone
  • synonym: smitten - suddenly and strongly affected by love
