mesmerization

noun
state of fascination
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛzmərəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌmɛzməraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a state of being completely fascinated, hypnotized, or entranced by something or someone

Examples

Total mesmerization
The magician's performance held the entire audience in a state of mesmerization.
She watched the flickering candlelight with complete mesmerization.
The slow, rhythmic music created a feeling of mesmerization among the listeners.
The dancer's graceful movements caused a sense of mesmerization among the audience.
He listened in mesmerization as the storyteller described the ancient legend.
The children's mesmerization by the colorful display was obvious to everyone in the room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boredom - the state of being uninterested or weary
  • antonym: distraction - something that takes attention away
  • synonym: entrancement - a state of being carried away with wonder or delight
  • synonym: fascination - the state of being intensely interested or attracted
  • synonym: hypnosis - a trance-like state of focused attention and heightened suggestibility
