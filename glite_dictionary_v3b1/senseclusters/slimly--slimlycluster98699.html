slimly

adverb
in a slim or slender way
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪmli / (AmE), / ˈslɪmli / (BrE)
In a slim or slender manner.

Examples

To be slimly built
He was slimly built, with a frame that looked almost delicate.
The dancer spun slimly across the stage, her movements light and graceful.
The model moved slimly through the narrow doorway, barely brushing the sides.
