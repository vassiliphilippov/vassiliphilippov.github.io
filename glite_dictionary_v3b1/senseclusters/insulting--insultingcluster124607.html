insulting

adjective
expressing extreme disrespect or contempt
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈsʌltɪŋ / (BrE)
showing or expressing extreme disrespect or contempt towards someone; offensive or rude.

Examples

Insulting words
He found the suggestion insulting and refused to respond.
Her insulting comments embarrassed everyone in the room.
The comedian's insulting jokes offended several members of the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offensive - causing someone to feel deeply hurt, upset, or angry
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness and honor toward someone
