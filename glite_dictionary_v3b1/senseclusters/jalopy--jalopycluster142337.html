jalopy

noun
old, unreliable car
9.0
Pronunciation: / dʒəˈlɑpi / (AmE), / dʒəˈlɒpi / (BrE)
an old car in bad condition and often unreliable

Examples

An old jalopy
His grandfather still drives the same jalopy he bought fifty years ago.
She laughed as the jalopy sputtered and coughed down the street.
We barely made it up the hill in that rusty old jalopy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clunker - an old car in poor condition
  • synonym: lemon - a car that is defective or constantly gives trouble
