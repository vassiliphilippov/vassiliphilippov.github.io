legal relation

noun
law-regulated professional relationship
1.0 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈliː.gəl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈlɪ.gəl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈliː.gəl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈlɪ.gæl rɪ.ˈleɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a professional relationship that is regulated or defined by law, especially between parties such as a lawyer and a client

Examples

A legal relation with client
The confidentiality rules apply only if a legal relation exists between the client and the attorney.
There is a legal relation between a tenant and a landlord, established through a lease agreement.
When two parties enter into a contract, a legal relation is created and governed by the terms of the agreement.
