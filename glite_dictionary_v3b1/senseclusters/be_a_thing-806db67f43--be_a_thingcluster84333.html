be a thing

verb
become popular or common; trend
5.4
Pronunciation: / bi ə θɪŋ / (AmE), / biː ə θɪŋ / (BrE)
to become popular, common, or widely recognized, especially as a trend or phenomenon

Examples

Be a thing in fashion
Bell-bottom jeans used to be a thing in the seventies.
Do you think working from home will still be a thing next year?
I didn't know oat milk would be a thing so soon.

Related Terms

  • synonym: go viral - to become very popular very quickly online
