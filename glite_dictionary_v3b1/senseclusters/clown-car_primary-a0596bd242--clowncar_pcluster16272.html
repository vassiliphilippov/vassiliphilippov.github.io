clown-car primary

noun

Pronunciation: /ˈklaʊn.kɑːr ˈpraɪ.mə.riː/ /; / /ˈklaʊn.kɑːr ˈpraɪ.mɛr.iː/ (AmE), /ˈklaʊn.kɑː ˈpraɪ.mə.riː/ /; / /ˈklaʊn.kɑː ˈpraɪ.mɛr.iː/ (BrE)
