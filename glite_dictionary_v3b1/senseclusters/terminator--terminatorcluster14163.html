terminator

noun
something that ends
Pronunciation: / ˈtɜːrməˌneɪtər / (AmE), / ˈtɜːmɪneɪtə / (BrE)
a person or thing that brings something to an end or causes it to stop

Examples

Process terminator
The software terminator automatically closes unused programs to save memory.
As the project terminator, she was responsible for shutting down all operations at the end.
A special terminator is used in electronics to signal the end of a data line.
The project manager acted as the terminator when she officially closed the program.
A timer can serve as a terminator for the experiment, stopping it at the right moment.
He was known as the terminator of unproductive meetings in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: finisher - a person or thing that completes or ends something
  • antonym: initiator - a person or thing that begins something
