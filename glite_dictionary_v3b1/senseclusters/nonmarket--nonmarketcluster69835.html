nonmarket

noun
activities outside markets
7.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈmɑːr.kɪt/ /; / /ˈnɑːn.mɑːr.kɪt/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈmɑː.kɪt/ /; / /ˈnɒn.mɑː.kɪt/ (BrE)
the part of an economy or activities not involving buying and selling in markets, such as household work or public services

Examples

Nonmarket economy
Volunteering at a local shelter is considered part of the nonmarket economy.
Household chores like cooking and cleaning are examples of nonmarket activities.
Many public services, such as education and healthcare, belong to the nonmarket sector.

Related Terms

  • synonym: informal economy - economic activities not regulated by the government or taxed
  • antonym: market - the system or place where goods and services are bought and sold
