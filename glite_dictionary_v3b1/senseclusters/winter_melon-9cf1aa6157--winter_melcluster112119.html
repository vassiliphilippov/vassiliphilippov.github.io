winter melon

noun
a type of large, pale-fleshed melon
6.4 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntɚ ˌmɛlən /; / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌmɛl.ən / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌmɛlən /; / ˈwɪn.tə ˌmɛl.ən / (BrE)
a large, round or oblong fruit with smooth green or pale skin and pale flesh, commonly used in Asian cooking; also, the plant that produces this fruit

Examples

A winter melon fruit
Winter melon can be cooked or candied for use in desserts and drinks.

After harvesting, winter melon can be stored for several months without spoiling.
