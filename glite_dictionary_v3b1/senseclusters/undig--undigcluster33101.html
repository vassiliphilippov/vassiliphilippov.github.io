undig

verb
to reverse digging
9.9
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈdɪg/ /; / /ə.ˈdɪg/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈdɪg/ /; / /ə.ˈdɪg/ (BrE)
to remove earth or material that was previously dug, or to reverse the process of digging.

Examples

To undig a hole

When the pipe was found in the wrong place, workers needed to undig the area to fix it.
