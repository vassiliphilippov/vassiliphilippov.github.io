good story

noun
amusing or entertaining anecdote
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈstɔːri / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
an entertaining, amusing, or interesting story or account of events (sometimes used sarcastically to express disbelief)

Examples

A good story told
He told a good story about his adventures in college that made everyone laugh.
The meeting was dull until someone broke the ice with a good story.
Whenever my uncle comes over, he always has a good story to share.
"Oh, that's a good story," she replied, raising an eyebrow in disbelief.
He kept everyone at the table entertained with a good story about his travels in India.
Sometimes, a good story is all you need to lighten the mood.
