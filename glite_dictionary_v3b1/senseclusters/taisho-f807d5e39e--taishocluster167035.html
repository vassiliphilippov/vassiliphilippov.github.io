Taisho

noun
Japanese military rank
Pronunciation: / taɪˈʃoʊ / (AmE), / taɪˈʃəʊ / (BrE)
a high-ranking Japanese military officer, equivalent to a general or admiral

Examples

A Taisho officer
He was promoted to Taisho after many years of distinguished service in the army.
The Taisho led his troops with great skill during the decisive battle.
During the ceremony, the veteran was honored with the title of Taisho for his leadership.

Related Terms

  • synonym: admiral - a senior naval officer
  • synonym: general - a senior army officer
