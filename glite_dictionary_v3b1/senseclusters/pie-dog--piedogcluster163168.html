pie-dog

noun
ownerless mongrel dog in Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ dɒɡ / (AmE), / ˈpaɪ dɒɡ / (BrE)
An ownerless, often half-wild mongrel dog commonly found around villages in Asia, especially India.

Examples

A pie-dog in village
At dusk, a lonely pie-dog wandered through the dusty lanes searching for scraps.
He threw a stick towards a pie-dog that was rummaging near the market stalls.
The pie-dogs are a common sight in many Indian towns, living off what they can find.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mongrel - a dog of no definable type or breed
  • synonym: pariah dog - a wild or semi-feral dog found in India and Southeast Asia
