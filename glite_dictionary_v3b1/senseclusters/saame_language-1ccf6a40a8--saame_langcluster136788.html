Saame language

noun
language spoken by the Sámi people
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑː.meɪ ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈsɑː.meɪ ˈlaŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the language or group of related languages spoken by the Sámi people in northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula

Examples

The Saame language family
Linguists consider the Saame language an important part of the Uralic language family.
Several dialects of the Saame language are spoken across Norway, Sweden, Finland, and Russia.
The revitalization of the Saame language is crucial for Sámi cultural identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Lapp - historical, now considered offensive, term for the Sámi people
  • synonym: Sámi - indigenous people inhabiting northern Scandinavia and the Kola Peninsula
