break

noun
ending of a relationship or association
Pronunciation: / breɪk / (AmE), / breɪk / (BrE)
an ending of a relationship or association between people or groups, often involving disagreement or separation

Examples

A break in relations
A serious break occurred in diplomatic relations after the incident.
After years of close cooperation, there was a sudden break in their friendship.
The prolonged disagreement led to a break between the two former partners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: separation - the act of moving apart or being kept apart
  • synonym: split - a division or break between groups or people
  • antonym: union - the act of joining or uniting people or things
