totes

noun
large carry bags
2.5
Pronunciation: / toʊts / (AmE), / təʊts / (BrE)
large open bags with handles, used for carrying items such as groceries or books; plural of tote

Examples

Many people use reusable totes instead of plastic bags when shopping.
