roasting

noun
public humorous mockery
5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.stɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.stɪŋ/ (BrE)
The act or event of humorously mocking, teasing, or satirizing a person (often a celebrity) in front of an audience for entertainment.

Examples

A celebrity roasting
The charity event included a roasting of the retired actor, full of playful jokes.
She felt nervous before the roasting where colleagues would tease her on stage.
Late-night shows often feature celebrity roasting that mixes insult and affectionate humor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mockery - the act of making fun of someone or something, often by imitation or insult
  • synonym: roast - an event or performance in which a person is humorously mocked by friends or comedians
