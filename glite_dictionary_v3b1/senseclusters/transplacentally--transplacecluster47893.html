transplacentally

adverb
through the placenta
Pronunciation: / ˌtrænzpləˈsɛntəli / (AmE), / ˌtrænspləˈsɛntəli / (BrE)
by way of or through the placenta, especially from mother to fetus during pregnancy

Examples

Pass transplacentally antibodies
Certain antibodies can pass transplacentally to provide immunity to the fetus.
The virus may be transmitted transplacentally during the later stages of pregnancy.
Doctors monitor for diseases that could spread transplacentally from mother to child.

  • synonym: placentally - by means of the placenta
