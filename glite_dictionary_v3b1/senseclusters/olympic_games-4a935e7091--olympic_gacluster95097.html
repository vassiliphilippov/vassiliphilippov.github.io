Olympic Games

noun
international multi-sport event held every four years
Pronunciation: / əˈlɪmpɪk ɡeɪmz / (AmE), / əˈlɪmpɪk ɡeɪmz / (BrE)
an international sports competition held every four years in which athletes from many countries participate in a variety of sporting events

Examples

Modern Olympic Games
Athletes around the world train for years to compete in the Olympic Games.
The Olympic Games unite nations through the spirit of sportsmanship and friendly competition.
The city was honored to be chosen as the host of the Olympic Games.
