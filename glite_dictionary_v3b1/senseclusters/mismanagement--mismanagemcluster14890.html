mismanagement

noun
careless or inefficient management
1.0 4.3
Pronunciation: /mɪs.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /mɪs.ˈmæn.ədʒ.mənt/ (AmE), /mɪs.ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/ /; / /mɪs.ˈmæn.ədʒ.mənt/ (BrE)

Examples

Poor mismanagement of resources

The company suffered huge losses due to financial mismanagement at the highest level.
Years of mismanagement left the hospital in crisis.
