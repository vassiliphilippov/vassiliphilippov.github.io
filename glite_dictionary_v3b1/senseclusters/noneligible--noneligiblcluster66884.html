noneligible

adjective
not qualified
5.8
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈɛlɪdʒəbəl / (BrE)
not meeting the necessary requirements to participate or qualify for something

Examples

Noneligible for scholarship
Students who are noneligible for the scholarship should not submit an application.
The company rejected all noneligible bids during the selection process.
If you do not meet the age requirement, you are considered noneligible for this contest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ineligible - not allowed or qualified to do or receive something
