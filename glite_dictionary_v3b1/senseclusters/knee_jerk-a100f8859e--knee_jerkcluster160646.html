knee jerk

noun
automatic leg reflex caused by knee tap
4.3 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ni d í…úrk / (AmE), / niÀê d í…úÀêk / (BrE)
an involuntary reflex in which the lower leg suddenly kicks out after the patellar tendon just below the knee is sharply tapped

Examples

A knee jerk reflex
A sharp tap below the kneecap usually triggers a noticeable knee jerk.
Her knee jerk was normal, indicating healthy nerve function.
The doctor tested the knee jerk response during the physical examination.

Synonyms

  • knee-jerk reflex
    3.9 0.00/m
    knee-jerk reflex names the sudden automatic extension of the lower leg, while knee jerk is a shorter, informal term.
  • patellar reflex
    6.0 0.01/m
    patellar reflex is the medical name used in exams and reports, while knee jerk is the everyday phrase people often use.
