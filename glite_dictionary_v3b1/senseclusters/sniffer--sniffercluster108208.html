sniffer

noun
a person who sniffs
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪfər / (AmE), / ˈsnɪfə / (BrE)
a person who sniffs, especially habitually or in a noticeable way.

Examples

A noisy sniffer

Some dogs are trained as sniffers to detect illegal substances, but the term can also refer to people.
