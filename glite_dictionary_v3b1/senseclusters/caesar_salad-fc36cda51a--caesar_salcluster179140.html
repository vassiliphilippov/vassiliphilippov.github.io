Caesar salad

noun
salad with romaine, croutons, and rich dressing
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːzər ˈsæləd / (AmE), / ˈsiːzə ˈsaləd / (BrE)
a salad, usually consisting of romaine lettuce, fried croutons, and a dressing made with raw egg, garlic, anchovies, olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, and Parmesan cheese

Examples

A Caesar salad dish
He ordered a Caesar salad with his grilled chicken at the café.
The Caesar salad was topped with plenty of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
Would you like a Caesar salad as a starter or a main course?
