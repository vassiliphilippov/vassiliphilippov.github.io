human ecology

noun
sociological study of humans and their environments
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌhjuːmən ɪˈkɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌhjuːmən ɪˈkɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of sociology that studies the relationships between human populations and their environments

Examples

Human ecology studies
A degree in human ecology offers insights into sustainable development and population dynamics.
She is conducting research in human ecology to understand how urban environments influence social behavior.
The conference covered various topics related to human ecology, including migration and resource management.
