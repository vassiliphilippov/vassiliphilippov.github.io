heartfully

adverb
sincerely; with feeling
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrtfəli / (AmE), / ˈhɑːtfəli / (BrE)
in a heartfelt, sincere, or enthusiastic way; showing genuine emotion or strong feeling

Examples

To heartfully thank
She heartfully expressed her gratitude to everyone who helped her during the crisis.
The teacher heartfully congratulated the students on their achievements at the ceremony.
He apologized heartfully for his mistake, hoping to make amends.
The children sang heartfully at the school concert, filling the room with joy.
She thanked her supporters heartfully after winning the award.
He cheered heartfully for his favorite team during the championship game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiastically - in a way that shows excitement or eager interest
  • antonym: halfheartedly - in a way that lacks enthusiasm or energy
  • antonym: heartlessly - in a cruel or unfeeling manner
  • synonym: sincerely - in a genuine and honest way
