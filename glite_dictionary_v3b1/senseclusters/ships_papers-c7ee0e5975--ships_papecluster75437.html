ship's papers

noun
legal documents required on a ship
5.3
Pronunciation: / ʃɪps ˈpeɪpərz / (AmE), / ʃɪps ˈpeɪpəz / (BrE)
official documents that a ship must carry by law, providing details about ownership, cargo, crew, and voyage
