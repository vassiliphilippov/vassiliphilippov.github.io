lunch

noun
midday meal
1.9
Pronunciation: / lʌntʃ / (AmE), / lʌntʃ / (BrE)
a meal eaten in the middle of the day

Examples

A quick lunch
Business meetings are often scheduled over lunch to save time.
She packed a sandwich and fruit for her lunch today.
We usually have lunch together at noon in the cafeteria.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breakfast - the first meal of the day, eaten in the morning
  • synonym: dinner - the main meal of the day, often eaten in the evening
  • degree lower: snack - a small amount of food eaten between meals
