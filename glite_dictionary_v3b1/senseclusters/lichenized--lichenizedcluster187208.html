lichenized

adjective
part of a lichen
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈlɪkənaɪzd / (BrE)
describing a fungus that lives in a symbiotic relationship as part of a lichen

Examples

Lichenized fungus species
Many lichenized fungi can survive in harsh environments.
The lichenized state allows the fungus to obtain nutrients from its photosynthetic partner.
Researchers study lichenized organisms to understand how they adapt to extreme climates.
