sweetness

noun
pleasant sugary taste
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswitnəs /; / ˈswit.nəs / (AmE), / ˈswiːtnɪs /; / ˈswiːt.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of having a sweet, sugary taste

Examples

The sweetness of honey

She added honey to her tea to increase its sweetness.
The sweetness of ripe mangoes is irresistible in summer.
This dessert lacks sweetness and tastes rather bland.
