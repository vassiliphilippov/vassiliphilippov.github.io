spring-flowering

adjective
flowering in spring
4.1
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ ˈflaʊɚɪŋ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ ˈflaʊərɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) producing flowers in the spring season.

Examples

Spring-flowering plants
Gardeners love spring-flowering shrubs for their vibrant early season colors.
Many spring-flowering bulbs, such as tulips and daffodils, are planted in autumn.
The park was filled with spring-flowering trees in full bloom.
