hair drier

noun
electric device for drying hair
Pronunciation: / hɛr ˈdraɪ.ər / (AmE), / heə ˈdraɪ.ə / (BrE)
a hand-held electric device that blows warm air to dry and style hair

Examples

A hair drier device
After washing her hair, she used a hair drier to style it quickly before work.

The hotel room included a complimentary hair drier in the bathroom.
