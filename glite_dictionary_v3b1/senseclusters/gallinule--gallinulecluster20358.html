gallinule

noun
small aquatic bird of the genus Gallinula
Pronunciation: / ˈɡælɪˌnul / (AmE), / ˈɡalɪnjuːl / (BrE)
a small aquatic bird of the genus Gallinula, distinguished by a frontal shield and resembling a domestic hen; found in marshy habitats

Examples

A gallinule bird
Many birdwatchers come here hoping to see a rare species of gallinule.
The young gallinule ventured out from its hiding place in search of food.
We spotted a brightly colored gallinule gliding across the marsh among the reeds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moorhen - another name for the common gallinule (Gallinula chloropus)
  • degree lower: rail - related waterbirds of the family Rallidae
