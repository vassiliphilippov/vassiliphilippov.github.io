affluent

noun
wealthy person
Pronunciation: / ˈæfluənt / (AmE), / ˈæfluənt / (BrE)
a person who has a great deal of money and is financially well off

Examples

An affluent investor
Many affluents are investing in sustainable housing projects.
Programs are designed to appeal to both the middle class and affluents.
The new marketing strategy targets urban affluents seeking luxury brands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: poor - lacking sufficient money to live at a standard considered comfortable or normal in society
  • synonym: rich - having a lot of money or valuable possessions
