ping

verb
contact someone briefly, often as a reminder
Pronunciation: / pɪŋ / (AmE), / pɪŋ / (BrE)
to contact someone briefly, usually by electronic message or call, often as a reminder

Examples

To ping a friend
Can you ping me when the package has been delivered?
He pinged his colleague a quick message before the meeting started.
I'll ping you later to see if you've finished the report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: message - to send a written or electronic communication
  • synonym: remind - to cause someone to remember something
