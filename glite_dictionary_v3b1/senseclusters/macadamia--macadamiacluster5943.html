macadamia

noun
edible nut
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈdeɪmiə / (AmE), / ˌmækəˈdeɪmiə / (BrE)
a round, edible nut with a rich, buttery flavor, often used in baking and snacks

Examples

Macadamia nut

A macadamia cookie is one of my favorite treats during the holidays.
The chef added roasted macadamia to the salad for a buttery flavor.
