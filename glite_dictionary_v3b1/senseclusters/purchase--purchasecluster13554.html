purchase

noun
the act of buying
1.1 16/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀêt É…ôs / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀêt É…ôs / (BrE)
the act of buying something, especially something that has been paid for

Examples

A purchase receipt

Synonyms

    4.6 14/m
