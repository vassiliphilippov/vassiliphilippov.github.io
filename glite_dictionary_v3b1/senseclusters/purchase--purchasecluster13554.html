purchase

noun
the act of buying
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝːtʃəs / (AmE), / ˈpɜːtʃəs / (BrE)
the act of buying something, especially something that has been paid for

Examples

A purchase receipt
Customers will receive a receipt after each purchase is completed.
She regretted her purchase once she saw the item on sale.
The purchase of the new car was a major decision for the family.

  • antonym: sale - the exchange of a product or service for money from the seller's perspective
