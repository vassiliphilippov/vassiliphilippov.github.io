atonic

adjective
lacking muscular tone
Pronunciation: / əˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / əˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
(of a muscle or tissue) lacking normal muscular tone or strength; having decreased or absent tonus.

Examples

Atonic muscle
In atonic bladder conditions, the organ fails to contract efficiently.
The newborn exhibited atonic limbs, indicating possible neurological issues.
The patient's arm muscles became noticeably atonic after the stroke.
