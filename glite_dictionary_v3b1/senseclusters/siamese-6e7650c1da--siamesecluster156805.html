Siamese

noun
a breed of slender, blue-eyed cat
Pronunciation: / saɪəˈmiz / (AmE), / saɪəˈmiːz / (BrE)
a breed of slender, short-haired cat with blue eyes and a pale coat marked with darker coloring on the ears, face, paws, and tail

Examples

Siamese cat
At the cat show, her Siamese won first place in its category.
I've always wanted a Siamese because of their playful and vocal personalities.
The Siamese is known for its striking blue eyes and elegant build.
