sacred ibis

noun
African wading bird venerated by Egyptians
8.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪ.krəd ˈaɪ.bɪs / (AmE), / ˈseɪ.krɪd ˈaɪ.bɪs / (BrE)
