nonwashable

adjective
not able to be washed
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈwɑʃəbəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈwɒʃəbəl / (BrE)
(of a fabric or object) not able to be washed safely with water or cleaning agents.

Examples

Nonwashable fabric
Please do not put nonwashable items in the washing machine, as they may be damaged.
Some paints are nonwashable and will leave permanent stains on fabric.
The sweater was labeled nonwashable, so I had to take it to the dry cleaner.
