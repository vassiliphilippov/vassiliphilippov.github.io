unarmed

adjective
not carrying weapons
3.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɑrmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɑːmd / (BrE)
not carrying or equipped with weapons.

Examples

Unarmed person
He walked into the meeting unarmed, trusting everyone there.
The police found the suspect was unarmed when they arrested him.
The protestors marched unarmed to show they meant no harm.
