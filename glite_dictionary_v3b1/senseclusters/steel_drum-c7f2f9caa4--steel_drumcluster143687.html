steel drum

noun
Caribbean percussion instrument made from an oil drum
2.6 3.2
Pronunciation: /stiːl drʌm/ (AmE), /stiːl drʌm/ (BrE)
a percussion instrument from the Caribbean, made from a metal oil drum with flattened areas that produce different tones when struck

Examples

Play the steel drum
She learned to play the steel drum while visiting Trinidad.
The band played lively tunes on the steel drum during the festival.
You can hear the unique sound of the steel drum in many Caribbean songs.
