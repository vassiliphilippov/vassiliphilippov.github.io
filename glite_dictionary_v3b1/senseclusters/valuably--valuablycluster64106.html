valuably

adverb
in a valuable way
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈvæljuəbli / (AmE), / ˈvæljuəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is useful, important, or of great worth

Examples

To valuably contribute
She valuably contributed to the team's success by sharing her expertise.
His advice was valuably received during the critical meeting.
The volunteers valuably assisted in organizing the community event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: importantly - in a way that is significant or of great importance
  • synonym: usefully - in a way that is helpful or serves a purpose
  • antonym: worthlessly - in a way that lacks value or usefulness
