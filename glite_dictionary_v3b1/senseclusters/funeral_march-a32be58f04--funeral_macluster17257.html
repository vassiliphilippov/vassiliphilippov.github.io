funeral march

noun
slow music for a funeral procession
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuː.nɚl mɑːrtʃ / (AmE), / ˈfjuː.nərəl mɑːtʃ / (BrE)
a slow, solemn piece of music composed or played for a funeral procession

Examples
