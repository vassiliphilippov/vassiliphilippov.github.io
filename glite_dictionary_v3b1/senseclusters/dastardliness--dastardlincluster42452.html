dastardliness

noun
contemptible cowardice
Pronunciation: / ˈdæstərdliːnəs / (AmE), / ˈdɑːstədliːnəs / (BrE)
The quality of being cowardly in a contemptible or despicable way.

Examples

The dastardliness of betrayal
Everyone in the village condemned the dastardliness of his betrayal.
Her dastardliness knew no limits when she abandoned her friends in need.
Historians debated the dastardliness of the general's actions during the war.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravery - courageous behavior or character
  • synonym: cowardice - lack of courage
