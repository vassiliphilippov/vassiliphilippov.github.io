Dobsonian

noun
telescope mount design
Pronunciation: / dɑbˈsoʊniən / (AmE), / dɒbˈsəʊniən / (BrE)
a simple, portable reflecting telescope or its mount, designed for easy use and construction, popular among amateur astronomers

Related Terms

  • synonym: alt-azimuth mount - a telescope mount that moves in altitude and azimuth
  • antonym: equatorial mount - a telescope mount aligned with Earth's axis for tracking stars
  • synonym: reflector - a telescope that uses mirrors to gather and focus light
  • antonym: refractor - a telescope that uses lenses to gather and focus light
