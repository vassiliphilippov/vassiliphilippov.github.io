vermilion

noun
vivid red or orange-red color
6.4
Pronunciation: / vərˈmɪliən / (AmE), / vəˈmɪliən / (BrE)
A bright red or scarlet color, sometimes tinged with orange.

Examples

A vermilion color
She wore a vermilion dress that captivated everyone's attention at the party.
The artist used a striking shade of vermilion to highlight the sunset in her painting.
Traditional Chinese temples are often painted in vibrant vermilion hues.
