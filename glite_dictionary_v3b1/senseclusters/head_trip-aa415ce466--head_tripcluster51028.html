head trip

noun
an act of self-gratification or pleasure
1.4
Pronunciation: / hɛd trɪp / (AmE), / hɛd trɪp / (BrE)
an experience that is intense, unusual, or stimulating for the mind, often involving deep thought or strong emotions

Examples

A head trip experience
For him, climbing that mountain was more of a head trip than a physical challenge.
She dismissed the retreat as just another head trip for self-absorbed people.
The artist said making music is a head trip, something he does for his own pleasure.
That movie was a real head trip—I couldn't stop thinking about it for days.
The new virtual reality game was such a head trip that it felt completely immersive.
Traveling alone through the jungle turned out to be an incredible head trip for her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ego trip - an activity undertaken mainly to increase one's own sense of self-importance or pleasure
  • antonym: letdown - a disappointing experience; an event less exciting than expected
  • synonym: rush - a sudden feeling of excitement or exhilaration
