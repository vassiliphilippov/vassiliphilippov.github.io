poultry

noun
meat of domesticated birds
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊltri / (AmE), / ˈpəʊltri / (BrE)
the meat or flesh of chickens, turkeys, ducks, or geese that are raised for food

Examples

Poultry meat
Consuming undercooked poultry can lead to foodborne illnesses.
There are strict regulations on the storage and handling of poultry in restaurants.
We bought fresh poultry from the farmer's market for dinner.
