bright yellow

adjective
vivid, intense yellow color
1.0
Pronunciation: / braɪt ˈjɛloʊ / (AmE), / braɪt ˈjɛləʊ / (BrE)
a vivid and intense shade of yellow, often used to describe objects or colors that are strikingly yellow and highly visible

Examples

The artist chose bright yellow for the sunflowers to make the painting more lively.
