hypophyseal stalk

noun
narrow structure linking pituitary gland and hypothalamus
Pronunciation: / haɪˌpɑfəˌsiəl stɒk / (AmE), / haɪˌpɒfɪˌsiːəl stɔːk / (BrE)
the slender, funnel-shaped structure that connects the pituitary gland to the hypothalamus in the brain

Examples

The hypophyseal stalk connects brain
Damage to the hypophyseal stalk can disrupt hormonal communication between the brain and the pituitary gland.
MRI imaging revealed a lesion near the hypophyseal stalk causing endocrine disturbances.
The neurosurgeon carefully preserved the hypophyseal stalk during the procedure to maintain hormonal balance.
