Cyathea medullaris

noun
tall tree fern of New Zealand and Australia
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪˈiːθi.ə mɛdʊˈlɛrɪs / (AmE), / saɪˈæθɪə mɛdʊˈlɑːrɪs / (BrE)
