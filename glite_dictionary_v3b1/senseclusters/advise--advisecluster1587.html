Examples

To advise a friend

She often advises her friends on financial matters.

Synonyms

  • counsel
    4.0 6.48/m
    counsel gives formal guidance often on personal or legal problems, while advise is general guidance about what someone should do.
  • recommend
    2.1 89/m
    recommend suggests a good or suitable choice and often encourages it, while advise means giving overall guidance about what to do.
  • suggest
    1.6 151/m
    suggest offers an idea or plan for someone to consider, while advise gives fuller guidance about what they should do.
