shot put

noun
athletics event: throwing a heavy ball
Pronunciation: / ʃɑt pʊt / (AmE), / ʃɒt pʊt / (BrE)
a track and field event in which competitors throw a heavy spherical object as far as possible

Examples

The shot put event
He trained for months to qualify for the Olympic shot put final.
She broke the national record in the women's shot put at the championship.
The shot put requires both strength and precise technique.
