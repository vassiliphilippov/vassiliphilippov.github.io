clinic

noun
outpatient healthcare facility
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈklɪ.nɪk/ (AmE), /ˈklɪ.nɪk/ (BrE)
a healthcare facility where people receive medical treatment or advice without staying overnight

Examples

A medical clinic
After the accident, she received treatment at a local clinic.

Many people without health insurance rely on free neighborhood clinics for medical care.
She went to the local clinic for a routine check-up.
The hospital runs a special clinic for diabetes patients every Wednesday.
