wokewash attempt

noun
superficial display of social justice concern
Pronunciation: / ˈwoʊkˌwɑʃ əˈtɛmpt / (AmE), / ˈwəʊkˌwɒʃ əˈtɛmpt / (BrE)
an action or campaign that superficially displays concern for social issues while misrepresenting or exaggerating actual commitment to social justice

Examples

A wokewash attempt
Activists urged the university to move beyond mere wokewash attempts and address systemic inequalities.
Some brands engage in wokewash attempts to attract socially conscious consumers without meaningful change.
The corporation's diversity campaign was dismissed as a wokewash attempt by critics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: authentic activism - genuine, committed advocacy for social justice
  • synonym: greenwash attempt - superficial engagement with environmental issues for profit or PR
