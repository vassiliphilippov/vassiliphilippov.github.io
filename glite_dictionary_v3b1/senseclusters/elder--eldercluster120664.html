elder

noun
senior church official
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛldɚ / (AmE), / ˈɛldə / (BrE)
a senior member or official in certain Christian churches, often responsible for spiritual oversight and leadership within the congregation.

Examples

Church elder
She was recently appointed as a elder in her local Presbyterian church.
The elder presided over the weekly church meeting and guided the congregation.
The board of elders discussed important matters concerning the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deacon - a church officer who assists in ministry and service
  • synonym: pastor - a minister in charge of a Christian church or congregation
