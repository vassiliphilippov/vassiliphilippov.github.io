maverick

adjective
independent-minded person
Pronunciation: / ˈmævərɪk / (AmE), / ˈmævərɪk / (BrE)
an independent person who has different ideas and behaviors from others, especially one who resists adhering to established rules or traditions

She has always been considered a maverick in the scientific community.
The company values maverick thinkers who challenge the status quo.
