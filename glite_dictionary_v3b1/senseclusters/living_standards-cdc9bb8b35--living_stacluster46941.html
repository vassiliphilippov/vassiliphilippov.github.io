living standards

noun
level of material comfort and wealth
1.0 3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌstændɚdz / (AmE), / ˈlɪvɪŋ ˌstændədz / (BrE)
The level of material comfort and wealth available to a person, group, or society, measured by the quantity and quality of goods and services they can access.

Examples
