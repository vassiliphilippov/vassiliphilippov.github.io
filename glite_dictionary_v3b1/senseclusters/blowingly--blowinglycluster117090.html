blowingly

adverb
extremely impressively
Pronunciation: / ˈbloʊɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈbləʊɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely surprising, impressive, or overwhelming

Examples

Blowingly impressive view
The view from the mountain was blowingly beautiful and left us speechless.
His performance was so blowingly good that the audience gave a standing ovation.
She solved the puzzle in a blowingly clever way that surprised everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amazingly - in a way that causes great surprise or wonder
  • synonym: incredibly - in a way that is hard to believe; extremely
