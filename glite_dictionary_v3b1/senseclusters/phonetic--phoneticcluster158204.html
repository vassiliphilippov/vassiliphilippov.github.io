phonetic

adjective
relating to speech sounds
3.7
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɛtɪk / (AmE), / fəˈnɛtɪk / (BrE)
relating to the sounds of human speech or the study and representation of these sounds

Examples

A phonetic alphabet
A phonetic alphabet represents the sounds of spoken language.
She is studying phonetic transcription in her linguistics class.
The dictionary includes phonetic symbols for pronunciation guidance.
He is interested in the phonetic differences between English and Spanish.
Students practiced writing words in the phonetic alphabet to improve pronunciation.
The linguist conducted a phonetic analysis of regional accents.

  • synonym: phonological - relating to the systematic organization of sounds in languages
