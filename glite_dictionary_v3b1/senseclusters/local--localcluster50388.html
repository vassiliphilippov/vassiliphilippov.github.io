local

adjective
affecting a restricted area
2.6 1.9
Pronunciation: /ˈloʊ.kəl/ (AmE), /ˈləʊ.kəl/ (BrE)
relating to or affecting only a particular part or area, especially of the body.

Examples

Local pain
She experienced only local side effects around the injection site.
The dentist administered a local anesthetic before filling the cavity.
The infection appears to be local and hasn't spread to other organs.
