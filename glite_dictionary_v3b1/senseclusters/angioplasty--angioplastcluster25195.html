Synonyms

  • PTA
    5.0 0.08/m
    PTA is a balloon procedure that widens a narrowed artery, while angioplasty is the medical method to repair or unblock vessels with a balloon.
  • PCI
    5.1 0.23/m
    PCI uses a catheter and often a stent to open coronary arteries, while angioplasty is mainly about unblocking vessels with a balloon.
