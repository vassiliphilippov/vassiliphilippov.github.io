ubuntu

noun
African humanist philosophy
8.6
Pronunciation: /uː.ˈbuːn.tuː/ /; / /ʊ.ˈbʊn.tuː/ (AmE), /ʊ.ˈbʊn.tuː/ /; / /uː.ˈbuːn.tuː/ (BrE)
a philosophy from southern Africa that emphasizes kindness, humanity, and the importance of community and mutual support

Examples

Ubuntu philosophy
The principle of ubuntu teaches that our humanity is tied to the humanity of others.
Leaders in the community often refer to ubuntu when encouraging cooperation and mutual respect.
Practicing ubuntu means helping others and recognizing our shared connection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: individualism - the belief in the importance of individual independence and self-reliance
