earthshine

noun
moonlit by Earth
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθˌʃaɪn / (AmE), / ˈɜːθˌʃaɪn / (BrE)
the faint light visible on the dark part of the moon, caused by sunlight reflected from Earth

Examples

Earthshine on moon
On clear nights, you can sometimes see earthshine illuminating the moon's dark side.
Astronomers study earthshine to learn about Earth's atmosphere and reflectivity.
The crescent moon was outlined by a soft earthshine after sunset.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moonlight - the light that comes from the moon
