yawning

adjective
extremely wide open; gaping
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
very wide open, especially suggesting an opening that could engulf or swallow something; gaping.

Examples

A yawning gap
A yawning hole in the road forced drivers to take a detour.
He stood on the edge of the yawning pit, feeling dizzy from its depth.
The climbers peered into the yawning chasm below them.
