gangbuster

noun
very successful person
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæŋˌbʌstər / (AmE), / ˈɡæŋˌbʌstə / (BrE)

Examples

Gangbuster success
The new smartphone was a gangbuster in the market, selling out within hours.
Her performance in the play was a real gangbuster and received standing ovations.
After months of hard work, their project turned out to be a gangbuster for the whole team.
