flood of visitors

noun
large influx of visitors
3.0
Pronunciation: / flʌd əv ˈvɪzɪtɚz / (AmE), / flʌd əv ˈvɪzɪtəz / (BrE)
a very large number of people arriving at the same place, especially within a short period of time

Examples

A flood of visitors
A sudden flood of visitors overwhelmed the small coastal town during the festival.
Staff worked overtime to manage the flood of visitors arriving for the concert.
The museum experienced a flood of visitors after the new exhibit was announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: throng - a large, densely packed crowd of people
  • antonym: trickle of visitors - a very small, slow number of visitors
