hipbone

noun
large bone forming half of the pelvis
1.0 3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪpˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɪp.bəʊn / (BrE)
the large, flaring bone forming one half of the pelvis, made up of the ilium, ischium, and pubis
