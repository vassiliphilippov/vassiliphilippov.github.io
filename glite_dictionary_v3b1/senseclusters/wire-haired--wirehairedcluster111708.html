wire-haired

adjective
having stiff, coarse hair (esp. dogs)
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪɚˌhɛrd / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəˌhɛəd / (BrE)
(especially of dogs) having stiff, coarse, and wiry hair.

Examples

Wire-haired dog
A wire-haired coat requires regular grooming to prevent matting.
She adopted a wire-haired dachshund from the animal shelter last year.
The wire-haired terrier is known for its energetic personality and distinctive appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: smooth-haired - (esp. of dogs) having smooth, short hair
