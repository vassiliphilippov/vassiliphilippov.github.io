unstuffy

adjective
fresh or well-ventilated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstʌfi / (AmE), / ʌnˈstʌfi / (BrE)
not stuffy; pleasantly fresh, well-ventilated, or free from mustiness or congestion.

Examples

Unstuffy air
The newly renovated office felt unstuffy and comfortable, even on a hot day.
After her cold improved, her nose was finally unstuffy and she could breathe easily.
We chose the restaurant because its unstuffy atmosphere made dining there enjoyable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: airy - having plenty of fresh air; well-ventilated
  • antonym: stuffy - lacking fresh air; unpleasantly warm or musty
