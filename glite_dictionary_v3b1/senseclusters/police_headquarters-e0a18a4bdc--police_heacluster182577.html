police headquarters

noun
main police office in an area
1.0 2.9
Pronunciation: / pəˈlis ˈhɛdˌkwɔrtərz / (AmE), / pəˈliːs ˈhɛdˌkwɔːtəz / (BrE)
the central office or main station for police in a particular city, district, or jurisdiction, from which officers are dispatched and administrative activities are managed
