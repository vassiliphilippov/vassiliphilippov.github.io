hepatic lobe

noun
anatomical section of the liver
7.9
Pronunciation: / hɪˈpætɪk loʊb / (AmE), / hɪˈpætɪk ləʊb / (BrE)
any of the distinct anatomical sections that make up the liver in humans and other animals

Examples

The hepatic lobe of liver
A tumor was detected in the caudate hepatic lobe during the scan.
Damage to a hepatic lobe can affect overall liver function.
The right hepatic lobe is significantly larger than the left in most adults.

  • synonym: liver - large organ in the abdomen involved in metabolism and detoxification
