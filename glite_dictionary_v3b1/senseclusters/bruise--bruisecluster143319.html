bruise

verb
injure feelings; wound pride
4.2 1.69/m
Pronunciation: / bruz / (AmE), / bruÀêz / (BrE)
to injure someone's feelings or pride with an unkind remark or action

Examples

Bruise his pride
He tried not to let the rejection bruise his self-esteem.
Her careless words bruised his confidence more than she realized.
The criticism bruised his pride, but he learned from the experience.
