coronary artery

noun
an artery supplying blood to the heart
7.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈkɔː.rə.nə.riː ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ /; / /ˈkɑː.rə.nə.riː ˈɑːr.tə.riː/ (AmE), /ˈkɒ.rə.nə.riː ˈɑː.tə.riː/ (BrE)
