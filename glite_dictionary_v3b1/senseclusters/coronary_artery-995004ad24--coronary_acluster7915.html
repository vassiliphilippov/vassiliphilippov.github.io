coronary artery

noun
an artery supplying blood to the heart
3.9 1.43/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…îr…ôÀån…õri Àà…ëÀêrÀåt…õri / (AmE), / Ààk…ír.…ôÀån…ôri Àà…ëÀê.t…ôri / (BrE)

Examples
