wing

noun
faction within a group or party
3.3
Pronunciation: / wɪŋ / (AmE), / wɪŋ / (BrE)
A group within a political party, legislature, or organization that holds distinct views or has a particular function.

Examples

The left wing of a party
Debate broke out between the moderate and conservative wings of the committee.
She belongs to the environmental wing of the organization.
The left wing of the party opposed the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: faction - a small organized dissenting group within a larger one, especially in politics
