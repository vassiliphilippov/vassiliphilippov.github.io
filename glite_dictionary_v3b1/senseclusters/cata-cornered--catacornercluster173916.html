cata-cornered

adjective
diagonally positioned or placed
Pronunciation: / ˌkæt.əˈkɔr.nɚd / (AmE), / ˌkæt.əˈkɔː.nəd / (BrE)
Situated or positioned diagonally; placed at an angle across from something else, especially across an intersection or open area.

Examples

Cata-cornered position
He ran cata-cornered through the playground, cutting across instead of following the path.
She parked her car cata-cornered from the main entrance to the store.
The two houses sat cata-cornered across the busy intersection.
