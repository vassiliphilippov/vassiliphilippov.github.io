Synonyms

  • little by little
    2.3 2/m
    little by little means small changes happening over time, while gradually means a slow, steady development happening step by step.
  • bit by bit
    2.7 0.6/m
    bit by bit describes things changing in small pieces, while gradually refers to a slow steady change over time.
  • incrementally
    3.8 0.7/m
    incrementally means increasing by small amounts, often in measured steps, while gradually is a general word for slow development.
  • progressively
    4.1 2/m
    progressively means growing or getting stronger over time, often in clear stages, while gradually just means happening slowly.
  • slowly
    1.0 60/m
    slowly describes low speed or pace, while gradually emphasizes slow change or development over time rather than speed alone.
  • step by step
    1.8 1/m
    step by step shows doing one stage after another in order, while gradually focuses on slow change without implying a clear plan.
  • in stages
    3.5 0.3/m
    in stages means completing parts one at a time, while gradually stresses that something changes slowly over time rather than by whole shifts.
  • steadily
    2.1 6/m
    steadily means moving or changing in a regular, continuous way, while gradually highlights small, slow steps rather than constant speed.
  • day by day
    1.6 2/m
    day by day describes change that happens each day in small amounts, while gradually is a broader word for slow development over time.
