gradually

adverb
slowly and steadily, in stages
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrædʒuəli / (AmE), / ˈɡrædʒjʊəli / (BrE)
In a slow and steady way, happening or developing little by little.

Examples

To gradually change
She gradually began to feel more comfortable in her new job.
The colors in the sky gradually faded as the sun set.
The temperature gradually increased as spring turned to summer.

Related Terms

  • antonym: rapidly - very quickly; at a fast rate
  • synonym: slowly - at a slow speed; not quickly
