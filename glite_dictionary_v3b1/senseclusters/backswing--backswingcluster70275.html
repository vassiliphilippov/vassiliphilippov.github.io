backswing

noun
return to earlier state
5.3
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.swɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.swɪŋ/ (BrE)
a movement or return toward a previous state, tendency, or position, often used figuratively for opinions or trends

Examples

Political backswing
The country experienced a political backswing after years of rapid reform.
There was a noticeable backswing in public opinion following the controversial decision.
After the initial excitement, a backswing brought the market back to previous levels.

  • synonym: reversal - a change to an opposite direction or position
