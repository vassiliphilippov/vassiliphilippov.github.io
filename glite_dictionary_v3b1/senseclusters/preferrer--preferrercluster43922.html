preferrer

noun
person who prefers
4.2
Pronunciation: / prɪˈfɜrər / (AmE), / prɪˈfɜːrə / (BrE)
a person who likes or chooses one thing over another

Examples

Preferrer of tea
As a preferrer of tea, she rarely drinks coffee.
The preferrer of quiet places will enjoy this peaceful park.
Each preferrer in the group shared their favorite type of music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chooser - a person who selects or picks something
  • synonym: selector - a person who makes a choice from a range of options
