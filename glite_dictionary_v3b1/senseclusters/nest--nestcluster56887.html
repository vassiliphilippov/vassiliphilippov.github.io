nest

noun
criminals, spies, or similar group in one place
Pronunciation: / nɛst / (AmE), / nɛst / (BrE)
a place where criminals, spies, or weapons are hidden or operate

Examples

A nest of spies
Intelligence agencies believe the city is a nest of foreign spies.
The operation was designed to break up a nest of criminals who controlled the neighborhood.
The police uncovered a nest of smugglers operating out of the old warehouse.
The soldiers advanced cautiously toward the enemy's nest in the hills.
The troops were ordered to destroy the machine-gun nest before moving forward.
They located a sniper nest hidden among the trees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bunker - a reinforced underground shelter, typically for defense
  • synonym: den - a place where people with a particular (typically bad) purpose gather, e.g. 'den of thieves'
  • synonym: emplacement - a prepared position for weapons or military equipment
