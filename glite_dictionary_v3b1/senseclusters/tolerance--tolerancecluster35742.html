tolerance

noun
permissible range of variation
4.8 3.45/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ëÀêl…ôr…ôns / (AmE), / Ààt…íl…ôr…ôns / (BrE)
the allowable amount of variation of a specified quantity, especially in technical or scientific contexts

Examples

Tight tolerance
Engineers must consider the tolerance of each component during assembly.
The tolerance for temperature variation in this experiment is very small.
This part was rejected because it exceeded the required tolerance limits.

Synonyms

  • leeway
    4.3 1.92/m
