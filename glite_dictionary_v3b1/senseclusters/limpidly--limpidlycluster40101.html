limpidly

adverb
in a clear, lucid manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪmpɪdli / (AmE), / ˈlɪmpɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that is clear and easily understood; lucidly.

Examples

Written limpidly
Her essay was written so limpidly that even difficult concepts seemed simple.
The documentary presents its arguments limpidly, leaving no room for confusion.
The professor explained the complex theory limpidly, making it easy for everyone to understand.
