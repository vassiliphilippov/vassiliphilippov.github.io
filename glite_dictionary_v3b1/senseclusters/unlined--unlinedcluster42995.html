unlined

adjective
smooth, without lines or wrinkles
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈlaɪnd / (AmE), / ʌnˈlaɪnd / (BrE)
(of skin or surface) smooth, without lines, wrinkles, or creases.

Examples

Unlined skin
Despite his age, his unlined face gave him a youthful appearance.
Her hands were remarkably unlined for someone who worked outdoors all her life.
The actor's unlined cheeks made him perfect for the role of a young prince.
