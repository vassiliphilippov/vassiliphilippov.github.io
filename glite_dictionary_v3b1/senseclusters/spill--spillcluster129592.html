spill

noun
channel for excess water at a dam
1.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /spɪl/ (AmE), /spɪl/ (BrE)
a channel that carries excess water over or around a dam or other obstruction, especially to prevent flooding

Examples

Dam spill
A blockage in the dam’s spill can lead to potentially dangerous increases in water level.
After a week of rain, water began to pour down the spill and into the river below.
The engineers inspected the spill to ensure it could handle heavy rains during the monsoon season.
