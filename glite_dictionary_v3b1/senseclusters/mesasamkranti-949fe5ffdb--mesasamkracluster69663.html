Mesasamkranti

noun
Hindu solar holiday; new astrological year
9.9
Pronunciation: / meɪ.sə.sʌmˈkrɑːn.ti / (AmE), / meɪ.sə.sʌmˈkrɑːn.ti / (BrE)
A Hindu solar holiday marking the beginning of the new astrological year, observed when the sun enters the constellation Aries.

Examples

Mesasamkranti festival
Mesasamkranti is observed when the sun enters Aries, signifying renewal and new beginnings.
Celebrations for Mesasamkranti mark the arrival of the new astrological year in many Hindu communities.
Many families perform special rituals on Mesasamkranti to welcome good fortune and prosperity.
