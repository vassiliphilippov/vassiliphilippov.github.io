statement

noun
an action or thing expressing an idea nonverbally
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsteɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈsteɪtmənt / (BrE)
an action or thing that expresses an idea, opinion, or feeling without using words

Examples

Fashion statement
Buying an electric car is often a statement about one’s environmental concerns.
Her bold dress made a statement at the party, turning everyone's head.
His silence during the meeting was a statement in itself.
