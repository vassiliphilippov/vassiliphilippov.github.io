growable

adjective
able to be grown
Pronunciation: / ˈɡroʊəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrəʊəbəl / (BrE)
able to be grown or cultivated, or capable of increasing in size or amount

Examples

Growable plants
Tomatoes are easily growable in most home gardens with enough sunlight.
The company is looking for growable markets to expand its business.
Not all plants are growable in cold climates due to harsh weather conditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonviable - not able to grow or develop
