guestworker

noun
person allowed to work temporarily in a foreign country
3.2 5.2
Pronunciation: /gest.ˈwɜːr.kər/ /; / /ˈgest.wɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /gest.ˈwɜː.kə/ /; / /ˈgest.wɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a person who is given temporary permission to work in a country where they are not a citizen or permanent resident

Examples

A guestworker visa
After her visa expired, the guestworker had to return to her home country.
Many industries rely on guestworkers to fill temporary labor shortages during busy seasons.
The government introduced a new policy to protect the rights of guestworkers.
