call in

verb
telephone a place to report or inform
Pronunciation: / kɔl ɪn / (AmE), / kɔːl ɪn / (BrE)
to telephone a place, especially an office, radio station, or workplace, often to give information or report something

Examples

To call in to work
I decided to call in and share my opinion with the hosts.
If you can't come to work, you need to call in before your shift starts.
Thousands of listeners call in to the radio show every morning.
