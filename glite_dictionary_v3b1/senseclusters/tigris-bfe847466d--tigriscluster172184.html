Tigris

proper noun
river in western Asia
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡrɪs / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡrɪs / (BrE)
a major river in western Asia, flowing through Turkey and Iraq and joining the Euphrates to form the Shatt al-Arab
