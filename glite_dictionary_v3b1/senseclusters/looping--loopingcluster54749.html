looping

noun
repeating audio/video clip
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈluːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈluːpɪŋ / (BrE)
(in media and digital content) the practice or effect of repeating a short piece of audio or video continuously, or a short piece created to be played repeatedly.

Examples

Looping video
The artist posted a looping clip that played on repeat across the feed.
We used a short looping soundtrack under the scene to create an unsettling effect.
During the exhibition, visitors enjoyed a looping video installation that never stopped.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loop - a short sequence of sound or video designed to repeat continuously
  • synonym: repeat - to play or perform something again, often continuously
