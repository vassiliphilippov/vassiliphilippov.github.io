end of story

x
matter is settled; nothing more to discuss
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɛnd əv ˈstɔri / (AmE), / ˌɛnd əv ˈstɔːri / (BrE)
used to indicate that a matter is settled and no further discussion is necessary

Examples

It is the end of story
He refused to debate further, saying it was the end of story.
I've made up my mind about moving, and that's the end of story.
They announced the results and told everyone it was the end of story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: case closed - used to announce that something has been conclusively decided
  • synonym: full stop - an expression used to indicate the conclusion of a discussion
  • antonym: open question - something not settled or decided
