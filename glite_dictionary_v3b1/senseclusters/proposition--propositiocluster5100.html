proposition

noun
task or undertaking to be dealt with
Pronunciation: / ˌprɑː.pəˈzɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ˌprɒp.əˈzɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a task, project, or situation that must be confronted or dealt with, often implying it may be difficult or challenging

Examples

A tough proposition
Completing the marathon in under three hours is a tough proposition for most runners.
Launching a new product in this market can be a risky proposition.
Negotiating with that company turned out to be a complicated proposition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breeze - something very easy to do (as antonym/contrast)
  • synonym: challenge - a difficult task or problem to be faced
  • synonym: undertaking - a task or assignment to be attempted
