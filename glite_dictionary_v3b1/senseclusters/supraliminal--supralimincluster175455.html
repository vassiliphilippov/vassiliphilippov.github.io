supraliminal

adjective
consciously perceptible
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːprəˈlɪmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˌsuːprəˈlɪmɪnəl / (BrE)
above the threshold of conscious perception; able to be consciously noticed or detected

Examples

A supraliminal stimulus
The scientist ensured that the stimulus was supraliminal so participants could consciously detect it.
Unlike subliminal messages, supraliminal cues are easily noticed by the audience.
The study focused on the effects of supraliminal advertising on consumer behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perceptible - able to be seen, heard, or noticed
  • antonym: subliminal - below the threshold of conscious perception
