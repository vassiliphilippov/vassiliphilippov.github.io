on-off switch

noun
a switch with 'on' and 'off' positions
Pronunciation: / ɑn ˈɔf swɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɒn ˈɒf swɪtʃ / (BrE)
a switch with two positions, typically 'on' and 'off', used to control the flow of electricity or the operation of a device

Examples

The on-off switch
Make sure the on-off switch is in the 'off' position before cleaning the fan.
She flicked the on-off switch to start the machine.
The lamp has a simple on-off switch by its base.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toggle switch - a switch operated by a lever that moves back and forth
