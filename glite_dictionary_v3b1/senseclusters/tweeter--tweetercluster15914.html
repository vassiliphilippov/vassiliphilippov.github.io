tweeter

noun
small, chirping bird
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːtər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːtə / (BrE)
a small bird that makes quick, high-pitched sounds

Examples

A small tweeter bird
A tweeter perched on the branch and sang brightly.
We heard a tweeter outside our window early in the morning.
The forest was alive with the sounds of tweeters calling to each other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: songbird - a bird known for singing musical notes
  • synonym: warbler - a small bird that sings with a trilling or warbling sound
