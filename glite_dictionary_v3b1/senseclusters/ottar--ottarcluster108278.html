ottar

noun
essential oil from flowers
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːtɑr / (AmE), / ˈɒtɑː / (BrE)
a fragrant essential oil or perfume obtained from flowers, especially roses

Examples

A bottle of ottar
A small bottle of ottar can scent hundreds of liters of perfume.
The perfumer used pure ottar of rose to create a luxurious fragrance.
The prized ottar was carefully extracted from thousands of rose petals.
