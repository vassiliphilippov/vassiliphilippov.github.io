derisorily

adverb
in a mocking or scornful way
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈrɪsərɪli / (AmE), / dɪˈrɪsərɪli / (BrE)
In a disrespectful and mocking manner; showing scorn or ridicule.

Examples

To speak derisorily
He shrugged derisorily, refusing to take the complaint seriously.
She laughed derisorily at his suggestion, making it clear she found it ridiculous.
The critics responded derisorily to the new policy proposals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: derisively - in a way that shows ridicule or mockery
