habitableness

noun
suitability for living; habitability
Pronunciation: / həˈbɪtəblnəs / (AmE), / həˈbɪtəblnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being suitable for living in or on; suitability for habitation

Examples

The habitableness of land
Experts debate the habitableness of the region after the natural disaster.
Scientists study the habitableness of distant planets to determine if life could exist there.
The habitableness of the cabin depended on repairing the broken windows and leaking roof.

Related Terms

  • synonym: habitability - the quality of being habitable; suitability for habitation
  • antonym: uninhabitability - the quality of being uninhabitable
