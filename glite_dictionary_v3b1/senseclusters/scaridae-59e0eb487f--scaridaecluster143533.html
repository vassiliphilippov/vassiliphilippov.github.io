Scaridae

noun
family of parrotfishes
9.9
Pronunciation: /skə.ˈrɪ.diː/ (AmE), /skə.ˈrɪ.diː/ (BrE)
A family of brightly colored marine fishes known as parrotfishes, characterized by their beak-like teeth fused into a parrot-like structure; found primarily in tropical reefs.
