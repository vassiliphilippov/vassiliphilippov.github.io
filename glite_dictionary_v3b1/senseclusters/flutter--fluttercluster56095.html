flutter

verb
to wink or bat the eyelashes briefly
2.6 3.1
Pronunciation: /ˈflʌ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈflʌ.tə/ (BrE)
(of eyelashes or eyelids) to move or close and open quickly and lightly, as in briefly winking or batting the lashes.

Examples

To flutter eyelashes
He couldn't help but notice her as she fluttered her lashes playfully.
She likes to flutter her eyelashes when she's trying to be charming.
The child shyly fluttered her eyelids before answering the question.

Related Terms
