hash

noun
'#' symbol used to tag topics (hashtag)
3.5
Pronunciation: /hæʃ/ (AmE), /hæʃ/ (BrE)
the symbol '#' used before a word or phrase on social media to mark it as a topic or tag (a hashtag); also the name of the symbol itself.

Examples

Hash symbol
She added a hash to her post so it would appear under the travel tag.
On Twitter, people often follow a hash to find conversations about the event.
Please include the hash #climatechange in your caption to reach concerned readers.

Related Terms
