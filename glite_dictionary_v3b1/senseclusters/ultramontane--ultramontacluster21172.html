ultramontane

adjective
relating to ultramontanism; papal authority
Pronunciation: / ˌʌltrəˈmɑnˌteɪn / (AmE), / ˌʌltrəˈmɒnteɪn / (BrE)
Relating to or supporting ultramontanism, especially regarding the authority of the Pope over local churches.

Examples

Ultramontane beliefs
His views were considered ultramontane during a period of intense debate within the church.
Many 19th-century Catholics identified as ultramontane in their support for the centralization of church power.
The ultramontane position emphasized papal authority over regional church leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ultramontanism - the belief in the supreme authority of the Pope over local churches
