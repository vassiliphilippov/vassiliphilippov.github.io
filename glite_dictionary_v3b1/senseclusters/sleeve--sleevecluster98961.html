sleeve

noun
bariatric surgery (sleeve gastrectomy)
7.9 0.77/m
Pronunciation: / sliÀêv / (AmE), / sliÀêv / (BrE)
(in medicine) a bariatric surgical procedure (sleeve gastrectomy), or the narrow, tube-shaped stomach left after most of the stomach is removed to reduce food intake.

Examples

Gastric sleeve surgery
After years of dieting, she chose a gastric sleeve to help her lose weight quickly.
The surgeon explained the risks of a sleeve gastrectomy before scheduling the operation next month.
Many patients report feeling full after small portions following their sleeve surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sleeve gastrectomy - the surgical procedure of removing part of the stomach to create a sleeve-shaped stomach.
