threader

noun
tool for threading ribbon through loops
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɛdər / (AmE), / ˈθrɛdə / (BrE)
a small tool used to help put thread, ribbon, elastic, or similar material through a needle, small opening, or fabric

Examples

A threader tool
She used a threader to pull the ribbon through the drawstring casing.
The threader made sewing her costume much easier.
Without a threader, it would have taken much longer to thread the elastic.
She used a threader to help her sew the button on.
A threader makes it much easier to get the thread through the tiny eye of a needle.
If you have trouble threading needles, a threader can save you a lot of time.
