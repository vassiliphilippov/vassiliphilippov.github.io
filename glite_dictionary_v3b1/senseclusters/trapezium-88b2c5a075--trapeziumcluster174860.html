Trapezium

noun
multiple stars in Orion constellation
Pronunciation: / trəˈpiːziəm / (AmE), / trəˈpiːziəm / (BrE)
a group of four closely spaced stars at the center of the Orion Nebula, forming a trapezoidal pattern in the constellation Orion

Examples

The Trapezium cluster
Astronomers observed the Trapezium cluster through a powerful telescope.
From a dark location, you may glimpse the Trapezium with binoculars.
The Trapezium is one of the most famous features in the Orion Nebula.
