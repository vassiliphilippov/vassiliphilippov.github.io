cozy atmosphere

noun
warm and inviting environment
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊzi ˈætməsˌfɪr / (AmE), / ˈkəʊzi ˈætməsfɪə / (BrE)
a warm, comfortable, and inviting environment that encourages relaxation and a sense of well-being

Examples

A cozy atmosphere at home
After a long day at work, she craved the cozy atmosphere of her favorite coffee shop.

The living room had a cozy atmosphere thanks to the soft lighting and plush furniture.
