brainwork

noun
mental effort
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnwɜːk / (BrE)
mental effort or activity involving thinking, reasoning, or problem-solving

Examples

Intense brainwork

After a long day of brainwork, she liked to relax with a walk in the park.
Creative brainwork is essential for developing innovative products.

Related Terms
