Synonyms

  • expenditure
    5.6 5/m
    expenditure means money spent or the act of spending, while outlay refers to the actual money paid for something.
  • outgo
    7.0 0.02/m
    outgo is an older term for money paid out, while outlay is the common term for money spent on goods or projects.
  • expense
    2.7 20/m
    expense is an amount spent for a specific need or business, while outlay is any money paid for buying or making something.
  • spending
    1.7 50/m
    spending is the general act of using money, while outlay is the concrete sum paid for a particular purchase or project.
  • cost
    1.0 110/m
    cost is the price someone must pay to buy something, while outlay is the actual money spent by the buyer.
  • disbursement
    6.7 0.4/m
    disbursement is the act of paying out money from a fund or account, while outlay is the amount spent overall.
  • disbursal
    7.7 0.03/m
    disbursal is the formal payment or distribution of funds, while outlay is the total money spent on an item or activity.
