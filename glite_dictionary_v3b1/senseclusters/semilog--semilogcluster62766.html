semilog

adjective
one axis logarithmic
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛmiˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈsɛmiˌlɒg / (BrE)
describing a graph or scale in which one axis is logarithmic and the other is linear

Examples

Semilog graph scale
The scientist used a semilog plot to display the exponential growth of the bacteria.
When data spans several orders of magnitude, a semilog graph can make trends easier to see.
A semilog scale is often used in engineering to represent frequency responses.

Related Terms

  • antonym: linear - using or relating to a straight-line scale
  • synonym: logarithmic - using or relating to a logarithmic scale
