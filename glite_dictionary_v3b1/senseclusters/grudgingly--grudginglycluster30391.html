grudgingly

adverb
in an unwilling or reluctant manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows unwillingness or reluctance

Examples

To grudgingly accept
He grudgingly completed his chores after much persuasion from his parents.
She grudgingly accepted their invitation, even though she was still upset with them.
They grudgingly admitted that the other team had played better.

  • synonym: reluctantly - in an unwilling or hesitant way
