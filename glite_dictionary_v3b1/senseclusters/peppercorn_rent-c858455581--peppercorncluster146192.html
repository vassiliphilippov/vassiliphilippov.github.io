peppercorn rent

noun
very low or nominal rent
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpɚˌkɔrn rɛnt / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəkɔːn rɛnt / (BrE)
an extremely small or nominal rent, often used to meet the legal requirement for a contract rather than to generate income

Examples

A peppercorn rent
Many historical buildings are preserved by charging a peppercorn rent to responsible tenants.
The lease was granted for a peppercorn rent to help the community center stay open.
We pay only a peppercorn rent for the use of the premises, thanks to the local council.
