jelly doughnut

noun
doughnut filled with jelly or jam
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛli ˈdoʊnʌt / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛli ˈdəʊnʌt / (BrE)
A doughnut with a soft, raised texture, filled with jelly or jam.

Examples

A jelly doughnut

She bit into the jelly doughnut and got jam on her fingers.
The café is famous for its freshly made jelly doughnuts.
