walkie

noun
informal short walk
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔki / (AmE), / ˈwɔːki / (BrE)
an informal short walk, often taken for exercise or to walk a pet or child

Examples

A morning walkie
Every morning, she takes her dog for a quick walkie before work.
The toddler was excited to go on a walkie with his mother in the park.
After dinner, they enjoyed a relaxing walkie around the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: hike - a long walk, especially in the countryside
  • synonym: stroll - a slow, relaxed walk
