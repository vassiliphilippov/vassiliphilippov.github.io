brace

noun
rope on a ship for swinging a yard
4.8
Pronunciation: / breɪs / (AmE), / breɪs / (BrE)
A rope on a square-rigged ship used to swing a yard and secure it in position.

Examples

A ship's brace
A tangled brace could delay turning the ship quickly in strong winds.
He checked each brace before the storm to ensure the rig was secure.
The sailors pulled on the brace to adjust the angle of the yard to the wind.
