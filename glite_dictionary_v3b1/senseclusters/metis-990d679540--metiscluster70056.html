Metis

noun
person of mixed Indigenous and European ancestry in Canada
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪtɪs, məˈtiːs / (AmE), / ˈmeɪtɪs, ˌmɛtɪs / (BrE)
a member of a group in western Canada who is of mixed Indigenous and European ancestry, especially of First Nations and French or Scottish descent

Examples

Metis communities
Many Metis communities maintain their unique culture and language.
She is proud of her Metis heritage and traditions.
The Metis played a key role in the history of the Canadian prairies.
