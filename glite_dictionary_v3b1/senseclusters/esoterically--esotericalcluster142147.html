esoterically

adverb
in a secretive way
Pronunciation: / ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌiːsəˈtɛrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is only understood by a small, specialized group

The philosopher spoke esoterically, making his ideas difficult for most people to understand.
Some ancient texts are written esoterically to hide their true meaning from outsiders.
