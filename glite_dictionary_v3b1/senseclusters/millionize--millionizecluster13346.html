millionize

verb
to express in millions
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪljəˌnaɪz / (AmE), / ˈmɪljəˌnaɪz / (BrE)
to express numbers or amounts in units of one million, usually by dividing values by one million

Examples

To millionize the numbers
The financial report was millionized to make the large numbers easier to compare.
When presenting the data, she decided to millionize all the figures for clarity.
To simplify the chart, the analyst millionized the revenue numbers.
