noise

noun
outcry or protest
1.0
Pronunciation: / nɔɪz / (AmE), / nɔɪz / (BrE)
a loud public expression of protest, complaint, or opposition

Examples

Public noise
Despite all the noise surrounding the decision, the policy did not change.
The new law stirred up a lot of noise from angry citizens.
There was plenty of noise in the media after the controversial announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: approval - the act of approving something, opposite of protest or complaint
  • synonym: clamor - a loud and confused noise, especially of people shouting or protesting
  • synonym: outcry - a strong expression of public disapproval or anger
