hornito

noun
lava mound or dome
9.9
Pronunciation: /hɔːr.ˈniː.toʊ/ /; / /oʊr.ˈniː.toʊ/ (AmE), /hɔː.ˈniː.təʊ/ /; / /ɔː.ˈniː.təʊ/ (BrE)
a small mound or dome of lava that forms on the surface of a lava flow, created by gas pressure beneath the crust
