rootsy

adjective
traditional in style
Pronunciation: / ˈruːtsi / (AmE), / ˈruːtsi / (BrE)
having a strong connection to traditional, folk, or original styles, especially in music or art

Examples

Rootsy music
The singer's rootsy voice gave the song a timeless quality.
Critics praised the album for its rootsy blend of country and blues influences.
They decorated the restaurant in a rootsy style with wooden tables and vintage posters.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authentic - genuine or true to original traditions
  • synonym: folksy - having a simple, traditional, or rural style
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, not traditional
