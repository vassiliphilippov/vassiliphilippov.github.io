acre

noun
unit of area (4,840 sq yds)
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪkər / (AmE), / ˈeɪkə / (BrE)
A unit of area used in English-speaking countries, equal to 4,840 square yards or approximately 4,047 square meters.

Examples

An acre of land
The farmer owns over fifty acres of land in the countryside.
They bought a house on a one acre plot near the lake.
Wildflowers covered every acre of the open meadow by spring.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hectare - metric unit of area equal to 10,000 square meters
