Lathyrus latifolius

noun
species of perennial climbing plant
Pronunciation: / ləˈθaɪrəs ˌlætɪˈfoʊliəs / (AmE), / ləˈθaɪrəs ˌlætɪˈfəʊliəs / (BrE)
A perennial climbing plant of central and southern Europe with purple, pink, or white flowers, naturalized in North America; also known as perennial pea.
