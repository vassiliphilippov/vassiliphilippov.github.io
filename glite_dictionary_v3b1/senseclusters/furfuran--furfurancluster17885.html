furfuran

noun
chemical compound used in nylon production
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝːˌfjʊˌræn / (AmE), / ˈfɜːˌfjʊˌran / (BrE)
a colorless, toxic, flammable liquid chemical used as an intermediate in organic synthesis, notably in the production of nylon
