bean beetle

noun
a beetle pest of bean plants
7.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /biːn ˈbiː.təl/ (AmE), /biːn ˈbiː.təl/ (BrE)
a small beetle, originally from Mexico, that feeds on the foliage of bean plants and is considered a pest in agriculture

Examples

Bean beetle pest
Farmers monitor their fields closely to prevent bean beetle infestations.
The bean beetle causes significant damage to crops by feeding on bean leaves.
The agricultural extension office provides advice on controlling the bean beetle.
