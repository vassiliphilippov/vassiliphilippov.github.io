outplacement

noun
job search assistance
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpleɪsmənt / (AmE), / ˈaʊtˌpleɪsmənt / (BrE)
a service provided to employees who have been laid off to help them find new employment

Examples

Outplacement services
After the merger, the company offered outplacement services to help employees transition to new jobs.
Many organizations now include outplacement support as part of their severance packages.
The outplacement program provided resume workshops and interview coaching for laid-off staff.

Related Terms

  • synonym: career counseling - professional advice and support for career development
  • antonym: unemployment - the state of not having a job
