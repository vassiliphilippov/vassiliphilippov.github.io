pinstripe

noun
fabric with thin parallel stripes
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌstraɪp / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌstraɪp / (BrE)
a very thin, parallel stripe, especially as a pattern on fabric or suits
