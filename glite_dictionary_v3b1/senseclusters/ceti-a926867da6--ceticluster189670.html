Ceti

proper noun
star name genitive
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːtaɪ / (AmE), / ˈsiːtaɪ / (BrE)
the Latin genitive form used in star names to indicate a star belongs to the constellation Cetus

Examples

Star Ceti name
The star Tau Ceti is one of the closest Sun-like stars to Earth.
Astronomers use the name Ceti to show a star belongs to the constellation Cetus.
In the night sky, several stars are named with the genitive form Ceti.
