dent

noun
noticeable reduction or effect
5.5
Pronunciation: / dɛnt / (AmE), / dɛnt / (BrE)
an appreciable effect, especially a noticeable reduction or lessening in something

Examples

A dent in costs
Donating every month can make a real dent in world hunger.
Her shopping spree really made a dent in her credit card balance.
The repairs put a dent in our savings, but the car is running again.

  • antonym: increase - an instance of becoming greater in amount
