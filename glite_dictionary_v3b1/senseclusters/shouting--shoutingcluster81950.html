shouting

noun
encouragement by cheering or yelling
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃaʊtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making loud cries or noises, especially to show excitement, support, anger, or pain

Examples

Loud shouting crowd
All the shouting from the stands made it hard to hear the referee's whistle.
Despite the shouting and applause, the visiting team remained focused.
The home team's shouting gave the players an extra boost during the final minutes.
Her shouting startled everyone in the quiet library.
The shouting from the crowd grew louder as the game reached its climax.
We could hear shouting coming from the house next door.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cheering - expressing approval or encouragement by shouting
  • antonym: whispering - the act of speaking very softly
  • synonym: yelling - the act of making a loud, sharp cry
