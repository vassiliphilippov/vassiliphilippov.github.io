Synonyms

  • shout
    1.0 9.62/m
    a shout is a single loud cry used to show strong feeling or get attention, while shouting is the general act of making loud cries.
  • yelling
    1.0 3.19/m
    yelling is the act of making loud, often angry or excited cries, while shouting covers loud cries in many situations.
  • hollering
    5.9 0.01/m
    hollering is a raw, long, often high-pitched cry used in fields or crowds, while shouting means making loud cries.
  • bellowing
    5.9 0.19/m
    bellowing is a very loud, deep roar like an animal's voice, while shouting is any loud human cry not always so deep.
