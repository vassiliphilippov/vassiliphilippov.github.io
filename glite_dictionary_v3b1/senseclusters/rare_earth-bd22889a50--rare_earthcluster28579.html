rare earth

noun
metallic element in the lanthanide series

any of a set of metallic chemical elements in the lanthanide series, with atomic numbers 57 through 71, often used in electronics and industry.

Examples

Rare earth metals

Synonyms

  • rare-earth element
    6.3 0.10/m
    rare-earth element is the formal written name used in science and industry, while rare earth is the shorter, common everyday phrase.
  • lanthanide
    9.9 0.07/m
    lanthanide is the scientific name chemists use in textbooks and papers, while rare earth is the industry and public term.
  • lanthanoid
    9.9 0.00/m

  • lanthanon
    9.9 0.00/m
    lanthanon is an older, rare spelling seen in historical chemistry, while rare earth is the modern common name used today.
