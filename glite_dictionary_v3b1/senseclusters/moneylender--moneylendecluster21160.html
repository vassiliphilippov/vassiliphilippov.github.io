moneylender

noun
person who lends money at high interest
4.1 4.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌn.ɪ.len.dər/ (AmE), /ˈmʌn.ɪ.len.də/ (BrE)
a person or organization that lends money to others, especially at unreasonably high rates of interest

Examples

A moneylender charges interest
He borrowed from a moneylender when he could not get a bank loan.
Many villages have at least one moneylender who sets high interest rates.
The moneylender demanded repayment within a month, or else he would seize the property.
