roadrunner

noun
fast-running bird of the American Southwest
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊdˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊdˌrʌnə / (BrE)
A large, fast-running terrestrial bird with a long tail and crest, found in the southwestern United States and Mexico.

Examples

The roadrunner bird
In cartoons, the roadrunner is famous for always outsmarting its pursuers.
Many tourists hope to spot a roadrunner while visiting the American Southwest.
The roadrunner darted across the desert in search of prey.
