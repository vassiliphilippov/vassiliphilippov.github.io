Idalian

noun
Aphrodite worshipper
9.9
Pronunciation: / aɪˈdeɪliən / (AmE), / aɪˈdeɪliən / (BrE)
a follower or devotee of the goddess Aphrodite, especially one associated with the ancient cult at Idalion in Cyprus

Examples

Idalian worshipper
The ancient poet described an Idalian offering flowers at the temple of Aphrodite.
Archaeologists discovered artifacts belonging to an Idalian near the ruins of Idalion.
Legends say that every Idalian participated in the spring festival honoring the goddess.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aphrodite - the ancient Greek goddess of love and beauty
  • synonym: Venusian - a follower or devotee of Venus, the Roman goddess of love
