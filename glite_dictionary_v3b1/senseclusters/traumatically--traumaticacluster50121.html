traumatically

adverb
severely and disruptively
Pronunciation: / trəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / trɔːˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that causes sudden and severe disruption or long-lasting negative effects, not limited to emotional impact

Examples

Traumatically changed
The company was traumatically affected by the sudden economic downturn.
After the accident, the community changed traumatically in many ways.
The landscape was traumatically altered by the powerful earthquake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disruptively - in a way that interrupts or disturbs normal activity
  • antonym: peacefully - in a calm and undisturbed manner
