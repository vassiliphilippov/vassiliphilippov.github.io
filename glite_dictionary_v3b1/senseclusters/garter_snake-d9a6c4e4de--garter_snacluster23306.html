garter snake

noun
nonvenomous striped North American snake
8.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑːr.tər sneɪk/ (AmE), /ˈgɑː.tə sneɪk/ (BrE)
A common name for any of several nonvenomous North and Central American snakes of the genus Thamnophis, typically featuring longitudinal stripes.

Examples

A garter snake slithering
A garter snake slithered through the grass beside the pond.
Children often catch garter snakes because they are harmless and easy to handle.
The distinctive stripes make the garter snake easy to identify in the wild.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ribbon snake - a slender, striped North American snake closely related to the garter snake
