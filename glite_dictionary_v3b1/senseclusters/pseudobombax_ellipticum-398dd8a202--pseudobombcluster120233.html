Pseudobombax ellipticum

noun
a tropical tree with dense, hairy flowers
Pronunciation: / ˌsjuːdoʊˈbɒmbæks ɛˈlɪptɪkəm / (AmE), / ˌsjuːdəʊˈbɒmbaks ɛˈlɪptɪkəm / (BrE)
a tropical tree native to Mexico and Central America, known for its dense, hairy flowers with long narrow petals clustered at the ends of branches before the leaves appear
