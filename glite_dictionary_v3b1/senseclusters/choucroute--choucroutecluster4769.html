choucroute

noun
French sauerkraut dish
Pronunciation: / ʃuːˈkruːt / (AmE), / ʃuːˈkruːt / (BrE)
a French dish of sauerkraut typically served with sausages and various meats, especially from the Alsace region

Examples

Alsatian choucroute platter
The restaurant is famous for its traditional choucroute served with smoked sausages and potatoes.
During our trip to Strasbourg, we enjoyed a hearty plate of choucroute garnie.
He learned to make authentic choucroute after visiting the Alsace region in France.
