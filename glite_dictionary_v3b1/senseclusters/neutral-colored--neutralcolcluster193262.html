neutral-colored

adjective
having a subdued, unobtrusive color
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːtrəl ˌkʌlɚd / (AmE), / ˈnjuːtrəl ˌkʌləd / (BrE)
having a color that is unobtrusive or does not draw attention, typically in muted or subdued tones such as beige, grey, or taupe

Examples

Neutral-colored clothes
A neutral-colored scarf goes well with almost any outfit.
She prefers to wear neutral-colored dresses for business meetings.
The living room was decorated with neutral-colored furniture that created a calming atmosphere.
Many offices use neutral-coloured carpets to create a calming atmosphere.
She preferred neutral-coloured clothing to avoid drawing too much attention.
The living room was decorated with neutral-coloured walls and simple furnishings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brightly-colored - having a vivid or striking color, attracting attention
  • synonym: muted - having a soft, subdued, or restrained color or tone
  • synonym: neutral-colored - having a color that is subtle and not attention-grabbing (American spelling)
