reciprocator

noun
device for back-and-forth motion
7.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɪprəˌkeɪtər / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪprəkeɪtə / (BrE)
a person or device that responds in kind or produces a back-and-forth motion

Examples

The reciprocator moves piston
The reciprocator in the engine moves the piston back and forth to generate power.
A skilled reciprocator always returns favors in equal measure.
The automated system uses a reciprocator to evenly coat the surfaces with paint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actuator - a device that causes a machine or device to operate
  • synonym: oscillator - a device that moves or swings back and forth in a regular rhythm
