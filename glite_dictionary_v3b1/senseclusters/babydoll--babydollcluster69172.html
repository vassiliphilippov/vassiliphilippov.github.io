babydoll

noun
toy baby doll
Pronunciation: / ˈbeɪbiˌdɑl / (AmE), / ˈbeɪbiˌdɒl / (BrE)
a toy doll designed to look like a baby, usually played with by children

Examples

A babydoll toy
The little girl hugged her favorite babydoll tightly as she fell asleep.
He bought a new babydoll for his niece's birthday present.
After the tea party, the children lined up each babydoll on the windowsill.

Related Terms

  • antonym: action figure - a small toy figure representing a character, often from a movie or comic
  • synonym: doll - a small model of a human figure used as a child's toy
