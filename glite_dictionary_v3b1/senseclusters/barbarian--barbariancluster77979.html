barbarian

adjective
uncivilized; lacking refinement
7.6 0.36/m
Pronunciation: / b…ëÀêrÀàb…õri…ôn / (AmE), / b…ëÀêÀàb…õÀêri…ôn / (BrE)
(of people, customs, or behavior) uncivilized, lacking refinement or civilized influences.

Examples

Barbarian customs
Centuries ago, Roman writers often described their enemies as barbarian peoples.
Some regarded the unfamiliar customs as barbarian and refused to accept them.
The barbarian tribes showed little respect for the traditions of the conquered city.
