nonliteracy

noun
inability to read or write
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈlɪtərəsi / (AmE), / nɒnˈlɪtərəsi / (BrE)
the state of being unable to read or write, or not having enough reading and writing skills for daily life

Examples

Adult nonliteracy
Efforts to reduce nonliteracy have become a priority in many rural communities.
High rates of nonliteracy can limit economic opportunities for individuals and families.
The government launched a campaign to address nonliteracy among adults.
Programs have been created to reduce nonliteracy among adults in rural communities.
High rates of nonliteracy can limit job opportunities and access to important information.
The government is investing in education to address the problem of nonliteracy in the population.

Related Terms

  • synonym: illiteracy - the inability to read or write
  • antonym: literacy - the ability to read and write
