romanticist

noun
a follower or artist of the Romantic Movement
Pronunciation: / roʊˈmæntɪsɪst / (AmE), / rəʊˈmantɪsɪst / (BrE)
an artist, writer, or thinker who is a follower of the Romantic Movement or influenced by Romanticism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy

Examples

Romanticist painter
As a romanticist, she believed in the power of emotion over reason.
Many romanticists sought inspiration from nature and the sublime.
The painter was a leading romanticist in nineteenth-century Europe.

  • synonym: romantic - having characteristics of the Romantic Movement in art, literature, or philosophy
