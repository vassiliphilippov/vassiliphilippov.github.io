immunologist

noun
specialist in immunology
Pronunciation: / ˌɪmjəˈnɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌɪmjʊˈnɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a medical scientist who specializes in the study and treatment of the immune system and related disorders

Examples

An immunologist studies diseases
After years of research, the immunologist developed a new treatment for autoimmune diseases.
My sister consulted an immunologist to understand her frequent allergies.
The hospital hired a leading immunologist to head their vaccine development program.
