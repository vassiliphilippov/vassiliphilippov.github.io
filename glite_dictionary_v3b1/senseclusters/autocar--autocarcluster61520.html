autocar

noun
early motor vehicle
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɔtoʊˌkɑr / (AmE), / ˈɔːtəʊˌkɑː / (BrE)
an early term for a motor vehicle, especially an automobile powered by an engine

Examples

A vintage autocar
The museum displayed a restored 1903 autocar as part of its vintage collection.
In the early 20th century, owning an autocar was a symbol of wealth and innovation.
He read about the invention of the autocar in a book on transportation history.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automobile - a road vehicle powered by an engine, used for transporting people
  • synonym: car - a four-wheeled motor vehicle for transporting passengers
