finish

verb
end a relationship with someone
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfɪnɪʃ / (BrE)
to bring an association or relationship with someone to an end; to put an end to one's involvement with another person

Examples

To finish a friendship
Her harsh words finally finished him with their friendship.
His betrayal finished her with the company.
The argument finished me with Tom for good.

Related Terms

  • synonym: break up - to end a romantic relationship
  • antonym: reconcile - to restore a relationship after a break
  • synonym: split up - to end a romantic relationship or partnership
