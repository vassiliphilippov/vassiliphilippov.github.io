wastefully

adverb
in a wasteful manner
4.2 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈweɪst.fəl.iː/ /; / /ˈweɪst.fʊl.iː/ (AmE), /ˈweɪst.fʊl.iː/ /; / /ˈweɪst.fəl.iː/ (BrE)
In a way that uses resources without care or concern for efficiency or conservation; in a wasteful manner.

Examples

Use wastefully water
He used water wastefully, leaving the taps running all day.
Many people wastefully discard food that could still be eaten.
The manager criticized the staff for working wastefully and not following the efficiency guidelines.
