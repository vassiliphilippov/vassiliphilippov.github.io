white

noun
white sports trousers
Pronunciation: / waɪt / (AmE), / waɪt / (BrE)
(usually plural) Trousers made of flannel, gabardine, tweed, or white cloth, typically worn for sports like cricket or tennis.

Examples

Cricket whites
He kept his whites immaculately clean throughout the tournament.
The cricket team wore their whites for the match.
You should bring a pair of whites for tennis practice tomorrow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trousers - pants covering each leg separately, outer garment
