small-leaved linden

noun
species of linden tree with small leaves
9.9
Pronunciation: / smɔl livd ˈlɪndən / (AmE), / smɔːl liːvd ˈlɪndən / (BrE)
A large European linden tree (Tilia cordata) with small, dark green leaves, often grown as an ornamental.

Examples

A small-leaved linden tree
Many European parks feature avenues lined with small-leaved linden trees.
She admired the glossy foliage of the small-leaved linden in the botanical garden.
The small-leaved linden is often planted along city streets for its shade and beauty.
