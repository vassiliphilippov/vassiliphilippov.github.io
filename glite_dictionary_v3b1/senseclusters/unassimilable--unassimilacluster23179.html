unassimilable

adjective
not able to blend
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈsɪləməbl̩ / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈsɪlɪməbl̩ / (BrE)
not able to be changed to resemble neighboring sounds in speech or to be absorbed into something else

Examples

Unassimilable word
The foreign word remained unassimilable in the local dialect, keeping its original pronunciation.
Some sounds in this language are unassimilable and do not blend with others in speech.
Linguists study why certain consonant clusters are unassimilable in specific languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inassimilable - not able to be assimilated or absorbed
