chugger

noun
slow, noisy machine
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃʌɡər / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌɡə / (BrE)
an engine, machine, or vehicle that moves slowly while making regular chugging noises

Examples

Old chugger engine
The old chugger finally made it up the hill after several noisy attempts.
We could hear the chugger coming down the road long before we saw it.
Despite its age, the little chugger still gets the job done on the farm.
