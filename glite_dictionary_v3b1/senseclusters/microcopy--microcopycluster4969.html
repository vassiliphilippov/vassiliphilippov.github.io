microcopy

noun
short UI text guiding users
7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmaɪ.kroʊ.kɑː.piː/ (AmE), /ˈmaɪ.krəʊ.kɒp.iː/ (BrE)
Very short pieces of text in a user interface (such as button labels, help lines, form hints, or error messages) that guide users or explain actions.

Examples

Helpful microcopy on buttons
The app's onboarding relies on microcopy to explain features clearly.
Always test your microcopy with users to avoid confusing error messages and dropouts.
She rewrote the button's microcopy to make the call to action more direct.

Related Terms

  • synonym: label - short text used to name or describe a UI control or element
