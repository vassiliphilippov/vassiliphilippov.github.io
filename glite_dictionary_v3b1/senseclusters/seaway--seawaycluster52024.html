seaway

noun
lane or route at sea for ships
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈsiːweɪ / (BrE)
A regularly used route or lane through the sea for ships and vessels.

Examples

A seaway route
Cargo ships use the seaway to travel from one continent to another.
Heavy fog forced all vessels to delay entry into the busy seaway.
The government monitors the seaway for safety and navigation hazards.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shipping lane - a route in the sea used by commercial ships
