basidial

adjective
relating to basidia in fungi
Pronunciation: / bəˈsɪdiəl / (AmE), / bəˈsɪdiəl / (BrE)
relating to or characterized by basidia (the spore-producing cells of certain fungi, especially basidiomycetes).

Examples

Basidial cells
Identification of fungi relies on examining their basidial characteristics.
The basidial layer is where spores are produced in certain mushrooms.
Under the microscope, the basidial cells appeared swollen and full of spores.
