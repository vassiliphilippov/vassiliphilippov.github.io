Mach

noun
speed relative to sound
6.9
Pronunciation: / mɑk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
a unit used to express the speed of an object as a multiple of the speed of sound in air

Examples

Mach 2 speed
The experimental aircraft flew at Mach 3 during the test.
Breaking the Mach 1 barrier was a major achievement in aviation history.
The pilot reported reaching Mach 2.5 before descending.

Related Terms

  • antonym: subsonic - slower than the speed of sound
  • synonym: supersonic - faster than the speed of sound
