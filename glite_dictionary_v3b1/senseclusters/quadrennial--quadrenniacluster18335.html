quadrennial

noun
four-year period
7.2
Pronunciation: / kwɑːˈdrɛniəl / (AmE), / kwɒˈdrɛniəl / (BrE)
a period of four years, or an event that happens every four years

Examples

An Olympic quadrennial
The Olympic Games are held every quadrennial, attracting athletes from around the world.
The committee reviews its policies at the end of each quadrennial to ensure they remain effective.
Budget planning for the university is based on a quadrennial to align with major projects.
The Olympic Games are a major quadrennial that attracts athletes from around the world.
Every quadrennial brings a new round of presidential elections in the United States.
The committee planned the festival as a quadrennial to celebrate the town's history.
