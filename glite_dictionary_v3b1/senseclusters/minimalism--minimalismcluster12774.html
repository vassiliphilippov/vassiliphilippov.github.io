minimalism

noun
art movement focused on simplicity
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪnɪməˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm / (BrE)
A style in art, music, or design that is characterized by extreme simplicity of form, color, or content, especially a movement in painting and sculpture that began in the 1950s.

Examples

Minimalism in art
He was inspired by minimalism when designing the gallery space.
Her paintings reflect the principles of minimalism in their use of simple geometric shapes.
Many critics consider minimalism a radical departure from traditional sculpture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: simplicity - the quality or condition of being easy to understand or do; plainness
