kindling

noun
material used to start a fire
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪndlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɪndlɪŋ / (BrE)
Small sticks or other materials that catch fire easily and are used to start a fire.

Examples

A pile of kindling
Before lighting the fireplace, she arranged kindling and paper beneath the logs.
Dry kindling is essential for starting a fire quickly.
They gathered some kindling and larger logs to build a campfire.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tinder - material, like dry grass or paper, used to start a fire easily
