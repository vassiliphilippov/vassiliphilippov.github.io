IHS

proper noun
US health agency
Pronunciation: / aɪ eɪtʃ ɛs / (AmE), / aɪ eɪtʃ ɛs / (BrE)
the United States federal agency responsible for providing health care services to American Indian and Alaska Native people

Examples

IHS health services
The IHS works to improve health outcomes for Native American communities across the country.
After moving to Alaska, she began working as a nurse for the IHS.
Many tribal clinics receive funding and support from the IHS to provide essential services.

  • synonym: Indian Health Service - the full name of the US federal agency providing health care to Native Americans
