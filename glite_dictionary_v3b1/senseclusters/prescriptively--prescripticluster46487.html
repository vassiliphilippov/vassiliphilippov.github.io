prescriptively

adverb
in a rule-setting way
6.9
Pronunciation: / prɪˈskrɪptɪvli / (AmE), / prɪˈskrɪptɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that sets rules or prescribes how something should be done

Examples

Explain prescriptively
The teacher explained grammar prescriptively, insisting on strict rules for every sentence.
Some dictionaries approach language prescriptively, telling users what is considered correct usage.
He criticized the committee for acting prescriptively rather than allowing for flexibility.

Related Terms

  • antonym: descriptively - in a way that describes how things are, not how they should be
