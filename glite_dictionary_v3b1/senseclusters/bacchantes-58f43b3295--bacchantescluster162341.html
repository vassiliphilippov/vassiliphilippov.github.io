Bacchantes

noun
female revelers
Pronunciation: / bækˈkæntiːz / (AmE), / bækˈkæntiːz / (BrE)
women who participate in wild, frenzied, or drunken celebrations, often used figuratively for female revelers

Examples

Wild Bacchantes party
The play depicted a group of Bacchantes dancing wildly in the forest under the moonlight.
During the festival, the women acted like Bacchantes, singing and celebrating late into the night.
Some critics described the crowd as modern Bacchantes lost in the excitement of the concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partygoer - someone who attends parties
  • synonym: reveller - a person who enjoys noisy festivities
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
