griller

noun
intense questioner
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡrɪlər / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪlə / (BrE)
a person who questions someone intensely or aggressively, especially during an interview or interrogation

Examples

Tough griller
The griller did not let the witness avoid any difficult questions during the hearing.
As a skilled griller, she quickly uncovered the truth in every interview.
Facing the experienced griller, the candidate struggled to answer the tough questions.
