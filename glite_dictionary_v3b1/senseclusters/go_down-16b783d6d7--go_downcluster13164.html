go down

verb
be swallowed (food/drink)
4.7 2.5
Pronunciation: /goʊ daʊn/ (AmE), /gəʊ daʊn/ (BrE)
(of food or drink) to be swallowed or to pass down the throat, especially with a particular quality (e.g., easily or with difficulty).

Examples

Food goes down easily
His first bite of the spicy dish wouldn't go down without a grimace.
The medicine didn't go down easily, so she had to drink some water.
This soup goes down smoothly on a cold day.
