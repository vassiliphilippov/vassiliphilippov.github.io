exfil

noun
removal operation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛksfɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛksfɪl / (BrE)
an operation, route, or vehicle used to remove people or data from a dangerous or sensitive area, or an instance of such removal

Examples

A secret exfil route
The team waited for the exfil signal before moving to the extraction point.
After the mission, the exfil was carried out by helicopter under cover of darkness.
They planned the exfil route carefully to avoid detection by security forces.

Related Terms

  • synonym: evacuation - the process of moving people from a place of danger
  • synonym: exfiltration - the act of removing people or data from a dangerous area
  • antonym: infil - an operation to enter a hostile or sensitive area
