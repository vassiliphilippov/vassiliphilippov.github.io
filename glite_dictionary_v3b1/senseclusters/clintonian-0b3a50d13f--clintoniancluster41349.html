Clintonian

adjective
of DeWitt Clinton
Pronunciation: / klɪnˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / klɪnˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to DeWitt Clinton, especially his policies or support for public works such as the Erie Canal in New York.

Examples

Clintonian public works
The Clintonian vision for New York included ambitious infrastructure projects like the Erie Canal.
Many historians admire the Clintonian approach to government investment in public works.
The city's development followed a distinctly Clintonian philosophy of modernization and progress.
