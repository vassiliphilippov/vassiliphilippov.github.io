dermic

adjective
of or relating to the dermis
Pronunciation: / ˈdɝː.mɪk / (AmE), / ˈdɜː.mɪk / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the dermis, the thick layer of living tissue below the epidermis of the skin.

Examples

Dermic layer
Certain medications are administered using dermic patches for easier absorption.
Researchers studied the effects of sunlight on dermic cells in laboratory conditions.
The dermic layer contains blood vessels and nerves essential for skin health.
