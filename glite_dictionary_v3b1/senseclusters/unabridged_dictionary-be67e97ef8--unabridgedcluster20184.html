unabridged dictionary

noun
a complete, comprehensive dictionary
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.əˈbrɪdʒd ˈdɪk.ʃəˌner.i / (AmE), / ˌʌn.əˈbrɪdʒd ˈdɪk.ʃən.ər.i / (BrE)
