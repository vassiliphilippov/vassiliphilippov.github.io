coffee

noun
a drink made from coffee beans
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.fi / (AmE), / ˈkɒf.i / (BrE)
A hot drink made by brewing ground roasted coffee beans with water.

Examples

A cup of coffee
She always starts her morning with a cup of coffee.
The aroma of freshly brewed coffee filled the kitchen.
Would you like some milk or sugar in your coffee?

Related Terms

  • antonym: tea - a hot drink made by infusing dried tea leaves in water
