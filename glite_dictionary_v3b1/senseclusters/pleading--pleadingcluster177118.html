pleading

adjective
expressing an urgent or begging request
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpliː.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈpliː.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
showing or expressing a strong, often emotional request or appeal; begging for something earnestly

Examples

Pleading eyes

She gave him a pleading look, hoping he would change his mind.
