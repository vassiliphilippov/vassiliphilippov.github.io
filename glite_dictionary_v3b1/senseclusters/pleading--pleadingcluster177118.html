pleading

adjective
expressing an urgent or begging request
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpliːdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpliːdɪŋ / (BrE)
showing or expressing a strong, often emotional request or appeal; begging for something earnestly

Examples

Pleading eyes
His pleading voice made it difficult to refuse his request.
She gave him a pleading look, hoping he would change his mind.
With pleading eyes, the child asked for another piece of cake.

Related Terms

  • synonym: begging - asking for something urgently or desperately
  • antonym: demanding - insisting firmly on something
