jointed charlock

noun
Eurasian weed with podlike fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd ˈtʃɑrˌlɑk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɔɪntɪd ˈtʃɑːlɒk / (BrE)
A Eurasian weed with yellow, mauve, or white flowers and podlike fruits.
