virginiana

noun
species name for Virginia
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜːrdʒɪˈniːənə / (AmE), / vɜːdʒɪˈniːənə / (BrE)
a Latin species name element meaning 'from Virginia', used in scientific names to indicate origin from Virginia

Examples

Diospyros virginiana
The scientific name for the American persimmon is Diospyros virginiana.
Quercus virginiana is commonly known as the southern live oak.
Many plants and animals with virginiana in their names are native to Virginia.
