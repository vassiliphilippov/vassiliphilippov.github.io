peripherally

adverb
on or relating to the edge; secondarily
6.4 5.5
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈrɪf.ər.ə.liː/ /; / /pə.ˈrɪ.frə.liː/ (AmE), /pə.ˈrɪf.ər.ə.liː/ /; / /pə.ˈrɪ.frə.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner relating to or situated on the edge or outer part of something; concerning a minor or secondary role or aspect.

Examples

Participate peripherally
She participated peripherally in the project, handling only minor tasks.
The illness affects the brain peripherally rather than directly.
The signal was detected peripherally by sensors on the edge of the device.
