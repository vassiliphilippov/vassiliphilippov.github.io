adenomatous

adjective
gland-like tissue
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌædɪˈnoʊmətəs / (AmE), / ˌædɪˈnəʊmətəs / (BrE)
relating to or having the characteristics of glandular tissue, especially as found in benign tumors (adenomas)

Examples

A biopsy confirmed the presence of adenomatous cells in the sample.
The doctor explained that the adenomatous tissue was not cancerous but should still be removed.
An adenomatous polyp was detected during the patient's routine colonoscopy.
Pathologists identified the growth as adenomatous after examining the biopsy sample.
