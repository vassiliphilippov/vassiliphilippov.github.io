mark

verb
to brand or stigmatize as disgraceful
Pronunciation: / mɑrk / (AmE), / mɑːk / (BrE)
to openly or formally accuse or brand someone as disgraceful or shameful

Examples

To mark someone as guilty
Once accused, he was publicly marked and could never regain his former reputation.
She was unfairly marked by rumors and lost many friends.
The scandal marked him as an outcast in the community.

Related Terms

  • synonym: denounce - to publicly declare something or someone to be wrong or evil
  • synonym: stigmatize - to describe or regard as worthy of disgrace or disapproval
