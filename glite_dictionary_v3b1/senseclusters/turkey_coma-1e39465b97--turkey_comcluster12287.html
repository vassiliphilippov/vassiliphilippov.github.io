turkey coma

noun
drowsiness after a large meal, esp. turkey
9.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtɜːr.kiː ˈkoʊ.mə/ (AmE), /ˈtɜː.kiː ˈkəʊ.mə/ (BrE)
A state of drowsiness or sleepiness experienced after eating turkey or a very large meal, especially during holiday feasts.

Examples

A turkey coma after dinner
Everyone lounged around in a turkey coma after Thanksgiving dinner.
Half the family was in a turkey coma before dessert was even served.
I tried to stay awake, but the turkey coma was too strong.
