literature

noun
published writings on a subject
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬ərətʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlɪtərətʃə / (BrE)
all the published writings on a particular subject or in a specific area, especially technical or academic works

Examples

Medical literature
New studies have added important insights to the medical literature on the disease.
She reviewed all the recent literature on climate change before writing her report.
The safety literature for this medication lists several possible side effects.
