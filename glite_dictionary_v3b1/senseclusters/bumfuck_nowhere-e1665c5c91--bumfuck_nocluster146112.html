bumfuck nowhere

noun
extremely remote and insignificant place (vulgar slang)
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌm.fʌk ˈnaʊ.wer/ /; / /ˈbʌm.fʌk ˈnaʊ.her/ (AmE), /ˈbʌm.fʌk ˈnəʊ.weə/ /; / /ˈbʌm.fʌk ˈnaʊ.weə/ (BrE)
(vulgar slang) A very remote, desolate, or insignificant place, often with nothing of interest and far from civilization.

Examples

Lost in bumfuck nowhere
After hours of driving, they realized they were lost in bumfuck nowhere.
Nobody visits during winter because it's basically bumfuck nowhere out here.
The town was so small it felt like bumfuck nowhere to anyone who visited.
