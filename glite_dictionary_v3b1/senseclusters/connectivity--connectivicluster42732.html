Synonyms

  • interconnectivity
    6.3 0.19/m
    interconnectivity describes devices or networks that can link and share data, while connectivity names the general state or ability to communicate.
  • interconnection
    4.4 1.60/m
    interconnection is the process or actual links between systems that let them work together, while connectivity is the overall ability to connect and communicate.
  • interconnectedness
    6.8 0.80/m
    interconnectedness shows how parts are mutually related and affect each other, while connectivity focuses on the practical ability of systems to link and communicate.
