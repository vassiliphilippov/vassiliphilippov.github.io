styleless

adjective
lacking style or elegance
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪl.ləs / (AmE), / ˈstaɪl.ləs / (BrE)
Lacking in style, elegance, or fashionable appearance.

Examples

A styleless design
Although the house was expensive, the interior felt cold and styleless.
His styleless suit made him stand out at the fashion-forward party.
The artist's early works were considered styleless, lacking the flair of his later pieces.
