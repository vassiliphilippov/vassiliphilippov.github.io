simple

adjective
plain; unornamented
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪmpəl / (AmE), / ˈsɪmpəl / (BrE)
plain and without decoration or ornament; not elaborate or fancy in design or style.

Examples

A simple dress
The bride wore a simple white gown with no lace or embellishments.
The house had a simple design that felt warm and welcoming.
They chose simple furniture with clean lines for the living room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - not decorated or elaborate; simple in design or style
