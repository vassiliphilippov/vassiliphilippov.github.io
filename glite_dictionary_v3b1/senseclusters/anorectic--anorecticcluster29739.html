anorectic

noun
person with anorexia nervosa
6.1 8.0
Pronunciation: /æn.ə.ˈrek.tɪk/ (AmE), /æn.ə.ˈrek.tɪk/ (BrE)
a person who suffers from anorexia nervosa, an eating disorder characterized by an abnormally low body weight and fear of gaining weight.
