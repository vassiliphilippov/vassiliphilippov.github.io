criminal

noun
person who has committed a crime
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəl / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnl / (BrE)
a person who has committed a crime or has been legally convicted of a crime

Examples

A criminal escapes
Many believe that education can help reform a criminal and reduce repeat offenses.
The criminal was finally brought to justice after years of evading the authorities.
The police arrested the criminal after a long investigation.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocent - a person not guilty of a crime
  • synonym: lawbreaker - a person who violates the law
  • synonym: offender - a person who commits an illegal act
