stitcher

noun
person who finishes sewing garments
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪtʃər / (AmE), / ˈstɪtʃə / (BrE)
a person or machine that sews pieces of material together using stitches

Examples

A stitcher sews fabric
After years as a stitcher, she became the head of the costume department.
Each costume is checked by a skilled stitcher before it is approved for use.
The stitcher carefully hemmed the delicate fabric to finish the dress.
The stitcher spent hours repairing the torn jacket with careful needlework.
Modern factories often use an automated stitcher to assemble garments quickly.
As a skilled stitcher, she could create beautiful embroidery by hand.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seamstress - a woman whose occupation is sewing
  • synonym: sewing machine - a machine used for stitching fabric together
  • synonym: tailor - a person who makes or alters garments
