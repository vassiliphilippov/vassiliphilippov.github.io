hamfat

noun
slang for bad performer
Pronunciation: / ˈhæmˌfæt / (AmE), / ˈhæmˌfæt / (BrE)
an old slang term for an amateur or poor performer, especially in theater

Examples

A hamfat actor
The director refused to cast a hamfat in the leading role of the play.
Back in the day, a hamfat was often laughed at by the audience.
He was called a hamfat after forgetting his lines on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amateur - a person who is not a professional in a particular activity
  • synonym: novice - a person new to or inexperienced in a field
  • antonym: professional - a person who is skilled and paid for their work
