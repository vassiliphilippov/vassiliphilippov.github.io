roundup

noun
gathering of livestock for counting or sale
4.6 0.39/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆraÊŠnd.ÊŒp / (AmE), / ËˆraÊŠnd.ÊŒp / (BrE)
an act of gathering together livestock, such as cattle or horses, so they can be counted, branded, or sold

Examples

Cattle roundup
During the roundup, skilled cowboys ride out to gather strays from the hills.
Every spring, the ranchers organize a roundup to bring in all the cattle from the open range.
The annual roundup is an important event for the local farming community.
