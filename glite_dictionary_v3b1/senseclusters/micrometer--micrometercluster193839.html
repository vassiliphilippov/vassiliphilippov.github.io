micrometer

noun
precision instrument for small measurements
3.8 5.5
Pronunciation: /maɪ.ˈkrɑː.mɪ.tər/ /; / /maɪ.ˈkroʊ.mɪ.tər/ (AmE), /maɪ.ˈkrɒ.mɪ.tə/ /; / /maɪ.krə.ˈmiː.tə/ (BrE)
an instrument used for measuring extremely small distances or thicknesses with great accuracy, especially in mechanical engineering and machining

Examples

He calibrated the micrometer before measuring the diameter of the wire.

The technician used a micrometer to check the thickness of the metal sheet.
