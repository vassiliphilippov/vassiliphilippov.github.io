account representative

noun
client account manager in business
Pronunciation: / əˈkaʊnt ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / əˈkaʊnt ˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
a person responsible for managing and servicing the accounts of clients in a business such as an advertising agency or brokerage.

Examples

Account representative manages clients
As an account representative, she handles several major clients for the firm.
Many companies rely on an account representative to provide excellent customer service and maintain client relationships.
The account representative scheduled a meeting to discuss the new campaign strategy.
