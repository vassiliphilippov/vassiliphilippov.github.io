gamma-interferon

noun
immune system protein produced by T cells
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɡæmə ˌɪntərˈfɪrɑn / (AmE), / ˌɡæmə ˌɪntəˈfɪərɒn / (BrE)
a form of interferon produced mainly by T cells and macrophages that plays a role in activating phagocytes and regulating the immune response
