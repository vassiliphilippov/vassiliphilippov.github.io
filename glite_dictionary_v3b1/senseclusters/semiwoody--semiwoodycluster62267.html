semiwoody

adjective
partly wooden
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛmiˈwʊdi / (AmE), / ˌsɛmiˈwʊdi / (BrE)
partly made of wood or having both wooden and non-wooden parts in its structure or composition

Examples

Semiwoody fence
The architect designed a semiwoody fence that combined wooden panels with steel posts.
Some shrubs have semiwoody stems that are not fully hard like tree trunks.
They chose a semiwoody table with a wooden top and metal legs for their kitchen.

  • degree higher: wooden - made entirely of wood
