sooner

adverb
rather; more willingly
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːnər / (AmE), / ˈsuːnə / (BrE)
Used to indicate preference for one thing over another; rather; more willingly.

Examples

Sooner do it myself
He said he'd sooner walk than take the crowded bus.
I would sooner stay at home than go to that party.
I'd sooner not discuss this issue right now.
