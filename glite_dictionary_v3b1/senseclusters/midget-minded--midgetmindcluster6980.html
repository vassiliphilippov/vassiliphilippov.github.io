midget-minded

adjective
having a narrow or limited perspective
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdʒɪt ˌmaɪndɪd / (AmE), / ˈmɪdʒɪt ˌmaɪndɪd / (BrE)
Having a narrow or limited perspective; lacking breadth of vision or ambition.

Examples

A midget-minded person
Don't allow midget-minded critics to discourage your ambition.
She accused the committee of being midget-minded about new initiatives.
Their midget-minded approach kept the project from reaching its full potential.

Related Terms

  • synonym: narrow-minded - unwilling to consider new ideas; having a limited outlook
  • antonym: open-minded - willing to consider new ideas; receptive to a wide range of perspectives
