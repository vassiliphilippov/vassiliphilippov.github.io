exceptionalism

noun
belief in uniqueness
8.8
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛpʃənəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛpʃənəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the belief or condition that a person, group, or nation is unique, special, or superior, and may be an exception to general rules or standards

Examples
