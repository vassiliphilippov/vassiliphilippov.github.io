spanker

noun
person who delivers a spanking
Pronunciation: / ˈspæŋkər / (AmE), / ˈspaŋkə / (BrE)
a person who strikes someone, especially a child, with an open hand as a form of punishment

Examples

A strict spanker
As a teacher, he refused to play the role of spanker when discipline was needed.
Her uncle was the designated spanker in the family, much to the children's dismay.
Stories of the old school spanker are less common in modern education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slapper - a person who slaps someone with the open hand
