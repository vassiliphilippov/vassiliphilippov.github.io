ninepins

noun
bowling game with nine pins
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪnpɪnz / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnpɪnz / (BrE)
A traditional bowling game in which a ball is rolled at a set of nine standing wooden pins.

Examples

Playing ninepins game
In nineteenth-century England, ninepins was a popular pastime among villagers.
The group decided to play a game of ninepins at the old community center.
The rules of ninepins differ slightly from modern bowling games.
