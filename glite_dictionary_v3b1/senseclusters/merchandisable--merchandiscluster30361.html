merchandisable

adjective
suitable for sale
5.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààm…úÀêrt É…ônda…™z…ôb…ôl / (AmE), / Ààm…úÀêt É…ônda…™z…ôb…ôl / (BrE)
able to be marketed or sold as merchandise

Examples

A merchandisable product
The new toy is highly merchandisable because it appeals to children of all ages.
Designers focus on creating merchandisable products that can succeed in a competitive market.
A simple logo makes the item more merchandisable for fans and collectors.

Synonyms

  • sellable
    3.1 0.07/m
    sellable is suitable to be offered for sale by anyone, while merchandisable suggests being prepared and sold as merchandise.
  • marketable
    3.8 2.23/m
    marketable often implies demand or easy sale, while merchandisable focuses on being able to be sold as merchandise.
  • merchantable
    6.9 0.01/m
    merchantable means legally fit and of acceptable quality for sale, while merchandisable is a general idea of sellability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marketable - able to be sold easily or attractively
  • antonym: unsellable - not able to be sold
