merchandisable

adjective
suitable for sale
Pronunciation: / ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzəbəl / (AmE), / ˈmɜːtʃəndaɪzəbəl / (BrE)
able to be marketed or sold as merchandise

Examples

A merchandisable product
The new toy is highly merchandisable because it appeals to children of all ages.
Designers focus on creating merchandisable products that can succeed in a competitive market.
A simple logo makes the item more merchandisable for fans and collectors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: marketable - able to be sold easily or attractively
  • antonym: unsellable - not able to be sold
