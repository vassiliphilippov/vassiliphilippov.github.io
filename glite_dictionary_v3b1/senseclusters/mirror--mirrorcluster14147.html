mirror

verb
reflect as a mirror does
4.1 2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrər /; / ˈmɪrɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɪrə / (BrE)
to reflect or show something, like a mirror

Examples

To mirror feelings
Art often mirrors society by showing both its beauty and its flaws.
Her expression mirrored his concern as they listened to the news.
The lake mirrored the stunning colors of the sunset perfectly.
Her emotions mirrored the fear she saw in the crowd.
The company’s growth mirrors the expansion of the entire industry.
The novel mirrors real-life events that happened in the author’s hometown.
