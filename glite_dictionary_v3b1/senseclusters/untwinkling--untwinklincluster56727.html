untwinkling

adjective
not sparkling
8.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
not shining, sparkling, or twinkling; lacking a flickering or bright quality

Examples

Untwinkling lights
The untwinkling lights in the hallway made the atmosphere feel gloomy.
Through the fog, only the untwinkling outline of the city skyline was visible.
Her eyes were untwinkling with sadness after hearing the news.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sparkling - shining with flashes of light
