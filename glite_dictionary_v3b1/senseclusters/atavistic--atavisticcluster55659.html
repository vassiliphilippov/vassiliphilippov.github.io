atavistic

adjective
relating to ancestral reversion
Pronunciation: / ˌæt̬əˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌatəˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characterized by atavism; reverting to or suggesting the characteristics of a remote ancestor or primitive type.

Examples

An atavistic instinct
Some people believe that aggression is an atavistic instinct inherited from distant ancestors.
The artist's work has an atavistic quality, echoing prehistoric cave paintings.
The tribe's atavistic rituals have survived for thousands of years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ancestral - relating to family ancestors
  • antonym: modern - relating to the present or recent times, in contrast to ancestral or ancient
