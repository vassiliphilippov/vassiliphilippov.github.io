densify

verb
to make denser
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛnsɪˌfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈdɛnsɪˌfaɪ /; / ˈdɛnsɪfaɪ / (BrE)
to make something more dense or compact, or to become more dense (for example, by adding more buildings, people, or material in a space)

Examples

To densify a city
The city plans to densify the downtown area by building more apartment complexes.
Manufacturers use special techniques to densify the material for greater strength.
Efforts to densify the forest have helped restore local wildlife habitats.
Engineers use pressure to densify the material for greater strength.

Certain processes can densify soil, making it harder for water to pass through.

As more people move in, the neighborhood will gradually densify over time.
