reassimilate

verb
to absorb again
7.3
Pronunciation: /riː.ə.ˈsɪm.ɪ.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈsɪm.ɪ.leɪt/ (AmE), /riː.ə.ˈsɪm.ɪ.leɪt/ /; / /rɪ.ə.ˈsɪm.ɪ.leɪt/ (BrE)
to absorb or incorporate something again, especially nutrients or substances, into an organism or system

Examples

To reassimilate nutrients
After the drought, the plants slowly began to reassimilate nutrients from the enriched soil.
It took several weeks for her body to reassimilate vitamins after the illness.
Scientists observed how the cells could reassimilate certain substances after being deprived of them.
