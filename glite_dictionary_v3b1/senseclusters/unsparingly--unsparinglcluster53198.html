unsparingly

adverb
in a relentless or harsh manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspɛrɪŋli / (AmE), / ʌnˈspɛːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows no restraint or moderation; without holding back (can be harsh or generous depending on context)

Examples

Criticize unsparingly
He criticized the government's response unsparingly, leaving no aspect unexamined.
She judged her own performance unsparingly, determined to learn from every mistake.
The editor spoke unsparingly of the novel's weaknesses in her review.
The coach praised his team unsparingly after their impressive victory.
She donated unsparingly to charities throughout her life.
Critics wrote unsparingly about the flaws in the new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: generously - in a way that shows a willingness to give more than expected
  • antonym: gently - in a kind or mild manner
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or unkind manner
  • synonym: lavishly - in a very abundant or extravagant way
  • antonym: sparingly - in a way that is careful or limited, not using much
