inelegantly

adverb
in a way that is not elegant
6.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛlɪɡəntli / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛlɪɡəntli / (BrE)
In a manner that lacks elegance or grace.

Examples

To inelegantly stumble
He inelegantly stumbled over his words during the speech.
The dancer inelegantly lost her balance during the performance.
They inelegantly stacked the chairs, making the room look messy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awkwardly - in an awkward or clumsy manner
  • antonym: gracefully - in a graceful, elegant manner
