back

verb
have the rear part facing something
5.9
Pronunciation: / bæk / (AmE), / bæk / (BrE)
(of a building or area) to have the rear part facing or adjacent to a particular place.

Examples

House backs the park
His house backs the woods, providing plenty of shade in summer.
Our garden backs onto the river, giving us a lovely view.
The theater backs a busy street, so it can get noisy during the day.
