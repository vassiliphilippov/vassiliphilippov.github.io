unmoved

adjective
not affected emotionally
2.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ən.ˈmuːvd/ /; / /ʌn.ˈmuːvd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈmuːvd/ /; / /ən.ˈmuːvd/ (BrE)
not affected by emotion or excitement; showing no visible reaction to something that might affect others.

Examples

He stood unmoved
Despite their efforts to persuade him, he stood unmoved by all arguments.
Even after hearing the tragic news, she remained unmoved and calm.
The jury was unmoved by the defendant's emotional plea.

Related Terms

  • antonym: affected - emotionally touched, especially in a way that produces a visible reaction
  • synonym: impassive - showing no emotion; not easily excited or upset
  • synonym: indifferent - having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned
