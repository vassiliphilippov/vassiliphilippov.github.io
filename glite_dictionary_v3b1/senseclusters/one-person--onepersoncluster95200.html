one-person

adjective
for one person only
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈpɝːsən / (AmE), / wʌn ˈpɜːsən / (BrE)
designed for or intended to be used by only one person

Examples

A one-person show
The auditorium was packed for her acclaimed one-person show.
This tiny office is really only suitable as a one-person workspace.
We decided to buy a one-person tent for our solo camping trips.
