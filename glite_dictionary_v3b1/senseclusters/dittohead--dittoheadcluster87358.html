dittohead

noun
uncritical follower
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdɪt.oʊ.hed/ (AmE), /ˈdɪ.təʊ.hed/ (BrE)
a person who uncritically agrees with and repeats the opinions of a leader or commentator, especially in media or politics

Examples

Many dittoheads agree
Many dittoheads called in to support the radio host's controversial statements.
He was labeled a dittohead because he never questioned the commentator's views.
Critics argue that dittoheads simply repeat what they hear without critical thinking.
He proudly called himself a dittohead and listened to the radio show every day.
Many dittoheads gathered at the event to show their support for the host.
She was labeled a dittohead because she agreed with everything Rush Limbaugh said.

Related Terms

  • antonym: critic - a person who expresses an unfavorable opinion of something
  • synonym: follower - a person who supports or admires a leader
  • antonym: independent thinker - a person who forms their own opinions
  • synonym: yes-man - a person who always agrees with their superior
