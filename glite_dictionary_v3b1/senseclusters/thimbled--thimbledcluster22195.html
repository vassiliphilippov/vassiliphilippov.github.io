thimbled

adjective
marked with small dents
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəld / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəld / (BrE)
having small dents or marks resembling those made by a thimble.

Examples

Thimbled metal surface
The old brass plate was thimbled with tiny dents from years of rough handling.
After dropping the metal cup, she noticed it was thimbled all over its surface.
The craftsman examined the thimbled finish and decided to polish the object again.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dimpled - having small indentations or depressions on the surface
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface; free from roughness or indentations
