brokenness

noun
spiritual humility
8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈbroʊ.kən.nəs/ /; / /ˈbroʊk.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈbrəʊ.kən.nəs/ /; / /ˈbrəʊk.nəs/ (BrE)
a state of deep humility, repentance, or spiritual contrition, often felt after recognizing one's faults or failures

Examples

Spiritual brokenness
After the loss, he felt a deep brokenness that led him to reflect on his life.
Her brokenness was evident as she spoke honestly about her past mistakes.
Many people find strength in their brokenness and use it as a starting point for personal growth.
