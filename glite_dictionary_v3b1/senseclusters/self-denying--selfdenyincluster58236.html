self-denying

adjective
willing to deny one's own interests
3.4 5.6
Pronunciation: /self.dɪ.ˈnaɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /səlf.dɪ.ˈnaɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /self.də.ˈnaɪ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /self.dɪ.ˈnaɪ.ɪŋ/ /; / /səlf.dɪ.ˈnaɪ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
