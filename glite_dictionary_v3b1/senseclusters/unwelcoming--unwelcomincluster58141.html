unwelcoming

adjective
not inviting
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈwɛlkəmɪŋ / (BrE)
not friendly or inviting; making people feel uncomfortable or not wanted

Examples

An unwelcoming place
The staff at the hotel were so unwelcoming that we decided to leave early.
A cold, unwelcoming wind blew through the empty streets.
She found the new school environment unwelcoming and struggled to make friends.
The manager's unwelcoming tone discouraged customers from returning to the restaurant.
We found the atmosphere in the office rather unwelcoming on our first day.
Her unwelcoming expression made it clear that visitors were not appreciated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - lacking warmth or friendliness
  • synonym: hostile - unfriendly or antagonistic
  • synonym: inhospitable - harsh and difficult to live in or visit
  • antonym: welcoming - friendly and inviting
