landsliding

verb
to slide downhill
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈlændˌslaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlændˌslaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
to move rapidly downhill as a mass of earth or rock due to gravity.

Examples

The hill is landsliding
After days of heavy rain, the mountain began landsliding dangerously close to the village.
Engineers monitored the hillside to prevent it from landsliding onto the road below.
The news reported that several homes were damaged when the slope started landsliding last night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: falling - to drop or come down freely under gravity
  • synonym: sliding - to move smoothly along a surface
