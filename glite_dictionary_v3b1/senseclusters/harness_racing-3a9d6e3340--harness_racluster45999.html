harness racing

noun
horse racing with horses trotting or pacing in sulkies
6.9 3.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhɑːr.nəs ˈreɪ.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhɑːr.nɪs ˈreɪ.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈhɑː.nəs ˈreɪ.sɪŋ/ /; / /ˈhɑː.nɪs ˈreɪ.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
A form of horse racing in which drivers ride in lightweight two-wheeled carts (sulkies) pulled by horses that trot or pace rather than gallop.

Examples

Harness racing horses
Her grandfather was a champion in harness racing during the 1960s.
Many people travel to watch harness racing events during the summer season.
The fairground will be hosting a harness racing competition this weekend.
