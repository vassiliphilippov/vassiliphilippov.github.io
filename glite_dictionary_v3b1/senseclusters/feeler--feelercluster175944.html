feeler

noun
sensory appendage on insect/crustacean head
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfilər /; / ˈfiːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈfiːlə / (BrE)
a long, thin part of an animal (such as an insect or crustacean) used for touching and sensing the environment

Scientists observed the movement of the insect's feeler as it explored its surroundings.
