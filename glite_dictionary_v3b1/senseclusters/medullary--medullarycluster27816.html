medullary

adjective
of the medulla oblongata
Pronunciation: / məˈdʌləri / (AmE), / mɪˈdʌləri / (BrE)
relating to the medulla oblongata, the lower part of the brainstem

Examples

Medullary brain region
A tumor in the medullary region can have serious neurological consequences.
Researchers studied the medullary centers responsible for vital autonomic functions.
The patient showed medullary damage, leading to problems with breathing and heart rate.
