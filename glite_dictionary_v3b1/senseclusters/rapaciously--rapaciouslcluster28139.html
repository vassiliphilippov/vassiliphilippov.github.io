rapaciously

adverb
in a greedy or grasping manner
Pronunciation: / rəˈpeɪʃəsli / (AmE), / rəˈpeɪʃəsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows greed or an excessive desire to take or consume more than is needed.

Examples

To rapaciously grab
He rapaciously grabbed everything on the table, leaving nothing for the others.
Investors rapaciously sought profits, showing little concern for ethics or regulations.
The organization rapaciously exploited natural resources without regard for future generations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generously - in a generous or giving manner (opposite of greedily)
  • synonym: greedily - in a greedy or eager manner
