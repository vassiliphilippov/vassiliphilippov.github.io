gastroenterology

noun
study of the digestive system and its diseases
1.8 5.1
Pronunciation: /gæs.troʊ.en.təˈrɑː.lə.dʒiː/ (AmE), /gæs.trəʊ.en.təˈrɒl.ə.dʒiː/ (BrE)
The branch of medicine concerned with the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders of the stomach, intestines, and related organs.

Examples

Gastroenterology study
Advances in gastroenterology have greatly improved the treatment of ulcers and other stomach problems.
She decided to specialize in gastroenterology after finding an interest in digestive system disorders.
The hospital has an excellent gastroenterology department for diagnosing intestinal diseases.
