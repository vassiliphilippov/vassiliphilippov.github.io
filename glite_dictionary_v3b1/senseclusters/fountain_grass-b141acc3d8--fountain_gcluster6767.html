fountain grass

noun
ornamental grass with arching flower plumes
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊntən ɡræs / (AmE), / ˈfaʊntɪn ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a tall perennial ornamental grass with long, arching flower plumes, native to tropical Africa and Asia, often used in landscaping

Examples

Fountain grass plant
Fountain grass adds a graceful, airy quality to garden borders with its waving flower plumes.
She chose fountain grass to create a soft, natural look along her driveway.
The landscaping plan included several varieties of fountain grass for their drought tolerance and visual appeal.
