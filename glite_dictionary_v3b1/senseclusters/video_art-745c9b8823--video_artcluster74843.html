video art

noun
art involving moving images and sound
1.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪd.ɪ.oʊ ɑːrt/ /; / /ˈvɪd.joʊ ɑːrt/ (AmE), /ˈvɪd.ɪ.əʊ ɑːt/ /; / /ˈvɪd.jəʊ ɑːt/ (BrE)
A form of artistic expression that uses moving images and often sound, typically produced and exhibited using video technology.

Examples

Video art exhibition
Many emerging artists are experimenting with sound and video art to push creative boundaries.
She explored political themes in her video art installations.
The museum is featuring an exhibition of contemporary video art from around the world.
