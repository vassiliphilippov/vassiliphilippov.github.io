Sergei Prokofiev

proper noun
Russian composer (1891–1953)
Pronunciation: / sərˈɡeɪ proʊˈkoʊfiːɛf / (AmE), / səˈɡeɪ prəˈkɒfiːɛf / (BrE)
(1891–1953) Russian composer renowned for his ballets, symphonies, and operas, considered one of the major figures of 20th-century classical music.

Examples

Sergei Prokofiev ballet music
Sergei Prokofiev composed the famous ballet 'Romeo and Juliet'.
Many critics regard Sergei Prokofiev as one of the most influential composers of the 20th century.
The music of Sergei Prokofiev is still performed in concert halls around the world.
