apportion

verb
to distribute or allocate in shares
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɹʃən /; / əˈpɔr.ʃən / (AmE), / əˈpɔːʃ(ə)n /; / əˈpɔː.ʃən / (BrE)
to divide and give out parts of something among people or groups

Examples

To apportion blame fairly
It is important to apportion blame fairly when mistakes are made.
Resources must be apportioned according to the needs of each project.
The committee will apportion the funds among the various departments.
After the meal, they apportioned the remaining food equally.
It is difficult to apportion blame when both sides are at fault.
The manager must apportion resources fairly among all departments.

  • synonym: distribute - to give out or share among a number of recipients
  • antonym: withhold - to refuse to give or grant something
