apportion

verb
to distribute or allocate in shares

to divide and give out parts of something among people or groups

Examples

It is important to apportion blame fairly when mistakes are made.

Synonyms

  • parcel out
    4.8 0.24/m
    parcel out divides things into separate small parts and gives them to people, while apportion is more formal and used for shares.
  • portion
    3.0 0.18/m
    portion gives smaller shares to people in a simple way, while apportion sounds more formal and often divides legally.
  • distribute
    2.0 8.85/m
    distribute means to give out or spread items to many people or places, while apportion focuses on dividing precise shares.
  • allocate
    4.5 13/m
    allocate assigns resources or duties for a specific plan or purpose, while apportion divides and gives out shares among people.
