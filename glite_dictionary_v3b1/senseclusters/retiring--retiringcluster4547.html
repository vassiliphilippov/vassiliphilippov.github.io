retiring

adjective
shy; not drawing attention
5.8
Pronunciation: / rɪˈtaɪərɪŋ /; / rɪˈtaɪɚɪŋ / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪərɪŋ / (BrE)
shy and quiet; preferring not to draw attention to oneself

Examples

Retiring personality
Although Emma was talented, her retiring nature made her avoid the spotlight.
He has always been a retiring type who prefers to listen rather than talk.
The retiring student sat quietly at the back of the classroom.
Despite her accomplishments, she remained a retiring presence at the office events.
He is a retiring man who rarely speaks in public.
The new student was polite but rather retiring in classroom discussions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diffident - lacking self-confidence; modest or shy
  • antonym: outgoing - sociable and enthusiastic about meeting new people
  • synonym: shy - nervous or timid about meeting or speaking to people
  • synonym: unassuming - not pretentious or arrogant; modest
