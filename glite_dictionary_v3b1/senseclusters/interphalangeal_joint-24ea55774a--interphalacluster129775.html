interphalangeal joint

noun
joint between finger or toe bones
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tɚ.fəˈlæn.dʒi.əl dʒɔɪnt / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.fəˈlæn.dʒɪ.əl dʒɔɪnt / (BrE)
any of the joints between the phalanges (bones) of the fingers or toes.

Examples

The interphalangeal joint of finger
An injury to the interphalangeal joint can limit movement in the affected finger.
Arthritis often affects the interphalangeal joints of elderly patients' hands.
Swelling around the interphalangeal joint was visible after he jammed his toe.

Related Terms
