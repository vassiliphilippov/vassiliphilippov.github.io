affinage

noun
refining or maturing process
Pronunciation: / æfəˈnɑːʒ / (AmE), / ˌæfɪˈnɑːʒ / (BrE)
the process of refining or maturing a product, especially cheese or metals, to improve its quality

Examples

The affinage of cheese
The affinage of cheese requires careful control of temperature and humidity.
During the affinage of metals, impurities are removed to achieve higher purity.
Skilled artisans oversee the affinage process to ensure the best flavor in specialty cheeses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aging - the process of allowing something to mature over time
  • synonym: refinement - the process of making something pure or improved
