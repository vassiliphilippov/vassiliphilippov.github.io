untenability

noun
impossibility to defend
9.1
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ten.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /ʌn.tə.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ʌn.ten.ə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ /; / /ʌn.tə.nə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
