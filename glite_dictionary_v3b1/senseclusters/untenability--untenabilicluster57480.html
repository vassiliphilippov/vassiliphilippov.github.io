untenability

noun
impossibility to defend
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌʌntəˈnæbɪlɪti /; / ˌʌntəˌnæbɪˈlɪti / (AmE), / ˌʌntɪnəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being impossible to defend, justify, or maintain

Examples

The untenability of proposal
The untenability of the proposal was exposed by the expert's detailed analysis.
After reviewing the evidence, the judge commented on the untenability of the defendant's claims.
The scientist admitted the untenability of the old theory in light of new discoveries.
The untenability of their argument became clear after new evidence was presented.
Due to financial losses, the untenability of the business model was soon apparent.
Political pressure led to the untenability of the leader's position within the party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indefensibility - the state of being impossible to defend
  • antonym: tenability - the quality of being able to be defended or justified
  • synonym: unsustainability - the quality of not being able to continue or be maintained
