unmanageableness

noun
quality of being unmanageable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænɪdʒəblnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænɪdʒəblnəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being difficult or impossible to manage or control.

Examples

The unmanageableness of anger
Her anger contributed to the unmanageableness of the group situation.
The unmanageableness of the new software frustrated even the most experienced users.
They were concerned about the unmanageableness of the rapidly expanding project.

Related Terms

  • antonym: manageability - the quality of being easy to manage or control
  • synonym: unmanageability - the quality or state of being unmanageable (variant)
