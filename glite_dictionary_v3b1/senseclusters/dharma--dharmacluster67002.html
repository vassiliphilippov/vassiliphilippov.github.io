dharma

noun
cosmic principle or moral duty (Indian religions)
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑrmə / (AmE), / ˈdɑːmə / (BrE)
In Hinduism, Buddhism, Sikhism, Jainism, and other religions, the fundamental principle that orders the cosmos and guides individual conduct; moral or religious duty.

Examples

Follow dharma duties
He struggled to fulfill his dharma as a teacher and a father.
In Buddhism, following one's dharma leads to enlightenment.
The concept of dharma is central to Hindu philosophy and impacts every aspect of life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: karma - the concept of action and its consequences in Indian religions
