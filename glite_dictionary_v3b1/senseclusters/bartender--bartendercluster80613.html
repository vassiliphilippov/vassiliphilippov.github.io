bartender

noun
person who serves drinks at a bar
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːrˌtɛndər / (AmE), / ˈbɑːˌtɛndə /; / ˈbɑːtɛndə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to mix and serve drinks, especially alcoholic ones, at a bar or restaurant

Examples

The bartender serves drinks
After work, he often chatted with the bartender while waiting for his friends.
She asked the bartender for a glass of water between drinks.
The bartender recommended a unique cocktail that I had never tried before.
The bartender recommended a new cocktail to the guests at the bar.
After a long shift, the bartender cleaned the glasses and wiped down the counter.
She worked as a bartender while studying at university to earn extra money.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barmaid - female bartender (chiefly British)
  • synonym: barman - male bartender (chiefly British)
  • antonym: customer - a person who buys goods or services
  • synonym: waiter - a person who serves food and drinks in a restaurant
