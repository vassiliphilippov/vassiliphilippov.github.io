internment camp

noun
camp for detaining prisoners or civilians
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈtɜrn.mənt kæmp / (AmE), / ɪnˈtɜːn.mənt kamp /; / ɪnˈtɜːn.mənt kæmp / (BrE)
a place where people are kept under guard, usually during war, for political or military reasons
