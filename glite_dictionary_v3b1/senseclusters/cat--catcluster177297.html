cat

noun
a small domesticated or wild feline mammal
4.0
Pronunciation: / kæt / (AmE), / kæt / (BrE)
a small domesticated animal with soft fur and retractable claws, or any large wild member of the cat family such as a lion or tiger

Examples

A pet cat
After adopting a cat, Lena found her home much livelier and more comforting.
Children laughed as the playful cat chased a ball of yarn across the floor.
The cat jumped gracefully onto the windowsill to watch the birds outside.
The African lion is a magnificent cat living on the savannah.
The tiger is the largest cat found in Asia.
Unlike domestic pets, most cats in this category are fierce predators.

Related Terms

  • synonym: big cat - a large member of the Felidae family, such as a lion or tiger
  • antonym: dog - a domesticated carnivorous mammal commonly kept as a pet; often contrasted with 'cat'
  • antonym: domestic cat - a small domesticated feline animal, typically kept as a pet
  • synonym: feline - relating to or affecting cats or other members of the cat family
