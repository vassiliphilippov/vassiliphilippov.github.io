vote

verb
express choice in an election
Pronunciation: / voʊt / (AmE), / vəʊt / (BrE)
to express a choice or opinion in an election, meeting, or poll

Examples

To vote in elections
All citizens are encouraged to vote in the upcoming election.
She decided to vote for the independent candidate after much consideration.
You must register if you wish to vote in the referendum.
All members will vote on the proposal at tomorrow's meeting.
She voted in favor of building the new community center.
We are voting to decide where to go for dinner.
Every citizen is encouraged to vote in the general election.
She voted against the proposal after much consideration.
We will vote on the new club president next week.

  • synonym: elect - choose someone for a position by voting
