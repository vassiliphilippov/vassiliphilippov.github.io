Coscoroba

noun
genus of waterfowl containing the Coscoroba swan
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːskəˈroʊbə / (AmE), / ˌkɒskəˈrəʊbə / (BrE)
A genus of waterfowl in the family Anatidae, containing only the Coscoroba swan native to southern South America.

Examples

The Coscoroba swan
Among South American waterfowl, the Coscoroba stands out for its swan-like appearance.
