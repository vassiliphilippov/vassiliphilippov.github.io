fishing permit

noun
official license to fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃɪŋ pɚˌmɪt / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃɪŋ pəˌmɪt / (BrE)
an official document granting permission to fish in a particular area for a specified period

Examples

A fishing permit card
He bought a seasonal fishing permit to fish in national parks.
Without a fishing permit, you can be fined for fishing in protected waters.
You must carry your fishing permit at all times when fishing in this lake.
