lederhosen

noun
traditional Bavarian leather shorts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdərˌhoʊzən / (AmE), / ˈleɪdəˌhəʊzən / (BrE)
Traditional knee-length leather shorts with suspenders, especially worn by men and boys in Bavaria and other Alpine regions.

Examples

Traditional lederhosen outfit
During Oktoberfest, many men dress up in lederhosen as part of the celebration.
He bought a pair of lederhosen on his trip to Munich.
Traditional dance performances often feature dancers in colorful lederhosen.
