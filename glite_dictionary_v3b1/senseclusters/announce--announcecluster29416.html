announce

verb
to foreshadow or presage
1.0
Pronunciation: / əˈnaʊns / (AmE), / əˈnaʊns / (BrE)
to give a warning or indication of something that will happen; to foreshadow or presage

Examples

Clouds announce rain
Distant thunder announced the arrival of the cold front.
The first drops of rain announced the impending storm.
The scent of fresh bread announced that breakfast was nearly ready.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conceal - to prevent something from being seen or known; opposite of foreshadow
  • synonym: herald - to be a sign that something is about to happen
  • synonym: predict - to say that something will happen before it happens
