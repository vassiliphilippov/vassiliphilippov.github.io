thunderstick

noun
noisemaker at events
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈθʌn.dər.stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈθʌn.də.stɪk/ (BrE)
a long inflatable plastic stick that spectators bang together to make noise at sporting events or concerts

Examples

Bang thundersticks at games
Fans waved and banged their thundersticks to support the home team during the final match.
The sound of thundersticks filled the stadium as the concert began.
Each child received a colorful thunderstick to cheer for their favorite player.
