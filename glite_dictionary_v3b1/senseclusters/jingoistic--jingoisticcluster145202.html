jingoistic

adjective
fanatically patriotic
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɪŋɡoʊˈɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌdʒɪŋɡəʊˈɪstɪk / (BrE)
Displaying extreme or fanatical patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy.

Examples

A jingoistic attitude
Her jingoistic attitude made it difficult for her to appreciate other cultures.
Some media outlets have been criticized for their jingoistic coverage of the conflict.
The politician's jingoistic rhetoric alarmed many international observers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chauvinistic - displaying excessive or prejudiced loyalty or support for one's own cause, group, or gender
  • degree lower: patriotic - having or expressing devotion to and vigorous support for one's country
