nonviscid

adjective
not sticky or glue-like in texture
8.0 9.9
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈvɪsɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈvɪsɪd / (BrE)
(of a substance) not sticky or glue-like in texture; lacking viscosity.

Examples

Nonviscid oil
Oil is typically nonviscid compared to honey or syrup.
The solution remained nonviscid even after hours of thickening attempts.
To prevent clogging, a nonviscid substance is often preferred in laboratory apparatus.
