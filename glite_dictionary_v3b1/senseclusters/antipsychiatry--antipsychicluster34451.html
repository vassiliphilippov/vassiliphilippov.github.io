antipsychiatry

adjective
opposed to psychiatry
9.1
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.triː/ /; / /æn.ə.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.triː/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.triː/ /; / /æn.ə.saɪ.ˈkaɪ.ə.triː/ (BrE)
