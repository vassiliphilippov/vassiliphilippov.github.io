antipsychiatry

adjective
opposed to psychiatry
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪsaɪˈkaɪətri / (AmE), / ˌæntɪsaɪˈkaɪətri / (BrE)
critical of or opposed to the principles and practices of psychiatry, especially as promoted by the antipsychiatry movement

Examples

Antipsychiatry views
The antipsychiatry movement gained attention in the 1960s for challenging traditional mental health treatments.
Her antipsychiatry views led her to question the effectiveness of psychiatric medication.
Some authors have published antipsychiatry critiques of the mental health care system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: critical - expressing disapproval or finding fault
  • antonym: psychiatric - relating to psychiatry or psychiatrists
