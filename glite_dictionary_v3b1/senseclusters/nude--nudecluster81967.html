nude

adjective
skin‑tone beige colour
Pronunciation: / nud / (AmE), / njud0d / (BrE)
Of a pale brown or beige colour intended to resemble human skin; used especially for clothing, footwear, makeup, and interior finishes to provide a skin‑tone or neutral appearance.

Examples

Nude lipstick
She chose a nude lipstick that matched her warm skin tone for the wedding.
The bride wore a simple nude dress to keep the look understated and elegant.
Interior designers often use nude tones to create a calm, neutral background for art.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beige - a light brownish-yellow colour often used as a neutral background tone
  • synonym: skin-coloured - having a colour similar to human skin; often used interchangeably with 'nude' in fashion and cosmetics
