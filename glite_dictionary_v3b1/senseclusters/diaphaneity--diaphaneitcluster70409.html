diaphaneity

noun
delicate clarity
9.9
Pronunciation: / daɪˌæfəˈniːɪti / (AmE), / daɪˌæfəˈniːɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being light, delicate, or translucent, often used to describe appearance, style, or clarity
