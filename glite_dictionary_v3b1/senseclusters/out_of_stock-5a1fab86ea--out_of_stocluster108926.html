out of stock

adjective
unavailable for sale; sold out
1.0
Pronunciation: / aʊt əv stɑk / (AmE), / aʊt əv stɒk / (BrE)
Not available for sale or use because all have been sold or used up.

Examples

Out of stock items
Several sizes are out of stock due to high demand.
The toy she wanted was out of stock at every store in town.
We're sorry, but this item is currently out of stock online.

Related Terms

  • antonym: in stock - available for sale or use
  • synonym: sold out - all items have been sold; no longer available
