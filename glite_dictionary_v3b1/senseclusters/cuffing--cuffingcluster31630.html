cuffing

noun
handcuffing action
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌfɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of putting handcuffs on someone, especially as part of an arrest or restraint by police

Examples

Police cuffing a suspect
The officer's quick cuffing of the suspect prevented him from reaching the weapon.
Proper cuffing techniques are taught to all new police recruits.
The video showed the cuffing of the protester during the demonstration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: arrest - the act of detaining someone by legal authority
  • antonym: release - the act of setting someone free from restraint
