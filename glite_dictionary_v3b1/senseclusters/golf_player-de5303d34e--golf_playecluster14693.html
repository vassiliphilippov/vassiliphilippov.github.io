golf player

noun
person who plays golf
Pronunciation: / ɡɑlf ˈpleɪər / (AmE), / ɡɒlf ˈpleɪə / (BrE)
a person who plays the sport of golf, either professionally or as an amateur

Examples

A golf player swings
He became a golf player after retiring from professional football.
She is an accomplished golf player who has won several local tournaments.
The club offers lessons for any aspiring golf player regardless of experience.
