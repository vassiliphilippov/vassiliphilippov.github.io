Synonyms

  • storyteller
    2.3 5.01/m
    storyteller performs and entertains with prepared stories for an audience, while teller simply recounts events or stories to others.
  • narrator
    3.5 12/m
    narrator speaks the voice inside a book or film, while teller is any person who tells stories or recounts events aloud.
  • raconteur
    8.6 0.26/m
    raconteur charms listeners with witty, short anecdotes, while teller may tell stories without that skill or playful style.
  • conteur
    9.9 0.03/m
    conteur tells stories in a stylish, often literary way, while teller refers generally to anyone who recounts events or tales.
