upbeat

noun
a period of optimism or well-being
Pronunciation: / ˈʌpˌbit / (AmE), / ˈʌpˌbiːt / (BrE)
a period or state of happiness, optimism, or prosperity; a positive and buoyant condition

Examples

On the upbeat
After years of struggle, the company found itself on the upbeat once again.
Economists are reporting an upbeat in consumer confidence this quarter.
The community is experiencing an upbeat thanks to the new job opportunities.
