Notoryctus

noun
genus of Australian marsupial moles
9.9
Pronunciation: / noʊ.təˈrɪk.təs / (AmE), / nəʊ.təˈrɪk.təs / (BrE)
A genus of marsupials known as marsupial moles, native to Australia, and the only genus in the family Notoryctidae.

The family Notoryctidae consists of only one genus, Notoryctus.
