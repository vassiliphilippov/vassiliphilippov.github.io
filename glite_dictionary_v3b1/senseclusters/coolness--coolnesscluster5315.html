coolness

noun
calm self-assurance under stress
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːlnəs / (AmE), / ˈkuːlnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being calm and composed, especially under stress or in difficult situations

Examples

Coolness under pressure
Despite the pressure, she maintained her coolness throughout the interview.
His coolness under fire earned him the respect of his peers.
The coach admired her coolness when the team was losing.
Even when faced with accusation, his coolness never faltered.
Maintaining coolness under pressure is essential for emergency responders.
She showed remarkable coolness during the crisis and reassured everyone around her.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agitation - a state of nervousness or unrest
  • synonym: calmness - the quality of being free from agitation
  • synonym: composure - calmness and self-control, especially under pressure
  • antonym: panic - sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety
