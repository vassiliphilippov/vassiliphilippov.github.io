gothicism

noun
admiration for Goths
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːθɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈɡɒθɪˌsɪzəm / (BrE)
an interest in or admiration for the historical Goths, their culture, or their influence on art and history
