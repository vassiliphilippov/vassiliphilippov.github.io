forlornness

noun
state of deep sadness or abandonment
8.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / f…ôrÀàl…îrn.n…ôs / (AmE), / f…ôÀàl…îÀên.n…ôs / (BrE)
A feeling of deep sadness, loneliness, or abandonment, especially when one has been left alone or neglected.

Examples

Deep forlornness feeling
He wrote poems filled with forlornness, reflecting on lost love and solitude.
The forlornness in her eyes was impossible to ignore after everyone had left.
The abandoned house seemed to radiate a sense of forlornness that made visitors uneasy.

Synonyms

  • desolation
    7.6 0.47/m
    desolation is an intense, empty sadness often pictured as a barren place, while forlornness is personal loneliness from neglect.
  • loneliness
    2.3 8.86/m
    loneliness is feeling sad and alone in everyday life, while forlornness implies deeper abandonment and long-lasting despair.
  • forsakenness
    9.3 0.00/m
    forsakenness is the state of being left completely alone and abandoned, while forlornness focuses on the deep sad feeling that follows.

  • antonym: hopefulness - the state of being full of hope or optimism
  • synonym: melancholy - a deep, persistent sadness or gloom
