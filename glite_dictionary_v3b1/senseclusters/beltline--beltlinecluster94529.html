beltline

noun
waist area
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛltˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɛltˌlaɪn / (BrE)
the line or area around the waist of a garment or the part of an object corresponding to the waist

Examples

Dress beltline
The beltline of the dress sits just above the hips.
He adjusted the beltline of his trousers to make them more comfortable.
The car's beltline gives it a sleek and modern appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: waistline - the part of a garment or body at the waist
