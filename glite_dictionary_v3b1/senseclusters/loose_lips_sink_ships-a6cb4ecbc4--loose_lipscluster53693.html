loose lips sink ships

Pronunciation: / luːs lɪps sɪŋk ʃɪps / (AmE), / luːs lɪps sɪŋk ʃɪps / (BrE)
used to warn that careless talk can cause trouble or danger, especially by revealing sensitive information

Examples

Loose lips sink ships warning
