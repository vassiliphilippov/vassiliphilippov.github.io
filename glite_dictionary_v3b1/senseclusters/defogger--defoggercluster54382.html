defogger

noun
anti-fog device or spray
3.6
Pronunciation: / diˈfɑɡər / (AmE), / diˈfɒɡə / (BrE)
a device used to remove or prevent fog, condensation, or frost from glass surfaces, especially in cars or on mirrors

Examples

Car window defogger
The car's rear window defogger cleared the glass in just a few minutes.
Before swimming, she sprayed a defogger on her goggles to keep them clear.
He used a defogger to remove the condensation from his bathroom mirror after a hot shower.
She turned on the defogger to clear the windshield before driving.
The car's rear window defogger worked quickly on the cold morning.
If your defogger is broken, it can be difficult to see out of the windows in winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demister - a device that removes mist or condensation from glass surfaces
  • antonym: fogger - a device that produces fog or mist
