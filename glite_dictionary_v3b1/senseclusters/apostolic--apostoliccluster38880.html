apostolic

adjective
relating to the Apostles or their teachings
Pronunciation: / ˌæpəˈstɑlɪk / (AmE), / ˌapəˈstɒlɪk / (BrE)
Relating to the Apostles or their teachings, especially those of the early Christian church.

Examples

The apostolic teachings played a central role in shaping early Christian doctrine.
The church claims to preserve an apostolic tradition dating back to the first disciples.
