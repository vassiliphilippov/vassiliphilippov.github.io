Pictish

noun
ancient Scottish people
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈpɪtɪʃ / (BrE)
a member of the Picts, an ancient people who lived in what is now northern and eastern Scotland

Examples

A Pictish warrior
Archaeologists discovered a burial site belonging to the Pictish in northern Scotland.
Legends about the Pictish have fascinated historians for centuries.
Artifacts from the Pictish reveal their advanced skills in metalwork and stone carving.
