nonutility

adjective
not useful
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnjuːˈtɪləti / (AmE), / ˌnɒnjuːˈtɪləti / (BrE)
not useful or not serving any practical purpose

Examples

Nonutility objects
The museum displayed several nonutility objects created solely for artistic expression.
He argued that the design was beautiful but entirely nonutility in everyday life.
Some inventions are criticized for their nonutility despite their creative appearance.
