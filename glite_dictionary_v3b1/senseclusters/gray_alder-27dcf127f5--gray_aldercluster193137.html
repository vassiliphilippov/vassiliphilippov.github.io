gray alder

noun
species of alder tree (Alnus incana)
8.5
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪ ˈɔldɚ /; / ɡreɪ ˈældɚ / (AmE), / ɡreɪ ˈɔːldə /; / ɡreɪ ˈældə / (BrE)
a species of alder tree (Alnus incana) native to Europe and Asia; a deciduous tree with gray/grey smooth bark and leaves, typically found in moist soils, sometimes introduced to North America.
