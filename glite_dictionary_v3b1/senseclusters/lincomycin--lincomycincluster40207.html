lincomycin

noun
antibiotic from Streptomyces bacteria
8.1
Pronunciation: / lɪŋkəˈmaɪsɪn / (AmE), / lɪŋkəˈmaɪsɪn / (BrE)
an antibiotic obtained from certain Streptomyces bacteria, used especially to treat infections caused by penicillin-resistant bacteria

Examples

Lincomycin antibiotic treatment
Some patients allergic to penicillin can be effectively treated with lincomycin.
The doctor prescribed lincomycin to treat her resistant bacterial infection.
The effectiveness of lincomycin against certain Gram-positive bacteria has made it valuable in clinical settings.
