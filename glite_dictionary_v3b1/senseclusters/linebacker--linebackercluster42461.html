linebacker

noun
defensive football player behind the linemen
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪnˌbækər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪnˌbækə /; / ˈlaɪnbækə / (BrE)
a defensive player in American or Canadian football who lines up just behind the line of scrimmage and is responsible for stopping running plays or defending against passes
