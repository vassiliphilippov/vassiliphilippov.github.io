buyback

noun
the repurchase of something previously sold
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪbæk / (BrE)
the act of purchasing something that one previously sold, especially by a company buying back its own stock or assets

Examples

A buyback agreement
After selling the land, the developer negotiated a buyback agreement with the city.
Several firms are engaging in buybacks to utilize excess cash reserves.
The company announced a buyback of its shares to increase shareholder value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: repurchase - the act of buying something again
