Synonyms

  • overcome
    2.9 30/m
    overcome means successfully dealing with a problem or feeling, while get over is the common phrasal verb for recovery.
  • get past
    3.0 7/m
    get past means moving beyond an obstacle or bad feeling, while get over focuses on recovering or finishing the upsetting experience.
  • get through
    2.8 8/m
    get through means managing or surviving a difficult time, while get over emphasizes fully recovering after it ends.
  • bounce back
    3.5 3/m
    bounce back means recovering quickly after illness or trouble, while get over simply means recovering without implying speed.
  • surmount
    6.3 1.0/m
    surmount means successfully climbing over a difficulty in a formal sense, while get over is more everyday and casual.
  • rebound
    4.3 4/m
    rebound means returning to a better state after decline, while get over means recovering from illness or feeling in general.
