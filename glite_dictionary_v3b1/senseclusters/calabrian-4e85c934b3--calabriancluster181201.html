Calabrian

noun
Pleistocene geological stage
Pronunciation: / kəˈleɪbriən / (AmE), / kəˈleɪbriən / (BrE)
a geological stage of the early Pleistocene epoch, dating from about 1.8 to 0.78 million years ago

Examples

Calabrian period fossils
Fossil records from the Calabrian indicate significant climatic changes early in the Pleistocene.
The Calabrian is known for the spread of early human ancestors across Eurasia.
Geologists study sediment layers from the Calabrian to understand ancient environmental shifts.
