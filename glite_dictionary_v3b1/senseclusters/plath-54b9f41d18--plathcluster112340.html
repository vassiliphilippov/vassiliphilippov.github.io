Plath

proper noun
US writer and poet (1932–1963)
Pronunciation: / plæθ / (AmE), / plɑːθ / (BrE)
(1932–1963) United States writer and poet known for her confessional style and the novel 'The Bell Jar'.

Examples

Plath the poet
Contemporary poets frequently reference Plath as a major influence on their writing.
Many readers have been moved by Plath's honest portrayal of mental illness in her poetry.
