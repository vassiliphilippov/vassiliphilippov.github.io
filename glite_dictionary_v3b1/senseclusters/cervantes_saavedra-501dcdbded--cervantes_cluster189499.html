Cervantes Saavedra

proper noun
Spanish author of 'Don Quixote'
Pronunciation: / sərˈvæn.tiːz sɑːˈvɛd.rə / (AmE), / səˈvɑːn.teɪz sæˈveɪ.drə / (BrE)
(1547–1616) Spanish writer best known for 'Don Quixote,' which satirizes chivalry and had a major influence on the development of the novel.
