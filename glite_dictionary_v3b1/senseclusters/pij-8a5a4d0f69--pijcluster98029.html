PIJ

noun
militant Palestinian organization (acronym)
9.9
Pronunciation: /piː aɪ dʒeɪ/ (AmE), /piː aɪ dʒeɪ/ (BrE)
an acronym for Palestinian Islamic Jihad, a militant Palestinian organization founded in 1979 that seeks the creation of an Islamic state in Palestine and the destruction of Israel, and is smaller and more militant than Hamas.

Examples

PIJ militant group
Members of PIJ have claimed responsibility for several operations against Israeli targets.
The Israeli government blamed PIJ for the recent rocket attacks in the region.
Unlike Hamas, PIJ focuses solely on armed resistance and does not participate in Palestinian elections.
