brick wall

noun
insurmountable obstacle
Pronunciation: / brɪk wɔl / (AmE), / brɪk wɔːl / (BrE)
A barrier or obstacle that is impossible or extremely difficult to overcome.

Examples

A brick wall obstacle
After hours of brainstorming, they realized they had run into a brick wall.
Every attempt to fix the error felt like hitting a brick wall.
The new policy has become a brick wall for many small businesses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrier - something that blocks movement or progress
  • synonym: obstacle - a thing that blocks one's way or prevents progress
