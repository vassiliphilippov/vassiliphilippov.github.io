dazedly

adverb
in a stunned or confused manner
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪzɪdli / (AmE), / ˈdeɪzɪdli / (BrE)
In a way that shows confusion or lack of full awareness, as if stunned or bewildered.

Examples

To walk dazedly
He stumbled dazedly through the dark corridor after the sudden blow to his head.
She looked dazedly around the chaotic room, trying to make sense of the situation.
The student blinked dazedly at the difficult question, unsure how to proceed.

  • synonym: dazed - being in a state of confusion or stunned
