iconographer

noun
designer of icons
8.3
Pronunciation: /aɪ.kə.ˈnɑːg.rə.fər/ (AmE), /aɪ.kə.ˈnɒg.rə.fə/ (BrE)
a person who designs or creates pictorial symbols, especially icons for digital interfaces, applications, or signage

Examples

App iconographer
The company hired an experienced iconographer to update all the app's visual symbols.
As an iconographer, she specializes in creating simple, intuitive icons for user interfaces.
A skilled iconographer can make digital tools easier to use through clear visual design.
