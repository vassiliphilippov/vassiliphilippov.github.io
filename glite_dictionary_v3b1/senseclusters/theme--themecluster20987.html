theme

noun
essay written for a school assignment
Pronunciation: / θim / (AmE), / θiːm / (BrE)
an essay written by a student as a school assignment, often on a particular topic

Examples

A school theme
For homework, we had to write a theme about our summer vacation.
His theme on friendship was thoughtful and well-written.
The teacher collected all the students' themes at the end of class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: composition - an essay, especially as a school exercise
  • synonym: paper - a short written work by a student
