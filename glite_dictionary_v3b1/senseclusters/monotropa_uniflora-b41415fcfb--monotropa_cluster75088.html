Monotropa uniflora

noun
species of woodland plant (ghost plant/Indian pipe)
9.9
Pronunciation: /mə.noʊ.ˈtroʊ.pə juː.nɪ.ˈflɔːr.ə/ (AmE), /mɒ.nə.ˈtrəʊ.pə juː.nɪ.ˈflɔː.rə/ (BrE)
a small, waxy, white or pinkish-white woodland plant with scalelike leaves and a nodding flower that turns black with age, commonly known as the ghost plant or Indian pipe
