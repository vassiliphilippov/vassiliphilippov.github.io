one-off

noun
something that occurs only once
Pronunciation: / wʌn ˈɔf / (AmE), / wʌn ˈɒf / (BrE)
an event or thing that happens only once and is not repeated

Examples

A one-off event
Although it was meant to be a one-off, the success led to annual repeats.
The concert was a one-off, never to be repeated.
This charity event is a one-off, so don't miss your chance to participate.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recurring event - an event that happens repeatedly
  • synonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
