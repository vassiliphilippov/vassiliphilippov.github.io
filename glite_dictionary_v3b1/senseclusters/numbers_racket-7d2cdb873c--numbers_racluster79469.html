numbers racket

noun
illegal lottery operation
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌmbɚz ˌrækɪt / (AmE), / ˈnʌmbəz ˌrakɪt / (BrE)
an illegal lottery or betting operation in which people bet on numbers, usually operated outside government regulation.

Examples

The numbers racket operation
During the 1930s, many organized crime groups controlled the city's numbers racket.
Police arrested several suspects in connection with the illegal numbers racket operating in the neighborhood.
Rumors spread that the store owner was running a numbers racket out of the back room.
