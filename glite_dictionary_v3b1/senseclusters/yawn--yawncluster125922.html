yawn

verb
be wide open or gape (of a space)
Pronunciation: / jɑn / (AmE), / jɔːn / (BrE)
(of a space or opening) to be wide open or to gape widely.

Examples

A gap yawns
A chasm seemed to yawn before them, blocking their path.
The doors yawned open to reveal the grand hall inside.
Vast empty windows yawned in the ruined building, filled with darkness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gape - to open wide, especially of the mouth or an opening
