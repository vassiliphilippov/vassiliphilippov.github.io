unworking

adjective
ineffective; not working
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈwɜrkɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈwɜːkɪŋ / (BrE)
not producing the intended effect or result; ineffective.

Examples

Unworking solution
The new software update was completely unworking and failed to fix the problem.
If your password is unworking, try resetting it through the help page.
Their plan for reducing costs proved unworking after expenses continued to rise.

Related Terms

  • antonym: effective - producing the intended result
  • synonym: ineffective - not producing the intended result
