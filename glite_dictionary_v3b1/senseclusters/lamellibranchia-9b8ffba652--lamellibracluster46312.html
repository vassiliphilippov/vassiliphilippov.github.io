Lamellibranchia

noun
class of bivalve mollusks
9.9
Pronunciation: / ləˌmɛlɪˈbræŋkiə / (AmE), / ləˌmɛlɪˈbraŋkɪə /; / læˌmɛlɪˈbraŋkɪə / (BrE)
a former taxonomic class of mollusks (such as oysters, clams, scallops, and mussels) with two-part shells and gill structures in the form of lamellae
