Synonyms

  • ring out
    3.6 2.00/m
    ring out means to sound loudly and clearly for a long moment, while ring means to make a ringing sound or echo loudly.
  • resound
    5.3 1.21/m
    resound means to fill a place with loud sound that spreads around, while ring means to make a clear, sharp sound or echo.
  • reverberate
    6.6 1.48/m
    reverberate means sound that echoes and bounces many times in a space, while ring means a single loud or echoing tone.
  • echo
    1.4 3.60/m
    echo means a sound repeated after it bounces off a surface, while ring means to make a ringing sound that may not repeat.
