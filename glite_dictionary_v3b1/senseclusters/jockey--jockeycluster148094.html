jockey

noun
operator of a vehicle or machine
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːki / (AmE), / ˈdʒɒki / (BrE)
A person who operates a particular type of vehicle, machine, or apparatus, especially as part of their job or as an enthusiast.

Examples

A truck jockey
After years as a truck jockey, he knew every route through the city.
She became a forklift jockey at the warehouse to earn extra money.
The company is looking for a skilled computer jockey to manage their systems.
