Ch'in Shih Huang Ti

proper noun
first Emperor of unified China, 3rd century BC
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʧɪn ʃɪ hwɑŋ ti / (AmE), / ʧɪn ʃɪ hwæŋ ti / (BrE)
the first Emperor of the Qin dynasty who unified China, commissioned much of the Great Wall, and established standardized systems of currency, weights, and laws; died 210 BC.
