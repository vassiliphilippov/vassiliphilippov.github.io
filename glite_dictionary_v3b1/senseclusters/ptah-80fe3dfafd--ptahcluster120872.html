Ptah

proper noun
a creator god in ancient Egyptian mythology
Pronunciation: / ptɑː / (AmE), / ptɑː / (BrE)
an important ancient Egyptian god, regarded as the creator of the world and the patron deity of craftsmen, especially worshipped in Memphis

Examples

Ptah god of craftsmen
In ancient Egypt, Ptah was worshipped as the god of creation and craftsmanship.
Many temples dedicated to Ptah were located in the city of Memphis.
Mythology describes Ptah as the father of gods and men.
