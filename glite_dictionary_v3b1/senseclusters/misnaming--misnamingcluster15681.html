misnaming

noun
wrong naming
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪsˈneɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪsˈneɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of using the wrong or inappropriate name for someone or something

Examples

The misnaming of product
The misnaming of the new product led to confusion among customers.
His constant misnaming of colleagues' names was embarrassing during meetings.
A simple misnaming in the report caused a major misunderstanding.
The misnaming of the city in the article led to confusion among readers.
Historical misnaming of plants has caused problems in scientific classification.
She apologized for the misnaming of her colleague during the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mislabeling - the act of giving something an incorrect label
  • antonym: naming - the act of giving a name to someone or something
