hundred-and-twentieth

adjective
ordinal number: 120th
Pronunciation: / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈtwɛntiəθ / (AmE), / ˌhʌndrəd ən ˈtwɛntɪəθ / (BrE)
the ordinal number matching the count of 120th in a sequence; coming after the one hundred and nineteenth and before the one hundred and twenty-first.

Examples

The hundred-and-twentieth page
On page hundred-and-twentieth you’ll find the answer to your question.
She finished in hundred-and-twentieth place in the marathon, far behind the leaders.
This is the hundred-and-twentieth anniversary of the school’s founding.
