multiplicand

noun
number to be multiplied
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkænd / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkænd / (BrE)
a number that is to be multiplied by another number (the multiplier)

Examples

The multiplicand number
If you change the multiplicand, the answer will be different.
In 7 × 3 = 21, 7 is the multiplicand and 3 is the multiplier.
To solve the problem, multiply the multiplicand by the multiplier.
