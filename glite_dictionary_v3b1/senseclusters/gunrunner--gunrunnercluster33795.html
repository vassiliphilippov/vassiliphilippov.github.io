gunrunner

noun
person who smuggles guns
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡʌnˌrʌnər / (AmE), / ˈɡʌnˌrʌnə / (BrE)
a person who smuggles guns illegally from one place to another for sale or distribution

Examples

A gunrunner smuggles weapons
He was suspected of being a gunrunner, but there was not enough evidence to convict him.
Rumors spread that a gunrunner was supplying weapons to both sides of the conflict.
The authorities arrested the notorious gunrunner at the border after a long investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smuggler - a person who illegally transports goods
