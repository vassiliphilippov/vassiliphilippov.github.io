rub away

verb
remove by rubbing
Pronunciation: / rʌb əˈweɪ / (AmE), / rʌb əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to remove or eliminate something by rubbing it repeatedly

Examples

To rub away mud
He used a cloth to rub away the mud from his shoes.
Over time, repeated cleaning will rub away the paint on the surface.
She tried to rub away the pencil marks from the paper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: erase - to remove written or drawn marks
  • synonym: wipe off - to clean or remove by wiping
