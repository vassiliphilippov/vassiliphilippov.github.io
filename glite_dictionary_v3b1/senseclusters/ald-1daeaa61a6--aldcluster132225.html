ALD

noun
rare genetic disorder
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ ˌɛl ˈdi / (AmE), / ˌeɪ ˌɛl ˈdiː / (BrE)
a rare genetic disorder, adrenoleukodystrophy, that damages the nervous system and adrenal glands

Examples

Early diagnosis of ALD is important for managing symptoms and planning treatment.
