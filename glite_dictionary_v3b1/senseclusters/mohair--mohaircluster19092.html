mohair

noun
silky fabric from Angora goat's hair
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊˌhɛr / (AmE), / ˈməʊˌheə / (BrE)
A fabric or yarn made from the silky hair of the Angora goat, known for its soft texture and lustrous sheen.

Examples

Soft mohair fabric
He inherited a vintage sofa upholstered in deep green mohair.
Her winter coat was made of fine mohair that felt wonderfully soft to the touch.
The designer favored mohair for its elegant sheen and durability in suits.
