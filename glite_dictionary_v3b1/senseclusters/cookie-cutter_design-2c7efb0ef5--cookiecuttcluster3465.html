cookie-cutter design

noun
stereotyped, uniform design
7.2 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtɚ dɪˈzaɪn / (AmE), / ˈkʊki ˌkʌtə dɪˈzaɪn / (BrE)
a design that is uniform, stereotyped, and lacking individuality or originality

Examples

A cookie-cutter design
Many suburban developments are criticized for being filled with cookie-cutter designs.
She wanted her restaurant to stand out, not to have a cookie-cutter design like all the chains.
The new office building received criticism for its cookie-cutter design and lack of creativity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: standardized - conforming to a standard; uniform
  • antonym: unique - being the only one of its kind; unlike anything else
