certificate

noun
formal finance document giving rights to income
1.0
Pronunciation: / sərˈtɪf.ɪ.kət / (AmE), / səˈtɪf.ɪ.kət / (BrE)
a formal document, especially in finance, that gives the holder a right to receive interest or dividends

Examples

Investment certificate
He invested in a certificate that guaranteed regular interest payments.
Many people keep their stock certificate in a safety deposit box.
The certificate entitled her to annual dividends from the company.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bond - a certificate issued by a government or corporation promising to repay borrowed money with interest
  • synonym: security - a financial asset such as a stock or bond
