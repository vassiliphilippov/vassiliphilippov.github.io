rhumba

verb
to dance the rhumba
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌmbə / (AmE), / ˈrʌmbə / (BrE)
to perform the rhumba, a ballroom dance of Cuban origin characterized by a slow rhythm and sensuous movements.

Examples

To rhumba gracefully
Last summer, I learned how to rhumba during a dance class in Havana.
The couple stunned the judges when they began to rhumba to the slow, rhythmic music.
They rhumba expertly across the polished dance floor every Saturday night.
