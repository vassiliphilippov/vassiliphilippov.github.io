courageousness

noun
quality of having courage
Pronunciation: / kəˈreɪ.dʒəs.nəs / (AmE), / kəˈreɪ.dʒəs.nəs / (BrE)
The quality of having courage; the ability to face danger, pain, or difficulty without being overcome by fear.

Examples

Great courageousness
He displayed incredible courageousness in standing up for what was right.
Her courageousness during the rescue inspired everyone at the scene.
The courageousness of the firefighters saved many lives that night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: courage - the ability to do something that frightens one
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of courage to face danger or pain
