courageousness

noun
quality of having courage
5.4 4.0
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈreɪ.dʒəs.nəs/ (AmE), /kə.ˈreɪ.dʒəs.nəs/ (BrE)
The quality of having courage; the ability to face danger, pain, or difficulty without being overcome by fear.

Examples

The courageousness of the firefighters saved many lives that night.
