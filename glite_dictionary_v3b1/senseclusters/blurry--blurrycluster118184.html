blurry

adjective
indistinct; not clear in outline
Pronunciation: / ˈblɝː.i / (AmE), / ˈblʌr.i / (BrE)
not clearly defined; indistinct or hazy in outline or appearance.

Examples

A blurry picture
After crying, her makeup looked blurry in the mirror.
His vision became blurry after he took off his glasses.
The photograph was too blurry to make out any details.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - easy to perceive, understand, or see; not blurry
  • synonym: fuzzy - having a frizzy texture or appearance; indistinct or blurred
  • antonym: sharp - having clear and distinct outlines or details
