msasa

noun
species of African tree
7.8
Pronunciation: / məˈsɑːsə / (AmE), / məˈsɑːsə / (BrE)
A small shrubby African tree (Brachystegia spiciformis) with compound leaves and racemes of small, fragrant green flowers.
