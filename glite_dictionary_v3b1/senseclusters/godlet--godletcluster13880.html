godlet

noun
minor idolized person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑdlət / (AmE), / ˈɡɒdlət / (BrE)
a person who is treated as if they are a minor god, often admired or given excessive importance

Examples

Internet godlet
After his viral video, the young musician became a godlet among his fans.
Some critics argue that social media can turn anyone into a temporary godlet.
The actor's arrogant behavior made him seem like a self-appointed godlet on set.

Related Terms

  • synonym: celebrity - a famous person who receives a lot of public attention
  • synonym: idol - a person who is greatly admired or revered
  • antonym: nobody - a person of no importance or fame
