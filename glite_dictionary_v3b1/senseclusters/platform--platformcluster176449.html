platform

noun
political party's official policy statement
Pronunciation: / ˈplæt.fɔːrm / (AmE), / ˈplætfɔːm / (BrE)
a formal statement of the aims and principles of a political party, particularly as presented to the public before an election

Examples

Party platform
She disagreed with several points in the official platform released by her party.
The party updated its platform to address climate change and social justice issues.
Voters often compare each candidate's platform before making a decision.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manifesto - a public declaration of policy and aims, especially before an election
