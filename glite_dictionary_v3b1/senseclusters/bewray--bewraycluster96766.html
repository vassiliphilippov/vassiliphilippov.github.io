bewray

verb
reveal or disclose (archaic)
8.4 9.9
Pronunciation: /bɪ.ˈreɪ/ /; / /bə.ˈreɪ/ (AmE), /bɪ.ˈreɪ/ /; / /bə.ˈreɪ/ (BrE)
(archaic) To reveal or disclose (something), often unintentionally.

Examples

To bewray a secret
He dared not bewray the secret entrusted to him by his king.
Her trembling voice did bewray her anxiety to the gathered council.
The letter, found in haste, would bewray their conspiracy if discovered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betray - to reveal unintentionally or treacherously
  • antonym: conceal - to keep something secret or hidden
