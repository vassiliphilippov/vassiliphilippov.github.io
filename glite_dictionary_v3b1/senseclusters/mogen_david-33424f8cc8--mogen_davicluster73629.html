Mogen David

noun
six-pointed star; symbol of Judaism

Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.ɡən ˈdeɪ.vɪd/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.ɡən ˈdeɪ.vɪd/ (BrE)
