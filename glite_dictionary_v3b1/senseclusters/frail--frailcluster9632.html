frail

noun
physically weak person
Pronunciation: / freɪl / (AmE), / freɪl / (BrE)
a person, especially an elderly one, who is physically weak or delicate

Examples

Support for frails
The care home provides support for the frails in the community.
Volunteers visited several frails to help with daily tasks and companionship.
After her fall, she joined a group for frails to stay active and social.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invalid - a person made weak or disabled by illness or injury
  • antonym: robust - strong and healthy; vigorous
