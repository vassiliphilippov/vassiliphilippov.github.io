plowwoman

noun
woman who plows fields
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplaʊˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈplaʊˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman who ploughs or plows land or fields, especially with a plough and draft animals or machinery, often as an occupation.
