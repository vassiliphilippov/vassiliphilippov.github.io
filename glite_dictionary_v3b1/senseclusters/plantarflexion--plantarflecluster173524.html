plantarflexion

noun
downward foot movement
Pronunciation: / ˌplæntɑrˈflɛkʃən / (AmE), / ˌplæntɑːˈflɛkʃən / (BrE)
the movement or position of pointing the foot downward, so the toes move away from the leg
