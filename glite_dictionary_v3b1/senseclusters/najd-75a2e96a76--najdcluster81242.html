Najd

proper noun
central region of the Arabian Peninsula
Pronunciation: / nɑːdʒd / (AmE), / nɑːdʒd / (BrE)
a central plateau region in the Arabian Peninsula, formerly an independent sultanate, now part of Saudi Arabia
