usurious

adjective
excessively high (interest rates/charges)
9.1
Pronunciation: / juˈsjʊriəs / (AmE), / juˈsjʊəriəs / (BrE)
(of interest rates or charges) greatly exceeding reasonable or legal limits, especially to an unfair or illegal degree.

Examples

Usurious interest rate
He borrowed money from a loan shark at a usurious rate.
Many payday lenders have come under scrutiny for their usurious practices.
The bank was accused of charging usurious interest rates on short-term loans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exorbitant - (of a price or amount) much too high; excessive
  • synonym: extortionate - (of a price or demand) much too high; exorbitant
