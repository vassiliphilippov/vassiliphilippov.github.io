Synonyms

  • replete
    6.6 0.8/m
    replete is formal and often used in writing to mean richly supplied, while full of is everyday and more general.
  • packed with
    2.5 3/m
    packed with often means tightly filled, especially with people or things, while full of simply says something contains much.
  • chock-full
    4.1 1/m
