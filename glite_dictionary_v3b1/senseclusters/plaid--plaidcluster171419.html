plaid

noun
cloth with crisscrossed bands; tartan pattern
Pronunciation: / plæd / (AmE), / plad / (BrE)
A cloth pattern featuring crisscrossed horizontal and vertical bands in multiple colors, often used in clothing or textiles.

Examples

A plaid cloth
He covered the table with a bright plaid cloth for the picnic.
She wore a red and black plaid scarf to keep warm.
The blanket had a traditional Scottish plaid design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tartan - a woolen cloth woven in one of several patterns of colored checks and intersecting lines
