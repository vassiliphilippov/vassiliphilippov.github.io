revenge

verb
punish or hurt in return for a wrong
4.4
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɛndʒ / (AmE), / rɪˈvɛndʒ / (BrE)
to punish or hurt someone in return for an injury or wrong suffered

Examples

To revenge a friend
After being cheated, he vowed to revenge himself on those responsible.
She could not resist the urge to revenge the insult inflicted on her family.
They plotted to revenge the defeat they suffered last year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: avenge - to inflict punishment in return for a wrong, especially to uphold justice
  • antonym: forgive - to stop feeling angry or resentful toward someone for an offense or mistake
