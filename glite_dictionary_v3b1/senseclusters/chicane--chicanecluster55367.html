chicane

noun
bridge hand with no trumps
Pronunciation: / ʃɪˈkeɪn / (AmE), / ʃɪˈkæn / (BrE)
In bridge, a hand that contains no cards of the trump suit.

Examples

Bridge chicane
Holding a chicane can be both an advantage and a disadvantage in bridge.
If you declare a chicane, the scoring can change significantly.
She was dealt a chicane, making her strategy much more challenging.
