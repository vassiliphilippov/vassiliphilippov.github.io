Synonyms

    reverse is the exact, often physical or situational opposite, while opposite is a general word for something directly different.
  • contrary
    3.5 10/m
    contrary often refers to something different from what is stated or expected, while opposite names a direct difference.
  • antonym
    3.2 0.1/m
    antonym is a single word that has the opposite meaning of another word, while opposite can mean any kind of difference.
  • antithesis
    7.9 0.8/m
    antithesis is a formal, often abstract direct opposite in ideas or characters, while opposite is a more general everyday term.
