endogenously

adverb
in an internal or self-generated way
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈdɑdʒənəsli / (AmE), / ɛnˈdɒdʒənəsli / (BrE)
In a way that originates from within an organism, system, or process, rather than from external factors.

Examples

To endogenously produce
Plants can regulate certain compounds endogenously in response to stress.
Some diseases are triggered endogenously due to genetic mutations.
The hormone was produced endogenously rather than introduced from outside the body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exogenously - in a way that originates from outside an organism or system
