democratizer

noun
one who spreads access
6.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːkrəˌtaɪzər / (AmE), / dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something more accessible or democratic to a wider group of people

Examples

Democratizer of information
The internet has become a powerful democratizer of knowledge around the world.
Education is often seen as a democratizer that gives everyone a fair chance.
Social media acts as a democratizer by allowing more voices to be heard.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oppressor - a person who keeps others in hardship or limits their rights
  • synonym: reformer - a person who works to improve or change something for the better
