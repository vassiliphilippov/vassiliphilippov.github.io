recycled metal

noun
metal reused from previous products
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsaɪkəld ˈmɛt̬l̩ / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪkəld ˈmɛtl̩ / (BrE)
metal that has been processed and reused after being recovered from previous products or waste materials.

Examples

Recycled metal parts
Many modern cars contain parts made with recycled metal to reduce environmental impact.
The artist created a striking statue entirely from recycled metal.
Using recycled metal in construction helps conserve natural resources and energy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scrap metal - metal discarded from old vehicles, machinery, or buildings and suitable for reprocessing
