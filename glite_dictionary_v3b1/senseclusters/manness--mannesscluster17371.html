manness

noun
male qualities
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.nəs / (AmE), / ˈmæn.nəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being a man, especially in terms of traditional male characteristics or identity

Examples

Traditional manness
He felt that his manness was questioned when he showed his emotions.
The novel explores the idea of manness in different cultures and societies.
Some people believe that manness is defined by strength and independence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: masculinity - qualities or attributes regarded as characteristic of men
