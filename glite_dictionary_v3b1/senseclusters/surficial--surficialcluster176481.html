surficial

adjective
on or near the earth's surface
5.7
Pronunciation: / sərˈfɪʃəl / (AmE), / səˈfɪʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or occurring on or near the surface of the earth, especially in geological contexts.

Examples

Surficial earth features
Erosion has altered the surficial features of the landscape.
Mapping surficial soils is essential for understanding groundwater movement.
The scientist studied the surficial deposits left by the glacier.
