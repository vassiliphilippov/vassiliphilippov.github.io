haaf

noun
Shetland deep-sea fishing
9.9
Pronunciation: / hɑːf / (AmE), / hɑːf / (BrE)
a traditional form of deep-sea fishing practiced in Shetland, Scotland, using open boats far from shore

Examples

Haaf fishing trip
The fishermen prepared their boat for a long day of haaf in the North Sea.
In the past, entire communities depended on haaf for their main source of income.
Stories of dangerous haaf journeys are still told in Shetland today.
