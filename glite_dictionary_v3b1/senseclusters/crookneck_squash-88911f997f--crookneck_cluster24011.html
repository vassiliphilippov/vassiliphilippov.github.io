crookneck squash

noun
yellow squash with a curved neck
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʊkˌnɛk skwɑʃ / (AmE), / ˈkrʊk.nɛk skwɒʃ / (BrE)
A type of yellow squash with a thin, curved neck and somewhat warty skin, commonly used as a vegetable.

Examples

A fresh crookneck squash
At the farmers' market, I bought a fresh crookneck squash for tonight's stir-fry.
She sliced the crookneck squash and added it to the vegetable casserole.
Young crookneck squash are especially tender and flavorful when lightly steamed.
