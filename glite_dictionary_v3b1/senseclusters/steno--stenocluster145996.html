steno

noun
shorthand for stenographer
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a stenographer or the practice of stenography, especially in an office or legal setting

Examples

Office steno
The steno typed up the minutes of the meeting before lunch.
She worked as a steno in a busy law office for several years.
During the trial, the steno captured every word spoken in the courtroom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secretary - a person employed to handle correspondence and clerical work
  • synonym: stenographer - a person who transcribes speech using shorthand
