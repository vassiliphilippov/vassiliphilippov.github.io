maim

verb
injure seriously and permanently
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: / meɪm / (AmE), / meɪm / (BrE)
to wound or injure someone seriously, often resulting in permanent damage or disfigurement

Examples

To maim a soldier
Many soldiers were maimed for life during the conflict.
The accident badly maimed his left arm, leaving him unable to work.
The explosion maimed several bystanders in the crowded marketplace.
