persecutory

adjective
relating to oppression
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrsɪkjəˌtɔri / (AmE), / pəˈsɛkjʊtəri / (BrE)
relating to or involving the harassment, oppression, or unfair treatment of people, especially because of their beliefs or identity

Examples

Persecutory laws
The new law was criticized for its persecutory measures against religious minorities.
She described her childhood as difficult due to her father's persecutory attitude.
Many refugees flee their countries to escape persecutory governments.

Related Terms

  • antonym: protective - intended to keep someone safe from harm
