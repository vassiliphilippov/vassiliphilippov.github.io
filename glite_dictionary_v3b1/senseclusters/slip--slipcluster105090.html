slip

noun
small sheet of paper (form or receipt)
Pronunciation: / slɪp / (AmE), / slɪp / (BrE)
a small sheet of paper, especially one used for a particular purpose such as a form or receipt

Examples

A claim slip
He lost the luggage claim slip at the airport.
Please attach the payment slip to your application form.
The teller handed me a slip to fill out before withdrawing cash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: form - an official document with spaces to fill in information
