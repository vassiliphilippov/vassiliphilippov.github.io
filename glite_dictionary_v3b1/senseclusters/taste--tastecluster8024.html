Synonyms

  • preference
    2.1 20/m
    preference is choosing one thing over others in everyday choices, while taste includes both that liking and a sense of style.
  • appreciation
    2.9 11/m
    appreciation is understanding and valuing quality in art or things, while taste is personal liking plus a sense of style.
  • discernment
    6.6 8/m
    discernment is noticing subtle quality and judging wisely in art or behavior, while taste mixes liking and style sense.
  • predilection
    8.4 1/m
    predilection is a strong liking often used in formal speech, while taste is a more general liking or sense of style.
  • penchant
    8.2 4/m
    penchant is a strong, habitual liking or tendency for something, while taste covers both casual likes and a sense of style.
