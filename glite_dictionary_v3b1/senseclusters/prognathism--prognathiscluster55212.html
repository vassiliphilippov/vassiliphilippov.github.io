prognathism

noun
the condition of a projecting jaw
9.9
Pronunciation: / proʊɡˈnæθɪzəm / (AmE), / prɒɡˈnæθɪzəm / (BrE)
The condition in which the jaws, especially the lower jaw, project forward more than is typical.

Examples

Lower jaw prognathism
Some prehistoric hominids exhibited marked prognathism in their skulls.
Surgical procedures can address severe prognathism when it causes functional or aesthetic issues.
The orthodontist diagnosed the child with prognathism, recommending early intervention to correct the jaw alignment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: retrognathism - the condition of having a receding jaw
