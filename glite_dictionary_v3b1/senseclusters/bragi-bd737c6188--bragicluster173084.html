Bragi

proper noun
Norse god of poetry and music
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɑːɡi / (AmE), / ˈbrɑːɡi / (BrE)
(in Norse mythology) the god of poetry and music, son of Odin.

Examples

Bragi god of poetry
In Norse mythology, Bragi is revered as the god of poetry and eloquence.
Stories say that Bragi greeted fallen warriors in Valhalla with beautiful songs.
The skalds would often invoke Bragi at the start of their verses.
