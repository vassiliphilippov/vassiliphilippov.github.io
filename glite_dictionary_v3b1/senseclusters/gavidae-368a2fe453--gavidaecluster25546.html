Gavidae

noun
the loon family of aquatic birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡævɪˌdiː / (AmE), / ˈɡævɪˌdiː / (BrE)
A family of aquatic birds known as loons, characterized by their diving ability and found in northern regions.
