rezone

verb
to change land zoning
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈzoʊn / (AmE), / ˌriːˈzəʊn / (BrE)
to officially change the zoning classification or designated use of a piece of land or property

Examples

After much debate, the town decided not to rezone the historic district.
