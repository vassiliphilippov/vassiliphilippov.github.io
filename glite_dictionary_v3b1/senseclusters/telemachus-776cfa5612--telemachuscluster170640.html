Telemachus

proper noun
Christian martyr monk
Pronunciation: / təˈlɛməkəs / (AmE), / təˈlɛməkəs / (BrE)
a Christian monk and martyr who, according to tradition, was killed for attempting to stop gladiatorial games in ancient Rome

Examples

Telemachus stops gladiators
The story of Telemachus is often cited as an example of courage and faith.
Many historians debate the details of Telemachus and his intervention in the Roman arena.
A church was later built to honor Telemachus and his sacrifice.
