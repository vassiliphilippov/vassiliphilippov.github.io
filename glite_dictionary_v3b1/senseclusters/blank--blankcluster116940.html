blank

adjective
not written or printed on
1.0
Pronunciation: / blæŋk / (AmE), / blæŋk / (BrE)
(of a surface) not written or printed on; having nothing on it.

Examples

Blank page
Please sign your name in the blank space provided at the bottom.
She opened her notebook to a blank page to take new notes.
The application form had several blank fields that needed to be completed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: empty - containing nothing; not filled or occupied
  • antonym: filled - containing as much as possible; not empty
