grounds

noun
land around a building
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡraʊndz / (AmE), / ɡraʊndz / (BrE)

Examples

School grounds

The estate's grounds were beautifully landscaped with trees and flower beds.
The school hired a gardener to maintain the grounds throughout the year.
