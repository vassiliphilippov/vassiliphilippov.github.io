ramen

noun
Japanese noodle soup
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːmən / (AmE), / ˈrɑːmən / (BrE)
a Japanese dish of wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often with toppings like meat and vegetables; can also refer to the instant packaged version

Examples

A bowl of ramen
He enjoyed a steaming bowl of ramen after a long day at work.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy miso ramen topped with pork and egg.
Many college students eat instant ramen because it is quick and affordable.
After a long day, he made a bowl of ramen for a quick dinner.
Many college students eat instant ramen because it is cheap and easy to prepare.
The restaurant is famous for its spicy pork ramen topped with green onions and egg.
