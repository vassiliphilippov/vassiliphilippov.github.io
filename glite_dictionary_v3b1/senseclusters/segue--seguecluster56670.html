segue

verb
move smoothly to the next section or topic
9.9 5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ / (BrE)
to move smoothly and unhesitatingly from one state, subject, or section to another, especially in music or conversation

Examples

The speaker segued seamlessly from the introduction into the main argument.
Without pausing, the band segued into their next song.
