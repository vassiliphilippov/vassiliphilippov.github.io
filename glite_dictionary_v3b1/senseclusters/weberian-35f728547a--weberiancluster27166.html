Weberian

adjective
of Weberian apparatus
Pronunciation: / weɪˈbɪəriən / (AmE), / weɪˈbɪəriən / (BrE)
relating to the Weberian apparatus or the small bones connecting the swim bladder and inner ear in certain bony fishes

Examples

Weberian bones in fish
The Weberian structures in these fish enhance their ability to detect underwater sounds.
Researchers examined the Weberian ossicles to learn more about fish hearing mechanisms.
Carp are known for their well-developed Weberian apparatus, which improves their sense of hearing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ossicular - relating to small bones, especially in the ear
