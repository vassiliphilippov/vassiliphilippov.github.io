swatting

noun
prank emergency call
Pronunciation: / ˈswɑːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈswɒtɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making a false emergency call to send police or a SWAT team to someone's address as a prank or harassment

Examples

Police swatting prank
The celebrity became a victim of swatting when police stormed his home after a fake call.
Authorities are cracking down on swatting because it wastes emergency resources and endangers lives.
After several incidents of swatting, the city introduced stricter penalties for false emergency reports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harassment - aggressive pressure or intimidation towards someone
  • synonym: prank call - a telephone call made as a joke or to trick someone
