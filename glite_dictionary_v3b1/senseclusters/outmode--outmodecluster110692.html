outmode

verb
make outdated or obsolete
Pronunciation: / aʊtˈmoʊd / (AmE), / aʊtˈməʊd / (BrE)
to make something unfashionable, outdated, or obsolete

Examples

To outmode old technology
Digital streaming has outmoded the need for CDs and cassettes.
Once essential, these skills were outmoded by technological advances.
The new regulations may soon outmode traditional payment methods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modernize - to make something more modern or up to date
  • synonym: obsolete - no longer produced or used; out of date
