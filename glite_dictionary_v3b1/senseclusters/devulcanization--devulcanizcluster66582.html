devulcanization

noun
reversing vulcanization
9.9
Pronunciation: / diˌvʌlkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌvʌlkənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of reversing the vulcanization of rubber to make it soft and reusable

Examples

Rubber devulcanization process
The factory uses devulcanization to turn old rubber tires into new products.
Advances in devulcanization have made rubber recycling more efficient and environmentally friendly.
Researchers are developing better devulcanization methods to reduce industrial waste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: vulcanization - the process of treating rubber with sulfur to make it more durable
