genip

noun
tropical tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / dəˈnip / (AmE), / dʒəˈniːp / (BrE)
A tropical American tree (Melicoccus bijugatus) that produces a small edible fruit with green leathery skin and sweet, juicy, translucent pulp.

Examples

Genip tree
She made a delicious juice from fresh genip pulp.
The children eagerly collected genip from the tree during the summer harvest.
Vendors at the local market were selling bags of ripe genip along the roadside.
