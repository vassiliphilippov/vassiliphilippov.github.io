antlike

adjective
industrious or organized
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈæntˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈæntˌlaɪk / (BrE)
showing qualities similar to those of ants, especially being hardworking, disciplined, or highly organized

Examples

Antlike work habits
Her antlike attention to detail meant every file was perfectly labelled and filed in the cabinet.
The team worked in an antlike manner to finish the project ahead of schedule.
His antlike discipline impressed everyone in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: industrious - hardworking and diligent
  • antonym: lazy - unwilling to work or use energy
  • synonym: organized - arranged or structured in a systematic way
