impassive

adjective
not showing emotion
5.9
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpæsɪv / (AmE), / ɪmˈpɑːsɪv / (BrE)
not showing any emotion or feeling; expressionless

Examples

An impassive face
Despite the shocking news, her impassive expression did not change.
He remained impassive during the heated argument, never showing any anger or frustration.
The judge's impassive demeanor made it hard to guess what he was thinking.
Despite the chaos around her, Maria remained impassive and focused on her task.
He listened to the bad news with an impassive expression, giving nothing away.
The judge's impassive face made it impossible to know which way she was leaning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: emotional - showing or expressing strong feelings
  • synonym: emotionless - showing no emotion
  • synonym: expressionless - not showing any emotion through facial expression
  • antonym: expressive - showing or capable of showing feeling
  • synonym: stolid - showing little or no emotion, often dull or unresponsive
