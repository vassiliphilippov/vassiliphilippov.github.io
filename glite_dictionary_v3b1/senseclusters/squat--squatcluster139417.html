squat

verb
perform a bending exercise with knees
Pronunciation: / skwɑt / (AmE), / skwɒt / (BrE)
to perform a movement or exercise where you bend your knees and lower your body close to the ground, then stand up again

Examples

To squat with weights
He goes to the gym every morning to squat heavy weights.
If you want to strengthen your legs, you should learn how to squat properly.
She injured her knee because she tried to squat with too much weight.
Many athletes squat during certain strength training exercises.
She squatted beside the campfire to warm her hands.
The farmer squatted down to examine the young plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crouch - to bend the knees and lower the body close to the ground
