purringly

adverb
like a purr
Pronunciation: / ˈpɜrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpɜːrɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that resembles or suggests purring, often softly and contentedly

Examples

To speak purringly
The kitten nestled in her lap and purred purringly as she stroked its fur.
He spoke purringly, his voice low and soothing, trying to calm the nervous child.
The engine ran purringly after the mechanic finished the repairs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: noisily - in a loud or disruptive manner
  • synonym: softly - in a gentle or quiet manner
