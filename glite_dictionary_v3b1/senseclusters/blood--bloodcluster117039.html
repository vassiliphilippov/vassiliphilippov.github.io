blood

noun
red bodily fluid that circulates in the body
Pronunciation: / blʌd / (AmE), / blʌd / (BrE)
the red fluid that circulates in the arteries and veins of humans and other vertebrates, carrying oxygen and nutrients to the tissues and removing waste products

Examples

Donate blood
Donating blood can help save the lives of accident victims.
Injuries caused a great deal of blood to spill onto the floor.
The doctor drew a sample of blood for testing.
