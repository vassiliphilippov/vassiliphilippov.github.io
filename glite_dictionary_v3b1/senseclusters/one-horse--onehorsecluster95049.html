one-horse

adjective
small and insignificant (esp. of a place or institution)
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌn hɔrs / (AmE), / ˈwʌn hɔːs / (BrE)
(of a place, institution, etc.) small, insignificant, and remote; lacking importance or sophistication.

Examples

A one-horse town
Critics dismissed the school as just another one-horse college with no real reputation.
She grew up in a one-horse town and dreamed of moving to the city.
The company started as a one-horse operation but expanded rapidly over the years.

  • synonym: insignificant - not important or notable
