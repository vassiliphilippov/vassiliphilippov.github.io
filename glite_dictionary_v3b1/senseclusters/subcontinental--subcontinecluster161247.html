subcontinental

adjective
of a large landmass
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌbkɑːntɪˈnɛntəl / (AmE), / ˌsʌbkɒntɪˈnɛntəl / (BrE)
relating to a large, distinct part of a continent, especially the Indian subcontinent in South Asia

Examples

Subcontinental cuisine
The subcontinental cuisine is known for its rich spices and diverse flavors.
Monsoon patterns greatly affect the subcontinental climate each year.
Many languages are spoken across the subcontinental region of South Asia.
