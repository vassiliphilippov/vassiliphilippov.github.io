who'da thought

x
expression of surprise at the unexpected
9.9
Pronunciation: / huːdə ˈθɔt / (AmE), / huːdə ˈθɔːt / (BrE)
(informal) Used to express surprise at an unexpected outcome or turn of events.

Examples

Who'da thought she'd move
Who'da thought she'd move to another country after all these years?
Who'da thought we'd actually finish the project on time?
He became famous overnight—who'da thought it was possible?
