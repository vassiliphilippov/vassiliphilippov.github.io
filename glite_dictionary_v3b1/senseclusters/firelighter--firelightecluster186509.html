firelighter

noun
material for lighting fires
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌlaɪtər / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌlaɪtə / (BrE)
A small block or piece of chemical material that burns easily and is used to help start a fire, especially in a fireplace or barbecue.

Examples

A firelighter block
He used a firelighter to get the barbecue going quickly.
Most supermarket packs of firelighters are easy to break apart and ignite.
Place a firelighter under the logs before lighting the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kindling - small sticks or other material used to start a fire
