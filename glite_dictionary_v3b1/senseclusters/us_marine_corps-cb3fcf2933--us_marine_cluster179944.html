US Marine Corps

proper noun
U.S. military branch specializing in amphibious operations
Pronunciation: / ju ɛs məˈrin kɔr / (AmE), / juː ɛs məˈriːn kɔː / (BrE)
the branch of the United States Armed Forces responsible for providing power projection from the sea, specializing in amphibious operations
