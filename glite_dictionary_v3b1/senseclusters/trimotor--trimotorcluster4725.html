trimotor

noun
three-engined aircraft
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtraɪˌmoʊtər / (AmE), / ˈtraɪˌməʊtə / (BrE)

Examples

A classic trimotor

In the early days of commercial aviation, the trimotor was a popular choice for airlines.
