bani

noun
Romanian coin unit
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːni / (AmE), / ˈbɑːni / (BrE)
a monetary unit of Romania, equal to one hundredth of a leu

Examples

Fifty bani coins
The shopkeeper gave me fifty bani in change after my purchase.
A loaf of bread in Romania might cost just a few bani more this year.
He collected old bani coins as a hobby during his travels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cent - a monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a basic unit of currency
  • degree higher: leu - the basic monetary unit of Romania
