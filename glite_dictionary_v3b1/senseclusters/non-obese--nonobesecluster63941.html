non-obese

noun
person not obese
4.3
Pronunciation: / nɑn oʊˈbis / (AmE), / nɒn əʊˈbiːs / (BrE)
a person who is not obese, especially in medical or research contexts

Examples

A non-obese person
The study compared metabolic rates between non-obese and obese individuals.
Researchers selected only non-obese participants for the control group.
Doctors recommend different treatments for non-obese patients with diabetes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: normal weight - having a body weight within the healthy range
  • antonym: obese - having excessive body fat
