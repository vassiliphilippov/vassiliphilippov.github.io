Scottishness

noun
state or quality of being Scottish
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈskɑː.tɪʃ.nəs / (AmE), / ˈskɒt.ɪʃ.nəs / (BrE)
The state or quality of being Scottish; the characteristics, culture, or identity associated with Scotland or its people.
