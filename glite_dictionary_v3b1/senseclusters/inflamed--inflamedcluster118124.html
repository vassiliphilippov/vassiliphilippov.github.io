inflamed

adjective
hot, swollen, and red due to inflammation
1.1
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfleɪmd / (AmE), / ɪnˈfleɪmd / (BrE)
(of a part of the body) hot, swollen, red, and often painful, especially as a result of infection or injury.

Examples

Inflamed skin after injury
After scratching the mosquito bite, the skin became red and inflamed.
Her inflamed throat made it difficult for her to swallow.
The doctor examined his inflamed eyes and recommended rest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: healthy - not inflamed, free from infection or injury
  • synonym: irritated - showing signs of irritation or mild inflammation
  • synonym: swollen - enlarged by fluid or inflammation
