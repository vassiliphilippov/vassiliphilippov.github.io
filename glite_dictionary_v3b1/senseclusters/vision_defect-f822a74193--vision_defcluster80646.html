vision defect

noun
error in a computer vision system
6.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8v\u026a\u0292\u0259n d\u026a\u02ccf\u025bkt / (AmE), / \u02c8v\u026a\u0292(\u0259)n d\u026a\u02ccf\u025bkt / (BrE)
(in computing, especially computer vision) an error or failure in a vision system or algorithm that causes incorrect interpretation of visual data.

Examples

A vision defect in software
The autonomous vehicle stopped because a sudden vision defect misidentified a pedestrian as a shadow.
Engineers traced the frequent false alarms to a subtle vision defect in the object-detection model.
After retraining the network, the team eliminated the vision defect that caused misclassification under low light.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computer vision error - a fault or mistake in a system that processes visual information from images or video
  • synonym: misclassification - the incorrect labelling of an object or class by an algorithm
