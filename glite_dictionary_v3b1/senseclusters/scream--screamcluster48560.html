scream

noun
something or someone very funny
Pronunciation: / skɹim / (AmE), / skɹiːm / (BrE)
a person or thing that is extremely funny, especially a joke

Examples

A real scream
That comedian was a scream—I couldn't stop laughing at his jokes.
The story she told at dinner was a total scream and had everyone in stitches.
You’ve got to meet Tom; he’s a real scream at parties.
