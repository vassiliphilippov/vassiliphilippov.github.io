multifilament

adjective
made of many filaments
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈfɪləmənt / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈfɪləmənt / (BrE)
consisting of or made from many small filaments or threads

Examples

Multifilament string
The tennis racket was strung with multifilament strings for better comfort and power.
Doctors often use multifilament sutures because they are flexible and easy to handle.
A multifilament cable is less likely to break than a single-strand wire.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monofilament - made from a single filament or thread
