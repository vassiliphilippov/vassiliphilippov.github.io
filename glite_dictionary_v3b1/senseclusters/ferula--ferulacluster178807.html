ferula

noun
bishop's staff
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrjʊlə / (AmE), / ˈfɛrjʊlə / (BrE)
a ceremonial staff carried by a bishop or the pope as a symbol of office

Examples

Pope's ferula
The pope held the ferula as he led the procession into the cathedral.
A silver ferula was presented to the new bishop during the ceremony.
The ornate ferula symbolized the bishop's spiritual authority over the diocese.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crosier - a ceremonial staff carried by high-ranking Christian clergy
