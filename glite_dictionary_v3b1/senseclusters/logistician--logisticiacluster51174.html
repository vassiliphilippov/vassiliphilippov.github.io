logistician

noun
supply chain manager
Pronunciation: / ləˌdʒɪˈstɪʃən / (AmE), / ləˌdʒɪˈstɪʃən / (BrE)
a person whose job is to plan, organize, and manage the movement and storage of goods, resources, or information

Examples

A logistician plans shipments
The company hired a logistician to improve their supply chain.
As a logistician, she ensures that products are delivered on time and in good condition.
During the crisis, the logistician coordinated the distribution of emergency supplies efficiently.
The logistician ensured that all medical supplies arrived on time during the emergency.
As a logistician, she coordinated shipments between factories and warehouses across the country.
A skilled logistician can save a company both time and money by optimizing delivery routes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coordinator - a person who organizes people or resources
  • synonym: dispatcher - a person who sends out vehicles or workers to where they are needed
  • synonym: logistics manager - a person responsible for overseeing logistics operations
  • synonym: supply chain manager - a person who oversees the entire supply chain process
