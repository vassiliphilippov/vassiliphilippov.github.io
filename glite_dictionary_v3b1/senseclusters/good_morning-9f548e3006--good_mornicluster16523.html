good morning

noun
morning greeting
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd ˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ɡʊd ˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
a polite expression used to greet someone in the morning

Examples

Say good morning
A quick good morning can brighten someone's entire day.
Every day when he arrived at the office, he smiled and said good morning to his colleagues.
She greeted her neighbors with a cheerful good morning as she walked her dog.
Every time the sun rises, he starts his day with a warm good morning to his family.
It's polite to greet your neighbors with a cheerful good morning each day.
She smiled and said, "Good morning!" as she walked into the office.
