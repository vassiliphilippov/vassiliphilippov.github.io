zodiacal

adjective
relating to the zodiac
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈdaɪ.ək.əl / (AmE), / zəʊˈdaɪ.ək.əl / (BrE)
relating to or connected with the zodiac, especially the zodiacal constellations or signs.
