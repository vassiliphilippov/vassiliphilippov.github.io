formable

adjective
able to be shaped
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrməbəl / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməbəl / (BrE)
able to be shaped, molded, or formed into a particular shape

Examples

Formable metal parts
The metal is formable enough to be used in intricate designs.
Certain plastics remain formable when heated, making them useful in manufacturing.
Artists prefer clay that is highly formable for sculpting detailed figures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexible - able to bend easily without breaking
  • synonym: malleable - easily shaped or bent
  • antonym: rigid - not able to be bent or changed in shape
