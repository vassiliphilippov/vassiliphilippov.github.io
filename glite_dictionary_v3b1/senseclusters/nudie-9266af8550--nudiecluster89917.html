Nudie

proper noun
famous tailor's nickname
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːdi / (AmE), / ˈnjuːdi / (BrE)
the nickname of Nudie Cohn (1902–1984), an American tailor known for designing rhinestone-covered suits for country and western entertainers

Examples

Nudie Cohn suits
Many country music stars wore suits made by Nudie during their performances.

Collectors value the unique style that Nudie brought to western fashion.
