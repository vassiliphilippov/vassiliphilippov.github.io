deliveryman

noun
man employed to deliver goods
1.8
Pronunciation: / dɪˈlɪvəriˌmæn / (AmE), / dɪˈlɪvəriˌman / (BrE)
a man whose job is to deliver goods to customers' homes or businesses

Examples

The deliveryman delivers packages
The deliveryman dropped off the package at my front door.
The pizza deliveryman arrived faster than we expected.
We gave a generous tip to the deliveryman who brought our groceries in the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delivery person - gender-neutral term for someone who delivers goods
