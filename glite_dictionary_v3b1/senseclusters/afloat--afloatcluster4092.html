afloat

adjective
covered with water; flooded
Pronunciation: / əˈfloʊt / (AmE), / əˈfləʊt / (BrE)
covered or flooded with water; awash

Examples

Basement afloat after rain
After the storm, the basement was completely afloat with several inches of water.
By morning, the tent was afloat due to the leaky roof and relentless downpour.
The heavy rains left the entire street afloat and impassable for cars.
