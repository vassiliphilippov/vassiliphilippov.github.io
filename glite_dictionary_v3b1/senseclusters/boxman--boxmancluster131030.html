boxman

noun
warehouse packer
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑksmæn / (AmE), / ˈbɒksmən / (BrE)
a worker who packs, stacks, or handles goods in boxes, especially in a warehouse or factory

Examples

Warehouse boxman
The boxman quickly organized the packages for delivery in the busy warehouse.
As a boxman, he was responsible for making sure all goods were safely packed.
Each boxman must follow strict safety rules when handling heavy boxes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loader - a worker who loads goods onto vehicles or into storage
  • synonym: packer - a person who packs goods for transport or sale
