granulosis

noun
disease with tissue granules
Pronunciation: / ˌɡræn.juˈloʊ.sɪs / (AmE), / ˌɡran.juˈləʊ.sɪs / (BrE)
a disease marked by the formation of small granules or grain-like structures in body tissues

Examples

Granulosis in tissue
The pathologist identified granulosis in the biopsy sample from the patient's liver.
Researchers are studying how granulosis develops in different animal species.
Early detection of granulosis can improve treatment outcomes for affected individuals.

Related Terms

  • synonym: granulomatosis - a disease involving the formation of granulomas in tissues
