Anemone cylindrica

noun
North American anemone species with thimble-shaped fruit
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛməni sɪˈlɪndrɪkə / (AmE), / əˈnɛməni sɪˈlɪndrɪkə / (BrE)
A species of anemone native to North America, characterized by cylindrical clusters of fruit that resemble thimbles.
