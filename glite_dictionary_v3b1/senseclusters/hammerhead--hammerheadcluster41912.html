hammerhead

noun
shark with a hammer-shaped head
9.5 3.7
Pronunciation: /ˈhæm.ər.hed/ (AmE), /ˈhæm.ə.hed/ (BrE)
A medium-sized shark with eyes set at either end of a distinctive, flattened, hammer-shaped head, found in warm waters worldwide.

Examples

Hammerhead shark
Many divers have spotted hammerhead sharks off the coast of Hawaii.
Scientists are studying how the hammerhead's unique shape helps it navigate through the ocean.
The hammerhead uses its wide head to improve vision and locate prey.
