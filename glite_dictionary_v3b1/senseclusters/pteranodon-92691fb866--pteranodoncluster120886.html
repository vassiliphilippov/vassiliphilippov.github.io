Pteranodon

proper noun
extinct flying reptile
9.9
Pronunciation: / təˈrænədɒn / (AmE), / təˈrænədɒn / (BrE)
a large extinct flying reptile with a long toothless beak that lived during the Late Cretaceous period

Examples

A Pteranodon skeleton
The museum displayed a life-sized Pteranodon skeleton hanging from the ceiling.
Scientists believe the Pteranodon used its long wings to glide over ancient seas.
Children were fascinated by the huge wingspan of the Pteranodon in the exhibit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pterosaur - an extinct group of flying reptiles
