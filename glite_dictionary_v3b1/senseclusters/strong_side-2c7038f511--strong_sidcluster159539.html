strong side

noun
side with best cards
6.0
Pronunciation: / strɔŋ saɪd / (AmE), / strɒŋ saɪd / (BrE)
in card games, the partnership or side that holds most of the high cards or is expected to win the most tricks in a hand

Examples

Strong side wins tricks
In bridge, the strong side often takes the lead in bidding and play.
The team realized they were the strong side after counting their high cards.
Strategies can change quickly if the strong side is not obvious at first.
