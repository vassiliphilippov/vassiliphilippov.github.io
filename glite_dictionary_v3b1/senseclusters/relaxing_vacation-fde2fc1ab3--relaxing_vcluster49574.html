relaxing vacation

noun
holiday for rest and leisure
Pronunciation: / rɪˈlæksɪŋ veɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈlæksɪŋ vəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
a holiday taken primarily for rest and leisure, with minimal stress or planned activities

Examples

A relaxing vacation at the beach
A relaxing vacation gives you the time to recharge and forget about daily worries.
After a stressful year at work, we booked a relaxing vacation on a quiet tropical island.
Many people dream of spending their relaxing vacation in a luxurious resort by the sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: holiday - an extended period of leisure and recreation, especially away from home
  • antonym: working holiday - a holiday during which one works, either in employment or voluntarily
