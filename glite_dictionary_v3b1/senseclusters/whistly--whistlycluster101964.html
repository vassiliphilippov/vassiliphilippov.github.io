whistly

adjective
like a whistle
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪsli / (AmE), / ˈwɪsli / (BrE)
making or having a sound that resembles a whistle

Examples

A whistly sound
The kettle let out a whistly sound as the water began to boil.
A whistly breeze swept through the empty hallway late at night.
Her voice took on a whistly tone when she tried to whisper.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hissing - making a sharp sibilant sound
  • synonym: whistling - producing a high-pitched sound like a whistle
