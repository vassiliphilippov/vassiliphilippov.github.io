sandboard

noun
board for sand dunes
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board designed for sliding down sand dunes, similar in shape and use to a snowboard

Examples

A sandboard for dunes
He learned to ride a sandboard on the desert dunes.
Tourists rented a sandboard to experience the thrill of sliding down the sandy hills.
The shop offers lessons for beginners who want to try sandboarding for the first time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skateboard - a board with wheels used for riding on hard surfaces
  • synonym: snowboard - a board used for sliding down snow-covered slopes
