fringe benefit

noun
extra benefit given by an employer
9.9 3.5
Pronunciation: / frɪndʒ ˈbɛnəfɪt /; / frɪndʒ ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (AmE), / frɪndʒ ˈbɛnɪfɪt / (BrE)
an extra advantage or perk, such as health insurance or a company car, provided by an employer in addition to salary or wages

Examples

A fringe benefit package
A car allowance is considered a fringe benefit for many managers.
One of the fringe benefits of working here is free gym membership.
The company offers a range of fringe benefits, including dental insurance and travel allowances.
A company car was included as a fringe benefit with his new position.
Many employees consider health insurance to be the most valuable fringe benefit offered by their employer.
The company offers generous fringe benefits, including tuition reimbursement and gym memberships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allowance - a sum of money granted regularly, often by an employer for a specific purpose
  • synonym: perk - an extra benefit or advantage, especially in a job
  • antonym: salary - regular payment for employment
