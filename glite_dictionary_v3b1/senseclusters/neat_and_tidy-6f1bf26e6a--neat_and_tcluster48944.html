neat and tidy

adjective
clean and well-organized
Pronunciation: / nit ənd ˈtaɪdi / (AmE), / niːt ənd ˈtaɪdi / (BrE)
(of a place or object) clean, orderly, and well-organized; free from mess or clutter.

Examples

A neat and tidy room
He likes to keep his appearance neat and tidy for work.
She keeps her desk neat and tidy so she can always find what she needs.
The hotel room was surprisingly neat and tidy when we arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: messy - untidy or disorganized
  • synonym: tidy - orderly and clean
