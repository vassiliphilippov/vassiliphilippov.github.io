coextensively

adverb
with equal extent
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊɪkˈstɛnsɪvli / (AmE), / ˌkəʊɪkˈstɛnsɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that covers exactly the same area, range, or scope as something else

Examples

Apply coextensively to regions
The two laws apply coextensively to all regions within the country.
Their responsibilities were defined coextensively to avoid any overlap or confusion.
The boundaries of the two districts are drawn coextensively for administrative purposes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concurrently - at the same time
  • synonym: equally - to the same degree or amount
