bicentennial

noun
200th anniversary or its celebration
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.sənˈtɛn.i.əl / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.senˈten.i.əl / (BrE)
The 200th anniversary of a significant event, or a celebration held to mark it.

Examples

A city's bicentennial
A special coin was minted to commemorate the city's bicentennial.
Teachers organized a project on the bicentennial of the Declaration of Independence.
The country celebrated its bicentennial with fireworks, parades, and historical exhibitions.
