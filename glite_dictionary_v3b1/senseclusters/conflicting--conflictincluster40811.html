Synonyms

  • incompatible
    5.1 0.04/m
    incompatible cannot work or exist together because of strong difference, while conflicting means opposed or in disagreement in general.
  • at odds
    4.8 5.68/m
    at odds describes a clear disagreement between people or groups, while conflicting is a neutral adjective for things that oppose one another.
  • at variance
    7.7 0.63/m
    at variance sounds formal and points to disagreement in facts or views, while conflicting is more general opposition between things.
  • inconsistent
    4.7 10/m
    inconsistent says things do not match or stay the same over time, while conflicting means they are opposed or in disagreement.
  • contrary
    4.5 17/m
    contrary is strongly opposite in nature or idea, while conflicting focuses on active disagreement or incompatibility between things.
  • clashing
    4.9 0.00/m
    clashing suggests a sharp, visible or loud conflict, while conflicting is a broader term for things that disagree or oppose each other.
  • discordant
    7.4 1.16/m
