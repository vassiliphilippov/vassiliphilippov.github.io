jumbojet

noun
very large passenger jet plane
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌm.boʊˌdʒɛt / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌm.bəʊˌdʒɛt / (BrE)
a very large jet-powered passenger aircraft, typically designed to carry a substantial number of passengers over long distances

Examples

A jumbojet airplane

When the jumbojet landed, dozens of service vehicles rushed to meet it.
