bandager

noun
person applying bandages
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbændɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈbændɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person or device that applies bandages to wounds or injuries

Examples

The bandager applies bandages
The bandager quickly wrapped the wound to stop the bleeding.
In the first aid class, each student took turns being the bandager for practice.
A reliable bandager is essential in any emergency medical kit.

Related Terms

  • synonym: medic - a medical professional, especially in the military
  • synonym: nurse - a person trained to care for the sick or injured
