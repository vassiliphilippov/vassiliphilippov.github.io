neonatal

adjective
relating to newborn infants (first month)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌnioʊˈneɪtəl / (AmE), / ˌniːəʊˈneɪtəl / (BrE)
relating to or affecting newborn infants, especially during the first month after birth

Examples

Neonatal care
Advances in neonatal medicine have significantly improved survival rates for newborns.
She underwent training in neonatal nursing to care for infants with complex medical needs.
The hospital's neonatal unit provides specialized intensive care for premature babies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postnatal - relating to the period after childbirth
  • antonym: prenatal - relating to the period before birth
