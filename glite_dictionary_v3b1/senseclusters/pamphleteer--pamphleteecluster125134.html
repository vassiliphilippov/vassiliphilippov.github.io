pamphleteer

noun
writer of political or advocacy pamphlets
Pronunciation: / ˌpæmfləˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌpamflɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
a person who writes pamphlets, especially to promote a particular political or social cause

Examples

A pamphleteer writes pamphlets
In the eighteenth century, a skilled pamphleteer could influence public opinion across Europe.
Many political movements relied on the passionate words of a dedicated pamphleteer to rally support.
The famous pamphleteer distributed leaflets criticizing the government's new policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: propagandist - person who spreads or creates propaganda
