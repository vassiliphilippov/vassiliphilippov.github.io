curling stone

noun
stone used in the game of curling
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːlɪŋ stoʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɜːlɪŋ stəʊn / (BrE)
A heavy, rounded granite stone with a handle, used for sliding across ice in the game of curling.

Examples

A curling stone handle
A standard curling stone weighs nearly 20 kilograms and is made of polished granite.
During practice, each team member took turns sliding the curling stone down the ice.
The player carefully released the curling stone, aiming for the center of the target.

  • synonym: stone - a hard, solid, nonmetallic mineral matter of which rocks are made
