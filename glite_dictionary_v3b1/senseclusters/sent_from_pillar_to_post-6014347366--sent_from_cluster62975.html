sent from pillar to post

x
repeatedly sent elsewhere for help
9.0
Pronunciation: / sɛnt frəm ˈpɪlɚ tə poʊst / (AmE), / sɛnt frəm ˈpɪlə tə pəʊst / (BrE)
to be repeatedly sent from one person or place to another, usually without achieving help or a solution to a problem

Examples

Sent from pillar to post repeatedly
After the accident, he was sent from pillar to post in search of insurance information.
Jane felt exhausted after being sent from pillar to post by government agencies.
The refugees were sent from pillar to post before finally finding a place to stay.
