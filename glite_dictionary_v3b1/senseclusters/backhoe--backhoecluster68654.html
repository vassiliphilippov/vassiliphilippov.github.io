backhoe

noun
excavating machine with a backward-drawn bucket
7.8 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.hoʊ/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.həʊ/ (BrE)
A mechanical excavator with a digging bucket on the end of a hinged arm, designed to draw the bucket backward to move earth.

Examples

A backhoe excavator
He learned to operate a backhoe as part of his job on the worksite.
Several backhoes were parked outside the excavation area after the day's work was done.
The construction crew used a backhoe to dig the foundation for the new building.

Related Terms

  • synonym: excavator - a machine used for digging and moving large amounts of earth
