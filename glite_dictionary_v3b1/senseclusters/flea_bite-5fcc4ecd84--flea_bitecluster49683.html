flea bite

noun
minor inconvenience or annoyance
Pronunciation: / ˈfliː ˌbaɪt / (AmE), / ˈfliː ˌbaɪt / (BrE)
A very minor inconvenience or annoyance, especially compared to something much more serious.

Examples

Just a flea bite
Compared to the challenges she had faced last year, this setback was just a flea bite.
Missing one meeting is a flea bite compared to the problems we've had before.
The money lost in the deal was a flea bite to the wealthy investor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nuisance - something that causes slight annoyance
  • synonym: trifle - something of little value or importance
