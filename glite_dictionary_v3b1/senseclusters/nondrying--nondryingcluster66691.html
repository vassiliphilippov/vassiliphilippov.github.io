nondrying

adjective
not becoming dry
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈdraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈdraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not becoming dry or not intended to dry when exposed to air

Examples

Nondrying oil paint
This paint is made with a nondrying oil base.
The lotion contains a nondrying formula to keep your skin moisturized.
Artists sometimes prefer nondrying oils for blending colors over longer periods.

Related Terms

  • antonym: drying - becoming dry or causing to become dry
