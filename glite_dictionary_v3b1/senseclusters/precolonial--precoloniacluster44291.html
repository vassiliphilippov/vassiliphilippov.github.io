precolonial

adjective
before colonization
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌpriːkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
relating to the period before a region or country was colonized by foreign powers

Examples

Precolonial culture
The museum displays precolonial artifacts from the region.
Historians study precolonial societies to understand their cultures before foreign influence.
Traditional music from the precolonial era is still performed at local festivals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: colonial - relating to the period when a region was under foreign control
  • antonym: postcolonial - relating to the period after a region was colonized
