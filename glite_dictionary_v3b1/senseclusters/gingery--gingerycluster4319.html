gingery

adjective
bright orange-brown in color
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪn.dʒɚ.i / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪn.dʒ.ər.i / (BrE)
(especially of hair or fur) having a bright orange-brown color.

Examples

Gingery hair color
Her gingery hair glowed in the afternoon sunlight.
His beard was thick and gingery, matching the color of autumn leaves.
They adopted a gingery kitten from the animal shelter.
