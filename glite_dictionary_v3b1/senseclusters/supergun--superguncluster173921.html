supergun

proper noun
Iraqi experimental weapon
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːpərˌɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈsuːpəˌɡʌn / (BrE)
the unofficial name for a large experimental artillery weapon developed for Iraq in the late 1980s, known as Project Babylon

Examples

Iraq's supergun project
The international community was alarmed by reports of Iraq's supergun project in the late 1980s.
Investigators seized components of the supergun linked to Iraq's Project Babylon during the raid.
The supergun was designed to launch projectiles over extremely long distances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Project Babylon - the Iraqi project to build a massive artillery gun in the 1980s
  • synonym: artillery - large military guns used in warfare
