civil discourse

noun
polite and respectful discussion
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪvəl dɪsˈkɔrs / (AmE), / ˈsɪvəl dɪsˈkɔːs / (BrE)
polite and respectful conversation or discussion, especially about controversial or political topics

Examples

Respectful civil discourse
Social media can make civil discourse challenging, but it is still important.
The meeting maintained a tone of civil discourse despite opposing viewpoints.
Universities strive to encourage civil discourse among students on difficult issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argument - a reasoned exchange of opposing views
  • synonym: debate - a formal discussion on a particular topic
  • antonym: hostile exchange - conversation characterized by anger or disrespect
