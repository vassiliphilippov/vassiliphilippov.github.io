snitch

noun
person who informs the police
2.2 6.0
Pronunciation: /snɪtʃ/ (AmE), /snɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a person who secretly informs the police or authorities about someone else's wrongdoing, especially to avoid trouble for themselves

Examples

A school snitch
He refused to be a snitch, even when the police offered him a deal.
Nobody likes a snitch in the neighborhood because they can't be trusted.
The thief was caught after a snitch tipped off the police about the robbery.

  • synonym: rat - a person who betrays others, especially by informing to the authorities
