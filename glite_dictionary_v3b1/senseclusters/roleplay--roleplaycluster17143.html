roleplay

verb
perform a role on stage or in drama
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlˌpleɪ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlˌpleɪ / (BrE)
to perform a specific character or role, especially as part of a play, drama, or theatrical production

Examples

To roleplay a character
During the workshop, the students were asked to roleplay different scenarios to practice their acting skills.
He will roleplay the main antagonist in this summer's community play.
She loves to roleplay historical figures in her school theater productions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: act - to perform a role in a play or film
  • synonym: perform - to take part in a dramatic presentation
