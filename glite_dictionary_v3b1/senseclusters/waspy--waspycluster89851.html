waspy

adjective
like a wasp
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑspi / (AmE), / ˈwɒspi / (BrE)
resembling a wasp in appearance, especially having a narrow waist or black-and-yellow stripes.

Examples

Waspy waist
The model wore a waspy belt that accentuated her narrow waist.
The insect's waspy coloring made it easy to mistake for a real wasp.
Her new dress had a waspy silhouette with bold, striped patterns.
