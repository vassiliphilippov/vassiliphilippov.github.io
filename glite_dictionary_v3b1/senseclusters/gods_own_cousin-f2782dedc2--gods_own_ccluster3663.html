God's own cousin

noun
someone behaving as if greatly superior
Pronunciation: / ɡɑdz oʊn ˈkʌzən / (AmE), / ɡɒdz əʊn ˈkʌzən / (BrE)
A person who acts with excessive self-importance or behaves as if they are superior to everyone else.
