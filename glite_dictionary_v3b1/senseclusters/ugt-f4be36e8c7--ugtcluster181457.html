UGT

proper noun
Brazilian trade union
Pronunciation: / juː dʒiː tiː / (AmE), / juː dʒiː tiː / (BrE)
the abbreviation for 'União Geral dos Trabalhadores', a major national trade union federation in Brazil

Examples

Brazilian UGT union
The UGT played a key role in negotiating new labor agreements last year.
Many workers joined the UGT to seek better representation and support.
The UGT has a long history of organizing strikes and advocating for workers' rights in Brazil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: CUT - Central Única dos Trabalhadores, another major Brazilian trade union federation
  • synonym: trade union - an organization of workers formed to protect their rights and interests
