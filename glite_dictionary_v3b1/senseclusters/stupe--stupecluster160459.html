stupe

noun
medicated cloth compress
9.9
Pronunciation: / stuːp / (AmE), / stjuːp / (BrE)
a piece of cloth soaked in a hot, medicated liquid and applied to the body to relieve pain or inflammation

Examples

Warm stupe treatment
The nurse placed a warm stupe on the patient's sore arm to ease the pain.
After the injury, a medic soaked a stupe in herbal solution and applied it to the swelling.
Doctors sometimes recommend using a stupe to help reduce inflammation in affected areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: compress - a pad of material applied to the body to relieve pain or reduce inflammation
  • synonym: poultice - a soft, moist mass applied to the body to relieve soreness and inflammation
