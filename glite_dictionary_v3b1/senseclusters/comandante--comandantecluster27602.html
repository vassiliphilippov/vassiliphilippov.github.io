comandante

noun
guerrilla leader title
9.8
Pronunciation: / koʊˌmɑːnˈdɑːnteɪ / (AmE), / kəˌmænˈdænteɪ / (BrE)
a title or form of address for the leader of a guerrilla or revolutionary movement, especially in Spanish-speaking countries

Examples

Rebel comandante
The villagers welcomed comandante Rojas when he returned to lead the rebel column.
During the revolution, everyone respected the authority of comandante García.
The young fighter dreamed of one day becoming a comandante in the movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commander - a person in authority, especially over a military group
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
