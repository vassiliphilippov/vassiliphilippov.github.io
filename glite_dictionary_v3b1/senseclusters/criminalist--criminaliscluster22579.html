criminalist

noun
forensic science expert
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪmɪnəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈkrɪmɪnəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who uses scientific techniques to collect and analyze evidence in order to solve crimes

Examples

The criminalist examines evidence
The criminalist carefully examined the blood samples collected from the crime scene.
A skilled criminalist can often find clues that others might overlook.
Police relied on the criminalist to match the DNA evidence to a suspect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detective - a person who investigates crimes, usually for the police
  • synonym: forensic scientist - a specialist who applies scientific methods to investigate crimes
