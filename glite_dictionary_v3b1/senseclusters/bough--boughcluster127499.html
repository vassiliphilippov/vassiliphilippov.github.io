bough

noun
main branch of a tree
Pronunciation: / baʊ / (AmE), / baʊ / (BrE)
a main branch of a tree, especially one of the larger or more prominent branches

Examples

A large bough
A bird built its nest on the highest bough of the oak tree.
After the storm, a heavy bough lay broken on the ground.
The children enjoyed swinging from a thick bough over the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: branch - a limb growing out from the trunk or a bough of a tree
