incongruently

adverb
not fitting or matching
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkɑŋɡruəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəntli / (BrE)
in a way that does not fit, match, or agree with something else

Examples

Placed incongruently in room
The cheerful decorations were incongruently placed in the otherwise serious office.
He laughed incongruently during the tense meeting, surprising everyone.
The modern furniture stood incongruently among the antique pieces in the old house.
The two shapes were drawn incongruently, so they did not match when overlapped.
If you solve the equation incongruently, your answer may not fit the required conditions.
The patterns on the tiles were arranged incongruently, creating an uneven appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: congruently - in a way that fits or matches well
  • synonym: inconsistently - in a way that is not consistent or regular
