tableting

noun
making tablets
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪblɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈteɪblɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of forming substances into tablets, especially in pharmaceutical manufacturing

Examples

Pharmaceutical tableting process
Advances in tableting have improved the consistency of pharmaceutical products.
The tableting process requires precise control of pressure and ingredients.
Automated tableting machines can produce thousands of tablets per hour.

Related Terms

  • antonym: encapsulation - the process of enclosing substances in capsules
  • synonym: granulation - the process of forming grains or granules from a powdery substance
