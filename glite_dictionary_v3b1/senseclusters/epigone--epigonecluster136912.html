epigone

noun
inferior imitator of a distinguished person
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈe.pɪ.ɡoʊn/ /; / /ˈe.pə.ɡoʊn/ /; / /ˈe.pɪ.ɡɑːn/ /; / /ˈe.pə.ɡɑːn/ (AmE), /ˈe.pɪ.ɡəʊn/ /; / /ˈe.pə.ɡəʊn/ (BrE)
