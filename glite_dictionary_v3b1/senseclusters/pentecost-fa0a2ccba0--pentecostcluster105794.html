Pentecost

noun
Christian festival seventh Sunday after Easter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.tɪˌkɑst / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.tɪˌkɒst / (BrE)
The seventh Sunday after Easter, commemorating the descent of the Holy Spirit upon the Apostles; a major Christian festival.

Examples

Christian Pentecost festival
In Scotland, Pentecost was traditionally observed as a quarter day for settling rents.
Many churches hold special services on Pentecost to celebrate the coming of the Holy Spirit.
The feast of Pentecost marks an important moment in the Christian liturgical calendar.
