yourself

pronoun
reflexive 'you'
Pronunciation: / jərˈsɛlf / (AmE), / jɔːˈsɛlf / (BrE)
the reflexive form of 'you', used when the subject and object of the verb are the same person being spoken to

Examples

Help yourself to food
If you want more tea, please help yourself.
You should take care of yourself when you are feeling sick.
Did you make this cake yourself or did someone help you?

Related Terms

  • synonym: himself - the reflexive form of 'he'
  • synonym: myself - the reflexive form of 'I'
