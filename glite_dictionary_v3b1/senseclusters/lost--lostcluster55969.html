lost

adjective
deeply absorbed in thought
Pronunciation: / lɔst / (AmE), / lɒst / (BrE)
deeply absorbed in thought; unaware of one's surroundings because of being preoccupied by one's own thoughts

Examples

Lost in thought
During the meeting, Maria seemed completely lost, staring blankly at her notebook.
He was so lost in the book that he didn't hear me come in.
She sat by the window, lost in thought as the rain fell outside.

Related Terms

  • synonym: absentminded - forgetful or inattentive because of being preoccupied
  • synonym: preoccupied - deeply engrossed or absorbed in thought
