Texas bluebonnet

noun
blue-flowered lupine native to Texas
5.0 6.8
Pronunciation: /ˈteks.əs ˈbluː.bə.nət/ /; / /ˈteks.əs ˈbluː.bɑː.nət/ (AmE), /ˈteks.əs ˈbluː.bɒn.ət/ /; / /ˈteks.əs ˈbluː.bə.nət/ (BrE)
a blue-flowered plant (Lupinus texensis), native to Texas and the southwestern US, known as the state flower of Texas and sometimes poisonous to livestock
