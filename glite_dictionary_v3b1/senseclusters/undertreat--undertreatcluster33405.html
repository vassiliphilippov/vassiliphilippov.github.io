undertreat

verb
to give insufficient care
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈtriːt / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈtriːt / (BrE)
to give less attention, care, or processing to something than is necessary or appropriate, resulting in inadequate results
