mental disorder

noun
disturbance of mental health
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛntəl dɪsˌɔrdər / (AmE), / ˈmɛntəl dɪsˌɔːdə / (BrE)
A condition involving disruption in a person’s thinking, feeling, or behavior, regarded as a disturbance of mental health.

Examples

A mental disorder condition
Anxiety is one of the most common types of mental disorder worldwide.
Many people with a mental disorder can lead fulfilling and productive lives.
The stigma surrounding mental disorder often prevents individuals from seeking help.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mental illness - illness affecting the mind, often implying severe disturbance
  • synonym: psychological disorder - disorder of the mind or emotions
