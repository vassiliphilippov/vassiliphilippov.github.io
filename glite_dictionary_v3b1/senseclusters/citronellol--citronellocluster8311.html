citronellol

noun
fragrant chemical compound
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlɔl / (AmE), / ˌsɪtrəˈnɛlɒl / (BrE)
a fragrant chemical compound found in citronella oil, commonly used in perfumes and insect repellents

Examples

Citronellol in perfume
Many natural insect repellents contain citronellol as an active ingredient.
The fresh scent in this soap comes from added citronellol extracted from plants.
Perfume manufacturers often use citronellol to create floral and citrus notes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: citronella oil - an essential oil from citronella grass, used as an insect repellent
  • synonym: geraniol - a fragrant alcohol found in essential oils, used in perfumes
