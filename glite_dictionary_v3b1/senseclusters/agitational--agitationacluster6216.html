agitational

adjective
promoting protest
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌædʒɪˈteɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or intended to promote agitation, protest, or political action

Examples

Agitational protest
The organization launched an agitational campaign to raise awareness about climate change.
He wrote several agitational articles calling for political reform.
The agitational speeches inspired many people to join the protest.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calming - intended to make people less agitated or upset
  • synonym: provocative - intended to cause a reaction or strong emotion
