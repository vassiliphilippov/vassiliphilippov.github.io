Germanization

noun
adapting to German form
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒɜːrmənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdʒɜːmənaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of adapting foreign words, names, or forms to German pronunciation, spelling, or grammar

Examples

Germanization of names
The Germanization of place names was common in regions under German rule.
Linguists often analyze the Germanization of foreign words in historical documents.
The Germanization of surnames sometimes occurred when families moved to German-speaking areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anglicization - the process of making something English in form or character
  • synonym: romanization - the process of converting something to Roman or Latin form
