ill-equipped

adjective
lacking necessary equipment or preparation
Pronunciation: / ɪl ɪˈkwɪpt / (AmE), / ɪl ɪˈkwɪpt / (BrE)
not having the necessary equipment, resources, or preparation for a particular purpose or situation

Examples

An ill-equipped team
Due to budget cuts, the firefighters were ill-equipped for the forest fire.
She felt ill-equipped to deal with the challenges of her new job.
The small hospital was ill-equipped to handle a large influx of patients after the accident.
