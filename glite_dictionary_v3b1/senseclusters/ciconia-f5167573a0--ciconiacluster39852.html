Ciconia

noun
genus of storks (family Ciconiidae)
9.9
Pronunciation: / sɪˈkoʊniə / (AmE), / sɪˈkəʊniə / (BrE)
A genus of storks in the family Ciconiidae, including several European species such as the white stork.

Examples

Ciconia species
Researchers have studied the breeding behavior of Ciconia storks in the wild.
Several species in the genus Ciconia are renowned for their long migrations.
The white stork, Ciconia ciconia, is a familiar sight in parts of Europe.
