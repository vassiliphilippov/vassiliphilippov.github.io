downhill

adverb
toward the bottom of a hill
Pronunciation: / ˌdaʊnˈhɪl / (AmE), / ˌdaʊnˈhɪl / (BrE)
In the direction toward the bottom of a hill or slope.

Examples

To go downhill fast
After reaching the top, they cycled downhill through the forest.
The children raced downhill on their sleds after the first snowfall.
Water flows downhill because of gravity.

Related Terms

  • antonym: uphill - in the direction toward the top of a hill or slope
