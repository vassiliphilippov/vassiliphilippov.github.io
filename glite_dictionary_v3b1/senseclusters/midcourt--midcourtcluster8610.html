midcourt

noun
middle-court player
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌkɔːt / (BrE)
a player who mainly occupies or plays in the middle area of the court, especially in sports like netball or basketball

Examples

A skilled midcourt
The coach praised the midcourt for their quick decision-making during the match.
A strong midcourt can control the flow of the game and support both defense and attack.
She trained hard to become the team's best midcourt in netball.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center - a player positioned in the middle of the court or field
  • antonym: wing - a player who plays near the side of the court or field
