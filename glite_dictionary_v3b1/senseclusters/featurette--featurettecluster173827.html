featurette

noun
short background article
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːtʃəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌfiːtʃəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a short article or piece in a newspaper or magazine that provides background or supplementary information, often with a human-interest angle

Examples

Magazine featurette
The magazine included a featurette about the artist's creative process.
I enjoyed reading the featurette on local volunteers in this week's issue.
Each Sunday, the newspaper publishes a featurette highlighting community events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: column - a regular article or section in a newspaper or magazine
  • synonym: feature - a special article or report in a newspaper or magazine
