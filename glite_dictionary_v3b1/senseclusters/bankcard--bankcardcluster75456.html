bankcard

noun
payment or ATM card
Pronunciation: / ˈbæŋkˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈbæŋkˌkɑːd / (BrE)
a plastic card issued by a bank that allows the holder to make payments or withdraw money from their account

Examples

A bankcard payment
She paid for her groceries with her bankcard.
You can withdraw cash from any ATM using your bankcard.
Many online stores accept payment by bankcard for convenience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cash - money in the form of coins or banknotes
  • synonym: credit card - a card that allows purchases on credit up to a certain limit
  • synonym: debit card - a card that allows direct access to funds in a bank account
