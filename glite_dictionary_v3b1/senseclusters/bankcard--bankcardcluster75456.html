bankcard

noun
payment or ATM card
2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbæŋk.kɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈbæŋk.kɑːd/ (BrE)
a plastic card issued by a bank that allows the holder to make payments or withdraw money from their account

Examples

A bankcard payment

You can withdraw cash from any ATM using your bankcard.
Many online stores accept payment by bankcard for convenience.
