wych elm

noun
species of Eurasian elm tree
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɪtʃ ɛlm / (AmE), / wɪtʃ ɛlm / (BrE)
A species of Eurasian elm (Ulmus glabra) with broad leaves, often planted as a shade tree.

Examples

A wych elm tree
Several wych elms were planted along the avenue to enhance the landscape.
The botanist identified the tree as a wych elm due to its distinctive leaves.
The old wych elm in the village square provides shade on hot summer days.
