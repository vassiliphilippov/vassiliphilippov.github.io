train dispatcher

noun
person managing train operations
1.8
Pronunciation: / treɪn dɪˈspætʃər / (AmE), / treɪn dɪˈspætʃə / (BrE)
a person responsible for directing and managing the operations of trains in a railway yard or on a rail network

Examples

Train dispatcher controls trains
A skilled train dispatcher can prevent accidents by carefully monitoring track activity.
If you want information about a delayed train, the train dispatcher is the person to ask.
The train dispatcher coordinated the arrivals and departures to keep the yard running smoothly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: train controller - person who supervises and directs railway traffic
