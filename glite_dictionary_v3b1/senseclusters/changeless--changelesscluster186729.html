changeless

adjective
not changeable; remaining the same
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃeɪndʒləs / (AmE), / ˈtʃeɪndʒləs /; / ˈtʃeɪn(d)ʒləs / (BrE)
not changing; staying the same over time
