match plane

noun
woodworking tool for making tongues and grooves
5.3
Pronunciation: / mætʃ pleɪn / (AmE), / matʃ pleɪn / (BrE)
a hand plane equipped with cutting blades to form tongues and grooves on the edges of boards for joining

Examples

A match plane tool
He inherited an old match plane from his grandfather's collection of woodworking tools.
Proper use of a match plane ensures tight-fitting joints in wooden flooring.
The carpenter used a match plane to create precise grooves along the edge of each board.
