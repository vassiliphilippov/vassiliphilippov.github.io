scoffer

noun
person who mocks or jeers
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔfər / (AmE), / ˈskɒfə / (BrE)
A person who mocks, jeers at, or treats someone or something with contempt or derision.

Examples

A scoffer mocks
Despite the scoffers, she continued to pursue her unconventional ideas.
He was known as a scoffer of traditional beliefs, constantly challenging those around him.
The internet is full of scoffers who delight in mocking others anonymously.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admirer - someone who looks at something or someone with approval or respect
  • synonym: jeerer - a person who jeers or shouts derisively
  • synonym: mockery - ridicule or contempt; subject of laughter
