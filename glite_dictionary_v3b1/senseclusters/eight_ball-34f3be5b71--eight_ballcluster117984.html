eight ball

noun
black ball numbered 8 in pool
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈeɪt bɔl / (AmE), / ˈeɪt bɔːl / (BrE)
the black ball numbered 8 in pool or billiards, which must be the last ball pocketed in certain games

Examples

The eight ball in pool
He sank the eight ball to win the game.
In eight-ball pool, you must pocket the eight ball last.
She scratched while shooting at the eight ball, losing the match.
