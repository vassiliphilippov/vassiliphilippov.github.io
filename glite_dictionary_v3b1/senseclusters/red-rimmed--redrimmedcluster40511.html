red-rimmed

adjective
with a red border or rim
6.3 5.3
Pronunciation: /red rɪmd/ (AmE), /red rɪmd/ (BrE)
Having a rim or border of red color, especially used to describe eyes with redness around the eyelids.

Examples

Red-rimmed eyes
His allergies always leave him with red-rimmed eyelids in the morning.
She walked in with red-rimmed eyes after the emotional movie.
The child's red-rimmed eyes were a sign that she had been up all night crying.
