trichome

noun
plant hair or outgrowth
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtraɪ.koʊm/ /; / /ˈtrɪ.koʊm/ (AmE), /ˈtraɪ.kəʊm/ /; / /ˈtrɪ.kəʊm/ (BrE)
a small hair or hairlike outgrowth on the surface of a plant, alga, or similar organism, often serving protective or sensory functions
