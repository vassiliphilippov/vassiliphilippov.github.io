chine

noun
boat edge or river valley
9.5
Pronunciation: / tʃaɪn / (AmE), / tʃaɪn / (BrE)
a sharp edge or angle on the hull of a boat, or a steep-sided river valley, especially on the coast

Examples

Boat chine edge
The sharp chine of the boat allowed it to turn quickly in the water.
We hiked down a narrow chine to reach the secluded beach.
The designer added a pronounced chine to improve the vessel's stability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: keel - the central structural base of a boat's hull
  • synonym: valley - a low area of land between hills or mountains
