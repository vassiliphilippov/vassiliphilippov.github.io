unfalsifiable

adjective
cannot be disproved
9.5
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfɑːl.sɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈfɔːl.sɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfɒl.sɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ /; / /ʌn.ˈfɔːl.sɪ.faɪ.ə.bəl/ (BrE)
not able to be proven false by observation, experiment, or evidence

Examples

Unfalsifiable scientific claim
The claim that invisible fairies exist is unfalsifiable because it cannot be tested.
Some philosophical arguments are criticized for being unfalsifiable and therefore unscientific.
A good scientific theory should not be unfalsifiable, but open to being tested and disproved.
