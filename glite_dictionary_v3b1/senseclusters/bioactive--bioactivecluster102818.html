bioactive

noun
biologically active substance
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪ.oʊˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌbaɪ.əʊˈæktɪv / (BrE)
a substance, especially from food or plants, that has an effect on living cells or organisms

Examples

A bioactive in food
Scientists are studying a new bioactive found in green tea for its potential health benefits.
The supplement contains several bioactives that may help support immune function.
Many fruits and vegetables are rich in bioactives that can positively affect human health.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nutrient - a substance that provides nourishment essential for growth and life
  • synonym: phytochemical - a chemical compound produced by plants, often with health effects
