supermassive

adjective
extremely massive
Pronunciation: / ˌsuːpərˈmæsɪv / (AmE), / ˌsuːpəˈmæsɪv / (BrE)

Examples

The supermassive star emits an incredible amount of energy compared to our sun.
Scientists study supermassive objects to understand the formation of galaxies.
