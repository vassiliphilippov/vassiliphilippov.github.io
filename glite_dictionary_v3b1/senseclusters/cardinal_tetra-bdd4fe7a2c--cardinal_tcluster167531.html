cardinal tetra

noun
a small, vividly colored aquarium fish
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrdɪnəl ˈtɛtrə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːdɪnəl ˈtɛtrə / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored aquarium fish (Paracheirodon axelrodi) with vivid red and blue stripes, native to streams in Brazil and Colombia
