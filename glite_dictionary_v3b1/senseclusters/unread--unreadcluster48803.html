unread

adjective
not yet read
2.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɛd / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɛd / (BrE)
not yet read or looked at by anyone

Examples

Unread messages
I noticed several unread emails in my inbox this morning.
The book sat unread on the shelf for months before I finally picked it up.
She left the letter unread, unsure if she wanted to know its contents.
