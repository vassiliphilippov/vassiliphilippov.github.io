floppy-wristed

adjective
showing weakness or lack of confidence
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑpi ˌrɪstɪd / (AmE), / ˈflɒpi ˌrɪstɪd / (BrE)
(of a gesture or action) showing weakness or lack of confidence.

The coach criticized his player's floppy-wristed attempt at shooting the basketball.
