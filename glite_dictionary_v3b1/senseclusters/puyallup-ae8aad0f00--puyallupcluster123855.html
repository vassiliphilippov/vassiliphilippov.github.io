Puyallup

proper noun
river in Washington
9.9
Pronunciation: / pjuːˈæləp / (AmE), / pjuːˈæləp / (BrE)
a river in Washington State, USA, that flows from Mount Rainier's glaciers to Commencement Bay in Puget Sound

Examples

Puyallup River
The Puyallup River is known for its annual salmon migration.
Many communities along the Puyallup depend on its water for agriculture.
Glacial melt from Mount Rainier feeds the Puyallup throughout the year.
