womanist

adjective
relating to womanism
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊmənɪst / (AmE), / ˈwʊmənɪst / (BrE)
relating to womanism or its principles, especially focusing on the experiences and rights of women, particularly Black women

Examples

Womanist ideas
Her womanist perspective brought attention to the unique challenges faced by Black women in society.
The conference featured several womanist speakers discussing social justice and equality.
A womanist approach often highlights the intersection of race, gender, and culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: feminist - relating to the advocacy of women's rights and equality
