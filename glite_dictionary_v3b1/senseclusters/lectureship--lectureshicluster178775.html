lectureship

noun
position or post of a lecturer
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛktʃərˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˈlɛktʃəʃɪp / (BrE)
the position or post of a lecturer, especially at a college or university

Examples

A lectureship position

She was awarded a lectureship in English literature at the university.
The university announced several new lectureships to expand their academic staff.
