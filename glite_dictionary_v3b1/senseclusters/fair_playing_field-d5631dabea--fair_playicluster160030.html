A situation in which all participants have an equal chance to succeed, with no unfair advantages or disadvantages for anyone.

Examples

A fair playing field
It's important in any competition to maintain a fair playing field for all participants.
The government introduced new regulations to create a fair playing field for small businesses.
Without a fair playing field, some competitors will always be at a disadvantage.
