social anthropologist

noun
person studying social and cultural anthropology
Pronunciation: / ˈsoʊʃəl ˌænθrəˈpɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˈsəʊʃ(ə)l ˌænθrəˈpɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a specialist in anthropology who studies social and cultural patterns, especially kinship systems, institutions, and social relationships within societies

Examples

Social anthropologist studies cultures
As a social anthropologist, she has written extensively on the role of family in different societies.
Many social anthropologists analyze rituals and ceremonies to understand cultural relationships.
The social anthropologist conducted fieldwork in a remote village to study traditional kinship structures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultural anthropologist - an anthropologist focused on culture and cultural variation
