untackled

adjective
not stopped in sports
7.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtækəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈtækəld / (BrE)
not having been brought down, stopped, or held by an opponent in contact sports such as football or rugby

Examples

Untackled player
The running back remained untackled as he dashed across the field for a touchdown.
She managed to stay untackled throughout the entire play, impressing her teammates.
After a quick move, the player was still untackled and advanced the ball further.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tackled - having been brought down or stopped by an opponent
  • synonym: unopposed - not challenged or blocked by an opponent
