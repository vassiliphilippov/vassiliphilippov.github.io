technocracy

noun
AI- or algorithm-driven government
8.1 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / t5bkc8n51kr59si / (AmE), / t5bkc8n52kr59si / (BrE)
(in contemporary usage) a system of governance in which policy decisions are made or heavily directed by automated algorithms or artificial intelligence systems rather than by elected officials or human experts.

Examples

Technocracy by AI
Critics warn that a technocracy powered by AI could erode democratic accountability and transparency.
In the film, an oppressive technocracy used surveillance algorithms to decide citizens' daily lives.
Some scholars argue that algorithmic decision-making might create a post-human technocracy without elected leaders.

Related Terms

  • synonym: AI governance - systems in which artificial intelligence tools are used to set, implement, or enforce policies
  • synonym: algorithmic governance - the use of automated algorithms to make or guide public decisions and administrative processes
  • antonym: democracy - a system of government by the people, typically through elected representatives; contrasted with rule by automated systems
