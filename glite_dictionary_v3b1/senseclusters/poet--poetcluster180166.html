poet

noun
a writer of poems
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊ.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈpəʊ.ɪt / (BrE)
a person who writes poems, especially one who is admired for their imaginative or expressive use of language

Examples

A famous poet
Emily Dickinson is considered a great poet of American literature.
Many cultures honor the poet for capturing the emotions of their time.
The young poet performed his latest work at the festival.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bard - a poet, often one reciting epic or heroic poetry
  • synonym: poetess - female poet (archaic usage)
  • degree lower: writer - a person who writes books, stories, articles, etc.
