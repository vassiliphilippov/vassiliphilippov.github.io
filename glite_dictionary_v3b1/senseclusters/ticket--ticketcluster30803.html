ticket

noun
list of party's election candidates
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkət / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt / (BrE)
a list of candidates put forward by a political party to contest an election for various public offices

Examples

A party ticket
Many voters prefer to support the entire ticket instead of choosing candidates individually.
The party selected a diverse ticket for the general election.
The presidential nominee announced his running mate and completed the ticket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slate - a list of candidates nominated for election as a group
