unmounted

adjective
not on horseback
5.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmaʊntɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmaʊntɪd / (BrE)
not on horseback or not having a rider; on foot, especially referring to people or animals usually ridden

Examples

Unmounted soldier
The soldiers marched unmounted after leaving their horses at the camp.
During the parade, the police officers were unmounted and walked alongside the crowd.
After the race, the jockeys stood unmounted beside their tired horses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismounted - having gotten off a horse or vehicle
  • antonym: mounted - on horseback or having a rider
