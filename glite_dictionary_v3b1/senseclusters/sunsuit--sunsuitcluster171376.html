sunsuit

noun
child's lightweight top-and-shorts outfit
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌn.suːt / (AmE), / ˈsʌn.suːt / (BrE)
A child's lightweight outfit combining a sleeveless top and shorts, typically worn for play in warm weather.

Examples

A sunsuit for children
She packed several sunsuits before their seaside holiday.
The old photograph shows children playing on the beach, each wearing a sunsuit.
When summer arrived, she dressed her toddler in a colorful sunsuit to keep him cool.
