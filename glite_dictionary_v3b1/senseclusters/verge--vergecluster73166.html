verge

verb
to approach or come close to (something)
5.1 0.70/m
Pronunciation: / v…ùd í /; / v…úrd í / (AmE), / v…úÀêd í / (BrE)
to come very close to or almost be something

Examples

Verge on disaster
Her excitement verged on hysteria as the concert began.
His reckless comments verged on disrespectful.
The situation verges on chaos whenever the manager is away.
The conversation verged on becoming an argument, but they managed to stay calm.
Her excitement verged on impatience as she waited for the results.
The old house verges on the edge of the forest, almost hidden by the trees.

  • come close
