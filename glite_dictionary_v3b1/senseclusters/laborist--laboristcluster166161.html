laborist

noun
hospital childbirth doctor
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪbərɪst / (AmE), / ˈleɪbərɪst / (BrE)
a doctor, usually an obstetrician, who specializes in caring for women during labor and delivery in a hospital

Examples

Hospital laborist
The hospital hired a new laborist to provide round-the-clock care for women in labor.
As a laborist, she manages all deliveries and emergencies in the maternity ward.
Patients appreciated having a dedicated laborist available during their childbirth experience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: midwife - a trained professional who assists women during childbirth
  • synonym: obstetrician - a doctor specializing in pregnancy and childbirth
