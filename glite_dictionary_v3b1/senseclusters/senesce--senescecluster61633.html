senesce

verb
to grow old; to age
4.5
Pronunciation: / səˈnɛs / (AmE), / sɪˈnɛs / (BrE)
to grow old; to undergo the process of aging

Examples

To senesce naturally
As humans senesce, their cells become less efficient at repair.
Many species of trees senesce at a predictable age in the wild.
The scientist studied how cells senesce over time in culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: age - to grow older; to show signs of aging
  • antonym: rejuvenate - to make or become young again
