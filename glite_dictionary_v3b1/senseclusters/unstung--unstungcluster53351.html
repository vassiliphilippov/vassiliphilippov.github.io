unstung

adjective
not stung
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstʌŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈstʌŋ / (BrE)
not having been stung, especially by an insect or animal that can sting

Examples

Remain unstung by wasps
Despite the swarm of wasps nearby, she remained unstung throughout the afternoon.
The beekeeper was proud that his careful handling left him unstung after a long day.
Most of the hikers finished the trip unstung by any insects, thanks to their protective clothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stung - having been stung by an insect or animal
