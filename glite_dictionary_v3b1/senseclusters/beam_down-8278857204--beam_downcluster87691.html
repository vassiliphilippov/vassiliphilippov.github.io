beam down

verb
shine brightly from above
4.1 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / biÀêm da än / (AmE), / biÀêm da än / (BrE)
to shine brightly from above, especially referring to sunlight or another strong light source

Examples

Sun beams down
During the summer, the sun beams down relentlessly in the desert.
Harsh lights beamed down on the stage, illuminating the performers.
The morning sun started to beam down on the quiet valley.

Synonyms

    2.5 3.19/m
