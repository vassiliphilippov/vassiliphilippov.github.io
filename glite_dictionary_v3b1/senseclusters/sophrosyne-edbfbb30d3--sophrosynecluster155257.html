Sophrosyne

proper noun
female given name
9.9
Pronunciation: / soʊˈfrɒsɪni / (AmE), / səˈfrɒsɪni / (BrE)
a female given name inspired by the ancient Greek concept of temperance, self-control, and moderation

Examples

Sophrosyne as a name
They named their daughter Sophrosyne to honor the value of self-control.
At the ceremony, Sophrosyne gave a speech about the importance of moderation in life.
The name Sophrosyne reflects a tradition of choosing meaningful names from ancient philosophy.
