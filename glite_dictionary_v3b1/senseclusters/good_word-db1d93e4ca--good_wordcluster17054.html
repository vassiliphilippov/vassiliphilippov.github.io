good word

noun
statement of praise or recommendation
Pronunciation: / ɡʊd wɝd / (AmE), / ɡʊd wɜːd / (BrE)
a statement of praise or recommendation about someone or something; a commendation

Examples

Say a good word
I always try to have a good word for anyone who works hard.
If you see the manager, could you say a good word on my behalf?
She put in a good word for me when I applied for the job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recommendation - an act of endorsing or supporting someone or something
