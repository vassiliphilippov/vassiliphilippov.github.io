unmotivating

adjective
not inspiring interest
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈmoʊtɪˌveɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈməʊtɪveɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
not making someone feel interested, eager, or willing to do something

Examples

An unmotivating speech
The manager's speech was so unmotivating that the team felt discouraged.
An unmotivating environment can make it hard for employees to stay productive.
She found the repetitive tasks at her job extremely unmotivating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: uninspiring - not making someone feel excited or interested
