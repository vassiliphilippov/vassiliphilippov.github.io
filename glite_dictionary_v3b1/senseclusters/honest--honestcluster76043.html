honest

adjective

Pronunciation: / ˈɑnəst /; / ˈɑːnəst /; / ˈɑːnɪst / (AmE), / ˈɒnɪst / (BrE)
truthful, fair, and genuine; not cheating, lying, or pretending (used for people, actions, statements, or things)
