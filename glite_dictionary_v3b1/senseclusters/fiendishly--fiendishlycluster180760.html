fiendishly

adverb
in a wicked or extremely clever way
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːndɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈfiːndɪʃli / (BrE)
In an extremely cruel, wicked, or clever way; resembling or characteristic of a fiend.

Examples

A fiendishly clever plan
She devised a fiendishly clever plan to escape undetected.
The puzzle was fiendishly difficult, taking hours to solve.
The villain laughed fiendishly as his plot unfolded.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diabolically - in a devilish or wicked manner
