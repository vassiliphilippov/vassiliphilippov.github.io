student center

noun
college/university building for student activities
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstuːdənt ˈsɛntər / (AmE), / ˈstjuːdənt ˈsɛntə / (BrE)
A building or area in a college or university where students can gather, socialize, and participate in organized activities.

Examples

Most of the campus events are held in the student center each semester.
She spends her afternoons studying and relaxing with friends at the student center.
The university recently renovated its student center to include more meeting rooms and cafés.
