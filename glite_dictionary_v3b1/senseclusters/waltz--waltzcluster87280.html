waltz

noun
music in triple time for waltzing
Pronunciation: / wɔlts / (AmE), / wɔːlts / (BrE)
a piece of music in triple time, written for or in the style of a waltz dance

Examples

A waltz melody
He composed several waltzes inspired by Vienna's musical tradition.
The orchestra performed a beautiful waltz during the gala.
The pianist played a lively waltz that delighted the audience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polka - a lively dance and its music in 2/4 time
