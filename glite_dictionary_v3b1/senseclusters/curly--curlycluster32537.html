curly

adjective
having curls or waves (hair)
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːli / (AmE), / ˈkɜːli / (BrE)
(of hair) having curls or waves.

Examples

Curly hair
He wished his hair was less curly and easier to comb.
Her curly hair bounced as she walked down the street.
The stylist recommended a special shampoo for people with curly hair.

Related Terms

  • antonym: straight - (of hair) not curly or wavy; smooth and even
  • synonym: wavy - having a slight curl or series of curves
