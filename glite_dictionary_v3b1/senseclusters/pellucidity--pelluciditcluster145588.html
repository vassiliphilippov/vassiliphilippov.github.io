pellucidity

noun
clarity; clearness of expression
Pronunciation: / pəˌluːˈsɪdədi / (AmE), / pɛlˌjuːˈsɪdɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being clear and easy to understand; clarity of expression or thought

Examples

Pellucidity of writing
Critics praised the novel for the pellucidity of its narrative style.
Her writing is admired for its pellucidity and precision, avoiding unnecessary jargon.
The pellucidity of the professor's lectures made complex topics accessible to all students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clarity - the quality of being clear and easy to understand
  • antonym: obscurity - the state of being difficult to understand
