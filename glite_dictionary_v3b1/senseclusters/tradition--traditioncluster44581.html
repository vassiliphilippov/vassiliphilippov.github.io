tradition

noun
long-established custom or practice
Pronunciation: / trəˈdɪʃən / (AmE), / trəˈdɪʃ(ə)n /; / trəˈdɪʃən / (BrE)
a custom, belief, or way of doing something that is passed down within a group or family over a long time
