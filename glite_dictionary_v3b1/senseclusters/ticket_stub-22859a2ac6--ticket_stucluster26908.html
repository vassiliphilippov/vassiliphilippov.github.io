ticket stub

noun
part of a ticket kept as a receipt or proof
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪkɪt stʌb / (AmE), / ˈtɪkɪt stʌb / (BrE)
the part of a ticket that is returned to the holder after admission as proof of purchase or entry

Examples

A ticket stub kept
He kept the ticket stub from his first concert as a souvenir.
Please hold on to your ticket stub until the end of the performance.
The usher asked to see my ticket stub before letting me back into the theater.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticket - a printed piece of paper giving the holder a certain right, typically to enter a place or travel by public transport
