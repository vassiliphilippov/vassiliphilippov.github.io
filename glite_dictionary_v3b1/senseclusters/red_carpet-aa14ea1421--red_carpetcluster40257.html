red carpet

noun
strip of red carpet for dignitaries
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / rɛd ˈkɑrpət /; / rɛd ˈkɑrpɪt / (AmE), / rɛd ˈkɑːpɪt / (BrE)
a long, narrow strip of red carpet laid out for important guests to walk on at formal or ceremonial events

Examples

The organization rolled out the red carpet for its visiting dignitaries.
Walking the red carpet is a highlight for many celebrities during award season.
