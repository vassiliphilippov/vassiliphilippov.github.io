aggravation

noun
provocation that causes anger
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˌæɡ.rəˈveɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌæɡ.rəˈveɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
unfriendly or provoking behavior that causes anger or resentment

Examples

Classmate aggravation
His constant aggravation of his classmates eventually got him sent to the principal's office.
Repeated aggravation from the team manager pushed the players to protest.
The aggravation she received from her neighbor made her move away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: assuagement - the act of making something less severe or intense
  • synonym: irritation - the state of being annoyed or provoked
  • synonym: provocation - an action or statement that angers or irritates
