common touch

noun
an ability to relate to ordinary people
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən tʌtʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən tʌtʃ / (BrE)
the ability to connect with and relate to ordinary people, especially as a quality admired in public figures
