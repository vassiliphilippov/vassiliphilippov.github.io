oyster plant

noun
edible root of the salsify plant
Pronunciation: / ˈɔɪ.stɚ ˌplænt /; / ˈɔɪstɚ ˌplænt /; / ˈɔɪstər ˌplænt / (AmE), / ˈɔɪ.stə ˌplɑːnt /; / ˈɔɪstə ˌplɑːnt / (BrE)
a Mediterranean plant (salsify) with long white roots and purple flowers, grown for its edible root that tastes like oyster
