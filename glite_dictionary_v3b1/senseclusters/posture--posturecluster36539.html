Examples

To posture for a photo

Synonyms

    3.8 6.88/m
    pose is an intentional position held briefly for a camera or artist, while posture is arranging the body's position more generally.
  • sit
    5.9 5.53/m

  • model
    4.1 3.73/m
    model is to display clothes or pose professionally for a photographer or artist, while posture is the simple act of placing the body.
