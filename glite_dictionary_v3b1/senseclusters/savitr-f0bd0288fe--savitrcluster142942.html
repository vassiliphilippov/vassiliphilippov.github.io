Savitr

proper noun
Hindu god of the life-giving sun
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːvɪtər / (AmE), / ˈsævɪtə / (BrE)
an important Hindu god representing the sun, especially in its life-giving aspect, venerated in ancient Vedic religion

Examples

Savitr god
Savitr is often depicted as driving a golden chariot across the sky.
Many hymns in the Rig Veda are dedicated to Savitr, praising him as the inspirer of life.
The morning prayer known as the Gayatri Mantra is addressed to Savitr.
