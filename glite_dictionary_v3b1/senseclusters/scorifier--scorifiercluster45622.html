scorifier

noun
metallurgical vessel
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskɔːrɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / ˈskɒrɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
a small vessel used in metallurgy to fuse ores at high temperatures for analysis or testing

Examples

A scorifier vessel
The technician carefully poured the ore sample into the scorifier before starting the fusion process.
After heating in the scorifier, the metal separated from the impurities for easier analysis.
A scorifier is essential equipment in laboratories that test the composition of metal ores.
