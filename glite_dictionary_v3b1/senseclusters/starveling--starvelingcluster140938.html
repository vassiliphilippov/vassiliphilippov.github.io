starveling

noun
a starving person
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrvlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːvlɪŋ / (BrE)
a person who is starving or suffering from extreme hunger

Examples

A starveling child
The abandoned kitten was a pitiful starveling seeking scraps in the alley.
The poet wrote about the lives of the starvelings in the city slums.
They pitied the starveling who wandered the winter streets in rags.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pauper - a very poor person
