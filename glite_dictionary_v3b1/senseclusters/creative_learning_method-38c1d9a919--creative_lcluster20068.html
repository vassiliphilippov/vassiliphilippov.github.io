creative learning method

noun
innovative educational approach
2.3
Pronunciation: / kɹiˈeɪtɪv ˈlɝnɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (AmE), / kɹiˈeɪtɪv ˈlɜːnɪŋ ˈmɛθəd / (BrE)

Examples
