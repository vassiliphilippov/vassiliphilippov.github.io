pituitary

noun
gland at base of brain; hormone regulator
4.9 5.5
Pronunciation: /pɪ.ˈtuː.ɪ.tə.riː/ /; / /pə.ˈtuː.ə.riː/ (AmE), /pɪ.ˈtjuː.ɪ.tər.i/ /; / /pə.ˈtjuː.ɪ.tər.i/ (BrE)
a small gland at the base of the brain that regulates growth and controls other glands in the endocrine system
