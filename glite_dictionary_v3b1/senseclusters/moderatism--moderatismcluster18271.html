moderatism

noun
middle-of-the-road political philosophy
9.9 6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈmɑː.də.rə.tɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈmɒd.ə.rə.tɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the political philosophy of avoiding extreme left or right positions by adopting a moderate course of action

Examples

Moderatism in politics
Her commitment to moderatism made her popular among those tired of partisan debates.
Many politicians embrace moderatism to appeal to a broader range of voters.
The party promotes moderatism as a way to unify the country and avoid polarization.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extremism - the holding of extreme political or religious views
  • synonym: moderation - the avoidance of excess or extremes, especially in behavior
