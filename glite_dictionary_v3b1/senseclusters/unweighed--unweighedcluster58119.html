unweighed

adjective
not considered
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪd / (BrE)
not considered, evaluated, or taken into account when making a decision or judgment

Examples

Unweighed risks
Several important risks remained unweighed during the planning process, leading to unexpected challenges.
The report was incomplete because it left many factors unweighed and unexamined.
Decisions based on unweighed opinions can result in poor outcomes for the team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: considered - thought about carefully before making a decision
  • synonym: ignored - not noticed or given attention
