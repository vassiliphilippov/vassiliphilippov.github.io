nagami kumquat

noun
a shrub producing oval kumquats
7.7
Pronunciation: / nəˈɡɑːmi ˈkʌmˌkwɑt / (AmE), / nəˈɡɑːmi ˈkʌmkwɒt / (BrE)
A small evergreen shrub native to East Asia that bears oval, orange kumquat fruits, commonly grown for its edible fruit and ornamental value.

Examples

A nagami kumquat shrub

The nagami kumquat is prized both for its tangy fruit and its decorative appeal in gardens.
Unlike other citrus trees, the nagami kumquat tolerates cooler temperatures well.
