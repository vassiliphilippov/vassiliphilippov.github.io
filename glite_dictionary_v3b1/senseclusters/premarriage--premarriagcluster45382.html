premarriage

adjective
before marriage
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈmærɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈmærɪdʒ / (BrE)
relating to the time, activities, or conditions that exist or happen before a marriage

Examples

Premarriage counseling session
They attended a premarriage counseling session to prepare for life together.
The couple discussed important premarriage topics such as finances and family planning.
Many cultures have special premarriage traditions to celebrate the upcoming wedding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postmarriage - relating to the period after marriage
