ineptness

noun
lack of skill or ability
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɛpt.nəs /; / ɪˈnɛptnəs / (AmE), / ɪˈnɛpt.nəs /; / ɪˈnɛptnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being inept; lack of skill, ability, or competence

Examples

His ineptness at work
His ineptness at handling the equipment led to several delays during the project.
She was frustrated by the ineptness of her coworker's attempts to solve the problem.
The team's ineptness was evident in their disorganized approach to the competition.
Because of his ineptness, the simple task turned into a major complication.
Her ineptness at public speaking made the presentation uncomfortable for everyone.
The team's ineptness was obvious when they failed to follow basic instructions.

  • antonym: competence - the ability to do something successfully or efficiently
