confessionally

adverb
relating to confession
Pronunciation: / kənˈfɛʃənəli / (AmE), / kənˈfɛʃənəli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to religious confession, the act of confessing, or the doctrines of a particular church

Examples

Confessionally organized school
The school was organized confessionally, following the teachings of a specific denomination.
Debates in the country are often divided confessionally along religious lines.
The ceremony was conducted confessionally, emphasizing the importance of faith and repentance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: secularly - in a way that is not connected with religion
