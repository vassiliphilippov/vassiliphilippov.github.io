embroidery stitch

noun
a decorative stitch used in embroidery
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɛmˈbroʊdəri stɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪmˈbrəʊdəri stɪtʃ / (BrE)
A single stitch made with thread and a needle as part of a decorative design in embroidery.
