letter

noun
written message sent to someone
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛtər / (AmE), / ˈlɛtə / (BrE)
a written message addressed to a person or organization, typically sent by mail or email

Examples

A letter to a friend
He wrote an angry letter to the company complaining about their service.
Please send your application in the form of a letter addressed to Human Resources.
She received a long letter from her grandmother describing her trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: email - an electronic message sent via the internet
  • degree lower: invitation - a written request to attend an event
  • synonym: note - a brief written message
