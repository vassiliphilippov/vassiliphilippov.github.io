Synonyms

  • henceforward
    7.5 0.07/m

  • hereinafter
    8.9 0.1/m
    hereinafter appears in legal documents to refer to a later part of the text, while hereafter means future time more generally.
  • hereon
    8.1 0.00/m
    hereon means from this point onward after an event or moment, while hereafter covers general future time and document use.
  • thereafter
    4.1 11/m
