hereafter

adverb
from now on; in the future
9.1
Pronunciation: / hɪrˈæftɚ / (AmE), / hɪəˈɑːftə / (BrE)
from this time onward; in the future (including in documents or statements)

Examples

Hereafter in this document
Hereafter, all employees must submit reports by Friday.
She decided to change her behavior hereafter based on what she learned.
The policy will be enforced hereafter to improve efficiency.
All parties to the contract shall be referred to as 'Participants' hereafter.
For the purposes of this agreement, the term 'Company' hereafter refers to XYZ Corporation.
The policies described hereafter are subject to change with prior notice.

  • synonym: henceforth - from this time onwards
  • synonym: hereinafter - in the following part of this document; after this point
