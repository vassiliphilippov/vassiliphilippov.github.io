stola

noun
clerical vestment
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈstoʊ.lə/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊ.lə/ (BrE)
a long, narrow band of cloth worn around the neck by Christian clergy during religious services as a vestment

Examples

Priest's stola
The priest placed the stola over his shoulders before beginning the ceremony.
Each color of the stola represents a different season in the church calendar.
She admired the intricate embroidery on the bishop's stola during the Easter service.
