bison

noun
large wild humped animal
5.2 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪsən / (AmE), / ˈbʌɪsən / (BrE)
A large wild animal of the cattle family with a humped back, shaggy mane, and short horns, native to North America and Europe.
