nerveless

adjective
lacking strength or energy
Pronunciation: / ˈnɜrvləs / (AmE), / ˈnɜːvləs / (BrE)
lacking strength, energy, or firmness; weak or feeble.

Examples

Nerveless hands
After the illness, his nerveless arms could barely lift the tray.
Her nerveless handshake revealed just how exhausted she felt.
The team became nerveless and failed to respond to their coach's encouragement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strong - having physical power or strength
  • synonym: weak - lacking physical strength or energy
