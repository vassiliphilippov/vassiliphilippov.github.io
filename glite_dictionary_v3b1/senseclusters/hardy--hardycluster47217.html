hardy

adjective
strong and able to withstand hardship
3.9 2.73/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ëÀêrdi / (AmE), / Ààh…ëÀêdi / (BrE)
strong and able to survive or endure difficult conditions; tough and resilient

Examples

Hardy plants survive
Only hardy plants can thrive in such a harsh climate.
