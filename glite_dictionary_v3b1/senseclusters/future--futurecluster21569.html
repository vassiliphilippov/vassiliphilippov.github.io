future

noun
contract for future delivery (finance)
7.9 5.89/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfj ät É…ö / (AmE), / ÀàfjuÀêt É…ô / (BrE)
a contract for the sale or purchase of a commodity or financial instrument at a predetermined price for delivery at a specified time in the future.

Examples

Oil futures rose sharply in response to the unrest in the Middle East.
The Chicago Board of Trade specializes in commodity futures markets.
