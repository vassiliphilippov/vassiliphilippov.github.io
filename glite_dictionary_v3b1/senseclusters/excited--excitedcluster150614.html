excited

adjective
having increased energy (chemistry, physics)
2.2 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪk.ˈsaɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsaɪ.təd/ (AmE), /ɪk.ˈsaɪ.tɪd/ /; / /ɪk.ˈsaɪ.təd/ (BrE)
(of a molecule or atom) being in a state of increased energy and reactivity, typically after absorbing energy.

Examples

Excited electrons
In the experiment, scientists observed the behavior of excited electrons.
The molecule became excited after absorbing a photon.
When the atom is in an excited state, it can release energy as light.
