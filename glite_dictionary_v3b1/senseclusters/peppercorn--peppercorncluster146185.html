peppercorn

noun
dried berry used as spice
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərˌkɔrn / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəkɔːn / (BrE)
the dried berry of the pepper plant, used whole or ground as a pungent seasoning

Examples

A peppercorn spice
She added a few peppercorns to the stew for extra flavor.
The recipe called for freshly ground peppercorn to season the steak.
Whole peppercorns are often used in pickling recipes to add a spicy kick.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pepper - the spice made from ground peppercorns or the plant itself
