dime

noun
packet of drugs sold for $10 (slang)
Pronunciation: / daɪm / (AmE), / daɪm / (BrE)
(slang) A packet of illegal drugs sold for ten dollars.

Examples

A dime bag
He was caught with a dime of marijuana in his backpack.
On the street, a dime usually refers to a ten-dollar bag of drugs.
The dealer offered me a dime behind the convenience store.
