vis-à-vis

preposition
in relation to; regarding
Pronunciation: / ccvid0z59c8vid0 / (AmE), / ccvid0z59c8vid0 / (BrE)
(formal) in relation to; with regard to; compared with.

Examples

Vis-à-vis the problem
The committee discussed the budget vis-à-vis the projected decrease in tax revenue next year.
Her comments were helpful vis-à-vis implementing the new company-wide performance metrics.
We need clear guidelines vis-à-vis employee safety when opening stores after the lockdown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: regarding - used to introduce the subject that is being talked about; concerning.
  • synonym: with respect to - in connection with; concerning a particular subject.
