gatekeeping

adjective
controlling access
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋ / (BrE)
serving to control or limit access to or participation in something, often by excluding or restricting others

Examples

Gatekeeping policy
The gatekeeping policies at the club made it difficult for new members to join.
Some organizations have a gatekeeping attitude that discourages fresh ideas.
Critics argue that gatekeeping practices can prevent talented people from advancing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inclusive - allowing everyone to participate or be included
  • synonym: restrictive - limiting or controlling what is allowed
