anticlericalism

noun
opposition to clergy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəˌlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəlɪzəm / (BrE)
opposition to the influence or involvement of the clergy or religious leaders, especially in public or political affairs
