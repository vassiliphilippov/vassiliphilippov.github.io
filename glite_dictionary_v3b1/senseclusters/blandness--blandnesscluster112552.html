blandness

noun
the quality of being bland or ingratiating
9.0 3.4
Pronunciation: /ˈblænd.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈblænd.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality of being pleasantly smooth, unremarkable, or ingratiating in manner or appearance

Examples

Social blandness
Critics accused the presenter of blandness, saying his remarks lacked originality or passion.
His blandness made him likable at first, but over time, people found him insincere.
The diplomat's blandness allowed her to ease tensions during the heated negotiations.
