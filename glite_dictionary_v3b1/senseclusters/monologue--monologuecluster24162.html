monologue

noun
long dramatic speech by one person
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnəˌlɔɡ /; / ˈmɑː.nə.lɔːɡ /; / ˈmɑːnəˌlɔg / (AmE), / ˈmɒnəlɒɡ /; / ˈmɒn.ə.lɒɡ /; / ˈmɒnəlɒg / (BrE)
a long speech by one person, especially in a play, film, or conversation
