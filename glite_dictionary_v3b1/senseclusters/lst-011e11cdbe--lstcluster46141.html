LST

sym
astronomical time system
Pronunciation: / ɛl ɛs ti / (AmE), / ɛl ɛs ti / (BrE)
abbreviation for Local Sidereal Time, a timekeeping system used by astronomers to track the Earth's rotation relative to the stars

Examples

Astronomers use LST
The observatory calculated LST to determine the best time for viewing distant galaxies.
Before starting the telescope, the astronomer checked the current LST on the control panel.
Accurate LST readings are essential for aligning telescopes with celestial objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sidereal time - timekeeping based on Earth's rotation relative to distant stars
  • antonym: solar time - timekeeping based on the position of the sun
