tartare

noun
raw seasoned dish
6.7
Pronunciation: / tɑrˈtɑr / (AmE), / tɑːˈtɑː / (BrE)
a dish made of finely chopped or ground raw meat or fish, usually served with seasonings and condiments

Examples

Tartare sauce
She decided to try the salmon tartare for the first time at the new restaurant.
The chef prepared a classic beef tartare with capers and onions.
Many people enjoy tartare as a delicacy, especially when made with fresh ingredients.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carpaccio - thinly sliced raw meat or fish served as an appetizer
  • synonym: sashimi - thinly sliced raw fish served without rice
