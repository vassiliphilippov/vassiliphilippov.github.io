tartare

noun
raw seasoned dish
6.7
Pronunciation: /tɑːr.ˈtɑːr/ (AmE), /tɑː.ˈtɑː/ (BrE)
a dish made of finely chopped or ground raw meat or fish, usually served with seasonings and condiments

Examples

Tartare sauce

The chef prepared a classic beef tartare with capers and onions.
Many people enjoy tartare as a delicacy, especially when made with fresh ingredients.
