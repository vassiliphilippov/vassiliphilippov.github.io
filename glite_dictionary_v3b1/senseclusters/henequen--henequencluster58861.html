henequen

noun
plant fiber for rope
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛnəˈkɛn / (AmE), / ˌhɛnɪˈkɛn / (BrE)
a strong natural fiber from the leaves of the henequen plant, used to make rope, twine, or mats

Examples

Henequen fiber rope
The artisan braided the coarse henequen into sturdy mats sold at the market.
Farmers in Yucatán harvest henequen to produce strong twine and rope.
Traditional hats made from henequen are popular souvenirs in some regions of Mexico.
