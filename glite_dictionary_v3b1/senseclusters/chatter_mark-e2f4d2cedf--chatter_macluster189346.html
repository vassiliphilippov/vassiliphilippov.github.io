chatter mark

noun
glacial marks on rock surfaces
5.4 9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃætɚ mɑːrk /; / ˈtʃæt̬ɚ mɑrk / (AmE), / ˈtʃætə mɑːk / (BrE)
a mark, groove, or indentation left on a surface by a vibrating or repetitive action, such as by a glacier on rock or a cutting tool on metal
