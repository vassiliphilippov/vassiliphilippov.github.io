(of a projectile or object) to bounce off a surface after striking it, especially at an angle.

Examples

To ricochet off walls
He threw a stone, and it ricocheted across the surface of the lake.
Shrapnel ricocheted around the room after the explosion.
The bullet ricocheted off the metal door and hit the wall.
