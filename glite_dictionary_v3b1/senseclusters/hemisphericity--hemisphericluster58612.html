hemisphericity

noun
association with a hemisphere
Pronunciation: / ˌhɛmɪsfəˈrɪsəti / (AmE), / ˌhɛmɪsfəˈrɪsəti / (BrE)
the quality or state of being related to or limited to one hemisphere, especially in political, cultural, or climatic contexts

Examples

Hemisphericity of politics
The conference explored the hemisphericity of climate change impacts in the Southern Hemisphere.
Debates about hemisphericity often arise in discussions of international trade agreements.
Researchers analyzed the hemisphericity of cultural trends between North and South America.
