puppet ruler

noun
ruler with no real power; controlled by others
7.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp åp…™t ÀàruÀêl…ôr / (AmE), / Ààp åp…™t ÀàruÀêl…ô / (BrE)
a ruler who holds office in name only, with real power exercised by another person or group, often placed in power by a foreign nation or despot

Examples

A puppet ruler
After the coup, the general installed a puppet ruler to legitimize his regime.
Historians often debate whether the emperor was truly independent or merely a puppet ruler.
The new president was seen by many as a puppet ruler controlled by foreign powers.

Synonyms
