degunkification project

noun
project to clean or declutter
9.1
Pronunciation: / diˌɡʌŋkɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˌprɑːdʒɛkt / (AmE), / diˌɡʌŋkɪfɪˈkeɪʃən ˌprɒdʒɛkt / (BrE)
an informal project or initiative focused on removing accumulated dirt, grime, or clutter from a space or object

Examples

A degunkification project
As part of spring cleaning, we launched a degunkification project for the entire garage.
Her degunkification project transformed the cluttered workspace into an organized haven.
The team began a degunkification project in the communal kitchen after months of buildup.

  • synonym: decluttering - the act of removing unnecessary items from a space
