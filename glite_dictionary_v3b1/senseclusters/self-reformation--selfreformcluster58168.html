self-reformation

noun
improvement of oneself
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛlf rɪˌfɔːrˈmeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɛlf rɪˌfɔːˈmeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making significant improvements to one's own character or behavior

Examples

Self-reformation process
After a period of reflection, she committed herself to self-reformation and personal growth.
The novel explores the protagonist’s journey of self-reformation after making poor choices in his youth.
Therapists often encourage self-reformation as a means of overcoming negative habits.

Related Terms

  • antonym: self-destruction - the act of harming or destroying oneself
  • synonym: self-improvement - the process of making yourself better in some way
