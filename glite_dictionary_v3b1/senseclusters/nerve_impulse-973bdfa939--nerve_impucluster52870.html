Examples

A nerve impulse signal
A nerve impulse is generated when a neuron is stimulated by a chemical or physical signal.
Damage to the nerve fiber may disrupt the nerve impulse and cause loss of sensation.
The speed at which a nerve impulse travels can affect reaction times.

Synonyms
