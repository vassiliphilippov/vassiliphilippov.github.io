black eye

noun
embarrassing or damaging setback
4.0
Pronunciation: / blæk aɪ / (AmE), / blæk aɪ /; / blak aɪ / (BrE)
an event or situation that causes embarrassment or damage to someone's reputation

Examples

A black eye for reputation
Losing such an important client was a black eye for the sales team.
The athlete's suspension was a black eye for the entire sport.
The scandal has given the company a serious black eye in the media.
After the incident, the whole team suffered a black eye in the press.
The restaurant's health violation was a major black eye for its reputation.
The scandal gave the company a black eye that took years to overcome.
