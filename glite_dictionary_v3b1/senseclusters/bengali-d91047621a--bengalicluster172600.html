Bengali

noun
the main language of Bangladesh and West Bengal
1.0
Pronunciation: / bɛnˈɡɑli / (AmE), / bɛnˈɡɑːli / (BrE)
the Indo-Aryan language spoken by the Bengali people, mainly in Bangladesh and the Indian state of West Bengal
