fish cake

noun
fried patty of fish and potatoes
Pronunciation: / fɪʃ keɪk / (AmE), / fɪʃ keɪk / (BrE)
A small, usually fried patty made from flaked fish mixed with mashed potatoes or other ingredients.

Examples

Homemade fish cakes
My grandmother makes the best homemade fish cakes using cod and fresh herbs.
She ordered a plate of fish cakes with a side salad for lunch.
The recipe calls for shaping the mixture into fish cakes and frying them until golden brown.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fish ball - a ball-shaped mixture of minced fish and other ingredients, often boiled or deep-fried
