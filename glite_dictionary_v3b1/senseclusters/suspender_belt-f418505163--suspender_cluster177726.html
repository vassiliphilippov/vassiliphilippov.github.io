suspender belt

noun
garment for holding up stockings
Pronunciation: / səˈspɛndər bɛlt / (AmE), / səˈspɛndə bɛlt / (BrE)
a wide band of elastic material worn around the waist with attached straps to hold up stockings, typically worn by women

Examples

A suspender belt strap
She wore a black suspender belt under her dress for the party.
The shop sells a wide variety of suspender belts in different colors.
Vintage fashion often features stockings supported by a suspender belt.
