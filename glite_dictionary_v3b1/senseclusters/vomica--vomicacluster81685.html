vomica

noun
pus-filled cavity
Pronunciation: / ˈvɑːmɪkə / (AmE), / ˈvɒmɪkə / (BrE)
a cavity in the body, especially in the lungs, that is filled with pus or other fluid

Examples

A vomica in lung
The X-ray revealed a vomica in the upper lobe of the patient's lung.
Treatment focused on draining the vomica to prevent further infection.
A large vomica can cause severe respiratory symptoms if left untreated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abscess - a swollen area in body tissue filled with pus
  • synonym: cyst - a closed sac in the body filled with fluid or other material
