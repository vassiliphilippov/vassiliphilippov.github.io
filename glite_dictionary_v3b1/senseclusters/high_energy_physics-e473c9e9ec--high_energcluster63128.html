high energy physics

noun
field studying subatomic particles and forces
2.8 5.3
Pronunciation: /haɪ ˈen.ər.dʒiː ˈfɪz.ɪks/ /; / /haɪ ˈen.ər.dʒɪ ˈfɪz.ɪks/ (AmE), /haɪ ˈen.ə.dʒɪ ˈfɪz.ɪks/ (BrE)
