bedsitter

noun
small furnished sitting and bedroom
7.0 5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛdˌsɪtər / (AmE), / ˈbɛdsɪtə / (BrE)
a small furnished room that serves as both a sitting room and a bedroom, often with some basic kitchen or bathroom facilities

Examples

A bedsitter room
After the divorce, she moved into a small bedsitter in north London.
His bedsitter was cramped but cozy, with just enough space for a bed and a tiny kitchenette.
Many students in the city rent bedsitters near their universities due to the lower cost.
