performability

noun
suitability for performance
8.4
Pronunciation: /pər.fɔːr.mə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /pə.fɔː.mə.ˈbɪl.ə.tiː/ (BrE)
the quality of a musical piece, play, or other artistic work that makes it practical or suitable to be performed

Examples

Play's performability
The performability of the new play was praised by both actors and directors.
Composers often consider performability when writing music for school bands.
The arrangement improved the performability of the piece for amateur musicians.

Related Terms

  • synonym: perform - to present a work of art to an audience
  • synonym: playability - the ease with which something can be played or performed
  • antonym: unperformable - not able to be performed
