tincture

noun
coloring or dyeing substance
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪŋktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈtɪŋktʃə / (BrE)
a substance used for coloring or dyeing something; a coloring agent.

Examples

Red tincture dye
A red tincture was added to the solution, giving it a bright hue.
Ancient manuscripts often mention a tincture derived from plants for dyeing fabrics.
The artist mixed a tincture into the paint to achieve a richer shade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pigment - a substance that gives color to something
