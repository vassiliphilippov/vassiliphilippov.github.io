racial grievance industry

noun
groups exploiting racial issues for gain
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪʃəl ˈɡrivəns ˈɪndəstri / (AmE), / ˈreɪʃəl ˈɡriːvəns ˈɪndəstri / (BrE)
a term, often used pejoratively, for groups or individuals who are seen as exploiting racial issues for profit, personal influence, or attention, thereby shaping public debate and sometimes perpetuating social division

Examples

The racial grievance industry debate
Debates about the role of the racial grievance industry are common in political commentary.
He claimed that the racial grievance industry benefits from ongoing social tensions.
Some critics argue that the racial grievance industry stifles genuine discussion about important issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: identity politics - political activity or movements based on particular social identities
  • synonym: social justice warrior - a person who promotes socially progressive views, sometimes used disparagingly
