metrically

adverb
in relation to meter (poetry/music)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtrɪkəli / (AmE), / ˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to or is measured by meter, especially in poetry or music.

Examples

To analyze metrically
He analyzed the sonnet metrically to discover its formal structure.
The composer preferred pieces that were metrically complex and challenging.
The two stanzas are metrically identical, following the same rhythmic pattern.
