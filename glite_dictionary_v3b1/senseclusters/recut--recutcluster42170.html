recut

noun
new edited version
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.kʌt/ /; / /ˈrɪ.kʌt/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.kʌt/ /; / /ˈriː.kʌt/ (BrE)
a new version of a film, song, or other work that has been edited or cut again, often with changes from the original.

Examples

A director's recut
The director's recut of the movie included several scenes that were not in the theatrical release.
Fans were excited to hear the recut of the classic album with improved sound quality.
After negative reviews, the studio released a recut to address the audience's feedback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: edit - a version of a work that has been changed or revised
  • antonym: original - the first or initial version of a work
  • synonym: remix - a new version of a song created by altering the original recording
