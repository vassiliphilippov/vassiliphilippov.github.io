rada

noun
Eastern European council
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdə / (BrE)
a legislative assembly or council, especially in some Eastern European countries such as Ukraine

Examples

The rada assembly
The Ukrainian rada passed several new laws last year.
Delegates gathered in the rada to discuss the proposed reforms.
The country's constitution was amended by a vote in the national rada.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congress - a formal meeting or assembly for discussion and decision-making
  • synonym: parliament - the highest legislative body in a country
