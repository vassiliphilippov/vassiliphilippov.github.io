bucket

noun
cloud storage container
4.2
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌk.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈbʌk.ɪt/ (BrE)
(in cloud computing) a named logical container used to store and organise objects (files) in a cloud storage service.

Examples

Cloud storage bucket
She uploaded the final report to the project's bucket on the cloud storage service.
The devops engineer set lifecycle rules to delete old objects from the bucket automatically.
Multiple teams shared access to the company's logs buckets for easier incident investigation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cloud storage - online services for storing and accessing data over the internet
  • synonym: container - a receptacle or logical space used to hold or organise items, physical or digital
