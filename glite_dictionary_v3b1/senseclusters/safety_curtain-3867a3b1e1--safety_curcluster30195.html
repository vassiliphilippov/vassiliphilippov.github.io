safety curtain

noun
fireproof theater curtain for safety
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪfti ˌkɜrtən / (AmE), / ˈseɪfti ˌkɜːtən / (BrE)
A heavy, fireproof curtain in a theater, designed to be lowered between the stage and the audience to prevent the spread of fire.

Examples

The safety curtain lowers
During the renovation, they replaced the old safety curtain with a newer, more advanced model.
If a fire breaks out backstage, the safety curtain can quickly separate the stage from the auditorium.
The theater manager explained that the safety curtain is lowered during intermissions for the audience's protection.
