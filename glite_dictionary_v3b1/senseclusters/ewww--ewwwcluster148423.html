ewww

interjection
disgust exclamation
Pronunciation: / juː / (AmE), / juː / (BrE)
an informal exclamation used to express disgust, distaste, or strong dislike

Examples

Ewww gross food
When she saw the moldy bread, she said, "ewww, that's so gross!"
He touched something sticky under the table and immediately exclaimed, "ewww!"
The kids shouted "ewww!" when they found a worm in the apple.
When she found a bug in her salad, she shouted ewww and pushed the plate away.
He wrinkled his nose and said ewww after smelling the garbage.
The children all yelled ewww when they saw the slimy worm on the sidewalk.
