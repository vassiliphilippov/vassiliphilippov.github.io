popularization

noun
act of making something widely accepted
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑpjələrəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌpɑːpjələrəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌpɑː.pjə.lɚ.əˈzeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpɒpjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌpɒp.jʊ.lə.raɪˈzeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act of making something widely known, accepted, or easy to understand for the general public
