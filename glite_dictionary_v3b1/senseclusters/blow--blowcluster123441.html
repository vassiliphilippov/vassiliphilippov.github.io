blow

noun
a strong hit or strike
Pronunciation: / bloʊ / (AmE), / bləʊ / (BrE)
a strong hit or impact with a fist, weapon, or other object

Examples

A hard blow
A sudden blow knocked the book out of his hand.
He received a heavy blow to the shoulder during the match.
The vase broke after a sharp blow from the broom handle.
The boxer delivered a powerful blow to his opponent's jaw.
The sudden blow to his head left him unconscious for a few seconds.
The vase shattered on the floor with a loud blow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hit - an act of striking something or someone
  • synonym: strike - a hit made with a hand, weapon, or tool
  • degree lower: stroke - a gentle or light impact
  • degree lower: tap - a light and gentle touch or blow
