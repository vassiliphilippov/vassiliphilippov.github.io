undutifulness

noun
lack of devotion to duty or obligations
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈduːtəfʊlnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈdjuːtəfʊlnəs / (BrE)
a lack of devotion or respect toward one's duties or obligations, especially toward authority or parents

Examples

Undutifulness toward parents
His constant undutifulness toward his parents disappointed the entire family.
In traditional societies, undutifulness to elders was considered a serious offense.
The undutifulness of some employees was noticed by management during the crisis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dutifulness - showing devotion or respect toward one's duties or authority
