ketogenic

adjective
producing ketone bodies
6.8
Pronunciation: /kiː.tə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /kiː.toʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (AmE), /kiː.təʊ.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ /; / /kiː.tə.ˈdʒen.ɪk/ (BrE)
relating to or causing the production of ketone bodies in the body, especially through diet or metabolism

Examples

A ketogenic diet
A ketogenic diet is often used to help control certain types of epilepsy.
Some amino acids are classified as ketogenic because they are broken down into ketone bodies.
The ketogenic pathway becomes more active during prolonged fasting.
Many people follow a ketogenic diet to help manage their weight.
A ketogenic meal plan typically includes plenty of healthy fats and very few carbohydrates.
Doctors sometimes recommend a ketogenic approach for certain medical conditions like epilepsy.
