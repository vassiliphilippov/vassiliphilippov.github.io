arteriogram

noun
X-ray image of an artery with contrast medium
7.0
Pronunciation: / ɑrˈtɪriəˌɡræm / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪəriəˌɡræm / (BrE)
an X-ray image of an artery after injection of a contrast medium to make the artery visible

Examples

An arteriogram image
An arteriogram revealed a blockage in the femoral artery that required immediate attention.
Before surgery, the surgeon reviewed the patient's arteriogram to plan the procedure.
The doctor ordered an arteriogram to assess the patient's blood flow to the heart.

Related Terms

  • synonym: angiogram - an X-ray image of blood vessels using contrast medium
