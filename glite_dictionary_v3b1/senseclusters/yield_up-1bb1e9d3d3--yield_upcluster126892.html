yield up

verb
surrender or give up under pressure
7.4 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / jiÀêld  åp / (AmE), / jiÀêld  åp / (BrE)
to surrender or give up something, especially under pressure or by force

Examples

To yield up secrets
He refused to yield up any secrets despite the threats.
The ancient tomb finally yielded up its treasures to the archaeologists.
The defeated army was forced to yield up its weapons to the victors.
