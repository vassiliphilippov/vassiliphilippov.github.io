Spartacus

proper noun
historical male name
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑːrtəkəs / (AmE), / ˈspɑːtəkəs / (BrE)
a masculine given name, especially known as the name of a famous leader of a slave revolt in ancient Rome

Examples

The name Spartacus
The story of Spartacus has inspired countless books and movies about freedom and resistance.
They named their son Spartacus to honor the courage of the ancient rebel.
In history class, we learned how Spartacus led a revolt against the Roman Empire.
