Examples

Accidental asphyxiation
Improper use of certain chemicals can lead to accidental asphyxiation.

Synonyms

  • asphyxia
    8.6 0.21/m
    asphyxia is the medical term often used in reports and exams, while asphyxiation names the process of losing oxygen.
  • suffocation
    5.2 0.83/m
    suffocation is the everyday word for being unable to breathe, while asphyxiation is a more formal medical name for that oxygen loss.

Related Terms
