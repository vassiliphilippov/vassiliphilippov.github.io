    A clinical condition of oxygen deprivation often caused by choking or drowning
    vs asphyxiation: A condition caused by a lack of oxygen resulting in unconsciousness or death; commonly due to choking, drowning, or other means that prevent normal breathing.
  • suffocation
    5.2 1.0/m
    The state of being unable to breathe because of oxygen loss
    vs asphyxiation: the state or act of being unable to breathe because of lack of oxygen, which can cause serious harm or death
