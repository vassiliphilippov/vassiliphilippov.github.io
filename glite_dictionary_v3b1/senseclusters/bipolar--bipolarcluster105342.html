bipolar

adjective
occurring in both polar regions
Pronunciation: / baɪˈpoʊ.lɚ / (AmE), / baɪˈpəʊ.lə / (BrE)
Relating to, or occurring in, both the Arctic and Antarctic polar regions.

Examples

Bipolar regions
