Synonyms

  • Quechua
    9.6 0.08/m
    Quechua is the common English name for the Indigenous Andean people of the Inca ruling class, while Kechua is a variant spelling.
  • Quichua
    9.9 0.13/m
    Quichua names the Andean people and their language, mainly in Ecuador and parts of Peru, while Kechua refers specifically to the Peruvian Inca group.
