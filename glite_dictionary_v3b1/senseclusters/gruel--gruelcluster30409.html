gruel

noun
thin porridge
7.4 6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːəl / (AmE), / ˈɡruːəl / (BrE)
a thin liquid food made by boiling ground cereal such as oatmeal or cornmeal in water or milk

Examples

A bowl of gruel

In the old novel, gruel is often mentioned as a meager meal for the poor.
The orphans were given only a bowl of gruel for dinner each night.
