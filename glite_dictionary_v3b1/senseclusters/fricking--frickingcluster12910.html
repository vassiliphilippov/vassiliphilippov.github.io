fricking

adverb
slang intensifier
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪkɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈfrɪkɪŋ / (BrE)

Examples

So fricking tired
I am fricking hungry after that long hike.
That was a fricking amazing concert last night.
He was fricking annoyed when he missed the bus again.
