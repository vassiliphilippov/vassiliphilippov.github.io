Synonyms

  • mechanical device
    3.2 0.3/m
    mechanical device is a whole machine or tool used to do a job, while mechanism is a moving-part assembly that makes it work.
  • mechanical system
    4.3 0.3/m
    mechanical system is a set of connected parts that work together across a machine, while mechanism is a smaller assembly of moving parts.
  • workings
    3.9 0.01/m
    workings are the internal parts or processes you see inside a machine, while mechanism names the moving parts that perform a function.
