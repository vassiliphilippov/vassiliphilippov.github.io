mechanism

noun
device with moving parts
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛk.əˌnɪz.əm / (AmE), / ˈmɛk.ə.nɪz.əm / (BrE)
A piece of machinery made up of moving parts that work together to perform a specific function.

Examples

Safety mechanism
Engineers designed a safety mechanism to prevent accidental shutdowns.
The clock's mechanism is made up of dozens of tiny gears and springs.
The locking mechanism on my suitcase broke during the trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: machine - a device using mechanical power with fixed and moving parts
