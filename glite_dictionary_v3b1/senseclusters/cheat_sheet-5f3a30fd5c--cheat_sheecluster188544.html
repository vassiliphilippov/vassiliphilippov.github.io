cheat sheet

noun
concise reference notes
4.8
Pronunciation: / tʃit ʃit / (AmE), / tʃiːt ʃiːt / (BrE)
a summary of important information, often used for quick reference or as an aid in exams

Examples

A cheat sheet for exams
Before the exam, Mark quickly glanced at his cheat sheet one more time.
She wrote formulas on a cheat sheet to help her during the math test.
The programming cheat sheet listed all the useful keyboard shortcuts.
