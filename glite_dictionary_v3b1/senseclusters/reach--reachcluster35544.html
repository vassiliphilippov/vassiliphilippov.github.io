reach

noun
the act of extending one's arm or body
Pronunciation: / ritʃ / (AmE), / riːtʃ / (BrE)
the act of stretching out an arm or other part of the body to touch or grasp something

Examples

A long reach
His long arms give him an impressive reach in basketball games.
The remote was just out of my reach, so I stood up to get it.
With a quick reach, she grabbed the book from the top shelf.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stretch - the act of extending or stretching a body part
