ovigerous

adjective
bearing eggs
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvɪdʒərəs / (AmE), / əʊˈvɪdʒərəs / (BrE)
carrying or bearing eggs, especially used to describe animals such as crustaceans or insects

Examples

Ovigerous female shrimp
The scientist identified several ovigerous females among the shrimp population.
During the breeding season, ovigerous crabs are often found hiding under rocks.
Researchers studied the behavior of ovigerous insects in their natural habitat.
