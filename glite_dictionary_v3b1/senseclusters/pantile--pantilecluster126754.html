pantile

noun
S-shaped roofing tile
8.2 7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpæn.taɪl/ (AmE), /ˈpæn.taɪl/ (BrE)
A roofing tile with an S-shaped profile, designed so that adjacent tiles interlock by overlapping curves.

Examples

Terracotta pantiles
Terracotta pantiles are often seen in Mediterranean architecture.
The old cottage was roofed with traditional pantiles, giving it a distinctive look.
Workers replaced the broken pantile to prevent rain from leaking in.
