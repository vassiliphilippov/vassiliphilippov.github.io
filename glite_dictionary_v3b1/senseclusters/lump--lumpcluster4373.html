lump

noun
a large shapeless piece
Pronunciation: / lʌmp / (AmE), / lʌmp / (BrE)
a piece or mass of a substance that does not have a regular shape

Examples

A lump of sugar
She dropped a lump of sugar into her tea.
The miner found a lump of gold in the riverbed.
We bought a big lump of cheese for the picnic.
Add a lump of sugar to your tea, if you like it sweet.
He found a lump of clay on the playground.
There was a mysterious lump in her mashed potatoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blob - a drop or small lump of a thick liquid or viscous substance
  • synonym: chunk - a thick, solid piece cut or broken off something; synonym of 'lump' in this sense
  • synonym: clump - a compact group or mass, especially of something soft or wet
  • synonym: mass - a body of matter with no definite shape
