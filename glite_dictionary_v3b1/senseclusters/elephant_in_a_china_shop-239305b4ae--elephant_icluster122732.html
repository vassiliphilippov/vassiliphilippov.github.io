elephant in a china shop

noun
clumsy or insensitive person in delicate situations
8.5 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɛlɪfənt ɪn ə ˈtʃaɪnə ʃɑp / (AmE), / ˈɛlɪfənt ɪn ə ˈtʃaɪnə ʃɒp / (BrE)
