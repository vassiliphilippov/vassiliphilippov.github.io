western gray squirrel

noun
large gray squirrel native to western U.S.
3.3 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛstɚn ɡreɪ ˈskwɝːrəl /; / ˈwɛstɚn ɡreɪ ˈskwɝəl / (AmE), / ˈwɛstən ɡreɪ ˈskwɪrəl / (BrE)
