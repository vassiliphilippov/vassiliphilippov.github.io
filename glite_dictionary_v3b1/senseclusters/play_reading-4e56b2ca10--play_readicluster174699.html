play reading

noun
reading aloud of a play by a group
3.5
Pronunciation: / pleɪ ˈriːdɪŋ / (AmE), / pleɪ ˈriːdɪŋ / (BrE)
a performance in which a group reads a play aloud, usually without full staging or memorization

Examples

A play reading session
A play reading allows actors to focus on language and character without the pressure of full staging.
She joined a local theatre group that organizes monthly play readings of new scripts.
The community center is hosting a play reading of Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' this Friday evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: staged reading - a dramatized reading of a play with minimal movement and props
