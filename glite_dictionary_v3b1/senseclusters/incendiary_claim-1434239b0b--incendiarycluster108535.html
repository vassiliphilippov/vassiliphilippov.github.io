incendiary claim

noun
provocative or inflammatory statement
7.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri kleɪm / (AmE), / ɪnˈsɛndiəri kleɪm / (BrE)
a statement intended to provoke strong emotional reactions, anger, or controversy

Examples

Incendiary claim about corruption
Her incendiary claim about government corruption led to a heated debate.
Social media fueled the spread of incendiary claims during the election campaign.
The article made several incendiary claims that sparked public outrage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: allegation - a claim or assertion, often without proof
  • synonym: provocative statement - a statement intended to elicit a strong reaction
