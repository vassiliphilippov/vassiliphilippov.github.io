total hysterectomy

noun
removal of the uterus and cervix
4.7 4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊ.t̬əl hɪˌstɛr.əˈktə.mi / (AmE), / ˈtəʊ.təl hɪˌstɛr.əˈktə.mi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which the uterus and cervix are completely removed

Examples
