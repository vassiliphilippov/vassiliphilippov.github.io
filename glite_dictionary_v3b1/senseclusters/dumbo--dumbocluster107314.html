dumbo

noun
deep-sea octopus
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌmboʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʌmbəʊ / (BrE)
a small deep-sea octopus of the family Grimpoteuthidae, known for its ear-like fins above the eyes

Examples

A deep-sea dumbo
The marine biologist was thrilled to spot a dumbo gliding gracefully through the deep ocean.
Researchers filmed a dumbo using its ear-like fins to swim above the seafloor.
A rare dumbo was discovered during the deep-sea expedition, fascinating the entire crew.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Grimpoteuthis - a genus of deep-sea octopuses with ear-like fins
  • synonym: octopus - a soft-bodied, eight-armed sea animal
