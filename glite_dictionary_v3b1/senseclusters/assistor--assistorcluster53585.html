assistor

noun
player making assists
Pronunciation: / əˈsɪstər / (AmE), / əˈsɪstə / (BrE)
a player who makes the pass or action that directly leads to a teammate scoring, especially in sports

Examples

Top assistor in soccer
The team's top assistor delivered another perfect pass to set up the winning goal.
As an assistor, she always looks for opportunities to help her teammates score.
Coaches value a reliable assistor who can read the game and create chances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playmaker - a player who creates scoring opportunities for teammates
  • antonym: scorer - a player who scores points or goals
