Synonyms

  • smoke
    1.0 47/m
    smoke is the general act of inhaling and exhaling burning vapour, while puff often means a short, light inhalation and exhale.
  • draw
    4.1 4.67/m
    draw means to pull air or smoke into the mouth or lungs, while puff implies both inhaling and exhaling the smoke.
  • drag
    8.1 0.70/m
    drag means inhaling smoke often in a strong pull from a cigarette, while puff is usually a softer, smaller breath out.
  • toke
    9.5 0.20/m
    toke means taking smoke from a joint or pipe in informal settings, while puff is a general word used for any smoke or vapor.
  • take a hit
    8.9 0.21/m
    take a hit means inhaling smoke or vapor from a cigarette, joint, or device, while puff usually means a small, quick breath out.
  • whiff
    7.8 0.00/m
    whiff means a quick strong exhale or a brief smell, while puff implies a deliberate inhale then a soft exhale of smoke.
