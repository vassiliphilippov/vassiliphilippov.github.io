polywater

noun

a supposed form of water with unusual physical properties, once believed to exist but later disproven

Examples

The polywater theory
In the 1960s, researchers claimed to have discovered polywater with properties different from ordinary water.
The scientific community eventually determined that polywater was the result of contamination, not a new substance.
Many articles were published about polywater before its existence was disproven.
