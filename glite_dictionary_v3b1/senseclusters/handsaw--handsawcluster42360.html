handsaw

noun
saw used with one hand
Pronunciation: / ˈhændˌsɔ / (AmE), / ˈhændˌsɔː / (BrE)
a saw operated with one hand, used for cutting wood

Examples

A handsaw tool
He used a handsaw to cut the wooden plank to the required length.
It's easier to make precise cuts with a handsaw than with a power saw for some tasks.
The old handsaw in the garage still works perfectly for small projects.
