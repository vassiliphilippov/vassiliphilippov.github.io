interdependence

noun
mutual reliance between entities
2.9 4.2
Pronunciation: /ɪn.tər.dɪ.ˈpen.dəns/ /; / /ɪn.tər.də.ˈpen.dəns/ (AmE), /ɪn.tə.dɪ.ˈpen.dəns/ /; / /ɪn.tə.də.ˈpen.dəns/ (BrE)
The condition of two or more people or things relying on or being affected by each other.

Examples

Strong interdependence among species
Strong ecological interdependence exists among species in the rainforest.
The interdependence of countries in the global economy has grown significantly over the years.
Their friendship was built on trust and interdependence rather than competition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dependence - reliance on someone or something
  • antonym: independence - freedom from reliance on others
