inviting

adjective
attractive and tempting
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪ.tɪŋ / (BrE)
attractive in a way that tempts or encourages someone to approach, take part, or accept

Examples

An inviting smile
She smiled with an inviting look that encouraged him to join the conversation.
The bakery's display of fresh pastries was truly inviting.
The warm light and comfortable chairs made the room look especially inviting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tempting - having an appeal that makes someone want to do something
  • antonym: uninviting - not attractive or tempting
