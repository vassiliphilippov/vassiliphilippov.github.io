egomaniacal

adjective
obsessed with oneself
Pronunciation: / ˌiːɡoʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (AmE), / ˌiːɡəʊməˈnaɪəkəl / (BrE)
having an extreme and unhealthy obsession with oneself and one's own importance

Examples

Egomaniacal behavior
His egomaniacal behavior alienated many of his friends and colleagues.
The movie's villain is portrayed as an egomaniacal leader who cares only about his own power.
She was accused of being egomaniacal after constantly talking about her achievements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: humble - having a modest view of one's own importance
  • synonym: narcissistic - having an excessive interest in oneself
  • synonym: self-centered - focused only on oneself and one's own needs
