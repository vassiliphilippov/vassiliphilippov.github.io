carved

adjective
shaped or decorated by carving

Pronunciation: / k…ërvd / (AmE), / k…ëÀêvd / (BrE)
shaped, decorated, or made by cutting into the surface of something; formed by carving

Examples

A carved pattern
Our front door features a beautifully carved pattern.
The ancient temple is supported by pillars carved with mythical figures.
The museum displayed several carved masks from Africa.

  • incised

  • engraved

  • etched
    5.1 0.01/m
