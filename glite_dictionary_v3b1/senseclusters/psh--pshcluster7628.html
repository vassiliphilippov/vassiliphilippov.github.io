psh

interjection
attention-getting sound
Pronunciation: / pʃ / (AmE), / pʃ / (BrE)
used to make a brief, quiet sound to attract someone's attention or signal them discreetly

Examples

Quiet psh for attention
He gave a quick psh to let his friend know it was time to leave.
With a soft psh, she signaled the cat to come closer.
The teacher used a quiet psh to get the students' attention without interrupting the class.
