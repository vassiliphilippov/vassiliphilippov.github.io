bell push

noun
button for ringing a bell
6.2
Pronunciation: / bɛl pʊʃ / (AmE), / bɛl pʊʃ / (BrE)
a button mounted on a wall or door that you press to ring a bell, especially at an entrance

Examples

The bell push button

The old-fashioned bell push beside the gate had stopped working.
