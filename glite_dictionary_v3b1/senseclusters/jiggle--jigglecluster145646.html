jiggle

verb
move quickly back and forth
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡl̩ / (BrE)
to move lightly or quickly up and down or to and fro in short, rapid movements

Examples

To jiggle the handle
Please don't jiggle the table while I'm writing.
She jiggled the handle to get the door open.
The coins jiggled in his pocket as he walked.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shake - to move (something) up and down or from side to side with quick movements
  • synonym: wiggle - to move or cause to move up and down or from side to side with small quick movements
