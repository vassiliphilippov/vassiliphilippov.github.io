jiggle

verb
move quickly back and forth
5.0 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪɡəl / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪɡl̩ / (BrE)

Examples

To jiggle the handle

She jiggled the handle to get the door open.
The coins jiggled in his pocket as he walked.
