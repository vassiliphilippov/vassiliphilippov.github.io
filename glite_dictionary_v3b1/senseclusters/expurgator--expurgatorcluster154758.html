expurgator

noun
automated content-filtering program
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈek.spɜːr.ɡeɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈek.spə.ɡeɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈek.spɜː.ɡeɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈek.spə.ɡeɪ.tə/ (BrE)
(computing) A software program or automated system that detects and removes or filters obscene, offensive, or inappropriate content from digital text, images, or other media.

Examples

Automatic expurgator
The platform's expurgator automatically filters profanity from user comments in real time.
After reports of hate speech, the social network updated its expurgator to block images with slurs.
Hospitals use multiple expurgators to remove patient-identifying information before research sharing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: censor - to examine and suppress or remove material considered unacceptable
  • synonym: content filter - a tool or system that blocks or removes unwanted material from digital content
