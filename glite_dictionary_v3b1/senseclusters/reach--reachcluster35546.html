reach

noun
audience size; number of people exposed
Pronunciation: / riːtʃ / (AmE), / riːtʃ / (BrE)
The estimated number of distinct people who have seen or been exposed to a piece of content, advertisement, or campaign, especially on media or online platforms.

Examples

Social media reach
The campaign's reach on social media surpassed two million users.
Advertisers focused on unique reach rather than total impressions for clearer audience measurement.
We measured the weekly reach for each post to understand which topics engaged people most.

Related Terms

  • synonym: audience - the group of people who receive or view content; similar concept used in media measurement
