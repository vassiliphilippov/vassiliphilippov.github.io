flow

verb
hang or fall smoothly; drape gracefully
1.9
Pronunciation: / floʊ / (AmE), / fləʊ / (BrE)
(of hair, fabric, etc.) to hang or fall in a smooth, graceful way.

Examples

Long hair flows
Golden locks flowed down the child's back, catching the sunlight.
Her silk scarf flowed gracefully over her shoulders.
The evening gown flowed elegantly to the floor, accentuating her movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drape - to hang or arrange loosely and gracefully
  • synonym: hang - to be suspended or arranged so as to fall freely
