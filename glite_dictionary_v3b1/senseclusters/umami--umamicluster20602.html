umami

adjective
savory taste
5.0
Pronunciation: / uːˈmɑːmi / (AmE), / uːˈmɑːmi / (BrE)
having a pleasant savory taste, often described as meaty or brothy

Examples

Umami flavor sauce
The soup had a rich umami flavor that made it especially satisfying.
Mushrooms are often praised for their natural umami qualities in vegetarian dishes.
Adding soy sauce can enhance the umami taste of many recipes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bitter - having a sharp, pungent taste or smell; not sweet
  • synonym: savory - having a salty or spicy flavor rather than a sweet one
  • antonym: sweet - having the taste characteristic of sugar or honey
