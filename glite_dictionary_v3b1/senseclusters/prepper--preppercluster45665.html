prepper

noun
person preparing for emergencies
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈprep.ər/ (AmE), /ˈprep.ə/ (BrE)
a person who actively prepares for possible emergencies or disasters, often by stockpiling supplies and learning survival skills

Examples

A prepper stocks supplies
As a dedicated prepper, she keeps her pantry stocked with nonperishable food and water.
Online forums connect preppers who share tips about disaster preparedness and survival techniques.
The storm prompted many preppers to check their emergency supplies and generators.
