vert

adjective
heraldic green
9.9
Pronunciation: / vɜrt / (AmE), / vɜːt / (BrE)
green in color, used in heraldry to describe a shield, field, or charge that is green

Examples

Vert shield
The knight's shield was painted vert to symbolize hope and joy.
In the coat of arms, the field is described as vert with a silver lion.
The artist chose a vert background for the family crest to represent growth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: green - the color between blue and yellow in the spectrum
  • antonym: gules - red in heraldry
