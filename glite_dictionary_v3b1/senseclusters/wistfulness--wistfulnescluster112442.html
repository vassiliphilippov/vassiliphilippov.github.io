wistfulness

noun
vague, sad longing
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪstfəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈwɪstfəlnəs / (BrE)
A feeling of vague or regretful longing, often with a touch of sadness.

Examples

Deep wistfulness
His wistfulness grew stronger every time he looked at old photographs of his family.
There was a hint of wistfulness in her eyes as she spoke about her childhood.
With a sense of wistfulness, he watched the sun set over the familiar landscape he would soon leave.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contentment - a state of happiness and satisfaction
  • synonym: melancholy - a feeling of deep sadness or sorrow
  • synonym: nostalgia - a sentimental longing for the past
