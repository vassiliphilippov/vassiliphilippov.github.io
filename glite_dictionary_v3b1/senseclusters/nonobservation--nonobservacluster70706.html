nonobservation

noun
failure to comply
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌɑbzɚˈveɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌɒbzəˈveɪʃən / (BrE)
the failure to observe or comply with a rule, law, or requirement

Examples

Nonobservation of rules
The company was fined for nonobservation of safety regulations.
Repeated nonobservation of the dress code led to stricter enforcement at the school.
His nonobservation of the agreement resulted in legal action against him.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliance - the act of following a rule or requirement
  • synonym: violation - the act of breaking or disregarding a rule or law
