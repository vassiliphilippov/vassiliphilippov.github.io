heap of

noun
large amount or pile of something
Pronunciation: / hip əv / (AmE), / hiːp əv / (BrE)
a large amount or pile of something, often used informally to refer to a large quantity

Examples

A heap of clothes
After the party, we had a heap of dirty dishes to clean.
She received a heap of compliments on her performance.
There were a heap of books scattered around his desk.
