raiment

noun
fine or decorative clothing
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪmənt / (AmE), / ˈreɪm(ə)nt / (BrE)
clothing, especially fine or decorative garments, often used in literary or formal contexts.

Examples

Ceremonial raiment
He exchanged his humble clothes for the ceremonial raiment of his office.
Literary descriptions often refer to silken raiment shimmering in the candlelight.
The queen appeared before her subjects in splendid raiment adorned with jewels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attire - clothes, especially formal or elegant ones
  • synonym: garment - an item of clothing
