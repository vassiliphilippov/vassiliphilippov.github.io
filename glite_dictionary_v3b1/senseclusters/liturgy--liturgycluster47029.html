liturgy

noun
Christian rite with consecrated bread and wine
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪtərdʒi / (AmE), / ˈlɪtədʒi / (BrE)
a Christian rite or sacrament commemorating the Last Supper by consecrating bread and wine

Examples

Orthodox liturgy service
During the Orthodox liturgy, the priest consecrates bread and wine.
The liturgy includes prayers, hymns, and the celebration of the Eucharist.
The Sunday liturgy is attended by hundreds of parishioners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Eucharist - the Christian sacrament of Holy Communion, commemorating the Last Supper
  • synonym: Mass - the main eucharistic service in the Roman Catholic Church
