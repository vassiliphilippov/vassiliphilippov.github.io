quadricycle

noun
early four-wheeled vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɑːdrɪˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈkwɒdrɪˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
an early four-wheeled vehicle, especially a horseless carriage or experimental automobile from the late 19th or early 20th century

Examples

Antique quadricycle
The museum displayed a restored quadricycle built in 1896 by early automobile pioneers.
Henry Ford's first vehicle was a quadricycle that he assembled in a small workshop.
Collectors value the quadricycle for its historical significance in the development of cars.
