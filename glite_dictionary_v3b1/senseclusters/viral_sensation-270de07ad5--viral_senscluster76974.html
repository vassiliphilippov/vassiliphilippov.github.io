viral sensation

noun
something that quickly becomes very popular online
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪɹəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈvaɪrəl sɛnˈseɪʃən / (BrE)
something that rapidly becomes extremely popular or widely shared, especially on the internet or social media

Examples

A viral sensation online
Her dance moves made her an instant viral sensation on TikTok and Instagram.
The funny cat video became a viral sensation overnight, amassing millions of views in just a few days.
The quirky commercial turned into a viral sensation, boosting the company's sales enormously.
