diesel

noun
engine using compression ignition
3.4 4/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀêz…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀêz…ôl / (BrE)
An internal-combustion engine in which fuel oil is ignited by the heat of compression, rather than by a spark.

Examples

Diesel engine
Many marine vessels use a diesel for greater efficiency and torque.
She prefers a diesel in her car due to its lower fuel consumption.
The truck is powered by a reliable diesel that performs well even in cold weather.

    2.8 1/m

  • diesel motor
