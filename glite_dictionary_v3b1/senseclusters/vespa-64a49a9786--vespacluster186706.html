Vespa

noun
genus of wasps (hornets, yellow jackets)
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛspə /; / ˈvɛs.pə / (AmE), / ˈvɛspə /; / ˈvɛs.pə / (BrE)
a genus of large wasps that includes hornets and some species of yellow jackets

Examples

Vespa wasps
Many Vespa wasps build their nests in tree hollows or under eaves.
Scientists recently discovered a new Vespa species in Southeast Asia.
The Asian giant hornet belongs to the genus Vespa, distinguished by its size and potent sting.
In Europe, the Vespa crabro is known as the European hornet.
Scientists studied the nesting behavior of several Vespa species in tropical forests.
The Vespa genus includes the world's largest wasp species, the Asian giant hornet.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hornet - a large stinging wasp of the genus Vespa
  • synonym: yellow jacket - a North American wasp of the genera Vespula or Dolichovespula, related to Vespa
