global warming

noun
increase in Earth's average atmospheric temperature
1.9 2.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɡloʊbəl ˈwɔrmɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɡləʊbəl ˈwɔːmɪŋ / (BrE)
a gradual increase in the average temperature of the Earth's atmosphere, primarily due to human activities such as burning fossil fuels, resulting in climate change

Examples

Global warming effects
Many governments are introducing policies to combat global warming.
Scientists warn that global warming will lead to rising sea levels and more extreme weather events.
The effects of global warming can already be seen in shrinking glaciers and melting sea ice.

  • synonym: climate change - long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns in a place
