fortified

adjective
secured with defensive military works
6.8 5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrtɪfaɪd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːtɪfaɪd / (BrE)
(of a place or building) strengthened and secured with defensive military works such as walls or bastions.

Examples

Fortified castle walls
Many medieval towns were fortified with thick stone walls and watchtowers.
The fortified castle stood atop the hill, making it difficult for enemies to attack.
The villagers took shelter in a fortified church during the invasion.
