manless

adjective
without human crew
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmæn.ləs / (AmE), / ˈmæn.ləs / (BrE)
operated, controlled, or functioning without any people or human crew on board, especially referring to vehicles or stations

Examples

Manless spacecraft
The agency launched a manless spacecraft to explore the distant planet.
Many modern drones are manless and can be operated remotely from the ground.
The research station was designed to be manless and function automatically in harsh environments.

  • synonym: unmanned - operated without people on board
