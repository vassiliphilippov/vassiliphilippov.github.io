drainer

noun
source of exhaustion
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪnər / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnə / (BrE)
a person or thing that gradually uses up someone's energy, enthusiasm, or resources

Examples

A real drainer
All those long meetings became a real drainer for the whole team.
Dealing with constant complaints at work can be a major drainer on your motivation.
He avoided negative people because he found them to be an emotional drainer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burden - something that causes worry or difficulty
  • antonym: energizer - something that gives energy or enthusiasm
