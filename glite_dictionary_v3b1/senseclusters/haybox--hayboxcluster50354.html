haybox

noun
insulated cooking box
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌbɑks / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌbɒks / (BrE)
a box insulated with hay or similar material, used to keep food hot and allow it to finish cooking slowly

Examples

Soup in a haybox
After boiling the soup, she placed the pot in a haybox to finish cooking without extra fuel.
During the camping trip, they used a haybox to keep their meal warm for hours.
A traditional haybox can save energy by using insulation to cook food slowly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: oven - an enclosed compartment for cooking food by heating
  • synonym: slow cooker - an electric pot for cooking food slowly at a low temperature
