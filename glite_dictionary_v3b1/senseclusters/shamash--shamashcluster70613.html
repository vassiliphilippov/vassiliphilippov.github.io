shamash

noun
Hanukkah helper candle
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʃɑˈmɑʃ / (AmE), / ʃəˈmaʃ / (BrE)
the helper candle on a Hanukkah menorah, used to light the other candles

Examples

The shamash candle
The shamash is always placed higher or apart from the other candles on the menorah.
Each night of Hanukkah, we use the shamash to light the new candle.
He carefully lit the shamash before starting the Hanukkah ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: menorah - a candelabrum with spaces for candles, used during Hanukkah
