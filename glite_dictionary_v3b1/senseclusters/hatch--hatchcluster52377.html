hatch

sit on eggs to incubate

(of a bird or other animal) to sit on eggs in order to incubate them until they are ready to hatch.

Examples

Birds hatch eggs
Many birds take turns to hatch the eggs until they are ready to break open.
Penguins must hatch their eggs in extreme cold by balancing them on their feet.
The mother hen will hatch her eggs by sitting on them for several weeks.

Synonyms
