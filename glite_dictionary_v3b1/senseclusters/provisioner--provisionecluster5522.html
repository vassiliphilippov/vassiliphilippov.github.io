provisioner

noun
automated supplier of computing resources
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪʒənər / (AmE), / prəˈvɪʒənə / (BrE)
(in computing) a software component or service that automatically allocates, configures, or makes available computing resources (for example, storage volumes, virtual machines, or containers) on demand.

Examples

Cloud provisioner
Kubernetes uses a dynamic provisioner to allocate storage volumes on demand.
During deployment, the cloud provisioner automatically created virtual machines and attached networks.
Security audits check that third-party provisioners follow policies when they create user environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: provision - to supply or make available resources or services; in computing, to allocate and configure resources.
  • synonym: resource manager - a system or service that controls allocation and lifecycle of computing resources.
