pug

noun
temporary ad hoc gaming group
Pronunciation: / pʌg / (AmE), / pʌg / (BrE)
(informal, gaming) a temporary, ad hoc group of players assembled to play a single match, dungeon, raid, or mission together.

Examples

A pug group
Before the raid they formed a quick pug since their usual group was offline.
We waited ten minutes for the last player to join the pug and start the mission.
Random pugs can be unpredictable, but sometimes they bring surprisingly skilled players.
