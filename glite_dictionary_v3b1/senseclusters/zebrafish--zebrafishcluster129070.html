zebrafish

noun
striped tropical fish
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛbrəˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈziːbrəˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small tropical freshwater fish with black and white stripes, commonly used in scientific research

Examples

The zebrafish model
The zebrafish is a popular model organism in genetic studies.
Aquariums often display the striking black and white stripes of the zebrafish.
Researchers observed the development of embryos in the zebrafish under a microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: danio - a small freshwater fish of the same genus as the zebrafish
  • synonym: guppy - a small tropical freshwater fish often kept in aquariums
