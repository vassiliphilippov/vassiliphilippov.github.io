pepper shrub

noun
plant with blotched foliage and black fruits
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpər ʃrʌb / (AmE), / ˈpɛpə ʃrʌb / (BrE)
An evergreen shrub or small tree with foliage conspicuously blotched with red and yellow, and bearing small black fruits.

Examples

A pepper shrub plant
She planted a pepper shrub to add vibrant colors to her backyard.
The garden features a rare pepper shrub known for its colorful leaves.
The small black fruits hanging from the pepper shrub attract many birds.
