forwards

adverb
toward the front; in a forward direction
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrwərdz / (AmE), / ˈfɔːwədz / (BrE)
in the direction that is in front or ahead; toward a place or position ahead

Examples

Move forwards quickly
She leaned forwards to get a better view of the painting.
The crowd began to move forwards as the doors opened.
The dog took a few cautious steps forwards before stopping.
She had to lean forwards to reach the highest shelf in the cupboard.
The car rolled forwards a few inches before coming to a stop.
We began to walk forwards along the narrow path through the forest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: forward - toward the front; in a forward direction (main adverb form, more common in American English)
