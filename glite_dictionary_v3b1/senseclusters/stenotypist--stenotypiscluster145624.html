stenotypist

noun
shorthand recorder
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnoʊˌtaɪpɪst / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊˌtaɪpɪst / (BrE)
a person who quickly types or records spoken words using a stenotype machine, especially in legal or official settings

Examples

The stenotypist types quickly
The stenotypist captured every word of the meeting for the official record.
During the trial, a stenotypist transcribed the lawyers' arguments in real time.
She trained as a stenotypist to work in courtrooms and business conferences.
The stenotypist captured every word of the witness's testimony during the trial.
A skilled stenotypist can keep up with even the fastest speakers in a courtroom.
She trained for months to become a certified stenotypist for legal proceedings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: court reporter - a person who transcribes spoken words in court
  • synonym: stenographer - a person who transcribes speech using shorthand
  • synonym: transcriber - a person who makes a written copy of spoken words
  • degree lower: typist - a person who types documents
