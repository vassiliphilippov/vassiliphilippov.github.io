appraisingly

adverb
in a judging manner
Pronunciation: / əˈpreɪzɪŋli / (AmE), / əˈpreɪzɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows you are judging the value, quality, or worth of something

Examples

Look appraisingly at
He glanced appraisingly at the new employee during the interview.
The chef tasted the soup appraisingly before serving it to the guests.
She examined the antique vase appraisingly, considering its possible value.

Related Terms

  • antonym: admiringly - in a way that shows respect or approval
  • synonym: critically - in a way that involves careful judgment or evaluation
