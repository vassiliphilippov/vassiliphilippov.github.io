chakra

noun
circular object
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑːkrə / (AmE), / ˈtʃʌkrə / (BrE)
a wheel or circular object, especially a disk or ring, originally from Sanskrit meaning 'wheel'

Examples

A chakra wheel
The ancient statue held a chakra in one of its hands as a symbol of power.
During the festival, a large wooden chakra was paraded through the streets.
Artisans carefully painted each chakra before it was placed on the temple altar.
