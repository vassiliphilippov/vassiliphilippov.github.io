scrub

verb
cancel or postpone a scheduled event
Pronunciation: / skrʌb / (AmE), / skrʌb / (BrE)
to cancel or postpone something that was scheduled, often at short notice

Examples

To scrub a meeting
Due to bad weather, they had to scrub the outdoor concert at the last minute.
NASA had to scrub the rocket launch because of technical issues.
We decided to scrub our weekend plans and stay home instead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: call off - to cancel something that had been planned
  • synonym: cancel - to decide that something planned will not happen
