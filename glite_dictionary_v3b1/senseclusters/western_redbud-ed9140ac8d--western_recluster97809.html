western redbud

noun
North American flowering shrub
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwe.stərn ˈred.bʌd/ /; / /ˈhwe.stərn ˈred.bʌd/ (AmE), /ˈwe.stən ˈred.bʌd/ (BrE)
