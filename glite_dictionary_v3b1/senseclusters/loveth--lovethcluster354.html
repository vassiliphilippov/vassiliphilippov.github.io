loveth

verb
archaic 'loves'
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌvɪθ / (AmE), / ˈlʌvɪθ / (BrE)
archaic or poetic third person singular present form of 'love', meaning he/she/it loves

Examples

He loveth wisdom
He that loveth wisdom shall find happiness.
The poet wrote, 'She loveth with all her heart and soul.'
In old texts, one often reads that God loveth the righteous.
