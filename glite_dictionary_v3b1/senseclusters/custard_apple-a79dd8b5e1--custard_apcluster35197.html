custard apple

noun
sweet tropical fruit of Annona tree
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstɚd ˌæpəl /; / ˈkʌstərd ˌæpəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəd ˌæpəl / (BrE)
a tropical tree of the genus Annona, or its sweet, soft, edible fruit

Examples
