windigo

noun
delusional syndrome
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndɪˌɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈwɪndɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a culture-bound syndrome in which a person believes they are turning into a windigo and may crave human flesh

Examples

Windigo syndrome
Some Indigenous communities have traditional stories about the windigo and its terrifying hunger.
The psychiatrist diagnosed the patient with windigo syndrome after hearing about his unusual fears.
Researchers have studied cases of windigo in northern Canada to understand culture-bound syndromes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: psychosis - a severe mental disorder with loss of contact with reality
  • synonym: wendigo - a mythical cannibalistic creature in Algonquian folklore
