topographically

adverb
in relation to surface features or topography
Pronunciation: / ˌtɑːpəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌtɒpəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
In a way that relates to the arrangement or features of the physical surface of an area.

Examples

Described topographically
Islands that are close geographically may be quite different topographically.
The map is organized topographically, showing elevation contours and landforms.
The region is topographically diverse, with mountains, valleys, and plains all within a short distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: topographic - relating to the arrangement of the physical features of an area
