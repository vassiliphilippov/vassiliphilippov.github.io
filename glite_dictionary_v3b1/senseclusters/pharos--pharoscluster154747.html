pharos

noun
ancient lighthouse; large beacon tower
9.9 0.03/m
Pronunciation: / Ààf…õ…ôro äs / (AmE), / Ààf…ëÀêr…ís / (BrE)
an ancient lighthouse, especially the Lighthouse of Alexandria, or any large lighthouse or beacon tower guiding ships

Examples

An ancient pharos
In literature, a pharos often symbolizes guidance and hope amidst darkness.
Many sailors relied on the light from the pharos to guide their ships safely to harbor.
The ancient pharos at Alexandria was one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
