poisonous

adjective
containing poison; harmful if taken in
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɔɪ.zən.əs/ /; / /ˈpɔɪz.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈpɔɪ.zən.əs/ /; / /ˈpɔɪz.nəs/ (BrE)
containing or producing poison; able to cause illness or death if eaten, touched, or inhaled

Examples

Poisonous mushrooms
Some mushrooms are poisonous and should never be eaten.
The snake's bite was poisonous and required immediate medical attention.

Some plants are poisonous to pets and should be kept out of reach.
The scientist warned us that the liquid in the container is highly poisonous.
You should never eat those mushrooms because they are poisonous.
