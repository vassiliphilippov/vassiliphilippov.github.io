cuirass

noun
armor covering the chest and back
Pronunciation: / kwɪˈræs / (AmE), / kwɪəˈrɑːs / (BrE)
a piece of armor covering the chest and back, worn as part of a suit of armor, especially in medieval times

Examples

A cuirass of armor
His heavy cuirass protected him from sword blows during battle.
Restorers carefully cleaned the century-old cuirass for the museum exhibit.
The knight wore a polished cuirass that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breastplate - a piece of armor covering the chest
