precipitator

noun
device removing particles from gases by electrostatic means
2.3 7.6
Pronunciation: /prɪ.sɪ.pɪ.ˈteɪ.tər/ (AmE), /prɪ.sɪ.pɪ.ˈteɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a device that removes dust and other particles from gases by using electrostatic precipitation

Examples

Electrostatic precipitator device
An electrostatic precipitator efficiently captures dust particles before they escape into the atmosphere.
Regular maintenance of the precipitator is necessary to ensure optimal removal of particulate matter.
The factory installed a new precipitator to reduce air pollution from its smokestacks.
