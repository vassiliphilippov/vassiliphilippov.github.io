sensualness

noun
quality of being sensual; desire for sensual pleasures
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛnʃuəlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɛnʃʊəlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being sensual, especially the appetite or desire for sensual pleasures

Examples

The sensualness of pleasure
His sensualness was evident in the way he appreciated fine food, music, and art.
She was both admired and judged for her unapologetic sensualness in expressing her desires.
The novel explores the tension between spiritual devotion and sensualness.
