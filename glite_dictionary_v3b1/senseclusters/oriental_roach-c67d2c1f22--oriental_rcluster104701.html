oriental roach

noun
a large dark brown cockroach species
Pronunciation: / ɔːr.iˈɛn.təl roʊtʃ / (AmE), / ɒ.riˈɛn.təl rəʊtʃ / (BrE)
a large, dark brown species of cockroach (Blatta orientalis) originally from Asia, now found in many parts of the world

Examples

The oriental roach
Homeowners struggle to eliminate oriental roaches due to their resilience.
The oriental roach is often found in damp basements and crawl spaces.
Unlike the German cockroach, the oriental roach prefers cooler environments.
