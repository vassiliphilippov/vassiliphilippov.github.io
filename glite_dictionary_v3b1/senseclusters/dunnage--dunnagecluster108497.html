dunnage

noun
packing waste material
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌnɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈdʌnɪdʒ / (BrE)
waste material or discarded packing used to protect cargo during transport or left over after unloading

Examples

Remove old dunnage
The workers cleared the dunnage from the ship's hold after unloading the cargo.
Old pallets and plastic wrap were left as dunnage in the warehouse corner.
Please dispose of any dunnage before the next shipment arrives.

Related Terms

  • synonym: packing material - material used to protect goods during shipping
  • synonym: rubbish - waste material or unwanted things
