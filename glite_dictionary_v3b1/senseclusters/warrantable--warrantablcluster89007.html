warrantable

adjective
covered by warranty
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrəntəbl̩ / (AmE), / ˈwɒrəntəbl̩ / (BrE)
eligible to be covered by a warranty or considered acceptable under a guarantee or justification

Examples

Warrantable repair
The repair was warrantable because the product was still under the original guarantee.
Only warrantable claims will be accepted by the insurance company for reimbursement.
His actions were considered warrantable given the circumstances and the evidence provided.

Related Terms

  • synonym: justifiable - able to be shown as reasonable or warranted
  • antonym: unwarranted - not justified or authorized
