vegetation

noun
passive, monotonous inactivity
7.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ve.dʒə.ˈteɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a state of passive and monotonous inactivity, likened to the unchanging existence of plants

Examples

A life of vegetation
After months of illness, he lapsed into a state of vegetation.
The long, dull winter brought on a mood of listless vegetation for many residents.
Their holiday was spent in a haze of sleep and vegetation.
