foundation of

noun
basis or groundwork for something
1.0
Pronunciation: / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ʌv / (AmE), / faʊnˈdeɪʃən ɒv / (BrE)
the underlying basis or principle on which something is built, developed, or established

Examples
