cast

noun
throwing a fishing line with a rod
Pronunciation: / kæst / (AmE), / kɑːst / (BrE)
the act of throwing a fishing line into the water using a rod and reel

Examples

A fishing cast
He practiced his cast every morning at the lake.
Her first cast landed the bait near a group of reeds.
With a smooth cast, he sent the lure far out into the river.
