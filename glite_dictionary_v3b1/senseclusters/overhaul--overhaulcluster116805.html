overhaul

verb
to repair or renovate thoroughly
4.7 4.6
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhɔl / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl / (BrE)

Examples

The company decided to overhaul its outdated computer systems to improve efficiency.
