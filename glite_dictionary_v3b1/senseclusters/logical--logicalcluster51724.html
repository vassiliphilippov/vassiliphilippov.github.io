logical

adjective
capable of clear and rational thinking
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑdʒɪkəl /; / ˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
able to think or reason in a clear, consistent, and rational way

Examples

Logical thinker
After the accident, it took her some time to become logical and coherent again.

She is a very logical thinker, able to solve complex problems with ease.
His argument was carefully structured and extremely logical.
It's important to stay logical when making difficult decisions.
She has a very logical approach to solving complex problems.
