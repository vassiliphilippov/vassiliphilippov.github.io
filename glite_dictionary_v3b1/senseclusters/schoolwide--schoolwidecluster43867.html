schoolwide

adjective
affecting an entire school
4.8
Pronunciation: / skuːlˌwaɪd / (AmE), / skuːlˌwaɪd / (BrE)
Happening or existing throughout all parts of a school or involving all its members.

Examples

A schoolwide policy
A schoolwide policy regarding uniforms was introduced this year.
The principal announced a schoolwide effort to improve attendance.
There will be a schoolwide assembly on Friday morning.
