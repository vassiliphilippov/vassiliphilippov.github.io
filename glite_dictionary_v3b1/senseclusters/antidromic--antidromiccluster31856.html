antidromic

adjective
nerve impulse in the opposite direction to normal
9.3 9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.ˈdroʊ.mɪk/ /; / /æn.tɪ.ˈdrɑː.mɪk/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.ˈdrɒ.mɪk/ /; / /æn.tɪ.ˈdrəʊ.mɪk/ (BrE)
