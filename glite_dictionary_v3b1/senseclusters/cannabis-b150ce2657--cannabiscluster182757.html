Cannabis

noun
genus of hemp and marijuana plants
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkænəbɪs / (AmE), / ˈkænəbɪs / (BrE)
A genus of annual flowering plants native to central Asia and widely naturalized in temperate regions, including hemp and marijuana.

Examples

Cannabis plant fibers
Farmers in some regions cultivate Cannabis for its strong hemp fibers.
Scientific research on Cannabis has expanded greatly in recent decades.
The Cannabis genus includes both hemp and marijuana varieties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemp - plant of the species Cannabis sativa, used for fiber
  • synonym: marijuana - psychoactive drug from Cannabis plants
