pinwheel roll

noun
sweet spiral-shaped baked roll
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌwiːl roʊl / (AmE), / ˈpɪnˌwiːl rəʊl / (BrE)
A sweet baked roll shaped like a pinwheel, spread with cinnamon and sugar and often filled with jam before baking.

Examples

A pinwheel roll with cinnamon
She brought a plate of pinwheel rolls to the party, and they disappeared quickly.
The bakery's pinwheel roll is filled with raspberry jam and sprinkled with cinnamon sugar.
We enjoyed freshly baked pinwheel rolls with our morning coffee.
