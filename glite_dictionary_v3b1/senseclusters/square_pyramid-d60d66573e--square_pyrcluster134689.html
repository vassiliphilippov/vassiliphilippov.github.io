square pyramid

noun
pyramid with a square base
Pronunciation: / skwɛr ˈpaɪrəmɪd / (AmE), / skweə ˈpɪərəmɪd / (BrE)
A three-dimensional geometric figure with a square base and four triangular faces that meet at a single point (the apex).

Examples

A square pyramid shape
A square pyramid has five faces: one square and four triangles.
The architect designed the roof in the shape of a square pyramid.
We built a model of a square pyramid using straws and glue for our geometry class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pyramid - a solid with a polygonal base and triangular faces that meet at a point
