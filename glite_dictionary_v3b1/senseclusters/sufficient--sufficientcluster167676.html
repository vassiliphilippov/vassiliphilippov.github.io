Synonyms

  • enough
    1.0 300/m
    enough is the everyday word for having as much as you need, while sufficient sounds more formal and booklike.
  • adequate
    2.7 11/m
    adequate describes something that meets basic needs of quality or amount, while sufficient stresses having just enough for a purpose.
  • satisfactory
    2.3 4/m
    satisfactory means acceptable to people or standards, while sufficient focuses on having enough to meet the specific need.
  • good enough
    1.9 30/m
    good enough is a casual phrase meaning acceptable in practice, while sufficient is more neutral and often used in formal contexts.
