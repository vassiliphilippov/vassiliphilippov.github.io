where

adverb
place or position
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɛr / (AmE), / weə / (BrE)
used to ask or refer to the place or position of something or someone

Examples

Where are my keys
Where did you leave your keys after coming home last night?
Can you tell me where the nearest train station is located?
She wondered where her friends had gone during the lunch break.
