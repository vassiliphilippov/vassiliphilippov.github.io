bush pea

noun
genus of plants with trifoliate leaves and showy flowers

Pronunciation: / b ä É pi / (AmE), / b ä É piÀê / (BrE)
Any of several plants of the genus Thermopsis, characterized by trifoliate leaves and yellow or purple racemose flowers.
