Euphorbia antisyphilitica

noun
shrub producing candelilla wax
9.9
Pronunciation: / juːˈfɔːrbiə ˌæntaɪˌsɪfəˈlɪtɪkə / (AmE), / juːˈfɔːbɪə ˌæntaɪˌsɪfɪˈlɪtɪkə / (BrE)
A wax-coated shrub native to northern Mexico and the southwestern United States, known for yielding candelilla wax.

Examples

Euphorbia antisyphilitica wax shrub
Euphorbia antisyphilitica grows abundantly in the arid regions of Texas and northern Mexico.
