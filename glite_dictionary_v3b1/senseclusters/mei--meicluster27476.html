mei

noun
Japanese ornamental tree with fragrant blossoms
Pronunciation: / meɪ / (AmE), / meɪ / (BrE)
A Japanese ornamental tree with fragrant white or pink blossoms and small yellow fruits, valued for its beauty and often associated with gardens and traditional art.

Examples

The mei tree
Artists often paint scenes featuring the mei due to its cultural significance in Japan.
In the tea garden, a single mei stood as a symbol of renewal and beauty.
The mei tree in her garden was covered in delicate pink blossoms each spring.
