needle

verb
provoke or annoy persistently
7.3 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈniː.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈniː.dəl/ (BrE)
to deliberately annoy or provoke someone, especially by repeatedly criticizing or teasing them

Examples

To needle a friend
He needled his friend by constantly reminding him of his embarrassing mistake.
Her brother loved to needle her about her messy room until she finally cleaned it.
The journalist kept needling the politician with difficult questions at the interview.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irritate - to make someone annoyed or impatient
  • synonym: provoke - to stimulate or incite someone to feel or do something
