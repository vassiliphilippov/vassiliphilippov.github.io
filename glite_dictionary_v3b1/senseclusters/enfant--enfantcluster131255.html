enfant

noun
French term for child
9.0
Pronunciation: / ɑ̃ˈfɑ̃ / (AmE), / ɑ̃ˈfɑ̃ / (BrE)
a child, especially as referred to in French or in French cultural contexts

Examples

The enfant in class
The French teacher called her students her enfants during the lesson.
He was an enfant when his family moved to Paris.
In literature, the term enfant is often used to evoke innocence or youth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: child - a young person who is not yet an adult
