intergroup

noun
multi-group committee
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈɡruːp / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈɡruːp / (BrE)
a committee or organization formed by members from different groups to discuss or address shared issues or interests

Examples

An intergroup committee
The city council formed an intergroup to address issues affecting several neighborhoods.
An intergroup was established to coordinate disaster relief efforts among local organizations.
Members of the intergroup met monthly to discuss community development projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coalition - a group formed by different organizations or parties for a common purpose
  • synonym: committee - a group of people appointed for a specific function
