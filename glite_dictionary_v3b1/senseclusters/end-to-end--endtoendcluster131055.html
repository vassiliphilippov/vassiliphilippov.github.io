end-to-end

adjective
technical: protection between endpoints
6.0 0.2/m
Pronunciation: / cc5bnd t59 c85bnd / (AmE), / cc5bnd t8a c8end / (BrE)
(in computing and information security) describing a system, connection, or process in which data or communication is protected or processed only at the origin and destination endpoints so that intermediaries cannot read, alter, or access the plaintext.

Examples

End-to-end encryption
The messaging app now offers end-to-end encryption to protect user privacy.
Our QA team runs end-to-end tests to ensure the whole service works under load.
Manufacturers must secure the end-to-end supply chain to prevent tampering with products.

Video Examples

Related Terms

  • synonym: point-to-point - a direct connection or communication channel between two specific endpoints, without intermediate relays
  • antonym: server-side - processing or handling performed on central servers rather than on the communicating endpoints; often contrasted with end-to-end approaches
