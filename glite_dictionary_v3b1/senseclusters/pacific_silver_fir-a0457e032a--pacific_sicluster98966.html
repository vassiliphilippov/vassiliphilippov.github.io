Pacific silver fir

noun
a fir tree native to western North America
8.6
Pronunciation: / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈsɪlvər fɝ / (AmE), / pəˈsɪfɪk ˈsɪlvə fɜː / (BrE)
a medium to tall fir tree native to western North America, noted for its conical crown, tiered branches, and orange-scented leaves when crushed

Examples

The Pacific silver fir tree
The Pacific silver fir is known for its elegant, symmetrical shape and soft needles.
We spotted a stand of Pacific silver fir trees while hiking in the Cascades.
When you crush the leaves of a Pacific silver fir, they release a faint citrus aroma.
