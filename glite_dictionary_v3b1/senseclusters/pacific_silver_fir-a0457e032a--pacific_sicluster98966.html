Pacific silver fir

noun
a fir tree native to western North America
8.6 6.1
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈsɪ.fɪk ˈsɪl.vər fɜːr/ (AmE), /pə.ˈsɪ.fɪk ˈsɪl.və fɜː/ (BrE)
