giant

noun
person of great importance or reputation
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt / (BrE)
a person who is extremely important, successful, or influential in a particular field or area

Examples

A political giant
Many regard Nelson Mandela as a political giant of the twentieth century.
She is considered a giant in the world of physics for her groundbreaking discoveries.
The company honored several giants of the industry at the annual gala.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dwarf - a person of little importance or stature (in contrast to 'giant', metaphorically)
  • synonym: luminary - a person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere
