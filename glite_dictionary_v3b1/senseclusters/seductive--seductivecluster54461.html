seductive

adjective
alluring; tempting
9.4 4.4
Pronunciation: /sɪ.ˈdʌk.tɪv/ /; / /sə.ˈdʌk.tɪv/ (AmE), /sɪ.ˈdʌk.tɪv/ /; / /sə.ˈdʌk.tɪv/ (BrE)
Tending to attract or tempt someone into a desired action or state; alluring or enticing.

Examples

A seductive smile

The seductive aroma of freshly baked bread drew customers into the bakery.
The advertisement used seductive images to attract potential buyers.

  • antonym: repellent - able to repel or drive away; unappealing
