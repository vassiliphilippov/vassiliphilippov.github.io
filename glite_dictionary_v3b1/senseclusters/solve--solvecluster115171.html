solve

verb
find an answer to a problem
1.5
Pronunciation: / sɑlv / (AmE), / sɒlv / (BrE)
to find the answer to a problem, question, or mystery; to understand or explain something difficult

Examples

We need to solve this problem before it gets any worse.
