electronic music

noun
music created using electronic devices
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑnɪk ˈmjuˌzɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk ˈmjuːzɪk / (BrE)
music that primarily uses electronic devices and technology to produce, modify, or manipulate sound.

Examples

Electronic music production
Many clubs feature electronic music as their main attraction on weekends.
She composes electronic music using computers and synthesizers.
The rise of electronic music has influenced many other music genres since the 1970s.

Related Terms

  • antonym: acoustic music - music produced using non-electronic instruments
  • synonym: synthpop - a style of popular music with synthesizer-based sounds
