kolinsky

noun
Eurasian weasel; fine fur
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈlɪnski / (AmE), / kəˈlɪnski / (BrE)
a small Eurasian weasel known for its fine, soft fur, often used to make high-quality brushes

Examples

Wild kolinsky animal
The painter invested in a kolinsky brush for its superior quality and precision.
A kolinsky is prized in the art world for the softness of its fur.
Many luxury watercolor brushes are made from the tail hair of the kolinsky.
