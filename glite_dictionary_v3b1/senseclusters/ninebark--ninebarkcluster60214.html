ninebark

noun
ornamental shrub
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪnˌbɑrk / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnˌbɑːk / (BrE)
a North American shrub with peeling bark and clusters of small white or pink flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant

Examples

Flowering ninebark shrub
The ninebark in the garden blooms with delicate white flowers every spring.
She chose ninebark for the landscaping because of its colorful foliage and easy care.
After a few years, the ninebark developed its characteristic peeling bark, adding texture to the yard.
