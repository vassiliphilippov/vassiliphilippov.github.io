springing cow

noun
cow about to give birth
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɪŋɪŋ kaʊ / (AmE), / ˈsprɪŋɪŋ kaʊ / (BrE)
A cow that is about to give birth.

Examples

A springing cow
Farmers pay special attention to a springing cow because she could deliver at any time.
Proper nutrition is important for a springing cow during the final days of pregnancy.
The veterinarian checked the springing cow to ensure she was healthy before calving.
