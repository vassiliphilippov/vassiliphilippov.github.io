Anas americana

noun
species of North American wigeon duck
Pronunciation: / ˈænəs əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnə / (AmE), / ˈænəs əˌmɛrɪˈkeɪnə / (BrE)
a species of North American wigeon, a duck whose male has a white crown; also known as the American wigeon

Examples

The Anas americana duck
A group of Anas americana fed peacefully along the marshy shoreline.
Birdwatchers often spot flocks of Anas americana migrating across lakes in the spring.
The male Anas americana is easily identified by its striking white crown and green eye patch.
