unthreaded

adjective
without screw threads
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθrɛdɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈθrɛdɪd / (BrE)
not having a helical thread or grooves for screwing; smooth and not designed to be joined by screwing.

Examples

Unthreaded bolt
The mechanic needed an unthreaded bolt for the special fitting.
This pipe is unthreaded, so it cannot be screwed into the connector.
Always check if the rod is unthreaded before attempting to attach it.

Related Terms

  • antonym: threaded - having a helical groove for screwing
