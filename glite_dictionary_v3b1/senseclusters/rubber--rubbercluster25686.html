rubber

noun
synthetic elastic material
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌb.ər/ (AmE), /ˈrʌ.bə/ (BrE)
a tough, elastic material made from the latex of certain plants or produced synthetically, used to make many products
