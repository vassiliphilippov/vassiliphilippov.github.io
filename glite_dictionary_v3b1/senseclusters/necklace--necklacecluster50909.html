necklace

verb
to execute with burning tire
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛkləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛklɪs / (BrE)
to kill or injure someone by forcing a burning tire around their neck and setting it on fire, usually as a form of execution or punishment.

Examples

To necklace a victim
During the riots, several people were necklaced by angry mobs as a form of punishment.
The international community condemned the brutal act of necklacing used against political opponents.
He was accused of organizing attacks in which victims were necklaced in public.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lynch - to kill someone, especially by hanging, without a legal trial
