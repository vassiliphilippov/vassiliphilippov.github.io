dead cat bounce

noun
temporary market recovery
9.9 7.4
Pronunciation: /ded kæt baʊns/ (AmE), /ded kæt baʊns/ (BrE)
a brief and temporary recovery in the price of a declining market, often followed by a continued downward trend

Examples

A dead cat bounce
After several weeks of falling prices, the stock market experienced a brief dead cat bounce.
Analysts cautioned that the rally might just be a dead cat bounce rather than a true recovery.
Despite yesterday's dead cat bounce, investors remained pessimistic about the long-term outlook.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rebound - a recovery in value after a decline
  • antonym: slump - a period of decline in value or activity
