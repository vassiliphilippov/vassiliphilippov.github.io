cheeseboard

noun
tray for serving cheeses
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board or tray on which a selection of cheeses is served, often as part of a meal or at a gathering

Examples

A cheeseboard with grapes
She arranged grapes and crackers alongside the cheeseboard for the party.
The restaurant offers a delicious cheeseboard with local and imported varieties.
We enjoyed a glass of wine and a cheeseboard after dinner.
