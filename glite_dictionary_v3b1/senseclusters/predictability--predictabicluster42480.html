predictability

noun
quality of being predictable
Pronunciation: / prɪˌdɪktəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / prɪˌdɪktəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the quality of being able to be predicted or expected in advance

Examples

The predictability of events
Some people find comfort in the predictability of routines and daily habits.
The predictability of the train schedule makes commuting much easier for everyone.
The movie's plot suffered from too much predictability and lacked surprises.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unpredictability - the quality of being not able to be predicted
