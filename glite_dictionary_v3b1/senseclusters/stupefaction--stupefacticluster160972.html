stupefaction

noun
state of stunned astonishment
Pronunciation: / ˌstuːpəˈfækʃən /; / stuːpəˈfæʃən /; / ˌstuː.pəˈfæk.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌstjuːpɪˈfækʃən /; / stjuːpɪˈfækʃən /; / ˌstjuː.pɪˈfæk.ʃən / (BrE)
a state of being so shocked or astonished that you cannot think or react properly
