methyl salicylate

noun
organic compound; wintergreen-scented pain reliever
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːθəl səˈlɪsɪˌleɪt / (AmE), / ˈmiːθəl səˈlɪsɪˌleɪt / (BrE)
