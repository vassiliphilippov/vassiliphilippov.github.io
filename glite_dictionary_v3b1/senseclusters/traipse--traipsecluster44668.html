traipse

verb
walk or wander aimlessly
7.6 6.1
Pronunciation: /treɪps/ /; / /tʃreɪps/ (AmE), /treɪps/ /; / /tʃreɪps/ (BrE)
to walk or move about casually or aimlessly, often with little energy or enthusiasm

Examples

To traipse through fields

She didn't mind traipsing from one store to another while looking for bargains.
We spent the afternoon traipsing around the city in search of the perfect café.
