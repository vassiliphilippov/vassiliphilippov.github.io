Wellsian

noun
admirer of H. G. Wells
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlziən / (AmE), / ˈwɛlziən / (BrE)
a person who strongly admires, follows, or imitates the works, style, or ideas of H. G. Wells

Examples

A devoted Wellsian
As a proud Wellsian, she often referenced The Time Machine in her lectures.
Many Wellsians gather annually to discuss the impact of science fiction on society.
He became a Wellsian after reading several of H. G. Wells's visionary novels.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disciple - a follower or devoted supporter of a particular person or idea
  • synonym: imitator - someone who copies the style or ideas of another
