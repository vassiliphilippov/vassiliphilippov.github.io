flannel-cake

noun
thin pancake cooked on a griddle
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈflænəl ˌkeɪk / (AmE), / ˈflæn(ə)l ˌkeɪk / (BrE)
A thin, flat cake made from batter and cooked on both sides on a griddle; similar to a pancake.

Examples

A flannel-cake on griddle
Many families have their own way of flipping flannel-cakes to make sure they're golden on both sides.
She made a stack of flannel-cakes for breakfast, serving them with honey and butter.
The old cookbook includes a recipe for traditional flannel-cake using simple ingredients.
