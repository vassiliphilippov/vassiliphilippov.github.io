punctiliousness

noun
attention to small details or correct behavior
7.1
Pronunciation: / pʌŋkˈtɪliəsnəs / (AmE), / pʌŋkˈtɪliəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being very careful to pay attention to small details or correct behavior

Examples

Great punctiliousness in work
He approached every task with such punctiliousness that nothing was ever overlooked.
Her punctiliousness about grammar made her an excellent editor.
The punctiliousness of his preparations impressed the entire team.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carelessness - lack of attention or consideration; negligence
  • synonym: meticulousness - the quality of showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise
