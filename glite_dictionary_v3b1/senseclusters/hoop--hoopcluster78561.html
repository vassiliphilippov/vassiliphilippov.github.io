hoop

noun
croquet: small metal arch
Pronunciation: / huːp / (AmE), / huːp / (BrE)
A small metal arch placed in the ground through which balls are hit in the game of croquet.

Examples

Croquet hoop
Each hoop on the course must be passed in the correct order.
He straightened the hoop before starting his turn.
The player aimed carefully and sent the ball through the first hoop.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wicket - small arch in croquet through which balls pass
