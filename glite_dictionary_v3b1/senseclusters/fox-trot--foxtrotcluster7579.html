fox-trot

noun
a ballroom dance with alternating long and short steps
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑksˌtrɑt / (AmE), / ˈfɒksˌtrɒt / (BrE)
a ballroom dance in quadruple time with alternating long and short steps performed to lively music

Examples

The fox-trot dance
He learned to dance the fox-trot for his sister's wedding reception.
The band played a classic tune perfect for a lively fox-trot.
We watched the couples elegantly perform the fox-trot across the polished floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tango - a ballroom dance of Latin American origin
  • synonym: waltz - a ballroom dance in triple time
