resort hotel

noun
hotel in a resort area with many amenities
3.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɔrt hoʊˈtɛl / (AmE), / rɪˈzɔːt həʊˈtɛl / (BrE)
a hotel in a resort area that offers a wide range of amenities and recreational facilities for guests, especially for people on vacation
