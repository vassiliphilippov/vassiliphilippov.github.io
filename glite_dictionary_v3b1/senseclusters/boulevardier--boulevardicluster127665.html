boulevardier

noun
a fashionable frequenter of city boulevards
Pronunciation: / ˌbuləˌvɑrˈdjɛr / (AmE), / ˌbuːləˌvɑːdˈjeə / (BrE)
a man who frequents fashionable city boulevards, especially in Paris, often regarded as sophisticated or socially active

Examples

The boulevardier in Paris
Always impeccably dressed, the boulevardier made his daily rounds along the Champs-Élysées.
He fancied himself a modern boulevardier, sipping espresso at every trendy café in town.
The novel depicts an aging boulevardier reminiscing about the glamour of Parisian nightlife.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flâneur - a person who strolls and observes in urban settings, especially Paris
