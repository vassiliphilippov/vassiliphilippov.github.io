airfield

noun
area for aircraft takeoff and landing
6.3 3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɛrˌfild / (AmE), / ˈeəˌfiːld / (BrE)
an area of land designed for the takeoff and landing of aircraft, often without full airport facilities

Examples

A small airfield
After a long flight, the pilot finally landed at the remote airfield.
Several planes were parked along the edge of the airfield.
The small military airfield was hidden among the hills.
