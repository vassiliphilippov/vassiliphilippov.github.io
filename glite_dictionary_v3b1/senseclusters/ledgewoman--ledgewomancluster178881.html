ledgewoman

noun
female quarry worker who splits stone
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛdʒˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈlɛdʒˌwʊmən / (BrE)
A woman employed in a quarry to split off blocks of stone from a ledge.
