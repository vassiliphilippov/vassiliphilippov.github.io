swadeshi

noun
supporter of local goods
9.9
Pronunciation: /swə.ˈdeɪ.ʃiː/ /; / /swə.ˈde.ʃiː/ (AmE), /swə.ˈdeɪ.ʃiː/ /; / /swə.ˈde.ʃiː/ (BrE)
a person who supports or participates in the movement to promote domestic products and reduce reliance on foreign goods, especially in India

Examples

A proud swadeshi
As a proud swadeshi, he always chose locally made products over imported ones.
The swadeshi led a campaign encouraging people to buy goods produced in their own country.
During the independence movement, many swadeshi refused to use foreign textiles.
