Resedaceae

noun
the mignonette plant family
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛzəˈdeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / ˌrɛsɪˈdiːsiː / (BrE)
A family of mainly Mediterranean herbs, including mignonette, characterized by herbaceous plants with small, often fragrant flowers.

Examples

Resedaceae plant family
Botanists study the characteristics of Resedaceae to understand plant evolution.
Many species in the Resedaceae family are native to the Mediterranean region.
The genus Reseda, belonging to Resedaceae, includes popular garden mignonettes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mignonette - a plant of the genus Reseda, especially Reseda odorata, known for fragrant flowers
