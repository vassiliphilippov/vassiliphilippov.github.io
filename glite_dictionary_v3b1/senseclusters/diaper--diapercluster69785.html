diaper

noun
absorbent garment for babies or incontinent persons
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdaɪ.pɚ / (AmE), / ˈdaɪ.ə.pə / (BrE)
A soft, absorbent garment worn by babies or incontinent people, fastened at the waist and between the legs to absorb excrement.

Examples

A baby diaper
Disposable diapers are convenient for travel and outings.
He learned how to put a clean diaper on his newborn son.
The mother quickly changed the baby's diaper after she noticed it was wet.
