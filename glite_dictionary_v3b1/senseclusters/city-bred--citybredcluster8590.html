city-bred

adjective
having city upbringing or manners
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪti brɛd / (AmE), / ˈsɪti brɛd / (BrE)
Having the upbringing, manners, or characteristics typical of someone from a city.

Examples

City-bred manners
He was proud of his city-bred manners and modern outlook.
The city-bred children were fascinated by the open spaces of the farm.
Unlike her city-bred colleagues, she found life in the countryside quite challenging.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urban - relating to, characteristic of, or situated in a city
