raise a middle finger

verb
make an obscene hand gesture
8.3
Pronunciation: / reɪz ə ˈmɪdəl ˈfɪŋɡər / (AmE), / reɪz ə ˈmɪdəl ˈfɪŋɡə / (BrE)
to make an obscene gesture by extending the middle finger upright, usually as an insult or expression of contempt

Examples

To raise a middle finger at insult
She couldn't help but raise a middle finger at the rude comment.
Teenagers sometimes raise a middle finger as a rebellious gesture.
When the driver cut him off, he raised a middle finger out the window.
