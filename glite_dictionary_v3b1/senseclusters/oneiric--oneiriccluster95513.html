oneiric

adjective
relating to dreams; dream-like
Pronunciation: / oʊˈnaɪrɪk / (AmE), / əʊˈnaɪrɪk / (BrE)
Relating to, or suggestive of dreams.

Examples

A oneiric dream
As he drifted to sleep, his thoughts became oneiric, wandering through impossible landscapes.
Her paintings possess a distinctly oneiric quality, filled with surreal imagery and muted colors.
The film is noted for its oneiric atmosphere, blurring the boundaries between reality and fantasy.
