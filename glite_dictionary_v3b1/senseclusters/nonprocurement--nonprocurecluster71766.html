nonprocurement

adjective
not involving procurement
8.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.prə.ˈkjʊr.mənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.prə.ˈkjʊə.mənt/ (BrE)
not relating to procurement; used to describe activities or agreements where procurement rules do not apply.

Examples

Nonprocurement activities
The organization followed nonprocurement procedures for internal transfers of equipment.
A nonprocurement transaction does not require the same level of oversight as a standard contract.
They signed a nonprocurement agreement to bypass the usual bidding process.
