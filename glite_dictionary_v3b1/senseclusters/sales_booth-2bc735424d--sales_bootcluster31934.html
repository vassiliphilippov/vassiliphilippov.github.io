sales booth

noun
stall for selling goods at events
Pronunciation: / seɪlz buθ / (AmE), / seɪlz buːð / (BrE)
a small, temporary structure or stall used to display and sell goods, typically at a market, fair, or exhibition

Examples

A sales booth at market
She worked at a sales booth selling handmade jewelry during the weekend market.
The charity set up a sales booth to raise funds at the local fair.
Vendors at the street festival each managed their own sales booth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stall - a small, open-fronted shop or booth for selling goods at a market
