grind away

verb
study or work intensively
7.2
Pronunciation: / ɡraɪnd əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ɡraɪnd əˈweɪ / (BrE)
to study or work persistently and intensively, especially before an exam or deadline

Examples

To grind away at textbooks
He spent every evening grinding away at his textbooks before the big test.
Most students grind away at the library when exam season arrives.
She had to grind away for weeks to prepare for her medical finals.
