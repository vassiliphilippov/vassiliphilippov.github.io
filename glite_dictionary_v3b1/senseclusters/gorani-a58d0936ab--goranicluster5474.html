Gorani

noun
Kurdish song or ballad
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊˈrɑːni / (AmE), / ɡɔːˈrɑːni / (BrE)
a traditional Kurdish song or ballad, often featuring religious or narrative themes

Examples

A Gorani song
The musician played a beautiful Gorani that told the story of his ancestors.
During the celebration, everyone joined in singing a traditional Gorani.
She learned to perform a Gorani from her grandmother, preserving their cultural heritage.
