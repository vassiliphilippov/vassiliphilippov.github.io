Tetrapturus

noun
a genus of billfish
Pronunciation: / tɛˈtræptʃərəs / (AmE), / tɛˈtraptjʊərəs /; / tɛˈtræptjʊərəs / (BrE)
a genus of large marine fishes (billfish, marlins, spearfish) in the family Istiophoridae
