curietherapy

noun
radiation therapy using radium
Pronunciation: / ˌkjʊriˈɛrəpi / (AmE), / ˌkjʊərɪˈɛrəpi / (BrE)
a medical treatment in which radium is used for radiation therapy, especially in the treatment of cancer

Examples

Curietherapy treatment
Advances in radiation techniques have largely replaced curietherapy with safer methods.
Early forms of curietherapy required direct handling of radium by medical staff.
The patient underwent curietherapy as part of her cancer treatment plan.
