antiblue filter

noun
screen feature reducing blue light
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæn.tiˈblu ˈfɪl.tɚ / (AmE), / ˌæn.tiˈblu ˈfɪl.tə / (BrE)
a feature or device in a screen or display that reduces the amount of blue light emitted, often to reduce eye strain or improve sleep quality

Examples

The antiblue filter feature
I activated the antiblue filter on my phone to reduce eye strain at night.
Many modern laptops come with a built-in antiblue filter setting.
She installed an antiblue filter app on her tablet after reading about the effects of blue light.
