regrind

verb
to grind again
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɡraɪnd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɡraɪnd / (BrE)

Examples

To regrind a knife

If the drill bit is worn out, you can regrind it to restore its effectiveness.
The factory regularly regrinds old tools to extend their lifespan.
