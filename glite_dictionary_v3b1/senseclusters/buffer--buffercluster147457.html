buffer

verb
lessen or protect against impact
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌfɚ /; / ˈbʌfər / (AmE), / ˈbʌfə / (BrE)
to reduce, absorb, or protect against impact, force, or harm

Examples

To buffer the impact
A healthy savings account can buffer you against unexpected expenses.
The airbags are designed to buffer passengers from sudden impacts.
Trees and hedges help buffer the house from strong winter winds.
The thick walls help buffer the house from outside noise.
Parents often try to buffer their children from the harsh realities of life.
Extra savings can buffer you against unexpected expenses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cushion - to soften the effect of a blow or impact
  • antonym: expose - to leave unprotected or subject to harm
  • synonym: protect - to keep safe from harm or injury
