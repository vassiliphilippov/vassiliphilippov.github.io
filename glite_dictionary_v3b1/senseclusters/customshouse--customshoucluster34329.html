customshouse

noun
government building for customs clearance
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstəmzˌhaʊs / (AmE), / ˈkʌstəmzˌhaʊs / (BrE)
A government building where customs duties are collected and where ships are cleared for entry to or exit from a country.

Examples

The customshouse building
He worked as an officer at the old customshouse on the waterfront.
The cargo was inspected at the customshouse before being allowed into the country.
The historic customshouse now serves as a maritime museum.

Related Terms

  • synonym: customs office - office or agency responsible for customs duties
