stir-fry

noun
dish quickly fried with stirring
5.4 1.9
Pronunciation: /stɜːr fraɪ/ /; / /ˈstɜːr.fraɪ/ (AmE), /stɜː fraɪ/ /; / /ˈstɜː.fraɪ/ (BrE)
a dish made by quickly frying small pieces of food in a little hot oil while stirring them continuously

Examples

A chicken stir-fry

Vegetarians often enjoy a tofu stir-fry served over steamed rice.
