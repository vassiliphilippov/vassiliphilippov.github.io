ankus

noun
elephant goad with a spike and hook
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋkəs / (AmE), / ˈæŋkəs / (BrE)
A goad with a sharp spike and a hook, used by elephant drivers to control elephants.

Examples

A ankus tool
A traditional ankus is often beautifully decorated and passed down through generations of elephant handlers.
She examined the ankus, noticing the sharpness of its hook designed to control massive animals.
The mahout used his ankus with great care while guiding the elephant through the narrow path.
