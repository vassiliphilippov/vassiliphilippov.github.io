noncommittally

adverb
without clear opinion
7.4
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.kə.ˈmɪt.ə.liː/ /; / /nən.kə.ˈmɪt.ə.liː/ (AmE), /nɒn.kə.ˈmɪt.əl.iː/ /; / /nən.kə.ˈmɪt.əl.iː/ (BrE)
in a way that does not show a clear opinion, decision, or commitment

Examples

Answer noncommittally
She answered noncommittally when asked if she would attend the meeting.
The manager nodded noncommittally, giving no hint of approval or disapproval.
He shrugged noncommittally and changed the subject to avoid giving a direct answer.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ambiguously - in a way that is unclear or open to more than one interpretation
  • antonym: decisively - in a way that shows a clear decision or firm opinion
