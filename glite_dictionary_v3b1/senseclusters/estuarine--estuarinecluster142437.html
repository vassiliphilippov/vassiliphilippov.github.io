estuarine

adjective
relating to estuaries
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɛs.tju.əˌrin / (AmE), / ˈɛs.tjʊəˌriːn / (BrE)
relating to or found in an estuary, the tidal mouth of a river where fresh and salt water mix

Examples

Estuarine fish habitat
Many fish species rely on estuarine habitats for breeding and feeding.
Scientists study estuarine water quality to monitor environmental health.
The river delta forms a complex estuarine ecosystem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: estuary - the tidal mouth of a large river, where fresh and salt water meet
