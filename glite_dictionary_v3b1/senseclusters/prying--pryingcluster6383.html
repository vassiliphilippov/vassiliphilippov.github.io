prying

noun
offensively nosy behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈpraɪɪŋ / (BrE)
Offensively curious or nosy behavior; the act of inquiring into matters that do not concern one.

Examples

Unwanted prying
She resented his constant prying into her personal affairs.
Such blatant prying is not welcome in this office.
The letter was carefully hidden to avoid the prying of nosy neighbors.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: curiosity - a desire to know or learn about anything, often related to inquisitiveness
  • synonym: intrusiveness - the quality or state of interrupting or intruding where one is not wanted
