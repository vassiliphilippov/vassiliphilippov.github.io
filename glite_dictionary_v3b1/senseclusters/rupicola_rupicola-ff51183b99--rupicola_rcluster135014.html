Rupicola rupicola

noun
South American bird with bright plumage
Pronunciation: / ruːˈpɪkələ ruːˈpɪkələ / (AmE), / ruːˈpɪkələ ruːˈpɪkələ / (BrE)
A species of tropical bird found in northern South America, known for the male's brilliant red or orange plumage and distinctive disk-shaped crest; commonly called the Guianan cock-of-the-rock.
