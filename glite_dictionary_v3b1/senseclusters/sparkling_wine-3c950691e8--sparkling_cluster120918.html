sparkling wine

noun
wine with bubbles (effervescent)
Pronunciation: / ˈspɑrklɪŋ waɪn / (AmE), / ˈspɑːklɪŋ waɪn / (BrE)
A wine containing dissolved carbon dioxide, which makes it fizzy and effervescent when opened.

Examples

A glass of sparkling wine
Prosecco is a popular type of sparkling wine produced in Italy.
She prefers sparkling wine to still wine at celebrations.
We toasted the new year with a glass of sparkling wine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: champagne - A sparkling wine specifically from the Champagne region of France
