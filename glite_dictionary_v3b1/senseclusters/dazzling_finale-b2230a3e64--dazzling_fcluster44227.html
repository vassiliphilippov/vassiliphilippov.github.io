dazzling finale

noun
spectacular ending performance
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdæzlɪŋ fəˈnɑli / (AmE), / ˈdæzlɪŋ fəˈnɑːli / (BrE)
a spectacular and impressive performance or event at the end of a show or series

Examples

A dazzling finale
After a gripping three hours, the play ended with a dazzling finale that brought the crowd to its feet.
The dance troupe saved their most difficult moves for a dazzling finale everyone will remember.
The fireworks gave the concert a truly dazzling finale that left the audience cheering.
