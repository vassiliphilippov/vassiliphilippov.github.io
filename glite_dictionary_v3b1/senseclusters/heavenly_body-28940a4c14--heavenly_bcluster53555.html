heavenly body

noun
natural object in space (star, planet, etc.)
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛvənli ˈbɑdi / (AmE), / ˈhɛvənli ˈbɒdi / (BrE)
A natural object in space, such as a star, planet, or moon, visible in the sky.

Examples

A heavenly body in space
Ancient civilizations often worshipped the Sun and other heavenly bodies.
Astronomers use special equipment to study the movement of each heavenly body in our solar system.
The telescope allowed us to observe a distant heavenly body in remarkable detail.
