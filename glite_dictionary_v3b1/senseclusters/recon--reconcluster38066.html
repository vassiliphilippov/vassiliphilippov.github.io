recon

noun
information-gathering mission
5.8 1.73/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêk…ën / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêk…ín / (BrE)
a mission or activity to gather information, especially for military purposes

Examples

A recon mission
The soldiers went on a recon to map out enemy positions before the attack.
We did a quick recon of the area to find the safest route.
Before the expedition, the team performed a recon to check for any hazards.
The soldiers went on a recon to check for enemy activity before advancing.
We did a quick recon of the area before setting up camp.
Before the main event, the team performed a recon to plan their approach.
