cecily

noun
aromatic herb plant
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛsɪli / (AmE), / ˈsɛsɪli / (BrE)
a plant with aromatic leaves, also known as sweet cicely, used in cooking and herbal remedies

Examples

Fresh cecily leaves
The chef added fresh cecily to the salad for a hint of sweetness.
Gardeners often grow cecily for its fragrant leaves and delicate white flowers.
Some herbal teas include cecily because of its pleasant aroma and flavor.
