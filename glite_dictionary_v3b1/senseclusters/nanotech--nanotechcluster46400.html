nanotech

noun
nanotechnology company
Pronunciation: / ˈnænoʊˌtɛk / (AmE), / ˈnænəʊˌtɛk / (BrE)
technology, products, or companies that use or develop nanotechnology (working with materials and devices at the nanoscale)

Examples

Nanotech company

The company invested heavily in nanotech to develop new, more efficient batteries.
Researchers believe nanotech will revolutionize the way we design everyday products.
