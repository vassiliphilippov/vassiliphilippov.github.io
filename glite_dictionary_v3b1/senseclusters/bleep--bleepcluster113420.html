bleep

noun
short electronic warning sound
3.7 3.76/m
Pronunciation: / bliÀêp / (AmE), / bliÀêp / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched electronic sound, used as a signal, warning, or to cover offensive language

Examples

A bleep sound
A loud bleep signaled that someone was trying to call on the walkie-talkie.
He woke up to the bleep of his alarm clock every morning.
The microwave made a bleep when the timer finished.
The interview was full of bleeps whenever someone used strong language.
A loud bleep interrupted the broadcast to censor the offensive word.
He laughed when the cartoon character's words were replaced by a bleep on TV.

Synonyms

  • beep
    1.0 5.58/m
    a beep is a short electronic signal used for alerts or confirmations, while a bleep often censors or covers offensive words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beep - a short, high sound as made by a horn or electronic device
  • synonym: buzz - a continuous low sound, often made by electronics
