The act of protecting against disease by introducing a vaccine into the body to induce immunity.

Examples

Mass vaccinating campaign
Vaccinating large segments of the population can help prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
Public health campaigns emphasize the importance of vaccinating children at an early age.
Schools often have policies regarding the vaccinating of students against common illnesses.

Synonyms

  • vaccination
    3.0 5.61/m
    vaccination names the formal procedure of giving a vaccine in clinics and records, while vaccinating refers to the action of giving a vaccine.
  • inoculation
    4.0 1.05/m
    inoculation is the medical act of introducing a vaccine or antigen into the body, while vaccinating means giving a vaccine to protect someone.
  • immunization
    3.7 2.62/m
    immunization is the process of becoming protected against a disease after a vaccine or treatment, while vaccinating is the act of giving a vaccine.
