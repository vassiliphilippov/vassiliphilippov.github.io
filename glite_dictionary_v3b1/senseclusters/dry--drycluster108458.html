dry

adjective
eaten without sauce or garnish
Pronunciation: / draɪ / (AmE), / draɪ / (BrE)
(of food) served or eaten without any added spread, sauce, or garnishing.

Examples

Dry toast
He prefers his toast dry, without any butter or jam.
Some people enjoy eating dry cereal as a snack.
The chicken was served dry, without any sauce or gravy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plain - (of food) not elaborate or dressed with sauce
