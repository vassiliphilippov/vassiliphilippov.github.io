premeiotic

adjective
before meiosis
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːmaɪˈɑːtɪk / (AmE), / ˌpriːmaɪˈɒtɪk / (BrE)
occurring or existing before the process of meiosis in cells

Examples

The premeiotic stage
The premeiotic stage prepares the cell for the complex process of meiosis.
Researchers observed changes in gene expression during the premeiotic phase.
Proper chromosome alignment is established in the premeiotic period of cell development.

Related Terms

  • antonym: meiotic - relating to or occurring during meiosis
