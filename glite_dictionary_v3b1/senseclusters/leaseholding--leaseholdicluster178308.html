leaseholding

noun
property under lease
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈliːs.hoʊ.dɪŋ/ /; / /liːs.ˈhoʊ.dɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈliːs.həʊ.dɪŋ/ /; / /liːs.ˈhəʊ.dɪŋ/ (BrE)
