intergrade

verb
to blend gradually
8.0
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ˈtɜːr.ɡreɪd/ /; / /ˈɪn.tər.ɡreɪd/ (AmE), /ɪn.ˈtɜː.ɡreɪd/ /; / /ˈɪn.tə.ɡreɪd/ (BrE)
to blend or merge gradually between two forms, species, or groups, creating intermediate types
