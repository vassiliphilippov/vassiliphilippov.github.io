Examples

Negative externality from pollution
Air pollution is a classic example of a negative externality caused by factories.
Vaccination can create a positive externality by protecting people who are not vaccinated.
The government introduced a tax to reduce the externality of carbon emissions.
Noise from the construction site is an externality that affects nearby residents.
Government policies often aim to reduce negative externalities like pollution.
