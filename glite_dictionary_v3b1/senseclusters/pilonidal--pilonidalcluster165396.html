pilonidal

adjective
relating to hair cysts
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪləˈnaɪdəl / (AmE), / ˌpɪləˈnaɪdəl / (BrE)
relating to or containing a nest of hairs, especially in a cyst near the tailbone

Examples

Pilonidal cyst

A pilonidal sinus may require minor surgery to remove the infected tissue.
