horny structure

noun
rigid body part made of keratin
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrni ˈstrʌktʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːni ˈstrʌktʃə / (BrE)
a rigid body part made primarily of keratin, such as a horn, nail, or hoof

Examples

A horny structure on animals
Bird beaks are another example of a horny structure in the animal kingdom.
In many mammals, hooves serve as a protective horny structure for the feet.
The rhinoceros's horn is a horny structure composed entirely of keratin fibers.
