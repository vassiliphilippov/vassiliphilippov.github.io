treadless

adjective
without grooves
7.1
Pronunciation: /ˈtred.ləs/ /; / /ˈtred.les/ (AmE), /ˈtred.ləs/ /; / /ˈtred.les/ (BrE)

Examples

After years of use, the bicycle's once-grippy tires became almost completely treadless.
