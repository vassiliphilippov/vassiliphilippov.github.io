treadless

adjective
without grooves
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɛdləs / (AmE), / ˈtrɛdləs / (BrE)
lacking a pattern or grooves on the surface, especially referring to a tire without tread

Examples

Treadless tire
It is unsafe to drive on treadless tires, especially in rainy weather.
The mechanic warned that the car's treadless tires needed to be replaced immediately.
After years of use, the bicycle's once-grippy tires became almost completely treadless.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bald - having no tread left, especially on a tire
  • antonym: treaded - having a pattern or grooves on the surface
