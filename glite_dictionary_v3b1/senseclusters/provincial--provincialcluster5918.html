provincial

adjective
narrow-minded or unsophisticated (from the provinces)
9.9 7.3
Pronunciation: / prəˈvɪnʃl̩ / (AmE), / prəˈvɪn.ʃəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of people from the provinces, especially suggesting a narrow or limited outlook; unsophisticated or lacking in urban polish.

Examples

Provincial attitude
He found the town charming but a bit provincial compared to the capital.
His views were criticized as provincial and lacking awareness of global issues.
Many city dwellers considered her tastes too provincial for the metropolitan art scene.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cosmopolitan - familiar with and at ease in many cultures; worldly
  • synonym: parochial - having a limited or narrow outlook or scope
