fish

noun
cold-blooded aquatic vertebrate
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /fɪʃ/ (AmE), /fɪʃ/ (BrE)
A cold-blooded aquatic animal that has a backbone, usually breathes through gills, and typically has fins and scales.

Examples
