riffler

noun
quick page turner
9.8
Pronunciation: /ˈrɪ.flər/ (AmE), /ˈrɪ.flə/ (BrE)
a person who quickly turns or flips through pages, papers, or a stack of cards, often without careful attention

Examples

Fast riffler of pages
The riffler barely glanced at each page before moving on to the next document.
As a habitual riffler, she often missed important details in her paperwork.
You could tell he was a riffler by the way he flipped through the cards so quickly.
