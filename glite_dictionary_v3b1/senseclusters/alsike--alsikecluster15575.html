alsike

noun
forage clover plant
Pronunciation: / ˈælˌsaɪk / (AmE), / ˈælˌsaɪk / (BrE)
a type of clover with pinkish-white flowers, grown for animal feed, especially in cooler northern regions

Examples

Alsike clover plant
The farmer planted alsike to provide nutritious feed for his cattle during the winter.
Fields of alsike bloom with pale pink flowers in the early summer months.
Because alsike grows well in wet soils, it is popular in northern pastures.
