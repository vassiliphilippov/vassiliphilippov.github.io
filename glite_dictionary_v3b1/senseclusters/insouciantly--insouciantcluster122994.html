insouciantly

adverb
carefree manner
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈsuːsiəntli / (AmE), / ɪnˈsuːsiəntli / (BrE)
in a relaxed, unconcerned, or carefree manner

Examples

To answer insouciantly
He answered the difficult question insouciantly, as if it required no thought at all.
The cat insouciantly stretched out on the warm windowsill, ignoring the noise outside.
She insouciantly waved away their concerns, confident everything would work out.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anxiously - in a worried or uneasy manner
  • synonym: nonchalantly - in a calm and unconcerned way
