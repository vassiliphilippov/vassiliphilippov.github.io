sirih

noun
betel leaf offering
9.9
Pronunciation: / siːˈriː / (AmE), / siːˈriː / (BrE)
a decorative arrangement or offering of betel leaves used in traditional ceremonies and social rituals, especially in Southeast Asia

Examples

Ceremonial sirih offering
During the engagement ceremony, the elders exchanged sirih as a symbol of respect and unity.
A beautifully arranged sirih was placed at the center of the table for the traditional celebration.
Guests admired the intricate patterns of the sirih offering at the cultural event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: betel leaf - the leaf of the betel plant, often used in rituals and for chewing
