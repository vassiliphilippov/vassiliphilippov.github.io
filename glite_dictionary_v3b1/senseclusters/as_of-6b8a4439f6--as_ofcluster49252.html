starting from a specified date or time
Pronunciation: / æz əv / (AmE), / æz əv / (BrE)
Starting from a particular date, time, or event mentioned.

Examples

As of January
As of January 1st, all products will include a warranty.
As of next Monday, the library will have new opening hours.
As of this morning, the weather forecast predicts rain for the weekend.
