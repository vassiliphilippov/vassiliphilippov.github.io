do a cannonball

verb
jump into water with a splash
Pronunciation: / du ə ˈkæn.ən.bɔl / (AmE), / duː ə ˈkæn.ən.bɔːl / (BrE)
to jump into water while pulling the knees to the chest so as to make a big splash

Examples

To do a cannonball dive
At the pool party, Tim decided to do a cannonball off the diving board.
Every summer, she loves to do a cannonball into the lake to cool off.
The children challenged each other to see who could do a cannonball and make the biggest splash.
