spinal anesthetic

noun
anesthetic injected into the spinal canal
Pronunciation: / ˈspaɪnl ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk /; / ˈspaɪnəl ænəsˈθɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˈspaɪnl ˌænɪsˈθɛtɪk /; / ˈspaɪnəl ˌanəsˈθɛtɪk / (BrE)
An anesthetic that is injected into the spinal canal to induce loss of sensation, typically in the lower body, during surgery or medical procedures.

The surgeon recommended a spinal anaesthetic for the knee replacement surgery.
