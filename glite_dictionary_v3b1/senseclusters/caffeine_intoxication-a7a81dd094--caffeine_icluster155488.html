caffeine intoxication

noun
condition from excessive caffeine intake
2.9 4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkæf.iːn ɪn.tɑːk.sɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈkæf.ɪn ɪn.tɑːk.sɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈkæf.iːn ɪn.tɒk.sɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ˈkæf.ɪn ɪn.tɒk.sɪ.ˈkeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a medical condition caused by consuming excessive amounts of caffeine, resulting in symptoms such as restlessness, insomnia, and rapid heartbeat

Examples

Caffeine intoxication symptoms
