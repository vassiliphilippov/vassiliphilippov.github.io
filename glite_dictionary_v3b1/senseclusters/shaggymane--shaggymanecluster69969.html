shaggymane

noun
edible mushroom with shaggy white cap
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈʃæɡiˌmeɪn / (AmE), / ˈʃaɡ.i.meɪn / (BrE)
A common edible mushroom with a tall, shaggy white cap that matures to release black spores.
