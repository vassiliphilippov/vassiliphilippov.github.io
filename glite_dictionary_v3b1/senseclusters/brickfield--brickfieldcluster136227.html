brickfield

noun
place where bricks are made
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɪkˌfiːld / (AmE), / ˈbrɪk.fiːld / (BrE)
an area or site where bricks are made and sometimes sold

Examples

A brickfield site
Regulations were introduced to reduce pollution from the brickfield on the outskirts of town.
She grew up near a noisy brickfield that operated day and night.
The old factory was once a bustling brickfield employing hundreds of local workers.
