overconscientious

adjective
excessively careful
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərkɑːnʃiˈɛnʃəs / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkɒnʃɪˈɛnʃəs / (BrE)
excessively careful to do what is right or to fulfill duties, often to an unnecessary degree

Examples

An overconscientious worker
Her overconscientious approach meant she spent hours double-checking every detail.
Being overconscientious can sometimes lead to unnecessary stress at work.
He is so overconscientious that he checks his work five times.

Related Terms

  • antonym: careless - not giving enough attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: meticulous - showing great attention to detail
