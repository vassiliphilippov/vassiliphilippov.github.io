hamsa

noun
sacred swan or goose
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌmsə / (AmE), / ˈhʌmsə / (BrE)
a swan or goose, especially in South Asian tradition, symbolizing purity, spiritual knowledge, or the soul

Examples

A hamsa bird
The ancient sculpture depicts a hamsa gliding gracefully across a lotus-filled pond.
In Hindu mythology, the hamsa is often associated with wisdom and purity.
The poet described the soul as a hamsa flying toward enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goose - a large waterbird with a long neck, related to the swan
  • synonym: swan - a large, graceful waterbird with a long neck
