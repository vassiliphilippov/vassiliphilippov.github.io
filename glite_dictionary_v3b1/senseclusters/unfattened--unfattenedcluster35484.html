unfattened

adjective
not fattened (especially livestock)
7.7 7.2
Pronunciation: /ʌn.ˈfæt.ənd/ /; / /ən.ˈfæt.ənd/ (AmE), /ʌn.ˈfæt.ənd/ /; / /ən.ˈfæt.ənd/ (BrE)
(of market animals) not having been fattened and therefore not in optimal condition for marketing.

Examples

An unfattened animal
Because they were unfattened, the lambs fetched a lower price at the market.
Only unfattened cattle are left in the pasture after the spring roundup.
The farmer decided not to sell the unfattened calves until they gained more weight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fattened - (of animals) having been made fat, usually to prepare for market
