grievant

adjective
causing sorrow
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːvənt / (AmE), / ˈɡriːvənt / (BrE)
causing or expressing grief, sorrow, or distress; grievous (chiefly archaic).

Examples

Grievant loss
They recalled the grievant losses from the war with heavy hearts.
The poem described a grievant farewell between old friends.
Such grievant news spread quickly through the small village.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grievous - causing great pain or suffering
  • antonym: joyful - full of happiness and joy
