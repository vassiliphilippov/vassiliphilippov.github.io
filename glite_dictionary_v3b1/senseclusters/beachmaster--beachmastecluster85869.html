beachmaster

noun
dominant male seal
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːtʃˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈbiːtʃˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a dominant male animal, especially a seal, that controls and defends a breeding area on a beach

Examples

Seal beachmaster
The beachmaster fought off several challengers to maintain his territory during the breeding season.
Only the strongest male becomes the beachmaster and mates with most of the females.
Researchers observed the beachmaster barking loudly to warn other males away from his section of the beach.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alpha male - the dominant male in a group of animals
