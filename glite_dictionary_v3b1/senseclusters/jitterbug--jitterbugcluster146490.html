jitterbug

verb
to dance the jitterbug
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪtərˌbʌɡ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪtəbʌɡ / (BrE)
to dance the jitterbug, a fast-paced dance popularized in the 1930s and 1940s, typically performed to swing music

Examples

To jitterbug to music
Every Saturday night, they would jitterbug until midnight at the old club.
The couples jitterbugged across the wooden floor to the lively swing band.
We learned how to jitterbug at the dance class last summer.
