acculturational

adjective
relating to acculturation
4.1
Pronunciation: / əˌkʌlt͡ʃəˈreɪʃənl̩ / (AmE), / əˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of acculturation; pertaining to the process by which individuals or groups adopt elements of another culture.

Examples

Acculturational changes
Language shifts are among the most prominent acculturational effects observed in multicultural societies.
The program supports students facing acculturational stress during their adaptation to a new culture.
The researchers examined acculturational changes within immigrant communities over several generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acculturation - the process of adopting the cultural traits of another group
