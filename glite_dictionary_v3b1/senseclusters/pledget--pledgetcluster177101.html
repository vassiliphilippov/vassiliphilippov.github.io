pledget

noun
absorbent medical pad
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛdʒɪt / (AmE), / ˈplɛdʒɪt / (BrE)
a small pad of absorbent material used in medicine or surgery to stop bleeding, protect a wound, or apply medication

Examples

A pledget of cotton
The nurse placed a pledget on the wound to absorb the blood.
After the injection, a small pledget was used to stop the bleeding.
Doctors often use a sterile pledget during minor surgical procedures.
The surgeon placed a pledget on the incision to absorb excess blood.
After cleaning the wound, the nurse applied a sterile pledget to protect it.
A small pledget soaked in medicine was used to treat the infected area.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bandage - a strip of material used to bind a wound or protect an injured part of the body
  • synonym: gauze - a thin, transparent fabric used for medical dressings
  • synonym: swab - a small piece of absorbent material used for cleaning or applying medicine
