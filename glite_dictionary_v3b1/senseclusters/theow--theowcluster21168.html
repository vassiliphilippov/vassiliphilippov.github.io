theow

noun
person under domination
9.9
Pronunciation: / θiːoʊ / (AmE), / θiːəʊ / (BrE)
a person who is completely controlled or dominated by something, such as a passion or habit (archaic, figurative)

Examples

Theow to desire

In the old tale, the hero was a theow of fate, powerless to change his destiny.
She warned him not to become a theow of his bad habits.
