Habenaria chlorantha

noun
a species of southern European orchid
Pronunciation: / ˌhæbəˈnɛriə kloʊˈrænθə / (AmE), / ˌhabəˈnɛːrɪə kləʊˈranθə / (BrE)
A species of orchid native to southern Europe, distinguished by its dark green, relatively large, less fragrant flowers, sometimes placed in the genus Habenaria.

Examples

Habenaria chlorantha orchid
Botanists often distinguish Habenaria chlorantha from similar orchids by its larger, greenish flowers.
The Habenaria chlorantha can be found in damp meadows throughout southern Europe.
Unlike some related species, Habenaria chlorantha is only mildly fragrant.
