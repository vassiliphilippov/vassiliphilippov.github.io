bitter lemon

noun
lemon-flavored carbonated drink
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪt̬ɚ ˈlɛmən / (AmE), / ˈbɪtə ˈlɛmən / (BrE)
a carbonated soft drink with a tart, slightly bitter lemon flavor, commonly served as a mixer or on its own

Examples

A bitter lemon drink
She ordered a bitter lemon with her lunch at the café.
Some people enjoy mixing gin with bitter lemon for a refreshing cocktail.
The bar offers both tonic water and bitter lemon as popular mixers.
