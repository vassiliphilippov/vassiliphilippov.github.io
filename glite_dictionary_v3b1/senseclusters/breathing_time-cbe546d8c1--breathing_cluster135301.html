breathing time

noun
short respite or pause for rest
Pronunciation: / ˈbriðɪŋ taɪm / (AmE), / ˈbriːðɪŋ taɪm / (BrE)
a short period of rest or relief before continuing with something difficult

Examples

A breathing time break
After hours of intense negotiation, both sides called for a breathing time to reconsider their positions.
She used the short breathing time between meetings to grab a coffee and collect her thoughts.
The pause gave the exhausted runners a much-needed breathing time before the final sprint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: respite - a short period of rest from something difficult or unpleasant
