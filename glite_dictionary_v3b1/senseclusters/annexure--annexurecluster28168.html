annexure

noun
attached building
Pronunciation: / əˈnɛkʃər / (AmE), / əˈnɛkʃə / (BrE)
a building or section that is joined to or associated with a larger main building; an annex.

Examples

Office annexure
The conference was held in the new annexure built behind the main office.
They converted the old storage area into an annexure for additional classrooms.
Guests were directed to the annexure for the evening reception.

Related Terms

  • synonym: annex - a building added to a main building
  • antonym: main building - the central or primary building to which others are attached
  • synonym: wing - a part of a building that sticks out from the main part
