dream

noun
someone or something wonderful
Pronunciation: / dɹim / (AmE), / dɹiːm / (BrE)

Examples

A dream job
He’s a dream to work with—always patient and enthusiastic.
That vintage car is an absolute dream for collectors.
The chocolate cake was a dream from the first bite to the last.
