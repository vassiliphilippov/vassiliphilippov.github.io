unnoticed

adjective
not noticed or observed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈnoʊtɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈnəʊtɪst / (BrE)
not seen, observed, or remarked upon; not noticed.

Examples

Go unnoticed
Her quiet entrance went completely unnoticed by the group.
Many small acts of kindness often go unnoticed.
The mistake on the form remained unnoticed for weeks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ignored - deliberately not noticed; disregarded
  • synonym: observed - noticed; seen or perceived
