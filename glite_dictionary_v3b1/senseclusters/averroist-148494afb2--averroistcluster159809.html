Averroist

noun
follower of Averroes
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈvɛroʊɪst / (AmE), / əˈvɛrəʊɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or follows the philosophical ideas of Averroes, especially his interpretations of Aristotle

Examples

An Averroist thinker
The professor was considered an Averroist because of his emphasis on reason in philosophy.
During the Middle Ages, many scholars identified as Averroists in academic debates.
Her research focused on the influence of Averroist thought in European universities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Aristotelian - a follower of Aristotle's philosophy
  • antonym: Platonist - a follower of Plato's philosophy
