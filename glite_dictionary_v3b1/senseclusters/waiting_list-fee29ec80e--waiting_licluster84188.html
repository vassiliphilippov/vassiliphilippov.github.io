waiting list

noun
list of people waiting for something
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪtɪŋ lɪst / (AmE), / ˈweɪtɪŋ lɪst / (BrE)
a list of people who are waiting for something that cannot be immediately obtained, such as a service, opportunity, or item

Examples

On the waiting list
After applying for the course, she found herself on the waiting list.
If there is a cancellation, someone from the waiting list will be offered the spot.
The hospital has a long waiting list for surgeries this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: priority list - a list giving precedence to certain people or items
  • synonym: queue - a line or sequence of people awaiting their turn
