dogsled

noun
sled pulled by dogs
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɡˌslɛd / (AmE), / ˈdɒɡˌslɛd / (BrE)
a sled that is pulled by dogs, typically used for travel or transport over snow and ice

Examples

A dogsled race
Children gathered to watch the dogsled team arrive at the winter festival.
Racing a dogsled requires both strength and skill from the musher and the dogs.
The explorers loaded their supplies onto the dogsled before setting out across the frozen landscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sled - a vehicle for traveling over snow, usually on runners
