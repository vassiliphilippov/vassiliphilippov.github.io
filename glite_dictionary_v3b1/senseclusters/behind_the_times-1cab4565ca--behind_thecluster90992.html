behind the times

adjective
outdated; not current
Pronunciation: / bɪˈhaɪnd ðə taɪmz / (AmE), / bɪˈhaɪnd ðə taɪmz / (BrE)
Not keeping up with current developments, styles, or knowledge; outdated.

Examples

Behind the times style
His refusal to use social media makes him seem behind the times to younger colleagues.
If we don't invest in new equipment, we'll soon be behind the times.
Many of the policies at the old firm are behind the times and need updating.

  • synonym: outdated - old-fashioned; no longer current
