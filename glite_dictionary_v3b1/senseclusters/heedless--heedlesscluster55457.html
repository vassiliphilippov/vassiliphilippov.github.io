heedless

adjective
not paying attention; careless
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhidləs / (AmE), / ˈhiːdləs / (BrE)
not paying attention or showing care; careless or unconcerned

Examples

Heedless of danger
He drove through the rain heedless of the slippery roads.
Her heedless remarks hurt his feelings more than she realized.
Many accidents happen because people are heedless of safety warnings.
Her heedless comments sometimes hurt people's feelings without her realizing it.
Ignoring all advice, he spent his money with heedless abandon.
The hikers acted in a heedless manner, wandering off the trail despite the warnings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attentive - paying close attention to something
  • synonym: careless - not giving sufficient attention or thought to avoiding harm or errors
  • synonym: reckless - without regard for danger or consequences
