eudemonic

adjective
promoting happiness and well-being
Pronunciation: / juːdɪˈmɑnɪk / (AmE), / juːdɪˈmɒnɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or producing happiness and well-being; conducive to human flourishing.

Examples

A eudemonic life
Many philosophers argue that a eudemonic life is one focused on personal growth and meaningful relationships.
Practicing gratitude has been shown to have a eudemonic effect on people's well-being.
The organization's mission is to create a eudemonic environment for all its employees.
