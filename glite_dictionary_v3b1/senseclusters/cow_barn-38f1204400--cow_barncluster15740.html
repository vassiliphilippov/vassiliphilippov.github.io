cow barn

noun
barn for keeping cows
1.2 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / ka ä b…ërn / (AmE), / ka ä b…ëÀên / (BrE)
a barn where cows are housed, especially for shelter or milking

Examples

The cow barn
A new ventilation system was installed in the cow barn to improve air quality for the animals.
During the storm, the cows were moved into the cow barn for safety.
The farmer cleaned the cow barn every morning before milking began.

Synonyms

  • cowshed
    2.4 0.06/m
    cowshed is a simple shelter for keeping and feeding cows, while cow barn often refers to a farm building used for milking and shelter.
  • byre
    9.9 0.08/m
    byre is an older British word for a small building that keeps cows, while cow barn means a farm building used for shelter and milking.
