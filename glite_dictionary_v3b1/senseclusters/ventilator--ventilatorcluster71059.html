ventilator

noun
device for air circulation
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛn.tɪˌleɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɛn.tɪ.leɪ.tə / (BrE)
A device or machine designed to circulate air, especially by introducing fresh air or removing stale air from a space.

Examples

Room ventilator
Please open the ventilator to let in some fresh air during the meeting.
The hospital installed a new ventilator system to improve air quality in the wards.
The old warehouse relied on a large ventilator mounted in the ceiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: air conditioner - device for cooling and circulating air
  • synonym: fan - device for producing a current of air
