sniffles

verb
to inhale through nose
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪfəlz / (AmE), / ˈsnɪfəlz / (BrE)
makes repeated small inhalations through the nose, usually because of a cold or to show sadness

Examples

He sniffles quietly
Whenever he catches a cold, he sniffles all day long.
She sniffles quietly during sad scenes in movies.
The child sniffles after playing outside in the chilly wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sniff - to draw air audibly through the nose
  • degree higher: sob - to cry noisily while taking short breaths
