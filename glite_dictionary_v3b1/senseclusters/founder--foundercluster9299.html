founder

noun
a worker making metal castings
Pronunciation: / ˈfaʊndər / (AmE), / ˈfaʊndə / (BrE)
a worker who casts metal by pouring it into molds

Examples

Skilled founder
As a founder, he specialized in creating intricate bronze sculptures.
Centuries ago, every village relied on a founder to make tools and weapons.
The experienced founder skillfully poured the molten iron into the sand molds.
