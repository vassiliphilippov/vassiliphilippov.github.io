Gustavus VI

proper noun
King of Sweden (1950–1973)
Pronunciation: / ɡʊˈstɑːvəs sɪksθ / (AmE), / ɡʊˈstɑːvəs sɪksθ / (BrE)
(1882–1973) King of Sweden from 1950 to 1973, the last monarch to hold significant political power in the country.

Examples

Gustavus VI King of Sweden
