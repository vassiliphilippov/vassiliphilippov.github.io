Irish setter

noun
a chestnut-brown Irish dog breed
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪrɪʃ ˌsɛtər / (AmE), / ˈaɪərɪʃ ˌsɛtə / (BrE)
a breed of dog from Ireland with a silky, chestnut-brown or mahogany-red coat, known for its friendly temperament and athletic build

Examples

An Irish setter dog
After much research, we decided to adopt an Irish setter puppy from a local breeder.
An Irish setter requires regular exercise and grooming to stay healthy and happy.
The Irish setter is known for its beautiful, glossy red coat and energetic nature.
