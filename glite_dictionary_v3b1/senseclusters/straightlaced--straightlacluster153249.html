straightlaced

adjective
excessively proper or strict
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (AmE), / ˈstreɪtˌleɪst / (BrE)
having very strict moral or social standards; excessively proper or conventional.

Examples

A straightlaced person
Despite his straightlaced reputation, he enjoys the occasional joke.
Her parents are so straightlaced that they won't let her go to parties.
The school's straightlaced dress code requires all students to wear uniforms.
