intraocular

adjective
within the eyeball
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈɑːkjələr / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəʊˈækjʊlə / (BrE)
located or occurring within the eyeball

Examples

An intraocular lens
The surgeon inserted an intraocular lens during the cataract operation.
Elevated intraocular pressure can be a sign of glaucoma.
The patient received an intraocular injection to treat the infection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: extraocular - situated or occurring outside the eyeball
  • synonym: ocular - relating to the eye
