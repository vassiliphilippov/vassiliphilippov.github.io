plotter

noun
computerized drawing device
Pronunciation: / ˈplɑːt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈplɒtə / (BrE)
a computer-controlled device that draws graphs, diagrams, or pictures on paper, typically used in engineering or design applications

Examples

Computer plotter
Before digital screens, a plotter was essential for creating large technical diagrams.
Our office upgraded to a new plotter that prints architectural drawings much faster.
The engineer used a plotter to produce detailed building plans.

Related Terms

  • synonym: printer - a device for printing text or pictures on paper
