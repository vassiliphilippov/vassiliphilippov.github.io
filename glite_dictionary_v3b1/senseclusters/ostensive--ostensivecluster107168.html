ostensive

adjective
showing or demonstrating clearly
Pronunciation: / ɑːˈstɛnsɪv / (AmE), / ɒˈstɛnsɪv / (BrE)
showing or demonstrating something clearly, especially by pointing it out or giving an explicit example; manifestly demonstrative

Examples

Ostensive gestures
Her ostensive gestures made her meaning clear to everyone in the audience.
Philosophers often use ostensive teaching to clarify difficult concepts.
The teacher gave an ostensive definition of the word by pointing to a real-life example.
