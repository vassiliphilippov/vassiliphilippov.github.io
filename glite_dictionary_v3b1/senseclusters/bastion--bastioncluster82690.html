bastion

noun
projecting part of a fortification
6.0 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈbæs.tʃən/ /; / /ˈbæs.tɪ.ən/ (AmE), /ˈbæs.tɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈbæs.tʃən/ (BrE)
a strong part of a fort or defensive wall that sticks out and is used for protection

Examples

Stone bastion
