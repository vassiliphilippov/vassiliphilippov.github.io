peroneus

noun
muscle of the lower leg
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / p…õr.o äÀàniÀê.…ôs / (AmE), / p…õr.…ôÀàniÀê.…ôs / (BrE)
A muscle of the lower leg that helps move the foot, especially in eversion and plantar flexion.

Examples

Peroneus muscle
An injury to the peroneus brevis can cause pain on the outer side of the leg.
Physical therapy may help strengthen the peroneus muscles after a sprained ankle.
The peroneus longus muscle plays a crucial role in stabilizing the foot during running.
