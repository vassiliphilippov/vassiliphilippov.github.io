Audenesque

adjective
in the style of W. H. Auden
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːdənˈɛsk / (AmE), / ˌɔːdənˈɛsk / (BrE)
Characteristic of or reminiscent of the style of W. H. Auden, especially in poetry.

Examples

Audenesque poetry style
Critics described his latest work as refreshingly Audenesque in both tone and structure.

The poem’s Audenesque qualities include precise rhyme and intellectual themes.
