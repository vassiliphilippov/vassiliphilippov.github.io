snap brim

noun
a hat brim that can be turned up or down
Pronunciation: / snæp brɪm / (AmE), / snæp brɪm / (BrE)
a hat brim that can be turned up on one side and down on the other, typically found on fedoras and similar hats

Examples

A snap brim hat
His fedora featured a stylish snap brim that he angled just above his eyes.
