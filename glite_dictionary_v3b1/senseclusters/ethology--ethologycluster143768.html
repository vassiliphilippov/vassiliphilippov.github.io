ethology

noun
study of animal behavior in the wild
Pronunciation: / ɪˈθɑlədʒi / (AmE), / iːˈθɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of animal behavior, especially as it occurs naturally in the wild

Examples

Modern ethology study
Modern ethology often involves long-term field studies to understand animal behavior.
She decided to pursue a career in ethology after observing wild chimpanzees in Africa.
The professor's research in ethology has shed light on the social structures of wolves.

  • synonym: zoology - the scientific study of animals
