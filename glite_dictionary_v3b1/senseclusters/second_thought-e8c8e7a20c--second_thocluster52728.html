second thought

noun
reconsideration after making a decision
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛkənd θɔt / (AmE), / ˈsek.ənd θɔːt / (BrE)
an act of reconsidering a decision or opinion after initially forming it

Examples

Second thoughts about decision
After accepting the job offer, he began to have second thoughts.
On second thought, I think I'll stay home tonight.
She made the purchase without second thought, but later regretted it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: doubt - feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction
  • degree higher: regret - a feeling of sadness or disappointment over something that has happened or been done
