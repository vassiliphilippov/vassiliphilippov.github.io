twitch

noun
sudden involuntary muscle movement
5.5 3.0
Pronunciation: /twɪtʃ/ (AmE), /twɪtʃ/ (BrE)
a sudden, small, involuntary muscle movement, often caused by nerves or stress

Examples

A muscle twitch
He felt a twitch in his eyelid right before the interview.
Her nervousness showed in the occasional twitch of her mouth.
The doctor explained that the twitch in my hand was nothing to worry about.
