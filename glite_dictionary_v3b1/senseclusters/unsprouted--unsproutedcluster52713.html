unsprouted

adjective
not germinated
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈspraʊtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈspraʊtɪd / (BrE)
not having begun to grow shoots or sprouts

Examples

The unsprouted seeds
After two weeks in the garden, the beans were still unsprouted.
Store the seeds in a cool, dry place to keep them unsprouted until planting season.
The package contained both sprouted and unsprouted grains for different recipes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: sprouted - having begun to grow shoots or sprouts
