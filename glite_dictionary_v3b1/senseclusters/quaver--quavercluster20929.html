quaver

noun
tremulous, wavering sound
4.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkweɪvər / (AmE), / ˈkweɪvə / (BrE)
A tremulous or wavering sound, especially in a person's voice, caused by nervousness or emotion.

Examples

A quaver in voice
The old man's quaver made his story even more moving.
There was a noticeable quaver in her voice as she spoke to the crowd.
With a quaver in his tone, he began his emotional speech.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tremor - a slight shaking or trembling movement or sound
  • synonym: vibration - a rapid rhythmic movement or sound
