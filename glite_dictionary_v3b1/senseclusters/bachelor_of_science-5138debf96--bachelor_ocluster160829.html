Bachelor of Science

noun
undergraduate degree in science
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbætʃələr əv ˈsaɪəns / (AmE), / ˈbætʃələ əv ˈsaɪəns / (BrE)
an undergraduate academic degree awarded for a course or program in science, typically taking three to five years to complete

Examples

A Bachelor of Science degree
After earning her Bachelor of Science, she went on to pursue a master's degree.
He received his Bachelor of Science in biology from a prestigious university.
Many companies require a Bachelor of Science for entry-level engineering positions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Bachelor of Arts - an undergraduate degree in arts or humanities
  • synonym: Bachelor’s degree - an undergraduate academic degree awarded by colleges and universities
