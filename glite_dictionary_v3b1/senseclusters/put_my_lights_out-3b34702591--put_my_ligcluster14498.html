put my lights out

verb
knock out or render unconscious/asleep
Pronunciation: / pʊt maɪ laɪts aʊt / (AmE), / pʊt maɪ laɪts aʊt / (BrE)
to make someone fall asleep or become unconscious; to render someone unable to function, typically suddenly or forcefully

Examples

To put my lights out suddenly
Listening to the rain last night put my lights out almost instantly.
One hard punch to the jaw would have put my lights out for sure.
The medication was so strong it put my lights out within minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock out - to make unconscious, especially by a blow
  • antonym: wake up - to cause to stop sleeping or regain consciousness
