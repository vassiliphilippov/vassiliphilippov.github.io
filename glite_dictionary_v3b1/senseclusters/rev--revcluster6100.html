rev

verb
increase engine speed
4.9 0.84/m
Pronunciation: / rÉ›v / (AmE), / rÉ›v / (BrE)
(of an engine or vehicle) to increase the running speed of the engine, especially by pressing the accelerator.

Examples

To rev the engine

She likes to rev the engine before taking off down the road.
The mechanic told me not to rev the car when it's cold.
