iridaceous

adjective
relating to the iris plant family
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈdeɪʃəs / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈdeɪʃəs / (BrE)
of or relating to plants of the family Iridaceae, such as irises and related species

Examples

Iridaceous flowers
Many gardens feature iridaceous flowers such as irises and crocuses for their vibrant colors.
Soil conditions must be well-drained to support most iridaceous species.
The botanist specialized in iridaceous plants native to temperate regions.
