mango tree

noun
evergreen tree producing mango fruit
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡoʊ tri / (AmE), / ˈmaŋɡəʊ triː / (BrE)
A large evergreen tropical tree (Mangifera indica) cultivated for its sweet, juicy, oval fruit called mango.

Examples

A mango tree fruit
The farmer planted a young mango tree in the orchard last spring.
Their backyard boasts three mango trees that produce delicious fruit every summer.
Under the shade of the old mango tree, children gathered to play during the hot afternoon.
