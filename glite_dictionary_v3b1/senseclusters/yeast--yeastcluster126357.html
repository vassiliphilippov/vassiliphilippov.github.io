yeast

noun
substance for bread rising and fermentation
Pronunciation: / jiːst /; / jist / (AmE), / jiːst / (BrE)
a type of fungus used in baking and brewing to make dough rise or to ferment drinks

Examples

Fresh yeast for bread
She bought fresh yeast from the bakery to make bread at home.
The yeast ferments the sugars in the wort to produce beer.
You need to add yeast to the dough to make it rise.
Brewers rely on yeast to convert sugars into alcohol during fermentation.
Some people are allergic to yeast and avoid foods containing it.
The bread rose quickly because the yeast was very active.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baking powder - a chemical leavening agent used in baking
  • synonym: fungus - any of a group of spore-producing organisms feeding on organic matter
  • synonym: sourdough - fermented dough used as leaven in baking
