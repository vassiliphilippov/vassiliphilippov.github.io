mist

verb
spray or cover with fine droplets
Pronunciation: / mɪst / (AmE), / mɪst / (BrE)
to spray fine droplets of liquid onto something, especially to moisten, cool, or clean it

Examples

To mist plants

She used a bottle to mist the delicate leaves every morning.
The florist gently misted the flowers to keep them fresh.
They were misting the seedlings every morning to keep the soil damp and healthy.
The florist gently misted the flowers to keep them fresh throughout the day.
Please mist the leaves with water to increase the humidity for the plant.
