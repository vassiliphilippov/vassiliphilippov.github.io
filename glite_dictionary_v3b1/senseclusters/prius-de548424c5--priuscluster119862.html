Prius

proper noun
Toyota hybrid car
Pronunciation: / ˈpriːəs / (AmE), / ˈpriːəs / (BrE)
a hybrid car model manufactured by Toyota, known for its fuel efficiency and low emissions

Examples

A Prius car
She bought a Prius because she wanted a more environmentally friendly vehicle.
Many taxi companies use the Prius for its excellent fuel economy.
His family has driven a Prius for over ten years without major issues.
