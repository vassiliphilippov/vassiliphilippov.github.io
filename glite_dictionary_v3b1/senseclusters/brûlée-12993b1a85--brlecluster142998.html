brûlée

adjective
caramelized surface
Pronunciation: / bruːˈleɪ / (AmE), / bruːˈleɪ / (BrE)
having a surface that is burned or caramelized, especially by applying heat to create a crisp, browned layer on food

Examples

Brulee sugar topping
The chef presented a brûlée apple tart with a perfectly crisp sugar topping.
For dessert, we enjoyed brûlée bananas served with vanilla ice cream.
She prefers her oatmeal with a brûlée sugar crust for extra crunch.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: burnt - damaged or blackened by burning
  • synonym: caramelized - having sugar browned by heat
