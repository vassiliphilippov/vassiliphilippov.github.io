skylounge

noun
high-floor lounge
Pronunciation: / ˈskaɪˌlaʊndʒ / (AmE), / ˈskaɪˌlaʊndʒ / (BrE)
a comfortable lounge area located on a high floor, often featuring large windows and scenic views

Examples

The skylounge area
The hotel’s skylounge offers breathtaking views of the city at night.
We relaxed in the skylounge after a long day of meetings.
Many guests gather in the skylounge to watch the sunset over the skyline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lobby lounge - a lounge area located in the lobby of a hotel or building
  • synonym: rooftop bar - a bar located on the roof of a building, often with views
