barbecue pit

noun
outdoor pit for barbecuing meat
3.0 2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑːr.bɪ.kjuː pɪt / (AmE), / ˈbɑː.bɪ.kjuː pɪt / (BrE)
a hole or constructed area in the ground where wood or charcoal is burned to create hot coals for cooking meat outdoors

Examples

A barbecue pit outdoors
After digging a barbecue pit, they lined it with stones and filled it with hot coals.
The smell of smoke drifted from the backyard barbecue pit all afternoon.
We roasted a whole pig in the barbecue pit for the annual family gathering.
