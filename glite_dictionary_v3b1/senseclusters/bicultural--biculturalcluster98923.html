bicultural

adjective
involving two cultures
5.0
Pronunciation: / baɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / baɪˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving two different cultures

Examples

Bicultural family life
Growing up in a bicultural household helped him appreciate both his parents' traditions.
The school offers a bicultural curriculum to support students from diverse backgrounds.
Her bicultural identity allows her to navigate both American and Japanese customs easily.
