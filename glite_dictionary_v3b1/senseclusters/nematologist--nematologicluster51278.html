nematologist

noun
nematode scientist
Pronunciation: / ˌnɛməˈtɑlədʒɪst / (AmE), / ˌniːməˈtɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a scientist who studies nematodes, which are microscopic roundworms

Examples

A nematologist studies worms
The nematologist discovered a new species of roundworm in the rainforest soil.
Farmers often consult a nematologist to help control crop-damaging nematodes.
After years of research, the nematologist published her findings in a scientific journal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parasitologist - a scientist who studies parasites
  • synonym: zoologist - a scientist who studies animals
