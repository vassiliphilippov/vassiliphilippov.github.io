A small evergreen tree (Arbutus unedo) native to Ireland, southern Europe, and Asia Minor, with scarlet berries that are edible but generally bland.

Examples

The Irish strawberry tree
Although the Irish strawberry is edible, its berries are not as sweet as common strawberries.
He mistook the Irish strawberry for a traditional strawberry due to its color.
The garden featured an Irish strawberry tree with clusters of bright red fruit.
