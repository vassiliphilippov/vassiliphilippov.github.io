austere

adjective
severely simple in style or appearance
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈstɪr / (AmE), / ɔːˈstɪə / (BrE)
having a plain and simple style or appearance; without unnecessary decoration or luxury

Examples

Austere room
Her austere bedroom had plain white walls and no pictures or ornaments.
The austere furnishings gave the office a cold, businesslike feel.
The house was decorated in an austere style with only the essentials on display.

Related Terms

  • antonym: elaborate - involving many details and complicated design
  • synonym: plain - simple or ordinary in character or appearance
