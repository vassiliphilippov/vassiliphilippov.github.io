whichever

pronoun
any one from a group
Pronunciation: / wɪˈtʃɛvər / (AmE), / wɪˈtʃɛvə / (BrE)
used to refer to any one person or thing from a group, without specifying which.

Examples

Pick whichever you like
You can choose whichever you like from the menu.
Take whichever of these books interests you the most.
I will support whichever decision you make.

Related Terms

  • synonym: whatever - any thing or amount from a group
  • synonym: whoever - any person from a group
