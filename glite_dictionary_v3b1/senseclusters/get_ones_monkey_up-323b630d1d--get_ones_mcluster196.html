get one's monkey up

verb
become angry or upset
7.5
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt wʌnz ˈmʌŋki ʌp / (AmE), / ɡɛt wʌnz ˈmʌŋki ʌp / (BrE)

Examples

He tends to get his monkey up when people criticize his work.
