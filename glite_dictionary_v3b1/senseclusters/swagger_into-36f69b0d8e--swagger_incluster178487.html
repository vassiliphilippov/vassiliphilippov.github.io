swagger into

verb
enter confidently and arrogantly
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈswæɡɚ ˈɪntu / (AmE), / ˈswæɡə ˈɪntuː / (BrE)
to enter a place in a very confident and often arrogant way

Examples

To swagger into a room
He swaggered into the boardroom as if he already owned the company.
She swaggered into the club, turning heads with her confidence.
The star athlete swaggered into the locker room, basking in his team's praise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: march into - to walk into a place in a determined or forceful way
  • antonym: slip into - to enter quietly or unobtrusively
  • synonym: strut into - to walk into a place in a proud or confident way
