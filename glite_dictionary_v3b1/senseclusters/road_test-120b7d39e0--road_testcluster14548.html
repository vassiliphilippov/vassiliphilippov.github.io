road test

noun
official test of vehicle roadworthiness
4.9
Pronunciation: / roʊd tɛst / (AmE), / rəʊd tɛst / (BrE)
an official test to check whether a vehicle is safe and suitable for use on the road

Examples

Pass a road test
Before a used car can be sold, it usually has to pass a road test.
She was nervous during the road test required for her driving license.
The mechanic performed a road test to ensure the car was functioning properly.
