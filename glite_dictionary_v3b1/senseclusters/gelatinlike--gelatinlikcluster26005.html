gelatinlike

adjective
thick and jelly-like
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛlətənˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛlətɪnˌlaɪk / (BrE)
Having a thick, semi-solid consistency similar to that of gelatin.

Examples

Gelatinlike texture
Some marine creatures have a gelatinlike body that helps them float.
The dessert had a strange, gelatinlike texture that many found unappealing.
The soup cooled into a gelatinlike mass overnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gelatinous - having the consistency of gelatin or jelly
