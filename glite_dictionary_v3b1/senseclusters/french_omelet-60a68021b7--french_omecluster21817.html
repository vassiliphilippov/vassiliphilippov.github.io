French omelet

noun
lightly cooked, folded omelet
Pronunciation: / ˌfrɛntʃ ˈɑːmlət / (AmE), / ˌfrɛntʃ ˈɒmlət / (BrE)
An omelet made by quickly cooking beaten eggs and sliding them onto a plate, producing a soft, lightly folded form.

Examples

A classic French omelet
A classic French omelet is known for its tender, creamy interior.
She watched a video to learn how to fold a proper French omelet.
The chef expertly prepared a French omelet with chives and Gruyère cheese.
