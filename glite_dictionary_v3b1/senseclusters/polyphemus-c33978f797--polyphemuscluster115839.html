Polyphemus

noun
North American silk moth
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːlɪˈfiːməs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈfiːməs / (BrE)
a large North American silk moth with brown wings and prominent eye-like spots, scientifically known as Antheraea polyphemus

Examples

Polyphemus moth
A Polyphemus moth clung to the window, its large eyespots visible even from a distance.
The biology class studied the life cycle of the Polyphemus moth in the school laboratory.
She was amazed to find a Polyphemus resting on her garden fence one morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: luna moth - a large pale green North American moth related to the Polyphemus moth
