SIGINT

noun
intelligence from intercepted electronic signals
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.ɪnt / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.ɪnt / (BrE)
Intelligence information collected by intercepting and analyzing electronic signals, such as communications and electronic transmissions.

Examples

SIGINT intelligence gathering
Advances in technology have greatly increased the scope of SIGINT operations.
She specialized in SIGINT analysis during her career with the intelligence agency.
The military relies heavily on SIGINT to monitor enemy communications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: COMINT - intelligence obtained from intercepted communications
  • synonym: ELINT - intelligence derived from electronic signals other than communication
