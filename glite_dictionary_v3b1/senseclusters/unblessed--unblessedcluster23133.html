unblessed

adjective
lacking something desirable
4.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈblɛst / (AmE), / ʌnˈblɛst / (BrE)
lacking something considered desirable or fortunate; not endowed with a desired quality or possession.

Examples

An unblessed life
He felt unblessed by luck throughout his difficult journey.
The village remained unblessed with clean drinking water for years.
Their small town was unblessed by a hospital or regular medical care.
