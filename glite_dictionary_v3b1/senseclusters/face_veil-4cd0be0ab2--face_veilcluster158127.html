face veil

noun
semi-transparent covering for the face
Pronunciation: / ˈfeɪs veɪl / (AmE), / ˈfeɪs veɪl / (BrE)
A piece of semi-transparent material worn to cover the face, often for religious or cultural reasons.

Examples

A face veil worn
Many women wear a face veil as part of their traditional attire.
She lifted her face veil to greet the visitors politely.
The museum displayed a 19th-century face veil made of delicate lace.

Related Terms

  • synonym: veil - a piece of fine material worn by women to protect or conceal the face
