air shaft

noun
vertical duct for ventilation

a vertical passage or duct in a building, mine, or other structure that allows air to circulate for ventilation purposes

Examples

An air shaft in building
Miners built an air shaft to improve ventilation deep underground.
The engineers checked the air shaft monthly to make sure it was clear of debris.
The old apartment building uses an air shaft to bring fresh air to the inner rooms.

Synonyms

  • air well
    7.4 0.01/m
    an air well is a vertical shaft in a building that draws cool air, while an air shaft is a general ventilation passage.
  • ventilation shaft
    4.5 0.13/m
    a ventilation shaft is a passage that can be vertical or horizontal to move fresh air, while an air shaft is usually vertical.
  • airway
    4.5 0.04/m
    an airway is a duct used to give ventilation in mines and tunnels, while an air shaft is a vertical passage in buildings.
  • air passage
    3.5 0.23/m
    an air passage is a general channel for moving air in buildings or mines, while an air shaft is a specifically vertical ventilation shaft.
