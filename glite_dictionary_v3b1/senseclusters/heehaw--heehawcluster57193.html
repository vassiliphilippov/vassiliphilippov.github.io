heehaw

verb
to laugh loudly
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhiˌhɔ / (AmE), / ˈhiːˌhɔː / (BrE)
to make a loud, harsh laugh or sound similar to a donkey's bray

Examples

To heehaw loudly
The children heehawed at the clown's silly dance during the party.
He heehawed so loudly that everyone in the restaurant turned to look.
Whenever she hears a really funny joke, she can't help but heehaw with laughter.
The children began to heehaw when they heard the clown's silly joke.
He couldn't stop heehawing at the ridiculous scene in the movie.
Her loud heehaw echoed through the quiet library, making everyone turn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bray - to make a loud, harsh cry like a donkey
  • antonym: giggle - to laugh in a light, silly way
  • synonym: guffaw - to laugh loudly and coarsely
  • antonym: snicker - to laugh quietly in a partly suppressed way
