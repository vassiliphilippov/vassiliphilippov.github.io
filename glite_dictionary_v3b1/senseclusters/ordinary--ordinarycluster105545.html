ordinary

adjective
common or usual; not special
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdəˌnɛri / (AmE), / ˈɔːdnəri / (BrE)
not unusual or special; normal, average, or commonly encountered

Examples

Ordinary people
For an ordinary day, she always wears comfortable clothes.
It's just an ordinary sandwich, nothing special about it.
The performance was rather ordinary and did not impress the judges.
Despite his fame, he prefers to lead an ordinary life.
She wore a simple, ordinary dress to the event.
The meal was fairly ordinary, without any special flavors or presentation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: average - typical; not out of the ordinary
  • synonym: common - usual, not different from others, easily found
  • antonym: extraordinary - very unusual or remarkable
