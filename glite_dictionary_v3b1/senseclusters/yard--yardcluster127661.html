yard

noun
horizontal pole for supporting a sail
1.4 7.2
Pronunciation: /jɑːrd/ (AmE), /jɑːd/ (BrE)
a long horizontal pole on a mast, from which a square sail or lateen is set and spread

Examples

A ship's yard
Each yard was carefully secured to the mast with strong ropes.
Sailors climbed the rigging to furl the sail on the upper yard.
The main yard was lowered to reduce the ship’s sail area in the strong wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boom - a spar used to extend the foot of a sail
  • degree higher: mast - vertical pole on a ship that supports sails
