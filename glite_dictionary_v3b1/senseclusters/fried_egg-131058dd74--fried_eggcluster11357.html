fried egg

noun
egg cooked by frying in oil or butter
1.7 1.0
Pronunciation: / fraɪd ɛɡ / (AmE), / fraɪd ɛɡ / (BrE)
An egg cooked by frying it in oil or butter, usually until the white is set and the yolk remains intact.

Examples

A fried egg breakfast
He ordered a fried egg with toast for breakfast.
I prefer my fried egg with a runny yolk.
The chef flipped the fried egg to cook it on both sides.
