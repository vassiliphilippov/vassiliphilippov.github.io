floccose

adjective
covered in soft woolly tufts (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈflɑːˌkoʊs / (AmE), / ˈflɒk.əʊs / (BrE)
(of plants) covered with or bearing tufts of soft, woolly hairs.

Examples

A floccose plant
Botanists noted the floccose covering on the underside of the flower.
Some mosses exhibit a distinctively floccose texture on their leaves.
The stem of the plant appeared floccose under the microscope.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tomentose - (of plants) covered with densely matted hairs
