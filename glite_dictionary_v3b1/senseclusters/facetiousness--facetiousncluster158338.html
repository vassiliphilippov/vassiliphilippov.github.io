facetiousness

noun
playful or inappropriate humor
6.4
Pronunciation: / fəˈsiːʃəsnəs / (AmE), / fəˈsiːʃəsnɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being humorous or treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate or playful humor

Examples

Her facetiousness in meetings
Critics complained that her facetiousness was inappropriate during the meeting.
His facetiousness sometimes made it hard for people to know when he was being sincere.
The teacher's facetiousness lightened the mood even during difficult lessons.

Related Terms

  • synonym: humor - the quality of being amusing, comic, or funny
  • antonym: seriousness - the state of being serious and not joking
