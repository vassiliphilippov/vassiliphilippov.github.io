taxonomist

noun
designer of information taxonomies
8.9

(in information management) a professional who designs, implements, and maintains taxonomies or controlled vocabularies to organize digital content, metadata, or information for indexing, search, and retrieval.

Examples

Digital taxonomist
The company hired a taxonomist to design its internal taxonomy for the customer support knowledge base.
Experienced taxonomists helped tag thousands of documents to improve search accuracy across the archive.
During the migration, our librarian worked with a taxonomist to map inconsistent metadata fields.

Related Terms

  • synonym: metadata specialist - someone who defines metadata schemas and standards to describe and manage digital resources
