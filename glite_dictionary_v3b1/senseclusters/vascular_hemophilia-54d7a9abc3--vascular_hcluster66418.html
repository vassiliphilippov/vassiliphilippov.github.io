a rare inherited bleeding disorder caused by a deficiency of a coagulation factor, leading to prolonged mucosal bleeding; also known as von Willebrand disease.

Examples

Vascular hemophilia bleeding disorder
Genetic testing can confirm a case of vascular hemophilia in patients with unexplained prolonged bleeding.
The doctor diagnosed her with vascular hemophilia after repeated episodes of nosebleeds and easy bruising.
Unlike classic hemophilia, vascular hemophilia often presents with bleeding from the mucous membranes.

Synonyms
