American laurel

noun
North American evergreen shrub
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈlɔrəl / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈlɔːrəl / (BrE)
a North American evergreen shrub with glossy leaves and white or pink flowers, commonly found in woodland areas

Examples

The American laurel shrub
Hikers admired the clusters of white blossoms on the American laurel along the trail.

Wild American laurel thrives in the understory of eastern U.S. forests.
