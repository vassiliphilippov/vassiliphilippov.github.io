extraction

noun
process of obtaining a substance from a mixture
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstræk.ʃən / (AmE), / ɪkˈstræk.ʃən / (BrE)
the process of obtaining a substance from a mixture or compound by chemical, physical, or mechanical means

Examples

Chemical extraction process

The extraction of caffeine from coffee beans is a common industrial process.
The laboratory focused on the extraction of DNA from blood samples.
