massager

noun
person giving massages
1.3
Pronunciation: / məˈsɑdʒɚ / (AmE), / məˈsɑːdʒə / (BrE)
a person who rubs or kneads parts of the body to stimulate circulation or promote relaxation

Examples

Professional massager
After a stressful week, she visited her favorite massager for a relaxing session.
I booked an appointment with a skilled massager to help relieve my back pain.
The hotel spa employs a massager who is highly recommended by guests.
