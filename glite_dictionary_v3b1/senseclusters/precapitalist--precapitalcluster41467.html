precapitalist

noun
person before capitalism
9.6
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.ɪst/ /; / /prə.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.ɪst/ (AmE), /priː.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.ɪst/ /; / /prə.ˈkæp.ɪ.təl.ɪst/ (BrE)
a person who lived in or belonged to a society before the development of capitalism

Examples

A precapitalist farmer
A precapitalist often relied on traditional methods of farming and local trade.
The museum exhibit showed how a typical precapitalist community organized its economy.
Historians study the life of a precapitalist to understand early social structures.
