glottochronology

noun
study of language divergence timing
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɡlɑːtoʊkrəˈnɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌɡlɒtəʊkrəˈnɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the study of the time of divergence between related languages by comparing their core vocabularies

Examples

Glottochronology study
A team of researchers used glottochronology to estimate the age of the Austronesian language family.
Recent advances in glottochronology have challenged traditional views about language evolution.
She specialized in glottochronology and analyzed how Indo-European languages diverged over millennia.
