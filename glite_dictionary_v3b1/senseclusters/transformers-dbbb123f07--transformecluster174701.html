Transformers

proper noun
robot franchise
Pronunciation: / trænzˈfɔrmərz / (AmE), / trænsˈfɔːməz / (BrE)
a popular franchise featuring fictional robots that can change into vehicles or other objects

Examples

The Transformers franchise
The Transformers franchise includes movies, cartoons, and action figures enjoyed worldwide.
Many children collect Transformers toys that change from robots into cars or planes.
The first Transformers film was released in 2007 and became a box office hit.
