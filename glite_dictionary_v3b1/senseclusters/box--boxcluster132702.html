box

noun
amount contained in a box
1.1
Pronunciation: / bɑks / (AmE), / bɒks / (BrE)
a container, usually rectangular and often with a lid, used for holding or storing things, or the amount it holds

Examples

A box of toys
Only one box of cereal was left on the shelf.
She sent me a box of her favorite teas from London.
We bought a box of cookies for the road trip.
Can you help me carry that box into the garage?
He opened the box to reveal a set of old postcards.
She kept her childhood drawings in a large box under the bed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bag - a container made of flexible material, used for holding things
  • synonym: carton - a light box or container, usually made of cardboard
  • synonym: case - a container used to hold or protect something
  • synonym: packet - the quantity contained in a packet
