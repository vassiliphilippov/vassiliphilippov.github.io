box

noun
amount contained in a box
1.1 1.0
Pronunciation: / bɑks / (AmE), / bɒks / (BrE)
a container, usually rectangular and often with a lid, used for holding or storing things, or the amount it holds

Examples

A box of toys

We bought a box of cookies for the road trip.
Can you help me carry that box into the garage?
He opened the box to reveal a set of old postcards.
She kept her childhood drawings in a large box under the bed.
