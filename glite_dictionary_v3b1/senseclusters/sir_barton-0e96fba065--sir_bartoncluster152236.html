Sir Barton

proper noun
first horse to win the American Triple Crown (1919)
Pronunciation: / sɜr ˈbɑrtən / (AmE), / sɜː ˈbɑːtən / (BrE)
the thoroughbred racehorse that won the American Triple Crown in 1919, becoming the first horse to do so.
