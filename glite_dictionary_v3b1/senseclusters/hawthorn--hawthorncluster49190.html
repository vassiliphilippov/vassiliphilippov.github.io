hawthorn

noun
a spring-flowering shrub or small tree (Crataegus)
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔːˌθɔrn / (AmE), / ˈhɔːˌθɔːn / (BrE)
a small tree or shrub of the genus Crataegus, with white or pink spring flowers and red fruits, often found in hedgerows

Examples

A hawthorn tree
Birds often make their nests among the dense branches of the old hawthorn.
The hawthorn in the garden is covered in white blossoms every spring.
We picked red berries from the wild hawthorn for making jelly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mayflower - another name for the hawthorn tree, especially in reference to its blossom
