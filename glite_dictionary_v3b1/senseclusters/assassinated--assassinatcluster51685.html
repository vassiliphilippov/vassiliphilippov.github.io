assassinated

adjective
murdered by sudden attack (often political)
Pronunciation: / əˈsæsəˌneɪtɪd / (AmE), / əˈsæsɪneɪtɪd / (BrE)
Murdered suddenly and often for political or religious reasons, usually by a surprise attack.

Examples

An assassinated leader
He became famous after writing a biography about a assassinated journalist.
Several assassinated leaders are remembered as martyrs in their countries' histories.
The president was assassinated while attending a public event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: murdered - killed deliberately by another person
  • antonym: protected - defended from harm or attack
