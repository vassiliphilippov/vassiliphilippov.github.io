quizzer

noun
a person who gives a quiz or test
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪzər / (AmE), / ˈkwɪzə / (BrE)
a person who administers a quiz or test, especially to determine someone's knowledge or qualifications

Examples

The quizzer asks questions
As the official quizzer, she asked the participants ten challenging questions.
Our weekly trivia night always has a different quizzer leading the event.
The quizzer handed out the tests to each student in the classroom.
