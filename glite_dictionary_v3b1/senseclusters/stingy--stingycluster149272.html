stingy

adjective
unwilling to spend; miserly
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪndʒi / (AmE), / ˈstɪndʒi / (BrE)
Unwilling to spend money, time, or resources; mean or miserly.

Examples

Stingy with money
Despite being wealthy, she was surprisingly stingy with her donations.
Don't be stingy with your compliments when someone does a good job.
My landlord is so stingy that he refuses to turn on the heating in winter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give or share; opposite of stingy
  • synonym: mean - unwilling to spend money; similar to stingy
  • synonym: miserly - showing extreme unwillingness to spend money
