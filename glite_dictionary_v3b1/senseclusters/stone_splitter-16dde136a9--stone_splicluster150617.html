stone splitter

noun
machine for splitting stone
3.5
Pronunciation: / stoʊn ˈsplɪtər / (AmE), / stəʊn ˈsplɪtə / (BrE)
a machine used to split natural stone, such as granite, marble, or sandstone, into pieces

Examples

A stone splitter machine
A skilled operator can use a stone splitter to produce slabs of uniform thickness.
Modern stone splitters can handle massive blocks of granite with ease.
The quarry invested in a new stone splitter to increase production efficiency.
