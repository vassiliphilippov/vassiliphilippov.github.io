harp

noun
curved supports for a lampshade
8.6 7.4
Pronunciation: /hɑːrp/ (AmE), /hɑːp/ (BrE)
A pair of curved metal supports that hold a lampshade in place, typically attaching to the lamp base and fitting around the bulb socket.

Examples

Lamp harp replacement
She replaced the old harp with a sturdier one to fit her new lampshade.
The lampshade won't stay up unless you fix the harp.
Without a proper harp, the lampshade kept slipping off the lamp.
