Aedes

noun
a genus of disease-carrying mosquitoes
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈiː.diːz/ /; / /ˈeɪ.diːz/ (AmE), /ˈiː.diːz/ /; / /ˈeɪ.diːz/ (BrE)
