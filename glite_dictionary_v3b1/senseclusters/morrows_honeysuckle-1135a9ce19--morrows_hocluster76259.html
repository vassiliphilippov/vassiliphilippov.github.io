Morrow's honeysuckle

noun
species of honeysuckle shrub from Japan
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːroʊz ˈhʌniˌsʌkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɒrəʊz ˈhʌniˌsʌkl̩ / (BrE)
A grey deciduous honeysuckle shrub (Lonicera morrowii) native to Japan, with paired white flowers that turn yellow.

Examples
