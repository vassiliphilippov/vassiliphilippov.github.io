playfellow

noun
companion in play
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈpleɪˌfɛloʊ / (AmE), / ˈpleɪˌfeləʊ / (BrE)
a companion, especially a child, with whom one plays

Examples

A playfellow friend
As a child, Lily was rarely seen without her favorite playfellow by her side.
During recess, each playfellow found a partner for the game of tag.
He missed his old playfellow after moving to a new city.
