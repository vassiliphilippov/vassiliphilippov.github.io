finesse

noun
delicate skill in handling situations
Pronunciation: / fəˈnɛs / (AmE), / fɪˈnɛs / (BrE)
delicate and skillful handling of a difficult or sensitive situation

Examples

Diplomatic finesse
She handled the negotiations with remarkable finesse, impressing everyone involved.
The chef decorated the cake with the finesse of a true artist.
Winning the customer’s trust required a great deal of finesse and patience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clumsiness - lack of skill or finesse in handling situations
  • synonym: tact - skill and sensitivity in dealing with others
