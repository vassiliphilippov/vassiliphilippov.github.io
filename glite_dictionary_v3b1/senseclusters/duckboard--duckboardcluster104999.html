duckboard

noun
slatted walkway over wet ground
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌkˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdʌkˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A slatted wooden or metal walkway laid over muddy or wet ground to provide a dry path.

Examples

A duckboard walkway
During the rainy season, the only way in was via the old duckboard.
Soldiers carefully crossed the duckboard to avoid sinking into the mud.
The hiking trail included a long duckboard section through the marsh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boardwalk - a wooden walkway, typically along a beach or over wet ground
