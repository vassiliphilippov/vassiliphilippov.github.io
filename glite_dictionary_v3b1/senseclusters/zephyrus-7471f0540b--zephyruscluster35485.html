Zephyrus

noun
gentle west wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛfɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈzɛfɪrəs / (BrE)
the gentle west wind, especially as personified in Greek mythology or used in poetry and literature

Examples

Zephyrus brings spring
In the ancient poem, Zephyrus brought the promise of spring to the land.
A soft Zephyrus cooled the garden as the sun set behind the hills.
Artists often depict Zephyrus as a youthful figure blowing gentle winds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breeze - a gentle wind
  • antonym: gale - a very strong wind
