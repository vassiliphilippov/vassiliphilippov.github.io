othering

adjective
causing exclusion
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈʌðərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌðərɪŋ / (BrE)
describing language, behavior, or attitudes that treat people as fundamentally different or alien, leading to exclusion or marginalization

Examples

Othering language
The politician's othering remarks fueled division within the community.
Critics pointed out the othering language used in the article about immigrants.
Teachers are encouraged to avoid othering behavior in diverse classrooms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alienating - making someone feel isolated or estranged
  • antonym: inclusive - including all people and treating them fairly and equally
