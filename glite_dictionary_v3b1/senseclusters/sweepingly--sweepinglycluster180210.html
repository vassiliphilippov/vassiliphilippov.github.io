sweepingly

adverb
in a broad or generalizing manner
Pronunciation: / ˈswipɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈswiːpɪŋli / (BrE)
In a broad, generalizing, or comprehensive manner.

Examples

To sweepingly dismiss
He sweepingly dismissed their concerns without listening to individual opinions.
Some commentators sweepingly predict that technology will completely transform education in the next decade.
The new policy was sweepingly applied to all departments, affecting hundreds of employees.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broadly - in a general or wide-ranging way
  • antonym: narrowly - in a limited or restricted way
