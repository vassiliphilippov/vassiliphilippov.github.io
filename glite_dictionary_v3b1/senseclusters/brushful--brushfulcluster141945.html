brushful

noun
amount held by brush
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌʃfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbrʌʃfʊl / (BrE)
the amount that can be held or applied by one brush at a time

Examples

A brushful of paint
She dipped the brush and used a brushful of paint on the canvas.
He applied a brushful of glue to the edge of the paper.
The artist needed only one brushful to finish the final stroke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: handful - the amount that can be held in one hand
  • synonym: spoonful - the amount that can be held in one spoon
