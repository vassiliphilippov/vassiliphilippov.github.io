froot

adjective
fruit-flavored (informal)
Pronunciation: / fruːt / (AmE), / fruːt / (BrE)
informal: having a taste or flavor like fruit, especially in artificial or processed products

Examples

Froot flavor candy
The new soda has a strong froot flavor that kids seem to love.
She bought a pack of froot candies for the road trip.
Many cereals use the word froot to describe their artificial fruit taste.
