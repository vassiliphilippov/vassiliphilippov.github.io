unbaited

adjective
without bait
6.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbeɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈbeɪtɪd / (BrE)
not having bait attached or applied, especially to a hook or trap

Examples

An unbaited hook
He cast his fishing line with an unbaited hook.
The trap remained unbaited, so no animals were caught overnight.
She realized her line was unbaited after waiting for hours without a bite.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baited - having bait attached or applied
