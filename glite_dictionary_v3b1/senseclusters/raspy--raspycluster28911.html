raspy

adjective
harsh, grating (voice or sound)

(of a voice or sound) harsh and grating; unpleasantly rough or coarse to the ear.

Examples

A raspy voice
After shouting all day, his voice became weak and raspy.
The old record had a raspy sound that added to its charm.
The singer's raspy voice gave the song a unique edge.

    6.2 0.09/m

  • gravelly
    6.5 0.62/m
    gravelly is low and rough, like a deep voice with grit, while raspy focuses on a coarse, grating tone.
  • scratchy
    3.7 0.69/m

  • croaky
    4.4 0.06/m

  • hoarse
    3.4 2.62/m

  • harsh-voiced
    6.8 0.00/m
