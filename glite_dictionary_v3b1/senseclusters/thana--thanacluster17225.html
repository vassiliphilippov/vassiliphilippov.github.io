thana

noun
local administrative unit
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈtɑːnə / (BrE)
a police station (especially in South Asian countries such as India, Bangladesh, and Pakistan)

Examples

Local thana
The villagers reported the theft at the nearest thana early in the morning.
Each thana is responsible for maintaining law and order in its area.
During the census the village was listed under the neighboring thana rather than the district office.
He reported the theft at the local thana.
The new thana was built to serve the growing population of the district.
Residents gathered outside the thana to demand action after the incident.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: district - an area of a country or city, especially one regarded as a distinct unit
  • synonym: police station - a building where police officers work and the public can report crimes
  • synonym: precinct - a district of a city or town as defined for police purposes
