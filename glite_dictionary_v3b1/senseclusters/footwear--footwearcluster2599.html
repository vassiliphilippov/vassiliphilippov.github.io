footwear

noun
items worn on the feet
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfʊtˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈfʊtˌweə /; / ˈfʊtˌwɛə / (BrE)
things that people wear on their feet, such as shoes, boots, and sandals

Examples

The shop offers a wide selection of footwear for all occasions.
His job requires him to wear protective footwear at all times.
It's important to choose the right footwear for hiking in rough terrain.
The museum has an exhibit on traditional footwear from around the world.
