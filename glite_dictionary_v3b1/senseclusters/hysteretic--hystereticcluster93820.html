hysteretic

adjective
showing hysteresis
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌhɪstəˈrɛtɪk / (AmE), / ˌhɪstəˈrɛtɪk / (BrE)

The scientist explained the hysteretic nature of the magnetic material to the students.
