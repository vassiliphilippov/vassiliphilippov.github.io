longe

verb
to exercise a horse
Pronunciation: / lʌndʒ / (AmE), / lʌndʒ / (BrE)
to exercise a horse by guiding it in a circle on a long rein or line from the ground

Examples

To longe a horse
The trainer decided to longe the horse before the riding lesson began.
She carefully longed the stallion to help it relax and focus.
Before mounting, he always longes his horse to warm it up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lunge - to exercise a horse on a long rein in a circle
