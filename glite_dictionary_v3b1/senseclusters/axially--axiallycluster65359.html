axially

adverb
in the direction of an axis
Pronunciation: / ˈæk.si.ə.li / (AmE), / ˈæk.si.ə.li / (BrE)
in a direction parallel to or along an axis

Examples

Placed axially along
The component was inserted axially into the tube for optimal alignment.
The scientist measured the displacement axially to ensure accuracy.
Turbines are often designed to move fluids axially rather than radially.

Related Terms

  • antonym: radially - in a direction from the center or radius outward
