throbbingly

adverb
with strong pulses
Pronunciation: / ˈθrɑbɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈθrɒbɪŋli / (BrE)
in a manner that pulses, beats, or vibrates strongly and repeatedly

Examples

Throbbingly painful
The pain in his head returned throbbingly every few minutes, making it hard to concentrate.
The city lights glowed throbbingly against the night sky, reflecting off the river.
Her heart beat throbbingly as she waited for the results to be announced.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pulsatingly - in a way that moves or beats with a strong, regular rhythm
  • antonym: steadily - in a regular, even, and continuous way
