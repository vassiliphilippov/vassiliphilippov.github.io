Synonyms

  • microcircuit
    7.3 0.06/m
    microcircuit is a very small complete circuit built into a single chip, while integrated circuit refers to that full circuit on one semiconductor.
  • microchip
    3.7 1.88/m
    microchip is a small piece of silicon that contains tiny electronic parts, while integrated circuit names the set of connected components that do a whole circuit's job.
  • chip
    4.1 14/m
    chip is the physical piece of semiconductor that carries circuits, while integrated circuit describes the complete set of electronic components on that chip.
