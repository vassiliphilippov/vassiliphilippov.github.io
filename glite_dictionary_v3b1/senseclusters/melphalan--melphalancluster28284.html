melphalan

noun
chemotherapy drug for cancer treatment
8.5
Pronunciation: /mel.ˈfæl.ən/ (AmE), /mel.ˈfæl.ən/ (BrE)
a drug used in chemotherapy to treat multiple myeloma and certain other cancers

Examples

Melphalan chemotherapy drug
After being diagnosed with multiple myeloma, she began treatment with melphalan and steroids.
Doctors may prescribe melphalan as part of a conditioning regimen before a stem cell transplant.
The side effects of melphalan can include nausea and a decrease in blood cell counts.
