worst timeline

noun
most negative possible scenario
Pronunciation: / wɝst ˈtaɪm.laɪn / (AmE), / wɜːst ˈtaɪm.laɪn / (BrE)
a hypothetical or imagined version of events or reality in which the outcomes are especially bad or unfavorable

Examples

The worst timeline
After hearing the news, she sighed and said it felt like we were in the worst timeline.
He joked that losing the championship was proof they’d entered the worst timeline.
Online discussions often refer to 2020 as the worst timeline because of all the global crises.
