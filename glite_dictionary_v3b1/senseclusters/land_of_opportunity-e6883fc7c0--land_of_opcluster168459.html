land of opportunity

noun
a place promising opportunity and success
1.0
Pronunciation: / lænd əv ɑːpərˈtunəti / (AmE), / lænd əv ɒpəˈtjuːnəti / (BrE)
a country or place regarded as providing the chances for personal advancement and success, especially through hard work

Examples

A land of opportunity
After years of hardship, she finally found a land of opportunity in Canada.
Many immigrants viewed the United States as the land of opportunity where they could build a better future.
People are still drawn to cities that are advertised as a land of opportunity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: promised land - a place regarded as a paradise or a place of fulfillment and hope
