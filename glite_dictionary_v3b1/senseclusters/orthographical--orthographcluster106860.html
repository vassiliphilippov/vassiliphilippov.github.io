orthographical

adjective
relating to projection
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrθəˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌɔːθəˈɡræfɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to orthographic projection or the accurate two-dimensional representation of three-dimensional objects in technical drawing and engineering

Examples

Orthographical drawing
The engineer created orthographical drawings to ensure every measurement was precise.
Students learned how to make orthographical projections in their technical drawing class.
The orthographical view of the machine revealed all its internal components clearly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: perspective - a drawing technique showing objects as they appear to the eye
