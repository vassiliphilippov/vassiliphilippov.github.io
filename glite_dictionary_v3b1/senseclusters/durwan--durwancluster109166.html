durwan

noun
building caretaker
Pronunciation: / dʊrˈwɑːn /; / dərˈwɑːn / (AmE), / dʊəˈwɑːn /; / dɜːˈwɑːn / (BrE)
a person, especially in South Asia, who looks after the entrance and upkeep of an apartment building or complex (doorkeeper or caretaker)

Examples

The durwan greets residents
The durwan greeted residents every morning as they left for work.
After a storm, the durwan quickly cleared the entrance of debris.
Residents appreciated the durwan for keeping the building clean and safe.
The durwan greeted everyone who entered the apartment building with a polite nod.
Residents relied on the durwan to keep the entrance secure at all times.
Every morning, the durwan swept the steps and opened the main gate for deliveries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caretaker - a person employed to look after a building
  • synonym: concierge - a person in a building who assists residents and visitors
  • synonym: doorman - a person employed to guard the entrance of a building
  • synonym: janitor - a person who cleans and maintains a building
