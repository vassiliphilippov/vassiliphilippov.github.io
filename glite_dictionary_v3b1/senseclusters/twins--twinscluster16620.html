twins

noun
virtual replicas (digital twins)
6.1 0.11/m
Pronunciation: / tw63nz / (AmE), / tw63nz / (BrE)
(in computing and engineering) virtual replicas of physical objects, systems, or processes used for simulation, monitoring, analysis, and optimization.

Examples

Digital twins of machines
Manufacturers create twins of their assembly lines to test process changes before implementation.
The city used twins to simulate traffic flow and plan the new transport routes.
Our energy team analyzes twins continuously to predict maintenance needs and reduce downtime.

Synonyms

  • twin
    1.2 8.20/m
    a twin is a single exact copy for many uses, while twins are virtual replicas used in engineering for simulation, monitoring, and analysis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: digital twin - a virtual representation of a physical object or system used to simulate and analyze real-world behaviour
  • synonym: simulation - the imitation of a process or system for study or training; often performed using a digital model
