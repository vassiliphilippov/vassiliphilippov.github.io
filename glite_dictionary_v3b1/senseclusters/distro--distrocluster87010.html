distro

noun
informal distributor
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪstrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a company or business that buys goods from manufacturers and sells or supplies them to shops or other businesses

Examples

Local distro company
The local distro delivers restaurant supplies to dozens of cafes every morning.
If you want to stock that product, you should contact the main distro in your area.
Many small businesses rely on a reliable distro to keep their shelves full.

Related Terms

  • synonym: distributor - a company that supplies goods to shops or businesses
  • antonym: retailer - a business that sells goods directly to consumers
