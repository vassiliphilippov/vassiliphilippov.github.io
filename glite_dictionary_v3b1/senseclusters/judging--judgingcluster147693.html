judging

noun
the process of forming an opinion or decision
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒʌdʒ.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒʌdʒ.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the process of forming an opinion, making a decision, or reaching a conclusion after considering facts and evidence

Examples

Effective judging process
Effective judging requires gathering all the relevant facts before forming an opinion.
He tried to avoid judging the situation until he knew all the details.
Her constant judging of others made her unpopular with her peers.
