tonic

adjective
invigorating, energizing
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑnɪk / (AmE), / ˈtɒnɪk / (BrE)
(of something) giving a feeling of vigor or well-being; invigorating and refreshing.

Examples

Tonic effect
Many people find a tonic climate invigorates both body and mind.
Spending weekends by the sea has always had a tonic influence on his mood and health.
The tonic effect of a brisk walk in fresh air was exactly what she needed after a long day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: enervating - causing one to feel drained of energy
  • synonym: invigorating - giving strength or energy
  • synonym: refreshing - making you feel less tired or hot
