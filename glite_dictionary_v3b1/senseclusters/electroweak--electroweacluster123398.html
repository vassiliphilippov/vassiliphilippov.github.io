electroweak

noun
unified fundamental forces
8.2
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɛktroʊˈwiːk / (AmE), / ɪˌlɛktrəʊˈwiːk / (BrE)
the unified interaction of the electromagnetic and weak nuclear forces, or the scientific theory describing this unification

Examples

Electroweak theory
The electroweak theory is a key part of the Standard Model in particle physics.
Physicists study the electroweak interaction to understand how fundamental forces are related.
Discoveries about the electroweak have helped explain the behavior of subatomic particles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: electromagnetic - relating to the combined electric and magnetic fields
  • synonym: weak force - a fundamental force responsible for radioactive decay
