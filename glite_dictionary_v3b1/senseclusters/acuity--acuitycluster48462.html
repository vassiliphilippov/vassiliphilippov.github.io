acuity

noun
sharpness or keenness of vision
8.1 1.16/m
Pronunciation: / …ôÀàkjuÀê…ôti / (AmE), / …ôÀàkjuÀê…™ti / (BrE)
the sharpness or keenness of vision, especially as measured by the ability to distinguish fine detail

Examples

Visual acuity
Children with high acuity can pick out tiny objects from a distance.
Her visual acuity was tested with a standard eye chart during the examination.
Poor lighting can affect acuity and make it difficult to see small details clearly.

Synonyms

  • visual acuity
    4.9 0.33/m
    visual acuity is the clinical measure of how clearly a person sees in an eye test, while acuity is the general sharpness of vision.
  • sharp-sightedness
    6.6 0.00/m
    sharp-sightedness describes someone who sees small details well in daily life, while acuity is the technical term for the sharpness of vision.
