jujube

noun
chewy fruit-flavored jellied candy
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒuːˌdʒuːb / (AmE), / ˈdʒuːˌdʒuːb / (BrE)
A chewy, fruit-flavored jellied candy, sometimes medicated to soothe a sore throat.

Examples

A jujube candy
My grandmother always kept jujube candies in her purse for us.
She bought a bag of jujubes at the corner store before the movie.
Some cough drops taste just like jujube gummies.
