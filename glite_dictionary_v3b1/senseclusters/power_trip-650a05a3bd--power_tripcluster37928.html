power trip

noun
abuse of control for self-gratification
4.7 6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈpaʊ.ər trɪp/ /; / /paʊr trɪp/ (AmE), /ˈpaʊ.ə trɪp/ /; / /pɑː trɪp/ (BrE)
an action or behavior in which someone seeks pleasure from abusing or exercising authority or control over others, often excessively or for self-importance

Examples

A power trip behavior
Don't let his power trip get to you; just focus on your work.
She complained her supervisor’s constant criticism was just a power trip.
The manager was accused of going on a power trip after making unnecessary new rules.
