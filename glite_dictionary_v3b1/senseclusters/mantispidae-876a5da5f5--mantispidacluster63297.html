Mantispidae

noun
family of insects known as mantidflies
Pronunciation: / mænˈtɪspɪˌdiː / (AmE), / manˈtɪspɪˌdiː /; / mænˈtɪspɪˌdiː / (BrE)
a family of predatory insects (mantidflies or mantispids) with raptorial front legs and a resemblance to mantises
