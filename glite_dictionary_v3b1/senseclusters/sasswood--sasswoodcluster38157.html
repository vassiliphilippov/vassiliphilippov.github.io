sasswood

noun
wood for carving
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsæsˌwʊd / (AmE), / ˈsæsˌwʊd / (BrE)
the wood from the sasswood tree, used for timber, fuel, or making carved objects

Examples

Because of its durability, sasswood is popular for making small carved objects.
