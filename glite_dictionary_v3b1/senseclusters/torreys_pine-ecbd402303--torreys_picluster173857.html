Torrey's pine

noun
rare pine tree of California
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔːriz paɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɒriz paɪn / (BrE)
a medium-sized pine tree (Pinus torreyana) native to southwestern California, known for its long, cylindrical cones and unique five-needle clusters
