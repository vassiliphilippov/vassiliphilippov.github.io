pictorialist

adjective
artistic, not literal
8.6
Pronunciation: /pɪk.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.lɪst/ /; / /pɪk.ˈtɔːr.ɪə.lɪst/ (AmE), /pɪk.ˈtɔːr.ɪ.ə.lɪst/ /; / /pɪk.ˈtɔːr.ɪə.lɪst/ (BrE)
relating to a style of photography or art that emphasizes artistic composition, mood, or tone over literal realism.

Examples

Pictorialist style photography
The exhibition featured pictorialist photographs that focused on mood rather than sharp detail.
Her pictorialist approach to painting created dreamy, atmospheric landscapes.
Many early twentieth-century photographers adopted a pictorialist style to elevate photography as an art form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artistic - showing creative skill or taste
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they actually are
