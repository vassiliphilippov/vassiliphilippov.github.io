Synonyms

  • impassable
    5.3 0.88/m
    impassable describes a road or path you cannot travel across, while impenetrable focuses on a solid barrier you cannot enter.
  • untraversable
    9.1 0.01/m
    untraversable means you cannot travel across an area at all, while impenetrable emphasizes not allowing passage or entry through it.
  • unbreachable
    7.7 0.16/m
    unbreachable says you cannot break through a barrier, while impenetrable stresses that nothing can pass into or enter it.
  • impervious
    7.5 2.22/m
    impervious often means liquids or influences cannot pass through, while impenetrable refers to something you cannot physically enter.
  • impregnable
    7.9 0.67/m
    impregnable describes a place you cannot capture or defeat, while impenetrable means you cannot physically get through or enter it.
  • inaccessible
    7.9 3.38/m
    inaccessible means you cannot reach or use a place for many reasons, while impenetrable means you cannot pass through its material barrier.
