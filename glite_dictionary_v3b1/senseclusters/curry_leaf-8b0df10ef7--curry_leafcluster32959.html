curry leaf

noun
aromatic leaf used in Indian cooking
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɜːri liːf / (AmE), / ˈkʌri liːf / (BrE)
The aromatic leaf of the curry tree, used as a seasoning in South Indian and Sri Lankan cooking.

Examples

Fresh curry leaves
Dried curry leaves are sometimes used when fresh ones are not available.
She added a handful of curry leaves to the hot oil to release their aroma.
The recipe calls for fresh curry leaves to enhance the flavor of the dish.
