afterglow

noun
lingering pleasure after an event
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæf.tɚˌɡloʊ / (AmE), / ˈɑːf.təˌɡləʊ / (BrE)
a feeling of pleasure or satisfaction that remains after a positive or enjoyable experience

Examples

Afterglow of success
He basked in the afterglow of his recent promotion.
The afterglow of her wedding day lasted for weeks.
There was a warm afterglow in the room after everyone shared their good news.

Related Terms

  • synonym: euphoria - a feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness
  • antonym: letdown - a feeling of disappointment following excitement or success
