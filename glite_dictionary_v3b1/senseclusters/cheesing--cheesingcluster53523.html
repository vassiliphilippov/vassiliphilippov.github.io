cheesing

noun
smiling broadly for a photo
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃiːzɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃiːzɪŋ / (BrE)
Informal. the act of smiling broadly or posing with an exaggerated smile, especially for a photograph or for the camera.

Examples

Cheesing for the camera
The influencer's cheesing for the camera made the portrait look warm and approachable.
At the wedding, everyone joined in cheesing as the photographer shouted, 'Say cheese!'.
His constant cheesing in selfies gave him a playful, slightly embarrassed online persona.
