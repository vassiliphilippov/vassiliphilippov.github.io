haft

noun
handle of weapon or tool
4.2
Pronunciation: / hæft / (AmE), / hɑːft / (BrE)
the handle of a weapon or tool, such as a knife, axe, or sword.

Examples

Haft of a knife
He gripped the haft of his axe tightly before swinging it at the log.
She replaced the worn haft of the kitchen knife with a new wooden handle.
The old sword's haft was made from polished bone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: blade - cutting part of a weapon or tool (opposite of handle)
  • synonym: hilt - the handle of a sword or dagger
  • synonym: shaft - long handle of a tool or weapon
