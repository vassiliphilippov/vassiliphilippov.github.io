jerboa kangaroo

noun
small Australian marsupial (brush-tailed rat kangaroo)

Pronunciation: / dʒərˈboʊ ə ˈkæŋɡəˌru / (AmE), / dʒəˈbəʊ ə ˈkæŋɡəruː / (BrE)
