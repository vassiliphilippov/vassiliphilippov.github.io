minette

noun
small cat or kitten
8.9
Pronunciation: / miˈnɛt / (AmE), / miˈnɛt / (BrE)
a small cat or kitten, especially a female, sometimes used as a term of endearment

Examples

A cute minette
The children crowded around the box to pet the tiny minette that had wandered into the yard.
She adopted a playful minette from the animal shelter last weekend.
Every morning, the little minette would curl up on the windowsill to watch the birds.

  • synonym: kitten - a young cat
