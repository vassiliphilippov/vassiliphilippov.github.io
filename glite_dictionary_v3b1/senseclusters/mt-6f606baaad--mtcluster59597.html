MT

noun
metric ton (1,000 kg)
3.9
Pronunciation: / ɛm ti / (AmE), / ɛm ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'metric ton', a unit of weight equal to 1,000 kilograms

Examples

5,000 MT of steel
Last year, our factory produced over 5,000 MT of steel.
The country aims to reduce its CO₂ emissions by 10 MT annually.
The shipment weighed 20 MT and was delivered on schedule.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ton - unit of weight; in the US, commonly 2,000 pounds
  • synonym: tonne - unit of weight equal to 1,000 kilograms
