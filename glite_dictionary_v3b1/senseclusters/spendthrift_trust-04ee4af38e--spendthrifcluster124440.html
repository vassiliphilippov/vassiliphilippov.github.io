spendthrift trust

noun
trust protecting beneficiary from own improvidence
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft trʌst / (AmE), / ˈspɛndˌθrɪft trʌst / (BrE)
a trust established to provide for a beneficiary while protecting the assets from the beneficiary’s inability to manage money or from creditors.

Examples

A spendthrift trust protects assets
A spendthrift trust can ensure that the beneficiary receives financial support without direct control over the assets.
Setting up a spendthrift trust is a common way to support a child with money management issues.
The inheritance was placed in a spendthrift trust to protect it from his poor financial decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: trust - a legal arrangement in which a person or organization manages property for someone else's benefit
