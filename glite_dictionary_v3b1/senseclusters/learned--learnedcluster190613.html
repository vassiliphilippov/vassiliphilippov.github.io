learned

adjective
obtained by machine learning
6.2
Pronunciation: /ˈlɜːr.nɪd/ /; / /lɜːrnd/ (AmE), /ˈlɜːn.ɪd/ /; / /lɜːnd/ (BrE)
(in machine learning and AI) produced or obtained by a learning algorithm from data rather than designed or specified manually; said of models, parameters, features, or representations.

Examples

Learned model parameters
The model's learned embeddings captured subtle relationships between words across multiple languages.
Engineers fine-tuned the system until the learned parameters generalized well to unseen test examples.
Rather than handcrafting features, the team relied on learned representations extracted automatically from raw sensor data.

Related Terms

  • antonym: handcrafted - designed or created manually rather than learned automatically from data
  • synonym: trained - adjusted or fitted by a learning algorithm on data (often used of models or parameters)
