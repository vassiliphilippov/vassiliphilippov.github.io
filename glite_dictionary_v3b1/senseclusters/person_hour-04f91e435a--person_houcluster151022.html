person hour

noun
work done by one person in one hour
3.6 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùÀês…ôn a är / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀês…ôn a ä…ô / (BrE)
A unit of measurement equal to the work performed by one person in one hour, commonly used to estimate or record effort in industry or project management.

Examples

One person hour
Each additional feature in the software adds several person hours to the development schedule.
The renovation project is estimated to require 300 person hours to complete.
We tracked team productivity by recording the total person hours spent on each task.

Synonyms

    3.4 0.28/m
