literary review

noun
critical discussion of literary works
4.4 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪt̬.ə.rer.i rɪˈvju / (AmE), / ˈlɪt.ər.əri rɪˈvjuː / (BrE)
an article, essay, or report that discusses, evaluates, or critiques a literary work or works

Examples

A literary review article
Before submitting their manuscripts, many authors seek out a literary review to understand how their work might be received.
Her literary review of the novel provided unique insights into the author's style and themes.
The journal published a literary review that challenged popular interpretations of the poem.
