actioner

noun
mechanical trigger device
Pronunciation: / ˈækʃənər / (AmE), / ˈækʃənə / (BrE)
a mechanical device or component that produces movement or initiates an operation in a machine or system

Examples

Machine actioner
The technician replaced the faulty actioner to restore the machine's automatic functions.
A new actioner was installed to improve the efficiency of the assembly line.
Without a working actioner, the system cannot start or stop as required.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actuator - a device that causes a machine or system to operate
