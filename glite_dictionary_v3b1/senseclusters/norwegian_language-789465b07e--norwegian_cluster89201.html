Norwegian language

noun
the language of Norway
Pronunciation: / nɔːrˈwiː.dʒən ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / nɔːˈwiː.dʒən ˈlæŋ.ɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
the Scandinavian language spoken in Norway, with two official written forms: Bokmål and Nynorsk

Examples

Norwegian language learning
He learned the Norwegian language to communicate better with his relatives in Oslo.
Many schools offer Norwegian language courses for students interested in Scandinavian studies.
The novel was originally written in the Norwegian language before it was translated into English.
