midnight sun

noun
sun visible at midnight in polar regions
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdnaɪt ˌsʌn / (AmE), / ˈmɪdnaɪt ˌsʌn / (BrE)
the sun visible at midnight during continuous daylight periods in summer within the Arctic or Antarctic Circles

Examples

The midnight sun shines
Photographers love capturing the unique light of the midnight sun in northern Scandinavia.
The midnight sun can be seen in Alaska during the summer months, making nights remarkably bright.
We traveled to Norway in June to witness the midnight sun above the Arctic Circle.
