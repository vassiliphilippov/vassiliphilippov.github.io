sweaty

adjective
nervous or tense
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈswɛti / (AmE), / ˈswɛti / (BrE)
showing or causing nervousness or anxiety, especially in a tense or stressful situation.

Examples

Sweaty hands
He always gets a little sweaty before giving a big presentation at work.
The interview became sweaty when the questions turned personal and unexpected.
You could tell she was feeling sweaty as she waited for the exam results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxious - feeling worried or uneasy
  • antonym: calm - not feeling nervous or anxious
