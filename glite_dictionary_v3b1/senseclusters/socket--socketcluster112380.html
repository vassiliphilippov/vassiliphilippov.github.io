socket

noun
bony hollow for a structure
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑk.ɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɒk.ɪt / (BrE)
A hollow in a bone into which another structure fits, such as where an eye or a tooth is set.

Examples

Eye socket
After his accident, he fractured the socket of his shoulder.
The dentist examined the empty socket where the tooth had been removed.
The eye sits comfortably in its protective bony socket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cavity - a hollow space within a body or bone
