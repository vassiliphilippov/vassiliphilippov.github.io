brain candy

noun
entertaining but not intellectually demanding material
1.8
Pronunciation: / breɪn ˈkændi / (AmE), / breɪn ˈkændi / (BrE)
Something that is enjoyable to experience but does not require much thought or intellectual effort.

Examples

Watch brain candy shows
After a long day at work, I like to watch some brain candy on TV to unwind.
Romantic comedies are often considered brain candy because they're fun but not too deep.
That podcast is pure brain candy—entertaining but not mentally demanding.
