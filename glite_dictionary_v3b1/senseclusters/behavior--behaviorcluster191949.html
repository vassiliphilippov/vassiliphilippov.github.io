Synonyms

  • conduct
    3.7 30/m
    conduct is a word for how someone acts in a situation, often in rules or work, while behavior is more general everyday actions.
  • demeanor
    4.9 6/m
    demeanor shows a person's outward manner and attitude to others, while behavior covers all ways a person acts.
  • manner
    1.0 15/m
    manner describes the style or method someone uses to do things, while behavior means how someone acts in situations.
  • deportment
    7.6 0.4/m
    deportment refers to polite or proper behavior and posture in social settings, while behavior covers general actions and reactions.
