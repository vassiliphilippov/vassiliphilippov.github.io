uptight

adjective
tense or unable to relax
1.9
Pronunciation: / ʌpˈtaɪt / (AmE), / ʌpˈtaɪt / (BrE)
anxious, tense, or unable to relax, often due to stress or worry

Examples

An uptight person
Don't be so uptight; try to enjoy yourself at the party.
He became noticeably uptight when asked about his plans for the future.
She always gets uptight before an important meeting at work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nervous - easily agitated or alarmed
  • antonym: relaxed - free from tension or anxiety
