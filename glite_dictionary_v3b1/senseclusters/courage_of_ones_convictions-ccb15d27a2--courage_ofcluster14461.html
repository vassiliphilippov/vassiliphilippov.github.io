courage of one's convictions

noun
bravery to stand by one's beliefs
Pronunciation: / ˈkɝːrɪdʒ əv wʌnz kənˈvɪkʃənz / (AmE), / ˈkʌrɪdʒ əv wʌnz kənˈvɪkʃənz / (BrE)
the ability to act according to one's beliefs or principles, even in the face of criticism or opposition

Examples

Courage of one's convictions
Despite public criticism, he always shows the courage of his convictions.
It takes real courage of one's convictions to challenge an unfair system.
She demonstrated the courage of her convictions when she refused to compromise her principles.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integrity - the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles
