nuance

verb
to add subtle distinctions
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnuːɑːns / (AmE), / ˈnjuːɑːns / (BrE)
to give subtle differences or distinctions to something

Examples

To nuance a painting
The artist nuanced the colors in her painting to create a more realistic effect.
He carefully nuanced his argument to address the concerns of his audience.
The chef nuanced the dish with a hint of lemon to balance the flavors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refine - to improve by making small changes
  • antonym: simplify - to make something easier or less complex
