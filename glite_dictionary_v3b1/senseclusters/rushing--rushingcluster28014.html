rushing

noun
the act of moving hurriedly and carelessly
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌʃɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrʌʃɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of moving quickly and often carelessly; hurried movement or action

Examples

Constant rushing
His constant rushing often leads to mistakes at work.
She spilled coffee because she was rushing out the door.
The rushing of students in the hallway created a lot of noise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haste - excessive speed or urgency of movement
  • antonym: leisure - free time and relaxed pace, opposite of hurry
