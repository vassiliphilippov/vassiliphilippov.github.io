amantadine

noun
Parkinson's and flu drug
8.1
Pronunciation: / əˈmæntəˌdin / (AmE), / əˈmantədiːn / (BrE)
a drug used to treat Parkinson's disease and certain viral infections, especially influenza A

Examples

Amantadine drug treatment
The neurologist prescribed amantadine to help control his Parkinson's symptoms.
Some doctors use amantadine to treat certain cases of influenza A.
After starting amantadine, she noticed an improvement in her movement and energy levels.
