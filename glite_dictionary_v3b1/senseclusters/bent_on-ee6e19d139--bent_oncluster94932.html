Synonyms

    4.4 2/m
    bent means strongly determined in general, while bent on emphasizes determination toward a specific goal, often harmful.
  • hell-bent
    7.5 1/m
    hell-bent is recklessly determined to do something no matter the danger, while bent on is firm but not always reckless.
  • dead set
    6.5 0.7/m
