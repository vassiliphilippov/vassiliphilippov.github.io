garden roller

noun
tool for flattening lawns or gardens
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrdən ˈroʊlər / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːdən ˈrəʊlə / (BrE)
a heavy cylindrical tool, often made of metal, used to flatten or level the surface of a lawn or garden

Examples

A garden roller tool
After seeding the new lawn, he used a garden roller to press the soil evenly.
Rolling the turf with a garden roller helps to promote better grass growth.
The maintenance team borrowed a garden roller from the neighboring park for the weekend project.
