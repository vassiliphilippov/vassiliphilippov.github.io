Alaska Standard Time

noun
time zone used in most of Alaska
Pronunciation: / əˈlæskə ˈstændɚd taɪm / (AmE), / əˈlæskə ˈstændəd taɪm / (BrE)
the standard time used in most of Alaska, nine hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-9)

Examples

Alaska Standard Time zone
Anchorage observes Alaska Standard Time during the winter months.
Meetings are scheduled at 10 a.m. Alaska Standard Time.
