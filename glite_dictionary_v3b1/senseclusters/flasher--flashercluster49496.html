flasher

noun
a signaling light that flashes

Examples

Emergency flasher light
Ambulances are equipped with a flasher that signals when they're responding to emergencies.
The security system uses a red flasher to alert guards to a breach.
When the car broke down, she turned on the flasher to warn other drivers.

Synonyms

  • flashing light
    2.0 0.88/m
    a flashing light blinks on and off to warn people, while a flasher often names the specific signal device used in alarms or vehicles.
  • blinker
    3.2 0.24/m
    a blinker is the car's small turning signal light, while a flasher is any light that flashes to send a message.
  • signal light
    3.2 0.08/m
    a signal light shines or burns to send messages over distance, while a flasher specifically turns on and off to signal.
  • strobe light
    3.7 0.39/m
    a strobe light gives very rapid, bright pulses for effect or study, while a flasher usually gives slower flashes as a simple signal.
