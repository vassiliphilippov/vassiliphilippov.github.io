unkillable

adjective
impossible to defeat
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkɪləbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈkɪləbəl / (BrE)
impossible or extremely difficult to kill, destroy, or stop

Examples

An unkillable machine
The old computer seemed unkillable, surviving every power surge and accident.
Her unkillable optimism inspired everyone around her, even during tough times.
The team's unkillable spirit helped them win despite being behind for most of the game.
The hero faced an unkillable monster that returned after every defeat.
Some weeds in the garden seem almost unkillable no matter what you do.
The legend spoke of an unkillable warrior who survived every battle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: invincible - impossible to defeat or overcome
  • antonym: mortal - able to die; not immortal
