cramp

verb
to secure with a clamping device
6.0 5.5
Pronunciation: /kræmp/ (AmE), /kræmp/ (BrE)
to secure or hold tightly with a cramp, a device used to clamp objects together

Examples

To cramp two boards
He used a steel device to cramp the two boards together while the glue dried.
Make sure to cramp the frame joints tightly so they set correctly.
The carpenter cramped the freshly glued panel for several hours.
