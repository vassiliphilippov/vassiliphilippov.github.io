sea pig

noun
deep-sea holothurian (sea cucumber) with unique appearance
7.6
Pronunciation: / si pɪɡ / (AmE), / siː pɪɡ / (BrE)
a deep-sea holothurian (sea cucumber), especially of the family Elpidiidae, noted for its unusual, pig-like appearance and distinctive locomotion on ocean floors

Examples

A sea pig animal
Children at the aquarium marveled at the strange look of the sea pig.
Scientists were excited to spot a group of sea pigs crawling along the ocean floor.
The sea pig survives in some of the deepest and coldest parts of the ocean.
