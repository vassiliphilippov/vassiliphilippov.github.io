B

noun
unit of sound intensity (10 decibels)
8.8
Pronunciation: / biː / (AmE), / biː / (BrE)
a unit of sound intensity equal to 10 decibels, used in acoustics and electronics

Examples

B unit level
Because a B is quite large, the decibel is more commonly used in practice.
The signal strength was measured as 2 B above the reference level.
This amplifier increases the audio output by 1 B.
