treated

adjective
having received medical care
Pronunciation: / ˈtriːtɪd / (AmE), / ˈtriːtɪd / (BrE)
Given medical care or subjected to a medical procedure or treatment.

Examples

Treated patient
A treated patient is more likely to recover quickly than an untreated one.
After being treated for her illness, she returned home feeling much better.
The wound was carefully treated to prevent infection.

Related Terms

  • antonym: untreated - not having received medical care or treatment
