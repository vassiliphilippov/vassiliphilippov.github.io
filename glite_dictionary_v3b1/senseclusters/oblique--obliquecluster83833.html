oblique

adjective
slanting; not parallel or perpendicular
7.0
Pronunciation: / əˈbliːk, oʊˈbliːk / (AmE), / əˈbliːk, əʊˈbliːk / (BrE)
slanting or inclined in direction, course, or position; neither parallel nor perpendicular nor right-angled

Examples

Oblique angle
In geometry class, we learned that an oblique triangle has no right angle.
The artist painted the shadow using oblique lines to show the angle of the sun.
They built the ramp at an oblique angle to the main entrance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parallel - side by side and having the same distance continuously between them
  • antonym: perpendicular - at an angle of 90 degrees to a given line or surface
  • synonym: slanted - having a slanting direction; at an angle
