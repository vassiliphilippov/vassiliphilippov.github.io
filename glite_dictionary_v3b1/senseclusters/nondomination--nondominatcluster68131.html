nondomination

noun
absence of domination
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌdɑməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌdɒmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the condition or principle of not being dominated by others or not dominating others
