manhandler

noun
rough handler
Pronunciation: / ˈmænˌhændlər / (AmE), / ˈmænˌhændlə / (BrE)
a person or device that moves or handles someone or something in a rough or forceful way

Examples

A rough manhandler
The security guard acted as a manhandler when removing the protester.
A special manhandler was used to move the heavy equipment safely.
She complained that the bouncer was a manhandler when escorting people out.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: abuser - a person who treats someone cruelly or violently
  • synonym: handler - a person who manages or controls something or someone
