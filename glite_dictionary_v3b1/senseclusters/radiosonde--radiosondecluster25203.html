radiosonde

noun
weather balloon instrument
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪdioʊˌsɑnd / (AmE), / ˈreɪdiəʊˌsɒnd / (BrE)
a small instrument carried by balloon to measure and transmit atmospheric data such as temperature, pressure, and humidity
