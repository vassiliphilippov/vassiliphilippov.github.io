anthropocentrically

adverb
from a human-centered view
Pronunciation: / ˌænθrəpəˈsɛntrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌænθrəpəˈsɛntrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that regards humans as the most important or central beings

Examples

Acting anthropocentrically
The policy was developed anthropocentrically, prioritizing human interests over environmental concerns.
Many traditional philosophies view the world anthropocentrically, ignoring the needs of other species.
The documentary criticized how society often acts anthropocentrically when making decisions about nature.

Related Terms

  • antonym: biocentrically - in a way that considers all living things as central
  • synonym: egocentrically - in a way that centers on oneself
