convergence

noun
mathematical series approaching a finite limit
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɜrdʒəns / (AmE), / kənˈvɜːdʒəns / (BrE)
the property of a mathematical series or sequence approaching a specific value as more terms are added

Examples

Mathematical convergence
Mathematicians studied the convergence of infinite sequences in their research.
Tests can determine the convergence or divergence of a mathematical series.
The convergence of the series means that its sum approaches a fixed number.
