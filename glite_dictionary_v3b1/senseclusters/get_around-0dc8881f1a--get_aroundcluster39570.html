get around

verb
socialize widely; be very sociable
2.2
Pronunciation: / ɡɛt əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ɡɛt əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
(informal) To socialize widely or go to a lot of different places; to be active in many social situations.

Examples

To get around town
If you want to know what's happening in town, just get around and talk to different groups.
Jake really likes to get around and meet new people at every opportunity.
She used to get around a lot more before she started working full-time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mingle - to move among and talk to people at a social event
  • antonym: stay in - to remain at home and not go out socially
