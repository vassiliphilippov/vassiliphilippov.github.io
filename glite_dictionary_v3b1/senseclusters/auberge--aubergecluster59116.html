auberge

noun
small hotel or inn, esp. in France
Pronunciation: / oʊˈbɛrʒ / (AmE), / əʊˈbɛəʒ / (BrE)
a small hotel or inn, especially one in France or with a French style, providing lodging for travelers

Examples

A auberge hotel
The chef at the auberge prepared a delicious traditional dinner for the guests.
The rustic auberge overlooked the rolling hills and vineyards.
We spent a charming weekend at a countryside auberge in Provence.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hostel - a budget-oriented accommodation for travelers
  • synonym: inn - a small hotel or lodging establishment
