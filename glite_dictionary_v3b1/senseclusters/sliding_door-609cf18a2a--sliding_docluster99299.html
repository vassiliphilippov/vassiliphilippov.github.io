sliding door

noun
missed chance for significant change
Pronunciation: / ˈslaɪdɪŋ dɔr / (AmE), / ˈslaɪdɪŋ dɔː / (BrE)
A missed opportunity or chance that could have led to a significant change in one's life.

Examples

Sliding door moment
Accepting the overseas position turned out to be a sliding door moment for his career.
Missing the train that day was a sliding door that changed everything for her.
She often wonders if that breakup was a sliding door in her life.
