myoma

noun
benign tumor of muscle tissue
Pronunciation: / maɪˈoʊmə / (AmE), / maɪˈəʊmə / (BrE)
A benign tumor composed of muscle tissue, typically occurring in the uterus or other organs.

Examples

A myoma tumor
Although a myoma is noncancerous, it can still affect a woman's quality of life.
Surgical removal of a myoma may be necessary if it causes severe symptoms.
The doctors discovered a myoma during her routine pelvic exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leiomyoma - a benign tumor composed of smooth muscle cells
