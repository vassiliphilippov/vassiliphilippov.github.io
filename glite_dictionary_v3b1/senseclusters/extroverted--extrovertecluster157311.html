extroverted

adjective
outgoing, socially confident
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkstroʊˈvɜrtɪd /; / ˈɛkstroʊˌvɜrtɪd /; / ˈɛkstrəˌvɜrtɪd / (AmE), / ˌɛkstrəʊˈvɜːtɪd /; / ˈɛkstrəʊˌvɜːtɪd / (BrE)
outgoing and socially confident; interested in social interaction and external things rather than inner thoughts
