Synonyms

  • southwest
    1.0 1.54/m
    southwest is the standard single-word form for the direction, while south-west is the hyphenated directional form seen in some texts.
  • southwestward
    2.7 0.04/m
    southwestward suggests movement toward the southwest over distance on a map, while south-west names the general direction or place.
  • southwesterly
    3.7 0.04/m
    southwesterly describes a place or wind that faces the southwest, while south-west simply marks the direction toward that area.
