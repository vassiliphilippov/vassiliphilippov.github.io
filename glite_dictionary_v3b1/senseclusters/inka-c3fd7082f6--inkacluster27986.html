Inka

proper noun
member of the indigenous Inka (Inca) people and empire
Pronunciation: / ˈiŋkə / (AmE), / ˈɪŋkə / (BrE)
a member of the Quechua-speaking people of the Cuzco Valley in Peru who established a vast empire in South America before the Spanish conquest in the early 16th century
