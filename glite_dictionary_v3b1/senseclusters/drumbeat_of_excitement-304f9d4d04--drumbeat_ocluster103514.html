drumbeat of excitement

noun
growing sense of excitement or anticipation
1.1
Pronunciation: / ˈdrʌmˌbit əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (AmE), / ˈdrʌmˌbiːt əv ɪkˈsaɪtmənt / (BrE)
a steadily increasing sense of excitement or anticipation, often felt collectively as an event approaches

Examples

A steady drumbeat of excitement
As the team approached the final game, a drumbeat of excitement spread through the town.
The drumbeat of excitement in the office built steadily as the launch time neared.
There was a palpable drumbeat of excitement among the students before the results were announced.
