bikeway

noun
path for bicycles
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪkˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈbaɪkˌweɪ / (BrE)
a path or road designed specifically for people riding bicycles

Examples

A bikeway path
The city built a new bikeway along the river to encourage cycling.
Cyclists prefer using the bikeway because it is safer than riding on the main road.
A well-maintained bikeway can make commuting by bicycle much more enjoyable.
