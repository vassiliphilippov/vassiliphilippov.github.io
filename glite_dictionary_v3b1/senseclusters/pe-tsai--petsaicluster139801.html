pe-tsai

noun
type of Chinese cabbage
Pronunciation: / piːˈtsaɪ / (AmE), / piːˈtsaɪ / (BrE)
A variety of Chinese cabbage with an elongated head of broad stalked leaves, commonly used as a vegetable in East Asian cuisine.

Examples

Fresh pe-tsai vegetable
At the market, there was a large selection of pe-tsai ready for pickling.
She added chopped pe-tsai to the soup for a crisp, fresh flavor.
Stir-frying pe-tsai with garlic is a popular dish in many Asian households.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bok choy - a different variety of Chinese cabbage with a similar use
  • synonym: napa cabbage - another name for this variety of Chinese cabbage
