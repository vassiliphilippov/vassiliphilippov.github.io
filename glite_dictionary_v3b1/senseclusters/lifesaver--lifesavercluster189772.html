lifesaver

noun
rescuer or helper
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪfˌseɪvər / (AmE), / ˈlaɪfˌseɪvə / (BrE)
a person or thing that rescues someone from danger or helps in a difficult situation

Examples

You are a lifesaver
The quick-thinking nurse was a real lifesaver during the crisis.
That portable charger was a lifesaver when my phone battery died on the trip.
Her advice proved to be a lifesaver when I was struggling with my assignment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rescuer - a person who saves someone from danger
  • synonym: savior - someone who saves another from danger or difficulty
  • antonym: threat - a person or thing likely to cause harm or danger
