yawningly

adverb
to a wide extent
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjɔnɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈjɔːnɪŋli / (BrE)
to a very great or wide extent, especially describing a large gap, difference, or opening

Examples

Yawningly wide gap
The two candidates held yawningly different views on the economy.
There is a yawningly large gap between the two cliffs.
The results showed a yawningly obvious difference in performance between the teams.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrowly - to a small or limited extent
  • synonym: widely - to a great extent or over a large area
