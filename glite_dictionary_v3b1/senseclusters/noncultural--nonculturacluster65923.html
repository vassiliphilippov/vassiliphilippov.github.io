noncultural

adjective
not cultural
6.7
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈkʌltʃərəl / (AmE), / nɒnˈkʌltʃərəl / (BrE)
not related to or influenced by culture or cultural factors

Examples

Noncultural factors
The research examined noncultural influences on learning, such as genetics and environment.
They tried to separate noncultural factors from those shaped by tradition.
Some diseases have noncultural causes that are purely biological.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cultural - related to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society
