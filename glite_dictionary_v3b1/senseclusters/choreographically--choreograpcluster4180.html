choreographically

adverb
in a coordinated way
Pronunciation: / ˌkɔːriəˈɡræfɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkɒriəˈɡræfɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is carefully planned and arranged, especially like choreography or dance movements
