Tb

noun
chemical symbol for terbium
Pronunciation: / ti bi / (AmE), / tiː biː / (BrE)
the chemical symbol for terbium, a metallic element of the rare earth group used in lasers and found in minerals such as apatite, monazite, xenotime, and ytterbite

Examples

Tb element
Scientists use Tb to refer to terbium when discussing its properties in chemistry texts.
The mineral xenotime contains traces of Tb, making it an important ore for rare earth elements.
When labeling the periodic table, don’t forget to include Tb among the lanthanides.
