sleeping beauty

noun
woman or girl sleeping soundly or beautifully
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈsliːpɪŋ ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a woman or girl who is sleeping soundly or appears especially beautiful while asleep; also used in allusion to the fairy tale character

Examples

She is a sleeping beauty
After the long journey, Maria became our own sleeping beauty, dozing peacefully in the back seat.
She looked like a sleeping beauty as she napped on the couch, undisturbed by the noise around her.
The photographer captured the essence of a sleeping beauty in the soft morning light.
