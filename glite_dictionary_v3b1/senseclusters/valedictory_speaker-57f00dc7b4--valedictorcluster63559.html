valedictory speaker

noun
student delivering farewell speech at graduation
Pronunciation: / ˌvælɪˈdɪktəri ˈspiːkər / (AmE), / ˌvælɪˈdɪktəri ˈspiːkə / (BrE)
the student, typically with the highest academic standing, who delivers the farewell speech at a graduation ceremony

Examples

The valedictory speaker delivers speech
Each year, the school holds auditions to select the valedictory speaker.
Emma was chosen as the valedictory speaker for her outstanding academic achievements.
The valedictory speaker delivered an inspiring message to the graduating class.
