melancholically

adverb
with deep sadness
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛlənˈkɑːlɪkli / (AmE), / ˌmɛlənˈkɒlɪkli / (BrE)

Examples

To speak melancholically

The music played melancholically in the background, setting a somber mood.
She smiled melancholically as she remembered her old friends.
