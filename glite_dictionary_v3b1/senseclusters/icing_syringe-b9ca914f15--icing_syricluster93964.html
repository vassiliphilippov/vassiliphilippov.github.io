icing syringe

noun
kitchen utensil for decorating cakes
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsɪŋ səˈrɪndʒ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsɪŋ sɪˈrɪndʒ / (BrE)
A plastic or metal kitchen utensil with a plunger, used to apply icing or decorations to cakes and pastries.
