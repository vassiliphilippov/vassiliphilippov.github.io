(in neuroscience) a wave-like pattern of neural activity that propagates across brain tissue or cortical regions.

Examples

Traveling wave in the brain
EEG recordings revealed a traveling wave of activity moving from the visual to frontal cortex.
During sleep, researchers observed a traveling wave sweeping across the animal's hippocampus and neocortex.
The model predicted how a localized stimulus could trigger a traveling wave across cortical layers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neural oscillation - rhythmic or repetitive patterns of neural activity; related concept describing periodic activity in neural tissue
  • antonym: standing wave - a spatially stable pattern of activity that does not travel; contrasts with a traveling wave
