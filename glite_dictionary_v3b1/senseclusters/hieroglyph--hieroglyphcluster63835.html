hieroglyph

noun
picture symbol in ancient Egyptian writing
5.0 8.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhaɪrəˌɡlɪf / (AmE), / ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪf / (BrE)
a symbol used in a system of writing that uses pictures, especially as used in ancient Egypt

Examples

Egyptian hieroglyph
Each hieroglyph in the tomb told a part of the pharaoh's story.
Scholars study ancient hieroglyphs to learn more about Egyptian civilization.
The Rosetta Stone was crucial in deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphs.
