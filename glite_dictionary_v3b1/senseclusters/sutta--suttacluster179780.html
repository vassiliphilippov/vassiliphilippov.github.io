sutta

noun
Buddhist scripture
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːtə / (AmE), / ˈsuːtə /; / ˈsʊtə / (BrE)
a Buddhist scripture or discourse, especially one found in the Pali Canon and attributed to the Buddha
