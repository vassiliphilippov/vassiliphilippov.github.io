darning needle

noun
large-eyed sewing needle
7.4 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈdɑːr.nɪŋ ˈniː.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈdɑː.nɪŋ ˈniː.dəl/ (BrE)
A long needle with a large eye, used for sewing with heavy thread or for embroidery.

Examples

A darning needle for yarn
A darning needle is essential when you're working with thick yarn or thread.

The old sewing box contained a silver darning needle passed down from her grandmother.
