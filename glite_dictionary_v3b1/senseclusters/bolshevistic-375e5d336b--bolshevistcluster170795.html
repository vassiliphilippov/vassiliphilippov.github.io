Bolshevistic

adjective
of or relating to Bolshevism
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌboʊlʃəˈvɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌbɒlʃɪˈvɪstɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of Bolshevism or its adherents.

Examples

Bolshevistic ideas
Her speeches had a distinctly Bolshevistic tone, advocating radical change.
The Bolshevistic approach to property was met with both enthusiasm and opposition.
The government feared the spread of Bolshevistic ideas among the working class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bolshevik - A member of the Russian Communist Party that seized power in 1917
