maidservant

noun
female household servant
7.1 0.33/m
Pronunciation: / Ààme…™dÀås…úrv…ônt / (AmE), / Ààme…™dÀås…úÀêv…ônt / (BrE)
a female domestic worker employed in a household, typically for cleaning, cooking, or other service tasks

Examples

A maidservant in house
In Victorian novels, the maidservant often played an important role in the household.
The maidservant brought tea and biscuits to the guests in the parlor.
The wealthy family employed a maidservant to help with the daily chores.

Synonyms
