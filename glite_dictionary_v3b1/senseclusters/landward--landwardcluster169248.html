landward

adverb
toward land; away from the sea
Pronunciation: / ˈlændwɚd / (AmE), / ˈlændwəd / (BrE)
toward the land; in the direction away from the sea or coast.

Examples

Look landward from coast
From the lighthouse, she gazed landward to spot any signs of the village.
Strong winds blew landward all afternoon, carrying the salt spray inland.
The hikers moved landward, leaving the rocky shore behind them.

Related Terms

  • antonym: seaward - toward the sea or away from land
