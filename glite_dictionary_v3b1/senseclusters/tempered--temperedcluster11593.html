tempered

adjective
moderated by a balancing element
Pronunciation: / ˈtɛmpɚd / (AmE), / ˈtɛmpəd / (BrE)
(of something) moderated or made less extreme by the addition of a counterbalancing element.

Examples

Tempered by kindness
Her anger was tempered by her understanding of the situation.
His criticism was tempered by sincere concern for the team's progress.
The harsh rules were tempered with compassion for new members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moderated - become less extreme or intense
