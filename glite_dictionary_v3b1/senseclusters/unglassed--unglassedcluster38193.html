unglassed

adjective
without eyeglasses
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡlæst / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡlɑːst / (BrE)
not wearing eyeglasses or spectacles; without glasses on

Examples

Unglassed face
He walked into the room unglassed, surprising his friends who had never seen him without spectacles.
The portrait showed her unglassed, highlighting her expressive eyes.
After years of wearing glasses, she finally appeared unglassed at the reunion.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glassed - wearing eyeglasses or spectacles
  • synonym: unbespectacled - not wearing spectacles or glasses
