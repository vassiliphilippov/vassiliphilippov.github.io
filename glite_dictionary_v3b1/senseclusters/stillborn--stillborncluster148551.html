stillborn

adjective
born dead (of a baby)
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈstɪlˌbɔrn / (AmE), / ˈstɪl.bɔːn / (BrE)
(of a baby) born dead, showing no signs of life at birth.

Examples

Stillborn baby
A memorial service was held for families of stillborn infants.
She mourned the loss of her stillborn child with deep sorrow.
The doctor gently explained that the baby was stillborn.

  • antonym: liveborn - born alive
