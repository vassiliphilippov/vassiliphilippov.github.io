subconsciousness

noun
mental state below conscious awareness
4.6
Pronunciation: / səbˈkɑn.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒn.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the part of the mind that is not fully aware but influences feelings, thoughts, and actions

Examples

The subconsciousness mind
Dreams often provide insights into our subconsciousness and hidden desires.
He was unaware of how much his subconsciousness influenced his daily decisions.
Hypnosis aims to access the subconsciousness to change unwanted behaviors.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: unconsciousness - the state of being unconscious or unaware, especially due to incapacity
