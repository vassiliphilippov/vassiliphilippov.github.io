subconsciousness

noun
mental state below conscious awareness
Pronunciation: / səbˈkɑn.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / sʌbˈkɒn.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the part of the mind that is not fully aware but influences feelings, thoughts, and actions
