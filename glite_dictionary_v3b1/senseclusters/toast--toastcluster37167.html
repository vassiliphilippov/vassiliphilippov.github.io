toast

noun
brief on-screen notification (GUI)
6.1 0.05/m
Pronunciation: / toast / (AmE), / tast / (BrE)
(in computing) a short, transient on-screen notification used in a graphical user interface to convey brief information or status without interrupting the user.

Examples

A toast message
After saving the photo, the app showed a small toast saying 'Saved successfully' at the bottom.
Developers added a toast to notify users when the offline edits were synced to the server.
On Android, toast notifications appear briefly without interrupting the user's interaction with the app.

Related Terms

  • synonym: notification - a message or alert that informs a user about something in an application or system
  • synonym: popup - a window or message that appears on screen, often to alert or inform the user
