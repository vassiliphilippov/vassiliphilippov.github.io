purple ground cherry

noun
South American annual plant with purple edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈpɝpəl ɡraʊnd ˈtʃɛri /; / ˈpɝː.pəl ɡraʊnd ˈtʃɛr.i / (AmE), / ˈpɜːpl ɡraʊnd ˈtʃɛri /; / ˈpɜː.pəl ɡraʊnd ˈtʃɛr.i / (BrE)
an annual plant that bears edible purple or yellow-purple fruits, similar to small tomatoes
