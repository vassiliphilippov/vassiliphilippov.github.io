hangman's halter

noun
rope for executions by hanging
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋmənz ˈhɔltər / (AmE), / ˈhæŋmənz ˈhɔːltə / (BrE)
a rope used by a hangman to execute people sentenced to death by hanging

Examples

The hangman's halter rope
No one wanted to be the one to fashion the hangman's halter for the execution.
Stories of the old west often mention outlaws meeting their end at the hangman's halter.
The condemned man stared at the hangman's halter with fear and resignation.
