impregnation

noun
material used for impregnating
Pronunciation: / ɪmˌprɛɡˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪmˌprɛɡˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
A substance or material used to impregnate something, especially to fill, saturate, or permeate it.

Examples

Protective impregnation
After treatment, the stone's surface shimmered due to the special impregnation applied.
Scientists tested a new impregnation that would make the fabric fire-retardant.
The impregnation gave the wood a water-resistant quality, making it suitable for outdoor use.
