woop

verb
to shout with excitement
Pronunciation: / wuːp / (AmE), / wuːp / (BrE)
to shout or cry out loudly, especially in joy or excitement

Examples

To woop loudly
The crowd began to woop as the band took the stage.
She could not help but woop with joy when she heard the good news.
Fans woop and clap every time their team scores a goal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: yell - to shout loudly
