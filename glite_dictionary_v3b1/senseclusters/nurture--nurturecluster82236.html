nurture

noun
care and encouragement for growth and development
4.3 3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnɝː.tʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈnɜː.tʃə / (BrE)
the care and encouragement given to someone, especially a child, to help them grow and develop as an accepted member of society

Examples

Parental nurture
Good nurture is essential for healthy emotional development in children.

The debate between nurture and nature continues in psychology.
