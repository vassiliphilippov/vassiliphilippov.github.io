gossip and hearsay

noun
unverified rumors or information
3.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈgɑː.səp ənd ˈhɪr.seɪ/ /; / /ˈgɑː.sɪp ənd ˈhɪr.seɪ/ /; / /ˈgɑː.səp ænd ˈhɪr.seɪ/ (AmE), /ˈgɒs.ɪp ənd ˈhɪə.seɪ/ /; / /ˈgɒs.ɪp ænd ˈhɪə.seɪ/ (BrE)
unverified information or rumors, often passed from person to person without confirmation of their truth
