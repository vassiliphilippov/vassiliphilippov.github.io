bushytail woodrat

noun
bushy-tailed rodent of the genus Neotoma
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃi teɪl ˈwʊdˌræt / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃi teɪl ˈwʊdˌræt / (BrE)
any of several bushy-tailed rodents of the genus Neotoma found in western North America, known for hoarding food and objects

Examples

A bushytail woodrat hoarding food
A bushytail woodrat can sometimes be seen dragging shiny objects back to its den.
Scientists studied the bushytail woodrat to understand its food-hoarding habits.
The bushytail woodrat constructs large nests of sticks and debris near rocky outcrops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pack rat - rodent of the genus Neotoma known for hoarding behavior
