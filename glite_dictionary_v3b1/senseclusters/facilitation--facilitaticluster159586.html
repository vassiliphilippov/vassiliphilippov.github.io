facilitation

noun
the act of making something easier
1.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / fəˌsɪləˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / fəˌsɪlɪˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of making something easier or helping it progress more smoothly

Examples

Meeting facilitation
Effective facilitation can improve communication and decision-making in meetings.
Technological advances have led to the facilitation of remote learning.
The facilitation of the workshop was handled by an experienced trainer.
Social facilitation can increase performance when others are present.
Technological advances have led to the facilitation of many daily tasks.
The facilitation of communication improved teamwork across the departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assistance - help or support; the action of assisting
  • synonym: help - giving assistance or support
  • antonym: hindrance - something that makes a process more difficult
  • antonym: obstruction - the act of blocking or hindering progress
