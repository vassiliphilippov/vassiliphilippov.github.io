bust-up

noun
serious argument ending a relationship
9.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /ˈbʌst.ʌp/ (AmE), /ˈbʌst.ʌp/ (BrE)
A serious argument or quarrel, especially one that results in the end of a relationship or friendship.

Examples

A bust-up argument

The band had a major bust-up and decided to go their separate ways.
There was a bust-up between the two partners over money.
