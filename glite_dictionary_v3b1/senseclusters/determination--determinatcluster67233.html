determination

noun
firmness of purpose
3.0 10/m

the quality of being firm or resolute in purpose; the ability to persist in achieving a goal despite difficulties

Examples

Determination to succeed
Her determination to finish the race inspired everyone watching.
Through sheer determination, he overcame all the challenges he faced.
With enough determination, you can accomplish almost anything.
