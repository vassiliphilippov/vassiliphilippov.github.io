pasty

noun
small meat pie or turnover
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsti / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsti / (BrE)
A small pastry case filled with meat, vegetables, or other ingredients, baked or fried; especially one typical of Cornwall, England.

Examples

Cornish pasty
She made a batch of homemade pasties for the picnic.
The traditional Cornish pasty is filled with beef, potatoes, and onions.
We bought a hot pasty from the bakery for lunch.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pie - a baked dish of fruit, meat, or vegetables covered with pastry
  • synonym: turnover - a type of pastry made by folding dough over a filling
