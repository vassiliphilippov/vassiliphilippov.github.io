cater-cornered

adjective
diagonally opposite or across
9.5 6.8
Pronunciation: / ˌkeɪtɚˈkɔrnɚd / (AmE), / ˌkeɪtəˈkɔːnəd / (BrE)
Situated or positioned diagonally across from something; placed at a slant across a space or intersection.

Cater-cornered position

Their house is cater-cornered from the corner store on the opposite side of the street.
