uncollectable

adjective
unable to stay composed
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəˈlɛktəbəl / (BrE)
unable to remain calm or composed, especially due to uncontrollable emotion such as laughter.

Examples

Uncollectable with laughter
She became completely uncollectable when she heard the hilarious story.
During the surprise party, he was so uncollectable that he could barely speak.
The audience was uncollectable with laughter after the comedian's final joke.

Related Terms

  • antonym: composed - calm and in control of one's emotions
  • synonym: hysterical - unable to control emotions, especially laughter or crying
