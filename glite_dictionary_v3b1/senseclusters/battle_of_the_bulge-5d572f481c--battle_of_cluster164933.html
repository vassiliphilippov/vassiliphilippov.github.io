Battle of the Bulge

noun
World War II battle in the Ardennes, 1944–45
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈbæɾl əv ðə bʌldʒ / (AmE), / ˈbætl əv ðə bʌldʒ / (BrE)
a major battle during World War II, fought in December 1944–January 1945, when German forces launched a surprise counteroffensive against Allied troops in the Ardennes region of Belgium and Luxembourg

Examples

The Battle of the Bulge fight
Many veterans of the Battle of the Bulge shared stories of fighting in harsh winter conditions.
The Battle of the Bulge was one of the last major German offensives on the Western Front.
The surprise attack at the Battle of the Bulge nearly split the Allied forces in two.
