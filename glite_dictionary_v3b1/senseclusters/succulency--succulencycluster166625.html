succulency

noun
juicy quality; appetizing moistness
3.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌk.jə.lən.si / (AmE), / ˈsʌk.jʊ.lən.si / (BrE)
the quality of being juicy and pleasant to taste

Examples

The succulency of leaves
Gardeners value some plants for the succulency of their leaves, which store water efficiently.
She praised the succulency of the lamb, noting how tender and juicy it was.
The succulency of the ripe peaches made them irresistible on a hot summer day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: succulence - the quality of being juicy and tasty
