mark of Cain

noun
sign of guilt or disgrace
8.0 8.6
Pronunciation: /mɑːrk əv keɪn/ (AmE), /mɑːk əv keɪn/ (BrE)
a sign or symbol that marks someone as guilty, shameful, or an outcast (from the Biblical story of Cain)
