Arctic

noun
region around the North Pole
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrktɪk / (AmE), / ˈɑːktɪk / (BrE)
the region of the Earth surrounding the North Pole, characterized by extreme cold and polar conditions, lying north of the Arctic Circle

Examples

The Arctic region
Polar bears are native to the Arctic and depend on sea ice for hunting.
Scientists are studying the effects of climate change in the Arctic region.
The beauty of the northern lights attracts many tourists to the Arctic each year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Antarctic - the region around the South Pole, opposite of the Arctic
