externalize

verb
make something external, objective, or visible
4.8 7.0
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈstɜrnəˌlaɪz /; / ɪkˈstɝː.nə.laɪz /; / ɪkˈstɝrnəˌlaɪz / (AmE), / ɪkˈstɜːnəlaɪz /; / ɪkˈstɜː.nə.laɪz / (BrE)
to express or treat an internal thought, feeling, or process as something outside yourself or observable
