recipiency

noun
openness to ideas
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɪpiənsi / (AmE), / rɪˈsɪpiənsi / (BrE)
the quality or state of being receptive or able to receive ideas, impressions, or influence

Examples

Recipiency to new ideas
Her recipiency to feedback helped the team improve the product quickly.
The teacher valued the students' recipiency to new learning methods.
A high level of recipiency can lead to personal and professional growth.
