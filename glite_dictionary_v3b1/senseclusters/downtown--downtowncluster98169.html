downtown

noun
the city center or business district
1.0 38/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆdaÊŠnËŒtaÊŠn / (AmE), / ËˆdaÊŠn.taÊŠn / (BrE)
the central business district or main area of a city or town

Examples

A busy downtown
Many new restaurants have opened in downtown over the past year.
The city's downtown is bustling with people during the workweek.
We stayed at a hotel close to downtown so we could walk to all the attractions.
During the festival, thousands of people gathered in downtown for the parade.
The company recently moved its headquarters to downtown to be closer to clients.
We went to downtown for dinner and a movie after work.

Synonyms

  • business district
    2.4 0.87/m
    a business district is the main area for shops and offices, while downtown is the central commercial area of a city.
  • city center
    1.1 1.27/m
    a city center has main shops, offices, and public buildings, while downtown especially means the city's commercial business district.
  • central city
    3.3 0.44/m
    a central city refers to the main urban area with cultural and business activities, while downtown names the central commercial district.

Related Terms

  • synonym: city center - the central part of a city, especially in British English
  • antonym: uptown - a residential or less commercial part of a city, often away from the business district
