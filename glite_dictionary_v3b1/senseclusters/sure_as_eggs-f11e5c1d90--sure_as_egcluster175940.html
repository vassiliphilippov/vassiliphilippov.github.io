sure as eggs

adverb
without a doubt; certainly
9.1
Pronunciation: / ʃʊr əz ɛɡz / (AmE), / ʃɔːr əz ɛɡz / (BrE)
Certainly; without a doubt; used to emphasize that something will definitely happen or is unequivocally true.

Examples

It's going to rain later, sure as eggs.
You can count on her support, sure as eggs.
