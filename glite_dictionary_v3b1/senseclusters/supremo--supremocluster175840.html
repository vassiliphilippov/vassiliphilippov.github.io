supremo

noun
most important person in an organization
8.4 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / suÀàpriÀêmo ä / (AmE), / suÀàpriÀêm…ô ä / (BrE)
a person who holds the highest position of authority or importance in an organization or activity

Examples
