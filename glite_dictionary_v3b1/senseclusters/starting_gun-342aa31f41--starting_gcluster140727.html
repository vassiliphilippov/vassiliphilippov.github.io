starting gun

noun
gun for signaling the start of a race
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtɪŋ ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtɪŋ ɡʌn / (BrE)
a gun fired to signal the beginning of a race or other timed event

Examples

The starting gun fires
After the starting gun sounded, the athletes dashed off the blocks.
Spectators held their breath until the starting gun marked the official beginning of the marathon.
The runners took their positions, waiting for the starting gun to fire.
