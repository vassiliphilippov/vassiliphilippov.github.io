clear

adjective
distinct and undimmed (sound or color)
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /klɪr/ (AmE), /klɪə/ (BrE)
(of a sound or color) free from anything that lessens purity, sharpness, or intensity; distinct and unmuddied.
