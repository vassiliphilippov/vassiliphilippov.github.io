racist

noun
person with racist beliefs
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: /ˈreɪ.sɪst/ (AmE), /ˈreɪ.sɪst/ (BrE)
a person who believes that one race is superior to others and discriminates or is prejudiced against people of other races

Examples

A racist person

Many activists are focused on challenging the views held by every racist in their community.
The politician was widely condemned as a racist after making offensive remarks about immigrants.
