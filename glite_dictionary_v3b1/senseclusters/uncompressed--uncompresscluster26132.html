uncompressed

adjective
not reduced in size
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnkəmˈprɛst / (AmE), / ˌʌnkəmˈprɛst / (BrE)
not reduced in size or quality by compression, especially referring to files or data

Examples

An uncompressed video file
The uncompressed video file was too large to send by email.
Photographers often prefer to work with uncompressed images for better editing quality.
You need more storage space to save uncompressed audio tracks on your device.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compressed - reduced in size or quality by compression
