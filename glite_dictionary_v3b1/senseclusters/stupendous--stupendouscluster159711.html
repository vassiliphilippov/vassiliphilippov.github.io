stupendous

adjective
extremely impressive, immense
7.9
Pronunciation: / stuˈpɛndəs / (AmE), / stjuːˈpɛndəs / (BrE)
extremely impressive or large in size, amount, or degree; astonishing or immense.

Examples

A stupendous effort
She made a stupendous effort to finish the project ahead of schedule.
The mountain range offered a stupendous view that left us speechless.
The team achieved a stupendous victory, breaking all previous records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: colossal - extremely large or great
  • synonym: prodigious - remarkably or impressively great in extent or size
  • antonym: tiny - very small in size or amount
