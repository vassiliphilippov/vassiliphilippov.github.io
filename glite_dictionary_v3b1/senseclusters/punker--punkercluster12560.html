punker

noun
practical joker
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈpʌŋkə / (BrE)
a person who plays practical jokes or tricks to embarrass or fool others

Examples

A punker plays tricks
The punker hid everyone's shoes as a joke during the sleepover.
After being fooled by the punker, everyone laughed at the harmless prank.
No one wanted to sit next to the punker because of his constant tricks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joker - someone who jokes or plays tricks on others
  • synonym: prankster - a person who plays tricks or practical jokes
  • antonym: victim - a person who is tricked or made fun of
