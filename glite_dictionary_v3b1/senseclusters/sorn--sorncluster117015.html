sorn

noun
beggar; sponger
9.9
Pronunciation: /sɑːrn/ /; / /sɔːrn/ (AmE), /sɔːn/ (BrE)
a person who obtains food or lodging by imposing on others, especially by begging; now archaic or dialectal

Examples

A wandering sorn
The villagers were wary of the wandering sorn who appeared at their doors each winter.
In old tales, a sorn would travel from farm to farm seeking shelter and food.
During the festival, the town saw more than one sorn hoping for a free meal.

Related Terms
