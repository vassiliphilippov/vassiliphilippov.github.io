repoussé

noun
metalworking technique
9.9
Pronunciation: /rə.puː.ˈseɪ/ (AmE), /rə.puː.ˈseɪ/ (BrE)
a method of decorating metal by hammering the reverse side to create a raised design, or the raised design itself

Examples

Repousse method
The silver vase was decorated with intricate repoussé patterns that caught the light beautifully.
Artisans used the repoussé technique to create raised floral designs on the copper plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chasing - a technique of detailing the front of metal to refine a design
  • synonym: embossing - a technique for creating raised designs on a surface
  • antonym: engraving - cutting or carving a design into a hard surface
