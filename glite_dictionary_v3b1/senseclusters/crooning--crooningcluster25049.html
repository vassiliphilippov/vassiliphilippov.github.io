crooning

noun
sentimental singing style
Pronunciation: / ˈkruːnɪŋ /; / ˈkruː.nɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkruːnɪŋ /; / ˈkruː.nɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of singing in a soft, gentle, and often sentimental way
