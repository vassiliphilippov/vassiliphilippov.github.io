reception

noun
the act of receiving
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsɛpʃən / (AmE), / rɪˈsɛpʃən / (BrE)
the way in which someone or something is received, welcomed, or accepted by others

Examples

Warm reception

The reception of the new policy by the community was overwhelmingly positive.
We were delighted by the warm reception we received at the conference.
Her remarks were met with an enthusiastic reception by the audience.
The artist was surprised by the warm reception in her hometown.
The new policy received a mixed reception from the public.
