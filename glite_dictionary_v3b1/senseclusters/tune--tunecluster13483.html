tune

verb
adjust for better functioning
Pronunciation: / tun / (AmE), / tjuːn / (BrE)
to adjust (a machine, system, or device) so that it works more efficiently or performs as desired

Examples

To tune a machine
Technicians regularly tune the broadcasting equipment to ensure clear transmission.
The mechanic will tune your car to improve its fuel efficiency.
You may need to tune the air conditioner for optimal performance in hot weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adjust - to change something slightly to make it work better
  • synonym: calibrate - to set or correct the measurements of an instrument
