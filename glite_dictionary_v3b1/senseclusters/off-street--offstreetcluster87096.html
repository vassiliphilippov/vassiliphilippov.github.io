off-street

adjective
not situated on a public street
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔf.strit / (AmE), / ˈɒf.striːt / (BrE)
Situated away from a public street, especially referring to facilities such as parking or loading areas that are not directly on the street itself.

Examples

Off-street parking
Because the event was crowded, finding off-street parking proved to be a challenge.
Residents in the neighborhood have access to off-street parking spaces behind their homes.
The new shopping center offers ample off-street loading zones for delivery trucks.
