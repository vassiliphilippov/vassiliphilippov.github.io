cancer

noun
disease caused by uncontrolled cell growth
2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkænsər / (AmE), / ˈkænsə / (BrE)
A serious disease characterized by the uncontrolled division of abnormal cells in a part of the body, which can spread to other parts.

Examples

Lung cancer
Early detection of cancer can greatly increase the chances of successful treatment.
Many organizations raise funds for cancer research and awareness programs.
She lost her father to cancer last year.
