Synonyms

  • malignant tumor
    5.0 0.21/m
    malignant tumor is a mass of cancerous cells that often grows and spreads, while cancer is the disease these tumors form in the body.
  • malignant neoplasm
    5.2 0.05/m
    malignant neoplasm is the medical term for a cancerous growth that invades tissue, while cancer is the wider disease people talk about.
  • malignancy
    8.0 0.87/m
    malignancy is the presence of harmful cancer cells that may spread, while cancer is the name for the disease those cells cause.
  • malignant neoplastic disease
    7.4 0.00/m
    malignant neoplastic disease is a very formal medical phrase for a spreading tumor, while cancer is the common word patients and doctors use.
