fiber optic cable

noun
cable for transmitting data as light

a cable consisting of thin strands of glass or plastic (optical fibers) used to transmit data as pulses of light, enabling high-speed communication

Examples

A fiber optic cable
A fiber optic cable can carry enormous amounts of information over long distances without significant loss.
Many internet providers use fiber optic cable to deliver fast connections to homes and businesses.
Technicians spent the weekend repairing the damaged fiber optic cable that connects the two cities.
Engineers installed a fibre optic cable under the ocean to connect the two continents.

The new data center will rely on a fibre optic cable network for fast internet speeds.

Synonyms

  • fiber
    3.7 8.22/m
    fiber can mean the single glass strand or the whole cable system, while fiber optic cable means the assembled multi-strand data cable.
  • optical fiber
    4.2 0.53/m
    optical fiber is a single thin glass strand that carries light, while fiber optic cable is the full multi-strand cable used for data.
