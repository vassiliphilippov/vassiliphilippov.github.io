shyly

adverb
in a shy or timid manner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪ.li / (BrE)
In a shy, timid, or bashful manner.

Examples

To shyly smile
He shyly introduced himself to the group, his voice barely above a whisper.
She shyly accepted the compliment, hardly meeting his gaze.
The child looked at the stranger and smiled shyly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boldly - in a confident and courageous manner
  • synonym: timidly - in a manner showing a lack of courage or confidence
