debutant

noun
person introduced to society
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪbjuˌtɑnt / (AmE), / ˈdeɪbjuːtɑːnt / (BrE)
a young person, especially a young woman, who is formally introduced to society at a special event or ball

Examples

A debutant at the ball
The young debutant was nervous before her first formal dance.
Each debutant was presented to the guests during the elegant ceremony.
As a debutant, she wore a traditional white dress and gloves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debutante - a young woman making her formal entrance into society
