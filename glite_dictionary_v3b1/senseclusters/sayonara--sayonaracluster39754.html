sayonara

noun
farewell; goodbye (often with finality)
Pronunciation: / saɪəˈnɑrə / (AmE), / saɪəˈnɑːrə / (BrE)
used to say goodbye or farewell, often with a sense of finality or departure

Examples

It’s not just a temporary goodbye; this is truly sayonara for us.
