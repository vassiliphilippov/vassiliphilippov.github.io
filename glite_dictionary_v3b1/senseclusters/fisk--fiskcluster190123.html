fisk

verb
to critique point by point
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɪsk / (AmE), / fɪsk / (BrE)
to analyze and criticize a text in detail, often addressing each point individually

Examples

To fisk an article
The blogger decided to fisk the politician's speech, addressing each claim with counterarguments.
After reading the editorial, she spent hours fisking its main points on her website.
He enjoys fisking articles that contain misleading information or logical fallacies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debunk - to expose the falseness of an idea or claim
  • antonym: praise - to express approval or admiration
