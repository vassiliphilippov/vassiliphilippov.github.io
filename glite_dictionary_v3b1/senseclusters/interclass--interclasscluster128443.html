interclass

adjective
between school classes
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈklæs / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈklɑːs / (BrE)
relating to competitions, events, or activities held between different classes within a school.

Examples

Interclass competition
The school organized an interclass debate to encourage friendly competition among students.
She won a medal in the annual interclass chess tournament.
Teachers praised the teamwork shown during the interclass sports day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: intraclass - within the same class or group
