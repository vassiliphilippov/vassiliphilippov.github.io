Queensland kauri

noun
Australian timber tree, Agathis robusta
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkwiːnzlænd ˈkaʊri / (AmE), / ˈkwiːnzlænd ˈkɔːri / (BrE)
An Australian timber tree (Agathis robusta) that resembles the kauri but has much lighter, softer wood.

Examples

The Queensland kauri tree
Several Queensland kauri trees grow in the botanical gardens of Brisbane.
The Queensland kauri is prized for its high-quality timber used in furniture making.
Unlike other conifers, the wood of the Queensland kauri is relatively soft and light.

Related Terms
