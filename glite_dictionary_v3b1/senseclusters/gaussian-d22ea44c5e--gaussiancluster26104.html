Gaussian

noun
bell-shaped distribution
Pronunciation: / ˈɡaʊsiən / (AmE), / ˈɡaʊsiən / (BrE)
a mathematical function or distribution with a symmetrical bell-shaped curve, often used in statistics and probability

Examples

Gaussian curve
The scientist modeled the noise in the experiment as a Gaussian to simplify the analysis.
Many natural phenomena, such as heights and test scores, follow a Gaussian distribution.
The software can generate random numbers based on a Gaussian for simulations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: normal distribution - a probability distribution with a symmetrical bell-shaped curve
  • antonym: uniform distribution - a probability distribution where all outcomes are equally likely
