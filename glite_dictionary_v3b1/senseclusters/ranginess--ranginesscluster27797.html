ranginess

noun
quality of being long-limbed and slender
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪndʒi.nɪs / (AmE), / ˈreɪndʒɪnɪs / (BrE)
The quality of being long-limbed and slender; having a lanky or thin appearance.

Examples

The ranginess of a dancer
Her ranginess made her stand out in the crowd of dancers.
Some breeds of dogs are prized for their ranginess and agility.
The basketball coach admired the player's ranginess as an advantage on the court.
