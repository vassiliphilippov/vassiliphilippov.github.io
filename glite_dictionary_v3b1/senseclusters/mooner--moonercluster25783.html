mooner

noun
habitual daydreamer
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnər / (AmE), / ˈmuːnə / (BrE)
a person who is often dreamy, idle, or lost in thought, especially by daydreaming or pining over someone or something

Examples

A dreamy mooner
The teacher noticed the mooner staring out the window instead of paying attention to the lesson.
At lunch, everyone teased Jake for being such a mooner whenever he thought about his crush.
Her friends called her a mooner because she was always lost in her own thoughts.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dreamer - a person who spends a lot of time thinking about pleasant things that are unlikely to happen
  • synonym: idler - a person who avoids work or spends time doing nothing
  • antonym: realist - a person who accepts and deals with things as they really are
