mooner

noun
habitual daydreamer
8.2 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàmuÀên…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàmuÀên…ô / (BrE)
a person who is often dreamy, idle, or lost in thought, especially by daydreaming or pining over someone or something
