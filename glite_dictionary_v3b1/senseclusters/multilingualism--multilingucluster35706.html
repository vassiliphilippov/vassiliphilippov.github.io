multilingualism

noun
use of several languages
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈlɪŋɡwɪlɪzəm / (BrE)
the ability to use or understand several languages fluently

Examples

Personal multilingualism
Her multilingualism allowed her to work in several different countries with ease.
Schools are increasingly recognizing the benefits of multilingualism in a globalized world.
His multilingualism impressed the interviewers during the job application process.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: bilingualism - the ability to use two languages fluently
  • degree lower: monolingualism - the ability to use only one language
