face cream

noun
moisturizing or cleansing cream for the face
Pronunciation: / feɪs kriːm / (AmE), / feɪs kriːm / (BrE)
A cream applied to the skin of the face, used to soften, moisturize, or clean it, especially as part of a beauty routine.

Examples

Apply face cream
I always remove my makeup before bed and use a gentle face cream.
She applies face cream every night to keep her skin hydrated.
This face cream is suitable for sensitive and dry skin types.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moisturizer - a cosmetic lotion or cream used to prevent dryness in the skin
