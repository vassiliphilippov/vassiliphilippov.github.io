grimace

verb
to twist the face to show pain or discomfort
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪˈmeɪs / (AmE), / ˈɡrɪməs / (BrE)
to twist your face in an ugly or contorted way, usually to express pain, disgust, or wry amusement

Examples

To grimace in pain
He grimaced in pain when the doctor pressed on his ankle.
She grimaced as she bit into the sour lemon.
The child grimaced at the sight of the broccoli on her plate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scowl - to frown in an angry or bad-tempered way
