game warden

noun
wildlife protection officer
Pronunciation: / ɡeɪm ˈwɔrdn̩ / (AmE), / ɡeɪm ˈwɔːdn̩ / (BrE)
an official responsible for managing, protecting, and overseeing game animals and wildlife, often enforcing laws related to hunting and fishing

Examples

The game warden arrives
After reporting the injured deer, we waited for the game warden to arrive.
Many game wardens work in remote areas to protect endangered species.
The game warden patrolled the forest to ensure hunters followed the rules.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ranger - a person in charge of managing and protecting natural reserves or parks
