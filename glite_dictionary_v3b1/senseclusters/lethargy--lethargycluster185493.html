lethargy

noun
lack of energy or enthusiasm
9.6 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈle.θər.dʒiː/ /; / /ˈle.θər.dʒɪ/ (AmE), /ˈle.θə.dʒiː/ /; / /ˈle.θə.dʒɪ/ (BrE)
a state of tiredness, lack of energy, or inactivity (sometimes due to illness)
