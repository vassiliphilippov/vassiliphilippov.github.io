tongue

noun
animal tongue used as meat
Pronunciation: / tʌŋ / (AmE), / tʌŋ / (BrE)
the soft, movable organ in the mouth used for tasting, swallowing, and speaking; also, the tongue of an animal, sometimes eaten as food

Examples

A tongue in the mouth
In some countries, tongue is considered a delicacy and is cooked with spices.
The chef served slices of pickled tongue as a starter.
We ordered beef tongue tacos at the market.
Doctors use a depressor to examine a patient's tongue.
She burned her tongue on the hot soup.
The cat licked its fur with its rough tongue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gum - the tissue surrounding the teeth
  • synonym: offal - the entrails and internal organs of an animal used as food
  • synonym: palate - the roof of the mouth
