handle

verb
touch or hold with the hands
3.5 1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈhændl̩ / (AmE), / ˈhændl / (BrE)
to touch, hold, use, or control something with your hands, especially carefully or skillfully

Examples

To handle a vase
He carefully handled the fragile glass vase.
Please do not handle the artifacts on display.
The store clerk asked me not to handle the merchandise.
She handled the situation calmly and efficiently, impressing her colleagues.
The athlete handled the ball expertly, weaving through the defenders.
The chef handled the sharp knives with remarkable precision.
