unrestrictive

adjective
not limiting; free from constraint
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪv / (AmE), / ˌʌn.rɪˈstrɪk.tɪv / (BrE)
not limiting or imposing restrictions; open or free from constraint

Examples

An unrestrictive style
Her parenting style is notably unrestrictive, giving her children plenty of freedom.
The new policy is unrestrictive and allows employees to work from anywhere.
They preferred an unrestrictive approach to dress codes at the school.

Related Terms
