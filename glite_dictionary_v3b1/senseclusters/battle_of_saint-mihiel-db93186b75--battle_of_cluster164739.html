Battle of Saint-Mihiel

proper noun
World War I battle in France, 1918
Pronunciation: / ˌbæɾəl əv sænt miˈɛl / (AmE), / ˌbætl əv sæ̃ miˈjɛl / (BrE)
a major World War I battle in 1918, where American forces launched their first offensive in France as part of the Meuse-Argonne operation

Examples

Battle of Saint-Mihiel 1918
A memorial was erected in France to honor soldiers who fought at the Battle of Saint-Mihiel.
Historians often discuss the tactics used during the Battle of Saint-Mihiel as a model for later offensives.
The Battle of Saint-Mihiel marked a turning point for American involvement in World War I.
