defame

verb
damage someone's reputation with false statements
1.6 5.5
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfeɪm/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfeɪm/ (BrE)
to damage the reputation of someone by making false or malicious statements about them

Examples

To defame a person
He was furious when the newspaper defamed him with false accusations.
Spreading rumors that defame someone's character can result in serious consequences.
The company sued the blogger for having defamed its CEO online.
