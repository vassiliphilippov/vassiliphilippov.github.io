uncreased

adjective
not marked by creases or wrinkles
8.4
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkrist / (AmE), / ʌnˈkriːst / (BrE)
(of fabric or paper) smooth and without any creases or wrinkles.

Examples

An uncreased shirt
He wore a freshly ironed, uncreased shirt to the meeting.
I always pack my clothes carefully so they stay uncreased during the trip.
She made sure the invitation cards arrived uncreased and in perfect condition.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creased - marked by lines or folds from being folded or pressed
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • degree higher: wrinkled - having small lines or folds, especially on fabric or skin
