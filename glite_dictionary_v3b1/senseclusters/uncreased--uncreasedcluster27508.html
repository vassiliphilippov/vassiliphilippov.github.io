uncreased

adjective
not marked by creases or wrinkles
3.5 0.00/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàkrist / (AmE), /  ånÀàkriÀêst / (BrE)
(of fabric or paper) smooth and without any creases or wrinkles.

Examples

An uncreased shirt
He wore a freshly ironed, uncreased shirt to the meeting.
I always pack my clothes carefully so they stay uncreased during the trip.
She made sure the invitation cards arrived uncreased and in perfect condition.

Synonyms

  • creaseless
    4.6 0.01/m
    creaseless describes a surface kept smooth without folds, while uncreased often refers to fabric or paper specifically.
  • unwrinkled
    3.0 0.09/m
    unwrinkled means smooth and free of wrinkles on skin or fabric, while uncreased focuses on absence of fold lines in paper or cloth.
  • wrinkleless
    4.6 0.01/m
    wrinkleless describes a perfectly smooth surface with no wrinkles, while uncreased stresses the lack of creases or fold lines.
  • unrumpled
    5.3 0.02/m
    unrumpled looks neat and tidy without wrinkles, while uncreased simply means fabric or paper has no creases.

Related Terms

  • antonym: creased - marked by lines or folds from being folded or pressed
  • synonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
  • degree higher: wrinkled - having small lines or folds, especially on fabric or skin
