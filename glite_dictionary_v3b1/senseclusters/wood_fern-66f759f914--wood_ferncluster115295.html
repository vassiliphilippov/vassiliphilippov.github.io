wood fern

noun
fern of the genus Dryopteris
Pronunciation: / wʊd fɝn / (AmE), / wʊd fɜːn / (BrE)
any of various ferns of the genus Dryopteris, commonly found in woodland areas

Examples

A wood fern plant
Gardeners often plant wood fern as a hardy ground cover beneath trees.
The shady area behind the house was filled with wood ferns and wildflowers.
We identified the plant as a wood fern by its divided, lacy fronds.
