plenteous

adjective
abundant; existing in great supply
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈplɛn.ti.əs / (AmE), / ˈplɛn.tɪ.əs / (BrE)
affording or existing in great supply; abundant

Examples

A plenteous supply
Her garden is always plenteous with colorful flowers throughout the summer.
Thanks to the plenteous rainfall, the rivers were full and the fields were green.
The farmers celebrated a plenteous harvest after a season of good weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abundant - present in great quantity
  • synonym: plentiful - existing in large amounts; abundant
  • antonym: scarce - insufficient in amount; hard to find
