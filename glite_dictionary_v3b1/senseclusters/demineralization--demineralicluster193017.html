demineralization

noun
removal of minerals from a liquid
7.4
Pronunciation: / diˌmɪnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən /; / diˌmɪnəɹəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diːˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən /; / diˌmɪnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the removal of minerals and mineral salts, especially from water or other liquids
