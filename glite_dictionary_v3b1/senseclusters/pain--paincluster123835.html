pain

noun
emotional suffering or distress
1.0
Pronunciation: / peɪn / (AmE), / peɪn / (BrE)

Examples

Heart pain

She tries to hide her pain, but her eyes reveal everything.
The pain of saying goodbye was almost unbearable for her.
