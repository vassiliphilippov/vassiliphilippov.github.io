antirealist

noun
opponent of realism
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌæntaɪˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who opposes realistic representation, especially in art or criticism, and prefers nonrealistic or surreal styles

Examples

Antirealist style in art
The painter was known as an antirealist who rejected traditional techniques in favor of abstract forms.
Many critics labeled the director an antirealist because of his preference for dreamlike, symbolic scenes.
As an antirealist, she believed that art should express emotions rather than depict reality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: realist - a person who favors realistic representation in art or criticism
  • synonym: surrealist - an artist or writer who uses surreal, dreamlike imagery
