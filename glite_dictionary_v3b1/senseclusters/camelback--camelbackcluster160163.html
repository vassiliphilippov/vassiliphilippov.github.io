camelback

noun
camel's hump
Pronunciation: / ˈkæməlˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈkæməlˌbæk / (BrE)
the hump on a camel's back that stores fat and helps the animal survive in dry conditions

Examples

Camel's camelback
The camelback allows camels to travel long distances without needing food or water.
Scientists studied how the camelback stores fat to help the animal survive in the desert.
A healthy camelback is a sign that the camel is well-nourished.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hump - a rounded raised part on the back of some animals
