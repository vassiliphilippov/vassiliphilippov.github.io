Lowlander

noun
person from Scottish Lowlands
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌlændər / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌlændə / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the Lowlands region of Scotland.

Examples

Scottish Lowlanders
Many Lowlanders migrated to cities during the Industrial Revolution.
She is proud to call herself a Lowlander, with deep roots in southern Scotland.
Traditions differ greatly between the Lowlander and Highlander communities of Scotland.
