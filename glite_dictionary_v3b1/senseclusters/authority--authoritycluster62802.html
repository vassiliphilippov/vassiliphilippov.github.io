authority

noun
an authoritative written work or source
4.2 3.5
Pronunciation: /ə.ˈθɔːr.ə.tiː/ /; / /ə.ˈθɑːr.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒr.ə.tiː/ /; / /ɔː.ˈθɒr.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
