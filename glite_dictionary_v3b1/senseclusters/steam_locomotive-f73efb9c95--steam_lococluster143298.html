steam locomotive

noun
train engine powered by steam
8.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /stiːm loʊ.kə.ˈmoʊ.tɪv/ (AmE), /stiːm ləʊ.kə.ˈməʊ.tɪv/ (BrE)
A railway locomotive powered by a steam engine, typically using coal or wood to heat water and produce steam.
