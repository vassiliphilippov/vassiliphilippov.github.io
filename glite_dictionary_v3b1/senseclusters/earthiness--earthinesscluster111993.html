earthiness

noun
natural or soil-like quality
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθinəs / (AmE), / ˈɜːθinəs / (BrE)
the quality of being natural, simple, or resembling soil or earth in character or appearance

Examples

Natural earthiness
The earthiness of the artist's paintings made them feel warm and inviting.
She appreciated the earthiness of the bread, which reminded her of her childhood home.
The wine's earthiness balanced its fruity notes, creating a complex flavor.

Related Terms

  • antonym: artificiality - the quality of being artificial or not natural
  • synonym: naturalness - the quality of being natural or not artificial
  • synonym: simplicity - the quality of being simple or uncomplicated
