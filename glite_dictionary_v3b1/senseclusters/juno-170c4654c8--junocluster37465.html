Juno

proper noun
NASA spacecraft studying Jupiter
6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈdʒuː.noʊ/ (AmE), /ˈdʒuː.nəʊ/ (BrE)
A NASA robotic spacecraft launched in 2011 to study the planet Jupiter, including its atmosphere, magnetic field, and interior structure.

Examples

NASA Juno spacecraft
NASA's Juno spacecraft entered orbit around Jupiter after a long interplanetary journey.
Scientists used data from Juno to study Jupiter's magnetic field and atmospheric composition.
During the mission, engineers adjusted Juno's orbit to improve the probe's observations of the poles.
