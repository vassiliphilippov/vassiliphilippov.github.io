cojones

noun
slang for courage
Pronunciation: / koʊˈhoʊneɪz / (AmE), / kəˈhəʊneɪz / (BrE)
slang (often vulgar) for courage or boldness; literally, the testicles (used figuratively to mean bravery)

Examples

Have cojones to act
It takes real cojones to start your own business from scratch.
She showed her cojones by speaking out against the unfair policy.
You need some serious cojones to perform on stage in front of thousands.
He showed a lot of cojones by standing up to the boss in front of everyone.
The comedian's jokes took real cojones to deliver on live television.
After being hit in the cojones, he fell to the ground in pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balls - vulgar slang for testicles or courage
  • antonym: cowardice - lack of courage to face danger or difficulty
  • synonym: guts - courage or bravery in difficult situations
  • synonym: nerve - the ability to face danger or difficulty with courage
