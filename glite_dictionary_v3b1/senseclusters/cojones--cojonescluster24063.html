cojones

noun
slang for courage
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈhoʊ.neɪz/ /; / /kə.ˈhoʊ.niːz/ (AmE), /kə.ˈhəʊ.neɪz/ /; / /kə.ˈhəʊ.niːz/ (BrE)
slang (often vulgar) for courage or boldness; literally, the testicles (used figuratively to mean bravery)

Examples

After being hit in the cojones, he fell to the ground in pain.
