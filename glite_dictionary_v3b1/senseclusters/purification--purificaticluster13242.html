purification

noun
ceremonial cleansing or ritual purification
Pronunciation: / ˌpjʊr.ə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌpjʊə.rɪ.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of ceremonial cleansing from defilement or uncleanness, especially through religious or ritual actions

Examples

Purification ritual
Many cultures have special purification ceremonies before important religious events.
She underwent a ritual purification after returning from a long journey.
The purification of the temple was accompanied by prayers and offerings.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defilement - the act of making something impure
  • synonym: purify - to make clean or free from impurities
