sleepy-eyed

adjective
having drowsy or tired-looking eyes
Pronunciation: / ˈsliːpi aɪd / (AmE), / ˈsliːpi aɪd / (BrE)
Having eyes that show signs of drowsiness or the urge to sleep.

Examples

A sleepy-eyed cat
A sleepy-eyed cat lounged on the sunny windowsill, barely opening its eyes.
She arrived at breakfast looking sleepy-eyed after staying up all night to finish her project.
The sleepy-eyed toddler yawned and rubbed his face during the car ride home.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drowsy - feeling sleepy and lethargic
