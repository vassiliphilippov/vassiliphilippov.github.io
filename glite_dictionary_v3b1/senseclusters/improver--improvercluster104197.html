improver

noun
one who makes better
Pronunciation: / ɪmˈpruvər / (AmE), / ɪmˈpruːvə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes something better or helps it progress

Examples

A team improver
As an improver, she always looks for ways to make the team more efficient.
The new software acts as an improver for the company's workflow.
He was known as an improver who never settled for average results.

Related Terms

  • antonym: detractor - a person or thing that diminishes the value or quality of something
  • synonym: developer - a person who creates or improves something
  • synonym: enhancer - someone or something that improves or increases the quality of something
