mythologic

adjective
relating to myths or legends
Pronunciation: / mɪθəˈlɑdʒɪk / (AmE), / mɪθəˈlɒdʒɪk / (BrE)
Relating to or based on traditional myths or legends rather than fact or history.

Examples

A mythologic hero
Ancient poets often wrote about mythologic heroes who performed impossible feats.
He enjoys studying the mythologic traditions of ancient Greece.
The dragon is a popular mythologic creature in many cultures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mythical - existing only in myths; not real
