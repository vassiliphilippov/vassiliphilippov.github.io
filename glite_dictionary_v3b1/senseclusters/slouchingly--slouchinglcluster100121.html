slouchingly

adverb
in a slouching manner
Pronunciation: / ˈslaʊtʃɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈslaʊtʃɪŋli / (BrE)
In a slouching manner; with a bent or drooping posture or gait.

Examples

To walk slouchingly
He walked slouchingly into the meeting, giving the impression of boredom.
She leaned slouchingly against the wall, her energy spent after a long day.
The teenager sat slouchingly on the couch, barely listening to his parents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slouch - to stand, sit, or move in a lazy, drooping way
