haywire

noun
wire for tying bales of hay
Pronunciation: / ˈheɪˌwaɪɚ / (AmE), / ˈheɪˌwaɪə / (BrE)
Thin wire used for binding bales of hay.

Examples

A piece of haywire
A length of haywire was all he needed to quickly fix the broken gate.
Old fences on the property were held together with pieces of haywire.
The farmer used haywire to secure the loose bales in the truck.
