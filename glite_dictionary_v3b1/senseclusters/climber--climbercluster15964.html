climber

noun
someone who climbs mountains or rocks
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈklaɪmɚ / (AmE), / ˈklaɪmə / (BrE)
a person who climbs, especially mountains, rocks, stairs, or other high places

Examples

Mountain climber
As an avid climber, she spends most weekends scaling cliffs and exploring new routes.
The experienced climber reached the summit before noon despite harsh weather conditions.
The rescue team saved a stranded climber after he became stuck on the ledge.
Every climber must be prepared for sudden changes in weather.
She is an experienced climber who enjoys scaling steep cliffs.
The seasoned climber reached the summit just before dawn.

  • synonym: mountaineer - a person who climbs mountains
