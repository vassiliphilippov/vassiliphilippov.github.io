family room

noun
room for informal family activities
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ruːm / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ruːm / (BrE)
a room in a house used for informal entertaining, relaxation, and family activities

Examples

The family room
Children's toys are scattered all over the family room after playtime.
The new house has a spacious family room that opens onto the garden.
We spend most evenings together in the family room watching movies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: living room - a room in a house for general and formal social activities
  • degree higher: parlor - an older term for a sitting room in a private house
