self-assured

adjective
confident in yourself
Pronunciation: / sɛlf əˈʃʊrd / (AmE), / sɛlf əˈʃʊəd / (BrE)
confident in your own abilities or worth; showing self-confidence and assurance.

Examples

A self-assured person
After years of experience, she became noticeably more self-assured in her decisions.
He gave a self-assured presentation that impressed everyone in the room.
Maria is so self-assured that she handles criticism with grace and poise.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confident - having a strong belief in one's abilities
  • antonym: insecure - lacking confidence or assurance
