solicit

verb
offer sexual services for payment
Pronunciation: / səˈlɪsɪt /; / səˈlɪs.ɪt / (AmE), / səˈlɪsɪt /; / səˈlɪs.ɪt / (BrE)
to approach someone with an offer of sexual services or with sexual advances, often in public

Examples

To solicit clients
He was fined for attempting to solicit clients on the street.
It is illegal to solicit for prostitution in this area.
The police arrested her for trying to solicit men outside the nightclub.
He was fined for trying to solicit passersby in the park late at night.
It is illegal to solicit strangers for sexual favors in most countries.
The lawyer was dismissed from the bar for attempting to solicit women clients inappropriately.

Related Terms

  • synonym: court - to try to win the love or affection of someone, often with the intention of marriage
  • synonym: proposition - to make a sexual advance or offer to someone
  • synonym: prostitute - a person who offers sexual services for payment
