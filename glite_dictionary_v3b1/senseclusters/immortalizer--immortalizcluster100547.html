immortalizer

noun
one who makes famous
8.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈmɔːrtəˌlaɪzər / (AmE), / ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪzə / (BrE)
a person or thing that makes someone or something remembered forever

Examples

Immortalizer of heroes
The poet became the immortalizer of her country's heroes through her verses.
Through his groundbreaking inventions, he was hailed as the immortalizer of modern technology.
The documentary served as an immortalizer of the village's unique traditions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: forgetter - someone who fails to remember or disregards something
  • synonym: preserver - someone who keeps something from being lost or forgotten
