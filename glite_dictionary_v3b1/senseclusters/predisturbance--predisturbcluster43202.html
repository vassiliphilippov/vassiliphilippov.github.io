predisturbance

adjective
before disruption
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːdɪˈstɝːbəns / (AmE), / ˌpriːdɪˈstɜːbəns / (BrE)
describing a state or condition existing before a disturbance or disruption occurs

Examples

Predisturbance water quality
Scientists measured the predisturbance water quality to assess the impact of the construction project.
The predisturbance landscape provided a baseline for ecological restoration efforts.
Comparing predisturbance and postdisturbance data helps researchers understand environmental changes.
