suchlike

adjective
of the same or similar kind
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌtʃ.laɪk / (AmE), / ˈsʌtʃ.laɪk / (BrE)
of a similar kind; of the same type as something just mentioned.

Examples

Suchlike activities
He enjoys hiking, climbing, and other suchlike activities.
The antique shop sells old coins, stamps, and suchlike collectibles.
They brought sandwiches, drinks, and suchlike for the picnic.

Related Terms

  • antonym: different - not the same as another or each other
  • synonym: similar - having a resemblance in appearance, character, or quantity
