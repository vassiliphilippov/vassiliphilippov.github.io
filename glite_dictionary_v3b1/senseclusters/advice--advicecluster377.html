Synonyms

  • guidance
    2.1 23/m
    guidance offers ongoing help and information to solve problems and make decisions, while advice is a general opinion or suggestion.
  • direction
    1.0 8.56/m
    direction gives clear steps on what action to take or how to do something, while advice is more of a suggestion to consider.
  • recommendation
    2.5 45/m
    recommendation suggests a preferred choice or approves a person or thing, while advice is a general suggestion about what to do.
  • tip
    1.0 32/m
    tip gives a short, practical trick or hint to make tasks easier, while advice is broader and can be longer or more formal.
