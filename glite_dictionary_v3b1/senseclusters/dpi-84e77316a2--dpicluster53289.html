DPI

sym
image resolution unit
5.6
Pronunciation: / diː piː aɪ / (AmE), / diː piː aɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'dots per inch', a measurement of image resolution indicating the number of dots in a printed or digital inch

Examples

600 DPI image
The printer produces sharp images at 600 DPI.
For better quality, increase the DPI setting before printing your photos.
Graphic designers often work with images at a high DPI for professional results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: PPI - pixels per inch, a measurement of digital image resolution
  • synonym: resolution - the detail an image holds, measured in DPI or PPI
