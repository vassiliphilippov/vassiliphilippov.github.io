image resolution unit

Pronunciation: / diː piː aɪ / (AmE), / diː piː aɪ / (BrE)

Examples

600 DPI image
The printer produces sharp images at 600 DPI.
For better quality, increase the DPI setting before printing your photos.
Graphic designers often work with images at a high DPI for professional results.
