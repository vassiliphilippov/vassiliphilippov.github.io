Lechuguilla

proper noun
deep cave in New Mexico
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛtʃəˈɡiːjə / (AmE), / ˌlɛtʃəˈɡiːjə / (BrE)
the name of a very deep limestone cave in Carlsbad Caverns National Park, New Mexico, famous for its extensive passages and unique mineral formations
