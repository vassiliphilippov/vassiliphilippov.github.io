ins and outs

noun
detailed aspects or complexities
Pronunciation: / ɪnz ənd aʊts / (AmE), / ɪnz ənd aʊts / (BrE)
all the detailed or complicated aspects of something; the intricacies involved in a situation or subject.

Examples

The ins and outs of work
After working here for years, she knows all the ins and outs of the job.
Before you start investing, you should understand the ins and outs of the stock market.
It takes time to learn the ins and outs of running a successful restaurant.

  • synonym: details - individual elements or small particulars of something
