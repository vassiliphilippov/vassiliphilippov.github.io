ritualist

noun
person expert in rites and ceremonies
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈrɪtʃuəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who is expert in or specializes in rites and ceremonies, especially in religion or anthropology

Examples

Expert ritualist
As a ritualist, she studied the sacred rites of multiple ancient civilizations.
He became a renowned ritualist after years of fieldwork among indigenous tribes.
The museum hired a ritualist to curate the new exhibition on religious ceremonies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ceremonialist - a person who is skilled in or adheres strictly to ceremonies or rites
