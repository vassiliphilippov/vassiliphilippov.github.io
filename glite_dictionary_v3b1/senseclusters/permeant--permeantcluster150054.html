permeant

adjective
spreading throughout; able to penetrate
7.3 7.4
Pronunciation: /ˈpɜːr.mi.ənt/ /; / /ˈpɜːr.mɪ.ənt/ (AmE), /ˈpɜː.mi.ənt/ /; / /ˈpɜː.mɪ.ənt/ (BrE)
spreading or tending to spread throughout every part of a substance or area; able to penetrate or diffuse through.

Examples

Permeant smell

Some chemicals act as permeant agents, diffusing easily through membranes.
