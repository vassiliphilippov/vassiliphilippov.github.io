Carolina jasmine

noun
poisonous vine with yellow flowers
Pronunciation: / kəˈraɪ.lə.nə ˈdʒæz.ə.min / (AmE), / kəˈrɛl.ɪ.nə ˈdʒæz.ɪ.miːn / (BrE)
a poisonous evergreen vine native to the southeastern United States, with fragrant yellow, funnel-shaped flowers
