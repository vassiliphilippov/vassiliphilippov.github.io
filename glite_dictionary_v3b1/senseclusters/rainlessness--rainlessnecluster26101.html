rainlessness

noun
absence of rain; lack of rainfall
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈreɪnləsnəs / (BrE)
The condition of having no rain; the state of being rainless or experiencing a lack of rainfall.

Examples

The rainlessness period
Egypt's rainlessness makes the Nile essential for its agriculture.
Farmers struggle during periods of prolonged rainlessness, as crops wither without irrigation.
The rainlessness of the region has led to water scarcity issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drought - a prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall
