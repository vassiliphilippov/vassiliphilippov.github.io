subsidization

noun
the act of giving a subsidy
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌsʌb.sɪ.dəˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌsʌbsɪdəˈzeɪʃən /; / ˌsʌbsɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsʌb.sɪ.daɪˈzeɪ.ʃən /; / ˌsʌbsɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of providing financial assistance or support, especially by a government, to help an industry, activity, or group
