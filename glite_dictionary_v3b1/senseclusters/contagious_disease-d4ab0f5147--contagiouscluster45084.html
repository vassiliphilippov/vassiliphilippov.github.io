contagious disease

noun
disease easily spread by contact
Pronunciation: / kənˈteɪdʒəs dɪˈziz / (AmE), / kənˈteɪdʒəs dɪˈziːz / (BrE)
A disease that can be easily spread from one person to another by direct or indirect contact.

Examples

A contagious disease
Doctors wore protective clothing to avoid catching the contagious disease from their patients.
Measles is a highly contagious disease that can spread rapidly in unvaccinated populations.
School officials closed the building after several cases of a contagious disease were reported.

Related Terms

  • synonym: infectious disease - disease caused by organisms that can be transmitted
