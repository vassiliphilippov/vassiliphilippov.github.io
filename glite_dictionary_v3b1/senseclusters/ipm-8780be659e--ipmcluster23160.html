IPM

noun
pest control method
Pronunciation: / aɪ piː ɛm / (AmE), / aɪ piː ɛm / (BrE)
a method of controlling pests by combining different techniques to minimize harm to people and the environment

Examples

IPM pest control
Many farmers use IPM to reduce their reliance on chemical pesticides.
The school adopted IPM practices to manage insects without harming students or the environment.
Experts recommend IPM as a sustainable approach to pest control in agriculture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: integrated pest management - a strategy for controlling pests using multiple methods
  • antonym: pesticide - a chemical used to kill pests
