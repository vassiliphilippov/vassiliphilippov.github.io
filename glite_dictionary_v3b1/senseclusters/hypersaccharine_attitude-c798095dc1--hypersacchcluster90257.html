hypersaccharine attitude

noun
excessively sweet demeanor
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪpərˈsækəriːn ˈætɪˌtud / (AmE), / ˌhaɪpəˈsækəriːn ˈætɪtjuːd / (BrE)
An excessively sweet or overly positive manner or demeanor that may seem insincere or cloying.

Examples

A hypersaccharine attitude shown
Despite their hypersaccharine attitude, the apology did not feel genuine.
Her hypersaccharine attitude toward everyone often made her seem insincere.
I find his hypersaccharine attitude a bit much, especially in serious meetings.
