apolitical

adjective
not interested in or involved with politics
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (AmE), / ˌeɪpəˈlɪtɪkəl / (BrE)
Not interested in or connected with politics; having no political affiliations or opinions.

Examples

Apolitical person
Although he was surrounded by activists, Mark remained apolitical and never discussed government matters.
She prefers to keep her music apolitical, focusing instead on personal themes.
The charity is strictly apolitical and does not endorse any candidates or parties.

Related Terms

  • antonym: political - relating to politics or political activity
