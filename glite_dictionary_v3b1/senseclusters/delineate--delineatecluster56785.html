delineate

verb
to trace or outline the shape of
9.9 5.6
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈlɪ.niː.eɪt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈlɪ.niː.eɪt/ (BrE)
to outline, mark, or draw the shape or boundaries of something clearly

Examples

A clear map will delineate the boundaries of the property.
