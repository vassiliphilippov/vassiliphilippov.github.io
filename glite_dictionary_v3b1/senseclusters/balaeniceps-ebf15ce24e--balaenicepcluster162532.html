Balaeniceps

noun
genus of wading birds (shoebills)
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˌliːnəˈsɛps / (AmE), / bəˌliːnɪˈsɛps / (BrE)
a genus of large African wading birds, including the shoebill, and the only genus in the family Balaenicipitidae
