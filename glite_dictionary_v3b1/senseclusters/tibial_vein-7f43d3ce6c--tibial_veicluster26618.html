tibial vein

noun
vein in the lower leg
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪbiəl veɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɪbiəl veɪn / (BrE)
a vein in the lower leg that carries blood from the foot and lower leg to the popliteal vein behind the knee

Examples

Tibial vein in leg
A blockage in the tibial vein can cause swelling and pain in the lower leg.
Blood from the foot eventually drains into the tibial vein before reaching the heart.
The surgeon located the tibial vein during the procedure to ensure proper blood flow from the lower leg.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: popliteal vein - the vein behind the knee receiving blood from the tibial veins
