recurrence

noun
the act of happening again
2.3
Pronunciation: / rɪˈkɝːəns / (AmE), / rɪˈkʌrəns / (BrE)
the act or instance of something happening again, especially at regular intervals

Examples

The recurrence of events
The annual recurrence of the festival brings the whole community together.
The doctor was concerned about a recurrence of the infection after such a short period.
We hope to prevent any further recurrence of similar errors in the future.

Related Terms

  • antonym: interruption - the act of stopping something for a period of time
  • synonym: repetition - the act of doing something again
  • synonym: return - the act of coming or going back
