golden goose

noun
continual source of wealth or profit
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldən ɡus / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldən ɡuːs / (BrE)
a person, thing, or enterprise that continually generates considerable wealth or profit

Examples

A golden goose
Many investors search for a golden goose, but such opportunities are rare.
The new software quickly became the company's golden goose, bringing in millions each year.
When they sold their golden goose, profits began to decline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cash cow - a business or product that generates a steady income
