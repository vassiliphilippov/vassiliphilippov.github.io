distributed

adjective
spread or divided over an area
Pronunciation: / dɪˈstrɪb.ju.tɪd / (AmE), / dɪˈstrɪb.juː.tɪd / (BrE)
spread out, scattered, or divided over an area or among a number of people or things

Examples

Power distributed evenly
Power is distributed throughout the building by a network of cables.
Resources are unevenly distributed across the country, leading to regional differences.
The books were distributed among the students at the ceremony.

Related Terms

  • antonym: concentrated - gathered together or focused in one place
