Synonyms

  • dispersed
    4.2 0.00/m
    dispersed means sent or scattered widely across an area, while distributed often stresses being given out or placed among people or places.
  • scattered
    3.5 0.07/m
    scattered describes things lying apart and irregularly across an area, while distributed can also mean shared or allocated among people.
  • spread
    3.3 0.17/m
    spread is laid out or stretched over a surface or area, while distributed focuses on sharing or giving parts to people.
  • apportioned
    6.5 0.00/m
    apportioned means divided and given in planned shares, while distributed is more general and can mean spread out or handed out.
  • divided
    3.0 0.00/m
    divided describes something split into parts, often equally, while distributed also implies sharing those parts among people or places.
