Iroquois League

proper noun
confederation of Iroquois tribes in North America
8.6
Pronunciation: /ˈɪr.ə.kwɔɪ liːg/ /; / /ˈɪr.ə.kɔɪ liːg/ (AmE), /ˈɪr.ə.kwɔɪ liːg/ /; / /ˈɪr.ə.kɔɪ liːg/ (BrE)
a confederation of Native American tribes originally comprising the Mohawk, Oneida, Onondaga, Cayuga, and Seneca; the Tuscarora joined in 1722, forming the Six Nations
