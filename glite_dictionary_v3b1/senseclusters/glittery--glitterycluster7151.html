Examples

A glittery dress

Synonyms

  • glittering
    3.0 0.19/m
    glittering shines with many bright flashing points, while glittery often describes a surface covered in small visible bits.
  • sparkly
    2.3 1.10/m
    sparkly shines with many small, playful points of light on clothes or things, while glittery means being covered in glitter.
  • sparkling
    1.3 0.35/m
    sparkling gives a bright, lively shine that moves or flashes, while glittery suggests tiny glitter pieces on a surface.
  • glinting
    3.6 0.00/m
    glinting shows brief, sharp flashes when light hits, while glittery means many small glitter flashes across a surface.
  • twinkling
    3.3 0.00/m
    twinkling is small, soft flashes like distant stars, while glittery is a close, textured shine from bits of glitter.
  • scintillating
    8.2 0.05/m
    scintillating sends very bright, rapid flashes and sounds formal, while glittery mainly names a surface covered with glitter.
  • shimmery
    5.0 0.42/m
    shimmery has a soft, wavering glow like silk or water, while glittery has many tiny, sharp sparkles from glitter pieces.
  • gleaming
    3.2 0.06/m
    gleaming is a smooth, even shine from a clean surface, while glittery is rougher and shows many small, bright specks.
