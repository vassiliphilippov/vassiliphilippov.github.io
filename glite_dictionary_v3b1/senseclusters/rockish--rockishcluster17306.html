rockish

adjective
resembling rock
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑkɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈrɒkɪʃ / (BrE)
resembling or having the qualities of rock, especially in appearance or texture

Examples

Rockish surface
The rockish landscape stretched for miles, making it hard for plants to grow.
Her sculpture had a rockish texture that made it look as if it was carved from stone.
We struggled to set up our tent on the rockish ground near the mountain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rocky - full of or covered with rocks
  • antonym: smooth - having an even and regular surface
