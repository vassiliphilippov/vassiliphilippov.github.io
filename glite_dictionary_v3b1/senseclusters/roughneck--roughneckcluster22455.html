roughneck

noun
tough manual worker
6.9
Pronunciation: /ˈrʌf.nek/ (AmE), /ˈrʌf.nek/ (BrE)
a tough or hard-working manual laborer, especially one working on an oil rig or in construction

Examples

Oil roughneck
The roughneck climbed the steel beams with ease despite the strong wind.
Many roughnecks work long shifts on offshore oil platforms.
He started as a roughneck before becoming a supervisor in the construction company.
The roughneck climbed the tall structure to fix a broken pipe on the oil rig.
Many roughnecks work long hours in harsh weather conditions far from home.
After years as a roughneck, he became a supervisor on the drilling team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: driller - a worker who operates drilling equipment, especially on oil rigs
  • antonym: foreman - a person in charge of a group of workers
  • synonym: laborer - a person doing hard physical work
  • synonym: worker - a person who works, especially in manual jobs
