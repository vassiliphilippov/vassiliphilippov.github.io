Adonai

proper noun
Hebrew name for God
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːdəˈnaɪ / (AmE), / ˌadəˈnaɪ / (BrE)
a Hebrew word meaning 'Lord', used as a respectful name for God in Judaism

Examples

Prayers to Adonai
During the service, the rabbi recited prayers that included the name Adonai.
Many Jewish prayers address God as Adonai to show reverence.
The Torah is read aloud, and the word Adonai is spoken in place of the divine name.
