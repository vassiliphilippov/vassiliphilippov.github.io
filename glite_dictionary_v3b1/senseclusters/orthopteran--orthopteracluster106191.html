orthopteran

noun
insect of order Orthoptera (e.g. grasshopper, cricket)
9.9 8.8
Pronunciation: /ɑːr.ˈθɑːp.tər.ən/ /; / /ɔːr.ˈθɑːp.tər.ən/ (AmE), /ɔː.ˈθɒp.tə.rən/ (BrE)
