handout

noun
informational document
Pronunciation: / ˈhænˌdaʊt / (AmE), / ˈhænˌdaʊt / (BrE)
a sheet of paper or document given to people to provide information or aid learning

Examples

Lesson handout
The teacher gave each student a handout summarizing the main points of the lesson.
At the conference, attendees received a handout with the schedule and speaker information.
Please read the handout before our next meeting so you are prepared for the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leaflet - a printed sheet of paper containing information or advertising
  • synonym: pamphlet - a small booklet or brochure containing information
