incher

noun
device for small adjustments
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntʃər / (AmE), / ˈɪntʃə / (BrE)
a mechanical device or tool used to move or adjust a heavy object by very small amounts at a time

Examples

Incher tool
The workers used an incher to carefully move the large machine into position.
An incher allows for precise adjustments when installing heavy industrial equipment.
They rented an incher to shift the safe a few centimeters without damaging the floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jack - a device for lifting or moving heavy objects
  • synonym: winch - a machine for pulling or lifting heavy objects
