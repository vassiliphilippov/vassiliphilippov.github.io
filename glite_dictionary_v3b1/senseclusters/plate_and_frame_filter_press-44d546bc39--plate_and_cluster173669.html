plate and frame filter press

noun
industrial filter press with plates and frames
6.3
Pronunciation: / pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltər prɛs / (AmE), / pleɪt ənd freɪm ˈfɪltə pres / (BrE)
a type of filter press consisting of a series of plates and frames assembled alternately between supports, used for separating solids from liquids in industrial processes
