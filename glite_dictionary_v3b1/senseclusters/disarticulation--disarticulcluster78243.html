disarticulation

noun
separation of parts
8.9
Pronunciation: / ˌdɪsɑːrˌtɪkjəˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌdɪsɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the separation or disconnection of parts that are normally joined, such as in a structure or mechanism

Examples

Bridge disarticulation
The earthquake led to the disarticulation of several sections of the old bridge.
Engineers inspected the machinery for signs of disarticulation after the sudden malfunction.
The sculpture was designed to show the disarticulation of its moving parts.

Related Terms

  • antonym: connection - the act of joining or linking things together
  • synonym: disconnection - the act of separating or detaching
  • synonym: separation - the act of moving things apart
