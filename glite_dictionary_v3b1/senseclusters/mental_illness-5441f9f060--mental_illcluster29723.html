mental illness

noun
disease or disorder of the mind
2.0 2.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmen.təl ˈɪl.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈmen.təl ˈɪl.nəs/ (BrE)
a condition that affects a person's thinking, feeling, behavior, or mood, often seriously enough to require medical or psychological intervention

Examples

Mental illness treatment
Access to proper treatment can help those suffering from mental illness lead fulfilling lives.
Many people still misunderstand what it means to have a mental illness.
She has lived with a mental illness since her early twenties.
