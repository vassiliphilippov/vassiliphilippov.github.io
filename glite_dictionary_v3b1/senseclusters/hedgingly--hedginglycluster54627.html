hedgingly

adverb
uncertainly or evasively
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhedʒ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈhedʒ.əŋ.lɪ/ (AmE), /ˈhedʒ.ɪŋ.lɪ/ /; / /ˈhedʒ.əŋ.lɪ/ (BrE)
in a way that avoids giving a clear or definite answer, often to express uncertainty or caution

Examples

To answer hedgingly
He replied hedgingly when asked about his plans for the future.
The spokesperson answered hedgingly, careful not to make any promises.
She hedgingly suggested that the project might be delayed.
