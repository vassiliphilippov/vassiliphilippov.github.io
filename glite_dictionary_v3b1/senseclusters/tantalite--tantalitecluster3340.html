tantalite

noun
tantalum oxide mineral; tantalum ore
6.0 9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtæn.tə.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈtæn.tə.laɪt/ (BrE)
A dark mineral consisting of a tantalum oxide of iron and manganese, occurring with niobite or in coarse granite; an important ore of tantalum.

Examples

Dark tantalite mineral
Tantalite and columbite are often found together in igneous rocks.
Mining companies value deposits rich in tantalite for the extraction of tantalum.
The geologist identified the black crystals as tantalite in the sample.
