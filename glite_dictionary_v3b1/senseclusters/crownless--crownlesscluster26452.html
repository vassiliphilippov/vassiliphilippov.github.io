crownless

adjective
without a crown
3.0 7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈkraʊn.ləs/ /; / /ˈkraʊn.les/ (AmE), /ˈkraʊn.ləs/ /; / /ˈkraʊn.les/ (BrE)
Lacking a crown; not provided with a crown, especially in reference to a ruler who has not yet been formally crowned.

Examples
