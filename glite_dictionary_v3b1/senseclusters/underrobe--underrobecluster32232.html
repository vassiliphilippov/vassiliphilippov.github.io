underrobe

noun
garment under robe
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndərˌroʊb /; / ˈʌndɚˌroʊb / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌrəʊb / (BrE)
a garment worn underneath a robe or dress, usually as an underlayer in formal or ceremonial clothing
