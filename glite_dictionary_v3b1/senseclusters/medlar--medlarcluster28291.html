medlar

noun
South African fruit with leathery skin
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛd.lɚ / (AmE), / ˈmɛd.lə / (BrE)
a small brownish edible fruit with leathery skin, often used in preserves, from the medlar tree

Examples

The ripe medlar has a unique, tangy flavor ideal for making jellies and preserves.
