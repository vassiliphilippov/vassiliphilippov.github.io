kingside

noun
chessboard's king side
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈkɪŋ.saɪd/ /; / /ˈkɪŋ.zaɪd/ (AmE), /ˈkɪŋ.saɪd/ /; / /ˈkɪŋ.zaɪd/ (BrE)
the side of a chessboard where the king begins the game, typically the right side for each player

Examples

The kingside of the board
The player moved his rook to the kingside to prepare for castling.
She launched an aggressive attack on her opponent's kingside early in the game.
After castling on the kingside, the king was well protected by pawns.
