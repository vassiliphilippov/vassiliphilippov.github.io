household linen

noun
sheets, towels, and similar home cloths
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊshoʊld ˈlɪnən / (AmE), / ˈhaʊshəʊld ˈlɪnɪn / (BrE)
Articles made of cloth, such as sheets, towels, and tablecloths, used in the home.

Examples

Household linen items
Proper care of household linen can help it last for many years.
She put fresh household linen on all the beds before the guests arrived.
The store carries a wide selection of household linen such as towels and tablecloths.
