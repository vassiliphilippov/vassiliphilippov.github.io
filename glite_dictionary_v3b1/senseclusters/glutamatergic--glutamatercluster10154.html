glutamatergic

adjective
using glutamate
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɡluːtəˌmeɪˈtɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡluːtəˌmætˈɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
relating to or involving glutamate as a neurotransmitter, especially in nerve cells

Examples

Glutamatergic pathway
Certain glutamatergic pathways are essential for learning and memory in the brain.
Researchers studied the effects of drugs on glutamatergic transmission in the nervous system.
A glutamatergic neuron releases glutamate to communicate with other nerve cells.

Related Terms

  • antonym: GABAergic - relating to or using GABA as a neurotransmitter
  • synonym: cholinergic - relating to or using acetylcholine as a neurotransmitter
