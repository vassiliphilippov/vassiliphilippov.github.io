cinnamon

noun
tropical tree; source of cinnamon spice
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪnəmən / (AmE), / ˈsɪnəmən / (BrE)
A tropical Asian tree (genus Cinnamomum) with aromatic yellowish-brown bark, which is the source of the spice cinnamon.

Examples

Cinnamon tree bark
Farmers carefully strip the bark from the cinnamon to harvest the spice.
The cinnamon in her backyard blooms with fragrant leaves each spring.
The island is famous for its vast cinnamon plantations.
