Philohela minor

noun
species of North American woodcock
Pronunciation: / faɪloʊˈhiːlə ˈmaɪnər / (AmE), / fɪləʊˈhiːlə ˈmɪnə / (BrE)
A small species of long-billed woodcock, native to North America, prized as a game bird; also known as the American woodcock.
