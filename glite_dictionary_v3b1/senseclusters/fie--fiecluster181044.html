fie

interjection
exclamation of disapproval
9.9
Pronunciation: / faɪ / (AmE), / faɪ / (BrE)
an old-fashioned exclamation used to express disapproval, disgust, or shame

Examples

Fie upon you
Fie upon those who would betray their friends for money.
He cried, 'Fie!' when he saw the mess left in the hall.
Fie on such cowardly actions in the face of danger.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bravo - an exclamation expressing approval or praise
  • synonym: shame - an exclamation expressing shame or reproach
