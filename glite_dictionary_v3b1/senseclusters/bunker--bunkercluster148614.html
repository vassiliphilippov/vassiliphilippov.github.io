bunker

verb
to take shelter
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋkər / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkə / (BrE)
to shelter or protect oneself in a secure place, especially during danger or threat

Examples

To bunker down
During the storm, the family bunkered in their basement until it was safe to come out.
Many residents bunker down when a hurricane warning is issued for their area.
He bunkered himself in his apartment for days during the citywide emergency.

Related Terms

  • antonym: expose - to leave unprotected or without shelter
  • synonym: shelter - to take cover or find protection from danger
