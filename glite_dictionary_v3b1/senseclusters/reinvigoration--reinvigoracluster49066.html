reinvigoration

noun
renewed energy
Pronunciation: / ˌriːɪnˌvɪɡəˈreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːɪnˌvɪɡəˈreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or state of becoming energetic, strong, or active again

Examples

Team reinvigoration
The team's reinvigoration after the break was clear in their improved performance.
A weekend in the countryside brought a sense of reinvigoration to everyone.
The new manager's ideas led to the reinvigoration of the entire department.
The community center underwent a reinvigoration after the renovation project was completed.
A vacation can provide much-needed reinvigoration for people feeling burned out at work.
The team's reinvigoration was evident in their improved performance this season.
