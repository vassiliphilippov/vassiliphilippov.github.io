butterfly valve

noun
rotating disc valve for regulating flow
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərˌflaɪ vælv / (AmE), / ˈbʌtəflaɪ vælv / (BrE)
A valve consisting of a disc that rotates to regulate or stop the flow of fluid, commonly used as a throttle in carburetors and pipelines.

Examples

A butterfly valve disc
A butterfly valve offers fast operation and is commonly used where frequent flow regulation is needed.
The mechanic replaced the damaged butterfly valve in the car's carburetor to restore proper airflow.
Turning the handle of the butterfly valve quickly stops water flow through the pipe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: throttle - a device controlling the flow of fuel or power in an engine
