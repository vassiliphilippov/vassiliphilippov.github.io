Bast

proper noun
Egyptian goddess with a cat or lion head
9.9
Pronunciation: / bæst / (AmE), / bɑːst / (BrE)
An ancient Egyptian goddess depicted with the head of a lioness or cat, associated with protection, fertility, and the life-giving power of the sun.

Examples

Bast goddess
In Egyptian mythology, Bast was often depicted as a lioness or a woman with a cat's head.
Modern art frequently draws inspiration from the imagery of Bast, blending feline features with human forms.
Worshippers built temples to Bast in the ancient city of Bubastis.
