African American

noun
a Black American of African ancestry
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˌæfrɪkən əˈmɛrɪkən / (AmE), / ˌaf.rɪ.kən əˈmɛr.ɪ.kən / (BrE)
A person from the United States who has ancestry from Africa, especially Black Americans whose families have lived in the US for generations.

Examples

African American artists
Many African American artists have had a significant impact on American music.
She is proud to identify as an African American and celebrate her cultural heritage.
The museum features exhibits on the history and contributions of African Americans in the United States.
