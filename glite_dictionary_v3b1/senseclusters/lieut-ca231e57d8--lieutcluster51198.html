Lieut.

noun
military rank abbreviation
Pronunciation: / liˈut. / (AmE), / liˈut. / (BrE)
abbreviation for the military rank 'Lieutenant', used before a person's name

Examples

Lieut. Johnson
Lieut. Johnson received a medal for his bravery during the mission.
The orders were delivered by Lieut. Carter to the command post.
After the ceremony, Lieut. Evans addressed the new recruits.
