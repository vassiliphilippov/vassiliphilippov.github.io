pendulous

adjective
hanging down loosely
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛn.dʒə.ləs / (AmE), / ˈpɛn.djʊ.ləs / (BrE)
(especially of branches or flower heads) hanging down loosely; drooping.

Examples

Pendulous flower stems
Many orchids produce pendulous flower stems that hang gracefully below the leaves.
The pendulous branches of the willow tree touched the surface of the pond.
The old chandelier hung in a pendulous fashion from the cracked ceiling.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drooping - hanging or bending downward
  • antonym: erect - upright or raised position, not hanging downward
