vanishing point

noun
point where parallel lines appear to meet
8.2 5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ pɔɪnt/ /; / /ˈvæn.ə.ʃɪŋ pɔɪnt/ (AmE), /ˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ pɔɪnt/ /; / /ˈvæn.ə.ʃɪŋ pɔɪnt/ (BrE)
the point in a perspective drawing or photograph where parallel lines appear to meet or disappear in the distance
