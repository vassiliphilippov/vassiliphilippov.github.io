huffingly

adverb
with heavy breathing
Pronunciation: / ˈhʌfɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈhʌfɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that shows heavy breathing or panting, often due to effort or excitement

Examples

Arrive huffingly
He arrived huffingly at the finish line, clearly exhausted from the race.
The children ran up the hill and stood huffingly at the top, catching their breath.
After chasing the bus, she entered the classroom huffingly and apologized for being late.

Related Terms

  • synonym: breathlessly - in a way that shows difficulty breathing, often from exertion
  • antonym: calmly - in a relaxed and unexcited manner
