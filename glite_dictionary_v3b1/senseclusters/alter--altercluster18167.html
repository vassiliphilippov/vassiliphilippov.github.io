alter

verb
remove the ovaries of a female animal
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɔltər / (AmE), / ˈɔːltə / (BrE)
to surgically remove the ovaries of (a female animal), typically to render it sterile; to spay

Examples

To alter a dog
If you alter your dog, you can help prevent unwanted litters.
Many animal shelters require that pets be altered prior to leaving the facility.
The veterinarian will alter all rescued female cats before they are adopted.
