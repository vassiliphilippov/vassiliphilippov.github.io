rope burn

noun
skin abrasion from rope friction
7.3
Pronunciation: / roʊp bɝn / (AmE), / rəʊp bɜːn / (BrE)
An abrasion, typically on the skin, caused by friction with a rope.

Examples

A rope burn injury
Always wear gloves to avoid rope burn when rigging sails.
He got a painful rope burn on his hand after climbing the tree.
The rope burn around her wrist was red and sore.
