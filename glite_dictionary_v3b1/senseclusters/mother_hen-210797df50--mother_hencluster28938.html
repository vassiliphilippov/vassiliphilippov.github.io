mother hen

noun
a hen with chicks
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌðɚ hɛn / (AmE), / ˈmʌðə hɛn / (BrE)
A hen that has chicks and looks after them.

Examples

A mother hen with chicks
A mother hen will fiercely protect her young from any sign of danger.
Farmers watched the mother hen lead her brood across the yard every morning.
The mother hen clucked softly as her chicks gathered around her.
