spoon

noun
an amount filling a spoon
Pronunciation: / spun / (AmE), / spuːn / (BrE)
An amount of something that fills a spoon; a spoonful.

Examples

A spoon of sugar
He added two spoons of honey to his tea.
I always take my coffee with a couple of spoons of sugar.
The recipe calls for three spoons of olive oil.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spoonful - the amount a spoon will hold
