cowlick

noun
tuft of hair growing in a different direction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊ.lɪk / (AmE), / ˈkaʊ.lɪk / (BrE)
A small section of hair that grows in a different direction from the rest and resists lying flat.

Examples

A cowlick on head
He tried to comb down the cowlick at the back of his head, but it kept springing up.
Her son has a stubborn cowlick that always sticks straight up in the morning.
No matter how she styled her hair, the cowlick refused to lie flat.

  • synonym: tuft - a small cluster of hair or fibers
