Brassica rapa perviridis

noun
Asian leafy green vegetable
Pronunciation: / brəˌsɪkə ˈræpə ˌpɜr.vɪˈri.dɪs / (AmE), / brəˌsɪkə ˈræpə ˌpɜː.vɪˈrɪ.dɪs / (BrE)
An Asian leafy green plant cultivated for its edible foliage and sometimes its swollen root, used in cooking and salads.
