Salafist

adjective
of Salafism
Pronunciation: / ˈsæləfɪst / (AmE), / ˈsæləfɪst / (BrE)
relating to Salafism or to the beliefs and practices of Salafists, a conservative movement within Sunni Islam.

Examples

Salafist beliefs
The new law was criticized by some Salafist leaders for not aligning with their interpretation of religious principles.
A Salafist group organized the event to promote traditional Islamic values in the community.
The documentary explored the influence of Salafist ideology on youth culture in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Islamist - relating to political movements seeking to implement Islamic principles
  • antonym: secular - not connected with religious or spiritual matters
