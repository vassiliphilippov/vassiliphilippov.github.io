exoticism

noun
quality of being exotic or foreign
8.2 6.1
Pronunciation: /ɪg.ˈzɑː.tɪ.sɪz.əm/ (AmE), /ɪg.ˈzɒ.tɪ.sɪz.əm/ (BrE)
the quality of being unusual, interesting, or strikingly different, especially because it comes from a foreign or distant place or culture
