neuropteron

noun
insect of the Neuroptera order
7.8
Pronunciation: / nuˈrɑːptərən / (AmE), / njʊˈrɒptərən / (BrE)
an insect of the order Neuroptera, typically having biting mouthparts and four large, net-veined membranous wings

Examples

A neuropteron insect
A neuropteron fluttered by the window, its wings shimmering in the sunlight.
Scientists study the wing patterns of the neuropteron to understand insect evolution.
The antlion is a well-known example of a neuropteron found in many sandy habitats.

Related Terms
