hooper

noun
hoop performer
6.7 0.20/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàhuÀêp…ôr / (AmE), / ÀàhuÀêp…ô / (BrE)
a person who performs dances or tricks using one or more hoops, especially hula hoops

Examples

Skilled hooper with hoops
The talented hooper amazed the crowd with her fast and graceful moves.
At the festival, a young hooper spun multiple hoops around her arms and legs.
He became a skilled hooper after practicing with hula hoops every day.

Synonyms

    8.1 0.00/m
