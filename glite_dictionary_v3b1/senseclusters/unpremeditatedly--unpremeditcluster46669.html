unpremeditatedly

adverb
without planning
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpriːˈmɛdɪteɪtɪdli / (AmE), / ˌʌnpriːˈmɛdɪteɪtɪdli / (BrE)
in a way that is not planned or thought out in advance

Examples

Laugh unpremeditatedly
She laughed unpremeditatedly at the joke, surprising everyone with her reaction.
The artist began painting unpremeditatedly, letting inspiration guide each brushstroke.
He spoke unpremeditatedly, without considering the impact of his words.

Related Terms

  • antonym: deliberately - in a way that is planned or intended
  • synonym: spontaneously - in a way that happens naturally, without planning
