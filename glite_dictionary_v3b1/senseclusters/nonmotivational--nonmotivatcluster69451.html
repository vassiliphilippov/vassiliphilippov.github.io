nonmotivational

adjective
not motivating
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌmoʊtəˈveɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌməʊtɪˈveɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not related to or intended to increase motivation

Examples

A nonmotivational speech
The training session was entirely nonmotivational and failed to inspire the team.
Her speech was informative but rather nonmotivational for the audience.
Students found the textbook to be nonmotivational compared to interactive learning tools.

Related Terms

  • antonym: motivational - intended to increase motivation
