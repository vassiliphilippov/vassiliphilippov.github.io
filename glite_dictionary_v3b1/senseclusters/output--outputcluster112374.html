output

verb
(of a computer) produce data or results
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌpʊt / (AmE), / ˈaʊt.pʊt / (BrE)
(of a computer or machine) to produce or deliver data, information, or a result.

Examples

To output data

The system will output a summary report once the data is processed.
When you press that button, the device begins outputting detailed information to the screen.
