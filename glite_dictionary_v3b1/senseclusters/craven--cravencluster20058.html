craven

noun
an abject coward
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪ.vən / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.vən / (BrE)
A person who is extremely cowardly; an abject coward.

Examples

A craven runs away
In the story, the craven begged for mercy rather than stand his ground.
No craven would dare challenge such a powerful foe.
The knight was branded a craven for fleeing the battlefield.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
  • antonym: hero - a person admired for courage or noble qualities
