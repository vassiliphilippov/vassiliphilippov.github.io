carpool

noun
shared car arrangement
2.9 1.22/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ërÀåpuÀêl / (AmE), / Ààk…ëÀêÀåpuÀêl / (BrE)

Examples

By joining a carpool, she met new colleagues who lived nearby.
