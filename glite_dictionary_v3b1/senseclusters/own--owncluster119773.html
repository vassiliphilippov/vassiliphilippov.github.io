own

verb
defeat decisively (slang)
9.7
Pronunciation: / oʊn / (AmE), / əʊn / (BrE)
(slang, informal) to defeat, humiliate, or dominate someone decisively, especially in games, sports, or competitive online interactions.

Examples

To own the competition
Skilled players can own opponents with quick strategy in ranked matches.
She owned the tournament after winning three matches without losing a set.
During the final round, he enjoyed owning rivals with unexpected, clever plays.
