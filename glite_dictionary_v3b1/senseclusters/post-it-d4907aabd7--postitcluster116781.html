Post-It

noun
repositionable adhesive note
2.5
Pronunciation: /ˈpoʊst.ɪt/ (AmE), /ˈpəʊst.ɪt/ (BrE)
A small slip of notepaper with a re-adherable strip of adhesive on the back, used for temporarily attaching notes to documents and surfaces.

Examples

A Post-It note
He handed me a Post-It with the meeting time written on it.
She wrote a reminder on a yellow Post-It and stuck it to her computer monitor.
There are Post-Its all over my desk with important phone numbers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sticky note - a generic term for adhesive notepaper
