crowder

noun
one who crowds
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈkraʊdər / (AmE), / ˈkraʊdə / (BrE)
a person or thing that crowds or fills a space, making it more crowded

Examples

Festival crowder
During the festival, the streets were filled with crowders making it hard to walk.
The new furniture acted as a crowder in the small living room.
Security asked several crowders to step back from the entrance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intruder - a person who enters a place where they are not wanted
  • synonym: occupant - a person who is present in a place
  • antonym: vacator - a person who leaves or makes space
