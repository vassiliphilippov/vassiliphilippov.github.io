osmolarity

noun
solute concentration
8.8
Pronunciation: /ɑːz.moʊ.ˈlær.ə.tiː/ /; / /ɑːs.moʊ.ˈlær.ə.tiː/ (AmE), /ɒz.əʊ.ˈlær.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the concentration of dissolved particles in a solution, measured as osmoles per liter

Examples

The osmolarity of solution
The osmolarity of the intravenous fluid must match that of the patient's blood.
Scientists measured the osmolarity to ensure the solution was safe for use in experiments.
Changes in osmolarity can affect how water moves across cell membranes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: molarity - the concentration of a solution measured in moles per liter
  • synonym: osmolality - the concentration of dissolved particles per kilogram of solvent
