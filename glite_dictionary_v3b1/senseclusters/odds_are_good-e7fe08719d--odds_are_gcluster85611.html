odds are good

x
chances are favorable
Pronunciation: / ɑdz ɑr ɡʊd / (AmE), / ɒdz ɑː ɡʊd / (BrE)
it is likely that a particular event or outcome will happen; the chances are favorable

Examples

The odds are good
If we leave early, the odds are good we'll avoid the traffic.
The doctor said the odds are good that the treatment will be successful.
With her qualifications, the odds are good she'll land the job.

Related Terms
