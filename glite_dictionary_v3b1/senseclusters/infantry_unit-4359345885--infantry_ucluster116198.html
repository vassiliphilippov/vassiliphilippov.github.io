infantry unit

noun
group of foot soldiers in the military
Pronunciation: / ˈɪn.fən.tri ˌjuː.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈɪn.fən.tri ˌjuː.nɪt / (BrE)
a military group consisting of soldiers who fight on foot rather than in vehicles or on horseback

Examples

The infantry unit
After months of training, the infantry unit was finally deployed overseas.
Every infantry unit in the division received new equipment last week.
The infantry unit marched steadily across the open field at dawn.
