stubborn breakout

noun
persistent skin blemish or acne
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌb.ɚn ˈbreɪk.aʊt / (AmE), / ˈstʌb.ən ˈbreɪk.aʊt / (BrE)
a skin blemish or acne that is difficult to treat or does not clear up easily

Examples

A stubborn breakout
Many teenagers struggle with a stubborn breakout despite good skincare habits.
She tried several creams, but her stubborn breakout refused to go away.
Stress and hormones often led to a stubborn breakout on his forehead.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blemish - a small mark or flaw on the skin, especially one that spoils the appearance
