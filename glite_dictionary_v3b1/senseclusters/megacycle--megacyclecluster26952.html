megacycle

noun
unit of frequency; one million cycles per second
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛɡəˌsaɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛɡəˌsaɪkəl / (BrE)
A unit of frequency equal to one million cycles per second, chiefly used in electronics and radio.
