broomstraw

noun
straw broom
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːmˌstrɔ / (AmE), / ˈbruːmˌstrɔː / (BrE)
a simple broom made from straw or dried stalks, used for sweeping floors or outdoor areas

Examples

A broomstraw broom
She swept the kitchen floor with an old broomstraw that still worked well.
The farmer kept a broomstraw by the door to clean the porch every morning.
After the harvest, they made a broomstraw from leftover stalks to tidy the barn.

Related Terms

  • synonym: broom - a cleaning tool with a long handle and bristles for sweeping
  • synonym: brush - a tool with bristles used for cleaning or painting
