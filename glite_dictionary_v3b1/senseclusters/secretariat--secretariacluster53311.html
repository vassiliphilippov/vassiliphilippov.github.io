secretariat

noun
administrative office for managing records; esp. in organizations
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌsɛkrəˈtɛriət / (AmE), / ˌsɛkrəˈteəriət / (BrE)
an administrative office or department responsible for managing records, correspondence, and other secretarial duties, especially in large or international organizations

Examples

The secretariat office
She applied for a job in the administrative secretariat of the organization.
The secretariat is responsible for maintaining accurate records and handling correspondence within the company.
The United Nations secretariat oversees the implementation of decisions made by its member states.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secretary - person employed to handle correspondence and records
