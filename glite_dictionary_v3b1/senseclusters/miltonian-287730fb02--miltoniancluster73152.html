Miltonian

adjective
of John Milton
Pronunciation: / mɪlˈtoʊniən / (AmE), / mɪlˈtəʊniən / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the English poet John Milton or his works

Examples

Miltonian poetry style
The epic poem was written in a distinctly Miltonian style, with elaborate language and classical references.
Her analysis focused on the Miltonian themes of good and evil in the text.
The professor discussed how the Miltonian influence shaped English literature in the seventeenth century.
