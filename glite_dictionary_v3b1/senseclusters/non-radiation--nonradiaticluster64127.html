non-radiation

adjective
not emitting radiation
Pronunciation: / nɑn ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (AmE), / nɒn ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃən / (BrE)
not producing, emitting, or involving radiation, especially electromagnetic or ionizing radiation.

Examples

Non-radiation equipment
The laboratory uses non-radiation equipment to ensure the safety of its workers.
A non-radiation process was chosen to prevent any exposure to harmful rays.
Doctors recommended a non-radiation imaging technique for the patient's diagnosis.

Related Terms

  • synonym: non-radioactive - not emitting harmful radiation
  • antonym: radioactive - emitting harmful radiation
