bell

noun
flared opening of a tube or device
Pronunciation: / bɛl / (AmE), / bɛl / (BrE)
the flared opening at the end of a tubular device, such as a brass musical instrument or a laboratory apparatus

Examples

Bell of a trumpet
He carefully cleaned the bell of the saxophone to ensure clear sound.
The chemical vapor escaped through the bell at the end of the tube.
The trumpet's bell gleamed under the stage lights.
