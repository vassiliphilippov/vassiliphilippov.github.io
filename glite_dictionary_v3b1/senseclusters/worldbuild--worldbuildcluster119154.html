worldbuild

verb
to create imaginary worlds
8.8
Pronunciation: /ˈwɜːrld.bɪld/ (AmE), /ˈwɜːld.bɪld/ (BrE)
to create and develop the details of an imaginary world, especially for stories, games, or role-playing campaigns

Examples

To worldbuild a story
The author spent months worldbuilding before writing the first chapter of her fantasy novel.
Game designers often collaborate when worldbuilding to ensure consistency in their fictional universe.
He enjoys worldbuilding as much as writing the actual story.

  • synonym: invent - to create something new that did not exist before
