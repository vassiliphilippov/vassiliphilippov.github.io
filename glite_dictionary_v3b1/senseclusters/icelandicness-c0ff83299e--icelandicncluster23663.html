Icelandicness

noun
quality of being Icelandic
9.5
Pronunciation: / aɪˈslændɪknəs / (AmE), / aɪˈslændɪknəs / (BrE)
The quality or state of being Icelandic; the characteristics or attributes associated with Iceland or its people.

Examples

The Icelandicness of culture
Her writing captures the Icelandicness of the landscape and the spirit of its people.
The film explores themes of Icelandicness through its music and storytelling.
There is a unique Icelandicness in the way the community celebrates its traditions.
