merging

noun
the process of joining together
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːdʒɪŋ /; / ˈmɜrdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmɜːdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of joining or combining things together so that they become one

Rapid merging of traffic lanes caused a slowdown during rush hour.
