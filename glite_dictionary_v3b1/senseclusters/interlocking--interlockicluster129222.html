interlocking

noun
state of being fitted or meshed together
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈlɑːkɪŋ /; / ˌɪn.tɚˈlɑː.kɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈlɒkɪŋ /; / ˌɪn.təˈlɒ.kɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of connecting or fitting things closely together so they move as one or cannot move independently
