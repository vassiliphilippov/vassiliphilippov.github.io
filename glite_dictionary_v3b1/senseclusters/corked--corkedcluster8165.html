corked

adjective
wine tainted by a faulty cork
9.9 6.1
Pronunciation: /kɑːrkt/ /; / /kɔːrkt/ (AmE), /kɔːkt/ (BrE)
(of wine) tainted in flavor by a bad cork, especially one contaminated with excess tannin or other faults from the cork itself.

Examples

Corked wine taste
A corked bottle can ruin the experience of even the finest vintage.
The sommelier immediately recognized that the wine was corked and offered to replace the bottle.
We couldn't enjoy the dinner because the red wine tasted distinctly corked.

  • synonym: tainted - contaminated or spoiled, especially in flavor or quality
