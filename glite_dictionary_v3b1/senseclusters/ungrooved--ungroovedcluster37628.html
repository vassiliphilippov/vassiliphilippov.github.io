ungrooved

adjective
without grooves
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡruːvd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡruːvd / (BrE)
not having grooves or channels cut into the surface

Examples

Ungrooved surface
The ungrooved tiles are harder to grip when wet.

The ungrooved handle felt slippery compared to the textured one.
