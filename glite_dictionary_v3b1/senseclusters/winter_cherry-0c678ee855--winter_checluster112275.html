winter cherry

noun
ornamental shrub with cherry-like fruit
7.5 6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.tər ˈtʃer.iː/ /; / /ˈwɪn.ər ˈtʃer.iː/ /; / /ˈwɪn.tər ˈtʃer.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.tə ˈtʃer.iː/ /; / /ˈwɪn.tə ˈtʃer.ɪ/ (BrE)
a plant grown for its bright, decorative fruit or papery covering, often used as an ornament; some types are poisonous
