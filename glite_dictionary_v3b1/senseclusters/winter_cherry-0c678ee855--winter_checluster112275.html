winter cherry

noun
ornamental shrub with cherry-like fruit
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌtʃɛr.i /; / ˈwɪntər ˌtʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˌtʃɛr.i /; / ˈwɪntə ˌtʃɛri / (BrE)
a plant grown for its bright, decorative fruit or papery covering, often used as an ornament; some types are poisonous

Examples

Bright winter cherry plant

The winter cherry is often used in floral arrangements for its bright, lantern-like pods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: physalis - any plant of the genus Physalis, such as the ground cherry or cape gooseberry
