horse pill

noun
something hard to accept or endure
Pronunciation: / hɔrs pɪl / (AmE), / hɔːs pɪl / (BrE)
something that is difficult to accept, tolerate, or endure

Examples

A horse pill to swallow
Losing in the finals was a horse pill for the team after months of hard work.
The cancellation of the event was a horse pill for the organizers.
The new regulations are a horse pill for many employees to swallow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitter pill - something unpleasant that must be accepted or endured
