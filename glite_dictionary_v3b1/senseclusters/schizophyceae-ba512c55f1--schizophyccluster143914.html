Schizophyceae

noun
obsolete group: blue-green algae
Pronunciation: / skɪˌzoʊfaɪˈsiː.iː / (AmE), / skɪˌzɪfəˈsiː.iː / (BrE)
a former taxonomic group used for blue-green algae, now usually classified as cyanobacteria

Examples

Schizophyceae blue-green algae
Many older textbooks refer to blue-green algae as Schizophyceae rather than cyanobacteria.
Research into Schizophyceae contributed to our understanding of photosynthetic microorganisms.
The group Schizophyceae is no longer used in modern biological classification.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Cyanophyceae - class of blue-green algae (cyanobacteria)
  • synonym: cyanobacteria - photosynthetic bacteria, also known as blue-green algae
