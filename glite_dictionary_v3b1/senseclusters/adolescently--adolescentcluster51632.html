adolescently

adverb
in a teenage way
Pronunciation: / ˌædəˈlɛsəntli / (AmE), / ˌadəˈlɛsəntli / (BrE)
in a way that is typical of an adolescent or the teenage years

Examples

To speak adolescently
She spoke adolescently about her dreams for the future, full of hope and excitement.
The group acted adolescently, laughing loudly and making jokes in the hallway.
He sometimes responds adolescently to criticism, showing impatience or frustration.
She laughed adolescently at the joke, unable to hide her excitement.
He acted adolescently when he didn't get his way, pouting and complaining.
The group responded adolescently to the new rules, rolling their eyes and whispering.

Related Terms

  • synonym: childishly - in a way that is typical of a child
  • antonym: maturely - in a way that shows maturity or adult qualities
  • synonym: youthfully - in a way that is typical of young people
