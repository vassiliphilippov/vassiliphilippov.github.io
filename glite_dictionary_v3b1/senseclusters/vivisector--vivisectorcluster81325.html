vivisector

noun
animal experimenter
Pronunciation: / ˌvɪvəˈsɛktər / (AmE), / ˌvɪvɪˈsɛktə / (BrE)
a person who performs operations on live animals for scientific or experimental purposes

Examples

Animal vivisector
The vivisector defended his work as necessary for medical progress.
Animal rights groups protested outside the laboratory of the well-known vivisector.
The ethics of being a vivisector are debated in many scientific communities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: experimenter - a person who conducts scientific tests or experiments
  • synonym: researcher - a person who carries out academic or scientific investigation
