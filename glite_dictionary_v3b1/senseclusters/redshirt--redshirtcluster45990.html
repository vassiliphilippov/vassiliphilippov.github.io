redshirt

noun
athlete not competing; expendable character
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌʃɜrt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌʃɜːt / (BrE)
a college athlete who does not compete in official games for a season to extend their period of eligibility, or a minor fictional character likely to die early in a story

Examples

College redshirt athlete
The coach decided to keep the freshman as a redshirt to give him more time to develop.
In many science fiction shows, a redshirt is a character who is likely to be killed off quickly.
After his injury, he became a redshirt and sat out the entire season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: benchwarmer - an athlete who rarely or never plays in games
  • synonym: extra - a minor character in a film or story with little importance
  • antonym: starter - an athlete who regularly plays at the beginning of games
