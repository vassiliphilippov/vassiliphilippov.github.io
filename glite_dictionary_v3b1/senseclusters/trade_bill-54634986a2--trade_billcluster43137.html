trade bill

noun
proposed law on foreign trade
1.5
Pronunciation: / treɪd bɪl / (AmE), / treɪd bɪl / (BrE)
a proposed law designed to regulate foreign trade, especially between countries

Examples

A trade bill proposal
A controversial trade bill was introduced to limit certain imports and protect domestic industries.
Many exporters are monitoring the progress of the new trade bill through Congress.
The Senate debated the trade bill for hours, focusing on potential impacts to local manufacturers.

  • synonym: bill - a draft of a proposed law presented for approval
