colonoscopy

noun
examination of the colon using a scope
6.1 1.64/m
Pronunciation: / Àåko ä.l…ôÀàn…ëÀê.sk…ô.pi / (AmE), / Àåk…íl.…ôÀàn…ís.k…ô.pi / (BrE)
a medical procedure in which a flexible instrument is inserted into the colon to visually examine its interior, usually under sedation

Examples

A colonoscopy procedure
After turning fifty, my doctor recommended that I have a colonoscopy as a routine screening procedure.
She was nervous before her first colonoscopy, but the medical staff explained the process thoroughly.
The colonoscopy revealed several benign polyps, which were removed during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endoscopy - visual examination of the interior of a bodily organ or cavity using an endoscope
  • degree lower: sigmoidoscopy - examination of the sigmoid colon with a scope
