quillon

noun
sword crosspiece
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkwɪlən / (AmE), / ˈkwɪlɒn / (BrE)
a crosspiece on a sword or dagger, forming part of the guard to protect the hand

Examples

The quillon on sword
The ornate quillon on the sword was decorated with silver engravings.
He gripped the hilt just above the quillon to better control his blade.
The quillon prevented his hand from slipping onto the sharp blade during the duel.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guard - the part of a sword that protects the hand
  • synonym: hilt - the handle of a sword, including the guard and pommel
