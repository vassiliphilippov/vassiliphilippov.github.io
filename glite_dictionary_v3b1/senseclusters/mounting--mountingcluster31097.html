mounting

noun
process of rising or increasing
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊn.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈmaʊn.tɪŋ / (BrE)
an event or process in which something rises to a higher level, such as altitude, temperature, or intensity

Examples

Mounting pressure
The mounting of temperature inside the greenhouse worried the farmers.
There was a noticeable mounting of excitement as the concert drew closer.
With the mounting of public pressure, the government agreed to reconsider its decision.

Related Terms

  • antonym: diminishing - becoming less in size or importance
  • synonym: increase - becoming greater in size, amount, or degree
