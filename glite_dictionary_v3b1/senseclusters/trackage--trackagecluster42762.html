trackage

noun
railroad track system
Pronunciation: / ˈtrækɪdʒ / (AmE), / ˈtrækɪdʒ / (BrE)
the system or total amount of railroad tracks in a particular area

Examples

Railroad trackage system
The city expanded its trackage to support more trains.
Old industrial areas often have unused trackage left behind from former rail lines.
The new project will add several miles of trackage to improve freight transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: railway - a system of tracks along which trains run
  • degree lower: track - a single pair of rails for trains
