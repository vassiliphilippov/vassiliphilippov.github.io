pothunter

noun
nonprofessional or unethical archaeologist
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊthʌntər / (AmE), / ˈpɒthʌntə / (BrE)
a person who excavates archaeological sites for personal gain or without professional standards, often disregarding scientific methods

Examples

Illegal pothunter
Authorities warn that a pothunter can destroy valuable historical context while collecting relics.
Museums often refuse to acquire items obtained by a pothunter.
The ancient site was damaged by a pothunter who removed artifacts without permission.
