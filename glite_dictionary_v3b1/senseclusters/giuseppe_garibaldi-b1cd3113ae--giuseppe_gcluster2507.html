Giuseppe Garibaldi

proper noun
Italian patriot and unification leader
6.5
Pronunciation: / dʒuˌzɛpeɪ ˌɡærɪˈbɑldi / (AmE), / dʒuːˌzɛpeɪ ˌɡærɪˈbɑːldi / (BrE)
