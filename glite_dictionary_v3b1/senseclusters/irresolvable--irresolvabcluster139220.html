irresolvable

adjective
unable to distinguish
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈzɑːlvəbəl / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈzɒlvəbəl / (BrE)
not able to be separated, distinguished, or resolved into different parts or elements, especially by observation or instruments

Examples

Irresolvable details
The details of the distant galaxy remained irresolvable even with the most powerful telescope.
In the blurry photograph, the two figures were completely irresolvable from each other.
Some scientific problems are irresolvable with current technology and require new methods to study.

Related Terms

  • synonym: indistinguishable - impossible to tell apart or recognize as different
  • antonym: resolvable - able to be separated or distinguished into parts
