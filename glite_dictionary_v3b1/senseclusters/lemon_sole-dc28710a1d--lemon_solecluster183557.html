lemon sole

noun
edible flatfish of North Atlantic waters
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛm.ən soʊl /; / ˈlɛmən soʊl / (AmE), / ˈlɛm.ən səʊl /; / ˈlɛmən səʊl / (BrE)
a type of flatfish (Microstomus kitt) found in European and North Atlantic waters, valued as food for its delicate flesh
