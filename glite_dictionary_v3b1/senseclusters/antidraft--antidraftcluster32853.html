antidraft

adjective
against conscription or drafts
Pronunciation: / ˈæntiˌdræft / (AmE), / ˈæntiˌdrɑːft / (BrE)
designed to prevent or oppose conscription into the military or to block unwanted air currents

Examples

Antidraft protest
The university hosted an antidraft rally to protest the new military policy.
She installed an antidraft strip on the door to keep the cold air out.
Many young people joined antidraft movements during the conflict.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anticonscription - opposed to compulsory military service
  • antonym: pro-draft - supporting compulsory military service
