GPM

proper noun
satellite precipitation mission
7.3
Pronunciation: / dʒi pi ɛm / (AmE), / dʒiː piː ɛm / (BrE)
an international satellite mission that measures global rainfall and snowfall to support weather forecasting and climate research

Examples

GPM satellite mission
Scientists use data from GPM to study global rainfall patterns and improve climate models.
The GPM mission helps meteorologists predict severe weather events more accurately.
Thanks to GPM, researchers can monitor snowfall in remote regions around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: TRMM - Tropical Rainfall Measuring Mission, a satellite mission for measuring tropical rainfall
  • synonym: weather satellite - a satellite used to monitor weather and climate conditions
