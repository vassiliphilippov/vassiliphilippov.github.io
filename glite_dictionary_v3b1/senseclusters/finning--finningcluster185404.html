finning

noun
removal of fish fins
7.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈfɪn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the practice of removing fins from fish, especially sharks, often for commercial purposes

Examples

Finning sharks
Many countries have banned finning to protect shark populations.
Environmental groups are raising awareness about the dangers of finning in the fishing industry.
Strict regulations now limit finning to help preserve marine ecosystems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: overfishing - catching too many fish so that populations decline
  • synonym: shark finning - the removal of fins from sharks, often resulting in their death
