toll collector

noun
person who collects toll payments
5.2
Pronunciation: / toʊl kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / təʊl kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to collect money from drivers at a toll road, bridge, or similar facility

Examples

A toll collector booth
She worked as a toll collector on the busy highway for more than a decade.
The toll collector handed me a receipt after I paid the fee at the booth.
Years ago, a friendly toll collector greeted everyone passing through the bridge.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticket collector - person who collects tickets, especially on public transport
