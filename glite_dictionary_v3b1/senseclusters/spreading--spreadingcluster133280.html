spreading

noun
becoming distributed or extended widely
2.0
Pronunciation: / ˈsprɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsprɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of distributing, extending, or expanding something over a wider area or among more people

Examples

The spreading of stains
He noticed the gradual spreading of stains on the ceiling after the heavy rain.
Modern technology is responsible for the spreading of information across the globe.
The rapid spreading of wildfires caused extensive damage to the forest.
His influence was notable because of the spreading of his ideas through social media.
The rapid spreading of the disease alarmed the health officials.
There was a slow spreading of the fire across the dry grasslands.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compression - the act of pressing something into a smaller space
  • antonym: concentration - the act of focusing or gathering into one place
  • synonym: dispersal - the process of distributing things or people over a wide area
  • synonym: expansion - the process of becoming larger or more extensive
