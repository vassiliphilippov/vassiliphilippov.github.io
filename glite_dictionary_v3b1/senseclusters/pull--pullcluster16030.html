pull

verb
hit a ball towards the player's facing side
2.3 4.7
Pronunciation: /pʊl/ (AmE), /pʊl/ /; / /pəl/ (BrE)
(in sports such as cricket, baseball, or golf) to hit a ball so that it goes in the direction the player is facing during their swing, often towards the side of their dominant hand.

Examples

To pull the ball
He tried to pull the ball but it went straight to the fielder.
She often pulls the ball when playing short-pitched deliveries.
The batter pulled a fastball deep into right field for a home run.

Related Terms
