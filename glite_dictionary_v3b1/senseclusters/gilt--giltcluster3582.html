gilt

noun
thin gold or gold-like coating
Pronunciation: / ɡɪlt / (AmE), / ɡɪlt / (BrE)
A thin layer of gold or a substance with a similar appearance applied as a coating.

Examples

Gilt on the frame
Centuries of wear had caused the gilt on the church altar to fade.
The antique box featured intricate designs highlighted with bright gilt.
The ornate picture frame was covered in a layer of gilt that gleamed in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gilding - the process or result of applying gold leaf or gold paint
  • synonym: veneer - a thin decorative covering of fine material
