accordion

noun
portable musical instrument with bellows and reeds
3.4
Pronunciation: / əˈkɔr.di.ən / (AmE), / əˈkɔː.di.ən / (BrE)
A portable musical instrument with a keyboard and buttons, played by expanding and contracting bellows to force air through reeds.

Examples

Play the accordion
He played a lively tune on his accordion during the festival.
She learned to play the accordion from her grandfather.
The band added a rich sound to their songs by including the accordion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concertina - A similar free-reed musical instrument, smaller and hexagonal in shape.
