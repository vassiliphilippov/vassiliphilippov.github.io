proposer

noun
person who suggests or proposes something
5.1
Pronunciation: / prəˈpoʊzər / (AmE), / prəˈpəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who puts forward a suggestion, idea, or formal proposal

Examples

Main proposer of a plan
As the proposer of this idea, she answered all the committee’s questions.
Every motion must have a proposer and a seconder before it is discussed.
The proposer outlined his plan at the start of the meeting.
As the proposer of the amendment, she addressed the assembly first.
Each motion required a proposer and a seconder before it could be debated.
The proposer explained the reasons behind the new policy during the conference.

Related Terms

  • antonym: opposer - person who opposes a suggestion or proposal
  • synonym: seconder - a person who formally supports a motion after it has been proposed
  • synonym: suggester - person who suggests something
