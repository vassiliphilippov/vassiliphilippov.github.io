leukodystrophy

noun
rare brain disease
9.9
Pronunciation: /luː.koʊ.ˈdɪs.trə.fiː/ /; / /luː.koʊ.ˈdɪs.trə.fɪ/ (AmE), /luː.kəʊ.ˈdɪs.trə.fiː/ /; / /luː.kəʊ.ˈdɪs.trə.fɪ/ (BrE)
a rare inherited disease that affects the white matter of the brain, leading to progressive loss of neurological function

Examples
