nonrecreational

adjective
not for recreation; work-related
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnrɛkriˈeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
not intended for recreation; involving work, employment, or practical purposes rather than leisure or enjoyment.

Examples

Nonrecreational activities
City parks are open for both recreational and nonrecreational activities.
He switched to a nonrecreational job in the film industry, making documentaries instead of videos for fun.
Most of the nonrecreational fishing in the area is carried out by local professionals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: recreational - relating to or denoting activity done for enjoyment rather than work
