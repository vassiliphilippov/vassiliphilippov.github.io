grammatical constituent

noun
part of a grammatical structure
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈmætɪkəl kənˈstɪtʃuənt / (AmE), / ɡrəˈmætɪkəl kənˈstɪtjʊənt / (BrE)
a word, phrase, or clause that forms a part of a larger grammatical structure within a sentence
