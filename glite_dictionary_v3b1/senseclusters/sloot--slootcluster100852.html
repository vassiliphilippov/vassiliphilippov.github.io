sloot

noun
narrow drainage ditch
Pronunciation: / slut / (AmE), / sluːt / (BrE)
a narrow artificial channel or ditch, especially for draining water, used regionally or in Dutch contexts

Examples

Dutch sloot
Farmers regularly clean the sloot to prevent flooding in the fields.
A narrow sloot separates the two meadows behind the farmhouse.
Children watched ducks swimming in the sloot after the spring rain.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: canal - an artificial waterway for navigation or irrigation
  • synonym: ditch - a long narrow excavation in the ground for drainage
