illiberalism

noun
opposition to liberal values
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˈlɪbərəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ɪˈlɪbərəlɪzəm / (BrE)
a belief system or policy that restricts individual freedoms and opposes open, democratic society

Examples

Political illiberalism
The government's shift toward illiberalism has sparked protests among citizens demanding more rights.
Many scholars debate the causes behind the recent rise of illiberalism in several countries.
Some leaders promote illiberalism by limiting press freedom and restricting public demonstrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: authoritarianism - a system favoring strict obedience to authority at the expense of personal freedom
  • antonym: liberalism - a political philosophy emphasizing individual freedoms and open society
