bond

verb
form a close emotional connection
2.9 4.85/m
Pronunciation: / bÉ‘nd / (AmE), / bÉ’nd / (BrE)
to develop a close relationship or strong emotional connection with someone

Examples

To bond with friends
Difficult times often help people to bond more closely.
She quickly bonded with her new teammates over their shared goals.
Spending a weekend together helped the family bond again after the argument.
It can take time for parents to bond with their newborns.
The two teammates quickly bonded during the training camp.
Volunteering is a great way for people to bond over shared interests.
