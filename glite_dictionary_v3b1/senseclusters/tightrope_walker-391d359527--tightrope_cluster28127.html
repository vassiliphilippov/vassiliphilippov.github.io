tightrope walker

noun
person performing acrobatics on a tightrope
5.5 0.28/m
Pronunciation: / Ààta…™tÀåro äp Ààw…îÀêk…ôr / (AmE), / Ààta…™t.r…ô äp Ààw…îÀê.k…ô / (BrE)
an acrobat who performs feats or routines while balancing on a tightrope or slack rope, usually high above the ground

Examples

A tightrope walker performs
Becoming a skilled tightrope walker takes years of practice and intense concentration.
During the parade, a tightrope walker balanced carefully between two tall buildings.
The tightrope walker captivated the audience with her daring stunts above the circus ring.

Synonyms

  • ropewalker
    8.3 0.00/m
    a ropewalker walks along a high suspended rope and performs tricks, while a tightrope walker is the common everyday name for that performer.
  • funambulist
    9.9 0.00/m
    a funambulist is a formal, bookish word used in writing or by experts for a rope performer, while a tightrope walker is the everyday term.
  • ropedancer
    8.3 0.00/m
    a ropedancer shows graceful, dance-like moves on a stretched rope for entertainment, while a tightrope walker emphasizes balance and height.
