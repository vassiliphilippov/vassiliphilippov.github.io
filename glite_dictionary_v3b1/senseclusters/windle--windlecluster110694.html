windle

noun
thread winding tool
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪndəl / (AmE), / ˈwɪndəl / (BrE)
a simple tool, bobbin, or spindle used for winding thread or yarn

Examples

A wooden windle
The artisan used a windle to neatly organize the spun yarn.
She learned how to wind thread onto a windle in her weaving class.
A wooden windle made the process of preparing yarn much easier.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bobbin - a cylinder or reel for winding thread or yarn
  • synonym: spindle - a rod used for spinning and winding thread or yarn
