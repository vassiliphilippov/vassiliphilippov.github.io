nonsystematically

adverb
without order
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnsɪstəˈmætɪkli / (AmE), / ˌnɒnsɪstəˈmætɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that is not systematic, organized, or planned

Examples

Selected nonsystematically
The researcher selected participants nonsystematically, leading to a less reliable study.
Books were arranged nonsystematically on the shelves, making it hard to find anything.
He approached the problem nonsystematically, trying different solutions without a clear plan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: randomly - in a way that happens without a specific pattern or plan
  • antonym: systematically - in a methodical and organized way
