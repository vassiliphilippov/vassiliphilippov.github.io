loosey

noun
single cigarette
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈluːsi / (AmE), / ˈluːsi / (BrE)
a single cigarette sold individually rather than as part of a pack, especially illegally

Examples

Buy a loosey
He bought a loosey from a vendor when he forgot his pack at home.
Some corner stores illegally sell a loosey to customers who do not want a full pack.
She asked the clerk if she could get just one loosey instead of buying a whole pack.
