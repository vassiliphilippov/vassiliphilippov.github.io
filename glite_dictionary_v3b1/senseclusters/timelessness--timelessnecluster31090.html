timelessness

noun
the quality of being unaffected by time
1.0 5.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtaɪm.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈtaɪm.lɪs.nɪs/ (AmE), /ˈtaɪm.ləs.nəs/ /; / /ˈtaɪm.lɪs.nɪs/ (BrE)
