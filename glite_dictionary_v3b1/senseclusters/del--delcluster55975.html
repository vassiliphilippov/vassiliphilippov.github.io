del

noun
keyboard delete key
6.3
Pronunciation: / dɛl / (AmE), / dɛl / (BrE)
the key on a computer keyboard labeled 'Del' or 'Delete', used to remove characters, files, or other items

Examples

Del key
If you want to erase the selected text, just press the del key.
Accidentally hitting the del key can remove important files from your computer.
The del key is often found near the end of the keyboard's top row.

Related Terms

  • synonym: backspace - a keyboard key that deletes the character to the left of the cursor
  • synonym: delete key - a keyboard key used to remove text or files
