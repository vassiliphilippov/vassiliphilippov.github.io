winter heath

noun
dwarf European shrub with early bell-shaped flowers
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪntər hiːθ / (AmE), / ˈwɪntə ˌhiːθ / (BrE)
a low-growing European shrub that blooms in early spring, bearing bell-shaped red or pink flowers

Examples

The winter heath shrub
She chose to plant winter heath because it blooms earlier than most other shrubs.
The winter heath adds a splash of color to gardens even when snow remains on the ground.
Tiny bees gathered around the winter heath, drawn by its bright flowers in late winter.
