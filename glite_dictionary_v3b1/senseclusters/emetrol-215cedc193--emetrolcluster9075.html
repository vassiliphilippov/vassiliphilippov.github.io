Emetrol

noun
anti-nausea brand-name medicine
Pronunciation: / ˈɛmɪˌtroʊl / (AmE), / ˈɛmɪˌtrɒl / (BrE)
A trademarked over-the-counter medicine used to relieve nausea and vomiting, usually mint-flavored.

Examples

Bottle of Emetrol
After feeling queasy from the car ride, she took some Emetrol to settle her stomach.
He always keeps a bottle of Emetrol in his medicine cabinet for emergencies.
The pharmacist recommended Emetrol for his mild nausea.
