nine

noun
playing card with nine pips
4.4
Pronunciation: / naɪn / (AmE), / naɪn / (BrE)
A playing card with nine pips, being one of four cards in a standard deck numbered nine.

Examples

Nine of clubs
He was dealt the nine of clubs and hoped for a straight.
If you play the nine now, you might have a better chance to win.
There are only four nines in a standard deck of cards.
