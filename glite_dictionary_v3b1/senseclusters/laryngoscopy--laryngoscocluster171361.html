laryngoscopy

noun
examination of larynx
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːskəpi / (AmE), / ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒskəpi / (BrE)
a medical examination of the larynx using a special instrument called a laryngoscope

Examples

A laryngoscopy exam
The doctor recommended a laryngoscopy to investigate the cause of her hoarseness.
During the laryngoscopy, the specialist carefully examined the patient's vocal cords.
A laryngoscopy can help detect tumors or other problems in the throat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endoscopy - a procedure using an instrument to examine the inside of the body
  • synonym: laryngoscope - an instrument used to examine the larynx
