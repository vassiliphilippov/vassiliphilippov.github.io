pass down

verb
send props/state to child components

(in programming, especially UI/component-based frameworks) to transmit or propagate a value, property (prop), or state from a parent component to its child components.

Examples

To pass down props
In React, components pass down props from parents to children during rendering.
To reduce duplication, the lead developer passes down only the necessary state from the top component.
After the refactor, the toolbar passed down its theme settings to every nested child widget.
