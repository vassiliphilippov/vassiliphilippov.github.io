sucrase

noun
enzyme that breaks down sucrose
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːˌkreɪs / (AmE), / ˈsuːˌkreɪz / (BrE)
an enzyme that catalyzes the breakdown of sucrose into glucose and fructose
