compellingly

adverb
in a convincing way
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpɛlɪŋli / (AmE), / kəmˈpɛlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that strongly attracts attention or convinces people

Examples

Writes compellingly
The author writes so compellingly that readers cannot put the book down.
She spoke compellingly about the need for environmental protection at the conference.
The documentary presents its arguments compellingly, making viewers reconsider their opinions.

He argued compellingly for the need to protect the environment.
