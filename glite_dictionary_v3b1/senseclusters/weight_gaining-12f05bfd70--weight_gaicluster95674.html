weight gaining

noun
bodybuilding to increase muscle mass and weight
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈweɪt ˌɡeɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
The process or practice of increasing muscle mass and body weight, especially as part of a bodybuilding regimen.

Examples

Weight gaining routine
His weight gaining routine included high-protein meals and heavy lifting sessions.
Many bodybuilders go through a weight gaining phase before preparing for competition.
Some athletes focus on weight gaining during the off-season to build strength.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bulking - period of eating more to gain muscle mass, often used in bodybuilding
