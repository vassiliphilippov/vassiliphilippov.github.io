vibratory plate compactor

noun
machine for compacting surfaces in construction
Pronunciation: / ˈvaɪbrəˌtɔri pleɪt kəmˈpæktɚ / (AmE), / ˈvɪbrətəri pleɪt kəmˈpæktə / (BrE)
an engine-powered machine with a vibrating flat plate, used for compacting soil, sand, gravel, and similar materials in construction work
