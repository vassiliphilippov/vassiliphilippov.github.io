feed steamer

noun
device for steaming animal feed (obsolete)
Pronunciation: / fid ˈstimər / (AmE), / fiːd ˈstiːmə / (BrE)
a device formerly used to apply steam to animal feed before it was given to farm animals

Examples

The feed steamer device
Farmers once relied on the feed steamer to soften grains for livestock.
The old barn still had a rusty feed steamer in the corner, unused for decades.
With modern equipment, the feed steamer has become a relic of agricultural history.
