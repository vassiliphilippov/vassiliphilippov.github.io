consciousness

noun
awareness of self and surroundings
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑn.ʃəs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈkɒn.ʃəs.nəs / (BrE)
the state of being aware of oneself, one's thoughts, and one's surroundings

Examples

Lose consciousness
After the accident, he lost consciousness for several minutes and could not remember what happened.
She slowly regained consciousness and opened her eyes as the anesthesia wore off.
The patient drifted in and out of consciousness during the night, making communication difficult.

Related Terms

  • synonym: awareness - knowledge or perception of a situation or fact
  • antonym: unconsciousness - the state of not being aware of oneself or one's surroundings
