boneheaded

adjective
very stupid; lacking intelligence
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnˌhɛdəd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnˌhɛdɪd / (BrE)
(informal) very stupid; lacking intelligence or good judgment.

Examples

A boneheaded mistake
Her boneheaded comment caused everyone to roll their eyes in disbelief.
I can't believe he made such a boneheaded decision at the last minute.
That was a truly boneheaded mistake that cost the team the game.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brainless - very stupid; having no intelligence
  • synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
