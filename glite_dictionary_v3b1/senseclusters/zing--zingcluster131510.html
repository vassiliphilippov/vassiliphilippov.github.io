zing

noun
vivacity; energetic quality
Pronunciation: / zɪŋ / (AmE), / zɪŋ / (BrE)
a quality of enthusiasm, energy, or liveliness; brisk vitality, especially in a person's personality or manner

Examples

Add zing to life
He greeted us every morning with his trademark zing and enthusiasm.
Her speech really had some zing and captivated the entire audience.
The new advertising campaign gave the brand a much-needed zing.
