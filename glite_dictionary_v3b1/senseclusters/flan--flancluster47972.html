flan

noun
open pastry with fruit or custard filling
8.6
Pronunciation: / flæn / (AmE), / flæn / (BrE)
an open pastry or tart with a sweet or savory filling, often served as a dessert

Examples

The bakery offers both sweet and savory flans every morning.
He learned to make a traditional Spanish flan as part of his cooking class.
