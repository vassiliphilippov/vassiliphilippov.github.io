unrefrigerated

adjective
not needing refrigeration
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
not kept cold or in a refrigerator

Examples

Unrefrigerated food storage
The medication can be kept unrefrigerated for up to two weeks without losing its effectiveness.
Many canned foods are designed to remain unrefrigerated until they are opened.
He left the juice unrefrigerated on the counter overnight, but it was still safe to drink.
Milk should not be left unrefrigerated for long periods.
The sandwiches were served unrefrigerated at the picnic, so we ate them quickly.
Leaving meat unrefrigerated can cause it to spoil and become unsafe to eat.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chilled - made cold, especially in a refrigerator
  • antonym: perishable - likely to spoil or decay if not refrigerated
  • antonym: refrigerated - kept cold in a refrigerator
  • synonym: shelf-stable - able to be stored safely at room temperature
