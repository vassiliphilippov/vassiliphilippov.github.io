specter of doubt

noun
overwhelming sense of uncertainty
Pronunciation: / ˈspɛktɚ əv daʊt / (AmE), / ˈspɛktə əv daʊt / (BrE)
a strong and persistent sense of uncertainty or distrust that troubles someone

Examples

A specter of doubt
A specter of doubt haunted him throughout the negotiations, making every decision difficult.
Despite her careful preparation, the specter of doubt lingered in her mind before the exam.
The specter of doubt can undermine even the most confident plans if left unaddressed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: confidence - feeling of self-assurance or certainty
  • synonym: feeling of uncertainty - being unsure or not confident about something
