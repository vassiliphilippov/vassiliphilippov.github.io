kishka

noun
Jewish stuffed dish
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪʃkə / (AmE), / ˈkɪʃkə / (BrE)
a traditional Jewish or Eastern European sausage or dish made by stuffing a casing (often intestine) with flour, grain, meat, or vegetables
