xenophile

noun
admirer of foreign cultures
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛnəˌfaɪl / (AmE), / ˈzɛnəˌfaɪl / (BrE)
a person who is attracted to or admires foreign people, cultures, or customs

Examples

A xenophile person
As a xenophile, she loved learning new languages and traveling abroad.
His xenophile nature made him eager to try foods from every country he visited.
The club attracted many xenophiles interested in international art and music.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cosmopolitan - a person who is comfortable and familiar with many different cultures
  • antonym: xenophobe - a person who dislikes or fears foreigners or foreign things
