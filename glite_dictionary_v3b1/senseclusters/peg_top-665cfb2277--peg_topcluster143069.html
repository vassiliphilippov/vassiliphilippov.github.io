peg top

noun
pear-shaped spinning toy
7.1
Pronunciation: / pɛɡ tɑp / (AmE), / pɛɡ tɒp / (BrE)
a spinning toy with a pear-shaped wooden body and a metal pin at the tip, which is spun by winding and releasing a string

Examples

A peg top toy
A brightly painted peg top was his favorite childhood toy.
He wound the string carefully before letting the peg top whirl across the table.
The children took turns spinning the peg top on the old wooden floor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinning top - a toy that can be spun on an axis, balancing on a point
