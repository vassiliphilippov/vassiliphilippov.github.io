ejaculator

noun
person who utters a sudden exclamation
9.1
Pronunciation: / ɪˈd͡ʒækjəˌleɪtər / (AmE), / ɪˈd͡ʒækjʊleɪtə / (BrE)
a person who suddenly utters an exclamation; one who exclaims abruptly

Examples

An excited ejaculator
In the old novel, the character is described as an ejaculator because of his frequent sudden remarks.
She became an unwitting ejaculator when she shouted out during the lecture.
The poet was known as a passionate ejaculator of profound words during his performances.
