off-centered

adjective
not centered; away from the center
Pronunciation: / ˌɔfˈsɛntɚd / (AmE), / ˌɒfˈsɛntəd / (BrE)
Not situated at or aligned with the center; positioned away from the central axis.

Examples

Off-centered necklace
Her necklace sat off-centered and drew attention to the unique design.
The logo on the shirt appeared off-centered after it was printed.
The picture frame was hung a little off-centered on the wall, which bothered him greatly.
