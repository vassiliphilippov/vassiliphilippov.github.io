maternalistic

adjective
showing motherly instincts or qualities
2.1
Pronunciation: / məˌtɝːnəˈlɪstɪk / (AmE), / məˌtɜːnəˈlɪstɪk / (BrE)
Displaying or characterized by maternal instincts or qualities, such as nurturing, caring, or protective behavior.

Examples

Maternalistic care
He appreciated his manager's maternalistic concern about his well-being during his illness.
Her maternalistic approach made everyone on the team feel cared for and supported.
The teacher's maternalistic attitude was evident in the way she nurtured her students.
