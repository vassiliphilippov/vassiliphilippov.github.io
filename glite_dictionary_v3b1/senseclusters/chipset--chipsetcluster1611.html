chipset

noun
computer control components
4.8
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɪp.set/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɪp.set/ (BrE)
a group of electronic components or integrated circuits in a computer or device that control communication and data flow between the processor, memory, and other parts
