buckskinned

adjective
yellowish-brown color
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌkˌskɪnd / (AmE), / ˈbʌkˌskɪnd / (BrE)
having the light yellowish-brown or pale tan color resembling buckskin leather or the coat of a buckskin horse

Examples

Buckskinned color
The artist chose a buckskinned shade for the background of the painting.
Her jacket was made from soft, buckskinned leather that matched her boots.
The dog had a beautiful buckskinned coat that shimmered in the sunlight.
