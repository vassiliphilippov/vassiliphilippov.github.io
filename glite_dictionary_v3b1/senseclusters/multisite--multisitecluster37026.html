multisite

noun
website management system
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmʌl.tɪ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.saɪt/ (AmE), /ˈmʌl.tɪ.saɪt/ /; / /ˈmʌl.tə.saɪt/ (BrE)
a system or platform that allows management of multiple websites from a single installation or dashboard

Examples

Website multisite
The company uses a multisite to control all its regional websites from one place.
Setting up a multisite can save time when managing several related websites.
With a multisite, updates and plugins can be applied across all sites at once.
