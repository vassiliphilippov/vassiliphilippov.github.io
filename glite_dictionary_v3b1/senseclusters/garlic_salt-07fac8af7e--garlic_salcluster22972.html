garlic salt

noun
seasoning of garlic and salt
2.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑrlɪk sɔlt / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːlɪk sɒlt / (BrE)
a seasoning made by mixing ground dried garlic with salt, used to add flavor to food

Examples

Garlic salt seasoning
She sprinkled garlic salt over the popcorn for extra flavor.
The recipe calls for one teaspoon of garlic salt and half a teaspoon of pepper.
You can substitute regular salt with garlic salt to give your dishes a savory boost.
