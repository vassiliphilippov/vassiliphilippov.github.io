parotid vein

noun
vein draining the parotid gland
7.4
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈrɑː.tɪd veɪn/ (AmE), /pə.ˈrɒt.ɪd veɪn/ (BrE)
one of the veins that drain blood from the parotid gland and empty into the retromandibular vein

Examples

The parotid vein
During surgery on the parotid gland, care must be taken to avoid damaging the parotid vein.
Inflammation in the area can sometimes affect the parotid vein and cause swelling.
The parotid vein helps drain blood from the parotid gland into the retromandibular vein.
