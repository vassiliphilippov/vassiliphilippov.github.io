Duke Ellington

proper noun
jazz composer, pianist, and bandleader
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈduːk ˈɛlɪŋtən / (AmE), / ˈdjuːk ˈɛlɪŋtən / (BrE)
(1899–1974) United States jazz composer, pianist, and bandleader, widely regarded as one of the most influential figures in jazz history.
