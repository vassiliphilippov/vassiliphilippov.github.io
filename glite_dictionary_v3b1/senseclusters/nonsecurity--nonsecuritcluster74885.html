nonsecurity

noun
asset not a security
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.sɪˈkjʊr.ə.ti / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.sɪˈkjʊə.rɪ.ti / (BrE)
an asset, instrument, or transaction that is not legally classified as a security in finance

Examples

Nonsecurity asset
The accountant explained that the loan was a nonsecurity and not subject to certain regulations.
Some financial products are classified as nonsecurities and do not require SEC registration.
The company invested in a nonsecurity asset to diversify its portfolio.
