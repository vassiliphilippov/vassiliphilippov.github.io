draw

noun
a tie in a game or contest
Pronunciation: / drɔ / (AmE), / drɔː / (BrE)
a contest or game that finishes with both sides having the same score, so that there is no winner

Examples

A 1-1 draw
Both teams were disappointed with the draw, as they had hoped for a decisive result.
Despite their best efforts, the football game was a draw at full time.
The chess match ended in a draw after both players repeated moves.

Related Terms

  • antonym: loss - being defeated in a contest or game
  • synonym: tie - a game or contest with an equal score
  • antonym: win - victory in a contest or competition
