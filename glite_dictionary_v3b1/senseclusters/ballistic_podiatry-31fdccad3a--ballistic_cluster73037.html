ballistic podiatry

noun
study of foot mechanics in running athletes
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪk poʊˈdaɪətri / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪk pəˈdaɪətri / (BrE)
a specialized branch of podiatry focused on the mechanics of the foot during running, especially for athletes and in treating related injuries

Examples

Ballistic podiatry techniques
Ballistic podiatry techniques can enhance running performance and prevent sports injuries.
Many elite runners seek advice from professionals specializing in ballistic podiatry before major competitions.
The rise of ballistic podiatry has improved the treatment of common foot issues among athletes.
