flummoxed

adjective
confused; puzzled
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌməks̩t / (AmE), / ˈflʌməkst / (BrE)
confused and unable to understand what is happening or what to do

Examples

To be flummoxed completely
She was flummoxed by the sudden change in the schedule and did not know what to do.
The complex math problem left the entire class flummoxed for several minutes.
He looked completely flummoxed when he realized he had lost his keys again.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clear - able to understand easily; not confused
  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • synonym: perplexed - completely baffled or puzzled
