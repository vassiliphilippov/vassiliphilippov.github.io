tangle

noun
confused mass; complicated situation
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡəl / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəl / (BrE)
a confused mass or complicated situation in which things are mixed together and difficult to separate or understand

Examples

A tangle of problems
After the debate, the politicians were left in a tangle of conflicting priorities.
She found herself caught in a tangle of emotions she couldn't easily explain.
The cables behind the desk formed a tangle that took hours to straighten out.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mess - a situation that is dirty or badly organized
  • antonym: order - a state in which everything is arranged methodically
