crude

adjective
offensively indecent; tastelessly vulgar
Pronunciation: / krud / (AmE), / kruːd / (BrE)
offensive or indecent in a way that shows a lack of taste or refinement

Examples

Crude joke
He was fired after making a crude joke during the meeting.
Her crude remarks shocked everyone at the dinner table.
Some comedians rely on crude humor that isn't to everyone's taste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coarse - rough or vulgar in manner or speech
  • antonym: refined - elegant and cultured in appearance, manner, or taste
  • synonym: vulgar - lacking sophistication or good taste; indecent
