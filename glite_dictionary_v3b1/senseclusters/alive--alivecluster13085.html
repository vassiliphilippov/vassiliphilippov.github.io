Synonyms

  • living
    1.0 0.00/m
    living means existing and breathing in everyday situations, while alive is the general state of not being dead.
  • live
    1.0 10/m
    live often appears before nouns to show something has life, while alive usually follows verbs to mean not dead.
  • surviving
    2.7 0.00/m
    surviving emphasizes remaining after others vanish or die, while alive simply means not dead or still living.
  • extant
    8.6 0.5/m
    extant describes things like species or books still existing in records, while alive means living and not dead.
