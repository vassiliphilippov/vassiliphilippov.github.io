Macanese

noun
person from Macau
Pronunciation: / ˌmækəˈniːz / (AmE), / ˌmækəˈniːz / (BrE)
a person from Macau, or someone belonging to the Macanese community of mixed Portuguese and Asian ancestry

Examples

A Macanese person
The Macanese have a unique culture that blends Portuguese and Chinese traditions.
Many Macanese speak both Cantonese and Portuguese fluently.
A Macanese restaurant in the city serves traditional fusion dishes from Macau.
