coho

noun
pinkish flesh of a type of salmon
9.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌhəʊ / (BrE)
The pinkish, fatty flesh of a small salmon found in the Pacific Ocean and Great Lakes, often used as food.
