buncombe

noun
nonsense; absurd or misleading statements
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋkəm / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkəm / (BrE)
nonsense or ludicrously false statements, especially when meant to mislead or deceive.

Examples

Political buncombe
During the debate, she dismissed her opponent’s argument as buncombe.
His claims about the miracle cure were pure buncombe.
We should not be fooled by political buncombe during the election season.

Related Terms

  • synonym: balderdash - senseless talk or writing; nonsense
  • synonym: nonsense - spoken or written words with no meaning or truth
