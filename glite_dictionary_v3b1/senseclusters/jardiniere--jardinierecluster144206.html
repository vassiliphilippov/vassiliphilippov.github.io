jardiniere

noun
decorative plant container
Pronunciation: / ˌʒɑːrdnˈjɛr / (AmE), / ˌʒɑːdɪnˈjɛə / (BrE)
a decorative container or stand, often made of ceramic or metal, used indoors for holding plants or flowers
