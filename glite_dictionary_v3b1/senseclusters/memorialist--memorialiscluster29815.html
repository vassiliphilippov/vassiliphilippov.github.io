memorialist

noun
designer of monuments
7.7
Pronunciation: / məˈmɔːriəlɪst / (AmE), / mɪˈmɔːriəlɪst / (BrE)
a person who designs, creates, or erects physical memorials or monuments to commemorate people or events

Examples

Memorialist designs monuments
The city commissioned a renowned memorialist to create a statue honoring local heroes.
As a skilled memorialist, she has designed several monuments across the country.
The memorialist carefully considered the community's wishes before starting the project.
