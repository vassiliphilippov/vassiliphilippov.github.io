(of a woman or female mammal) to secrete milk and feed it to a young offspring; to give suck to.

Examples

To lactate after birth
After childbirth, she was able to lactate and nurse her newborn daughter.
Certain medications can affect a woman's ability to lactate successfully.
Some mammals continue to lactate for several months after giving birth.

Synonyms

  • give suck
    9.8 0.02/m
    give suck describes a female feeding her baby at the breast, while lactate is the medical term for producing and feeding milk.
  • breastfeed
    2.6 2.14/m
    breastfeed means feeding a baby directly from the mother's breast, while lactate is the broader, scientific term for producing milk.
  • nurse
    2.3 4.59/m

  • suck
    2.5 3.57/m
