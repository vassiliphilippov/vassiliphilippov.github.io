genus Odobenus

noun
genus of walruses (Odobenidae)
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒiːnəs oʊdəˈbiːnəs / (AmE), / ˈdʒiːnəs əʊdəˈbiːnəs / (BrE)
The type genus of the family Odobenidae, consisting of walruses.

Examples

Genus Odobenus walruses
Paleontologists have studied fossils belonging to the genus Odobenus to understand the evolution of walruses.
The genus Odobenus is represented today by a single living species, the walrus.
Unlike some other marine mammals, the genus Odobenus has prominent tusks used for both defense and locomotion.
