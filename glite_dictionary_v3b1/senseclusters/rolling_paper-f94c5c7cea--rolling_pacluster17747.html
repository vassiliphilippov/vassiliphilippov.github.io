rolling paper

noun
paper for rolling cigarettes
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋ ˌpeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ ˌpeɪpə / (BrE)
a thin, strong paper used for rolling cigarettes, designed to burn evenly and control the burning of tobacco

Examples

A pack of rolling paper
