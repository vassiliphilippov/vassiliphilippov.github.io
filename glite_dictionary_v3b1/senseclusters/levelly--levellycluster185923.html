levelly

adverb
calm and steady manner
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛvəli / (AmE), / ˈlɛvəli / (BrE)
in a calm, steady, or even manner, without showing strong emotion or variation

Examples

Speak levelly
She answered the difficult question levelly, without letting her nerves show.
The judge spoke levelly to maintain order in the courtroom.
Despite the chaos, he gave instructions levelly so everyone knew what to do.

Related Terms

  • synonym: calmly - in a peaceful and relaxed way
  • antonym: excitedly - in a way that shows excitement or strong emotion
  • synonym: steadily - in a regular, even, and continuous way
