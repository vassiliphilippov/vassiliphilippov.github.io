vegetarian

noun
person who does not eat meat or fish
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌvɛdʒəˈtɛriən / (AmE), / ˌvɛdʒɪˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
a person who does not eat meat or fish, and sometimes avoids all animal products, usually eating mainly plants, grains, nuts, and fruits

Examples

A vegetarian person
At the dinner party, they prepared a special dish for their vegetarian guest.
Many restaurants now offer a variety of vegetarian options on their menus.
My sister has been a vegetarian since she was twelve years old.

Related Terms

  • antonym: omnivore - person who eats both plant and animal foods
  • degree higher: vegan - person who abstains from all animal products
