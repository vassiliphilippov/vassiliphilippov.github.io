shininess

noun
quality of being shiny
Pronunciation: / ˈʃaɪni.nəs / (AmE), / ˈʃaɪni.nɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being shiny or having a smooth, reflective surface that shines with light

Examples

The shininess of metal
Artists often try to capture the shininess of metal objects in their paintings.
She admired the shininess of her new shoes under the light.
The shininess of the freshly polished floor made the entire room look brighter.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dullness - the lack of brightness, shininess, or gloss
  • synonym: lustre - a gentle sheen or soft glow, especially that of a partly reflective surface
