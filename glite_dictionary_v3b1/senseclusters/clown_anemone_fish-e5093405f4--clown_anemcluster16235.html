clown anemone fish

noun
a brightly colored marine fish (Amphiprion) living among anemones
9.1 6.3
Pronunciation: /klaʊn ə.ˈnem.ə.niː fɪʃ/ /; / /klaʊn ˈæn.ɪ.moʊn fɪʃ/ (AmE), /klaʊn ə.ˈnem.ə.nɪ fɪʃ/ /; / /klaʊn ˈæn.ə.məʊn fɪʃ/ (BrE)
a small, brightly colored marine fish of the genus Amphiprion, noted for its symbiotic relationship with sea anemones and its orange-and-white banded body
