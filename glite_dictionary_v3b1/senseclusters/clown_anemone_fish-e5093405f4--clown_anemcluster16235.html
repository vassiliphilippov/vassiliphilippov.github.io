clown anemone fish

noun
a brightly colored marine fish (Amphiprion) living among anemones
9.1
Pronunciation: / klaʊn əˈnɛmoʊ fɪʃ / (AmE), / klaʊn əˈniːməni fɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, brightly colored marine fish of the genus Amphiprion, noted for its symbiotic relationship with sea anemones and its orange-and-white banded body

Examples

The clown anemone fish swims
A clown anemone fish will often live among the tentacles of sea anemones for protection.
Many aquariums feature clown anemone fish in displays of coral reef habitats.
The clown anemone fish is famous for its vibrant orange color with white stripes.

Related Terms
