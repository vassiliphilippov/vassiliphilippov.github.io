lycee

noun
French secondary school for ages 15–18
Pronunciation: / liˈseɪ / (AmE), / liˈseɪ / (BrE)
A secondary school in France that prepares students for the baccalauréat, typically attended between ages 15 and 18.

Examples

A French lycee
After graduating from the lycee, she enrolled at a university in Paris.
He spent three years at the lycee studying for his final exams.
The French lycee system is known for its rigorous academic standards.
