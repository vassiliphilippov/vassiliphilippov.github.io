sandbar shark

noun
a species of shallow-water shark
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsændˌbɑr ʃɑrk / (AmE), / ˈsændˌbɑː ʃɑːk /; / ˈsandbɑː ʃɑːk / (BrE)
