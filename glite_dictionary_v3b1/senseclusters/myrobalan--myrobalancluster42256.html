myrobalan

noun
small Asian tree with edible fruit
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪrəˈbælən / (AmE), / ˌmɪərəˈbælən / (BrE)
A small Asian tree that bears edible red or yellow fruit, often used in traditional medicine and cooking.

Examples

Myrobalan tree fruit
Gardeners in southern Asia sometimes plant myrobalan trees for their colorful harvest.
Some jams are made using the tart fruit of the myrobalan.
The dried fruit of the myrobalan is often used in Ayurvedic medicine.
