fiberoptics

noun
light transmission through glass or plastic fibers
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪbərˈɑptɪks / (AmE), / ˌfaɪbərˈɒptɪks /; / ˌfaɪbəˈɒptɪks / (BrE)
The transmission of light signals through thin glass or plastic fibers, mainly used for high-speed data communication and imaging.
