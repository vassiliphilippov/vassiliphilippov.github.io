tap

verb
pay by contactless touch
Pronunciation: / tæp / (AmE), / tæp / (BrE)
to make a contactless payment by touching a contactless card or electronic device to a card reader.

Examples

To tap to pay
Many shoppers now tap to pay with contactless cards at checkout.
She tapped her phone on the terminal and the payment processed instantly.
During rush hour commuters tap their cards at the gate to enter the station.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contactless payment - a method of payment in which a card or device transmits payment information by proximity or touch
  • synonym: pay - to give money in exchange for goods or services
