geobiology

noun
study of life and Earth
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒiːoʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌdʒiːəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the scientific study of how living organisms interact with the Earth's geology and environment

Examples

Geobiology study
She decided to major in geobiology because she loves both biology and geology.
The field of geobiology explores how microbes influence the formation of rocks and minerals.
Recent advances in geobiology have revealed new insights into the origins of life on Earth.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biogeochemistry - the study of chemical processes involving living organisms and the Earth
  • synonym: biology - the science of living organisms
  • synonym: geology - the science of the Earth's physical structure and substances
