geobiology

noun
study of life and Earth
7.2
Pronunciation: /dʒiː.oʊ.baɪ.ˈɑː.lə.dʒi/ (AmE), /dʒiː.əʊ.baɪ.ˈɒ.lə.dʒi/ (BrE)
the scientific study of how living organisms interact with the Earth's geology and environment

Examples

Geobiology study

Recent advances in geobiology have revealed new insights into the origins of life on Earth.
