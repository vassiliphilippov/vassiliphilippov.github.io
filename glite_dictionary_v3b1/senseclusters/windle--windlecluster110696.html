windle

verb
to twist or coil
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈwɪn.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈwɪn.dəl/ (BrE)
to twist, wind, or coil something, especially in a spiral or circular manner

Examples

To windle thread
She windled the thread carefully onto the wooden spool.
The artisan windled the wire into a tight spiral for the jewelry piece.
He windled the rope around the post to secure the tent.

Related Terms
