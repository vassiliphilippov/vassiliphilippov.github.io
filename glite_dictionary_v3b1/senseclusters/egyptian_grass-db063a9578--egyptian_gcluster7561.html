Egyptian grass

noun
a creeping grass with finger-like spikes
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ɡræs / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒɪpʃən ɡrɑːs / (BrE)
a creeping grass with finger-like spikes, often found in tropical and subtropical regions

Examples

Egyptian grass plant
Farmers in arid regions often cultivate Egyptian grass for its drought resistance.
