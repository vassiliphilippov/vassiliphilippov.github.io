mower

noun
machine or tool for cutting grass
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊər / (AmE), / ˈməʊə / (BrE)
A machine or tool used for cutting grass, especially on lawns or fields.

Examples

A mower machine
He pushed the mower across the lawn on Saturday morning.
Our old mower broke down just before summer started.
The gardener sharpened the blades of the mower before using it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lawnmower - a machine with one or more revolving blades for cutting grass on a lawn
