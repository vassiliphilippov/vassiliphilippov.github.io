gamble

noun
money risked in hopes of gain
1.2 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈgæm.bəl/ (AmE), /ˈgæm.bəl/ (BrE)
an act of risking money on a game or event for the chance of monetary gain

Examples

A gamble on horses
He placed a gamble on the horse in the third race.
She lost her entire week's wages on a single gamble.
The lottery ticket was just a small gamble for him.
