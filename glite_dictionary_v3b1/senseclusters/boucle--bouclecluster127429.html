boucle

noun
fabric with a looped, knobby surface
Pronunciation: / buˈkleɪ / (AmE), / buːˈkleɪ / (BrE)
a fabric with a looped or knotted surface, often made from uneven yarn giving it a knobby texture

Examples

A boucle fabric
She chose a pale blue boucle for the upholstery to add texture to the room.
The designer's new coat is made from a soft boucle that feels warm and luxurious.
This sweater features a distinctive boucle that makes it stand out from ordinary knits.
