moth-resistant

adjective
not easily damaged by moths
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔθ rɪˌzɪstənt / (AmE), / ˈmɒθ rɪˌzɪstənt / (BrE)
(of material or fabric) resistant to damage caused by moths.

Examples

Moth-resistant fabric
She always stores her winter clothes in moth-resistant bags.
The carpet was treated with a moth-resistant solution.
The sweater is made from moth-resistant wool to prevent insect damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mothproof - treated so as to be resistant to moths
