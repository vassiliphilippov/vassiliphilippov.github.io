antivivisectionist

adjective
opposed to animal experiments
9.9
Pronunciation: /æn.tɪ.vɪ.vɪ.ˈsek.ʃən.ɪst/ (AmE), /æn.tɪ.vɪ.vɪ.ˈsek.ʃən.ɪst/ (BrE)
opposed to performing operations or experiments on live animals, especially for scientific research
