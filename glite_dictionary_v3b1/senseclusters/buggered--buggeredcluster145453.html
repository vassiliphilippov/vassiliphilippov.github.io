buggered

verb
to injure (informal)
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌɡəd / (BrE)
to injure, damage, or strain a part of the body, often through overuse or accident (informal, British English).

Examples

Buggered his ankle
He buggered his knee playing football last weekend and now he can barely walk.
I think I've buggered my back after lifting all those boxes by myself.
She realized she had buggered her wrist after hours of typing without a break.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injure - to cause harm or damage to a part of the body
  • synonym: strain - to injure by overexertion or excessive use
