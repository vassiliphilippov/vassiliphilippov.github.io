cornice

verb
to provide with a molding (cornice)
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrnɪs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːnɪs / (BrE)
to furnish, supply, or form with a cornice (a decorative molding at the top of a wall, window, or building)
