hatchway

noun
an opening with a hatch, esp. on a ship
Pronunciation: / ˈhætʃˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈhatʃ.weɪ / (BrE)
an opening in the floor, roof, or a deck, often fitted with a cover or hatch, especially one providing access between the decks of a ship

Examples

Ship hatchway cover
He disappeared through the hatchway at the back of the warehouse.
Make sure the hatchway is secure before the storm arrives.
The crew climbed down the hatchway to reach the lower deck.
