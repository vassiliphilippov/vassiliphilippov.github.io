jati

noun
Hindu social group/caste
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɑːti / (AmE), / ˈdʒɑːti / (BrE)
(in Hinduism) any of the thousands of traditional social groups in India, usually determined by occupation and hereditary status.

Examples

Traditional jati groups
Anthropologists have studied how jatis shape social interactions and identity.
Each jati traditionally had its own specific occupation and community rules.
The jati system influences many aspects of daily life in rural India.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caste - a system of social stratification in India; a social class
