Regulus

noun
genus of kinglets (birds)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛɡjələs / (AmE), / ˈrɛɡjʊləs / (BrE)
a genus of small passerine birds known as kinglets, found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere

Examples

Among European birds, members of the Regulus genus are some of the smallest.

The Regulus genus includes charming birds like the goldcrest and firecrest.
