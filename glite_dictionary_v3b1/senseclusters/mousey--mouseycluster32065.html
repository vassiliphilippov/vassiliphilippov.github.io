mousey

adjective
dull pale brown or grey color
8.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmaʊsi / (AmE), / ˈmaʊsi / (BrE)
(of hair or color) having a dull, pale brown or greyish shade, like that of a mouse.

Examples

Mousey hair color
He wore a mousey brown sweater that blended into the background.
She always felt her mousey hair made her look dull among her more glamorous friends.
The paint in the hallway had faded to a mousey shade after years without sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brownish - somewhat brown in color
  • synonym: drab - lacking brightness or interest; dull (especially color)
  • antonym: vivid - producing powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind or having intense color
