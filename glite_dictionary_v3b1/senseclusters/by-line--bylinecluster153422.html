by-line

noun
author's name line in articles
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈbaɪˌlaɪn / (BrE)
a line in a newspaper or magazine naming the author of an article or story

Examples

A by-line in a newspaper
Editors often decide who receives the by-line for complicated stories.
Her name in the by-line brought her recognition in the field.
The article's by-line listed two journalists as co-authors.

Related Terms

  • synonym: credit line - a line in a publication giving credit to the creator
