thirstily

adverb
in a thirsty manner
6.7 1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈθɝː.stə.li / (AmE), / ˈθɜː.stɪ.li / (BrE)
In a way that shows a strong desire for drink or as if very thirsty.

Examples

Drink thirstily
After hiking for hours, he drank thirstily from the cold stream.
She eyed the water bottle thirstily, barely able to wait for her turn.
The children gulped their lemonade thirstily after playing soccer in the sun.
