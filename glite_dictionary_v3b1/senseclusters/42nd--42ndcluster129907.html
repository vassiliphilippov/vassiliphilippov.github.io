42nd

adjective
ordinal number for forty-two
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrti ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈfɔːti ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
being the ordinal number corresponding to forty-two in counting or order; coming after the forty-first and before the forty-third.

Examples

42nd place
He finished the race in 42nd place out of hundreds of runners.
My office is on the 42nd floor of the skyscraper downtown.
The company is celebrating its 42nd anniversary this year.
