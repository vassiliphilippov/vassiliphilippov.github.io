trona

noun
sodium mineral
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊnə / (AmE), / ˈtrəʊnə / (BrE)
a naturally occurring mineral used as a source of sodium compounds, especially in industrial processes

Examples

Trona mineral deposits

Large reserves of trona are found in the western United States.
After processing trona, the factory produces chemicals used in water treatment.
