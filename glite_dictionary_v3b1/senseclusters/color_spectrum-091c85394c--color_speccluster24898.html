color spectrum

noun
range of colors dispersed by light
2.0 3.3
Pronunciation: /ˈkʌl.ər ˈspek.trəm/ /; / /ˈkɔːl.ər ˈspek.trəm/ (AmE), /ˈkʌl.ə ˈspek.trəm/ (BrE)
the range of colors that appears when visible light is dispersed, especially by a prism, showing the progression from red to violet

Examples

The color spectrum range
Artists often study the color spectrum to understand how different pigments interact with light.
The scientist demonstrated the color spectrum by shining a flashlight through a piece of glass.
When sunlight passes through a prism, it splits into a color spectrum ranging from red to violet.
