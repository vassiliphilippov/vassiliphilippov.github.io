tow car

noun
vehicle for towing other vehicles
3.3 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / to ä k…ër / (AmE), / t…ô ä k…ëÀê / (BrE)
a vehicle equipped with special apparatus for towing or hauling away other vehicles, especially ones that are disabled or illegally parked

Examples

A tow car arrives
After the accident, a tow car quickly arrived to pull the damaged sedan away.
The city sent a tow car to remove the illegally parked vehicle from the street.
We had to call a tow car when our engine broke down on the highway.

Synonyms

  • tow truck
    2.7 1.29/m
    tow truck is a truck with lifting gear for damaged or illegally parked vehicles, while tow car is any vehicle used to tow.
  • wrecker
    5.9 0.21/m
    wrecker is a truck with a crane or hoist for heavy towing, while tow car is a general term for a vehicle that tows others.
