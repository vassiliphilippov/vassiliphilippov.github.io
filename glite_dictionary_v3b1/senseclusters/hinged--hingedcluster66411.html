hinged

adjective
with hinges
2.9
Pronunciation: / hɪndʒd / (AmE), / hɪndʒd / (BrE)
having one or more hinges that allow movement, especially for opening and closing

Examples

Hinged door
The cabinet has a hinged door that swings open easily.
She opened the hinged lid of the storage box to look inside.
Many windows are hinged so they can be opened for fresh air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to be moved or opened
  • synonym: movable - able to be moved or opened
