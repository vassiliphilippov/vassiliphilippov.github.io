candleholder

noun
object for candles
1.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkændlˌhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈkændlˌhəʊldə / (BrE)
an object used to hold a candle upright, often also catching any dripping wax

Examples

A candleholder on table
She placed a beautiful candleholder in the center of the dining table for decoration.
The antique candleholder was made of brass and had intricate designs.
He bought a glass candleholder to keep the candle steady during dinner.
She placed a lit candle in the antique candleholder on the table.
The elegant candleholder added a warm glow to the dining room.
He bought a modern glass candleholder as a centerpiece for the living room.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chandelier - a decorative hanging light with branches for candles or bulbs
