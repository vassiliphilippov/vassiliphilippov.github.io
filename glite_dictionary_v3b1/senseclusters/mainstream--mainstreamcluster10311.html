mainstream

verb
to integrate into norm
4.6
Pronunciation: /ˈmeɪn.striːm/ (AmE), /ˈmeɪn.striːm/ (BrE)
to integrate or introduce an issue, policy, or practice into the standard operations of institutions or society

Examples

To mainstream new ideas
Many schools are mainstreaming students with special needs into regular classrooms.
The government is mainstreaming environmental policies across all departments.
Nonprofit organizations are mainstreaming mental health awareness in their community programs.

Related Terms
