Aptenodytes

noun
genus of large penguins
Pronunciation: / æpˌtɛnoʊˈdaɪtiːz /; / æpˌtɛnəˈdaɪtiːz / (AmE), / æpˌtɛnəʊˈdaɪtiːz /; / æpˌtɛnəˈdaɪtiːz / (BrE)
the scientific genus of large penguins that includes the emperor penguin and the king penguin
