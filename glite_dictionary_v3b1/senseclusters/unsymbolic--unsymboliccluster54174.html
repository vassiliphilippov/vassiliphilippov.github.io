unsymbolic

adjective
not symbolic; lacking symbolism
6.7
Pronunciation: / ʌn.sɪmˈbɑ.lɪk / (AmE), / ʌn.sɪmˈbɒ.lɪk / (BrE)
Not representing, signifying, or standing for something else; lacking symbolic meaning.

Examples

An unsymbolic meaning
The artist preferred an unsymbolic approach, focusing on realism rather than hidden meanings.
This monument is intended to be purely unsymbolic, offering no deeper interpretation.
Unlike much modern poetry, her writing remained refreshingly unsymbolic.
