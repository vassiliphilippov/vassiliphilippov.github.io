Zephyrus

proper noun
Greek god of west wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈzɛfɪrəs / (AmE), / ˈzɛfɪrəs / (BrE)
the Greek god of the west wind, often associated with gentle spring breezes in classical mythology

Examples

Zephyrus the wind god
In Greek mythology, Zephyrus was known for bringing gentle spring breezes.
Artists often depicted Zephyrus as a winged youth blowing the west wind.
The poet described the arrival of Zephyrus as the start of a new season.
