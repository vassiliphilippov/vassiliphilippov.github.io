start fresh

verb
begin again with a clean slate
Pronunciation: / stɑrt frɛʃ / (AmE), / stɑːt frɛʃ / (BrE)
to begin again without carrying over previous problems or burdens; to make a new start

Examples

To start fresh again
After the move to a new city, they decided to start fresh and reinvent themselves.
She erased the whiteboard so the team could start fresh with new ideas.
Sometimes it's best to put the past behind you and start fresh.

Related Terms

  • synonym: make a fresh start - to begin again with renewed effort or a new approach
  • synonym: start over - to begin again from the beginning
