rewa-rewa

noun
New Zealand tree with red flowers and valuable timber
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌreɪwəˈreɪwə / (AmE), / ˌreɪwəˈreɪwə / (BrE)
a slender, elegant tree of New Zealand, known for its racemes of red flowers and valuable mottled red timber; also called New Zealand honeysuckle

Examples
