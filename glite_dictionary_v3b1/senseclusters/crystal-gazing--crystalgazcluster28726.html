crystal-gazing

noun
fortune telling with a crystal ball; idle speculation
9.9 8.1
Pronunciation: /ˈkrɪs.təl.ɡeɪ.zɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkrɪs.təl ˈɡeɪ.zɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈkrɪs.təl.ɡeɪ.zɪŋ/ /; / /ˈkrɪs.təl ˈɡeɪ.zɪŋ/ (BrE)
