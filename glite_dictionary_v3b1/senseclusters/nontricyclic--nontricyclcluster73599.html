nontricyclic

noun
antidepressant drug not categorized as tricyclic or MAOI
Pronunciation: / nɑnˌtraɪˈsɪklɪk / (AmE), / nɒnˌtraɪˈsɪklɪk / (BrE)
a type of antidepressant drug that is not a tricyclic and does not act by inhibiting monoamine oxidase (MAO)

Examples

A nontricyclic antidepressant
Many patients respond better to nontricyclic antidepressants due to fewer side effects.
The new nontricyclic medications have become increasingly popular in treating depression.
Your doctor may prescribe a nontricyclic if tricyclic drugs are ineffective for you.

Related Terms

  • antonym: tricyclic - antidepressant drug with a three-ring chemical structure
