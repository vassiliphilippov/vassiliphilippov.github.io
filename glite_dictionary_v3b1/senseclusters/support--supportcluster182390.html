support

verb
be a regular customer or client of
2.6 1.51/m
Pronunciation: / s…ôÀàp…îrt / (AmE), / s…ôÀàp…îÀêt / (BrE)
to be a regular customer or client of (a business or organization) by choosing to buy from or use their services repeatedly

Examples

To support a business
Many art lovers support the gallery by attending every exhibition.
She has supported the same hair salon for over ten years.
We always support local businesses when shopping for groceries.

Synonyms
