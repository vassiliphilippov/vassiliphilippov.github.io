transport

noun
extreme emotional ecstasy
Pronunciation: / ˈtrænspɔrt / (AmE), / ˈtrænspɔːt / (BrE)
a state of being carried away by overwhelming emotion, especially joy or ecstasy

Examples

Transport of joy
Poets often write about being in a transport of inspiration when their creativity flows.
She listened to the symphony in a transport of delight, her eyes closed and a smile on her lips.
The news of his victory sent him into a transport of joy he had never known before.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: delight - great pleasure or satisfaction
  • synonym: ecstasy - an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement
  • synonym: rapture - a feeling of intense pleasure or joy
