rendering

noun
giving something as duty or acknowledgment
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛn.dɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɛn.dər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of giving or presenting something, such as thanks or payment, especially as a duty or in acknowledgment of obligation

Examples

Rendering of thanks
She considered the rendering of service to the community a moral obligation.
The rendering of thanks is an important part of the ceremony.
The judge commended the defendant for his rendering of restitution to the victims.

Related Terms

  • synonym: offering - something given as a gift or contribution
  • antonym: withholding - the act of holding back or refusing to give
