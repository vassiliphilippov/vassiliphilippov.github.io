lap

verb
move gently (water) against something
7.9 2.5
Pronunciation: / læp / (AmE), / læp / (BrE)
(of water) to wash or move gently against something, usually with a soft, repeated sound.

Examples

Waves lap the shore
As the tide rose, the sea lapped over our bare feet.
The small waves lapped the sides of the wooden boat as it floated gently.
Water laps quietly against the dock on summer evenings.

Related Terms

  • synonym: wash - to flow over or against something, especially repeatedly
