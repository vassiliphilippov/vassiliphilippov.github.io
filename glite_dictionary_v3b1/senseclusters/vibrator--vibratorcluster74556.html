vibrator

noun
device producing vibrations, often for massage
1.7
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈbreɪtər / (AmE), / vaɪˈbreɪtə / (BrE)
a mechanical device that produces vibrations, used for massage, stimulation, or as part of a machine

Examples

A vibrator device
Many physical therapists recommend a vibrator for at-home muscle relaxation.
She used a vibrator to relieve tension in her shoulders after a long day.
The therapist applied a handheld vibrator to the sore muscle.
Before use, make sure the vibrator is securely attached to the machine.
In some musical instruments, a vibrator is essential for sound production.
The vibrator in the alarm caused the surface to shake gently.
