deer in the headlights

noun
person frozen in fear or surprise
6.7 5.9
Pronunciation: /dɪr ɪn ðə ˈhed.laɪts/ /; / /diːr ɪn ðə ˈhed.laɪts/ (AmE), /dɪə ɪn ðə ˈhed.laɪts/ /; / /dɪə ɪn ðiː ˈhed.laɪts/ (BrE)
A person who is so startled, surprised, or confused that they are unable to move or respond.

Examples

A deer in the headlights
Caught off guard by the sudden question, he was a deer in the headlights.
She stood on stage looking like a deer in the headlights when the spotlight hit her.
When the teacher called on her unexpectedly, Emma froze like a deer in the headlights.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frozen - unable to move due to fear or surprise
  • synonym: paralyzed - unable to move or react; immobilized by fear or shock
