Synonyms

  • all right

  • acceptable
    2.5 40/m
    acceptable describes something that meets a set standard or rule, while OK is less formal and fits everyday speech.
  • good enough
    1.9 20/m
    good enough means just enough to meet a need or task, whereas OK is a more general, neutral approval for many situations.
  • fine
    1.0 136/m
    fine feels like a mild positive judgment or quality, while OK is more neutral and often signals simple acceptability.
  • adequate
    2.7 28/m
    adequate means barely enough to meet a need or requirement, whereas OK is more casual and often less precise.
  • decent
    2.3 22/m
    decent suggests a fairly good standard or respectability, while OK is a lower, more casual sign of simple acceptability.
