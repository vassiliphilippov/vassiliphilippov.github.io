Cheiranthus asperus

noun
prairie rocket plant with orange-yellow flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / kaɪˈræŋθəs ˈæspərəs / (AmE), / kaɪˈrænθəs ˈæspərəs / (BrE)
A biennial or short-lived perennial plant of the prairie rocket group, with orange-yellow flowers, native to western North America and found as far east as Minnesota and Kansas; sometimes classified in the genus Cheiranthus.
