stolon

noun
horizontal plant stem forming new plants
7.7 7.3
Pronunciation: /ˈstoʊ.lən/ (AmE), /ˈstəʊ.lɒn/ (BrE)
a horizontal stem or runner that grows from the base of a plant and produces new plants from buds at its tips

Examples

Underground stolons
Many grasses spread quickly thanks to their underground stolons.
The strawberry plant reproduces by sending out stolons that develop new roots and shoots.
You can propagate spider plants by cutting off their stolons and planting the small plantlets.

Related Terms

  • synonym: runner - a slender stem that grows horizontally along or under the ground and puts forth roots and shoots at intervals
