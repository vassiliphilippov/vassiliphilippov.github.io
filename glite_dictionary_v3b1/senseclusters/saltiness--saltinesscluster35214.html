saltiness

noun
the quality of being salty
4.3 1.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsɑːl.tɪ.nəs/ /; / /ˈsɔːl.tɪ.nəs/ (AmE), /ˈsɒl.tɪ.nəs/ (BrE)
the quality or degree of tasting salty or containing salt; the presence of a salty flavor

Examples

Saltiness of the soup
She complained about the saltiness of the potato chips.

The saltiness of the chips made me quite thirsty.
The saltiness of the soup was overpowering, so I had to add more water.
