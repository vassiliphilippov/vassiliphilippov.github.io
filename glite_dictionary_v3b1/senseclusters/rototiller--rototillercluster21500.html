rototiller

noun
garden soil machine
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊtoʊˌtɪlər / (AmE), / ˈrəʊtəʊˌtɪlə / (BrE)
a machine with rotating blades used to break up and loosen soil in gardens or fields

Examples

A rototiller machine
He rented a rototiller to prepare the backyard for a vegetable garden.
The rototiller made it much easier to break up the hard soil in spring.
After using the rototiller, the ground was ready for planting flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cultivator - a machine or tool used to prepare soil for planting
  • synonym: plow - a farm tool used to turn over soil
