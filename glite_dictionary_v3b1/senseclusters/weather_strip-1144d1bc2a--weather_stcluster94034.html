weather strip

noun
material strip sealing doors/windows from cold
9.9 4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛðɚ ˌstrɪp / (AmE), / ˈwɛðə ˌstrɪp / (BrE)
A narrow strip of material attached to the edges of doors or windows to seal joints and prevent cold air, wind, or water from entering.

Examples

A weather strip on door
A good weather strip helps reduce heating bills in cold climates.
The window's weather strip was old and needed replacement before winter.
We installed a weather strip on the front door to keep out the draft.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weatherstripping - material or process used to seal doors and windows against weather
