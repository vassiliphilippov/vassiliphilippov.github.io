water elm

noun
ornamental tree native to eastern North America
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtər ɛlm / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtə ɛlm / (BrE)
a type of tree with spreading branches that grows in wet or moist places, especially in North America (Planera aquatica) or Eurasia
