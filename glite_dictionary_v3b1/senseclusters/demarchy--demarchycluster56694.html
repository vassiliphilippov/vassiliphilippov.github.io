demarchy

noun
local Greek administration
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˈmɑːrki / (AmE), / dɪˈmɑːki / (BrE)
the administration, office, or jurisdiction of a demarch, especially in ancient Greek city-states

Examples

Ancient Greek demarchy
The demarchy of the ancient Greek deme was responsible for organizing local festivals.
Historians study the demarchy to understand how local government functioned in Athens.
Each demarchy managed its own finances and civic duties within the city-state.

Related Terms

  • synonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population
  • synonym: municipality - a city or town with its own local government
