mycelial

adjective
of fungal filaments
Pronunciation: / maɪˈsiːliəl / (AmE), / maɪˈsiːliəl / (BrE)
relating to or resembling mycelium, the mass of threadlike filaments in fungi

Examples

Mycelial growth patterns
Scientists studied the mycelial growth patterns to understand how the fungus spread.
The mycelial network beneath the soil connects different plants and trees.
A white, mycelial mat was visible on the decaying log after the rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fungal - relating to or caused by fungi
