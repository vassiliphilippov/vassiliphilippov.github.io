victimless crime

noun
crime with no direct victim
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪktɪmlɪs kraɪm / (AmE), / ˈvɪktɪmləs kraɪm / (BrE)
a crime that does not seem to have any direct victims, such as those involving consenting adults

Examples

A victimless crime
Debate continues over whether drug use should be classified as a victimless crime.
Prostitution is often cited as an example of a victimless crime in legal discussions.
Some people believe that gambling is a victimless crime, but others disagree.
