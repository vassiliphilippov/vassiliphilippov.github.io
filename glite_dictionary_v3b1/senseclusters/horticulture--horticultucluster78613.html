horticulture

noun
cultivation of plants and gardens
Pronunciation: / ˈhɔrtɪˌkʌltʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɔːtɪˌkʌltʃə / (BrE)
the art or science of cultivating gardens, fruits, vegetables, or ornamental plants

Examples

Horticulture science
A successful career in horticulture requires both scientific knowledge and practical experience with plants.
Advances in horticulture have led to more beautiful and productive gardens around the world.
She pursued a degree in horticulture to better understand plant cultivation and landscape design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agriculture - the science or practice of farming, including cultivation of the soil and rearing of animals
  • synonym: botany - the scientific study of plants
