dismember

verb
to divide into parts, cut up
7.1
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈmɛmbər / (AmE), / dɪsˈmɛmbə / (BrE)
to divide or cut up something, especially a body or organization, into parts; to separate into pieces, often violently or forcibly

Examples

To dismember a company
After the company was acquired, its divisions were dismembered and sold off individually.
The ancient kingdom was gradually dismembered by invading forces.
The rebels threatened to dismember the statue as a symbol of resistance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dismantle - to take apart a structure or machine
  • synonym: fragment - to break into smaller parts
