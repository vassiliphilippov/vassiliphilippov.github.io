lat

noun
broad muscle of the upper back
Pronunciation: / læt / (AmE), / læt / (BrE)
an informal term for the latissimus dorsi, a broad, flat muscle on either side of the upper back

Examples

Lat workout
He worked out his lats every day to build a strong upper body.
The pull-up is a great exercise for developing your lat muscles.
You can really feel the burn in your lats after those rows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: latissimus dorsi - the large, broad muscle on each side of the upper back
