moonwalker

noun
person who walked on Moon
Pronunciation: / ˈmuːnˌwɔkər / (AmE), / ˈmuːnˌwɔːkə / (BrE)
a person who has walked on the surface of the Moon

Examples

Apollo moonwalker
Neil Armstrong became the first moonwalker in 1969 during the Apollo 11 mission.
Only a small number of people have ever been a moonwalker in human history.
The documentary featured interviews with every living moonwalker about their experiences on the lunar surface.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astronaut - a person trained to travel and work in space
