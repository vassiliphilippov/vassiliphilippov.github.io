clique

verb
to form exclusive group
Pronunciation: / kliːk / (AmE), / kliːk / (BrE)
to form or join a small, exclusive group of people

Examples

To clique together
The new students quickly cliqued with others who shared their interests.
After a few weeks, the interns cliqued and started spending lunch breaks together.
It can be hard to make friends if people have already cliqued in the class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exclude - to leave someone out of a group or activity
  • synonym: group - to come together as a collection of people
