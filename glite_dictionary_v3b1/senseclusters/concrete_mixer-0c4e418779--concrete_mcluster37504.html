concrete mixer

noun
machine for mixing concrete
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑŋ.krit ˈmɪk.sɚ / (AmE), / ˈkɒŋ.kriːt ˈmɪk.sə / (BrE)
A machine with a large revolving drum in which cement, sand, gravel, and water are mixed to produce concrete.

Examples

A concrete mixer machine
A portable concrete mixer is ideal for small building projects at home.
The construction crew loaded sand and gravel into the concrete mixer before adding water and cement.
