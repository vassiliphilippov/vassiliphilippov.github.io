concrete mixer

noun
machine for mixing concrete
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːn.kriːt ˈmɪk.sər/ /; / /ˈkɑːŋ.kriːt ˈmɪk.sər/ (AmE), /ˈkɒn.kriːt ˈmɪk.sə/ /; / /ˈkɒŋ.kriːt ˈmɪk.sə/ (BrE)
