collaborative

noun
joint project
Pronunciation: / kəˈlæbərətɪv / (AmE), / kəˈlæbərətɪv / (BrE)
a project or activity in which people work together to achieve a common goal

Examples

A collaborative between teams
The science museum launched a collaborative with several universities to develop new exhibits.
This community garden is a collaborative that brings neighbors together to grow fresh food.
The documentary was a collaborative between filmmakers from different countries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: competition - the act of trying to win or be more successful than others
  • synonym: cooperation - the process of working together for a common purpose
  • synonym: partnership - a relationship where people or groups work together
