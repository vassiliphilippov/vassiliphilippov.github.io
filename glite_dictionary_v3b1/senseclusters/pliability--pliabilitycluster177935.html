pliability

noun
ability to bend easily

Pronunciation: /plaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /pliː.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (AmE), /plaɪ.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ /; / /pliː.ə.ˈbɪl.ɪ.tɪ/ (BrE)
