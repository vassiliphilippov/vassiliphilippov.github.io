pliability

noun
ability to bend easily
Pronunciation: / ˌplaɪəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌplaɪəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
The quality of being easily bent or shaped without breaking.

Examples

Muscle pliability
Her yoga routine improved the pliability of her muscles over time.
The pliability of clay allows artists to mold it into various shapes.
The pliability of the material made it ideal for creating intricate designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flexibility - the ability to bend easily without breaking
  • antonym: rigidity - the quality of being stiff and not easily bent
