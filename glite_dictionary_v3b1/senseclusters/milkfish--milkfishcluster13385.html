milkfish

noun
edible fish flesh
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlkˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈmɪlkˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
the flesh of the milkfish, used as food, especially in Southeast Asian cuisine

Examples

Grilled milkfish dish
Grilled milkfish is a traditional dish in many Filipino households.
The restaurant serves milkfish with a tangy sauce and steamed rice.
She bought fresh milkfish from the market to prepare for dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: salmon - the flesh of a popular edible fish, often pink in color
  • synonym: tilapia - the flesh of a freshwater fish commonly eaten as food
