asparagus bean

noun
a bean with exceptionally long, edible pods
Pronunciation: / əˈspær.ə.ɡəs ˌbin / (AmE), / əˈspær.ə.ɡəs ˌbiːn / (BrE)
A cultivated variety of bean with very long, slender, edible pods, often used in Asian cuisine; also known as yardlong bean or snake bean.

Examples

Fresh asparagus beans
The market sells bundles of asparagus beans that are nearly a meter long.
Unlike regular green beans, asparagus bean retains a tender texture even after cooking.
We stir-fried some fresh asparagus bean with garlic for dinner last night.
