molding

noun
preliminary sculpture model
5.2 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊldɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈməʊldɪŋ / (BrE)
an object or sculpture made by shaping material in a mold, including preliminary models or finished pieces

Examples

A molding sculpture
Each molding serves as a prototype for the finished sculpture.
The artist created a molding in clay before casting the final statue in bronze.
The delicate details were preserved in the molding, ensuring the final piece was accurate.
Artists often create a molding to reproduce their designs multiple times.
The intricate molding above the fireplace was crafted in the 18th century.
The museum displayed an ancient molding made of bronze.
She specializes in the moulding of figures using traditional techniques.
The artist displayed a beautiful moulding created from clay in the exhibition.
This antique moulding depicts a scene from Greek mythology.
