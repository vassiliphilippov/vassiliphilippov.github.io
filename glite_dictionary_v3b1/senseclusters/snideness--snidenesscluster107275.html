snideness

noun
mocking attitude
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈsnaɪdnəs / (AmE), / ˈsnaɪdnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being mocking, sarcastic, or showing contempt in speech or behavior

Examples

Mocking snideness
His snideness during the meeting offended several of his colleagues.
People often avoid her because of her constant snideness in conversations.
The review was full of snideness rather than constructive criticism.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mockery - behavior or speech that makes fun of someone or something
  • synonym: sarcasm - the use of irony to mock or convey contempt
  • antonym: sincerity - the quality of being honest and genuine
