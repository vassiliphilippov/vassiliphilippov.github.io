negligibly

adverb
insignificantly
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbli / (AmE), / ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is so small or unimportant as to be not worth considering

Examples

Increase negligibly
The temperature increased negligibly overnight, so it still felt cold in the morning.
Her mistake affected the results negligibly, so no changes were needed.
The new policy will negligibly impact most employees' daily routines.
