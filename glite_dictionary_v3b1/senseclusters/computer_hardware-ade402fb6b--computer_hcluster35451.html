computer hardware

noun
physical components of a computer system
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpjuːtər ˈhɑrˌdwɛr / (AmE), / kəmˈpjuːtə ˈhɑːdweə / (BrE)
The mechanical, electronic, magnetic, and electrical components that make up a computer system, such as the central processing unit, memory, and drives.
