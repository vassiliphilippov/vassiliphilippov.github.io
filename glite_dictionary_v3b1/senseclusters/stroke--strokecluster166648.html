stroke

noun
the leading rower in a crew boat
7.4
Pronunciation: / stroʊk / (AmE), / strəʊk / (BrE)
the oarsman in a rowing boat who sits nearest the stern and sets the pace for the rest of the crew

Examples

Stroke sets the pace
As stroke, she communicated with the coxswain to coordinate the team's effort.
He was selected as the stroke for the university rowing team.
The stroke sets the rhythm that the rest of the crew must follow during the race.
