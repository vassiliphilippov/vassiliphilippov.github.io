ballistic identification

noun
forensic method of matching bullets to guns
2.2
Pronunciation: / bəˈlɪstɪk aɪˌdɛntəfəˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / bəˈlɪstɪk aɪˌdɛntɪfɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of determining which gun fired a bullet by analyzing the unique marks left on the bullet and cartridge case
