knaidlach

noun
matzo meal dumplings
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkneɪdlɑx / (AmE), / ˈkneɪdlɑx / (BrE)
small dumplings made from matzo meal, traditionally served in soup during Jewish holidays such as Passover

Examples

Many families have their own special recipe for knaidlach passed down through generations.
During Passover, it is traditional to serve knaidlach in a clear broth.
