level

noun
tool showing if a surface is horizontal
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈle.vəl/ (AmE), /ˈle.vəl/ (BrE)
a tool consisting of a tube of liquid with an air bubble that indicates whether a surface is horizontal when the bubble is centered

Examples

A spirit level
Every carpenter carries a trusty level for accurate measurements.
She checked the shelf with a level to make sure it was perfectly horizontal.
The picture hung crookedly until I used a level to fix it.
