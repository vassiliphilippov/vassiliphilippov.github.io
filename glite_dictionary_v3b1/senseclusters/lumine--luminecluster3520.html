lumine

verb
to light up
9.9 0.01/m
to light up or illuminate, especially in a poetic or old-fashioned way.

Examples

To lumine a room
The stained glass was lumined by the morning sun, casting colors across the floor.
Her face was lumined with joy as she entered the bright room.
In the poem, the night sky is lumined by countless stars.
