Panax

noun
genus of plants including ginseng
Pronunciation: / ˈpæn.æks / (AmE), / ˈpæn.æks / (BrE)
A genus of perennial herbs native to eastern North America and Asia, including the ginseng plants, known for their aromatic tuberous roots.
