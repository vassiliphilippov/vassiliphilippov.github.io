Hieracium aurantiacum

noun
a European orange-flowered hawkweed weed
9.9
Pronunciation: / haɪˌræsiəm ɔːˌrænˈtaɪəkəm / (AmE), / hɪəˌreɪsiəm ɔːˌranˈtɪəkəm / (BrE)
A species of European hawkweed with bright orange-red flower heads, often considered a troublesome weed, especially in northeastern North America.

Examples

Hieracium aurantiacum orange flowers
Hieracium aurantiacum spreads quickly and can outcompete native wildflowers in meadows.
Gardeners are advised to remove Hieracium aurantiacum to prevent it from becoming invasive.
The bright color of Hieracium aurantiacum makes it easy to spot among other weeds.
