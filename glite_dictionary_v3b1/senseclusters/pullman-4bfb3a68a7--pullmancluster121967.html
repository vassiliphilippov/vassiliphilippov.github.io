Pullman

noun
luxurious railway passenger car
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊlmən / (AmE), / ˈpʊlmən / (BrE)
a luxurious railway passenger car, especially one designed for both day and night travel, often equipped with sleeping accommodations and other comforts

Examples

A Pullman car
In the early twentieth century, riding in a Pullman was considered a symbol of luxury and status.
She reserved a berth in a Pullman for the overnight train to Chicago.
The travelers enjoyed a comfortable journey in the Pullman as it crossed the countryside.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: coach - a standard railway passenger car, usually less luxurious
  • synonym: sleeper - a railway car equipped with beds or berths for sleeping
