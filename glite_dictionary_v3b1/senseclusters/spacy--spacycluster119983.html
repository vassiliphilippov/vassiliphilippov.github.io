spacy

adjective
dazed or stupefied, as if drugged
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈspeɪsi / (AmE), / ˈspeɪsi / (BrE)
feeling or appearing dazed, stupefied, or inattentive, as if under the influence of drugs or completely absorbed in one's own thoughts

Examples

He looked spacy all morning, barely responding to anything we said to him.
Some students appeared a bit spacy during the early Monday lecture.
