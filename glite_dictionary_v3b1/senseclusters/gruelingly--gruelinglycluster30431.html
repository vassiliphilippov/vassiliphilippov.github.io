gruelingly

adverb
extremely demanding
Pronunciation: / ˈɡruːəlɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈɡruːəlɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that is extremely tiring, demanding, or exhausting

Examples

To climb gruelingly
The hikers climbed the mountain gruelingly for hours before reaching the summit.
She trained gruelingly every day to prepare for the marathon.
The exam period was gruelingly intense for all the students.

Related Terms

  • antonym: easily - in a way that is not difficult or demanding
  • synonym: exhaustingly - in a way that causes great tiredness
