oral communication

noun
spoken communication
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrəl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈɔːrəl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
the sharing of information or ideas by speaking rather than writing

Examples

Oral communication skills
Children develop oral communication abilities before they learn to write.
Effective oral communication skills are essential in many professions.
The interview focused on the candidate's ability in oral communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: verbal communication - communication using words, especially spoken
  • antonym: written communication - communication using written words
