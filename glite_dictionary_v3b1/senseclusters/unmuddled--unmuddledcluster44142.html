unmuddled

adjective
clear, not confused
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmʌdəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈmʌdəld / (BrE)
clear and not confused; made free from disorder or muddle

Examples

An unmuddled mind
His thoughts became unmuddled after a good night's sleep.
The instructions were unmuddled and easy for everyone to follow.
She gave an unmuddled summary of the complex topic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clear - easy to understand; not confused
  • antonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
