ordinal

adjective
relating to a taxonomic order (biology)
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːrdnəl / (AmE), / ˈɔːdɪnəl / (BrE)
relating to or denoting a taxonomic order in the classification of organisms.

Examples

Ordinal classification
Recent studies have led to changes in the ordinal names of several plant groups.
Some ordinal distinctions can only be made using genetic analysis in modern taxonomy.
The ordinal classification of these species places them in the Carnivora order.
