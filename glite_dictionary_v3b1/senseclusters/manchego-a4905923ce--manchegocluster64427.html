Manchego

noun
Spanish sheep's cheese
Pronunciation: / mænˈtʃeɪɡoʊ / (AmE), / manˈtʃeɪɡəʊ / (BrE)
a firm, flavorful cheese made from sheep's milk, originating from the La Mancha region of Spain

Examples

Manchego cheese
She added slices of Manchego to the cheese platter for a Spanish touch.
The rich flavor of Manchego pairs well with olives and cured meats.
At the market, he bought a wedge of aged Manchego for the weekend picnic.
