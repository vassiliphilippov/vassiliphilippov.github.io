hoof pick

noun
tool for cleaning a horse's hooves
7.5
Pronunciation: / hʊf pɪk / (AmE), / hʊf pɪk / (BrE)
A hooked tool used for cleaning mud, dirt, and debris from a horse's hooves, often with a stiff brush attached.

Examples

A hoof pick tool
After the trail ride, he carefully used the hoof pick to remove stones from the horse's feet.
Every rider should carry a hoof pick in their grooming kit.
She cleaned the mud from the horse's hoof using a hoof pick.
