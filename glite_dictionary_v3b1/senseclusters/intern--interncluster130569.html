intern

noun
medical graduate in practical training
4.5 2.96/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…™n.t…ùn / (AmE), / Àà…™n.t…úÀên / (BrE)
a graduate or advanced student in medicine who is gaining supervised practical experience in a hospital or clinic before obtaining full qualification

Examples

A medical intern
As an intern, she was responsible for monitoring patients during her hospital rotations.
Every intern must work overnight shifts as part of their training.
He completed his year as an intern before starting his residency.

Synonyms

  • medical intern
    3.4 0.02/m
    medical intern is the exact hospital term for a trainee doctor who cares for patients under supervision, while intern is broader.
  • interne
    8.8 0.00/m
    interne is used in some hospitals or older texts and sounds formal or French-influenced, while intern is the common modern term.
  • houseman
    8.1 0.16/m
    houseman is a British junior doctor training on wards during early postgraduate years, while intern is the general trainee title in hospitals.
  • house physician
    6.1 0.02/m
    house physician lives in the hospital and cares for patients as a resident doctor, while intern is any trainee gaining supervised experience.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: resident - a doctor receiving advanced training in a hospital after completing an internship
