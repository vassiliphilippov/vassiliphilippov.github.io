batwing

adjective
shaped like a bat's wing; wide, tapering sleeve style
Pronunciation: / ˈbætˌwɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbatˌwɪŋ / (BrE)
(of clothing) having wide sleeves that are attached with deep armholes and taper towards the wrist, resembling the wing of a bat.

Examples

Batwing sleeves
She wore a stunning evening dress with batwing sleeves.
The designer's new collection features several batwing tops in bold colors.
Vintage fashion often includes sweaters with exaggerated batwing arms.
