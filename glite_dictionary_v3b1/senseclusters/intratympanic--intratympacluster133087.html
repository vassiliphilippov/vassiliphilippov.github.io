intratympanic

adjective
within the middle ear
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrətaɪmˈpænɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrətɪmˈpænɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Intratympanic injection

Doctors may use intratympanic steroids to treat sudden hearing loss.
An intratympanic procedure delivers medication directly into the middle ear.
