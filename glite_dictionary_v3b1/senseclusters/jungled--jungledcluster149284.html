jungled

adjective
overgrown with plants
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒʌŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈdʒʌŋɡəld / (BrE)
overgrown with dense plants or vegetation, resembling a jungle

Examples

A jungled area
The abandoned lot quickly became jungled after a few months of neglect.
We had to cut a path through the jungled garden to reach the shed.
After the rainy season, the park looked completely jungled and wild.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cleared - free from plants or vegetation
  • synonym: overgrown - covered with plants that have grown in an uncontrolled way
