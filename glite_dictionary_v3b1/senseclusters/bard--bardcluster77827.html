bard

noun
ornamental horse covering
Pronunciation: / bɑrd / (AmE), / bɑːd / (BrE)
an ornamental covering for a horse, especially one used in ceremonial or parade contexts

Examples

Ceremonial bard on a horse
At the tournament, every steed had a unique bard displaying its rider's heraldic colors.
The knight's horse wore a richly decorated bard during the royal procession.
The museum displayed a medieval bard made of velvet and embroidered with gold thread.
