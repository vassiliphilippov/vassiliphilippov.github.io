clutch

adjective
reliable under pressure
Pronunciation: / kl8ct83 / (AmE), / kl8ct83 / (BrE)
(informal) Performing reliably and effectively in a difficult or high-pressure situation; able to produce the right action at a crucial moment.

Examples

Clutch player
In the final seconds, the veteran wide receiver delivered a clutch catch to win the game.
She pulled off a clutch save during the penalty shootout that kept her team alive.
Gamers praised his clutch plays that turned the match after a huge comeback.

Related Terms

  • antonym: choke - to fail to perform at a critical moment because of pressure or nerves
  • synonym: reliable - consistently good in quality or performance; able to be trusted
