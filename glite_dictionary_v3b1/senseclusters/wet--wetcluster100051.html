wet

adjective
producing or secreting milk
7.9
Pronunciation: / wɛt / (AmE), / wɛt / (BrE)
(of a woman or female animal) producing or secreting milk; lactating.

Examples

Wet nurse

That cow is wet and ready to be milked.
