lighting fixture

noun
device that holds artificial lights
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌfɪkstʃɚ / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtɪŋ ˌfɪkstʃə / (BrE)
a device fixed in place for holding or supporting artificial lights, such as lamps or bulbs, to provide illumination

Examples

A lighting fixture device
All lighting fixtures must meet safety regulations before installation.
An elegant lighting fixture can completely change the ambiance of a room.
The kitchen was remodeled with new lighting fixtures above the island.
