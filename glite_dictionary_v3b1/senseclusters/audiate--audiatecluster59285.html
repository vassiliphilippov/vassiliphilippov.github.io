audiate

verb
to imagine music mentally
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɔːdieɪt / (AmE), / ˈɔːdieɪt / (BrE)
to hear or imagine music or sounds in your mind without any actual sound being present

Examples

To audiate a melody
Musicians often audiate a melody before playing it on their instrument.
She tried to audiate the song in her head before singing it aloud.
During practice, the student was asked to audiate the rhythm before clapping it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: imagine - to form a mental image or concept of something
  • synonym: visualize - to form a mental image, especially of something not present
