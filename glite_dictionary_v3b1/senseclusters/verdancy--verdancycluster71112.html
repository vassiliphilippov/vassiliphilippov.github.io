verdancy

noun
lush greenness of vegetation
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈvɝːdənsi / (AmE), / ˈvɜːdənsi / (BrE)
the lush green appearance or rich growth of vegetation

Examples

The verdancy of plants
After the spring rains, the verdancy of the countryside was breathtaking.
Photographers are often attracted to the verdancy found in rainforests.
The verdancy of the new park made it a favorite spot for summer picnics.

Related Terms

  • antonym: barrenness - the state of being empty, lacking vegetation or fertility
  • synonym: greenness - the quality of being green in color, especially in plants
