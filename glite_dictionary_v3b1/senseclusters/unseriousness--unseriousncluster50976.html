unseriousness

noun
lack of seriousness
7.0
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɪriəsnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɪəriəsnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of not being serious or showing a lack of seriousness

Examples

Her unseriousness in meetings
Her unseriousness made it difficult for the team to focus on the important issues.
The teacher was frustrated by the students' unseriousness during the exam.
Despite the gravity of the situation, his unseriousness lightened the mood in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frivolity - lack of seriousness or sense
  • antonym: seriousness - the quality of being serious
