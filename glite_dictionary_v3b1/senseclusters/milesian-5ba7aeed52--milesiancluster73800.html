Milesian

adjective
of ancient Miletus
9.9
Pronunciation: / maɪˈliːʒən / (AmE), / maɪˈliːzɪən / (BrE)
relating to Miletus, its people, or their culture in ancient Greece

Examples

Milesian philosopher
The Milesian school produced some of the earliest Greek philosophers.
Artifacts from the Milesian period reveal a rich and advanced culture.
He studied the Milesian influence on ancient Greek architecture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ionian - relating to Ionia, a region of ancient Greece including Miletus
