Department of Energy

proper noun
U.S. government energy department
Pronunciation: / dɪˌpɑrtmənt əv ˈɛnɚdʒi / (AmE), / dɪˌpɑːtmənt əv ˈɛnədʒi / (BrE)
the federal executive department of the U.S. government responsible for national energy policy and nuclear safety, established in 1977.

Examples
