balls-up

noun
a badly botched or muddled situation
8.1
Pronunciation: / bɔlz ʌp / (AmE), / bɔːlz ʌp / (BrE)
a situation that has been badly botched or muddled; a complete mess, especially as a result of careless or inept action

Examples

A balls-up situation
He admitted the event was a huge balls-up and apologized to everyone involved.
The project turned into a total balls-up when no one checked the schedule.
They tried to fix the system, but only made a bigger balls-up of it.
