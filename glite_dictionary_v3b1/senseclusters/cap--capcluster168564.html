cap

noun
artificial crown for a tooth in dentistry
1.3
Pronunciation: / kæp / (AmE), / kap / (BrE)
(Dentistry) An artificial covering placed over a broken or decayed tooth to restore its shape and function; dental crown.

Examples

Dental cap
After the root canal, she needed a cap on her front tooth.
If your cap feels loose or uncomfortable, schedule a dental visit.
The dentist recommended a cap to protect the cracked molar.
