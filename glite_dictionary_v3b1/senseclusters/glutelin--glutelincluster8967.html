glutelin

noun
protein found in cereal seeds
3.5 8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡluːtəlɪn / (AmE), / ˈɡluːtɛlɪn / (BrE)
a type of simple protein found in the seeds of cereal grains, such as wheat or rice, and soluble in dilute acids or alkalis

Examples

Glutelin protein
Research shows that glutelin makes up a substantial portion of rice protein content.
Some individuals may be sensitive to glutelin proteins found in various grains.
Wheat flour contains a high concentration of glutelin, which contributes to its baking properties.
