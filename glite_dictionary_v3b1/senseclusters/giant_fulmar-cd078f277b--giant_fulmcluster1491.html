giant fulmar

noun
large Antarctic petrel species
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈfʊlmɑr / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈfʊlmɑː / (BrE)
a large brownish petrel found chiefly in the Antarctic seas

Examples

The giant fulmar bird
The giant fulmar is a powerful flier commonly seen gliding over Antarctic waters.

We spotted a giant fulmar circling the ice as we approached the research station.
