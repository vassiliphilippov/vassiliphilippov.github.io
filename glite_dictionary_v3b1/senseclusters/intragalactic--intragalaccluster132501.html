intragalactic

adjective
within a galaxy
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəɡəˈlæktɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəɡəˈlæktɪk / (BrE)
existing or occurring within a single galaxy

Examples

Intragalactic dust clouds
The telescope captured detailed images of intragalactic dust clouds in the Milky Way.
Researchers are interested in intragalactic phenomena such as star formation and black holes.
Science fiction often explores intragalactic travel between distant star systems.
