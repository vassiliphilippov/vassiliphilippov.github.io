drifting

noun
aimless wandering
4.5 4.1
Pronunciation: /ˈdrɪf.tɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈdrɪf.tɪŋ/ (BrE)
the activity or process of moving from place to place without a definite purpose or destination

Examples

There was a sense of drifting in his life after college, as he couldn't settle anywhere.
