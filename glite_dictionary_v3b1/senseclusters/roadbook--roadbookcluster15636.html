roadbook

noun
motorsport navigator's route notebook
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ro\u028ad.b\u028ak / (AmE), / \u02c8r\u0259\u028ad.b\u028ak / (BrE)
(in motorsport) a navigator's notebook containing detailed route instructions, distances, diagrams (often tulip diagrams), and hazard notes used during rally stages or cross-country races.

Examples

Rally roadbook
The navigator studied the roadbook before the stage to anticipate the next corner.
At the start she checked the competitor's roadbook for hazards and distance markers.
During the desert rally, their roadbook handed to the co-driver was smeared with dust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pacenote - shorthand notes describing upcoming turns and hazards used by rally co-drivers
  • synonym: route book - a booklet of directions and distances used to navigate a course
