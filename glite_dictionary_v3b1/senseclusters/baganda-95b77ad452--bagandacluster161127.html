Baganda

proper noun
Ugandan ethnic group
Pronunciation: / bəˈɡɑːndə / (AmE), / bəˈɡændə / (BrE)
the largest ethnic group of central Uganda, known for their distinct culture and language

Examples

The Baganda people
The Baganda have a long history of traditional music and dance.
Many Baganda live in the region surrounding Uganda's capital, Kampala.
The cultural practices of the Baganda are celebrated throughout the country.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Ganda - the language spoken by the Baganda people
  • synonym: Ugandan - a person from Uganda
