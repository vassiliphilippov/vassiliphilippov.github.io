ornithologically

adverb
relating to birds
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɔrnəˈθɑlədʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌɔːnɪˈθɒlədʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to the scientific study of birds

Examples

To study ornithologically
The island is ornithologically significant because it is home to several rare bird species.
Researchers described the area as ornithologically diverse after observing dozens of bird species.
The park is ornithologically important for conservation efforts focused on endangered birds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: zoologically - in a way that relates to the scientific study of animals
