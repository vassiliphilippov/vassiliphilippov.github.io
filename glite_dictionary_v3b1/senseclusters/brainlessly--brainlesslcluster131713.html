brainlessly

adverb
without thinking
Pronunciation: / ˈbreɪnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbreɪnlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a lack of intelligence or thought

Examples

To brainlessly agree
He brainlessly agreed to the plan without asking any questions.
She brainlessly repeated the rumor without checking if it was true.
They brainlessly followed the crowd, not knowing where they were going.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carefully - with attention and thought
  • synonym: carelessly - without taking care or attention
  • synonym: thoughtlessly - without careful consideration or thought
