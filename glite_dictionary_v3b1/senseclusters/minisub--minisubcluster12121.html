minisub

noun
small submersible vessel
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌsʌb / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌsʌb / (BrE)
A small submersible vessel designed for one or two people, used in naval operations or underwater exploration.

Examples

A minisub vessel
The navy ordered several advanced minisubs for special underwater missions.
The research team deployed a minisub to investigate the coral reef at great depths.
Using a minisub, the crew was able to approach the sunken wreck undetected.
