inspirit

verb
encourage, give new spirit to
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspɪrət / (AmE), / ɪnˈspɪrɪt / (BrE)
to fill (someone) with energy, courage, or determination; to encourage or invigorate

Examples

To inspirit someone
His mentor's words inspirited him to tackle the new project with confidence.
She hopes her book will inspirit readers to pursue their dreams.
The uplifting music seemed to inspirit the entire team before the match.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dishearten - to cause someone to lose confidence or spirit
  • synonym: encourage - to give support, confidence, or hope to someone
