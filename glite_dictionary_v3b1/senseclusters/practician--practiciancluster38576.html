practician

noun
person who practices a profession
Pronunciation: / prækˈtɪʃən / (AmE), / prækˈtɪʃən / (BrE)
a person who practices a learned profession, especially one requiring specialized education or training

Examples

A practician in medicine
After years of study, she became a respected practician in her field.
It is essential for a practician to stay updated on current developments in the profession.
The conference was attended by every leading practician in the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: practitioner - a person engaged in the practice of a profession, especially medicine or law
