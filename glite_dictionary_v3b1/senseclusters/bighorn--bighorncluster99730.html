bighorn

noun
wild sheep with large curved horns of western North America
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪɡˌhɔrn / (AmE), / ˈbɪɡˌhɔːn / (BrE)
a wild sheep of mountainous regions of western North America, known for its large, curved horns

Examples

A bighorn sheep
I saw a herd of bighorn on my hiking trip last summer.
Scientists tracked the movements of several bighorn in the Rocky Mountains.
The bighorn is an iconic symbol of the American West.
