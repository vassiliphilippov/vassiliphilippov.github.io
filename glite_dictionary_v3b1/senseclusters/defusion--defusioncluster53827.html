defusion

noun
separation after fusion
8.8 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / diÀàfjuÀê í…ôn / (AmE), / diÀàfjuÀê í…ôn / (BrE)
the process of separating things that were previously joined together by fusion, such as materials or parts.

Examples

Defusion of materials
The defusion of the two metal parts was necessary to repair the machine.
Scientists studied the defusion of cell membranes under different conditions.
After the accident, engineers investigated the cause of the unexpected defusion in the reactor core.
