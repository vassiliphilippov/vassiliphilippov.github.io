beaverboard

noun
light fiberboard material
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːvərˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbiːvəbɔːd / (BrE)
a lightweight fiberboard made from wood fibers, used especially for walls and ceilings in construction

Examples

Beaverboard wall panels
The attic walls were covered with beaverboard instead of plaster.
They replaced the damaged beaverboard panels during the renovation.
Old houses sometimes have beaverboard ceilings that need to be updated.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drywall - a panel used for constructing interior walls
  • synonym: fiberboard - a board made from compressed wood fibers
