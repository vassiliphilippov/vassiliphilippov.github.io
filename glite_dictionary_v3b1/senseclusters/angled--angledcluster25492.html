angled

adjective
set at an angle
Pronunciation: / ˈæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈaŋɡəld / (BrE)
forming or set at a particular angle; not parallel or perpendicular

Examples

Angled parking
Please use angled parking on this side of the street.
The architect designed an angled roof to improve rain drainage.
We found an angled mirror that lets you see around corners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: parallel - side by side and having the same distance continuously between them
  • antonym: straight - extending or moving uniformly in one direction only; without a curve or bend
