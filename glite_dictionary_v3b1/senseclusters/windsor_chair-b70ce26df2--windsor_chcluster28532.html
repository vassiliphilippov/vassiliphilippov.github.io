Windsor chair

noun
wooden chair with spindle back
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnzər tʃɛr / (AmE), / ˈwɪnzə tʃɛə / (BrE)
A wooden chair with a solid seat and a back consisting of multiple thin, turned spindles, often with splayed legs.

Examples

A Windsor chair design
A classic Windsor chair can add charm to any farmhouse kitchen.
He sat in a sturdy Windsor chair by the window, reading his book.
She bought a set of antique Windsor chairs for her dining room.
