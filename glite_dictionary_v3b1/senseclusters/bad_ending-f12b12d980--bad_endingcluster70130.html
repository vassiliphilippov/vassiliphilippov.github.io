bad ending

noun
unsatisfactory conclusion to a story or event
Pronunciation: / bæd ˈɛndɪŋ / (AmE), / bad ˈɛndɪŋ / (BrE)
an unsatisfactory or disappointing conclusion to a story, event, or experience

Examples

A bad ending
Many viewers were disappointed by the bad ending of the season finale.
She always avoids books with a bad ending because she prefers uplifting stories.
The concert had a bad ending when the sound system failed during the last song.

Related Terms

  • antonym: happy ending - a satisfactory or positive conclusion to a story
  • degree higher: tragic ending - an ending involving tragedy or suffering
  • synonym: unsatisfying ending - an ending that fails to satisfy expectations
