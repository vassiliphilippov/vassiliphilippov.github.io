protonate

verb
to add a proton
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtəˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəneɪt / (BrE)
to add a proton to a molecule or atom, typically during a chemical reaction

Examples

To protonate a molecule
Chemists often protonate molecules to change their chemical properties.
In acidic solutions, water can protonate ammonia to form ammonium ions.
The reaction will not proceed unless you protonate the substrate first.
