superposable

adjective
able to coincide exactly when superimposed
6.5
Pronunciation: / suː.pɚˈpoʊ.zə.bəl / (AmE), / ˌsuː.pəˈpəʊ.zə.bəl / (BrE)
able to coincide exactly with another object when superimposed

Examples

Patterns superposable exactly
He demonstrated that the patterns were superposable by aligning them perfectly on the screen.
In chemistry, molecules that are mirror images but not superposable are called enantiomers.
The two transparent shapes are superposable when one is rotated over the other.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congruent - (of geometric figures) having the same shape and size
