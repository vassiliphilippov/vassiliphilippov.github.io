gyro

noun
device for maintaining orientation; gyroscope
9.9 6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪˌroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪərəʊ / (BrE)
a device consisting of a spinning wheel or disc mounted so that its axis can turn freely in any direction, used to maintain orientation or stability by resisting changes in direction

Examples

Gyro sensor
Engineers installed a gyro in the drone to improve its stability during flight.
If the gyro malfunctions, the navigation instruments may give inaccurate readings.
The ship's autopilot system relied on a gyro to maintain a steady course.
