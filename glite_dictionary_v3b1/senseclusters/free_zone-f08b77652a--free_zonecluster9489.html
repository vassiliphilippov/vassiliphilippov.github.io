free zone

noun
area near a port for duty-free storage
4.1 0.29/m
Pronunciation: / friÀê zo än / (AmE), / friÀê z…ô än / (BrE)
an area near a port where goods intended for export can be stored without the payment of customs duties

Examples

A free zone area
Foreign investors are attracted to the free zone because of its tax and duty exemptions.
Goods can remain in the free zone for up to a year before being re-exported or cleared for customs.
The company expanded its operations by setting up a warehouse in the free zone near the coastal port.

Synonyms

  • free port
    4.1 0.11/m
    a free port lets goods be imported, stored, and exported without customs duties, while a free zone is near a port for export storage only.
  • entrepot
    9.9 0.14/m
    an entrepot is a port or warehouse where goods are stored and sent out again, while a free zone is near a port for export storage without duties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free port - a port where imported goods can be stored duty-free before re-export
