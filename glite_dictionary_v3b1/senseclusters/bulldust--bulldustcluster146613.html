bulldust

noun
nonsense; fine dust
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊldʌst / (BrE)
Australian slang for nonsense or for fine, powdery dust found in dry, outback areas.

Examples

Total bulldust
He dismissed the rumors as complete bulldust and refused to believe them.
After driving through the outback, the car was covered in red bulldust.
Don't listen to his story—it's just a load of bulldust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonsense - words or ideas that are silly or untrue
  • synonym: rubbish - something that is not true or is worthless
