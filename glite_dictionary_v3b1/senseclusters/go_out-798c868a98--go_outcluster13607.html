go out

verb
become unfashionable
1.7
Pronunciation: / ɡoʊ aʊt / (AmE), / ɡəʊ aʊt / (BrE)

Examples

Styles go out

Skinny jeans began to go out a few years ago, replaced by looser styles.
Some fashion trends go out quickly, only to come back in style later.
