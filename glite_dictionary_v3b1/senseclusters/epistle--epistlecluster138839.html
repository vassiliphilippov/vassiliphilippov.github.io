epistle

noun
formal or lengthy letter
3.5
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪsəl / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪsəl / (BrE)
a formal or lengthy letter, often written in a serious or literary style

Examples

A formal epistle
He wrote a lengthy epistle explaining his reasons for leaving the company.
In the eighteenth century, poets often composed epistles to friends and patrons.
She received an epistle from her aunt that detailed family history.
