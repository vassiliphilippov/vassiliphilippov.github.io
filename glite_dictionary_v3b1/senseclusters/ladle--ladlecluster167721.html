ladle

noun
deep spoon with a long handle for serving liquids
2.9 1.8
Pronunciation: /ˈleɪ.dəl/ (AmE), /ˈleɪ.dəl/ (BrE)

Examples

She used a ladle to serve the hot soup into the bowls.
The chef dipped the ladle into the pot of stew to check the thickness.
