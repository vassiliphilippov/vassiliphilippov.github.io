ovality

noun
deviation from circularity
Pronunciation: / oʊˈvælɪti / (AmE), / əʊˈvælɪti / (BrE)
the amount or quality by which something is oval or not perfectly round

Examples

The ovality of pipe
Engineers measured the ovality of the pipe to ensure it met manufacturing standards.
High ovality in a wheel can cause vibrations and uneven wear during operation.
The technician recorded the ovality of each cylinder before assembly.
The ovality of the tire was checked to prevent uneven wear on the road.
Engineers monitor the ovality of pipes to ensure they meet safety standards.
A high degree of ovality can affect the performance of rotating machinery.
