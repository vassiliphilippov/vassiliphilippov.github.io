antimodernist

adjective
opposed to modern ideas
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈmɑːdərnɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈmɒdənɪst / (BrE)
opposed to modern ideas, values, or styles, especially in art, culture, or society

Examples

Antimodernist attitude
The museum's antimodernist exhibition celebrated traditional techniques and rejected new artistic trends.
Her antimodernist views were evident in her preference for classic literature over contemporary works.
Some critics described the movement as antimodernist because it resisted changes in social norms.

Related Terms

  • antonym: modernist - supporting modern ideas, values, or styles
  • synonym: traditionalist - favoring traditional ideas or practices
