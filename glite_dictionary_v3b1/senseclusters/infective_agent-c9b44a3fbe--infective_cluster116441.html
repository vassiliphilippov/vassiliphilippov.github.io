infective agent

noun
microorganism causing infection
4.5
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɛktɪv ˈeɪdʒənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɛktɪv ˈeɪdʒənt / (BrE)
A microorganism or substance capable of causing infection in a host organism.

Examples

The infective agent causes illness
Once the infective agent enters the body, it can multiply rapidly and cause illness.
Researchers are working to identify the infective agent behind the mysterious disease.
The doctor explained that the flu virus is an infective agent responsible for seasonal outbreaks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pathogen - a microorganism that can cause disease
