bluegill

noun
a North American freshwater sunfish
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbluˌɡɪl / (AmE), / ˈbluːɡɪl / (BrE)
a small freshwater sunfish (Lepomis macrochirus) native to eastern and central North America, valued as a food and sport fish
