plate

noun
positive electrode in a vacuum tube
Pronunciation: / pleɪt / (AmE), / pleɪt / (BrE)
the positively charged electrode in a vacuum tube that collects electrons emitted by the cathode

Examples

Vacuum tube plate
A high voltage is applied to the plate to ensure proper electron flow.
The engineer replaced a faulty plate in the old radio's vacuum tube.
Without a properly functioning plate, the vacuum tube cannot amplify electrical signals.
