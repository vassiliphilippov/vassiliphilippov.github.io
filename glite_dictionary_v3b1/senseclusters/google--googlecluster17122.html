google

verb
search online using Google
Pronunciation: / ˈɡuɡəl / (AmE), / ˈɡuːɡl / (BrE)
to search for information on the internet using the Google search engine

Examples

To google information
I spent an hour googling cheap flight deals last night.
If you don't know the answer, just google it.
She googled her favorite author to find recent news.
If you don't remember the address, just google the restaurant and check the reviews.
She googled her symptoms and decided to call a doctor after reading worrying posts.
They're googling competitors to gather public information before the meeting tomorrow.
