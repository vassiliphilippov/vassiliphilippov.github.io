conversation

noun
informal talk between people
1.0 132/m
Pronunciation: / Àåk…ëÀên.v…ôrÀàse…™. É…ôn / (AmE), / Àåk…ín.v…ôÀàse…™. É…ôn / (BrE)
a talk, especially an informal one, between two or more people, in which news and ideas are exchanged

Examples

A conversation between friends
She joined the group and listened quietly to their conversation.
The conversation soon turned to their upcoming holiday.
We had a long conversation about our plans for the future.

Synonyms

  • talk
    1.0 43/m
    a talk is a direct exchange where people speak and share ideas aloud, while a conversation is a broader informal exchange.
  • chat
    1.0 12/m
    a chat is a short, friendly, informal talk between people, while a conversation can be longer and cover more serious topics.
  • discussion
    1.4 144/m
    a discussion is an exchange of ideas often aimed at solving a problem or making a decision, while a conversation is more casual.
  • dialog
    4.3 0.00/m
    a dialog is a focused talk aimed at resolving an issue or reaching agreement, while a conversation is more informal and open.
  • chitchat
    2.0 0.76/m
    chitchat is light, often silly small talk about unimportant things, while a conversation can be deeper and share real news or ideas.

  • antonym: monologue - a long speech by one person during a conversation
