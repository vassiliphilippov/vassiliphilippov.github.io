Synonyms

  • Ganesha
    7.9 0.06/m
    Ganesha is the common name used in stories and worship, while Ganapati is the Sanskrit name often used in formal texts.
  • Ganesh

    Ganesh is a simple English spelling seen in conversation and signs, while Ganapati is the full Sanskrit form used in scriptures.
  • Ganesa
    9.9 0.01/m
    Ganesa appears as a rarer variant and may stress prophecy in some texts, while Ganapati is mainly the name for the elephant-headed god.
