Ganapati

proper noun
Hindu god of wisdom; remover of obstacles
Pronunciation: / ˌgɑːnəˈpʌti / (AmE), / ˌɡænəˈpʌti / (BrE)
A Hindu god of wisdom, intellect, and remover of obstacles, usually depicted with an elephant's head. Also known as Ganesha.

Examples

Statue of Ganapati
During the festival, the streets were filled with colorful statues of Ganapati.
Many devotees pray to Ganapati at the start of new ventures to seek his blessings.
Stories about Ganapati's wisdom and humor are popular in Hindu mythology.
