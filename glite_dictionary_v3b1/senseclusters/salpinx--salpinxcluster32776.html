salpinx

noun
anatomical tube in uterus or ear
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsæl.pɪŋks / (AmE), / ˈsæl.pɪŋks / (BrE)
a tubular anatomical structure, especially the Fallopian tube in the uterus or the Eustachian tube in the ear

Examples
