Vulpecula

proper noun
constellation in the Northern Hemisphere
9.9
Pronunciation: / vʌlˈpɛkjələ / (AmE), / vʌlˈpɛkjʊlə / (BrE)
A faint constellation in the Northern Hemisphere, located near Cygnus and Sagitta.
