tweener

noun
ambiguous character
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈtwiːnə / (BrE)
a performer, especially in professional wrestling or entertainment, who is neither clearly a hero nor a villain

Examples

Wrestling tweener character
The new wrestler was introduced as a tweener, making it hard for fans to choose sides.
Her tweener role allowed her to switch between helping heroes and villains in the storyline.
Many fans enjoy the unpredictability of a tweener character in wrestling matches.
