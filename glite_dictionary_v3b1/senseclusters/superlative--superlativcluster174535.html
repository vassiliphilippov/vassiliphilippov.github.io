superlative

noun
the highest degree, the utmost level
Pronunciation: / suːˈpɝːlətɪv / (AmE), / suːˈpɜːlətɪv / (BrE)
the highest degree or quality attainable; something or someone at the utmost level of excellence or achievement

Examples

A superlative of skill
Her performance last night was a superlative of artistic achievement.
The judge described the cake as a superlative among all the entries in the competition.
Winning the Nobel Prize is considered a superlative in the field of literature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acme - the highest point or stage; peak; zenith
  • synonym: peak - the highest or most important point
  • synonym: pinnacle - the highest point of achievement or development
