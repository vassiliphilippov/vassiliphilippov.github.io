motocross

noun
off-road racing motorcycle
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈmoʊ.tə.krɑːs/ /; / /ˈmoʊ.toʊ.krɑːs/ (AmE), /ˈməʊ.tə.krɒs/ (BrE)
a motorcycle designed for racing on rough, off-road tracks, featuring strong suspension and knobby tires

Examples

A motocross bike
He bought a new motocross to ride over rough trails and practice jumps every weekend.
The motocross handled the rocky terrain with ease thanks to its strong suspension.
She entered her first race with a borrowed motocross and finished in the top ten.
