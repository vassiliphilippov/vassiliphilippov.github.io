Oligoporus leucospongia

noun
a species of whitish fungus found on conifer logs
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɑlɪˈɡɑpɔɹəs ˌljuːkoʊˈspʌndʒiə / (AmE), / ˌɒlɪˈɡɒpərəs ˌljuːkəʊˈspʌndʒɪə / (BrE)
A species of pore fungus with a soft, whitish, cottony cap, found on conifer logs in high-elevation forests of the western United States and nearby Canada.
