manilla

noun
envelope or currency bracelet
8.1
Pronunciation: / məˈnɪlə / (AmE), / məˈnɪlə / (BrE)
a strong, usually brown envelope used for documents, or a metal bracelet once used as currency in West Africa

Examples

Manilla envelope
She placed the contract in a manilla envelope before sending it to the office.
Historically, traders used manilla bracelets as a form of currency in West Africa.
Please organize the papers and put them in a manilla for safekeeping.

Related Terms

  • synonym: currency - a system of money in general use in a country
  • synonym: envelope - a flat paper container for letters or documents
