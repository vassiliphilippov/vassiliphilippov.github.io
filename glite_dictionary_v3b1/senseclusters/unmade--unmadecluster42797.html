unmade

adjective
bed: not having sheets arranged
3.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmeɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmeɪd / (BrE)
(of a bed) not having the sheets and blankets arranged neatly after being used.

Examples

An unmade bed
He always feels uncomfortable seeing an unmade bed in the morning.
She left the house in a hurry, leaving her unmade bed behind.
The hotel staff quickly tidied up the unmade beds before the next guests arrived.

Related Terms

  • antonym: made - (of a bed) with sheets and blankets neatly arranged
