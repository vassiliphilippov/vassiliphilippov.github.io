golden pothos

noun
variegated evergreen houseplant
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊl.dən ˈpoʊ.θɑs / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊl.dən ˈpəʊ.θɒs / (BrE)
a climbing evergreen plant with variegated green and yellow leaves, widely grown as a houseplant

Examples

A golden pothos plant
A golden pothos can thrive in low-light conditions, making it ideal for offices.
She placed a golden pothos on the windowsill to brighten up the room.
The leaves of the golden pothos are streaked with vibrant yellow and green.
