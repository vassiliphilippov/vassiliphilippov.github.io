Lycra lout

noun
flashy or inconsiderate cyclist in tight Lycra
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪkrə laʊt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪkrə laʊt / (BrE)
a cyclist who wears tight-fitting Lycra clothing and is regarded as inconsiderate or showy, often in a negative or critical way

The park was crowded with one Lycra lout after another racing along the paths.
