audition

noun
test performance for a role or job
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: / əˈdɪʃ.ən / (AmE), / ɔːˈdɪʃ.ən / (BrE)
a short performance given to demonstrate a performer's suitability for a role or job, especially in music, theatre, or film

Examples

A audition for a role
His impressive audition earned him a spot in the orchestra.
Hundreds of dancers attended the audition, hoping to be selected for the show.
She was nervous before her audition for the lead role in the play.
