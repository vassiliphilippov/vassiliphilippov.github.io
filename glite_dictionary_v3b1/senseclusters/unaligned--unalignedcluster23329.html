unaligned

adjective
not allied; neutral
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈlaɪnd / (BrE)
not officially connected with or supporting any political alliance, group, or side; neutral in international or political contexts

Examples

Unaligned countries
Several countries remained unaligned during the international conflict to avoid taking sides.
The organization is proud to be unaligned with any political party or movement.
He preferred to stay unaligned in the debate, listening to both arguments before deciding.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allied - joined or connected with a group or side
  • synonym: neutral - not supporting any side in a conflict or dispute
  • synonym: nonaligned - not aligned with any political or military alliance
