rakishly

adverb
in a jaunty or stylish manner
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪkɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈreɪkɪʃli / (BrE)
In a stylish, confident, and slightly unconventional or jaunty manner.

Examples

To dress rakishly
He leaned rakishly against the doorframe, exuding charm and confidence.
She wore her hat rakishly tilted to one side, drawing admiring glances.
The actor smiled rakishly as he entered the room, completely at ease.

Related Terms

  • antonym: awkwardly - in a clumsy or uncomfortable manner
  • synonym: jauntily - in a lively, cheerful, and self-confident manner
