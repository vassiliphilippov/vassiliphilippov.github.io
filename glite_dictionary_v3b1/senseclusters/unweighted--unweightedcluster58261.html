unweighted

adjective
not adjusted in value
4.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈweɪtɪd / (BrE)
not given extra importance, value, or influence in a calculation or analysis

Examples

Unweighted results
The survey results were reported as unweighted figures, without any adjustments for demographics.
In the analysis, each response was treated as unweighted to ensure fairness.
The average was calculated using unweighted data, so every entry counted equally.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral - not favoring any side or value
  • antonym: weighted - given extra importance or value in a calculation
