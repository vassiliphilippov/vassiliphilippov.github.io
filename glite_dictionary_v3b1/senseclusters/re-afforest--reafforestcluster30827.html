re-afforest

verb
to restore a forest after deforestation
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriː.æˈfɔːr.ɪst / (AmE), / ˌriː.əˈfɒr.ɪst / (BrE)
to reestablish or restore a forest on land that was previously deforested, especially after clear-cutting or fire

Examples

To re-afforest land
After the wildfire, the government decided to re-afforest the entire hillside.
Environmental organizations are working hard to re-afforest areas that were previously logged.
The initiative to re-afforest degraded land will improve local biodiversity and air quality.
