watercannon

noun
vehicle-mounted high-pressure crowd control hose
1.0 4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔtɚˌkænən / (AmE), / ˈwɔːtəˌkænən / (BrE)
a large, powerful hose mounted on a vehicle and used to direct high-pressure water streams, typically to disperse crowds in riot control.

Examples

A watercannon vehicle
A watercannon can deliver a powerful stream that quickly disperses a large crowd.
Activists criticized the use of watercannons against peaceful demonstrators.
The police used a watercannon to break up the protest on the main street.
