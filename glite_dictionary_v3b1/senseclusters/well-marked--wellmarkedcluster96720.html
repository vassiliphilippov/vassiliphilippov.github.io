well-marked

adjective
clearly indicated; easily recognized
Pronunciation: / wɛl ˈmɑrkt / (AmE), / wɛl ˈmɑːkt / (BrE)
clearly and easily distinguished or indicated; easy to recognize or follow because of clear signs or boundaries.

Examples

A well-marked boundary
The boundary between the two properties is well-marked by a tall, white fence.
The hiking trail was well-marked, so no one got lost along the way.
The symptoms of the illness are well-marked and easy for doctors to identify.

  • antonym: obscure - not clearly expressed or hard to perceive
