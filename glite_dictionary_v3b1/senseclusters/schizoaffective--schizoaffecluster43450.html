schizoaffective

adjective
with mood and psychotic symptoms
Pronunciation: / ˌskɪtsoʊəˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / ˌskɪtsəʊəˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
relating to a mental disorder that includes both mood disorder and psychotic symptoms

Examples

Schizoaffective disorder
The doctor explained that schizoaffective disorders require a combination of treatments.
Her diagnosis was changed to schizoaffective after new symptoms appeared.
People with schizoaffective conditions may experience both mood swings and hallucinations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bipolar - relating to a mood disorder with episodes of mania and depression
  • synonym: schizophrenic - relating to a mental disorder with psychotic symptoms such as delusions or hallucinations
