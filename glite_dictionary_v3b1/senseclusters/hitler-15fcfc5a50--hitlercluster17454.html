Hitler

proper noun
German Nazi dictator (1889–1945)
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪtlɚ / (AmE), / ˈhɪtlə / (BrE)
(1889–1945) Adolf Hitler, German leader of the Nazi Party, who was dictator of Germany from 1934 to 1945 and instigated World War II.

Examples

Hitler the dictator
Many historians study the actions of Hitler to understand the causes of World War II.
References to Hitler's regime often appear in discussions of totalitarianism.
The rise of Hitler dramatically changed the course of European history.

  • synonym: dictator - a ruler with total power over a country
