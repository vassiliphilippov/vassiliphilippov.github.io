unmourned

adjective
not grieved for; causing no mourning
9.9 0.05/m
Pronunciation: /  ånÀàm…îrnd / (AmE), /  ånÀàm…îÀênd / (BrE)
Not grieved for or not lamented after death; having received no mourning.

Examples

An unmourned death
Her departure from the company was largely unmourned by her colleagues.
The old factory fell into ruin, unmourned and forgotten by the town.
The unpopular leader died unmourned by the majority of his people.
