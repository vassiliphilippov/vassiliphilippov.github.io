Mexican bean beetle

noun
a beetle pest of bean plants
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪkən bin ˈbiːtəl / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪkən biːn ˈbiːtl̩ / (BrE)
a beetle originally from Mexico that feeds on the leaves of bean plants, often considered a pest in the United States

Examples

The Mexican bean beetle pest
Controlling the spread of the Mexican bean beetle is important for healthy bean harvests.
Farmers worry about the damage caused by the Mexican bean beetle to their crops.
The larvae of the Mexican bean beetle feed on bean leaves, causing significant defoliation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beetle - any of an order of insects with hard forewings
  • synonym: pest - an animal harmful to crops or livestock
