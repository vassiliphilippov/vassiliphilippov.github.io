urosepsis

noun
urinary tract infection
Pronunciation: / ˌjʊroʊˈsɛpsɪs / (AmE), / ˌjʊərəʊˈsɛpsɪs / (BrE)
a serious infection that begins in the urinary tract and spreads to the bloodstream

Examples

Urosepsis infection
Early diagnosis of urosepsis can greatly improve a patient's chances of recovery.
Doctors treated the elderly man for urosepsis after his urinary tract infection worsened.
Antibiotics are essential in managing cases of urosepsis to prevent further complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sepsis - a life-threatening response to infection in the body
  • synonym: urinary tract infection - an infection in any part of the urinary system
