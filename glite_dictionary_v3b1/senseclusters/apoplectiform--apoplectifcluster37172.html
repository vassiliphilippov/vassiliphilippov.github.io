apoplectiform

adjective
resembling apoplexy (stroke)
Pronunciation: / ˌæp.əˈplɛk.tɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˌap.əˈplɛk.tɪ.fɔːm / (BrE)
Resembling or characteristic of apoplexy (stroke), especially in sudden onset or symptoms.

Examples

Apoplectiform symptoms
Doctors observed apoplectiform changes indicating acute brain dysfunction.
Some brain lesions may present with apoplectiform symptoms, such as sudden paralysis or loss of consciousness.
The patient was admitted with an apoplectiform episode that closely mimicked a stroke.

Related Terms

  • synonym: apoplectic - relating to or showing symptoms of apoplexy
