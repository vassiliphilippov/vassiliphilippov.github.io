producer

noun
person who oversees making of a show or film
1.0
Pronunciation: / prəˈdusər / (AmE), / prəˈdjuːsə / (BrE)
a person who finds financing for and supervises the making and presentation of a show, film, or program

Examples

Film producer
After years of struggle, she finally convinced a producer to back her film project.
Every TV show needs a skilled producer to manage the budget and oversee the team.
The Broadway musical had a new producer who brought fresh ideas to the production.

Related Terms

  • synonym: director - person who supervises the creative aspects of a play, film, or TV program
