coach horse

noun
strong horse for drawing coaches
Pronunciation: / koʊtʃ hɔrs / (AmE), / kəʊtʃ hɔːs / (BrE)
a strong horse bred and used for drawing coaches

Examples

A coach horse team
During the 19th century, a team of coach horses was essential for mail coaches.
The coach horse stood patiently as the carriage was prepared for the long journey.
The powerful build of a coach horse made it ideal for long-distance travel over rough roads.

Related Terms

  • synonym: draft horse - a large horse bred to draw heavy loads
