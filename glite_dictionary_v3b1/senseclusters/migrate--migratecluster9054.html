migrate

verb
move software or data between systems
4.3 2.03/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8ma\u026a\u02cc\u0261re\u026at / (AmE), / \u02c8ma\u026a\u0261re\u026at / (BrE)
(in computing) to move data, applications, or workloads from one system, platform, or environment to another, typically during upgrades, consolidation, or cloud transitions.

Examples

To migrate data
The company decided to migrate its email system to the cloud.
They will migrate the customer database overnight to reduce service interruption.
Engineers are migrating several legacy applications to a modern container platform.

Related Terms

  • synonym: move - to change the location of something; used broadly for changing system or storage locations
  • synonym: transfer - to move something from one place or person to another, including data between systems
