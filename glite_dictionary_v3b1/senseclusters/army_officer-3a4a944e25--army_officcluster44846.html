army officer

noun
an officer in the army
Pronunciation: / ˈɑrmi ˌɑfɪsər / (AmE), / ˈɑːmi ˌɒfɪsə / (BrE)
a person who holds a position of authority as an officer in the army

Examples

An army officer commands troops
After years of dedicated service, he retired as an army officer.
Her father is an experienced army officer stationed overseas.
The army officer gave a briefing to the new recruits.
