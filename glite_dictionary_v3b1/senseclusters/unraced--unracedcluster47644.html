unraced

adjective
not competed
5.8
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈreɪst / (AmE), / ʌnˈreɪst / (BrE)
not having taken part in a race or competition

Examples

An unraced horse
The young horse remained unraced until its third year.
Many trainers prefer to keep their best athletes unraced before a major competition.
The car was completely unraced when it was sold to the new team.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inexperienced - lacking experience or practice
  • antonym: raced - having participated in a race
