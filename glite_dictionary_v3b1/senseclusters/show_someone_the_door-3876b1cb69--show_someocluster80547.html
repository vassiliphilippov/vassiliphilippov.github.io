show someone the door

verb
forcefully dismiss someone

Pronunciation: /  Éo ä Ààs åmw ån √∞…ô d…î…π / (AmE), /  É…ô ä Ààs åmw ån √∞…ô d…îÀê / (BrE)
to ask someone to leave a place, especially in a forceful or unfriendly way; to dismiss or eject someone

Examples

To show someone the door
After his repeated complaints, the manager decided to show him the door.
If you insult the staff again, we will have to show you the door.
She politely but firmly showed him the door after the meeting.
