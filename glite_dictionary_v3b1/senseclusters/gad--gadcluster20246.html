gad

noun
a sharp spike on a rider's heel for urging a horse
Pronunciation: / ɡæd / (AmE), / ɡæd / (BrE)
a sharp metal spike attached to a rider's heel, used to urge a horse forward; a spur

Examples

A horse gad
A sharp gad can make a stubborn horse obey its rider.
He decorated his boots with an ornate silver gad.
The cowboy pressed his gad against the horse to make it move faster.
