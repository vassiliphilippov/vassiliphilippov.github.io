Malaysian

noun
a native or inhabitant of Malaysia
Pronunciation: / məˈleɪʒ(ə)n / (AmE), / məˈleɪziən / (BrE)
a person who is a native or inhabitant of Malaysia

Examples

A Malaysian person

The government invited all Malaysians to participate in the national census.
