kouprey

noun
rare Southeast Asian ox
Pronunciation: / kuːˈpreɪ / (AmE), / kuːˈpreɪ / (BrE)
a rare wild ox native to Southeast Asia, possibly extinct, known for its long horns and slender build

Examples

The kouprey ox
The kouprey is considered one of the world's rarest large mammals.
Researchers have spent years searching for signs of the elusive kouprey in remote forests.
Many believe the kouprey may already be extinct due to habitat loss and hunting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: banteng - a species of wild cattle found in Southeast Asia
  • antonym: domestic cattle - cattle that have been domesticated for farming
  • synonym: gaur - a large wild ox native to South and Southeast Asia
