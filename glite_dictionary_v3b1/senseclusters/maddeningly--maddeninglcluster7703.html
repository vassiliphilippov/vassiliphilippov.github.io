maddeningly

adverb
extremely; very
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˈmædənɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈmædənɪŋli / (BrE)
to an extreme or remarkable degree, often used as an emphatic intensifier without implying annoyance

Examples

Maddeningly simple answer
The solution to the puzzle was maddeningly simple, surprising everyone in the room.
She was maddeningly calm even when the situation became stressful.
The instructions were maddeningly clear, leaving no room for confusion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extremely - to a very great degree
  • synonym: incredibly - to a remarkable or extraordinary degree
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree
