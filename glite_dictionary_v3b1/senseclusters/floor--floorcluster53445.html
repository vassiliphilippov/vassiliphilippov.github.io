floor

verb
surprise or shock greatly
Pronunciation: / flɔɹ / (AmE), / flɔː / (BrE)
to surprise or shock someone greatly, leaving them unable to react for a moment

Examples

To floor the crowd
His incredible performance floors the audience every time.
I was completely floored by her unexpected kindness.
The news of his sudden resignation really floored everyone in the office.

Related Terms

  • synonym: astonish - to surprise someone very much
  • antonym: bore - to make someone feel bored or uninterested
  • synonym: stun - to shock or overwhelm someone
