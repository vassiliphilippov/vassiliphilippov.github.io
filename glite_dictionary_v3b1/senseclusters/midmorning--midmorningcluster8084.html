midmorning

adjective
in the middle of morning
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˌmɪdˈmɔrnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌmɪdˈmɔːnɪŋ / (BrE)
happening or scheduled in the middle part of the morning, usually between early morning and noon

Examples

Midmorning meeting
The midmorning sun was already warm as they walked through the park.
She always enjoys a cup of coffee during her midmorning break at work.
A midmorning snack helps him stay focused until lunchtime.
