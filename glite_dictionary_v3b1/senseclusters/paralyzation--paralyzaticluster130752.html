paralyzation

noun
inability to act
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpærəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpærəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
a state in which action, movement, or decision-making is stopped or severely limited

Examples

Paralyzation of work
The paralyzation of the transportation system left thousands of commuters stranded for hours.
Fear of failure can lead to paralyzation when making important life decisions.
After the cyberattack, the company experienced complete paralyzation of its online services.

  • synonym: immobilization - the act of preventing movement or action
  • synonym: paralysis - loss of ability to move or act
