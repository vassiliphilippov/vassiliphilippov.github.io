wonkish

adjective
overly technical style
9.6
Pronunciation: /ˈwɑːŋ.kɪʃ/ /; / /ˈwɔːŋ.kɪʃ/ (AmE), /ˈwɒŋ.kɪʃ/ /; / /ˈwɔːŋ.kɪʃ/ (BrE)
interested in or showing a style that is very technical, academic, or detailed, often in a way that is hard for most people to understand

Examples

A wonkish style
The professor's wonkish explanation left many students confused about the main point.
Her wonkish articles are filled with data and policy details that few readers can follow.
Some people enjoy wonkish debates about economic theory, but others find them boring.
His wonkish explanations sometimes confused people who were not experts in the field.
The report was filled with wonkish details that only specialists would appreciate.
She has a wonkish interest in government policy and statistics.

Related Terms

  • synonym: academic - relating to education or scholarly study
  • antonym: accessible - easy to understand or approachable
  • antonym: practical - focused on real-world use rather than theory or detail
  • synonym: technical - using specialized or complex language
