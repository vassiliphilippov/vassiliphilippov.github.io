motor mower

noun
lawn mower with a motor
6.8 3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtər ˌmoʊər / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˌməʊə / (BrE)
A lawn mower powered by a gasoline or electric motor rather than by manual effort.

Examples

A motor mower machine
He trimmed the grass with his new motor mower over the weekend.
She prefers using a motor mower because it's less physically demanding than pushing a traditional mower.
Unlike manual versions, a motor mower can cover large areas quickly and efficiently.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lawn mower - a machine for cutting the grass on a lawn
  • antonym: manual mower - a lawn mower operated by hand power, without a motor
