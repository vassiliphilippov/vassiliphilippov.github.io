multilane

adjective
having two or more lanes (road)
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌleɪn / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌleɪn / (BrE)
(of a road or highway) having two or more lanes for traffic moving in the same direction or in opposite directions.

Examples

A multilane road
Driving on a multilane road requires extra caution when changing lanes.
The city is planning to expand the highway into a multilane thoroughfare to reduce congestion.
Traffic moved smoothly thanks to the newly constructed multilane expressway.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dual carriageway - a road with two carriageways for traffic in each direction, typically with multiple lanes
  • antonym: single-lane - (of a road) having only one lane for traffic
