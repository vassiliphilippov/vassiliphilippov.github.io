Udo

proper noun
island near Jeju
Pronunciation: / ˈuːdoʊ / (AmE), / ˈuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a small island off the east coast of Jeju Island in South Korea, famous for its beaches and scenic views

Examples

Udo Island
We spent the afternoon exploring the beautiful beaches of Udo and taking photos of the coastline.
Many tourists visit Udo for its clear waters and peaceful atmosphere.
The ferry to Udo departs several times a day from Jeju Island.
