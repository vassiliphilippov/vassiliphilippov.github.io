rajah

noun
Indian prince or king
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːdʒə / (AmE), / ˈrɑːdʒə / (BrE)
(in India) a king or prince; a ruler of a princely state or territory.

Examples

The rajah ruled
Ancient palaces from the time of the rajahs still stand in Rajasthan.
The rajah welcomed the British envoy with great ceremony.
The region was once governed by a powerful rajah and his family.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: maharajah - a great Indian king; a ruler of a large principality in India
  • antonym: queen - a female ruler equivalent to a rajah
