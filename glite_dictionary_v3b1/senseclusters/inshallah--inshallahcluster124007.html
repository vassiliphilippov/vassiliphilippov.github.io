inshallah

interjection
if God wills
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.ʃə.ˈlɑː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈʃɑː.lə/ (AmE), /ɪn.ʃə.ˈlɑː/ /; / /ɪn.ˈʃɑː.lə/ (BrE)
used to express hope that something will happen if it is God's will, or to indicate uncertainty about the future

Examples

Inshallah, it happens
We will finish the project by next week, inshallah.
She said she would visit us soon, inshallah.
The team hopes to win the championship this year, inshallah.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hopefully - used to express a wish or hope for something to happen
  • synonym: maybe - used to express uncertainty or possibility
