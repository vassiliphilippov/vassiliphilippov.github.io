pervertedly

adverb
in a distorted way
9.6
Pronunciation: /pə.ˈvɜːr.tɪd.liː/ /; / /pə.ˈvɜːr.təd.liː/ (AmE), /pə.ˈvɜː.tɪd.liː/ /; / /pə.ˈvɜː.təd.liː/ (BrE)
in a manner that corrupts, distorts, or twists the original meaning, purpose, or intent of something

Examples

Pervertedly changes meaning
The story was pervertedly changed to fit the director's controversial vision.
He pervertedly interpreted the law to justify his actions.
Some viewers felt the message was pervertedly twisted for dramatic effect.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corruptly - in a dishonest or morally wrong way
  • synonym: distortingly - in a way that changes something so that it is no longer true or accurate
  • antonym: faithfully - in a way that remains true to the original
