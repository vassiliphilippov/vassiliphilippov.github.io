overcontrolling

adjective
excessively controlling
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvɚkənˈtroʊlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ / (BrE)
tending to control other people's actions or decisions too much

Examples

Overcontrolling parent
His overcontrolling attitude made it difficult for his team to share their ideas.
Parents who are overcontrolling may unintentionally limit their children's independence.
She realized her overcontrolling nature was affecting her friendships.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domineering - asserting control over others in an overbearing way
  • antonym: permissive - allowing much freedom and not controlling
