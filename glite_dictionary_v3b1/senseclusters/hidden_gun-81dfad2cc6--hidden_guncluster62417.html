hidden gun

noun
a gun concealed from view
Pronunciation: / ˈhɪdən ɡʌn / (AmE), / ˈhɪdən ɡʌn / (BrE)
a gun or firearm that is concealed from view

Examples

A hidden gun found
During the inspection, a hidden gun was found taped behind the dashboard.
She carried a hidden gun in her purse for self-defense.
The officer searched the room and discovered a hidden gun under the mattress.
