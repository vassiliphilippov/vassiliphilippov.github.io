Tubercularia

noun
genus of fungi with red or pink nodules
9.9
Pronunciation: / tuːˌbɝːkjəˈlɛəriə /; / tuːˌbɝːkjəˈlɛriə / (AmE), / tjuːˌbɜːkjʊˈleəriə / (BrE)
a genus of fungi in the family Tuberculariaceae, with red or pink nodules (conidia), some of which can cause dieback in woody plants
