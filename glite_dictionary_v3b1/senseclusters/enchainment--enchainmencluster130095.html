enchainment

noun
linking in sequence
9.3
Pronunciation: / ɛnˈtʃeɪnmənt / (AmE), / ɛnˈtʃeɪnmənt / (BrE)
the linking together of ideas, sentences, or elements in a sequence

Examples

Enchainment of ideas
The enchainment of ideas in her essay made the argument easy to follow.
In music, composers often use enchainment to create smooth transitions between themes.
The teacher praised the student's enchainment of sentences in the story.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a relationship or link between things
  • antonym: disjunction - a lack of connection or separation between things
