underclothing

noun
clothes worn under other clothing
Pronunciation: / ˈʌndɚˌkloʊðɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʌndəˌkləʊðɪŋ / (BrE)
Clothes worn next to the skin and beneath outer garments.

Examples

Set of underclothing
He packed several sets of underclothing for his trip to the mountains.
Proper underclothing can help keep you warm during winter.
The camp required everyone to bring an extra pair of underclothing.

Related Terms

  • antonym: outerwear - clothes worn over other clothes, especially outdoors
  • synonym: underwear - clothes worn under outer clothes, typically next to the skin
