Ryukyu

adjective
of the Ryukyu Islands
Pronunciation: / riˈjuːkjuː / (AmE), / riˈjuːkjuː / (BrE)
relating to the Ryukyu Islands, their people, languages, culture, or products

Examples

Ryukyu culture
The museum features a collection of Ryukyu pottery and textiles.
She studied the unique Ryukyu languages spoken on the islands.
Traditional Ryukyu music is performed with special instruments and vibrant costumes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Japanese - relating to Japan, its people, or culture
  • synonym: Okinawan - relating to Okinawa, the largest of the Ryukyu Islands
