butterless

adjective
without butter
2.1
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtərləs / (AmE), / ˈbʌtələs / (BrE)
not containing, made with, or covered in butter

Examples

Butterless croissant
He ordered a butterless croissant to avoid extra calories.
Some recipes call for butterless pastry to make them vegan-friendly.
Her butterless toast was topped with homemade jam instead.

Related Terms

  • antonym: buttery - containing or covered with butter
  • synonym: plain - without any added toppings or extras
