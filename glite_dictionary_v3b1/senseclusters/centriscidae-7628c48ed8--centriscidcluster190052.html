Centriscidae

noun
family of marine fishes (shrimpfishes)
Pronunciation: / sɛnˈtrɪsɪˌdi /; / sɛnˌtrɪsɪˈdiː / (AmE), / sɛnˈtrɪsɪˌdiː /; / sɛnˌtrɪsɪˈdiː / (BrE)
a family of small, slender marine fishes called shrimpfishes, known for their long, tubular snouts and unusual vertical swimming
