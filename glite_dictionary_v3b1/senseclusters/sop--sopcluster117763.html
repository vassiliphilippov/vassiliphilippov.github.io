sop

verb
dip or soak something in liquid
7.3 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / sÉ‘p / (AmE), / sÉ’p / (BrE)
to dip or soak something, especially bread, briefly in a liquid such as soup or sauce

Examples

To sop bread in soup
After dinner, they sopped the last of the gravy with biscuits.
He sopped up the stew with a piece of crusty bread.
She loves to sop her bread in olive oil before eating it.

Synonyms
