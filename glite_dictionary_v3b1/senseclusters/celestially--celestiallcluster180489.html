celestially

adverb
in a heavenly way
7.6
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛstʃəli / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛstɪəli / (BrE)
in a way that is supremely pleasing, blissful, or heavenly; extremely good or beautiful.

Examples

Sings celestially
The choir sang so celestially that the audience was moved to tears.
She decorated the room celestially, with soft lights and shimmering fabrics.
The dessert tasted celestially sweet, unlike anything I had tried before.

Related Terms

  • synonym: divinely - in a way that is like a god or heavenly
  • antonym: earthly - in a way that is ordinary or of this world
  • synonym: heavenly - in a way that is like heaven; blissfully
