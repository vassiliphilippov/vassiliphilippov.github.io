fairish

adjective
moderately fair; not excessive
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛrɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈfeərɪʃ / (BrE)
moderately fair in amount, degree, or quality; not excessive or extreme

Examples

A fairish amount
He earns a fairish wage working at the factory.
The weather was fairish for our picnic—neither perfectly sunny nor particularly rainy.
We had a fairish chance of winning the game, despite being the underdogs.
