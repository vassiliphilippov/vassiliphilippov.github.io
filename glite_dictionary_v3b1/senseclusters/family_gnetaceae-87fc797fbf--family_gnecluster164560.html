family Gnetaceae

noun
a botanical family of seed plants
Pronunciation: / fæməli dʒnɛˈteɪsiː / (AmE), / fæmɪli dʒnɛˈteɪsiː / (BrE)
A botanical family of plants with small unisexual flowers and fleshy or winged fruits, including the genus Gnetum and sometimes Ephedra and Welwitschia.
