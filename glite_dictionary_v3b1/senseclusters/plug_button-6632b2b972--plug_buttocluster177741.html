plug button

noun
cover for holes or screws in furniture
8.0 4.1
Pronunciation: / plʌɡ ˈbʌt̬n̩ / (AmE), / plʌɡ ˈbʌt.ən / (BrE)
A small, flat piece, typically of plastic, metal, or wood, used to cover holes or hide visible screws in furniture or carpentry.

Examples

A plug button cover

If a plug button falls out, the screw underneath may become visible.
The plug button gave the furniture a neat and finished appearance.
