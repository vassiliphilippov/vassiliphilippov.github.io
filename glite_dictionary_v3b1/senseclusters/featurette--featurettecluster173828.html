featurette

noun
short supplementary film
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːtʃəˈrɛt / (AmE), / ˌfiːtʃəˈrɛt / (BrE)
a short film or video, often included as extra content to provide behind-the-scenes information or special features

We watched a featurette that explained how the special effects were created.
