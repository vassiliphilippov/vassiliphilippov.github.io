gloomy

adjective
feeling sad or hopeless
2.5 1.90/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…°luÀêmi / (AmE), / Àà…°luÀêmi / (BrE)

Examples

Feeling gloomy

The weather was so gloomy that it seemed to match his mood.
