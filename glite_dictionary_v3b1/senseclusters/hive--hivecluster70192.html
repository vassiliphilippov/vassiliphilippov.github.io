hive

verb
to gather or place bees in a hive
Pronunciation: / haɪv / (AmE), / haɪv / (BrE)
to gather (a swarm of bees) into a hive; to place or collect in a hive

Examples

To hive a swarm
After finding the queen, they carefully hived the new colony in a wooden box.
It takes patience to hive bees without disturbing them too much.
The beekeeper skillfully hived the swarm that had gathered in the orchard.
