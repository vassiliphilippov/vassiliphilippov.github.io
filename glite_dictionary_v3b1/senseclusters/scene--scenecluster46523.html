scene

noun
painted parts of a stage set
3.7 2.87/m
Pronunciation: / siÀên / (AmE), / siÀên / (BrE)
the painted elements of a stage set designed to represent a particular place or environment

Examples

Stage scene
A beautifully painted scene gave the illusion of a bustling city square.
He admired the detailed scene that transformed the stage into a medieval village.
The theater crew spent hours arranging the scene before the opening night.
