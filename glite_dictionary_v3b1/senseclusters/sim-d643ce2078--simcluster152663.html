Sim

noun
simulation device
Pronunciation: / sɪm / (AmE), / sɪm / (BrE)
an abbreviation for a simulation or simulator, especially a program or device used for training or testing

Examples

Flight Sim
The pilot spent several hours in the Sim practicing emergency landings.
Before flying the real aircraft, trainees must complete exercises in the Sim.
The new Sim offers a realistic experience for both beginners and experienced pilots.

Related Terms

  • synonym: simulation - the process of imitating a real process or system
  • synonym: simulator - a device or program that imitates real conditions for training or testing
