horn

noun
hard, pointed growth on animal's head
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɔrn / (AmE), / hɔːn / (BrE)
a hard, pointed growth on the head of certain animals, such as cattle, goats, or rhinoceroses

Examples

Mountain goats use their horns to establish dominance within the herd.
She admired the curved horn of the ram displayed in the museum.
The bull charged, lowering its horn in a show of aggression.
