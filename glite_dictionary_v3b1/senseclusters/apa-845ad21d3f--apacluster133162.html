APA

noun
citation style, organization
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˌeɪ piː ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌeɪ piː ˈeɪ / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the American Psychological Association or its widely used academic citation style

Examples

APA citation style
All references in your essay should follow the APA format for consistency.
The professor asked students to use APA style when citing their sources.
She is a member of the APA and attends their annual conferences.

Related Terms

  • antonym: Chicago style - another major academic citation style
  • antonym: MLA - a different academic citation style used in humanities
