depleted

adjective
reduced or diminished in quantity
2.2 3.9
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈpliː.tɪd/ /; / /dɪ.ˈpliː.təd/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈpliː.təd/ /; / /dɪ.ˈpliː.tɪd/ (BrE)
no longer having enough of the necessary or desired amount; reduced in quantity, power, or value

Examples

The depleted water supply

His energy was depleted after running the marathon in the intense heat.
The depleted inventory meant the store was unable to meet customers' demands.
