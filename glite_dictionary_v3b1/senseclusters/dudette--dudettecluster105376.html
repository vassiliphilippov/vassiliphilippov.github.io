dudette

noun
female slang term
Pronunciation: / duːˈdɛt / (AmE), / djuːˈdɛt / (BrE)
a slang term for a woman or girl, used as the female counterpart to 'dude'

Examples

A dudette friend
She laughed and said, 'Thanks, dudette!' after her friend complimented her outfit.
The group of surfers cheered as one dudette caught the biggest wave of the day.
He greeted his sister with a playful, 'What's up, dudette?' when she joined the party.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dude - a slang term for a man or boy
  • synonym: gal - an informal term for a girl or woman
