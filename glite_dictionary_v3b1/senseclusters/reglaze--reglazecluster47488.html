reglaze

verb
to replace window glass
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈɡleɪz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈɡleɪz / (BrE)
to replace or repair the glass or glazing in a window or door, often by fitting new panes or sealant

Examples

To reglaze a window
After the storm shattered several windows, the homeowners decided to reglaze the entire front of the house.
The old greenhouse needed someone to reglaze its cracked and missing panes before winter arrived.
A professional was called in to reglaze the antique door to preserve its original appearance.

Related Terms

  • antonym: break - to damage or destroy something so that it is in pieces
  • synonym: repair - to fix or restore something that is damaged
  • synonym: replace - to put something new in the place of something old or broken
