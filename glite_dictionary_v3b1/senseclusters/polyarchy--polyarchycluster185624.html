polyarchy

noun
rule by many
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑliˌɑrki / (AmE), / ˈpɒliˌɑːki / (BrE)
a form or system of government in which power is held by many people or groups rather than by a single ruler

Related Terms

  • antonym: autocracy - a system of government by one person with absolute power
  • synonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population, typically through elected representatives
  • antonym: monarchy - a government ruled by a single person, such as a king or queen
  • antonym: oligarchy - a form of government in which power is held by a small group
