hahahaha

noun
written laughter
6.3
Pronunciation: / hɑːhɑːhɑːhɑː / (AmE), / hɑːhɑːhɑːhɑː / (BrE)
a written representation of loud or extended laughter, often used in online messages or texts

Examples

A big hahahaha
When she read the funny comment, she replied with a big hahahaha in the chat.
His story was so ridiculous that everyone responded with a loud hahahaha online.
I could tell he found the meme hilarious because he sent a long hahahaha back.

Related Terms

  • synonym: giggle - a light, silly laugh
  • synonym: lol - an abbreviation for 'laugh out loud', used to show amusement in writing
