unreformed

adjective
not improved
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfɔrmd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfɔːmd / (BrE)
not changed, improved, or made better from an earlier or original state

Examples

Unreformed system
The country's legal system remained unreformed for decades after independence.
Many critics argue that the education sector is still largely unreformed.
Despite new leadership, the organization stayed unreformed in its practices.

Related Terms

  • antonym: reformed - changed or improved for the better
  • synonym: unchanged - remaining the same as before
