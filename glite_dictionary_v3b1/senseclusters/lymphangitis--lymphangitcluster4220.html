lymphangitis

noun
inflammation of a lymph vessel
7.3
Pronunciation: / lɪm.fænˈdʒaɪ.tɪs / (AmE), / lɪm.fænˈdʒaɪ.tɪs / (BrE)
Inflammation of a lymph vessel, often marked by red streaks and swelling in the affected area.

Examples

Red streaks in lymphangitis
Prompt treatment of lymphangitis is important to prevent further complications.
Swelling and pain in the limb suggested an underlying case of lymphangitis.
The patient was diagnosed with lymphangitis after red streaks appeared on his arm.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cellulitis - inflammation of subcutaneous connective tissue
  • synonym: phlebitis - inflammation of a vein
