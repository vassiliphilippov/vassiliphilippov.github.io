fogbound

adjective
enveloped or covered in fog
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑɡˌbaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈfɒɡˌbaʊnd / (BrE)
Enveloped or covered in thick fog, often making movement or visibility difficult.

Examples

A fogbound airport
All flights were delayed because the fogbound airport was deemed unsafe for takeoff.
As we drove through the fogbound valley, the world outside looked ghostly and unreal.
The fogbound coastline made navigation hazardous for ships last night.
