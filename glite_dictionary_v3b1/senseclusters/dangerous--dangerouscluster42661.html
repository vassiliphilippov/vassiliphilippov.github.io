Synonyms

  • unsafe
    1.1 5.23/m
    unsafe is not free from harm and often describes places or objects, while dangerous means likely to cause serious harm.
  • hazardous
    5.2 9.01/m
    hazardous involves a clear health or safety risk in work or transport, while dangerous is a general threat of harm.
  • perilous
    7.5 3.13/m
    perilous feels dramatic and extreme, often used in stories, while dangerous is a common word for real threats.
  • harmful
    2.6 13/m
    harmful mainly describes things that cause damage or bad effects, while dangerous stresses a real risk of injury.
  • injurious
    7.0 0.86/m
    injurious is formal and used in law or medicine about injury, while dangerous is everyday and covers many risks.
  • risky
    3.6 15/m
    risky suggests a chance of loss or failure in actions or choices, while dangerous means likely to cause harm directly.
