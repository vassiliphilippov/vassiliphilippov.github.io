dangerous

adjective
likely to cause harm or risk
Pronunciation: / ˈdeɪndʒərəs / (AmE), / ˈdeɪndʒərəs / (BrE)
likely to cause harm, injury, or risk; involving danger or threat

Examples

Dangerous situation
It is dangerous to drive on icy roads without proper tires.
That was a dangerous decision, but it saved many lives.
The chemicals stored in that cabinet are extremely dangerous if handled incorrectly.
Handling toxic chemicals without proper equipment can be extremely dangerous.
People were evacuated from the dangerous area near the collapsing bridge.
The mountain trail becomes especially dangerous during heavy rainstorms.
