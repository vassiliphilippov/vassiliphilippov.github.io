tablinum

noun
Roman office room
Pronunciation: / təˈblaɪnəm / (AmE), / təˈbliːnəm / (BrE)
a room in an ancient Roman house, usually used as an office or reception area

Examples

The tablinum room
The wealthy merchant welcomed his guests into the tablinum to discuss business matters.
Archaeologists discovered ancient scrolls in the ruins of a Roman tablinum.
The tablinum in the villa overlooked the beautiful central garden.
