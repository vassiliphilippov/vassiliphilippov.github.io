lightweight

adjective
not heavy compared to similar items
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtˌweɪt / (BrE)
Weighing less than other items of the same type or used for similar purposes.

Examples

Lightweight jacket
She prefers lightweight jackets for traveling because they are easy to pack.
The company has developed a lightweight metal for use in aircraft construction.
These lightweight running shoes make jogging much more comfortable.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heavy - having great weight; difficult to lift or move
  • synonym: light - having little weight; not heavy
