sup

noun
a small amount of liquid
Pronunciation: / sʌp / (AmE), / sʌp / (BrE)
a small quantity of liquid, especially a swallow or sip of a drink or liquid food

Examples

A sup of tea
He took a cautious sup of the hot tea before speaking.
Just a sup of soup was left in the bowl.
She asked for a sup of wine to warm herself.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sip - a small mouthful of liquid
