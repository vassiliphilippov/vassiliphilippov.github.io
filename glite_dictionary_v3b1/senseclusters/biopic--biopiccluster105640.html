biopic

noun
film about real life
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbaɪoʊˌpɪk / (AmE), / ˈbaɪəʊˌpɪk / (BrE)
a film that dramatizes the life of a real person

Related Terms

  • synonym: biographical film - a film based on the life of a real person
  • antonym: documentary - a non-fiction film presenting facts about real events or people
  • synonym: drama - a film or play that tells a serious story with emotional themes
