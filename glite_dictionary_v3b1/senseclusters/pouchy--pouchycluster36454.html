pouchy

adjective
swollen or baggy
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˈpaʊtʃi / (AmE), / ˈpaʊtʃi / (BrE)
having pouches or appearing swollen or baggy, especially under the eyes

Examples

Pouchy eyes
After a long night of little sleep, his pouchy eyes were hard to hide.
The old dog had a pouchy face that made him look gentle and wise.
She tried to cover her pouchy under-eyes with makeup before the meeting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: baggy - loose and hanging in folds
  • synonym: swollen - enlarged or puffed up, often due to fluid
  • antonym: tight - closely fitted; not loose or baggy
