side issue

noun
a less important or secondary issue
2.0
Pronunciation: / saɪd ˈɪʃu / (AmE), / saɪd ˈɪsjuː / (BrE)
an issue or problem that is less important than the main subject being discussed or considered

Examples

A side issue arises
Although the budget was debated, the controversy over parking was only a side issue.
Her comments about the weather were just a side issue during the meeting.
We shouldn't get distracted by the side issue when the main problem remains unsolved.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: irrelevance - something not related or pertinent to the main subject
  • antonym: main issue - the most important problem or subject under discussion
