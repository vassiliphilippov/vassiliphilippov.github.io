prescription medicine

noun
drug available only by doctor's order
2.6
Pronunciation: / prəˈskrɪpʃən ˈmɛdəsɪn / (AmE), / prɪˈskrɪpʃən ˈmɛdɪsɪn / (BrE)
a drug that can only be obtained with a written order from a qualified medical practitioner
