belly dancer

noun
female performer of belly dance
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛli ˌdænsər / (AmE), / ˈbɛli ˌdɑːnsə / (BrE)
a woman who performs belly dance, a Middle Eastern dance style characterized by rhythmic, expressive movements of the torso and hips

Examples

Belly dancer performs
At the festival, a belly dancer performed to traditional Middle Eastern music.
She trained for years to become a professional belly dancer in Egypt.
The belly dancer captivated the audience with her graceful movements and sparkling costume.

Related Terms

  • synonym: belly dance - a Middle Eastern dance focusing on movements of the hips and torso
