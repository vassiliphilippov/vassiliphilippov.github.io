pyralid

noun
a tropical moth; crop pest
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪ.rə.lɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɪə.rə.lɪd / (BrE)
a slender-bodied, long-legged moth, usually from tropical regions, whose larvae are known as crop pests

Examples

A pyralid moth
Scientists are developing new methods to control pyralid infestations in tropical agriculture.
Several species of pyralid moths are known to damage stored grains.
The pyralid larvae devastated this year's corn crop across several regions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moth - an insect related to butterflies, many of which are nocturnal
