aftercooler

noun
air-cooling device
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈæftɚˌkuːlɚ / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəkˌuːlə / (BrE)
a device that cools air or gas after it has been compressed, often used in engines or compressors

Examples

An aftercooler device
The mechanic installed a new aftercooler to improve engine performance.
An aftercooler helps reduce the temperature of compressed air before it enters the engine.
Regular maintenance of the aftercooler is important for efficient operation of the compressor.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intercooler - a device that cools air between stages of compression
