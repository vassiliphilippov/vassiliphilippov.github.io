Limnodromus griseus

noun
a species of dowitcher (wading bird)
9.9
Pronunciation: / lɪm.noʊˈdroʊ.məs ˈɡraɪ.si.əs / (AmE), / lɪm.nəˈdrəʊ.məs ˈɡriː.si.əs / (BrE)
A species of dowitcher, a wading bird with a grey back, commonly known as the short-billed dowitcher, found in North America.
