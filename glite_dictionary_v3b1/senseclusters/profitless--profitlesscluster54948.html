profitless

adjective
not yielding profit or benefit
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑfɪtləs / (AmE), / ˈprɒfɪtləs / (BrE)
not producing any profit or benefit; unprofitable or useless.

Examples

A profitless debate
Hours of debate proved profitless, as no agreement was reached.
The company abandoned its profitless ventures to focus on more lucrative opportunities.
Their journey through the desert was long and profitless, yielding nothing of value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fruitless - failing to achieve the desired result; unproductive
  • synonym: unprofitable - not yielding profit or financial gain
