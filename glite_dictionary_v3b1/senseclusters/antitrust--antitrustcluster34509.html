antitrust

adjective
preventing unfair competition in business
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈtrʌst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈtrʌst / (BrE)
(of laws or regulations) intended to prevent unfair competition and promote fair business practices in trade and commerce.

Examples

Antitrust law
He specializes in antitrust law and advises international corporations.
New antitrust regulations aim to protect smaller businesses from unfair competition.
The government launched an antitrust investigation into the merging companies.

Related Terms

  • antonym: monopoly - exclusive control over the supply of a product or service
