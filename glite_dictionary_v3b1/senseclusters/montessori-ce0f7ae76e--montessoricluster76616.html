Montessori

noun
Montessori school
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːntəˈsɔːri / (AmE), / ˌmɒntəˈsɔːri / (BrE)
a school or class that uses the Montessori method, emphasizing self-directed activity and hands-on learning

Examples

A Montessori school
Their son attends a Montessori that encourages children to learn at their own pace.
Many parents choose a Montessori for its focus on independence and creativity.
The new Montessori in town quickly became popular among families seeking alternative education.

Related Terms

  • synonym: progressive school - a school using modern, student-centered teaching methods
  • antonym: traditional school - a school using conventional teaching methods
