depoliticization

noun
removal of politics
8.6
Pronunciation: / diˌpɑːlɪtɪsəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / diˌpɒlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something less political or removing political influence or control from it

Examples

Many experts argue that depoliticization of the judiciary is essential for fair legal decisions.
