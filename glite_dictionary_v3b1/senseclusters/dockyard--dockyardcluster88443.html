dockyard

noun
place where ships are built or repaired
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑkˌjɑrd / (AmE), / ˈdɒkˌjɑːd / (BrE)
an area or establishment by the water where ships are built, equipped, or repaired

Examples

The dockyard repairs ships
After the storm, several fishing boats were taken to the dockyard for repairs.
He found a job as a welder at the local dockyard.
The navy's largest dockyard employs hundreds of skilled workers.
