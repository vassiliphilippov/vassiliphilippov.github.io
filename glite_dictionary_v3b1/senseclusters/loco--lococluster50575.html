loco

noun
slang for crazy person
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈləʊkəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who acts in a crazy or eccentric way

Examples

A real loco
Everyone thought he was a bit of a loco at the party.
She acted like a total loco when she danced on the table.
People called him a loco because of his wild ideas.
