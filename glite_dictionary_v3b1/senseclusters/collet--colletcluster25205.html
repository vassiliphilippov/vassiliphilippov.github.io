collet

noun
metal band to prevent splitting of wood
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːlɪt / (AmE), / ˈkɒlɪt / (BrE)
a metal cap or band fitted around the end of a wooden pole or tool to prevent splitting

Examples

Tool collet
A collet should be installed at the end of the broom handle to stop it from cracking.
The craftsman secured the axe head with a brass collet for extra durability.
Without a collet, wooden stakes can easily split during use.
