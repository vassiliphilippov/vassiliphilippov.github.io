Martyrs of al-Aqsa

proper noun
Palestinian militant group active since 2000s
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑr.tɚz əv æl ˈɑːk.sə / (AmE), / ˈmɑː.tɪəz əv æl ˈæk.sə / (BrE)
a Palestinian militant organization, an offshoot of al-Fatah, known for armed operations primarily in the West Bank and for high-profile attacks in Israel, especially in the early 2000s
