caffre

noun
offensive term for Black Africans
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkæfɚ / (AmE), / ˈkæfə / (BrE)
An offensive and insulting term historically used to refer to Black Africans, especially in South Africa; now widely recognized as extremely offensive and unacceptable.
