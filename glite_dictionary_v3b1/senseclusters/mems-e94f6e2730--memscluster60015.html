MEMS

noun
micro engineering technology
Pronunciation: / mɛmz / (AmE), / mɛmz / (BrE)
the technology or tiny devices that combine mechanical and electrical components to sense, control, or interact with the environment

Examples

MEMS devices in phones
Many smartphones use MEMS sensors to detect movement and orientation.
Advances in MEMS have led to smaller and more efficient medical devices.
Engineers are developing new MEMS components for use in automotive safety systems.
Many modern cars use MEMS to deploy airbags in the event of a collision.
Smartphones rely on MEMS to detect changes in orientation and movement.
Engineers are developing new MEMS for medical devices and wearable technology.

Related Terms

  • synonym: actuator - a device that moves or controls a mechanism
  • synonym: microelectronics - the study and manufacture of very small electronic designs and components
  • synonym: nanotechnology - the science of manipulating materials on an atomic or molecular scale
  • synonym: sensor - a device that detects or measures physical properties
