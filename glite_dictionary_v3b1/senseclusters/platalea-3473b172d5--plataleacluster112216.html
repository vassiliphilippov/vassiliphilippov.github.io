Platalea

noun
genus of wading birds (spoonbills)
9.9
Pronunciation: /plə.ˈteɪ.liː.ə/ /; / /plæ.ˈteɪ.liː.ə/ (AmE), /plə.ˈteɪ.lɪ.ə/ /; / /plæ.ˈteɪ.lɪ.ə/ (BrE)
A genus of large, long-legged wading birds known as spoonbills, characterized by their distinctive spoon-shaped bills, and the type genus of the family Plataleidae.
