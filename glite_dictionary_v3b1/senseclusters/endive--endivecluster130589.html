endive

noun
leafy vegetable with frilled leaves
Pronunciation: / ˈɛnˌdaɪv, ˈɛndɪv /; / ˈɛn.daɪv / (AmE), / ˈɛndɪv, ɑ̃ˈdiːv /; / ˈɛndaɪv, ˈɑːndɪv / (BrE)
a leafy vegetable of the daisy family, with crisp or bitter leaves, often used in salads
