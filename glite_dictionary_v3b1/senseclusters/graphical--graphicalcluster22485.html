graphical

adjective
relating to or using graphs
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪkəl / (AmE), / ˈɡrafɪkəl / (BrE)
relating to or presented by means of a graph or graphs, especially in data presentation or statistical analysis.

Examples

Graphical chart
A graphical representation of the survey findings highlighted key trends.
Our software offers a graphical interface for visualizing complex data sets.
The results were presented in a graphical format, making them easier to understand.
