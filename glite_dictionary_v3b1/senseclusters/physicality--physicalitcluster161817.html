physicality

noun
embodied presence or tactile engagement
Pronunciation: / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti /; / ˌfɪzɪˈkæləti / (AmE), / ˌfɪzɪˈkælɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being physical, especially in terms of bodily presence, movement, or embodiment

Examples
