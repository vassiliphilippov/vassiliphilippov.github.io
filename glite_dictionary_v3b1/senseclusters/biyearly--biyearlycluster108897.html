biyearly

adjective, adverb
once every two years
Pronunciation: / baɪˈjɪərli /; / baɪˈjɪrli / (AmE), / baɪˈjɪəli / (BrE)
happening or done every two years; biennial (used as adjective or adverb)

Examples

Biyearly event schedule

The board meets biyearly to discuss the company's long-term strategy.
The committee holds its biyearly meeting in July.
The event is biyearly and draws experts from around the world.
We publish a biyearly report to track our progress.
