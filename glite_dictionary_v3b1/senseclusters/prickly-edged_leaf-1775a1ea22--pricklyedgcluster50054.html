prickly-edged leaf

noun
leaf with spiny margins
Pronunciation: / ˈprɪkli ɛdʒd lif / (AmE), / ˈprɪkli ɛdʒd liːf / (BrE)
a leaf with sharp or spiny margins along its edges

Examples

A prickly-edged leaf
Care should be taken when handling a prickly-edged leaf to avoid getting scratched.
The holly bush is known for its characteristic prickly-edged leaves, which deter many herbivores.
We studied the adaptation of the prickly-edged leaf in arid environments during biology class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: lobed leaf - a leaf with lobes or rounded projections
  • synonym: spiny leaf - a leaf with spines or stiff, sharp outgrowths
