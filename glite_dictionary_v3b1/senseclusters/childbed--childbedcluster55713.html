childbed

noun
period of labor and childbirth
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃaɪldˌbɛd / (AmE), / ˈtʃaɪldˌbɛd / (BrE)
the time when a woman is giving birth and the period immediately after childbirth

Examples

During her childbed, she was attended by a skilled midwife.

Related Terms

  • antonym: pregnancy - the state of carrying a developing fetus within the body
  • synonym: puerperium - the period following childbirth when the mother's body returns to normal
