California black oak

noun
large Pacific coast oak tree
7.8
Pronunciation: / kæləˈfɔrniə blæk oʊk / (AmE), / kælɪˈfɔːniə blæk əʊk / (BrE)
a large deciduous tree (Quercus kelloggii) native to the Pacific coast of North America, known for its deeply lobed, bristle-tipped leaves and dark bark
