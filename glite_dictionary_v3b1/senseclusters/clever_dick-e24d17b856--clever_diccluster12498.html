clever Dick

noun
someone who flaunts their cleverness
Pronunciation: / ˈklɛvɚ dɪk / (AmE), / ˈklɛvə dɪk / (BrE)
A person who shows off their intelligence in an annoying or boastful way.

Examples

A clever Dick
Don't be such a clever Dick; nobody likes a show-off.
Everyone got tired of Mark acting like a clever Dick during the meeting.
He's a real clever Dick, always correcting people even when it isn't necessary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: know-it-all - someone who acts as if they know everything
  • synonym: smart aleck - a person who is irritatingly clever or sarcastic
