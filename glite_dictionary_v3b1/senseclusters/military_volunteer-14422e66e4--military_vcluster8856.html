military volunteer

noun
person who enlists freely for military service
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪlɪˌtɛri ˌvɑlənˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˈmɪlɪtəri ˌvɒlənˈtɪə / (BrE)
A person who freely enlists for military service, rather than being conscripted.

Examples

Military volunteers join army
Many military volunteers joined the army at the start of the conflict.
The ceremony honored every military volunteer who served in recent missions.
Unlike conscripts, military volunteers choose to serve their country of their own free will.

Related Terms

  • antonym: conscript - a person who is compulsorily enlisted for military service
  • synonym: volunteer - a person who freely offers to do something
