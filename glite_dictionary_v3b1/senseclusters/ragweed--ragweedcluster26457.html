ragweed

noun
allergy-causing weed; toxic to cattle
5.2 5.0
Pronunciation: /ˈræg.wiːd/ (AmE), /ˈræg.wiːd/ (BrE)
a plant of the genus Ambrosia, common in North America and Europe, known for causing allergies (hay fever) and often considered a troublesome weed
