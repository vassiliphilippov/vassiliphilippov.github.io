Thar Desert

proper noun
desert region in India and Pakistan
6.1
Pronunciation: / θɑr ˈdɛzərt / (AmE), / θɑː ˈdɛzət / (BrE)
A large arid region to the east of the Indus River, located in northwestern India and southeastern Pakistan, known for its sandy terrain and sparse vegetation.

Examples

The Thar Desert region
Many traditional villages are scattered throughout the Thar Desert.
The Thar Desert is home to unique wildlife adapted to harsh conditions.
The climate of the Thar Desert is characterized by extreme temperatures and low rainfall.
