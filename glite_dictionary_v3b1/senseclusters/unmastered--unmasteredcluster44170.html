unmastered

adjective
not sonically finished
8.1
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmæstɚd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmɑːstəd / (BrE)
not having undergone the final mastering process in audio production; not sonically finished for release

Examples

Unmastered audio track
The band released an unmastered track to give fans a preview of their new album.
Listening to the unmastered version, you can hear subtle differences before the final mix.
Producers often compare the unmastered and mastered versions to check for improvements.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mastered - having completed the final audio mastering process
  • synonym: unfinished - not completed or finalized
