Examples

Artillery barrage

  • shelling
    8.3 2.32/m
    shelling is the firing of artillery shells across an area, while barrage is often planned to protect troops or saturate.
  • drumfire
    9.9 0.01/m
    drumfire is rapid, sustained artillery firing that sounds like a drum, while barrage focuses on wide, protective continuous bombardment.
  • bombardment
    7.0 3.20/m

  • cannonade
    9.9 0.03/m
    cannonade is a sustained attack by heavy cannons in older language, while barrage is modern continuous artillery fire for area effect.
  • artillery fire
    6.9 0.44/m
