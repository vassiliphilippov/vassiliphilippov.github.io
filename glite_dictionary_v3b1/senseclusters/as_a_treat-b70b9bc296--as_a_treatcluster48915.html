as a treat

adverb
as a special gift or reward
Pronunciation: / əz ə trit / (AmE), / əz ə triːt / (BrE)
used to indicate that something is given or done as a special gift or reward

Examples

As a treat for children
I let the children stay up late as a treat on Fridays.
She brought homemade cake to the office as a treat for her colleagues.
They took their mother to a fancy restaurant as a treat for her birthday.
