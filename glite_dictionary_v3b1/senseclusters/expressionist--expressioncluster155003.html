expressionist

adjective
of or relating to expressionism
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsprɛʃənɪst / (AmE), / ɪkˈsprɛʃənɪst / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of expressionism, an artistic style that emphasizes the depiction of emotions or subjective experiences rather than objective reality.

Examples

Expressionist painting style
Her work has an expressionist quality, focusing more on inner emotions than on realistic details.
Many expressionist artists sought to evoke moods through bold colors and dramatic brushstrokes.
The museum hosted an exhibition of expressionist paintings from the early 20th century.
