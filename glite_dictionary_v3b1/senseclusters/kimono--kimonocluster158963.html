kimono

noun
traditional Japanese robe
Pronunciation: / kɪˈmoʊnoʊ / (AmE), / kɪˈməʊnəʊ / (BrE)
a traditional Japanese long, loose robe with wide sleeves, tied with a sash, now also used for similar styled robes.

Examples

A silk kimono
His mother keeps her favorite kimono carefully folded in a wooden chest.
She wore a beautiful silk kimono at the festival.
The fashion designer drew inspiration from the traditional kimono for her latest collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: robe - a long, loose outer garment
