leprosy

noun
chronic infectious disease (Hansen's disease)
6.0 5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛprəsi / (AmE), / ˈlɛprəsi / (BrE)
A chronic infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium leprae, characterized by skin lesions, nerve damage, and muscle weakness.
