spermatogenic

adjective
producing sperm cells
Pronunciation: / ˌspɜːrmətəˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌspɜːmətəˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to or promoting the production of sperm cells

Examples

Spermatogenic hormones
The doctor explained that certain hormones have a spermatogenic effect on the testes.
Researchers are investigating drugs that could enhance spermatogenic activity in infertile men.
A deficiency in vitamins may negatively impact the spermatogenic process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spermatogenesis - the process of sperm cell development
  • antonym: sterile - unable to produce offspring
