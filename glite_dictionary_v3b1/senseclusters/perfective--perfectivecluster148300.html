perfective

noun
aspect expressing completed action
Pronunciation: / pərˈfɛktɪv / (AmE), / pəˈfɛktɪv / (BrE)
(Grammar) the aspect of a verb that shows an action or event has been completed

Examples

In Russian, verbs distinguish between the perfective and imperfective aspects.
Spanish uses the preterite tense to convey a perfective meaning.

She studied the perfective in her introduction to Slavic grammar.
The perfective is commonly contrasted with the imperfective aspect in many languages.
