minibike

noun
small, lightweight motorcycle
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪniˌbaɪk / (AmE), / ˈmɪniˌbaɪk / (BrE)
a small motorcycle with a low frame, small wheels, and elevated handlebars, typically used for recreation or by children

Examples

Riders must wear helmets when operating a minibike at the park.
The minibike zipped down the trail, kicking up a cloud of dust.
