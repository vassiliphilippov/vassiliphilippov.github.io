lens

verb
to film or photograph
7.0
Pronunciation: / lɛnz / (AmE), / lɛnz / (BrE)
to film or photograph something using a camera lens, especially in a professional or artistic way

Examples

To lens a film
The movie was beautifully lensed by an award-winning director of photography.
She lensed the entire wedding, capturing every special moment on camera.
Many nature documentaries are lensed in remote locations to show rare wildlife.
