age

noun
distinctive period in history
Pronunciation: / eɪdʒ / (AmE), / eɪdʒ / (BrE)
A period in history marked by a particular characteristic, event, or figure.

Examples

The Age of Discovery
Many believe that we are entering a new age of technology.
The Renaissance was an age of great artistic achievement.
We live in a litigious age when lawsuits are common.
