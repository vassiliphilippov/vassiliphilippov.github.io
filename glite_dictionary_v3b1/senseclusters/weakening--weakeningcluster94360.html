weakening

noun
act of making something weaker
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːkənɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwiːkənɪŋ / (BrE)
the act or process of becoming weaker or making something less strong

Examples

Weakening of support
Several factors contributed to the weakening of the country’s economy.
The constant rainfall resulted in a weakening of the bridge’s foundations.
There has been a noticeable weakening in her voice since the illness.
Doctors are monitoring the weakening of her immune system due to the illness.
The weakening of the bridge structure was caused by years of neglect.
There has been a noticeable weakening of the company's financial position this year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: strengthening - the act of making something stronger
