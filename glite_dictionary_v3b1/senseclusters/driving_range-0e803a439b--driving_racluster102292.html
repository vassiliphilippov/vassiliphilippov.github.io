driving range

noun
maximum travel distance for a vehicle
3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈdraɪ.vɪŋ reɪndʒ/ (AmE), /ˈdraɪ.vɪŋ reɪndʒ/ (BrE)
the maximum distance a vehicle can travel on a full tank of fuel or a full battery charge under typical conditions.

Examples

Car driving range
Before buying an EV, she compared the advertised driving range of several models.
On cold days, the EV's actual driving range can fall significantly below the advertised figure.
Long-distance drivers check different driving ranges for city and highway conditions before planning stops.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mileage - distance travelled by a vehicle, sometimes used to refer informally to how far a vehicle can go on a tank or charge.
  • synonym: range - the distance something (especially a vehicle) can travel without refuelling or recharging; often used for electric vehicles.
