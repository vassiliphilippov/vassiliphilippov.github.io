youngish

adjective
somewhat young
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjʌŋɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈjʌŋɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat young; fairly young but not very young.

Examples

A youngish person
He has a youngish energy despite being in his forties.
She looked youngish for her age, with hardly any wrinkles.
The club attracted a youngish crowd on most nights.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: middle-aged - in middle age; neither young nor old
  • degree lower: young - at an early stage of life; not old
