dashi

noun
Japanese soup stock
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːʃi / (AmE), / ˈdaʃi / (BrE)
a Japanese soup stock made from dried fish and kelp, used as a base in many Japanese dishes

Examples

Japanese dashi stock
The chef prepared miso soup using homemade dashi.
You need to make dashi first before adding other ingredients to the soup.
Authentic Japanese cuisine often starts with a base of dashi for rich flavor.
