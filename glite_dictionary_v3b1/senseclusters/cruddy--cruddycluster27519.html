cruddy

adjective
slightly unwell
8.2 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆkrÊŒdi / (AmE), / ËˆkrÊŒdi / (BrE)
feeling slightly ill, unwell, or generally in poor health

Examples

Feel cruddy today
I woke up feeling cruddy after staying up too late last night.
He called in sick because he was still feeling cruddy from the cold.
After eating the questionable seafood, she felt a bit cruddy all afternoon.
