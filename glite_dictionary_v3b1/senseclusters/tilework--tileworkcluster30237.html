tilework

noun
decorative tiled surface
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪlˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈtaɪlˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a surface or object decorated or covered with tiles, often forming a pattern or design for architectural or ornamental purposes

Examples

Decorative tilework
The palace entrance was adorned with beautiful tilework in blue and white patterns.
Restorers carefully cleaned the ancient tilework to reveal its original colors.
Modern architects often incorporate tilework to add texture and color to their designs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mosaic - a picture or pattern made by arranging small pieces of colored stone, glass, or tile
  • antonym: plasterwork - decorative work made from plaster on walls or ceilings
