how's the weather

x
informal question about current weather conditions
4.5
Pronunciation: / haʊz ðə ˈwɛðɚ / (AmE), / haʊz ðə ˈwɛðə / (BrE)
An informal question used to ask about the current weather conditions in a particular place.

Examples

Ask how's the weather
Every morning, she checks in and asks, how's the weather in your city?
He texted me just to ask, how's the weather over there today?
When you travel, people often want to know how's the weather wherever you are.

Related Terms

  • synonym: weather report - information or forecast about the weather
  • synonym: what's the weather like - another phrase asking about the current weather
