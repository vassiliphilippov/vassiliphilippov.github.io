warning light

noun
light indicating danger or caution
2.7 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwɔrnɪŋ laɪt / (AmE), / ˈwɔːnɪŋ laɪt / (BrE)
a light that turns on to signal danger, a malfunction, or the need for caution, especially on a vehicle or equipment

Examples

Red warning light
A red warning light illuminated on the instrument panel, alerting the pilot to a possible system failure.
If the temperature warning light comes on, you should stop the car immediately.
The check engine warning light flashed on as I was driving to work.
