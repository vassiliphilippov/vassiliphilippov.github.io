prest

noun
loan or advance
9.9
Pronunciation: /prest/ (AmE), /prest/ (BrE)
an archaic term for a loan or advance of money, especially from a government or authority

Examples

A royal prest

Sailors were often given a prest before setting out on long journeys.
Records show that a prest was granted to the builder for the cathedral's construction.

Related Terms
