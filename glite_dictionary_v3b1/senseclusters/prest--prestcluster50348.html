prest

noun
loan or advance
9.9
Pronunciation: / prɛst / (AmE), / prɛst / (BrE)
an archaic term for a loan or advance of money, especially from a government or authority

Examples

A royal prest
The merchant received a prest from the crown to fund his voyage.
Sailors were often given a prest before setting out on long journeys.
Records show that a prest was granted to the builder for the cathedral's construction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advance - money paid before it is due or before work is completed
  • antonym: gift - something given voluntarily without payment
  • synonym: loan - an amount of money that is borrowed and expected to be paid back
