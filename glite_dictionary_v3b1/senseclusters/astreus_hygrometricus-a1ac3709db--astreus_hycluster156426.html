Astreus hygrometricus

noun
species of earthstar fungus
Pronunciation: / əˈstrius haɪˌɡroʊməˈtrɪkəs / (AmE), / əˈstriːəs haɪˌɡrəʊməˈtrɪkəs / (BrE)
a species of earthstar fungus widely found in sandy soils, recognized by its pale tan spore mass (gleba) and star-shaped fruiting body.
