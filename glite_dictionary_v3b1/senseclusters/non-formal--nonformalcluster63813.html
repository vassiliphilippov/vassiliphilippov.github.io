non-formal

adjective
outside formal education
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈfɔrməl / (AmE), / nɒnˈfɔːməl / (BrE)
relating to organized and intentional education or training that occurs outside the formal school system.

Examples

Non-formal education
The organization provides non-formal education programs for adults who did not finish school.
Many skills are learned through non-formal training outside traditional classrooms.
Volunteers attended a non-formal workshop to improve their teaching techniques.

Related Terms

  • antonym: formal - following official or established rules or systems, especially in education
  • synonym: informal - not following official or established rules or systems
