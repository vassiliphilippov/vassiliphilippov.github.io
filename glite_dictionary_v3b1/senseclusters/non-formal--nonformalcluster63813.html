non-formal

adjective
outside formal education
4.4
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈfɔːr.məl/ /; / /nə.ˈfɔːr.məl/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈfɔː.məl/ /; / /nə.ˈfɔː.məl/ (BrE)
relating to organized and intentional education or training that occurs outside the formal school system.

Examples

Non-formal education

Many skills are learned through non-formal training outside traditional classrooms.
Volunteers attended a non-formal workshop to improve their teaching techniques.
