fireback

noun
Southeast Asian pheasant
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪərˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfaɪəˌbæk / (BrE)
a pheasant of the genus Lophura, native to Southeast Asian forests, known for the male's bright plumage

Examples

A fireback pheasant
The male fireback displayed its colorful feathers to attract a mate in the dense forest.
Birdwatchers traveled to Malaysia hoping to spot a rare fireback in its natural habitat.
A photograph of a fireback was featured in the wildlife magazine's special on Southeast Asian birds.
