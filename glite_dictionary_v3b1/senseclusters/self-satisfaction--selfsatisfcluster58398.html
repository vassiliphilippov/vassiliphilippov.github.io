self-satisfaction

noun
pleasure or contentment with oneself
1.0 3.9
Pronunciation: /self.sæt.ɪs.ˈfæk.ʃən/ (AmE), /self.sæt.ɪs.ˈfæk.ʃən/ (BrE)
A feeling of being pleased with oneself or one's own achievements, often without critical awareness of one's faults or shortcomings.

Examples

Self-satisfaction feeling
After winning the award, a look of self-satisfaction spread across his face.
Her self-satisfaction prevented her from seeing the need for improvement.
There is a fine line between self-satisfaction and arrogance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complacency - a feeling of smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself
  • antonym: humility - a modest or low view of one's own importance
