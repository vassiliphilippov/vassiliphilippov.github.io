prosociality

noun
the tendency to act for others' benefit
8.2 7.3
Pronunciation: /proʊ.soʊ.ʃə.ˈlɪ.tiː/ /; / /prə.soʊ.ʃə.ˈlɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /prəʊ.səʊ.ʃə.ˈlɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
