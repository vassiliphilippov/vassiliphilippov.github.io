indicatively

adverb
in a showing way
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdɪkətɪvli / (AmE), / ɪnˈdɪkətɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows, points out, or suggests something clearly

Examples

Nods indicatively
She nodded indicatively toward the empty chair, inviting him to sit down.
The teacher pointed indicatively at the map to show the location of the city.
He raised his hand indicatively to signal that he had a question.

Related Terms

  • synonym: demonstratively - in a way that shows feelings or intentions openly
  • synonym: suggestively - in a way that hints at or implies something
