mamacita

interjection
admiring exclamation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌmɑːməˈsiːtə / (AmE), / ˌmaməˈsiːtə / (BrE)
an exclamation used to express strong attraction, admiration, or excitement toward a woman, often in a playful or flirtatious way

Examples

Mamacita! So beautiful
When the actress walked by, someone in the crowd shouted mamacita with excitement.
He saw her new dress and exclaimed, 'mamacita! You look amazing today.'
At the party, his friends teased him by calling out 'mamacita' whenever he blushed.
