pinto

adjective
multicolored patches
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪnˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɪntəʊ / (BrE)
having irregular patches of two or more colors, especially on the coat of an animal such as a horse

Examples

Pinto pony coat
The rancher admired the pinto pony grazing in the field.
She chose a pinto calf for the county fair because of its unique markings.
A pinto horse stood out among the herd of solid-colored animals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: solid - having a single, uniform color
  • synonym: spotted - having spots or patches of color
