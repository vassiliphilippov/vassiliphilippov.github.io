fire in the belly

noun
strong motivation or passionate ambition
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfaɪɚ ɪn ðə ˈbɛli / (AmE), / ˈfaɪə ɪn ðə ˈbɛli / (BrE)
a strong feeling of determination, passion, or ambition that motivates someone to achieve or pursue something

Examples

Fire in the belly for success
Her fire in the belly was evident in the way she tackled every challenge.
The coach inspired the players, giving them the fire in the belly they needed to win.
You can see the fire in the belly of anyone who truly believes in their cause.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apathy - lack of interest or motivation
  • synonym: drive - determination to achieve things
  • synonym: passion - strong and barely controllable emotion or motivation
