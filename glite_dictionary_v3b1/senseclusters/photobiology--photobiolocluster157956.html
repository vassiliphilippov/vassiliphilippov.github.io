photobiology

noun
study of light effects
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˌfoʊtoʊbaɪˈɑlədʒi / (AmE), / ˌfəʊtəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi / (BrE)

Examples

Photobiology research
Researchers in photobiology investigate how sunlight influences plant growth and animal behavior.
Advances in photobiology have led to new treatments for certain skin conditions.
The university offers a course in photobiology for students interested in light and life sciences.
