boronia

noun
fragrant floral oil
9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈroʊniə / (AmE), / bəˈrəʊniə / (BrE)
a fragrant essential oil or perfume note derived from the flowers of plants in the Boronia genus

Examples

Boronia perfume note
The perfumer added a few drops of boronia to give the scent a sweet, floral note.
She prefers perfumes with a hint of boronia for their unique and delicate aroma.
The essential oil of boronia is prized for its rich, fruity fragrance in luxury perfumes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jasmine - a fragrant oil or scent from jasmine flowers
  • synonym: rose oil - a fragrant essential oil extracted from rose petals
