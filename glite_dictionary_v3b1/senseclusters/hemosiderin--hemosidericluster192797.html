hemosiderin

noun
iron-containing pigment from hemoglobin breakdown
6.9 9.3
Pronunciation: / ˌhiːmoʊˈsɪdərɪn / (AmE), / ˌhiːməʊˈsɪdərɪn / (BrE)
a granular brown pigment consisting of iron oxide, formed from the breakdown of hemoglobin and found in tissues, especially in cases of excess iron storage or bleeding
