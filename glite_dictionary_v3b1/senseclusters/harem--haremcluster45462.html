harem

noun
part of a house for women (in Muslim households)
8.2 1.95/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…õr…ôm / (AmE), / Ààh…ëÀêr…õm / (BrE)
The part of a Muslim household reserved for wives, concubines, and female relatives.

Examples

The harem quarters
Guides explained how the women lived secluded lives within the harem.
She wrote her thesis on the roles of women in the Ottoman harem.
The palace harem was strictly off-limits to all men except the sultan.

Synonyms

  • hareem
    9.9 0.02/m
    hareem is a traditionally secluded, private area where a household's women live, while harem names the section for wives and female relatives.
  • harim
    9.9 0.02/m
    harim is the household area kept off-limits to outside men and used by family women, while harem refers to the part for wives and female relatives.
