bunker

verb
refuel a ship with coal or oil
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌŋkɚ / (AmE), / ˈbʌŋkə / (BrE)
to fill a ship's fuel compartment (bunker) with coal or oil; to refuel a ship.

Examples

To bunker a ship
All vessels must bunker in port to ensure they have sufficient fuel.
The crew began to bunker the ship before the long voyage.
They bunkered with oil at the last stop to prepare for crossing the Atlantic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: refuel - to supply fuel to a vehicle, aircraft, ship, etc.
