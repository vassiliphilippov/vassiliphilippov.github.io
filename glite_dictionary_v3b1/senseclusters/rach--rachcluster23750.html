rach

noun
central plant stem
9.9
Pronunciation: / ræk / (AmE), / ræk / (BrE)
the main axis or central stem of a compound leaf or an inflorescence in plants

Examples

Fern rach
The rach of the fern supported several pairs of delicate leaflets.
Botanists examined the rach to determine the health of the plant.
Each flower was attached to the rach by a short stalk.
