Titoist

adjective
of Tito's policies
Pronunciation: / ˈtiːtoʊɪst / (AmE), / ˈtiːtəʊɪst / (BrE)
relating to Josip Broz Tito, his policies, or the political movement associated with him, especially in Yugoslavia.

Examples

Titoist policy
The country's Titoist approach set it apart from other Eastern European states during the Cold War.
Many historians study the unique features of Titoist socialism in Yugoslavia.
The leader's Titoist stance promoted independence from Soviet influence.
