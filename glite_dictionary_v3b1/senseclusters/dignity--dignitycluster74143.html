dignity

noun
high rank or official position
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪɡ.nə.t̬i / (AmE), / ˈdɪɡ.nɪ.ti / (BrE)
A high rank, position, or title; an office or station of honor or authority.

Examples

A royal dignity
He ascended to a position of great dignity in the royal court.
The ambassadors carried themselves with the dignity of their high office.
The ceremony was attended by several men of dignity and influence.
