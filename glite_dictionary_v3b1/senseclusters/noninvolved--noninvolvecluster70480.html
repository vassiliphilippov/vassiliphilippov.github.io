noninvolved

adjective
emotionally detached
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnɪnˈvɑlvd / (AmE), / ˌnɒnɪnˈvɒlvd / (BrE)
not emotionally engaged, connected, or showing personal attachment; detached or uninvolved.

Examples

Noninvolved attitude
The therapist remained noninvolved to ensure an unbiased perspective during the session.
As a judge, she must stay noninvolved with the parties in the case.
He kept a noninvolved attitude during the argument, refusing to take sides.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detached - not emotionally involved or connected
  • antonym: engaged - emotionally involved or connected
  • synonym: uninvolved - not taking part or not emotionally connected
