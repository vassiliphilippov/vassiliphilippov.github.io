inspirator

noun
air-drawing device
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈspaɪreɪtər / (AmE), / ɪnˈspaɪreɪtə / (BrE)
a device that draws in air or gas, often to mix it with another substance in engineering or medical applications

Examples

Air inspirator device
The technician replaced the faulty inspirator to ensure the machine mixed air and gas correctly.
In some medical devices, an inspirator helps deliver oxygen to patients efficiently.
The engine's performance improved after installing a new inspirator to regulate airflow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: injector - a device that introduces fluid or gas into a system
  • synonym: venturi - a tube or device that uses a constriction to draw in air or fluid
