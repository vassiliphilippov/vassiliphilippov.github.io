Melampsora

noun
genus of rust fungi
Pronunciation: / mɛˌlæmpˈsɔrə /; / məˌlæmpˈsɔrə / (AmE), / mɛˌlæmpˈsɔːrə /; / mɪˌlæmpˈsɔːrə / (BrE)
a genus of rust fungi with sessile, one-celled teliospores arranged in a single layer, often found on plants
