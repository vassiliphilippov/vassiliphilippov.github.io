lachrymation

noun
production or shedding of tears
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌlæk.rɪˈmeɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌlæk.rɪˈmeɪ.ʃ(ə)n / (BrE)
The production or shedding of tears, especially in excess or as a medical symptom.

Examples

Excessive lachrymation
Allergic reactions often cause excessive lachrymation and nasal discharge.
Exposure to strong fumes instantly triggered lachrymation in everyone present.
The doctor noted persistent lachrymation as a symptom of conjunctivitis.

Related Terms

  • antonym: dry eye - a condition where there is a lack of tear production
  • synonym: tearing - the act of producing tears
