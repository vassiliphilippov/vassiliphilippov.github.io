weigh down

verb
make heavy or press down
1.7 3.0
Pronunciation: /weɪ daʊn/ (AmE), /weɪ daʊn/ (BrE)
to make something heavier or cause it to bend or sink by placing weight on it

Examples

To weigh down the shelf
Heavy books can weigh down the shelves over time.
She felt her backpack weighing down her as she walked up the hill.
The thick snow began to weigh down the tree branches dangerously.
