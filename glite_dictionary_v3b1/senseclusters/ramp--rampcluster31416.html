ramp

verb
to increase gradually
4.5
Pronunciation: / ræmp / (AmE), / ræmp / (BrE)
to increase or make something greater, especially gradually or in stages

Examples

To ramp up production
The company plans to ramp up production next year to meet higher demand.
Governments often ramp spending during times of economic crisis.
They decided to ramp their efforts after seeing early success.
