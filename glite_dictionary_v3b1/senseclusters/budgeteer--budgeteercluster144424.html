budgeteer

verb
to manage a budget
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌbʌdʒəˈtɪr / (AmE), / ˌbʌdʒɪˈtɪə / (BrE)
to plan, prepare, or manage a budget, especially by allocating funds for a project or organization

Examples

To budgeteer a project
The committee asked her to budgeteer the annual conference to avoid unnecessary expenses.
He learned how to budgeteer effectively while working for a nonprofit organization.
They hired a consultant to budgeteer the construction project from start to finish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: budget - to plan or control the amount of money to be spent
  • antonym: overspend - to spend more money than planned or allowed
