saman

noun
large tropical American leguminous tree
Pronunciation: / sɑːˈmɑn / (AmE), / səˈmɑːn / (BrE)
A large tropical American tree (Samanea saman) with wide-spreading branches, bipinnate leaves, globular clusters of flowers with crimson stamens, and seed pods eaten by cattle.

Examples

A saman tree

The impressive canopy of the saman made it a favorite resting spot.
