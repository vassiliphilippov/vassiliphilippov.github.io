skoal

verb
to make a toast
9.9
Pronunciation: / skoʊl / (AmE), / skəʊl / (BrE)
to make a toast by raising a glass and drinking in celebration or as an expression of good wishes

Examples

To skoal the couple
Everyone gathered around the table to skoal the newlyweds and wish them happiness.
At midnight, we all skoaled to welcome the new year with laughter and hope.
He raised his glass and shouted 'Skoal!' before taking a sip of wine.
