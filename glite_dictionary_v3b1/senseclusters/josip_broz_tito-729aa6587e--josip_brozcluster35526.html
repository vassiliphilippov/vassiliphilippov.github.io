Josip Broz Tito

proper noun
Yugoslav leader and statesman (1892–1980)
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈjoʊsɪp ˈbroʊz ˈtiːtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈjɒsɪp ˈbrɒz ˈtiːtəʊ / (BrE)
(1892–1980) Yugoslav statesman and military leader who led the resistance against German occupation during World War II and later established and ruled a communist state in Yugoslavia.
