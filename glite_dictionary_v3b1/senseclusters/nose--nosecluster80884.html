nose

noun
a very small distance (race margin)
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
a very small distance, especially the margin by which a race is won or lost

Examples

Win by a nose
Our team missed first place by just a nose this year.
She edged out her opponent by a nose in the spelling bee.
The runner won the competition by a nose after a thrilling finish.

  • synonym: hair's breadth - an extremely small amount or margin
