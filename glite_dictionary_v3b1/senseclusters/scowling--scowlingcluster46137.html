scowling

adjective
having a sullen or unfriendly look
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊ.lɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskaʊ.lɪŋ / (BrE)
Having a sullen or unfriendly expression; showing displeasure or anger by frowning.

Examples

Scowling face
A scowling guard stood at the entrance, watching everyone who passed by.
Her scowling face made it clear she was not pleased with the decision.
The teacher gave a scowling look that silenced the noisy students.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frowning - having a facial expression showing displeasure or concentration
  • antonym: smiling - having a happy or friendly facial expression
