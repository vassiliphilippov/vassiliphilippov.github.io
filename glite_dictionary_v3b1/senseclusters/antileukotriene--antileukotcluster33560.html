antileukotriene

adjective
blocking leukotrienes
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˌluːkəˈtraɪiːn / (AmE), / ˌæntiˌluːkəˈtraɪiːn / (BrE)
acting to block or inhibit the effects of leukotrienes, especially to reduce inflammation in asthma and allergies
