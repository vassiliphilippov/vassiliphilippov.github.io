unshy

adjective
confident, not timid
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnˈʃaɪ / (AmE), / ˌʌnˈʃaɪ / (BrE)
not shy; confident and willing to interact with others without hesitation

Examples

An unshy child
The unshy child greeted everyone at the party with a big smile.
Her unshy attitude helped her make friends quickly at her new school.
He was so unshy that he volunteered to speak first in front of the class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confident - sure of oneself; not shy or nervous
  • synonym: outgoing - friendly and socially confident
  • antonym: shy - nervous or timid in the company of others
