South Dravidian

noun
subgroup of Dravidian languages in southern India
Pronunciation: / saʊθ drəˈvɪdiən / (AmE), / saʊθ drəˈvɪdiən / (BrE)
a subgroup of Dravidian languages spoken primarily in southern India, including languages such as Kannada, Malayalam, Tamil, and Tulu

Examples

South Dravidian languages
Kannada is the most widely spoken South Dravidian language.
Many South Dravidian languages share similar grammatical structures and vocabulary.
