murmurous

adjective
making soft, murmuring sounds
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈmɝːmərəs / (AmE), / ˈmɜːmərəs / (BrE)
filled with or characterized by soft, low, continuous sounds; murmuring or gently rustling.

Examples

A murmurous breeze
A murmurous breeze drifted through the trees on that quiet summer night.
The murmurous conversation in the café created a pleasant background while she read her book.
We walked along the murmurous stream, listening to its gentle flow.
