seeded

adjective
arranged by ranking in a tournament draw
5.7 4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈsiː.dɪd/ /; / /ˈsiː.dəd/ (AmE), /ˈsiː.dɪd/ /; / /ˈsiː.dəd/ (BrE)
(of a competitor in a tournament) given a place in the draw according to ranking, so as to avoid meeting other top competitors in the early rounds.

Examples

Seeded player
All seeded teams received byes into the second round.
She was seeded second in the international tennis tournament.
The top seeded players won't face each other until the semifinals.

Related Terms

  • antonym: unseeded - not ranked or assigned a preferential position in a tournament
