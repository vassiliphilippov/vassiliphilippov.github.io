pot of gold

noun
elusive reward or treasure
Pronunciation: / pɑt əv ɡoʊld / (AmE), / pɒt əv ɡəʊld / (BrE)
a reward or treasure, especially one that is hoped for but hard to attain

Examples

A pot of gold
After years of hard work, she finally found her pot of gold when her invention succeeded.
He realized there was no easy pot of gold waiting at the end of the project.
Many people chase after the latest fad, thinking it will lead them to a pot of gold.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jackpot - a large financial prize or reward, especially from gambling
  • synonym: windfall - an unexpected gain or piece of good fortune
