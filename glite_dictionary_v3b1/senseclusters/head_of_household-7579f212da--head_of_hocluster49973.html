head of household

noun
person managing a household/family
1.0
Pronunciation: / hɛd əv ˈhaʊsˌhoʊld / (AmE), / hɛd əv ˈhaʊshəʊld / (BrE)
the person who is recognized as having primary responsibility for managing a household or family

Examples

The head of household role
She was recognized as the head of household after her husband passed away.
Traditionally, the eldest male was considered the head of household, but this is changing.
When filling out tax forms, you must indicate if you are the head of household.

Related Terms

  • synonym: householder - a person who owns or occupies a house
  • antonym: member of household - any person belonging to a household but not in charge
