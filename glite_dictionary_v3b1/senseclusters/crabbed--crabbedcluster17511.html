crabbed

adjective
irritable or bad-tempered
Pronunciation: / ˈkræbɪd, ˈkræbd / (AmE), / ˈkræbɪd, ˈkræbd / (BrE)
(of a person or their manner) annoyed and irritable; bad-tempered.

Examples

Crabbed reply
He answered in a crabbed tone that made everyone uncomfortable.
My crabbed neighbor yelled at the kids for playing too loudly in the street.
She became quite crabbed whenever she was interrupted during her work.

Related Terms

  • antonym: good-natured - having a pleasant and cheerful disposition
  • synonym: irritable - easily annoyed or angered
