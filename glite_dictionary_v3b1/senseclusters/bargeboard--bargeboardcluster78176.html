bargeboard

noun
side board of barge
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑrdʒˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈbɑːdʒˌbɔːd / (BrE)
a board forming part of the side or gunwale of a barge or other flatboat, used in nautical contexts

Examples

Boat bargeboard
The carpenter replaced the damaged bargeboard before the vessel set sail.
Years of exposure to water had weakened the old bargeboard on the flatboat.
Sailors checked each bargeboard to ensure the barge was ready for the journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gunwale - the upper edge of the side of a boat
  • synonym: plank - a long, flat piece of timber used in building boats
