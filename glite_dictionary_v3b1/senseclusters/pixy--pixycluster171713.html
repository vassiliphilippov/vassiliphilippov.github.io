pixy

noun
mischievous fairy in folklore
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪksi / (AmE), / ˈpɪksi / (BrE)
In folklore, a small mischievous fairy or sprite, especially one from Devon and Cornwall in England.

Examples

A mischievous pixy
According to legend, a pixy can lead travelers astray on misty nights.
The children believed a pixy had hidden their toys in the garden.
The old tales warn of the tricks a mischievous pixy might play on farmers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fairy - a small imaginary being of human form with magical powers
  • synonym: imp - a small, mischievous supernatural being
