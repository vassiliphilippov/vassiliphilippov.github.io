cap opener

noun
tool for removing bottle caps
Pronunciation: / ˈkæp ˌoʊpənər / (AmE), / ˈkæp ˌəʊpənə / (BrE)
A tool used for prying off the caps of bottles, especially metal bottle caps.

Examples

A cap opener tool
He reached for the cap opener to remove the stubborn bottle cap.
Please hand me the cap opener; I can't open this soda by hand.
The picnic basket included a cap opener for the glass bottles.
