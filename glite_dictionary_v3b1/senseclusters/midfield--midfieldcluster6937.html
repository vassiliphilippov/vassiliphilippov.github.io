midfield

noun
middle area of a playing field
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌfild / (AmE), / ˈmɪd.fiːld / (BrE)
the central part of a playing field, especially in sports such as football or lacrosse

Examples

The midfield area
He dominated the midfield and controlled the pace of the game.
Several players crowded the midfield, making it hard to advance.
The ball was passed quickly through the midfield to the strikers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center circle - the circle at the center of a football (soccer) pitch
