hexed

adjective
affected by a spell or bad luck
Pronunciation: / hɛkst / (AmE), / hɛkst / (BrE)
(informal) Under a spell or believed to be experiencing bad luck as if cursed; causing or accompanied by misfortune.

Examples

To be hexed by magic
Ever since she dropped that mirror, she has felt completely hexed.
Some people believe the old house is hexed because of its troubled history.
The team claimed they were hexed after losing every close game this season.

  • synonym: jinxed - affected by bad luck; unlucky
