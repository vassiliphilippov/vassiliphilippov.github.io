welk

noun
pustule or sea snail
9.9
Pronunciation: / wɛlk / (AmE), / wɛlk / (BrE)
an old word for a small blister or pustule, or in some dialects, a type of sea snail

Examples

A welk on skin
The fisherman collected a welk from the rocky shore.
In old medical texts, a welk was used to describe a skin blister.
She found a small welk on her hand after working in the garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pustule - a small inflamed elevation of the skin containing pus
  • synonym: whelk - a type of marine snail with a spiral shell
