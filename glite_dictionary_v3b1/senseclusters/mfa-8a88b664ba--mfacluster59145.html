MFA

noun
Master of Fine Arts degree
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˌɛm ˌɛf ˈeɪ / (AmE), / ˌɛm ˌef ˈɑː / (BrE)
an advanced university degree in fine arts, typically awarded after completing a postgraduate program in creative or performing arts

Examples

MFA degree in art
His MFA thesis exhibition attracted local art critics and collectors.
Many creative writing instructors hold an MFA in their field.
She earned her MFA in painting from a prestigious art school.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: PhD - Doctor of Philosophy, an advanced academic degree
