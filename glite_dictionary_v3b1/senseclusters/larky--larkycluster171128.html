larky

adjective
playful or fun-loving
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlɑːrki / (AmE), / ˈlɑːki / (BrE)
playful, full of fun, or inclined to joke around

Examples

A larky mood
The children were in a larky mood, laughing and chasing each other around the yard.
He always gets a bit larky after hearing good news.
Their larky behavior made the meeting much more enjoyable for everyone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mischievous - enjoying playing tricks or causing harmless trouble
  • antonym: serious - not playful or joking; thoughtful and earnest
