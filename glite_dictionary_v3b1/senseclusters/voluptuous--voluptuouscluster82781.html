voluptuous

adjective
attractively curvaceous (of a woman's body)
Pronunciation: / vəˈlʌptʃuəs / (AmE), / vəˈlʌptjʊəs / (BrE)
(of a woman's body) attractively curvy and full-figured, often suggesting sensual or sexual appeal

Examples

Voluptuous figure
Artists often depict goddesses as voluptuous, celebrating the beauty of the female form.
She wore a dress that accentuated her voluptuous curves at the party.
The actress was praised for her voluptuous figure and classic Hollywood glamour.
She was a voluptuous woman, with curves that drew everyone's gaze at the party.
The actress is famous for her voluptuous figure and confident demeanor on screen.
The artist preferred to paint voluptuous models, celebrating the beauty of the human body.

Related Terms

  • antonym: chaste - not having sexual nature or intention
  • synonym: curvaceous - (of a woman) having an attractively curved body
  • synonym: seductive - sexually attractive and likely to seduce or attract attention
  • synonym: sensual - giving or expressing physical, especially sexual, pleasure
