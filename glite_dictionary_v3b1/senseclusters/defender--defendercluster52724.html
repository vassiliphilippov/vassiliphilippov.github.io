defender

noun
person who protects
1.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛndər /; / dɪˈfɛndɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛndə / (BrE)
a person who protects or stands up for someone or something

Examples

A defender of rights
As a defender of the weak, he gained great respect in his community.
She acted as a defender of animal rights, always speaking out against cruelty.
The castle needed a defender to protect it from invaders.
She was praised as a brave defender of civil rights during the protest.
The city relied on every defender to hold the walls against the invading army.
The goalkeeper and the last defender worked together to stop the opposing team from scoring.
