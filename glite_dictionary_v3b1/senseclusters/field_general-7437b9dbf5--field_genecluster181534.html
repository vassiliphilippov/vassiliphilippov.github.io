field general

noun
quarterback or leader in American football
Pronunciation: / ˈfiːld ˌdʒɛnɚəl /; / ˈfiːld ˌdʒɛnərəl / (AmE), / ˈfiːld ˌdʒɛnərəl / (BrE)
(in American football) a player, especially the quarterback, who leads and directs the team's play on the field; sometimes used figuratively for someone with tactical leadership
