nonteaching

noun
school support staff
3.8
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈtiː.tʃɪŋ/ /; / /nə.ˈtiː.tʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈtiː.tʃɪŋ/ /; / /nə.ˈtiː.tʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
a person working at a school or educational institution who does not have teaching duties, such as administrative or support staff

Examples

A nonteaching employee
The nonteaching staff organized the school event while the teachers supervised the students.
Many nonteaching employees work in the library, cafeteria, or office of a school.
During the holidays, only nonteaching personnel were present to handle administrative tasks.
