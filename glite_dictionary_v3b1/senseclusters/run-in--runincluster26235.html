run-in

noun
an angry dispute
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌn ɪn / (AmE), / ˈrʌn ɪn / (BrE)
an angry argument or dispute with someone

Examples

Several run-ins with neighbors
After several run-ins with the neighbors, they decided to move.
He had a run-in with his boss over the new company policy.
Their last run-in ended with both of them refusing to speak for days.

Related Terms

  • synonym: argument - a disagreement, often angry or heated
  • synonym: quarrel - an angry argument or disagreement
