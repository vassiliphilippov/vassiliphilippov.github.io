unglazed

adjective
not having a shiny coating
6.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡleɪzd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡleɪzd / (BrE)
(of a surface or object) not having a shiny or glassy coating; matte or unfinished.

Examples

Unglazed pottery surface
The artist preferred using unglazed pottery for its natural, rustic appearance.
These cookies are delicious even when they are unglazed and simple.
We chose unglazed tiles to create a softer look in the kitchen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glazed - having a shiny or glossy coating
