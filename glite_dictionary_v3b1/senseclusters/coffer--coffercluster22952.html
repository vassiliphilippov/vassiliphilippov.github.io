coffer

noun
ornamental sunken ceiling panel
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːfər / (AmE), / ˈkɒfə / (BrE)
an ornamental recessed panel in a ceiling or dome, used especially in architecture for decorative effect

Examples

Ceiling coffer
