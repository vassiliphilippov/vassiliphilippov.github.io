weight

noun
relative importance or influence
1.0
Pronunciation: / weɪt / (AmE), / weɪt / (BrE)
importance or influence given to something; the degree to which something is considered significant or influential

Examples

Weight of opinion
Her words carry a lot of weight in the boardroom discussions.
In making his decision, the judge considered the weight of the evidence.
The committee gave more weight to public opinion than to expert advice.

Related Terms

  • antonym: insignificance - the lack of importance or influence
  • synonym: significance - the quality of being worthy of attention; importance
