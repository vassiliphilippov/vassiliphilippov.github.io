mistreatment

noun
bad or unfair treatment
5.4
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈtriːtmənt / (AmE), / mɪsˈtriːtmənt / (BrE)
the act or practice of treating someone or something badly or unfairly

Examples

Child mistreatment
Laws are in place to prevent the mistreatment of vulnerable children.
She suffered years of mistreatment at the hands of her employer.
The investigation revealed shocking mistreatment of animals at the facility.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abuse - cruel or violent treatment of someone
  • antonym: care - the process of protecting or looking after someone or something
  • synonym: maltreatment - cruel or inhumane treatment
