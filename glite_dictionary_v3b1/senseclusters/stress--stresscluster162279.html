stress

noun
mental or emotional strain
1.0 2.4
Pronunciation: /stres/ (AmE), /stres/ (BrE)
a state of mental or emotional strain or tension caused by difficult or demanding situations

Examples

Work stress
After the accident, she found it difficult to cope with the ongoing stress in her life.
Many people experience high levels of stress at work during busy periods.
Yoga and meditation can help manage stress and improve mental well-being.
Continuous exposure to high levels of stress can affect your physical health.
He found it difficult to manage the stress of his new job.
Therapists teach techniques to cope with stress and reduce anxiety.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anxiety - a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease
  • antonym: relaxation - the state of being free from tension and anxiety
  • antonym: relief - the reduction or removal of stress or pain
  • synonym: strain - mental or emotional tension resulting from adverse or demanding circumstances
