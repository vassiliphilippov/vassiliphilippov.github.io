race driver

noun
person who drives racing cars
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪs ˌdraɪvər / (AmE), / ˈreɪs ˌdraɪvə / (BrE)
a person who drives racing cars competitively, especially at high speeds

Examples

Professional race driver
She trained for years to become a professional race driver.
The race driver celebrated his victory on the podium.
The experienced race driver skillfully overtook his rivals in the final lap.
