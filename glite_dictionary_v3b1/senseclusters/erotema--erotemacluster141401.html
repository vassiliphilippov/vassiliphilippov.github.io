erotema

noun
question mark symbol
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛrəˈtiːmə / (AmE), / ˌɛrəˈtiːmə / (BrE)
the punctuation mark (?) used at the end of a sentence to indicate a question

Examples

An erotema mark
The teacher reminded the student to add an erotema at the end of each question.
Without an erotema, the sentence looked like a statement instead of a query.
In some languages, the erotema appears at both the beginning and end of a question.

Related Terms

  • antonym: period - the punctuation mark (.) used to end a statement
  • synonym: question mark - the symbol (?) used to indicate a question
