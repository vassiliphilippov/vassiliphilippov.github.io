moisten

verb
make slightly wet
Pronunciation: / ˈmɔɪsən / (AmE), / ˈmɔɪsn̩ / (BrE)
to make something slightly wet or damp

Examples

To moisten the brush
Before applying the paint, moisten the brush slightly with water.
The baker moistened the dough with a little milk.
You can moisten the cloth to help remove the stain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dampen - to make something slightly wet
  • antonym: dry - to remove moisture from something
