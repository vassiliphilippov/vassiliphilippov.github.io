non-cash

adjective
not using money
4.0
Pronunciation: / nɑn kæʃ / (AmE), / nɒn kæʃ / (BrE)
not involving the direct use or transfer of physical money, such as coins or banknotes

Examples

Non-cash rewards
The company provided non-cash rewards like gift cards and extra vacation days to employees.
Many modern transactions are now non-cash, using credit cards or digital payments instead.
The bonus was given in the form of non-cash benefits rather than a direct payment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cash - involving physical money such as coins or banknotes
  • synonym: cashless - not using or involving cash
