skipper

noun
small fast butterfly
Pronunciation: / ˈskɪpər / (AmE), / ˈskɪpə / (BrE)
a small, fast-flying butterfly with a stout body, often found near water or flowers

Examples

Skipper butterfly
A skipper darted quickly from one flower to another in the garden.
Children watched as a tiny skipper hovered above the pond's surface.
The bright orange skipper was easy to spot among the green leaves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: butterfly - an insect with large, colorful wings
  • synonym: moth - a nocturnal insect similar to a butterfly
