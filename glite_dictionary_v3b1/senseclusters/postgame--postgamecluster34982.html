postgame

noun
after-game period
Pronunciation: / ˈpoʊstˌɡeɪm / (AmE), / ˈpəʊstˌɡeɪm / (BrE)
the period immediately after a sports game, or an event, broadcast, or discussion that takes place at that time

Examples

Postgame show
The coach answered questions during the postgame press conference.
Fans stayed in the stadium for the postgame celebration after their team won.
The network aired a special postgame show analyzing the key moments of the match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: postmatch - the period immediately after a match, especially in British English
  • antonym: pregame - the period or activities before a sports game
