clumsiness

noun
lack of grace or coordination
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmzinəs /; / ˈklʌmzinɪs / (AmE), / ˈklʌmzɪnəs /; / ˈklʌmzinɪs / (BrE)
the quality of being awkward or lacking grace in movement or behavior

Examples

Physical clumsiness
Despite his clumsiness, everyone found his awkward charm endearing.

Her clumsiness was evident when she tripped over the rug during the meeting.
