ninefold

adverb
by a factor of nine
Pronunciation: / ˈnaɪnˌfoʊld / (AmE), / ˈnaɪnfəʊld / (BrE)
By nine times as much; increased or multiplied by a factor of nine.

Examples

Increased ninefold
CO2 emissions were reduced ninefold as a result of the new energy policies.
Her workload grew ninefold after the merger was completed.
In the past decade, the company's profits have increased ninefold.
