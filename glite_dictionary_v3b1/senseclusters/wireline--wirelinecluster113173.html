wireline

noun
physical data cable
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈwaɪərˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈwaɪəlaɪn / (BrE)
a cable or physical line used to transmit signals or data, especially in telecommunications

Examples

A wireline cable
The company upgraded its wireline network to improve internet speeds for customers.
Many rural areas still rely on wireline connections for telephone service.
Technicians inspected the wireline infrastructure after the storm caused outages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cable - a thick wire or group of wires used to carry electricity or signals
  • antonym: wireless - using radio waves instead of physical cables to transmit signals
