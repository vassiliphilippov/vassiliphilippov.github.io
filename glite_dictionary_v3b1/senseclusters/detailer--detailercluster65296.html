detailer

noun
personnel assigner
8.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈteɪlɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈteɪlə / (BrE)
an officer or administrator, especially in the military, who assigns personnel to specific positions, duties, or ships

Examples

Military detailer
The detailer reviewed the list of available officers before making assignments for the new mission.
After speaking with the detailer, she learned she would be stationed overseas for her next deployment.
Each sailor met with the detailer to discuss their upcoming duties and possible transfers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - a person responsible for managing operations or personnel
  • synonym: assigner - a person who allocates tasks or positions to others
