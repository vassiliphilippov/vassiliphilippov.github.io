kneecap

noun
bone at front of the knee
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈniːˌkæp / (AmE), / ˈniːˌkap / (BrE)
the small, flat, triangular bone at the front of the knee joint, protecting the knee

Examples

A broken kneecap
She slipped on the wet floor and injured her kneecap.
The doctor explained that a fractured kneecap can make walking very painful.
Wearing knee pads can help protect your kneecaps during sports.

Related Terms

  • synonym: patella - the anatomical name for the kneecap
