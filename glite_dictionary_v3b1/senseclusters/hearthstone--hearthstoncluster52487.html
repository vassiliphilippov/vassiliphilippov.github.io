hearthstone

noun
a stone forming the hearth of a fireplace
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑrθˌstoʊn / (AmE), / ˈhɑːθˌstəʊn / (BrE)
a flat stone forming the floor of a fireplace or the area in front of it

Examples

The hearthstone floor
After a long walk in the snow, the dog curled up by the hearthstone to warm itself.
She carefully swept the ashes from the hearthstone every morning.
The old cottage still had its original hearthstone, worn smooth by years of use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hearth - the floor of a fireplace, often extending into a room
