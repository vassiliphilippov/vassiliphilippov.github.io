penurious

adjective
very unwilling to spend money
8.2
Pronunciation: / pəˈnjʊriəs / (AmE), / pɪˈnjʊərɪəs / (BrE)
unwilling to spend money; extremely frugal or miserly

Examples

Penurious millionaire
Despite his wealth, Mr. Jones remained penurious and rarely donated to charity.
He was so penurious that he patched his clothes instead of buying new ones.
Their penurious lifestyle meant they avoided even minor luxuries.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give money or help freely
  • synonym: parsimonious - unwilling to spend money or use resources; stingy or frugal
