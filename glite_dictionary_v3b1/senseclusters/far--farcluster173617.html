far

adverb
at/to/by a great distance
1.4
Pronunciation: / fɑr / (AmE), / fɑː / (BrE)
At, to, or by a great distance in space; a long way away.

Examples

To travel far
I don't want to move far from my family.
The mountains looked far away on the horizon.
They had to walk far to reach the next village.

Related Terms

  • antonym: near - at or to a short distance in space
