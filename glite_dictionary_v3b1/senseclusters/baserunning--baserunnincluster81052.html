baserunning

noun
running between bases
3.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbeɪs.rʌn.ɪŋ/ (AmE), /ˈbeɪs.rʌn.ɪŋ/ (BrE)
the act or skill of running between bases in baseball or softball, including speed, timing, and strategy

Examples

Aggressive baserunning

Improving baserunning is essential for players who want to be more effective on offense.
