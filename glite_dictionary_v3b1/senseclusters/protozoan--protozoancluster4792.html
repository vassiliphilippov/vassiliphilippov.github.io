protozoan

noun
microscopic single-celled organism, not photosynthetic
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊtəˈzoʊən / (AmE), / ˌprəʊtəˈzəʊən / (BrE)
a minute, usually single-celled organism that is not photosynthetic, typically classified within a group of simple microorganisms formerly considered animals

Examples

A protozoan cell
A protozoan can be found in many pond water samples examined under a microscope.
Some scientists study the complex life cycles of protozoan species.
The disease is caused by a protozoan parasite transmitted by mosquitoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protist - any member of the group of mainly single-celled microscopic organisms, including protozoa and algae
