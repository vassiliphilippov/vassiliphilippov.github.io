unembraced

adjective
not hugged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪmˈbreɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪmˈbreɪst / (BrE)
not held or hugged in someone's arms; not embraced or closely held.

Examples

Unembraced child
The child stood quietly in the corner, feeling unembraced as the others greeted each other warmly.
After the argument, she left the room unembraced and alone.
He watched the happy families and felt painfully unembraced during the holiday gathering.

Related Terms

  • antonym: embraced - held closely in someone's arms
  • synonym: unhugged - not hugged or held in an embrace
