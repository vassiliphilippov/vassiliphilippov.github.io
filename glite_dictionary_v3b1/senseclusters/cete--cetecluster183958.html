cete

noun
group of badgers
9.9
Pronunciation: / siːt / (AmE), / siːt / (BrE)
a group of badgers, used as a collective noun, chiefly in historical or poetic contexts

Examples

A cete of badgers
At dusk, a cete of badgers emerged from the hedgerow and trotted across the field.
The old poem described a cete gathering beneath the moonlit trees.
Naturalists once referred to a group of badgers as a cete in their writings.
