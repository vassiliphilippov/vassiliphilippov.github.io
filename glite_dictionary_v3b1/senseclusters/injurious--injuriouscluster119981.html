Synonyms

  • harmful
    2.6 8/m
    harmful causes damage or bad effects in many situations, while injurious sounds more formal and often refers to physical injury.
  • damaging
    3.1 4/m
    damaging means causing a loss or harm, often to things or reputation, while injurious focuses on causing actual injury.
  • deleterious
    8.5 0.4/m
    deleterious often describes harm to living things or health in formal texts, while injurious is a general word for causing injury.
  • dangerous
    1.5 130/m
    dangerous means likely to cause harm because of risk or threat, while injurious stresses causing actual harm or injury.
