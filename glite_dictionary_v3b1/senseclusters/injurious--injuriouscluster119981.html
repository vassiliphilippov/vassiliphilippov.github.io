injurious

adjective
harmful; causing injury or damage
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈdʒʊriəs / (AmE), / ɪnˈdʒʊəriəs / (BrE)
Causing or likely to cause harm or injury.

Examples

Injurious substances
Employers must ensure the workplace is free from injurious substances.
Repeated exposure to loud noise can be injurious to your hearing.
The chemical was found to be injurious to aquatic life.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmful - causing harm or damage
  • antonym: harmless - not causing harm or injury
