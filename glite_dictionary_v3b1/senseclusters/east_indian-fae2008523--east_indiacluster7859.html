East Indian

noun
native of the East Indies
3.3
Pronunciation: / ist ˈɪndiən / (AmE), / iːst ˈɪndiən / (BrE)
a native or inhabitant of the East Indies, especially the Indian subcontinent or Southeast Asia

Examples

An East Indian person
A group of East Indians gathered to celebrate a traditional festival.
Many East Indians settled in coastal cities during the 19th century.
The cuisine of East Indians is renowned for its rich spices and diverse flavors.

Related Terms

  • antonym: West Indian - a native or inhabitant of the West Indies
