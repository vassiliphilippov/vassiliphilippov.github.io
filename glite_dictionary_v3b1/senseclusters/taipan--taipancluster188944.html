taipan

noun
highly venomous Australian snake
7.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪˌpæn / (AmE), / ˈtaɪ.pan / (BrE)
a large, highly venomous snake of northeastern Australia, belonging to the genus Oxyuranus.

Examples

The taipan snake
Antivenom is essential for treating bites from the taipan.
During their trip to Queensland, the researchers encountered a taipan in the wild.
The taipan is considered one of the most dangerous snakes in the world due to its potent venom.

Related Terms
