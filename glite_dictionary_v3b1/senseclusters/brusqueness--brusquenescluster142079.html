brusqueness

noun
abrupt, blunt manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbrʌsk.nəs / (AmE), / ˈbrʊsk.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being blunt or abrupt in manner, often to the point of rudeness

Examples

His brusqueness surprised
Despite his brusqueness, he genuinely cared about his team.
He answered the questions with a brusqueness that suggested impatience.
Her brusqueness sometimes offends people who are not used to direct communication.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bluntness - the quality of being uncompromisingly forthright
  • antonym: politeness - the quality of being courteous and considerate
