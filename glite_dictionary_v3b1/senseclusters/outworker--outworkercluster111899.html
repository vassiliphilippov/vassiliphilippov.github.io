outworker

noun
home-based worker
6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈaʊ.twɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /ˈaʊ.twɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a person who does paid work at home or outside the main workplace for an employer

Examples

Many outworkers sew
Many outworkers sew garments from their own homes to earn extra income.
The company relies on outworkers to assemble products in remote locations.
As an outworker, she enjoys the flexibility of setting her own schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: freelancer - a person who works independently for different companies
