scrubber

noun
person who cleans surfaces with a brush
Pronunciation: / ˈskrʌbər / (AmE), / ˈskrʌbə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to clean surfaces, typically using a brush, especially floors or decks

Examples

A floor scrubber
After the party ended, a scrubber arrived to clean up the mess on the deck.
As a scrubber, she took pride in leaving every floor spotless.
The scrubber worked tirelessly to remove the stains from the restaurant floor.
