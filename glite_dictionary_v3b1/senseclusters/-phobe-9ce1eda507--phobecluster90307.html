-phobe

x
person who fears
8.6
Pronunciation: / foʊb / (AmE), / fəʊb / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns meaning a person who fears or dislikes a particular thing.

Examples

An arachnophobe person
Someone who fears spiders is called an arachnophobe.
A technobphobe avoids using modern devices and technology.
The term xenobphobe describes a person who dislikes foreigners.

Related Terms

  • antonym: -phile - a suffix meaning a person who loves or has a strong liking for something
  • synonym: -phobia - a suffix meaning an extreme fear or dislike of something
