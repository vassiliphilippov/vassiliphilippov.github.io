williwaw

noun
violent coastal wind
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪliˌwɑ / (AmE), / ˈwɪliˌwɔː / (BrE)
a sudden, violent gust of cold wind blowing down from a mountainous coast, especially in polar regions
