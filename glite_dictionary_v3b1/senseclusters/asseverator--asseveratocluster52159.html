asseverator

noun
one who affirms or asserts strongly
Pronunciation: / əˈsɛv.əˌreɪ.tɚ / (AmE), / əˈsɛv.əˌreɪ.tə / (BrE)
a person who asserts or affirms something strongly as being true

Examples

A asseverator of truth
History remembers her as a fearless asseverator of truth against popular opinion.
In the heated debate, each asseverator insisted their facts were indisputable.
The asseverator maintained his account even under intense questioning.
