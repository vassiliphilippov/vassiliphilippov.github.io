homesteading

noun
self-sufficient living
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌstɛdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌstɛdɪŋ / (BrE)
the practice of living self-sufficiently on one's own land, often by growing food and raising animals

Examples

Modern homesteading lifestyle
Many people are turning to homesteading to reduce their dependence on supermarkets and modern conveniences.
The couple started homesteading by planting a large vegetable garden and keeping chickens.
Books and online forums offer advice for those interested in homesteading and sustainable living.

Related Terms

  • synonym: self-sufficiency - the ability to provide for oneself without outside help
  • synonym: subsistence farming - farming that provides only enough food for the farmer and their family
