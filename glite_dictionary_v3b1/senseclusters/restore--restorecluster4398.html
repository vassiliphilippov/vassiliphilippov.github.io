restore

verb
to bring back to health or energy
Pronunciation: / rɪˈstɔr / (AmE), / rɪˈstɔː / (BrE)
to bring back to a former, original, or normal condition, especially to health or energy; to make someone feel renewed or revitalized

Examples

To restore your energy
A good night's sleep can restore both your body and mind.
Her confidence was restored after she finished the project successfully.
Taking a short walk outside often helps to restore my energy during a busy day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exhaust - to drain of energy or strength
  • synonym: revive - to give new strength or energy to
