somber

adjective
dark or dull in color
Pronunciation: / ˈsɑːmbər / (AmE), / ˈsɒmbə / (BrE)
(of color or appearance) dark or dull in tone; lacking brightness or vividness.

Examples

Somber colors
She wore a somber gray dress that matched the cloudy day.
The artist chose a somber palette for his painting, giving it a subdued atmosphere.
Their living room was decorated in somber tones, with shades of brown and black throughout.
She wore a sombre brown dress that matched the mood of the funeral.
The overcast sky gave a sombre appearance to the entire city.
The room looked sombre with its faded curtains and shadowy corners.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drab - dull, lacking brightness or color
  • synonym: dull - Lacking brightness, vividness, or sheen.
  • antonym: vivid - bright, intense in color
