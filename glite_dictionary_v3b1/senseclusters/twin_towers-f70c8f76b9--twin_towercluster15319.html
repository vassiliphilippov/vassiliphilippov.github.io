twin towers

noun
the two World Trade Center skyscrapers in New York City
3.6
Pronunciation: /twɪn ˈtaʊ.ərz/ /; / /twɪn taʊrz/ (AmE), /twɪn ˈtaʊ.əz/ (BrE)
