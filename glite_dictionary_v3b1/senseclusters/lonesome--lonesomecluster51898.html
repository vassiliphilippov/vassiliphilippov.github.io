lonesome

noun
feeling of loneliness
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊnsəm / (AmE), / ˈləʊnsəm / (BrE)
a feeling of sadness or loneliness caused by being alone

Examples

A deep lonesome
After the holidays ended, he was left with a deep lonesome that lingered for days.
Moving away from her family brought a sense of lonesome she had never felt before.
Sometimes, a quiet evening alone can turn into a strong lonesome.

Related Terms

  • antonym: companionship - the state of having company or friends
  • synonym: loneliness - the state of being alone and unhappy about it
  • synonym: solitude - the state of being alone, often by choice
