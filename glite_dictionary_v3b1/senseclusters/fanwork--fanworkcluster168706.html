fanwork

noun
creative work by fans
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌwɜrk / (AmE), / ˈfænˌwɜːk / (BrE)
a creative work, such as art, fiction, or video, made by fans based on an existing media or franchise

Examples

Fanwork inspired by games
She shared her latest fanwork inspired by her favorite video game on social media.
Many artists create fanwork to express their love for popular movies and books.
The convention featured an exhibition of fanwork from different fandoms around the world.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fanfiction - stories written by fans using characters or settings from existing works
  • antonym: original work - a creative work not based on existing media or franchises
