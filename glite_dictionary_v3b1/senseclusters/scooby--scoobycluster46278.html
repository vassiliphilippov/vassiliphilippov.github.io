scooby

noun
clue or hint
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːbi / (BrE)
an informal British term for a clue or piece of information that helps someone understand something

Examples

No scooby at all
I have not got a scooby how to fix this computer problem.
After reading the instructions twice, she still didn't have a scooby what to do next.
He looked at the puzzle and admitted he didn't have a scooby.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hint - a small piece of advice or suggestion to help someone
