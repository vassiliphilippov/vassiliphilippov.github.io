negator

noun
counterbalancing spring device
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡeɪtər / (AmE), / nɪˈɡeɪtə / (BrE)
a constant-force spring or device used to counterbalance weight in mechanical systems such as blinds or reels

Examples

Negator spring mechanism
The engineer installed a negator to help the window blind move smoothly and stay balanced.
A broken negator can cause the reel to unwind unevenly and damage the mechanism.
Technicians often replace the negator in old shutters to restore proper function.

Related Terms

  • synonym: counterweight - a weight that balances another weight
  • synonym: spring - a device that stores mechanical energy and releases it
