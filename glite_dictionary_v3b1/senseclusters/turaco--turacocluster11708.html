turaco

noun
large, brightly crested African bird
Pronunciation: / ˈtʊrəˌkoʊ / (AmE), / ˈtjʊərəkəʊ / (BrE)
a large African bird known for its bright crest and vivid plumage, belonging to the family Musophagidae

Examples

A turaco bird
Many birdwatchers travel to Africa hoping to catch a glimpse of the rare violet turaco.
The Ross's turaco is easily identified by its striking blue feathers and red crest.
We spotted a brightly colored turaco perched high in the trees during our safari.
