foreignness

noun
the state of being foreign or unfamiliar
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrɪnnəs / (AmE), / ˈfɒrɪnnəs / (BrE)
the quality or state of being foreign, unfamiliar, or alien

Examples

The foreignness of the customs made her feel out of place.
