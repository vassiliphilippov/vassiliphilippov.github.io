dirtbag

noun
bag of soil
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɜrtˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈdɜːtˌbæɡ / (BrE)
a bag filled with soil or earth, used for gardening, landscaping, or as a weight.

Examples

A dirtbag of soil
The gardener used a dirtbag to support the base of the new tree.
After the heavy rain, they stacked each dirtbag along the edge of the garden to prevent erosion.
He carried a dirtbag from the truck to the backyard for the landscaping project.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sandbag - a bag filled with sand, used as a weight or barrier
