jerk

noun
dried, seasoned strips of meat
9.9
Pronunciation: / dʒɝk / (AmE), / dʒɜːk / (BrE)
meat, especially beef, that is cut into strips and dried in the sun, often after being seasoned

Examples

Dried jerk meat
Native peoples preserved meat by making jerk, allowing it to last for months.
The traveler packed some jerk to snack on during the long journey.
Traditional Caribbean recipes call for seasoned jerk prepared over an open flame.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jerky - meat that has been dried and salted, especially in strips
