garage sale

noun
private sale of used household items
4.6
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈrɑːdʒ seɪl / (AmE), / ɡəˈrɑːʒ seɪl / (BrE)
An outdoor sale of used personal or household items, typically held at the seller's home.

Examples

A garage sale event
Many neighbors stopped by the garage sale looking for bargains.
She decided to clear out her attic and hold a garage sale this weekend.
We found a vintage lamp at the garage sale on Saturday.
