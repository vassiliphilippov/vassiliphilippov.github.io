oppositely

adverb
in an opposite position or direction
5.8 0.08/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.p…ô.z…™t.li / (AmE), / Àà…íp.…ô.z…™t.li / (BrE)
In an opposite position or manner; in a way that is directly contrary to something else.

Examples

Views oppositely aligned
Her views are oppositely aligned to those of her colleagues.
The tracks run oppositely along the sides of the river.
The two doors are placed oppositely on either side of the corridor.

Synonyms

  • contrarily
    6.5 0.06/m
    contrarily is used to say something happens in an opposing way, while oppositely means directly in an opposite manner.
  • conversely
    4.6 8.20/m
    conversely often introduces a statement that reverses an idea, while oppositely focuses on being directly contrary in manner.
  • contrariwise
    9.2 0.00/m
    contrariwise sounds old-fashioned and means the opposite way, while oppositely is more neutral and describes direct contrast.
  • inversely
    5.8 1.60/m
    inversely describes an opposite relation where one thing changes as another does, while oppositely means a direct contrary manner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: opposite - completely different; contrary
