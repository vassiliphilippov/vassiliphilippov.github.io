oppositely

adverb
in an opposite position or direction
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑː.pə.zɪt.li / (AmE), / ˈɒp.ə.zɪt.li / (BrE)
In an opposite position or manner; in a way that is directly contrary to something else.

Examples

Views oppositely aligned
Her views are oppositely aligned to those of her colleagues.
The tracks run oppositely along the sides of the river.
The two doors are placed oppositely on either side of the corridor.
