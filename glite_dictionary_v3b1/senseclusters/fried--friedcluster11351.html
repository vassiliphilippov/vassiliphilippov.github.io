fried

adjective
cooked in fat or oil
Pronunciation: / fraɪd / (AmE), / fraɪd / (BrE)
(of food) cooked in hot fat or oil.

Examples

Fried chicken
I ordered fried chicken with a side of mashed potatoes.
She prefers her eggs fried rather than boiled.
The smell of fried onions filled the entire kitchen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: baked - (of food) cooked by dry heat in an oven
  • synonym: fry - to cook in hot fat or oil
  • antonym: grilled - (of food) cooked by direct heat, especially on a grill
