pearlwort

noun
small flowering plant of the genus Sagina
9.9 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / Ààp…ùlÀåw…ùt / (AmE), / Ààp…úÀêlÀåw…úÀêt / (BrE)
Any of various low-growing plants of the genus Sagina, having small, spherical, pearl-like flowers.

Examples

Pearlwort plant
Pearlwort often grows in rocky or sandy soils, especially in alpine regions.
Botanists identified the tiny white flowers as belonging to the pearlwort family.
The garden path was lined with pearlwort, giving it a soft, green appearance.
