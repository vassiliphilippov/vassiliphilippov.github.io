dipso

noun
slang for heavy drinker
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪpsoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdɪpsəʊ / (BrE)
a slang term for a person who drinks alcohol excessively or is habitually drunk

Examples

Old dipso at the bar
Everyone in the neighborhood knew the old dipso who sat outside the bar every evening.
He was called a dipso because he rarely went a day without drinking.
The movie features a lovable dipso who always finds himself in trouble.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drunkard - a person who is habitually drunk
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who never drinks alcohol
