tons

noun
a very large number or amount
5.7
Pronunciation: / tʌnz / (AmE), / tʌnz / (BrE)
(informal) a very large number or amount of something.

Examples

tons of work
She has tons of experience in marketing.
There are tons of reasons to visit Paris in the spring.
We received tons of applications for the job opening.
