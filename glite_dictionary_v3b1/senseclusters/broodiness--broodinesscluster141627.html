broodiness

noun
gloomy thoughtfulness
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbruːdinəs / (AmE), / ˈbruːdinəs / (BrE)
a state of deep, often gloomy or thoughtful mood, marked by being pensive or preoccupied

Examples

His broodiness after trouble
After the argument, his broodiness made it hard for him to focus on his work.
Her sudden broodiness worried her friends, who tried to cheer her up.
The rainy weather seemed to increase his broodiness and quiet mood.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerfulness - the quality of being happy and optimistic
  • synonym: melancholy - a feeling of deep sadness or gloom
  • synonym: pensiveness - the quality of being deeply thoughtful
