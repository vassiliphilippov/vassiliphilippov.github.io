arc lamp

noun
lamp producing light via electric arc
Pronunciation: / ɑrk læmp / (AmE), / ɑːk læmp / (BrE)
A lamp that emits light by creating an electric arc between two electrodes.

Examples

An arc lamp light
An arc lamp produces brilliant light by maintaining an electric arc between two carbon rods.
Early street lighting often relied on arc lamps before incandescent bulbs became widespread.
The old theater was once illuminated by a single arc lamp positioned above the stage.

  • synonym: arc light - light source created by an electric arc
