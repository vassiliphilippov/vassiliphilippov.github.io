Irtysh

proper noun
major river in Asia, Ob tributary
Pronunciation: / ɪrˈtɪʃ / (AmE), / ɪəˈtɪʃ / (BrE)
a major river in Asia, originating in the Altai Mountains in northern China and flowing northwest as a tributary of the Ob River
