quality of life

noun
level of satisfaction with living conditions
6.8 2.9
Pronunciation: /ˈkwɑː.lə.ti laɪf/ (AmE), /ˈkwɒ.lɪ.ti laɪf/ (BrE)
the degree of personal satisfaction or dissatisfaction felt with the cultural, intellectual, and general conditions under which you live, apart from material wealth

Examples

The quality of life index
Healthcare advances have greatly improved the quality of life for elderly people in the region.
She chose the job that offered a better quality of life over a higher salary.
Urban parks and green spaces contribute significantly to the quality of life in cities.
