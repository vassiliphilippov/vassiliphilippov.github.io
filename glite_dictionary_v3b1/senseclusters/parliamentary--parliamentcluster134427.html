parliamentary

adjective
relating to parliament or its procedures
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːrləˈmɛn.tə.ri / (AmE), / ˌpɑː.lɪˈmɛn.təri / (BrE)
Relating to, enacted by, or in accordance with a parliament or its members.

Examples

Parliamentary committee
A parliamentary committee will review the proposed legislation next week.
Several countries operate under a parliamentary system rather than a presidential one.
The government promised parliamentary reform to modernize its procedures.

  • synonym: legislative - relating to laws or the making of laws
