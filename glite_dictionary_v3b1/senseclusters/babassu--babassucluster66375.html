babassu

noun
Brazilian palm tree with oil-rich nuts
3.8 9.9
Pronunciation: / bəˈbɑːsuː / (AmE), / bəˈbæs.uː / (BrE)
A tall palm tree native to northern Brazil, known for its hard-shelled nuts that yield valuable oil and a type of vegetable ivory.

Examples

Babassu palm tree
Large plantations of babassu stretch across northern Brazil.
Local artisans craft buttons from the vegetable ivory produced by the babassu nut.
The babassu provides oil that is widely used in cooking and cosmetics.
