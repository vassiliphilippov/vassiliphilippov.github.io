swingarm

noun
motorcycle rear suspension
Pronunciation: / ˈswɪŋˌɑrm / (AmE), / ˈswɪŋˌɑːm / (BrE)
a hinged part that connects the frame to the rear wheel of a bicycle or motorcycle, allowing the wheel to move up and down

Examples

Motorcycle swingarm
The mechanic replaced the swingarm to improve the bike's performance.
A damaged swingarm can affect the stability and safety of a motorcycle.
Modern mountain bikes often use a lightweight swingarm for better suspension.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suspension - the system that supports a vehicle and absorbs shocks
