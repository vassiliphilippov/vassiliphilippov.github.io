pull in

verb
attract; draw in (people, money, attention, etc.)
5.8
Pronunciation: / pʊl ɪn / (AmE), / pʊl ɪn / (BrE)
to attract or draw (people, attention, money, etc.), especially by appealing qualities or effective means

Pull in customers

The new marketing campaign managed to pull in thousands of new customers within a month.
