Examples

Synonyms

  • potential drop
    7.0 0.01/m
    a potential drop is the fall in electrical potential between two circuit points, while voltage names that difference measured in volts.
  • potential difference
    4.1 0.18/m
    a potential difference describes the different electric potential between two circuit points, while voltage is the common name used to report it.
  • emf
    8.8 0.52/m
    an emf is the energy per unit charge a source supplies in a circuit, while voltage is the difference measured between two points.
  • electric potential
    5.1 0.07/m
    electric potential is the amount of energy per unit charge at a single point, while voltage measures the difference between two points.
