newsboy

noun
boy who delivers newspapers
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈnuzˌbɔɪ / (AmE), / ˈnjuːzbɔɪ / (BrE)
a boy who delivers or sells newspapers, especially as a job

Examples

A newsboy selling papers
In old photographs, you can often see a newsboy standing on the corner shouting headlines.
The newsboy braved the rain to make sure every house received its evening paper.
The newsboy tossed the newspaper onto our porch every morning before sunrise.
