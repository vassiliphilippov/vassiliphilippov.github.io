blowout

noun
blow-dried hairstyle or salon service
Pronunciation: / c8blo8acca8at / (AmE), / c8bl598acca8at / (BrE)
a hair-styling service or the finished hairstyle produced by blow-drying and styling, usually to create smoothness and volume.

Examples

Salon blowout
She booked a salon blowout to get a sleek, voluminous look for the interview.
After washing, he did his own blowout with a round brush and heat protectant spray.
Bridal stylists often schedule trial blowouts so brides can choose their preferred finished look.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blow-dry - to dry and style hair using a hair dryer and brush; also the resulting hairstyle.
  • synonym: hairdo - a particular way of styling someone's hair.
