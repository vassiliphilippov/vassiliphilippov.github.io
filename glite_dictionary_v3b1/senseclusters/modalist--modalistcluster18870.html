modalist

noun
modality specialist
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊdəlɪst / (AmE), / ˈməʊdəlɪst / (BrE)
a linguist or grammarian who studies modality, especially modal verbs and expressions of possibility or necessity

Examples

A modalist linguist
The modalist presented her findings on the use of modal verbs in English.
As a dedicated modalist, he analyzed how different languages express necessity and possibility.
Many linguists consult a modalist when researching the grammar of modality.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grammaticalian - a person who studies grammar
  • synonym: modal - relating to mood or modality in grammar
