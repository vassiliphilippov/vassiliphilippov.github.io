cordyceps

noun
parasitic fungus genus
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrdɪˌsɛps / (AmE), / ˈkɔːdɪˌsɛps / (BrE)
a genus of parasitic fungi that infect insects and other arthropods, often replacing their host's tissue
