irresponsiveness

noun
lack of responsibility
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˌɪrɪˈspɑːnsɪvnəs / (AmE), / ˌɪrɪˈspɒnsɪvnəs / (BrE)
the quality of not behaving in a responsible way or not accepting responsibility for actions

Examples

Irresponsiveness at work
His irresponsiveness to important emails caused delays in the project.
The manager was frustrated by the team's irresponsiveness during the crisis.
Parents were concerned about the school's irresponsiveness to safety complaints.

Related Terms

  • synonym: irresponsibility - the state of not being responsible
  • antonym: responsibility - the quality of being responsible
