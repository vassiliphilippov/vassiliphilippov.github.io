shoot

noun
young growth on a plant
5.4
Pronunciation: / ʃuːt / (AmE), / ʃuːt / (BrE)
a new growth on a plant, typically a young branch, stem, or sprout emerging from a seed or the main plant.

Examples

A new shoot
After pruning the rose, several new shoots appeared in the spring.
He gently touched the delicate shoot emerging from the seed.
The young shoot grew quickly towards the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: branch - a limb or offshoot from the main stem of a tree
  • synonym: sprout - a new shoot or bud of a plant
