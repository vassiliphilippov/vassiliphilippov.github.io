midcourt

adjective
in the middle of court
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪdˌkɔrt / (AmE), / ˈmɪdˌkɔːt / (BrE)
located in or relating to the middle area of a sports court, especially in basketball or tennis

Examples

Midcourt defense
The player made a quick move from the midcourt area to score a basket.
She intercepted the ball at midcourt and started a fast break.
Coaches often instruct defenders to apply pressure at midcourt during close games.

Related Terms

  • synonym: center court - the central area of a sports court
  • antonym: sideline - the boundary line or area at the side of a sports court
