Cepheid

adjective
of variable stars
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːfiɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːfiɪd / (BrE)
relating to Cepheid variable stars or their properties, especially in astronomy.

Examples

Cepheid distance method
The astronomers used Cepheid measurements to determine the distance to the nearby galaxy.
A Cepheid variable star can help scientists estimate cosmic distances with greater accuracy.
The Cepheid period-luminosity relation is crucial for understanding the scale of the universe.
