do

verb
be sufficient; be adequate
1.0 1498/m
Pronunciation: / du / (AmE), / duÀê / (BrE)
to be sufficient or adequate in quality or quantity for a particular purpose

Examples

Do for dinner
A simple salad will do for dinner tonight.
It doesn't matter which room we get; any will do.
Will this old pen do, or do you need a new one?

Synonyms

  • serve
    1.8 126/m
