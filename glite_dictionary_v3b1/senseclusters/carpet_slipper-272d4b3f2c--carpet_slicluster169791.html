carpet slipper

noun
soft indoor slipper
4.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːrpɪt ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈkɑːpɪt ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
A soft, comfortable, low-sided slipper designed to be worn indoors, easily slipped on and off.

Examples

A pair of carpet slippers
A pair of warm carpet slippers makes winter mornings much more comfortable.
He padded across the kitchen floor in his carpet slippers, looking for his morning newspaper.
She kicked off her carpet slippers and curled up on the sofa with a book.
