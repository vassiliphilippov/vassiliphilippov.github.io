surrealist

adjective
dreamlike or bizarre
Pronunciation: / səˈriːəlɪst / (AmE), / səˈrɪəlɪst / (BrE)
having qualities that are dreamlike, bizarre, or not realistic, often resembling the style of surrealism

Examples

Surrealist style painting
The artist's surrealist paintings often feature melting clocks and floating objects.
Her dreams had a surrealist quality, blending reality with impossible events.
The play's surrealist atmosphere made the audience question what was real and what was imagined.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fantastical - very strange, imaginative, or unreal
  • antonym: realistic - representing things as they are in real life
