subregion

noun
smaller area of region
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌbˌriːdʒən / (AmE), / ˈsʌbˌriːdʒən / (BrE)
a smaller area that forms part of a larger region

Examples

A subregion of a country
The country is divided into several subregions for administrative purposes.
Each subregion has its own unique traditions and customs.
Scientists studied the climate differences between the northern and southern subregions.
