pro-democracy

adjective
supporting democracy
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˌproʊ dɪˈmɑːkrəsi / (AmE), / ˌprəʊ dɪˈmɒkrəsi / (BrE)
supporting or favoring a system of government in which power is held by the people, typically through elected representatives

Examples

Pro-democracy groups
Pro-democracy groups called for fair elections and greater political freedom.
The new law was criticized by many pro-democracy activists as a threat to civil rights.
International observers praised the pro-democracy movement for its peaceful demonstrations.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anti-democracy - opposed to democracy or democratic principles
  • synonym: democratic - relating to or supporting democracy
