Picris

noun
genus of yellow-flowered herbs (bitterweed)
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪkrɪs /; / ˈpaɪkrɪs / (AmE), / ˈpɪkrɪs / (BrE)
a genus of yellow-flowered plants in the daisy family, commonly called bitterweed, native to the Old World and often containing a bitter-tasting substance
