earpiece

noun
small device worn in the ear for listening
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌpis / (AmE), / ˈɪəpiːs / (BrE)
A small device that fits over or in the ear and converts electrical signals into sound, used to listen to electronic audio discreetly.

Examples

A earpiece device
My earpiece stopped working, so I couldn't hear the call.
She wore a wireless earpiece to listen to music while jogging.
The security agent communicated covertly using his earpiece.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earbud - a small earpiece inserted directly into the ear canal
  • synonym: headphone - a device with a pair of earphones worn over the ears for listening to audio
