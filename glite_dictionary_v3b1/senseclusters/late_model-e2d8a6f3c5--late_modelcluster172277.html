late model

noun
recently manufactured vehicle
4.2
Pronunciation: / leɪt ˈmɑdəl / (AmE), / leɪt ˈmɒdl / (BrE)
a vehicle that was manufactured or designed recently, typically referring to a car, truck, or other mode of transport less than a few years old

Examples

A late model truck
Most buyers are looking for a late model truck that is still under warranty.
She decided to trade in her old car for a late model sedan with more features.
The dealership specializes in selling both classic and late model vehicles.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: new - recently made or introduced
  • antonym: old model - a vehicle that is not recent, usually older in design or manufacture
