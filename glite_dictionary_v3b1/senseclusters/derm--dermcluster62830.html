derm

noun
dermatology department
8.2
Pronunciation: / dɜrm / (AmE), / dɜːm / (BrE)
an informal abbreviation for a dermatology clinic or hospital department

Examples

Derm clinic
She scheduled an appointment at the derm to check a suspicious mole.
After waiting for weeks, he finally got a call from the derm about his test results.
The derm was busy with patients seeking treatment for various skin conditions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clinic - a place where people receive medical treatment
  • synonym: dermatology - the branch of medicine dealing with skin and its diseases
