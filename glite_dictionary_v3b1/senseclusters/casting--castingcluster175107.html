casting

noun
process of shaping material in a mold
Pronunciation: / ˈkæstɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkɑːstɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of shaping material (such as metal or plaster) by pouring it into a mold and letting it harden

Examples

Metal casting process
Modern manufacturing often relies on casting to produce complex parts efficiently.
The bronze statue was created by casting molten metal into a detailed mold.
We studied the casting process in our art class to make ceramic figures.
