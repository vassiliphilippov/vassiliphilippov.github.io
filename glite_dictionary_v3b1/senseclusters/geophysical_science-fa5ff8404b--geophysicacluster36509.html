geophysical science

noun
study of Earth's physical properties
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒi.oʊˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl ˈsaɪ.əns / (AmE), / ˌdʒiː.əˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl ˈsaɪ.əns / (BrE)
The branch of geology that uses physical methods and principles to study the properties and processes of the Earth.

Examples

Geophysical science studies Earth
Advances in geophysical science have improved our understanding of earthquakes and volcanic activity.
His research in geophysical science helped uncover new details about the Earth's core.
The conference brought together experts from various fields of geophysical science.

Related Terms
