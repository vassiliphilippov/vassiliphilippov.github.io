latinized

adjective
made like Latin
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈlætəˌnaɪzd / (AmE), / ˈlætɪnaɪzd / (BrE)
made to look, sound, or conform more closely to the Latin language in style or form

Examples

Latinized scientific names
Many scientific names are latinized for consistency.
The latinized version of the word made it easier to use in academic texts.
Some city names have latinized forms that differ from their original spellings.
