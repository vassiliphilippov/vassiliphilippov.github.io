correctional facility

noun
prison or place for rehabilitating offenders
3.9 3.5
Pronunciation: /kər.ˈek.ʃə.nəl fə.ˈsɪl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /kə.ˈrek.ʃə.nəl fə.ˈsɪl.ɪ.ti/ (BrE)
a building or set of buildings where people are confined as punishment and are intended to be rehabilitated

Examples

A correctional facility
After his conviction, he was transferred to a correctional facility in another state.
Concerns were raised about overcrowding in the city's main correctional facility.
The new correctional facility offers vocational training to help inmates reintegrate into society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detention center - an institution for the detention of people, especially youths or immigrants
  • synonym: jail - a place for the confinement of people accused or convicted of a crime
  • synonym: prison - a building where people are legally held as punishment
