blastular

adjective
relating to a blastula (embryonic stage)
Pronunciation: / ˈblæstjələr / (AmE), / ˈblɑːstjʊlə / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a blastula, an early stage in embryonic development

Examples

Blastular stage
Microscopic analysis revealed several blastular features in the developing embryo.
The researcher identified blastular cells during the fourth day after fertilization.
This stage is referred to as the blastular phase in embryology textbooks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blastula - an early stage of the embryo, consisting of a hollow sphere of cells
