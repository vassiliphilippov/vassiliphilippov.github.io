enforcer

noun
person who enforces rules
4.3
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfɔrsər / (AmE), / ɪnˈfɔːsə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make sure that rules or laws are obeyed

Examples

Law enforcer
The enforcer checked every ticket at the entrance to the stadium.
As an enforcer, she made sure the company policies were followed by all employees.
Local enforcers patrol the area to ensure that the new regulations are respected.

Related Terms

  • synonym: officer - a person with official authority, especially in law enforcement
  • synonym: regulator - a person or body that supervises a particular industry or activity
  • antonym: violator - a person who breaks rules or laws
