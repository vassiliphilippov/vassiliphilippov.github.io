cruciferous plant

noun
plant of the Cruciferae (Brassicaceae) family
Pronunciation: / kruˈsɪfərəs plænt / (AmE), / kruˈsɪfərəs plɑːnt / (BrE)
any plant belonging to the Cruciferae (Brassicaceae) family, often characterized by four-petaled flowers; includes vegetables such as cabbage, broccoli, and mustard.

Examples

A cruciferous plant family
Cabbage, kale, and cauliflower are all examples of a cruciferous plant.
Eating a cruciferous plant like broccoli can provide important nutrients and antioxidants.
Scientists have studied the cancer-fighting properties of many cruciferous plants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brassica - any plant of the genus Brassica, a member of the cruciferous family
