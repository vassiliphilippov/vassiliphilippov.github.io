gall

noun
shameless boldness; impertinence
Pronunciation: / ɡɔl / (AmE), / ɡɔːl / (BrE)
rude boldness; shameless or impertinent behaviour

Examples

The gall to ask
I can't believe he had the gall to ask for a raise right after being late again.
It takes a lot of gall to demand an apology after behaving so badly.
She showed remarkable gall by interrupting the meeting to criticize her manager.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chutzpah - extreme self-confidence or audacity
  • synonym: nerve - shameless boldness or audacity
  • antonym: timidity - lack of courage or confidence
