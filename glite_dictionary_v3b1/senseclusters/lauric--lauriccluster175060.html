lauric

adjective
containing lauric acid
Pronunciation: / ˈlɔrɪk / (AmE), / ˈlɔːrɪk / (BrE)
relating to or containing lauric acid, a fatty acid commonly found in coconut oil and palm kernel oil

Examples

Lauric acid content
Coconut oil is known for its high lauric acid content, which has various health benefits.
The soap manufacturer prefers lauric oils because they produce a rich lather.
Researchers studied the effects of lauric compounds on bacterial growth.
