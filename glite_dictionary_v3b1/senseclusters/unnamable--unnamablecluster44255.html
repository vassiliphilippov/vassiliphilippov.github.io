unnamable

adjective
impossible to describe
7.7
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈneɪməbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈneɪməbəl / (BrE)
impossible to name, describe, or express in words

Examples

The artist tried to paint the unnamable beauty of the sunset.
He felt an unnamable fear as he entered the abandoned house.
