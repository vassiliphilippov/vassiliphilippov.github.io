Chorizagrotis

proper noun
genus of moths with cutworm larvae
Pronunciation: / kəˌraɪzəˈgroʊtɪs / (AmE), / kɒˌrɪzəˈɡrəʊtɪs / (BrE)
A genus of moths whose larvae are known as army cutworms, some of which are agricultural pests.
