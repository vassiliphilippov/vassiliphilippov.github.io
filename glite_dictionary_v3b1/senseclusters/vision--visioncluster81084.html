vision

noun
machine interpretation of images/video
5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈvɪ.ʒən/ (AmE), /ˈvɪ.ʒən/ (BrE)
(in computing) the field or capability of systems and algorithms to interpret and understand visual input from images or video.

Examples

Computer vision system
Researchers trained a robot's vision system to recognize pedestrians in crowded city streets.
The company uses vision algorithms to inspect products for defects on the assembly line.
Students learned how vision models detect faces, track movement, and classify objects in videos.

Related Terms

  • synonym: computer vision - the branch of artificial intelligence that enables computers to interpret images and video
  • synonym: image recognition - the process of identifying objects, people, scenes, or actions in images or video
