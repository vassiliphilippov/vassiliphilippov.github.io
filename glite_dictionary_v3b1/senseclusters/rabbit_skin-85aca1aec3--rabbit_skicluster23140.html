rabbit skin

noun
animal-based glue
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈræbɪt skɪn / (AmE), / ˈræbɪt skɪn / (BrE)
a strong natural glue made by boiling rabbit skins, used by painters and woodworkers as an adhesive

Examples

Rabbit skin glue
The artist prepared her canvas with a layer of rabbit skin glue before painting.
Restorers often use rabbit skin as a traditional adhesive for repairing old wooden furniture.
He learned how to make rabbit skin glue from his grandfather, who was a skilled woodworker.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hide glue - a natural adhesive made from animal hides
