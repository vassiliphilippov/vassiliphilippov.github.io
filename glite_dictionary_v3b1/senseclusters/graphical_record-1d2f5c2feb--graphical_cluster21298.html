graphical record

noun
visual representation of data
Pronunciation: / ˈɡræfɪkəl ˈrɛkərd / (AmE), / ˈɡræfɪkəl ˈrɛkɔːd / (BrE)
a visual representation of numerical or statistical data, often plotted as points with reference to axes

Examples

A graphical record of data
A graphical record makes it easier to spot trends in the collected data.
The scientist presented a graphical record to illustrate the correlation between temperature and humidity.
They analyzed the graphical records to predict future sales.

Related Terms

  • synonym: graph - a diagram showing the relation between variable quantities
