Morisco

adjective
of Spanish Moriscos
Pronunciation: / mɔːˈriskoʊ / (AmE), / mɒˈrɪskəʊ / (BrE)
relating to the Moriscos, a group of former Muslims in Spain who converted to Christianity, or to their culture, customs, or language

Examples

The exhibition featured Morisco textiles that blended Islamic and Christian artistic styles.
Historians study Morisco documents to learn about cultural adaptation in early modern Spain.
The architecture in the old quarter shows clear Morisco influences in its decorative patterns.
