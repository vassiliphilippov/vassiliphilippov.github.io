noun
geological fault formed by compression

Examples

Synonyms

  • overthrust fault
    9.6 0.00/m
    overthrust fault forms when strong horizontal pressure pushes older rock over lower layers, while thrust fault involves upper rocks moving up over lower ones by compression.

Related Terms

  • antonym: normal fault - a fault where the hanging wall moves downward relative to the footwall
