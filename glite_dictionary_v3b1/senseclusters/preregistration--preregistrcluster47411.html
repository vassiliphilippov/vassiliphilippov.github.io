preregistration

noun
study plan registration
7.1
Pronunciation: /priː.re.dʒɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prɪ.re.dʒɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /priː.re.dʒɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /prɪ.re.dʒɪ.ˈstreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
the formal public registration of a research study's hypotheses, methods, or analysis plan before data collection begins

Examples

Research preregistration
Many scientific journals encourage preregistration to increase transparency in research.
The research team completed the preregistration of their study before collecting any data.
By requiring preregistration, the organization hopes to reduce bias in published results.
