multipath

adjective
multiple signal paths
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltipaθ / (AmE), / ˈmʌltipaːθ / (BrE)
relating to the transmission or reception of signals or data along multiple paths at the same time

Examples

Multipath signal transmission
Wireless networks often experience multipath interference due to signals reflecting off buildings.
The new receiver uses advanced technology to reduce errors caused by multipath effects.
Engineers must consider multipath propagation when designing communication systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: single-path - involving only one path for signal transmission
