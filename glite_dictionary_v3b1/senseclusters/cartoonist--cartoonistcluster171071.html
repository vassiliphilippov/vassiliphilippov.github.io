cartoonist

noun
a person who draws cartoons
Pronunciation: / kɑːrˈtuːnɪst / (AmE), / kɑːˈtuːnɪst / (BrE)
a person whose job is to draw cartoons, especially as a profession or for publication

Examples

A cartoonist draws cartoons
As a cartoonist, she finds inspiration in everyday life for her drawings.
Many famous cartoonists use humor to comment on politics and society.
The cartoonist submitted a new comic strip to the newspaper this morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: animator - a person who creates animations or animated movies
  • synonym: illustrator - a person who draws or creates pictures for books, magazines, etc.
