seq

noun
bibliographic abbreviation
9.9 0.21/m
Pronunciation: / siÀêk / (AmE), / siÀêk / (BrE)
a bibliographic abbreviation meaning 'and the following', used after a number to indicate subsequent items or pages

Examples

Page 10 seq
The instructions can be found on page 12 seq of the manual.
For more details, see section 5 seq in the report.
The law is explained in articles 100 seq of the code.

Synonyms

  • seqq.
    9.9 0.00/m
    seqq. is written in citations to show several following pages or sections, while seq is the shorter form placed after a number to show following pages.
