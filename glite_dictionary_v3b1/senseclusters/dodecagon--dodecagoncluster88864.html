dodecagon

noun
twelve-sided polygon
Pronunciation: / ˌdoʊˈdɛkəˌgɑn / (AmE), / ˌdəʊˈdɛkəɡən / (BrE)
A polygon with twelve straight sides and twelve angles.

Examples

A dodecagon shape
A dodecagon has exactly twelve sides and twelve angles.
In geometry class, we learned how to calculate the area of a regular dodecagon.
The artist designed the window in the shape of a dodecagon.
