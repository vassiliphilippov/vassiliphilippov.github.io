transpersonal

adjective
beyond the individual
9.0
Pronunciation: / trænsˈpɜrsənl / (AmE), / trænzˈpɜːsənl / (BrE)
relating to experiences, states, or aspects that extend beyond the individual self, often involving broader or spiritual dimensions

Examples

Transpersonal experiences
The psychologist specialized in transpersonal approaches that address spiritual growth.
Some meditation practices aim to create transpersonal experiences that connect individuals to a greater whole.
The book explores transpersonal states of consciousness beyond ordinary awareness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: personal - relating to or affecting a particular person
  • synonym: spiritual - relating to the human spirit or soul
