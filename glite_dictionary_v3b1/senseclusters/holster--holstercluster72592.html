holster

noun
holder or belt for carrying tools
7.5 4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhoʊl.stər/ (AmE), /ˈhəʊl.stə/ /; / /ˈhɒl.stə/ (BrE)
a case or holder worn on the body for carrying a handgun or small tools

Examples

A gun holster
His holster kept all his essential tools within easy reach during the repairs.
She reached into her tool holster for a wrench to tighten the bolt.
The electrician wore a holster full of screwdrivers and pliers while working on the wiring.
Leather holsters are popular among gun enthusiasts for their durability and style.
She made sure her revolver was secure in its holster before leaving the house.
The officer drew his pistol from the holster on his hip.
