hobblebush

noun
North American shrub
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑbəlˌbʊʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒbəlˌbʊʃ / (BrE)
a North American shrub with large leaves and clusters of white flowers, often found in forests

Examples

The hobblebush shrub
The hobblebush blooms in the spring along forest trails.
Hikers admired the broad leaves and white flowers of the hobblebush beside the path.
In autumn, the leaves of the hobblebush turn a striking shade of red.
