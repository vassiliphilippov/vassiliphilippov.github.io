dear

adjective
expensive; costing a lot
2.7
Pronunciation: / dɪr / (AmE), / dɪə / (BrE)
(especially British English, formal or old-fashioned) costing a lot; expensive.

Examples

Dear prices
Imported products are often quite dear in this country.
That dress looks lovely but it is rather dear at this price.
The rent for that apartment is much too dear for me to afford.
