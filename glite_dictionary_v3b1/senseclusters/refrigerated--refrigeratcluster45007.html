refrigerated

adjective
made or kept cold by refrigeration
Pronunciation: / rɪˈfrɪdʒəˌreɪtɪd / (AmE), / rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪtɪd / (BrE)
(of food, containers, or environments) made or kept cold by means of refrigeration to preserve freshness or safety.

Examples

Refrigerated container
It is recommended to store leftovers in a refrigerated container within two hours of cooking.
The meat must be refrigerated immediately after purchase to prevent spoilage.
We rented a refrigerated van to transport the vaccines to remote areas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chilled - cooled to a low temperature but not necessarily by mechanical refrigeration
