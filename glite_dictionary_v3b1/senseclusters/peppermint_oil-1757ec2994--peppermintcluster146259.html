peppermint oil

noun
essential oil from peppermint, used as flavoring
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛpərˌmɪnt ɔɪl / (AmE), / ˈpɛpəmɪnt ɔɪl / (BrE)
an essential oil obtained from the peppermint plant, used as a flavoring and for its medicinal properties

Examples

A drop of peppermint oil
Many people add a drop of peppermint oil to tea for its refreshing flavor.
The pharmacist recommended peppermint oil to help relieve digestive discomfort.
You can find peppermint oil in both health stores and supermarkets.
