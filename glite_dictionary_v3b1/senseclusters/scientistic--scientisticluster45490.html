scientistic

adjective
overly scientific
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪənˈtɪstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪənˈtɪstɪk / (BrE)
relating to a strong or excessive belief in science or scientific methods, sometimes ignoring other perspectives

Examples

Scientistic thinking
His scientistic approach ignored the social and ethical aspects of the problem.
Some critics argue that a scientistic worldview can overlook important human values.
The article was written in a scientistic style, focusing only on data and experiments.
The policy was criticized for its scientistic disregard of cultural traditions.
Her scientistic attitude led her to dismiss any non-scientific explanations.
Some argue that a scientistic worldview can overlook important ethical considerations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scientific - relating to science or the scientific method
  • antonym: unscientific - not based on or following scientific methods
