Cape primrose

noun
African houseplant with showy flowers
Pronunciation: / keɪp ˈpraɪmˌroʊz / (AmE), / keɪp ˈpraɪmˌrəʊz / (BrE)
Any of several African plants of the genus Streptocarpus, popular as houseplants for their showy blue or purple flowers.
