fendered

adjective
having fenders
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛndərd / (AmE), / ˈfɛndəd / (BrE)
equipped with or having a fender or fenders, especially on a vehicle

Examples

A fully fendered bicycle
The vintage bicycle was fully fendered to protect riders from mud and water.
Many classic cars are fendered, giving them a distinctive look compared to modern designs.
He preferred a fendered motorcycle for riding in wet weather.
