learner

noun
machine-learning model/program
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈlɜːr.nər/ (AmE), /ˈlɜː.nə/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially machine learning) a program, algorithm, or statistical model that improves its performance by learning from data and is used to make predictions or decisions.

Examples

Machine learner
The company tested a new learner that predicts customer churn from historical data.
Researchers compared several learners on the benchmark dataset to find the most accurate model.
After deployment, the learner continued updating its weights with streaming user feedback.

Related Terms

  • synonym: classifier - a type of learner that assigns items to categories based on input data
  • synonym: model - a mathematical or computational representation used to make predictions from data
