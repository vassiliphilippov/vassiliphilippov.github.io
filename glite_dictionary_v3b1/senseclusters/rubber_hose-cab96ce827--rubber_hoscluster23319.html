rubber hose

noun
flexible rubber tube
Pronunciation: / ˈrʌbər hoʊz / (AmE), / ˈrʌbə həʊz / (BrE)
a flexible tube made of rubber used to convey liquids or gases

Examples

A rubber hose pipe
A rubber hose can become brittle if left in the sun for too long.
She used a rubber hose to water the garden every morning.
The mechanic connected the rubber hose to the engine to check for leaks.
