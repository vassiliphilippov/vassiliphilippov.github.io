mopey

adjective
sad and low-energy
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊpi / (AmE), / ˈməʊpi / (BrE)
feeling sad, gloomy, or lacking energy, often in a quiet or withdrawn way

Examples

Feeling mopey today
He was feeling mopey after his team lost the game.
Don't be so mopey; things will get better soon.
She spent the afternoon mopey and quiet in her room.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cheerful - feeling happy and positive
  • synonym: gloomy - feeling sad or depressed
