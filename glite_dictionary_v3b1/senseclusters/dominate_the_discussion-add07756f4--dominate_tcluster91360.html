dominate the discussion

verb
control the conversation
1.0 4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːməˌneɪt ðə dɪˈskʌʃən / (AmE), / ˈdɒmɪneɪt ðə dɪˈskʌʃ.ən / (BrE)
to take control of or have a disproportionately large influence on a conversation or debate

Examples

To dominate the discussion strongly
He tends to dominate the discussion during our team meetings, leaving little room for others to speak.
Maria didn't want to dominate the discussion, so she encouraged everyone to share their thoughts.
Whenever controversial topics arise, John often dominates the discussion with strong opinions.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: contribute - to take part in a conversation without controlling it
  • synonym: monopolize the conversation - to take exclusive control of the conversation
