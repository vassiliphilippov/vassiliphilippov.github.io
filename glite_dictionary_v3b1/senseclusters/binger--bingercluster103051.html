binger

noun
person who binge-watches media
7.8
Pronunciation: /ˈbɪn.dʒər/ /; / /ˈbɪŋ.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈbɪn.dʒə/ /; / /ˈbɪŋ.dʒə/ (BrE)
informal: a person who repeatedly consumes large amounts of media in a short time, especially by binge-watching television shows or streaming series.

Examples

TV binger
After discovering the streaming series, she became a relentless binger who finished it in a weekend.
Weekend weather kept him indoors, so he turned into a binger of true crime documentaries.
Although usually moderate, he admitted being a binger when a new season dropped.
