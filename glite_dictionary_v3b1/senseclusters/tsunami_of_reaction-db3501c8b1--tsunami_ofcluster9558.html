tsunami of reaction

noun
an overwhelming surge of responses
1.7
Pronunciation: / tsuˈnɑmi əv riˈækʃən / (AmE), / tsuˈnɑːmi əv rɪˈækʃən / (BrE)
A sudden and overwhelming surge of responses or reactions, often following an event or announcement.

Examples

A tsunami of reaction hits
After the CEO resigned, the company faced a tsunami of reaction from investors and employees alike.
The celebrity's tweet triggered a tsunami of reaction from her followers.
The controversial law produced a tsunami of reaction across social media platforms.
