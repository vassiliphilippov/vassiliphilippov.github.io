miombo

noun
African woodland tree
9.9
Pronunciation: /miː.ˈɑːm.boʊ/ /; / /mɪ.ˈɑːm.boʊ/ (AmE), /miː.ˈɒm.bəʊ/ /; / /mɪ.ˈɒm.bəʊ/ (BrE)
a type of tropical African tree, especially from the genera Brachystegia, Julbernardia, or Isoberlinia, that dominates miombo woodlands

Examples

A miombo tree
The miombo provides important shade and resources for many animals in the woodland.
Local communities rely on miombo trees for firewood and building materials.
A single miombo can grow quite tall and is easily recognized by its broad canopy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Isoberlinia - a genus of trees native to African woodlands
  • synonym: Julbernardia - a genus of African trees found in miombo woodlands
