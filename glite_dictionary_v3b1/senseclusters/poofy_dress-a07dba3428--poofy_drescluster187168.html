poofy dress

noun
dress with a puffed-out shape
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈpuːfi drɛs / (AmE), / ˈpuːfi drɛs / (BrE)
A dress that has a full, puffed-out shape, often created with layers or extra fabric.

Examples

A poofy dress
She looked stunning in her poofy dress at the wedding.
The little girl spun around, making her poofy dress flare out even more.
Vintage fashion often includes the iconic poofy dress popular in the 1950s.
