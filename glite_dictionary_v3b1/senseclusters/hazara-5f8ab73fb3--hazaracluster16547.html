Hazara

noun
Afghan ethnic group
9.6
Pronunciation: / həˈzɑːrə / (AmE), / həˈzɑːrə / (BrE)
a member of an ethnic group primarily living in central Afghanistan, known for their distinct culture and language

Examples

A Hazara person
The Hazara have a long history in the mountainous regions of central Afghanistan.
Many Hazara communities maintain their own language and traditions.
He learned about the art and music of the Hazara during his travels.

Related Terms
