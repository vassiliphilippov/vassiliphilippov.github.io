science fiction

noun
fictional genre involving advanced science or technology
1.0 2.3
Pronunciation: /ˈsaɪ.əns ˈfɪk.ʃən/ (AmE), /ˈsaɪ.əns ˈfɪk.ʃən/ (BrE)
A literary genre that deals with imaginative concepts such as advanced science and technology, space exploration, time travel, and their impact on society.
