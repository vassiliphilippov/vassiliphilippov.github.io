polyposis

noun
multiple polyps condition
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑliˈpoʊsɪs / (AmE), / ˌpɒlɪˈpəʊsɪs / (BrE)
a medical condition in which numerous polyps develop in an organ, especially the colon

Examples

Colon polyposis
The patient was diagnosed with polyposis after several growths were found during the examination.
Family history can increase the risk of developing polyposis in the colon.
Treatment for polyposis often involves regular monitoring and sometimes surgery to remove the polyps.

Related Terms

  • synonym: polyp - a small growth projecting from a mucous membrane
