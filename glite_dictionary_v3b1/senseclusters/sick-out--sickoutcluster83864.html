sick-out

noun
workplace protest action
8.2
Pronunciation: /ˈsɪk.aʊt/ /; / /sɪk aʊt/ (AmE), /ˈsɪk.aʊt/ /; / /sɪk aʊt/ (BrE)
a form of protest in which workers collectively call in sick to express dissatisfaction or demand change

Examples

Union sick-out protest
The nurses organized a sick-out to protest unsafe working conditions at the hospital.
A sick-out by bus drivers caused major delays across the city this morning.
Management responded quickly after employees staged a sick-out demanding higher wages.
