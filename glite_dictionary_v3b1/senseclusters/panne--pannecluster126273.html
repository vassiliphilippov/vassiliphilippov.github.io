panne

noun
lustrous velvet fabric
9.1
Pronunciation: / pæn / (AmE), / pan / (BrE)
a type of velvet fabric with a shiny, flattened surface created by pressing the pile in one direction

Examples

Panne velvet fabric
The designer chose panne for the evening gown to give it a luxurious sheen.
Her jacket was made of deep blue panne that caught the light beautifully.
They admired the soft texture of the panne used in the upholstery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: satin - a smooth, glossy fabric
  • synonym: velvet - a soft fabric with a dense pile
