phagocytize

verb
to absorb completely
Pronunciation: / fəˈɡɑːsəˌtaɪz / (AmE), / fəˈɡəʊsɪtaɪz / (BrE)
to absorb, engulf, or completely incorporate something, often used figuratively for organizations or entities.

Examples

To phagocytize a company
The larger company aimed to phagocytize its smaller competitors to dominate the industry.
White blood cells phagocytize harmful bacteria to protect the body from infection.
The new policy allowed the agency to phagocytize several independent departments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: engulf - to completely surround or swallow up
  • antonym: release - to let go or set free
