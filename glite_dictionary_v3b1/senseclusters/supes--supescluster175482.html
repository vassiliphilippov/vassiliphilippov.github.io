supes

verb
to serve subpoena
9.9
Pronunciation: / suːps / (AmE), / suːps / (BrE)
to serve a subpoena on someone or order them to appear in court, especially in informal usage.

Examples

To supes a witness

If the witness does not respond, the district attorney supes them to appear next week.
Every time someone ignores a summons, the office supes them again.
