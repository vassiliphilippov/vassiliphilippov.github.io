nonjudicial

adjective
outside judicial process
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑndʒuˈdɪʃəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒndʒuːˈdɪʃəl / (BrE)
not involving a court, judge, or the formal judicial process

Examples

A nonjudicial process
The company used a nonjudicial process to resolve the contract dispute.
Foreclosure can sometimes proceed through nonjudicial means, avoiding the court system.
They agreed to a nonjudicial settlement to save time and legal costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: extrajudicial - not authorized by or not involving the court system
  • antonym: judicial - relating to courts or judges
