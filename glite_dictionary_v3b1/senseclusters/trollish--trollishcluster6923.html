trollish

adjective
provocative or disruptive
Pronunciation: / ˈtroʊlɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈtrɒlɪʃ / (BrE)
behaving in a deliberately annoying, provocative, or disruptive way, especially on the internet

Examples

Trollish comments online
The forum moderator warned users about posting trollish remarks that could upset others.
His trollish behavior online made it difficult for anyone to have a serious discussion.
Ignoring trollish comments is often the best way to prevent further arguments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disruptive - causing disorder or interruption
  • synonym: provocative - intended to cause a reaction or annoyance
  • antonym: respectful - showing politeness or consideration
