cagily

adverb
in a cagey or evasive way
Pronunciation: / ˈkeɪ.dʒɪ.li / (AmE), / ˈkeɪ.dʒɪ.li / (BrE)
In a cagey manner; acting in a cautious, shrewd, or evasive way.

Examples

Answer cagily
He answered cagily, not revealing his true intentions.
She cagily avoided giving a direct response to the difficult question.
The politician spoke cagily when pressed about the controversial issue.
