creamer

noun
coffee additive
Pronunciation: / ˈkriːmər / (AmE), / ˈkriːmə / (BrE)
a liquid or powder added to coffee or tea to make it taste creamier, often as a substitute for milk or cream

Examples

Coffee creamer powder
She always puts a little creamer in her morning coffee.
Some people prefer non-dairy creamer because they are lactose intolerant.
The hotel room included packets of instant coffee and powdered creamer for guests.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cream - the thick, fatty part of milk used in drinks and cooking
  • synonym: milk - a white liquid produced by mammals, often added to coffee or tea
