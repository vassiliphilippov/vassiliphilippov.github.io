hen

noun
adult female chicken
Pronunciation: / hɛn / (AmE), / hɛn / (BrE)
an adult female chicken, or more generally, an adult female bird (especially one kept for laying eggs)

Examples

A hen lays eggs
The hen laid three eggs this morning.
The farmer bought ten more hens for his coop.
They keep a hen and a rooster in their backyard.
Every evening, the hen returns to the coop to roost.
On the farm, a mother hen watched over her chicks protectively.
The hen laid three eggs yesterday morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chicken - a domestic fowl, Gallus gallus domesticus
  • antonym: rooster - an adult male chicken
