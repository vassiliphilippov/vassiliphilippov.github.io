bonelessly

adverb
limply or weakly
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊnlɪsli / (AmE), / ˈbəʊnlɪsli / (BrE)
in a way that is limp, lacking firmness, or without apparent strength or structure

Examples

Collapsed bonelessly
After the exhausting hike, he sank bonelessly into his chair and closed his eyes.
The cat stretched bonelessly across the sunny windowsill, barely moving all afternoon.
She fell bonelessly onto the bed, too tired to even pull up the covers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: firmly - in a strong or steady way
  • synonym: limply - in a way that lacks firmness or strength
