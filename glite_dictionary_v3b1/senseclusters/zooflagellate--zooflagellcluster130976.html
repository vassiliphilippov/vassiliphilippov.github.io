zooflagellate

noun
a protozoan with flagella, not photosynthetic
9.9
Pronunciation: / zoʊˈflædʒəˌleɪt / (AmE), / zəʊˈflædʒɪlət / (BrE)
a flagellate protozoan that lacks chlorophyll and does not perform photosynthesis, distinguished from plant-like flagellates

Examples

A zooflagellate protozoan
Researchers observed a zooflagellate swimming rapidly under the microscope.
Some zooflagellates live in the digestive tracts of termites and help break down cellulose.
Unlike plant-like flagellates, zooflagellates obtain nutrients by absorbing them from their environment.

Related Terms

  • synonym: protozoan - a single-celled microscopic organism of the kingdom Protista
