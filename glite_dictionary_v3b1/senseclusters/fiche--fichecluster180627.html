fiche

noun
information card or token
Pronunciation: / fiːʃ / (AmE), / fiːʃ / (BrE)
a small card or token used to record information or as a counter in games or systems

Examples

Attendance fiche
Each student received a fiche to record their attendance.
The casino uses a colored fiche as a betting chip at the tables.
Please hand in your fiche at the end of the session to confirm your participation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: card - a piece of stiff paper used for recording or identification
  • synonym: chip - a small disc used as a counter in games like poker
  • synonym: token - a small object used as a symbol or substitute, often in games
