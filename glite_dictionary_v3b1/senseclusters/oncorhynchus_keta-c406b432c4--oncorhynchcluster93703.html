Oncorhynchus keta

noun
a species of Pacific salmon
9.9
Pronunciation: / ɑŋkəˈraɪŋkəs ˈkiːtə / (AmE), / ɒŋkɒˈrɪŋkəs ˈkiːtə / (BrE)
a large species of Pacific salmon with small spots on its back, important as a food fish

Examples

Large Oncorhynchus keta salmon
