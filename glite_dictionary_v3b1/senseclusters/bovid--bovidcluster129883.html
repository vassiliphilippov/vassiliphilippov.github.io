bovid

adjective
relating to cattle or the Bovidae family
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊ.vɪd / (AmE), / ˈbəʊ.vɪd / (BrE)
relating to or belonging to the genus Bos (cattle) or the family Bovidae, which includes cattle, bison, buffalo, and antelopes

Examples

Bovid species
Many bovid animals, such as antelopes and buffalo, play important roles in the ecosystem.
Researchers compared DNA samples from several bovid populations across Africa.
The bovid skull displayed in the museum comes from an ancient species of wild cattle.
