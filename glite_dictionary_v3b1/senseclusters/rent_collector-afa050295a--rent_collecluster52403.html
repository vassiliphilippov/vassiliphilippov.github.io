rent collector

noun
person collecting rents on behalf of owner
5.1
Pronunciation: / rɛnt kəˈlɛktər / (AmE), / rɛnt kəˈlɛktə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to go from house to house collecting rents for a property owner

Examples

Rent collector job
She worked as a rent collector for several years before finding a new career.
The rent collector visited each apartment building at the end of the month.
When the rent collector arrived, the tenants handed over their payments in cash.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landlord - a person who rents out land, a building, or accommodation
  • antonym: tenant - a person who rents land or property from a landlord
