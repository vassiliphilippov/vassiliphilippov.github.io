formality

noun
an established procedure or social custom
1.0 3.6
Pronunciation: /fɔːr.ˈmæl.ə.tiː/ /; / /fɔːr.ˈmæl.ɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /fɔː.ˈmæl.ə.tiː/ /; / /fɔː.ˈmæl.ɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
an established action, procedure, or requirement that follows rules, customs, or etiquette, often done out of tradition or convention
