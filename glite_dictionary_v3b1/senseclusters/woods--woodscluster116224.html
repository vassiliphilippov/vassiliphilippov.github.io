woods

noun
a large area of trees; forest
Pronunciation: / wʊdz / (AmE), / wʊdz / (BrE)
a large area covered chiefly with trees and undergrowth; a forest, especially one smaller than a forest but larger than a grove

Examples

The woods near home
After the rain, the woods smelled fresh and earthy.
The mysterious cabin was hidden deep in the woods.
They went for a walk in the woods near their house.
