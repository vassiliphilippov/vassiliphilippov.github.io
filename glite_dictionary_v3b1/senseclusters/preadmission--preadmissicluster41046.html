preadmission

adjective
before official entry
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːədˈmɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌpriːədˈmɪʃən / (BrE)
happening, required, or provided before someone is officially admitted to a place, program, or institution

Examples

Preadmission test
All patients must complete a preadmission screening before being scheduled for surgery.
The university requires a preadmission interview for all international students.
She attended a preadmission orientation session before starting her new job.

Related Terms

  • antonym: postadmission - happening after someone is officially admitted
  • synonym: preliminary - happening before something important as a preparation
