incubation

noun
support program for startups
5.3
Pronunciation: /ɪŋ.kjuː.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.kjuː.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /ɪŋ.kjuː.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ /; / /ɪn.kjuː.ˈbeɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a program or supportive environment provided (often by a business incubator) to help early-stage startups develop through mentoring, office space, resources, and networking.

Examples

Incubation program for startups
The city launched an incubation program to support biotech startups.
During incubation, founders received office space, mentorship, and access to investor networks.
Several startups left the incubation with prototype products and early paying customers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: business incubator - an organization that offers space, support, and resources to help new businesses grow
  • synonym: startup accelerator - a time-limited program providing intensive mentoring, resources, and often investment to startups
