primus

noun
leading person
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈpraɪməs / (AmE), / ˈprʌɪməs / (BrE)
the most important or leading person in a group or organization

Examples

Team primus
After years of dedication, she became the primus of the research team.
In the council, the primus is responsible for making the final decision.
He was recognized as the primus among his peers for his leadership skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chief - the person with the highest rank or authority
  • antonym: follower - a person who supports and is guided by another
  • synonym: leader - a person who leads or commands a group
