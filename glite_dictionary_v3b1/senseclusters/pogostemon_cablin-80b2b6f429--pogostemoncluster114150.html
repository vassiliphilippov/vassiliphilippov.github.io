Pogostemon cablin

noun
aromatic shrub; source of patchouli oil
Pronunciation: / poʊˈgoʊstəˌmɒn ˈkæblɪn / (AmE), / pəˈɡɒstɪˌmɒn ˈkab.lɪn / (BrE)
A small, East Indian shrubby mint whose fragrant leaves produce oil used in perfumes, also known as patchouli.
