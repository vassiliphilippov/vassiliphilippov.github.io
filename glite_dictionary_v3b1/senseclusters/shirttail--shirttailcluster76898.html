shirttail

noun
brief addendum to a news article
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɝːtˌteɪl / (AmE), / ˈʃɜːtˌteɪl / (BrE)
An additional short note or comment at the end of a newspaper article.

Examples

Article shirttail
Please check if there is a shirttail before sending the article to print.
Sometimes, a shirttail provides context that didn't fit in the main body of the piece.
The editor added a shirttail to the story clarifying a minor detail.
