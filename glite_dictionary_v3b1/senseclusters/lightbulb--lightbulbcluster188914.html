Synonyms

  • light bulb
    1.8 3.24/m
    light bulb is the common two-word spelling seen in everyday writing in English, while lightbulb is its single-word form.
  • electric light bulb
    2.1 0.04/m
    electric light bulb emphasizes that the lamp uses electricity and sounds more specific, while lightbulb is the usual everyday term.
  • incandescent lamp
    4.1 0.24/m
    incandescent lamp is the technical, formal name often used in stores or science, while lightbulb is the casual everyday word people use.
  • bulb
    1.3 11/m
    bulb is a short, informal word that can mean a plant or a lamp, while lightbulb clearly means the electric lamp.
