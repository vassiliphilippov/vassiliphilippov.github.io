bulletproofing

noun
making bullet-resistant
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of making something resistant to bullets, especially for protection or security

Examples

Bulletproofing a car
The company specializes in bulletproofing vehicles for security purposes.
After bulletproofing the windows, the bank felt much safer against potential threats.
Advances in bulletproofing technology have made protective gear lighter and more effective.
