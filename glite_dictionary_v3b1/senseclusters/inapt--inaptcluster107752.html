inapt

adjective
not suitable; awkward in expression
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæpt / (AmE), / ɪˈnapt / (BrE)
not appropriate or lacking elegance and grace in expression; awkward or unsuitable in the context.

Examples

An inapt reply
Her inapt reply made the situation even more uncomfortable.
His choice of words was inapt for such a solemn occasion.
The comedian's inapt comment fell flat with the audience.

Related Terms

  • antonym: apt - appropriate or suitable in the circumstances
  • synonym: inept - lacking skill or ability; incompetent
