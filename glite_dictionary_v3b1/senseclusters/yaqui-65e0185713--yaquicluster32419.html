Yaqui

proper noun
indigenous people
Pronunciation: / ˈjɑːki / (AmE), / ˈjɑːki / (BrE)
a member of an indigenous people native to northern Mexico and the southwestern United States

Examples

A Yaqui person
The Yaqui have preserved many of their traditional customs and ceremonies.
A group of Yaqui artists displayed their work at the cultural festival.
Many Yaqui live in the Sonoran region of Mexico and Arizona.
