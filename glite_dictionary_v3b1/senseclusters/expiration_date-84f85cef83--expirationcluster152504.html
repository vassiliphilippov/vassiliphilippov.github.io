expiration date

noun
date marking end of product's usability
Pronunciation: / ˌɛkspəˈreɪʃən deɪt / (AmE), / ˌɛkspɪˈreɪʃən deɪt / (BrE)
the date printed on food, medicine, or other products indicating the last day it is considered safe or effective to use

Examples

The expiration date on medicine
Be sure to check the expiration date on the medicine before taking it.
He threw away the yogurt because the expiration date was last week.
Many grocery stores remove items from shelves after their expiration date has passed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: best-before date - date up to which food retains best quality
