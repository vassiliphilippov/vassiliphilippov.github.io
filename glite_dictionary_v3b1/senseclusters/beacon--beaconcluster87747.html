beacon

verb
to attract attention
6.0 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàbiÀêk…ôn / (AmE), / ÀàbiÀêk…ôn / (BrE)
to attract people, attention, or support by shining brightly or standing out, like a beacon.

Examples

To beacon visitors
The festival lights were beaconing visitors from all over the city.
Her inspiring speech beaconed hope to everyone in the audience.
The new community center is beaconing support from local residents.
