tabular matter

noun
information arranged in a table
Pronunciation: / ˈtæbjələr ˈmætər / (AmE), / ˈtabjʊlə ˈmatə / (BrE)
information presented in the form of a table or tables, especially in written or printed work

Examples

Tabular matter in reports
Editors required all data to be included as tabular matter for clarity and easy reference.
Much of the appendix consisted of tabular matter displaying the survey results.
The statistical report contained extensive tabular matter summarizing annual growth figures.

Related Terms

  • synonym: table - a set of data arranged in rows and columns
