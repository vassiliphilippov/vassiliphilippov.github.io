molten

adjective
liquefied by heat
5.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊltən / (AmE), / ˈməʊltən / (BrE)
made liquid by being heated to a very high temperature

Examples

Molten glass
During the glassblowing process, sand is heated until it becomes molten glass.
Streams of molten lava flowed down the sides of the volcano.
The blacksmith poured the molten metal into the mold to shape a new sword.

Related Terms

  • synonym: melted - having undergone melting; in a liquid state due to heat
