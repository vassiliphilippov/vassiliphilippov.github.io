goliath frog

noun
largest living frog species of Africa
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈlaɪəθ frɑɡ / (AmE), / ɡəˈlaɪəθ frɒɡ / (BrE)
the largest living frog species, native to central Africa, reaching lengths of up to a foot and weighing as much as 10 pounds
