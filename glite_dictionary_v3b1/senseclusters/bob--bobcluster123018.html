bob

noun
fishing line float
8.3
Pronunciation: / bɑb / (AmE), / bɒb / (BrE)
a small float, typically made of cork or plastic, attached to a fishing line to indicate when a fish bites.

Examples

Fishing bob
A red and white bob drifted gently on the surface of the lake.
She attached the bob to the line before casting her fishing rod.
The fisherman watched the bob closely for any sign of movement.
