cocoa

noun

A hot drink made from cocoa powder, milk, and sugar, usually served warm or hot.

Examples

A cup of cocoa
On cold winter nights, I love to drink a cup of cocoa by the fireplace.
She prepared mugs of steaming cocoa for her children after they finished playing in the snow.
Would you like some cocoa with marshmallows or just plain?

Synonyms

  • chocolate
    1.0 4.75/m
    chocolate is the everyday name for the sweet hot drink, while cocoa refers to the same drink made from cocoa powder.
  • drinking chocolate
    1.1 0.01/m
    drinking chocolate is a thicker, richer hot beverage often made from melted chocolate, while cocoa is a simpler drink made from cocoa powder.
  • hot chocolate
    1.0 1.85/m
    hot chocolate can be made with either melted chocolate or cocoa powder and milk, while cocoa usually means the powder-based warm drink.
