nauseousness

noun
feeling sick
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈnɔːʃəsnəs / (AmE), / ˈnɔːzɪəsnəs / (BrE)
the state of feeling sick to the stomach or wanting to vomit

Examples

Sudden nauseousness
After eating the spoiled food, he was overcome by a wave of nauseousness.
The spinning ride at the amusement park caused intense nauseousness for several passengers.
She tried to ignore her nauseousness during the long car journey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nausea - a feeling of sickness with an urge to vomit
  • antonym: wellness - the state of being in good health
