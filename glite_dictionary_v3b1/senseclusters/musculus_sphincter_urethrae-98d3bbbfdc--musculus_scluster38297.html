musculus sphincter urethrae

noun
urethral constrictor muscle
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌs.kjə.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tər juːˈriː.θriː / (AmE), / ˈmʌs.kjʊ.ləs ˈsfɪŋk.tə juːˈriː.θriː / (BrE)
A striated sphincter muscle that encircles and constricts the urethra, helping control urination.

Examples

The musculus sphincter urethrae muscle
Contraction of the musculus sphincter urethrae allows voluntary control over the release of urine.
Damage to the musculus sphincter urethrae can result in urinary incontinence after prostate surgery.
The surgeon carefully preserved the musculus sphincter urethrae during the procedure to prevent complications.

Related Terms

  • synonym: external urethral sphincter - A voluntary muscle that controls urination in both sexes
