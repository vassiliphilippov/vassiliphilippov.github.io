uncharitably

adverb
harshly or unkindly
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtʃærɪtəbli / (AmE), / ʌnˈtʃærɪtəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is harsh, unkind, or lacking generosity toward others

Examples

Speak uncharitably about
She spoke uncharitably about her neighbor's mistakes at the meeting.
The critics reviewed the film uncharitably, focusing only on its flaws.
He judged his friend's actions uncharitably without knowing the full story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: charitably - in a kind or generous way
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or unkind manner
  • synonym: unkindly - in a way that is not kind or considerate
