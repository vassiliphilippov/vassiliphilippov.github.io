basket flower

noun
ornamental annual plant with basket-like bracts

Pronunciation: /ˈbæ.skɪt ˈflaʊ.ər/ /; / /ˈbæ.skɪt ˈfloʊ.ər/ (AmE), /ˈbɑː.skɪt ˈflaʊ.ə/ /; / /ˈbɑː.skɪt ˈfləʊ.ə/ (BrE)
