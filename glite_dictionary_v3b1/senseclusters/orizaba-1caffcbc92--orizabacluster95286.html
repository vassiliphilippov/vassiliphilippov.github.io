Orizaba

proper noun
city in east central Mexico
Pronunciation: / ɔːrɪˈzɑːbə / (AmE), / ɔːrɪˈzɑːbə / (BrE)
A city in east central Mexico, west of Veracruz, known as a popular resort.

Examples

Orizaba city
After landing in Veracruz, we drove to Orizaba to explore the colonial architecture.

The annual carnival in Orizaba attracts visitors from across Mexico.
