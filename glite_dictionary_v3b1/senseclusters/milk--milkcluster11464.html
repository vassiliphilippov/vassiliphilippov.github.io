milk

verb
exploit to the maximum
8.9 6.2
Pronunciation: /mɪlk/ (AmE), /mɪlk/ (BrE)
(informal) to exploit a situation or resource to the greatest possible extent, often for personal gain.

Examples

The company tried to milk the patent for all the revenue it could generate.
