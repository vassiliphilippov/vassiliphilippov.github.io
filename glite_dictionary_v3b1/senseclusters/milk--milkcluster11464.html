milk

verb
exploit to the maximum
Pronunciation: / mɪlk / (AmE), / mɪlk / (BrE)
(informal) to exploit a situation or resource to the greatest possible extent, often for personal gain.

Examples

To milk the situation
He was milking the situation for every bit of publicity he could get.
It seemed like she was milking his sympathy after her minor accident.
The company tried to milk the patent for all the revenue it could generate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exploit - to make full use of or derive benefit from
