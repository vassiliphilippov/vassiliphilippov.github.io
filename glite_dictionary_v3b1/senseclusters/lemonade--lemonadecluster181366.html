lemonade

noun
sweetened lemon-flavored drink
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛm.əˈneɪd / (AmE), / ˌlɛm.əˈneɪd / (BrE)
A sweetened drink made from lemon juice, water, and usually sugar.

Examples

A glass of lemonade
She poured a glass of lemonade to cool off on the hot afternoon.
The children set up a lemonade stand at the end of the driveway.
Would you like some homemade lemonade with your lunch?
