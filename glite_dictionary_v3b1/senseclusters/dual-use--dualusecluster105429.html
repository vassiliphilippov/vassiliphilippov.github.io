dual-use

adjective
serving two purposes

designed or suitable for serving two different purposes (often used for things that can be used for both civilian and military purposes)

Examples

Dual-use goods
Many dual-use goods require special licensing before they can be exported.
The laboratory developed a dual-use technology that could be used for both civilian and military applications.
