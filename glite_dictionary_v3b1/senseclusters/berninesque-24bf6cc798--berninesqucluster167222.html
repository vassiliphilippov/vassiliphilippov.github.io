Berninesque

adjective
in Bernini's style
9.9
Pronunciation: / bɜːrˈniːnɛsk / (AmE), / bɜːˈniːnɛsk / (BrE)
in the style or manner of the sculptor Gian Lorenzo Bernini, especially reflecting Baroque grandeur and drama

Examples

Berninesque style
The museum's new wing features Berninesque sculptures that capture movement and emotion.
Her design for the plaza included a Berninesque fountain as its centerpiece.
The ornate ceiling was decorated in a Berninesque fashion, full of dramatic curves and figures.
