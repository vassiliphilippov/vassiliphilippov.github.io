smartwatch

noun
digital connected watch
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsmɑːrtˌwɑːtʃ /; / ˈsmɑrtˌwɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈsmɑːtˌwɒtʃ / (BrE)
a digital or electronic watch worn on the wrist that tells time and provides smart features like notifications, messaging, and fitness tracking
