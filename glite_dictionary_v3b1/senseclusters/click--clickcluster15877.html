click

noun
pressing a mouse button
1.0
Pronunciation: / klɪk / (AmE), / klɪk / (BrE)
an act of pressing and releasing a button on a computer mouse or similar device

Examples

A mouse click
She selected the file and made a quick click to open it.
The sound of the mouse click echoed in the quiet office.
With a single click, you can open the document on your desktop.
