flotant

adjective
floating or buoyant
Pronunciation: / floʊˈtɑnt / (AmE), / fləʊˈtɑnt / (BrE)
floating on or in water; buoyant.

Examples

Flotant lily pads
The lily pads were flotant on the surface of the pond.
After the storm, debris remained flotant in the flooded streets.
The artist painted a scene with flotant leaves drifting down the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buoyant - able to float or rise in a liquid
  • antonym: sinking - going down below the surface of water
