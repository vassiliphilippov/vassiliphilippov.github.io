changeling child

noun
mythical child substituted by fairies
9.9 8.7
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃeɪn.dʒlɪŋ tʃaɪld/ (AmE), /ˈtʃeɪn.dʒlɪŋ tʃaɪld/ (BrE)
(in folklore) a child believed to have been secretly substituted by fairies for the parents' real child.

A changeling child in folklore
In some legends, a changeling child could only be revealed through special rituals.
Old stories describe families discovering a changeling child after their baby mysteriously changed behavior.
