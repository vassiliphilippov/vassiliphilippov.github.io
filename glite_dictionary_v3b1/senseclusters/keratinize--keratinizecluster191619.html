keratinize

verb
to become impregnated with keratin
8.0
Pronunciation: / kəˈræt̬.ə.naɪz /; / ˈkɛr.ə.təˌnaɪz /; / ˈkɛr.ə.tɪˌnaɪz / (AmE), / kəˈræt.ɪ.naɪz /; / ˈkɛr.ə.tɪˌnaɪz / (BrE)
(of cells or tissue) to become filled with keratin and harden or toughen

Examples
