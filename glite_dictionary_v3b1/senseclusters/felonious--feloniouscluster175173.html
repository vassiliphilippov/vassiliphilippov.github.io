felonious

adjective
relating to a felony or serious crime
Pronunciation: / fəˈloʊniəs / (AmE), / fɪˈləʊniəs / (BrE)
Relating to or constituting a felony; involving or having the nature of a serious crime.

Examples

Felonious assault
He was charged with felonious assault after the incident downtown.
The evidence revealed a felonious intent to commit burglary.
The prosecutor argued that the defendant's actions were clearly felonious and warranted a severe penalty.
