surgical dressing

noun
protective wound cover used after surgery
4.3 3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝdʒɪkəl ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːdʒɪkəl ˈdrɛsɪŋ / (BrE)
A sterile cotton or gauze covering applied to protect incisions or wounds after surgery.

Examples

A surgical dressing cover
After the stitches were removed, the doctor replaced the surgical dressing with a smaller adhesive bandage.

The nurse carefully applied a clean surgical dressing to the patient's incision after the operation.
