puppetmaster

noun
puppet operator
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌpɪtˌmæstər / (AmE), / ˈpʌpɪtˌmɑːstə / (BrE)
a person who operates and controls puppets during performances or shows

Examples

A skilled puppetmaster
The talented puppetmaster brought the marionettes to life on stage, delighting the audience.
Children gathered around to watch the puppetmaster perform at the street fair.
After years of practice, she became a renowned puppetmaster known for her creative shows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: puppeteer - a person who manipulates and controls puppets
