vent-hole

noun
opening for release of air or gas
4.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛntˌhoʊl / (AmE), / ˈventˌhəʊl / (BrE)
A small hole or opening made for the escape of gas, air, or vapor.

Examples

A vent-hole for steam
Steam escaped from a tiny vent-hole at the top of the pressure cooker.
The miners drilled a vent-hole to release dangerous gases from the tunnel.
The technician checked each vent-hole to ensure proper airflow through the system.
