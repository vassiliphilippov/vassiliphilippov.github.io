yuri

noun
genre about women’s romance
Pronunciation: / ˈjʊri / (AmE), / ˈjʊəri / (BrE)
a genre of Japanese media that focuses on romantic or emotional relationships between women

Examples

Yuri manga series
She enjoys reading yuri manga in her free time.
The new anime series is popular among fans of yuri stories.
Many bookstores in Japan have a section dedicated to yuri comics.

Related Terms
