unsettledness

noun
state of unease
5.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (AmE), / ʌnˈsɛtəldnəs / (BrE)
the state of being uneasy, unstable, or not resolved

Examples

Unsettledness in the office
There was a sense of unsettledness in the office after the sudden changes.
Her unsettledness grew as she waited for the test results.
Periods of unsettledness can make it difficult to focus on daily tasks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calmness - the state of being peaceful and free from disturbance
  • synonym: instability - the state of being likely to change or not firmly established
  • synonym: uncertainty - the state of being unsure or not having confidence
