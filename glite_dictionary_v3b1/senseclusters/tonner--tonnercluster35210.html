tonner

noun
vehicle by tonnage
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌnər / (AmE), / ˈtʌnə / (BrE)
a vehicle or vessel with a capacity or weight of a specified number of tons

Examples

A tonner vehicle
The company purchased a new five-tonner for transporting goods.
They loaded the crates onto the tonner before heading to the warehouse.
A two-tonner is often used for smaller deliveries in the city.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lorry - a large vehicle for transporting goods by road
  • synonym: truck - a large road vehicle for carrying goods
