foursquare

adjective, adverb
in a square or solid position
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrˌskwɛr / (AmE), / ˈfɔːˌskwɛː /; / ˈfɔːˌskweə / (BrE)
square in shape or placed in a solid, square position

Examples

Foursquare on the ground
He placed the crate foursquare on the floor, making sure it wouldn't tip over.
The house sat foursquare on its foundation, sturdy and immovable.
The monument stood foursquare against the horizon, unshaken by the wind.
The ancient temple stood on a foursquare base made of stone.
They admired the foursquare design of the new city plaza.
The artist painted a foursquare frame around the central image.

Related Terms

  • synonym: square - having four equal sides and four right angles
  • synonym: squarely - in a direct, straight, or square position or manner
