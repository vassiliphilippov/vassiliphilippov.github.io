devil's darning needle

noun
climbing plant with creamy white flowers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɑrnɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (AmE), / ˈdɛvəlz ˈdɑːnɪŋ ˌniːdəl / (BrE)
A common climbing plant of eastern North America (Clematis virginiana) that sprawls over other plants and produces panicles of small creamy white flowers.
