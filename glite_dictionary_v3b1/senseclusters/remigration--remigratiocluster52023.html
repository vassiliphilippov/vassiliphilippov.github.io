remigration

noun
migrating again
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˌriːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːmaɪˈɡreɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of migrating again, especially after having migrated before, sometimes returning to a previous place

Examples

The remigration process
After several years abroad, his remigration brought him to a new continent.
The family considered remigration after facing challenges in their new country.
Economic opportunities often influence the decision for remigration among migrant workers.
After several years abroad, his remigration to his hometown was celebrated by his family.
The study focused on the remigration of workers who had left the city during the recession.
Government policies can influence the rate of remigration among former residents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emigration - the act of leaving one's own country to settle in another
  • synonym: immigration - the act of coming to live permanently in a foreign country
  • synonym: migration - the movement from one place to another, especially to live or work
  • synonym: return - the act of going back to a previous place
