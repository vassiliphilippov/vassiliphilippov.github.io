Examples

To be intoxicated with alcohol
Even a small amount of alcohol can leave some people feeling intoxicated.
He felt intoxicated by the thrill of winning the championship.
She was clearly intoxicated after several glasses of wine at the party.
Guests were asked to leave if they appeared intoxicated during the party.
She had never seen her friend so intoxicated before.
The officer suspected the driver was intoxicated after noticing erratic movements.

Synonyms

  • under the influence
    5.8 2.01/m
    under the influence is a legal and neutral phrase for being impaired by alcohol or drugs, while intoxicated is a general medical word.
  • drunk
    1.6 42/m
    drunk is a casual word for being impaired by alcohol, while intoxicated covers both alcohol and drugs and sounds more formal.
  • inebriated
    6.5 0.34/m
    inebriated is a formal or old-fashioned word meaning drunk from alcohol, while intoxicated also includes impairment caused by drugs.
