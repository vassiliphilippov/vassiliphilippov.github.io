collared pika

noun
small mammal of northwestern North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑlɚd ˈpaɪkə / (AmE), / ˈkɒləd ˈpiːkə / (BrE)
a small, rodent-like mammal of the species Ochotona collaris, found in Alaska and northwestern Canada, resembling a rabbit but with short ears and no tail

Examples

Researchers observed a collared pika storing grass for the winter months.
