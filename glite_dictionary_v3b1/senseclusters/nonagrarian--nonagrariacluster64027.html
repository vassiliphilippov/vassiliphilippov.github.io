nonagrarian

adjective
not agricultural
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnəˈɡrɛriən / (AmE), / ˌnɒnəˈɡreəriən / (BrE)

Examples

Nonagrarian economy
The country is developing a strong nonagrarian sector focused on technology and services.
Many nonagrarian communities rely on manufacturing and trade for their livelihoods.
The government encouraged nonagrarian businesses to diversify the economy beyond farming.
