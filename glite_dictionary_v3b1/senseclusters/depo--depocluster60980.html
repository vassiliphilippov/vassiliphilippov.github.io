depo

noun
vehicle storage place
Pronunciation: / ˈdiːpoʊ / (AmE), / ˈdepəʊ / (BrE)
a place where vehicles such as buses or trains are kept, stored, or maintained

Examples

Bus depo
The city buses are parked at the depo every night for cleaning and repairs.
After a long day, the train finally arrived at the depo for maintenance.
All delivery vans must return to the depo before midnight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depot - a place for storing vehicles or goods
  • synonym: garage - a building for housing vehicles
