European toad

noun
a common toad species native to Europe
5.8
Pronunciation: / jʊˈroʊpiən toʊd / (AmE), / jʊəˈrəʊpiən təʊd / (BrE)
a common species of toad (Bufo bufo) native to Europe, known for its warty skin and terrestrial habits

Examples

The European toad
During spring, European toads migrate to ponds and lakes to breed.
Many predators avoid the European toad because of its toxic skin secretions.
The European toad is frequently found in gardens and woodlands across the continent.

Related Terms
