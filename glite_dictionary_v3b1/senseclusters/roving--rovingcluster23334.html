roving

noun
fiber bundle for spinning
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrəʊvɪŋ / (BrE)
a long, narrow bundle of fibers that have been drawn out and slightly twisted, used in spinning yarn or thread

Examples

Roving of fibers
The spinning mill processed the roving into fine yarn for weaving.
Each bale of roving was carefully inspected before being sent to the next stage.
She learned how to draft roving by hand before spinning it into thread.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sliver - a loose, untwisted strand of fibers prepared for spinning
  • degree higher: yarn - spun thread used for knitting, weaving, or sewing
