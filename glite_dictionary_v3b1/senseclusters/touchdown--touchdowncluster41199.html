touchdown

noun
moment an aircraft lands
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtʌtʃˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈtʌtʃ.daʊn / (BrE)
the moment when an aircraft or spacecraft lands and its wheels or landing gear make contact with the ground or runway

Examples

Smooth touchdown
After a long flight, the passengers applauded upon touchdown.
The flight crew prepared the cabin for touchdown at the destination airport.
The pilot executed a perfect touchdown despite the heavy rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landing - an instance of coming or bringing something to land, especially an aircraft
