ultralight

noun
lightweight aircraft
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈʌl.trə.laɪt/ (AmE), /ˈʌl.trə.laɪt/ (BrE)
a very small, lightweight aircraft designed for short recreational flights, usually carrying one or two people

Examples

Fly an ultralight
He learned to fly an ultralight over the coast last summer.
The ultralight soared gracefully above the fields, offering a stunning view of the countryside.
Many aviation enthusiasts enjoy building their own ultralight for weekend flights.
