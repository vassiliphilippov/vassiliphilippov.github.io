brow

noun
arch of hair above the eye
5.9
Pronunciation: / braʊ / (AmE), / braʊ / (BrE)
the arch of hair growing above each eye; an eyebrow

Examples

A raised brow
She raised her brow in surprise, not sure what he meant.
Sweat dripped from his brow as he ran the last mile.
The actor's thick, dark brow gave him an intense look on stage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eyebrow - the ridge of hair growing above the eye
