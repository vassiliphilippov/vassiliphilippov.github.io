plank-bed

noun
bed made of wooden boards only
3.6
Pronunciation: / plæŋk bɛd / (AmE), / plæŋk bɛd / (BrE)
a bed made from wooden boards, typically without a mattress

Examples

A plank-bed in prison
He spent the night uncomfortably on a plank-bed in the prison cell.
She complained about having to sleep on a plank-bed with no mattress.
The old barracks still had rows of plank-beds lined against the walls.
