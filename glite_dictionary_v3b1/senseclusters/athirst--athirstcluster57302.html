athirst

adjective
extremely eager or desirous
Pronunciation: / əˈθɜrst / (AmE), / əˈθɜːst / (BrE)
very eager or thirsty for something (literally or figuratively); extremely desirous

The hero, athirst for adventure, set out on a journey across the sea.
In the old tale, the villagers were athirst during the long drought.
