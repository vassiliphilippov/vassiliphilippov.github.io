kernel

noun
core or essential part
5.4 1.92/m
Pronunciation: / Ààk…ùÀê.n…ôl / (AmE), / Ààk…úÀê.n…ôl / (BrE)
the central, most important, or essential part of something, especially of an idea, experience, or argument.

Examples

The kernel of an idea
After hours of discussion, we finally reached the kernel of the issue.
She tried to get past the details and focus on the kernel of the matter.
The kernel of his argument was that everyone deserves equal opportunity.
