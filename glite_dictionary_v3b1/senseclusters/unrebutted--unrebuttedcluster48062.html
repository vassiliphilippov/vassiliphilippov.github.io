unrebutted

adjective
not challenged
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈbʌtɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈbʌtɪd / (BrE)
not challenged, denied, or disproved by argument or evidence

Examples

An unrebutted statement
The witness's statement remained unrebutted during the entire hearing.
Because the evidence was unrebutted, the court accepted it as fact.
His accusation went unrebutted by anyone present at the meeting.

Related Terms

  • antonym: contested - challenged or disputed
  • synonym: undisputed - not questioned or doubted
