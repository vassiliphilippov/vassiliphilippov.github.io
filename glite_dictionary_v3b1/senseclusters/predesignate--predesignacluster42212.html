predesignate

verb
to assign in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈdɛzɪɡˌneɪt / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈdɛzɪɡneɪt / (BrE)
to designate or assign something in advance before it is needed or used

Examples

To predesignate seats
The organizers predesignated seats for each guest to avoid confusion at the event.
Each emergency shelter was predesignated for a specific group of residents.
The funds were predesignated for educational purposes before the project began.

  • synonym: preassign - to assign beforehand
