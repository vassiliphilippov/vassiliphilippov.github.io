churchy demeanor

noun
overly pious or moralistic behavior
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɝːtʃi dɪˈmiːnɚ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɜːtʃi dɪˈmiːnə / (BrE)
an overly pious or moralistic manner or behavior, especially in a way that appears self-righteous or insincere

Examples

A churchy demeanor of solemnity
He tried to hide his excitement behind a churchy demeanor of solemnity and restraint.
Her churchy demeanor made her seem judgmental rather than understanding.
Many students rolled their eyes at his churchy demeanor during the assembly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pious - deeply religious or showing strong religious devotion
  • synonym: sanctimonious - making a show of being morally superior to others
