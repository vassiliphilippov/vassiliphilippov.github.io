churchy demeanor

noun
overly pious or moralistic behavior
9.7 7.6
Pronunciation: /ˈtʃɜːr.tʃɪ dɪ.ˈmiː.nər/ (AmE), /ˈtʃɜː.tʃɪ dɪ.ˈmiː.nə/ (BrE)
