progestational

adjective
relating to the phase after ovulation in the reproductive cycle
Pronunciation: / proʊˌdʒɛˈsteɪʃənəl /; / proʊˌdʒɛˈsteɪʃənl / (AmE), / prəʊˌdʒɛˈsteɪʃənəl /; / prəʊˌdʒɛˈsteɪʃənl / (BrE)
relating to progesterone, drugs with similar effects, or the phase after ovulation when the body prepares for possible pregnancy
