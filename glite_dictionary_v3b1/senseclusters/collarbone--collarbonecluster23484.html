collarbone

noun
bone linking shoulder blade and breastbone
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːlɚˌboʊn / (AmE), / ˈkɒləbəʊn / (BrE)
the bone that connects the shoulder blade to the breastbone

Examples

The collarbone bone
He wore a sling to support his arm after injuring his collarbone.
She broke her collarbone during a cycling accident.
The doctor explained that the collarbone helps stabilize the shoulder.
