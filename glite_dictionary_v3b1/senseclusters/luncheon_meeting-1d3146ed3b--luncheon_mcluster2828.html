luncheon meeting

noun
business meeting held over lunch
Pronunciation: / ˈlʌntʃən ˈmiːtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈlʌn.tʃən ˈmiː.tɪŋ / (BrE)
a business meeting held during lunch, where participants discuss work while eating

Examples

A luncheon meeting
During the luncheon meeting, the manager outlined several new policies.

We finalized the partnership details at yesterday's luncheon meeting.
