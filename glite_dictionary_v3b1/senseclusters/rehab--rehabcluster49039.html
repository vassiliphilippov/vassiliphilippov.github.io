rehab

verb
to undergo rehabilitation
Pronunciation: / ˈriːhæb / (AmE), / ˈriːhæb / (BrE)
to undergo treatment in a rehabilitation program, especially to recover from injury, illness, or addiction

Examples

To rehab after injury
After his injury, he had to rehab for several months before returning to work.
Many athletes rehab intensively to speed up their recovery after surgery.
She decided to rehab at a specialized clinic to overcome her addiction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: recover - to return to a normal state of health or strength
  • antonym: relapse - to fall back into a former state, especially after improvement
