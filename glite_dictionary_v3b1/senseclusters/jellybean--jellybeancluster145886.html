jellybean

noun
superficial young man
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɛliˌbin / (AmE), / ˈdʒɛliˌbiːn / (BrE)
an excessively fashionable or superficial young man, especially one concerned with appearance rather than substance

Examples

A stylish jellybean
In the 1920s, a jellybean was known for spending hours perfecting his look.
The group laughed at the jellybean who always wore the latest styles but rarely joined serious conversations.
He was called a jellybean because he cared more about his clothes than his studies.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dandy - a man overly concerned with his appearance and clothes
  • synonym: fop - a man who is excessively concerned with fashion and appearance
  • antonym: plain - not concerned with fashion or appearance
