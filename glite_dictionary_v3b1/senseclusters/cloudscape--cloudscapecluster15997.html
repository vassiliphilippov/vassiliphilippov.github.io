cloudscape

noun
sky with clouds
5.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklaʊdˌskeɪp / (AmE), / ˈklaʊdˌskeɪp / (BrE)
a view or artistic representation of a sky filled with clouds, often as the main subject

Examples

A cloudscape photo
The photographer captured a stunning cloudscape just before the storm rolled in.
She admired the cloudscape painted across the evening sky from her window.
Many artists are inspired by the changing colors in a dramatic cloudscape.

Related Terms

  • synonym: landscape - a painting or photograph of natural scenery on land
  • synonym: seascape - a painting or photograph of the sea
