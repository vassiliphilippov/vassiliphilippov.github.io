seamlessness

noun
smooth continuity
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːmləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːmləsnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being smooth, continuous, and without any interruptions or gaps

Examples

The seamlessness of transition
The seamlessness of the transition impressed everyone at the company.
Users praised the seamlessness of the new mobile app interface.
She admired the seamlessness of the fabric, which had no visible joins.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuity - the state of being continuous and without interruption
  • antonym: interruption - a break or gap in continuity
