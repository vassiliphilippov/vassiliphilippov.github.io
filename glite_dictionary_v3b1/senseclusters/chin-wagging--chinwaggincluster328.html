chin-wagging

noun
light informal conversation
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪnˌwæɡɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪnˌwæɡɪŋ / (BrE)
informal chatting or conversation, especially of a light or social nature

Examples

Friendly chin-wagging
There was plenty of chin-wagging before the meeting officially began.
They enjoy a bit of chin-wagging after work to unwind from the day.
We spent the whole afternoon chin-wagging over coffee at the cafe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gossip - casual conversation about other people, often involving rumor
