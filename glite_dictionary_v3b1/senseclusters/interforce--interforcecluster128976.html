interforce

noun
force between bodies
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɪntərˌfɔrs / (AmE), / ˈɪntəˌfɔːs / (BrE)
a force that acts between two or more bodies or systems

Examples

Interforce between particles
The interforce between the two charged particles caused them to move apart.
Scientists study the interforce that holds molecules together in a chemical compound.
Understanding the interforce among planets helps explain their orbits in the solar system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interaction - a mutual or reciprocal action or influence
  • antonym: internal force - a force acting within a single body or system
