hydroxybenzoic acid

noun
a derivative of benzoic acid with a hydroxyl group
7.6
Pronunciation: / haɪˌdrɑksiˌbɛnˈzoʊɪk ˌæsɪd / (AmE), / haɪˌdrɒksiˌbɛnˈzəʊɪk ˌæsɪd / (BrE)
a crystalline compound derived from benzoic acid by replacing one hydrogen atom with a hydroxyl group, used in organic chemistry and found in some plants
