hydroxybenzoic acid

noun
a derivative of benzoic acid with a hydroxyl group
7.6
Pronunciation: /haɪ.drɑːk.sɪ.ben.ˈzoʊ.ɪk ˈæs.əd/ (AmE), /haɪ.drɒk.sɪ.ben.ˈzəʊ.ɪk ˈæs.ɪd/ (BrE)
