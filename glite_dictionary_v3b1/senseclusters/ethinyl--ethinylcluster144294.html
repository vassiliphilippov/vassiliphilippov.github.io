ethinyl

adjective
relating to ethinyl group
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθɪnɪl / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪnɪl / (BrE)
containing or relating to an ethinyl group, especially as a chemical prefix or modifier.

Examples

Ethinyl group
