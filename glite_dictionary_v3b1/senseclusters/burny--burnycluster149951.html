burny

adjective
tasting burnt
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɜːrni / (AmE), / ˈbɜːni / (BrE)
having a taste or smell that is like something burnt or charred, often from overcooking or roasting

Examples

Burny taste in soup
The soup had a slightly burny flavor after being left on the stove too long.
He wrinkled his nose at the burny smell coming from the toaster.
The over-roasted beans gave the coffee a burny aftertaste that lingered.

Related Terms

  • synonym: charred - having been burned so as to be blackened or scorched
  • antonym: fresh - having a clean, pure, or unspoiled taste or smell
  • synonym: smoky - having a flavor or smell of smoke
