resoundingly

adverb
in an emphatic or unmistakable way
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzaʊndɪŋli / (AmE), / rɪˈzaʊndɪŋli / (BrE)
In a very emphatic or unmistakable manner; with strong and clear effect.

Examples

To be resoundingly rejected
His proposal for the new project was resoundingly rejected by all members of the board.
The candidate resoundingly defeated her opponent in the election.
The team's efforts were resoundingly praised by the committee at the awards ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: emphatically - in a forceful or definite way
