Azolla caroliniana

noun
species of small aquatic fern
Pronunciation: / əˈzɑːlə ˌkɛrəˌlɪˈnaɪ.ə / (AmE), / əˈzɒlə ˌkærəˈlɪniə / (BrE)
A small free-floating aquatic fern native to the eastern United States and tropical America, often naturalized in parts of Europe.
