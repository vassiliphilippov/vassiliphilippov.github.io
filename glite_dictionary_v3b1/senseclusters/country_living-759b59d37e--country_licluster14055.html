country living

noun
rural lifestyle
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ˈlɪvɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ˈlɪvɪŋ / (BrE)
a lifestyle characterized by living in the countryside, often associated with simplicity and closeness to nature

Examples

She prefers country living because of the fresh air and peaceful surroundings.
