lousy service

noun
poor or unsatisfactory service
Pronunciation: / ˈlaʊzi ˈsɝvɪs / (AmE), / ˈlaʊzi ˈsɜːvɪs / (BrE)
service that is poor in quality or unsatisfactory

Examples

Poor lousy service
Despite the beautiful decor, the restaurant's lousy service ruined our evening.
Many customers complained on social media about the lousy service they received.
We decided not to return to the hotel because of their lousy service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bad service - service that is of poor quality
  • antonym: excellent service - service that is exceptionally good
