nonrepresentative

adjective
not typical or representative
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑːnˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv / (BrE)
Not typical of a group or class; not serving as a representative example.

Examples

A nonrepresentative example
A single unusual event is nonrepresentative of the company's overall safety record.
Critics argue that the artwork is nonrepresentative of the artist's usual style.
The survey results were considered nonrepresentative due to the small and biased sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: representative - typical of a group or class; serving as an example or symbol
