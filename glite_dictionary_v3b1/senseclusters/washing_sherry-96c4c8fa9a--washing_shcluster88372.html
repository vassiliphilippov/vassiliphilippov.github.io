washing sherry

noun
light sherry served before meals
8.3
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑʃɪŋ ˈʃɛri / (AmE), / ˈwɒʃɪŋ ˈʃɛri / (BrE)
A light, low-alcohol sherry wine served before meals as an aperitif.

Examples

A glass of washing sherry

Many people in Britain enjoy a washing sherry before beginning their evening meal.
