apply

verb
put substance on a surface
Pronunciation: / əˈplaɪ / (AmE), / əˈplaɪ / (BrE)
to put a substance onto a surface in order to cover or coat it

Examples

To apply paint
She applied a fresh coat of paint to the old chair.
The technician carefully applied the adhesive to the back of the tile.
You should apply sunscreen evenly before going outside.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remove - to take something off a surface
  • synonym: spread - to extend a substance or layer over a surface
