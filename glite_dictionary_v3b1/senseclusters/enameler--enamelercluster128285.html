enameler

noun
applier of enamel
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnæmələr / (AmE), / ɪˈnæmələ / (BrE)
a person or device that applies enamel to objects for decoration or protection

Examples

A skilled enameler
The skilled enameler created beautiful patterns on the metal plate.
A modern enameler can quickly coat hundreds of items in a single day.
She trained as an enameler to work with jewelry and decorative art.
The enameler carefully coated the jewelry with a glossy finish.
As an experienced enameler, she creates colorful designs on metal plates.
Many museums display the work of a skilled enameler from the 19th century.

Related Terms

  • synonym: artist - a person who creates art, especially professionally
  • synonym: coater - a person or machine that covers objects with a layer of material
  • synonym: craftsman - a skilled worker who makes or repairs things by hand
  • synonym: painter - a person who applies paint to surfaces
