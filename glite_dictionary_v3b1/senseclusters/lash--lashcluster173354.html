lash

noun
eyelid hair; eyelash
1.6
Pronunciation: / læʃ / (AmE), / læʃ / (BrE)
any of the short, curved hairs that grow along the edge of the eyelid; an eyelash

Examples

A long lash
A single lash fell onto her cheek as she made a wish.
His dark lashes framed his bright blue eyes.
She brushed mascara onto her lashes before leaving for the party.
