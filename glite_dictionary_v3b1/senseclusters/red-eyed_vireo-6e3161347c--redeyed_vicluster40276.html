red-eyed vireo

noun
North American bird with red eyes
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛdˈaɪd ˈvɪrioʊ / (AmE), / ˌrɛdˈaɪd ˈvɪəriəʊ / (BrE)
A small North American songbird with red irises, olive-grey upperparts, and white underparts.

Examples

A red-eyed vireo bird
A red-eyed vireo built its nest near the edge of the clearing.
The forest was filled with the song of several red-eyed vireos during our hike.
We spotted a red-eyed vireo singing high in the maple tree.
