dole

verb
to distribute in portions
Pronunciation: / doʊl / (AmE), / dəʊl / (BrE)
to give out or distribute something, especially in small amounts, often to people in need

Examples

The charity will dole out blankets to the homeless during the winter months.
She had to dole the snacks carefully so everyone got a fair share.
