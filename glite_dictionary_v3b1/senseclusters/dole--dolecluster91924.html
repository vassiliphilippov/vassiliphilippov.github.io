Synonyms

  • give out
    2.3 4.63/m
    give out is a neutral term for sharing or distributing items to people, while dole emphasizes small portions often given to those in need.
  • hand out
    1.8 7.63/m
    hand out means physically passing items to a group, while dole suggests giving small, often need-based, portions rather than general distribution.
  • dispense
    3.7 1.39/m
    dispense is a formal word for distributing goods or medicine in an organized way, while dole focuses on small portions for people in need.
