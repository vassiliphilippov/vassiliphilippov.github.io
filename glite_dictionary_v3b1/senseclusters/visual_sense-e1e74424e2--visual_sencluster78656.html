visual sense

noun
sense of sight; ability to see
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒuəl sɛns / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒjʊəl sɛns / (BrE)
the ability to perceive the world by sight; the sense of seeing

Examples

The visual sense of sight

Owls have a highly developed visual sense that allows them to hunt at night.
The scientist studied how the brain processes information from the visual sense.
