inquisitively

adverb
in a curious or questioning way
1.7
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈkwɪz.ə.tɪv.li / (AmE), / ɪnˈkwɪz.ɪ.tɪv.li / (BrE)
In a manner showing curiosity or a desire to know or learn more about something.

Examples

Look inquisitively at
He looked inquisitively at the device, trying to figure out how it worked.
She asked inquisitively about his travels, wanting to hear every detail.
The child peered inquisitively into the box, eager to see what was inside.
