Colorado River hemp

noun
tall annual plant native to southwestern US
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəˌlɑːrɑːdoʊ ˈrɪvər hɛmp / (AmE), / kəˌlɒrədəʊ ˈrɪvə hɛmp / (BrE)
A tall annual plant native to the southwestern United States, widely grown as green manure and formerly used for its strong bast fiber by Indigenous peoples.
