nasi

noun
cooked rice (Malay/Indonesian)
Pronunciation: / ˈnɑːsi / (AmE), / ˈnɑːsi / (BrE)
cooked rice, especially as a staple food in Malay and Indonesian cuisine, often served with other dishes

Examples

A plate of nasi
She ordered a plate of spicy chicken with nasi and vegetables for lunch.
In Indonesia, nasi is often served with a variety of side dishes and sauces.
The aroma of freshly cooked nasi filled the kitchen as the family prepared for dinner.
