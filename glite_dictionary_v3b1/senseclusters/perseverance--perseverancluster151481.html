perseverance

noun
persistent determination
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌpɜrsəˈvɪrəns /; / ˌpɜːrsəˈvɪrəns / (AmE), / ˌpɜːsɪˈvɪərəns / (BrE)
the quality of continuing to try or work hard even when things are difficult or take a long time

Examples

Strong perseverance
Achieving your goals often requires great perseverance and hard work.
Despite facing numerous obstacles, he showed remarkable perseverance in pursuing his dreams.
Her perseverance paid off when she finally graduated at the top of her class.
Despite many setbacks, his perseverance helped him achieve his dream of becoming an author.
Her perseverance in studying every night paid off when she passed the exam with top marks.
The athlete’s perseverance was evident as she trained through injuries and disappointments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: determination - firmness of purpose; resoluteness
  • synonym: persistence - the continued or prolonged existence of something
  • antonym: resignation - acceptance of something undesirable but inevitable
  • synonym: resilience - the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties
