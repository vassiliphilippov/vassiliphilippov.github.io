cardiogenic

adjective
heart formation
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑrdioʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌkɑːdiəʊˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
relating to the origin or formation of the heart, especially in embryonic development

Examples
