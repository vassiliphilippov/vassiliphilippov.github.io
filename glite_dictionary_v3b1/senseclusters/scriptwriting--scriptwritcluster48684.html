scriptwriting

noun
writing scripts
Pronunciation: / ˈskrɪptˌraɪtɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈskrɪptˌraɪtɪŋ / (BrE)
the activity or process of writing scripts for films, plays, television, or other performances

Examples

Scriptwriting for movies
She enrolled in a workshop to improve her scriptwriting for television dramas.
Good scriptwriting is essential for creating engaging and believable characters in a film.
He made a career change from journalism to scriptwriting for stage productions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: playwriting - the writing of scripts for plays
  • synonym: screenwriting - the writing of scripts specifically for films or television
