uhuru

proper noun
independence landmark name
9.9
Pronunciation: /uː.ˈhuː.ruː/ /; / /ə.ˈhuː.ruː/ (AmE), /uː.ˈhuː.ruː/ /; / /ə.ˈhuː.ruː/ (BrE)
the name given to public places or institutions, such as parks or buildings, to commemorate independence, especially in some African countries
