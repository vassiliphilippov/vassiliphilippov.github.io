pushrod

adjective
using pushrods
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃˌrɑd / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌrɒd / (BrE)
relating to or using pushrods, especially in reference to an internal-combustion engine whose valves are operated by pushrods

Examples

Pushrod engine
The mechanic specialized in repairing pushrod engines found in classic American cars.
Many older vehicles feature a pushrod design instead of modern overhead cam systems.
Some racing enthusiasts prefer the simplicity of pushrod technology for its reliability.

Related Terms

  • antonym: overhead cam - relating to engines with camshafts above the valves, not using pushrods
