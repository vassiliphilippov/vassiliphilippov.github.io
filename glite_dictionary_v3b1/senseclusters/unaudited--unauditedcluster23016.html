unaudited

adjective
not officially reviewed
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɔdɪtɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈɔːdɪtɪd / (BrE)
not officially examined, checked, or reviewed by an audit or formal inspection process (especially for financial statements or results)

Examples

Unaudited financial statements
The company submitted unaudited financial statements to the board for preliminary review.
Several unaudited transactions were flagged during the system upgrade for further investigation.
It is risky to make decisions based on unaudited data that has not been verified.
The company released its unaudited financial results to the public last week.
Investors should be cautious when reviewing unaudited statements, as they may contain errors.
The report is still unaudited and subject to further verification by external accountants.
