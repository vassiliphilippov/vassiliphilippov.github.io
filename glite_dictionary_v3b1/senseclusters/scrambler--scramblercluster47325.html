scrambler

noun
person who moves quickly and unpredictably
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈskræm.blɚ / (AmE), / ˈskræm.blə / (BrE)
a person who moves quickly and unpredictably, especially to avoid obstacles or evade others, often used in sports contexts

Examples

Fast scrambler in sports
During the game, the young player proved himself to be quite the scrambler, dodging defenders at every turn.
Her reputation as an agile scrambler on the tennis court intimidated her opponents.
The team's quarterback is an excellent scrambler who can avoid being tackled easily.

Related Terms

  • synonym: runner - a person who runs, especially in sports
  • antonym: stationary - not moving; not intended to be moved
