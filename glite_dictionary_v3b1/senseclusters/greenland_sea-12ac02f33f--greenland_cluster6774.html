Greenland Sea

noun
sea area north of Iceland in the Arctic Ocean
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːnˌlænd siː / (AmE), / ˈɡriːnlənd siː / (BrE)
the part of the Arctic Ocean situated north of Iceland, between Greenland and the Svalbard archipelago

Examples
