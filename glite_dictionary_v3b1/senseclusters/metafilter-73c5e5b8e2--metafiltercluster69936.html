MetaFilter

proper noun
online discussion community
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtəˌfɪltər / (AmE), / ˈmɛtəˌfɪltə / (BrE)
an online community and weblog where users share links, ask questions, and discuss various topics

Examples

MetaFilter online community
I found an interesting article about climate change on MetaFilter yesterday.
Many people use MetaFilter to ask questions and get advice from the community.
Discussions on MetaFilter often cover a wide range of topics, from technology to culture.
