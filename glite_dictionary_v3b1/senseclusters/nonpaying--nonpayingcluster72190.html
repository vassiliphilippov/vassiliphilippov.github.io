nonpaying

adjective
without payment
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpeɪɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpeɪɪŋ / (BrE)
not receiving or providing any payment, wages, or salary for work or a position

Examples

Nonpaying job
She took a nonpaying internship to gain experience in the publishing industry.
Many volunteers work in nonpaying positions to help their communities.
The organization relies on nonpaying staff to run its programs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: paid - receiving payment for work or services
  • synonym: unpaid - not receiving payment for work or services
