Pollyannaish outlook

noun
excessively optimistic perspective
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈænəɪʃ ˈaʊtlʊk / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈænəɪʃ ˈaʊtlʊk / (BrE)
An excessively or unrealistically optimistic perspective, often disregarding potential difficulties or negative aspects.

Examples

A Pollyannaish outlook on life
Even in the face of layoffs, he maintained a Pollyannaish outlook about the company's future.
Her Pollyannaish outlook worried her friends, who thought she ignored real dangers.
It's important to stay realistic and avoid adopting a Pollyannaish outlook when making big decisions.

Related Terms

  • synonym: optimism - the tendency to focus on the positive aspects or expect the best outcome
  • antonym: pessimism - the tendency to focus on the negative aspects or expect the worst outcome
