initiate

noun
person newly introduced to secret knowledge
Pronunciation: / ɪˈnɪʃiˌeɪt /; / ɪˈnɪʃiət / (AmE), / ɪˈnɪʃɪeɪt /; / ɪˈnɪʃɪət /; / ɪˈnɪʃieɪt / (BrE)
a person who is new to a group or has been admitted to a group, especially after a ceremony or special process
