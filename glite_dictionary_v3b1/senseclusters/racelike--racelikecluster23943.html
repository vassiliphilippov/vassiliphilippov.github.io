racelike

adjective
similar to a race
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪslaɪk / (AmE), / ˈreɪslaɪk / (BrE)
having qualities or characteristics similar to a race or competition, especially involving speed or rivalry

Examples

Racelike competition
The students worked at a racelike pace to finish the project before the deadline.
There was a racelike excitement in the air as the doors opened for the big sale.
Her racelike determination pushed her to complete the course faster than anyone else.

Related Terms

  • synonym: competitive - involving or determined by competition
  • antonym: leisurely - unhurried or relaxed, not rushed
