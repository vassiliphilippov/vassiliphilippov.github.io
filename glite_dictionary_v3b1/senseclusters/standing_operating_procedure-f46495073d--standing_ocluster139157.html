standing operating procedure

noun
routine procedure to be followed regularly
5.7 3.2
Pronunciation: /ˈstænd.ɪŋ ˈɑː.pə.reɪ.tɪŋ prə.ˈsiː.dʒər/ /; / /ˈstænd.ɪŋ ˈɔː.pə.reɪ.tɪŋ prə.ˈsiː.dʒər/ (AmE), /ˈstænd.ɪŋ ˈɒp.ə.reɪ.tɪŋ prə.ˈsiː.dʒə/ (BrE)
a routine or established method of carrying out tasks, especially prescribed by an organization to be followed regularly

Examples

The standing operating procedure

Before every meeting, the manager reviews the standing operating procedure with the team.
The hospital has a detailed standing operating procedure for handling emergencies.
