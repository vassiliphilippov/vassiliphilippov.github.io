zabaglione

noun
Italian foamy custard dessert
7.8
Pronunciation: / zɑbəˈjoʊni / (AmE), / ˌzabəˈjəʊneɪ / (BrE)
an Italian dessert made from egg yolks, sugar, and wine, whisked together over gentle heat to form a light, foamy custard, served hot or cold

Examples

Classic zabaglione dessert
For dessert, she prepared a classic zabaglione topped with fresh berries.
Homemade zabaglione is surprisingly simple but tastes rich and elegant.
The chef served warm zabaglione alongside almond biscotti after dinner.
