uninterpreted

adjective
without assigned meaning
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnɪnˈtɜrprətɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnɪnˈtɜːprɪtɪd / (BrE)
not given a specific meaning or semantic interpretation within a formal system or model.

Examples

Uninterpreted symbol in logic
In formal logic, an uninterpreted symbol has no assigned meaning in the system.
The function remained uninterpreted until the programmer defined its purpose.
Some variables in the equation are intentionally left uninterpreted for generality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: interpreted - given a specific meaning or explanation
  • synonym: undefined - not given a value or meaning
