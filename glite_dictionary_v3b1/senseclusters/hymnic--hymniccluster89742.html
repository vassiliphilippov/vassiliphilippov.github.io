hymnic

adjective
praising or exalted
8.9
Pronunciation: /ˈhɪm.nɪk/ (AmE), /ˈhɪm.nɪk/ (BrE)
having the qualities of a hymn, such as being lofty, exalted, or expressing enthusiastic praise; relating to hymns or hymn-like poetry
