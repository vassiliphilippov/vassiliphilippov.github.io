clingy

adjective
excessively dependent or attached
3.2 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈklɪŋ.iː/ (AmE), /ˈklɪŋ.iː/ (BrE)
(of a person) excessively dependent on or emotionally attached to someone, often needing constant attention or reassurance.

Examples

A clingy person
He found his girlfriend's clingy behavior overwhelming and needed some space.
My little sister is very clingy and hates to be left alone at daycare.
Toddlers can sometimes become clingy when they feel unwell or anxious.

Related Terms

  • antonym: independent - not needing or relying on others
  • synonym: needy - requiring a lot of attention, affection, or emotional support
