laydown

noun
easy victory
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈleɪˌdaʊn / (BrE)
an outcome or victory that is almost certain or very easy to achieve

Examples

An easy laydown
With their star player back, the match was a laydown for the home team.
Everyone thought the election would be a laydown, but the results were surprisingly close.
The quiz was such a laydown that most students finished in just a few minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sure thing - something certain to happen or succeed
  • antonym: upset - an unexpected defeat of a favorite
  • synonym: walkover - an easy or uncontested victory
