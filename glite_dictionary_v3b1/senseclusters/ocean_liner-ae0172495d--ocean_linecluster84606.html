ocean liner

noun
large passenger ship for scheduled sea routes
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊʃən ˌlaɪnər / (AmE), / ˈəʊʃən ˌlaɪnə / (BrE)
a large ship designed for carrying passengers and cargo on regular long-distance sea routes

Examples

Luxury ocean liners
Luxury ocean liners offered elegant dining rooms and spacious cabins.
Many immigrants crossed the Atlantic by ocean liner in the early 20th century.
The Titanic was one of the most famous ocean liners ever built.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cruise ship - a large ship used mainly for pleasure voyages, where the voyage itself and the ship's amenities are part of the experience
