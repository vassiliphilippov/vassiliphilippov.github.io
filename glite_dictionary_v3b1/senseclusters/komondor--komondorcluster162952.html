komondor

noun
Hungarian breed of large, shaggy white dog
7.6 8.4
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈmɑːn.dɔːr/ /; / /koʊ.ˈmɑːn.dɔːr/ (AmE), /kə.ˈmɒn.dɔː/ /; / /kɒ.ˈmɒn.dɔː/ (BrE)
A large, powerful Hungarian breed of dog with a long, thick, shaggy white coat, traditionally used for guarding livestock.

Examples

A komondor dog
A komondor requires regular grooming to keep its thick, shaggy fur clean.
Farmers often rely on the komondor to protect their sheep from predators.
The komondor is well known for its distinctive corded coat, which resembles dreadlocks.
