streamlined

adjective
shaped to reduce resistance to flow
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈstriːmˌlaɪnd / (AmE), / ˈstriːmlʌɪnd / (BrE)
having a shape or design that allows smooth movement through air or water by reducing resistance

Examples

Streamlined body shape
Dolphins have streamlined shapes that help them glide easily through water.
Engineers designed the train to be as streamlined as possible for high-speed travel.
The new car features a streamlined body to improve its speed and fuel efficiency.

  • synonym: aerodynamic - designed to reduce air resistance
