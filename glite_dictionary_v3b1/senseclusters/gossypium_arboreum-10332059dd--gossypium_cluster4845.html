Gossypium arboreum

noun
a species of Indian cotton plant
Pronunciation: / ˌɡɑːˈsɪpiəm ˌɑːrbɔːˈriəm / (AmE), / ˌɡɒsɪpiəm ˌɑːbərˈiːəm / (BrE)
