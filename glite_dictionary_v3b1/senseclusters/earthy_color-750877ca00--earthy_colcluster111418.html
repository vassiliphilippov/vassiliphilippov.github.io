earthy color

noun
natural, muted earth-like color
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɜrθi ˈkʌlər / (AmE), / ˈɜːθi ˈkʌlə / (BrE)
a natural, muted color that resembles earth or soil, such as brown, ochre, or olive

Examples

Earthy color tones
Autumn fashion collections often highlight earthy colors such as brown, khaki, and burnt orange.
She chose earthy colors like terracotta and moss green for her living room walls.
The artist was known for his use of earthy colors to create a warm, rustic atmosphere in his work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: neutral color - a color not strongly associated with hue, e.g., grey, beige, or white
  • degree higher: pastel color - a pale, soft hue, less intense than earthy but similarly muted
