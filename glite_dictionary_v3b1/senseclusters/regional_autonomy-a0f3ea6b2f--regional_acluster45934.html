regional autonomy

noun
self-government of a specific region
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈriːdʒənəl ɔːˈtɑːnəmi / (AmE), / ˈriːdʒənəl ɔːˈtɒnəmi / (BrE)
the right or condition of self-governance for a specific region within a larger state or country, allowing it to make certain decisions independently of the central government

Examples

Regional autonomy rights
Countries with regional autonomy often allow local parliaments to manage education and health care.
Growing demands for regional autonomy have shaped national politics in recent years.
The constitution grants a high degree of regional autonomy to protect distinct cultural identities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: autonomy - independence or self-government
  • antonym: centralization - the concentration of control under a central authority
