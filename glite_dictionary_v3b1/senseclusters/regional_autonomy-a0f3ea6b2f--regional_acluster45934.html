regional autonomy

noun
self-government of a specific region
3.5 5.4
Pronunciation: /ˈriː.dʒən.əl ə.ˈtɑː.nə.miː/ /; / /ˈriːdʒ.nəl ə.ˈtɑː.nə.miː/ /; / /ˈriː.dʒən.əl ɔː.ˈtɑː.nə.miː/ (AmE), /ˈriː.dʒən.əl ɔː.ˈtɒ.nə.miː/ /; / /ˈriːdʒ.nəl ɔː.ˈtɒ.nə.miː/ /; / /ˈriː.dʒən.əl ə.ˈtɒ.nə.miː/ (BrE)
the right or condition of self-governance for a specific region within a larger state or country, allowing it to make certain decisions independently of the central government
