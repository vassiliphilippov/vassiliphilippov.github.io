puerperal

adjective
relating to period after childbirth
9.9 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / pjuÀà…ùÀêp…ôr…ôl / (AmE), / pj ä…ôÀàp…úÀêr…ôl / (BrE)
relating to, or occurring during, the period shortly after childbirth, especially in reference to the woman who has just given birth

Examples
