puerperal

adjective
relating to period after childbirth
Pronunciation: / pjuˈɝːpərəl / (AmE), / pjʊəˈpɜːrəl / (BrE)
relating to, or occurring during, the period shortly after childbirth, especially in reference to the woman who has just given birth

Examples

Puerperal fever
She was monitored for any signs of puerperal complications during the postpartum period.
The patient developed a puerperal infection shortly after delivery.
There is an increased risk of puerperal fever if sterile procedures are not followed.
