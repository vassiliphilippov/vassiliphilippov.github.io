hydraulically

adverb
by hydraulic means; using fluid pressure
Pronunciation: / haɪˈdrɔːlɪkli / (AmE), / haɪˈdrɒlɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that involves or uses the movement or pressure of liquids, especially water or oil, in mechanical systems.

Examples

Raised hydraulically
The cylinder was raised hydraulically during the test procedure.
The machine operates hydraulically for greater lifting power.
This dam can be opened hydraulically in case of flooding.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mechanically - in a mechanical manner, by means of machinery
