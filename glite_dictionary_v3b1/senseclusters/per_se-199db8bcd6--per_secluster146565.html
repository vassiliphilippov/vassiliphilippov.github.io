Examples

Per se meaning

Synonyms

  • in and of itself
    6.0 3.53/m
    in and of itself highlights something considered alone without other factors or effects, while per se describes something's nature when seen alone in a formal way.
  • intrinsically
    6.4 3.33/m
    intrinsically points to a thing's essential nature or qualities, while per se says the thing by itself, ignoring outside factors.
