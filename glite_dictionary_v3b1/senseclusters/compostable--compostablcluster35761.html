compostable

noun
item for composting
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpoʊstəbəl / (AmE), / kəmˈpɒstəbl / (BrE)
a material or item that can break down naturally and safely in composting conditions

Examples

Sort compostables
The city encourages residents to separate compostables from regular trash to reduce landfill waste.
All food scraps and paper napkins are considered compostables in our recycling program.
Please place compostables in the green bin for weekly collection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: biodegradable - able to be broken down naturally by microorganisms
  • antonym: noncompostable - not able to break down in composting conditions
