katabasis

noun
military retreat
9.9
Pronunciation: / kəˈtæbəsɪs / (AmE), / kəˈtæbəsɪs / (BrE)
an organized military retreat or withdrawal, especially a movement from higher to lower ground

Examples

Army katabasis retreat
The army's katabasis was carefully planned to avoid enemy ambushes during the descent.
After days of fighting uphill, the troops began a katabasis to regroup in the valley below.
Historical accounts describe the famous katabasis of the ancient soldiers as a turning point in the campaign.

Related Terms

  • antonym: advance - a forward movement of troops toward the enemy
  • synonym: retreat - the act of withdrawing troops from enemy forces
