aero-

x
prefix: air or oxygen
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈɛroʊ / (AmE), / ˈeərəʊ / (BrE)
a prefix meaning 'air' or 'oxygen', used in scientific terms to indicate a relation to air or oxygen.

Examples

Aero-bic exercise
The word aero- is used in 'aerobic' to describe exercises that require oxygen.
Scientists use the prefix aero- in terms like 'aerobiology' to refer to the study of airborne organisms.
In chemistry, aero- helps indicate processes that involve oxygen or air.

Related Terms

  • antonym: anaero- - prefix meaning 'without air' or 'without oxygen'
