Proteus

noun
genus of aquatic salamanders
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtiəs / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtiəs / (BrE)
A genus of aquatic salamanders, the type genus of the family Proteidae.

Examples

A Proteus salamander
Researchers studied the adaptations of Proteus to its unique cave environment.
Some species in the Proteus genus are completely blind due to their subterranean habitat.
The olm, a species within the genus Proteus, is found in subterranean waters of the Dinaric Alps.
