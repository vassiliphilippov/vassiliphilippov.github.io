doyley

noun
small decorative mat for dishes or bowls
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔɪli / (AmE), / ˈdɔɪli / (BrE)
A small, often decorative, mat of lace or linen placed under a dish, bowl, or plate to protect the surface or for decoration.

Examples
