antiracist

adjective
opposed to racism
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst / (BrE)
opposed to racism or actively supporting racial equality

Examples

Antiracist policy
The school adopted an antiracist policy to ensure equal treatment for all students.
She attended several antiracist workshops to learn how to support diversity in her community.
Many organizations are now taking antiracist actions to address inequality.

Related Terms

  • antonym: racist - showing or supporting racist attitudes
