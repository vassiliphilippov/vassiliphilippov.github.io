shuttering

noun
closing with shutters
5.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃʌtərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈʃʌtərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of closing or covering windows or openings with shutters or similar panels

Examples

Shuttering the windows
The shuttering of the old building was completed before the storm hit the town.
After the festival, workers began the shuttering of all the temporary stalls.
Proper shuttering can help protect homes from severe weather damage.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boarding up - covering windows or doors with boards for protection
  • antonym: opening - removing covers or barriers from windows or doors
