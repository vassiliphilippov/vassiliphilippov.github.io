harmony

noun
agreement of opinions
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑːrməni /; / ˈhɑːr.mə.ni / (AmE), / ˈhɑːməni /; / ˈhɑː.mə.ni / (BrE)
agreement or compatibility between people, ideas, or things; a situation where things work well together

Examples

Harmony between people
After the discussion, the two leaders reached a surprising harmony of views.
Living in harmony with others requires patience and understanding.
Their approach to the problem was marked by harmony rather than conflict.
Living in harmony with your neighbors leads to a happier community.
The team worked in perfect harmony to achieve their goals.
They finally reached a harmony of opinions after a long discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: concord - agreement or harmony between people or groups
  • synonym: consensus - general agreement among a group
  • antonym: discord - lack of agreement or harmony between people or things
