instillment

noun
introduction of a liquid drop by drop
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈstɪlmənt / (AmE), / ɪnˈstɪlmənt / (BrE)
the act of introducing a liquid drop by drop, often by pouring or injection

Examples

The instillment of medicine
Modern devices automate the instillment of nutrients into intravenous lines.
Proper instillment techniques are crucial for administering certain eye drops.
The instillment of medication into the child's ear relieved the infection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instillation - the act of instilling, especially with reference to administration of a liquid drop by drop
