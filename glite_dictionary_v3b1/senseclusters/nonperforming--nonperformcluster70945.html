nonperforming

adjective
failing to fulfill obligations
6.6
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.pər.ˈfɔːr.mɪŋ/ (AmE), /nɒn.pə.ˈfɔː.mɪŋ/ (BrE)
failing to carry out a required duty or obligation, especially under a contract or agreement

Examples

Nonperforming loan
The bank classified the loan as nonperforming after several missed payments.
A nonperforming asset can negatively impact a company's financial health.
The contractor was declared nonperforming after failing to fulfill the contract's specified work milestones.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compliant - fulfilling all required duties or obligations
  • synonym: defaulting - failing to meet a required duty or payment
  • synonym: delinquent - failing to do what is required by law or duty
