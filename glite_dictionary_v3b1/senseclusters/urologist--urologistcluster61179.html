urologist

noun
specialist in urinary and male reproductive health
Pronunciation: / jʊˈrɑːlədʒɪst / (AmE), / jʊəˈrɒlədʒɪst / (BrE)
a doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the urinary system and male reproductive organs

Examples

A urologist doctor
After experiencing persistent pain, she made an appointment with a urologist.
He was referred to a urologist for further evaluation of his symptoms.
The urologist explained the treatment options for his kidney stones.

Related Terms

  • synonym: urology - the medical specialty concerning the urinary tract and male reproductive system
