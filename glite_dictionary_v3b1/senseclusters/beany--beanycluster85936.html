beany

noun
small round cap formerly worn by students
Pronunciation: / ˈbiːni / (AmE), / ˈbiːni / (BrE)
a small, round skullcap, especially one formerly worn by schoolboys and college freshmen as part of a uniform or tradition

Examples

A beany cap
At the reunion, alumni laughed about the days when a beany was part of the official uniform.
He found his old striped beany from his school days in a forgotten drawer.
The college tradition required every freshman to wear a beany during orientation week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: skullcap - a small, close-fitting cap covering the top of the head
