sphinxlike

adjective
mysterious or enigmatic
9.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsfɪŋksˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈsfɪŋksˌlaɪk / (BrE)

She remained sphinxlike throughout the interview, revealing little about her true intentions.
