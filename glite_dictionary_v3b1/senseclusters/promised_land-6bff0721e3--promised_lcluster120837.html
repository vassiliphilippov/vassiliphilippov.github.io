Promised Land

noun
the ultimate spiritual goal or ideal place
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːmɪst lænd /; / ˈprɑːmɪst ˌlænd / (AmE), / ˈprɒmɪst lænd /; / ˈprɒmɪst ˌlænd / (BrE)
a place or situation where one achieves happiness, fulfillment, or an ultimate goal, especially after effort or as a spiritual reward

Examples

Reach the Promised Land
For many, forgiveness and redemption are the path to the Promised Land.
She regarded inner peace as her own personal Promised Land.
The preacher spoke about striving for the Promised Land in our daily lives.
After years of study and perseverance, she finally arrived at her Promised Land of professional success.
For some, retirement is the Promised Land after a lifetime of hard work.
Many immigrants viewed America as the Promised Land offering hope and opportunity.

  • synonym: paradise - a place or state of perfect happiness
  • synonym: utopia - an imagined place of ideal perfection
