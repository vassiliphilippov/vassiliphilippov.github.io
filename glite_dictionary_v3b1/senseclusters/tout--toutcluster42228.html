tout

noun
person reselling tickets for profit
8.7
Pronunciation: / taʊt / (AmE), / taʊt / (BrE)
a person who buys tickets for an event and resells them at a profit, often illegally or without authorization

Examples

Ticket tout
He bought the last two tickets from a tout minutes before the concert started.
Many fans criticized the presence of touts who made it harder for them to get seats at a fair price.
The police arrested a tout outside the stadium for selling tickets above face value.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scalper - person who resells tickets at a profit (especially American English)
