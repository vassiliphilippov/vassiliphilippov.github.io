Bolívar

proper noun
Venezuelan independence leader
Pronunciation: / boʊˈliːvɑr / (AmE), / bəˈliːvɑː / (BrE)
Simón Bolívar (1783–1830), a Venezuelan military and political leader who led independence movements in northern South America.
