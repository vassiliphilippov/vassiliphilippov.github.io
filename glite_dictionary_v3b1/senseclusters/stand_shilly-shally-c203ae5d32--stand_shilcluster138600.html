stand shilly-shally

verb
to be indecisive; to hesitate
Pronunciation: / stænd ˈʃɪli ˌʃæli / (AmE), / stænd ˈʃɪli ˌʃæli / (BrE)
to be indecisive or hesitate; to waver between options without making a decision

Examples

Don't stand shilly-shally
Don't stand shilly-shally—take action before it's too late!
She stood shilly-shally at the crossroads, uncertain about which path to take.
Whenever a big decision comes up, he tends to stand shilly-shally and delay making a choice.

  • synonym: hesitate - pause before taking action due to uncertainty
