eccentrically

adverb
in an unusual or unconventional manner
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈsɛntrɪkli / (AmE), / ɪkˈsɛntrɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is unconventional, unusual, or odd.

Examples

To behave eccentrically
He always acts eccentrically at parties, telling jokes that no one expects.
She eccentrically decorated her home with furniture made from recycled tires.
The professor dressed eccentrically, pairing a bright orange hat with purple shoes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bizarrely - in a very strange or unusual manner
  • antonym: conventionally - in a way that follows accepted standards or customs
