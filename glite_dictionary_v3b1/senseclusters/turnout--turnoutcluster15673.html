turnout

noun
outfit; set of clothes for an occasion
7.1 0.04/m
Pronunciation: / Ààt…ùnÀåa ät / (AmE), / Ààt…úÀêna ät / (BrE)
an outfit or set of clothes, especially one worn on a particular occasion

Examples

Smart turnout for dinner
His formal turnout for the wedding was praised by all the guests.
She arrived at the party in a stylish turnout that caught everyone's attention.
The press commented on the impressive turnout chosen by the actress for the award ceremony.
