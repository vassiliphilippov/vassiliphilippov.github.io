spice cookie

noun
cookie flavored with spices
8.3
Pronunciation: / spaɪs ˈkʊki / (AmE), / spaɪs ˈkʊki / (BrE)
A cookie flavored with a mixture of spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, or ginger.

Examples

A spice cookie
I prefer spice cookies to chocolate chip ones because of their rich, warm flavors.
My grandmother always bakes her famous spice cookies for the holidays.
The aroma of freshly baked spice cookies filled the kitchen.
