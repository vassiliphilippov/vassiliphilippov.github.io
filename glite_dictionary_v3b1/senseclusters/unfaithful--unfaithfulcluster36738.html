unfaithful

adjective
not remaining loyal in a romantic relationship
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfeɪθfəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈfeɪθfəl / (BrE)
having sexual relations with someone other than your husband, wife, boyfriend, or girlfriend; not remaining loyal in a romantic relationship

Examples

Unfaithful husband
After ten years of marriage, she discovered that her husband had been unfaithful to her.
Rumors spread quickly when it became clear he was unfaithful in their relationship.
She forgave him, even though he had been unfaithful more than once.
