psychodiagnostic

noun
mental health assessment
Pronunciation: / ˌsaɪkoʊdaɪəgˈnɑstɪk / (AmE), / ˌsaɪkəʊdaɪəgˈnɒstɪk / (BrE)
a test, assessment, or procedure used to diagnose mental or psychological disorders

Examples

A psychodiagnostic test
The psychologist recommended a psychodiagnostic to better understand the patient's symptoms.
Before starting treatment, a thorough psychodiagnostic was conducted to assess the mental health condition.
Many clinics use a psychodiagnostic as part of their standard evaluation process.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diagnosis - the identification of a disease or condition
  • synonym: psychological test - an assessment to measure mental functions and behaviors
