astro-

x
star-shaped prefix
Pronunciation: / ˈæstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈæstrəʊ / (BrE)
a combining form meaning 'star-shaped' or having radiating parts, used in biology and anatomy

Examples

Astro-cyte cell
The prefix astro- is used in 'astrocyte' to describe a star-shaped cell in the brain.
In biology, astro- often appears in terms for structures with radiating parts.
The word 'astrosphere' uses the prefix astro- to indicate a star-shaped region.
