readout

noun
information displayed by a device
5.8 1.06/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàriÀêdÀåa ät / (AmE), / ÀàriÀêd.a ät /; / ÀàriÀêdÀåa ät / (BrE)
information or data shown or printed by an electronic device, such as on a screen or printout

Examples

Digital readout
Please check the screen for the latest readout of the experiment's results.
The digital thermometer gave a clear readout of the patient's temperature.
The technician recorded the pressure readout from the gauge.
A digital readout on the dashboard displays the car's speed and fuel level.
After the experiment, the scientist checked the readout on her computer.
The machine provides a readout of the patient's heart rate and blood pressure.

Synonyms

  • reading
    4.2 25/m
    reading gives a single measured value from an instrument for checking, while readout is the device's displayed data or printed panel.
  • output
    3.7 9.58/m
    output is the data signal produced by a device for other systems or processing, while readout is the on-screen or printed display.

Related Terms

  • synonym: display - visual presentation of information on a screen
  • synonym: output - the information produced by a device or system
