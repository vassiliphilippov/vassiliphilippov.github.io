saltwort

noun
Old World marsh plant with prickly leaves
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔltˌwɔrt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːltˌwɜːt /; / ˈsɔːltwət / (BrE)
a type of plant that grows in salty coastal areas or salt marshes
