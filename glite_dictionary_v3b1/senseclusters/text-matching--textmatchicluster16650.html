text-matching

noun
meaning-based text similarity
4.4
Pronunciation: /tekst ˈmæt.ʃɪŋ/ (AmE), /tekst ˈmæt.ʃɪŋ/ (BrE)
(in computing and natural language processing) the process of determining whether two pieces of text are similar in meaning rather than just in exact wording or character sequences.

Examples

Text-matching for meaning
The platform relies on text-matching to detect duplicate questions based on meaning, not exact wording.
Researchers improved their chatbot by training a model for semantic text-matching across informal user messages.
Plagiarism detectors now use advanced text-matching that recognizes paraphrases and similar ideas.

Related Terms

  • synonym: paraphrase detection - the task of identifying whether two sentences express the same idea using different words
  • synonym: semantic similarity - a measure of how similar two pieces of text are in meaning
