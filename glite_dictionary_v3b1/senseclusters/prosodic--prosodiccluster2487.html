prosodic

adjective
relating to rhythm and intonation in speech
9.9
Pronunciation: / prəˈsɑdɪk / (AmE), / prəˈsɒdɪk / (BrE)
relating to the rhythmic and intonational aspects of speech, such as pitch, stress, and juncture
