crossway

noun
a road or street junction
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɔsˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈkrɒsˌweɪ / (BrE)
a place where one road or street crosses another; a junction or intersection

Examples

A crossway junction
Pedestrians should be careful when crossing the crossway during rush hour.
The two highways meet at a crossway just outside the city limits.
There is a busy crossway at the center of town.

Related Terms

  • synonym: intersection - a place where two or more roads cross
