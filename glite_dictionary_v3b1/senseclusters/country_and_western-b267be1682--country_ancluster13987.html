country and western

noun
music genre from southern U.S.
1.0 2.7
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌntri ənd ˈwɛstɚn / (AmE), / ˈkʌntri ənd ˈwɛstən / (BrE)
A style of popular music originating in the southern United States, typically featuring simple melodies and lyrics, and played mainly on stringed instruments such as the guitar, banjo, or fiddle.

Examples

Classic country and western songs
He learned to play the guitar by listening to classic country and western songs.
Her favorite genre is country and western, and she never misses a local concert.
The radio station plays mostly country and western hits from the 1950s and 1960s.
