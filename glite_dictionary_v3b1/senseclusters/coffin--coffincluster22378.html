coffin

verb
place in a coffin
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑf.ɪn / (AmE), / ˈkɒf.ɪn / (BrE)
to place (a dead body) into a coffin for burial or cremation

Examples

To coffin a body
After the ceremony, they coffined the remains with great care.
All the deceased were coffined before the service began.
The family watched as the undertakers coffined her gently.
