pfft

noun
sound of dismissal
9.9
Pronunciation: / pfːt / (AmE), / pfːt / (BrE)
a short, sharp sound made by blowing air through the lips, often used to express contempt, dismissal, or mild amusement

Examples

A quick pfft
With a quick pfft, she dismissed the idea as completely ridiculous.
He ended the conversation with a loud pfft and walked away.
Whenever someone mentions that rumor, I just give a little pfft and move on.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scoff - an expression of scorn or disbelief
  • synonym: tsk - a sound made to express disapproval or annoyance
