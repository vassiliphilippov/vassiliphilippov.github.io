motor scooter

noun
small motorcycle with low-powered engine
Pronunciation: / ˈmoʊtər ˌskuːtər / (AmE), / ˈməʊtə ˌskuːtə / (BrE)
a small motorcycle with a platform for the operator's feet, small wheels, and a low-powered engine
