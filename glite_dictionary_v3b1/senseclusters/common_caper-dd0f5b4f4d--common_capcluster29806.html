common caper

noun
Mediterranean shrub grown for pickled flower buds
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmən ˈkeɪpər / (AmE), / ˈkɒmən ˈkeɪpə / (BrE)
a sprawling, spiny shrub of the Mediterranean region, cultivated for its edible flower buds, which are pickled and used in cooking
