year-round

adjective
all year long; throughout the year
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈjɪr ˌraʊnd /; / jɪr ˈraʊnd / (AmE), / ˈjɪə ˌraʊnd /; / jɪə ˈraʊnd / (BrE)
happening, used, or available throughout the entire year; not limited to a particular season

Examples

Year-round market
Because of the mild climate, the market remains year-round and never closes.
The hotel has a year-round heated pool for guests to enjoy.
They offer year-round classes at the community center for all ages.
Many residents enjoy the mild climate, which allows for year-round gardening.
The indoor pool is open to guests year-round no matter the weather.
This museum offers year-round programs for children and families.
