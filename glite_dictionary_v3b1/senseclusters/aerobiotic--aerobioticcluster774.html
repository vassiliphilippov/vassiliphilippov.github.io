(of an organism) requiring oxygen to live or function; living or active only in the presence of oxygen.

Examples

Aerobiotic bacteria
Some bacteria are aerobiotic and require air to survive and grow.
The aerobiotic organisms thrived in the oxygen-rich environment provided in the laboratory.
Unlike anaerobic species, aerobiotic microbes cannot grow without access to oxygen.

Synonyms

  • aerophilic
    9.5 0.00/m

  • aerophilous
    9.9 0.00/m
