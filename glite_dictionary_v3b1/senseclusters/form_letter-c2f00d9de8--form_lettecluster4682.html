form letter

noun
a standardized letter sent to many people
2.7 2.7
Pronunciation: /fɔːrm ˈlet.ər/ (AmE), /fɔːm ˈlet.ə/ (BrE)
a letter that is produced in multiple copies, often using a standard template, and sent to a large number of recipients, usually with minimal or no personalization

Examples

A form letter
A form letter can save time, but it may seem impersonal to the recipient.
Many customers were disappointed to receive only a form letter in response to their complaints.
The company sent a form letter to all applicants thanking them for their interest.

Related Terms

  • synonym: circular letter - a letter sent to many people with identical content
