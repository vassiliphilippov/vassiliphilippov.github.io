overhunting

noun
excessive animal hunting
Pronunciation: / ˌoʊvərˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌəʊvəˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of hunting animals excessively, leading to a decline in their populations or possible extinction

Examples

Strict laws were introduced to prevent overhunting and protect wildlife populations.
The decline of the elephant population is partly a result of overhunting for ivory.
