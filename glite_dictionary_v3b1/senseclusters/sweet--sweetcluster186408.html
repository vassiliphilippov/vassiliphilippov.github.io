sweet

noun
the sugary taste sensation
Pronunciation: / swit / (AmE), / swiːt / (BrE)
the taste or flavor of sugar or something sugary; the sensation of sweetness

Examples

The sweet of honey
Children often prefer snacks with a pronounced sweet over those that are salty or bitter.
Some medicines are given a sweet flavor to make them easier for children to take.
The dessert had just the right amount of sweet, making it very enjoyable.
Children often crave the sweet of candy after dinner.
The ripe strawberries had a natural sweet that filled the room with aroma.
This fruit lacks the usual sweet and tastes a bit sour today.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bitter - having a sharp, pungent taste; one of the main tastes
  • antonym: salty - tasting of or containing salt; one of the main tastes
  • antonym: sour - having an acidic taste; one of the main tastes
  • antonym: umami - a taste sensation produced by certain amino acids, savory
