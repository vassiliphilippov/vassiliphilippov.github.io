incunabulum

noun
early printed book
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnkjəˈnæbjələm / (AmE), / ˌɪnkjʊˈnæbjʊləm / (BrE)
a book printed before the year 1501, during the earliest period of printing with movable type in Europe
