toquilla

noun
palm fiber for hats
9.9
Pronunciation: / toʊˈkiːjə / (AmE), / təˈkiːjə / (BrE)
a type of palm fiber from South America used to weave Panama hats and other items

Examples

Toquilla straw hat
The artisan skillfully wove the toquilla fibers into a beautiful Panama hat.
Ecuador is famous for producing high-quality toquilla used in traditional crafts.
She learned how to prepare and dye toquilla for weaving baskets and hats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: raffia - fiber from a palm tree used for weaving
  • synonym: straw - dried stalks of grain plants used for weaving or packing
