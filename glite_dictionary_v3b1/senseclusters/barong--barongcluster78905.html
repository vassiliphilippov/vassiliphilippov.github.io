barong

noun
Filipino formal shirt
Pronunciation: / bəˈrɔŋ / (AmE), / bəˈrɒŋ / (BrE)
a traditional embroidered formal shirt from the Philippines, typically worn by men on special occasions

Examples

A barong shirt
He wore a beautifully embroidered barong to the wedding ceremony.
The president delivered his speech dressed in a traditional barong.
Many Filipino men choose a barong instead of a suit for formal events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: guayabera - a lightweight, embroidered shirt worn in Latin America
  • antonym: suit - a formal set of clothes typically worn by men
