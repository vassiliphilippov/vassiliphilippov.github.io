narrowed

adjective
reduced in width or scope
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛroʊd /; / ˈnæroʊd / (AmE), / ˈnærəʊd / (BrE)
made or become narrower, smaller, or more limited in size, width, or scope

Examples

A narrowed road

His narrowed focus allowed him to excel in his studies.
She looked at him with narrowed eyes, clearly suspicious of his motives.
The road passes through a narrowed section between two tall cliffs.
