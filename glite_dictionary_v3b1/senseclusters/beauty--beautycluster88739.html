beauty

noun
outstanding example of its kind
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈbjuːti / (AmE), / ˈbjuːti / (BrE)
a person or thing that is an outstanding example of its kind, especially in a pleasing or remarkable way.

Examples

A real beauty
He restored a vintage car and now it's an absolute beauty.
That goal was a real beauty, perfectly struck from thirty yards out.
You made a cake? Wow, that's a beauty!
