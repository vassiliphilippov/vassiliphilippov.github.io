stenotype

noun
shorthand typing system
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈstɛnoʊˌtaɪp / (AmE), / ˈstɛnəʊˌtaɪp / (BrE)
a system of shorthand typing used with a special machine for rapid transcription, especially in courtrooms or official settings

Examples

Stenotype system
She learned stenotype to work as a court reporter.
The accuracy of stenotype makes it essential for legal proceedings.
Many official transcripts are produced using the stenotype method.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shorthand - a method of rapid writing using symbols or abbreviations
  • synonym: transcription - the act of making a written copy of spoken words
