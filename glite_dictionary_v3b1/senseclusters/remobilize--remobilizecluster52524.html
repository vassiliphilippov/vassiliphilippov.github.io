remobilize

verb
to make usable again
6.8
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈmoʊ.bə.laɪz/ /; / /riː.ˈmoʊ.bə.laɪz/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪz/ /; / /riː.ˈməʊ.bɪ.laɪz/ (BrE)
to make something able to move or be used again, or to organize or prepare again for movement or action, especially after a pause or interruption

  • synonym: mobilize - to prepare or organize for movement or use
