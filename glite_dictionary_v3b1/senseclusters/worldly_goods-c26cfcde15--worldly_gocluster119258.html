worldly goods

noun
material possessions owned by a person
Pronunciation: / ˈwɝːldli ɡʊdz / (AmE), / ˈwɜːldli ɡʊdz / (BrE)
all the possessions and property owned by a person, especially material and tangible items

Examples

All worldly goods

They donated their worldly goods to charity before joining the monastery.
When she moved to the new country, she sold most of her worldly goods.
