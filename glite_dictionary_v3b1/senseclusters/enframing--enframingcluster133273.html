enframing

noun
adding a frame
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / ɪnˈfreɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of placing or constructing a frame around an object, such as a picture, window, or door

Examples

Enframing a window
The enframing of the window was completed with elegant wooden trim.
Careful enframing can enhance the beauty of a painting and protect it from damage.
The restoration project included the enframing of several historic doorways in the old mansion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: framing - the act of building or putting a frame around something
