deblocking

noun
removal of image artifacts
8.1
Pronunciation: / diˈblɑkɪŋ / (AmE), / diˈblɒkɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of removing blocking artifacts from digital images or video to improve visual quality

Examples

Deblocking a video
The video player applies deblocking to make low-quality footage look smoother.
Advanced deblocking techniques can greatly enhance the clarity of compressed images.
Engineers developed a new deblocking algorithm to reduce visible squares in digital photos.

Related Terms

  • antonym: compression - the process of reducing the size of data, often causing artifacts
  • synonym: filtering - the process of removing unwanted components from a signal or image
