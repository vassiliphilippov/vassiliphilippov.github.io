counterindoctrination

noun
opposing indoctrination
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkaʊntərɪnˌdɑktrəˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkaʊntərɪnˌdɒktrɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act or process of opposing, challenging, or undoing indoctrination, often by teaching or deprogramming

Examples

Counterindoctrination programs
The school introduced counterindoctrination programs to encourage independent thinking among students.
Activists promoted counterindoctrination to help communities resist propaganda.
Workshops on counterindoctrination taught participants how to question biased information.
After leaving the cult, he participated in counterindoctrination sessions to help rebuild his critical thinking skills.
The organization offers counterindoctrination programs for individuals affected by extremist groups.
Her family sought professional counterindoctrination to help her recover from years of manipulation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deprogramming - the process of undoing previous indoctrination, especially in cults
  • synonym: education - the process of teaching or learning, especially in a school or college
  • antonym: indoctrination - the process of teaching someone to accept a set of beliefs uncritically
