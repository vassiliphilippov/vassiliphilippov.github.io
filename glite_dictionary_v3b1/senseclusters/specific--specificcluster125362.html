specific

adjective
clearly stated or precise
Pronunciation: / spəˈsɪfɪk / (AmE), / spəˈsɪfɪk /; / spɪˈsɪfɪk / (BrE)
clearly defined, precise, or relating to a particular thing, person, or situation (not general)

Examples

Related Terms

  • antonym: general - not limited to one particular person, place, or thing
  • synonym: precise - marked by exactness and accuracy of detail
  • antonym: vague - not clearly expressed or detailed
