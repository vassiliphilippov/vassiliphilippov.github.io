Synonyms

  • unworkable
    5.1 1.12/m

  • impossible

  • unrealizable
    8.5 0.11/m
    unrealizable describes something that cannot be made real or carried out, while unfeasible refers to plans that are impracticable.
  • unachievable
    6.3 0.32/m

  • unattainable
    6.3 1.63/m

  • unfulfillable
    8.7 0.04/m

  • undoable
