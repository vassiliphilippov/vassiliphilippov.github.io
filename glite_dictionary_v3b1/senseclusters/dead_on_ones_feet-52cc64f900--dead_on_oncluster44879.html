dead on one's feet

adjective
extremely tired or exhausted
5.2
Pronunciation: / dɛd ɑn wʌnz fit / (AmE), / dɛd ɒn wʌnz fiːt / (BrE)
extremely tired or exhausted, usually as a result of physical or mental effort

Examples

He ran the entire race but was dead on his feet when he finally reached the finish line.
