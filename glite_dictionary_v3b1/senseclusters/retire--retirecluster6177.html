retire

verb
to go to bed; prepare for sleep (formal, old-fashioned)
4.6
Pronunciation: / rəˈtaɪr / (AmE), / rɪˈtaɪə / (BrE)
(formal) To go to bed or prepare to go to sleep, especially at night.

Examples

To retire for the night
After a long day, she decided to retire early and get some rest.
Gentlemen, shall we retire for the evening?
He always likes to retire at the same hour every night.
