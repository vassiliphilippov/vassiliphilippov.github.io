divine messenger

noun
supernatural being acting as God's messenger
Pronunciation: / dəˈvaɪn ˈmɛsəndʒɚ / (AmE), / dɪˈvaɪn ˈmɛsɪndʒə / (BrE)
a being, often supernatural, who serves as a messenger from God or a deity

Examples

Divine messenger Gabriel
Gabriel is commonly known as a divine messenger in various religious traditions.
Many religions describe the appearance of a divine messenger to deliver an important revelation.
Some believers prayed for a divine messenger to guide them through difficult times.
