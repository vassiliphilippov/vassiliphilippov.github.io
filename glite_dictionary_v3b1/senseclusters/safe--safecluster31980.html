safe

noun
condom, contraceptive device
Pronunciation: / seɪf / (AmE), / seɪf / (BrE)
(informal) A contraceptive device in the form of a thin sheath, usually of rubber or latex, worn over the penis during sexual intercourse to prevent pregnancy or infection.

Examples

A safe in his wallet
He always carries a safe in his wallet, just in case.
It’s important to use a safe to protect against sexually transmitted infections.
She bought a box of safes at the pharmacy before her trip.

Related Terms

  • synonym: condom - a sheath-shaped barrier device, used during sexual intercourse
