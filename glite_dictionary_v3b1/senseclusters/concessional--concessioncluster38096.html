concessional

adjective
relating to a concession
7.6
Pronunciation: / kənˈsɛʃənəl / (AmE), / kənˈsɛʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to or involving a concession, especially a right, license, or contract granted by an authority.

Examples

Concessional license granted
The company received a concessional license to operate the new railway line.
Many developing countries benefit from concessional loans with lower interest rates.
The city council offered concessional terms to encourage investment in public transport.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contractual - relating to a contract or agreement
  • antonym: restrictive - imposing limitations or restrictions
