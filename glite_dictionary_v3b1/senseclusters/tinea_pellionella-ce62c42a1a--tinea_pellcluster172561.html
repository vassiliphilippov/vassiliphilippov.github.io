Tinea pellionella

noun
a species of clothes moth
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪniə ˌpɛliəˈnɛlə / (AmE), / ˈtɪniə ˌpɛliəˈnɛlə / (BrE)
a species of clothes moth whose larvae live in silken tubes made from their food material, often causing damage to fabrics.

Examples

Tinea pellionella moth larvae
Tinea pellionella is commonly found in households where natural fibers are present.
Scientists studied the unique silk tubes spun by Tinea pellionella for protection.
The larvae of Tinea pellionella can cause serious damage to woollen garments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: clothes moth - a moth that feeds on and damages fabrics
