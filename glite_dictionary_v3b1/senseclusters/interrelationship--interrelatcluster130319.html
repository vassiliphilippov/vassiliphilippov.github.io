interrelationship

noun
mutual or reciprocal relationship
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.tər.rɪˈleɪ.ʃənˌʃɪp / (AmE), / ˌɪn.tə.rɪˈleɪ.ʃən.ʃɪp / (BrE)
the way in which two or more things or people are connected to and affect each other, often in a mutual or reciprocal manner

Examples

The interrelationship between factors
Economic growth and environmental protection have a complex interrelationship.
Scientists study the interrelationship between different species in an ecosystem.
The interrelationship of history and culture shapes national identity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: relationship - the way in which two things are connected
