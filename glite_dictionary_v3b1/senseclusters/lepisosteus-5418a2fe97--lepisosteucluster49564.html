Lepisosteus

noun
genus of freshwater gars
Pronunciation: / ˌlɛpɪˈsɑstiəs / (AmE), / ˌlɛpɪˈsɒstiəs / (BrE)
A genus of freshwater fishes known as gars, native to North and Central America, distinguished by their elongated bodies and long jaws filled with sharp teeth.
