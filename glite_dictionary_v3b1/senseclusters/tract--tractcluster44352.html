tract

noun
system of connected body parts with a function
Pronunciation: / trækt / (AmE), / trakt / (BrE)
a system of connected body parts that work together for a specific function, such as digestion or breathing

Examples

Digestive tract
Doctors observed inflammation in her urinary tract.
The digestive tract includes the stomach, intestines, and other organs.
The infection spread throughout the respiratory tract.

Related Terms

  • synonym: system - set of connected things working together as a whole
