conversancy

noun
familiarity or knowledge of something
4.3
Pronunciation: / kənˈvɝː.sən.si / (AmE), / kənˈvɜː.sən.si / (BrE)
the state of being familiar with or knowledgeable about something; familiarity or acquaintance

Examples

Conversancy with law
A judge must demonstrate conversancy with all relevant aspects of the law.
Her conversancy with the latest technology impressed the interviewers.
His conversancy in foreign affairs is apparent from his thoughtful analysis.
