sneeringly

adverb
in a contemptuous or scornful manner
Pronunciation: / ˈsnɪrɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈsnɪərɪŋli / (BrE)
In a way that shows contempt or scorn; with a sneer.

Examples

To speak sneeringly
He laughed sneeringly at the idea, dismissing it with a wave of his hand.
She replied sneeringly to his suggestion, making it clear she thought it was foolish.
The critic wrote sneeringly about the play, mocking its lack of originality.

  • synonym: contemptuously - in a manner showing contempt
