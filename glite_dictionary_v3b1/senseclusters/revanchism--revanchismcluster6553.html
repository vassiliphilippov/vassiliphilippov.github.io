revanchism

noun
policy to regain territory
9.9
Pronunciation: / rɪˈvɑːntʃɪzəm / (AmE), / rɪˈvɑːntʃɪzəm /; / rɪˈvantʃɪzəm / (BrE)
a policy or attitude of trying to recover lost territory or status, often with a sense of revenge, especially in politics

Critics accused the new administration of revanchism rather than focusing on progress.
