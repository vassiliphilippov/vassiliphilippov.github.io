ejaculate

verb
to eject semen suddenly
2.9
Pronunciation: / ɪˈdʒækjəˌleɪt / (AmE), / ɪˈdʒækjʊleɪt / (BrE)
(of a male or a male animal) to suddenly eject semen from the penis, typically during orgasm.

Examples

To ejaculate semen
Certain medical conditions may make it difficult or impossible for a man to ejaculate.
During sexual intercourse, the male typically ejaculates at the moment of orgasm.
Some animals are able to ejaculate multiple times within a short period during mating season.
