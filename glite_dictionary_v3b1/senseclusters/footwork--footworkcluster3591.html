footwork

noun
Chicago electronic music and dance style
7.7
Pronunciation: /ˈfʊt.wɜːrk/ /; / /ˈfʊ.twɜːrk/ (AmE), /ˈfʊt.wɜːk/ /; / /ˈfʊ.twɜːk/ (BrE)
a fast-paced style of electronic dance music from Chicago and the street dance associated with it, characterized by rapid, syncopated beats and intricate, fast foot movements.

Examples

Chicago footwork
The club's night showcased Chicago footwork with rapid percussion and intricate foot patterns.
Producers often sample old records to create modern footwork tracks with shuffled hi-hats.
Dancers practiced the complex footwork for hours before performing at the festival.
