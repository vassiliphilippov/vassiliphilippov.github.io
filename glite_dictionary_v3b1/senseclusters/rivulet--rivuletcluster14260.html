rivulet

noun
a small stream of water
4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪv.jə.lət / (AmE), / ˈrɪv.jʊ.lət / (BrE)
a very small stream of water

Examples

A rivulet of water
A narrow rivulet of water trickled down the side of the mountain.
During the rainstorm, rivulets formed on the window panes and raced downward.
She watched a rivulet meander through the lush green meadow.
