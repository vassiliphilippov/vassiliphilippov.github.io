carrier

noun
person who delivers mail
2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈkɛriər /; / ˈkæriɚ /; / ˈkæriər / (AmE), / ˈkæriə / (BrE)
a person or company that delivers or transports things such as mail, newspapers, goods, or messages

Examples

Mail carrier
Rural carriers often drive long distances to complete their routes.

A job as a newspaper carrier teaches responsibility to many young people.
My brother worked as a carrier after school, delivering newspapers around the neighborhood.
The carrier always arrived early to make sure everyone got the morning paper on time.
A courier carrier delivered the documents to the lawyer's office.
The airline is the national carrier of Japan, connecting dozens of destinations.
We chose a reliable carrier to ship our furniture across the country.
