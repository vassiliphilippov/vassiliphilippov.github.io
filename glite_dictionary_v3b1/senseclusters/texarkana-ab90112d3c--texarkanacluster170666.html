Texarkana

proper noun
city on the Arkansas-Texas border
Pronunciation: / ˌtɛksɑːrˈkænə / (AmE), / ˌtɛksɑːˈkɑːnə /; / ˌtɛksɑːˈkænə / (BrE)
a city located on the border between Texas and Arkansas, consisting of two adjacent cities with the same name (one in each state)
