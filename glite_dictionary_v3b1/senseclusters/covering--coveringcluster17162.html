covering

noun
musical cover version
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkʌv.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
a performance or recording of a song that was originally performed or recorded by another artist.

Examples

A covering song

Her acoustic covering of the pop single went viral after the live show.
They released several coverings of 1980s songs on their tribute album.
