ricey

adjective
like rice
Pronunciation: / ˈraɪsi / (AmE), / ˈraɪsi / (BrE)
having the taste, smell, or qualities of rice, or consisting largely of rice

Examples

Ricey flavor
The casserole had a pleasantly ricey aroma that filled the kitchen.
She prefers a ricey texture in her pudding rather than something completely smooth.
After cooking, the soup became thick and ricey, perfect for a cold evening.

Related Terms

  • synonym: grainy - having a texture or quality like grains
