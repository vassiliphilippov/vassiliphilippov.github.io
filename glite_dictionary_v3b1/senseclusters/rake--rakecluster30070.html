rake

verb
scrape gently or lightly
5.0
Pronunciation: / reɪk / (AmE), / reɪk / (BrE)
to scrape or scratch something lightly or gently, often so as not to cause damage

Examples

To rake the skin
If you rake the skin too hard, it might start to bleed.
She managed to rake her nails across the surface without leaving a mark.
The cat gently raked my arm with its paw as it stretched.
