lemma

noun
headword in a dictionary entry
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛm.ə / (AmE), / ˈlɛmə / (BrE)
the headword or main entry in a dictionary, glossary, or similar reference work; the stated subject of an annotation or literary passage

Examples

Dictionary lemma
Each lemma in the glossary is accompanied by a brief definition.
The lemma for this verb is listed at the top of the dictionary entry.
The lemma introduces the subject of the annotation in the ancient manuscript.

Related Terms

  • synonym: headword - the principal word under which a definition appears in a dictionary
