eucalyptus tree

noun
tree of the genus Eucalyptus
2.6
Pronunciation: / juːˈkæləptəs triː / (AmE), / juːˈkælɪptəs triː / (BrE)
A tree of the genus Eucalyptus, native chiefly to Australia, known for aromatic leaves and peeling bark.

Examples

A eucalyptus tree leaf
A koala spends most of its time in a eucalyptus tree eating leaves.
The eucalyptus tree is native to Australia but has been planted in many parts of the world.
We planted a young eucalyptus tree in our backyard to provide shade and fragrance.
