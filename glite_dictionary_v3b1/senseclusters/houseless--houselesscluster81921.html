houseless

noun
people without homes
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈhaʊsləs / (AmE), / ˈhaʊsləs / (BrE)
people who do not have a home to live in; the homeless

Examples

Help the houseless
The city opened shelters to help the houseless during the winter months.
Volunteers distributed food and blankets to the houseless in the downtown area.
Many organizations work to support the houseless and help them find stable housing.
