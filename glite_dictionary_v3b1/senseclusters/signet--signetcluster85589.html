signet

noun
a seal used to mark documents officially
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪɡ.nɪt / (AmE), / ˈsɪɡ.nɪt / (BrE)
a small seal, especially one set in a ring, used for marking documents officially or for authentication

Examples

A signet ring
Each important family once had a unique signet for authenticating their correspondence.
He pressed his signet into the soft wax to seal the letter.
The royal decree bore the king's signet, making it official.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seal - a device used to make an impression in wax or other material for authentication
