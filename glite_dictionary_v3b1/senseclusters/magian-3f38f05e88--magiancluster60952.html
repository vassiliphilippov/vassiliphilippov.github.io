Magian

noun
magician or sorcerer
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒiən / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒiən / (BrE)
a magician or sorcerer, especially one who practices magic or occult rites

Examples

A Magian sorcerer
The villagers whispered stories about the mysterious Magian who lived at the edge of the forest.
Legends say a powerful Magian once protected the ancient city from harm.
During the festival, a traveling Magian amazed the crowd with his magical tricks.

Related Terms

  • synonym: magician - a person who performs magic or illusions
  • degree lower: priest - a person authorized to perform religious rites
  • synonym: sorcerer - a person who claims or is believed to have magical powers
