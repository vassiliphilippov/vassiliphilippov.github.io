unrefunded

adjective
not eligible for refund
6.3
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnrɪˈfʌndɪd / (AmE), / ˌʌnrɪˈfʌndɪd / (BrE)
not eligible to be refunded or returned for a refund

Examples

Unrefunded tickets
Tickets purchased at a discount are often unrefunded and cannot be exchanged.
Please note that all sale items are unrefunded according to store policy.
The airline made it clear that promotional fares would remain unrefunded under any circumstances.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nonrefundable - not able to be refunded
  • antonym: refundable - able to be refunded
