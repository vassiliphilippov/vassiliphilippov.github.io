air crew

noun
personnel operating an aircraft
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɛr ˌkru / (AmE), / ˈeə ˌkruː / (BrE)
The group of people responsible for operating an aircraft during a flight, including pilots and cabin staff.

Examples

The air crew members
After a long journey, the tired air crew was finally able to rest.
Each member of the air crew has a specific role during the flight.
The air crew worked efficiently to ensure all safety procedures were followed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flight crew - the personnel responsible for the operation and safety of an aircraft during flight
  • antonym: ground crew - the personnel who maintain and service aircraft on the ground
