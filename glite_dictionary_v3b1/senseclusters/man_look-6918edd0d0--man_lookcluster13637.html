man look

noun
an expression showing confusion or misunderstanding
8.7
Pronunciation: / mæn lʊk / (AmE), / man lʊk / (BrE)
an expression of confusion or incomprehension, typically associated with men and used humorously to suggest difficulty in understanding or finding something

Examples

A man look of confusion
He couldn't find the salt and just stood there with a man look on his face.
She laughed at his man look when he tried to figure out the remote.
When she asked him where her keys were, he gave her a man look.
