hydrologically

adverb
in relation to water
Pronunciation: / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɑdʒɪkli / (AmE), / ˌhaɪdrəˈlɒdʒɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to the movement, distribution, or properties of water

Examples

To be hydrologically connected
The two lakes are hydrologically connected through underground streams.
This area is hydrologically sensitive due to frequent flooding during heavy rains.
The dam was designed to function hydrologically with the natural flow of the river.

Related Terms

  • synonym: geologically - in a way that relates to the structure or history of the earth
