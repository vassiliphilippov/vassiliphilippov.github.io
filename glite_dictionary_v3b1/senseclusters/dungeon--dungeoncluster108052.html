dungeon

noun
video-game level or area
4.5
Pronunciation: /ˈdʌn.dʒən/ (AmE), /ˈdʌn.dʒən/ (BrE)
(in video games and role-playing games) a designed game area or level, often a multi-room environment containing enemies, traps, puzzles, or treasure for players to explore and clear.

Examples

Video game dungeon
The party descended into the dungeon to battle goblins and solve ancient puzzles.
She spent the afternoon exploring a new dungeon full of traps and hidden treasure.
In the game's latest update, the developers added randomized dungeons for endless replayability.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instance - a separate, replayable copy of a game area or dungeon used in multiplayer games.
  • synonym: level - a self-contained stage or area within a video game where players complete objectives.
