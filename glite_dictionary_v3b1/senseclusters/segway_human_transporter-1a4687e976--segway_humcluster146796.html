Segway (Human Transporter)

noun
two-wheeled self-balancing personal transporter
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˈhjuː.mən trænsˌpɔr.t̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈsɛɡ.weɪ ˈhjuː.mən trænsˌpɔː.tə / (BrE)
A trademarked, self-balancing personal transportation device with two wheels, designed for use on level pedestrian surfaces.
