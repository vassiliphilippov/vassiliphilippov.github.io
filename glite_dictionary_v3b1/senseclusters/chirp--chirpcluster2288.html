chirp

noun
a short, sharp bird or insect sound
2.9
Pronunciation: / tʃɝːp / (AmE), / tʃɜːp / (BrE)
a short, high-pitched, sharp sound made especially by small birds or insects

Examples

A bird's chirp
At dawn, the chirp of sparrows filled the garden with a pleasant melody.
The sudden chirp startled her as a robin flew past her head.
We could hear the chirp of crickets coming through the open window at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tweet - a short, high-pitched sound, especially by a small bird
