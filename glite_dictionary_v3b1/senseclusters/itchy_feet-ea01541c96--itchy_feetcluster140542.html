itchy feet

noun
restless desire to travel
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɪtʃi fit / (AmE), / ˈɪtʃi fiːt / (BrE)
a strong or irresistible desire to travel or move from place to place

Examples

Itchy feet to travel
After a year in the same job, Sarah started to get itchy feet.
His itchy feet led him to travel across six continents in five years.
I've got itchy feet again and want to book another trip abroad soon.
