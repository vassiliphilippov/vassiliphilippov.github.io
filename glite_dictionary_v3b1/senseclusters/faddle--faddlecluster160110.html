faddle

noun
trivial fuss
a trivial fuss, matter, or piece of nonsense that is unimportant or not worth attention

Examples

A silly faddle
His constant faddle about minor issues made the meeting drag on much longer than necessary.
She dismissed the rumors as mere faddle and focused on her work.
Let's not waste time on this faddle when we have more important things to discuss.

Related Terms

  • antonym: importance - the quality of being significant or valuable
  • synonym: nonsense - ideas or talk that is silly or has no meaning
  • synonym: triviality - something unimportant or insignificant
