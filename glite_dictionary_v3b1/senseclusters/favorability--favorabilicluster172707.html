favorability

noun
degree of advantage
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˌfeɪvərəˈbɪləti / (AmE), / ˌfeɪvərəˈbɪləti / (BrE)
the degree to which something is advantageous, suitable, or likely to produce a desired result

Examples

Favorability of the conditions
The favorability of the weather made it possible to hold the event outdoors.
Analysts discussed the favorability of the new policy for small businesses.
Her decision depended on the favorability of the current economic climate.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advantageousness - the quality of being beneficial or helpful
  • synonym: suitability - the quality of being right or appropriate for a particular purpose
  • antonym: unfavorability - the degree to which something is disadvantageous or unsuitable
