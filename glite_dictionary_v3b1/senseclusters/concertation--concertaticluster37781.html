concertation

noun
joint agreement
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑːnsərˈteɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌkɒnsəˈteɪʃən / (BrE)
the process or result of people consulting and working together to reach an agreement or coordinated plan

Examples

Careful concertation between groups
The new policy was the result of careful concertation between government officials and community leaders.
International concertation is essential when addressing global environmental challenges.
After several meetings, the two companies reached a concertation on their future collaboration.
The new environmental policy was created through concertation between local authorities and community groups.
Effective concertation among team members led to a successful project outcome.
International agreements often require careful concertation between different countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: agreement - a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement between parties
  • synonym: collaboration - the act of working together with others
  • antonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument
  • synonym: consultation - the act of discussing something with others before making a decision
  • antonym: disagreement - a lack of consensus or harmony between parties
