national anthem

noun
official song representing a country
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈnæʃənəl ˈænθəm / (AmE), / ˈnæʃənəl ˈanθəm / (BrE)
an official song adopted by a country to represent it, usually played at ceremonies and national events

Examples

The national anthem song
Every country has its own national anthem that is recognized worldwide.
Students stood at attention while the national anthem was sung at the assembly.
The orchestra played the national anthem before the start of the football match.
