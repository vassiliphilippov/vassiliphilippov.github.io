antihunting

adjective
against animal hunting
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈhʌntɪŋ / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈhʌntɪŋ / (BrE)
opposed to hunting or intended to prevent or restrict hunting

Examples

Antihunting laws
Several antihunting groups protested outside the city hall during the debate on new wildlife laws.
The country passed strict antihunting legislation to protect endangered species from extinction.
Many conservationists support antihunting measures to preserve animal populations.
The antihunting movement has gained support among animal rights activists.
Many antihunting organizations campaign for stricter wildlife protection laws.
She wore an antihunting badge to show her opposition to the sport.
