tropical pitcher plant

noun
a tropical carnivorous plant (Nepenthes)
Pronunciation: / ˈtrɑpɪkəl ˈpɪtʃər plænt / (AmE), / ˈtrɒpɪkəl ˈpɪtʃə plɑːnt / (BrE)
