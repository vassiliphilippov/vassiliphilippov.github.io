noncommissioned officer

noun
military officer from enlisted ranks
5.1 5.0
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑn.kəˈmɪʃ.ənd ˈɑː.fɪ.sər / (AmE), / ˌnɒn.kəˈmɪʃ.ənd ˈɒ.fɪ.sə / (BrE)
a member of the armed forces who has been given authority by promotion from the enlisted ranks but is not a commissioned officer

Examples

A noncommissioned officer supervises troops
Each noncommissioned officer was responsible for supervising the enlisted personnel in their unit.
He was promoted to noncommissioned officer after several years of exemplary service.
The training program for noncommissioned officers focuses on leadership and management.
