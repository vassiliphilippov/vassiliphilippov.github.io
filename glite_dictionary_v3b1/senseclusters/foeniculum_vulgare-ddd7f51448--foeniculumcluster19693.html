Foeniculum vulgare

noun
the fennel plant, used for seasoning
7.5
Pronunciation: / foʊˌnɪkjəl vʊlˈɡɛri / (AmE), / fəʊˌnɪkjʊl vʌlˈɡɑːreɪ / (BrE)
the fennel plant, a strongly aromatic herb with aniseed-scented leaves and seeds used for seasoning in cooking and herbal medicine

Examples

Foeniculum vulgare seeds
Herbalists recommend infusions made from Foeniculum vulgare to aid digestion.
In the Mediterranean, both the leaves and bulbs of Foeniculum vulgare are common ingredients in cooking.
The seeds of Foeniculum vulgare are often used to flavor bread and pastries.
