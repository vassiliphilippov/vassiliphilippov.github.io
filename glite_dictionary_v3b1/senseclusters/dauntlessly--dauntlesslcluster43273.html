dauntlessly

adverb
in a fearless manner
Pronunciation: / ˈdɔntləsli / (AmE), / ˈdɔːntləsli / (BrE)
In a way that shows no fear; bravely and boldly.

Examples

To dauntlessly face danger
Explorers ventured dauntlessly into the uncharted wilderness.
He faced the criticism dauntlessly, determined to defend his beliefs.
She dauntlessly opposed the unfair policy, despite the risks involved.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cowardly - in a way that shows lack of courage
  • synonym: fearlessly - without fear; courageously
