bulleted

verb
to add bullet points
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊlɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈbʊlɪtɪd / (BrE)
marked or formatted with bullet points, especially in a list or document.

Examples

To bullet the topics
The teacher bulleted the key topics on the board for everyone to see.
She bulleted her resume to make her achievements stand out.
For clarity, the instructions were bulleted in the manual.

Related Terms

  • synonym: list - to arrange items in a sequence, often with symbols or numbers
  • antonym: numbered - marked or formatted with numbers in a list
