Panama Canal Zone

proper noun
historical area containing the Panama Canal
Pronunciation: / ˌpænəˌmɑ kəˈnæl zoʊn / (AmE), / ˌpanəmə kəˈnal zəʊn / (BrE)
a former zone comprising a strip of land across the Isthmus of Panama, including the Panama Canal, once governed by the United States

Examples

The Panama Canal Zone area
The United States controlled the Panama Canal Zone until 1979.
The treaty returned sovereignty of the Panama Canal Zone to Panama.
Workers from many countries came to live in the Panama Canal Zone during the construction era.
