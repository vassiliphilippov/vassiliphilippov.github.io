PTT

noun
postal and telecom agency
9.1
Pronunciation: /piː tiː tiː/ (AmE), /piː tiː tiː/ (BrE)
a government department or agency responsible for postal, telegraph, and telephone services, especially in some countries or historically

Examples

National PTT agency
Before privatization, the national PTT controlled all telephone and mail services.
Many European countries once had a single PTT managing communications infrastructure.
The PTT was reorganized into separate postal and telecom companies in the 1990s.
