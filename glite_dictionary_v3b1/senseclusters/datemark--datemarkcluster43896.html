datemark

verb
to mark with date
6.3
Pronunciation: /deɪt.ˈmɑːrk/ (AmE), /deɪt.ˈmɑːk/ (BrE)
to mark or stamp something with a date to show when it was made, received, or processed

Examples

To datemark a package
Please datemark all returns from this week before we send them to accounting for processing.
The laboratory technician must datemark each sample before storage.
It is important to datemark documents to keep track of when they were received.

  • synonym: timestamp - to record or mark the time and date on something
