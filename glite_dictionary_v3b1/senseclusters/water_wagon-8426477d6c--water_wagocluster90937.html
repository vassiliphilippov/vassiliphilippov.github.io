Examples

During the drought, the water wagon provided much-needed supplies to the isolated camp.

Synonyms

    6.2 0.02/m

  • watering cart
    3.2 0.00/m
    watering cart is a vehicle with a tank and sprinkler that sprays roads, while water wagon serves larger supply and dust control.
  • water truck
    3.2 0.2/m
    water truck is a motor vehicle with a tank used for dust control or firefighting, while water wagon often names older supply vehicles.
  • water tank truck
    3.2 0.01/m
    water tank truck is a large motor vehicle with a fixed tank for moving much water, while water wagon often is smaller and not motorized.
  • water cart
    4.3 0.00/m
    water cart is a small cart that sells or carries drinking water to people, while water wagon supplies larger crews or sprinkles roads.
