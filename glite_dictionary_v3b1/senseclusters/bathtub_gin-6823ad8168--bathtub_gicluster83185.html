bathtub gin

noun
illegally homemade gin
Pronunciation: / ˈbæθˌtʌb dʒɪn /; / ˈbætˌtʌb dʒɪn / (AmE), / ˈbɑːθˌtʌb dʒɪn /; / ˈbætˌtʌb dʒɪn / (BrE)
homemade or illegally made gin, especially produced during the Prohibition era in the United States

Examples

Bathtub gin during Prohibition
