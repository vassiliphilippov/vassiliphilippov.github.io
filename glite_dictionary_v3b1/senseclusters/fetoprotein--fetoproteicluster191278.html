fetoprotein

noun
protein antigen found in fetuses or some cancers
Pronunciation: / ˌfiːtoʊˈproʊtiːn /; / ˌfiːtoʊˈproʊtin / (AmE), / ˌfiːtəʊˈprəʊtiːn / (BrE)
Any of several proteins usually found in the fetus, and sometimes present in adults with certain cancers, serving as antigens.
