Purkinje cell

noun
type of neuron in the cerebellum
8.3
Pronunciation: /pɜːr.ˈkɪn.dʒiː sel/ /; / /pɜːr.ˈkɪn.dʒeɪ sel/ (AmE), /pə.ˈkɪn.dʒiː sel/ /; / /pə.ˈkɪn.dʒeɪ sel/ (BrE)
