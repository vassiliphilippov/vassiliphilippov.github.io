nuttiness

noun
nut-like flavor
Pronunciation: / ˈnʌtinəs / (AmE), / ˈnʌtinəs / (BrE)
a flavor or aroma that is similar to that of nuts, often used to describe food or drink

Examples

Nuttiness in cheese
The nuttiness of the aged cheddar complemented the sweet fig jam on her plate.
Coffee lovers often appreciate the subtle nuttiness found in certain roasts.
The wine's nuttiness made it a perfect match for the creamy pasta dish.

Related Terms

  • synonym: earthiness - a flavor or aroma reminiscent of soil or the earth
  • antonym: sweetness - the quality of tasting sweet
