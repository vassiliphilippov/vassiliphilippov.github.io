mad

adjective
wildly excited; emotionally uncontrollable
3.3
Pronunciation: / mæd / (AmE), / mad / (BrE)
showing or characterized by uncontrolled excitement or extreme emotion

Examples

Mad excitement
After winning the lottery, she did a mad dance around her living room.
The crowd became mad with excitement as the band appeared on stage.
There was a mad rush for tickets when the event was announced.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling nervousness, anger, or other strong emotions
  • synonym: delirious - in a state of wild excitement or ecstasy
  • synonym: frantic - wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion
