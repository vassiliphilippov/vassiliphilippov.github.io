fastback

adjective
car with sloping rear
5.5
Pronunciation: / ˈfæstˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈfɑːstˌbæk / (BrE)
having a sloping rear roofline that extends smoothly down to the back of a car, describing a specific car body style

Examples

Fastback car style
The new model features a fastback design that gives it a sporty appearance.
Many classic cars from the 1960s were built in the fastback style.
He prefers the look of a fastback over a traditional sedan.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hatchback - a car with a rear door that swings upward to provide access to the cargo area
  • antonym: sedan - a car with a separate trunk and a fixed roof
