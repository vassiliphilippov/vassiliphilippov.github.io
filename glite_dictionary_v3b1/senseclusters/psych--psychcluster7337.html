psych

interjection
just kidding
9.9
Pronunciation: / saɪk / (AmE), / saɪk / (BrE)
an exclamation used to indicate that a previous statement or action was a joke or not meant seriously

Examples

Psych! Just kidding
I told her I had finished all the work—psych, I haven't even started yet!
He handed me a gift and then shouted psych as he took it back.
You really thought I was moving away? Psych! I'm staying right here.

Related Terms

  • synonym: just kidding - used to show that something said was not serious
  • antonym: seriously - used to show that something is meant sincerely
