manners

noun
polite or social behavior
Pronunciation: / ˈmænərz / (AmE), / ˈmænəz / (BrE)
polite or social behavior; the way a person behaves towards others, especially according to social conventions

Examples

Good manners
Good manners are important in every culture.
His lack of manners offended some of the guests.
The child was praised for his excellent manners at the dinner table.

Related Terms

  • synonym: etiquette - the customary code of polite behavior in society
  • antonym: rudeness - lack of manners; impolite behavior
