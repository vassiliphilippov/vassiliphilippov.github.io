pearled

adjective
polished grain
5.2
Pronunciation: /pɜːrld/ (AmE), /pɜːld/ (BrE)
having had the outer hull removed by polishing, especially referring to grains like barley.

Examples

Pearled barley
The recipe calls for pearled barley to give the soup a smooth texture.
After processing, the grains became pearled and ready for cooking.
You can find pearled wheat in most health food stores.
