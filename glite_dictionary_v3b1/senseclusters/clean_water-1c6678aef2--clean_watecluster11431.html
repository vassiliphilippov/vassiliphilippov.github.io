clean water

noun
water free from impurities
Pronunciation: / klin ˈwɔtər / (AmE), / kliːn ˈwɔːtə / (BrE)
water that is free from impurities and safe to drink or use

Examples

Clean water supply

The organization helps provide clean water to remote villages.
