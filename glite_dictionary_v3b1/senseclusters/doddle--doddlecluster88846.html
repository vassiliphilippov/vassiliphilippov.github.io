doddle

noun
something very easy to do
3.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑː.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈdɒd.əl / (BrE)
A task or activity that is very easy to do.

Examples

An easy doddle
Compared to last year's race, this marathon was an absolute doddle.
Finishing the exam was a doddle for her, as she had studied thoroughly.
He said the repair job was a doddle, taking him just a few minutes.
