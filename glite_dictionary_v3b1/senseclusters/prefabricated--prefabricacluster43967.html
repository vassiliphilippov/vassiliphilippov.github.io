prefabricated

adjective
made in advance
4.3
Pronunciation: /priː.ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ /; / /prɪ.ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ (AmE), /priː.ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ /; / /prɪ.ˈfæ.brɪ.keɪ.tɪd/ (BrE)
made in advance, usually in sections or parts, so it can be quickly assembled or used

Examples

Prefabricated building parts

Many schools use prefabricated classrooms to quickly add space for students.
