prewired

adjective
wired in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈwaɪɚd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈwaɪəd / (BrE)
equipped with wiring in advance or having built-in tendencies from birth

Examples

The apartment is prewired
The new apartment is prewired for high-speed internet and cable television.
Some animals are prewired with instincts that help them survive from birth.
The office building was prewired to support advanced security systems.

Related Terms

  • antonym: custom-wired - wired according to specific requirements
  • synonym: preset - set or adjusted in advance
