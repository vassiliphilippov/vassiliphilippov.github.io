infographic

noun
interactive data graphic
3.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnfoʊˈɡræfɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪk / (BrE)
a visual representation of information or data, such as a chart, diagram, or image, often designed to make complex ideas easy to understand (sometimes interactive)
