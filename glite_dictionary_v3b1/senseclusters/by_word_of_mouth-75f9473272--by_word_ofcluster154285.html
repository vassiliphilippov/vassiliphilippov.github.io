by word of mouth

adverb
orally, through spoken communication
Pronunciation: / baɪ wɝd əv maʊθ / (AmE), / baɪ wɜːd əv maʊθ / (BrE)
by speaking to people rather than writing; orally

Examples

By word of mouth communication
Many customers hear about our restaurant by word of mouth.
News of the event spread quickly by word of mouth throughout the town.
The secret was kept for years because it was only passed on by word of mouth.
Much of their business comes by word of mouth rather than through advertising.
The news about the new café spread quickly by word of mouth.
These traditional legends have been passed down by word of mouth for generations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: orally - by spoken means, not written
