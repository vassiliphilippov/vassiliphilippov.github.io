cradle

verb
run with the ball in a lacrosse stick
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkreɪ.dl̩ / (AmE), / ˈkreɪ.dl̩ / (BrE)
(in lacrosse) to run while carrying the ball in the stick, moving the stick to keep the ball in the pocket.

Examples

To cradle the ball
He expertly cradled the ball down the field during the final seconds of the game.
She cradles as she dodges past defenders toward the goal.
You must learn to cradle properly to maintain control of the ball while running.
