stain

noun
dye used in microscopy to highlight structures
5.7
Pronunciation: / steɪn / (AmE), / steɪn / (BrE)
a dye or coloring substance used in microscopy to make specific structures visible under a microscope

Examples

A microscope stain
Applying the stain made the bacteria easier to observe under the microscope.
Different types of stain are used depending on which cellular structures need to be examined.
The tissue sample was treated with a special stain to highlight the cell nuclei.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dye - a substance used to add color, especially to materials or tissues in scientific contexts
