personifier

noun
one who embodies a quality
Pronunciation: / pərˈsɑːnɪˌfaɪər / (AmE), / pəˈsɒnɪˌfaɪə / (BrE)
someone or something that represents or embodies an abstract quality or idea in human form

Examples

The personifier of justice
In the novel, the main character is the personifier of justice and fairness.
The statue served as a personifier of freedom for the entire nation.
Artists often use animals as a personifier of human emotions in their work.

Related Terms

  • synonym: embodiment - a person or thing that represents a quality or idea
  • synonym: symbol - something that stands for or suggests something else
