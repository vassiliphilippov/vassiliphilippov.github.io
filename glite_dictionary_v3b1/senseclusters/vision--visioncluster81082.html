vision

noun
a vivid mental image
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈvɪʒən / (AmE), / ˈvɪʒ.ən / (BrE)
a vivid mental image or the ability to imagine something not present or real

Examples

A vision of success
He was inspired by a vision of a world at peace.
She described her vision of the future as bright and hopeful.
The artist tried to capture the vision that appeared suddenly in her mind.
Artists sometimes draw inspiration from a sudden vision that appears in their minds.
He claimed to have seen a vision of an angel in his sleep.
She described her vision of a peaceful future, even though it seemed impossible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dream - a series of thoughts, images, or emotions occurring during sleep
  • synonym: hallucination - an experience involving the apparent perception of something not present
  • synonym: imagination - the faculty of forming new ideas or images not present to the senses
