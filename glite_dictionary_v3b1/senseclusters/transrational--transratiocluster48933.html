transrational

adjective
beyond rational thought
Pronunciation: / trænzˈræʃənəl / (AmE), / trænzˈræʃənəl / (BrE)
going beyond or surpassing the limits of rational thought or logical reasoning

Examples

Transrational experience
Some spiritual experiences are described as transrational and cannot be explained by logic alone.
The artist's work explores transrational ideas that challenge conventional understanding.
Meditation sometimes leads to transrational states of awareness beyond ordinary thought.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irrational - not logical or reasonable
  • synonym: mystical - relating to spiritual or supernatural experiences beyond ordinary understanding
