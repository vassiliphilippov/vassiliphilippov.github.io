hotplate

noun
portable electric cooking appliance
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtˌpleɪt / (AmE), / ˈhɒtˌpleɪt / (BrE)
a portable electric appliance used for heating, cooking, or keeping food warm

Examples

An electric hotplate
After the storm knocked out the gas, we used an electric hotplate to make breakfast.
She brought a hotplate to the dorm room so she could cook simple meals.
They kept the soup warm on a hotplate during the party.
