matey

noun
informal friend
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪti / (AmE), / ˈmeɪti / (BrE)
a friendly or informal way to refer to a friend or companion, often used in nautical or playful contexts

Examples

Hey, matey!
The captain greeted his loyal matey with a hearty laugh as they set sail.
At the costume party, everyone called each other matey and spoke like pirates.
You can always count on a true matey to help you out in tough times.

Related Terms

  • synonym: buddy - a close friend or companion
  • antonym: enemy - a person who is actively opposed or hostile
  • synonym: pal - an informal term for a friend
