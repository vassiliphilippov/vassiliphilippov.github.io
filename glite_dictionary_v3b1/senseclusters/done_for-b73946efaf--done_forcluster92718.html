done for

adjective
doomed to failure or extinction
Pronunciation: / dʌn fɔr /; / dʌn fɔɹ / (AmE), / dʌn fə /; / dʌn fɔː / (BrE)
doomed to fail, be destroyed, or finished; unable to be saved

Examples

Done for and lost

If the rescue team doesn't arrive soon, we're all done for.
After that mistake, she knew her chances were done for.
If we don't get help soon, we're done for.
The car broke down in the middle of nowhere, and I thought we were done for.
