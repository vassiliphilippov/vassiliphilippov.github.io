horse-drawn vehicle

noun
vehicle pulled by horses
5.4
Pronunciation: / hɔrs drɔn ˈviɪkəl / (AmE), / hɔːs drɔːn ˈviːɪkl̩ / (BrE)
A vehicle with wheels that is pulled by one or more horses, used for transport of people or goods.

Examples

A horse-drawn vehicle carriage
Before the advent of automobiles, horse-drawn vehicles were common in both cities and rural areas.
The old city tour takes visitors in a horse-drawn vehicle through historic streets.
The parade featured an ornate horse-drawn vehicle decorated with flowers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: carriage - a horse-drawn vehicle for people
  • synonym: wagon - a horse-drawn vehicle for transporting goods
