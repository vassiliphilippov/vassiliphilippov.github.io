bread-bin

noun
container for storing bread
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɛd bɪn / (AmE), / ˈbrɛd bɪn / (BrE)
A container for storing bread or cake to keep it fresh.

Examples

The bread-bin lid
Please put the loaf in the bread-bin to keep it from going stale.
She keeps fresh rolls in a bread-bin on the kitchen counter.
The old-fashioned bread-bin was made of metal and had a sliding lid.
