entrepreneurialism

noun
support for entrepreneurship
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəlɪzəm / (AmE), / ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːrɪəlɪzəm / (BrE)
the quality, attitude, or support for starting and running new businesses, especially with innovation and risk-taking
