spatterware

adjective
decorated with spots
Pronunciation: / ˈspætərˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈspætəwɛə / (BrE)
decorated with a pattern of small splashes or spots of color, especially on pottery or enamelware

Examples

Spatterware design
The antique shop displayed a beautiful spatterware bowl with red and green spots.
Collectors often seek out spatterware dishes for their unique, colorful patterns.
She admired the spatterware teapot, which stood out among the plain white cups.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plain - not decorated or patterned; simple
  • synonym: speckled - covered or marked with many small spots or patches
