fruitarian

adjective
fruit-based diet
Pronunciation: / fruːˈtɛriən / (AmE), / fruːˈtɛəriən / (BrE)
relating to a diet that consists mainly or entirely of fruit, nuts, and seeds, or to fruitarianism.

Examples

Fruitarian diet plan
She adopted a strictly fruitarian diet for health reasons.
The restaurant offers several fruitarian options for customers who avoid grains and vegetables.
His fruitarian lifestyle means he eats mostly fresh fruit and some nuts every day.

Related Terms

  • antonym: omnivorous - eating both plant and animal foods
  • synonym: vegan - relating to a diet that excludes all animal products
