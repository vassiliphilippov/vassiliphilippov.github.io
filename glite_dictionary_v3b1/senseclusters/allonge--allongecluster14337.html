allonge

noun
fencing thrust
9.9
Pronunciation: / əˈlɑːnʒ / (AmE), / əˈlɒnʒ / (BrE)
a forward thrust in fencing made by extending the arm and body to reach the opponent

Examples

A fencing allonge
The fencer executed a swift allonge to score a point against her rival.
Mastering the allonge is essential for anyone learning the basics of fencing.
He surprised his opponent with a perfectly timed allonge during the final match.
