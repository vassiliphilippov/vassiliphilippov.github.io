first quarter

noun
lunar phase when the Moon is half-lit
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfɝst ˌkwɔrt̬ɚ / (AmE), / ˈfɜːst ˌkwɔːtə / (BrE)
the phase of the Moon when it has completed about a quarter of its orbit around the Earth and appears half-illuminated from Earth

Examples

The first quarter Moon
Astronomers use the first quarter as a reference point in tracking lunar cycles.
The first quarter moon was clearly visible in the evening sky.
Tides are usually less extreme during the first quarter of the Moon.

Related Terms

  • antonym: last quarter - the lunar phase when the Moon is again half-illuminated, after three-quarters of its orbit
