anis

noun
tropical cuckoo birds
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈæniz / (AmE), / ˈæniz / (BrE)
the plural form of ani, referring to several tropical American cuckoos of the genus Crotophaga with long tails and harsh calls

Examples

A flock of anis
A flock of anis flew across the open field, calling loudly to each other.
Researchers observed several anis sharing a nest in the dense undergrowth.
The guide pointed out the distinctive long tails of the anis perched on the fence.
