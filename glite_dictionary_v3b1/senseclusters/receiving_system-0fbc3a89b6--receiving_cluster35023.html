receiving system

noun
equipment for receiving radio or TV signals
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsivɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (AmE), / rɪˈsiːvɪŋ ˈsɪstəm / (BrE)
a set of equipment designed to receive radio or television signals

Examples

A receiving system device
