repass

verb
to review again
Pronunciation: / riˈpæs / (AmE), / riˈpɑːs / (BrE)
to review or examine something again, especially written material or notes

Examples

To repass notes
Before the exam, she decided to repass her notes one more time.
It is helpful to repass your essay for mistakes before submitting it.
He always repasses important documents to ensure accuracy.
