promotional

adjective
relating to publicity or advertising
Pronunciation: / prəˈmoʊʃənəl / (AmE), / prəˈməʊʃənəl / (BrE)
Relating to the publicizing or advertising of a product, event, or service, especially to increase sales or public awareness.

Examples

Promotional offer
Many airlines offer promotional fares during the off-season to attract more tourists.
The company is launching a promotional campaign to increase its market share.
We received a free sample as part of a promotional event at the supermarket.

Related Terms

  • synonym: advertising - the activity or profession of producing advertisements
  • synonym: marketing - actions for promoting and selling products or services
