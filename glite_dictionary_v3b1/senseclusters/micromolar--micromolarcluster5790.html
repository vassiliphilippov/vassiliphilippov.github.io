micromolar

adjective
very dilute concentration
7.1
Pronunciation: / ˌmaɪkroʊˈmoʊlər / (AmE), / ˌmaɪkrəʊˈməʊlə / (BrE)
relating to a concentration of one millionth of a mole per liter in a solution

Examples

Micromolar concentration
The scientist measured the drug at a micromolar concentration to test its effectiveness.
Enzyme activity was observed only when the substrate was present in micromolar amounts.
They diluted the solution to a micromolar level for the chemical analysis.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: millimolar - relating to a concentration of one thousandth of a mole per liter
  • degree lower: nanomolar - relating to a concentration of one billionth of a mole per liter
