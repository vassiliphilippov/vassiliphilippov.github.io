cupflower

noun
plant with upturned bell-shaped flowers (Nierembergia)
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌpˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈkʌpˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
any of several plants of the genus Nierembergia, distinguished by their upturned, bell-shaped flowers.

Examples

Bell-shaped cupflower
Botanists admire the delicate structure of the cupflower and its bell-shaped blossoms.
She planted a row of cupflowers along the edge of the path for a splash of color.
The garden was filled with purple cupflowers blooming in the summer sun.
