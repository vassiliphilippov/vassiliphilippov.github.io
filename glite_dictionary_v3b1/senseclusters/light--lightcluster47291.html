light

adjective
weak, dizzy, or about to faint
Pronunciation: / laɪt / (AmE), / laɪt / (BrE)
feeling weak and unsteady, as if about to faint or lose consciousness

Examples

Light headed
After standing up too quickly, she suddenly felt light and had to sit down.
He became light from hunger and almost collapsed during the long hike.
Whenever I skip breakfast, I tend to feel light by midmorning.

  • synonym: faint - lacking strength or likely to lose consciousness
