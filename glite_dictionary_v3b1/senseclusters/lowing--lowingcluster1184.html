lowing

noun
sound of cattle
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈləʊɪŋ / (BrE)
the deep, mooing sound made by cows or other cattle

Examples

The lowing of cows
The gentle lowing of the cows could be heard across the quiet valley.
As dusk fell, the lowing from the barn signaled it was time to bring the herd in.
Children listened to the lowing of cattle while walking along the country road.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bellowing - a loud, deep animal cry, especially by cattle
  • synonym: mooing - the characteristic sound made by cows
