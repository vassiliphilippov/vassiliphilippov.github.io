3-membered

adjective
having a ring of three members (chemistry)
Pronunciation: / θri ˈmɛmbɚd / (AmE), / θri ˈmɛmbəd / (BrE)
(of a chemical compound) having a ring structure consisting of three atoms or groups as members.

Examples

A 3-membered ring
Chemists studied the stability of the 3-membered ring under different conditions.
Cyclopropane is a classic example of a 3-membered ring compound.
The molecule contains a 3-membered heterocycle that is highly strained.
