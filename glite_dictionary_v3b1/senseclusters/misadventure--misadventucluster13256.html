misadventure

noun
unfortunate incident; mishap
3.7 5.5
Pronunciation: /mɪs.əd.ˈven.tʃər/ (AmE), /mɪs.əd.ˈven.tʃə/ (BrE)
an unfortunate incident; a mishap or accident, typically one that is minor or not seriously harmful

Examples

A misadventure happened

They laughed about the misadventure after realizing no serious harm had been done.
What began as a fun day at the beach turned into a misadventure when their boat drifted away.
