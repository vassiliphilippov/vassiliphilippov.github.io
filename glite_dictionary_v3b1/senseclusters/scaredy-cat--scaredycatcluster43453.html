scaredy-cat

noun
easily frightened person
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈskɛrdi kæt / (AmE), / ˈskeədi kæt / (BrE)
a person who is easily frightened or scared, especially in situations that are not very dangerous

Examples

A little scaredy-cat
My little brother is a scaredy-cat and refuses to watch scary movies with us.
Don't be such a scaredy-cat; the roller coaster is perfectly safe.
Even though she was called a scaredy-cat, she eventually tried the haunted house.

Related Terms

  • antonym: brave - showing courage and not easily frightened
  • synonym: coward - a person who lacks courage
