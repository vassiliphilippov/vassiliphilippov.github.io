subpar

adjective
below standard
4.5 1.02/m
Pronunciation: / Àås åbÀàp…ër / (AmE), / Àås åbÀàp…ëÀê / (BrE)
below the usual or expected standard; not as good as normal or desired

Examples

Subpar performance
His performance on the exam was subpar compared to his usual high scores.
The restaurant received negative reviews for its subpar food and slow service.
Despite the subpar weather, the event continued as planned.
