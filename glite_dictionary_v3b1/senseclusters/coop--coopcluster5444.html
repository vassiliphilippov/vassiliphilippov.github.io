coop

noun
small poultry shelter
1.6 2.50/m
Pronunciation: / kup /; / kuÀêp / (AmE), / kuÀêp / (BrE)
a small building or enclosure for keeping birds or small animals, especially chickens

Examples

Chicken coop
After the storm, the chickens were safe inside their coop.
The farmer collected eggs from the coop every morning.
They built a new coop for their growing flock of hens.
After cleaning the coop, she refilled the water and food trays for the birds.
He built a new coop in his backyard for his rabbits.
The chickens were safe and warm inside the coop for the night.
