cowshed

noun
building for housing cows
Pronunciation: / ˈkaʊˌʃɛd / (AmE), / ˈkaʊʃɛd / (BrE)
a building or shelter for housing cows

Examples

A cowshed building
After milking, the cows returned to the cowshed to rest.
The farmer built a new cowshed to protect his herd during the winter.
We could smell the fresh hay from the cowshed every morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: byre - a cowshed, especially in British English
  • antonym: stable - a building for housing horses or other livestock
