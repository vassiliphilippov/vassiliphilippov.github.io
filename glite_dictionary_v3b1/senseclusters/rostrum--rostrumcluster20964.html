rostrum

noun
beaklike projection on insect head
Pronunciation: / ˈrɑːstrəm / (AmE), / ˈrɒstrəm / (BrE)
a beaklike projection at the front of the head of certain insects, such as weevils

Examples

Insect rostrum
Detailed drawings of the insect's rostrum reveal adaptations for piercing.
Some aquatic insects possess an elongated rostrum for sucking plant sap.
