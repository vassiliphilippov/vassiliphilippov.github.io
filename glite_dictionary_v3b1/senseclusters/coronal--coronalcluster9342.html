coronal

noun
circular wreath of flowers or foliage
Pronunciation: / kəˈroʊnəl / (AmE), / kəˈrəʊnəl / (BrE)
A circular arrangement of foliage or flowers worn or placed as an ornament, especially as a wreath or garland.

Examples

A coronal of flowers
A delicate coronal of roses adorned the bride's head during the ceremony.
The festival queen wore a coronal made of wildflowers and ivy.
Upon the ancient statue rested a faded coronal placed there by worshippers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: garland - a decorative chain or ring of flowers or leaves
  • synonym: wreath - a circular band of flowers or leaves, used as decoration or symbol
