appointed

adjective
furnished (tastefully)
6.2
Pronunciation: / əˈpɔɪntɪd / (AmE), / əˈpɔɪntɪd / (BrE)
(of a room or building) provided with furniture and accessories, especially in a tasteful or stylish way.

Examples

Well appointed room
The apartment was fully appointed with everything they needed for a comfortable stay.
The hotel rooms were elegantly appointed with luxurious linens and modern furnishings.
They moved into a newly appointed office that impressed their clients.
