Sendero Luminoso

proper noun
Maoist guerrilla group in Peru
9.9 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / senÀàd…õ…æo l äÀàmino äso ä / (AmE), / s…õnÀàd…õÀê…æ…ô ä l äÀàmiÀên…ôs…ô ä / (BrE)
