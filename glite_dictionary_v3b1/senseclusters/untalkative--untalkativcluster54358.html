untalkative

adjective
not inclined to talk much
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɔkətɪv / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɔːkətɪv / (BrE)
tending not to talk much; disinclined to speak.

Examples

An untalkative person
Although he was friendly, he remained untalkative during most meetings.
Her untalkative nature made her a good listener.
Some children are more untalkative and prefer to observe rather than speak.

Related Terms

  • synonym: taciturn - reserved or saying little in speech
  • antonym: talkative - inclined to talk a great deal
