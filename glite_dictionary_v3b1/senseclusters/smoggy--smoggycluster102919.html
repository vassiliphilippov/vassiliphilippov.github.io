smoggy

adjective
filled with smoke and fog; polluted
5.1 2.0
Pronunciation: /ˈsmɔː.gɪ/ /; / /ˈsmɑː.gɪ/ (AmE), /ˈsmɒ.gɪ/ (BrE)
(of air or weather) filled or clouded with a mixture of smoke and fog; heavily polluted by smog.

Examples

A smoggy day
Her asthma worsened whenever the weather turned smoggy.
On particularly smoggy days, people are advised to stay indoors.
The city's skyline was barely visible under the smoggy sky.
