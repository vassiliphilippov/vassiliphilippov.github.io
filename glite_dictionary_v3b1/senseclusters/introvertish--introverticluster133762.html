introvertish

adjective
somewhat introverted
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈvɜrtɪʃ / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈvɜːtɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat introverted in manner or personality.

Examples

Somewhat introvertish person
He's introvertish, so he prefers spending weekends at home rather than at big parties.
My introvertish tendencies make networking events a bit draining for me.
Though she's not shy, her introvertish side comes out in large groups.

Related Terms
