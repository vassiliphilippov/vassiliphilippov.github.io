Synonyms

  • labor agreement
    3.2 0.25/m
    labor agreement is a negotiated deal between workers and management in a workplace, while labor contract is the formal written record of those terms.
  • collective agreement
    3.5 0.11/m
    collective agreement is made between an employer and union representatives to set pay and rules, while labor contract names the formal agreement.
  • employment contract
    2.7 0.59/m
    employment contract is an individual written deal between one employee and an employer, while labor contract often covers wider worker groups and terms.
