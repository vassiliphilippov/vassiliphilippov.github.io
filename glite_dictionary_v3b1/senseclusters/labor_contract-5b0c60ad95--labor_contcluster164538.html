labor contract

noun
agreement outlining work terms
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪ.bɚ ˌkɑn.trækt / (AmE), / ˈleɪ.bə ˌkɒn.trækt / (BrE)
a formal agreement between workers and employers that sets wages, benefits, and conditions of employment

Examples

A labor contract agreement
Many disputes between management and employees stem from disagreements over the labor contract.
The union negotiated a new labor contract that included better pay and healthcare benefits.
Under the labor contract, workers are entitled to paid vacation and sick leave.
