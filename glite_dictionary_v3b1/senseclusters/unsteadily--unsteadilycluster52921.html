unsteadily

adverb
in an unstable or shaky manner
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈstɛdəli / (AmE), / ʌnˈstɛdɪli / (BrE)
in a manner lacking stability, balance, or firmness; not steadily

Examples

To walk unsteadily
His voice trembled unsteadily as he delivered the bad news.
She climbed the stairs unsteadily, grasping the handrail for support.
The glass was placed unsteadily on the edge of the table, about to fall.

Related Terms

  • antonym: steadily - in a steady or firm manner
