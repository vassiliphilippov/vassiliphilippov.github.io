unthirsty

adjective
not eager or ambitious
8.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈθɜːrsti / (AmE), / ʌnˈθɜːsti / (BrE)
not eager for or desiring something, especially attention, praise, or success; lacking strong ambition or craving (figurative)

Examples

Unthirsty for fame
She was unthirsty for recognition and preferred to work quietly behind the scenes.
Even after the promotion, he remained unthirsty for more power or attention.
Unlike many of his peers, Mark was unthirsty for praise and focused on personal growth.

Related Terms

  • antonym: ambitious - having a strong desire for success or achievement
  • synonym: content - satisfied with what one has; not wanting more
  • antonym: thirsty - eager for something, especially attention or success
