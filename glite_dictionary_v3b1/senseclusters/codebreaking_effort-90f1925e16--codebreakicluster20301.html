codebreaking effort

noun
activity to decipher coded messages
6.3 4.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊdˌbreɪkɪŋ ˈɛfərt / (AmE), / ˈkəʊdˌbreɪkɪŋ ˈefət / (BrE)
an operation or organized activity focused on deciphering coded or encrypted messages, especially in a military or intelligence context

Examples
