Guaira

proper noun
waterfall on Brazil–Paraguay border (now submerged)
9.4
Pronunciation: / ɡwaɪˈɪrə / (AmE), / ɡwaɪˈɪərə / (BrE)
a former large waterfall on the border between Brazil and Paraguay, submerged after the construction of the Itaipu Dam.

Examples

The Guaira Falls
After the construction of the Itaipu Dam, the Guaira Falls disappeared beneath the reservoir.
Many tourists visited Guaira before it was submerged in the early 1980s.
The Guaira Falls were once among the most powerful waterfalls in the world.
