wasteful

adjective
using resources carelessly
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈweɪstfəl /; / ˈweɪst.fəl / (AmE), / ˈweɪstfəl /; / ˈweɪst.fəl / (BrE)
using more of something than necessary; not careful with resources; not efficient

Examples

Wasteful spending

The company's wasteful spending led to financial problems.
Try to avoid wasteful use of paper in the office.
It is wasteful to leave the lights on when no one is in the room.
She criticized the project as both expensive and wasteful.
The company's wasteful spending led to a significant financial loss.

  • antonym: thrifty - using resources carefully and avoiding waste
