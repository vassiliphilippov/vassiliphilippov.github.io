Conium

noun
genus of toxic plants (hemlock)
Pronunciation: / koʊˈnaɪəm / (AmE), / kəʊˈniːəm / (BrE)
A small genus of highly poisonous biennial plants in the family Apiaceae, which includes the hemlock.

Examples

Conium poisonous plant
Ingesting even a small amount of Conium can be fatal.
Scientists collected samples from several Conium species for their research.
The poisonous plant Conium maculatum has a long history in Greek culture.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hemlock - a highly poisonous plant in the genus Conium, especially Conium maculatum
