natural family planning

noun
contraception by tracking fertility
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈnæt͡ʃɚɫ ˈfæmɪli ˈplænɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈnatʃ(ə)rəl ˈfæmɪli ˈplænɪŋ / (BrE)
A method of birth control based on avoiding sexual intercourse during the fertile period of a woman's menstrual cycle, without using drugs or devices.

Examples

Natural family planning method
Natural family planning requires careful monitoring of the menstrual cycle.
Many couples choose natural family planning for religious or personal reasons.
Some people find natural family planning to be a more acceptable alternative to hormonal methods.

Related Terms

  • synonym: contraception - the prevention of pregnancy by various methods
  • antonym: sterilization - the process of making someone unable to reproduce
