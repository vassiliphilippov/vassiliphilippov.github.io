Shen-pao

noun
deity of the Taoist Trinity
Pronunciation: / ʃɛn ˈpaʊ / (AmE), / ʃɛn ˈpaʊ / (BrE)
A member of the Taoist Trinity, identified with Lao-tse in Taoist tradition.

Examples

Shen-pao of Taoism
According to some traditions, Shen-pao is considered an incarnation of Lao-tse.
In ancient texts, Shen-pao is frequently depicted alongside other members of the Taoist Trinity.
Pilgrims offered incense to Shen-pao during religious festivals at the temple.
