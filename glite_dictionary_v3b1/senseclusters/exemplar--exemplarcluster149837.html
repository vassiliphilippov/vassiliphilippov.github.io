exemplar

noun
something serving as a model to imitate
6.7
Pronunciation: / ɪɡˈzɛmplɑr / (AmE), / ɪɡˈzɛmplɑː / (BrE)
a person or thing that is a typical or excellent example to be imitated

Examples

An exemplar of honesty
He strives to be an exemplar of honesty and integrity at work.
The teacher used her as an exemplar of diligence in the classroom.
This award honors those who serve as an exemplar in community service.

Related Terms

  • synonym: model - a person or thing regarded as an excellent example
  • synonym: paragon - a person or thing regarded as a perfect example
