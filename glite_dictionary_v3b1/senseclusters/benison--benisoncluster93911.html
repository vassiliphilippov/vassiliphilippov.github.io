benison

noun
a spoken blessing
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˈbɛnɪsən / (AmE), / ˈbɛnɪsən / (BrE)
A spoken blessing or benediction, often given formally or with solemnity.

Examples

A benison of peace
May the benison of peace be with you throughout the year.
The priest bestowed his benison upon the gathered crowd.
With a gentle smile, she offered her benison before the journey began.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blessing - a prayer or request for divine favor
  • antonym: malediction - a curse, the opposite of a blessing
