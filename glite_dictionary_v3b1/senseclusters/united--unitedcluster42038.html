united

adjective
joined or acting as one
Pronunciation: / juːˈnaɪtɪd /; / juˈnaɪtɪd / (AmE), / juːˈnaɪtɪd /; / juˈnaɪtɪd / (BrE)
joined or combined as one group; acting together in agreement or with shared effort

Examples

A united group
A united community can overcome almost any challenge.
The countries issued a united statement calling for peace.
The team stood united against their opponents in the championship match.
Only through a united response can the community overcome such challenges.
The nations acted in a united manner to achieve peace.
The two companies made a united effort to address the environmental issue together.

Related Terms

  • antonym: divided - separated into parts; not acting as one
  • synonym: joint - involving two or more people or groups together
  • antonym: separate - not joined or united; apart
  • synonym: unified - made into or caused to be a unit; brought together
