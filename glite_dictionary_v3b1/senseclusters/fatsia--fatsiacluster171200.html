fatsia

noun
evergreen ornamental shrub
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfætsiə / (AmE), / ˈfætsiə / (BrE)
an evergreen shrub with large, glossy leaves, often grown as a houseplant or ornamental plant

Examples

The fatsia plant
The large leaves of the fatsia make it a popular choice for brightening up living rooms.
She watered her fatsia regularly to keep its foliage healthy and green.
A mature fatsia can add a tropical feel to any garden or indoor space.
