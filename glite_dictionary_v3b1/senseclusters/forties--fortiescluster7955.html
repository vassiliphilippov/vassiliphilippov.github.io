forties

noun
the decade of one's life from 40 to 49
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔr.tiz / (AmE), / ˈfɔː.tiz / (BrE)
the period of a person's life between the ages of 40 and 49; the decade when someone is in their forties

Examples

In your forties
He took up running again when he entered his forties.
Many people experience career changes during their forties.
She found a renewed sense of purpose in her forties.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: fifties - the time of life between 50 and 59
  • degree lower: thirties - the time of life between 30 and 39
