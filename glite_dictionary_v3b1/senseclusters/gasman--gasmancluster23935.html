gasman

noun
man working for a gas company
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæzˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈɡɑːzmən / (BrE)
a man who is employed by a gas company, especially to install or repair gas equipment

Examples

The gasman installs pipes
As a gasman, he often visited homes to perform safety inspections.
The gasman arrived to check the boiler for leaks this morning.
When the heater broke down, we called the gasman to fix it.
