push-button

noun
finger-operated switch
Pronunciation: / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (AmE), / ˈpʊʃˌbʌtən / (BrE)
a small switch that is activated by pressing it with a finger

Examples

A push-button switch
To start the coffee machine, simply press the push-button on the front panel.
The elevator doors opened after she tapped the push-button next to the floor numbers.
Many modern devices use a push-button instead of a traditional switch for convenience.

Related Terms

  • synonym: switch - a device for making and breaking the connection in an electric circuit
  • synonym: toggle - a switch that moves between two positions
