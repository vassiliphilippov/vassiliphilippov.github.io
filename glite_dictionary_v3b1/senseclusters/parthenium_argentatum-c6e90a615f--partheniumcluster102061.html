Parthenium argentatum

noun
shrub native to Texas and northern Mexico, rubber source
Pronunciation: / pɑːrˈθiːniəm ˌɑːrdʒənˈteɪtəm / (AmE), / pɑːˈθiːnɪəm ˌɑːdʒənˈteɪtəm / (BrE)
