life jacket

noun
buoyant sleeveless jacket for keeping afloat
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪf ˌdʒækɪt / (AmE), / ˈlaɪf ˌdʒækɪt / (BrE)
a sleeveless jacket filled with buoyant material or designed to be inflatable, worn to keep a person afloat in water

Examples

Wear a life jacket
Every passenger must wear a life jacket before getting into the boat.
She grabbed her life jacket from under the seat as soon as the alarm sounded.
The children learned how to fasten their life jackets during swimming lessons.
