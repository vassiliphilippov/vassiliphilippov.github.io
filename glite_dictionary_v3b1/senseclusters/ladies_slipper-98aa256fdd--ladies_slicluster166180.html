ladies' slipper

noun
North American wildflower with pouchlike lip
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpər / (AmE), / ˈleɪdiz ˈslɪpə / (BrE)
a wildflower of the orchid family, mainly found in North America, with an inflated pouchlike lip resembling a slipper

Examples

Ladies' slipper orchid
Because ladies' slipper orchids are difficult to cultivate, they are rarely seen in gardens.
Hikers were delighted to spot a cluster of ladies' slippers growing in the forest clearing.
The ladies' slipper is prized by wildflower enthusiasts for its unique shape and rare appearance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: moccasin flower - another name for the ladies' slipper orchid
  • degree higher: orchid - any plant of the diverse and widespread family Orchidaceae
