pyrobitumen

noun
solid altered bitumen
Pronunciation: / ˌpaɪroʊˈbɪtʃuːmən / (AmE), / ˌpaɪərəʊˈbɪtjʊmən / (BrE)
a solid, insoluble organic material found in rocks, formed by the thermal alteration of bitumen

Examples

Solid pyrobitumen material
The geologist identified pyrobitumen in the ancient rock formation during the field study.
Extraction of oil from shale often leaves behind solid pyrobitumen as a residue.
Researchers are studying how pyrobitumen forms under high temperatures deep underground.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bitumen - a black, sticky substance found in natural deposits or made from petroleum
  • synonym: kerogen - a solid organic matter in sedimentary rocks that can produce oil or gas
