shortcut

noun
a quicker or shorter route
1.6 4.46/m
Pronunciation: / Àà É…îrtÀåk åt / (AmE), / Àà É…îÀêt.k åt / (BrE)
a route that is shorter than the usual way, taken to save time or distance

Examples

A shortcut home
He found a shortcut that cut ten minutes off his daily commute.
If you know a good shortcut, please show me.
We decided to take a shortcut through the park to get home faster.

Synonyms

  • cutoff
    3.6 0.36/m
    a cutoff is a short alternative road that avoids longer routes, while a shortcut is any route taken to save time or distance.
  • cut-through
    4.9 0.03/m
    a cut-through is a route through a built area used to avoid traffic, while a shortcut is any shorter way to save time.
  • crosscut
    4.9 0.26/m
    a crosscut is a path that cuts diagonally across land and may shorten distance, while a shortcut is any quicker route taken to save time.
