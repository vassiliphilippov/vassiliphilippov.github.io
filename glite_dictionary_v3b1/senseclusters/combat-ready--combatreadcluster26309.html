combat-ready

adjective
fully prepared for military action
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑmˌbæt ˈrɛdi / (AmE), / ˈkɒmbæt ˈrɛdi / (BrE)
(of troops, equipment, or forces) fully prepared and available for immediate military action or operations.

Examples

Fully combat-ready troops
Before deployment, the commander made sure every vehicle was combat-ready.
It took several months to get the new fighter jets combat-ready.
The troops were declared combat-ready after weeks of intensive training.
