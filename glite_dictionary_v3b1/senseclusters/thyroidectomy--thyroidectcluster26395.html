thyroidectomy

noun
surgical removal of the thyroid gland
5.8
Pronunciation: / θaɪˌrɔɪdəˈktəmi / (AmE), / θaɪˌrɔɪdˈɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical operation to remove all or part of the thyroid gland

Examples

A thyroidectomy operation
A thyroidectomy is often performed to treat thyroid cancer or severe hyperthyroidism.
After her thyroidectomy, she needed daily medication to replace the lost hormones.
The surgeon explained the risks and benefits of a thyroidectomy to the patient.

Related Terms
