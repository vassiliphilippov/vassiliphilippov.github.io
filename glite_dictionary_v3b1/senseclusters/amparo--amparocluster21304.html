amparo

noun
legal protection remedy
Pronunciation: / ɑmˈpɑɾoʊ / (AmE), / amˈpɑːrəʊ / (BrE)
a legal remedy used to protect constitutional rights, especially in some Spanish-speaking countries

Examples

File an amparo petition
The activist filed an amparo to challenge the new law in court.
In Mexico, citizens can request an amparo if they believe their rights are violated.
The judge granted an amparo to stop the government from enforcing the policy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: habeas corpus - a legal action to seek relief from unlawful detention
  • synonym: injunction - a court order requiring someone to do or stop doing something
