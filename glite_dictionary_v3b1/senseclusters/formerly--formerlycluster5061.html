formerly

adverb
at an earlier time
Pronunciation: / ˈfɔrmərli / (AmE), / ˈfɔːməli / (BrE)
at an earlier or previous time; in the past

Examples

Used formerly
My grandfather formerly worked as a carpenter before retiring.
She formerly lived in Paris before moving to New York.
This building was formerly a factory, but is now an art gallery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: now - at the present moment
  • synonym: once - at some time in the past; previously
  • synonym: previously - at a previous or earlier time
