validator

noun
blockchain verifier
7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈvæl.ɪ.deɪ.tər/ /; / /ˈvæl.ə.deɪ.tər/ (AmE), /ˈvæl.ɪ.deɪ.tə/ /; / /ˈvæl.ə.deɪ.tə/ (BrE)
a participant in a blockchain or distributed ledger system who verifies transactions and helps create or confirm new blocks, often by staking cryptocurrency

Examples

Blockchain validator
Each validator must stake a certain amount of cryptocurrency to participate in the network.
The blockchain relies on every validator to check transactions and maintain security.
If a validator acts dishonestly, they risk losing their staked funds.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attacker - a participant who tries to compromise or disrupt a blockchain network
  • synonym: miner - a person or computer that adds transactions to a blockchain, especially in proof-of-work systems
