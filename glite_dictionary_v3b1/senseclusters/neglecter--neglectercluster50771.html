neglecter

noun
a person who neglects responsibilities or people
Pronunciation: / nɪˈɡlɛktər / (AmE), / nɪˈɡlɛktə / (BrE)
a person who neglects someone or something, especially by giving little attention or care

Examples

A neglecter of duties
As a boss, he was considered a neglecter who ignored his team's concerns.
She did not want to be seen as a neglecter of her parental duties.
The children suffered because their neglecter failed to provide basic care.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carer - a person who gives attention and care to others
  • synonym: neglector - a person who neglects someone or something
