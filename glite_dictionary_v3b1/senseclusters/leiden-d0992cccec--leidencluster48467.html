Leiden

proper noun
city in western Netherlands
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪdən / (AmE), / ˈleɪdən / (BrE)
A city in the western Netherlands, known for its historic university and as the residence of the Pilgrim Fathers before their voyage to America in 1620.

Examples

Leiden city in Netherlands
Many tourists visit Leiden to explore its picturesque canals and museums.
The Pilgrim Fathers lived in Leiden before journeying to America.
The University of Leiden is one of the oldest in the Netherlands, founded in 1575.
