dukkha

noun
Buddhist suffering
9.9 0.12/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀêk…ô / (AmE), / Ààd äk…ô / (BrE)
in Buddhism, the idea that life is full of suffering, dissatisfaction, or unease
