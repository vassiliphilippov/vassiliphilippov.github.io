nostalgia

noun
sentimental longing for the past
5.1
Pronunciation: / nəˈstældʒə / (AmE), / nɒˈstaldʒə / (BrE)
A sentimental longing or wistful affection for a period in the past.

Examples

Feeling nostalgia for childhood
He felt a wave of nostalgia as he walked through his old neighborhood.
Sometimes nostalgia can make the past seem better than it really was.
Watching old family movies filled her with nostalgia for her childhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: homesickness - sadness caused by being away from home
