passingly

adverb
exceedingly; very greatly
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋli / (BrE)
to a very great degree; exceedingly; used as an intensifier, especially in older English.

Examples

Passingly brave
The knight was passingly brave, earning the admiration of all who knew him.
She was passingly fair, with beauty that poets often described in their verses.
His generosity was passingly praised in the old village records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: exceedingly - to a very great degree; extremely
  • antonym: slightly - to a small degree
  • synonym: very - to a high degree
