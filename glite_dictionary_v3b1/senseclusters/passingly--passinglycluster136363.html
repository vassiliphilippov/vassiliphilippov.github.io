passingly

adverb
exceedingly; very greatly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpæsɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈpɑːsɪŋli / (BrE)
to a very great degree; exceedingly; used as an intensifier, especially in older English.

Examples
