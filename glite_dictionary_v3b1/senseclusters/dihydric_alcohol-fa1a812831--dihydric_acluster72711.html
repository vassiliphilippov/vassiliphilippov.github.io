dihydric alcohol

noun
alcohol with two hydroxyl groups
7.4
Pronunciation: /daɪ.ˈhaɪ.drɪk ˈæl.kə.hɑːl/ /; / /daɪ.ˈhaɪ.drɪk ˈæl.kə.hɔːl/ (AmE), /daɪ.ˈhaɪ.drɪk ˈæl.kə.hɒl/ (BrE)
