adaptive

adjective
having a capacity to adapt
Pronunciation: / əˈdæptɪv / (AmE), / əˈdaptɪv / (BrE)
having the ability to adjust or change in response to new conditions or environments

Examples

An adaptive system
Modern technology needs to be adaptive to meet the demands of a rapidly changing world.
She has an adaptive approach to solving unexpected problems at work.
The chameleon's adaptive coloring helps it survive in different environments.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adaptable - able to adjust easily to new conditions
  • antonym: rigid - not able to change or adapt
