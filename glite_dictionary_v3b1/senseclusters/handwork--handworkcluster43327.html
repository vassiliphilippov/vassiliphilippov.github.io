handwork

verb
to make by hand
4.6
Pronunciation: /hæn.ˈdwɜːrk/ /; / /hænd.ˈwɜːrk/ (AmE), /hæn.ˈdwɜːk/ /; / /hænd.ˈwɜːk/ (BrE)
to work on, shape, or finish something by hand rather than by machine

Examples

To handwork leather
She carefully handworked the leather edges to give the bag a polished look.
The artist handworks each piece to ensure it is unique and detailed.
These decorations were handworked by skilled artisans over many hours.
