nonallergenic

adjective
not causing allergies
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˌælərˈdʒɛnɪk / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˌaləˈdʒɛnɪk / (BrE)
not likely to cause an allergic reaction in most people

Examples

Nonallergenic soap
The doctor recommended using nonallergenic soap for sensitive skin.
Many bedding products are now made with nonallergenic materials to help allergy sufferers.
Parents often choose nonallergenic toys for their young children to avoid allergic reactions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: allergenic - likely to cause an allergic reaction
  • synonym: hypoallergenic - unlikely to cause an allergic reaction
