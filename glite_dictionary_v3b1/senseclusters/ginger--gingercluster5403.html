ginger

adjective
orange-brown (especially of hair/fur)
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒɪndʒɚ / (AmE), / ˈdʒɪndʒə / (BrE)
(of hair, fur, or a similar substance) having a bright orange-brown color.

Examples

Ginger hair color
Children often tease him for his ginger locks, but he wears them with pride.
She was immediately noticed because of her striking ginger hair.
The ginger cat curled up on the windowsill, basking in the sunlight.

Related Terms

  • synonym: auburn - reddish-brown, typically of hair
  • antonym: blond - light yellowish color, especially of hair
  • synonym: redhead - person with red or orange-brown hair
