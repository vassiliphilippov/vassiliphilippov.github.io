ungraspable

adjective
impossible to understand
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈɡræspəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈɡrɑːspəbəl / (BrE)
impossible to understand or comprehend

Examples

Ungraspable idea
The true nature of the universe may always remain ungraspable to us.
For many students, the advanced theory was completely ungraspable at first.
Some abstract art can seem ungraspable without background knowledge.
