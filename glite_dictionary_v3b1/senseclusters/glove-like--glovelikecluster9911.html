glove-like

adjective
snug fit
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlʌvˌlaɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡlʌvˌlaɪk / (BrE)

Examples

Glove-like fit
The dress was so glove-like that it seemed tailored just for her.
He appreciated the glove-like feel of his new running shoes.
The phone case fit the device in a glove-like manner, leaving no gaps.
