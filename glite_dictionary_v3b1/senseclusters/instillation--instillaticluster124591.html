instillation

noun
liquid given drop by drop
Pronunciation: / ˌɪn.stəˈleɪ.ʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪn.stɪˈleɪ.ʃən / (BrE)
A liquid substance that is administered drop by drop, especially for medical purposes.

Examples

A saline instillation
An instillation of saline solution can help clear the sinuses.
Doctors recommend using only a prescribed instillation for eye infections.
The nurse administered an instillation of antibiotic drops into the patient’s ear.

Related Terms

  • synonym: instill - to introduce a substance drop by drop
