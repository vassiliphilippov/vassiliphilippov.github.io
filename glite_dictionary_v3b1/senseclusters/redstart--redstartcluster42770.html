redstart

noun
small North American warbler with orange markings
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌstɑrt / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌstɑːt / (BrE)
a small songbird with a reddish or orange tail or breast, found in North America, Europe, or Asia
