needily

adverb
in a dependent way
Pronunciation: / ˈniːdɪli / (AmE), / ˈniːdɪli / (BrE)
in a way that shows a strong desire for attention, help, or affection

Examples

Needily seeks attention
The child clung needily to his mother after waking from a nightmare.
She smiled needily, hoping for reassurance from her friends.
He looked needily at the teacher, waiting for approval.

Related Terms

  • synonym: desperately - in a way that shows great need or urgency
  • antonym: independently - in a way that does not rely on others
