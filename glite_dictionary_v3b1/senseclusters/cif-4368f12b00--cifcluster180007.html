CIF

noun
shipping contract term
6.6
Pronunciation: /siː aɪ ef/ (AmE), /siː aɪ ef/ (BrE)
an abbreviation for 'cost, insurance, and freight', used in shipping contracts to indicate that these costs are included in the price

Examples

CIF shipping terms
The contract specified delivery terms as CIF New York, so the seller covered shipping and insurance.
When buying goods internationally, understanding CIF can help you know what costs are included.
The invoice listed the price as CIF Rotterdam, meaning the buyer would not pay extra for freight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: FOB - an abbreviation for 'free on board', a shipping term indicating the seller pays for delivery to the port
