so there

interjection
expression of defiance or emphasis
8.1
Pronunciation: / soʊ ðɛr / (AmE), / səʊ ðeə / (BrE)
Used to emphasize defiance, assertiveness, or to end an argument, often after stating one's position.

Examples

So there!
I'm not changing my mind, so there!
She said she finished the project first, so there.
You can't borrow my notes, so there.
