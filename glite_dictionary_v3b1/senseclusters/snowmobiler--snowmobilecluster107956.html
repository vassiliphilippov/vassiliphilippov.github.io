snowmobiler

noun
snowmobile rider
Pronunciation: / ˈsnoʊmoʊˌbiːlər / (AmE), / ˈsnəʊməʊˌbiːlə / (BrE)
a person who rides or operates a snowmobile, especially for recreation or sport

Examples

An experienced snowmobiler
The experienced snowmobiler led the group through the snowy trails.
Every winter, hundreds of snowmobilers gather for the annual race across the frozen lake.
As a new snowmobiler, she took safety lessons before exploring the backcountry.
Many snowmobilers travel to the mountains each winter to enjoy the fresh powder.
The club organizes events for snowmobilers of all skill levels.
Experienced snowmobilers always check the weather before heading out on the trails.
