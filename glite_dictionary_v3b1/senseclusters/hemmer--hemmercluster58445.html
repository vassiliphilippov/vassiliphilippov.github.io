hemmer

noun
machine for hemming
8.4
Pronunciation: /ˈhem.ər/ (AmE), /ˈhem.ə/ (BrE)
a machine used in garment production to fold and stitch hems quickly and efficiently

Examples

Industrial hemmer machine
The tailor used a hemmer to finish the edges of the new shirts quickly.
Modern factories rely on an automated hemmer to increase garment production speed.
After the old hemmer broke down, production slowed until it was repaired.
