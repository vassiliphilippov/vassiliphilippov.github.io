gravimetric

adjective
relating to measurement by weight
Pronunciation: / ˌɡrævəˈmɛtrɪk / (AmE), / ˌɡravɪˈmɛtrɪk / (BrE)
relating to the measurement of weight or mass, especially as used in scientific analysis; involving techniques that determine quantities using weighing methods

Examples

Gravimetric analysis method
A gravimetric method was chosen for detecting changes in soil moisture content.
Pharmaceutical laboratories often use gravimetric techniques to quantify the purity of compounds.
The gravimetric analysis revealed the exact amount of dissolved minerals in the water sample.

Related Terms

  • antonym: volumetric - relating to measurement by volume
