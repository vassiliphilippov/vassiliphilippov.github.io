let out a scream

verb
emit a sudden loud cry
2.3
Pronunciation: / lɛt aʊt ə skrim / (AmE), / lɛt aʊt ə skriːm / (BrE)
to emit a loud, sudden cry, typically because of fear, pain, or surprise

Examples

To let out a scream of fear

She let out a scream when the door slammed shut behind her.
The audience let out a scream as the magician disappeared from the stage.
