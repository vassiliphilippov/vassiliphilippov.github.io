deadhead

noun
an unproductive or nonpaying person
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌhɛd / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌhɛd / (BrE)
a person, especially an employee, who avoids work or does not contribute productively; someone who benefits without paying or contributing

Examples

Lazy deadhead worker
It's important for organizations to identify and deal with deadheads to remain efficient.
She complained that too many deadheads were slowing down the team’s progress.
The company decided to lay off several deadheads who were drawing salaries but not contributing to projects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: shirker - a person who avoids work or responsibility
  • antonym: worker - a person engaged in productive work
