openable

adjective
can be opened
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊpənəbəl / (AmE), / ˈəʊpənəbəl / (BrE)

Examples

Openable window
The skylight is openable, allowing fresh air to enter the room.
For safety reasons, not all windows in the building are openable.
The suitcase has an openable compartment for easy access to your documents.
