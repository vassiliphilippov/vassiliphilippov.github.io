torta

noun
flat round cake
Pronunciation: / ˈtɔrtə / (AmE), / ˈtɔːtə / (BrE)
a flat, round cake, often sweet, found in various cuisines, especially in Spanish- and Italian-speaking countries

Examples

A sweet torta cake
He ordered a slice of torta for dessert after his meal.
The bakery displayed a beautiful chocolate torta in the window.
For her birthday, Maria baked a traditional Spanish torta with almonds and honey.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cake - a sweet baked dessert made from flour, sugar, and other ingredients
  • synonym: pie - a baked dish with a pastry base and filling
