greenie

noun
novice or beginner
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈɡriːni / (AmE), / ˈɡriːni / (BrE)
a person who is inexperienced or new to a particular activity or field

Examples

A new greenie
As a greenie, she asked many questions during her first week on the job.
The coach assigned a mentor to each greenie on the team to help them learn quickly.
Even though he was a greenie, he was eager to take on new challenges.

Related Terms

  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
  • synonym: rookie - a person who is new to an activity or organization
