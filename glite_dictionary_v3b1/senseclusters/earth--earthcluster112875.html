earth

noun
world of human affairs (not spiritual)
Pronunciation: / ɝːθ / (AmE), / ɜːθ / (BrE)
the world of human affairs and concerns, as distinguished from the spiritual realm or afterlife

Examples

Matters of earth
He believed in focusing on the here and now, on matters of earth rather than the afterlife.
Many religious texts contrast the transience of earth with the promises of heaven.
Monks are taught to renounce the pleasures of earth to seek spiritual enlightenment.

Related Terms

  • antonym: heaven - the spiritual realm regarded as the abode of God or the afterlife
  • synonym: world - the earthly realm or affairs, humans and their society
