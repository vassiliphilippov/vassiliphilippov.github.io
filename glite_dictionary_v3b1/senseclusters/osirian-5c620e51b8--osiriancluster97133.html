Osirian

noun
follower of Osiris
Pronunciation: / oʊˈsaɪriən / (AmE), / əʊˈsɪəriən / (BrE)
a person who worships or is devoted to the ancient Egyptian god Osiris, especially a member of his cult

Examples

An Osirian priest
The ancient Osirian believed in life after death and performed elaborate burial rituals.
Artifacts found in the tomb suggest the deceased was an important Osirian priest.
During the festival, every Osirian wore symbols representing Osiris and his resurrection.

Related Terms

  • synonym: follower - a person who supports or admires a particular person or set of ideas
  • synonym: worshipper - someone who shows religious devotion to a deity
