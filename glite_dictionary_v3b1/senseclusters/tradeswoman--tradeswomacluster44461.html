tradeswoman

noun
female skilled worker
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtreɪdzˌwʊmən / (AmE), / ˈtreɪdzˌwʊmən / (BrE)
a woman who works in a skilled trade or manual occupation, such as construction or carpentry

Examples

A tradeswoman builds houses
The city is seeing more tradeswomen entering construction and electrical work.
As a successful tradeswoman, she encourages young women to pursue careers in the trades.
Many tradeswomen are breaking barriers in fields traditionally dominated by men.

Related Terms

  • synonym: craftswoman - a woman skilled in a particular craft
  • antonym: tradesman - a man who works in a skilled trade or manual occupation
