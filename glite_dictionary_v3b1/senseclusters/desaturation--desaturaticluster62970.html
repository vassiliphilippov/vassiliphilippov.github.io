desaturation

noun
drop in blood oxygen
7.6
Pronunciation: /diː.sæ.tʃə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.sæ.t.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /diː.sæ.tʃə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ /; / /diː.sæ.t.jə.ˈreɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a decrease in the level of oxygen saturation in the blood, often detected by medical monitoring

Examples

Oxygen desaturation event
The nurse noticed a sudden desaturation on the patient's monitor and called for assistance.
During exercise, people with lung disease may experience desaturation and require supplemental oxygen.
Continuous monitoring helps detect episodes of desaturation in patients after surgery.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hypoxemia - an abnormally low level of oxygen in the blood
  • antonym: saturation - the state of being fully saturated with oxygen
