rave review

noun
enthusiastic, highly positive evaluation or feedback
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪv rɪˌvju / (AmE), / ˈreɪv rɪˌvjuː / (BrE)
a written or spoken evaluation expressing great enthusiasm and strong praise, especially for a performance, product, or service

Examples

A rave review praises
After reading the rave review, I decided to book tickets for the play.
Her bakery opened last week and already boasts a rave review from the city’s toughest food critic.
The film received a rave review from almost every major newspaper.

  • synonym: glowing review - highly positive endorsement or evaluation
