foumart

noun
European polecat; woodland mustelid
8.4
Pronunciation: / ˈfuːˌmɑrt / (AmE), / ˈfaʊˌmɑːt / (BrE)
A dark brown mustelid (the European polecat) found in Eurasian woodlands, known for emitting an unpleasant odor when threatened.

Examples

A foumart animal
A startled foumart releases a strong odor to deter predators.
Farmers once trapped the foumart for its fur, despite its foul-smelling defense.
The foumart is rarely seen during the day, preferring to hunt at dusk.
