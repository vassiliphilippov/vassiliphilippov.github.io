lyricism

noun
exaggerated enthusiasm or expression
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪˌsɪzəm / (AmE), / ˈlɪrɪsɪz(ə)m / (BrE)
unrestrained and exaggerated enthusiasm, especially in expression or feeling

Examples

Emotional lyricism
Her acceptance speech was marked by a lyricism that moved the entire audience.
The author's lyricism was evident in his passionate descriptions of childhood.
There was a certain lyricism in the way he spoke about his dreams.
