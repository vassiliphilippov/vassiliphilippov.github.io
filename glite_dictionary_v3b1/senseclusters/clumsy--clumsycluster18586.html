clumsy

adjective
lacking grace or elegance in expression
Pronunciation: / ˈklʌmzi / (AmE), / ˈklʌmzi / (BrE)
(of expression) lacking elegance, polish, or grace; awkward or inept.

Examples

Clumsy sentence

The novel's dialogue felt clumsy and unnatural at key moments.
