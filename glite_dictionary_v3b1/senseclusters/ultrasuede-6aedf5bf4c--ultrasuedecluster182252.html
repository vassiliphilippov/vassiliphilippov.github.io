Ultrasuede

proper noun
synthetic suede fabric
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈʌltrəˌsweɪd / (AmE), / ˈʌltrəˌsweɪd / (BrE)
a brand name for a soft, durable synthetic fabric designed to look and feel like suede

Examples

Ultrasuede brand
The designer chose Ultrasuede for the jacket because it is easy to clean and looks elegant.
Many modern sofas are covered in Ultrasuede to provide a luxurious yet practical finish.
She prefers Ultrasuede handbags because they are lightweight and resist stains.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suede - a type of soft leather with a napped finish
