crikey

interjection
expression of surprise
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈkraɪki / (AmE), / ˈkraɪki / (BrE)

Examples

When he saw the size of the bill, he exclaimed, 'Crikey!'
Crikey, that storm came out of nowhere and surprised everyone.
