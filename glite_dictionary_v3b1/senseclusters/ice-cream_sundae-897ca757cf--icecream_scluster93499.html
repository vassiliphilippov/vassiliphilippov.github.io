ice-cream sundae

noun
ice cream dessert with toppings
Pronunciation: / ˈaɪsˌkrim ˈsʌndeɪ / (AmE), / ˈaɪsˌkriːm ˈsʌndeɪ / (BrE)
A sweet dessert consisting of ice cream topped with syrup, fruit, nuts, whipped cream, or other sweet toppings.

Examples

A ice-cream sundae dessert
After dinner, we all shared a huge ice-cream sundae with chocolate sauce and cherries.
Every birthday party at the shop features their signature ice-cream sundae bar.
She ordered a classic ice-cream sundae topped with whipped cream and a cherry at the diner.
