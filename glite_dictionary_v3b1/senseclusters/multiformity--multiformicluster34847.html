multiformity

noun
variety of forms
Pronunciation: / ˌmʌltɪˈfɔːrməti / (AmE), / ˌmʌltɪˈfɔːmɪti / (BrE)
the state or quality of having many different forms, shapes, or appearances

Examples

The multiformity of cultures
The multiformity of cultures in the city makes it a fascinating place to live.
Scientists were amazed by the multiformity of life found in the rainforest.
The designer's collection was praised for its multiformity and creativity.

Related Terms

  • synonym: diversity - the state of being diverse or varied
  • antonym: uniformity - the state of being the same or unvaried
