etheric

adjective
non-physical; airy
9.8
Pronunciation: /iː.ˈθe.rɪk/ /; / /ɪ.ˈθe.rɪk/ (AmE), /iː.ˈθe.rɪk/ /; / /ɪ.ˈθe.rɪk/ (BrE)
relating to something light, airy, or belonging to a non-physical or spiritual realm

Examples
