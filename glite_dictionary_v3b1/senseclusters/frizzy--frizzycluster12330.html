frizzy

adjective
tightly curled hair texture
2.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɪzi / (AmE), / ˈfrɪzi / (BrE)
(of hair) very tightly curled and often difficult to control; forming small, tight curls or waves.

Examples

Frizzy hair
He tried using gel to tame his frizzy curls before the meeting.
Her frizzy hair was difficult to brush after swimming in the sea.
Humidity always makes my frizzy hair even more unruly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: curly - (of hair) forming curls or ringlets
  • antonym: straight - (of hair) having no curls or waves
