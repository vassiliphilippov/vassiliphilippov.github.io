precordial

adjective
in front of the heart (medical)
9.9
Pronunciation: / priˈkɔrdiəl / (AmE), / priˈkɔːdiəl / (BrE)
situated in front of the heart; relating to the region of the chest over the heart (the precordium)

Examples

Precordial pain
A sharp precordial discomfort may be an early sign of heart issues.
Doctors listened carefully to precordial murmurs during the cardiac examination.
The patient complained of severe precordial pain after physical exertion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: precordium - the area of the chest in front of the heart
