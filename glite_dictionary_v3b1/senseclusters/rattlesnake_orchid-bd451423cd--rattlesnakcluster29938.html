rattlesnake orchid

noun
orchid of the genus Pholidota
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈræt.əlˌsneɪk ˈɔr.kɪd / (AmE), / ˈræt.əl.sneɪk ˈɔː.kɪd / (BrE)
Any of several orchids of the genus Pholidota, which have numerous white to brown flowers in spiraling racemes resembling a rattlesnake's tail.

Examples

Rattlesnake orchid flowers
Botanists studied several species of rattlesnake orchid in the cloud forest region.
The rattlesnake orchid is prized by collectors for its unusual flower spikes.
The spiraling racemes of the rattlesnake orchid inspired its unusual name.
