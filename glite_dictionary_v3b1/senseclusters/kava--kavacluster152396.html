kava

noun
alcoholic drink from kava roots
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑː.və / (AmE), / ˈkɑː.və / (BrE)
A mildly intoxicating beverage made from the aromatic roots of the kava plant, traditionally consumed in parts of the South Pacific.

Examples

A bowl of kava
At the ceremony, everyone sat in a circle and shared a bowl of kava.
She bought some dried kava roots to brew the traditional drink at home.
The taste of kava is earthy and slightly bitter, but its relaxing effects are popular.

Related Terms

  • synonym: kava plant - a tropical shrub whose roots are used to make kava
