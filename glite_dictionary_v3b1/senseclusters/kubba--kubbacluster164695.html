kubba

noun
domed Islamic tomb
Pronunciation: / ˈkuːbə / (AmE), / ˈkuːbə / (BrE)
a small domed building or tomb in Islamic architecture, often marking a grave or shrine

Examples

Ancient kubba building
The ancient kubba stood at the edge of the village, marking the resting place of a revered leader.
Pilgrims visited the kubba every year to pay their respects at the saint's tomb.
Restoration work on the old kubba preserved its beautiful domed roof and intricate tilework.

Related Terms

  • synonym: mausoleum - a large, stately tomb or building housing a tomb
  • synonym: shrine - a place regarded as holy because of its associations with a sacred person or event
