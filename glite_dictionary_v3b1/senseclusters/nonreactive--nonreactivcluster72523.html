nonreactive

adjective
no reaction (test)
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnriˈæktɪv / (AmE), / ˌnɒnriˈæktɪv / (BrE)

Examples

A nonreactive test result can provide reassurance to patients awaiting a diagnosis.
