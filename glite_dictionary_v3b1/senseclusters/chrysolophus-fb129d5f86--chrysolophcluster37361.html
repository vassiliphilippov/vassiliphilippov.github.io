Chrysolophus

noun
genus of golden pheasants
9.9
Pronunciation: / krɪˈsɒləfəs / (AmE), / krɪˈsɒləfəs / (BrE)
A genus of brightly colored pheasants known as golden pheasants, native to forests in China and parts of Asia.
