drunk

noun
a person who is intoxicated by alcohol
Pronunciation: / drʌŋk / (AmE), / drʌŋk / (BrE)
a person who is drunk or who habitually drinks too much alcohol

Examples

A drunk on the street
After the party, he was stumbling like a drunk down the street.
Some people avoid bars because they don't like dealing with a drunk.
The drunk in the alley was singing loudly as people walked by.
Everyone knew him as the town drunk who spent his days at the pub.
She refused to date a drunk after her last relationship.
The local bar is always full of drunks late at night.

Related Terms

  • synonym: alcoholic - a person addicted to alcohol
  • synonym: inebriate - a person who is drunk
  • antonym: sober - not affected by alcohol; not drunk
  • antonym: teetotaler - a person who abstains from alcohol
