mustard tree

noun
a poisonous evergreen South American shrub
5.3
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌstərd tri / (AmE), / ˈmʌstəd triː / (BrE)
an evergreen South American shrub, naturalized in the United States, that is sometimes responsible for poisoning livestock

Examples

Poisonous mustard tree
Researchers are studying the spread of the mustard tree across southern states.
The farmer lost several cows after they ate leaves from the mustard tree.
You should be careful not to let livestock graze near a mustard tree in that area.
