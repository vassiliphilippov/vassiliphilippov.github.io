aftertreatment

noun
treatment after procedure
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæftərˌtriːtmənt / (AmE), / ˈɑːftəˌtriːtmənt / (BrE)
a process, equipment, or device used after an initial procedure to improve results or reduce negative effects (especially emissions)

Examples

An aftertreatment system
The factory installed a new aftertreatment system to reduce air pollution from its exhaust.
Proper aftertreatment of wastewater is essential to meet environmental regulations.
The doctor recommended a special aftertreatment to speed up the patient's recovery.
The factory installed a new aftertreatment system to reduce harmful emissions from its machinery.
Proper aftertreatment is essential to ensure that wastewater meets environmental standards.
The mechanic explained that the truck's aftertreatment unit needed to be replaced due to a malfunction.
