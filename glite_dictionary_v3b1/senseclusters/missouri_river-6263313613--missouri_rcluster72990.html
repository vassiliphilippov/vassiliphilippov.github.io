Missouri River

proper noun
the longest river in the United States
2.7
Pronunciation: / mɪˈzʊri ˈrɪvər / (AmE), / mɪˈzʊəri ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
the longest river in the United States, flowing southeast from Montana to join the Mississippi River near St. Louis
