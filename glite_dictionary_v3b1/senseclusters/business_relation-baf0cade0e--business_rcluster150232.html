business relation

noun
relationship between business enterprises
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪznəs rɪˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˈbɪznəs rɪˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
a relationship or association between different business enterprises, such as partnerships, alliances, or commercial agreements

Examples

International business relation
International business relation issues are often discussed at global economic forums.
Our company values strong business relations with suppliers around the world.
The merger will improve business relations between the two tech giants.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partnership - an association between two or more businesses based on shared interests or purposes
