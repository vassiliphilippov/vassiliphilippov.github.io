dock

verb
cut short an animal's tail
8.5 0.10/m
Pronunciation: / dÉ‘k / (AmE), / dÉ’k / (BrE)
to remove part of an animal's tail, usually for medical, practical, or cosmetic reasons

Examples

To dock a tail
It is common practice to dock the tails of certain dog breeds.
Many animal welfare organizations oppose the routine docking of animals' tails.
The farmer had to dock the lambs' tails for health reasons.
