Euryale

proper noun
second eldest Gorgon in Greek mythology
Pronunciation: / jʊˈraɪ.əli / (AmE), / jʊˈraɪ.əli / (BrE)
In Greek mythology, the second eldest of the three Gorgons, sisters to Medusa and Stheno.

Examples

Gorgon Euryale
Few myths focus solely on Euryale, but she plays a key role in the Gorgons' legend.
In ancient art, Euryale is often depicted alongside Medusa and Stheno.
The myth describes Euryale and her sisters as immortal beings with deadly powers.
