feeder

noun
outdoor device for feeding birds
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈfidər / (AmE), / ˈfiːdə / (BrE)
a container or device placed outdoors to provide food for wild birds or other animals

Examples

A bird feeder
Every winter we refill the feeder so the local birds have enough to eat.
She set up a new feeder in the garden to attract finches and sparrows.
Squirrels kept stealing seeds from the feeder outside our window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bird table - a platform for putting out food for birds outdoors
  • antonym: birdhouse - shelter for birds, usually for nesting (not food)
