hold

verb
aim or direct (tool or device) at target
Pronunciation: / hoʊld / (AmE), / həʊld / (BrE)
to aim, point, or direct something (such as a tool or device) towards a target or object

Examples

To hold a camera
Carefully hold the flashlight towards the dark corner so you can see better.
She held the microphone close to the speaker to capture his voice clearly.
When fighting a fire, you must hold the extinguisher nozzle at the base of the flames.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aim - to point or direct (something) with a particular purpose
