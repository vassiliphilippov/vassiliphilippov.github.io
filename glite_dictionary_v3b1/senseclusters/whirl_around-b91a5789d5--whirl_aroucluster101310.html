whirl around

verb
move/spin rapidly in circles
Pronunciation: / wɜrl əˈraʊnd / (AmE), / wɜːl əˈraʊnd / (BrE)
to move rapidly and continuously in a circular or spinning motion, often through the air

Examples

To whirl around quickly
Dust and debris whirled around the construction site during the storm.
The dancers whirled around the stage in a blur of color and motion.
The leaves began to whirl around as the autumn wind picked up.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spin - to turn around quickly; rotate rapidly
