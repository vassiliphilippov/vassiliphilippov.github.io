gooey

adjective
soft and sticky
9.6 2.7
Pronunciation: /ˈguː.iː/ /; / /ˈguː.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈguː.iː/ /; / /ˈguː.ɪ/ (BrE)
soft and sticky in texture or consistency, often in a way that is messy or viscous.

Examples

A gooey texture
After playing in the mud, the children’s hands were gooey and needed washing.
The chocolate cake was so gooey that it stuck to the knife.
Warm cheese makes the pizza deliciously gooey in the center.
