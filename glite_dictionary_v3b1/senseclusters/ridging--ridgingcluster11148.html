ridging

noun
formation of ridges
6.7
Pronunciation: / ˈrɪdʒɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈrɪdʒɪŋ / (BrE)
the formation of raised lines or ridges on a surface

Examples

Ridging on the surface
The ridging on the cake gave it a decorative look.
Repeated freezing and thawing caused ridging on the surface of the road.
The potter admired the subtle ridging left by her fingers on the vase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corrugation - the process of forming parallel ridges and grooves
  • antonym: smoothing - the process of making a surface even and flat
