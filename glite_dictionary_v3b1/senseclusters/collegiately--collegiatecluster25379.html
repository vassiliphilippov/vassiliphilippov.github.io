collegiately

adverb
in a cooperative way
6.2
Pronunciation: / kəˈliːdʒiətli / (AmE), / kəˈliːdʒiətli / (BrE)
in a manner that shows friendly cooperation and mutual respect among colleagues or peers

Examples

Worked collegiately together
The team members discussed the project collegiately to ensure everyone’s ideas were considered.
Problems are often solved more effectively when people work collegiately rather than competitively.
The board handled the disagreement collegiately, maintaining a positive and respectful atmosphere.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cooperatively - in a way that involves working together with others
  • antonym: individually - in a way that is done alone or separately
