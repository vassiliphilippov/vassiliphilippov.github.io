Mollusca

noun
phylum of soft-bodied invertebrate animals
7.3
Pronunciation: /mə.ˈlʌs.kə/ (AmE), /mɒ.ˈlʌs.kə/ (BrE)
a large group (phylum) of invertebrate animals with soft bodies, often with a shell, including snails, clams, squids, and octopuses
