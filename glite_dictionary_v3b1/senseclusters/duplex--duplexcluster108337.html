duplex

noun
apartment on two floors
3.5 1.33/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàduÀê.pl…õks /; / ÀàduÀåpl…õks / (AmE), / ÀàdjuÀê.pleks /; / ÀàdjuÀêpl…õks / (BrE)
a house or apartment divided into two separate units or with rooms on two floors

Examples

A duplex apartment
After moving to the city, they rented a duplex in a historic neighborhood.
She enjoys living in a duplex because it feels more like a house than a single-level apartment.
Their new duplex has spacious living areas upstairs and bedrooms downstairs.
The duplex has two identical apartments, each with its own kitchen and living room.
They moved into a spacious duplex on the edge of the city.
We decided to rent the lower unit of a duplex while saving for a house.

Synonyms

  • duplex house
    3.5 0.00/m
    duplex house is a building split into two separate homes sharing a wall, while duplex can also mean a two-floor apartment.
  • duplex apartment
    3.4 0.06/m
    duplex apartment is an apartment with rooms on two floors joined by stairs, while duplex can also mean two separate units.

Related Terms

  • synonym: maisonette - a small house, especially one that is part of a larger building, typically with two floors
  • synonym: semi-detached house - a house joined to another on one side
