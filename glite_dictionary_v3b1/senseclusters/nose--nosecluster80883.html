nose

noun
front section of an aircraft or similar vehicle
7.4
Pronunciation: / noʊz / (AmE), / nəʊz / (BrE)
the front or projecting part of something, such as an aircraft, tool, or container

Examples

Nose of the plane
During launch, the nose of the rocket experienced significant pressure.

He ducked under the nose of the gun to avoid being seen.
The nose of the drill allows precise starting points on the metal surface.
Be sure the nose of the oil can is clean before lubricating the machinery.
The teapot's nose allows for a smooth, controlled flow of tea.
The watering can has a long nose to make pouring easier.
