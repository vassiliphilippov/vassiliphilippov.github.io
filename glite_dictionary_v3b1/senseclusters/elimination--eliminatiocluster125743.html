elimination

noun
murder of a competitor
1.3
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlɪməˈneɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌlɪmɪˈneɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of murdering or removing a competitor, especially to gain an advantage

Examples

Ruthless elimination of rivals
In the criminal underworld, elimination of opponents is often seen as the only way to rise to power.
Police suspect that the sudden elimination of the competitor was linked to the business dispute.
The gangster ordered the elimination of his main rival to secure his control over the territory.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assassination - the murder of a prominent person for political or ideological reasons
