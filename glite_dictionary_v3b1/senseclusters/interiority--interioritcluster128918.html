interiority

noun
inner focus
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntɪriˈɔrəti / (AmE), / ˌɪntɪəriˈɒrɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being inward or focused on inner thoughts, feelings, or character

Examples

The interiority of thoughts
The author uses stream-of-consciousness writing to reveal the interiority of the protagonist.
Meditation can help people connect with their own interiority and understand their emotions.
Modern art often tries to express the interiority of the artist rather than just external reality.
The novel explores the interiority of its main character through detailed inner monologues.
Artists often try to express their own interiority in their work, revealing personal thoughts and feelings.
The architect focused on the interiority of the space to create a sense of calm and privacy.

Related Terms

  • antonym: exteriority - the state or quality of being outside or external
  • antonym: extroversion - the quality of being outgoing and focused on the external world
  • synonym: interior - the inner part or inside of something
  • synonym: introspection - the examination of one's own thoughts and feelings
