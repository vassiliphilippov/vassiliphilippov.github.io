snow day

noun
day when events are cancelled due to snow
4.9
Pronunciation: / snoʊ deɪ / (AmE), / snəʊ deɪ / (BrE)
A day when classes or other scheduled activities are cancelled because of heavy snowfall.

Examples

A snow day off
Children love snow days because they get an unexpected holiday from school.
School was closed yesterday because of a snow day.
We spent the entire snow day building a giant snowman in the yard.

Related Terms

  • synonym: school closure - the shutting down of schools temporarily, often for safety or emergency reasons
