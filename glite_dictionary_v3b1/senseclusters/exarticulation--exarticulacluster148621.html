exarticulation

noun
joint amputation
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌɛksɑːrtɪkjuˈleɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɛksɑːtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən / (BrE)
the surgical removal of a limb by cutting through a joint rather than through the bone itself

Examples

Surgical exarticulation
The surgeon performed an exarticulation at the knee to remove the damaged leg.
In some cases, exarticulation is preferred over traditional amputation for better prosthetic fitting.
After the accident, the doctors recommended exarticulation of the finger at the joint.

Related Terms

  • synonym: amputation - surgical removal of a limb or part of a limb
  • synonym: disarticulation - surgical separation of a limb at a joint
