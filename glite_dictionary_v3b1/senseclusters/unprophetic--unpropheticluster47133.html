unprophetic

adjective
not able to predict the future
Pronunciation: / ˌʌn.prəˈfɛt.ɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌn.prəˈfɛt.ɪk / (BrE)
not showing the ability to predict or foresee what will happen; not prophetic.

Examples

An unprophetic comment
His unprophetic comments failed to anticipate the economic downturn.
History proved her unprophetic warnings to be completely misplaced.
The analyst's unprophetic outlook led many investors astray.

Related Terms

  • antonym: prophetic - having the ability to predict or foresee future events
