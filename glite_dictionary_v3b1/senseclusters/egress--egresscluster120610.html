egress

noun
the act of going out or an exit
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈiːˌɡrɛs / (AmE), / ˈiːˌɡrɛs /; / ˈiːɡrɛs / (BrE)
the act of going out, leaving, or emerging from a place; an exit or way out

Examples

An egress door
During the fire drill, everyone practiced using the nearest egress.
Natural light aids the egress of workers at the end of their shift.
The emergency egress was clearly marked with a glowing green sign.
A curtain of fog obstructed the egress of the sun in the early morning.
The scientist timed the egress of butterflies from their cocoons with great precision.
The seeds' sudden egress from the soil marked the beginning of spring.

  • synonym: exit - a way out; the act of leaving a place
