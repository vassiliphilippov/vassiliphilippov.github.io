West Indian smallpox

noun
mild smallpox variant in the West Indies
9.1
Pronunciation: /west ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈsmɔːl.pɑks/ /; / /west ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈsmɑːl.pɑks/ (AmE), /west ˈɪn.dɪ.ən ˈsmɔːl.pɒks/ (BrE)
