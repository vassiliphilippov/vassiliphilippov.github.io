devoir

noun
French school essay
Pronunciation: / dəˈvwɑr / (AmE), / dəˈvwɑː / (BrE)
a short essay or written assignment, especially as given to students in French schools

Examples

A school devoir
The teacher collected each student's devoir at the end of the lesson.
For homework, we had to write a devoir about our favorite book.
In French schools, a devoir is often assigned to practice writing skills.

Related Terms

  • synonym: assignment - a piece of work given to students as part of their studies
  • synonym: essay - a short piece of writing on a particular subject
