infuriated

adjective
extremely angry
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈfjʊrieɪtɪd / (AmE), / ɪnˈfjʊərieɪtɪd / (BrE)
Extremely angry; filled with intense fury or rage.

Examples

Infuriated by unfair rules
He was so infuriated by the unfair treatment that he threatened to resign.
She became infuriated when she found her car had been towed without warning.
The infuriated crowd demanded answers from the officials after the announcement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - not showing or feeling strong emotions such as anger
  • synonym: enraged - filled with rage; very angry
  • synonym: furious - feeling or showing strong anger
