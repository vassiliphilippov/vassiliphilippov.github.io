nefarious

adjective
wicked; criminal; evil
Pronunciation: / nəˈfɛriəs / (AmE), / nɪˈfeəriəs / (BrE)
(of an action or activity) wicked or criminal; evil in nature.

Examples

Nefarious activities
He was known for his nefarious activities in the underground crime network.
The authorities uncovered a nefarious plot to embezzle funds from the charity.
The detective was determined to expose the nefarious schemes of the corrupt officials.

Related Terms

  • antonym: innocent - not guilty or evil; blameless
  • synonym: villainous - wicked; characteristic of a villain
