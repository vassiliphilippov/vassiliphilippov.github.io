seafood sauce

noun
sauce served with seafood, usually ketchup and horseradish
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌfud sɔs / (AmE), / ˈsiːfuːd sɔːs / (BrE)
a tangy sauce, typically made from ketchup, horseradish, and lemon juice, served with seafood dishes

Examples

A classic seafood sauce
A classic seafood sauce combines ketchup, horseradish, and a squeeze of lemon.
Please bring some extra seafood sauce for the oysters.
She dipped the shrimp into the seafood sauce before taking a bite.
