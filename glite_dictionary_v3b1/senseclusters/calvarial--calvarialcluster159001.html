calvarial

adjective
of the skull cap
Pronunciation: / kælˈvɛriəl / (AmE), / kælˈvɛːriəl / (BrE)
relating to the calvaria, the dome-like upper part of the skull that encloses the brain

Examples

Calvarial fracture
The doctor noted a calvarial fracture after reviewing the patient's CT scan.
Surgery was required to repair the calvarial defect caused by the injury.
Researchers studied the thickness of calvarial bones in different age groups.
