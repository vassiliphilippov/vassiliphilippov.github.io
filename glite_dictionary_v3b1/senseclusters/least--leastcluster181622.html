least

noun
smallest amount or importance
2.3
Pronunciation: / list / (AmE), / liːst / (BrE)
the smallest amount or degree; something that is of the lowest importance or significance

Examples

The least I can do
Doing this for you is the least I can do.
She didn't care in the least about their objections.
That is the least of my worries right now.

  • antonym: most - the greatest amount or degree
