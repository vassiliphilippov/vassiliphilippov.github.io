gastro

noun
informal gastropub
Pronunciation: / ˈɡæstroʊ / (AmE), / ˈɡæstrəʊ / (BrE)
an informal term for a gastropub, a restaurant or bar serving high-quality food and drinks

Examples

Trendy gastro bar
We decided to try the new gastro in town for dinner last weekend.
The gastro was packed with people enjoying craft beers and gourmet burgers.
After work, they met at a local gastro to relax and catch up.
