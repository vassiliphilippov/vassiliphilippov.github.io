doldrum

noun
state of inactivity
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˈdoʊldrəm / (AmE), / ˈdɒldrəm / (BrE)
a period of low spirits, inactivity, or boredom

Examples

In a doldrum mood
After losing his job, he found himself in a doldrum for several months.
The team tried new strategies to break out of their midseason doldrum.
She realized she was stuck in a doldrum and needed a change in her routine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: depression - a state of low mood and lack of activity
  • antonym: excitement - a state of lively activity and enthusiasm
  • synonym: slump - a period of decline or reduced activity
