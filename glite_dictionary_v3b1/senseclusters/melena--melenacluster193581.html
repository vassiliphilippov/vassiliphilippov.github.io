melena

noun
dark, blood-tainted feces (medical)
Pronunciation: / məˈlinə /; / məˈliːnə / (AmE), / mɪˈliːnə /; / məˈliːnə / (BrE)
The passage of abnormally dark, tarry feces containing blood, usually as a result of gastrointestinal bleeding.

Examples

Dark melena stool
Gastrointestinal bleeding is often indicated by the appearance of melena.
Patients experiencing melena should seek medical attention promptly.

An episode of melaena may indicate a serious medical condition requiring prompt attention.
The doctor suspected gastrointestinal bleeding when the patient presented with melaena.
The nurse noted that the patient's melaena had a distinct, foul odor and sticky texture.
