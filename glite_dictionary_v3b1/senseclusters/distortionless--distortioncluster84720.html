distortionless

adjective
without distortion
Pronunciation: / dɪsˈtɔrʃənləs / (AmE), / dɪsˈtɔːʃənləs / (BrE)
not affected by distortion; maintaining the original shape, form, or signal without alteration

Examples

Distortionless signal
The engineer designed a distortionless transmission line for the new communication system.
For accurate results, the measurement device must provide a distortionless reading.
The audio equipment is praised for its distortionless sound quality even at high volumes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: distorted - changed from the original shape or form; affected by distortion
  • synonym: undistorted - not changed in shape or form; free from distortion
