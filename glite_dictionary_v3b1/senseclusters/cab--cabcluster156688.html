cab

noun
driver's compartment in a vehicle
Pronunciation: / kæb / (AmE), / kab / (BrE)
the part at the front of a vehicle, such as a truck, train, or crane, where the driver or operator sits

Examples

Truck cab
Modern trucks often have comfortable sleeping areas behind the cab.
The crane operator climbed into the cab to start his shift.
The engineer waved from the cab of the locomotive as the train departed.
