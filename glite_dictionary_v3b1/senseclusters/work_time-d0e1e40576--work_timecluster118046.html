work time

noun
period when one is required to work
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɝk taɪm / (AmE), / wɜːk taɪm / (BrE)
the period during which a person is expected or required to be working, especially as set by an employer or by law

Examples

Work time hours
During work time, employees are expected to stay focused on their assigned tasks.
He prefers to keep his personal phone off during work time.
Lunch breaks are not counted as part of your work time.

Related Terms

  • antonym: leisure time - time when one is not working; free time
  • synonym: working hours - the times of day during which employees are expected to be at work
