bearberry

noun
evergreen subshrub with red berries of the North
9.9 7.5
Pronunciation: /ˈber.bər.ɪ/ (AmE), /ˈbeə.bər.ɪ/ (BrE)
a shrub with red berries, often found in northern or central regions, whose berries are eaten by bears
