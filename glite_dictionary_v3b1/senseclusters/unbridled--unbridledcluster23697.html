unbridled

adjective
not restrained; uncontrolled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbraɪdəld / (AmE), / ʌnˈbraɪdld / (BrE)
(of feelings, actions, or desires) not restrained or controlled; without limits or moderation.

Examples

Unbridled ambition
He pursued his dreams with unbridled ambition, never letting anything stand in his way.
Her unbridled enthusiasm inspired everyone around her to join the project.
The protests were marked by unbridled anger and passionate speeches.

Related Terms

  • antonym: restrained - kept under control; restrained
  • synonym: unchecked - not restrained or held in check
