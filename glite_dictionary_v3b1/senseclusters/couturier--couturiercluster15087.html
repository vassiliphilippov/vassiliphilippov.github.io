couturier

noun
fashion designer of custom-made clothing
6.6
Pronunciation: / kuːˈtʊri.eɪ / (AmE), / kuːˈtjʊə.ri.eɪ / (BrE)
a designer of fashionable, custom-made clothing, especially for women

Examples

A couturier designs dresses
As a young couturier, he learned the importance of attention to detail in every stitch.
She dreams of becoming a couturier and creating elegant gowns for celebrities.
The renowned couturier unveiled his latest collection at Paris Fashion Week.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashion designer - a person who designs clothing with a focus on current trends
  • synonym: modiste - a maker or seller of fashionable women's hats and clothing
