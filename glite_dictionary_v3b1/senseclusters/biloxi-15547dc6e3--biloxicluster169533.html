Biloxi

noun
a Siouan language of the Biloxi people
Pronunciation: / bɪˈlɑːksi / (AmE), / bɪˈlɒksi / (BrE)
the Siouan language formerly spoken by the Biloxi people of the United States, now considered extinct.

Examples

The Biloxi language
Few written records remain of Biloxi, making it difficult to reconstruct fully.
Linguists have studied the structure of Biloxi to better understand the Siouan language family.
The last native speaker of Biloxi passed away in the early twentieth century.
