manager

noun
person who controls resources or finances
Pronunciation: / ˈmænɪdʒər / (AmE), / ˈmænɪdʒə / (BrE)
a person responsible for controlling resources, finances, or operations in an organization or enterprise

Examples

Company manager
As a project manager, she oversees both the financial and human resources.
It is the manager's job to allocate resources effectively within the company.
The manager of the department approved the new budget proposal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: administrator - person responsible for running a business, organization, etc.
  • antonym: employee - person who works for another, usually not controlling resources
  • synonym: supervisor - person overseeing work or workers
