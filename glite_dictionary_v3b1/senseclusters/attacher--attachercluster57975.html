attacher

noun
person or device fastening
6.8
Pronunciation: / əˈtætʃər / (AmE), / əˈtatʃə / (BrE)
a person or device that attaches or fastens something to another object

Examples

The attacher device
The attacher quickly fixed the badge to each visitor's shirt at the entrance.
A new type of attacher makes it easier to secure cables neatly behind your desk.
Each package was labeled by an attacher before being sent out for delivery.

Related Terms

  • antonym: remover - a person or tool that takes something off
