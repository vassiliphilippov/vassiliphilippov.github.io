pileup

noun
road accident with multiple vehicle collisions
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪlˌʌp / (AmE), / ˈpaɪlʌp / (BrE)
a crash involving several vehicles on the road, often as a result of one initial collision

Examples

Highway pileup
A chain-reaction pileup can be especially dangerous in icy weather.
The highway was closed for hours because of a massive pileup involving more than twenty cars.
Yesterday’s fog was blamed for the seven-vehicle pileup on Main Street.

Related Terms

  • synonym: collision - an instance of one moving object or person striking against another
  • synonym: crash - an accident involving vehicles striking each other or an object
