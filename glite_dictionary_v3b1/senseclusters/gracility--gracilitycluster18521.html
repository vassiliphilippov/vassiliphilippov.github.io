gracility

noun
elegance and beauty of movement
Pronunciation: / ɡrəˈsɪl.ə.ti / (AmE), / ɡrӕˈsɪl.ɪ.ti / (BrE)
elegance and beauty of movement or expression; graceful quality.

Examples

Surprising gracility
He moved through the crowded room with surprising gracility.
The gracility of the dancer impressed the entire audience.
The sculpture captures the gracility of a bird in flight.
