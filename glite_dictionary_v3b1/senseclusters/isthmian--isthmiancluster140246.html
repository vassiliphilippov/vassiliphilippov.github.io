isthmian

adjective
relating to an isthmus
Pronunciation: / ˈɪsθmiən / (AmE), / ˈɪsθmiən / (BrE)
relating to, or forming an isthmus (a narrow strip of land connecting two larger land areas)

Examples

Isthmian games
He studied the ancient isthmian games held near Corinth in Greece.
Much of the region's commerce depends on the isthmian passage across the country.
The isthmian landform connects the mainland to the peninsula, enabling migration of species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: isthmus - a narrow strip of land connecting two larger land areas
