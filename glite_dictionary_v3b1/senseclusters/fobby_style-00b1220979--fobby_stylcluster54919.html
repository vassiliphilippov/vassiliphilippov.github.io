fobby style

noun
quirky or awkward fashion style
7.1 8.7
Pronunciation: / ˈfɑːbi staɪl / (AmE), / ˈfɒbi staɪl / (BrE)
A manner of dressing or presenting oneself perceived as quirky, awkward, or unfashionable, often associated with recent immigrants, especially from East Asia.

Examples

Fobby style clothes
His fobby style was obvious from his patterned shirts and oversized backpack.
She embraced her fobby style, wearing playful socks and thick-rimmed glasses to school.
Some people make fun of his fobby style, but he wears whatever makes him comfortable.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nerdy - fashion or behavior that is socially awkward; similar connotation to 'fobby style'
  • antonym: trendy - very fashionable; the opposite of unfashionable style like 'fobby style' often is
