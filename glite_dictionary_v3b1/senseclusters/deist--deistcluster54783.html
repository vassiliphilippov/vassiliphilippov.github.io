deist

adjective
relating to deism
9.7 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàdiÀê…™st / (AmE), / ÀàdiÀê…™st / (BrE)
Relating to or characteristic of deism, the belief in the existence of a supreme being, specifically of a creator who does not intervene in the universe.

Examples

Deist philosophy
Her deist beliefs distinguish her from traditional religious communities.
Some founding fathers of the United States were known for their deist views.
The philosopher espoused a deist perspective in his writings on faith and reason.

Synonyms

  • deistic
    9.7 0.11/m
    deistic describes ideas or works influenced by belief in a non-intervening creator, while deist names a person who holds that belief.

Related Terms

  • antonym: atheist - not believing in the existence of a god or gods; rejecting theism
  • antonym: theist - believing in the existence of a god or gods, especially a personal god
