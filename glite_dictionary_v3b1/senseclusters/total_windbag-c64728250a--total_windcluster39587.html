total windbag

noun
person who talks too much, boringly
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtoʊt̬l ˈwɪndˌbæɡ / (AmE), / ˈtəʊtl ˈwɪndˌbaɡ / (BrE)
a person who talks excessively, especially about trivial matters, and often bores others

Examples

A total windbag talks

Nobody likes sitting next to a total windbag on long flights.
She's smart, but some people unfairly label her as a total windbag because of her enthusiasm.
