Pamiri

noun
Eastern Iranian language
9.9
Pronunciation: / pɑːˈmiːri / (AmE), / pɑːˈmiːri / (BrE)
any of several Eastern Iranian languages spoken in the Pamir Mountains of Central Asia

Examples

Pamiri language
She published a grammar of the Pamiri spoken in the Bartang valley.
Many people in the region speak a Pamiri as their first language.
Linguists are working to document the endangered Pamiri languages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Iranian - a branch of the Indo-Iranian languages including Persian, Kurdish, and others
  • antonym: Tajik - a Western Iranian language spoken in Tajikistan and surrounding regions
