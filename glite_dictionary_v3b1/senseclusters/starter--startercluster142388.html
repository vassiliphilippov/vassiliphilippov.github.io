starter

noun
person beginning an activity
6.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑrtɚ / (AmE), / ˈstɑːtə / (BrE)
a person who is beginning to take part in an activity, sport, or field for the first time

Examples

A starter in chess
As a starter in the chess club, he is eager to learn new strategies.
Don't feel intimidated if you're only a starter in the world of programming.
The class offers support for every starter, no matter their background.

Related Terms

  • synonym: beginner - a person just starting to learn a skill or take part in an activity
  • antonym: expert - a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area
  • synonym: novice - a person new to and inexperienced in a job or situation
