tilter

noun
person who jousts in a tournament
Pronunciation: / ˈtɪltər / (AmE), / ˈtɪltə / (BrE)
a person who participates in a tilt or joust, especially in medieval tournaments

Examples

Medieval tilter
As a skilled tilter, he was celebrated throughout the kingdom for his bravery.
Each tilter wore a suit of armor designed for the tournament.
The medieval tilter prepared his lance before riding into the arena.

Related Terms

  • synonym: joust - a combat between two mounted knights with lances
