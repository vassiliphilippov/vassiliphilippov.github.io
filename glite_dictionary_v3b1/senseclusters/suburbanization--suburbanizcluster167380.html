suburbanization

noun
growth of suburbs
Pronunciation: / səˌbɝːbənəˈzeɪʃən /; / səˌbɜrbənəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / səˌbɜːbənʌɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of people, businesses, or services moving from cities to suburbs, leading to the growth of suburban areas
