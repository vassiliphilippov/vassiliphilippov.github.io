Vaccinium pennsylvanicum

noun
lowbush blueberry shrub of northeast North America
9.9
Pronunciation: / vækˈsɪniəm ˌpɛn.səlˈvænɪ.kəm / (AmE), / vækˈsɪniəm ˌpɛn.səlˈvæn.ɪ.kəm / (BrE)
A low-growing deciduous shrub native to northeastern North America, known for its compact racemes of flowers and sweet dark blue berries, commonly called the lowbush blueberry.
