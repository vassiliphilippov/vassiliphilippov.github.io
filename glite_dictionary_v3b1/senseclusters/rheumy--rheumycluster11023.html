rheumy

adjective
watery or affected with rheum (e.g., eyes)
Pronunciation: / ˈruːmi / (AmE), / ˈruːmi / (BrE)
(of a part of the body, especially the eyes) moist, watery, or affected with rheum; sometimes related to symptoms of arthritis or age.

Examples

Rheumy eyes
After a sleepless night, her rheumy eyes betrayed her exhaustion.
His rheumy eyes glistened in the candlelight as he spoke.
The dog's rheumy gaze made him look older than his years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: watery - containing or producing water or moisture
