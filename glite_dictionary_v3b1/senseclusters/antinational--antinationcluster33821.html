antinational

noun
opponent of nationalism
Pronunciation: / ˌæntiˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌæntiˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)
a person who opposes or acts against the interests or values of their own nation or nationalism

Examples

An antinational critic
The journalist was labeled an antinational for criticizing government policies.
Some people called the protestors antinational because they opposed the new law.
Debates about who is considered an antinational often become heated in political discussions.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nationalist - a person who strongly supports their nation's interests
  • antonym: patriot - a person who loves and supports their country
