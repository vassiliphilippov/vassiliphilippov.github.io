thiazide

noun
diuretic drug for hypertension
9.9 6.5
Pronunciation: /ˈθaɪ.ə.zaɪd/ (AmE), /ˈθaɪ.ə.zaɪd/ (BrE)
any of a group of drugs used as diuretics to treat high blood pressure by blocking sodium reabsorption in the kidneys

Examples

Thiazide diuretic drug
He experienced increased urination after starting a thiazide medication.
Many patients respond well to thiazide diuretics for controlling hypertension.
The doctor prescribed a thiazide to help manage her high blood pressure.

  • synonym: diuretic - a substance that promotes diuresis (urine production)
