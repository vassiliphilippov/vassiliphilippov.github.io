acidimetry

noun
analysis of bases using standard acid solutions
9.9
Pronunciation: /ə.sɪ.ˈdɪ.mə.tri/ /; / /æ.sɪ.ˈdɪ.mə.tri/ (AmE), /ə.sɪ.ˈdɪ.mə.tri/ /; / /æ.sɪ.ˈdɪ.mə.tri/ (BrE)
the process of determining the amount of a base in a substance by measuring with a standard acid solution
