paint job

noun
the act or result of painting a surface
Pronunciation: / peɪnt dʒɑb / (AmE), / peɪnt dʒɒb / (BrE)
the act or result of painting a surface, especially as a coating applied to something such as a wall, car, or building

Examples

A paint job on walls
After years of neglect, the living room desperately needed a paint job.
The paint job on the old car made it look brand new.
They hired professionals to give the kitchen cabinets a fresh paint job.

Related Terms

  • synonym: painting - the process or art of applying paint
