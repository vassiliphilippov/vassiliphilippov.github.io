Dilleniaceae

noun
family of tropical shrubs, trees, and climbers
9.9
Pronunciation: / dɪˌlɛniˈeɪsi.i /; / dɪˌlɛniˈeɪsiˌi / (AmE), / dɪˌlɛniˈeɪsiː.iː /; / dɪˌlɛniˈeɪsiːˌiː / (BrE)
a family of mainly tropical shrubs, trees, and climbing plants with leathery leaves or flattened, leaflike stems, including genera such as Dillenia and Hibbertia
