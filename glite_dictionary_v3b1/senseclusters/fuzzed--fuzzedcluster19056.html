fuzzed

adjective
covered with fine light hairs
Pronunciation: / fʌzd / (AmE), / fʌzd / (BrE)
Covered with fine, light hairs; having a soft, downy surface.

Examples

A fuzzed surface
After the haircut, his scalp appeared slightly fuzzed in the sunlight.
The old teddy bear was fuzzed around the edges from years of use.
The peaches looked fuzzed, making them feel soft to the touch.
