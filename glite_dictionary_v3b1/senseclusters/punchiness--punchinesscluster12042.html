punchiness

noun
dazed or weak state
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpʌntʃinəs / (AmE), / ˈpʌntʃinəs / (BrE)
a state of feeling slightly dazed, weak, or unsteady, often after shock, illness, or lack of sleep

Examples

Morning punchiness
After staying up all night, he felt a lingering punchiness that made it hard to concentrate.
The punchiness from her cold left her feeling unsteady during the meeting.
Travelers often experience punchiness after long flights and little sleep.

Related Terms

  • antonym: alertness - the state of being fully awake and attentive
  • synonym: grogginess - a state of being dazed or lacking full alertness
