winter-flowering

adjective
blooming in winter (plants)
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪn.tɚ ˌflaʊ.ɚ.ɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwɪn.tə ˌflaʊ.ər.ɪŋ / (BrE)
(of a plant) blooming or producing flowers during the winter season.

Examples

Winter-flowering plants
Gardeners often appreciate winter-flowering plants for their ability to brighten up bleak winter landscapes.
Several species of jasmine are known for being winter-flowering in milder climates.
The winter-flowering camellias add color to the garden during the coldest months.
