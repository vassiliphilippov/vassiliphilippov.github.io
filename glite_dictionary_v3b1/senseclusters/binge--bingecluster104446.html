binge

verb
watch large amounts of media at once
5.4 3.7
Pronunciation: / bɪndʒ / (AmE), / bɪndʒ / (BrE)
to do something, such as eating, drinking, or watching, in large amounts over a short period of time

Examples

During the holidays, people often binge more than they realize.

  • synonym: overeat - to eat more food than necessary or healthy
  • synonym: overindulge - to allow oneself to have too much of something enjoyable
  • antonym: restrain - to hold back or limit oneself
