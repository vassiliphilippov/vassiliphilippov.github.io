selectable

adjective
able to be chosen
Pronunciation: / səˈlɛktəbəl / (AmE), / sɪˈlɛktəbəl / (BrE)
able to be chosen for a position, team, or role based on merit or ability

Examples

The committee considered her highly selectable because of her impressive qualifications.
