St. John

proper noun
river in Maine and New Brunswick
5.0
Pronunciation: / seɪnt dʒɑn / (AmE), / seɪnt dʒɒn / (BrE)
A river originating in Maine, flowing northeast through New Brunswick, and emptying into the Bay of Fundy.

Examples
