alogical

adjective
lacking logic
9.9
Pronunciation: / eɪˈlɑːdʒɪkəl / (AmE), / əˈlɒdʒɪkəl / (BrE)
not following logical reasoning or lacking logic

Examples

An alogical explanation
Her explanation was so alogical that nobody could understand her point.
The theory was dismissed as alogical by most scientists in the field.
It is difficult to debate with someone whose arguments are completely alogical.

Related Terms
