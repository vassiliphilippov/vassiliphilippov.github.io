discount house

noun
shop selling goods at reduced prices
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt haʊs / (AmE), / ˈdɪsˌkaʊnt haʊs / (BrE)
A retail store or sales outlet that sells goods at prices lower than those usually charged.

Examples

A discount house store
Many people prefer buying appliances from a discount house instead of paying full price at regular stores.
She always shops at the local discount house to save money on groceries.
The new discount house attracted customers with its low prices and wide selection of goods.
