sea lettuce

noun
a type of edible green seaweed
Pronunciation: / si ˈlɛt̬.ɪs / (AmE), / si ˈlɛt.ɪs / (BrE)
an edible green seaweed with thin, crinkled, translucent fronds, commonly found in shallow marine waters

Examples

Fresh sea lettuce
Divers often spot sea lettuce growing on rocks in shallow ocean waters.
Many coastal salads use sea lettuce for its crisp texture and salty flavor.
The sushi was topped with fresh sea lettuce harvested that morning.

Related Terms

  • synonym: seaweed - a general term for marine algae, especially edible types
