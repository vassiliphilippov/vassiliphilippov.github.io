transactionally

adverb
involving transactions
Pronunciation: / trænˈzækʃənəli / (AmE), / trænˈzækʃənəli / (BrE)
in a manner that relates to or involves transactions, especially in business or computing contexts

Examples

Transactionally process payments
The system processes payments transactionally to prevent errors and ensure accuracy.
Data is updated transactionally so that all changes are completed together or not at all.
In modern banking, accounts are managed transactionally to maintain security and consistency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: commercially - in a way that relates to commerce or business
