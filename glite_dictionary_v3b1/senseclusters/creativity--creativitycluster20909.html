creativity

noun
ability to create or invent
Pronunciation: / ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvəti /; / cckrid0e6ac8t6av59ti / (AmE), / ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvɪti /; / cckrid0e6ac8t6av6ati / (BrE)
the ability to use imagination or original ideas to create something; inventiveness (in people or computers/AI)

Examples
