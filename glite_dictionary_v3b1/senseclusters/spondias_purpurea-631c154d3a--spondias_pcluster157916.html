Spondias purpurea

noun
tropical American shrub with purplish fruit
Pronunciation: / spɑnˈdaɪ.əs pɜːrˈpjʊr.i.ə / (AmE), / spɒnˈdɪ.əs pəːˈpjʊə.ri.ə / (BrE)
a tropical American shrub or small tree with purplish, edible fruit, also known as the jocote or red mombin

Examples

Spondias purpurea fruit
Farmers in Central America cultivate Spondias purpurea for its flavorful, juicy berries.
Some markets sell Spondias purpurea under the name 'jocote' during the summer season.
The fruit of the Spondias purpurea is often eaten fresh or used in jams and drinks.
