norm

noun
a typical standard or pattern
3.7 25/m
Pronunciation: / n…îrm / (AmE), / n…îÀêm / (BrE)
a standard or pattern of behavior, achievement, or belief that is considered typical or expected within a particular group or society

Examples

Social norm
Challenging the social norm can lead to innovation and change.
In that culture, it is the norm to remove your shoes before entering a home.
Wearing jeans to work has become the norm in many offices today.

Synonyms

    2.7 71/m

  • convention

  • custom
    1.3 16/m
    custom is a long-held traditional way people do things in a place or time, while norm is the general expected behavior.
