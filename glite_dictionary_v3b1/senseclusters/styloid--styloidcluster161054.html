styloid

noun
pointed bone projection
Pronunciation: / ˈstaɪlɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈstaɪlɔɪd / (BrE)
a slender, pointed projection or process on a bone, often referred to as a styloid process in anatomy

Examples

Wrist styloid
The doctor explained that the styloid was fractured during the fall.
Pain near the wrist can sometimes be traced to the styloid process.
X-rays revealed a small break at the base of the styloid.

Related Terms

  • synonym: process - a projection or outgrowth on a bone
