Tarquin

proper noun
legendary last king of Rome
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtɑr.kwɪn / (AmE), / ˈtɑː.kwɪn / (BrE)
according to legend, the seventh and last Etruscan king of Rome, who reigned from 534 to 510 BC and was expelled for his cruelty

Examples

Tarquin the Proud
Tarquin was said to have earned his people's hatred through acts of cruelty and oppression.
