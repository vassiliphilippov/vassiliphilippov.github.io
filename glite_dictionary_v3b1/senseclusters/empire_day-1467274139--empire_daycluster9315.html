Empire Day

(historical, British) An annual observance on 24 May, commemorating the birthday of Queen Victoria and celebrating the British Empire.

Examples

Empire Day celebration
In the early 20th century, schools across Britain celebrated Empire Day with patriotic songs and parades.
Many people today have never heard of Empire Day, but it was once a significant occasion in the British calendar.
The origins of Empire Day can be traced back to 1902, marking Queen Victoria's birthday.
