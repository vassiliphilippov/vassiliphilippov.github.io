Examples

Happened afterward
She felt a great sense of relief afterward.
There was a celebration at the park afterward.

Synonyms

  • subsequently
    4.8 16/m
    subsequently appears in formal writing and reports to show sequence clearly, while afterward is more common in speech.
  • thereafter
    4.1 11/m
    thereafter often describes what continues from a past time, while afterward usually points to a single later moment.
  • later
    1.0 0.00/m
    later is a common, flexible word for a time after now, while afterward sounds slightly more formal and definite.
  • later on
    1.0 13/m
    later on is casual and people use it in speech to mean sometime after, while afterward is neutral and fits both speech and writing.
  • after
    1.0 22/m
    after usually serves as a preposition needing an object or clause, while afterward is a standalone adverb meaning later.
