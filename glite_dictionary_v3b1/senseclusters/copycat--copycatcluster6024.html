copycat

noun
a person who imitates others
3.3 2.17/m

a person who imitates or copies the words or behavior of another, often without permission or originality

Examples

A copycat in class
Children often call each other a copycat when they mimic each other in games.
He's such a copycat; he bought the same jacket as me right after I did.
The new phone's design was criticized for being a blatant copycat of its competitor.
