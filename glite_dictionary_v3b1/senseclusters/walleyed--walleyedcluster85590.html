walleyed

adjective
having eyes that turn outward
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːl.aɪd / (AmE), / ˈwɒl.aɪd / (BrE)
having eyes that turn outward, especially both eyes looking in different directions (divergent strabismus)

Examples

Walleyed eyes
She was born walleyed, but surgery corrected her vision.
The actor’s nearly walleyed stare added to the character’s eccentricity.
The old dog became walleyed as his eyesight faded.

Related Terms

  • antonym: cross-eyed - having eyes that turn inward toward the nose (convergent strabismus)
