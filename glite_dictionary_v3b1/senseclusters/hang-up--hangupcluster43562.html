hang-up

noun
emotional or psychological inhibition
5.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhæŋ.ʌp / (AmE), / ˈhæŋ.ʌp / (BrE)
an emotional problem or inhibition that makes it difficult for someone to act normally; a psychological obstacle

Examples

A social hang-up
Don't let your old hang-ups get in the way of trying something new.
His hang-up over his appearance stopped him from joining the swimming club.
She has a hang-up about speaking in public and avoids giving presentations whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • synonym: complex - an emotional problem or obsession influencing behavior
  • antonym: confidence - a feeling of self-assurance, especially from appreciation of one's own abilities
  • synonym: inhibition - a feeling that makes one self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed way
