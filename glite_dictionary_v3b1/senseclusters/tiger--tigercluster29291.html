tiger

noun
large striped Asian wild cat
Pronunciation: / ˈtaɪɡɚ / (AmE), / ˈtaɪɡə / (BrE)
a large wild cat found in the forests of Asia, recognized by its orange-brown coat with black stripes; now considered endangered

Examples

A wild tiger
At the zoo, the children were fascinated by the sight of a sleeping tiger.
Conservationists are working hard to protect the remaining wild tigers from extinction.
The tiger moved silently through the dense jungle, its stripes blending with the shadows.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leopard - spotted large wild cat native to Africa and Asia
  • synonym: lion - large tawny wild cat of Africa and India
