meta

noun
dominant strategies
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛtə / (AmE), / ˈmɛtə / (BrE)
the set of strategies, character choices, or tactics currently considered most effective in a competitive game or environment

Examples

Understanding the current meta is essential for players who want to compete at a high level.
Many gamers discuss how the meta changes with each new patch or balance adjustment.
