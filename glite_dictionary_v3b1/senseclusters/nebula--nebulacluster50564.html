nebula

noun
medicinal solution for spraying
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛbjələ / (AmE), / ˈnɛbjʊlə / (BrE)
A medicinal liquid preparation designed to be administered as a fine spray, typically using an atomizer.

Examples
