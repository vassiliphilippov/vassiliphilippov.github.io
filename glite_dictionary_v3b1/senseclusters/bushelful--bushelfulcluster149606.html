bushelful

noun
amount filling a bushel
Pronunciation: / ˈbʊʃəlfʊl / (AmE), / ˈbʊʃəlfʊl / (BrE)
the amount that fills a bushel, a unit of dry measure equal to about 35 liters or 8 gallons

Examples

A bushelful of apples
They picked a bushelful of apples from the orchard before noon.
The farmer sold a bushelful of corn at the market every week.
We gathered nearly a bushelful of tomatoes from the garden this summer.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: barrelful - the amount that fills a barrel
  • synonym: bushel - a unit of dry measure equal to about 35 liters or 8 gallons
  • degree lower: handful - the amount that can be held in one hand
