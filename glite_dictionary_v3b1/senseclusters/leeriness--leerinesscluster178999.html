leeriness

noun
suspicious caution
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪrɪnəs / (AmE), / ˈlɪərɪnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being cautious or suspicious, especially about someone or something that may be dangerous or untrustworthy

Examples

Her leeriness of strangers
Her leeriness of unfamiliar foods made her hesitant to try new dishes at the restaurant.
There was a sense of leeriness among the neighbors after several break-ins occurred in the area.
His leeriness toward online deals kept him from making impulsive purchases.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suspicion - a feeling that someone or something is possibly dangerous or dishonest
  • antonym: trust - belief that someone or something is reliable or honest
  • synonym: wariness - cautiousness or carefulness about possible danger or trouble
