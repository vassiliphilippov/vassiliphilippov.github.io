cream violet

noun
North American violet with creamy flowers
7.0
Pronunciation: / kɹim ˈvaɪ.ə.lət / (AmE), / kɹiːm ˈvaɪ.əlɪt / (BrE)
A leafy-stemmed violet native to eastern North America, with large white or creamy flowers faintly marked with purple.

Examples

A cream violet flower
She pressed a cream violet between the pages of her notebook to preserve its delicate beauty.
The garden featured a rare cream violet among the more common purple varieties.
Wild cream violets bloom in shaded woodlands each spring.
