thug

noun
violent criminal or troublemaker
3.9
Pronunciation: / θʌɡ / (AmE), / θʌɡ / (BrE)
a violent person, especially a criminal or troublemaker who attacks or threatens others

Examples

A street thug
A group of thugs vandalized several shops during the night.
He acted like a thug, threatening anyone who stood in his way.
The police arrested the thug who was causing trouble in the neighborhood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bully - a person who uses strength or power to harm or intimidate those who are weaker
  • synonym: criminal - a person who commits a crime
