corn spurrey

noun
a small European weed with white flowers
5.7
Pronunciation: / kɔrn ˈspɜri / (AmE), / kɔːn ˈspɜːri / (BrE)
a small European annual weed (Spergula arvensis) with whorled leaves and small white flowers, often found in fields and cultivated ground

Examples

Corn spurrey plant
Corn spurrey is recognized by its slender whorled leaves and star-shaped white flowers.

Weeds like corn spurrey can be difficult to control in agricultural fields.
