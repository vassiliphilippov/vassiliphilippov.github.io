turbinectomy

noun
nasal surgery
Pronunciation: / ˌtɜrbɪˈnɛktəmi / (AmE), / ˌtɜːbɪˈnɛktəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure to remove or reduce the size of the nasal turbinates to improve airflow through the nose

Examples

A turbinectomy surgery
The doctor recommended a turbinectomy to help the patient breathe more easily through his nose.
After undergoing turbinectomy, she noticed a significant improvement in her nasal airflow.
A turbinectomy may be performed when medication does not relieve chronic nasal congestion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: adenoidectomy - surgical removal of the adenoids
  • synonym: septoplasty - surgical correction of a deviated nasal septum
