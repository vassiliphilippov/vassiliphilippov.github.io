undammed

adjective
not blocked by dam
5.5
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈdæmd / (AmE), / ʌnˈdæmd / (BrE)
not blocked, controlled, or held back by a dam

Examples

Undammed river
The undammed river supported a wide variety of fish and plant life.
After the old structure was removed, the stream became undammed for the first time in decades.
Many conservationists advocate for keeping waterways undammed to protect natural habitats.

Related Terms

  • synonym: free-flowing - able to move or flow without obstruction
