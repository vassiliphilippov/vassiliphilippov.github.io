deadline

noun
latest time for completion
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (AmE), / ˈdɛdˌlaɪn / (BrE)
A point in time by which something must be finished or submitted.

Examples

Meet the deadline
Missing the deadline could result in a penalty.
She worked late to finish the report before the deadline.
The team raced against the deadline to complete the project on time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: due date - the expected date something is due
  • antonym: extension - a later time allowed beyond an original deadline
