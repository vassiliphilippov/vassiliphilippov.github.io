Morisco

noun
converted Spanish Muslim
Pronunciation: / mɔːˈriskoʊ /; / mɔˈriskoʊ / (AmE), / mɒˈrɪskəʊ / (BrE)
a member of a group of former Muslims in Spain who converted to Christianity after the Reconquista, especially in the 16th and 17th centuries
