ostomy

noun
surgical procedure creating a waste elimination opening
5.3 7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːstəmi / (AmE), / ˈɒstəmi / (BrE)
a surgical procedure in which an artificial opening is created in the body to allow for the elimination of bodily wastes

Examples

An ostomy surgery

Doctors discussed both the risks and benefits of performing an ostomy to treat her condition.
