unpragmatic

adjective
not practical
7.5
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnpræɡˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌʌnpræɡˈmætɪk / (BrE)
not dealing with things in a practical, sensible, or realistic way

Examples

An unpragmatic idea
Her unpragmatic ideas often led to unnecessary complications in the project.
It would be unpragmatic to ignore the budget when planning the event.
The manager criticized the proposal for being too unpragmatic and idealistic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: impractical - not sensible or realistic in approach
  • antonym: pragmatic - dealing with things sensibly and realistically
