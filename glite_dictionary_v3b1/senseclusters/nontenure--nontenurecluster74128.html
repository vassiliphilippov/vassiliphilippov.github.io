nontenure

noun
lack of tenure
7.8
Pronunciation: /nən.ˈten.jər/ /; / /nɑːn.ˈten.jər/ (AmE), /nən.ˈten.jə/ /; / /nɒn.ˈten.jə/ (BrE)
the state or condition of not having permanent academic tenure, often referring to temporary or insecure academic positions

Examples

Nontenure position
Many university instructors work in nontenure positions with contracts renewed each year.
She accepted a nontenure track job while searching for a permanent academic role.
Concerns about job security are common among nontenure faculty members.
