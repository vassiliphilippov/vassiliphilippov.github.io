hood ornament

noun
decorative car emblem on hood
Pronunciation: / hʊd ˈɔrnəmənt / (AmE), / hʊd ˈɔːnəmənt / (BrE)
a decorative emblem mounted on the front of a car's hood, typically symbolizing the vehicle's manufacturer

Examples

A hood ornament on car
Many vintage cars feature a unique hood ornament that reflects the brand's history.
She polished the hood ornament to make sure it shone for the car show.
The classic Rolls-Royce is known for its distinctive hood ornament called the Spirit of Ecstasy.
