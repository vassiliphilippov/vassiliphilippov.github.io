markdown

noun
price reduction
3.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑrkˌdaʊn / (AmE), / ˈmɑːkˌdaʊn / (BrE)
a reduction in the price of a product or service, usually to encourage sales

Examples

A big markdown sale
The electronics store announced major markdowns on televisions for the weekend.
After the holidays, many shops offer markdowns to clear out old inventory.
You can find great deals during seasonal markdowns at your favorite clothing stores.
The store announced a major markdown on all summer clothing to clear out inventory.
After the holidays, many electronics go on markdown to attract more customers.
She waited for a markdown before buying the expensive jacket she wanted.

Related Terms

  • synonym: discount - a reduction in the usual price of something
  • antonym: markup - an increase in the price of something above its cost
