weakly interacting massive particle

noun
hypothetical particle; candidate for dark matter
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwik.li ɪn.tɚˈækt.ɪŋ ˈmæs.ɪv ˈpɑr.tɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈwiːk.li ɪn.təˈræk.tɪŋ ˈmæs.ɪv ˈpɑː.tɪ.kəl / (BrE)
