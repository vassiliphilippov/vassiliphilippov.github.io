fishnet

noun
net for catching fish
Pronunciation: / ˈfɪʃˌnɛt / (AmE), / ˈfɪʃˌnɛt / (BrE)
A net made of thin threads, used for catching fish.

Examples

A fishnet for fishing
A large fishnet was cast over the side of the boat to catch mackerel.
He hauled the heavy fishnet filled with shimmering, silver trout onto the shore.
The fishermen repaired the torn fishnet before heading out to sea.

Related Terms

  • synonym: net - a piece of mesh fabric used for catching animals or objects
