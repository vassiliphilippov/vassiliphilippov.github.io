mannerism

noun
distinctive personal habit or behavior
6.7 4.3
Pronunciation: /ˈmæn.ə.rɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /ˈmæn.ə.rɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
a habitual or characteristic way of behaving, speaking, or moving that is distinctive to an individual

Examples

A nervous mannerism
His unique mannerism made him instantly recognizable to his fans.
People often imitate the mannerisms of their favorite celebrities.
She had a nervous mannerism of tapping her fingers on the table.

  • synonym: idiosyncrasy - a characteristic, habit, or mannerism that is peculiar to an individual
