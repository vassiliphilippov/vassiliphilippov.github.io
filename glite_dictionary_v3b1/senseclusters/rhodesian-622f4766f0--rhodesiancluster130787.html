Rhodesian

noun
person from Rhodesia
8.0
Pronunciation: / roʊˈdiːʒən / (AmE), / rəʊˈdiːʒən / (BrE)
a person from Rhodesia, a former country in southern Africa now known as Zimbabwe and Zambia

Examples

A Rhodesian person
Her grandfather was a proud Rhodesian who lived through the country's transition to independence.
Many Rhodesians emigrated to other countries after the political changes in the 1980s.
The museum displayed artifacts from the era when Rhodesians governed the region.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Zambian - a person from Zambia
  • synonym: Zimbabwean - a person from Zimbabwe
