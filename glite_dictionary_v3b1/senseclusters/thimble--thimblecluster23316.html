thimble

verb
to insert metal thimble
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈθɪmbəl / (AmE), / ˈθɪmbəl / (BrE)
to insert or secure a metal thimble into the eye of a rope or cable to protect it from wear

Examples

To thimble a cable
The workers thimbled the ends of the cables before attaching them to the anchor points.
It is important to thimble the rope eye to prevent fraying under heavy loads.
After they thimbled the wire, the connection was much more secure.

Related Terms

  • synonym: reinforce - to make something stronger or more durable
