flummox

verb
to bewilder or confuse utterly
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈflʌməks / (AmE), / ˈflʌməkʃ /; / ˈflʌməks / (BrE)
to confuse or bewilder someone completely, making them unsure what to do or think

Examples

To flummox someone
He was flummoxed by the unexpected question during the meeting.
Her answer seemed to flummox the interviewer for a moment.
The complex instructions completely flummoxed the new employees.
The complicated instructions flummoxed everyone in the class.
He was flummoxed by the sudden change in the schedule.
Her unexpected question flummoxed the panel and stopped the discussion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bewilder - to cause someone to become perplexed and confused
  • antonym: clarify - to make something easier to understand
  • synonym: confuse - to make someone unable to think clearly or understand
  • synonym: perplex - to make someone feel completely baffled
  • synonym: puzzle - to confuse or baffle someone mentally
