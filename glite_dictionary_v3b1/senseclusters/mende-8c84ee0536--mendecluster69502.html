Mende

proper noun
West African people or language
9.1
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛndeɪ / (AmE), / ˈmɛndeɪ / (BrE)
a member of a West African ethnic group mainly found in Sierra Leone and Liberia, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

Mende people
The Mende have a long history of storytelling and music in West Africa.
She is learning to speak Mende to communicate with her grandmother.
Many Mende people live in the southern and eastern regions of Sierra Leone.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Krio - a language widely spoken in Sierra Leone
  • synonym: Temne - another major ethnic group in Sierra Leone
