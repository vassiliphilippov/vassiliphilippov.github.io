melodious

adjective
having a pleasant, musical sound
Pronunciation: / məˈloʊdiəs / (AmE), / mɪˈləʊdiəs / (BrE)
having a pleasant, musical, or tuneful sound

Examples

A melodious sound
A melodious breeze rustled gently through the trees on that quiet evening.
He played a melodious tune on the piano that captivated the audience.
The melodious sound of her singing filled the entire room with warmth.
His voice was so melodious that everyone stopped to listen.
She woke to the melodious singing of birds outside her window.
The melodious sound of the violin filled the concert hall.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harmonious - having components that are musically pleasing together
  • synonym: tuneful - having a pleasant or melodious sound
