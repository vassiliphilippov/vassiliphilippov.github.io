hearty

adjective
providing abundant nourishment (of food)
2.7 1.70/m
Pronunciation: / Ààh…ërti / (AmE), / Ààh…ëÀêti / (BrE)
(of food) providing abundant nourishment; satisfying and filling.

Examples

Hearty meal
After hours of hard work, the farmers gathered for a hearty meal together.
The inn serves hearty soups that are perfect for a cold winter's day.
We enjoyed a hearty breakfast before setting out on our hike.

Synonyms

  • square
    2.3 0.57/m
    a square meal fills you and fully satisfies hunger, while hearty describes food that is nourishing and very filling.
  • substantial
    5.0 38/m

  • solid
    3.0 33/m
    solid describes something strong, reliable, or of good quality, while hearty refers to food that is filling and nourishing.
