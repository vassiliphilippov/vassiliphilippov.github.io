haircutter

noun
person who cuts hair
1.8
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛrˌkʌtər / (AmE), / ˈheəˌkʌtə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to cut hair professionally

Examples

A skilled haircutter
She made an appointment with her regular haircutter before the wedding.
The haircutter recommended a new style that suited his face shape.
After moving to a new city, he had to find a new haircutter he could trust.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barber - a person whose job is to cut men's hair
  • synonym: hairdresser - a person who cuts and styles hair
