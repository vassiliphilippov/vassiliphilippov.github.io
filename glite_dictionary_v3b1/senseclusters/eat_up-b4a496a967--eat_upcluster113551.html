eat up

verb
finish all the food
1.0 1.0
Pronunciation: /iːt ʌp/ /; / /iːt əp/ (AmE), /iːt ʌp/ /; / /iːt əp/ (BrE)
to eat all the food that is served or on your plate; to finish eating everything
