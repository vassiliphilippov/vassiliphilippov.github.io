precharged

adjective
filled in advance
Pronunciation: / ˌpriːˈtʃɑrdʒd / (AmE), / ˌpriːˈtʃɑːdʒd / (BrE)
filled or supplied with a charge or pressure before being used

Examples

A precharged air tank
The technician installed a precharged air tank to save time during repairs.
Many modern devices come with precharged batteries for immediate use out of the box.
Using a precharged extinguisher ensures it is ready in case of emergency.

  • antonym: empty - not filled with a charge or pressure
