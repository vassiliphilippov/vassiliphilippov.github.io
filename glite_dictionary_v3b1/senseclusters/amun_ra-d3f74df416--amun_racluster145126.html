Amun Ra

proper noun
ancient Egyptian sun god
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːmən ˈrɑː / (AmE), / ˌɑːmən ˈrɑː / (BrE)
the Egyptian sun god, regarded as the supreme deity of the universe, formed by merging Amen and Ra, especially prominent during Theban supremacy

Examples

Amun Ra god
During a period of Egyptian history, Amun Ra was worshipped as the king of the gods.
Many ancient temples were dedicated to Amun Ra in Thebes, reflecting his importance in Egyptian religion.
Priests at Karnak conducted elaborate ceremonies in honor of Amun Ra.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Amen - Egyptian creator god, merged with Ra to form Amun Ra
  • synonym: Ra - Egyptian sun god, associated with midday sun
