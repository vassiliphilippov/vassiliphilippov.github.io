très chic

adjective
very stylish or fashionable
Pronunciation: / tɹeɪ ʃiːk / (AmE), / tʁɛ ʃiːk / (BrE)
Very stylish or fashionable; elegant and sophisticated.

Examples

Tres chic style
His apartment, decorated in a très chic style, impressed all his guests.
She wore a très chic black dress to the award show.
The new restaurant has a très chic atmosphere with its minimalist design.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fashionable - following the current fashion; stylish
  • synonym: stylish - fashionably elegant; having style
  • antonym: unfashionable - not in accordance with current fashion
