sign off

verb
to approve formally
Pronunciation: / saɪn ˈɔf / (AmE), / saɪn ˈɒf / (BrE)
to give formal approval to something, often by signing a document or authorizing it officially

Examples

Sign off a form
The manager must sign off on all expense reports before they are processed.
Before the project can begin, the city council needs to sign off on the final plans.
She refused to sign off on the contract until all her concerns were addressed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: approve - to officially accept or agree to something
  • synonym: authorize - to give official permission for something
  • antonym: reject - to refuse to accept or approve something
