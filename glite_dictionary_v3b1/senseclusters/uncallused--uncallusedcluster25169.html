uncallused

adjective
emotionally sensitive
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈkæl.əst / (AmE), / ʌnˈkæl.juːst / (BrE)
not emotionally hardened or insensitive; easily affected or hurt, lacking emotional toughness.

Examples

Uncallused feelings
Her uncallused nature made her deeply affected by every criticism she received.
The teacher recognized his uncallused response to the harsh feedback and offered encouragement.
Growing up in a supportive family, he remained uncallused despite facing many challenges.

Related Terms

  • antonym: callous - emotionally hardened or insensitive
  • synonym: sensitive - easily affected by the feelings or actions of others
