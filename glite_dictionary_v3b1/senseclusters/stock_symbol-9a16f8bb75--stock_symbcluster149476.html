stock symbol

noun
identifier for public companies on stock exchanges
3.2
Pronunciation: / stɑk ˈsɪmbəl / (AmE), / stɒk ˈsɪmbəl / (BrE)
A unique set of letters used to identify a publicly traded company on a stock exchange.

Examples

Stock symbol on exchange
Apple's stock symbol is AAPL on the NASDAQ.
Make sure you enter the correct stock symbol when placing a trade.
You can find a company's stock symbol by searching on the stock exchange website.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticker symbol - set of characters representing a security listed on an exchange
