untaxable

adjective
not subject to tax
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtæksəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtæksəbəl / (BrE)
not required to pay tax or not subject to taxation by law

Examples

Untaxable income
The scholarship funds were considered untaxable by the government.
Some types of compensation are untaxable under current tax laws.
He was relieved to learn that the inheritance was untaxable income.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax-free - not subject to tax
  • antonym: taxable - required to pay tax
