untraceable

adjective
impossible to trace or track
3.6
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtreɪsəbəl / (AmE), / ʌnˈtreɪsəbəl / (BrE)
Unable to be found, tracked, or identified by searching or investigation.

Examples
