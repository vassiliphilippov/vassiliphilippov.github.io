coalman

noun
man who delivers coal
5.2 7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊl.mæn / (AmE), / ˈkəʊl.mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to deliver coal to homes or businesses, especially in the past

Examples

The coalman delivers coal

The coalman delivered a heavy sack of coal to our house every Monday morning.
The children waved to the coalman as he drove his cart down the snowy street.
