u-turn

noun
complete reversal of direction
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː tɝːn / (AmE), / ˈjuː tɜːn / (BrE)
an act of turning completely around in the opposite direction, especially in a vehicle; a complete reversal of position or course

Examples

Make a u-turn
After several months, the company executed a u-turn in its policy regarding remote work.
Making a u-turn on this road is illegal and could result in a fine.
The driver made a quick u-turn at the intersection when he realized he was going the wrong way.

Related Terms

  • synonym: about-face - sudden change of opinion or direction
