shopkeep

noun
small shop owner
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɑpˌkiːp / (AmE), / ˈʃɒpˌkiːp / (BrE)
a person who works in or runs a small shop, such as an owner, manager, or shop worker

Examples

The shopkeep arranges goods
The shopkeep arranged the new products neatly on the shelves every morning.
After years in the city, he became a beloved shopkeep in the small town.
Customers appreciated the shopkeep for remembering their favorite items and offering friendly advice.
The friendly shopkeep greeted every customer with a smile as they entered the store.
After school, she worked as a shopkeep in the local bookstore to earn extra money.
The shopkeep quickly found the item I was looking for and rang it up at the counter.

Related Terms

  • degree lower: clerk - a person who works in a shop assisting customers
  • degree higher: manager - a person responsible for controlling or administering a business
  • synonym: shop assistant - a person who serves customers in a shop
  • synonym: shopkeeper - a person who owns or manages a shop
