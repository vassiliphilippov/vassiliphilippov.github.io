custody battle

noun
legal dispute over child custody
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈkʌstədi ˈbætəl / (AmE), / ˈkʌstədi ˈbætl̩ / (BrE)
A legal dispute between parents to determine who will have custody of their children after separation or divorce.

Examples

A custody battle case
After months of negotiation, the custody battle finally ended in court.
Emotions often run high during a custody battle involving young children.
The couple went through a lengthy custody battle after their divorce.
