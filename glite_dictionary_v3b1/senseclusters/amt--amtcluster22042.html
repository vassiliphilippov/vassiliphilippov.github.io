amt

noun
US minimum tax system
Pronunciation: / eɪ ɛm ti / (AmE), / eɪ ɛm ti / (BrE)
an abbreviation for the Alternate Minimum Tax, a US tax system ensuring certain taxpayers pay a minimum amount of tax

Examples

Pay the amt tax
High-income individuals may have to pay the amt in addition to regular income tax.
Tax software can help you determine if the amt applies to your situation.
The accountant explained how the amt could affect our total tax bill this year.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tax - a compulsory financial charge imposed by a government
