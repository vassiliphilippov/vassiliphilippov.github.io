killifish

noun
small fish kept as aquarium pets or bait
Pronunciation: / ˈkɪliˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / ˈkɪlɪˌfɪʃ / (BrE)
a small, mostly marine or freshwater fish of the family Cyprinodontidae, often kept in aquariums or used for bait and mosquito control

Examples

A killifish species
Killifish are often introduced to ponds to help control mosquito populations.
Fishermen sometimes use killifish as bait when catching larger species.
The aquarium enthusiast added a brightly colored killifish to his tank.
