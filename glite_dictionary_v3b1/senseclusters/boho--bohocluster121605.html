boho

noun
bohemian-style item
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˈboʊhoʊ / (AmE), / ˈbəʊhəʊ / (BrE)
a clothing item or accessory designed in the bohemian style, often featuring flowing shapes, ethnic patterns, or vintage details

Examples

A boho dress
She wore a colorful boho dress to the summer music festival.
The shop sells handmade boho jewelry with beads and feathers.
His living room is decorated with boho cushions and patterned rugs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bohemian - a person or style associated with artistic, unconventional living
  • synonym: vintage - clothing or items from a previous era, often considered stylish
