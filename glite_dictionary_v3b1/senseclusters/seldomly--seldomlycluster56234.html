seldomly

adverb
infrequently
Pronunciation: / ˈsɛldəmli / (AmE), / ˈsɛldəmli / (BrE)
in a rare or infrequent manner; not often

Examples

He seldomly visits
He seldomly visits his hometown these days.
The old machine seldomly works without making strange noises.
She seldomly eats dessert after dinner, preferring fruit instead.

Related Terms

  • antonym: frequently - happening often or regularly
  • synonym: rarely - not often; on few occasions
