altercation

noun
noisy argument or quarrel
6.5
Pronunciation: / ˌɔltərˈkeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌɔːltəˈkeɪʃən / (BrE)
A noisy or heated argument, especially in public.

Examples

A altercation between drivers
An altercation broke out between the two drivers after the accident.
Police were called to the bar following a loud altercation among several patrons.
The heated altercation in the hallway drew everyone's attention.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - harmony or accordance in opinion or feeling
  • synonym: argument - an exchange of diverging or opposite views, typically a heated one
  • synonym: dispute - a disagreement, argument, or debate
