ory

x
noun/adjective suffix
Pronunciation: / ɔːri / (AmE), / ˈɔːri / (BrE)
a suffix used to form nouns or adjectives, often indicating a place or relating to something

Examples

Suffix ory in words
The word 'laboratory' contains the suffix ory, indicating a place for scientific work.
Adding ory to 'compuls' forms 'compulsory', turning it into an adjective.
Many English words, such as 'factory' and 'dormitory', end with the suffix ory.
