stormproof

adjective
resistant to storms
Pronunciation: / ˈstɔrmˌpruf / (AmE), / ˈstɔːmˌpruːf / (BrE)
protected against or able to withstand the effects of storms

Examples

Stormproof windows
All the windows in the house are stormproof to protect against severe weather.
The company offers stormproof doors and shutters for coastal homes.
They installed a stormproof roof before hurricane season began.

Related Terms

  • antonym: storm-battered - damaged or beaten by storms
  • synonym: waterproof - not allowing water to pass through; protected against water
