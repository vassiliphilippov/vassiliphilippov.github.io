dustcloth

noun
cloth for dusting surfaces
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌstk.lɔθ / (AmE), / ˈdʌstk.lɒθ / (BrE)
A piece of cloth used for removing dust from surfaces.

Examples

A dustcloth for cleaning
After spilling flour on the counter, he used a dustcloth to clean it up.
Every Saturday morning, Maria dusted the furniture with a soft dustcloth.
She grabbed a dustcloth to wipe down the bookshelves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: duster - a cloth or tool for removing dust
  • synonym: rag - a worthless piece of cloth, often used for cleaning
