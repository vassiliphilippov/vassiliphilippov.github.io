Romanize

verb
write using the Latin alphabet
2.3 5.5
Pronunciation: /ˈroʊ.mə.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈrəʊ.mə.naɪz/ (BrE)
To write words, especially from another script, using the Latin (Roman) alphabet.
