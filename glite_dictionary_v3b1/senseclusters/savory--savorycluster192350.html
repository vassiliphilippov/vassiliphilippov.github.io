savory

adjective
morally acceptable, respectable
5.0
Pronunciation: / ˈseɪvəri / (AmE), / ˈseɪvəri / (BrE)
morally respectable or acceptable; having a good reputation.

Examples

Savory reputation
Despite rumors, his background remained entirely savory and free of scandal.
She preferred companies with a more savory history when considering a new job.
The politician did his best to keep his dealings savory and above reproach.
He has a savoury reputation in both business and public life.
Some feared his associates were not entirely savoury characters.
The committee found her background to be wholly savoury.
