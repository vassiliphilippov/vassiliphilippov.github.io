buttonweed

noun
creeping perennial weed
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌtnˌwid / (AmE), / ˈbʌtnˌwiːd / (BrE)
a weedy plant with small, round, button-like flowers, often forming mats; can refer to several species, especially Diodia virginiana or plants of the genus Spermacoce
