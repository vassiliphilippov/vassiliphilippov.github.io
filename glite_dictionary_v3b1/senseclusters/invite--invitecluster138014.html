invite

noun
informal invitation
Pronunciation: / ɪnˈvaɪt / (AmE), / ɪnˈvaɪt / (BrE)
an informal invitation to an event or gathering

Examples

A party invite
Did you send out the invites for your wedding yet?
I never received my invite, so I missed the celebration.
She was thrilled to get an invite to the exclusive launch party.
