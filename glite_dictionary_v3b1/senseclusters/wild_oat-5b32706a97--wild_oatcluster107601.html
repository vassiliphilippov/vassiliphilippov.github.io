wild oat

noun
a grass species, often a weed
2.4
Pronunciation: / waɪld oʊt / (AmE), / waɪld əʊt / (BrE)
a grass of the genus Avena, especially Avena fatua, commonly found in meadows and pastures and often considered a weed

Examples

A patch of wild oat
During spring, patches of wild oat can easily be spotted in the meadows.
Many herbicides are used to control wild oat infestations in cereal crops.
The farmer was concerned about wild oat spreading throughout the field.

Related Terms

  • synonym: oat - a cereal plant cultivated for its seed, used as food and fodder
