trunk line

noun
main telephone line between exchanges
Pronunciation: / trʌŋk laɪn / (AmE), / trʌŋk laɪn / (BrE)
a main telephone line that connects two telephone exchanges or switching centers directly, used for routing multiple calls.

Examples

Telephone trunk line
A fault in the trunk line can disrupt service across large regions.
Engineers worked overnight to repair the damaged trunk line connecting the two exchanges.
The company upgraded its trunk line to handle increased call volume between cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: telephone line - a wire or cable that carries telephone signals
