undisplaced

adjective
not moved from position
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndɪsˈpleɪst / (AmE), / ˌʌndɪsˈpleɪst / (BrE)
not moved from its original position, especially referring to a bone or object that remains aligned after injury or impact

Examples

An undisplaced fracture
The X-ray confirmed that the fracture was undisplaced and did not require surgery.
An undisplaced bone break often heals faster than a displaced one.
The archaeologist was pleased to find the artifact undisplaced after centuries underground.

Related Terms

  • antonym: displaced - moved from its original position
  • synonym: unaltered - not changed or modified
