snuggled

adjective
pressed close for warmth or affection
Pronunciation: / ˈsnʌɡəld / (AmE), / ˈsnʌɡəld / (BrE)
drawn or pressed close to someone or something, typically for warmth, comfort, affection, or protection

Examples

Snuggled in arms
She felt safe as she lay snuggled in her grandmother's arms.
The cat was snuggled up on the sofa, purring contentedly.
The cottage was snuggled among the trees, hidden from view.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nestled - settled or pressed closely and comfortably; sheltered
