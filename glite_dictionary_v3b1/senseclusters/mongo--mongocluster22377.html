mongo

noun
monetary subunit of Mongolian tugrik
Pronunciation: / ˈmɑːnɡoʊ / (AmE), / ˈmɒŋɡəʊ / (BrE)
A monetary subunit of the tugrik, the currency of Mongolia, equal to one hundredth of a tugrik.

Examples

A mongo coin
A hundred mongo make up one tugrik in the Mongolian currency system.
I found an old mongo coin among my grandfather's collection of foreign money.
The price of a snack in Ulaanbaatar was just a few mongos back in the 1980s.
