winnower

noun
grain separator
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪnoʊər / (AmE), / ˈwɪnəʊə / (BrE)
a person or machine that separates grain from chaff by blowing air through it

Examples

Grain winnower
The winnower separated the wheat from the chaff quickly and efficiently.
Farmers used a traditional winnower to clean the grain after harvesting.
A modern winnower can process large amounts of grain in a short time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: harvester - a person or machine that gathers crops
  • synonym: thresher - a machine that separates grain from plants
