cannibalistically

adverb
in a cannibal-like way
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˌkænɪbəˈlɪstɪkli / (AmE), / ˌkænɪbəˈlɪstɪkli / (BrE)
in a manner that resembles or relates to cannibalism, especially by acting like a cannibal.

Examples

Act cannibalistically toward people
The villain in the story behaved cannibalistically when he threatened his captives.
Some animals may act cannibalistically under extreme conditions such as hunger.
The tribe was falsely accused of living cannibalistically by early explorers.
