ethically

adverb
in a morally correct manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɛθɪkli / (AmE), / ˈɛθɪkli / (BrE)
In a manner that is in accordance with principles of ethics or moral standards.

Examples

To act ethically
It is sometimes challenging to make decisions ethically in a competitive business environment.
She believes it is important to conduct research ethically at all times.
The company acted ethically by refusing to use misleading advertising.

Related Terms

  • synonym: morally - in a manner concerned with principles of right and wrong
  • antonym: unethically - in a manner not conforming to moral principles
