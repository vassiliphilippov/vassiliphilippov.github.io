shave

verb
touch lightly or graze while passing
6.0 3.1
Pronunciation: /ʃeɪv/ (AmE), /ʃeɪv/ (BrE)
to pass very close to and lightly touch the surface of something without significant contact

Examples

To shave the surface
Her new dress barely shaved the floor as she walked by.
The cyclist shaved the edge of the curb as he turned the corner.
The plane's wing shaved the treetops during its emergency landing.

Related Terms
