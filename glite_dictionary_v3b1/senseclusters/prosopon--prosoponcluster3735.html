prosopon

noun
theological person
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈprɑːsəˌpɒn / (AmE), / ˈprɒsəˌpɒn / (BrE)
a term in theology and philosophy referring to a person, face, or individual identity, especially in discussions of the Trinity.
