wood mouse

noun
a mouse living in wooded areas
Pronunciation: / wʊd maʊs / (AmE), / wʊd maʊs / (BrE)
a small mouse that inhabits wooded areas, especially species found in North and South America and Europe

Examples

The wood mouse in forest
The wood mouse is often found foraging among the leaf litter in forests.
Unlike the house mouse, the wood mouse prefers rural and woodland environments.
We spotted a wood mouse darting into a pile of old logs near the campsite.
