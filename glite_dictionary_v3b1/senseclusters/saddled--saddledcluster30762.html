saddled

adjective
burdened with responsibility or duty
Pronunciation: / ˈsædld / (AmE), / ˈsædld / (BrE)
Burdened or encumbered with an unwanted responsibility, duty, or problem.

Examples

Saddled with debt
After the sudden resignation of her boss, she was saddled with extra duties at work.
He found himself saddled with debt after the failed investment.
Many students feel saddled with loans after graduating from university.

Related Terms

  • synonym: burdened - carrying a heavy load or responsibility
  • antonym: unburdened - not burdened or encumbered; free from a load or responsibility
