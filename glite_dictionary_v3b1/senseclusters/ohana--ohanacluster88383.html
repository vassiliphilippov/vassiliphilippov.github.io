ohana

noun
Hawaiian extended family
9.9
Pronunciation: / oʊˈhɑːnə / (AmE), / əʊˈhɑːnə / (BrE)
a Hawaiian term for family, including close friends and relatives who are treated as family

Examples

Hawaiian ohana family
In Hawaiian culture, ohana means more than just your immediate relatives.
She invited her entire ohana to the wedding, including close friends.
He believes that everyone in his community is part of his ohana.

Related Terms

  • synonym: family - a group of people related by blood or close ties
  • degree lower: friend - a person with whom one has a close relationship
