starbase

noun
space station base
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈstɑːrˌbeɪs / (AmE), / ˈstɑːˌbeɪs / (BrE)
a base or station located in space, used especially for supporting spacecraft operations

Examples

A starbase station
The crew docked their ship at the starbase for repairs.
Scientists at the starbase monitored the progress of the approaching comet.
After months in deep space, the astronauts were relieved to arrive at the starbase.

Related Terms

  • synonym: space station - a large structure in space where people can live and work
  • synonym: spaceport - a base for launching or receiving spacecraft
