innovatively

adverb
with new ideas
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈɪnəˌveɪtɪvli / (AmE), / ˈɪnəvətɪvli / (BrE)
in a way that introduces new ideas, methods, or approaches

Examples

To act innovatively
The team approached the challenge innovatively, finding a solution no one had considered before.
She always thinks innovatively when designing new products for the company.
By working innovatively, the engineers reduced costs and improved efficiency.

Related Terms

  • synonym: creatively - in a way that uses imagination or original ideas
  • antonym: traditionally - in a way that follows established customs or practices
