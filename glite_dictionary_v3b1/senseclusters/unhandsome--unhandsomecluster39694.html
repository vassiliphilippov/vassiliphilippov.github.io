unhandsome

adjective
unkind or unfair
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈhænsəm / (AmE), / ʌnˈhansəm / (BrE)
unkind, ungenerous, or unfair in behavior or treatment toward others

Examples

Unhandsome behavior
It would be unhandsome of her to claim the prize without thanking her teammates.
His unhandsome remarks made the meeting uncomfortable for everyone.
The company acted in an unhandsome way by refusing to honor the agreement.

Related Terms

  • antonym: generous - willing to give or share freely
  • synonym: unfair - not just or equitable
  • synonym: unkind - not friendly or considerate
