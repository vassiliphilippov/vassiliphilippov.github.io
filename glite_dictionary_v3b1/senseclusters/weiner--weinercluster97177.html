weiner

noun
slang insult
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwiːnər / (AmE), / ˈwiːnə / (BrE)
an informal, insulting term for a person who is weak, cowardly, or ineffectual

Examples

Don't be a weiner
Stop being such a weiner and just ask her out already.
He was called a weiner for refusing to try the roller coaster.
Nobody wants to be seen as a weiner in front of their friends.

Related Terms

  • synonym: loser - a person who is unsuccessful or ineffectual
