thrash

verb
to dance the slam dance violently
Pronunciation: / θræʃ / (AmE), / θræʃ / (BrE)
(of a person) to dance energetically in a violent or frenzied manner, especially by performing the slam dance in punk or heavy metal music contexts.

Examples

Fans thrash in concert
Fans thrashed in the mosh pit, matching the music's intensity.
He loves to thrash at punk concerts with his friends.
The crowd began to thrash wildly as the band hit the chorus.

Related Terms

  • synonym: slam dance - a style of dancing involving aggressive body contact, popular at punk rock concerts
