untempting

adjective
not attractive or desirable
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtɛmptɪŋ /; / ʌnˈtɛmp.tɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtɛmptɪŋ /; / ʌnˈtɛmpt.ɪŋ / (BrE)
not attractive or appealing; not causing desire or temptation

Examples

The untempting leftovers remained untouched in the refrigerator for days.
