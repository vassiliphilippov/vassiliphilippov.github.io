cut

noun
share of profits or proceeds
1.0
Pronunciation: / kʌt / (AmE), / kʌt / (BrE)
a share or portion of profits or proceeds, especially from a business or enterprise

Examples

A cut of profits
After selling the company, each partner received a cut of the profits.
She only agreed to help if she was promised a cut of the earnings.
The agent takes a cut from every contract he negotiates.

Related Terms

  • synonym: percentage - a part of a whole, expressed as a percent, often used for profits
  • synonym: share - a portion of something that has been divided between several people
