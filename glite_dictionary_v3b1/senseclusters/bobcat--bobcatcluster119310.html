bobcat

noun
North American wild cat
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑbˌkæt / (AmE), / ˈbɒbˌkæt / (BrE)
a medium-sized wild cat native to North America, having a short tail, tufted ears, and spotted fur

Examples

A bobcat in forest
A bobcat was spotted near the hiking trail yesterday.
Many farmers install fences to keep bobcats away from their livestock.
The bobcat is known for its short "bobbed" tail and tufted ears.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lynx - a wild cat with tufted ears, found in northern latitudes
