syndromic

adjective
relating to a syndrome
6.9
Pronunciation: / sɪnˈdroʊmɪk / (AmE), / sɪnˈdrəʊmɪk / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of a syndrome or a group of symptoms, especially when identifying or managing disease based on patterns of signs and symptoms
