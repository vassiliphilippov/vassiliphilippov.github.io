wild mango tree

noun
African tree with edible mango-like fruit
1.7 5.9
Pronunciation: / waɪld ˈmæŋɡoʊ triː / (AmE), / waɪld ˈmaŋɡəʊ triː / (BrE)
An African tree bearing edible yellow fruit similar to mangos, valued for its oil-rich seeds and durable, termite-resistant wood.

Examples

wild mango tree wood
Furniture made from wild mango tree wood is resistant to termite damage.
The wild mango tree grows abundantly in several regions of West Africa.
Villagers often use the seeds from the wild mango tree to extract valuable oil.
