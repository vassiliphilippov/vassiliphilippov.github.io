academic

noun
school subject or scholar
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˌækəˈdɛmɪk / (AmE), / ˌækəˈdɛmɪk / (BrE)
a subject or area of study at a school, college, or university, or a person who studies or teaches at such an institution

Examples

Her academics improve
She excels in her academics and always gets top grades.
Many academics spend years researching their chosen fields at universities.
The school offers a wide range of academics for students to choose from.

Related Terms

  • antonym: practical - relating to real situations and actions rather than ideas or theories
  • synonym: scholar - a person who studies a subject in great detail, especially at a university
  • synonym: student - a person who is studying at a school or college
