spring frog

noun
North American marsh-dwelling frog
9.6
Pronunciation: / sprɪŋ frɔɡ /; / sprɪŋ frɑɡ / (AmE), / sprɪŋ frɒɡ / (BrE)
a common North American frog found in or near marshes and ponds, usually green or brownish with white-edged dark spots
