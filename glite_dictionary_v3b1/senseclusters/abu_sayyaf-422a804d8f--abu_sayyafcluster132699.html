Abu Sayyaf

proper noun
Islamist militant group in the Philippines
Pronunciation: / ˌɑːbu saɪˈjɑːf / (AmE), / ˌɑːbu saɪˈjæf / (BrE)
an Islamist militant group based in the southern Philippines, known for acts of terrorism including kidnapping, bombing, and extortion, seeking the establishment of an independent Islamic state.

Examples

Abu Sayyaf group
News reports detailed how Abu Sayyaf was responsible for bombings in the southern provinces.
Several countries have designated Abu Sayyaf as a terrorist organization due to its violent activities.
The government launched a military offensive against Abu Sayyaf after a series of kidnappings.
