eunuchoid

noun
person with eunuch-like features
Pronunciation: / ˈjuːnəˌkɔɪd / (AmE), / ˈjuːnəkɔɪd / (BrE)
a person who has physical characteristics similar to those of a eunuch, such as tall stature and long limbs, often due to hormonal imbalance

Examples

A eunuchoid patient
The doctor described the patient as a eunuchoid because of his tall, slender build and long arms.
Certain hormonal disorders can cause a person to develop eunuchoid features during adolescence.
A eunuchoid may require specialized medical care to address underlying endocrine issues.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eunuch - a man who has been castrated, especially one formerly employed to guard women's living areas
