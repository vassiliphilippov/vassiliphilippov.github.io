pinewood

noun
wood from pine trees
Pronunciation: / ˈpaɪnwʊd / (AmE), / ˈpaɪnwʊd / (BrE)
wood from pine trees, often used in construction or furniture making

Examples

Pinewood furniture
The furniture in the living room was made of solid pinewood.
They chose pinewood for the flooring because of its warm color and affordability.
A pleasant scent filled the workshop as he sanded the pinewood boards.
The cabin was built entirely from pinewood harvested in the local forest.
She prefers pinewood furniture because it is both affordable and attractive.
A pleasant scent filled the room as the fire burned pinewood logs.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hardwood - wood from broad-leaved trees, usually harder than softwood
  • synonym: softwood - wood from coniferous trees, generally softer than hardwood
