bubble dance

noun
solo dance using balloons for concealment
3.4
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌb.əl ˌdæns / (AmE), / ˈbʌb.əl ˌdɑːns / (BrE)
A solo dance performance in which large balloons are used to conceal and reveal the dancer's body, similar to a fan dance but with balloons instead of fans.

Examples

A bubble dance show
Fans eagerly awaited the famous bubble dance that was the highlight of the evening.
He wrote an article about the history of the bubble dance in burlesque shows.
The performer captivated the audience with her graceful bubble dance at the cabaret.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fan dance - a solo dance using large fans for concealment
