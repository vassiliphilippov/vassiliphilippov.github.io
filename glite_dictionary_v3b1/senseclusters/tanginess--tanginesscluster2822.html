tanginess

noun
sharp, tart flavor or quality
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋ.i.nəs / (AmE), / ˈtæŋ.i.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being tangy; a sharp, pleasantly tart, and often slightly spicy taste or smell

Examples

The tanginess of food
Her homemade pickles are loved for their crisp texture and subtle tanginess.
The salad dressing had a distinctive tanginess that woke up my taste buds.
You can add a bit of lemon juice to enhance the tanginess of the sauce.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acidity - the quality of being acid or sour
  • antonym: sweetness - the quality of being sweet in taste
