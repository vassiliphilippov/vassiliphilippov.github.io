coordinate

noun
a number locating a position on an axis
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˌkoʊˈɔrdn̩ɪt /; / ˈkoʊ.ɔːr.dn̩.ɪt / (AmE), / kəʊˈɔːdɪnət /; / kəʊˈɔː.dɪ.nət / (BrE)
each of a set of numbers that shows the position of a point or object, especially in mathematics or on a map

Examples

She recorded the GPS coordinate to mark the exact spot of the discovery.
To plot the line, you need to find the coordinate of each end point.
