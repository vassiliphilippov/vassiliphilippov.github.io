recreator

noun
person enjoying leisure
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛkriˌeɪtər / (AmE), / ˈrɛkriˌeɪtə / (BrE)
a person who takes part in recreational activities for enjoyment, relaxation, or health

Examples

Park recreator
The park was filled with recreators enjoying a sunny afternoon of games and picnics.
As a regular recreator, she values hiking and swimming for both fun and fitness.
Local recreators often organize group activities to promote health and social connection.
