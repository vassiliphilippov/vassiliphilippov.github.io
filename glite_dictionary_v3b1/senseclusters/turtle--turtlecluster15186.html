turtle

verb
to overturn completely (nautical)
9.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtɝːtəl /; / ˈtɜrtl̩ / (AmE), / ˈtɜːtəl /; / ˈtɜːtl̩ / (BrE)
to turn completely upside down or capsize, especially of a boat or vessel

Examples

A sudden wave caused the yacht to start turtling, and the crew scrambled to free themselves.
If the boat turtles, everyone must know how to react quickly and safely.
During the storm, several small sailboats turtled in the harbor.
