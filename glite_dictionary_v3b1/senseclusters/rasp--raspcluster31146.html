rasp

verb
speak in a harsh voice
Pronunciation: / ræsp / (AmE), / rɑːsp / (BrE)
to speak or say something in a harsh, grating voice

Examples

To rasp a warning
He managed to rasp a warning through his clenched teeth.
Her voice began to rasp after hours of shouting at the concert.
With a sore throat, he could only rasp out a few words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: growl - to utter a low, guttural sound, often in anger
  • antonym: whisper - to speak very softly using the breath
