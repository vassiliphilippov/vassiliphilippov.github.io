synergic

adjective
acting together
Pronunciation: / sɪˈnɜrdʒɪk / (AmE), / sɪˈnɜːdʒɪk / (BrE)
working together to produce a combined effect greater than the sum of individual effects

Examples

A synergic effect
The synergic relationship between the two teams led to faster project completion.
Researchers found that the chemicals had a synergic effect when used together.
A synergic approach can often solve complex problems more effectively than working alone.

Related Terms

  • antonym: antagonistic - acting in opposition or working against each other
  • synonym: synergistic - working together to produce a greater effect
