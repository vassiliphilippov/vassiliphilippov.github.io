cucumber

noun
edible green-skinned fruit used as a vegetable
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkjuːkʌmbɚ /; / ˈkjuː.kʌm.bɚ / (AmE), / ˈkjuːkʌmbə /; / ˈkjuː.kʌm.bə / (BrE)
a long, green fruit with crisp, watery flesh, usually eaten raw as a vegetable or pickled
