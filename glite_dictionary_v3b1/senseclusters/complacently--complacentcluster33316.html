complacently

adverb
in a self-satisfied manner
Pronunciation: / kəmˈpleɪsəntli / (AmE), / kəmˈpleɪsəntli / (BrE)
In a way that shows self-satisfaction, often without awareness of potential dangers or defects.

Examples

Smile complacently
He smiled complacently after hearing the praise, unaware of the underlying criticism.
She complacently assumed that everything would go according to plan, not preparing for any setbacks.
The company had complacently ignored early warnings about the risks, leading to bigger problems later.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smugly - in a self-satisfied, superior way
