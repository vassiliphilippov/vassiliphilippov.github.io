wave angle

noun

Examples

Ocean wave angle
Coastal engineers adjusted their models after measuring the wave angle of incoming swells.
A small change in wave angle can dramatically increase longshore sediment transport and beach erosion.
They used buoys and radar to record the wave angle approaching the headland during the storm.
