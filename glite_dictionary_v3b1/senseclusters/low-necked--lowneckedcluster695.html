low-necked

adjective
having a low-cut neckline (garment)
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊˌnɛkt / (AmE), / ˈləʊˌnɛkt / (BrE)
(of a garment) having a neckline that is cut low, exposing more of the neck and chest than usual.

Examples

A low-necked dress
For formal events, he advised avoiding clothing that was too low-necked.
She wore a low-necked dress to the party, which drew many compliments.
The actress was photographed in a stunning low-necked gown on the red carpet.
