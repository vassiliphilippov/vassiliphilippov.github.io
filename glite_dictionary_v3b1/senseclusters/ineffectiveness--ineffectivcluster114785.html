Synonyms

  • inefficacy
    8.7 0.08/m
    inefficacy stresses lack of power to produce results in practice, while ineffectiveness describes general inability to achieve a desired effect.
  • ineffectuality
    9.1 0.06/m
    ineffectuality shows a lack of power to work in a situation, while ineffectiveness names the general failure to produce results.
  • ineffectualness
    8.4 0.00/m
    ineffectualness sounds more formal or rare in use and focuses on lacking power, while ineffectiveness is the common term for failure to work.
  • futility
    7.0 3/m
    futility means something is pointless and cannot succeed, while ineffectiveness means it simply fails to produce the desired result.
