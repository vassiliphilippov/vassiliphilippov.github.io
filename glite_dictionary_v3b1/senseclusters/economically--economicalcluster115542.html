economically

adverb
in relation to economics or the economy
Pronunciation: / ˌikəˈnɑmɪkli /; / ˌɛkəˈnɑmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that relates to economics, the economy, or finances

Examples

Economically important country
