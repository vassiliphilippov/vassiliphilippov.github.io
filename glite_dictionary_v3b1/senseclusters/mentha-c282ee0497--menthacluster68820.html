Mentha

noun
genus of mint plants
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛnθə / (AmE), / ˈmɛnθə / (BrE)
A genus of aromatic plants in the mint family, including species such as spearmint and peppermint.

Examples

Mentha plants
Gardeners often plant different varieties of Mentha for their aromatic leaves.
Many culinary herbs, such as spearmint and peppermint, belong to the genus Mentha.
The essential oils derived from Mentha are widely used in medicines and confectionery.
