smackeroo

noun
playful slap
Pronunciation: / ˌsmækəˈruː / (AmE), / ˌsmækəˈruː / (BrE)
an informal term for a quick, sharp slap or light blow, often given playfully

Examples

A playful smackeroo
He gave his friend a playful smackeroo on the back after the joke.
The little girl giggled after receiving a gentle smackeroo from her mother.
During the game, someone got a quick smackeroo for not paying attention.

Related Terms

  • synonym: smack - a sharp slap or blow
  • degree lower: tap - a light touch or gentle hit
