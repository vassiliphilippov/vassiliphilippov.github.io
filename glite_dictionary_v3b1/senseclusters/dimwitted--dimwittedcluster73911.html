dimwitted

adjective
unintelligent; slow to understand
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪmˌwɪtɪd / (AmE), / ˈdɪmˌwɪtɪd / (BrE)
not intelligent or slow to understand things

Examples

A dimwitted person
He made a dimwitted comment that made everyone laugh.
The dimwitted character in the movie kept getting into trouble.
It was dimwitted of her to leave her keys in the car.

Related Terms

  • antonym: clever - quick to understand and learn things
  • synonym: dull - slow to understand or not clever
  • synonym: stupid - lacking intelligence or common sense
