gauzily

adverb
in a delicate manner
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔːzɪli / (AmE), / ˈɡɔːzɪli / (BrE)
in a way that is thin, light, or delicate like gauze

Examples

Mist hangs gauzily
The morning mist hung gauzily over the quiet fields.
She decorated the room gauzily with sheer white fabrics for the celebration.
The dancer moved gauzily across the stage, her dress floating behind her.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicately - in a gentle or light manner
  • antonym: heavily - in a dense or weighty manner
