almond-shaped

adjective
having the shape of an almond
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːlmənd ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈɑːmənd ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
having a shape resembling that of an almond; typically used to describe eyes that are oval with a slightly pointed end.

Examples

Almond-shaped eyes
He sketched a face with almond-shaped eyes and delicate features.
She admired the actress's almond-shaped eyes, which gave her an exotic look.
The leaves were almond-shaped, tapering gently at both ends.
