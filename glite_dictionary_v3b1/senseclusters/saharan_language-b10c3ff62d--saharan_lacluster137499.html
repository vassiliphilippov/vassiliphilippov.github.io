Saharan language

noun
a Nilo-Saharan language from Chad region
Pronunciation: / səˈhɑrən ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒ / (AmE), / səˈhɑːrən ˈlaŋɡwɪdʒ / (BrE)
A member of a group of Nilo-Saharan languages spoken in parts of Chad and neighboring regions.

Examples

A Saharan language group
Many people in Chad communicate using a Saharan language.
Researchers are studying the similarities among various Saharan languages.
The Saharan language family includes several tongues spoken across central Africa.
