somniferous

adjective
sleep-inducing; soporific
Pronunciation: / sɑmˈnɪfərəs / (AmE), / sɒmˈnɪfərəs / (BrE)
causing or inducing sleep; soporific

Examples

Somniferous tea
Certain teas are believed to have somniferous properties and are used as natural sleep aids.
He found the lecture so somniferous that he struggled to stay awake.
The medication had a somniferous effect, making her feel drowsy within minutes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: soporific - tending to induce drowsiness or sleep
  • antonym: stimulating - making one feel more active or awake
