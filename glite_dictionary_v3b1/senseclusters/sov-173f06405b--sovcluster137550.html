SOV

noun
single-occupancy vehicle
Pronunciation: / ˌɛs oʊ ˈvi / (AmE), / ˌɛs əʊ ˈviː / (BrE)
an abbreviation for a single-occupancy vehicle, meaning a car occupied only by the driver

Examples

SOV car trip
Many cities are trying to reduce the number of SOV trips to ease traffic congestion.
Using an SOV during rush hour is often discouraged to promote carpooling.
Some highway lanes are restricted to vehicles with more than one occupant, excluding SOV drivers.

Related Terms

  • antonym: HOV - high-occupancy vehicle, a car with multiple passengers
  • antonym: carpool - a vehicle shared by several people traveling together
