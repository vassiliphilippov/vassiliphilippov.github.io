vacuum bottle

noun
insulated bottle for liquids
Pronunciation: / ˈvæk.juːm ˈbɑː.tl̩ / (AmE), / ˈvæk.juːm ˈbɒ.tl̩ / (BrE)
a bottle with double walls separated by a vacuum, used to keep liquids hot or cold by reducing heat transfer

Examples

A vacuum bottle of coffee
He filled his vacuum bottle with coffee to keep it hot during the long drive.
On the camping trip, we relied on a vacuum bottle to keep our tea warm.
The scientist stored the chemicals in a vacuum bottle to maintain a stable temperature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: thermos - brand name often used generically for a vacuum bottle; insulated flask
