froe

noun
wood-splitting tool
Pronunciation: / froʊ / (AmE), / frəʊ / (BrE)
a tool with a long, straight blade and handle, used for splitting wood along the grain

Examples

A froe tool
The carpenter used a froe to split the log into thin planks for the fence.
With a mallet and a froe, she carefully separated the wood along the grain.
Traditional shingle makers often rely on a froe for precise wood splitting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: axe - a tool with a heavy bladed head for chopping wood
  • synonym: wedge - a piece of wood or metal with a pointed edge used to split material
