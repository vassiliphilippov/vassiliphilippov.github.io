Rhus laurina

noun
aromatic evergreen shrub native to California
Pronunciation: / ɹʌs lɔːˈɹiːnə / (AmE), / ɹʌs lɔːˈriːnə / (BrE)
A small aromatic evergreen shrub native to California, bearing clusters of leaves and producing whitish berries; sometimes classified under the genus Rhus.

Examples

Rhus laurina shrub
Gardeners in California often plant Rhus laurina for its drought tolerance and attractive berries.
The hills were dotted with Rhus laurina, adding fragrance to the spring air.
Wildlife are attracted to the berries of Rhus laurina during the late summer months.
