unring

verb
to remove a ring
9.9
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈrɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈrɪŋ / (BrE)
to remove a ring from something, especially from an animal's nose.

Examples

To unring a pig
The veterinarian had to unring the pig after it developed an infection.
Farmers sometimes unring cattle if the ring becomes damaged or unnecessary.
It is important to unring animals carefully to avoid causing them pain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: remove - to take something away from a place or position
  • antonym: ring - to put a ring on, especially an animal's nose
