Synonyms

  • excise
    7.2 0.7/m
    excise is a formal verb meaning to remove something completely by cutting, while cut out is more general and used in everyday speech.
  • edit out
    3.2 0.5/m
    edit out is used in film or text editing to remove parts smoothly, while cut out also covers physical cutting from a larger piece.
  • cut away
    3.4 0.2/m
    cut away means to remove by cutting off a part from the main piece, while cut out can mean shaping or deleting from text.
