isometrically

adverb
in isometric view
8.1
Pronunciation: / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (AmE), / ˌaɪsəˈmɛtrɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that uses isometric projection, showing three dimensions with equal scale along each axis so objects appear undistorted.

Examples

Drawn isometrically
The architect presented the building isometrically to help clients understand its three-dimensional structure.
In many classic video games, environments are displayed isometrically to give a sense of depth.
Engineers often draw mechanical parts isometrically for clearer visualization of their shapes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: orthogonally - in a way that uses orthogonal projection, showing objects without perspective distortion
