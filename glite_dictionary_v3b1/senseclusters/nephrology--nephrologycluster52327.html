nephrology

noun
medical study of kidneys
Pronunciation: / nɛˈfrɑlədʒi / (AmE), / nɪˈfrɒlədʒi / (BrE)
the branch of medicine that deals with the study and treatment of the kidneys and their diseases

Examples

Nephrology medicine
After completing her residency, she decided to specialize in nephrology.
Medical students often find nephrology challenging due to the complexity of kidney function.
Recent advances in nephrology have improved the prognosis for patients with chronic kidney disease.
