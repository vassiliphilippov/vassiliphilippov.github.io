meathead

noun
slang for unintelligent person
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈmiːthɛd / (AmE), / ˈmiːthɛd / (BrE)
a slang insult for a person (usually a man) who is very muscular but not very intelligent

Examples

A meathead insult

He was tired of being labeled a meathead when he actually enjoyed reading and learning.
The movie features a group of meatheads who get into trouble at the gym.
The football team was full of meatheads who spent hours in the gym.
She called her brother a meathead after he broke the door by accident.
Some people think all bodybuilders are meatheads, but that's just a stereotype.
He can be such a meathead when he refuses to listen to advice.
Stop acting like a meathead and try to solve the problem calmly.
They called him a meathead after he made another silly mistake in class.
