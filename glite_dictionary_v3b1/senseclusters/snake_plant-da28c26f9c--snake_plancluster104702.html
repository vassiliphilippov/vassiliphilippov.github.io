snake plant

noun
a common houseplant with upright, rigid leaves
3.3 3.2
Pronunciation: /sneɪk plænt/ (AmE), /sneɪk plɑːnt/ (BrE)
A stemless plant with long, narrow, rigid leaves, often grown indoors as a houseplant; also known as mother-in-law's tongue.

Examples

A snake plant leaf
A snake plant needs little water and thrives in low light conditions.

The snake plant is popular for its ability to purify indoor air.
