macaw

noun
large, colorful long-tailed parrot
3.0
Pronunciation: / məˈkɔ / (AmE), / məˈkɑː / (BrE)
a large, brightly colored parrot with a long tail, native to Central and South America

Examples

A colorful macaw
Her favorite pet was a blue-and-gold macaw that could mimic human speech.
Many macaws live in the tropical rainforests of Brazil and other parts of South America.
The vibrant feathers of the macaw caught everyone's attention at the aviary.

Related Terms

  • synonym: parrot - a bird known for its ability to mimic sounds; family Psittacidae
