war god

noun
deity associated with war and victory
1.0
Pronunciation: / wɔr ɡɑd / (AmE), / wɔː ɡɒd / (BrE)
a deity believed to influence or grant victory in warfare or battle, often worshipped in various mythologies

Examples

The war god Ares
In ancient Greece, Ares was considered the principal war god who embodied the spirit of battle.
Many cultures have a war god to whom soldiers pray for strength and victory.
Mars, the Roman war god, was honored in many festivals before campaigns.
