luminaria

noun
decorative paper lantern
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌluːməˈnɑːriə / (AmE), / ˌluːmɪˈnɑːriə / (BrE)
a decorative lantern or small light, often with a candle inside, used to light pathways or for festive occasions
