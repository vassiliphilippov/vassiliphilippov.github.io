like a tin soldier

x
standing very still and rigidly
4.1
Pronunciation: / laɪk ə tɪn ˈsoʊldʒər / (AmE), / laɪk ə tɪn ˈsəʊldʒə / (BrE)
Standing perfectly still and upright, in a stiff and rigid manner.

Examples

Stand like a tin soldier
During the inspection, she stood like a tin soldier without moving a muscle.
He froze like a tin soldier when the spotlight hit him on stage.
The children lined up like a tin soldier when the teacher walked in.
