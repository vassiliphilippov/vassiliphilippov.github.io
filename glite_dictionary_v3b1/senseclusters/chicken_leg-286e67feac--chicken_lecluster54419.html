chicken leg

noun
lower joint of a chicken, as food
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn lɛɡ / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪk.ɪn lɛɡ / (BrE)
the lower joint or drumstick of a chicken, often eaten as food

Examples

Grilled chicken leg
He ordered a spicy grilled chicken leg from the menu.
She prefers the flavor of the chicken leg over the breast.
The recipe calls for two chicken legs and a variety of spices.

Related Terms

  • synonym: drumstick - the lower part of the leg of a chicken or other bird as food
