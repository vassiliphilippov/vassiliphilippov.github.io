frosty

adjective
emotionally cold or unfriendly
3.6 0.5/m
Pronunciation: / Ààfr…ëÀêsti / (AmE), / Ààfr…ísti / (BrE)
cold and unfriendly in manner or attitude; showing little warmth or cordiality.

Examples

Frosty reception
After their argument, there was a frosty silence between them.
Her response to the suggestion was noticeably frosty, making everyone else feel uncomfortable.
They received a frosty welcome upon arriving at the meeting.
