phytohaemagglutinin

noun
plant protein clumping cells
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfaɪtoʊˌhiːməˈɡluːtənɪn / (AmE), / ˌfaɪtəʊˌhiːməˈɡlʌtɪnɪn /; / ˌfaɪtəʊˌhiːməˈɡluːtɪnɪn / (BrE)
