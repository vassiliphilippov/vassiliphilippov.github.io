stripper

noun
tool for removing wire insulation
5.1
Pronunciation: /ˈstrɪp.ər/ (AmE), /ˈstrɪp.ə/ (BrE)
a hand tool used to remove the insulating covering from electrical wires so the metal conductor is exposed.

Examples

Wire stripper
The electrician reached into his toolbox and grabbed a stripper to remove the wire insulation.
She used a small handheld stripper to prepare the wires before connecting the light fixture.
When crimping the connector, make sure the copper is clean after you use the stripper.
