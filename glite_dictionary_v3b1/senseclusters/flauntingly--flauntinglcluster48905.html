flauntingly

adverb
boldly showing off
Pronunciation: / ˈflɔːntɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈflɔːntɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that boldly or openly shows off or draws attention to oneself or something

Examples

To flauntingly show off
He wore his expensive watch flauntingly at the party to impress everyone.
The celebrity arrived flauntingly in a bright red sports car.
She spoke flauntingly about her recent achievements in front of her classmates.
He flauntingly wore his expensive watch at every opportunity, drawing attention from everyone.
The protesters flauntingly ignored the police orders and continued their demonstration.
She flauntingly broke the dress code, wearing bright colors to the formal event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brazenly - in a bold and shameless way
  • synonym: defiantly - in a way that shows resistance or disobedience
  • antonym: modestly - in a humble or unassuming way
  • synonym: ostentatiously - in a showy or pretentious way
