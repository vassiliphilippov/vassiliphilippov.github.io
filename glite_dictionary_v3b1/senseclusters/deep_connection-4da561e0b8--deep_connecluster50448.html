deep connection

noun
strong emotional bond or closeness
Pronunciation: / dip kəˈnɛkʃən / (AmE), / diːp kəˈnɛkʃən / (BrE)
a strong emotional bond or sense of closeness between people, places, or things

Examples

A deep connection between friends
Many lifelong friendships are based on a deep connection that survives the test of time.
She felt a deep connection with her hometown, even after living abroad for years.
The therapist helped the couple rediscover the deep connection they once shared.

Related Terms

  • synonym: attachment - affectionate connection or tie to someone or something
  • synonym: bond - a strong feeling of connection or attachment between people
  • antonym: distance - lack of closeness or emotional connection
