shalom

noun
Hebrew greeting
Pronunciation: / ʃɑːˈloʊm / (AmE), / ʃəˈlɒm / (BrE)
a Hebrew word meaning peace, used as a greeting or farewell

Examples

Shalom greeting
He smiled and said shalom as he entered the room.
At the end of the meeting, she waved and wished everyone shalom.
The rabbi greeted the congregation with a warm shalom.

Related Terms

  • synonym: goodbye - a common farewell
  • synonym: hello - a common greeting
  • synonym: peace - a state of calm and harmony
