Synonyms

  • exclusive right
    7.1 1.13/m
    exclusive right is legally reserved to one person or group and bars others, while privilege is a broader special advantage given to someone.
  • prerogative
    8.2 4.30/m
    prerogative is an official right linked to a role or office, while privilege is a general special advantage given to a person or group.
