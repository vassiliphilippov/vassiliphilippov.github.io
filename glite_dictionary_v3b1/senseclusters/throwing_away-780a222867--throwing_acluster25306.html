throwing away

noun
the act of discarding something
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊɪŋ əˈweɪ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊɪŋ əˈweɪ / (BrE)
the act of disposing of something considered useless or unwanted

Examples

The throwing away of trash
His constant throwing away of useful items annoyed his family.
The throwing away of plastic waste seriously harms the environment.
We should reduce unnecessary throwing away of food to prevent waste.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposal - the action or process of getting rid of something
  • antonym: retaining - the act of keeping something rather than getting rid of it
