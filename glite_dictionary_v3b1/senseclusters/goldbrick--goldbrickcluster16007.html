goldbrick

noun
lazy or shirking person
Pronunciation: / ˈɡoʊldˌbrɪk / (AmE), / ˈɡəʊldˌbrɪk / (BrE)
a person who avoids work or shirks responsibilities, especially by pretending to be busy (often used about soldiers)

Examples

Lazy goldbrick
He developed a reputation as a goldbrick after repeatedly dodging his responsibilities.
No one wanted to rely on the goldbrick in the group for this important task.
The manager was quick to call out any goldbrick who tried to avoid their share of work.
Don't be a goldbrick; everyone here must pull their own weight.
The officer quickly spotted the goldbrick who was always trying to avoid real work.
The sergeant called him a goldbrick for skipping drills whenever possible.

Related Terms

  • antonym: hard worker - a person who works diligently
  • synonym: shirker - a person who avoids work or duty
  • synonym: slacker - a person who evades work or effort
