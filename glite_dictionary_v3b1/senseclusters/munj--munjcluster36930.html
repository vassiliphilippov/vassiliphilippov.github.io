munj

noun
a tough Asiatic grass used for ropes and baskets
9.5
Pronunciation: / mʊndʒ / (AmE), / mʊndʒ / (BrE)
A tough Asiatic grass (Saccharum munja) whose stems are used for making ropes and baskets.

Examples

munj grass stems
Artisans often use munj to craft durable ropes sold in local markets.
Planting munj helps prevent soil erosion along riverbanks in some Asian regions.
The villagers gathered munj to weave strong baskets for the upcoming festival.
