heat of solution

noun

Examples

The heat of solution change
Chemists often measure the heat of solution to understand solubility and reaction energetics.
The heat of solution for sodium chloride is slightly endothermic.
When ammonium nitrate dissolves in water, its heat of solution is strongly negative, causing the mixture to cool.
