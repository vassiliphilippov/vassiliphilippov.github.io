unclick

verb
to release mouse button
4.2
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈklɪk / (AmE), / ʌnˈklɪk / (BrE)
to release a mouse button after having pressed it on a computer interface

Examples

To unclick the mouse
After you finish dragging the file, simply unclick the mouse to drop it in the folder.
To deselect the option, you must unclick the checkbox on the screen.
He forgot to unclick the mouse, so the icon kept moving around the desktop.

Related Terms

  • antonym: click - to press and release a mouse button
  • synonym: deselect - to remove a selection on a computer interface
  • degree higher: select - to choose an item on a computer interface
