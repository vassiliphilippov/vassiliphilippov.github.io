lotos

noun
mythical forgetful plant
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈloʊtoʊs / (AmE), / ˈləʊtɒs / (BrE)
a mythical plant in Greek and Roman stories whose fruit or flowers caused forgetfulness and a desire to remain in a dreamy state

Examples

Mythical lotos fruit
In ancient myths, eating the lotos made travelers forget their homes and families.
The poet described a land where people lived in a lotos-induced dream, free from worries.
Sailors who tasted the lotos never wanted to return to their old lives.
