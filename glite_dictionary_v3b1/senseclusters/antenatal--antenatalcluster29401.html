antenatal

adjective
before birth; occurring during pregnancy
8.1 5.5
Pronunciation: /æn.tiː.ˈneɪ.təl/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈneɪ.təl/ (AmE), /æn.tiː.ˈneɪ.təl/ /; / /æn.tə.ˈneɪ.təl/ (BrE)
occurring or existing before birth, especially in relation to pregnancy or the care of an unborn child

Examples

Proper antenatal care
Proper antenatal care can significantly improve health outcomes for both mother and child.
She attended regular antenatal classes to prepare for childbirth.
The doctor advised him to take his wife for antenatal checkups every month.
