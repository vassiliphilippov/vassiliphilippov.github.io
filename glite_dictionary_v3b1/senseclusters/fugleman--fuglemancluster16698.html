fugleman

noun
leader or spokesperson, especially political
Pronunciation: / ˈfjuːɡəlmən /; / ˈfjuːɡləmən / (AmE), / ˈfjuːɡlɪmən /; / ˈfjuːɡləmən / (BrE)
a person who leads or sets an example for others to follow, especially in group or public activities
