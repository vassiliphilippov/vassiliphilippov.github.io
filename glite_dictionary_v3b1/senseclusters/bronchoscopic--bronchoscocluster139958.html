bronchoscopic

adjective
relating to bronchoscopes or bronchoscopy
Pronunciation: / ˌbrɑŋ.kəˈskoʊ.pɪk / (AmE), / ˌbrɒŋ.kəˈskɒp.ɪk / (BrE)
relating to the use of a bronchoscope or procedures involving the examination of the interior of the bronchi.

Examples

Bronchoscopic biopsy
A bronchoscopic biopsy was required to obtain a sample from the lung tissue.
Complications following a bronchoscopic procedure are rare but can include minor bleeding.
The doctor performed a bronchoscopic examination to investigate the source of the patient's cough.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bronchoscope - a medical instrument for examining the bronchi
  • synonym: bronchoscopy - the procedure of using a bronchoscope to examine the bronchi
