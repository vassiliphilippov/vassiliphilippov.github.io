bronchoscopic

adjective
relating to bronchoscopes or bronchoscopy
5.5 6.0
Pronunciation: /brɑːŋ.kə.ˈskɑː.pɪk/ /; / /brɑːŋ.koʊ.ˈskɑː.pɪk/ (AmE), /brɒŋ.kə.ˈskɒp.ɪk/ /; / /brɒŋ.kəʊ.ˈskɒp.ɪk/ (BrE)

Examples

The doctor performed a bronchoscopic examination to investigate the source of the patient's cough.
