aspersorium

noun
device for sprinkling holy water
9.2
Pronunciation: / əˌspɜːrˈsɔːriəm / (AmE), / əˌspɜːˈsɔːriəm / (BrE)
A vessel or tool, often a short-handled device with a globe containing a sponge, used for sprinkling holy water in religious ceremonies.

Examples

Ceremonial aspersorium
During the ceremony, the acolyte carried the aspersorium down the aisle.
She gazed at the ornate silver aspersorium on the altar, marveling at its craftsmanship.
The priest dipped the aspersorium into the holy water before sprinkling the congregation.
