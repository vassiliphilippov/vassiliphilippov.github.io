bottlefeed

verb
feed an infant with a bottle
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑt̬lˌfid / (AmE), / ˈbɒtlˌfiːd / (BrE)
to feed an infant or young child with milk or formula from a bottle rather than from the breast

Examples

To bottlefeed a baby
After returning to work, she decided to bottlefeed her baby.
It's important to bottlefeed newborns with sterilized bottles to prevent infection.
Parents may choose to bottlefeed for various medical or personal reasons.

Related Terms

  • antonym: breastfeed - to feed a baby with milk from the mother's breast
