wispily

adverb
thin or delicate manner
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪspɪli / (AmE), / ˈwɪspɪli / (BrE)
in a thin, delicate, or faint manner, like a wisp

Examples

Mist rises wispily
Mist rose wispily from the surface of the quiet lake at dawn.
Her hair fell wispily around her face as she walked in the breeze.
Smoke drifted wispily from the chimney into the cold evening air.

Related Terms

  • synonym: delicately - in a gentle or fragile way
  • synonym: faintly - in a way that is not strong or clear
  • antonym: thickly - in a dense or heavy way
