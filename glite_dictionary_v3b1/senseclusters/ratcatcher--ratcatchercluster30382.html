ratcatcher

noun
person who catches rats
6.0
Pronunciation: / ˈrætˌkætʃər / (AmE), / ˈrætˌkætʃə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to catch and remove rats, especially to control infestations

Examples

City ratcatcher
The old ratcatcher walked through the city with his traps and tools.
In the past, a ratcatcher was essential for keeping towns free of disease.
The ratcatcher explained how he safely removed rodents from homes.
