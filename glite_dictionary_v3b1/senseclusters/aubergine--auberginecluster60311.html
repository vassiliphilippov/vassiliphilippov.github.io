aubergine

noun
egg-shaped purple vegetable; eggplant
5.1 0.09/m
Pronunciation: / Àào äb…ôrÀå íin / (AmE), / Àà…ô äb…ô íiÀên / (BrE)
An egg-shaped vegetable with smooth, typically dark purple skin, though sometimes white or yellow, also known as eggplant.

Examples
