aubergine

noun
egg-shaped purple vegetable; eggplant
Pronunciation: / ˈoʊbərˌʒin / (AmE), / ˈəʊbəʒiːn / (BrE)
An egg-shaped vegetable with smooth, typically dark purple skin, though sometimes white or yellow, also known as eggplant.

Examples

Grilled aubergine
Grilled aubergine is a popular Mediterranean dish.
She cooked a delicious curry with chopped aubergine and spices.
The recipe calls for two medium aubergines, sliced thinly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: eggplant - an egg-shaped vegetable, especially in American English
