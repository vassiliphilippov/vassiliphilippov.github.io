state

noun
condition or situation of something
2.2
Pronunciation: / steɪt / (AmE), / steɪt / (BrE)
the condition or situation of something or someone at a particular time, including mental or emotional condition

Examples

A happy state
His mental state improved after a good night's sleep.
She was surprised by the poor state of the hotel room.
The building was in a dangerous state after the fire.
Don't get yourself into a state over something so minor.
I found him in a terrible state when I arrived, clearly upset and confused.
She was in such a state after the argument that she couldn't stop crying.

Related Terms

  • antonym: calm - a state of tranquility or peace
  • synonym: condition - the particular state that someone or something is in
  • synonym: mood - temporary state of mind or feeling
  • synonym: situation - the set of circumstances in which one finds oneself
