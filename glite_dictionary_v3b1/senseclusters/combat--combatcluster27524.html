combat

noun
a fight between military forces
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːmˌbæt /; / ˈkɑːmˌbæt, kəmˈbæt / (AmE), / ˈkɒm.bæt /; / ˈkɒmbæt, kəmˈbæt / (BrE)
a fight or battle, especially between opposing forces or during a war

Examples

Hand-to-hand combat
Close combat requires both skill and bravery on the battlefield.
He was wounded in combat during the final days of the war.
The soldiers prepared for combat as the enemy approached their position.
During the tournament, the two teams engaged in fierce combat for the championship.
The government is funding new programs to prevent violent combat in city neighborhoods.
The soldiers returned from combat deeply changed by their experiences.

Related Terms

  • synonym: battle - a fight between large organized armed forces
  • synonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, often a prolonged fight
  • antonym: peace - a state of tranquility or absence of war
