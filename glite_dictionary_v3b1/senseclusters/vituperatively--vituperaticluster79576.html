vituperatively

adverb
harshly or abusively
9.9
Pronunciation: / vaɪˈtuːpərətɪvli / (AmE), / vɪˈtjuːpərətɪvli / (BrE)
in a manner that involves harsh, abusive, or strongly critical language

Examples

To speak vituperatively
The critic wrote vituperatively about the new film, leaving no kind words for the director.
During the debate, she responded vituperatively to her opponent's accusations.
He addressed the staff vituperatively, causing many to feel upset by his harsh words.

Related Terms

  • synonym: abusively - in a way that is insulting or harsh
  • antonym: gently - in a kind or mild manner
  • synonym: harshly - in a severe or unkind manner
