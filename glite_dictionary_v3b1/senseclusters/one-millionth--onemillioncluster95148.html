one-millionth

noun
one part in a million; 1/1,000,000
9.1 3.0
Pronunciation: /wʌn ˈmɪl.jənθ/ /; / /wən ˈmɪl.jənθ/ (AmE), /wʌn ˈmɪl.jənθ/ /; / /wɒn ˈmɪl.jənθ/ (BrE)
