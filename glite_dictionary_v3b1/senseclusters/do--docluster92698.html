Synonyms

  • perform
    1.3 108/m
    perform is used for tasks, shows, or duties done in formal settings, while do is more general everyday action.
  • carry out
    1.8 31/m
    carry out is used for following orders or plans to completion, while do is a more general way to take action.
  • execute
    3.6 13/m
    execute is formal and often means following a precise plan or order, while do is more casual and general.
  • act
    1.0 65/m
    act is to take deliberate action in a situation or to respond, while do simply means to perform an action.
  • make
    1.0 216/m
    make often means create or cause something physical, while do usually means carry out an action or task.
