elastomeric

adjective
stretchable material
Pronunciation: / ɪˌlæstəˈmɛrɪk / (AmE), / ɪˌlæstəˈmɛrɪk / (BrE)
describing a material that can stretch and return to its original shape

Examples

Elastomeric material
The engineer recommended using elastomeric seals for better flexibility in the machine.
These elastomeric bands can stretch to twice their original length without breaking.
The sportswear company developed a new elastomeric fabric for greater comfort and movement.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elastic - able to return to original shape after stretching
  • antonym: rigid - not able to bend or be stretched
