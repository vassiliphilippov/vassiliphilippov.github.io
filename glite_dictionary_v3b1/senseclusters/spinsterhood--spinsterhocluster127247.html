spinsterhood

noun
the state of being a spinster
5.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪnstərˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈspɪnstəhʊd / (BrE)
the state or condition of being a spinster, typically referring to an elderly unmarried woman

Examples

Embrace spinsterhood
After years of enjoying her independence, she gladly embraced spinsterhood.
Her family worried that she would be condemned to spinsterhood if she did not marry soon.
Literature often depicts spinsterhood as a lonely and isolated existence, but that stereotype is fading.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spinster - an unmarried woman, typically older and beyond the usual age for marriage
