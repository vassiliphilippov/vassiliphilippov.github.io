Fossores

proper noun
obsolete insect group
9.9
Pronunciation: / fɑːˈsɔːriːz / (AmE), / fɒˈsɔːriːz / (BrE)
an obsolete scientific name for a group of burrowing insects, especially certain wasps and bees

Examples

Fossores wasps
In older entomology texts, Fossores refers to a group of burrowing wasps and bees.
The classification of Fossores is no longer used in modern insect taxonomy.
Nineteenth-century scientists often grouped certain insects under the name Fossores.
