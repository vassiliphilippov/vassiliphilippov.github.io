prank

noun
a mischievous or playful act; practical joke
Pronunciation: / præŋk / (AmE), / præŋk / (BrE)
a mischievous or playful act done especially to cause amusement at someone else's expense

Examples

A funny prank
He became famous online for posting videos of elaborate pranks on strangers.
Pulling the fire alarm as a prank is not only disruptive but also dangerous.
The students played a harmless prank on their teacher for April Fool's Day.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hoax - a humorous or malicious deception
  • synonym: practical joke - a trick played on someone to make them look foolish
  • synonym: trick - a cunning or mischievous act, often to fool someone
