panoramically

adverb
in a wide view
Pronunciation: / ˌpænəˈræmɪkli / (AmE), / ˌpænəˈræmɪkli / (BrE)
in a way that shows or covers a wide, comprehensive view of an area or scene

Examples

To see panoramically
The mountain could be admired panoramically from the observation deck.
The documentary presents the landscape panoramically, capturing every detail from above.
She described the city panoramically, mentioning every district and landmark in her speech.

Related Terms

  • antonym: narrowly - in a limited or restricted way
  • synonym: widely - over a large area or range
