calculable

adjective
able to be calculated or estimated
Pronunciation: / ˈkælkjələbəl / (AmE), / ˈkalkjʊləbəl / (BrE)
able to be calculated or estimated; possible to determine by calculation

Examples

A calculable amount
Only a few variables were calculable with the data provided, limiting the accuracy of the forecast.
The benefits of the investment are calculable and justify the cost involved.
The manager took a calculable risk in launching the new product line.

Related Terms

  • antonym: incalculable - impossible to calculate or estimate
