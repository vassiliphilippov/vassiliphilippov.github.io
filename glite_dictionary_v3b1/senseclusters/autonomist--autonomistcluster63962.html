autonomist

noun
supporter of self-government
Pronunciation: / ɔˈtɑːnəmɪst / (AmE), / ɔˈtɒnəmɪst / (BrE)
a person who supports or advocates for self-government or autonomy for a group or region

Examples

An autonomist leader
The autonomist argued passionately for the region's right to govern itself.
Many young people joined the autonomist movement after the new laws were introduced.
As an autonomist, she believed local communities should make their own decisions.
The autonomist argued that workers should have direct control over their workplaces.
Many young activists identified as autonomists during the political protests.
The book explores the history of the autonomist movement in Europe.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anarchist - a person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy
  • antonym: collectivist - a person who supports collective control over production and distribution
  • synonym: separatist - a person who supports separation from a larger group or country
  • antonym: unionist - a person who supports political union or remaining part of a larger group
