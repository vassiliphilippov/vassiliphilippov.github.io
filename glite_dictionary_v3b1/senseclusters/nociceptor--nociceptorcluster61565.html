nociceptor

noun
pain-sensing nerve cell
9.7
Pronunciation: /noʊ.ˈsɪs.ɪp.tər/ /; / /nə.ˈsɪs.ɪp.tər/ (AmE), /nəʊ.ˈsɪs.ɪp.tə/ /; / /nə.ˈsɪs.ɪp.tə/ (BrE)
a sensory nerve cell that detects and responds to pain or potentially harmful stimuli

Examples

A nociceptor detects pain
A nociceptor detects tissue damage and sends pain signals to the brain.
Researchers study how a nociceptor responds to different types of injuries.
When you touch something hot, a nociceptor quickly alerts your nervous system.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pain receptor - a nerve cell that responds to pain
