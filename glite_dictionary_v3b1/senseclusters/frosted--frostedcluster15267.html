frosted

adjective
glass with a roughened, opaque surface
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑstɪd / (AmE), / ˈfrɒstɪd / (BrE)
(of glass) having a roughened or semi-opaque surface that scatters light, usually to provide privacy or diffuse illumination.

Examples

Frosted glass window
She preferred drinking from frosted glasses because they kept beverages cooler longer.
Sunlight filtered softly through the frosted panels of the office door.
The bathroom window was made of frosted glass to ensure privacy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: etched - (of glass) decorated or roughened by acid or abrasive action
