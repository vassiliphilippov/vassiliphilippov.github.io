muscleman

noun
man with very developed muscles
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌsəlˌmæn / (AmE), / ˈmʌsəlˌman / (BrE)
a man with well-developed muscles, especially one who develops them through special exercises or bodybuilding

Examples

Gym muscleman
After years of weightlifting, he became quite the muscleman in his local gym.
The muscleman flexed his arms, impressing the crowd at the fitness expo.
The movie features a heroic muscleman who saves the city from danger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bodybuilder - a person who strengthens and enlarges the muscles of their body through intensive exercise
  • antonym: weakling - a person lacking physical strength
