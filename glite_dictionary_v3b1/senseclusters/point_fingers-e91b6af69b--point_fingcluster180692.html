point fingers

verb
to accuse or assign blame
4.8
Pronunciation: / pɔɪnt ˈfɪŋɡərz / (AmE), / pɔɪnt ˈfɪŋɡəz / (BrE)
to accuse or blame someone for something, especially in situations where responsibility is uncertain or shared

Examples

To point fingers at others
After the incident, management tried not to point fingers at any particular employee.
Instead of pointing fingers, the team took collective responsibility and moved forward.
It's not helpful to point fingers when we should be working together to solve the problem.

Related Terms

  • synonym: accuse - to say someone has done something wrong or illegal
  • synonym: blame - to assign responsibility for a fault or wrong
