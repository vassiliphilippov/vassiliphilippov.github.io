recyclable

adjective
able to be recycled and reused
1.0
Pronunciation: / rɪˈsaɪkləbəl / (AmE), / rɪˈsaɪkləbl / (BrE)
Able to be processed and used again, especially after undergoing special treatment.

Examples

Recyclable plastic bottle
Many cities have laws requiring the use of recyclable containers.
Please place all recyclable materials in the blue bin.
The packaging is completely recyclable, which helps reduce waste.

Related Terms

  • antonym: nonrecyclable - not able to be recycled or reused
  • synonym: reusable - able to be used again without remanufacturing
