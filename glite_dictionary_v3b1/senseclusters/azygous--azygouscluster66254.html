azygous

adjective
occurring singly; not paired (anat.)
Pronunciation: / əˈzaɪɡəs / (AmE), / əˈzɪɡəs / (BrE)
(of a body part or structure) occurring singly and not forming one of a pair, especially in anatomical contexts.

Examples

An azygous body part
An azygous lobe of the lung may be present as a normal anatomical variant.
The azygous vein drains the thoracic wall and upper lumbar region.
Unlike paired organs, the azygous muscle is found singly in the soft palate.
