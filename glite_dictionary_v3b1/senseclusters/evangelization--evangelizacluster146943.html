evangelization

noun
spreading religious beliefs
8.0
Pronunciation: / ɪˌvændʒələˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ɪˌvændʒəlaɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the act of spreading religious beliefs or messages to others, especially to convert them to a particular faith

Examples

Christian evangelization
The church organized a week of evangelization in the local community.
Many volunteers participated in the evangelization campaign to share their faith.
Modern evangelization often uses social media to reach a wider audience.
The church organized a campaign for the evangelization of the local population.
Modern evangelization often uses social media to reach a wider audience.
He dedicated his life to the evangelization of people in distant countries.

Related Terms

  • synonym: missionary work - the activity of sending people to spread religious beliefs
  • synonym: proselytism - the act of trying to convert people to another religion or belief
