parietomastoid suture

noun
skull suture between parietal and temporal bones
9.9
Pronunciation: / pəˌraɪət̬oʊˈmæstɔɪd ˈsuːtʃɚ / (AmE), / pəˌraɪətəʊˈmæstɔɪd ˈsuːtʃə / (BrE)
the suture between the parietal bone and the mastoid part of the temporal bone in the skull
