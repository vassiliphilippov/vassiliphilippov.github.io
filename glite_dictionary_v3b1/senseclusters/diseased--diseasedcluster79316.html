diseased

adjective
affected by disease
2.0
Pronunciation: / dɪˈzizd / (AmE), / dɪˈziːzd / (BrE)
affected by disease; showing signs or symptoms of disease.

Examples

Diseased heart
A diseased heart can lead to serious complications if left untreated.
The doctor recommended removing the diseased tissue to prevent further infection.
The tree had to be cut down because its trunk was diseased and rotten inside.
