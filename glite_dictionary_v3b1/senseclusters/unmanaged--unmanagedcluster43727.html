unmanaged

adjective
not centrally controlled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈmænɪdʒd / (AmE), / ʌnˈmænɪdʒd / (BrE)
not controlled, organized, or taken care of by anyone or any system

Examples

An unmanaged device
The IT department identified several unmanaged devices connected to the corporate network.
Using unmanaged code can increase performance but may also introduce security risks.
Many small businesses rely on unmanaged switches because they are simpler and less expensive.
The unmanaged forest became a haven for wildlife over the years.
If left unmanaged, small problems can quickly grow into major issues.
The company struggled with its unmanaged finances after the accountant resigned.

Related Terms

  • synonym: independent - not controlled by others; self-governing
  • antonym: maintained - kept in good condition or taken care of
  • antonym: managed - controlled or administered by a management system
  • synonym: neglected - not given proper care or attention
