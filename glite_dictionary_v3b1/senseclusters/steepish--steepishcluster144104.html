steepish

adjective
somewhat steep; moderately inclined
Pronunciation: / stiːpɪʃ / (AmE), / stiːpɪʃ / (BrE)
Somewhat steep; having a moderately sharp slope or incline.

Examples

A steepish slope
The driveway is steepish, so be careful when backing out in winter.
The trail up the hill was steepish, but still manageable for most hikers.
We took a steepish shortcut down to the river to save some time.
