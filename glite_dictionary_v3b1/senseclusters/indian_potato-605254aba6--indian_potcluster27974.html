Indian potato

noun
North American vine with edible tubers
Pronunciation: / ˈɪndiən pəˈtoʊtoʊ /; / ˈɪndiən pəˈteɪtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈɪndiən pəˈtəʊtəʊ /; / ˈɪndiən pəˈteɪtəʊ / (BrE)
a North American plant with edible tubers, traditionally used as food by Native Americans
