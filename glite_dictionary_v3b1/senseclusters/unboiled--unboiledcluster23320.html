unboiled

adjective
not boiled
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈbɔɪld / (AmE), / ʌnˈbɔɪld / (BrE)
not boiled or not having been cooked in boiling water

Examples

Unboiled potatoes
The chef prefers to use unboiled potatoes for this particular dish.
Please bring me two unboiled eggs for the salad.
Some recipes require unboiled milk to achieve the right texture.
