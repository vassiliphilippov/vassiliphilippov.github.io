prong

noun
pointed projection or spike
Pronunciation: / prɑŋ / (AmE), / prɒŋ / (BrE)
a thin, pointed projection or spike, especially one of several on an object such as a fork or antler

Examples

A fork prong
Each prong of the deer’s antler curved gracefully upward.
The electrical plug has three prongs for safety reasons.
The fork was missing a prong, making it difficult to use.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spike - a long, pointed piece projecting from a surface or object
  • synonym: tine - a prong or sharp point, such as on a fork or antler
