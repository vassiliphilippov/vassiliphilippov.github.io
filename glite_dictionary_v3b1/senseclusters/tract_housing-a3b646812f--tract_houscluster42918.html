tract housing

noun
rows of similar houses built together
8.4 3.3
Pronunciation: /trækt ˈhaʊ.zɪŋ/ /; / /trækt ˈhaʊ.sɪŋ/ (AmE), /trækt ˈhaʊ.zɪŋ/ /; / /trækt ˈhaʊ.sɪŋ/ (BrE)
