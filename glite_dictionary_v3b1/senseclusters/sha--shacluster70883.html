sha

particle
Nigerian discourse particle
Pronunciation: / ʃa / (AmE), / ʃa / (BrE)
a colloquial particle in West African and Nigerian English used to soften statements, express contrast, or mean 'anyway' or 'still'

Examples

Nigerian sha particle
I was tired, sha, I finished all my work before leaving.
She said she would come, sha, I am not sure if she will.
He did not study much, sha, he still passed the exam.

Related Terms

  • synonym: anyway - used to change the subject or return to a previous topic
  • synonym: still - used to indicate something continues despite circumstances
