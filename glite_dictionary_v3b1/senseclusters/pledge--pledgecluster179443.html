pledge

noun
ceremonial drink in someone's honor
Pronunciation: / plɛdʒ / (AmE), / plɛdʒ / (BrE)
a ceremonial drink taken to honor or toast a person or event

Examples

A friendship pledge
He took a pledge with his friends to seal their enduring friendship.
Let us drink a pledge to the health of our host before dinner.
The guests raised their glasses in a solemn pledge to the newlyweds' happiness.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toast - a salutation or drink in honor of someone; raising a glass in celebration
