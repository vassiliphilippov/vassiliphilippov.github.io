carbonize

verb
unite with carbon (chemistry/metallurgy)
2.4 6.7
Pronunciation: /ˈkɑːr.bə.naɪz/ (AmE), /ˈkɑː.bə.naɪz/ (BrE)

Examples

Some industrial processes carbonize organic materials at high temperatures to create carbon-rich products.
The engineers developed a technique to carbonize the surface of the alloy for enhanced durability.
