steeple

noun
tall, pointed church tower
4.9 1.16/m
Pronunciation: / ÀàstiÀêp…ôl / (AmE), / ÀàstiÀêpl / (BrE)
a tall, narrow tower on a building, especially a church, that often tapers to a point at the top

Examples

Church steeple
Lightning struck the steeple during last night's thunderstorm, damaging the roof below.
The old village church has a beautiful steeple that can be seen from miles away.
Workers carefully restored the clock on the cathedral's steeple as part of the renovation project.

Synonyms

  • spire
    5.8 2.51/m
    a spire is a slim pointed top on a roof, while a steeple is the taller church tower below it.
  • church tower
    2.1 0.07/m
    a church tower is a broad vertical part of a church often with bells, while a steeple ends in a point.

Related Terms

  • synonym: spire - a tapering conical or pyramidal structure on the top of a building, especially a church tower
