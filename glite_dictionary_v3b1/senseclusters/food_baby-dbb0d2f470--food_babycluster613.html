food baby

noun
bloated stomach after eating
9.8
Pronunciation: / fud ˈbeɪ.bi / (AmE), / fuːd ˈbeɪ.bi / (BrE)
a bloated stomach or the feeling of fullness after eating a large meal, giving the appearance of a temporary, rounded belly

Examples

A food baby belly
After Thanksgiving dinner, I felt like I was carrying a food baby for hours.
He posted a picture of his food baby on social media after the big brunch.
She laughed about her food baby after eating an entire pizza by herself.
