samovar

noun
Russian metal tea urn
Pronunciation: / ˈsæməˌvɑr / (AmE), / ˈsaməˌvɑː / (BrE)
A metal urn with a spigot at the base, traditionally used in Russia to boil water for tea.

Examples

A samovar urn
During their visit to Moscow, they admired an antique samovar in a traditional tea house.
She filled her glass with hot water from the samovar before adding loose leaf tea.
The ornate samovar sat in the center of the table, steaming gently and ready for afternoon tea.
