nationhood

noun
state or status of being a nation
2.4
Pronunciation: / ˈneɪʃənˌhʊd / (AmE), / ˈneɪʃənhʊd / (BrE)
the condition of being an independent nation or having the qualities that constitute a nation

Examples

Debates about nationhood often center on issues of identity and sovereignty.
The celebrations marked the 50th anniversary of the country's nationhood.
