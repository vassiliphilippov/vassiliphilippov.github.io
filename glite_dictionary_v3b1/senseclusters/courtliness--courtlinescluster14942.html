courtliness

noun
elegance or refined manners
Pronunciation: / ˈkɔrtlinəs / (AmE), / ˈkɔːtlɪnəs / (BrE)
elegant and refined manner or behavior, especially resembling that of a royal court

Examples

With courtliness and grace
Despite his humble origins, he carried himself with a natural courtliness.
He spoke to her with such courtliness that she felt truly honored.
The courtliness of the ambassador impressed everyone at the dinner.

Related Terms

  • antonym: boorishness - rude or insensitive behavior; lack of refinement
  • synonym: elegance - graceful and stylish appearance or manner
