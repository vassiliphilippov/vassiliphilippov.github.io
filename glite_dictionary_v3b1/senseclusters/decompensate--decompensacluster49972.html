decompensate

verb
to lose normal function
8.9
Pronunciation: / diˈkɑːmpənˌseɪt /; / diːˈkɑːmpənˌseɪt / (AmE), / diˈkɒmpənseɪt /; / diːˈkɒmpənseɪt / (BrE)
