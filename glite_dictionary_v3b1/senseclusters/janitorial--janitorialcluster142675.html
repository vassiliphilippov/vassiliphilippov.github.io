janitorial

adjective
building cleaning duties
Pronunciation: / ˌdʒænɪˈtɔriəl / (AmE), / ˌdʒænɪˈtɔːriəl / (BrE)
relating to the cleaning and maintenance of buildings, especially as done by a janitor

Examples

Janitorial service
The company hired a new janitorial service to keep the building clean.
He worked in a janitorial position while attending college at night.
All janitorial supplies are stored in the closet near the main entrance.
