tenuto

noun
musical hold mark
9.1
Pronunciation: /tə.ˈnuː.toʊ/ /; / /te.ˈnuː.toʊ/ (AmE), /te.ˈnjuː.təʊ/ /; / /tə.ˈnjuː.təʊ/ (BrE)
a musical notation mark indicating that a note should be held for its full value or slightly emphasized

Examples

Tenuto marking
The pianist carefully observed the tenuto marks to give each note its full value.
A small horizontal line above the note signaled a tenuto in the sheet music.
The conductor reminded the orchestra to play the passage with tenuto for greater expression.
