reproduction

noun
biological process of producing offspring
Pronunciation: / ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən / (AmE), / ˌriːprəˈdʌkʃən / (BrE)
the process by which living things produce offspring (babies, young, or new individuals)

Examples

Animal reproduction
Education about human reproduction is part of the biology curriculum.
Scientists study the reproduction of endangered species in captivity.
Sexual reproduction allows for genetic variation in the offspring.
Scientists study cell division as part of the broader process of reproduction.
Sexual reproduction in plants often involves pollination by insects or the wind.
Through reproduction, animals ensure the continuation of their species.

Related Terms

  • synonym: procreation - the act or process of producing offspring
  • antonym: sterility - the inability to produce offspring; antonym for reproduction
