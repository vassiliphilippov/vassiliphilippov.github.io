Myrmeleon

noun
genus of antlions (Myrmeleontidae)
Pronunciation: / mɪrˈmiːliːˌɒn /; / mɪrˈmiːliːɒn / (AmE), / mɪˈmɛliːən /; / mɪˈməʊliːɒn / (BrE)
a genus of insects in the antlion family (Myrmeleontidae), known for their slender bodies and predatory larvae that build pitfall traps
