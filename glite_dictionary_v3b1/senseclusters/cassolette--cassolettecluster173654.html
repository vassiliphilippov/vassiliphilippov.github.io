cassolette

noun
small serving dish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌkæsəˈlɛt / (AmE), / ˌkæsəˈlɛt / (BrE)
a small dish or container used for cooking and serving individual portions of food

Examples

Seafood cassolette
The chef served the seafood in a hot cassolette straight from the oven.
Each guest received a cassolette filled with creamy potatoes and cheese.
She bought a set of ceramic cassolettes for her dinner parties.

Related Terms
