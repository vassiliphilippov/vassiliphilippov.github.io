benumbed

adjective
lacking physical sensation
9.0
Pronunciation: / bɪˈnʌmd / (AmE), / bɪˈnʌmd / (BrE)
Deprived of physical sensation or feeling, typically as a result of cold, shock, or emotion.

Examples

Benumbed fingers
After the long walk in the snow, her fingers were completely benumbed.
He sat motionless, benumbed by the shock of the news.
The climber's toes became benumbed from exposure to the icy wind.

Related Terms

  • synonym: numb - deprived of the power of sensation
