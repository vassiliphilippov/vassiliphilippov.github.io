hunk

noun
large irregular piece or lump
5.9
Pronunciation: / hʌŋk / (AmE), / hʌŋk / (BrE)
a large piece or lump of something, especially food, with no definite shape

Examples

A hunk of bread
He threw a hunk of coal onto the fire to keep it burning.
She sliced a hunk of cheese and placed it on the plate.
There was a hunk of bread left from last night's dinner.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chunk - a thick, solid piece cut or broken off something
  • degree lower: sliver - a small, thin, narrow piece of something cut or split off
