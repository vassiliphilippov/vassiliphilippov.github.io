walla

noun
person in charge
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːlə / (AmE), / ˈwɒlə / (BrE)
a person who is involved with or in charge of a particular thing or activity, especially in South Asia

Examples

Chai walla
The chai walla served tea to the customers at the station.
We called the repair walla when the fan stopped working.
Every morning, the newspaper walla delivers papers to our doorstep.
