Examples

Nominalistic philosophy
His interpretation of language is firmly nominalistic, denying the existence of universals beyond words.
She wrote a thesis examining nominalistic perspectives in contemporary metaphysics.
The debate between nominalistic and realistic philosophies shaped medieval thought.

Related Terms
