drainboard

noun
sloping board by sink for draining dishes
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪnˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈdreɪnˌbɔːd / (BrE)
A sloping board or surface beside a kitchen sink, designed to allow washed dishes or utensils to drain into the sink.

Examples

Kitchen drainboard
After washing the plates, she placed them on the drainboard to dry.
He wiped the drainboard clean after doing the dishes.
The water collects on the drainboard and flows back into the sink.
