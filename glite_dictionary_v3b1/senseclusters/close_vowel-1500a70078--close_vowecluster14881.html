close vowel

noun
vowel pronounced with tongue near roof of mouth
6.3 6.1
Pronunciation: / kloʊs ˈvaʊəl / (AmE), / kləʊs ˈvaʊəl / (BrE)
a vowel sound pronounced with the tongue positioned close to the roof of the mouth, as in the vowel of 'see'
