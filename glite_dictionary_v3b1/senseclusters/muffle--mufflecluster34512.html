muffle

noun
kiln with inner chamber for low-temperature firing
8.5 7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌf.əl / (AmE), / ˈmʌf.əl / (BrE)
a kiln with an inner chamber used for firing objects at a low temperature, often to protect them from direct flame or smoke

Examples

A muffle kiln
She used a muffle to fire delicate glassware that could not withstand high temperatures.
The pottery was placed in the muffle to ensure an even, gentle firing.
The technician carefully loaded the specimens into the muffle for heat treatment.
