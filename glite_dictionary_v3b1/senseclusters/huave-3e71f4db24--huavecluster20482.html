Huave

proper noun
indigenous Mexican people
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɑːveɪ / (AmE), / ˈwɑːveɪ / (BrE)
an indigenous people of Mexico, especially in Oaxaca, or the language spoken by this group

Examples

The Huave people
The Huave have lived along the Pacific coast of Oaxaca for centuries.
Researchers are working to document the Huave language before it disappears.
Many Huave communities celebrate traditional festivals unique to their culture.
