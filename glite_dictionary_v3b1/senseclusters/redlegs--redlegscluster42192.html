redlegs

noun
artillery soldiers
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈrɛdˌlɛɡz / (AmE), / ˈrɛdˌlɛɡz / (BrE)
an informal name for soldiers of the Royal Regiment of Artillery in the British Army, referring to artillerymen

Examples

British redlegs artillerymen
The redlegs played a crucial role in the defense of the city during the siege.
During the parade, the redlegs marched proudly in their distinctive uniforms.
Stories of the bravery of the redlegs are still told among artillery regiments today.
