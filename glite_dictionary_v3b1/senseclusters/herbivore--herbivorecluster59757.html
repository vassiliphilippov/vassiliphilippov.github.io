herbivore

noun
person who eats mainly plants (informal)
3.4 0.06/m
Pronunciation: / \u02c8h\u025crb\u026a\u02ccv\u0254r / (AmE), / \u02c8h\u025c\u02d0b\u026a\u02ccv\u0254\u02d0 / (BrE)
informal (figurative) a person who mainly eats plants; a vegetarian or vegan.

Examples

A herbivore diet
She calls herself a herbivore and avoids all animal products.
After switching diets, he became known around the office as a herbivore.
Many restaurants now offer special menus for the modern herbivore seeking plant-based options.

Synonyms

  • vegetarian
    1.9 4.61/m
    vegetarian does not eat meat or fish but may eat dairy and eggs, while herbivore is an informal word for someone who mainly eats plants.
  • vegan
    3.0 1.42/m
    vegan avoids all animal foods and products and follows strict rules, while herbivore is an informal word for someone who mainly eats plants.

Related Terms

  • antonym: carnivore - a person who eats primarily meat (used informally/figuratively)
  • synonym: vegan - a person who does not eat or use animal products
  • synonym: vegetarian - a person who does not eat meat (often eats plant-based foods)
