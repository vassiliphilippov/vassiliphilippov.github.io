chairman of the board

noun
leader of a company's board of directors
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛɚˌmæn əv ðə bɔrd / (AmE), / ˈtʃeəˌmæn əv ðə bɔːd / (BrE)
the person who leads the board of directors of a company or organization

Examples

Chairman of the board leads meeting
After years on the board, he was elected chairman of the board in 2020.
As chairman of the board, she presided over the annual shareholder meeting.
The chairman of the board called for a vote on the proposed merger.

Related Terms

  • synonym: chairperson - a gender-neutral term for the person leading a board or meeting
  • synonym: president - another senior role in some organizations, sometimes synonymous with chairman
