giant taro

noun
large tropical plant with edible root
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtɑːroʊ / (AmE), / ˈdʒaɪənt ˈtærəʊ / (BrE)
A large tropical perennial plant with very large, erect or spreading leaves, cultivated for its edible rhizome and shoots, and sometimes grown as an ornamental.

Examples

Large giant taro plant
Farmers in Southeast Asia often plant giant taro for its starchy edible rhizomes.
The giant taro added a dramatic touch to the garden with its huge green leaves.
The leaves of the giant taro can grow over a meter long in favorable conditions.
