STP

noun
automated end-to-end transaction processing
7.4 0.00/m
Pronunciation: / cc5bs.tid0c8pid0 / (AmE), / cc5bs.tid0c8pid0 / (BrE)
Abbreviation for straight-through processing: automated, end-to-end handling of financial or business transactions so that they are completed without manual intervention.

Examples

STP payment system
The bank's STP program allowed thousands of payments to settle without manual checks.
Implementing STP shortened the trade settlement cycle and reduced settlement fails across counterparties.
Regulators encourage banks to adopt STP to improve operational resilience and lower processing costs.

Related Terms

  • synonym: automation - use of technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention
  • antonym: manual processing - handling transactions or tasks by human operators rather than by automated systems
