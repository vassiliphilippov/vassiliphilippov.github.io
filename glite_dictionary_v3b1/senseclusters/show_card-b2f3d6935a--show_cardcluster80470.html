show card

noun
poster advertising a show or play
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊ ˌkɑrd / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊ ˌkɑːd / (BrE)
A poster or card used to advertise a show, play, or other entertainment event.

Examples

Old show cards
Old show cards from Broadway musicals are now collectors' items.
She designed a colorful show card to promote the school play.
The theater displayed a large show card in the window to attract passersby.
