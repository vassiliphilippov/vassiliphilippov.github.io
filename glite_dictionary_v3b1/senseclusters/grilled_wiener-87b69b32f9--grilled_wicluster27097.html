grilled wiener

noun
sausage cooked on a grill
3.8
Pronunciation: / ɡrɪld ˈwiːnɚ / (AmE), / ɡrɪld ˈviːnə / (BrE)
A wiener sausage that has been cooked on a grill, often served in a bun as a hot dog.

Examples

A grilled wiener sandwich
At the cookout, everyone enjoyed a grilled wiener fresh from the barbecue.
She put a grilled wiener in a bun and added mustard for lunch.
Would you like your grilled wiener with onions and relish?
