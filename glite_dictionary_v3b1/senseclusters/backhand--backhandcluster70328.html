backhand

adjective
stroke made with back of hand facing forward
2.2 3.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbæk.hænd/ (AmE), /ˈbæk.hænd/ (BrE)
(of a stroke in racket sports) made with the back of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke, typically across the body.
