Foley

noun
sound effects artist
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊli / (AmE), / ˈfəʊli / (BrE)
a person who creates or performs sound effects for film, television, or radio productions

Examples

Foley artist
The Foley added the sound of footsteps to make the scene more realistic.
A skilled Foley can create convincing rain sounds using just a few props.
During post-production, the Foley worked closely with the director to match every sound effect.

Related Terms

  • antonym: actor - a person who performs in films, television, or theater
  • synonym: sound designer - a person responsible for the overall sound in a production
