vacherin

noun
meringue dessert
9.8
Pronunciation: / ˌvæʃəˈræn / (AmE), / vaʃəˈrɛ̃ / (BrE)
a dessert made of crisp meringue filled or topped with whipped cream and ice cream, sometimes served frozen

Examples

A vacherin dessert
For dessert she ordered a rich vacherin topped with fresh berries and whipped cream.
The chef prepared a delicate vacherin with layers of meringue and vanilla ice cream.
Guests enjoyed the frozen vacherin at the end of the summer dinner party.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pavlova - a meringue-based dessert with cream and fruit
