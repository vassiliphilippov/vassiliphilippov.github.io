intranational

adjective
within one country
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntrəˈnæʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌɪntrəˈnæʃənəl / (BrE)

Examples

Economic growth can be influenced by both intranational and international factors.
The conference discussed intranational challenges such as regional inequality and infrastructure.
