drowse

verb
sleep lightly; doze
6.7 4.9
Pronunciation: /draʊz/ (AmE), /draʊz/ (BrE)
to sleep lightly or be half asleep, often for a short time

Examples

To drowse in a chair
I tend to drowse on trains, even if I'm not tired.

She began to drowse as the train gently rocked back and forth.
