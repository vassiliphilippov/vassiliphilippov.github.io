belowstairs

adjective
servants' quarters
Pronunciation: / bɪˈloʊˌstɛrz / (AmE), / bɪˈləʊˌstɛəz / (BrE)
relating to or characteristic of the servants' quarters or life in a large house, especially in the past

Examples

Belowstairs life
The belowstairs staff gathered in the kitchen to discuss the day's duties.
Her belowstairs upbringing shaped her understanding of the household's strict social order.
In old British novels, the belowstairs world is often filled with gossip and intrigue.

Related Terms

  • synonym: domestic - relating to household or home life
  • antonym: upstairs - relating to the upper floors, often where the family lives
