Anschluss

noun
connection or junction
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑnʃlʊs / (AmE), / ˈanʃlʊs / (BrE)
a connection, joining, or junction between two things, such as cables, pipes, or transport lines

Examples

Pipe Anschluss
The engineer checked the Anschluss between the two pipes for any leaks.
A secure Anschluss is essential for the safe operation of electrical equipment.
The train's Anschluss to the main line allowed for faster travel between cities.

Related Terms

  • synonym: connection - a point where two or more things are joined
  • synonym: junction - a place where things, such as roads or wires, join
  • antonym: separation - the act of moving things apart or keeping them unconnected
