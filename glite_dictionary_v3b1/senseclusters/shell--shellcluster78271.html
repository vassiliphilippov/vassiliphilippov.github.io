shell

verb
remove an outer covering or husk
5.8 2.1
Pronunciation: / ʃɛl / (AmE), / ʃɛl / (BrE)
to remove the shell or outer covering from something, such as nuts, peas, or seafood (or for seeds, to fall out of the pod or husk)
