rosé wine

noun
pinkish wine made from red grapes
5.2 2.8
Pronunciation: /roʊ.ˈzeɪ waɪn/ /; / /roʊz waɪn/ (AmE), /rəʊ.ˈzeɪ waɪn/ /; / /rəʊz waɪn/ (BrE)
A type of wine with a pinkish color, made from red grapes whose skins are removed early in the fermentation process.

Examples

A glass of rose wine
She prefers rosé wine over red or white, especially during summer.
The menu recommends pairing seafood dishes with a light rosé wine.
We enjoyed a chilled bottle of rosé wine on the terrace this afternoon.
