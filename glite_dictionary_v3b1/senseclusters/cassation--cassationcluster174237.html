cassation

noun
court of last resort
9.9
Pronunciation: /kə.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ /; / /æ.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (AmE), /kə.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ /; / /æ.ˈseɪ.ʃən/ (BrE)
a high court, especially in civil-law countries, that reviews and can overturn lower-court decisions

Examples

Cassation court decision
The case was brought before the cassation after several appeals failed.
Lawyers argued that only the cassation could overturn the controversial verdict.
In some countries, the cassation serves as the final authority on legal disputes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: appeals court - a court that hears appeals against lower court decisions
  • synonym: supreme court - the highest judicial court in a country or state
