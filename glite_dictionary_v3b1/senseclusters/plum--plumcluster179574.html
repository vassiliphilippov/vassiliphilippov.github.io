plum

noun
desirable job or assignment
Pronunciation: / plʌm / (AmE), / plʌm / (BrE)
a job, position, or assignment that is very desirable or rewarding, often because it comes with special advantages or privileges

Examples

A plum job
After years of working late, she finally landed a plum job at the top firm.
She was thrilled to receive a plum assignment in the marketing department.
The ambassador’s post is considered a plum for any diplomat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: prize - something earned or won as a reward
