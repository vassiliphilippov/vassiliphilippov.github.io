donna

noun
Italian woman of rank or title
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdɑːnə / (AmE), / ˈdɒnə / (BrE)
A title of respect for an Italian woman of rank, particularly in historical or formal contexts.

Examples

A donna of rank
At the opera, she was greeted as if she were a true donna.
Renaissance painters often depicted noble donnas in their works.
The famous donna of Venice was renowned for her beauty and intelligence.

Related Terms

  • antonym: don - a title for an Italian or Spanish man of high rank
  • synonym: signora - a polite form of address for an Italian woman, especially a married woman
