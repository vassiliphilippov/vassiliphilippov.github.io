eventful

adjective
full of significant events
Pronunciation: / ɪˈvɛntfəl /; / ɪˈvɛntfl̩ / (AmE), / ɪˈvɛntf(ʊ)l /; / ɪˈvɛntfʊl / (BrE)
full of important, interesting, or unusual events

Examples

Our trip to the city turned out to be quite eventful after we missed our train.
The play’s eventful plot kept the audience engaged from start to finish.
