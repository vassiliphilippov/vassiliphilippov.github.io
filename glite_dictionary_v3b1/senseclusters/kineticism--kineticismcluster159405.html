kineticism

noun
quality of movement
9.5
Pronunciation: /kɪ.ˈne.tɪ.zəm/ (AmE), /kɪ.ˈne.tɪ.zəm/ (BrE)
the quality, theory, or practice of using movement or motion, especially as a key principle in art or science
