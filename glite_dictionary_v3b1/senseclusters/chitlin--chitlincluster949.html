chitlin

noun
pig intestines dish
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɪtlɪn / (AmE), / ˈtʃɪtlɪn / (BrE)
the small intestines of a pig, cleaned, cooked, and eaten as food, especially in Southern U.S. cuisine
