ulnar vein

noun
vein in the medial forearm
Pronunciation: / ˈʌlnɚ veɪn / (AmE), / ˈʌlnə veɪn / (BrE)
any of several veins in the forearm accompanying the ulnar artery, draining blood from the medial side of the forearm.

Examples

The ulnar vein in arm
An ultrasound revealed a blood clot in his ulnar vein.
During surgery, the ulnar vein was carefully preserved to maintain proper circulation.
The doctor explained how the ulnar vein works together with arteries to support blood flow.

Related Terms

  • antonym: radial vein - a vein of the forearm on the lateral side
  • synonym: ulnar artery - an artery running alongside the ulnar vein in the forearm
