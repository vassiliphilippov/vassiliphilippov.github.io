Pedilanthus tithymaloides

noun
a tropical American shrub with emetic properties
Pronunciation: / ˌpiːdɪˈlænθəs ˌtɪθɪməˈlɔɪdiːz / (AmE), / ˌpiːdɪˈlænθəs ˌtɪθɪməˈlɔɪdiːz / (BrE)
