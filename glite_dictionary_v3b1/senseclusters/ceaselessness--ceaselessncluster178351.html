ceaselessness

noun
continuous, unending quality
6.2
Pronunciation: / ˈsiːsləsnəs / (AmE), / ˈsiːslɪsnəs / (BrE)
the state or quality of continuing without pause or ending

Examples

The ceaselessness of rain
He admired her ceaselessness in the face of constant obstacles at work.
The ceaselessness of the rain made it impossible to leave the house all weekend.
There is a certain beauty in the ceaselessness of the ocean waves.

Related Terms

  • synonym: continuity - the unbroken and consistent existence or operation of something
  • antonym: interruption - the action of stopping something for a period of time
