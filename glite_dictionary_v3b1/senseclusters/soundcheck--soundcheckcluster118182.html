soundcheck

noun
audio equipment test
Pronunciation: / ˈsaʊndˌtʃɛk / (AmE), / ˈsaʊndˌtʃɛk / (BrE)
a test of sound equipment and levels before a performance or event to ensure everything works properly

Examples

Pre-show soundcheck
The band arrived early for their soundcheck before the concert.
Technicians completed the soundcheck to make sure the microphones and speakers were working.
Without a proper soundcheck, the performance could have serious audio problems.
