touch wood

verb
said to avoid bad luck
9.9
Pronunciation: / tʌtʃ wʊd / (AmE), / tʌtʃ wʊd / (BrE)
Said when one wants to prevent bad luck after mentioning something positive or hopeful, often while physically touching wood.

Examples

To touch wood for luck
I haven't caught a cold all winter, touch wood.
Our travel plans are still on schedule, touch wood.
We've never lost a match here, touch wood.

Related Terms

  • synonym: knock on wood - North American equivalent of 'touch wood', used to avoid bad luck
