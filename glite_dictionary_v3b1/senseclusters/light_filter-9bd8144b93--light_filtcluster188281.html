light filter

noun
transparent device reducing certain light
1.0 3.5
Pronunciation: /laɪt ˈfɪl.tər/ (AmE), /laɪt ˈfɪl.tə/ (BrE)
a device or material that is transparent and reduces the amount or certain wavelengths of light passing through it

Examples

A light filter device
A red light filter can enhance contrast in black and white photography.
Scientists often use a light filter when observing samples under a microscope.
The photographer attached a light filter to his camera lens to reduce glare.
