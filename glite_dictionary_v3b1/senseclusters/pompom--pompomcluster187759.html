pompom

noun
fluffy decorative ball
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑmˌpɑm / (AmE), / ˈpɒmˌpɒm / (BrE)
a small ball of fluffy material used as a decoration or waved by cheerleaders at events

Examples

Cheerleader pompom
The cheerleader waved a bright pompom during the game.
She attached a colorful pompom to her winter hat for extra flair.
Children made crafts using yarn to create their own pompoms in art class.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tassel - a bunch of threads tied together at one end and used as a decoration
