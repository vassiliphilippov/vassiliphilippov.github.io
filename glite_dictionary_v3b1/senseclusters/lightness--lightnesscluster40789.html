lightness

noun
quality of being illuminated or luminous
Pronunciation: / ˈlaɪtnəs / (AmE), / ˈlaɪtnəs / (BrE)
the quality of being luminous or illuminated in an object or scene, especially as represented in art or photographs

Examples

Lightness in a photo
Adjust the lightness of the image for a softer appearance.
In photography, the lightness of a subject can dramatically change the mood of a picture.
The artist skillfully used lightness and shadow to convey depth in the painting.

Related Terms

  • synonym: brightness - the attribute of being full of light or shining strongly
  • antonym: darkness - the absence or low level of illumination
