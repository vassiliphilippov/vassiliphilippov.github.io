grade

noun
group of students at the same school level
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɡreɪd / (AmE), / ɡreɪd / (BrE)
a group of students who are taught together and are usually at the same level in school, such as the first grade, second grade, etc.

Examples

Fifth grade class
He made many friends when he joined a new grade after moving to the city.
My daughter is in the third grade this year and loves her teacher.
Students in each grade follow a different curriculum suited to their level.
