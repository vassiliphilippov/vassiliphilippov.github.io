defile sacred ground

verb
violate a holy place
5.7
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfaɪl ˈseɪkrəd ˈɡraʊnd / (AmE), / dɪˈfaɪl ˈseɪkrɪd ˈɡraʊnd / (BrE)
to violate or show disrespect to a place regarded as holy or spiritually significant

Examples

To defile sacred ground
Archaeologists took special precautions to avoid accidentally defiling sacred ground during their excavation.
Many believe it is a great sin to defile sacred ground with violence or destruction.
The construction project was halted after locals claimed it would defile sacred ground.
