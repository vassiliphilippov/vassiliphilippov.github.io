completion

noun
generated output from a prompt

Pronunciation: /kəm.ˈpliː.ʃən/ (AmE), /kəm.ˈpliː.ʃən/ (BrE)
(in computing, especially AI and text/code generation) the output produced by a model or program in response to a prompt; a generated continuation such as text, code, or data.
