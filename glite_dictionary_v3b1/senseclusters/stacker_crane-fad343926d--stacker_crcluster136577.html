stacker crane

noun
crane for automated warehouse storage/retrieval
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈstækər kreɪn / (AmE), / ˈstækə kreɪn / (BrE)
a crane equipped with a forklift-like mechanism, used in automated warehouses to store and retrieve goods under computer control

Examples

Stacker crane in warehouse
A stacker crane can operate in narrow aisles, maximizing warehouse space usage.
Modern distribution centers often use stacker cranes to move pallets quickly and efficiently.
The company invested in a new stacker crane to automate its storage system.
