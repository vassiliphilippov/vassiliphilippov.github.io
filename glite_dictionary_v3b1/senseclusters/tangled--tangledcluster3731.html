tangled

adjective
twisted together in a confused mass
1.3
Pronunciation: / ˈtæŋɡəld / (AmE), / ˈtæŋɡəld / (BrE)
twisted together in a confused or disordered mass, making it difficult to separate or untangle

Examples

Tangled hair
Branches and vines formed a tangled barrier across the narrow path.
Her tangled hair took a long time to brush in the morning.
The old fisherman struggled with the tangled nets on the boat.

Related Terms

  • synonym: matted - twisted or sticking together in a thick mass, especially hair or wool
