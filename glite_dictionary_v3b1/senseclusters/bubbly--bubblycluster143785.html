bubbly

adjective
cheerful and lively (personality)
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈbʌbli / (AmE), / ˈbʌbli / (BrE)
(of a person) cheerful, lively, and full of high spirits.

Examples

A bubbly personality

Her bubbly personality made her popular at every party she attended.
The new teacher has a bubbly energy that keeps her students engaged.
