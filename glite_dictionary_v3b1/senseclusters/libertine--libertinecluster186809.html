libertine

adjective
unrestrained by moral convention
5.8
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪbərˌtin / (AmE), / ˈlɪbətiːn / (BrE)
Showing or characterized by a disregard for moral conventions, especially in sexual matters; unrestrained by conventional or moral rules.

Examples

Libertine behavior
He was known for his libertine lifestyle, ignoring the strict codes of his family.
Her libertine attitude shocked her more conventional friends.
The author's libertine characters scandalized Victorian society.

Related Terms

  • synonym: debauched - indulging in or characterized by sensual pleasures to excess
  • synonym: dissolute - lax in morals; licentious
  • antonym: pure - free from moral fault or guilt; innocent
