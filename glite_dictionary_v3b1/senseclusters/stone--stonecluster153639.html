stone

noun
emotional hardness or unresponsiveness (figurative)
9.9
Pronunciation: / stoʊn / (AmE), / stəʊn / (BrE)
used figuratively to refer to a lack of feeling, expression, or movement; emotional unresponsiveness or hardness.

Examples

Stone heart
After years in politics, he developed a stone where his heart should be.
She spoke with a stone in her voice, unmoved by his plea.
The judge's stone was evident when she showed no reaction to the sobbing defendant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cold - emotionally distant; lacking in warmth or feeling
  • synonym: heart of stone - an idiom meaning a very unfeeling or cruel nature
