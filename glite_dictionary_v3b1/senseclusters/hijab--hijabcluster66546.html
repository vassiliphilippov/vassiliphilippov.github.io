hijab

noun
Muslim women's hair and neck covering
5.0 5.2
Pronunciation: / hɪˈdʒɑb /; / hɪˈdʒɑːb / (AmE), / hɪˈdʒɑːb / (BrE)
a headscarf or covering worn by some Muslim women, or the practice of wearing it, as a sign of modesty
