rainspout

noun
roof water pipe
Pronunciation: / ˈreɪnˌspaʊt / (AmE), / ˈreɪnˌspaʊt / (BrE)
a pipe or channel attached to a building that carries rainwater from the roof to the ground

Examples

A rainspout pipe
After the storm, leaves clogged the rainspout and caused water to overflow.
The old house needed a new rainspout to prevent water from damaging the foundation.
Children watched as water gushed from the rainspout during the afternoon rain.

Related Terms

  • synonym: downspout - a pipe that carries rainwater from a gutter down to the ground
  • synonym: gutter - a channel at the edge of a roof for carrying off rainwater
