affirmance

noun
expression of support
Pronunciation: / əˈfɜrməns / (AmE), / əˈfɜːməns / (BrE)
an act, word, or gesture that gives support, confidence, or encouragement to someone

Examples

Teacher's affirmance
The teacher's affirmance helped the student feel more confident about her presentation.
Receiving regular affirmance from friends can boost your self-esteem.
His kind words of affirmance made a big difference during a difficult time.

Related Terms

  • synonym: affirmation - a statement or action showing support or approval
  • antonym: criticism - the act of expressing disapproval or finding fault
  • synonym: encouragement - the act of giving someone support or confidence
