berserk

noun
frenzied Norse warrior
7.2
Pronunciation: / bərˈzɜrk / (AmE), / bəˈzɜːk / (BrE)
A member of a group of ancient Norse warriors who worked themselves into a wild frenzy before battle and fought with reckless savagery.

Examples

A Viking berserk
Legends say a true berserk felt no steel and knew no fear in battle.
Stories of the berserks describe their trance-like fury and disregard for pain.
The berserk charged into battle, feared by both enemies and allies.
