submissiveness

noun
willingness to yield or comply
Pronunciation: / səbˈmɪs.ɪv.nəs / (AmE), / səbˈmɪs.ɪv.nəs / (BrE)
the quality of being willing to accept the authority or will of another person; readiness to yield or comply.

Examples

Her submissiveness to orders
Her submissiveness made her an easy target for aggressive personalities at work.
Some cultures place a high value on submissiveness in relationships and social interactions.
The trainer praised the dog's submissiveness during the obedience class.

Related Terms

  • antonym: defiance - open resistance; bold disobedience
  • synonym: docility - the quality of being easily taught, led, or managed
  • synonym: obedience - the act of complying with a command or request
