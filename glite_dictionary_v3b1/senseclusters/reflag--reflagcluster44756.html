reflag

verb
to change ship's registry
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈflæɡ / (AmE), / ˌriːˈflæɡ / (BrE)
to assign a new flag or country of registration to a ship, or to mark something again with a flag

Examples

To reflag a ship
The shipping company decided to reflag its fleet to benefit from lower taxes.
After new regulations were introduced, several vessels were quickly reflagged to another country.
Authorities may require owners to reflag their boats if they move to a different jurisdiction.

Related Terms

  • antonym: de-register - to remove a ship from a country's official registry
  • synonym: register - to officially record or enroll a ship under a country's registry
