blue fig

noun
Australian tree with blue fruit
7.9
Pronunciation: / bluː fɪɡ / (AmE), / bluː fɪɡ / (BrE)
An Australian tree with hard white timber, glossy green leaves, white flowers, and small glossy blue fruits containing a single seed.

Examples

Blue fig tree
Birds are attracted to the blue fig when its bright fruit ripens in summer.
Several blue fig trees were planted along the nature reserve trail.
The blue fig is valued for its attractive blue fruit and strong timber.
