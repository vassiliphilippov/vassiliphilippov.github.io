trading card

noun
digital collectible card (e.g., NFT)
3.5
Pronunciation: /ˈtreɪ.dɪŋ kɑːrd/ (AmE), /ˈtreɪ.dɪŋ kɑːd/ (BrE)
a digital collectible card used in online platforms or games, often represented as a unique token (for example, an NFT), that can be collected, traded, or used in gameplay.

Examples

Digital trading card

Players traded their limited-edition trading cards to build stronger decks for the online tournament.
After the drop, she sold a rare trading card for cryptocurrency and bought new artwork.
