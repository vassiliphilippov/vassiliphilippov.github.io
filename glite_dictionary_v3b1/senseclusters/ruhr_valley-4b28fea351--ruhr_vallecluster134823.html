Ruhr Valley

proper noun
industrial region in northwestern Germany
Pronunciation: / rʊr ˈvæli / (AmE), / rʊə ˈvæli / (BrE)
an industrial and coal mining region in the valley of the Ruhr River in northwestern Germany, noted for its heavy industry and economic significance

Examples

The Ruhr Valley region
Coal production in the Ruhr Valley peaked before environmental regulations led to changes in the region's economy.
During the early 20th century, the Ruhr Valley was a focal point of economic and political significance in Europe.
The Ruhr Valley became the industrial heartland of Germany during the 19th century.
