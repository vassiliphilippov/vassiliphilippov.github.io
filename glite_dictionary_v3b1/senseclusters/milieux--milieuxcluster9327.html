milieux

noun
social environments
Pronunciation: / miːlˈjɜːz / (AmE), / miːlˈjɜːz / (BrE)
the plural form of 'milieu', meaning social or cultural environments in which people live or interact

Examples

Artistic milieux
She moved easily between different artistic milieux during her career.
Writers often draw inspiration from the various milieux they have experienced.
The city is known for its diverse cultural milieux that attract creative people.

Related Terms

  • synonym: background - the circumstances or situation in which something exists
  • synonym: milieu - a social or cultural environment
  • synonym: setting - the place or type of surroundings where something is positioned
