pectoralis

noun
chest muscle (anatomy)
9.9 5.7
Pronunciation: /pek.ˈtɔːr.əl.ɪs/ /; / /pek.ˈtɑːr.əl.ɪs/ (AmE), /pek.ˈtɒr.əl.ɪs/ /; / /pek.ˈtɔːr.əl.ɪs/ (BrE)

Examples
