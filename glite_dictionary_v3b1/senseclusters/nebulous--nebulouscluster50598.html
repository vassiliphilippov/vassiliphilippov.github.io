nebulous

adjective
relating to a nebula; diffuse or cloudy
Pronunciation: / ˈnɛb.jə.ləs / (AmE), / ˈnɛb.jʊ.ləs / (BrE)
Relating to or resembling a nebula; cloudy, hazy, or diffuse in appearance, especially in reference to astronomy.

Examples

Nebulous clouds in space
The astronomer described the nebulous shapes visible in the deep-space images.
This region of the galaxy is filled with nebulous clouds of gas and dust.
Traditionally, the solar system's formation was explained by the nebulous hypothesis.
