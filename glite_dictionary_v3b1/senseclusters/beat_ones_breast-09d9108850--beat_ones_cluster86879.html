beat one's breast

verb
express deep regret or sorrow
9.6 7.2
Pronunciation: /biːt wʌnz brest/ (AmE), /biːt wʌnz brest/ (BrE)
to express deep regret, sorrow, or remorse, often by striking oneself on the chest as a traditional sign of contrition

Examples

To beat his breast sadly
Amidst his guilt, he beat his breast and begged for forgiveness.
She beat her breast in grief after hearing the tragic news.
They beat their breast and wept during the solemn ceremony.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lament - to express passionate grief or sorrow
  • antonym: rejoice - to feel or show great joy or delight
