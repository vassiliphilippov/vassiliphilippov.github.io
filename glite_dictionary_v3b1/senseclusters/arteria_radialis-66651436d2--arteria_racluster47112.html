arteria radialis

noun
radial artery of the forearm
5.7
Pronunciation: / ɑːrˈtɪəriə rəˈdeɪliɪs / (AmE), / ɑːˈtɪərɪə rəˈdeɪlɪɪs / (BrE)
the anatomical term for the radial artery, a branch of the brachial artery that runs from below the elbow along the forearm to the wrist and palm

Examples

The arteria radialis artery
During the procedure, the surgeon carefully avoided damaging the arteria radialis.
Knowledge of the arteria radialis is essential for medical students studying the circulatory system.
The arteria radialis is often used to measure a person's pulse near the wrist.
