cherrystone clam

noun
medium-sized edible hard clam
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɛrˌstoʊn klæm /; / ˈtʃɛriˌstoʊn klæm / (AmE), / ˈtʃɛəˌstəʊn klam /; / ˈtʃɛriˌstəʊn klam / (BrE)
a medium-sized hard clam (quahog), larger than a littleneck but smaller than a chowder clam, commonly eaten cooked or raw
