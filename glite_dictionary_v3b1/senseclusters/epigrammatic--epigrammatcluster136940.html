epigrammatic

adjective
concise and witty; like an epigram
Pronunciation: / ˌɛpɪˌɡræˈmætɪk / (AmE), / ˌɛpɪɡrəˈmætɪk / (BrE)
expressed in a concise and witty manner, like an epigram

Examples

Epigrammatic humor
He is known for his epigrammatic humor, always ready with a clever, concise response.
Her writing style is epigrammatic, packing sharp observations into just a few words.
The professor delivered epigrammatic remarks that made the entire class laugh and think.

Related Terms

  • synonym: aphoristic - expressed in a concise and pithy manner
  • antonym: verbose - using more words than are needed
