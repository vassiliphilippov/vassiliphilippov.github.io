chartered

adjective
hired for exclusive temporary use
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃɑrtərd / (AmE), / ˈtʃɑːtəd / (BrE)
(of a vehicle or service) hired for the exclusive temporary use of a person or group, especially for transportation

Examples

A chartered coach
The school hired a chartered coach to take students to the museum.
Their company arranged a chartered plane for the conference attendees.
We traveled on a chartered bus for the entire trip.
After the ceremony, guests traveled together in a chartered coach back to the city.
The tour group arrived at the airport in a chartered bus that picked them up from their hotel.
They took a chartered flight to the remote island, organized especially for the event.

Related Terms

  • synonym: leased - hired or rented for a period of time
  • synonym: private - intended for or restricted to the use of a particular person or group
  • antonym: public - open to or shared by all people; not restricted
