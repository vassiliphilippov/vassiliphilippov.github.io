zoom lens

noun
camera lens with adjustable magnification
1.9 3.1
Pronunciation: /zuːm lenz/ (AmE), /zuːm lenz/ (BrE)
a camera lens with a variable focal length that allows the photographer to change magnification without changing lenses

Examples

A zoom lens camera
Modern cameras often come equipped with a versatile zoom lens as standard.
The photographer switched to a zoom lens to capture the action from a distance.
Using a zoom lens, she brought distant objects sharply into focus.
