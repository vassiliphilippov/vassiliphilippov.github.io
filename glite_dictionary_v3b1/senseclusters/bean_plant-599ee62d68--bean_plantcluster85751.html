bean plant

noun
plant grown for edible beans or pods
5.3 1.3
Pronunciation: /biːn plænt/ (AmE), /biːn plɑːnt/ (BrE)
Any of various leguminous plants cultivated for their edible seeds or pods, such as the common bean or broad bean.
