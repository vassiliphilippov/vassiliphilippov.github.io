yuk

verb
to laugh loudly
8.6
Pronunciation: / jʌk / (AmE), / jʌk / (BrE)
to laugh loudly or in a silly, boisterous way

Examples

To yuk at jokes
The group yukked at every joke the host made during the party.
They yuk together whenever they watch old comedy movies.
She yukked so loudly that everyone in the restaurant turned to look.
The kids yukked at the clown's silly tricks during the party.
She always yuks when she hears a really bad pun.
They yukked so much at the movie that people turned to look at them.
The kids were yukking so loudly that the teacher had to ask them to quiet down.
They yukked it up at the party, making everyone around them laugh too.
Whenever he hears a silly joke, he can't help but yuk uncontrollably.
