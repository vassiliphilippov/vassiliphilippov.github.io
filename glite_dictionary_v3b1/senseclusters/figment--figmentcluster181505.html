figment

noun
something imagined or invented
3.3 5.2
Pronunciation: /ˈfɪg.mənt/ (AmE), /ˈfɪg.mənt/ (BrE)
Something invented, imagined, or made up, especially something unreal or fictitious.

Examples

A figment of imagination
Her story about being chased by aliens was dismissed as a figment of her imagination.
Many believe the monster in the lake is just a figment of local legend and not real.
The rumor was nothing more than a figment created to stir up excitement.
