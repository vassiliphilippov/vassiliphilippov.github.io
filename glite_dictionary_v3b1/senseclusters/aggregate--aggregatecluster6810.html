aggregate

adjective

(of a fruit or structure) formed of a dense cluster of separate units, such as carpels, florets, or drupelets.

Examples

Aggregate fruit
An aggregate flower head is composed of many small florets.
Raspberries are classified as aggregate fruits due to their clustered drupelets.
Some plants produce aggregate seed structures, each originating from a separate ovary.
