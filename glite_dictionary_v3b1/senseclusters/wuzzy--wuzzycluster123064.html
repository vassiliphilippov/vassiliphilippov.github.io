wuzzy

adjective
soft and fuzzy
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈwʌzi / (AmE), / ˈwʌzi / (BrE)
soft and fuzzy in texture or feeling, giving a cozy or cuddly impression

Examples

Wuzzy blanket
She wrapped herself in a wuzzy blanket on the cold night.
The kitten's wuzzy fur made it irresistible to cuddle.
He bought a pair of wuzzy slippers to keep his feet warm in winter.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cozy - giving a feeling of comfort, warmth, and relaxation
  • synonym: fuzzy - having a soft, fine texture like fur or short hair
  • antonym: rough - having an uneven or harsh texture
