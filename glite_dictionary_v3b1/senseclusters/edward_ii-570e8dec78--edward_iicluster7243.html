Edward II

proper noun
King of England, 1307–1327
Pronunciation: / ˈɛdwərd ðə ˈsɛkənd / (AmE), / ˈɛdwəd ðə ˈsɛkənd / (BrE)
(1284–1327) King of England from 1307 to 1327, noted for his defeat at Bannockburn and eventual deposition.

Examples

Edward II of England
Edward II faced a famous defeat at the Battle of Bannockburn against the Scots.
