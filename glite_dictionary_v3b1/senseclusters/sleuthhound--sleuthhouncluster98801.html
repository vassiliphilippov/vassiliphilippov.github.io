sleuthhound

noun
persistent detective or investigator
Pronunciation: / ˈsluːθˌhaʊnd / (AmE), / ˈsluːθˌhaʊnd / (BrE)
A detective, especially one who persistently follows clues and investigates mysteries.

Examples

A clever sleuthhound
Her reputation as a sleuthhound grew with each solved mystery.
Journalists acted like sleuthhounds, sniffing out every detail of the scandal.
The private sleuthhound tracked down the missing heir with dogged determination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: detective - a person, usually a police officer, whose job is to investigate crimes and catch criminals
