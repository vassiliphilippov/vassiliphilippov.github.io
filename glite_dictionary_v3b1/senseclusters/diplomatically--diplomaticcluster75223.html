diplomatically

adverb
in a diplomatic or tactful manner
1.3 4.4
Pronunciation: /dɪ.plə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kliː/ /; / /dɪ.plə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kə.liː/ (AmE), /dɪ.plə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kliː/ /; / /dɪ.plə.ˈmæt.ɪ.kə.liː/ (BrE)
In a manner that shows skill in dealing with people or situations without causing bad feelings; with tact and sensitivity.

Examples

To act diplomatically
He handled the difficult negotiations very diplomatically.
It's important to phrase your feedback diplomatically in a professional setting.
She responded diplomatically to his criticism, avoiding any argument.

Related Terms

  • antonym: bluntly - in a very direct way, often without considering other people's feelings
  • synonym: tactfully - in a way that shows sensitivity and skill in dealing with others or with difficult issues
