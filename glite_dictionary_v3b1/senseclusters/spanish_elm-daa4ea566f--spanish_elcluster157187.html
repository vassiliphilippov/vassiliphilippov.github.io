Spanish elm

noun
tropical American timber tree
9.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspænɪʃ ɛlm / (AmE), / ˈspænɪʃ ɛlm /; / ˈspanɪʃ ɛlm / (BrE)
a large tropical American tree (Cordia alliodora) valued for its strong, durable wood and its creamy white flowers
