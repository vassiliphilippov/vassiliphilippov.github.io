milliamp

noun
unit of electric current
4.0
Pronunciation: /ˈmɪl.ɪ.æmp/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ə.æmp/ (AmE), /ˈmɪl.ɪ.æmp/ /; / /ˈmɪl.ə.æmp/ (BrE)

Examples

A milliamp unit
The device requires only a few milliamps to function efficiently.
A current of 10 milliamps is considered safe for most electronic circuits.
The LED draws less than one milliamp when operating at low brightness.
