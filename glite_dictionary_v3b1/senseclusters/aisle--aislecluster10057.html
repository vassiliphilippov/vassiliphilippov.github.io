aisle

verb
to add aisles
9.6
Pronunciation: / aɪld / (AmE), / aɪld / (BrE)
to provide or arrange a building or space with one or more aisles, or to divide into aisles.

Examples

To aisle a hall
The architect aisled the church to create separate walkways for the congregation.
They aisled the exhibition hall to make it easier for visitors to navigate.
The renovated supermarket was aisled to improve the flow of shoppers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: partition - to divide a space into separate sections
