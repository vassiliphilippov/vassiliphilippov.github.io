multiview

adjective
showing multiple views
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (AmE), / ˈmʌltiˌvjuː / (BrE)
involving or displaying multiple views or perspectives at the same time

Examples

Multiview display mode
The new television offers a multiview feature, allowing you to watch several channels at once.
Architects use multiview drawings to present different angles of a building design.
The software's multiview mode helps editors compare multiple video clips side by side.

Related Terms

  • synonym: panoramic - showing a wide or complete view
  • antonym: single-view - showing only one view or perspective
