seaside

noun
area by the sea, often a resort
1.1 2.67/m
Pronunciation: / ËˆsiËŒsaÉªd / (AmE), / ËˆsiËsaÉªd / (BrE)

Examples

The seaside town

Walking along the seaside promenade in the evening was the highlight of our trip.
We spent our summer holiday at the seaside enjoying the fresh air and sunshine.

    1.0 35/m
    coast is the long area of land beside the sea in general, while seaside is the specific place people visit for holidays and fun.
