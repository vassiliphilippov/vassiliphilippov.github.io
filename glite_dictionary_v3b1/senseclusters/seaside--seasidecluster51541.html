seaside

noun
area by the sea, often a resort
1.5
Pronunciation: / ˈsiˌsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈsiːsaɪd / (BrE)
the area near the sea, especially where people go for holidays or vacations

Examples

The seaside town
Many families visit the seaside town every year for its sandy beaches and attractions.
Walking along the seaside promenade in the evening was the highlight of our trip.
We spent our summer holiday at the seaside enjoying the fresh air and sunshine.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coast - the land next to the sea; seashore
  • antonym: inland - situated away from the sea or coast
