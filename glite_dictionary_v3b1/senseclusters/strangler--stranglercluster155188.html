strangler

noun
person who kills by strangling
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈstræŋɡlər / (AmE), / ˈstræŋɡlə / (BrE)
a person who kills someone by squeezing their throat tightly so that they cannot breathe

Examples

A serial strangler
Newspapers dubbed him the 'Midtown Strangler' because of his crimes.
The city was terrified when another strangler attack was reported.
The police finally caught the strangler after months of investigation.
