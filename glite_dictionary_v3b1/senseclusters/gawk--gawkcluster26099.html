gawk

verb
to stare open-mouthed or stupidly
Pronunciation: / ɡɑk / (AmE), / ɡɔːk / (BrE)
to stare openly and stupidly; to look at someone or something with awkward amazement or curiosity

Examples

To gawk at people
Please don't gawk—it's impolite to stare at people like that.
She couldn't help but gawk in amazement at the celebrity walking by.
Tourists would often gawk at the towering skyscrapers in the city center.

Related Terms

  • antonym: glance - to take a brief or hurried look
  • synonym: ogle - to stare at someone in a way that shows sexual attraction
  • synonym: stare - to look fixedly or vacantly at someone or something
