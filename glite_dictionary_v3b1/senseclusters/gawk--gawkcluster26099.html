gawk

verb
to stare open-mouthed or stupidly
3.6 5.0
Pronunciation: /gɔːk/ (AmE), /gɔːk/ (BrE)
to stare openly and stupidly; to look at someone or something with awkward amazement or curiosity

Tourists would often gawk at the towering skyscrapers in the city center.
