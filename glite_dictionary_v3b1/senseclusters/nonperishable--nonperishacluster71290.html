nonperishable

adjective
not likely to spoil
3.2
Pronunciation: / ˌnɑnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl /; / nɑnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl / (AmE), / ˌnɒnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl /; / nɒnˈpɛrɪʃəbəl / (BrE)
not likely to spoil, decay, or go bad quickly; able to last a long time without deteriorating (especially of food)
