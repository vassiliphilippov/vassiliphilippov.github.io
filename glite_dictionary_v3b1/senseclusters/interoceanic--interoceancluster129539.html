interoceanic

adjective
between oceans
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəroʊˈʃænɪk / (AmE), / ˌɪntərəʊˈsiːænɪk / (BrE)
connecting, existing, or occurring between two or more oceans

Examples

An interoceanic railway
The construction of an interoceanic railway would greatly improve global trade routes.
An interoceanic canal can shorten the journey for ships traveling between continents.
Scientists study interoceanic currents to understand how water moves between the world's oceans.

  • synonym: transoceanic - crossing or spanning an ocean
