hand out

verb
to ask or solicit for something
3.3
Pronunciation: / hænd aʊt / (AmE), / hænd aʊt / (BrE)
to solicit or ask for something, especially in expectation of receiving a gift, favor, or monetary help (often used as 'have one's hand out')

Examples

Hand out for money
At city hall, he always has his hand out for funding opportunities.
She's not afraid to put her hand out when she needs help.
The organization had its hand out to sponsors for support.

Related Terms

  • synonym: request - to politely or formally ask for something
