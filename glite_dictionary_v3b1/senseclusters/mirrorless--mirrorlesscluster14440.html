mirrorless

adjective
without a camera mirror
Pronunciation: / ˈmɪrərləs / (AmE), / ˈmɪrələs / (BrE)
describing a camera that does not use a mirror in its design, typically resulting in a lighter and more compact body

Examples

Mirrorless camera model
Many professionals now use mirrorless cameras for travel photography because they are lighter than traditional models.
The new mirrorless model offers fast autofocus and excellent image quality.
She switched from a DSLR to a mirrorless camera to reduce the weight of her gear.

Related Terms

  • antonym: DSLR - a digital camera that uses a mirror and prism system
  • synonym: compact - small and easy to carry
