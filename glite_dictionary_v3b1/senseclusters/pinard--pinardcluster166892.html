pinard

noun
fetal stethoscope
Pronunciation: / piˈnɑrd / (AmE), / piˈnɑːd / (BrE)
a simple, hollow, trumpet-shaped instrument used by midwives and doctors to listen to a fetus's heartbeat during pregnancy

Examples

A pinard stethoscope
The midwife placed the pinard on the woman's belly to check the baby's heartbeat.
A traditional pinard is often made of wood and shaped like a small horn.
Doctors in some countries still use a pinard for prenatal examinations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fetoscope - a device for listening to fetal heart sounds
  • synonym: stethoscope - an instrument used to listen to sounds in the body
