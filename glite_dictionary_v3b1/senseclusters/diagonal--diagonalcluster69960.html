diagonal

adjective
having a slanted or oblique direction
7.7 2.7
Pronunciation: / daɪˈæɡənl̩ /; / daɪˈæɡənəl / (AmE), / daɪˈæɡənl̩ /; / daɪˈaɡən(ə)l / (BrE)
slanted or at an angle; not at a right angle to the sides of a shape
