acrophobic

adjective
afraid of heights
Pronunciation: / ˌækrəˈfoʊbɪk / (AmE), / ˌækrəˈfəʊbɪk / (BrE)
Suffering from acrophobia; having an abnormal fear of high places.

Examples

Acrophobic person
As an acrophobic person, she avoided glass elevators at all costs.
His acrophobic tendencies made mountain climbing impossible.
The acrophobic tourist stayed far from the edge of the observation deck.

Related Terms

  • synonym: acrophobia - an extreme or irrational fear of heights
