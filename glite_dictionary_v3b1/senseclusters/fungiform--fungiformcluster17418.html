fungiform

adjective
mushroom-shaped
Pronunciation: / ˈfʌndʒɪˌfɔrm / (AmE), / ˈfʌndʒɪfɔːm / (BrE)
having a shape similar to a mushroom, especially used to describe certain anatomical structures.

Examples

Fungiform papillae
The doctor explained that the fungiform papillae on the tongue are important for tasting food.
Some sea creatures have fungiform structures that help them blend into their environment.
The scientist noted the fungiform appearance of the new cell growth under the microscope.
