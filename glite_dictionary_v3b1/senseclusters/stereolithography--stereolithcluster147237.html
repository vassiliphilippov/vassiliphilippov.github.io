stereolithography

noun
3D printing format
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˌstɛrioʊlɪˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / ˌstɪəriəʊlɪˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
a method or file format for representing the surface geometry of 3D objects, widely used in 3D printing technology

Examples

Stereolithography file format
The designer exported the 3D model as a stereolithography file for the printer.
Many 3D printers accept stereolithography files because they accurately describe object surfaces.
Learning about stereolithography helped her understand how digital models become physical objects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: 3D printing - the process of making three-dimensional objects from digital files
  • synonym: CAD - computer-aided design, the use of computers to create precise drawings or models
