Examples

Gula goddess healing
According to ancient texts, Gula was revered as the goddess who could cure diseases.
Legend says that Gula often worked alongside Ninurta, her husband, in healing rituals.
Priests and priestesses dedicated temples to Gula in several Babylonian cities.
