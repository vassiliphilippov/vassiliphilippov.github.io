refi

verb
to refinance a loan
8.2
Pronunciation: / ˈriːfaɪ / (AmE), / ˈriːfaɪ / (BrE)
to get a new loan to replace an existing one, usually to obtain better terms or a lower interest rate

Examples

To refi a mortgage
They chose to refi their home loan when interest rates dropped significantly.
After talking to her bank, she decided to refi to reduce her monthly payments.
Many homeowners consider whether to refi when market conditions change.
