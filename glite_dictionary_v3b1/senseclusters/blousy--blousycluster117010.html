blousy

adjective
rounded and plump
7.0
Pronunciation: / ˈblaʊzi / (AmE), / ˈblaʊzi / (BrE)
having a soft, rounded, and slightly full or plump appearance, especially of a face or figure

Examples

Blousy cheeks
Her blousy cheeks gave her a cheerful and healthy look.
The artist painted a woman with a blousy figure, full of warmth and life.
After the long winter, his face appeared more blousy than before.

  • antonym: slender - thin in an attractive or graceful way
