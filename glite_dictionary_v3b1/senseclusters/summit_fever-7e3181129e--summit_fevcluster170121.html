summit fever

noun
an obsession with reaching a mountain summit
8.1 7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈsʌmɪt ˈfiːvɚ / (AmE), / ˈsʌmɪt ˈfiːvə / (BrE)
an intense desire or obsession with reaching the top of a mountain, sometimes causing climbers to take excessive risks

Examples

Summit fever in climbers
After her first successful ascent, she developed summit fever and began planning more expeditions.
His summit fever blinded him to the dangers of worsening weather conditions.
Many climbers suffer from summit fever and ignore warning signs of exhaustion.
