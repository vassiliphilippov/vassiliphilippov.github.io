vegetable tallow

noun
waxy fat from plants used as tallow
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈvɛdʒtəbl ˈtæloʊ / (AmE), / ˈvɛdʒtəbəl ˈtæləʊ / (BrE)
a waxy fat derived from certain plants, such as bayberry, used similarly to animal tallow

Examples

Vegetable tallow from bayberry
Harvesting bayberry for vegetable tallow was a common practice in colonial America.
Some soap manufacturers prefer vegetable tallow over animal fat for ethical reasons.
The candles were made from vegetable tallow instead of beeswax.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tallow - a hard fatty substance made from rendered animal fat, used in making candles and soap
