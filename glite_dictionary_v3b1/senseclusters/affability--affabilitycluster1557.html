affability

noun
friendliness; approachability
8.6
Pronunciation: / ˌæfəˈbɪlɪti / (AmE), / ˌafəˈbɪlɪti / (BrE)
the quality of being friendly, pleasant, and easy to talk to

Examples

Friendly affability
He was renowned for his affability and good humor in all situations.
Her affability made everyone feel comfortable at the party.
The manager's affability encouraged employees to share their ideas.

Related Terms

  • antonym: aloofness - a quality of being distant and unfriendly
  • synonym: friendliness - the quality of being friendly
