amrit

noun
sacred drink
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːmɹɪt / (AmE), / ˈæmɹɪt / (BrE)
a sacred drink in Hinduism and Sikhism believed to grant immortality or spiritual purity

Examples

Sikh amrit ceremony
During the Sikh initiation ceremony, participants drink amrit as a symbol of spiritual rebirth.
In ancient Hindu stories, the gods and demons fought over amrit to gain immortality.
The priest prepared amrit for the sacred ritual, following traditional customs.
