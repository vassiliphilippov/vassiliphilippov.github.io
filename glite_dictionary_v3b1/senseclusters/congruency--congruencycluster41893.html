congruency

noun
mathematical equivalence
8.5
Pronunciation: / kənˈɡruːənsi / (AmE), / kənˈɡruːənsi / (BrE)
the mathematical relationship between numbers that have the same remainder when divided by a given number (modulus)

Examples

Number congruency
In number theory, congruency helps determine if two numbers are equivalent under a specific modulus.
The teacher explained congruency by showing how 14 and 4 are congruent modulo 5.
Understanding congruency is essential for solving many problems in modular arithmetic.

Related Terms

  • synonym: congruence - the state of being in agreement or harmony, especially in mathematics
  • antonym: difference - the result of subtracting one number from another
  • synonym: equivalence - the condition of being equal or interchangeable in value, function, or meaning
