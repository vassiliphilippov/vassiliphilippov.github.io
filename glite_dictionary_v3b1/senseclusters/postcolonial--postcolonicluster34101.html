postcolonial

adjective
after colonial rule
6.6
Pronunciation: / ˌpoʊstkəˈloʊniəl / (AmE), / ˌpəʊstkəˈləʊniəl / (BrE)
relating to the period after colonial rule, especially the effects or conditions in culture, literature, or national identity

Examples

Postcolonial literature
The novel explores postcolonial identity and the challenges faced by former colonies.
Many universities offer courses in postcolonial literature and theory.
Her research focuses on postcolonial perspectives in modern African art.
The novel explores postcolonial identity and the struggle to define national culture.
Many postcolonial societies work to reclaim their traditional languages and customs.
Economic development in postcolonial countries often faces unique historical challenges.
