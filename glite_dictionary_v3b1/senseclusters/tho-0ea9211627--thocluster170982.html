Tho

noun
language of the Tho people, Vietnam
Pronunciation: / toʊ / (AmE), / təʊ / (BrE)
The language of the Tho people, a branch of the Tai languages spoken mainly in North Nghe An Province and nearby areas of Vietnam.

Examples

Tho language
He traveled to Thanh Hoa Province to research the dialects of Tho.
Linguists have documented the vocabulary and grammar of Tho in order to preserve it.
The Tho language is still actively spoken in some rural villages of northern Vietnam.
