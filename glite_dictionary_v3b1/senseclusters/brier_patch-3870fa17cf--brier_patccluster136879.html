brier patch

noun
thicket of prickly shrubs
7.6
Pronunciation: / ˈbraɪər pætʃ / (AmE), / ˈbraɪə pætʃ / (BrE)
An area thickly covered with tangled, prickly shrubs or bushes, especially briers.

Examples

A brier patch area
Legend says Br'er Rabbit was happiest whenever he was thrown into a brier patch.
The rabbit darted into the brier patch to escape the fox.
We got scratched up trying to make our way through a brier patch.
