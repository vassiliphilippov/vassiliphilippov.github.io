wear sunscreen

verb
have sun protection lotion on skin
Pronunciation: / wɛr ˈsʌnˌskrin / (AmE), / wɛə ˈsʌnˌskriːn / (BrE)
to apply and have sun protection lotion on your skin, especially to prevent sunburn

Examples

To wear sunscreen daily
Even on cloudy days, you should wear sunscreen to avoid harmful UV rays.
It's important to wear sunscreen when spending long hours outside in the summer.
Many doctors recommend that children wear sunscreen every day to protect their skin.
