Sabine River

proper noun
river between Texas and Louisiana, USA
Pronunciation: / səˈbin ˈrɪvɚ / (AmE), / səˈbiːn ˈrɪvə / (BrE)
a river in eastern Texas that flows south into the Gulf of Mexico, forming part of the border between Texas and Louisiana
