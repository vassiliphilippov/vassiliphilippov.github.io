occupant

noun
person living in or using a place
1.0 2.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːkjəpənt / (AmE), / ˈɒkjʊpənt / (BrE)
a person who lives in or uses a particular house, room, seat, or place for a period of time

Examples

The occupant of a seat
Every seat had an occupant by the time the ceremony began.
Only one occupant was found in the building during the fire.
The new occupant of the office introduced herself to the team.

  • synonym: resident - a person who lives somewhere permanently or for a long time
  • synonym: tenant - a person who rents and occupies property
