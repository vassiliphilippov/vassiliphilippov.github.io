snow plant

noun
bright red mountain plant found in western North America
5.8
Pronunciation: / snoʊ plænt / (AmE), / snəʊ plɑːnt / (BrE)
a fleshy, bright red flowering plant (Sarcodes sanguinea) native to mountain forests of western North America, often appearing while snow is still on the ground

Examples

The snow plant blooms
Each spring, the appearance of the snow plant excites botanists and nature lovers alike.
The vibrant red color of the snow plant stands out dramatically against the white snow.
We spotted a rare snow plant on our hike through the Sierra Nevada forests.
