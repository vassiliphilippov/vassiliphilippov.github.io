penetrance

noun
gene expression rate
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnɪtrəns / (AmE), / ˈpɛnɪtrəns / (BrE)
the proportion of individuals with a particular gene who actually display the associated trait or condition

Examples

Genetic penetrance
The penetrance of the mutation is high, so most people with the gene develop the condition.
Doctors study penetrance to understand why some genetic diseases do not affect every carrier.
Low penetrance means that not everyone with the gene will show symptoms.

Related Terms

  • synonym: expressivity - the degree to which a trait is expressed in individuals with a gene
