on a half shell

adjective
shellfish served in only one shell half
4.7
Pronunciation: / ɑn ə hæf ʃɛl / (AmE), / ɒn ə hɑːf ʃɛl / (BrE)
(of shellfish) served on the bottom shell only, without the top shell, typically as a dish or appetizer.

Examples

On a half shell scallops
Fresh scallops on a half shell are a popular appetizer at seaside restaurants.
The restaurant specializes in oysters on a half shell with spicy sauce.
We ordered a dozen clams on a half shell as a starter.
