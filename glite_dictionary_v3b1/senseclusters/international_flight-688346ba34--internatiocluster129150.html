international flight

noun
flight between countries
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl flaɪt / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈnæʃənəl flaɪt / (BrE)
a flight that departs from one country and arrives in another country.

Examples

An international flight to London
Be sure to arrive early for your international flight to allow time for security checks.
Her first international flight was an exciting experience she will never forget.
My international flight to Paris was delayed because of bad weather.

Related Terms

  • antonym: domestic flight - a flight within the same country
