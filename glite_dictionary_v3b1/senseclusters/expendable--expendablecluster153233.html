expendable

adjective
intended to be used up or sacrificed
Pronunciation: / ɪkˈspɛndəbəl / (AmE), / ɪkˈspɛndəbəl / (BrE)
intended to be used up or sacrificed, especially when considered of little significance compared to the overall objective.

Examples

Expendable supplies
He was considered expendable by his superiors and given the riskiest assignments.
The company uses only expendable materials in its packaging to save on costs.
The soldiers were treated as expendable resources in the failed mission.

Related Terms

  • synonym: disposable - intended to be thrown away after use
  • antonym: indispensable - absolutely necessary or essential
