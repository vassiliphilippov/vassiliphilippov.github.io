housekeeper

noun
background maintenance process
6.0
Pronunciation: /ˈhaʊs.kiː.pər/ (AmE), /ˈhaʊs.kiː.pə/ (BrE)
(computing) a background program, daemon, or component that performs routine maintenance tasks (for example: cleanup of temporary files, freeing memory, or archiving old data) to keep a system orderly.

Examples

System housekeeper program
The server's housekeeper cleaned up stale sessions and freed memory overnight.
During low-traffic hours the system housekeeper automatically archived old logs to reduce storage.
Our cloud provider runs nightly housekeepers on each node to reclaim disk space.

Related Terms

  • synonym: daemon - a background program that runs without direct user interaction, often to provide services or perform maintenance.
  • synonym: maintenance process - a routine job or service that performs upkeep tasks such as cleanup, archiving, or resource reclamation.
