amebiasis

noun
infection with amoebae causing illness
4.8 8.0
Pronunciation: /ə.miː.ˈbaɪ.ə.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.miː.ˈbaɪ.ə.sɪs/ (AmE), /ə.miː.ˈbaɪ.ə.sɪs/ /; / /eɪ.miː.ˈbaɪ.ə.sɪs/ (BrE)
