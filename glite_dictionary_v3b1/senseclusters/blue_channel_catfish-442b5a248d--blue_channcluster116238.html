blue channel catfish

noun
a species of large North American catfish
5.9
Pronunciation: / blu ˈtʃænəl ˈkætˌfɪʃ / (AmE), / bluː ˈtʃæn.əl ˈkat.fɪʃ / (BrE)
a large catfish native to the Mississippi Valley, known for its bluish coloration and widely found in rivers of central North America
