shoulder

noun
joint between arm and torso
Pronunciation: / ˈʃoʊl.dɚ /; / ˈʃoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈʃəʊl.də /; / ˈʃəʊldə / (BrE)
the part of the body where the arm joins the torso, between the neck and the upper arm
