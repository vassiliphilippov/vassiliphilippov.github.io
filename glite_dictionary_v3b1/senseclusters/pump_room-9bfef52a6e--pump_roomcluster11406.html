pump room

noun
private room for nursing parents
4.0
Pronunciation: /pʌmp ruːm/ (AmE), /pʌmp ruːm/ /; / /pʌmp rʊm/ (BrE)
a private room in a workplace, public building, or other facility provided for nursing parents to use a breast pump or to express and store breast milk.

Examples

Office pump room
The office now provides a private pump room for nursing mothers to express milk during shifts.
Airport staff directed her to the nearest pump room where she could sit and plug in her device.
The university installed a secure pump room so parents could store supplies and nurse between classes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: lactation room - a room provided for breastfeeding or expressing breast milk; often used interchangeably with 'pump room'.
  • synonym: mother's room - an informal term for a private room for nursing parents to feed or express milk.
  • synonym: nursing room - a private space for breastfeeding or expressing milk, commonly found in public buildings and workplaces.
