in a circle

preposition
arranged in circular shape
1.0
Pronunciation: / ɪn ə ˈsɝkəl / (AmE), / ɪn ə ˈsɜːkl̩ / (BrE)
arranged so as to form or resemble the shape of a circle

Examples

Sit in a circle
The children arranged their desks in a circle for story time.
The dancers stood in a circle before the performance began.
We all sat in a circle and introduced ourselves to the group.

Related Terms
