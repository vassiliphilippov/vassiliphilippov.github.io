buff

noun
enthusiast or expert admirer
Pronunciation: / bʌf / (AmE), / bʌf / (BrE)
A person who is very interested in and knowledgeable about a particular subject.

Examples

A movie buff
As a movie buff, she attends every film festival in the city.
He's a real history buff and spends his weekends visiting museums.
Many buffs lined up early to buy tickets for the band's reunion concert.

Related Terms

  • synonym: enthusiast - a person who is very interested in a particular activity or subject
