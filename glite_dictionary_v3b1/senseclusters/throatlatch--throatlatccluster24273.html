throatlatch

noun
strap under horse's throat
Pronunciation: / ˈθroʊtˌlætʃ / (AmE), / ˈθrəʊtˌlætʃ / (BrE)
a strap on a horse's bridle that passes under the throat to help keep the bridle in place

Examples

Horse throatlatch strap
Before riding, she made sure the throatlatch was secure on the horse's bridle.
The trainer explained how to adjust the throatlatch for a comfortable fit.
A loose throatlatch can cause the bridle to slip during a ride.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bridle - the headgear used to control a horse
  • synonym: halter - a strap or rope for leading or tying an animal
