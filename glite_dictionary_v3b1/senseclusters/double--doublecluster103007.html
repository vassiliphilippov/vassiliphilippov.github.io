double

verb
become twice as much
1.2
Pronunciation: / ˈdʌb.əl /; / ˈdʌbəl / (AmE), / ˈdʌb.əl /; / ˈdʌbəl / (BrE)
to become twice as much in size, number, or amount; to serve in two roles or functions

She often doubles as both nurse and cook in the household.
The family car doubles as a delivery vehicle for their small business.
