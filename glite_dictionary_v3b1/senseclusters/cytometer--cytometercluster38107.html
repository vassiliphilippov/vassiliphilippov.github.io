cytometer

noun
cell-counting device
8.2
Pronunciation: / saɪˈtɑːmɪtər / (AmE), / saɪˈtɒmɪtə / (BrE)
a device used to count and analyze cells, especially in laboratory or medical settings

Examples

A cytometer device
The laboratory technician used a cytometer to analyze the patient's blood sample.
Modern research often relies on a cytometer to quickly count thousands of cells.
Before starting the experiment, she calibrated the cytometer to ensure accurate results.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flow cytometer - a type of cytometer that analyzes cells as they flow in a fluid stream
  • synonym: hemocytometer - a device for counting blood cells
