pivotable

adjective
can rotate easily
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪvətəbəl / (AmE), / ˈpɪvətəbəl / (BrE)
able to turn or rotate easily around a central point or axis

Examples

Pivotable screen stand
The pivotable monitor stand lets you adjust the screen for better comfort.
A pivotable camera can follow movement across a wide area.
The designer chose a pivotable lamp so the light could be directed as needed.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - not able to move or rotate
  • synonym: rotatable - able to be turned around a central point
