tumidity

noun
slight swelling or puffiness
Pronunciation: / tuˈmɪdəti / (AmE), / tjuːˈmɪdəti / (BrE)
a slight swelling or state of being swollen, especially of an organ or part of the body

Examples

Persistent tumidity of tissue
Chronic inflammation may result in persistent tumidity of the affected tissue.
Slight tumidity of the eyelids can be a symptom of an allergic reaction.
The doctor noted a tumidity in the patient's abdomen during the examination.

Related Terms

  • synonym: swelling - an abnormal enlargement of a body part
