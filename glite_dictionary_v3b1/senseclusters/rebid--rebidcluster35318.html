rebid

verb
to make another offer
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈbɪd / (AmE), / ˌriːˈbɪd / (BrE)
to make another offer or bid, especially after an initial attempt was unsuccessful or rejected

Examples

To rebid the contract
After their first proposal was rejected, the team decided to rebid for the contract.
The auction rules allowed each participant to rebid if they wished to increase their offer.
She chose not to rebid after losing the initial round of the auction.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bid - to offer a price or proposal, especially at an auction
