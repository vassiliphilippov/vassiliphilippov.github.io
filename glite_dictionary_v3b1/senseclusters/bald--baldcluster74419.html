bald

adjective
blunt, undisguised, or unconcealed (esp. statement/lie)
9.3 4.9
Pronunciation: /bɔːld/ /; / /bɑːld/ (AmE), /bɔːld/ (BrE)
(of a statement or lie) presented bluntly and without any attempt to disguise or conceal the truth or falsity.

Examples

A bald lie
He told a bald lie to everyone in the meeting, not even trying to hide the truth.
Her explanation was a bald excuse that fooled no one.
The report presented the bald facts without any attempt at sugarcoating.

Related Terms

  • synonym: blatant - completely obvious, conspicuous, or unsubtle
