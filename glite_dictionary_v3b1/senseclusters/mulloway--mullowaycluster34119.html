mulloway

noun
large Australian food fish
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈmʌl.əˌweɪ / (AmE), / ˈmʌl.əʊ.weɪ / (BrE)
a large food fish (Argyrosomus japonicus) native to Australia, valued for its flesh and sometimes difficult to distinguish from the maigre.
