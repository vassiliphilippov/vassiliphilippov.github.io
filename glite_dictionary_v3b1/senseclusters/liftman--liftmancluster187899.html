liftman

noun
male lift (elevator) operator
Pronunciation: / ˈlɪft.mæn / (AmE), / ˈlɪft.mæn / (BrE)
a man whose job is to operate a lift (elevator), especially in a public building

Examples

Liftman operates elevator
During her visit to London, she was surprised by the presence of a liftman in the department store.
The liftman greeted passengers with a polite nod as they entered the lift.
When the building upgraded to automatic lifts, the role of the liftman was made redundant.

Related Terms

  • synonym: elevator operator - a person who operates an elevator
