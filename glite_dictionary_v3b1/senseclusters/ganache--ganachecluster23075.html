ganache

noun
chocolate-cream mixture
6.3
Pronunciation: / ɡəˈnɑʃ / (AmE), / ɡəˈnɑːʃ / (BrE)
a smooth mixture of chocolate and cream used as a filling or topping in desserts

Examples

Chocolate ganache topping
The chef poured warm ganache over the cake to give it a glossy finish.
She made chocolate truffles by rolling chilled ganache into small balls.
A layer of rich ganache made the dessert extra decadent.

Related Terms

  • synonym: frosting - a sweet topping for cakes made from sugar and butter or cream
  • synonym: icing - a sweet, often hard, coating for cakes and pastries
