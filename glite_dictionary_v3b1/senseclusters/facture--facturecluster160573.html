facture

noun
invoice or bill
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈfæktʃər / (AmE), / ˈfæktʃə / (BrE)
a written statement listing goods supplied or services rendered and the amount owed; an invoice or bill

Examples

A facture for goods
The company sent a facture detailing all the items delivered last month.
Before making the payment, she carefully checked the facture for any errors.
Please forward the facture to our accounting department for processing.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bill - a statement of money owed for goods or services
  • synonym: invoice - a document listing goods or services provided and the amount due
  • antonym: receipt - a document acknowledging payment received
