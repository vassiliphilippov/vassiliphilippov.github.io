Aulostomus

noun
genus of trumpetfishes
Pronunciation: / ɔːˈlɒstəˌməs / (AmE), / ɔːˈlɒstəməs / (BrE)
A genus of elongate, tubular-snouted fishes known as trumpetfishes, belonging to the family Aulostomidae, found in tropical marine waters.

Examples

The Aulostomus fish
A member of the Aulostomus genus can often be seen hovering vertically near coral.
Researchers have observed unique feeding habits in Aulostomus species.
The Aulostomus genus includes several species of trumpetfish found in coral reefs.
