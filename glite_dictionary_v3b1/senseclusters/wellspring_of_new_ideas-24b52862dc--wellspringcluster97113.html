wellspring of new ideas

noun
source of abundant creativity or inspiration
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈwɛlˌsprɪŋ əv nu aɪˈdiəz / (AmE), / ˈwɛlˌsprɪŋ əv njuː aɪˈdɪəz / (BrE)
a source from which many new and creative ideas originate or are continuously produced
