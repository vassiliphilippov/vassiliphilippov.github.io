achromatin

noun
unstained part of cell nucleus
Pronunciation: / əˈkroʊmətɪn / (AmE), / əˈkrəʊmətɪn / (BrE)
The part of a cell nucleus that does not take up colored stains or dyes, and appears lightly colored or uncolored under a microscope.
