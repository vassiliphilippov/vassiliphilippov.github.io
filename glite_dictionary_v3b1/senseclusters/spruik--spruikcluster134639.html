spruik

verb
to promote publicly
9.9 0.01/m
Pronunciation: / spruÀêk / (BrE)
to promote or advertise something enthusiastically, especially in public or to attract attention

Examples

To spruik new products
The salesman began to spruik the new product to everyone passing by.
She was hired to spruik the event on the busy street corner.
At the market, vendors spruik their goods to attract more customers.
