straight

noun
poker hand of five consecutive cards
6.4
Pronunciation: / streɪt / (AmE), / streɪt / (BrE)
A poker hand consisting of five consecutive cards of any suit.

Examples

A poker straight
A straight is ranked higher than a flush in some games, but lower in others.
He won the game with a straight, beating my three of a kind.
She was dealt a straight from five to nine and went all in.

Related Terms

  • antonym: flush - a poker hand of five cards all of the same suit, not in sequence
