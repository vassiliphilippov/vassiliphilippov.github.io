sword-shaped

adjective
shaped like a sword blade
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔrd ʃeɪpt / (AmE), / ˈsɔːd ʃeɪpt / (BrE)
(especially of leaves or objects) shaped like the blade of a sword; long and narrow with pointed ends.

Examples

Sword-shaped leaves
Gladiolus flowers grow tall stalks with sword-shaped leaves.
The designer chose a sword-shaped handle for the new line of cutlery.
The plant's leaves are distinctly sword-shaped, making it easy to recognize.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ensiform - having the shape of a sword blade
