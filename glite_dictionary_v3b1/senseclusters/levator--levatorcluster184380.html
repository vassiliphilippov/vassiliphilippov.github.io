levator

noun
muscle that lifts a body part
8.7
Pronunciation: / lɪˈveɪtər / (AmE), / lɪˈveɪtə / (BrE)
(Anatomy) A muscle that lifts or elevates a part of the body, such as the eyelid or lip.
