multiculturalist

adjective
supporting many cultures
6.5
Pronunciation: /mʌl.ɪ.kʌl.ˈtʃər.əl.ɪst/ /; / /mʌl.iː.kʌl.ˈtʃər.əl.ɪst/ (AmE), /mʌl.ɪ.kʌl.ˈtʃə.lɪst/ /; / /mʌl.iː.kʌl.ˈtʃə.lɪst/ (BrE)
supporting or relating to the presence and equal value of multiple cultural or ethnic groups within a society

Examples

Multiculturalist education policy
The school adopted a multiculturalist approach to teaching history and literature.
Her multiculturalist views helped create a welcoming environment for all students.
A multiculturalist policy encourages respect for different traditions and backgrounds.

Related Terms

  • synonym: inclusive - open to people of all backgrounds and groups
  • antonym: xenophobic - having a fear or dislike of people from other countries or cultures
