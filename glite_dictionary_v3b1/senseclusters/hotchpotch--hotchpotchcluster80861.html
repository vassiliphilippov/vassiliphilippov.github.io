hotchpotch

noun
a confused mixture
9.7
Pronunciation: / ˈhɑtʃˌpɑtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhɒtʃpɒtʃ / (BrE)
a mixture of different things, often poorly matched or jumbled together

Examples

Hotchpotch of ideas
Her essay seemed like a hotchpotch of unrelated ideas and facts.
The antique shop was filled with a hotchpotch of furniture from different eras.
We ended up eating a hotchpotch of leftovers for dinner.
