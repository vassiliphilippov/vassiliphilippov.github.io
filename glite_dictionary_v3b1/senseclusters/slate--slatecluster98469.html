slate

noun
list of political candidates
Pronunciation: / sleɪt / (AmE), / sleɪt / (BrE)
a list of candidates put forward by a political party for election to public office

Examples

A slate of candidates
A diverse slate of candidates was presented to appeal to a broad range of voters.
The party announced its slate for the upcoming city council elections yesterday.
Voters will choose from among several slates representing different political parties.

Related Terms

  • synonym: ticket - a list of candidates nominated by a party, especially in American English
