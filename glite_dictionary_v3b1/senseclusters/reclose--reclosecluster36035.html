reclose

verb
to close again
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˌriːˈkloʊz / (AmE), / ˌriːˈkləʊz / (BrE)
to close something again after it has been opened

Examples

To reclose the box
After pouring some cereal, please reclose the box to keep it fresh.
You should always reclose the lid on the paint can when you are done.
If you reclose the package properly, the cookies will stay crisp.

Related Terms

  • synonym: close - to shut something so that it is no longer open
  • antonym: open - to make something not closed or shut
