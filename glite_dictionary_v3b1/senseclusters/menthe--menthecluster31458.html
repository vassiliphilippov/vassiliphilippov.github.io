menthe

noun
mint liqueur
Pronunciation: / mɑ̃t / (AmE), / mɑ̃t / (BrE)
a mint-flavored liqueur, especially as used in French cuisine and drinks

Examples

A glass of menthe
After dinner, she enjoyed a glass of menthe to refresh her palate.
The bartender added a splash of menthe to the cocktail for a cool, minty flavor.
He bought a bottle of menthe as a souvenir from his trip to France.

Related Terms

  • synonym: crème de menthe - a sweet, mint-flavored alcoholic liqueur
  • synonym: mint - an herb with a fresh, cool flavor
