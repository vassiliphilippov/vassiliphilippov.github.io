Mexitil

noun
antiarrhythmic drug (brand name)
Pronunciation: / ˈmɛksɪtɪl / (AmE), / ˈmɛksɪtɪl / (BrE)
A brand name for the drug mexiletine, used to treat abnormal heart rhythms (ventricular arrhythmias).
