family Gekkonidae

noun
family of geckos (lizards)
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈfæməli ɡɛˌkoʊˈnaɪdiː / (AmE), / ˈfæmɪli ɡɛˌkɒnɪˈdiː / (BrE)
A large family of small to medium-sized lizards, commonly known as geckos, found in warm climates around the world.

Examples

Family Gekkonidae lizards
Members of the family Gekkonidae are known for their ability to climb smooth surfaces.
Scientists have discovered a new species within the family Gekkonidae in Southeast Asia.
The family Gekkonidae includes some of the most vocal lizard species.
