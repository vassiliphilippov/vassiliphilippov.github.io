unaroused

adjective
not excited or awakened
Pronunciation: / ˌʌnəˈraʊzd / (AmE), / ˌʌnəˈraʊzd / (BrE)
not stimulated, excited, or awakened; lacking interest or response

Examples

Unaroused by news
Despite the thrilling announcement, she remained unaroused and continued reading her book.
The students appeared unaroused by the teacher's attempts to make the lesson more interesting.
Even after several alarms, he was still unaroused and slept through the morning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: excited - feeling or showing enthusiasm or eagerness
  • synonym: unmoved - not affected emotionally or not showing excitement
