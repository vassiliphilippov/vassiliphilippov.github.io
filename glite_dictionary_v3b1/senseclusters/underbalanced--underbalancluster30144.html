underbalanced

adjective
insufficiently balanced
Pronunciation: / ˌʌndərˈbælənst / (AmE), / ˌʌndəˈbælənst / (BrE)
not properly balanced or having less balance than is needed for correct operation or stability

Examples

Underbalanced engine
The engine was underbalanced, causing excessive vibration during operation.
If the load is underbalanced, the crane may become unstable and dangerous.
An underbalanced washing machine can make a lot of noise while spinning.

Related Terms

  • antonym: balanced - having the correct or proper balance
  • synonym: unbalanced - not evenly or properly balanced
