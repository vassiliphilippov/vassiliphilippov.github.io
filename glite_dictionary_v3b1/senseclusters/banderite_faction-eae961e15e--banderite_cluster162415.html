Banderite faction

noun
political group supporting Stepan Bandera's ideology
Pronunciation: / bænˈdɛraɪt ˈfækʃən / (AmE), / banˈdɛrʌɪt ˈfakʃən / (BrE)
a political group that supports or adheres to the ideology of Stepan Bandera, a Ukrainian nationalist leader
