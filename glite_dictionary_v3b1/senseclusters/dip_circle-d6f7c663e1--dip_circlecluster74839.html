dip circle

noun
instrument for measuring magnetic dip
9.0
Pronunciation: / dɪp ˈsɝːkəl / (AmE), / dɪp ˈsɜːkl / (BrE)
an instrument used to measure the angle of magnetic dip in the Earth's magnetic field

Examples

A dip circle instrument
He calibrated the dip circle before taking readings from the aircraft.
Modern navigational tools have largely replaced the traditional dip circle in field experiments.
The scientist used a dip circle to determine the inclination of Earth's magnetic field at the site.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dip needle - a magnetic needle mounted to measure the vertical component of Earth's magnetism
