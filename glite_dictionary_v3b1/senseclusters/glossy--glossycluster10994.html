glossy

noun
photograph on shiny paper
7.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɑːsi / (AmE), / ˈɡlɒsi / (BrE)
A photograph printed on smooth, shiny paper.

Examples

A glossy photo
He prefers glossy prints because the colors look more vibrant.
She keeps a collection of old family glossies in a photo album.
The editor asked for a glossy for the magazine's cover story.

Related Terms

  • antonym: matte - a photograph printed on dull, non-glossy paper
