major-league team

noun
team in the highest-level sports league
Pronunciation: / ˈmeɪdʒɚ liːɡ tiːm / (AmE), / ˈmeɪdʒə liːɡ tiːm / (BrE)
A team that competes in the highest professional or competitive division of a particular sport.

Examples

A major-league team
He dreamed of playing for a major-league team ever since he was a child.
Sponsorship deals are often more lucrative for a major-league team than for smaller clubs.
The city was thrilled when it finally got a major-league team in baseball.

Related Terms

  • antonym: minor-league team - a team that competes in a lower or supporting league
