pattypan

noun
small baking mold
Pronunciation: / ˈpætipæn / (AmE), / ˈpætipæn / (BrE)
a small, shallow baking pan or mold used for making individual tarts, cakes, or patties

Examples

A metal pattypan
She placed the batter into each pattypan before sliding the tray into the hot oven.
The recipe called for the use of a pattypan to shape the small cakes.
After baking, he removed the tarts from the pattypan and let them cool.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cake pan - a pan used for baking cakes
  • synonym: muffin tin - a baking pan with multiple cup-shaped molds for muffins or cupcakes
