tamper-proof

adjective
resistant to tampering
4.2
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmpər pruːf / (AmE), / ˈtæmpə pruːf / (BrE)
designed to prevent or reveal any unauthorized access, alteration, or interference

Examples

Tamper-proof packaging
The new packaging is tamper-proof to ensure the product's safety for consumers.
Many electronic devices now come with tamper-proof screws to prevent unauthorized repairs.
A tamper-proof seal was placed on the envelope to protect its contents.

Related Terms

  • synonym: secure - protected against threats or unauthorized access
  • antonym: vulnerable - easily harmed or accessed without permission
