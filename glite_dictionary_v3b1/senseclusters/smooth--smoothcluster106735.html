smooth

adjective
polished and sophisticated in manners
4.3 3.6
Pronunciation: / smuð / (AmE), / smuːð / (BrE)
(of a person or their manners) confidently agreeable, polite, and sophisticated, often in a way that seems effortless or polished.

Examples

Smooth talker
She was impressed by his smooth compliments and effortless conversation.
The salesperson's smooth approach made the customers feel comfortable and welcome.
With his smooth manner, the diplomat quickly gained everyone's trust at the meeting.
