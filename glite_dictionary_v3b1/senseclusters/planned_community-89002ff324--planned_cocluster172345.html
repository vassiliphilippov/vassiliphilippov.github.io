planned community

noun
residential area designed to a plan
2.5
Pronunciation: / plænd kəˈmjunəti / (AmE), / plænd kəˈmjuːnəti / (BrE)
a residential area designed and built according to a detailed plan, often intended for a particular group of residents or with certain amenities

Examples

A planned community
Developers hope the planned community will attract young families seeking safe and modern neighborhoods.
He moved to a planned community designed for retirees, featuring accessible homes and social clubs.
The new planned community offers parks, shops, and schools all within walking distance.

Related Terms

  • synonym: suburb - an outlying district of a city, especially a residential one
