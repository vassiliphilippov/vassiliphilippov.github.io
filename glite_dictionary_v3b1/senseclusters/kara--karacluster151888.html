kara

noun
Sikh steel bracelet
Pronunciation: / kəˈrɑ / (AmE), / kəˈrɑː / (BrE)
a steel bracelet worn by Sikhs as a symbol of faith and commitment to their religion

Examples

A kara bracelet

The kara is one of the five articles of faith that Sikhs are expected to wear.
During the ceremony, each child received a kara as a symbol of their commitment.
