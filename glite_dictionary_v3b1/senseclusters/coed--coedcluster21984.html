coed

noun
mixed-gender school
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈkoʊˌɛd / (AmE), / ˈkəʊˌɛd / (BrE)
a school, college, or institution that admits both male and female students

Examples

A coed school
After years at an all-boys school, he transferred to a coed for his final year.
Many parents prefer a coed because it reflects real-world diversity.
The university became a coed institution in the 1960s, admitting both men and women.

Related Terms

  • synonym: coeducational - educating both males and females together
  • antonym: single-sex school - a school that admits only boys or only girls
