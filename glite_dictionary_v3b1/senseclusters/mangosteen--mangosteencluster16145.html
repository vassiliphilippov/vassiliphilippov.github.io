mangosteen

noun
tropical fruit with sweet, juicy flesh
7.8
Pronunciation: / ˈmæŋɡəˌstiːn / (AmE), / ˈmæŋɡəstiːn / (BrE)
A round, tropical fruit with thick purple rind and juicy, sweet white flesh, often said to taste like a blend of peach and pineapple.

Examples

A mangosteen fruit
A single mangosteen contains juicy segments with a delicate, sweet flavor.
Many travelers to Southeast Asia seek out fresh mangosteen in local markets.
The chef topped the dessert with slices of ripe mangosteen for a tropical touch.
