egestion

noun
expelling waste
Pronunciation: / iˈdʒɛstʃən /; / ɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (AmE), / iˈdʒɛstʃən /; / ɪˈdʒɛstʃən / (BrE)
the process of expelling or discharging undigested waste from the body after digestion
