Orientalia

proper noun
academic journal title
Pronunciation: / ˌɔːriənˈteɪliə / (AmE), / ˌɔːriənˈteɪliə / (BrE)
the title of one or more scholarly journals publishing research on the languages, history, and cultures of the Near East and Asia

Examples

Orientalia journal
Her article on ancient Persian scripts was published in Orientalia this spring.
Many leading scholars contribute to Orientalia with research on Asian history.
The library subscribes to Orientalia for its collection of Near Eastern studies.
