infecundity

noun
lack of productivity
9.9
Pronunciation: /ɪn.fə.ˈkʌn.dɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.fɪ.ˈkʌn.dɪ.tiː/ (AmE), /ɪn.fə.ˈkʌn.dɪ.tiː/ /; / /ɪn.fɪ.ˈkʌn.dɪ.tiː/ (BrE)
the state of being unproductive or unable to produce creative, intellectual, or physical output

Examples

Infecundity of land
The infecundity of the soil made farming in the region extremely difficult.
Critics noted the infecundity of his recent work compared to his earlier, more creative period.
Periods of infecundity can be frustrating for artists seeking inspiration.

Related Terms

  • synonym: barrenness - the quality of being unproductive or unfruitful
  • antonym: fecundity - the ability to produce many new ideas, offspring, or results
  • synonym: sterility - the state of being unable to produce offspring or results
