single dwelling

noun
standalone house
4.3
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪŋɡəl ˈdwɛlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsɪŋɡl ˈdwɛlɪŋ / (BrE)
A house that stands alone and is not attached to any other houses.

Examples

A single dwelling house
The city council approved the construction of a new single dwelling on Oak Street.
The insurance policy covers damage to your single dwelling caused by storms and fire.
Unlike apartments, a single dwelling provides more privacy and a private yard.
