false asphodel

noun
a plant of the genus Tofieldia
Pronunciation: / fɔls ˈæsfəˌdɛl / (AmE), / fɔːls ˈæsfədɛl / (BrE)
a plant of the genus Tofieldia, characterized by linear mainly basal leaves and small spike-like clusters of flowers

Examples

The false asphodel plant
The delicate white flowers of the false asphodel appeared in early summer.
Unlike true asphodel, false asphodel thrives in wetter, boggy soils.
We found a patch of false asphodel growing near the edge of the marsh.
