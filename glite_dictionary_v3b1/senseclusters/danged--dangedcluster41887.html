danged

interjection
mild exclamation
Pronunciation: / dæŋd / (AmE), / dæŋd / (BrE)
used as a mild exclamation to express annoyance, surprise, or frustration

Examples

Danged! That hurt
He slammed the door and shouted, "danged!" when he saw the damage.
"Danged! I forgot my keys again," she muttered as she checked her bag.
When the computer crashed, he exclaimed, "danged! I lost my work!"

Related Terms

  • degree higher: damned - used as a stronger exclamation to express anger or emphasis
  • synonym: darned - used as a mild exclamation to express annoyance or surprise
