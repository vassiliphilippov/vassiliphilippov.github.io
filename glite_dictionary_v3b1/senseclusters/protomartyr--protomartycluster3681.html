protomartyr

noun
first martyr
Pronunciation: / ˈproʊtoʊˌmɑːrtər / (AmE), / ˈprəʊtəʊˌmɑːtə / (BrE)
the first person to die for a cause, especially for a religious faith

Examples

The protomartyr saint
Saint Stephen is often called the Christian protomartyr for his early sacrifice.
The legend describes her as the protomartyr of the movement, inspiring many others to follow.
Historians debate who should be recognized as the true protomartyr of the faith.

Related Terms

  • synonym: martyr - a person who dies for their beliefs or cause
