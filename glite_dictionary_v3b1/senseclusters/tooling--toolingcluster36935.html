tooling

noun
manufacturing tool preparation
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtuːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the process of designing, making, or preparing specialized tools, dies, or molds needed for manufacturing products

Examples

Tooling for production
The factory invested heavily in new tooling to improve production efficiency.
Proper tooling is essential for manufacturing high-quality automotive parts.
Delays in tooling can push back the entire product launch schedule.

Related Terms

  • synonym: fabrication - the process of constructing products by assembling parts
  • synonym: machining - the process of shaping materials using machines
