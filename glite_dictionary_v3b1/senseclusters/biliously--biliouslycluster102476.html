biliously

adverb
sickly yellow manner
Pronunciation: / ˈbɪliəsli / (AmE), / ˈbɪliəsli / (BrE)
in a manner that is sickly yellow or greenish-yellow, resembling the color of bile.

Examples

Biliously colored curtains
The old curtains hung biliously in the dim light, giving the room a gloomy feel.
He painted the walls biliously, making the entire space look unwelcoming.
The soup looked biliously green, so no one wanted to try it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: sickly - in an unhealthy or pale manner
  • antonym: vividly - in a bright or intense manner
