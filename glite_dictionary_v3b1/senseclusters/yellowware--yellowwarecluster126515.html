yellowware

noun
yellow pottery
Pronunciation: / ˈjɛloʊˌwɛr / (AmE), / ˈjɛləʊˌweə / (BrE)
a type of yellow-glazed earthenware pottery, especially popular in the 19th century for kitchenware and tableware

Examples

A yellowware pot
Collectors often search for rare pieces of yellowware at antique markets.
The kitchen shelves were lined with old yellowware bowls and plates.
She inherited a beautiful yellowware pitcher from her grandmother.

Related Terms

  • antonym: porcelain - a white, fine, translucent ceramic material
  • synonym: stoneware - a durable type of pottery fired at high temperatures
