agita

noun
indigestion discomfort
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɑːdʒɪtə / (AmE), / ˈædʒɪtə / (BrE)
a feeling of heartburn, indigestion, or gastric discomfort, often experienced as burning pain in the chest or throat

Examples

Stomach agita
After eating the spicy meal, he suffered from severe agita that kept him awake all night.
She always gets agita when she eats too quickly or skips breakfast.
The rich dessert gave me a bit of agita before I went to bed.

Related Terms

  • synonym: heartburn - a burning pain in the chest caused by stomach acid
  • synonym: indigestion - discomfort or pain in the stomach after eating
