flip

verb
toss a coin to choose
Pronunciation: / flɪp / (AmE), / flɪp / (BrE)
to toss something, especially a coin, into the air so that it turns over and lands, to decide between two alternatives or to see which side comes up

Examples

To flip a coin
He flipped the coin high into the air and watched it spin.
If you're still unsure, you could just flip a coin to decide.
We decided who would go first by flipping a coin.

Related Terms

  • synonym: toss - to throw something lightly or casually
