crumble

noun
small broken piece
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈkrʌmbəl / (AmE), / ˈkrʌmbəl / (BrE)
a small piece that has broken off from something, especially baked food like bread or cake

Examples

A crumble of cake
There were a few crumbles left on the table after the cake was eaten.
She brushed the crumbles off her lap after finishing her muffin.
The child picked up the crumbles from the floor and threw them away.

Related Terms

  • degree higher: chunk - a thick, solid piece of something
  • synonym: crumb - a very small piece of dry food, especially bread or cake
