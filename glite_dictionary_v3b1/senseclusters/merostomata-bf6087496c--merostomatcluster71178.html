Merostomata

noun
group of arthropods including horseshoe crabs and sea scorpions
Pronunciation: / ˌmɛrəˈstɑmətə / (AmE), / ˌmɪərəˈstɒmətə /; / ˌmɛərəˈstɒmətə / (BrE)
a zoological group (subclass) of arthropods that includes horseshoe crabs and extinct sea scorpions (Eurypterida)
