snail flower

noun
tropical vine with coiled, showy flowers
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈsneɪl ˌflaʊ.ər / (AmE), / ˈsneɪl ˌflaʊ.ə / (BrE)
a perennial tropical American vine with showy yellow and purple flowers whose coiled corolla resembles a snail shell; sometimes classified in the genus Phaseolus

Examples

The snail flower plant
Botanists often admire the coiled keels of the snail flower's blooms.
The snail flower is prized in gardens for its unique, spiraled blossoms.
