itch

noun
contagious skin infection (scabies)
Pronunciation: / ɪtʃ / (AmE), / ɪtʃ / (BrE)
A contagious skin disease caused by the itch mite, characterized by persistent itching and skin irritation; also known as scabies.

Examples

Itch from mites
Doctors diagnosed his rash as itch, which requires specific treatment.
She suffered from severe itch after being exposed to contaminated bedding.
The old medical texts warned sailors about outbreaks of itch during long voyages.

Related Terms

  • synonym: scabies - a contagious skin disease marked by itching and small raised red spots, caused by the itch mite
