chan

noun
Chinese Buddhist school
9.9
Pronunciation: /tʃæn/ (AmE), /tʃæn/ (BrE)
a Chinese school of Buddhism that focuses on meditation and direct personal insight, known as 'Zen' in Japan

Examples

Chan Buddhism
Many monks in China practice chan as a path to enlightenment.
The teachings of chan emphasize meditation over ritual or scripture.
Scholars often compare chan with its Japanese counterpart, Zen.
