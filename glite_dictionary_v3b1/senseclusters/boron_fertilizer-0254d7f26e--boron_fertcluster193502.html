boron fertilizer

noun
fertilizer containing boron
Pronunciation: / ˈbɔːrɑn ˈfɜːrtəˌlaɪzər /; / ˈbɔɹˌɑn ˈfɝtəˌlaɪzɚ / (AmE), / ˈbɔːrɒn ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzə / (BrE)
A fertilizer containing boron, used to supply plants with this essential micronutrient.

Examples

Boron fertilizer use
Excessive use of boron fertilizer can cause toxicity and harm plant growth.
Many crops benefit from the use of boron fertilizer to correct nutrient deficiencies in the soil.
The gardener applied boron fertilizer to help improve the flowering of her plants.
Proper application of boron fertiliser can help prevent deficiencies in certain crops.
The farmers decided to use a boron fertiliser to improve their sunflower yield.
Too much boron fertiliser can harm plants, so careful dosage is important.
