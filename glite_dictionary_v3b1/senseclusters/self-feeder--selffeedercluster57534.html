self-feeder

noun
automatic machine supplying materials
9.9 5.8
Pronunciation: /ˈself.fiː.dər/ (AmE), /ˈself.fiː.də/ (BrE)
a machine or device that automatically supplies a material, especially food for animals or raw material for a process, without manual intervention

Examples

A self-feeder machine
By installing a self-feeder, farmers reduce the time spent manually feeding the animals.
She checked the self-feeder every morning to make sure it was dispensing food properly.
The automatic self-feeder ensures that the livestock have constant access to fresh grain.
