spittle insect

noun
insect that produces protective froth
7.2
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪt̬l ˌɪnˌsɛkt / (AmE), / ˈspɪtl ˌɪnsɛkt / (BrE)
a small jumping insect that lives in a mass of protective froth secreted by itself or its larvae

Examples

A spittle insect jumps
A spittle insect produces frothy bubbles on plant stems to protect its young.
A scientist studied how the spittle insect leaps from one plant to another.
The garden was dotted with the froth created by spittle insects.

Related Terms

  • synonym: froghopper - a synonym for 'spittle insect', the common name for insects in the family Cercopidae
