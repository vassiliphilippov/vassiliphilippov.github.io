dinner plate

noun
large plate for the main course
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪnɚ pleɪt / (AmE), / ˈdɪnə pleɪt / (BrE)
A large plate on which the main course of a meal is served and eaten.

Examples

A dinner plate set
After finishing her meal, she stacked her dinner plate with the others for washing.
She placed a grilled steak on his dinner plate and handed it to him.
The restaurant uses white ceramic dinner plates for all main courses.

Related Terms

  • synonym: plate - a flat dish, typically circular, from which food is eaten or served
