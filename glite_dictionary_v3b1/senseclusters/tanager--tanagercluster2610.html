tanager

noun
brightly colored American woodland songbird
Pronunciation: / ˈtænədʒər / (AmE), / ˈtanɪdʒə / (BrE)
A small songbird found chiefly in the Americas, known for its brightly colored males and typically inhabiting woodland areas.

Examples

A bright tanager bird
A bright-red tanager flashed through the canopy during our morning hike.
Many birdwatchers travel to South America to spot rare species of tanager.
The male tanager is known for its vibrant plumage and melodic song.
