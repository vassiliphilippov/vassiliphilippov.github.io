Reno

proper noun
city in western Nevada, US
Pronunciation: / ˈriːnoʊ / (AmE), / ˈriːnəʊ / (BrE)
A city in western Nevada, United States, known for its gambling casinos and for historically being a center for easy divorce and remarriage.

Examples

Reno city
Many couples traveled to Reno in the 20th century to obtain a quick divorce.
She won a small fortune at the casinos during her visit to Reno.
