whittler

noun
person who carves wood as a hobby
Pronunciation: / ˈwɪtlɚ / (AmE), / ˈwɪtlə / (BrE)
a person who carves small pieces of wood, usually as a hobby or pastime

Examples

A whittler with wood
As a child, he became a skilled whittler, making animals and figurines from sticks.
Many whittlers enjoy showing their creations at local craft fairs.
The old whittler sat on the porch, shaping a piece of pine into a tiny train.
