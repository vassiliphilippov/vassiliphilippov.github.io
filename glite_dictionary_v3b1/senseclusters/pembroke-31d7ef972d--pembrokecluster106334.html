Pembroke

noun
a breed of corgi dog with a short tail
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛmˌbroʊk / (AmE), / ˈpɛm.brʊk / (BrE)
a breed of corgi that is smaller, has straight legs, pointed ears, and a short tail, officially known as the Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Examples

Pembroke corgi
The Queen is famous for her love of Pembroke corgis.
Their new puppy is a playful Pembroke with a charming personality.
You can often spot a Pembroke by its short tail and upright ears.
