myopia

noun
inability to see distant objects clearly
6.6 0.81/m

A condition of the eye in which distant objects appear blurred because their images are focused in front of the retina; also known as nearsightedness.

Examples

Myopia eye condition
Because of his myopia, he could read books easily but struggled to recognize friends across the street.
Laser surgery can sometimes correct myopia in adults.
She was diagnosed with myopia and needed glasses to see the whiteboard at school.

Synonyms

  • nearsightedness
    3.7 0.22/m
    nearsightedness means seeing close things clearly but distant objects as blurred, while myopia is the medical name for that condition.
  • shortsightedness
    6.5 0.01/m
    shortsightedness is a common everyday name for the problem, while myopia is the formal medical term doctors use.
