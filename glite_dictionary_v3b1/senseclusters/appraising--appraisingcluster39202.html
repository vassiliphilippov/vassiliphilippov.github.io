appraising

adjective
carefully judging or evaluating
Pronunciation: / əˈpreɪzɪŋ / (AmE), / əˈpreɪzɪŋ / (BrE)
carefully judging or evaluating the value, quality, or significance of something

Examples

An appraising look
He spoke with an appraising tone, weighing each word carefully.
She gave him an appraising look before deciding whether to trust him.
The manager's appraising glance lingered on the candidate's résumé.
