schoolchild

noun
a child who goes to school
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈskuːlˌtʃaɪld / (AmE), / ˈskuːlˌtʃaɪld / (BrE)
a child who attends school, especially one in primary or secondary education

Examples

A schoolchild in class

Each schoolchild received a free lunch at noon.
The schoolchild carried her backpack as she walked to class every morning.
