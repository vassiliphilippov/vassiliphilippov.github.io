draper

noun
dealer in fabrics and sewing materials
4.6
Pronunciation: / ˈdreɪpər / (AmE), / ˈdreɪpə / (BrE)
a person or shop that sells cloth, fabrics, and sometimes clothing and sewing materials, especially as a traditional occupation

Examples

A draper shop
My grandmother used to buy all her fabric from the local draper.
She asked the draper for advice on the best material for curtains.
The draper displayed an array of colorful textiles in his shop window.

Related Terms

  • synonym: haberdasher - a dealer in small articles for sewing, such as buttons and ribbons
