polygraphist

noun
lie detector operator
Pronunciation: / ˌpɑːliˈɡræfɪst / (AmE), / ˌpɒliˈɡræfɪst / (BrE)
a person who is trained to operate a polygraph, or lie detector, machine

Examples

The polygraphist operator
The polygraphist carefully monitored the subject's responses during the test.
After the interview, the polygraphist prepared a detailed report for the investigators.
A skilled polygraphist must understand both the machine and human behavior.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interrogator - a person who questions someone closely, often in a legal context
  • synonym: polygraph examiner - a person who conducts polygraph tests
