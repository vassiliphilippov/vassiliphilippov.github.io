big tree

noun
giant sequoia tree of California
3.6
Pronunciation: / bɪɡ tri / (AmE), / bɪɡ triː / (BrE)
An extremely tall evergreen tree of the Sierra Nevada in California, known as the largest living organism; also called giant sequoia.

Examples

The big tree sequoia
He was amazed by the sheer size of the oldest big tree in the forest.
The big tree in California attracts thousands of tourists every year.
We visited the national park to see a big tree that is over 2,000 years old.
