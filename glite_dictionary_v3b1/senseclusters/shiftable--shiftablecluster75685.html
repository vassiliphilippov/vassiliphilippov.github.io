shiftable

adjective
able to be moved
4.4
Pronunciation: / ˈʃɪftəbəl / (AmE), / ˈʃɪftəbəl / (BrE)
able to be moved or changed from one place, position, or arrangement to another

Examples

Shiftable furniture
The lightweight chairs are shiftable and can be arranged in different ways for each event.
We bought shelves that are shiftable to fit our changing storage needs.
The partitions in the office are shiftable, allowing the space to be reconfigured easily.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fixed - firmly attached and not able to be moved
  • synonym: movable - able to be moved from one place to another
