siding

noun
exterior building covering material
Pronunciation: / ˈsaɪdɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈsaɪdɪŋ / (BrE)
material applied to the exterior walls of a building to protect it from the weather and improve its appearance

Examples

Wooden siding
Over time, wooden siding can become warped or damaged by moisture.
The builders installed new siding on the house to improve insulation.
Vinyl siding comes in a variety of colors and textures for modern homes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cladding - external layer for covering buildings
