unicycle

noun
one-wheeled pedal vehicle
5.9
Pronunciation: / ˈjuː.nɪˌsaɪ.kəl / (AmE), / ˈjuː.nɪˌsaɪ.kəl / (BrE)
a vehicle with a single wheel that is propelled by pedals, typically ridden by balancing on top

Examples

A unicycle trick
Learning to ride a unicycle takes a lot of practice and patience.
She can ride a unicycle across the stage without losing her balance.
The street performer amazed everyone by juggling while balancing on a unicycle.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bicycle - a vehicle with two wheels that is powered by pedals
  • synonym: tricycle - a vehicle with three wheels that is powered by pedals
