live birth

noun
birth of a living child
Pronunciation: / laɪv bɜrθ / (AmE), / laɪv bɜːθ / (BrE)
the birth of a baby that shows any sign of life, regardless of gestational age

Examples

A live birth
A live birth is officially registered even if the baby survives only briefly.
She researched the rate of live births in different countries.
The hospital reported an increase in live births last year.

Related Terms

  • antonym: stillbirth - the birth of an infant that is not alive
