frosty glance

noun
an unfriendly or cold look
Pronunciation: / ˈfrɑsti ɡlæns / (AmE), / ˈfrɒsti ɡlɑːns / (BrE)
An unfriendly or cold look intended to show disapproval or disdain.

Examples

Frosty glance of disapproval
After the meeting, her boss shot her a frosty glance that made everyone uncomfortable.
He received a frosty glance from his friend when he arrived late for dinner.
The teacher's frosty glance silenced the entire classroom instantly.

Related Terms

  • synonym: icy stare - a very cold or unfriendly look
  • antonym: warm smile - a friendly and pleasant facial expression
