unfried

adjective
not damaged by heat
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfraɪd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfraɪd / (BrE)
not damaged, burned, or destroyed by overheating or an electrical fault, especially in electronics (informal)

Examples

Unfried circuit board
Luckily, the processor was unfried after the power surge and still worked perfectly.
The technician checked the circuit board and confirmed it was unfried despite the overheating.
After the repair, we were relieved to find the graphics card remained unfried.

Related Terms

  • antonym: fried - damaged or destroyed by overheating or electrical fault
  • synonym: intact - not damaged or impaired; in one piece
