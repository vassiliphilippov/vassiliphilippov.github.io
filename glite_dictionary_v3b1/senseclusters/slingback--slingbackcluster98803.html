slingback

noun
a women's shoe with a strap around the heel
Pronunciation: / ˈslɪŋˌbæk / (AmE), / ˈslɪŋˌbæk / (BrE)
a woman's shoe or sandal with a strap that passes around the back of the heel instead of a full back

Examples

A slingback shoe
My new slingbacks have a comfortable strap that keeps them secure.
She wore her favorite slingbacks to the party last night.
These elegant slingbacks go perfectly with a summer dress.

Related Terms

  • antonym: mule - a woman's shoe with no back or constraint around the heel
  • synonym: pump - a woman's formal shoe, typically with a low-cut front
