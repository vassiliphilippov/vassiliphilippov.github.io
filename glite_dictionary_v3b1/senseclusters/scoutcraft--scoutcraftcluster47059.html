scoutcraft

noun
outdoor skills
Pronunciation: / ˈskaʊtkræft / (AmE), / ˈskaʊtkrɑːft / (BrE)
the skills and knowledge required for outdoor activities such as camping, navigation, and survival, especially in scouting

Examples

Scoutcraft skills outdoors
He practiced scoutcraft by learning how to build a shelter in the woods.
Many summer camps teach scoutcraft to help children become more independent outdoors.
Her knowledge of scoutcraft made her an excellent leader on hiking trips.
