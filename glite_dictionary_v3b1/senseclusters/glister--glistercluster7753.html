glister

verb
to shine brightly
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡlɪstər / (AmE), / ˈɡlɪstə / (BrE)
to shine with a sparkling or gleaming light

Examples

Jewels glister brightly
The jewels glistered in the display case under the bright lights.
After the rain, the leaves glister with tiny droplets of water.
Her eyes glistered with excitement as she opened the present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: gleam - to shine with a soft or reflected light
  • synonym: glitter - to shine with a bright, shimmering, reflected light
