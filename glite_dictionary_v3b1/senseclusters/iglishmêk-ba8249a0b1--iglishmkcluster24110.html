Iglishmêk

noun
Dwarvish sign language from Tolkien's works
Pronunciation: / ˈɪglɪʃmɛk / (AmE), / ˈɪglɪʃmɛk / (BrE)
a constructed sign language devised by J. R. R. Tolkien as used by the Dwarves of Middle-earth

Examples

Iglishmêk language
Many fans study Iglishmêk to better understand Tolkien's fictional cultures.
The Dwarves communicated secretly using Iglishmêk during council meetings.
Unlike spoken tongues, Iglishmêk relies entirely on coded hand gestures instead of sound.
