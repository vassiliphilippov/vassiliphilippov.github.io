-struck

adjective
deeply affected by something (in compounds)
Pronunciation: / strʌk / (AmE), / strʌk / (BrE)
(in combination) deeply affected or overwhelmed by a particular emotion, event, or condition.

Examples

To be thunderstruck
After hearing the news, he stood thunderstruck and speechless.
Many fans felt star-struck when meeting their favorite actor.
She was awe-struck by the beauty of the sunset.
