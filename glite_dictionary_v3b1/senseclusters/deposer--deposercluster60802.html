deposer

noun
person giving a deposition
6.1
Pronunciation: / dɪˈpoʊzər / (AmE), / dɪˈpəʊzə / (BrE)
a person who testifies or gives a deposition, especially in a legal context

Examples

Courtroom deposer
Each deposer must swear an oath before providing their testimony.
In the transcript, the deposer clarified the details of the incident for the record.
The deposer answered questions from both attorneys during the deposition.

Related Terms

  • synonym: deponent - a person who gives testimony under oath
  • synonym: witness - a person who gives evidence in court
