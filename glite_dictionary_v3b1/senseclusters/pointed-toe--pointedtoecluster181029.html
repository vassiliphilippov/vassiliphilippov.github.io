pointed-toe

adjective
having a pointed toe
1.4
Pronunciation: / ˈpɔɪn.tɪd ˌtoʊ / (AmE), / ˈpɔɪn.tɪd ˌtəʊ / (BrE)
(of a shoe or similar item) having a toe that comes to a point rather than being rounded or square.

Examples

Pointed-toe shoes
His pointed-toe shoes gave him a classic and stylish look.
She wore a pair of elegant pointed-toe heels to the party.
The vintage store had several styles of pointed-toe boots on display.

Related Terms
