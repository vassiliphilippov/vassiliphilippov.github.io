blinkingly

adverb
in a blinking way
6.1
Pronunciation: /ˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.li/ /; / /ˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (AmE), /ˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.li/ /; / /ˈblɪŋ.kɪŋ.liː/ (BrE)
