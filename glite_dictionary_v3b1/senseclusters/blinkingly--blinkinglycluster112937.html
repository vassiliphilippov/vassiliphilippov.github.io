blinkingly

adverb
in a blinking way
6.1
Pronunciation: / ˈblɪŋkɪŋli / (AmE), / ˈblɪŋkɪŋli / (BrE)
in a way that involves blinking or as if blinking repeatedly, often due to surprise or bright light

Examples

Look blinkingly at light
She looked blinkingly at the sudden flash of the camera.
After coming out of the dark theater, he stepped into the sunlight blinkingly.
The child stared blinkingly at the unfamiliar faces in the room.

Related Terms

  • synonym: rapidly - in a quick or fast manner
  • antonym: steadily - in a continuous and even manner
