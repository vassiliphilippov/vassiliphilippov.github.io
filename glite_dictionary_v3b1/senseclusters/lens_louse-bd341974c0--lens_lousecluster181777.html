lens louse

noun
parasitic aquarium pest
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈlɛnz laʊs / (AmE), / ˈlɛnz laʊs / (BrE)
a small parasitic organism that infests aquariums and fish tanks, often attaching to fish or surfaces and considered a pest

Examples

A lens louse infestation
If left untreated, a lens louse infestation can harm fish health.
Regular tank maintenance can help prevent the appearance of a lens louse in your aquarium.
The aquarist spotted a lens louse clinging to one of the goldfish.
