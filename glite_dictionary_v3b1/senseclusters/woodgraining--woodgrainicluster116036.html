woodgraining

noun
imitation of the appearance of wood grain
Pronunciation: / ˈwʊdˌɡreɪnɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈwʊdˌɡreɪnɪŋ / (BrE)
the process or effect of creating a texture or appearance that resembles the natural grain of wood

Examples

Woodgraining technique
Many furniture restorers use woodgraining techniques to give old items a fresh, natural look.
Modern paints and tools make woodgraining much easier for DIY home decorators.
The artist's skill in woodgraining made the metal door look just like natural oak.
