homestretch

noun
final straight part of a racetrack
Pronunciation: / ˈhoʊmˌstrɛtʃ / (AmE), / ˈhəʊmˌstrɛtʃ / (BrE)
the straight section of a racetrack leading to the finish line

Examples

Racetrack homestretch
The horse surged ahead during the homestretch and won the race.
The runners gave everything they had as they entered the homestretch.
With the finish line in sight, the crowd cheered louder in the homestretch.
