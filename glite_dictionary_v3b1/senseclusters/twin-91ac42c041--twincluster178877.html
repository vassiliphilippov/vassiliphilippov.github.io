Twin

noun
person born under Gemini sign (astrology)
Pronunciation: / twɪn / (AmE), / twɪn / (BrE)
(Astrology) A person born under the sign of Gemini, traditionally associated with the period May 21 to June 20.

Examples

A Twin in Gemini
As a proud Twin, she believed in horoscopes and personality traits.
He celebrates his birthday in June, so he is a Twin according to astrologers.
Many Twins are said to be adaptable and curious by nature.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Gemini - the third sign of the zodiac, covering late May and early June
