unfancied

adjective
not expected to win
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈfænsiːd / (AmE), / ʌnˈfænsiːd / (BrE)
not expected to succeed or not considered popular or attractive, especially in a competition or contest

Examples

Unfancied team wins
The unfancied candidate won the election against all expectations.
Despite being unfancied at the start, the team made it to the championship game.
Many were surprised when the unfancied artist's work became popular overnight.

Related Terms

  • antonym: favored - expected to win or succeed
  • synonym: underdog - a person or team expected to lose
