earmold

noun
hearing aid insert
6.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɪrˌmoʊld / (AmE), / ˈɪəˌməʊld / (BrE)
a soft, custom-shaped piece that fits inside the ear to hold a hearing aid in place and direct sound

Examples

A earmold for hearing aid
The audiologist carefully fitted the new earmold to ensure comfort and effectiveness.
She cleaned her earmold every day to keep her hearing aid working properly.
Custom earmolds can help prevent feedback and improve sound quality for hearing aid users.
The audiologist took an impression to make a new earmold for his hearing aid.
A comfortable earmold can greatly improve the fit and performance of a hearing device.
She cleaned her earmold daily to keep it free from wax and debris.

  • synonym: hearing aid - a device worn in or behind the ear to help with hearing loss
