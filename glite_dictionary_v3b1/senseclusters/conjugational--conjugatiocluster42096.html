conjugational

adjective
relating to genetic transfer
9.4
Pronunciation: / ˌkɑndʒəˈɡeɪʃənəl / (AmE), / ˌkɒndʒʊˈɡeɪʃənəl / (BrE)
relating to conjugation in biology, especially the transfer or joining of genetic material between cells or organisms

Examples

Conjugational DNA transfer
The scientists studied the conjugational mechanisms that allow bacteria to exchange DNA.
Certain antibiotics can inhibit conjugational transfer of resistance genes among microbes.
Understanding conjugational processes is important for controlling the spread of genetic traits in bacteria.
