kleptocracy

noun
corrupt government
9.9
Pronunciation: / klɛpˈtɑkrəsi / (AmE), / klɛpˈtɒkrəsi / (BrE)
a government or state in which those in power use their authority to steal national resources for personal gain

Examples

A kleptocracy government
The country was ruled by a kleptocracy that enriched only a small group of leaders.
International organizations condemned the kleptocracy for stealing billions from public funds.
After years under a kleptocracy, the nation struggled to rebuild its economy.

Related Terms

  • synonym: corruption - dishonest or illegal behavior by those in power
  • antonym: democracy - a system of government by the whole population, typically through elected representatives
