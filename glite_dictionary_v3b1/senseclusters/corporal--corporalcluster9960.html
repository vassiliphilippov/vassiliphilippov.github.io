Synonyms

  • corporeal
    8.4 1/m
    corporeal is used for the physical, visible body in formal or literary contexts, while corporal is a more common word for bodily matters.
  • bodily
    4.0 3/m
    bodily describes everyday physical feelings or actions people notice, while corporal sounds more formal and appears in legal or medical talk.
  • somatic
    7.9 2/m
    somatic is a technical term used in medicine or biology, while corporal is the general word for body-related things.
