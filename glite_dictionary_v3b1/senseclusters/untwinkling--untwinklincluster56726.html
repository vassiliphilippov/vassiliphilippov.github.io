untwinkling

adjective
steady, unmoving gaze
9.3
Pronunciation: / ʌnˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (AmE), / ʌnˈtwɪŋklɪŋ / (BrE)
not blinking or moving, with a steady and unchanging expression or gaze.

Examples

Untwinkling stare
The cat watched the mouse with an untwinkling stare, waiting for the perfect moment to pounce.
His untwinkling eyes made it clear he was completely focused on the task.
She held an untwinkling gaze throughout the entire interview, never looking away.

Related Terms

  • antonym: twinkling - shining with a flickering or sparkling light
  • synonym: unblinking - not blinking or moving the eyes
