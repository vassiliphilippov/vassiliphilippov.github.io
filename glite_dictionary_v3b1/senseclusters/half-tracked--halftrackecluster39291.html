half-tracked

adjective
vehicle with tracks at rear, wheels at front
6.5 6.6
Pronunciation: /ˈhæf.trækt/ (AmE), /ˈhɑːf.trækt/ (BrE)
(of a vehicle) having caterpillar tracks on the rear and wheels in front, typically for better traction and mobility over rough terrain.

Examples

A half-tracked vehicle
Engineers designed a new half-tracked machine for improved performance on snow.
The army deployed several half-tracked vehicles to navigate the muddy terrain.
The old half-tracked transport was restored for the military museum.
