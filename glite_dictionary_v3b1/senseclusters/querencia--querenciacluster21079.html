querencia

noun
bull's favored spot
Pronunciation: / keɪˈrɛnsiə / (AmE), / keˈrɛnθiə / (BrE)
the area in a bullring where a bull feels safest and strongest, often returning there during a fight

Examples

Bullring querencia
The matador watched as the bull retreated to its querencia after each charge.
Understanding a bull's querencia is crucial for anyone studying bullfighting techniques.
The animal refused to leave its querencia, making the fight more challenging for the matador.

  • synonym: refuge - a place of safety or shelter
