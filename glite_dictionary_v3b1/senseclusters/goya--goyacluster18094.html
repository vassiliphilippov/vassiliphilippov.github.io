goya

noun
transport into fantasy
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɔɪə / (AmE), / ˈɡɔɪə / (BrE)
a feeling of being deeply transported into an imagined world, as if a story or fantasy were real

Examples

In goya state
As the storyteller spoke, the children listened in goya, completely absorbed by the tale.
Reading the fantasy novel filled her with a sense of goya that lasted long after she finished.
He experienced goya while watching the magical world unfold on the movie screen.

Related Terms

  • antonym: disillusionment - a feeling of disappointment when something is not as good as imagined
  • synonym: enchantment - a feeling of great pleasure and wonder, as if under a spell
