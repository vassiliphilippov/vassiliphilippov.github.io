nonlucid

adjective
not clear-minded
8.4
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.ˈluː.sɪd/ /; / /nən.ˈluː.sɪd/ (AmE), /nɒn.ˈluː.sɪd/ /; / /nən.ˈluː.sɪd/ (BrE)
not in a clear or rational state of mind; confused, disoriented, or delirious

Examples
