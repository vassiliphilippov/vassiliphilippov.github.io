nonlucid

adjective
not clear-minded
Pronunciation: / nɑnˈluːsɪd / (AmE), / nɒnˈluːsɪd / (BrE)
not in a clear or rational state of mind; confused, disoriented, or delirious

Examples

Nonlucid patient
After the surgery, she was still nonlucid and unable to answer questions properly.
The patient became nonlucid as his fever worsened throughout the night.
During the episode, he appeared nonlucid and did not recognize his family members.

Related Terms

  • synonym: confused - unable to think clearly or understand
  • synonym: delirious - in a disturbed state of mind, often due to illness
  • antonym: lucid - thinking or expressing oneself clearly
