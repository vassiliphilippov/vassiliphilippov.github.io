dahoon

noun
wood from dahoon tree
Pronunciation: / dəˈhuːn / (AmE), / dəˈhuːn / (BrE)
the wood or timber of the dahoon tree, valued for its resistance to rot and used in carpentry or turning

Examples

Dahoon wood
The craftsman selected dahoon for the table legs because of its durability.
Because dahoon resists decay, it is often chosen for outdoor furniture.
They carved the decorative bowl from a single piece of dahoon.
