defensor

noun
protector or defender
Pronunciation: / dɪˈfɛnsɔr / (AmE), / dɪˈfɛnsɔː / (BrE)
a person who defends or protects something, especially in a historical or formal context

Examples

Defensor of the city
The defensor of the ancient fortress was celebrated in local legends.
During the trial, a skilled defensor argued on behalf of the accused.
In medieval times, a defensor was often responsible for the safety of a town.

Related Terms

  • antonym: attacker - a person who tries to harm or defeat someone or something
  • synonym: defender - a person who protects someone or something
  • synonym: protector - someone who guards or shields from harm
