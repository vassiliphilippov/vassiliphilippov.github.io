lumbosacral

adjective
of the lower back and pelvic spine
8.7
Pronunciation: / ˌlʌm.boʊˈseɪ.krəl / (AmE), / ˌlʌm.bəʊˈseɪ.krəl / (BrE)
relating to the lower back and the back part of the pelvis between the hips, especially the region where the lumbar spine meets the sacrum

Examples

An MRI was ordered to investigate the lumbosacral area for possible nerve compression.
He underwent surgery to relieve pressure on his lumbosacral spine.
