chattery kid

noun
talkative or noisy child
2.5
Pronunciation: / ˈtʃætəri kɪd / (AmE), / ˈtʃætəri kɪd / (BrE)
A child who talks a lot and makes a lot of noise, often in an excitable way.

Examples

A chattery kid in class

One chattery kid dominated the conversation at the birthday party.
The teacher struggled to quiet the chattery kid during the lesson.
