fanfic

verb
to write fan fiction
Pronunciation: / ˈfænˌfɪk / (AmE), / ˈfænˌfɪk / (BrE)
to write fan fiction using characters, settings, or worlds from an existing work of fiction

Examples

To fanfic a book
Many young writers fanfic their favorite book series to explore new storylines.
She loves to fanfic popular movies and share her stories online.
During the summer, he decided to fanfic the adventures of his favorite superheroes.

Related Terms

  • antonym: plagiarize - to copy someone else's work without permission
  • synonym: write - to create text or stories
