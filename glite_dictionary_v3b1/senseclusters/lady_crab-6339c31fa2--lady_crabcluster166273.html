lady crab

noun
a spotted crab of the US Atlantic coast
2.3
Pronunciation: / ˈleɪdi kræb / (AmE), / ˈleɪdi kræb / (BrE)
a brightly spotted crab found on sandy beaches along the Atlantic coast of the United States, noted for the distinct spots on its shell

Examples

Bright lady crab
Children often collect lady crabs in buckets while exploring tide pools along the beach.
Fishermen sometimes use lady crabs as bait for larger fish.
The lady crab is easily identified by the purple spots on its shell.
