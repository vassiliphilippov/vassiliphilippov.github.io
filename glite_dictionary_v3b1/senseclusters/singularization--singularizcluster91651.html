singularization

noun
making singular
Pronunciation: / ˌsɪŋɡjələrəˈzeɪʃən / (AmE), / ˌsɪŋɡjʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən / (BrE)
the process of making something singular or treating it as a single entity

Examples

Singularization of a noun
The singularization of certain nouns can change the meaning of a sentence.
Teachers often focus on singularization when introducing new grammar rules to students.
In some languages, singularization is more complex than pluralization.
