enterprise

noun
a business or company
Pronunciation: / ˈɛn.tɚˌpraɪz / (AmE), / ˈen.tə.praɪz / (BrE)
A business or company, especially one that is new, difficult, or requires initiative.

Examples

A tech enterprise
She started a new enterprise focused on renewable energy solutions.
Success in such a competitive enterprise requires innovation and determination.
The software enterprise grew rapidly over the last five years.

Related Terms

  • synonym: company - an organization that sells goods or services in order to make money
  • synonym: corporation - a large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity
