Synonyms

  • business enterprise
    3.2 0.52/m
    business enterprise is a formal word for an organized profit-making company, while enterprise suggests something new or risky needing initiative.
  • business concern
    3.2 0.11/m
    business concern refers to an ongoing commercial organization with staff, while enterprise often highlights a new, bold, or risky company project.
  • commercial enterprise
    3.4 0.67/m
    commercial enterprise focuses on buying and selling for profit, while enterprise can mean any business, especially a new or challenging venture.
