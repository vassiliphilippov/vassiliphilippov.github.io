defeat

verb
to win a victory over
1.3 1.8
Pronunciation: /dɪ.ˈfiːt/ (AmE), /dɪ.ˈfiːt/ (BrE)
to win a victory over someone or something in a contest, conflict, or competition; to overcome

Examples

To defeat an opponent
He hopes to defeat the defending champion in tomorrow’s race.
It took her years to defeat her fear of public speaking.
The team managed to defeat its rivals in the final match.

Related Terms

  • synonym: conquer - to take control of (a place or people) by military force
  • antonym: lose - to be defeated; fail to win
  • synonym: overcome - to succeed in dealing with or controlling (a problem or difficulty)
  • antonym: surrender - to stop resisting to an opponent or authority
