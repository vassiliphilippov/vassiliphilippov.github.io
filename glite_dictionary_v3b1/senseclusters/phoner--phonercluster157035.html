phoner

noun
person making calls
Pronunciation: / ˈfoʊnər / (AmE), / ˈfəʊnə / (BrE)
a person whose job is to make phone calls, especially for sales, surveys, or information gathering

Examples

A phoner at work
The company hired a new phoner to conduct customer satisfaction surveys every week.
As a phoner, she spent most of her day speaking with clients over the phone.
Each phoner was given a list of numbers to call for the fundraising campaign.

Related Terms

  • synonym: caller - a person who makes a phone call
  • synonym: telemarketer - a person who sells products or services by phone
