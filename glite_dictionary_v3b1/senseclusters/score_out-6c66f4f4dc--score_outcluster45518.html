score out

verb
to cross out or erase written words
5.1 4.2
Pronunciation: /skɔːr aʊt/ /; / /skoʊr aʊt/ (AmE), /skɔː aʊt/ (BrE)
to remove by drawing a line through written words; to cross out or erase something written, especially by rubbing or marking it.

She scored out her ex-partner’s name from all her old diaries.
