Christian name

noun
first/given name, esp. at baptism
4.1
Pronunciation: / ˈkrɪstʃən neɪm / (AmE), / ˈkrɪstʃən neɪm / (BrE)
a person's first name, especially one given at baptism or christening in a Christian context

Examples

The Christian name
His Christian name is John, but everyone calls him Jack.
In some traditions, a Christian name is chosen at the baptism ceremony.
She wrote her Christian name and surname on the form.

Related Terms

  • synonym: given name - the name given to a person at birth or baptism, as distinct from a surname
  • antonym: surname - a hereditary name shared with other family members, as opposed to a given name
