Louboutin

proper noun
French shoe designer
Pronunciation: / luːˈbuːtæn / (AmE), / luːˈbuːtæn / (BrE)
Christian Louboutin, a French fashion designer known for creating luxury shoes with signature red soles.

Examples

Louboutin the designer
She saved for months to buy a pair of Louboutin heels for her wedding.
The signature red soles instantly identified the shoes as Louboutin creations.
Fashion magazines often feature Louboutin in their luxury accessories sections.

  • synonym: designer - a person who creates original clothing or accessories
