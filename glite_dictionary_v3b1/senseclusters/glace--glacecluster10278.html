glace

adjective
smooth and shiny
8.1
Pronunciation: / ɡlɑːˈseɪ / (AmE), / ɡlæˈseɪ / (BrE)
having a smooth, shiny, or glazed surface, especially referring to fabrics or polished finishes

Examples

Glace fabric
The designer chose a glace fabric to give the dress a luxurious sheen.
Her shoes had a glace finish that caught the light beautifully at the party.
The invitation was printed on glace paper for an elegant touch.
