volumetric

adjective
relating to 3D volumetric media
7.9
Pronunciation: / \u02ccvo\u028a.lju\u02c8m\u025b.tr\u026ak / (AmE), / \u02ccv\u0252l.ju\u02ccm\u025b.tr\u026ak / (BrE)
(in media and computing) relating to the creation, capture, representation, or rendering of three-dimensional content represented as volumes rather than flat images or meshes.

Examples

Volumetric video
The studio showcased volumetric capture technology for immersive VR scenes.
They used volumetric video to record the actor's performance for the VR experience.
Streaming platforms are beginning to support volumetric media so viewers can move within recorded scenes.

Related Terms

  • synonym: three-dimensional - having or appearing to have height, width, and depth; existing in three dimensions
  • synonym: volumetric video - video technology that records and represents subjects as 3D volumes so viewers can change viewpoint
