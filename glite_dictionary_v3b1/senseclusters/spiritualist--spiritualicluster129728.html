spiritualist

adjective
relating to spiritualism
Pronunciation: / ˈspɪr.ɪ.tʃu.ə.lɪst / (AmE), / ˈspɪr.ɪ.tjʊə.lɪst / (BrE)
relating to the belief or practice of spiritualism, especially the idea that the spirits of the dead can communicate with the living

Examples

Spiritualist beliefs
She attended several spiritualist meetings out of curiosity about the supernatural.
The spiritualist community held séances to try to contact the spirits of loved ones.
This literature is filled with spiritualist beliefs and references to communication with the afterlife.
