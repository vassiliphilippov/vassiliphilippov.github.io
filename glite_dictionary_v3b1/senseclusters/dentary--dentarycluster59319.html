dentary

noun
lower jaw bone
9.2
Pronunciation: /ˈden.tər.iː/ /; / /ˈden.triː/ (AmE), /ˈden.tər.iː/ /; / /ˈden.triː/ (BrE)
the main bone forming the lower jaw in most vertebrates, especially mammals

Examples

A dentary bone
The paleontologist carefully examined the dentary to identify the species of the fossil.
In mammals, the dentary is the only bone in the lower jaw.
Damage to the dentary can affect an animal's ability to eat.
