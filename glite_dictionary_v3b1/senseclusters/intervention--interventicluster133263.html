intervention

noun
the act of inserting or interposing
Pronunciation: / ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃən / (AmE), / ˌɪntəˈvɛnʃən / (BrE)
the action or process of putting something in between or among other things; interposing.

Examples

Intervention in discussion
Her intervention in the conversation shifted the topic to safer ground.
The intervention of new evidence changed the outcome of the deliberations.
The sudden intervention of clouds blocked the sunlight from reaching the valley.

Related Terms

  • synonym: interposition - the act of placing or inserting between
