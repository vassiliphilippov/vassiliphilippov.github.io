dental hygienist

noun
professional who provides preventive dental care
Pronunciation: / ˈdɛntəl haɪˈdʒinɪst / (AmE), / ˈdɛntəl hʌɪˈdʒiːnɪst / (BrE)
a person trained and licensed to clean teeth, examine oral health, and provide preventive dental care under the supervision of a dentist

Examples

The dental hygienist cleans teeth
During my appointment, the dental hygienist took x-rays before the dentist arrived.
She decided to become a dental hygienist because she enjoys helping people maintain healthy smiles.
The dental hygienist carefully cleaned my teeth and gave me tips on oral hygiene.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dentist - a medical professional who treats diseases and conditions of the teeth and mouth
