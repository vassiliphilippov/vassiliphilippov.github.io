gobble

verb
eat quickly without chewing properly
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈɡɑːbəl / (AmE), / ˈɡɒbəl / (BrE)
to eat food quickly and often noisily, without proper chewing

Examples

To gobble food quickly
He was so hungry that he gobbled his lunch in just a few minutes.
Please don't gobble your food; try to eat more slowly.
The children gobbled their breakfast before running outside to play.

Related Terms

  • synonym: bolt - to eat food quickly, often without chewing properly
  • synonym: devour - to eat something quickly and eagerly
  • antonym: nibble - to eat something by taking small bites
