red alert

noun
highest state of warning
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛd əˈlɝt / (AmE), / ˌrɛd əˈlɜːt / (BrE)
the highest state of alert, especially warning of imminent danger or enemy attack

Examples

Issue a red alert
Hospitals issued a red alert due to the sudden influx of patients from the accident.
The city was placed on a red alert after receiving credible threats of an attack.
When the hurricane approached, authorities declared a red alert and urged people to evacuate.
