takeoff booster

noun
first stage of a multistage rocket
Pronunciation: / ˈteɪkˌɔf ˈbuːstər / (AmE), / ˈteɪkɒf ˈbuːstə / (BrE)
the first stage of a multistage rocket that provides the initial thrust required for liftoff.
