breathe one's last

verb
to die (idiom)
3.8
Pronunciation: / briːð wʌnz læst / (AmE), / briːð wʌnz lɑːst / (BrE)

Examples

To breathe his last breath

Surrounded by her children, she breathed her last in the quiet of the night.
