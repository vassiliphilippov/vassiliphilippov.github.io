Brechtian

noun
Brecht follower
9.9
Pronunciation: /ˈbrek.tɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈbrek.ti.ən/ (AmE), /ˈbrek.tɪ.ən/ /; / /ˈbrek.ti.ən/ (BrE)
a person who supports or uses the theatrical methods and ideas of Bertolt Brecht, especially those that encourage critical thinking in the audience

Examples

A true Brechtian
As a dedicated Brechtian, she always encouraged actors to break the fourth wall.
The play was directed by a well-known Brechtian who emphasized social commentary.
Many young directors today consider themselves Brechtian in their approach to theater.
