endothelial

adjective
relating to the endothelium
Pronunciation: / ˌɛndoʊˈθiːliəl / (AmE), / ˌɛndəʊˈθiːliəl / (BrE)
relating to or situated in the endothelium, the thin layer of cells lining blood vessels and some other internal surfaces of the body

Examples

Endothelial cells
Endothelial cells play a crucial role in the regulation of blood flow.
Damage to the endothelial layer can lead to vascular diseases.
Some medications help improve endothelial function in patients with heart problems.

Related Terms

  • synonym: endothelium - the thin layer of cells lining blood vessels and internal body surfaces
