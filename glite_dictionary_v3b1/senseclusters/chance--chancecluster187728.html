chance

verb
to take a risk
Pronunciation: / tʃæns / (AmE), / tʃɑːns / (BrE)
to do something risky in the hope of gaining a positive result; to take a risk

Examples

To chance a guess
He decided to chance the shortcut through the woods despite the storm.
She didn't have a ticket but chanced entering the concert anyway.
We could miss the rain if we chanced leaving now.

Related Terms

  • synonym: risk - to do something that may result in loss or failure
  • synonym: venture - to undertake a risky or daring journey or course of action
