lambkin

noun
a very young lamb
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈlæm.kɪn / (AmE), / ˈlæm.kɪn / (BrE)
A very young or small lamb, often used as a term of endearment.

Examples

A small lambkin
He wrote a poem about a lost lambkin seeking its mother in the dusk.
She gently cradled the tiny lambkin in her arms to keep it warm.
The children watched as the lambkin wobbled on its unsteady legs in the meadow.
