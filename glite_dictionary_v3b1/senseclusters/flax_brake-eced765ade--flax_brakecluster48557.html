flax brake

noun
machine for separating flax fibers
9.9
Pronunciation: / flæks breɪk / (AmE), / flæks breɪk / (BrE)
a machine used to separate the woody part of flax stems from the fibrous material.

Examples

A flax brake machine

The old farmer demonstrated how to use the flax brake to process raw flax.
