granola-head

noun
health-conscious, environmentally aware person (slang)
7.5 7.9
Pronunciation: /grə.ˈnoʊ.lə hed/ /; / /ˈgræ.nə.lə hed/ (AmE), /grə.ˈnəʊ.lə hed/ /; / /ˈgræ.nə.lə hed/ (BrE)
An informal, often mildly humorous or critical term for a person who is notably health-conscious and environmentally aware, especially in diet and lifestyle.
