freight

noun
goods transported in bulk
7.9 3.2
Pronunciation: /freɪt/ (AmE), /freɪt/ (BrE)
goods transported in bulk, or the act of transporting goods commercially

Examples

Freight train

Railroads have played a major role in American freight transport for over a century.
