epitomize

verb
be a perfect example of
6.6
Pronunciation: / ɪˈpɪtəˌmaɪz / (AmE), / ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz / (BrE)
to be a perfect example of something; to embody the main characteristics of a particular quality or type

Examples

To epitomize bravery
He epitomized bravery during his years of service.
Her generosity truly epitomizes the spirit of the holiday season.
These paintings epitomize the style of the impressionist era.
His calm demeanor epitomises professionalism in a high-stress environment.
She truly epitomises the spirit of generosity through her selfless actions.
The new library epitomises modern architecture in both form and function.
