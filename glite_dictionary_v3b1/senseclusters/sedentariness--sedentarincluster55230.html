sedentariness

noun
state of inactivity
8.2
Pronunciation: / sɛˈdɛntərinəs / (AmE), / sɛˈdɛntərinəs / (BrE)
the state of being inactive or spending most of the time sitting with little physical movement

Examples

Prolonged sedentariness
Prolonged sedentariness can increase the risk of heart disease and other health issues.
Many office jobs contribute to sedentariness because employees sit for most of the day.
Doctors recommend regular movement to reduce the negative effects of sedentariness.

Related Terms

  • antonym: activity - the state of being active or moving
  • synonym: inactivity - lack of physical activity or movement
