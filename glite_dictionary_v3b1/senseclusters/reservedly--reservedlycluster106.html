reservedly

adverb
in a restrained or formal manner
6.1
Pronunciation: / rɪˈzɝːvɪdli / (AmE), / rɪˈzɜːvɪdli / (BrE)
In a manner that is restrained, formal, or unwilling to show feelings or opinions.

Examples

To speak reservedly
She answered reservedly, choosing her words with care.
The manager spoke reservedly about the company’s future plans.
Though he approved of the decision, he expressed his support reservedly.

Related Terms

  • antonym: openly - in an open or frank manner
