order Mysidacea

noun
order of small shrimp-like crustaceans
Pronunciation: / ˈɔrdər ˌmaɪsɪˈdeɪsiə / (AmE), / ˈɔːdə ˌmʌɪsɪˈdeɪsɪə / (BrE)
a taxonomic order of small, shrimp-like crustaceans commonly known as opossum shrimps, found in marine and freshwater environments.

The order Mysidacea is distinguished by the presence of a brood pouch in females.
