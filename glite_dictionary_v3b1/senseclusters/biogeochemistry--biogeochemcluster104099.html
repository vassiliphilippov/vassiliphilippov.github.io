biogeochemistry

noun
study of chemical cycles
8.8
Pronunciation: / ˌbaɪoʊˌdʒiːoʊˈkɛmɪstri / (AmE), / ˌbaɪəʊˌdʒiːəʊˈkɛmɪstri /; / ˌbʌɪəʊˌdʒiːəʊˈkɛmɪstri / (BrE)
