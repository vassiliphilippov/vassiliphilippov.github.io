penholder

noun
container for pens
2.9
Pronunciation: / ˈpɛnhoʊldər / (AmE), / ˈpɛnhəʊldə / (BrE)
a container or device designed to hold one or more pens, typically placed on a desk

Examples

Desk penholder
She placed her favorite pen in the penholder on her desk.
The penholder was filled with colorful pens and markers for easy access.
He bought a new penholder to keep his workspace tidy and organized.
