headgear

noun
framework for hoisting at a mine shaft
3.3
Pronunciation: / ˈhɛdˌɡɪr / (AmE), / ˈhɛdˌɡɪə / (BrE)
The supporting framework and hoisting equipment situated above the shaft at the entrance of a mine, used for raising ore or workers.

Examples

Mine headgear
Routine inspections of the headgear are essential for ensuring the safety of miners and equipment.
The collapse of the headgear halted all operations until repairs could be made.
The old coal mine still has its original headgear standing as a historical monument.
