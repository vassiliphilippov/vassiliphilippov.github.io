outboard

adjective
away from midline of vessel/aircraft
Pronunciation: / ˈaʊtˌbɔrd / (AmE), / ˈaʊtbɔːd / (BrE)
Situated or positioned away from the central line or axis of a vessel or aircraft, especially towards the side.

Examples

Outboard wing section
Maintenance was required on the outboard section of the airplane's left wing.
Place the emergency equipment in the outboard compartments for easy access.
The outboard engines are mounted far from the boat's centerline.

Related Terms

  • antonym: inboard - located closer to the center or midline of a vessel or aircraft
