tuan

noun
respectful male title
Pronunciation: / ˈtuːɑːn / (AmE), / ˈtuːɑːn / (BrE)
a respectful title used to address or refer to a man, especially in Malay-speaking regions

Examples

Addressing tuan politely
The villagers addressed the British officer as tuan during the ceremony.
He greeted the elder with a respectful bow and called him tuan.
In some regions, it is customary to refer to male teachers as tuan.

Related Terms

  • antonym: madam - a polite or respectful way of addressing a woman
  • synonym: sir - a polite or respectful way of addressing a man
