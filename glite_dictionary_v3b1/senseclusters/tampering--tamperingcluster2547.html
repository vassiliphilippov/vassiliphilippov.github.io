tampering

noun
illegal or secret alteration
3.6
Pronunciation: / ˈtæmpərɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈtæmpərɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of altering something secretly or improperly, often to gain an advantage or cause harm

Examples

Any tampering with system
Any tampering with the security system will result in an alarm being triggered.
Election tampering is a serious offense that undermines trust in the process.
The investigation revealed evidence of tampering with the official records.

Related Terms

  • synonym: manipulation - the action of manipulating something, especially for a fraudulent purpose
