dissocial

adjective
not sociable
8.4
Pronunciation: / dɪˈsoʊʃəl / (AmE), / dɪˈsəʊʃəl / (BrE)
not sociable; tending to avoid social contact or the company of others

Examples

Dissocial personality traits
After years in the wilderness he became rather dissocial, preferring his own company to crowded rooms.
Her dissocial behavior made it difficult for her to make new friends at school.
The dissocial man rarely attended family gatherings or community events.

Related Terms

  • synonym: antisocial - avoiding the company of others or hostile to social order
  • antonym: sociable - enjoying the company of others; friendly
  • synonym: unsociable - not enjoying or seeking the company of others
