Synonyms

  • nullify
    7.2 0.7/m
    nullify makes something legally invalid and ends its legal effect, while strike down is when a court declares a law void.
  • quash
    8.1 0.5/m
    quash officially rejects a court order or decision as invalid, while strike down refers specifically to a court declaring a law no longer in effect.
  • rescind
    7.9 2/m
    rescind officially cancels an order, agreement, or law by authority, while strike down is a court ruling that removes a law's validity.
  • revoke
    6.2 4/m
    revoke officially takes away the legal force of a rule, license, or law, while strike down usually describes a court's invalidation of a law.
  • cancel
    1.0 10/m
    cancel makes something no longer valid or active in general situations, while strike down means a court formally declares a law invalid and ends its effect.
