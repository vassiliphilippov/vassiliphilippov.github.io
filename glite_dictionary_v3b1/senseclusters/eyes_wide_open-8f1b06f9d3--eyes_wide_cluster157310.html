eyes wide open

adverb
fully aware of consequences
1.5
Pronunciation: / aɪz waɪd ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / aɪz waɪd ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
with full awareness of the possible consequences or risks involved; not ignorant or naive about what might happen

Examples

With eyes wide open decision
He made his decision with his eyes wide open, so he can't blame anyone else for the outcome.
She accepted the job offer with her eyes wide open, knowing it would mean long hours and hard work.
They entered into the agreement eyes wide open, fully understanding the associated risks.

Related Terms

  • antonym: eyes shut - unaware, not conscious of risks or consequences
