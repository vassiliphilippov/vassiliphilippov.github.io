eyes wide open

adverb
fully aware of consequences
1.5 4.3
Pronunciation: / aɪz waɪd ˈoʊpən / (AmE), / aɪz waɪd ˈəʊpən / (BrE)
with full awareness of the possible consequences or risks involved; not ignorant or naive about what might happen

Examples

They entered into the agreement eyes wide open, fully understanding the associated risks.
