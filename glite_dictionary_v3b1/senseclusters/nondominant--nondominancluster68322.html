nondominant

adjective
less used side
6.0
Pronunciation: /nɑːn.də.ˈmɪn.ənt/ (AmE), /nɒn.də.ˈmɪn.ənt/ (BrE)
relating to the side of the body or brain that is less used or not preferred, such as a hand or eye.

Examples

Nondominant hand
She learned to write with her nondominant hand after injuring her right arm.
The test measured coordination in both the dominant and nondominant legs.
Artists sometimes practice drawing with their nondominant hand to improve their skills.
