cava

noun
Spanish sparkling wine
Pronunciation: / ˈkɑːvə / (AmE), / ˈkɑːvə / (BrE)
a sparkling white or rosé wine from Spain, typically served at celebrations and made using traditional methods

Examples

A glass of cava

At Spanish weddings, it is common to serve cava for the toast.
We enjoyed a glass of cava while watching the fireworks on New Year's Eve.
