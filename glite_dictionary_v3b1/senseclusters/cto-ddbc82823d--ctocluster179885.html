CTO

noun
technology executive
4.6
Pronunciation: / si ti oʊ / (AmE), / siː tiː əʊ / (BrE)
the executive in a company who is responsible for its technological direction and strategy

Examples

Tech CTO role
The CTO presented the company's new software strategy at the annual meeting.
After ten years as a software engineer, she was promoted to CTO of the startup.
Every CTO must stay updated on the latest technology trends to keep the company competitive.
