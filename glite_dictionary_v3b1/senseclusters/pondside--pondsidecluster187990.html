pondside

noun
area beside a pond
3.7
Pronunciation: / ˈpɑndsaɪd / (AmE), / ˈpɒndsaɪd / (BrE)
the area of land located beside or next to a pond

Examples

A pondside picnic
They built a small cabin on the pondside for weekend getaways.
Children played quietly along the pondside while their parents fished.
Wildflowers grew in abundance at the pondside during spring.
