American English orthography

noun
U.S. English spelling conventions
Pronunciation: / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔrˈθɑɡrəfi / (AmE), / əˈmɛrɪkən ˈɪŋɡlɪʃ ɔːˈθɒɡrəfi / (BrE)
the set of spelling conventions and rules used for English in the United States, differing in some aspects from other varieties of English

Examples

American English orthography rules
