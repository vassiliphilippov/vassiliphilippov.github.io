remittance man

noun
man living abroad supported by family money
9.9 8.4
Pronunciation: /rɪ.ˈmɪt.əns mæn/ /; / /rəm.ˈɪt.əns mæn/ /; / /riːm.ˈɪt.əns mæn/ (AmE), /rɪ.ˈmɪt.əns mæn/ /; / /rəm.ˈɪt.əns mæn/ (BrE)
a man who lives abroad and is supported by money sent regularly from his family or home country, often because he is considered a source of embarrassment or trouble at home

Examples

A remittance man abroad
He became known as a remittance man, living quietly in Canada on funds from his wealthy family.
Stories of the remittance man often evoke images of lonely outcasts surviving on grudging charity from home.
The British colonies were once filled with remittance men seeking new beginnings and anonymity.
