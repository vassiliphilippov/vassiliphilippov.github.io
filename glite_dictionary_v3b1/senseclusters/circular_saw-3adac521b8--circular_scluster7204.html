circular saw

noun
power saw with a rotating circular blade
Pronunciation: / ˈsɝːkjələr sɔ / (AmE), / ˈsɜːkjʊlə sɔː / (BrE)
a power-driven saw with a circular steel blade having teeth around its edge, used for cutting wood, metal, or other materials

Examples

A circular saw blade
Always wear safety goggles when operating a circular saw.
He bought a new circular saw for his home renovation project.
The carpenter used a circular saw to cut the plywood quickly and accurately.
