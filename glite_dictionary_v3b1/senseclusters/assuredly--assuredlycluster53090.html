assuredly

adverb
certainly; without doubt
Pronunciation: / əˈʃʊrədli / (AmE), / əˈʃʊərɪdli / (BrE)
Without any doubt; certainly; with confidence.

Examples

To assuredly succeed
He will assuredly pass the exam if he continues to work this hard.
Her statement was assuredly true, based on all available evidence.
The museum will assuredly be open tomorrow, despite the holiday.

Related Terms

  • synonym: certainly - without any doubt; surely
  • antonym: doubtfully - in a way that shows uncertainty or lack of confidence
