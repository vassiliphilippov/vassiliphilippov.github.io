mistiming

noun
timing error
Pronunciation: / mɪsˈtaɪmɪŋ / (AmE), / mɪsˈtaɪmɪŋ / (BrE)
an error or problem caused by doing something at the wrong time

Examples

A mistiming error
The dancer's mistiming caused her to miss the cue for the next move.
A slight mistiming in the relay race led to a dropped baton.
His mistiming of the punch meant he missed the target completely.
The mistiming of his speech made the audience confused about the main point.
Her mistiming during the piano performance caused her to miss several notes.
A simple mistiming in the kitchen can ruin an entire meal.

Related Terms

  • synonym: miscalculation - an incorrect judgment or estimation
  • antonym: synchronization - the act of occurring at the same time or rate
  • antonym: timing - the choice or control of when something happens
