cloddishly

adverb
rudely or boorishly
9.6
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑːdɪʃli / (AmE), / ˈklɒdɪʃli / (BrE)
in a rude, boorish, or ill-mannered manner.

Examples

Acts cloddishly
He cloddishly pushed past everyone in line without saying excuse me.
The guest cloddishly ignored the host's instructions and made a mess in the living room.
She cloddishly interrupted the conversation, not caring about anyone else's opinion.

Related Terms

  • synonym: boorishly - in a rough or uncultured manner
  • antonym: politely - in a respectful and considerate way
  • synonym: rudely - in an impolite or disrespectful way
