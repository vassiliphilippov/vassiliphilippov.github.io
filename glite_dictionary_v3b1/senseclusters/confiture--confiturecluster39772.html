confiture

noun
fruit preserve or jam
5.0
Pronunciation: / kɒn.fɪˈtjʊr / (AmE), / kɒn.fɪˈtjʊə / (BrE)
A preserve or jam made by cooking fruit with sugar, often used especially of French-style preserves.

Examples

Fruit confiture
Her grandmother's apricot confiture is famous throughout the village.
The recipe calls for a generous spoonful of cherry confiture layered on the cake.
We were served fresh bread with homemade confiture at breakfast.

Related Terms

  • synonym: jam - sweet spread made from fruit and sugar
  • synonym: preserve - food made by preserving fruit in sugar
