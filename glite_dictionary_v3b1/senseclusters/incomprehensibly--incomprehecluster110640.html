incomprehensibly

adverb
in an unclear way
8.0
Pronunciation: / ˌɪnˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsəbli /; / ˌɪnkɑːmˈprɛhənsəbli / (AmE), / ˌɪnˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsəbli /; / ˌɪnkɒmˈprɛhənsəbli / (BrE)
in a way that is impossible or extremely difficult to understand

Examples

Speak incomprehensibly fast
The professor spoke so incomprehensibly that most students could not follow the lecture.
The technical manual was written incomprehensibly, making it hard for users to assemble the product.
He explained the rules incomprehensibly, leaving everyone confused about how to play the game.
The instructions were written so incomprehensibly that no one could follow them.
He spoke incomprehensibly fast, making it hard for the audience to keep up.
The plot of the movie became incomprehensibly complex after the first hour.
