back forty

noun
remote section of farmland
8.6
Pronunciation: / bæk ˈfɔɹ.ti / (AmE), / bæk ˈfɔː.ti / (BrE)
an area of land, especially the remote or less accessible part of a farm, often measuring forty acres

Examples

The back forty land
He spent Saturday clearing brush from his back forty to prepare for planting.
The old barn sits way out on the back forty, far from the main house.
They let the cattle graze on the back forty during the summer months.
