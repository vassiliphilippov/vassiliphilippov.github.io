Poncirus

noun
genus of thorny shrubs; trifoliate orange
Pronunciation: / pɒnˈsaɪrəs /; / ˈpɒn.sɪ.rəs / (AmE), / pɒnˈsɪərəs /; / ˈpɒn.sɪ.rəs / (BrE)
a small genus of thorny shrubs or small trees in the citrus family, especially known for the trifoliate orange
