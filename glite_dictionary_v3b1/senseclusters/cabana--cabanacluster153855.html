cabana

noun
small shelter by sea or pool for changing clothes
6.5
Pronunciation: / kəˈbænə / (AmE), / kəˈbænə / (BrE)
a small shelter, often tent-like, typically located beside the sea or a swimming pool and used as a dressing room or relaxation area

Examples

A cabana by the beach
She changed into her swimsuit in the cabana by the beach.
The resort offers private cabanas for guests looking to relax in comfort.
We rented a cabana near the pool to have some privacy and shade.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hut - a small, simple shelter or dwelling
