representationalist

adjective
about representing reality
Pronunciation: / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəlɪst / (AmE), / ˌrɛprɪˌzɛnˈteɪʃənəlɪst / (BrE)
relating to the belief or practice that perceptions, ideas, or artworks represent external reality.

Examples

Representationalist theory of art
The artist's representationalist style focused on painting realistic landscapes and city scenes.
Some philosophers argue from a representationalist perspective about how we perceive the world.
Her representationalist approach to literature emphasizes accurate depictions of everyday life.

Related Terms

  • antonym: abstract - not representing external reality directly
  • synonym: realist - relating to representing things as they are in reality
