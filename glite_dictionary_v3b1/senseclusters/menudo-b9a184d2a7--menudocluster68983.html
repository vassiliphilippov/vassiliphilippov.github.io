Menudo

proper noun
Puerto Rican boy band
Pronunciation: / meˈnuːdoʊ / (AmE), / meˈnuːdəʊ / (BrE)
a Puerto Rican boy band formed in the 1970s, known for launching the careers of several Latin pop singers
