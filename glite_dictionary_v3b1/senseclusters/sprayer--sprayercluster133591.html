sprayer

noun
device that sprays liquid as a mist
1.0
Pronunciation: / ˈspreɪ.ɚ / (AmE), / ˈspreɪ.ə / (BrE)
a device or container for turning a liquid, such as water or perfume, into a fine mist or spray

Examples

Handheld sprayer
After cleaning, he used a disinfectant sprayer to sanitize the kitchen surfaces.
She filled the sprayer with water to mist her plants every morning.
The perfume sprayer released a fine, pleasant mist with each press.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atomizer - a device that turns liquid into a fine spray, especially used for perfume
