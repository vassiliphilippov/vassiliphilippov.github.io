rolling wave

noun
long sea wave advancing to shore
7.4
Pronunciation: / ˈroʊlɪŋ weɪv / (AmE), / ˈrəʊlɪŋ weɪv / (BrE)
a long, heavy sea wave moving toward the shore

Examples

A rolling wave crashes
As the storm approached, the rolling waves grew taller and more powerful.
From the cliff, we watched each rolling wave crash against the rocks below.
The surfer waited patiently for the perfect rolling wave to carry him to shore.
