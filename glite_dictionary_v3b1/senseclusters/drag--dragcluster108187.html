drag

noun
the act of pulling with effort or force
Pronunciation: / dræɡ / (AmE), / dræɡ / (BrE)
the act of pulling something along with effort or force, especially over a surface

Examples

A long drag
After the drag through the thick mud, her boots were completely covered.
The long drag up the mountain left them all breathless.
You could see the marks left by the heavy drag of the sled across the snow.

Related Terms

  • synonym: pull - the act of exerting force to move something toward oneself
