mantichora

noun
a mythological monster with a man's head, lion's body, and scorpion's tail
Pronunciation: / mænˈtɪkərə / (AmE), / mænˈtɪkərə / (BrE)
a mythical creature with the head of a man (often with horns), the body of a lion, and the tail of a scorpion
