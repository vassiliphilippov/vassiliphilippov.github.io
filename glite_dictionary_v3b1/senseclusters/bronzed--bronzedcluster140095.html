bronzed

adjective
having suntanned skin
5.3
Pronunciation: / brɑːnzd / (AmE), / brɒnzd / (BrE)
(of skin) having a tan or light brown color from exposure to the sun.

Examples

Bronzed skin
She returned from her holiday with a beautifully bronzed complexion.
The lifeguard stood at the poolside, his skin bronzed by countless hours in the sun.
Their faces were bronzed from weeks spent hiking in the mountains.

Related Terms
