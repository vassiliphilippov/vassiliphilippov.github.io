stratospheric

adjective
of the stratosphere
6.9
Pronunciation: / ˌstrætəˈsfɪrɪk / (AmE), / ˌstrætəˈsfɪərɪk / (BrE)
relating to or occurring in the stratosphere, the layer of Earth's atmosphere above the troposphere

Examples

Stratospheric clouds
The scientists observed stratospheric changes after the volcanic eruption.
Ozone depletion is a major stratospheric concern for environmentalists.
Weather balloons are often launched to collect stratospheric data for research.

Related Terms

  • synonym: atmospheric - relating to the atmosphere
  • antonym: tropospheric - relating to the troposphere, the lowest layer of Earth's atmosphere
