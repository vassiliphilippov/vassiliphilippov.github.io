farmworker

noun
person working on farm
1.6
Pronunciation: /ˈfɑːrm.wɜːr.kər/ (AmE), /ˈfɑːm.wɜː.kə/ (BrE)
a person who does physical or manual work on a farm, such as planting, harvesting, or caring for animals

Examples

A farmworker plants crops
Many farmworkers start their day before sunrise to tend to the crops.
The rights of farmworkers are often discussed in debates about fair labor practices.
During harvest season, the farm hired extra farmworkers to help with the workload.
The farmworker started planting seeds early in the morning.
Many farmworkers travel from region to region following the harvest seasons.
A farmworker must often work long hours outdoors in all kinds of weather.

Related Terms

  • synonym: farmhand - a worker employed to do manual labor on a farm
  • synonym: laborer - a person who does physical work, especially outdoors
