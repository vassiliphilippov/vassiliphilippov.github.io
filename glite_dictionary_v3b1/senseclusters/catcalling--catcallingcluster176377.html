catcalling

noun
public verbal harassment
Pronunciation: / ˈkætˌkɔlɪŋ / (AmE), / ˈkætˌkɔːlɪŋ / (BrE)
the act of making unwanted, often sexual or disrespectful comments or noises in public, usually directed at women
