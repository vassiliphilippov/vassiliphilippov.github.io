songfulness

noun
quality of being suitable for singing
Pronunciation: / ˈsɔŋfʊlnəs / (AmE), / ˈsɒŋfʊlnəs / (BrE)
the quality or property of being suitable for singing or marked by a melodious character

Examples

The songfulness of poetry
Critics praised the songfulness of her poetry, noting its natural rhythm and cadence.
Many composers aim for a sense of songfulness in their choral works.
The songfulness of the melody made it a favorite among folk singers.

Related Terms

  • synonym: tunefulness - the quality of being tuneful or melodious
