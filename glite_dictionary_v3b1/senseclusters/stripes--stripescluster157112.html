stripes

noun
military badge showing rank or service
Pronunciation: / straɪps / (AmE), / straɪps / (BrE)
A V-shaped badge or line on a military uniform, especially on the sleeve, indicating a person's rank or length of service.

Examples

Three stripes on sleeve
After his promotion, three stripes appeared on his sleeve.
New recruits hope to earn their stripes through hard work and dedication.
She wore her stripes proudly after years of service in the army.
