salvageable

adjective
able to be saved or recovered
Pronunciation: / ˈsæl.vɪ.dʒə.bəl / (AmE), / ˈsæl.vɪ.dʒə.bəl / (BrE)
able to be saved, repaired, or restored after damage, loss, or destruction

Examples

A salvageable item
Despite the fire damage, much of the artwork was still salvageable.
She wondered if their friendship was salvageable after the argument.
The mechanic said that the engine might be salvageable with some effort.

Related Terms

  • antonym: irreparable - not able to be repaired or saved
  • synonym: recoverable - able to be recovered or regained
