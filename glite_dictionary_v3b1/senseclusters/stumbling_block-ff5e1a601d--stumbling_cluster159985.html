stumbling block

noun
obstacle; hindrance to progress
Pronunciation: / ˈstʌmblɪŋ ˌblɑk / (AmE), / ˈstʌmblɪŋ ˌblɒk / (BrE)
a problem or obstacle that makes it difficult to achieve something or move forward

Examples

A stumbling block problem
Lack of funding has been a major stumbling block for the project.
Language differences can serve as a stumbling block in international business.
The committee identified several stumbling blocks to reaching an agreement.
Financial difficulties were the main stumbling block to launching their new business.
Regulatory approval has become a stumbling block for the drug's release.
The lack of experience proved to be a serious stumbling block for the young team.
