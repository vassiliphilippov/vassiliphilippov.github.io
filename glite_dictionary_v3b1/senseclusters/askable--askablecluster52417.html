askable

adjective
suitable to ask
Pronunciation: / ˈæskəbəl / (AmE), / ˈɑːskəbəl / (BrE)
able to be asked or suitable for asking, especially of a question or topic

Examples

Askable question

Some topics are not askable in certain cultures due to social norms.
The teacher assured the students that no subject was too sensitive or not askable in her class.
