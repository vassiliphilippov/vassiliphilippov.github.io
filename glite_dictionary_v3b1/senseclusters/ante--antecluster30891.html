ante

noun
initial stake in poker or gambling game
7.7
Pronunciation: / ˈæn.ti / (AmE), / ˈæn.ti / (BrE)
an amount of money that each player must put into the pot before a hand is dealt in a game of poker or similar gambling games

Examples

A poker ante
Each player must place an ante before the cards are dealt.
If you don't have enough to cover the ante, you can't join the game.
She tossed her ante into the pot without hesitation.

Related Terms

  • synonym: stake - an amount of money risked on the outcome of a game or contest
