aspect

noun
the visual appearance of a place or scene
4.8
Pronunciation: / ˈæs.pɛkt / (AmE), / ˈæs.pɛkt / (BrE)
the appearance or view of a place, especially the way it is seen by the eye

Examples

The aspect of the valley
From the garden, the aspect of the valley was particularly beautiful in the afternoon sun.
The southern aspect of the house offers stunning mountain views.
We chose the picnic spot for its pleasant aspect over the lake.
