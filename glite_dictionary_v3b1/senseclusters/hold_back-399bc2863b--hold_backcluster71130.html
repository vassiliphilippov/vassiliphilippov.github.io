hold back

verb
to delay action; to hesitate
Pronunciation: / hoʊld ˈbæk / (AmE), / həʊld ˈbæk / (BrE)
to hesitate or delay taking action; to wait before doing something

Examples

To hold back from acting
Despite his excitement, he decided to hold back and wait for more information.
She wanted to speak up, but something made her hold back.
The committee chose to hold back the final decision until all members were present.

Related Terms

  • synonym: hesitate - to pause before saying or doing something, often due to uncertainty
