pixel peeper

noun
someone obsessed with tiny details
Pronunciation: / ˈpɪksəl ˌpipər / (AmE), / ˈpɪksəl ˌpiːpə / (BrE)
(informal) a person who scrutinizes images or documents for the smallest flaws or details, often to an excessive degree

Examples

Pixel peepers in forums
Design forums are full of pixel peepers who love to analyze every image detail.
Instead of enjoying the photo, he became a pixel peeper and criticized the resolution.
Our manager is a true pixel peeper, noticing every tiny imperfection in the report.

Related Terms

  • synonym: nitpicker - someone who finds small or unimportant faults in something
