Bosniak

noun
ethnic group from Bosnia
Pronunciation: / ˈbɑzniˌæk / (AmE), / ˈbɒzniˌæk / (BrE)
a member of a South Slavic ethnic group primarily from Bosnia and Herzegovina, often associated with the Muslim community

Examples

A Bosniak person
The Bosniak community has a rich cultural heritage in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
He learned about the traditions of his Bosniak ancestors from his grandparents.
Many Bosniak families celebrate important holidays together in Sarajevo.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Bosnian - a person from Bosnia and Herzegovina
  • antonym: Croat - a member of a South Slavic ethnic group mainly from Croatia
  • antonym: Serb - a member of a South Slavic ethnic group mainly from Serbia
