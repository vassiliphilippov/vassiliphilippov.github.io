phosphoenolpyruvate

noun
metabolic compound
9.9
Pronunciation: / ˌfɑsfoʊˌinɑlpaɪˈruːveɪt /; / ˌfɑsfoʊˌinəlpaɪˈruːveɪt / (AmE), / ˌfɒsfəʊˌiːnɒlpaɪˈruːveɪt / (BrE)
