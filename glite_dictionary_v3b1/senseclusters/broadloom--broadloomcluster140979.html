broadloom

noun
carpet woven on a wide loom
Pronunciation: / ˈbrɔːdˌlum / (AmE), / ˈbrɔːdˌluːm / (BrE)
A carpet woven on a wide loom, typically sold in large rolls and designed to cover a floor with minimal seams.

Examples

A broadloom carpet
Replacing the old area rugs with broadloom gave the office a seamless look.
The entire living room was covered in plush broadloom.
They chose a neutral-colored broadloom carpet for the hotel suite.
