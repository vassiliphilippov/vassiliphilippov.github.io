difference

noun
disagreement or argument
Pronunciation: / ˈdɪf.ɚ.əns / (AmE), / ˈdɪf.ər.əns / (BrE)
a disagreement or argument about something important, especially between two or more people or groups

Examples

Difference of opinion
A difference of opinion led to heated debates in the meeting.
The couple cited irreconcilable differences as the reason for their divorce.
They tried to resolve their differences before signing the contract.

Related Terms

  • antonym: agreement - harmony in opinion or feeling; a negotiated and typically legally binding arrangement
  • synonym: conflict - a serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one
  • synonym: dispute - an argument or debate, especially over matters of opinion
