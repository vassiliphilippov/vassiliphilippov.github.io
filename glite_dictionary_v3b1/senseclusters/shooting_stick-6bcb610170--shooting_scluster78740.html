shooting stick

noun
photographer's monopod for stabilizing camera
7.0
Pronunciation: /ˈʃuː.tɪŋ stɪk/ (AmE), /ˈʃuː.tɪŋ stɪk/ (BrE)
(in photography) a lightweight monopod or single‑leg camera support, sometimes called a 'shooting stick', used to steady a camera or lens for sharper images.

Examples

Camera shooting stick
The wildlife photographer steadied his lens on a shooting stick to capture the distant subject.
She carried a lightweight shooting stick instead of a tripod on long hikes.
When the light was low, he mounted the camera on a shooting stick to avoid blur.

Related Terms

  • synonym: camera support - a device such as a monopod or tripod used to stabilise a camera
  • synonym: monopod - a single‑leg camera support used to steady cameras and reduce motion blur
