clogger

noun
maker of clogs
Pronunciation: / ˈklɑɡər / (AmE), / ˈklɒɡə / (BrE)
a person who makes, repairs, or sells wooden shoes called clogs

Examples

Skilled clogger crafts shoes
The village clogger crafted sturdy wooden shoes for the farmers every season.
As a skilled clogger, she learned the traditional methods from her grandfather.
The old shop still has a sign that reads 'Master clogger since 1890'.

Related Terms

  • synonym: cobbler - a person who repairs shoes
