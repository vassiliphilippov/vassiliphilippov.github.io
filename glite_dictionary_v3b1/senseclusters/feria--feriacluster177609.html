feria

noun
ordinary ecclesiastical weekday
Pronunciation: / ˈfɛriə / (AmE), / ˈfɪəriə / (BrE)
(in ecclesiastical use) a weekday on which no liturgical feast or holiday is celebrated; an ordinary weekday.

Examples

Church feria
In the medieval church calendar, each feria was marked by specific prayers and readings.
Secunda feria referred to Monday, while Dominicus meant Sunday.
There is no feast today; it is a simple feria in the liturgical calendar.
