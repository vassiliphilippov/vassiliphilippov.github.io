honestly

adverb
sincerely; truly (intensifier)
1.1 27/m
Pronunciation: / Àà…ëÀê.n…™st.li / (AmE), / Àà…ín.…™st.li / (BrE)
Used to emphasize that what you are saying is true, sincere, or reflects your real opinion.

Examples

Honestly speaking
Honestly, I don't think I've ever been more surprised in my life.
Honestly, I have no idea how they managed to finish so quickly.
Honestly, you should have told me about this sooner.
