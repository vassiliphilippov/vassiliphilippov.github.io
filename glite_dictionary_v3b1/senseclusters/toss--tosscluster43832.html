toss

verb
throw a coin in the air to decide something
Pronunciation: / tɑs / (AmE), / tɒs / (BrE)
to throw something, especially a coin, lightly into the air so as to decide something based on which side lands up

Examples

To toss a coin
He reached into his pocket and tossed a coin to decide who would go first.
She tossed the coin in the air while everyone watched closely for the result.
Whenever they can't agree, they simply toss a coin to settle it.

Related Terms

  • synonym: flip - to turn over with a quick movement, especially a coin for decision making
