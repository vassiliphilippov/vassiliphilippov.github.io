store

noun
retail shop for goods or services
1.0 168/m
Pronunciation: / st…îr / (AmE), / st…îÀê / (BrE)
A place where goods or services are sold to the public; a retail shop.

Examples

A grocery store
I stopped at the store on my way home to pick up some groceries.
Many small towns have only one general store serving the whole community.
She works at a clothing store in the city center.

Synonyms

  • shop
    1.0 65/m
    shop is a small, often local place for buying goods, while store is the general word for a retail selling place.
  • retail store
    1.0 2.40/m
    retail store is a business selling goods directly to consumers in small quantities, while store is a common name for shops that sell to the public.
  • sales outlet
    2.6 0.04/m
    sales outlet is a point where products are sold, often by a brand or distributor, while store means any retail shop.
  • outlet
    2.3 6.92/m
    outlet is a shop that often sells a single brand or discounted items, while store is a general place selling goods to the public.
