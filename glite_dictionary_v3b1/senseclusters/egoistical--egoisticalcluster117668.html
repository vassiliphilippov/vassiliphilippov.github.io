egoistical

adjective
selfish; self-centered
7.6 0.02/m
Pronunciation: / ÀåiÀê…°o äÀà…™st…™k…ôl / (AmE), / ÀåiÀê…°…ô äÀà…™st…™k…ôl / (BrE)
Thinking only of yourself and your own interests, not considering others; selfish.

Examples

An egoistical person
Her egoistical attitude made it difficult for her to form close friendships.
The manager's egoistical behavior alienated many of his employees.
They accused him of being egoistical and ignoring the needs of the team.

Synonyms

    1.0 16/m
    selfish cares only about personal gain and comfort, while egoistical focuses on putting one's own interests before others.
  • self-centered
    3.9 2.69/m
    self-centered thinks mostly about personal needs and feelings, while egoistical acts to protect one's own interests over others.
  • egocentric
    7.4 0.92/m
    egocentric views events from a personal point of view, while egoistical prioritizes personal gain over other people's needs.
  • self-serving
    6.4 3.26/m
    self-serving makes choices that benefit oneself, often ignoring harm to others, while egoistical means thinking only of personal interests.
  • self-seeking
    6.9 0.02/m
    self-seeking seeks personal advantage or profit, sometimes at others' expense, while egoistical simply puts self before others.
  • self-absorbed
    6.3 1.63/m
    self-absorbed is wrapped in personal thoughts and feelings, while egoistical acts to gain personal benefit over others.
  • self-involved
    7.0 0.39/m
    self-involved focuses on one's own activities and problems, while egoistical puts personal advantage above other people's needs or feelings.
  • egotistical
    6.6 1.53/m
    egotistical talks and shows pride in oneself, while egoistical mainly means acting to protect one's own interests.
  • narcissistic
    7.8 3.36/m
    narcissistic seeks admiration and has an inflated self-image, while egoistical focuses more on getting personal advantage over others.
