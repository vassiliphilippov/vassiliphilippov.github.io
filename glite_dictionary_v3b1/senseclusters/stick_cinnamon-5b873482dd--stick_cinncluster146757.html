stick cinnamon

noun
cinnamon in dried, rolled strips
4.0 2.5
Pronunciation: / stɪk ˈsɪnəmən / (AmE), / stɪk ˈsɪnəmɪn / (BrE)
Cinnamon sold in the form of dried, rolled strips of bark, rather than ground into powder.

Examples

A stick cinnamon
She added a stick cinnamon to the pot to infuse the cider with a warm flavor.
The recipe called for both stick cinnamon and cloves to enhance the aroma of the stew.
You can buy stick cinnamon at most supermarkets, usually near the baking spices.
