Sunnah

noun
Islamic traditional practice based on Muhammad's example
Pronunciation: / ˈsuːnə / (AmE), / ˈsʊnə / (BrE)
(in Islam) the body of traditional social and legal custom and practice, based on the teachings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad, regarded as a model for Muslims.

Examples

The Sunnah of Muhammad
Many Muslims strive to follow the Sunnah in their daily lives.
Scholars debate which practices are truly part of the Sunnah.
The teachings of the Sunnah complement the guidance found in the Quran.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Hadith - recorded sayings and actions of Muhammad
