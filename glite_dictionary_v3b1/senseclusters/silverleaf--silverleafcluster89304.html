silverleaf

noun
plant or tree disease
Pronunciation: / ˈsɪlvərˌliːf / (AmE), / ˈsɪlvəliːf / (BrE)
a plant with silvery leaves, or a fungal disease that causes leaves of trees to turn silvery

Examples

A silverleaf disease
The gardener pointed out a silverleaf growing near the fence.
The old plum tree was infected with silverleaf and had to be treated.
Many people admire the unique color of the silverleaf in the botanical garden.

Related Terms

  • synonym: powdery mildew - a fungal disease affecting plants
  • synonym: silver birch - a type of tree with pale bark and silvery leaves
