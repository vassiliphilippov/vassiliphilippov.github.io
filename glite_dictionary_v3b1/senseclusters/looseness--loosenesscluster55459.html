looseness

noun
dissolute sensual indulgence
3.1
Pronunciation: / ˈluːs.nəs / (AmE), / ˈluːsnəs / (BrE)
(dated) Dissolute or immoral indulgence in sensual pleasures; lack of restraint in conduct.

Examples

Looseness of morals
He was criticized for the looseness of his lifestyle and disregard for social norms.
Religious leaders often warned against the dangers of looseness among the youth.
The novel shocked some readers with its depiction of looseness and moral decline.

Related Terms

  • synonym: dissoluteness - immoral or excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures
  • synonym: licentiousness - lacking legal or moral restraints, esp. in sexual conduct
