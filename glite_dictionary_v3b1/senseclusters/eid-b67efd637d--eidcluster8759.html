Eid

proper noun
Islamic festival
4.2
Pronunciation: / iːd / (AmE), / iːd / (BrE)
a major Islamic festival celebrated with prayers, gatherings, and feasts

Examples

Eid celebration
Muslims around the world gather to celebrate Eid with family and friends.
On Eid, people often wear new clothes and share festive meals.
Children look forward to receiving gifts and sweets during Eid celebrations.

Related Terms

  • synonym: Christmas - a major Christian festival celebrating the birth of Jesus
  • synonym: Eid al-Adha - the festival of sacrifice in Islam
  • synonym: Eid al-Fitr - the festival marking the end of Ramadan
